<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Wilkinson Asset Management is searching for a Leasing Consultant.</p>
<p>The Leasing Consultant is responsible for</p>
<ul>
<li>Enhancing the residential experience for residents residing at WAM communities.</li>
<li>Responsible for the consumer life cycle including acquisition of consumers, excellent resident service, retention and separation of residents.</li>
<li>Provides information about the apartments and the community to the prospective resident and schedules visits to the community.</li>
<li>Responsible for leasing apartments at an acceptable determined percent of total prospects.</li>
<li>Accurately and efficiently prepares and assists with all leasing paperwork (i.e. applications, lease agreements, addenda, employment checks, credit checks and leasing move-in packets.)</li>
<li>Maintains an accurate and in-depth knowledge of all aspects of the community, particularly in areas such as rent and pricing information, market comps, vacancies, apartment availability, lease expiration's, square footage, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED) equivalent is required.</li>
<li>A minimum of one year sales experience preferred.</li>
<li>Customer service, hospitality, or product sales background desirable.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and organizational skills necessary.</li>
<li>Computer literacy, including experience with computerized property management and/or accounting software preferred.</li>
<li>A flexible work week schedule, a valid driver’s license, and reliable transportation are required.</li>
</ul>
