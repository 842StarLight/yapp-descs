We are currently seeking a full-time Systems Engineer / Network Administrator. Our IT Solutions Consultants are responsible for all technology aspects of their assigned clients. They are able to plan, design, implement and support business level technology solutions. They are involved in all stages of a project – from assisting the sales team in pre-sales meetings to providing ongoing support to the client. Great communication skills are critical as you will need to offer clear and precise explanations to both technical and non-technical individuals. We earn the trust of our customers by listening carefully and working closely with them to come up with the perfect solution for their needs.
<br><br>
Core Technical Competencies
<br><ul>

<li>Ensure compliance with company technical and quality standards/procedures as well as the highest degree of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Escalates problems within parameters.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains client relationships and identifies opportunities to extend existing agreements.</li>
<li>Communicate sales leads.</li>
<li>Maintains effective communication with other team members.</li>
