<h2>Digital Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are looking for an enthusiastic<b> Digital Marketing Specialist</b> to help us in our digital marketing efforts. You will be an integral part of the development and execution of marketing plans to reach targets from brand awareness to product promotion.</p>
<p>
The Digital Marketing Specialist is tasked with assisting in customer communication, strategic plan development (to include conducting research, making marketing recommendations and creating media plans) and assisting with identifying and developing growth opportunities. The digital marketing specialist is responsible for developing and executing online marketing strategies in promoting the organization's products utilizing digital platforms, including social media, web analytics, e-mail marketing, and search engine optimization, etc. They will also collaborate with the marketing and sales teams to develop editorial strategy and publishing schedules with the goal of promoting the brand. This candidate should be able to grasp consumer behavior trends and generate creative ideas. They should also be well-versed in specialized marketing concepts, principles and tactics.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and responsible for assisting in the Marketing team may include but are not limited to the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with the team to help plan and create direct email and ad campaigns</li>
<li>Help create unique and relevant topics/ posts/ ads that suit audiences' interest through Facebook, Instagram and other relevant channels</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Upload articles/blogs, images, and videos to the website and other media outlets</li>
<li>Assist in creating the story-lines; timing, segmentation, and strategy behind all email marketing campaigns
</li>
<li>Help maintain and oversee the company's website, including the configuration and editing of product categories, copy and image proofing; concepts for graphics, cross-sells, promotions, and flash sales</li>
<li>Responsible for creating and executing paid and unpaid social media Ad campaigns via Facebook, Instagram, and Google</li>
<li>Responsible for expanding and executing well-thought digital advertising plans and designs, including landing pages, content marketing, and SEO; video and other sponsored media campaigns</li>
<li>Responsible for generating new leads by converting site traffic through calls-to-action, landing pages, and offers.</li>
<li>Work with the Content Writer to help build out relevant and trending category pages as new products are added to the site</li>
<li>Assist in analyzing marketing data (campaign results, conversion rates, traffic etc.) to help shape future marketing strategies</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Maintaining project timelines with the internal teams</li>
<li>Assisting on Ecom strategies as it relates to the following:<ul>
<li>
Pulling product images and formatting for sites specifications</li>
<li>Creating content and posts for social media platforms</li>
<li>Inventory planning and reporting</li>
<li>Identifying best sellers and strategy to capitalize on those data insights</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
An undergraduate degree, or equivalent experience, in Marketing, Business Administration, Communications or other related field that shows ability of high proficiency for detail, general marketing knowledge, communication, and analytics</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written skills
</li>
<li>Strong understanding of industry trends and e-commerce web tools; understanding of SEO concepts; familiarity with Google Apps and Google Analytics</li>
<li>Knowledge and capabilities of WordPress CMS, basic HTML, and CSS concepts.
</li>
<li>Great eye for design and basic skills with Adobe CC (In-Design, Photoshop, Illustrator)</li>
<li>Strong understanding of social media platforms and how each platform works for engaging its audiences</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Word and Excel</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, with strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills; team player</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of marketing elements (including traditional and digital marketing such as SEO/Social media etc.) and market research methods</li>
</ul>
