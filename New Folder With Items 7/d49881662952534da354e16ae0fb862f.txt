<h2>Social Media Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Carlisle Construction Materials (CCM), is a global leader in the building materials industry. We've been around since 1917. In just the past six years, our company has grown by nearly 70%.
<br><br>
If you love a good challenge, you'll fit right in.
<br><br>
We are looking for a Social Media Coordinator to join our Marketing team in Carlisle, PA.
<br><br>
The Social Media Coordinator must be a self-motivated individual who gives strong attention to detail, has experience writing for social media, and is task-driven. The chosen candidate will be required to develop, maintain, and execute social media campaigns for CCM's commercial roofing businesses to support the marketing automation initiatives. Additional responsibilities will include assistance with e-communications, maintaining a marketing database within Marketo (marketing automation software), and providing monthly measurement/performance reports.
<br><br>
This position may require occasional travel.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop editorial calendar for various social media outlets across commercial roofing brands</li>
<li>Collaborate with Marketing, Sales and Product Development teams to ensure that content is informative and engaging</li>
<li>Facilitate online conversations with customers and respond to queries</li>
<li>Help create and define social media strategies</li>
<li>Ensure branding and marketing messages are consistent</li>
<li>Provide monthly metrics and reports to measure success rates</li>
<li>Assist in development and execution of e-communications</li>
</ul>
