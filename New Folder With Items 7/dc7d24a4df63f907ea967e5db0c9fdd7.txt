<p>If you have a passion for making a difference in the lives of older people, we’d love to talk to you.</p>
<p><br>
In the role of Advertising Manager, you will be responsible for directing advertising activities and developing creative, consistent, brand-specific ad campaigns.<br>
<br>
<b>Advertising Manager Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the overall performance of the company’s advertising spend. Develops strategies for growth based on opportunity markets, media mix, ROI, and business goals.</li>
<li>
Responsible for strategic planning, campaign performance, and budget allocation of digital and traditional advertising.</li>
<li>
Drives the development and execution of media plans across channels including Digital, Mobile, Search, Social Media, Print, and Out of Home. In certain markets, radio and tv are a focus.</li>
<li>
Oversees the implementation, optimization and performance of media campaigns.</li>
<li>
Manages multiple digital and media agencies and drives performance that exhibits a deep skillset in agency management.</li>
<li>
Partners with Marketing Analytics and Finance to implement performance tracking, testing, and thorough campaign reporting. Tracks advertising efforts though the entire marketing funnel, from lead acquisition to conversion.</li>
<li>
Collaborates extensively with team members in Content, Creative Services, Field Marketing, and Sales.</li>
<li>
Leverages a compelling understanding of the usage behaviors of key customer segments: residents, family member influencers, and referents.</li>
<li>
Ability to draw actionable insights from campaign data, media attribution, and market data.</li>
<li>
Plays a key role as the company’s advertising expert.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years experience in advertising with experience across multiple advertising formats and channels.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated knowledge of SEM, Digital, and Print advertising. Experience in outdoor, radio, and television is a plus.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success with lead generation and awareness building.</li>
<li>
Experience managing media and advertising agencies is a significant plus.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success working with cross-functional teams and building strong relationships internally and externally.</li>
<li>
A solid understanding of best practices for inbound marketing.</li>
<li>
Experience with landing page best practices, A/B testing fundamentals, and conversion optimization.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated expertise in working with data and creating reports that tell a story and drive decision making.</li>
<li>
BA preferred, or equivalent experience.</li>
</ul>
