<h2>Product Support Service Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Company Information: </b></p>
<p>Kalmar keeps global trade moving. Our innovative solutions contribute to every fourth container movement in the world and help transform the future of cargo handling. We believe in collaboration, delivering on our promises and that people make the difference.</p>
<p>Kalmar offers the widest range of cargo handling solutions and services to ports, terminals, distribution centers and to the heavy industry. Kalmar is the industry forerunner in terminal automation and in energy-efficient container handling, with one in four container movements around the globe being handled by a Kalmar solution. Through its extensive product portfolio, global service network and ability to enable a seamless integration of different terminal processes, Kalmar improves the efficiency of every move.</p>
<p><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<p>We are looking for a self-motivated, energetic team player to join our organization. This position will play an important part in and contribute to the success of our US organization.</p>
<p>This position is responsible for providing maintenance &amp; repair service to all brands of Container handling equipment at customer locations with a focus on Electrical repair and diagnosis. The candidate will also be responsible for assisting personnel in the field, with documentation, report of labor, work orders and inspections.</p>
<p>If you are seeking a challenging and rewarding career, are motivated and have a strong work ethic, and are interested in working in a world-class, global environment with state-of-the-art projects and technology then we invite you to apply for our role. Preference will be given to military veterans and those with advanced technical training and experience.</p>
<p><b>Benefits: </b></p>
<p>We offer a supportive, challenging and diverse working environment with competitive compensation and fantastic best-in-class benefits packages, including 401(k), medical, dental, vision and life insurance plans with paid vacation and nine paid holidays. We value passion and commitment and reward your performance.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Providing timely diagnosis/repair and regular preventative maintenance to the Kalmar product lines, utilizing hand, air and electric hand tools;</li>
<li>Communicate with the customer to determine equipment problems and, when necessary, travel to customer location to make adjustments/repairs on units.</li>
<li>Must be capable of using Microsoft Word, Excel, Access and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Assist with the commissioning of new equipment into service. Ensure that equipment is operating according to specifications. Train the customer to operate the equipment. Provide the customer with equipment service manuals and other materials according to contract specifications. This includes the preparation and updating of PowerPoint presentations that will be used for training customers.</li>
<li>Maintain follow up with the customer to provide additional information or to resolve problems.</li>
<li>Relay design or functional problems identified in field operations to Engineering. Relay information concerning customer needs and problems to Sales and Engineering and assist with current production projects as needed.</li>
<li>Program and test Electronic Equipment for Spare Parts or Warranties.</li>
<li>Install software upgrades in the field.</li>
<li>All visits will be documented with a call report once a trip has been completed.</li>
<li>Make travel reservations including, airline, and hotel and car rental. A valid Driver’s License and Passport are required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma required</li>
<li>1+ year of experience in mechanical or electrical troubleshooting, and equipment repairs.</li>
<li>Candidates must have a Valid Driver’s license, and be able to obtain a TWIC card and Waterfront pass.</li>
<li>Preference will be given to Veteran’s and those with an advanced technical degree and/or training</li>
<li>Commercial or Technical background</li>
<li>Specific experience with Motors, Controls, PLC’s, hydraulics, steelwork and/or rigging is helpful.</li>
<li>Candidates must be able to work at elevated heights</li>
<li>Experience in commercial and/or large electronic equipment a plus</li>
<li>Strong aptitude toward control system logic</li>
<li>Theoretical and applied knowledge of pneumatic, hydraulic and electrical systems related to mobile equipment preferred</li>
<li>Good organization skills</li>
<li>Demonstrate a strong work ethic, dependability, and initiative.</li>
<li>Good communication skills</li>
<li>Fluent in English both spoken and written</li>
<li>Good problem-solving skills</li>
</ul>
