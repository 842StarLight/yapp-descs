<h2>Financial Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Coloplast has an exciting opportunity for a Financial Analyst (Commissions), to join our North American Finance Team.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You will manage the sales force commission plan, design, and execution for US organizations. In this role you will also deliver high quality analysis and collaboration across the organization, with fact-based discussions on sales performance management and sales compensation. The daily work will take place in a talented and dynamic team environment with frequent exposure to our North American Chronic Care leadership team.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Major Areas of Accountability
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage sales force commission plan design and execution for the US organization (includes development of commission statements, ranking reports, target setting and liaising between management, sales reporting entities and payroll to process payment)
</li>
<li>Manage Consumer Advisor and Consumer Sales commission plan design and execution
</li>
<li>Develop a set of standard reporting services to commercial functions
</li>
<li>Maintain analytics to track sales force performance on a monthly basis and provide reports to the stakeholders
</li>
<li>Lead the development and implementation of rules and processes for proper data governance model
</li>
<li>Conduct complex analyses and recommend appropriate actions for businesses
</li>
<li>Monitor sales &amp; market developments that impact commercial performance and identify areas of opportunity; report developments and opportunities to Sales leaders
</li>
<li>Maintain data integrity through appropriate follow-up, and adjust data governance model as needed; Partner with Business Intelligence to understand Sales data
</li>
<li>Participate in key strategic projects to improve our commercial performance
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business, Economics, Finance or equivalent
</li>
<li>Minimum two years of experience in finance or business analysis, preferably with experience working in the healthcare or medical device industry
</li>
<li>Ability to troubleshoot issues in Microsoft Excel and explain the findings to Sales personnel in non-technical language<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities
</b></p>
<p><i>The successful candidate will experience in developing and implementing standard reports and data management to a variety of audiences across functional areas:
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with developing and/or executing sales compensation models preferred
</li>
<li>Proven track record with consistent high performance in data analysis and financial/sales presentations
</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and complete tasks efficiently and independently with a high degree of accuracy to meet deadlines
</li>
<li>Strong analytical, investigative and documentation skills
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to make effective decisions independently and to challenge the status quo and create best practices
</li>
<li>Outstanding communications and interpersonal skills, including developing relationships at all levels in an organization and directly influencing business objectives; Must be comfortable and confident in interactions with Sales leaders
</li>
<li>Self-motivated and high energy person who thrives in an entrepreneurial, fast-paced environment characterized by growth, change, and opportunity
</li>
<li>Highly proficient in Microsoft Office platform products (Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, Word)
</li>
<li>Experience with Cognos, Enterprise One, and data warehouse structures<br>
</li>
</ul>
