<h2>Talent Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We’re on the search for a strategic marketer, skillful storyteller and social media maven to help elevate Adobe's brand as an employer of choice in a competitive talent market. We’re looking for someone who’s passionate about online and experiential marketing, messaging, and data analytics. You’ll thrive in this role if you’re a creative and analytical thinker. This role is based in San Jose, California. If you’re up for the challenge, please continue reading.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you’ll do
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Build and strengthen Adobe’s employer brand – Communicate Adobe’s unique employee value proposition to candidates and employees through impactful marketing programs across offline and online channels.
</li>
<li>Lead career-related online properties – Maintain and optimize content on Adobe’s Career and Diversity sites, as well as career channels like LinkedIn, Glassdoor, BuiltIn, The Muse, etc. You’ll also be responsible for building landing pages for events and monitoring traffic to our online sites, making recommendations for improvement, and working with external partners.
</li>
<li>Grow and run Adobe’s social media presence – The world needs to experience Adobe Life. You’ll be responsible for executing and handling social media strategy and campaigns for Adobe Careers, Adobe Life and Adobe For All (our diversity and inclusion initiative). This includes driving paid campaigns on Twitter and Instagram, as well as producing short social media videos that highlight Adobe Life. You will partner with cross-functional teams to drive followership and growth of Adobe’s recruitment channels and our hashtags (#AdobeLife and #AdobeForAll), including handling daily social media posts and engagement.
</li>
<li>Create memorable experiences that drive engagement – You’ll have the creative license to conceptualize and implement employee and candidate activations that drive engagement and support our recruitment needs. You’ll have the opportunity to craft impactful activations for our annual Adobe For All Summit; our cultural moments throughout the year; award recognitions, and more.
</li>
<li>Attract and engage University talent – You’ll be the main point of contact for all Talent Marketing needs for University recruiting including running the production of Adobe’s presence at the annual Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing event.
</li>
<li>Data analysis to drive decision-making – Monitor and report on site analytics, identify key trends to improve results or influence planning decisions, and measure marketing efforts against overall objectives. You’ll also produce a quarterly Talent Marketing dashboard.
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you need to succeed
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, PR or a similar field.
</li>
<li>Shown work experience as a talent/recruitment marketing specialist (2+ years). Candidates with marketing or advertising experience are also welcomed to apply.
</li>
<li>Creative thinker and dedicated problem solver who can conceptualize innovative and impactful talent marketing ideas.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of recruitment marketing and employer branding strategies.
</li>
<li>Experience in social media content development and management.
</li>
<li>Experience conceptualizing and implementing experiential activations.
</li>
<li>Comfortable designing collateral for website, print and social media.
</li>
<li>Experience developing and editing short videos.
</li>
<li>Hands on experience with marketing analytics software and data reporting.
</li>
<li>Experience with recruitment marketing and content management software preferred.
</li>
<li>Shown understanding of the recruitment process and candidate journey.
</li>
<li>Great collaborator with strong communications and social skills.
</li>
<li>Extraordinary writing skills.
</li>
<li>Experience using software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere, Microsoft PowerPoint, Word and Excel.
</li>
<li>Pay acute attention to detail and have a high bar for quality.
</li>
<li>Energetic, go-getter, positive attitude and a great teammate.
</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and prioritize daily workload.
</li>
</ul>
