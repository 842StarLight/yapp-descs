<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Company Introduction:<br>
At Flockjay, we're on a mission to make tech accessible to people from diverse backgrounds. We believe that education and access change lives, and every member of the workforce deserves economic opportunity. We invest in our students, train and coach them for free, and only get paid if they get a job.<br>
<br>
Our company provides training and support for the most in-demand tech jobs, and guarantees final round interviews for high-paying jobs at top companies. Whether we are developing curriculum for our online sales academy, or providing coaching to our students, people at Flockjay are builders and teachers at heart.<br>
<br>
We have an experienced team and an incredible network of support from industry leaders, advisors, and investors like Y Combinator. Our team is small and growing - join us and make a meaningful impact. We’re just getting started!<br>
<br>
Overview:<br>
Who You Are:<br>
<br>
Are you a curious and self-motivated leader who loves the thrill of building &amp; nurturing key partnerships? Do you thrive in a dynamic, team-focused environment of diverse and passionate coworkers who inspire you to be better every day? Are compassion and continuous learning core to who you are? If so, please consider joining our team!<br>
<br>
You will have the opportunity to make a tremendous impact from day one by developing key enterprise partnerships and building trust with our top-tier platform partners. You will qualify, advise and educate potential partners, and partner with sales team leaders to drive revenue by negotiating complex opportunities. You won’t be afraid to pick up the phone, get out of the office, and make things happen.<br>
<br>
Your success in this role is defined by strong communication skills, a coachable mindset, problem-solving ability, and deep empathy for our students and customers. You’ll report directly to our Founder/CEO and work closely with other team leads on strategic partnerships initiatives that affect the company.<br>
