<h2>ATM Services Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Team Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Remote Banking Technologies team handles high volume hardware, software, and application support requests. This team works a variable and flexible schedule, including nights and weekends during on-call rotation, to ensure the hardware and software applications are fully functional, secure, and available at all times. This team utilizes significant technology resources and capabilities to improve the efficiency of their work and to ensure the hardware and software solutions in place remain innovative compared to industry benchmarks.</p>
<p><b>
Specialist Role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>To provide maintenance for the ITM/ATM (Interactive Teller Machine/Automated Teller Machine) fleet, onsite and remotely, including hardware and software updates, upgrades, patching, testing, and development.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To ensure ITMs/ATMs are fully functional and available to members at all times.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To provide software and application support via a ticketed query system.</p>
</li>
<li><p>To schedule ITM/ATM vault cash, conduct machine alarm testing, machine inspections, and assist in branch and teller support.
</p></li>
<li><p>Respond to inquiries and maintenance requests from internal and external members pertaining to ITMs/ATMs.
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintain current knowledge of ITM/ATM hardware/software and be aware of industry trends.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Provide support for other Vantage Credit Union initiatives and projects (software/hardware installs/upgrades, application testing, etc.) and coordinate with vendors as needed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Act as branch liaison as the branch transformation expert on product, service, and process enhancements related to branch transformations.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Major Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
30%</p>
<p><b>
Focus on Individual Contributions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Accuracy Rate- Ensure member transactions are processed accurately by following established policies and procedures. Performs accounting functions including ITM/ATM and department reconciliation and analyses.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ITM/ATM Servicing- Ensure all machines are fully equipped for proper function at all times. Provide first line maintenance and perform regular ITM/ATM inspections and cleaning for assigned machines. Monitor extended hours service for machines based on our on-call rotation schedule. Adhere to our Service Level Agreement by responding to ticketed queries within 10 minutes and servicing machines within 2 hours of being notified.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sound Decision Making- Ensure your decision making is handled in a timely fashion and adherence to risk guidelines with every member interaction.</p>
</li>

<p>Operational Integrity- Ensure all confidential and sensitive information and data are secured and protected at all times. Adhere to department guidelines, processes, policies, and procedures.</p>
