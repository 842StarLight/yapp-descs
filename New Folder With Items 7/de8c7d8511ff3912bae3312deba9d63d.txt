<h2>Senior Commercial Fire Alarm Installer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Description
<br><br>
Senior Commercial Fire Alarm Installer
<br><br>
-
<br><br>
WD30058952337
<br><br>
What
<br><br>
<b>you will do:</b><br>
Under
<br>general supervision, perform programming, commissioning, troubleshooting and
<br>repair of Fire Alarm Systems per contract obligations. Possesses technical
<br>competence on Fire Alarm Systems and the ability to resolve or guide others
<br>through systems problem resolution. Provides detailed information to
<br>communicate design to customers, installation or service team and
<br>subcontractors.
<br><br>
How
<br><br>
<b>you will do it:</b><br>
Adhere to all OSHA and Company Safety
<br>Standards with a high degree of regard to employee, subcontractor and
<br>customer safety.
<br><br>
Configure low voltage systems, which
<br>utilize both core (Simplex/Tyco) and third party fire alarm products and
<br>systems /electrical technology as defined by the project specification
<br>while maintaining quality and ensuring customer satisfaction.
<br><br>
Read and interpret complicated blueprints,
<br>diagrams, submittals, specifications software/systems, programs,
<br>schematics and operational product manuals.
<br><br>
Execute projects on time and within
<br>allocated installation hours.
<br><br>
Exhibit excellent customer service skills
<br>by consistently communicating with the operations team staff and project
<br>managers to ensure customer satisfaction.
<br><br>
Assists engineering and sales by
<br>providing technical recommendations to produce job layouts and bid
<br>proposals.
<br><br>
Complete field reporting and required
<br>project/safety documentation daily, through communication devices such as
<br>smart phones and laptop computers.
<br><br>
Participate in a scheduled On-call
<br>rotation.
<br><br>
Perform other duties as assigned.
<br><br>
Qualifications
<br><br>
What
<br><br>
<b>we look for:</b><br>
Required
<br><br>
Technician License
<br><br>
Preferred
<br><br>
Five (5) years’ experience in addressable and conventional
<br>Fire Alarm Systems
<br><br>
– OR -
<br><br>
Two (2) year associate degree in a technical field such as
<br>electronics, electricity or equivalent.
<br><br>
Ability to work flexible hours including early mornings to
<br>meet customer requirements.
<br><br>
Excellent computer skills (Microsoft Office Suite).
<br><br>
Strong oral and written communication skills.
<br><br>
Excellent customer interaction skills.
<br><br>
Demonstrated ability to multi-task in a fast-paced
<br>environment.
<br><br>
Knowledge of test equipment to include oscilloscope,
<br>multi-meters, digital analyzers, battery analyzers, frequency meters,
<br>decibel meters and communication devices.
<br><br>
Pass criminal background check and drug test.
<br><br>
Capable of performing physical labor to include carrying
<br>and moving equipment and tools up to 50-70 lbs.
<br><br>
Able to work in unusual and sometimes difficult position
<br>such as climbing ladders, scaffolding and high lift equipment up to 40
<br>feet, working within ducts, crawl spaces, above ceilings, etc.
<br><br>
Have a valid driver’s license and a driving record that
<br>meets company requirements.
<br><br>
Ability to troubleshoot and make repairs, unaided by
<br>management.
<br><br>
Familiarity with NFPA 72, 70, and other applicable
<br>National, State and Local codes.
<br><br>
Ability to retain any licenses that are required.
<br><br>
Self-starter needed that can work with minimum supervision.
<br><br>
Completion of manufacturers training within 12 months.
<br><br>
NICET II certification or higher preferred.
<br><br>

<br><br>
Supportive team
<br>environment, flexible schedule, mobile device, laptop, company vehicle. Add to
<br>that a competitive salary, best in class benefits package that starts on day 1,
<br>which includes medical, dental &amp; vision, 401(k), tuition reimbursement,
<br>paid time off and career growth opportunities.
