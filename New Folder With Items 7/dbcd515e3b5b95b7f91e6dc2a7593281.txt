<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY: </b><br>Provides excellent customer service as a first point of contact to retail customers and maintains the sales floor to ensure an exciting shopping experience. Partners with team members in a fast-paced environment to expedite merchandise for sale and display on the sales floor; provides operational sales and support to management team.</p>
<p><b>NOTE TO APPLICANT: </b><br>Please consider the job location when reviewing open positions and apply for those jobs to which you can travel. As a member of Goodwill of Greater Washington's Retail Staff, and as a condition of employment, you will be required to work a set schedule in support of the store opening, production, and closing operations. <b>This schedule will include work on some weekends and holidays.</b></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>1. Provide professional and courteous service to all customers inside the store, outside the store, and on the phone.<br>2. Demonstrate accurate cash handling skills to include but is not limited to making change, operate and maintain the cash register, check and charge card machines, and use of a calculator.<br>3. Maintains knowledge of merchandise and answers customer's questions courteously and promptly.<br>4. Keep floor merchandise neat and orderly (racks, shelves, bins, and hangers), and assist customers with purchase selection if requested.</p>

<li>Multilingual with complete fluency in English.</li>
</ul>
