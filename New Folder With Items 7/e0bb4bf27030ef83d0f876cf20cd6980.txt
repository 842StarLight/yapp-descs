<h2>Admin Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
</div></div>
<p>Assists the Community Manager and the Board of Directors with the overall operation of the Association. Provides administrative support and has regular contact with homeowners and board members.</p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<p>Meets regularly with assigned Community Managers to ensure they are receiving full support and aware of work progress and issues requiring their attention.</p>
<ul>
<li>Receives and responds to calls and emails related to their portfolio of assigned associations from homeowners, Board members and vendors. Follows through on various requests and issues.</li>
<li>Coordinates sale and lease applications form inception to Board approval, lease renewals, voting certificates, etc.</li>
<li>Processes and tracks violation notices to comply with association CC&amp;R's. Obtains and summarizes proposals on various projects for community managers.</li>
<li>Prepares and assists community managers with monthly board packages.</li>
<li>Performs duties of community managers as needed, including attending monthly meetings and/or property inspections in the manager's absence. Except where prohibited by State Law or Statues.</li>
<li>Processes incoming proxies by alphabetizing and logging on to membership listing. Attends annual meetings to provide administrative support with the community managers.</li>
<li>Dispatches routine work orders, monitor for completion and refer difficult or complex problems or situation to Manager.</li>
<li>The preceding functions have been provided as examples of the types of work performed by employees assigned to this job classification. Management reserves the right to add, modify, change or rescind the work of different assignment positions.</li>
<li>Assists with other jobs and duties as necessary.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br><div>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>
<ul>
<li><b>Knowledge and Skills</b></li>
<li>Computer proficient with software applications programs such as Word and Windows.</li>
<li>Effective written and verbal communication with management, staff, residents, contractors and vendors; organize, prioritize and complete tasks independently. May be expected to work extended hours or overtime.</li>
<li>Interpersonal skills necessary to effectively interface with all levels of personnel and work in a team environment.</li>
<li>Knowledge of business correspondence including rules of grammar, sentence structure, punctuation, and spelling.</li>
<li>May be expected to work extended hours or overtime.</li>
<li>Ability to interpret and follow verbal and written instructions.</li>
<li>Ability to operate standard office equipment.</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of Company policies, procedures and forms.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to maintain confidentiality of records and to use discretion in the performance of daily activities.</li>
<li>Requires the ability to prioritize and handle multiple actions simultaneously with frequent interruptions and critical time constraints.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<br>
</div></div>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Education and Experience</b></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
</div></div>
<p>Typically requires a high school education and 2 years of directly related customer service experience. Prior training or coursework in community relations desired. Knowledge of community association practices desired.</p>
