<h2>Marketing Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Veneer Services® is a growing company and a great place to work.</p>
<p>We're looking for a marketing assistant to help us continue our growth as we reach into new sectors and continue to develop innovate equipment.</p>
<p>Veneer Services® supplies veneer, plywood, and sawmill industries with manufacturing equipment and engineering. Our material handling division, Biomass Engineering &amp; Equipment, provides bulk material handling solutions for a variety of industries. The company is best known for its innovative SMART Conveyors—the most advanced drag conveyor system on the market. CIA Veneer, based in Iowa, produces architectural veneer panels.</p>
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>The Marketing Assistant will report to the Marketing Manager and is responsible for qualifying leads, developing and distributing content, communicating with clients, maintaining advertising campaigns, assuring consistent branding, and maintaining the websites for the company and its divisions: Veneer Services (www.veneerservices.com), Biomass Engineering &amp; Equipment (www.biomassengineeringequipment.com), and CIA Veneer (www.ciaveneer.com).</p>
<p><b>Core Job Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the sales team and Marketing Manager to develop and qualify leads</li>
<li>Make contact with potential and new clients via phone or email</li>
<li>Email campaigns: design, write, schedule, distribute using marketing software</li>
<li>Social media: Post and identify leads (LinkedIn, YouTube)</li>
<li>Content creation: blogs, articles, press releases, emails, video, graphics, illustrations</li>
<li>Print: Brochures, business cards, other print material</li>
<li>CRM: Data entry, minor system administration</li>
<li>Technical editing: machine manuals</li>
<li>Other: Design- and photography-related tasks for engineering, office, and production; ensure branding is applied to products; update reports; customer-relations tasks</li>
<li>Scheduling, developing, and hosting webinars</li>
<li>Websites: develop and update</li>
<li>Organizing trade shows</li>
</ul>
