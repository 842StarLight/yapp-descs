About this Role
<br>-
<br><br>
The Enterprise Account Executive is responsible for managing relationships and selling Parsable's Cloud base solution into a set of 30 - 40 new business prospect accounts, within the key vertical markets of Consumer Package Goods, Energy and Industrial Manufacturing all of significant size/complexity. The AE acts as a strategic adviser for prospects with prime responsibility of driving the new sales of the Parsable solution using consultative and value based selling techniques, interacting with multiple levels of people within their set of target accounts. Parsable is a fast growing Cloud based software solution that is transforming the way deskless workers execute work. 80% of the worlds workforce works away from a desk. These 2.7 billion people are exposed on a daily basis to challenges around efficiency, safety and quality. That's where the Parsable Connected Worker platform comes in. Currently deployed in over 60 countries with major global deployments at fortune 500 companies such as Georgia Pacific, Halliburton, Heineken, Honeywell, PepsiCo, Sealed Air, Shell, Schlumberger, Smurfit Kappa and TechnipFMC.
