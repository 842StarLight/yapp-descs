<h2>Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Solution Architect would work closely and proactively with On-Field Business Development team and extended sales teams to clearly articulate and demonstrate Nexthink value proposition. Solution Architect gather unique customer requirements. Once understood, documented, and validated, these requirements are configured and customized into comprehensive demonstration(s) &amp; presentation(s) of the capabilities of Nexthink. This individual must develop an acute understanding of customer requirements, competitor capabilities and emerging technologies, and having built that understanding, must work with the Business Development Executive (BD) to uniquely position and differentiate Nexthink in order to win the business.</p>

Responsibilities:</b></p>

<li>
Ability to design, develop and maintain Sales Demo Setups.</li>
<li>

<li>
Work in team to deliver creative options for customers and enable sales.</li>

<li>
Design and deliver customer presentations articulately and confidently that target the customers’ technical and business needs</li>

<li>
