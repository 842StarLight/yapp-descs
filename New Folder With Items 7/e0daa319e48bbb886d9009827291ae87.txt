<h2>Director of Enterprise Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Introduction
<br>The Director of Enterprise Sales, reporting directly the SVP of Enterprise Sales, is responsible for realizing revenue through the entire sales process across international payments and risk management solutions – planning, lead generation, developing business cases, closing and implementing and maximizing opportunities.
<br><br>
Main Responsibilities
<br><br>
<b>The job duties include, but are not limited to:</b><br>
Achieve monthly sales and / or channel goals as assigned by management
<br>Develop new business within a specified territory and or vertical market(s)
<br>Develop new and targeted product strategies that add short term and long term revenue
<br><br>
As a Director of Enterprise Sales, you will be responsible for planning and executing strategic and tactical plans to achieve the region’s revenue targets through the acquisition of new business relationships. The primary focus of the Director of Enterprise Sales to sustain and grow the usage of the company’s services within their geographic region and product focus. In addition, this position can, over time, also manage a regional team and help them achieve their financial targets.
<br><br>
This position requires the ability to analyze current environments, identify potential business opportunities, create a compelling business solution for prospects, and implement the solution and to generate revenue by making sales to both existing and new customers.
<br><br>
<b>Key Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
Present the Company’s global payment solutions to corporations in the US and potentially to Global Businesses to successfully convert these prospects into the Company’s clients. Important is to evaluate acquisition cost vs. revenue – targeting, medium, large organizations and verticals.
<br>Customarily and regularly engaged away from Company and home office locations to solicit new prospects and make sales.
<br>Achieving your personal revenue/profit targets by prospecting and attracting new business to Cambridge with the identified target companies.
<br>Expanding the depth of our client relationships with new business opportunities by conducting professional and effective relationship management.
<br>Leveraging your industry network to open up new opportunities for Cambridge by; attracting new talent, identifying potential client opportunities, providing industry insight, building market awareness of Cambridge and representing us in the industry.
<br>Working closely with the other senior leaders of the North American business to grow our overall presence, revenue and status as a highly respected service provider in the cross-border payments category and an excellent place to work.
<br>Tracking and updating opportunities and account management via Salesforce (or other CRM) for weekly reviews.
