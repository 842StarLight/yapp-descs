<h2>Director of Business Development (South Texas)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position: Director of Business Development</b></p>
<p><b>
Location: San Antonio, Texas</b></p>
<br>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p>Yates is proud to be an industry leader in construction services. To be a top of class organization, Yates is dedicated to hiring individuals who understand the importance of safety; instills integrity in all aspects of their lives; are passionate about what they do; and committed to providing value to our clients.</p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<p>
Reporting to the South Texas Division Manager, this position includes the development and deployment of a measurable strategy, marketing and sales plan for the South Texas market focused on selling construction services. This business development position would be a component of the overall Yates Construction business development team, leveraging corporate resources to progress the overall Yates Companies work acquisition efforts.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor s business degree required with marketing or management emphasis desired, or equivalent combination of professional training and experience.
</li>
<li>Minimum of 10 years experience in the construction industry or in planning and implementing business development strategies, proposals and presentations.
</li>
<li>Proven track record of selling construction or a related service.</li>
<li>Ideal candidate would have some construction field experience or business development experience.</li>
<li>Must possess excellent communication skills [verbal, written and graphic communication] and the ability to collaborate with marketing in the production of highly professional proposals and presentations.</li>
<li>Computer skills: Demonstrated working knowledge of Microsoft Excel, Word, PowerPoint; Adobe Creative Programs with the ability to put together proposals and presentation material without the assistance of others.</li>
<li>Know, believe in and practice company s Core Values and Corporate Vision.
</li>
<li>Represent the company at professional organizations. Be an active member of the community and member of appropriate community organizations</li>
<li>Have a Positive Can Do Attitude. Lead internally by example.</li>
<li>Take pride and ownership in Company Projects.</li>
<li>Contribute to continuous company improvement. Look for opportunities to improve the business.</li>
<li>Strong networking, communication and listening skills.</li>
<li>Ability to mine for information â€“ ask questions and be ready to listen.</li>
<li>Marketing of company s abilities and unique qualities to potential clients.</li>
<li>Follow-up with potential and past clients to ensure satisfaction</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Author a detailed, targeted and measurable business development plan for the South Texas market. Execute the plan and make adjustments as necessary as market conditions change. Support will be provided to business development representative from the entire Yates business development and marketing teams.</li>
<li>Provide strategies, tactics and plans for new business development. Track efforts and return on investment against spend and time spent. Cultivation of new business opportunities within a geographic, financial and product type parameters.
</li>
<li>Organize efforts with the CRM. Research and prioritize potential customers and new business opportunities. Present progress to management team. Conduct ongoing market research to determine the health of the market and help dictate company s focus and strategy for business development. This is a comprehensive and vital task.
</li>
<li>Become an active member in trade associations and other professional organizations to continue to develop a network of potential clients and leads and promote the brand.</li>
<li>Create thorough proposals and presentations with the help of the Senior Director of Business Development and marketing team. Work to make more creative and engaging proposals through leveraging lessons learned from the entire business development and marketing team. Supervise the development of proposals and presentations.
</li>
<li>Create a network of references. Attend trade shows and other relevant events as needed to support the entire Yates organization. Travel will be expected throughout the Southeast.</li>
<li>Meet client s needs prior to contract execution. Provide advice, liaison, planning, etc. to prospective and current clients. Assist management and estimating with sales process and final close.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with the other Business Development personnel throughout the Company to assure that the overall company message and identity is consistent across the enterprise.
</li>
<li>Stay current on industry standards, contracts, and terms.
</li>
</ul>
