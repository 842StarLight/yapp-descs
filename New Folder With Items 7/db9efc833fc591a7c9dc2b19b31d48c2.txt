<h2>Crop Insurance Specialist</h2>

<p><b>Description/Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The leading provider of credit and financial services to the agricultural sector, AgChoice Farm Credit, has a challenging opportunity for an individual to manage a complex crop insurance portfolio. We would like to hire an Agent to work out of the Meadville branch and serve Western PA.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Deliver effective insurance services to assure continued association growth and profitability.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide quality, professional service to meet and/or exceed customer expectations.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide technical training and direction for branch product referrals in the insurance area and contribute to the association's overall level of knowledge concerning crop insurance.</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain insurance product knowledge and expertise and enhance Crop Growers image and reputation</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Education and/or experience equivalent to a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Agriculture, Economics or Finance.</p></li>
<li><p>
PA property and casualty licenses required during the first 3 months.</p></li>
<li><p>
Strong analysis, judgment and communication, oral or written, skills.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to be assertive and decisive in arriving at sound business decisions which serves the best interest of the association and customer.</p></li>
<li><p>
Strong attention to detail.</p></li>
<li><p>
Current awareness of economic developments, and productions technology affecting agriculture in the region.</p></li>
<li><p>
A working knowledge of computer software and hardware.</p></li>
<li><p>
Farm background with related practical experience.</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain valid driver's license.</p></li>
<li>
Sales experience preferred.</li>

Successful candidate should reside near Meadville area.</li>
