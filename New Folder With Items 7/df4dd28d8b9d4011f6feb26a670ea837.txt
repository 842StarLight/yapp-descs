<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Optoro is a fast-growing technology company that is revolutionizing the retail industry. Every year, more than 15% of retail goods are returned or simply never sell. This creates tons of unnecessary waste and costs retailers billions.
<br><br>
Our mission is to make retail more sustainable by eliminating all waste from returns. Our technology platform connects every returned item to its best home, thereby increasing profitability for retailers, giving consumers great deals, and reducing environmental waste.
<br><br>
Backed by some of the top investors in the country - including Kleiner Perkins, Revolution Growth, and UPS - Optoro is powered by its collaborative, unconventional, and resourceful employees who love solving big problems. We are looking for individuals with similar creativity and energy to help build a lasting company focused on the triple bottom line.
<br><br>
Product Marketing Manager
<br><br>
Reporting to the Director of Product Marketing, the Product Marketing Manager will be responsible for the go-to-market of our industry-first Returns Optimization Platform, the foundational software platform that is used by leading retailers across the country. As a key member of our small, but critical Product Marketing team, you will help with be involved in all aspects of the Product and Sales process, from go-to-market strategy to product packaging and positioning to sales training. In this role, you will be the expert in buyers, how they buy and their buying criteria and will use that knowledge to empower both the Product and Sales teams as we drive adoption of our industry-leading software platform.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Go-to-Market Strategy: Help drive definition, planning, and execution of the RLMS product line in deep partnership with Product Management and Client Development.</li>
<li>Business Case &amp; Benchmarking: Create business cases and strategies for product capabilities required to meet market needs. Independently gather, analyze and synthesize data to prioritize market trends &amp; client needs, identify best practices, and articulate product requirements.</li>
<li>Product Messaging / Positioning: Develop, refine and collaborate with Corporate Marketing to implement high impact and differentiated positioning and messaging. Develop strategies to target accounts and generate sales leads.</li>
<li>Sales Enablement: Develop and deliver high-impact sales tools such as collateral, presentations, case studies, demos, competitive positioning, etc. Create sales training materials that cover industry trends, value proposition, and competitive positioning.</li>
<li>Competitive Intelligence: Develop informed point of view on where competition is headed or will emerge (and why), and how to win.</li>
<li>Buying / Sales Cycle Analysis: Blueprint the buyer's journey and map buyer decisions / information needs at each stage.</li>
<li>Subject Matter Expertise: Serve as a subject matter expert on solution in support of marketing programs, press, and analyst relations.</li>
</ul>
About You
<br><br>
<ul>

<li>You have a demonstrated ability to build trust and develop strong relationships with both Sales organizations and Product organizations</li>
<li>You are a pragmatic and practical self-starter; an outcomes-oriented person who can juggle multiple workstreams, loves to problem-solve, and can efficiently guide a collaborative process.</li>
<li>You are a great collaborator who likes bridging the gap between technical and client-facing teams</li>
<li>You are an analytical yet creative thinker, and truly enjoy client-facing interactions</li>
<li>You have a good understanding of the sales process for enterprise software and how best to support sales teams and equip them for success.</li>
<li>You thrive on challenge, and take pride in being an excellent teammate.</li>
<li>You have a passion for growth, excellence, and continuous improvement of existing processes.</li>
</ul>
