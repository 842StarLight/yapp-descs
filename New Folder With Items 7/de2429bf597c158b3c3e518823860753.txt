<h2>DIRECTOR, PUBLISHER DEVELOPMENT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<div>Sightly is in expansion mode and looking for a publisher development leader. Historically, Sightly has been a<br>
YouTube platform however organizational success and strategy for the future demands Sightly to expand its<br>
inventory sources. Sightly has built its success by driving clients media objectives through tangible results within<br>
the YouTube ecosystem. The next success era for Sightly will be to continue the excellent performance combined<br>
with great customer service outside of the YouTube platform.<br>
The Director of Publisher Development at Sightly will be responsible for building and growing direct<br>
publisher/channel partner relationships from the ground up. This leader will have experience in structuring supply<br>
deals with premium and tier two publishers from inception to completion. They will syndicate existing YouTube<br>
creators’ content as well create new deals with the open marketplace via DV360. This supply-side seller must<br>
possess an entrepreneurial drive to “build the chessboard” vs “move pieces around a chessboard.”<br>
This position sits within the Partnerships team and reports to the SVP, Performance and Partner Strategy at Sightly.<br>
This team is growing within the overall organization and sits at the intersection of demand, supply, and<br>
innovations.<br>
This is a full-time position and is based in NYC.</div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>AS THE DIRECTOR, PUBLISHER DEVELOPMENT AT SIGHTLY, YOU WILL:</b></h3>
</div></div>
<div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Be an individual contributor and excellent collaborator</li>
<li>Source and evaluate new inventory supply partnership opportunities and manage the vetting of proposals<br>
and inquiries</li>
<li>Work with leadership to define and refine deal structures</li>
<li>Build supply accounts and set up payment rules, placements, campaigns, yield management floors, and CPM optimizations</li>
<li>Lead the management of deal IDs and collaborate with Sales and Account Management throughout the process</li>
<li>Maintain active conversations with publishers through regular and consistent engagement, demonstrating market knowledge and industry expertise</li>
<li>Ensure publisher retention and lead relationship management, ensuring high publisher satisfaction</li>
<li>Track, manage, and own publisher pipeline and account activities in Salesforce</li>
<li>Be a deal-maker and closer, negotiating agreements and building long-term relationships with publishers</li>
<li>Prepare ongoing analysis on revenue generated/yield from supply partnerships</li>
<li>Understand where Google fits into the broader technology space and how the various entities throughout<br>
the media supply chain interact with one another.</li>
</ul></div></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></h3>
</div></div>
<div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Must possess self-awareness and strong sense of curiosity with a drive to execute</li>
<li>A passion for ad tech and the internet</li>
<li>Deep knowledge of the digital advertising ecosystem and mechanics</li>
<li>In-depth understanding of the programmatic ecosystem and relevant facets including data, yield<br>
optimization, mediation, trading desks, ad exchanges, DSPs/SSPs, and DMPs</li>
<li>Grasp of the video ecosystem, including CTV, OTT, Video Players, and Video Ad Servers</li>
<li>Established relationships within the digital media publishing industry</li>
<li>Previous experience with deal IDs in DV360 is required</li>
<li>Previous experience with Salesforce is required</li>
<li>Previous experience with a Video platform is a plus</li>
<li>Previous experience with YouTube is a plus</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and writing communication skills and a great storyteller</li>
<li>Ability to present persuasive arguments to support recommendations internally and externally</li>
<li>Excellent negotiation skills with a proven track record of building and growing supply</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills necessary to interact with clients, management, and peers</li>
<li>Excellent troubleshooting, analytical, and problem-solving abilities, along with an ability to collaborate cross-functionally</li>
<li>Generally have a minimum 7+ years in business development or partnerships role with a leading ad tech company (SSPs preferred)</li>
<li>Sense of humor</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div>

</div></div>
<div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Created a standardized process for the nuts and bolts operational set-up of new inventory/supply</li>
<li>Collaborated on training sales and account management teams on mechanics of supply side set-up</li>
<li>Established relationships with appropriate Google contacts for troubleshooting supply side ad ops</li>
<li>Built positive rapport with all Sightly team members across all departments</li>
<li>Built yield analysis model and demonstrated progressive yield optimizations across most/all publishers</li>
</ul></div></div>
