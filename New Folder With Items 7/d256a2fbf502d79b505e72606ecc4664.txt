<p><b>Fire Alarm Technician</b></p>
<p>
Western States Fire Protection is looking for a Fire Alarm Technician. The qualified Technician should be outgoing; highly self-motivated and must be able to problem solve. He/she will have strong communication skills and a professional image. This person must be able to handle all aspects of a new or retrofit project from install to programming, perform service calls and repairs, and have strong troubleshooting skills.
</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work on and repair fire alarm, security, access, and video systems from various manufactures. Able to complete installations and repairs per NFPA72 and other standards</li>
<li>Use company software to complete work orders, daily time sheet, and inter-company communications. All work is completed on iPads or iPhones, some minimal technology skills needed</li>
<li>Analysis and troubleshooting of fire alarm systems including Ground-Faults, Shorts, Wire Integrity, Termination, Panel, Fuses, Battery Testing and much more</li>
<li>Programming of dialers and fire alarm panels for monitoring utilizing traditional POTS lines, cellular, and IP technologies</li>
<li>Complete basic repairs and installations including moves, adds, relocates for all types of systems and devices</li>
<li>Complete final reports, deficiency reports and possible bids via a computer and/or tablet</li>
<li>Interact with clients, field personnel and fire marshals</li>
<li>Help grow the business. When slow, be capable of seeking opportunities with existing and new customers. Sales training, business cards, other will be provided</li>
<li>Read and understand construction drawings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent is required</li>
<li>Minimum of two years of service or install experience is required</li>
<li>Washington 06 or 01 Electrical license is required
</li>
<li>NICET II in Fire Alarm is required
</li>
<li>Field installation experience is required</li>
<li>Solid knowledge of NFPA 70 &amp; 72 codes is required</li>
<li>Possess an understanding of expenses and scheduling</li>
<li>Hold a valid driver's license; In accordance with Company Policy</li>
<li>Willing to pass a post-offer drug test, background and reference check</li>
</ul>
