<h2>Business Manager - Lunchmeats</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your Opportunity
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><i>The position summary states the general nature and purpose of the job. Overall accountabilities are defined in this section.</i></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Are you a driven and motivated business management professional who thrives in working in a faced paced environment, collaborating with great teams, sharing new ideas, and diving into analytics to seek out opportunities to grow the business? Then join one of the country’s top consumer packaged goods companies, Smithfield Foods! We have built a culture that places enormous value on each individual, our animals, the environment and the communities we all share. Smithfield Foods and the Business Management teams are evolving rapidly and looking for impactful self-starter business management professionals!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As a Business Management Team member, you would assist in managing the profitability of a specific category in our business. Whether it is bacon, ham, fresh pork or another one of our categories you will work cross-functionally with everyone from our sales team to our plant operations to deliver the best product to our customers and consumers.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Business Manager is responsible for developing the overall plans/programs for a distinct product or brand, or a significant segment thereof. The Business Manager is responsible for a specific category P&amp;L. They assist in the development of new/existing products and markets in order to effectively contribute to the achievement of the company's business and marketing objectives.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Core Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><i>Responsibilities and tasks are written as follows:</i></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Responsible for meeting overall company price &amp; volume budgets for fiscal year within outlined parameters (% of growth). Volumes must be met within targeted growth. Responsible for managing all pricing activities to maximize profit potential.</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsible for meeting product group/product line profitability objectives for fiscal year based on specified budget. Responsible for total P&amp;L for a major product group which includes sales volume, sales dollars, cost of goods, gross margin, shuttle and storage expenses, plant variances, direct marketing expenses, net contribution, and profit before taxes.</div></li>
<li><div>
Primary contact for the Sales team. Communicate product specifications, inventory levels, pricing, costing and lead time requirements. Also responsible for developing strong communication with other functional areas and departments.</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsible for SKU and Price Management. Negotiates, reviews and approves pricing and trade agreements. Participates and evaluates market research, promotions, public relations and advertising. Attends product reviews to compare the execution of the written specs and gain insight into what drives variances and yield to the standards.</div></li>
<li>
Considered the category expert over a specific segment.</li>
</ul>
