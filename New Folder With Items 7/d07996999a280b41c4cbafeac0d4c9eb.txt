<h2>Technical Customer Support Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Customer Support Representatives are the voice of our company to the people who matter the most, our customers.</b></p>
<p>Successful Customer Support Representatives truly enjoy helping people and ensure that every single client is given the best possible experience. They are technically proficient, but more importantly, they are highly empathetic to people who aren’t.</p>
<p>They’re also incredibly effective problem solvers who approach new challenges with curiosity and enthusiasm for creating new solutions. They’re not afraid to ask for help when they need it because our leadership team is happy to support them.</p>
<p>Finally, they are motivated to exceed our expectations because they know that advancement is always possible for high achievers.</p>
<p>If you feel like you’ll fit right in with our team, please apply.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Shape the best possible customer experience for every single customer</li>
<li>Actively listen to customers, understand their issues, and communicate resolutions effectively both verbally and written.</li>
<li>Display your knowledge through effective problem-solving that will require resourceful, conceptual thinking.</li>
<li>Work well under pressure with the ability to be patient and empathize with customers.</li>
<li>Identify and escalate priority issues per customer specifications.</li>
<li>Document customer interactions and solutions in CRM / ticket system.</li>
<li>Quickly learn new software, hardware, and related concepts.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Care genuinely about customer support and about the role it plays in making a customer-centric team successful.</li>
<li>Understand the Internet and how it works. Ideally, know small businesses networking concepts such as DHCP, DNS, routing and basic firewall rules.</li>
<li>Have strong skills with desktop computers and troubleshooting technical issues.</li>
<li>Have prior experience doing live customer support by phone and/or chat and know how to do it well.</li>
<li>Know when to ask for help.</li>
<li>Skilled at explaining technical problems succinctly and clearly.</li>
<li>You are a naturally empathetic and articulate communicator.</li>
<li>Technical degree or combination of relevant certifications and experience.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Why you should work with us: </b></p>
<p>Workwell Technologies is a cutting-edge tech company with incredible employee perks like daily lunch, smoothie bar, coffee, and snacks. We also offer monthly massage breaks, an on-site basketball court, a fitness facility and more.</p>
<p>We are looking for the right person(s) who fit in with our fast-paced culture.</p>
<p>An industry leader in providing innovative solutions to small businesses nationwide, we provide products that allow local businesses to compete with their national competitors. Our product line-up includes time and attendance solutions, point-of-sale solutions, as well as other cloud-based services.</p>
<p>Our team has varying start times between 5am and 9:30am, end times between 1:30pm and 6pm. Weekend shifts may be required.</p>
