<h2>Product Owner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are a <b>young</b> and <b>unconventional</b> software company created in 2011 by experienced pricing software and pricing consulting professionals. Our <b>vision</b> is to <b>become and stay the leading SaaS pricing and CPQ solution provider</b>. We are <b>agile</b> company that brings good ideas to life quickly and is committed to <b>market driven innovation</b>. We are very flexible so our customers are from diverse industries and sizes with brands like Robert Bosch, Kärcher, Avery Dennison, Cox Automotive, Iron Mountain and many others. We are a solid and sustainable company <b>which is and will be strongly growing globally</b> for the years ahead. Great time to join us and grow with us outside the borders.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Your mission?</b></p>
<p>
You will be responsible for developing and maintaining the PricefxPlasma product, our big-data pricing benchmarking platform. The Product Owner will define the roadmap for the product and create detailed user stories. Working closely with all key stakeholders the Product Owner will tailor requirements and specification to meet the need of the target audience.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
You will be in charge of…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan and prioritize the product backlog</li>
<li>Write user stories including functional diagrams or flows and defining acceptance criteria</li>
<li>Talking to customers to gather regular feedback and oversee user testing</li>
<li>Adding value to the product based on user feedback and requirements</li>
<li>Making recommendations to senior stakeholders based on data and feedback</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales and Marketing to define communication strategy and content towards the stakeholders</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>
What should you have to succeed?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in an Agile software development</li>
<li>Experience managing internal company backlogs</li>
<li>Experience with statistics/benchmarking</li>
<li>Excellent stakeholder and communication skills</li>
<li>Strong technical knowledge</li>
<li>Able to make decisions and lead team meetings</li>
<li>Strong analytical thinking</li>
<li>Advanced English</li>
<li>Very good team player</li>
<br>
</ul>
