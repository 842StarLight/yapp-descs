<div><b>
Position Summary</b></div>
<p>
The Sr. Accountant will be directly involved with month-end close, day to day general ledger accounting, international accounting and financial reporting, and working with the Company’s external auditors. The Senior Accountant will have exposure to most areas of the company in the month-end close process.<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Active role in the financial close process (Domestic and/or Foreign Subsidiaries)</li>
<li>
Preparation of month end journal entries and reconciliations</li>
<li>
Set up and processing of allocations among all department and cost centers for both Domestic and Foreign Subsidiaries</li>
<li>
Compile various reporting schedules for Quarterly/Annual Filings (Domestic and Foreign subsidiaries)</li>
<li>
Assist in preparing the quarterly cash flow and flux templates
</li>
<li>Work closely with Accounts Payable, Payroll, Tax and Treasury teams to ensure day-to-day operational needs are met, and accounting entries are properly recorded.
</li>
<li>Maintain documentation of internal controls in compliance with Sarbanes-Oxley Act
</li>
<li>Research technical accounting issues and prepare necessary memos</li>
<li>
Apply knowledge and experience with advanced accounting principles to assess the accuracy and proper entry of financial data and documentation; identify and recommend solutions to resolve discrepancies.
</li>
<li>Participate in the implementation and ongoing enhancements of the company’s ERP system.
</li>
<li>Perform special projects or ad hoc analyses, as required</li>
</ul>
