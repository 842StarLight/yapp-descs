<h2>Plant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
The Plant Manager is responsible for directing the business, financial, technical, manufacturing and resource functions of the facility. Guides facility in its support of internal and external customer requirements. Directs continuous improvement activities (Lean, Kaizen, BOS, TVM, etc.) and coordinates the development of the facilities people, processes and capabilities.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
1.Direct daily plant activities, including supervision of salaried staff.<br>
2.Plan and manage all financial aspects of the facility (AOP, capital, etc.)<br>
3.Plan and manage all quality, environmental health and safety compliance and regulator aspects<br>
4.Champion of plant continuous improvement activities (BOS, Lean, TVM, Kaizen)<br>
5.Identify and track key measures to monitor facility/function effectiveness, and set and monitor staff goals and objectives.<br>
6.Drive development of facilities processes and future capabilities<br>
7.Ensure all people and customer requirements are maintained to the highest standard.<br>
8.Employee must perform his/her job within acceptable safety, environmental, quality, productivity and performance standards as outlined in plant policies, handbooks and applicable work instructions.<br>
9.Overtime as required/requested.<br>
<br>
<b>ADDITIONAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
1.Champion development of all team members<br>
2.Succession Planning<br>
3.Interface, planning, visits with customers, engineers, and sales<br>
4.Supplier development<br>
5.Champion Strategic Planning team<br>
<br>
<ul><li>This list may not be all-inclusive and may include other duties as assigned by the supervisor.</li></ul>
