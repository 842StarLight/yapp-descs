<h2>Director, Customer Success</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Director of Customer Success will be the voice of our customers within Jumpshot. You will oversee all things support and customer success related including account management, onboarding, adoption, advocacy, and retention. The ideal candidate will be equally passionate about Jumpshot products, customer outcomes, and leading and inspiring a team.
<br><br>
Roles and Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Define customer success plans, targets and objectives; ensure that the appropriate level of resources and capabilities exist within the team to achieve the goals.</li>
<li>Recruit, attract and retain a team of customer success professionals.</li>
<li>Develop, implement and accomplish short, medium and long-term vision for customer success.</li>
<li>Manage customer success activities, onboarding and adoption, customer success management, renewals, cross-sell / up-sell sales leads, customer advocacy.</li>
<li>Expand revenue in accounts through cross-sell and upsell. Influence customer lifetime value through higher product adoption, customer satisfaction, retention and overall health scores.</li>
<li>Define operational metrics/KPIs for the team; ensure there are adequate systems for tracking metrics; create cadence for reporting progress with executive management.</li>
<li>Builds support across a wide range of stakeholders; creates a strong network of relationships among peers, partners, decision makers and stakeholders.</li>
<li>Develop deep technical knowledge of Jumpshot products to assist team with troubleshooting and issue resolution.</li>
<li>Work with Marketing to build out customer reference program.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years relevant experience in business operations in a SaaS company, or a similar background.</li>
<li>Substantial leadership experience, with 5+ years at senior management / director level.</li>
<li>Strong leadership skills and a drive to make a large impact on the company and the customers.</li>
<li>Strong business and technical acumen and executive presence, combined with deep analytical and organizational skills; strong understanding of value drivers in recurring revenue business models.</li>
<li>Results-oriented individual with a history of setting and attaining goals and meeting aggressive timelines.</li>
<li>Confident, capable, enthusiastic self-starter with minimal need for direction.</li>
<li>Passion for data, analytics, and process: ability and desire to dive into data to understand metrics.</li>
<li>BA or BS degree or equivalent experience is required.</li>
</ul>
</div>
