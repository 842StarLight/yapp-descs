Job Summary<br>
</div>
<div>
<p><br>
<br>
<b>Summary:</b> Obtains or receives merchandise, totals bills, accepts payment and makes change for customers in retail store.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Stocks shelves, counters and tables with merchandise.
</li>
<li>Sets up advertising displays and arranges merchandise to promote sales.
</li>
<li>Stamps, marks or tags prices on merchandise.
</li>
<li>Obtains merchandise requested by customer or receives merchandise selected by customer.
</li>
<li>Answers customers' questions about location, price and use of merchandise.
</li>
<li>Interacts with customers and resolves customer complaints in a friendly, service-oriented manner.
</li>
<li>Wraps and bags merchandise.
</li>
<li>Totals merchandise price and tax to determine final bill amount.
</li>
<li>Accepts payment and makes change.
</li>
<li>Removes and records amount of cash in register at end of shift.
</li>
<li>Keeps record of sales, prepares inventory and orders merchandise.
</li>
<li>Complies with sanitation, ServSafe and safety requirements.
</li>
<li>Maintains clean and safe work environment; performs job safely.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.<br>
</li>
</ul>
