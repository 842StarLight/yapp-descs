<h2>Technical Installation Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The <b>Technical Installation Support Specialist</b> is responsible for providing support during the sales process. This individual also escalates issues as necessary to supervisors or other departments within the organization. The <b>Technical Installation Support Specialist</b> assists with standard and non-standard requests such as legacy product, customer support and provides solutions across platforms for products and services.</p>
<p><b>
Experience Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Hands on experience with ERP and CRM systems</li>
<li>
Proficiency with MS Dynamics 365, particularly MS Excel</li>
<li>
In-depth understanding of sales principles and customer service practices</li>
<li>
Background and familiarity with burglar alarm, camera, and automation systems</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>
Analytical and multitasking skills</li>
<li>
Teamwork and motivational skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education Required:</b></p>

Bachelor’s Degree or related work experience preferred</li>
</ul>
