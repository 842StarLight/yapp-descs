<h2>Manager Senior, Business Intelligence Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Purpose of Job</b></h2>
Manages teams on using data from internal and external sources to provide insight to analyst and decision-makers. Manages a team responsible for collaborating with stakeholders and other data and analytical leaders to define data and analytic needs for business functional areas. Prototypes solutions for displaying information based on business needs. Provides consultation to users and lead cross-functional teams to address business issues.<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Requirements</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborates and participates in strategic direction across enterprise with other data and analytical leaders.</li>
<li>
Subject matter expert in consulting and advising business partners on decision support solutions.</li>
<li>
Ensures teams maintain understanding of business processes, supporting data and applications and strategy direction.</li>
<li>
Directs quality work efforts, team assignments and problem resolution.</li>
<li>
Applies industry best practices and leverage in house resources and solutions.</li>
<li>
Advises management on utilization of decision support solutions to inform business decisions.</li>
<li>
Serves as a liaison between the business and IT teams; partners with IT to build USAA core analytics capabilities.</li>
</ul>

Bachelor's Degree in Math, Computer Science or a related discipline.<br>

OR<br>
A Master's Degree in Math, Computer Science or a related discipline.<br>
OR<br>
A PhD in Math, Computer Science or a related discipline.</li>
<li>
If Bachelors degree, 6 or more years experience in data management or information technology function and 2 or more years of direct and/or indirect management experience<br>

OR<br>
If PhD, 3 or more years experience in data management or information technology function</li>
