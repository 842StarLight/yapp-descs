<h2>Loan Processor - Commercial</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Loan Processor will be responsible for processing from start to finish, all new loans and loan modifications.
 </p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the building of Loan Files (hard or electronic, as applicable) according to the Bank’s credit policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Prepares Introduction Letter to applicant with a detailed list of informational items necessary to process the loan.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Collect incoming informational items requested from applicant/borrower and review to ensure accuracy.</p></li>
<li><p>
Collaborate and effectively communicate with internal staff (Sales, Credit, Loan Servicing, Accounts Payable, etc. departments) and external clients (Client/Borrower, Title/Escrow Agents, Notary Service and any other applicable agencies that are needed to process subject loan (i.e. State Guaranty Agency, DMV service).</p></li>
<li><p>
Initiates Title/Escrow order to obtain Preliminary Title Report for review.
</p></li>
<li><p>Gathers third party reports (i.e. credit, OFAC, Westlaw, UCC search, Flood Determination Certificate, Tax Verification).</p></li>
<li><p>
Prepares insurance coverage letter of request required for collateral being taken for subject loan. Upon receipt of insurance coverage requested, responsible for reviewing per initial letter of request.</p></li>
<li><p>
Advance loan proceeds (draw request) as approved per Credit Approval Memorandum (“CAM”).</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage borrower funds received for processing costs incurred (to be deposited to bank “GL” account).
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensure outstanding invoices associated with loan request are paid prior to or at closing.
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintain knowledge and comprehensive understanding of all types of entities in order to prepare Commercial Loan Documents that are not specifically ordered from Documentation department (i.e. Subordination, SNDA, Estoppel Certificate).</p></li>
<li><p>
Ensure notes are input in communication software with all updates identifying stage of process.</p></li>
<li><p>
Submit request for UCC filing (if applicable) and place tax service order with appropriate vendor.
</p></li>
<li><p>Submit loan documentation request upon receipt of Credit Approval Memorandum (“CAM”). Loan documents are to be reviewed upon receipt from loan documentation department for accuracy.
</p></li>
<li><p>Coordinate document signing per type of loan (i.e. Modification via e-sign, Origination/Purchase/Re-finance via escrow/title and/or Relationship Manager).</p></li>
<li><p>
Review executed loan documents verifying all conditions have been satisfied.</p></li>
<li><p>
Prepare loan file for funding (i.e. complete Funding Journal, Modification Fee Sheet, Wire Request Form, payment of invoice(s), note payoff(s)) and boarding (i.e. complete checklist).</p></li>
<li><p>
Create ticklers for outstanding conditions and trailing documents (i.e. final title policy(ies) and recorded documents) in communication software (“Salesforce”). Review final title policy(ies) and recorded documents upon notification through communication software (“Salesforce”).</p></li>
<li><p>
Audit funded loan file and transfer to file room.</p></li>
<li><p>
Review scanned loan files after funding.</p></li>
<li><p>
Assist Portfolio Managers with annual review third party reports.</p></li>
<li><p>
Assist Processing Manager with Special project or assignment as necessary.<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<ul><li>Minimum 1+years’ of experience in commercial loan processing funding or related work experience</li></ul>
