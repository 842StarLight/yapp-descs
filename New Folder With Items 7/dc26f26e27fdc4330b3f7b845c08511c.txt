<p>
<b>The Role!</b></p>
<p>
As a Zoës General Manager, you will create a fun and upbeat environment! You will manage both the front and back of house operations and therefore, prior kitchen experience is a huge plus. You will also have responsibility for the hiring and training of team members and for the finances and P&amp;L of your restaurant.</p>
<p>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Providing leadership, guidance and training to assistant managers and hourly team members
</li>
<li>Maximizing profits by optimizing P&amp;L and other financials
</li>
<li>Maintaining inventory and managing food costs
</li>
<li>Handling HR needs as they arise, including recruiting, training, disciplinary action and terminations
</li>
<li>Growing grow sales and increase profits through exceptional guest service, word of mouth advertising and marketing
</li>
<li>Embracing and promoting the Zoës Kitchen culture
</li>
<li>Making the guests feel welcome and ensuring that your team members provide the best possible service
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The Skills!</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2-5 years restaurant management experience
</li>
<li>Minimum 1 year general manager experience
</li>
<li>Strong financial and P&amp;L management skills
</li>
<li>High-energy, motivational and fun personality
</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving and conflict-resolution skills
</li>
<li>Ability/flexibility to work 45-55+ hours per week including mornings, evenings, weekends and/or holidays
</li>
<li>Must be 21 years of age of older
</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver license<br>
</li>
</ul>
