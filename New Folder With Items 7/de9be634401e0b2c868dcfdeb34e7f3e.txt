<div><b>
About the Role</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As a Sales Director on the Public Sector team, you will initially target new business opportunities within the U.S. Federal government. As you grow your business, you will be responsible for strategic advisory and relationship management. This includes:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Strategy Development:</div>
<ul>
<li>Research target Department/Agencies and develop prospecting campaigns
</li>
<li>Dive deep in understanding their mission and the potential for alignment with the Medallia value proposition</li>
<li>Engage existing Medallia partner ecosystem and cultivate relationships with Medallia Partners for joint go-to-market approaches relating to departments and specific opportunities</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Customer Discovery:</div>
<ul>
<li>Go high-and-wide within organizations to understand the full scope of opportunity</li>
<li>Lead entire sales cycle from initial opportunity creation to finalizing opportunity outcomes, including outreach campaigns, driving the discovery process at meetings with both prospects and clients, forecasting, selling with Value Framework, and ultimately closing the deal</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Execution:</div>
<ul>
<li>Meeting execution</li>
<li>Develop relationships both internally and externally</li>
<li>Participate in internal team meetings to collaborate with supporting ecosystem on opportunities, resolve customer issues, share best practices, and work with cross functional teams</li>
</ul>
</div>
