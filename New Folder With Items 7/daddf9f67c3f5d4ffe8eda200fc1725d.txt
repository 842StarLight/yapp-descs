<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary</b></h2>
<p></p>
<br>
Are you a dynamic IT people leader, with experience in and real passion for Marketing and UX concepts? Do you love leading large and complex initiatives and have proven the ability to deliver results? Are you adaptive to change, agile in your learning, and have entrepreneurial thinking? Thrivent is seeking a Technology Manager for our Web &amp; Marketing Solutions teams within our IT Division. We need a leader who can grow Thrivent’s Web and Integration development talent and technology! In this highly visible role you will have the opportunity to work with every area of IT, multiple business partners, and Senior Leadership. This team supports technology solutions which are essential to Customer Experience, Marketing, and Sales.<br>
<br>
As our Marketing and Interactive Solutions Manager, you will be responsible for the people, processes, and tools that deliver against our strategies. This includes the management of all operational aspects surrounding Thrivent’s current practices and solutions, and helping to build out the future. As a leader, you will collaborate with business and IT leadership to define processes and solutions for CX, Marketing, and Sales, while partnering with Analytics, MDM, CRM, and other solution owners.<p></p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Duties and Responsibilities</b></h2>
<p>
You are trusted with the development and implementation of IT applications and applications systems, including analysis and programming activities, that enable Customer Experience, Marketing, and Sales activities. We will look to you to be the IT strategic and tactical liaison with the business for IT solution discovery, development and deployment while participating in IT strategy development, providing trusted guidance to the business and within IT. In this role, you have the exciting opportunity to help be the bridge between old and new technologies.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Builds and maintains an all-star team; selects, develops, coaches, rewards and recognizes team members</li>
<li>
Responsibility for the development of the technology vision, strategy and roadmaps for digital engagement in conjunction with business partners and other IT leaders, along with the execution and measurement of the set objectives</li>
<li>
Defines and delivers UX proven practices, standards, methods and guidelines to meet organizational needs</li>
<li>
Makes operational and strategic decisions that may have significant impact, on functionality, quality or effectiveness of business unit activities</li>
<li>
Participates in, supports and executes business initiatives, plans and policies which are often complex in nature and have a significant impact on the business unit's profitability and strategic direction</li>
<li>
Monitors budget, expenses and scope and facilitates project delivery and support</li>
<li>
Initiates, designs and monitors continuous improvement activities for program/department/business</li>
<li>
Sets performance targets and evaluates/enforces service delivery against targets</li>
<li>
Establishes and supports an environment in which all personnel can develop and apply their talents to meet the technology goals</li>
<li>
Leads IT professionals who design/develop or acquire/adapt application software. Establishes the priority of requests and ensures appropriate staffing aligned to meet business objectives</li>
<li>
Communicates strategy effectively to team members so that connections are made between daily task prioritization and organizational or divisional strategic objectives.</li>
<li>
Executes on initiatives that Directors &amp; VPs have deemed as priorities</li>
</ul>
