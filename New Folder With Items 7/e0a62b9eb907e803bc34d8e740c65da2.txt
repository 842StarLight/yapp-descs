<h2>Project Quotation Specialist Sr.</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<b>Summary:</b><br>
1. Must know Compress Software
<br><br>
2. Must be able to review and interpret P&amp;IDs and drawings
<br><br>
3. Must be able to create an estimate without a drawing by using product knowledge and resources provided by the Company.
<br><br>
Sales Quotations for Production Equipment and Electrostatic products and order administration.
<br><br>
Job Duties/Responsibilities
<br><br>
Prepare and complete formal quotation packages for Production Equipment (vessel, piping and skid fabrication) packages to clients and consult with outside Sales Rep. Team.
<br>Evaluate, construct and maintain material cost analysis to create accurate estimates for plant managers for fabrication. Lead meetings with subject matter experts, developing proposal deliverables for Scot of Work requirements accordingly before RFQ due date.
<br>Implement client's technical specifications, and prepare proposals according to client drawings, clarification, exceptions and expectations.
<br>Review and interpret P&amp;IDs and detail drawings of product lines to estimate cost.
<br>Create change orders, budget cost and identify actual cost through company's ERP database.
<br>Affectively communicate cost estimate to senior management for approval.
<br>Administer proposal/project handover meetings for projects awarded. Coordinate with engineering to review contract and any required client reports.
<br><br>
Construct and maintain material cost analyst on common and exotic parts.
<br>Administer project orders handoffs to Admin. Assistants to and prepare new order packages for contract review and any required client reports.
<br>Communicate with all departments involved in completing quotations.
<br>Coordinate with all departments involved in completing project orders.
<br>Coordinate with Administrative Assistant of projects for schedule witness inspections and documentation packages.
<br>Identify and present variable pricing to Management Team and competitive information as available.
<br>Assign to Admin. Assistant, Letters of Credit, Commercial Invoice and Chamber of Commerce Approvals when required
<br><br>
<b>Job Requirements:</b><br>
Skills/Knowledge
<br><br>
Production Equipment knowledge, problem solving ability and possess strong written and verbal communication skills.
<br>Advanced computer skills in MS Professional, Adobe and Excel. Proficient using Codeware Compress - Pressure Vessel Design Software and Navision (ERP) Software. Ability to effectively communicate with engineering and interpret P&amp;IDs.
<br>Work in a fast paced, constantly changing environment. Skilled in coordinating and managing several estimates and written proposals..
<br>Deal with all types of personalities, internal &amp; external.
<br><br>
Education
<br><br>
College degree in Business and/or Technical field
<br><br>
Experience
<br><br>
Minimum Seven (7) years job-related experience in Oil &amp; Gas Process Equipment.</div>
