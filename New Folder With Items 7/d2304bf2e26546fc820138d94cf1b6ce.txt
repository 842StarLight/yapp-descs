<p>
JOB DESCRIPTION:</p>
<p>
AEG and the LA Kings seek a Junior Accountant. The Junior Accountant will perform entry-level accounting work for the LA Kings Finance department. This position requires an understanding of basic accounting principles and accounting functions. Responsibilities include banking activities, maintenance of financial records, performing month-end closing procedures, preparing billings, performing financial analysis, accounts payable and accounts receivable. The position will interface with the Staff Accountant and Sr. Finance Manager to support various tasks on a daily basis.</p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Collect and scan new invoice submissions for the LA Kings, Kings Care and AEG Sports vendors into ImageNow for Accounts Payable; Review vendor statements to verify invoices match check requests/ImageNow requests to approve for processing; Process invoices in the Image Now system</li>
<li>Monitor accounts to ensure payments are up to date and resolve invoice discrepancies; Prepare and record cash transactions; Collect and enter new customer receipt submissions for LA Kings and Kings Care for Accounts Receivable. Prepare settlement for AEG Sports Rugby; upload monthly journal entries and payroll</li>
<li>Review statements in the JD Edwards accounting software to resolve discrepancies and ensure consistency</li>
<li>Reconciles bank accounts; Assist in the clearing of suspense accounts</li>
<li>Monitor fixed asset activity and generate month-end depreciation entries</li>
</ul>
