<h2>Medical Registration and Authorization Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Title</b></h3>
Medical Registration and Authorization Specialist<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h3>
<p><b>
In this role, you have the opportunity to:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Act as a liaison between sales, patients, medical personnel, and other internal/external stakeholders. In addition, this role will review documentation and accurately collect required financial and demographic information for all patients.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>You are responsible for (but not limited to):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Obtaining and entering Medicaid authorizations for treatment from various payers and following up on authorization requests, running reports, and identifying expiring authorizations</li>
<li>
Accurately maintaining patient files and current authorization information</li>
<li>
Coordinating with various locations regarding authorization statuses and assist with insurance verification and other billing office related activities</li>
<li>
Reviewing claims for the accuracy of procedures, diagnoses, demographic and insurance information</li>
<li>
Obtaining and entering referrals, payer changes, equipment upgrades, information updates, etc.
</li>
<li>Reviewing and correcting all claims/charge denials and edits that are communicated via Explanation of Benefits (EOB)</li>
<li>
Additional duties and responsibilities as required and/or assigned</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
To succeed in this role, you should have the following skills and experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Associates Degree in Medical Billing and Coding or Accounting, preferred</li>
<li>
1+ years of experience in medical billing, documentation review, and/or data entry
</li>
<li>Familiarity with CPT and ICD-10 coding and medical terminology</li>
<li>
Must be technically savvy and comfortable using software, including, but not limited to:
<ul><li>Billing Software, MS Office, Insurance Portals, and Electronic Claims Submissions</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
High aptitude to learn new programs, system integrations, and business processes</li>
<li>
Excellent customer service skills with strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage relationships with various insurance payers</li>
<li>
Strong work ethic with proven track record of accuracy, dependability and consistency</li>
<li>
Must be able to think independently, multitask, have strong problem solving skills, and have a continuous improvement mentality.</li>
<li>
Ability to gain the cooperation of others in pursuit of company goals ·</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are a part of</b></p>
<p>
A culture where collaboration and team building is embraced! You will be recognized for your individual achievements as well as your contribution to the team dynamic!</p>
