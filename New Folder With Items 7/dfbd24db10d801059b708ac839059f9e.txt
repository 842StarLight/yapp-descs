<h2>Account Executive – Digital Advertising & Events – Princeton</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>ISMG is currently seeking advertising sales professionals who would be responsible for selling digital media and custom event sponsorships. We are looking for a person with a passion for digital media, a desire to be at the leading edge one of the fastest growing markets – security- and someone with the ability to learn and contribute quickly!</p>
<p>
Responsibilities include, but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Aggressively calling on prospects (marketing personnel) at vendor companies who may have interest in sponsoring content or advertising.</li>
<li>Networking with prospects to find other relevant contacts who may have interest in sponsorship/advertising opportunities.</li>
<li>Selling lead generation, sponsorship/brand awareness and event advertising options including: banner ads, e-news sponsorships, webinars, podcasts, custom content, etc.</li>
<li>Working off of a defined account list to find decision-makers and identify prospects.</li>
<li>Maintaining relationships with current customers to maximize renewal business.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements/Desired Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor’s Degree.</li>
<li>At least 2-4 years of advertising sales experience, preferably B2B.</li>
<li>Knowledge of digital media products.</li>
<li>Experience in selling to advertising agencies is a plus!</li>
<li>Exceptional presentation skills.</li>
<li>Master at objection handling.</li>
<li>Must have a strong command on technology use.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to complete RFP’s in accordance with prospect expectations.</li>
<li>Ability to concisely convey value proposition.</li>
</ul>
