<h2>Senior Field Service Engineer/Technician - Sealer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p><b>Join ABB and work in a team that is dedicated to creating a future where innovative digital technologies allow greater access to cleaner energy.
</b></p>
<p>ABB Inc., in Auburn Hills, MI is seeking for an Senior Field Service Engineer/Technician to join our Robotics and Motion team, the first major global industrial robotics manufacturer to produce robots in the United States. ABB is the leading supplier of industrial robots, modular manufacturing systems and service and has installed more than 250,000 robots worldwide.<br>
This role is responsible for Responsiveness, communication and continuous follow-up are key components of providing excellent customer service to our customers. As a member of the ABB US Robotics Service Team, the Robotics Field Service Engineer/ Technician - Sealer position has a high impact on corporate revenues and on effectively servicing customers. This person is a primary interface between the customer and the ABB Robotic Service Organization and plays a significant role in determining the Company’s image as viewed by the customer. The field organization is greatly dependent on this person’s efficient follow-through and communications.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Perform on-call emergency repairs of ABB Robotic system equipment in the field.</li>
<li>Provide system integration support of ABB robots to designated build shops as well as the end users.</li>
<li>Perform robot program changes such as logic, software and path alterations through either off-line or on-line methodology.</li>
<li>Have mechanical aptitude for performing any mechanical component repair or replacement.</li>
<li>Provide robot specific or systems training in an informal environment to customers as required.</li>
<li>Have knowledge of PLC communications and other robotic peripheral equipment.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate with customers (external and internal) in high pressure situations and maintain strong customer relationships.</li>
<li>Ability to be proactive to support customer needs.</li>
<li>Strong initiative in finding work activities during slow periods.</li>
<li>Capable of traveling on short notice and handle rigors of air travel.</li>
<li>Submit Sales leads for additional Robotic and system opportunities.</li>
<li>Communicate work location and status daily.</li>
<li>Ability to think analytically.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your background</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma and 7 years of experience with automation, customer support, maintenance, OR</li>
<li>Associate’s degree and 5 years of experience with automation, customer support, maintenance, OR</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree and 4 year experience with automation, customer support, maintenance, OR</li>
<li>Preferred Qualification:</li>
<li>B.S. Technical degree preferred</li>
<li>Minimum of 1 year closed loop servo control system understanding &amp; troubleshooting,
</li>
<li>Sealer Process and application experience</li>
<li>Troubleshooting through deductive reasoning</li>
<li>Formal training or equivalent experience with PLC’s preferred with the ability to read and understand Ladder Logic, Computer skills (DOS, Windows)</li>
<li>Open to travel 80% of the time</li>
<li>Candidate must already have a work authorization that would permit them to work for ABB in the USA</li>
</ul>
