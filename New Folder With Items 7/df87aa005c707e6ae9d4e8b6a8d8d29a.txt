<h2>Administrative Assistant- Boise</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Administrative Assistant- Boise</b><b>(</b><b>Job Number:</b> <b>R111345</b><b>)</b>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>
Description</b><div>
</div>
<div><b>
JOB SUMMARY:</b></div>
<div>
Provides administrative support to the Zone or Regional Manager. Key performance elements include, answering phones, taking messages, distributing mail, maintaining manager’s calendar and appointments, typing letters and correspondence when necessary.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES &amp; ACCOUNTABILITIES:</b></div>
<div>
1. Maintains the Zone or Regional Manager’s calendar and schedule appointments, conferences, and events. Responsible for event planning – entering events into Event Connect, online RSVP system, and Event Connect Marketing Toolbox; organizing logistics of the event, and completing follow-up procedures including Sunshine Act transfer of value reporting. May be responsible for reserving conference rooms and coordinating audio/visual equipment and catering.</div>
<div>
2. Answers phones, takes messages, and may check and respond to voicemails as directed.</div>
<div>
3. Responsible for distributing incoming and outgoing mail, such as mass mailings, appropriately.</div>
<div>
4. Types letters and correspondence upon request, including e-mails. Makes photocopies, faxes, and distributes literature and material as directed. Maintains departmental and confidential files.</div>
<div>
5. Responsible for generating and downloading various reports from company databases. Also responsible for coordinating and distributing weekly, monthly, and other regular reports.</div>
<div>
6. Updates and may setup various spreadsheets and databases containing company sales, financial and/or operations information. May be responsible for heavy data entry.</div>
<div>
7. Creates presentations for Zone and Regional Manager on Microsoft power point, or similar software.</div>
<div>
8. Makes travel arrangements and prepares business expense reports for travel and/or sales.</div>
<p>
9. Participates in special projects and performs other duties as required.</p>
<p>
In addition to the essential duties and responsibilities listed above, all positions are also responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li><div>Meeting company standards pertaining to quantity and quality of work performed on an ongoing basis, performing all work related tasks in a manner that is in compliance with all Company policies and procedures including WorldWide Business Standards.
</div></li>
<li><div>Adhering to Company policies, procedures, and directives regarding standards of workplace behavior in completing job duties and assignments.</div></li>
</ul>
</div>
