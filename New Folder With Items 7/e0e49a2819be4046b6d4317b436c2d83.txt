<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As an Account Executive, your mission is to drive revenue for the company through new software sales, superior territory management, and excellent prospecting skills. Account Executives are tasked with building relationships with prospective customers, representing the brand at sales events and tradeshows, confidently delivering software solutions and securing new business through contract negotiations.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage a defined sales territory by identifying, developing and closing new business</li>
<li>
Build a pipeline utilizing your SalesForce or CRM skills to document and track activities</li>
<li>
Execute a consultative and solutions selling process; build value, identify decision makers, understand needs/reason for change, demo solutions, negotiate and close deals to meet and exceed quota targets</li>
<li>
Set and manage customer expectations pre/post-sale to ensure high levels of customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Consistently meet or exceed sales goals and objectives as well as assisting in reaching departmental goals and objectives</li>
<li>
Attend various industry conferences and events throughout the year to represent the brand, build rapport with existing relationships and network with prospective clients</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledge of market and industry trends, competitors and leading customer strategies</li>
<li>
Collaborate effectively with Sales Engineers and other pre-sale resources</li>
<li>
Specifically responsible for field sales, telemarketing and client services that include delivering demonstrations, contract proposals and presenting product related technical information</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Self-starter, motivated with an entrepreneurial mindset</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in related field or 2-3 years of software and/or staffing sales experience</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate business and technical information clearly</li>
<li>
Exceptional time management skills, process focused</li>
<li>
Excellent presentation skills</li>
<li>
Proven ability to be a successful closer</li>
<li>
Ability to be a Change Agent</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 25% to client sites in the US and possibly Canada</li>
</ul>
