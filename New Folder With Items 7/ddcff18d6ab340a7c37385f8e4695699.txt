<p><b>Position Description:
</b></p>
<p>National Decision Support Company (NDSC), a Change Healthcare Company, is seeking a Product Manager. The ideal candidate will have a history of success in growing new and existing product lines, designing and delivery products that meets our customers’ needs. This candidate will combine strategic thinking, exceptional communication skills and the ability to manage complex initiatives with the team. Working closely with our customers, business partners, engineering, marketing and sales, the product manager will drive the growth and scope of our product roadmap as well as guide the release of new products, initial delivery models and enhancements within CareSelect's product suite.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Define the vision of new product capabilities while taking feedback from our customers and internal teams.
</li>
<li>Create a strong infrastructure for NDSC to deliver new product to the market.
</li>
<li>Work closely with development to ensure the successful release of products.
</li>
<li>Represent the needs of the customer by defining and prioritizing product requirements.
</li>
<li>Work closely with all internal departments on the go-to-market process to ensure the successful release of new products.
</li>
<li>Develop product roadmaps to guide our future strategy in product development.
</li>
<li>Assist in the sales process for newer product initiatives.
</li>
<li>Push our current product set to include higher value added features for our customers.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements:

<li>2+ years’ experience as a product/project manager

<li>Prior experience in a technical / product leadership position
</li>
