<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION SUMMARY DESCRIPTION</b></h1>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p>Work within Compliance Department on various pre-funding and post-closing Quality Control (QC) audits. Responsibilities include conducting daily mortgage file audits and providing important and timely feedback to the operation staff, preventing mortgage loans from closing with significant defects such as misrepresentation, inaccurate data, and/or inadequate documentation, working with third-party post-fund QC vendor, reviewing and remediating deficiencies identified by the third-party post-fund QC vendor, communicating deficiencies to the appropriate parties and otherwise facilitating corrective action as appropriate. Will provide recommendations regarding deficiencies and identify training needs for various roles within the Company including Sales and Operations functions.</p>

<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SPECIFIC RESPONSIBILITIES</b></h1>
</div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Major Area of Responsibility</b></p>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Specific Responsibilities Related to Major Area</b></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p>Perform pre-funding QC audits of loans final underwriting approved but not yet closed.</p>
</div>
<div><p>Audit files selected by the QC Manager within the QC System specific to loan type is to be completed on each audited file.
</p></div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p>Review and remediate audit findings from third-party QC vendor.</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>Review and remediate findings from our third-party post-fund QC vendor by acquiring the necessary documentation and information from the appropriate personnel within the Company and/or the consumer.</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p>Summarize and log audit findings in the QC System for reporting purposes.</p>
<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Summarize audit findings and prepare audit reports for management.</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p>Participate in the development of future enhancements to the loan audit process.</p>
<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Participate in the development of future enhancements to the loan audit process in an effort to continually meet regulatory requirements, investor standards, and efficiency objectives. This includes monitoring audit results and making recommendations for change when appropriate, the development of audit tools, and evaluation of third party vendor solutions.</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p>Conduct vendor sampling audits of third-party QC vendor.</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>Conduct vendor sampling audits of third-party QC vendor’s reviews to adhere to agency/investor QC requirements.</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p>All other compliance related assignments as required for agency/regulatory compliance.</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>This may include, file preparation for state/agency/internal audits, HMDA data integrity testing, vendor management reviews, etc. This is not an all-inclusive list.</p>
