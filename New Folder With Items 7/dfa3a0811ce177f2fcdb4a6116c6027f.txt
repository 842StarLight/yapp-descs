<h2>Sales Operations Manager - WWFO</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>NVIDIA has continuously reinvented itself over two decades. Our invention of the GPU in 1999 sparked the growth of the PC gaming market, redefined modern computer graphics, and revolutionized parallel computing. More recently, GPU deep learning ignited modern AI — the next era of computing. NVIDIA is a “learning machine” that constantly evolves by adapting to new opportunities that are hard to solve, that only we can pursue, and that matter to the world. This is our life’s work, to amplify human creativity and intelligence. We are looking for a Sales Operations Manager to join the Sales Operations team. Do you have exceptional analytical skills, impeccable attention to detail, and a creative mindset to tackle unstructured questions in a dynamic, fast-paced environment? Then make the choice to join us today.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>In this role, you will partner with sales leaders, regional sales operations teams, business operations and other groups to align business and financial goals.
</li>
<li>You will be responsible for the monthly forecast processes and they need meaningful improvement. The partner forecast needs to be re-imagined and modified to align with Enterprise sales out process vs. the historical consumer sales in perspective.
</li>
<li>We also need to build a better output for monthly sales forecast, by redesigning the inputs and supporting with statistical models.
</li>
<li>You will lead a weekly sales-commit process to measure/report on quarterly achievement to sales targets and forecast accuracy.
</li>
<li>Lead ongoing analysis of business performance to support daily operations and to improve sales performance and ensure that risks, opportunities, and business drivers are understood and highlighted.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with finance, sales, bus dev, and bus ops to drive alignment, consistency, and automation.
</li>
<li>Continuous identification of opportunities for process improvement. Assist sales management in understanding and resolving process bottlenecks and inconsistencies towards operational excellence.
</li>
<li>Perform ongoing and ad hoc reporting and support to sales leadership and internal business partners.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What we need to see:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>10+ years experience in business operations, forecasting, sales process, sales finance and/or analytics and business analysis experience with record of strong achievement
</p></li>
<li>BA/BS in Business Administration, Finance, Economics, or equivalent
</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize business needs, meet tight deadlines, and communicate effectively
</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency in Excel and experience with financial systems and data visualization tools
</li>
<li>Willingness and ability to adapt to rapid business changes
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Ways to stand out from the crowd:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with both direct and indirect sales models
</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce.com
</li>
</ul>
