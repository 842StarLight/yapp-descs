<div>With an abundant spirit of optimism, openness, and enterprise, Moss Adams helps organizations realize greater potential. The tax specialty group (TSG) at Moss Adams is comprised of tax professionals who go beyond compliance–providing deep technical and strategic guidance to clients so they're better equipped to tackle their most vexing challenges and embrace emerging opportunity. Within TSG, the State &amp; Local Tax team helps clients compete and succeed by staying up-to-date on the ever-changing, complex state and local tax rules. These business advisors specialize in solutions that span income tax, indirect tax, property tax, and tax controversy matters. Join one of the fastest growing groups at Moss Adams and start realizing your potential.</div>

<b>Responsibilities</b>:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Assist with management of clients and their needs</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform technical review of partnership and corporate state tax returns and work papers with attention to multistate tax issues such as apportionment, state modifications, state credits, nexus, method of filings, and flow-through taxation</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist with various tax consulting projects including research and writing projects related to tax planning, nexus studies, voluntary disclosure applications, audit defense, and penalty abatement</div></li>
<li><div>
Research and documentation of client sales and use taxability of specific transactions</div></li>
<li><div>
Analyze taxpayer data and be able to identify opportunities for prospects and clients</div></li>
<li><div>
Keep up to date on state and local tax law changes</div></li>
<li><div>
Participate in business development activities as needed</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop internal relationships within the firm and help grow external branding</div></li>
</ul>
