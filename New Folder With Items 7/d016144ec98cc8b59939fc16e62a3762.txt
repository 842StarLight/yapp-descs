<br>
<br>
This position will work closely with the current accounting staff and management, including but not limited to:
<ul>

<li>Preparation of work papers and financial statements.</li>
<li>Review financial statements</li>
<li>Payroll &amp; sales tax preparation.</li>
<li>Communication with clients including client office visits</li>
<li>Knowledge of QuickBooks and Excel a must.</li>
</ul>

<li>Exceptional analytical skills</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple engagements while meeting deadlines</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail</li>
</ul>
