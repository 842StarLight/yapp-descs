<h2>Client Development Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>How We Think About People:</b><br>
PrecisionLender is built on a single overarching belief… that helping our clients succeed is the only way to ensure that our company succeeds. Everything we do, every decision we make, reflects that belief.
<br><br>
We start by hiring for character traits like empathy, modesty, respect, trust, reliability, and patience. After that, we stay out of the way and let our people do what's best for the client.
<br><br>
<b>Company Overview:</b><br>
PrecisionLender is modernizing commercial banking. Our sales and negotiation solution empowers bankers with actionable, in-the-moment insights and coaching, so they win better deals and build strong, more profitable relationships. Andi®, PrecisionLender's virtual insights analyst, augments banker strengths and intelligence with the latest technology and data, delivering the best recommendations at exactly the right time. More than 12,000+ bankers at 200+ banks – ranging from $1B to $1T in assets – use our solution.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do Here:</b><br>
As a Client Development Associate (CDA), you'll play an integral part in helping identify what prospective clients really care about, what's slowing them down, and how we can help them. In this role, you will have the opportunity to be a part of a team that significantly contributes to the overall growth and success of both our organization and our clients. The ideal CDA is an organized critical thinker who understands the value of story telling to connect with prospective clients.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Analyze and research target Enterprise accounts, seeking out strategic initiatives, executive-level organizational structure, key business segments, and financial performance</li>
<li>Support our Director of Client Development as they guide Enterprise prospects through every step of the sales process, including: initial discussions, product demos, partnership proposal, due diligence, contract negotiation, and coordinating our executive team, internal consultants and other staff into the process as needed</li>
<li>Coordinate onsite visits with prospective clients and ensure internal team has the background and materials needed to conduct a successful discussion</li>
<li>Quarterback RFP process, working with subject matter experts to accurately and effectively respond with PrecisionLender messaging</li>
<li>Draft complex proposals for clients and work alongside analytics team to build business cases that support PrecisionLender's value</li>
<li>Become a subject matter expert on account portfolio, able to prepare presentations for account strategy meetings &amp; management deal reviews</li>
<li>Work with marketing team and external partners on ways to engage target contacts through executive outreach, direct mail programs and events</li>
<li>Maintain the integrity of sales systems (CRM) and internal processes through execution and documentation, constantly seeking ways to help us grow and improve</li>
</ul>
