<br>
<b> </b>As a Workday Functional Senior Manager, you should expect to be involved in the following:</div>
<ul>
<li>Oversee all functional work streams on global Workday implementation projects
</li>
<li>Serve as the escalation point for critical functional issue resolution
</li>
<li>Manage on and off-shore functional teams
</li>
<li>Provide subject matter expertise to aid in decision making related to the functional solution
</li>
<li>Lead, mentor, counsel and manage performance metrics of project staff
</li>
<li>Conduct working sessions with clients to gather, understand, and analyze business requirements.
</li>
<li>Advise clients on industry standards and leading practices.
</li>
<li>Understand Workday’s Implementation Methodology and apply it on all engagements.
</li>
<li>Provide the Project Manager with status updates and keep them apprised of overall project status.
</li>
<li>Architect Workday HCM solution to ensure that all work streams are cohesive and cross functional impacts are considered and mitigated
</li>
<li>Demonstrate strong client and stakeholder management to achieve project objectives
</li>
<li>Ensure the client takes advantage of Workday best practices.
</li>
<li>Support business development activities such as solutioning, proposal creation, orals support and SOW creation.
</li>
<li>Drive innovation through the creation of new industry leading methods and assets</li>
</ul>
