<b>Client Implementation Manager – Corestream</b>
<br>
<br>
Corestream (corestream.com) is seeking a Client Implementation Manager which delivers successful implementation of various benefits projects through execution and coordination of all phases of project deliverables. Project Managers will lead client meetings on business and technical requirements and incorporate these requirements into detailed project plans. If you are an individual looking for a challenging opportunity in a growing company and you meet the requirements listed below, please submit your resume for consideration.
<br>
<br>
As a Client Implementation Manager your role is to onboard new clients and manage eligibility and payroll file integrations for voluntary benefit offerings within the Company’s product portfolio. This position provides project coordination with the client, various internal teams and external providers utilizing project management tools to ensure on time delivery of voluntary benefit launches. This position also collaborates with the sales division to ensure an effective client onboarding experience and supports clients 45 days post launch to ensure effective payroll integrations.
