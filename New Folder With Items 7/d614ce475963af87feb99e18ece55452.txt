<p>
Controls Technician</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Summary</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Controls Technician maintains and troubleshoots production equipment from various suppliers. This position identifies and prioritizes controls / electrical opportunities to maximize operations performance within a facility.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and maintain electrical and controls system documentation.</li>
<li>
Execute small and medium projects involving PLC 5, SLC 500, Controls Logix, Panel View Plus and Allen Bradley VFDs</li>
<li>
Identify and prioritize controls / electrical opportunities to maximize operations performance within a facility.</li>
<li>
Plan and execute projects in a manner which minimizes downtime.</li>
<li>
Maintain and troubleshoot production equipment from various suppliers.</li>
<li>
Quickly identify root causes of problems, as mechanical, controls or joint problems and coordinate resources necessary to address them.</li>
<li>
Develop control software to achieve the balance between mechanical and controls changes to effectively handle all containers.</li>
<li>
Maintain Allen Bradley hardware and software: PLCs, VFDs, HMIs, Networks and various electrical systems.</li>
<li>
Be part of the team that supervises the electrical installation /integration of equipment and controls.</li>
<li>
Follow the CCNNE Controls and Electrical Specifications.</li>
<li>
Work with the CCNNE Controls Group for the following purposes:</li>
<li>
Improve the designs / programs begun by others. Implement and propose improvements to the CCNNE Controls and Electrical Specifications. Suggest which controls projects to be handled by CCNNE Controls Group and which by the Plant Controls Specialist.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Education</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
High School Diploma or GED</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Experience and Skills<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 years of working experience in industrial environments.</li>
<li>
Electronic/controls experience a must.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Allen Bradley PLCs, VFDs, HMIs, and Networks (Device Net, ControlNet, Remote IO, Data Highway Plus, and Ethernet).</li>
<li>
Experience with various Human Machine Interfaces.</li>
<li>
Identify the most critical work that needs to be done. Coordinate and organize resources and people in effective ways to accomplish short, medium and long terms goals.</li>
<li>
Present complex technical problems in a simple way ( use exception reporting to keep managers informed</li>
<li>
Work well in cooperation with others to achieve measurable results in time allocated or less.</li>
<li>
Thinking Analytically - Concentrate on analyzing essential facts and be very methodical in solving problems.</li>
<li>
Computer Skills - Proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel, etc.</li>
<li>
Thinking Creatively - Be able to develop creative solutions to challenging problems and be receptive to new ideas.</li>
</ul>
