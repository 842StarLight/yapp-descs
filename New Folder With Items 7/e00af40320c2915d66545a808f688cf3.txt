<h2>Accounts Receivable and Collections Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>The Accounts Receivable and Collections Specialist is responsible for managing the accounts receivables and collections process while maintaining exceptional customer relations.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working closely with the Accounting Manager, will analyze, reconcile and collect accounts receivables.</li>
<li>Prepare and submit customer invoices in accordance with established payment terms.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain good customer relations regarding the collection of overdue account payment.</li>
<li>Advise, arrange and execute payment options/terms.</li>
<li>Process credit card, ACH, or electronic check payments.</li>
<li>Generate open order report for reconciliation and problem resolution.</li>
<li>Create and process invoices on all completed fulfillment sales orders and/or provided services.</li>
<li>Work directly with internal departments regarding A/R and collections process and procedures.</li>
<li>Prepare invoice revisions to include refunds, credits and authorized adjustments.</li>
<li>Update customer master data to ensure reporting is correct and account activity notes have been recorded.</li>
<li>Researches short pays to determine if any adjustments or collections are necessary.</li>
<li>Assist in streamlining all collection processes and recommend improvements.</li>
<li>Performs any other duties as may be required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>This position has no supervisory responsibilities.</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or any combination of education, training and experience that demonstrates the ability to perform the duties of the position.</li>
<li>Minimum two (2) years accounts receivable/collections experience</li>
<li>Hight degree of accuracy and attention to detail</li>
<li>Must be able to effectively communicate in English, both written and oral, with customers, vendors, and employees.</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills.</li>
<li>Must be proficient in using Microsoft Office 365</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PREFERRED EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates or BA degree in accounting</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze and interpret accounts receivable reports.</li>
</ul>
