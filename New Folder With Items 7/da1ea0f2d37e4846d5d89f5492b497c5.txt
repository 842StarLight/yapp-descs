<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Prepare, red line, negotiate and manage commercial contracts including software contracts, services agreements, statements of work, amendments and related legal agreements</li>
<li>Engage in frequent communications with both internal and vendor’s legal teams</li>
<li>Responsible for entering contracts into contract management tools</li>
<li>Liaises with departments such as finance and legal to ensure agreements are in compliance with legal and budgetary guidelines and policies.</li>
<li>Acts as the liaison and point of contact for both internal and cross-functional projects.</li>
<li>Evaluate project status and resource utilisation; implement changes as needed; review status reports prepared by project personnel and modify schedules or plans as required</li>
<li>Create project plans and builds consensus among diverse sets of stakeholders.</li>
<li>Support management through project planning and coordination to meet commitments on time and within resource limitations; with client and management approval, assist with the determination of requirements, priorities and monitoring of progress.</li>
<li>Work within program discipline and develop expertise as the documentation subject matter expert (SME), to expedite revisions, corrections, and changes to previously published documentation.</li>
<li>Meet regularly with client management for status updates.</li>
<li>Lead daily operations and directs the implementation of standards, procedures and best practices that ensures a cohesive, consistent and uniformed global program.</li>
<li>Provide ongoing communication of planning, project status, issues and risks in a timely fashion to internal security teams, cross functional management and executive teams.</li>
<li>Manage performance metrics to evaluate progress and success.</li>
<li>Design standardised processes and Best Practices from initiation to completion in partnership with SMEs, including project scope, documentation, measurements,communication and implementation to stakeholders and management.</li>
<li>Manage end-to-end project plans ensuring on-time delivery while driving execution.</li>
<li>Researches and evaluates industry standards and benchmarks to support projects.</li>
<li>Fosters strong cross-pyramid partnerships and able to effectively prioritise workload</li>
<li>demands to meet project deadlines.</li>
<li>Create sustainable communication themes in conjunction with systematic routines,processes, and preparedness activities for client to execute consistently.</li>
<li>Distinctive problem-solving and analytical skills combined with strong business acumen</li>
<li>and technical saviness.</li>
</ul>
