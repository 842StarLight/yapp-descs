<h2>Technical Support Audiologist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>POSITION TITLE:
</b></p>
<p>Technical Support Audiologist</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PRIMARY FUNCTION:
</b></p>
<p>To provide excellent customer service by addressing technical issues with the customer in a timely and responsive manner, ensuring remake and credit return rates are low and offering technical support to our customers, both in house and in the field.
</p>
<p><b>OBJECTIVES OR GOALS:
</b></p>
<p>To interact with customers, Field Sales staff, Field Audiology Support and manufacturing to provide a high level of technical support to GN Resound customers, both in house and in the field.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
AREAS OF RESPONSIBILITY:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as Technical Support for incoming customer calls for GN product and software issues and/or forward to appropriate person(s).</li>
<li>Review incoming new and remake orders in a timely manner on an as needed basis.
</li>
<li>Follow up on technical issues with customers with respect to incoming orders and repairs.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Field Sales, Product Management and Manufacturing to the deliver expectations of GN customers.</li>
<li>Initiates new processes to improve effectiveness in meeting customer expectations and in operating efficiently as a team.</li>
<li>Interacts with Manufacturing and Quality Departments to provide feedback on customer issues and stay current on company policies.</li>
<li>Assist with the development of tools that result in more effective and efficient customer service.
</li>
<li>Other duties as required (i.e. customer service back-up).</li>
<li>Maintain good standing with professional regulatory body, including Continuing Education Units (CEU).<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION:
</b></p>
<p>University level education and training in Audiology is required.</p>
