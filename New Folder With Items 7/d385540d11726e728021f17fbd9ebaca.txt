<h2>Web Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p>The Web Marketing Specialist will develop and implement the company’s web-based marketing strategy to promote the company’s brand, attract visitors and potential leads, and generate internet sales.</p>
<ul><li>
<i>Duties/Responsibilities: </i>*</li></ul>
<p><b>_</b>_<br><i>*</i>*</p>
<ul>
<li>Plans and implements web-based marketing strategies and campaigns via the company’s website(s).</li>
<li>Collaborates with sales to fully understand product and communication needs.</li>
<li>Gathers the materials necessary to understand the project and competition in the area such as literature or previous marketing campaigns; analyzes these materials to determine the most effective communications technique</li>
<li>Collaborates with web development and design partners; provides guidance on marketing-related aspects of the company’s web presence.</li>
<li>Develops and expands company’s web presence through social media, email, web advertising, and other online sources; promotes company brand through these channels.</li>
<li>Drafts, implements, maintains, and revises online marketing campaigns to drive sales and revenue to the company.</li>
<li>Coordinates with other team members to ensure active promotions and campaigns are represented on the website(s).</li>
<li>Collaborates with web design partners to monitor and improve search engine optimization (SEO) results for the company’s site(s).</li>
<li>Maintains current knowledge of trends and developments in online marketing.</li>
<li>Assists with budget preparation for marketing department.</li>
<li>Creates and coordinates multimedia packages.</li>
<li>Composes draft speeches and/or presentations for senior management and assists with finalizing presentations.</li>
<li>Evaluates success of campaigns when completed.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<i>Required Skills/Abilities: </i>*</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of marketing principles and strategies.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Proven ability to plan and implement marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail.</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills with a proven ability to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Proficient with HTML and web design.</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite or similar software.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Education and Experience: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business, Communications, or related field required.</li>
<li>One to three years of related experience preferred.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
<i>Physical Requirements: </i>*</li></ul>
<p><b>_</b>_<br><i>*</i>*</p>
<p>· Prolonged periods sitting at a desk and working on a computer.</p>
<p>· Must be able to lift up to 15 pounds at times.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
