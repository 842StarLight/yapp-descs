<h2>Vice President of Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>BAE Systems’ Intelligence &amp; Security Sector (I&amp;S) is seeking a Vice President of Business Development for its Air Force Solutions (AFS) business area. Reporting directly to the Sector Vice President, Business Winning, this executive will partner with their business area leadership team to drive the organization’s strategic growth objectives by developing and refining an understanding of customers’ most important needs and creating/leading winning capture strategies. With responsibility for a staff of business development professionals serving USAF strategic programs, aircraft readiness &amp; modernization, sensor systems, and enterprise Information Technology (IT), the selected individual will lead customer engagement and pipeline growth. In addition, this individual will plan and recommend BD and marketing strategies to achieve maximum customer and market penetration and to drive tactical and strategic growth. The position is located in McLean Virginia and will require frequent travel to company an/or Customer locations.
<br>
<br>
<b>Specific responsibilities include:</b><ul>
<li>
Grow the opportunity pipeline to 10X of AFS annual revenues, to include existing as well as adjacent markets/customers, through diligent and timely identification/qualification of new business opportunities by leveraging current technologies, customer intimacies, and inter-company collaboration</li>
<li>
Develop and execute strategic and tactical plans for the pursuit and successful capture of key opportunities</li>
<li>
Lead and manager team of BD professionals to maximize customer engagement with efficient territory management</li>
<li>
Oversee major capture activities for the business area to including managing and developing a team of capture managers in the organization</li>
<li>
Obtain marketing intelligence and competitive data pertaining to potential targeted pursuits, and develop marketing strategies</li>
<li>
Participate in bid decisions, development of cost strategies and phase reviews</li>
<li>
Establish, build, and maintain customer relationships and assess competitor capabilities aligned to specific customers in intelligence community.</li>
<ul>
<li>Support creation and execution of multi-year business development strategies</li>
<li>Participate in IRAD and B&amp;P planning as well as overhead budgets</li>
</ul>
</ul>
