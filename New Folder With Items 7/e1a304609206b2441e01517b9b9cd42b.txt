<h2>Manager, S&OP - Cordis</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The primary function of the S&amp;OP Manager is to ensure that all Global teams are adhering to the S&amp;OP process as set forth for their specific areas. This means ensuring regular and timely meetings occur, standard agendas are used, action registers are published and metrics are reported and leveraged to guide future decision making where relevant. This role will develop S&amp;OP process metrics to help ensure the process. The Manager will also assist in the preparation of Executive S&amp;OP materials to provide global visibility to key demand, supply, capacity, service and performance areas.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities/Accountabilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Help develop standard S&amp;OP processes across Demand &amp; Supply teams within each region across all product lines for the Medical Solutions, Cordis and International business</li>
<li>
Create standard educational materials for cross-functional business partners so that roles and expectations are clearly defined across Sales, Marketing, Finance, Plants, and Regional Supply Chain teams</li>
<li>
Conduct training for Demand and Supply teams as the new process is being rolled-out</li>
<li>
Develop standard templates, meeting agendas, calendars, and other tools to leverage across geographies and product lines that allow for aggregation from disparate systems</li>
<li>
Leverage metrics and scorecards for teams to use to measure performance and help drive future decisions</li>
<li>
Create S&amp;OP process metrics and scorecards to drive consistent process</li>
<li>
Audit different meetings each month to ensure content and discussion delivers on intended purpose</li>
<li>
Work with cross-functional departments to solve process deficiencies</li>
<li>
Develop Executive agendas and aggregate data from various process segments</li>
<li>
Continue to optimize process as it matures and set and drive toward process success metrics</li>
<li>
Research and remain abreast of latest tools, technologies, and best practices</li>
</ul>
