<div>
Description</div>
<div>
<div>
<div>
As part of Application and Marketing department for Latin America and Mexico, based in Mexico, you will be responsible for clinical application support, as part of the marketing and sales support team, to support and grow Radiometer’s customers.
</div>
<div><b>The job</b></div>
<div>
To become successful, reporting to the General Manager, your responsibilities and tasks will among others include:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li><div>
Take responsibility as Product Manager for Radiometer´s Blood Gas and Immunoassay products.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop, coordinate and execute actions plans to promote products under your responsibility both internally with sales employees and with customers.</div></li>
<li><div>
In coordination with the sales team, to plan prospection strategies looking for new business opportunities in Blood Gas and Immunassay market segment.</div></li>
<li><div>
Scientific and applications support to current customers, with visits on regular basis.</div></li>
<li><div>
Support to the sales team for training of current and new customers</div></li>
<li><div>
Actively promote activities in the customers, to get evidence of the high performance of the Radiometer products. Posters, publications, workshops, conferences, etc.</div></li>
<li><div>
Under your own responsibility, to lead new projects in new segments, establishing relationships with key opinion leaders</div></li>
<li><div>
Coordinate marketing activities for Radiometer products with our headquarter in Copenhagen and reports to them.</div></li>
<li><div>
Make and supervise technical offers for public tenders.</div></li>
</ul>
