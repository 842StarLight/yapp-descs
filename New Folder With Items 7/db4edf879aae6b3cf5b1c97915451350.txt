<h2>Road Warrior - Advertising Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>This is the perfect role for the business development/sales</i></b><b><i> road warrior.</i></b><b><i>..you will be on the road 70% of the time (with overnight travel) serving local businesses in a vast territory. If you love to travel and enjoy the thrill of being in a new location every week...read on!</i></b></p>
<p>Do you want to be a part of a positive and fun organization? LPi is growing its Advertising Sales team nationwide. At LPi our mission is connecting a dedicated readership with local business through print, digital sales, and online advertising. We are looking for candidates with a proven track record of sales success, excellent communication skills, fierce work ethic, and excellent communication skills. This is a great role for a sales professional that will want to drive new business development and make an immediate impact on local communities.</p>
<p><b> A Week in Your New Career</b></p>
<p>Each week you will build a relationship with an existing LPi customer by working from their church or organization’s office. Your days will be spent making phone calls and visiting local businesses on behalf of our customers to help get their advertising word out in the local community! The new ad revenue you generate from local businesses allows us to provide our print and digital solutions to the customer at minimal cost.</p>
<p><b> What Makes This Job Unique</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Travel to churches weekly working in designated location designed to strengthen relationship and generate additional sales for publication and digital offerings</li>
<li>Contact local businesses to advertise on digital &amp; printed bulletins distributed weekly</li>
<li>Collect materials from advertisers to place ads</li>
<li>Process and track orders in a CRM</li>
<li>Manage your schedule and book of business that is provided</li>
<li>Adaptability to be in a different location each week (Mon.-Thurs.)</li>
<li>A hunter mentality – this role is 100% new business development</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work remotely - travel 80%+ annually</li>
<li>Ability to make 50+ business calls daily for new business development</li>
<li>Experience prospecting your own leads</li>
<li>Resilience and ability to close sales in a short cycle environment</li>
<li>Exceptional time management skills</li>
<li>A solid understanding of Microsoft Office (esp. Excel, Word, and Outlook)</li>
<li>2 years of proven B2B sales experience or recent grads with B2B sales experience (preferred but not required)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualities That Will Help You Succeed</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Professional that wants to work with small local businesses</li>
<li>Sales minded, entrepreneurial spirit that thrives on closing business</li>
<li>Self-motivated, ability to work independently as you will be working remotely</li>
<li>Persistence and proven success making high volume new business development calls</li>
<li>Versatility minded (you will be working a different location/city each week)</li>
<li>Passion to help businesses grow by offering quality advertising solutions</li>
<li>Proven organizational skills</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial spirit (we provide you with a book of business to manage)</li>
<li>Enjoy the benefits of a short sales cycle</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation &amp; Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Guaranteed minimum base salary</li>
<li>Uncapped commissions earning potential</li>
<li>Health, Dental, Vision, benefits, 401k, HSA</li>
<li>Expenses reimbursed weekly</li>
<li>2 weeks of paid training in a classroom environment and in the field</li>
<li>Ongoing field training &amp; development</li>
<li>Continual coaching to develop your skills and ensure your continued success</li>
</ul>
<p>LPi is the industry leader in stewardship communications that serve as a single source for connecting communities through printed materials and online services. Specializing in Catholic churches, LPi offers communication tools for religious and community organizations that include ad-supported bulletins and newsletters, and solutions for online donations, church websites, and communication networks. Family-owned since 1972 and backed by industry-leading customer service, LPi builds relationships with nearly 100,000 local businesses to support its more than 4,000 customers with advertising.</p>
