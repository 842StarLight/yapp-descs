<h2>Mechanical Designer/Detailer (Brighton, MI)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Miller Industries, LLC is a family owned and operated company, located in Fenton, Michigan and was formed in July 2014. We are the parent company to Performance Fabricating, Flex Air, Miller Industries Representatives, and Farm Pod, all located under the roof of our 106,000 sq. foot building. We specialize in custom steel fabrication, conveyor fabrication, robotic welding and plasma cutting, air supply systems and sales, representing numerous companies in HVAC applications. Our products consist of conveyor systems, structural steel projects, stairs and platforms, baggage handling systems, air handling systems and powder-coated metal products. We provide our high-quality products to automotive, healthcare, data center, and industrial customers around the world. Our products are proudly made in the USA.</p>
<p><b>
We are currently seeking a Mechanical Designer/Detailer for our office in Brighton, MI.</b></p>
<p>
The Mechanical Designer/Detailer is responsible for various mechanical designs, layouts, details, calculations and documentation related to conveyor equipment and material handling systems.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work directly with the Project Manager and Project Engineer to produce mechanical designs of conveyor components and material handling systems.</li>
<li>Prepare detail shop drawings of the mechanical designs to meet the customer requirements.</li>
<li>Generate 3D models of mechanical designs based on design documents as required.</li>
<li>Production of 2D detail shop drawings from 3D models and design documents.</li>
<li>Reviewing, checking and validation of 2D detail shop drawings for completeness and accuracy.
</li>
<li>Maintain and organize project required documentation to support the detail shop drawing package.</li>
<li>Travel to customer project sites to perform field checks as required.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as required and assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Associates degree in Mechanical Engineering, B.S. MET, Cad Design or Equivalent</li>
<li>A minimum of 5 years' experience in reading and understanding mechanical design drawings and documents.</li>
<li>Understands the basics of mechanical design practices.</li>
<li>Familiar with structural elements such as platforms, ladders, stairs and miscellaneous steel.
</li>
<li>Excellent planning and organizational skills for a team oriented environment.</li>
<li>Ability to work on multiple projects as required<b>.</b>
</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and is highly motivated. Must be proactive and can work with minimal instruction.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
AutoCAD, AutoCAD 3D, Advance Steel, Tekla &amp; SDS2 detailing software.</li>
<li>Autodesk Inventor, Solid works and Navisworks</li>
<li>MS Office Suite</li>
</ul>
