<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div>
<div></div>
<div>Reconcile revenue records to the General Ledger for all areas under the Oglesby Union: Art Center, Crenshaw Lanes/Games, Guest Services, Student Activities Center, Club Downunder, Flea Market, Union Productions, ASLC Films and Flying High Circus, to ensure that all revenue is accounted for, and properly posted under the correct revenue account code and department ID/fund combination. Ensure sales taxes are charged appropriately and keep records of customersâ€™ tax exemption forms. Prepare monthly Sales Tax Report and the corresponding form for payment. Receive, post and deposit payments for space rental, ATM space rental and transactions fees. Maintain Accounts Receivable records for Art Center, Games, ASLC Films and Oglesby Union Operations in compliance with the Florida State University policies and procedures. Track and resolve outstanding payment issues and generate monthly aging reports. Maintain daily deposits and sales inventory sheets. Serve as a liaison for the Oglesby Union for all revenue transactions and sale tax with the University.<br>
<br>
Enter requisitions for all areas under the Oglesby Union using the Spear Mart system. Complete and submit Purchase Order Change Request Forms as needed. Serve as back-up to ensure Purchasing Card transactions are verified with appropriate expense account codes, descriptions, and in compliance with the State of Florida and Florida State University policies and procedures.<br>
<br>
Devise ways to optimize inventory control procedures. Continuously evaluate/monitor products' prices, and analyze variances on a monthly basis to achieve revenue goals. Record deliveries and daily sales reports to reconcile resales items inventory, and physically verify inventory on a quarterly basis for Art Center, Crenshaw, ASLC Films and Flying High Circus.<br>
<br>
Serve as liaison to University's PCI Compliance Office. Update PCI Compliance manual to meet University and PCI requirements annually. Ensure appropriate staff complete required PCI Compliance training annually. Provide training about cash handling procedures to new staff; to include distribution of required cash and credit card policies, obtain signatures, and maintain documentation.<br>
<br>
Responsible for the monthly audit of change and petty cash. Process refunds for Debit card transactions for all collection points. Complete Expenditure Transfer Journals Entry forms. Process unencumbered payments (ePRFs). Monitor past due Purchase Orders and run monthly queries to review outstanding Purchases Orders, 30 days past the due date, verify if services/goods have been received, and obtain the corresponding invoice or, if applicable close the encumbrance.<br>
<br>
Serve as backup for daily deposits, complete the Revenue Deposit Form. Assist with the reconciliation of the General Ledgers to ensure accuracy. Complete Expenditure Transfer Journal Entry forms and other duties as assigned.
</div>
</div>
</div></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h2>
<div>
<div></div>
<div>Bachelor's degree; or high school diploma/equivalency and four years of experience related to the duties of this position.<br>
<br>
(Note: a combination of post high school education and experience equal to four years.)<br>
<br>
Knowledge of accounting systems and practices.<br>
<br>
Knowledge of and ability to apply Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP).<br>
<br>
Knowledge of electronic data processing as it applies to fiscal and accounting activities.<br>
<br>
Knowledge of the concepts, principles, and practices of data collection.<br>
Computer skills to include experience with Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook, and PowerPoint or comparable software.<br>
<br>
Ability to perform accurately in a detail-oriented environment.<br>
<br>
Ability to prioritize, organize and perform multiple work assignments simultaneously.<br>
<br>
Ability to compile, review, and reconcile data for accuracy, completeness, and compliance.<br>
<br>
Ability to communicate effectively verbally and in writing.<br>
<br>
Ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships.
