<b>SUMMARY:</b><br>
Under the general direction of the Senior Director, Systems and Accounting and Director of Accounting, Operations, the Accountant, Operations is responsible for general accounting functions, including reviewing and reconciling accounting data, updating and maintaining documentation of accounting processes and maintaining internal controls. The Accountant, Operations will have strong analytical and reasoning skills with a thorough knowledge of accounting.
<br><br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
Accounts Receivable
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Process incoming AR payments and GL postings for both Simpleview and Destination Travel Network.</li>
<li>Maintain Customer Accounts and database; reconcile any outstanding AR issues.</li>
<li>Communicate with customers via phone, email or mail while maintaining a professional and polite demeanor. Ensure documentation is maintained for all calls made and follow up across all clients.</li>
<li>Run AR Aging: Review and monitor A/R aging weekly.</li>
<li>Make collection calls as needed and/or directed by management; maintain collections schedule on Calendar.</li>
<li>Customer Inquiries: Field customer calls identifying credit card charges and supplying invoice copies, statements and any other customer requirements as needed.</li>
<li>Reconcile A/R, and other related balance sheet accounts monthly.</li>
<li>Month-end close, including preparation of journal entry processing.</li>
<li>Process Cash Sales (Invoices): SimplePay and Summit.</li>
</ul>
Other Duties
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Simple Pay portal account analysis and reconciliation.</li>
<li>Pay Per Click – administrator, account analysis and reconciliation.</li>
<li>Prepare journal entries as needed.</li>
<li>Prepare fixed asset and internally developed software reconciliations.</li>
<li>Assist with monthly close procedures and ensure deadlines are met.</li>
<li>Assist in the audit of the company’s financial statements.</li>
<li>Assist in the implementation of accounting systems and processes improvement.</li>
<li>Support other team members as required.</li>
<li>Other ad hoc analyses, reports, and projects as needed by management.</li>
</ul>

<li>Advanced Excel skills and experience with Word and other Office/Windows applications.</li>
<li>Team player that works well with others and effectively across departments.</li>
<li>Ability to meet deadlines, identify/solve problems and identify/propose potential efficiencies.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask effectively in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Organized with strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>CPA, or studying towards admission.</li>
</ul>
