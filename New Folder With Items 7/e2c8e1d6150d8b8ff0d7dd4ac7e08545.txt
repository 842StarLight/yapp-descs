<p>Cadence is seeking a web UX / UI Designer to be part of its dynamic web marketing team, which is focused on enhancing user experience to optimize lead capture, generate more self-service sales, improve conversion rates and delight our customers. This position plays a key role in the overall marketing mix for Cadence and offers the opportunity to play a significant role in designing customer-facing websites for one of the industry’s leading technology companies. You must be confident enough to debate, discuss and influence internal stakeholders on new user experience strategies while partnering with business group leaders across the company to achieve visible objectives. <p></p>
<p><b>Title: </b>Web UX/UI Designer
</p>
<p><b>Location: </b>San Jose, CA
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with product owners and campaign managers to create engaging web experiences that convey product leadership, increase customer acquisition and improve functionality for our web site interfaces
</li>
<li>Deliver wireframes, visual design, prototypes to articulate functionality and user experience
</li>
<li>Expand upon existing template designs, UI components, and style guidelines
</li>
<li>Design with an awareness and understanding of browser sizes, device capabilities, and product functionality
</li>
<li>Utilize A/B testing, web analytics, user research, and best practices to help inform your design solutions
</li>
<li>Work closely with web developers to ensure your designs are built to your specifications
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Must Haves:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4+ years of experience creating design that drives user engagement and other large-scale web-based experiences targeting B2B market
</li>
<li>A portfolio of design projects
</li>
<li>Proficiency in design techniques, such as user research, concept sketching, storyboarding, UI design patterns, interaction prototyping, etc.
</li>
<li>Strong experience in using design tools such as Sketch, InVision, Adobe CS and Zeplin
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A bachelor's degree in graphic design or related UX degree is preferred
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of front-end skills (HTML, CSS and JS)
</li>
<li>Knowledge of industry best practices for SEO and accessibility
</li>
<li>Self-starter that can collaborate actively with others in a cross-functional team
</li>
<li>Excellent time-management, multi-tasking, strong communication skills; and the ability to quickly prioritize tasks
</li>
</ul>
