<h2>Business Development Sr. Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Microsoft is on a mission to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. Our culture is centered on embracing a growth mindset, a theme of inspiring excellence, and encouraging teams and leaders to bring their best each day. In doing so, we create life-changing innovations that impact billions of lives around the world. You can help us achieve our mission.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Payments and Commerce Strategy and Business Development Sr. Manager is a new, industry-focused role within Microsoft’s Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Cloud Business Development team. The individual in this role will use their strategic business development experience and expertise in the Payments, Commerce, and/or Retail industry to develop a deep understanding of customer needs in order to influence Microsoft’s Payments industry strategy and accelerate digital transformation of the $2T Payments sector through innovative partnerships with industry-leading companies and disruptors.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
By 2020, a forecasted $4T will be spent on digital transformation across all industries. 85% of enterprises will use AI and Intelligent Cloud services to digitally transform their businesses in areas such as product transformation, operational efficiency, empowering employees, and customer engagement. At Microsoft we have the opportunity to deliver the intelligent cloud + intelligent edge services that deliver these outcomes. The newly chartered Payments and Commerce Business Development team will play a pivotal role influencing how Microsoft will serve customers through such digital transformation.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Strategy Development: Collaborate with stakeholders across the company (engineering, marketing, sales, finance, legal) in development of business and product strategy, translate business and product strategy into an ecosystem strategy, and identify and architect partnerships and M&amp;A opportunities that can drive significant new businesses for Microsoft.</li>
<li>
Transaction Execution: Identify, formulate and execute custom-structured partnership deals that accelerate growth opportunities for Microsoft in the Payments &amp; Commerce sector, focused on our AI &amp; Intelligent Cloud business. Leverage strong executive communication, negotiation, and influence skills to build internal alignment and external buy-in for new strategies and partnerships. Drive early deals that lay the groundwork for the optimal partner ecosystem that supports digital transformation in the Payments space. Help identify inorganic growth opportunities.</li>
<li>
Deal Governance: Monitor complex deal performance and track efficacy relative to expectations post-close. Maintain ongoing relationship with partners to ensure compliance and commitments are fulfilled.</li>
<li>
External Relationships: Maintain a network of relationships across the industry with partners, potential partners, acquisition targets, competitors, investors, thought leaders, etc. Leverage relationships in opportunity identification and transaction execution.</li>
<li>
Industry Expertise: Be an expert in AI &amp; Intelligent Cloud solutions especially as it relates to business and value chain transformation within the Payments and Commerce sector. Serve as a trusted advisor to stakeholders.</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<div><b>
Qualifications (Knowledge, Skills and Abilities)</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Planning and Strategy: Business strategy and market development expertise with deep analytical and business modeling skills. Strong 360-degree strategic thinking is critical to success in the role.
</li>
<li>Passion: Create great end to end customer experiences including technology services that open new growth opportunities and innovations in business models and partner alliances. Desire and capacity to stay current on rapidly evolving technologies, global market dynamics, and competitive dynamics.</li>
<li>
Influence: Ability to influence, drive results, and contribute to the success of others in a complex and highly ambiguous technical and business environment including ability to communicate and influence all levels from senior executives on down and all functions (including engineering, marketing, sales, services, finance and legal)</li>
<li>
Collaboration: Independently leading cross-disciplinary virtual teams and driving key business development initiatives for moderately complex deals from creation to implementation in partnership with senior Microsoft executives.
</li>
<li>Key interpersonal traits: Motivation, creativity, adaptability, unquestionable ethics and integrity, bias towards data-based decision making and a willingness to take calculated risks</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>

Required/Minimum Qualifications</b></p>

<li>
Understanding of Payments, Commerce, Retail, and/or Fintech industries</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel up to 25%</li>
<li>
Candidates should have high fluency (written and spoken, business and technical) in English</li>

<li>
Relevant experience in structuring, influencing/negotiating, and managing complex commercial deals and/or acquisitions
</li>
<li>How cloud computing is impacting the sectors of Payments, Commerce, Retail, and/or Fintech</li>

Solid familiarity with US and international business and social culture in Europe and Asia.</li>
