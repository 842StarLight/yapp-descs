<b>
The role:</b> The Forge Capital Systems Partnership Team is responsible for forging new product partnerships in the private company ecosystem and in targeted industry verticals. Team members typically have a background in entrepreneurship, project management, and product partnerships. As one of <b>Forge’s Capital Systems Associates</b>, you will drive new business through collaborating with distribution channels and partners to manage market activity, grow deal flow, and source demand and supply when needed, in addition to inbound interest from our clients. The ideal candidate is a deal maker at heart who thrives on finding product market fit, and someone who loves to help partners solve problems and derive value with disruptive technology.</div>
<div>
<br>

<div>
Through these partnerships, we’re developing new strategies that will take Forge to the next level, such as utilizing third-party data sources to dramatically expand our company information, working with strategic partners to improve users’ ability to pursue opportunities with companies, and improve our overall product offerings.</div>

<div>
Forge is a premier trading and settlement firm for a wide range of investors in the private markets. Established in 2014 as Equidate, with the vision of facilitating the sale of pre-IPO equity, the company empowers investors and shareholders by enabling liquidity in the private markets. Forge was founded by Y Combinator alumni and backed by top investors including Tim Draper, FT Partners, and Munich Re. Forge's platform allows employees and investors in privately held innovation firms to liquidate a portion of their shares and provides private and institutional investors access to top companies like Spotify, Lyft and 23andMe before their IPO.</div>
