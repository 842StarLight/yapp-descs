<b>Job responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Become a subject matter expert of the AlertMiner software, One-to-One communications, and One-to-One sales process to effectively retain customers</li>
<li>Identify customers in a position to trade vehicles for a newer version for about the same</li>
</ul>
monthly payment
<br><ul>
<li>Understand custom search capabilities to identify additional opportunities; i.e. retail to lease conversions, high-interest-rate customers, and customers without recent outreach</li>
<li>Proactively contact customers through emails and phone calls to ask for their trade and to notify them of exclusive and expiring offers</li>
<li>Delegate outbound phone calls to sales managers and sales consultants to optimize efficiency</li>
<li>Respond to inbound leads from One-to-One mailers, URLs, and emails from organic outreach</li>
<li>Rectify database as needed by inputting proper mileage from vehicle history reports, marking sold lease vehicles, updating customer contact information, etc.</li>
</ul>
