<h2>Senior Manager Product Experience</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>

<p>Senior Manager Product Experience</p> - (1900024E)</div>
<div>
<div>
</div>
<p><b>Main objective / Context</b></p>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The Product Experience senior manager leads a team of managers within the region who serve as both subject matter experts across CWT’s portfolio that lead both local &amp; regional market positioning activity and insight (e.g. market, customer, industry, competition). This is also combined with a client facing role related to competitively positioning and demoing those product &amp; service offerings.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The subject matter (market positioning) expert role collaborates closely with CWT’s Customer Organization in support of articulating and bringing to life CWT value propositions. This applies for both individual product &amp; services as well as CWT’s overall end to end differentiated positioning activity in front of both clients and prospects.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Ultimately the role supports CWT’s Sales &amp; PM teams win new sales, improve client retention and increase product and service revenues by developing and executing strategies that effectively position CWT’s value propositions with clients, prospects and industry thought leaders. This includes but is not limited to: strategizing and executing tasks to promote ongoing penetration, growth and success of CWT’s corporate travel products, innovation concepts, and pilots regionally.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>To be successful, this individual must be able to develop, present and demo client-specific product and technology and positioning strategies to differentiate CWT’s value; confidently represent CWT’s strategic position on technology trends at industry events and other client facing opportunities; capable to learning/understanding client needs and translate these into opportunities sharing knowledge internally with key stakeholders.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The role requires a person that has a strong ability to drive collaboration and buy-in from senior level stakeholders, presentation skills and exceptional gravitas in front of senior buyers, and the ability to problem solve ‘outside the box’. This position also provides thought leadership and manages customer product feedback through direct observation and facilitation of regional customer focus groups. This is a highly visible role in the organization and plays a critical part in successfully demonstrating CWT’s value to our most significant clients and prospects.</div>
<br>

</li></ul>

<p>o Direct Product Sales &amp; Client Engagements—conduct product discussions, demonstrations, reviews and presentations as requested by clients, prospects, and internal teams</p>
<p>o Ability to support sales and PMs when determining customer pain points and decision triggers through high-gain questioning of internal and prospects/clients (travel buyers)</p>
<ul><li> Contribute to the Global Product Marketing responsibilities by providing essential feedback on product and service strategies that will effectively help deliver improved client value</li></ul>
<p>o Contribute to the x.Builder framework, helping to manage and maintain groups of internal and external customers to gain insights, input and feedback on product sales strategy and development needs. Ensure feedback is properly assessed and utilized in product management, launch management, communications, deployment, education etc.</p>
<p>o Anticipate, communicate, and help clear roadblocks that may deter proposed recommendations (such as technology interdependency concerns, configuration limitations, client culture, etc.)</p>
<p>o Remain agile throughout client facing positioning work to adjust recommended product and service strategy according to additional client discovery</p>
<ul><li> Sales Enablement: Develop and execute strategies to prepare CWT sales and program management teams to effectively communicate and best position the value of CWT and our propositions</li></ul>
<p>o Recommend best product utilization, messaging, and mix to offer value to client need - help contribute to brief product marketing content teams to develop differentiated messaging and fit for purpose sales enablement collaterals the product positioning to internal and external stakeholders</p>
<p>o Support marketing communication efforts to develop self-service tools (for product discussions and demonstrations)</p>
<p>o Support the delivery of sales/program management education sessions (e.g. internal Webinars re: product value proposition and/or feature/functionality updates)</p>
<p>o Create, lead and manage regular interactions with customer facing teams to support education sessions on all areas of product value, technical capabilities &amp; limitations</p>
<p>o Coach other marketing areas/teams (e.g. communications and pursuit) to provide better understanding of internal and external client needs, challenges, and expectations around products</p>
<ul><li> Train, develop and lead the team providing guidance, purpose and maximizing performance</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Responsibilities/duties include, but not limited to:</li></ul>
<p>o Ability to confidently and knowledgeably discuss, demonstrate, and present CWT products and their positioning</p>
<p>o Ability to speak authoritatively about the value propositions and interdependencies of the full range of products and services available</p>
<ul><li> Internal Product SME — maintain a deep level of CWT product expertise and act as a subject matter expert</li></ul>
<p>o Self-guide individual training to ensure appropriate level of product expertise is maintained across entire CWT portfolio. Product expertise should be maintained in each of the following aspects: technical capabilities and limitations, strategic purpose, competitive landscape and client value proposition</p>
<p>o After initial on-boarding, expectation is for individual to regularly self-assess and then build product knowledge as needed primarily through direct research, training material review, and conversations with subject matter experts</p>
<p>o Scan the market for new technology and/or industry developments that will impact client/prospect challenges and assess how CWT technology solutions are positioned to respond. Be able to intelligently discuss and present trends and CWT response to these trends.</p>
