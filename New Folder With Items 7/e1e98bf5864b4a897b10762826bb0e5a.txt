<h2>Email Marketing & Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview</b><br>
We’re looking for an Email Marketing and Operations Manager to join our growing marketing team. This role is for a multi-talented, energetic person who is prepared to roll up their sleeves to drive demand through field and channel marketing and marketing automation systems. They will have proven, hands-on experience in supporting corporate marketing and marketing operations. Our ideal candidate is a marketing automation expert at creating, executing and improving campaigns that produce measurable top-of-funnel impact results. This role reports to the Director of Lead Generation.<br>
<br>
<b>What We Do</b><br>
We provide easy, comprehensive Secure Access solutions that provide visibility and seamless, protected connectivity between users, devices, things and services. Our suites uniquely integrate cloud, mobile, application and network access to enable hybrid IT. Today, more than 80% of the Fortune 500 and over 20,000 enterprises and service providers across every vertical entrust Pulse Secure to empower their mobile workforce to securely access applications and information in the data center and cloud while ensuring business compliance.<br>
<br>
<b>Your Impact</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Contribute to the development of a multi-touch, integrated demand generation strategy that targets and reaches the right prospects, collaborating closely with the Director, Lead Generation</li>
<li>Be responsible for overall marketing automation system and ensure we’re A/B testing and optimizing the database</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with Field and Channel marketing team to create/support Field and Channel communications and event follow up campaigns and tracking</li>
<li>Drive email event marketing to drive awareness and registrations for Field and Channel events</li>
<li>Working along with the Demand Generation Manager, collaborate with sales in defining and maintaining target accounts</li>
<li>Continually refine campaigns and programs to improve lead velocity and conversion results</li>
<li>Generally contribute to improving overall demand generation strategy, programs and infrastructure</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Your Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 3+ years’ experience running demand generation programs</li>
<li>Past success in a B2B high-tech company with high ASP offerings; SaaS or Enterprise Software a strong plus</li>
<li>Proven ability to execute day-to-day without the need for additional consultants and contractors</li>
<li>Savvy in end-to-end inbound marketing, including smart content, smart lists, and other lifecycle stage optimization tactics; account-based background a strong plus</li>
<li>Creativity around how prospects and customers are engaged to feed A/B testing and continuous improvement</li>
<li>Hands-on expertise with Marketo and Salesforce.com. Familiar with Insight Square is a plus</li>
</ul>
