<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Overview:
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Are you a data scientist who has experience making data models? Do you have experience with Python, R or data visualization? We’re looking for an analytical, detail-oriented problem-solver to join our team as a Data Scientist I, where you’ll work alongside a group of data scientists and data analysts to scrutinize and interpret system data and provide full-fledged insights to help our customers make better decisions. In addition, you will also have the opportunity to work with other data scientists and analysts to build algorithms that power product features and support business &amp; sales teams. If you’re a top-notch communicator with a passion for data and coding, let’s talk!</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul><li>Extract actionable insights from huge volumes of data using data mining, statistics, and database techniques</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct studies, rapid pilots, and identify metrics</li>
<li>
Answer questions related to advanced models, analysis and data science.</li>
<li>
Apply best practices on data quality issues, root causes and remedies to be implemented by the data engineers</li>
<li>
Work directly with business teams and leaders to understand requirements and communicate results</li>

</ul>
