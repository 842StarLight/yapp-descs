<h2>Sales & Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Wolters Kluwer Health's Clinical Effectiveness (CE) </b><b>organization </b>is a fast-growing and innovation-driven healthcare information technology (HIT) provider working on the front lines of clinical care. Our talented team of physician and pharmacist editors, technologists, and product visionaries collaborate to provide advanced clinical decision support solutions that measurably improve clinical effectiveness by helping healthcare professionals provide optimal care for their patients.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Sales &amp; Business Development Representative for Clinical Effectiveness </b>is responsible for executing new sales and account management activities to achieve goals, exceed revenue targets, and drive profitable growth for CE products and solutions within an assigned territory. This position will generate new revenue where the primary focus will be selling CE solutions into Pharma Companies, Financial Institutions, Wholesaler Groups in the Commercial market.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>***This position works from a home office and can be based in the Central, FL or Washington, D.C. areas.
</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Learns and maintains a deep understanding of Clinical Effectiveness solutions, customers, and the industry as a whole by maintaining a high-level mastery of all processes, systems and applications utilized within the department.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to master product, marketing, technical, and executive information provided in a timely manner (e.g., features, benefits, pricing, intended use, value proposition) for a wide breadth of products.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Continuously seeks sales opportunities to support a healthy sales pipeline by reviewing and evolving a list of prospects (e.g., by geographical territory, by client segment) and researching contact information for decision-makers and influencers.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complies with and executes the sales process to meet revenue targets and sales quotas by meeting with potential clients face-to-face and conducting presentations and demonstrations; negotiating pricing and contract details; finalizing contracts and sales; and updating the CRM database throughout the client development process.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maximizes revenue for CE products and solutions within the territory or client segment by designing and implementing a strategic direction for top accounts; working with all internal teams to improve the customer experience company-wide; regularly contacting and meeting with prospects to defend revenue and maintain or improve satisfaction (e.g., remaining informed of their business needs, reinforcing the value provided); expanding usage or selling modified or upgraded solutions to meet current or future client needs.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Represents Wolters Kluwer within the industry by developing and maintaining comprehensive knowledge of Wolters Kluwer products, industry trends and general business and financial acumen through various sources and initiatives.
</li></ul>
