<div>The Army &amp; Air Force Exchange Service, also known as “the Exchange,” is the retailer on US Army and Air Force installations worldwide. In addition to US bases in Germany, Italy, England, Turkey, South Korea and Japan, the Exchange operates stores for US troops in Iraq, Afghanistan and other countries in the region.
