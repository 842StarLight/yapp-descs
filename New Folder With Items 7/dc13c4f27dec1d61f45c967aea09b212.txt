<b>
Our Company:</b><br>
<div>At Spectrum Enterprise, our goal is to foster an engaging work environment that encourages our team members to reach their full potential. We promote a culture of excellence that celebrates diversity, innovative thinking and dedication to consistently exceeding client expectations.<br>
<br>
Spectrum Enterprise is a national provider of fiber-based technology solutions that believes connectivity is fundamental to the progress of every organization. We are committed to helping our clients achieve their business goals by providing the digital infrastructure that enables them to outperform. Our portfolio includes fiber Internet, Voice, Video, TV and cloud Infrastructure solutions as well as network and managed services. For more information visit enterprise.spectrum.com.</div>
<br>
<b>
Highlights:</b><br>
<div>As a Strategic Account Executive, you ensure client satisfaction by serving as a large client advocate and ensure satisfaction. You are passionate about creating positive clients experience with our voice, data, cloud, and video solutions and services. You are a consultative sales professional that can solve complex business problems with our simple networking solutions. You influence the right people both inside and outside of Spectrum Enterprise to provide exceptional service and create long-term relationships with large enterprise accounts.<br>
<br>
You have a developed ability to identify roadblocks and overcome obstacles to increase business while enhancing the client experience. Your primary focus is outlining cost-effective combinations of fiber-based services, point-to-point Ethernet connections, Internet access, WiFi, and managed services that deliver on short-term goals while creating sustainable value for clients and demand for the future. You flourish in an office environment and traveling frequently to capture accounts across an assigned footprint. You report directly to Sales Management for goals, guidance, and assistance.</div>

What you will do:</b><ul>
<li>
Be an impactful member of the sales team through the achievement of monthly revenue goals.</li>
<li>
Cultivate and maintain key business-to-business (B2B) relationships with large enterprise accounts within your assigned territory or market segment.
</li>
<li>Grow account base by consulting with new organizations to empower them with simple client-centric technology solutions and deliver sales proposals on product features and benefits.</li>
<li>
Drive the sales process by identifying target markets, industries, and contacts for the product portfolio.</li>
<li>
Generate leads by building and maintaining relationships in the corporate and IT community.</li>
<li>
Entice clients by creating informative and lucrative proposals that align with their business needs.</li>
<li>
Build an extensive network through referrals, prospecting phone calls, industry events and peers.</li>
<li>
Ensure the service implementation process by supervising all phases of the sales cycle.</li>
<li>
Effectively close deals through conversations and negotiations with C-level executives.</li>
<li>
Influence senior leadership decisions by providing weekly reports on sales funnel, sales call activity and 30/60/90-day forecasts.</li>
<li>
Perform additional duties related to the position as assigned.</li>
</ul>
