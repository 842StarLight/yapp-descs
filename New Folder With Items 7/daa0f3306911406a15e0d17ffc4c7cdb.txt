<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">LRW is a global force in the full-service custom market research field, recently ranked as one of the Top 25 Global Research Firms by Marketing News. Partnering with Fortune 500 companies, we are able to provide insight and have impact on real world business problems. We specialize in strategic development, new product development and testing, advertising, customer satisfaction, and tracking research. LRW conducts its research using a variety of methods including telephone, mail, and Internet surveys, mystery shopping, and store audits for clients in such industries as retail, health care, entertainment, financial services, and automotive. Founded by Arnold Fishman in 1973, LRW has operations in Boston, Chicago, Austin, Portland, Philadelphia, Los Angeles, New York City, and London.
<br><br>
As an Account Manager, you will bring your leadership, research expertise, business intelligence and analytic thinking to successfully help clients solve complex business problems through providing high quality customized marketing research. Applying your business expertise, you will identify and pursue business opportunities with clients and manage a set of client relationships to build your business and meet financial goals. Using your industry and research knowledge, you will develop proposals, design studies, and manage the execution of multiple projects to effectively provide business insights through high-quality client deliverables that support our unique ability to use market research to have business impact. Leveraging your initiative to solve problems and ability to mentor people, you will provide senior-level guidance to project teams through analytic support, report writing review and client delivery on various research quantitative and qualitative methodologies. Your ability and initiative to meet deadlines, extract meaningful insights and recommendations, communicate with clients and manage their expectations will be met with growth opportunities.
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Generate business by partnering with business development executive to lead and grow client relationships.</li>
<li>Continually cultivate and expand existing relationships while identifying new business opportunities.</li>
<li>Develop client rapport and manage expectations through communications that develop trust and build strong relationships.</li>
<li>Manage costs and quality of all projects sold.</li>
<li>Develop proposals, design marketing research studies, and direct the execution of those studies.</li>
<li>Support a team as they execute on your projects, ensuring they produce thorough, high-quality deliverables while achieving excellent financial results.</li>
<li>Guide analysis for multiple projects; be available to serve as a team resource for decision making and direction.</li>
<li>Apply solid business understanding to deliver actionable, impactful findings to clients.</li>
<li>Travel to meet clients as needed, for proposal and report delivery.</li>
<li>Be a vocal team leader, formally and informally.</li>
<li>Support or lead company initiatives.</li>
</ul>
REQUIREMENTS
<br><br>
<b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to make decisions, exercise judgment, and interface effectively with all levels of staff.</li>
<li>Strategic thinker with strong business acumen.</li>
<li>Proficient in building and maintaining client relationships.</li>
<li>Preferred expertise in one or more business verticals or study methodologies.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to meet or exceed sales goals.</li>
<li>Willingness to travel, domestic and abroad, as necessary to meet goals</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Proficient analyst and report writer.</li>
<li>Experience utilizing a structured sales methodology preferred (such as Target Account Selling, Strategic Selling, or Solution Selling).</li>
<li>Able to guide project managers, multiple projects and teams simultaneously to deliver quality deliverables with strong financial performance.</li>
<li>Goal-oriented, entrepreneurial, team player.</li>
</ul>
<b>Education and/or Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A./B.S. in related field such as Business, Economics, or Social Sciences. M.A. or MBA preferred.</li>
<li>At least 5 years of Marketing or Marketing Research experience within a specialized industry or Research Supplier, plus 2+ years experience in sales or business development with a marketing research, advertising research, or consulting services organization.</li>
</ul>
