<h2>Senior Operations Leader: Health & Beauty</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>About the Company<br>The Fountain Spa, voted best spa in Bergen County for 12 consecutive years, is an upscale day spa servicing two locations in northern New Jersey. The Fountain Spa offers a variety of services including facials, massages and body treatments, along with a full menu of salon services.</p>
<p>Professional Objective:<br>Primary responsibilities are to provide leadership, direction, and supervision of the staff in the daily operations of the Spa. The Senior Operations Leader oversees the delivery of memorable guest experiences. This person ensures the spa produces targeted sales and profit while building a loyal base of satisfied customers and employees.</p>
<ul>
<li>Facilitates smooth workflow by ensuring that employees/team members have what they need to operate at peak efficiency. Troubleshoots and constantly improves systems and processes.</li>
<li>Provides an inspiring example of spa citizenship, responsibility, mutual respect and resourcefulness. Demonstrates and protects the spa’s core values.</li>
<li>Ensures that spa clients are satisfied with services and products. Masterfully resolves customer complaints. Constantly builds and enhances the goodwill of the company.</li>
<li>Facilitates and models continuous improvement. Constantly searches for ways to improve quality of customer and employee experience.</li>
<li>Oversees employee recruitment, selection, development, training and retention. Makes employee development and retention a top priority.</li>
<li>Oversees the review and appraisal of employee performance at regular intervals, maintains detailed records on personnel issues, implements corrective plans when necessary, and participates in termination proceedings as needed.</li>
<li>Helps create and is primarily responsible for carrying out the financial plan of the spa, including its sales and operating budget. Meets weekly with the spa’s accountant and owner to review financial performance.</li>
<li>Oversees the condition of the spa, its facilities and equipment. Identifies potential safety hazards.</li>
<li>Interfaces with spa owners and/or upper level management to set standards for service quality, customer satisfaction, financial performance, personnel issues, and the condition of spa facilities.</li>
<li>Oversees the administration of the spa’s systems, including telecommunications and computer systems. Masters the spa’s management software and uses it to analyze and improve performance.</li>
<li>Participates in the application of the spa budget and is responsible for carrying out the financial plan of the company.</li>
<li>Manages communication in the spa. Mediates problems, creates and presents training programs, effectively communicates and enforces company rules and regulations, and maintains satisfactory performance standards among all levels of supervised employees.</li>
<li>Oversees staff work schedules. Oversees or provides emergency coverage. Is on call seven days a week for emergencies unless other emergency management arrangements have been made.</li>
<li>Schedules, plans, and facilitates effective and worthwhile staff meetings. Conducts weekly operations meetings with representatives/team leaders from each department.</li>
<li>Assists in continued development, updating and refinement of employee manual and operations protocols.</li>
<li>Inspects and measures all systems and processes constantly to ensure that there is compliance. Holds team leaders accountable for their teams’ compliance. Enforces consequences if standards are not met.</li>
</ul>
<p>Education and Experience:<br>Minimum of five years top management experience in high quality, luxury-oriented resort spa, destination spa facility or hospitality establishment. Strong administrative and management skills, including development of operating budgets.</p>
<p>Language &amp; Computer Skills:<br>Excellent service skills including etiquette and ability to work with the public. Knowledge of communications skills, organization skills, ability to deal with diverse clientele. Efficient computer skills (word processing, excel, google docs) is required.</p>
<p>Reasoning Ability:<br>Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of variables in situations where only limited standardization may exist. Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram or schedule form.</p>
<p>Employee Relations:<br>Interact with all levels of Spa Team and effective communication with Sales and Marketing Team. Motivate and train Boutique Team. Experience managing a staff of 50+ employees.</p>
<p>Materials &amp; Products:<br>Control of all Boutique Merchandise, responsible for security, safety and physical upkeep of Spa Boutique. Knowledge of inventory control, buying and merchandising, computer, knowledge of Spa products desirable.</p>
