<br>Essential Duties include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
<br><br>
Satisfies the transportation needs of vehicle purchasers.
<br>Approaches, greets, and offers assistance or direction to any customer who enters the dealership showroom or sales lot.
<br>Assists customers in selecting a vehicle by asking questions and listening carefully to their responses.
<br>Explains fully product performance, application, and benefits to prospects.
<br>Describes all optional equipment available for customer purchase.
<br>Offers test drives to all prospects. Follows dealership procedure to obtain proper identification from customer prior to test drive.
<br>Utilizes dealership sales control and follow-up system.
<br>Exhibits high level of commitment to customer satisfaction.
<br>Knows and understands the federal, state, and local laws which govern retail automobile sales.
<br>Establishes personal income goals that are consistent with dealership standards of productivity, and devises a strategy to meet those goals.
<br>Attends product and sales training courses as requested by sales manager.
<br>Keeps abreast of new products, features, accessories, etc., and their benefits to customers.
<br>Knows and understands equity and values, and is able to explain depreciation to the customer.
<br>Ensures that the sales manager has an opportunity to meet each customer.
<br>Turns 100 percent of closed deals to finance and insurance manager, along with properly completed paperwork (insurance information, trade title, etc.).
<br>Writes complete sales orders and processes paperwork in accordance with established dealership policies.
<br>Prepares sold vehicles for customer delivery prior to customer arrival.
<br>Delivers vehicles to customers, ensuring that the customer understands the vehicle's operating features, warranty, and paperwork.
<br>Introduces customers to service department personnel to emphasize to them the quality and efficiency of service repairs and maintenance available in the dealership's service department.
<br>Schedules first service appointment.
<br>Follows up on all post delivery items, tag/title work, “we-owes”, and special requests to be sure that all customer expectations are met.
<br>Maintains an owner follow-up system that encourages repeat and referral business and contributes to customer satisfaction.
<br>Maintains a prospect development system.
<br>Reviews and analyzes actions at the end of each day, week, month, and year to determine how to better utilize time and plans more effectively.
<br>Attends sales meetings.
<br>Maintains professional appearance.
