<b>Job Description:</b><br>

<br><br>
We are currently recruiting for a Marketing Data Analyst located in our Support Center (Global Headquarters) located in Irving, TX.
<br><br>
What will you be doing?
<br><br>
The Marketing Data Analyst will analyze marketing, customer, and sales data to measure and monitor performance for Michaels various channels. This Team Member will proactively mine data and produce analytical output. This individual will work with large scale and complex traditional and non-traditional data sources to identify opportunities that enhance performance and efficiency of marketing initiatives.
<br><br>
How will you do it?
<br><br>
Work with large scale and complex traditional and non-traditional data sources to identify opportunities that enhance performance and efficiency of marketing initiatives
<br>Analyze marketing, customer, and sales data to measure and monitor performance by channel
<br>Analyze website performance and customer online engagement using data analytics and Adobe Analytics
<br>Provide SQL/R programming, under general direction, in the execution of data analysis that will contribute to project deliverables
<br>Update and modify reporting and dashboards that support business partners across the company
<br>QA/QC data and report output to ensure accuracy
<br>Appropriately account for the timeliness and quality of all assignments
<br>Manage multiple projects concurrently
<br>Generate ad hoc analysis to drive short term strategies
<br>Other duties as assigned
<br><br>
<b>Job Requirements:</b><br>
What Skills you will need to do your job?
<br><br>
Bachelor’s degree in relevant quantitative discipline (statistics, economics, mathematics, computer science, or a related scientific discipline)
<br><br>
<b>Fluent in data fundamentals:</b> SQL, data manipulation using a procedural language (R, Python), statistics, experimentation, and modeling
<br><br>
Advanced Excel with higher level math and logic skills
<br><br>
Well versed in data mining (databases, querying languages, SQL)
<br><br>
Demonstrated skill communicating complex analysis to non-experts
<br><br>
Strong analytical thinker in an unstructured/ whitespace environment
<br><br>
Ability to work in a fast-paced team environment, multi-task, and prioritize accordingly to deliver on multiple projects while maintaining a service-oriented, customer–centric focus
<br><br>
Strong written and verbal communication skills
<br>High level of accuracy and attention to detail required</div>
