<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>FAE/Mentoring Engineer</b></h5>
<p>Imagine what it is like to deliver products that dramatically improve your customer's success in building their systems. Now imagine working with, not for, a supportive management team that will always put your customer's success first.</p>
<p>
Think how fun it is to work with customers who have enjoyed this level of commitment from their communications software vendor.</p>
<p>
Objective Interface Systems, Inc., is seeking a Field Application/Mentoring Engineer to be based at our headquarters in Northern Virginia. Objective Interface is a growing, profitable company with an aggressive plan for self-funded growth. We sell the leading embedded and real-time communications framework. We are the leader in our industry due to the dedication and commitment of our employees. Our organizational culture is passionate about our products, customers, and employees. We really love our work and the way our work helps others.</p>
<p>
Our signature product, ORB<i>express</i>, leads the pack in top-speed communications software and is the proven fastest and smallest ORB on the market today. We provide embedded and real-time connectivity software development tools to worldwide telecommunications, data communications, transportation, industrial automation, consumer electronics, and military and aerospace markets.</p>
<p>
You will never be bored. The applicant accepting this position will support account representatives on sales calls, create and deliver presentations at customer sites, conferences and seminars, teach training classes, provide consulting/mentoring services, write articles for trade magazines and work with customers in resolving questions and issues they might have.</p>
<p>
Engineers or sales engineers who have significant experience supporting the sales process for real-time operating systems and/or software development tools are strongly encouraged to apply.</p>
<p>
In-depth experience working with real-time operating systems and/or software development tools to embedded system engineers is essential. This position requires strong presentation skills, including presenting at conferences and customer sites. We need self-starters who can take the initiative on their own.</p>
<p>
The successful candidate will be located in, or willing to relocate to the Washington D.C./Northern Virginia metro region.</p>
<p>
TRAVEL REQUIREMENT: Position requires moderate amounts of travel, including some international travel.</p>
<p>
CITIZENSHIP: Candidates must be a U.S. citizen.</p>
<p>
We have an excellent benefits program, including medical, dental, 401(k), life insurance, flexible spending accounts, disability plans, vacation time and other benefits.</p>
