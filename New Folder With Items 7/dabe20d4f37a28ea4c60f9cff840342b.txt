<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2>

<div>Sotheby's International Realty, Inc. is seeking a full-time Marketing Coordinator for our Carmel brokerage office. The Marketing Coordinator is responsible for managing the marketing and advertising operations for the Carmel market, while maintaining a reliable, personable, team-oriented and professional disposition. As such the Marketing Coordinator must understand and promote the value and applicability of the brand/company marketing options both generally and in response to specific needs.<br>
<br>
Description:<ul>
<li>
Organize, schedule and produce weekly and monthly marketing calendar for Marketing Manager</li>
<li>
Assist Marketing Manager in digital and social media strategy</li>
<li>
Manage multiple social media accounts ie. Instagram, Facebook, Twitter ect.</li>
<li>
Measure and analyze performance of email, web activity and digital marketing programs with the help of Marketing Manager and Interactive Manager
</li>
<li>Analyze and report marketing campaign performance to Marketing Manager</li>
<li>
Production of quarterly Market Updates</li>
<li>
Assemble images and copy for all listing (or sale) marketing opportunities (including local and global print ads, and on-line ads, custom publications, and advertorials) according to style specifications and quality control guidelines. Track all advertising by property.</li>
<li>
Producing weekly stats for agents and to maintain familiarity with local market conditions and competitive environment and suggest improvements and new ideas in response to them.</li>
<li>
Assist in management of Marketing Assistant and agents to develop marketing materials as needed</li>
<li>
Writing copy for email newsletter</li>
<li>
Willing to learn and develop presentation stills either with agents, clients or colleagues</li>
<li>
Coordinate reservations for bi-yearly magazine</li>
<li>
Liaison with copywriters and photographers</li>
<li>
Record PR Placements and other marketing materials</li>
<li>
Upload, organize and activate listing videos</li>
<li>
Heavy agent support with general office duties.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Qualifications<ul>
<li>
Four year degree, preferably in marketing or advertising, and/or equivalent years of experience</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
An eye for detail/proofing, design, and understanding of branding</li>
<li>
Must have Advanced working knowledge of MS Office (mainly Excel); and proficient in Adobe Creative Suite</li>
<li>
Must have solid experience in Digital &amp; Social Media Marketing</li>
<li>
Very strong administrative skills including accurate data entry</li>
<li>
Customer service orientation – strong training and support skills</li>
</ul>

<p><b>About us</b></p>
<div>Realogy is a global provider of real estate services with a singular mission: We serve agents. This strategy is aimed at growing the base of high-performing independent sales agents at our company-owned and franchisee brokerages, providing services to make them more productive and their businesses more profitable.<br>
<br>
While you won’t see our name on For Sale signs, you will see those of our industry-leading brands: Better Homes and Gardens Real Estate, Century 21, Citi Habitats, Climb Real Estate, Coldwell Banker Real Estate, Corcoran, ERA Franchise Systems and Sotheby’s International Realty to name just a few. Our four business units work together to provide a full-service solution to meet the needs of agents and their homebuying clients, from the initial listing until the keys are turned over.<br>
<br>
Realogy was recently designated a Great Place to Work® for the second consecutive year, recognizing the company’s commitment to providing meaningful work and an environment where employees can grow and succeed. Realogy has 16,600 offices and approximately 302,000 affiliated brokers and agents worldwide. We’re the leader in residential real estate sales, with $6.1 billion in revenue in 2018, nearly 1.4 million real estate transactions and approximately 16 percent of market share of the U.S. residential real estate market. We’re there to serve agents who help people find and buy houses, move, and finance their dream.<br>
<br>
Sotheby's International Realty was founded in 1976 as a real estate service for discerning clients of Sotheby's auction house. Today, the company's global footprint spans nearly 1,000 offices located in 72 countries and territories worldwide, including 43 company-owned brokerage offices in key metropolitan and resort markets. In February 2004, Realogy entered into a long-term strategic alliance with Sotheby’s, the operator of the auction house. The agreement provided for the licensing of the Sotheby’s International Realty name and the development of a franchise system. The franchise system is comprised of an affiliate network, where each office is independently owned and operated. Sotheby’s International Realty supports its affiliates and agents with a host of operational, marketing, recruiting, educational and business development resources. Affiliates and agents also benefit from an association with the venerable Sotheby’s auction house, established in 1744. For more information, visit www.sothebysrealty.com.<br>
