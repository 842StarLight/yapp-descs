<p><b>
Job Summary</b>
</p>
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>
<div>Atonix Digital develops and offers a suite of analytics software powered by the ASSET360® cloud-based platform. We extract insights from digital data, by connecting the power of math and data to drive business decisions. Our solutions create a multidimensional view of systems, infrastructure assets and processes to boost efficiency, increase accuracy of planning and improve day-to-day decision-making.<br>
<br>
As a Product Manager for ASSET360, you will work with the customers, partners and prospects to understand how enhancing ASSET360 capabilities can help solve their evolving business and asset management requirements. You should also be familiar with SaaS offerings and how emerging Data Analytics, Machine Learning and AI technologies could be used to enhance Atonix Digital offerings. You will contribute to the development of sales and marketing enablement tools that allow our customers and partners to make the most out of ASSET360 platform. You will work with Industry and other Product Managers at Atonix Digital to optimize the capabilities of the overall ASSET360 platform to maximize end user value.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate will be passionate about helping our customers be wildly successful and the Atonix Digital team deliver game changing capabilities. You will be thoughtful, thorough and inspire others with your insight while encouraging others participate. You will be technical enough to have spirited debates with our seasoned software team and creative enough to help craft compelling positioning statements to support marketing.</div>
</div>
<br>
</div></div></div></div>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b>
</p>
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>
<div>The Product Manager will be responsible for product strategy, pricing, research, and requirements prioritization. The Product Manager will interact with Industry Managers for understanding of industry-specific requirements and priorities, with the platform team for delivery and clarification of requirements and use cases, with the marketing and sales team for product messaging, and with customers, partners, and competitors for detailed understanding of the market. This position will be a direct report to the Solutions Director.<br>
As a Product Manager you will:<br>
<li>Meet remotely or in person with customers and partners to understand current product fit and identify opportunities for new products or enhancements.</li>
<br>
<li>Perform competitive analysis.</li>
<br>
<li>Refine, prioritize and develop business cases for product requirements as input to road-mapping and resource allocation.</li>
<br>
<li>Write requirements as input to engineering.</li>
<br>
<li>Work with developers, user experience designers to solve problems the best way possible.</li>
<br>
<li>Work with technical writers to document features and capabilities.</li>
<br>
<li>Work with marketing to produce web pages, data sheets, whitepapers, FAQs and other collateral that helps promote Atonix Digital products.</li>
<br>
<li>Attend conferences and user group meetings to talk about Atonix Digital and our portfolio of products.</li>
<br>
<li>Work with sales and pre-sales to respond to RFPs and RFIs.</li>
</div>
