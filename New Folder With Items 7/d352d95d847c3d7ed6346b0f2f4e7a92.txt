<h2>Case Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<ul><li><b>Please note that this position is located on-site and cannot be performed remotely</b></li></ul>
<p><b>
Position Overview:</b></p>
<p>
The Field Case Designer provides illustrations, advance sales concepts, and marketing support to the producers in order to increase life insurance and annuity sales.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assists producers with the design of their cases, which includes illustration preparation, use of advance sales concepts, and insuring that all appropriate marketing materials are available for the sale.</li>
<li>Implements marketing program lead generation programs, and the development and organization of seminars and informational meetings.</li>
<li>Participates in joint work opportunities where needed</li>
<li>Monitors progress and measures advanced sales results</li>
<li>Insures office has the most current versions of all marketing materials.</li>
<li>Complies with all company and site policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Remains current in profession and industry trends.</li>
<li>Successfully completes regulatory and job training requirements.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite, Outlook, Internet and software applications, including illustrations, advanced sales software and proposals</li>
<li>Strong communication skills (written &amp; oral); and presentation skills</li>
<li>Excellent planning and organizational skills and the ability to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Ability to make a positive contribution as demonstrated by learning new skills and making suggestions for process/procedure improvement.</li>
<li>Ability to work with others in a collaborative team environment.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<br>
<br>
Required Experience<br>
<p><b>Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3 or more years of experience in life insurance and other related advanced sales concepts</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Education/Licenses/Professional Designations:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>College degree required</li>
<br>
</ul>
