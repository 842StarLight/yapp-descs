<h2>Trakstar Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Trakstar is looking for energetic, tech-savvy, and creative B2B Sales Reps. to enable our customers to be wildly successful. You’ll be joining our exceptional sales development team to drive huge impact in a fun and exciting company with significant growth potential.</p>
<p>
As an Account Executive, you’ll be instrumental in assessing prospect needs, using web conferencing software to demonstrate the tool, and maintaining contact to close the deal.</p>
<p>
We have a fast-growing customer base of our Enterprise SaaS product, especially in Government, Technology, Healthcare, and Professional Services sectors.</p>

<p><b>
What you’ll do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Present and sell products and services to potential clients</li>
<li>Prepare presentations, proposals and sales contracts.</li>
<li>Adjusts content of sales presentations by studying the type of sales outlet or trade factor.</li>
<li>Follow up on with leads and referrals resulting from incoming activity and field activity.</li>
<li>Maintain sales materials and current product knowledge.</li>
<li>Identify sales prospects and contact these and other accounts as assigned.</li>
<li>Understand the present the benefits of the system to potential customers.</li>

<li>Creatively develop and implement special sales activities to meet targets.</li>
<li>Manage accounts through quality checks and other follow-up.</li>
<li>Identify and resolve client concerns.</li>
<li>Communicate new product and service opportunities, special developments, information, or feedback gathered through field activity to appropriate company staff.</li>

<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
