<h2>Design Engineer, Hargray Communications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p>Hargray’ s Mission – To Envision and deliver customer delight</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>At Hargray, we are looking for people to join our OSP Design Engineering team who are extremely detail-oriented and extraordinarily passionate. We thrive on designing simple and scalable solutions to complex problems and delivering leading-edge technology to our customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>We are looking for exceptionally ambitious and communicative hands-on people who are comfortable working with ever-changing processes and procedures as part of a cross-functional team, have experience working in fast-paced environments, and who have the passion, skills, and dedication to take our network to the next level.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Responsible for the design and cost estimates pertaining to new-build and over-build of Hargray’s OSP facilities.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops overall OSP system design for new developments and over-builds for copper, HFC and fiber facilities.</li>
<li>Prepares conduit drawings for power companies and developers.</li>
<li>Prepares and provides splicing diagrams to construction engineers for copper, HFC and fiber platforms.</li>
<li>Develops HFC system design parameters.</li>
<li>Determines fiber loss budgets, quantities, and routes for Metro-Ethernet, DSLAM(s), camera sites, T-1, HFC nodes, transport and customer pt-to-pt systems.</li>
<li>Evaluates new OSP products and designs for possible implementation.</li>
<li>Assists in the preparation of the annual engineering budget.</li>
<li>Initiates OSP work orders for construction.</li>
<li>Uses CAD/AM/FM design software to produce copper, HFC and fiber designs and bill of materials.</li>
<li>Forecasts service demands to develop short, medium and long-range plans.</li>
<li>Works with network engineers to incorporate network elements.</li>
<li>Prepares and maintains detailed assignment documents on fiber projects and communicates these with facilities engineering.</li>
<li>Prepares detailed drawings, cost estimates and material requirements for the planned installation of OSP facilities.</li>
<li>Works with facilities to prepare methods and procedures for cutover on new or rearranged plant facilities.</li>
<li>Provides current new development land base and notifies mapping technicians when all changes are complete.</li>
<li>Provides as-built HFC drawings to mapping technicians.</li>
<li>Work with mapping technicians to provide/create/update special maps for OSP, sales, and management.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned to ensure the effective operation of the department.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b><i>Job Requirements</i></b></h2>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>Prior experience as OSP Design Engineer or 5+ years OSP experience in cable construction and maintenance.</li>
<li>Must demonstrate an understanding of communications distribution networks on fiber, copper, and HFC platforms.</li>
<li>Solid knowledge in OSP construction methods and practices as set by RUS, Telecordia, and BICSI.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in basic electrical theory.</li>
<li>Experience in Microstation CADD software desired.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office software including Visio, Excel, and Word.</li>
</ul>
