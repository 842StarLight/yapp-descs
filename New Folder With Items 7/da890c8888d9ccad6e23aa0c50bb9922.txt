<div>
<p>Are you curious, motivated, and forward-thinking? At FIS you’ll have the opportunity to work on some of the most challenging and relevant issues in financial services and technology. Our talented people empower us, and we believe in being part of a team that is open, collaborative, entrepreneurial,
</p>

<p><b>About the team
</b></p>
<p>This role is in our Retail Digital Payments Division. We have several products and eBanking applications that this team works with.
</p>
<p><b>What you will be doing<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>You will be planning, writing, and managing a variety of internal and external corporate communications. You will also need to draft and delivered product enhancement communications to our clients.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Marketing intensive role with client presentation
</li>
<li>You will manage the marketing relationship for our clients who are preparing to launch a new person to person payment application.
</li>
<li>You will need to develop storyboards and scripts for product videos
</li>
<li>You will be evaluating and selecting vendors for research projects including surveys and usability studies.
</li>
<li>You will be contributing to the research, strategy, and launch of user-experience improvements for applications used by financial institutions.
</li>
<li>You will be able to design strategies based on user and client goals.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you bring:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science, information system or marketing background or the equivalent of education, training, or work experience. Master’s degree desirable.
</li>
<li>Proficiency in solutions design and requirements definition disciplines leveraging model driven design-based tools and techniques including conceptual solution component models, business process models, use cases, etc.
</li>
<li>Develops material for product launches such as press releases, launch materials, customer presentations, videos and sales training materials
</li>
<li>Creates strategic product line roadmaps to drive acceptance of the product and highlight differentiating features
</li>
<li>Manages development, implementation and delivery of the company’s product education programs
</li>
<li>Develops and maintains relationships with internal and external clients.
</li>
<li>Excellent analytical, decision-making, problem-solving, team, and time management skills
</li>
<li>Designs and develops product profiles, competitive analysis, presentations, demos, white papers and product updates for organizational marketing materials.<br>
</li>
</ul>
