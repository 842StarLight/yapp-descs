<h2>Personal Teller Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Assists Personal Teller Manager with overall daily management of Personal Teller Center operations and agents to ensure service quality and efficiency, referral and cross-sell opportunities, account and loan growth, and deepen member loyalty.</li>
<li>Coaches and trains current and new agents to ensure operation accuracy and achievement of service standards.</li>
<li>In real time, virtually processes member transactions including, but not limited to, deposits, payments, transfers, and withdrawals from a remote location by means of the Personal Teller Machine (PTM) system.
</li>
<li>Develops and maintains a thorough understanding of the hardware, software/applications, and processes that comprise Personal Teller Center functions and services, including but not limited to: our core transactional system, Teller Enterprise, PTMs, balancing and accounting processes, reporting, imaging and CRM applications, etc.</li>
<li>When appropriate, takes advantage of cross-sell and referral opportunities, and provides members with solutions to financial questions/issues.</li>
<li>Meets or exceeds individual service levels, productivity, and quality standards.
</li>
<li>Performs other individual and/or team tasks as assigned by the Personal Teller Manager, Director of Remote Experience and/or Chief Member Experience Officer.</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Excellent math/analytical ability.</li>
<li>Sales ability and cross-selling skills.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office, ability to use 10-key, and be willing to learn other programs and applications as needed.</li>
<li>Can comfortably discuss member transactions, and credit union products and services via the remote PTM platform. The position requires being comfortable, professional, and friendly on camera with members.</li>
<li>Be a positive team player who appreciates working with a unified team, values appropriate feedback, input, and guidance from others, and shares ideas.</li>
<li>Have effective communication and listening skills, possess above-average written and verbal communication skills (including correct spelling and proper grammar usage), and comfortably deal with members and employees at all levels.</li>
<li>Be sensitive to executive protocol, able to recognize and deal with different personalities in a positive manner, and have a high degree of poise and tact as a visible representative of the support departments, and ultimately, the credit union.
</li>
<li>Able to motivate staff and keep things positive and team-oriented, while having the capacity to effectively organize and prioritize work.
</li>
<li>Integrity and high moral responsibility.</li>
<li>Professional appearance and demeanor.</li>
<li>Analytical ability, with the incisiveness to ask discerning questions.</li>
<li>Possess the ability to evaluate each situation as its own and think outside the box for solutions.</li>
<li>Willing to put in the time necessary to get the job done. Be flexible and have a strong ability to adapt to changing situations.</li>
<li>Possess a high school diploma or equivalent.
</li>
<li>One or more years of job-related experience in a financial institution (Credit Union or Bank), with at least one year of supervisory experience is preferred.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li><b>This position starts at $17.50/hr, based on experience. The shift will be switching to a 10:30am-7:30pm, once we extend hours in the near future.</b></li></ul>
<p><b>**This position will be moving to Weldon Springs in Summer 2020</b></p>
