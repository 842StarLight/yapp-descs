Our TeamAnalytical Ecosystem Product Management is responsible for charting and executing product and capabilities directions to achieve end user satisfaction, competitive distinction and ultimately to generate business benefits for Teradata. Overall, Teradata is recognized by media and industry analysts as a future-focused company for its technological excellence, sustainability, ethics, and business value.Product/Capability OverviewToday our technologies offer industry-leading functions enabling business to enjoy an “always on” pattern for key analytical systems. However, what’s next for the industry? We believe that Teradata is poised to define the ultimate “always on” end state for the data answering industry at large and are looking for talent to help define that state, logically plan and execute with the team to achieve the goal.Your OpportunityAs a Product Owner (PO), in the Analytical Ecosystem, you will be responsible for defining, planning and executing Teradata’s solutions for an always on infrastructure. You should be eager to work with our customers, with an engineering mindset, to provide Sales and Marketing the products they need to meet Teradata’s goals. We need you, as the candidate, to understand complex technical market needs and then translate these needs for consumption by a variety of teams using an Agile framework.This PO position is for a well-rounded candidate with relevant experience in technical software product ownership/management (or equivalent experience), agile development, accompanied by deployment and business proficiencies including competitive analysis, pricing, and licensing.Key responsibilities Basic qualification: Preferred Qualifications:
