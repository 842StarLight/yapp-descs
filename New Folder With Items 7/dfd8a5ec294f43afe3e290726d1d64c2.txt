<p>Revenue Growth Manager</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>CARE FOR SKIN – CARE FOR PEOPLE:</p>
<p>For more than 130 years, care for consumers and our people is what has driven us. Every day, our global brand icons, such as NIVEA, Eucerin, and Aquaphor, make more than 500 million consumers in over 200 countries feel good in their skin. Join this world of care and start your career in an environment that offers you the freedom to drive our international business with your entrepreneurial spirit. Shape your professional future from day one and explore our collaborative culture with smart, courageous teams that all share one exciting goal: developing skin care products of the next generation.
</p>
<p>WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT FROM BEIERSDORF NORTH AMERICA:</p>
<p>You will join an entrepreneurial, innovative, and fast paced culture where every employee’s contributions make a big impact. Our teams act with Care, Simplicity, Courage, and Trust to continuously improve as leaders and deliver superior skin care products.</p>
<p>Are You Up For The Challenge?</p>
<p>Revenue Growth Management continues to be a key lever in driving sustainable and efficient business growth in today’s ever changing CPG environment. Understanding Revenue Growth and how to most efficiently drive Net Sales, Profit, Market Share and Volume are important factors in delivering our overall business objectives. We are actively looking for a manager with strong analytical skills, business/commercial acumen and prior CPG experience to join our team. The role will aim to deliver market share, net sales and profit objectives through a key focus on Pack and Price architecture, promotional evaluation, and brand/channel strategy. This highly visible position will work collaboratively across sales, marketing, RGM and finance teams.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul><li> Develop and simplify key department processes including promotional tracking and evaluation</li></ul>
<ul><li> Steer the promotional evaluation process through pre and post evaluation and leverage knowledge to drive improvements</li></ul>
<ul><li> Define and share clear channel (and regional) strategies for the organization and lead the identification of opportunities for pricing, product and promotional improvement based on these learnings</li></ul>
<ul><li> Consolidate, analyze and track key sales KPIs: sales, innovation performance, pricing and promotional intensity. Provide insight and recommend/ steer corrective action</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead the creation and implementation of promotional guidelines by category and channel based on post evaluation of previous activities and elasticity studies. Monitor/ report on adherence; identify outlier activity and work with sales/ senior leadership to correct</li></ul>
<ul><li> In conjunction with the Team Lead, steer the annual customer business planning process and quarterly general business meetings</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead the creation, implementation and tracking of the MAPPS process (Merchandising, Assortment, Price, Promotion and Shelf) by channel. Actively complete the price and promotion component by channel for assigned categories</li></ul>
<ul><li> Participate in and provide customer and category insights into the S&amp;OP process.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure key stakeholders are informed, trained and executing against key department outputs</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work closely with Marketing and Finance to set up detailed pricing and product portfolio strategies by category/channel</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<ul>
<li>BS/BA in relevant field, Marketing/Business Administration preferred</li>
<li>5+ years’ prior experience developing strategic, omni-channel customer and promotional strategies for CPG (client or agency side, health and beauty experience preferred)</li>
<li>Ability to think strategically and to translate internal brand strategy into retailer strategy.</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills to influence and secure cross-functional support</li>
<li>Must have excellent analytical skills and business acumen to build strong business cases for change.</li>
<li>Must be technology savvy and have strong office productivity software skills (especially with PowerPoint and Excel)</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills, innovative attitude, flexible, proactive, service-minded and a strong team player</li>
<li>Self-motivated, able to multi-task and meet tight deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to think strategically and increase effectiveness at achieving overall strategies in a highly collaborative environment.</li>
<li>Ability to develop strong relationships across many functions and external partners.</li>
</ul>
