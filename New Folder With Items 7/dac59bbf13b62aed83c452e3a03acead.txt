<h2>Business Unit Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<div>
<b>GENERAL SUMMARY:</b> Responsible for leading cross-functional team in all areas of production, continuous improvement, and customer satisfaction. Will participate and be a key Customer and Supplier contact for problem resolution. Will promote positive climate throughout the manufacturing facility.</div>
<div><b>DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Directs activities through subordinate Team Leader(s) to attain production goals consistent with safety, quality, delivery, housekeeping, waste elimination, and effectiveness objectives.</li>
<li>
Reviews and establishes material, equipment, and manpower resource requirements.</li>
<li>
Accountable for consistent application of policy and procedures throughout the plant.</li>
<li>
Coordinates and leads activities with other functions such as materials, engineering, quality, human resources and other support departments.</li>
<li>
Assists in establishing budgets for conducting departmental activities and accompanying manufacturing projects; forecasts manpower equipment and supply needs; and is held responsible for managing department within established budgetary guidelines.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned</li>
<li>
Comply with and support the health, safety, and environmental programs, policies and procedures.<br>
</li>
</ul></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Requirements</b></h2>
<b>Education Requirements</b><br>
Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Business Administration, Operations Management or related field.<br>
<br>
<b>Experience and Skill Requirements</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 8 years of leadership experience in a high volume, fast paced automotive manufacturing environment or equivalent leadership experience.</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership experience in long-range planning, formulating business strategies and project management.</li>
<li>Demonstrated problem resolution skills i.e. 7D, 5P, 5-why etc.</li>
<li>Strong manufacturing and business knowledge.</li>
<li>Able to operate PC based software programs and automated business systems.</li>
<li>Must be proficient in data collection and analysis.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills both oral and written with strong presentation skills required.</li>
<li>Prior experience working on a cross-functional team preferred.</li>
<li>Team building and conflict management skills.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple priorities.</li>
<li>Previous lean manufacturing experience preferred.</li>
<li>Green belt/Black belt experience desired.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to effectively teach, lead, mentor and motivate personnel at all levels.</li>
</ul>
