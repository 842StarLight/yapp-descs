<h2>Director of Partner Development - US</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Tinyclues is seeking a Director of Partner Development, to manage our strategy and efforts to build a partner ecosystem, consisting of reseller and technology partners, cooperative sales partners, and agencies and system integrators. This position can be based in our Paris or NY office, and will be responsible for identifying and engaging key partner prospects, creating commercial and technical models and agreements, and working with our product, marketing, and sales teams to create a valuable partner network.</div>
</div></div></div>
<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div>
<p>By joining Tinyclues, you will become part of a fast-growth start-up with breakthrough technology &amp; global ambitions. 100+ companies are already using our solution, in the verticals of fashion, retail, and travel-hospitality, with amazing results and experiences. These solutions are delivered in concert with many technologies and SaaS applications, offering a robust market for partnerships.</p>
<div>As with many disruptive solutions, the challenges of selling Tinyclues are not so much the competition as finding new ways to engage the market with partners. Through your commitment to success and your intelligence in identifying the market's key ecosystem players, you will feel the exhilaration of offering a unique and revolutionary product, changing the way B2C marketers engage their customers and generate more revenue, without ever compromising their customer lifetime value.</div>
</div>
</div></div>
