<div>
<b>
Summary </b>The Commercial Account Executive is an account management position with AssuredPartners, one of the largest and fastest growing independent insurance agencies in the country. This role requires management of a book of <b>Small to Middle Market Commercial Lines</b> business. We are looking for a candidate that is interested in working with an upbeat, energic team of Account Executives and Producers that can add to our success with their experience and attitude. The Account Executive will provide a high level of support and customer service to clients. The position requires a significant degree of independent judgment and discretion in order to meet those service levels. Duties include assisting in marketing and customer service in coordination with the department.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
The Account Executive has superior product knowledge and is familiar with the specific <b>Commercial Insurance</b> products offered by the Agency's carriers. The Account Executive will assist in the renewal of existing accounts.</p>
<p>
The Account Executive is responsible for assisting clients with service needs and making changes to existing accounts while recommending proper insurance coverage. This position offers a very competitive salary with comprehensive benefits.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<p>Works with Producers to provide clients with full exposure analysis and reviews methods of handling risks. Meets directly with clients and leads accounts through the renewal process per department procedures.</p>
<p>
Maintains records according to Agency standards to ensure that procedures are properly followed, and that information is readily accessible.</p>
<p>
Assists in the development of strong personal relationships with appropriate insurance carrier personnel. Assists Agency in developing carrier relationships by becoming familiar with and executing strategies developed for satisfying carrier goals and objectives.</p>
