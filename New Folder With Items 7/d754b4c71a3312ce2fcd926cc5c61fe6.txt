<h2>Trim Design Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>BASIC FUNCTION:</b></p>
<p>
Provide administrative support for the development, production and launch of new trimming collections.
</p>
<p><b>PRINCIPAL RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>
Under the supervision of the Executive Design Director, the Trim Design Administrator will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Regularly proofread all materials related to new collections: sample books, memo sample cards press releases, seasonal look books, purchase orders, spec sheets, yardage requirement sheets, and others as requested.</li>
<li>Coordinate and maintain book production schedule for each collection, tracking all collections' launch timelines</li>
<li>Communicate by phone, email and texting on a regular basis with various internal departments (sample department, marketing, purchasing, etc.) and external vendors (mills, sample makers, etc.) to confirm timing and quantities of new collection goods being shipped</li>
<li>Promptly relay any possible delays or other issues to management
</li>
<li>Train on and become proficient on Dynamic</li>
<li>Take minutes and action steps at Product Development meetings as requested, and distribute to all in a timely manner</li>
<li>Compose and respond to correspondence with mills and sample makers</li>
<li>Create and maintain new item lists</li>
<li>Gather sales data from internal systems as required for Executive Design Director to conduct analysis</li>
<li>Conduct research on unique names for collections, patterns and colors, including the meaning behind such names to facilitate subsequent writing of press releases by Executive Design Director</li>
<li>Research and collect historical data of trimmings as requested</li>
<li>Some flexibility in work hours, but will be expected to participate in team meetings during certain hours and may need to respond to electronic communications from overseas vendors outside of regular business hours</li>
</ul>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<ul>
<li>Extensive computer software skills in Microsoft Office (particularly Word, Excel and PowerPoint 2010 or newer) and some experience with Project Management software</li>
<li>Strong project management skills and ability to multi task under tight deadlines</li>
<li>Demonstrated professional English verbal and written communications skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated proofreading experience with alphanumerical data</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, including ability to detect anomalies in data patterns</li>
<li>Demonstrated research skills in a professional context</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to meet deadlines in a professional context</li>
<li>Highly organized and self-disciplined</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and complete assigned work in a high-quality and timely manner</li>
<li>Professional demeanor when interacting with internal peers, managers or external entities (no prima donna / drama egos)</li>
<li>2+ years experience in an administrative support role</li>
<li>2+ years experience dealing with third party vendors/suppliers</li>
<li>1+ years of sales or related financial data compilation and analysis</li>

</ul>
