<h2>Operations Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>PRIMARY PURPOSE:<br>
The Operations Analyst provides support to the primary Bank Operation areas – Deposit Operations, Retail Support Services, Loan Servicing and Wealth Operations - with reporting, report and process automation, financial analysis and accounting, and project management. The incumbent must be resourceful and have a strong proficiency in financial and bank operation concepts, practices, and procedures and the concepts, practices, and procedures, for financial and operations reporting, as well as knowledge of associated Regulatory compliance. This position relies on extensive experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals and perform a variety of complicated tasks on an independent basis. An increased degree of creativity and latitude is required relative to the position’s responsibilities and functions. The Operations Analyst may lead, direct, and supervise the work of others.<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
1. Provides support to the primary Bank Operation areas – Deposit Operations, Retail Support Services, Loan Servicing and Wealth Operations - with reporting, report and process automation, financial analysis and accounting, and project management.<br>
2. Assists Operations Departments with budget preparation and monthly variance reporting.<br>
3. Assists Loan Servicing with various reporting accounting for charge-offs, reserves, delinquencies, and loan and REO sales.<br>
4. Assists Operations Departments with special projects, conversions and report and process automation.<br>
5. Prepares special reports for senior management and regulatory agencies as required.<br>
6. Coordinates the preparation of various Loan Servicing, Deposit Operations and Retail Support Services reports for Board and Management.<br>
7. Interacts with Accounting and IT on behalf of the Operations Group for certain financial reporting and activities.<br>
8. Prepares classified loan data for senior management and Board of Directors.<br>
9. Various accounting, reporting and analysis responsibilities for the Operations Group.<br>
JOB SPECIFICATIONS:<br>
Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills with strong supervisory, organizational, oral and written communications skills.</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of accounting principles, and an excellent knowledge of financial institution policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Superior analytical and technical finance skills required.</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office Suite and automated financial software programs required.</li>
<li>Ability to manage staff and vendor relationships.</li>
<li>Knowledge of financial and bank operation concepts, practices, and procedures for financial and operations reporting, as well as knowledge of associated Regulatory compliance.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Experience &amp; Education<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent with a degree or concentration in accounting/finance preferred.</li>
<li>Extensive bank accounting and operations experience</li>
</ul>
<br>
Internal and External Contacts<br>
<ul><li>Interacts and coordinates with various Bank departments including, but not limited to Senior Management, Operations, Audit, Compliance, Legal, IT, Accounting and Loan Origination.</li></ul>
<br>
Working Conditions/Physical Requirements<br>
Office environment. Ability to operate computer. Ability to communicate in order to exchange simple to complex information with individuals and groups. Ability to travel throughout Bank footprint.<br>
