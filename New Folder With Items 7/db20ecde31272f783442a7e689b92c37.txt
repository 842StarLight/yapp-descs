<p><b>About Us
</b></p>
<p>CAST Software is revolutionizing the way companies like SONY, Boeing, AT&amp;T and BMW develop state of the art, stable and secure software, improving and protecting businesses and brands worldwide. CIOs across the globe depend on Software Intelligence from CAST to measure the health of their applications, uncover major security risks, and drive digital transformation with confidence. Software engineers use CAST software blueprints to gain critical insight into complex software, avoid and remediate structural flaws, and design modern architecture for legacy applications. With over 400 customers worldwide and growing, our team is ready to expand.</p>
<p><b>
What you'll do</b></p>
<p>
This NY City based position offers tremendous growth for a Hunter, Builder and Doer. It is a very rewarding opportunity for a stellar sales executive, who masters insight selling and can make things happen without RFPs in a ‘Cold’ environment.</p>
<p>
Responsible for closing the next set of large and visible references in the North America Enterprise Markets (cross vertical) and help take the organization to the next level.<br>
Define and take charge of NA sales strategy for CAST product and service offerings for this Market and collaborate cross-departmentally to ensure superlative customer experience.</p>
<p><b>
What you'll have</b></p>
<p>
You are a very strong and sharp sales executive (individual contributor) who can help CAST increase its NA Enterprise revenue.</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge and experience selling complex technical solutions into IT organizations across multiple verticals such as: High Tech, Utilities, Aero/Def, Telecom, Healthcare (including payers), Transportation</li>
<li>A strong C-suite rolodex within leading industry accounts</li>
<li>7 to 15 years of Enterprise Software or Application Management Services sales</li>
<li>Experience leading net new business sales in enterprise software sales with services</li>
<li>Excellent understanding of the Application Services business, pertaining to both systems integrator and end customers</li>
<li>An understanding of Application Design Management (ADM) practices and processes</li>
<li>Ability to develop and bridge productive C-level relationships</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record of quota over-achievement</li>
<li>Ability to lead by actions and influence</li>
<li>Work hard play hard mentality</li>
<li>Passion, Energy, Intelligence, Vigor, Vim</li>
<li>Excellent forecasting, reporting, communication skills</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required; Graduate degree preferred</li>
</ul>
