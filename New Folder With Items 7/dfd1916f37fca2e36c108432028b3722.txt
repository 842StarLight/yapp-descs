<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job description
</b></h2>
<p><br>
Review all annuities and life insurance for completeness and suitability. Sales collected from the sales rep will be evaluated for pertinent information. Question any inappropriate documents via e-mail or telephone and will document each sale with resolution of findings. Approve daily mutual fund, bond, UIT and CD trades for suitability and will review e-mail to identify issues. Process original paperwork and forward it to the appropriate internal departments and outside vendors.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities</p>
<p><br>
Review fixed and variable annuities and life insurance for completeness and suitability.<br>
Approve daily mutual fund, bond, UIT and CD trades for suitability.<br>
Review new accounts for suitability and completeness.<br>
Processes paperwork and send it to the appropriate vendors.<br>
Review daily email review system (SMARSH) to identify customer complaints, sales practice issues, unapproved advertising/sales literature, and inappropriate content.<br>
