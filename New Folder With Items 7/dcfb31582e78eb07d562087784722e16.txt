<h2>Payroll Accounting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Payroll / GL Processing</b></p>
<p>This position will process the corporation payroll a cloud-based human capital management software system. After payroll has been processed, the payroll information will be entered into the company's general ledger system. We seek a payroll / accounting professional who is accurate, knowledgeable and experienced with payroll and tax compliance in accordance with State and Federal regulations.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>Processes payroll (hourly, non-exempt, exempt, commissioned).</p>
<p>Reviews time sheets, enters exceptions.</p>
<p>Calculates variable compensation for sales and management. Payroll processes the monthly sales commissions.</p>
<p>Verifies payroll accuracy.</p>
<p>Processes separate payrolls for yearly bonus programs.</p>
<p>Processes garnishments, child support orders, tax levies, quarterly tax verifications, third party sick pay and worker compensation reports.</p>
<p>Performs year end responsibilities to include ACA and W-2 verification and mailing.</p>
<p>Ensures payroll system has accurate earning, deduction and benefit codes.</p>
<p>Enters payroll information into the company’s general ledger system. Understands debits and credits. Verifies accuracy.</p>
<p>Performs other related duties as assigned.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>3+ years of prior Lead Payroll Processor experience with “in-house processing”.</p>
<p>Advanced level Excel competency; proficient with formulas, pivot tables and VLOOKUP.</p>
<p>Strong understanding of payroll compliance and corresponding tax requirements.</p>
<p>Extremely detail oriented, organized and systematic in approach to payroll and GL tasks.</p>
<p>Exceedingly accurate with entry, processing and reconciliation of payroll and GL. Double (and triple) checks own work for completeness and accuracy.</p>
<p>Strong work ethic and a positive “whatever it takes” attitude.</p>
<p>Willing to acknowledge mistakes and proactively implement steps to prevent reoccurrence.</p>
<p>A dedicated and focused individual contributor, as well as an enthusiastic team player.</p>
<p>Discreet, able to handle confidential and proprietary information appropriately.</p>
<p>Possesses high levels of integrity and discretion.</p>
<p>Excellent/professional verbal and written communication skills.</p>
<p>Ability to interact with individuals from diverse levels within a corporation and to provide excellent customer service.</p>
<p>Prior experience using a 3rd party payroll system such as Ceridian, Namly, ADP, Paycom, Ultimate, Paycor.</p>
<p>Takes initiative to anticipate and resolve potential issues proactively.</p>
<p>Proven track record of reliability and responsibility.</p>
<p>Willingness to work overtime, as needed, from October through January and for departmental projects.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
