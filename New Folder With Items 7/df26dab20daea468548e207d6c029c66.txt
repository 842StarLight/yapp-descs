<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Account Manager is responsible for managing existing and new customer relationships to meet and/or exceed company sales revenue and profit objectives.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage new and existing customer relationships</li>
<li>Maintains a thorough knowledge of products</li>
<li>Presents products to customer</li>
<li>Follows through with customer to ensure satisfaction</li>
<li>Identifies and prioritizes all existing and prospective customers within his/her territory and keeps mailing list current.</li>
<li>Studies product information, attends seminars, supervises tests of products</li>
<li>Attends and contributes to company sales meetings and technical sessions.</li>
<li>Provides customers with all literature and promotional materials they need to enhance their productivity.</li>
<li>Provides the Vice-President with information from the market in regards to trends, new products, and market share in existing and potential accounts.</li>
<li>Proactively solve problems for customers</li>
<li>Build and sustain positive customer relationships</li>
<li>Provide and coordinate technical support as needed</li>
<li>Support and expand onsite sales and service</li>
<li>Communicate customer and market issues to company management</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
<li>Perform all work in accordance to ISO processes and procedures</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High levels of product knowledge</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Competent with the use of computer software specific to the operation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma required</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a related field preferred</li>
<li>2-5 years’ experience in a similar position required in Industrial Sales</li>
<li>Previous sales or customer service experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>BENEFITS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>401K Plan and Company Match</li>
<li>Tuition Reimbursement</li>
<li>Medical, Dental and Vision Insurance</li>
<li>Company paid Long Term and Short Term Disability</li>
<li>Life Insurance</li>
<li>Paid Time Off</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>BlackHawk Industrial is an Equal Opportunity Employer</li></ul>
