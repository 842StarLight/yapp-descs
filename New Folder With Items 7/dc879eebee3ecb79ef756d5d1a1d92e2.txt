<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are in search of extraordinary individuals who want to be part of a rapidly growing company. We are revolutionizing the way that caregivers find work and helping them receive higher wages, training, and recognition for the services they provide. We are disrupting the industry and looking for driven individuals to join our team.</p>
<p>
The Business Development Representative will represent ShiftMed in the community and help bring in additional business for the company. The Business Development Representative role will include working with hospitals, skilled nursing facilities, assisted living facilities, staffing companies and other providers in the senior care industry. They will need to identify, develop, and maintain new business opportunities who are in need of Care provider staffing services. They will also need to attend events in the community increasing community awareness of ShiftMed's mission and values. <b>This position will serve the Detroit Territory so we are only speaking to candidates</b> <b>local to Detroit!</b></p>
<p><b>
Business Development Representative</b> <b>Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Negotiating terms and contracts with staffing partners</li>
<li>Perform prospect visits to physician offices, hospitals, assisted living and skilled nursing facilities to educate and inform on ShiftMed staffing services</li>
<li>Attend community networking events to create additional business relationships</li>
<li>Keep abreast of trends in the industry and identify new opportunities for ShiftMed</li>
<li>Prepare presentations using ShiftMed marketing materials</li>
<li>Work with Director of Nursing or Scheduling team in each facility to identify needs</li>
<li>Field Referral calls and discuss opportunities that are brought to ShiftMed</li>
</ul>
