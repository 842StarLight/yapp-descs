<p>
Primary Job Function:<br>
<br>
The primary task of the Ready Mix Driver is driving to and from the plant and work sites. However, in between the driver must hose down his rig to keep it clean. The driver also must walk on uneven and slippery surfaces at the work sites and the plant.<br>
<br>
Primary responsibility is to drive concrete mixer to work sites and discharge load. This is in a variety of settings and conditions including uneven ground and may require unloading and attach extension chutes weighing thirty to fifty pounds to end of the primary chute.<br>
<br>
The required regular lifting of 25- 50 pounds. May oil, grease, service and make normal operating adjustments to equipment. May perform other related duties, such as lifting and moving of transported materials.<br>
<br>
Must be familiar with delivering to operating paver, roller, broom tractor, concrete curb/gutter machine for loading/unloading, etc. May perform other related duties.<br>
<br>
The job requires climbing, stooping, bending, squatting, stretching, driving, sitting, shifting gears, pulling, and occasional lifting of up to 50 pounds. Uses hand tools such as wrenches, screwdrivers, and shovels.<br>
<br>
May be exposed to extremes of hot or cold weather. Overtime is required. Must pass DOT physical/drug screen. Adheres to Company safety policy and rules at all times.<br>
<br>
The applicant is required to meet PREFERRED MATERIALS Driver Qualification Standards. Must have the following Commercial Drivers’ License and necessary endorsements by 49 CFR 383.91: combination vehicle (Group A or B). The job duties described herein are not exhaustive and may be supplemented.<br>
<br>
Qualifications:<br>
<br>
Must be able to Read, Write, and Communicate Effectively<br>
Class A or B drivers license with the current medical card (or ability to obtain one).<br>
Minimal moving violations<br>
Able to lift or move 50 pounds<br>
Good interpersonal skills. Ability to establish and maintain respectful relationships with other staff.<br>
Ability to read and interpret written information such as safety rules, sales tickets, operating instructions, and procedure manuals.<br>
<br>
Preference: forklift certified<br>
