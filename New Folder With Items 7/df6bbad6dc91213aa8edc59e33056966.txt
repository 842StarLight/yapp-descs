<p><b>Pulse Centers Account Manager </b></p>
<p>Pulse Centers is currently looking for a dynamic, high-performing Account Manager with experience selling technology and/or other services over the phone, by email, and via web-based presentations. We are looking for someone who has a proven background of developing long-term relationships with a book of clients and can liaise between clients and internal associates to deliver stellar service and solutions for client needs.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities of Account Manager: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create detailed business plans to facilitate the attainment of goals and quotas</li>
<li>Manage our current customer database as well as new customers upon point of sale.</li>
<li>Input all contacts and daily activity into our CRM software-Netsuite</li>
<li>Present products to our customer base and sell additional systems</li>
<li>Update the ISR community on dialog with their customers in full transparency</li>
<li>Provide after-sales support to enhance the clients’ dedication</li>
<li>Remain in frequent contact with the clients to stay ahead of their needs</li>
<li>Respond to concerns and resolve issues, striving for client satisfaction and retention</li>
<li>Keep records of sales and client data</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications for Account Manager</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Established phone presence</li>
<li>Knowledge of market research, sales and negotiating principles</li>
<li>Outstanding knowledge of Google Drive, Google Sheets, MS Office;</li>
<li>Excellent communication/presentation skills and ability to build relationships</li>
<li>Organizational and time-management skills</li>
<li>A business acumen (minimum of 3 to 5 years Business Experience)</li>
<li>Enthusiastic and passionate</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience Required or Preferred</b></p>
<ul>
<li>knowledge of CRM software (eg. Netsuite) is a preferred</li>
<li>Proven experience as an Account Executive/Manager, or in other sales/customer service role</li>
<li>A history of bringing in new business and hitting sales quotas</li>
<li>Outbound prospecting/account management in a competitive environment.</li>
<li>History of providing multi-channel customer support to new and potential clients (e.g.: phone, email, chat, Facebook and customer forums)</li>
<li>experience in the Medical and Health/wellness space is preferred</li>
</ul>
