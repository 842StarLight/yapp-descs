<h2>Front Desk Administrative Assistant - Clinton SC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
Primarily responsible for front office processes and sales. The successful candidate must be a person with an optimistic outlook on life, have excellent interpersonal skills, and proven sales and marketing success.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>*Must have medical office or eye care experience*</b><br>
<br>
<b>Education and Experience:</b><br>
1. High school diploma or equivalent. Further education preferred.<br>
2. At least one year experience in eye care, or medical environment.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Skills and Abilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Represents the practice professionally</li>
<li>Follows up on all details in a timely and complete manner</li>
<li>Outstanding Telephone Skills – cheerful voice, articulate, knowledgeable, efficient</li>
<li>Ability to effectively triage calls from patients to ensure appropriate scheduling.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and communication skills both verbal and written</li>
<li>Maintain a positive, upbeat attitude and ability to work as a team member and maintain positive working relationships.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously in a fast paced environment.</li>
<li>Well organized with attention to detail and prioritize tasks well</li>
<li>Treat patients with respect, empathetic with patients’ needs and concerns</li>
<li>Ability to resolve conflict, problem solve and make critical decisions</li>
<li>Ability to move, lift, carry, push, pull and place objects weighing less than or equal to 10 pounds without assistance.</li>
<li>Ability to maintain confidentiality of sensitive information</li>
<li>Office equipment and computer proficiency required in Microsoft Office: Word, Excel, Outlook.</li>
<li>Ability to read, understand and follow oral and written instructions</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with all HIPAA, State and Federal regulations</li>
<li>Proficient in social media Facebook and Twitter</li>
<li>Proven sales record a must</li>
</ul>
 <br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<b>1. Process Patients</b>
<ul>
<li>Answer telephones and triage calls</li>
<li>Schedule appointments</li>
<li>Verify patient insurance benefits</li>
<li>Process patients</li>
<li>Process medical records</li>
<li>Process patient payments and balance daily charges</li>
<li>Manage and organize inventory of office supplies and forms</li>
<li>Perform other tasks as requested by practice manager or physicians</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>2. Patient Relations</b>
<ul>
<li>Welcome and acknowledge patients according to company standard</li>
<li>Anticipate and address patient needs, assist individuals both in the office and over the phone.</li>
<li>Monitor busy or unanswered lines, check back with callers on hold to update status, take messages.</li>
<li>Log patient contact information, patient status and notes into computer to allow for proper lead tracking.</li>
<li>Enter and locate insurance information, appointments, and financial info. Stand, sit or walk for an extended period of time.</li>
<li>Ensure adherence to confidentiality and quality standards.</li>
<li>Establish bond with prospective and established patients</li>
<li>Screen refractive surgery patient consults in the office as well as on the phone.</li>
<li>Monitor the tracking program for refractive surgery patients. Tracking calls, conversion rates, and no shows</li>
<li>Conduct refractive surgery closings</li>
<li>Knowledgeable about all aspects of Refractive Surgery</li>
<li>Able to communicate the benefits of Lasik Surgery as well as overcome objections from patients.</li>
<li>Follow through with patient questionnaire surveys of refractive surgery patients, to help improve future experiences of surgery patients.</li>
<li>Follow up with phone call if post op questionnaire has not been received.</li>
<li>Maintain a kudos book of testimonial letters and thank you notes.</li>
<li>Perform other reasonable job duties as requested by Supervisor.</li>
</ul>
<br>
**<b>NextGen and/or Practice Management Software &amp; EHR experience desired</b> **</div></div>
