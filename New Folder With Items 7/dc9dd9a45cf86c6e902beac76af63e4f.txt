<p>Triad Systems Engineering, Inc.<br><b>Systems Analyst </b><br><b>Position Description</b></p>
<p><b>Job Title: </b>Systems Analyst<br><b>Department: </b> Support Department<br><b>Supervisor: </b>Jay Ewing</p>
<p>Triad Systems Engineering, Inc. is a Systems Integrator specializing in design, support and management of real time automation and control systems. Our primary customer segment operates oil gathering and fuel loading terminals in the U.S.</p>
<p><b>Overview of the Position </b></p>
<p>As a systems analyst you will provide remote assistance to our customers for their mission critical 24x7 automation systems. This includes troubleshooting hardware and/or software issues that occur with the automation and peripheral systems. You will work with the customer and Triad team members to analyze, diagnose, and solve these issues in real-time. All issues (support cases) are to be recorded in our ticketing system including time spent and work performed on each issue/case. This information is used for reporting and analysis by Triad management and our customers to better understand the types of issues that arise and the time and effort required to resolve each issue. Certain issues will be escalated based on our pre-defined SLA’s (Service<br>Level Agreements) with each customer.</p>
<p><b>Success in This Position Includes: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>A love for helping customers solve system and computer problems</li>
<li>Enjoy talking to customers over the phone</li>
<li>A natural desire to won the customer problem and follow up and follow through until the problem is resolved</li>
<li>Committed to continuous learning and always diving deeper into the systems we support</li>
<li>Ability to look for the root cause of a problem and proactively solve both the immediate problem/issue and the root cause so that the problem is unlikely to occur again in the future.</li>
</ul>
