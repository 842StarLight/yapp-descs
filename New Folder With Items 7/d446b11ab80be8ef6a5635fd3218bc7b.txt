<p><b>ABOUT INMOMENT</b></p>

InMoment™ is the leader in Experience Intelligence (XI) — in other words, we help organizations deliver positive, memorable experiences in every moment.</p>
<p>
We do that through our cloud-native XI Platform, which gives brands meaningful intelligence regarding customer experience, employee experience, and market experience so that they can take action on factors that actually impact the bottom line.</p>

Our technology, together with our deep domain knowledge, helps more than 500 of the world’s leading brands in 95 countries attract and retain their most profitable customers and most valuable employees.</p>
<p><b>
ABOUT THE JOB</b></p>
<p>
InMoment has an exciting opportunity for a self-motivated, enterprise sales individual contributor, located in a major US metropolitan area: Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Dallas, Southern California, Philadelphia, NYC, or Washington DC. This position will focus on business solutions for Retail, Financial Services, Healthcare, Hospitality and other industries striving to improve experiences for customers and employees.</p>
<p>
The Sales Director will play a critical role in generating new business as well as developing and managing successful relationships with a growing partner and consultancy ecosystem. The successful candidate will enjoy a team-selling atmosphere, dynamic compensation plan, engaged management team, collaborative marketing campaigns, as well as a fun and spirited culture.</p>
<p>
Ideal candidates will have successful individual contributor experience selling SaaS solutions in operations, customer service, analytics, finance, or marketing teams. The candidate must excel at qualifying and closing new opportunities over a complex and competitive sales cycle.</p>
<p>
You will be responsible for an assigned geographical territory, and will be reporting into InMoment’s Corporate HQ in Salt Lake City, Utah.</p>
