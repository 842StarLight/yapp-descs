<h2>R&D Scientist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>Directs research and development projects in order to meet organization’s needs for technology development, new product development, as well as improvement and cost reduction of existing products and processes.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Direct research programs for new product development and product/process improvement of current products. Develop product constructions and processes, including all experimentation/ evaluation and specifications assembly. Design &amp; conducts chemical experiments on both lab- and production-scale. Responsible for complex basic research and/or product development projects. Design and specifies production scale processes and conducts validation thereof.</li>
<li>Prepares test samples and conduct tests on chemicals and/or materials as required to support programs. Develops new tests as necessary to support projects. Analyzes technical data from both experiments and property testing to draw conclusions. Documents project work in lab notebooks, issues reports, and maintains files on projects. Completes any necessary regulatory forms.</li>
<li>Consults with technical director, operating and sales personnel on group priorities and strategy. Active role in setting overall R&amp;D direction &amp; strategy. Participates in business planning process with one or more business as appropriate. Establishes program priorities, objectives and responsibilities for group. Audits activities and communicates progress. Ensures effective communication of R&amp;D program progress for own work and for overall group.</li>
<li>Participates and leads cross-functional product development teams.</li>
<li>Participates and occasionally leads establishment and implementation of R&amp;D policies and procedures. May be responsible for specified lab areas.</li>
<li>Interface with customers on new product applications and facilitate patent activity to protect new technology. Networks externally to assure highest level of competence is maintained.</li>
<li>Develops skills and evaluates performance of department personnel. Mentors and influences other associates.</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as assigned by management.</li>
<li>Makes identifiable and substantial contributions to continuous improvement. Performs all duties consistent with established AR guidelines consistent with ISO-9001 and those elements of GMP deemed necessary to support our business (as outlined in the Quality Manual). Actively complies with and endorses Department and Company objectives.</li>
<li>Responsible for the proper disposal of hazardous waste into the proper labeled containers and drums.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Additional Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>Performs other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</p>
<p><b>Job Specifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS degree (or higher) in chemistry, chemical engineering , polymer science or materials science and/or significant industrial experience. D. degree preferred but not required.</li>
<li>Greater than 3 years adhesive formulation, mixing process and coating process experience.</li>
<li>Supervisory experience preferred.</li>
<li>Familiarity with coated adhesive applications.</li>
<li>Demonstrated technology and/or product development capability.</li>
<li>Excellent program prioritization and coordination skills, ability to manage multiple priorities</li>
<li>Must communicate fluently in English</li>
<li>Ability to maintain confidentiality of AR information</li>
<li>Excellent oral &amp; written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Requires an aptitude to use sensitive test equipment</li>
<li>Knowledge of computers &amp; related word processing, spreadsheet &amp; e-mail software – Microsoft preferred</li>
<li>Ability to employ statistical analysis techniques.</li>
</ul>
