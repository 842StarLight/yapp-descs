<h2>Pharmacy Government Policy & Regulations Principal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>**Position can be filled in the Chicago, Helena, Albuquerque, Tulsa or Richardson office**</b></p>
<div>
JOB PURPOSE:<br>
This position is responsible for leading the representation of Enterprise Pharmacy in pharmacy regulatory and governmental agencies policy/process, reviews, and execution planning. Primary liaison between Enterprise Pharmacy and state plans; partnering with key stakeholders across state plans, enterprise partners (Lines of Business, Government Relations), and Enterprise Pharmacy sales and customer engagement team to align policy strategy with execution roadmap to ensure constant alignment with changing demands of the marketplace, and regulatory and legislative requirements. Reviews and interprets new regulations and understands implications to the Enterprise. Oversee the Affordable Care Act Patient Protections regulatory program and work streams including budget, resources and monitoring delivery by business leads. Work with Government Relations team to understand national pharmacy landscape. May manage staff supporting enterprise/state review and execution of network policies and planning.<br>
<br>
JOB REQUIREMENTS:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor Degree in Business, Science, Political Science, or Health Administration</li>
<li>7 years of experience in legislative and regulatory advocacy</li>
<li>Experience in health or public policy development and practice and policy research</li>
<li>Knowledge of regulatory aspects and policy issues of health insurance and managed care</li>
<li>Demonstrated skill and proficiency in policy related analytical skills, presentation and problem solving</li>
<li>Experience articulating corporate position and the business implications for numerous and wide-ranging audiences</li>
<li>Experience working with others in a cross-functional team environment</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
PREFERRED JOB REQUIREMENTS:<br>
<ul>
<li>Pharmacy/PBM industry knowledge / experience</li>
<li>Master Degree in Business or Health Administration</li>
<li>Public policy experience</li>
</ul>
