<h2>Senior Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Position Description:</p>
<p>The Senior Accountant position is project-based as well as being responsible for a variety of Accounting duties. Duties include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and file all property taxes, nexus taxes and sales &amp; use tax</li>
<li>Record fixed assets and related depreciation</li>
<li>Process cash portions of weekly payroll</li>
<li>Review general ledger coding consistent with the established budget</li>
<li>Various month end tasks including month end journal entries, processing of month end in accounting system and preparation of internal financial statements</li>
<li>Assist with monthly budget file preparation and review</li>
<li>Various projects including, heavy financial analysis at times</li>
<li>Assist CFO/Controller as needed</li>
<li>All other duties as deemed necessary</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Qualifications:</p>
<p>Education &amp; Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Accounting and/or Finance</li>
<li>3-5 years of experience in Accounting including: AP, AR, invoicing, GL and Payroll</li>
</ul>
<p>Candidate must possess:</p>
<ul>
<li>Great attitude and attendance</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail and desire for consistency and accuracy</li>
<li>Strong time management and multi-tasking skills</li>
<li>Outstanding written and oral communication skills including the ability to communicate both through email and in person</li>
<li>Extensive, proven experience with Microsoft products, especially Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of accounting principles</li>
<li>Research and resolution, data analysis, financial analysis and documentation skills</li>
<li>Be able to work independently and with a team in a fast-paced and high volume environment</li>
<li>Have the confidence to make decisions or know when to involve the manager</li>
<li>Self-motivated, self-directed, ability to continue to work through a project goal independently</li>
<li>Ability to handle confidential and/or sensitive information</li>
<li>Some knowledge of software-based ERP, order entry, CRM or related systems</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>2 years of supervision experience</li>
<li>Cost Accounting experience</li>
<li>Knowledge of debt covenants</li>
<li>Knowledge of financial/cash projection tools and processes</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Supervising: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>Accounting: 3 years (Required)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's (Required)</li></ul>
</div>
