<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What You Will Do to Succeed:</b></h3>
<p>The ideal ESG Analyst candidate possess intellectual curiosity, technology industry knowledge and expertise, superior writing and presentation skills, outstanding <i>client</i> relations skills, experience conducting survey- and interview-<i>based</i> market research, and an ability to manage multiple ongoing projects simultaneously.</p>
</div>
</div></div></div>
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div><ul>
<li>Provide market coverage and <i>client</i> support as a <i>part</i> of ESG’s cybersecurity team.</li>
<li>Cover, research, and write on such security-related topics as:
<ul>
<li>Identity and access management (i.e., strong authentication, password management, and single sign-on).</li>
<li>Data security (i.e., data encryption, data classification, data leakage prevention, enterprise risk management, etc.).</li>
<li>Cloud security (e.g., cloud access security brokers).</li>
<li>Network security (i.e., firewall, IDS / IPS, network encryption, vulnerability scanning, etc.).</li>
<li>Application and database security (i.e., application firewalls, database monitoring tools, etc.).</li>
<li>Endpoint/mobile security (i.e., antivirus, anti-spyware, mobile security, etc.).</li>
<li>Web and messaging security (i.e., URL filtering, virus scanning, anti-SPAM, etc.).</li>
<li>Security management and operations (i.e., SIEM, log management, patch management, etc.).</li>
<li>Security analytics.</li>
<li>Managed security services.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Support this market segment <i>by</i> performing the following activities and more:
<ul>
<li>Create published <i>client</i>-sponsored content including white papers, webinars, and videos.</li>
<li>Initiate and develop ongoing segment coverage, content, and themes. This includes: conducting regular briefings with IT vendors and creating ESG publications (e.g., briefs, blogs, etc.) <i>based</i> on industry events, product/solution/service announcements, and other material industry activity.</li>
<li>Develop segment research topics, and execute syndicated, multi-<i>client</i>, and custom research projects as agreed upon <i>by</i> the ESG Research and Analyst management team. This will include conducting both quantitative/survey-<i>based</i> and qualitative/interview-<i>based</i> primary research.</li>
<li>Deliver ESG <i>client</i> consulting services that advise ESG clients on subject matters related to designed segment coverage area.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>These advisory services may entail:
<ul>
<li>Guiding IT vendors on market trends, competitive analysis, product/strategy, and business planning.</li>
<li>Assisting venture capital firms and other institutional investors with expert opinion on market dynamics and specific technologies and vendors.</li>
<li>Advising IT professionals on best practices and vendor selection.</li>
<li>Maintaining a visible, public-facing market presence that leverages and highlights segment-relevant research, analysis, and analyst opinion via social media (blog, Twitter, etc.) and speaking engagements (<i>client</i>-sponsored events, industry trade events, etc.).</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>ESG Analysts also play a key role in providing sales and marketing support for ESG products and services. Responsibilities in this area include:
<ul>
<li>Create appropriate marketing strategies for relevant ESG content and offerings.</li>
<li>Proactively and reactively <i>work</i> with media outlets to promote new research findings and analyst opinions.</li>
<li>Take an active role in identifying sales <i>opportunities</i> and assist ESG <i>Client</i> Relations staff in product positioning, project scoping, and proposal generation.</li>
<li>Support all business development efforts as requested <i>by</i> ESG’s <i>Client</i> Relations team.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul></div></div></div></div>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>6-8+ years of direct and/or related experience in fields such as cybersecurity vendor marketing, product marketing, product management, business development, sales, or consulting; IT industry analysis and market research; IT media; or security management/execution.</li>
<li>Cybersecurity or other technology vendor experience strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Previous IT analyst/firm experience a plus, but not required.</li>
<li>Proven industry knowledge, research and analysis skills, documented writing ability, superior project management and <i>client</i> relations skills, strong presentation skills, and excellent interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Must have above-average intellectual curiosity and ability to quickly learn new topic areas as warranted <i>by</i> projects.</li>
<li>Must be able to take initiative in all aspects of <i>job</i> and be comfortable working both independently and in team environments.</li>
<li>Must be willing to <i>work</i> in a small, entrepreneurial company.</li>
<li>Must be comfortable working in a project-<i>based</i>, <i>deadline</i>-driven environment.</li>
<li>BA/BS required. Advanced degree preferred, but not necessary.</li>
<li>Geographic <i>location</i> in Greater Boston (MA) strongly preferred.</li>
</ul>
