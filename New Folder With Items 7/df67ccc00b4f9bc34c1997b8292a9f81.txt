<p><b>

<p></p>
<div>
<p>
The Mechanical Engineer (ME) will work as part of the Company product marketing team. The ME will have a demonstrated background in Project Engineering and will work with a cross-functional Integrated Product Team (IPT) to ensure that products brought to market are successful in a variety of Customer applications. The ME must be able to clearly articulate technical differentiators of a product and support competitive bid and proposal efforts. Experience in the development of airborne and ground-based systems to support special-mission aircraft modifications, sensor system development and installations is required.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist Company Marketing Department to help identify strategic business opportunities and pursuits.</li>
<li>
Develop winning ideas and strategies during bid and proposal development to enable new program capture.</li>
<li>
Support proposal preparation, cost estimation and development of basis of estimates.</li>
<li>
Support product launch activities and assist with Tradeshows and Conferences.</li>
<li>
Be required to have regular customer communications and interface.</li>
<li>
Set highest ethical standards for self and others.</li>
<li>
Other tasks as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div>
