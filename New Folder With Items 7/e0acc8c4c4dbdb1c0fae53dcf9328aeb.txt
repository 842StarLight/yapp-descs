<h2>Assistant Property Manager (Self Storage)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>*Summary</p>
<p>*The Assistant/Relief Manager for this self storage company provides extensive management experience and a strong work ethic to perform all operations and marketing/sales functions at the facility during the Onsite Manager’s days off. They are responsible for overseeing all aspects of the day-to-day operations of our self-storage facilities and demonstrating a sense of “pride-of-ownership” as if they owned the facility themselves during their working hours.</p>
<p>Benefits, monthly commissions and 401k programs offered!</p>
<p>*<i>Essential Functions</i></p>
<p>*Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the sales, marketing, occupancy, and delinquency management of the facility</li>
<li>Provides outstanding customer service including assessing and resolving customer problems and professionally handling telephone and walk-in inquiries</li>
<li>Shows, rents and cleans self-storage units</li>
<li>Answer phones, assist tenants with storage unit questions and accounts</li>
<li>Data entry: posting payments, entering NSF checks on the system; following Allspace policy &amp; procedure at all times.</li>
<li>Removing red locks from units when tenants have paid their rent and, over locking units when tenants are delinquent paying their rent or as otherwise directed or required.</li>
<li>Billing/Invoice preparation and mailing.</li>
<li>Calling delinquent tenants for account collection.</li>
<li>General management of the office including but not limited to opening and closing the office on time each day, keeping and balancing petty cash, making daily bank deposits and preparing daily, weekly and monthly reports as required by management</li>
<li>Keeping the facility rented at optimum levels by effectively utilizing advertising, promotional and customer relation skills as approved by management</li>
<li>Maintains all required tenant documentation in a neat and orderly manner as directed by management</li>
<li>Controls delinquencies through telephone contact and letters on a scheduled basis including sending out monthly invoices</li>
<li>Cleans space(s) as vacated, hallways, and grounds as needed. Clean office and restrooms daily</li>
<li>Maintains a neat, clean, safe and secure facility including minor maintenance and daily lock checks as well as visual inspection daily</li>
<li>Assist in preparing units for auction as prescribed by law, and approved by management</li>
<li>Effectively communicates with management and fellow associates</li>
<li>Other functional duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Qualifications Requirements</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior self-storage experience preferred but not required</li>
<li>Self-Storage Software Experience is a plus</li>
<li>Customer service, retail sales, marketing experience is preferred</li>
<li>Successful record of accomplishment of sales and marketing skills in the self-storage or retail industry is preferred</li>
<li>Ability to pay close attention to detail</li>
<li>Valid driver's license and reliable form of transportation are required in order to perform essential functions of this job</li>
<li>Weekend availability is required</li>
<li>Possess the eagerness and work ethics to succeed in a customer service environment</li>
</ul>

<li>Good written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Good organizational skills</li>
<li>Proactive, self-starter, goal oriented and ability to work independently</li>
<li>Maintain a positive attitude and composure</li>
<li>Collaborative Team Player</li>
<li>Must maintain and hold a valid State Driver’s License</li>
<li>Uphold Company’s Vision, Mission Statement and Core Values</li>
</ul>
