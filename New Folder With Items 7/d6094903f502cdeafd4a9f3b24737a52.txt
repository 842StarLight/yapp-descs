About the Role
<br><br>
Our Customer Experience team is responsible for designing and delivering world-class experiences for our customers. We partner with our customers to understand their goals and work towards providing value while driving long-term relationships for mutual success. As the Technical Support Specialist, you will serve as the bridge between Kustomer and the amazing people who use our product.
<br><br>
<b>You'll be responsible for:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Executing day-to-day customer support operations including responding to questions and following up on reported issues.</li>
<li>Mastering the Kustomer platform in order to efficiently and effectively manage customer relationships, including reporting on trends, SLAs, and customer satisfaction metrics.</li>
<li>Developing client training programs/documentation by identifying common issues.</li>
<li>Empathizing with the customer's perspective and providing the best solutions that are aligned with Kustomer's core values.</li>
<li>Strategizing ways to improve our service, streamline our processes, and better serve our customers</li>
<li>Communicating with the larger CX team, as well as Technology, Marketing, and Sales to enhance the customer journey</li>
<li>Escalating technical issues and triage support to other team members where necessary, including creating bug reports with clear repro steps</li>
<li>May involve handling sensitive personal data</li>
</ul>
<b>Your qualities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You have demonstrated knowledge and solid understanding of the following languages: JavaScript, JSON, Rest API, HTML, but this does not mean you need to be an engineer.</li>
<li>You posses 2-3+ years of professional experience; experience with SaaS companies and startups a plus.</li>
<li>Available for 2nd shift and on-call rotation.</li>
<li>You're an excellent communicator who enjoys problem solving and friendly conversation, translating technical concepts across various customer profiles.</li>
<li>You can think your way through problems and enjoy helping our customers solve them. (Even if the problems aren't well-defined, even if the usual solutions aren't working, and even if it's the fifth person you've helped solve that problem today.)</li>
<li>You possess a strong sense of ownership and determination to get things done.</li>
<li>You're attentive to the little details and you believe people's experience matters.</li>
<li>You drive towards efficiency, thinking on your feet to find creative solutions to requests and directional changes.</li>
<li>You like to think about problems holistically, and how decisions impact future actions.</li>
</ul>
