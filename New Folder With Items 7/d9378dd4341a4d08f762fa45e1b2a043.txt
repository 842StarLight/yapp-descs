<b>Overview</b><br>
<br>
IDC is the most respected global technology market research firm operating in over 110 countries worldwide. Our collaborative, innovative and entrepreneurial culture is the perfect place for you to discover your future.<br>
<br>
IDC's Enterprise Applications Research group is looking to fill the role of <b>Research Manager, EAM and Enterprise Applications. </b>Join IDC's team and be a key component to delivering the most respected software and cloud data and trends in the industry! Upon joining IDC, you can expect a fun and fast-paced work environment along with a culture that rewards, recognizes, and respects achievement.<br>
<br>
The Research Manager role is a relatively public role with many client-facing and industry-facing responsibilities, including regular interaction with major software vendors, cloud services providers and industry players.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<br>
The ideal Research Manager will be responsible for conducting and delivering the research published in the Enterprise Application research service, primarily focused on the Enterprise Asset Management space. The primary research focus will explore enterprise asset management across all industries, evaluating the common denominators, differentiators and innovation aspects now and into the future. In addition, this role will provide secondary support to the broader enterprise applications research service, helping to provide research and consulting coverage for applications such as ERP, supply chain management, procurement, finance, and more.<br>
<br>
Primary responsibilities will include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Interacting with clients
</li>
<li>Conducting market research
</li>
<li>Writing research reports</li>
<li>
Producing custom research (i.e. white papers, ROI studies, market assessments, etc.)</li>
<li>
Delivering public speaking engagements and webcasts (i.e. speaking at industry conferences and customer events)</li>
</ul>
<br>
Additional responsibilities include managing the EAM research agenda, helping with marketing activities, conducting business development, providing sales support, and acting effectively as a client advisor for industrial companies and technology suppliers.<br>
<br>
