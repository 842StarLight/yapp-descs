<h2>District Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for driving restaurant sales and profits by providing clear direction and leadership<br>
to restaurant management that focuses on a guest-centric culture, including consistently<br>
delivering excellent guest service and food quality, executing Jack in the Box brand standards,<br>
and by promoting the Company's vision, mission and values. Span of control: 6-8 restaurants.</p>
<p><b>
Key Duties/Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Oversees execution and ensures adherence with all Company and restaurant systems,<br>
procedures, and food safety requirements. Conducts restaurant audits and regularly monitors<br>
performance. Educates and consults with managers to ensure their understanding of restaurant<br>
operational and financial performance, including knowledge of income and expense reports<br>
and quality and service reports; ensures reports are reviewed each Period. Thinks strategically<br>
and helps managers to identify trends and implement action plans to improve restaurant sales<br>
and profit. Conducts business reviews, monitors and investigates fraud reports, reviews cash<br>
management reports, monitors labor usage and inventory levels; takes accountability and<br>
follows-up on action items to ensure operational excellence and financial excellence.</p>
<p>
Performs an active role in promoting the Jack in the Box restaurant in the local community,<br>
including: networks with community leaders to establish relationships; participates and<br>
represents the Company in community events; keeps informed of local competitor activities.</p>
<p>
Promotes a guest-centric culture and holds restaurant managers accountable for continuously<br>
improving the guest experience, including: reviews guest experience reports i.e. Voice Of<br>
Guest, Earn the Next Visit, guest relations calls, coaches and empowers restaurant<br>
management to deliver on all aspects of the guest experience including food quality and safety,<br>
speed of service, employee friendliness, problem resolution, cleanliness and order accuracy.</p>
<p>
Provides constructive and actionable feedback; follows-up with managers to ensure timely and<br>
professional resolution of guest complaints and guest issues; contacts guest as needed.</p>
<p>
Conducts high impact visits to the restaurants to observe all areas of operations and evaluate<br>
the guest experience.</p>
<p>
Ensures all security equipment in the restaurants is operational; acts as the primary liaison with<br>
Law Enforcement at the restaurant level; conducts cash loss investigations including<br>
interviewing employees, identifying policy violations and responsibility parties and reporting to<br>
the Asset Protection Department; conducts periodic asset protection surveys.</p>
<p>
Develops relationships with restaurant employees and promotes a work environment of mutual<br>
respect; seeks and acts upon employee feedback; addresses employee relations issues and<br>
ethics calls; conducts employee investigations and resolves issues. Ensures a restaurant<br>
environment that is fun, clean and safe; ensures employees understand and acknowledge<br>
monthly safety lesson plans; conducts post injury investigations including reporting lost time<br>
claims to the Asset Protection Department and providing witness statements, videos and police reports to the Risk Management Department; understands and utilizes Jack in the Box systems, processes, and tools; and complies with policies, procedures, and regulatory requirements.</p>
<p>
Utilizes effective recruiting sources and methods to attract and select talented employees while<br>
effectively balancing external hiring with internal promotions by conducting workforce<br>
assessment and succession planning. Reviews applications and resumes; conducts interviews;<br>
extends employment offers; initiates background checks and conducts new hire orientation and<br>
onboarding.</p>
<p>
Oversees the ongoing training and development of internal and external candidates for<br>
management and team leader positions; partners with management to identify development<br>
needs and implement development plans; and ensures succession management by leading<br>
training sessions and workshops. Builds an engaged workforce through teaching, leading and<br>
coaching; motivates and inspires management team to achieve high performance by being a<br>
role model and providing regular performance assessment and feedback.</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>
Experience - Multi-unit leadership experience in restaurant, retail or related industry.<br>
Knowledge/Skills/Abilities - Requires ability to speak, read, and write effectively in English;<br>
excellent interpersonal skills and collaborative management skills. Maintains professionalism<br>
and optimistic attitude in all situations. Is a self-starter who takes initiative and willingly accepts<br>
responsibility. Good listener, self-motivator and can motivate others. Proficient knowledge of<br>
personal computers and related software applications Microsoft Office. Demonstrates<br>
integrity and ethical behavior.</p>
<p>
Physical Requirements - Ability to move freely throughout the restaurant; ability to drive a<br>
motor vehicle and travel to restaurants and other business locations approximately 85%-90% of<br>
time.</p>
<p><b>
Competencies:</b></p>
<p>
Organizational Competencies</p>
<p>
Customer Focus - Is dedicated to meeting the expectations and requirements of internal and<br>
external customers; gets first-hand customer information and uses it for improvements in<br>
products and services; acts with customers in mind; establishes and maintains effective<br>
relationships with customers and gains their trust and respect.</p>
<p>
Dealing with Ambiguity - Can effectively cope with change; can shift gears comfortably; can<br>
decide and act without having the total picture; isn't upset when things are up in the air;<br>
doesn't have to finish things before moving on; can comfortably handle risk and uncertainty.</p>
<p>
Learning on the Fly - Learns quickly when facing new problems; a relentless and versatile<br>
learner; open to change; analyzes both successes and failures for clues to improvement;<br>
experiments and will try anything to find solutions; enjoys the challenge of unfamiliar tasks;<br>
quickly grasps the essence and the underlying structure of anything.</p>
<p>
Problem Solving - Uses rigorous logic and methods to solve difficult problems with effective<br>
solutions; probes all fruitful sources for answers; can see hidden problems; is excellent at<br>
honest analysis; looks beyond the obvious and doesn't stop at the first answers.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Competencies:</b></p>
<p>
Confronting Direct Reports - Deals with problem direct reports firmly and in a timely manner;<br>
doesn't allow problems to fester; regularly reviews performance and holds timely discussions;<br>
can make negative decisions when all other efforts fail; deals effectively with troublemakers.</p>
<p>
Developing Direct Reports and Others - Provides challenging and stretching tasks and<br>
assignments; holds frequent development discussions; is aware of each person's career goals;<br>
constructs compelling development plans and executes them; pushes people to accept<br>
developmental moves; will take on those who need help and further development; cooperates<br>
with the developmental system in the organization; is a people builder.</p>
<p>
Directing Others - Is good at establishing clear directions; sets stretching objectives; distributes<br>
the workload appropriately; lays out work in a well-planned and organized manner; maintains<br>
two-way dialogue with others on work and results; brings out the best in people; is a clear<br>
communicator.</p>
<p>
Functional/Technical Skills - Has the functional and technical knowledge and skills to do the job<br>
at a high level of accomplishment.</p>
<p>
Hiring and Staffing - Has a nose for talent; hires the best people available from inside or<br>
outside; is not afraid of selecting strong people; assembles talented staffs.</p>
<p>
Motivating Others - Creates a climate in which people want to do their best; can motivate many<br>
kinds of direct reports and team or project members; can assess each person's hot button and<br>
use it to get the best out of him/her; pushes tasks and decisions down; empowers others;<br>
invites input from each person and shares ownership and visibility; makes each individual feel<br>
his/her work is important; is someone people like working for and with.</p>
<p>
Priority Setting - Spends his/her time and the time of others on what's important; quickly zeros<br>
in on the critical few and puts the trivial many aside; can quickly sense what will help or hinder<br>
accomplishing a goal; eliminates roadblocks; creates focus.</p>
<p>
Drive for Results - Can be counted on to exceed goals successfully; is constantly and<br>
consistently one of the top performers; very bottom-line oriented; steadfastly pushes self and<br>
others for results.</p>
<p>
Sizing Up People - Is a good judge of talent; after reasonable exposure, can articulate the<br>
strengths and limitations of people inside or outside the organization; can accurately project<br>
what people are likely to do across a variety of situations.</p>
<p>
Building Effective Teams - Blends people into teams when needed; creates strong morale and<br>
spirit in his/her team; shares wins and successes; fosters open dialogue; lets people finish and<br>
be responsible for their work; defines success in terms of the whole team; creates a feeling of<br>
belonging in the team.</p>
<p>
Time Management - Uses his/her time effectively and efficiently; values time; concentrates<br>
his/her efforts on the more important priorities; gets more done in less time than others; can<br>
attend to a broader range of activities.</p>
<p>
Managing Vision and Purpose - Communicates a compelling and inspired vision or sense of core<br>
purpose; talks beyond today; talks about possibilities; is optimistic; creates mileposts and<br>
symbols to rally support behind the vision; makes the vision sharable by everyone; can inspire<br>
and motivate entire units or organizations.</p>
