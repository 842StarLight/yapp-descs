<p><b>About opendorse
</b></p>
<p>opendorse is the leading athlete marketing platform that helps the biggest brands in sports share content on social. Founded by two former major college athletes, opendorse was built on seeking to make it easy for athletes and brands to work together to engage their fans.</p>
<p>
+ More than 7,100 athletes use opendorse to share content on social</p>
<p>
+ More than 1,700 brands use opendorse to send content to athletes to share through their social media channels</p>
<p>
We connect marketers with athletes to build social media campaigns. We help brands, teams, and leagues to distribute content where today's fans want to consume it — in the social media feeds of the world's top athletes.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who we are looking for</b>
</p>
<p>opendorse is hiring a motivated Director, Business Development with entrepreneurial drive and outstanding sales experience. This candidate will be tasked with selling our technology and managed service offering to brands and professional sports teams. This sales executive will focus on building and scaling the business, requiring a track record of successfully generating revenue in business development and sales roles (preferably in sports and/or digital media). They will be responsible for demonstrating opendorse’s platform and capabilities to marketing departments at professional sports properties.</p>
<p>
The candidate should have a comprehensive understanding of B2B sales process in the SaaS environment, how to navigate buying committees, and generate new business within the sports ecosystem.</p>
<p>
This role will report to the President and will work alongside our Account Executives, SDRs, and marketing team to ensure quarterly bookings targets are achieved. The candidate will help grow opendorse’s pro sports team partner base by leading new business pursuits through independent action - from initial outreach to qualifying, demonstrating products and services, positioning, presenting, negotiating, and closing of business.</p>
<br>
