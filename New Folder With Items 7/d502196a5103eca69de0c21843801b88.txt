<h2>Order Management Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Novaspect, Inc, an Emerson Impact Partner, the global leader of process systems and solutions, is currently offering an opportunity for an Order Management Specialist to join our fast paced, customer oriented valve repair team in our Minooka, IL location. The successful candidate will perform various order management and support functions including order entry, purchasing, and expediting that support our valve repair business. We are searching for an individual with a solid understanding of purchase order/invoice business systems. A strong knowledge of computer applications including MS Word, Excel and the internet is required. In addition, the candidate should demonstrate excellent written and verbal communication skills.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage aspects of all customer orders (order entry, purchasing, expediting, order status, etc)
</li>
<li>Manage work orders and functional objects in our business system as required to support valve repairs</li>
<li>Ensure customer purchase order is in compliance with service quote</li>
<li>Work with team members to ensure customer schedules and expectations are fulfilled</li>
<li>Ability to learn and work in multiple business systems and applications quickly and efficiently</li>
<li>Communicate and work with other operations team members as required</li>
<li>Support documentation requests for customer orders</li>
<li>Actively participate with team members to achieve company goals</li>
<li>Support post order customer requests
</li>
<li>Collaborate with Midwest Valve Services Order Management team</li>
<li>Other duties as required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to manage difficult or emotional situations and respond promptly to internal customer needs</li>
<li>Quickly embraces, learns and applies new technology and/or procedures</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, verbal communication skills and proven ability maintain high customer service</li>
<li>Strict attention to detail</li>
<li>Proven ability to build and support teamwork</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Extensive and exceptional customer service practices</li>
<li>Minimum of 1 year of experience in a sales or manufacturing environment</li>
<li>Previous industrial valve and/or valve repair experience preferred</li>
<li>High computer application literacy including Microsoft Office Suite, with advanced Microsoft Excel skills</li>
<li>ERP business management software experience preferred</li>
</ul>
