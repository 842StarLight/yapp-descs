<b>Job Description</b>
</div>
<div>
<div>This role oversees account management for key corporate booking tools including Concur, Deem and Serko. This position is responsible for implementing a sales strategy, proactively shaping the Sabre-CBT relationships with key stakeholders across the enterprise and finding new revenue upside within the existing business.</div>
<div> <b>Core Values:</b>
</div>
<div>You will reach this key objective by demonstrating our core values. Core values are essential, setting the tone for our actions and guiding our direction. Our six core values are:</div>
<ul><li>Passion</li></ul>
<ul><li>Curiosity</li></ul>
<ul><li>Rigor</li></ul>
<ul><li>Accountability</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partnership</li></ul>
<ul><li>Transparency</li></ul>
<div><b>General Description:</b></div>
<ul><li>Solely responsible for the achievement of Sabre business objectives for third party corporate booking tools.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish trusted relationships with key sales, technology, and operations leaders across CBT organizations to foster short-term and long-term strategies to increase revenue, while lowering operating costs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Observes market and technology trends and develops ideas for innovative new ways to partner and expand the relationship.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Implements best practice processes and tools for managing activities, driving maximum production.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborates across the organization and at all levels to ensure optimal performance. Works closely with Regional Leaders, Marketing, Product Management, Technology, Operations, Finance and Legal teams to ensure activities are aligned with the overall corporate strategy</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures the strategic direction of the Company is implemented to maximize profit.</li></ul>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Job Requirements</b>
</div>
<div>
<ul><li>3+ years domain experience in the travel sector (agency, airline, hospitality) preferred, focused on distribution and sales strategy with proven success growing revenue and developing deep, long-term customer relationships</li></ul>
<ul><li>5+ years extensive commercial experience, leveraging a solution-oriented approach to selling and advanced negotiation skills in a globally-matrixed environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Extensive understanding of the corporate travel market landscape including competitive information, key trends, strengths, opportunities and threats</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven ability to influence cross-functional teams within a matrix organization with skills to drive effective decision making</li></ul>
<ul><li>Able to challenge the status quo and current ways of operating, bringing new and innovative strategies to the team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Exceptional communications skills. Exhibits a professional presence required to command respect both internally and externally, including the ability to present externally and potentially to Board members</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strategic thinker with a strong commercial acumen displaying the highest ethical standards and impeccable character</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree required, Master’s degree is helpful.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Location: Southlake, Texas, USA</li></ul>
<ul><li>Travel: 25%</li></ul>
