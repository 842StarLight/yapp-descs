<h2>Salesforce Implementation Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>CapTech Consulting is looking for an experienced Salesforce Implementation Consultant to join us as a leader in our growing Salesforce service. Responsibilities include translating the client’s vision for CRM into working Salesforce tools, guiding business stakeholders through workshops, and defining solutions that meet both near- and long-term client objectives. As a Salesforce Implementation Consultant, you’ll help our clients achieve success by advocating for their business needs and end users throughout each stage of the implementation process.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide technical and functional expertise to Salesforce project team</li>
<li>
Document and implement configuration changes and data-mapping requirements</li>
<li>
Guide development teams on functionality requiring custom logic to meet requirements and demonstrate solution functionality to business stakeholders as needed</li>
<li>
Develop business-specific and/or application-level approaches and plans for testing, training, communicating, rolling out, and offering post-production support</li>
<li>
Lead or facilitate sessions to gather, define, and document business requirements</li>
<li>
Complete Salesforce product certifications to successfully advise and direct clients on best practices for both business process and platform optimization</li>
<li>
Assist our business development team with developing prototype demonstrations</li>
<li>
Identify and map relevant system integrations</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, MIS or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>
Two plus years’ hands-on experience with enterprise-level Salesforce applications</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Salesforce platform tools and languages such as Apex, Lightning Components, Process Builder, Flow, and Workflow.</li>
<li>
Salesforce certification(s) preferred, willingness to obtain certifications required</li>
<li>
Content knowledge: Deep experience designing and implementing Salesforce solutions in the sales, service, marketing, and data clouds</li>
<li>
Client training: Develop client training plans and lead train-the-trainer and end-user training sessions as required</li>
<li>
Change management and scope control: Experience performing impact analysis for potential changes in scope and ability to administer change management processes within a specified framework, including regularly reviewing issue management and change control logs to understand status and identify potential risk situations</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Salesforce platform tools and languages such as Apex and Lightning</li>
<li>
Project management: Demonstrated analytical skills and experience with complete lifecycle of complex enterprise projects in addition to understanding core project management concepts, theories, practices, and methods</li>
<li>
System configuration: Ability to effectively design a scalable solution to meet client business needs and participate in system configuration when necessary</li>
<li>
Analytical techniques: Experience leading requirements gathering sessions and documenting outcome, including designing process flows and helping define test scripts</li>
</ul>
