<h2>Banquet Captain - SentryWorld Private Events & Banques</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
At Sentry, our business is built around people. We pride ourselves on the promise to deliver efficient and effective services to our internal and external customers. The SentryWorld Banquet &amp; Special Events team is seeking an energetic and enthusiastic Banquet Captain. This role is the perfect fit for someone who is looking to increase his/her experience and responsibility in event planning, while ensuring that the customer is receiving exceptional customer service throughout an event. The Banquet Captain will partner with the Event Coordinator &amp; Event Manager to deliver the successful execution of assigned banquet functions and will also act as a team lead in banquet set-up, food and beverage service, and as a co-leader in the front of house banquet operations. A professional and friendly demeanor is essential.</p>
<b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><ul>
<li>Oversees food and beverage service to ensure consistency throughout the event
</li>
<li>Collaborates with Event Coordinator and Head Banquet Chef on menu details, food preparation activities and service standards
</li>
<li>Collaborates with Event Coordinator on scheduling banquet attendant personnel and assigns specific tasks or services while ensuring that safety and sanitation codes are followed</li>
<li>Inspects function rooms and equipment prior to events for cleanliness, proper inventory and set-up.
</li>
<li>Oversees clean-up of function room and proper breakdown and storage of equipment.</li>
<li>Assists banquet attendants with their duties in any capacity needed such as banquet service, set-up, food preparation, clean-up, etc.</li>
</ul>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>0-2 years of related work experience.</li>
<li>Must be able to obtain TIPS Certification.
</li>
<li>Strong communication and organizational skills.
</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and leadership skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively under time constraints and deadlines.
</li>
<li>Work extended/non-standard hours including nights, weekends and holidays as needed.
</li>
</ul>
