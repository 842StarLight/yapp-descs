<h2>PT CSR I-Retail</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
Provides superior service to bank customers by performing banking transactions, opening new accounts and expanding customer relationships.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintains and balances a cash drawer, performs ATM and assists with end of night balancing.</li>
<li>Receives, posts, pays out funds, balances and proofs customer accounts.</li>
<li>Opens new accounts for checking, savings, business accounts, IRA, etc.</li>
<li>Identifies customer needs and recommends specific products and/or services by use of CNA process.</li>
<li>Expands customer relationships by making appropriate referrals, such as investments, mortgage and commercial loans and handling consumer loans by taking applications and assisting with processing.</li>
<li>Maintains and balances cash drawer.</li>
<li>Accepts and posts credit card and loan payments and payoffs.</li>
<li>Performs account research and problem solving as needed.</li>
<li>Cross-trains in other areas of the financial center, such as ATM, Vault, Savings Bonds, Safe Deposit, Wire Transfers, other ancillary services and assists with opening and closing financial center.</li>
<li>Contributes to the achievement of financial center sales/service goals by meeting/exceeding individual goals.</li>
<li>Upholds customer satisfaction by supporting external and internal customers and answering questions/requests in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Maintains customer confidence and protects operations by following the Privacy Policy and keeping information confidential.</li>
<li>Complies with the bank procedures and follows regulatory/operational/security guidelines. Adheres to the Check Handling Agreement. Successful Mystery Shop scores. Minimal bank monetary losses.</li>
</ul>
<b>Secondary Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Makes tele-consulting as necessary.</li>
<li>Provides support by performing clerical duties, such as answering telephone, maintaining accurate records, faxing, copying and filing.</li>
<li>Contributes to the team effort by performing other job-related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Standards:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in technical processing of all phases of the CSR I position (transactions, opening/closing, new accounts opening, savings, DDA, Commercial DDA, CDs, IRA’s, balancing ATM, Safe Deposit processing), balances at 90% or better and assist in finding outages as evidenced by management observation and balancing documentation. *</li>
<li>Embraces the “ASK” program and strives to achieve personal sales goals (referrals) as evidenced by sales reports and tracking forms. *</li>
<li>Demonstrates proficiency with ERB process through audit of the CNA folders. *</li>
<li>Demonstrates excellent customer service/sales skills both in person and on telephone as evidenced by management observation. *</li>
<li>Good understanding of all areas of compliance regulations (Reg. CC, Reg. E, TISA, Privacy Act, BSA, etc.) as evidenced by absence of regulation violations.</li>
<li>Must have completed and passed all mandatory training sessions, classroom, CBT and self-study as scheduled by manager within specified timelines or as classes are available.</li>
<li>Answers calls within 3 rings and returns phone calls or follows up on requests and/or questions within 24-hours.</li>
<li>Maintains regular attendance and punctuality as evidenced by attendance records.</li>
<li>Proficient in consumer loan processing (application and basic processing) as evidenced by minimal errors reported by Consumer Lending Quality Control and management observation.</li>
</ul>
<b>Physical Demands:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Talking—Ability to express or exchange ideas by means of the spoken word.</li>
<li>Hearing—Ability to receive detailed information through oral communication.</li>
<li>Seeing—Ability to view a computer screen for an extended period of time and/or identify individuals visually.</li>
<li>Standing—Ability to stand on feet for long periods of time.</li>
<li>Walking—Ability to use feet and legs to move from one place to another specifically in narrow/confined spaces.</li>
<li>Finger dexterity—Ability to work with fingers for handling coins or other small objects and use a keyboard.</li>
<li>Reaching—Ability to extend hand/arms in any direction to move/handle objects.</li>
<li>Lifting—Ability to lift and/or move up to 25 pounds.</li>
</ul>
<b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent required.</li>
<li>Minimum 1 year customer service or cash handling experience/skills required.</li>
<li>Able to communicate with individuals at all levels, provide superior customer service, and work well with others is required.</li>
<li>Able to demonstrate a professional courteous manner, present a well-groomed, business-like appearance and use proper telephone etiquette/grammar is required.</li>
<li>Able to work a flexible schedule and skills in accuracy, cash handling, and math are required.</li>
<li>Basic computer skills and the ability to learn industry-related software are required.</li>
</ul>
