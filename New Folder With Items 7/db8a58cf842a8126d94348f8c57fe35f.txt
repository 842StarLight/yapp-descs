Individual devices include lighting controls, lighting components, controllers for various building systems (including HVAC, lighting, shades and access control), power supplies, and prismatic skylights. <b>
Job Summary</b>
</div>
<div><div>The <b>Director of Operations </b>(Plant Manager) in <b>Winona, MN</b> directs and coordinates all activities related to the scheduling, fabrication, finishing &amp; assembly of finished goods. The role will be responsible to coordinate the continuous improvement of activities; develop employees by expanding their skills &amp; knowledge, enhancing a culture of disciplined execution &amp; accuracy of information. Whey will also ensure plant capacity meets or exceeds sales forecast. The Director of Operations will be responsible for direct reports of four Area Managers, schedulers, several supervisors and an hourly staff of over 300 employees and develop future leaders within each functional area.</div></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Key Tasks &amp; Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Lead all associates to understand, improve &amp; execute plant fundamentals, (Safety &amp; Environmental compliance, 5S, Quality, Delivery, Maintenance, Productivity &amp; cost, (Units / op hr &amp; budget)</li>
<li>
Manage the continuous improvement process, with all employees to increase throughout &amp; capacity, reduce lead time and improve quality</li>
<li>
Utilize visual management tools, and coach all associates to remove constraints, (Plan, Do, Check, Act) in a focused factory environment</li>
<li>
Participate in the Plant Leadership, (Director of Operations, Director of Assembly, Director of Engineering &amp; Director of Human Resources) to direct a transformation of culture focused on customer service, continuous improvement &amp; cost reductions</li>
<li>
Identify gaps between the plant and current production capacity and develop plans with peers to have the right capacity in place to meet the plan</li>
<li>
Evaluate performance of all team members and assess organizational fit, identify opportunities for improvement &amp; ensure the development of future leaders</li>
<li>
Review process audits &amp; create plans to mitigate gaps. Participate in Corporate cross functional teams for performance review, strategy, plant compliance &amp; process improvement</li>
<li>
Ensure that there is a process in place to manage the parameters, data and integrity of information in information systems</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
