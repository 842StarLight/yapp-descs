<h2>IT Managed Services Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p><i>The role of the Dedicated IT Managed Services Sales Executive is to sell managed IT solutions (computer hardware, software and monitoring/management services) to small and medium sized current customers and new clients. This role is highly engaged with the customer to provide a predictable, budgeted IT spend enabling their business to grow, keep technology current and provide any/all future IT solutions in their environment. </i></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for generating own leads and planning daily activity to reach the most potential customers each day</li>
<li>Identify prospects who meet ideal customer criteria (small to mid-sized businesses of 20-150 users, Windows Active Directory and Windows client/server operating systems)</li>
<li>Works with internal team to develop technology solution</li>
<li>Maintain and move ideal customers through sales funnel to consistently achieve sales quota</li>
<li>Conduct pre-call planning to identify opportunities and call objective</li>
<li>Secure appointments with CEO, CFO, COO or business owner for initial qualifying meetings</li>
<li>Conduct initial qualifying meetings to confirm ideal customer criteria and gain understanding of customer needs and expectations relative to Managed IT services</li>
<li>Confirm need and gain customer commitment to move forward in the process</li>
<li>Completes customer needs assessment</li>
<li>Writes proposals/statement of work for customer needs and proposed solutions</li>
<li>Maintain and update CRM database</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS </b>(SKILLS, KNOWLEDGE AND ABILITIES REQUIRED)</p>
<ul>
<li>Attention to detail, good organizational skills, able to manage multiple priorities</li>
<li>Able to work in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate technical information/training in laymen terms</li>
<li>Is a reliable source of information</li>
<li>Maintains skills as technology advances</li>
<li>Collaborates with internal and external resources throughout sale process</li>
<li>Excellent listener</li>
<li>Above average presentation skills</li>
<li>Effectively uses a consultative sales approach and closes deals</li>
<li>Understands common SMB business/technology needs and able to develop well thought out questions and answers to address needs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Business, MIS or equivalent</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of selling managed IT services</li>
<li>Previous work history with MST or IT consulting firm or support experience in Information Technology preferred</li>
<li>Additional OS knowledge (i.e. Linux, Unix, MAC OS) and A+, N+, MCP and relevant certifications a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 3 years (Required)</li></ul>
