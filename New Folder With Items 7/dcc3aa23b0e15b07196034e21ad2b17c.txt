<h2>Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide the highest quality of service to the customer at all times, set example for immediate and hotel wide staff.</li>
<li>Develop a Marketing Plan by gathering historical and competitive data, then setting goals and determining action plans to meet those goals.</li>
<li>Work with the General Manager in preparing the annual sales and marketing budget. Update action plans and financial objectives quarterly.</li>
<li>Develop new business through obtaining accounts from competition, lateral development of existing accounts and contacting new customers in the market. The solicitation of new and saturation of existing business should be through a combination of in-house appointments, telephone, and personal calls.</li>
<li>Entertain and maintain close relations with the major accounts tourism and business associations, and community leaders to develop positive rapport and ensure repeat business.</li>
<li>Prepare and interpret month end reports. Monitor and analyze trends so we are always prepared and proactive to changes rather than re-active. Know your competitors business and ensure hotel is receiving its fair share.</li>
<li>Suggest and provide advertising or promotional support, holiday packages, corporate clubs, etc.</li>
<li>Complete weekly reports and submit those required to the General Manager.</li>
<li>Attend any brand required training as well as other meetings requested by the General Manager.</li>
<li>Inform General Manager of potential opportunities/concerns with clients and progress of special projects.</li>
<li>Monitor room inventory and rate programs be knowledgeable of occupancy, average rate, and REVPAR goals.</li>
<li>Perform various other duties as assigned to meet business objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Hotel Management, Business and/or Marketing degree.</li>
<li>Minimum of three (3) years hotel sales management experience required.</li>
<li>Experience with hotel sales software such as SalesPro preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Hotel Sales: 2 years (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Additional Compensation:</p>
<ul><li>Bonuses</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
