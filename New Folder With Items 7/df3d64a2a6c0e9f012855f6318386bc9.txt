Job Description</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
At Vail Resorts, we’re looking for litigators who want to continue honing their skills, but in an in-house setting. We’re looking for someone to handle all aspects of a case from initial investigation, pleadings, written discovery, depositions, motion practice, and, yes, even trial work. In the last several years, we have handled nearly a dozen trials and arbitrations, in which our in-house litigators played a significant role.</div>
<div>
Vail Resorts has a full service legal department that handles all aspects of the varied and complex legal issues faced by a publicly traded company. The legal department is made up of numerous specialized teams, including a Litigation Team that handles and oversees litigation and claims ranging from guest liability matters associated with year-round recreational activities, to a broad range of commercial disputes, as well as regulatory and governmental matters.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As part of the Vail Resorts Litigation Team, Litigation Counsel will be primarily responsible for handling claims and lawsuits involving all companies in the Vail Resorts organization. While we do work with many outside lawyers in the multitude of domestic and international jurisdictions in which we do business, we feel strongly that the both our company and our individual lawyers benefit from our litigation team developing and maintaining the skills necessary to be effective litigators as well as strong business advocates. Accordingly, your role will include a mixture of overseeing outside counsel and being lead counsel yourself on numerous matters that are handled internally:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Handle guest liability / personal injury and commercial matters, which includes investigating claims; interviewing witnesses; drafting answers; drafting briefs/motions; drafting/responding to discovery; taking/defending depositions; conducting hearings and trial; handling settlement conferences and mediations; and handling appeals;</li>
<li>
Manage outside counsel handling all types of litigation, including development and management of budgets and alternative fee arrangements; provision of guidance on strategy and tactics of defense; and direct involvement in cases, as advisable;</li>
<li>
Become deeply involved and engaged with the business, supporting the Litigation Team, and business leaders on litigation issues;</li>
<li>
Proactively consider lessons learned for each matter and monitor trends in claims and litigation both inside and outside of the Company and advise and communicate regarding same within the Legal Department and with the business unit leaders;</li>
<li>
Provide advice and counsel with respect to Company activities, processes, and documentation, as requested;</li>
<li>
Conduct other litigation related tasks including effectively managing electronic discovery and legal holds, responding to subpoenas, and defending depositions of employees in third-party cases.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
