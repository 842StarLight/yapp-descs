<h2>Tennis Pro Shop Assistant</h2>

<p>Functional Responsibilities:</p>
<p>AdministrativeDuties</p>
<p>· Adheres to weekly schedules to ensureproper coverage. Reviews time sheets andother payroll documentation for accuracy and submits as appropriate.</p>
<p>· Administersreceipt and dispatch of services, inventory, and supplies according to companypolicy.</p>
<p>· Consistentlyanswers phone and emails and delivers messages to the appropriate personal.</p>
<p>· Maintainscleanliness and maintenance within Pro Shop and Fitness Center.</p>
<p>Tennis Pro Shop Sales andMerchandising</p>
<p>· Performs basic operations activitiesincluding cash handling and reporting, price changes, merchandise handling, andopen and closing duties</p>
<p>· Engages in sales, inventory receipt, classbilling and performing services for members including court, clinic, and eventreservations.</p>
<p>· Maintainsan awareness of customers to both increase service levels and reduce securityrisks; remains current on operational policies regarding payment and exchanges,and security practices.</p>
<p>· Modelssales expectations by utilizing various techniques and communicating productknowledge to the members; recommends merchandise selections or helps to locateor obtain merchandise based on member needs and desires.</p>
<p>· Maintainsstandards in merchandise handling, presentation, loss prevention, and all otherduties as directed by Management.</p>
<p>· Participates in andfacilitates visual directives including store sets and merchandisereplenishment.</p>
<p>Tennis Courts</p>
<p>· Records court and clinicreservations. Follows up with billing asdirected by Management.</p>
<p>· Basic understanding of clay and hard courtreadiness and playability. Communicateswith Facility Management to ensure that courts are ready for play upon memberrequest. Contacts members if courts willnot be playable at time requested.</p>
<p>· Ensures proper set up of ball machine uponmember request. Store machine properlyafter play is completed.</p>
<p>· Attends match days as necessary. Assistfood runners and follows Food and Beverage protocols.</p>
<p>Fitness Center</p>
<p>· Schedulesgroup fitness classes monthly. Ensures employeesand membership are aware of changes to group fitness schedule.</p>
<p>· Assistspersonal trainers and Management in advertising new classes.</p>
<p>· Managesreservations and billing for group fitness classes.</p>
<p>· Providesmembers with appropriate information to schedule personal or buddytraining. Bills personal and buddingtraining as required.</p>
<p>Message and StretchTherapy</p>
<p>· Provides members with appropriateinformation to schedule message or stretch therapy session. Bills sessions as required.</p>
<p>· Maintains appropriate volume in Tennis ProShop when message or stretch therapy session is in progress.</p>
<p>· Assists therapist in advertising specialsand events.</p>
<p>Events and Functions</p>
<p>· Maintainsknowledge of current specials, events, and policies both within the Pro Shopand at the Clubhouse.</p>
<p>· Assisting Management in advertising,planning, and setting up events.</p>
<p>· Runs food from Clubhouse to Tennis ProShop and oversees serving and clean up as required.</p>
<p>MemberServices</p>
<p>§ Promotesmember service by greeting and assisting members; responds to member inquiriesand complaints in a professional and timely manner.</p>
<p>§ Builds and maintains a solid professional relationship withmembership</p>
<p>§ Able to identify areas of improvement to enhance the memberexperience.</p>
