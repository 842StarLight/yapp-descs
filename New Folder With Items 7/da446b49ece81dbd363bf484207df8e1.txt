<h2>Strategic Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Scope of Position:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage a portfolio of accounts athigh level to ensure customer satisfaction and retention</li>
<li>Using the required documents and in accordance with the SalesForce Queue Policy, clearly and concisely present spec changes and IT issues and projects to IT and Change Management via SalesForce</li>
<li>Track and monitor IT projects in the SalesForce and Target Process queues and manage ETAs (internally and<br>
externally)</li>
<li>Coordinate internal involvement in order to meet client expectations (IT, Ops, Client Services, etc.)</li>
<li>Work closely with Client Services to ensure client satisfaction and problem resolution and keep Client Services apprised of account changes through regular phone and email communications.</li>
<li>Schedule and facilitate regular calls (how often depends on the client)- the purpose of the calls is to discuss program health, best practices, new products/initiatives, etc.</li>
<li>Client specific training for new and existing CSRs</li>
<li>Annual top to bottom account analysis-spec review, enhancement recommendations, proposals (system and spec), YOY analysis, SLAs- were they met, why, suggestions to improve if not met, contract commitments- were they met for the year, why, etc.</li>
<li>Prepare Quarterly Business Review (QBR) Presentations including background statistics (run through CART),<br>
program enhancements, pending projects, industry trends, and other topics as required by the client</li>
<li>Attend on-site QBR’s as needed</li>
<li>Ensure QBR Presentations and QBR notes are uploaded in Salesforce and to the Wiki in a timely manner</li>
<li>With the help of the CSR’s, ensure that the Wiki is continually updated and documented (i.e. Specs, fee<br>
schedules, etc.)</li>
<li>Recognize and capitalize on opportunities to upsell</li>
<li>Track SLAs and ensure contract commitments are met</li>
<li>Prepare EOM reports in accordance with provided requirements</li>
<li>Track and coordinate contract renewal as needed</li>
<li>Other assigned tasks on an as needed basis</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education &amp; Qualification Requirements</b>:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED Required.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred, or 1-3 years in a similar support role</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office required</li>
 </ul>
<p><b>Required Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven account management or other relevant experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate, present and influence credibly and effectively at all levels</li>
<li>Experience in delivering client-focused solutions based on customer needs</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage multiple projects at a time while paying strict attention to detail</li>
<li>Excellent listening, documentation and presentation skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated and able to thrive in a results-driven environment</li>
<li>Natural relationship builder with integrity, reliability and maturity</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize among competing tasks</li>
<li>Critical thinking and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Excellent time and project management skills.</li>
<li>Keen attention to detail and adherence to deadlines</li>
<li>Expert at Microsoft Office Suite, Google Apps, Salesforce and help desk support software</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>**EASTERN AND CENTRAL TIME ZONE CANDIDATES ONLY**</p>
