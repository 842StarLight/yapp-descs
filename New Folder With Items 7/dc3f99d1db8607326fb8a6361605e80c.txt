Description</div>
<div>
<div>
The Clinical Application Specialist provides the necessary training and education to customers in the Pre-analytical, Analytical and Post-analytical operation of their instruments.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:</div>
<div>
Provide continuing education to Radiometer customers. This would include advance training product workshops in assigned region.<br>
Respond to customer needs concerning clinical questions or problems.<br>
Establish a routine and continual follow up for current and prospective customers to determine satisfaction with Radiometer products.<br>
Assist in installations and demonstrations as needed to support sales and SITs.<br>
Perform various duties as directed by the Senior Manager, Commercial Excellence and Regional Sales Manager.</div>
<p>
</p>
