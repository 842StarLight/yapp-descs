<div><b>
Sales Executive 1 Fiber</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Overall Purpose:<div>
This role is a dedicated Fiber Based Broadband Acquisition seller to proactively support new fiber building sales opportunities.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Key Roles/Responsibilities:</div>
<div>
Responsible for generating new revenue from customers in buildings where AT&amp;T pre-deploys network assets like Fiber/Ethernet multiplexing.</div>
<div>
Primary responsibility of the position will be to sell AT&amp;T strategic products/services as detailed in the respective compensation plan title abstracts to customers within all AT&amp;T segments according to current Rules of Engagement and customers with competitors in the marketplace.</div>
<div>
From a technical standpoint, the position will verify AT&amp;T technical architecture understanding of property, and responsible for updating internal fiber assets accurately.</div>
<div>
From a negotiation standpoint, the position owns the relationship with the property manager, securing AT&amp;T ability to solicit customers in exchange for advanced network assets.</div>
<div>
The position interfaces with customers/across account teams to properly serve/approach customers.</div>
<div>
Position performance based on new sales revenue, customer satisfaction, maximizing building penetration to ensure a return on pre-positioned network assets.</div>
<div>
The position will work at a local level with engineers to leverage new architecture providing a superior customer experience.</div>
<div>
Responsible for meeting all customers within the Fiber Based Broadband building, identifying opportunities, proposing and closing sales, and may require collaboration with the customer assigned account team.</div>
<div>
Achieve sales objectives by structuring/securing new high-speed data services with customers located in designated Fiber Based Broadband buildings.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This job key applies to employees spending more than 50% of their time performing duties directly related to making sales away from their offices at customers place of business/home.</div>
<div>
Duties directly related to making sales includes: Meeting with customers engaged in sales activities at the customers site, communicating with customers via phone, teleconference, e-mail, etc., related to proposed solution/sale etc., traveling to and from the customers premise, preparing proposals, presentations, or bids, developing strategic plans related to the customer and the proposed solution/sale, researching and developing customer solutions with ATT external partners, including design and engineering, and researching customer business and industry to identify new sales opportunities.</div>
<div>
Education: Typically requires a Bachelor degree.</div>
<div>
Experience: Typically requires 2-5 years direct work experience.</div>
<div>
Supervisory: No.</div>
