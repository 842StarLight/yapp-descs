<b>Organization Overview</b><br>
For over 70 years Buckland has been working to help companies across the world experience global trade in a better way. Buckland employs a unique approach to global trade that focuses on combining trusted technologies and talented people that know how to use them. As a customer-focused company, Buckland provides customers with a single source of unmatched Customs Brokerage (Canadian, US, Mexican), Trade Managed Solutions, Freight Forwarding, Trade Technologies and Warehousing/Distribution Services. Buckland strives to provide customized services in a memorable way.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Position Summary</b></div>
<div>
To perform the duties of Supervisor of Southbound Operations, the candidate will supervise the Laredo Southbound Operations team, working with the Team Leaders over each department / shift. They must be able to solve problems and work without direct supervision and be able to guide, direct, and delegate. Coordinate the process of documentation handling and advances for clearance, monitoring and control of all entry/entry summary preparation to ensure shipments are entered in accordance with the MX Regulations. The candidate must provide the highest level of customer service to our valued customers. They must be willing to communicate directly with other Supervisors, Team Leaders, and the MX Operations Manager and filter communication thru to the operations staff.</div>
