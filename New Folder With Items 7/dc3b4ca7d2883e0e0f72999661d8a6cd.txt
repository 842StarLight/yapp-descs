<h2>Senior Full-Stack Web Developer (Software Engineer)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>As a proud advocate for manufacturing in the U.S.A., Kenall has become a champion in developing advanced lighting control systems widely used in parking garages/lots, manufacturing facilities, warehouses, stairwells and multi-purpose rooms. The control systems monitor the environment, manage light levels, and provide notification services from our lighting fixtures. Image sensing technology integrated into the system also allows for additional video analytics to be released in future products.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Kenall has an open position for a <b>Senior Full-Stack Web Developer (Software Engineer) </b>who will be a key member of the new product development team.</div>
