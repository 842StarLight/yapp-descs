<h2>Lead Food Technologist- Projects</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div><h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details</b></h2></div>
<div><h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Description</b></h3></div>
<p></p>
<ul>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<li>
Serve as a Project Leader for commercialization projects from ideation through final execution at plant and distribution to customer</li>
<li>
Assist in the development and maintenance of a detailed project plan to monitor and track project progress</li>
<li>
Develop comprehensive project timelines to be shared with team members and customers</li>
<li>
Coordinate meetings and project follow ups with R&amp;D, Marketing/Sales, Plant Operations, Supply Chain, Quality, Graphics, Purchasing, and Finance to ensure all opportunities are on course to meet the customer’s expectations and deadlines</li>
<li>
Assist with new product development from concept to scale up including formulation and prototype development</li>
<li>
Provide oral and written progress updates to stakeholders and business leaders. Must be able to clearly articulate technical product information in a format that is understandable to non-technical audiences.</li>
<li>
Report and escalate to management as needed</li>
<li>
Use and continually develop leadership skills</li>
<li>
Perform other related duties as assigned</li>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education and Experience Required:</b></p>
<li>
BS Food Science, Food Technology, or related scientific field</li>
<li>
4+ years’ work experience</li>
<li>
Combination of project leadership and food product development experience</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>
Strong project management skills</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to lead multi-faceted teams, provide clear priorities and expectations</li>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred Education, Experience, and Skills:</b></p>
<li>
Knowledge of Food Safety – FSMA, HACCP, GMPs, etc.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of regulatory compliance for nutritional labeling</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of the Code of Federal Regulations</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams</li>
<li>
Strong time management, planning and organizational skills</li>
<li>
High level of accuracy and attention to detail</li>
<li>
Entry level knowledge of JDE EnterpriseOne software</li>
<li>
Entry level knowledge of Wrike project management software</li>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Physical requirements</b></p>
<p>
Qualified candidates must be able to perform the following physical job requirements, with or without reasonable accommodation:</p>
<li>
Sitting at a work station for extended periods of time</li>
<li>
Stand and walk on a frequent basis</li>
<li>
Moderate lifting of up to 25 pounds and occasional lifting up to 50 pounds</li>
<li>
Use lab equipment – scales, moisture, and others as needed</li>
<li>
Safely work in a variety of environments including office, laboratory, and/or manufacturing plant settings where occupational noise and other hazards may exist.</li>
</ul>
