<h2>Principal Product Owner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">At Qualia, we are fundamentally transforming the way real estate professionals, lenders, and homebuyers manage real estate transactions, one of the nation's largest industries. About the Role
<br>--------------
<br><br>
We're looking for an entrepreneurial product manager with astute product insight to own a new product line designed for Fortune 500 companies.
<br>----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
<br><br>
Your Day to Day
<br>---------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Own an entire, nascent product line. Owning a product at Qualia means:</li>
<li>Being the resident expert in the business opportunity, the strategy, &amp; the customer.</li>
<li>Driving execution of the product that best capitalizes on the opportunity.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with Engineering and Design to implement elegant, robust, and sophisticated web products.</li>
<li>Write clear specs that communicates problems and criteria clearly.</li>
<li>Rapidly iterate based off new information.</li>
<li>Distill complex problems into meaningful insights, constantly moving the team toward solutions.</li>
<li>Work in partnership with Sales and Customer Success to evangelize the product and gain a deeper understanding of the customer.</li>
</ul>
The Skills That Will Likely Make You a Match
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years experience building extraordinary enterprise software as a Product Manager.</li>
<li>You have been solely responsible for the success of a product line and/or delivered a product to market from scratch.</li>
<li>You have a track record of making decisions in the face of uncertainty. You use your analytical framework to validate assumptions and are open and curious to learn from outcomes.</li>
<li>You love enabling people to do their best work, whether it's the customers you are servicing or your collaborators on the engineering floor.</li>
<li>You prefer to move fast.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication.</li>
<li>Experience in the real estate, lending, or insurance industries is a plus.</li>
</ul>
