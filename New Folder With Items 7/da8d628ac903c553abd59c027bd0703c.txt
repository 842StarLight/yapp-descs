<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Dematic is a leading supplier of integrated automated technology, software and services to optimize the supply chain. Dematic employs over 7,000 skilled logistics professionals to serve its customers globally, with engineering centers and manufacturing facilities located around the world. Dematic is one brand under the KION Group of companies and has implemented more than 6,000 integrated systems for a customer base that includes small, medium and large companies doing business in a variety of market sectors.<br>
<br>
Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, Dematic is a member of KION Group, a global leader in industrial trucks, related services and supply chain solutions. Across more than 100 countries worldwide, the KION Group designs, builds and supports logistics solutions that optimize material and information flow within factories, warehouses and distribution centers. The company is the largest manufacturer of industrial trucks in Europe, the second-largest producer of forklifts globally and a leading provider of warehouse automation.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Role
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Dematic Corp. has an immediate need for an Customer Success Account Manager ito support the continual growth of Dematic’s North America Customer Service business. This position is responsible for assisting and selling Dematic’s product upgrades and solutions to current key and regional accounts.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Role and Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Manages assigned customer services contracts to:<ul>
<li>
Ensure agreed upon scope of contract is being delivered to customer.</li>
<li>
Ensure a high level of customer satisfaction occurs with each assigned customer services account.
</li>
<li>Renews all assigned customer services contract accounts each year—including quote kick-off, pricing strategy, proposal generation &amp; submission, and order entry.
</li>
<li>Proactively pursues positive net change adds for each assigned customer service account by adding services not currently provided
</li>
<li>After understanding customer and communicating with After Market account manager the customer success account manager will prepare and present other services, and products the customer will benefit from, and buy as a positive net change to the service contract</li>
<li>
Proactively communicates with key customer contacts within assigned customer service account a minimum of once per month. Communication will consist of delivering a value proposition relative to services which the customer could benefit from.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Communicates and collaborates with After Market Account Managers to share information/initiatives/personnel changes on assigned accounts and updates within SFDC accordingly.</li>
<li>
Acts as the point person for resolving service issues that may arise on assigned accounts</li>
</ul>
