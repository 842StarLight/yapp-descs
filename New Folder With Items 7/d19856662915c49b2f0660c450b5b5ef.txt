<p><b>Senior Vice President of Sales</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Senior Vice President of Sales provides leadership for the company’s sales function. As the company’s senior-most sales leader, the SVP of Sales is accountable for overall sales organization performance, the profitable achievement of the sales organization’s goals, and for aligning sales objectives with the company’s business strategy. The SVP of Sales will manage VPs and Directors of Sales for both brick &amp; mortar and ecommerce revenue streams (excluding haggar.com). The SVP of Sales will also manage the VP of RMAs (retail merchandise associates) and In-Store Visual Presentation. The SVP of Sales is a strategic thinker and data driven leader with strong financial skills.</p>
<p>The SVP of Sales reports to the President, Chief Executive Officer.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Strategic Planning</b></p>
<ul><li> Develop, implement, and manage annual and long-term strategic plans that align with the company’s strategic priorities and financial goals. Present the strategic plans to ownership, management, and the associate base at Haggar.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Align the sales organization’s objectives with the company’s business strategy through active participation in the company’s strategic planning, budgeting, and financial forecasting process.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop and execute specific strategic plans to pursue new business opportunities, working collaboratively with leaders in other functional areas including product development, research and insights, marketing/digital advertising, inventory planning, finance, and IT.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Plan and lead semi-annual national sales meetings</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare and present an annual risk assessment to ownership and management.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Financial Responsibility and Revenue and Contribution Margin Growth</b></p>
<ul><li> Plan, present, and manage all of the P&amp;L responsibilities related to the executive sales position (sales, returns and allowances, contribution margin, SG&amp;A, and other key financial metrics).</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with internal partners in product development, sourcing, inventory planning, marketing (digital, social, and traditional) and in-store merchandising to successfully launch new franchise programs, grow key categories, and seize new opportunities with current and potential retail partners (both brick &amp; mortar and ecommerce).</li></ul>
<ul><li> Meaningfully increase the company’s contribution margin dollars with retail partners (current and new) outside of the department store / mid-tier channel. Those channels include mass, clubs, brands for less, athletic/sports, and new channels.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Collaboration with Internal Teams</b></p>
<ul><li> Collaborate with the Chief Digital Officer (CDO) to grow ecommerce and omni-channel sales with an optimized digital marketing investment. Deliver meaningful YOY sales and contribution margin growth with our retail partners, Amazon, and new pure play customers. Collaborate with the CDO on ecommerce updates to ownership, the CEO, and management.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Partner with the Chief Merchant to prepare and conduct quarterly product selling reviews with sales, planning, design, marketing, and other areas.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Partner with SVP Planning and Analytics to effectively manage and control the company’s total SKU count, inventory levels and excess inventory</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Work collaboratively with teams in IT/BI, Research and Insights, Inventory Planning (VMI), and Finance to utilize technology and data analytics to assess, manage, and plan the business on a regular and ongoing basis. Lead the sales organization to become a data-driven team that continually utilizes the most effective technology to generate on-demand, valuable information to guide decision-making.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with our Research &amp; Insights team to understand the current Haggar consumer, other target demographics, and important consumer trends of today and tomorrow. Establish targets for market share gains as well as absolute unit and dollar growth in key categories.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborate with the VP Creative Director and Brand to develop and execute Haggar’s brand strategy and vision</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead the sales organization to be effective and timely communicators of all relevant information to internal functional teams.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>External Partners</b></p>
<ul><li> Spend considerable time face-to-face with existing and potential retail partners to develop relationships and plan meaningful and profitable growth strategies for all parties.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work closely with our licensees to help maximize their presence in-store and online and partner with them to present the Haggar brand in the most effective and impactful manner with retail partners.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work closely with the VP of RMAs and In-Store Visual Presentation to manage and optimize the RMA team through a data driven model.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Monitor retailer, market, and competitive activity and provide updates to the CEO, management, and other key players on our team.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Training and Talent Development</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Provide essential leadership for training, skillset development, collaboration/partnership building within the organization, and other efforts to continually improve and enhance the hard and soft skills of the sales team.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage the sales organization’s performance management process. This includes the establishment of sales organizational principles for managing performance, prioritizing performance measures for all sales and other jobs, and leading the structured discipline of professional development and two-way feedback.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for ensuring all associates in the sales organization are held accountable for assigned responsibilities and results.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide leadership to the sales team, while fostering a culture of accountability, professional development, high-performance, and ethical behavior.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Organizational Alignment</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Reports to the President, Chief Executive Officer</li></ul>
<ul><li> Member of the Executive Committee</li></ul>
<ul><li> Direct reports include Vice Presidents of Sales, Directors of Sales, and VP of RMAs and In-Store Visual Presentation.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Bachelor's Degree from an accredited college or university</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills, Knowledge, Abilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 15 years of sales management</li>
<li>Experience working with manufacturers and/or retailers in all (or most) of the following channels: mid-tier and department stores, mass, brands for less, clubs, and specialty</li>
<li>Experience profitably growing ecommerce in an omni-channel environment</li>
<li>Highly proficient understanding of Retail Math</li>
<li>Leadership and managerial skills, including project management assignments</li>
<li>Strong negotiation skills</li>
<li>Advanced working knowledge of Outlook and Microsoft Office Suite products including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Effective and compelling presentation skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and problem-solving skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Working Conditions and Physical Demands:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to travel frequently on domestic trips (30% - 40% of the time).</li>
<li>Ability to represent the company at various industry functions, community events, and business dinners.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Core Behavioral Attributes</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to problem-solve, multi-task and manage time appropriately</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and ability to effectively manage conflict</li>
<li>Detail oriented and excellent follow-through skills</li>
<li>Ability to analyze, interpret, and share information effectively and promptly</li>
<li>Highly motivated, professional, &amp; dependable</li>
<li>Contribute positively to the team dynamic; self-motivated to support the entire organization.</li>
<li>Practiced in written communication as well as verbal communication</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Accountabilities and Performance Measures</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Achieves, or exceeds, assigned organizational objectives for sales, contribution margin, expense management, and other financial and strategic goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supports the achievement of strategic objectives critical to all functional areas within the company.</li></ul>
