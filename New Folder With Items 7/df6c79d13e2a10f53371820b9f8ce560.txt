<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>The Proposal Administrator creates RFI/RFQ/RFP proposals for a national sales team. This position will produce and deliver proposals tailored to each customer in a fast paced, multi-tasking environment by utilizing our proposal library and software system. The Proposal Administrator is responsible for coordinating and servicing all aspects of a project-based proposal. This person will also play a crucial role in reviewing and maintaining the companyâ€™s proposal knowledge base archive.</p>
<p><b>Job Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as central point of contact for RFI/RFQ/RFP/Quotes.</li>
<li>Follow up with Sales executives and conducting fact-finding calls as necessary</li>
<li>Provide daily status updates for all ongoing activities.</li>
<li>Work with SMEs and content providers to monitor deadlines and ensure proposal completion and submittal</li>
<li>Compile all content into proposal/quote response, review and update as needed</li>
<li>Manage electronic proposal files and maintain knowledge base</li>
<li>Write and lay out proposals and quote documents as necessary</li>
<li>Assist with management of the proposal content library, including ongoing editorial maintenance and quality control of all materials used in RFP responses</li>
<li>Complete pre-qualification documentation processing as may be required</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned or required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>Minimally two years related experience required. Bachelors degree or equivalent preferred. Intermediate skills with Microsoft Office and Adobe suite required, advanced skills preferred. Ability to multitask managing multiple projects with varying deadlines and work on cloud-based systems required. Must be able to prioritize and be timely with submittals and work proactively. Strong work ethic and ability to work independently and as a part of a team required. Excellent writing, editing, and communication skills essential.</p>
