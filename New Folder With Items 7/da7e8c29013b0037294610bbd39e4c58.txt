<h2>Participant Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Towson, MD</b></h2>
<p>The 401(k)/403(b) Participant Advisor will be part of the team that provides on-demand financial planning and fiduciary investment advice to the employees of our corporate retirement plan clients. You will work on a 1:1 basis determine the financial needs of clients and to provide advice and make recommendations on products and services that best meet their needs. Duties may include assessing assets, liabilities, cash flow, insurance coverage, tax status, and financial objectives to establish investment strategies. The candidate will be working as part of the firm’s Institutional Client Group.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Review and provide on-demand financial planning recommendations and analysis</li>
<li>Review and recommend 401(k) plan investment strategies</li>
<li>Lead 1:1 participant meetings (in-person or via web/phone conference)</li>
<li>Conduct group education sessions (in-person and/or webinars) on various financial planning topics and issues</li>
<li>Utilize Greenspring’s financial wellness and fiduciary advice platform to engage and support employees</li>
<li>Work with the Institutional Client Group to track and report on activities and outcomes for ongoing client management</li>
<li>Interact with plan sponsors and retirement plan committees in both the pre-sales and post-sales process</li>
<li>Document all activities in Salesforce</li>
<li>Embody Greenspring’s Core Purpose and Core Values</li>
</ul>
