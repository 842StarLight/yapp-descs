<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>What we offer</b></p>
<p>You will work on exciting and challenging topics together with a team in an ultra-modern, innovative and creative environment. Intensive on-the-job training with expert colleagues guarantees you will quickly become familiar with your duties and perform them independently. Performance related pay and the opportunity for personal and professional development are of course part of the package. Since 2009 Evonik Industries AG has been certified as a family-friendly company by the German Hertie Foundation.</p>
<p><b>Click here to learn more about Evonik from our employees</b></p>
<p>The primary responsibility for this role is to manage customer orders from receipt to shipment and invoicing. This includes communicating the needs of our customers internally to business unit management, production and logistics. pricing, etc.</p>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide Customer Service - Accepting and amending all orders. Maintenance of databases including successfully entering orders in SAP. Daily review of open order, duplicate order, to ensure customer requirements and delivery dates are met. Customer relations including problem resolution by phone, email or in person, and new customer processing. Enter any related customer complaints into SAP.</li>
<li>General Administrative Support - maintain customer files. Assist sales and marketing with business data as required. Back-up to Customer Service Asst. with departmental mail. When both the Customer Service Asst and Receptionist are out, CSR is responsible for running Open order, Invoice, Duplicate order and Invoice tracking reports.</li>
<li>Provide backup to other customer service representatives for vacation and/or sick time coverage.</li>
<li>Participate in department workgroups, Safety stories and Safety presentations as assigned to help improve customer service processes and interactions with other departments and the Safety Culture at Evonik</li>
<li>Phone system: positive pleasant voice for answering phone calls</li>
<li>Customer Service Skills:
<ul>
<li>Respond to customer inquiries</li>
<li>Enter customer complaints</li>
<li>Enter customer sales orders</li>
<li>Expedite &amp; track shipments</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and teamwork skills</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and multi task</li>
<li>Ability to work in a high-stress complex atmosphere</li>
<li>Drive customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Ability to communicate verbally &amp; electronically</li>
<li>Orders received via, phone, fax, email, EDI, Elemica<br>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-3 years of experience and/or training in Customer Service</li>
<li>HS Diploma or equivalent; or Associates Degree or Training Certification in related area</li>
<li>Experience in working with customers in a business environment. Works from established guidelines and procedures under general supervision.</li>
<li>Understanding logistics/Operations and impact of fulfilling customer's order. Must have high level of interpersonal skills. Must have strong negotiation skills, problem solving skills, demonstrated oral and written communication skills; demonstrated ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously in a fast-paced environment, and the ability to work independently and as a team member.</li>
<li>Knowledge of printers, computers and general office equipment necessary.<br>
Knowledge of and/or the ability to learn a variety of computer software applications in word processing, database, and presentation software (MS Word, Excel, Access, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>SAP experience preferred<br>
</li>
</ul>
