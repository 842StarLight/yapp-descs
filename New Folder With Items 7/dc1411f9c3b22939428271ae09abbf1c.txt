<div>Job Summary:
<p><b>Summary and Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
The Financial Analyst provides planning, analysis, and decision making support and works closely with the Finance Director in formulating the financial aspects of the annual plan and forecast for the North America operations. The Financial Analyst also assists with month end close activities primarily in the area of Accounts Receivable working closely with the Credit and Collections Manager.</p>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead the forecasting and planning cadence for the North America operations and process in coordination with the Finance Director and Senior Manager, FP&amp;A</li>
<li>
Work closely with managers throughout the organization to prepare sales, profit and departmental spending forecasts</li>
<li>
Provide ad-hoc analysis and support as needed</li>
<li>
Perform weekly/monthly cash flow analysis</li>
<li>
Perform analytical procedures during month end close</li>
<li>
Analyze and post certain banking transactions</li>
<li>
Maintain intercompany subsidiary ledger</li>
<li>
Prepare and post standard and non-standard journal entries</li>
<li>
Perform and consolidate sales backlog data for the division</li>
<li>
Reconcile general ledger accounts including accounts receivable and customer advances</li>
<li><div>
Perform other reasonably related tasks as assigned by management</div></li>
</ul>
Requirements:

Experience working with Hyperion</div></li>
