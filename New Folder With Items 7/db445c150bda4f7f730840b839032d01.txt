<h2>Account Manager - 163082</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The Account Manager serves as the primary representative and liaison for accounts and develops and executes plans to grow business in assigned territory.<br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.<br>
1. Responsible for soliciting, developing, and maintaining favorable contracts with all potential managed care accounts in territory.<br>
2. Responsible for forecasting and achieving KFP (Key Financial Performance) Targets and meet company KFP thresholds.<br>
3. Responsible for assisting with the onboarding of new Sales Employees which includes hands on training in the field;<br>
4. Promotes company products and services with physicians, case managers, social workers, hospital personnel, home health agencies and other traditional referral sources in a professional and creative manner.<br>
5. Must be prepared with quarterly strategic plans on top ten active prospective accounts.<br>
