<div>The MetLife Global Brand &amp; Marketing (GBM) team has been evolving in line with MetLife’s transformation to a company that delivers greater value for our customers, employees and shareholders, Today, GBM is a strategic, customer-focused marketing practice that is dedicated to driving business impact across the globe. With a focus on customer led value propositions, relevant customer engagement and fostering a purpose driven brand, the team is committed to enhancing MetLife’s relationship with its customers and helping them navigate life’s twists and turns. GBM has 450+ employees in global, regional and local roles.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p><b>
The Role</b>:</p>
<p>
This role sits within the Global Marketing Strategy &amp; Sciences (GMSS) function. This group has overall responsibility for identifying insights to define customer, channel and portfolio strategies to better anticipate and deliver on customer needs and marketing opportunities, while measuring impact and standards. In addition, this team oversees MetLife Brand Strategy and the planning process for Marketing globally.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Within GMSS, the Global Marketing Strategy &amp; Planning team is focused on identifying and defining strategies that enable the MetLife brand to anticipate and deliver on customer needs, perceptions and motivations that are critical to driving stronger customer relationships that contribute to growth. Our approach to strategy focuses on combining deep customer insights (and analytics) with a pragmatic, operationally-focused approach to problem-solving.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>As a global Center of Excellence, the group is responsible for driving marketing strategy and planning excellence through expertise, capabilities and support to enable:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Customer solutions via differentiated value propositions that address key customer needs</li>
<li>
Customer loyalty strategies that increase persistency, renewal, up/cross-sell and referrals</li>
<li>
Customer demand strategies to increase purchase intent and generate new sales</li>
<li>
Planning discipline to align marketing programs and resources to drive the greatest impact against business priorities and objectives</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The AVP, Global Marketing Strategy, will operate as a strategic marketer with responsibility to advance MetLife’s core strategic goal to deliver the right solutions to the right customers, with emphasis on the “customer.” He or she will build stakeholder support and drive One MetLife alignment in a highly-matrixed organization through direct and influence leadership.</p>
