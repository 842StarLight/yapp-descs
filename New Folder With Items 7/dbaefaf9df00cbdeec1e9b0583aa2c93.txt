<h2>Senior Director, Sales Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary:</b></h3>
<p>We have a new opportunity for a Senior Director of Sales Operations to play a critical role in achieving Stratasan’s ambitious growth objectives. This role will support these objectives by optimizing critical business processes, leading and developing the outbound sales program, and ensuring consistent pipeline management. This requires collaboration with cross-functional departments such as Sales, Marketing, Product, Customer Success, and Stratasan’s leadership teams. A competitive spirit, motivation, integrity, vision, teamwork, and passion are essential to success in this role.</p>
<p>
This is a fantastic opportunity to support a fast growing company where your responsibilities will have a direct impact on overall success. This role will report to the Senior Vice President of Sales and will work closely with senior leadership to drive and direct growth and success.</p>
