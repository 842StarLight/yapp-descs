As a global talent hub, we are working every day to hire and groom the best talent in Media Analytics. <b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b> Build trust-based relationships with Client Service and Nielsen clients; works independently to build relationships within and across Nielsen, MA and client organizations by understanding clients and the marketplace, leveraging prior experience and client service acumen. Partner with leadership within the client vertical/team to develop a comprehensive and achievable strategic plan for the business within a client account. Contribute to and maintain revenue pipeline. Present opportunities to help clients grow their business through consultative selling skills; grow revenue through sales of incremental products within established client accounts. Act as an internal champion for the client by working collaboratively with internal partners and the team to coordinate efforts for clients where appropriate: case resolution management, contract renewals and amendments, pipeline management and other support tasks. Have a comprehensive understanding of each MA product and solution and how it can be used to improve business performance specific to unique client needs. Independently create and deliver captivating client presentations, with a strong presence that inspires confidence &amp; credibility. Prospect to identify the most valuable leads and be proactive in anticipating client needs. Serve as a mentor and guide Research Analyst support team to drive increasing value in client deliverables. Engage in other responsibilities as needed.
<br><br>
<b>A LITTLE BIT ABOUT THE ROLE:</b><br>
The Media Analytics Client Solutions – Manager/Director will consult with clients to provide media targeting, cross-platform measurement solutions, and insights that address client business questions. The primary client base is National Broadcast, Cable and Content Producer clients. For a specific engagement, the Manager/Director will identify relevant solutions to meet client’s needs, and work with relevant business partners on proposals and business development. In addition, the Manager/Director will oversee the delivery of services to clients, and present findings to the client, ensuring they receive maximum value from deliverables. The ideal candidate will possess a demonstrated track record in driving solutions-oriented growth, building key relationships both internally and externally, listening for and uncovering client needs, delivering robust presentations and client communications, adapting to the needs of a dynamic, ever-changing environment, and developing key contacts in the media industry.
<br><br>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b> 8-15 years in media or marketing research, tv network/studio or digital company, advertising agency, or other media company Bachelor’s Degree Industry knowledge - Intermediate/advanced media industry knowledge; understands client landscape to proactively pitch Nielsen solutions to clients Analytics - Analytical aptitude; ability to add depth/insight to projects delivered by adding client/industry context; Ability to balance fitting analytics into client framework/perspective while also challenging assumptions Consultative Skills - Build and manage expectations and relationships with all stakeholders; Organized, with the ability to prioritize and juggle multiple projects at once Internal/External Communication - Team player mentality; working cross-functionally is second nature; Actively listens to client challenges, proactively provides insight to clients in anticipation of their questions and needs; translates needs appropriately Sales Skills - Intermediate negotiation skills; understands and can adapt to various negotiation strategies; Ability to forecast revenue goals, track performance, and manage pipeline; Focuses on the most promising prospects, contributes to account planning Innovative Product/Tech Savvy Mindset - Leads and socializes ideas to generate group/company-wide momentum; Keeps up-to-date with the latest tech trends and understands what tools and resources our clients use to do their work
