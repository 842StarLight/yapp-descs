<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Head of Product role is ultimately responsible for the growth and success of our product portfolio. This includes the ongoing enhancement of existing legacy and modern products, as well as new customer-focused innovation.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Synthesizes market intelligence, client insights and competitive information to drive our product marketing strategy and competitive positioning
</li>
<li>Prioritizes our investments in product roadmap in order to maximize License revenue potential and minimize client attrition.
</li>
<li>Directs Product Management resources to maintain and enhance existing product lines to market leadership position within their target segment
</li>
<li>Supports the Sales function with technical sales capability
</li>
<li>Engages the industry to share and create thought leadership in order to enhance Greenstone’s brand in the marketplace as a ‘Partner of choice’
</li>
<li>Owns the performance of the product-based P&amp;L versus forecast, and creates forecasts to support the QSR process in collaboration with the Sales Manager and Client Success Director.
</li>
<li>Maintains the Company’s relationships with 3rd party vendors, specifically around areas such as maintaining and enhancing any integrations to 3rd party systems, and process flow for provisioning software on behalf of clients from 3rd party vendors.
</li>
</ul>
