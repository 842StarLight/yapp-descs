<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">GISinc is a small business whose core purpose is to create insight through spatial technology. Since 1991, our team of professionals has been using Geographic Information Systems to solve staffing, technology, and business needs in markets ranging from Federal, State, and Local Governments to some of the largest public and privately-held companies in the world. If you are passionate about achieving results by using your technical skills to sell solutions, please submit your resume using the link below.
<br><b>Role:</b> Business Development Manager
<br><br>
<b>Role Summary:</b><br>
The Business Development Manager will be responsible for driving business development activities for the Enterprise Business Unit focusing on State &amp; Local Government agencies. The role is responsible for identifying and pursuing new revenue opportunities to expand the current client base throughout the Pacific NW. In addition, the Business Development Manager will leverage existing clients as well as relationships with business partners to identify new revenue opportunities to achieve annual sales goal. Qualified candidates will use their knowledge of leading GIS technologies to grow the business, hit revenue targets and provide existing and potential clients with guidance on technology trends and solutions.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Be a hunter! Aggressively prospect into State &amp; Local government organizations</li>
<li>Identify and pursue new revenue opportunities within current customer base</li>
<li>Set and meet sales targets</li>
<li>Define and effectively deliver the value proposition for location technologies to government clients</li>
<li>Respond to solicitations, produce proposals, and generate estimates</li>
<li>Track and report on the sales pipeline</li>
<li>Provide internal and external thought-leadership within the State &amp; Local Government spaces</li>
<li>Establish oneself as a recognized expert in location technologies for governments through regular blogging, tweeting, and participation in related industry forums and conferences</li>
<li>Report to the Director of Sales for the Enterprise Business Unit</li>
</ul>
<b>Competencies and Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Capable of presenting new technologies and ideas to decision makers</li>
<li>Consultative in nature! Possess the ability to understand and solve the client's business problem(s)</li>

<li>Knowledge of the sales pipeline and CRM systems</li>
<li>Ability to engage clients, track and qualify leads, and close sales</li>
<li>Exceptional customer service orientation</li>
<li>Excellent follow-up and organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, writing, and presentation skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work productively with a wide range of people</li>
<li>Self-motivated, driven and pro-active</li>
</ul>
