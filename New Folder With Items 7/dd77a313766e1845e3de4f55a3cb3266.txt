<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Action Mailing &amp; Printing Solutions is a leader in Direct Mail, Printing and Marketing Solutions. We are expanding our team and seeking experienced Account Managers to support our growing business.</p>
<p><b>Who we are looking for</b></p>
<p>A self-motivated team player who is eager to use their client relationship and project management skills to bring value to our business.</p>
<p>The Account Manger is a primary contact for our clients. In this role you will work closely with the Sales Executives to deliver superior customer service and project management from beginning to end to include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Placing project orders in our software program.</li>
<li>Create and maintain project documentation.</li>
<li>Work with clients to determine specific project objectives and help to develop strategies and tactics to effectively deliver on those objectives.</li>
<li>Move project through departments with accurate and detailed instruction.</li>
<li>Communicating with clients to obtain project data, instruction, proofing and approvals.</li>
<li>Ensure accurate shipping and billing information is provided.</li>
<li>Answer questions, resolve issues, make corrections and provide professional knowledge and instruction.</li>
<li>Assess new business opportunities through the client relationship.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other responsibilities include</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Stay current on project work flow and report status or get updates at daily production meeting.</li>
<li>Maintain inventory counts.</li>
<li>Collaborate with project team to communicate areas of art, data, production and shipping needs are met and resolve issues.</li>
<li>Manage post project documentation.</li>
<li>Learn about our products and services, print industry processes and mail requirements.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Ability Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent time management, organizational and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work well on multiple tasks and under pressure.</li>
<li>Active team player with a positive can-do attitude.</li>
<li>Capable of working independently in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Appropriate sense of urgency.</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications Desired</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Print industry experience is highly desired.</li>
<li>College degree or 2 years of work experience as Project Coordinator, Project Manager or Account Manager; responsible for multiple accounts.</li>
<li>Production or manufacturing experience may be helpful but not required.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>

</ul>
