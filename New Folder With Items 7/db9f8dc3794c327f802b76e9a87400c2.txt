<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Google's mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful. Our Devices &amp; Services team combines the best of Google AI, Software, and Hardware to create radically helpful experiences for users. We research, design, and develop new technologies and hardware to make our user's interaction with computing faster, more seamless, and more powerful. Whether finding new ways to capture and sense the world around us, advancing form factors, or improving interaction methods, the Devices &amp; Services team is making people's lives better through technology.
</p>
<p>
As part of the Google Hardware, Global Product Partnerships team, you’ll be working closely with Pixel product + engineering teams to inform and execute a thoughtful partnerships strategy, while sharing your learning and progress with a broader Google Hardware BD team. Your role will be to identify and negotiate strategic technology and content partnerships that help fill key competitive gaps or amplify product differentiation in close partnership with product teams to better understand the roadmap and the opportunities presented. You should be quick to recognize opportunity, action-oriented, organized, and well versed in a broad range of technologies and trends. You evaluate inbound inquiries, quantify opportunities, build partnerships, collaborate with internal functional groups, and independently negotiate complex partnership agreements that often require deep financial modeling and scenario analysis. You're a true thought leader who can find, digest and share industry trends and access key influencers when appropriate. You open doors with potential partners, chat with them about their businesses and synthesize those opportunities to senior leadership on both sides.
</p>
<p>Google’s Global Partnerships team powers extraordinary user experiences through partnerships that solve users’ needs, advance our partners’ goals, and further Google’s mission. Our team provides meaningful solutions — from helping publishers and developers grow their businesses through monetization solutions, to helping our long-term partners anticipate and navigate change in a way that delivers economic and reputational value. We build and distribute products through partnerships across a multitude of product areas, including Ads, Search &amp; Assistant, Geo, Platforms &amp; Ecosystems, Devices &amp; Services, Health, Retail, Payments, Next Billion Users, and more. We collaborate across Google, ensuring a coordinated approach when engaging with our most complex and strategic partners.</p>
</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Conceive and execute partnerships that bring differentiated value and meaningful impact to Pixel products in the marketplace.</li>
<li>Lead exploratory discussions with potential product partners and negotiate and structure partnership agreements to complement a complex product portfolio and roamap.</li>
<li>Communicate and collaborate with a broad range of cross-functional constituents (Product, Engineering, Marketing, Channel Sales, Finance, Legal and external executives) to achieve buy-in and follow through on these partnerships.</li>
<li>Lead communications, and develop and grow strategic partnerships proactively including understanding partner priorities, quantifying financial impact and joint value, assessing deal performance, and implementing launch of deployments.</li>
<li>Maintain a pulse on partner feedback, provide prompt and proper resolution of project, business, and technical challenges, and provide meaningful feedback to respective Google teams to improve the partner and user experience. Grow key business metrics and deliver on implementation schedules.</li>
</ul>
