<h2>HVAC DDC Controls Engineer (SSE-III)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>We are looking for a HVAC DDC Controls Engineer (SSE-III) to join a Bellevue, WA office!</b></p>
<p>At <b>Sunbelt Controls</b>, we strive to lead the industry with building control products and solutions that reduce energy and operational cost, which help our customers be more efficient and competitive in today's world. We translate emerging technologies into value for our customers by creating smarter, more efficient buildings through our professional solutions, services, and critical systems integration.</p>
<p>The position requires the individual to apply their technical knowledge and experience for the successful database programming and custom graphics development and subsequent installation of this programming into the Building Management System for all assigned projects. Additionally, this role supports the startup, commissioning, and troubleshooting of the assigned projects. The position requires a high level of experience in computer skills and the understanding of computer-operated systems, including network protocols. This role will also require responsibility for documenting all field as-built conditions, as well as, the commissioning process documents. Strong leadership skills are a necessity to lead large projects in the field with multiple team members. In addition, the technical nature of this position will also assume as necessary, Project Engineering tasks and responsibilities with respect to design engineering and field coordination. The position is required to closely interact with the Project Manager and any assigned Project Engineering staff to assist with the timely and successful completion of each project or pre-sales activities.</p>
