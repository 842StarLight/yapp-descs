<h2>Sales, Connected Care Account Manager (Greenville, SC)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Title</b></h3>
Sales, Connected Care Account Manager (Greenville, SC)<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h3>
<p><b>
In this role, you have the opportunity to</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Be an integral part of this dynamic team as an Account Manager of the Connected Care team. This position offers you the opportunity to play a vital role in increasing market penetration and to the overall growth of our business. As a Philips employee, you get the chance to operate at the heart of a worldwide company that continues to transform itself, to become ever more market-driven and consumer-focused.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are responsible for</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Deep insights of customer needs, with the proven ability to define and provide Marketing &amp; Analytics Solutions</li>
<li>
Establishing executive level relationships within your assigned accounts. 3-5 executive level contacts per institution</li>
<li>
Owns the customer experience including challenges with current solutions and vendors/partners</li>
<li>
Own, manage and drive the Business Line partnering with Enterprise AE, RSM's, and Zone Connected Care Regional Business Manager (CC RBM).</li>
<li>
Design and quote a value rich connected care solution with the ability to Communicate Solution via a winning proposal to ultimately become the preferred vendor to win business</li>
<li>
Quarterly Business Plan aligned with RSM and CC RBM.</li>
<li>
Ability to develop and execute MyPlan for assigned accounts and keep current.</li>
<li>
Execute and proactively manage WOW</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to develop strategies to convert competitive accounts.</li>
<li>
Understands national and regional market dynamics.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to complete Business Territory Planning</li>
<li>
Completes Install Base Planning (MyPlan)</li>
<li>
Identify market trends, adapt engagement strategy and development of Competitive selling strategies</li>
<li>
Understanding of Clinical Practice and workflow changes</li>
<li>
Owns Account specific quarterly account plans including Install Base &amp; EOL Strategies.</li>
<li>
Partner with customer to sell solutions and services within your assigned territory.</li>
<li>
Establish Value Proposition that aligns with customer needs</li>
<li>
Funnel consists of active opportunities across the portfolio, including solutions &amp; service/SMAs</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are a part of</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Philips Connected Care sales force. As a goal-driven Account Manager- Connected Care you’re the prime person responsible for generating sales volume by developing credible long-term relationships with our customers.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
To succeed in this role, you should have the following skills and experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 8 years of field sales experience with a Bachelor’s degree or 5 years and a Master’s degree; or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>
Medical Capital Sales Experience, Preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to work with prospects to develop strong business solution cases coupled with solid closing skills</li>
<li>
Results-oriented approach, high energy, balanced with a “take charge” attitude with teamwork and collaboration</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal, presentation, and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Deep Product Knowledge and expertise within the Philips Patient Monitoring product lines.
</li>
<li>Ability to quickly demonstrate expertise an establish credibility with clinical decision makers.</li>
<li>
Confident with developing relationships with C-suite executives.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate the Connected Care Strategy.
</li>
<li>Ability to assess potential applications of company products to meet customer needs and prepare detailed product specifications for the development and implementation of customer products/applications/solutions.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
In return, we offer you</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Sharpen your talents with new challenges in our dynamic organization. As a market-driven company, we’re used to listening to our customers &amp; apply the same thinking to our employees. We offer a competitive salary, outstanding benefits and flexibility in a career with a positive and supportive atmosphere in which to develop your talents further.</p>
