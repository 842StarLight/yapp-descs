<div>The Project Manager is responsible for the leadership of projects that are cross-functional and have a higher degree of complexity. Focused dedication of workload, managing controlled amount independently, as well as in partnership with Director - CRM Strategy. Serve as the point of contact for the Business case project. The Project Manager together with the leadership is directly responsible for delivery of assigned projects within acceptable timeframes and quality standards. The Project Manager is fully knowledgeable of best practices in project management. The Project Manager may be responsible for multiple projects running concurrently. The Project Manager may be responsible for a small vertical market or integration project. Lead efforts focused on applying rigor in methodologies to structure and implement projects that align with Business Unit plans, Customer strategic initiatives, or Process Improvement efforts. Act as change agent to foster a customer-centric, quality-oriented culture while driving measurable business improvements. Use enterprise change management principles in projects.</div>
