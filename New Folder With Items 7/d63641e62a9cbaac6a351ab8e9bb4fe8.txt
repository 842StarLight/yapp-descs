<p>
The Role:</p>
<p>
The Restaurant Sever is responsible for serving food and beverage items in a knowledgeable, friendly and helpful manner to all guests.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
What You Need:</p>
<p>
Required:</p>
<ul>
<li>(State or County Licensee or Certification Requirement)/ Responsible Alcohol Awareness Training Certification or Equivalent</li>
<li>2-5 years high volume experience in restaurant operations</li>
<li>Working knowledge of high-volume restaurant operations and safe, responsible alcohol service</li>
<li>Standard food preparation/presentation</li>
<li>Skill in guest relations</li>
<li>Wine &amp; Liquor knowledge</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tables at one time efficiently and with an upbeat, friendly attitude; read guests in order to anticipate their needs</li>
<li>High School Diploma</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Preferred:</p>
<ul><li>Point of Sales knowledge, preferably MICROS</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Physical Demands/Working Environment:</p>
<ul>
<li>Working environment is fast-paced, often loud and stressful</li>
<li>Position requires extended periods of prolonged standing and working on your feet</li>
<li>Must be able to lift or move up to 25 lbs. using proper lifting techniques</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
What You’ll Do:</p>
<ul>
<li>Greet all guests at table</li>
<li>Respond to guest inquiries regarding menu, wine list, Music Hall</li>
<li>Inform guests of daily specials and make recommendations</li>
<li>Suggestively sell menu items, beverages, desserts, etc.</li>
<li>Anticipate and respond to guest needs</li>
<li>Respond to guest concerns/complaints regarding food or service and correct errors or resolve complaints with a friendly, positive attitude</li>
<li>Organize and prioritize service per appropriate service sequence</li>
<li>Take menu, beverage and bar orders from guests and communicate orders to kitchen and/or bar through micros terminal</li>
<li>Check food orders prior to serving to ensure that they are correct, properly presented, and that special requests have been met</li>
<li>Deliver food and beverages to table</li>
<li>Perform opening/running/closing duties according to HOB policy</li>
<li>Maintain line of sight/atmosphere control by circulating through work area throughout shift</li>
<li>Ability to memorize menus and daily specials</li>
<li>Pre-bus tables: remove dirty plates, glasses, etc., from table once guest has finished</li>
<li>Clean spills on tables or around work area</li>
<li>Maintain HOB safety and sanitation standards</li>
<li>Prepare guest checks (on micros terminal) and check for accuracy</li>
<li>Receive payment and process cash, credit card or traveler’s check transactions</li>
<li>Have knowledge of all music, promotional or special events in the house</li>
<li>Assist other servers or other employees as needed</li>
<li>Required to occasionally work Special Events that are scheduled throughout the venue</li>
</ul>
