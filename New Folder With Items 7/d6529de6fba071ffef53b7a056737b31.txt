<h2>Director of Engineering for Client ad Platforms</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Location: CA - Oakland
</b></p>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
We have a great opportunity for a Director of Engineering for Client Ad Platforms - We are looking for someone who wants the speed and agility of a self-contained agile environment, with the resources and stability of a $5B+ company. We are looking for someone who can manage diverse, smart, and driven engineers while balancing personnel strengths with business requirements.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
As part of this team, you will own the strategy, development, and deployment of our ads solution for both 1st and 3rd party products across iOS, Android, Consumer Electronics Devices and Web platforms. SiriusXM + Pandora reaches 100MM Monthly Active Users with advertising and drives greater than $1B in revenue with a large dedicated engineering and product team focused on advertising. We are among the top digital publishers from a revenue perspective, and we are the largest seller of digital audio in the world, bigger than rest of world combined. You will build an engineering vision for the platform and associated services, then guide your team to synthesize technology solutions that strengthen our internal and external deliverables.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
This is a senior position with broad management responsibilities and a hand in defining and executing strategic initiatives. You will be responsible for the team that reshapes the platform and applications that enable us and our partners' to launch devices successfully.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Supervisory Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Will have multiple management direct reports as well as individual contributors that will require direction, mentorship and career development.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>S. Computer Science or equivalent and 15+ years relevant industry experience</li>
<li>7+ Years' experience in ads technology and services</li>
<li>10+ years of experience managing teams in shipping software products at scale</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements and General Skills:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>You are comfortable navigating both the technology and business context in a technical ecosystem</li>
<li>You are passionate about delivering quality solutions</li>
<li>You can articulate and can evangelize new ideas</li>
<li>Architect new systems for doing ad optimization at scale
</li>
<li>You can innovate through iteration; using prototypes and experiments to navigate solution ambiguity</li>
<li>Work with product and sales teams to design and implement new ad products</li>
<li>You have experience in full stack engineering and strong software skills</li>
<li>You are familiar with cloud-based architectures and streaming services</li>
<li>Must have legal right to work in the U.S.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Technical Skills:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
