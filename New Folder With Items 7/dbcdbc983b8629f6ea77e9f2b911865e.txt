<b>General Description:</b><b><br>
</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsible for providing pre/post-sales support to Trade and Internal customers for flow measurement products and solutions. This position will specialize in measurement and control applications, specifically utilizing Daniel M&amp;C technology.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential functions:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Work directly with customer to provide pre/post sales application and product support.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work directly with customer to troubleshoot product functionality issues.
</div></li>
<li><div>Field general and technical questions about Daniel products and services.</div></li>
<li><div>
Review customer specifications to verify/determine the application needs of the customer.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist in or develop the technical write-up for each proposal, including exceptions and QC requirements.</div></li>
<li><div>
Convey differentiation of Daniel product versus competitor product to customer.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide backup support for Inside Sales Engineer group to maintain service levels.</div></li>
<li><div>
Train and educate Inside Sales Engineer group, Outside Sales group and Daniel Representatives on new technology.</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsible for evening &amp; weekend emergency phone coverage; biweekly.</div></li>
</ul>
