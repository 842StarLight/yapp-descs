<h2>Client Services Advocate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a critical care account manager, you will optimize the client experience from on-boarding to retention to growth. You will be maintaining post-sales contact with customers to facilitate a positive and productive long-term relationships. You will be empowered to do what is right for the customer to attain success and create loyalty.
</p>
<p>Our mission to bring value to our customers, employees and communities with a high touch and high-tech philosophy.</p>
<p><b>
What You'll Be Doing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ensure client business success through productive, effortless use of our software and guiding corrective actions when necessary.</li>
<li>
Takes initiatives in customer satisfaction via process improvement plans and efficient reporting and flow of information.</li>
<li>
Promote client satisfaction and loyalty by understanding each business's unique (and evolving) needs, delivering value and exceeding expectations.</li>
<li>
Identify clients with extraordinary needs, coordinate a team to enumerate needs and build action plans, and manage plans to successful outcomes in steady state.</li>
<li>
Improve and advocate for customer experience by collaborating cross-functionally with Product &amp; Engineering teams to actualize requests.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Who Are We Looking For:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Relentless focus on satisfying customer needs.</li>
<li>
Friendly, energetic, and resilient personality.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written skills.</li>
<li>
Collaborative, inclusive communicator and team player.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What Experience You'll Need to Have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
1 – 3 years of experience in the related area as an individual contributor.</li>
<li>
1 – 3 years of project management.</li>
<li>
Influence management across organizational boundaries.</li>
<li>
Stellar customer relationship and conflict management.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What Education You'll Need to Have:</b></p>
<p>
Associates degree in computer science, business or related field required; Bachelor's degree in business, computer science or related field desired or equivalent experience.</p>
<p><b>
What Experience Would Be Beneficial:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Practical pharmacy software experience, specifically with SmithTech brands.</li>
<li>
Excel and PowerPoint proficiency.</li>
</ul>
