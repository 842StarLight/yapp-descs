<h2>Senior Product Manager - Public Sector</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>The Position:</b><br>
As a Product Manager, you will be responsible for developing and driving the vision for the Apptio's products in the Public Sector. Apptio is a leader in the Enterprise SaaS market today – having defined the TBM category and established market leadership. This role will have you defining and leading what we deliver into the future – delivering new offerings and features at a rapid pace. You will develop product value propositions, scenarios and use cases, drive market validation, and drive execution in partnership with engineering, product design, and program management.
<br><br>
<b>What we want you to do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage the entire product planning life cycle from vision to strategic planning to execution.</li>
<li>I am actively engaging with Prospects &amp; Customers in understanding their use cases and determining the value for the Public Sector.</li>
<li>Develop product specifications covering value proposition, use cases, scenarios, and product features and working with engineering, product design, and other stakeholders, track execution.</li>
<li>Working with product marketing, ensure delivery of these applications within Apptio's marketing and sales motions.</li>
</ul>
<b>Basic Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years experience as a product manager; experience in the Public Sector IT or Finance domain is preferred</li>
<li>Technical background especially in data analytics</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent customer interaction skills. Deep passion for understanding prospect/customer and market feedback.</li>
<li>Knowledge of IT, Financial processes and Business Intelligence areas is strongly desired</li>
<li>Exhibits potential to participate in company-level strategy conversation and map that to priorities.</li>
</ul>
**Apptio benefits include Company-Paid employee health, dental, vision, life, and disability insurance, and generous contributions to a health savings account. We also offer participation in a flexible spending account, 401k, and other voluntary programs.
