<h2>Life Insurance Specialist - St. Petersburg. FL</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<br>
<div>The Auto Club Group (ACG) provides membership, travel, insurance and financial services offerings to approximately 9 million members and customers across 11 states and 2 U.S. territories through the AAA, Meemic and Fremont brands. ACG belongs to the national AAA federation and is the second largest AAA club in North America.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>This position is responsible for marketing and selling life insurance and annuities offered through AAA Life Insurance Company and meeting or exceeding production requirements as determined by the Company.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Although sales is the primary function, this position also serves as the advocate or professional source for related questions or guidance to members and clients. Position promotes AAA as the expert and provider of choice for insurance and financial services.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Duties include, but are not limited to:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Prospecting for and marketing the various life and annuity products offered by AAA Life through ACG to members and non-members primarily via outbound telephone calls and emails to prospective clients and responding to client and member questions. Builds referral relationships with customers and other sales representative within AAA to generate interest in all products offered by AAA and ACG.</li></ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Maintaining personal production records and entire process of a sale from application to placement with monitoring through underwriting to facilitate a timely issuance of a policy and to maintain high quality client service.</li></ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Assists clients in completing Needs Analysis and resolving issues with their policies purchases through AAA. Works with AAA Life and brokerage carriers through the underwriting process to facilitate issuance of a policy; meeting the needs of the customer.<br>
</li></ul>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Performs other duties as necessary<br>
</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Previous participation in an Insurance industry training program</li>
<li>MS Office knowledge is preferred.</li>
</ul>
