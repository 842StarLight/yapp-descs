<h2>BIM / AutoDesk Technical Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Construction Industry Solutions (COINS) has been providing business software and services to the construction, engineering, home building and service sectors for more than 30 years. Our global team has accumulated over 4,000 man-years of unrivalled, incremental industry knowledge that we put at the service of our clients.</p>
<p><b>
Job Role</b></p>
<p><b>
The BIM Technical Specialist is a member of our COINS Autodesk team based out of Irving, Texas. </b>This opportunity is focused on delivering professional services, consulting, training services, technical support, and technical sales assistance to our customers. As a BIM Technical Specialist, the role is primarily related to Autodesk Building Solutions applications in the architecture, MEP, and structural engineering sectors.</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with customers to help define their needs and qualify solutions to address them.</li>
<li>Assist clients in the adoption of new technology through classroom instruction, mentoring, follow-up technical support and related activities.</li>
<li>Provide software and industry related technical presentations at seminars, workshops, and client sites.</li>
<li>Create new training material – including interpreting customer requirements, producing the agenda, and creating content and datasets.</li>
<li>Attend pre-sales meetings (both in person and remotely over the phone) with COINS sales team members to help promote and explain Autodesk products and workflows.</li>
<li>Create white papers and record technical presentations to support COINS sales and implementation efforts.</li>
<li>Continually develop and expand your technical skills and software knowledge to stay ahead of our customers current requirements.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency with Autodesk Revit (Architecture and MEP), BIM 360, AutoCAD, and Navisworks, as well as Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint experience.</li>
<li>Other Autodesk solutions are a plus – such as AutoCAD Architecture, AutoCAD MEP, and 3ds Max Design.</li>
<li>Knowledge of BIM workflows and industry standards as they pertain to the architectural, engineering and construction industries.</li>
<li>A four-year Bachelor's degree desired, concentrating on architectural, engineering and/or construction</li>
<li>3-5 years of industry related experience in technical training or professional practice in an architectural, engineering and/or construction</li>
<li>An equivalent combination of education and experience will also be considered.</li>
<li>Both speaking and writing abilities are essential in this role – presenting products in their best light, and teaching complex technical workflows.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze and interpret user requirements.</li>
<li>Ability to respond effectively to customer inquiries and problems.</li>
<li>Strong organizational, problem solving and time management skills.</li>
</ul>
