<h2>Vice-President, Sales (Commercial)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Trustwave is a leading cybersecurity and managed security services provider that helps businesses fight cybercrime, protect data and reduce security risk. Offering a comprehensive portfolio of managed security services, security testing, consulting, technology solutions and cybersecurity education, Trustwave helps businesses embrace digital transformation securely. Trustwave is a Trustwave company and the global security arm of Trustwave, Optus and NCS, with customers in 96 countries.</p>
<p>
The Vice President – Sales (Commercial) will lead Sales and business growth in the assigned customer segments for mid-market and enterprise clients. They will lead a team of Sales Representatives, Account Managers, and Account Executives as well as an extended Sales support team including other support functions.</p>
<p>
The person will establish and maintain C-suite relationships with customers, partners, vendors, and relevant governing Security bodies with the intent to establish a presence influence and enable Trustwave to increase market share, business growth and profitability.</p>
<p><br>
The VP will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Coaching their existing sales team in the achievement of each territory’s sales quota, effective pricing and margin sustainability, client relationship management and lead generation, as well as the acquisition, development and retention of productive sales talent.</li>
<li>
Developing and executing on revenue plans and working with the Senior VP to develop strategic/top-level sales and management activities including strategic and tactical planning, go to market strategy formulation, and budgeting for assigned region.</li>
<li>
Aligning with inside sales, our channels, as well as our alliance partnerships and will be active in leading those activities.</li>
<li>
Interacting regularly with the global operational and delivery portions of our business and will be viewed as strategic leader and partner for the region.</li>
<li>
Working with various groups including our internal marketing organization to acquire leads and promote our services, as well as working with internal marketing to enhance existing and create exciting new sales collateral for the geographical footprint.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Document and demonstrated experience leading a sales organization, including managing large geographic territories and business partnerships</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in coaching, training, and motivating teams, as well as successfully selecting talent and integrating talent into an effective team</li>
<li>
Proven record of developing and managing a team to achieve and exceed their sales quota goals and manage pipeline</li>
<li>
Ability to adapt and navigate through complex business issues in a fast paced, constantly changing environment</li>
<li>
Passion for continuously learning about and leveraging new technology</li>
<li>
A drive for self-directed learning and personal development</li>
<li>
Entrepreneurial spirit</li>
<li>
Highly self-motivated, aggressive, energetic, creative, and personable</li>
<li>
Professional presence required and ability to effectively interface with executives.</li>
<li>
This role requires strong written and verbal communication skills as well as strong listening skills, ensuring success as the main point of contact for your portfolio of clients</li>
<li>
Diverse experience networking and prospecting using social media, service organizations, cold calling, and other sales skills to help achieve revenue goals</li>
<li>
Previous experience using Salesforce.com and proficiency using Microsoft Office are strongly preferred</li>
<li>
Solution selling experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>
Education:</p>
<p>
We prefer college educated applicants, but at minimum, high school diploma or equivalent is required for employment.</p>
