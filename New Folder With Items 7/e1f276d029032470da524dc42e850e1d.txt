<h2>Acitve911-Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">ACTIVE911 JOB DESCRIPTION - SALES MANAGER
<br><br>
Active911 (A911) is a leading innovator in creating technologies to help first responders save lives by getting to emergencies faster. Using a SaaS based rapidly iterating subscription service, A911 provides location and incident details related from Computer Aided Dispatch Systems (CAD)s, to assist fire, rescue, police, and other first-responder emergency medical professionals to get to the scene of emergencies faster and with more accurate information on the scope of the incident. Active 911 was created by a Firefighter with a vision to actively alert responding emergency personnel.
<br><br>
Today, A911 serves over 12,000 agencies with over 400,000 individual users. Most of this growth has occurred organically given the recognized value of the product and the social network of the emergency responder community. Active 911 is seeking a Sales Manager to lead the future growth of the organization and extend its market dominant position to other domestic and international opportunities as well as penetrating new channels of emergency notification users and the launch of newly developed related technology.

Sales Manager
<br><br>
Active911 is seeking a Sales Manager to lead the future growth of the organization and extend its market dominant position to other domestic and international opportunities as well as penetrating new channels of emergency notification users and the launch of newly developed related technology.
<br><br>
We are seeking a sales and marketing leader who can build a new team of business development professionals within our emergency alert ecosystem and become an integral part of the management team at Active911.
<br><br>
<b>POSITION OVERVIEW:</b><br>
The Sales Manager is accountable for building and managing a new outbound sales team with three individual contributors: Inside Sales Representative, Outside Sales Representative, and Channel Sales Manager as well as overseeing the Marketing Manager. This sales leader will have responsibility for contributing to and implementing a new sales strategy to double revenues over the next two years through internal lead conversion, outbound prospecting to the current target market, and developing new channel revenue opportunities through intermediaries.
<br><br>
The sales manager will also be accountable for driving sales growth and profitability of new activations through the current SaaS subscription service resulting in regular MRR increases for the organization. This role will interact with existing marketing, operations, and customer service managers to incorporate strategic sales planning throughout the company and will report directly to the General Manager.
