<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Our General Manager is very important to the success of each CREAM store. Their mission will be to foster teamwork and create a positive and fun work environment. We are looking for passionate, hardworking people who are willing to go the extra mile for a customer or fellow team member. Great attitude, smile and positive energy are the basic MUSTs.
<br><br>
If you have prior experience in restaurant management, general hospitality management or quick serve restaurant please apply for this wonderful opportunity.
<br><br>
Requirements/Responsibilities
<br>The GM is responsible for all operational aspects of the CREAM store that includes but is not limited to: P&amp;L, staffing, customer service, and inventory management.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Outgoing, fun and friendly personality with focus on customer service &amp; satisfaction</li>
<li>Creating and maintaining a positive team environment and high staff morale</li>
<li>P&amp;L and financial reporting (sales, food and labor costs, store-wide)</li>
<li>Proven leadership skills in accessing and developing talent</li>
<li>General restaurant supplies/equipment purchasing and management</li>
<li>Mastery of front and back end restaurant software (POS, Online Ordering)</li>
<li>4+ years in a management or supervisory capacity within the restaurant industry</li>
<li>2+ years of QSR GM experience preferred</li>
<li>ServSafe Manager Certified</li>
</ul>
<b>Physical Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to stand for long periods of time</li>
<li>Climbing, reaching, walking, sitting, grasping, repetitive motions all coupled with a great deal of smiling</li>
<li>Able to bend and lift up to 50 lbs.</li>
<li>The physical demands and work environment characteristics described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</li>
<li>Great pay</li>
</ul>
