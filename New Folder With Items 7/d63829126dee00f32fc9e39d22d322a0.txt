<h2>SMTS IT Proj Manager - Salesforce & Einstein Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>You will help create and deliver system enhancements and dashboards for Einstein, Salesforce CRM, and related integrations including aligning to our mobile first strategy. Overseeing Einstein project work in JIRA to create user stories and managing projects through the agile system development lifecycle (SDLC) process, review deliverables from IT, and host calls with various teams to receive feedback and gain alignment on Einstein strategy and then work to execute that roadmap.</p>
<p>
This role will also be leading large projects and enhancements and will be responsible for engaging Finance, Compliance, Legal, Training, Go-To-Market (GTM), and others to ensure successful delivery and utilization in the field. You will help us quantify competitive business segments, evaluate potential operational changes, and design new approaches and methodologies. Working across the company, you’ll use your analytical expertise to identify trends, issues and opportunities, all with the goal of improving the customer experience and finding ways to make our business even better.</p>

<p><b>
What we’re looking for...</b>
</p>
<p>We are looking for someone that has an in depth knowledge of agile software development methodologies as well as Einstein/Salesforce and related components in the Salesforce ecosystem. This person should be a self-starter who requires little supervision, has an eye for detail and strong analytical skills. A successful candidate will be expected to work on complex problems, propose creative solutions, and push for the right solution not the convenient one. This process includes contributing to building the business case and measures that prove out the ROI/business value of the enhancements.</p>
<p>
You are also good with numbers and you love to dig into data to find the story. Designing reports and spreadsheets is a fun afternoon for you. You are detail-oriented and know how to focus on what really matters. You understand the importance of accuracy in data analytics and reporting. You are a self-starter and a multitasker who can work independently under tight timelines. You want to make an impact by providing the data that will help improve the experience of our customers.</p>
