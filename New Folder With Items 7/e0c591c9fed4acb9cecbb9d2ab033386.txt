<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who we're looking for</b></h3>
<p>
We are looking for someone to build and lead Just One Dime's marketing team and create a full-fledged marketing plan which includes funnels, Facebook ads, Google ads, YouTube ads, a sales team, and email marketing plan.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>Your responsibilities include tracking business results, performing cost-benefit analyses and monitoring production KPIs. To be a successful Marketing Operations Manager, you must have experience in the same or similar role, managing all business operations and setting goals across the entire company. You must be able to set specific KPI's and drive results, measure results, and have an optimization plan for when KPI's are not met.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>You should also demonstrate great management skills, know how to hold team members accountable, motivate them, and manage multiple people while generating strong results. You must be able to communicate the vision of the company across all levels. Ultimately, you will ensure that all marketing functions are driving sales with our company.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What you'll be doing</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Ensure all systems operate smoothly and align with our quality standards</li>
<li>Maximize the efficiency of all business procedures</li>
<li>Establish production KPIs and conduct quality assessments</li>
<li>Monitor daily operations and address potential issues when they arise</li>
<li>Build processes that meet our business objectives and ensure compliance</li>
<li>Monitor financial data and recommend solutions that will improve profitability</li>
<li>Coordinate with the HR department to ensure our hiring plans meet our business needs</li>
<li>Take into account the needs of external and internal stakeholders and incorporate them into new strategic plans</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Plan and execute all digital marketing, including SEO, marketing database, email, social media and display advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Design, build and maintain our social media presence</li>
<li>Measure and report performance of all digital marketing campaigns, and assess against goals (ROI and KPIs)</li>
<li>Identify trends and insights, and optimize spend and performance based on the insights</li>
<li>Brainstorm new and creative growth strategies</li>
<li>Plan, execute, and measure experiments and conversion tests</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal teams to create landing pages and optimize user experience</li>
<li>Utilize strong analytical ability to evaluate end-to-end customer experience across multiple channels and customer touch points</li>
<li>Instrument conversion points and optimize user funnels</li>
<li>Collaborate with agencies and other vendor partners</li>
<li>Evaluate emerging technologies. Provide thought leadership and perspective for adoption where appropriate</li>
</ul>

<li>Proven experience managing marketing technology systems</li>
<li>Excellent project and people management skills</li>

<li>Highly organized and autonomous, but also collaborative and able to work cross-functionally</li>

<li>Strong analytical skills and data-driven thinking</li>
