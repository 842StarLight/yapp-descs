<h2>District Manager-Mississippi</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Responsible for providing solid leadership within a multi-unit environment in all aspects of retail, including recruiting, training, people development, clientele development, store standards, profitability and implementation/execution of all corporate/field initiatives. Primary contact for General Managers.
</p>
<p><b>Use your skills, experience &amp; talents to be part of something BEAUTIFUL! </b>As a District Manager you will perform the following essential functions…….</p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for meeting or exceeding all district budgeted sales targets.</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring superior Guest service with a high level of focus on Beauty Club enrollment.</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring all sales, marketing, and advertising communications are disseminated to store management with accountability for implementation.</li>
<li>
Responsible for coaching and counseling all store management with effective selling techniques relative to growing top line sales through the implementation and execution of corporate and field initiatives.</li>
<li>
Participates in district sales and expense planning with achieved results in district locations.</li>
<li>
Ensures all district internal/external product training classes are communicated and implemented in the scheduled timeframe.</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring that all store management and associates have been properly trained relative to corporate/field training program guidelines.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the development of managerial and operational skill sets of store management.</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring that all district performance/compensation reviews are completed within the established timeframe for all stores
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Undergraduate degree, 8 yrs. relevant work experience or equivalent combination of education and relevant work experience.
</li>
<li>6 yrs. retail management experience, multi-unit experience required.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates significant competency in sales, products and service.
</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills.
</li>
<li>Solid computer skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
</li>
<li>Ability to build a strong team environment.
</li>
<li>Travel required
</li>
<li>No extraordinary working conditions
</li>
</ul>
<p>For positions located in San Francisco: pursuant to the San Francisco Fair Chance Ordinance, we will consider for employment qualified applicants with arrest and conviction records.</p>
