<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Atlanta/Denver
<br>Position Summary
<br>The Sales Development Representative acts as a layer between marketing and sales – handling all first
<br>contact with new clients and building relationships through marketing activity – following up with
<br>prospects from events, campaigns, direct mail, email, etc. You will be working with some of the largest
<br>(AEC) Architectural, Engineering and Construction companies in North America. Our SaaS product set is
<br>revolutionizing the Pre-Fabrication market across multiple verticals. The goal of a Sales Development
<br>Representative is to take a lead or prospect from a marketing campaign, qualify the lead through
<br>opportunity discovery, set an appointment with a Subject Matter Expert, Sales VP, or other Company
<br>Executive, and assist with follow through until a quote is delivered. If you thrive in a team sales
<br>environment this is the role for you.
<br>Company Background
<br>eVolve (www.evolvemep.com) has revolutionized the prefabrication workflow to dramatically
<br>accelerate the process of creating prefab deliverables to your business. We not only have developed a
<br>highly desirable platform to facilitate prefabrication, we also understand the unique needs and nuances
<br>within the MEP world. We offer a smooth transition from AutoCAD to Revit and our “just in time”
<br>environment reduces overhead while increasing productivity. Partnered with Applied Software
<br>(www.asti.com), one of the largest AEC, Construction, Fabrication and Manufacturing System integrators
<br>in North America, we have created a tremendous breakthrough solution to meet the demands of the
<br>MEP prefabrication market.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
Be able to hit the ground running and start contacting new prospects 20 days from start date
<br>Respond rapidly to inbound leads through email and phone
<br>Master the identification of target prospects at target companies using Google, LinkedIn,
<br>Salesforce.com and other prospecting technologies
<br>Conduct sales development best practices with email, phone, and social drips to connect with new
<br>prospects.
<br>Skillfully build interest and create opportunities with new prospects
<br>Coordinate demos on Subject Matter Executive’s calendar and log activities in CRM
<br>Exceed quota of 10+ qualified leads on second full-time month
<br>Efficiently and gracefully counter top of the funnel objections, prospecting, cold calling, online, as well
<br>as have excellent interpersonal &amp; technical skills.

<br>Intermediate to Advanced Word, Excel &amp; PowerPoint skills
