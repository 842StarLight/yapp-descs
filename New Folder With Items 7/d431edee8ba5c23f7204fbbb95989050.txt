<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Summary and Essential Duties</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>
This position is responsible for supervising, training and scheduling stockers in assigned territory.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Identifying sales opportunities within assigned territory
</li>
<li>Assisting RSM in writing orders for assigned customers within assigned territory
</li>
<li>Examining merchandise displays to identify items in need of replenishment
</li>
<li>Communicating competitive information, i.e. new items in your assigned territory to Field Operations Manager
</li>
<li>Pulling back-stock from storage area to refill displays, rotating stock using FIFO system in the process
</li>
<li>Selling in store displays and coordinating resets
</li>
<li>Setting up advertising signs and displays of merchandise on shelves, counter, or tables, as appropriate or as requested to attract customers and promote sales
</li>
<li>Traveling between store locations in assigned territory, or to other locations as requested, using own vehicle
</li>
<li>Performing other duties as assigned to meet business needs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma or GED
</li>
<li>One to three months related experience and /or training preferred
</li>
<li>Current driver's license and auto insurance required</li>
</ul>
