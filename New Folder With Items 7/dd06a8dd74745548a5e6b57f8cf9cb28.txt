<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Designs, develops and implements marketing programs strategies to support the organization's products. Uses specific marketing strategies and media (e.g., print, broadcast, digital) to launch and position products. Identifies and implements marketing strategies and programs in collaboration with sales and technical teams.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Manages field-marketing services for company collection centers including advertising, promotion, recruitment, and center support activities. Facilitates center donor recruitment and retention activities along with donor advertising and promotional campaigns.
</p>
<p>1) Facilitates collection center and regional promotional materials in accordance to overall business vision and best practices opportunities. Partners daily with management and peers to accomplish goals including ongoing assessment of budget as relates to center liter production, donor repeatability, donor acceptance/rejection, conversion rates, cost structures, promotional campaigns and other identifiable metrics.
</p>
<p><b>2) </b>Develops, executes and implements national corporate marketing campaigns and growth strategies in conjunction with operational objectives. Works closely with Operations management to meet production goals.
</p>
<p><b>3) </b>Facilitates implementation of promotional materials including creation, timelines, training of internal stakeholders and managing promotional life cycle.
</p>
<p><b>4) </b>Evaluates effectiveness of marketing and advertising strategies including development of program success metrics, evaluation criteria, feedback mechanisms and Return on Investment analysis.
</p>
<p><b>5) </b>Works with external media agencies to develop and execute advertisements and analyze the effectiveness of targeted strategy campaign. Builds effective working relationships with key external customers, including thought leaders and patient advocacy groups. Oversees local media placements for all collection centers.
</p>
<p><b>6) </b>Directs all graphic designers and marketing teams on all production, creation and design of marketing materials.
</p>
<p><b>7) </b>Provides facilitation and strategic input for business development opportunities.
</p>
<p><b>8) </b>Coordinates center activities with public relations, community relations and corporate communications functions within company. Utilizes marketing assets including new donor recruitment resources, lapse donor recruitment resources, donor repeatability, donor loyalty and customer service materials.
</p>
<p><b>9) </b>Other job-related duties as assigned.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Position Minimum Requirements
</p>
<ul>
<li>BA degree in business, marketing or related field or equivalent combination of relevant work experience and education required
</li>
<li>Minimum of two (2) years of direct marketing experience or on-site collection center experience required
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience in recruitment and retention of blood donors or other volunteers desired
</li>
<li>Understands complex problems to collaborate and explore alternative solutions
</li>
<li>Ability to make decisions which have significant impact on the immediate work unit and monitor impact outside immediate work unit
</li>
<li>Ability to compose materials such as detailed reports, work-related manuals, publications to limited scope or impact, etc. and/or to make presentations outside the immediate work area
</li>
<li>Overnight travel required up to 15% of the time
</li>
<li>Fast paced environment with frequent interruptions
</li>
</ul>
