<h2>Senior Process Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>JB RADIATOR SPECIALTIES, INC. is a diversified manufacturer of engine cooling, oil cooling, and air cooling products. JBRS is one of the largest, technologically advanced metal fabrication heat exchange manufacturers in the Western United States. JBRS provides customers with the very latest technical expertise and state-of-the art manufacturing processes.</p>
<p>The Senior Process Engineer is responsible for engineering cost effective methods for manufacturing the product. Activities frequently are projects where results, budgets, and completion dates are critical. The position routinely interfaces with sales, design engineering, production, quality, shipping/logistics, and purchasing regarding objectives, issues, and open projects.</p>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul><li>Develops, implements, and validates new processes, materials, or design concepts. Efforts may include creating specifications; supervise projects during the assembly, installation, and startup of equipment projects; validation testing plans/reports; creating work instructions; working with suppliers; and project handoff to production.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manages projects that improve or maintain production efficiency, utilization of resources, and product quality. Recommends changes or upgrades to equipment, work methods, or other aspects of the process.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Leads the development, documentation, implementation, control, and improvement of production processes.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Resolves production process problems quickly.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establishes clear goals and execute plans to achieve them.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identifies process bottlenecks/non-value added and devises solutions to resolve them. May include design workplace layouts and production re-organizations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Is a technical resource that bridges the gap between production and outside suppliers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contributes to design review meetings by suggesting design changes that reduce cost, improve quality and manufacturability, or improve supply chain issues.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participates in cost reduction efforts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides technical analysis of customer issues and field failures to reduce warranty costs. These include failure analysis of products, create analysis reports, provide internal and external design recommendations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Routinely uses 3D Cad software (SolidWorks) to design, reverse engineer, and improve production tools or equipment. The individual will 3D model parts, assemblies, and sheetmetal components; create manufacturing drawings.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Routinely uses MRP and ERP software (Microsoft Dynamics GP and Salesforce) to review product Bill of Materials (BOM), review product order status, customer complaints, and failure analysis information.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Trains and coaches employees on equipment, software, standards and best practices regarding product testing and support, when needed.</li></ul>

<p>Work Environment - This position operates in a professional office environment. This role routinely uses standard office equipment and engineering equipment such as computers, phones, photocopiers, filing cabinets, fax machines, engineering measurement equipment (calipers, micrometer, tape measure, etc.).</p>
<p>Physical Demands - While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to talk or hear. The employee frequently is required to stand, walk; use hands to finger, handle or feel; and reach with hands and arms.</p>
<p>Education - BS degree in Mechanical Engineering, Manufacturing Engineering, Industrial Engineering or a related field of engineering.</p>
<p>Experience - Five or more years of progressive and relevant engineering experience in manufacturing, preferably in the manufacturing/machining of metal components and sheet metal fabrication. Track record must demonstrate success in leading a team of engineers, preferably in precision machining or metals fabrication. Must be familiar with a wide variety of manufacturing processes including metal fabrication, welding, brazing, soldering, painting, assembly, testing, material handling, and machining.</p>
<p>Preferred Education and Experience - MBA.Experience in Six Sigma or Lean Manufacturing concepts/practices. Experience in the manufacturing process of aluminum products. These include aluminum CAB or indexing furnaces, brazing flux, and aluminum cleaning methods/chemicals. Experience in the manufacturing process of soldering copper-brass components. These include ovens, solder, soldering flux, and cleaning methods/chemicals. Experience working with sheet metal fabrication (laser, punching, and forming). Experience with metal joining methods (welding, soldering, and fasteners). Experience in an ISO 9001 environment.</p>
