<p>Account Development Representative – North America<br>
</p>
<p>CloudCheckr is a fast-paced, innovative cloud management software company who partners with some of the world’s largest corporations and managed cloud services providers. We are looking for a high-energy, driven Account Development Representative with software-as-a service (SaaS) and B2B sales experience to join our successful Global Sales Group. CloudCheckr team members are obsessed with our customer’s success. We view the world from their perspective because, only then, can we empower and service them effectively.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b>An as Account Development Representative you will engage and qualify leads to create a quality pipeline for our Account Executives. In this role you will be responsible for building interest within your territory across all listings. CloudCheckr has a strong marketing arm which delivers leads through campaigns and participation in trade events.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage a successful sales funnel consisting of a combination of incoming from marketing, mining Salesforce (SFDC) with campaigns to qualify &amp; identify prospects, &amp; independent research outside SFDC
</li>
<li>Effectively &amp; independently deliver the CloudCheckr value proposition, articulating our product, integrations, &amp; features via phone</li>
<li>Follow the CloudCheckr process for lead generation &amp; sales</li>
<li>Pipeline activities including cold call &amp; prospecting with daily &amp; monthly targets to generate quality leads from multiple channels</li>
<li>Prequalify Director, VP, &amp; C-level executives to evaluate CloudCheckr’s full suite of applications</li>
<li>Set appointments for Account Executives with verified quality leads</li>
<li>Work with Marketing team to ensure lead generation &amp; follow through efficiency</li>
<li>Maintain an accurate &amp; up-to-date log of all prospect interactions in the CRM</li>
<li>Develop engaging creative copy for email marketing campaigns to reach prospects in all stages of the marketing &amp; sales cycle</li>
<li>25 calls per day<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven successful lead generation experience in B2B SaaS sales</li>
<li>Proficient in Salesforce or equivalent CRM tools</li>
<li>Experience in cloud technology (or ability to learn)</li>
<li>Comfortable cold-calling/emailing</li>
<li>Proficient MS Office skills</li>
<li>Excellent written &amp; verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Energetic phone presence &amp; excellent listening skills</li>
<li>Intelligent fast learner capable of articulating value</li>
<li>Driven, highly motivated &amp; competitive</li>
<li>Ability to enable &amp; embrace change</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills; able to manage priorities and report on progress<br>
</li>
</ul>
