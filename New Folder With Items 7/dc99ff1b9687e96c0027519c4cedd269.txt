<h2>Strategy & Operations Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Strategy &amp; Operations Associate</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Who We Are:</b></div>
<div>At Kaseya, we set stretch goals and aim to crush them every day. We’re leading the evolution of software for the IT industry through constant innovation. We pride ourselves on delivering outstanding results for our customers, which in turn has led to extraordinary growth. That’s where you come in.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Kaseya is looking for a <b>Strategy &amp; Operations</b> <b>Associate</b> to focus on strategic initiatives and modeling for our customer-facing teams. The ideal candidate for this role is a whiz with data; they can model any business scenario and update their analyses to reflect new business realities. They want to take on the challenge of using data and modeling to drive strategy and execution in a fast-paced environment. They can summarize the findings of their work so that all levels – from sales reps to executives – are strategically aligned.</div>
<div>
<br>
This role will work on strategic initiatives for customer-facing functions, including Account Management, Support, and Professional Services. The role reports to the Director of Strategy &amp; Operations and will work closely with multiple executives, including the Chief Operating Officer and Chief Customer Officer.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Roles &amp; Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Interpret business challenges related to our customer base; Drive strategic initiatives and custom analyses to address them</li>
<li>Lead the use of data as a story, translating complex analytical work into easy to understand insights and recommendations</li>
<li>Develop impactful, measurable strategies through rigorous analysis to drive revenue growth and meaningful interactions with customers</li>
<li>Create living, breathing documentation for strategic initiatives to track progress against our goals</li>
<li>Work on special projects for the Chief Operating Officer and Chief Customer Officer</li>
<li>Present key strategies to executives and department leaders; ensure day-to-day strategic alignment for those initiatives</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Skills &amp; Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>2-4+ years’ experience in strategy, sales operations, investment banking / PE, or consulting</li>
<li>Expertise in recurring revenue business models and / or familiarity with B2B software is a major plus</li>
<li>Experience leading strategic projects and providing solutions-oriented findings</li>
<li>Strong analytical and modeling skills with ability to work across multiple sources of data; Excel / PowerPoint ninja</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills with a proven ability to work collaboratively</li>
</ul>
</div>
