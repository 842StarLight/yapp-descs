<div>Summary
</div>
<div>
<p>Do you consider yourself to be a technology savvy person? Do you enjoy being involved in many projects where your knowledge is required? If this sounds like you, then the position of IBM SYstem i Software Developer with HOM Furniture is for you!!</p>
<p>
HOM Furniture ranks as one of the top 50 furniture companies nationwide and has 17 locations throughout the midwest! The position of IBM System i Software Developer's main goal is to support our transactional application infrastructure in the areas of sales, service, inventory, accounting and financial systems along with interfaces providing system integration to internal and external systems. Will be responsible for supporting existing applications, enhancing system functionality and scalability. In addition this person will have the opportunity to work with IBM's latest development toolsets involving web-enablement and web-services. This person will work closely with users and department heads and will be involved in all phases of a project including: planning, reviewing specifications, documentation, scheduling, development, testing and support.</p>
