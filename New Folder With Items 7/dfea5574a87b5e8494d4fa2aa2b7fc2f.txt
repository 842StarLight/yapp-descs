<h2>Business Relationship Manager - Information Technology</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Role and Responsibility:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create a seamless ability for business partners to leverage the services and expertise of the technology organization to obtain the solutions necessary to ensure success
</li>
<li>Be the initial point of contact for business partners to engage the technology organization and provide leadership for the engagement (bringing technology and the business together) and enhance the relationship
</li>
<li>Manage the expectations of business partners (IC through executive level) regarding the engagement with the technology organization (schedule Concept Estimation, facilitate the conversation, be a partner for, confirm the solution meets the business need, schedule next steps)
</li>
<li>Act as a liaison and as a technical contact to market executives, business partners, external state regulatory contacts, and external vendors
</li>
<li>Represent IT in project and/or business meetings and engagements
</li>
<li>Partner with the market operational leadership to support new and existing market product initiatives including mandates and regulatory audits
</li>
<li>Provides technical leadership and guidance to market leadership and market technical resources
</li>
<li>Assess, analyze, and identify impacted teams and then appropriately engage those teams
</li>
<li>Understand the business interest in order to be a partner for the requestor with the impacted technology teams and external vendors
</li>
<li>Facilitate the communication and understanding of high level business needs between business areas and within technology amongst peers, managers, directors and executive level
</li>
<li>Understand the overarching business model as well as the intricacies and nuances of the functional business areas
</li>
<li>Support business planning and budgeting and understand key financial indicators (such as ACR, MCR)
</li>
<li>Collaborate with architecture to ensure initiatives align with and support the overall architecture goals
</li>
<li>Understand the role of technology and technology innovation in business areas to flesh out competitiveness-enhancing initiatives
</li>
<li>Collaborate with business partners to develop initiatives that foster business differentiation
</li>
<li>Build, maintain and continuously improve the effort estimation process
</li>
<li>Provide financial data to support the assessment of new initiatives
</li>
<li>Provide analytics of business engagement to Senior Technology Leadership
</li>
<li>Define requirements for management information dashboards
</li>
<li>Perform any other job related instructions, as requested<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>Requirements
<p><b>Education / Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor of Science/Arts (BA/BS) degree in Computer Science, Information Systems Management, or other related field or equivalent years of relevant work experience is required
</li>
<li>Minimum of five (5) years of business relationship management experience is required
</li>
<li>Experience developing relationships with key business stakeholders and business leaders is required
</li>
<li>Experience with general IT applications and infrastructure environment; engineering systems, customer facing solutions (E-Commerce, Mobility, end user platforms), HRIS, Data warehouse is required
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Competencies / Knowledge / Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven ability to effectively interact, develop relationships, and collaborate with key client stakeholders in all levels within the organization
</li>
<li>Effective written and verbal communication skills with demonstrated ability to explain technical information to non-technical people and non-technical information to technical people
</li>
<li>Propose ideas and actions that gain acceptance from peers, managers, directors and executive management.
</li>
<li>Proven business acumen that leads to positive results
</li>
<li>Ability to manage competing priorities and adapt to a fast-paced environment
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and within a team environment
</li>
<li>Ability to develop, prioritize and accomplish goals
</li>
<li>Excellent listening and critical thinking skills
</li>
<li>Strong problem solving skills with attention to detail
</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and high level of professionalism
</li>
<li>Gains understanding of business strategies
</li>
<li>Intermediate to advanced proficiency with Microsoft Office to include Word, Excel, PowerPoint and SharePoint
</li>
<li>Presentation and analytical skills
</li>
<li>Stays abreast of industry trends in business and technology
</li>
<li>Project management skills
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Licensure / Certification:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BRM Certification, SAFe Certification, PMP Certification or ITIL Certification preferred
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>General office environment; may be required to sit/stand for long periods of time</li>
</ul>
