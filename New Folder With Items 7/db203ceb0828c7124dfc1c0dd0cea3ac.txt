<h2>Customer Relationship Manager (Madison, WI)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Forte is the industry-leading provider of software and services in the critical areas of clinical trial management, clinical data management and research administration for cancer centers, academic medical centers and health systems.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Job Duties (in order of priority)</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate a positive and productive long-term relationship with each assigned customer through effective account management.</li>
<li>Cultivate and maintain strategic customer relationships</li>
<li>Sell to a portfolio of key accounts including presenting information, generating proposals, coordinating contractual agreements with the Forte legal team</li>
<li>Provide account leadership for the customer's major Forte initiatives (this would include some project management and solutions consulting)</li>
<li>Develop strategy based on specific needs of the customer.
</li>
<li>Identify sales/revenue opportunities.</li>
<li>Work in close collaboration with internal teams including product management/development, project management, and product support</li>
<li>Communicate meaningful customer feedback to Marketing, Sales and Product Management/Development teams</li>
<li>Liaise with internal teams to ensure proper pre-and post-sales service</li>
<li>Work with marketing to provide ideas for new customer facing collateral, slides, case studies, etc.</li>
<li>Participates in both client and internal team communications and meetings.</li>
<li>Manage internal and external expectations, ensuring daily project deadlines and priorities are met within all teams/departments to meet or exceed customer expectations.</li>
<li>Analyze existing processes and procedures and identify deficiencies; participate in process improvement efforts.</li>
<li>Track, maintain, and analyze records of customer survey participation and results.
</li>
<li>Lead by example displaying a positive, service first attitude to internal / external clients.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
