<h2>Product Manager - Revenue Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">As the Revenue Operations Product Manager, you will work with engineering, business operations, sales, and finance teams to redefine and evolve the OpenSesame commerce and licensing platform. You'll be responsible for leading a cross-functional team's alignment on the vision and execution across all of OpenSesame's financial services and payment experiences. You possess a blend of business and technical savvy: a big-picture vision, and the drive to make that vision a reality.
<br><br>
The Product team at OpenSesame is responsible for developing our strategic roadmap and delivering high-value products and features for our customers. We define, design, launch and support the OpenSesame platform with learning administrators and individual learners. We are obsessed with delivering a fantastic customer experience.
<br><br>
Role Specific Job Duties
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Autonomously drive the evolution of the OpenSesame commerce platform</li>
<li>Manage the entire product life cycle from strategic planning &amp; roadmap development to tactical execution</li>
<li>Define detailed product requirements, clear and consistent epics and user stories with acceptance criteria and supporting materials.</li>
<li>Track technology trends in commerce and finance arenas and validate product requirements and specifications in collaboration with colleagues, lead customers and channel partners</li>
<li>Play a lead role in the product life cycle by breaking down product requirements into user stories, participating in development sprints and taking the product/project to market launch</li>
<li>Work closely with internal customers to successfully launch new features, insuring that sales &amp; CSM, finance, and support teams have what they need to be successful</li>
<li>Measure product performance after a new feature is implemented</li>
<li>Analyze and report on the performance of features in production, reflecting on successes and identifying areas for continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Represent the product in front of customers (external and internal) in both pre and post sales situations</li>
<li>Manage ongoing tradeoffs in market fit, usability cost, and performance. Analyze market and competitive trends to inform new recommendations</li>
<li>Help identify and establish key technology partnerships</li>
</ul>
Required Skills/Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in a related field preferred</li>
<li>4-6 years experience in enterprise SaaS platforms, finance or revenue operations preferred</li>
<li>Extensive experience in full software development lifecycle duties including requirements gathering, design, estimation, prototyping, implementation, and quality assurance</li>
<li>Customer-facing communication skills required</li>
<li>Proven track record of successful new product development with an agile technology team, preferably using Scrum</li>
</ul>
</div>
