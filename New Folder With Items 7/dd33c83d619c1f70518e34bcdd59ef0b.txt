<p><b>Junior Field Service Representative- South Florida</b></p>
<p>
IT BEGAN WITH A FLIP-FLOP. But not just any flip-flop. A sandal that revolutionized the industry, bringing biomechanical support to a quintessential summer staple. From these humble beginnings in Australia to the bustling San Francisco Bay Area, Vionic has grown from a fledgling business to a global footwear brand. We’re bringing together style and science and reimagining the look of supportive footwear.</p>
<p><br>
<br>
Vionic has been named a 2018 Gallup Great Workplace Award Winner, and is the only company recognized in the fashion and retail sector. Vionic is one of just 39 companies globally to receive the prestigious award, which celebrates companies that achieve exceptional performance by creating a culture of high-reaching employee engagement. Gallup has rated Vionic among the top 2% of all companies based on "Employee Engagement” exceeding the national average by 65 percentage points. We are also officially certified and recognized as a Marin/Bay Area/California Green Business.</p>
<p><b>
In a Nutshell</b></p>
<p>
The Junior Field Service Representative’s primary function is to be the brand ambassador for the Vionic Group LLC brand creating goodwill within retail stores and at select Vionic supported events. Each Junior Field Service Representative will be an advocate on the road for Vionic where you will share a wealth of information on anything from company values and technical product information, to industry trends and emerging lifestyle ideals. The Junior Field Sales Representative’s main focus is to educate and execute the vision of Vionic Group, by effectively merchandising, educating, and driving brand awareness through the training of the retail associates.</p>
<b>
What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Submit regular GSC (Go Spot Check) Missions that record the details of store visits and Vionic events.</li>
<li>
Ensure that Vionic marketing objectives are implemented in each account, including appropriate use of POP, imagery, advertising etc.</li>
<li>
Develop a very strong relationship with senior management and internal teams including customer care, key accounts, product development, operations, and marketing.</li>
<li>
Maintain constant communication with Field Lead.</li>
<li>
Learn the most effective 2nd Gap out-selling techniques and educate sales associates accordingly.</li>
<li>
Build constructive business relationships with accounts to maximize the success of Vionic products.</li>
<li>
Provide regular follow up on any events to the appropriate Sales Directors and Field Leads.</li>
<li>
Work with Supervisor and Lead to build bi-annual SMART goals and work to meet or exceed them.</li>
<li>
Embody the Vionic/Caleres core values while working with our retailers and internal Vionic counterparts.</li>
<li>
Attend, represent and promote Vionic at tradeshows or other special events.</li>
</ul>
