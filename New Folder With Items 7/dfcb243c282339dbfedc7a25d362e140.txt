<h2>SAP FICO Practice Principal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The SAP FICO Architect will be responsible for applying business and technical expertise to design, implement and support new business capabilities into SAP systems and recommend SAP and Accounting best practices.</p>
<p>
This role will lead or participate in local or global projects across Finance areas. The applicant is expected to drive continuous improvements and SAP best practices in support of the company vision and roadmap.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:<br>
Practice Principals:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Should be thought leader in improving Applexus Services</li>
<li>Serve as a champion in following the SAP Activate and Applexus Running Start Methodology as it will be defined and ensuring staff understand and follow said methodology.</li>
<li>Participate in presales serving as a subject matter expert in front of clients and helping to scope and estimate projects</li>
<li>Mentor assigned consultants</li>
<li>Responsible for professional growth of assigned consultants</li>
<li>Closely monitor consultant availability/billing status</li>
<li>Forecast consultant availability for new projects, reserving them for specific engagements as needed</li>
<li>Understand the Sales pipeline to determine if hiring additional consultants for the assigned roles is appropriate vs. sub contractors</li>
<li>Interviewing candidates for W2 Full time, W2 hourly and contract positions – primary screening person</li>
<li>Must achieve personal utilization targets through both supervisory and “hands on” client work</li>
<li>The candidate will be responsible for configuring and delivering S4 HANA &amp; SAP FICO solution for multiple enhancements in the consulting environment.</li>
<li>Facilitate design workshops (Blueprint), assessments, planning sessions for projects..</li>
<li>Create Solution Designs for Small, Medium and Large sized project</li>
<li>Write functional specifications for enhancement, reports, etc. when needed.</li>
<li>Configure SAP Finance and Controlling module as per the requirements from the Business practices.</li>
<li>Effectively collaborate with onshore or offshore team for delivering solutions.</li>
<li>Provide support to end users for issue resolution, user acceptance testing, and training.</li>
<li>Support SAP Finance master data administration.</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in computer science, Business, (or related) or the equivalent in work experience.</li>
<li>15 + years of experience in the Information technology profession focused on ERP design and management.</li>
<li>10+ years of SAP FICO related modules with SAP configuration and implementation experience, , roll-out, design, consulting, support of sap projects specially in the area of financial accounting and controlling.</li>
<li>Deep understanding of the integration with other ECC modules (especially MM, PP and SD)</li>
<li>Experience with SAP CO-PA, Product Costing and Material Ledger</li>
<li>Experience on transfer pricing and intercompany markup projects.</li>
<li>Ability to identify, clarify and resolve project issues and risks as needed.</li>
<li>Good analytical problem solving and fact-finding skills.</li>
<li>Well-developed written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Strong relationship building skills, internally within IT and with business users.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and manage time and resources efficiently and effectively.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently.</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>
Background in Retail industry preferred</li>
<li>Certification in SAP FICO module is preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with S4/HANA or Simple Finance</li>
</ul>
