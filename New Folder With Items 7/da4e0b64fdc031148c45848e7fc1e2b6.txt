<h2>Senior Estimator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>As a Senior Estimator, you are responsible for evaluating bid specifications and drawings, ensuring that we know everything required to successfully bid and win the project. This person will also need to entail aggressive follow-up with subcontractors to ensure that bids are received. While also work with the Project Management teams to follow-up on bids and budgets to close projects.
<br>
<br>
<b>PRINCIPAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for bid administration in preparing full estimates on complex work.</li>
<li>
Provide full estimating on all types of projects (large, small, complex, hard bid, negotiated) and help train, support, and mentor sales and estimating teams</li>
<li>
Determine subcontractors to include in bidding process</li>
<li>Solicits and maintains communication with subcontractors and vendors</li>
<li>Prepares subcontractor bid packages.</li>
<li>
Transmits addenda and other bid information to subcontractors</li>
<li>Coordinate and interface with owners, architects, engineers, project managers and other project team members to resolve any technical matters during planning and development, negotiation and contract execution</li>
<li>Ensures that we have the proper coverage from subcontractors on bid day. Review estimates and subcontractor proposals for accuracy</li>
<li>Shows creativity and resourcefulness to gain better pricing from subcontractors</li>
<li>Submits 100% of bids and budgets by the bid deadline no exceptions.</li>
<li>
Efficiently performing all stages of an estimate including: estimate organization, take-off, pricing, review and closeout.</li>
<li>
Communicate project progress/status with management.</li>
<li>
Prepare, analyze and maintain accurate quantities and thorough take-offs from plans.</li>
<li>
Estimate time, materials and equipment required for job completion.</li>
<li>
Organize, research, and strategically plan for estimate preparation, pricing, scheduling, and final submission of price package</li>
<li>
Review plans and specifications for overall project understanding to identify risk in difficult work, operation, sequencing, project restrictions and time constraints</li>
<li>
Perform select quantity take off, pricing, site visits, pre-bid meetings, and review of the bid forms</li>
<li>
Understand and apply project constructability to the estimate format and costing</li>
<li>
Discuss and establish bid strategies, concerns, and advantages</li>
<li>
Establish work scopes for subcontractors</li>
<li>Establish General Conditions by determining a job schedule and job specific needs</li>
<li>
Bring all document reviews and estimating completion to closure prior to bid deadlines</li>
<li>
Track awarded contracts as required; may include estimating and pricing extra work items, change orders, materials, etc.</li>
<li>
Ensure all working documents and data are maintained to back up estimate figures</li>
<li>
Demonstrate competency, accuracy, and organization during bids</li>
<li>
Assist in buyout process and budget set-up</li>
<li>
Ensure that team members, including subcontractors, understand initial project requirements and on-going updates to those requirements</li>
<li>
Other duties, projects, and responsibilities as assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS</b>
<ul>
<li>4 years’ experience working Federal Construction Industry is preferred</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Construction Management, Civil Engineering or similar equivalent experience is preferred</li>
<li>
Ability to prepare construction schedules in MS Projects or Primavera</li>
<li>
Experience using RSMeans Software is a plus</li>
<li>
Ability to prepare estimates with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Familiar with conceptual and schematic estimating for Design Build Projects</li>
<li>
Organized and detailed with proven ability to fulfill all responsibilities for multiple, complex estimates simultaneously</li>
<li>
Able to remain calm, focused, and effective under time-pressure situations</li>
<li>
Possess basic understanding of construction contract law and generally accepted business practices</li>
<li>
Proven positive and professional attitude, and strong customer service skills</li>
<li>
Strong teamwork orientation, initiative, communication, problem-solving, and leadership skills</li>
<li>
Proficient with building/estimating techniques</li>
<li>
Proficient in MS Office Suite</li>
<li>
Current driver’s license and ability to gain approval for government installations access</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
