<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Please review the following job description:
</b></h3>
<p></p>Identify, select, develop, manage, service, and expand business relationships in an assigned market by offering a full range of loan, deposit, and other non-interest income services to existing and prospective single family residential (SFR) developer/builder clients. Under guidelines of Integrated Relationship Management (IRM), refer business and personal financial needs of business clients to the appropriate IRM partners or Relationship Managers.
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b><br>
Following is a summary of the essential functions for this job. Other duties may be performed, both major and minor, which are not mentioned below. Specific activities may change from time to time.
<br>
1. Handle loan requests from $250,000 up to a total credit relationship equal to the in-house limit. Demonstrate understanding of major technical aspects of residential construction/development lending, and have a clear understanding of credit and finance.
<br>
2. Develop, underwrite, and service SFR construction/development loans for builder/developer clients including management of past dues and maturing loans.
<br>
3. Work with manager, Market President, senior Relationship Managers and Credit Officers to develop skills in prospecting, client management, call execution, IRM and credit underwriting.
<br>
4. Profile clients and prospects to develop financial and credit information, to ascertain needs, abilities, earnings, and risk; request necessary information, such as financial and operating statements.
<br>
5. Evaluate and extend credit and terms in accordance with policy and lending authority.
<br>
6. Assume responsibility for proper documentation and data integrity.
<br>
7. Monitor loans to ensure complete conformity with terms and to ascertain any developing trends.
<br>
8. Aggressively call on potential and existing clients to maintain productivity and profitability in lending relationships; successfully execute the BB&amp;T Decathlon Sales and Consultative Sales and Retention Processes.
<br>
9. Monitor, maintain and update sales activity, pipeline, and other pertinent information using the Bank’s contact management system.
<br>
10. Keep abreast of changing business and economic developments which impact the SFR construction/development loan portfolio, its profitability, and banking in general.
<br>
11. Ensure commercial banking relationships are fully developed, including specific efforts to serve the retail banking needs of our clients’ owners/management/associates by partnering with Wealth Management advisors, Private Bankers and Relationship Managers.
<br>
12. Identify and market non-credit services by partnering with Financial Services Specialists in sales meetings, client and prospect calls, and developing and delivery of custom solutions.
<br>
13. Represent the Bank through community and professional involvement in groups and local home builder associations to develop contacts for potential business.
</p>
<p></p>

</b><br>
The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
<br>
1. Bachelor's degree or equivalent education and related training
<br>
2. BB&amp;T’s Leadership Development Program graduate or similar training program at another financial institution
<br>
3. Ten years of related experience
<br>
4. Thorough understanding of acquisition and development underwriting, appraisal valuations, and construction methodologies
<br>
5. Excellent knowledge of the local single family market
<br>
6. Demonstrated proficiency in basic computer applications, such as Microsoft Office software products
<br>
7. Excellent verbal and written communication skills
<br>
8. Ability to travel, occasionally overnight</p>
