<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Brief</b></h2>
<p>Wave Broadband is looking for a Marketing Manager to manage the B2C Direct Marketing program and support additional sales and digital marketing efforts.</p>
</div>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and deliver the Residential Direct Marketing Program to multiple segments on a regular cadence
</li>
<li>Work with the marketing group to establish creative direction, offer strategy and messaging by segment</li>
<li>Manage several variables in target direct marketing/mail versions including phone number list, promo codes</li>
<li>Additional projects will include campaign components of product launches, customer marketing programs, marketing and collateral for sales teams and digital campaign support</li>
<li>Secure estimates, manage invoices</li>
<li>Budget management</li>
<li>Manage multiple vendors
</li>
<li>Set schedules and deadlines and ensure all contributors meet timelines</li>
<li>Internal support – Assess and manage inbound requests or escalate to appropriate internal partners</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Direct mail and email management experience including copy and creative</li>
<li>Segmentation and list management</li>
<li>Demonstrated skills in assessing and optimizing campaign performance
</li>
<li>Experience extending campaigns to print and digital a plus</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to manage vendors and all project elements for on-time delivery</li>
<li>Creative, or able to coach agency partners to deliver increasingly engaging creative work</li>
<li>Good eye and a feel for customer friendly copy in a voice consistent with the brand</li>
<li>Highly detail oriented, able to multi-task confidently</li>
<li>Excellent proofreading skills</li>
<li>Self-starter and be able to work independently</li>
<li>Analytical and strategic mindset</li>
<li>A bias for action – proactive in identifying new opportunities</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects with good humor and a positive approach</li>
<li>5 to 8 years of experience needed</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or higher in marketing, business, communications or related field</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Nice to Have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Digital advertising experience
</li>
<li>Ability to create and deliver digital creative for media placement</li>
<li>B2B experience</li>
</ul>
