<p><b>About LISNR:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
LISNR powers transactions and connects the online to offline customer journey globally with the most advanced Ultrasonic Data Platform. Originally developed as a device independent method of communicating short messages to consumers, the software solution inserts, then extracts data using inaudible tones through standard speakers and microphones. Today, companies like Jaguar, Land Rover, Ticketmaster, Visa and more use this solution to create frictionless applications for consumers around the world. Founded in 2012, LISNR has offices in California and Ohio. Major investors include Intel, Jump Capital, Mercury Fund, R/GA, and Synchrony Financial.</p>
