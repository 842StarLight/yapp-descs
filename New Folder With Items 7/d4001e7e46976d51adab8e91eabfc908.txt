<p><b>Product Marketing Manager
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>PROS helps organizations to evolve their selling and business processes to meet the needs of buyers who are increasingly expecting faster, more personalized, and more intelligent experiences. The Product Marketing Manager will be a key member of the Solutions Marketing team, reporting to the Solutions Strategy Director and focused on marketing the PROS price optimization solution.
</p>
<p>The Solutions Marketing team supports the greater organization in discovery and definition of the key market problems, competitive positioning, and product strategy. The Product Marketing Manager plays an essential role in developing, building alignment on, and executing the portfolio go-to-market plan for the PROS pricing solutions portfolio. The Product Marketing Manager will work across the organization and across the marketing team to foster compelling content for demand generation, thought leadership, and sales enablement.
</p>
<p>According to Gartner, the market for B2B pricing solutions is merely 10% penetrated, but has grown at a rate of &gt;30% for the past couple years and is expected to grow quickly – in short, this is an exciting opportunity for marketing leaders interested in championing the go-to-market strategy of a high growth portfolio.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Company - PROS:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>PROS Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: PRO) provides AI-powered solutions that optimize selling in the digital economy. PROS solutions make it possible for companies to price, configure and sell their products and services in an omnichannel environment with speed, precision and consistency. Our customers, who are leaders in their markets, benefit from decades of data science expertise infused into our industry solutions.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>A Day in the Life of the Product Marketing Manager might include the following:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Research, understand, educate on, and advocate for the target buyer’s needs and market trends.
</li>
<li>Support the messaging and positioning for brand awareness, demand generation, sales enablement, and competitive differentiation.
</li>
<li>Support the development of meaningful educational, thought leadership content that guides the target market audience through awareness of a need for price optimization, the how-to of applying price optimization, and the selection of a price optimization solution. This content would include a variety of formats that include: research papers, eBooks, interactive web experiences, videos, social posts, blogs, presentations, and podcasts.
</li>
<li>Develop sales and partner enablement materials that help in the identification of and education for buyers who would benefit from PROS solutions.
</li>
<li>Foster engagement and enthusiasm in the community by promoting the stories and leadership of our customers.
</li>
<li>Provide insight and support to company leadership to inform the identification and development of new offerings (products, solutions or services).
</li>
<li>Oversee the launch planning and execution for key solution updates.<br>
</li>
</ul>
