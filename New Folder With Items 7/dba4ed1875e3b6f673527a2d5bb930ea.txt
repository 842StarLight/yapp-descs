<h2>Full-Time Center Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Center Associate delivers world-class customer service to all retail customers by receiving and processing packages for courier shipment and operating copiers, fax machines, binding equipment, laminating machines, and point-of-sale devices. The Associate expertly advises our valued customers by providing accurate information on our array of products, services, and best-value options and exhibits confidence by knowing best practices related to the industry. This is a fast paced work environment which requires the ability to multitask effectively.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate has previous retail sales experience, fast learner, strong computer and internet skills, a high school diploma or GED, a friendly and genuinely helpful demeanor, and a professional appearance.<br>
<br>
At this moment we are also looking for someone that lives close by that can handle our morning or afternoon shifts. We are the only UPS Store open 7 days, so availability on weekends is a plus.<br>
<br>
Notes:<br>
This is NOT a warehouse job.<br>
This is NOT a desk job.</p>
<br>
