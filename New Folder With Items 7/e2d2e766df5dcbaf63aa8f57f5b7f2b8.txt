<h2>Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Office Manager is responsible for timekeeping and payroll transmission. The Office Manager may also check the accuracy of figures, calculations, and postings pertaining to business transactions recorded by other workers.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepares Invoices for the Accounting Department to input</li>
<li>
Correctly pull all daily, weekly and periodic reports and distribute them to department managers (daily sales, weekly sales and labor, A/P reports, GL reports)</li>
<li>
Maintain a high level of integrity and confidentiality</li>
<li>
Ensure all store office functions are completed correctly and on time</li>
<li>
Keep office supplies in stock and to budget
</li>
<li>Customer service responsibilities to include handling telephone calls when needed</li>
<li>
Ensure Office Manager's office remains clean and organized</li>
<li>
Assists the payroll department in ensuring all time has been entered accurately and in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Process all payroll related paperwork; new hire, transfer, payroll/personnel changes, separation, LOAs, direct deposit ensuring necessary information is sent to the Central Support Office and maintained in proper files.
</li>
<li>Ensure receipt of proper I-9 identification and timeliness and receipt of re-certification when needed</li>
<li>
Prepare and maintain Team Member files ensuring accuracy, compliance, and confidentiality.
</li>
<li>Alert Support office in a timely and accurate manner to all garnishments, loan verifications, subpoenas and unemployment claims.</li>
<li>
Assist Support Office in reminding Team Members to enroll in benefits, and to inform about benefit eligibility</li>
<li>
Provide team member with necessary information and forms regarding work related injuries</li>
<li>
Ensure policy and OSHA regulations are followed regarding Workers Compensation and safety</li>
<li>
Ensure all related workers compensation paperwork is completed accurately and maintained in proper files</li>
<li>
Assist in hiring process including job postings and screening applicants when needed</li>
<li>
Work with Support Office to ensure that all federal, state and company leaves of absences ensuring compliance, accuracy and timeliness</li>
<li>
Maintain strict confidentiality as it pertains to all human resource issues</li>
<li>
Understand and adhere to federal and state employment and wage and hour laws</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul><li>
High school diploma at a minimum with an associate degree in business or accounting preferred, along with 1-2 years' experience in a related job, or an acceptable combination of education and experience.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Detail-oriented to avoid making errors and to recognize errors made by others.</li>
<li>
Discreet and trustworthy due to frequent contact with confidential material.</li>
<li>
Strong communication and interpersonal skills with the ability to communicate clearly and concisely, verbally and in writing.</li>
<li>
Strong aptitude for numbers.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Outlook, Excel, and Word and knowledge of specialized bookkeeping or accounting software.</li>
<li>
Ability to operate calculator, computer, and other general office equipment.</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational skills, with the ability to prioritize work and perform several tasks concurrently with ease and professionalism.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Physical Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Able to sit for long periods of time</li>
<li>
Some heavy lifting involved.</li>
</ul>
