<h2>Director/VP of Digital Strategy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>This role requires a proven successful track record in leading innovative digital teams, nurturing client relationships, evolving progressive digital platforms and E-Commerce businesses for our digital marketing group based out of Columbus Ohio.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build a high-performance and versatile digital team capable of driving transformational change for the organization; who can stay on top of cutting-edge technology trends and apply our go-to-market strategy</li>
<li>Design and manage broader digital strategy and execution for digital experience including website, content marketing, digital advertising, organic and paid search, and social media.</li>
<li>Develop a scalable and agile team to support incremental growth of the digital marketing group through strategic resource management.</li>
<li>Oversee key digital communication channels (blog, email and other online content), leading efforts to produce relevant and engaging communications that result in measurable impact.</li>
<li>Establish KPIs and metrics to report on performance of people, processes and programs and transform data into actionable insights and recommendations.</li>
<li>Provide leadership and management of the digital budget, operational metrics and mentorship/ development of a varied digital marketing talent.</li>
<li>Other functions as needed by management.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b><i>SKILLS &amp; EXPERIENCE REQUIRED:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required in business, marketing, or related field.
</li>
<li>7-10 years directly related, progressively responsible work experience.</li>
<li>5-7 years’ hands on experience leading a digital marketing and e-commerce team with a for-profit business organization.</li>
<li>Subject matter expertise in web production, site operations, UI/UX, content and digital marketing strategy and digital marketing tactics
</li>
<li>Experience in analytics and digital research ideal</li>
<li>Experience working with high revenue, small team style organizations.</li>
<br>
</ul>
