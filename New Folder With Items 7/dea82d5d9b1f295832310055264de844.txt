<h2>Manager Systems Engineering- NE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Silver Peak <b>Systems Engineer Manager, Enterprise Accounts</b> is the technical leader for all pre-sales system engineers within the Northeast Enterprise region. In this leadership role you will be required to hire, train and provide your team with the tools, training and guidance to be successful selling the Silver Peak SD-WAN solution. This role also requires that you have the skill, ability and willingness to step in and work alongside your team as an added member to assist with selling, architecting, installing and supporting Silver Peak in customer networks.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Why Silver Peak:</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
</p>
<p>Silver Peak is currently at the forefront one of the most exciting and disruptive trends in the history of the networking industry - the software-defined wide area network, or SD-WAN. Enterprises and service providers alike are aggressively turning to Silver Peak to help provide cutting-edge SD-WAN solutions to more effectively and efficiently connect their users to applications. Silver Peak is growing rapidly and building out our sales and marketing organizations to help drive our significant growth. We are currently seeking experienced and professional sales engineers to capitalize on our momentum, to drive direct enterprise sales, and to expand our market penetration around our products in this rapidly growing market.</p>
<p><b>
Who we are looking for:</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
</p>
<p>The Silver Peak <b>Systems Engineer Manager, Enterprise Accounts</b> is the technical leader for all pre-sales system engineers within the Northeast Enterprise region. In this leadership role you will be required to hire, train and provide your team with the tools, training and guidance to be successful selling the Silver Peak SD-WAN solution. This role also requires that you have the skill, ability and willingness to step in and work alongside your team as an added member to assist with selling, architecting, installing and supporting Silver Peak in customer networks.</p>
<p><br>
You will be based in a metro area in the Northeast United States and travel will be 50% - 75%. You will report to the Director of Technical Sales.</p>
<p><b>
What you will do (aka the Responsibilities):</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
Recruit and hire new pre-sales system engineers (SE) into sales territories</p>
<ul>
<li>
Train and mentor new system engineers to be experts in the Silver Peak product line</li>
<li>
HR responsibility for 8-10 direct reports including management, training, expenses and reviews</li>
<li>
Support the SE team through the evaluation of Silver Peak products, review architectures and mentoring through complex deployments</li>
<li>
Act in a role as the senior technical advisor in your territory providing advanced sales support, product detail and roadmap when required</li>
<li>
Manage and oversee the technical evaluation process for all open opportunities in your region</li>
<li>
Provide hands-on technical product installation support to the SE, customers and partners</li>
<li>
Provide direct SE support when the territory SE Is unavailable (time conflicts, PTO, backfill and newly created territories)</li>
<li>
Travel to customers, partners and industry events to support the sale of Silver Peak products</li>
<li>
Act as a point of escalation for pre and post sales technical issues</li>
<li>
Consistently relay product feedback and requirements to the product management team</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you are great at (aka the Requirements):</b></p>
<p>
Networking</p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to demonstrate a working knowledge of networking protocols such as TCP/IP, UDP, NAT, PAT, IPSEC, VPN, VoIP, QoS,</li>
<li>
Experience with VLANs and IP subnetting (IPV4 and IPV6)</li>
<li>
Knowledge and experience using routing protocols such as OSPF and BGP</li>
<li>
Recent experience using the CLI on major router and switch vendors (Cisco, Juniper)</li>
<li>
Experience using and configuring enterprise firewalls</li>
<li>
Troubleshooting skills that include hands-on experience with the configuration of networking equipment</li>
</ul>
<p>
Software Skills</p>

<li>
Experience using cloud providers such as Amazon, Microsoft Azure and VMware vCloud Air</li>
<li>
Microsoft Visio</li>
<li>
Microsoft Office Suite (Outlook, PowerPoint, Word, Excel)</li>

Microsoft Windows, Apple OS X, Linux Operating Systems</li>

Personal Skills</p>

Experience managing, hiring and training team members</li>

Team player willing to jump in and help out direct reports, peers and other teams within the company</li>

Comfortable presenting to a large group of people</li>

Strong, effective communication skills (written and verbal)</li>

Internal drive and positive attitude to go above and beyond whenever necessary to compete and win business</li>

</ul>

 Your Education, Experience and Certification:</b></p>
