<p>
Enterprise Information Security</p>
<p>
Since 1852, customers have trusted that Wells Fargo would keep their assets secure from theft and always available. Today, maintaining customer trust remains our underlying operating principle.</p>
<p>
Enterprise Information Security’s (EIS) vision is to provide Wells Fargo world leading cyber security risk management. Through a framework that addresses policy, process, operations, people, and technology, EIS protects Wells Fargo’s infrastructure, corporate data, and customer assets, and ensures alignment with applicable regulations and laws. EIS is part of Wells Fargo's Corporate Risk organization and is led by the Chief Information Security Officer.</p>
<p>
Technology Relationship Manager 2 – Cyber Security - Offensive Security Research Team</p>
<p>
We have a new opportunity for a Technology Relationship Manager 2 (TRM2) role on the Cyber Security team within the Cyber Threat Management group and will share responsibility in supporting the team in several areas, specifically project workflow, project management, vendor management, and threat information coordination. This key position will work closely with the rest of the Offensive Security Research and CTM leadership teams to educate partners on the mission and services provided by the team. The successful candidate will interact with the Blue Teams and Cyber Threat Intelligence to further develop those relationships to the benefit of the security of the bank. This role will be accountable for developing workflows, processes, threat research, and documentation in support of OSRT operations and activities. The position will support large-scale enterprise technical projects with high visibility and will help build the relationship between the business lines and other EIS teams. This position will help leverage existing OSRT data to provide trending analysis and insights into the value and use of research generated by the team.</p>
