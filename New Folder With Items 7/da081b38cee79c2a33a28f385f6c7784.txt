<p><b>
What's Your Role:</b></p>
<p>
Plus is seeking a Senior Consultant/Developer, that will contribute to identifying, communicating, and solving highly visible business practices, processes, and technology issues for our clients. The Senior Consultant/Developer will participate in delivering business solutions in a team oriented, collaborative environment. The focus of the senior consultant is to participate in design, solution architecture, technical architecture, and development and implementation efforts of business solutions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
How You'll Contribute:</b></p>
<p><b>
Provides consulting services</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Guides clients and team members to resolve requirements of varying complexity</li>
<li>
Determines implementation options using best practices approach</li>
<li>
Architects technical solutions to the appropriate needs of the client, based on Salesforce platform and client IT environment, both internally and cloud based</li>
<li>
Analyzes client data requirements and interprets into migration and/or integration transformation specifications</li>
<li>
Develops, unit tests and modifies Apex code using structured methodology</li>
<li>
Develops and deploys solutions that meet client requirements in a scalable and repeatable manner</li>
<li>
Provides clear and concise estimates to sales and delivery teams</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Communicates clearly and consistently</b></p>

Participates in team meetings and updates internal systems in a timely manner</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Meets professional standards</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develops quality deliverables and provides guidance to others on deliverables</li>

Adheres to project deadlines and commitments</li>
