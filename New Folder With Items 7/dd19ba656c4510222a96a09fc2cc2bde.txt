<p><b>
Opportunity summary:</b> As a Plant Manager, you are responsible for the day-to-day activities of maintaining the plant facility operations, including safety policies, personnel, housekeeping, maintenance, production activities, customer requirements, planning and scheduling. You will serve as a member of the management leadership team and must contribute to achieving sales objectives. You will also serve as a coach to team members and assist in the development and evaluation of direct reports.
</p>
<p><b>How you will impact WestRock:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Provide leadership and guidance on the coordination of plant activities through planning with Superintendents to ensure the manufacturing objectives are accomplished in a timely and cost-effective manner</li>
<li> Review customer requirements and specifications to identify improvement opportunities and implement solutions to increase customer satisfaction</li>
<li> Coordinate and maintain ongoing, effective, safety plans to ensure program adoption and implementation
</li>
<li> Offer new ideas to support fulfillment of department goals and procedures for continuous improvement, retention of customer base, company profitability, and employee job satisfaction
</li>
<li> Develop and manage defined communication channels across various stakeholder groups to build effective relationships and produce superior products</li>
<li> Manage P&amp;L, capital expenditures, manufacturing costs and operating efficiencies to improve plant profitability
</li>
<li> Monitor and control waste management to manage production costs and reduce environmental impacts</li>
</ul>
