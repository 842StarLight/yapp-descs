<p></p>
<p>(RN) Clinical Program Consultant - Pacific Northwest Region
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Role Summary:
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Provides expert content/professional leadership on complex Clinical Program Management assignments/projects. Exercises considerable creativity, foresight, and judgment in conceiving, planning, and delivering initiatives. Uses deep professional knowledge and acumen to advise functional leaders. Focuses on providing thought leadership within Clinical Program Management but works on broader projects, which require understanding of wider business. Recognized internally as a subject matter expert. Represents company externally and serves as conduit to educate and enable care coordination between stakeholders resulting in an optimal healthcare experience and customer satisfaction. Serves as clinical advisor to the sales organization, providing ongoing product and industry knowledge/education that positively influence cost, quality and customer satisfaction. Registered Nurse with a current active licensure and clinical practice experience.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Job Description
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Clinical Consultant, as part of the Market Facing Clinical Organization represents Cigna externally and enables the successful alignment between our most Complex Clients, Provider Partners and Stakeholders and Cigna's Clinical Programs, Products and Services. The Clinical Consultant supports the Sales Organization for key Cigna clients and contributes toward the successful execution of Value-Based partnerships such as Cigna’s Collaborative Care Organizations (CCC), working cross-organizationally to influence and drive change that results in improved quality, affordability and satisfaction for our customers.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities:
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Serves as conduit to educate and enable care coordination between stakeholders resulting in an optimal healthcare experience and customer satisfaction
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Partners with account teams and matrix partners, providing data analysis, clinical insights and consultative strategic recommendations that positively impact short and long-term program goals
</li></ul>
<p></p>

</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Registered Nurse with a current active licensure
</li></ul>

</li></ul>

</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven industry experience in a Health Sector related field (Health Insurance; Healthcare Professional and/or Delivery System Entities or Allied Health Industry)
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent time management, organizational, research, analytical, communication and interpersonal skills with demonstrated ability to build strong relationships and influence diverse teams
</li></ul>
<p></p>
