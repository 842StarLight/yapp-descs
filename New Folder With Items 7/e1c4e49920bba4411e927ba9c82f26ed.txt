<h2>Marketing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>POSITION PURPOSE</b><br>
<br>
Develop, coordinate, and implement multiple channels of communications to promote internally and externally Compass Health Network including through publication management, social media, and marketing.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS - JOB SPECIFIC</b><br>
<br>
<ul><li>Publication management of, but not limited to, the following:</li></ul>
o Compass Connection (bimonthly internal newsletter in printable and electronic formats)<br>
o Compass Viewpoint (monthly exterior newsletter in printable and electronic formats)<br>
o Provider Directory (biannual magazine-style publication listing of behavioral health, family and pediatric health, and dental health care providers)<br>
o Clinician Directory (biennial magazine-style publication listing of psychologists and therapists)<br>
o Location Directory (biennial magazine-style publication listing of Compass Health Network clinic locations)<br>
o Year in Review (annual magazine-style publication highlighting successes of organization)<br>
<ul>
<li>Help develop and implement multi-platform social media strategy and content calendar for agency; or oversee intern assigned to duties if applicable</li>
<li>Assist with website management, including content management and serving as liaison to website developer</li>
<li>Arrange for photos of all leadership, medical providers, and clinicians upon their hiring. Develop relationships with studio photographers across state where Compass Health Network holds orientation, establish quality standards at cost-effective rate, post photos on website and provide photo to team member upon receipt</li>
<li>Write narratives for brochures, websites, posters, and other collateral materials</li>
<li>Work internally and with outside agencies to ensure information on web properly &amp; positively reflects agency</li>
<li>Work with agency staff to market and promote fundraising events and other community activities</li>
<li>Manage material on all digital signs outside of clinics, assuring timely and fresh material</li>
<li>Maintain photo and video library to ensure current and accurate images are available for publication, advertising, and other needs as identified</li>
<li>Assist with events as needed</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
