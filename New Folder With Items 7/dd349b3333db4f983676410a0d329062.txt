<h2>Regional Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
<br>
We are looking for entrepreneurial minded sales leaders to concentrate exclusively on formulating and executing a sales strategy resulting in revenue growth and new customer acquisition. Leveraging your relationship with <b>Channel Partners</b> is essential to scale and grow your business.. Strong candidates have a proven track record of consistently <b>exceeding sales quotas</b> and thrive in an environment where they are in control of their earnings.<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Aggressively build, nurture, manage and support new sales and upsell existing customers in the assigned territory</li>
<li>
Build strong and effective relationships with channel partners, resulting in force multiplier revenue growth opportunities</li>
<li>
Leverage other team members within the overall organization to close business and exceed monthly, quarterly and annual bookings objectives</li>
<li>
Work programmatically to prospect, identify, qualify and develop sales pipeline</li>
<li>
Work with Marketing to conduct channel focused webinars, seminars, trade shows and other marketing related events</li>
<li>
Maintain a high volume of sales activity via calls, emails, &amp; social selling</li>
<li>
Work as part of a sales team toward reaching quarterly territory sales quota.</li>
<li>
Promote and evangelize product offerings within the market</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Must Have Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience selling enterprise security technology in a fast-paced and competitive market</li>
<li>
Superior volume of relationships in the Information Security community within the assigned territory</li>
<li>
Ability to prove existing relationships with key Channel Partners in territory</li>
<li>
Must have experience generating new business</li>
<li>
A proven record of consistently exceeding a sales quota of $2MM+ with the ASP of $75,000</li>
<li>
Requires strong selling skills into enterprise accounts with an understanding of large business organizations and their buying cycles.</li>
<li>
BA/BS Degree or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>
Strong technical and non-technical communication skills, including strong verbal, analytical and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>
Position requires frequent travel</li>
</ul>
