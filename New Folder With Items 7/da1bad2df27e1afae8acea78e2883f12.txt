<b>Job Description</b><br>
<br>
In this newly created role, you'll solve complicated problems for employers administering COBRA on behalf of former employees. You'll work closely with our software client success team to ensure our employer clients are getting as much value as possible while also giving former employees the high level of support they need in a time of transition. You'll use your analytical and communication skills to answer healthcare questions for individuals and families we're helping. The team members in this role will be integral to client adoption of our software and will contribute directly to the success of BerniePortal. This is an excellent opportunity for anyone who loves solving puzzles and is interested in learning, and growing with the organization.<br>
<br>
<b>Will you learn and grow?</b><br>

Without a doubt! You'll be on the cutting edge of technology with the best product in the industry. You'll learn the inner workings of benefits and HR for small to medium size employers. You'll become a more effective communicator, problem solver, and doer. You won't be afraid to roll up your sleeves to solve an important issue for a customer. And you'll gain technical knowledge in a growing high-demand industry.
