<p>Are you looking for a high energy, strategic, and fast-paced position as a Director, Digital and Demand Generation? Join Relias, the company changing lives throughout the world by helping healthcare organizations improve their clinical outcomes!</p>

In addition to unrivaled content, Relias offers pre-hire assessments and in-depth analytics that allows for the demonstration of skill and performance, resulting in better patient, resident and client outcomes, as well as better financial results. We are passionate about our product and our clients; what we deliver is truly something you can be proud to represent. Our clients think so, too – we proudly boast a retention rate close to 100%!</p>

<p></p>
<p>The Director, Digital &amp; Demand Generation is responsible for managing a team of 11 marketers: vertical-specific marketing managers, customer marketing, outbound marketing managers and digital specialists to drive demand for Relias’ solutions that meets or exceeds pipeline goals and, ultimately, drives revenue growth. This role will also manage paid search marketing and paid social marketing across the business. This is a cross-functional role within the organization, working closely with Strategic/Product Marketing and Sales teams to significantly impact our high-growth business through multiple channels.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>To be successful, you must be a revenue-centric marketing professional and performance-focused people manager with experience in inbound and outbound marketing. This role will focus on ensuring your team is creating effective marketing campaigns that drive enough leads through the marketing funnel (from new names to qualified leads to new opportunities) to meet the needs of their assigned sales teams.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU’LL BE DOING:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Structuring a high-performing, multi-channel marketing campaigns aligned with business goals and market dynamics</li>
<li>Developing and implementing a successful digital marketing strategy with our core partners.</li>
<li>Developing a deep understanding of our customers across the healthcare continuum, their buying process and our solutions.</li>
<li>Working in close collaboration with other teams within Marketing (Creative, Events, Product Marketing, Content Marketing, Marketing Operations) to build cross-functional cohesion.</li>
<li>Working with Alliances/Partnership team to expand reach.</li>
<li>Understanding demand generation programs that support both Inside Sales and Enterprise Sales teams.
</li>
<li>Using marketing and sales data to drive priorities for your team, setting goals, and identifying opportunities for improvement.</li>
<li>Collaborating closely with product marketing peers to ensure that a clear, effective go-to-market strategy is in place for every new product release and product update.
</li>
<li>Supervising and coaching a team of Demand Gen marketing managers to ensure they hit their new name and MQL targets and meet pipeline coverage targets.</li>
<li>Supervising and coaching a team of Outbound and Digital marketers to ensure campaigns are executed properly to hit new name and MQL targets.</li>
<li>Troubleshooting with other departments when things aren’t going well or there is a breakdown in communication and step in to support direct reports when needed.</li>
<li>Keeping up with industry trends and leverage innovative marketing tactics to drive successful campaigns.</li>
<li>Setting launch goals and building marketing campaigns programs to reach those goals. This can include strategy, channel optimization, marketing programs and message testing
</li>
<li>Taking ownership and initiative to support the marketing team and company through a pro-active and positive attitude</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
