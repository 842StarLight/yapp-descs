<h2>Property Manager/Maintenance Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Title</b>:</p>
<p>Property Manager</p>
<p><b>Company and Location: </b></p>
<p>Park One Properties, Inc - Walnut Creek, CA</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b>:</p>
<p>This is an advanced level, professional position with job growth and career potential within the property management industry. Candidate hired will be managing the day to day operations of the maintenance department.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities and Tasks: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize our property management software to create and process dozens of open work orders</li>
<li>Troubleshoot property maintenance issues and dispatch to appropriate vendors</li>
<li>Obtain, review, and evaluate vendor quotes for various repairs and projects.</li>
<li>Communicate with property owners regarding the management of projects</li>
<li>Review properties for compliance with regulatory and safety guidelines</li>
<li>Conduct property inspections to ensure they meet turn over standards</li>
<li>Manage and maintain a lockbox tracking system</li>
<li>Perform regulatory audits on vendors to ensure they comply with insurance requirements</li>
<li>Create and disseminate final move out notes to tenants</li>
<li>Post legally required notices to properties</li>
<li>Work with the finance department to ensure invoices are processed in a timely manner</li>
<li>Supervise/manage on-site managers at local apartment buildings we manage</li>
<li>Help the company improve by developing and implementing efficient and productive corporate processes, procedures, and systems.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications and Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years’ experience in the property management field</li>
<li>Experience with property management software like Appfolio or Yardi</li>
<li>Experience in and/or knowledge of maintenance or construction management is preferred, but not required.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in construction management, engineering or some other technical field is preferred but not required.</li>
<li>Strong interest in real estate, construction, and business. Experience in these fields is not required but candidates should have a genuine interest in these fields.</li>
<li>Candidate must be a super multi-tasker and be able to handle multiple projects at one time.</li>
<li>Extensive experience with mobile communication devices such as smartphones, tablets, &amp; laptops.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills.</li>
<li>Candidate must be pro-active and require minimal to no-supervision.</li>
<li>Candidate is required to meet deadlines for work completion and be able to swiftly transition into a different job as required.</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate well, have a working cell phone (text/email), and have a reliable method of transportation/valid driver's license.</li>
<li>Willingness and ability to work and travel throughout the East Bay.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Hours: </b></p>
<p>This is a full-time position.</p>

<p><b>Company Info: </b></p>
<p>Park One Properties, Inc is a full-service real estate brokerage offering sales, leasing, and property management services within the entire Bay Area. Property management is the cornerstone of our client services. We manage over 700 units within our service area. Our management inventory includes a wide variety of residential and commercial properties including condominiums, single family homes, apartment buildings, retail strip centers, multi-tenant light industrial buildings, office buildings, etc. The success of our business is based on excellent customer service and over 50% of our new management business comes through referrals from existing clients.</p>
