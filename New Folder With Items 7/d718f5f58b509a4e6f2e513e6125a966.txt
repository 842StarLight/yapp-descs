<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Introduction</b></h2>
<br>
<p>
Allina Health is dedicated to the prevention and treatment of illness and enhancing the greater health of individuals, families and communities throughout Minnesota and western Wisconsin. A not-for-profit health care system, Allina Health cares for patients from beginning to end-of-life through its 90+ clinics, 12 hospitals, 16 pharmacies, specialty care centers and specialty medical services that provide home care, senior transitions, hospice care, home oxygen and medical equipment, and emergency medical transportation services. Allina Health is a vibrant, growing organization with opportunities to suit your professional skills and a diverse work environment to match your specific interests. We believe employees are our greatest asset and are dedicated to helping you develop and maximize your professional skills.</p>
<p>
The Commons, located in Minneapolis, is the headquarters of Allina Health, a not-for-profit health care organization serving patients and communities. Approximately 1,600 corporate and administrative service employees work at offices within Midtown Exchange, the site of the historic Sears retail complex at Chicago Avenue and E. Lake Street. This community landmark, adjacent to the campus of Abbott Northwestern Hospital, part of Allina Health, is the second largest building in Minnesota and includes offices, an internationally themed public market and residential units.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<br>
<p>
The Digital Marketing Associate role is responsible for collaborating with the marketing team and managing digital campaigns. As a digital marketer will repurpose content across multiple channels that drive traffic and ultimately leads to brand awareness, consideration, and demand. In addition, this role will understand how to effectively use social media channels to grow brand reach and promote engagement and community building through paid and organic channels.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage end-to-end digital projects through the implementation of social/digital strategy</li>
<li>
Write and execute day-to-day content and communications for social media platforms using established brand personality and identity</li>
<li>
Schedule and implement editorial calendar &amp; activity. Primary platforms used: Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, Twitter, YouTube, MyAccount, LinkedIn</li>
<li>
Execute customer activations and promotions</li>
<li>
Communicate and update internal teams on marketing campaigns, lead gen and project status</li>
<li><p>
Assist with proofing of adverting Search Engine Marketing, online posts, presentations, collateral and any material for the marketing and sales team for quality, accuracy, and consistency.</p></li>
<li>
Monitor social media on a daily basis and engage in conversations on behalf of the brand. Ability to connect with an audience and create meaningful relationships</li>
<li><p>
Assist the marketing team to develop and monitor online ads, ensure strong CTA and control of budget/targeting. Analyze performance metrics.</p></li>
<li>
Maintain website(s) and supporting programs to optimize SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMIZATION, updates and event promotions.</li>
<li>
Post content on social media channels</li>
<li><p>
Build and manage social media profiles and presence, including Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.</p></li>
</ul>
