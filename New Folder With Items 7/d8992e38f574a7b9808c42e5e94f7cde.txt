<h2>Sales and Marketing Operations Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">PromoteIQ delivers intelligent vendor marketing solutions built for the next generation of e-commerce. Our solutions help retailers implement, automate, and scale brand-funded marketing programs on e-commerce sites. PromoteIQ is a New York City-based technology company that works with the US's largest e-commerce retailers. Learn more about us at https://www.promoteiq.com ( https://www.promoteiq.com ).
<br><br>
Who we're looking for
<br><br>
At PromoteIQ, our Demand Sales team is focused on developing and managing partnerships with key decision makers at brands and agencies and unlocking marketing budgets. As Sales and Marketing Operations Specialist on the Demand Sales team, you will work collaboratively across the sales organization to develop impactful and measurable initiatives to accelerate revenue growth and enable efficiency. You will partner with key leadership stakeholders to advise on sales growth strategy, focused on how our processes, people, and tools can best work together. On any given day, you may be collaborating with Demand Sales team members to identify and maintain key customer segments, develop and execute marketing programs, or supporting ongoing sales reporting needs.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with the Demand Sales team to identify gaps and areas of opportunity</li>
<li>Prepare and provide reporting and analyses for clients and internal teams on a regular cadence</li>
<li>Help to design, implement and optimize demand generation programs, including, but not limited to, email marketing, webinars, collateral production, customer stories and other B2B marketing channels</li>
<li>Own ongoing management of sales tools and technologies. Maintain database health across sales/marketing tools (Salesforce, etc.) - ensure data cleanliness &amp; completeness</li>
<li>Provide recommendations to improve efficiencies in daily workflows and processes for sales team</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS in Business or quantitative field</li>
<li>1 years experience in sales development/sales, operations, or analyst role</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce, specifically reporting</li>
<li>Keen analytical skills - ability to break down a problem into its core parts; identifying implications, timeframes and sequences</li>
<li>Ability to work cooperatively with individuals across the organization and independently when appropriate</li>
<li>Proficiency with Excel</li>
<li>Fast-learner, organized and self-motivated, with exceptional attention to detail</li>
<li>Flexible, able to quickly adapt to evolving business needs</li>
</ul>
Preferred Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Background in B2B SaaS/tech/start-up</li>
<li>Experience with email marketing technologies</li>
</ul>
