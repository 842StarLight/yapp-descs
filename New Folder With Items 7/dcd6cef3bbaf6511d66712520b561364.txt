<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review customer contracts and billings for non-standard terms, multiple elements, and to ensure that revenue has been recognized in accordance with US GAAP
</li>
<li>Perform ASC 606 (Revenue from Contracts with Customers) contract analysis
</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales, legal, and finance to appropriately structure non-standard sales agreements
</li>
<li>Perform month-end duties including account reconciliation, computation of revenue/deferred revenue, royalty accrual estimates, and review of revenue trends
</li>
<li>Interact with external auditors to gather relevant support and ensure compliance with internal controls
</li>
<li>Perform ad-hoc analysis as necessary to support business partners
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor Degree in Accounting is required. CPA preferred.
</li>
<li>Minimum 3years of assurance/audit experience in Public Accounting. Big 4 or national tiered firm preferred.
</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of US GAAP, specifically revenue recognition rules (ASC 605 and ASC 606). Knowledge and experience with software revenue recognition (ASC 985) a plus
</li>
<li>Prior experience with contract review and industry experience in revenue accounting a plus
</li>
<li>Prior experience with business combination accounting (ASC 805) a plus
</li>
<li>Strong administrative, organizational, and problem solving skills
</li>
<li>Experience with larger ERP systems and related systems (Oracle and Hyperion) and proficiency with excel
</li>
<li>Ability to work as a team player as well as autonomously; project management and multi-tasking skills essential
</li>
</ul>
