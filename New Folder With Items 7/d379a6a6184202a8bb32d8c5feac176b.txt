<h2>Engineering Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div><div>
<div><div>
<div><div>Artisan Technology Group is seeking candidates to fill full-time and internship openings for the Engineering Technician position and join our cutting-edge and highly-technical team in a goal-oriented, challenging, and intellectual atmosphere.
<br>
<br>
Our company's primary focus is the sales and support of high-end electronic test and measurement equipment for commercial, industrial, and military applications. The Engineering Technician is a full-time position designed for a technically oriented person with an electronics background who is proficient at testing and troubleshooting. This position will be exposed to wide variety of specialized electronic test and measurement equipment.
<br>
<br>
For our Engineering Technician Internships, the internship is developed for students to assist our engineers and technicians with short-term projects and offers exposure to a wide variety of specialized electronic equipment.
</div></div>
<br>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<div><div><b>Job Description
</b></div></div>
<br>
<div><div>The primary duties of the Engineering Technician will be to evaluate and repair incoming and outgoing equipment and to verify the accuracy of technical documentation.<br>
<br>
<p><b>Principal Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Equipment Evaluation and Repair</li>
<li>Verify Technical Accuracy of Website Information</li>
<li>Refine Documented Test Procedures</li>
<li>Work with equipment vendors to obtain documentation, software, and technical information
</li>
<li>Coordinating with team members and other internal departments of the organization
</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<br>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<div>
<div><b>Knowledge/Experience/Skills:</b></div>
</div>
<br>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Good problem-solving skills are required.</li>
<li>Excellent organization, communication, and computer skills are required.</li>
<li>Training is on-site and set to the pace and capability of the candidate.</li>
<li>Interaction and collaboration with other engineers and technicians within as well as outside the company is ongoing and encouraged.
</li>
<li>Enrolled in a College or University working towards or graduate with an Electrical Engineering or Computer Engineering Degree
</li>
<li>Technical skills required - familiarity with the following
<ul>
<li>Capacitors, Resistors, Diodes, etc.
</li>
<li>Soldering and Minor Circuit Board Work</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<br>
<div><div>Work is performed in our well-stocked technical lab and is primarily hands-on. Candidates must have or must be working towards receiving an electrical engineering or computer engineering degree.
<br>
<br>
<b>Full-Time Position:</b><br>
<br>
This position is Full-Time, 40 hours per week, standard workday, Monday through Friday at our facility in Champaign, IL. Starting wage range is $18.00 - $24.00 per hour and is commensurate with background, qualifications, and experience. Full-Time benefits include Healthcare, Dental and Life Insurance, Healthcare Flexible Spending Account (FSA), Dependent Care Flexible Spending Account (FSA), 401k, Fitness Program, Paid Vacation, and Paid Holidays.
<br>
<br>
<b>Part-Time / Internship Position:</b><br>
<br>
Part-Time position: 20-30 hours per week, standard workday, Monday through Friday. Wages commensurate with background, qualifications, and experience. Starting wage range is $15.00 - $22.00 per hour and is commensurate with background, qualifications, and experience.</div></div>
