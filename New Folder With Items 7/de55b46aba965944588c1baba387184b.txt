<h2>Customer Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><p>Gallagher is a global leader in insurance, risk management and consulting services. We help businesses grow, communities thrive and people prosper. We live a culture defined by The Gallagher Way, our set of shared values and guiding tenets. A culture driven by our people, over 30,000 strong, serving our clients with customized solutions that will protect them and fuel their futures.</p></div>
<div>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Responsible for sales support activities such as account analyses, research, and sales promotion.</div></li>
<li><div>Focus is on implementation of marketing plans and initiatives.</div></li>
<li><div>Assists in the development of account specific marketing plans.</div></li>
<li><div>Maintains and manages timelines so that marketing milestones are met.</div></li>
</ul>
<div>Additional Considerations:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborates with the regional marketing team to track campaign effectiveness and overall success.</li>
<li>
Responsible for preparing reports of findings, illustrating data graphically and translating complex findings into written text and in addition, measure the effectiveness of marketing, advertising, and communications programs and strategies.</li>
<li>
Assists producers with renewal and new business processes; this includes accompanying producers as they meet with client, prospects, and underwriters as needed.</li>
<li>
Gathers data on competitors and analyze their prices, sales, and method of marketing and distribution.</li>
<li>
Attends staff conferences to provide management with information and proposals concerning the promotion, distribution, design, and pricing of company products or services.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<div>Required:</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>2 or more years related experience.</li>
</ul>
<div>
Desired:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Master degree in a related field (Public Relations or Communication/Marketing) and 1 year related experience).</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience working with:<ul>
<li>
document management software (i.e. Adobe Systems Adobe Acrobat)</li>
<li>
electronic mail software (i.e. Microsoft Outlook)</li>
<li>
graphics or photo imaging software (i.e. Adobe Systems Adobe Photoshop)</li>
<li>
office suite software (i.e. Microsoft Office)</li>
<li>
presentation software (i.e. Microsoft PowerPoint)</li>
<li>
spreadsheet software (i.e. Microsoft Excel)</li>
<li>
sales and marketing software (i.e. Salesforce)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Previous insurance knowledge and experience managing client relationships.</li>
<li>Knowledge and previous experience with account servicing functions (processing invoices, check requests, endorsements, etc.).</li>
</ul>
<p>Work Traits:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in using technology as a tool to maximize productivity and quality.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Comfortably engages others in consultative discussion.
</li>
<li>Effectively manages/balances multiple and sometimes competing priorities.</li>
<li>Works in a self-directed manner.</li>
</ul>
</div>
