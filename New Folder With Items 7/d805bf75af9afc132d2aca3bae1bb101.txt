<h2>Director, Construction</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>To oversee the construction and commissioning of new construction including overall project management and budgetary controls with an emphasis on mission critical electrical &amp; mechanical systems.
<p><b>Essential Functions:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage multiple data center construction projects
</li>
<li>Manage the life cycle of Design/Build projects
</li>
<li>Manage technical and contractual issues during the projects
</li>
<li>Conduct weekly project status meetings and provide weekly status reports
</li>
<li>Oversee all engineers, subcontractors and vendors working on the projects
</li>
<li>Act with decisive authority to ensure company policies are followed
</li>
<li>Read and interpret architectural and engineering construction documents
</li>
<li>Set and work within budgets, as well as monitor project costs and contractor productivity on both time &amp; materials projects and change orders
</li>
<li>Keep proper, organized records of all check lists, safety records, and other project correspondence
</li>
<li>Work with the VP of Data Center Operations and CFO to establish Capital Budgets and Forecast
</li>
<li>Strive to find cost savings through procurement, re-design, and project management
</li>
<li>Work with Sales to provide projected cost for budgetary quotes on potential customer proposals
</li>
<li>Provide assistance to Operations during emergency situations
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Certified Project Manager (PMI) or similar project management certification (equivalent work experience may be substituted for certification)
</li>
<li>Must be able to handle multiple construction projects simultaneously
</li>
<li>Must be willing to travel between 35-50% of the time as well as oversee multiple projects in multiple states simultaneously
</li>
<li>Proven record of leadership, strong verbal and written communications abilities, superior interpersonal and organizational skills, computer proficiency, capacity to multi-task in a fast-paced environment, impeccable integrity and can-do attitude are all essential to success
</li>
<li>Must be able to implement strong cost controls, have the ability to identify potential problems and resolve them in a timely manner, monitor and enforce schedule goals and project quality
</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office software &amp; Microsoft Project version 2003 or higher
</li>
<li>A proven record of accomplishments entering new cities and putting together construction projects from beginning to end ensuring the timelines are met
</li>
<li>A thorough understanding of contracting strategies including but not limited to lump sum, cost plus, fee-based, T&amp;M
</li>
<li>A thorough understanding of material take-off &amp; cost estimating experience, and a working understanding of labor rates, general conditions, overhead, &amp; profit
</li>
<li>Knowledge of AutoCad is a plus
</li>
<li>Knowledge of network &amp; operating systems support a plus
</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills
</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Ability to develop and document procedures
</li>
<li>Must be able to work with people at all levels internally and within the customer &amp; vendor environments
</li>
<li>Must be able to work in a team environment
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience/Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>15 years’ experience working in mission critical data center constructions, UPS system installations, generator installations and have overall critical facility knowledge
</li>
<li>Minimum of 15 years project management experience in one of the following fields: Electrical Contracting, General Contracting or Construction Management
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education: </b>Four years of College resulting in a Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Certifications: </b>None
</p>
