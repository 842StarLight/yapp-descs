<h2>Project Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Enviva is seeking a detail-oriented and self-reliant Buyer to support our Capital Projects team the project under construction in MS. The Buyer will report to the Director of Procurement and will successfully administer the purchasing function for a major industrial construction project for new wood pellet biomass fuel facility.</p>
<ul>
<li>Support the cost-effective procurement of minor equipment, services and supplies for the project.
</li>
<li>Executes procurement activities for the projects.
</li>
<li>Conduct competitive bid and contracting process for site equipment and service contracts.
</li>
<li>Participates in or directly negotiates purchase orders and contracts and ensures compliance with contracts for products and/or services; collaborates with Procurement, Project Manager and Corporate counsel on unique conditions and/or situations.
</li>
<li>Administer the vendor qualification process and financial credit reviews.
</li>
<li>Administers the approval process for purchasing documents, ensures proper approvals and archives approvals.
</li>
<li>Implements and follows auditable procedures consistent with accounting protocols and reconcile on a monthly basis with Project Manager and Project Controls.
</li>
<li>Works closely with the Project Manager to insure decisions are cohesive with the project.
</li>
<li>Negotiates extended mechanical warranties, liquidated damages, payment terms, and two-year performance warranties.
</li>
<li>Enters and issues purchase orders and change orders in the purchasing/accounting system.
</li>
<li>Proactively expedites deliveries of goods to meet the project completion schedule.
</li>
<li>Organizes and retains electronic and hard copies of all documents in proper locations.
</li>
<li>Develop and submit monthly and weekly purchasing reports to project management and project controls.
</li>
<li>Close out contracts and purchase orders as the work is completed and materials delivered and at the completion of the project.</li>
</ul>
