<h2>Embedded Software Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>POSITION SUMMARY
</div>
<div>Provide engineering support for the development, modification, testing, and production of products for
</div>
<div>industrial applications.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Work with multi‐disciplinary engineering team and customer to identify and recommend hardware needs and requirements for project/product.
</div></li>
<li><div>Modify or assist in the development of software architecture and design for new products, prototype, or redesign; including sourcing build environments and writing technical support and control documentation.
</div></li>
<li><div>Read Block Diagrams and detail circuit schematics and layout of proposed or existing hardware and assess the software and driver requirements.
</div></li>
<li><div>Participates in cross‐divisional Edge IoT projects and/or proposals.
</div></li>
<li><div>Develop, oversee, and document automated tests for electrical hardware design and products as well as final operating drivers and systems.
</div></li>
<li><div>Evaluate and investigate product failures, and recommend repair or design modifications based on specifications, cost, resources, and risks.
</div></li>
<li><div>Use reliability and quality tools and software to assess hardware designs.
</div></li>
<li><div>Work with hardware engineers, project management, quality engineering, production and customers to refine or modify hardware design or overall product.
</div></li>
<li><div>Support manufacturing group in production of product and troubleshoot any related design issues.
</div></li>
<li><div>Integrate, verify and validate new product designs and/or changes, provide technical support, including documentation and reports for internal Sales staff and external customers.
</div></li>
<li><div>Design, develop, implement, verify, and debug validate embedded device drivers and higher level code, system software and UIs.
</div></li>
<li><div>Perform root cause analysis and develop solutions as necessary; utilizing configuration management tools, defect tracking tools, programmers and debuggers.
</div></li>
<li><div>Analyze and understand existing products and their implementation to determine best design and method to add and/or change features and functionality.
</div></li>
<li><div>Other duties as assigned and required for business operations.
</div></li>
<li><div>Experience working in embedded systems design with preemptive, multitasking real‐time operating systems.<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications<br>
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<ul><li><div>Bachelor of Science required. Preferably in Electrical Engineering, Physics, Electronic Engineering, Computer E ngineering, or Computer Science.<br>
</div></li></ul>
<div></div>
<ul><li><div>Range of 0‐10 years combined work experience in areas including embedded software design, electronics, and hardware driver development. 5 years preferred.<br>
</div></li></ul>
<div></div>
<div>SKILLS AND ABILITIES REQUIREMENTS<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Ability to creatively approach challenges and cleverly solve difficult problems.
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to independently identify daily design and customer support tasks, self‐start and motivate.
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to manage several projects and prioritize appropriately, with minimal supervision.
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to work in a collaborative team environment as well as independently.
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to learn and work in virtual environments (CAD &amp; Hardware build packages) in‐which candidate’s skills are low but persevere and overcome skill gaps autonomously.
</div></li>
<li><div>Some experience programming with embedded systems designs and multithreaded real‐time operating systems is required.
</div></li>
<li><div>Solid programming experience. Preferably in C or C++, .NET, and Java programming languages.<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>SKILLS AND ABILITIES PREFFERED<br>
 </div>
<div></div>

</div></li>
<li><div>Experience and proficient in the use of laboratory test equipment such as multi‐meters, scopes, logical analyzers, data loggers, and custom computing devices.
</div></li>
<li><div>Proficient in the application of engineering analysis methods and interpreting results to convey information and provide documentation.
</div></li>
<li><div>Experience building prototypes/demos based on drawings, specifications, and/or other information.
</div></li>
<li><div>Experience creating drawings, controlled documents and design configurations from concepts.
</div></li>
<li><div>Experience with full cycle product development.
</div></li>
<li><div>Experience with soldering and removing through hole and surface mount components.
</div></li>
<li><div>Experience designing in computer aided design software packages and a set of disparate hardware/software build development environments.<br>
<div></div>
<br>
