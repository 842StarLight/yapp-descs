<h2>Web & Owned Channel Strategy Vice President, Commercial Bank</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Commercial Banking delivers extensive industry knowledge, local expertise and seamless global capabilities to clients—including corporations, municipalities, financial institutions, real estate investors and owners, and not-for-profit organizations—with annual revenue generally ranging from $20 million to $2 billion. We offer a comprehensive set of financial products and services including credit, asset-based lending, real estate finance and treasury. We also provide clients with access to investment banking and asset management services.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The Commercial Banking Marketing Team is looking for a talented Web Strategist to transform our site into a best-in-class B2B website reflective of our brand. This role is best suited for a high achiever with deep expertise in creating phenomenal user experiences on responsive, SEO-optimized websites that drive sales pipeline and meet the diverse needs of prospects, customers and partners. The ideal candidate has outstanding communications and project management skills, a solid understanding of the technology, business, and community aspects of web strategy, and are excited about working with a high performance, results oriented team.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Specifically, the web strategist will be responsible for crafting web strategy including but not limited to web competitor analysis, information architecture strategy, personalization strategy, engagement channel strategy, and more. We are expecting you to have a strong understanding of latest industry trends and a thorough understanding of digital marketing as it relates to driving demand.
</div>

</ul>

<li>

<li>

<li>
Conduct Digital Quality Assurance as needed.</li>

<li>

<li>
Develop recommendations to optimize CB’s website critical path and the information architecture, technology stack, and other elements associated with doing so.</li>
