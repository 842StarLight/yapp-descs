<h2>Manager of General Accounting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary
</b><br>
</p>
<p>This position is responsible for overseeing General Accounting procedures and staff for the APG Media of Florida Region and all of its publications. Setting up and entering information for the publications, to assist the Advertising staff with problems. Work closely with Accounts Payable and Payroll as well as other departments as needed.
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Job Functions
</b><br>
</p>
<p>The successful candidate must be a flexible, attentive to details, diplomatic, and able to multi-task. This position will work with different personality types and across a number of publications. Candidates for this position should have strong interpersonal skills and client orientation and exhibit an ability to work effectively with internal and external contacts as a team member as well as a team leader. It would be beneficial for candidates to be conscious of deadlines, manage their time well, organized, and eager to learn.
<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions
</b><br>
</p>
<p>· Perform job duties and conduct self in accordance with company core values.
<br>
</p>
<p>· Initiate and nurture effective, professional relationships with internal and external contacts.
<br>
</p>
<p>· Responsible for the daily accounting requirements of established multi-location business.
<br>
</p>
<p>· The position will perform monthly analysis and reconciliation of all accounts, month-end and annual closing journal entries and generation of financial statements
<br>
</p>
<p>· Supports the CFO with account analysis, frequent requests for data and information to the parent company
<br>
</p>
<p>· Works with the business unit managers to provide analysis and information needed to operate the business and prepare annual budgets
<br>
</p>
<p>· Provides information to CPA firm for the annual audit
<br>
</p>
<p>· Prepares monthly and annual state tax returns (property taxes, sales taxes, abandoned property, etc.)
<br>
</p>
<p>· Works closely with Billing Manager, Payroll Manager, Accounts Payable Manager to ensure accurate recording of business transactions.
<br>
</p>
<p>· Works with our local bankers on wire transfers, positive pay, etc.
<br>
</p>
<p>· Works with the financial software vendor to keep organization fiscally compliant<br>
<br>
</p>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Benefits
</b></h3>
<p>The fringe benefits include medical (split between employer and employee), dental and vision options (employee paid). There are three company-sponsored benefits, including short-term disability, long-term disability and a term life insurance policy. The company pays for these six major holidays (Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year’s, Memorial Day, Independence Day and Labor Day). In addition, employees accrue 3.33 hours of paid leave per pay period in the first year of employment (80 hours) and increasing to 5.0 hours per pay period (120 hours) after their one-year anniversary of employment. The company has the option of making a discretionary match to the 401K retirement plan after year’s end.</p>
