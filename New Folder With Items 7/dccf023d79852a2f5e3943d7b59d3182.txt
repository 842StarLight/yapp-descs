<h2>Lead UX Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Vbrick's mission is to simplify the complexity of creating, managing and delivering video for use within the enterprise helping organizations unleash the power of video. Customers use Vbrick to seamlessly embed video communications within their core business applications including corporate communications, live executive broadcasts, sales and employee onboarding, product training and knowledge transfer, distance learning and marketing. With more than a thousand customers in over 100 countries, Vbrick is a leading provider of enterprise video platforms.</p>
<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
</b></h2>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview</b></h2>
<p>We are looking for a Lead UX Designer to join our Product team to help us revolutionize the Enterprise Video user experience for some of the largest and well-known companies in the world. This position reports directly to the Vice President of Product.</p>
<p>
Our team envisions a world where large enterprises can leverage the power of video to engage, empower and transform their organizations without worry. We do this combining consumer grade experiences like YouTube, Netflix or Twitch with enterprise level security and workflows.</p>
<p>
This role is based at our headquartersin Herndon, VA.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What you'll do:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Drive the User Experience for our flagship product called Rev, providing a consumer grade user experience for the enterprise.</li>
<li>Work closely with our product managers</li>
<li>Lead and improve our UX and UI design process</li>
<li>Perform qualitative and quantitative UX research to inform product refinements, refactoring and feature designs.</li>
<li>Define and maintain the Rev UI Design System</li>
<li>Hands-on UI design from low fidelity prototypes through to high fidelity mockups and CSS specifications.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b> </b><b>What you'll bring:</b>
</h2>
<ul>
<li>BA or equivalent experience in graphic design, human factors, or similar experience</li>
<li>5+ years of UX and UI design experience</li>
<li>3+ years of design experience for enterprise software or SaaS application</li>
<li>Portfolio of work demonstrating breadth and depth in UX and visual design</li>
<li>Proven ability to gather qualitative and quantitative UX research from a variety of sources</li>
<li>Extremely strong visual design skills and attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to work and innovate at a variety of scales, from large systems-level thinking down to technical specifications for your designs</li>
<li>Ability to both speak up with your informed opinion, and to carry through with excellence on product directives</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>You'll hit it out of the park if:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>You have experience with Material Design or other design systems</li>
<li>You have experience working on consumer or enterprise video products</li>
<li>You have prior experience with Balsamiq, InVision or similar prototyping tools</li>
<li>You have hands on experience with UI design tools like Photoshop, Adobe XD or Sketch</li>
<li>You have hands on web development experience</li>
<br>
</ul>
