<h2>Manager, Regional Training</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Reports directly to the Vice President of Certification and Field Training, and is responsible for all aspects of training within the assigned regional group of Maaco locations. Assist the regional operations team in identifying opportunities to improve the operational execution of shops within the territory by creating action items that will increase the center’s performance in Sales, Car Count, ARO, and Customer Experience metrics. Protect and Promote Maaco’s brand image, processes, and quality standards. In efforts that are in line with the company’s performance and profitability.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Ø Achieve Performance and Profitability through Training and KPI metrics by coaching, managing and consulting Maaco locations.</p>
<p>Ø Partner with Regional Operations team to identify training &amp; development needs.</p>
<p>Ø Perform scheduled center visits addressing the team’s action items.</p>
<p>Ø Drive progress through Maaco’s Certification program.</p>
<p>Ø Drive participation in marketing and operational initiatives</p>
<p>Ø Determines training requirements by studying sales and marketing strategic plans and current sales results; reviewing results of trainer coaching; evaluating training effectiveness.</p>
<p>Ø Evaluate and report training program(s) effectiveness, and implement changes as needed.</p>
<p>Ø Gathers feedback from trainees during and following training to determine instructional and content effectiveness.</p>
<p>Ø Utilize observations and data driven insights to provide directional coaching to improve conversion, retention, car count, sales, and NPS performance.</p>
<p>Ø Works shoulder to shoulder with the Team to set direction and champion all elements of People Development.</p>
<p>Ø Administers and controls training G&amp;A budget goals.</p>
<p>Ø Support market meetings and peer-to-peer related group meetings</p>
<p>Ø Drive franchisee engagement in Maaco’s continuous improvement plans</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent job experience in related field.</li></ul>
<ul><li> 2- 5 years of multi-unit (franchise/chain) training experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to provide financial review and analysis to Business Partners</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to provide sales, marketing and operational leadership.</li></ul>
<ul><li> P&amp;L knowledge/experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Dedication to impacting results…with a “whatever it takes” attitude.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent interpersonal communications skills with all levels (Pit Tech to President).</li></ul>
<ul><li> Willing and able to travel approximately 90%.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficient in group training, presentations and public speaking.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite, or other customer management software, or other dashboard management software.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience in facilitating group learning events</li></ul>
