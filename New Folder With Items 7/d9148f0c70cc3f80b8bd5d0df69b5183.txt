<li>
Driving a deeper customer understanding and empathy across the product and technology teams using personas</li>
<li>
Utilizing relevant research methods to gather user experience insights and translate these into measurable connected experience outcomes aligned to business goals</li>
<li>
Communicating user experience vision in a way that brings the customer journey to life</li>
<li>
Defining business impact of proposed roadmap deliverables</li>
