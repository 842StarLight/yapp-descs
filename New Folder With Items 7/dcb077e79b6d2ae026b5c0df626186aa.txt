<h2>Senior Marketing Operations Manager, Lyft Business</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">At Lyft, community is what we are and it's what we do. It's what makes us different. To create the best ride for all, we start in our own community by creating an open, inclusive, and diverse organization where all team members are recognized for what they bring.
<br><br>
Working with Lyft Business makes the ride matter. Thousands of organizations partner with Lyft Business to move people they care about — from employees and customers, to patients, travelers, and more. Together, we design transportation experiences and programs that make the ride matter.
<br><br>
The Lyft Business Marketing team engages and converts SMBs and large enterprises alike — including airports, healthcare organizations, travel &amp; hospitality, universities, and more. Our work is challenging and rewarding and we are looking for customer-focused team members.
<br><br>
We are looking for a Senior Marketing Operations Manager to join our Marketing team dedicated to Lyft Business. As a Senior Marketing Operations Manager, you will work closely with Marketing, Sales, and Sales Operations to orchestrate systems and processes, drive alignment across Marketing and Sales, and build scalable solutions. You will shape the success of Lyft Business by outlining the long-term vision for our tech stack and processes, owning lead management and automation, overseeing the marketing tech stack, maintaining the integrity of our marketing database, and leading flow and assignment.
<br><br>
*****************
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
*****************
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead strategy and creation of the overall Marketing Operations roadmap to reduce inefficiencies</li>
<li>Manage the marketing platforms and systems, including the evaluation and implementation of new technologies</li>
<li>Build relationships and partner with other Lyft Operations teams to ensure tech and process alignment</li>
<li>Build and implement a strategy for data governance to ensure data cleanliness and enrichment</li>
<li>Identify, design, and execute new processes that enable marketers to be more effective in their day to day work (e.g. intake process, campaign naming conventions)</li>
<li>Train new and existing team members on Marketing Automation Platform (MAP) use and application</li>
<li>Support execution of marketing programs (e.g. nurture programs, lead scoring)</li>
<li>Manage Lyft Business owned web property and increase our SEO and organic traffic</li>
<li>Build and execute A/B and multivariate tests, analyze and share learnings from the data, and continuously find ways to improve our campaigns and website</li>
</ul>
***********
<br><br>
<b>Experience:</b><br>
***********
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>7+ years experience in B2B marketing operations or demand generation</li>
<li>Proficiency with marketing automation software and CRM (preferably administrative experience with Pardot and Salesforce CRM)</li>
<li>Strong operations and data analytical skills with an understanding of measuring ROI and funnel management</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to clearly and simply articulate ideas and results</li>
<li>Team player that can work in a fast paced environment and prioritize projects</li>
<li>Experience managing and implementing other martech solutions such as A/B testing platforms, multi-touch attribution, and sales engagement tools preferred</li>
</ul>
