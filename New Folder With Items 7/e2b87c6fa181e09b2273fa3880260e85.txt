<h2>IT Project Sr. Manager/Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Amkor is recruiting for a senior project manager/business analyst for corporate enterprise systems, based at its corporate headquarters in Tempe, AZ. The successful candidate will be part of the Information Technology team focused on SaaS (Software as a Service) based systems such as Salesforce, SuccessFactors, Concur and other web applications. The role will be the main point of contact for offshore resources coordinating IT-related activities including project management, interfaces/integrations, configuration management for these SaaS-based and web applications.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b><br>
• Plans, directs, and manages implementation of small to medium projects
<br>
<ul>
<li>Ensures business objectives are defined; project documentation
</li>
<li>Work with functional areas to understand business processes and aid users in becoming more efficient and effective in using SaaS and enterprise web applications
</li>
<li>Partner with business liaisons, technical teams, and key solutions partners to help build the right technical solutions
</li>
<li>Coordinate project resources to ensure that projects are delivered on time and within budget
</li>
<li>Keep stakeholders updated throughout the development cycle and act as a liaison with relevant parties, including the project management office, quality assurance, other development teams, production support, infrastructure support, etc. to facilitate the release process
</li>
<li>Serve as the point of contact for Amkor offshore team to manage resource's availability, work assignments, time allocations, and review of time sheets/ invoices
</li>
<li>Work with offshore resources to help analyze and explain the requirements, choose the right methodology, review the delivery quality and improvise<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Required Qualifications:
</b><br>
• 8+ years of experience working with information systems or supporting IT services
<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or a related field preferred
</li>
<li>Expertise with cloud applications like Salesforce, SuccessFactors, Concur, and/or custom web application development
</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience with Integrated Cloud Services (for developing interfaces with 3rd party applications)
</li>
<li>Proven experience in API Design, API life cycle management &amp; Service Oriented Architecture
</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience with managing offshore developers
</li>
<li>Knowledge of software development life cycle (SDLC) methodologies (specially Agile &amp; Waterfall) and techniques
</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem solving skills
</li>
<li>Knowledge of application design activities tools and techniques, knowledge and experience with implementing purchased application software; knowledge of application testing and ability to execute
</li>
<li>Experience working with systems that handle sensitive data and with SOX controls and change management processes<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications:
</b><br>
• Experience with Oracle Agile PLM system or SAP ERP preferred</p>
