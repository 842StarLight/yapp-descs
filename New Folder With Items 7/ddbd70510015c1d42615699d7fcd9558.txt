<h2>Distribution Coordinator - Warehouse Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Summary:</b> Supervises worker(s) and performs activities associated with counter sales, ordering, receiving, storing, inventorying, issuing, and shipping materials, supplies, tools, equipment, and parts, in stockroom, warehouse, or yard by performing the following duties.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b>Included are the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Pulls orders for shipment from warehouse and pipeyard inventory including customer and inter-company orders for delivery. Verifies the accuracy of all shipments. Pulls all orders for next day shipment before leaving work.
</li>
<li>Stages and prepares orders for shipment while making sure the items are packaged neatly.
</li>
<li>Separates material to shipping points. Loads outgoing shipments onto trucks and ships orders on common carriers. Ensures all tagged material is double checked before shipment.
</li>
<li>Fills bin areas to keep the products available for pulling orders. Keeps racks and bin areas neat. Also maintains the cleanliness and organization of the warehouse and grounds.
</li>
<li>Completes all shipping paperwork and computer entry in a timely manner and notifies salesperson if the material is short.
</li>
<li>Checks all material received including transfers and customer returns against the purchase order, vendor packing lists, or other documentation. Checks the packing lists to verify material is being delivered to the correct place before unloading the skids.
</li>
<li>Inspects the product for damage. Notes in detail any damage on the freight bill.
</li>
<li>Tags and separates the material that has been received.
</li>
<li>Completes receiving documentation and properly enters all receiving information into the computer system.
</li>
<li>Puts away stock material and restocks the primary order fill locations.
</li>
<li>Plans layout of stockroom, warehouse, and other storage areas, considering turnover, size, weight, and related factors of items stored.
</li>
<li>Advises employee on care and preservation of items received, stored, and shipped; methods and use of equipment in handling, storing, maintaining, and shipping stock; and related problems.
</li>
<li>Studies records and recommends remedial actions for reported nonusable, slow moving, and excess stock. Monitors non-stock items and assists with disposal.</li>
<li>Reviews records for accuracy of information and compliance with established procedures, and to determine adequacy of stock levels.
</li>
<li>Responsible for coordinating a successful year-end inventory. Schedules work for special and periodic inventories.
</li>
<li>Traces history of items to determine reasons for discrepancies between inventory and stock control records and recommends remedial actions to resolve discrepancies.
</li>
<li>Determines work procedures, prepares work schedules, and expedites workflow.
</li>
<li>Issues written and oral instructions.
</li>
<li>Assigns duties and examines work for exactness, neatness, and conformance to policies and procedures.
</li>
<li>Studies and standardizes procedures to improve efficiency of subordinate.
</li>
<li>Maintains harmony among workers and resolves grievances.
</li>
<li>Adjusts errors and complaints.
</li>
<li>Performs duties of subordinate Driver/Distribution Associate, when necessary.</li>
<li>Reports for work on a regular basis.</li>
<br>
</ul>
