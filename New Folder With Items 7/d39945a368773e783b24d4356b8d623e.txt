<h2>Site Manager- Seymour, TX</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li> Directly responsible for on-site wind turbine generator OEM functions to assure turbine performance meets or exceeds customer expectations</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage requests received from customer and coordinate all activities on site with customer.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage all OEM projects at the site</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage and supervise subcontractors hired by OEM at the site</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare daily, weekly and monthly product performance reports</li></ul>
<ul><li> Conduct and review safety analyses and review potential electrical and mechanical hazards</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain and assure all mandated safety and regulatory training isn completed and renewed</li></ul>
<ul><li> Coordinate directly with AWPNA engineering and post-sales departments to drive continuous product improvement</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide training and coaching to on-site operations and maintenance teams to enhance their understanding of WTG functionality</li></ul>
<ul><li> Report component non-conformities to AWPNA Quality Assurance department for warranty or internal material review board processing</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage the inventory of tools, spare parts, and consumables including the management of specialty tools that require calibrations</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage the completion and storage of required documentation related to all corrective and preventative tasks on the wind turbine generators</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assure the company is complying with inter-company and external service agreements/contracts</li></ul>
<ul><li> Meet company quality and safety standards to include federal, state, and local laws, rules and regulations where applicable</li></ul>
<ul><li> Participate in annual budget creation and review and track monthly O&amp;M expenses</li></ul>
<ul><li> Travel to headquarters, industry related conferences or training events as required (5 -10% travel)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Analyze down time availability and adjust allocations</li></ul>
<ul><li> Support and maintain local, public and landowner relations</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage, coordinate, and communicate, warranty and retrofit implementation projects to customer</li></ul>
<ul><li> Coordinate data sharing with engineering and perform engineering studies</li></ul>
<ul><li> Coordinate on and off site logistics</li></ul>
<ul><li> Other responsibilities as assigned</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent work experience</li></ul>
<ul><li> 2 – 5 years in a leadership position with 5 - 7 years experience in power generation equipment support with 3 years concentration in the wind turbine operations and maintenance required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Be able to work independently, demonstrate strong leadership skills, and have experience supervising a team</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to utilize JD Edwards and Maximo software systems</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to think and analyze situations in a fast paced environment</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work in a flexible and changing schedule as required in order to meet operational needs including managing several crossing processes, different work teams and simultaneous demanding situations</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strengths will include excellent communication and analytical skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to train and coach WTG technicians</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to successfully interact with key customer contacts and other internal departments</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent written and oral communications skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to perform technical and economic feasibility analysis</li></ul>
<ul><li> Fluency in Spanish is a plus Ability to meet specific OSHA and ANSI weight limits of 250 to 300lbs. max</li></ul>
