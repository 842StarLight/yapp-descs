<p>We are currently looking for a skilled Senior CRM Product Manager to join the Education Publishing team within Wiley. The ideal candidate will Partner with the Sales leadership and key stakeholders to establish a CRM product roadmap that is tightly aligned to the business strategy and goals, to enable and improve sales productivity.<br>

<p></p>
<p><b>About the role
</b></p>
<p>The Senior Product Manager for the Sales CRM is the primary connection between the Sales leadership team, the Sales users, and the Technology team. The Senior Product Manager is responsible for developing a product vision and roadmap for the CRM that is based on the needs of the business and working with the Technology team to execute on that roadmap based on Agile development methodologies.<br>
</p>
