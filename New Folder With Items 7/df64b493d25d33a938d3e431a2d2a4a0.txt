<p><b>Job Title</b>: Director, PPI Business System<br>
<b>Requisition ID: </b>101833BR<br>
<br>
When you join us at Thermo Fisher Scientific, you’ll be part of a smart, driven team that shares your passion for exploration and discovery. With revenues of $24 billion and the largest investment in R&amp;D in the industry, we give our people the resources and opportunities to make significant contributions to the world.<br>
<br>
<b>How will you make an impact?
</b><br>
<br>
The Director, Practical Process Improvement will be part of a global team designed to facilitate implementation of the PPI Business System within and across specified functional groups in the Customer Channels Group. This position will focus globally and partner with functional and regional leaders to drive continuous improvements in day-to-day business processes through Strategy Deployment and project execution. The PPI Business System Director will also utilize standard Parent/Project A3 templates and other project and program management tools to visually manage efforts and drive progress against targets. Leveraging project management expertise and tools/knowledge of the PPI Business System to support and help execute or lead projects or Strategic Initiatives is a key responsibility of this position. Also as part of the PPI Business System discipline, the individual will learn and facilitate a wide range of continuous improvement tools and methodologies, including, but not limited to, standard work, PDSA, process mapping, 5Whys, RCCMs, error-proofing, and 8-step problem solving as needed to help achieve project success.<br>
<br>
This position will be located at our Pittsburgh, PA location and relocation assistance is approved.<br>
<br>
