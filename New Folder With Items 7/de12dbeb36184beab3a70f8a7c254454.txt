<h2>Technical Support Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Plex Systems® delivers industry-leading ERP, MES, and Industrial IoT solutions to manufacturers across process and discrete industries. Plex pioneered cloud solutions for the production line, connecting suppliers, machines, people, systems, data, and customers with capabilities that are easy to configure, deliver continuous innovation, and reduce IT costs.</p>
<p>
Plex is a rapidly growing software company consistently recognized as a top workplace. In support of our ongoing growth, we are looking for a Technical Support Representative to ensure ongoing customer satisfaction and continuing strong customer relationships, creating a “Customer for Life”. We are seeking candidates with customer facing experience supporting software applications to join our Support Services team. This position is is located in our Irvine, CA office. Additional responsibilities include the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide excellent customer support for customers using PLEX including: answering questions, explaining products and services, troubleshooting, and other business priorities</li>
<li>
Gather information, research and manage user support requests</li>
<li>
Escalate requests, as needed, in a timely fashion and to the correct product team</li>
<li>
Manage timelines and deliverables with internal departments and customers</li>
<li>
Test and implement software enhancements</li>
<li>
Provide functional guidance and training to customers on new enhancements, modules and features</li>
<li>
Advise customers about available service(s) and assess customer needs</li>
<li>
Utilize and enhance Knowledge Management System</li>
<li>
Build formal and informal relationships with customers and internal team members</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A Bachelor’s Degree, preferably in Computer Science or Engineering</li>
<li>
Minimum of 2 years of previous technical support service experience within a highly technical environment</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience troubleshooting customer escalations within a ticketing-based workflow, while achieving SLA requirements</li>
<li>
Prefer experience using or supporting an ERP/MRP system preferred</li>
<li>
Troubleshooting / problem solving skills</li>
<li>
Experience building and maintaining good customer relationships</li>
<li>
Ability to use analytical skills to resolve business issues</li>
<li>
Ability to manage and prioritize many requests simultaneously</li>
<li>
Organized and detail oriented</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently, without close supervision, as well as part of a team</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate complex issues to varying levels of users in a professional, positive, friendly and understandable manner</li>
<li>
Fluency in languages such as Italian, Spanish, German, Portuguese or Mandarin are a plus</li>
<li>
Willingness to work non-standard hours as needed</li>
<li>
Manufacturing experience preferred, including knowledge of EDI, Production &amp; Inventory Planning/Scheduling, or Quality/Tooling</li>
</ul>
