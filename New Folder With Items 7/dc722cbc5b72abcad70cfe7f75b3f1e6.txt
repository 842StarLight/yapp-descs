<p><b>Senior Financial Analyst</b></p>
<p>Position Profile:</p>
<p>Business partnering the multi channel commercial/sales team on all customer facing financial matters, primarily through the provision of sound analysis, pricing and tradespend recommendations, thereby enabling effective decision making and profitability maximization.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Works closely with the commercial team to provide solid financial acumen on ongoing projects, ensuring integrity of data</li>
<li>Provides recommendations on pricing levels, for strategic bids/tenders on both existing portfolio and new product developments.</li>
<li>Assists with tradespend analysis and control and providing recommendations to the commercial team tailored to suit needs.</li>
<li>Performing post evaluation exercises, and subsequently making recommendation on necessary corrective actions</li>
<li>Assists with analyzing the impact of future commodity positions and provide consequential pricing recommendations</li>
<li>Assists with ongoing process development and improvements to maximize efficiency</li>
<li>Analyzes sales, financial, costing and operational data and extracts and defines relevant information
<p>Interprets data for the purpose of determining the root causes of past sales, financial, costing and operational performance</p>
</li>
<li>Assembles spreadsheets and draws charts and graphs used to illustrate reports</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as assigned by management<br>
<p></p>
<p>Experience / Skills:</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Team Skills: Ability to work independently and within a group setting</li>
<li>Time Management: Prioritize tasks and time effectively and efficiently.</li>
<li>Continuous Improvement: Ability to seek improvement opportunities within area of responsibility</li>
<li>Professionalism &amp; Relationships: Ability to maintain a high level of professionalism and customer service</li>
<li>Keep highly confidential information secure.</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills (Excel, Access/relational database, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Experience with an Enterprise Resource Planning system (ERP) such as LX, SAP, Oracle, JD Edwards, etc.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication (verbal and written) skills.</li>
<li>Analytical problem solver with ability to recommend solutions</li>
<li>Proactive and creative thinker, willing to think 'outside the box'</li>
<li>Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and handle multiple tasks at once</li>
<li>Line operational finance experience a plus</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience as a Financial/Business Analyst</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in a multinational/international company or strategy consulting practice.<br>
<p></p>
<p>Education:</p>
<p>Required: Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Business or related field</p>
</li>
