<h2>Product Content Coordinator</h2>

<b>Division Information</b><br>
<p>Hardware and Home Improvement (HHI) is a major manufacturer and supplier of residential locksets, residential builders' hardware, faucets, and commercial locksets and doors, with a portfolio of renowned brands, including Kwikset®, Weiser®, Baldwin®, National Hardware®, Stanley®, FANAL®, Pfister™, EZSET®, and Tell. HHI is a leader in its key markets with #1 positions in U.S. residential locksets (Kwikset), Canada residential locksets (Weiser), U.S. luxury locksets (Baldwin), and U.S. builders' hardware (Stanley-National Hardware), #2 in Mexico residential hardware (Fanal), #3 in U.S. retail plumbing (Pfister), and #1 in U.S. retail commercial locksets. Headquartered in Orange County, California, HHI has a global sales force and operates manufacturing and distribution facilities in the U.S., Canada, Mexico and Asia. HHI is a division of Spectrum Brands Holdings, Inc.</p>
<br>
<b>
Job Summary</b><br>
<p>The Product Content team is responsible for the accuracy and timely delivery of all product related information distributed to our retail and wholesale partners for in-store and online sales. The STEP Product Information Management System is the platform for managing product content.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Product Content Coordinator will help gather, enrich, organize and manage product related content using STEP Product Information Management System for multiple brands and platforms while satisfying requests for content to be distributed to internal or external business partners.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
This key role provides a unique opportunity to work closely with external E-commerce, Retail and Wholesale channel partners when fulfilling data requests as well as with internal Channel Marketing, Product Marketing and Sales Teams when gathering and enriching product data and content. As the Product Content Coordinator trained on HHI’s Stibo STEP Product Information Management (PIM) system, you will manage, import/export and audit product data. You are responsible for working with the Digital Marketing Manager to define and implement new processes to improve the flow of content between internal customers and our systems and external partners and their systems. This position interfaces with several departments while coordinating data flow including IT, Engineering, Product Marketing, Channel Marketing, Brand Marketing, Sales, Graphic Design, Sourcing and Operations. This position allows for excellent visibility throughout the company and the opportunity to learn how all parts of the business function.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<b>
Primary Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>(30%) Gather, enrich and organize product data in HHI’s STEP product information management system with a focus on improving quality, consistency and completeness</li>
<li>(30%) Working closely with the Sales Team, actively engage and manage one or two key channel partner data accounts as identified by the Digital Marketing Manager for data management (Channel partner accounts include but are not limited to Amazon.com, Home Depot, Lowe’s,Menards)</li>
<li>(20%) Fulfill data requests as needed for other partners</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>(10%) Support product content audits and cleansing efforts within STEP</li>
<li>Ensure data quality standards are met</li>
<li>Gain product knowledge for multiple brands and product types</li>
<li>Coordinate with cross-functional teams within various departments to gather required product data</li>
<li>Manage product related content used to drive sales through E-Commerce, Retail and Wholesale channel partner e-commerce websites as well as other HHI internal applications and sites</li>
<li>Perform and document procedures for data enrichment</li>
<li>Train and educate co-workers on data collection</li>
<li>Foster relationships with other departments that provide and use data</li>
<li>Improve productivity through reuse of product data</li>
<li>Fulfill internal product data requests from product teams</li>
<li>Become owner and expert at managing select 3rd party tools to distribute and upload data to channel partner accounts</li>
<li>Fulfill job requests with our major retail partners interfacing with their product data teams</li>
<br>
</ul>
