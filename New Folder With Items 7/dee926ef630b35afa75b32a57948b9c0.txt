<p><b>
Job Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
Under the general guidance of the Director of Finance, directs the day-to-day operations of the Accounting staff, assuring that a highly productive and well trained staff is always maintained. The Sr. Assistant Director of Finance assures the accuracy, consistency, and timeliness of the financial reporting and Accounting functions while providing information and assistance to the Director of Finance.</p>
<p><b>
Duties &amp; Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for overseeing all accounting/finance duties and management reporting for multi-site Hotel, food and beverage venues including but not limited to: full cycle month end closes, financial statement preparation, complex account analysis, reconciliation, as well as budgeting and forecasting for Hotel, Restaurant, Night Life Divisions</li>
<li>Ensuring data integrity and accuracy as well as development and documentation of system controls, operational procedures and business process improvements</li>
<li>Supporting the implementations and upgrades of all front of house systems</li>
<li>Maintain balance sheet analysis on a monthly basis with full supporting</li>
<li>Investor reporting, payroll entries, coordination of year end audits, consolidations, and will work on special projects as needed</li>
<li>Supervise, manage and mentor their staff in all areas of accounting including but not limited to technical training, professional development and conducting and delivering performance reviews</li>
<li>Ensure compliance of budgets and cost controls; and will also be responsible to train venue Managers in financial accounting as needed</li>
<li>Ensure all legal, treasury, and tax documentation is properly maintained and secured, and that all statuary and fiscal reporting requirements are satisfied, which includes any governmental requirements for permits and licenses</li>
<li>Assist Director of Finance in ensuring hotel’s compliance to all regulatory licenses and permits, leases, contracts, legal agreements, and the proper execution of all operational taxes. Provide assistance to management in enforcing compliance on all these items.</li>
<li>Budgeting and Forecasting</li>
<li>Financial Analysis</li>
<li>Auditing and creating additional set of books for the Shared Services</li>
<li>Auditing Allocations</li>
</ul>

<p><b>SPECIFIC JOB KNOWLEDGE, SKILL AND ABILITY</b></p>

The individual must possess the following knowledge, skills and abilities and be able to explain and demonstrate that he or she can perform the essential functions of the job, with or without reasonable accommodation.</p>

<li>Experience in Hospitality, Restaurants and Nightlife Accounting preferred</li>
<li>4+ years accounting experience including 4 or more years at supervisory/management level, preferably in an upscale or lifestyle brand hotel</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of GAAP</li>
<li>Proven team leader with a high level of energy and motivation with a proven track record of living the company's values</li>
<li>An intermediate to proficient understanding of Computer systems such as: Opera, Microsoft Word, Excel &amp; Outlook is preferred</li>

<li>Ability to spend extended lengths of time viewing a computer screen</li>

<li>Ability to work independently and to partner with others to promote an environment of teamwork</li>
