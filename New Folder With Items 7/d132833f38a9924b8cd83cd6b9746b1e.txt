<h2>Accounts Payable Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Maintain Accounts payable Vendor files and records. Complete Payable month end closing and reports. Complete daily and projection reports as directed. Ensure that Vendors and suppliers are paid within established time limits. Receive incoming payable invoices,verify and match P.O's and packing slips to payable invoices. Circulate payable invoices for payment approvals.</p>
<p>Prepare, balance and enter payable invoices to ERP / Accounting software packages.</p>
<p>Prepare, payable checks for delivery (mail,email,fax). Prepare annual 1099's and 1096.</p>
<p>Provide administrative support in order to ensure effective and efficient office operations.</p>
