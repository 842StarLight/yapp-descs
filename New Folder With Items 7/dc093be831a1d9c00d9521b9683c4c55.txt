<h2>Sample Processing Technician 1</h2>

<div>

When you join our family, you will have a significant impact on the health and well-being of people across the globe. Whether your background is in life sciences, finance, IT, sales or another area, your skills will play an important role in the work we perform. <div><div>
<div>
<b>
Job Summary</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>Perform a variety of activities related to log in, sample processing, storage, shipping of stored materials and client support.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Perform daily tasks including sample receipt, processing, and storage of samples.</li>
<li>Label collection tubes/syringes and/or vials.</li>
<li>Will generate data and blood forms.</li>
<li>Communicate across company functional groups.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Train on blood related procedures / techniques.</li>
<li>Maintain blood supply inventory closet and excel file. Order supplies, as needed.</li>
<li>Maintain inventory of ~50 pipettes and ensure calibration 2x annually.</li>
<li>Clean / Maintain inventory of ~20 centrifuges.</li>
<li>Assign centrifuges and pipettes to study and post using excel spread sheet.</li>
<li>Perform all other related duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<b><i>Education:</i></b><i> </i>High school diploma or General Education Degree (G.E.D.) required. Associate’s degree in a science preferred.</li>
<li>
<b><i>Experience:</i></b><b> </b>2-4 years’ experience in a laboratory setting required. GLP experience preferred.</li>
<li>An equivalent combination of education and experience may be accepted as a satisfactory substitute for the specific education and experience listed above.</li>
<li>
<b><i>Certification/Licensure:</i></b> None.</li>
<li>
<b><i>Other:</i></b> Knowledge of GLP.</li>
<li>
<b><i>Other:</i></b> Must be detail-oriented and have good verbal and written communication skills. Must have intermediate computer skills and knowledge of Microsoft Office software. Able to work on assignments that are semi-routine in nature in which ability to recognize deviation from accepted practice is required. Able to receive and understand general instructions on routine work and detailed instructions on new assignments.</li>
<br>
</ul>
