<h2>Sales Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Inside Sales person will focus on customer acquisition. This position involves cold calling new potential clients, client business referrals, or web leads. Provide prospective customers/clients with all services offered, and additional presentations as needed. Must be energetic, well-spoken, and eager to close sales and increase revenue.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Inside Sales Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Cold calling; making 25+ outbound calls to potential clients per day</li>
<li>Understanding client needs and offering solutions and support</li>
<li>Researching potential leads from business directories, web searches, or digital resources</li>
<li>Qualifying leads from digital campaigns, conferences, references, tradeshows, etc.</li>
<li>Creating and maintaining a list/database of prospect clients</li>
<li>Presenting live demos and delivering information to potential clients</li>
<li>Answering potential client questions and follow-up call questions.</li>
<li>Tracking weekly, monthly, and quarterly performance and sales metrics</li>
<li>Building pipelines to close sales</li>
<li>Closing sales and working with client through closing process</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience selling SaaS products</li>
<li>Comfortable making cold calls and talking to new people all day</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills; the ability to call, connect and interact with potential customers</li>
<li>Persuasive and goal-oriented</li>
<li>Possesses an energetic, outgoing, and friendly demeanor</li>
<li>Eager to expand company with new sales, clients, and territories</li>
<li>Self-motivated and self-directed</li>
<li>Able to multitask, prioritize, and manage time efficiently</li>
<li>In-depth understanding of company services and its position in the industry</li>
<li>High school degree or equivalent</li>
<li>Tenacity to handle rejection and continue on with a positive attitude when reaching next potential client</li>
<li>Knowledge of sales process from initiation to close</li>
<li>Ability to work independently or as an active member of a team</li>
<li>Previous experience in outbound call center, inside sales experience, or related sales experience preferred</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, including Microsoft Office Suite (Word, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Excel) and CRM/Hubspot experience preferred</li>
</ul>
