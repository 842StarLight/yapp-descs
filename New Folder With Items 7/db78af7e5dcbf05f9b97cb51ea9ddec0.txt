<h2>Commercial Product Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>What you will do:</b></div>
<div>
This position is responsible for sales support reporting to the Audubon Pa Branch. Sales support will be for both Residential and Commercial products with the emphasis on Commercial. The tasks will include product support for all departments, evaluating and configuring application, and job take offs quoting York equipment/accessories/and any related parts and supplies for both internal employees as well as our customers.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
How you will do it:</b></div>
<div>
1. Provides product and selling support through quote preparation for the Account Managers, co-workers, customers, and others who may influence the purchasing decision.</div>
<div>
2. Performs take-offs from plans in order to prepare professional proposals and submittals to increase and support sales.</div>
<div>
3. Provide basic technical assistance to Account Managers, co-works, customers, and anyone else inquiring about York products and how they fit into applications. (We have separate Service Managers who handle the in depth technical assistance).</div>
<div>
4. Provide product compatibility when replacements are needed for applications that are no longer available or provide an alternate way of doing a project and upsell.</div>
<div>
5. Provide product updates to Account Managers, customer service reps, or customers through emailed updates and product presentations.</div>
<div>
6. Become a resource for customers to call directly for take-offs and quotes to support them and the Account Managers.</div>
<div>
7. Works with architects, engineers, builders, developers, and utilities as directed by the Account Manager and Sales Manager to provide technical assistance or information for favorable product specification.</div>
<div>
8. Maintains competitive pricing, product data, and provide analysis to Sales Manager.</div>
<div>
9. Other duties may be assigned.</div>
