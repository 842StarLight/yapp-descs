<h2>Supply Chain Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Supply Chain Administrative Assistant Opportunity Available at Green Sprouts, Inc. (formerly i play., Inc.)</b></p>
<p>For 37 years, Green Sprouts, Inc. (formerly i play., Inc.) has been a family-owned and -operated company. Our products are based on these principles.</p>
<ul>
<li>Pioneering safer materials for health and wellness</li>
<li>Designing from the baby’s perspective for whole development</li>
<li>Supporting parenting naturally with ease</li>
<li>Minimizing our impact for future generations</li>
</ul>
<p>We operate a design and development facility and a distribution center on the French Broad River in Asheville, North Carolina. For more information about Green Sprouts, you can visit www.greensproutsbaby.com.</p>
<p>We are currently seeking a Supply Chain Administrative Assistant to provide administrative support for all Supply Chain activities, including Departmental Strategy &amp; Management, Vendor Relationship Management, Product Information Management, Procurement and Inventory Optimization.</p>
<p><b>Department Purpose of Supply Chain: </b></p>
<p>To ensure the timely flow of quality products from our vendors to our customers in ways that support profitable sales growth and financial health.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<p><b>Departmental Strategy &amp; Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain departmental library of Standard Operating Procedures</li>
<li>Provide administrative support for tracking departmental metrics</li>
<li>Provide administrative support for all service vendors including, but not limited to:</li>
<li>Obtain quotation from service vendor</li>
<li>Match up quotation and enter Service Purchase Order in the company’s accounting software</li>
<li>Match up and receive Goods Receipt Purchase Orders</li>
<li>Match up and enter Invoices to request payment from Finance</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Vendor Relationship Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide administrative support for the New Vendor Setup Process including, but not limited to:</li>
<li>Obtain signed Vendor Agreement and document in appropriate location</li>
<li>Document Vendor Setup including Payment Terms</li>
<li>Provide administrative support for Strategic Business Allocation including, but not limited to:</li>
<li>Obtain signed Active Item Quote Sheets and document in appropriate location</li>
<li>Work with Product Development, Quality and Finance to create Vendor Scorecards and present to Vendor.</li>
<li>Work with relevant teams to administer Vendor Chargebacks according to Vendor Terms</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Product Information Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with Marketing, Product Development and Supply Chain to develop and maintain accurate Item Master information including, but not limited to:</li>
<li>Create new Item Numbers</li>
<li>Assign Harmonized Tariff Schedule (HTS) Codes</li>
<li>Catalog Item Weights &amp; Dimensions</li>
<li>Provide administrative support for maintaining and optimizing Item Costs including, but not limited to:</li>
<li>Work with Vendors to sign Tiered Base Cost Quote Sheets and to document these in the appropriate location</li>
<li>Work with Packaging Design to document Packaging Costs in the appropriate location</li>
<li>Process Landed Costs to accurately account for Freight &amp; Duty Costs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Procurement</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide administrative support for the Procurement Process including, but not limited to:</li>
<li>Assist in Purchase Order (PO) initiation</li>
<li>Assist in Production status monitoring</li>
<li>Assist in Shipment coordination with Logistics</li>
<li>Assist in Payment coordination with Finance</li>
<li>Assist in Inventory receipts</li>
<li>Provide administrative support for all Supply Chain-related Document Management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Inventory Optimization</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide administrative support for Inventory Optimization through Sales &amp; Operations Planning (S&amp;OP) including, but not limited to:</li>
<li>Assist in administrative activities related to Inventory Analysis</li>
<li>Assist in creating Bills of Materials and Work Orders with Production</li>
<li>Assist in tracking the Purchasing Budgets</li>
<li>Assist in administrative activities related to Excess Inventory Initiatives</li>
</ul>
