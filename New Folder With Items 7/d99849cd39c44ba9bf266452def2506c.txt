<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>JOB SUMMARY</div>
<div><b>Viamedia, a progressive, multi-media national advertising company is seeking a highly motivated, energetic, and technology driven sales professional to join our growing team. Viamedia media team members possess strong integrity and drive to deliver maximum value for our client’s media investments. Knowledge across digital, traditional, social and emerging media channels is critical to the success of this role. An inquiring mind, positive attitude and desire to learn are also key as the world of integrated media is constantly evolving and Viamedia prides itself on being at the forefront of innovation and ingenuity.</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities include creating and proposing cross-media advertising campaigns to both an established local account list and new customers. Must have a strong sense of creativity and a solid understanding of the sales process to successfully compete in our fast-paced, energy driven environment. Direct sales experience or educational equivalent required. Media sales experience is preferred with a proven sales background. Great benefits and excellent training.</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS OF THE POSITION:</div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Accountable for multiple budgets including traditional video and digital.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Must be able to determine the advertising needs of the client and make recommendations.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Upsell existing key account advertisers, while driving retention and new business.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ability to identify clients’ marketing budgets and move incremental revenues to Viamedia’s cross platform solution, while protecting revenue shares within traditional video and our digital products.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Creating innovative multimedia recommendations that integrate both branding &amp; proof of performance strategies.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Reviewing of account list, forecasting, pipeline and digital metrics with their manager on a weekly basis.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Network within the local community, and to a broader audience as it relates to our digital offerings.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Attend and complete all aspects of sales training programs</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Positive and professional demeanor representative of the best in Viamedia.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Work ethic representative of the best in Viamedia.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Consistent sales performance versus goal for all revenue lines of responsibility</div>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
