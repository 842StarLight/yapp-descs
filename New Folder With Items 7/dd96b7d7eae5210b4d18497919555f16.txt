<h2>IT Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>.<p><br>
<b>
RELAXED WORK ENVIRONMENT, DEDICATED TEAM, &amp; GROWING COMPANY</b></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<p>
As our Director of IT, you will be responsible for HelpSystems’ internal IT systems, infrastructure, and the operations of the internal IT support team. You will own HelpSystems’ internal enterprise applications and business systems to ensure we are optimally leveraging tools and technologies, in the context of HelpSystems’ business strategy.</p>
<p>
You will partner with business function owners to develop and execute a technical strategy that helps achieve our business goals, deliver on operational objectives, reduce security risks, and drive end-user satisfaction, while maintaining reliable and effective operations and systems companywide. You will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead, grow and develop the global IT organization.</li>
<li>
Ensure alignment of the IT service delivery with the company’s business objectives and assist with the definition of the multi-year IT strategy.</li>
<li>
Set the technical direction and manage the operational support of our global IT program.</li>
<li>
Drive technological planning by prioritizing technology initiatives and coordinating the evaluation, deployment, and management of current and future technologies.</li>
<li>
Structure and operate a global service desk to support our employees.</li>
<li>
Own budgeting and recommend improvements for cost savings.</li>
<li>
Analyze the costs, value and risks of IT services offerings.</li>
<li>
Perform diligence on potential company acquisitions.</li>
<li>
Lead integration activities of acquired companies.</li>
<li>
Coach, mentor and develop direct and indirect staff.</li>
<li>
Handle technical escalations, provide timely resolution of problems or engage lead staff for assistance.</li>
<li>
Support security standards (antivirus/firewall/patching/two-factor authentication/access) on all endpoints.</li>
<li>
Define and maintain operating procedures, best practices and customer service KPIs.</li>
<li>
Participate in on-boarding, training and mentoring team members.</li>
<li>
Drive quality and adherence to best practices within the team.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience and aptitude in managing an IT Team as part of an operational business strategy.</li>
<li>
10+ years of progressive enterprise application implementation experience.</li>
<li>
7+ years of application management experience with responsibility for back and front office infrastructure including marketing, sales, customer support, finance, HR and operations..</li>
<li>
Track record of scaling company business services for fast growth across multiple locations.</li>
<li>
Detailed knowledge of IT operations processes and technology (i.e. Windows, Mac, servers, storage, networking, etc.)</li>
<li>
Strong working knowledge of Active Directory and GPO in a hybrid Windows/Mac environment.</li>
<li>
Experience with professional audio/video conferencing.</li>
<li>
Experience leading dispersed or multi-office customer service delivery teams.</li>
<li>
Excellent technical problem solving and troubleshooting skills.</li>
<li>
Outstanding internal customer service skills.</li>
<li>
Strong communications skills (written and verbal), comfortable speaking across all organizational levels and departments to complex technical issues and topics.</li>
<li>
Expertise managing technology partners to deliver business applications and support services.</li>
<li>
Familiar with SSO, MFA technology, cloud-centric applications, platforms and infrastructure.</li>
<li>
Experience working with external IT / Security Standards such as NIST SP 800-53 and ISO 27000.</li>
</ul>
