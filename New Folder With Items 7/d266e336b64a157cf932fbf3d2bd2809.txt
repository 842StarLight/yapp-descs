<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Do you love exciting, new opportunities? We are in search of an Account Executive with Programmatic experience who can identify, source, pitch, develop and close Peer39 revenue opportunities with agencies, trading desks, and brand clients. Peer39 is the world's largest pre-bid data solution available in Programmatic Real-Time-Bidding. Through the four pillars of Brand Safety, Context, Ad Fraud, and Placement Quality, we enable buyers to access-and sellers to provide-the safest, highest-quality, most relevant page environments for advertisers to serve their ads. Our page level-semantic engine is based on a sophisticated AI engine geared towards providing campaign lift and return on ad spend. Peer39 is integrated into all major DSPs and covers over 80% of the programmatic advertising traffic.

We are newly independent and expanding our global commercial operations. This is a unique opportunity to join a fast-growing organization at the center of the growing contextual advertising market.

Peer39 is an independent data company that provides the largest data set available in the digital advertising ecosystem. Every day, the industry's leading brands, agencies, and publishers trust Peer39's AI-powered semantic analysis engine to provide a holistic understanding of page content, meaning, and sentiment. We do this by analyzing the relationship between words on a page, the content of a video, or in an app, ensuring appropriate classification. As people's time and attention become more fragmented, Peer39 believes that to succeed; you need as much contextual understanding of your audience as possible. We believe that it's as much about the digital environment as it is about the physical environment. Only then can you deliver the right ad to the right user in the right context.</div>
