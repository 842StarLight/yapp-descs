<h2>Associate Principal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Avalere Health is currently looking for an Associate Principal to join its Health Economics &amp; Advanced Analytics (HEAA) Practice.
<br><br>
The HEAA practice works with life sciences companies and other healthcare sectors to quantify and communicate the real-world value of healthcare interventions and to align our clients’ business strategies with the evidentiary demands of an increasingly value-driven marketplace. We are driven by deep subject matter expertise, differentiated and proprietary real-world data assets, and an emphasis on sound business strategy coupled with rigorous research and advanced analytic capabilities.
<br><br>
Primary responsibility is to lead HEOR projects that utilize retrospective claims database analysis methodologies; required activities include leading cross-functional project teams to develop and execute study protocols and statistical analysis plans, analyzing healthcare claims data, interpreting results, summarizing findings, and advising on strategy
<br><br>
Serves as a principal HEOR subject matter expert within the team and across the firm
<br><br>
Supports generation of business with new clients and grows business with existing clients
<br><br>
Uses superior consulting acumen to identify client gaps and opportunities
<br><br>
Responsible for driving team goal achievement around sales, revenue and project management
<br><br>
Collaborates with clients to develop and implement strategic and tactical plans
<br><br>
Takes responsibility for project completion; responsible for strategic consultation and for the use of best methods to address research needs
<br><br>
Responsible for or manages other project staff in project delivery, particularly client communication, client management and dissemination
<br><br>
Responsible for all final deliverables for projects on which s/he is senior expert in terms of accuracy and quality and financial project performance
<br><br>
Solves complex client issues in a timely and appropriate manner
<br><br>
Assists in HEAA practice management
<br><br>
Complies with all security policies and procedures
