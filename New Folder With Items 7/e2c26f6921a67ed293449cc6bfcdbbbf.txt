<h2>Customer Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The <b>Manager, Customer Marketing </b>is a leader in developing and executing customer marketing activities for national accounts across the foodservice and retail grocery channels of trade. This includes both Private Label as well as branded products across the coffee, tea and specialty beverage categories.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Effectively partner with key cross functional partners across sales, supply chain, manufacturing, finance and legal/QA to drive key business initiatives.</li>
<li>
Create sales solutions that can be utilized/leveraged by the channel sales team to support the presentation and discussion they will have with their customers.</li>
<li>
Participate in top to top strategic business reviews with national foodservice branded operators.</li>
<li>
Ability to analyze business results, identify key data insights and present finding to sales leadership team.</li>
<li>
Present Marketing plans at regional and national sales meetings.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with internal and external partners to design impactful, customer focused communications and product offerings.</li>
<li>
Work closely with key members of the sales and marketing teams on customer meetings and trade events to present capabilities and customer programs.</li>
<li>
Provide training and education assistance to the sales force and key customers.</li>
<li>
Manage customer marketing requests as needed.</li>
<li>
Act as primary point of marketing contact for sales organization.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in marketing, advertising or related field required.</li>
<li>
5+ years of foodservice marketing experience required (ideally c-store or consumer packaged goods.)</li>
<li>
Strong project management, organizational analytics and communication skills.</li>
<li>
Proficient in operating Windows and associated Microsoft Office applications, including: MS Word, Excell, PowerPoint, etc.</li>
<li>
Must have a positive, team-oriented attitude that contributes to the overall culture of the marketing team and perception of the marketing and sales team within the organization.</li>
<li>
Self-starter that is resourceful and can work autonomously.</li>
<li>
Able to meet deadlines, follow through on commitments and deliver on promises.</li>
<li>
Comfortable presenting to executive leadership, key customers and national sales organization.</li>
<li>
Strive continuous growth and improvement.</li>
<li>
Ability to build effective relationships while working well with others; high levels of self-awareness, empathy; be able to reach consensus.</li>
<li>
Effective written and verbal communication across all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>
Able to listen and understand the perspective of other while asserting and gaining support of own ideas.</li>
<li>
Ability to deal well with ambiguity. Effectively adapt with change and shift gears comfortably and can make decisions without having total picture.</li>
<li>
Should be able to identify top priorities and shift focus when necessary.</li>
<li>
Desire to establish high standards of performance for self and others.</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
