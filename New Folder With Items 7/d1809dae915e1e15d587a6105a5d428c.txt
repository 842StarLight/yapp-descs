<p><b>Director – Marketing (B2B)</b></p>
<p>The Director of Marketing – B2B is responsible for developing, executing and tracking marketing programs that generate new opportunities and advance existing opportunities for the sales team within targeted accounts and contacts. This position will act as a liaison between the marketing team and the sales teams to build marketing programs that will maximize the value of targeted accounts. The Director of Marketing – B2B will work with the demand generation and creative teams to build marketing plans that include both inbound and outbound tactics, as well as managing 3rd party vendors to achieve aggressive sales targets.
</p>
<p><b>WHO YOU ARE</b><br>
<b>You:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Raise the bar</b> by taking the initiative to achieve results and make excellence your goal.</li>
<li>
<b>Create lifelong fans </b>by making customer experience your top priority.</li>
<li>
<b>Are authentic</b> and say what you mean, share how you feel, and are your true self.</li>
<li>
<b>Are a team player</b> and foster a sense of community by recognizing and valuing the unique contributions of our team.</li>
<li>
<b>Believe it’s possible</b> and are the optimist who looks at challenges as opportunities and brings solutions to the table.</li>
<li>
<b>Embrace change</b> and welcome change that comes your way learning from it.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WHAT YOU’LL DO</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and build lead generation and target account development strategies and campaigns</li>
<li>Build a strategic roadmap to support sales enablement efforts for the inside and outside sales teams</li>
<li>Create growth and nurture programs for specific accounts and contacts</li>
<li>Develop appropriate KPIs and maintain reporting around project success</li>
<li>Work with the sales teams to coordinate, communicate, and optimize the impact of marketing activities</li>
<li>Work with demand generation team to build marketing plans and campaigns that generate new opportunities</li>
<li>Track lead flow to ensure the appropriate sales channels are appropriately handling generated opportunities.</li>
<li>Work with the brand team to maintain key account personas, customer journeys and specific product-related solutions</li>
<li>Work with the technology team to maintain, groom and fill gaps in customer and account data</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to improve marketing effectiveness through better audience segmentation, marketing techniques, and processes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>HOW YOU’LL GET THERE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of experience managing B2B marketing programs for medium/large businesses</li>
<li>Deep knowledge of B2B marketing strategies, and demonstrated success in launching programs that significantly grew sales pipeline</li>
<li>Demonstrated understanding of how to utilize marketing automation, CRM platforms and other technologies to drive B2B strategies and success</li>
<li>Experience with targeting, segmentation and list acquisition to build prospect lists for demand gen campaigns.</li>
<li>Proven track record building innovative and multi-part demand generation programs</li>
<li>Experience in on-site and event-based marketing tactics, including trade shows</li>
<li>Experience developing successful sales enablement programs</li>
<li>5+ years of experience building, launching and reporting on campaigns with Salesforce Marketing Cloud (Exact Target)</li>
<li>Track record of building relationships with sales teams and supporting their success</li>
<li>Experience with the Sandler sales method preferred</li>
<li>SaaS experience is preferred</li>
</ul>
