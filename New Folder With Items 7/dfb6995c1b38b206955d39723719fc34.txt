<p><b><i>
Position Summary:</i></b></p>
<p><br>
</p>
<p>
An ADT Health Territory Account Executive will be responsible for managing existing government and private pay accounts as well as growing new business opportunities across multiple channels within assigned market territory. This front-line sales role is responsible for engaging with accounts from multiple sales channels to ensure the ADT Health product is being sold. The ADT Health Territory Account Executive and work closely and take direction from their assigned Health Regional Sales Manager to properly manage assigned market territory. The ADT Health Territory Account Executive will coordinate with the Health Regional Sales Manager and the ADT Government Contracting Team regarding enterprise and government opportunities. This is primarily an account management role with a secondary focus on business development to identify additional opportunities with the goal of growing sales volume from multiple channels within the assigned geography. The types of accounts that this position will be responsible for managing will include:
</p>
<div><ul>
<li>Existing ADT Residential and Small Business field sales reps as well as technicians across multiple Sales &amp; Service (SSO) locations
</li>
<li>ADT dealer channel sales locations</li>
<li>
Regional and national enterprise 3rd party partnerships</li>
<li>
Bulk business contract sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Home Owners Association (HOA) locations</li>
<li>
Other business opportunities</li>
</ul></div>

Coaching and ride-alongs with sales representatives.</li>

<li>Maintain and support the SSO training of the Health product line and equipment to ensure the sales representatives are selling the Health product.</li>

Supports execution of a sound business plan to engage in each market with the managers and sales representatives to promote growth of the ADT Health organization.</li>

Coordinates with the Regional Health Sales Manager to identify new revenue opportunities with third party partners.</li>
