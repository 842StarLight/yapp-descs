<h2>Strategic Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<b>Location: Chicago, IL</b><br>
<b>Reports to: Regional Sales Manager, West</b>
</div></div></div>
<div>
<p>
The Strategic Sales Executive is responsible for increasing the PureCars’ footprint in the automotive-advertising tech space in his/her assigned key region with an emphasis on mid-large franchised dealer groups. S/he will create and execute new sales as well as occasional up-sells to the existing customer base. The right candidate will determine the appropriate dealerships to target within his/her key assigned territory, identify the dealerships’ decision makers, their buying process, and their pain points to secure new business.</p>
<p><br>
<b>
Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Formulate sales plan and prospect assigned territory for new customers focusing on franchised dealer groups via cold-calls, inbound lead follow-up and existing relationships</li>
<li>
Deliver presentations online and in face to face meetings highlighting our proprietary solutions, benefits, and advantages to key dealership personnel including Dealer Principals, General Managers, General Sales Managers, and E-Commerce Managers</li>
<li>
Educate dealers on digital strategy to prove the value of PureCars’ platform</li>
<li>
Overcome objections to achieve sales quota for assigned region</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a pipeline of activity to achieve sales quota within sales region while accurately forecasting opportunities and reporting activities using Salesforce.com</li>
<li>
Remain current with product updates, digital marketing and our unique value proposition in the marketplace</li>
<li>
Work with PureCars’ Success Manager/s to ensure the territory has the highest level of service and retention</li>
<li>
Follow corporate guidelines for travel and other expenses and submit timely and accurate expense reports</li>
<li>
Conduct touch points with customers throughout the year</li>
<li>
Contribute to the ongoing improvement of the team</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Skills &amp; Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA or higher, preferred</li>
<li>
<b>
Minimum 5 years</b><b> </b>demonstrated success <b>selling solutions into the Retail Automotive Vertical OR equivalent successful track record selling SEM solutions in B2B retail environment</b>
</li>
<li>
Exposure and demonstrated knowledge of online advertising. Paid search and display, a plus</li>
<li>
Salesforce experience, a plus</li>
<li>
Readiness to travel up to 50%</li>
<li>
Solid interpersonal and communication skills in order to close deals and deliver presentations</li>
<li>
Tenacious, self-starter able to work independently with minimal supervision and collaboratively</li>
<li>
Comfortable using iOS mobile devices and web-based applications, such as Clearslide</li>
<li>
A valid driver’s license, car insurance, and a reliable vehicle</li>
</ul>
