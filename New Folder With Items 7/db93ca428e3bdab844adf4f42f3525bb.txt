<p><b>
FUNCTION OVERVIEW:</b></p>
<div>
The Agile Scrum Master in GIS GPO will lean on their technology expertise to deliver the best in class solutions to support business goals and evolve capabilities. The Agile Scrum Master will coach, guide and mentor delivery teams to execute against a backlog of use cases to deliver value in a product model for Global Quality (GQ) and Global Product Safety and Regulatory Affairs (GSR) projects. This role will assist in the transition from a Waterfall project delivery model to an Agile/SCRUM product delivery model at a portfolio and project level for the Global Quality and Global Product Safety + Regulatory functions. These functions serve as critical pillars to innovation-focused product development. This role will serve as a core team member for financial forecasting, project/product management, IT consultation, and process-related IT Innovation throughout GIS GPO by leading hackathons, design thinking events and driving best practices with a team of Regional Innovation Leads across the globe. Improving life at home in a digital world for 90% of consumers is a monumental opportunity for our technologists. Working within the Global Information Systems (GIS) &amp; Global Product Organization (GPO) at Whirlpool Corporation means curating the latest technology trends such as artificial intelligence, big data analytics, IoT, edge computing, and large-scale digital applications into new products and services for our customers worldwide. Are you ready?</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Understand emerging business trends, drive innovative solutions and deliver value by releasing new features, crushing defects, minimizing risk and reducing technical debt throughout the portfolio</li>
<li>
Understand Global Quality (GQ) and Global Product Safety and Regulatory Affairs (GSR) capabilities and transform them into technology roadmaps</li>
<li>
Regularly interface with engineers, architects, DevOps, business relationship managers, GQ and GSR Leaders, and enterprise subject matter experts to deliver new product requests to the platform</li>
<li>
Facilitates daily scrum, sprint planning, sprint demo and retrospectives</li>
<li>
Must be an expert in estimation and planning; creating useful, reliable and practical plans for software development platforms</li>
<li>
Acts as servant first, caring about the team's needs before considering self</li>
<li>
Understands the fundamentals of iterative and incremental development</li>
<li>
Knowledge of use cases, backlog tracking, burndown charts, say/do ratio, velocity reporting</li>
<li>
Responsible for removing impediments for the agile team that is preventing/blocking progress</li>
<li>
Product Backlog: Manage the prioritized list of user stories maintained by the Product Owner</li>
<li>
Sprint Backlog: Manage the Product Backlog user stories that the team committed to doing in the sprint holding the tasks, assignments, and status of work</li>
<li>
Burn Charts: Generate Burndown charts showing the work completed (burnup) and the work left to do (burndown). Updated daily by the Scrum Master following the Daily Scrum meeting</li>
<li>
Manage Sprint Retrospective Log: The Scrum Master will track the results of the lessons learned at the end of each sprint and log the finding to improve performance for the next sprint</li>
<li>
Sprint Velocity Chart: Generate velocity charts showing story points committed and completed for each sprint</li>
<li>
Impediments Log: Track impediments that need resolution</li>
<li>
Parking Lot: Tracks items that need to be revisited at another time.</li>
<li>
Anticipates and prevents problems or roadblocks before they occur</li>
<li>
Demonstrated customer focus - evaluates decisions through the eyes of the customer building strong customer relationships</li>
<li>
Maintain strong relationships with peers and partners</li>
<li>
Communicates effectively through presentations to senior-level business and technology leaders to ensure delivery agility and business value are always embedded in solutions</li>
<li>
Champion Whirlpool's culture of learning by upskilling on new technologies and mentoring less experienced members of the team</li>
<li>
Able to facilitate difficult conversations to balance enterprise technology and business strategies while meeting local business requirements across regions</li>
<li>
Being proactive to leverage solutions across regions where appropriate</li>
<li>
Values inclusion and diversity through daily interactions within a global team across various time zones</li>
<li>
Willing to travel up to 15% domestically and globally (China, Brazil, India, Europe)</li>
</ul>
