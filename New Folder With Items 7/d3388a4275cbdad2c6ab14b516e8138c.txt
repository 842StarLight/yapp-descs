<h2>Marketing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>Shark Tank-featured, cause-driven snack company is looking for a highly creative marketer to help develop and promote our newest healthy snack brand concepts and drive our youth-oriented mental health social action programs.</p>
<p>Ability to think imaginatively and produce exceptional work is critical.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop brand strategies, brand identities, and marketing plans for new healthy snack brand concepts.</li>
<li>Work with R&amp;D production and design teams to create and refine products and packaging for new snack brands.</li>
<li>Develop and execute marketing strategies for promotion and sale of new brands. Target consumers directly and through mass market retailers, schools, and e-commerce channels.</li>
<li>Leverage web and mobile assets, social media, e-newsletters, and traditional and digital advertising to drive awareness and sales.</li>
<li>Conceive and/or develop websites, landing pages, blogs, e-newsletters, social media campaigns, ad campaigns, and press communications.</li>
<li>Develop, promote, and execute youth-oriented mental health social action programs. Propel programs in unique and creative ways, using packaging, product inserts, national and grassroots activities, participatory websites, social media platforms, media exposure, and more.</li>
<li>Participate in forging the company’s direction as a key member of the management team.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional creativity.</li>
<li>Extremely high standards and the ability to produce extraordinary work.</li>
<li>Broad range of skills and sensibilities is a plus, potentially including writing, design, strategy development, product development, packaging, and communication across digital and traditional channels.</li>
<li>Interest in food, wellness, healthy eating, snacking, or youth-oriented mental health is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ABOUT THE COMPANY: </b></p>
<p>Peaceful Fruits is a cause-driven manufacturer and marketer of delicious healthy snacks based in Northeast Ohio. It led the way in putting fruit back in fruit snacks, and it champions improvements in other snack categories. It helps sustain traditional agriculture in the Amazon rainforest by sourcing ingredients there. It proudly supports adults with disabilities in full wage jobs. They make each of the company’s snacks. A Certified B Corporation, it meets the highest standards of verified social and environmental performance and transparency.</p>
<p><b>TO APPLY: </b></p>
<p>Send resume and cover letter.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
