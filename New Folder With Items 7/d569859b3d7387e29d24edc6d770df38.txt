<h2>Regional 3rd Party Account Coordinator - Wyndham Vacation Rentals (Breckenridge)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Envision your career with one of the world's largest vacation rental companies, Wyndham Vacation Rentals. With over 9,000 rental units and more than 2,500 associates across North America you'll discover the rewards of working in an energetic environment with caring colleagues. Wyndham Vacation Rentals is committed to an aggressive growth plan, and we are always seeking to hire top talent to help us deliver exceptional "Count on Me!" service to our customers.</div>
<div>
Wyndham Vacation Rentals' full-time associates enjoy <b>excellent health benefits</b> as well as a generous <b>401k plan</b> and a <b>paid time-off</b> program.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Job Duties</b>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Manage all aspects of vacation rental experiences for 3rd Party Guests for CO and UT locations. Respond immediately to inquiries and booking requests via 3rd Party dashboard/application, provide best count on me service to 3rd Party guests and encourage positive experience and reviews before, during and after stay. Handle all facets of accounts receivable relating to 3rd Party Reservations. Ensure accuracy of reservation details including requests and auditing of arrival dates, unit size, # of people traveling, etc.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Essential Job Functions</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
1. Become expert user of 3rd Party platform and culture to best fit each guest’s needs, use sales techniques and follow up to ensure guest satisfaction and maintain Superhost status based on the standards the 3rd Party requires. Respond to guest inquiries through the 3rd Party dashboard or app as quickly as possible and always within 24 hours. Answer questions knowledgeably and secure reservations in V12. (30%)</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
2. Manage all 3rd Party reservations on a daily basis paying close attention to close in reservation book windows. Confirm and process in PMS system according to standard operating procedures. Audit arrivals in advance and oversee reservations booked throughout the day. Set realistic expectations with guests and ensure each guest received preferred touchpoints. (30%)</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
3. Responsible for posting a review for each guest with assistance of each front desk and receive a 5 Star review at least 80% off the time. (10%)</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
4. Work with management to tend to guest’s questions or issues while in house and work with housekeeping, maintenance, property management order to maintain positive experience for guest. Communicate guest requests and expectations to front desks, housekeeping, and check in locations as applicable. (10%)</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
5. Assist in resolution of 3rd Party guest issues in a timely manner and promptly notify leadership. Responsible for filing a claim for any guest damages with 3rd Party within 24 hours of departure using their resolution tool. (10%)</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
6. Responsible for posting of wire payments, including posting and reversing to AR prior to arrival. (10%)</div>
