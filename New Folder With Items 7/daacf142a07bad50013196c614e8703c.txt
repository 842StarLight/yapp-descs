<h2>Manager, Professional Services - Delivery</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Do you want to be the leader of a team of experts who transform our customers ever evolving IT environments?</p>
<p>
As a Manager, Professional Services - Delivery, you will lead a team of highly skilled Consultants and Architects in our South East Asia and Korea (SEAK) professional services team in support of effective services delivery and business growth in the region. You will manage the development of people within the organization, including approval of training plans, career development planning, conducting annual performance evaluations, coaching/counselling for their individual professional growth, developing recruitment and hiring plans. As the leader of the high performing technical team, you will lead the team in development and delivery of the innovative technical solutions, support the services business growth and execution strategies through internal partnerships with the field sales organization, alliances and partner program. You will also maintain necessary business financials and controls, pursue strategic business development opportunities.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
You will manage your team’s performance, utilization, career growth, coaching/counselling and development in line with our business goals and plans</li>
<li>
Develop recruitment and hiring plans</li>
<li>
Participate in the regional skills and resource planning as well as regional professional services business planning</li>
<li>
Work closely with the Delivery Management, Technical Practices Leads and our Enablement teams to ensure that we are enabling individuals at the right time and with the right skills</li>
<li>
Deliver the revenue and margin objectives of the business</li>
<li>
Maintain in depth technical knowledge and be the leader in the innovation of VMware’s delivery approach and methodology</li>
<li>
Be close to the execution of strategic projects as well as managing escalations where required</li>
<li>
Support our services sales team by representing VMware professional services in customer meetings and showcasing value proposition, and success stories of the team</li>
<li>
Work with our services sales team to develop quality services proposal or SOW definition, by ensuring appropriate requirements and expectations are met</li>
<li>
Be responsible for ensuring that we have the right skills and available capacity within our subcontracting partners as required</li>
<li>
Play a key role in the Delivery Management team helping to shape the direction of organization as we shift to an “as a service” delivery model</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Required Skills &amp; Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>
You are passionate about people development and have successfully developed or managed high performing technical teams</li>
<li>
You have experience in customer facing consulting positions for an IT vendor, Systems integrator or consulting company</li>
<li>
You hold a proven track record of successful delivery of enterprise IT projects as a team lead, manager, project manager or individual contributor such as consultant or architect</li>
<li>
You have 8+ years experience in enterprise IT related positions</li>
<li>
You possess practical understanding of project management methods like PMP, PRINCE 2 or Scrum (certification in these is not necessary)</li>
<li>
You have several years experience selling, or supporting the sales of professional services, including the development of Statements of Work, pricing, and large-scale proposal development including but not limited to RFI, RFQ and RFPs</li>
<li>
You hold a Bachelors or Masters degree or have the equivalent training and experience</li>
<li>
Superb communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
People development experience is preferred</li>
<li>
You are able to travel up to 50 percent of the time, including international</li>
<li><p>
You have experience with modern software development methodologies, with emphasis on software and hardware architecture, and infrastructure design and development</p></li>
<li><p>
You have experience in infrastructure architecture and design of complex distributed systems environments, including failover techniques, recovery/rollback, and application partitioning</p></li>
<li><p>
You understand of modern IT production datacenter and Internet (ISP/ASP) hosting operations</p></li>
</ul>
