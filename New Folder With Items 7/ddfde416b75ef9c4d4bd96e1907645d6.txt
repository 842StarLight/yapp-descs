<h2>Escrow Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>As an Escrow Officer in the Texas market, you will</b><br>Serve as one of the primary Escrow Officers for the state of Texas. You will help drive growth in the Texas market while also overseeing the completion of all title and closing work. You will also help us lead the charge in proving that the real estate closing industry is ready for novel technology, lower pricing, and better service in Texas.</p>
<p>You will enjoy a unique opportunity to combine your closing/escrow skill set to sell Blueprint's services to potential clients in a rich startup environment with a host of growth opportunities.</p>
<p><b>What you may work on</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the growth, performance, and the P&amp;L for the Texas market</li>
<li>Serve as primary account manager for all Texas-based enterprise clients (iBuyers, Builders, Investors) and provide fast a proactive communication to all POCs.</li>
<li>Oversee title and closing for projects in Texas; review and sign closing documents; attend closings</li>
<li>Liaise with processing team to ensure best-in-class service</li>
<li>Collaborate with other Business Development/Operations team members to share best practices</li>
<li>Work with Business Development Managers to help build local lead generation strategy; and take meetings with potential prospects as set by the Business Development Manager when necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we are looking for in you</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have at least 2-3 years of experience conducting closings.</li>
<li>You are a <b>Licensed Escrow Officer or Real Estate Attorney</b>
</li>
<li>A track record for provide best in class customer service to clients</li>
<li>Experience servicing large builder, investor, or iBuyer clients.</li>
<li>Proactive communication approach with a passion for bringing solutions to the table</li>
<li>Current or prior experience in a business development/sales role preferred, but not required</li>
<li>You have a passion for sales, revenue, and growth</li>
<li>You are persistent, resilient, entrepreneurial, humble, mature, confident, and client-minded; you command attention and respect in a room with multiple parties</li>
<li>You have fun at work and are excited to join a fast-growing startup that operates at the intersection of real estate, law, and insurance</li>
</ul>
