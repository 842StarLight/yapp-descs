<h2>Creative Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Oakmont Senior Living, a fast-growing and fast pased luxury senior living company, is looking for a full-time, Creative Director.</b> This position will be based out of our home office in Windsor, CA.
</p>
<p>We are seeking a seasoned graphic designer who is comfortable in a fast-paced work environment. We offer a competitive salary and medical, dental, and vision benefits, as well as 401K, paid vacation, paid sick time and paid holidays.</p>
<p><b>Please include a cover letter with salary requirements.</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement an integrated brand marketing strategy across multiple channels, including website, advertising and printed collateral.</li>
<li>Lead team of graphic designers, provide direction, and ensure design of projects including brochures, ads, invitations, logos, newsletters, web content, and other graphics align with brand standards.</li>
<li>Enhance Oakmont’s design request processes to improve results.</li>
<li>Serves as a collaborator with sales, marketing, communications, VPs, and our Oakmont communities to develop new brand strategies to increase occupancy.</li>
<li>Ensure brand consistency across all platforms.</li>
<li>Develop an in-depth understanding of the brand’s consumer in order to enhance brand experience.</li>
<li>Collaborate with designers and community marketing teams to deliver effective solutions to creative challenges.</li>
<li>Create meaningful and relevant collateral that drives engagement.</li>
<li>Manage companywide timelines to meet the needs of communities’ collateral and advertising requirements.</li>
<li>Other duties and projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of experience in graphic design</li>
<li>Supervisory experience required</li>
<li>Strong planning and organizational skills</li>
<li>Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite (Mac OS Platform)</li>
<li>Experience managing project workflow and creative direction</li>
<li>Strong creative and design skills</li>
<li>Experience developing a brand identity</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills</li>
<li>Excellent written, verbal, and interpersonal communication skills</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
<li>Web design experience a plus
</li>
<li>This is a full-time in-house position</li>
<li>Local candidates only please</li>
</ul>
<p>Oakmont is an Equal Employment Opportunity Employer. We are committed to hiring, developing, and promoting team members based on individual ability and job performance.</p>
