<h2>Senior Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>ANM is looking for a <b>Senior Account Manager</b> to join our amazing team in Cheyenne, Wyoming!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Who We Are:</p>
<p>ANM is a fast growing IT consulting firm focused on building long-term, customer focused relationships. We offer engineering excellence and quality customer service with a local focus. Our specialties include designing, developing and deploying solutions for enterprise networking, data center &amp; cloud, collaboration, security, audio visual and Salesforce.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive Salary</li>
<li>Full benefit packages (Medical, Dental, Vision, Life Insurance, Short Term and Long-Term Disability, Critical Illness and Accident Insurance)</li>
<li>Paid Time Off</li>
<li>401K Plan</li>
<li>Flexible Spending Account</li>
<li>Employee Recognition Rewards</li>
<li>Career training geared towards professional growth</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Who We Are Looking For:</p>
<p>A Sr Account Manager at ANM is responsible for the full sales lifecycle with our company’s customers. Account Managers up-sell products and services by developing a thorough understanding of our customer’s business and technology needs. Account Managers also identify prospective customers and opportunities.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>What You Will Do As a Sr Account Manager?</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for business development while maintaining customer satisfaction within an assigned territory</li>
<li>Conduct onsite/online presentations on services and products offered</li>
<li>Achieve and maintain a positive rapport with customers and partners</li>
<li>Qualify new sales opportunities for current customers</li>
<li>Consistently attain annual sales target assigned</li>
<li>Work with sales administration team to ensure that quotes are provided and order requests are processed accurately</li>
<li>Enter all work as activities or service tickets as required</li>
<li>Attend and participation at weekly sales meetings</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>REQUIRED EDUCATION, TECHNICAL SKILLS AND/OR EXPERIENCE:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in Information Technology selling: IT services and products, management of IT services, or IT consulting</li>
<li>Outstanding communication and organizational skills</li>
<li>Self-starter with ability to build relationships, manage customer commitment, negotiation and closure of sales</li>
<li>Enjoy working with customers and external audiences</li>
<li>Solid skill in preparing written communications and materials</li>
<li>Good verbal communication skills, strong work ethic, and a positive attitude.</li>
<li>Demonstrate proven leadership skills and time management experience.</li>
<li>High degree of integrity in handling confidential information.</li>
<li>Ability to work collaboratively as part of a team.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently with minimal direction.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>TRAVEL:</p>
<p>Travel is required for this position. May require out-of-the-area and overnight travel. Must have reliable transportation, valid driver’s license and motor vehicle insurance.</p>
