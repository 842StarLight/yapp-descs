What You Will Be Doing
<br><br>
ChargePoint is seeking an Applications Engineer to work with strategic partners and customers to build applications that leverage our technology in industry changing ways. The Applications Engineer will work directly with Product Management and Engineering to drive the direction of our products in order to support these key initiatives. The Applications Engineer will be responsible for building relationships with third party developers, creating application examples using ChargePoint hardware and software products, providing technical content for product documentation, and solving technical integration problems. ChargePoint has a wide variety of partners and customers in this fast paced industry, and the Applications Engineer will work with Electric Vehicle Charging Station Manufacturers, Auto Manufacturers, Utilities, and Electric Vehicle Station Owners and Operators.
<br><br>
What You Will Bring to ChargePoint
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead technical integration projects with ChargePoint engineering and third party engineering staff members</li>
<li>Ensure technical issues are resolved on time to meet customer schedules and program commitments</li>
<li>Provide developer support for integration projects</li>
<li>Develop technical content and application examples using ChargePoint technology and third party systems</li>
<li>Travel 10-25% of your time for face to face meetings with partners and customers</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or Electrical Engineering</li>
<li>2+ years of experience of customer facing work</li>
<li>2+ years of experience working with electronic equipment and/or developing software</li>
<li>Knowledge of high power electrical systems and understanding of proper safety protocols</li>
<li>Ability to write and deploy code and scripts a plus</li>
<li>Proficiency with technical debugging procedures such as reading logs, validating connectivity, executing test procedures, and measuring voltage and current levels</li>
<li>Linux command line experience</li>
<li>Passion for solving tough problems</li>
<li>German or French speaking a plus.</li>
</ul>
