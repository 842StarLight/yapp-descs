<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Ads Finance is part of Google’s Financial Planning and Analysis (FP&amp;A) organization, providing financial and analytical support to Google’s business organization. Our work is integral to the operations and strategic decision-making of the commercial teams across all our advertising products. From understanding market and usage trends to exploring the productivity and return of our various investments, the Finance organization is an important partner and advisor to the commercial leaders. GCAS (Global Customer and Agency Solutions) is the team that works with our largest global brand advertiser and agency clients to form deep partnerships which will drive growth in our business, particularly for our programmatic businesses. This role provides an amazing opportunity for an analyst who wants to be a critical part of a fast-growing segment of Google’s advertising business. You will help lead finance/analytical projects and provide deep analytical support to help sales teams understand the key levers driving their business. Your experience and skills with data and analytics will allow you to develop thoughtful and innovative ways to examine business situations, enabling you to provide new perspective on the business. You are a time manager, and able to prioritize ruthlessly on the few things that truly matter.
</p>
<p>The name Google came from "googol," a mathematical term for the number 1 followed by 100 zeros. And nobody at Google loves big numbers like the Finance team when providing in depth analysis on all manner of strategic decisions across Google products. From developing forward-thinking analysis to generating management reports to scaling our automated financial processes, the Finance organization is an important partner and advisor to the business.</p>
