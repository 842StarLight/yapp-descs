<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<p>
We are looking for a talented individual to lead a team of deeply technical Data Platform &amp; Advanced Analytics/Artificial Intelligence Cloud Solution Architects (CSA) to drive growth of Azure Services for a set of Enterprise accounts across different industries. Key responsibilities include:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This CSA Manager role will directly manage a team of full-time employees Data Platform &amp; Advanced Analytics CSAs that will cover Enterprise customers in their respective territory. This is an exciting role that will help fuel Azure Customer success through customer retention and growth.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Key responsibilities include: · Hire, on-board, develop, coach, mentor and evaluate. Set a clear bar for excellence. Build a high performing team that will be diverse and inclusive · Must possess exceptional cross-team collaboration and communication. Partner with all required Microsoft stakeholders in the subsidiary to drive CSA role clarity, account coverage, project assignment and effective cross-team collaboration · Coach and enable your team to accelerate Azure projects to production, including guidance on architectural design, technical recommendations, blocker escalations and technical resource orchestration · Ensure your team delivers high quality production deployments that are resilient and successful · Drive operational excellence, including timely consumption opportunity management and high-quality reporting and insights</p>
