<h2>Assisted Living Sales Professional</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>LifeServices Assisted Living currently seeks a <b>full-time Assisted Living Sales Professional</b>, for immediate hire, for the greater Cleveland, Ohio area.</p>
<p>LifeServices is a privately held company with its central office in Erie, PA. The successful candidate joins a recognized leader in the field of assisted living and a “close-knit” LifeServices Sales Team that operates nine assisted living communities in western/central Pennsylvania and the greater Cleveland, Ohio area. We share a passion for service and care to older people and we’re seeking our next member to our Team!</p>
<p>Visit us at: <b><i>LifeservicesAssistedLiving.com</i></b> .</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Present our Community’s services and philosophy of care to prospective residents and their adult children.</li>
<li>Sell our apartment homes to qualified residents consistent with occupancy goals.</li>
<li>Compile and maintain an automated sales management lead bank for ongoing sales at our Community.</li>
<li>Arrange and process Applications for Residency, obtain approval for residency, and coordinate the move-in process for new residents and families.</li>
<li>Assist in organizing advertising and other marketing activities.</li>
<li>Assist with conducting community presentations to organizations and associations.</li>
<li>Assist in generating age/health qualified prospective residents through the developed referral network and other marketing activities.</li>
<li>Assist with organizing and attending Community events that promote the capabilities of the Community to the general population and target market.</li>
<li>Participate in an “On Call” rotation with the Community management team for Community emergencies.</li>
</ul>
<p>The successful candidate will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Have at least a high school diploma; post high school education preferred.</li>
<li>2-4 years' experience in assisted living sales<b>, or any consultation sales, and/or “selling intangibles”.</b>
</li>
<li>Be able to work occasional evenings and weekends for a few hours;</li>
<li>Have access to full-time transportation;</li>
<li>Be proficient in Outlook, Word, Excel, and capable of learning additional software;</li>
<li>Be usually available immediately on an "unscheduled basis" when residents and families seek assistance.</li>
</ul>
<p>For this full-time position, we provide market rate compensation plus monthly bonuses and a strong benefit package that includes: vacation; health, dental, and vision insurance and a 401(k) with a company match.</p>
<p><b>Travel </b></p>
<p>Limited local and occasional travel out of town.</p>
