<h2>Regional Support Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>MG Properties Group has been actively involved in the acquisition, development, rehabilitation and management of apartment properties since 1992. MG Properties Group manages over 70 apartment communities, which consists of over 17,000 apartment homes in California, Arizona, Nevada, Washington and Oregon with a total market value of over $4 billion</b><b>!</b><br>
</div>
<div>
The Regional Support Manager is directly accountable for all property operations while serving a property or assignment. Assisting in the necessary job functions for assorted office positions may be required due to a leave of absence, vacant position and a new acquisition or special need at a given community. The Support Manager will be accountable for all property operations associated with the position to which he/she is assigned. The purpose of the RSM is to effectively lead, manage and coordinate persons, activities and available resources in order to accomplish corporate objectives as set forth by the Senior Vice President of Property Management. These objectives will include maximizing occupancy levels and property values, assist in training and mentoring, on-boarding new employees, and serve the acquisitions teams as needed. Once assigned to a job site, the Regional Support Manager will report to the site supervisor of that community or portfolio. In addition to other responsibilities, the Regional Support Manager will act as a troubleshooter and mentor to new employees within the assigned region.
</div>
