<p>Do you thrive on empowering others? Do you love meeting new people and building relationships? If so, we may have a great position for you! Progressive Leasing has an opening for a field-based position in the<b> Northern Tennessee</b> area<b> </b>as a <b>Territory</b> <b>Sales Representative</b>. In this role, you will act as the face of our brand, fostering relationships within major national retail locations to help drive sales by connecting customers to our web-based lease-to-own finance technology, which provides a faster, more robust, and seamless customer experience. Reporting to the Territory Sales Manager, you will be trusted to deliver features and experiences, which support the retailer’s strategic initiatives.</p>

WHO IS PROGRESSIVE LEASING?</div>
<div>
Prog Leasing, LLC, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Aaron’s, Inc (NYSE: AAN), is the largest and longest-tenured virtual lease-to-own provider in the United States. The company’s mission is to provide simple and affordable purchase options for credit-challenged consumers. Over the last 19+ years, Progressive Leasing’s fair and transparent NO CREDIT NEEDED lease-to-own option has helped millions of customers and their families, even if they have less-than-perfect credit or an inability to pay for their purchase upfront.
