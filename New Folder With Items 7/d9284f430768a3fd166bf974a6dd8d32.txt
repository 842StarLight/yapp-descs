<h2>Power & Drives Systems Field Service Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role Summary:</b></h2>
The Power &amp; Drives Systems Field Service Engineer role is considered a mid-career role within the business and expected to be in individual of broad technical capability across the range of product mentioned. The successful candidate is will perform a wide range of engineering tasks and ensure proper installation, service and commissioning of products and services. Provides maintenance, breakdown emergency support and remote troubleshooting.<p></p>
<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<div>
As the Power &amp; Drives Systems Field Service Engineer you will:<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Perform troubleshooting activities at site locations to diagnose problems, determine appropriate resolution, and implements corrective actions</li>
<li>
Develop requisite documentation to execute work e.g. commissioning procedures, maintenance procedures daily logs etc</li>
<li>
Perform installation and commissioning activities at customer site locations</li>
<li>
Be capable of working unsupervised and leading small teams</li>
<li>
Execute on site project management and technical leader duties to resolve customer issues and provide customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Under the direction of a senior engineer performs a wide range of engineering tasks to ensure the technical integrity and proper installation and commissioning of GE drive systems, switchgear, transformers and generators</li>
<li>
Develop presentations and provides training and instruction to customers on equipment operations</li>
<li>
Coordinate additional opportunities to serve the customer with the customer service team, parts, other FSE’s, repair services, sales, and product engineering teams</li>
<li>
Advises the customer of recommended upgrades, parts, and service opportunities to improve customer productivity and sell GE parts and services</li>
<li>
Coordinating maintenance activities for the customer and planning for inspections</li>
<li>
Provide support to engineering teams working on customer CMU’s projects<br>
</li>
<li>Provide support the bidding process for the customer CMU’s projects</li>
<li>
Identify new opportunities &amp; development of effective offerings to improve in-service equipment reliability / availability</li>
<li>
Promotes sales of maintenance services</li>
<li>
Performs other related engineering tasks to facilitate proper commissioning and customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Provides on call support in response to customer calls for service. Ensures appropriate response and service is provided to customer</li>
<li>
Complete timely service reports and other administrative requirements</li>
<li>
Support business growth opportunities and marketing campaigns<br>
</li>
</ul>
