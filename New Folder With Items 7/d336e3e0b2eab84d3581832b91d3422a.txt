<h2>Temporary Radio Traffic Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary</b></h3>
<p>Traffic Assistants provide sales support to the Traffic Department, Sales Managers and Account Executives for our local radio stations</p>
<br>
<div>
<ul>
</ul>
<div>
<div>
<p>We are LBI Media, Inc., the largest privately-held, minority-owned Spanish-language broadcaster in the United States, which owns TV and radio stations operating in top Hispanic markets throughout the country; parent company of the Estrella TV Network, one of the fastest growing television networks in the U.S. We are currently looking for talented and passionate people who want to grow with us.</p>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Assist with entering commercial copy</li>
<li>Assist in order entry of commercial contracts and material in Marketron Traffic System</li>
<li>Maintain continuity and traffic files updated</li>
<li>Communicate via email and/or phone with a vast variety of advertising agencies</li>
<li>Communicate with various departments within the company</li>
<li>Assist in pulling daily management reports</li>
<li>Will cross train for backup of other areas of traffic and continuity</li>
</ul></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Strong computer knowledge (Microsoft Office Word, Excel, PowerPoint).</li>
<li>Organizational skills and the ability to prioritize.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications skills with a strong customer service orientation,</li>
<li>Radio broadcast traffic and/or agency experience.</li>
<li>Detail oriented and ability to meet tight deadlines.</li>
<li>Prior experience working with Marketron</li>
<li>Bilingual: ability to read, write and speak English/Spanish.</li>
<li>College Degree in related field preferred, but not necessary</li>
</ul></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Must be willing to work in Burbank, CA</li>
<li>Must be willing to undergo a pre-employment background investigation</li>
<li>Must have legal authorization to work in the United States</li>
<li>Must be willing to work overtime on short notice</li>
<li>Must be 18 years or older</li>
</ul></div>
