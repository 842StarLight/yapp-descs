<br>About the Role
<br>--------------
<br><br>
We are seeking a SaaS Sales Engineer who is an expert in Work Management, Collaboration and SaaS technology. A Sales Engineer in this role will daily present the value proposition and benefits of our solutions to our customers and prospect. Additionally, in support of our sales force. You will participate in the design, validation, and deployment of pilot, proof of concepts, and proof of values for customers.
<br><br>
Previous experience in SaaS, Cloud technology is required, with the ability to lead business process/technical discussions identify the right solution for our customer's needs. Previous experience required in a customer-facing role such as sales consultant, technical solutions engineer or solutions architect.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide presales technical / solution support to prospective clients and customers, while ensuring customer satisfaction with minimal supervision during the sales cycle.</li>
<li>Present and articulate advanced product features and benefits, product future direction and overall work management and workflow solutions.</li>
<li>Design, validate and present Wrike solutions to include advanced product concepts, future direction, and 3rd party complimentary products.</li>
<li>Design, develop, deploy and manage customer specific proof of concepts (PoC) and proof of value (PoV) projects</li>
<li>Develop and deliver high quality Wrike presentations and demonstrations.</li>
<li>Understand the competitive landscape and develop and maintain expert knowledge in SaaS project management, workflow, and task management solutions</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a general knowledge of Wrike's API - understanding its capabilities and assisting customers in the types of solutions and extensions that can be developed using the API.</li>
<li>Act as a technical resource for less experienced Customer Success Managers, Sales Development and Sales representatives.</li>
<li>Develop and manage customers' references through high quality technical, professional client relationships.</li>
<li>Assist Sales Executives in closing deals and expanding accounts</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills are required.</li>
<li>20% Travel expected</li>
</ul>
