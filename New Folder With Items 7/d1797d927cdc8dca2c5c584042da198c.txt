<p>Telemundo Atlanta is looking for an <b>Advertising Traffic &amp; Sales Coordinator</b>. We are looking for a candidate that is smart, forward-thinking and possesses great communication skills, customer service skills and is capable of working under deadlines. This position will support the sales team and management by ensuring that sales commitments and events are executed in a timely and effective manner. Job responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee, quality check, approve and maintain marketing commitments with advertisers and organizations.</li>
<li>Serve as a liaison between sales, production and traffic operations.</li>
<li>Creating sales materials in PPT, Word, Excel and drafting marketing emails.</li>
<li>Oversee, create and maintain sales cycle efficiency and accuracy.</li>
<li>Accurately enter orders and traffic instructions.</li>
<li>Spot Reconciliation of orders and enter makegoods.</li>
<li>Quality Check to ensure spots are uploaded accurately and timely.</li>
<li>Assist Sales team to keep sales initiative and process moving forward.</li>
<li>Quality Check Sales Packages, Sales Materials to ensure client/trade commitments can be met efficiently and effectively.</li>
<li>Marketing reports to Network and Clients as need.</li>
<li>Work with Sales and Marketing teams to execute client commitments such as digital posts, on-air elements, sponsorships and events.</li>
<li>Event Coordination for Signature Events.</li>
</ul>
<p>Candidate must be positive, outgoing, detail-oriented, hard-working and above all a team player. Must have proficient knowledge of Microsoft Word, Powerpoint, Excel and basic project management software apps. Ideal candidate must possess problem solving and decision-making skills, as well as stick to processes and deadlines.</p>
<p>Awareness of digital marketing and social media, a plus. Candidate must have an 'owner' attitude with accountability and strong work ethic.</p>
