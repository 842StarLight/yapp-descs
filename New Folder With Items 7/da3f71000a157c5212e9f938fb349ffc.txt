<h2>Store Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Responsible to create the Vera Bradley experience through overall store operations management. Responsibilities include exceptional customer service; visual merchandising; staff management, development and training; upholding store image standards; general store operations; and sales goal achievement.
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES (Note: Other duties may be assigned)
</b></h3>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Hire, train, motivate and retain a team of qualified associates that communicate and represent the Vera Bradley philosophy
</li>
<li>Maintain payroll budget, and manage controllable expenses to maximize profitability
</li>
<li>Develop Vera Bradley loyalty by creating an outstanding experience for both associates and customers
</li>
<li>Lead the team with exceptional sales and strong MOD presence while on the floor, setting the pace for team
</li>
<li>Promotes community involvement to drive brand awareness and loyalty
</li>
<li>Communicate the Vera Bradley brand aesthetic, philosophy and lifestyle to the sales associates and guests
</li>
<li>Uphold the Vera Bradley brand standards through exceptional visual merchandising
</li>
<li>Provide a clean, organized and inviting environment for both customers and associates
</li>
<li>Oversee daily, weekly, monthly processes to ensure operational integrity
</li>
<li>Manage all human resource related tasks including recruiting, onboarding and I-9 compliance, performance management, payroll and scheduling, health and safety, and store security
</li>
<li>Maintain a knowledge of established policies and procedures
</li>
</ul>
