<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Corporate IT Support Analyst is accountable for adding value to business processes by serving as the main point of contact for technical support requests related to computer systems, hardware, networks, or related software. Responds to queries, analyzes the issue, isolates the problem, and determines and implements solutions. Manages day-to-day IT support and prioritizes and executes plant projects. Additional responsibilities can include supporting business applications and triaging with level two and three support teams, utilizing Service Now ticketing system.</p>
<p><b>
Core Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supports functional users in completion of their assigned tasks through effective technical support and business process analysis</li>
<li>Providing technical assistance and support for incoming queries and issues related to computer systems, ERP systems, software, and hardware</li>
<li>Provide an environment of trust, transparency, and camaraderie in all interactions with coworkers.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with people across the organization</li>
<li>Maintain agreed upon service levels for IT Support</li>
<li>Support for PCs, laptops, printers, cell phones, tablets and other network equipment</li>
<li>Supporting users remotely using VPN, VNC, GoToMeeting, Remote Desktop and other tools</li>
<li>Install, modify, and repair computer hardware and software</li>
</ul>
