<h2>Retail Sales Associate-Durant Square</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Goodwill Greater East Bay is a non-profit organization with a mission to create pathways to opportunities and success for job seekers facing challenges to work. Being a part of our team means you are working towards making your community a better place by helping people find jobs and develop a sense of pride and self-esteem. We are a social enterprise and do good business that values and empowers our employees. Goodwill Greater East Bay also provide job opportunities, education and training to people with disabilities and other barriers to employment, improving the lives of individuals, families, and communities. There is never too much Good! Join the Goodwill team – purpose, passion, and people.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>The Retail Store Associate works under the direction of Retail Store Management to maintain the efficient operation of a retail store by delivering outstanding customer service to achieve established goals for sales, production, safety and expense control.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide excellent customer and donor service</li>
<li>Present a friendly, cooperative attitude to the general public and store staff</li>
<li>Perform accurate operations of a cash register</li>
<li>Receive and accurately record donations from public</li>
<li>Sort and price donations</li>
<li>Merchandise sales floor to meet company standards and guidelines</li>
<li>Handles routine customer service inquiries, complaints and questions</li>
<li>Gives instruction and work leadership to the apprentice associates.</li>
<li>Assures that apprentices are following safe working processes and Goodwill’s policies</li>
<li>Handles cash and daily receipts according to Goodwill policies</li>
<li>Completes retail store records and Agency documents as assigned by the Retail Store management</li>
<li>Ensures efficient equipment and supplies are requested and received</li>
<li>Following guideline and policies and procedures</li>
<li>Assist with other duties as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualification: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Must be 16 or older (requires <i>student work permit</i>)</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>A passion for serving others</li>
<li>Ability to provide work leadership to others</li>
</ul>
