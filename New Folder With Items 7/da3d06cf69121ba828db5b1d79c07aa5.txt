<p><b>Summary: </b>The Marketing Manager for the San Diego Gulls Hockey Club is responsible for the development, procurement, management, and execution of marketing related duties pertaining to the San Diego Gulls. This opportunity requires creative and analytical skills in the office, with leadership and customer service skills needed in the sales environment.
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"></h1>
<p><b><i>Examples of Essential Duties/Responsibilities:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Provide marketing support to executive team
</b></li>
<li><b>Overall program coordination, execution and development of all marketing programs (ie. Kids Clubs, new/renewal season seat programs, special events-Tailgates, post-game skates, theme nights, Fan Appreciation, etc.)
</b></li>
<li><b>Interact with existing partners and obtain new partners on special events promotions/events
</b></li>
<li><b>Overall planning of promotional and events calendar with Director of Marketing
</b></li>
<li><b>Work with multiple departments to assist in marketing needs
</b></li>
<li><b>Work with affiliated organizations to adopt best practices whenever possible.
</b></li>
<li><b>Coordinate with accounting department secure storage of cash and credit card receipts after each game/event.
</b></li>
<li><b>Identify growth opportunities for the organization
</b></li>
<li><b>Work with Director of Marketing and ticket sales staff to develop campaigns that maximize demand for branded products.
</b></li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Advertising lead and liaison:
</b><ul>
<li><b>Sales collateral development and creation
</b></li>
<li><b>Direct mail, outdoor, digital, social, TV and radio advertising coordination lead
</b></li>
<li><b>Creative copy and adverting developer
</b></li>
<li><b>Work with graphic designer to develop consistent on-brand collateral and publications (print, digital, outdoor, etc.)
</b></li>
<li><b>Communicate and coordinate with outside marketing agencies
</b></li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Promotional Merchandise:
</b><ul>
<li><b>Serve as the central coordinator of all promotional product delivery, storage, and distribution
</b></li>
<li><b>Maintain inventory by conducting scheduled physical inventories and periodic cycle counts.
</b></li>
<li><b>Respond to and fulfill promotional merchandise requests from internal departments.
</b></li>
<li><b>Promotion Giveaway Execution: communicate distribution logistics, coordinate labor (Street Team, contracted services &amp; Gulls Girls), post event inventory.
</b></li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Street Team:
</b><ul>
<li><b>Manage, develop, and advance marketing initiatives within the local community via “Street Team” promotional staff
</b></li>
<li><b>Schedule and procure Street Team marketing opportunities within the San Diego community.
</b></li>
<li><b>Work with Human Resources to hire Street Team members.
</b></li>
<li><b>Process payroll for event/game day Street Team staff.
</b></li>
<li>
<b>Schedule and supervise game day and non-game day Street Team members, while ensuring all marketing locations are properly staffed.
</b><br>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Event Coordinator </b><b>:
</b><ul>
<li><b>Schedule and oversee projects for the Marketing Event Coordinator.
</b></li>
<li><b>Process payroll for Event Coordinator.
</b></li>
<li><b>Attend monthly arena meetings with Event Coordinator and be main point of contact for communication between the San Diego Gulls and Pechanga Arena San Diego.
</b></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li><b>Other duties pertaining to the marketing department, as assigned.
</b></li></ul>

</b></li>
<li><b>Excellent communication skills, written, and oral.
</b></li>
<li><b>Strong attention to detail and solid organizational skills.
</b></li>

</b></li>

<li><b>Experience preparing marketing presentations
</b></li>
