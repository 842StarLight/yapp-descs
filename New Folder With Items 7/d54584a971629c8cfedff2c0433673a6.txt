<h2>Distributor Sales Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Distributor Sales Account Executive</b></p>
<p>Director: Director of Sales</p>
<p>Reports to: Senior Manager, Distributor Sales</p>
<p><i>The most successful Account Executives at SAGE are positive, high energy individuals that are passionate about our products and services and that are eager to help clients and develop relationships. They approach clients with conviction knowing that the products offered will aid them in their business endeavors. This position has no commission caps and pays equal commission on new and renewal business. SAGE maintains an average employee tenure of 7 years for this team, ranging from 17 years to under 1 year.</i></p>
<p><i>This position will take over an existing book of business which the candidate will manage, cultivate and expand upon. SAGE offers business management software, websites, marketing tools and more that help our clients become more efficient and effective in their pursuits. Once a prospect is secured as a client, this candidate will manage the relationship by becoming a resource, coach and consultant to help advise best practices.</i></p>
<p>The Distributor Sales Account Executive position at SAGE is responsible for the end-to-end sales and management of customer relationships. This role includes, but is not limited to:</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage a book of business via active account management, pursuing new sales via leads from CRM system, networking, tradeshows, customer referrals, leads and all other means of developing business</li>
<li>Maintain an active pipeline of forecasted opportunities to meet monthly, quarterly and annual quota objectives</li>
<li>Effectively speak at engagements and group training sessions</li>
<li>Collaborate with team members and other departments to provide customers with highest level of service</li>
<li>Acquire and maintain a working knowledge of complete capabilities of our service offerings</li>
<li>Travel to and attend tradeshows to obtain leads, demo software, and sell products and services</li>
<li>Consult with active clients and leads to create the best software and web packages for them</li>
<li>Maintain a comprehensive knowledge of the promotional products industry and latest trends</li>
</ul>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, behavior and/or ability required.</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office programs including: World, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and interpersonal relationship skills</li>
<li>2+ years’ experience in sales, required</li>
<li>SaaS experience preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of website platforms and terminology</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business, marketing, sales or other related degree preferred</li>
<li>CRM Experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Insurance coverage - medical, dental, vision, supplemental</li>
<li>Complete insurance coverage - life, long term disability</li>
<li>401(k) with company match</li>
<li>Holiday pay and Paid Time Off</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About SAGE: </b></p>
<p>SAGE, based in Addison, Texas, is the leading provider of information, marketing and business management solutions to the promotional products industry. In addition to SAGE's flagship SAGE Online®, SAGE Mobile®, and SAGE Web® research and business management services, SAGE also provides the industry with other research services, order management, website and email services, e-commerce solutions, end-buyer catalogs, artwork services, payment processing, tradeshows, tradeshow management services, and lead retrieval software. SAGE is also the exclusive technology partner of the Promotional Products Association International (PPAI) and of Promotional Product Professionals of Canada (PPPC).</p>
