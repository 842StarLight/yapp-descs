<p><b>Overview of the Database Administrator Position</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
DevOps Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work directly with the DevOps team and Senior DevOps manager to test system integrity</li>
<li>Design and implement build, deployment, and configuration management</li>
<li>Test implemented designs</li>
<li>Build and test automation tools for infrastructure provisioning</li>
<li>Handle code deployments in all environments</li>
<li>Monitor metrics and develop ways to improve</li>
<li>Provide technical guidance and educate team members and coworkers on development and operations</li>
<li>Brainstorm for new ideas and ways to improve development delivery</li>
<li>Consult with peers for feedback during testing stages</li>
<li>Build, maintain, and monitor configuration standards</li>
<li>Maintain day-to-day management and administration of projects</li>
<li>Document and design various processes; update existing processes</li>
<li>Improve infrastructure development and application development</li>
<li>Follow all best practices and procedures as established by the company</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>DevOps Requirements and Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in CS, engineering, software engineering, or related field</li>
<li>Minimum 1 year previous experience in development and operations, or related IT, computer, or operations field</li>
<li>Previous experience with software development, infrastructure development, or development and operations</li>
<li>Experience with Linux infrastructures, database SQL, scripting such as JavaScript, PHP, Python, Perl, etc.</li>
<li>Up-to-date on the latest industry trends; able to articulate trends and potential clearly and confidently</li>
<li>Good interpersonal skills and communication with all levels of management</li>
<li>Able to multitask, prioritize, and manage time efficiently</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Database Administrator Job Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Install and maintain the performance of database servers.</li>
<li>Develop processes for optimizing database security.</li>
<li>Set and maintain database standards.</li>
<li>Manage database access.</li>
<li>Performance tuning of database systems.</li>
<li>Install, upgrade, and manage database applications.</li>
<li>Diagnose and troubleshoot database errors.</li>
<li>Recommend and implement emerging database technologies.</li>
<li>Create and manage database reports, visualizations, and dashboards.</li>
<li>Create automation for repeating database tasks.</li>
<li>Be available for on-call support as needed.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Database Administrator Qualifications/Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Familiar with command of SQL and SQL tools</li>
<li>Detailed knowledge of database security, backup and recovery, and performance monitoring standards</li>
<li>Understanding of relational and dimensional data modeling</li>
<li>Linux shell scripting skills</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Impeccable attention to detail</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience Requirements</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field</li>
<li>One year of experience in database administration, information technology, database architecture, or a related field</li>
<li>MCSE/MCSA certifications preferred</li>
<li>Experience with Linux and Windows Server environments</li>
<li>Extensive experience with database technologies (Oracle, PostgreSQL, Couchbase)</li>
<li>Experience with cloud services a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
