<h2>Strategic Engagement Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The Strategic Engagement Manager is a leadership position for our newly created implementation team. The individual in this role will provide project management and execution during the client implementation process both at inception and throughout their implementation experience. Keeping our customers successful and thriving is paramount to our business. The Strategic Engagement Manager will have unique skills in guiding customers to find value in Siteimproves business solutions.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage all aspects of project management and coordination, including scope and change request management.</li>
<li>
Work closely with project sponsor and stakeholders in defining project goals and objectives as well as planning and delivering the project</li>
<li>
Lead the development and implementation of a coordinated set of plans and programs for all phases to meet the goals and priorities of the projects</li>
<li>
Participate in establishing practices, templates, policies, tools and partnerships to expand and mature these capabilities for the organization</li>
<li>
Ensure the prime objective of “delivering moments of wow to our customers” is always met</li>
<li>
Establish and manage communication and risk management plans</li>
<li>
Monitor and track project progress and work efforts on a daily/weekly basis, and report project status to senior management and other stakeholders</li>
<li>
Communicate clearly with management, customers, sales, and developers to ensure business requirements are translated accurately for implementation</li>
<li>
Recommend product enhancements and new product opportunities to the Product Management team.</li>
<li>
Work closely with sales and implementation services to scope and estimate customer engagements (pre-sale)</li>
<li>
Concurrently work on multiple customer implementation engagements</li>
<li>
Work across multiple time zones
</li>
<li>20+% travel may be required and may require working outside of hours</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div></div>

What We Require of You</b></p>

</li>

</li>
<li>Valid Passport.
</li>
</ul>
