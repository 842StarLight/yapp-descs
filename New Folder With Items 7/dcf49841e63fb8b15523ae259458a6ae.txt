<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<div>BDO’s Core Purpose is Helping People Thrive Every Day. Our Core Values reflect how we manage our work, our relationships and ourselves. As an employee of the firm, you will live true to our Core Values of people first, being exceptional every day in every way, embracing change, feeling empowered through knowledge and choosing accountability. Our Core Values are the standards by which we conduct ourselves day in and day out, both internally and externally.<br>
<br>
We are looking for a detail-oriented team player who is quick to learn and can manage various tasks toward successful completion. This role will be an integral part of our sales tax compliance team. This role will be primarily responsible for assisting with the time-sensitive preparation and filing of state and local sales/use tax returns. This role includes assisting with online tax return filings, resolving tax notices and maintaining client tax confidential information. Our compliance solution utilizes Vertex software and customized web-portal<br>
Duties will include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Provide administrative support to sales tax compliance team</li>
<li>Support analysts with online filing of returns</li>
<li>Prepare mailing of tax returns and checks</li>
<li>Scan, upload, copy, verify, and file required documents and information</li>
<li>Assist team with entering payments</li>
<li>Review tax identification numbers and filing frequency for accuracy</li>
<li>Assist with projects such as amended returns, address change, name change, closures, etc.</li>
</ul>
Other Duties and Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Process tax returns and filings for small entity</li>
<li>Sales tax reconciliation</li>
<li>Prepare month end reporting</li>
<li>Assist team with resolving notices</li>
<li>Preparing sales/use tax registrations</li>
<li>Preparation of business licenses</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
<li>Must be able to work under tight monthly deadlines, with strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to display sound judgement and problem solve issues</li>
<li>Ability to work independently on routine work, with more guidance on new projects or assignments</li>
<li>Ability to build productive internal/external working relationships</li>
<li>Ability to follow detailed practices and procedures in order to resolve issues</li>
<li>Comfortable communicating with clients and tax jurisdictions</li>
</ul>
</div>
