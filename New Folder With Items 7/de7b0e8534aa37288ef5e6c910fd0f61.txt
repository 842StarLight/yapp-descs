<b><i>Position Summary: </i></b>Orlando City SC is seeking hardworking, positive, and passionate individuals to work as Ticket Office Representatives for the upcoming season(s) at the Orlando City Stadium. The Ticket Office Representative is a part-time/game day position and is responsible for selling tickets and providing exceptional customer service to the public at the stadium on event/game days. <b><i>This position is hourly, and applicants must live in the Central Florida area</i></b><br>
<br>
<b><i>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</i></b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Sell tickets to customers using the Ticketmaster software.</li>
<li>Provide exceptional customer service while processing ticket orders and/or solving ticket related issues.</li>
<li>Be prepared to answer general questions regarding seating, pricing, stadium policies, and event details.</li>
<li>Accurately process customer ticket orders.</li>
<li>Accurately balance cash drawer at the end of each shift.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b><i>Qualifications:</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>Previous customer service and cash handling experience.</li>
<li>Computerized ticketing experience preferred.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills and able to listen and communicate effectively.</li>
<li>Proven job reliability, dedication, strong work ethic and responsibility.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and as a member of the team.</li>
<li>Ability to count money and make change accurately.</li>
<li>Ability to take and follow instructions.Exceptional communications skills. Position will work closely with the public.</li>
<li>Possess strong computer skills and be able to comprehend basic mathematics.</li>
<li>Available to work most events at the Orlando City Stadium (evenings, weekends, and holidays may apply).</li>
</ul>
