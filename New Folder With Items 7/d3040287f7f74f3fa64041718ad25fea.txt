<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>McKeany-Flavell is looking for a client-focused sales superstar with knowledge and interest in our areas of expertise to join our team.</p>
<p><b>PRIMARY JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maximize sales to primary accounts through client visits, assessing and negotiating needs</li>
<li>Support sales managers as the secondary representative on specific accounts</li>
<li>Create, meet and exceed personal sales and client contact goals</li>
<li>Glean market information and industry trends through customer and supplier contact</li>
<li>Provide category managers sales updates and important market intelligence acquired through daily</li>
<li>activity</li>
<li>Convey market dynamics to clients in a formal presentation format</li>
<li>Develop and accomplish annual travel plan and manage expenses to budget. Visit primary accounts at least twice and secondary accounts once a year. Meet total travel days as set in budget.</li>
<li>Forecast and manage annual sales plan to meet or exceed targeted goal</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MENTORSHIP</b></p>
<p>At McKeany Flavell our sales cycle is unique and complex. We understand that it takes mentorship from a seasoned professional to excel in this position. We are committed to investing time in our Sales Representative. In turn, we expect a goal-oriented go-getter who will leverage this mentorship to achieve greater sales results.</p>
<p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s or master’s degree in business, economics, finance and/or a unique combination of skills and experience related to food ingredient or agricultural commodities</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills. Bi-lingual (English/Spanish) a plus.</li>
<li>Sales experience with demonstrated success. Preferred emphasis in commodities or agriculture</li>
<li>Solid negotiation and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to research and network sources for important industry data</li>
<li>Self-driven and independent, yet flexible enough to still be a team player</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple accounts and achieve progress and results</li>
<li>Willingness to travel</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Ability to learn and integrate new software into daily workflow (i.e. company CRM, proprietary database, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ABOUT THE COMPANY</b></p>
<p>McKeany-Flavell is a commodity commercial intelligence company. From our roots as a West Coast-based sugar brokerage in 1948, our expertise has expanded from sweeteners to include a full spectrum of commodity ingredients, including edible oils, cocoa, chocolate, nutmeats, and fruit juice concentrate. We pride ourselves on finding the ideal match between ingredient suppliers and customers and achieving fair deals for all parties.</p>
<p>After more than six decades in the food and beverage industry, we know the players and the game. Our market intelligence—in the form of consultation, reports, presentations and recent launch of our IQ digital platform—gives our clients the edge in forecasting market direction and ingredient trends and pricing. We share our expertise with over 90 national and multi-national food and beverage companies. All our experience and effort combines to accomplish our vision: empowering our clients with commercial intelligence, supply chain expertise, and risk management solutions.</p>
<p><b>What do we provide to our client base? Vital services that deliver.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Unique commercial intelligence that helps our clients manage costs, optimize market timing and position themselves competitively in the markets and ingredient categories we follow.</li>
<li>Valuable support for clients in sourcing, negotiating price and delivering core ingredients to meet their procurement needs.</li>
<li>A full assortment of customer services from logistics and contract management to order entry and tracking.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Where are we headed? Toward technological solutions with a personal touch.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Publications and our online platform: </b>After decades of providing our expertise in the form of print-based written reports and publications, we are re-envisioning our intelligence delivery, and recently launched IQ our digital platform that delivers on-demand content to an increasingly tech-savvy client base. We aim to improve our clients’ user experience without sacrificing the relationships and personal touch that are the foundation of McKeany-Flavell’s brand.</li>
<li>
<b>Marketing, advertising and automating lead generation: </b>We are currently engaged in a comprehensive digital marketing effort to improve lead generation by leveraging our intelligence in the form of free content (i.e. blog posts, white papers, etc.), and automating our sales funnel. More leads equal more opportunity for our team of talented sales professionals.</li>
</ul>
