Tektronix is currently seeking an Applications Engineer to support solutions within its Performance Signal Sources business. This role will include software and digital signal generation algorithm development across various applications, including radar, communications, and electronic warfare, interfacing with our arbitrary waveform generators, real-time spectrum analyzers, and future ASIC ADCs, DACs, and APIs. The position will support pre- and post-sale activity by providing direct support to sales, field applications engineers, and customers.
<br><br>
The candidate will also be responsible for helping generate marketing collateral such as application notes and demo guides, and training field engineers supporting Arbitrary Waveform Generator customers. The position will interface with key stakeholders to include engineering, product managers, and field sales. Candidates must be able to communicate effectively to a variety of audiences, both written and verbally.
<br><br>
This position will be based in Beaverton, Oregon and require periodic domestic and international travel.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Integrate and enhance complex RF systems, and verify their ability to meet emerging customer requirements</li>
<li>Work with system engineers and field applications engineers to troubleshoot customer issues and manage customer expectations</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with system design engineers, product marketers, and the sales team to ensure team success in achieving organizational goals</li>
<li>Code development and validation using various languages: Python, C++, C#, and VHDL</li>
<li>Enable pre- and post-sales activities by supporting field sales and applications engineers</li>
<li>Champion and support continuous improvement initiatives across the organization – kaizen is our way of life!</li>
<li>Create application notes and demo guides</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field</li>
<li>Good understanding of RF and microwave fundamentals, to include radar and EW applications</li>
<li>Experience with independent test and measurement equipment setup and operation</li>
<li>3-5 years of experience supporting technical products</li>
<li>Able to plan, prioritize, and excel in a fast moving, cross-functional team environment</li>
<li>U.S. citizen or permanent resident (i.e. “U.S. person”)</li>
<li>Able to travel domestically and internationally</li>
</ul>
