<p>Position:<br>
Full-time Temporary - 6 months with possibility to hire as full-time<br>
<br>
<b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>This role is a strategic designer’s dream job.
</b></p>
<p>Life Chiropractic College West is looking for a multi-channel advertising designer who is passionate about storytelling through captivating design and compelling copy and thrives working on complex projects in a fun but fast-paced environment. In this role, you will have the opportunity to work closely with our internal clients to lead Life West advertising and campaigns to include, brand marketing content, sales enablement assets, social media designs and much much more. You will help ensure campaigns are resonating with our audiences across all channels and are consistent with the brand, to drive awareness, sentiment, engagement, and ultimately conversion. You will keep up with best practices, maintaining knowledge of the latest tactics and technologies in addition to our audiences and the competitive landscape. Advanced knowledge of advertising, design, layout, color theory, messaging, and typography principles and best practices, are essential to this role.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
</b></p>

<p><b>CORE VALUES</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The vision of Life West is to create a brighter future for humanity through graduating chiropractors who have a deep understanding of service, and of the innate potential of the human body to heal and adapt to its environment. The Doctor of Chiropractic program prepares graduates for personal and professional fulfilment in a climate of giving, doing, loving, and serving.</p>
