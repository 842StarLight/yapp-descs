<h2>Design Technician III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Provides technical support to the sales, customer service, and field service functions related to complex component projects; uses advanced knowledge of component engineering and field requirements to process sales orders, perform estimating and takeoff services.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Produces ELP layouts, performs engineering and takeoff services using ability to understand blueprints and customer requirements for specific projects.</li>
<li>Analyzes margins and quality standards to ensure a competitive and timely quote.</li>
<li>Interacts with and advises sales and customer service staff on technical aspects of customer inquiries and consults with engineering staff when more in-depth analysis is required; able to answer most questions using own knowledge and experience.</li>
<li>Writes engineered wood orders that reflect knowledge of equipment used in plant and how to optimize efficiency of cutting and assembly; demonstrates knowledge of loading conditions and utilizes design software packages.</li>
<li>Implements and monitors design control procedures, as necessary.</li>
</ul>
Associate's degree or equivalent from two-year college or technical school and three to five years of directly related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.
