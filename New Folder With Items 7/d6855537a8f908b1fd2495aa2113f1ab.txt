<h2>Purchasing Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Purchasing Agent Job Description: </b></p>
<p>We are a major wood and furniture hardware distributor. The Purchasing Agent reports to the President and is responsible for identifying suppliers, researching goods and services, processing purchase orders, and verifying delivered items.</p>
<p>To be successful as a Purchasing Agent you should be able to critically assess suppliers, products, and services, and negotiate the best possible deals. Ultimately, an outstanding Purchasing Agent has a keen sense of business and maintains meticulous transaction records.</p>
<p><b>Purchasing Agent Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Researching and comparing suppliers, goods, and services.</li>
<li>Selecting the most suitable suppliers in terms of reliability, product quality, and cost-efficiency.</li>
<li>Monitoring inventory levels and determining purchase needs.</li>
<li>Requesting cost proposals and negotiating contracts and prices.</li>
<li>Preparing and processing purchase orders.</li>
<li>Maintaining records of all transactions, purchase orders, receipts, and payments.</li>
<li>Scheduling and verifying purchase deliveries.</li>
<li>Building and maintaining positive, long-term supplier relations.</li>
<li>Able to multitask and handle pressure.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Purchasing Agent Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school degree/GED required.</li>
<li>Degree in business administration, procurement, or a related field preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum 3 - 5 years previous experience as a Purchasing Agent.</li>
<li>Proficiency in inventory management software programs.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and negotiation skills.</li>
<li>Strong organization and documentation skills.</li>
<li>Strong research and analytical skills.</li>
</ul>
