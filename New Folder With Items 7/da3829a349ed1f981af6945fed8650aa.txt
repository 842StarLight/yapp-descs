<h2>Sr. Electrical Eng. Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p>Great Lakes Chemical Corporation, (A Company of the LANXESS Group), is currently seeking an enthusiastic, motivated, and talented individual for the position of Senior Electrical Eng. Project Manager in El Dorado, Arkansas.</p>

At LANXESS, we are "Energizing Chemistry". This phrase perfectly embodies our philosophy and operating principles as well as our view of the entire chemical industry. To us, energizing chemistry can be described very simply:</p>
<ul>
<li>Be quick, agile and resilient</li>
<li>Focus on the essentials</li>
<li>Continuously improve performance and products to help customers, the environment, employees and society as a whole.</li>
</ul>
<p>LANXESS, as a part of the greater chemical industry, has the performance capabilities and innovative strength to meet these commitments. We believe that by following these principles and energizing chemistry, we can improve the quality of life of people across the world. Further, we see that our innovations and products are making important contributions to society.</p>
<p>
It is our corporate responsibility to create lasting value for all shareholders and to consciously address the impact of our actions. The only way to guarantee the future success and sustainability of our company is to balance our strategy with the demands of the economy, ecology and society. At LANXESS, we take a long-term view and use our knowledge, ability, and experience to develop product and technologies that will protect the climate and environment and improve people's quality of life.</p>
<p>
We are responsible employers and support social and cultural projects all over the world. Corporate responsibility is integral to every aspect of our enterprise. With our global headquarters in Cologne, Germany, LANXESS is comprised of 19,000 employees and 75 production sites in 19 countries with manufacturing, sales, and customer service capabilities in all region of the world.</p>

<p>
<b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage multiple capital expansion projects (4 to 10 projects) from concept to completion utilizing the stage gate FEL process for central, south, west plants, and brine field.</li>
<li>Leading, Developing, and implementing capital projects from FEL 1,2,3, Execute and Commission</li>
<li>Effectively leads mid to large-scale projects ($1 – 10 mm) which typically involve new products, significant technology shifts, or new business venture; projects may involve evaluation of a new technology and whether to invest in commercialization</li>
<li>Ensures accurate project cost estimating, cost control, and scheduling</li>
<li>Assist process owners and business/site leadership to implement performance improvement strategies and evaluate engineering designs for best practices</li>
<li>Leads a coordinated effort amongst engineers and personnel from multiple disciplines to achieve BU goals for project execution</li>
<li>Expert level individual contributor, manager of small team of professionals or project leader who guides others in resolving complex issues in specialized area, best practice resource in disciplinary field, technical and professional mentorship to all levels of engineers</li>
<li>Evaluation of multiple engineered solutions to best meet BU objectives</li>
<li>Optimizes engineering and manufacturing work processes</li>
<li>Supports evaluation or commercialization of new product line</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum BS in Electrical Engineering,</li>
<li>PMP certified or Prof. Eng. (PE) is a plus.</li>
<li>Minimum of 10 years’ experience in project management or manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>Expert in technical field of competence</li>
<li>Proven experience in specialty chemicals and El Dorado sites a plus</li>
<li>Plans &amp; Aligns, Collaborates, Ensures Accountability, Drive Results, Optimizes work processes, Courage, Manages Complexity, Builds effective teams, Effective Oral and written communications</li>
<li>Experience in specialty chemicals a plus and especially knowledge of El Dorado sites</li>
</ul>
