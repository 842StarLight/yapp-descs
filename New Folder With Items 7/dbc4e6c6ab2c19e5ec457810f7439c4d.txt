<div><b>
Job Summary- Inside Sales / Sales Development Representative</b></div>
<div>
We are currently seeking entrepreneurial-minded candidates for the position of Inside Sales / Sales Development Representative. As a Sales Development Representative you will be responsible for developing leads and answering inbound inquiries via form fill, phone calls, and content download. You will also facilitate meetings with prospects for sales qualification.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We employ smart, curious, and adventurous team members. We hire individuals who do their best work in a collaborative environment and have a passion for innovation. Othot provides employees with a competitive compensation and benefits package. We offer flexible working arrangements and many great perks. We'd love to see if you're a good fit for our team and if Othot is a good fit for you!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Duties and Responsibilities
</b></div>
<div>Inbound Lead Development</div>
<ul>
<li>
Engage with Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) via phone to qualify and potentially set meetings for Account Executives (AEs – i.e., sales executives)</li>
<li>
Work with marketing and sales teams to convert MQLs to Sales Qualified Leads (SQLs)</li>
<li>
Manage MQL meeting schedules and completions</li>
<li>
Support development and improvement of internal assessments of MQLs</li>
</ul>
<div>
Sales Automation</div>
<ul>
<li>
Help support the application, tracking and reporting of digital marketing activities</li>
<li>
Manage productivity tools that assist the processing of MQL qualification and SQL generation</li>
<li>
Assist in planning and execution of, and immediate follow up for, major events including webinars and conferences</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Qualifications + Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>
1-3 years of sales and marketing experience or inbound/outbound call experience with prospects</li>
<li>
Willingness and has confidence to engage prospects via 1:1 phone calls</li>
<li>
Basic understanding or knowledge of inbound marketing channels and tactics</li>
<li>
Experience working with a marketing automation and/or CRM platform or a willingness to learn to effectively track leads/MQLs</li>
<li>
Ability to work closely with AEs and marketing personnel in support of cross-functional and prospective customer communications</li>
<li>
Ability to balance competing priorities and manage several time-sensitive projects/deals in a fast-paced sales environment</li>
<li>
Strong interest in higher education and conversational about technology and/or machine learning/AI solutions</li>
</ul>
