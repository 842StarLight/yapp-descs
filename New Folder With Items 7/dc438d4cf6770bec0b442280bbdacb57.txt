<p>
Adams Products, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Description Summary<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Reads job specifications to determine machine adjustments and material requirements</li>
<li>
Set stops or guides to specific lengths as indicated by scale, rule or templates</li>
<li>
Observes machine operation to detect product defects or machine malfunction</li>
<li>
Reviews product for inconsistencies or abnormal appearances assuring product meets quality requirements</li>
<li>
Performs routine machine maintenance</li>
<li>
Assists other operators to set up machines and any other necessary tasks to adhere to deadlines and completion of product</li>
<li>
Makes sure all housekeeping is maintained in a clear and orderly fashion in assigned areas</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent; or at least one month of related experience; or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>
A minimum of 3 years of experience operating production machines</li>
<li>
Basic understanding of electronics and programmable controllers</li>
<li>
Know the basics of hydraulics, electricity, and tools</li>
<li>
Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals</li>
<li>
Ability to add, subtract, multiply, and divide, in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions and decimals</li>
<li>
Experience working with Microsoft Suite to include Word, Excel, etc.</li>
</ul>
