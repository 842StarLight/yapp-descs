<br>
<b>Nature of Work</b><br>
This is a part-time seasonal/temporary position that provides ticket sales for concerts, theatre performances, sporting events, trade shows, and other ticketed events held at the Mayo Civic Center. The role of the Box Office is to provide efficient and convenient methods of distributing tickets and event information.<br>
<br>
At the Mayo Civic Center we strive to be world class in everything we do. To achieve that goal in the Box Office, we build our team with people who are passionate about delivering the highest standards of customer care.<br>
<br>
<b>Work Schedule</b><br>
Mayo Civic Center is currently seeking both daytime and event staff. Daytime staff work primarily 3-4 hour shifts Monday-Saturday. Event staff work shifts that are scheduled "as needed" based on event booking, and are approximately 3-6 hours in length and occur on evenings, weekends and holidays. A staff member can work both daytime and event shifts, based on scheduling needs.<br>
<br>
<b>Starting Salary</b><br>
Starting wage is $11.36 per hour.<br>
<br>
<b>Limited Term Appointment</b><br>
The Mayo Civic Center will be transitioning to a private management firm at the end of 2019. While this position will not continue under City of Rochester employment beyond December 31, 2019, there may be an opportunity to apply for employment with the new management firm.<br>
<div><i>
Applications will be accepted until all positions are filled.</i></div>

 DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: <i>The work below is representative of the scope of work performed within this job classification. Individual job duties will vary based on work assignment.</i><ul>
<li>Serve as the first point of contact to welcome all Mayo Civic Center visitors, customers, clients, staff and guests.
</li>
<li>Contribute to an exceptional customer service experience for all visitors and guests to the Mayo Civic Center.
</li>
<li>Sell tickets to Mayo Civic Center events using the TICKETMASTER ticketing system
</li>
<li>Collect payments by cash, check, credit cards, gift certificates, or vouchers
</li>
<li>Accurately issue change due, receipts and refunds to maintain a balanced cash drawer
</li>
<li>Reconcile cash drawer against daily sales report at the end of shift
</li>
<li>Provide event information regarding performance times, venue location, ticket pricing and seating arrangements
</li>
<li>Explain Box Office policies and resolve routine problems regarding ticket sales
</li>
<li>Answer telephone calls, take accurate messages, and pass them on to the appropriate person in a timely manner
</li>
<li>Act as a "Visitor Center" by providing lodging, food, entertainment and other miscellaneous information to visitors of Rochester
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned or necessary</li>
</ul>
