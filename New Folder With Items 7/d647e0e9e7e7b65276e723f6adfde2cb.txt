<h2>Head of Sales and Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>The goal of Blueprint Title Company is simple: streamline residential real estate closings for buyers, sellers, and real estate service professionals. We bring innovative technology, process, and pricing to the $16 Billion Title Insurance industry. Founded in January 2017, Blueprint Title operates over a broad, multi-state footprint and is quickly expanding.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
We are a team of driven individuals with a passion for disrupting title insurance and modernizing the closing experience. We are looking for high performers to share our mission and join in our growth. As a member of the Blueprint Title team, you will help us lead the charge in proving that the real estate closing industry is ready for novel technology, lower pricing, and better service.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
What you will work on</b></div>
<div>
As the Director of Sales, you will drive significant and sustained revenue growth by personally closing large (and small) enterprise deals, designing sales process, and leading an exceptional sales organization. You view sales as a forum for you to operate as a player, coach, and architect. Specific responsibilities include:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Developing and leading a team of highly motivated sales professionals in existing markets, new markets, and headquarters
</li>
<li>Personally closing and managing key customer relationships</li>
<li>Consistently exceeding all revenue and KPI goals</li>
<li>Implementing CRM and reporting systems, processes, and other infrastructure to drive operational efficiency
</li>
<li>Collaborate with leadership to provide customer feedback and identify opportunities to satisfy customer needs</li>
<li>Engage the onboarding and processing teams to provide exceptional service to new and continuing clients</li>
</ul>
