<p><b>Overview:</b> Platform innovation is a critical component of SAP’s Intelligent Enterprise strategy and establishes SAP as a leading (cloud) platform company. The Platform &amp; Technology Sales group will include a Digital Sales team, whose role is to create and execute sales campaigns to drive revenue that benefits their assigned Market Unit.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Objective:</b> As part of the Digital Sales organization, this person will identify pipeline, progress sales cycles, and close deals in a relatively untapped set of accounts.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Activities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Penetrate assigned Market Unit Dormant/Net New Name and Upper GB accounts</li>
<li>Drive awareness and deliver revenue around SAP’s innovative offerings such as iOT, ML, AI, and Blockchain</li>
<li>Identify emerging customers and companies by leveraging whitespace tools and driving prospecting and demand generation activities in the assigned territory</li>
<li>Utilizes inbound and/or outbound techniques to identify, qualify and close new opportunities.</li>
<li>Understand SAP’s P&amp;T value proposition, product offering(s) and competitive nature to develop solution proposals.</li>
<li>Design a territory plan, build incremental pipeline and maintain weekly forecast updates on current and future quarter(s) for territory</li>
<li>Leverage a Digital Selling Strategy including Marketing Automation, Social, Digital and Content tools.<br>
</li>
</ul>
