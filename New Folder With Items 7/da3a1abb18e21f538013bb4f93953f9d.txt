<h2>Global Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>SUMMARY:</b> The Global Product Manager is responsible for the overall success of the product and service portfolios and for delivering measurable business results through product and service solutions that meet both market needs and organizational goals.</p>
<p>
The critical functions of the position include the development and execution of new and existing product and service strategies based upon industry, competitive, and regulatory trends, as well as customer and distributor interactions in the field. Key responsibilities include defining the vision for new products and services, optimizing product and service portfolios, leading the new product development process, establishing pricing guidelines, and managing marketing communications while helping to ensure revenue and customer satisfaction goals are met.</p>
<p>
This position will require both domestic and international travel.</p>
<p>
Alpha Technologies, a Roper company, is the world leader in the design, manufacture, service and support of precision rubber and polymer laboratory instrumentation and data acquisition/analysis software systems.</p>
<p>
Essential Job Functions – Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
Define and execute short and long-term global product and service strategies and roadmap positioning Alpha as the partner of choice and ensuring maximum customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Work with cross-functional teams to optimize the product and service portfolio - routinely assess and rationalize product line and solution offering</li>
<li>
Manage and drive the overall financial performance of product lines for growth and profitability</li>
<li>
Conduct and report out on global market research and analysis including market size, market trends, competitive analysis, and client purchasing behaviors</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain competitive landscape for the market globally, identifying competitor capabilities and provide assessment of Alpha’s performance</li>
<li>
Identify ideas for new product/service development and modifications by assimilating VOC, industry data/trends, customer needs, internal feedback, competitive analysis, and new technologies</li>
<li>
Conduct customer visits globally to help determine customer dynamics, drivers, current and future needs, opportunities, purchasing behaviors, and specific geographical considerations for both products and services</li>
<li>
Lead new product/service development process, product launches and project plans</li>
<li>
Lead communication of new product/service project status, progress, and risks</li>
<li>
Develop market and customer requirements and specifications critical for the commercial and technical success of new and enhanced products/services</li>
<li>
Validate new product/service concepts</li>
<li>
Work closely with the sales organization to define global pricing guidelines and update pricing as needed to ensure competitiveness and maximum profitability</li>
<li>
Analyze global sales and price position to develop strategies for product/service growth and profit improvement</li>
<li>
Provide content for development of marketing materials, sales presentations, trade shows, and training programs</li>
<li>
Lead the Marketing Communications team to develop product/service marketing strategies, including post-release communication of features, product/service capabilities, messaging and branding</li>
<li>
Proactively identify and overcome business issues by working collaboratively with cross-functional teams</li>
</ul>
