<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>End User Computing Consultant</b></h3>
<p><b>
Who We Are
</b></p>
<p>Burwood Group is not your average technology consulting firm. We are an innovative, culture-driven, industry leader and we are growing our team by leaps and bounds. Founded in 1997 in Chicago (and still headquartered here), we've grown to be a 200+ employee firm with people and offices spread from Southern California to NYC. Our specialty is closing the gap between business strategy and technology solutions. Our clients view Burwood as a trusted advisor and partner who can help them select the best technology for the job, manage and execute a first-rate implementation, and measure project success by the business outcomes achieved.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What You’ll Do</b></h4>
<p>
As an End User Computing Consultant, you will work in the Service Delivery group as part of a multi-disciplinary consulting practice that delivers solutions using a wide variety of IT Automation technologies and solutions, including: application and desktop virtualization, application delivery controllers, management and orchestration platforms, and cloud solutions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and deliver technology solutions for customers, with an exceptionally high focus on quality</li>
<li>
Work closely together with other Burwood Group project team members</li>
<li>
Provide pre-sales engineering activities, such as building bills of material and developing statements of work</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Build strong and continuous relationship with our clients, becoming their go-to advisor</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Provide training and knowledge transfer to clients to ensure they are able to successfully maintain solutions post-implementation</li>
<li>
Act as a mentor and provide technical guidance to peers</li>
</ul>

<li>

<li>
