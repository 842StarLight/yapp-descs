<div><b>ROLE OVERVIEW</b></div>

<div>As an Assistant Manager for Tribute Properties, you are a multi-talented, personable individual with sales expertise and a passion for building relationships. Here are the roles you'll play:</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>The Team Leader</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Mastermind of resident experiences. Creating an irresistible ambiance and vibe</li>
<li>Sales, Sales, Sales!</li>
<li>Energetically participate and take the lead in the leasing and renewal process by engaging with prospective and existing residents</li>
<li>Be the know it all in the community sub-market, including market conditions, competitor pricing, and new or pending lease-up developments</li>
<li>Act as right hand to the Community Manager, providing leadership, delegation, and supervision to the office, service and concierge teams, making essential determination</li>
<li>Be the campaign manager of community outreach with local businesses &amp; non-profits</li>
<li>You are a team-player. You are united with teammates in delivering an epic experience to current and prospective residents</li>
<li>Thrive outside of the box, you are an idea person and engineer innovative solutions</li>
