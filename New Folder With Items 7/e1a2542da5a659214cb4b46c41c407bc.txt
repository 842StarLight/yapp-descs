<h2>Automotive Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Reliable Imports &amp; RV is experiencing a RECORD year! We are currently seeking an experienced Sales Manager to add to our award winning team!</p>
<p>The <b>Sales Manager</b> is responsible for profitability in the department and for customer retention. To achieve this, they must effectively manage sales personnel, have a strong knowledge of the market, and an in-depth understanding of all sales departments’ financial data as well as strong customer relations skills.</p>
<p>The sales manager is expected to uphold the highest ethical standards in every aspect of the job.</p>
<p>Job duties for a sales manager include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Meeting with sales staff to plan and implement objectives for achieving sales and gross profits.</li>
<li>Hiring and monitoring the performance of the sales staff, holding sales meetings and conducting sales training.</li>
<li>Overseeing standards for displaying and merchandising vehicles as well as reviewing and initialing all promotions before they are finalized.</li>
<li>Coordinating the appropriate supply of vehicles and ordering/acquiring vehicle inventory accordingly.</li>
<li>Meeting with the dealership’s general manager to review forecasts and profits for each department.</li>
<li>Working directly with the general manager on making recommendations on both short and long-range advertising plans, sales promotions, staffing needs, lease promotions and compensation plans.</li>
<li>Attending to customer complaints, ensuring that a high level of customer satisfaction is obtained.</li>
<li>Experience in a dealership sales environment preferred. Additionally, needs to be comfortable managing people.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requires strong communication skills to deal with customers, employees and vendors, as they represent the dealership.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>sales: 1 year (Required)</li>
<li>Automotive Sales: 1 year (Required)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Preferred)</li></ul>

<ul>

</ul>
