<h2>Risk Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What does a great Risk Manager do?
</b></p>
<p>Under general supervision of the Director of Risk Operations, the Risk Manager will provide leadership for a team of approx. 15 Risk Analysts as well as consultative loss mitigation collaboration and recommendations support to a large population of Risk Office client partners.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Basic Qualifications for Consideration:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree required: Master’s degree preferred</li>
<li>
Minimum 3+ years of dynamic and innovative team leadership in a challenging technical or financial environment</li>
<li>
Minimum 4+ years of risk management, card fraud, and financial services experience</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Additional Skills, Experience, and Education:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated internal and external negotiation skills with senior executive</li>
<li>
Strong English writing skills, including occasional power point deck creation</li>
<li>
Strong relationship and communication skills to ensure proper client interaction in managing fraud issues (i.e. articulating event, mitigating liability, training and documentation)</li>
<li>
Must possess a high level of computer skills (to effectively utilize multiple EFT systems, Excel, Word and PowerPoint)</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of workstations and database software</li>
<li>
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Ability to prepare and analyze data in a logical manner and be able to summarize findings to both a technical and non-technical audience</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Travel required:</b> 10-15%</p>
<p></p>
