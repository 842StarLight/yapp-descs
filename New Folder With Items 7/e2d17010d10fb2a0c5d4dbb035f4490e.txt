<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Product Marketing defines and drives the go-to-market plans to maximize adoption of new products and features. Owns defining the positioning and segmentation for new and existing products and stays current with customer, competitor, and market trends to make informed and data-driven plans to drive adoption of MINDBODY features and products.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
JOB</b><b> SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
Product Marketing Managers help shape the future of a product or family of products in the beauty, fitness and wellness space - including MINDBODY Online, Booker, Frederick, and FitMetrix - in service of 60,000+ business customers. Partner with product management, sales, engineering, marketing, and research to fully understand customer needs, inform product decisions, and communicate feature delivery internally and externally. Determine end-to-end go-to-market strategies, including the positioning, development of the strategy, and managing the execution of key launches and initiatives. Drive successful outcomes and help us deliver value our customers and the MINDBODY business at large.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS AND REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<br>
<div>
<div></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
<p>Bachelor's degree in business or related field.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Minimum 2 to 4 years’ experience in software product marketing, product management, or marketing in the software, SaaS, technology, e-commerce, health, fitness or wellness space. Experience with MINDBODY or similar products a plus.</p>
</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>
<p>Experience with customer engagement platforms, marketing automation or similar digital marketing business services for small- to medium-sized businesses.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrated track record of success driving marketing initiatives for SaaS products, features, services, and mobile apps.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience working across multiple functions and with various team leads to deliver results.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to understand technical terms and processes and translate to customer/user value. Understanding of market research, customer segmentation, and positioning.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to rapidly prioritize and adapt to changing customer dynamics.</p>
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>
<p>Presentation and communication skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Uphold MINDBODY’s core values and culture.</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<p></p>
<p><b>PRINCIPAL</b><b> DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<br>
<div>
<div></div>
<div><ul><li>
<p>Work with Product, Sales and Marketing Leaders to keep Product Marketing initiatives prioritized.</p>
</li></ul></div>
</div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>
<p>Drive execution of the go-to-market strategy by owning key elements of the product launch process.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build communications strategy and compelling messaging to drive awareness &amp; consideration for products, as well as drive product adoption among existing customers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Define target buyer and user segments for products and services to drive marketing programs strategy and provide insights to internal teams.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborate with the marketing program managers to create targeted campaigns that drive awareness, preference, purchase and adoption.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Establish a rapport with individual Product Managers and be a key stakeholder on our product Core Teams to uncover &amp; understand the details of a product release, and translate that information into strategy for internal education, messaging documentation and readiness for product launch.</p>
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>
<p>Partner with Product &amp; Marketing to plan and drive communication and engagement within customer journey workflow and adoption initiatives.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide visibility to key GTM departments on key initiatives and the product roadmap.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Stay on top of industry trends by attending industry events, monitoring competitors and partner activity, reviewing customer feedback &amp; market trends regularly.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide regular education for both internal and external stakeholders on our product features and their benefits.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Measure and optimize the customer journey touchpoints in order to drive product feature adoption and usage.</p>
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>
<p>Be an evangelist for the product within the company, helping everyone understand key positioning and messages.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist in the creation and maintenance of a competitive intelligence repository.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Contribute to global go-to-market plans to help grow and scale the business.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work with business development and product management to influence product roadmap, research, and pitch revenue expansion opportunities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>All other duties as assigned.</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul></div></div>
