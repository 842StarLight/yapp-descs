<div><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>The Business Strategy Manager will join the Portfolio Management Group of Oracle’s Cloud Business Group organization. You will have the unique responsibility for providing data-driven perspective to product, go-to-market, and other growth initiatives for Oracle’s extensive business portfolio.
</div>
<div>Specifically, you will asked to focus on different areas of the portfolio depending on the business need. You will be asked to provide analytical assessment of strategic initiatives or business operations that could span across product, go-to-market and business functions.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>You will partner across internal business teams (Finance, Product Strategy, Sales Leadership, Regional GTM etc.) to gather key insights, strengths and weaknesses of the current Oracle’s business strategies.</div>
<div>Work is non-routine and complex and similar to that of a management consultant. This is a critical and high visibility role spanning a broad range of exciting cloud technologies and products.<br>
