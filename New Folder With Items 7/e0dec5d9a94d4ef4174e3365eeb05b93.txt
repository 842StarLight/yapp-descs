<h2>Real EstateListing Agent - Austin (South Central/Southwest Austin)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Redfin is redefining real estate in the consumer's favor. As a full-service brokerage, we combine our own technology and real estate agents to make buying or selling a home faster, easier, and more efficient. At Redfin, you’ll have marketing and field support from start to finish for every deal, plus top-shelf tools that streamline your workflow and give you a competitive edge. If your passion is serving customers in a collaborative, tech-savvy environment, join us.</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>
As a Redfin Real Estate Listing Agent, you'll spend your time working with clients, not finding them. We'll handle the prospecting, advertising, and logistics so you can focus on what you love about real estate, while earning a steady income and top-tier benefits.</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>
The Role</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div><b>
Work within your desired territory, building relationships with clients while sharing your expertise to help them navigate their home sale</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>
Meet all your clients through Redfin or by referral—no cold-calling or door-knocking required</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>
Receive support from a team of real estate professionals responsible for marketing, open houses, showings, inspections, and the closing process</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>
Create instant CMAs and answer client questions on the spot with mobile tools that give you access to competitive data and up-to-date market statistics</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>
Benefit from our online system that lets you track the transaction process and communicates automatically with clients so they stay up to date with every step</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>
Maximize client follow-up with our automated system that tracks client and market activity on Redfin.com and lets you know when it's the right time to reach out</b></div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Skills &amp; Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div><b>
Real estate license and a history of closing listing transactions (required)</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>
Knowledge of your local real estate market</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>
Customer-advocate mindset and a commitment to delivering the best client experience possible</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>
Experience using technology to communicate with clients and access competitive market data</b></div></li>
</ul>
