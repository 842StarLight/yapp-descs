<b>POSITION SUMMARY: </b>Assists with operations training and leasing success aspects of new property takeovers and transitions. Fills in at properties that have a Community Manager vacancy. Travels to properties that are behind market in leasing and perform</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>KEY DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Weekly monitor overall company leasing trends, follow up speed and quality</li>
<li>Conduct on-site sales training as needed</li>
<li>Strategize and create action plans for properties struggling to meet leasing goals</li>
<li>Facilitate a positive environment that enhances the experience for our employees &amp; residents</li>
<li>Build and maintain relationships with Community Managers; boost morale during times of stress</li>
<li>Set up model apartments and leasing resources for new properties</li>
<li>Work with investors and clients to ensure leasing expectations are being met</li>
<li>Compile competitor data into market survey reports for new acquisitions</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>EXPERIENCE &amp; QUALIFICATIONS:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2+ years of experience with sales or leasing</li>
<li>Willing to travel 2-3 weeks each month during the busy leasing season</li>
<li>Ability to use and quickly learn technology – Google Apps, Microsoft Office, cloud-based management and dashboarding software</li>
<li>Ability to manage projects and multitask several processes at once under stressful conditions</li>
<li>Experience with Entrata Software or familiarity with PM software is a plus</li>
<li>Team Player – able to inspire team members to high levels of performance and to provide constructive feedback as needed -Bachelor’s Degree preferred, or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Client-ready” professional demeanor and presentation</li>
</ul>
