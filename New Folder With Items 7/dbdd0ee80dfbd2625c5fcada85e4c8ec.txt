<b>Job Description</b><br>

<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li><b>Job description</b></li>
</ul>
<p>We’re seeking an organized and driven individual to take ownership of customer support and success at TerrAvion. The ideal candidate should enjoy helping people and solving problems, whether that’s by doing a quick phone call or creating a process for the team to use.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>– Lead the support team
<ul>
<li>Create processes and scripts to triage, assign, and resolve support tickets</li>
<li>Manage the creation of materials (docs, videos, etc.) to address common questions</li>
<li>Evaluate support team performance, manage scheduling, etc.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>– Provide feedback to the company
<ul>
<li>Report on team performance, and ways we can improve</li>
<li>Help train the company on how to respond to customer questions</li>
<li>Create a customer satisfaction measurement</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>– Manage Enterprise Projects
<ul>
<li>Be the point of contact for enterprise customer questions</li>
<li>Manage enterprise customer projects &amp; track important milestones</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<div><div><ul>
<li>3+ years combined customer support, sales, teaching, or management experience</li>
<li>Love of efficiency, order, and continual improvement</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication</li>
<li>Desire for responsibility &amp; career advancement</li>
<li>Not afraid of technology</li>
<li>Patience &amp; empathy</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
</ul></div></div>
