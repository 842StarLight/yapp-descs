<p>The Sr. Director Optimization Buy-Side is responsible for all facets of the development, execution and management of the sales strategy for the Buy-Side in the AMER region. This position is responsible for delivering against revenue targets and accelerating growth for the segment across all asset classes, within each asset class, and in each of CME Group’s business units. Strong sales leadership capabilities, extensive industry knowledge and contacts, and a track record of success in growing relevant business are required. This role requires strong collaboration, coordination and leadership with other CD&amp;S regional segment leads globally.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Principal Accountabilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and execute sales strategy for accelerating revenue growth from existing and new key clients</li>
<li>
Develop, execute and lead CME Group’s Optimization Buy-Side in the AMER sales strategy and ensure alignment with the global segment strategy</li>
<li>
Understand global competitive landscape and impact on client segment and advance the development and execution of specific country plans within region to address these challenges and opportunities</li>
<li>
Ensure alignment of internal resources to meet strategic account plans; manage the allocation of budget and resources</li>
<li>
In collaboration with the Regional CD&amp;S Head, Business Line Heads and Marketing, develop strategies to optimally position CME Group products across relevant client segment by:</li>
<li>
Increasing the number of clients/segment penetration, increasing number of asset classes/products traded per client, increasing overall and per-client volume, and/or expanding trading hours.</li>
<li>
Leverage CRM system to proactively initiate communication and coordination across segments to drive cross selling efforts</li>
<li>
Manage and/or coach a team of sales representatives</li>
<li>
Represent CME Group to the media, industry, conferences, trade shows and associations as necessary</li>
<li>
Understand competitive landscape and develop/align growth and go-to-market client segment strategies</li>
</ul>

Sales management experience</li>
<li>

<li>
Well-developed networking and relationship building skills, complemented by negotiating acumen and sound business judgment</li>
<li>
Proven abilities to influence others and to achieve tangible business results</li>
</ul>
