<h2>Head of Cloud Strategy and Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Calypso Technology is seeking a Head of Cloud Strategy and Operations, with at least 10 years’<br>
experience .The ideal candidate is a subject matter expert responsible for managing and driving<br>
cloud initiatives, supporting sales, services, and support in achieving the growth objectives with<br>
Calypso’s overall cloud strategy into global markets and regions.</p>
<div>
<b>
Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Own and manage Calypso’s cloud strategy</li>
<li>Responsible for promoting and supporting the strategy and growth of Calypso’s products in</li>
</ul>
cloud-based solutions<br>
<ul>
<li>Driver of cloud operations end to end, including managing and meeting defined SLAs</li>
<li>Provide leadership to colleagues, partners and other organizational segments in order to enable</li>
</ul>
global sales, presales and services resources to appropriately support market penetration and<br>
adoption<br>
<ul><li>Chartered with engaging in a variety of activities with interaction that is involved but is not limited</li></ul>
to, engaging with prospects, customers, external partners, sales, marketing, R&amp;D and<br>
professional services<br>
<ul><li>As a client advocate, work with clients to provide strategic and tactical recommendations for</li></ul>
successful use of Calypso’s cloud deployment<br>
<ul><li>Interact with different levels of management within Calypso i.e. partners, prospects and</li></ul>
customers<br>
<ul>
<li>Lead and manage the cloud strategy &amp; operations teams</li>
<li>Help develop, run and manage KPIs regarding the quality of service being delivered to our</li>
</ul>
customers<br>
<ul>
<li>Work with Product Management on product design and enhancements</li>
<li>Stay abreast on future trends in cloud solutions</li>
<li>Ensure the regions are well equipped to execute the cloud strategy</li>
<li>Enhance and refine cloud platform whilst maintaining stability of the platform</li>
</ul>
Skills &amp; Requirements<br>
<ul><li>10+ years of experience in Cloud Strategy, Technical Account Management or Professional</li></ul>
Certifications in leading cloud vendors such as AWS, Azure<br>
<ul>
<li>Experience working with large enterprise clients</li>
<li>Educated to degree level (or equivalent)</li>
<li>Able to thrive in a dynamic, fast paced, entrepreneurial and result-oriented culture.</li>
<li>Willingness and ability to engage with global contacts (Internal and External)</li>
<li>Deep understanding of software development life cycles</li>
<li>A strong customer advocate with the ability and willingness to engage directly with senior levels</li>
</ul>
of management both internally and externally (from C-level to Business Sponsors)<br>
<ul>
<li>Ability lead through influence and integrity</li>
<li>Process and detail oriented</li>
</ul>
