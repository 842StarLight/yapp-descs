<h2>Store Merch Ops Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>Collaborates with store operations, merchandising, and marketing teams to develop merchandising and store workload</p>
<p>
plans, presents recommendations, executes and finalizes projects, and ensures project deadlines are met. Acquires</p>
<p>
resources and coordinates the efforts of field management, internal departments, and/or contractors to deliver projects in</p>
<p>
accordance with plan.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p>1. Develops merchandising and workload project plans and timelines, estimates resources needed, and presents recommendations to achieve project plans.</p>
<p>
2. Responsible for communicating project and workload plan expectations, timelines, and changes. Coordinates efforts of all parties involved to ensure delivery of projects in accordance with plan.</p>
<p>
3. Partners with various departments to maximize merchandising operations and sales opportunities, and drive workload planning enhancements.</p>
<p>
4. Maintains the store workload communication tools including but not limited to, the task administration system and all workload planning calendars.</p>
<p>
5. Identifies and resolves issues and conflicts that arise with workload planning and task administration.</p>
<p>
6. Provides review and approval for planograms and planoguides for store displays, attends display approval walkthroughs, and merchant hindsight and strategy meetings.</p>
<p>
7. Develops, maintains, and delivers progress reports, requirements documentation, and communications.</p>
<p>
8. Prepares and coordinates the distribution of a variety of store deliverables.</p>
<p>
9. Tracks project milestones and deliverables.</p>
<p>
10. Develops and maintains best practices and tools for project execution.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>1. High school diploma or equivalent required; College degree preferred.</p>
<p>
2. Minimum of 3 years experience in a retail environment required.</p>
<p>
3. Ability to travel by car or air, for extended periods of time including overnight travel required.</p>
<p>
4. Excellent written and verbal skills with the ability to maintain clear/effective communications with all levels of management required.</p>
<p>
5. Proficient PC/software skills including InDesign, Excel and Word preferred.</p>
<p>
6. Ability to work under general supervision and make independent decisions required.</p>
<p>
7. Ability to prioritize and manage multiple projects required.</p>
<p>
8. Mobility to move freely throughout a store and the ability to lift, carry, push, and pull a minimum of 25 pounds on a regular basis required. Frequently required to stand for long periods of time, bend, stoop or kneel.</p>
