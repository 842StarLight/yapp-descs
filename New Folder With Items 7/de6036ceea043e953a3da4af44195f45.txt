<h2>Technical Support Representative - IBM MaaS360</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Introduction</b><br>
Are you a technical support detective who can identify, troubleshoot, and resolve problems? IBM is looking for multi-tasking, customer-focused detectives to understand client's issues and work with development (and funnily enough, Watson) to solve them. Even the best detectives need additional tools on the crime scene. Our global group of detectives leverages JIRA, SFDC, and the MaaS360 solution to investigate customer problems. Our primary "crime scene" could be located anywhere around the globe &amp; is tailored to any business or industry.<br>
<br>
<b>Your Role and Responsibilities</b><br>
<br>
In this role, you will work directly with the customer’s IT management and/or First Level Support to integrate and troubleshoot MaaS360. You will also work closely with different groups in our organization to help our Pre-sales engineers implement customer lifecycle management, collaborate with our R&amp;D organization to improve our product by providing feedback on customer needs/requirements, and help our sales organization achieve successful customer engagements.<br>
<br>
Your Responsibilities<ul>
<li>
Troubleshoot web-based solutions and system software.</li>
<li>
Diagnose and resolve customer reported system incidents, problems, and events.</li>
<li>
Work with IBM's internal development to ensure timely issue resolution.</li>
<li>
Prioritize current customer volume with existing cases/tickets.</li>
</ul>
Abilities &amp; Skills<ul>
<li>
Accurately define incidents, problems, and events in the trouble ticketing system.</li>
<li>
Develop, update, and/or maintain standard operating procedures (SOPs).</li>
<li>
Design capabilities to find solutions to less common and more complex system problems.</li>
<li>
Understanding of system administration concepts for operating systems such as IOS, Android, Windows OS, and other mobile platforms.</li>
<li>
Deliver technical training to educate others or meet customer needs.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Cloud-based knowledge management technologies and concepts related to security, governance, procurement, and administration.</li>
</ul>
<b>
This position is located in Blue Bell, PA or Austin, TX</b><br>
<br>
<b>Required Professional and Technical Expertise</b><br>
We believe you are a good fit for this role if you are someone that can identify the solutions to niche system problems and are adaptable in the face of changing priorities. From a technical expertise perspective, you will succeed in this job if you have 1 year of experience in:<br>
<ul>
<li>Troubleshooting complex issues primarily related to software or web-based solutions</li>
<li>
Working as an IT administrator and operating within a ticketing system</li>
<li>
At least 1 year of Technical support experience.</li>
<li>
Good understanding of various operating system like Windows (XP, 7 and Server), MAC, iOS, Android, and Blackberry</li>
<li>
Experience in testing and troubleshooting across previously mentioned technical domains.</li>
<li>
Previous experience supporting internal and external customers, with passion and ability to educate and train them on their new MDM solutions.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Preferred Professional and Technical Expertise</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2 Plus years of related experience</li>
<li>
Demonstrated hands-on experience with smartphones, tablets, and laptops in a business environment</li>
<li>
Experience with BigFix a plus</li>
</ul>
