<h2>Demand Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The Demand Planner – Retail, is in charge of planning demand for the Retail customers. They will develop accurate demand forecasts based on statistical modeling, customer input, sales and product management input, and market analysis. They will be a key contributor to improving customer experience through superior product availability. Collaborate with the Product Management and Sales teams on the impact of various sales and marketing plans (New product introductions, Life Cycle Management, promotions and incentives). In addition this position will assist with supply balancing, prioritization, vendor compliance, and daily reporting.
<br>
This position is a key contributor to the success of the Supply Chain organization, driving the “Right Product, Right Place, Right Time” philosophy. The right candidate will have experience in a planning organization, be a strong process owner, and demonstrate the ability to work collaboratively across all functions.
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Produce demand forecasts by SKU by point of distribution
</li>
<li>Develop Key Account Supply Chain Planning support
</li>
<li>Lead the Product Review segment of the S&amp;OP process
</li>
<li>Help drive process and operational improvements for service metrics (turns, on time delivery)
</li>
<li>Monitor inventory performance and service levels to ensure objectives are met
</li>
<li>Maintain accuracy of planning data in ERP and planning systems
</li>
<li>Assist in improving planning processes and systems
</li>
<li>Routinely interact with internal and external customers
</li>
</ul>
