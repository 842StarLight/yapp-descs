<h2>Optometric Technician/Optician</h2>

<p>Our optometry practice is looking for a full time time optometric technician/optician.</p>
<p>We're looking for an always smiling and friendly associate with a true interest in communicating with each patient and who is eager to help them in any way possible. You need to carry yourself professionally while connecting comfortably with each patient who walks in the door. Previous optometry experience is extremely beneficial, however, the skills required to be an optometric technician and optician CAN be taught, . There is an expectation of a very fast learning curve with solid retention. You must also be very comfortable on the computer to learn new software. This multitasking position encompasses many roles in the office and skills such as, front desk, reception, money management, online and phone insurance verification, operating pre-testing equipment, glasses sales and opening/closing the office are some of the duties under this job description. There are many times where multitasking requires you to efficiently handle several responsibilities at once, and to know how to prioritize each task properly while calmly managing each of them successfully is a MUST. The expected hours worked per week will range from 25-40. Since our office is open 6 days per week your availability must be flexible, including most or all Saturdays every month. If you believe you would be a great match for this position, please respond by e-mail with: 1) Cover Letter 2) Resume 3)References</p>
