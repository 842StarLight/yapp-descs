<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details
</b></h2>
<p><b>Manager, Business Development Associates<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>· Are you a strong leader? Do you want to build the future of Salesforce's best in class sales team? Managing a team of Business Development Associates at Salesforce is an opportunity to build your own leadership skills and develop top tier talent. We are seeking motivated, hands-on leaders to join our team.
</p>
<p>· We hire extraordinary leaders that thrive in diverse environments, have a passion for analysis and understand the importance of flawless execution.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Description:
</p>
<p>· This is a spectacular opportunity to develop your skills as a sales development manager and leader at Salesforce. You will work alongside industry leaders to develop our Associate Sales Development Representatives.
</p>
<p>· You will lead a team of BDAs (Business Development Associates), which works strategically with our Business Development (BDR) teams. As a manager, you will be pivotal to developing the foundational skills that will make the BDAs successful in sales at Salesforce. You will be very hands-on in ensuring BDAs meet their competencies, including: the Salesforce Platform, Business Acumen, Professionalism, Selling Skills, and Salesforce Systems and Processes.
</p>
<p>· This people management role has a unique emphasis on operational rigor, execution and optimization<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities:
</p>
<p>· Train &amp; develop BDAs into sales-ready candidates for future Sales Development Representative, Business Development Representative and Account Executive positions
</p>
<p>· Manage and coach to both performance and enablement objectives
</p>
<p>· Motivate Individuals and Team to exceed objectives through coaching, regular broadcast of results, and creative incentives
</p>
<p>· Identify and make recommendations for improvement in the areas of Process, Efficiency, Productivity
</p>
<p>· Regularly report on Team/Individual Results
</p>
<p>· Work closely with recruiting to reach hiring and promotion targets
</p>
<p>· Will be responsible for hiring, developing, and promoting approximately 30 BDAs each year
</p>
<p>· Manage and execute on day-today and systematic operations for the BDA program and BDAs you manage directly<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Required Skills:
</p>
<p>· You have proven experience successfully managing people to achieve performance goals
</p>
<p>· Three to five years of experience in software sales, preferably in CRM or enterprise applications, with a consistent record of overachievement of quota expectations
</p>
<p>· Outstanding communication, organizational and time management skills
</p>
<p>· Strong academic achievement
</p>
<p>· Superior problem solving and project management skills<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Preferred Skills:
</p>
<p>· Bachelor's degree
</p>
