<p><b>Industrial Cleaning Sales Manager - Petro-Chemical industry exp. required</b></p>
<p>HydroChemPSC — Eastern Midwest U.S.</p>
<p>HydroChemPSC is looking for an experienced Industrial Cleaning Sales Manager, with Petro/Chemical Industry experience required. You will have a proven track record. This position offers great pay as well as strong incentives for those who succeed.</p>
<p>HydroChemPSC is the premier industrial cleaning and environmental services company in North America supporting the upstream, downstream and utility sectors. HydroChemPSC offers an exceptional three-pronged safety system, innovative career development platform, committed customer service, deep industry expertise, and advanced technology and automation. We are committed to safety, people, growth, service and performance. We provide the safest, most efficient operational experience for our customers and the most comprehensive training and career development for our employees.</p>
<p>WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR: An outside sales representative that is a seasoned professional who possesses the ability to analyze markets, prioritize targets, initiate an effective strategy to identify key decision makers, set up and facilitate meetings with a focus on becoming a trusted advisor and progressing the sales cycle to meet clients’ need, and closing new business for HydroChemPSC. The ideal candidate will possess excellent communication and organizational skills, maintain a structured, forward looking schedule, and have relevant experience with like clients in the energy and manufacturing sectors in the area of responsibility.</p>
<p>PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Executes marketing plan for assigned clients to ensure success in delivering profitable sales revenue.</li>
<li>Maintains a high standard of professionalism from dress to presentations to market HydrochemPSC brand</li>
<li>Communicates to management by delivering periodic and adhoc management reports indicating sales volume, potential sales, and areas of proposed client base expansion.</li>
<li>Sources new potential customers</li>
<li>Collaborates with existing clients and operations team to generate scope growth opportunities.</li>
<li>Closes sales on a direct basis and in accordance with appropriate procedures.</li>
<li>Analyzes sales trends and market conditions to increase sales.</li>
<li>Reviews market analyses to determine customer needs and volume potential; implement sales strategies to achieve goals.</li>
<li>Analyzes sales statistics to promote sales.</li>
<li>Delivers sales presentations to clients in coordination with sales and operations representatives.</li>
<li>Develops relationships with existing and prospective clients.</li>
<li>Provide timely estimates and complex bid responses with a high degree of quality and accuracy.</li>
<li>Effectively uses and routinely updates computer-based client management software.</li>
</ul>
