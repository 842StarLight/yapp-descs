<h2>Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Who We Are</b></p>
<p>
StemExpress is a leading biospecimen provider helping the global research community accelerate the pace of disease prevention and cure discovery.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
We are searching for a Sales Coordinator to support our VP of Sales and Marketing. This individual would work directly with our Sales Team to help improve companywide communication and optimize our existing sales workflow.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who You Are</b></p>
<p>
A highly detail-oriented individual who enjoys working in a fast-paced environment, has an analytical mind, and is always thinking a few steps ahead. You are a college graduate with a bachelor's degree in Business Administration or related field and have 3-5 years of practical experience supporting a sales team or sales executive. In your past work experiences, you have gained a solid understanding of B2B sales and become proficient in SalesForce management or similar CRM software. You pride yourself on having superb communication skills and can comfortably and confidently speak with both internal and external stakeholders. You have a proven track record of successful project management skills and find that you naturally conduct all of your business with a friendly and professional disposition. Lastly, you are very trustworthy and able to exhibit a high degree of integrity and confidentiality when needed. Most of all, you enjoy collaborating with your peers to improve current processes and are looking for company you can grow and develop your talents with.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Specifics</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Report to the sales leadership team and provide administrative support including calendar management, document and presentation creation, as well as phone and sales email management.</li>
<li>Prepare sales reports to track, analyze, and measure key metrics and KPI's for the sales leadership team.
</li>
<li>Monitor our CRM and Enterprise Resource Management Systems to ensure accuracy and thoroughness.</li>
<li>Review NDA's, contracts, and other documents for discrepancies that my need to be brought to our Executive Team's attention.
</li>
<li>Engage in light market research on pricing, services, and products throughout the industry to support company alignment with future market needs.</li>
<li>Ensure all client requests are responded to promptly and have proper expectations of our processes and timelines.</li>
<li>Address any customer complaints and work with sales leadership team to develop solutions.</li>
<li>Partner with internal departments to improve existing processes and work together to resolve complex issues as they relate to our sales workflow.
</li>
<li>Support organizing and training activities for the entire sales team, including preparation for employee onboardings.</li>
<br>
</ul>

</ul>
