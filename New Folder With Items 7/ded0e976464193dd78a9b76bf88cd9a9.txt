<b>PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES</b><br>
Technology &amp; Innovation (T&amp;I) is responsible for SAP’s overall platform and technology development such as SAP Cloud Platform and SAP HANA. In addition, T&amp;I drives the incubation and adoption of intelligent technologies like Analytics, SAP Leonardo Machine Learning and IoT across SAP’s product portfolio. The T&amp;I board area is also in charge of SAP’s global innovation agenda and builds new business model- and market driven innovations to help customers realize the most business value.</div>
<div>As part of Technology &amp; Innovation, New Ventures &amp; Technologies is tasked to deliver an era of organic growth at SAP through technical innovation and business incubation. We do this through by exploring and developing meaningful new technologies to fuel transformative growth at and building great products with an insanely great team. With more than €20 billion in annual sales, SAP manages an enormous diversity of businesses. However, moving the needle inside of SAP’s dominant enterprise business requires adding more than incremental innovation – it requires the development of massively disruptive organizations.</div>
<div><b>EXPECTATIONS AND TASKS</b></div>
<div>As Senior Operations Specialist for Strategic Planning and Operations you will have the great opportunity to:</div>
<ul>
<li>Manage and drive financial performance for New Ventures &amp; Technologies (NVT) functions</li>
<li>Focus on leveraging tools and systems in order to ensure transparency for the steering of the NVT in close partnership with Controlling and the T&amp;I Board Area</li>
<li>Close cooperation with operations team members in NVT and other business units</li>
<li>Actively leading various activities to ensure overall financial performance, like quarter end close, financial forecast and modeling, budgeting as well as latest estimates.</li>
<li>Safeguarding financial compliance</li>
<li>Act as role model for using our own SAP products as catalyst for increased efficiency</li>
<li>Create, write and communicate briefings, presentations and memos for senior management and interpret, analyze and evaluate business plans and operating/financial plans</li>
<li>Drive standardization of planning, reporting and definitions and rolling out specific processes across all levels of the organization</li>
<li>Be responsible for other strategic initiatives and project documentation, as assigned</li>
</ul>
