<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2>

<p><b>Logiwa</b> is a Chicago-based B2B SaaS company with R&amp;D operations overseas. We provide cloud-based</p>
<p>
warehouse and inventory management (WMS) software for small-to-medium sized “New Age” B2C</p>
<p>
fulfillment businesses. As we are growing rapidly with double digit month/month growth, we are looking</p>
<p>
for highly motivated <b>Sales Development Representative.</b></p>
<p>
We are backed by leading North American and European funds including Runway Ventures (NYC),</p>
<p>
Launch Capital(CT), Spider Capital (SF), Otimo Retail (Montreal), Revo Capital (Netherlands).</p>
<p><b>
Job brief</b></p>
<p>
We’re looking for a results-driven <b>Sales Development Representative</b> to actively seek new business</p>
<p>
opportunities, engage and build relationships with potential customers. You will provide complete and</p>
<p>
appropriate solutions for every potential customer to boost top-line revenue growth, customer acquisition</p>
<p>
levels, and profitability.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated success in prospecting both inbound and outbound leads for a B2B SaaS organization</li>
<li>Qualify leads from marketing campaigns as sales opportunities</li>
<li>Present our company to potential prospects</li>
<li>Identify prospect's needs and suggest appropriate products/services</li>
<li>Proactively seek new business opportunities in the market</li>
<li>Generate new sales leads through a mixture of outbound phone and email prospecting, following up on marketing campaigns and inbound inquiries</li>
<li>Work directly with marketing to discover opportunities from leads, and set appointments from those leads</li>
<li>Report to the VP of Sales on sales results</li>
<li>Effective utilization of our CRM (Hubspot) to help drive your day to day activity</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Preferably 1-2 years, minimum experience in sales or client success in a software/tech company</li>
<li> BA/BS Degree required</li>
<li> An understanding of B2B software sales</li>
<li> Affinity for software and knowledge of the software development process</li>
<li> Strong sense of urgency for reaching goals and deliverables while managing multiple requestsconcurrently
</li> <li>Solid understanding of client pain points and business needs, with a solutions-oriented and consultative approach to problem-solving
</li> <li>This is an entry-level Inside Sales position in our Downtown Chicago Office</li>
</ul>
