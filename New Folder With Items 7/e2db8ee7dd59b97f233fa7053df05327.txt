<p><b>
About SCAN</b></p>
<p>
Our mission is simple: Keeping Seniors Healthy and Independent–that’s been our mission for almost 40 years. Rated as one of the top Medicare Advantage plans in California, SCAN Health Plan employees are passionate about what they do. Understanding that success is based on achieving the mission, SCAN employees are afforded with the training and tools necessary to do their jobs and they are rewarded for their efforts and recognized as experts in their fields. Ask anyone who works here and we’ll proudly tell you that SCAN’s commitment has never wavered. We are as steadfast in our pledge to help our members live healthy and independent lives.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
The job</b></p>
<p>
Contribute to the overall success of SCAN through the participation in and support of sales efforts, member retention, community outreach and special events.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
You will</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Build rapport with prospective members at sales presentations/events.</li>
<li>
Present educational or training materials at selected events, with managerial approval.</li>
<li>
Communicate own personal experience as a SCAN member to prospects and newly enrolled members in order to enhance the membership experience.</li>
<li>
Initiate outbound Welcome Calls, Birthday Calls and other membership outreach activities.</li>
<li>
Maintain client confidence and prospect operations by keeping information confidential.</li>
<li>
Support overall success of SCAN by contributing to other programs, as requested.</li>
<li>
Contribute to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Your qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Current SCAN Membership, required.</li>
<li>
Must be proficient with operating a computer, including logging into and maneuvering around multiple systems such as time and attendance, online web conferencing programs, and call center software.</li>
<li>
Substantial experience with computer programs (MS Office Products) and internet browsing.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills including the ability to articulate one’s own personal SCAN membership experience to new members and/or prospects.</li>
<li>
Ability to speak in front of large groups. Public speaking experience, preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently as well as part of a team.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a changing environment.</li>
<li>
Maintain valid driver’s license, automobile insurance and reliable transportation.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
CONDITIONS OF WORK</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
One day per month, attendance (in-person or via GoToMeeting) is required at Long Beach corporate headquarters for participation in group meeting for training, sharing and updates.</li>
<li>
Up to 35% of position responsibilities can include participation at special events in the community that could entail driving up to 50 miles one way and/or standing up to one hour or walking some distance in order to reach the facility from parking location.</li>
<li>
Light to moderate use of computer equipment and moderate to heavy use of phone equipment. Up to 50% of position duties require sitting and making outbound telephone calls for up to 3 hours at a time.</li>
</ul>
