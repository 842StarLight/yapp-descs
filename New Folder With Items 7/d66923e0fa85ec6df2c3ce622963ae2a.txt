<h2>Marketing and Social Media Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Be a part of the thrill as we reintroduce the world to the Space Needle. Hearts will race and imaginations will soar on this two-level experience featuring the world’s first and only revolving glass floor and tilting glass walls on the outdoor open-air deck. Step up. Step out. Step into your future.</p>
<p><b>GENERAL POSITION SUMMARY</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>Responsible for developing and implementing the social media strategy for the Space Needle in order to increase our online presence and improve our marketing efforts as well as supporting the Marketing Director in creating, implementing, tracking and optimizing our digital and traditional marketing and event campaigns to increase attendance and awareness of the Space Needle and Chihuly Garden and Glass.</p>
<p>This position supports the Space Needle LLC and Center Art LLC/Chihuly Garden and Glass’ organization by exhibiting the following behaviors: integrity, discretion, professionalism, leadership, humility, respect, accountability, and team building.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop, implement and manage the Space Needle social media strategy</li>
<li>Create and oversee social media content for the Space Needle (including photography and video content)</li>
<li>Define KPI’s and measure the success of Space Needle social media campaigns</li>
<li>Write and keep updated a company social media standards SOP</li>
<li>Stay up to date with the latest social media best practices and technologies</li>
<li>Develop and manage the email strategy (currently HubSpot)</li>
<li>Develop multi-media campaigns for event sales or seasonal programs</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal and external clients to create positive communication and strong relationships</li>
<li>Coordinate and implement marketing events</li>
<li>Work and oversee events as needed</li>
<li>Back-up Creative &amp; PR Teams as needed</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE AND SKILLS REQUIRED</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Experience in marketing/advertising/hospitality/tourism preferred</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office, particularly Word, Excel, PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Experience in graphic design &amp; video</li>
<li>Can work in a fast-paced/urgent office environment</li>
<li>Ablilty to work occasional evenings or weekends as requested</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
<li>Self-motivated, creative and team oriented</li>
<li>Conscientious in quality and guest service</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Able to lift and carry 35 pounds and stand for multiple hours at events.</li>
</ul>
