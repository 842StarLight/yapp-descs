<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the team</b></h2>
We are a multidisciplinary team that includes instructional design, training delivery, communications, and quality analysis. As members of the Sales Training and Communication team we act as leaders with vision within Zillow. We develop scalable and flexible training solutions in a rapidly changing sales and support environment.<br>
<br>
If you're looking to grow your career in an environment that allows you to see projects through the entire content development process, work on meaningful initiatives while using the latest technologies, and share new ideas, this could be the dream role for you!<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About the role</b></h2>
<p>
In this role, you'll have the chance to create high-quality, comprehensive instructor-led training, eLearning, and blended learning solutions. Instructional Designers are key contributors within the sales organization, crafting and developing effective content in support of multiple proficiencies including sales skills, business systems, industry knowledge, products, services, and leadership development.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Our Instructional Designers collaborate with support and sales managers across multiple offices to develop new competencies and build high-visibility training content.</p>
<p>
You'll use the following technologies to design and deliver content to learners:</p>
<ul>
<li>
MS Office applications including PowerPoint and Word</li>
<li>
Articulate Storyline and Articulate 360</li>
<li>
Camtasia</li>
<li>
Learning Management System</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who you are</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
2 to 5 years of experience in training, content development, or instructional design</li>
<li>
Broad subject matter expertise in instructional design methodologies and adult-learning best practices</li>
<li>
Design and develop innovating learning solutions</li>
<li>
Able to analyze training needs and recommend the right solutions for complex business solutions</li>
<li>
Work closely with subject matter experts and stakeholders to identify target audience’s training needs</li>
<li>
Develop observable, measurable and relevant learning objectives</li>
<li>
Possess general Learning Management System knowledge; Publish SCORM or Tin Can-compliant courses in a LMS</li>
<li>
Measure the results of training using Kirkpatrick’s 4 levels of learning evaluation</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Business, Instructional Design, Adult Learning, or a related field</li>
<li>
Ability to travel to multiple training sites as needed (approximately 10%)</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Technical skills and attributes</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Authoritative knowledge of multiple types of software including Articulate Storyline (or similar rapid eLearning developer tools), Microsoft Office, and Camtasia (or similar video editing software)</li>
<li>
Proficient at publishing and tracking SCORM or Tin Can compliant eLearning courses in a Learning Management System</li>
<li>
Strong project management skills and able to prioritize multiple projects in a highly organized way</li>
<li>
Strong written communications and technical writing skills</li>
<li>
Strong technical and troubleshooting skills</li>
</ul>
