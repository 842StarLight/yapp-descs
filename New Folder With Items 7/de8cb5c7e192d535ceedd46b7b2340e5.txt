<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND</b> <b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b> <i>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</i>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Leads the local team to manage the safety/compliance performance, packaged gas sales, welding products and hard goods sales, cylinder production and distribution (if applicable), inventory, support on A/R and back office operations.</li>
<li>
Provides leadership to deliver a high level of personal and team safety performance and regulatory compliance.</li>
<li>
Leads the Safety Culture to a performance goal of zero accidents, zero incidents.</li>
<li>
Supports the direct sales and leads retail and inside sales efforts to deliver profitable growth of the Area business.</li>
<li>
Optimizes profitability of the Area business by actively managing controllable expenses, local inventory and development of continuous improvement projects to generate hard cost savings/profit improvement as a function of War on Waste.</li>
<li>
Develops area local sales strategies in support of Regional, Zone, and Division strategies; e.g. Hard goods, Private Label, Shielding Gases, Spec Gases, Etcâ€¦</li>
<li>
Supports sales representatives in customer activities, new business acquisition, contract negotiations and customer service requirements.</li>
<li>
Coordinates and manages communications in the site and between Area, Region and Zone Management.</li>
<li>
Reports area activity in a timely manner to MTG management.</li>
<li>
Accountable for reconciliation of cash, gas and hard good inventory, customer owned cylinder balances and if applicable gallons delivered vs. gallons sold.</li>
<li>
Shares accountability for collections results.</li>
<li>
Plans for succession and develops employees for advancement within the company.</li>
<li>
Participates in semi-annual inventories.</li>
<li>
Responsible for reconciliation of cash, gas and hard-goods inventory, customer owned cylinder balances and as appropriate propane (e.g. gallons delivered from vendor vs. gallons sold) as well as daily close out location procedures</li>
<li>
Perform other project and duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
