<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Senior Analyst of Business Planning reports to the Senior Manager of Business Planning and will be responsible for the sales, marketing and supply chain analysis required to understand, predict, and enhance mobile phone sales and availability at Samsung’s second largest account. This person oversees marketing budget, sales performance analysis, contracts administration and associated recommendations which support mobile planning, operations, and customer demand.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Role and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work directly with customers to review, recommend/negotiate terms, and redline contract supplements for the upcoming promotions, new product launches, joint business agreements, updates to MPA and NDAs.
</li>
<li>Based on the contract, individual will be required to fully understand financial implications, identify/assess risk and provide invoicing guideline to ensure compliance to the terms.
</li>
<li>Develops relationships with customer personnel across sales operations, buying, and distribution
</li>
<li>Collaborates with marketing and sales teams to optimize marketing development trade spend through review, input and ROI analysis
</li>
<li>Assist with 3-6 month horizon business planning, including sales and marketing analytics, forecasting, inventory management and supply planning
</li>
<li>Accurate and timely analyses of competitive and consumer sales behavior and impact to Samsung sales
</li>
<li>Assist with ad hoc strategic projects, including planning, tracking, reporting, and documentation of all aspects of the projects
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor's degree in business management, finance or accounting is preferred, and at 4+ years of equivalent work experience in marketing, contracts, analytics, business planning, or finance
</li>
<li>Superior Excel modeling skills required, including but not limited to VLOOKUP and PIVOT.
</li>
<li>Ideal candidate will have at least 2-4 years’ experience in the telecommunications industry, particularly wireless preferred but not required
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills required (both written and verbal)
</li>
<li>The ability to develop and maintain excellent working relationships with all appropriate levels within and outside the company.
</li>
<li>Experience using advanced enterprise planning or financial planning systems is highly desired. (SAP, etc.)
</li>
<li>Read and interpret sales and marketing documents, invoices, contracts or related document
</li>
<li>Proven success in a highly dynamic, deadline-driven environment.
</li>
<li>Should have advanced MS Excel and MS PowerPoint knowledge and skills.
</li>
<li>Strong communication, both written (PPT presentations) and oral skills.
</li>
<li>The ability to work either independently or in a team environment to achieve personal and team project goals, including the completion of assignments within and exceeding established time frames.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Necessary Skills/Attributes
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate ability to interact at the most senior sales level within customer’s organizations and within the SEA organization. The ability to negotiate with and convince others, including customer leadership, VP’s, directors &amp; managers, staff, and vendors with opposing views to accept/approve plans, technical, and project recommendations.
</li>
<li>The ability to plan, organize, and prioritize multiple business development programs and simultaneous performance objectives
</li>
<li>Ability to make professional sales and business presentations in writing, through emails and reports, or orally, including complex business matters to an audience of high skills, management, and operational experience
</li>
<li>Ability to direct sales leadership to achieve customer contracted objectives and team goals and to achieve results within established time frames and requirements.
</li>
</ul>
