<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
The primary responsibilities of this position are:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible and accountable for implementing, training and operating of revenue management function for real estate portfolio (approximately 230 properties) to optimize revenue.</li>
<li>
Envision, create, collaborate, document, train, and implement the entire traffic tracking, unit/resident status (in Yardi), competitor updates, and rental rate decision process.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with property management team to achieve overall objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with property management team to ensure consistent application of revenue optimization and pricing policies throughout the portfolio.</li>
<li>
Lead regular pricing call with all properties.</li>
<li>
Collaborate/influence overall strategy and pricing decisions with property management team:<ul>
<li>
Evaluate historical, current, and projected supply and demand.</li>
<li>
Assess comparable properties when applicable.</li>
<li>
Revise/update Yardi and Yardi reporting to ensure data integrity, as applicable.</li>
<li>
Set optimization parameters.</li>
<li>
Monitor compliance with policies and decisions.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Provide reports and/or presentations to members of management team.</li>
<li>
Evaluate/recommend, contract for, and implement software, if applicable.<ul><li>
Ensure all information reporting systems are accurate and up-to-date.</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Provide in-house training related to revenue management process and software as required.</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and other legal commitments, including compliance department related to Section 42, Section 8, and other financing tools.</li>
<li>
Be responsive to requests or questions about revenue management functions.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
