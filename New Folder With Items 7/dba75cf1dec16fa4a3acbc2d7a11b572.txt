<h2>Lead Salesforce Developer / Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">ComResource is looking for a Lead Salesforce Developer / Analyst.
<br><br>
The Lead Salesforce Developer / Analyst works in the Information Technology organization and is responsible for the development and support of software applications. This person works on a team (usually) or alone (occasionally) as a high-level technical expert in designing, developing, testing, debugging, and supporting new software or enhancements to existing software in support of a very complex business model. They play a key role in mentoring the software development staff and communicating with the business community and offshore development resources. This candidate should have a deep technical knowledge and a thorough understanding of the business application of software solutions.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Software analysis and design activities for new software or enhancements to existing software. Coding of new software applications and/or the modification of existing software applications. This will entail the review and understanding of project scope, requirements, project design and the I.T development life cycle. The D/A, Lead will also be required to perform code reviews and provide high level estimates on with limited information supporting the project governance process.</li>
<li>Software development activities for new software or enhancements to existing software. Support (issue resolution) of existing software applications. Any newly created or modified applications will require support time to correct software bugs. Must respond quickly to critical issues.</li>
<li>Support of new and existing software. Analysis (scope and requirements) and Design. The D/A, Lead will also have involvement with requirements gathering, documenting requirements and design creation. This may include leading (guiding and mentoring) a team of Developers in the design phase.</li>
<li>Interaction//communication with business users and off shore resources. The D/A, Lead will be required to communicate with business users regarding requirements, design reviews and prototype presentations, etc. Communication to lead and support offshore development resources will also be required.</li>
<li>Will provide technical and process coaching//mentoring of staff members including off shore resources. The D/A, Lead will have the responsibility of assisting other team members in the understanding of processes and technologies. This position may or may not manage employees based on location of incumbent</li>
<li>When executing projects with offshore resources (but not limited to offshore projects), time may be split 60/40 or 70/30 between technical leadership vs. actual coding.</li>
<li>Will provide technical design for projects. This is a major requirement when working with offshore resources.</li>
<li>Will provide technical assistance with environment setup/configuration.</li>
<li>Will provide guidance regarding technical -related processes such as development builds, testing, etc.</li>
<li>Will be the liaison for information/direction regarding process or technical changes within the organization (i.e.: build procedures, coding standards, etc.)</li>
<li>Will review prototypes</li>
<li>Will perform code reviews</li>
<li>Will estimate project work and will review project estimates of team members</li>
<li>Will perform design and estimation activities for pre-governance TSR work</li>
<li>Will assist the Project Manager in finalizing project schedule</li>
<li>May be involved with several projects at one time within regards to design and support</li>
<li>Will develop technical direction for the project and ensure technical quality of the code delivered</li>
<li>Responsible for providing day-to-day end-user support (maintaining and improving workflows, email alerts, custom objects, complex formula fields, page layouts, process builders, flows, lead assignment rules, configuring communities, creating sharing rules and troubleshooting bugs)</li>
<li>Create and maintain documentation on business processes, system configuration and training materials</li>
</ul>
