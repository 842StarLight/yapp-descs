<h2>Junior Transformation Strategist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Are you a passionate, outcome-driven team player that likes to be working on cutting-edge strategy and innovation for Continuous Testing? Look no further! Tricentis is looking to strengthen team Insomnia with another player.</p>
<p>
Together with a global team of experts, your responsibility is to mature the customer lifecycle, ecosystem and methodologies Tricentis offers their customers and the community to achieve Quality at Speed. You will be creating Continuous Testing strategies and tools that help accelerate the adoption of Continuous Testing in the industry. You act as an evangelist internally, at customers and on conferences to enthuse others, to remove resistance and to gain traction. You, together with your team, will be the face of many strategic initiatives that expand the ecosystem and will be working closely together with – and not limited to - Sales, Customer Experience, Product, Academy, Partner Enablement and Marketing to drive joint outcomes, while fostering collaboration to achieve the best outcome possible.</p>
<p>
Team Insomnia has Insomniacs across the world – in Vienna, San Francisco and Sydney. We’re looking for an addition to our team in the New Jersey area.</p>
<p>
Tricentis has an open, collaborative culture – we act as one team. We care about you and respect you as an individual and we do everything we can to give back to the community. Trust and accountability are double sized, and we empower you to become your best self.</p>
<p><b>
Your Role</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Be a member of Team Insomnia – lead strategic initiatives, mature the customer lifecycle and enhance the ecosystem</li>
<li>
Work closely with customers and other departments to identify and prioritize key value drivers and implement solutions and improvements that help customers achieve their strategic objectives</li>
<li>
Create and enhance strategies and tools that lead to further adoption of Continuous Testing in the industry</li>
<li>
Explain how Continuous Testing fits into the DevOps toolchain and CI/CD pipelines through blogs, videos, webinars and conference talks</li>
<li>
Lead customers through their Digital Transformation journey and put quality on their agendas</li>
<li>
Identify, collect and act upon industry trends and movements to continuously evolve our models and messages</li>
<li>
Expand the ecosystem through engagement and listening to the testing community forming strategic partnerships</li>
<li>
Create and deliver community-level to executive level presentations on Digital Transformation and Continuous Testing</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other departments to provide tactical support in targeted opportunities and to expand the footprint within existing accounts.</li>
</ul>
</div>
