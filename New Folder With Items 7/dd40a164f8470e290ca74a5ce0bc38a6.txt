<h2>Dealer Relationship Manager- Northern California</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>This position will cover the Northern California region including San Francisco, Oakland and San Jose</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for activating new dealership enrollments with franchised auto dealers. Maintains relationships with existing dealerships, provides training on credit program,</p>
<p>
solicits contracts for qualified applicants and assists in resolving funding concerns.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul><li>Compiles lists of prospective dealerships and schedules visits to stimulate interest in the company's auto finance programs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Works with the credit and funding department to meet established volume objectives.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conducts training on FIFS credit guidelines with the dealership's finance manager to effectively maximize production.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicates frequently with the Area Training Manager and keeps him/her abreast of contract production.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepares log of dealer visits and submits expense reports timely to manager.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Works with loan officer and funder to assist dealer in resolving problems with contracts received in house.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyze data to identify challenges and trends within assigned markets.</li></ul>
<ul><li>This position involves regular access to specified personal information of consumers, including, but not limited to, Social Security numbers and dates of birth.</li></ul>

Education and/or Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li> The knowledge, skills and abilities are typically acquired through the completion of a high school diploma or a general education degree (GED); five to seven years successful experience as a Dealer Relationship Manager for an indirect subprime finance company in a successful production environment.</li>
<li> Proven retail automotive experience, preferably as a sub-prime finance manager, finance director or sales manager.</li>
<li> Language Skills Ability to read and comprehend simple instructions, short correspondence, and memos.</li>
<li> Ability to write simple correspondence.</li>
<li> Ability to effectively present information in one-on-one and small group situations to dealers, and other team members.</li>
<li> Mathematical Skills</li>
<li> Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, interest, commissions and percentages.</li>
<li> Reasoning Ability</li>
