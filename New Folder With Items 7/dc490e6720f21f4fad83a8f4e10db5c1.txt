<h2>LDP Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>JOB SUMMARY
</b></div>
<div>Textron's Leadership Development Program (LDP) provides early career professionals a mix of challenging work experiences with multi-industry movement, mentoring, access to leaders and training. Over the course of two years, our participants build valuable skills and networks at multiple Textron businesses. Our goal is to accelerate our participant’s professional development to create high-quality talent to fill key roles in the organization.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>We’re looking for a new Program Manager to oversee the delivery of 1-3 LDP functions (Engineering, Supply Chain, Finance, IT, Marketing &amp; Sales, Communications, and/or HR), bring skills in project management and a desire to work to continuously improve the LDP to keep our program best in class. If you are someone who can develop strong working relationships across the organization, work collaboratively with colleagues, and be the driver of positive change within your team, this may be the role for you!<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>You can expect to put your past experiences to work within an agile, collaborative and creative environment. You’ll be responsible for the recruitment, placement and development of our program participants and will do this by partnering with enterprise top-level executives and key business and HR partners at each of the Textron business units.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Major responsibilities and initiatives include</b>:
</div>
<ul>
<li>Partnering with <b>functional leadership</b> and <b>Talent Acquisition</b> to provide <b>guidance on recruiting strategy</b> and <b>support of recruiting efforts</b> – this can include partnership in the creation of recruiting materials, engagement with Textron interns to drive improvements in intern to full-time conversion, LDP candidate management through Textron’s applicant management system and participation in on-campus recruiting activities
</li>
<li>Collaboration with <b>functional leadership</b> and <b>business units</b> to support <b>LDP workforce planning</b> and <b>participant placement</b> for incoming and current participants, as well as for program completion</li>
<li>
Partnering with <b>business unit management</b> on <b>LDP assignments</b>, <b>participant performance</b> and <b>training &amp; development needs</b> and providing <b>support for</b> <b>employee relation issues</b>, escalating to appropriate parties as necessary</li>
<li>
<b>
Mentoring</b> and <b>coaching</b> <b>LDP participants</b>
</li>
<li>
Creating and delivering <b>regular program updates</b> to <b>business</b> and <b>functional leadership</b> on current and future state
</li>
<li>Planning and execution of <b>LDP function-specific curricula</b> and related <b>LDP face to face meetings</b>
</li>
<li>
<b>Continuous improvement</b> of <b>standard processes for LDP</b>, <b>leadership</b> in <b>special projects</b> and <b>backup support</b> of <b>other LDP functions</b>, as needed</li>
<li>
Protect and maintain the privacy of employee personnel records including, but not limited to, personal and compensation information</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<div><b>
More about you:</b></div>
<div>You’ll need to have a <b>bachelor’s degree</b> in a related field (ideally one of the functional areas for LDP) and a history of effectiveness in <b>partnering well with leadership</b> and other key stakeholders. These things will lead to you being able to sustain an environment of quality program execution, best practice sharing and excellence. We’ll expect you to be a <b>critical thinker</b> with success in <b>managing multiple challenges and prioritizing projects.</b> Additionally, we’ll look to you to bring your <b>passion</b> for bringing out the best in people and desire to help <b>early career professionals</b> develop essential skills for the future through your <b>mentorship.</b> We hope you’ll also come with previous experience in recruiting, have knowledge of HR practices and have had some experiences managing others.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>More about our team:</b><br>
You will spend time with a diverse group of people who are just as motivated, talented, and creative as you are. We take pride in contributing to the success of Textron by infusing our business with early career talent that make valuable contributions to Textron every day.</div>
