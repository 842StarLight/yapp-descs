<h2>eBranch Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Overview</b></p>
<p>Delivers welcoming service by electronic means and phone as a representative of the credit union through positive communication skills. Provides professional service and sales of deposit products, loan products, and convenient services through effective interpersonal skills and proficient knowledge. Responsible for meeting established Credit Union and Branch goals through active participation in sales programs. Accurately and efficiently processes a wide variety of financial inquiries, account openings, loan originations, loan closings, service requests, and transactions. Balances transactions and completes necessary reports. Work in a constantly changing technology environment that requires flexibility, creative thinking, and problem-solving skills.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions, Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Delivers friendly, helpful phone service through:
<ul>
<li>Preparing for daily interactions with a professional image and an organized workstation.</li>
<li>Maintaining proficient knowledge of TruStone products and processes.</li>
<li>Taking responsibility for waiting emails and calls in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Greeting, talking, and emailing with members using a smile, pleasant tone, professional language, and full attention.</li>
<li>Understanding the member?s objectives by asking appropriate questions and listening to the member?s responses.</li>
<li>Identifying and recommending the best solution to strengthen the member?s financial well-being, including transferring the member to the appropriate staff to fulfill their needs.</li>
<li>Processing the transaction accurately and efficiently.</li>
<li>Closing the email string or call by confirming member satisfaction and thanking the member.</li>
<li>Following up with the member, as appropriate.</li>
<li>Researching member?s questions, finding the correct answer, and responding in a timely manner.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Verifies and processes requested electronic loan applications through online channels. Personally notifies approved members of loan status, completes loan details, and schedules loan closings when applicable. Follows-up with members regarding outstanding loan pre-approvals to ensure maximum number of loan closings.</li>
<li>Answers member e-mails promptly, secured messages and chat sessions accurately, and in a professional manner while maintaining a high level of security for both the member and TruStone Financial. Personally calls or refers members to appropriate departments to complete requests that cannot be securely completed via e-mail.</li>
<li>Verifies and processes new membership applications and new account applications submitted online.</li>
<li>Promotes all TruStone products and services with an emphasis on TruStone?s online services to both members and staff.</li>
<li>Routinely supports the Contact Center call queue as needed.</li>
<li>Troubleshoots problems encountered by members or staff using any TruStone online service by using knowledge of computer operating systems, browsers and third party vendors. Alleviates member and employee concerns with online services and identifies solutions for satisfaction and understanding.</li>
<li>Participates in technology-related projects. Creates new processes to implement new technology into everyday business.</li>
<li>Secures the credit union?s assets by handling negotiable instruments in a consistent manner, properly identifying members, keeping member information confidential, balancing at the end of each business day, and completing necessary reports that summarize daily activities.</li>
<li>Recognizes member needs for advanced products such as business, in depth IRA, investment, and mortgage products and seamlessly refers members to appropriate staff.</li>
<li>Demonstrates a work style that is consistent with TruStone?s values and takes personal responsibility for own learning and development to support those values and the Contact Center?s success.</li>
<li>Maintains up to date knowledge and adheres to all regulations, policies, procedures, and standards that pertain to this position.</li>
<li>Cross-sell the Credit Union?s products and services. Promote business for the Credit Union by maintaining good member relations and referring members to appropriate staff for new services.</li>
<li>Achieves individual and branch sales goals through new business sales, referrals and retention of account relationships.</li>
<li>Completes referrals, sales leads and service events in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Responsible for regular and reliable attendance at the workplace.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
