<h2>Medical Device Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Company Description: </b></p>
<p>For over 60 years Winco has been a leader in the manufacturing of furnishings. Attention to detail and quality continue to be the cornerstones on which we design and manufacture all of our products.</p>
<p><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<p>We are looking for a Product Marketing Manager to join our team! This position is located in Ocala, Florida. We are open to offering relocation for the right candidate.</p>
<p><b>Product Marketing Manager Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Direct and perform all aspects of product development, product changes and product promotions, including development procedures, specifying components, packaging and header design, product positioning, pricing, catalog and sell sheet copy, training aides and point of purchase merchandisers.</li>
<li>Manage and communicate sales information and product changes both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Represent the sales &amp; marketing department in group meetings.</li>
<li>Regularly perform pricing and competitive analysis by customer channel and recommend strategies to improve market position, improve quality or reduce costs.</li>
<li>Produce, maintain and update annual forecast. Develop and maintain product database with product specifications, artwork and catalog copy for dealer co-op advertising and catalogs programs.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain relationships with vendors to help facilitate the timely completion of projects.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Product Marketing Manager Desired Education/Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven work experience in product management (in the Medical Furniture industry a plus)</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Life Sciences preferred but similar field experience may be considered.</li>
<li>5 years’ experience in product management or marketing. Strong marketing &amp; market analysis skills.</li>
<li>Familiarity with upstream/downstream marketing a plus.</li>
</ul>
