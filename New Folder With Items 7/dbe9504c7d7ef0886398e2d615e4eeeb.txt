Main Purpose of Job:<br>
<ul><li>SUPPORT CATEGORY MANAGERS (CM) IN THE ACTIVATION OF THE CUSTOMER CATEGORY STRATEGIC PLAN – a primary responsibility of this position is to partner with the CM in the development of Strategic Category plans in collaboration with the customer, internal insights and research resources. Additionally, this position will know how to utilize and use technology that will enable efficient modular processes and analysis. Assist in activating category strategies by using the tools, insights, and analytics from CCSD, Enable Hub, and retailer-specific data. Applies a strong focus on category assortment and merchandising to drive category growth.</li></ul>
