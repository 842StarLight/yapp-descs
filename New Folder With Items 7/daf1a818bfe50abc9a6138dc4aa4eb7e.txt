<div><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>The Mid-Market Account Executive is results oriented with strong communication skills. This position is primarily responsible generating new business with SMB and mid-market prospects. These responsibilities include identifying prospects, building a qualified sales pipeline using both outbound and inbound leads and closing business against quota. The Mid-Market rep will be highly motivated and drive positive prospect experiences that support the company’s overall sales goals with a proven record sales record.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>DUTIES &amp; ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Proactively call and establish relationships with new customers and secure contracts to achieve monthly revenue goals.</li>
<li>Create relevant and targeted lists of prospects through multiple avenues to build a consistent pipeline of new business.</li>
<li>Partner with Business Development Reps to set meetings with target prospective clients, as well as coordinate additional follow-up activities as needed.</li>
<li>Learn and advocate for SentryOne’s full suite of products and services.</li>
<li>Coordinate with marketing team to develop marketing plans that accelerate our customer sales cycle.</li>
<li>Consistently and accurately record all sales activities in Salesforce.com.</li>
<li>Provide forecasts on best case and most likely sales volumes over relevant time periods.</li>
<li>Work with onboarding and delivery teams to proactively address any obstacles with new customers.</li>
<li>Work closely with other departments to ensure completion of the sale and/or other sales operations requirements.</li>
<li>Be a positive representative of the company and its brand in the marketplace.</li>
<li>Attend market and company events that drive business prospecting for your territory</li>
</ul>
<div><b>COMPETENCIES</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Ability to Multitask</b>: Proven ability to manage multiple issues and/or projects successfully bringing them all to resolution in a timely manner.</div>
<div>
<b>Problem Solving: </b>Anticipates problems, recognizes and accurately evaluates the signs of a problems and analyzes current procedures for possible improvements.</div>
<div>
<b>Customer Service</b>: Listens and responds effectively to customer questions; resolves customer problems to the customer’s satisfaction. Respects all internal and external customers and uses a team approach when dealing with customers to exceed customer expectations.</div>
<div>
<b>Detail Orientation</b>: Thoroughness in accomplishing a task through concern for all the areas involved, no matter how small. Monitors and checks work or information and plans and organizes time and resources efficiently.</div>
<div></div>

<li>Experience with working in a CRM environment</li>
<li>Proven ability to achieve and exceed personal quotas and goals</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and accurate documentation</li>
<li>Professional communication skills and ability to connect with IT business leaders</li>

</ul>
