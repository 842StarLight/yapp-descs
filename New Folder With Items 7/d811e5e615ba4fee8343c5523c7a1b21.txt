<h2>Product Management - Sr Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This position is responsible for managing the marketing activities necessary for the new product development, commercialization, product management and commercial support of Dexcom CLARITY diabetes management software and Dexcom Electronic Health Record (EHR) integration product portfolios.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Senior Product Manager in Marketing will have an understanding of the market dynamics of diabetes software management, EHRs in both hospital and stand-alone clinic settings, therapy outcomes and patient management. They will develop commercial strategies for clinic efficiency, therapy adherence and other key workflows that drive preference for Dexcom solutions. They will maintain a working knowledge of competitive offerings and programs, and how such programs are marketed and integrate into HCP systems. This person is expected to demonstrate strong leadership capabilities and will be the marketing champion for defining new products, new markets and strategic trade-offs. As such, this role will work collaboratively with cross functional teams, marketing partners and other key stakeholders. This person is a part of the upstream marketing team and will need to be an effective team player, responsible for leading and contributing to shared team goals and responsibilities.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>(other duties may be assigned)</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify new business opportunities that leverage Dexcom’s core competencies and develop business case &amp; strategy to justify and communicate opportunity to leadership.</li>
<li>Perform market research and develop strategic plan for new business opportunities.</li>
<li>Partner with R&amp;D and Business Development to incubate new ideas into viable business opportunities.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Portfolio Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify &amp; define product portfolio opportunities (e.g. features, benefits, claims, intended use, etc.) to address target market opportunities, add value, and uniquely meet customer needs.</li>
<li>Develop rationale to support new product/business development opportunities</li>
<li>Manage process to align key corporate stakeholders to approved portfolio of products and services.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>New Product Development</p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as commercial lead and cross-functional team partner to provide strategic &amp; product guidance to product development project team; responsible for the execution of marketing deliverables.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Drive the development of marketing requirements, priorities, and plans to achieve corporate objectives for new and extended products and services; represent the “voice of the customer”</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Market Research</p>
<ul>
<li>Plan and conduct market research projects to identify the voice-of-the-customer, customer insights, market trends and needs, new business opportunities, product concept testing and product requirements.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Perform market analyses including market sizing, customer segmentation, competitive &amp; gap analyses, and market opportunity.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Product Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the application of life-cycle business development methodologies and tools, including opportunity identification, qualification, positioning, competitive analysis, lifecycle management and related activities.</li>
<li>Develop business cases and financial impact analyses of potential features/products and prioritize according to overall financial, market, and strategic impact. Communicate priorities clearly along with the reasoning to the cross-functional teams to drive alignment.</li>
<li>Develop marketing strategies to ensure commercial success and achieve corporate objectives as it relates to new products, product line extensions, and partnerships.</li>
<li>Assist with sales and downstream marketing to coordinate commercial readiness, commercialization/launch, and development of field-based sales tactics to ensure commercial success.</li>
<li>Submit invention disclosures and help generate novel ideas, solutions and intellectual property.</li>
<li>Prioritize and manage multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>Responsible for understanding and complying with quality standards and requirements.</li>
<li>Negotiates project deliverables/cost and manages outside agencies and suppliers.</li>
<li>This position may assume and perform other duties.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>No direct supervisory responsibilities at this time. However, the successful candidate will have the capacity to lead others, including the potential for direct supervisory responsibilities in the future.</p>
<br>
<p></p>

</b></p>

The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>

<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>Master's degree or equivalent; Eight (8) or more years related experience and/or training (at least 6 in Marketing or equivalent); or equivalent combination of education and experience.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Engineering, science or marketing degrees desired. Previous experience in Product Management preferred.</li>
<li>Previous experience in biosensors, diabetes, digital health, healthcare technology, consumer electronics, mobile devices and/or medical devices highly desired.</li>
<li>Experience in the diabetes field or in developing and marketing mobile applications and/or cloud-based systems is highly preferred.</li>
<li>Experience defining product requirements and working with R&amp;D to ensure development of successful products based on customer and business requirements.</li>
<li>Must have strong presentation, influence, and communication skills.</li>
<li>Willing to travel up to 25% of the time.</li>
</ul>
