<h2>Senior Forecast Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Core Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Help facilitate &amp; utilize inputs from S&amp;OP process across multiple business units</li>
<li>Individual will assist in production constraint and allocation model forecasting for certain items in portfolio.</li>
<li>Communicates and interacts with customer service, supply planning, sales and finance to inform forecasts at the item/dc level.</li>
<li>Makes recommendations on changes or actions needed to meet actual and forecasted demand</li>
<li>Identifies opportunities to improve the efficiency &amp; effectiveness of forecast planning</li>
<li>Evaluating the impact of key changes, risks, and opportunities and developing scenarios considering trade-offs that drive overall business performance</li>
<li>Reports Key Performance Indicators (eg. forecast accuracy)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Excel is required; familiarity with data modeling is a plus.</li>
<li>Must be comfortable working with computers and be able to learn new software</li>
<li>Must be willing and able to learn and acquire new skills and abilities.</li>
<li>Must have excellent quantitative and analytical skills with the ability to translate analytical skills into high-level analysis. Experience developing forecasts a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to build &amp; understand statistic models for forecasting</li>
<li>Ability to easily model &amp; re-model network data for scenario planning</li>
<li>Requires excellent written and communication skills with the ability to communicate a message well across all levels of the organization. Must be able to not only provide data, but guide decisions independently and recommend solutions to problems and issues. Must be able to guide planning and data gathering meetings to successful resolutions.</li>
<li>Minimum 4-5 years’ experience with forecasting</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Personal Characteristics:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Possess a “can do” attitude and believes anything is possible with the right focus.</li>
<li>Able to work independently and “roll up your sleeves” to make things happen.</li>
<li>High intensity team player who thrives in a fast paced, high intensity, dynamic environment.</li>
<li>Constantly questions the way things are done in a constructive manner and thrives on change that improves results.</li>
<li>Open (no agendas), honest and trustworthy.</li>
<li>Effective leadership and strategic influencing skills.</li>
<li>Results oriented individual with a desire to manage change through to fruition.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work across functions, business units and geographies – a strong background in a matrix organization is beneficial.</li>
<li>Effective business systems thinker and a good understanding of business process knowledge.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to deal effectively with others in a diverse environment.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and manage multiple projects at one time.</li>
<li>Strong stakeholder management focus.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Proficient with personal computers and business applications.</li>
</ul>
