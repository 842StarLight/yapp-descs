<h2>Senior Copywriter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>Responsible for helping to develop concepts and communications for integrated cross channel initiatives. Oversees the Copy team that produces written content for the Company's portfolio of brands: Marciano, G by Guess, Guess Factory and Guesskids. Serves as the Company copy authority and general site editor. Mentors and manages a team of writers and acts as the direct Supervisor for the Jr. Copywriter.<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:
<br>
Develop and write headlines, sub-headlines, calls to action and subject lines for homepages, landing pages, special features, promotions and e-mail blasts that drive sales by compelling the customer to take action. Works closely with Web Merchandisers, Graphic Designers and the Online Creative Director to produce best-in-class websites that connect with customers and make for an inspiring and seamless shopping experience. Partner with various departments including Customer Relations Management (CRM), Marketing, Advertising, Social Media, Visuals, Public Relations (PR) and Design to align with Company-wide goals and provide copy for certain initiatives including: e-mail acquisitions, loyalty program initiatives, events, direct mail campaigns, catalogs, mailers and in-store signages. Apply filters to all written communication to ensure accuracy and brand voice. Contribute to brand development and brand engagement through all written materials.<br>
Compose sales driven copy which combines commerciality with a strong editorial, fashion-friendly voice. Write high volume creative product descriptions for the e-Commerce sites that seamlessly blend editorial and technical information, utilizes Search Engine Optimization (SEO) practices and persuades the customer to purchase. Collaborate with Designers and Buyers as necessary to communicate the most accurate product information.<br>
Inspire strong, shopper-relevant writing and conceptual leadership for copywriting team. Edit and proofread copy to ensure that the message speaks to the targeted audience and is consistent, adheres to the in-house style guide and uses the appropriate brand voice. Correct and revise copy until approved. Maintain organized filing system of key information materials on spelling usage, brand style and other legal specifications and update files on a regular basis. Uphold high standards for proper grammar usage and sentence structure for all written communication. Mentors team of Writers to develop copywriting skills.<br>
Be a strategic thinker, question the standard, continually look for the "why" in the work. Work closely with other creatives on finding and developing the big idea translating the strategy. Develop the language of concept that brings the strategy to life, resonates with targeted audience. Develop familiarity with Piperline products in order to identify features and benefits and ensure copy is thorough, accurate and engaging.<br>
<br>
EDUCATION: Bachelor's Degree<br>
<br>
YEARS OF EXPERIENCE: 6-8 Years</div>
