<h2>Senior Contracts Specialist- Public Sector</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Senior Contracts Specialist, working with the SHI Salesforce and SHI Management, develops, negotiates, and administers contracts for resale of software, hardware and related IT services, cloud agreements for Customers and Agreements with Partners/Vendors. The successful candidate will be responsible for working within the Contracts Team to ensure all such agreements reflect SHI’s acceptable standards, mentoring and guiding other team members, and acts as point of escalation.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include but are not limited to: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Aid in the preparation of contractual provisions and the administration of contract proposals</li>
<li>Review both Customer and Partner/Vendor Agreement terms and conditions to ensure consistency with SHI standards for risk</li>
<li>Interface between the SHI Salesforce and applicable internal departments to develop contractual documents</li>
<li>Negotiation of terms and conditions with customers and partners/vendors</li>
<li>Final Preparation of contract documents and distribution to Customer or Partner/Vendor and applicable internal SHI department/team member(s)</li>
<li>Manage high volume activity while maintaining response SLAs are met and escalating as required</li>
<li>Work closely with Management to enhance and improve Best Practices</li>
<li>Work closely with Management to mentor and train Contract Specialists in the review and negotiation processes, including guidance on SHI processes</li>
</ul>
