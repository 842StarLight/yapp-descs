<h2>Senior Inside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Mitel is a global market leader in business communications, powering more than two billion business connections with our cloud, enterprise and next-gen collaboration applications.<br>
</p>
<p>
With more than 70 million users in nearly 100 countries, Mitel is the only company that wakes up every day exclusively focused on helping customers take their communications from where they are today to where they expect them to be.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Mitel Sales Center is an energetic, success-driven, and team-oriented environment, focused on driving revenue for Mitel. The right candidate will be customer centric, with experience in selling solutions as a service. It’s important for this candidate to be highly responsive, possess the skill set to tailor solutions best suited to meet a customer’s needs, and take control of a deal cycle.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
We’re looking for an experienced sales representative to join our <b>Plano, TX</b> team that thrives in a fast-paced, highly competitive, technology-driven environment. As the gatekeeper of a given territory, you will be responsible for quarterbacking deal cycles, strategically leveraging field resources, and collaborating on how to best drive territory growth.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
The Inside Sales Representative is responsible for maximizing revenues in order to achieve or exceed monthly/quarterly/yearly sales goals through all lead sources.</li>
<li>
As an active participant in an assigned territory, the ISR will participate in coordinated efforts to increase channel mindshare, ensure maximum lead close rate, and optimize the end user experience.</li>
<li>
As a subject matter expert, you maintain thorough knowledge of Mitel products, systems, and services, in order to sell customers on right Mitel solution.</li>
<li>
Effectively deliver demonstration of Mitel solutions to prospects.</li>
<li>
ISR will participate in outbound call campaigns for targeted market or specific account base.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate Salesforce.com discipline in pipeline management, sales activity and forecast accurately.</li>
<li>
Constantly learn and adapt to a highly competitive landscape.</li>
<li>
Keeps up-to-date knowledge of the industry, as well as the competitive posture of the company, and prepares activity and forecast reports as requested.</li>
<li>
Plan and prioritize personal sales activities and prospect contact towards achieving agreed business aims while also managing personal time and productivity.</li>
<li>
Attend training to develop relevant product knowledge, sales techniques, and other skills.</li>
<li>
Position requires minimal business travel</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of technology solution-based selling (UCaaS, SaaS, VoIP, Cloud applications).</li>
<li>
Proven track record of exceeding quotas and goals.</li>
<li>
Experience with consultative sales methodology.</li>
<li>
Skilled in discovery, conducting presentations, online web demos, and capturing deal progress in SFDC.</li>
<li>
Highly responsive individual that thrives in a fast-paced, high-growth, dynamic, and team-oriented environment.</li>
<li>
Excellent written and oral communication skills, selling skills, presentation skills, and time management skills are essential.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to build credibility and clearly articulate a value proposition.</li>
<li>
In-depth experience with Microsoft Office tools and SalesForce.com.</li>
<li>
Creative with business planning and implementing sales initiatives.</li>
<li>
Telecommunications background is preferred but not required.</li>
</ul>
