<p>We’re on a mission. We want manufacturers to succeed. <p>
In support of our ongoing growth, we are looking for a Inside Sales Representative to generate qualified sales leads and build a business pipeline by calling pre-defined prospect lists. This is an important position within our organization, and success at this job may open career opportunities throughout PLEX. This position has a combined salary and commission pay structure. The position is located in our Troy, MI headquarters. Additional responsibilities include the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop viable leads through prospect qualification and analysis</li>
<li>
Manage and track leads in Salesforce database</li>
<li>
Collect and record important prospect information</li>
<li>
Research target companies to identify potential contacts</li>
<li>
Understand prospect business needs and issues</li>
<li>
Understand and communicate Plex Systems’ competitive advantages</li>
<li>
Achieve monthly, quarterly and yearly call volume and qualified lead goals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to learn quickly</li>
<li>
Effective communication skills verbal, written and interpersonal</li>
<li>
Competitive personality, able to handle rejection and move forward</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and able to work with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail and documentation skills</li>
<li>
High energy</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Microsoft Office, including Outlook</li>
<li>
Previous Inside Sales, Lead Generation or Qualified Appointment setting experience preferred</li>
</ul>
