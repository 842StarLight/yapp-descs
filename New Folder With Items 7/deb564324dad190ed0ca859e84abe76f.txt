<h2>Sales Operations Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>The Opportunity:</b><br>
Shutterstock is seeking an enthusiastic Sales Operations Specialist to provide day-to-day operational support for the Global Enterprise Sales team, including: troubleshooting, tracking, prioritizing and escalating reported issues.
<br><br>
Seated in our New York City office and reporting to the Manager, Global Sales Operations, this position will maintain and improve sales operations by monitoring key systems and processes; identifying gaps, resolving issues short term and recommending scalable long term solutions.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Act as a Help Desk resource for the Global Enterprise Sales team</li>
<li>Use strong problem solving skills, interpersonal skills and ability to develop working relationships with the various teams within the company</li>
<li>Analyze present processes and identify improvement and enhancement opportunities</li>
<li>Document process information with the use of process flow diagrams</li>
<li>Test and execute processes to ensure that the right results are achieved</li>
<li>Collate feedback on process performance and use the collected data as the basis for future process improvement projects</li>
<li>Monitor and measure the effectiveness of new processes to ensure consistent value delivery</li>
<li>Communicate complex information clearly and persuasively to create engagement and support across multiple levels within the organization</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor's Degree with 2 - 3 years of sales/service operations experience</li>
<li>A minimum of 1 year of experience using Salesforce</li>
<li>You are a strong communicator with a high degree of analytical skills</li>
<li>Proven experience in business process designs and documentation</li>
<li>Ability to multitask using superior time-management and organization skills</li>
<li>Proven ability to work effectively within all organizational levels and departments to achieve desired results; the ability to work independently and as a member of a team.</li>
<li>You're adaptable to a changing environment</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>JIRA experience</li>
<li>Familiarity with Lucidchart or a similar program</li>
<li>Knowledge of Change and Release Management processes (Agile)</li>
<li>Experience with leading Marketing Automation tools</li>
<li>Experience with Apttus, DocuSign, Distribution Engine, Cirrus, Eloqua, SalesLoft</li>
</ul>
