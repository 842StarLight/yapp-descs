<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work flexible hours, as admission/marketing responsibilities may dictate.</li>
<li>
Two years of bookkeeping experience within a healthcare setting, including, but not limited to: managing accounts receivable, accounts payable, and payroll functions.</li>
<li>
Software proficiency such as Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively with residents, families, staff, vendors and the general public Must have knowledge of billing and collection practices/techniques</li>
<li>
Superior organization skills and attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to multi-task.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Proactively reach out to and respond to inquiries from hospital discharge planners, families and other referral sources.</li>
<li>
Serve as the initial liaison with resident and families on facility services and issues.</li>
<li>
Interview resident or resident's representative to obtain necessary personal and financial data to determine eligibility for admission.</li>
<li>
Conduct marketing and tours of facility for prospective clients.</li>
<li>
Assign accommodations for new admissions related to nursing care needs, resident and resident's families preference, availability of space, and other information</li>
<li>
Ensure all admissions paperwork is completed thoroughly, accurately and on a timely basis.</li>
<li>
Maintain records of admissions and discharges, and compile occupancy census data.</li>
<li>
Develop presentations and events aimed at community education and awareness on facility specialty programs and services.</li>
<li>
Coordinating and managing business office functions, including employee safety, accounts receivable, accounts payable and customer service</li>
<li>
Oversee reception activities.</li>
<li>
Ensure timely and accurate submission of billings to appropriate reimbursement agencies and private payers.</li>
<li>
Set up and maintain financial files for residents: billing, admission papers, state and Medicare reimbursement documents; coordinate and collect accounts receivable and past-due accounts</li>
<li>
Review payment policies and procedures with new admissions and their responsible parties</li>
<li>
Complete receipt records, post deposits, run cash receipts batch listings, research/prepare/post adjustments, call in deposits and enter census,</li>
<li>
Administer and reconcile resident trust accounts and petty cash</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
