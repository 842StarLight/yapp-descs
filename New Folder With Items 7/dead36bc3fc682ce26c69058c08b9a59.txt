<h2>Account Executive, Media Sales (Atlanta)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Soofa is the neighborhood news feed that connects a community with screens everyone can see and anyone can use.</p>
<p>
We need an experienced sales professional to work alongside SMB and regional brands who are looking for a hyperlocal marketing vechicle in Soofa neighborhoods in Atlanta.</p>
<p>
You will provide a consultative approach to every sale and help our clients grow their impact on a local level and enhance their communities.</p>
<p>
Success in this role is measured by hitting and exceeding monthly sales goals, consistently staying ahead of daily metrics, and handling rejection in stride, all while operating in a fast-paced, team-focused environment.</p>
<br>

Responsibilities</b></p>
