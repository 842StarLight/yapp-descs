<h2>Software Customer Support (B2B, SaaS)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Do you pride yourself on delivering best-in-class customer service? Are you a tech-savvy problem solver looking for a position where no two days are ever the same? BuildingLink is looking to hire a full-time Customer Support Associate (SaaS) to be on one of the most highly praised customer support teams in the industry!
</p>
<p>We are a rapidly growing SaaS company, providing an incredibly robust, cross-platform (web, mobile, and desktop) product to nearly 5,000+ properties globally, and a growing customer base of nearly two million property managers and building staff across the globe.</p>
<p>
We’re seeking an ambitious, charismatic, dependable, and versatile candidate who takes tremendous pride in delivering best-in-class customer service. The ideal candidate is a creative problem solver, who will become an expert in all things BuildingLink, and possess a knack for tackling a diverse range of challenges head on.</p>
<p>
This role reports into the Training &amp; Support Team Lead. As the face of the company, you will establish and nurture long-term relationships with customers, from low-tech residents to high-value property managers, offering assistance, guidance, troubleshooting, and creative workarounds.</p>
<p><b>
About the Position</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Taking ownership of customer issues and questions from inquiry to resolution.</li>
<li>Approaching each customer interaction with a high sense of urgency, coupled with immense care, patience, and professionalism.</li>
<li>Delivering a consultative experience to understand and solve for the customers’ pain points.</li>
<li>Escalating complex technical bugs to our development team for investigation and updating customers on developments and fixes throughout the process.</li>
<li>Achieving advanced domain knowledge of our products and applications.</li>
<li>Collaborating closely with various cross-functional teams (Development, Product Management, and Sales) to ensure customer requests are handled in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Demonstrating a willingness to take initiative, proactively volunteering for additional opportunities, such as product ownership, project management, and intra-department initiatives.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
