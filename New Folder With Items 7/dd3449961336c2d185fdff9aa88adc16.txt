<div><div><b>Management</b></div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
Manage the activities and workflow associated with accounting operations to ensure all activities add value and are performed at the lowest possible cost.</div></div>

<div><div>
</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>

<div><div>
</div></div>
<br>

Responsibilities</b></div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
Provides assistance for the monthly preparation of the financial statements, including the preparation of journal entries. Responsible for reporting function of company financial statements. Continue to analyze the financial statement process and the related systems to increase efficiencies in preparation and value added components.</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
Prepares and distributes supplemental reporting for other areas of the company. This also includes analysis of the monthly financial statements.</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
Provides assistance for the monthly capital projections.</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
Performs and manages account analysis and reconciliations to ensure proper accounting and data processing.</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
Work directly with, and provide documentation for, outside auditors at year-end. Coordinate staff to pull data necessary for testing purposes. Make sure all information requested by auditors is provided in a timely manner and accurate manner. Also, manage other state sales tax or other audits.</div></div>
