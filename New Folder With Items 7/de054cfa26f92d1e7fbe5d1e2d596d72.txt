<h2>Front Desk Clerk/ Receptionist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Title</b>: Front Desk Customer Service Representative</p>
<p><b>
Reports to</b>: General Manager</p>
<p><b>
FLSA Status:</b> Non-exempt</p>
<p><b>
Summary</b>: Promotes quality customer service and processes sales and registration transactions. Acts as the first point of contact for potential and current Goldfish customers and as such is responsible for presenting a positive image for the company.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Answers telephones, responds to inquiries, takes messages and screens and directs phone calls in a professional manner.</li>
<li>Processes student registrations using effective sales/customer service techniques.</li>
<li>Schedules and manages birthday/pool parties. Supervises birthday party staff.</li>
<li>Resolves customer concerns/complaints using a professional approach.</li>
<li>Assists clients with purchases of merchandise and vending.</li>
<li>Greets parents and students as they report to the front desk. Checks in students on the attendance tracking system.</li>
<li>Maintains cleanliness of the front desk area, snack shack area, changing areas, restrooms, and observation area.</li>
<li>Monitors snack shack area and provides guidance to snack shack staff.</li>
<li>Checks voicemail and email correspondence and responds in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Updates informational displays with accurate and timely promotions and literature.</li>
<li>Provides occasional administrative support for management personnel to include the General Manager, and the Asst. General Manager.</li>
<li>Makes collection calls to resolve open account problems.</li>
<li>Prepares twice daily student lesson schedules for use by instructors and management staff.</li>
<li>Enforces safety rules and regulations to prevent accidents. Administers first aid when necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education/Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED.</li>
<li>Some college preferred.</li>
<li>Two years previous customer service and/or administrative office experience required.</li>
<li>Intermediate level computer skills required using Word, Excel and other software systems.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Certifications and licenses:</b> CPR, First Aid and AED certification required.</p>
