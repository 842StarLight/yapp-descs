Tasks
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Attach identifying tags to containers or mark them with identifying information.</li>
<li>Read work orders or receive oral instructions to determine work assignments or material or equipment needs.</li>
<li>Move freight, stock, or other materials to and from storage or production areas, loading docks, delivery vehicles, or containers, by hand or using forklifts, or other equipment.</li>
<li>Maintain inventory accuracy including cycle counts</li>
<li>Delivering/kitting scheduled parts to fab and assembly</li>
<li>Assist shipping and receiving as needed</li>
<li>Maintenance of parts and inventory areas</li>
</ul>
