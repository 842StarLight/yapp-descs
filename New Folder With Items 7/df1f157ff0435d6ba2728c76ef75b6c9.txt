<h2>GENERAL MANAGER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p>The General Manager has the overall responsibility for operating a profitable and enjoyable restaurant, where the quality of service and ambience are continually challenged and improved, and where an environment that fosters creativity, free thinking and intelligent decision-making is encouraged. The General Manager is responsible for maintaining an effective program for training and developing the entire restaurant team including FOH and BOH. In addition, the General Manager with the Executive Chef oversees the inventory and ordering of food and supplies and ensures consistent high quality of food preparation and service. The General Manager will:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Build sales</li>
<li>
Build a culture of hospitality and financial responsibility</li>
<li>
Develop managers to develop people</li>
<li>
Be a student of the business</li>
<li>
Set speed and direction</li>
<li>
Promote accountability</li>
<li>
Create an energized environment</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Leadership</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Visionary and inspirational with managers and employees</li>
<li>
Practices empathetic and educated listening</li>
<li>
Prepares and executes great one-on-ones with hourlies</li>
<li>
Runs effective, concise, inspirational line ups consistently</li>
<li>
Runs effective work group meetings</li>
<li>
Runs effective pre-shift meetings</li>
<li>
Is decisive</li>
<li>
Acts and follows through with integrity</li>
<li>
Takes risks and is willing to fail</li>
<li>
Knows when to act and when to stand back</li>
<li>
Sets the culture</li>
<li>
Implements change in the most positive light</li>
<li>
Sees the future and is a student of the business</li>
<li>
Is never satisfied with mediocrity</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Integrity</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Able to make appropriate decisions without personal emotion</li>
<li>
Separates self from hourlies in and out of work</li>
<li>
Runs restaurant as if owner</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Problem Solving/Judgment</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Consistently makes decisions that are in the best interest of guest and employees</li>
<li>
Alert to all aspects of the operation throughout the shift</li>
<li>
Keeps regional manager promptly and fully informed of all issues (i.e. problems, unusual matters of significance and positive events)</li>
<li>
Takes prompt corrective action where necessary or suggests alternative courses of action</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Recruiting &amp; Hiring
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain appropriate staffing levels and remain abreast of any changes which may impact staffing needs</li>
<li>
Recruit and hire a crew of quality employees</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring all employees’ personnel files are completed at time of hire to include; application, interview evaluation, reference check, employee data sheet, federal and state tax forms etc.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Training
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Implement company systems as well as train staff in service, guest relations, safety, use of equipment, etc.</li>
<li>
Work with the Executive Chef to provide on-going training and understanding of menu items and preparation to service staff</li>
<li>
Provide consistent and frequent feedback to staff</li>
<li>
Teach and coach staff on a daily basis</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Coaching, Teaching, and Confronting Incompetence</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Can perform one minute management</li>
<li>
Uses "See it, Say it" approach to coaching on the floor</li>
<li>
Practices "Praise in public, punish in private"</li>
<li>
Holds hourly and management team accountable</li>
<li>
Fosters an environment of continuous learning with new information, testing, and reading</li>
<li>
Confronts incompetence in a mature and timely manner</li>
<li>
Follows up on feedback with confirmation of the change in behavior</li>
<li>
Documents all performance feedback in a timely manner</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Service Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Personally greets the staff as they arrive and takes an interest in them, thereby building loyalty</li>
<li>
Holds service staff accountable</li>
<li>
Confronts incompetence</li>
<li>
Constantly pursues great service while coaching the staff</li>
<li>
Enjoys other restaurants service and learns from the experience</li>
<li>
Promotes an environment of teamwork</li>
<li>
Gives inspiring and informative line ups</li>
<li>
Can write an effective schedule avoiding overtime, while maintaining proper staffing levels</li>
<li>
Actively works the floor looking to improve service, help the staff, and/or speed up the turn times</li>
<li>
Effectively runs the front desk and quotes the wait</li>
<li>
Makes an impact by driving the shift</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Bar Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Can effectively order what is needed</li>
<li>
Manages to appropriate inventory levels</li>
<li>
Ensures that pour cost is managed and controlled</li>
<li>
Manages purveyor relationships appropriately</li>
<li>
Manages service at the bar to ensure it is up to the same standard as the dining room</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Kitchen Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Can have open and intelligent dialog with the chef without intimidation</li>
<li>
Is comfortable doing a mid-shift walk through of the kitchen to validate prep levels and food quality for the dinner shift</li>
<li>
Can place all BOH orders for one week without running out of product</li>
<li>
Can properly staff and schedule the kitchen staff</li>
<li>
Is the strongest expeditor in the building</li>
<li>
Pushes food back and demands perfect execution from the line</li>
<li>
Participates in and follow up on EcoSure and Health Inspections</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Floor Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Capable of running the front door on a busy shift with a wait</li>
<li>
Exudes hospitality at the front door</li>
<li>
Coaches the host team and continuously educates that staff about the restaurant as a whole</li>
<li>
Controls the flow of business to set the team up for a successful shift</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Facilities Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
General monthly maintenance programs are adhered to</li>
<li>
Cleanliness of physical plant is noticeable; regularly scheduled programs are in place</li>
<li>
Knowledgeable of water/sewer, gas, and electric shut off locations</li>
<li>
Fire extinguishers are serviced prior to or upon expiration</li>
<li>
Works with the Building Maintenance/Management Company/Landlord to constantly maintain the back dock, trash areas and front sidewalks as well as any communal trash cans</li>
<li>
All pest control is performed on a regular basis</li>
<li>
All plants and flowers are maintained</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Guest Connection</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Knows and practices the "art" of touching tables</li>
<li>
Uses great guest communication, both verbal and written</li>
<li>
Executes guest recovery and follows up appropriately</li>
<li>
Gives support to staff decisions (i.e.: cutting a guest off)</li>
<li>
Excellent problem solver</li>
<li>
Builds guest loyalty</li>
<li>
Cultivates a regular clientele</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Financial &amp; Profitability</b></p>
<p>
The General Manager is responsible for working with the rest of the management team to maximize sales and optimize profits through the management of food, labor costs and controllable.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Determines staffing needs to ensure guest satisfaction based on current and projected business levels and ensures that scheduling and employee shifts are efficiently managed</li>
<li>
Maintains acceptable levels of inventory for the restaurant operation</li>
<li>
Estimate food and beverage costs.</li>
<li>
Supervises portion control and quantities of preparation to minimize waste</li>
<li>
Estimates food and other miscellaneous needs, place orders with distributors, and schedules the delivery of fresh food and supplies at appropriate times</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring that all financial (invoices, reporting) and personnel/payroll related administrative duties are completed accurately, on time and in accordance with company policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Responsible for achieving forecasted business results, and is involved in budget preparation</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Sales Building / Local Store Marketing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Is aware of the local market business environment</li>
<li>
Participates in area business groups or affiliations</li>
<li>
Knows neighboring businesses and their operators</li>
<li>
Contributes to charities that are tied to our company or your guests</li>
<li>
Joins networks that further our place in the business community</li>
<li>
Rewards repeat guests with VIP treatment</li>
<li>
Participates in media events such as wine tastings and food events, including buy outs</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Presentation Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Represents themselves and their team well</li>
<li>
Represents the company well and always in a positive light</li>
<li>
Presents line ups and meetings effectively</li>
<li>
Job interviews conducted professionally, including follow up</li>
<li>
Does not air out any inappropriate company business with hourly employees or guests</li>
<li>
Does not gossip or spread rumors or initiate drama</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Administrative</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
New hire paperwork, tax forms, PAF’s and E-verify are completed in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Performs all payroll functions perfectly</li>
<li>
Codes invoices in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Utilizes the shift planner and the financial workbook</li>
<li>
Knows the work comp and guest accident procedures</li>
<li>
Is aware of sales and labor budgets and is able to discuss then intelligently at all times</li>

Has great P &amp; L Management skills</li>

Uses labor scheduling tools, including Hot Schedules and Aloha Enterprise to plan labor</li>

<p><br>
Required Experience:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Prior previous restaurant experience required
</li>
<li>Prefer a minimum of three years (or greater) of experience managing in full service dining establishments</li>
<li>
Must have a current ServSafe Certification</li>
<li>
Must have an Alcohol Certification</li>
<li>
Must have taken the Wage and Hour course</li>
<li>
Must have taken the Interview course</li>
<li>
Must have a Harassment Certification</li>
<li>
Has developed at least 4 hourlies into greater roles (host to server, back server to server, etc.)</li>
<li>
Has developed at least 1 hourly into management position (key hourly or sous)</li>
<li>
Has passed knowledge test on all F&amp;B specs, recipes, etc.</li>
<li>
Is proficient in Microsoft Excel, Word, Outlook</li>
<li>
Be able to work in a standing position for long periods of time (up to 8 hours)</li>
<li>
Be able to safely lift &amp; easily maneuver trays of food frequently weighing up to 20-25 pounds</li>
<li>
Be able to communicate clearly &amp; effectively in the predominant language(s) of our guests, managers, kitchen and dining room personnel</li>
<li>
Must have exceptional grooming habits</li>
</ul>
