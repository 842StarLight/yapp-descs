<p><b>We are seeking a Customer Support Representative.</b></p>
<p><b>HOW YOU WILL CONTRIBUTE:</b></p>
<p>We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated individual to join our team, providing customer service and inside sales support to our distributors and Regional Sales Managers. The job responsibilities include addressing customer questions about our product line, receiving telephone orders and inquiries, investigating and correcting billing/shipping errors, preparing sales reports as required and maintaining customer files and documents.</p>
<p><b>WHAT WE OFFER YOU:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A fulfilling career with the ability to contribute to an Industry leader</li>
<li>A comprehensive suite of benefits</li>
<li>Competitive salary commensurate with experience</li>
<li>A generous 401k match</li>
<li>Profit sharing for all full-time employees</li>
</ul>
<p><b>HOW YOU'LL MAKE A DIFFERENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receive telephone calls and answer questions pertaining to inventory availability, order status, ship dates, vehicle applications, distributor location referrals, pricing, invoice inquiries, product inquiries, freight inquiries, promotions and programs, and literature requests.</li>
<li>Provide proper follow-up by returning all customer inquiries promptly.</li>
<li>Process orders received via telephone, fax, e-commerce, e-mail, and postal mail into TCM (ERP System). Solicit sales of new or additional services. Maintain customer files via hard copy and electronic format.</li>
<li>Help manage an effective and cost sensitive freight schedule by working closely with direct supervisor and scheduling coordinator of the Milwaukee and Rockland Customer Support department (where applicable).</li>
<li>Maintain superior customer service by tracking missing orders, answering / correcting complaints concerning billing, service rendered, shipping errors, shipping damage and freight claims, shortages, and returning voice mail messages. Timely and thorough recording of pertinent errors into CARMA database and generation of RMA records when appropriate.</li>
<li>Refer customer concerns of service or product failure to the designated department for investigation.</li>
<li>Gather materials needed to produce (program/promotional) mailings to distributors. Ability to assist/manage/implement other projects as needed with minimal supervision.</li>
</ul>

</ul>

<li>Developing ability to effectively utilize CRM software to conduct searches, answer questions, produce reports, and record relevant customer interactions.</li>
