 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Digital
<br>Engineering Architect - Sales

Cognizant Technology Solutions is seeking a highly qualified

<br>applies them in an orchestrated manner to achieve the goals of the practice. The

<br>the Enterprise Portfolio of applications and make pragmatic judgements

<br>system.

<br>application development.

<br>requests and identify opportunities in line with account strategies.
<br>Good understanding of the value flow across the whole SDLC
