<h2>Director of Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>Due to sustained company growth, we are seeking a Director of Business Development to expand regulatory affairs (RA) consulting services and client development.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<div><ul>
<li>Identify target organizations and prospects for developing new RA consulting services.
</li>
<li>Working with marketing, ensure a consistent, organized approach to prospect outreach
</li>
<li>Qualify prospects and develop effective proposals for contracting and consulting services
</li>
<li>Support proposal development and RFP responses.</li>
<li>Develop relationships with internal stakeholders and collaborate across the organization to identify top RA consulting leads.
</li>
<li>Exhibit strong communications skills to effectively articulate value proposition to clients</li>
<li>Attend pharmaceutical industry events and conferences for networking opportunities</li>
<li>Leverage existing client relationships and services by expanding into additional RA functional areas, including advertising &amp; promotion, labeling and regulatory strategy</li>
<li>Negotiate and close new RA consulting projects working with client procurement personnel and RA executive decision makers
</li>
<li>Work with CEO and business development team to develop new business development strategies and plans annually</li>
<li>Assist with interviewing and the selection process for hiring new RA consultants</li>
<li>Work with a strong sense of urgency and customer satisfaction</li>
<br>
</ul></div>
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Required attributes:
</b></p>
<div><ul>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Highly motivated and driven</li>
<li>Strong work ethic</li>
<li>An outgoing personality and positive outlook</li>
<li>Excellent networking and relationship building skills</li>
<li>Keen attention to detail</li>
<li>Individuals interested in working within a small company, entrepreneurial environment</li>
</ul></div>
<p><b>Required background:</b></p>
<div><ul>
<li>BS/BA degree. Advanced degree a plus.
</li>
<li>8+ years pharmaceutical industry experience. Focus areas may include RA, clinical, scientific, quality, medical, marketing, other.
</li>
<li>5+ years of pharmaceutical RA experience. Preferably w/in regulatory strategy, advertising &amp; promotional compliance or product labeling.
</li>
<li>Prior sales and marketing experience beneficial but not required</li>
<br>
</ul></div>
<p></p>
<p>Opus Regulatory, Inc. offers the benefits of both a dynamic entrepreneurial environment and financial stability. We provide hands-on training, a competitive starting compensation plan, high income earning potential, and a comprehensive benefits plan (see below).
</p>
<div><p><b>Please respond with a resume and cover letter to:</b><br>
humanresources@opusregulatory.com</p></div>
