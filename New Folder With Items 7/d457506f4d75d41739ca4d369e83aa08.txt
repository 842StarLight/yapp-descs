<h2>Associate Director, Consultative Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>IQVIA™ is the leading human data science company focused on helping healthcare clients find unparalleled insights and better solutions for patients. Formed through the merger of IMS Health and Quintiles, IQVIA offers a broad range of solutions that harness the power of healthcare data, domain expertise, transformative technology, and advanced analytics to drive healthcare forward.</p>
<p>IQVIA Technologies, Design and Planning (D&amp;P) provides data, technology and analytics to optimize clinical trial performance and costs. Our products and data assets offer decision support and insights into key operational areas within clinical R&amp;D such as CDP and protocol design in early planning, enrollment planning and management, cost forecasting / budgeting, site selection and trial feasibility.</p>
<p><b>
Role Summary</b>:</p>
<p>
The D&amp;P Consultative Services (CS) team focuses on educating and supporting both potential and existing clients on the value and use of IQVIA Technologies products. The Consultative Services (CS) team are a group of Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) with industry and D&amp;P expertise who support clients in the use and adoption of our technologies and provision of services. As a member of the CS team, the role requires significant trial experience preferably in Trial Management &amp; Operations, which will provide the deep understanding of the unique challenges of our general market and our individual prospects/clients.</p>
<p>
This will enable the potential candidate to successfully:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work in partnership with Sales to assess client’s business gaps and requirements</li>
<li>
Subsequently display and “sell” the value of the IQVIA Technology product suite as it relates to a specific prospect/client’s needs</li>
<li>
Identify and consult on areas of process integration and innovation that can be impacted and/or improved though use of the IQVIA Technology product suite</li>
<li>
Assist clients with creation and implementation of best practices</li>
<li>
Support roll out, product use and adoption, inclusive of product training, adoption plans, stakeholder and user assessment, and change management, within client organizations to achieve desired outcomes</li>
</ul>
<p>The ideal candidate is a well-versed industry leader with strong experience across clinical operations and trial management and a consultative bent. Ideally the candidate will have study management experience and preferable if they have had industry hands-on experience with IQVIA Technologies e.g. Study Optimizer (or similar) to plan for and manage clinical trials; is self-motivated, tenacious, creative and enjoys solving complex problems. Client facing or customer management skills, whether internal to an organization or external facing, is a benefit, to support assessment and understanding client's needs to solve various trial operations related problems regarding the IQVIA Technology product use.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support business growth by delivering product overview and demonstrations to support Sales and Marketing</li>
<li>
Support business growth through identification and delivery of service offerings (study design, feasibility, enrollment planning, site identification)</li>
<li>
Develop content for webinars, articles, and industry presentations as a thought leader in clinical operations such as site selection, enrollment planning, recruitment etc.</li>
<li>
Outline adoption, rollout and training solutions for customers with a focus on impact to process</li>
<li>
With a focus on change management, share and implement best practices to drive client success</li>
<li>
Deliver change management and business process workshops</li>
<li>
Plan, schedule, and manage the development of high-quality, engaging training for each IQVIA Technology D&amp;P product release, including continuous innovation in evolving the training suite</li>
<li>
Deliver training to D&amp;P customers (e.g. large and mid-sized Pharma, Biotech and CROs). Complete custom training and services projects on an as needed basis</li>
<li>
Grow the training suite of products to meet customer needs (e.g., Planning services, Administrator training). Influence and support product development by validating/refining product requirements, challenging/validating UI designs and testing during development with the goal of ensuring features are user-appropriate</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Critical knowledge and skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong hands-on experience of clinical trial operations especially study management and/or enrollment planning at the global, central or country level</li>
<li>
Expert knowledge of D&amp;P tools or industry equivalent especially Study Optimizer or equivalent</li>
<li>
Experience training others or involved in the roll out of enterprise tools/applications and business processes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BSc in scientific field minimum, preferably Life Sciences or Nursing</li>
<li>
Minimum of 8 years life sciences industry experience in a clinical operations role, managing pharmaceutical clinical trials, clinical trials resources, and utilizing project management skills</li>
<li>
Experience in building business cases and consulting in enterprise software implementation</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience defining and implementing an overall product strategy</li>
<li>
Excellent presentation, writing and editing skills especially in a customer facing environment (internal/external) such as client engagement, business case meetings and/or training.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal, communication, presentation, and facilitation skills</li>
<li>
Desire and ability to learn and work with new technologies</li>
<li>
Able to multi-task and meet aggressive deadlines in a dynamic environment</li>
<li>
Detail-oriented, with exceptional follow-through and superior organizational skills</li>
<li>
Strong problem-solving skills; intellectually curious with a desire to learn new skills</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work both independently and as part of a team; experience working as part of a distributed team</li>
<li>
Desire to stay abreast of, develop, and share best practice internally as well as externally to customers</li>
<li>
Willing and able to travel</li>
</ul>
