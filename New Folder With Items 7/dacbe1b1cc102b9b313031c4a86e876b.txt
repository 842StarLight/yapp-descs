<div>
<p><b>About the Company</b></p>
<p>Hutchison Creek Company, North Jersey based Investment Company, is specialized in residential real estate property development across the nation. We are committed to delivering the highest level of service, accessibility, integrity and astounding creativity to our clients. We also dedicate ourselves on behalf of our clients to incomparable commitment to excellence. Most of our properties are located in the Hudson Valley NY area and the corporate office is located in Norwood, NJ.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a skilled and assertive Residential Property Manager. Important traits include: someone who is thorough, committed and knows how to drill down to core issues. The best candidates will be proactive and action plan oriented.</p>
<ul>
<li>Attract tenants by advertising vacancies</li>
<li>Establish appropriate rental rates by conducting market research, determining costs and considering profit goals</li>
<li>Obtain referrals from current tenants; explaining advantages of location and services; showing units.</li>
<li>Review daily calendar and determine what planning is required for the day in terms of tours, move-ins, move-outs, inspection and vendor activity</li>
<li>Maintain property by investigating and resolving tenant complaints; enforcing rules of occupancy; inspecting vacant units and completing repairs; planning renovations; contracting with landscaping and snow removal services</li>
<li>Monitor monthly rental collection and ensure optimal cash flow by setting a date to do collection</li>
<li>Contact tenants to strictly enforce late fees and discuss other issues that require immediate attention</li>
<li>Schedule and oversee maintenance calls in timely manner</li>
<li>Enforce occupancy policies and other procedures by confronting violators</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Proven 3-year work experience as property manager</li>
<li>In depth knowledge of all rules and regulations surrounding property management</li>
<li>Fully understanding property management and its financial aspects</li>
<li>Competency in MS Office and relevant databases and software, such as Word, Outlook, Excel</li>
<li>Excellent communication skill, well-developed customer service skill and sales skills</li>
</ul>
