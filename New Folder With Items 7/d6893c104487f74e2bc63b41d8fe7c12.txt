<h2>Vista Point of Sale Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY
</b></p>
<p>The Vista Point of Sale Specialist (POSS) will act as the subject matter expertise on all things involving Emagine' s POS. This person will provide strategic direction, recommendations, and insights that frame thinking, inform decisions and fuel business growth. The POSS is responsible for understanding Emagine' s business requirements, operations, and industry trends to apply their in-depth knowledge of all current POS technologies that empower them. This person suggests creative and innovative solutions, and leads the development and integration activities to enhance the POS Environment and overall Guest Experience.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for POS platform implementation, integration, governance, and overall management of the Vista POS System and Emagine locations as it relates to POS configuration.</li>
<li>Serve as the primary technical resource and Subject Matter Expert for all POS configuration, including database management.</li>
<li>Continually assess emerging trends and research alternate solutions to make recommendations associated with the development, testing, issue resolution and implementation of POS solutions</li>
<li>Establish and maintain a mapping of POS applications and technologies to the Stakeholder’s capabilities</li>
<li>Direct and oversee all technical functions in relation to Emagine' s POS to ensure efficient operations of the theater and effectively resolve POS related issues.</li>
<li>Recommend, schedule and perform chain wide hardware and software improvements and upgrades to the POS</li>
<li>Direct and oversee project relationships with internal and external stakeholders to remove obstacles in achieving organizational goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Facilitate business improvement by developing collaborative relationships with stakeholders to understand detailed functional and non-functional needs for POS services.</li>
<li>Develop test strategies, plans, scripts to validate the completeness of desired business functional and non-functional requirements; lead functionality testing where necessary, and oversee release planning and execution prior to deployment</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
