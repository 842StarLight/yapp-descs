Your day to day</div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary</b> – <b><i>(Role Summary)</i></b></p>
<p>
This role supports the Americas Development organization through leadership in data management and analysis, as well as assisting with support of departmental digital platforms. Responsibilities include analyzing business opportunities, developing reporting, and maintaining the tools that house both internal and overall lodging industry data to support the company’s Development sales effort. Additionally, this role will assist the Director to design and implement technology solutions, improve business processes, train users, define maintenance processes and communicate effectively about projects and initiatives to internal and external stakeholders.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> – <b><i>(Key Activities)</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Steward the data owned or managed by IHG’s Development team; includes audits and developing reporting to support the Development sales process</li>
<li>
Work with members of the Development organization to define new approaches to analyze both internal and external data to support the sales process</li>
<li>
Fulfill ad hoc reporting requests to support larger IHG organization</li>
<li>
Work with Director on defining more efficient business processes with the use of technology; includes documenting process flows, training materials, and maintenance to ensure processes are being adhered to</li>
<li>
Work with internal stakeholders on new enhancements to existing systems; Facilitate requirement gathering, design and implementation with the support of 3rd party delivery partners.</li>
<li>
Train users, define maintenance processes and communicate effectively about Development Support system projects and initiatives</li>
</ul>

<div>What we need from you</div>
<div>
<p><b>Education</b> –</p>
<p>
Bachelor's degree from a four-year college or university in Business, Finance, Mathematics, Hospitality Management/Administration, or related field required.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Experience</b> –</p>
<ul>
<li>
6+ years experience in technology development, process improvement and project management. Previous experience or knowledge of real estate or hotel development is a strong advantage.</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical and research skills; ability to identify and pro-actively solve problems and skilled at translating data into key insights, operational implications, and recommended next steps.</li>
<li>
Direct experience with Salesforce and Tableau a plus.</li>
<li>
Ability to deal with all levels of the organization effectively.</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal skills for purpose of providing information to clients, vendors and senior management and staff.</li>
<li>
Ability to work to tight deadlines and juggle multiple projects, without sacrificing accuracy.</li>
<li>
Experience working in a franchised business is helpful.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated strong proficiency in Microsoft software such as Excel, Access, Word; relational databases; and understanding of processes to maintain data integrity.</li>
