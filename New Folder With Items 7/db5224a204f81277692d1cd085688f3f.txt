Responsibilities:</div>
<div>
 Define and implement testing strategy and framework including processes, phases, traceability, tools, and quality criteria to be used across simple and complex IT projects. Responsibility for the planning, management and co-ordination of all testing activities and evaluating risk potential during all stages of a project. Coordinate with project managers to set testing expectations and incorporate testing activities into initial project planning. Assist with identifying and documenting testing scope. Work with technical and operational resources to establish appropriate testing scenarios and provide instruction on proper script development. Define defect management, triage, and reporting procedures appropriate to the project. Ensure enterprise IT testing methodology is applied and adhered to consistently across projects. Manage testing application selection, implementation, and ongoing support. Enable test automation. Lead and mentor the testing team and designated testing resources. Other duties as assigned.</div>
