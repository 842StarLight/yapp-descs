You stay up-to-date on new Oil &amp; Gas, Utilities, Chemicals, or Natural Resources trends and technologies so you can share your expertise across any team or project. <p><b>
The work:</b></p>
<ul><li> Understand the ins and outs of Salesforce so you can create the right solution to solve your client’s unique challenge</li></ul>
<ul><li> Contribute new products, workflows and ideas to influence the industry</li></ul>
<ul><li> Identify risks that threaten project success and recommend work-arounds</li></ul>
<ul><li> Get creative designing, prototyping and building Salesforce systems</li></ul>
<ul><li> Keep Salesforce running smoothly by testing and adjusting as needed, then documenting fixes</li></ul>
<ul><li> Be an effective leader, mentor, and champion of your team
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Advise clients on the impacts of SaaS adoption to their governance and operating models, and helping client plan for and/or implement change</li></ul>
<ul><li> Help your clients and team stay up-to-date on industry standards and best practices
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Be ready to build those air miles with frequent weekly travel: Monday-Thursday as needed</li></ul>
