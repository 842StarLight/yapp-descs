<h2>General Manager (Hotel)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Be... an Innovator, a Motivator, a Leader, a Team Player; Most of all, Be Baywood!</p>
<p><i>With our "It's My Pleasure!" philosophy we have become one of the Nation's fastest growing hotel development &amp; management companies. Get to know us at www.baywoodhotels.com and discover why we believe "It's Better at Baywood!"</i></p>
<p>Join us as the <b>General Manager </b>at our <b>Hampton Inn Uvalde</b></p>
<p><b>Experience</b><b>: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2 years experience as a Manager in similar type &amp; size hotel</li>
<li>2 years additional management experience (of which 1 year is in hotels)</li>
<li>Proven track record of increasing profit (GOP) while exceeding Brand &amp; Corporate goals for guest and associate satisfaction</li>
<li>Local market experience preferred</li>
<li>Hilton experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>Responsible for the upkeep and the efficient, profitable operations of the hotel by providing a safe, clean, environment where employees provide, and guests experience, flawless customer service that is legendary throughout the industry.</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Duties</b></p>
<p><b>Hotel Profitability: </b></p>
<p><i>Ensures the attainment of established budgeted goals for all departments of Hotel</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitors compliance with labor standards and staffing guidelines by all departments</li>
<li>Monitors compliance with annually established room rate plan</li>
<li>Monitors operating expense tracking system for all departments</li>
<li>Administers approved incentive programs</li>
<li>Conducts required meetings (ex. Daily Huddle, weekly staff) to ensure interdepartmental communication and coordination of mutual goals</li>
<li>Audits departmental procedures and performance. Modifies procedures as needed.</li>
<li>Monitors rooms inventory and merchandising procedures.</li>
<li>Conducts quarterly rate surveys of competitive hotels and monitors program for competitive analysis and price-value assessment</li>
<li>Monitors and ensures compliance with amenity programs, franchise and company standards, as well as promotional materials</li>
<li>Assures compliance with established Manager On Duty (M.O.D.) Program</li>
<li>Participates in scheduled meetings as required by corporate. This includes revenue management, safety, budget, sales &amp; regional operations meetings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Asset Management: </b></p>
<p><i>Exceeds Brand and/or Baywood quality standards ensuring a favorable franchise inspection grade for the property</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Submits annual Capital and Repair &amp; Maintenance (R&amp;M) budgets for approval by Corporate Office</li>
<li>Ensures completion of all approved Capital and R&amp;M items, coordinating with Regional Operations Manager and Manager of Purchasing</li>
<li>Conducts daily inspections of guest rooms to ensure housekeeping is meeting/exceeding cleanliness standards</li>
<li>Conducts bi-weekly property inspections and approves action plans to include time-table to resolve problems</li>
<li>Attends Asset Management meetings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Guest Satisfaction/Public Relations: </b></p>
<p><i>Promotes guest satisfaction in an effort to obtain repeat business of leisure and business markets</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluates all guest complaints and ensures corrective action is taken by department heads</li>
<li>Monitors guest responses, responding as needed; executes action plan to correct issues</li>
<li>Hosts social events/receptions as required by the Brand and/or Baywood Hotels</li>
<li>Plays active role in community through affiliation with various community organizations to promote and maintain positive image for hotel</li>
<li>Participates in the BEAR (<b>B*aywood Hotels'</b>E<b>ducation</b>A<b>nd</b>R*each Initiatives) activities and other company-sponsored community service &amp; fundraising events</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Human Resources: </b></p>
<p><i>Ensures employees are provided with the necessary structure, motivation, and training to satisfy their needs and achieve organizational goals</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Interviews, selects, trains, and counsels associates</li>
<li>Ensures that all new hires are given the proper onboarding experience</li>
<li>Ensures that staff is properly trained by their managers</li>
<li>Ensures adequate staffing levels are maintained, adhering to budgetary and staffing guidelines</li>
<li>Ensures compliance of all department heads with goal-oriented job descriptions and reviews goals with each individual</li>
<li>Maintains associate files, ensuring all documents are current (including job descriptions and version of handbook)</li>
<li>Ensures that performance evaluations are being issued as required by corporate</li>
<li>Encourages promotion from within, associate engagement and development through goal setting, employee training and participating in all corporate mandated associate-focused initiatives</li>
<li>Ensures hotel is in compliance with all Baywood policies, Federal and State laws with regards to all personnel practices, safety initiatives, &amp; labor-related postings</li>
<li>Payroll processing and reconciliation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Personal Development: </b></p>
<p><i>Ensures continual efforts are made to increase management abilities and knowledge</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Attends annually a minimum of one advanced management training seminar</li>
<li>Attends classes to become knowledgeable or maintain proficiency in the latest industry-related technology</li>
<li>Attends franchise &amp; Baywood mandated training courses as required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Consistently models the behavior of a 'Baywood Ambassador' who: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains a professional image, including grooming, verbiage, and body language, at all times</li>
<li>Warmly greets both guests and coworkers (using the 10/5, first &amp; last rule) every time they come in contact</li>
<li>Actively listens to guests, responding appropriately to their questions and/or concerns. Seeks guidance and/or assistance from others to ensure satisfaction</li>
<li>Fosters teamwork by offering assistance to others, as needed</li>
<li>Continuously monitors the surroundings at work, in order to identify any cleanliness, safety, security, or building upkeep issues; reports discrepancies to the proper department</li>
<li>Contributes to a safe work environment by following proper safety guidelines in matters of attire, utilizing protective gear, and following safety procedures</li>
<li>Recommends other Baywood properties to our guests, when appropriate</li>
<li>Consistently demonstrates our <b>It's My Pleasure</b>service philosophy by identifying and seizing each golden opportunity to exceed guest expectations</li>
<li><b>Additional duties may be added at any time at the discretion of management</b></li>
</ul>
