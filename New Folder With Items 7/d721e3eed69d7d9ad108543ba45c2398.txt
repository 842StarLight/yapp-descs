<h2>Marine Assembly & Installation Apprentice</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Marine Assembly &amp; Installation Apprentice works with production personnel to fabricate finish product, measures and patterns boats for covers, and to install them when complete. This person will be working in close collaboration with the Production Manager, engineering teams, and floor production personnel.</p>

Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Two years experience in a custom fabrication shop preferred.</li>
<li>
Sewing experience required. Industrial sewing experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Construction or installation experience required. Including the use of basic hand tools.</li>
<li>
Calculate math accurately.
</li>
<li>Current Drivers License; and proof of a safe driving record which meets established Rainier Standards obtained by a motor vehicle report from the appropriate states.
 </li>
</ul>
