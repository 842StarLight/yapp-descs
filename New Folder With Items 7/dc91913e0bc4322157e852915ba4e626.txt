<h2>Technical Project Manager/Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<p>The Software Development Department is seeking a Project Manager/Business Analyst, who will be joining our global PMO team. The position is located in the Buena Park Ria headquarters office. The Project Manager/Senior Business Analyst possesses excellent people management skills and has strong knowledge of the software development lifecycle. We are on the lookout for a goal-oriented project manager with a business analyst profile capable of managing our efforts to continue delivering effective solutions to our organization.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Apply Project Management Methodologies and tools in Software Development Life-cycles (SDLC) to effectively reflect business models’ and requirements’ goals.</li>
<li>
Develop detailed project plans that can be effectively tracked and reported.</li>
<li>
Manage changes to projects’ scope, timelines, and budget while keeping business objectives in mind.</li>
<li>
Analyze reports and serve as a liaison between management and technical teams.</li>
<li>
Gather and elicit Business Requirements by interviewing customers, stakeholders, reviewing existing business documentation, and documenting existing processes and systems.</li>
<li>
Measure project performance and perform risk management using appropriate tools and techniques.</li>
<li>
Influence/Lead technical teams.</li>
<li>
Report on projects’ status and escalate to management as needed.</li>
</ul>

</div>

Qualifications<div>
</div>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 5 years of experience in software implementation project management and business analysis in a cross-functional environment.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in technical/business fields, preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple projects/teams simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Experience with dealing directly with Business Stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Experience with managing projects following both Waterfall and Agile methodologies.</li>
<li>
Experience with leading and managing teams developing large-scale backend systems that support a variety of customer facing applications.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in SQL and Database design considerations.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with MS Project, Smartsheet, or other Project Management tools.</li>
<li>
PMP/PMI-ACP certification - preferred.</li>
<li>
Must be a team player with a positive attitude.</li>
<li>
Flexibility and mindset to operate in a unique and fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>
People management and negotiation skills.</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills.</li>
</ul>
