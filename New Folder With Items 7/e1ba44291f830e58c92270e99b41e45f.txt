<h2>Guest Experience Specialist - Van Buren</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Company Profile</b></p>
<p>A Guest Experience Specialist is someone who excites others and provides a special customer service experience to guests at our micro markets, whether is assisting someone on how to utilize our kiosks to hosting a raffle and game at some our grand openings. A specialist should be able to take initiative to help both the team and customers, welcome new challenges and projects, and love working with new people and environments.
</p>
<p>We are a super outgoing team that loves to have fun on the job. We bring this excitement and personality to our market visits and store openings to engage customers and show that Market 24/7 is the place to be!</p>
<p>Continental’s latest innovation. Market 24/7, is a revolutionary self-checkout vending market. The state-of-the-art micro-market provides hundreds of snack, beverage and fresh food products in a totally customizable retail space located in the workplace. Market 24/7 uses merchandising technology to customize product offerings to customer preferences, making it a great choice for clients looking for more variety for their employees. This expanded product offering also means more opportunity to offer local items, increase fresh food offerings, and more premium, healthy options.</p>
<p>From humble beginnings as a coffee and vending provider in 1989, Continental has grown into Michigan’s largest food management company. Today, our 1800 employees provide a wide range of custom dining, refreshment services and catering solutions through an impressive lineup of brands, including: Continental Café &amp; Catering, Co.Cuisine, Continental Canteen, Market Twenty 4 Seven, Infinity &amp; Ovation Yacht Charters, Waterview Loft at Port Detroit, and Forte Belanger. Dedicated to the simple, yet powerful mission: <i>Delight Our Guest. Every Meal. Every Day</i>.
</p>
<p>Job Description</p>
<ul>
<li>Visits Market stores daily to assist merchandisers and to educate customers how the market works and products served.</li>
<li>Replenish products within the stores as needed</li>
<li>Regular market visits for touching up and perfecting store displays and appearance.</li>
<li>Assist with grand openings and store promotions such as passing out fruit on Earth Day at Eastern Michigan University or U of M, or advertising new concepts such as the GuestRaunt (hot food served in markets).
</li>
<li>Host raffles and giveaways at customers such as Quicken Loans, Little Caesars, and WXYZ.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate creative thinking by suggesting alternative procedures to work flow or possible areas of improvement.</li>
<li>Communicates effectively customers via email, phone, and face to face.</li>
<li>Excite guests through product promotions and meet customer needs</li>
</ul>
