<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Google Cloud teams work with schools, companies, and government agencies to make them more productive, mobile, and collaborative. On this team, you will drive Google Cloud business relationships by analyzing partner business performance, identifying methods of increasing partner and Google returns, pitching ideas to and identifying opportunities within partners, and coordinating across Google teams to deliver.</p>
<p>
The GTM Partner Team is leading the effort to build the world’s leading Cloud Partner Ecosystem. We are shaping the future of how technology is used in the workplace, and invite you to be part of a fast-growing, business critical team in this rapidly growing area.</p>
<p>As a Partner Development Manager (PDM), you'll be responsible for the recruitment, growth, and creation of new practice areas and offerings for Google’s thriving Ecosystem of Services partners. You will focus on growing the practices of partners who specialize in the Google Cloud Business. Your responsibilities will include creating and managing executive relationships with existing and new partners, offering them strategic advice and thought leadership together with day to day management. You will develop close relationships with our Partner Sales Managers (PSM’s) in order to grow the relevance and impact of your assigned Partners to the Google Sales team with the overall goal of growing Google Cloud Business.</p>
<p>
Google Cloud helps millions of employees and organizations empower their employees, serve their customers, and build what’s next for their business — all with technology built in the cloud. Our products are engineered for security, reliability and scalability, running the full stack from infrastructure to applications to devices and hardware. And our teams are dedicated to helping our customers and developers see the benefits of our technology come to life.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Manage partner business performance and map partner capabilities/priorities with Google's goals and strategies to meet business targets; drive quarterly business reviews.</li>
<li>Drive executive connections with sales leadership and partner leadership, including partner value propositions into Partner Sales Manager (PSM) teams.</li>
<li>Help partners grow their Google practice and product portfolio through joint development of solutions and services; develop and provide custom presentations on Google’s Cloud Platform technologies to partners.</li>
<li>Drive partners to build a partner sourced pipeline, nominate partners to receive inbound/outbound leads, and ensure overall pipeline tracking accuracy and health. Drive regional demand generation activities/events with partners and support engagement with partners.</li>
<li>Promote Google’s strategy at industry events, shape industry agenda and Google’s approach to partnership strategy with stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
