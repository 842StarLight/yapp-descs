<h2>Senior Director, Enterprise Marketing Programs</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">InVision is the digital product design platform used to make the world's best customer experiences. We provide design tools and educational resources for teams to navigate every stage of the product design process, from ideation to development. Today, more than 5 million people use InVision to create a repeatable and streamlined design workflow; rapidly design and prototype products before writing code, and collaborate across their entire organization. That includes 100% of the Fortune 100, and organizations like Airbnb, Amazon, HBO, Netflix, Slack, Starbucks and Uber, who are now able to design better products, faster.
<br><br>
Our team is in search of a Senior Director, Enterprise Marketing Programs to help us change the way digital products are designed.
<br><br>
<b>About the Team:</b><br>
The Enterprise Marketing team is responsible for growing InVision's self serve and enterprise revenue and is a critical piece of our company's growth. We partner with virtually everyone on the marketing team to generate top-of-funnel demand, support product launches and events, and create integrated customer acquisition strategies that drive revenue growth.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work cross-functionally to develop and execute marketing plans while managing activities including logistics, scheduling, and budgeting</li>
<li>Manage multiple projects and campaigns simultaneously to meet deadlines and budgets</li>
<li>Proactively communicate plans and tactical details across executives, sales, and marketing to build strong relationships and alignment</li>
<li>Apply KPIs, analytics, and insights to better profile, target, and re-target prospects; drive continual optimization of performance</li>
<li>Collaborate with the creative team to develop effective marketing assets to support programs</li>
<li>Understand and keep up with industry trends that impact and highlight customer pain points to help target messaging</li>
</ul>
<b>What you'll bring:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of experience in B2B enterprise marketing and/or content marketing with previous people management experience</li>
<li>Experience marketing SaaS and cloud-based solutions strongly preferred</li>
<li>Experience using both online and offline tactics</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple priorities in fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with cross-functional teams with the ability to build rapport, influence, and deepen relationships with stakeholders, especially with Sales</li>
<li>Event management experience</li>
</ul>
