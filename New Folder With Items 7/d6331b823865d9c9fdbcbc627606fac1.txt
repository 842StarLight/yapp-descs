<div><b>
What do our Customer Service Representatives (CSR) do?</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
CSRâ€™s are responsible for performing sales order, purchase order and warehouse transfer entries. They are also invoicing, credit memo invoicing, inventory adjustments, receiving, daily sales register update, month end update and customer service duties in an efficient and accurate manner.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Perform daily order entry and billing functions; generate shipping orders, invoices for mailing and initiate back orders</li>
<li>
Enter appropriate customer information into the computer system in an accurate manner</li>
<li>
Answer questions from sales staff and customers regarding products and orders using information available from the inventory and distribution management software</li>
<li>
Monitor billings for accuracy and communicate with personnel to resolve discrepancies or verify unusual items</li>
<li>
Research discrepancies and debit memos to accurately process, issue and post credits/debits to customer accounts as necessary</li>
<li>
Reconcile inventory</li>
<li>
Assist in daily and month end closing by assuring all appropriate information is entered into computer</li>
<li>
Other duties may be assigned</li>
</ul>

What do you need?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Associates degree or equivalent
</li>

<li>
