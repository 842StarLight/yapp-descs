<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>In this key position the Logistics Supervisor is primarily responsible for every aspect of the Living Spaces Logistics SOP and secondarily, in partnership with the Visual Supervisor, for every aspect of Living Spaces Brand SOP. The ultimate goal for the Logistics Supervisor is to ensure world-class CPU service, shipping and receiving, and product processing. This is accomplished by building and developing team members through the execution of Foundational Excellence (SOP, Talent, and Leadership).
