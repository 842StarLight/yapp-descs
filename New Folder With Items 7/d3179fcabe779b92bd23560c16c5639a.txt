<h2>Exit Gate Attendant - Full Time</h2>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>General Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Are you searching for an opportunity to be a part of a team? Do you find it important to pay close attention to detail while still being able to uphold security standards? If you are a detail oriented person looking for a career in general security, Dollar Thrifty is looking for you!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As a Gate Attendant your general responsibilities include but are not limited to:</div>
<ul><li>
 Greet customers and complete necessary rental information by scanning and checking the rental agreement using the handheld unit, checking customer identification, scanning the vehicle asset tag, and checking the tags to verify the movement of vehicle</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Provide optional sales items, as directed by management, while using approved sales techniques to disclose rates, terms and conditions of rental and obtain customer signatures.</li>
<li>
Maintain location and company security measures to mitigate theft of company assets; perform sight monitoring of lot; ensure smooth transition of vehicles exiting through security gate.</li>
<li>
Assist customers with directions, and review and resolve rental contract and vehicle issues.</li>
<li>
Manually log and release vehicles from lot during computer system outages or unavailability.</li>
<li>
Perform related responsibilities as required or assigned.</li>
</ul>
