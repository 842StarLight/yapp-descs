<h2>Product Development Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Position summary:</b> The Product Development Engineer will analyze, revise and develop saw blade concepts/designs/technologies in conjunction with development of manufacturing processes, tools &amp; equipment. S/he will also drive growth by improving market share in alignment with the company’s strategic plans on saw blades.
<br>
<br>
<b>Purpose:</b> To develop and execute saw blade development program incorporating novel and efficient cutting concepts that enhances company’s competitive position.
<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Job Functions:</b>
<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Revise existing and develop novel innovative saw blade products.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Assist in the selection of correct machinery/equipment and to lead process improvement projects to maximize process efficiency and reliability to enhance the quality &amp; profitability.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Identify new cutting tool materials, suppliers, and other sources.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Develop and execute internal/external tests to evaluate competitive position of new/current products.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Work with manufacturing and process engineers to improve manufacturing processes.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Provide cutting tool design and application expertise to Product Development team.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Provide technical support and technology transfer to plant operations, sales and marketing teams and customers to improve overall performance
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Support development of key technologies that enhance long-term proprietary strategic position.
</li></ul>
<br>
<b>The person must also be able to:</b>
<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Represent The M. K. Morse Company in a professional manner.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Understand and follow company procedures to be able to work within the system and effect change.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Support continuous improvement initiatives throughout the organization.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Assist in the review of patents relevant to product management.
</li></ul>
<br>
<b>Skills and Experience:</b>
<br>
<br>
<ul><li>2-5 years of experience in manufacturing or product development is required, cutting tool experience preferred; ideally in cutting tool development, design or application.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Strong project management and team leadership skills to lead engineers, technicians and operators.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Effective oral and written communications skills.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Ability to work in a cross-functional team environment and with internal and customers &amp; suppliers.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Ability to manage multiple projects and priorities in a timely manner.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Self-motivated, assertive and able to work with minimal supervision and initiate and drive projects to complete successfully in a timely manner.
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Possess the intellectual curiosity to break the rules in problem solving by thinking outside of the box
</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Computer proficiency in office software, and CAD/CAM utilities.
</li></ul>
<br>
<b>Education Requirements:</b> BSME or related required, Master’s or PhD preferred</div></div>
