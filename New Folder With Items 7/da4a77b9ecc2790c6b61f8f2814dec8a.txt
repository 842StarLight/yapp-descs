<p>
As National Account Manager, fortify and develop business with high profile pure-play e-commerce accounts like Amazon, Wayfair, and other leading e-commerce platforms and marketplaces to increase sales and market share. Introduce and advocate necessary changes to optimize Elkay’s brand awareness and success thru e-commerce platforms and marketplaces. Own development and execution of a multi-year strategic plan aligned with organization’s omni-channel strategy.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Specific responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and execute strategic plan to realize full market share potential through mastery of e-commerce fundamentals and brand optimization.</li>
<li>
Lead cross-functional team to ensure strategy and plan is being executed consistently across every touchpoint between Elkay and each platform / marketplace.</li>
<li>
Partner with product and marketing teams on: product assortment, pricing, online content, marketing, advertising and promotions.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with and inspire all other internal departments to challenge norms in effort to execute strategic vision / plan and meet customer requirements: Supply Planning, Warehousing, Logistics, Manufacturing, IT, Finance, Accounting, Customer Care.</li>
<li>
Execute plans in compliance with customer requirements and Elkay processes: Due diligence for customer requirements and supplier agreements, negotiation of all program terms, resolve system conflicts and restraints.</li>
<li>
Sales Planning and Forecasting - Monitor sales and inventory levels to assess risks and properly communicate volume expectations for manufacturing and supply planning.</li>
<li>
Monitor and analyze performance and market trends to plan and/or react as necessary: Sales, ratings and reviews, competitor performance, product listings - site presence.</li>
</ul>
