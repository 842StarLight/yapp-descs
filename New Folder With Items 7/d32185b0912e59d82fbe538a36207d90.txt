<p><b>Website Account Manager</b></p>
<p>
<b>What you’ll do as our Website Account Manager</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand the client’s business challenges to design effective solutions that achieve their desired outcomes</li>
<li>Work with clients to define requirements and develop an executable plan</li>
<li>Build client knowledge and credibility quickly</li>
<li>Keep projects on-time and on-budget by proactively managing risk and resources.</li>
<li>Communicate with clients proactively and respond to inquiries the same day.</li>
<li>Write technical website documentation</li>
<li>Manage Proactive Maintenance Program</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years’ experience working with clients as the primary contact managing website projects in a variety of industries including e-commerce, lead generation and mid-complex brochure websites.</li>
<li>3+ years as an account manager in an agency environment (marketing, design/development agency)</li>
<li>Experience leading and guiding teams to build large web sites, web apps, and/or mobile apps</li>
<li>Must be extremely organized and detail oriented</li>
<li>Experience managing projects from beginning to end</li>
<li>Proficiency with cloud software tools (Invision, Dropbox, Wrike, Semrush)</li>
<li>Outstanding verbal and written communication skills with audiences of all levels, including strong presentation development and delivery as well as business and technical writing</li>
<li>Effective communication of project needs to team members</li>
<li>Experience managing project budgets, forecasts, and resources</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Technical Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be proficient at WordPress Page Builders such as Avada, Elementor, Guttenberg and be able to manage some WordPress ACF’s</li>
<li>Must have a thorough knowledge of WordPress plugin interactions and have the ability to research recommendations and utilize needed plugins</li>
<li>Must understand staging and development environments</li>
<li>Must have the ability to understand and manipulate content and minor template changes</li>
<li>Must use a keen attention to detail to find bugs, errors, and missed requirements on websites in preparation for client review or launch</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Nice to haves:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on web development/programming experience (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL)</li>
<li>Marketing/Design/UI/UX experience</li>
<li>Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics, Google Search Console, SEO and website performance evaluation experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’ s degree in Computer Science or Business/related field; or equivalent work experience</li>
</ul>
