Job Description
<br>Technical Program Manager will join the Business Systems team in supporting ecosystem integrity, scalability and data quantity to meet the business growing requirements and strategies. Our team supports Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, Professional Services and Finance teams. This role will manager Projects which include system implementation, ecosystem architecture, system optimization and custom development. System include are all SAAS tech stack and the UserTesting Platform integrations.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage all aspects of project management from planning, resourcing, communications, timeline, budget, change, risks and issues</li>
<li>Facilitate the definition of project objectives, scope, and schedule</li>
<li>Think and act strategically around process and efficiency.</li>
<li>Create project plans, document system and business processes, and work with different departmental stakeholders to reflect current business model.</li>
<li>Leverage organizational resources to improve capacity for project work</li>
<li>Coordinate with internal partners for successful transitions from planning phases through launch</li>
<li>Communication and present project status to cross-functional teams</li>
<li>Designing, implementing, and leading the day-to-day update process of these schedules to appropriately identify schedule issues early</li>
<li>Prepare upfront estimates of time and resource utilization, and update over the course of the project.</li>
<li>Facilitate change control and risk mitigation.</li>
<li>Analyze risk and mitigation accordingly, including contingency plans and initiating corrective action.</li>
<li>Working with outside development contractors</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>PMP/PMI or related Project Management certification preferred</li>
<li>Minimum of 7 years of project management experience</li>
<li>Proven experience in managing software-as-a-service projects.</li>
<li>Experience or deep knowledge with solutions such as CRM, CMS, Marketing Automation, Ticket Management.</li>
<li>Proven experience working in a fast pace SAAS startup environment.</li>
<li>Able to manage changing requirements and priorities</li>
<li>Strong systems-level critical thinking skills</li>
<li>Strong leadership, diplomatic and motivational skills including the ability to lead up, across and down multiple teams. Holding teams accountable for their commitments, removing roadblocks to their work</li>
<li>Experience working with remote teams</li>
<li>Comfortable in a fast-paced team environment that requires strong time-management and prioritization skills and a varying degree of ambiguity</li>
<li>Diplomacy and interpersonal skills of the highest quality</li>
</ul>
