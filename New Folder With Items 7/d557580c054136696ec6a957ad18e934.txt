<p><b>The opportunity:</b>
</p>
<p>As an OpenText Senior Solution Consultant, your role is to work with Account Executives across various aspects of the sales process. You provide technical assistance and expertise to the customers and prospects looking to leverage OpenText’s products in their environment or to embed into their existing application(s).</p>
<p><b>You are great at:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of statistical and predictive modeling concepts and recommendation and optimization algorithms.</li>
<li>Working on complex problems that may require research and deep knowledge of the solution and address broad design issues for future projects.</li>
<li>Providing technical leadership to deliver successful solutions to large corporations and government agencies.</li>
<li>Applying both technical and business skills to create and build solutions to meet the customer’s business requirements.</li>
<li>Developing best practices for implementation projects</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What it takes:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS degree in Computer Science, Information Science, Engineering, Statistics, Mathematics or related field degree.</li>
<li>At least 2 years of professional experience in Analytics/Data Science.</li>
<li>Experience with Jupyter Notebook</li>
<li>Expertise on Spark in general and MLlib specifically (PySpark, Scala, SparkR)</li>
<li>Programming skills and advanced knowledge of Python and R.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and creative mindset.</li>
<li>Experience with SQL, ETLs and data structures.</li>
<li>Experience working with large, multi-dimensional real datasets and real business projects.</li>
<li>Able to work in a team environment.</li>
<li>Willing to travel. In some cases, travel could be international.</li>
<li>Experience with Java will be appreciated.</li>
<li>Deep learning/Neural Networks knowledge will be appreciated.</li>
<li>Experience creating rich, meaningful data visualizations will be appreciated.</li>
<li>Knowledge/Experience with big data technologies will be appreciated.</li>
<li>Experience with business intelligence tools will be appreciated.</li>
<li>Experience with text mining and analyzing unstructured content will be appreciated.</li>
<li>Demonstrates effective understanding and usage of the terrain map - understanding key roles (influencer, champion, etc) and how to influence them.</li>
<li>Completes extensive research at the Account level - customer goals and strategy, competitive positioning and defines OT sales plays to address these.</li>
<li>Takes full responsibility for customer focused activities.</li>
<li>Can lead RFX answering processes as well as customer facing engagements like POC's.</li>
<li>Seeks input from the various parties involved and makes sure deadlines are met.</li>
<li>High level understanding of all the main OT products in the BU.</li>
<li>Analyze and understand the customer's business process and KPI's. Identify where OT can bring value to the customer and how different products can play together to support the customers goals.</li>
<li>Demonstrates ability to deliver business solutions in the customer/partner industry or market.</li>
<li>Builds compelling Value Propositions to support opportunities.</li>
<li>Identifies and develops relationships with key business contacts.</li>
<li>Jointly works with others to plan workshops and prep materials.</li>
<li>Contributes to Customer workshops</li>
