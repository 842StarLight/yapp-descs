<p>
Georgia-Pacific creates long-term value by using resources efficiently to provide innovative products and solutions that meet the needs of customers and society, while operating in a manner that is environmentally and socially responsible, and economically sound. Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia-Pacific is one of the world's leading manufacturers and marketers of building products, tissue, packaging, paper, cellulose and related chemicals. The company employs more than 30,000 people at approximately 300 locations in North and South America.</p>

<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We are seeking an experienced and enthusiastic Marketing Writer to join the Content team that supports the GP PRO business in its marketing. This role would support a variety of digital and traditional marketing avenues for commercial customers in an exciting, collaborative and fast-paced environment. The Marketing Writer will write like a pro and think like a marketer to build brand affinity in the Healthcare space.</p>
<p>
This role is ultimately about storytelling based on industry insights and driving a message that resonates within the Healthcare industry. The developer will work with multiple stakeholders including category, sales and marketing leaders. The ideal candidate must have proven writing skills, strong content vision and leadership, stellar facilitation and project management skills, and the proven ability to manage multiple deadlines and priorities to consistently meet partner expectations. This position reports to the Director, Content Development.</p>
<div></div>
<br>

Create briefs for, direct and execute tactics and deliverables, which may include messaging, copywriting, ad copy, video content, web content, case studies, white papers, testimonials</li>

Delegate and direct work as needed to others (video production, outside resources etc.)</li>
