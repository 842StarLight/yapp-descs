<h2>Policy and Business Development Analyst - Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Brooklyn, NY</b></h4>
<div>
<p>PowerMarket is a growing company working at the intersection of energy, finance and policy. We provide utilities with a software platform that allows them to deliver distributed generation programs like energy efficiency and solar to their customers and are looking for an Energy Policy and Business Development Analyst to help us.</p>
<p>In this position you will analyze the current North American clean energy policy landscape to determine states and provinces that represent the best opportunities for distributed generation programs like deep retrofit energy efficiency, oil-to-gas conversions, solar rooftops, and electric vehicle infrastructures.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Understand and parse existing data in DSIRE and ACEEE renewable energy databases
</li>
<li>Cull public data into qualitative and quantitative research for presentation to management
</li>
<li>Participate in the strategic discussions surrounding the research</li>
<li>Prioritize research and seek new sources of data</li>
<li>Contribute to our internal knowledge base of policy and data assets</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>You are passionate and open-minded about the energy industry.</li>
<li>Experience working in the utility industry or demonstated understanding of utility industry concepts like avoided cost, LMP, dispatch, load duration, capacity markets, DTCs, LSEs, ISOs and RSOs
</li>
<li>Experience working with environmental commodities or deep understanding of environmental commodity markets and instruments like RECs SRECs, WhiteTags, Offsets, Carbon Credits
</li>
<li>Experience working in energy policy or deep understanding of RPS, EEPS, RGGI, CA32, Cost Recovery, Regulatory Frameworks, Cap and Trade, Carbon Taxes
</li>
<li>You have an Bachelors or Masters degree (preferred) in a relevant field and substantial work experience
</li>
<li>You are extremely comfortable in a distrubuted work environment and have complete command of MS Office, mobile productivity, and GoogleDocs environment
</li>
<li>Demonstrated fluency (the ability to read, write and edit) HTML and CSS</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills. Can present findings concisely and effectively
</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>To Apply</b></h4>
<p>Send a current resume and brief cover letter to: careers@powermarket.io</p>
