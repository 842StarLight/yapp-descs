<p><b>
About the Role
</b></p>
<p>The Finance Manager, Revenue, will join a fast-paced, growing Corporate FP&amp;A team and will support CrowdStrike in forecasting revenue accurately. Among other responsibilities, in this role you will have the unique opportunity to work with various people across the organization, such as Sales, Accounting, and Data Analytics. This position is located in Irvine, CA.</p>
<p><b>
Additional Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Improving Sales data accuracy – work closely with Sales, Sales ops and Data Analytics team to improve the accuracy of Sales reports</li>
<li>
Bookings and ARR Forecast – validate the actual bookings and ARR per month and producing forecast by category and product.</li>
<li>
Revenue forecast - review Sales pipeline data and guide the management team on the realistic monthly, quarterly and yearly achievement</li>
<li>
Forecast process improvement – drive efficiency around revenue forecasting cycle</li>
<li>
Ad hoc and Special projects</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What You’ll Need</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Education – Bachelor’s degree, MBA is a plus</li>
<li>
6+ years of experience in SaaS Revenue accounting and/or forecasting</li>
<li>
Knowledge on GAAP Rev recognition for SaaS business model</li>
<li>
Computer Skills – the ability to demonstrate the highest skill levels working in Excel and PowerPoint; familiarity working with Saleforce.com pipeline data, Adaptive Insights Planning, and NetSuite accounting data a plus.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with SaaS Company Metrics – experience calculating and comparing churn, dollar retention rates, cohort growth rates, lifetime value, customer acquisition cost, etc.</li>
<li>
Other – demonstrated experience or interest related to cybersecurity and/or SaaS.</li>
</ul>
