<div>Leonard Bus Sales, a recognized leader in the distribution and support of quality school buses, is looking for a <b>Service Parts Runner</b> with a diversified background in parts and ability to multi-task.</div>
<div></div>

<div>This position will serve as a strategic partner to the Service and Parts teams with a focus on coordinating and delivering needed parts for internal shop work; ensuring maximum productivity, accuracy, profitability, efficiency, quality control, and alignment with company and departmental goals and guidelines. Looking for an individual to foster, manage, and promote positive internal and external relationships through excellent communication and customer service while assisting in the development of and adherence to sound practices and processes.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Reviews and fulfills parts request tickets created by shop technicians.</li>
<li>Ensures that cores are properly documented.</li>
<li>Properly codes and tags warranty parts.</li>
<li>Assists team members in researching parts as needed.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Minimum Requirements of the position are:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent combination of education, training and experience, which provides the required knowledge, skills, and abilities.</li>
<li>Minimum of five (5) years previous experience in a parts warehouse.</li>
<li>Computer proficiency in web-based programs and applications, Microsoft Office Suite applications and related departmental software to include researching and looking up information.</li>
<li>Experience in computerized inventory software, and ordering processing systems.</li>
<li>Forklift experience.</li>
<li>Prior parts experience preferred.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Certifications or Licenses Required:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Valid New York driver’s license or driver’s license in state of residence required.</li>
<li>Forklift certification desired.</li>
</ul>
