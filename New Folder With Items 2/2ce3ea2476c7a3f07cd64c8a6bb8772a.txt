<p><b>Position Summary
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Sony Corporation of America </b>is seeking a Senior Tax Analyst to join its Global Tax Office in New York City. Reporting to the Director, State and Local Tax, the Sr. Tax Analyst will support the sales and income tax compliance and reporting functions. The candidate will work closely with the Sales and Income Tax Managers and will be required to demonstrate an advanced understanding of tax laws and regulations including states’ responses to Wayfair and Federal Tax Reform. Additionally, the candidate must be well organized and self-motivated with excellent analytical and communication skills.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Senior Tax Analyst supports the sales and income tax functions and will be responsible for performing designated assignments including:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Process and prepare tax returns
</li>
<li>Reconcile tax accounts
</li>
<li>Maintain tax calendars (i.e., monitor due dates)
</li>
<li>Support tax audits; this includes compiling data as needed
</li>
<li>Research and review sales and income tax nexus
</li>
<li>Update taxability matrices
</li>
<li>Monitor data feeds to ensure automation of sales tax data
</li>
<li>Perform research of sales and income tax laws
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Requires a Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting or Finance, and a minimum of 5 years of progressive sales and income tax experience
</li>
<li>CPA and/or Masters in Tax preferred
</li>
<li>Solid understanding of federal and state income tax concepts
</li>
<li>Experience working for one of the Big Four firms or a large multinational company desired
</li>
<li>Hands-on experience preparing sales and income tax returns
</li>
<li>Advanced technical research skills
</li>
<li>Exceptional attention to detail, communication, and presentation skills
</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience with Vertex, Taxware and/or Corptax software
</li>
<li>All candidates must be authorized to work in the USA
</li>
</ul>
