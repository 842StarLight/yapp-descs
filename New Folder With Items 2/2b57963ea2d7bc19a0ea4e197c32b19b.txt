<h2>Full Time Rental Sales Agent Downtown San Francisco BUSH Street</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>National and Alamo has an exciting opportunity for a Rental Sales Agent at the Bush Street location in Downtown San Francisco. The Rental Sales Agent provides a superior, friendly, and efficient transaction (at time of rental and return) using the company approved sales and service techniques. Facilitates the rental process through verification and documentation of all necessary driver information to provide for an efficient and timely rental and return experience. Performs all responsibilities with a focus on the wants and needs of our customers and in accordance with our Quality Standards.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Get on the fast track to a more rewarding career with National Car Rental and Alamo Rent A Car - teams that are committed to quality, innovation, customer satisfaction and employee development.<br>
<br>
Our philosophy is to take care of our customers and employees first. We know if we do this, success will follow - for both our company and our employees.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position pays $15.84/hour plus commission and requires a valid drivers license.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li><div>Use company approved sales and service techniques when determining customer needs</div></li>
<li><div>
Offer optional products to meet customer wants and needs.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare all rental and return documents accurately and completely.</div></li>
<li><div>
Qualify each customer using our company rental requirement guidelines.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide customers assistance with directions, maps, local area information, appropriate service information, etc.</div></li>
<li><div>
Review rental parameters with all customers to ensure a complete understanding of our rates and service charges.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure that the return date and time on the rental agreement is accurate.</div></li>
<li><div>
Review all charges at the time of vehicle return.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare the Rental Agreement Folder with all required information.</div></li>
<li><div>
Answer the phones to assist customers in a friendly, helpful and prompt manner.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist customers by effectively resolving all customer service issues.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist and coordinate customers within the queue to minimize any wait time and promote the most efficient service method as required.</div></li>
</ul>
