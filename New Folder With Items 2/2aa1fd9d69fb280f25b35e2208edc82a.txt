<h2>Director of Manufacturing Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>With our strong investment in research and development and our culture of continuous improvement, Hexcel is the industry leader in the manufacturing of composite materials and industrial fabrics. We invite you to join the Hexcel team at various manufacturing sites, sales offices and R&amp;T centers around the globe. Become a part of the “strength within.”
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Hexcel is currently seeking a <b>Director of Manufacturing Solutions </b>within the IT organization for our <b>Salt Lake City, UT, USA, Fibers </b>location. T his position will deliver and support Hexcel’s current and future state manufacturing operations management (MOM) processes, capability and tools. This Leader will employ an Agile methodology to create solutions in an Operational Technology (OT) manufacturing setting with a focus to accelerate the delivery of value for shop floor Operators and functional teams such as Quality, EHS and manufacturing management.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The selected individual will be responsible for but not limited to the following obligations:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Development of the roadmap for manufacturing operation capability in corporation with the MOM governance committee
</li>
<li>The delivery of value through capability and process support using Hexcel’s chosen Agile framework and MOM platform and tools
</li>
<li>Partnership with Plant Managers ensuring Site teams are effectively using solutions to deliver consistent execution and ongoing improvements
</li>
<li>Management of deployment of solutions to Site Operations and functional teams
</li>
<li>Instrumental contribution to support services ensuring maximized availability of manufacturing systems and solutions of effective change management practices for manufacturing systems
</li>
<li>Management of the internal Agile product delivery team as well as 3rd party developers and system integrators (SI’s)
</li>
<li>Responsible for overseeing or directly managing complex MOM programs/projects from design through development and into production.
</li>
<li>Creates strategies for contingency planning and risk mitigation.
</li>
<li>Maintaining a continuous delivery model based on the Safe4 Agile methodology including a robust cadence of program increments and SCRUM sprints within multiple value streams
</li>
<li>Performing team assessment and evaluations.
</li>
<li>Designing and maintaining project and technical documentation.
</li>
<li>Management of a matrixed global team of architects, analysts, engineers and support staff
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Operational Technologies, Computer Science, Manufacturing Engineering or related field
</li>
<li>Ten plus year of progressive IT or Manufacturing systems leadership experience
</li>
<li>Business, manufacturing and technical savvy, able to understand the business perspective that drives manufacturing capability requirements
</li>
<li>Credibility at all levels of an organization, able to talk with shop floor staff about day-to-day challenges and equally comfortably with senior Leadership regarding strategy and decisions
</li>
<li>Strong communication and facilitation skills with large project experience and success
</li>
<li>Broad understanding of automation, MOM/MES and real time software in manufacturing environments
</li>
<li>Strong relationship building skills able to build and lead ad hoc teams within and between organizations
</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in multi-Site, enterprise scale global projects in IT or manufacturing domains
</li>
<li>Experience with manufacturing and predictive analytics, while not required, is a plus
</li>
<li>Experience leading the sustained use of an Agile construct and tools to drive accelerated delivery of value to internal or external Customers
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Eligible candidate must be: U.S. citizen, U.S. national, a person lawfully admitted for permanent residence, a temporary resident under sections 210(a) or 245(A) of the Act, a person admitted in refugee status, a person granted asylum. Hexcel (NYSE: HXL) is a global leader in advanced composites technology, a leading producer of carbon fiber, and the world leader in honeycomb manufacturing for the commercial aerospace industry.
</p>
