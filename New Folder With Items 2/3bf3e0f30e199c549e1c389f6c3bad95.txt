<h2>Director, Account Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>WHAT WE DO MATTERS:</b><br>
Here at The Knot Worldwide, we believe in doing work that matters. In 15 countries around the world, our leading family of brands (The Knot, WeddingWire, Bodas, GigMasters, The Bump, How They Asked, Lasting, and more) inspire, inform, and celebrate our communities as they move through life's milestones. From the proposal to creating a home, and starting a family together, we're there for every step of the journey. Our couples and business partners depend on us. They're all in. So are we.
<br><br>
<b>ABOUT THE ROLE AND OUR TEAM:</b><br>
TKWW is looking for a client centric leader with a positive attitude, strong campaign management experience and digital media business acumen to lead our all-star Account Management team. The Director of Account Management is responsible for managing a team of managers and individual contributors who own the end to end execution of The Knot Worldwide's direct and programmatic digital ad campaigns. In this role, you will handle a range of digital media formats (display, social, email, video, etc.), be a strong people manager, develop sophisticated media packages that meet client needs and roll-up your sleeves to troubleshoot and act as a point of escalation for your team.
<br><br>
The person in this role ensures a high level of client experience and independently manages the team to establish best practices, expectations for execution and prioritize tasks. In this role, you will maintain strategic top-level relationships and be a trusted advisor for our clients.
<br><br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead the team through coaching and maintain a positive, customer centric attitude</li>
<li>Actively manage workload across the team, balancing client relationships with workload of individual team members</li>
<li>Attract, recruit, train and retain top talent and maintain a high-performing team</li>
<li>Ensure timelines to respond to client RFPs are met, that campaign elements are launched and campaign reports are all delivered on schedule with meaningful and relevant insights</li>
<li>Proactively review delivery pacing and performance against client KPIs, and recommend reallocations and optimizations, as needed</li>
<li>Work with clients to uncover business needs, develop strategies and identify products to help achieve client goals</li>
<li>Oversee the development and negotiation of campaign terms &amp; conditions</li>
<li>Observe team activities and workflow, make positive improvements within the team as well as in the hand-offs with other related internal departments</li>
<li>Implement process improvements to gain efficiencies and enhance performance</li>
<li>Be an expert in our ad product offerings and capabilities</li>
<li>Lead regular team meetings, deliver presentations regarding operational goals and updates</li>
<li>Independently problem solve and coach the team to navigate against new variables</li>
<li>Ensure the order management system is up to date and accurate for billing / revenue recognition</li>
</ul>
<b>SUCCESSFUL DIRECTOR, ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT CANDIDATES HAVE:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of relevant professional experience in sales support in digital advertising</li>
<li>3+ years of experience building and managing successful account management teams</li>
<li>Excellent organization, communication and analytical skills</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and excellent written, verbal and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple clients and deliverables simultaneously in a high energetic/fast paced environment, operate with a sense of urgency</li>
<li>Desire to take ownership of all clients' questions and concerns</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Communications, Marketing, Business, Finance, or equivalent</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of: Salesforce, Operative, MOAT, GAM, 3rd party Ad Servers/Tags/Tracking, Agency Media Planning Tools, Social, Paid Media and Syndication</li>
<li>Proficient in Google suite, PowerPoint and Excel</li>
</ul>
