<p><b>What You Will Do
</b></p>
<p>An Associate Technical Developer at The North Face is responsible for driving the development, engineering, fit execution and specifications for innovative, technologically advanced products of the highest level.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We build products that are coveted and trusted by our customers in a fun, fast paced environment. We are passionate, precise, team oriented engineers with a love of the outdoors and a commitment to sustainability.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>How You Will Make A Difference
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages the development process of company product lines as assigned.
</li>
<li>Plans and tracks the development schedule of all products to ensure a timely completion following the predetermined product development calendar.
</li>
<li>Ensures both the technical superiority and the product quality/integrity of all products falling under their responsibilities.
</li>
<li>Develops products, recommends materials, and construction techniques that meet Design expectations and meet or exceed predetermined gross margin targets.
</li>
<li>Ensures execution of fit intent and fit consistency through clear analysis, documentation, communication, adherence to fit standards, and use of block patterns.
</li>
<li>Creates the specifications for the products, publishes, and maintains all records, style files, and samples associated with the product and related programs.
</li>
<li>Partners with Product and Material Sourcing and manufacturers to develop new materials, construction techniques, processes, and samples used to create innovative and technically superior products each season.
</li>
<li>Interacts with Product Director, Product Manager, Design, Marketing, Sales, and Quality Assurance to distribute product technical details and assure the accuracy, legality, and integrity of all product performance claims.
</li>
<li>Performs these and other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
