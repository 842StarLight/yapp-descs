<p><b>Job Title: </b>Vertical Marketing Manager
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>About our group:
</b></p>
<p>Our Seagate’s B2B Product Marketing team is responsible for go-to-market strategies, product launch, messaging, positioning, branding, content and ecosystem development for the company’s multi-billion dollar portfolio of enterprise, surveillance and NAS HDD and SSD storage devices, systems and related software.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>About the role - you will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Lead vertical marketing content creation, ecosystem development, campaign support and go-to-market initiatives for the world’s leading Enterprise storage devices and systems portfolio.
</p></li>
<li><p>Work with cross-functional business development, sales, product and marketing teams around the world to shape the buyers’ journey, identify opportunity and drive breakthrough revenue growth within a multi-billion-dollar technology pioneer.
</p></li>
<li><p>Create impactful messaging, narratives and content that addresses the needs of vertical-market-oriented solution providers and end users while clearly articulating advantageous technical outcomes and business successes.
</p></li>
<li><p>Successfully position, differentiate and drive preference for Seagate’s cutting-edge data storage products in a hypercompetitive marketplace.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>About you:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Contagious enthusiasm and passion for excellence
</p></li>
<li><p>Skillful presentation and public speaking abilities in addressing working teams, large audiences, executives, industry analysts and press
</p></li>
<li>You may also possess a high degree of business acumen with an aptitude for insightful analysis while internalizing and reliably-delivering to aggressive business objectives.
</li>
<li>Be a conscientious, innovative and detail-oriented thought leader who inspires passion, energy and enthusiasm for innovative solutions to customers’ most urgent information technology challenges.
</li>
<li>Have a gift for crafting and delivering compelling stories and value propositions with a command of the latest marketing tools, techniques, and media.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your experience includes:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The ability to demonstrate success in the hands-on international marketing of technology products to B2B
</li>
<li>Ability to successfully lead, innovate and openly collaborate across diverse functional organizations, time zones and cultures
</li>
<li>Excellent storytelling, writing, editing and content creation skills with a keen eye for quality, usability and impact
</li>
<li><p>Ability to demonstrate an impressive body of professional work
</p></li>
<li><p>Expertise in vertical marketing for the technology industry in one or more verticals such as: Media &amp; Entertainment, Telecommunications, Cloud/Edge Computing, Autonomous Vehicles, Security, Energy, Govt/Edu, Banking/Finance, Healthcare.
</p></li>
<li>Bachelor’s and/or equivalent experience
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</p>
<p>Our Longmont product-design campus is nestled against the foothills with exceptional views of the Rocky Mountains. Here at work, you can grab breakfast and lunch in the on-site cafeteria or get an afternoon espresso, prepared by a professional barista. Our 1,500+ employees enjoy an active on-site experience from sporting activities (get in a few laps at lunch on our 1-mile walking path around campus, play ping-pong or volleyball, or stop in our 24- hour fitness center for a group or individual workout) to community service and many employee resource groups including Pride!, Women’s Leadership Network and a Young Professionals Network.
</p>
