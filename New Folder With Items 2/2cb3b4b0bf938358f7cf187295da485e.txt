<p><b>
Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Forecast Analyst s primary responsibility is to develop and manage a forecast model to fit distribution, production, and supply chain needs. Forecast models will be built primarily on customer delivery history, point-of-sale data, and seasonality. The Analyst is responsible for identifying any other predictive variables that can be effectively incorporated into these forecast models. The Analyst will also be expected to collaborate with other internal departments and Atlas customers to improve the accuracy of forecasted demand. Both short-term and long-term demand plans are required to meet Atlas business needs. Demand planning will be done using Atlas forecasting software. The development of a demand plan to reliably identify delivery frequency and upcoming service needs are the principal goals for this position. Additionally, the Forecast Analyst will work with Operations, Logistics, and Finance Departments to develop and analyze KPI reports to explain business trends.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop standard forecasting protocol for projecting customer demand that fits Atlas business objectives of operational efficiency and customer service.</li>
<li>Leverage demand projections to identify delivery service needs at the customer level and generate delivery orders through an automated forecasting and orders system</li>
<li>Manage daily forecast exceptions to enhance the forecasting model and improve customer service</li>
<li>Provide long-term demand plans and assist in developing sales and staffing budgets</li>
<li>Define optimal safety stock for each distribution location, given service levels, and confidence in forecast, by location</li>
<li>Solicit feedback from key players in other departments to identify anomalies that may impact demand</li>
<li>Support analyses to optimize supply chain, including fleet size and driver staffing requirements</li>
<li>Develop and document SOP for all forecasting-related processes</li>
<li>Track and identify the impact of weather events on customer demand</li>
<li>Assist in development of annual financial budgets and quarterly forecasts</li>
<li>Communicate ongoing forecast performance with management on a weekly basis</li>
<li>Identify trends in demand and report on the anticipated impact</li>
<li>Develop KPIs related to customer demand</li>
<li>Create and distribute KPI reports and analyze these reports to identify business improvement opportunities</li>
</ul>
