<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Adams Products, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Description Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Install, adjust and repair production equipment and special purpose machines as assigned</li>
<li>
Operate a variety of hand and power tools to repair, grease, weld, cut, change parts and complete tasks as assigned</li>
<li>
Test malfunctioning machinery, determine repairs and discuss with head mechanic for approval and repair as needed</li>
<li>
Install equipment and electrical components and test to ensure proper functioning</li>
<li>
Dismantle equipment to gain access to and remove defective parts using hoists, cranes, hand tools and power tools</li>
<li>
Lubricate and clean parts and equipment to ensure proper operation</li>
<li>
Other duties may be assigned as needed</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements / Qualifications</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
At least 2 years of experience in a manufacturing setting performing manufacturing maintenance</li>
<li>
Experience with Besser equipment is preferred</li>
<li>
Previous experience in plant machine maintenance; hydraulics, electrical, ability to trouble shoot PLC’s, welding (preferred but can work around)</li>
</ul>
