<div><b>Position Summary:</b></div>
<p>The Commercial Coordinator – Deltar Fasteners will report to the Sales Manager – Deltar Fasteners and will be responsible to assist engineers and sales with all aspects of project management and overall customer service.
</p>
<div><b>Primary Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Interacting with ITW Management, Sales professionals, and multiple Global ITW Plant Operations related to quotes using the ITW Quote Database software</li>
<li>Sales responsibility involves working directly with customer engineering and purchasing personnel
<ul>
<li>Sell product which involves part searches
</li>
<li>Interaction with ITW engineering
</li>
<li>Test data requests/submissions
</li>
<li>Final price negotiation
</li>
<li>Sales Report write-ups, etc.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Project Management responsibilities being the central point of contact for the customer
<ul>
<li>Duties include maintain Bill of Materials, assigning part numbers, obtaining quotes, creating sales literature, and working with operations and sales from all ITW North America divisions to ensure initial set-up and spot buy orders and payment, etc.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Maintain forecast database (SAPHRAN software)
<ul>
<li>Review and sign off purchase orders for accuracy against quote and resolve anomalies with management</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Responsibility for tool cost recovery from customer ensuring all tool purchase orders and tool expenditures are recorded correctly and re-negotiated with customer accounting and purchase management when necessary</li>
<li>Interface between Operations and Sales for any customer requirements
 <ul>
<li>Customer forms, packaging data requests, MSDS, supplier surveys, etc.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Understand and draft Annual Plan/Long-Range Plan documents for presentation to Commercial Leader</li>
<li>Maintain database of current open project activity for Sales professionals</li>
<li>Shared General Office Administrative duties as needed</li>
<li>Other tasks and duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
