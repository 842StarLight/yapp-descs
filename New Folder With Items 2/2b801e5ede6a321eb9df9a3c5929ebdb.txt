<h2>Entry Level Business Analyst - HRIS</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>ON Semiconductor is seeking a professional, self-driven and motivated business analyst, to join the HR Information Systems (HRIS) team, located in Phoenix, AZ. As an HRIS Business Analyst, you will have the opportunity to make a difference to On Semiconductor’s Cloud HR business processes and technology support management. This role involves working closely with the HR business partners, IT and Technology vendors on leading industry platforms, including Oracle Cloud HCM, Oracle Cloud Taleo Recruit, and other HR peripheral tools and applications that support HR operations.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>In this role you will:</div>
<ul>
<li>Perform the analysis and triage HRIS issue tickets to appropriate SMEs/Leads</li>
<li>
Supply thorough customer service, maintain positive client/customer relations, and work efficiently</li>
<li>
Contribute towards the HR work intake activities and help leadership with informed decision making, resourcing and escalation as appropriate</li>
<li>
Liaison with IT and Technology vendors on various HR business problem solutions, getting involved in compiling knowledge and research inputs for solution/enabler, getting product demo
</li>
<li>Learn Oracle Cloud HCM Core HR functionality and utilize the gained knowledge in</li>
<ul>
<li>
Providing support to the HR Business by partnering with them on understanding business requirements and challenges</li>
<li>
Analyze and identify options to support intended business outcomes</li>
<li>
Work closely with IT and technology vendors to ensure quality and timeliness of solution delivery</li>
<li>
Execute testing of new system configuration and integration</li>
<li>
Will lead integrity, accuracy and proper use of HR information retrieved from the systems, and acts as a liaison with the HR functional teams and IT team and supporting the HR team with production support and reporting requests</li>
</ul>
<li>
Collaborate with peers to identify opportunities for enterprise-wide improvements</li>
<li>
Continue to establish standard tools and practices that help increase availability, so as to focus on strategic opportunities</li>
<li>
Perform testing as needed during system upgrades, patches, or new system implementations</li>
<li>
Support additional HRIS initiatives, as needed</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>

<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree in Business, Information Technology or related field</li>
<li>
Must be a self-starter with the ability to manage multiple projects and priorities with urgent deadlines, maintaining excellent customer service skills, while working in a fast-paced team environment</li>
<li>
Ability to adapt to a flexible schedule</li>
<li>
1+ years professional experience in an IT environment and HR business processes is strongly preferred</li>
<li>
HR Data analysis experience desirable, should be skilled in researching and analyzing information related to HR</li>
<li>
Ability to take direction from multiple sources and communicate deltas appropriately</li>
<li>
Advanced PowerPoint and Advanced Excel skills preferred, Visio a plus</li>
<li>
Some exposure to project planning is preferred</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to influence critical business outcomes</li>
<li>
Strong influencing skills and the ability to interact with management</li>
<li>
Good written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Highly flexible and able to quickly adapt to changing priorities</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills, detail oriented and accurate</li>
<li>
Willingness and ability to learn new tools and applications</li>
</ul>
<div>ON Semiconductor (NASDAQ: ON) is driving energy efficient innovations, empowering customers to reduce global energy use. The company is a leading supplier of semiconductor-based solutions, offering a comprehensive portfolio of energy efficient power and signal management, logic, standard and custom devices. The company’s products help engineers solve their unique design challenges in <b>automotive, communications, computing, and consumer, industrial, medical and military/aerospace applications. </b>ON Semiconductor operates a responsive, reliable, world-class supply chain and quality program, and a network of manufacturing facilities, sales offices and design centers in key markets throughout North America, Europe and the Asia Pacific regions.</div>
