<h2>User Experience Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>The Role</b><p></p>
<p>Help us build experiences that empower people. If you’re passionate about people, front-end coding and innovating on the latest technology, come join us! We’re a collaborative team of user experience designers and researchers. Our team shapes the creative and user experience vision for our benefits administration platforms. We aim to provide an inclusive benefit enrollment experience that’s easy to use and helps people make educated benefit choices. Benefits are complex, always changing, and different from region to region. They’re also an essential part of most people’s lives, which is why our work is so important. That’s where you come in! We’re hoping you’ll join our UX team, and bring your passion for front-end development and desire to help people.</p>
<div>
<b>
Note: Employment-based non-immigrant visa sponsorship and/or assistance is not offered for this specific job opportunity.</b><p></p>
<p>As a User Experience Engineer, you’ll:</p>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop proof-of-concepts, and translate design artifacts into functional prototypes</li>
<li>Develop configuration and management processes to support ongoing sales demonstrations</li>
<li>Enhance site interactions with meaningful animations</li>
<li>Create reusable front-end components and libraries</li>
<li>Document and maintain design systems with code and use examples</li>
<li>Work closely with product, UX and development team members to produce and maintain code</li>
<li>Advocate for best practices and standards across the organization</li>
<li>Conduct accessibility and performance audits, and create resolution strategies</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Requirements
</b></p>
<p>We’re looking for someone who loves creative problem-solving. Innovation is encouraged, and you’ll have the opportunity to learn and recommend improvements as our product enters new markets. Strong communication skills are essential, as you are the liaison between our designers and developers. You should have experience and be willing to learn new things.</p>
<p>
Our technical goal is to provide usable and performant apps. But, we also want to make sure people understand and enroll in benefits that work well for them.</p>
<br>
<div>
<br>
As a User Experience Engineer, we ask that you have:</div>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience designing, developing, and maintaining production software systems</li>
<li>Expert semantic HTML ability</li>
<li>Proficiency in front-end development technologies, including JS and Angular/React, CSS and Sass</li>
<li>Proficiency in responsive design patterns and practices</li>
<li>Competency in cross-browser and device testing and debugging</li>
<li>Competency in website accessibility</li>
<li>Familiarity with Agile software development</li>
<li>Ability to work with co-located and remote collaborators</li>
<li>A college degree, or professional certificate equivalent, in Computer Science/Engineering, Communication, Digital Media, or related program.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Environment:</b></p>
<div>
We believe in a collaborative work environment and a culture that thrives on learning new things. Our company values user experience, and you’ll have the opportunity to collaborate locally with multiple engineering teams, as well as UX designers across our global organization. Your professional growth is highly valued, and our management team welcomes your feedback to assist you with your career development.</div>

<li>Flexible work schedule (work-life balance is important to us!)</li>
<li>Company provided coffee, tea, and snacks</li>
<li>Competitive compensation and benefit package</li>
</ul>
