<p><b>Position Summary</b><b>:</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>Serve existing clients by planning, managing and executing effective communications/marketing programs. (May also include supporting new clients as needs change.) Key day-to-day agency liaison responsible for supporting the development of creative solutions to meet established client objectives. Responsible for managing multiple projects across multiple tactics and client contacts, to ensure all deadlines are met and quality solutions delivered.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b><b>: </b>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform the following satisfactorily; other duties may be assigned. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>
Interact closely with clients</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and manage strong/positive relationships through hands-on account management which will include regular face-to-face meetings, telephone, and email communication.</li>
<li>Communicate the client's goals and represent the client's interests to the team.</li>
<li>Resolve conflicts effectively and guarantee compliance on client deliverables and budgets.</li>
</ul>
<p>Interact with internal team(s)</p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with strategists, writers, art directors, interactive designers, programmers, traffic/ production and account director to ensure that we deliver the highest quality work that reflects the client's objectives.</li>
<li>Handle campaign and project briefings properly, ensuring necessary creative details and/or project assignment details are provided.</li>
<li>Work with assigned creative lead to ensure deliverables meet the client's project objectives.</li>
<li>Gather, monitor and analyze performance data and support ongoing strategy on a monthly basis by client.</li>
<li>Ensure that client issues are dealt with in an efficient manner, informing the Account Director of any problems that may arise.</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Project management duties across campaigns; develop timelines and set deadlines for clients and the internal team.</li>
<li>Ensure account plans for each client are updated with all current and relevant information concerning the client and the campaign.</li>
<li>Actively seek opportunities to educate clients on services offered to increase the revenue generated in conjunction with cross-selling on existing projects.</li>
<li>Identify and cultivate new business opportunities from clients.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Own the estimating process; work with accounting to manage billing in a timely manner.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ensure that all processes and procedures are completed, quality standards are met, and projects are profitable.</li>
<li>Assist with special projects as assigned by the Account Director.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><br>
</p>
<p><b>Competencies:</b> To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following.</p>
<p><b>
Analytics - </b>Collects and researches data. Designs workflows and procedures. Identifies data relationships and dependencies. Synthesizes complex or diverse information. Uses intuition and experience to complement data.</p>
<p><b>
Communications</b> - Exhibits good listening and comprehension. Expresses ideas and thoughts in written form. Expresses ideas and thoughts verbally. Keeps others adequately informed. Selects and uses appropriate communication methods.</p>
<p><b>
Cooperation</b> - Displays a positive outlook and pleasant manner. Establishes and maintains effective relations. Exhibits tact and consideration. Offers assistance and support to co-workers. Works actively to resolve conflicts. Works cooperatively in group situations.</p>
<p><b>
Managing People</b> - Develops subordinates' skills and encourages growth. Includes subordinates in planning. Makes self-available to subordinates. Provides direction and gains compliance. Provides regular performance feedback. Takes responsibility for subordinates' activities.</p>
<p><b>
Planning and Organization</b> - Integrates changes smoothly. Plans for additional resources. Prioritizes and plans work activities. Sets goals and objectives. Uses time efficiently. Works in an organized manner.</p>
<p><b>
Problem Solving</b> - Develops alternative solutions. Gathers and analyzes information skillfully. Identifies problems in a timely manner. Resolves problems in early stages. Works well in group problem-solving situations.</p>
