<h2>Software Product Manager, Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The promise of a connected future requires increasingly complex networks that are capital intensive to build. Stonecrop Technologies, established in 2001, leverages software, mobile technology and supply chain expertise to reduce the cost and time required to develop, build and maintain telecommunications networks. Stonecrop continues to disrupt the way communications networks are deployed through a system of coordinated software applications and processes that align network design, supply chain, and installation. Operating a national integrated logistics network with nearly 2 million square-feet of distribution space across 90+ U.S. cities, our employees embody our company values: teamwork, innovation, and self-directed initiative.
<br><br>
As an Operations Product Manager, you will work with internal and external stakeholders to understand the needs of Stonecrop’s logistics and warehouse operations, identify software and process opportunities, and own the creation of a product vision and roadmap. You will work collaboratively with a Product Team to iteratively develop your solutions in a build-measure-learn framework. The ideal candidate will possess a unique blend of industry, business, and technical savvy; a big-picture vision, and the drive to make that vision a reality, while at the same time be able to jump into the weeds and develop clear, complete, concise business requirements.
<br><br>
The Operations Product Manager must enjoy spending time with customers and stakeholders to understand their problems and find innovative solutions for the broader market. You must be able to communicate with all areas of the company. You will also serve as the internal and external evangelist for your product offering, occasionally working with the sales channel and key customers.
<br><br>
Job Duties
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Own the Product Roadmap for your product area</li>
<li>Identify new product opportunities through user research and collaboration with internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>Evangelize your product vision both internally and with customers to gain support for your ideas</li>
<li>Work with internal stakeholders to map out operational processes, identify gaps, and develop process enhancements and software solutions to help the operations business run as efficiently as possible</li>
<li>Define and document product requirements and assist in the prioritization and timing of features from the product roadmap.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with engineering, product, QA, and business stakeholders to deliver products, features, and enhancements in an Agile environment</li>
<li>Own the development, deployment, and adoption of your products throughout the product life cycle</li>
</ul>

<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent practical experience</li>
<li>3+ years of experience defining product roadmaps and driving from conception through delivery</li>
<li>Experience modeling business processes using LucidCharts or similar tool</li>
<li>Familiarity with Logistics, Operations, Inventory Control, Supply Chain, or Warehousing</li>
<li>Excellent communication, collaboration, and teamwork skills</li>
<li>Advanced analytical, problem-solving and troubleshooting skills with proactive customer</li>
</ul>
service skills

<li>Ability to balance competing priorities</li>

<li>Deep understanding of Agile product development, including Scrum, Kanban</li>
<li>Knowledge of Product Management, Engineering, User Experience best practices</li>
<li>Experience leading through influence without direct authority</li>
</ul>
