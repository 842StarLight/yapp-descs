<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p><b>Senior Business Analyst</b></p>
<p>At our Company, we grow People, Brands, and Businesses! We are seeking a dedicated Senior Business Analyst to support the National Director of Retail Operations to make a discernible difference across operations with accurate and insightful data analysis. Demonstrate knowledge of and experience with software tools to create, edit, and analyze complex reports, graphs, and presentations. Be able to make sound decisions and the ability to take initiative and accountability for the accuracy of information. Independently take ownership of projects to completion by effectively coordinating all the necessary resources. Resolve complex, non-routine problems, and issues where no precedent has been established or exists with no supervision.</p>

<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>

<li><p>
Evaluate data/reports to stem insights showing value to client team.</p></li>
<li><p>
Spearhead communication between operations and client to provide adequate actionable direction resulting in positive results.</p></li>
<li><p>
Utilizing various data systems, extracts raw data from reporting system and conducts an in-depth analysis of customer product flow. Consults with internal business partners or external clients to present data and recommends actions to assist the client with meeting business objectives.</p></li>

Extracts data from third-party market research resources, integrates with internal data and conducts trend analysis. Interprets results and presents recommendations to the business partner.</p></li>

Works closely with business partners to develop report specifications based on client needs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Manages ad hoc projects based on business partner or client requests.</p></li>
<li><p>

</ul>
