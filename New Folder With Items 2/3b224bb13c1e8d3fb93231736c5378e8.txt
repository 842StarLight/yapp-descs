<h2>Transaction Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Phoenix Capital, Inc., a leading advisory firm to the mortgage banking industry is seeking a qualified Transaction Analyst to join our Transaction Management Division. This role will play an important part in Phoenix Capital’s transaction execution, including comparative analysis, industry research, and marketing processes. S/he will be responsible for supporting Transaction Managers, Portfolio Analysts, sales staff and executive management.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Pricing and comparative analysis of real-time Phoenix transactions; including regularly updating pricing models</li>
<li>Assist Transaction Managers in marketing process for portfolios of mortgage servicing rights (MSRs), whole loans, or M&amp;A transactions; including manipulation of loan data, PowerPoint presentations, and administrative functions</li>
<li>Prepare presentation material for client specific presentations and perform general industry analyses for marketing purposes (e.g. synthesize complex data, trends, analyses, etc. from a variety of sources and formats into thorough, but concise reporting for internal and external purposes)</li>
<li>Track and analyze industry transactions</li>
<li>Coordinate with our Analytics team to review MSR valuations pertaining to live transactions</li>
<li>Assist in various research projects</li>
<li>Other projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications, Knowledge, and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent written and verbal business communication as the Analyst will quickly become exposed to executive level audiences both internally and externally</li>
<li>Strong analytic abilities, financial aptitude, and attention to detail</li>
<li>Advanced Excel, database management and PowerPoint skills</li>
<li>Experience with Alteryx, SQL, or other advanced database management/reporting tools a plus</li>
<li>Capacity to learn new software programs including discounted cash flow modeling applications</li>
<li>Exceptional time management skills and ability to organize and prioritize tasks</li>
<li>Ability to perform under pressure</li>
<li>Comfortable with the challenges and opportunities that come with working for a smaller, merit-based, entrepreneurial organization</li>
<li>Residential secondary marketing experience (capital markets), and/or understanding of mortgage bond pricing and discounted cash flow analysis a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education &amp; Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Undergraduate and/or advanced degrees in Business, Finance, Economics, or MIS preferred</li>
<li>Mortgage banking experience, valuation software, etc. a plus but not required</li>
<li>2-5+ years’ experience preferred</li>
</ul>
