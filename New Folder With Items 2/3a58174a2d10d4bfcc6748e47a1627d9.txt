<h2>Payroll & Accounts Payable Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The Payroll &amp; Accounts Payable (PR/AP) Manager is responsible for managing the organization’s payroll and accounts payable functions. This position manages the payroll process, performs applicable PR/AP tasks, as well as manage the PR/AP staff.
</p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ensures timely and accurate processing of payroll and accounts payable(s).</li>
<li>
Assists in the development, implementation and maintenance of PR/AP systems, policies and procedures as applicable. Ensures applicable policies are adhered to.</li>
<li>
Responsible for managing wage allocations to the proper departments, including the maintenance of accurate information in the PR/AP systems. Coordinates changes with agency staff and management as needed.</li>
<li>
Monitors PR/AP accuracy and compliance to reflect audit standards.</li>
<li>
Responsible for monitoring the performance, training and development of PR/AP staff.</li>
<li>
Ensures payroll software is updated to reflect changes in payroll and benefits rules/policies.</li>
<li>
Reviews/analyzes payroll, benefits and tax procedures, implements necessary changes leading to best practices.</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Required Experience:<br>
<p><b>EDUCATION</b></p>
<ul><li>
Bachelor’s Degree in a business related field or equivalent experience.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
For attained Bachelor’s Degree 3 years’ experience required; for equivalent experience at least 5 years of experience required.</li>
<li>
Must have at least 3 years demonstrated supervisory experience.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of payroll methods and accounting principles.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Federal/State tax and Department of Labor regulations.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience in payroll and accounting systems.</li>
<li>
Experience with the Microsoft Suite (proficient in Excel and Word).</li>
<li>
Demonstrated organization skills, time management and multitasking in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>
Customer service/inter-personal skills experience.</li>
</ul>
