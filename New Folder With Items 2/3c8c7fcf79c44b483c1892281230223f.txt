<h2>Executive Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Relieves the executive of administrative type functions in order to increase the time an executive has available for executive level responsibilities. May handle a wide variety of situations and conflicts involving the clerical and administrative function of the office. Responsible for confidential and time sensitive material.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Receives, screens, and routes telephone calls, mail and correspondence. Sensitive to priorities and importance.</li>
<li>Arranges and schedules appointments, receives visitors, disseminates information pertaining to general company policies and procedures, and uses own judgment in handling routine matters in order to conserve principal's time.</li>
<li>With guidance and specific instruction, assists in compiling research as it pertains to the establishment of procedures for department projects and administrative support requirements. Collects, verifies, and calculates data from internal and external sources, and provides specific information to principal.</li>
<li>On own initiative and for items that require principal's signature, composes responses to correspondence and other memoranda which outline or explain administrative policies or procedures.</li>
<li>Handles details for maintenance of office functions such as ordering supplies and services, processing expense reports, and filing.</li>
<li>May be required to attend meetings and conferences to assist principal with administrative details.</li>
<li>Assists other principals or secretaries in area as requested, and may be called upon to perform any or all of the work assigned as regular duties for jobs in subordinate classifications.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Typically requires general education and/or vocational training plus 4 to 6 years of related experience.<br>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma or equivalent required.</li>
<li>Minimum of 65+ wpm and the ability to deal effectively with confidential matters.
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
