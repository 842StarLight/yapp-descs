<h2>Public Sector Sales Director - TOLA</h2>

<p><b>Applications Sales Director - Public Sector
</b></p>
<p>AST is seeking a <b>Public Sector, Applications Sales Director, </b>who is energized by solving problems and improving customers' lives. You'll collaborate with a talented and supportive team to continuously deliver new and exciting innovations to our clients. This key role will be an integral part of our national sales team.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate is highly adaptable, experienced and self-motivated. The position will need a leader who thrives in a very fast-paced, enriching environment and desires a meaningful sales career with excellent earning potential.</p>
<p><b>
What We're Looking For</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 10 years of experience selling into the Professional Services ecosystem in Public Sector.</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of SaaS solutions, including any of the following: Enterprise Resource Planning, Enterprise Performance Management, Customer Relationship Management or Business Intelligence.</li>
<li>
Track record of working with public sector customers and expanding a solution footprint by cross-selling.</li>
<li>
Capable of finding and developing new accounts</li>
<li>
Successful track record of meeting or exceeding sales targets/quotas</li>
<li>
Desire to work in a fast-paced environment with a diverse group of people</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently, take initiative with minimal supervision yet participate as a team member with a willingness to help where needed</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal, written communication, active listening and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Excellent presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Willingness to adapt to new projects quickly with a can-do, jump-right-in attitude</li>
<li>
The ideal candidate will sit in the greater Dallas area, though we will consider others within the TOLA area.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What You Will Be Doing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Selling technology (non Saas) products and services directly into strategic accounts or via partners into medium and large sized accounts</li>
<li>
Working to achieve maximum sales profitability, growth, and account penetration within an assigned territory. Territory will be geography and product based.</li>
<li>
Selling to the territory directly or via the Partner ecosystem</li>
<li>
Managing accounts throughout the entire sales process, and collaborating with team members in marketing, Pre-sales and delivery support to uncover all customer needs</li>
<li>
Traveling to customer sites to identify / developing sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Participating in the development, presentation, and sales of a value proposition</li>
<li>
Providing onsite customer presentations with C-levels</li>
<li>
Liaising with customer contacts for the purpose of managing the customer relationship, identifying new opportunities and maximizing sales</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What We Bring</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You'll work alongside gifted professionals, who are great and collaborating and sharing ideas.</li>
<li>
Competitive salary, great benefits (medical, vision, dental), 401K.</li>
<li>
Access to AST Academy, so you can sharpen your skills in tech and management.</li>
<li>
Room to grow. We want our team to thrive in their careers at AST. You invest your time and talent with us and we'll invest in you.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Why Join Us?</b> AST is a global technology brand with a long history of creating innovative solutions as a systems integrator. Our team has the outstanding ability to motivate, inspire, and collaborate, delivering award-winning results to our clients. At AST, we're lifelong learners, who tackle challenges together and win together. If you are a smart, driven person, who thrives on taking technology to the next level, you've come to the right place.</p>
