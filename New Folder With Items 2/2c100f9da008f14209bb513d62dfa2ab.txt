<h2>Wound Care Specialist (RN/LPN/PT/PTA Required) Las Vegas, NV</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Exciting opportunity available for a Nurse or Physical Therapist with wound care experience</b><b>. </b></p>
<p>American Medical Technologies (AMT), a progressive company in the senior care setting, is the leading independent provider of wound care solutions for long term care (LTC) facilities in the United States. Servicing qualifying residents in 6250 LTC facilities, AMT is building on this success by developing new programs, products and services to improve the quality of care in the senior care market. With this exciting new direction, AMT is recruiting individuals who want to be a part of a high growth company focused on becoming the leader in senior care.</p>
<p><b>Job Description, Wound Care Specialist/Account Manager </b></p>
<p>AMT has high expectations for our team of professionals. We look for candidates with a strong work ethic, an outgoing attitude, good communication skills, high energy, the ability to work independently, and strong skills in selling and influencing decision-makers. AMT also wants candidates to be a good fit in the area of senior care, with a customer-centric approach and the clinical and professional expertise to make a significant contribution to both our customers and our company. Strengths in these AMT core values translates into excellent job performance and results in a successful career with AMT.</p>
<p><b>Job duties include</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Scheduling rounds with assigned LTC facilities to facilitate timely ordering of eligible products</li>
<li>Providing education to LTC staff on appropriate product utilization</li>
<li>Selling, securing and retaining new business in the LTC arena</li>
</ul>
<p>Job performance is measured by customer satisfaction, volume of business managed and superior execution of the AMT vision: <b>“Improving the quality of life of senior care patients through innovative and value based programs to support their long term care needs across all settings.”</b></p>
<p><b>Requirements for consideration include: </b></p>
<ul>

<li>Experience in wound care</li>
<li>LPN/LVN/PTA or higher clinical designation</li>
</ul>
<p>Additionally, special consideration will be afforded those with wound care certification and experience with Microsoft office and medical record software.</p>
