<div>
Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) are responsible for identifying and creating new qualified sales opportunities in Target Accounts for their regional Account Executives. The SDR will become a trusted resource and develop relationships with prospects, acting as the initial point of contact. This role will be primarily focused around researching the Target Accounts in your territory and performing outreach through emails, calls, and social media. You will have the opportunity to be closely mentored and professionally developed by more seasoned enterprise sales professionals.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Promoting and marketing Bluecore's business through extensive and persistent cold calling, emails and communication(s) to prospective clients</li>
<li>
Scheduling Initial Prospecting Meetings for the Sales Team</li>
<li>
Providing in depth information to the Sales team to enable the most qualified and productive demos</li>
<li>
Researching and evaluating additional leads/prospects through web research</li>
<li>
Meet or exceed assigned quota of a minimum of 40 calls / 30 emails per day</li>
<li>
Working together with the Sales Director to grow a specific sales territory</li>
<li>
Working with the Sales Director to support the acquisition of new accounts in the region</li>
<li>
Help as needed to build awareness on special projects / events</li>
<li>
Maintaining all contacts/opportunities and recording all progress in Salesforce</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
