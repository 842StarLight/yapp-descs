<h2>Mortgage Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Salal Credit Union is looking for a Mortgage Advisor to join our team! Work from home benefits!</b></h1>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Read about the primary position duties of this position...</b></h1>
<p>
Receive sales leads from branch employees and other departments. Serve as the liaison between back-office mortgage lending staff and members by providing excellent communication between all parties. Establish and develop outside contacts with organizations, realtors, lenders, and other affinity sources to market mortgage loan services to increase lending market share with quality loan volume. Connect with coworkers, members and/or external customers by providing friendly and efficient service while working together to solve problems quickly.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
<b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<p>
Assist branch staff and members with mortgage questions and inquiries.</p>
<p>
Outreach to existing members via phone or e-mail for sales opportunities.</p>
<p>
Follow-up on assigned mortgage referrals from branch staff and other departments, and work to develop leads into loan applications.</p>
<p>
Meet with members at various branch locations and report to the corporate office for meetings as requested.</p>
<p>
Collection of documentation and completion of assigned loan applications. Review new mortgage applications to ensure compliance and accuracy.</p>
<p>
Monitor mortgage rates daily, communicate with members regarding rate locks, and submit rate lock requests to back-office staff.</p>
<p>
Manage loan pipeline to ensure progress of all loans and monitor all rate locks.</p>
<p>
Communicate underwriting decisions to borrowers.</p>
<p>
Expedite loans in process by regularly reviewing pipeline. Correspond with borrowers, back-office team, and other third-parties throughout transaction to ensure a smooth and transparent loan process.</p>
<p>
Establish and develop outside contacts to promote mortgage loan referrals that meet established Salal Credit Union and investor guidelines.</p>
<p>
Complete all education, training and professional development required for position.</p>
<p>
Use discretion and independent judgment in all areas of planning, implementation, communication, education, selling and follow-up of mortgage affinity business development activities.</p>
<p>
Demonstrate product knowledge and meet annual product and service certification requirements. Enter identification into credit reporting software to generate credit reports as needed. Required to communicate in writing and create collection letters and management reports.</p>
<p>
Proactively refer members and prospective members to Personal Financial Representatives and Member Service Representatives by marketing all Credit Union products and services as opportunities are identified.</p>
<p>
Establish and develop outside contacts with organizations, realtors, lenders, and other affinity sources to market mortgage loan services to increase lending market share with quality loan volume.</p>
<p>
Provide exceptional customer service by maintaining thorough knowledge of lending programs, policies, procedures and regulatory requirements. Demonstrate a commitment to professional ethics in the performance of all lending activities and ensures compliance with all Federal and State regulations.</p>
<p>
Comply with all state and federal regulations and compliance standards, including: Fair Lending, Regulation X &amp; Regulation Z, Identify Theft Protection Plan ITPP, Bank Secrecy Act BSA, Customer Information Privacy CIP, Office of Foreign Assets Control OFAC as well as Credit Union policies and procedures. Complete required compliance training.</p>
<p>
Ensure required records are retained in accordance with the Salal Records Retention Program and preserved in a careful and secure manner.</p>
<p>
Use network PC workstation to access core system software to perform account queries, and specialized functions for accounts as needed. Utilize all Internet service delivery systems and Microsoft Office Suite to perform duties as needed. Operate and maintain department equipment and troubleshoot as needed.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>
Previous Experience: </b>One year of financial industry or related sales experience required. Mortgage industry experience preferred.</p>
<p><b>
Education: </b>High School Diploma or GED. Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience preferred.</p>
<p><b>
Demonstrated Abilities:</b> Must be self-motivated to meet job expectations and goals. Must be eager to learn and able to thrive in a fast-paced environment. Express thoughts clearly and concisely, both verbally and in writing. Build strong relationships with affinity sources. Work independently and meet deadlines with minimal supervision and the capability to work in a team environment as needed. Interact positively with co-workers, management and the public. Market products and services in a manner which is responsive and professional. Working knowledge of federal and state consumer lending regulations. Evaluate verification of income records, collateral valuation and purchase contracts. Demonstrate leadership skills and abilities. Listen to cues and ask proactive questions to select appropriate product or service to refer.
</p>
<p><b>Physical Requirements:</b> Sitting or standing continuously for periods of four hours, communicating verbally, communicating in writing, faculty of seeing and hearing and operating a keyboard. Lifting and carrying usually not to exceed 20 pounds.</p>
<p><b>
Mental Requirements:</b> Interpersonal interactions, problem solving, analyzing/evaluating, decision making, discretion/confidentiality, math skills, teamwork, read/write/speak English, creativity and task handling.</p>
<p><b>
Working Conditions:</b> Work is performed in an office environment but also requires mobility within the office. May be required to travel to other Credit Union facilities and various locations for job related training, meetings and events.
