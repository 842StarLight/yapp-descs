<h2>Market Executive, Middle Market Banking</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Great opportunity to join our Global Commercial Banking Team! The Bank of America Southern California Global Commercial Banking market is seeking a Market Executive (ME) to maintain and grow our industry leading market share position within the Southern California marketplace. The Middle Market segment of the Global Commercial Bank serves public and private companies with annual revenues of $50 million to $2 billion. The position will office in either Newport Beach or San Diego.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Market Executive (ME) functions as the market leader with responsibility for revenue and profitability, developing and executing the market strategy, client satisfaction, coaching and inspecting the Client Management Process, and hiring and developing talent. The majority of the ME’s time is divided among three areas: coaching and developing relationship managers (RMs) and client teams; client meetings and events; developing and executing the market strategy, in collaboration with the market leadership team. The Southern California ME is responsible for the San Diego, Newport Beach and Inland Empire offices.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Scope of Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
Reports to the Pacific Southwest Regional Executive</li>
<li>
Manages a team of Relationship Managers and Commercial Associates</li>
<li>
Invests time with the market’s clients, prospects, and key referral sources</li>
<li>
Is responsible for strategically allocating resources in the market</li>
<li>
Has direct accountability for the optimal allocation of Relationship Managers and collaborates with the market leadership team to determine the optimal allocation of credit products, treasury solutions, and sales support resources to capitalize on the market opportunity</li>
<li>
Manages the team’s pipeline of deals and facilitates hand-offs between members of the client teams</li>
<li>
Continually enhances team performance through effective recruiting, coaching, development, and performance management activities</li>
<li>
Is an integral member of the Pacific Southwest leadership team and develops strong relationships the leaders of the business and key support partners and other MEs</li>
<li>
At times, the ME may lead or participate in enterprise projects or initiatives</li>
<li>
Is also expected to be the “face of the bank” to the business community within their market</li>
</ul>
