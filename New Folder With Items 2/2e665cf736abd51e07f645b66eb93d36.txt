<h2>Talent Acquisition Partner (MA)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li><div>Manage full-cycle recruiting from sourcing and scheduling through to hire across a variety of departments (Sales, Engineering, Marketing, Support, G&amp;A).</div></li>
<li><div>
Implement and execute ongoing sourcing strategies that help keep the team ahead of our hiring goals.</div></li>
<li><div>
Partner with hiring managers to establish best practices and set expectations for each open position.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide weekly reports outlining the status of each position.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide quarterly reports that increase organizational transparency for the TA team.</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage our applicant tracking system, making sure that all candidate information and activity is properly logged.</div></li>
<li><p>
Partner with the Sr. Manager of TA to continuously improve our process, efficiency, candidate and hiring manager experiences and employer brand.</p></li>
</ul>

What is Litmus looking for in a candidate?</b></p>
<ul>
<li><div>
3+ years in a full-cycle recruiting position (corporate or agency).</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong organizational skills and attention to detail. You are process oriented and pride yourself on not letting anything fall through the cracks!</div></li>
<li><div>
Excellent listening skills. You really hear what it is the hiring managers need and have engaging and meaningful conversations with candidates that enable you to determine their level of fit.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong sense of urgency. You understand how important it is to keep the process moving and to keep the lines of communication open.</div></li>
<li><p>
High level of professionalism. You are excited about representing Litmus and being an ambassador for our employer brand though every candidate interaction</p></li>
</ul>
