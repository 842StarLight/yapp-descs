<h2>Deliver Manager-Florida</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Do</b></h2>
<p>
In this key client facing role, you will transform our customer engagements by advising and collaborating on network and IT outcomes. You will ensure excellent service delivery and growth through your ability to develop trusted advisor relationships across our client’s organization.</p>
<p>
Cisco's Global Enterprise Customer Experience group is seeking a consultative and experienced Services Delivery Leader to inspire a diverse, cross-functional team to achieve delivery excellence and profitable revenue growth in CX</p>
<p>
You will develop an internal delivery staff, providing direction and support on client deliverables, quality assurance, and coaching/mentoring on services solutions and client interaction. In this role, you will be required to travel 50% of the time to support client delivery engagements and client value creation activities.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Job Responsibilities Include:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
You will lead a successful customer delivery operation, ensuring quality and realization of customer outcomes.</li>
<li>
A large portion of your responsibilities will entail understanding delivery risks, issues, escalations and fine-tune project and program execution.</li>
<li>
You will also align and scale customer experience delivery resources to enable customer business priorities.</li>
<li>
You execute upon customer contract to achieve customer objectives</li>
<li>
You are capable of driving workload utilization of delivery resources through appropriate skillset mapping and customer alignment.</li>
<li>
You own demand and capacity management to ensure excellence in our customer delivery</li>
<li>
Finding new opportunities for portfolio delivery expansion and efficiency.</li>
<li>
Promote and drive global delivery through global centers</li>
<li>
Enable for Professional Services revenue growth, new deal development</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who You'll Work With</b></h2>
<p><b><i>
"At Cisco, everything starts with our customers, and our Customer Experience organization exists to ensure they are consistently having the best possible experience with our technology."</i></b><b> - </b>Chuck Robbins, CEO of Cisco</p>
<p>
You will work with our top-notch executives and IT talent in our Global Enterprise Segment, and partner with Cisco’s brightest talent in Customer Experience, sales, operations and engineering working on the state-of-the-art solution’s Cisco is taking to market for customers who are redefining their businesses.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who You Are</b></h2>
<p>
You should have a background as a seasoned Services General Manager with 10-15 years of delivery experience. You have experience in vertical industry roles developing strategic opportunities; contributing to the vertical practice and interacting with similar colleagues in other theaters through engagement participation and sharing of and knowledge.</p>
<p>
You are a self-starter who is driven to ensure customers are adopting and receiving the best value for the Services they have purchased and when you see challenges, you naturally find opportunities to help a customer enhance and grow their business through new services solutions. You like to stretch your capabilities and positively disrupt the environment you are in. You have a keen ability to communicate the strategic value Cisco Services brings to our customers, and are able to share the message across all customer levels and within our sales organization. Creating a bridge between our customer requirements and services opportunities is your natural communication style.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Additional Skills Required:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Driving Collaboration across Functions. You are able to utilize resources across the company, partners, consultants and the customer to drive success</li>
<li>
Executive Relationship Management &amp; Presence. You achieve results in a fast-changing environment, through influence and communication at the most senior levels in an organization</li>
<li>
Relationship Management. You are highly focused on building and managing key customer and Cisco relationships to help drive greater customer intimacy and business alignment.</li>
<li>
Business and Financial Acumen. Your success depends on your knowledge, insight, and understanding of business concepts, tools, and processes for making sound decisions. Strong P&amp;L Management</li>
<li>
Operational Excellence. You drive Operational Excellence in service delivery execution and management of account level profitability through your ability to manage numerous projects and senior partners concurrently. Additionally, you have experience deploying consulting methodology and project management.</li>
<li>
Innovation. You continuously keep innovative thought processes and ideas at the forefront of service delivery execution and service solutions</li>
</ul>
