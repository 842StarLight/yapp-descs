<p><b>Role: </b></p>
<p>Burner is searching for a Marketing Coordinator who will serve as a dedicated marketing, data, and content powerhouse across the companies Sales and Customer Success teams. From time to time, the Marketing Coordinator may manage assigned accounts in the role of an accounts manager. The ideal candidate will be a well-organized multitasker with a passion for assisting others in the workgroup and contributing to the team's success.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage annual budget to optimize dollars and drive results</li>
<li>Work with external partners (media partners, vendors, agencies and freelance designers) managing multiple projects and work streams simultaneously</li>
<li>Work with internal cross-departmental leadership to support key events and programming</li>
<li>Writing copy for and designing presentations, proposals and websites</li>
</ul>
