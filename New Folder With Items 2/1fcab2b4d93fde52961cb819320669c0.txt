<h2>Business System Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Role: </b> Business System Analyst - Wholesale</p>
<p><b>Team Lead: </b> Senior Manager of Business Applications</p>
<p>The Business Systems Analyst is responsible for partnering with business teams to support process improvement with technology solutions that drive our growth and objectives. The Business Systems Analyst will be responsible for supporting, maintaining, and training on BlueCherry and other enterprise systems. The Business Systems Analyst will be responsible for following the Software Development Life Cycle to assist the business in testing and implementing the latest technology solutions.</p>
<p><b>You can expect to…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor support queue, provide first line support, and work with internal technical resources and 3rd party software vendors to provide technical solutions for system issues</li>
<li>Support, maintain and monitor all electronic transactions, ensuring efficient functioning of EDI and compliance of all EDI communication.</li>
<li>Serve as the main point of contact for EDI related inquiries; troubleshoot issues stemming from utility errors; address inquiries from trading partners and communicate to other IT teams and business users.</li>
<li>Conduct application troubleshooting</li>
<li>Seek to understand business processes and problems, and offer solutions based on leveraging systems, processes, and data flow</li>
<li>Maintain and execute detailed test plans for unit, system, and user acceptance testing</li>
<li>Partner with the business to translate business requirements into functional/technical specifications</li>
<li>Create and maintain training programs and documentation as needed</li>
<li>Take on ad-hoc projects as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>To ensure success you must be…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Apparel industry experience preferred</li>
<li>Detailed understanding of product development business processes and workflow</li>
<li>Detailed understanding of merchandising business processes and workflow</li>
<li>Retail, eCommerce, &amp; Wholesale</li>
<li>Understanding of merchandising and product development business reporting requirements</li>
<li>Costing, Sales Audit</li>
<li>Detailed understanding of Fulfillment Center processes and workflow specifically relating to retail and wholesale inventory.</li>
<li>Understanding of Accounting process and workflow</li>
<li>Sales Audit, Week-end/Month-end/Year-end closings, &amp; Physical inventory</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal communication, effectively receiving and communicating feedback</li>
<li>Ability to plan, manage time, and make decisions in a fast-paced environment with high accuracy and attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to adapt well to change in direction and priority in a fast-paced and deadline-oriented environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>To join our team you must have…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Technology related field or experience equivalent a plus</li>
<li>2 – 3 years of business systems analyst experience in a corporate environment</li>
<li>Experience with data manipulation in Excel is required</li>
<li>2 – 3 years of experience supporting Blue Cherry, JDA MMS or other large ERP systems is required</li>
<li>Experience in the use of and support of EDI transactions</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills with the ability to manage small and medium-sized projects from the requirements stage through post go live support</li>
<li>Experience working with third party software vendors</li>
<li>Ability to identify issues with business process, suggest alternate solutions, and properly document changes in process</li>
<li>Experience in migration projects, UAT, parallel testing, and regression testing</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $90,000.00 to $130,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>ERP: 3 years (Required)</li>
<li>EDI transactions: 3 years (Required)</li>
</ul>
