<p><b>Position Summary: </b>The Marketing Product Leader is a key role that supports the overall Lucifer Lighting team, working closely with senior leadership to create marketing plans, technical collateral, and concept level product designs and prototypes. An ideal candidate is articulate, passionate and creative.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Planning</b>
<ul>
<li>Work with EVP to create plans for how marketing collateral will support product launches, managing and generating marketing collateral across platforms (print and digital)</li>
<li>Partner with Engineering and Product Support to ensure technical product information is always current
</li>
<li>Manage Marketing GANTTs</li>
<li>Act as liaison between the EVP and Sales/ Marketing/ Product Development</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Creating</b>
<ul>
<li>Work with VP of Product to create photometric charts, diagrams, and other materials as requested</li>
<li>Work with VP of Product to create concept CAD models, assemblies and renderings.</li>
<li>Work with Product Support and Engineering to create installation guides
</li>
<li>Load and program CPQ and website with current information (product, pricing, documentation, etc.)
</li>
<li>Modify existing and create new webpages</li>
<li>Create, layout and disseminate technical marketing collateral including, new product e-blasts, and other supporting collateral (webinars, sales)</li>
<li>Produce and create training courses related to product, brand, history, etc.</li>
<li>Produce content for internal presentations</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Supporting</b>
<ul>
<li>Help the team as required relating to events (tradeshows, factory trips, etc.)</li>
<li>Maintain contacts in Salesforce CRM</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Intermediate to expert abilities in Microsoft Office Suite</li>
<li>Intermediate to expert abilities in parametric modeling software (SolidWorks preferred, Pro-E, etc.)</li>
<li>Knowledge of manufacturing processes including but not limited to Die Casting, Machined parts, Injection molding and extrusions.
</li>
<li>Experience in product development industries. Lighting preferred, aerospace, consumer electronics, oil/gas is acceptable.
</li>
<li>Experience in Adobe Creative Suite and 3D animation and video editing software a plus
</li>
<li>Very strong oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Fast-paced, driven and energetic</li>
<li>Independent, self-starter</li>
<li>Experience in the lighting application and theory a plus. Working knowledge of IES files, Reflected Ceiling Plans, etc.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>

</b></p>
<ul>

</li>
<li>4 years’ experience performing work described herein</li>
<li>Proficiency in HTML /CSS required, proficiency in Python/JavaScript preferred</li>
</ul>
