<h2>Business Development Director (MSB's) - Regulatory Compliance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The position of Business Development Director (BDD), reporting to the RCG Director of Sales, is responsible for <b>organic business development within Money Service Businesses (MSB’s.)</b> The role is primarily an inside sales position although outside face-to-face sales activity may be required. Included in their responsibilities are: sales lead prospecting; evaluation of business opportunities; coordination of sales support and company efforts with prospects; development of proposals and sales contracts; and closing net new business opportunities to meet or exceed targeted goals. The BDD will be expected to accurately present the full suite of CSI products and services.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities Include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Identification and development of new sales opportunities within MSB territory</b></li>
<li>Development of business plan including targeted accounts, goals, and strategies</li>
<li>Management of individual sales prospects from initial contact through contract execution</li>
<li>Preparation of detailed pricing (quotes), sales proposals, contracts, and other documents</li>
<li>Planning and leadership of the efforts of company resources in support of sales efforts</li>
<li>Continuous pipeline management and accountability for the status of all sales prospects within territory using Microsoft Dynamics CRM</li>
<li>Continuous objective evaluation of the viability of individual sales prospects; utilizing CSI sales process (qualification, diagnostic / scoping calls, price negotiation)</li>
<li>Disengaging as early as possible from prospects unlikely to result in a sale</li>
<li>Participation in industry trade events as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Minimum of 5 years’ financial services/software or regulatory compliance sales experience (including full sales cycle responsibilities)
</b></li>
<li><b>Working knowledge of regulatory compliance/information security services/enterprise risk management is required
</b></li>
<li>Software, technology, professional services sales experience is a plus</li>
<li>A Bachelor's degree is required in addition to relevant experience</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Consultative, solution-selling skills with demonstrated ability to penetrate high-profile corporations</li>
<li>Aptitude for technology, business process, and analysis tools</li>
<li>Executive presence and business acumen to influence senior decision-makers</li>
<li>Ability to focus on personal quotas, activities and strategy while engaging with other team members</li>
<li>Required travel as needed</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
