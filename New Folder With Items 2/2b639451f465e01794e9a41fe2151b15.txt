<h2>Seeking Clean Air Act (CAA) Program Manager / Leader / Expert</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Cornerstone Environmental, Health and Safety, Inc. in Zionsville, IN was founded in 1990 with the mission of delivering innovative and sustainable solutions for protecting human health and the environment. Our team focuses on assisting industrial and manufacturing clients in adhering with environmental and safety regulations that protect the environment, their workers and their community.
<br>
<br>
For the position of Clean Air Act (CAA) Program Manager, we are seeking a highly motivated individual with a passion for regulatory compliance who is also driven to improve the environmental performance of client companies and to lead / mentor / grow a staff of like-minded individuals.
<br>
<br>
Primary Responsibilities:
<ul>
<li>Manage daily operations of Air Program department (supervise, mentor and grow the knowledge and skills of team members)</li>
<li>
Consult with clients on air quality compliance and permitting issues, including regulatory analyses of complex industrial operations; major and minor source air permit applications, and state-level regulations</li>
<li>
Provide guidance to clients regarding timing and strategy of production and control equipment installation</li>
<li>
Work with agencies to negotiate acceptable conditions related to enforcement actions and ensure the client’s best interests are represented</li>
<li>
Oversee management and delivery of all Air Program projects to meet or exceed client expectations as well as internal goals established by Cornerstone management</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Director of Environmental Services to drive continual improvement activities, ensure quality objectives are achieved, and increase department productivity / efficiency</li>
<li>
Analyze Air Program operational metrics and financial performance of business unit</li>
</ul>Requirements (must have):·
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or higher in engineering or a scientific field is required</li>
<li>
8 – 10+ years background in air permitting and compliance (consulting experience in the focus areas above is a strong plus)</li>
<li>
Extensive experience in preparation and submittal of air permit applications as well as negotiating permit conditions with regulatory agencies</li>
<li>
Experience in project management related to stack testing / performance testing</li>
<li>
Able to audit various manufacturing operations to determine air compliance status</li>
<li>
High degree of computer literacy; proven proficiency in learning and using software (Outlook, Word, Excel, Salesforce and other mission critical applications)</li>
</ul>Additional Qualifications:
<ul>
<li>Excellent organizational skills, and a goal-oriented approach to task completion</li>
<li>
Experience interacting with governmental agencies, at the state and Federal level</li>
<li>
Able to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic work environment</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of state and federal for other environmental regulations, and the ability to research and understand environmental regulations and apply them to client situations</li>
<li>
Air modeling experience is a plus</li>
</ul>Cornerstone offers an excellent benefits package including:
<ul>
<li>Competitive compensation (which is commensurate with experience)</li>
<li>
Health and dental insurance</li>
<li>
401k program available</li>
<li>
Minimal travel (with overnight travel being the exception)</li>
</ul>Please note: this position does not include selling as a part of its responsibilities since Cornerstone has a separate, dedicated sales staff.
