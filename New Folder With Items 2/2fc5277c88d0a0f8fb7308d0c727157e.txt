About the Job</b></p>
<div>
FRESH BEGINNINGS is looking for a high-energy, results-oriented Marketing Coordinator to join our growing, dynamic team. The Marketing Coordinator will have well-rounded experience in and/or strong understanding of all facets of marketing including digital marketing, SEO strategies, and the ability to fully utilize the benefits of email campaigns, blogs, and social media platforms. In this role, you will be focused on delivering creative marketing strategy, creating strong communication between marketing, graphics, and the sales teams, and building solutions to increase business and traffic on the web, within the Advertising Specialty segment, and searching out additional marketing avenues for our products.</div>
