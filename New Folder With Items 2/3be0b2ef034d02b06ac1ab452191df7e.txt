<h2>Sr. Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Sr. Program Manager role is multi-faceted with strong interaction with multiple Engineering disciplines including Systems, Electrical, Mechanical, and Software design teams as well as Manufacturing, Repair, Design, and Quality functions across the broader organization in support of multiple concurrent projects. It is essential that the candidate has a power electronic systems background and significant customer interfacing and presentation skills.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>In this role the incumbent’s responsibilities will include, but not be limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Planning</b>: Develop plans, schedules, and budgets and identify resources required on assigned programs or projects. Identify risk areas and develop contingency plans to eliminate or mitigate risk that could result in schedule delays or failure to meet technical specification or schedule requirements.</li>
<li>
<b>Management:</b> Coordinate the activities of Engineering, Materials, Manufacturing, Quality Assurance and other personnel to ensure that all technical and delivery requirements expected by our customers are met on assigned programs or projects.</li>
<li>
<b>Communication</b>: Interface with customers on matters such as program issues, schedules, and budgets. Coordinate all correspondence with appropriate AI2 personnel (e.g. President, Engineering, Sales, etc.) and in a way that maintains our customer’s confidence in AI2.</li>
<li>
<b>Production</b>: Monitor and manage all production deliverables assigned to engineering and ensure timely delivery of products. Work with appropriate engineering staff to ensure products are released from Engineering to Manufacturing in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
<b>Budget</b>: Manage the opening, execution, and closing of engineering budgets in a way consistent with AI2’s accounting systems, with the goal of ensuring costs are gathered accurately on each engineering project.</li>
<li>
<b>Continuous Improvement: </b>Take the initiative to research, develop, propose, evaluate and/or implement new methods, processes, procedures, or tools that would improve the quality, timeliness, or cost effectiveness of products or services provided to AI2’s customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<p>The successful candidate will have a background which includes the following demonstrable experience, skills and education:</p>
<ul>
<li>B.S.E.E. (or related technical degree) required; M.S.E.E. (or advanced technical or business degree) preferred</li>
<li>A minimum of 10 years of related technical and/or management experience</li>
<li>PMP certification preferred</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership, managerial, and organizational skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to develop, articulate, and present technical or business information appropriately to targeted audience (including technical and non-technical) and at all business levels in written, verbal, and presentation forms.</li>
<li>Design background in power electronics, including power supplies, magnetics, and control theory, is desired.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Authorities:</b></p>
<p>Ensure compliance with applicable processes and procedures during the course of daily job function</p>
<p><b>Physical Demands:</b></p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is occasionally required to stand, walk, sit, use hands to finger, reach with arms and hands, climb stairs, stoop, kneel, crouch, talk or hear. The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 30 lbs.</p>
<p><b>Work Environment:</b></p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is exposed to weather conditions prevalent at the time and an air-conditioned and heated office. The noise level is occasionally high.</p>
