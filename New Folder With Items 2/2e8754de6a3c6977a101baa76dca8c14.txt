<h2>Director Data Intelligence, Strategy and Capabilities, Oncology</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>AstraZeneca’s vision in Oncology is to help patients by redefining the cancer-treatment paradigm, with the aim of bringing six new cancer medicines to patients between 2013 and 2020. A broad pipeline of next generation medicines is focused principally on four disease areas - breast, ovarian, lung and hematological cancers. As well as other tumor types, these are being targeted through four key platforms: immunotherapy, the genetic drivers of cancer and resistance, DNA damage repair, and antibody drug conjugates, underpinned by personalized healthcare and biomarker technologies.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Data Enablement &amp; Capabilities Lead role combines the role of Strategy, Marketing &amp; Execution of the capabilities. Lead role is accountable for developing and implementing an emerging capability to deliver the infrastructure needed and the execution of complex, actionable, big-data analytics that support strategic, closed-loop marketing efforts (Customer, Consumer &amp; Digital) across US Oncology Portfolio (Women’s Franchise, IO Franchise, Hematology Franchise, Lung Franchise). The role will use strong analytical and critical thinking skills to development relevant strategy and communication channels based on the US Oncology commercial strategy across 4 franchise. Keys to success in this position are superlative communication, consultative and influencing skills predicated on a strong foundation of data analysis proficiency, Clinical acumen, unconventional Marketing strategy &amp; emerging technologies like Big data, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning etc.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Accountabilities/Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop a vision for Data driven execution and aligns and engages individuals around the vision</li>
<li>Brings an innovative and future focused approach to work; willing to experiment to drive impact to the top line</li>
<li>Support brand teams in the development of strategy, especially the use of secondary data and analytics to support business goals, and high-level design of programmatic needs</li>
<li>Provide brand and market evaluation, interpretation, and communication of results synthesizing key data and insights spanning a host of different sources and types of data</li>
<li>Leverage Oncology experience to engage with brand and medical stakeholders to derive and visualize patient journeys , HCP Network, using primary &amp; secondary data</li>
<li>Develop, deliver and execute a trigger platform, leading a matrix team with representation from across the Commercial organization, including marking science, marketing, and sales operations</li>
<li>Identify and implement technology solution to integrate triggers with the sales force execution platform and the marketing channels</li>
<li>Drive implementation of the sales team facing tools that provide insight into the customer behavior derived from secondary data</li>
<li>Drive customer behavior analytics to understand referral networks, treatment influencers, KOLs, scientific leadership and publications</li>
<li>Create insightful reports and presentations that clearly and succinctly highlight the actionable insights combined with business implications/strategic &amp; tactical options</li>
<li>Identify business needs proactively and build analysis with foresight</li>
<li>Keep abreast on key Oncology trends and best practices to help solve challenges and capitalize on opportunities</li>
<li>Develop and maintain budget to support data driven execution deliverables</li>
<li>Maintain effective relationships with contractors and other third-party vendors through oversight of the vendor management process</li>
<li>Leverage/apply AZ insight best practices as appropriate</li>
</ul>
