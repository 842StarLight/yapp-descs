<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>We’re a technology company working in the loyalty e-commerce industry. Our solutions enhance the management and monetization of loyalty currencies for more than 50 of the world’s largest loyalty brands, from frequent flyer miles and hotel points to retailer and credit card rewards. Supported by our unparalleled loyalty industry experience and technological expertise, we bring state-of-the-art loyalty commerce platforms and products to individuals and businesses in today’s loyalty marketplace.</p>
<p>
Our casual, collaborative office is where our strong workplace culture begins. Our people are what make us great, so we empower them with the freedom to think big and the resources to make things happen. We communicate directly, lead by example, and make sure our team members know how much they are appreciated. Passion for life and work is important to us, and we want to see it in you, too!</p>
<p>
Points is looking for a Product Manager to join our Product Management team for a permanent position in our downtown San Francisco office.</p>
<p>
We’re an industry-leading organization that is continuously reshaping how consumers interact with their loyalty programs. We work with the world’s largest airline, hotel, financial, and retail rewards programs, to tackle complex challenges and come up with innovative e-commerce solutions. If you’d like to be a part of it, we’d love to hear from you.</p>
<p>
We’re looking for a smart &amp; enthusiastic person with a passion for working with our software engineering team to join Points. As a Product Manager, you will be responsible for executing product strategy to bring world-class solutions from conception to delivery. You will aggregate customer requirements, prioritize new capabilities, build product roadmaps and manage the lifecycle of new and existing Partners. You’ll be working directly with our Partners to ensure their on-boarding is smooth from start to finish, ensuring a great Partner experience. This also includes working with existing Partners to ensure their ongoing needs are met with any new feature launches.</p>
<p>
What line of business does this Product Manager work on? Funny you should ask! It’s for our Points Travel brand. It’s both a website (www.pointshound.com) and a white labeled solution. Do you love travel and collecting loyalty points? This might be the job made for you!</p>
<p>
Reporting to Director, Product Management, Points Travel, you will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Acting as the single point of contact for all inbound marketing services requests</li>
<li>
Translating Partner requirements into user stories and functional design specification documents for use by the development engineers based in and out of San Francisco</li>
<li>
Managing the Software Development Life-cycle from planning to tactical activities for your assigned features</li>
<li>
Leading product discovery process by working with potential Partners &amp; internal stakeholders by conducting interviews, market research, and any other step necessary to define business requirements</li>
<li>
Managing the product backlog, prioritization and leverage a strong understanding of product requirements to drive prioritization</li>
<li>
Creating and maintaining internal and external documentation of your assigned software features</li>
<li>
Supporting Sales &amp; Business Development teams by answering prospective Partner questions on the Points Travel platform functionality and helping to design solutions to meet Partner requirements.</li>
<li>
Working closely with internal finance, fraud, and support teams to define reporting requirements and customer service workflows</li>
<li>
Assuming a project management / delivery responsibility during growth phase of business</li>
<li>
Keeping up to date on industry best practices, trends, and current events</li>
</ul>
</div>
