<h2>Digital Product Owner - Digital Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p><b>Position Description</b></p>
<p>
Reporting to the Digital Product Director, the Product Owner - Digital Sales will drive innovation and manage change on the digital sales storefront across several business lines.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops, manages and implements digital product backlog and roadmap</li>
<li>Participates in daily/weekly agile routines with the team (stand ups, planning, refinement)</li>
<li>Leverages website performance data and industry trends to recommend and lead enhancements to site merchandising, content, features and tools</li>
<li>Balances a sales and service minded approach to create incremental business value while ensuring compliance with brand standards and industry regulations</li>
<li>Owns the test &amp; learn agenda for the storefront, proactively building a pipeline of ideas and concepts for A/B and multi-variate testing</li>
<li>Partners with UX, content strategy, product, &amp; marketing/ CRM teams to deliver an integrated channel strategy for all Ally products</li>
<li>Cogently presents strategy and content recommendations to partners across the organization, including senior and executive level management</li>
<li>Triages production issues with business and technology partners to ensure swift and satisfactory resolution with minimal impact to users and the business</li>
<li>Creates a consistent, proactive reporting routine for standard program updates and all testing efforts</li>
<li>Remains engaged in all steps of the enhancement and sprint process, including business validation/testing and participation in regularly scheduled technology releases</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Five to Seven years of professional digital product management &amp; marketing experience, with a focus on digital merchandising and conversion optimization (A/B &amp; multi-variate testing)</li>
<li>Passionate about delivering an amazing customer experience and using VOC to measure success</li>
<li>Critical thinker, driven by data, strong analysis skills and an enthusiasm for digital</li>
<li>Thrives in a fast-paced, self-directed, team environment and is comfortable presenting ideas and concepts across all levels, both inside and external to the organization</li>
<li>Detailed-oriented and able to perform tasks at all levels, including some routine tasks.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in business, marketing or related field</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong organizational and planning skills</li>
<li>Able to grasp technical concepts and convey them in a simple, friendly manner
</li>
<li>Strong PC skills required: Excel, Word and PowerPoint proficiency is a must</li>
<li>Experience with web analytics tools preferred (Adobe, Google)</li>
<br>
</ul>

<div><b>Ally Overview</b></div>
<p>
Ally Financial Inc. (NYSE: ALLY) is a leading digital financial services company and a top 25 U.S. financial holding company offering financial products for consumers, businesses, automotive dealers and corporate clients. Ally's legacy dates back to 1919, and the company was redesigned in 2009 with a distinctive brand, innovative approach and relentless focus on its customers. Ally has an award-winning online bank (member FDIC), one of the largest full service auto finance operations in the country, a complementary auto-focused insurance business, a growing digital wealth management and online brokerage platform, and a trusted corporate finance business offering capital for equity sponsors and middle-market companies.
</p>
<p><b>Business Unit/Enterprise Function
</b></p>
<div>The Digital Transformation group has the responsibility to plan, design, curate and evaluate Ally's vast ecosystem of digital experiences. With a shared commitment to simplifying complex financial solutions, we represent a collaboration of user experience experts, digital product specialists and industry thought leaders.</div>
