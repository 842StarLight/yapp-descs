<h2>Customer Success Manager Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">As the leader of our Customer Success Management team, you will be responsible for leading and growing a best in class CSM organization responsible for driving value realization, customer satisfaction and renewals.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will have a strong track record in defining and optimizing the customer journey, driving best practices around value realization, creating operational rigor across a global organization, and improving renewal rates. You will need to be able to work well cross functionally across products, engineering and sales. You will need to be a great communicator, a strong leader, and be able to manage a transactional business.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Optimize the customer journey and ensure consistency of execution. Ensure standardization and templates are built that will optimize the customer experience</li>
<li>Escalation management - personally manage escalations and help develop better internal processes</li>
<li>Value realization – Find ways for CSMs to deeply understand customer objectives and become trusted advisors. Determine how to define, drive and demonstrate ROI delivered</li>
<li>Lead cross-functionally to drive customer success. Help with company-wide initiatives that will help improve the customer experience and foster a culture of Customer Success. Advocate for, collaborate and help implement changes in other departments</li>
<li>Drive alignment with sales and field technical team. Ensure we have a smooth motion, a true one team approach to managing customers and ensuring they have a great experience.</li>
<li>Recruit, mentor and groom a best in class team</li>
<li>Achieve operational excellence</li>
<li>Continuously communicate metrics to team and ensure execution consistency</li>
<li>Work with team on a day to day basis to strategize on account issues, proactively working to help customers early in the customer journey</li>
<li>Report on metrics each week, understand themes</li>
<li>Quarterly analysis, drive actions to improve</li>
<li>Key Metrics</li>
<li>Renewal rate – weekly forecasting</li>
<li>Product adoption metrics</li>
<li>Velocity of adoption</li>
<li>NPS</li>
<li>Cost / ARR ratio</li>
<li>Professional Services and Premium Support bookings</li>
<li>Team member satisfaction and retention</li>
<li>Own the financial model for your team, including projections of costs and new hires, and forecasts of renewals</li>
<li>Candidate time to fill</li>
</ul>
<b>Desired Qualifications, Skills and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>8-10 years of leading CSM teams, with a focus on driving both value realization and revenue retention within an existing install base</li>
<li>Experience with a B2B SaaS model with an enterprise software solution addressing a technical audience (Engineers, DevOps, IT Ops personnel)</li>
<li>Excellent customer management skills</li>
<li>Track record of achievement</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
</ul>
