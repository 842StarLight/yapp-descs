<h2>Full Time Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>We are seeking a Full-time Property Manager for our self-storage facility located in South Williamsport, PA. The ideal candidate will be a motivated self-starter with a professional appearance and attitude, someone who is passionate about providing excellent customer service. This person should also be a quick learner with basic computer, general office, and excellent maintenance skills. Our managers enjoy a competitive wage, benefits, and schedules that may be adjusted to the employee's needs. Our Full-time employees work 5 days per week up to 40 hours. Please note our leasing office is open Saturdays and maybe a required workday. This position offers on-the-job paid training.</b></p>

<ul><li><b>Shows, rents and cleans self-storage units as well as actively selling and maintaining supplies of moving and packing materials</b></li></ul>

<ul><li><b>Keeping the facility rented at optimum levels by effectively utilizing advertising, promotional and customer relation skills as approved by management</b></li></ul>
<ul><li><b>Maintains all required tenant documentation in a neat and orderly manner as directed by management</b></li></ul>
<ul><li><b>Controls delinquencies through telephone contact and letters on a scheduled basis including sending out monthly invoices</b></li></ul>
<ul><li><b>Cleans space(s) as vacated, hallways, and grounds as needed. Clean office and restrooms daily</b></li></ul>
