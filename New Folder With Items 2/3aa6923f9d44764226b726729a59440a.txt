<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for a professional, career minded coordinator, with experience in entering and processing orders for our retail division.</p>
<p><b>Job functions: </b></p>
<ul><li>Responsible for the successful processing of an order through its entire life cycle. From the initial client contact, through entry, production, shipping and invoicing.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to track and maintain multiple orders in various stages of the order cycle.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate scope of work and details of the order between the vendor, customer, production and shipping.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide timely feedback and flow of information between the customer, vendors and internal departments to ensure successful and timely completion and delivery of the order.</li></ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities Required: </b></p>
<p>ABILITIES AND SKILLS</p>

<li>Organizational skills for tracking details and physical products for samples and orders.</li>
<li>Hands on organization of open orders and sample production.</li>
<li>Adaptability/Flexibility - Job requires being open to change (positive or negative) and to considerable variety in the workplace.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate clearly and effectively with customers and vendors verbally and through email.</li>
<li>\Proficient in Excel and ability to learn order entry software.</li>
</ul>
