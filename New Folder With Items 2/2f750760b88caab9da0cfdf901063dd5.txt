<h2>Marketing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Guaranty Corporation is a privately-held Louisiana corporation formed in 1926. It conducts business in radio broadcasting, streaming TV, digital media sales, the senior living facility market, hospitality and commercial real estate. Our company is looking for a Marketing Director who can wrap their arms around many different projects in a diverse, and expanding, set of industries. We need to advance the marketing narrative and brand awareness for each of our business segments while adhering to a cohesive, forward-thinking marketing strategy. Chiefly, we are looking for our new Marketing Director to develop, implement, and be accountable for: marketing plans, including promotional calendars and programs, new product introductions and other marketing projects for each of Guaranty’s subsidiaries.</p>
<ul>
<li>Guaranty Media: includes five FM radio stations
<ul> <li>Eagle 98.1</li>
<li>Tiger 100.7</li>
<li>Talk 107.3</li>
<li>104.5/104.9 ESPN Radio</li>
<li>Guaranty Sports Network</li>
<li>Gatorworks – Digital Marketing Agency</li>
<li>Windsor Senior Living Facility – Mandeville, LA</li>
<li>Guaranty Real Estate Management</li>
<li>Hospitality</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Primary Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Development and implementation of corporate marketing and brand strategy including each of the Guaranty Corporation entities.</li>
<li>Contribute to ongoing conversations regarding evolving marketing or business opportunities.</li>
<li>Create internal and external marketing plans for each entity as needed.</li>
<li>Strengthen brand awareness and reach outside of Baton Rouge area with radio, streaming video, senior living, and digital marketing.</li>
<li>Develop and manage marketing budgets.</li>
<li>Conduct customer, brand, and product research initiatives.</li>
<li>Offer strategic advice to develop innovative advertising, public relations, social media and events.</li>
<li>Coordinate the design and delivery of advertising campaigns across print, web, TV, outdoor, and more.</li>
<li>Manage agency providers, consultants, freelancers, and other suppliers, including contract negotiation and management.</li>
<li>Monitor the operation of each company’s website, social media, and email marketing system and provide analytics review.</li>
<li>Overseeing implementation of the Marketing strategy – including campaigns, events, promotions, digital marketing, and PR.</li>
<li>Assist the sales teams in growing existing accounts and generating new business through the creation of Non Traditional Revenue opportunities.</li>
<li>Develop social media strategy and direct programs to improve social media reputation and recognition.</li>
<li>Approve artwork for internal and external use, including web, print, vehicle wraps, promotional items, etc.</li>
<li>Help to develop and grow creative services department at radio stations and creative lead at marketing agency, acting as a mentor for creative needs.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Operations Manager, Promotions and Program Directors, and Sales on wide-scale radio station promotions.</li>
<li>Identify and expand upon opportunities to reach non-traditional listenership. Including, but not limited to, out-of-market streaming video users, on-demand audio users, and podcast listeners.</li>
<li>Spearhead community involvement via the Guaranty Foundation, our non-profit foundation.</li>
<br>
</ul>
