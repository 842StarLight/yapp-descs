<h2>Technical Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Do you want to use your outstanding technical abilities to build lasting customer relationships and solutions? Do you want to work with a platform and technology where you can help customers solve critical business problems? As a member of our Customer Success team you’ll have the opportunity to unleash your expertise to wow customers. We provide solutions for mission critical business and technology problems across a vast array of industries and technologies. With us, you’ll deliver solutions that customers previously thought infeasible or impossible to solve. Join us and you’ll find we have a supportive home team, great customers, and a product that customers love.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
The ExtraHop Customer Success team focuses on successful outcomes for our customers. As a Technical Customer Success Manager, you will play a leading role in accomplishing the following goals:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze customer network requirements and promote the highest value application of ExtraHop in our customer's environment</li>
<li>
Engage ExtraHop end users to provide targeted assistance and enablement</li>
<li>
Play a leading role assessing customer needs and coordinating high value services such as ExtraHop Professional Services and advanced training</li>
<li>
Lead and facilitate new customer’s initial onboarding and introduction to ExtraHop</li>
<li>
Champion customer needs and opportunities for product enhancement with ExtraHop development teams</li>
<li>
Assess and document customer health and maturity levels to improve customer engagement strategy</li>
<li>
Play a leading role in establishing 'trusted advisor' customer relationships including facilitating customer meetings, working sessions and QBRs</li>
<li>
Operate as the nexus for customer engagement and strategy with other ExtraHop teams such as Sales, Marketing, Product and Support</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Qualifications and Experience</b></p>
<p>
We’re looking for Technical Customer Success Managers who can help us grow to the next level. As a starting point for this position, you’ll need the following background:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience in management information systems or information technology</li>
<li>
Minimum of three years experience in information technology</li>
<li>
Effective communication skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Excellent attention to detail</li>
<li>
Independence, motivation, and a desire for project ownership</li>
<li>
Intermediate to advanced knowledge of networking principles (layers 2 through 4 of the OSI model) and application protocols (e.g. HTTP, TNS TDS, CIFS, NFS, and DNS)</li>
<li>
Experience with project management a plus</li>
<li>
Data interpretation experience using the ExtraHop platform a plus</li>
<li>
Data customization and configuration experience of the ExtraHop platform a plus</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to work with customers, conduct customer calls and meetings and understand their needs</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel up to 25%</li>
</ul>
