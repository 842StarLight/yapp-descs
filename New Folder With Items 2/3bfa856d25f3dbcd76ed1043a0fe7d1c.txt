<h2>Growth Manager, Team Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Do you want to work in a fun, smart, and energetic environment where you will be challenged, rewarded AND inspired? Come work with top performers at CJ Affiliate to make a real difference in how top brands run their businesses. We are the world’s leading affiliate marketing network - we transform digital marketing with the help of big data and business intelligence. We help advertisers drive more sales and expand their reach … and we want to hear from you!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Our Onboarding Team Lead will play a key role in support of CJ's publishers. This individual will manage Growth Managers and will convey CJ’s value proposition.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Do these things interest you? You will:
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>Manage, lead and develop a team of 2 - 4 direct reports on the Onboarding Team to fulfill on company and division-wide goals.</div>
<div>
Effectively onboard, educate, grow and manage a portfolio of new top tier publishers joining CJ.</div>
<div>
Develop a deep understanding of publisher's business and marketing objectives to create affiliate marketing strategies that deliver high impact results for Advertisers.</div>
<div>
Define platform feature and capability enhancements as a product stakeholder on behalf of new publishers.</div>
<div>
Create efficient systems and processes to improve the onboarding process for new publishers.</div>
<div>
Build and grow publisher revenues through partnership negotiations and investment.</div>
<div>
Develop and lead presentations for internal advertiser account teams, advertiser clients and prospects that showcase new publisher pipeline and high potential publishers.</div>
<div>
Convey CJ's value proposition, address client objections and function as point of escalation for publishers managed by the onboarding team.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
What we look for:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
Bachelor's degree from a four-year university.</div>
<div>
5+ years of experience working in a marketing role-ideally digital performance marketing.</div>
<div>
2+ year(s) of direct management experience.</div>
<div>
Detail oriented, proactively build your knowledge of industry trends and deliver accurate analyses.</div>
<div>
Clear and effective communicator-within CJ and when interfacing with clients-using solid business acumen, demonstrating affiliate marketing expertise.</div>
<div>
Critical thinker and problem solver-able to influence/develop account strategies that contribute to publishers' overall objectives.</div>
</div>
