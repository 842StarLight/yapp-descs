<h2>Employee Training & Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Mustang Cat, the authorized Caterpillar earthmoving equipment, power systems, and vocational truck dealer in Southeast Texas, has one single focus: partnering with our customers to help them solve their toughest problems. As a Cat dealer with more than 60 years of experience, we have dedicated ourselves to providing customers complete solutions for all their equipment needs.
<br><br>
Mustang’s reputation is built upon solving our customers’ problems and providing world-class service. Our heritage of innovation and relentless pursuit of continuous improvements is focused on improving our customer’s business and being an asset to the communities we serve.
<br><br>
This position is responsible for the development, implementation and management of all Mustang Cat non-technical training, employee development, and leadership initiatives. This includes:
<br><ul>
<li>Development of overall training and development strategy including budgeting, reporting, vendor selection, and calendar development</li>
<li>Collaborate with leaders throughout the organization to assess training needs including soft skills, software, customer service, sales, and leadership training</li>
<li>Oversight of leadership development, management/sales training, and new-to-leadership initiatives</li>
<li>Develop customized curriculum to fill training gaps and conduct employee assessment and development courses as needed</li>
<li>LMS administration</li>
<li>Oversight of all mentoring initiatives</li>
<li>Management and development of all training staff</li>
</ul>
<b>The qualified candidate will possess the following:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree majoring in Learning and Development, Human Resources, Organizational Development, Business, Psychology, or other related field of study</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years leadership experience in Training/Education</li>
<li>Additional experience in the field of Human Resources, Organizational Development, Supervisory Management, or Military</li>
<li>Previous responsibility for assessment, content creation and delivery of employee development initiatives</li>
<li>Demonstrated abilities in program development, supervisory training, soft-skills training, etc.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communicator</li>
<li>Exceptional technical writing, presentation and facilitation skills</li>
<li>Must be able to take complex topics and break them down into simple concepts</li>
<li>Must be a strong values-driven leader and a collaborative team member able to engage associates from all levels of the organization</li>
</ul>
Mustang Cat has many exciting opportunities for exceptional people who want a career with an industry leader. If you are looking for a solid, growth-oriented company with a commitment to its customers and its employees, Mustang Cat is the place for you.
