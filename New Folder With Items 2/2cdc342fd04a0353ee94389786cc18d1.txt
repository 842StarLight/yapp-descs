<h2>IDeaS Sr. Associate Accounting Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>The staff accountants are responsible for the Backoffice tasks for accounts receivables and payables. The billing and collections of accounts receivables from the existing client base and all other aspects of collections in an international environment, is to be completed by the staff accountants. The accounts payable section is to be managed by the staff accountants who will be responsible for the day to day data entry, expense realization, vendor master, and payment disbursement.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Major Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Process payments to, and on behalf of foreign offices in accordance with standard operating procedures.</li>
<li>Maintains accurate client collection files within Finance system.</li>
<li>Prepares various aging reports for management and sales associates as required.</li>
<li>Communicates with clients (internal and external) to resolve invoicing questions and assure timely payment.</li>
<li>Invoice and credit issuance based upon contractual terms and outlines.</li>
<li>Maintains purchase order requisition process and system. Investigates and resolves discrepancies before processing invoices into the accounting system. Selects and processes invoices for payment and maintains vendor files.</li>
<li>Understand and maintain all sales tax records, including both customer and vendor sales and use tax. 1099 reporting and maintaining of.</li>
<li>Maintains fixed asset accounting records.</li>
<li>Process payments to, and on behalf of foreign offices in accordance with standard operating procedures.</li>
<li>Reconcile and analyze various GL accounts and reports as requested.</li>
<li>Prepare schedules and work papers for auditors.</li>
<li>Prepare and enter select journal entries into the Great Plains accounting system.</li>
<li>Prepare various analytical and metric reports for management as required.</li>
<li>Maintains month-end closing activities.</li>
<li>Performs other duties, as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
