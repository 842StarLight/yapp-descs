<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Harman International is a global leader in connected car technology, lifestyle audio innovations, design and analytics, cloud services and IoT solutions. HARMAN's legendary brands have been introducing important innovations in audio for more than 50 years, and we continue to further develop new ways of enriching the audio/visual experience. From state-of-the-art surround sound systems in the home to in-car audio to market leading headphones, HARMAN is delivering premium audio experiences in the home and on the go. The Lifestyle Audio organization has more than 7,600 employees with engineering, manufacturing, sales and marketing centers around the globe.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
VP of Digital – Business Unit Interface will be responsible for digital leadership, strategic program execution, talent development and operational excellence for HARMAN Lifestyle Audio Division. This role will report functionally to the Chief Digital &amp; Information Officer and operationally to the Division President, Lifestyle Audio.</p>
<p></p>

Supervisory Responsibilities:</b><b> </b>This role will have responsibility for <b>10-20+ </b>full time employees and <b>50+</b> contractors with expectation for organizational growth. These will be mission based, co-located digital deployment teams.</p>
