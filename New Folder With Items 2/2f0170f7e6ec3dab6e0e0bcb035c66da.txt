<h2>Senior Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>As the Information Company, our mission at OpenText is to create software solutions and deliver services that redefine the future of digital. Be part of a winning team that leads the way in Enterprise Information Management.</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>The opportunity:</b>
</p>
<p>OpenText is looking for a Senior SaaS Product Manager with razor sharp analytical skills, familiarity with enterprise and consumer products, a deep understanding of online and direct sales and marketing processes, bias toward action, strong collaboration and communication skills to join our OpenText Hightail product team.<br>
<br>
The Product Manager is responsible for defining product direction and strategy, owns the product roadmap and builds innovative products that creative professionals don't just use, but love.<br>
<br>
The Product Manager is an individual contributor role that thrives on both quantitative and qualitative data and on the opportunity to push the boundaries of web, desktop and mobile products - all in the pursuit of continued success as a trusted cloud service and creative collaboration tool. The Product Manager also nurtures a healthy relationship with strategic customers and partners as well as cross-functional collaborators from Marketing, Sales, Engineering, UX Design, QA, Customer Success and Customer support.<br>
<br>
If you are passionate about building innovative and delightful consumer and enterprise software products and are not afraid to dive into technical discussions with engineers and architects one day and and a CXO level presentation on the value and direction of your product the next day, then we’re looking for you.<br>
<br>
As a Sr. Product Manager, you will join a dynamic and fast-paced team and work cross-functionally to define, design, build and launch the next generation of Creative Collaboration and File Sharing products for today’s customers.</p>
<p><b>You are great at:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand and lead the analysis of the creative collaboration environment, customers, and product metrics to determine the right feature set to drive engagement and usage on Hightail and our next generation offerings.</li>
<li>Drive global product requirements definition, product planning and product design (including writing specifications) of new product features and enhancements.</li>
<li>Develop a comprehensive product roadmap to deliver on the business goals for Hightail in 2020 &amp; beyond.</li>
<li>Work with the product development team and other cross-functional teams to bring features to life.</li>
<li>Clearly communicate product benefits to our users and internal stakeholders</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What it takes:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS degree in a technology-related field</li>
<li>Must be passionate about the creative process, design and user experience, emerging web technologies, and community building with a demonstrated track record of visionary execution</li>
<li>At least 3-4 years experience in a product management or equivalent role preferably in building SaaS products, with a demonstrated ability to drive product planning, development, and launch in a collaborative environment</li>
<li>Preferably a keen understanding of the most recent trends around creative collaboration, file sharing and the creative process</li>
<li>Able to manage and lead across highly cross-functional teams in an agile manner</li>
<li>Must have excellent oral and written communication skills and be able to interact with and understand technical subjects/emerging technologies and their relevance to the marketplace</li>
</ul>
