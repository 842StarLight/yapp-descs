<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><b>Sales Development Representative</b></div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
ReachForce, an Austin-based SaaS company, serves the emerging and fast growing marketing technology (MarTech) market, where a fundamental shift in B2B marketing and sales approaches drives the need for high quality big data solutions. With a core competency in maximizing the production delivered from marketing and sales automation, ReachForce solves for data quality, completeness and unification across cloud-based services.</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We are currently hiring <b>Sales Development Representatives</b> for our growing sales team. This position is perfect for ambitious, results-oriented individuals who excel at generating new revenue opportunities in a high-volume, phone-based sales role. In return for your success, you'll be rewarded with a great compensation package and the opportunity to develop your sales career at a fast-growing company with a strong culture of promoting from within.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The <b>SDR</b> is responsible for qualifying sales leads for the regional sales teams. This is a prospecting role that requires a combination of business acumen, creativity, and initiative to develop new business from various sources.and drive qualified meeting creation.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key duties and responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li><p>
Research target companies and contacts and engage via cold-calling and email</p></li>
<li><div>
High call activity of 50 to 100 calls per day</div></li>
<li><div>
Qualify inbound opportunities</div></li>
<li><div>
Effectively manage and overcome prospect objections</div></li>
<li><div>
Schedule qualified meetings for the regional sales team</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage leads, information, and activities in a CRM system, following established process</div></li>
<li><p>
Consistently meet or exceed quarterly objectives and key results to achieve individual quotas and regional territory goals</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li><p>
Bachelor’s degree</p></li>
<li><div>
2+ years of lead qualification experience in a B2B sales environment</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience working in a CRM system, Salesforce.com preferred</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong written and verbal communication skills and presentation skills</div></li>
<li><div>
Organized and self-disciplined</div></li>
<li><div>
Team player</div></li>
<li><div>
Solid work history with excellent references</div></li>
<li><p>
Completion of a formal sales training program a big plus!</p></li>
</ul>
