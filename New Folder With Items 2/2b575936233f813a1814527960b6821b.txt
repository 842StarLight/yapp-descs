<h2>Dealership Performance Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>J&amp;L Marketing is the national leader in creating profitable events for the automotive industry. These creative campaigns increase our dealership's sales, profits, and market share. The Dealership Performance Specialist will represent J&amp;L in the industry and is a vital component to the success our dealership clients have. We are looking for someone with excellent communication skills, a dynamic personality, who can energize a room, motivate and train others.</p>
<p>Roles and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Train Customers on how to use the J&amp;L Easy Car Buy Software and Dashboard</li>
<li>Coaching and Training Dealerships on Proven Best Practices on all of J&amp;L Products that align with Dealership Process</li>
<li>Provide refresher and new feature/functionality to clients</li>
<li>Energize and Motivate the Dealership Personnel to fully capitalize off of their marketing campaign</li>
<li>Assist and/or develop training material and keep updated</li>
<li>Work with other departments on issues or challenges</li>
<li>Gather and Provide Feedback to different departments for improvement ideas</li>
<li>Analyze and present dealership performance reporting</li>
<li>Continuously strive to better understand the industry and the product at a higher level to become an expert</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Willingness to learn the Automotive Industry and stay updated</li>
<li>Must be comfortable communicating very productive, educated conversations with high level Executive and Director Level Decision Makers regarding their current business state and upcoming goals and initiatives.</li>
<li>High Energy and ability to Multitask and maintain composure under pressure</li>
<li>Strong Problem Solving Skills</li>
</ul>
