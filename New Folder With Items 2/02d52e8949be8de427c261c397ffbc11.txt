<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>A busy Outpatient Physical Therapy Central Billing Office Rome, GA is currently seeking a strong Patient Account Reps to perform Billing, Payment posting &amp; Collections experience. The ideal candidate has current experience working in a similar billing company environment.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We require the individual to be dependable, responsible, great multi-tasking skills, pay close attention to detail and works well in a fast paced environment.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Post payments daily</li>
<li>
Bill, upload and correct claims in clearinghouse</li>
<li>
Status unpaid claims and patient accounts</li>
<li>
Balance and complete daily deposit</li>
<li>
Process Refunds</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience with Electronic Billing, Collections and all aspects of A/R cycle preferred.</li>
<li>
Must be proficient in Word &amp; Excel.<br>
<ul>
<li>Previous experience with medical software required.</li>
<li>Experience in a billing office and or medical clinic, heavy medical billing, heavy A/R, collections (from insurance companies &amp; patients), insurance follow-up and coding &amp; verification.</li>
<li>Team player attitude, energetic, able to multi-task, with a focus on excellent customer service are key.</li>
<li>Must be a self-starter with strong organizational skills.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
