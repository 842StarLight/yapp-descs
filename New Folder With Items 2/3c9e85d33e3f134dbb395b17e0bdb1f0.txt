<h2>Wall Panel Design Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>Employee is responsible for converting sales orders into mechanical drawings</p>
<p>Read architectural and structural drawings and understand scope, scale and detail</p>
<p>Review specifications to ensure design of wall panels meets said specifications</p>
<p>Demonstrate effective communication, cooperation and technical ability when preparing drawings</p>
<p>Problem solving through proactivity, communication and critical thinking</p>
<p>Ability to ask questions when needed</p>
<p>Ability to perform tasks and manage time with minimal oversight</p>
<p>Complete all assigned jobs to meet current design schedule</p>
<p><b>Job Requirements</b></p>
<p>Knowledge of intellibuild, AutoCad, Revit, or other design software</p>
<p>General knowledge of the construction industry</p>
<p>Highly motivated with a positive attitude</p>
