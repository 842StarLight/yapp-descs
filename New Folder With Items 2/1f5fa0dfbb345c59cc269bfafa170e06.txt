<h2>Enterprise Account Executive - Commercial - Omaha Nebraska</h2>

<p>SHI has a dynamic career opportunity for an experienced Sales Professional focused on new business development within our Commercial Sales organization. The Account Executive’s role will focus on developing new business with our existing customers and acquire new customers across a focused Named Account List. Working with our Sales Leadership, Internal Support, and our Training and Development Teams a successful candidate will be enabled to position SHI’s Innovative Solutions and World Class Support to their Target Customer List.</p>

</ul>
