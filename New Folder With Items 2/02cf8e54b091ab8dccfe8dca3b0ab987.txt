Responsibilities include:</p>
<p>
Acts as a liaison between client area and technical organization by planning, conducting, and directing the analysis of highly complex business problems to be solved with automated systems. Provides technical assistance in identifying, evaluating, and developing systems and procedures that are cost effective and meet business requirements. Works with user groups to provide training, resolve questions, assess user needs, and recommend changes. Prepares specifications for system changes. Recommends and initiates systems testing. Acts as an internal consultant within technology and business groups by using quality tools and process definition/improvement to re-engineer technical processes for greater efficiencies. Provides direction and guidance to less experienced staff.</p>
<p>
This Business Systems Consultant will be responsible for supporting the Call Handling Capabilities leader and their needs. This candidate would be focused on core call handling capabilities such as telephony, CTI, call routing, call recording, quality, workforce management, advanced analytics and would be responsible for consulting with line of business &amp; operational stakeholders regarding contact center solutions.</p>
<p>
Job Summary:</p>
<p>
The Investment Contact Center (ICC) Strategic Strategy and Technology department is seeking a Business Systems Consultant 5 who will be responsible for successfully fulfilling the following functions within the Team Member Applications group:</p>
<ul>
<li>In this role you will serve as subject matter expert for call handling capabilities and solutions within ICC.</li>
<li>Provide consultative and hands-on assistance in identifying, evaluating, and recommending solutions that are cost effective and meet business requirements.</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between client area and technical area by planning, conducting, and directing the analysis of complex business problems to be solved by advanced solution capabilities.</li>
<li>Provide input on contact center design and integration for Telephony, IVR, CTI, Routing, Recording, Workforce Management, and Desktop solutions.</li>
<li>Evaluate call routing rules to identify areas of improvement that improve customer experience and reduce costs.</li>
<li>Prepare systems specifications and facilitate changes as a result of business requirements.</li>
<li>Monitor application effectiveness through performance metrics, analysis, and reports to understand trends and identify improvement opportunities.</li>
<li>Serve as consultant and subject matter expert by providing expertise and support during projects or initiatives.</li>
<li>Ensure effective risk management including identification, assessment, mitigation and escalation as necessary.</li>
<li>Facilitate and/or perform user acceptance testing on call handling applications as needed in support of new enhancement, production support, or security patch releases.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Location: St. Louis, MO or Charlotte, NC are the preferred locations. Position is also open across the ICC Wells Fargo footprint.</p>
<p>
Applicants will need to have a flexible schedule as testing and implementation events may occur outside of normal business hours including weekends.</p>
