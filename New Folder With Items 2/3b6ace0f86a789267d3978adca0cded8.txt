<h2>Director of Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Reporting to the CEO/President, the Director of Marketing is responsible for establishing and implementing strategic and tactical communication plans designed to capitalize on market opportunities and generate demand. Our Director of Marketing will build a creative, integrated, multi-channel marketing department that builds brand awareness and reputation, provides a steady flow of sales leads, and measures the return on marketing program investments. The ideal candidate will be self-motivated, resourceful, and have impeccable communication and leadership skills.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop a comprehensive and aligned strategic marketing plan</li>
<li>Implement strategic plan with the assistance of project managers/stakeholders</li>
<li>Manage marketing budget for all staffing, resources, and capital purchases</li>
<li>Evaluate new media technologies, such as social media sites, blogs, RSS, video, webcasts, etc.</li>
<li>Manage the creating of all marketing collateral and sales support tools</li>
<li>Be accountable for measuring and reporting on campaign results</li>
<li>Enhance customer relationship management processes and technologies</li>
<li>Perform market segmentation, targeting and positioning for each product line</li>
<li>Execute integrated online/offline, search engine marketing, advertising, public relations, tradeshows, and other campaigns to generate demand and sales leads</li>
<li>Conduct research and evaluation of vendor solutions and manage relationships</li>
<li>Work directly with sales and channel partners to support their efforts</li>
<li>Develop a strategic decision-making, prioritization, and governance process</li>
<li>Identify areas for process improvement, i.e. product/project management</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of marketing trends, developments, and best practices</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p><b>Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4-year degree, or equivalent combination of education and work experience</li>
<li>Minimum of 7 years of experience in a marketing management position</li>
<li>Exceptional knowledge of marketing strategies, concepts and practices</li>
<li>Strong project management and performance measurement skills</li>
<li>Ability to develop strong relationships and work with senior level executives</li>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Able to compile and analyze performance data to develop metrics that support decision-making for resource allocation and subsequent campaigns</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
