<h2>District Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>District Manager: </b>United Hardware Distributing Co. is looking for a District Manager for our Sales department.</p>
<p>
United Hardware Distributing Co. is a full-line wholesale hardware distributor providing products, retail programs and support services to independent retailers. Headquartered in Plymouth, MN, United Hardware services dealers from its 400,000 square foot distribution center in Milbank, SD and delivers to locations in 18 states.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Make store visits to existing dealers to sell United Hardware s programs and services; establish and work on goals to improve retailer s business; train dealers and employees in retail operations; assist and train dealers on how to plan, work and market the buying shows; give constructive advice on store improvements; provide dealers with relevant information; perform store evaluations</li>
<li>Assist dealers in category and market acquisition</li>
<li>Work with dealers to maintain up-to-date membership applications and records</li>
<li>Assist dealers in completing store budgets, advertising budgets, and cash flow analysis. Review financial reports with dealers and make recommendations on improving store operations. Instruct dealers on inventory and purchase control. Assemble forecasts for stores.</li>
<li>Assist existing dealers when selling their stores</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver formal presentations to prospective dealers</li>
<li>Organize, coordinate, and, when appropriate, conduct store meetings and group meetings</li>
<li>Assist and train dealers in the preparation of Dealer s Choice Circulars, newspaper, radio and other media ads and work with them on how to make coop ad claims</li>
<li>Assist dealers in planning and coordinating store resets, expansions and relocation including preparation of capitalization and category selection</li>
<li>Communicate with corporate office departments about market trends within assigned district i.e. new items or advertising/marketing strategies.</li>
<li>Maintain a valid driver s license and drive an automobile to execute job responsibilities</li>
<li>Ability to lead and motivate multiple independent retailers to develop and innovate their current business strategies</li>
<li>Ensure that strategies, change initiatives and business opportunities are communicated in compelling ways to the district</li>
<li>Create a relentlessly positive atmosphere</li>
<li>Ability to lead and train on basic retail merchandising, seasonality and inventory control concepts</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of Company operations, programs and policies</li>
<li>Knowledge of retail hardware and lumber operations and procedures</li>
<li>Basic hardware product knowledge</li>
<li>Knowledge of the operating and franchise requirements and distribution methods of the Company's competitors</li>
<li>Retail store management experience required (multi-store management experience a plus)</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite programs &amp; navigating web based applications</li>
<li>Strong human relation and organizational skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated with the ability to organize, prioritize and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Extensive travel within the district required</li>
<li>Experience in CRM tools a plus</li>
<li>Marketing experience &amp; social media knowledge a plus</li>
</ul>
