<p></p>
<p>Applications Scientist: Liquid Handling and Automation</p>
<p>Location: Northeast US, Boston, MA</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Applications Scientist is the sales and application person responsible for the Liquid Handling platform based application solutions within the Americas. This person will be the technical resource to assist and support the generalist sales person with the goal of winning the sale. An application solution is defined as a combination of software, software scripts, hardware and biology that target a specific application. These application solutions could include instrument components from one or more Business Units, collaboration with third party instrument and/or reagent vendors and interaction with the Market Managers in the Business Units. The person in this position will act as an expert and knowledge source for the Tecan generalist sales team</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your main tasks:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Become a technical expert within the Liquid Handling automation field, providing specific application expertise to gain customer confidence during pre- and post-sales process.</li>
<li>Encourage the sales generalists to shift focus from product features to the customer’s application through coaching and continuing education.</li>
<li>Delivers market input on current “hot applications” including trends seen at customers</li>
<li>Responsible for all aspects of the applications sale, from pre-sale support, including application analysis, solution design and configuration, instrument demonstration (if required), through to installation and providing higher level complexity post sale support.</li>
<li>Maintain up-to-date references of local Liquid Handling platform customers to be disseminated to sales generalists as needed.</li>
<li>Provide applications support and training for members of the service organization.</li>
<li>Work directly with Liquid Handling applications and product management to ensure excellent flow of information from both the field and customers to product management and back again</li>
<li>Participation in marketing and sales meetings at Tecan sites when appropriate.</li>
<li>Collect and maintain up-to-date competitive and market information to disseminate to the sales force as well as the marketing and manufacturing sites.</li>
<li>Involvement in marketing activities, including but not limited to, planning of the shows to attend, deciding which companies to collaborate with, assistance with the Website design material review, review and critique marketing materials, etc.</li>
<li>Ensure any application data entry into CRM is done accurately and regularly, where appropriate</li>
<li>Ability to travel (often on short notice)</li>
