<h2>Group & Golf Sales Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Group &amp; Golf Sales Specialist</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Group &amp; Golf Sales Specialist is responsible for providing a first class comprehensive group and golf vacation booking service for our travel agent partners and direct clients. The Group &amp; Golf Sales Specialist will have a primary focus on delivery top group and golf vacations while providing a helpful and informative touch to each and every customer.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage group opportunities of 10 or more passengers as well as complex group and golf vacations.</li>
<li>Create tailor-made travel itineraries with great attention to detail while being extremely customer-focused.
</li>
<li>Maximize conversion by following up on quotes in an established time frame.</li>
<li>Handle inquiries by phone/mail and send accurate group/golf price quotes.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain positive working relationships internally and externally.</li>
<li>Effectively communicate with clients and strategic partners (DMC's and Vendors) while quoting and confirming bookings.</li>
<li>Plan and sell complete vacation packages, based on the clients need and discuss recommendations.
</li>
<li>Provide exceptional customer service, while fulfilling the duties of a Sales team member with enthusiasm and diligence.
</li>
<li>Performs other work-related duties as required and assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Passionate interest in geography, with strong international travel knowledge.</li>
<li>Possess the proper skills to persuade customers and influence their decision making, both for their own benefit and that of the company.
</li>
<li>Highly motivated, working both independently and collaboratively.
</li>
<li>Proficient in internet research skills for customer information.
</li>
<li>Ability to discern the best and most compatible options for accommodations, transportation and travel dates while maintaining budget.
</li>
<li>Ability to analyze rates, understand operating logistics and manage markups.
</li>
<li>Confident, competitive and a team player with an eye for detail.
</li>
<li>Excellent command of both written and verbal communication skills, required.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and quickly interpret complex itineraries.
</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of computer reservation systems- Microsoft Office applications on Word, Excel and Outloook.
</li>
<li>Significant personal travel experience, a plus.</li>
</ul>
