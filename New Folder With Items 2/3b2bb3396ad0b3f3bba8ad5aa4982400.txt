<h2>Territory Manager, Growth</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The Growth Territory Manager at Conga will guide prospective and existing customers through their evaluation of our growing solution set. This sales executive will design and execute new business, cross-sell and up-sell strategies to drive revenue within the territory mid commercial segment (customer size: 1-200). The Territory Manager will partner closely with Marketing and Customer Success to drive the growth and ensure the success of our customer base.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Use insights and a consultative selling approach to teach prospective and existing customers about their industry and their customers; challenge them to modernize and grow their business; assist them in implementing digital technology and refine office processes</p></li>
<li><p>
Effectively design and execute territory plan, account plans and opportunity plans to maximize revenue within assigned selling patch</p></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>
Successfully manage sales opportunities through all stages of the sales cycle, from prospecting to close to account growth</p></li>
<li><p>
Demonstrate subject matter expertise on all Conga solutions and services. Understand and communicate the business value and ROI of our solutions.</p></li>
<li><p>
Engage prospects and customers in dialogues related to business process and infrastructure growth, including their planned and potential needs</p></li>
<li><p>
Successfully leverage resources within the greater Conga team; Engage leadership to determine the strategy and methods to ensure success across the entire assigned market segment; Leverage our partner ecosystem to surround and grow deals</p></li>
<li><p>
Exceed monthly sales KPIs and quotas</p></li>
<li><p>
Execute detailed product presentations and web demonstrations of our software capabilities to C-level executives and decision makers</p></li>
<li><p>
Travel to territory for on-site meetings, QBRs, and industry events when needed</p></li>
<li><p>
Leverage Salesforce.com and other tools to track the success of each program</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
