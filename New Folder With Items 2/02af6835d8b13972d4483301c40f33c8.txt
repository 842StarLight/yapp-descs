<h2>Systems Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Email resume and cover letter to jobs@crawfishsystems.com</p>
<p>
As a Crawfish Systems Engineer you will be responsible for providing technical services and support to our customers and Crawfish sales team in pre-and post- sales engagements. Your Professional Services will include on-site support and remote services. The Systems Engineer must be a responsible, highly motivated, minimally managed individual capable of working in dynamic high-security environments.</p>
<p></p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></h4>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Assists sales team as required in areas including solution design, problem resolution, and client presentations.</li>
<li>Performs onsite professional services including installations, upgrades, troubleshooting, data migrations, and assessments.</li>
<li>Generates detailed documentation, presentations, and technical drawings.</li>
<li>Maintains technical expertise in a broad range of related technologies.</li>
<li>The candidate handles hardware and software issues related to storage systems.</li>
<li>Reports directly to Crawfish Systems management.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Requirements</b></h4>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated ability to have completed multiple, complex technical projects.</li>
<li>Must have strong troubleshooting and analysis abilities; possess strong customer service and technical problem solving skills; and be someone who embraces challenges.</li>
<li>A minimum of 8 years of industry experience in storage/system software and/or engineering product support role is required.</li>
<li>Bachelor of Science in Engineering or Information Technology or equivalent related experience is required.</li>
<li>In-depth Technical Knowledge In the Following Areas:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Storage technologies including RAID, SAN, NAS, Fibre Channel, iSCSI, Deduplication, Storage virtualization.</li>
<li>Storage systems &amp; components including NetApp, Hitachi, EMC, 3Par, Brocade, Cisco, Qlogic, Emulex.</li>
<li>Backup, Archival and file system solutions and technologies including NetBackup, BackupExec, Commvault, Legato, NDMP, Quantum DXi, DataDomain, VTL, Stornext, Enterprise vault.</li>
<li>Disaster Recovery and High availability design and implementation.</li>
<li>System Virtualization solutions including VMware Vsphere, Microsoft , and Citrix.</li>
<li>Operating systems including Microsoft Windows Server platforms and various Linux offerings.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Provide Subject-Matter Expert-level consultations.</li>
<li>Ability to provide off-hours support.</li>
</ul>
