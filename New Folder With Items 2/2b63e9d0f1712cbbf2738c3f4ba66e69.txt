<div>
<br>
Champs Sports has a brand <b>NEW</b> position open on their Marketing Team. We are looking to bring on a Senior Brand Marketing Manager to our Champs family here in Florida. </div>
<div></div>
<div>As the Senior Manager you will partner with the buying team to identify and prioritize key product stories. Collaborate with internal and external partners to drive fully integrated demand creation efforts, including go-to-market strategies, curated launch calendars, visual merchandising solutions, targeted media campaigns, digital marketing, event activations, PR and social media plans. Support the roll out of new retail transformation pilots and omni-channel optimization efforts. Communicate plans across the division and facilitate brand alignment. Collaborate and maintain a positive relationship with Champs Sports vendor partners to develop and execute joint campaigns. And develop team capabilities while sustaining a culture within our team that is aligned with that of the company and enhances our productivity and effectiveness.</div>

RESPONSIBILITIES</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Contribute to the development of short and long-term marketing objectives, strategies, and tactics to deliver against agreed upon brand, business, and financial goals.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain internal and external stakeholder relationships to deliver best-in-class marketing solutions against key initiatives to drive. awareness and preference that align with Champs Sports strategy and positioning.</li>
<li>
Set social media strategy across all social platforms to drive store traffic and sales.</li>
<li>
Oversee influencer marketing strategy as it relates to Champs Sports specific programming and support.</li>
<li>
