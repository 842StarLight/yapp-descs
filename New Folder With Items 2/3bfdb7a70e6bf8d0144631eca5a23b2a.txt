<div><b>
Corporate Functions – Marketing &amp; Communications</b></div>
<div>
As part of this team, you’ll play a key role in helping Accenture execute its strategy. You’ll work with a diverse team of innovative marketing experts who focus on the following areas: global brand management and advertising; global events and sponsorships; internal communications; marketing research; media and industry analyst relations; corporate and financial communications; internet marketing; recruitment marketing; client-centric marketing; and industry, service line and geographic marketing campaigns.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>
YOUR ROLE</b>: CMT Digital Marketing Senior Manager</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
The Accenture Marketing &amp; Communications Delivery Excellence team is looking for a creative, highly-motivated, data-driven marketer with a proven ability to strategize, execute, and optimize high-quality digital marketing campaigns. The Digital Marketing Lead will be aligned to Communications &amp; High Tech, one of 5 Operating Groups. In this role they will develop an in-depth understanding of audience and audience behavior to ensure digital programs and tactics are designed effectively.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
This position will play an integral role in delivering an optimized client experience working closely with the Campaign Management lead and directly with the Marketing &amp; Communications lead for their area, while also partnering with the Social Media team on social strategy and execution.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Key Responsibilities:</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate closely with the campaign manager and social media lead within their respective area and cross functional teams to design and implement digital marketing programs for medium and largescale marketing campaigns.
</li>
<li>Work directly with marketing and communication leads and social media leads within their respective area on smaller marketing programs to design and implement digital marketing tactics that support business objectives.
</li>
<li>Manage activation of content across multiple channels, ensuring proper tracking is implemented.
</li>
<li>Manage overall web presence for their specific area of the business, proactively analyzing page level performance and recommending actions that ensure maximum effectiveness of marketing objectives as well as creating optimal customer experience.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with Analytics &amp; Insight team to analyze performance of digital channels and specific marketing programs and identify and execute optimizations to maximize effectiveness.
</li>
<li>Create strong cross functional relationships with peers in other business units to foster shared learning and leverage best practices.
</li>
<li>Support digital marketing programs outside of your specific group alignment when appropriate and needed (i.e. amplification of enterprise level campaigns, peak periods within BD &amp; Sales).</li>
</ul>
</div>
