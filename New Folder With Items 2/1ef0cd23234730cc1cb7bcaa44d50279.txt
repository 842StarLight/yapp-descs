<h2>International Market Development Manager (IOS)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>SUMMARY OF DUTIES</b>
</div>
<p>This position is responsible for the development, management and execution of strategic marketing for a portfolio of products in international markets.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS PERFORMED</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute marketing strategy, plans, and forecasts.</li>
<li>Perform market research functions-including the collection, dissemination, and analysis of industry, medical procedures, competitor information, and market trends.</li>
<li>Identify new markets and growth opportunities as part of a strategic business plan.</li>
<li>Manage product portfolio, including price analysis, new product launches, campaigns, etc.</li>
<li>Answer product/ market-related questions from Sales Representatives.</li>
<li>Collaborate with corporate marketing team and provide the needed context and insights from international markets for projects.</li>
<li>Work with marketing communications and regional markets to develop: localized marketing collateral, digital, and web content.</li>
<li>May manage department employees by interviewing, hiring, coordinating work assignments, training, evaluating work, disciplining, and other related duties.</li>
<li>Lead or support the creation of Voice of Customer activities such as:
<ul>
<li>Creation of local customer engagement and advisory boards</li>
<li>Collection of customer input on new trends, indications, and needs</li>
<li>Collection and verification of input for existing R&amp;D projects</li>
<li>Create a global KOL/speaker network and database for area of responsibility.</li>
<li>Develop customer segmentation and targeting plans by geography and execute tactics accordingly.</li>
<li>Deliver sales force and distributor training content and programs to increase revenue and profitability including:
<ul>
<li>Product specification training</li>
<li>Relevant procedural training</li>
<li>Product application</li>
<li>Selling and cross-selling process (how to sell and with what)</li>
<li>Features &amp; benefit added values summary</li>
<li>Related health economic factors which may influence decision making</li>
<li>Competitive landscape and objections handling</li>
<li>Provide the international leadership team updates on sales metrics and analysis of portfolio and objectives.</li>
<li>Identify relevant congresses, educational events, and other opportunities to participate in industry educational events that drive brand awareness, education, and ultimately product adoption and revenue growth.</li>
<li>Understand the competitive landscape and strategically distill information back to internal customers.</li>
<li>Perform all other duties, as needed.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUMMARY OF MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul><li>Education and/or experience equivalent to a Bachelor's Degree in Marketing, Business or allied health (nursing, physician assistant, technologist) or related field.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work-related experience equivalent to six years of marketing and/or sales experience in the medical device field.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of and ability to execute strategic market thinking including basic models such as S.W.O.T analysis and principles of marketing.</li>
<li>Ability to demonstrate leadership, expertise, innovation, vision, and company values.</li>
<li>Global product launch experience.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to influence at a variety of levels within the organization.</li>
<li>Demonstrated strong analytical, problem-solving, interpersonal, and communication skills - verbal and written.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Ability to manage large and high priority projects efficiently and effectively and to meet deadlines in a timely manner.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Good organizational skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent presentation skills and the ability to prepare professional written and oral presentations and market analysis.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated computer skills preferably spreadsheets, word processing, database and other applicable software programs.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Travel approximately 40% of the time international/domestic.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>Merit Medical Systems, Inc.</b>, an Equal Opportunity Employer M/F/D/V, is a dynamic, progressive and innovative company with a strong global presence. Our culture is highly energetic, family friendly, values its employees, and promotes them as standard business practice.</p>
<p><b>
COMPANY HISTORY</b> Merit Medical Systems, Inc. (NASDAQ, MMSI) was founded in 1987 by Fred P. Lampropoulos. Merit develops, manufactures and markets single-use medical devices primarily for diagnosing and treating cardiovascular and vascular disease. Merit has been successful in introducing and marketing innovative, high-quality products through its expertise in new product design and its capabilities in injection and insert molding of plastics; the application of its proprietary electronic and sensor-based technologies; and wire, extrusion and catheter technologies.</p>
<p>
The Company is utilizing its competitive advantages and technologies to develop and offer products that address a wide range of needs related to cardiology and radiology procedures performed in hospitals. Sales are made primarily to U.S. hospitals through a direct sales force. The Company markets its products in over 60 countries worldwide, utilizing a direct sales force in Europe, and distributors for the remainder of the world.</p>
