<h2>Box Office & Theatre Office Student Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Student Office Assistant is assigned to both the Box Office and the Theatre Department Office. In the Box Office, the Box Office &amp; Theatre Office Student Assistant provides customer service for the audiences who attend UIW theatre productions.<br>
<br>
<i>This is a student position open to current </i><i>UIW</i><i> students.</i></p>
Essential Functions
<ul>
<li>Primary duties include: selling tickets, taking payments, answering phones, providing production-specific information, ushering during performances, and assisting with administrative tasks, many of which require familiarity with and skills using computer software;</li>
<li>Assists the Department’s Secretary in the Theatre Department Office with general administrative tasks that may include filing, photocopying, posting notices, etc.;</li>
<li>Because the Box Office &amp; Theatre Office Student Assistant publicly represents the Department and the University, professionalism in both appearance and behavior is required at all times;</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
