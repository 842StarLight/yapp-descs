<h2>Mechanical Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Purpose: Perform engineering duties in designing pressure vessels and heat exchangers.</p>
<p>Job Description</p>
<p>Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Hands on Designing Ability</li>
<li>Communicate with customers regarding technical issues and specifications.</li>
<li>Read and interpret technical drawings, schematics and customer specifications.</li>
<li>Assist drafters in developing the detail drawings for products using Compress, RCS, MatchCad, and Excel.</li>
<li>Design pressure vessels and heat exchangers to meet ASME Sections VIII-1, II, IX, TEMA, HTRI, API 660, and other company/customer requirements while applying knowledge of engineering principles.</li>
<li>Code calculations per ASME, TEMA, HTRI and company/customer specifications and requirements.</li>
<li>Specify system components or direct modification of pressure vessels and heat exchangers to ensure conformance with engineering design and performance specifications.</li>
<li>Assist sales team with preliminary design and bill of material in the estimating process.</li>
<li>Thermal/hydraulic design and verification of heat exchanger performance.</li>
<li>Check drawings for accuracy.</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills/Qualifications/Licensing:</p>
<ul>
<li>Verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Professional Engineer license preferred.</li>
<li>Knowledge of ASME Sections VIII-1, II, IX, TEMA, HTRI, and API 660.</li>
<li>Knowledge of the practical application of engineering (includes applying principles, techniques, procedures, and equipment to the design and production of pressure vessels and heat exchangers).</li>
<li>Knowledge of ASME material specifications, production processes, quality control, and other techniques for maximizing the effective manufacture and distribution of goods.</li>
<li>Critical thinking and complex problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Understanding of Microsoft Word, Excel, and other heat exchanger and pressure vessel design software.</li>
<li>HTRI preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p>Education: Bachelors Degree in Mechanical/Manufacturing Engineering.</p>
<p>Level/Salary Range: $75k - $90k based on experience.</p>
<p>Experience: Minimum three years experience working on pressure vessels and heat exchangers per ASME and TEMA perferred.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $75,000.00 to $90,000.00 /year</p>
