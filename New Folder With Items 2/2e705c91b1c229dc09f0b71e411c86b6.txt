<h2>Project Manager (A/V - Low Voltage)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>We are looking to add an experienced Field Project Manager to our growing Information Technology/Services team in support of our Low Voltage and A/V clients. Preferred candidates will have considerable experience in A/V integration projects including familiarity with systems such as AMX-Harmon, Bi-Amp, Crestron, Extron, NEC and Planar.</div>
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<div><b>Responsibilities will include but are not limited to:</b></div>
<div>Working closely with clients and internal parties to translate project requirements into achievable deadlines, ensuring schedules and budgets stay on track.</div>
<div>Leading project team members, providing performance feedback and supporting overall project efforts, achievement of goals/objectives and defined SLAs.</div>
<div>Communicating frequently with clients to identify and resolve issues, in a timely and transparent manner.</div>
<div>Develops and communicates project plans, engineering and technician schedules, status reports, communication plans and other documents as required.</div>
<div>Identifies subcontractor resources as needed and helps maintain a database of qualified subcontractors based on location and competencies.</div>
<div>Provides field support and assists with installation services as needed and required in support of project demands and timelines.</div>
<div>May assist with pre-sales process including RFPs, SOW, and RFQs.</div>
<div>Travels in support of project installations, client meetings and appointments as needed. Estimated at 25%.</div>
<div><b>Qualified candidates will possess the following skills and abilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Evidence of work history and proficiency in the MS Office Suite applications of Outlook, Project, PowerPoint, and SharePoint.</li>
<li>Evidence of work history and proficiency in Excel performing data evaluation, formulas, and analytics.</li>
<li>Communication skills – Evidence of work history with technical writing and oral presentations.</li>
<li>Experience in managing projects around the core concepts of Project Management Institute (PMI) methods, business process management, collaboration, and/or system development lifecycle.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in applying project, portfolio, enterprise architecture, and capital planning practices with an IT organization.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Education and Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Associate's degree or higher preferred</li>
<li>Must have a minimum of three (3) years of demonstrated experience in A/V or Network Systems integration, installing or managing IT projects or programs</li>
<li>Avixa/Infocomm CTS, CTS-I or CTS-D certification strongly preferred</li>
<li>Project Management Professional (PMP) certification preferred – must be willing to obtain</li>
<li>Certifications with AMX-Harmon, Bi-Amp, Cisco, Crestron, Extron, SMART, Shure or other A/V/Collaboration manufacturer preferred</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key Competencies/Behaviors</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to delegate as needed</li>
<li>Takes Initiative – sees tasks through to completion</li>
<li>Team oriented, works well with others</li>
<li>Planning and organizational skills</li>
<li>Adaptability and stress tolerance</li>
</ul>
