<h2>Commercial Insurance Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Locally owned and operated insurance agency currently seeking a friendly, knowledgeable and responsible Commercial Lines Customer Service Representative / Account Manager to join our team. You will be working with the account's main producer in servicing the client's insurance needs with professionalism and expertise.</p>
<p>If you have experience in any of the following areas: Workman's Comp, Commercial Umbrella, E&amp;O, Professional Liability, Commercial General Liability, Commercial Property, Commercial Auto, Employment Practices, Bonds, and Business Package, then send us your resume.</p>
<p>This position does not handle claims, annuities, life insurance, health insurance or personal lines.</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>** Prior Commercial Lines experience required</li>
<li>A valid California P &amp; C license is preferred</li>
<li>High school diploma, GED, or higher. College degree a plus</li>
<li>Must be self-directed with excellent time management skills</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written skills. First rate telephone skills are a must</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, including knowledge of Microsoft Office programs, email, internet and insurance related software</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Functions will include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>complete servicing of accounts,</li>
<li>quoting and marketing new business and renewals</li>
<li>contributing to a team effort by accomplishing all related tasks as necessary</li>
</ul>
<p><b>We know it's important to take care of our associates so we provide the following: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Medical Insurance</li>
<li>401(k)</li>
<li>Paid vacation, holidays and sick leave as well as a competitive salary</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ABOUT US</b></p>
<p>Established in 1980, we are an independent insurance agency; our goal is to assist our clients with all their insurance needs. We manage and plan for all their potential risks. As professionals, we assess their needs and offer a variety of insurance products to choose from. Service, whether it is in response to an insurance claim or general question, is the most important value we offer. We work hard to build a trusted relationship with our customers. We are an organization dedicated to our customers. We seek out the best companies that offer the appropriate coverages at a fair price.</p>
