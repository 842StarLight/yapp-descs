<h2>Digital Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Seeking an experienced, full-time Digital Sales Manager to independently develop and execute social media platforms along with managing marketing and sales campaigns for the company’s products. A primary responsibility of this position will be sales.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sell products online/e-commerce</li>
<li>Direct social media campaigns</li>
<li>Produce compelling content for social media</li>
<li>Design visual assets to be included in social media and email campaigns</li>
<li>Engage with other organizations and businesses on social media in order to promote company’s products and updates</li>
<li>Plan and execute paid advertising campaigns on social media, example: Facebook Ads</li>
<li>Plan, create and launch email campaigns; organize email lists</li>
<li>Analyze the performance of social media and email campaigns, create reports and produce innovative improvement strategies</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate Degree in Marketing, Communications, Digital Media or related field, or 3 years of related experience</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Understanding of online/e-commerce selling and demonstrated sales achievement in this format</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills</li>
<li>Great working knowledge of back-end social media management, ability to create and manage Facebook ads and integrate them with websites</li>
<li>Experience managing web-based email automation software, such as MailChimp, Constant Contact, SendGrid or similar</li>
<li>Ability to create, edit and customize email templates</li>
<li>Familiarity with web technologies, specifically HTML and CSS</li>
<li>Great visual design skills</li>
<li>Proficiency with graphic design software such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, Canva, or similar</li>
</ul>
<p>Please include your resume and salary requirement. We encourage candidates to submit writing samples/examples of visual design work. In lieu of an online portfolio, you are welcome to submit samples in a PDF attachment.</p>
<p><b>
About You First Services:</b></p>
<p>
Established in 2013, You First Services, Inc., seeks out advancements in healthcare through innovative products. The Company acquires and commercializes unique, healthcare-related technologies with high market potential.</p>
