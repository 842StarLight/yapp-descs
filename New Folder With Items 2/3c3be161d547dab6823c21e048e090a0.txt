<h2>Regional Loss Prevention Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Overview
<br><br>
Icahn Automotive Group LLC (Icahn Automotive) was formed by its parent, Icahn Enterprises L.P. (NASDAQ: IEP), to invest in and operate businesses involved in aftermarket parts distribution and service. Our businesses have a singular focus: provide premium automotive parts and services at a great value. The businesses of Icahn Automotive today consist of Pep Boys® automotive aftermarket retail and service chain, Auto Plus® automotive aftermarket parts distributor, Precision Tune Auto Care® owned and franchised automotive service centers, and AAMCO Total Auto Care franchised service centers. The businesses of Icahn Automotive total over 22,000 employees, over 2,000 company-owned and franchise locations and 25 distribution centers throughout the US, Canada, and Puerto Rico.
<br><br>
Position Summary
<br><br>
The Regional Loss Prevention Manager implements loss prevention and safety objectives, and administers loss prevention and safety programs. The position provides assistance and training to the field operations teams to address specific loss prevention and safety issues within an assigned span of control. The position also drives results by forging strong business relationships and working in a team environment to service internal and external customers.
<br><br>
This position will support 100 - 120 stores in central and southern CA. This person must reside in the Los Angeles metropolitan area.
<br><br>
Duties &amp; Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage the implementation of programs designed to protect Company assets from loss due to internal/external theft, fraud and unsafe practices within a designated geographical area.</li>
<li>Utilize all company and departmental activity performance reporting to identify risks and mitigate issues that reduce loss and maximize profitability.</li>
<li>Analyze major areas of shrink opportunity using reporting and software analysis tools.</li>
<li>Partner with Operations on the design and implementation of loss prevention policies that lessen shrink, protect Company assets, promote team member safety, and enhance customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Develop and implement training programs and initiatives to combat loss prevention issues, reduce shrink, and enhance safety.</li>
<li>Partner with Human Resources, Legal, and Field Leaders to conduct investigations, operational assessments and to create action plans that address areas of concern.</li>
<li>Adhere to standardized reporting and timely entry, follow up and completion of cases in LPMS.</li>
<li>Complete Loss Prevention Operational and Safety Store Assessments with emphasis on teaching and coaching by offering solutions to issues within company policy and procedure.</li>
<li>Execute the company Target Store Program by consistently reviewing performance metrics and the compliance to the program within region based on established criteria. (Approximately 20% of stores in region).</li>
<li>Effectively plan travel to all markets within the region, including overnight travel, to maximize productivity and minimize expenses. Come in at or under travel budget.</li>
<li>Assist as needed with investigations and mitigation of claims (WC, GL and Auto).</li>
<li>Assist with onboarding of all new Area Directors to also see the store through the eyes of Loss Prevention.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
