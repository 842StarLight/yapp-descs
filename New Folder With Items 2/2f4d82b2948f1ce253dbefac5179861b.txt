<h2>Account-Based Marketing (ABM) Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>As a Pluralsight Account-Based Marketing (ABM) Manager, you will be responsible for the execution of account research, ABM program design and execution, and reporting. The role is responsible for:</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide research on accounts and account contacts within account portfolio.</li>
<li>
Prioritize and group accounts according to opportunity, buyer need, persona, solution and buying cycle.</li>
<li>
Oversee offer creation and determine the optimal tactic mix for named accounts.</li>
<li>
Work cross-functionally with sales, digital marketing and field marketing to define and establish campaign workflows.</li>
<li>
Execute marketing programs on behalf of sales and show reps how to execute program elements on their own.</li>
<li>
Partner with content teams to provide campaign customization according to the analysis of buyer need</li>
<li>
Conduct A/B testing of messaging to improve response and conversion rates</li>
<li>
Produce weekly reports on campaign performance and provide recommendations for optimization.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you’ll own:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Execution of ABM programs, including account selection, account research and planning, leverage ABM and digital marketing technologies, as well as measurement and reporting.</li>
<li>
Drive personalization and scale focused on segments and personas.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain strong relationships with internal stakeholders, including sales, corporate marketing, product marketing, and field marketing.</li>
<li>
Try new and creative tactics, develop 1:1, and 1: few programs designed to penetrate targeted accounts.</li>
<li>
Provide data and insights on strategic accounts, as well as contacts within these accounts.</li>
<li>
Educate sales on the role of marketing within strategic accounts.</li>
<li>
Ensure that each activity is tracked and measured. Report on performance with a focus on pipeline and ROI. Utilize data and dashboards to inform future programs and investments.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Experience you’ll need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business, or comparable education/experience</li>
<li>
4+ years of experience in a SaaS-based B2B demand generation or ABM role or combination</li>
<li>
Proven track record designing and executing innovative account-based marketing programs.</li>
<li>
Experience with Marketo, Engagio, and Salesforce.com a plus</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate and collaborate well with others</li>
<li>
Data-driven &amp; extremely organized</li>
<li>
Flexibility to work in a rapidly changing environment</li>
</ul>
