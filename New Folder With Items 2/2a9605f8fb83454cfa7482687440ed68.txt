<p><b>Sales and Business Developer</b></p>
<p><b>Introduction: </b></p>
<p>We are looking for a highly motivated sales professional to work with our East Coast facility to grow and develop our sales and customer base. GenComm offers many things to its employees but above all the people is our difference. We have a strong family-oriented organization that is more concerned with ‘what it right’ instead of ‘who is right’! If this sounds like you and you are wanting to take on a challenge, contact us!</p>
<p><b>Company Info: </b></p>
<p>With offices in Salt Lake City, Utah and Manassas, Virginia, GENCOMM™ is principally engaged in the design and integration of advanced audio and visual communications systems.</p>
<p>As a systems integrator and information technology company, GENCOMM™ provides communications products and services to a wide variety of customers both nationally and internationally. Since its beginning, GENCOMM™ has provided thousands of communications solutions to customers in every state in the United States of America, in every province in Canada, and in numerous countries around the world.</p>
<p><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<p>You will have many different responsibilities and they will change as the East Coast business landscape changes. Here’s a view of what an average day would be:</p>
<ul>
<li>Contacting customers and showing them the latest developments in audio and video technology</li>
<li>Planning and meeting a yearly and quarterly sales goal</li>
<li>Working with Federal and Local Government to write contracts</li>
<li>Travel to and from customer sites</li>
<li>Using a basic CRM to track your opportunities and sales</li>
<li>Communicating with your Sales Director and making strategic growth plans</li>
<li>Job location is the greater DC area, with travel requirements to multiple points inside the territory - this will not have a routine commute</li>
<li>and other activities</li>
</ul>
