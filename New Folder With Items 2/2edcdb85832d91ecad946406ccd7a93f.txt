<h2>Account Executive (Wireless/Fiber)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>VIAVI (NASDAQ: VIAV) has a 90+ year history of technical innovations that have evolved to keep pace and address our customer’s most pressing business issues. We make equipment, software, and systems that help to plan, deploy, certify, monitor, and optimize all kinds of networks - like those for mobile phones, service providers, large businesses and data centers. VIAVI is also a leader in high performance thin film optical coatings and engineered diffusers, providing light management solutions to anti-counterfeiting, consumer electronics, automotive, defense and instrumentation markets.</p>
<p>We are the people behind the products that help keep the world connected – at home, school, work, at play, and everywhere in between. VIAVI employees are fierce about supporting customer success and we welcome people who bring their best every day to the company - to question, to collaborate and to push for solutions that will delight our customers.</p>
<p><b>Position/Team Overview</b>:</p>
<p>VIAVI Solutions is looking for a new team member for the North American Sales group who will have a primary focus on the Wireless and Fiber contractors &amp; independent operators in North America. Ideal candidate will be based in or near a large metropolitan city to allow for quick and easy access to a regional or international airport.</p>
<p>Applicant will be responsible for and focused on Wireless Telecom Contractors as well as Fiber Construction Companies/Contractors but other focuses may include Tier 3 Cable, Telco, &amp; Broadband Customers as well as their supporting Contractors. As part of this sales team you will report to the North American Cable Sales Manager while working in close cooperation with the national cable sales team, inside sales, systems &amp; solutions engineers, as well as manufacture rep partners to drive sales of both existing and new customers in assigned territory.</p>
<p>This role will also be responsible for meeting and/or exceeding committed forecasts, bookings targets, increasing market share and gaining new product approvals within assigned customer base and territory. This is an assigned quota carrying sales position.</p>
