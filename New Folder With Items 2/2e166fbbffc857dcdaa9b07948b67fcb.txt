<p><b>
About the Role</b></p>
<p>
The Portfolio &amp; Business Development Specialist will be responsible for managing the funnel of partner solutions, onboarding support, and Portfolio Marketing (mapping &amp; merchandizing) in SAP Digital Commerce properties.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This role will work with partners to bring their offerings to market digitally. the individual will own the portfolio/product funnel, prioritize those partner offerings with a revenue generating business case, and report on execution process. The ideal candidate has a mix of marketing and business development skills, enjoys being hands-on with office and technical tools, and is a people person (can work with anyone!).<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You have experience with marketing program management, but a good handle on business. As part of a highly collaborative team, you will not only focus on growth but also bring process improvement ideas to enhance operational efficiency.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
