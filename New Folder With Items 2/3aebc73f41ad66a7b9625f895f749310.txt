<h2>Purchasing Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Title: Purchasing Agent</p>
<p>Reports to: Purchasing &amp; Facilities Manager</p>
<p><b>Summary/Objective</b>The purchasing agent negotiates vendor terms, prices and schedules delivery using knowledge of budget and schedule requirements.</p>
<p><b>Job Duties and Accountabilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Edit, review, and process purchase requisitions for compliance with company policies, and procedures, regulations and requirements.</li>
<li>Prepare bids and quotes, comply with legal requirements for advertised bids, locate and</li>
</ul>
<p>review sources of supply and analyze bids for compliance and content.</p>
<ul>
<li>Assure authorized signatures are obtained in accordance with policies.</li>
<li>Distribute purchase orders and documents to vendors, sites, and departments.</li>
<li>Obtain confirmation from vendor on acceptance of issued purchase orders.</li>
<li>Maintain computer databases and master files on purchase orders, inventory items, and documentation.</li>
<li>Process purchase orders in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Assist staff in obtaining information on prices, availability and delivery of purchases.</li>
<li>Maintain vendor lists, vendor profiles, and inventory records in Pace. This includes adding new parts into inventory system and maintaining correct pricing for all inventory parts.</li>
<li>Work with Controller to make sure that all coding of inventory and purchased products are correct.</li>
<li>Contact vendors regarding purchase order status.</li>
<li>Assist in reconcile packing slips and invoices with accounts payable.</li>
<li>Follow up on back orders to obtain timely delivery.</li>
<li>Provide support lease agreements and contracts.</li>
<li>Work with Controller regarding fixed assets purchases and physical inventory system in accordance with regulations.</li>
<li>Respond to inquiries regarding status of purchase orders and requisitions.</li>
<li>Assist independent auditors in auditing purchasing orders.</li>
<li>Perform required audit activities.</li>
<li>Support in the budget development process.</li>
<li>Perform year-end closing duties such as closing of all purchase orders, cleaning of year-end files and transporting to storage etc.</li>
<li>Coordinates weekly cycle counts of inventory materials based upon computer-monitored suggested re-order listing; assists in conducting complete physical inventory count annually.</li>
<li>Organizes and maintains warehouse and inventory yard areas for efficient material storage and handling; maintains labeling system on each stock item; manually stocks inventory shelving with stock items received or returned.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates Degree in field related to printing technology, finance, business management.</li>
<li>3-5 years of purchasing experience or an equivalent combination of education and experience. Prefer experience in the print industry; digital wide format a plus.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Excel, Word, Outlook</li>
<li>Detail oriented, well organized, able to work calmly under pressure, work as a team member.</li>
<li>Must have excellent verbal and writing communication skills to facilitate transfer of information concerning new products, items, or any other specific information.</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable in current trends in printing.</li>
<li>Experience with EFI/PACE Software a plus.</li>
</ul>
