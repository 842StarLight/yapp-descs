<h2>Senior Manager , Product Management - Training Management Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Be a part of Boeing Global Services' Training &amp; Professional Services (T&amp;PS) team that is at the leading edge of flight safety, flight training, and flight operations. As the Senior Manager of Product Management for Training Management Solutions, you will have the opportunity to work with professionals from across the industry and positively affect safety and operations of Boeing flight operations and that of our customers, world-wide. In this high visibility position you will interact with pilots, managers, regulators, executives, peers, supporting team personnel, and Subject Matter Experts. The incumbent will be responsible for leading the creation and deployment of Operational Commercial Digital Learning Solutions for Pilots, Mechanics, Cabin Crew and additional Operational roles.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead the T&amp;PS Training Management Solutions Product Management team creating a culture and environment of execution and delivery</li>
<li>
Drive multiple concurrent product teams providing autonomy, while ensuring alignment to broader product portfolio and commitments</li>
<li>
Relentlessly push to understand customer needs and ensure customer is considered in prioritization and trade-off discussions</li>
<li>
Collaborate with teammates across TPS, BGS, BCA, Marketing, Sales, BR&amp;T, IT, SM, IPM, HorizonX, Strategy and beyond to bring new products to market</li>
<li>
Drive make/buy/partner analysis and decisions to create a holistic solution that create the most value for our customers and Boeing</li>
<li>
Set product vision and lead road mapping, goal setting, story writing, project execution, A/B testing, and product roll-out strategy</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Identify unmet needs and disruption opportunities and guide interdisciplinary teams to deliver innovative products to market.</li>
<li>
Test and iterate in small-scale pilots to validate concepts, refine them to achieve product-market fit, and then scale to drive vertical and horizontal economics</li>
<li>
Define learning agenda and key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure success/failure</li>
<li>
Regularly and systematically use data to uncover opportunities, track product performance, and drive decision making</li>
<li>
Leads a team through rigorous process of test and learn with focus on identifying and developing a minimum viable product (MVP)</li>
<li>
Prioritize, negotiate and remove blockers to orchestrate successful launch and optimization cycle</li>
<li>
Serve as subject matter expert, contributing practitioner, thought leader, career guide and mentor to teams</li>
<li>
Establish or participate in Design Thinking, Agile, and LEAN rituals which establish rhythm for the team, promote ongoing communication, and iterative refinement of roadmaps</li>
</ul>
