<p><b>
Group/Division</b>
</p>
<p>The Global Service Support Organization (GSS) team headquartered in Milpitas, CA is our service organization that consists of Service Sales and Marketing, Spares Supply Chain management, Field Operations, Engineering, Product Training, and Technical Support. The GSS organization partners with our field teams and customers in all business sectors to maintain the high performance and productivity of our products through a flexible portfolio of services. Our comprehensive services include: proactive management of tools to identify and improve performance; expertise in optics, image processing and motion control with worldwide service engineers, 24/7 technical support teams and knowledge management systems; and an extensive parts network to ensure worldwide availability of parts.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b>
</p>
<p>The successful candidate will work with a team of engineers to establish and improve tools maintenance through innovative solutions that include electrical design, using sensors, signal analysis, and data acquisition. Our tools are highly complex and electrical design is important in strong maintenance model of the tools in the field. The candidate will be able to review certain aspects of the design and suggest changes or additions to the tools to make them more supportable and predictable. The candidate will participate or drive certain designs that are quick to prototype and then drive the bench and system-level testing to prove the concept. The candidate will collaborate with systems engineers, software, and data engineers to prove these solutions in timely manner.</p>
<p>
We are looking for highly motivated self-driven individual with knowledge of electronics system design, electrical sub-system design, power systems, embedded system design, or motion/servo control system integration to support KLA products</p>
