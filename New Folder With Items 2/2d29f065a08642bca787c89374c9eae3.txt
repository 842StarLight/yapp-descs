<h2>Buyer-Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for executing the supply chain strategy for assigned commodities. Accountable and responsible for developing and implementing a supply chain strategy that:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Delivers the lowest total cost,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reduces inventory, and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maximizes customer satisfaction through world class on time delivery, lead time and quality metrics.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Principle Duties (includes, but may not be limited to):</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>For assigned commodities, develop and lead the execution of supply chain strategy that supports Crane Resistoflex objectives for Material Cost Reduction, Supplier OTD, Inventory Reduction, Supplier Quality, and Supplier Lead Times.This would include actions to rationalize the supply base, drive increased sourcing from low cost sources, leverage the material spend with other Crane business units, and negotiate and implement Supplier Agreements.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain awareness of world markets and emerging material related issues in the industry and position the supply chain to flexibly respond accordingly.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Establish and facilitate strategic teams to leverage supplier capabilities in product design, cost reduction, lead time reduction and customer satisfaction.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work closely with the site supply chain, manufacturing, and engineering teams to proactively identify and develop external capability to support strategic out-sourcing opportunities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Plan and monitor implementation of re-sourcing initiatives to successfully prime supplier’s supply chains for initial production to ensure uninterrupted flow of product to customers as required.Oversee and facilitate multilevel material planning to ensure the integrity of the supplier’s supply chain in support of planned deliverables.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Obtain competitive bids, analyze costs and negotiate/award contracts to vendors on the basis of total costs to the company commensurate with financial risks, quality standards, and delivery capabilities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Leverage technology solutions to maximize effectiveness and efficiency of the strategic sourcing process.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Drive increasing inventory turns by developing, implementing and managing innovative material replenishment processes, including, but not limited to Kanbans, vendor managed inventory, and supplier consignments.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Consistent with Crane’s Operational Excellence focus, lead Value Stream Mapping and Kaizen events that will eliminate waste and result in significant benefits (Growth, Safety, Quality, Delivery, Cost) throughout the Supply Chain.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Drive continuous improvement in the supply base by communicating objective performance metrics, focused on lead time reduction, on-time delivery improvement, quality improvement, and cost reduction.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide effective support to new product introduction teams, proposal development, and subcontracting opportunities as they develop.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Engender a spirit of cooperation throughout the supply chain to ensure responsiveness to the customer.</p>
</li>
<li>Accomplishes all tasks as appropriately assigned or requested.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS/EXPERIENCE</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS/BA in business or technical discipline</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Broad knowledge of the marketplace and manufacturing technologies for assigned commodities as desired.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong negotiation and contract management skills.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Self starter. Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>In depth knowledge of supplier qualification processes and supplier performance metrics.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Direct experience implementing lean concepts (Value Stream Mapping, Kaizen Leadership, 5S, etc.). Lean/Six Sigma certification (black belt or green belt) is desired.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience implementing a pull system/kanban approach to component flow.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience with global sourcing.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Excellent analytical, problem solving/decision making skills; ability to make timely, effective, fact based decisions. Including a strong working knowledge of work processing, spreadsheet and scheduling software.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and demonstrated ability to perform and produce results in multifunctional teams in a dynamic work environment.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Proven ability to develop collaborative relationships and influence up, down, and across organizational lines.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience with eSourcing tools</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Willingness to travel 5%</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Certification in APICS and/or ISM is highly desired.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Customer-oriented focus with a creative, aggressive and winning attitude.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Good organizational and multi-task skills.</li>
</ul>
