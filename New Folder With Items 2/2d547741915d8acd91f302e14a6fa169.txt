<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Dematic is a leading supplier of integrated automated technology, software and services to optimize the supply chain. Dematic employs over 7,000 skilled logistics professionals to serve its customers globally, with engineering centers and manufacturing facilities located around the world. Dematic is one brand under the KION Group of companies and has implemented more than 6,000 integrated systems for a customer base that includes small, medium and large companies doing business in a variety of market sectors.<br>
<br>
Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, Dematic is a member of KION Group, a global leader in industrial trucks, related services and supply chain solutions. Across more than 100 countries worldwide, the KION Group designs, builds and supports logistics solutions that optimize material and information flow within factories, warehouses and distribution centers. The company is the largest manufacturer of industrial trucks in Europe, the second-largest producer of forklifts globally and a leading provider of warehouse automation.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Role
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Dematic Corp. has an immediate need for an After Market Account Manager ito support the continual growth of Dematic’s North America Customer Service business. This position is responsible for assisting and selling Dematic’s product upgrades and solutions to current key and regional accounts.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Role and Responsibilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The After Market Account Manager is the face of Dematic with the customer. The After Market Account Manager presents product upgrades and enhancements from Dematic, and demonstrates Dematic’s lifecycle value by communicating the lifecycle solutions that Dematic brings to the marketplace. This role gathers initial customer needs and collaborates with Dematic engineering and solution development teams to provide the best solution to the customer.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Develops and implements plans for strategic accounts.
</li>
<li>Drives revenue retention/growth, account profitability, and customer satisfaction/loyalty.</li>
<li>
Pursues long-term product strategy that maximizes profits.</li>
<li>
Develops an understanding of the customers' organizational structure and key buying influences related to product enhancements and upgrades.</li>
<li>
Performs customer presentations articulating the value proposition of product/solution offerings.</li>
<li>
Provides management with suggestions for improving volume, market share and price levels.
</li>
<li>Develops forecasts, budgets and operating plans for sales channels.</li>
</ul>
