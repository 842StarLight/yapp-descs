<p><b>Position Summary</b><br>
The primary responsibility of the Inside Sale/Technicial Support Representative is to use problem solving skills to provide the highest degree of technical support to our customers and Account managers by and coordinate activities.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
? Provide support as needed to customers, outside sales staff and management.<br>
? Expediting of sales orders and communicating status of the orders with those involved.<br>
? Process requests for quotation. Requests over $1000.00 need to be coordinated with the appropriate<br>
outside salesman.<br>
? Process customer orders received via fax, phone, e-mail and mail.<br>
? Become efficient in the use of Trulinx, Phconnect, Goldmine and TOPS.<br>
? Review requests for corrective action from customers and submit reports/results to inside sales manager and quality manager.<br>
? Utilize good communication skills and present yourself in a professional manner.<br>
? Maintain a database of action items.<br>
? Provide price-discounting information for entry in Trulinx.<br>
? Utilize your time and assets wisely.<br>
? Actively seek out and suggest ideas for changes that will enhance departmental and/or company effectiveness and profitability.<br>
? Maintain a positive and professional attitude with customers when faced with unreasonable demands or attitudes. Obtain complete information about customer problems or complaints and ensure that they are resolved in a timely manner. Advise your supervisor of unresolved or ongoing problems with quality of customer service.<br>
? Process customer returns and requests for credit in accordance with company policy and procedures.<br>
? Processing of returns should be given the same attention that would be given to new order entry.<br>
? Comply with all company safety policies and procedures. Report all injuries to your supervisor. Report any safety violations, questions or concerns to your supervisor.<br>
? Keep all promises made to your customers that are within Comoso control.<br>
? Support Store sales as needed.<br>
? Support Store receiving and put away as needed.<br>
? Handles technical trouble shooting walk in customers.<br>
? Support store cycle counting and bin management responsibilities.<br>
? Make hose assemblies when needed.<br>
? Inside Sales Reps will be added to help groups both e-mail and phone, where deemed necessary.</p>
<p><b>Nonessential Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
? Individuals will be faced with additional duties and responsibilities.</p>
