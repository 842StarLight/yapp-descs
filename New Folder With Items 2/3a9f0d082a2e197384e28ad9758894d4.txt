<p><b>Retention Admin</b></p>
<p>
VineBrook Homes is an internally managed Midwest/Heartland-based real estate investment company, focused on acquiring, renovating, leasing and operating single-family homes as rental properties. We began operations in late 2007. We own or manage over 6,000 Single-Family Rental homes SFR in 12 states and 18 major Midwest/Heartland markets. Additionally, VineBrook Homes is currently undergoing the next major expansion of its strategy, expecting to drive toward 10,000-15,000+ Homes.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Overview</b>
</p>
<p>The Retention Administrator is responsible for securing lease renewals from existing residents, in an effort to maintain resident occupancy. This is accomplished by executing upon a renewal plan as defined by the Portfolio Manager. In addition to executing leases, the Retention Administrator is responsible for assisting current residents with concerns regarding their lease or their home. The Retention Administrator must maintain a positive relationship with our current residents by providing excellent customer service.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Functions as the primary point of contact for all existing resident lease experations and process all lease renewals.</li>
<li>
Providing customer service to current residents with regards to lease changes.</li>
<li>
Enforcing Lease and Resident Expectations.</li>
<li>
Provides Support with Resident Relations.</li>
<li>
Special Projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

Qualifications</b></p>

1-2 years' Experience in property Management</li>
