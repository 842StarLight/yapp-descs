<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
As one of the world's leading providers of CDN and Cloud Services and the largest CDN providers in China, ChinaCache North America offers both a challenging and rewarding career opportunity in an environment that puts your dream into action. Our beliefs in providing high performance, reliable and flexible solution services drive us to stay on top of technology and build one of the most advanced and dependable networks.</p>
<p>
Based in the greater Los Angeles area, we assist foreign companies in various industries across the globe in entering the China market and guiding them through the complex Internet landscape in China. ChinaCache is headquartered in Beijing. We are proud of the creative and passionate individuals who enjoy being part of the lively, unique cross-cultured environment. We are currently looking for a passionate, energetic, and driven Business Development Representative to enhance our salesforce.</p>
<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
BDR is an entry-level role where you will be reporting to the Sales Director and the BDR team leader as a dotted-line reporting structure. You will have a career path to promote to the Sales Account Manager based on the objective completion within 12-18 months. In this role, your main goal is to support and work directly with the Sales and Marketing team, and to expand the US market via different channels (mainly outbound/outreach and event networking). You will also be expected to attend meetings, meet the channel partners and trade shows and exhibitions to keep up with the market trends.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reach out to prospective customers who have registered an interest in our solution to identify the business needs, technical requirements, and procurement process. Then convert to a qualified opportunity and forward it to the Sales Director.</li>
<li>Generate new opportunities or schedule meetings with relevant people who showed interest in hearing about the ChinaCache solutions through LinkedIn, own network, or other sources. Conduct business development and strategy plan together with the Sales Director</li>
<li>Support Sales Reps with appointment setting, discovery calls, initial quotations, and administrative tasks.</li>
<li>Obtain basic technical requirements and initiate detailed product discussions - training provides</li>
<li>Clarify customer needs beyond what may have been initially apparent to the prospect.</li>
<li>Use available outreach and sales tools and document activity accurately in Salesforce</li>
<li>Engage and keep regular contact with your pipeline accounts</li>
<li>There will be a small amount of travel required when deemed necessary for your development within the role.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experienced in inside sales, telemarketing, or telesales business environment. -technology-focused advantageous but not critical</li>
<li>History of working to a KPI of the qualified achievement set forth monthly or quarterly</li>
<li>Work in a technology-focused organization with an understanding of Cloud Computing, SaaS, Collaboration, CDN, or a Degree in a Computer Science field advantageous</li>
<li>Outstanding skills in communication and relationship building</li>
<li>Ability to extract information and business requirement from prospects.</li>
<li>Self-motivated and organized</li>
<li>Desire to work cohesively with the Sales Director and strive for continuous progress</li>
<li>Capability to quickly understand and demonstrate technology and business value propositions.</li>
<li>Familiar with Salesforce, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Skype for Business, Office &amp; Zoom, etc.</li>
<li>Knowledge of additional languages are an advantage but not essential</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Additional Information</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive compensation package with full benefits</li>
<li>Comprehensive medical and dental care, matching 401K, paid sick, personal and vacation days</li>
</ul>
