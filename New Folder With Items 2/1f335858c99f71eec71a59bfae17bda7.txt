<h2>Mortgage Loan Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Residential Home Funding Corp. is among the largest and most respected mortgage bankers in the country. </b>We are truly passionate about helping our customers as well as the communities we live and work in.</p>
<p>We give back to the communities through charitable giving, charitable events and our registered charity, RHF Foundation Inc.</p>
<p><b>We take pride in maintaining a comprehensive mortgage product platform, fostering communication, supporting our sales personnel, and remaining an innovative leader in the industry</b>.</p>
<p><b>The Platform: </b> We will recruit Loan Officers to your team / branch. We offer a full spectrum of mortgage programs; Qualified Mortgages (QM) and full line of Non-Qualified Mortgages (NON-QM), access to Institutional Investors and Hedge Funds, In-House Commercial Lending, internal marketing team and wide-ranging processing and underwriting capabilities without overlays.</p>
<p><b>Opening Doors: </b> We provide you with a unique value proposition to offer your business partners making opening doors easy. We maintain a dedicated marketing and advertising department to support you.</p>
<p><b>The Mortgage Loan Officer: </b> generates and develops their business through relationships and networks, completes applications, conducts preliminary analysis, identifies information gaps from borrower(s), submits loan applications to processing and underwriting for approval. Prompt communication with operational employees; provide all required information to underwriting, etc.</p>
