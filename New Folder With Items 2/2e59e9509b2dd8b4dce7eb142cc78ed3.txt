<h2>Enterprise Sales Executive (Orlando)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About The Opportunity
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Here at Grubhub we’ve been dedicated to giving diners the most convenient way to order food from their favorite restaurants (whether that’s a late night Chalupa from Taco Bell or a salad for lunch from a local restaurant the day after they enjoyed said late night Chalupa).<br>
<br>
While we are food-obsessed, we are also customer-obsessed. We look to constantly innovate our technology so our diners’ food experience is memorable, restaurant owners get more business and individuals across the country looking for work can deliver the food from the restaurant to the diner flawlessly. We take great pride in knowing that we are a part of 20+ million diners food ordering experience and we are partnered with 125,000 restaurants in 2,400 US cities across our suite of apps (Grubhub, LevelUp, Seamless, Tapingo, AllMenus and MenuPages).<br>
<br>
Want to be a part of the biggest movement in the US that is moving eating forward? If so, we want to talk to you - and hear what’s your favorite restaurant for food delivery!</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Some Challenges You’ll Tackle
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Grubhub’s Corporate Sales team connects companies to thousands of restaurants with fast online ordering, professional delivery, and streamlined billing management. Our pitch? It makes sense to have a corporate account for office supplies...it makes sense for food, too!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We’re looking for an experienced Sales Executive to successfully prospect and close new corporate accounts in the LA market. You’ll focus on finding mid-market and enterprise companies who realize the value in streamlining their food ordering processes - from catering to individual meals to group orders - and gaining more control over their time and budget.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This is a great opportunity for an accomplished sales executive with a track record of bringing in new logos and busting quotas.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Accelerating the growth of enterprise sales through selling Grubhub’s products and services</li>
<li>
Developing a strong understanding of key differentiators and the competitive landscape</li>
<li>
Diligently prospecting. You will work to develop a strategic plan that ensures target prospects know your name and how you can help them</li>
<li>
Build relationships and sell consultatively to drive buying decisions of VP, Director, and C-Level executives at enterprise companies</li>
<li>
Work with customers to quantify opportunities via a detailed needs analysis, and creates solutions to deliver and ensure a smooth end to end sales process</li>
<li>
Manage pipeline and sales activity with an ability to accurately forecast</li>
<li>
Find creative solutions to challenging problems</li>
<li>
Effectively communicate thoughts and ideas with peers</li>
<li>
Willing to risk comfort level and step into unknown projects/tasks</li>
<li>
And other exciting projects assigned by your manager</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
