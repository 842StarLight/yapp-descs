<h2>Product Adoption Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview:
</b></p>
<p>Are you ready to help change the world? At Salsa Labs we build software to help nonprofits make a difference through best in class CRM, marketing automation, advocacy, Peer-2-Peer and online fundraising tools. At Salsa, we’re not your typical SaaS company - we represent a community of more than 3,000 nonprofit and political organizations dedicated to everything from ending hunger to eliminating nuclear weapons to providing playgrounds for children. Sound like a great place to work? It most definitely is.</p>
<p>
Salsa Lab’s Client Adoption program combines product implementation and on-boarding with online fundraising and constituent engagement best practices to give our non-profit customers a unique plan for reaching their fundraising goals. Our Product Adoption Specialists are responsible for leading the execution of Adoption projects including delivering 1:Many and 1:1 client presentations, guiding clients through asset development in the Salsa Labs product suite and providing strategic fundraising advice to clients regarding constituent segmentation, messaging and follow-up.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Own end-to-end delivery of Product Adoption activities including delivering client webinars and customer trainings (via web conferencing technology such as GoToMeeting)</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain client resources such as training guides</li>
<li>
Achieve 80% successful client adoption of Salsa’s product(s) within 60 days of project kickoff.</li>
<li>
Ensure Salsa’s Adoption program integrates fundraising and donor engagement best practices.</li>
<li>
Track client performance outcomes and product functionality use related to adoption services.</li>
<li>
Continuously identify opportunities for improvement to refine program and improve customer outcomes</li>
<li>
Work closely with Product On-boarding and Training teams to ensure program execution remains a smooth and effective experience for Salsa customers</li>
<li>
Deliver Product Adoption focused case studies, references, and showcase customer champions</li>
<li>
Work with Customer Success leadership to expand Product Adoption services to all Salsa products and customer segments.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What We’re Looking For:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years in client facing role such as customer service, sales, fundraising development, customer success, customer support, etc.</li>
<li>
Experience developing online fundraising, digital marketing, or donor engagement programs for nonprofit organizations</li>
<li>
Experience developing marketing/training collateral such as blogs, whitepapers, industry research, etc.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with nonprofit-specific online fundraising &amp; CRM software tools</li>
<li>
Familiarity with online engagement best practices</li>
<li>
Experience with Software-as-a-Service on-boarding/implementation/adoption a plus.</li>
<li>
Exceptional presentation and client communication skills (both written and verbal)</li>
<li>
A willingness to go the extra mile to kick start our client’s success</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Microsoft Office, Google business software</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience</li>
</ul>
