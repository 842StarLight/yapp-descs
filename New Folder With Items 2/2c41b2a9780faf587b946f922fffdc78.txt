<h2>Product Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b> This position will play an integral role in our company’s growth with innovation and new categories, as well as growing existing product lines. This Product Development position will be responsible for the, development and launch of profitable, marketable new products that focus on sporting goods. New product development will align with our company’s strategic vision, growth plans and profit objectives. The Product Development team member will be responsible for all aspects of the product launch from trend research, concept design, initial ideation stage through production, line management, and all stages of product life cycle.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and source consumer packaged products for the sporting goods industry.</li>
<li>Proactively develop innovative and cutting-edge products</li>
<li>Work closely with foreign and domestic suppliers to facilitate the manufacturing and distribution of products</li>
<li>Maintain a high level of detail with domestic and foreign suppliers, as well as in-house and contract team members</li>
<li>In-house product line expert supporting Sales and Marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of competitive products and market trends</li>
<li>Promote a safety culture</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to possess and expand knowledge of hunting and fishing items, designs, and industry trends that appeal to customers across a variety of price points</li>
<li>Maintain an in-depth understanding of hunting market and demographics as well as key competitors and customer base</li>
<li>Manufacturing knowledge of consumer-packaged products</li>
<li>Ability to think and react in a high energy, high sku, fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Good organizational skills including prioritizing, scheduling, time management, and meeting deadlines</li>
<li>Strong aptitude for learning new computer systems</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills, including grammar.</li>
<li>Ability to engage in multiple tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail</li>
<li>Effective problem-solving ability</li>
<li>Ability to recognize and react to changing work demands</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Effective team player with excellent interpersonal skills supporting a team environment</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Able to prioritize and effectively manage multiple projects without missing critical deadlines
</li>
<li>Ability to identify trends and product voids in marketplace
</li>
<li>Strong analytical and communication abilities, with the ability to work across functions
</li>
<li>Excellent teamwork and interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational skills and strong attention to detail
</li>
<li>High level of computer proficiency</li>
<li>Self-starter who can work independently on several tasks/projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Has a positive, “can do” attitude</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Nice to Have:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Technical design software knowledge: Adobe Creative Suite, Solid Works or similar</li>
<li>Passionate Outdoorsman with the knowledge to create unique hunting, fishing and camping items in multiple categories</li>
<li>Knowledge of metal fabrication or injection molding a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to travel domestically and internationally.</li>
<li>Standard office conditions</li>
<li>Regularly required to sit, stand, reach, bend and move about the office</li>
<li>Able to lift up to 25 lbs., infrequently</li>
</ul>
