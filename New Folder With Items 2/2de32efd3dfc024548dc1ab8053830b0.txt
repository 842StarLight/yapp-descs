At First Insight (FI) we are all about the ultimate outcome â€“ the delivery of measurable results to our customers. Our innovative solutions in advanced predictive analytics have taken the retail sector by storm, resulting in years of consistent, significant growth.</p>
<p>
We are seeking a high-performance, self-motivated, professional looking for an opportunity to help drive our business as a Key Account Manager. <div><b>
Responsibilities &amp; Duties</b></div>
<p>
The Key Account Manager's (KAM) primary responsibility is increasing customer loyalty by ensuring that:</p>
<ul>
<li>
FI solutions become an integral part of the customer's workflow</li>
<li>
FI solutions are adopted within and across our clients' functions.</li>
<li>
Opportunities for FI growth are identified with the customer</li>
</ul>
<p>
As a KAM at First Insight, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<i>
Demonstrate</i> how First Insight's solutions can positively impact a customers' strategic and tactical goals and deliver significant positive impact to operational performance.</li>
<li>
<i>
Showcase</i> current successes, highlighting the value and ROI of our products and solutions Transfer knowledge on how to best utilize the output of FI's solution suite to the customer.</li>
<li>
<i>
Guide</i> the customer in effective processes and methodologies to integrate FI solutions.</li>
<li>
<i>
Engage</i> in current activities in the retail industry and communicate to customers the strategic trends, opportunities and challenges that face them.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Specific accountabilities include:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage multiple customer implementations and projects/tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>
Assess &amp; evaluate new customer requirements against the scope of the project</li>
<li>
Manage project schedules, budgets, resources and tasks to ensure that quality solutions are delivered within time and cost constraints</li>
<li>
Identify, document, and manage project risks during implementations; escalate issues as appropriate</li>
<li>
Work with Business Analysts/Solution Engineers to translate business needs into technical requirements</li>
<li>
Prepare project status reports and presentations to keep management, customers and others informed of the project status and issues</li>
<li>
Drive usage and adoption of FI's software solution throughout a customer's organization</li>
<li>
Build relationships with key individuals at all levels of the customer</li>
<li>
Educate/train potential customers on how to use our solution</li>
<li>
Present and articulate advanced solutions and benefits and product future direction</li>
<li>
Facilitate executive level quarterly recaps</li>
<li>
Coordinate with virtual teams of sales, sales consultants, value engineering, development and delivery</li>
<li>
Act as an evangelist to position First Insight solution and methodology opportunities across the extended customer organization</li>
<li>
Educate &amp; Train potential customers on how to use our solution</li>
</ul>
