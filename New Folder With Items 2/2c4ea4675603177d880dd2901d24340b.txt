<b>
Job Overview and </b><b>Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<p><br>
Responsible for leading the ETL team in gathering data integration requirements, analyzing and mapping source and target data points, hands-on in writing code to integrate the data from various sources into Data warehouse. Actively involved in solving data integrity issues. Proficient in RDBMS with knowledge of Big Data. Manages the technical development independently and / or leveraging team consisting of both onsite and off-shore resources with the goal of delivering projects on-time and under budget. Engages and communicates with cross-functional stakeholders involved in work streams impacting their areas of responsibilities. Constantly learns and upgrades the skillset. Work closely with Project leads, IT SMEs, DBAs and Consultants to ensure timely delivery of the projects. Responsible for developing data integration strategy, processes, standards, tools and ensuring all work efforts have appropriate documentation for audit purposes utilizing MBUSA standards.<br>
<br>
The Data Integration Lead will be responsible for planning, organizing, developing, executing, evaluating and leading data integration projects from concept through delivery. Lead will gather business requirement, understand current and future data landscape and develop an effective information integration and delivery solutions for the enterprise.</p>
