<br><br>
The Named Account Executive is being brought in to close business with customer – and as such, you’ll have demonstrable experience in hitting quotas and targets consistently.
<br><br>
<b>Named Account Executive Role:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Create and deliver on a strategic territory plan</li>
<li>Coordinate internal resources to ensure prompt, accurate, and professional delivery of sales, marketing, and proposal materials to clients</li>
<li>Build and maintain relationship with client contacts to communicate strategic plans and status</li>
<li>Engage with clients over the phone, in person and via digital channels to ensure you stoke, capture and then deliver on client interest – you’ll take major revenue generating responsibility</li>
<li>Participate in user group meetings and trade shows as needed</li>
<li>Stay abreast of current industry trends, competitors, and current/new company products and services in order to educate potential and existing customers</li>
</ul>
<b>You:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>College degree educated (full Bachelor’s Degree required)</li>
<li>2 years’ experience or more in B2B phone marketing and/or sales</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record of closing business/selling over the phone</li>
<li>Relationship-led, you will possess a positive, customer orientated approach</li>
<li>Tenacious and resourceful</li>
<li>Ambitious, with an excellent work ethic</li>
</ul>
