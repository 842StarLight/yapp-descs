<h2>Communications Advisor II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<p>The individual in this role will be responsible for enhancing our internal initiatives via all mediums to improve how we connect with our various stakeholders. Works with the Internal Communications team and company leaders to create associate communications that inform and engage, while reflecting our employment brand.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>The incumbent(s) in this position should exhibit the following ACOSTA values:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>People Minded</b> – Must show dignity and respect to all people</li>
<li>
<b>Integrity</b> – Must exemplify the highest degree of ethical behavior</li>
<li>
<b>Results Oriented</b> – Must show passion, pride and commitment to succeed</li>
<li>
<b>Trust</b> – Must be honest, sincere and confident</li>
<li>
<b>Teamwork</b> – Must build trusting relationships</li>
<li>
<b>Innovation</b> – Must progress through a combination of creativity, common sense and vision</li>
<li>
<b>Balance</b> – Must maintain an optimistic attitude and keep perspective on what is important in life</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<ul><li> Identify, write, review and edit content to ensure compliance with established brand and style guidelines.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Lead large HR special projects and tasks</li>
<li>
Liaison to HR Operations / Total Rewards</li>
<li>
Lead or support sensitive communications as needed (org restructures, executive changes, etc.)</li>
<li>
Intranet strategy and long-term plan</li>
<li>
Maintain calendar of key meetings</li>
<li>
Associate engagement communications strategy and execution</li>
<li>
Emergency communications strategy and execution</li>
<li>
Communications liaison to Foodservice, Reach, ActionLink</li>
<li>
Develop and manage “communications ambassadors” for business unit representation</li>
<li>
Primary administrator for MyAcosta</li>
<li>
Create and maintain leader biographies as needed</li>
<li>
Support or lead other projects or tasks as assigned</li>
</ul>
<ul><li> Source, develop and publish content across internal communications channels.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Serve as the general webmaster for the intranet and work closely with other team members and other departments to create and execute appropriate strategies for intranet content.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Create and execute communication strategies to support corporate recognition programs; IT initiatives or product rollouts; emergency communications, and others as assigned;
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage and procure distribution lists to support all internal communications initiatives.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Contribute to and help maintain the annual editorial content calendar.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Support and/or execute internal communications tactics via a range of technologies,including standard email, an email management tool, SharePoint Online (intranet), survey tools, web-based meeting software, and other tools as appropriate.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Actively engage with the Internal Communications team on department strategy.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Coordinate with freelance staff, as necessary</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain calendar of key meetings</li></ul>
<ul><li> Participate enthusiastically in day-to-day team operations and activities</li></ul>
<br>
</div></div>
<br>
</div></div>
</div>

<div>
<b>Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>A bachelor’s degree or equivalent in a related area such as journalism or communications, multimedia communications, integrated communications, and a minimum of four years’ experience working in a related field, preferably in a corporate communications environment with constituents in multiple locations.</p>
<br>
</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<div><div>
<ul><li> Superior written and oral communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Able to listen, ask appropriate questions, and use judgment to produce communication plans and deliverables</li></ul>
<ul><li> Familiarity with professional communications standards, including writing style guides and corporate communication best practices</li></ul>
<ul><li> Utmost attention to detail, excellent organizational skills
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to simultaneously balance multiple projects with competing priorities while maintaining a positive attitude</li></ul>
<ul><li> Creativity and innovation competencies</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proven team player with strong interpersonal skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Advanced proficiency in SharePoint and the entire Microsoft Office suite</li></ul>
