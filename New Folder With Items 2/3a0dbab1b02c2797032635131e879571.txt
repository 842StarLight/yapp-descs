<p><b>Position Description:</b></p>
<p>The Quality Lead will be responsible for defining the Quality Process for CGI's Advantage and ensuing that it is adhered.</p>
<p>#LI-JG1</p>
<p><b>Your future duties and responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>The following responsibilities are listed be category:</p>
<p>Quality:</p>
<ul><li>Develop, pilot and scale quality management strategy for several key products under Advantage Practice</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify Quality process gaps or inefficiencies in workflows and drive efforts to close these gaps via documentation, process, training and tools</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drive quality through both software and process improvements and partner with our development teams to drive engineering excellence</li></ul>
<ul><li>Own, build, deploy and evolve quality standards and quality metrics to drive continuous improvement in delivery and support</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish a Quality Engineering program, based on the principles of Agile, DevOps, “Shift left”, automation and TDD/BDD</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drive continuous improvement in quality supported by relevant metrics</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track and communicate outcomes over time for comparison, improvements and/or trends</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrate improvements and also identify inefficiencies utilizing quality metrics</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with Product Managers, SMEs, Design and Testing Team to share feedback coming out of reviews with clear expectations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Serve as the primary point of contact for all Product Quality Programs for cross-functional teams across global offices</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate with SE Quality Lead to ensure practices in Advantage align with overall SE Quality initiatives and practices</li></ul>
<p>Testing:</p>
<ul><li>Collaborate closely with product Managers, Design leads and testing leadership to drive a healthy test and release culture in order to deliver continuous product quality improvement</li></ul>
<ul><li>Design test strategies and create appropriate test harnesses, data, and tools that increase the speed of release cycles and allow for constant high quality results</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help testing leads to identify, develop, and formalize testing strategy and best practices across all key products</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead Quality Assurance testing efforts with large (60+ members) offshore/onshore team.</li></ul>
<p>Automation:</p>
<ul><li>Develop Automation strategy and build reusable automation test suites primarily</li></ul>
<ul><li>Familiarity with Page Object Model design pattern utilizing Robot framework.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Test data strategies</li></ul>
<ul><li>CI/CD strategies utilizing quality gates.</li></ul>
<p>Client interaction:</p>
<ul><li>Articulating the Advantage Quality Process</li></ul>
<ul><li>Pre-Sales Support</li></ul>
<p><b>Required qualifications to be successful in this role:</b></p>
<p>The candidate should be able to demonstrate working experience in the following areas:</p>
<ul><li>Bachelors in Computer Science, or equivalent</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum 10 years IT industry experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>7+ years of quality management experience and general product quality initiatives</li></ul>
<ul><li>Hands-on with experience in defining processes and solutions for releasing high quality software in a continuous delivery environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in various automation tools and frameworks, and well versed with software development and release cycles</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience working with Offshore teams and managing teams located remotely</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with Agile(Scrum/Kanban) or DevOps and rapid release cycles (Preferred)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with following tools (Robot, JIRA)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Deep knowledge of QA concepts and methodology</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with Cloud computing, SAAS (Preferred)</li></ul>
