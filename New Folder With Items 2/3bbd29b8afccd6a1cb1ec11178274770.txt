<h2>Executive Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Ackert Inc. is an innovative B2B software company that provides cutting-edge sales technology to law firms all over the globe.</p>
<p>We are looking for an experienced Executive Assistant to be an integral part of our team.</p>
<p>With a fun-loving startup culture and ample opportunity for growth and advancement, Ackert Inc. is the perfect place to let your entrepreneurial spirit thrive.</p>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Calendar management and travel arrangement for CEO &amp; Business Development Director</li>
<li>Coordinate and schedule meetings &amp; interviews</li>
<li>Manage CEO’s day-to-day and free up his time as you are able</li>
<li>General administrative and clerical tasks</li>
<li>Light project management duties</li>
<li>Edit podcasts</li>
<li>Provide support to all departments as needed</li>
<li>Plan and manage external and internal events (conferences, offsite meetings, team lunches, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Our ideal candidate</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have at least 5 years of experience in an EA role.</li>
<li>You are tech-savvy and can learn web-based applications quickly.</li>
<li>You're a self-starter that thrives in a dynamic and fast-paced startup culture.</li>
<li>You are kind-hearted and can take care of the office.</li>
<li>You proactively anticipate needs and can think at a strategic level.</li>
<li>You are flexible and comfortable with ambiguity.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation &amp; benefits: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>This is a full-time role, 40 hours per week, 9am-5pm, Monday through Friday.</li>
<li>Base pay will between $21 - $25 per hour, depending on experience.</li>
<li>We provide paid vacation, paid sick days, health/dental/vision insurance, and the opportunity to work remote once a week.</li>
<li>We often have company-sponsored game nights, happy hours, and other fun activities.</li>
<li>Our bright and stylish office is located in Encino on the vibrant Ventura Boulevard, near plenty of restaurants, bars and gyms.</li>
</ul>
