<h2>Senior Systems Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>AV&amp;C, an award winning experience design &amp; creative technology studio, seeks a top tier AV/Media Systems Design Engineer to lead the design, development, and implementation of unique media &amp; interaction installations in the built environment. This is a full time position, in our Tribeca, NYC studio.
</p>
<p><b>We:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Work very hard and have fun creating custom technology solutions for blue-chip brand and architectural clients, globally.</p></li>
<li><p>
Are a multidisciplinary group of designers, technologists &amp; creative coders who leverage cutting edge technical approaches to do our creative bidding, and accomplish unprecedented outcomes in both temporary and permanent installations.</p></li>
<li><p>
Contribute to amazing projects with top shelf collaborators for world class clients &amp; brands.</p></li>
<li><p>
Draw on expertise in fields ranging from AV &amp; IT systems, show control, custom electronics, embedded systems, sensors &amp; machine vision, industrial automation, etc to create highly custom, purpose built systems that enable our software layers to deliver a designed user experience.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
You:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Have mastery in combining proven and emerging AV and IT technologies into robust integrated systems that enable powerful experiences.</p></li>
<li><p>
Have excellent visual and written communication skills - from early hand sketches and memos to fully coordinated drawing sets and specification reports, the ability to clearly communicate the requirements of a project to clients, partners, and internal teams is of the utmost importance.</p></li>
<li><p>
Understand the architect/engineer workflow and construction process, and the ways technology systems design &amp; integration projects fold into the built environment.</p></li>
<li><p>
Have extensive experience reading, creating, and directing others in producing 2D/3D Architectural and Technical CAD drawings.</p></li>
<li><p>
Value collaboration in an interdisciplinary environment combining creative &amp; technical talents from many backgrounds.</p></li>
<li><p>
Are proficient in 2D drafting in Vectorworks or AutoCAD, with basic 3D Modeling skills in Vectorworks / AutoCAD / 3ds Max / Rhino / Sketchup a big plus.</p></li>
<li><p>
Are unfailingly creative in approaching design problems, while grounding all your work on a solid technical/reality foundation.</p></li>
<li><p>
Have the initiative and work ethic of someone who excels in a fast paced environment with highly motivated people doing world class work while having a blast.</p></li>
<li><p>
Have 4+ years of relevant professional experience</p></li>
<li><p>
Are permitted to work legally in the US</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The Job:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Take a lead role in the system design across all phases of the project</p></li>
<li><p>
Work with systems designers, systems engineers, and technicians while collaborating with creative, experience, and software design team members.</p></li>
<li><p>
Support the pre-sales concepting and budgeting process</p></li>
<li><p>
Consult with our clients and their teams on early concepts</p></li>
<li><p>
Design, Document, and Validate the implementation of project systems through integration, installation, commissioning and final hand-off.</p></li>
</ul>
<p>
This is a full time position with benefits, and salary commensurate with experience. Part time and project engagements may be considered.</p>
