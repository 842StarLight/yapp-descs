In addition, you will work with the VP of Finance in defining, documenting and communicating internal process and procedures. <b>About the Role:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Prepares client invoices and track and manage A/R aging and collections</li>
<li>Reviews customer agreements to ensure compliance with terms and appropriate revenue recognition accounting</li>
<li>Processes vendor payments and employee expenses</li>
<li>Process Semi-monthly Payroll, including tracking and payment of Sales Commissions and Bonuses</li>
<li>Sets up and monitor appropriate financial and cash controls</li>
<li>Leads the month-end close process, including posting all journal entries, maintaining detailed general ledger (GL) schedules, and reconciling key accounts, prepare monthly consolidation</li>

<li>Assists with tax filings and various audits</li>
<li>Evaluates and implements new and improved accounting processes and tools</li>
<li>Ad hoc financial projects as needed</li>
<li>Hiring and mentoring additional resources in Accounting as the company grows</li>
</ul>
