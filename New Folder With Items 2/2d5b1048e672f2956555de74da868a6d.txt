<p><b>Job Title: </b> Project Manager I</p>
<p><b>Reports To: </b> Director of Implementation</p>
<p><b>Location: </b> NW Austin</p>
<p><b>Position Type: </b> Full Time</p>
<p>One Medical Passport, Inc. isa dynamic medical software company looking for an entry to mid-level ProjectManager with the necessary skills to thrive in a creative and fast-paced environment.The Project Manager I role is to support the more senior Project Managers whilealso driving their own projects. They will be expected to bring clients “live”with various solutions while working closely with the Sales and CustomerSuccess teams in order to facilitate smooth transitions from one phase to thenext. This person is expected to workboth as part of a team and independently, withoccasional travel (up to 5%) to clients and Corporate Headquarters inWillington, CT. We are looking for someone that is familiarwith Software as a Service (SaaS), has Project Management experience, andexudes excellent communication skills both in person and a remote setting. Opporunityfor professional growth is available.</p>
<p><b>Project Manager I Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Assist other Project Managers in the software configurationprocess</p>
<p>· Conduct remote and onsite meetings with clients as part oftheir implementation</p>
<p>· Assist clients in tailoring the solutions they’ve purchasedin order to help them attain their goals</p>
<p>· Train nurses and other clinical staff to use One MedicalPassport solutions (both onsite and via webinar)</p>
<p>· Manage relationship and communication with client throughoutthe onboarding process</p>
<p>· Use Best Practices to guide clients through the configurationand go-live process</p>
<p>· Maintain a personal queue of projects and report theirstatuses to manager</p>
<p>· Identify and mitigate project risks and escalate them asnecessary</p>
<p>· Work with multiple departments to improve existing productsand create opportunity for growth</p>
<p>· Transition clients out of the “Implementation” phase to ongoingsupport</p>
<p><b>The ideal candidates should possess thefollowing: </b></p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree</p>
<p>· 1-2 years project management experience (SaaSexperience a big plus)</p>
<p>· <b>Excellent</b> verbal and writtencommunication skills, email and phone etiquette</p>
<p>· Thrives in a fast-paced environment and welcomesfrequent change and innovation</p>
<p>· Strong organizational and follow-up skills</p>
<p>· Solid workflow analysis skills</p>
<p>· Good independent work ethic and time managementskills</p>
<p>· An understanding of the web and online programs</p>
<p>· Above average desktop computer skills withexperience in MS Excel, Word, PowerPoint, and Adobe</p>
