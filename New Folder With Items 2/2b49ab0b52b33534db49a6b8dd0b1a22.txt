<br>
<p><b>
Job Description</b><br>
<br>
New Prosys Corp, a Pivot Company, is looking for an Accounting Manager to join our accounting team. This person will report to the Controller and be responsible for a set of financials for New Prosys. In addition, they will be responsible for preparation of required journal entries to complete monthly close process and timely reconciliations of all general ledger accounts. This full time position is located in Norcross, Georgia.</p>
<p><br>
<b>
Job Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the preparation of a set of financials for a division of the Company.</li>
<li>
Responsible for preparation of required journal entries to complete monthly close process (journal entries, accruals and account analysis, analytics for variances against budgets and prior periods).</li>
<li>
Responsible for timely reconciliations of all accounts on the related general ledger.</li>
<li>
Responsible for proper recognition and recording of all revenue transactions.</li>
<li>
Responsible for generation, review and presentation of all month-end financials to management.</li>
<li>
Responsible for working with senior accountants to provide all information to auditors for interim and year-end audits.</li>
<li>
Works directly with the auditors for financial and other audits (e.g. sales tax audits).</li>
<li>
Responsible for preparation of annual budget for the assigned division of the Company.</li>
<li>
Assists with supervision of senior accountants and delegation of responsibilities.</li>
<li>
Assists with other special projects as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Education / Experience</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Four-year College Degree in Accounting</li>
<li>
CPA preferred.</li>
<li>
Strong computer and software application skills including Microsoft Office products (Excel, Word, Power Point etc.) required and experience using Microsoft Dynamics - Great Plains and Management Reporter preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience in implementing/modifying policies and procedures with systems to achieve greater efficiencies in accounting operations a plus.</li>
<li>
Must be comfortable working in a high transaction volume accounting environment and possess the Excel skills required to complete the related reconciliations.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of accounting rules for cut-off, accrual and deferral and internal controls.</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail with highly organized skills.</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new processes quickly.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple processes in a fast-paced environment using multiple systems and databases.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel as required.</li>
</ul>
