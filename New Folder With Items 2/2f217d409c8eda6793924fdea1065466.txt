<p><b>
Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
Nowcom is within the umbrella of the Hankey Group of companies, and provides custom technology solutions to all companies within the group. Located in one of the wealthiest neighborhoods in Los Angeles, the Hankey Group is comprised of over 2,500 employees and total assets exceeding $8 Billion.</p>
<p>
In addition, Nowcom also creates its own product, DealerCenter that services the automotive industry by offering an innovative Dealer Management System to the market space. This all-in-one, cloud based solution delivers everything an automotive dealer needs to run their business including sales contracting and reporting, CRM capabilities, access to over 1,000 finance sources, and marketing tools to attract customers to their business. DealerCenter is a nationwide product with over 12,000 active clients and 20 years of experience and success.</p>
