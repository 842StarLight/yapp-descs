<h2>Contract Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Brainshark is looking for a Contract Administrator with experience in reviewing, negotiating, and editing various routine and complex agreements. This person will represent the company in negotiations with customers and vendors and will be able to provide support to multiple internal stakeholders on legal and business matters. You will be working directly with our sales team and other internal key company stakeholders.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Negotiate, draft, edit, and review, in conjunction with our legal team, all varieties of customer and vendor agreements including non-disclosure/confidentiality agreements (NDAs), SaaS software licensing agreements, contract amendments, data protection agreements (DPA)/GDPR addendums, master services agreements, order forms, and statements of work</li>
<li>
Manage multiple projects concurrently in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Take the lead in customer contract negotiations, review and analyze changes to contracts proposed by customers, provide commentary/summaries of edits for internal stakeholders, make edits to documents in redline and provide commentary regarding accepted and rejected terms</li>
<li>
Project manage agreement negotiations, update/revise agreements with applicable changes, track different drafts of agreements and changes over time, and ensure all agreements are error free</li>
<li>
Own the contract process by keeping track of customer contracts, follow up with customer legal/contracts departments to ensure that changes are reviewed, and contracts signed, by the appropriate parties
</li>
<li>Knowing who to work with on various contractual issues (i.e. Legal, Customer Service, IT/Data Security)</li>
<li>
Resolve issues with internal subject matter experts and sales/customers so that contracts are executed by all parties in a timely manner.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree (law school graduate a plus but not necessary)</li>
<li>
1-3 years related experience working with contracts or law school graduate degree equivalent</li>
<li>
Strong skills in reading and interpreting contracts
</li>
<li>Strong skills in using Microsoft Word (redlining documents using track changes, document comparison) and Adobe PDF</li>
<li>
Strong multitasking and communication skills with ability to articulate a point of view clearly and concisely</li>
<li>
Excellent negotiation skills resulting in win-win outcomes</li>
<li>
Competence in drafting and reviewing legal/commercial contract terms relevant to SaaS contracts â€“ such as software license, data privacy/security and order forms and statements of work preferred but not required</li>
<li>
Prior experience in using Microsoft Excel preferred but not required.
</li>
<li>Prior experience using Salesforce to manage workflow and documents preferred but not required<br>
</li>
</ul>
