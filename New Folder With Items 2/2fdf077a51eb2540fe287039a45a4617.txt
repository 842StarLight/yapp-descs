 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Looking to make a difference in the world
<br><br>
Are you interested in making dreams come true by helping create and protect healthy families Do you thrive in a fast-paced environment emphasizing personal initiative and collaboration Then we’re looking for you!
<br><br>
We have an immediate opening for an Email Marketing Specialist to join our team in West Los Angeles.
<br><br>
<b>Summary:</b> As a member of the Marketing team, this position will oversee email marketing program / campaign execution, including oversight of internal and external (agency, partner) resources. The Email Marketing Specialist will be responsible for email tactics, design, and development across the Stem Cell and Reproductive (Donor Egg and Donor Sperm) business units.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Researches, builds, executes and optimizes email marketing strategies and campaigns for a variety of programs, customers and potential customers to drive customer acquisition and retention goals.</li>
<li>Executes, documents and manages the strategic planning of email marketing, including themes and seasonal calendar for all email marketing programs, monitoring and reporting, and management and optimization of design, content, targeting and messaging of all email communications.</li>
<li>Owns end-to-end management of all email campaigns, including content, production, testing, setup, and deployment.</li>
<li>Builds, tests, analyzes, and optimizes email tracks, workflows, and lists based on segmentation and lead scoring strategy.</li>
<li>Creates, edits, and Q/A all aspects of emails (copy, brand consistency, links, tracking, UX, etc.).</li>
<li>Maximizes deliverability and works with ISPs to minimize spam folders.</li>
<li>Works with e-mail software provider for optimization.</li>
<li>Tracks and reports results from all email programs and campaigns and propose new campaigns.</li>
<li>Runs A/B tests, conducts analysis of results, and iterates based on findings.</li>
<li>Collaborates with designers and marketing team to develop, test and implement marketing emails.</li>
<li>Other job duties or projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>Education and/or Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing, Graphic Design, or related field</li>
<li>Minimum 3 to 5 years of experience developing in email / CRM marketing</li>
<li>Experience with Marketing Automation and email marketing platforms</li>
</ul>
<b>Computer Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Extensive skills working with Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Intermediate to advanced Adobe Photoshop skills</li>
<li>Experience with InDesign, Canva, or other design software a plus</li>
<li>HTML and CMS experience (WordPress) preferred</li>
<li>Other Skills and Abilities:</li>
<li>Experience and demonstrated track record of successfully managing diverse and scaled email campaigns and segmentation approaches</li>
<li>Proven ability to work with internal and external partners to effectively manage the end-to-end campaign development and optimization process, including creative development and analytics</li>
<li>Experience using Google Analytics, Salesforce, Hubspot and Marketo</li>
</ul>
<b>Other Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>There will be very limited need to travel – approximately 10-15% of time.</li>
</ul>
