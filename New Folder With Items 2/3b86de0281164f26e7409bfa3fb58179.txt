<h2>CAM Estate/Probate Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview:
</b></h1>
<p>Receive and verify notification of debtor death. Contact attorneys or executors to find out intentions for the debt.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary Responsibilities:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Depending on debtor intentions, continue to manage account until payment is received on both secured and unsecured accounts.
</li>
<li>Prepare analysis of collateral to determine potential loss mitigation options and/or default resolution.
</li>
<li>Negotiate loss mitigation options including settlements, short-sales, refinancing, etc.
</li>
<li>Manage the Probate Finder relationship, and make recommendations for process improvements.
</li>
<li>Perform skip-tracing.
</li>
<li>Order title searches and contact Credit Bureau or analyze credit reports to validate lien position.
</li>
<li>Determine if there is life insurance on the account, then gather appropriate information as necessary in order to file a claim; follow-up with insurance company for resolution.
</li>
<li>Monitor daily file from Probate Finder, research notifications and estate matches, manage the Proof of Claim and satisfaction of claim process.
</li>
<li>Receive and distribute incoming mail.
</li>
<li>Accept incoming calls from a variety of stakeholders, and determine appropriate action.
</li>
<li>Adhere to applicable compliance/operational risk controls in accordance with Company or regulatory standards and policies.
</li>
<li>Maintain M&amp;T internal control standards, including timely implementation of internal and external audit points together with any issues raised by external regulators as applicable.
</li>
<li>Promote an environment that supports diversity and reflects the M&amp;T Bank brand.
</li>
<li>Complete other related duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Scope of Responsibilities:
</b></h2>
<p>This position involves customer contact and collections activity.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Education and Experience Required:
</b></h2>
<p>A combined 3 years’ higher education and/or work experience, including a minimum of 1 year experience in collections or customer service.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Experience with word processing, spreadsheet and presentation software.
</p>
<p>Detail oriented.
</p>
<p>Strong verbal and written communication skills.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Education and Experience Preferred:
</b></h2>
<p>Bachelor’s degree.
</p>
<p>Knowledge of M&amp;T Bank systems.
</p>
<p></p>
