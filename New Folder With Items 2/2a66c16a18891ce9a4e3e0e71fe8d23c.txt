<h2>Senior Product Manager - Data Science</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Your </b><b>Role</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Tenable is looking for a Senior Product Manager that will play a significant role in the company vision of helping organizations understand and reduce their Cyber Exposure. The Senior Product Manager will be responsible for driving the product strategy and management of our data science engineering platform in support of our existing and nascent product offerings.<br>
<br>
This individual needs to be highly organized and adept at leading and influencing across cross-functional teams. S/he will provide indirect and informal leadership to other product managers as part of a larger, distributed team. This person should have a demonstrated track record of driving change informed through data, analysis, collaboration, and a strong understanding of the customer and their use cases. The ideal candidate should have excellent communication skills with the ability to drive outcomes across product, design, engineering, business, and enablement teams.</p>
<b>
Your Opportunity:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Partner both horizontally and vertically with other Product Managers to ensure alignment on strategy, deliverables, execution, and messaging.</li>
<li>
Actively partner with R&amp;D teams to define requirements and product strategy, resource availability, milestones, and dependencies between teams and products.</li>
<li>
Drive the creation, maintenance, and dissemination of the product roadmap.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Development, Field PM, and Product Marketing to ensure alignment between the teams on plans, execution, and messaging.</li>
<li>
Participate in meetings with Support, Sales, Customer Care, Channel, and other departments to keep them informed about product changes.</li>
<li>
Be an active participant in the execution of go-to-market and launch activities.</li>
</ul>
<b>
What you'll need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BS/MS in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Data Science, Statistics, Symbolic Systems or related (or equivalent experience)</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience building cloud-native software products as a results-driven Product Owner, with demonstrated abilities to clearly define &amp; communicate requirements</li>
<li>
5+ years of enterprise product management experience</li>
<li>
Broad knowledge of enterprise IT cloud SaaS markets and products</li>
<li>
Hands-on experience with statistics, data science, machine learning, deep learning, and/or distributed systems for advanced analytics</li>
<li>
Proven track record of strategy creation, development, alignment, execution, and delivery</li>
<li>
Strong oral, written, and presentation communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong project, time management, and team collaboration skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
And Ideally:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Cybersecurity domain expertise</li>
<li>
3-5 years of software development experience</li>
<li>
Experience working with remote/distributed teams</li>
<li>
Light travel (~10%) to customers, company offices, security conferences, etc.</li>
</ul>
