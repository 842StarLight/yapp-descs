<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You’ll Do</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Prospect new business partners (we provide leads!) across the U.S. and Canada through high volume outbound calls</li>
<li>
Assess business needs of small to medium sized businesses</li>
<li>
Consult with professionals on how to effectively market their business on Houzz.com</li>

Management of the full sales cycle, from securing new business partnerships to facilitating a smooth onboarding process for the professional</li>
<li>
Meet and exceed monthly sales expectations</li>
<li>
Day &amp; Overnight travel as needed (less than 5%)</li>
<li>
Ad Hoc responsibilities as required</li>
