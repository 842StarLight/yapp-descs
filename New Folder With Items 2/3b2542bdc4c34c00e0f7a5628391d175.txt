<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>JMG Realty, Inc. is seeking a Full-time Leasing / Marketing Consultant for an apartment community located in Raleigh, NC. The Marketing Consultant must be able to interact with all residents and prospective applicants in a friendly, inviting, and professional manner. The Marketing Consultant will conduct tours with prospects, answer calls, enter maintenance work orders, reply to email leads, prepare leases, and help with the day-to-day operation of the property.</p>
<p>Job Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>On-site experience as a Leasing Consultant is required</li>
<li>Some experience with OneSite software is preferred but not required</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and people skills</li>
<li>Ability to multitask</li>
<li>Must conduct weekly off-site marketing visits to surrounding businesses</li>
<li>Must be a strong team player with good verbal and writing skills and the ability to solve problems, manage time, and set priorities</li>
<li>Must be able to address the concerns of current and prospective residents in a friendly and professional manager</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented, professional, flexible, well organized, and possess strong follow-up skills</li>
<li>Working knowledge of computers, the internet, and Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Ability to work weekends</li>
</ul>
<p>Pre-employment includes criminal and drug screening. Excellent salary and benefits.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $13.00 to $14.00 /hour</p>
<p>Additional Compensation:</p>
<ul><li>Commission</li></ul>
