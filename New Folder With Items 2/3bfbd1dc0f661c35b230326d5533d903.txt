<h2>Home Care Marketer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position Title Home Care Marketer</b></p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>Responsible for developing and maintaining relationships with a wide variety of organizations and facilities within an assigned territory to promote Integrity Home Care + Hospice. The Home Care Marketer (also know as the Home Care Navigation) will be responsible for generating Clients and Caregivers for Private Services while increasing Private Care business in expansion areas to maintain continual growth.</p>
<div>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>The following statements describe the principal activities of this position identified and shall not be construed as a detailed exposition of all the duties that may be inherent in this position.</p>
<ul><li>Builds and maintains referral source relationships;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Builds and maintains close working relationships with Private Pay team with consistent and frequent communication in various forms to meet the needs of all teams;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Consistently visits identified established referral sources and identify new opportunities;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Meets and exceeds goals, developing a referral pipeline for clients and field staff with target referral sources necessary for revenue and growth goal attainment;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepares tactical action plans and maintains target lists.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prioritizes referral sources in accordance with the market sales plan;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Documents account-related information regularly providing input on referral opportunities;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identifies industry competitors and have working knowledge of their strengths and weaknesses;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand and provide basic payor source information and qualifications to referrals and referral sources;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists by providing information for development of marketing plans and collateral for Private Care growth in current and expanded regions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Active and consistent involvement in community functions and organizations that give opportunity to network with and educate potential referral sources and community partners on the benefits of home care, home health and hospice;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participates in weekly meetings with Private Care team;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Recognizes media opportunities, including social media and work with marketing and/or recruiting to articulate and assist in development of and/or participation in said opportunities;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attends training and self-direct training to obtain and continue knowledge and skills;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Works to process and practice shared lessons learned and best practices; Enforces Integrity’s mission and values through a consistent display of communication, professionalism and leadership in the community;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Travels throughout service territories;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Represents the Private Care team of Integrity Home Care + Hospice in a courteous and professional manner always;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Able and willing to write business proposals, territory assessments and follow up communication with referral sources and clients as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complies with Compliance Plan;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complies with HIPAA Policies. Maintains confidentiality of information relating to the patient and family. Will discuss only those aspects necessary to the care and treatment of patient and family with those directly involved in the patient’s care;</li></ul>
<ul><li>As business needs may change, may perform additional responsibilities assigned by supervisor that are reasonably related to the position.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and Experience Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>Education:</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in marketing, communications, social services or a related field required.</li></ul>
<p><b>Experience:</b></p>
<ul><li>Minimum two (2) years of experience in marketing, business development or sales; with emphasis on experience in similar industry or market segment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sales experience in health care, home care, home health, hospice or similar field is strongly preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strategic account or territory management skills and experience is preferred along with exposure to and experience with a selling methodology.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li>Effective written and oral communication and data entry skills;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of basic keyboarding, computer software including Microsoft Office;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be self-motivated, detailed and can multitask;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must work well in a fast-paced environment;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to establish rapport with others;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to plan and organize time effectively, work independently and show good judgment;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to deal effectively and courteously with clients, family and co-workers;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to interact and exhibit an empathetic attitude toward the sick and elderly.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Demands</b></p>
<ul><li>Frequent manual dexterity and visual acuity sufficient to effectively use and interact with computer keyboard and terminal</li></ul>
<ul><li>Frequent periods of working with computer terminals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Occasional carrying up to 25 pounds</li></ul>
<ul><li>Extended travel via auto within territory or geography Integrity services</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<ul><li>Fast paced and occasionally stressful work environment with tight deadlines;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prudent and efficient use of routine office equipment;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Exposure to mechanical and electrical office equipment;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complies with the agency dress code; projects a professional attitude toward self, clients, consumers, attendants and other staff members at all times;</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complies with department public relation policies regarding outside speaking and professional affiliations, clearing all such requests through the agency.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Conditions of Employment</b></p>
<ul><li>Successful candidate must have a satisfactory post-offer Medical Review</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must pass a pre-employment criminal background check.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have proof of Auto Insurance (minimum required by law), valid driver’s license, and reliable transportation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>May be required to complete a biennial health assessment or physical exam and submit to annual TB screenings and/or Hepatitis vaccinations, as well as possess a current CPR certification through an approved credential source.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Candidate must meet the requirements of the full job description as stated throughout the duration of employment with Integrity.</li></ul>
