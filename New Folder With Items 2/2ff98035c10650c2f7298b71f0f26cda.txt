<h2>Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Unlock Your Career Potential: Support at ADP.</b> Every day, thousands of organizations around the world rely on ADP. As a worldwide leader in business outsourcing solutions with 60+ years of experience and over half a million customers, our products and services enable companies large and small to do business. As part of ADP's support team, you play a shared role in our success - you'll work alongside a team of talented and highly motivated individuals at one of the most innovative companies in the world. Here, you'll find the tools and flexibility you need to create your own path, supported by comprehensive training and support to improve your skills and knowledge.</p>
<p><b>
ADP is hiring Senior Administrative Support professional.</b> In this position, you'll couple your positive attitude, superb organizational skills and deep understanding of ADP to ensure smooth functioning of activities and processes in support of one or more executives.</p>
<p>
We strive for every interaction to be driven by our <b>CORE</b> values: <b>Insightful Expertise, Integrity is Everything, Service Excellence, Inspiring Innovation, Each Person Counts, Results-Driven, - Social Responsibility.</b></p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform clerical, administrative and general office duties of a responsible and confidential nature</li>
<li>Route and answer correspondence</li>
<li>Independently establish, organize and maintain documents and initiate follow up as needed</li>
<li>Compile, analyze and summarize statistics and information in presentation formats</li>
<li>Communicate new policies and procedure to the department and coordinate the gathering of information as directed,</li>
<li>Create formulas/formats of moderate complexity and determine hardware/software requirements</li>
<li>May coordinate off-site meetings and conventions; assist with schedule management; arrange appointments, travel, meetings, conference calls and process expense reports</li>
<li>May coordinate and track training activities of internal associates</li>
<li>Maintain database as required</li>
<li>May assist in workforce scheduling of internal associates</li>
<li>Provide support to other members of management and department as requested</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS REQUIRED:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of administrative support experience in a similar environment</li>
<li>High School diploma with 3+ years of experience or equivalent education will be considered</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have Advanced level MS Excel experience with Pivot Tables, conditional formatting, Vlookup, Sumif, and Dateif, formulas</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years experience, supporting sales Executive leadership</li>
<li>Demonstrated organizational skills for time critical deadlines.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Self-motivation and ability to work with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Flexibility and prioritization is required regularly, and the ability to manage expectations of all levels of management is a critical component of this position.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION:
</b></p>
<div>Bachelors Degree or equivalent in experience and education.</div>
