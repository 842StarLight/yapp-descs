<h2>Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>DOMINO, the award-winning home design media company, part of the Multiply Media portfolio, is seeking an ambitious, creative, and collaborative Sales Manager to join our growing revenue team! You’ll be building our client relationships to produce revenue and contribute to our overall revenue goals. You’ll build new business, own renewals, and partner with the internal brand partnerships, creative and editorial teams to ensure successful campaigns.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Who’s a good fit?</b><br>
</p>
<p>The ideal candidate loves style and your social skills are next level. You’re well connected and you have digital sales experience along with a passion for tech, finance and auto. You’re a brand ambassador who knows how to win the confidence of clients and agencies that Domino is the best partner to amplify awareness and performance of its product or service. You’re not afraid to pursue clients directly, but know how to engage and build long-lasting relationships with agencies. You’re motivated to unlock budgets across channels to help tell the branded story. You have incredible instincts, think on your feet, and problem solve.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect, identify opportunities, negotiate and secure sponsorship programs to brands across select lifestyle categories</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a strong relationship with clients and agencies (media, social, PR, experiential)
</li>
<li>Effectively communicate with clients to understand KPIs prior to campaign launches and control the conversation when it comes to performance communication, when account manager needs assistance
</li>
<li>Monitor the digital media industry and socialize new digital advertising solutions for clients with the team
</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with marketing, creative and editorial teams for presale materials and seamlessly handoff relationships to account managers post sale</li>
<li>Based on client’s initiatives, know how to propose an omnichannel program
</li>
<li>Meet and exceed quarterly goals and objectives and maximize all revenue growth opportunities for Domino
</li>
<li>Advocate for a results-oriented and deadline-driven environment that thrives in challenges<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree, preferably in Sales, Advertising, Marketing, or Business</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years experience in direct sales experience, selling custom content packages, experiential, print, and/or integrated programs
</li>
<li>You have excellent verbal, written, and presentation skills and are eager to further refine and develop these skills</li>
<li>You are a highly organized and focused multi-tasker with strong attention to detail, analytical and project management skills, and can work independently within a team focused organization</li>
<li>You thrive in a fast-paced, deadline and detailed oriented environment</li>
<li>You believe in the power of teamwork, and are a team player</li>
<li>Fluent in Google spreadsheets and presentation, Excel and PPT, and have experience working within ad servers and other digital media tech systems<br>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
