<div><b>
About the Role</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As a Solutions Consultant at Clinc, you will play an instrumental role in our growth by developing new business within the conversational AI enterprise market. You will be expected to establish trusted advisor status with our key prospects and customers, and demonstrate innovation through the Clinc conversational AI platform. Your responsibilities include prospecting within the top Financial, Automotive, QSR, and Healthcare markets in the world, positioning Clinc as the conversational AI platform of choice for digital consumer experiences, and securing new business that helps our customers drive digital innovation. You will focus on a named account territory based on a mixture of geographic and vertical focused accounts. You'll have access to a dynamic team of Business Development Managers and Solutions Consultants for sales support plus an extended team that consists of the best industry talent in engineering, project management, and customer experience.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
You’ll help our customers drive digital innovation by</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Providing platform presentations and demonstrations (both remote and onsite).</li>
<li>
Effectively differentiating the technical aspects of our solution versus competitors.</li>
<li>
Running technical deep dive and architecture sessions with customers and partners.</li>
<li>
Facilitating workshops to understand customer’s business and find the best use cases to apply Conversational AI.</li>
<li>
Scoping the work effort and requirements for Proof of Concepts.</li>
<li>
Translating customer requirements to our Customer Experience and Engineering teams.</li>
<li>
Traveling to customer sites on a regular basis (50-75% is not unusual).</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
