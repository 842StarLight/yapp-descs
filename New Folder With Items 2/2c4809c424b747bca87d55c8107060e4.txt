<h2>Staff Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Objective</b>:</p>
<p>The Staff Coordinator is expected to perform a variety of duties in the coordination of scheduling service for clients. The Service Coordinator is responsible for scheduling clients and CAREGivers in order to provide the highest quality service to clients with emphasis on creating extraordinary relationships.</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities</b><b>: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reflect the values of Vasconcellos, Inc.,b.a. an independently owned and operated Home Instead Senior Care franchise.</li>
<li>Answer incoming calls in a friendly, professional and knowledgeable manner.</li>
<li>Create and maintain client and CAREGiver schedules with an emphasis on creating high quality matches and the development of extraordinary relationships.</li>
<li>Monitor, mediate, and log all client and CAREGiver activity utilizing the software system.</li>
<li>Follow up with all client and CAREGiver issues to ensure their problems are resolved.</li>
<li>Enter and maintain accurate client and CAREGiver records in the software system.</li>
<li>Increase client loyalty to Home Instead Senior Care by utilizing the consultative sales process to better meet our current client needs.</li>
<li>Follow up and communicate CAREGiver and client issues to ensure problems are resolved.</li>
<li>Design, test and implement a responsive contingency plan that ensures 100% maintenance of Service Hours.</li>
<li>Recognize and capture opportunities to increase service hours in scenarios to enhance and/or increase quality care.</li>
<li>Field new client inquiries over the phone in a knowledgeable manner, enter the information into the software system and work with team to communicate and prepare for the Care Consultation.</li>
<li>Conduct Service Inquiries following the consultative sales process.</li>
<li>Maintain regular attendance at the office to execute job responsibilities</li>
<li>Demonstrate open and effective communication with the franchise owner, colleagues, CAREGivers, clients and family members.</li>
<li>Adhere to all company policies, procedures and business ethics codes and ensures that they are communicated and implemented within the team.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Secondary Responsibilities</b><b>: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Participate as needed in all CAREGiver meetings</li>
<li>Perform any and all other functions and responsibilities deemed necessary</li>
</ul>
<p>Must be available to act as a backup resource to the On Call Supervisor</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Additional Compensation:</p>
<ul><li>Bonuses</li></ul>

<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul>

<li>Vision insurance</li>
<li>Retirement plan</li>
<li>Paid time off</li>
</ul>
<p>This Job Is Ideal for Someone Who Is:</p>
<ul>
<li>Detail-oriented -- would rather focus on the details of work than the bigger picture</li>
<li>People-oriented -- enjoys interacting with people and working on group projects</li>
<li>Dependable -- more reliable than spontaneous</li>
<li>Achievement-oriented -- enjoys taking on challenges, even if they might fail</li>
<li>High stress tolerance -- thrives in a high-pressure environment</li>
</ul>
<p>Schedule:</p>
