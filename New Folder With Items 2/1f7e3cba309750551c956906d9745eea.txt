<h2>Manager, Ad Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>You will be part of our world-class team growing Zynga’s in-game advertising business to new heights! We are experienced in mobile and online advertising within gaming and incredibly passionate about winning and innovating in this fast-growing area. Nobody is afraid to get their hands dirty and help out! We believe in building great internal and external relationships, working hard, inspiring others, being the industry thought-leaders and not forgetting to have fun!</p>
<p>
You will responsible for driving advertising revenues across platforms through supporting revenue optimization efforts and working with our network partners. You will be a key part of the team, driving business success through managing demand and identifying opportunities for growth and to drive efficiencies.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Partner with Zynga’s top tier game studios as lead POC for driving ad revenue and monitoring ad health metrics</li>
<li>
Manage relationships with 3rd party ad networks to ensure revenue is optimized and integrations are healthy</li>
<li>
Identify revenue growth opportunities by solidifying deals with our 3rd party ad networks</li>
<li>
Manage our Direct Sales AdOps trafficker that will report to you and lead conversations with Direct Sales team</li>
<li>
Assist in trafficking ads across a variety of 3rd party ad networks and SSPs</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot ad revenue issues by corresponding with internal and external tech teams and account managers</li>
</ul>
<b>
Skills and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years’ work experience in mobile ad revenue optimization or related field</li>
<li>
Results oriented and proactive personality with ability to lead ad revenue initiatives</li>
<li>
Strong familiarity with ad networks/DSPs/SSPs and Direct Sales campaign management</li>
<li>
Manager experience of at least 1 direct report</li>
<li>
BA or BS or equivalent experience is preferred</li>
<li>
Must be organized and detail oriented with great attention to accuracy</li>
<li>
Excellent written communication and verbal agility are necessary for cross-team collaboration</li>
</ul>

</ul>
