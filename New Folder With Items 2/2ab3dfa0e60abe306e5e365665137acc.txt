<h2>Digital Sales Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB DESCRIPTION
</h1>
<div>
<p><b>Purpose</b></p>
<p>To work strategically with the sales staff to generate creative solutions that deliver customer results through our digital assets, as well as, monitor campaign performance. The Digital Sales Account Manager will need to have expert knowledge of digital marketing including social media, OTT, email marketing, SEO and SEM.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Digital order entry with high level of accuracy.</li>
<li>Request, update and monitor digital creative scheduling.</li>
<li>Execute creative development and fulfillment for digital advertising campaigns.</li>
<li>Manage the startup process for digital campaigns including participation on all onboarding calls.</li>
<li>Monitor digital campaigns pacing and effectiveness throughout the month to ensure campaign delivery.</li>
<li>Retain and grow our digital revenue by proactively finding opportunities to optimize campaign performance.</li>
<li>Report monthly metrics and campaign results to the account managers and assist account executives/account managers in interpreting the results to the client.</li>
<li>Manage and communicate inventory sell through percentages and avails to staff for all digital solutions.</li>
<li>Educate staff and clients on effective marketing strategies and revenue growth tactics that utilize our digital product suite.</li>
<li>Work as a liaison with all 3rdparty vendors</li>
<li>Reconcile / audit 3rdparty client billing each month</li>
<li>Attend client campaign recap meetings as the digital fulfillment expert.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by Sales Managers.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Performance Tracking</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Successful campaign implementation, fulfillment and maintenance.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Google Analytics, TMS Unified Dashboard and other Campaign Dashboards.</li>
<li>Strong inter-office, inter-department relations.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Leading Indicators</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Increased number of campaign reviews.</li>
<li>Increased campaign optimizations due to campaign monitoring.</li>
<li>Increased client renewal.</li>
<li>Increased client satisfaction.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<br><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB REQUIREMENTS
</h1>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 years of account engagement in a professional, fast paced sales environment.</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively as part of a team and independently.</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tasks and projects effectively under deadline pressure.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively solve problems.</li>
<li>Creative and able to adapt quickly to change.</li>
<li>Position requires strong written and verbal communication skills, as well as strong organization and time management.</li>
<li>Familiar with SEO, SEM, Social Media, targeted display, mobile and video marketing strategies</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of Microsoft Office.</li>
<li>Proven track record of exceeding expectations</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>About TEGNA</b></p>
<br>

TMS is a one-stop shop that helps businesses thrive through an unmatched suite of services and solutions that reach consumers across television, email, social and over-the-top (OTT) platforms, including Premion, TEGNA’s OTT advertising service. Across platforms, TEGNA tells empowering stories, conducts impactful investigations and delivers innovative marketing solutions. For more information, visit www.TEGNA.com.</p>
