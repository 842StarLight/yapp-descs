<b>Scope</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<div>Responsible for providing structural and seismic engineering calculations and pipe system support designs.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>1. Review customer system requirements and offer Victaulic product solutions for piping system design needs.</div>
<div>2. Provide structural, seismic and other calculations.</div>
<div>3. Design pipe support products such as pipe anchors, skids, guides, bracing, small structures such as skids, etc.</div>
<div>4. Identify and assist in the development of new products.</div>
<div>5. Visit field installations to assist customers in using Victaulic products and optimizing performance.</div>
<div>6. Provide general support to sales organization including creating, organizing and conducting technical seminars.</div>
