<h2>Associate Project Engineer - Norcross, GA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Join the company that helps make American manufacturing possible. You’ll work alongside essential industries such as automotive, plastics, food, beverage and aerospace, while becoming an employee owner through our Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP). As a McNaughton-McKay employee, not only do you help the company succeed, you also share in the rewards.
<br><br>
<b>General Summary:</b><br>
This person is accountable for the engineering of, and coordination for, any assigned Engineered Solution Projects and Products with a focus on quality, cost, innovation, and on time delivery.
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
1.Monitors the manufacturing process at build vendor for compliance to schedule and detailed functional specification for assigned projects.
<br>2.Participates in the delivery of solution projects to cost/schedule/quality with high levels of customer satisfaction.
<br>3.Engineers and designs solutions using accepted standards and methodologies at McNaughton-McKay
<br>4.Assists account managers and project engineers with the identification, design, proposal generation, costing, and delivery of solutions.
<br>5.Oversees assembly of custom computer orders including any related software.
<br>6.Provides pre and post-sales technical support for the solution(s).
<br>7.Follows through on projects to insure established objectives were achieved. Establishes project management process and reviews current and future projects with solutions team to identify areas for continuous improvement relating to the project management process.
<br><br>
<b>Knowledge/Skills/Abilities:</b><br>
1.Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Engineering Technology or other related technical field required. In lieu of meeting the degree requirement, 4+ years professional experience in the key responsibilities detailed above or a combination of experience and education required.
<br>2.Ability to work with minimal supervision; organizes, prioritizes and works independently to obtain objectives.
<br>3.Demonstrates the ability to build positive relationships with team members, customers and vendors.
<br>4.Good verbal and written communication skills.
<br>5.Knowledge of Microsoft operating systems and Microsoft office. Experience with the business system a plus.
<br>6.Experience or willingness to learn AutoCAD, ePLAN, SolidWorks, and other design software.
<br>7.Experience in controls design or other skills demonstrating mechanical and/or electrical aptitude.
<br><br>
<b>Reporting Relationships:</b><br>
<b>Reports To:</b> Solutions Business Manager – Madison Heights
<br><b>Direct Reports:</b> None
