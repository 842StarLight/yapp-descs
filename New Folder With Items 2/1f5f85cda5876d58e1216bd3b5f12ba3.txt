<h2>Sr. Director, Services Architecture</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Ellucian designs technology that powers the educational mission of colleges and universities around the world. Inspired by that goal, Ellucian employees thrive as part of a high-performing global team committed to delivering a modern, connected, industry-leading experience for today’s students and higher education institutions. More than 2,500 institutions in 50 countries around the world look to Ellucian for the ideas and insights that will move education forward, helping people everywhere discover their futures through learning.</p>

<p><b>
Sr. Director, Digital Transformation &amp; Innovation (Services)</b></p>
<p><b>
Description of Job Duties
</b></p>
<p>Bring your strategic vision and leadership skills to Ellucian and make a difference! At Ellucian, we deliver a broad portfolio of technology solutions that help higher education institutions navigate change, achieve greater insights, and drive efficiencies. Right now, we are seeking a Senior Director for our Services Engineering organization to provide leadership and oversight associated with the services methodology, scoping, and pursuit of new business within the context of our SaaS, cloud, and enterprise solutions that ensure success for our Higher Education clients. This leader will be responsible for directing and managing the Services Engineering practice within the Global Digital Transformation team. This position is highly visible throughout Ellucian as it directly impacts revenue growth and overall performance of the company while setting the direction for Ellucian’s Professional Services Delivery.</p>
<p>
Specifically, the Senior Director of Services Engineering is responsible for the team that defines deployment and implementation strategies to ensure greatest value for our Higher Education customers and manages the pursuit of the most complex business opportunities for Professional Services. As such, they are ultimately accountable for the overall methodology that permeates every aspect of our services and proposing services offerings that drive success for our customers. The Services Engineering group sets the trajectory for a successful delivery of Professional Services engagements globally – setting the delivery teams up to ensure that financial targets are met (revenue, expense, margin), quality project delivery, positive customer experience, and effective employee engagement.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive the vision and execution of the Services Engineering team globally</li>
<li>
Own the methodology for high value cloud/SaaS implementation and deployment services</li>
<li>
Responsible for proposal profitability, burn down rate projections, and competitive positioning of services packaging and pricing</li>
<li>
Contributes to successful pursuits of high value services business globally, ranging from leading the pursuit in North America to providing support in global regional pursuits</li>
<li>
Ensure effective construction of service engagements aimed at achievement of customers’ desired outcomes</li>
<li>
Utilizes people/process/technology to optimize internal processes and drive efficiency within Ellucian</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
