<div><b>
Job Summary:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Reputation.com Sales Development Representative (SDR) / Inside Sales role holds an important position at the top of the sales funnel for the entire company. The SDR is often our customer’s first experience with Reputation.com so you will need to be able to make a great first impression to earn the right to qualify our leads and identify what the appropriate next steps should be.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Our successful SDRs are the Talent Pipeline that feeds our sales organization. They are the front line of the Reputation.com’s Sales Team, tasked with representing Reputation.com to effectively prospect, educate, qualify, develop target accounts and qualify leads.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Our SDR's are high-energy, self-motivated, agile and able to change hats on the fly. In this highly prized role, excellent phone and email skills and the ability to quickly assess customer opportunities will be critical to your success. You will be responsible for accurately tracking customer interactions and information in our CRM system. We're looking for professionals with strong organizational and follow up skills who work well in a team environment.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Articulate the Reputation.com business value proposition to decision makers to assess buying interest.</li>
<li>
Research accounts, identify key players, and generate interest.</li>
<li>
Work closely with the nationwide enterprise outside sales team to accelerate sales cycle and to extend reach into target accounts.</li>
<li>
Update prospect interaction in salesforce.com to ensure efficient lead management.</li>
<li>
Proactively prospect leads and qualified opportunity quotas to ensure revenue objectives.</li>
</ul></div>
