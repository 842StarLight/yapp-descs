<h2>Business Development Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Who We Are</b></p>
<p><i>Project Insight (PI®) is a leading work management software company based in Costa Mesa, CA. </i>Our mission at PI® is to make our clients’ <b><i>jobs easier</i></b> by leveraging the power of automation and data aggregation to improve business processes and efficiencies.</p>
<p><b>Who’s Right For This Job</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a team member who has a strong grasp on business development, account management, consulting and tech.</p>
<p>You make stuff happen! By the time your client or boss says that something needs to be done, you’ve already thought about it and have a plan. You’re a creative thinker who is self-motivated to solve problems, work independently and make your clients happy!</p>
<p>When a prospect or customer calls in with a question or technical challenge, your attitude and approach is positive. You are a leader who takes initiative to follow through and drive success every time!</p>
<p>We want you to learn our consultative onboarding/sales approach and make it your own. To be successful, you need strong communication superpowers to supplement your technology and business analysis skills. You get how companies and their inner departments work together to get stuff done. And you understand how different kinds of companies operate and can speak their lingo.</p>
<p>At PI, you’ll interface with our many types of customers including those who perform client facing professional services, software development, web development, marketing, IT, product development, and general project management.</p>
<p><b>The Skills You Bring</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Serves as trusted advisor to clients</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Address customer pain points, goals and requirements and effectively providing a solution</li>
<li>Demonstrate and train prospects/trials and customers on our software</li>
<li>Create training/support content for our community (i.e videos &amp; tech articles)</li>
<li>Apply a consultative approach with prospects and customers for product customization/software when required</li>
</ul>
