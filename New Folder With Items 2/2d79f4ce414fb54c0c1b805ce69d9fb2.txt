<h2>Bank Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Reports to: Vice President of Branch Administration</p>
<p>Qualifications for Job</p>
<p>Education – Bachelor’s Degree Preferred</p>
<p>Experience - Five years experience in a banking environment. Prior Assistant Manager and operational experience required.</p>
<p>Special Skills – Must possess the ability to develop positive customer relations, effectively communicate with customers and employees. Demonstrated ability to lead a team in the achievement of branch sales, service and operational objectives. Experience in developing and expanding customer relationships. Must have a basic knowledge of the operation of the following equipment and software applications: Teller software, Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel and other software applications used by the bank.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<p>Focus on business development within geographic area to include developing and deepening customer relationships. Call on existing and prospective customers to generate new or additional business. Refer potential lending opportunities to appropriate Business Development Officer. Direct the overall activities of the branch to ensure achievement of established goals and objectives. Prepare and administer budget for branch. Plan and schedules activities within the branch that support that bank’s overall objectives. Meet with customers to discuss personal financial needs and recommend ways in which the bank can serve their needs. Sell a full range of banking services to present and potential customers. Discuss and process loan application for on either a secured or unsecured basis; interviews loan applicants, gather necessary documentation, forward to the loan department and follow up with customers as needed. Complete loan settlements and submit completed loan package to the loan department. Provide the necessary approvals for check cashing, official checks, wire transfers, signature guarantees etc. (within established authority levels). Ensure all teller work is scanned daily. Responsible for monitoring and completing CDD and customer risk assessments on new customers and existing customers. Responsible for the completion of all required BSA Requirements and Compliance Guidelines. Demonstrate retention strategies to retain customers’ business. Responsible for reviewing reports and the collection of overdrawn accounts. Responsible for the completion of the monthly certification / audit package. Follow established guidelines for safe deposit box access. Monitor those that are delinquent. Maintain prescribed security controls to protect the facility against criminal and fraudulent operations. Participates in dual control process of ATM balancing and cash replenishment on a weekly basis. Ensures that the appearance of the ATM area and entire branch is neat and clean at all times. Attend bi-monthly Branch Manager meetings and review the covered information with Assistant Manager and team (as appropriate). Represent the bank through participation in a variety of civic, charitable, educational and business activities. Serve as liaison with BluePay in the establishment of merchant credit card processing. Responsible for identifying and referring potential merchant service customers to our BluePay representative. Demonstrate knowledge of credit card products offered through TCM Bank.</p>
<p>Leadership</p>
<p>Ensure that the branch is adequately staffed and that all personnel are trained in operational and branch procedures. Lead the branch in the achievement of sales and service objectives. Responsible for employee scheduling and completing employee weekly timesheet for payroll. Minimize branch turnover and overtime. Administer human resource policies within the branch. Complete the performance appraisal process and documentation. Participate in the interview process for potential new hires. Review management reports to determine productivity and/or problem areas and ensure that corrective action is taken. Evaluate performance and recommend personnel actions such as promotions transfers, salary increases, schedule changes and terminations. Banking Policies. Demonstrate complete knowledge and understanding of the BSA, SAR, and Harford Bank security procedures. Demonstrate a complete understanding of FDIC principles. Maintain security of customer information and adhere to the Privacy Policy. Follow Harford Bank’s check cashing guidelines. Successfully complete required compliance modules. Demonstrate consistent professional image and adhere to the bank’s dress code. Responsible for reading all internal communications distributed within the Branch.</p>
