<p>Description</p>
</div>
<div><div>
<p>The Controller is responsible for the accurate and timely financial reporting of Lundberg Family Farms and related entities, and analyses of operations, including interim and final financial statements with supporting schedules, for the guidance of management. This position is responsible for measuring the company’s financial performance and enforcing the company’s accounting policies, the maintenance of its fiscal records, and the preparation of financial reports. This position involves supervision over general accounting, property accounting, internal, auditing and budgetary controls. The Controller will be expected to understand company financial information and drivers of operational results. The position works with the Financial Planning &amp; Analysis team, collaborating on forecasts, crop bookings and other overlapping activities. The Controller is a key position in the finance team and is expected to be a driver of improvement and excellence while providing both requested and self-initiated analysis and reporting.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions/Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>
General Accounting</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and oversee all general accounting practices and functions.</li>
<li>Assure that duties, responsibilities and authority of each accounting position are clearly defined, effective, and communicated to staff.</li>
<li>Direct preparation of internal and external financial statements in accordance with U.S. generally accepted accounting principles.</li>
<li>Oversee monthly closing process, ensuring an effective close within four business days of month end, with the fifth day available for review and analysis.</li>
<li>Manage the year-end financial audit.</li>
<li>Manage and oversee tax preparation coordinating with outside tax preparers.</li>
<li>Ensure that all taxes are paid in an accurate and timely manner.</li>
<li>Actively manages daily treasury operations including cash balances, borrowing requirements, ACH, wire, and other payment operations.</li>
<li>Protect assets by establishing, monitoring, and enforcing internal controls.</li>
<li>Achieve budget objectives by scheduling expenditures, analyzing variances, communicating key information, and initiating correcting actions.</li>
<li>Responsible for balance sheet and cash flow forecasts and analysis</li>
<li>Analysis and oversight of customer credit management.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Personnel Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure compliance of company policies within all local, state, and federal regulations including, but not limited to, OSHA regulations and wage and hour laws.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Directly supervise the General Ledger Accountant and the Assistant Controller; indirectly supervise the AR, AP, and Deductions Teams who are directly supervised by the Assistant Controller.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assist direct reports in the diagnosis and correction of finance issues and technical problems; teach direct reports the solution and how it was arrived at to enable them to self-resolve similar issues that arise later.</li>
<li>Oversee development of direct reports, actively mentoring and working to advance their skillsets.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Supervise and evaluate accounting staff ensuring that staff has the highest level of skill necessary to meet company needs and objectives. Recommend staff changes for the accounting department.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Conduct annual performance assessments. Coach and develop staff by providing an environment that encourages innovation and professional development.</li>
<li>Draft and administer development plans and provide development opportunities.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Safety</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Adheres to safety programs in order to provide a safe and clean workplace for fellow employees within the regulations of all OSHA local, state and federal regulations.</li>
<li>Complete necessary trainings to ensure all Lundberg certifications required for role are maintained as current. Certifications may include, but are not limited to, Injury and Illness Prevention Program (IIPP), Back Safety, Forklift, Arc Flash, Confined Space, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Performs other assignments from time to time as requested by the Chief Financial Officer and other members of the Senior Leadership Team to achieve department and company business goals.</li>
<li>Maintain regular attendance in accordance with Lundberg Family Farms’ Attendance and Punctuality standards.</li>
<li>Complete training required for position and maintain applicable certifications.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Ability</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Technologically skilled with advanced knowledge of accounting and reporting software.</li>
<li>Ability to independently digest and act upon business facts to address situations that may or may not have been previously encountered.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of manual and automated accounting systems, well-developed financial analysis capabilities.</li>
<li>Ability to translate financial information into understandable metrics and verbiage</li>
<li>Excellent supervisory, communication and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Ability to manage a dynamic team of varying functions and experience levels</li>
<li>Demonstrate collaborative behavior with colleagues, supervisors and managers at all times.</li>
<li>Work well under pressure in order to meet multiple, and sometimes, competing deadlines.</li>
<li>Knowledge of combined and consolidated entities and related accounting.</li>
<li>Knowledge of accounting for sales activities transacted in foreign currencies and related hedge considerations.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
Minimum</p>
<ul>
<li>Five years of experience in financial management with increasing responsibilities, including financial reporting, analysis, and related financial functions.</li>
<li>Three years of supervisory experience managing a multi-person team across varying financial functions</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Three years experience with a full function ERP system, such as Microsoft Dynamics Navision</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree Accounting, Finance, or Business Administration.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Thorough knowledge of US GAAP</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Preferred</p>
<ul>
<li>Master’s Degree, CMA, or CPA</li>
</ul>

Work area is in an inside office environment with moderate temperatures and low background noise.</p>
