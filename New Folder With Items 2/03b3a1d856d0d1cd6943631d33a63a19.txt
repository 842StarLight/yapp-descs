<b>Manager, Forecasting</b><br>
<br>
The Forecasting Manager will be responsible for leading ongoing enhancement, maintenance, and production of patient based demand forecast for launched and future indications to support brand commercial planning efforts. Leading effort to enhance data/information gathering, forecast analysis, scenario enhancements, validation, and other related/relevant tasks. Collaborating with functional department leads to design, implement, and execute core patient based demand forecast processes with an emphasis on business partnering and customer service. Strong commercial forecasting and business acumen.<br>
<br>
<b>Key accountabilities/responsibilities:</b>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Forecasting: responsible for leveraging primary and secondary research insights and team inputs to enhance insights and forecast and run accurate, actionable, and evidenced based forecasting models while validating results and insights. The forecaster will also produce analogues, help formulate assumptions, identify information/knowledge gap and data needs with the forecast. Identify enhancements and/or execute enhancements to forecast model and inputs</li>
<li>Demand Management: collaborate closely with marketing peers/commercial teams across similar-to-different disease areas to develop patient-flow based demand forecasts and input assumptions. Work with FP&amp;A team to convert unit demand forecast into revenue forecast.</li>
<li>Sales Goal Setting and Performance Tracking: work with Sales analytics team to set field force sales targets. Provide forecast input and observations to marketing and analytics teams for performance tracking purposes.</li>
<li>Adhocs/Scenarios/Tracking : Own semi-complex to complex ad-hocs on demand tracking vs forecast and create clear deliverables for business leads. Run scenario modelling for various forecast scenarios. Provide monthly tracking metrics for demand actuals vs forecast</li>
</ul>
<br>

<li>Ability to build strong relationships with relevant stakeholders</li>
