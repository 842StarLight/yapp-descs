<p><br>
<b>WHAT YOU WILL DO (Responsibilities):</b></p>
<p><br>
As a Store Manager, we rely on your ability to generate sales momentum, build your team, and lead by example. Your leadership will set the tone for your team to meet and exceed performance goals. We look for you to also:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Nurture hospital relationships by meeting key hospital contacts to address concerns, introduce new marketing/product, and follow up on past requests.</li>
<li>
Recruit, train, coach and develop sales staff on all aspects of sales for Lori’s Gifts including, but not limited to, sales techniques, products, promotions, and procedures</li>
<li>
Actively manage and motivate sales team every single day to ensure store sales quotas are being met or exceeded</li>
<li>
Provide effective performance feedback to all staff and sets timeframe for achievement</li>
<li>
Consistently find ways to increase employee and location productivity, and drive sales</li>
<li>
Maintain appropriate level of staffing at location by recruiting, selecting, orienting and training employees</li>
<li>
Ensure availability of merchandise by maintaining inventory levels</li>
<li>
Market merchandise by adhering to advertising, sales promotion, and display plans</li>
<li>
Secure merchandise and protect employees by adhering to security standards</li>
<li>
Contribute to team effort by accomplishing related results as required</li>
<li>
Handle scheduling of staff at location to ensure appropriate shift coverage</li>
<li>
Handle all intensified customer service issues with professionalism</li>
<li>
Execute daily operational procedures with efficiency, diligence, and integrity</li>
<li>
Communicate, execute, and implement Lori’s Gifts policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
This is not an exhaustive list of duties or functions and may not necessarily comprise all the "essential functions" for purposes of the ADA.<br>
