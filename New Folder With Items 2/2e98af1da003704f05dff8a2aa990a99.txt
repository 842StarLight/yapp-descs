<h2>VP Content Creation - Calvin Klein</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>

<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b>:</p>
<p>
The VP of Content Creation is responsible for the on-brand, on-strategy execution all of multi-channel creative by translating global marketing strategies into creative strategies, concepts and executions consistent with the brand’s standards, aesthetic and voice, by business vertical.<br>
<br>
This strong team leader will deliver world class multi-channel digital-first Fashion / Lifestyle content ideas and creative direction to campaigns and “always on” content libraries that tap into cultural insights, influence target consumers and create cultural movements.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES/ACCOUNTABILITIES OF THE JOB</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead the creative vision and design aesthetic for campaigns across all channels under the leadership of the EVP of Global Creative</li>
<li>
Drive the achievement of creative goals and strategic solutions to meet brand, marketing and sales objectives, working closely with Marketing Leads, the VP of Strategy &amp; Innovation and divisional Sr. Directors, Art Directors and Content Strategists on your team</li>
<li>
Build out digital-first, mobile-first content libraries that can be adapted across all consumer touchpoints, from digital advertising content to in-store collateral/signage and traditional media</li>
<li>
Deliver creative campaign and content solutions through the creation of pitches and the overall management of creative production (including multi-channel creative concepts, casting directional, on-set art direction, layout design) through to release into market, working closely with the Marketing team</li>
<li>
Presents creative concepts, strategies and work to internal and external partners</li>
<li>
Continuously develop knowledge regarding new design trends, innovation in the creative field, customer experiences and technology</li>
<li>
Lead the training and development of your creative team, including delivering on guidance and clear direction, problem solving and people management</li>
<li>
Oversee the end-to-end development and completion of all global campaigns, including being accountable to the quality of all end products, approving final post production and deliveries</li>
<li>
Act as a brand steward and ensure clear and comprehensive communication to business partners and team members</li>
<li>
Collaborate closely with cross-functional team leaders in the development and execution of creative and design projects, from marketing to eComm teams, creative production and post-production teams</li>
<li>
Participate in consumer insights workshops and briefing sessions, acting as a creative strategy leader within your team and in working cross-departmentally as needed to clarify business needs and deliver creative solutions</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS &amp; EXPERIENCE</b>:</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Experience</b>:</p>
<p>
10+ years of creative experience in fashion advertising</p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have strong managerial/leadership competencies and experience, and a proven ability to inspire and nurture individual talents within a collaborative environment</li>
<li>
Progressive, cutting edge creative background</li>
<li>
Extensive experience working with Designers and creative driven culture</li>
<li>
Experience working in a corporate environment is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education</b>:</p>
<p>
Bachelor’s degree in business related field or equivalent experience</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Skills</b>:</p>
<p>
Superior understanding of the history of CALVIN KLEIN Advertising and the current Brand platform Must be “Digital First” and have expansive knowledge in this space. Must be a strategic thinker and creative problem solver, with an ability to create outstanding work across an expansive and diverse environment. Extraordinary attention to detail and understanding of the CALVIN KLEIN aesthetic. Excellent communication, written and verbal Must have excellent presentation skills Must be a good listener, and have the ability to influence others. Must have a strong desire to continually bring creative and cultural innovation to the process Must have the ability to multi-task and delegate creative projects in a fast-paced and ambitious work environment. Strong work ethic, and relentless commitment to quality and efficiency Ability to lead, inspire and manage teams. Flexibility to adjust quickly to changes and deliver on tight deadlines. Ability to navigate complexity Proficient in latest design software</p>
