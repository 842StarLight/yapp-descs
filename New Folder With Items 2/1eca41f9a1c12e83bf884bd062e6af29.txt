<h2>Advertising Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>THE COMPANY: </b></p>
<p>Headquartered in Ridgewood, NJ Ridgewood Medical Media, LLC is a health care publishing and media company. RMM publishes trade magazines for pharmacists and lab administrators. Many of the top health care equipment and service providers support their marketing efforts through advertising and custom communication solutions in our magazines.</p>
<p>Please visit the website of our publication at www.medlabmag.com.</p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION: </b></p>
<p>RMM is looking for an experiences B2B print/digital advertising sales executive with strong initiative and a desire for professional growth. Applicant must be a team player and able to multi-task priorities, identify and close sales opportunities in the role of Advertising Account Manager. The position requires excellent written and verbal communication skills and strong organizational skills. 3 to 5years proven ability to create and grow magazine ad sales business relationships is a necessity.</p>
<p>This Advertising Account Manager position, under the direction of the Accounts Director, will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prospecting, identifying &amp; establishing contact with key prospects</li>
<li>Building strong relationships with existing and new clients</li>
<li>Meeting all sales &amp; revenue targets</li>
<li>Monitor, support all accounts</li>
</ul>
