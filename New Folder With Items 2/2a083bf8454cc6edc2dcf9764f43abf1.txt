<h2>Sr. Network Engineer (Cisco ACI preferred)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Are you looking for a challenging role as a <b>Sr. Network Engineer</b>? InterVision Systems is a strategic service provider committed to unlocking value by delivering innovative technology through a consultative approach. We believe we will transform businesses through the evolutionary power of technology. Our people are the best in their field!</p>
<p><i>If you are looking for a career changing opportunity, we want to meet you!</i></p>
<p>InterVision is seeking a <b>Sr. Network Engineer</b> who is well versed in a variety of Networking technologies. The Sr. Engineer role is principally responsible for designing, implementing, operating, and supporting multi-tenant, scalable network solutions. The successful candidate is an IT professional with hands-on experience with a broad range of hardware and software, including routing, switching, security, SDWan and load balancing. In addition to superb technical skills, the candidate must have a strong customer service orientation and excellent communication skills. We are looking for a driven, self-motivated individual who can meet deliverables with minimal direction or oversight. Technical certifications and advanced knowledge of Cisco ACI, NX-OS, and IOS are a must. The candidate also must be able to complete certifications and train on solutions from leading networking vendors, including F5, Fortinet, Riverbed, Arista, Juniper and others.</p>
<p><b><i>Travel required up to 25%</i></b></p>
<p><b>We welcome your experience and talents to our team!</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 8 years of hands-on experience designing and installing complex network systems</li>
<li>Experienced automating implementations, configuration, and management</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of routing, switching, load balancing, network security solutions</li>
<li>Experience configuring, installing, upgrading, testing, and troubleshooting large-scale network environments</li>
<li>Technical certifications, experienced and advanced knowledge in multiple network technologies: Cisco/FirePower, Fortinet, Checkpoint, Palo Alto, Aruba, or other leading networking vendors.</li>
<li>Experience with F5, Riverbed, Aruba and VMware solutions is a big plus</li>
<li>Excellent analytical, problem-solving, verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Cisco CCNP certification or higher required, CCNP-Cloud strongly preferred</li>
<li>CCNA, CCIE, CISSP, CISA, Checkpoint, Juniper, Palo Alto Networks, Aruba, Fortinet, and Microsoft a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Some of the exciting work you will encounter:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The successful candidate will provide technical consulting services for Information Technology security projects</li>
<li>Perform post sales activities conducive to building a networking practice. Support Pre-Sales in scoping and pricing implement</li>
<li>Ability to deliver Infrastructure as Code solutions using Perl, Python, etc.</li>
<li>Complete project work on schedule and conforming to InterVision’s standard project methodology.</li>
<li>Architectural design, development, and implementation of network solutions from multiple vendors in the following areas: Routing, switching, and network security</li>
<li>Ability to design and execute project plans for system builds, migrations, integrations, and customization while accounting for risk and forecasting technical issues.</li>
<li>The successful candidate will need to develop and execute detailed implementation plans to ensure smooth and effective migrations to new technology and new versions of existing technology.</li>
<li>This position will occasionally be a customer facing. Thus, the candidate must have the communication skills, technical skills, and integrity to work with the Customer to design security solutions to meet and exceed customer expectations.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned and required. Duties and responsibilities may change from time to time without notice and include but are not limited to the duties described above.</li>
<br>
</ul>
