<h2>Account Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>Intrado is a global provider of communication and network infrastructure services. Intrado helps its clients more effectively communicate, collaborate and connect with their audiences through a diverse portfolio of solutions that include unified communications services, safety services, interactive services such as automated notifications, telecom services and specialty agent services. For 30 years, Intrado has provided reliable, high-quality, voice and data services. Intrado has sales and operations in the United States, Canada, Europe, the Middle East, Asia Pacific and Latin America.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Intrado’s Life &amp; Safety</b> segment has an <b>Account Development Specialist </b>opening in their <b>Account Management Department </b>in<b> Joplin or St. Louis, MO</b>. The Account Development Specialist will be responsible for maintaining their client relationships and growing revenue for their top National Education accounts. This is an account management role where you will be responsible for renewing client contracts getting ready to expire, build relationships with the client, act as a liaison and voice of the customer with internal teams and support sales in uncovering cross-sell opportunities. If you take pride in building &amp; maintaining client relationships and providing excellent customer service and have the ability to negotiate and diffuse difficult situations, this may be the position for you! Please apply and e-mail your resume to shellie.babin@west.com</div>
