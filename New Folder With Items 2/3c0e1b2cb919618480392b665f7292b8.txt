<h2>Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>nCino offers exciting career opportunities for individuals who want to join the worldwide leader in cloud banking.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
The Recruiter role will be focused on sourcing and recruiting top talent for nCino. This person will work directly with leadership to identify the ideal candidate qualifications and experience needed and then actively recruit candidates using LinkedIn, cold calling, email and other sourcing techniques. This person will also be responsible for leveraging and maintaining online recruiting tools such as the nCino website, LinkedIn, Meetups, job boards and other resources.<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Learn and understand the nCino culture, Bank Operating System and business model
</li>
<li>Learn and understand Workday, nCino’s internal applicant tracking system
</li>
<li>Meet with the client teams to understand hiring plans and ideal candidates, and develop recruiting plans for each of the different roles.
</li>
<li>Learn nCino recruiting pitch and scripts for cold calling purposes and lead generation
</li>
<li>Interview existing employees for roles being recruited for to understand and develop candidate profiles
</li>
<li>Utilize and improve current screening processes for screening technical candidates
</li>
<li>Become comfortable assessing an applicants’ relevant knowledge, skills, experience and aptitude
</li>
<li>Build a pipeline of candidates and begin filling open roles with sourced candidates
</li>
<li>Utilize recruiting reports and dashboards to measure results, goals and recruiting data
</li>
<li>Manage positions on the nCino website and job boards
</li>
<li>Build and enhance nCino's company profile on key online recruiting resources
</li>
<li>Identify local networking events to attend and enhance brand awareness
</li>
<li>Screen candidates for availability, interest level, qualifications and technical experience
</li>
<li>Assist with University recruiting efforts and other internal talent acquisition and human capital projects
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Qualifications
</b><br>
<b>Required:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent hands on experience
</li>
<li>2+ years as a recruiter or similar customer/sales/community facing role.
</li>
<li>Ability to sell a company, culture and job opportunity
</li>
<li>Understanding and experience with online recruiting and talent acquisition tools such as LinkedIn Careers, Glassdoor, Facebook, Meetups, etc.
</li>
<li>Creativity in approaching talent acquisition in a unique way
</li>
<li>Strong time management and organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills
</li>
<li>Proactive, motivated, self-starter with the ability to use their own initiative to complete the task on time with high quality
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Desired:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Workday experience
</li>
<li>Multi-lingual
</li>
<li>Experience performing initial screening interviews to assess candidate interest and fit
</li>
<li>Experience using an applicant tracking system
</li>
<li>Excellent leadership, problem-solving and analytical skills
</li>
<li>Knowledge of banking or SaaS industry
</li>
<li>Experience recruiting in the Financial or Software industries<br>
</li>
</ul>
