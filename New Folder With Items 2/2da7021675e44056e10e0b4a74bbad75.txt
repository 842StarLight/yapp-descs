<p><b>Receptionist &amp; Administrative Assistant
</b></p>
<p><b>Central Rent-A-Crane, Inc.</b></p>
<p><b>
Hammond, IN (46323)</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
Central Rent-A-Crane, Inc. is seeking a Receptionist &amp; Administrative Assistant with solid general office skills. This is a full-time, non-exempt position.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answer incoming telephone calls and provide excellent customer service</li>
<li>Maintain neatness and organization of reception desk and front areas</li>
<li>Various administrative tasks and office support activities such as processing documents, faxing, filing and data entry</li>
<li>Ensure accurate tracking, monitoring and recording of all charges, records and other entries</li>
<li>Assist with Accounting functions as directed by the Branch Controller</li>
<li>Comply with federal, state, and company policies, procedures, and regulations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Experience Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong initiative required; ability to work independently with minimal direct supervision</li>
<li>Must have solid general office skills including a working knowledge of Microsoft Excel and Word</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented and capable of growing in responsibilities</li>
<li>Must be able to sit for extended periods of time</li>
<li>Must be able to operate office equipment and technology</li>
<li>Previous experience with accounting functions such as Collections, Accounts Payable or Accounts Receivable are a plus</li>
</ul>
