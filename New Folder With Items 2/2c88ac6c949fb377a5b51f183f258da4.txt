<h2>Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Position Description:</b><br>
PHIcure’s Business Development Representatives hold the keys to PHIcure’s growth. They are the hunters who generate interest in PHIcure and fuel PHIcure’s sales pipeline. The successful candidate will have the opportunity to gain in-depth knowledge of healthcare billing and payment solutions in a web-based environment while building credibility, trust, and value in PHIcure. They will also gain valuable experience with leading marketing and sales products, processes, and people including FreshSales. Compensation for this position is a competitive base salary and uncapped commission with a bonus opportunity - unlimited earning potential!
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
Developing and maintaining a comprehensive knowledge of PHIcure’s solution and new product pipeline
<br><br>
Identifying new business opportunities by utilizing phone, email, PHIcure marketing initiatives, and networking events to qualify prospective clients
<br><br>
Strategically managing a sales territory of several states using leading sales and marketing technology
<br><br>
Enhancing PHIcure sales and marketing database by adding and updating account and contact information
<br><br>
Facilitating product demonstrations with prospective clients
<br><br>
