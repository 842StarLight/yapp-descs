About The Team:</b></p>
<p>
Sitting in the center of our development environment, the ITOM team is building a unique business-level service modeling, management, and mapping solution. <p>
The successful candidate is a natural leader who loves to drive strategic customer outcomes through both their own experience and more importantly through the efforts of their highly talented team. The ideal candidate will have experience building and developing high performing teams with relevant development background.</p>
