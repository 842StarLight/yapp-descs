<p><b>CO-DIRECTOR, PRE-ANALYTICS LAB OPERATIONS</b></p>
<p>Sema4 is currently seeking an experienced and results-oriented leader for the role of <b>Co-Director of Pre-Analytics Lab Operations</b>. This position will report to the Chief Diagnostics Officer and ensure the day-to-day operational excellence and logistics management of the Accessioning Departments of an expanding high-throughput clinical genetic testing laboratory at two sites. This is a full-time position requiring some evenings, weekends and travel. <b>Currently based in New York City (until laboratory moves to Stamford, CT in 2020- candidates must be able to work out of all locations) and Branford, CT</b>.
</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervise the daily activity of accessioning managers, supervisors and staff to ensure all samples are accessioned same-day with extremely high accuracy according to SOPs and other requirements.</li>
<li>Optimize cross-site scheduling according to the operational needs of the laboratory, clients and Sema4’s business.
</li>
<li>Build and manage metrics to provide feedback and accountability to staff. Define KPIs and standardize departmental methodologies.</li>
<li>Maintain documentation for QA, compliance, management, regulatory bodies and clients as well as laboratory operations.
</li>
<li>Act as a cross-functional resource for inter-disciplinary teams to streamline operations and efficiency while growing Sema4’s business and exceeding client expectations.
</li>
<li>Develop and oversee training and team building of new and existing staff on a continuous basis.</li>
<li>Create and manage departmental incentive programs.</li>
<li>Support the recruitment, hiring and development of effective teams with succession planning.</li>
<li>Administer departmental budgets.
</li>
<li>Liaise with all departments in the laboratory and management to ensure accuracy and swift corrective actions as needed based on client, test, market dynamics and specimen requirements.</li>
<li>Oversee daily accessioning operations including, but not limited to:
<ul>
<li>Specimen receipt and triage</li>
<li>Order entry and documentation retention</li>
<li>Quality check of all information input into the LIS/LIMS system</li>
<li>Referral testing and specimen send-outs
</li>
<li>Competency, safety and retraining of staff</li>
<li>Delivery of daily, weekly, monthly &amp; quarterly operational metrics</li>
<li>Departmental inventory and supply orders</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Provide thought leadership and recommendations for improving workflows, team structures and system configurations.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing mission critical, complex projects to deliver results within the authorized budgets and approved timelines.</li>
<li>Identify and transparently communicate issues and solutions both within the accessioning department and to leadership.</li>
<li>Forecast trends to meet changing requirements.
</li>
<li>Ensure team meets all regulatory requirements set forth by CLIA, CAP, NYSDOH, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required, BS in Biology or other healthcare field preferred. Master’s Degree a plus.
</li>
<li>Superior computer skills required, comfortable with new software tools and integration, advanced in Microsoft Office (Excel).
</li>
<li>Highly experienced with data metrics.
</li>
<li>Proven knowledge of lean management.
</li>
<li>Experience in root cause analysis, talented strategic manager.</li>
<li>Expert people manager, able to motivate a large team of 20 plus people on various levels.
</li>
<li>Exceptional in investigating, analyzing, recommending, implementing and monitoring workflow changes as needed.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills; ability to effectively work with all stakeholders, internal and external, including clients, sales, software engineers, management, lab directors and staff both within and outside of accessioning.</li>
<li>3-5 years of laboratory accessioning experience required. Experience in clinical genetic testing strongly preferred.
</li>
<li>2-3 years of people management required, preferably managing a team of 10 or more in an operational function.
</li>
<li>2-3 years of managerial experience within laboratory accessioning required.</li>
</ul>
