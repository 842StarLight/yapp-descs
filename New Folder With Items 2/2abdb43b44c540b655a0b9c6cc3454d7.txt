<div><b>Job Description:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>In this role, the <b>Cloud Advisory Consultant </b>will:</div>
<ul>
<li>Advise technical clients on cloud-enabled enterprise transformation agendas
</li>
<li>Assist in the creation of the value case for the client business challenges that would most benefit from migration to Azure Cloud
</li>
<li>Help clients understand cloud-oriented practices and approaches including DevOps, PaaS, containers, Hybrid cloud, migration and modernization of applications using cloud, and Big Data
</li>
<li>Be disciplined in business-management; adaptable to a culture of accountability; build a strong and active business network; meet sales targets and operational standards
</li>
<li>Develop and coach a high-performing team by hiring diverse talent, prioritizing development, leading by example and preparing people for more senior positions in other parts of the organization</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>

<li>Minimum of 3 years of client-facing consulting experience
</li>
</ul>
