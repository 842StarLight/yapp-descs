<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Account Manager, role requires a highly organized and high energy individual who is comfortable interfacing with a diverse audience ranging from marketing to corporate IT, and is able to tailor their message accordingly.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Leverage relationships in an assigned set of existing accounts to prospect and close new business opportunities with additional decision makers, including a wide range of marketing and demand generation titles, as well as corporate communications, training, procurement and sales.</li>
<li>
Partner with ON24's Client Success Team to summarize existing business in key accounts and facilitate introductions to client advocates and references.</li>
<li>
Track, organize, and close a large number of pending renewal opportunities.</li>
<li>
Consult with prospects to determine the best solutions for their specific needs. Recommend solutions, prepare and present proposals and get contracts executed.</li>
<li>
Conduct presentations and product demonstrations.</li>
<li>
Achieve all individual activity and revenue targets set by the company.</li>
<li>
Log sales activity (prospecting, opportunities, revenue, next steps) in Salesforce.com.</li>
<li>
Attend sales seminars, sales meetings or educational activities to stay up-to-date on the latest developments, trends, and regulations in the market place.</li>
<li>
Keep current with all ON24 product information, pricing and contract terms.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 Required Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2-5 years of direct sales experience, ideally B2B sales at a software company. SaaS sales experience a plus; selling into marketing, corporate communications, and/or training also preferred.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience in developing new business relationships and expanding into other divisions</li>
<li>
A true hunter mentality who strives for the close.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience of being able to conduct online sales presentations and product demonstrations.</li>
<li>
Strong technical aptitude.</li>
<li>
A Bachelor's degree in Communication, Business, Marketing, or related fields, or relevant experience.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Essential Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to converse in a professional and knowledgeable manner on various industry topics and Internet technologies in order to sell opportunities to prospective customers.</li>
<li>
Ability to create successful sales strategies for ON24 products and/or services.</li>
<li>
Must possess highly developed organizational, planning and management skills.</li>
<li>
Strong detail orientation with numbers, follow through and contract details.</li>
<li>
Must have superior written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>
Must be comfortable working in a fast-paced, quota-driven environment with changing needs and requirements.</li>
<li>
Professional experience with Salesforce.com, Microsoft Outlook, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>
Creativity, persistence, integrity, curiosity, and the will to succeed.</li>
</ul>
