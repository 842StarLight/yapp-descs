<h2>VP Enterprise Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Due to rapid growth and increased demand for our Data Privacy Compliance platform, WireWheel is seeking an experienced VP for Enterprise Sales. As the VP for Enterprise Sales, you will be responsible for leading a team to find and develop leads by targeting companies in the Global 2000. You will successfully navigate complex sales cycles. The VP of Enterprise Sales will develop a clear understanding of the privacy industry, including regulatory environment, and uses this knowledge to support all the primary responsibilities. The VP of Enterprise Sales is be responsible for the recruitment, hiring, training and development of the team as well as tracking all sales metrics.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Leads the sales organization to achieve sales goals for the Company</li>
<li>Leads complex intelligent sales cycle with c-suite executives</li>
<li>Manages sales process including qualification up through contract negotiation</li>
<li>Recruits, hire, train and manage a team of enterprise account executives</li>
<li>Tracks and reports on sales metrics and forecast to executive management</li>
<li>Actively uses Salesforce.com to manage sales process, and set standards for performance metrics</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree or equivalent combination of education, training and work experience</li>
<li>5 - 7 years of experience in enterprise software or SAAS sales leader required</li>
<li>Prior experience with Data Privacy, Cybersecurity or BPM a plus</li>
<li>A proven enterprise sales leader and mentor with people development skills</li>
<li>A self-starter, prepared to work in a fast-paced, demanding, high growth environment</li>
<li>Experience building successful sales teams</li>
<li>Early to Midrange startup experience a must</li>
<li>Experience leading successful company sales organizations and driving organizational sales goals</li>
<li>Experience with complex sales and C-Suite</li>
<li>Analytical and reporting skills required, with experience with Salesforce required</li>
<li>Articulate, with exceptional written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Travel required up to 50% domestically</li>
</ul>
