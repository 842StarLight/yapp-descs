<h2>Automotive Controller/Bookkeeper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>South East Dealership is seeking an individual with a heavy background in Accounting/Bookkeeping and Automotive Controller experience. This position is responsible for managing the accounting, bookkeeping, and general office procedures. Our ideal candidate has a strong working knowledge of CDK - Light Speed/EVO software as well as a full understanding of the daily facets of the dealership's operations. Competitive compensation and paid vacation.</p>
<p><b>Automotive Controller/Bookkeeper Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage all aspects of G/L, A/P, and Inventory including reconciliation of schedules</li>
<li>General Bookkeeping</li>
<li>Reconciles cash balance and oversees daily bank deposits</li>
<li>Reconciles monthly bank statement</li>
<li>Prepares daily cash sheet</li>
<li>Reviews all accounts payable transactions including bills, returns, and credit card charges ensuring accuracy of each bill and proper classification</li>
<li>Reconcile monthly credit card statements</li>
<li>Maintains a General Ledger maintenance</li>
<li>Ensures that necessary controls are in place to safeguard dealership assets</li>
<li>Verify figures with Sales and F&amp;I Departments/submit warranties</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Automotive Controller/Bookkeeper </b><b>Preferred </b><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2-3 years of Automotive or related Dealership accounting/bookkeeping</li>
<li>CDK - Light Speed/EVO experience is a plus*</li>
<li>Bachelor's Or Masters Degree in one of the following field of study, Accounting, Business</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge of Microsoft Office Products.</li>
<li>Ability to learn the Dealership Management System, Spreadsheet software, accounting software, and inventory software</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $45,000.00 to $65,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Accounting/Bookkeeping: 2 years (Required)</li>
<li>Controller: 2 years (Required)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's (Preferred)</li></ul>
