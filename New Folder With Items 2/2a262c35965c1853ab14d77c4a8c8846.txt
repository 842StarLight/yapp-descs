<h2>Sr. Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Windows is used by over a billion people each day in their pursuit to get things done. Each day we prove why Windows Pro is best for businesses: from small to mid-size companies to Enterprises. There are Windows Pro devices for every type of commercial customer: from firstline workers to information workers to power users. Our job is to drive Windows Pro marketing to increase customer love for Windows commercial devices and to stay competitive.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Team</b></div>
<div>
We are a team of go-getters with a philosophy centered on making big impact while having fun along the way. Honesty, a win-together attitude and above all, a good sense of humor are our building blocks for a strong cohesive team. We like to challenge the status-quo, and have a deep passion for technology and the impact it can deliver for our customers. We’re looking for people with multiple backgrounds and skills, from data heads, to warrior poets, business savvy communicators and social storytellers.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div>
The Windows Commercial Devices team is looking for an innovative<b> Sr. Product Marketing Manager</b> who is a strong thinker and leader to find the next billion-dollar opportunity for Windows Pro and our esteemed partners. You will play a strategic role to identify new opportunities for incremental sales by seeking customer/market insights, create marketing and investment plans then drive execution with field and partners.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Collect data/design research on customers, purchase behaviors, marketplace and competitors to identify new opportunities</li>
<li>
Understand the business issues for decision makers and develop a value proposition that resonates with them</li>
<li>
Substantiate the buying process: identify the decision makers, approvers and influencers</li>
<li>
Create plan that meet penetration, revenue and profitability expectations</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Seek incremental investments, determine where investments should be directed</li>
<li>
Partner with the OEM account teams and WW category teams to create programs and campaigns for SMB and Enterprise customers</li>
<li>
Collaborate with outbound on marketing material and campaigns, create objection handling materials for end user inquiries</li>
<li>
Track and monitor progress on OEM and partner programs and campaigns</li>
</ul>
