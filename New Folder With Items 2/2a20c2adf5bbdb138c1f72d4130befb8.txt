<h2>Assistant Landscape Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary</b></h2>
<p>Sound Landscapes, Inc, is growing! We are looking for an experienced, well-trained, and enthusiastic leader who takes pride in his or her work and can influence peers, subordinates, and supervisors to do the same. Dependability, Integrity, Reliability, and Flexibility are key traits of all Sound Employees. The ideal candidate has verifiable experience with lawn care operations and management and will step into the position with very little training and supervision.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b></h2>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b> management</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Adopt and embody the vision of Sound Landscapes.</li>
<li>Reports directly to the Operations Manager.</li>
<li>Perform duties required of Lawn Care Foremen, Crew Leads, and Crew Members (for Managers overseeing the same).</li>
<li>Must be available between 7:00 AM and 5:00 PM M-F. May be asked to work beyond 5:00 PM and some Saturdays. Overtime will be available for non-salaried positions.</li>
<li>Update periodical plans including Goal Setting and Reviews as required by Owner and Management Team.</li>
<li>Operate company owned vehicles (trucks and trailers).</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Crew Management</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Perform all tasks related to hiring, onboarding, training, performance management, and employee transitioning.</li>
<li>Manage and optimize employee-to-employee, employee-to-customer, and employee-to-vendor disputes and relationships.</li>
<li>Ensure company SOPs specific to the position are followed and updated through training and job site visits.</li>
<li>Maximize field productivity, customer satisfaction, and employee morale.</li>
<li>Fill in for Managers, Foremen, and Crew Leads as needed.</li>
<li>Provide labor support as needed for dispatched crews.</li>
<li>Communicate with Managers, Foremen, and Crew Leads through the software and other methods.</li>
<li>Update periodical plans including Goal Setting and Reviews as required by GM and LS Manager.</li>
<li>Research, Train, and Maintain current industry best practices, innovations, and regulations.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Account Management</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Sales: ABC – Always Be Contacting leads in order to maintain a full schedule and customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Learn and utilize the features and benefits of the software used by Sound.</li>
<li>Set-up new accounts and update client/customer information including job notes, upsells, and concerns in the software.</li>
<li>Maintain mutually beneficial relationships with suppliers, vendors, contractors, and clients.</li>
<li>Ensure all quality controls (QA &amp; QC) are clearly Landscape Foremen, Crew Leads, and Crew Members</li>
<li>Operate machinery including heavy equipment, power tools, and equipment related to landscaping and lawncare.</li>
<li>Perform jobsite setup and cleanup.</li>
<li>Perform general maintenance (corrective and preventative) on all vehicles and other equipment used.</li>
<li>Identify potential hazards (utility lines, gas lines, etc.).</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></h2>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma/GED</li>
<li>Valid Driver’s License</li>
<li>Ability to lift 75 lbs.</li>
<li>Strong Computer Skills.</li>
<li>Established interpersonal, leadership, communication, multi-tasking, and administrative skills.</li>
<li>4+ Years of Experience in the Landscaping/Lawncare industry, 2+ Years as Crew Lead or higher</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Preferred</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Bilingual (Spanish)</li>
<li>College Degree (Bus. Admin, Management, or Finance), Vocational or other Certification</li>
<li>Demonstrated skill in planning, directing, reporting, and administering company operations.</li>
<li>Understanding and ability to prepare reports and making verbal presentations.</li>
<li>6+ Years of Experience in the Landscaping/Lawncare industry, 3+ Years as Crew Lead or higher</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Compensation</b></h2>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Pay</b></h3>
