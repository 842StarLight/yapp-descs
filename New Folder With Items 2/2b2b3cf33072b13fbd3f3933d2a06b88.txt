<h2>Golf Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Apple Canyon Lake Property Owners Association </b></p>
<p><b>GOLF MANAGER</b></p>
<p><b>REPORTS TO: General Manager and Pro Shop Manager</b></p>
<p><b>CLASSIFICATION: Exempt</b></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY: </b>Responsible for the management of the golf operation, purchasing of goods and operation of the Golf Course promoting the safety, convenience and enjoyment of the users.</p>
<p><b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Purchasing merchandise</li>
<li>Creates and reconciles monthly physical inventory of merchandise products</li>
<li>Prepares and serves food and beverage orders in a bar setting</li>
<li>Maintain accurate records of golf course use (tee times; number of rounds; etc.)</li>
<li>Monitors weather conditions for safe golf play and determines closing of course due to severe conditions</li>
<li>Completes and maintains reports for the State of Illinois and ACLPOA</li>
<li>Provides exemplary customer service using highly developed interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Develops and maintains a positive working relationship with Pro Shop users, guests, golf leagues, event organizations as well as other local vendors</li>
<li>Plans and coordinates tournaments, leagues and clinic related activities</li>
<li>Promotes tournaments, leagues and clinic related activities with the Communications Director</li>
<li>Coordinates and prepares clean up and maintenance of the Pro Shop facility and surrounding area such as landscape, golf carts parking lot and patio</li>
<li>Maintains Pro Shop inventory for retail and records inventory in computer when received</li>
<li>Attends and participates in commission meetings related to Golf operations</li>
<li>Work with the Golf Commission to help plan and run committee golf events hosted by the ACLPOA golf course</li>
<li>Stays abreast of state laws pertaining to resale items (sales tax changes)</li>
<li>Manages all rental supplies (i.e. golf carts, clubs, etc.)</li>
<li>Writes monthly article on department activities for publishing in Apple Core.</li>
<li>Train staff about golf operation so that they understand the game and can help customers in everyday questions or issues regarding the golf course</li>
<li>Promote the golf course and golf events through the ACLPOA communications department to owners as well as to non-owners in surrounding communities.</li>
<li>Create new events for owners and non-owners to showcase the golf course and increase play. Events should be coordinated with the Pro Shop Manager so that staff can be prepared, and food and beverage sales can be maximized.</li>
<li>Look for alternative ways to promote the ACLPOA golf course to outside golfers looking at, but not limited to online websites, state PGA offices and charitable foundations.</li>
<li>Consult with Pro Shop Manager on merchandise purchases and maintain established acceptable inventory levels ensuring profitability through inventory turnover and cost of sales control guidelines.</li>
<li>Work with Pro Shop Manager in developing the annual operating budget, and manage by that budget, controlling general expenses for the entire golf operation.</li>
<li>Work with Pro Shop Manager, Building and Grounds Manager and General Manager in developing five-year plan for the golf course</li>
<li>Oversee golf course maintenance staff; giving them direction and setting priorities for the golf course</li>
<li>Evaluate the condition of the golf course on a regular basis; reporting issues to golf maintenance staff. Make changes to maintenance practices or procedures as necessary</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SECONDARY RESPONSIBILITIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES </b></p>
<ul><li>Works closely with the Pro Shop Manager and the General Manager contributing to a safe and healthy Golf Course and Pro Shop experience and amenity of its membership and public</li></ul>
<p><b>COMPETENCIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Thorough understanding of the operations and maintenance of a Pro Shop and management of course use</li>
<li>Competency on Association software</li>
<li>Highly developed interpersonal skills and ability to maintain high level of customer satisfaction</li>
</ul>
