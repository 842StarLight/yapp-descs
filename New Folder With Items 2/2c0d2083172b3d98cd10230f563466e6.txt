<h2>Digital PR Outreach Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>How the Position Works:*This is a paid internship program open on a rolling basis.*<br>
As a Digital PR Outreach Intern, you’ll gain hands-on experience learning a variety of industry tools and tactics of online public relations, business development, and Search Engine Optimization (SEO). This internship is ideal for students considering a career in Digital Marketing, Sales/Business Development, or Digital Public Relations. Your role will consist of promoting content marketing campaigns to organizations, bloggers, and select journalists using large scale email outreach.<br>
<br>
Week-to-week responsibilities include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Drafting and sending email pitches to website lists and contacts</li>
<li>Maintaining an outreach pipeline and online contact database</li>
<li>Reporting and projecting weekly key performance metrics, including open rate, reply rate, links, shares and mentions</li>
<li>Coordinating content between editorial team and bloggers</li>
<li>Researching and strategizing new audience segments</li>
</ul>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<ul>
<li>Currently enrolled in a four-year undergraduate degree program at an accredited college or university (Communications, Business, and Sales/Marketing majors preferred)</li>
<li>Minimum commitment of 15-20 hours per week for at least 8 weeks</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to consistently hit deadlines and quotas/targets</li>
<li>Outstanding written business communication skills</li>
<li>A genuine passion for learning about digital marketing and/or public relations</li>
</ul>
<br>
Preferred skills and experience:<br>
<ul>
<li>Experience with email marketing tools (e.g. MailChimp, Constant Contact, etc.) and/or CRM software</li>
<li>Basic proficiency with Microsoft Excel and/or Google Sheets</li>
</ul>
<br>
Cover Letter: Please send an email to ryan@tzhealthmedia.com explaining why you should be offered an internship.<br>
