<b>About the Team:</b><br>
At StreamSets we are building a DataOps platform that enables Data Engineering to deliver rapid, comprehensive, and curated data to their users for modern analytics, AI/ML and real-time applications. <b>The Role :</b><br>
In this role you will own the roadmap of all products that constitute our ‘Data Layer’ (Edge, Data Collector and Transformer). These products allow customers to ingest and process data from a variety of sources and destinations.
<br><br>
The ‘data’ landscape is large and complex; as a Product Manager for the data layer you are expected to have a deep, yet wide understanding of data platforms, cloud databases and services, analytics platforms, AI/ML platforms and IoT systems amongst others. You are familiar with ETL &amp; Spark.
<br><br>
The Product Management team works closely with Engineering to guide products from concept to launch. As a part of this team you will be responsible for understanding customer needs, constantly evaluating the industry/competitive landscape and developing a roadmap to deliver product that customers love. You will guide products through their lifecycle, focusing on analyzing, positioning, defining, packaging, creating demos and educating the sales organization and broader customers on the product.
<br><br>

<b>Who We Are:</b><br>
StreamSets built the industry’s first multi-cloud DataOps platform for modern data integration, helping enterprises to continuously flow big, streaming and traditional data to their data science and data analytics applications. The platform uniquely handles data drift, those frequent and unexpected changes to upstream data that break pipelines and damage data integrity. The StreamSets DataOps Platform allows for execution of any-to-any pipelines, ETL processing and machine learning with a cloud-native operations portal for the continuous automation and monitoring of complex multi-pipeline topologies.
<br><br>
Founded in 2014, StreamSets is backed by top-tier Silicon Valley venture capital firms, including Battery Ventures, New Enterprise Associates (NEA), and Accel Partners. Our founders have a deep understanding of the market, and our elite engineering team has delivered an award-winning, disruptive technology built from the ground up for the modern multi-cloud, big-data world. Our loyal customers include some of the world’s largest enterprises.
