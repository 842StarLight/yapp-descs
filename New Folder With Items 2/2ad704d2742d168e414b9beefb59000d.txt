<h2>Project Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The Project Coordinator is an integral member of the project team responsible for directing, organizing and controlling project activities, under the direction of the Director of Technical Operations. The Project Coordinator will work with the Sales and Operations teams and will assist with project kick-offs, equipment ordering and project closeout procedures. Responsibilities include monitoring and reporting on Service Department activities and customer follow up as needed.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Cross Regional Resource Coordination and Management
</li>
<li>Field Installation Calendar Management (Scheduling)
</li>
</ul>
<div>Cross Regional Travel Coordination
</div>
<ul>
<li>Understand, Monitor, Control, Update Travel Budgets and Expenses
</li>
<li>Manage and Maintain Project Folders and Files
</li>
<li>Support Project Managers and Field Teams with Timekeeping Compliance and Management
</li>
<li>Support Project Managers and Project Coordinators with Equipment and Materials Planning
</li>
<li>Coordination of Decommissioned Equipment from Customer Site
</li>
<li>Monitor Equipment Ordering Status and Work with Purchasing and Project Managers to Address Issues
</li>
<li>Support Project Team on Closeout Procedures
</li>
<li>Attend Project Meetings as Required
</li>
<li>Other Tasks as Requested or Required<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>DAILY</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Provide clerical support for project managers regarding equipment tracking, documentation logging and obtaining subcontractor quotes
</li>
<li>Check on equipment delivery status for active projects and follow up with order entry on any missing POs, etc.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>WEEKLY</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Run detailed Job Progress Reports weekly in FACTS for open projects
</li>
<li>Organize safety documentation to develop weekly safety topics
</li>
<li>Organize/maintain certifications and arrange training as needed for the field team<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>MONTHLY</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Organize monthly labor reports
</li>
<li>Compile monthly reports on company wide safety incidents, and assist in completing GC required reports
</li>
<li>Assist in talent recruitment efforts to provide low level application filtering and pursue recruitment opportunities
</li>
<li>Assist with on boarding orientation and schedule new hire training for field ops
</li>
<li>Reproduces needed forms for project preparation and service
</li>
<li>Provides efficient, accurate and effective administrative and clerical support to the Tech Ops department
</li>
<li>Attends training classes as required by the needs of the department and assigned by the management
</li>
<li>Handles other duties relative to the job as assigned by management<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
