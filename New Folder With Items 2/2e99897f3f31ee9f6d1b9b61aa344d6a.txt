<b>Who Are We?</b>
<p>We are Austin Fraser, an award-winning, international recruiting company with specialist consultants covering various industries globally. We initiated operations in the United States starting in Austin, TX in late 2015. After strong success, we established our second US presence in Denver, CO in January of 2017 and the opening of our third office in Dallas, TX in October 2018!</p>
