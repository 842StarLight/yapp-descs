<div>The IT Quality Assurance Analyst, in conjunction with team members, will develop and implement quality assurance standards and measures within the organization. This role is responsible for writing test plans, creating and executing both functional and automated tests, for tracking defects and fixes and reporting the progress of each. The Analyst will apply proven analytical and problem-solving skills to help validate IT processes through careful testing in order to maximize the benefit of business investments in IT initiatives. This is a mid-level position.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions</b><br>
<b>Strategy &amp; Planning</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Promote, foster and proactively implement the use of quality assurance best practices and standards on a daily basis.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain efficient automated scripts that utilize clean code and best practice coding standards.</li>
<li>
Proactively mentor and train Developers, QA Associates and Professional Apprentices on best practices and industry standards.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Acquisition &amp; Deployment</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Fulfill the QA effort which includes, and is not limited to: creating test plans, writing test cases, performing functional and exploratory testing, effectively communicating statuses, and coordinating meetings.</li>
<li>
Work with cross functional team members to define and understand functional &amp; non-functional requirements and capture business requirements through behavior driven test scripts.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Operational Management</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and maintain efficiencies in the automated script execution and script development through the implementation of automation best practices.</li>
<li>
Oversee and delegate work to QA Associates and Professional Apprentices, as needed.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Incidental Functions</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist with projects as required to contribute to efficiency and effectiveness of the work.</li>
<li>
Conduct research into new technologies, including tools, components, and frameworks.</li>
<li>
Attend management meetings on behalf of team.</li>
<li>
Some travel as required.</li>
<li>
Work outside the standard office 7.5 hour work day as required.</li>
</ul>
