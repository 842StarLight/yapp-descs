<p>
Jewell, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p><br>
The Store Manager will be responsible for managing a retail stores in Houston, TX in selling specific, related or general lines of products manufactured by the company. You’ll put your creativity to work with sales promotions, merchandising and pricing policies. The Store Manager will be responsible for supervising employees engaged in sales work, taking of inventories, reconciling cash with sales receipts, keeping operating records, or preparing daily record of transactions for accountant.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Plans and prepares work schedules and assigns employee to specific duties.</li>
<li>
Formulates pricing policies on merchandise according to requirements for profitability of store operations.</li>
<li>
Directs workers preparing merchandise displays and advertising.</li>
<li>
Orders merchandise or prepares requisitions to replenish merchandise on hand.</li>
<li>
Ensures compliance of employees with established security, sales, and recordkeeping procedures and practices.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Associate’s degree or two years of college or technical school and 2+ years previous sales experience or equivalent combination of education, training and experience.</li>
<li>
Prior experience in managing in a multi-site retail environment.</li>
<li>
Proven customer service skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively communicate verbally and in writing with customer, employees and company management.</li>
</ul>
