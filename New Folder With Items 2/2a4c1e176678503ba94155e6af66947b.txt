<h2>Sales Development Representative (ACOM Health)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ACOM Health (an Integrated Practice Solutions, Inc. company) is a healthcare software and services company offering practice management, electronic health record (EHR), and billing services to chiropractors nationwide. We're looking for driven Sales Development Representatives (SDR) to join our growing Inside Sales team, where they'll build relationships with our prospects and clients. You will do this through effective outbound prospecting, qualifying, educating, following up, and setting appropriate expectations with prospects.</p>
<p>
Our SDRs are high-energy, self-motivated, and agile. We're looking for professionals with strong organizational and follow up skills who work well in a collaborative environment. This position requires initiative, persistence, and the flexibility to grow with additional responsibilities. Excellent phone and email skills with the ability to quickly assess customer opportunities will be critical to your success. You will also be responsible for accurately tracking customer interactions and information in our CRM system – Microsoft Dynamics at present (likely Salesforce.com in the future.)</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain high number of outbound calls (minimum, 80-100 calls a day)</li>
<li>
Achieve monthly quota of demonstrations completed for software (1 demo booked per day)</li>
<li>
Answer prospect's questions with the goal of moving them to a demonstration</li>
<li>
Articulate ACOM Health's value proposition to decision makers to assess buying interest</li>
<li>
Maintain up-to-date software and industry knowledge</li>
<li>
Contact prospect base within assigned geographic areas via telephone to secure a demonstration</li>
<li>
Work with sales executives to get prospects from a demonstration to purchase</li>
<li>
Attend weekly sales meetings and special meetings as required</li>
<li>
Perform other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>

<li>

<li>
Have met or exceeded personal/team goals</li>
<li>
Customer service skills with the ability to listen and empathize</li>
<li>
Fearless attitude – willing to take intelligent risks</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a time-sensitive sales environment</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a team environment</li>
<li>
Strong email and web search skills</li>
<li>
Must be organized, detail and process oriented</li>
<li>
Time management skills</li>
<li>
Proficiency with standard corporate productivity tools (Microsoft Office Suite, CRM, email, phone skills)</li>
</ul>
<p>
Preferred Qualifications</p>

Microsoft Dynamics CRM or other enterprise-level CRM experience</li>
