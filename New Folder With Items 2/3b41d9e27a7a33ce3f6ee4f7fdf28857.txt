<h2>Assistant Golf Professional Seasonal FT Hours</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:</b> Outside Services (Seasonal)<br>
</p>
<p><b>DEPARTMENT: </b>Golf Operations<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
REPORTS TO:</b> Outside Services Supervisor</p>
<p><b>
JOB PURPOSE:</b> To provide superior service and maintain the finest amenities while promoting an atmosphere of excellence.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
SAFETY SENSITIVE: </b>Operates a motor vehicle or other vehicle</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assists members and guests with pre-golf check-in, special orders and any problem-solving situations, policy information and reinforcement.</li>
<li>Greets members out on the floor; actively sells with suggestions; assists with merchandising.</li>
<li>Assists with monthly inventory.</li>
<li>Assists in obtaining budgeted sales goals.</li>
<li>Strategizes to increase revenue and member satisfaction through category responsibility.</li>
<li>Golf course pace of play monitoring.</li>
<li>Tournament operations; assists with club tournament and weekly event operations.</li>
<li>Records daily sales via point of sale register.</li>
<li>Accurately records all daily play on tee sheets.</li>
<li>Responsibilities may include the opening and closing of the golf shop and delivery of daily paperwork.</li>
<li>Ensures the staging of carts and practice facilities are set up and maintained in line with individual course requirements.</li>
<li>Attends mandatory golf meetings.</li>
<li>Punctuality and attendance as set on the schedule including weekends and holidays.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by the Head Golf Professional.</li>
</ul> <p><b>LOCKER ROOM DUTIES AS ASSIGNED</b></p>
<p>
