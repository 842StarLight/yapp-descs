<h2>Manager, Learning and Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">--------
<br>Overview
<br>--------
<br><br>
The Manager of Learning &amp; Development (LDM) is responsible for all aspects of onboarding and training for DiscoverOrg customers.
<br><br>
We are looking for engaging personalities who can connect with our customers, igniting excitement about their learning journey with a high level of creativity and imagination. We welcome team-oriented candidates who are confident in their ability to help customers increase their proficiency with DiscoverOrg to solve their business challenges.
<br><br>
----------------
<br>Responsibilities
<br>----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate on-boarding and training sessions for customers, both new and existing, in accordance with established programs, to ensure immediate adoption and success while creating lasting impact.</li>
<li>Document and track trainings, attendees, notes and follow-ups for assigned customer base in Salesforce CRM.</li>
<li>Develop content for trainings for both customers and internal stakeholders (and employees, occasionally). This includes working with the Learning &amp; Development team to conduct needs assessments, meeting with internal stakeholders to understand product enhancements, and developing strategic methods to facilitate training.</li>
<li>Drive customer engagement beyond on-boarding &amp; fundamental trainings towards the highest degree of proficiency through our DiscoverOrg Certification program.</li>
<li>Customize each training based on audience/buyer personas and company profiles while leveraging their business challenges to formulate success strategies.</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with the Account Managers &amp; Customer Success Managers to schedule trainings and provide information that has a direct impact on customer health and retention.</li>
<li>Provide professional development training to DiscoverOrg employees quarterly. This includes developing content on a wide-range of topics and delivering trainings to interested employees.</li>
<li>Determine and apply assessment instruments to measure learner progress. Prepare monthly and annual reports on training program effectiveness.</li>
</ul>
--------------------
<br>Basic Qualifications
<br>--------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree, preferably in Business, Education, Human Resources or a related field.</li>
<li>2+ years proven work experience in sales or account management with 1+ of those years focused on training in sales and/or account management.</li>
<li>Excellent communication, presentation and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office and Google-Suite products.</li>
<li>2+ years' experience in Sales, Account Management, and/or Training</li>
</ul>
------------------------
<br>Preferred Qualifications
<br>------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency with Salesforce (Lightning).</li>
<li>Experience with learning management software.</li>
<li>Knowledge of the methods and principles of designing training programs, teaching individuals and groups, and assessing the impact of training initiatives.</li>
<li>Experience with engaging large Sales, Marketing and/or Recruiting audiences and improving technology adoption on a large scale.</li>
</ul>
