<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The DoD Account Executive is an individual contributor role with responsibility for driving new revenue in to US DoD agencies, field activities and other accounts, as a result of leveraging Federal Resellers, Partners FSIs, creativity, hard work and managing multiple complex sales processes to close business. You will have an excellent track record of over achievement working with DoD agencies and multiple decision makers to deliver business value. This role will work remotely with roughly 40% travel primarily within the DC Metro area, but should expect some travel to agency facilities throughout the U.S.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>You will manage the strategic selling cycle for high profile U.S. DoD Agency accounts, such as DISA, DARPA, DHA, DIA, MDA, NRO, MDA, NGA, etc.<br>
Define and execute a territory business plan that outlines the target accounts and strategies for driving productivity and high-quality pipeline that ultimately results in predictable outcomes and over achievement.<br>
Coordinate and Lead Key U.S. Federal program capture strategies to and through select U.S. Federal Systems Integrators in conjunction with the NB Federal team<br>
Establish and maintain long-term client relationships; qualifying new business in addition to participating in retention, cross sell, and up sell opportunities.<br>
Lead a complex sales cycle; orchestrating, leveraging and collaborating with additional resources – i.e. partners, Solutions (Presales) Engineers, Sales Management, and Executive leadership – when needed.<br>
Prepare accurate forecast, document daily activities in salesforce, and perform other task necessary to drive sales revenue and communicate activities to sales management.<br>
Work autonomously to meet and exceed assigned revenue targets &amp; goals<br>
Sell deeper and wider into assigned accounts. Identifying requirements from other departments within an assigned account and then configuring an appropriate offering from our portfolio to meet those needs.<br>
Serve as a Trusted Advisor – identifying, penetrating, and securing potential targets through a relationship-based approach.</div>
</div></div></div>
