<p><b>DUTIES
</b></p>
<ul><li>Contact prospective and current customers to solicit sales of company products and services.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Receive customer inquiries and perform follow up procedures to include providing price quotes,
</li></ul>
<p>new product information and receiving feedback regarding company products and services.
</p>
<ul><li>Follow up with sales leads generated through incoming calls, advertising campaigns and other
</li></ul>
<p>promotional efforts.
</p>
<ul><li>Travel to customer locations to present company products, services and pricing information in a
</li></ul>
<p>professional manner.
</p>
<ul><li>Apply technical knowledge of company products and services to best meet customer needs.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain appropriate records for the development of sales forecasts for each customer.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Submit appropriate paperwork to management such as expense reports and call reports in
</li></ul>
<p>accordance with training procedures.
</p>
<ul><li>Understand and support company sales policies and procedures to provide proper and effective
</li></ul>
<p>service to all customers and foster the development of long-term business relationships.
</p>
<ul><li>Assist and resolve customer complaints in a timely manner notify management of problems and
</li></ul>
<p>suggest corrective actions.
</p>
<ul><li>Attend training activities and trade shows on behalf of company to present information
</li></ul>
<p>regarding company products and services.
</p>
<ul><li>Perform other related duties as assigned.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS
</b></p>
<ul><li>Working understanding of the landscape industry and its associated products.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Education and/or experience in industry related sales strategies.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Success in developing strong relationships with vendors and loyalty from customers.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Committed to supporting and developing your market and providing service with a standard of excellence throughout the branch operation.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be P/C literate; proficient in Microsoft Windows, Excel, Word, and Outlook.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Have excellent verbal and written communication, organizational and follow-up skills.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of an ERP system is highly desirable. Agility software preferred, but not required.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>High School diploma or equivalent
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Acceptable driving record
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Able to pass pre-employment drug test
</li></ul>
