<p>
Are you a talented, motivated, and entrepreneurial individual looking to join a growing fashion footwear company? Find your fit with Marc Fisher Footwear as the new Production Coordinator! Marc Fisher Footwear is looking for a dynamic individual to join our team in our Greenwich, CT office. The Production Coordinator will be an individual with a desire and ability to learn new software and skills and use that knowledge to help support one of our many talented teams within our company. Strong Microsoft Excel skills required.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assists in daily management to production process (ex. email correspondence with overseas trading office in regards to all aspects of production, costing, delivery accelerations/delays, etc.)</li>
<li>
Conduct weekly sales trend analysis reporting to Executive Vice President of Sales and Sales Directors</li>
<li>
Responsible for entering Production contracts and Sales orders for FFANY customers</li>
<li>
Serves as direct support to brand manager with all day to day system maintenance</li>
<li>
Responsible for sending projections to agent</li>
<li>
Responsible for entering Cost sheets which capture profit margins</li>
<li>
Review daily email distro to ensure orders are received and processed in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Monitor and analyze shipping lists from factories</li>
<li>
Update production commitments with cost and shipping information</li>
<li>
Manage customer service inquires as it relates to specific brand management</li>
<li>
Additional responsibilities assigned as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The ideal candidate would possess the following requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Excel (pivot tables, v-lookups)</li>
<li>
Exceptional interpersonal communication and organization skills; team-oriented</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Extreme attention to detail</li>
<li>
Willing to take initiative and exercise strong problem solving skills</li>
<li>
Self-motivated with strong follow-up and completion skills</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Blue Cherry ERP Software a plus</li>
</ul>
