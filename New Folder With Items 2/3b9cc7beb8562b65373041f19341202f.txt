<b>Position Responsibilities</b><ul>
<li>
Conduct manual testing of site defects and new enhancements.</li>
<li>
Support holistic testing efforts for multiple projects and software releases, including integration, system, regression, performance and user acceptance testing.</li>
<li>
Develop, execute and maintain test plans, test scenarios, test cases and test scripts.</li>
<li>
Design automation frameworks and scripts and perform automation and manual testing of software defects and enhancements in mutli-tier eCommerce, desktop and mobile website environment.</li>
<li>
Coordinate testing across multiple web environments (eCommerce, mobile, punchout) for the company's U.S., Canada and Mexico websites.</li>
<li>
Review website specification documents provided by the Business Analysis team.</li>
<li>
Work with internal and external development teams and vendors.
</li>
<li>Track and report metrics.</li>
</ul>
