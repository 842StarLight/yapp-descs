<h2>HFC/Access Designer I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Position Details:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
Designs, coordinates plotting and mapping, and determines fiber node placement requirements for hybrid fiber-coax (HFC) and fiber optic networks. Creates accurate network maps, layouts, drawings and detailed electronic construction prints. Develops and maintains network specification files. Performs cost analysis for trenching, sweeping, node splits and upgrade projects.</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Primary Responsibilities and Essential Functions</b></div>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Prepares high-level bill of materials using mapping software.</li>
<li>
Updates As-Builts as construction is completed and releases the area.</li>
<li>
Creates accurate network maps and detailed electronic construction prints.</li>
<li>
Prepares layouts, drawings, and designs according to engineering specifications using applicable design software.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates basic ability and clear understanding of Radio Frequency and Power design.</li>
<li>
Provides ongoing support to other departments as needed in the effective use of all peripheral applications.</li>
<li>
Makes design decisions regarding construction and safety methods according to appropriate Cox, FCC, OSHA, NEC, MESC, VDOT, BOCA, Land Use Manual, and local, state and federal regulations and procedures and may be responsible for creation of design permitting for state agencies.</li>
<li>
May perform quality checks of mapping and network design and negotiate/approve design and/or construction changes.</li>
<li>
May estimate material and labor costs for special projects for fiber and coax up to the patch panel or customer premise.</li>
<li>
May be responsible for fiber availability and ring capacity checks and determination of reinforcement requirements.</li>
<li>
May coordinate project planning and site surveys with sales personnel, utility companies and Cox crews as necessary to include designing service to new and existing residential properties and commercial facilities.</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h4>
<div><b>
Minimum Qualifications</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
HS diploma, GED or relevant work experience</li>
<li>
1 or more years of experience preferred in related field (i.e. Engineering, Design, CAD Drafting, etc.)</li>
<li>
Requires strong knowledge of radio frequency, power design, coax network design, network powering, video fiber design and commercial fiber design.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills, relationship building, self-motivation, resourcefulness, self-starter, time management, organization, attention to detail, quality orientation, and collaborative skills to work effectively with teams throughout the organization.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
AS/AA or Technical Certification in related discipline desired (i.e. Design, Drafting, Electronics, Civil Engineering)</li>
<li>
Experience in HFC or Fiber Design</li>
<li>
Prefer certification in GIS technologies or electrical engineering</li>
<li>
Experience in telecommunications industry desired.</li>
</ul>
