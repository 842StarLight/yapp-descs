<b>The Role:</b> Regional Partner Manager
<br><br>
Vectra is seeking a self-starting, highly motivated channel sales professional to join our Partner Sales organization as a Regional Partner Manager (RPM).
<br><br>
As RPM you will own and manage the relationship between Vectra and the channel landscape, including VARs, SIs, MSSPs and distributors. All aspects of partner recruitment, partner enablement, business planning, sales, sales forecasting, joint marketing activities, etc. will be the responsibility of the RPM.
<br><br>
The RPM is responsible for managing the Vectra Channel partners in region to meet and exceed revenue targets, new customer logos, market share and other channel performance metrics defined in the Vectra partner program.
<br><br>
The Regional Partner Manager will need to be passionate about the extraordinary value Vectra is providing to our customers while also conducting themselves with professionalism, honesty, and integrity. Vectra has an aggressive, but methodical, approach to driving growth, and the successful candidate will need strong experience in channel management as well as in selling to end-customers carrying own quota.
<br><br>
<b>Typical duties &amp; responsibilities (but not limited to):</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Working with sales to identify, recruit &amp; enable new Partners, that match the ideal partner profile for Vectra.</li>
<li>Working with Channel Partners to identify and develop new business wins for Vectra.</li>
<li>Development and execution of a partner account plan in close coordination with the Regional Sales team. This will include strategic direction/goals, business planning, account mappings, lead / pipeline generation, know-how transfer, etc.</li>
<li>Working with the marketing team and the Channel Partners to develop and manage marketing plans to further Vectra business and develop a win/win relationship.</li>
<li>Manage the financial aspects of the Channel Partner relationship including revenue, renewals rate, margin, rebate, etc.</li>
<li>Manage the pipeline together with the partners, assisting the partner with required resources and closing the deals.</li>
<li>Representing the needs of the Channel Partner to Vectra.</li>
<li>Maintain high activity rates with focus partners including Onsite meetings, Partner facing calls, quarterly business reviews, etc.</li>
<li>Establishing a support mechanism to make Vectra the easiest and preferred vendor to work with and actively remove barriers to that gain.</li>
<li>Actively work with the Regional Sales Managers on regional channel strategies to achieve the key goals of Vectra and the Channel Partners.</li>
<li>Presentation and solution selling at the highest levels. Position Vectra solutions and articulate Vectra strategies (value propositions) at events and customer meetings.</li>
<li>Coordinate and manage interaction between Channel partners and other key Vectra teams including Regional Sales Managers, Marketing team, Sales Engineers, Business Operations, etc.</li>
<li>75% travel in region</li>
</ul>
