<h2>phiSAT Account Management Intern (Fayetteville)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>phiSAT Account Management Intern Fayetteville</b></p>
<p><b>
 Position Summary:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assists in sales support for Phigenics sales team.</li>
<li>
Assists in the development, implementation, and execution of internal processes and procedures to enhance and improve the Phigenics Support and Training Center.</li>
<li>
Assists in standardizing all company W.M.P. Water Management Programs and help facilitate appropriate resources necessary to resolve issues timely.</li>
<li>
Assist in managing expectations regarding project scope, deliverables, timelines, status and issues.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with various internal teams <i>Account Managers</i> and external teams <i>clients/customers and potential clients and customers</i> to ensure quality deliverables are completed.</li>
<li>
Accompany Account Managers to client meetings/water sampling events as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
BASIC QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Pursuing a 4-year undergraduate degree in Engineering Chemical, Mechanical, Environmental, Civil, Industrial or equivalent major</li>
<li>
Must have a valid driver's license and acceptable Motor Vehicle Record</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal, verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Excellent presentation and facilitation skills</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and directed</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Proficiency in MS Office Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Google-equivalent software</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent organizational, interpersonal, verbal, and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Willingness and ability to learn water management chemical applications, water safety issues, and associated equipment including automation</li>
<li>
Willingness to learn from a mentor and apply training at customer sites</li>
<li>
Strong mechanical aptitude</li>
<li>
Willingness and ability to work in commercial and manufacturing environments</li>
<li>
Desire to understand water chemistry and microbiology</li>
<li>
Desire to understand building water systems including HVAC systems</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Ability to adapt to a fast paced continually changing business and work environment while managing multiple priorities</li></ul>
<p><b>
Work Environment / Travel</b></p>
<p>
Position requires traveling to client sites in a region, participating in and eventually helping conduct meetings, servicing equipment, and collecting water samples. Some overnight travel may be involved. The wearing of PPE is sometimes required. The position often requires a great deal of walking around client sites, may include climbing stairs or ladders, and may require lifting to 25 pounds.<br>
</p>
