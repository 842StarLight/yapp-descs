<h2>Service Desk/NOC Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Manages a local team of NOC engineers</li>
<li>
Determines time and resources needed to complete assigned goals</li>
<li>
Trains, coaches and develops NOC engineering staff</li>
<li>
Identifies, develops, communicates, and implements process changes within the NOC</li>
<li>
Enforcing quality standards regarding documentation and data</li>
<li>
Improve and maintain exceptional levels of customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Completes special projects and miscellaneous duties as assigned.<br>
<br>
</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Business or Computer Science or equivalent and five years' experience providing IT helpdesk or NOC management; including systems and process improvement; or an equivalent combination of education and experience 5+ years of relevant management experience, sales support, etc.</li>
<li>
Demonstrable history of providing high levels of customer service</li>
<li>
Qualified candidates should have experience in managing a team of technicians and engineers in a customer-facing environment
</li>
<li>Can identify, research, resolve and document complex technical problems
</li>
<li>Familiar with troubleshooting the following: Cisco networks, Windows and MAC devices, virtualized environments, Cisco and Meta collaboration products,
</li>
<li>5+ years' experience with IT Service/Help Desk operations supporting 3000+ endpoints
</li>
<li>ITIL experience and certifications preferred
</li>
<li>Strong communication skills
</li>
<li>Equivalent combination of education, training and experience</li>
</ul>
