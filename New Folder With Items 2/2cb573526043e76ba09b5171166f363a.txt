<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>AGI Publishing, Inc., serving Northern California from Bakersfield to the Oregon border, is seeking account executives that will be responsible for presenting to business owners new digital advertising products and services that offer enhanced internet visibility, along with presenting traditional print and online advertising products. Suite of new products and services includes: Full service online listings management plus “NAP+W” data distribution, as well as account set up services for: Google My Business, Facebook, Yelp and Twitter accounts. Traditional advertising products include directories in print, online, and mobile formats. Our company is passionate about helping local businesses succeed in the communities we serve with effective and cost efficient digital and print advertising solutions.</p>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul><li>Account Managers will be responsible for the day-to-day outreach and activities necessary to identify and sell business-to-business sales opportunities. This includes identifying client needs, conducting sales presentations for decision makers, and creating an advertising plan tailored to the business.</li></ul>

<li>Ability to reach decision makers for face to face presentations</li>
</ul>
