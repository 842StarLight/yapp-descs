Job Responsibilities and Accountabilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Translate customer needs into achievable product performance requirements</li>
<li>Design and implement to the product architecture of trackside equipment (switch machines, switch circuit controllers, relays, etc.)</li>
<li>Ensure technical consistency and alignment with other metiers throughout a development</li>
<li>Provide technical support to Customer Care and Tender teams</li>
<li>Apply Alstom standard processes and contribute to continuous improvement efforts</li>
<li>Design conceptual models of products using CAD software</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>Essential Skills and Experience</b> (Essential Functions)</p>
<p><b>Experience in the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years Experience in the design and development of software modules / applications using C and C++</li>
<li>Software development in a Linux environment</li>
<li>Experience in VHDL and FPGA development</li>
<li>Experience working in an agile development environment</li>
<li>Leading a software development team</li>
<li>Development of software specifications (nominal and degraded modes, function, performance, RAMS, etc.)</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and configuration management</li>
<li>Ability to generate, read, and interpret user requirements</li>
</ul>
