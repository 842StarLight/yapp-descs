<h2>Fulfillment Team Member (eCommerce / Shipping department)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Pistils Nursery is seeking a Fulfillment Team Member to support our growing eCommerce operation. This position is primarily responsible for fulfilling online orders from our complex and ever-changing inventory, which includes specialty and perishable items such as hand-made pottery and live plants.</b></p>
<p><b>
Areas of Responsibility</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meets monthly Growth, Efficiency and Productivity benchmarks</li>
<li>Assists eCommerce team in order processing and fulfillment, prioritizing orders as directed by Lead, and packing shipments following best practices for safe and inexpensive shipments for our complex and ever-changing inventory.</li>
<li>Follows direction of Lead, working from pick lists, staging orders, watering and otherwise readying plant material for shipping and branding packing materials</li>
<li>Adheres to quality control standards, ensuring that all items and plants are clean and in sale-worthy condition prior to shipping. Ensures each box has been appropriately branded, and stamped as “fragile” or “contains live plants” as necessary.</li>
<li>Assists production team in maintaining stock of batched products / kit components as needed</li>
<li>Keeps shipping station organized, tidy and accessible, cleaning as directed after each shift</li>
<li>Works closely with Lead, Customer Experience Coordinator and Brand Director to respond to changes to orders as they arise, troubleshoot shipping issues and alter practices as needed</li>
<li>Makes suggestions to improve shipping practices</li>
<li>Assists in maintaining web shop inventory, alerting Lead and Warehouse Manager when stock levels are low, and pulling product from retail inventories as needed</li>
<li>Works quickly and efficiently to meet fulfillment benchmarks without sacrificing work quality and attention to detail</li>
<li>Appropriately cares for plant material prior to shipping</li>
<li>Cross-trained in shipping; supports eCommerce operations as needed</li>
<li>Assists in special projects as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements and Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-2+ years of experience in eCommerce / fulfillment and shipping</li>
<li>Plant knowledge strongly preferred but not required</li>
<li>Familiarity with eCommerce and shipping software solutions (Shopify and Shipstation)</li>
<li>Excitement to work in a growing business environment with potential for frequent changes and ambiguity</li>
<li>Comfort working both independently and with a small team</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize while multitasking, balancing productivity with high quality of work</li>
<li>Detail and process-oriented. Excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>Comfortable working under pressure to meet deadlines and goals</li>
<li>Comfort being on feet and engaging in physical tasks for majority of the work day</li>
<li>Ability to lift up to 50lb</li>
</ul>
<p><b>How to Apply</b></p>
<p>
Please submit a résumé and cover letter outlining your interest, experience and what you'll bring to the team.</p>
<p><b>
Position Details</b></p>
<p><i>
This is a part-time position starting at 20-26 hours per week with the potential to expand in high volume periods (such as holiday) and as the business grows, based at our warehouse location in North Portland.</i></p>
<p><i>
Benefits the opportunity to opt into Pistils' health insurance and 401k plans.</i></p>
