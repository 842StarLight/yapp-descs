<h2>Account Receivable Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Basic Functions</b>:</p>
<p>This role requires communication with customers, managers and directors across all departments while working directly with the finance team. This individual will also need to manage timely deliverable and prioritize responsibilities using sound judgment to effectively handle and respond to situations with minimal supervision.</p>
<p><b>Role and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proactively analyze delinquent accounts and follow up with collection calls/emails as necessary</li>
<li>Process and apply payments in NetSuite / Oracle</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between the Sales Department and Shipping to ensure invoices are paid before shipping takes place</li>
<li>Coordinate special requests by expediting orders and bill accordingly</li>
<li>Proactively identify/resolve account discrepancies between bookkeeping, shipping and warehouse</li>
<li>Conduct all invoicing functions for groups, contracts, wholesalers, and corporate accounts.</li>
<li>Ensure daily posting of Accounts Receivable payments received and maintain an up to date file for payment batches.</li>
<li>Maintain organized files for all Accounts Receivable.</li>
<li>Conduct weekly follow up collection calls 10-15 days to ensure timely payments received.</li>
<li>Ensure maintenance of all AR documents needed for research requests from guest and in-house users.</li>
<li>Ensure all disputes and requests for clarification is resolved and/or responded to in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Monitor Accounts Receivable aging to ensure collections efforts are timely and minimizing potential risk account.</li>
<li>Maintain open communications with all departments</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills and the ability to work well with both local and international agents and clients.</li>
<li>Strong organizational and time-management skills.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Self-disciplined and hands-on</li>
<li>Fluency in English and Spanish</li>
<li>Portuguese a plus</li>
<li>Must have strong customer service and organizational skills</li>
<li>Computer skills Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Power Point)</li>
<li>Netsuite / Oracle software is a Must</li>
<li>Ability to work under minimal supervision</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years’ experience in AR/collections</li>
<li>Netsuite / Oracle experience desired</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Finance or Accounting is a plus</li>
<li>Formal training and/or certifications are a plus</li>
<li>Undergraduate degree in business or a related discipline is highly preferred, however, relevant work experience will also be considered.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>AR/collections: 3 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>accounts payable: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Accounts Receivable: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's (Preferred)</li></ul>
