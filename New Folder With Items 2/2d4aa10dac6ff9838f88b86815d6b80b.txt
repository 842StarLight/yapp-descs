<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>You will be responsible for driving advertising revenue and partnerships with key clients in an assigned category and selling, negotiating and delivering fully integrated programs that will live across print, digital and video platforms. (This position is based in White Plains.)</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities: </b> Identify, develop and maintain relationships with key advertising decisions makers at agency and client levels throughout your territory. You will identify new programs to engage existing clients as well as drive new business.</p>
<p><b>Other responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and manage short- and long-term account plans for key clients.</li>
<li>Provide accurate and timely business reporting, including revenue estimates, annual projections and share of market analysis.</li>
<li>Partner with editorial and art departments to deliver new advertising opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Skills &amp; Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of experience of consultative integrated (multichannel) sales experience with proven ability to navigate relationships with both clients and agencies.</li>
<li>Ability to manage and grow an existing base of business, while continuing to drive new business.</li>
<li>Strong drive to beat targets, positive attitude and good work ethic.</li>
<li>Social Media knowledge a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
