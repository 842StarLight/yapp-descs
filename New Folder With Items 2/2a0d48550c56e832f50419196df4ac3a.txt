<p><b>Justice and Law Enforcement- Federal Business Development Executive</b></p>
<p>At MicroPact, our Sales team is about more than just closing deals. It’s about making people’s lives easier. Our customers are excited about our streamlined BPM and Case Management strategy. That’s why MicroPact is used in 97% of Federal Agencies and in 98% of all States, more than any other BPM product. It’s also why MicroPact continues to expand our footprint in the Public Sector marketplace in both Federal and State Agencies.</p>
<p>Your job as a Business Development Executive is to continue to expand our footprint into a larger Federal space. Whether it is selling an enterprise-wide solution for an existing customer or prying the door open at an untapped agency, we need talented Business Development executives who can evangelize the MicroPact solution.</p>
<p>At this time we are seeking Justice and Law Enforcement expertise to develop business within Federal Civilian agencies.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute a territory sales plan to meet and exceed the sales target</li>
<li>Facilitate the entire sales cycle including: cold calling, coordinating meetings, providing presentations, software demonstrations, solution selling, and contract negotiations within</li>
<li>Rapidly learn entellitrak and be able to communicate strategically with potential clients in order to identify requirements and propose how our solutions will meet their business need</li>
<li>Represent MicroPact and its solution in prospective client meetings, at conferences and other marketing events</li>
<li>Support the proposal development activities by ensuring a full understanding of client business needs, budget constraints, and competitive positioning</li>
<li>Utilize superior oral and written communication skills to develop new business opportunities</li>
<li>Understand your Federal prospects and develop strategies to increase sales and marketability of entellitrak solutions</li>
<li>Adhere to predetermined metrics; overachieve sales goals</li>
</ul>
