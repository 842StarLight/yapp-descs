<p><b>Job Category:
</b></p>Property Tax
<p></p>
<p><b>Opportunity Awaits at Altus Group!
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Altus Group is currently looking for a new Analyst to join our Hunt Valley office. We are looking for motivated individuals who want to explore the dynamic field of State &amp; Local Tax, consulting to privately held to Fortune 100 companies across many industries. You will have an opportunity to utilize your strong client service, research, and analytical skills in the short term which will prepare you for future work with and in front of clients, representing their interests before state and local tax authorities, economic development authorities and related municipal and state review boards.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with serving the compliance and consulting needs of our clients in the areas of real &amp; personal property tax, sales &amp; use tax, business license tax, unclaimed property reporting, and economic development credits and incentives negotiation
</li>
<li>Organizing, analyzing and summarizing data using Microsoft Office products, Adobe Professional, and other software
</li>
<li>Assisting consultants with development of appeal strategies and evidence submissions
</li>
<li>Conducting research and collecting data from internal databases and third-party sources, and completing technical writing summaries of the data when needed
</li>
<li>Completing work accurately and timely, meeting rigid and, oftentimes, recurring due dates
</li>
<li>Other miscellaneous projects focused on the state &amp; local needs of our clients
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>University or college degree, preferably in Accounting, Finance, Business, Economics, Mathematics, Statistics or equivalent
</li>
<li>This is an entry level position for recent graduates or degreed candidates with 1 to 2 years of experience
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (primarily Excel, Word and Outlook) and Adobe Professional
</li>
<li>Research and quantitative analytical skills
</li>
<li>Excellent written, oral, and interpersonal communication skills
</li>
<li>High degree of accuracy and attention to detail in a fast paced, high volume work environment
</li>
<li>Proactive and organized to meet tight deadlines
</li>
<li>Self-motivated with the ability to contribute both independently and as a team member
</li>
</ul>
