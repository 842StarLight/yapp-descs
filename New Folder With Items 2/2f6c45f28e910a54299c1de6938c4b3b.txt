<b>Company Overview:</b><br>
Brady Corporation is an international manufacturer and marketer of complete solutions that identify and protect people, products and places. Brady's products help customers increase safety, security, productivity and performance and include high-performance labels, signs, safety devices, printing systems and software. Founded in 1914, the Company has a diverse customer base in electronics, telecommunications, manufacturing, electrical, construction, medical, aerospace and a variety of other industries. Brady is headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin and as of July 31, 2018, employed approximately 6,200 people in its worldwide businesses. Brady's fiscal 2018 sales were approximately $1.17 billion. Brady stock trades on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol BRC. More information is available at www.bradycorp.com.
<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b> The Machine Operator will be responsible for operating equipment in the one or more value streams of the Good Hope operations. This position is responsible for the production of valuable, defect-free products that meet or exceed our customers' expectations. The machine operator will be successful in this position by executing to standard work and adhering to all company policies.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
1. Follow Schedule Boards or Hour by Hour boards
<br>2. Run samples and adjust print quality, registration, color settings and mixings to match order specifications.
<br>3. Perform final pre-run inspection of job, quality checks during the run, and job completion, meeting quality and quantity standards.
<br>4. Perform all required preventive maintenance of press.
<br>5. Handle and dispose of hazardous materials and chemicals according to procedures.
<br>6. Work from standard work, written instructions, and established procedures to complete assigned tasks.
<br>7. Address and resolve routine inquiries and problems, referring the more complex issues to the supervisors.
<br>8. Interact with different shifts to provide updates on current jobs, issues, or updates.
<br>9. All other duties as assigned
<br><br>
