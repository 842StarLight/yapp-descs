<h2>Field Service Representative (FSR)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>4M Research, Inc. 4M is a Woman owned small business established in 2007 and headquartered in Huntsville, AL. 4M specializes in Systems and Software Engineering and Analysis, Information Technology, Planning and Analysis, and Field Support Services to a variety of customers including MDA, NASA, and AMCOM. Our continuous and rapid growth is attributable to the caliber of employees 4M strives to hire and retain.<br>
<br>
4M Research is currently seeking experienced Field Support Representative FSR for CONUS defense work at SPAWAR Charleston, SC.<br>
<br>
<b>DUTIES</b><br>
<br>
At the direction of TACOM, oversee the installation, de-installation, basic troubleshooting and fault isolation, and maintenance repairs as needed. Maintain accurate inventory of Government Furnished Equipment GFE and Government Furnished Materials GFM.<br>
<br>

<ul>
<li>
Serving as technical expert performing technical inspections, repairs, maintenance and retrofits to the MaxxPro Family of Vehicles.</li>
<li>
<br>
Provides advanced diagnostics/fault isolation and maintenance repair assistance.</li>
<li>
<br>
Conduct training in and out of a classroom environment.</li>
<li>
<br>
Troubleshooting technical issues and determines appropriate repair.</li>
<li>
<br>
Executes scope of responsibility in accordance with work instructions, specifications, standards and process controls.</li>
<li>
<br>
Completes all process documentation accurately and in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
<br>
Maintaining a safe work environment and ensures compliance with safety objectives and policies.</li>
<li>
<br>
Responsible for maintaining facilities, tools, work area, and shop/office to a safe, organized, and clean state through daily 5S activities.</li>

<br>
Other duties as assigned.<br>
