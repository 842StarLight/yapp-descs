<h2>Sr. National Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Division Information</b><br>
<p>Hardware and Home Improvement (HHI) is a major manufacturer and supplier of residential locksets, residential builders' hardware, faucets, and commercial locksets and doors, with a portfolio of renowned brands, including Kwikset®, Weiser®, Baldwin®, National Hardware®, Stanley®, FANAL®, Pfister™, EZSET®, and Tell. HHI is a leader in its key markets with #1 positions in U.S. residential locksets (Kwikset), Canada residential locksets (Weiser), U.S. luxury locksets (Baldwin), and U.S. builders' hardware (Stanley-National Hardware), #2 in Mexico residential hardware (Fanal), #3 in U.S. retail plumbing (Pfister), and #1 in U.S. retail commercial locksets. Headquartered in Orange County, California, HHI has a global sales force and operates manufacturing and distribution facilities in the U.S., Canada, Mexico and Asia. HHI is a division of Spectrum Brands Holdings, Inc.</p>
<br>
<b>
Job Summary</b><br>
<p>Works with Customer Decision Makers to develop and implement account strategies for All HHI brands. Duties to include execution of promotional plan, management of product mix, development of national promotions, provide key metrics to account decision makers, and present quarterly business review updating strategies and results. Responsibilities also include strategic account planning, forecasting, demand planning, and driving overall customer profitability and sales growth.</p>
<br>
<b>
Primary Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>
<b>(20%)</b> Develops and implements strategic plans for customers to include: top-line sales plan, promotional plans, mix management, new product introductions.</li>
<li>
<b>(20%)</b> Coordinates and manages all projects for HHI Brands in all assigned accounts.</li>
<li>
<b>(20%)</b> Develops and maintains relationships with key merchandising, marketing and sales personal in assigned accounts. Including: sales meetings, new product presentations and trade shows.</li>
<li>Accurately forecasts future product demand and top line sales figures.</li>
<li>Communicates regularly with appropriate team members regarding account updates and plans.</li>
<li>Participates in a cross-functional role for other business elements (Manufacturing, Operations, Marketing, Customer Service, Quality, Finance, Legal).</li>
<li>Handle account inquiries and customer service issues. Develop a plan and structure for communicating and successfully executing promotions, special programs, new products, and other relevant activities to customers.</li>
<li>Advise Marketing and Sales management in development of overall business and marketing strategies as well as advertising plans as appropriate. Recommend opportunities for increased HHI penetration, based on knowledge of customers and their business, utilizing input from all available sources.</li>
<li>Maintain current knowledge of market conditions and trends, through own field research and sharing information with others in sales and marketing</li>
<li>Create, facilitate and manage promotional plans as well as forecast to HHI supply chain teams.</li>
<li>Collaborate with all assigned accounts to ensure that strategies are in-line with customer focus on new products, brand awareness and HHI product knowledge.</li>
</ul>
<p>Negotiates, develops and maintains all customer contracts and partnership agreements</p>
