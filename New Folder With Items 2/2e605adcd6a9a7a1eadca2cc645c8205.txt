<h2>Retail Loan Officer - UBT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Retail Loan Officer is responsible for developing customer relationships through origination of residential real estate and consumer loans in accordance with established lending objectives and goals, policies and procedures.
<br><br>
<b>Key Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
Originate, underwrite, and close residential real estate loans (in-house and secondary market mortgage loans, home equity loans, home equity lines of credit, bridge loans, construction loans), and consumer loans.
<br><br>
Responsible for generating new business through effective, proactive calling efforts on realtors, prospects and existing customers.
<br><br>
Refer customers to other areas of the Bank to enhance relationships and build strong internal partnerships.
<br><br>
Develop and maintain professional relationships with realtors, investors and other third party vendors.
<br><br>
Maintain a strong working knowledge of loan compliance, regulations and policies that pertain to residential real estate and consumer loans.
<br><br>
Assure confidentiality of customer information and adhere to all operational, security, risk and regulatory policies and procedures.
<br><br>
Price first mortgage residential real estate loans within standards to ensure customer satisfaction and bank profitability.
<br><br>
Communicate with secondary market investors regarding sale of mortgage loan packages, and resolve any discrepancies that may prohibit sale to the secondary market.
<br><br>
Maintain a working knowledge of loan processing procedures.
<br><br>
Assist other employees, departments and branches to promote teamwork and excellent communication.
<br><br>
Represent the bank and promote a positive image through active community involvement.
