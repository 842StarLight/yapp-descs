<h2>Marketing Social Media Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for a technically savvy, yet versatile, individual to join our growing real estate team as a marketing coordinator and provide creative ideas to help achieve our goals.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will be a self-starter with exceptional attention to detail. A passion for creativity and for real estate are a must! Responsibilities include overseeing marketing programs, listing launch, online marketing via websites as well as social media platforms. Knowledge of print and digital marketing is preferred. Video experience is a bonus. Your insightful contribution will help develop, expand and maintain our marketing channels.</p>
<b>
Compensation:</b><p>
$16 - $18 hourly</p>
