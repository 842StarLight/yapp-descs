<h2>Master Data Management, Senior Consultant</h2>

<div><b>
The Role</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Master Data Management (MDM) Senior Consultants provide full lifecycle delivery of Veeva commercial solutions to leading Life Sciences companies. MDM Senior Consultants lead the assessment, planning and implementation in the Veeva Commercial Cloud. The main objective of the MDM Consultant is to deliver high-quality solutions to drive Customer Success and serve as a thought leader and trusted adviser to our clients.
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Do</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Responsible for solution architecture and delivery, and oversight of junior project team members</li>
<li>
Lead client facing workshops; provide account leadership and sales support</li>
<li>
Become the subject matter expert on the full-suite of Veeva commercial solutions</li>
<li>
Participate in a global community of peers dedicated to implementation support and development of new best practices and accelerators</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in MIS, Computer Science, Computer Engineering or related degree preferred</li>
<li>
3 + years experience in data management solution delivery</li>
<li>
2+ years in a leadership role at a consulting or professional services firm</li>
<li>
Advanced knowledge of data governance, data quality measurement, data stewardship and/or system integration patterns</li>
<li>
Experience leading teams against deliverables</li>
<li>
