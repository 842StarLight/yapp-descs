<h2>Restaurant Server - House of Blues, San Diego</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p>
HOB Entertainment, a division of Live Nation Entertainment, offers exceptional club venues nationwide. House of Blues Venues provide several genres of live music, from Rock, Hip Hop, Country, Rock en Español, Blues, Jazz, Reggae and Pop. House of Blues venues are also known for their southern-style cuisine, juke-joint inspired atmosphere and the World Famous Gospel Brunch.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
House of Blues opened its doors on Thanksgiving Day in 1992, serving its first meal to the local shelters. Since opening, House of Blues has been committed to giving back to the community through the International House of Blues Foundation.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Entertainment, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Serve food and beverage items in a knowledgeable, friendly and helpful manner to all guests</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Greet all guests at table</li>
<li>
Respond to guest inquiries regarding menu, wine list, Music Hall</li>
<li>
Inform guests of daily specials and make recommendations</li>
<li>
Suggestively sell menu items, beverages, desserts, etc.</li>
<li>
Anticipate and respond to guest needs</li>
<li>
Respond to guest concerns/complaints regarding food or service and correct errors or resolve complaints with a friendly, positive attitude</li>
<li>
Organize and prioritize service per appropriate service sequence</li>
<li>
Take menu, beverage and bar orders from guests and communicate orders to kitchen and/or bar through micros terminal</li>
<li>
Check food orders prior to serving to ensure that they are correct, properly presented, and that special requests have been met</li>
<li>
Deliver food and beverages to table</li>
<li>
Perform opening/running/closing duties according to HOB policy</li>
<li>
Maintain line of sight/atmosphere control by circulating through work area throughout shift</li>
<li>
Have the ability to memorize menus and daily specials</li>
<li>
Pre-bus tables: remove dirty plates, glasses, etc., from table once guest has finished</li>
<li>
Clean spills on tables or around work area</li>
<li>
Maintain HOB safety and sanitation standards</li>
<li>
Prepare guest checks (on micros terminal) and check for accuracy</li>
<li>
Receive payment and process cash, credit card or traveler’s check transactions</li>
<li>
Have knowledge of all music, promotional or special events in the house</li>
<li>
Assist other servers or other employees as needed</li>
<li>
Required to occasionally work Special Events that are scheduled throughout the venue</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Required:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>San Diego Food Handlers Card, Responsible Alcohol Awareness Training Certification or Equivalent</li>
<li>
2-5 years high volume experience in restaurant operations</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of high volume restaurant operations and safe, responsible alcohol service</li>
<li>
Standard food preparation/presentation</li>
<li>
Skill in guest relations</li>
<li>
Wine &amp; Liquor knowledge</li>
<li>
Ability to handle multiple tables at one time efficiently and with an upbeat, friendly attitude; read guests in order to anticipate their needs</li>
<li>
High School Diploma</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
Preferred:
</i></b></p>
<ul><li>Point of Sales knowledge, preferably MICROS</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Physical Demands/Working Environment:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Working environment is fast-paced, often loud and stressful</li>
<li>
Position requires extended periods of prolonged standing and working on your feet</li>
<li>
Must be able to lift or move up to 25 lbs using proper lifting techniques</li>
</ul>
