<p><b><i>Title: Lead Host</i></b></p>
<p><b>
Department:</b> MRT Guest Services</p>
<p><b>
Category:</b> Seasonal FT, PT <b>FLSA Status:</b> Non-Exempt</p>

Salary/Grade Level:</b> DOE <b>Date Approved:</b> 01/12/2016</p>
<p><b>
Reports to (Supervises/Liaison):</b> Guest Services Manager/Supervisor; (Ticket Agents/Hosts)</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>
</div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for performing as an assistant to supervisor, and in a lead capacity by assigning and checking work and providing daily guidance/training to subordinate Hosts, and oversees efforts to prevent the public from becoming nonpaying riders.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
JOB DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Ensure that each customer receives prompt and. courteous service</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>People-oriented and able to provide superior customer service.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Display a positive attitude that is contagious, speaking highly of the company in the presence of customers or other associates.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Functions as the respective host supervisor (), with limited decision-making authority, during operational hours when manager is not on duty; striving to maintain and/or improve guest services.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assists the Guest Services Supervisor in establishing and nurturing a good working relationship between the Tramway and vendors, cruise ship sales staff, in order to secure and increase sales from, and offer quality services and products to, potential tramway guests.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Acts as one of the primary contacts to settle collected vouchers with cruise ship staff to ensure MRT will be paid accurately; accurately collects and exchanges cruise ship vouchers for tramway tickets.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Familiar with theater operations at the upper terminal, Operates projection and sound system for the theater complex.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>As an experienced Host, provides correct tour and local visitor information and assistance to guests at the lower terminal, and/or assistance/guidance to subordinate Hosts, explaining the practices, procedures and techniques of providing excellent customer service to all incoming and outgoing passengers.Assigns tasks; checks quality of work performance; and makes follow-up suggestions for improvement.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Train and supervise subordinate hosts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Performs other incidental and related duties as required and assigned.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
NECESSARY SKILLS AND KNOWLEDGE</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to meet time and attendance requirements.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to work all different shifts including holidays and weekends.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to attend all scheduled meetings, orientations and trainings.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to handle stress under pressure.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent communication and customer service skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to learn, understand, and follow oral and written instructions/directions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Skill in operating personal computer utilizing a variety of computer software.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Skill in establishing and maintaining cooperative working relationships with other employees.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to work directly with the public, providing information and assistance on a daily basis.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability/willingness to learn and pass on to guests those areas of interest to visitors, such as local history, people, culture, geography, etc.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS (education, experience, skills)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>High school diploma or equivalent.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Two (2) seasons of host, customer service or paraprofessional level experience involved with tourism, travel, and/or hospitality services.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to successfully pass a background check.</p>

<br>
<p></p>
<p>
WORKING CONDITIONS</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Aerial tramway and lift operation with the upper terminal located at an 1800 ft. elevation; daily commute up/down lift; possible frequent standing; frequent guest interaction. The Tram is a non-smoking facility with designated outside-smoking areas.</p>
