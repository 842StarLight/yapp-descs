<h2>Marketing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our office in Tinley Park is excited to open up a position for a Marketing Associate to join our growing team! We are ready and eager to bring in a candidate who can provide new ideas and innovation for our company and our clients. With proper training and hands-on mentorship, this individual will have the opportunity to learn and grow within our marketing department, opening up the doors for a long-term career that is ever changing and expanding!</p>
<p>
We are constantly evolving. Our Marketing firm provides solutions for some of the leading tech brands in the industry and we do this through integrity, enthusiasm, and leadership. As a Marketing Assistant, you must be willing to hold yourself to the highest standards in order to protect our client's brands, assist them with national expansions, and understand that our customers are our number one focus. You will work directly with our management staff to deliver top notch support and customer service, ensuring that all client accounts are being handled with precision focus and professionalism. This is a full-time, entry-level position where you will gain insight and knowledge in all facets of our business and have opportunity for growth within our company based on your performance and dedication to the role.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Stay up to date with the latest technology crazes while learning about the newest product innovations that have yet to hit the market.</li>
<li>
Know the ins and outs of each product that is being marketed and be able to guide a customer through the marketing materials that are provided.</li>
<li>
Be able to operate a tablet device in order to input customer information, pull up existing accounts, and be proficient in company's software development tools</li>
<li>
Communicate to Management staff in daily and weekly meetings, providing feedback and new ideas to help grow specific accounts and diffuse any issues.</li>
<li>
Work with assigned teams in the office in order to hit goals, learn new strategies, and generate required results for each account.</li>
<li>
Practice marketing and sales tactics so that services can be communicated properly and efficiently.</li>
</ul>
<p>
The Marketing Associate will be trained from day one on company policies and procedures, proper marketing strategies, required sales skills, professional communication, data entry processes, client accounts, products and services marketed, and all other marketing materials and systems used. The Associate will have an opportunity to develop and grow into a manager role, where he/she will oversee a team, manage specific accounts, and develop further leadership skills.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Desired skills and qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 2 years experience in a related field (customer service, sales, restaurant, leadership, management, retail)</li>
<li>
High school diploma. Bachelor's degree preferred.</li>
<li>
The availability to work full-time</li>
<li>
Reliable transportation to and from the office, as well as being able to commute to client's locations (all local).</li>
<li>
The willingness to learn and grow within the role.</li>
<li>
A background check is required for this position.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Benefits Provided:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Full time schedule / flexible hours</li>
<li>
Salary: $40,000 - $50,000</li>
<li>
Opportunity for advancement in the company</li>
<li>
Guaranteed base pay with added bonuses and commissions</li>
<li>
Company travel opportunities (domestic and international) optional</li>
<li>
Access to mentorship from business partners and Directors</li>
<li>
Fun atmosphere, open door policy, non-discrimination</li>
</ul>
