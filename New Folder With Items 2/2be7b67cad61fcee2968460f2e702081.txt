<h2>Admissions Counselor – ADP</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p><b>Post University is seeking an Admissions Counselor to join our online Accelerated Degree team.
</b></p>
<p><b>Do you want to help others change their lives through education?
</b></p>
<p><b>Are you looking for a challenging and rewarding opportunity to fully utilize your expertise in a growing and ever evolving organization?</b></p>
<p>
As an associate at Post, you will have an opportunity every day to change lives and be a part of building something truly special. We are looking for exceptional talent who understand that their purpose is to create a personal, memorable and delightful experience for our students and each other.</p>
<p>
We seek those that:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>thrive on working things out through collaboration and straight talk,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>who are willing to find and try creative solutions, and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>who take on complex challenges with a ‘we will overcome this’ attitude!</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>If you understand what it takes to be obsessive about service, be part of a team – willing to work toward the team’s priorities, not your own and who can genuinely embrace our culture and our mission…. then we’re interested in you!</p>
<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY</b>:<br>
As an <b>Admissions Counselor for our online Accelerated Degree Program</b>, you will work in our Enrollment Management Center and be responsible for responding to student inquiries regarding admission to the University’s online and evening &amp; weekend programs. This role requires excellent communication, listening, customer service and sales skills. The position includes training and opportunities for advancement.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Promptly respond to student inquiries regarding Post University and our program offerings.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Conduct mostly telephone and sometimes personal interviews to help prospective students identify their interests and choose an Online Degree Program that fits their personal and career goals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Data entry of student enrollment information to ensure full and proper documentation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Follow-up with prospective students/applicants and record results in the database to ensure excellent customer service throughout the enrollment process.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work closely with the Financial Aid, Office of Student Information, and other departments to assist students with their financing and advising needs.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in student registration and development activities designed to promote student awareness, retention and success.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work with academic advising and faculty to identify students who are having academic problems and strive to overcome these issues to promote student success and retention; coordinate discipline issues with student services.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Meet &amp; ideally exceed established team and individual enrollment goals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in weekly team and floor-wide meetings to address sales tactics, best practices and strategies.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Understand all applicable policies, procedures, regulations and laws regarding higher education enrollment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Perform other related duties as assigned.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p><b>
KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Bachelor’s degree is preferred; however, an equivalent combination of education, experience or training can be substituted for the bachelor’s degree.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience in a higher education admissions and/or recruiting position with a consultative sales approach to successfully promote our program is desired.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Prior call center, customer service, marketing, and/or sales experience a plus.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Superior relationship building, teamwork and communication skills including an excellent phone presence and personality.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent organizational and interpersonal skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent PC skills and proficiency in Microsoft Office, Internet applications and database software are a must.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be a self-starter with the ability to multi-task.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be able to work in a team-oriented, objected-focused environment.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Flexibility to adapt to a changing and dynamic environment.</p></li>
</ul>
