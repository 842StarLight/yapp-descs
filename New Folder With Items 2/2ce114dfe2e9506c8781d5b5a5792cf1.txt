<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>Symplicity is currently seeking an Account Executive on the West Coast. The Account Executive is a field sales role that will be responsible for growing business with targeted clients. When note on the road, the AE will work from his or her home office. The AE is responsible for developing a territory plan, sales call strategy, and growing new lines of business. The successful candidate will effectively communicate Symplicity’s value proposition, strategies, services, and products to clients while selling Symplicity’s full portfolio of higher-education products. The AE will work with multiple higher-educational institutions to meet the needs of our clients. For Symplicity, client satisfaction is paramount, and the Ae will need to lead resolution of issues when necessary, reporting directly to the VP of Sales. This is a field sales role and we are open to considering candidates all over the country.
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Responsible for growing business with existing Symplicity clients.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate leadership building and managing teams in support of client opportunities.</li>
<li>
Proactively position a positive business outcome to the clients’ current business problem.</li>
<li>
Drive and execute market and account management activities, qualifying and developing sales opportunities, leveraging specialty resources and partners.</li>
<li>
Drive vertical expertise within the segment and facilitate feedback to the product group and business operations on client requirements.</li>
<li>
Engage strategic partners for resources, fulfillment, and account management.</li>
<li>
Continuously analyze sales forecasts and appropriately modify based on outcomes.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
