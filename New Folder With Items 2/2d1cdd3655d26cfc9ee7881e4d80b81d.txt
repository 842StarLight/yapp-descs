<h2>Director of Tauck Director Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Director of Tauck Director Operations</b></p>
<br>
<p><br>
We are seeking an operational leader who has the vision, experience, and passion to contribute to our incredible culture and deliver on goals set for our internal TD Operations team and our global staff of 375+ Tauck Directors. This Director of Operations will drive performance and operational excellence within our TD Operations team and foster partnerships with internal colleagues to lead and participate in initiatives related to guest experience delivery.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
The Director of TD Operations will be involved in all facets of TDO team and TD management, including overall quality, performance, experience delivery and engagement with the goal of optimizing efficiencies, guest satisfaction and repeat business. With oversight of the TD Managers, this role includes motivating employees, monitoring performance against goals, quality control and analytical reporting. The role is ideal for performance-driven individuals with experience in managing people, process and technology.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<br>
<p><br>
1. Oversee and direct the day to day TDO Organization through operational performance, activities and targets to ensure guest and company standards are being met or exceeded.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
2. Maintains and improves team operations by monitoring team performance; identifying and resolving opportunities; preparing and completing action plans; identifying and initiating process improvement and quality assurance programs.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
3. Oversee the management of and give direction to Tauck Director Managers as it relates to Tauck Director Recruitment, training, development, scheduling, performance management, guest experience delivery, and financial/expense reporting.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
4. Analyzes workflow and monitors performance results of both internal management team and Tauck Directors (375). Prepare global TD performance reports by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing data and trends driving internal and guest experience goals, and as a result, increase service and revenue opportunities or operating efficiencies.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
5. Lead the TD Managers and Tauck Directors to meet the strategic goals and objectives set for staff as it pertains to productivity measures, gift resolution, guest satisfaction, employee engagement and departmental budgets.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
6. Manages the financial budgeting, resource utilization, workflow processes and expense management of the department â€“ includes $15M in payroll and departmental expenses.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
7. Updates and maintains compensation plans developed by VPTDO, HR and CFO.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
8. Directs TDM staff development plans including on-site training, in house training and direct observation.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
9. Partners with VPTDO to plan, lead and facilitate annual Tauck Director Seminar. This includes, in conjunction with Sr. Mgr Recruiting &amp; Training, the development of training modules, workshops and presentations to ensure critical business updates are relayed to worldwide staff.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
10. Implements and maintains annual departmental level planning for company strategic goals</p>
<br>
<p><br>
11. Monitors and reports up to VPTDO departmental productivity, efficiency and quality measurements -highlighting areas needing improvements and action steps to meet improvement targets.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
12. Operational liaison between the Tauck Director Workforce and internal organization including Information Technology, Human Resources, Guest Relations, Sales, Marketing, Finance, Worldwide Operations and General Management teams. Includes identifying opportunities to achieve greater efficiencies and expense/cost management.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
13. Leads operational efforts through cross departmental collaboration to meet overall organization guest satisfaction metrics as it relates to product delivery.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
14. Serves as back up to VPTDO for Team Leader of global Response TD Operations team</p>
<br>
<p><br>
15. Encourage solution management dedicated to providing a positive work environment, fostering teamwork and maintaining high team morale.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
16. Driving change and continuous improvement in a complex environment.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
17. Perform other duties and responsibilities as required or directed by management</p>
