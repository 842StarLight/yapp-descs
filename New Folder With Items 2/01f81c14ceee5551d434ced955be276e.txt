&lt; p&gt;* Uphold brand standards and company policies<p></p>
&lt; p&gt;* Provide consistently high quality product and service to customers<p></p>
&lt; p&gt;* Maximize sales and profit by meeting food and labor goals<p></p>
&lt; p&gt;* Ensure health, safety and cleanliness standards are upheld<p></p>
&lt; p&gt;* Foster productive and professional Team Member relationships<p></p>
&lt; p&gt;* Serve as store mentor and operations expert in all Domino’s store-level Team Member positions<p></p>
&lt; p&gt;* Build relationships with customers and community<p></p>
&lt; p&gt;* Cash handling, reconciliation and reporting<p></p>
<ul>
