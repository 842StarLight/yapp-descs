<p><b>
Job Title:</b> Accountant III</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
We seek an Accounting professional with strong knowledge of U.S. G.A.A.P. and advanced accounting and technical skills to join our Group accounting team as <b>Accountant III</b>, responsible for timely and accurate reporting of the $1,500 Million Laboratory Products Division. The position will be based at our flagship manufacturing, distribution and service business located in Asheville, NC.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepare for and actively participate in internal and external audits</li>
<li>
Completeness and accuracy of monthly account reconciliations, using Blackline software</li>
<li>
Balance Sheet flux analysis, including commentary on material variances</li>
<li>
Monthly Scorecard for trade receivables and aging of intercompany accounts</li>
<li>
Maintenance of local Approval Authority Matrix (AAM)</li>
<li>
Sunshine Act compliance</li>
<li>
SOX Key Spreadsheet testing</li>
<li>
Revenue cut-off testing</li>
<li>
Maintenance and review of the FOB Revenue deferral model</li>
<li>
Assist 3rd party vendor with sales tax filings and sales tax audits</li>
<li>
Coordination of interoffice mail</li>
<li>
Assist Accounts Payable with processing and compliance questions</li>
<li>
Month-end financial data loads using Cognos / HFM / CMR</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Frequent Contacts:</b></p>
<p>
This role will report to the Sr. Manager, Accounting and will work closely with the Asheville site Finance team and the North America Regional Shared Service Center.</p>
<p><b>
Minimum Requirements/Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor Degree in Accounting or Finance</li>
<li>
Must have a minimum of 3 years of Accounting experience</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of U.S. GAAP with demonstrated advanced accounting and technical skills</li>
<li>
Prior public accounting experience preferred</li>
<li>
Prior experience with revenue recognition/deferred revenue accounting desirable</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Other Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to work independently; self-motivated; high bias for action</li>
<li>
High-level Excel skills incl. use of Pivot tables and VLOOKUP functionality</li>
<li>
Possess team-building skills and the proven ability to work with a diverse group of people</li>
<li>
Ability to develop high levels of credibility and forge solid and positive professional relationships</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Adhere to OHS policies and procedures and ensure a safe and healthy workplace environment</li>
</ul>
