<p><b>Who you are: </b></p>
<p>A rising star with expertise of delivering sales in the technology sector looking for an opportunity to work in a fast growing tech services company. You have genuine accountability – e.g you’re not an overlay! You’re on an upward curve in your career but are thirsty to be challenged further. You’ve worked in the technology sector and have a real passion for it. You want to see your work have a large impact on your company and be a key part of its growth. You take great pride in delivering <b>Customer Satisfaction </b>(internally and externally) and <b>Employee Satisfaction </b>in your teams is paramount. <b>Integrity</b> is important to you but the big corporate machines don’t really interest you. You’re deeply <b>Passionate</b> about what you do but <b>Humble</b> enough to keep learning and recognize the importance of collaborating with your colleagues. You’ve got great <b>Discipline</b>, but can still work at <b>Speed</b> and love creating tangible results.</p>
<p><b>What you’d contribute</b>:</p>
<p>We want you to help us grow. Your passion sales will be obvious and your skills will show through your career. Your activities will have a real impact on our business. We’d love you to help us expand on our already substantial growth and expand your own experience.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Working with a team to develop an overall territory account plan and maximize opportunities to generate sales activity with customers, partners and publishers</li>
<li>Identify prospects and new customers in assigned geographic regions</li>
<li>Lead negotiations, coordinate complex decision-making processes and overcome objections in order to maximize opportunities</li>
<li>Manage a virtual team around deals and delivery to achieve/exceed monthly sales targets and net profit quotas</li>
<li>Show insight to our customers and partners regarding software publisher contracts and optimal usage of agreements</li>
<li>Evaluate software contract spend and utilization in a given organization in order to optimize spending patterns, technology usage, and implementation strategies.</li>
<li>Develop strong knowledge of industry trends by developing proactive professional relationships with software publisher representatives</li>
<li>Be the field resource to customers and partners for leading industry volume license offerings from top publishers</li>
<li>Provide leadership and licensing knowledge to customers and maintain a strong presence in the software industry community</li>
<li>Generate opportunities for meetings with key decision making personnel to drive the sales process forward</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you should really know</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Professional sales experience in high-tech or service-related industries, preferably software sales and software licensing experience</li>
<li>Proven solution selling experience with an emphasis on strong account and territory management</li>
<li>High level of knowledge of internal organization working and Information Technology trends</li>
<li>Ability to build relationships and quickly develop trust with C-level executives</li>
<li>Proven track record of consistently exceeding corporate objectives and quotas</li>
<li>The ability to thrive in a fast-paced sales environment</li>
<li>Highly motivated, results-oriented with strong skills in presenting, communicating, organizing, multi-tasking and time management</li>
<li>Solid problem-solving and consultative skills</li>
<li>Proficient with productivity tools like Outlook, Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Higher education preferred</li>
</ul>
