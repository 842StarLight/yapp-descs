The Role
<br>As an Accounts Payable/Accounts Receivable Supervisor on the UT team, you will be responsible for managing the company’s full AP &amp; AR processes and functions. The role will play an important part in UT’s success as we establish improved processes and enhance systems across all accounting functions.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Directly oversee the AP/AR daily activities and assist AP/AR Specialists with any issues</li>
<li>Develops advanced knowledge of processes and establishes self as a subject matter expert</li>
<li>Create and maintain documentation of processes, ensuring consistent &amp; scalable processes are in place with effective internal controls</li>
<li>Perform highly detailed review of vendor invoices and supporting documentation, including purchase orders and contractual agreements</li>
<li>Manage vendor payment process to ensure payments are made on time</li>
<li>Proactively investigate and resolve payment issues and vendor/customer inquiries</li>
<li>Coordinate and review of daily/weekly/monthly customer invoices</li>
<li>Review accounts receivable aging reports and ensure timely collection of funds</li>
<li>Make recommendations to improve quality of invoicing and collection procedures</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with sales organization regarding customer account issues</li>
<li>Perform staff development and provide constructive feedback for growth</li>
<li>Represent the accounting department with a high degree of professionalism</li>
<li>Assist during audits with data collection, worksheet preparation and addressing audit concerns</li>
<li>Actively participate in special projects to improve processes and systems that facilitate User Testing growth</li>
</ul>
<b>What You Bring:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of demonstrated experience working with accounts receivable and accounts payable in a B2B or SaaS company</li>
<li>3+ years of previous management experience</li>
<li>Experience providing reporting for management forecasting &amp; decision making</li>
<li>Mastery of systems; experience with Intacct &amp; Salesforce highly preferred</li>
<li>Superb attention to detail</li>
<li>Resourcefulness and eagerness to learn</li>
<li>Ability to self-manage multiple priorities</li>
<li>Superior communication skills, verbal and written</li>
<li>Ability to positively motivate others and develop team skills</li>
</ul>
