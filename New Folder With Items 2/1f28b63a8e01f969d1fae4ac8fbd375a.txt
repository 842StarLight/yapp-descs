<h2>HRIS Project Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This key position is client-facing and is responsible for the project management of all aspects of onboarding, implementation, and managing the initial stages of the client relationship. The HRIS Project Coordinator works closely with sales, client relations, IT, payroll, human resources, and benefits administration to ensure successful implementations and seamless integrations for all new clients. In addition, the HRIS Project Coordinator manages projects; building project plans, ensuring adherence to project schedules, for existing clients, specifically in support of implementing additional UltiPro functionality across the client organization. An essential responsibility is to develop excellent client relationships in order to meet/exceed client expectations, demonstrate HR knowledge, and deliver superior customer service.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Deliver best-in-class customer service to clients and employees at all times, with an emphasis on being responsive, professional, and accurate.</p></li>
<li><p>
Identify, formalize, and execute best practices in onboarding, implementation and project management in order to meet client HR and payroll needs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage and complete detailed, client specific HRIS project task lists and take ownership to ensure onboards and additional projects are successful.</p></li>
<li><p>
Attend internal implementation meetings to discuss new clients, custom HR solutions, expectations, and any unique challenges that the client may present.</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain regular and open communications to ensure an understanding of client needs as well as requirements in relation to setup and implementation.</p></li>
<li><p>
Gather the information necessary to gain a thorough understanding of client business process and requirements.</p></li>
<li><p>
Define and monitor project scope, escalating significant changes that will affect the overall outcome of the project.</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible for tracking time timely and accurately as it relates to capturing time per the project plan for billing.</p></li>
<li><p>
Obtains client sign off on project, tracks time accurately and timely as it relates to the project plan and submits project hours to be billed.</p></li>
<li><p>
Document client set-up requirements and communicate requirements to HRIS Configuration Analyst.</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop process and best practices for both internal and external clients.</p></li>
<li><p>
Request all necessary client conversion data and employee records.</p></li>
<li><p>
Test client configuration using defined test scripts and scenarios and document expected vs actual results.</p></li>
<li><p>
Track project and client communications.</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop and create customized client and employee forms and other training material.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ensure coordination of all aspects of implementation, including benefits administration, payroll, online services, training, data collection, and migration of existing HR requirements.</p></li>
<li><p>
Enhance and maintain effective communication with key engagement players including clients, insurance carriers and staff.</p></li>
<li><p>
Plan and conduct external client training. Develop administrative and instructional material for clients and employees.</p></li>
<li><p>
Serves as subject matter expert in researching and resolving escalated inquiries submitted to the HRIS mailbox.</p></li>
<li><p>
Drive process improvement and policy development initiatives that impact the HRIS team.</p></li>
<li><p>
Complete other projects and tasks as assigned by the department manager.</p></li>
<li>
<p>
Upsell and provide system demos for current and prospective clients.</p>

Education/Certifications</b></p>

<p>
 <b>Previous Work Experience</b></p>

Three to five years of HRIS or HR generalist or specialist experience. One to two years of project management experience. Systems implementation experience.</p>
<p>
 <b>Other Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<p>
Strong understanding of HR processes and data, including eligibility and enrollment rules, and benefit procedures in order to ensure correct implementation. Strong understanding of HRMS database design, structure, functions and processes. Thorough knowledge of MS Excel, Word and PowerPoint. Experience with an HRMS such as PeopleSoft, Lawson or ADP EV5, UltiPro. Effective organizational and interpersonal skills including written and verbal communication skills. Previous exposure to project-related activities through active participation in system-related projects.</p>
