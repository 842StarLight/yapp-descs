<p><b>Business Analyst, Campaign to Order</b></p>
<p>
Nutanix is building a world class organization and is looking for a top notch Business Analyst for delivering exceptional Campaign to Order experience. This position requires a highly dedicated individual who can deliver results in a high growth, ever-evolving environment. With minimal supervision and working at the highest technical level of most phases of business analysis, this position collaborates with Sales Ops, Marketing, Operations and PM to define &amp; deliver processes and business value.</p>
<p>
At Nutanix we are leveraging industry standard cloud based enterprise applications Salesforce.com, NetSuite and Zuora. All listed tasks and responsibilities below are deemed as essential functions to this position; however, business conditions may require reasonable accommodations for additional tasks and responsibilities.</p>
