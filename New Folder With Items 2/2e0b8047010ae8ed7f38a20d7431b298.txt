<p><b>About Frontier Communications:</b><br>
<br>
Frontier® Communications provides communications services to urban, suburban, and rural communities in 29 states. Frontier offers a variety of services to residential customers over its FiOS® and Vantage™ fiber-optic and copper networks, including video, high-speed internet, advanced voice, and Frontier Secure® digital protection solutions. Frontier Business™ offers communications solutions to small, medium, and enterprise businesses.<br>
<br>
<b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p>The Product Manager will identify and drive the commercial line up of product offerings for the Frontier CPE Division; 50% E911 offerings, 25% Cisco offerings and 25% Mitel offerings.</p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee and own the product lifecycle</li>
<li>Engage sales, sales engineering and marketing to define key functional requirements associated with offerings relative to the needs of our customer base</li>
<li>Review and compare technological assessments with industry innovations and incorporate into all messaging internally as well as externally</li>
<li>Conduct regular market and customer research</li>
<li>Develop and define the competitive landscape to illustrate customer value</li>
<li>Prepare, define and deliver product content for training sales and sales engineers</li>
<li>Clearly communicate product vision and strategies</li>
<li>This position is in an office environment and entails the use of standard office equipment such as computers, telephones, photocopiers, filing cabinets, fax machines and scanners</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as required</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Extensive product knowledge on CPE offers with Cisco, Mitel and the E911 environment</li>
<li>Computer literate</li>
<li>Focus to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Interpersonal and communication skills</li>
<li>Commitment to company values</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>

Physical Demands and Work Environment:
</b></p>
