<b>Job Description</b><br>
Reporting to the Integrated Air and Missile Defense (IAMD) Business Development (BD) Executive, Fort Sill Site Executive will support the planning, coordination and execution of the IAMD BD strategy as it relates to our customer at Fort Sill, Oklahoma. This leader will be responsible for executing concurrent planning for and coordination of a One Raytheon effort for the Fort Sill customer to grow Raytheon and IAMD pipeline. This person will be responsible for Customer Focused Marketing (CFM) efforts and closure on multi-million annual bookings. In addition, the selected leader will participate on capture teams, providing BD support to the Program Area Director (PAD), Capture Leader and pursuit teams.<br>
<br>
In addition to the Fort Sill Field Office responsibilities, this leader will also be accountable for establishing Doctrine Organization Training Materiel Leadership &amp; Education Personnel Facilities Policy (DOTMLPF-P) assessments and solutions to compete in a multi-billion market space that supports of our global air &amp; missile defense customers who use Raytheon as their trusted multi-layered AMD partner.<br>
<br>
This individual willdevelop, coordinate and lead the IDS Strategy that features a One Raytheon Multi-Domain Operations CONOPS in support of Multi-Domain Task Force concepts supporting US Combatant Commands (COCOMS).<br>
<br>
The Fort Sill Site Executive will build collaborative relationships working across the enterprise as required to ensure we communicate integrated solutions for air and missile defense modernization while maximizing the benefit of Raytheon’s investment. This leader will interface directly with the Customer Community and serve as the “Voice of the Customer” internal to the Company management. This individual will be accountable for ensuring Raytheon’s business growth and capture efforts accurately account for the market and geopolitical environments, aligns with the customer concerns and priorities and utilizes Position-To-Win strategies to position Raytheon for bookings with the customers. This individual will be responsible for direct coordination with the all Raytheon Businesses and USBD Field Offices to present “One Company” face to the customer. This leader will be the subject matter expert for the Integrated Defense Systems (IDS) product line(s) to the customer and will serve as the One Raytheon leader for all actions, opportunities and customers stationed at Fort Sill Oklahoma.<br>
