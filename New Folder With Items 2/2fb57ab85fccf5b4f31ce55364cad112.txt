<h2>Financial Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Financial Analyst will assist the VP Finance and the CFO in managing activities related to the Bank’s Treasury and Liquidity Management, Asset/Liability Management, Budgeting and Financial Reporting.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in preparation and delivery of actionable financial reporting and analysis items to support the Treasury and Asset/Liability Management function (Management and Board reporting).</li>
<li>Produce ad hoc financial reports and summary analysis for management. (i.e. download, organize and analyze large volumes of data from source systems).</li>
<li>Assist in managing the daily cash positioning and ensure adequate funding of all bank accounts.</li>
<li>Assist in preparing the Bank’s periodic budget and forecast plans using budgeting software; produce variance reports.</li>
<li>Effectively maintain compliance with the bank’s Finance and Treasury Policies and with bank regulatory guidelines; observe established internal controls and procedures.</li>
<li>Assist in due diligence efforts related to new brokers/vendors/systems.</li>
<li>Assist with settlement and documentation of bond purchase and sale transactions; periodic portfolio and broker review.</li>
<li>Prepare dashboards and performance measurement of the bank’s financial results and treasury activities.</li>
<li>Provide periodic status updates, including metrics on all tasks, issues, risks and key decisions.</li>
</ul>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in accounting, finance, mathematics, or related area.</li>
<li>Minimum 3-5 years of applicable experience in Financial Reporting, Finance or Treasury.</li>
<li>Banking experience performing similar duties is preferred.</li>
<li>Previous experience in treasury, investment management or Asset/Liability Management preferred.</li>
<li>Superior analytical, communication and mathematical skills;</li>
<li>Demonstrate flexibility in re-prioritizing tasks and work effectively in a regulated but not rigid environment; ability to multi-task, manage deadlines, work proactively.</li>
<li>High degree of proficiency in Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, treasury/banking software and other data management tools.</li>
<li>Must have a high degree of interpersonal skills to work with other team members, leadership management and third-party vendors.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
