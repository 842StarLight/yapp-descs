<div><p>The Wealth Management Advisor (WMA) is the investment planning and investment and insurance products lead in the local market. The WMA is responsible for facilitating the delivery of strategies and capabilities including portfolio management, trust administration, investment advisory and insurance. WMAs assist clients and families by gaining a thorough understanding of their unique goals and objectives and constructing appropriate goals based investment portfolios. WMAs advise clients using U.S. Bank's Asset Management Group (AMG) or U.S. Bancorp Investments' open architecture where appropriate, to deliver economic and market views, investment strategy, manager/fund/security research and due diligence, and portfolio construction resulting in tailored investment management and insurance services based upon a client's unique situation.<br>
