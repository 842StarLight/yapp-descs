<div><b>Position Summary:</b></div>
<div>
<p>The Gift Planning Specialist, reporting to the Director of Gift and Estate Planning, serves as a member of the Office of Gift and Estate Planning. The Specialist will be responsible for managing the University’s legacy society, the 1842 Heritage Society. This will entail substantial donor interaction, primarily by phone, but also through events and face-to-face visits locally. This position will also be responsible for assisting with the marketing and donor identification on behalf of the Office of Gift and Estate Planning. The Specialist will bring a balance of relationship building and creativity to this position. This position will have no supervisory responsibilities.</p>
<p>
Villanova is a Catholic university sponsored by the Augustinian order. Diversity and inclusion have been and will continue to be an integral component of Villanova University’s mission. The University is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action employer and seeks candidates who understand, respect and can contribute to the University’s mission and values.</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage a portfolio of donors for the express purpose of donor stewardship, including phone calls and local visits to donors. The donor portfolio may expand to include cultivating and soliciting planned giving prospects.</li>
<li>Develop, implement and/coordinate outreach to planned giving prospects. Proactive outreach can occur by phone, email/mail, or in-person, and will be based on existing data and analytics implemented by the Specialist in partnership with University Advancement colleagues.</li>
<li>Assist with the department’s proactive marketing plan to support existing planned giving donors and to discover new planned giving prospects. This will focus on interviewing donors for the creation of donor profile stories, assisting with the follow-up on annual or semi-annual surveys, and managing the production of brochures and other planned giving literature.</li>
<li>Support planned giving events, including development of invitation/mailing lists, coordination and distribution of invitations, and on-site event support.</li>
<li>Perform additional duties or projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<li>Knowledge of planned giving tools, techniques and giving vehicles</li>
<li>A strong knowledge of and desire to associate with Villanova University’s mission.</li>
<li>Ability to manage up and influence others</li>
