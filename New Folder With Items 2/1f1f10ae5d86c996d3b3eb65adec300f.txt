<p>Job Description</p>
<p><b>Must live in Naples, FL or Fort Myers, FL area.</b></p>
<p><b>OPERATIONS MANAGEMENT CANDIDATE SOUGHT BY REPUTA</b><b>BLE, 107-YEAR-OLD MID-SIZE SERVICES COMPANY</b></p>
<p>We have an extraordinary opportunity for an operations management candidate who has the attitude, drive and aptitude to learn the skills necessary to be successful.</p>
<p><b>Basic Function: </b></p>
<p>The Operations Manager is responsible for planning, directing, and controlling the operations and personnel necessary to provide the contracted services. All services will be performed with an emphasis on safety.</p>
<p><b>Major Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in developing, recommending, and revising a complete program for all operational and maintenance functions within the facility.</li>
<li>Responsibility for directing, controlling, and supervising such services as may be assigned by the contract.</li>
<li>Assist the business development and the general manager in making cost estimates for new work and additional work that is to be done in the customer facility.</li>
<li>Formulate or receive and recommend for approval department policies pertaining to the janitorial operation.</li>
<li>Control the costs of janitorial services and foster economics and cost reductions in all possible operations.</li>
<li>Coordinate approved training programs for all personnel under his/her jurisdiction.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain necessary standards of conduct and performance to ensure that his/her department’s operations and services are carried out efficiently.</li>
<li>Oversee the implementation of the quality control and safety programs.</li>
</ul>
<p>We offer the opportunity to join a team of highly-motivated professionals who share a commitment to excellence. Of course, you will receive a competitive salary and benefits package.</p>

<li>Bilingual (Read, write and converse fluently in English and Spanish)</li>
<li>Have management &amp; supervisory experience.</li>

<li>Training</li>
