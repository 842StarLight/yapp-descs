<h2>Vice President, IT Business Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Vice President of IT Business Solutions is responsible for building strong business relationships to drive prioritized IT portfolio comprised of projects and programs. The Vice President will lead a team of IT product owners, data, and the digital teams for enabling technology solutions for sales, marketing, customer service, medical management, claims operations, provider network management, and other administrative departments.<br>
<br>
The Vice President will drive consistency in defining business needs, as-is and to-be process definitions, requirements, analysis, solution design, delivery, and quality assurance functions. The leader will support technology and data strategy, implementation and operations, enabling cross-functional synergies across technology groups, managing outsourcing relationships, and coordinating with line-of-business leaders to understand needs and coordinate anticipated technology and product changes.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Drives IT product design, data needs, and quality criteria of all technology needs of the business – and holds direct reports accountable for supporting these efforts.</li>
<li>
Develops a prioritized portfolio and manages the portfolio to a delivery schedule, with input from the business and direct reports, and in coordination with the product engineering team.</li>
<li>
Coordinates with business unit heads and service managers/owners to develop and implement an IT portfolio strategy based on strategic business objectives. Holds direct reports accountable for implementing the strategy.</li>
<li>
Works with IT technology leaders to build anticipated technology needs while managing an active technology portfolio of prioritized business needs.</li>
<li>
Determines the further scope of integration of various cross-functional initiatives and brokers consensus among executive leaders to implement it</li>
<li>
Advises business units and line-of-business leaders on exploiting new technologies</li>
<li>
Works with a matrix of peers to drive technology implementation that includes infrastructure, security, and product engineering.</li>
<li>
Proactively manage and optimize IT budgets to drive efficiency and quality.</li>
<li>
Oversees the creation of a framework for evaluating technologies, making sourcing decisions, and creating scalable vendor management processes</li>
<li>
Oversees the demands of the business to meet strategic planning, talent management, IT budget management, performance management, communication, and functional coordination goals</li>
<li>
Oversees the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of enterprise data while improving on innovations, digital transformation and growth.</li>
<li>
Oversees the development of appropriate governance frameworks and mechanisms for the effective use of information and technology to create value and manage risk</li>

<br>
<b>Secondary Responsibilities:</b><br>
Reporting to the CIO, the VP is a member of the IT leadership team. The VP partners with various business units to develop and implement all technology solutions and identify strategies to optimize and grow various lines of business.<br>
The VP will be responsible for the IT products and solution design and quality assurance (product owners), digital transformation, and data management teams led by department directors. The VP is responsible for building strong relationships with line of business leaders to understand the needs and coordinate anticipated technology and product changes. The leader is accountable for optimizing performance by identifying new opportunities in process improvements, individually and through the leader’s direct reports. As with all senior leaders, from time to time, the VP is required to represent the company, and must have broad knowledge of all aspects of the business.<br>
<br>
The IT Business Solutions team will be accountable for product ownership, specifications, and quality. The IT business solutions team will work with a matrix of technology implementation teams to drive highest quality of technology solutions.<br>
<br>
