<h2>Marketing Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Additional Work Location(s) :</b></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>The qualified candidate will work directly with the Director of Global Marketing and support project management of product launches, marketing projects, invoicing and expenses, project budgets and other related tasks. This person will be the organizational talent that helps keep projects on track for Harman Professional Solutions marketing. They will be a skilled communicator who is an overachiever at meeting deadlines, proposing improved processes and finding inventive ways to keep all parties communicating about tasks.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Supervisory Responsibilities:</b> This position does not have any direct or indirect reports.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in the planning and project management of product launches.</li>
<li>
Work closely with all related departments to understand the business purpose of each project and the associated necessary deadlines.</li>
<li>
Support each project stakeholder and assist in gathering resources.</li>
<li>
Raise issues and conflicts as they arise; working closely with Director of Global Marketing to mitigate deadline related conflicts and resolve issues as needed.</li>
<li>
Assist in project post mortem process so findings can be documented and applied for future projects.</li>
<li>
 Assist in ordering product for projects, tracking invoices and budgets, aiding in expense reports and managing monthly reporting.</li>
<li>
Other duties, as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Basic Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul><li>
PMP Certification or a Bachelor’s degree or higher in Business Administration, Operations or Communications or related-field.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of applicable project management experience, must have experience in product launches or go-to-market planning.</li>
<li>
Must be self-motivated and able to work as part of a remote team.</li>
<li>
Experience working with global sales, marketing or product teams and influencing global stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Must be passionate about the small details while understanding and focusing on the bigger picture strategic goals.</li>
<li>
Self-motivated with a strong affinity for strategic problem solving and project ownership.</li>
<li>
Must have a demonstrated ability to juggle competing priorities and projects.</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal, oral, and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to translate information and feedback, develop insights, and work collaboratively alongside internal and external sales, product management, technical services, regional marketing and curriculum teams.</li>
<li>
Ability to take constructive criticism and channel it to build professional quality projects based on team and client input.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience with project management software, bonus points if there is direct experience with Workfront.</li>
<li>
Strong skillset using Microsoft Office Suite (PowerPoint, Excel, Word, Outlook); strong computer proficiency, and web conferencing (ex. GoToMeeting, Skype, Zoom.us, etc.).</li>
</ul>

</ul>
