<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary</b></h2>
<p>The recruiter plays a critical role in fueling Centre Technologies’ growth by hiring and retaining top talent. This role requires the use of recruiting expertise to identify and attract qualified and diverse candidates for entry level IT Services and Sales openings within Centre Technologies.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Learn the requirements for various roles in a fast paced, growing IT company</li>
<li>Coordinate recruiting events and job fairs, including advertising for positions, scheduling candidates and leading events</li>
<li>Working directly with hiring managers and candidates to bring on new employees to the Centre Technologies family</li>
<li>Providing follow-up correspondence to candidates on recruiting status via phone and email tracking recruiting activities and provide weekly activity reporting</li>
<li>Keep in regular contact with qualified candidates and maintain lists of available talent</li>
<li>Identifying opportunities for improving the candidate experience and interview process efficiency</li>
<li>Proactively going out to search for qualified candidates in person and on career and college websites, such as LinkedIn</li>
<li>Put your personal touch on various job postings, see them in action, and track performance</li>
<li>Update and maintain candidate data in our Applicant Tracking System (ATS)</li>
<li>Filter through resumes for qualified candidates in the ATS</li>
<li>Update internal job descriptions when necessary</li>
</ul>

</ul>
