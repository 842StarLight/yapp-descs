<b>Position Overview:</b>
</div>
<div>The VP, SaaS &amp; Infrastructure Operations is responsible for design, engineering, and management of the Entrust Datacard’s Global infrastructure and operational platforms for our commercial portfolio of solutions. This includes the traditional infrastructure and operational platforms such as data centers, networks, as well as new infrastructure services such as cloud services models. In this role, you will work with Business leaders, Enterprise IT and Technical Operations leaders, R&amp;D, and Sales organization to deliver global commercial IT infrastructure to host our growing portfolio of cloud solutions. Our organization is focused on accelerating value creation in global markets through innovative cloud and network strategies that align to better outcomes and lower costs for our global customers. If you thrive in a high performance, high accountability environment, this is truly a place to make an impact. This role reports directly to our Chief Information Officer.</div>
