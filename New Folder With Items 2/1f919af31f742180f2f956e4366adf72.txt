<h2>Software Development Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Cosairus LLC is a premier software development company specializing in building software solutions that help our clients reach business process goals. Cosairus develops custom software applications for clients in industries such as healthcare, finance and manufacturing. We currently have an opening for a Business Analyst (BA) to work on requirements gathering, estimation, and documentation of acceptance criteria. Candidates must be creative, highly motivated, responsible, and have a passion for solving complex, technical problems.</p>
<p>Job Summary</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will work with project managers, software development managers, software developers, QA, and external clients. The BA will act as the stakeholder advocate to ensure that project acceptance criteria and requirements are clearly defined.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>

<li>Create system design proposals</li>
<li>Creating functional specification documents</li>
<li>Creating software acceptance criteria</li>
<li>Assist project manager with development of completion estimates</li>
<li>Interfacing with Project Managers, and Software development teams</li>
<li>Interfacing with QA</li>
<li>Interfacing with client teams</li>
<li>Lead design sessions in prototyping new system</li>
<li>Ability quickly learn and understand business processes in multiple industries</li>
<li>Coordinate with other business analysts</li>
</ul>
