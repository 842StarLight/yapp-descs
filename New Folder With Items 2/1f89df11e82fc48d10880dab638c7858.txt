<b>General Description</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<div>The Senior Platform Design Engineer is responsible for designing and evolving supporting systems and tools that make up of the underpinnings of the Flexential Cloud Platform product offerings. These systems and tools range from capacity planning tools, OS provisioning, security &amp; configuration management and storage infrastructure to name a few. A successful candidate is curious and passionate, enjoys solving complex problems, values being a member of a high performing team but also can work autonomously, continuously evolves their skills and thinking, and has a thirst for building innovative technology solutions that people enjoy using. As a Senior Platform Design Engineer, you will be expected to successful teach and mentor your team members on new technologies and skills.</div>
