<h2>Technical Support Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Bored at your current job?
</b></h1>
<p><br>
Valmet is seeking great people to mentor into the next generation of paper industry professionals. Help us remain champions to our customers while being good neighbors and stewards of the earth reducing our impact to the environment.
<br>
<br>
The <i>Technical Account Specialist </i>will serve as the customer contact for assigned accounts, facilitating all inside sales functions. Primarily providing quotations and order acknowledgements for spare parts and consumable products, and general customer support.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
Receive customer inquiries for spare parts and consumables through direct communication or through the Product Sales Managers.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify specific parts utilizing Valmet documentation systems.
</li>
<li>Provide technical applications and customer quotations and order acknowledgements for spare parts and consumables.
</li>
<li>Provide technical support for spare part coordinators, customers, and other sales personnel.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements
</b></h2>
<p>Associate's Degree from a two year college in business or engineering or related field; 3 to 5 years customer service experience or experience in a technical capacity, preferably in the pulp and paper industry; or equivalent combination of education and experience.<br>
</p>
