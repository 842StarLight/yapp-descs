<div>This position provides graphic design and development support for the Sales Training Department and Partners. This role assists in ideating on concepts, user experience design, visual solutions, and developing visually compelling graphic design, animation, interactive digital components, online and web based learning content for our US Sales Organization performance team.

<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Principal Duties and Responsibilities</b></h5>
Assist instructional design team in visually displaying concepts through the use of storyboards or mood boards.
<br>
Produce online and offline content in various formats (print and digital) that meet the needs of the learners, instructors, and trainers.
<br>
Edit videos, audio, and other forms of online modules built in third party software or web based technologies.
<br>
Create and integrate interactive multisensory elements into classroom and course material.
<br>
Assist, support, and mentor commercial and product engine teams both at corporate and in a territory to ensure learning best practices are instituted and followed.
<br>
Monitor the brand assets and department visual identity.
