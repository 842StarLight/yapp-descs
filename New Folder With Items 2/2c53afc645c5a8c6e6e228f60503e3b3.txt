<p><b>Manager, Integrated Delivery Network (IDN) and Account Marketing
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Are you looking for a patient-focused company that will inspire you and support your career? If so, be empowered to take charge of your future at Takeda. Join us as a Manager, Integrated Delivery Network (IDN) and Account Marketing. This position is located in Cambridge, MA.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Here, everyone matters, and you will be a vital contributor to our inspiring, bold mission. As a Manager, IDN and Account Marketing working on the US Hematology team, you will be empowered to drive the portfolio through management of branded and unbranded initiatives in support of demonstrating payer coverage, health economic value, and comparative clinical efficacy, in support driving formulary access and product selection, and a typical day will include:</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
POSITION OBJECTIVES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The Manager, IDN and Account Marketing will work cross-functionally across the Brand marketing team, US Managed Markets, Reimbursement, Medical Affairs, and Field Sales for development and execution of programs and collateral development.</li>
<li>
The Manager will lead CMLR initiatives (Materials, Re-Approvals, &amp; Updates to Existing Resources)</li>
<li>
The Manager shares responsibility for Advertising &amp; Promotions (A&amp;P) expense targets and is responsible for managing the budget for his/her areas of accountability, including budget phasing, tracking and ongoing oversight.</li>
<li>
A successful candidate will assist in market research for respective initiatives, analysis of data from research for strategic decision making, and insights in support of advancing the Hematology portfolio within specialized hemophilia treatment centers, health systems and integrated delivery networks (IDNs), specialty pharmacies and distribution partners, and health care providers.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
POSITION ACCOUNTABILITIES:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Builds a Collaborative Culture:</p>
<p>
Builds and models a culture of questioning and constructive challenge and collaboration to foster openness and candid dialogue among cross functional partners. Listens to and carefully considers other’s perspectives, especially to manage and resolve conflict.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Leads with Strategic Agility:</p>
<p>
Develops bold strategic plans and crafts pragmatic solutions to implement them. Moves easily between addressing current needs and planning with informed insight. Considers marketplace and economic forces and trends and how it affects our business.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Navigates Change:</p>
<p>
Clearly communicates the need for change. Decides and acts, even when faced with uncertainty. Quickly focuses on the critical issues while prioritizing current work.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Develops Organizational Capability:</p>
<p>
Builds and improves effective internal systems and processes that align with organizational priorities, while inspires passion or</p>
<p>
growth and change in Takeda’s people. Builds and fosters effective teams in a global matrix organization. Considers and addresses business and development needs both locally and globally.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
EDUCATION, BEHAVIORAL COMPETENCIES AND SKILLS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required</li>
<li>
Generally, has 4-6 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry</li>
<li>
2+ years of experience in marketing required</li>
<li>
Field sales experience preferred</li>
<li>
Experience in marketing to strategic customers preferred (e.g., hospitals, specialty or retail pharmacies, wholesalers)</li>
<li>
Specialty biologics experience and/or payer marketing experience highly preferred</li>
<li>
Experience in development and execution of promotional collateral with the ability to articulate the value of the promotional materials to the sales organization</li>
<li>
Ability to leverage internal and external “voice of the customer” to anticipate and identify product and/or program needs</li>
<li>
Experience in payer/IDN messaging and aligning a communication strategy with branded and unbranded messaging</li>
<li>
Ability to apply Competitive Intelligence learnings into Marketing Plans, develop and execute tactics to address competitive threats</li>
<li>
Strong cross-functional collaboration skills and proven ability to advance and drive tactics in a matrix organization</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills, including proficiency with Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>
Proven ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>
Analytical skills, comfort with data</li>
<li>
Demonstrated strong leadership, interpersonal skills and excellent communication across functions, organizational levels and cultures to help deliver business results</li>
<li>
Strong project management skills with demonstrated ability to plan, prioritize, and execute</li>
<li>
Flexible and team-work oriented</li>
<li>
Strong familiarity with regulatory A&amp;P approval processes</li>
</ul>
