<div><b>Alliance Technical Program Lead – Google Cloud Platform</b></div>
<p>
Deloitte is an industry leader in our clients’ Digital transformations. We recognize that the backbone enabling our clients’ highest aspirations for analytics and cognitive solutions is next-gen Cloud infrastructure. From cloud consulting, to implementation, and ongoing managed services we offer a full scope of Cloud services and solutions. We want talent who is ready to innovate cloud services and empower our clients to leap frog forward in their technical capabilities. Building your career at Deloitte offers the opportunity to work with cutting-edge Cloud technologies to deliver next-Gen Cloud solutions. We embrace the tech pioneer mindset and always enable our talent to keep their skills evolving at the speed at which the technology marketplace is evolving.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<div>Cloud has transformed the way technology enables the business. It represents a radical shift in how IT services are obtained, used, and managed. Companies are navigating through the various cloud options and deciding between private, public, or a combination across different and dynamic provider ecosystems. Our employees are empowered to think outside of the box and provide innovative solutions to our customers.</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<div>The alliance technical program lead is responsible for establishing and maintaining
relationships with the GCP technical organization as well as Deloitte’s
leadership teams in an effort to identify go-to-market (GTM) opportunities that
drive value for our clients, Google, and Deloitte. The alliance technical program lead’s role in
delivering this value mainly consists of aligning Deloitte and Google offerings
to serve our joint clients.</div>
<br>
</div>

Work You’ll Do</b></p>
<div>
<div>
<b><i>Alliances </i></b><b><i>Technical Architect Responsibilities</i></b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
The alliance technical program lead is responsible for establishing and maintaining
</div>
<div>
relationships globally with the GCP technical organization, Google
counterparts, Deloitte’s leadership teams, country alliance leaders and
industry leaders and will evangelize Google Cloud Platform initiatives that
create value for our clients, Google, and Deloitte.</div>
<br>
<div>
The alliance technical program lead’s role in delivering this value mainly consists
</div>
<div>
of coordinating with the broad number of business and geographies across
Deloitte to insure a thorough understanding of Deloitte’s Google Cloud
offerings to drive the Google Cloud Alliance business goals.</div>
<br>
<div>
The Alliances Technical Program Lead will work with internal teams to expand their knowledge of GCP differentiators and value
</div>
<div>
propositions, GCP Solutions available and under development, acting as a front
line GCP Solutions and Offerings SME to the various lines of business.</div>
<br>
<div>
The Alliances Technical Program Lead requires an understanding of the underlying factors that drive customers to cloud,
</div>
<div>
current cloud trends around big data and machine learning, IT strategy and
planning skills, and develop long lasting relationships.</div>
<br>
<div>
The Alliances Technical Program Lead will champion GCP training and certification globally across Deloitte
</div>
<div>
working with Google and Deloitte training leaders insuring GCP training and
certification curriculums are understood, availability of training and access
to it.</div>
<br>
<div>
The Alliances Technical Program Lead also works closely with the Google Cloud Business Managers and/or Regional Alliance
</div>
<div>
Managers to optimize account planning efforts, is focused on supporting
business development, while also having strong operational ties to support,
standards, strategy, and product management, but is not a delivery or hands on
technical position.</div>
