<h2>Drafter</h2>

<p><b>Refrigeration Vessels &amp; Systems (RVS) located in Bryan, Texas</b> is searching for the ideal candidate to fill the position of <b>Drafter.</b></p>
<p><b>RVS</b>, a wholly-owned subsidiary of <b>EVAPCO, Inc.</b>, is an industry leading manufacturer of ASME pressure vessels and package systems used in the Industrial Refrigeration [Food &amp; Beverage Processing and Cold Storage Warehouse] and Oil &amp; Gas industries. RVS package systems come equipped with the latest in microprocessor technology to provide a total control solution for the refrigerant pump and liquid level management.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>Utilize SOLIDWORKS modeling software to develop product drawings, and complete bill of materials for ASME Pressure Vessels and Industrial Refrigeration Packaged Piping Systems, while adhering to product standards, customer specifications, ASME specifications, and project deadlines.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<p><b>Required:</b> 1-2 years academic and/or work experience using SOLIDWORKS and/or holds a SOLIDWORKS Certification.
</p>
<p>Basic knowledge of ANSI Standard 14.5</p>
<p>Must to be able to follow instructions and standard procedures</p>
<p>Ability and desire to learn new technologies, methodologies and industry standards</p>
<p>Disciplined, self-starter who works well both within a team, and independently with minimal supervision</p>
<p>Ability to multitask and manage time, while adhering to defined scope of work and published deadlines</p>
<p>Must be both personable and professional</p>
<p>Demonstrate excellent verbal and written communication skills</p>
<p>Ability to exercise adaptability when new situations arise</p>
<p>Detail oriented with excellent organizational skills</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<p>Make detailed drawings from projects specifications, sketches, ASME calculations and order write-up</p>
<p>Create bill of materials (BOM's) from models.</p>
<p>Updates project status in order management system
</p>
<p>Interacts with Sales Representative or Customer regarding design reviews
