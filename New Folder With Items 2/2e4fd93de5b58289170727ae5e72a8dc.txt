JOB DESCRIPTION
<br><br>
GYMGUYZ is seeking a highly skilled, energetic and experienced certified personal trainer to join our rapidly expanding team of fitness professionals. The certified Personal Trainer (fitness coach) will be responsible for ensuring the highest level of client service while providing personalized fitness instruction to each client. He or she must be able to adapt to various workplaces that include private homes, offices, parks or any setting of the client's choice. The Personal Trainer role at GYMGUYZ is an excellent career opportunity for those with past experience in personal training, fitness coaching, or instruction in other fitness areas such as yoga and pilates. Experience in group instruction is a plus.
