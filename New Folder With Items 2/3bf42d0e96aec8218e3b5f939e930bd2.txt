We are currently hiring a Microsoft Practice Development Advisor, a Cloud Channel Account Specialist II, Microsoft</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Build and maintain relationships within an assigned account base of existing customers</li>
<li>
Responsible for facilitating the introduction of Microsoft and Ingram products, programs and services technology capabilities, providing educational resources, and driving the sale of technical products, programs and/or services.</li>
<li>
Responsible for growing profitable sales, activations, and market share across the distribution channel by helping your assigned account base from a traditional computer reseller to a profitable Managed Service Provider (MSP) with steady annuity stream</li>
<li>
Deeply research into partner’s SaaS business to ascertain their level of expertise and then help them transform using a specific approach that emphasizes cross-selling, marketing programs, training and promotions that reward the partner for growth.</li>
<li>
Maintain a detailed understanding of Microsoft Office 365 product line and its associated key competitive products including life cycles, pricing programs, and technology advantages</li>
<li>
Proactively identify sales opportunities and close sales within their assigned base of Microsoft partners.</li>
</ul>
<p>
 May travel up to 10% (which may include international travel)</p>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of five years previous account management experience or four years’ experience with Ingram Micro in a sales/customer service capacity equivalent to a Channel Account Specialist</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree and a minimum of two years previous sales/account management experience (preferably in a related industry) or one year experience with Ingram Micro in a sales/account management capacity equivalent to a Channel Account Specialist.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of professional, effective telephone techniques, sales and account management skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to dig deeper into an organization and take advantage of cross and upsell opportunities.</li>
<li>
Ability to develop deep technical and product knowledge.</li>
<li>
Ability to expand that technical knowledge to become a Microsoft subject matter expert.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work as a team member</li>
<li>
Must respond to rapid change, perform duties with accuracy and with a strong degree of urgency</li>
<li>
Prioritize and perform work with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Able to handle large quantities of information while maintaining a high level of accuracy</li>
</ul>
