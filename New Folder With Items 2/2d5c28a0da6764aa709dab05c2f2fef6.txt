<h2>Operations Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
At Sentry, we recognize how important our support associates are to the overall success of our company. If you’re looking for a department and company to grow with, then you’ve come to the right place. We are looking for a detail oriented and driven individual to provide clerical and administrative support to our Commercial Lines team.</p>
<p>
We want you to be successful at supporting quotes, processing data, and issuing new or renewal business, while servicing existing accounts. If it sounds like we could be a strong partnership, then keep reading below!</p>
<b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><p>
You’ll play a key role in the success of many areas of our business. From underwriting to sales, your interactions with internal and external customers will be crucial for the success of our team. More specifically, here are a few examples of what you’ll do:</p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate and provide operations support effectively with internal and external contacts which may include underwriting, account managers, customers, agents, or agency staff</li>
<li>Work with account managers to review and complete applicants, while ensuring the application is fully finalized for analysis and proper underwriting</li>
<li>Communicate directly with the customers to gather information and enter quotes into our systems on behalf of the account managers</li>
<li>Use problem-solving skills and best judgment to work with internal business units to resolve problems and provide insight about service</li>
<li>Learn our billing and premium audit processes while staying in line with Sentry’s underwriting guidelines</li>
<li>Handle customer calls, emails, and faxes regarding service needs while staying current on procedures and policies</li>
</ul>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><ul>
<li>Associate degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>1-3 years of related work experience</li>
<li>Previous or comparable commercial lines experience with another company preferred</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills to handle day-to-day telephone calls received from independent agents, sales personnel, and customers</li>
<li>Knowledge of software applications, systems, processes and procedures used by the business unit</li>
<li>Knowledge of Sentry Insurance, its business operations, and products are a plus</li>
</ul>
