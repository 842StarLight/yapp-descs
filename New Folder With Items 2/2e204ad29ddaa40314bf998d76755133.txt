 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Summary of Job's Purpose:</b></div>
<div>
The Regional Director, Campaign Management is a member of the campaign management team, which provides services to our clients and ad sales through campaign management, delivery management, performance optimizations, creative troubleshooting, and programmatic channel management. The Associate Director, Campaign Management role is to provide experienced oversight, guidance and action to every project assigned. A strong candidate for this position will have the knowledge and experience to connect different advertising technology platforms, be creative and to think outside the box. As someone who will review ad serving logic for programmatic, and mobile web, you will be leaned on as an internal expert to work across the company along with external clients when necessary.</div>
