<h2>Enterprise Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>WHY WE'RE LOOKING</b></p>
<p>
As an Enterprise Account Executive, you will have the opportunity to help drive and shape the future of an emerging solution. Your responsibilities will include driving revenue, adoption, and market penetration into enterprise level Dental Service Organizations (DSO) accounts. The ideal candidate will possess both a sales and technical background that enables them to drive and build deep engagement at the C level. They should be a self-starter who is prepared to develop and execute against a rich DSO market and consistently deliver on revenue targets.</p>
<p>
This position will report to the Vice President of Sales and will require up to 25% national travel. Office location when not travelling may be in Bannockburn or Chicago.</p>
<p><b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as a high level consultative technology account executive</li>
<li>
Proactively build deep and strategic relationships at high levels within enterprise accounts</li>
<li>
Assure the execution of strategic account plans, including development and implementation of qualitative and quantitative metrics for achieving revenue growth</li>
<li>
Lead client prospecting, presentations, negotiations, contract development and account reviews with key stakeholders to ensure standards are being met; stay abreast of contract expirations dates and remain actively involved with renewals</li>
<li>
Utilize Salesforce to document sales activities (i.e., sales calls, customer events, opportunities, e-marketing campaigns) and share account intelligence with internal team members</li>
<li>
Act as a liaison between prospect/customer and RevenueWell teams as necessary</li>
<li>
Present sales opportunities to management and work with the internal departments/team members to close large complex sales</li>
<li>
Lead the process for preparing Requests for Information (RFIs) and Requests for Proposals (RFPs)</li>
<li>
Keep well-informed of industry trends, technological and process changes, new developments, services and other general information related to the Dental market</li>
<li>
Recommend strategies to continuously strengthen competitive position in markets</li>
<li>
Provide required status reporting and updates to management</li>
<li>
Demonstrate a high level of personal accountability</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree plus minimum of 5 years progressively-responsible sales experience with a focus on consultative selling to healthcare companies.</li>
<li>
Deep understanding of the dental industry and marketplace</li>
<li>
Tech savvy with experience in analytics including data warehousing, business intelligence (BI) &amp; reporting, custom relationship management (CRM) and marketing automation</li>
<li>
Proven track record of success; ability to meet and or exceed assigned quotas on a consistent basis</li>
<li>
Skilled in negotiations and sales techniques</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success accessing client executives, making solid executive presentations, and developing proposals</li>
<li>
Ability to confidently interact with senior management and executives both internally and externally</li>
<li>
Exhibit solid communication skills; ability to present accurate and compelling presentations</li>
<li>
Well-developed sense for our business; must be able to manage complexity and have the appropriate financial acumen to discuss ROI</li>
<li>
Highly organized with strong project management skills, follow-through and attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to work across many cross-functional teams for a singular goal</li>
</ul>
