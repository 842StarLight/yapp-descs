 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Digital Marketing Specialist</p>
<p>
We are seeking a dynamic Digital Marketer who is passionate about strategizing and executing on all areas of digital marketing for our clients. Propertybase is a software suite built specifically to help real estate brokerages market their businesses online, acting as a one-stop shop for their CRM, CMS, IDX, SEO, lead generation, email marketing, and mobile &amp; desktop websites. The Digital Marketing Specialist is part of the Digital Marketing team, which is an in-house agency that works exclusively with clients of Propertybase on executing digital marketing campaigns. This role has a heavy focus on SEO performance but can touch all aspects of digital marketing initiatives.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
What you’ll do:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Serve as the main customer point of contact for all digital marketing initiatives. Work with customers to identify their digital marketing goals and build creative, multi-month project plans that work towards achieving those goals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage the day-to-day activities across multiple search engines (Google and Bing) including campaign planning, implementation, performance review and optimization</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Perform keyword research, review and editing of ad copy, review and analysis of performance metrics and optimization of campaigns across multiple channels</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create and deliver easy-to-understand reports including key performance indicators and opportunities for improvement</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage technical execution of various email lists and campaigns, including CTAs and content used in email sends; segment lists based on behaviors and measure results to optimize for lead conversion</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Stay up-to-date with digital marketing trends and potential new channels and strategies, including: SEO, content, online advertising and email marketing. You will need to confidently display subject matter expertise to your clients in your communications.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work with cross-functional teams to gather all campaign details, promotions and creative; ensure all campaigns go live in an accurate and timely manner</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
</p>
<p>To succeed in this role, you’ll need:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>2-3 years of marketing experience with a customer focus</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strong knowledge of SEO best practices and execution. This includes on-page as well as offsite optimization techniques. Familiarity with how to extract relevant data from Google Search Console, Bing Webmaster Tools, Google Analytics and various other tools as necessary.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience with planning, building and optimizing advertising campaigns in AdWords and Facebook Business Manager. Demonstrate understanding of where, why and how to leverage search, display and retargeting/remarketing campaigns.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience planning, building and optimizing email marketing campaigns. Demonstrate understanding of important KPIs, email segmentation techniques, technical email best practices, and have hands-on experience building and iterating on drip campaigns.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Comfortable working for many different clients under tight deadlines.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Comfortable with writing content</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience with the real estate brokerage vertical is helpful but not required.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Willingness to wear many hats and try new things!</p></li>
</ul>
</div></div>
