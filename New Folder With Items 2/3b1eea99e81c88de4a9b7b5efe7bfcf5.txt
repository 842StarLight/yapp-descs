<h2>Estimator (Asphalt/Road Construction)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Located in Pittsburgh, PA, the <b>PJ DICK-TRUMBULL-LINDY</b> family of construction companies is a premier provider of comprehensive general contracting and construction management services. Consistently ranked one of the Top 100 builders nationally by <i>Engineering News-Record</i>, and locally one of Pittsburgh's <i>"Best Places to Work",</i> we attract the finest talent to deliver quality projects across our markets.</p>
<p><b>
LINDY PAVING, INC., </b>the Tri-State area's largest and most successful paving contractor and asphalt manufacturer, is seeking an Estimator to join it's team.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PRIMARY FUNCTION:</b></p>
<p>
Prepare portions of contract proposal estimates for one or more disciplines, including the determination of applicable project plans and specifications.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
TYPICAL DUTIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Review proposal specifications and drawings to determine scope of work and required contents of estimate.</li>
<li>Attend field pre-bid meetings and conference calls.</li>
<li>Prepare discipline estimates by calculating complete takeoff and pricing of scope of work.</li>
<li>Maintain files of working documents as back-up for estimate figures, including current (accurate) information on prices from suppliers through direct contact, sales brochures, price lists, etc.</li>
<li>Support other department personnel as required; may include preparing estimates in other disciplines or for operations (i.e., change orders).</li>
<li>May review design options and recommend best solution based on cost, engineering quality, or availability of materials.</li>
<li>Review and incorporate historical data from previous projects, purchase orders, subcontracts, cost reports, etc., into unit and man-hour figures.</li>
<li>Solicit subcontractor and supplier quotes and analyze those quotes.</li>
<li>Establish working relationships and rapport with subcontractors and suppliers in order to obtain favorable quotations.</li>
<li>Seek out and report leads and future work opportunities and market organization when circumstances allow.</li>
<li>Communicate methods of construction contemplated in the bid to Superintendent and monitor actual performance efficiency against bid units for future consideration.</li>
<li>Perform additional assignments per supervisor's direction.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
SKILLS, KNOWLEDGE, QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<p>
Educational and experience requirements include: four-year engineering degree or equivalent combinations of technical training and/or experience. Two(2) to five(5) years project management and/or engineering experience related to similar road construction is preferred. Advanced knowledge of assigned discipline, estimating techniques, and cost control, plus ability to interpret computerized cost data and systems essential. Must be proficient in Microsoft Excel and Word with the ability to quickly adapt and learn bidding software.</p>
