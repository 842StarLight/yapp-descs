<h2>SnowSports Desk Attendant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>As a <b>Snowsports Desk Attendant </b>you are responsible for analyzing the guests needs in person or over the telephone while taking reservations for the proper lesson package to meet their needs.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The <b>SnowSports Desk Attendant's </b>typical job functions include:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Assisting guests with ski or snowboard lesson reservations</li>
<li>
Completing express ski rentals including selecting skis, boots, poles and completing necessary rental paperwork</li>
<li>
Running point of sale and reservation software
</li>
<li>Answering resort based questions regarding restaurants, available activities and other inquires</li>
<li>
Working in a fast pace environment at times and the ability to multi-task</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This position may be for you if:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
You are at least 18 years old</li>
<li>
You have achieved a high school diploma or general education degree (GED)</li>
<li>
You are available to work days, evenings, weekends and holidays</li>
<li>
You enjoy a customer service position interacting with guests and working with team members</li>
<li>
You have knowledge of skiing and or snowboarding is very helpful but not required</li>
<li>
You have computer skills, prior cash handling experience and strong interpersonal skills</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
</div></div>
