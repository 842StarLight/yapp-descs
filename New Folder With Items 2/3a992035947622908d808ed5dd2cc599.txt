<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">NAVIS is hiring a Sales Development Representative (SDR) position in beautiful Bend, Oregon! This role will be an integral part of our growing Sales organization.
<br><br>
Work for a company that has been named on "Top Workplace" lists for EIGHT years running!
<br><br>
In this role, you will be a part of an outstanding Sales organization and will be able to learn from the best! If you are serious about a career in Sales, passionate about helping people, and want the work you do to truly make a difference in an ethical, honest manner, read on...
<br><br>
This position is responsible for the generation and development of new, qualified prospects, working in tandem with our Territory Sales Consultants for seamless hand-off into the Discovery Meeting stage of the NAVIS Consultative Selling process. Additionally, you will be providing valuable research and call plan creation for the sales department.
<br><br>
<b>Sales Development Duties (70% of the job):</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify and develop new clients through ethical sales methods and relevant relationships in partnership with Territory Sales Consultants</li>
<li>Plan and prioritize personal sales activities and prospect contact towards achieving agreed business objectives, including costs and sales while also managing personal time and productivity</li>
<li>Plan and manage business portfolio/territory according to NAVIS Consultative Selling strategy</li>
<li>Manage product/service mix, pricing and margins according to agreed aims</li>
<li>Use customer and prospect contact activities tools and systems, including, but not limited to Microsoft office suite, Salesforce (SFDC) and Enterprise, and update/keep relevant information held in these systems</li>
<li>Plan, carry out and support local marketing activities to agreed budgets and timescales, and integrate personal sales efforts with other organized marketing activities, e.g., product launches, promotions, advertising, exhibitions and telemarketing</li>
<li>Respond to and follow up on sales inquiries using appropriate methods</li>
<li>Monitor and report on market and competitor activities and provide relevant reports and information</li>
<li>Record, analyze, report and administer according to systems and requirements</li>
</ul>
Communicate, liaise, and negotiate internally and externally using appropriate methods to facilitate the development of profitable business and sustainable relationships
<br><ul>
<li>Attend and present at external customer meetings and internal meetings with other company functions necessary to perform duties and aid business development</li>
<li>Attend training and develop relevant knowledge, techniques and skills</li>
</ul>
<b>Sales Research Duties (30% of the job):</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Research and verify key decision makers, stakeholders, and information about prospects such as ADR, reservation services, marketing efforts, leisure / corporate / group business percentages, seasonality, etc. to assist sales department prospecting efforts</li>
<li>Research and prepare call plans for Sales Consultants</li>
<li>Thoroughly and accurately record data in our SalesForce.com (SFDC) CRM tool</li>
<li>Take part in sales and operational planning meetings to resolve any questions regarding the data you have gathered and analyzed, its interpretation within the sales team, and to aid in reaching service objectives for key prospective clients</li>
<li>Validate potential prospect lists, add new prospects to our database and verify accuracy of information</li>
<li>Partner with the Marketing team, as needed</li>
<li>Be the central point of contact for various sales teams when it comes to client research and analysis</li>
<li>Utilize SalesForce.com to capture and store research information</li>
</ul>
