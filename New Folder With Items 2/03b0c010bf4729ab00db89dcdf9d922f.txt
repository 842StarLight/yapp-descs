<h2>Sales Operations Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>US Field Segment Sales Operations Program Manager – Financial Services Enterprise Operating Unit</b></div>
<div>
<br>
The Business &amp; Sales Operations (BSO) group is the business center of excellence for cross-business execution performance, planning, and subsidiary operations. The team helps scale execution and drives clear business and market insights that result in Microsoft achieving its business objectives.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Field Segment Sales Operations Program Manager (SOPM) role reports to the Segment SOPM Manager responsible for leading and orchestrating processes across their segment within the US Subsidiary. The SOPM is a business partner and scales by utilizing standard platforms and processes. This role focuses on supporting the core sales and business operational processes, tools, metrics and communications that affect operations. This role is also responsible for partnering with finance team to plan and drive quota distribution process for assigned OU, along with maintenance throughout the fiscal year. The successful candidate will drive a seamless collaboration within the OU, HQ BSO team, Finance, and WWIC partners. This means the Sales Operations Program Manager assists in the following areas, responsible for maximizing impact with customers and field.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Lead in Sales transformation across the Enterprise and EOU:
</li>
<li>Focus on providing process optimization by understanding the desired business outcome</li>
<li>
Deliver end-to-end deep data analysis and “light up” actionable strategic insights</li>
<li>
Innovate to deliver standards which enables speed, efficiency and scale in the business</li>
<li>
Partner with OU &amp; Sales Excellence lead to drive sales process enablement, insight and execution locally<br>
Support local RoB and Sales Process Execution - leads operational rhythms &amp; provides guidance on timeline and required actions.
</li>
<li>Drive and land execution and operational excellence for Quota Distribution, Quota Maintenance, Revenue Disputes, Quota Audit, Territory Planning, Quota Tools etc.</li>
<li>
Partner with SELs to leverage standard reports to improve local execution and sales discipline.
</li>
<li>Train salespeople on sales tools and process, Opportunity Management, forecast guidance, rhythm, expectations.
</li>
<li>Drive adoption and usage of MSX insights as the standardized sales leader field reporting tool.
</li>
<li>Deliver insights through analytics to improve key pipeline metrics such as close rates, pipeline velocity.</li>
<li>
Support and provide insight into opportunity management and sales execution</li>
<li>
Identify Pipeline and Annuity opportunities and drive engagement</li>
<li>
Drive Pipeline and data hygiene:</li>
<li>
Lead and drive a culture of rigorous pipeline management in compliance with OU and WW standards.
</li>
<li>Ensure AST, MAL up to date</li>
<li>
Support M&amp;A activities, international disputes.</li>
<li>
Funnel seller feedback for iterative tool/reporting/process simplification and refinement
</li>
<li>Supports the equitable assignment of sales force quotas and ensures quotas are optimally allocated to all sales units providing insights, analysis and recommendations</li>
</ul>
