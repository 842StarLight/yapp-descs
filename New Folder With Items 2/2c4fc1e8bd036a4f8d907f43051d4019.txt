<h2>Senior Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Outlook for iOS and Android is looking for a Program Manager who will help us define and lead key innovative product experiences that users will love and IT will trust. We work iteratively, ship often, and put the user first. We want to delight our users with experiences that help them focus and be the most productive they can be at work and in life, while on the go, all while achieving our business goals that keep the lights on.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The challenges are big and so are the opportunities for impact you’ll have. Outlook is one of Microsoft’s most important businesses, with over 140 Million MAD and huge strategic importance. It’s the premium Microsoft brand for communications and time management, and a key part of our One Microsoft strategy. It’s also the app we’re depending on to be the tip of the spear for M365 penetration on iOS and Android. In this role you’ll be focused on bringing focus to our growth amongst enterprise customers – engaging the field, talking to tons of enthusiastic customers, and delivering on the scenarios that are most critical to them in a way that is simple, straightforward, and also delightful.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We have:</div>
<ul>
<li>An awesome opportunity to tackle unique and large product and business challenges</li>
<li>Diverse backgrounds, opinions, previous work histories, and experiences that translate into an exciting and resourceful cross-functional team across different office locations and time zones.</li>
<li>A growth mindset and a craving for building the best mobile product in the app stores!</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div>
We expect you to have:</div>
<ul>
<li>A growth mindset, too.</li>
<li>Demonstrated excellence building polished user experiences that people love.</li>
<li>Have a sense of pride and personal accountability for end-to-end quality, completeness, and resulting user experience.</li>
<li>Proven ability to influence and drive strategy at product level scope.</li>
<li>Outstanding cross-discipline and cross-team collaboration skills.</li>
<li>The ability to foster an engineering culture focused on customer satisfaction using a data informed approach.</li>
<li>Strong track record of not only individual excellence, but a demonstrated commitment to making others around you great.</li>
<li>A passion to drive process improvements to maximize our efficiency and impact of the team.</li>
<li>The drive to speak objectively and constructively about what is working well and what needs to improve, and help make change happen.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<div>
You might also have:</div>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of shipping production software</li>
<li>Mobile app development and/or design experience</li>
<li>A Bachelor or Masters degree in Computer Science or a related discipline, or the equivalent in real-world experience</li>
<li>Experience working directly with enterprise customers, sales and marketing.</li>
</ul>
