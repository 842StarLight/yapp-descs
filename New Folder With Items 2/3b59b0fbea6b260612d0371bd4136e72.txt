<h2>Marketing Coordinator (Corporate)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Position Title:</b> Marketing Coordinator
<br><br>
<b>Position Location:</b> Chicago
<br><br>
<b>Reporting to:</b> Global Director of Internal Comms
<br><br>
Who Are We?
<br><br>
Avison Young is the world’s fastest-growing commercial real estate services firm, with 120 offices providing sales, leasing and management services to owners and occupiers of properties around the world. We are rapidly building a global brand and are seeking a detail-oriented Marketing Coordinator to join our growing corporate marketing team.
<br><br>
This newly-created position will provide marketing, operational and administrative support for the global marketing function, focused on company-wide branding and marketing initiatives. This person will manage central resources such as the company’s Marketing hub and digital assets libraries; provide departmental support including scheduling, billing and purchasing; and handle projects and initiatives to meet branding and marketing objectives.
<br><br>
<b>Job functions:</b><br>
<b>Support Global Marketing Leadership:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Scheduling and project management</li>
<li>Organize marketing team meetings and events</li>
<li>Assist in marketing transition activities for new acquisitions</li>
<li>Manage advertising schedules, media buys and traffic</li>
<li>Create and populate marketing dashboards and track and report metrics</li>
<li>Support internal content creation and information sharing</li>
</ul>
<b>Organizational Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain central marketing and brand hub; artwork, policies, calendars, imagery files and project schedules</li>
<li>Update and post core marketing documents as appropriate for internal access and for public via avisonyoung.com</li>
<li>Prepare and manage budgets, vendor files and invoices</li>
<li>Maintain internal mailing lists and office marketing contacts communications</li>
<li>Field internal information requests</li>
<li>Bring creative thinking to projects, propose and implement new solutions, content, etc.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, communications, design or related field</li>
<li>1-2 years experience in a corporate or agency marketing, branding or communications environment; Professional services or commercial real estate experience a plus</li>
<li>Operational and/or administrative experience such as budgeting, reporting, document administration</li>
<li>Strong design sense and familiarity with visual branding and corporate identity</li>
<li>Ability to meet deadlines, prioritize tasks and work well under pressure</li>
<li>Exceptional judgment and attention to detail</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills; ability to work with staff at all levels, including senior executives and global team members</li>
<li>Proficiency in the Microsoft Office suite (Word, Powerpoint, Excel, Outlook) and Sharepoint. Some experience with Abode CreativeSuite/InDesign, HTML and Digital Asset Management (DAM) systems preferred</li>
</ul>
We offer a competitive salary and benefits package. If you are interested in this opportunity, please submit your resume and cover letter. Applicants must be eligible to work in Canada.
