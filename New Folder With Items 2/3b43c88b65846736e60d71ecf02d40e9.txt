<p><b>JOB PURPOSE</b></p>
<div>
<p>
Artisan Vehicle Systems is a global leader in battery powered underground mining vehicle technology. Based in Camarillo, California, Artisan is an original equipment manufacturer (OEM) of zero emission battery powered mining vehicles. Its core technology is its proprietary battery packs, electric motors, power electronics, software and control systems. Our underground mining machines are designed from the ground up around these high powered, highly reliable, field proven battery electric powertrains.</p>
<p>
Our company is in expansion mode and we are actively seeking candidates with a variety of talents and skill sets. Artisan Vehicle Systems is a growing, innovative and dynamic company searching for individuals who have the passion, desire, talent, motivation, commitment and pride in their work to join our team. We offer our employees a safe and friendly work environment with the opportunity for advancement, competitive compensation and benefit packages.</p>
<p>
Please visit our website: http://www.artisanvehicles.com</p>
<p>
The Procurement Specialist/Buyer is an experienced professional with an adept understanding of sophisticated products with large BOMs and negotiating delivery dates and pricing with suppliers in a manufacturing environment. Success for this position is measured by cost effective, on time delivery of all components needed for our products, proactive communication to internal staff and outside suppliers, thorough tracking of every component at each stage of the procurement process, production workflow efficiency, and on time deliveries to our customers. An understanding of electrical as well as mechanical engineering principles is very important.</p>
