All positions will include tasks of: Building and maintaining carrier relationships, managing carrier and customer accounts, and planning all aspects of freight movements. Providing exceptional service, exceptional communication, and meeting or exceeding all expectations for all customers. Managing Freight and securing transportation from our 18,000 + service providers. And monitoring transportation moves throughout the life cycle of the load</p>
</div>
<p></p>

<p>
Duties for Freight Broker will include: Tasks vary by day, ability to multi task a must, day will include use of Microsoft Outlook, Excel, and other user friendly software programs, critical thinkers looking for a challenge are preferred! Sales experience a plus, but not required. Day will include monitoring transportation with carriers via phone and email, negotiating with carriers to secure capacity to service our customers, and communication both internally and externally via phone and email. Attention to detail, with ability to set priorities and problem solve a must. Experience in fast paced environment on phones a plus! Trucking and transportation experience not required. 2-4 year degree required.</p>
