<h2>Materials Handler</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p><b>FUNCTION/POSITION DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>
Performs a combination of the following tasks: receive, ship and/or store component parts and materials for manufacturing operations. Uses production schedules, customer orders, work orders, shipping orders or requisitions to determine items to be moved, gathered or distributed. May be assigned to one particular work center or various work centers.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL JOB DUTIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assembles orders, fills requisitions, workorders, etc., and moves and/or distributes materials to shipping dept, trucks, or production.</li>
<li>Performs material receiving functions. Identifies, records, labels and places materials into stock. Follows-up with appropriate paperwork including purchase orders and RMA's.</li>
<li>Marks materials with identifying information using RF Barcode labels, paint per or other marking devices.</li>
<li>Records amounts of materials or items received or distributed by processing issues, transfers, receipts or sales orders. Key transactions into RF handheld or desk top computer.</li>
<li>Ships wholegoods/parts to destination and/or moves within production area. Packages and labels materials. May operate shipping company software systems.</li>
<li>Required to operate forklift.</li>
<li>Accurately records workorder information and inventory transactions.</li>
<li>Uses computer to access information or verify data.</li>
<li>May load and transport materials using company vehicle. May pickup and deliver parts/supplies from local vendors.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES:</b></p>
<p>
High school diploma or GED required.</p>
<ul>
<li>One to six months of previous material handling experience.</li>
<li>Ability to operate a forklift and hoist safely.</li>
<li>Basic math skills and computer data entry skills.</li>
<li>Must be physically capable of lifting 50 pounds alone and 100 pounds with assistance.</li>
<li>Good communication skills, both verbal and written.</li>
<li>High school diploma or GED required.</li>
<li>Able to work overtime to meet production deadlines. Able to work under pressure.</li>
<li>Able to wear appropriate personal protective equipment, including steel toe shoes.</li>
</ul> <p><b><i>Alamo Group is proud to be an Equal Opportunity, Affirmative Action employer and provides equal opportunity to minorities, females, protected veterans and individuals with disabilities (M/F/D/V). We maintain a drug-free workplace and perform pre-employment substance abuse testing.</i></b></p>
