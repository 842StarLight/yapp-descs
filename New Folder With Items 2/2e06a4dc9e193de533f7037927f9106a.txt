<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>Job Location<br>
Chicago, IL. or Scottsdale, AZ.<br>
<br>
Job Description<br>
InfoArmor provides industry-leading employee identity protection and advanced threat intelligence that enable its clients to combat an ever-changing cyber threat landscape. Our headquarters is in Scottsdale, Arizona. We are passionate about our culture and offer team members rewarding opportunities in a fast-growing, dynamic environment.<br>
<br>
Summary/Objective<br>
At InfoArmor, we are looking to transform the security of humankind with industry-leading identity protection and advanced threat intelligence to combat an ever-changing cyber threat landscape. Our innovative, customer-centric approach combined with our recent entry into the Allstate family of companies has positioned us to grow rapidly and disrupt the industry. We are passionate about our culture and offer team members rewarding opportunities in a fast-moving, dynamic environment.<br>
<br>
As a key member of the product marketing team, you will drive our marketing strategies that enable us to secure the places people work, relationships they build and data they share. The product marketing team at InfoArmor is responsible for market launch &amp; ongoing release strategies, positioning, development of marketing materials, campaigns, and communications to drive business outcomes. If you’re an energetic self-starter with a passion for managing strategic, complex and fast-paced projects in collaboration with other high-performers toward a common goal, this is the ideal role for you.<br>
<br>
Essential Functions<br>
<ul>
<li>Lead the go-to-market planning and strategy development for new products and features, including product positioning that sets InfoArmor’s products and services apart from others in the market and resonates with our target audiences</li>
<li>Conduct industry, competitive, and win/loss analysis to gain an in-depth understanding of emerging trends and opportunities, customer needs, and buyer behaviors</li>
<li>Work closely with the Product Management team to translate industry trends and customer needs into product requirements and product roadmap priorities</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product Management, Product Design, and Marketing to create and manage buyer personas as the market changes or new products/features emerge</li>
<li>Execute product launch plans, working closely with the Product Management, Marketing, Content, and Design teams to ensure effective and ongoing relevance in the market to drive demand generation and customer acquisition</li>
<li>Communicate the vision and value of our products and services to the sales, broker, and account management teams and develop sales tools, training and programs that enable the selling process</li>
<li>Create product-specific content to inform the development of external communications tactics including marketing programs, sales tools, proposal content, etc.</li>
<li>Develop marketing campaigns and assets that drive brand consideration and new user acquisition among target audiences</li>
<li>Plan, execute and optimize strategies for moving prospects through the funnel; leverage a multi-touch approach and integration among channels such as in-person, email, paid marketing channels, social media, PR, content, website optimization, etc.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Marketing and Content teams on lead generation campaigns and help develop clear and compelling product messaging</li>
<li>Develop marketing strategies, in close partnership with our Marketing and Account Management teams, that increase utilization and retention of InfoArmor’s products among our target audiences</li>
<li>Drive experimental tests to ensure our messaging, strategies and tactics are driving customer adoption efficiently and effectively</li>
<li>Monitor, assess and report on integrated marketing performance and provide strategic direction and guidance based on analysis of results</li>
<li>Set, track, and drive key business metrics for InfoArmor’s products, including launch benchmarks, pipeline goals, and retention objectives</li>
<li>Maintain deep understanding of broader enterprise marketing strategy and priorities as they evolve, orchestrate clear communication among cross-functional team</li>
</ul>
<br>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.<br>
<br>
Competencies<br>
<ul>
<li>Strategic understanding of marketing as it relates to achieving business objectives and outcomes</li>
<li>Strong expertise with most marketing channels and executional requirements, with advanced knowledge of digital marketing</li>
<li>Excellent project management skills for complex, integrated campaigns involving both internal and external resources, vendors and budgets</li>
<li>Analytical thinking with proven ability to execute on multiple projects at once with a keen sense of urgency</li>
<li>Self-starter with drive to succeed in a quickly changing business environment; comfort with high levels of ambiguity</li>
<li>Ability to continuously learn, adapt and optimize approaches based on new direction, insights and learnings</li>
<li>Outstanding cross-group collaboration, interpersonal, verbal and written communication, problem solving and presentation skills are required</li>
<li>Strong consumer advocacy with an ability to optimize experiences based on key insights, develop hypotheses, and translate them into actionable tests</li>
<li>Adept at influencing peers and managers, and building consensus</li>
<li>Ability to work with solid level of independence on new or unfamiliar projects of critical importance, leveraging foundational business, marketing and communication knowledge, problem-solving skills, and resources available for reference</li>
</ul>
