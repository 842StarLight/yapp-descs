<h2>SVP & Chief Counsel</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position </b><b>Overview</b>:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The SVP and Chief Counsel is responsible for management of all legal matters and the negotiation of business affairs deals for the Weather Channel television network and its digital service Local Now. This position will assess legal and business issues and present legally-sound solutions designed to further the objectives of the company. The SVP will consult on legal matters across the enterprise. Accountable for providing legal counsel and guidance in the ordinary and special activities of the network, network group or genre. The SVP is the most senior executive responsible for negotiating, reviewing and/or managing contracts for talent and production services for the network. This position will report to The President of Weather Group and will be based in Atlanta, Georgia.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Structuring, negotiating and documenting agreements, counseling client groups on a wide variety of legal matters, and coordinating with executives across company departments to effectively implement legal policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Establishes network legal policy or adheres to company legal policy and ensures that legal affairs of the network are properly handled through the effective direction of the legal department and management of outside law firms.</li>
<li>Oversees legal work of the network, including contracts, licensing, patents, copyright and trademark matters, labor law compliance, SEC filings and compliance, litigation, antitrust matters and legal policies for operations.</li>
<li>Develop strategy, analyze, and participate in corporate negotiations, including technology deals relating to the distribution of content, including digital distribution</li>
<li>Negotiate and review a wide variety of ancillary agreements and documentation to support the business, including but not limited to amendments, settlement agreements, assignment agreements, confidentiality agreements and services agreements.</li>
<li>Oversee corporate compliance, entity formation and management of corporate representation services.</li>
<li>Work with and advise company owned broadcast television stations on FCC and regulatory related matters.</li>
<li>Work closely with intellectual property, technology, legislative and regulatory groups to stay on top of trends and developments and effectively communicate implications to the companyâ€™s business leaders, especially as they relate to IP issues and evolving media and platforms.</li>
<li>Maintain an awareness and understanding of the latest developments in the law and technology as they relate to the distribution of media content.</li>
<li>Serve as a liaison with content producing divisions to ensure adherence to strategic focus and compliance with terms.</li>
<li>Consult on major business decisions as it relates to: FCC implications (ex: captioning) and privacy implications.</li>
<li>Pre-litigation and crisis management in coordination with Public Relations.</li>
<li>Partner with Advertising Sales on sponsorship and endorsement deals.</li>
<li>Manage IP portfolio to include clearance, prosecution and enforcement.</li>
<li>Conduct preliminary trademark and domain searches.</li>
<li>Conduct Fair Use training sessions to original programming and digital teams</li>
<li>Serve as Standards and Practices for Original and Live Programming</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Strategy/Planning:</p>
<ul>
<li>Makes regular input into the formulation of strategies for, and/or adjusts the approved strategies of, the company.</li>
<li>Develops short- to long-term objectives and budgets. Sets, approves and adjusts major policies and operating procedures.</li>
<li>Outcomes directed and influenced have material impact on the function's long-term results.</li>
<li>Evaluates performance of the function against desired outcomes.</li>
<li>Obtains and allocates resources to achieve strategic and operating goals.</li>
</ul>
<p>Negotiations:</p>
<ul>
<li>Interacts and negotiates with executive management to adjust plans and strategies.</li>
<li>Interacts with critical decision makers to influence business decisions.</li>
<li>Builds and enhances vital, top level internal and external relationships.</li>
</ul>
<p>Assignment of Work:</p>
<ul>
<li>Advises executives accountable for achieving major outcomes for the function.</li>
<li>Ensures that direct reports have appropriate resources to achieve defined objectives.</li>
<li>Directs inside counsel, and selects, retains and manages outside counsel.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Oversight Received:</p>
<ul>
<li>Work is reviewed from a relatively long-term perspective against corporate, division or network-level goals.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Required Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>An accredited law degree and 10+ years legal experience in a corporation or major law firm</li>
<li>Experience working in-house in the legal or business affairs department of a company in the media or entertainment industry, preferably at a cable or broadcast television network required.</li>
<li>Experience negotiating complex commercial agreements required.</li>
<li>Transnational experience negotiating mergers &amp; acquisitions, and joint ventures preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with implementing privacy law and policy within an organization required.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong, drafting, analytical, strategic, and organizational skills required.</li>
<li>Must be able to master components of business transactions and communicate clearly regarding complex issues.</li>
</ul>
