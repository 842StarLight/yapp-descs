<h2>Recruiting and Operations Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Based in Washington, DC, MPS is an SBA-certified HUBZone Small Business, dedicated to empowering our clients through innovative change management services, with a focus on organizational effectiveness. Our consultants have served executives in the federal sector for over a decade, working on several high-profile initiatives. We pride ourselves on being experienced consultants, trusted advisers, and reliable partners. As a designated Employer of National Service, our core value is a commitment to public service.</p>
<p><b>
Recruiting and Operations Coordinator</b></p>
<p>
The individual in this position will be primarily responsible for internal recruitment for MPS, and additionally working on special projects in human capital development and sales under the direction of the Director of Operations and deputy. The role will flex in providing primary support for recruitment along and working on other special projects depending on the strategic needs of the company and cycle throughout the year. Successful candidates will have an aptitude for program operations (people, processes, tools) as well as continuous program improvements.</p>
<p><i>
This position is located in downtown Washington, DC, with an optional partially-remote schedule.</i></p>
<p><b>
Key Duties</b></p>
<p><b>
Recruiting (Primary responsibility):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform hands-on recruitment for open consulting positions. Includes performing resume reviews, phone screens, coordinating interviews with contract teams and managers, and acting as liaison for other internal staff such as HR.</li>
<li>Manage recruitment efforts across contracts to meet corporate performance metrics for staffing open positions. Includes collaborating with internal consulting teams as they engage with the recruiting processes and developing and maintaining enterprise recruiting tools and trackers in support of the recruitment strategy.</li>
<li>Develop strategies for building recruiting capacity and an active pipeline of candidates for MPS core capabilities and developing capabilities, especially technical recruiting.</li>
<li>Track tasks and follows up with individuals to ensure activities are brought to a close.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Special Projects as assigned (Human Capital Development and Sales):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support staff develop and capacity building for a strategically-focused client facing and corporate roles to include the training identification and development.</li>
<li>Work with the Director of Operations to identify and support key human capital functions, tools, processes, as the program is built and matures.
</li>
<li>Support internal capacity building for a strategically-focused sales program (revenue generation services)</li>
<li>Work with the Director of Operations to identify and support key sales functions, tools, processes, and stakeholders as the program is built and matures.
</li>
<li>Track corporate activities and projects as assigned in support of the Director of Operations.</li>
<li><i>Additional projects/activities will be included based on strategic needs of the company and lifecycle of human capital development, sales, and recruiting.</i></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree</li>
<li>4 years of professional experience or Masters and 1 year of professional experience</li>
<li>Understanding of basic HR and recruitment principles</li>
<li>Experience with project management/coordination</li>
<li>Experience working with teams, managing logistics, and updating tracking documents</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Word and Excel</li>
<li>Understanding of basic sales principles</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Innate problem-solver who proactively seeks out and advises on solutions</li>
<li>Strategic thinker who can think several months out to create future value for the firm</li>
<li>Understanding of to build and keep business processes moving</li>
<li>Ability to work with little direction</li>
<li>Voracious learner with high learning agility who seeks out new information to solve strategic problems</li>
<li>Positive attitude, self-motivated, confident, and tenacious</li>
<li>Strong organizational and time-management skills</li>
<li>Ability to work with all levels of staff at the firm</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with technical recruiting or staffing</li>
<li>Familiar with Federal Consulting industry</li>
</ul>
