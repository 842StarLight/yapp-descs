<h2>Associate Conference Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>The Associate Conference Marketing Manager will deliver marketing plans to support event strategies, set up best practices to ensure growth plans deliver on-target revenue, contribute to the development of event marketing campaigns, and support the data build project.</p>
<p>You’ll own the delivery of conference marketing plans to support a B2B portfolio of 4 - 6 events per year of a range of sizes. These will include both established repeat events as well as launch conferences. Experience in digital/email marketing, direct mail, interactive/web marketing, and advertising efforts (SEO, SEM, PPC, social) is essential.</p>
<p><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Develop, execute, manage &amp; optimize the execution of integrated marketing campaigns for B2B conferences ensuring promotions hits targeted audiences on-time, on budget and produces projected attendance goals</p>
<p>· Work in partnership with internal teams including marketing, production, sales, operations, editorial, data services and design</p>
<p>· Track revenue &amp; expenses to ensure budgetary goals are met</p>
<p>· Provide marketing reports on a weekly basis including registration numbers &amp; revenue, tactics performed, analysis, and forward-looking recommendations to increase delegate attendance</p>
<p>· Recruit media partners for marketing support of the event; manage &amp; ensure activities of the program are executed per the agreement</p>
<p>· Monitor &amp; provide input into social media campaigns for each event</p>
<p>· Launch, update &amp; maintain event websites</p>
<p>· Collaborate with sales team to coordinate campaigns to drive awareness of sponsorship &amp; exhibitor opportunities</p>
<p>· Liaise with the subscriptions marketing team to share knowledge and stay up-to-date with new trends ensuring the implementation of best practice conference marketing tools and processes</p>
<p>· Use reporting analytics tools to review, analyze, and optimize user experiences</p>
<p>· Analyze ROI on marketing tactics and refine promotional plans on a continuous basis</p>
<p><b>The Individual: </b></p>
<p>· Highly organized with ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment</p>
<p>· Detail-oriented, strong problem solving and analytical skills.</p>
<p>· Ability to work collaboratively with other internal stakeholders and high-powered clients</p>
<p>· Strong presentation, writing, and communication skills</p>
<p>· Experience in B2B and/or product marketing preferred</p>
<p>· Positive attitude with an appreciation for teamwork</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>· Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent in relevant field</p>
<p>· At least 1 to 2 years overall marketing experience with conference marketing preferred</p>
<p>· Experience in private equity industry marketing preferred</p>
<p>· Proficient in Outlook, Excel, Word and PowerPoint</p>
<p>· Ability to travel to events if needed</p>
<p><b>About PEI: </b></p>
<p>PEI is the only global B2B information group focused exclusively on private equity, private real estate, private debt, infrastructure and investing. As these classes continue to grow in scale and significance – for investors, fund managers, financial practitioners and other service industries globally – PEI is positioned to provide unparalleled business knowledge and intelligence to these communities.</p>
<p>Formed in London in November 2001, PEI publishes globally recognized magazines and news websites, manages an extensive set of databases dedicated to alternative assets, runs more than 50 market-leading conferences globally and publishes a large library of specialist books.</p>
