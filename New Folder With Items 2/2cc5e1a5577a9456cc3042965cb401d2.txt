<h2>Commercial Lines Assistant I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview:
</b></h1>
<p>Supports Commercial Lines account management and sales staff by servicing needs of Insurance Agency clients.
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary Responsibilities:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Prepare automobile identification cards, Certificates of Insurance and other daily service needs for Commercial Lines clients as assigned.
</li>
<li>Maintain and document client files in appropriate system to support Agency document management and quality control procedures.
</li>
<li>Establish and maintain relationships with internal and external customers to ensure quality service and customer satisfaction of Commercial Lines clients.
</li>
<li>Adhere to applicable Agency procedures, workflows and servicing timelines to meet client needs, while minimizing risk to the organization.
</li>
<li>Understand and adhere to the Company’s risk and regulatory standards, policies and controls in accordance with the Company’s Risk Appetite. Identify risk-related issues needing escalation to management.
</li>
<li>Promote an environment that supports diversity and reflects the M&amp;T Bank brand.
</li>
<li>Maintain M&amp;T internal control standards, including timely implementation of internal and external audit points together with any issues raised by external regulators as applicable.
</li>
<li>Complete other related duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Education and Experience Required:
</b></h2>
<p>Associates’ degree,
</p>
<p>OR in lieu of a degree,
</p>
<p>A combined minimum of 2 years’ higher education and/or work experience
</p>
<p>Strong verbal and written communication skills
</p>
<p>Proficiency with pertinent word processing, spreadsheet, email and presentation software
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Education and Experience Preferred:
</b></h2>
<p>Minimum of 1 years’ relevant experience with direct client contact
</p>
<p>Ability to obtain State Brokers License
</p>
<p>Property &amp; Casualty industry experience
</p>
<p>Ability to effectively work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment
</p>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Location
</b></h1>Syracuse, New York, United States of America</div></div>
