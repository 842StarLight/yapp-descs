<h2>Business Development Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Xator Corporation is a trusted provider of Information Technology (IT) solutions, including cybersecurity, infrastructure support, and enterprise application development. Our work positively impacts the safety and security of personnel, facilities, and vital information throughout the world. The Director of Business Development (BD) within the Enterprise Technology and Cyber Security (ETCS) Strategic Business Unit (SBU) is a member of the Senior Leadership Team and reports directly Vice President. This position leads ETCS's business development activity from big picture thinking to day-to-day process management. The Director drives the creation of a culture that highly values performance, innovation, quality, efficiency, open communication, collaboration, learning, and operational integration.</p>
<p>The Director is responsible for driving the achievement of ETCS's annual BD targets and managing day to day BD operations. He/she is responsible for managing the successful execution of the day to day business development activities from prospecting and identifying new business to capture and contributing to the submission of high-quality proposals. The Director will also be involved in teaming decisions, pricing strategy, win strategies, proposal development, and closing these opportunities. Other responsibilities include marketing Xator's core strengths to clients and partners and developing new partnerships when necessary.</p>
<p>The position also requires strategic thinking and ability to leverage new partnerships and connections to break into new markets. The Director will lead and drive this process and actively work towards networking and building new partnerships, relationships, identifying new opportunities and identifying key people as potential consultants and hires.</p>
<p>This position is located in Fairfax VA.</p>
<br>
<p>
<br>
<b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES (ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BD and Capture Management and Process</li>
<li>Lead growth and new business development.</li>
<li>Responsible for identifying and qualifying new business development opportunities. These efforts will include; opportunity identification, qualification, strategy creation and end-to-end pursuit ownership for active business development opportunities.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining the pipeline for new business and capture/recapture efforts of existing contracts, ensuring accuracy of the data for all opportunities assigned under this position.</li>
<li>Work within Xator's bid processes and templates for each step of the BD process, actively communicate, develop quality control at each step</li>
<li>Manage the opportunity process with probability of win calculations, revenue projections, and direct input to the company budget and forecasting process.</li>
<li>Coordinate, manage, and communicate the capture process</li>
<li>Identify and follow up with partners for current and new bids</li>
<li>Perform initial analysis of proposed or actual request of capabilities, and RFPs to determine technical, schedule, and content reasonableness, and profitability.</li>
<li>Provide report regularly to the Senior Leadership Team on performance and goals</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and timely electronic files</li>
<li>Pursuing New Markets</li>
<li>Conduct market analysis and develop a pipeline of opportunities for new markets</li>
<li>Develop a plan to break into the market with specific goals</li>
<li>Identify and build relationships with new partners</li>
<li>Develop collateral to market and represent ETCS in the new areas</li>
<li>Identify potential hires/consultants for new markets</li>
<li>Teaming/Interfacing with Potential Partners/Subcontractors</li>
<li>Represent the Company to potential clients and partners and lead discussions on specific bids</li>
<li>Lead efforts to build partnerships and subcontractor relationships and negotiate partnership arrangements</li>
<li>Coordinate teaming agreements, subcontracts, and other agreements with partners</li>
<li>Preparing for opportunities to present to potential and current customers</li>
<li>Support corporate leadership in staying aware of and preparing new business development opportunities, including: Upcoming business development targets; Market outreach events; Contract Industry Day events and other strategic outreach events.</li>
<li>Prepare and present formal presentations to prospective and current customers based on their needs</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
</ul>

<b>Experience:</b></p>

<li>Experience in leading and managing the business development process from start to end</li>
<li>Must have extensive relationships within the Federal Government.</li>
<li>Experience in relationship management, market development, and with key partners.</li>
<li>Successful track record of capturing prime new business with Federal Government agencies</li>
<li>Outstanding written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Well-organized, attentive to detail, skilled in setting priorities and meeting deadlines, and able to handle multiple tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>Works well independently and in teams; proactive in anticipating work requirements and problem solving</li>

</ul>
