<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
This position, a key role in the fast-growing CallMiner sales team, is focused on creating highly qualified opportunities for our field sales directors. Responsibilities include following up on inbound leads from webinars, tradeshows, and website visitors; cold calling targeted accounts; participating in presentations via the web; and occasionally attending tradeshows. The ideal candidate has degree in business, sales, or marketing or equivalent training in business or sales experience in the software industry.</p>
<p><b>
Primary responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Following up with inbound leads and outbound ABM Programs</li>
<li>
Developing with assigned directors a prospecting/development plan</li>
<li>
Formulating effective voice mail and email messaging</li>
<li>
Understanding and being able to articulate our value proposition</li>
<li>
Willingness to prospect in all formats to produce opportunities</li>
<li>
Be able to make 50+ calls a day to prospects.</li>
<li>
Working closely with marketing to understand how things work and what we need to do to make things better</li>
<li>
Work well in a team and fast-paced environment</li>
</ul>
