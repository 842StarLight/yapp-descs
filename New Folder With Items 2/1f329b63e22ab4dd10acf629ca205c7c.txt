<h2>Junior Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li><b>Are you a recent grad looking to jump start your career?</b></li>
<li><b>Are you a go getter with an outgoing personality and strong attention to detail? Looking to learn as much as you can and grow within one company?</b></li>
<li><b>Does sales and hunting for leads interest you?</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>If so… </b><b>Then We are Looking for you!</b></p>
<p>EZFacility is looking for a Junior Sales Representative to work closely with our Sales and Marketing teams and manage incoming lead follow up, as well as process and strategy around lead generation. We are looking for a persistent hunter looking to launch their career in SAAS sales.</p>
<p>The Junior Sales Representative will have a unique opportunity to learn the sales process from the ground up and work on both inbound and outbound opportunities. In addition to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Hunt for marketing leads, while also managing the web-based chat</li>
<li>Manage the lead process to turn over to the Product Specialists</li>
<li>Manage, track, and report all data/activities in Salesforce</li>
<li>Build a book of leads that will convert into qualified opportunities</li>
<li>Respond to inbound calls, following up with leads generated by various marketing programs</li>
<li>High volume outbound calls to potential leads</li>
<li>Work closely with sales manager on various projects that are in support of the entire sales team</li>
<li>Work closely with Sales to develop targeted lists, call strategies, and messaging to drive sales qualified opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>JOB QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or relevant work experience</li>
<li>Persistent and professional phone skills, excellent listening skills</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to understand and simplify the value of EZFacility's complex solutions.</li>
<li>Strong research, opportunity qualification, and objection handling skills</li>
<li>Experience in MS Office (Word, Excel, Outlook)</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize, organize and multi-task effectively in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>SAAS experience preferred, but not required</li>
</ul>
