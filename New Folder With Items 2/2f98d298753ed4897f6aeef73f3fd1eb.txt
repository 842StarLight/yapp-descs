<h2>Jr. Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Artivest</b> is seeking a <b>Jr. Data Analyst</b> to join its growing Strategy team. You will support the Business Intelligence Manager by producing complex reporting, identifying data collection opportunities for future analytics, illustrating trends using data, and assisting in the ongoing deployment of Salesforce. We are looking for a highly detail-oriented and self-driven individual with an eagerness for learning new skills and improving efficiency.<br>
<br>
Artivest provides a technology-driven investment platform for fund managers, wealth managers, independent advisors, and qualified individual investors. Our technological, ﬁnancial, and operational expertise powers a seamless experience for investors and a scalable point of access for our enterprise clients. The asset management division of Artivest, formerly known as Altegris, is an investment research provider with deep expertise in alternative manager selection, structuring unique solutions, and providing portfolio management and oversight. Our broker-dealer affiliate distributes our products to wirehouses, independent broker-dealers, registered investment advisors, and directly to high-net-worth individuals and family offices. Currently our advisory affiliate manages over $2.4 billion in assets.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Play a key role as a data-focused liaison between our Sales and Operations teams and enhance our distribution efforts by providing analytical insight into our customer base</li>
<li>Perform data mining and aggregating, querying multiple data sources such as Excel and Salesforce</li>
<li>Import, clean, validate and/or model data, and interpret the results as input to decision making</li>
<li>Analyze data and provide historic results and forecasting measures to management</li>
<li>Translate high-level business requirements into functional specifications and reports</li>
<li>Run daily, weekly, and monthly reports to identify asset flows and trends in data</li>
<li>Identify and communicate any discrepancies and outliers in data</li>
<li>Identify methods that should be used to collect, analyze, and manage data, with the purpose of making recommendations to improve data quality and the efficiency of data systems</li>
<li>Interface with outside vendors to acquire accurate reports and data</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>

<ul>

<li>1-3 years of work experience, preferably in financial services</li>
<li>Expertise in Excel, including the ability to use VLOOKUP, Pivot Tables, and other data presentation tools</li>
<li>Highly organized and dependable</li>
<li>Ability to take initiative and work independently</li>
<li>Ability to quickly transition and reassess priorities to meet aggressive deadlines</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
