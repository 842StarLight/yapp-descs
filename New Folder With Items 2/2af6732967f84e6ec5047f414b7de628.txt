<h2>Corporate Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div><div>
<div>CEVA Logistics is currently seeking a Corporate Account Executive located in Honolulu, HI. This individual will be responsible for generating profitable revenue growth through marketing all products and services within a defined geographic territory by focusing 50% of time and effort on retaining and growing revenue with existing customers, and 50% of time focused on identifying and developing new business opportunities.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The level of role depends on candidate background, education, and experience:</div>
<div>Corporate Account Executive I (A5)</div>
<div>Corporate Account Executive II (A6)</div>
<div>Sr. Corporate Account Executive (A7)</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain functional knowledge of the products, services and operations offered by the company and actively participate in product training sessions.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Interact regularly with station and operations managers, and develop close and cooperative working relationship with operational staff to ensure the customer’s needs are met.</li>
<li>Schedule and conduct a pre-determined number of face-to-face sales calls as directed by the sales or station management.</li>
<li>Input sales call information into the sales data system, or other designated sales call software provided by the company and/or provide sales reports as directed by sales or station management.</li>
<li>Maintain a pre-determined number of target accounts and demonstrate active attention to those accounts and progress toward closing.</li>
<li>Meet sales threshold/quota as defined by the company.</li>
<li>Work cooperatively with other sales and operational staff to support a team-selling environment.</li>
<li>Perform responsibilities in accordance with the terms of the Sales Performance Policy, Commission Model, and Acknowledgement Form.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education and Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High School Degree or GED required.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred; or equivalent combination of work experience and education, or completion of the LDP program.</li>
<li>Minimum one to three years related in sales.</li>
<li>Minimum two years industry experience.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Certifications and Licenses</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Valid state issued driver’s license.</li>
<li>Acceptable driving record.</li>
<li>Proof of current automobile insurance.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Languages and Communication Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to read, write and speak English fluently.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate and interact effectively with multi-functional and diverse backgrounds.</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze, and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures, or governmental regulations.</li>
<li>Ability to write reports, business correspondence, and procedure manuals.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from management, employees, customers and vendors.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Computer Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Computer literate</li>
<li>Intermediate level proficiency in Microsoft Office, internet, web-based and job specific software applications.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Other Skills/Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated positive, competitive, confident personality with strongly developed persuasive skills and a customer-focused orientation.</li>
<li>Effective negotiation skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively in a team environment to achieve results, or work independently with little or no supervision.</li>
<li>Must be adaptable to changing needs of client(s) and goals of the company.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Must have strong problem solving skills and the ability to think and respond quickly to sales and service issues.</li>
<li>Effective planning and organizational skills to schedule appointments appropriately and to meet reporting deadlines.</li>
<li>Effective follow-up skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to analyze moderately complex administrative details.</li>
<li>Ability to compose, edit and proofread routine business letters, correspondence and reports.</li>
<li>Ability to generate complex, error-free charts, graphs, spreadsheets and presentations.</li>
<li>In-depth knowledge of the company’s products and services.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Travel is required at least 80 % of the time in this position. Travel identified is approximately 100 % domestic (within the United States).</div>
