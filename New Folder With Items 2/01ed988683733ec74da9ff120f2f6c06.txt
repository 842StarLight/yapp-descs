<h2>Business Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview</b></h2>
<div>
Are you passionate about data? Do you enjoy working with other smart people to find trends and patterns in the data? Do you want to work in a data-driven environment where your effort impacts the success of the customers? We are looking for a savvy Data Analyst to join our close knit team of data professionals. You look to expand your knowledge and experience in data extraction, visualization, and story-telling. You must be courageous but not a bully, strong but not rude and humble but not timid. In a couple words, emotionally intelligent.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<p><b>
Partner</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Consult and provide data driven recommendations on key business decisions and initiatives</li>
<li>
Proactively monitor and communicate opportunities on key performance indicators and metrics</li>
<li>
Build cross-functional relationships with business units to ensure long-term partnership success</li>
<li>
Support our Product Managers, Sales Leaders, Software Developers, Data Engineers, Data Scientists and key business partners on business initiatives and ensure successful partnerships with each team</li>
<li>
Partner with the data warehouse team in gathering requirements, designing, testing, and supporting the ongoing development of the data warehouse</li>
<li>
Drive a data fluent culture by educating and mentoring others in the field of data analysis</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Build</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage and track a variety of tasks and projects at one time</li>
<li>
Engage with Agile framework / Scrums / Sprints / JIRA</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain reports / dashboards / analysis using Periscope Data, Tableau, and other BI development tools</li>
<li>
Document and adhere to standard analytics and reporting processes</li>
<li>
Communicate findings clearly to stakeholders through intelligent visualizations, interpretation and story-telling</li>
<li>
Participate in the development process through the lense of data availability, consistency and completeness</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Experienced with writing SQL</li>
<li>
Experienced with BI and analytics tools (Tableau, Power BI, Periscope Data, Excel, etc.)</li>
<li>
Proficient with Excel pivot tables, v-lookups, and functions</li>
<li>
Ability to process requests, implement recommended solutions, and complete requests in a timely fashion</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Be self-directed and able to function both independently and as part of a team</li>
<li>
Must be highly organized with a strong attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to handle multiple projects and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Knowledgeable with data warehouse concepts (Kimball)</li>
</ul>
