<b>Summary/Objective:</b><br>
The Sr Project Engineer is responsible for primary design for small to large-scale capital projects in support of new product development, capacity expansion, and process improvement.</div>
<div>This role provides technical support to operations for process improvement and equipment malfunctions.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<p>1. Design mechanical systems to replace and/or expand upon existing production equipment. Prepare the necessary mechanical drawings, electrical drawings and P&amp;ID’s as required. Provide integration and software development as needed.</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>50%</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p>2. Coordinate large-scale capital projects from project planning through project completion including writing of the project, project leadership, vendor selection, equipment specifications, and presentation to stakeholders and overseeing of 3rd party design.</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>25%</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p>3. Manage all activities associated with external contractors including selection, design of scope of work, monitoring of on-site contractor personnel, coordination of internal resources, conflict resolution, monitoring and management of schedule, approving of payments and communication of changes.</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>10%</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p>4. Troubleshoot component and system issues involving MMI’s, sensors, motors and controllers. Provide maintenance support and TPM efforts</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>10%</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p>5. Provide timely communication to manager and other appropriate stakeholders on the status of all current projects</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>5%</p>
<br>
</div>

<p><b>About Rogers Corporation:</b></p>
<div>Rogers Corporation (NYSE:ROG) is a global leader in engineered materials to power, protect, and connect our world. With more than 180 years of materials science experience, Rogers delivers high-performance solutions that enable clean energy, internet connectivity, and safety and protection applications, as well as other technologies where reliability is critical. Rogers delivers Power Electronics Solutions for energy-efficient motor drives, e-Mobility and renewable energy; Elastomeric Material Solutions for sealing, vibration management and impact protection in mobile devices, transportation interiors, industrial equipment and performance apparel; and Advanced Connectivity Solutions for wireless infrastructure, automotive safety and radar systems. Headquartered in Arizona (USA), Rogers operates manufacturing facilities in the United States, China, Germany, Belgium, Hungary, and South Korea, with joint ventures and sales offices worldwide.</div>
