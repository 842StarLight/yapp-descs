<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About the Role</b></p>
<p>
This position is ideal for someone who loves spending time with people and building high-quality, trusted relationships. A career in account management is suited to someone who is forward-thinking, a pro at multitasking, adept at communication and up-to-date on the latest industry news and technologies. If you can communicate opportunities to clients before they may even recognize a need, and become an indispensable part of their business development, then this role is for you. The best account manager is a positive personality, a team player, a motivator, a fount of knowledge and above all else, a planner. It’s a fast-paced and stimulating environment, so a solid daily plan and the support of your teammates is essential to success.<br>
<br>
This career puts you front and center with the Symphony clients and the community. Your #1 objective in this career path is to determine what the client needs to do to achieve their business goals, and then collaborate with the Symphony teams to implement it. It is your responsibility to understand the client’s industry, to help develop long-term strategies and to manage their budgets. It is your responsibility to always do what is best for the client.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Help onboard new clients and provide customer service to assigned customer accounts</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Improve customer satisfaction by improving responsiveness and anticipating customer needs</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop new business opportunities with existing clients by promoting company products and services</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Understand clients’ business goals and develops unique strategies in collaboration with the Marketing and Development team to spur client growth</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Responsible for understanding the client’s industry, how their business interacts with customers, and what their core values are</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Lead agenda-based client meetings and conference calls with confidence, positivity and energy</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Lead internal project meetings to provide a vision and next steps for project and client objectives<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>3+ years of experience in a client-facing role</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Bachelor’s degree in communications, PR, marketing or related field</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Up to 10% business travel per year</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience working with healthcare and/or technology brands is preferred</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strong understanding of Google Analytics, WordPress CMS, and social media platforms</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proficient in all common business software including Microsoft Word, Excel, Powerpoint/Keynote, Google Apps, Adobe Acrobat, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strategic and tactical marketing experience in a digital environment with proven ability to drive the execution of marketing campaigns through multiple channels</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proven proactive approach with managing client relationships, staying attuned to marketing and technology plans/needs, seeing problems and offering solutions before they escalate<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
