<h2>Director of Client Development - Enterprise/Regional</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>How We Think About People:</b><br>
PrecisionLender is built on a single overarching belief… that helping our clients succeed is the only way to ensure that our company succeeds. Everything we do, every decision we make, reflects that belief.
<br><br>
We start by hiring for character traits like empathy, modesty, respect, trust, reliability, and patience. After that, we stay out of the way and let our people do what's best for the client.
<br><br>
<b>Company Overview:</b><br>
PrecisionLender is modernizing commercial banking. Our sales and negotiation solution empowers bankers with actionable, in-the-moment insights and coaching, so they win better deals and build strong, more profitable relationships. Andi®, PrecisionLender's virtual insights analyst, augments banker strengths and intelligence with the latest technology and data, delivering the best recommendations at exactly the right time. More than 12,000+ bankers at 200+ banks – ranging from $1B to $1T in assets – use our solution.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do Here:</b><br>
As a Director of Client Development, you'll play an integral part in helping identify what prospective clients really care about, what's slowing them down, and how we can help them. In this role, you will have the opportunity to be a part of a team that significantly contributes to the overall growth and success of both our organization and our clients. The ideal Client Development Manager is an outgoing, organized, and thoughtful relationship builder who genuinely cares about people and knows how to inspire action.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Turn sales qualified leads into deep, meaningful, relationships by learning what drives prospects, what keeps them up at night, and offering solutions to their challenges</li>
<li>Guide prospects through every step of the sales process, including: initial discussions, product demos, partnership proposal, due diligence, contract negotiation, and introducing consultants and other staff into the process as needed</li>
<li>Work closely with the marketing, alliances, and communications teams to deliver value at every touchpoint and expand our relationships within a bank</li>
<li>Maintain the integrity of sales systems (CRM) and internal processes through execution and documentation, constantly seeking ways to help us grow and improve</li>
</ul>
<b>What You're Awesome At:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Being an energetic self-starter that excels at working both individually and as a team</li>
<li>Communicating with individuals at all levels of an organization over the phone and via email</li>
<li>Managing multiple tasks simultaneously and being able to keep all those balls in the air</li>
<li>Listening way more than you talk, and always asking questions to better understand</li>
<li>Embracing change and adapting quickly and with a positive attitude</li>
<li>Going the extra mile and working in a team-based setting to create and accomplish goals together</li>
</ul>
