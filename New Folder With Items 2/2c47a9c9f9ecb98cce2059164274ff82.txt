<h2>Senior VP of Retail Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Vireo Health s mission is to build the cannabis company of the future by bringing the best of medicine, engineering and science to the cannabis industry. Vireo s physician-led team of nearly 400 employees provides best-in-class cannabis products and customer experience.
</p>
<p>Vireo is looking for an energetic and dedicated team player to lead a rapidly expanding national portfolio of retail dispensaries across multiple states. The Senior Vice President of Retail Operations will be responsible for managing the day-to-day business operations of existing dispensaries, assisting with the rapid rollout of new dispensaries, and ensuring a consistent customer experience in each Vireo dispensary.
</p>
<p>This is a highly collaborative position in which the Senior Vice President of Retail Operations would work closely with senior colleagues throughout the Company.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Recruit and develop Dispensary Management across all locations</li>
<li>Work with each Dispensary Manager to meet and exceed revenue and expense targets and to develop efficient retail workflows</li>
<li>Monitor each retail store s inventory, staffing, and other operational aspects to optimize store performance</li>
<li>Work with Chief Medical Officer to ensure that Pharmacy Team is providing best-in-class patient care
</li>
<li>Work with Chief Financial Officer and other team members to create and manage budgets for each retail location</li>
<li>Work with Chief Marketing Officer to implement promotions and other marketing/advertising initiatives</li>
<li>Work with eCommerce group to develop, promote and increase retail sales through digital channels</li>
<li>Work with Human Resources Department to negotiate and manager labor union contracts
</li>
<li>Work with Buyer / Planning on purchasing third-party manufactured products
</li>
<li>Work with Real Estate team to inform the design and layout of the company s future retail locations</li>
<li>Ensure all retail dispensaries comply with local, state and federal laws</li>
<li>Support the company s culture, mission and values</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of experience in a brick and mortar retail environment
</li>
<li>Experience managing an annual revenue of $25 million+</li>
<li>Deep experience with retail operations, merchandising, and human resources</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with proven ability to build and maintain relationships with colleagues and vendors</li>
<li>BSc/BA in related field; MBA or other relevant graduate degree is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Additional Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be a minimum of 21 years of age</li>
<li>Must have a legal driver s license and willing to travel</li>
</ul>

<b>Organizational Relationships</b><br>
Reports to Chief Operating Officer. Coordinates activities with Medical, Marketing and eCommerce teams.</p>
