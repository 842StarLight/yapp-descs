<h2>Client Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
ActiveProspect is a marketing SaaS company for online lead acquisition, enabling real-time data decisions. The product suite (LeadConduit, TrustedForm and SuppressionList) handles integrations, data verification, data enhancement, and consent documentation for legal compliance (TCPA). ActiveProspect has processed over 1 billion Internet leads for leading brands across a wide range of industries. Founded in 2004, ActiveProspect is headquartered in Austin, TX.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
ActiveProspect is seeking a Client Success Manager to manage a portfolio of enterprise clients. Successful candidates will be able to manage relationships end-to-end including proposing pre-sales product implementation solutions alongside Sales Directors, owning the client relationship end-to-end, including overseeing onboarding, proactively recommending solutions to meet ever-changing business problems, and identifying upsell and cross-sell opportunities.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Establish and maintain lasting relationships with each and every client in your portfolio, maintain ongoing and proactive communication to ensure needs are continually scoped and met, within and outside of our products.</p></li>
<li><div>
Work diligently to retain and increase revenue from existing clients. Navigate client organizations for new business opportunities, working closely with Sales Directors.</div></li>
<li><div>
Translate business objectives and requirements into specific, recommended product configurations, creating a succinct feedback loop between Product and Support.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure a smooth transition from the moment of sale to a complete onboarding. Understand new client needs and business, documenting the overall process flow for clients, including steps and systems outside of our products. Follow and recommend new processes to ensure a consistent and seamless client on-boarding each and every time.</div></li>
<li><div>
Resolve problems by rolling up your sleeves, diving in and figuring out a solution. Provide technical solutions, product guidance, and best practices, with the help of Support and Product Management.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work cross-functionally with Sales and Support teams to ensure an ideal client experience and program execution.</div></li>
<li><div>
Be an advocate for clients, delivering their feedback requests to help influence the Marketing and Product roadmaps to ensure ActiveProspect services and product offerings evolve with our customers' needs.</div></li>
<li><div>
Drive adoption of new ActiveProspect products and features, ensuring all customers are educated about our capabilities, products, and expertise.</div></li>
<li><div>
Understand our competitive landscape to push for features that differentiate our services.</div></li>
<li><div>
Communicate customer performance or churn indicators to supervisor, escalating immediate concerns.</div></li>
<li><p>
Assist in on-boarding new employees by serving as a product, market, and process expert. Conduct virtual and occasional onsite project meetings, training, and knowledge transfer sessions.</p></li>
</ul>
