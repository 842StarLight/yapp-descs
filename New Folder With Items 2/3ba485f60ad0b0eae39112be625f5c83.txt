Job Title

<br>Hourly

Reports To

Summary
<br>Serves as collection agent for all accounts deemed delinquent. Responsible for collection of collateral and the disposal of repossessed units.
<br><br>
Typical Job Entry Requirements
<br><ul>
<li>• High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>• Prior banking experience with collection experience a plus.</li>
<li>• Computer experience with a working knowledge of word processing and spreadsheet software programs.</li>
<li>• Good written and oral communication skills.</li>
</ul>
Primary Activities
<br>(Essential duties and responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.)
<br><ul>
<li>• Acts as a collection agent on all loan accounts deemed delinquent. This includes all charge-off activity and recoveries.</li>
<li>• Calls on customers with past due accounts and assists customers with financial counseling via phone, mail and home visits as necessary.</li>
<li>• Responsible for the retrieval and disposal of repossessed units by means of auction and/or sale to external customers.</li>
<li>• Assemble charge-off files</li>
<li>• Assist customers with loan cure programs</li>
</ul>
