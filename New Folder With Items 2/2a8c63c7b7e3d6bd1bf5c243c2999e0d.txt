<h2>Digital Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Primary Function:</b><br>
<br>
This role is responsible for the oversight and management of the digital marketing execution for key Affinity clients at Cartus, subsidiary of Realogy Holdings Corp. Realogy Holdings Corp. (NYSE: RLGY) is the leading and most integrated provider of residential real estate services in the U.S. that is focused on empowering independent sales agents to best serve today’s consumers. Realogy delivers its services through its well-known industry brands including Better Homes and Gardens® Real Estate, CENTURY 21®, Climb Real Estate®, Coldwell Banker®, ERA®, Sotheby's International Realty®. Cartus, the market leader in global mobility, offers a full spectrum of global relocation services to more than half of Fortune 50 companies and organizations of all sizes. Innovating and developing new ways to streamline the relocation lifecycle with Cartus developed technology such as LEAP, Leading Edge Analytics Practice, and the MovePro Suite of Tools, means Cartus can efficiently manage and move employees in over 185 countries while assisting with every phase of the relocation process.<br>
<br>
This role will be part of the Global Marketing team at Cartus and will be responsible for managing the implementation of digital marketing campaigns to reach the consumer and engage in home buying and home selling programs. The role requires an expertise in managing paid media campaigns including display, paid social and paid search while delivering and maximizing SEO/SEM in both approach and execution. The role will own the direct relationship with our digital advertising/media agency to develop creative and will be responsible for the execution of all paid media campaigns across all digital channels. This includes liaising with the Realogy brand teams to ensure synergy and alignment for all branded marketing efforts. Additional responsibilities include ownership of campaign performance and provide weekly/monthly KPI and metrics to ensure efficient and effective spend.<br>
<br>
<b>Major Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
The Manager of Digital Marketing will:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Manage the digital marketing for key affinity partnerships including initial planning, creative briefing, implementation, execution, monitoring, optimization, analysis and reporting</p></li>
<li><p>
Leverage performance-based media channels by building and executing digital media campaigns that include paid search, paid social, display, video ads, and emerging new media to drive leads and conversions</p></li>
<li><p>
Leverage opportunities that are unique to our industry including development of a social media influencer strategy that aligns with key objectives of our partner programs</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage post-campaign reporting and help identify key learnings in working with emerging platforms &amp; technologies</p></li>
<li><p>
Liaise with the Realogy brands to ensure that we are driving value for our programs and not cannibalizing branded marketing efforts</p></li>
<li><p>
Generate weekly, monthly and quarterly performance reports and dashboards, and recognize and troubleshoot data anomalies</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain an understanding of real estate industry trends and provide relevant insights to key clients on how to better manage media purchases</p></li>
<li><p>
Track and analyze all key metrics and KPI for all campaigns, social media, SEO &amp; SEM success</p></li>
<li><p>
Collaborate &amp; manage agencies and other vendor partners as necessary</p></li>
</ul>
