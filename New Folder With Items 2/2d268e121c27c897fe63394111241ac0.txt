<b>Summary:</b><br>
The Turtle Bay Resort resides only 55 minutes from the energetic pulse of Honolulu and Waikiki city, our oceanfront setting immerses guests in the natural tranquility of Oahu's North Shore lifestyle. Oahu's spectacular ocean views are on display from every one of the Resort's 452 magnificent accommodations, including our beach cottages, villas, guest rooms and suites. A luxury beach resort, Turtle Bay also features two lushly landscaped pools, championship golf courses by Fazio and Palmer, tennis courts, horseback riding, hiking and mountain bike trails, a surfing school, world-class dining and shopping.
<br><br>
Director of Finance
<br><br>
<b>BASIC FUNCTION:</b><br>
Seeking qualified, experienced Director of Finance to oversee the financial operations of the iconic Turtle Bay Resort. He/she will provide the strategic leadership required to achieve the fiduciary and quality goals of resort guests, owners, stakeholders and employees. The DOF will ensure the effective and efficient operation of all facets of the resorts while improving on the current model and be responsible for managing in a manner consistent with the mission, operating philosophy, standards and values of the property owners and Benchmark, a global hospitality company, the managing entity.
<br><br>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
Scope of this Position
<br><ul>
<li>Implement and oversee tight financial controls throughout property.</li>
<li>Report financial results to owners and management in accordance with hotel Accounting Principles each month.</li>
<li>Develop annual budget procedures and guidelines each year.</li>
<li>Schedule and conduct annual audit.</li>
<li>Direct the daily, weekly and monthly reporting process in compliance with Management and Ownership requirements.</li>
<li>Develop accurate and timely forecasts including labor management along with other departments</li>
<li>Direct control of costs and expenses in all areas. Task master.</li>
<li>Oversee compliance of local, state and federal laws.</li>
<li>Oversee risk management program, including all insurances.</li>
<li>High degree of owner contact.</li>
<li>Oversee financial training of all management.</li>
<li>In preparing for this position, candidate ideally will have worked in at least the following departments or positions: Hotel/Resort Controller, Assistant Director of Finance, Area Director of Finance</li>
<li>Manage capital expenditures according to budget</li>
<li>ROI calculations for projects and capital expenditures</li>
<li>Strategic analysis on current operations</li>
<li>Strategic planning for future operations and plans including financial modeling development and review</li>
</ul>
