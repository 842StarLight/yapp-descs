<p><b>Providence St. Joseph Health is calling a Technology Consultant PSJH to our location in Renton WA, Beaverton OR or Portland OR.</b></p>
<p>We are seeking a dynamic, self-starter to join our Technology Integration team as a Technology Consultant. This is a great opportunity for the seasoned IT professional with technical experience in Active Directory, Identity Management and Service Operations to contribute to our journey to Health 2.0. Come join our growing team that delivers technology integration initiatives in support of mergers, acquisitions, affiliations, sale of IT services, Epic technology or other efforts enabling collaboration or partnership with third parties</p>
<p>We are seeking a Technology Consultant PSJH to deliver business value by providing technical counsel and project leadership within the IS organization regarding technology integration initiatives and operational activities by leading groups of technologists, as needed, in undertaking these efforts. The work involved is usually highly important to the business objectives of the enterprise; high-risk; and/or time sensitive. All duties must be performed in a manner that promotes team success and reflects PSJH Mission and Values.</p>
<p><b>In this position you will have the following responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Demonstrates effective interactions and positive interpersonal skills in dealing with fellow employees, leaders, stakeholders, other department employees and vendors in order to maximize productivity and positive employee relations including virtual team leadership and interactions at the program level where required.</p></li>
<li><p>
Drives informed decision making about where to invest efforts based on established priorities and available resources. Leads virtual project teams in carrying out projects. Understands business needs and sets accountabilities that meet established goals.</p></li>
<li><p>
Leads multiple large, complex, sometimes high-risk projects. Develops, manages and executes project plans (objectives, goals, scope, schedules resources costs, and dependencies) that are integrated and synchronized with other areas of the business adjusting plans across the business as required to ensure successful delivery of commitments and effective use of resources.</p></li>
<li><p>
Serves as a liaison and technical leader for project-related activity. Manages and integrates groups of diverse disciplines in collaborative activities by acting as key liaison and facilitating cross-function communication.</p></li>
<li><p>
Lead IT initiatives by establishing a clear scope and delivering economic solutions with an emphasis in foundational to full integration services, technology assessments, solution design and service management integration.</p></li>
<li><p>
Lead technical assessment services producing reports, presentations, plans and budgets</p></li>
</ul>

<li><p>
5 years as a subject matter expertise in infrastructure technology, applications, operations or management</p></li>
<li><p>
Demonstrated skills in project plan design and implementation; directing projects, coordinating the work of operational teams, and manager project budgets.</p></li>
</ul>
