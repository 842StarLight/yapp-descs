<h2>Technical Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
The NK Technical Support Specialist assists customers with all aspects of technical support, usage guidance, and problem resolution relating to NK’s sports, weather and shooting products. We believe that excellent service and support is a critical part of our brand reputation, and you will be empowered to ensure that our customers at all levels are successful and happy with their purchase. You will also be the front line for problem reporting to the product team and will assist in defining and testing resolutions to problems. This is at-will employment.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Primary Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide prompt, courteous, knowledgeable and effective assistance to customers’ technical support inquiries by phone, chat and email.</li>
<li>Develop in-depth knowledge of product catalog: features, specifications, applications, configurations and pricing, and provide product selection guidance to customers.</li>
<li>Develop full working knowledge of, and be able to provide step-by-step support for, all functions of all of NK’s current products, including: Rowing and paddling timers and amplifiers, basic weather instruments, specialty weather instruments for firefighting, construction, agriculture and other applications, ballistics calculators for long-range shooting, supporting apps and software, etc.</li>
<li>Travel occasionally to trade shows and competitions to provide field support and light repair services.</li>
<li>Assist customers in obtaining answers to their more advanced technical support issues and product concerns. Escalate issues as needed to Technical Support, Product Management or Engineering.</li>
<li>Develop and apply knowledge of how our customers use the NK/Kestrel products to provide knowledgeable, insightful suggestions to improve their results and product experience.</li>
</ul>
<b>In Support of Customer Service Team:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Accurately enter and process customer orders and payments received via phone, fax, email and web using Sage 100 integrated business software.</li>
<li>Set up new customers following standard procedures and update customer accounts as required.</li>
<li>Confirm availability of stock, quote prices and shipping dates.</li>
<li>Operate multi-line VOIP phone system: answering in-bound calls, transferring calls to the correct person or department, making public address announcements.</li>
</ul>
Personal Qualities for Success in this Job
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Have the patience to handle difficult and repetitive questions.</li>
<li>Focus on accuracy and fixing errors in data where observed.</li>
<li>Take personal satisfaction in providing excellent customer service.</li>
<li>Possess curiosity and empathy for customers and their goals.</li>
<li>Are able to work independently with limited direction and able to work collaboratively and communicate effectively with others in a team environment.</li>
<li>Can think critically to solve problems for troubleshooting.</li>
<li>Have good “on-the-fly” computer research abilities to locate solutions if not personally known.</li>
<li>Have a technical bent and general comfort level with mobile devices, computers, standard office software and other forms of technology.</li>
</ul>
Competencies
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Work on a computer, sitting, for a full work day.</li>
<li>Use computers, e-mail applications and specialized software proficiently.</li>
<li>Enter data into computer systems quickly and accurately.</li>
<li>Operate a phone system and take calls with a positive, professional, pleasant phone manner with clear diction and communication.</li>
<li>Compose business emails using correct grammar, spelling and punctuation.</li>
<li>Research and digest web-based information quickly.</li>
<li>Retain and understand product details in memory and apply to communicate technical features.</li>
<li>Help customers make informed product choices based on their expressed interests.</li>
<li>Possess basic numerical, mathematical and currency proficiency.</li>
</ul>
Education and Experience
<br><br>
<b>Required:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High school degree or equivalent required, AA or BA degree desired.</li>
<li>2 years customer service experience.</li>
<li>Data entry experience.</li>
</ul>
<b>Desired:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>1 or more years in a technical support role of any sort.</li>
<li>4-year technical degree</li>
<li>Demonstrated advanced software proficiency</li>
</ul>

About NK
<br>NK is known worldwide for its waterproof speed measurement, timing and audio systems for competitive rowers and paddlers, used by virtually every school and club program throughout the world from novices to Olympic medal winners. NK’s Kestrel Weather &amp; Environmental Meters are portable weather instruments used by firefighters, military personnel, farmers, hikers, meteorologists, sports teams and thousands of other people who need to know the conditions where they work or play. Our Kestrel Ballistics Meters combine advanced ballistics calculators with accurate on the spot environmental to get military, law enforcement, competitive and recreational long range shooters on target. Our products ARE us - we design them, build them, sell them, and passionately stand behind them.
