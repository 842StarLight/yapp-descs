<h2>Enterprise Account Manager - AEC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The Enterprise Account Manager (EAM) qualifies and presents Autodesk Design &amp; Advanced Solutions Services Solutions to prospects and clients in the Architecture, Engineering and Construction space. The EAM develops an understanding of the decision-making of prospects and clients, conducts design and business process assessments, and delivers clear and consistent value propositions to executive decision-makers.</p>
<p><b>
 ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Full sales cycle from researching accounts to closing business</li>
<li>
Discovering, developing and managing business relationships with prospects and current customers to sell software, services, and other related solutions</li>
<li>
Selling wide and deep into accounts by identifying client needs and opportunities where IMAGINiT products and services will improve client productivity</li>
<li>
Leading account strategy sessions with current clients, prospects and internal teams</li>
<li>
Develop professional solution proposals which include products, services, implementation plans, and business justifications (ROI).</li>
<li>
Maintain an accurate Sales Pipeline, Activity, and Forecast in Salesforce</li>
<li>
Collaborating across IMAGINiT business enterprise to secure customer business</li>
<li>
Increasing IMAGINiT visibility in the industry by attending networking events, using social media, referrals, etc.</li>
<li>
Continuous personal training and development as the market changes moving towards cloud based services and the “internet of things”</li>
<li>
Maintain an accurate Sales Pipeline, Activity, and Forecast in Salesforce</li>
<li>
Up to 50% travel required.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>

<br>
<ul>

<li>
3 – 5 years of professional sales experience selling software or services to manufacturing companies.</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational and time management skills.</li>
<li>
Motivated self-starter.</li>
<li>
Understanding of the manufacturing process.</li>
<li>
Comfortable in business discussions with Senior Management and Executives.</li>

</ul>
