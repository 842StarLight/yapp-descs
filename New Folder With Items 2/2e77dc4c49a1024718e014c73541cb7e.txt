<p>
Summary</p>
<div>
<p>
The Director of Marketing is responsible for the overall strategic leadership and management of the functional areas of the Hotel, including electronic marketing, hotel marketing, print and web production, as well as public relations. Oversee execution of marketing initiatives as stated in the business plan (hotel and restaurant) to support revenue goals, continually seek out opportunities/partnerships for exposure of hotel, restaurant and spa. Handle all communications to the public, guests, and the media as it relates to marketing. And Develop a Guest Experience Program.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This role supports both hotels Ventana Big Sur and Carmel Valley Ranch.<br>
</p>
