<h2>Dispatcher/Customer Service</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Transportation never stops and neither do we. Our hard-working, dispatcher team learns many important responsibilities for our company. Looking for a reliable, detail-orientated Dispatcher ready to learn new challenges in every aspect of our Transportation needs; from our systems, to our customers, to our drivers.</p>
<p>Daily Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate with Drivers to determine pick-up and arrival times for deliveries.</li>
<li>Sustain active real-time communication between all departments within the company.</li>
<li>Ensure all load information and or changes are communicated accurately and quickly to customer service representatives.</li>
<li>Track and Trace all company freight.</li>
<li>Advise in the routing of drivers due to various events; inclement weather or alternate routes.</li>
<li>Work on-site with customers</li>
<li>Build customer revenue; ie: help bring in more customers</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional communication skills, both verbally and written.</li>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>History of reliable Work Performance</li>
<li>Excellent relationship building and planning skills.</li>
<li>Computer skills required.</li>
<li>McLeod Trucking Software Knowledge desired.</li>
<li>FMSCA hours of Service knowledge a must.</li>
<li>customer rapport and knowledge to bring to the table.</li>
</ul>
