<h2>Front Desk Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Summary
<br>The Front Desk Manager manages all aspects of the department including but not limited to operations, planning, in accordance with company’s policies and procedures. The Front Desk Manager provides leadership and support to all members of the Office, implements and enforces the MyEyeDr. Standards of Excellence in all areas supervised. The Front Desk Manager may be required to provide pretesting, eyeglass sales and contact lens support based upon Office volume and supervise Patients Service Coordinators.
<br><br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities
<br>Operations
<br><ul>
<li>Smart appointment scheduling. Fluent with phone scripts and price quotations.</li>
<li>Billing of accounts to include accurate statements and envelope processing.</li>
<li>Complete and audit insurance forms before daily mailings.</li>
<li>Insurance book kept updated and accurate.</li>
<li>Keep front desk supplies stocked and inventoried.</li>
<li>Responsible for three major insurance receivables. Ability to establish a contact person and knowledge of the resubmittal procedure. Run reports to calculate percentage.</li>
<li>Knowledge of all explanation of benefits forms.</li>
<li>Delegate some insignificant duties.</li>
<li>Audit and edit insurance claims through Enterprise for submission to Home Office.</li>
<li>Able to troubleshoot entries seen on the Office Production Report in detail.</li>
<li>Start cross training with Technicians or Opticians.</li>
<li>Can process refund and write-off requests in a timely fashion.</li>
<li>Oversee funds collection at date of service and/or dispensing.</li>
<li>Excellent patient relations.</li>
<li>Ensure that all yearly exams complete a recall card, record month and year in upper right corner of card. Forward to Home Office at month end.</li>
<li>Close &amp; balance POS software program at day end, completing daily deposit, ensuring that it properly balances.</li>
<li>Fax daily sales summary report to Home Office on a daily deposit form prior to 12:00 p.m. Maintain cleanliness and organized appearance of front desk always.</li>
</ul>
Managerial Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Knowledge of all insurances and able to file all claims.</li>
<li>Superb patient relations to include complaints and problem solving.</li>
<li>Familiar with optical terminology and application.</li>
<li>Can process all ledger entries and understand how it applies to the Office Production Report.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and correct all computer entries made in error.</li>
<li>Can perform at mid-level optician and technician levels.</li>
<li>Delegate and oversee all front desk projects.</li>
<li>Willing and capable of substituting at other offices.</li>
<li>Excellent Accounts Receivable collection.</li>
<li>Enforcing funds collection at date of service and/or dispensing.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and correct all money discrepancies.</li>
<li>Able to quote all prices to the detail involving insurance coverage and patient history.</li>
<li>Oversee office patient flow and recognize a timeline</li>
<li>Willing and able to train new front desk employees.</li>
<li>Can orchestrate any appointment schedule change, update or rescheduling.</li>
<li>Complete phone triage to include ophthalmic referrals.</li>
<li>Initiate training in the contact lens area until Contact Lens Tech is available.</li>
<li>Help maintain the attractive appearance of the office.</li>
<li>Attend all office meetings.</li>
<li>Participate in all training opportunities offered by MyEyeDr.</li>
<li>Other duties, as assigned.</li>
</ul>
Customer Service
<br><ul>
<li>Maintain company’s goal to “Exceed the Customer/Patient’s Expectation” which will be monitored by mystery visits, number of patient complaints, resolution of customer issues, phone answering and greeting</li>
<li>Respond to and resolves any patient concerns or complaints; reports any complaints that cannot be resolved at the General Manager level to the Regional Manager or Vice President of Business Development</li>
<li>Promote company brand to associates, patients, &amp; community</li>
</ul>
Work Experience
<br><ul>
<li>At least 1+ years of supervisory experience with increasing levels of responsibility.</li>
</ul>
Education
<br><ul>
<li>AA or BA/BS in related field preferred.</li>
</ul>
Job Skills, Knowledge, Abilities
<br><ul>
<li>Demonstrated success working and managing in a retail, office environment addressing a broad spectrum of customer requirements and preferences.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to effectively manage inventory control, sales controls and staffing controls to maximize the profitability of the office.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to drive sales growth and proactively address changing trends.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to establish positive patient rapport and build relationships to establish repeat customer-trends.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills (oral and written) with the proven ability to manage priorities in a fast paced, entrepreneurial environment.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, set priorities and ensure that patient commitments/deadlines are met.</li>
<li>Proficiency in utilization of automated equipment and systems standard to the optometry industry.</li>
</ul>
Working Conditions
<br><ul>
<li>This position is performed in a traditional retail office environment.</li>
<li>This position requires the ability to communicate and exchange information, utilize equipment necessary to perform the job, and move about the office.</li>
</ul>
