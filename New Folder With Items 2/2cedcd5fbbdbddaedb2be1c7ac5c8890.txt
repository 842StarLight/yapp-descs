<h2>Chemist - Road Science</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>OVERVIEW
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>As a Chemist, you will be responsible for developing innovative solutions to solve our customers unmet needs using our Road Science anti-strip product line. You will conduct both laboratory and field testing to support our asphalt additive product lines.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Evaluates the chemical and physical properties of various organic and inorganic substances (raw materials) in order to investigate their applications to asphalt chemical products.</li>
<li>
Evaluates customer’s needs; interfaces with clients to determine the product's future direction.</li>
<li>
Tests finished products for quality purposes and recommends changes to product specifications as necessary.</li>
<li>
Leads or participates in ongoing research projects, fostering creative ideas and promoting teamwork in the successful completion of assigned tasks.</li>
<li>
Designs and executes test programs, collects and analyzes data, performs sound scientific interpretation of the data, runs pilot tests and industrial tests, writes reports in a clear, concise and logical manner and coordinates information.</li>
<li>
Works with marketing and sales team to help determine the customer’s unmet needs.</li>
<li>
Maintain laboratory records in a neat and organized fashion.</li>
<li>
Work in laboratory safely and in accordance with safety procedures.</li>
<li>
Maintain and calibrate laboratory instrumentation and equipment.</li>
<li>
Develop novel test method to demonstrate Road Science product performance; creating performance standards.</li>
<li>
Writing, revising, and editing documents such as Safety Data Sheets (SDS) and Technical Data Sheets.</li>
<li>
Communicate clear and concise in both written and verbal forms to internal and external customers.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
<li>
Travel up to 25%</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
