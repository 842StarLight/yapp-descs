<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>This Role:</b> As a Product Marketing Manager, you'll be joining a growing team responsible for product positioning, sales enablement, campaign strategy, analyst relations and messaging. This is a great opportunity to have immediate impact supporting sales, influencing analysts and contributing to marketing campaigns. Successful candidates will have a baseline of experience and are looking to grow their career in a fast-paced environment in fast-growing segment of the cyber security market.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop messages for sales tools and marketing assets</li>
<li>Manage product launches including planning, messaging and communication</li>
<li>Create impactful sales tools and support enablement efforts for internal sales teams and channel partners</li>
<li>Develop campaign themes and concepts working with an agency, including messaging, target segments and content assets</li>
<li>Develop a solid understanding of the competitive landscape and develop compelling positioning, messages and sales tools</li>
<li>Communicate with industry analysts to increase awareness and drive a positive perception of Recorded Future</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A BS degree in a related field</li>
<li>2+ years of product marketing experience in enterprise security software</li>
<li>Threat Intelligence, Technology Integration Partners or Third Party Risk experience required</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate across teams in marketing, sales, research, and product</li>
<li>Strong analytical, written, and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Some travel required for internal meetings, conferences, and tradeshows</li>
</ul>
