<h2>Temporary - Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you a Graphic Designer who works well under deadlines, is flexible and can handle multiple, simultaneous projects? If so, this might just be the role for you! This opportunity will allow the Graphic Designer to learn more about the apparel industry along with exposure to photography, typography, video, print, and social media.</p>
<p><b>
 **This is a temporary role that will last till January 2020***</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
This role provides graphic design and production support to the Creative department. Under the supervision of the Creative and Art Directors, the graphic designer designs print and digital marketing, sales, and online materials, as well as performs production duties related to the creation and flow of graphic materials. In collaboration with our Marketing team, this position contributes to creating and executing creative campaigns by designing, engaging and attractive collateral, while maintaining the overall look of the brand.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Please attach or include a link to your portfolio.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create eye-catching graphics and appropriate designs and produce print and digital marketing collateral, catalogs, retail signage, banner ads, landing pages, emails and other brand promotion for multiple in-house brands while meeting deadlines</li>
<li>Ensure all work, both creative and technical, is reviewed for accuracy before given for approval</li>
<li>Source photos, with attention to detail with product and apparel styling</li>
<li>Creates web graphics for house websites and brand social media account</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<ul>
<li>Adobe Photoshop</li>
<li>Adobe InDesign</li>
<li>Mac OS</li>
<li>Adobe Illustrator</li>
<li>Microsoft Word</li>
<li>Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>An acute attention to detail</li>
<li>Keen eye for color</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Experience<br>
<ul>
<li>Associate's degree required. Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, from a four-year college or university, preferred.</li>
<li>1 to 3 years of related experience.</li>
<li>Must have excellent photo composition, layout and typography skills.</li>
<li>Experience in catalog publishing is a plus.</li>
<br>
</ul>
