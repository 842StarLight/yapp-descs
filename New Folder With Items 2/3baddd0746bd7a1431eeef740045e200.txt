<h2>Software Engineer Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you curious, motivated, and forward-thinking? At FIS, you’ll have the opportunity to work on some of the most challenging and relevant issues in financial services and technology. Our talented people empower us, and we believe in being part of a team that is open, collaborative, entrepreneurial, passionate and above all fun.
</p>
<p><b>ABOUT THE TEAM:
</b></p>
<p>FIS is looking for a <b>Software Engineer Specialist </b>to join our <b>Development </b>team in <b>Orlando, FL </b>. This teams supports a variety of state-of-the-art desktop applications.
</p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING? </b>In this role, you will code in a variation of languages and support a variety of desktop applications, as well as create custom workflows for our clients. In addition, you will:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze, design, program, debug and modify software enhancements and/or new products
</li>
<li>Interact with product managers and/or users to define system requirements and/or necessary modifications.
</li>
<li>Participate in software design meetings and analyse user needs to determine technical requirements.
</li>
<li>Write technical specifications based on conceptual design and stated business requirements.
</li>
<li>Write code, complete programming and perform testing and debugging of applications using current programming languages and technologies.
</li>
<li>Complete documentation and procedures for installation and maintenance.
</li>
<li>Train and communicate with internal Client Training, Client Relationship and Sales teams on systems application.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WHAT YOU BRING:
</b></p>
<p><b>The ideal candidate will have </b><b>10+ years of programming/software engineering experience. This is considered an expert level role with team leadership duties.
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>You have experience in a variety of languages to include: C#, HTML5, JavaScript, jQuery, XML, ASP.NET and Jason.
</b></li>
<li><b>You are very organized and detail-oriented
</b></li>
<li><b>You are experienced in a Windows environment
</b></li>
<li><b>You love analyzing issues and devising efficiencies to better the client experience
</b></li>
<li><b>You are looking to join our team and build a long-term career with FIS
</b></li>
</ul>
