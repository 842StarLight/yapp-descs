<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>MoxiWorks, headquartered in Seattle’s historic Pioneer Square District, is a leader in developing web-based tools to empower real estate professionals. We are growing at amazing speed. If you answered “yes” to the three above questions, we want to hear from you! Our company growth is your opportunity! Our employees don’t climb the corporate ladder, we take the corporate elevator.</p>
<p>Product Managers at MoxiWorks are maniacally dedicated to the end user, the constant evolution of products and processes constantly, and the development of product teams. They follow agile principles to coordinate resources and manage stakeholders, and they are expected to perform regular market research, staying immersed in the industry we serve.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with a team of developers, UX designers, and testers to create new products and develop features.</li>
<li>Prioritize a backlog of work, using input from stakeholders and the market.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain product objectives and key results.</li>
<li>Communicate and collaborate effectively with marketing, sales, and account management teams.</li>
<li>Constantly seek feedback and interviews from customers to stay tuned into our end users’ needs.</li>
<li>Distill data and metrics into user narratives and actionable tasks for product teams.</li>
<li>Research users, competitors, and the industry.</li>
<li>Collaborate with fellow Product Managers to manage shared resources and successfully launch large projects.</li>
<li>Present to client principals, MoxiWorks executives, and other key influencers.</li>
<li>Support the sales team as a subject matter expert, and occasionally join sales representatives on client calls.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Three or more years of experience in a product management or program management role, or any type of role that required cat herding in a SAAS environment.</li>
<li>Experience leading agile product teams of UX designers, developers, and testers.</li>
<li>Mind-blowing ability to communicate and collaborate with internal teams.</li>
<li>The inner confidence and motivation to do a kick-ass job without micromanagement.</li>
<li>Organizational skills to break large initiatives into discrete tactics.</li>
<li>Fluency in ticket management tools (JIRA or others).</li>
<li>Experience in the real estate industry is a plus.</li>
<li>Experience playing golf is an extra plus.</li>
</ul>
