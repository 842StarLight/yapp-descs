<p>CPG Category Management Analyst</p>
<p>Department: Client Services</p>
<p>Reports to: General Manager Analytics and Technology</p>
<p>JOB SUMMARY</p>
<p>The essential job functions of the CPG Category Management Analyst are to understand, develop and implement key initiatives within the CPG segment. Accurate reporting and analysis of sales performance across various Channels will be crucial to generate data-driven decisions to keep Burdette Beckmann Inc (BBI) Clients’ competitive in the marketplace and increase overall sales results.</p>
<p>The CPG Category Management Analyst will support BBI Sales Managers and Clients across the U.S. in multiple categories.</p>
<p>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul><li>Utilize and be proficient in Nielsen/IRI syndicated data and analytics to make recommendations on key business issues.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop sales presentations and make recommendations for the sales team utilizing a category perspective.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analysis and recommendation development may include new item distribution, distribution opportunities, shelf space management, merchandising strategies for various markets and customer performance.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review data from multiple sources, perform sales analysis and present findings – tell a story, through the data.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyze SKU productivity and make recommendations that identify potential opportunities to improve sales.</li></ul>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS/REQUIREMENTS</p>
<ul><li>BA/BS degree in Sales, Marketing, Finance or Accounting. Other degrees are maybe acceptable with a combination of retail, buying, and allocation experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>2+ years of related professional of Analytical experience ideally with a CPG company, retailer or syndicated data provider.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be computer proficient in either Nielsen or IRI data. POG software capabilities a plus.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be familiar with shopper insights and be able to identify trends.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Advanced Windows and Microsoft Office and expertise in Microsoft Excel and/or Access are essential (i.e. database management, pivot tables and charts, look-up formulas and macros).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong written, verbal communication and presentation skills are also necessary with the ability to distill complex analyses.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent organization, creative problem-solving, attention to detail, multitasking and a continuous improvement mentality are key qualities for success in this career.</li></ul>
