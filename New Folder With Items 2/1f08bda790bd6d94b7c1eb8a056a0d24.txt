<h2>Marketing Researcher</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">If you are looking for a 6 month, full time, paid internship at the cross-section of Sales and Marketing, Poppulo has the perfect opportunity for you. Join us in our Waltham office as a Market Researcher working at the intersection of the Sales and Marketing functions. In this role, you will have the chance to explore, learn, and create both independently and as part of a team. Our interns make real contributions to the company, take on significant responsibility, and get tremendous real-world experience in both the Sales and Marketing arenas.
<br><br>
<b>The Market Researcher Role:</b><br>
As a Market Researcher, you will help grow our business by assisting the Marketing, Sales, and Data Operations teams implement and refine a global, data-led strategy. Sitting within the Marketing department you will:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Monitor, review, and report on data collected in our Customer Relationship Management and Marketing Automation Platform systems, checking for completion and accuracy</li>
<li>Identify, assess and resolve any data quality issues you find</li>
<li>Enrich the data through external research and collaboration with our Sales and Customer Success teams</li>
<li>Collaborate with our Sales teams to effectively use the data to meet and exceed their quota targets</li>
<li>Support our Management team in utilizing the data to grow our business</li>
<li>Propose and implement improvements to our data quality best practice guidelines</li>
</ul>
<b>To succeed in this role you should have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong analytic skills to effectively review, analyze and manage large volumes of information</li>
<li>Excellent research skills</li>
<li>Outstanding attention to detail and a meticulous, organized approach to work</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>A passion for working with data and information systems and for understanding the impact of data on business processes</li>
</ul>
<b>Technical Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Microsoft Office with advanced knowledge of Excel</li>
<li>Knowledge of Salesforce and Hubspot would be beneficial</li>
</ul>
