<div>
We are currently looking for high energy, driven Enterprise Account Executives with knowledge of sales enablement and solid business to business experience. The Senior Enterprise Account Executive role is responsible for selling our sales enablement, learning, and marketing portfolio of products within the largest of our enterprise accounts. The Enterprise Account Executive’s primary responsibilities focus on executing successful sales strategies that will drive customer value for subscription licenses, and professional services. The Senior Enterprise Account Executive will also act as a mentor to other Enterprise Account Executives on the Enterprise Sales team, and help them develop skills to sell into larger enterprise accounts.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
MindTickle offers a fast-paced, innovative environment where you will be empowered to sell sales enablement, learning, and marketing solutions and be set up for success. Our goal is to build an organization of smart, ambitious Enterprise Account Executives, committed to our mission and focus on winning, but able to balance this with a respectful, healthy environment and lifestyle.

<div><ul>

<li>
Manage and forecast sales activity in salesforce.com, while creating satisfied customers</li>
<li>
Generate new business in existing accounts, across corporate families</li>
<li>
Facilitate communications with other team members and partners such as Salesforce to solve unique customer business challenges</li>
<li>

<li>
Maintain professional and ethical conduct with clients and staff
</li>

</li>
<li>Maintain confidentiality where appropriate as well a high degree of ethics</li>
