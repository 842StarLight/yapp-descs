<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Sell exhibit space in home show(s) via outbound telephone calls and limited face-to-face calls</li>
<li>Sell add-on advertising/marketing opportunities to your customers</li>
<li>Make 50-60 sales calls per day</li>
<li>Meet weekly, monthly and annual sales goals and related targets</li>
<li>Work in CRM program; every sales conversation to be documented and all information to be complete/detailed</li>
<li>Develop and maintain relationships with exhibitors; ensure customer satisfaction and provide excellent service</li>
<li>Through use of our consumer research, understand our attendee demographic and use this information to help sell in the right exhibitors, as well as help our exhibitors do an optimum job reaching their customers</li>
<li>Constantly seek out leads for potential exhibitors from every possible source such as advertisements in other media, internet sources, competitive shows, exhibitors from other Marketplace Events shows, association members, etc.</li>
<li>Constantly build and maintain sales database utilizing existing processes/guidelines for documenting records</li>
<li>Join relevant professional associations, regularly attend meetings and network with your membership</li>
<li>Attend all appropriate competitive shows/events and follow-up on leads immediately</li>
<li>Source local sponsorship leads</li>
<li>Understand our show features, marketing/promotional plan and utilize this information</li>
<li>Provide on-site floor management at the show and assist show management with the coordination of the move-in/move-out of the show</li>
<li>Bring personal energy, enthusiasm and flair as a member of the sales team, willing to put in whatever hours are necessary to get the job done</li>
<li>Ensure all contracts are signed and space is paid in full on time</li>
<li>Collection calls as needed</li>
<li>Travel to company meetings, shows and training</li>
<li>Other duties as required</li>
</ul>

</ul>
