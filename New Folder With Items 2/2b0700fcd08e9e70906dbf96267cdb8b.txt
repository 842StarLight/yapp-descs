<h2>Activations Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Univision is seeking a talented Activations Technician based in Austin, TX. The successful candidate will be responsible for technical and promotional duties, as assigned. This will include, but not be limited to: driving station vehicles, set-up of equipment (sound systems, tents, tables and banners) at station events, and interaction with station listeners.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute radio station promotional activities as directed through our vPromotions system
</li>
<li>Safely operate vehicles, prizes, equipment, and other promotional tools
</li>
<li>Execute creative On-Site Hoopla activation
</li>
<li>Attend regular meetings associated with promotions activities
</li>
<li>Maintain compliance with contests rules and regulations
</li>
<li>Assist in the day-to-day department operations as needed
</li>
<li>Participate in station remotes by greeting and interacting with listeners and clients in an enthusiastic and welcoming manner
</li>
<li>Motivate audiences at remotes by involving them in games and creatively distributing promotional items
</li>
<li>Drive company vehicles to remotes and events as necessary
</li>
<li>Set up equipment and organize set up for remotes
</li>
<li>Interact “live” with on-air talent as required
</li>
<li>Provide direct support to Activations Lead (Supervisors was written before), Director of Operations, Sales Managers and General Manager; assist Sales staff and Programming department
</li>
<li>Prepare good event recaps and reports by taking specific pictures as requested and follows-up with required documentation.
</li>
<li>Establish and maintain a good filing system
</li>
<li>Conduct premium inventory checks on a weekly basis for all stations (vehicles, equipment and promotional items)
</li>
<li>Maintain full compliance on rules, regulations and contract agreements for contest and events (legal requirements)
</li>
<li>Oversee the upkeep and repair of station vehicles
</li>
<li>Work closely and together as a team with other Promotion Lead and Technicians
</li>
<li>Interact professionally with clients and staff. Shows enthusiastic and welcoming personality to everyone
</li>
<li>Abide by organizational norms and supports organizational goals
</li>
<li>Remain calm and professional through stressful circumstances and adapts to different situations.
</li>
<li>Maintain a good driving record
</li>
<li>Timely attendance at all times
</li>
<li>Report all time worked from the previous week within a 48-hour period
</li>
<li>Regular check company communication via company email
</li>
<li>Follow through on required trainings and certifications in a timely manner
</li>
<li>Follows instructions as well as takes steps necessary to prevent mistakes and problems
</li>
<li>Maintains confidentiality at all times
</li>
<li>Performs other’s tasks as directed
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Skills &amp; Experience
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Possess and maintain a good driving record
</li>
<li>Availability to work throughout the week and weekends and rotating schedules as needed
</li>
<li>Able to interact with people all day long
</li>
<li>Professional appearance
</li>
<li>Moderate Microsoft applications skills (Word, Excel) required
</li>
<li>Requires excellent communication and interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>Must be able to lift a minimum of 50 lbs
</li>
<li>Requires highly motivated team player capable of working in a fast-paced environment
</li>
<li>Must be available to work a flexible schedule, primarily evenings and weekends, holiday and days of observance depending on station’s needs
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired Skills &amp; Experience
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Fluent in English and Spanish (read and write)
</li>
<li>High school graduate or GED preferred
</li>
<li>Ability to operate audio console as board operator for live mixes
</li>
<li>Previous work experience in events, marketing, promotions, customer service, sales and/or related field is a plus
</li>
<li>Previous work, volunteer or educational experience in broadcast, entertainment, arts, or advertising a plus
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Eligibility Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be willing to work from office in Austin, Texas
</li>
<li>Employment/education will be verified
</li>
<li>Must have unrestricted authorization to work in the United States
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Requirements </b>(optional)
</p>
<ul><li>Ability to stand, walk, bend, type, and sit for up to (8) hours
</li></ul>
