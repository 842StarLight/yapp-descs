<h2>CSR - Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our Dry Cleaning business is looking for experienced Customer Service Representatives to join our team. The successful candidate for this role will have strong communication skills, sense of urgency, and an understanding of the dry cleaning or garment care business. We offer weekly pay at a competitive rate, in a fully air conditioned plant!</p>
<p>Responsibilities will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Interacting with customers for drop off and pick up of garments.</li>
<li>Managing phone call inquiries, and service requests.</li>
<li>Accurately "tagging" in garments to be cleaned / pressed.</li>
<li>Utilizing the point of sale software to process transactions, input work orders, and trouble shoot when necessary.</li>
<li>Rack and organize cleaned garments.</li>
<li>Maintain the organization and cleanliness of customer service work areas.</li>
<li>Work with management team to stay updated on garment knowledge and company policy.</li>
<li>Daily open and closing of the retail location.</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Skills / Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to remain professional and courteous with customers at all times.</li>
<li>Strong verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze and trouble shoot customer service opportunities.</li>
<li>Consistent and reliable form of transportation.</li>
<li>Effective time management skills.</li>
</ul>
