<h2>QUICKBOOKS CONSULTANT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are seeking a part-time/full-time experienced QuickBooks consultant to engage with our small business clients to provide bookkeeping services and support as needed. The selected individual will work closely with a variety of small business owners to ensure the financial integrity of their business operations are being met through ongoing accurate recording of accounting information using QuickBooks.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate should have a strong understanding of accounting principles and payroll, must be proficient in QuickBooks, and have several years of experience working with small to medium sized businesses. This position requires the ability to work independently and the ability to take instruction/direction well. You will be working directly with clients as a project lead and performing multiple projects at once; therefore, excellent organizational and follow-up skills are required.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities for this position:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide bookkeeping and accounting services, including but not limited to accounts receivable, accounts payable, reconciliation, monthly close process setup, payroll submission &amp; reconciliation, monthly/quarterly payroll and sales and use tax reports.</li>
<li>Conduct client training at client site; troubleshoot systems; evaluate client needs, develop QuickBooks solutions to support existing business processes.</li>
<li>Assist with QuickBooks implementations and provide ongoing training and end-user support and maintenance after implementation.</li>
<li>Independently manage assigned clients, maintain client documentation, and present project status to all external and internal parties.</li>
<li>Perform other functions as assigned in accordance with business needs.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p>
<b>
Required skills, experience, and knowledge:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of detailed experience with QuickBooks Enterprise.</li>
<li>2+ years of experience with QuickBooks Desktop.</li>
<li>Current QuickBooks Desktop Certification or willing to obtain prior to employment.</li>
<li>Current QuickBooks Enterprise Solutions Certification or willing to obtain prior to employment.</li>
<li>Knowledge of accounting practices and principles and proficiency in bookkeeping and general ledger accounting.</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook and Access.</li>
<li>Strong project management skills with the ability to coordinate and prioritize workflow without supervision and perform work accurately under tight deadlines.</li>
<li>Capable of working in a structured environment in a professional setting.</li>
