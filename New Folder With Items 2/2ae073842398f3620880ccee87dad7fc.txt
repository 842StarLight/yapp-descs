<h2>Technical Solutions Engineer - Fire and Safety</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary: </b>Provides technical sales support, design, and estimates for Security and Fire systems.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Supports Sales by communicating with customers, engineers, architects, or other professional and technical personnel about aspects of products or services offered.</li>
<li>Reviews blueprints, plans, and other customer documents to design systems, develop and prepare cost estimates.</li>
<li>Delivers technical presentations.</li>
<li>Responds to technical questions and objections as well as business concerns.</li>
<li>Occasionally will assist in providing technical services to clients relating to use, operation, and maintenance of equipment.</li>
<li>Design Video, CCTV, Access Control, and Fire Alarm Solutions to a diverse customer market, both locally and nationally</li>
<li>Perform camera placement for CCTV systems</li>
<li>Provides technical training to ESCO sales staff.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal, written and interpersonal skills, including ability to communicate at technical and non-technical levels.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of local and wide area networks.</li>
<li>Extensive technical knowledge of specifically assigned systems: Access Control, CCTV, IP Protocols, NFPA Fire and Life Safety system requirements</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, manages multiple priorities, and follows through on projects.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands </b></p>
<p>While performing duties of this job, employee is required to stand, stoop, balance, use hands to handle or feel objects, tools, or controls, and reach with hands and arms. Employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 25 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by the job include close vision, distance vision, color vision, peripheral vision, depth perception, and the ability to adjust focus.</p>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>Three years of direct industry experience in the field</li>
<li>NICET Level II Fire (Preferred)</li>
<li>NICET Level I CCTV (Preferred)</li>
<li>ASIS Physical Security Certifications (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
