<h2>Member Services Team Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>This position will be responsible for dealing with all member accounts of the Asheville Racquet Club South and Asheville Racquet Club DT. The primary duty is to diligently work on member accounts so that each member account is set up to bill properly, resulting in all monies being successfully collected by ARC. This position requires the ability to be a great communicator and the ability to follow through with all duties. This position also requires a strong computer skillset in order to navigate through our software. A top priority will be to effectively work with a team of employees in the member services and sales departments and provide the upmost customer service to our members and affiliates.</p>
<p>Some job responsibilities include:</p>
<p>* Implementing established policies and procedures.</p>
<p>*Implementing departmental projects and goals</p>
<p>*Ensure audited accurate reporting deadlines are met; including:</p>
<ul>
<li>P&amp;L Reports: Aging, NSF, Comp, Billing Summary</li>
<li>Membership Snapshot</li>
<li>EOM Reports: New Members, Cancelled Members, Expired/Renewed PIFs, Membership Changes, Membership Refunds, YTD Membership Charts.</li>
<li>Front desk and caf√© drops for ARC South and ARC DT</li>
</ul>
<p>*Collaborate with Member Services Team Associate to ensure NSF communication guidelines are followed.</p>
<p>*Focus on hitting department monthly collection goals.</p>
<p>*Termination of members for non-payment.</p>
<p>*Demonstrate sound judgment, professionalism and optimism while handling more sensitive</p>
<p>member issues.</p>
<p>*Research and approve daily member services request; Refunds, Medical Holds, ARF Forms, early Termination request, and disputes turned into CC/Banks by members for monthly charges.</p>
<p>*Approve, Implement, and author payment plans or settlements for members with large past due balances.</p>
<p>*Extensive knowledge of ARC processes and procedures, including but not limited to</p>
<p>company used software; Club Automation, Outlook, Word and Excel.</p>
<p>*Coordinate with Sales Team to create and implement membership policies and procedures.</p>
<p>*Coordinate with Sales Teams on monthly commission reporting.</p>
<p>*Review contracts ensuring membership program integrity.</p>
<p>*Consistently communicate clear expectations, adding accountability from sales teams.</p>
<p>*Do weekly cash register drops for front desk and cafe of both locations; including reconciling receipts with actual drops and ensuring the accuracy of transactions</p>
