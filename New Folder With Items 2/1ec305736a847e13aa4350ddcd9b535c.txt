<h2>Manufacturing Master Scheduler/Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Reports To: </b> <b>Supply Chain Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>The Manufacturing Master Scheduler/Planner is a key member of the Supply Chain team, responsible for establishing production scheduling and ensuring actual production meets scheduled requirements.<br><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare daily and weekly production schedules and follows up with performance against estimates.</li>
<li>Forecast materials required to support forecasted production through work order management.</li>
<li>Track progression of production work orders investigate factors that affect schedules and modify plans as necessary.</li>
<li>Participate in continuous process improvement activities related to production planning and inventory management.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Operations and Logistics to optimize warehouse space and production line resources.</li>
<li>Play and integral role in new implementation and phase out of discontinued equipment with purchasing.</li>
<li>Utilize reporting tools in order to analyze trends, efficiency and plan future production orders.</li>
<li>Partner with Manufacturing, Purchasing and Quality Control to determine and prioritize processes, considering factors such as equipment, product specifications and raw material availability to facilitate production and shipping activities, or eliminate delays.</li>
<li>Communicate production issues and assist in the implementation of both short- and long-term solutions.</li>
<li>Work with Sales, Operation, Supply Chain and Engineering Management to develop log range forecasting process</li>
<li>Assist in monitoring, updating and making recommendations to achieve an accurate production forecast.</li>
<li>Communicate Proactively with customer service and sales to meet demand and establish effective relationships.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent Organizational Skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal/written skills</li>
<li>2 years minimum experience working in a purchasing or scheduling position within a manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>APICS training and certification preferred.</li>
<li>Desire to work in a fast paced, changing environment implementing a lean enterprise.</li>
<li>Fluent with the Microsoft Suite of products.</li>
<li>Experience with SAGE ERP preferred.</li>
</ul>
