<p><b>About BLDG Management</b><br>
<br>
BLDG Management is a Denver-based real estate management company specializing in multi-family and commercial property operations. Our owner-operator approach focuses on maximizing investment performance and is built on a culture of great people delivering an exceptional customer service experience.<br>
<br>
Why does BLDG feel different? Why do our properties outperform? A team of really, really great people! BLDG’s values system provides for a very demanding culture where going the extra mile is the rule not the exception. Hustle and a pursuit of greatness are rewarded, and mediocrity is challenged. We regularly refuse to compromise when compromise might be easier in the short run. Mostly, we care. A lot. The forces of mediocrity push hard in our business. BLDG pushes back.<br>
<br>
<b>Why we need you as our Leasing Associate</b><br>
<br>
BLDG is currently seeking a team oriented Leasing Associate. We are seeking a new employee who is not only willing to participate in our family-style environment, but within the whole of BLDG’s family which extends across the entire portfolio and home office. This position requires the desire to work in a team environment to support the company goals of signing new leases, customer retention (renewals) and customer service and accountability. We are looking for applicants who are interested in investing themselves in a rewarding and exciting career with room for growth.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
<b>Details of your position</b><br>
<br>
Hours: 40 hours per week; Schedule set Monday-Saturday, 8:30 am - 5:30 pm. Two scheduled days off/week<br>
Pay: DOE, bonus potential.<br>
Benefits: All full-time employees are awarded health, dental, and vision insurance.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
<b>Your responsibilities</b><br>
<br>
Interact directly with prospective and current residents to achieve maximum occupancy at the properties.<br>
<br>
Enter and update all lease activities in Property Solutions and generate leasing activity reports and availability list.<br>
<br>
Field phone calls, ascertain needs and qualifications and schedule appointments and showings for prospective tenants.<br>
<br>
Discuss availability of apartments, and accompany prospective tenants to walk through the available apartments.<br>
<br>
Process application, ensuring that it is completed in its entirety, obtaining all supporting documents and required information, and collecting the necessary fees and deposit.<br>
<br>
Follow up with applicants and prospects with phone calls, emails and letters.<br>
<br>
Prospect for new tenants with pro-active and creative marketing efforts, such as hosting an open house, sending/distributing mailers, launching referral program etc.<br>
<br>
Walk through the apartment with tenants on move-in day, take move-in pictures and have tenants sign move-in report for apartment; administer execution of lease form/agreements, obtain tenant's renter's insurance information, collect refurbishing fee, first month's rent and deliver keys.<br>
<br>
Initiate the development of advertising and marketing plan for community, and present to management for implementation. Seek new outlets and venues for broader market outreach;<br>
<br>
Prepare and present competitive property and market analysis to management on a monthly basis.<br>
<br>
Travel to local properties/apartment communities, as needed.<br>
<br>
<b>What you will bring to BLDG</b><br>
<br>
High School diploma or above.<br>
<br>
Minimum 2 years of experience in marketing, sales, customer service, leasing.<br>
<br>
Strong written and communication skills.<br>
<br>
Strong sales, time management, follow-up and organizational skills.<br>
<br>
Intermediate or advanced skills in Microsoft Office products (Word, Excel, Outlook, Publishing).<br>
<br>
Working Knowledge or ability to learn property management software (such as property solutions).<br>
<br>
Highly motivated, service oriented, self-starter.<br>
<br>
Available to work on weekends and evenings, required.<br>
<br>
Must have own vehicle and a valid driver’s license.<br>
<br>
All applications are greatly appreciated although only candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.<br>
