<h2>Project Manager, Campaigns & Products</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for an experienced Project Manager with a background in influencer marketing and campaign management. The Project Manager will be responsible for ensuring we reach our goals, whether they be client programs or internal projects. This role will be responsible for keeping our growing team in check through the full project life-cycle.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul><li>Act as the day-to-day contact for assigned clients on project needs from strategy ideation to key milestone communication</li></ul>
<ul><li>Interface between the client and internal teams to ensure clients goals are communicated and achieved</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with multiple departments to scope and estimate projects based on requirements.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create and manage timelines; identifying project milestones, individual tasks and resource allocation</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop client estimates and Statements of Work</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in the recruitment of specialized resources based on project and program needs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage internal and external resources to ensure project schedule, budget, quality and specification are met while monitoring hours spent internally and externally</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track campaign performance against set KPI’s and provide solutions based on results</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead the charge to identify improvements within the process, raise process issues, and identify resource allocation issues or conflicts.</li></ul>
<p>Desired Skills and Experience</p>
<ul><li>Minimum of 2 years experience as a project manager within the marketing or advertising space</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum of 1 year experience managing Influencer campaigns</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highly proficient in Sales CRM’s including Salesforce, Pipedrive or similar.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highly proficient in Google Suite - Sheets, Docs, Google Calendar and Gmail</li></ul>
<p>About MoreYellow<br>We are proud to work small, while serving the large. In under 10 years with an average of 10 people, we’ve been entrusted to work on the largest programs for the best entertainment and technology brands. Brands the likes of Dreamworks Entertainment, Disney, Warner Bros., NBC Universal, Logitech, NVIDIA and ASUS.</p>
