<p>eCommerce Item Setup &amp; Business Analyst</p>
<p>Position Summary</p>
<p>This position is primarily responsible for elevating the on-site experience for an expanding catalog of items across 10+ national and private label brands in the General Merchandise area. Primary focus centers on gathering and providing accurate ad hoc reporting and analysis through the use and development of multiple interactive analytical tools pertaining to all areas of the business (retail sales, on-site availability, content accuracy, and market share) to assure maximum sales potential is realized. Advanced analytical skillset and deep knowledge of information systems is required.</p>
<p>This role also manages new item setup &amp; works closely with Walmart.com category specialists and internal sales &amp; marketing teams to highlight and address product page content health issues affecting sales.</p>
<p>Essential Duties and Responsibilities of the position:</p>
<ul><li>Generate weekly and ad-hoc reports to monitor retail sales data and trends across a number of different systems and data sources.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyze, track, and recommend action based on content quality and improvements with regards to increases in sales conversion.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate potential site issues and challenges impacting sales or requiring adjustment of procedures to maximize potential. Requires analysis of the site from a customer's perspective.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Own new item setup and collaborate with merchandising teams to assure that setup is completed on-time and without error.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Submit SKU maintenance forms to assure timely transfer and/or merging of User Generated Content (UGC) for item platform transitions, addition of color extensions, or regulatory changes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Quarterly review of current site technologies, procedures, and 3rd party vendor</li></ul>
<p>capabilities, future site changes, and industry trends.</p>
<p>Skills / Knowledge / Experience / Education</p>
<ul><li>BA degree in Information Systems, Business administration, or work experience in related field.</li></ul>
<ul><li>2+ years of detailed eCommerce experience preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Expert analytical skillset required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Advanced Microsoft Access, Excel, Power Point skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Familiarity with HTML and web development standards</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to quickly learn new data systems, methodologies, and data visualization software</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working knowledge of VBA - Excel &amp; Access is preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Familiarity with retail systems including Retail Link &amp; Supplier Center</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work independently and manage time efficiently in order to meet changing deadlines and priorities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Superb communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Self-Starter with entrepreneurial attitude</li></ul>
