<h2>Demand Generation Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>About Castlight</b><br>
<div>At Castlight, our mission is to empower people to make the best choices for their health and to help companies make the most of their health benefits. We offer a health benefits platform that engages employees to make better healthcare decisions and can guide them to the right program, care, and provider. The platform also enables benefit leaders to communicate and measure their programs while driving employee engagement with targeted, relevant communications.</div>
<div>
To date, Castlight has partnered with more than 240 large enterprise customers, spanning millions of lives, to improve healthcare outcomes, lower costs, and increase benefits satisfaction.</div>
<br>
<b>
Description</b><br>
Are you ready to be the GM of a marketing machine that delivers more than $500M in annual pipeline?
<br>
<br>
Do you know how to leverage ABM tactics, marketing technology, and high-impact content to engage key decision makers at Fortune 500 companies?<br>
<br>
Castlight is looking for a dynamic demand generation leader to spearhead our B2B marketing efforts. You will be the master of all things pipeline, and you will have numerous tools and resources at your disposal. You will partner closely with our SDR team to amplify their reach, and you will partner with our content marketing, design, and product marketing teams to disseminate high-impact content to our market.<br>
<br>
Ideal candidates must have a solid command of marketing technology, account-based marketing tactics, and Salesforce.com reporting. In addition, we are looking for an all-around athlete who can also support the marketing organization’s broader operational effectiveness and strategic planning.<br>
<br>
<b>Key responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Be the general manager for our digital channel, responsible for more than $500m in annual pipeline</li>
<li>
Strategize, plan, execute, and optimize a set of world-class digital marketing programs (inbound and outbound) that leverage Marketo, Salesforce, and content marketing resources</li>
<li>
Spearhead efforts to list-build, segment, and maintain the data quality of our marketing database</li>
<li>
Project manage cross-functional account-based marketing (ABM) plays in partnership with our SDR team</li>
<li>
Own marketing key marketing reports around pipeline, conversion rates, and campaign performance; improve and manage opportunity attribution processes</li>
<li>
Operationalize lead capture and rapid SDR follow-up from field marketing events, webinars, and other inbound leads</li>
<li>
Manage paid and organic media programs, as well as SEO with support from agencies</li>
</ul>
