<div></div>
<div>The <b>Event Coordinator</b> is tasked with creating WOW guest experiences everyday within Main Events Birthday &amp; Group parties department while driving us towards our financial goals. Our <b>Event Coordinators</b> are often the first point of communication for our guests - and are expected to build and nurture long-lasting relationships with internal and external guests.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>What makes a great Event Coordinator?</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>18 years +
</div></li>
<li><div>The ability to build strong relationships through high-level communication skills, face to face, email and phone</div></li>
<li><div>Proficiency in computer software including Excel, Microsoft Word, and Customer Relationship Management</div></li>
<li><div>A strong attention to detail, strong organizational skills and the ability to focus under pressure</div></li>
<li><div>A spark of creativity and an innovation driven mindset</div></li>
<li>A desire to learn and grow within the company</li>
</ul>
<div><b>What will you be doing on a daily basis?</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Exceeding budgeted group sales targets under the Sales Managers direction, community outreach, handling inbound phone calls, and upselling our most inclusive packages.
</div></li>
<li><div>Embracing teamwork by assisting the Sales Manager in their development and execution of quarterly sales plans to maximize corporate and social market segments.</div></li>
<li><div>Developing and growing as an Event and Sales professional by attending and engaging in weekly sales meetings and by sharing strategic ideas to enhance the business.</div></li>
<li><div>Running it like you own it by enthusiastically communicating with all guests via face to face, phone, and email and by taking ownership of all follow-up.</div></li>
<li><div>Holding yourself accountable for measurable, high-quality, and timely results.</div></li>
<li><div>Proactively engage in outbound sales strategy/activity in an effort to grow event sales.
</div></li>
</ul>
