<p>About Moxie</p>
<p>Moxie Solar is one of the country's premier solar and energy savings company. We are seeking personable, self-motivated, customer oriented professionals to our exciting new department. We offer custom start-to-finish solutions for our commercial, agricultural and residential customers. Moxie was ranked the #1, #2 and #3 fastest growing company in the corridor in the last three consecutive years. <p>JOB SUMMARY</p>
<p>The District Marketing Manager is responsible for increasing regional exposure of Moxie Solar and its market share by generating new leads. New leads will be generated through canvassing, malls, shows, exhibits and any required advertising needs within a wide variety of venues to help all branches meet or exceed their sales goals.</p>
