<p><b>The Scoop:</b></p>
<p>The Clinical Programs Operations Manager provides oversight and management of the operational workflows necessary throughout the lifecycle of clinical products and services sold to MedImpact Clients including opportunity analysis, product sale, implementation, maintenance operations and reporting. The position supports the overall vision, goals, and objectives for the Health Services department and liaisons across departments to ensure that Health Servicesâ€™ assigned Business Unit Initiatives, are successful, meet corporate operational excellence criteria and yield revenue to meet target goals.</p>
<p><b>What You Get To Do:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides day-to-day management of workflows necessary to deliver clinical programs, products and services</li>
<li>Responsible for developing and maintaining operational plans</li>
<li>Responsible for developing and maintaining implementation questionnaires and managing the successful implementation of clinical products</li>
<li>Assists Client Teams in completing opportunity analysis questionnaires, implementation questionnaires, work orders, submission of billing codes and validation of test claims</li>
<li>Manages the day-to-day operations of ongoing clinical products including submission of benefit change requests and delivery of product communications materials</li>
<li>Manages the maintenance and updating of product toolkits to ensure that Sales and Account Management have resources that facilitate the sale of clinical management products</li>
<li>Support business process improvement initiatives, including evaluating and documenting processes and workflows, identifying gaps, and developing recommendations for improvement</li>
<li>Serve as a liaison between Health Services functional areas and ancillary business units, including Information Technology, Operations and Business Development to ensure timely project or product timelines</li>
<li>Complete all documentation throughout the product or project lifecycle, including creating project charters, business cases, business requirements, functional requirements, etc.; Obtain appropriate approvals at each phase of the project</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Need:
</b></p>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience</b></p>

<li>Ability to manage multiple, concurrent projects</li>
</ul>
