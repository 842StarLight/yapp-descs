<p><b>ROLE</b></p>
<p>As a Soaren Email Marketing Specialist you will work with other technical departments to ensure optimal deliverability of our email and text sends. You'll work directly with our customers to provide reporting, best practices, and recommendations to improve their email and text experience. The ideal candidate will be an expert in monitoring and alerting on inbox thresholds, and has an in depth knowledge of ISP’s.</p>
<p><b>Areas of expertise should include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communication - you should excel at both written and verbal communication. You should be able to understand concepts and design and document them appropriately.</li>
<li>Prioritization - you should be able to align with priorities as provided and be nimble and autonomous enough to adjust as priorities change. You should be capable of formally managing against timelines and communicate when timelines may need to shift for deliverables.</li>
<li>Analysis - you should have strong competency in analyzing both data and processes, and report on findings appropriately.</li>
<li>Agile – we are agile and you should be too. Things change rapidly and we have to adjust constantly.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides strategic guidance and recommendations on improving email deliverability and recipient engagement, improving program performance, best practices.</li>
<li>Ensure inbox deliverability for various types of messages, including transactional messages, promotional messages, and notifications.</li>
<li>Monitor delivery, inbox placement, engagement metrics, create plans to improve inbox placement, and IP reputation.</li>
<li>Oversee list cleaning and sunset policies.</li>
<li>Be proactive in gaining knowledge of new industry trends, emerging technology, ISP treatment of commercial email, industry groups, and recipient behavior.</li>
<li>Conduct in-depth investigations, reviews, and audits into email sending practices and provide actionable insights to improve deliverability.</li>
<li>Provide recommendations on additional tools to improve monitoring</li>
<li>Reactively troubleshoot deliverability issues for rapid resolution; including blacklists, spam traps and issues with ISPs.</li>
<li>Provide in-depth analysis of performance quality measurements.</li>
<li>Responsible for all aspects of email compliance in accordance with CAN-SPAM, CASL, and GDPR.</li>
<li>Monitor and improve the reputation of our sending infrastructure.</li>
<li>Intimate knowledge of reputation management strategy and KPIs.</li>
<li>Setup daily, weekly and executive level reports that track deliverability KPIs (inbox %, bounces, spam complaints, open rates, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications: </b></p>

<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology, or similar, or equivalent industry experience.</li>
</ul>
