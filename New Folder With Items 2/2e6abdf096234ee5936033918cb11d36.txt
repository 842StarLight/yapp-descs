<p><b>Position Overview:</b></p>
<p>As an Account Executive with Vendavo, you will be responsible for your territory across our four core vertical markets. You will be responsible for quarterbacking your opportunities throughout the funnel, generating leads, and executing on a close plan with support of the broader pre-sales team. The successful candidate has a proven track record of success in the following areas:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>As a Account Executive, you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You not only understand, but have demonstrated success in selling in a multi-touch, complex sale cycle.</li>
<li>You have demonstrated your ability to facilitate and orchestrate sales deals end-to-end.</li>
<li>You have demonstrated success working with C-Suite in the fortune 1000 market, selling technology and business value.</li>
<li>You are highly networked and know the territory and have contacts with in the geographical region assigned to you.</li>
<li>You have a proven track record of success with meeting or exceeding quarterly sales goals.</li>
<li>You have professional C-level presentation and communications skills</li>
<li>You have a proven ability to work with a small, growing, fast-paced company. Prior experience working in Private Equity a plus.</li>
<li>You not only understand the compensation plan, but are driven and focuses on executing to achieve results.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you need to be successful in this role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>7-10 years of enterprise software sales experience with 3-5 years experience selling into relevant markets with in chemicals, wholesale distribution, industrial manufacturing or high tech within the past 5 years.</li>
<li>Experience selling Business Intelligence, big data solutions, CPQ and/or pricing experience in B2B is a big plus.</li>
<li>Strong networking and relationship selling experience and pipeline management in the territory of this position.</li>
<li>You are self-organized and have a good balance of independence and collaboration.</li>
<li>You are outgoing, team-oriented and personable. You know how to roll up your sleeves and get scrappy. You are focused, passionate, and aggressive about exceeding your plan.</li>
<li>Experience using and managing your business in Salesforce is a plus</li>
<li>A technical degree is a plus.</li>
</ul>
