<h3 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b><i>Job</i></b><b> Description</b>
</h3>
<p>We are looking for self-motivated, service-oriented individuals who would be excited by a ground floor opportunity in the rapidly growing Field Automation Software field. myServiceForce launched a dynamic cloud computing service supporting mobile service companies in the small to medium field service industry. With millions of work orders processed and tens of millions of mobile synchronizations - the software has been fully production worthy for many years. The product portfolio is now in expansion mode.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The <i>job</i> will entail supporting, remotely by phone and computer connection, sales support, client activation, and ongoing customer support including Tier 1 and 2 trouble resolution. Work hours may include Day, Evening, and On-Call.</p>

<ul>
<li>Customer Service, either direct or call center
</li>
<li>General <i>knowledge</i> and competency in common software programs, e.g., Microsoft Excel, Word, etc.</li>
<li>Some sales or sales support <i>experience</i>
</li>
<li>Past <i>experience</i> in cellular, software support, or a field service industry (e.g. HVAC, Lawn or Pool Maintenance, etc.) is helpful, but not necessary</li>
<li>Competency in Spanish is also helpful</li>
</ul>
