<h2>Inbound Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
etailz aims to be the one-stop shop for everything ecommerce. We empower aspiring, existing, inspirational, and entrepreneurial brands to dominate online marketplaces through our innovative technology, tailored strategies, and mutually beneficial partnerships. Since our founding in 2008, we’ve generated over $750 million in sales on Amazon’s third-party marketplace, the largest and fastest growing segment of retail.
<br><br>
The etailz Inbound Marketing Coordinator helps coordinate campaigns for the department’s services including influencer campaigns, social ads, social media management, Amazon livestreams, Amazon coupons, and Amazon giveaways. This includes launching, optimizing, and reporting upon campaign performance. The Inbound Marketing Coordinator works with internal teams to build proposals and vital resources for partners that are mutually beneficial for both the partner and etailz.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Work with internal teams to recruit additional clients to participate in marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>Manage inbound marketing campaigns from start to finish to drive success, generating a positive ROI for both the client and etailz.</li>
<li>Analyze and assess the performance of campaigns and translate quantitative and qualitative data into recommendations and plans for revising the campaign strategy.</li>
<li>Understand and present the value proposition of etailz and inbound marketing both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Assist in creating partner-facing resources to educate potential partners on the department’s services.</li>
<li>Assist in managing several social media pages, including post creation and editorial calendar management.</li>
<li>Strategize with etailz’s Private Label team and implement inbound marketing campaigns to promote and grow private label brands.</li>
<li>Conduct tests to identify and prove best practices.</li>
<li>Support the team in growing future marketing services as opportunity is identified.</li>
<li>Other duties, as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing/Advertising/Communications preferred.</li>
<li>1+ years’ experience in digital marketing, preferably with experience in influencer and/or social media advertising.</li>
<li>Previous experience in social media management.</li>
<li>Strong awareness of influencer marketing and social media trends.</li>
<li>Exceptional Excel skills.</li>
<li>Excellent presentation, organization, and relationship building skills.</li>
<li>Superior verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work well in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Expert problem solver that thrives and welcomes a changing environment.</li>
<li>Attention to detail and goal-oriented.</li>
<li>Works well independently and as part of a team.</li>
<li>Applicants must be currently authorized to work in the United States for any employer. etailz, Inc., uses E-Verify to confirm employment eligibility of all newly hired employees.</li>
</ul>
</div>
