<h2>Sales Development Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are seeking a full-time Sales Development Associate to build our customer base and overall sales. We are looking for an entrepreneurial minded person with a go get 'em attitude to connect with potential customers, introduce the company and products, and build relationships. The position reports directly to the Director of Sales and Marketing.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Qualify leads from marketing campaigns as sales opportunities</li>
<li>Contact potential clients through cold calls and emails</li>
<li>Present our company to potential clients</li>
<li>Identify client needs and suggest appropriate products/services</li>
<li>Build long-term trusting relationships with clients</li>
<li>Proactively seek new business opportunities in the market</li>
<li>Attend Industry Trade Shows and Conferences representing Copper Range</li>
<li>Set up meetings or calls between (prospective) clients and the Sales Team</li>
<li>Report to the Sales and Marketing Director on (weekly/monthly/quarterly) sales results</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date with new products/services and new pricing plans</li>
<li>Work with Marketing on improving and perfecting Sales message, based on your interactions with potential customers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma</li>
<li>2-4 years Sales/Retail/Service or other customer-facing experience</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Basics of Microsoft Office (mostly Powerpoint, Excel, Word)</li>
<li>Interest in software and technology</li>
<li>Ready to learn</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Extras that help:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate or Bachelor's degree in Communications, Business, or related</li>
<li>Knowledge of Construction and/or Manufacturing Industries</li>
<li>Lead Generation experience</li>
<li>Experience with a CRM software</li>
</ul>
<p>If you enjoy selling the latest technologies, get jazzed by helping to build a meaningful enterprise, and want considerable say in your future, then please send us your resume with a cover letter right away to careers@copperrange.com.</p>
</div></div>
