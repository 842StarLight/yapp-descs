<p><b>Impact You'll Make:
</b></p>
<p>We are seeking a self-driven and high performing individual to support Transunion’s powerful solution: PRAMA. In this dynamic role, the Client Implementation Specialist will lead the onboarding and support of new and existing PRAMA customers with a focus on creating a seamless customer experience. Our team embraces an open idea-oriented and collaborative environment that fosters your individual contribution and independence to make an impact.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
Our core customers are in the financial services industry and this role is critical to helping our customers leverage the power of PRAMA to make business decisions in a fast paced and competitive marketplace. In this position, your impact includes the following:
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead the onboarding of new PRAMA customers to ensure a seamless onboarding experience
</li>
<li>Support existing customers, helping them understand PRAMA solution capabilities and how to leverage the power of the PRAMA solution suite
</li>
<li>Identify and implement process improvements in the onboarding and customer support process
</li>
<li>Assist in validation and testing of PRAMA solutions
</li>
<li>Help us build a better product by providing insights from your front-line customer interactions to help us enhance PRAMA solutions to meet customer needs
<br>
</li>
</ul>
