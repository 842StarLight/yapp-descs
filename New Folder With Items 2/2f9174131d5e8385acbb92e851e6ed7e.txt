<b>Benefits</b>:<br>
This is a full-time unclassified state position with full state retirement and insurance benefits, including paid health, dental, life, and disability insurance for the employee. The employee will receive a benefit allowance to assist insurance cost. Annual leave of 10 hours per month and sick leave of 10 hours per month begin accruing immediately.</p>
<p><b>
Basic Purpose</b>:<br>
Positions in this job family are assigned involving the supervision and management of business and support service activities of a state agency, institution or facility. This includes establishing and maintaining accounting procedures, fiscal reporting methods, procedures for payment of invoices and other expense vouchers, and other related activities. It may also include supervision of other business functions such as personnel, supply, procurement, and administrative services.</p>
<div>
<b>
Essential Functions</b>:<br>
<ul>
<li>Preparing annual budget for projects. Compiles data related to financial transactions and operations, revenues and expenditures.</li>
<li>Daily maintenance &amp; analysis of accounting data using The IEMS, CRIS, DEAMS, and ACES computer program software.</li>
<li>Maintain the account/record keeping ledger by entering all purchase orders and</li>
</ul>
Operations and Maintenance expenditures into the required accounting database.<br>
<ul>
<li>Cross check payment vouchers from the department of Oklahoma Military Department with applicable data base to insure all payments are accurate and valid.</li>
<li>Reconcile accounts monthly and verify expenditures balance with state and federal comptrollers.</li>
<li>Coordinating with CE work control to insure man-hours and all costs are captured and reordered per work order.</li>
<li>Match invoices to purchase orders. Transmit purchase orders, utility bills,</li>
</ul>
contract purchases, etc. to the Department of Military Affairs or base for<br>
payment. Invoices must be turned over in a timely manner, one day, to meet State requirements.<br>
<ul>
<li>Assures that all proper approvals and documentation are received before funds are obligated.</li>
<li>Recapitulate the cost of all material used per work order for the O&amp;M and RPS agreements, SRM and MILCON funding. ANG and state computer programs shall be maintained to facilitate this.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</b>:<br>
Required to perform duties assigned at this level include knowledge of accounting principles and practices; of business and public administration; of supply and procurement laws and regulations; of personnel management practices; of managerial functions and techniques; and of the principles and techniques of supervision.
</p>
<p>Ability is required to maintain and report financial data; to direct and supervise the work of others; to communicate effectively; and to establish and maintain effective working relationships with others.
</p>
<p><b>Education and Experience</b>:<br>
Requirements at this level consist of a bachelors’ degree in accounting, business or public administration and one year of professional experience in business management, accounting, or procurement or an equivalent combination of education and experience.</p>
<p><b>
Licenses/Certificates required</b>: None.</p>
