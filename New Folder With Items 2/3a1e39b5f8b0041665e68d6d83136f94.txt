<h2>Part-time Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>PART-TIME LEASING CONSULTANT</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a positive, enthusiastic, self-starting individual who thrives in a fast paced environment to provide the highest level of customer service possible to residents and prospective residents.</p>
<p><b>
Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>
Leasing</b></p>
<ul><li>
Greet prospective residents and provide information on floorplans, availability, amenities and other property information.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Obtain information on prospective leads and continue follow-up procedures according to company standards.</li>
<li>
Provide apartment and property tours to prospective residents.</li>
<li>
Answer telephones in an enthusiastic and professional manner.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Thoroughly familiar with the leasing process from application to lease signing to ensure that the lease is executed correctly.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Understands and complies with Fair Housing Laws and all company rules and regulations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Offer input and suggestions in regards to promotions, advertisements and other various strategies.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Monitor and post on social media</li>
<li>
Assist in the creation and delivery of marketing materials.</li>
<li>
Participate in the planning and execution of housing fairs and other on and off-site promotional events.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Understands and complies with Fair Housing Laws and all company rules and regulations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Completes market surveys as requested by the Property Manager, monitors area trends and stays current on the needs of prospective renters. Seek and analyze competitor marketing and sales materials both on and offline.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Administrative</b></p>
<ul><li>
Assist in maintaining resident data through the Entrata software system.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Monitors the curb appeal and community appearance. Assists with the upkeep of these areas as needed. Alerts management or maintenance personnel of any known issues.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Conducts follow-up with residents and prospective residents to assure highest level of customer service.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Maintains open and consistent communication with the Property Manager, Leasing Consultants and the Maintenance team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Assist with move-in/move-out process inspections, distributing key packets and literature, collecting rental payments and /or missing paperwork, etc. as directly by management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Assist with enforcement of building rules.</li></ul>
