<h2>Part-Time Center Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The UPS Store is seeking amazing staff members that desire to serve the public well. We are seeking someone that is excited to serve people in a relaxed environment. Examples would include anything from coaching people how to make copies to helping move all of their belongings to a different state.<br>
<br>
We will provide flexible scheduling, a positive working environment, and starting pay is 11/hour. We also give evaluations every 3 months (with opportunity for a raise every evaluation)! Part-time or Full-time hours are available. Our desire is to provide a positive working environment that encourages our customers to make us their "small business solution." We are seeking staff to work at our Broadway &amp; Alameda location, DTC (Arapahoe &amp; Yosemite) location, and also our Lakewood (Alameda &amp; Union) location. Please send a resume to Eric if you are interested!<br>
<br>
The Center Associate delivers world-class customer service to all retail customers by receiving and processing packages for courier shipment and operating copiers, fax machines, binding equipment, laminating machines, and point-of-sale devices. The Associate expertly advises our valued customers by providing accurate information on our array of products, services, and best-value options and exhibits confidence by knowing best practices related to the industry.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate has previous retail sales experience, strong computer and internet skills, a high school diploma or GED, a friendly and genuinely helpful demeanor, and a professional appearance. He or she must be a fast learner who makes an effort to learn all aspects of the business in the shortest possible time.<br>
</p>
