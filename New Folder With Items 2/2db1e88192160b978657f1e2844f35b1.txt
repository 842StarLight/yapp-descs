<h2>VP, Global Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The VP, Global Sales will be the driving force for identifying new business opportunities, developing strategic relationships and partnerships, growing and managing existing business, and overall realization of sales efforts.
<br><br>
<b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b><br>
Direct, manage and execute all sales, account management, and business development efforts, globally
<br>Implement greater sophistication and rigor into Industrial Scientific’s go-to-market strategy, including, but not limited to, developing the company’s sales process and approach, pricing strategies, and account and market segmentation across their existing and target markets
<br>Develop and lead a sales-centric culture within the organization to ensure that the team is maximizing revenue opportunities across both new and existing accounts
<br>Deliver yearly balanced portfolio performance across hardware, software, and service solutions
<br>Develop and execute a global sales plan aligned to the company’s commercial priorities, including
<br>Customer engagement plan that incorporates the company’s sales methodology
<br>Sales efforts that drive growth across the portfolio
<br>Channel partner engagement, actions and results plan
<br>Communications plan for week, month, and quarter to keep cross-functional team aligned
<br>Recognition that rewards both results and behaviors of sales and cross-functional teams
<br>Implement and champion Fortive Business System (FBS) tools across the organization
<br><br>
<b>Experience &amp; Qualifications:</b><br>
Ideally this executive has experience growing revenue from $200M - $500M as a go-to-market leader within an enterprise technology company
<br>Demonstrated ability to transform, manage, and scale a multi-channel, complex, consultative go-to-market strategy that maximizes revenue across current customers and identifies opportunities within new accounts and verticals. Ideally, this executive has led a transitional effort to implement greater complexity and process into a commercial organization, including, but not limited to, account segmentation, pricing strategies, operational rigor, etc.
<br>Experience selling into industrial and manufacturing customers is important. It is beneficial, but not required, that this candidate has sold into Industrial Scientific’s customer base which includes the Oil &amp; Gas, Mining, and Metals industries, among others
<br>Deep experience with global customers. It is crucial that this executive has overseen a geographically dispersed team across multiple regions and/or countries
<br>Track record of hiring and managing sales and account management teams at scale. Ideally, this executive has led a commercial organization through a transition of adding greater sophistication and rigor across his/her staff; able to put the infrastructure in place that scales, delegates, and holds people accountable; able to strategically set the path forward and tactically execute against the plan while working alongside cross-functional peers
<br>Entrepreneurial; growth company experience; this candidate needs to understand value creation and possess the ability to grow with the company as the platform and solution offering broadens
<br>Data-driven; analytical; exceptionally collaborative; highly driven and self-motivated; communicates well, inclusive, passionate about building a great business
