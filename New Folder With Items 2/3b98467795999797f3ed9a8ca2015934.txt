<div><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Work with the Business and IT stakeholders to understand their vision and challenges</li>
<li>
Conduct a detailed assessment of data landscape including data platforms, technology architecture, data flows, data consumption, data integration and document current state</li>
<li>
Understand pain points in current state</li>
<li>
Develop future state architecture and process/data flows to realize the modern data strategy</li>
<li>
Architect modern data supply chain and evaluate and recommend new tools and technologies</li>
<li>
Guide decisioning of cloud vs on-premise environments and assist with cloud service provider selection</li>
<li>
Document and present data strategy to clients, gain buy in, and grow strategic relationships</li>
<li>
Assist in development of capability roadmap</li>
<li>
Participate in setting objectives, scope and develop a roadmap for the data initiatives that support the client leadership in meeting their objectives</li>
<li>
Own the technical relationship with the client, be a technical subject matter expert and principle data evangelist across the planning effort that intersect the data discipline</li>
<li>
Educate clients and internal constituents on the available technologies and general best practices</li>
<li>
Help building a team by mentoring junior practitioners as they build complex strategy and solutions</li>
<li>
Provide specialized expertise, cross-industry perspective, and Thought Leadership in Big Data, Cloud, EIM and other Next Generation technology offerings</li>
<li>
Provide pre-sales support across clients</li>
</ul>

<div><b>Basic Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 15 years of technology architecture experience</li>
<li>
Minimum of 7 years of experience in data architecture and technology</li>
<li>
Minimum of 5 years of experience in architecture of modern data platforms using technologies such as Big Data and Cloud</li>
<li>
Minimum 5 years of management consulting experience</li>
<li>
Experienced in architecting Data Management solutions with capabilities, such as – Data Ingestion, Data Curation, Metadata and Catalog, Data Security, Data Modeling, Data Wrangling</li>
<li>
Experienced in architecting and developing Data Warehousing / BI / Reporting solutions using platforms and technologies such as Hadoop, Teradata, Netezza, Greenplum, MapReduce, Spark, etc.</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of modern data ingestion/curation/wrangling tools/technologies such as Informatica suite (BDM, EDC, BDQ, PowerExchange, PowerCenter, etc.), Talend suite, Alteryx, Streamsets, Attunity, BI tools such as Tableau/Qlikview</li>
<li>
Understanding of BI/Reporting, Advanced Analytics, Data Science, AI/ML and Self-Service solutions through to warehouse design, sourcing and logical data models</li>
<li>
Strong in building data lifecycle management solutions including Data Lake, Data Warehouse, Data Marts, ODS, ELT and ETL, Stream/real-time/Batch processing, CDC, etc.</li>
<li>
Deep technical expertise with Big Data and Cloud technologies such as Hortonworks, Cloudera, AWS, MS Azure, Google Cloud, Visualization Tools, NoSQL, Graph databases</li>
<li>
Understanding of different data modeling techniques such as Relational modeling, Dimensional modeling, Data Vault, etc.</li>
<li>
Strong communication, presentation, written and facilitation skills</li>
<li>
Superior critical thinking, analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Ability to interface with client at any level, executive to engineer</li>
<li>
Bachelors Degree or 12 years of professional experience</li>
<li>
Able to travel 100% (Monday to Thursday)</li>
</ul>

<li>
Experience in data governance and operating model</li>
