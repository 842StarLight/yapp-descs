<b>About Us</b>:<br>
<br>
As a not-for-profit organization, Partners HealthCare is committed to supporting patient care, research, teaching, and service to the community by leading innovation across our system. Founded by Brigham and Women’s Hospital and Massachusetts General Hospital, Partners HealthCare supports a complete continuum of care including community and specialty hospitals, a managed care organization, a physician network, community health centers, home care and other health-related entities. Several of our hospitals are teaching affiliates of Harvard Medical School, and our system is a national leader in biomedical research.<br>
<br>
We’re focused on a people-first culture for our system’s patients and our professional family. That’s why we provide our employees with more ways to achieve their potential. Partners HealthCare is committed to aligning our employees’ personal aspirations with projects that match their capabilities and creating a culture that empowers our managers to become trusted mentors. We support each member of our team to own their personal development—and we recognize success at every step.<br>
<br>
Our employees use the Partners HealthCare values to govern decisions, actions and behaviors. These values guide how we get our work done: Patients, Affordability, Accountability &amp; Service Commitment, Decisiveness, Innovation &amp; Thoughtful Risk; and how we treat each other: Diversity &amp; Inclusion, Integrity &amp; Respect, Learning, Continuous Improvement &amp; Personal Growth, Teamwork &amp; Collaboration.<div>

<b>
General Summary:</b><br>
The Center for Clinical Data Science (CCDS) aims to transform healthcare through cutting-edge data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. We combine a translational, product-focused, early-stage culture with the world-class researchers, physicians, and data/computing resources at Harvard-affiliated Massachusetts General and Brigham and Women’s Hospitals, as well as the broader Partners HealthCare System. The Center for Clinical Data Science provides the perfect environment to focus on using analytics to effect tangible advancements in medicine.<br>
<br>
We are looking for a forward thinking, passionate and driven Director to coordinate activities for our external relationships and to drive CCDS business development strategy. S/he will have a demonstrable record of success executing business strategy and priorities, managing projects and programs, conducting quantitative and financial analysis, and ensuring success.<br>
<br>
The candidate must have a strong sense of urgency, be a self-starter,comfortable working independently and sourcing input where required.<br>
<br>
Reporting to the Managing Director of Operations, the Director’s principal responsibility will be supporting day-to-day execution of business strategy and activities at the CCDS to support high growth, with primary responsibility for oversight of key accounts and BD activities. This highly visible position will interface considerably with executive leadership within the CCDS, across the Partners’ institutions, with researchers and clinicians, and with teams from our industry partners.<br>
<br>

<b>Principal Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and own the CCDS commercial strategy, including identifying new leads and driving opportunities for development of products with potential for commercialization.</li>
<li>Oversee market intelligence for product development activities by conducting competitive intelligence research and creating landscape analyses to define ‘white spaces’ and opportunities for commercial growth, including the monitoring of start-ups in the field of AI/ML as well as potential partners.</li>
<li>Engage potential business partners in close collaboration with Partners Business Development</li>
<li>Lead and facilitate meetings and working sessions related to strategy and product development with external stakeholders.</li>
<li>Work closely with the Partners Business Development team and the Partners Office of General Counsel to ensure the CCDS’s strategic interests are appropriately represented during interactions with prospective commercial partners</li>
<li>Manage ongoing partnerships, including by leading/providing input for generation of terms and statements of work, liasing with industry partners, planning meeting agenda, other document creation.</li>
<li>Serve as the point of contact with industry partners, informing them of new developments and any variances and ensure, to the extent possible, all parties remain aligned. Responsible for communicating contract terms to internal CCDS teams.</li>
<li>Collaborate with product teams to ensure alignment of project goals, milestones or other deliverables are executed on-time.</li>
<li>Assist in resource management across projects and in concert with internal research / development efforts – being mindful of opportunities for consolidation.</li>
<li>Provide project management for various ad-hoc activities throughout the CCDS as needed.</li>
<li>Develop, maintain and adhere to internal project management tools, processes and methodologies.</li>
<li>Become a subject matter expert on CCDS processes and operations, and remain up-to-date on related healthcare and technology industry news</li>
<li>Attend conferences, meetings, and industry events as a representative of CCDS and Partners HealthCare</li>
<li>Keep leadership appraised and provide feedback about what is and is not working.</li>
<li>Grow the team responsible for coordinating activities with industry partners.</li>
