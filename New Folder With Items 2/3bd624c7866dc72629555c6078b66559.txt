<h2>Customer Service Rep I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Accurately enter phone or email orders into software system. Assist customers in locating the desired product and answer pricing or discount questions.</li>
<li>Research and provide accurate details regarding shipment dates, expected delivery dates, invoice totals, discounts and related invoice or shipment details.</li>
<li>Field new account inquiries and assist customers in navigating company website.</li>
<li>Interact with retailers, sales representatives and consumers with courtesy and professionalism.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by manager.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Additional Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work within a team environment to attain department objectives.</li>
<li>Exhibit a positive attitude, support corporate directives and be adaptable to change.</li>
<li>Support of and involvement in company and department safety policies, procedures, programs and activities.</li>
<li>Maintain a clean and orderly work area.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Job Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or General Education Diploma (GED).</li>
<li>Three or more years of Customer Service experience. Call Center experience preferred, though not mandatory.</li>
<li>One or more years of data entry and 10-key experience.</li>
<li>An interest in or willingness to learn about nutritional supplements and the natural products industry.</li>
<li>Must have problem recognition and decision making abilities.</li>
<li>Good verbal, written and phone communication skills.</li>
<li>Experience with word processing and spreadsheet applications preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals.</li>
<li>Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, interest, proportions, and percentages.</li>
<li>Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out detailed but uninvolved written or oral instructions. Ability to deal with problems involving a few concrete variables in standardized situations.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Physical Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is frequently required to sit; use hands to finger, handle, or feel; reach with hands and arms; and talk or hear.</li>
<li>Employee must exert up to 20 lbs. of force occasionally, and/or up to 10 lbs. of force frequently, and/or a negligible amount of force constantly to move objects.</li>
<li>Specific vision abilities required by this job include near acuity, far acuity, depth perception, accommodation, color vision, and field of vision.</li>
<br>
</ul>
