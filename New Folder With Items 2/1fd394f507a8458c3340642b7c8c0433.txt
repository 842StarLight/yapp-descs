<h2>Sr. Account Executive - Pittsburgh</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>ROLE: Senior Account Executive</b></p>
<p><b>
LOCATION: Pittsburgh, PA</b></p>
<p>
As an Applied Cloud Systems <b>Sr. Account Executive</b> you are responsible for the development and management of Microsoft Azure, Office365, Dynamics CRM and Power BI customers. In addition, you will generate revenue by developing market potential through forecasting, lead generation, qualification, and closing sales for our leading Cloud services.</p>
<p><b><i>
Must have experience and knowledge with the Microsoft Ecosystem!</i></b><br>
<br>
<b>Your responsibilities also include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proactively engage with, and realize sales targets of a given geographic territory</li>
<li>
Increase customer base and recurring revenue</li>
<li>
Ensure that Applied Cloud Services is regarded to as the trusted and preferred partner for Microsoft Azure, Office365, Dynamics CRM and Power BI services</li>
<li>
Timely follow up on requests from internal and external customers</li>
<li>
Proactively reach out and assist engineers in the successful deployment of their Cloud proposition</li>
<li>
Close sales by building rapport with potential customers; explaining product and service capabilities; overcoming objections; preparing contracts</li>
<li>
Coordinate pre-sales assistance in the delivery of effective proposals to the prospects and customers</li>
<li>
Coordinate together with the Marketing Manager to ensure optimal lead generation and optimal use of marketing tools in the assigned territory</li>
<li>
Identify market potential by qualifying potential customers</li>
<li>
Initiate sales process by scheduling appointments; making initial presentations; understanding opportunity requirements</li>
<li>
Expands sales in existing accounts by introducing new products and services</li>
<li>
Contribute information to market strategy by monitoring competitive products and reactions from customers</li>
<li>
Recommend new products and services by evaluating current product results; identifying needs to be filled</li>
<li>
Update job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities</li>
<li>
Accomplish marketing and organization mission by completing related results as needed</li>
<li>
Some travel required: 25%</li>
</ul>
