<h2>Outside Sales Representative - Grand Prarie, TX</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>If you consider yourself outgoing, are active within the community and desire job flexibility for a work/family balance, we have an opportunity for you.</p>
<p>
Welcome Wagon is a national marketing company that welcomes families to their new homes by providing them with introductions to local businesses, professionals and services through print, digital and social media.</p>
<p>
This role is 100% outside advertising sales, home based and offers:</p>
<ul>
<li>
base salary plus commission</li>
<li>
benefits</li>
<li>
commission</li>
<li>
bonuses</li>
<li>
mileage reimbursement</li>

<p>
The position requires both phone and in-person sales skills (phone to set appointments, in-person to close). As a Sales Representative, you will sell consultative advertising including our Gift Book, Direct Mail, Digital Marketing and Social Media Programs within your local market.</p>
<p>
We have successful Community Marketing Executives (sales representatives) from various backgrounds such as:</p>
<p>
PTA/Volunteers<br>
Fundraising<br>
Stay at Home Moms Returning to the Workforce<br>
Teachers/Educators<br>
Business Owners<br>
Outside Sales Representatives</p>
<p>
Our successful representatives agree that their compensation is directly proportionate to the amount of hard work they put in day to day.</p>
<p>
All employment offers are contingent upon receipt of satisfactory references, motor vehicle reports, and demonstration of your legal right to work in the United States.</p>
