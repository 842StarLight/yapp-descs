<div><b>
Description</b></div>
<div>As Technical Sales Director, North America, for E&amp;P Software you'll leverage your geoscience background and business knowledge to grow the pipeline of business opportunities and achieve revenue targets. You'll contribute to regional business development and overall sales revenue growth by proactively leading and engaging the technical sales team, and driving collaboration between Sales, Technical Sales, and Technical Support.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborate with Sales, Marketing and Product Development to develop and implement strategy and objectives for the North America Technical Sales team aligned with World Area priorities and financial targets</li>
<li>
Manage a team of geoscience, engineering, and data management technical sales specialists, ensuring the team has the right skills, tools and processes to deliver customer adoption, customer retention, and revenue growth</li>
<li>
Evaluate, coach, and optimize the team’s performance, capacity, and technical capability</li>
<li>
Engage with customers at a high level to ensure current needs are being met, to uncover future needs and hidden issues, and to drive adoption of enterprise solutions and technical services</li>
<li>
Coordinate and staff for World Area and global Proofs-of-Concept, and other Business Development projects</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other World Area technical directors and Sales team to establish best practices, standards, and processes</li>
<li>
Ensure resources are managed to close large-scale World Area and global deals</li>
<li>
Track and provide timely reporting of key performance indicators for operations, financial, and corporate requirements, meeting all reporting deadlines</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>
Requirements</b></div>
Bachelor's or Master's degree in Geoscience or Engineering<div>
8 - 10 years' experience in geoscience technical sales including software and services experience<br>
