<h2>Shipping Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description</p>
<p><b>Atlas Roof Shingles &amp; Underlayments</b>- a <b>Division of Atlas Roofing Corporation</b>- is an industry leader that develops, sells and manufactures a full line of high-performance residential roof shingles, underlayments, and accessory systems and products. Atlas Roof Shingles &amp; Underlayments offers innovative, quality, and unmatched roofing and construction materials for homeowners, contractors, builders, and distributors.</p>
<p>Atlas Roofing Corporation, a privately owned company, established in 1982, is an international and multi-divisional manufacturer of roofing, sheathing, facer, and insulation product solutions. <b>Atlas protects because WE Care! Live, Work, Play!</b></p>
<p><b>Atlas Roof Shingles &amp; Underlayments</b> is seeking a <b>Shipping Manager</b> for the <b>Franklin</b><b>, OH</b> facility.</p>
<p><b>Shipping Manager Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure a safe work environment through strict adherence to Atlas’ safety policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Drive continuous improvement in the areas of safety and housekeeping.</li>
<li>Know and communicate safety message with focus on developing zero injury culture.</li>
<li>Recruit, interview, and select staff that have the right technical abilities and skills.</li>
<li>Support and sustain a culture where talent is developed through effective performance management, talent evaluation, and effective utilization of resources. Ensure workforce is trained in respective job duties.</li>
<li>Direct department supervisor and lead in creating, maintaining, and enforcing Shipping and Receiving policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Resolve problems concerning transportation, logistics, and customer.</li>
<li>Negotiate with suppliers and customers to improve supply chain efficiency and sustainability, as well as, negotiating transportation rates and services.</li>
<li>Plan appropriate warehouse layout design to maximize storage while maintaining a safe working environment.</li>
<li>Coordinate with Sales to assure on time delivery, to avoid late fines.</li>
<li>Oversee completion of all bills of ladings and other shipping documents through PeopleSoft.</li>
<li>Supervise all shipping personnel and Atlas truck drivers, if applicable, to maintain the most effective methods of shipment of finished goods and the receipt and storage of raw materials.</li>
<li>Schedule the maintenance of lease forklift fleet.</li>
<li>Maintain metrics, reports, process documentation, customer service/carrier logs, and/or training records for the department.</li>
<li>Oversee the remote warehouse functions.</li>
<li>Assist in monthly physical inventory of finished products and reconcile warehouse inventory variances.</li>
<li>Work closely with the Account Executives and other managers, to assure the highest degree of Customer Satisfaction, Quality and Efficiencies.</li>
<li>Compile information and prepare various reports and records.</li>
<li>Manage the pick and pull process to stage loads, send ASN, sticker shipments to comply with customer requirements.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Shipping Manager Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years’ experience in a manufacturing/warehouse environment, preferably in logistics.</li>
<li>Previous supervisory experience is desired.</li>
<li>Experience with Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software is desired.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Shipping Manager Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office Suite, includes, MS Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Fluent in English, includes, ability to read, write, speak, and comprehend.</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills, including ability to bring about consensus in areas of conflict.</li>
<li>Knowledge of materials and inventory control practices, production scheduling, and engineering in a manufacturing environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Shipping Manager Education, Licenses &amp; Certifications</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree (B.A. or B.S.) in related field from a 4-year college; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li></ul>
