<h2>Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Resonate has pioneered a completely new way to help leading brands and agencies build unbreakable relationships with their customers. Our proprietary Human Element is a 360 degree understanding of a person that combines peoples’ values and motivations with digital and physical experiences. The Human Element reveals why people choose, buy, advocate or abandon a brand. This means brands and agencies can design more resonant products, offers, content, messaging and creative.</p>
<p>
We’re looking for a driven, innovative, and team-oriented Customer Success Manager who thrives amidst the challenges of a fast-paced, start-up environment.</p>
<p>
The Customer Success Manager (CSM) is responsible for ensuring the success of a portfolio of Resonate customers. As a trusted advisor, the CSM will employ Resonate insights, best practices, and capabilities to deliver maximum value to our customers through effective onboarding, adoption activities and an ongoing strategic relationship. The results are increased customer satisfaction and retention of Resonate solutions.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>
Discover and understand each customer’s definition of success and ensure the customer achieves their desired business outcomes:</li></ul>
<div><ul>
<li>
Develop a comprehensive understanding of typical business challenges and common objectives across your portfolio and appropriately map Resonate capabilities to customer needs</li>
<li>
Promote maximum value from a customer’s investment through utilization of licensed capabilities and purchased services</li>
<li>
Identify risks to the customer achieving their stated business outcomes and work with the internal team to build a risk mitigation plan</li>
<li>
Work with customers to develop methodology for measuring margin or revenue created from Resonate products and services</li>
<li>
Effectively network within accounts from the C-Level down, with focus on understanding and delivering against the customer’s definition of success</li>
</ul></div>
<ul><li>
Drive onboarding, adoption, and continued usage of Resonate products and services:</li></ul>
<div><ul>
<li>
Deliver Resonate’s foundational training modules to drive platform adoption for new accounts and new users within an account</li>
<li>
Facilitate the use of Resonate’s attributes and online behavioral data to drive achievement against key customer use cases such as new customer acquisition, competitive conquesting and customer loyalty.</li>
<li>
Manage the implementation and execution of products relating to media execution, customer research/segmentation, and digital measurement strategies including:</li>
<ul>
<li>
Development of relevant customer profiles that align to the clients’ target audience</li>
<li>
Implementation of audience and campaign strategies that are suited for the client’s KPIs</li>
<li>
Pixel placement on client’s web pages and/or media to drive insights, track specific KPIs, and optimize performance metrics</li>
<li>
Platform data integration projects</li>
</ul>
</ul></div>
<ul><li>
Provide strategic recommendations employing Resonate insights, best practices, and capabilities</li></ul>
<div><ul>
<li>
Coordinate the use of Resonate’s reporting capabilities to track performance against customer’s business objectives</li>
<li>
Make strategic campaign optimization recommendations to clients based on performance metrics and data to help achieve campaign goals</li>
<li>
Deliver valuable insights to the client about their campaigns and make recommendations for ongoing/new campaigns</li>
</ul></div>
<ul>
<li>
Work cross-functionally with other internal teams such as sales, product, marketing, operations, and finance to ensure customer satisfaction, retention and expansion</li>
<li>
Contribute thought leadership and best practices, both internally and externally, to Resonate’s go-to-market strategy based on your direct engagement with customers</li>
</ul>
