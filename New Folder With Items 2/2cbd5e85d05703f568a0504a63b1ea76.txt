<h2>Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Summary
<br><br>
SMG, the leader in privately managed public assembly facilities has excellent and immediate opportunities for Interns at NRG Park in Houston, Texas. NRG Park is in the fast lane for opportunity and excitement. We are a one of a kind premier facility with four major venues within one giant park. NRG Center (one million+ sq. ft. Convention Center), NRG Stadium (home of the Houston Texans and the Houston Livestock Show &amp; Rodeo), NRG Astrodome ("The" Astrodome), and NRG Arena. We are seeking unique Part-Time Interns to join our team. Interns are expected to work a minimum of twenty (20) hours per week. The time period of the Internship can be adjusted to meet the requirements of the degree plan of the student, and/or with the approval of the General Manager.
<br><br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities
<br><br>
Depending on the department or departments an intern is selected for duties may include the following:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Shadow and assist varied positions within SMG NRG Park to learn a variety of skills which may include: graphics, billing, content development, sports marketing/ sponsorships, Technical Services Audio Visual, Scoreboard / Videoboard Operations, Smart City Networks (Event Setup), Security, Safety, Maintenance, Engineering, Parking, Finance and/or Operations.</li>
<li>Perform administrative duties, maintain files, organize documents, photocopy, fax, etc. as needed.</li>
<li>Maintain an effective working relationship with clients, employees, exhibitors, patrons and others encountered in the course of employment.</li>
<li>Organize and prioritize work to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>May perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
