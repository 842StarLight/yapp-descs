<p><b>About the Team</b></p>
<p>
You will be part of the eCommerce Engineering team helping deliver high value, high quality features to our industry leading eCommerce sites. At Williams-Sonoma we build 6 of the retail industries leading websites - Williams-Sonoma, West Elm, Mark and Graham and the Pottery Barn family. Engineers working at WSI get to work full-stack, extending our beautiful front-end user experience all the way through to enterprise services that support personalization, order management, and other server-side features.</p>
<br>

<p><b>
About the Role</b></p>
<p>
The Williams-Sonoma ECommerce Engineering organization is seeking an energetic and talented Software engineer with primary expertise in Front End Development and experience working in the area of Ecommerce Checkout. Full Stack development experience is preferred.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
As a Software Engineer in the Ecommerce organization you'll join a Scrum team building features and automated tests of those features. You'll work closely with senior engineers and Product Managers to understand user stories and assist in programming the features. Through programming and code reviews you'll be able to expand your skills and grow as an engineer.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You're excited about this opportunity because you will...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist senior engineers and product managers in designing new features</li>

<li>Write automated tests of new features to ensure they are of very high quality</li>
<li>Participate in Communities-of-Practice in those technologies in which you have an interest</li>
<li>Participate in continuous education programs to grow your skills both technically and in the Williams-Sonoma business domain</li>
<li>Serve as a member of a Scrum team and participate in all planning, stand-up and retrospective meetings</li>
</ul>
