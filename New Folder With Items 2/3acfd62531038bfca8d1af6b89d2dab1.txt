<p><i>
About the role</i></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
As a Salesforce Developer in our CRM practice, you'll be designing and engineering world-class solutions on the Salesforce platform, for some of Phoenix's most exciting brands. Professional variety is what drives us – you’ll be challenged by a wide variety of projects leveraging an array of innovative technologies while delivering for clients across a broad range of industries.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><i>
Responsibilities</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop custom solutions and integrations on Salesforce implementations</li>
<li>
Translate business requirements into well-architected solutions that best leverage the Salesforce platform and products</li>
<li>
Execute test plans and ensure a quality solution is delivered</li>
<li>
Provide estimates of the level of effort to implement a solution based on business requirements</li>
<li>
For each requirement, provide guidance on clicks vs code/customization</li>
<li>
Adhere to modern software development lifecycle best practices including iterative and incremental development, delivering working software frequently with attention to technical excellence</li>
<li>
Act as a subject matter expert in the sales cycle and at marketing events (e.g. sales meetings as necessary)</li>
<li>
Follow and understand new Salesforce product and technical capabilities resulting from product releases and acquisitions</li>
<li>
Familiarity with advanced Apex programming concepts including core design patterns, limits and bulk patterns, efficient and reliable trigger handling, asynchronous operations and effective unit testing</li>
<li>
Contribute to knowledge growth of the Salesforce practice through documenting and sharing best practices</li>
<li>
Participate in code reviews to ensure high code quality standards and design patterns are utilized</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><i>
Qualifications</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years of Salesforce platform experience</li>
<li>
Hands-on use of Apex, Visualforce, Visual Workflow, and/or Lightning components to create unique customer-based solutions</li>
<li>
JavaScript, HTML, and CSS development experience</li>
<li>
Web Services development or experience with Salesforce APIs (REST/SOAP/Bulk, etc.)</li>
<li>
Prior experience soliciting, gathering and analyzing user input and requirements</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of Salesforce configuration capabilities and the ability to recommend best practices and identify areas where custom development is required</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of Object Oriented Analysis and Design, database design and software development life-cycle</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate technical requirements to non-technical stakeholders and translate functional requirements into technical specifications</li>
<li>
Experience in integrating Salesforce with other business systems, using various integration platforms and tools</li>
<li>
Strong client-facing written, presentation and verbal communications skills</li>
<li>
Active Salesforce certifications or ability to achieve relevant certifications upon hire</li>
<li>
Passion and motivation for learning new technologies and programming languages</li>
<li>
Be humble and curious</li>
</ul>
