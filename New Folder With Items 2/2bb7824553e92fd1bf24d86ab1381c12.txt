<h2>Financial Planning Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Janney is an award-winning full-service financial services firm committed to understanding our clients' needs and providing advice beyond investments. We are equally committed to building a diverse workforce and inclusive culture where employees can use their talents to thrive and grow professionally with the firm.
<br><br>
One of the top wealth advisory teams at Janney is looking to further develop a highly motivated individual with a strong desire for a career in financial planning. This opportunity is for someone who is looking to grow while working alongside and learning from a highly talented team. A proactive learner with a positive attitude, team player mentality who thrives in a fast paced environment will be successful in this role.
<br><br>
The individual will gain exposure to a wide range of responsibilities associated with our high net-worth client needs. The individual will support financial planning efforts, provide administrative and sales support to learn the business and potentially be considered for Janney’s Financial Advisory Training Program. Using our firm’s comprehensive services and solutions, this individual will also help to implement strategies to fulfill the needs addressed in financial plans.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to embrace the team’s investment and financial planning philosophy focused on clients achieving their financial and personal goals</li>
<li>Utilize the current software program to help prepare customized financial plans, overall asset allocation analysis and reports for client meetings that range from simple to complex for the client base</li>
<li>Troubleshoot financial planning issues within our financial planning software and client reporting tools</li>
<li>Keep current on financial planning and investment topics, research issues, answer questions, and assist with finding solutions for the team</li>
<li>Consistently and effectively execute the firm’s processes and operational duties in a compliant manner</li>
<li>Handle meeting follow-up (logging, tracking and completing client open items) and communicate with clients (phone, email and written communications) as directed by senior team members to ensure development, implementation and monitoring of financial plans</li>
<li>Act as an internal consultant, as needed, to provide subject matter expertise to team members</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively, both orally and in writing, and a high comfort level in speaking with affluent clients and professionals at all levels of the organization</li>
<li>Strong team player mentality with a genuine ability to assist where needed</li>
<li>Skilled at critical thinking and analysis and can grasp concepts quickly</li>
<li>Strong client service skills and experience</li>
<li>Organized, detail-oriented and resourceful</li>
<li>Takes pride in accomplishing a wide variety of tasks in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Effective interpersonal skills and the ability to work well both individually and collaboratively in a team</li>
<li>High ethical standards</li>
<li>Ability to travel to Philadelphia occasionally for training and development opportunities</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree (concentration in Finance a plus)</li>
<li>Professional financial planning or investment experience preferred</li>
<li>FINRA Series 7 and 66 licenses, or the ability to obtain the following within 12 months of hire date: FINRA’s Securities Industry Essentials (SIE), FINRA Series 7 and 66 licenses</li>
<li>Certified Financial Planner (CFP®) designation a plus (or willingness to obtain)</li>
</ul>
