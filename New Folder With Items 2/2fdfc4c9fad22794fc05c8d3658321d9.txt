<h2>Training Instructor 2</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>The Training Instructor II is responsible for developing and conducting training for a functional or corporate audience. This position focuses on teaching specific areas of knowledge or on-the-job capabilities needed for certain positions. Observation of business unit practices and gathering and analyzing feedback may be required in order to develop training materials such as presentations, job aids, exercises, etc.<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Delivers a variety of instructor led courses (classroom or virtual format). Training may include but not limited to: skills development, sales training, product training, new hire training, and continuing education.
</li>
<li>Development and revision of training materials such as teaching aides, handbooks, PowerPoint presentations, eLearnings and reference guides, under minimal supervision.
</li>
<li>Gathers and analyzes training evaluation feedback to assess effectiveness and drive improvement; Ensures training rosters are collected and courses/attendees are tracked in the company’s learning management system.
</li>
<li>Direct observation of business unit practices, as needed. Observation may include but is not limited to: train the trainers, observation of existing training, shadowing business unit, co-facilitate.
</li>
<li>Ensures compliance with applicable federal, state and local laws and regulations. Completes all required compliance training. Maintains knowledge of and adhere to Flagstar’s internal compliance policies and procedures. Takes responsibility to keep up to date with changing regulations and policies.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Requirements </b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma, GED, or foreign equivalent required.
</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in education or related field is preferred.
</li>
<li>3-5 years training experience.
</li>
<li>1-3 years’ experience in financial services industry specifically related to Retail Banking is required.
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to demonstrate interpersonal and presentation skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to act as a professional role model.
</li>
<li>Time management and organizational skills with close attention to detail.
</li>
<li>Ability to learn and understand new technologies quickly.
</li>
<li>Experience using Microsoft Office products; experience with eLearning software such as Captivate is a plus.
</li>
<li>Ability to work regularly scheduled hours, 8:30-5:00 PM Monday – Friday; some weekend or evening hours may be required upon business need.
</li>
<li>0-15% travel depending on function supported.
</li>
<li><p>The job requires a moderate degree of physical exertion and stamina such as standing, sitting, walking, driving or infrequent lifting.
</p></li>
</ul>
