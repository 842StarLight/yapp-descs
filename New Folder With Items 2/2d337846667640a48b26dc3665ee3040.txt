<h2>Inventory Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Inventory Coordinator will be responsible in assisting of the purchasing and pricing of the company's ticket inventory. The Coordinator will be responsible for purchasing tickets on a daily basis, listing tickets for events and following pricing trends on the secondary ticket market.</p>
<p><b>
Job duties include, but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Purchase live event tickets via presales, on sales, direct contact with teams, venues and other primary/secondary ticketing sources</li>
<li>
Input and manage inventory using the Point of Sale and other third-party software</li>
<li>
Adjust prices of live listings by adhering to the pricing schedule as directed by the Inventory Manager and tracking ticket sales in order to maximize revenue and profitability</li>
<li>
Adhere to inventory management best practices as outlined by the Inventory Manager</li>
<li>
Maintain purchasing key performance indicators (KPIs) in order to meet departmental goals, strategies and objectives.</li>
<li>
Research upcoming live events</li>
<li>
Creating and executing strategies for acquiring profitable tickets</li>
<li>
Respond quickly and accurately to internal inquires pertaining to inventory issues</li>
<li>
Working as a team player to grow our business</li>
</ul>
