<h2>Remarketing Solutions Operations Compliance Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Remarketing Operations Compliance Analyst is responsible for all support functions including but not limited to research, issue resolution, and field (Remarketing Specialist team)/Auction Partner support. The Remarketing Operations Compliance Analyst is also responsible for monitoring operations of GM and GM Financial inventory through various GMF and Vendor procedures. This includes quality assurance review of various Remarketing functions and updating procedural documentation as necessary. This team member is responsible for completing a wide variety of complex tasks and has direct contact with other departments and external vendors including, but not limited to, dealerships, auto auctions, online auctions, inspection vendors, and vehicle reporting bureaus. Achieving optimal business results and department objectives in this position directly affects the financial results of the company.This position is responsible for providing superior service to both internal and external partners while ensuring vendor and departmental activities do not adversely impact customer wellbeing.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>JOB DUTIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Perform compliance reviews of, develop and maintain departmental documentation as directed by management</li>
<li>
Maintain a strong working knowledge of both upstream and downstream auction sales procedures</li>
<li>
Perform compliance reviews of GMF Remarketing processes of internal and external GMF employees Document “non-compliant” items/workflows and assist management with remediation in order to reduce risks</li>
<li>
Work directly with internal and external partners in the documentation and remediation of compliance related issues as directed by management</li>
<li>
Seek to improve efficiencies by initiating and sharing ideas for process and quality improvements</li>
<li>
Audit vehicle expenses to ensure contracted rates have been utilized, and that charges were approved by GM Financial according to current guidelines Document and invoice vendor on all “non-compliant” expenses</li>
<li>
Review and analyze 3rd party inspections for description accuracy and guideline compliance</li>
<li>
Perform compliance reviews of GMF vendors ensuring state and federal regulatory compliance as it applies to the sale of Repossessed and Off Lease collateral through Upstream and Downstream sale venues</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned</li>
<li>
Conform with all company policies and procedures</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
