<h2>Leasing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Do you enjoy working in a fast-paced environment supporting a top performing sales team? If so, come join our Order Processing Team!</p>
<p>General Description of Position:</p>
<p>Supporting the Sales Teams at an administrative level which includes getting sales orders credit approved, delivered, invoiced and funded by our 3rd party leasing partners.</p>
<p>To be successful in this role you will need to demonstrate the following skills and expertise:</p>
<p>Customer Focus and Sales Orientation</p>
<ul><li>Ability to manage customer expectations with ease and confidence</li></ul>
<ul><li>Is able to manage multiple responsibilities as they relate to customer requirements, reporting and communications</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drives exceptional customer value by partnering with sales consultants and following up on various account details.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Is a trusted advisor to both internal &amp; external customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must work well with customers, fellow order processors and other internal employees at all levels</li></ul>
<p>Essential Responsibilities</p>
<ul><li>Process equipment orders and enter orders into eAuto</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate with preferred leasing companies to receive best available financing</li></ul>
<ul><li>Be aware of the status of all pending orders/ leases.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Inventory status and tracking</li></ul>
<ul><li>Takes responsibility for delivering requirements on time and with a high degree of quality.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for disposition of equipment traded up/bought out and authorizing payment to close out existing accounts - This task is very time sensitive.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enter all orders into eAuto daily so information is pulled into Sales Database each evening</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work closely with assigned sales team to make sure paperwork on all orders is completed accurately and in timely manner</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other tasks as assigned</li></ul>
<p>Desired experience:</p>
<ul><li>1-2+ years working in an office environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Professional poise and strong communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>2 or 4 year college degree preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Is deadline driven with solid organizational and time management skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Pays attention to detail with the ability to multi-task with little to no errors</li></ul>
<ul><li>Possesses solid math and analytical skills and is comfortable learning new technology and/or software</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experienced in MS Office Suite with advanced knowledge of Excel</li></ul>
