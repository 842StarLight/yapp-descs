<h2>Manager - Tax Accounting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>CRST International, Inc. is seeking a detail oriented, tax professional to join their growing Finance team as a Manager - Tax Accounting located in our downtown Cedar Rapids, IA location.</div>
<div></div>

<div></div>

<ul>
<li>
<div>Coordinate federal, state and local income tax payments and filings</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Oversee fuel tax payments and filings</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Review fixed asset accounting for tax using BNA software and use BNA to prepare tax depreciation work papers</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Calculate quarterly income tax provision</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Prepare personal property tax payments and filings</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Prepare and file 5500 forms</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Oversee preparation and mailing of 1099 forms, prepare corrections and e-file with IRS</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Manage sales tax return preparation and compliance</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Responsible for other duties and projects as assigned</div>
</li>
</ul>

</li>
<li>
<div>HS Diploma or GED</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>AA or BA in relevant field</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>CPA certification a plus</div>
</li>
