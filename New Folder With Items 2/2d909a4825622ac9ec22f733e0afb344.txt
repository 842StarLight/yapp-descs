<h2>Seafood Assistant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Are you a retail seafood extraordinaire?! Do you love New Seasons Market?</p>
<p>Our Lake Oswego store is hiring a 2nd In Charge for our best in town Seafood department.</p>
<p><b>Position Description: </b>Partners with Meat and Seafood Manager to manage department and staff, including financial stewardship, customer service, department conditions, merchandising, food safety, work safety, communication, hiring, training and performance management. Ensures the department provides an exceptional customer shopping experience, with the highest level of service and product knowledge. Provides leadership in line with company values and promotes a positive and progressive work environment.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>Leadership</p>
<ul>
<li>Creates a positive and supportive work environment.</li>
<li>Promotes a collaborative team environment through recognition, leading by example and working alongside staff. Demonstrates approachability and fosters open communication. Creates an environment where staff are invested and encouraged to contribute ideas. Demonstrates a pro-active approach in resolving staff issues and conflicts.</li>
<li>Partners with department manager to build and sustain a high performing team. Contributes to the development of team members including interviewing and hiring, training and development and performance management.</li>
<li>Seeks opportunities to train and develop staff members to take on increased responsibilities. Demonstrates ability to adapt approach to different learning styles among staff.</li>
<li>Builds trust and respect through dependability, organization and follow through.</li>
<li>Demonstrates adaptability, flexibility and creativity in problem-solving and is solutions-oriented.</li>
<li>Bases decisions on analysis, experience, resources and circumstances. Prioritizes and acts decisively under pressure.</li>
<li>Demonstrates self-awareness and seeks opportunities for professional growth. Seeks and responds positively to feedback.</li>
<li>Partners with department manager to establish and maintain effective verbal and written communication systems.</li>
<li>Partners with department manager to ensure effective scheduling and labor allocation to meet business needs and labor budget.</li>
<li>Partners with department manager to prepare and deliver corrective actions and performance reviews in a timely manner. Follows documentation and compensation guidelines.</li>
<li>Communicates in a professional and constructive manner. Responds to and follows through on emails and other communications in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Demonstrates an understanding of basic Human Resources concepts; works with resources at store level to increase knowledge.</li>
<li>Acts as a steward of company resources and Vision/Mission/Values by training, modeling and promoting sustainable behaviors/operations.</li>
<li>Balances administrative tasks with presence on floor.</li>
</ul>
<p>Position Specific Functions</p>
<ul>
<li>Partners with department manager to manage and provide oversight to all areas of the department. Assesses, prioritizes and delegates tasks and production.</li>
<li>Ensures consistency of department conditions. Ensures all areas of department are stocked, rotated, faced and adhere to merchandising standards and category schematics.</li>
<li>Partners with department manager to ensure satisfactory in-stock conditions in department. Maintains appropriate inventory levels to achieve sales and margin goals.</li>
<li>Orders seafood products according to anticipated sales and seasonal needs. Trains staff to write orders and production schedules consistent with department goals and standards to ensure the highest level of product availability.</li>
<li>Ensures all Meat and Seafood items are cut, trimmed and processed to company standards.</li>
<li>Builds displays and arranges department products to make attractive presentations, highlighting featured/unique/seasonal items.</li>
<li>Partners with department manager to plan for weekly ads, events and holidays using tools and reports.</li>
<li>Maintains professional vendor relationships.</li>
<li>Coordinates with Promotions department to plan and conduct in-store tastings.</li>
<li>Ensures customer special orders are accurately placed and delivered.</li>
<li>Receives and unloads merchandise; checks quality, count and condition. Communicates any issues to management and merchandising team.</li>
<li>Stocks and rotates products with attention to product quality and expiration dates.</li>
<li>Maintains the organization, rotation and cleanliness of back stock areas.</li>
<li>Wraps, weighs and prices Meat and Seafood products according to department policy.</li>
<li>Assists customers with product selection. Shares product knowledge with customers. Makes recommendations and suggestions based on customer needs.</li>
<li>Ensures department compliance with company organic standards.</li>
<li>Partners with department manager to plan, lead and analyze the monthly perishable inventory.</li>
<li>Demonstrates ability to multi-task and manage own time. Adjusts priorities, productivity, efficiency and speed based on needs and conditions of department and store.</li>
<li>Observes all rules and policies as listed in the New Seasons Market Staff Handbook and Operations Manual.</li>
<li>Performs other duties and tasks as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>Customer Service and Merchandising</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains excellent department conditions, including appearance, cleanliness, signage, staffing and service levels. Models friendly and attentive customer service, coaches staff, recognizes accomplishments and identifies areas of opportunity.</li>
<li>Handles escalated difficult customer service situations and seeks solutions to delight the customer. Trains and coaches staff to do the same.</li>
<li>Reads and understands merchandising information. Partners with department manager to develop and execute merchandising plans to promote peak seasonal/ad item/holiday products, drive sales and create an optimal customer shopping experience.</li>
<li>Demonstrates excellent knowledge of products in the department and general understanding of culinary terminology, product labeling and common dietary restrictions.</li>
<li>Shares product knowledge with customers and staff. Makes recommendations and suggestions based on customer needs. Partners with department manager to establish consistent program for communicating product information and knowledge to staff.</li>
<li>Demonstrates an awareness of trends in the industry.</li>
</ul>
<p>Financial Stewardship</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates a solid understanding of labor, sales and margin goals and factors that influence them. Partners with department manager to meet labor and margin goals.</li>
<li>Ensures that department adheres to company invoice, credit, transfers and inventory procedures.</li>
<li>Coaches department to maintain appropriate, accurate and organized inventory and back stock. Handles damaged and spoiled product according to company policy.</li>
<li>Demonstrates creativity in merchandising and utilization of products in order to increase sales and minimize waste.</li>
<li>Leverages resources and labor to maximize efficiency, without compromising quality and service standards.</li>
</ul>
