<h2>Business Development Engineer - CA</h2>

<p>Precipart is enabling some of the greatest medtech and aerospace innovations coming out of California today. While you will work remotely and travel frequently, you will collaborate constantly with your Sales and Engineering teams in Farmingdale, New York via video conference and other digital connectivity platforms. You will be an integral member of the team.</p>
