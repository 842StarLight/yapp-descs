<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Salesperson works with professionalism and consistency to successfully achieve sales goals.</p>
<p><b>
Reporting Relationship</b></p>
<p>
The salesperson reports to the manager/franchisee as applicable.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Relationships</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Greets and meets showroom walk-in prospects as shared floor coverage requires.</li>
<li>Registers showroom prospects in walk-in lead tracking system.</li>
<li>Creates outside leads and sales other than those generated by the Idea Center and Design Studio’s advertising.</li>
<li>Qualifies each prospect’s wants and needs into a plan of sales actions.</li>
<li>Performs job site measuring and gathers the information necessary to create a cabinet layout, which meets the customer’s needs.</li>
<li>Photographs the remodeling jobs that are being measured to provide “before” photographs for the showroom album.</li>
<li>Gathers information on all job-related products in order to provide a presentation to the customer.</li>
<li>Assembles a quote and related information to present to the customer with the goal of creating a sale.</li>
<li>Revises quotes or drawings as customers’ wants and needs are assessed.</li>
<li>Collects payments from customers.</li>
<li>Communicates necessary information to KTU Design Services to enable them to complete a cabinet order. Or, if KTU Design Services is not used complete and place the order himself.</li>
<li>Completes the purchase order requisition to enable the Administrative Assistant to type the purchase order.</li>
<li>Initiates any job change orders.</li>
<li>Meets the installer at the job prior to installation to inspect and cover all necessary details.</li>
<li>Continues customer contact during the job and monitor all related activities.</li>
<li>Processes all credit card payments from the customer.</li>
<li>Work in a commission base position.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation from $40,000-$100,000 per year based on sales.</b></p>
</div></div>
