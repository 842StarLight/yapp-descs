<div><b>
The HRT team</b></div>
<div>
At Deloitte Consulting LLP, helping our clients exploit new technologies like employee engagement tools, advanced workforce planning and analytics we empower HR to better support the business and create value. HR organizations now reduce costs and improve operating effectiveness through process improvements, shared services, outsourcing and employee self-service, but that’s only part of the story, we help them to drive the value this delivers and help the business achieve its strategic objectives in performance and growth. Learn more about our HR Transformation practice.</div>

<div><b>
Payroll &amp; Workforce Management Solutions</b></div>
<div>
With the emergence of the gig economy, the change in the demographics of the workforce and the ever-evolving business needs, it’s clear that the workforce of tomorrow will look very different than the workforce of today. To be a true business partner with the <b>credibility</b> to lead the business – not just sit at the table - HR can’t just be managing talent, you need to be building the workforce of the future. Working with Deloitte enables you to gain the insights you need to build and execute a workforce strategy that will help the business survive today’s disruption.</div>

<div><b>
Qualifications</b></div>
<div>
Required:</div>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years of related experience</li>
<li>
Consulting experience required</li>
<li>
Superior knowledge of the HR function &amp; processes</li>
<li>
Experience with workforce software such as Workbrain, Workforce Software, Kronos, ADP eTime, or Ceridian suite of time and attendance software and system infrastructure</li>
<li>
Experience in one or more of the following areas: Workforce Management, labor cost optimization, payroll tracking, workforce analytics, interfaces using WIM</li>
<li>
Lead the full lifecycle of a project including, Business Analysis, Functional Specification, System Configuration, Design, Testing</li>
<li>
Experience leading requirements gathering workshops or facilitating meetings</li>
<li>
Experience leading the vendor selection process(client and supplier sides) including RFPs, RFIs, scoring and evaluating products and services</li>
<li>
Ability to interact at all levels of the client organization</li>
<li>
Strong problem solving and troubleshooting skills with the ability to exercise mature judgment</li>
<li>
Experience with and desire to mentor junior staff</li>
<li>
Up to 80% travel required</li>
<li>
Must be legally authorized to work in the United States without the need for employer sponsorship, now or at any time in the future.</li>
</ul>
<div>
 Preferred:</div>
<ul>
<li>
4+ years of related experience</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree strongly preferred</li>
<li>
External consulting experience with a big consulting firm.</li>
</ul>
