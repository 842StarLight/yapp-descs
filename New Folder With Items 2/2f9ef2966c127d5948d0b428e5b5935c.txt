<h2>Senior Producer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Job Description:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
Marketing, Sales &amp; Service plays an important role in Ford’s revitalization as a modern, progressive vehicle and mobility services company delivering a trusted customer experience. Candidates should have a vast depth of knowledge and expertise in implications of data and taking actions based on those, as well as have a passion for the lifetime value of a customer. Ford MS&amp;S embraces an audience-first mindset and way of doing business that improves customer loyalty, vehicle repurchase, as well as additional sales of vehicles, parts, accessories and services. We are the eyes, ears and voices of Ford, meeting customer demand by delivering the right products to the right dealers at the right time.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Come be a part of the future of Marketing at Ford. We are looking for an experienced producer with strategic thinking skills to support the North American brand team with the production side of the marketing business.<br>
<br>
Our ideal candidate is a seasoned advertising veteran with the skills to help build Ford’s internal production department. They should have extensive experience as an agency producer, agency business affairs, post-production producer and/or production company producer. This role will develop relationships and lead work with agency production personnel and vendors, and provide internal production expertise. The position is located in Dearborn, Michigan and includes travel to production locations.</div>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage content production across one or more nameplate(s) and work with multiple agencies
</li>
<li>Project, manage and balance production budgets
</li>
<li>Share production subject matter expertise with Ford’s internal team
</li>
<li>Work with the Head of Content Production to build production infrastructure
</li>
<li>Maintain existing vendor relationships and develop new relationships
</li>
<li>Provide insight on current production and technology trends
</li>
<li>Keep up with new vendors and production tactics
</li>
<li>Evaluate, solicit bids, and negotiate with vendors
</li>
<li>Manage multiple agency relationships
</li>
<li>Support production schedule and travel to production locations
</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Basic Qualifications</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Associate's degree
</li>
<li>5+ years advertising industry experience
</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Agency experience (producer or business affairs) or Production side (or post production) experience
</li>
<li>Knowledge of SAG, AICP, AICE guidelines
</li>
<li>Knowledge of film and digital capabilities and nomenclature
</li>
<li>Current knowledge of industry prices, trends, and vendor capabilities
</li>
<li>Experience managing agency/partner relations, either on the agency side or on the client side
</li>
<li>Proactive in thought and communication
</li>
<li>Extensive experience with production budgeting
</li>
<li>People person with strong leadership and mentoring skills
</li>
<li>Dynamic problem-solver who is always thinking of new ways to solve issues
</li>
</ul>
