<p><b>Job Decription</b></p>
<div>
<div>
<p>The senior project engineer position serves in multiple roles as a valuable member of the Systems team. This position will work in many functions of project delivery including detailed design engineering, concept engineering, and project management, on site integration support, customer support, and sales consulting. The individual will rotate through each participate in each function of this position based on our current workload, along with strengths and desires of the team members. The ultimate goal of the systems senior project engineer role is to put talented individuals in a position to make Bastian's large projects as successful as possible.</p>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<p>General Job Duties</p>
<ul>
<li>Rotate between roles depending on current project needs</li>
<li>Lead AutoCAD drawings, quotes, alternate designs, quickly and efficiently pricing a system.</li>
<li>Perform detailed design work laying out exact conveyor models, supporting structures, and all other items that need to be selected.</li>
<li>As project manager you will follow project management principles to ensure the project is completed on time and on budget.</li>
<li>As onsite integration assistant, you will help troubleshoot system ensuring smooth flow of product and increasing rate to peak performance.</li>
<li>Timely resolution of issues and customer satisfaction with Support</li>
<li>As site supervisor, assist with site installation to include material inventory, schedule tracking, and supervising subcontractors</li>
<li>Develop in depth understanding of all facets of material handling to include equipment, suppliers, and applications</li>
<li>Timely completion of all reports and logs to include time reports and travel expenses</li>
<li>Provide Customer support for ongoing and warranty projects</li>
<li>Maintain consistent positive and enthusiastic attitude</li>
<li>Pursue professional training and certifications (PMP, PE, etc)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree in Engineering (or related degree);</li>
<li>MBA or Master Degree in Engineering a plus.</li>
<li>5+ years of similar experience within an Automated Material Handling environment.</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership skills.</li>
<li>Strategic Selling Ability and customer service skills.</li>
<li>Excellent technical problem solving capability (hardware, controls)</li>
<li>Strong scheduling and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of financials, budgets, cash flow</li>
<li>Enjoys the Supply Chain Automation, Logistics Software business environment.</li>
<li>Willing to travel 20-40% (travel will vary depending on specific Projects, travel will be significantly less during engineering roles and higher during project management).</li>
<li>Enjoy working in team environment</li>
<li>Strong system engineering fundamentals.</li>
<li>Experience with AutoCAD, HyCad and other material handling design packages</li>
<br>
</ul>

Our team specializes in consulting, system design, project management, maintenance, and installation, while sourcing the best equipment and automation technology. We take great pride in providing exceptional service and flexibility to our customers.</p>
