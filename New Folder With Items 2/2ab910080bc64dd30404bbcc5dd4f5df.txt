<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Beneficial Use Marketing Representative</b></h4>
<p>Responsibilities include the use of company provided tools to identify organizations that produce or consume secondary chemicals and fuels <i>by</i> contacting perspective clients via telephone and e-mail. This position develops <i>opportunities</i> <i>by</i> identifying client challenges and issues. You will connect with key decision makers who are responsible for sustainability programs, environmental management, purchasing and general management to get agreement to further discussions with Altiras’ subject matter experts.</p>
<p>
This is an entry-level position. Knowledge of chemicals and petroleum products is helpful but not required. Success in this role <i>positions</i> the candidate for advancement into Altiras’ sales organization.</p>
<p>
If you possess the background we are seeking and believe you are a fit with Altiras, please <i>email</i> your <i>resume</i> to <i>careers</i>@altiras.com.</p>
