<h2>Product Engineer III (Power Products)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Title: Product Engineer III</b></p>
<p><b>Position Reports to: Project Engineering Manager</b></p>
<p><b>About the Company</b></p>
<p>With a vision of the future, Micropac Industries, Inc. is leading the way with innovation and product development for mission critical electronics. At Micropac, you will have the opportunity to learn, grow and create exciting programs for satellite, medical, defense/aerospace, and high reliability industrial applications. Micropac has applications on Mars and 30,000 feet underground. Our Core Values include creating a fun and innovative place to work while fostering an environment of teamwork and learning. Micropac is a publicly traded company (MPAD) with approximately 130 employees and a 50-year record of mission critical success.</p>
<p><b>About the Job</b></p>
<p>Responsible for electrical engineering support of assigned product categories of released production products and design of similar spin-off products that may evolve. The Product Engineer supports the external customer with product applications and the internal team with resolution of technical and quality issues that may arise. Internal customers include peer engineering groups and QA and manufacturing operations that may rely on his/her engineering expertise to assist in new designs or yield and/or process improvements. Works with New Business Development in the area of prototypes, samples, product documentation and cost projections as needed to quote and secure new business. This position will require some Mechanical Engineering aptitude in addition to the core Electrical Engineering disciplines.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>The essential functions and key responsibilities for the position include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong focus on Power Systems from component selection through system requirements definition, must have DC-DC converter design experience</li>
<li>Establish a strong focus on internal and external customer service</li>
<li>Provide technical support via direct communication to sales, marketing and customers</li>
<li>Provide technical support and assist in resolution of manufacturing problems as required</li>
<li>Help generate, review and approve all product documentation</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of BOM structure and change control process</li>
<li>Comprehensive understanding of product line designs and project cost make-up</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Team player in pursuit and support of cost reduction activities to achieve company goals</li>
<li>Work with other product engineers, Business Line Managers, Product Sales Managers and department managers to ensure technical documentation needs are planned and met</li>
<li>Review and approve Engineering Change Orders (ECO) applicable to product line responsibilities</li>
<li>Strong communication skills and demonstrated proficiency in MS Office platforms.</li>
<li>AutoCAD, SolidWorks, Cadence Orcad and PSpice design and modeling software proficiency.</li>
<li>Good interpersonal and training skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, resolve technical and manufacturing issues without detailed direction and work as part of a team</li>
<li>Leads Preliminary and Critical Design reviews for Power Products and Systems</li>
<li>Capability to accurately and efficiently work on multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Ability to travel “as required” to customer site(s) to provide technical support and training</li>
<li>Design and Development of Power Electronic Applications</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements: </b> <b>Product Engineer III </b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS degree in engineering or related discipline with an MS degree preferred</li>
<li>Minimum 8-10 years working experience as a Product Engineer or related engineering position with a strong focus on Power Systems Design</li>
<li>Demonstrates accuracy and proficiency in execution of job responsibilities along with strong interpersonal skills across all departments</li>
<li>Capable of leading a team in a company directed project or effort</li>
<li>Leads Micropac Power Systems Design team on new and existing products, subassemblies and next level assemblies</li>
</ul>
