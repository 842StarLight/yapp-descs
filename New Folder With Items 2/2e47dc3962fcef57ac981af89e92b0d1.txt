 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>
St. Petersburg, FL Insurance MGA/Wholesale Insurance Brokerage firm with corporate offices in Slidell, La has an immediate opportunity for experienced Multi-line Underwriters &amp; Brokers. We will consider remote opportunities to the right qualified candidates as an option, or for a person in a state where we are licensed. AL, FL, GA, LA, MS, OK, TX</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Underwrite new and renewal business for insurance company partners under general direction</li>
<li>Oversee the evaluation, classification, and rating of risk to determine acceptability, coverage, and pricing</li>
<li>Implement underwriting policies under the direction of the Management and ensure compliance with these policies</li>
<li>Monitor critical areas, such as loss ratio, costs, retention ratio and new business hit ratio, formulating corrective action where necessary</li>
<li>Participate in national and local advertising, telemarketing and agency visits/sales calls to our customers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications/ Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent combination of education and experience preferred</li>
<li>5+ Years of underwriting experience, excess &amp; surplus <b>MGA/Wholesale experience</b> <b>REQUIRED</b> in a Production or Production Management role.</li>
<li>Strong sales and marketing skills a must, business acumen, interest in continued education and professional development</li>
<li>Insurance license, CPCU, CIC or comparable designations preferred</li>
<li>Strong computer skills and ability to work with multi-faceted systems.</li>
<li>Demonstrate strong knowledge of UW Concepts, practices, and procedures</li>
</ul>
