<h2>Head of Marketing - Series B fintech/agtech startup</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Location:</b> Los Angeles, CA (Downtown)
<br><br>
<b>Reports to:</b> Chief Commercial Officer
<br><br>
Produce Pay is revolutionizing the fresh produce supply chain by providing immediate liquidity to farmers who grow fresh fruits and vegetables. Produce Pay is disrupting an industry by providing a technology-driven solution to a critical on-farm need. We are a Los Angeles-based startup that has raised over $95MM to transform the multi-billion dollar fresh produce industry.
<br><br>
We are looking for a dynamic Head of Marketing to build up Produce Pay’s marketing capabilities from the ground up. Success in this role means a dramatic increase in Marketing Qualified Leads (MQL) to our enterprise sales team. How you get there is up to you.
<br><br>
The ability to work independently is key to this cross-functional role. You’ll lead marketing efforts across our sales, product, business development, and executive teams to engage our key audiences across the fresh produce supply chain. You should have a reputation for being a star performer who’s held end-to-end ownership over a marketing funnel for a B2B product. You will conceive, manage, analyze, and optimize content and campaigns to drive MQL. If you enjoy experimentation and have a "growth hacker" mindset, then come join our crew.
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><ul>
<li>Drive customer acquisition through a diverse range of acquisition channels</li>
<li>Generate data-driven hypotheses and execute experiments across the AARRR funnel (acquisition, activation, retention, revenue, referral) to identify insights and key opportunities for improvement</li>
<li>Ramp up a content marketing strategy: research, write, and develop stories on a wide range of topics pertinent to fresh produce</li>
<li>Set up web analytics and business intelligence tools to better understand our customers and exploit those insights in marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Manage performance metrics and budgets to intelligently scale acquisition efforts.</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal teams / vendors to evaluate and optimize our growth strategy and performance</li>
<li>Distill Produce Pay value proposition into clear, compelling copy across our range of financial and analytic product offerings</li>
<li>Maintaining content calendars, planning for future stories, and driving timely production schedules</li>
</ul>
