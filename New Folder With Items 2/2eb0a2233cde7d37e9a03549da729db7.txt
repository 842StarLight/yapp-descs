<h2>Relationship Manager - FI Sales (Mid-Level)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Relationship Manager (RM) will contribute to the growth and expansion of their assigned existing Financial Institution accounts. Reporting directly to the VP of Relationship Management, the RM will work closely with leadership, operations and subject matter experts to pursue growth opportunities, drive transactions and improve customer satisfaction. This position is responsible for learning about their assigned client’s business, addressing their needs and executing sales strategies to expand Cardtronics product offerings to ultimately drive growth.</p>
<p>
Cardtronics continues to experience growth across the country and we are expanding our team to support that growth. We work hard. We expect a lot. We reward well. We want the best.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive business growth by expanding revenue with current client base through building pipeline and executing new sales strategies while maintaining strong working relationships with existing clients.</li>
<li>Develop a strategic plan for assigned clients in partnership with leadership</li>
<li>Create and maintain a pipeline of reasonable opportunities in a transparent manner.</li>
<li>Create in-depth proposals and present to c-level clients.</li>
<li>Assist in formulating marketing strategies to ensure customers are achieving full potential of their Cardtronics’ relationship.</li>
<li>Onboard new clients attained by the Sales Account Executive team.</li>
<li>Represent Cardtronics at key customer meetings and sponsored events.</li>
<li>Consult with assigned clients to share industry best practices.</li>
<li>Provide critical “voice of customer” feedback to leadership.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
