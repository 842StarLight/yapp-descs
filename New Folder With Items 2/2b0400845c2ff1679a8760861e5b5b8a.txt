 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Lane Shark USA - Administrative </b><b>Assistant</b></p>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>Lane Shark USA is seeking a motivated, enthusiastic Administrative Assistant to work with our sales and leading staff to ensure all daily operations responsibilities are fulfilled.</p>
<p><b>Position Overview</b></p>
<p>Reporting to the Operations manager the Administrative Assistant is responsible for providing administrative services to the Operations Manager and other supporting staff. This includes tracking sales data; maintaining promotional materials inventory; planning meetings and trade shows; maintaining databases; preparing reports.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Implements marketing and advertising campaigns by assembling and analyzing sales forecasts; preparing marketing and advertising strategies, plans, and objectives; planning and organizing promotional presentations; updating calendars.</li>
<li>Making sales call to potential clients.</li>
<li>Keeps promotional materials ready by coordinating requirements with leading staff</li>
<li>Supports sales staff by providing sales data, market trends, forecasts, account analyses, new product information; relaying customer services requests.</li>
<li>Researches competitive products by identifying and evaluating product characteristics, market share, pricing, and advertising; maintaining research databases.</li>
<li>Plans meetings and trade shows by identifying, assembling, and coordinating requirements; establishing contacts; developing schedules and assignments; coordinating mailing lists.</li>
<li>Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities; reading work related publications.</li>
<li>Accomplishes organization goals by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and different requests; exploring opportunities to add value to job accomplishments.</li>
<li>Manages all content on all social platforms.</li>
<li>Responsible for analyzing and responding to all requests on social platforms.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2 years experience in a professional marketing and or sales field.</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, especially in Apple platforms.</li>
<li>Strong social networking skills.</li>
<li>Proficient in all social platforms (Linkden, YouTube, FaceBook, Instagram, and Pinterest)</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>

<ul><li>Administrative Assistant: 2 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
