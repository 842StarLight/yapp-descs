<h2>Senior Manager of Project Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Plans, directs, leads and coordinates all activities of technology service delivery projects through all phases of a project from inception through completion.</li>
<li>
Consults with management and the sales teams to review service delivery program/project proposals to determine goals, time-frame, funding limitations, and procedures for accomplishing tasks, staffing requirements, allotment of resources, and the projects priority and feasibility within the program objectives.</li>
<li>
Develops program/project plans specifying goals, staffing, scheduling and allocation of available resources.</li>
<li>
Formulates and defines technical scope and objectives of the program and its related projects.</li>
<li>
Identifies and schedules program/project deliverables, milestones, and required tasks.</li>
<li>
Coordinates and responds to requests for changes from original specifications.</li>
<li>
Monitors program/project results. Develops quality assurance plans, communication plans, risk analysis and mitigation strategies.</li>
<li>
Tracks / documents lessons learned and develops improvement / training plans.</li>
<li>
Leads a team of Project Managers for assigned Program and all program/project deliverables.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What we’re looking for...</b>
</p>
<p>You'll need to have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or four or more years of work experience.</li>
<li>
Six or more years of relevant work experience.</li>
<li>
Supplier management experience.</li>
<li>
Experience supporting Enterprise Customers.</li>
<li>
Experience with Microsoft Office product suite, including PowerPoint, Excel &amp; Word.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Even better if you have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
A degree.</li>
<li>
Six or more years of proven Project Management experience with proven track record and experience with deploying and delivering complex solutions.</li>

High level technical understanding of networks/servers/cloud/storage.</li>
<li>
Broad Verizon product experience.</li>
<li>
Proven leadership skills and capabilities.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage and negotiate with required internal and external organizations.</li>

Knowledge and experience with Clarity.</li>

Project Management Professional (PMP) Certification.</li>

ITIL V3 Certification.</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel.</li>
</ul>
