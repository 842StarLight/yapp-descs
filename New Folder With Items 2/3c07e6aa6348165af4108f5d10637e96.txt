<p><b>
About the team:</b></p>
<p>
SAP Concur, a part of SAP, imagines the way the world should work, offering cloud-based services that make it simple to manage travel and expenses. By connecting data, applications and people, SAP Concur delivers an effortless experience and total transparency into spend wherever and whenever it happens. SAP Concur services adapt to individual employee preferences and scale to meet the needs of companies from small to large, so they can focus on what matters most. The company serves more than 30,000 clients representing more than 27 million users in over 150 countries.</p>

A few years ago, SAP Concur launched the SAP Concur App Center (www.concur.com/appcenter) offering customers a listing of pre-built solutions that work seamlessly with SAP Concur. The App Center offers a way for prospects to see how SAP Concur is different than the competition; a way for clients to find solutions that are prebuilt to work with what they already have; and a way for users to connect their SAP Concur accounts with the apps that let them travel simple. The foundation of the SAP Concur App Center is a myriad of partners across industries who provide simple, targeted solutions to SAP Concur client’s everyday problems. As a technical resource on the App Center platform, you will play crucial roles across the Partner Journey to help them succeed as an SAP Concur partner.</p>
