<h2>Southern California Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Southern California Account Executive</p>
<p>Three Way Logistics, Inc</p>
<p>Norwalk, CA</p>
<p>Three Way Logistics, Inc. is a leading full service logistics provider; supporting our customers with specialized services including warehousing, trucking, crating and machinery moving. Our company focuses on capital equipment moves including semiconductor, aerospace, medical markets. Our team is energetic and experienced to support growth in the Southern California Market.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>The Southern California Sales Executive will be responsible for achieving maximum sales profitability and growth within the Los Angeles market and its outlying areas. They will focus on machinery moving/rigging along with providing customers with full service logistics services (warehousing/distribution, local, and intrastate/interstate transportation).</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Promotes/sells/secure orders from prospective customers through a relationship-based approach.</li>
<li>Establish, develop and maintain business relationships with some current customers and prospective customers and generate new Southern California Logistics business.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Make telephone calls and in-person visits and presentations to existing and prospective customers.</li>
<li>Weekly account check-in via phone, email, or face to face meeting.</li>
<li>Maintain pipeline 40% above sales goals.</li>
<li>Develop and participate in quarterly business reviews of accounts.</li>
<li>Research sources for developing prospective customers and for information to determine their potential.</li>
<li>Create and provide written proposals for prospective customers.</li>
<li>Deal with and expedite the resolution of customer service issues.</li>
<li>Coordinate sales effort with Norwalk operations team to meet service needs.</li>
<li>Analyze the territory/market’s potential and determine the value of existing and prospective customers’ value to the organization.</li>
<li>Supply management with reports including forecasts, pipeline and opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree</li>
<li>2+ year’s sales experience with record of exceeding sales goals.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office (PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Experience working with customer relationship management software</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Driven and highly self-motivated.</li>
<li>Strong logistics or machinery moving industry knowledge.</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license with acceptable driving record.</li>
</ul>

<li>Employee’s office will reside in Norwalk, CA.</li>

</ul>
