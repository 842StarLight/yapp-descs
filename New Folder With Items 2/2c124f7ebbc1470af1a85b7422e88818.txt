<h2>Insights Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Insight Coordinator supports Sales Enablement activities by researching, developing and designing insight messaging materials; Supports growth plans of commercial leaders by compiling, formatting, and reporting information related to insight messages and pitches; Develops message strategies for industry conferences and events; Works closely with many different teams and departments to assess messaging support needs and provide guidance and assistance where appropriate.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The responsibilities of this position include, but are not limited to, the following:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support product and commercial leaders in developing sales insight messages.
</li>
<li>Maintain the Insight library (tracker) by checking, adjusting and reporting on progress.
</li>
<li>Create visual materials used in pitch packages including infographics, PowerPoints, and other design collateral.
</li>
<li>Work with subject matter experts to collect all necessary information for pitch support documents to bring them to completion.
</li>
<li>Supports sales presentations by developing customized collateral.
</li>
<li>Work with Marketing Associate to develop messaging strategies for various trade shows and market segment conferences.
</li>
<li>Work directly with many cross-functional teams and departments to accomplish message plans.
</li>
<li>Update job knowledge by participating in educational/training opportunities.
</li>
<li>Accomplish sales enablement and organization’s mission by completing related tasks as needed.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>To select this job as one of interest to you, at a minimum, you must have the following:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA or BS degree in Marketing, Communications, Sales or similar role.
</li>
<li>1-3 years’ work experience in roles related to degree.
</li>
<li>Graphic design experience required.
</li>
<li>Skilled in Adobe InDesign and other relevant graphic design software.
</li>
<li>Experience with PowerPoint creation and design.
</li>
<li>Strong organizational and time-management skills.
</li>
<li>Outstanding written and verbal communication, analytical, motivational, and leadership skills.
</li>
<li>Basic experience and knowledge of the grid level electric power system and utility industry.
</li>
<li>Available to travel domestically as needed.<br>
</li>
</ul>
