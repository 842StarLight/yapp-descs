<h2>FMS Financial Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview:
</b></p>
<p>Candidate will support the United States Navy, Program Executive Office Command, Control, Communications, Computers and Intelligence (PEO C4I), International C4I Integration Program Office (PMW 740) located in San Diego, California.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead Foreign Military Sales (FMS) case financial execution, including establishment, allocation and transfer of funding in Navy Enterprise Resource Planning (N-ERP)</li>
<li>
Process all standard funding documents, tracking charges and payments, posting obligations and deliverables in Management Information System, International Logistics (MISIL) and financial reconciliation</li>
<li>
Prepare required documentation for periodic financial status review meetings</li>
<li>
Identify, analyze and correct financial discrepancies, making recommendations, and preparing tools to prevent and correct financial and accounting errors</li>
<li>
Conduct FMS case reconciliation, financial anomaly resolution, and FMS case closure</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Minimum Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Completion of a Bachelor of Arts or Science degree from an accredited college or university</li>
<li>
At least three (3) years work experience in FMS financial management</li>
<li>
Demonstrated proficiency in Microsoft Office applications (Word, PowerPoint, Outlook)</li>
<li>
Extensive experience with Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>
Active Secret Security Clearance</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Preferred Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least three (3) years work experience using the following DoD financial systems: N-ERP, MISIL, Security Cooperation Information Portal (SCIP), Information Warehouse (IW), Defense Integrated Financial System (DIFS), Defense Case Accountability System (DCAS), Computer Optimized Batch Reconciliation Application (COBRA)</li>
<li>
Completion of DISCS SCM courses, including International Programs Security Requirements (IPSR) and SCM-Case Financial (SCM-CF)</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience performing financial management in a Security Cooperation/FMS environment with DoD Acquisition Commands</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience producing executive summaries, enterprise financial reporting and analysis and briefing packages to facilitate leadership decision-making</li>
</ul>
