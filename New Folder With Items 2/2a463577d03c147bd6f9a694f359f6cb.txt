<h2>Field Service Engineer - DALLAS, TEXAS</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>COMET Technologies USA is the headquarters as well as an important sales, distribution and service hub for the COMET Group. From this modern facility, we provide our innovative COMET X-ray and ebeam source technology and portable X-ray systems to leading manufacturers, research labs and government sites within North and South America.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description and Main Tasks
</b><br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Installation and repair of X-Ray inspection systems at the customers' location.
</li>
<li>Performs preventive maintenance on X-Ray systems.
</li>
<li>Provides system training to customers on maintenance, operation, and programming as required.
</li>
<li>Supervision of the customer and contractor personnel as required.
</li>
<li>Completes all job-related paperwork and submits to the Hudson office each week. This includes expense reports and service reports which detail problems encountered, solutions taken, and parts used.
</li>
<li>Maintains daily communication with supervisor and/or customer service reps and pro vides appropriate updates on job-related progress and schedule.
</li>
<li>Takes responsibility for personal improvement and identifies training needs to management.
</li>
<li>Makes effective use of downtime during periods in the office. This may include customer phone support, service/PM contract follow-up, and internal project support.
</li>
<li>Identify improvements/bugs in equipment design and software and report appropriately to engineering/customer service.
</li>
<li>Maintains a clean and professional attitude and appearance at all times.
</li>
<li>Reports to work on-time in accordance with office policy or daily customer schedule.
</li>
<li>Follows all appropriate safety procedures and actively promotes a safe work environment.
</li>
<li>Establish and maintain effective relationships within the organization worldwide in or der to develop a support network.
</li>
<li>Accomplish tasks as directed by management.
</li>
<li>Travel represents approximately 90% of total time.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Mechanical / Electrical Engineering Expertise, prefer a 2 year technical degree certification.
</li>
<li>Understanding of fundamental electronic principals.
</li>
<li>Understanding of basic 3-phase and single phase power systems and components.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of PLC logic and motion control systems.
</li>
<li>Strong technical writing and communication skills.
</li>
<li>Familiarity with common lab testing equipment such as multimeter, oscilloscope, and soldering tools.
</li>
<li>Strong PC and networking skills (Windows environment).
</li>
<li>Strong troubleshooting skills and ability to work alone with little or no technical support.
</li>
<li>2+ years of working experience with troubleshooting electronic equipment.
</li>
<li>Experience in an industrial environment.
</li>
</ul>

</b></p>

</li>

</li>

</li>
</ul>
