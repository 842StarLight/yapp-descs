<p><b>Business Development Associate</b></p>
<p><b>
Location: Dallas, TX Area</b></p>
<p>
Join our team as a <b>Business Development Associate</b> for a growing company that has a huge heart, fast-paced work environment, and great benefits! Work with passionate, talented professionals that are dedicated to serving our clients and feel rewarded knowing that your job MATTERS!</p>
<p><b>
The Company:</b></p>
<p>
Angels of Care Pediatric Home Health is a clinically-operated company that has been caring for the medically fragile community since 2000! The company was founded by a nurse with a huge heart, and today we pride ourselves on our values: <b>H</b>eart, <b>A</b>dvocacy, <b>L</b>ove, and <b>O</b>utreach! <b>#HALO</b></p>
<p><b>
The Position:</b></p>
<p>
The BDA is responsible for growing their assigned territory. The position requires that the BDA be responsible for building new client referrals and maintaining current clients. The BDA will work with providers, community resources, and AOC staff to accomplish goals. The BDA must be knowledgeable of their territory and the services provided by AOC. Proven ability to not only secure new business but also provide excellent customer service. Candidates with a background in Pediatric Speech, Occupational, and Physical Therapy will be preferred. BDA’s will be immersed in AOC vision and values and share our vision with prospective referral sources, clients and AOC staff. Other duties include, but are not limited to the following.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Utilizing company software programs in order to maintain and track referrals and current client information</li>
<li>
Overall Retention, Growth, and Customer Satisfaction of each branch in their territory</li>
<li>
Meeting monthly and quarterly goals given by upper management</li>
<li>
Submitting weekly activity reports to management team</li>
<li>
Qualifying potential new business for their branch</li>
<li>
Assisting with recruiting and career fairs</li>
<li>
Will exercise necessary cost control measures.</li>
<li>
Will strive to maintain positive internal and external customer service relationships.</li>
<li>
Must be able to demonstrate effective leadership skills.</li>
<li>
Must demonstrate effective communication skills by conveying necessary information accurately, listening effectively and asking questions when clarification is needed.</li>
<li>
Must be able to be depended upon to plan and organize work effectively and ensure its completion.</li>
<li>
Must be able to demonstrate reliability by arriving to work on time and taking breaks in expected time frames.</li>
<li>
Will be expected to meet all productivity requirements.</li>
</ul>
