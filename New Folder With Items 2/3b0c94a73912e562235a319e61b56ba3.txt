<h2>Dental Outreach Health Educator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Under the direction of the Dental Project Coordinator, the Outreach Health Educator will be responsible for outreach, education, recruitment and management of client activities on oral health delivery systems.</p>
<p>Assist in the fulfillment of the agency?s vision, ?For Those We Serve,? by empowering individuals who thrive as healthy, self-sufficient and contributing members of our communities.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES/ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<p>Responsible for client education, increasing enrollment, client retention and scheduling appointments with oral dental health providers participating in the Denti-Cal Program.</p>
<p>Provides education on oral hygiene and preventative services to clients.</p>
<p>Tracks client contact and provides patient reports on the number of appointments scheduled and the efforts made to remind patients of their appointments with the dentists.</p>
<p>Contacts dental providers to determine if the efforts to educate and support the client/family resulted in kept appointments.</p>
<p>Makes referrals to dentists who are participating in the incentive program to increase dental continuity of care for children enrolled in the Medi-Cal Program.</p>
<p>Updates and maintains client data files, family case files in accordance with the program requirements.</p>
<p>Provides excellent customer service, technical assistance or referral services including periodic telephone follow-ups to parents and providers who have questions about their specific requirements.</p>
<p>Coordinates and provides home visits to clients/families needing one-on-one education and information on oral dental health.</p>
<p>Contacts client/family to ensure that they have secured a dentist. Provides assistance, in the identification of a dentist or a dental care center as needed.</p>
<p>Assists client/family with initial appointment setting.</p>
<p>Provides patient support in assisting family in arriving at their scheduled dental appointments.</p>
<p>Attends strategic plan and oversight meetings.</p>
<p>Travels for agency business using personal vehicle.</p>
<p>Maintains regular attendance and punctuality.</p>
<p>Works evenings and weekends as required.</p>
<p>Performs other duties as assigned.</p>
</div>
