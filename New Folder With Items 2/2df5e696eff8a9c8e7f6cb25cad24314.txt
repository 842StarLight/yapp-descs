<h2>Logistics Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>1. This position is responsible for managing all systems and personnel associated with all aspects of Porta Kleen’s Logistics and mobile assets.</p>
<p>
2. Ensures the availability and deployment of all physical and human assets, required to exceed the customer’s expectations.</p>
<p>
3. The position is responsible for all Porta Kleen DOT compliance.</p>
<p>
4. The position assists all divisions, maintenance and sales to optimize efficiency, quality and costs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<p>
1. The position is responsible for managing and performing all HR functions associated with logistics personnel and indirect management of the drivers.</p>
<p>
2. Ensures equipment requirements, location and status are coordinated with maintenance, operations, sales, and the customer.</p>
<p>
3. Works closely with Sales and managers to make recommendations on execution of new and existing jobs.</p>
<p>
4. Monitors and recommends improvements for CDL driver and route driver staffing levels.</p>
<p>
5. The position must understand the drive the P&amp;L statement and meet or exceed budgeted thresholds. The position is responsible for maximizing transportation costs and route profitability.</p>
<p>
6. Initiate or recommend, as warranted, system changes to improve overall equipment utilization.</p>
<p>
7. This position handles a broad range of problems and emergencies across multiple branch and customer locations, and requires a high degree of freedom, responsibility and accountability for taking actions in response to them.</p>
<p>
8. The position is heavily involved in managing and improving quality and ensures company standards are being met.</p>
<p>
9. The position must drive and meet safety objectives. Identify opportunities for improvement and provide training and support to operations.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>
1. This position is responsible for manpower, equipment and quality in multiple geographically dispersed locations.</p>
<p>
2. Investigate and recommend appropriate state-of-the-art logistics technology to improve logistics.</p>
<p>
3. Due to the nature of the business, long non-traditional hours are required as a regular part of the job.</p>
<p>
4. Travel to the different Branch sites and customer sites as needed to ensure compliance and continuous improvement. 20% travel required.</p>
<p>
5. Has the authority to make unsupervised decisions consistent with the scope of responsibilities.</p>
<p>
6. Provides calculated recommendations to the overall strategic planning process.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
Required Skills<br>
<br>
Required Experience<br>
<p>1. A Bachelors Degree with a specialized focus on logistics or engineering preferred.</p>
<p>
2. Work experience / education in Supply Chain Management field a plus</p>
<p>
3. Computer skills including word processing, spreadsheet, database, Internet and enterprise applications.</p>
<p>
4. Ability and experience in applying creative and effective solutions to a broad array of complex problems.</p>
<p>
5. Professional ability and experience communicating will all levels in a fast-paced, multi-branch business environment.</p>
<p>
6. Ability to conduct himself/herself in a professional and ethical manner regarding sensitive business and employee information.</p>
