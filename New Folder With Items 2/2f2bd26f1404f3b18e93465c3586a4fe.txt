<b>Your job:</b><br>
Indeed is looking for a strategic Director of Inside Sales, Acquisition in our Scottsdale office. We are looking for candidates who have 7+ years of sales experience and who have experience managing existing books of business in inside and outside sales capacities. A strong candidate will have excellent communication and leadership skills, and a desire to be a part of the fastest growing Internet company.
<br><br>
<b>About you:</b><br>
Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Hire, Develop &amp; Manage a team of Inside Sales Account Executives</li>
<li>Direct the sales process, qualifying and positioning of Indeed products and services</li>
<li>Lead new business generation and up selling of existing staffing industry clients</li>
<li>Ensure delivery of monthly, quarterly and annual sales goals and manage a weekly revenue forecast</li>
<li>Rollout sales business rules, practices and policies to sales territories</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><ul>
<li>7+ years of sales experience, preferably within Acquisition</li>
<li>2+ years of sales management experience</li>
<li>Must be able to articulate success in hiring, managing and motivating a junior sales team (Account Executives are entry level positions and these teams require high touch management)</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills Experience with interactive media, including pay-per-click products</li>
<li>Exposure to the online recruitment industry is a plus</li>
<li>Familiarity with performance-based advertising products</li>
<li>Successful track record in sales-driven environments and superior leadership skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience forecasting revenue</li>
</ul>
