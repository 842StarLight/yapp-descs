<h2>Retail Success Manager - Minneapolis</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Salsify is looking for an experienced, driven Retail Success Manager to drive the expansion of our strategic alliances with the largest US retailers. Target.com will be the largest account this person will manage so it would be preferred for this candidate to have experience working at or supporting Target.com. This is a key strategic position with high visibility across the company and with the executive team.
<br><br>
We are looking for someone located in Minneapolis who is comfortable in a work from home/remote environment.
<br><br>
Our SaaS platform gives brand manufacturers - including Coca-Cola, GlaxoSmithKline, MARS, Campbell's, Levis, LEGO, L'Oreal, Bosch, 3M, Anheuser-Busch InBev, and hundreds of others - the ability to execute merchandising and branding strategies everywhere their products are seen on the digital shelf globally.
<br><br>
Core to the value of the platform is the ability for brands to control how their products are found &amp; displayed on major retailer sites and to enable brands to interact with end consumers at the moment of purchase via Chat and interactive rich media.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Relationship</li>
<li>Onboard new retailer relationships and own program roadmaps.</li>
<li>Walk the halls of the largest US retailers. Become essential to their employees and grow Salsify's brand recognition and regard with their employee base.</li>
<li>Engage merchandising, site, and digital product content teams to drive awareness and growth of joint programs.</li>
<li>Maintain org charts at strategic retailers; stay ahead of changes that impact Salsify.</li>
<li>Brand adoption</li>
<li>Propose joint marketing campaigns to brands that help retailers expand their merchandising programs and raise awareness of Salsify solutions.</li>
<li>Partner with Salsify's product marketing team on enablement for Salsify's go-to-market teams.</li>
<li>Advocate for Salsify brands within a retailer to ensure that brand content is leveraged to its fullest capacity on a retailer's family of websites.</li>
<li>Program optimization</li>
<li>Grow close relationships with key stakeholders at retailers, including product management, to (a) optimize and grow presence of existing capabilities (b) ensure we have clear communication and escalation paths, and (c) have visibility and influence on roadmaps.</li>
<li>Create new opportunities for brands to improve a retailer's eCommerce experience through richer interactions powered by Salsify.</li>
<li>New capabilities</li>
<li>Participate in a retailer's content team and technical planning meetings to understand how Salsify fits into their ongoing digital strategy.</li>
<li>Ensure that Salsify is not left out of any high visibility eCommerce-related initiative.</li>
<li>Work with Salsify's Retail account management team to identify opportunities to deploy new capabilities.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Have worked with a strategic retailer before, either as an employee, as a technology vendor, or as a supplier.</li>
<li>5+ years experience in customer-facing, problem-solving positions in fast-paced companies. High tech preferred though competitive suppliers or agencies are also valuable.</li>
<li>Comfortable with ambiguity; leverage experience in business and/or retail and develop a plan to drive program adoption, awareness, and advocacy of leadership</li>
<li>A "people person": you are enthusiastic and energized by spending your day interacting with many different people.</li>
<li>High Emotional Intelligence and the ability to communicate effectively with many different types of people.</li>
<li>Experience in client services, client success, business development, or solutions engineering.</li>
<li>Experience navigating large, complex Fortune 1000 companies.</li>
<li>Extremely effective written, verbal, and presentation communication.</li>
<li>Driven, ambitious, and optimistic. Never give up. Ability to continue trying despite multiple false starts.</li>
<li>Highly organized. Strong project or program management experience.</li>
<li>Curiosity to learn about new businesses and industries, and you can quickly grasp and make effective use of industry- and company-specific terminology</li>
</ul>
