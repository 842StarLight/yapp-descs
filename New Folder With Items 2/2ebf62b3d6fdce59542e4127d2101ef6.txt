<h2>Part-Time Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>As our new Business Development Representative you will be the voice and face of our dealership. We need someone with a winning attitude and a professional appearance ready to work in this fast paced, customer driven environment. While we prefer someone with experience, candidates with great potential will also be considered. We are a very well established dealership , a large advertising budget, great inventory and aggressive pricing. We are looking for a part time, goal oriented individual looking for future growth.<br>
This department handles 100% of all incoming Internet Leads into the dealership.</p>
<p></p>
<div>
Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li>
Use lead management tool to manage and measure lead activity
</li>
<li>Leverage auto responders, personalized email templates and phone guides to drive appointments and sales.</li>
<li>
Manage and measure all Internet lead activity and results including: Initial email response, Personalized emails, Initial phone call to set appointments, Confirm appointments to drive your show rate
</li>
<li>Ongoing customer contact to promote loyalty and retention
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<ul>
<li>
Attitude: Polite and courteous, can work independently. Patient persistence</li>
<li>
Motivation: Task oriented, likes being organized and efficient. Likes systems and structure.
</li>
<li>Communication Skills: Has to have good writing and listening skills, and be a clear communicator over the phone and online.
</li>
<li>Technical Skills: Experience with a computer and the Internet with the ability to make people feel at home over the phone or through e-mail. Should have basic sales and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>
Organizational Skills: Should be an analytical, reliable, and thorough person who is well organized. Must be able to set up and administrate systems for sorting and tracking leads.</li>
</ul>
