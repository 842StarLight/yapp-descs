<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At Granicus, we build technology for the people who are changing our world! We’re passionate about driving citizen engagement and building a more accessible government. We live this through our government partnerships with local municipalities, state and federal agencies.</p>
<p>
Our sales team is dynamic, driven to succeed and has a Challenger mindset. We are looking for a driven, resourceful and intellectually curious sales executive to join our team. This team member will focus on cross-selling within current clients in our local municipal market, leading the sales cycle from prospecting to shake-of-hand.</p>
<p>
We’re a leader in the government technology sector and have a diverse platform of SaaS solutions. What we do makes a difference and improve the quality of people’s lives.</p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead sales with a Challenger mindset
</li>
<li>Proactive client outreach to build awareness, teach and develop new sales opportunities</li>
<li>Teach customers about market trends, challenges and issues they did not know existed, and share best practices to develop new sales opportunities</li>
<li>Work to uncover customer goals, challenges and desired outcomes to understand their business and engineer creative solutions</li>
<li>Continually working to develop and expand our current partnership(s); always working with our long-term future in mind</li>
<li>Build and deliver engaging custom presentations to groups ranging from 1-20 people</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Cultivate relationships both horizontally and vertically through communications and conducting follow-up communications</li>
<li>Develop comprehensive sales strategy and business plans to target key accounts, develop association partnership, and exceed sales goals
</li>
<p></p>
<li>Lead sales meetings via phone, live video/web, and in-person</li>
<li>Manage internal resources, including sales support, marketing, and solutions consultants to present a compelling case to prospects</li>
<li>Meet and exceed quarterly sales targets</li>
<li>Manage all aspects sales in your territory including; prospecting, activity tracking, opportunity management, forecasting revenue, contract management and closing deals</li>
<li>Pursue leads generated by yourself and our inbound and outbound marketing efforts</li>
<li>Support management team in developing sales strategy</li>
<li>Use Salesforce to track sales activity, notes and key sales information, manage opportunities thru stages of the sales process</li>
 <li>Write and present quarterly business reviews to Sales Management, Senior Management, and peers</li>
<li>Participate in selected industry activities, organizations/associations, and trade shows as needed</li>
<li>Travel about 25-40%</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Who You Are:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You are confident, competitive, and driven</li>
<li>You are goal-oriented and self-motivated; an entrepreneurial spirit</li>
<li>You take initiative and willing to go the extra mile</li>
<li>You are resourceful; okay with the unknown and capable of working to find quick and clever ways to overcome difficulties
</li>
<li>You are collaborative by nature and enjoy working with others, including cross-functionally
</li>
<li>You have proven you can manage 30 or more active opportunities, encompassing one or more product solutions, resulting in winning 15 or more opportunities per year</li>
<li>You are capable of leading sales ranging in size from $5k - $100K ARR</li>
<li>You are excited about working in a fast-paced environment, managing multiple priorities and opportunities</li>
<li>You are exceptional at prospecting; proven ability and passion for leading prospecting activities, frequently breaking into new accounts</li>
<li>You are empathetic; you can understand others and formulate potential solutions
</li>
<li>You possess exceptional communication skills; written, oral and with presentations</li>
<li>You have proven success in software/SaaS/PaaS sales</li>
<li>You have a consistent record of outperforming quota in previous sales position(s)</li>
<li>You are comfortable working with C-level individuals</li>
<li>You have 3-5 years of progressive field sales experience</li>
<li>Experience working in the government sector is a plus</li>
<li>Experience in marketing, communications, technology, consultative services is a plus</li>
</ul>
