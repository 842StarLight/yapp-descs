<div>AWS is hiring a Senior Manager to lead a product and analytics team for AWS Financial Experience (AWS Fin-X). The AWS Fin-X team builds and operates the back and front end platforms that enable customers to understand their financial status and pay for services. Our services generate and send financial documents to customers, collect payments, and perform financial accounting and reporting for the AWS business.<br>
<br>
We are looking for an experienced product leader to own the strategy, development and implementation of product features to deliver world a class purchase and payments experience for AWS customers. You will lead a team that is obsessed about providing a great experience for all customers of all types, including individuals, small and medium sized businesses and large enterprises. This is an exciting opportunity in which you will define the long term vision for purchase and payment experience and help build the technical infrastructure to support AWS global expansion.<br>
<br>
You will work with our customers, partners, and teams across all of Amazon to ensure we build a compelling, comprehensive, and simple to use purchase and payment experience.<br>
<br>
As the Head of Product for the AWS Fin-X team, you will lead the strategy for payment acceptance globally. You will identify and negotiate contracts with payment partners in new geographies where AWS is expanding, and launch relevant payment methods. You will lead an analytics team to identify opportunities to improve profitabilty of the global AWS business by reducing cost of payments and bad debt.<br>
<br>
This is a highly visible role. You will be the business owner, working with customers and executive management to guide our business. We are looking for individuals with strong product management experience.<br>
<br>
The successful candidate must have exceptional problem solving skills, strong business judgment, demonstrated experience leading cross-functional teams and deep experience growing and managing product and business intelligence teams.<br>
<br>
The key responsibilities for this position are to:<br>
<ul>
<li>Recruit and grow a team of strong product managers</li>
<li>Lead the definition and delivery of services and tools that help customers pay their AWS bills.</li>
<li>Identify new product opportunities and write their business proposals.</li>
<li>Drive the coordination and clear communication of a multi-product roadmap, working across multiple groups.</li>
<li>Work with engineering and design teams to define fast-paced, but realistic schedules.</li>
<li>Create the pricing, product positioning, and go-to-market plans of new financial products.</li>
<li>Enable the sales team with product training and collateral.</li>
</ul>
