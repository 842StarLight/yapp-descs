About Mars, Incorporated</b></p>
<p>
Mars is a family-owned business with more than a century of history making diverse products and offering services for people and the pets people love. With almost $35 billion in sales, the company is a global business that produces some of the world’s best-loved brands: M&amp;M’s®, SNICKERS®, TWIX®, MILKY WAY®, DOVE®, PEDIGREE®, ROYAL CANIN®, WHISKAS®, EXTRA®, ORBIT®, 5™, SKITTLES®, UNCLE BEN’S®, MARS DRINKS and COCOAVIA®. Mars also provides veterinary health services that include BANFIELD® Pet Hospitals, Blue Pearl®, VCA® and Pet Partners™. Headquartered in McLean, VA, Mars operates in more than 80 countries. The Mars Five Principles – Quality, Responsibility, Mutuality, Efficiency and Freedom – inspire its more than 100,000 Associates to create value for all its partners and deliver growth they are proud of every day.</p>

<p><b>
Job Purpose</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Keep the business running, be the guardian of Operation Excellence across multiple sites, segments and markets, ensure no IT distractions and fast response of local IT resources</li>
<li>
Drive increased collaboration with <b>business partners</b> and <b>segment leaders directly, </b>leading an agenda as the most customer face team within IT</li>
<li>
Delivery all central demands rising from technology triggers to completion</li>
<li>
Lead &amp; delivery all business demands, either related to organic growth, merge&amp;acquisition and divestitures, for all squads: warehouses, factories, retails, hubs, offices, HQs, labs, pet services</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead people (5 or more associates, including remote ones) to deliver all on-site infrastructure services for multiple sites, including end user desk side support, asset lifecycle management &amp; local application hosting</li>
<li>
Ensure all services are delivered within agreed SLAs and XLAs</li>
<li>
Drive efficiency year over year; plus implement a disruptive agenda toward zero distance, digitalization and customer experience</li>
<li>
Accountable for managed services thru non-named contractors</li>
<li>
Accountable for business demands within the region as previously stated in the squads
</li>
<li>Remove bureaucracy, ensure services easy-to-find and easy-to-use</li>
<li>
Plan &amp; manage a budget of approx $4-5m (running, capital and MPAR)</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Context &amp; Scope</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The associate is expected to own all day to day work, driving their own workload &amp; priorities, and building relationships with key stakeholders inside &amp; outside the team
</li>
<li>A strong agenda with business leaders and BP leaders is expected to drive the future and influence strategic decisions</li>
<li>
Main focus relies on associate’s engagement, pipeline, development, as well as driving key initiatives related to ‘zero distance to customers’
</li>
<li>A game change behaviour and mindset, passionate about execution and getting things done from the customer’s eyes</li>
<li>
This role is designed to be onsite, for all units under his/her accountability and frequent face to face meetings, leading an agenda and acting as the IT ambassador on the ground</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Education &amp; Professional Qualification</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in IT is required</li>
<li>
ITIL v3/v4, Sourcing Governance, PMBoK certification are a plus</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Knowledge/Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
10+ years experience in IT service management, IT project management or similar.
</li>
<li>Experience managing IT changes challenging the status ‘quo’</li>
<li>
Experience dealing with customers at different levels (mainly General Managers and CFOs) of the business, leading service reviews and demonstrating results thru storytelling (facts and perception)</li>
<li>
Good understanding of infrastructure technology and how it translates to business results</li>
<li>
5+ yrs of experience managing and engaging people in a highly exposed environment and ability to work under pressure and thinking 2-steps ahead of customer’s demands</li>
</ul>
