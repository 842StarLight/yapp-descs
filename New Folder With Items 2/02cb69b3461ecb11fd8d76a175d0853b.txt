<p><b>
Job Description:</b></p>
<p>
As a Sales Representative, National Accounts in our Home Services vertical you will join a fast growing business and play a key role signing new large and strategic accounts.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Increase sales profits and achieve sales quotas by sourcing, soliciting, and opening new large/strategic accounts, focusing on prospective companies that are leaders within their respective industries</li>
<li>
Meet and exceed quota targets</li>
<li>
Research, follow-up on leads, and track opportunities to continually feed sales pipeline with qualified leads</li>
<li>
Build relationships from the ground up</li>
<li>
Plan, monitor, and adjust activities to ensure success</li>
<li>
Effectively and professionally represent QuinStreet and its products</li>
<li>
Work with account management post sale (in some cases) to ensure a productive long term relationship</li>
<li>
Utilize a consultative sales approach, which requires significant time and effort to educate prospects about QuinStreet’s value proposition</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
General Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors Degree in business related field</li>
<li>
A minimum of 3 years of experience in online or mobile media and/or advertising sales (performance based online media, advertising, etc.) preferred</li>
<li>
Must have a "Hunter" mentality</li>
<li>
Strong phone sales, prospecting, and closing skills</li>
<li>
Consultative sales experience</li>
<li>
Knowledge of online media business models, and common user experiences a strong plus</li>
<li>
Must have excellent communication, time management, and organizational skills</li>
<li>
A team player who is self-motivated and driven to succeed</li>
<li>
Strong focus on generating results</li>
<li>
Confident and energetic; professional and team orientated sales approach; Entrepreneurial mindset a must</li>
<li>
Strategic thinker</li>
<li>
Experience with the internet, and proficiency with software such as MS Word, Excel, Power Point, MS Outlook, and Salesforce.com</li>
</ul>
