<h2>Graphic Services Coordination Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Printpack Graphic Services is seeking a qualified candidate to fill the position of Graphic Services Coordination Manager. This position will be located in Atlanta, Georgia and will report to the Director of Graphic Services.<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
15% travel is involved; including some overnight travel
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>An Associate’s Degree in a related field; a Bachelor’s Degree is preferred
</li>
<li>10+ years of job related experience
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Additional Skills &amp; Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Macintosh OS / Microsoft Office
</li>
<li>Front-end graphic software or equivalent computer skills including:
<ul>
<li>Adobe Illustrator/Photoshop CC
</li>
<li>Pilot/Automation Engine
</li>
<li>Prepress Preflighting skills
</li>
<li>FileMaker Pro
</li>
<li>JDEdwards/ PRISM
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities include </b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead a team of Graphic Coordinators, QC Coordinators, and Interns at Graphic Services.
</li>
<li>Manage direct reports effectively using the HRD tools; to establish an environment for idea sharing and collaboration among direct reports and well as individual associate development.
</li>
<li>Provide subject matter expertise, teamwork, and coaching support to all Graphic Coordination, QC and Intern functions at Graphic Services; while ensuring customer needs are met.
</li>
<li>Drive process improvement initiatives positively impacting quality, productivity, and waste among Graphic Services Coordinators and QC.
</li>
<li>Identify and implement cost improvement opportunities impacting Graphic Coordination and QC functions.
</li>
<li>Manage, administer, and document Graphic Services Coordinator, QC, and Intern training, including reference documentation and work instructions to promote consistency.
</li>
<li>Member of the Graphic Services Leadership Team proactively identifying and resolving department needs for Graphic Services.
</li>
<li>Successful collaboration with other team members within Graphic Services, ensuring overall internal department efficiencies and customer satisfaction (examples may include digital scheduling, database improvements, system integration opportunities, job ticket accuracy, data entry reduction, resolving scheduling conflicts as required with Production Artist Manager, etc.)
</li>
<li>Provide functional area expertise on Project Management initiatives involving Graphics. Serve as Graphics point of contact and represent system functional needs while ensuring deliverables are completed according to project timelines (examples may include Customer billing project, pricing, system integrations with PRISM, etc.)
</li>
<li>Graphic Services point-person with new business initiation. Includes working with Sales, Customers, Operations Planning, Manufacturing, Product Development, CDA’s, etc. to collect and consolidate required information prior to hand-off to Graphics Coordinators. Efforts to include creation and refinement of documentation/ systems used to streamline these efforts.
</li>
<li>Graphic Services point-person for soliciting and documenting feedback on Graphic Services performance with Printpack facilities and Plant Graphics Supervisors/ Coordinators. Feedback, documented through surveys, discussions, etc. used to drive continuous improvement at Graphic Services.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Other Job Responsibilities include </b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee all billing and invoicing processes for Graphic Services. Responsibilities include consistent invoices practices among Graphic Services Coordinators; working with accounting to resolve any billing discrepancies; consistency of cost/pricing execution at Graphic Services.
</li>
<li>Point-person for SQC or claims issued to Graphic Services. Research issue, conduct root cause analysis, and submit response.
</li>
<li>Point-person for Graphic Services tradeshop partner integration and feedback. To include periodic partnership meetings reviewing results against specific metrics/ scorecard.</li>
</ul>
</div>
