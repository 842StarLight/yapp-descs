What do you get to do in this position?
<br>
<br>
<div>Performs assembly, startup, diagnostic, repair and preventive maintenance work as directed by the District Service Manager / Region Director on Company manufactured, as well as third party manufactured equipment.
<br>
<br>
<div>
<b>Essential Functions
</b><br>
<ul><li>Perform pre-site, startup, repair, preventive maintenance and field modification on Company manufactured equipment
</li></ul>as assigned by the Customer Care Center.
<br>
<ul>
<li>Provides site supervision to other FSE’s on assigned projects.
</li>
<li>Complete all administrative responsibilities on all work activities including field service reports, expense reports,
</li>
</ul>preventive maintenance and startup data sheets and daily time card completion.
<br>
<ul>
<li>Provide telephone support for customers and Field Service Associates.
</li>
<li>Work with customers, electricians, sales partners, technical support, DSM and RSD to resolve escalated issues.
</li>
<li>Must operate within all Company published guidelines.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate positive customer relationship building and management skills.
</li>
<li>Satisfactorily complete assigned product and customer relation skills training courses.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
Secondary Functions
<br>
<ul>
<li>Customer familiarization and Operator Training Class presentations.
</li>
<li>On the job training of Field Service Associates.
</li>
<li>Conducts training seminars for Field Service Associates and or approved Service Partners.
</li>
<li>Performs site visits with Service Partners, evaluates technical and professional performance.
</li>
<li>Assists Field Project Manager on larger system startups, will assume some project management responsibilities as required.
</li>
<li>Will act on behalf of the District Service Manager to resolve operational issues as required.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by the District Service Manager and or Regional Service Director<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><b>This job might be for you if:
</b></div>
<div>· Associate degree preferred or similar experience in electronics, electrical theory or similar discipline desirable and or
<br>
military training equivalent.
<br>
<ul><li>2-4 years of field service repair and customer service with UPS, electrical, electro-mechanical or electronics related equipment required. Minimum 2-3 years’ experience as a Field Service Engineer or equivalent industry experience.
</li></ul>Must be proficient at 3 phase multi module systems as required by the local District Service Manager.
<br>
<ul>
<li>Computer literacy with standard business applications.
</li>
<li>Complete competence at utilizing test and diagnostic equipment such as oscilloscope, digital voltmeter, AC clamp
</li>
</ul>meter, load banks and other electronic test devices.
<br>
<ul>
<li>Superior customer relations and interaction skills.
</li>
<li>Fully adept at reading and interpreting schematics, drawings and theory of operation manuals.
</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of principles of AC and DC power, various AC and DC supply circuit components.
</li>
<li>Driving and safe vehicle operation skills.
</li>
<li>Characteristics include self-starter, team player, punctual, personal and professional integrity, trainable, problem
</li>
</ul>solver, organized, professional appearance, flexible, customer oriented and safety oriented.
<br>
<ul>
<li>Capable of reciting and displaying appropriate safety techniques and practices associated with job requirements.
</li>
<li>Ability to write business reports, correspondence, procedures, effectively present information and respond to
</li>
</ul>customer questions.
<br>
<ul>
<li>Handle multiple tasks simultaneously.
</li>
<li>Must be able to stand, walk, and sit for extended periods of time. Must be agile and mobile with respect to finger,
</li>
</ul>hand, arm and leg movement.
<br>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to communicate (speak and hear) effectively to various intellectual levels.
</li>
<li>Must be able to lift and carry heavy objects up to 50 pounds.
</li>
<li>Must be able to see and focus clearly up close as well as short distances
</li>
</ul>
