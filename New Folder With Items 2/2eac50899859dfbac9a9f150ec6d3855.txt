<h2>Loan Support Systems Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div><div>
<div><div>Development, creation, and maintenance of standardized reports from the systems utilized by Loan Support (B2B, PRA, Laser Pro, Collateral Tracking) and the data warehouse. Analyze, review, and interpret data used to develop reports and make recommendations for improvement and follow up to management. Provide end-user training on report writing software and support users in ad-hoc report creation. Reporting includes, but is not limited to the following categories: Loan Support production, credit quality, vintage analysis, sales/ application/ pipeline reporting, and incentive compensation. Provides specialized system support to the Loan Support Division. Acts as business line System Administrator, overseeing testing, implementation, and adherence to compliance for systems used by the business group (Baker Hill’s B2B system &amp; Portfolio Risk Advisor, Harland Laser Pro, Collateral Tracking). Works closely with end-users to effectively leverage system capabilities. Coordinates with respective internal and external parties during implementation and upgrades to Loan Support systems, ensuring proper testing/ validation is completed.<br>
</div></div>
