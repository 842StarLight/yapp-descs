<h2>Inside Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION OVERVIEW
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>OnBrand24 </b>is a tech-enabled business process outsourcing provider that provides technology and call center support across the customer life-cycle. We are looking for an <b>Inside Sales Manager</b> in our<b> Beverly, MA </b>office to oversee a variety of business to business (B2B) and business to consumer (B2C) clients across a wide range of industries. The ideal candidate will offer a proven track record of sales excellence and the traits of a strong coach. They will take pride in critical thinking utilizing technology and analytics to exceed sales targets and deliver an exceptional customer experience. Your passion should consistently push everyone in the sales organization to do things bigger, better, and faster. In this roll you will be in a fast paced sales environment, interface with clients and senior leadership, and will be involved in all components of the business operation including recruitment, project management, client services, strategy, and continuous improvement. As a high growth organization with an entrepreneurial culture we are committed to a high octane, fun and collaborative work environment. As a member of the OnBrand24 team you will have a high degree of responsibility and autonomy giving you an opportunity to make a difference is the organizations shape, direction and growth.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SCHEDULE
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This is a full-time, Monday-Friday management position with normal business hours. Regular travel is not required however it's possible that some travel may be required from time to time to meet client and business requirements.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Inside Sales Managers are responsible for the day-to-day activity and development of outbound sales projects within a complex call center environment. The Inside Sales Manager is responsible for ensuring sales results and a quality customer experience. With direct reports across multiple clients and business process, the Inside Sales Manager will direct continuous improvement efforts, hold business meetings, drive strategy, and ensure client satisfaction while ensuring the overall adherence to corporate polices and procedure.</div>
<ul>
<li>
<br>
</li>
<li>
Manage metrics, performance criteria, policies and procedures to continuously improve call center productivity</li>
<li>
Directs sales forecasting activities and sets performance goals and objectives accordingly</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain strategy on ensuring customer satisfaction on all sales transactions</li>
<li>
Provide team motivation and development to maximize sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Responsible for the overall performance and productivity of direct reports</li>
<li>
Responsible for weekly payroll review and submission to ensure correct entries</li>
<li>
Responsible for driving the growth of revenue and profit originating from a call center</li>
<li>
Proven ability to meet performance targets, operating efficiencies, processing improvements, people development, and quality assurance</li>
<li>
Monitoring of individual, team and call center results to identify and act on both positive and negative performance</li>
<li>
Determining work procedures, preparing work schedules, and expediting work-flow</li>
<li>
Responsible for hiring, coaching and terminating call center employees</li>
<li>
Assists senior management in the development and mentoring of Supervisor team members</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>CANDIDATE QUALIFICATIONS
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The ideal candidate would share and understand the high growth objectives of OnBrand24. This position requires an advanced degree of leadership, creative thinking, and dedication to people. The ability to professionally represent OnBrand24 internally and client facing is a must. The right candidate will exhibit good business judgment and acumen and be both confident and flexible in their views. This position will require the ability to work with multiple business units to acquire operational knowledge and execute on departmental initiatives.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 5 years of sales or contact center experience</li>
<li>
Associate’s degree and/or relevant work experience</li>
<li>
Exceptional interpersonal &amp; communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong supervisory experience including staff development</li>

<li>
Demonstrated ability to drive sales through the actions of others</li>

Possess effective conflict resolution skills (both customer and agent conflict)</li>
<li>
Proven leader with advanced time management, planning, organizational and multi-tasking skills</li>
