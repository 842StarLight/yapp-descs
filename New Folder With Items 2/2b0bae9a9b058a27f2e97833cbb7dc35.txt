<h2>Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role:</b></h2>
<p><b>The accountant will be responsible for supporting our clients in the areas of accounts receivables and accounts payables</b></p>
<p><b>Accounts Receivables: Customer invoicing, recording of deposits, and supporting customer collections</b>
</p>
<p><b>Accounts Payables: Timely and accurate processing of client Accounts Payable transactions and ensuring proper documentation and maintenance</b><br>
</p>
<b>Key Success Factors:</b>
<ul><li><ul>
<li>
<b>Excellent communication skills via telephone, video conferencing, </b><b>email, etc. as interactions with client customers will be frequent
</b>
</li>
<li><b>Ability to learn new technology software skills quickly</b></li>
<li><b>Attention to details and commitment to accuracy</b></li>
<li><b>Co-operative team player</b></li>
<li><b>Extremely organized</b></li>
<li><b>Client-centric approach to all matters</b></li>
<li><b>Positive feedback from clients</b></li>
</ul></li></ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<p><b>Accounts Receivables:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><ul><li><ul>
<li><b>Processing of client invoices to customers</b></li>
<li><b>Posting customer payments</b></li>
<li><b>Downloading/ entry of revenues from 3rd party applications or banks</b></li>
<li><b>Customer sales tax exemption certificate administration</b></li>
<li><b>Preparation of sales tax reports</b></li>
</ul></li></ul></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Accounts Payables:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><ul><li><ul>
<li><b>Processing of vendor bills received for payment</b></li>
<li><b>Downloading and coding credit card transactions</b></li>
<li><b>Review monthly A/P ledgers for missing routine bills</b></li>
<li><b>vendor set-up</b></li>
<li><b>W-9 administration</b></li>
<li><b>Monthly 1099 vendor report</b></li>
</ul></li></ul></li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Performance Indicators:</b></h3>
<ul> <li><ul><li>
<ul>
<li><b>Efficient entry of transactions with average or above-average data entry time realizations and rates of accuracy
</b></li>
<li><b>Quality of work output (on-time and accurate)</b></li>
<li><b>Integration into the team</b></li>
<li><b>Client satisfaction</b></li>
</ul>
</li></ul></li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications:</b></h3>
<ul><li><ul><li><ul>
<li>
<b>Associates degree in accounting. College interns in their junior or</b><b> senior year with a major in accounting will be considered
</b>
</li>
<li><b>Prior bookkeeping experience required, specifically within a high volume environment</b></li>
<li><b>Strong technology skills</b></li>
<li><b>Experience working in a paperless environment strongly preferred</b></li>
<li><b>Strong interpersonal and relationship building skills</b></li>
<li><b>Team player with a positive “can-do” approach</b></li>
<li><b>Ability to multi-task and work independently with minimal supervision</b></li>
</ul></li></ul></li></ul>
