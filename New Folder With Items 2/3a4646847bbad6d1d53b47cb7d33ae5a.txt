<h2>Content Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The role:</b></p>
<div>
The Content Marketing Specialist will participate in content development (with a primary focus on <b>copywriting</b>) to drive demand, create brand awareness, and promote our solutions.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Reporting to the Marketing Director, this role will be instrumental in producing effective content that resonates with the needs of our audience and further defines our brand voice. Responsibilities include writing and editing inbound marketing content such as landing pages, blogs, press releases, emails, whitepapers, case studies, contributed editorial, advertising, social, and collateral.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>As the newest member of a fast-paced, highly collaborative marketing team, the Content Marketing Specialist will have the opportunity to work in many mediums, including print, digital, graphic, web, video, audio, and more. Success in this role depends upon a proactive and creative approach, paired with a fanatical dedication to driving marketing and sales results through content.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>You will:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Write captivating content for a variety of outlets, including our website, marketing and sales emails, landing pages, digital/print ads, editorial, and press releases.</li>
<li>
Participate in development of 1-2 free resources each month to drive leads, subscribers and awareness (examples include eBooks, case studies, whitepapers, infographics, guides, webinars, videos, newsletters, etc.).</li>
<li>
Supply the company blog on an ongoing basis to educate and inform existing contacts and to attract new site visitors through search, social, and our email subscribers.
</li>
<li>Work on cross-functional teams of designers, marketing operations, sales, and external influencers and industry experts to produce relevant, targeted content that meets the needs of both key stakeholders and our audience.</li>
<li>
Administer submission and review of contributed blog and other content, including proofreading, editing, fact-checking, and optimization.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties and projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
You have:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years working in marketing and communications.</li>
<li>
BA in English, writing, journalism, marketing, communications, or equivalent experience required.</li>
<li>
Strong written, verbal, interpersonal, and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Creative, highly organized, detail-oriented, results-motivated and agile.</li>
<li>
Experience with a variety of content mediums, including several of the following and willingness to learn more: written, graphic, video, podcast, webinar, web/email, social, photography, etc.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications, design applications (e.g., Adobe Creative Suite), video editing (e.g., After Effects), content management systems (e.g., Wordpress).
</li>
<li>Strong understanding of SEO, SEM and ability to apply best practices in content.</li>
<li>
Experience in B2B enterprise software and/or with eCommerce solutions and applications a plus.</li>
</ul>
