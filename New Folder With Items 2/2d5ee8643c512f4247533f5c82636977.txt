<b>Position Overview</b><br>
An Account Development Manager (ADM) is often the first human touch point prospects have with Big Squid. The ADM role is responsible to respond to inbound leads from marketing efforts and source leads through outbound calling and email prospecting efforts. This is a commission-based role that may require up to 20% travel throughout the year. Account Development Managers are assigned a territory and are expected to cultivate and qualify leads for the Big Squid sales team to pursue. This is a full-time position that includes benefits. We are seeking applicants in the greater Atlanta, Boston, New York and DC areas.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify companies and people that align with our ideal customer profile within your territory and contact them through phone, email, and social contacting efforts.</li>
<li>Qualify, usually over the phone, inbound and outbound leads to then pass to Account Executive to develop and close</li>
<li>Follow Big Squids sales methodology and processes</li>
<li>Leverage Salesforce and Salesloft technologies to track opportunity progress, sales activities such as emails, phone calls, meetings, tasks, events, next steps, action items, etc.</li>
<li>Work in tandem with Account Executive to develop a territory plan in order to fill and maintain sufficient pipeline</li>
<li>Travel onsite to trade show venues to represent Big Squid</li>
<li>Adhere to all company policies to ensure secure data system and safe working environment</li>
<li>Additional duties, as determined by management, to better accomplish objectives of the role</li>
</ul>
