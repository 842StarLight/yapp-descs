<p><b>Job Description &amp; Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Responsible for providing reporting, ad-hoc data and analytical support across operational areas of the business through translation of complex analytics into concise and consumable reports.</p>
<p><b>Major Tasks, Responsibilities and Key Accountabilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identifies, locates and extracts data from a variety of sources for use in reporting, analysis, and statistical modeling.</li>
<li>Tests, analyzes and problem solves data issues to ensure data integrity and provide technical support for end users self-service BI tool.</li>
<li>Participates in analytical investigations aimed at answering specific business problems using data with an emphasis on recommending actionable solutions based on insights.</li>
<li>Builds strong relationships with business partners and leaders to provide thought partnership on metrics and visualizations.</li>
<li>Collaborates and provides support to multiple departments to ensure the business intelligence, analytic, and reporting needs are met with technology, architecture, processes and tools.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains automated reports for daily consumption using specific formatting and presentation guidelines.</li>
<li>Documents data definition and report sources and for all reports.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Nature and Scope</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates skill in data analysis techniques by resolving missing/incomplete information and inconsistencies/anomalies in more complex research/data.</li>
<li>Nature of work requires increasing independence; receives guidance only on unusual, complex problems or issues. Work review typically involves periodic review of output by a supervisor and/or direct customers of the process.</li>
<li>May provide general guidance/direction to or train junior level support or professional personnel.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Located in a comfortable indoor area. Any unpleasant conditions would be infrequent and not objectionable.</li>
<li>Most of the time is spent sitting in a comfortable position and there is frequent opportunity to move about. On rare occasions there may be a need to move or lift light articles.</li>
<li>Typically requires overnight travel less than 10% of the time.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Typically requires BS/BA in a related discipline. Generally 2-5 years of experience in a related field OR MS/MA and generally 2-4 years of experience in a related field. Certification is required in some areas.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications &amp; Job Specific Details</b></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years’ experience in database marketing analysis including analytics, response analysis, ROI analysis, customer profiling or equivalent.</li>
<li>SAP BI or equivalent BI tool expertise required</li>
<li>Experience with visualization tools such as Tableau preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with database querying tools, languages and analytical platforms supporting database marketing, including SQL, required.</li>
</ul>
