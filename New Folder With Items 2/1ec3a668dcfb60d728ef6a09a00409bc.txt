<h2>Associate Account Manager - Digital Markets</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>What makes Gartner a GREAT fit for you? When you join Gartner, you’ll be part of a fast-growing team that helps the world become smarter and more connected. We’re the world’s leading research and advisory company, achieving consistent double-digit growth by steering clients toward the right decisions with business and technology insights they can’t find anywhere else. Our associates enjoy a collaborative work environment, exceptional training and career development — as well as unlimited growth opportunities. If you like working with a curious, supportive, high-performing team, Gartner is the place for you.<p></p>
<p>
The Associate Account Manager role is a combination of customer relations, sales, and support. If you enjoy helping, educating and persuading people to improve, look no further.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You Will do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You will spend your day talking to software marketers to help them get the most out of their digital market’s campaigns.</li>
<li>
Your challenge will be to identify the right ways - using both phone and email - to persuade and teach your vendors.</li>
<li>
Your primary responsibility will be to help your customers (software companies) meet and exceed their goals for driving sales from their Pay Per Click program. If you aren't already, you'll become extremely well versed in online marketing and lead generation best practices. As you reach out to software companies, you'll have the opportunity to help them improve their overall online marketing and lead generation efforts, as well as to learn from them.</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively with teammates who share management responsibilities with for all tier three vendors (GetApp, Capterra and Software Advice).</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You Will Need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree preferred</li>
<li>
Upbeat, positive and enthusiastic attitude</li>
<li>
Self-motivated / Strong work ethic</li>
<li>
Interest in marketing and technology</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and writing skills</li>
<li>
Proficient computer skills</li>
<li>
Advanced organization skills</li>
</ul>
