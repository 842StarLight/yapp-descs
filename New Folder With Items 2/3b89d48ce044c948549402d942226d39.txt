<b>Exciting work you will do:</b><br>
Use knowledge of Program and Project Management to increase client impact, develop your skill sets, and grow your career.
<br>Make your mark by working directly with clients as a visible and engaged member of the team.
<br>Collaborate across disciplines to deliver creative solutions to client challenges.
<br>Play a key role on your team by developing and delivering presentations and written deliverables, and organizing and facilitating client and internal meetings and workshops.
<br>Actively contribute to business development proposals and the identification of new opportunities.
<br>Establish positive relationships with clients and peers that build credibility, foster your support network, and empower career development.
<br>Develop a deeper understanding of our firm’s shared vision to build our clients’ capabilities and unleash their potential.
<br>Cultivate knowledge of our clients’ business models and increase your role in engagements.
<br>Responsible and accountable for delivering large projects on-time, on budget, and meeting functional and quality specifications.
<br>Forecast, manage, and report on project financials, planned vs actual project schedule, and resource utilization.
<br>Identify resources needed to complete work and collaborate with resource manager(s) to assemble appropriate skills/teams.
<br>Manage issues, risks, and changes using appropriate and agreed upon processes and tools established within the PMO.
<br>Provide weekly status reports and can communicate to executive leadership.
<br><br>
<b>What you will need:</b><br>
7+ years of experience in a Project Management role with a strong background in team development.
<br>Proven leadership experience with the ability to mentor/coach team in senior or lead capacity.
<br>Proven success delivering projects in Agile/Scrum environment.
<br>Experience with multiple project streams and leading teams of 5+.
<br>Large enterprise project experience ranging from $1million+
<br>Expert knowledge in Agile/Scrum methodology.
<br>Demonstrated proficiency in MS Office Suites of products, (i.e. Visio, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, etc.)
<br>Project Management training and/or project lead experience of 3+years
<br>Proven ability to effectively manage multiple tasks at various stages of completion.
<br>Strong facilitation skills; communicates effectively with the business to identify needs and evaluate alternative solutions as required.
<br>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
<br>Understanding of various technologies including applications and databases.
<br>Demonstrated proficiency in use of project planning tools (MS Project, Jira, Version One, etc.)
<br>Familiarity with Clarity, Ehour, or other Portfolio / Program Management Software
<br>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, or a related discipline or equivalent
<br>Project Management Certification (CAPM or PMP)
