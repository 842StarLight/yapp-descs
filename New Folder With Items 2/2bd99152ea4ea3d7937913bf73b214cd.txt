<div><b>
Assistant General Counsel: Global Privacy and Security</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>Position Overview:</div>
<ul><li>This position will be responsible for supporting and managing global legal privacy and security (“privacy”) strategies for Nike, Inc., including its affiliates.</li></ul>
<div>Strategy:</div>
<ul>
<li>Help implement Nike, Inc.’s global legal privacy strategy, with executive direction as necessary.</li>
<li>
Help set and drive consistent privacy policy and practices across Nike, Inc.</li>
<li>
Formulate and disseminate enterprise-wide policies for US and Global operations, in partnership and consultation with country/GEO counsel responsible for data protection.</li>
<li>
Help ensure the company has fully documented policies and practices in place, consistent with local laws.</li>
<li>
Identify issues of highest risk facing the company; propose and drive solutions to address those risks.</li>
</ul>
<div>Cross-functional leadership and engagement:</div>
<ul><li>Provide leadership and engagement with cross-functional teammates including, but not limited to, HR, global sales, information technology, information security, government affairs, communications, and audit.</li></ul>
<div>Embed our Approach:</div>
<ul>
<li>Effectively champion a principles-based privacy approach throughout the company.</li>
<li>
Drive programs and processes and decisions in support of strategy and principles.</li>
<li>
Surface and resolve issues that have the potential for brand impact for business and legal consideration.</li>
<li>
Conduct privacy impact assessments for large IT and enterprise services, programs and systems.</li>
<li>
Continually test and improve privacy processes and governance.
</li>
</ul>
<div>Expertise:</div>
<ul>
<li>Maintain knowledge of external business and legal landscape in privacy and proactively propose advisable business processes or practices.</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledge of the relevant information technology systems and applications to drive systematic solutions and improvements.</li>
<li>
Provide subject matter expertise regarding privacy and security provisions within commercial deals.
</li>
<li>Grounding in global laws and government activity related to privacy.</li>
<li>
Partner with government affairs on government policy/regulation affecting the company.
</li>
</ul>
<div>Education and Awareness:</div>
<ul>
<li>Create content and guide delivery of company-wide and roles-based education and awareness of privacy issues and their impact to our brand and business.</li>
<li>
Train agencies, vendors and other contractors in privacy matters.</li>
</ul>
<div>External Liaison:</div>
<ul><li>Liaise with privacy professionals at peer companies and industries.</li></ul>
<div>Monitoring:</div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Responsible for developing and augmenting internal efforts to create integrated privacy governance by monitoring the relevant legal, regulatory and policy developments, collaborating with external stakeholders.</li></ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div>
WHAT YOU BRING TO NIKE</div>
<ul>
<li>JD degree
</li>
<li>Member, in good standing, of at least one U.S. State Bar and willingness to commit to membership in the Oregon State Bar
</li>
<li>Minimum of 5-7 years privacy experience. Law firm and/or multinational corporation experience preferred.</li>
<li>
In-depth knowledge of global privacy and information security laws; ability to act as a “subject matter expert” in privacy</li>
<li>
Experience with cross-border data transfers and methods of ensuring compliance with data protection laws.</li>
<li>
Experience with privacy and security provisions in commercial agreements.
</li>
<li>IAPP Certification Preferred</li>
<li>
Ability to successfully draft and implement privacy policies and processes</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate privacy effectively at all levels</li>
<li>
Strong team player and ability to work cross-functionally in a complex organizational structure
</li>
<li>Sound risk assessment capabilities in rapidly evolving regulated areas
</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and work through ambiguity, focus on business requirements and drive results</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently within a multinational organization and be pragmatic and results focused</li>
<li>
Can take a dynamic approach to implementing a privacy program</li>
</ul>
