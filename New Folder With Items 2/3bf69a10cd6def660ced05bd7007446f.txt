<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Row House, a life changing boutique fitness experience centered around rowing, is coming to Chesterfield, MO. We are looking for an amazing, motivated talent to join our team as a General Manager to lead our studio to success!
</p>
<p>Row House is seeking an experienced General Manager to oversee membership sales and retention, operations and community outreach for our brand-new studio coming to Chesterfield, MO. This is an exciting opportunity to get in on the ground floor of the business leading our team and help us share the Row House brand and experience to all people, everywhere. Row House is one of 8 unique fitness brands under the Xponential Fitness umbrella: https://www.xponential.com. You may not have heard of us yet, but you probably know some of our sister brands like Club Pilates, CycleBar, Yoga 6 and Pure Barre. Row House is a boutique rowing concept that strives to give people of all fitness levels an efficient, high-cardio, full-body workout in less than one hour. It distinguishes itself from other group fitness concepts by creating an environment that raises everyone up so they come together, rowing on the same rhythm, with the same flow and energy. Rowing has long been recognized by serious athletes as one of the most effective forms of full-body exercise and Row House's workout stays true to form. Targeting the upper body, lower body, and core all at once, the brand's fitness program offers an energetic, endorphin-boosting workout in a safe, inspiring, and inclusive environment.</p>
