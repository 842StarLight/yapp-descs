<h2>Salesforce Administrator, Jr. Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About the Role
</b></p>
<p>The goTenna Business Operations team is seeking a Salesforce specialist to support and implement growth initiatives across the company. The successful candidate will love optimizing workflows and building new ones by translating business requirements into technical/system requirements. In a cross functional capacity, the role will work daily with sales, marketing, customer experience, operational fulfillment and finance teams.
</p>
<p><b>Key Activities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Performing Salesforce administrative functions, such as creating and configuring users and permission/profile sets as well as defining user roles, profiles, groups and security settings.</li>
<li>
Maintaining fields and mapping for both existing and custom objects.</li>
<li>
Debugging technical issues, by delivering timely fixes but also working on scalable longer-term solutions.</li>
<li>
Uphold defined business processes and implement new standards that continually enforce a high level of data integrity.</li>
<li>
Identify quick wins and implement best practices.</li>
<li>
Use of the Salesforce API to build read and write connections to third party applications.</li>
<li>
Install, configure, and administer Salesforce application extensions or third party tools/applications that integrate with Salesforce.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 1 year of experience in a Salesforce Administration role</li>
<li>
Current Salesforce Administrator Certification (or demonstrated experience equivalent)</li>
<li>
A solid understanding of Salesforce functionality and an interest in staying up to date with new features and developments</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication skills
</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency with Salesforce objects, fields, page layouts, workflows, process builder, validation rules, permissions, roles, reports, dashboards, and logical formulas</li>
<li>
Experience automating business processes using the process builder, flows, triggers and validation rules, enforcing data integrity.</li>
<li>
Experience using a sandbox environment to build and test new features.</li>
<li>
Developing custom pages and functionality using Visualforce or Lightning web components</li>
<li>
Curious about the development of Salesforce and how it can be impactful across the company to different teams.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Attributes</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Curious about the development of Salesforce and how it can be impactful across the company to different teams.</li>
<li>
Highly conscious about how Salesforce data is created and how it relates to future business intelligence.</li>
<li>
Genuine problem solver that puts themselves in the position of the user and optimizes overall workflow between teams.</li>
<li>
Asks relevant questions to learn and strengthen understanding.</li>
<li>
Adaptable to a changing working environment and can develop solutions for both the short and long term.</li>
<li>
Experience working with different Salesforce user types and coaching users of different experience levels</li>
</ul>
<p><b>It would be awesome if you had</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Salesforce Advanced Certification</li>
<li>
Experience with a company that delivers both hardware and software.</li>
<li>
Basic web development experience (HTML/CSS)</li>
<li>
Familiarity executing SOQL queries
</li>
<li>Experience writing Apex code and test classes</li>
<li>
Experience working with large data sets.</li>
</ul>
