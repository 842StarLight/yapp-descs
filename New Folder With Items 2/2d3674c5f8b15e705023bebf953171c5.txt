<div>
This position is responsible for managing creative and branding projects and activities to serve the needs of various business units across the enterprise. Responsibilities include overseeing the timelines and creative resource allocation needed to develop and implement materials and campaigns that meet strategic objectives.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>How you will make an impact:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Oversees the end-to-end workflow of the creative and brand team (content, design and web development) to ensure deadlines are met and the appropriate creative resources are allocated correctly.
</li>
<li>Develops detailed work breakdown schedules, project estimates, and resource plans that support scope and risk management. Conducts and facilitates effective project meetings, including issue tracking, status reports, and executive communications. Implements change, quality and performance management processes to control scope and monitor project progress. Ensures problems are effectively escalated and crisis management activated, as needed.</li>
<li>
Works with creative leadership to prioritize and plan quarterly project and production workloads.
</li>
<li>Connects the dots between all campaign assets to ensure brand standards and consistency across mediums.</li>
<li>
Owns the creative intake process for all production requests by working closely with cross-functional partners in Marketing, Sales, Operations, Legal, Compliance and IT.</li>
<li>
Partners with creative strategy team and leadership to produce deliverables for broader campaigns.
</li>
<li>Develops project specific tracking information and analyzes results.</li>
<li>
Ensures that materials are kept up-to-date reflecting the latest company policy and procedures.
</li>
<li>Works with outside vendors or agencies to produce content when needed.
</li>
<li>Collects collateral for annual DDPA audit and manages brand submissions with them throughout the year.
</li>
<li>Performs miscellaneous duties as assigned.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What we look for:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>A Bachelor’s degree preferred. Equivalent experience may be considered in lieu of degree.</li>
<li>
A minimum of 4 years related experience at an agency or an in-house creative/branding team.</li>
<li>
Detail oriented with problem-solving abilities.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with the creative process and roles.</li>
<li>
Working history with creative workflow tools.
</li>
<li>Strong organization, time and project management skills and multi-tasking abilities.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
