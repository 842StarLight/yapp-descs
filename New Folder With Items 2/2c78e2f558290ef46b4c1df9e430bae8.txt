This position
<br>As a smart and scrappy problem solver, you will work in topics that range from high level Sales Strategy that are transformational to our business to the nuts and bolts of Sales Operations that make our GTM operation efficient. You will do this through a combination of analytical insight, strong business acumen, ability to partner and influence both sales leaders as well as leaders in the cross functional roles that support sales like marketing, finance, HR, etc.
<br><br>
This particular role is focused on the Sales Development and Solution Engineering organizations, two key areas of our Go To Market Model. This position is an individual contributor and reports to the VP of of Sales Operations.
<br><br>
Why consider this role? You enjoy being a driver of change in a business and seeing fast results &amp; learn from it. You work hard yet efficiently, and you find time to have fun with colleagues. You have aspirations to go places and you are curious to see the road from $1B in revenue to $5B in five years! You have a life outside work and you want to work at a place that allows you to keep it.
<br><br>
This position reports to the Vice President of Sales Strategy and Operations
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Partner with sales leadership to help drive the business</li>
<li>Identify key opportunities for business improvement through combination of analytics, qualitative insights and good business sense</li>
<li>Conceptualize and run with projects that help improve our GTM</li>
<li>Run sales operations associated with SD and SE teams (e.g. forecasting, QBRs, etc)</li>
<li>Assist with ad hoc asks</li>
</ul>

<li>Ability to work in cross-functional teams</li>

<li>Comfortable working in flexible and fast-paced environment, comfortable with ambiguity</li>
<li>Results-driven, self-motivated and able to work independently</li>
<li>Intellectual curiosity</li>
</ul>
