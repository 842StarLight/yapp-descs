<p><b>Meet Timberland </b>.

<p>Founded in 1973, Timberland® is a global outdoor lifestyle brand based in Stratham, New Hampshire, with international headquarters in Switzerland and Hong Kong. Best known for its original yellow boot designed for the harsh elements of New England, Timberland today offers a full range of footwear, apparel and accessories for people who value purposeful style and share the brand’s passion for enjoying - and protecting - nature. At the heart of the Timberland brand is the core belief that a greener future is a better future. This comes to life through a decades-long commitment to make products responsibly, protect the outdoors, and strengthen communities around the world. Timberland is a VF Corporation brand. VF is one of the world’s leading apparel, footwear and accessories companies with socially and environmentally responsible operations, and a commitment to deliver innovative products and create long-term value for its employees, customers and shareholders.

<b>Let’s talk about the role </b>!
</p>
<p>The primary responsibility of the Global Sr Mgr. Merchandise Planning is to lead in building the global multi-year and seasonal Merch Financial Planning globally and across all Regions in collaboration with the Global Merchandising Category Leads<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>How You’ll Make A Difference…
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze the business on an on-going basis to understand core drivers, identify opportunities and risks
</li>
<li>Conduct global analytics and financial hind sighting; synthesize regional hind sighting for a Merchandise category
</li>
<li>Partner with Global Merchandising Category Leads on long-term and seasonal merch strategy with focus on financial KPIs
</li>
<li>Develop analytical framework used to inform financially and strategically relevant assortment strategies
</li>
<li>Lead SKU count analysis and conduct SKU rationalization exercises using ABC analysis or other quantitative analysis
</li>
<li>Prepare global SKU insights and opportunities for Global Merchandising based on historical performance and plan season financial objectives
</li>
<li>Partner with Regional Finance and Global Merchandising Category Leads to define global Merchandise Financial targets (sales, margin), globally and per Region, and drive alignment thoughout GTM to achieve set targets
</li>
<li>Liaise with Global Merchandising Category Leads to set and measure regional SKU and productivity
</li>
<li>Partner with Regional Merch planning to roll up seasonal MFPs by channel and category to align with financial and brand strategies
</li>
<li>Support Global Merchandising in defining category and global line plans and facilitate discussions to ensure plans align with financial strategies
</li>
<li>Oversight and reconcile regional forecast during development milestones to understand line adoption and track targets against MFP; highlight gaps and opportunities
</li>
<li>Responsible for the initial global demand plan that connects sales and product strategies to Operations/Supply Chain
</li>
<li>Own handoffs of seasonal merchandising financial plan to the operations team
</li>
<li>Partner with Supply Planning, ensuring forecasts are connected to strategic focuses
</li>
<li>Partner with Merchandising Operations to develop planning calendar to align Merchandising, Finance, Sales, RD&amp;D, and operations milestones/deliverables
</li>
</ul>
