<h2>Senior Product Marketing Manager, Windows</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Microsoft has a company mission to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. The company’s single largest business and cloud service to help people achieve more is the Windows operating system used by more than 1 billion users in every country in the world. We are looking for a senior product marketing manager who is interested to help us look deeply at our global customer base, define product and service ideas, develop the product ideas with customers in a design thinking process, and create a value proposition in partnership with our product engineering team for today’s users as well as over 200 million new Windows PCs each year.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
You’ll join as a <b>Sr Product Marketing</b> leader for core experiences on the Windows and Mobility Experiences product marketing management team who works on product features and services across the consumer and commercial segments. We are a product marketing management team that asks individual contributors to work across multiple products, services, and functions to deliver customer value for work and life. This is a great opportunity for product managers and marketers who want to shape one of the world’s most recognized brands and products that people trust as their ‘way of doing things’ every day. You wear the hats of a product manager and a product marketer in this role to create new products and features that delight our customers with our product makers as well as write the first draft of our product positioning and messaging for our go to market teams.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Define a global customer research program to define customer needs and opportunities with product engineering and designers</li>
<li>
Lead competitive research for products and services in category</li>
<li>
Develop the problem statements and solution story in partnership with product and design leaders</li>
<li>
Create customer design thinking and go to market experiments to test and validate product choices</li>
<li>
Define product positioning and messaging based on consumer needs, behaviors, preferences, and feedback</li>
<li>
Manage and track the customer journey, service usage, and satisfaction end-to-end (e.g. funnel)</li>
<li>
Build marketing and sales capability for new products and features through others to scale through our global organization and channels</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years technology product management, program management, management consulting, or similar positions</li>
<li>
Product development and launch experience in the technology industry</li>
<li>
Personal computer industry knowledge (core technology and value chain, hardware and software development, market makers, customer journey)</li>
<li>
Distinctive storytelling skills to influence product and marketing investment decisions with references</li>
<li>
Track record of thought leadership, problem solving, and program management to tackle tough problems in the face of adversity</li>
<li>
Strong desire to learn and grow as a leader with continuous feedback</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We’re looking for product management and marketing talent from diverse backgrounds ready to work on our current and next generation products. We hire great people who want to learn and grow the business today and lead the company tomorrow. We champion an open and trusting culture with a growth mindset and relentless focus on value creation for our customers. We look for product marketing managers to stand out as distinctive problem solvers, learn new information rapidly, and roll up their sleeves to help others in need as a team player. We never stop learning and looking for new ideas as a team in an industry that never stops moving forward.</div>
