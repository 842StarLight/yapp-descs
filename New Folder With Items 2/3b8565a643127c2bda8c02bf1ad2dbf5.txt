<h2>Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Payroll Network, a major Human Capital Management (HCM) Solution provider in the DC/Baltimore metro areas, seeks a <b>Sales Executive.</b></p>
<p>
Our 70 dedicated associates implement and support iSolved, a cloud-based HCM solution which just surpassed two million client employees. The impressive adoption of the iSolved platform reflects the value of having a single solution for all core components of workforce management: Payroll, HR, Benefits enrollment, Onboarding and Time Tracking. As a Founding Partner, Payroll Network implements and supports iSolved so clients can manage their employee life-cycle, from hire-to-retire!</p>
<p>
The Sales Executive is an outside "hunter" of new business-to-business sales. Our clients are employers with between 10 2,500 employees from a broad range of private sector industries. The Sales Executive approaches prospective clients, conducts requirements assessments, schedules and conducts all appointments with human resources, payroll and executive-level decision makers, conducts live demonstrations of our innovative SaaS-based human capital management (HCM) software, iSolved, prepares pricing proposals, and closes business to meet sales goals.</p>
<p>
A sales-focused organization, Payroll Network equips our Sales Executives with the best tools possible for unlimited opportunities for success, such as extensive web-based and classroom product training, technical sales support and a dream team of marketing and business development professionals with state-of-the art marketing automation and networking opportunities. Compensation includes base plus uncapped commission and excellent benefits, including automobile and cell phone allowances.</p>

Does this sound like you?</b></p>

Must be a citizen or legal resident of the United States or hold a visa that allows you to work in the United States without being sponsored by an employer.</p>
