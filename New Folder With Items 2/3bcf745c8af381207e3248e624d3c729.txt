<p>Commercial Flooring Project Manager</p>
<p>Whitt Carpet &amp; Tile Service, Inc. is a family-owned and operated flooring contractor that has been is business since 1959. We are currently looking for a Commercial Project Manager to add to our team. The qualified candidate will be an organized, self-motivated and detail oriented individual with a developed sense for the needs of individual projects. The candidate should have a basic understanding of both flooring and general construction practices.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<p>-Manage start-up through completion of flooring installation on commercial flooring projects ranging from approximately $5,000 - $1,000,000.</p>
<p>-Work with General Contractors, End Users, Architects, commercial clients and sales persons to assure successful completion of each project.</p>
<p>-Manage subcontractors and installers.</p>
<p>-Monitor flooring projects from start through completion for proper installation methods, layout, etc.</p>
<p>-Execute contracts, complete materials buyout, estimate/ process change orders, submit RFI’s &amp; submittal packages, schedule installations, track budget, conduct regular site visits, submit pay applications/ AIA form.</p>
<p>-Assure work is being performed in a safe manner and in accordance with Whitt Carpet’s safety program and OSHA standards; Conduct safety meetings with installers.</p>
<p>-Review estimates and work with estimator through project turnover process.</p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<p>-Minimum 2 years’ experience commercial construction management (flooring experience preferred).</p>
<p>-Education: Construction Management/ Construction Engineering degree or related field (equivalent in field experience considered)</p>
<p>-Ability to foresee potential problems before they start and find solutions with little help.</p>
<p>-Ability to work independently or with minimal supervision in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<p>-Ability to read and understand blue prints and construction specifications</p>
<p>-Knowledge of flooring and tile preferred (carpet, hardwood, resilient and ceramic tile)</p>
<p>-Strong communication skills</p>
<p>-Ability to perform detailed and accurate material take-offs</p>
<p>-Computer Skills: Microsoft Office Suite, understanding of on-screen take-off software</p>
<p>-Valid drivers license/ clean driving record</p>
