<h2>Senior Solutions Architect - Network (PA/OH)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Senior Solutions Architect is a pre-sales engineering and consulting position in Presidio's Western PA territory supporting the greater Pittsburgh territory. (This person will ideally be located in the Pittsburgh or Eastern Ohio area). We are looking for a thought leader who is able to assess and architect successful network infrastructure solutions. The SA will work with clients to architect and design solutions based on products and solutions which Presidio provides. Technologies include Digital Infrastructure, as well as other related network infrastructure products and technologies. Candidates should have 5+ years of experience in the design and/or implementation of network infrastructure. Should be able to explain complex systems and present to business clients. Responsibilities include customer presentations, proposals, &amp; service pricing.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
A successful Solutions Architect should be able to present and explain various technologies including networking, security, route/switch and wireless, guide them through requirements gathering, and formulate a solution consisting of hardware, software, licensing, and engineering services. Solutions Architects are responsible for creating scope of work, bill of materials, and task lists for various types of projects in support of these solutions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This person will need to demonstrate a high level of solutions design experience, be highly motivated about learning new products and technologies, able to effectively communicate with customers, ensure superior customer service, and maintain a strong customer relationship. You will also need to be able to demonstrate a firm understanding of business level requirements and how underlying technologies help deliver on those requirements. Implementation experience with the various products and technologies is a plus.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Meet with Presidio’s customers, collecting requirements for infrastructure solutions relating to security and networking</li>
<li>
Present and explain technologies to customers, guide them through requirements gathering and formulate a solution consisting of hardware, software, licensing, and engineering services</li>
<li>
Develop technical solutions for sale to Presidio customers.</li>
<li>
Work with Account Manager/Sales Team to develop customer relationships and solutions to assist in the sales process</li>
<li>
Create high level solutions designs/architecture and present to customers</li>
<li>
Creative Customer facing Presentations</li>
<li>
Create Bill of Materials &amp; Configurations for solutions</li>
<li>
Build Scope of Services, Task Lists and Professional Service Pricing</li>
<li>
Write Statements of Works</li>
<li>
Create technical deliverables including network assessments, capacity planning, detailed design documents, implementation plans, and configurations.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Required Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience and/or military experience</li>
<li>
5+ years of networking experience, including configuring, analyzing &amp; troubleshooting complex networks</li>
<li>
CCNP Security or R&amp;S certifications are highly preferred</li>
<li>
Additional certifications (Aruba, Juniper, Cisco, etc…) preferred</li>
<li>
Expert-level knowledge and experience with design, implementation and support of the following is highly preferred:</li>
<ul>
<li>
Complex routing and switching solutions (Cisco, Juniper, HP)</li>
<li>
Firewalls/VPNs (Cisco, Palo Alto, Juniper, etc.)</li>
<li>
Load balancing/Acceleration solutions (Cisco, F5, Netscaler)</li>
<li>
Network Access Control, including Cisco Identity Services Engine (ISE) implementation design, configuration, and deployment in a lab environment</li>
<li>
IDS/IPS solutions (Cisco, Tipping Point, etc. are optional)</li>
<li>
Authentication solutions including Cisco ACS and RSA</li>
<li>
SDN solutions such as Cisco ACI and VMware NSX</li>
<li>
SDWAN solutions such as Cisco Viptela, Citrix Netscaler, Meraki, etc…</li>
</ul>
<li>
Experience working as a consultant with multiple manufacturer partners is desirable.</li>
<li>
Proven experience in building enterprise-class solutions for large-scale global customers.</li>
<li>
Shown ability to design complete solutions using multiple technologies</li>
<li>
Experience and familiarity with Cloud services, providers, products</li>
<li>
Virtualization concepts, business benefits, ROI models</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Server and Storage Virtualization technology is required.</li>
<li>
Experience with networking protocols such as BGP, OSPF, etc</li>
<li>
Strong written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>
Candidate should be a team player with demonstrated ability to work without guidance.</li>
<li>
Effective interpersonal and active listening skills.</li>
<li>
Strong time management to effectively and efficiently manage one's own time.</li>
<li>
Ability to apply solutions, technology and products to a business opportunity.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Presidio is a leading North American IT solutions provider focused on Digital Infrastructure, Cloud and Security solutions to create agile, secure infrastructure platforms for middle-market customers. We deliver this technology expertise through a full life cycle model of professional, managed, and support services including strategy, consulting, implementation and design. By taking the time to deeply understand how our clients define success, we help them harness technology advances, simplify IT complexity and optimize their environments today while enabling future applications, user experiences, and revenue models. As of June 30, 2018, we serve approximately 8,000 middle-market, large, and government organizations across a diverse range of industries. Approximately 2,900 Presidio professionals, including more than 1,600 technical engineers, are based in 60+ offices across the United States in a unique, local delivery model combined with the national scale of a $2.8 billion dollar industry leader. We are passionate about driving results for our clients and delivering the highest quality of service in the industry. Presidio is majority owned by affiliates of investment funds managed by affiliates of Apollo Global Management, LLC (NYSE:APO).</p>
