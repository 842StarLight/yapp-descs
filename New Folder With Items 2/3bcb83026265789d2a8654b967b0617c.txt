<h2>Systems Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Lewan Technology, A Xerox Company, is seeking a Systems Engineer to join our growing team.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
As a <b>Lewan Systems Engineer</b>, you will be a billable engineer for commercial and enterprise accounts. You will support the core Microsoft technologies including Active Directory, Exchange, Sharepoint, Lync, SQL Server and clustering. We are seeking a strong IT generalist with advanced design and implementation skills for multiple core Microsoft and networking technologies widely used in the Midmarket space Windows server, Office 365, Exchange, backup, routers, firewalls, switching, Internet connectivity, Cloud computing, etc..</p>
<p><b>
Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Strong customer relations skills and a commitment to providing outstanding customer service</p></li>
<li><p>
Strong attention to detail</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to multitask and prioritize in order to meet deadlines</p></li>
<li><p>
Highly motivated and reliable self-starter</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to self-manage and work independently</p></li>
<li><p>
Act as the third level support for client issues via phone calls, email, or live chat</p></li>
<li><p>
Document issue resolution steps and final resolutions in Lewan's ticketing system</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop professional relationships with our partners and understand their escalation procedures</p></li>
</ul>
