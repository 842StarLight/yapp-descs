<h2>Retail Sales Associate</h2>

<br>
Sales Associate<br>
<br>
Grand Appliance and TV specializes in providing customers with a customer focused shopping experience in state-of-the-art showrooms featuring top quality brands. We are looking for smart, motivated individuals who want to earn strong commissions and grow within a rapidly expanding company.<br>
<br>
Duties and responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for engaging retail customers and businesses through all stages of the sales process; initial contact, qualifying needs and closing sales</li>
<li>Manage orders from point of purchase through completion.</li>
<li>Develop in store merchandising, including setup of displays, display maintenance and related tasks.</li>
<li>Promote Grand's advertising and marketing campaigns in store.</li>
<li>Additional duties as needed.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<ul>
<li>Must be friendly and customer-centric with strong organizational skills.</li>
<li>Ability to locate information quickly via online searches.</li>
<li>Understand different product lines and offerings.</li>
<li>Participate in initial, and continued, product training.</li>
<li>Ongoing management of a book of business for both retail and business-to-business customers.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Education and Experience:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred; applicable work experience and/or training will be considered in lieu of a Bachelor degree.</li>
<li>Experience selling major appliances or electronics is preferred, but not required</li>
</ul>
<br>
Benefits and Compensation:<br>
Compensation: This is a commission-based position. In addition to commissions, employees are eligible for bonuses through outside vendor spiffs and internal performance bonuses. Motivated sales professionals may earn from $70,000.00 to $100,000.00 per year.<br>
<br>
Benefits:<br>
Medical, Dental, Vision<br>
Life and AD&amp;D Insurance<br>
Short and Long-Term Disability Income Insurance<br>
401(k) retirement plan with employer matched contributions<br>
Paid holidays, PTO and more.<br>
