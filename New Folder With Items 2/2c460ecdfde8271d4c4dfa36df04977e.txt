<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Founded in 1959, Instrumentation Laboratory (IL) is a global leader in the development, manufacturing and distribution of diagnostic solutions for Acute Care Diagnostics, patient blood management and Hemostasis testing. Our success comes from a specific focus in these rapidly evolving diagnostic areas, our commitment to customers, and our dedication to innovation and quality. We’re passionate about providing medical professionals the most valuable and complete solutions to enhance patient care.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As an integral part of Werfen, a global healthcare company dedicated to delivering the highest quality <i>in vitro</i> diagnostic products, IL is supported by significant resources, outstanding scientific expertise and a tremendous knowledge base. IL is headquartered in Bedford, MA, USA.</div>
