<h2>Digital Marketing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Viome is an innovative biotech startup that applies AI and machine learning to biological data – e.g., microbiome, and transcriptome data – to provide direct-to-consumer personalized dietary and lifestyle recommendations for healthier living. We are a small, fast-growing, interdisciplinary team of passionate experts in biochemistry, microbiology, medicine, artificial intelligence, and machine learning with a shared mission to make illness optional.
<br><br>
We are seeking a Digital Marketing Analyst to join Viome’s Performance Marketing Team and help with the daily management of Paid Social, PPC &amp; Display monthly spend according to set CPA / ROAS goals. The role would add to the existing Performance Marketing team and report to the Director of Performance Marketing.
<br><br>
Our Digital Marketing Analyst should have a solid understanding of Performance Marketing, and effective ways to market to existing customers and acquire new customers. This role will help execute on Viome’s promotional calendar and to work closely with our sales and marketing teams to focus on Viome’s growth through innovative campaigns, a/b testing, data-driven analytics, and user feedback. The ideal candidate will be mission-driven, analytical, and possess a knack for finding creative ways to engage and retain current users and continue to develop new and emerging target markets within the health/wellness sector and beyond.
<br><br>
This is a full-time position at Viome’s office in Bellevue, WA.
