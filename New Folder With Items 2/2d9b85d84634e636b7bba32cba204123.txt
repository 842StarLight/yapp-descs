<p><b>Job Description Details
</b></p>
<p>Zulily is a retailer obsessed with bringing our customers something special every day. Unique products from up-and-coming brands are featured alongside favorites from top brands, including clothing, home decor, accessories, toys and gifts, giving customers something new to discover each morning at an incredible price. We are fast-paced, innovative and metric-driven, with a team passionate for delighting our customers.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Did you know that over 1/3 of Zulily's items for sale change every day? The Item Master team is responsible for the services that process all of Zulily’s product catalog of over 20 million items. We process millions of changes every day from our merchants and vendors; serving up product information worldwide for our online store, warehouses, external partners, and distribution centers. We build our own software and have several hard problems we need solved. The Item Master team has a direct and measurable impact on Zulily's $2B business.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are looking for a Software Engineering Manager, Vendor Services. Vender services underly our Vendor Portal (portal.zulily.com) is a key part of Zulily's growth strategy. You will work with product partners to set your roadmap. One of your first assignments will be to aggressively hire and grow your team.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Successful candidates are passionate about large scale distributed systems, high performance teams, and operational excellence. You work well in translating ambiguous business goals and can resolve competing priorities from multiple business partners.
</p>
