<h2>Manager, Revenue Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The New York Times is seeking an intellectually curious individual who is eager to dive into the various advertising analytics and optimization challenges in our digital advertising business.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Manager, Revenue Analytics will serve the Advertising teams by leading a team of analysts responsible for growing the business through actionable intelligence, rigorous data management, and easily understood data visualizations. The Manager, Revenue Analytics and analysts will partner with internal stakeholders to provide essential recommendations to crucial business decisions.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead and develop a team of analysts supporting the advertising growth across programmatic and direct sales channels. The team will collaborate with Operations, Ad Product, and Ad Platforms teams to implement yield solutions/recommendations.
</li>
<li>Owner of designing, developing, and maintaining of reporting platforms (dashboards and/or automated email reports) and reports that meet the needs of our internal decision makers. This will be accomplished in partnership with The New York Times Data &amp; Insights Group and Ad Technology teams.
</li>
<li>Responsible for creating and maintaining reporting models and insights for advertising initiatives, which may include producing revenue models assessing new and speculative ad product initiatives.
</li>
<li>Maintain strong understanding of ad platforms, data sources, and sales workflow, relying on these various inputs to develop data-driven recommendations for improving yield or operational efficiencies.
</li>
<li>Responsible for data reconciliation and quality assurance across direct and programmatic data sources. This includes creating necessary data requirements, policies, and processes to ensure automation, accuracy, and efficiencies.
</li>
<li>Owner and decision-maker of programmatic yield optimizations, quarterly rate cards, and day-to-day programmatic revenue performance. This will be achieved by partnering with Programmatic Solutions and Programmatic Operations to execute optimizations and rate adjustments.
</li>
<li>Provide key business insights to senior sales and advertising operations leadership based on sales performance trends. Visualize key metrics on an ongoing basis to communicate out to product, sales, and exec teams.
</li>
<li>Develop quantitative analysis, ad hoc reports, models, and A/B tests to support advertising decisions.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Education: BA/BS in Economics, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field
</li>
<li>4+ years of digital advertising experience required, yield or analytical experience preferred.
</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and ability to clearly synthesize large dataset into key insights.
</li>
<li>Highly proficient in the complexities of programmatic buying, selling, and technology.
</li>
<li>Experience with SQL queries, Google Analytics/Omniture, FreeWheel, Yieldex, Tableau/Domo strongly desired.
</li>
<li>Strong communication skills: ability to translate quantitative details into clear, succinct summaries and recommendations for peers, sales, and executive audiences. Possess an ability to work effectively and interface with clients, sales team, and product/platform teams.
</li>
<li>Background in digital ad operations, inventory/yield management. Deep familiarity (admin/manager experience) with DFP, XSM or comparable ad-serving and order management systems (Operative, FatTail, FreeWheel).
</li>
<li>Excellent time management and attention to detail
</li>
<li>A proven ability to do both tactical work and set an overall strategy when working on a project.
</li>
</ul>
