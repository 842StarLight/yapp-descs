<h2>Business Architect – Azure Application Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Customer success scaled through our customer aligned Cloud Solution Architects!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Do you have a passion for blazing new approaches that solve the most challenging customer scenarios and translating these solutions into scalable and reusable approaches through our field Customer Solution Architects (CSA)? If so, we want to talk with you. We are seeking an experienced Business Architect for Azure Application Development space who is responsible for designing, aligning and refining this portion of the Azure business strategy as executed by the field.</div>
<div>
<br>
Do you enjoy working with Microsoft’s field teams and our customers, and can you see a long-term vision to help drive execution strategy to enable our CSAs to unblock our customers? Do you have a record of accomplishments employing strong business and organizational skills, orchestrating across teams to deliver pragmatic and innovative solutions on time? Are you a growth minded leader who provides clarity, inspires change and creates energy across a broad base of teams, groups, customers and communities? If so, this role might be ideal for you.
</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div>
The Customer Success HQ team drive business usage of Azure and help our customers realize business value from their investments, by supporting the Customer Success field teams with programmatic and scalable assets. As the Business Architect, you will have:</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Business Acumen and Executive Presence</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Executive presence to build confidence and trust with corporate and field partners</li>
<li>Organized to drive ideation, planning and operationalization of business strategy</li>
<li>Drive field and engineering relationships across Microsoft to ensure we are focused on unblocking customers as they onboard to Azure</li>
<li>Versatile with proven ability to be adaptable in rapidly evolving business/org situations</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills, able to distill key insights from incomplete data</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Technical / Role Depth</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Expert knowledge of trends &amp; complications of core Azure application development, especially as it relates to how our customers adopt these technologies and evolve to a more cloud native mindset</li>
<li>Deep experience program managing and deploying programmatic strategies to our field teams. You will own the end to end planning and strategy for the app dev space</li>
<li>Role Excellence: Role design, accountabilities, comp, training requirements</li>
<li>Running the business: A14 blueprint management, weekly Scrum and overall business insights. You need to love spreadsheets and PBI as you will own the insights based on data.</li>
<li>Voice of the customer: programmatically, help design and participate in programs to gather feedback from our communities that represent the importance of our customers.</li>
<li>Validate and adjust strategy leveraging key Microsoft milestones (L1, SLT reviews, etc.), Act as v-team lead for critical Azure app dev services consumption strategies/blockers.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
The role is a programmatic, field facing role that is accountable for the end-to-end business, which is then executed by our field Cloud Solution Architects. This role represents a strong opportunity to have direct impact on the business and technical transformation of the our most important secret weapon: our CSAs!</div>
<div>
<br>
Success in this role depends on strong collaboration across a variety of customer, partner, and internal Microsoft stakeholders. This role is based in Redmond. Moderate travel will be required, 20%.</div>

Desired skills for this role include:</b></div>
