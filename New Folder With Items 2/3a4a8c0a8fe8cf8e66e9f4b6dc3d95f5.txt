<h2>Advertising Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Northwest Courier is a new, hyperlocal newspaper covering northwest Washington, DC, and we are looking for an experienced media sales professional to join our team.This position is responsible for creating and retaining advertising business to help grow the company.We’re looking for a self-starter with inside and outside direct sales experience as well as b2b sales experience.</p>
<p>Previous print/newspaper advertising sales experience is a plus. A strong knowledge of NW also a plus.</p>
<p>You will be expected to meet and exceed sales goals, make cold calls, and prospect for new customers.When calling and/or visiting new prospects, you will need to identify the key advertising decision makers and initiate &amp; cultivate productive relationships.</p>
<p>A strong work ethic is a must with a fearless approach to prospecting for new business.Looking for someone with strong interpersonal skills with excellent communication skills on the phone, in person, and in written communication.</p>
<p><b>Job: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sell advertising in our print and online products</li>
<li>Prospect for new business daily using online resources, phones, emails, and in person</li>
<li>Make sure ad payments are received on time</li>
<li>Conduct face-to-face presentations</li>
<li>Be a coachable and enthusiastic learner</li>
<li>Deliver outstanding customer service by acting as the liaison between advertisers and our graphic design team on matters such as ad design and ad updates</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>3+ years of sales experience (desired)</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, time management and follow-through skills</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment</li>
<li>Superior customer service and verbal/written communication skills</li>
<li>Experience working in a sales-oriented environment</li>
<li>Proven ability to communicate in a creative, persuasive style</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office and Excel</li>
</ul>
<p>Salary plus commission, with a high ceiling on earning potential for the right person.</p>
<p>Please submit resume and a cover letter.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $30,000.00 to $60,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>sales: 3 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's (Required)</li></ul>

<ul><li>Commission</li></ul>
