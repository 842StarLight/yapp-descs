<h2>Director of Solutions Consulting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>About the Opportunity</b><br>
Epsilon is seeking a Platform Solutions Consultant in its Enterprise Software Sales Division who will be responsible for presenting and highlighting the Agility Unite software product to prospective clients.
<div></div>
<br>
<div>These activities will include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Successfully demonstrate Epsilon's solution capabilities to prospective clients within the context of their business and marketing environment</li>
<li>Able to configure / customize demo environments (either individually or working with a remote team)</li>
<li>Understand and articulate value proposition of software products to help attract, engage and retain prospects/customers across verticals</li>
<li>Effectively collaborate with sales teams (including cross-functional representatives from sales, client services, product management and custom development) in the pursuit of new business opportunities</li>
<li>Serve as a subject matter expert in proposing and presenting Epsilon's Agility Unite offerings</li>
<li>Advise on best practices utilization of advanced product functionality</li>
<li>Be conversant in both the business and technical aspects of solution to confidently answer client questions and inquiries</li>
<li>Able to lead or co-lead client meetings, with a strong ability to consume, distill and process information in real-time in order to problem solve in the context of the solution</li>
<li>Build a comprehensive understanding of Agility Unite and go to market proposition and act as an evangelist with key prospects and targets</li>
<li>Partner with Product Management to identify market trends, solution benefits and contribute to direction of product roadmap</li>
<li>Prior Solution Consulting / Sales Engineering experience</li>
<li>Role will be 100% focused on product demonstrations</li>
<li>Foundational ability to tell a compelling story</li>
<li>High level of understand on customer experience and technology</li>
<li>Will require up to 50% travel</li>
<li>Minimum of 7 years' industry experience</li>
<li>Bachelor's or Advanced Degree preferred</li>
</ul>
</div>
