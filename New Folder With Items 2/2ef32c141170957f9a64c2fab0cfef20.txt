<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>GENERAL SUMMARY</b></h1>
<p>
Buyer I position is an introductory position in Purchasing. Under general supervision, this position is responsible for centralized procurement of commodities to provide the most efficient and cost effective means to support daily plant operations. Additionally, this position will support the Purchasing Department’s Management. Support of the Manufacturing and Engineering areas in general documentation, updates, and review is required. Buyer may assist in improvement activities and general staff support.</p>
