<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<li>Stay up-to-date on franchise promotions</li>
<li>Visit territory at least twice per year with a Brand Manager</li>
</ul>
