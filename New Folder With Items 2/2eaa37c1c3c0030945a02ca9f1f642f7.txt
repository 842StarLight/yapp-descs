<h2>Platform Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Optanix <b>Platform Support Specialist</b> <b>(PSS1)</b> will follow defined PaaS procedures in managing daily service tickets and providing the highest level of customer service satisfaction. The PSS Level 1 will be responsible for taking ownership of client specific requests, incidents and resolution process for the product support lifecycle. The PSS Level 1 will be an expert in the user experience utilizing the GUI for each Platform release that is deployed in the field. The PSS Level 1 will proactively review client trends, perform client root cause analysis and document executable plans and steps to reduce repeatable incidents using the Knowledge Database. This position requires consistent communication and feedback to other functional groups within Optanix, such as Customer Success Managers, Engineering, Product Management and RMS (Remote Managed Services).
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Core Competencies:</b></p>
<ul><li>General understanding of LAMP stack technologies (Linux, Apache, MySQL and PHP).
</li></ul>
<ul><li>General understanding of Network and Systems Administration.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Self-motivated, analytical, quick learner, organized, detail-oriented, result oriented, and ability to multitask.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong written and verbal communication skills, including ability to deliver customer presentations or trainings. Strong work</li></ul>
ethic, initiative and integrity.
