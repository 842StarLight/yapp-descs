<p><b>OVERVIEW:</b></p>
<p>
Ironclad’s Marketing Specialist will be in charge of leading our company's marketing efforts. This person is expected to represent Ironclad on the highest level to all vendors and partners, and through all digital and physical platforms. The Marketing Specialist is expected to have a deep conviction for brand perception, messaging and loyalty. They will be responsible for providing guidance to our marketing department, vendors and partners by developing and communicating strategies, planning and coordinating marketing efforts, and building awareness and positioning for Ironclad and its partners.<br>
</p>
<p><b>PRIMARY DUTIES AND ACTIVITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for creating/executing, managing, maintaining and constantly improving all Ironclad outlets. Including, but not limited to:
<ul>
<li>Ironclad Website, Shop, Ironclad Podcast</li>
<li>Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, Etc.</li>
<li>Ironclad SWAG, Mailers, Marketing materials</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Vendor + Partnership relationship management</li>
<li>Approves budgeting and sources all Ironclad Brand, PR, and Marketing materials, and design initiatives.</li>
<li>Maintains close oversight of all brand touch points.</li>
<li>Social Media Planning and Management.</li>
<li>Digital Asset management and maintenance.</li>
<li>Creates/Manages/Delegates Ironclad’s Physical Branding Assets</li>
<li>Responsible for all Ironclad branded documents, digital and physical, for internal and external use with brand continuity.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<li>Must be able to Produce, Schedule and publish Podcast</li>

<li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team<br>
