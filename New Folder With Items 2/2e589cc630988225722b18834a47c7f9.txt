<h2>Vice President, Branding and Design - ABC Entertainment</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>We are seeking a creative, talented and experienced leader to oversee the ABC Entertainment Marketing design team. The role is unique, as will be the right candidate. Reporting into the SVP of Creative, the position will be responsible for oversight over the on and off-air design team along with the ABC brand and identity. The candidate must have excellent design and creative skills. He/she must thrive in a fast-paced, high-volume environment and be able to lead, inspire and motivate a creative team with a management style that embraces innovation, teamwork, collaboration and mentorship.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities :</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Oversee all design for ABC Entertainment, both motion and still, for multiple platforms including but not limited to on-air, OOH, print and digital from concept to completion.</li>
<li>
Manage and evolve the creative style guide to maintain the integrity of the ABC brand and ensure the highest quality standards, policies and procedures internally and across all media platforms.</li>
<li>
Empower, motivate and manage a high-performing creative team of 20+ including, art directors, designers, producers and flame artists. Be a creative visionary with the ability to inspire new thinking and energize team to think beyond tradition and create break-out, eye-catching design.</li>
<li>
Oversee relationships with key network and show executives, talent, outside creative agencies, vendors and photographers.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the internal on-air creative and digital/social teams on artwork to ensure design assets are optimized for their needs.</li>
<li>
Direct the operations of an in-house design team that is responsible for building a diverse range of creative assets (on-air graphics, ads, evites, event signage, EST/SVOD graphics, premium graphics, synergy OOH placements) for internal departments including the Affiliate Marketing, Affiliates Sales &amp; Marketing, Publicity, Franchise Management, Digital Sales and Events teams</li>
<li>
Monitor creative budgets and sign off all on vendor invoices</li>
<li>
Supervise the organization and distribution of all show and network creative assets with internal and external partners</li>
</ul>
