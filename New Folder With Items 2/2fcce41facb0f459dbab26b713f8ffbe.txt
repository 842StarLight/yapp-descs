<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Rapidly-growing CPA firm in southern Orange County, NY is looking for a Staff Accountant. Must have a minimum of 3 years public accounting experience.</p>
<p>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare individual &amp; business tax returns</li>
<li>Prepare sales tax returns</li>
<li>Prepare quarterly &amp; year-end payroll tax returns</li>
<li>Monthly client bookkeeping using QuickBooks</li>
<li>Prepare insurance audits</li>
<li>Prepare forms 1099 &amp; 1096</li>
<li>Research &amp; correspond with Federal &amp; State agencies</li>
<li>Prepare financial statements</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience/skills REQUIRED:</p>
<ul>
<li>Accounting degree required, CPA or EA a plus</li>
<li>Must have strong written &amp; verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of QuickBooks, Excel, &amp; tax software (Proseries a plus)</li>
<li>Must be able to work independently and follow precise directions</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years of public accounting experience including bookkeeping using QuickBooks and Individual &amp; Business tax preparation.</li>
</ul>
<p>30-40 hour work week (off season), 40+ hours/week during tax season and deadlines, flexible hours and benefits available. This is not a temporary position. Please email your resume, along with salary history and requirements. No calls or in-person visits, applicants will be contacted for an interview if qualified.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
