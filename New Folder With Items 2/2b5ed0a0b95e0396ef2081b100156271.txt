<p><b>Business Systems Analyst</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Come join a team of experienced Technologists and Analysts who work on highly visible, technically challenging projects where you are responsible for planning, design and implementation of business processes. We are looking for a Sr. Business Systems Analyst to support Lam’s Logistics team, where you travel less than 15% of the time, have a schedule that allows work/life balance and your contributions are recognized.</p>
<p>As a critical member of the Global Operations team, you will be the adding strength as a SAP Logistics Execution (LE) resource. You will have the opportunity to evaluate and optimize systems and resources, manage re-engineering initiatives and ensure alignment with business growth objectives.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Primary Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze complex business problems to be solved with automated system solutions/process re-engineering</li>
<li>Lead cross-functional efforts to address business or systems issues as required</li>
<li>Serve as a lead BSA in medium to large size projects covering all phases of the project from inception through post mortem</li>
<li>Leads cross-functional efforts to address business or systems issues as required</li>
<li>Provide expertise in identifying, evaluating and developing systems requirements and procedures that are cost effective, meet user requirements and consider data quality requirements</li>
<li>Assists in all aspects of project management: Planning (Blueprint and Spec), Designing (Technical Spec), Test Plans (User acceptance testing), Training/Documentation (Roll-out), Cut-over, and Project Closure (Post Mortem)</li>
<li>Plans and executes unit, integration and acceptance testing to support IS corporate initiative.</li>
<li>Assist the business unit in troubleshooting and solving system problems.</li>
<li>Collaborate on preparation of training material and delivery of User training.</li>
<li>Provide the Business unit with systems customer support.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Technology or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>6+ years overall SAP experience with 3+ years of experience with Logistics; specifically hands-on configuration.</li>
<li>Logistics functions supporting Manufacturing (Lean and volume) a must.</li>
<li>Participation in at least one end-to-end project (analysis, design, config/development build, test, deployment including training and data conversion)</li>
<li>Hands-on implementation experience in the following SAP modules:<br>
MM-IM - Inventory Management<br>
WM - Warehouse Management</li>
<li>Implementation experience in the following is a plus</li>
<li>EWM - Extended Warehouse Management</li>
<li>LE-SHP - Logistics Execution / Shipping</li>
<li>LE-TRA - Logistics Execution / Transportation</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of some of the following modules: PP (Production Planning), SD (Sales and Distribution), MM-PUR (Purchasing), QM (Quality Management), PM (Plant Maintenance)</li>
<li>Strong relationship, interpersonal, communication and business process skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated planning and coordination skills with development initiatives.</li>
<li>Demonstrated project management experience.</li>
<li>Proven ability to achieve success without direct organizational authority functional lines.</li>
<br>
</ul>
