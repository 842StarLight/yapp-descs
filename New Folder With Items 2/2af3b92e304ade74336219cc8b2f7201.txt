<h2>Benefit Verification Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">ConnectiveRx is a leading, technology-enabled healthcare services company. We work strategically with hundreds of biopharmaceutical manufacturers to help commercialize and maximize the benefits of specialty and branded medications. Our mission is to simplify how patients get on and stay on therapy. We fulfill our mission by providing our customers with innovative services such as patient and provider messaging, the design and operation of copay, vouchers and patient affordability programs, and hub services, all of which accelerate speed-to-therapy and help improve outcomes for manufacturers, healthcare providers and patients.
<br><br>
ConnectiveRx was formed in 2015 by bringing together the industry-leading business of PSKW, PDR/LDM, Careform (2017) and The Macaluso Group (2018) to advance our technology-driven expertise in providing state-of-the-art commercialization solutions. To learn more about our company, visit ConnectiveRx.com

Job Description
<br><br>
This role establishes effective communication with patients/families, physicians, third party payors, pharmaceutical manufacturer representatives. Represents the company in a competent, professional and responsive manner. Interacts with the operations and quality team consistent with team-building and professional culture established by the company. Coordinates the care of patient to assure access to pharmacy products and services. Maintains high quality customer service standards in compliance with federal and state regulations and guidelines. Works in collaboration with team members and management to respond to the requests.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Verify insurance coverage for potential new patients and re-verify insurance coverage for existing patients in order to process patient prescription needs successfully and demonstrate excellent customer service to patients, healthcare professionals and insurance carriers</li>
<li>Collect the appropriate information needed to ensure insurance coverage for patient medication orders and document in computer systems</li>
<li>Process patient prescription needs successfully, reduce exposure to adverse events, and demonstrate excellent customer service to patients, healthcare professionals and insurance carriers</li>
<li>Contact insurance carriers to resolve insurance coverage issues</li>
<li>Interface with other departments in a collaborative effort to identify solutions which will promote customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Respond to messages and support tickets within designated timeframe.</li>
<li>Maintain or exceed designate quality production standards.</li>
<li>Communicate with other departments to resolve issues.</li>
<li>Communicate with providers, sales agents and account managers as necessary to problem solve.</li>
<li>Recommend innovative solutions to existing issues. • Obtain accurate demographic, insurance and financial information from healthcare professionals and patients to complete enrollment applications for new patients</li>
<li>Communicating medical information to customers, patients, team members via thorough understanding of products and service lines offered</li>
<li>Maintain a timely turnaround for insurance verifications</li>
<li>Performs in accordance to established ConnectiveRx values.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent, required.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in business, hospital administration, marketing, or related field preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years experience in customer service, insurance, benefits, marketing, sales or related area, required.</li>
<li>Health care environment with third party benefits verification experience, a plus.</li>
<li>Prior phone customer service experience with essential skills in navigating a software system to document conversations and outcomes, as well as managing follow-up actions, highly desired.</li>
<li>Knowledge of government and patient assistance programs.</li>
<li>Understanding of commercial insurance concepts including drug cards and major medical benefits.</li>
<li>Analytical and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge of and understanding of how to communicate information on health insurance, reimbursements, third party terms and medical terminology is preferred.</li>
<li>Computer and math skills are required with attention to detail and quality essential.</li>
<li>Ability to work with people in a team environment while meeting individual performance goals</li>
<li>Must have effective and empathetic communication style in managing in/outbound calls and communications with clients and team members.</li>
<li>Ability to build constructive and effective relationships; ability to use diplomacy and tact in resolving issues, and ability to diffuse even high-tension situations comfortably.</li>
<li>Experience in providing timely information needed by others in the process and being a team player.</li>
<li>Ability to find common ground and solve problems for the good of all; is cooperative and able to gain trust and support; ability to collaborate effectively.</li>
<li>Approachable, and dedicated to providing the highest quality of services.</li>
<li>Must possess core values of passion, innovation, integrity and accountability</li>
</ul>
<b>Working Conditions:</b><br>
While performing the duties of this job, the employee must be able to:
<br><ul>
<li>Perform primarily sedentary work with occasional lifting up to 20 pounds, and exerting up to 10 pounds of force occasionally, and a negligible amount of force frequently or constantly to lift, carry, push, pull or otherwise move objects.</li>
<li>A majority of the work day is in a call center environment with heavy phone contact/communications.</li>
<li>See, hear, talk and perform tasks requiring visual acuity, manual dexterity, grasping and other similar tasks requiring physical activity and repetitive motions.</li>
<li>Operate standard office and computer equipment and spend a majority of work day communicating via phone and data entry.</li>
</ul>
