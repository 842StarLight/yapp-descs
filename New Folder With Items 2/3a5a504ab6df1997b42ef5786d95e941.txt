<p>
Are you passionate about developing exceptional teams and executing winning strategies? Looking for a career move into management with an industry leading employer that offers exceptional earnings and career advancement potential? If so, we want to talk with you to discuss our exciting General Manager role which will also lead the sales efforts of the branch. You will be personally supporting the capture of new HVAC retrofit and comprehensive energy solutions projects, as well as service agreements in the greater Boston area. It is a great opportunity for a successful solutions sales professional with HVAC experience to make the move into management, or a general manager with a strong sales background ready to take a business to the next level. The selected candidate will manage the sales and operations teams, and will be responsible for profit/loss. This role reports to the Regional Senior Vice President.</p>
