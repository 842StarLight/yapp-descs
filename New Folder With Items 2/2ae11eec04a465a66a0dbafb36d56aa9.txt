<h2>Machine & Process Operator - 2nd shift</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Company Overview:</b></p>
<p>
Brady Corporation is an international manufacturer and marketer of complete solutions that identify and protect people, products and places. Brady's products help customers increase safety, security, productivity and performance and include high-performance labels, signs, safety devices, printing systems and software. Founded in 1914, the Company has a diverse customer base in electronics, telecommunications, manufacturing, electrical, construction, medical, aerospace and a variety of other industries. Brady is headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin and as of July 31, 2018, employed approximately 6,200 people in its worldwide businesses. Brady's fiscal 2018 sales were approximately $1.17 billion. Brady stock trades on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol BRC. More information is available at www.bradycorp.com.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The machine operator will be responsible for operating equipment to manufacture fiberglass, acrylic, HDPE, aluminum, stainless steel, &amp; porcelain using each department's dedicated machinery. This position is responsible for the production of valuable, defect-free products that meet or exceed our customers' expectations. The machine operator will be successful in this position by executing to standard work &amp; adhering to all company policies.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform production equipment set up, clean up, safe operation, &amp; operator level maintenance of assigned equipment</li>
<li>Identify &amp; maintain quality standards while meeting daily &amp; weekly production goals</li>
<li>Understand &amp; follow established standard work documents</li>
<li>Perform adjustments to production equipment as necessary</li>
<li>Communicate production performance to appropriate leaders &amp; departments</li>
<li>Support area goals for continuous improvement by serving on project teams as required</li>
<li>Package product according to specification</li>
<li>Adhere to Brady's attendance, safety, &amp; other company policies</li>
<li>Maintain general housekeeping &amp; clean production areas at end of every shift</li>
<li>Provide support within other production areas as needed &amp; other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>1 year experience in a manufacturing environment</li>
<li>Maintain strong attendance</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team environment</li>
<li>Ability to train others</li>
<li>Ability to read &amp; follow verbal &amp; written instructions</li>
<li>Flexible in schedule to work overtime, nights &amp; weekends as needed</li>
<li>Ability to lift 50 lbs repeatedly &amp; st&amp; for 100% of shift</li>
<li>Ability to bend, twist, crawl, climb &amp; reach to perform job duties</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Associates Degree</li>
<li>5+ years of manufacturing experience at 1 employer</li>
<li>CNC Machine operation &amp;/or programming (Lasers &amp; Routers)</li>
<li>Printing - Wide Web Inkjet, Computer-to-Plate, Screen Printing</li>
<li>Experience using SAP</li>
</ul>
