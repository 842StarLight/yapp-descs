<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Holiday Inn Express</b><br>
Provide leadership and demonstrate the company culture with every action and/or reactions. Plan strategically to maximize profits. Responsible for all major areas of hotel operations and services including, but not limited to: Front Office, Housekeeping, Maintenance and Food and Beverage to include but not limited to: personnel, budget performance and financial controls in accordance with established quality standards. Responsible for all operations for their hotel brand.<br>
<br>
<b>PRE REQUISITES</b><br>
The Company associates have access to guestrooms and property, character traits of honesty and trustworthiness are essential to this position. Therefore, associates must pass the appropriate security clearance, per company policy.
<ul>
<li>Five years experience supervising (at least) 15 associates.</li>
<li>
Bachelors Degree helpful but not required.</li>
</ul>
<b>
SUMMARY OF ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b><br>
Must be able to perform major life activities: Standing, stooping, lifting, bending, learning, reading, concentrating, thinking, talking, hearing and effectively communicate.<br>
<br>
For this position specifically:<ul>
<li>
Must be able to push or pull 60 pounds and lift and/or carry 30 pounds.</li>
<li>
Must be able to use hands to finger, handle or feel.
</li>
<li>Must be able to stand for eight hours, bend, stretch and reach.</li>
<li>
Must be able to speak and read English, the ability to communicate in another language may be helpful.</li>
<li>
Must be able to work effectively in a stressful atmosphere.</li>
<li>
Must be able to accept constructive criticism.
</li>
<li>Must be able to change activity frequently and cope with interruptions.</li>
<li>
Must be able to see differences in widths and lengths of lines such as graphs.
</li>
<li>Must display professionalism, honesty and trustworthiness at all times.
</li>
</ul>

<b>WORKING CONDITIONS/SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS</b><br>
Standing, walking for long periods of time while maintaining an aggressively friendly professional image. Be flexible in regard to work schedule. Be available as necessary 24/7, weekends and holidays.<br>
<br>
<b>POSITIONS FOR POSSIBLE ADVANCEMENT</b><br>
Before the next promotion, this person should develop and train her/his replacement. The ability to teach skills and competencies will enhance leadership skills in preparation for further promotion, potentially as a District Manager, Director of Operations, or Vice President of Operations.<br>
