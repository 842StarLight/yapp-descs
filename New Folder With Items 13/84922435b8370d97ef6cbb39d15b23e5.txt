<h2>Chief Financial Officer (CFO)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p>Department: Management</p>
<p>Location: Corvallis Main Campus Store<br>Status/Category: M (Full-time), benefit eligible, Exempt, Salaried</p>
<p>The Chief Financial Officer of the OSU Beaver Store is a member of the company’s Executive Management Team and reports directly to the Chief Executive Officer.</p>
<p>What we are looking for in our next CFO?</p>
<p>1. A well-rounded accounting professional with a strong analytical skill set<br>2. A strategic advisor who actively improves decision-making throughout the company<br>3. An energetic, hands-on leader who is highly adaptable to change<br>4. A continual learner who is always seeking to improve our methods, processes, and customer experience</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b></p>
<p><b>LEADERSHIP </b></p>
<p>1. As a member of company’s executive management team, provide the CEO and colleagues with timely and accurate financial reporting, analysis, and decision support. Contribute in the development and execution of the company’s strategic planning process using financial analysis and modeling.</p>
<p>2. Are responsible for full-cycle talent management, to include recruiting, training, coaching and talent development for assigned employees. Ensure effective performance management and maintain a culture of accountability. Develop cross-functional teams of employees with the ability to execute initiatives and meet operational standards. Develop and promote a team environment and provide leadership within the company to all staff levels.</p>
<p>3. Oversee Information Technology staff to ensure all company IT systems are functioning properly and meeting business needs.</p>
<p>4. Oversee Loss Prevention staff to ensure loss prevention program is meeting company asset protection and risk mitigation goals and objectives.</p>
<p>5. Ensure compliance with all company general policies, personnel policies, standards of conduct and procedures.</p>
<p><b>ACCOUNTING/FINANCE</b></p>
<p>1. Are responsible for all company financial management and reporting, including accounting, payroll, taxation, and administrative functions.</p>
<p>2. Provide decision support to CEO and Executive Team in all aspects of planning and operations.</p>
<p>3. Ensure a strong and comprehensive internal control environment is in place, and that internal controls are working as designed.</p>
<p>4. Apply analytical procedures to all financial accounts and ratios to ensure the accuracy of financial reporting and as a basis for communicating trends and recommend strategies.</p>
<p>5. Direct company investing and borrowing to meet capital, liquidity, and other company objectives.</p>
<p>6. Ensure that all funds, physical assets and other property of the company are appropriately safeguarded and administered.</p>
<p>7. Manage the company’s independent audit process, the filing of all appropriate corporate tax returns, and any other examinations by outside entities/agencies.</p>
<p>8. Monitor and prepare for changes in the university store industry, economic conditions and the regulatory environment and incorporate the potential impact into the company’s strategic plan.</p>
<p>9. Oversee and manage contractual relationships with external vendors and suppliers. Evaluate and negotiate contractual agreements.</p>
<p>10. Develop and maintain positive relationships in the areas of tax compliance, independent audits, banking, insurance, property management and maintenance, IT support, ERP system support, and institutional customer relations among others.</p>
<p>11. Ensure business processes are in alignment with the Company’s core values, business and risk mitigation objectives, and compliant with all applicable laws, rules and regulations.</p>
<p>12. Perform additional duties as directed by the CEO.</p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES</b></p>
<p>1. Must demonstrate managerial abilities including organization, project management, planning, and leadership.</p>
<p>2. Must demonstrate the ability to effectively interact and relate in a professional manner with other senior managers as well as subordinates.</p>
<p>3. Must demonstrate strong interpersonal skills and written and verbal communications skills).</p>
<p>4. Must have strong analytical and problem-solving skills and outstanding financial acumen, including a comprehensive understanding of GAAP, accounting transaction cycles and systems, financial and tax reporting, internal controls, and financial modeling.</p>
<p>5. Must demonstrate ability to design, implement and maintain financial management systems to produce timely, accurate, and reliable reporting and analysis.</p>
<p>6. Must have strong Excel skills and be able to effectively navigate/operate accounting, point-of-sale, enterprise resource planning (ERP), and other software used throughout the business.</p>
<p><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS </b></p>
<p>1. A Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting, Finance or Business Administration and<br>2. Four to six years of Accounting/Finance experience with at least 2 of those years in a managerial or leadership capacity or<br>3. Any equivalent combination of education and experience.</p>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS </b></p>
<p>1. CPA Certification<br>2. Experience in a CFO or Controller function<br>3. Experience in Retail management</p>
