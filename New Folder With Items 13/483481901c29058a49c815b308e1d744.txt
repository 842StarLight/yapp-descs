<h2>Business Development Manager - Technology Partner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>At Red Hat, we connect an innovative community of customers, partners, and contributors to deliver an open source stack of trusted, high-performing solutions. We offer cloud, Linux, middleware, storage, and virtualization technologies, together with award-winning global customer support, consulting, and implementation services. Red Hat is a rapidly growing company supporting more than 90% of Fortune 500 companies.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Red Hat Technology Partner Program team is looking for a Business Development Manager to join us in Westford, MA. In this role, you will work on a product strategy that is based on our vision of an open hybrid cloud platform that enables any application in any environment on any cloud and relies on a vibrant ecosystem of certified offerings and services from our technology partners made available to enterprise customers. You’ll become part of the Technology Partner Program team that offers partners the expertise, tools, and certification offerings needed to successfully participate in the commercial ecosystem. As a Business Development Manager, you should be an experienced technical business development professional who facilitates and maintains a proactive, targeted ecosystem development with priority partners and market segments, while working closely with other teams and functions across the organization.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary job responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Manage the proactive recruiting, onboarding, support, and promotion of assigned priority partners</li>
<li>
Participate in defining the requirements and roadmaps for partner offerings for certification and joint go-to-market strategies</li>
<li>
Participate in defining marketing positioning, promotion, and business development plans with priority partners for their certified offerings</li>
<li>
Lead the development and continuous updating of Red Hat's view of assigned market segments, most relevant partner offerings, and competitive analysis and its importance to Red Hat</li>
<li>
Manage the priority and coverage process with all involved Red Hat’s teams for assigned market segments</li>
<li>
Oversee the cross-functional collaboration on proactive, high touch partner recruitment, onboarding, support, and promotion</li>
<li>
Enable and coach virtual teams guiding ecosystem development in the assigned market segments</li>
<li>
Track and report partner engagement and progress for assigned market segments</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required skills
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of experience managing partners or partner programs for large IT companies</li>
<li>
Experience working with independent software vendors (ISVs) and value-added partner types</li>
<li>
Ability to work across multiple teams within Red Hat</li>
<li>
Creative and curious with a sense of humor and a professional attitude</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree</li>
</ul>
