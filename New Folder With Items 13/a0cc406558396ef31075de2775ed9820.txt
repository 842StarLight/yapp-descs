<h2>IT Support Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Title: </b> IT Support Technician</p>
<p><b>Compensation and Benefits: </b> Benefits available include medical, dental &amp; short-term disability insurance, 401K Plan with company matching, bonus potential and PTO.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>American AllWaste has an immediate opportunity for an IT Support Technician in its Nashville, TN office. This position’s primary role of responsibility will be to provide IT support to our staff, and where necessary coordinate outside vendors to complete IT tasks that can’t be handled in house or remotely. As a company that is currently expanding through acquisitions, this position will help in the adoption of new IT policies and system integration. This position will be based in Nashville but will provide remote assistance to all of our offices in Tennessee and Texas and any future offices.</p>
<p><b>Provide Support and Service: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support network hardware and software</li>
<li>Set up and upgrade computer systems</li>
<li>Provide application training to end-users</li>
<li>Perform administrative tasks and maintain documentation, as necessary</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Troubleshoot: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Computer and other IT equipment problems and provide resolutions</li>
<li>MS Office/Office 365 end-user issues</li>
<li>LAN/WAN connectivity issues</li>
<li>Network systems, including printers, firewalls, servers, and more</li>
<li>All other IT-related hardware and software</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Miscellaneous Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Inventory management of IT Assets</li>
<li>Onboarding and offboarding of users</li>
<li>Miscellaneous tasks to help support our sales team</li>
<li>Help with IT transition of newly acquired companies</li>
<li>“Clean Up” current systems and processes in support of best practices</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge of and experience with: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Network concepts, hardware, and software, including Microsoft operating systems</li>
<li>OS X, iOS, and Android operating systems</li>
<li>Active Directory, Exchange, and Office 365</li>
<li>Backup solutions, terminology, and methodology</li>
<li>Strong Excel Skills</li>
<li>Experience with NetSuite, Quickbooks and/or FileMaker (or similar database) is preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>Some travel is expected in the Tennessee area, when remote assistance is not sufficient.</p>
