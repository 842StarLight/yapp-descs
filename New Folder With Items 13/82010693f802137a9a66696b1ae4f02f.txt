<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Please review the following job description:
</b></h3>
<p></p>Responsible for working and managing a specific geographic territory in order to generate sales within the Sterling Capital mutual funds, separately managed accounts (SMA's), unified managed accounts (UMA's), and/or sub-advised products. Communicate the process and philosophy of the various Sterling Capital investment products to prospects and existing clients.
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>Following is a summary of the essential functions for this job. Other duties may be performed, both major and minor, which are not mentioned below. Specific activities may change from time to time.
</p>
<p>1. Prepare business plans and schedules and conduct outside sales calls to financial professionals to influence them to sell and promote Sterling Capital investment solutions within an assigned territory.
</p>
<p>2. Collaborate with Internal Sales Partner on identification and development of opportunities within the territory.
</p>
<p>3. Collaborate with Internal Sales Partner on relationship building and quarterly account reviews.
</p>
<p>4. Conduct one-on-one meetings, seminars and training sessions to financial professionals.
</p>
<p>5. Participate in all company conference calls and sales meetings.
</p>
<p>6. Coach individual sales associates on selling techniques.
</p>
<p>7. Conduct product and non-product specific educational seminars.
</p>
<p>8. Review periodicals on the market, fund performance, economy and product position strategy and manager philosophy to engage in topical conversations in the financial institutions environment.
</p>
<p>9. Manage territory to maximize sales with the efficient use of agreed-upon expenses. Budget estimated sales and client support expenses on an annual basis. Submit expense reports semi-monthly.
</p>
<p>10. Attend/conduct seminars and conferences.
</p>
<p>11. Work to retain existing assets and clients.
</p>
<p>12. Utilize Salesforce.com for Customer Relationship Management (CRM).
</p>
<p></p>
