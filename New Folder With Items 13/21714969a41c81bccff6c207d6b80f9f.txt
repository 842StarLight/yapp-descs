<h2>Principal Material Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Job Description:<br>
As a<b> Principal Supply Chain Program Manager</b>you’re a self-starter, capable of developing and achieving program objectives in a matrix environment and able to successfully interface with functional and program leadership. In short, you’re able to lead and influence without direct reporting relationships.<br>
<br>
In this highly visible role, you’re a member of the <b>Patriot Core Execution Team</b>, and as the Supply Chain interface to Patriot programs you’re the liaison between Supply Chain and the BMC4I Integrated Product Team. You’ll create and execute Material Product Strategies and the Material Product Plans to ensure the flawless execution of products and programs from the front end of the business through contract closure.<br>
<br>
<b>Key responsibilities will include:</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Accountability for the supplier management process</li>
<li>Communication of customer flow-through requirements</li>
<li>Involvement with daily supply chain activities associated with each of the programs, including financial reporting and analysis</li>
<li>Developing and executing material product strategies</li>
<li>Achieving product material budget objectives</li>
<li>Managing product purchase/subcontract requirements</li>
<li>Creating and tracking key product area performance metrics</li>
<li>Driving/participating in Six Sigma projects (both Supply Chain and Program) and partnering with internal and external customers</li>
<li>Supporting monthly program and IPT Reviews</li>
<li>Spearheading Risk &amp; Opportunities identification and mitigation/capture efforts</li>
<li>Involvement with supplier management efforts associated with suppliers critical to program success</li>
</ul>
<br>
