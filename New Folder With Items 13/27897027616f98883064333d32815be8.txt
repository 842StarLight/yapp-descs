<h2>Product Enablement Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Partner Enablement Program Specialist</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 <b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
Join the Sitecore Partner Success team as a Partner Enablement Program Specialist to lead the creation, management, delivery and measurement of success as you enable Sitecore’s Global Partner Ecosystem on key solutions including Sitecore Content Hub and Sitecore Experience Platform.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Why This Position Matters
</b></p>
<p>Sitecore Partners are the primary implementation and delivery channel for Sitecore solutions. Excellent partner enablement directly results in Sitecore’s ability to empower our partners to best scope, sell, implement and deliver their services with our software solutions, and help our mutual customers achieve excellent outcomes in digital transformation.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This is a highly visible, high-impact role carrying critical responsibilities to manage and influence hundreds of partners around the world in how they position, develop, and deliver exceptional digital experiences for mid-market and enterprise brands around the world.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The position’s mission is to help our partners to develop highly competent Sitecore practice with our solutions starting with how to discover opportunities, sell, position and demonstrate our offerings - through to achieving a deep understanding of solution architecture, installation/configuration, implementation, delivery, customization, key use cases, and customer workflows that result in a mutually happy customer and more business opportunities for both Sitecore and our Partners.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Responsibilities</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and execute strategies, programs, and content to achieve partner skill growth and build robust competency capabilities for specific Sitecore solutions, including Digital Asset Management and Content Marketing.</li>
<li>
Identify and own delivery of content and programs needed to best enable our Partners, which involves working collaboratively with product marketing, product management, training, technical marketing, sales, partner alliance teams, partner communications, and other internal Sitecore teams to create and publish the materials</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Help craft, create, and participate in online webinars, workshops, bootcamps, Partner Advisory Councils, and other online or in-person events that help us better understand, educate and assist our Partners</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Manage and administer content on Sitecore’s Enablement platform (Showpad) including development of learning paths by role and solution; maintaining the state of content on the platform including posting new or refreshed assets; and archiving mature assets.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Measure, track, analyze, and report out on state of partners progress across Sitecore’s enablement framework, highlighting gaps and recommending corrective steps and actions.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Drive partner enablement messaging, content, webinars, and messaging in communication for updates to company GTM, new solution releases, and product launches to ensure partners are well-prepared</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Energetic passion for our partners and desire to help them become their best with our solutions</li>
<li>
5-7+ years partner/sales enablement, solution/pre-sales engineering, or training experience in the software industry</li>
<li>
Ability to think strategically and transform plan into actions, results and outcomes</li>
<li>
Strong, clear, articulate written and verbal communications a must</li>
<li>
Comfortable and confident leading online meetings and webinars that scale from few to hundreds of attendees</li>
<li>
An outcome-driven self-starter focused on propelling our Partners through the Sitecore enablement framework</li>
<li>
Very strong organizational skills and project management skills a must</li>
<li>
Ability to work collaboratively across the organization, and drive alignment with stakeholder teams and leaders</li>
<li>
Ability to dive into data and provide analytics and insights to help summarize the state of our partners in their enablement journey</li>
<li>
Experience developing learning journeys or instructional design experience a benefit</li>
<li>
Experience with digital experience or web solution software (like Sitecore) a benefit</li>
<li>
Working knowledge and use of enablement platforms (like Showpad) a plus</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in business, training, instructional design or related field</li>
<li>
Travel about 10%</li>
</ul>
