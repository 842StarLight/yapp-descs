<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Atrium Centers is a leading provider of short-term post-acute rehabilitation and long-term nursing care. We currently operate skilled nursing centers in Ohio, Michigan, Kentucky, and Wisconsin.<br>
</div>
<div>
Our reputation is defined by our employees, the caring and skilled staff members who are the foundation of our award-winning communities.<br>
<br>
We are committed to treating each individual with respect and dignity in a homelike environment. Our professional and caring staff provides exceptional services tailored to the individual needs of residents and meeting the highest industry standards.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Regional Sales position to oversee the census development of 6 facilities located throughout WI and the Upper Peninsula of Michigan. Ideal candidate will possess skilled nursing admissions experience with health care related sales background</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Regional Sales Director will support the facility admissions teams with their assigned geographical area to develop their goals and objectives. Additionally, the Regional Sales Director will conduct/participate in a sales meeting approximately every four to six (4-6) weeks (or as needed), reviewing the status of the goals and objectives.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Regional Sales Director will suggest tools to use, policies to implement and procedures to follow that would enable a facility to operate a nursing home in good standings with all state and federal guidelines, achieve census targets and develop programming to meet identified need of the residents/patients in the community.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Organize and execute orientation for new Admissions team members in conjunction with facility protocol.</li>
<li>
Analyze data, anticipate trends, develop strategies, and report on future changes to meet and exceed company/ facility targets.</li>
<li>
Prepare and submit relevant reports in a timely and accurate manner. These should include:</li>
</ul>
<div><ul>
<li>
Site visit reports.</li>
<li>
Monthly Recap reports of facility: Admissions, D/Cs, ADC, Referral Patterns, Payor Source trends, and denials by facility with reasons.</li>
<li>
Additional Reports as requested by VP Sales and Marketing.</li>
</ul></div>
<ul>
<li>
Oversee implementation of facility Annual Referral Source Satisfaction Survey for all facilities within assigned region</li>
<li>
Facilitate the recruitment of Medical Directors and /or Physician Advisors in conjunction with the Director of Operations and VP Sales and Marketing.</li>
<li>
Monitor and report on market and competitor activities, providing relevant reports, information and developing appropriate strategic activities to overcome adverse changes.</li>
<li>
Conduct coaching ride-along with facilitystaff focusing on assessing and improving the sales skills of each team member.</li>
<li>
Coordinates advertising strategy and program with facility Marketing Team Members, Director of Operations, and VP Sales and Marketing.</li>
<li>
Responsible for meeting/exceeding census goals for assigned market. Should manage referral customer relationships in accordance with the facilities business development plan.</li>
<li>
Adhere to all Federal, State and local admissions rules, regulations and policies.</li>
<li>
Census Development/Admissions.</li>
<li>
Human Resource Management (interviewing for vacant positions).</li>
<li>
Recruitment and Retention of admissions and marketing staff.</li>
<li>
Customer Service training and education.</li>
<li>
Sales Training, Coaching and Mentoring.</li>
<li>
Strategic Marketing Recommendations.</li>
<li>
Business Development.</li>
<li>
Collateral Development in conjunction with VP Sales.</li>
<li>
Special Events Coordination.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
