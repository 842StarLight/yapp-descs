<h2>Chief Revenue Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary: </b><br>Zengistics is a transportation and freight management company specializing in helping commercial shippers move their freight throughout North America. Our mission is to provide optimal customer service by utilizing our transportation software platform to ensure that our customer’s product arrives safely and on time. Zengistics is consistently growing and is looking to add a new member to the team.</p>
<p>*<br>The Chief Revenue Officer will be responsible for directly leading a team of Inside &amp; Outside Sales Directors and Representatives. You will play a pivotal role directly influencing the future of Zengistics and be responsible for new business sales, current customer base sales, marketing, and partner strategy. Our ideal candidate is hands-on, resourceful and possesses strong leadership characteristics.<br>Essential Functions:</p>
<ul><li>Create a standardized outreach for current and future customers and coordinate its implementation across sales channels, customer management, and marketing and communications</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop growth strategies with the executive team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create accountability within the company by developing appropriate metrics and coordinating compensation and promotions with these metrics</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prospect and close relationships with key target customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor the revenue pipeline and leads, adjusting as necessary to create sustainable growth</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish both short-term results and long-term strategy, including revenue forecasting</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor the strategies and processes across the revenue cycle from customer acquisition to engagement to success</li></ul>
<ul><li>This role is preferred in Austin with frequent travel. We are open to remote candidates that can travel to Austin and customer visits on a regular basis</li></ul>
<p>Minimum Qualifications:</p>
<ul><li>5-10 Years of experience in Brokerage Logistics Sales</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience growing a sales team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with the development and roll-out of sales training programs</li></ul>
<ul><li>History of leading a team with rapidly growing results</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven experience building, mentoring, motivating and leading high performance sales teams and attracting and retaining top talent</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in quarterly planning and budgeting</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Open to travel (25-50% of the time)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Creativity, attention to detail and high organizational skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong computer skills, Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li></ul>
