Job Details</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>Are you interested in becoming a Property Manager and looking for a position that will allow you the opportunity to increase your knowledge and skills to help prepare for that transition? Then check out our Property Manager in Training career at <b>South Square Townhomes</b>!</p>
<p>PRG Real Estate is currently seeking an experienced multifamily housing professional in Durham, NC, to join their team as a Manager in Training (MIT). In this unique position, you'll undertake extensive on-the-job training to aptly and fully prepare yourself for consideration as a future Property Manager. As the MIT, you'll be responsible for all activities related to apartment rentals, move-ins and lease renewals, including generating and handling traffic, leasing apartments, qualifying prospects, preparing lease documentation and completing move-in procedures.</p>
<p><b>Essential Property Manager in Training Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assists the Property Manager in the hiring, training, motivating and supervising, as needed, of all on-site staff in order to achieve operational goals of assigned community</li>
<li>Responsible for the execution of the training function for new Marketing Representatives</li>
<li>Develops thorough knowledge of the market, as well as the rental community</li>
<li>Develops knowledge of rental policies and procedures as well as daily administrative requirements of the management office</li>
<li>Implements marketing and pricing strategies for the apartment community</li>
<li>Negotiates payment dates, collects past due balances, organizes property collections, and processes eviction paperwork</li>
<li>Effectively presents the model apartment and vacant rent ready units to prospects and demonstrates its benefits and features</li>
<li>Evaluates the sales performance of other marketing representatives by personal shops and outside evaluations</li>
<li>Maintains program to generate leads through cold calling on corporations and realtors</li>
<li>Maintains resident retention program</li>
<li>Provides the Property Manager with the development and implementation of promotional programs, including creative newspaper advertising, direct mail campaigns, and resident referral programs</li>
<li>Actively works in Yardi to create residents, resident transfers, lease renewals, etc.</li>
<li>Reviews and completes reports including but not limited to: GRP, variance, mid-month, accrual, delinquency, etc.</li>
<li>Accurately closes purchase orders, processes check requests and manual payables batches</li>
</ul>
