<h2>Sr Production Operations Manager, IMDb Originals</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>Experience designing, launching, and operating video production facilities for digital media content, e.g., (1) flexible studio stage space, (2) 1080p or 4K live control room, (3) audio tracking and mixing suites, (4) SAN/Live Transmission/Archive/Asset Management system room, (5) 4K post-production and color suites, (6) green-room, hair and makeup room/talent space</li>
<li>Budget and project timeline-building experience and software expertise (e.g. Movie Magic, FastTrack Schedule)</li>
<li>Project management experience</li>
<li>Management of freelance staffers and vendors</li>
<li>Familiarity with post-production (deep hands-on expertise not required)</li>
<li>Familiarity with live streaming video production and delivery</li>

<br>
<div>The IMDb Originals team creates video programming and event experiences for IMDb customers on all platforms. Our original content is distributed daily to audiences on the IMDb website and apps (250 million monthly unique visitors), the IMDb social media channels (13 million followers) and IMDb TV. The programming lineup includes talk/interview series (The IMDb Show), explainer series (IMDbrief), event coverage (Oscars, San Diego Comic-Con), talent-driven shows (UnMade), live shows (IMDb LIVE After the Emmys), custom branded productions (The IMDb Show On Location at the Kellogg’s Café), and more. We are seeking an experienced pro to manage production operations, particularly focused on our new production studio in Santa Monica CA.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Day to day management of video production staffing, equipment, technology, budgeting, and facilities</li>
<li>Define and lead current studio expansion and future enhancement</li>
<li>Oversee operations related to video post-production and distribution</li>
<li>Manage video production that takes place outside the IMDb Studio, including on-location event coverage and projects that do not involve on-camera production</li>
<li>Create and track production budgets, invoices, vendor setup, and other financial procedures</li>
<li>Work across the IMDb/Amazon organization to ensure that production operations are closely aligned with business/creative goals. Key stakeholders include executive producers, Sales, Marketing, Editorial, and Social teams</li>
</ul>
