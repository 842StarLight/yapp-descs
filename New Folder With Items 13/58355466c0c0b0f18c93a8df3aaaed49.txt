<h2>Digital Marketing Analyst (Contracted)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>JOB SUMMARY:</p>
<p>
The Digital Marketing Coordinator (Contracted) plays a critical role in the digital &amp; marketing department, assisting in the health of digital acquisition channels [Paid Search, Affiliate Marketing, Paid Social, Programmatic Display] and data team in North America. The right candidate will be a data-driven individual whom can effectively project manage many parallel programs and provide clear insights to drive growth in customer recruitment and store traffic &amp; revenue. He/she should have a combination of analytical, strategic, creative out-of-the-box ideas, and agile thinking to capitalize on timely opportunities. The Digital Marketing Coordinator, TEMP will work closely with the Digital Media Manager and Manager of Business Insights to drive incremental sales, new customer recruitment, and media budget management across channels for optimal results in the US and Canada.</p>
<p>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<p>
ACQUISITION</p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in developing, maintaining, and distributing digital media advertising campaigns.<ul>
<li>
Manage monthly Paid Search, Affiliate, paid social, and display paid media campaigns and optimize based on business objectives.</li>
<li>
Schedule paid campaign flight dates to support marketing calendar.</li>
<li>
Help manage day-to-day relationships with external agencies &amp; vendors.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Maintain a data-driven mindset consistently reviewing metrics and finding ways to optimize our plans.<ul>
<li>
Track &amp; synthesize ongoing digital trends to ensure we are best in class (in the retail sphere, beauty sphere, and among our wider competitive set).</li>
<li>
Analyze ongoing channel performance: new customer recruitment, revenue, and RoAS</li>
<li>
Benchmark channel performance and initiatives within the Beauty industry.</li>
<li>
Responsible for weekly, monthly, and quarterly data pulls and reporting</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Manage the full-cycle creative process, from briefing, creative strategy, and trafficking for all Acquisition channels<ul>
<li>
Create and control on-going large-scale creative brief process across digital acquisition channels, including but not limited to the following ad types: Facebook Ads, Display Banners, Paid Search Ad Copy and Affiliate Paid Placement creative.</li>
<li>
Coordinate brief schedule and plan with promotion schedule, liaising with the website and email team on customer-centric copy and seamless journey to conversion.</li>
<li>
Assist in preparing the weekly Affiliate Newsletters, strategically mapping to the most relevant URL.</li>
<li>
Provide all assets to agencies in a timely manner.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
DATA AND BUSINESS INSIGHTS</p>
<ul>
<li>
Design and perform detailed analyses to help deepen understanding of customer activity across retail and web channels. Techniques may include statistical analyses, mathematical models (conversions, churn, and retention), A/B testing recommendations, programs optimization, data mining, predictive analytics, data investigation, visualization, and reporting.</li>
<li>
Effectively communicate quantitative findings (storytelling with data), make and defend meaningful conclusions and recommendations, identify and interpret business trends to key stakeholders</li>
<li>
Use your mathematical, statistical and programmatic knowledge to search, analyze, and model in order to forecast the traffic and revenue of our campaigns and the sale of our products (both core and new launches)</li>
<li>
Create visualizations to make data easily accessible across the organization. Help create and execute data dashboards, in collaboration with cross functional teams, integrating multiple data sources.</li>
<li>
Use available data sources (Google Analytics, Tableau, BI Cognos) to provide feedback to our teams on performance trends.</li>
<li>
Be creative in approaches to solving problems and managing data. Ideal candidate will look for opportunities to improve status quo and implement new ones when necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p>
KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS:</p>
<p>
New Customer Recruitment, In-store &amp; Online Traffic, CPA, Sales Growth, ROAS</p>
<p>
REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<p>
EDUCATION</p>
<ul><li>
Bachelor's Degree or working towards graduation</li></ul>
<p>
EXPERIENCE</p>
<ul><li>
Prior experience related to Digital Acquisition or Data Analytics, preferably in Beauty and/or Premium brand experience</li></ul>
<p>
SKILLS</p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong critical thinking and analytical capabilities, particularly in customer segmentation</li>
<li>
Demonstrates an immediate sense of urgency in high pressure situations, acting systematically across various digital channels</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize and manage multiple projects simultaneously in a fast-faced and deadline-driven environment</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and results oriented with a high regard for attention to detail</li>
<li>
Must be proficient in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint). Proficient with analytical tools supporting data analysis, reporting, and visualization (Tableau, Advanced Excel, Google Analytics) a plus.</li>
</ul>
