<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<div><div><div>
<div>A Support Analyst is typically an in-house Level 2 support technician who is a Subject Matter Expert in one or more application services. The engineer is responsible for ensuring the system integrity and data integrity for ongoing successful operations. The engineer will also use advanced support techniques to analyse and resolve incidents that were raised through the Incident Management process. They use defined work instructions and troubleshooting scripts to debug complex issues associated with these applications / infrastructure services. Support Engineers in Shell are also actively encouraged to seek and deploy new automation opportunities to remove manual effort and to streamline operations.<br>
<br>
Key to this specific role in support of the Shell Environmental Compliance Reporting Service application is the ability to use SQL querying and other data manipulation techniques to validate the integrity of the data as moves through this system. This role has the latitude to use a variety of data interrogation techniques to improve and automate the system. Also key is the management of custom data transport tools.<br>
<br>
The engineer will document solutions to known errors to expedite resolution of incidents and perform root cause analysis to minimize reoccurrence. The engineer typically leads testing activities against various changes to the application / infrastructure services through the Development, Test, Staging environments and performs Post-Production testing to ensure technical changes have been correctly applied.<br>
<br>

<div>
This role is in the Projects and Technology (P&amp;T) Services and Operations Management (SOM) team, which is part of the ITSO (IT Services and Operations) organization, providing dedicated support and management of key IT operational services to all P&amp;T Business units as well as delivery of IT business infrastructure projects.<br>
Shell’s Projects and Technology (P&amp;T) business is the projects execution and technology engine to help Shell achieve our purpose of ”We power progress together by providing more and cleaner energy solutions”. P&amp;T is comprised of a broad landscape of business units including many technical disciplines such as Safety &amp; Environment, Research &amp; Development, Field Development Planning, Project Execution, Engineering &amp; Technical Services, Licensing &amp; Sales to Third parties, Contracting &amp; Procurement and new Technology and Digital operations.<br>
