<h2>Director Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Committee for Children is growing, and we're looking for an experienced <b>director of product management </b>to join our team! In this role, you'll partner with the VP of product to help codify CFC's short- and long-term product vision, and you'll implement a roadmap that delivers mission-aligned, digitally inspired products and services to a rapidly changing and growing social-emotional learning (SEL) marketplace.
</p>
<p>With a keen eye toward evolving and growing the product management discipline within the organization, the director of product management will provide day-to-day operational leadership for the product team, ensuring tight linkages across product priorities, markets, and customer requirements. In this role, you'll lead and mentor a team of product managers while inspiring cross-functional collaboration and alignment.
</p>
<p>Our ideal candidate will have proven success in a product leadership role and a solid understanding of the education and e-learning marketplace.
</p>
<p>This year, we're celebrating our 40th anniversary by setting an ambitious goal for the future: to positively transform the social-emotional well-being of 100 million children annually by 2028. To achieve this, CFC is looking for an exceptional leader to join our product team and enable our vision of “safe children thriving in a peaceful world.”</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities Include</b></p>
<p>Directing CFC's product strategy and roadmap
</p>
<ul>
<li>In collaboration with the VP of product, defining the product vision and strategy</li>
<li>
Building and incorporating education market and competitive understanding to ensure vision is forward-leaning, differentiated, and effective in delivering desired outcomes
</li>
<li>Building a product roadmap and strategy for developing innovative products and features while prioritizing on-time product delivery</li>
</ul>
<p>Directing product delivery and execution
</p>
<ul>
<li>Creating a framework to define prioritization, roadmap planning, and success metrics
</li>
<li>Working with product managers and scrum masters to translate the product vision into a technically feasible plan for execution, managing for capabilities, risks, and target market needs and constraints
</li>
<li>Continuing to develop and manage a scalable design and development process using Agile/Scrum methodologies
</li>
<li>Ensuring effective staffing, processes, and culture to deliver innovative digital and hybrid products with the proper balance of time, cost, and capability
</li>
<li>Developing and prioritizing features and add-on services that will increase the impact on student's SEL skills, knowledge, and goals; increase CFC's reach by attracting new clients and expanding existing implementations; increase the quality of CFC programs; and provide best-in-class implementation support to increase impact</li>
</ul>
<p>Developing effective working relationships<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Developing and leading a high-performing product team by aligning the team's goals with the organization's strategies</li>
<li>
Managing employee development, reward, and coaching activities to generate a shared understanding of the product management function, building on best practices and fostering creativity and innovation</li>
<li>
Leading and developing a diverse team of professionals, creating an atmosphere that unleashes creativity and maximizes impact
</li>
<li>Coordinating closely with the other directors</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A genuine passion for and intensely motivated by the mission, vision, and goal of positively affecting 100 million children each year</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree; advanced degree in education or MBA preferred</li>
<li>
3+ years of experience as a manager of product management in a B2B organization, preferably SAAS.</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience in leading and developing product management teams</li>
<li>
Experience managing multiple products in development across a single portfolio</li>
<li>
Demonstrated proficiency working with sales and marketing leaders to bring clarity to the marketplace on offerings, value, and vision.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated technical fluency and effectiveness in working closely with engineers and data scientists</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to develop and lead cross-functional teams, combined with strong stakeholder management, project management, and leadership skills</li>
<li>
Experience with agile development methodologies like Scrum</li>
<li>
Experience building new category offerings is a plus, and education or e-learning market experience is strongly desired</li>
<li>
Sound judgment paired with the ability to act independently and a willingness to resolve issues and problems in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Strategic planning skills, with experience leading a team through transformation</li>
<li>
Passion for coaching and developing high-performing teams</li>
<li>
Some travel required (up to 10%)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About Committee for Children</b></p>
<p>Formed in 1979, Committee for Children is on a mission to ensure that children everywhere can thrive. We are trailblazers, best known for our innovative social-emotional learning programs that help 15 million children each year learn the skills they need to flourish in school and in society. By lifting up children today, we're helping them create a safe and positive society for the future.</p>
<p>We have excellent employee tenure because we take great care of our most valued assets—our clients and employees. This role will offer some amazing opportunities to add value, contribute, and make a difference while enjoying new challenges, a collaborative and diverse team, professional development opportunities, and work/life balance.</p>
