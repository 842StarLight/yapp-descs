<h2>Full-time Teller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Full-time Teller</b></div>
<div><b>Swansea
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>GENERAL SUMMARY OF RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<div>Serve as ambassadors for the Company. Responsible for developing, expanding and retaining customer relationships. Responsible for providing a high quality of customer service in a friendly, professional manner. Responsible for accepting and processing customer transactions in a prompt, courteous and efficient manner.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>DESCRIPTION OF PRIMARY DUTIES:</b></div>
<div><b>SERVICE:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Greets customers and establishes a quality service environment by displaying a friendly and courteous attitude and using the customerâ€™s name at least once during the conversation.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Services customers by accepting and processing transactions with accuracy within policy guidelines i.e. deposits, withdrawals, payments, check cashing, issuing negotiable, etc.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Explains transaction policies and procedures to customers. Solicits assistance from supervisor as needed. Obtain supervisory approval for transactions outside authorized limit. Notifies management of suspicious and questionable transactions.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Refers large withdraws and account closures to management and/or sales staff for retention purposes.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Actively seeks out and maintains basic product knowledge of all banking products. This knowledge may be obtained through training courses, branch meetings, corporate communications, etc.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Safeguard assigned cash and balances cash drawer as outlined in teller balancing procedures.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Places hold on funds when appropriate and advises customer of funds availability.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Maintains dual control and adheres to all security policies and procedures as all times.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Performs daily branch operational balancing procedures, including but not limited to the balancing of individual teller drawer.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>TM balancing and servicing or Vault Teller responsibilities, if needed.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>If applicable, allows entry of safe deposit box holders.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Maintains professional appearance and conduct within the guidelines set forth in the Employee Handbook.</div>
</li>
</ul>
<div><b>SALES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Identifies sales opportunities and makes referrals to appropriate sales staff.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Reviews referrals goals on a frequent basis and formulates strategy for attainment of goals. Implements and revises individual sales strategy as needed to reach goals.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Proactively cross-sells other bank products and refer customers to appropriate departments when necessary.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Meet sales goals provided by management. Reviews sales/referral goals on a frequent basis and formulates strategy for attainment of goals. Implements and revises individual sales strategy as needed to reach goals.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Encourages the use of alternate delivery systems such as ATM, Debit Card, Telephone and Online Banking.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Participates in all sales meetings.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>May be required to work in other First Mid banking centers.</div>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>EDUCATION/TRAINING REQUIREMENTS</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>High school diploma required.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Minimum of six months cash management experience and/or training required; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Proficient in usage of Microsoft Office software, especially Excel, Outlook and Word.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Previous customer service experience of at least six months preferred.</div>
</li>
</ul>
