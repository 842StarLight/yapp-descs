<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>When most people say "work hard, play hard," they don't mean at the same time. <b>But we're not most people. </b>We're a little more awesome than that, and we're willing to bet that you are too.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>About the job:
</b></p>
<p>Our team is maniacally focused on powering healthy horses and happy riders. At SmartPak, the Marketing Coordinator is an integral member of the Brand &amp; Customer Experience team (BCET). In this role, you’re responsible for helping riders discover tried &amp; true best-sellers, the latest and greatest new supplements &amp; gear, and the steals and deals they’ll only find at SmartPak. You'll represent SmartPak’s products and services to customers by supporting the development and implementation of marketing and advertising campaigns; aiding in the tracking sales data; helping maintain promotional materials; and assisting with the planning of meetings and customer events.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you’ll do (big-picture responsibilities):
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support the development and distribution of marketing and sales materials. Assist in production of emails, banners, advertising, catalogs, or other promotional materials.
</li>
<li>Assistance with basic analysis and reporting of marketing initiatives and sales results
</li>
<li>Maintain tracking systems and reporting for marketing activities
</li>
<li>Coordinate promotional activities for new products/services
</li>
<li>Keep promotional materials ready by coordinating requirements with creative department; inventorying assets; placing orders; verifying receipt
</li>
<li>Accomplish organization goals by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and different requests and exploring opportunities to add value to job accomplishments
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you’ll also do (day-to-day):
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Gain expertise of SmartPaks product lines and policies
</li>
<li>Communicate ideas and messages cross-departmentally
</li>
<li>Work under deadline pressure and extra hours if needed on assignments
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>How you’ve done it before (experience + qualifications):
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree (in marketing or related field preferred)
</li>
<li>Equine background preferred
</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office programs
</li>
<li>Comfortable working independently as well as within a group
</li>
<li>Highly organized with excellent time management skills
</li>
<li>Self-motivated and able to work multiple projects simultaneously and thrive in a fast-paced environment
</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills
</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with excellent attention to detail
</li>
<li>Ability to occasionally travel (including weekend days) as needed for the company
</li>
<li>Ability to lift up to 50 pounds regularly
</li>
</ul>
