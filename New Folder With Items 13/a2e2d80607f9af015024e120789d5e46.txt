<p><b>Systems Support Technician</b></p>
<br>
<p>Family Express is seeking a candidate to provide tier 1, 2 and 3 support for our the company’s system wide hardware and software. Specific duties include the technical support of computers, device peripherals, networks, point of sale devices and telecommunications. Excellent interpersonal and communication skills as well as collaboration, teamwork and best practices are essential elements for success. Family Express’ vision is to be the finest small chain of convenience stores and fueling centers in the world. We seek and deploy cutting edge technologies that assist in making our company customer friendly, efficient and viable. Examples are our Family Express loyalty program (FE Perks), cloud computing, virtualization, IPADs, smart phones and an FE mobile application.</p>
