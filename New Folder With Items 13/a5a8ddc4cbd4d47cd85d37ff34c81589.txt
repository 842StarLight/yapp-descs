<h2>Entry Level Auditor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Entry Level Auditor will perform cost reconciliations on tangible business personal property, conduct on-site inspections, and perform revenue reconciliations. TMA is looking for interested candidates with accounting education and/or experience to train in this specialized area of business taxation in our Charlotte, NC office. TMA audit work gives candidates the opportunity to travel and experience multiple business types.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>Duties/Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Auditors perform full cost reconciliations on tangible personal property tax listings filed by businesses with various state and local tax departments.</li>
<li>This analysis will include reviewing chart of accounts, trial balances, general ledgers, depreciation schedules, profit and loss statements, income statements, lease agreements and a site visit of the facility.</li>
<li>Conduct interviews with the taxpayer or agent to determine that all appropriate information has been provided.</li>
<li>Conduct exit interview with the taxpayer or agent as to the results of the review.</li>
<li>Communicate with tax jurisdiction personnel on a regular basis.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<ul>
<li>Candidates must have at least nine (9) hours of accounting.</li>
<li>Accounting or Business Administration degrees preferred but not required.</li>
<li>Technical skills required: intermediate Microsoft Word and Excel software.</li>
<li>Traveling up to two (2) weeks a month when required.</li>
<li>Candidate must demonstrate excellent communication skills along with a professional presentation.</li>
<li>Pass State administered exams to obtain Professional designation.</li>
</ul>
