<h2>Digital Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>REPORTING RELATIONSHIPS<br>The role reports to the Global Marketing Director and manages two FTEs in the Commercial Marketing department.</p>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<p>Responsible for overseeing the implementation of integrated marketing campaigns and communications that support the company brand across all markets and lines of business.</p>
<p>Leads the end-to-end management of deliverables from brief writing, concept review, execution, and post launch analysis.</p>
<p>Plans and executes all digital marketing, including SEO/SEM, marketing database, email, social media and display advertising campaigns.</p>
<p>Delivers campaigns that gain internal partner buy-in and drive results at the organizational level and tactically for individual product lines.</p>
<p>Analyzes and measures digital and traditional campaign performance and ROI, leveraging analysis to identify optimization opportunities including design, content, copy, channel, and call-to-action.</p>
<p>Drives web traffic and generate new leads, while nurturing existing leads towards conversion.</p>
<p>Performs ad hoc copywriting, prototyping, and functional wireframing.</p>
<p>Oversees event, media and exhibition planning and execution.</p>
<p>Has strong understanding of search engine concepts, web traffic, and SEO tracking, tagging, and ranking.</p>
<p>Defines technology requirements working with IT team.</p>
<p>Manages project demand, prioritization and workflow to ensure team is focused on highest priority work.</p>
<p>Works closely with Sales to identify needs and develop content and approaches for demand generation and sales enablement.</p>
<p>Provides support to all regions in tailoring global strategies to local markets and sharing best practices with regional teams.</p>
<p>Develops a positive working relationship with key internal stakeholders and a deep understanding of business goals and objectives.</p>
<p>Position Requirements:<br>REQUIREMENTS</p>
<p>Strong people and project management skills that translate into excellence in execution, aligning key stakeholders, collaboration with cross-functional partners.</p>
<p>Highly organized, detailed oriented with the ability to swiftly manage timelines, teams, executive management and budgets.</p>
<p>Self-starter with the ability to work independently, both from a strategic and executionary standpoint.</p>
<p>Able to create capabilities that do not currently exist, with the ability to work closely with other business stakeholders on digital transformation.</p>
<p>Excellent organizational and project management skills, including the ability to plan, prioritize and execute multiple initiatives/deadlines autonomously and shift priorities as necessary.</p>
<p>Ability to think laterally and creatively and problem-solve while juggling multiple tasks.</p>
<p>Experience working with a CMS.</p>
<p>Experience managing events, trade exhibits and planning and executing media strategy.</p>
<p>Comfortable using Google Analytics and other marketing technologies.</p>
<p>Experience testing and troubleshooting marketing technology applications.</p>
<p>Previous international business experience.</p>
<p>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</p>
<p>5 – 7 years of experience leading digital marketing strategy in a B2B environment.</p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree in a relevant discipline such as Marketing, Communications, Computer Science, Data Analytics or Statistics.</p>
<p>Travel domestically and/or abroad required (approximately 10% – 20%).</p>
