<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS:</b></h3>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p><b>Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED Certificate <i>preferred.</i>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1 year of commercial driving experience <i>preferred.</i>
</li>
<li>Previous experience in hospitality, sales, marketing, promotions, and/or public relations is <i>preferred</i>.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Additional Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills with demonstrated patience, tact, and respect.</li>
<li>Must possess a valid California Class A and/or B License with a "P" passenger endorsement and air brake endorsement.</li>
<li>Driving record must have less than 50% of the permissible points allowed per California DMV.</li>
<li>Must apply for, receive, and maintain a Gaming License from the Tribal Gaming Agency.</li>
</ul>
