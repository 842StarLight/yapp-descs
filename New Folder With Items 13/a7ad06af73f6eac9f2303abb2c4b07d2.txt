<h2>Fixed Income, RIA Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h2>
<br>
<div><b>
Job Summary:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Under limited supervision, uses specialized knowledge and skills obtained through education and experience to market taxable and tax free fixed income products to financial advisors and clients via printed publication, electronic media and personal presentation. Guidance is provided to perform varied, complex, activities that require evaluation, originality and ingenuity in making moderately complex decisions. Regular contact with financial advisors and customers is required.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li> Coordinates with members of the fixed income trading desk to generate timely marketing materials in electronic format for fixed income products and services targeted at financial advisors and investors.</li>
<li> Designs, develops and manages marketing strategies</li>
<li> Responds to inquiries from Financial Advisors about the department’s investment philosophies, products and services.</li>
<li> Coordinates schedules and conducts seminars and conferences.</li>
<li> Oversees the distribution of marketing literature.</li>
<li> Prepares financial information reports.</li>
<li> Conducts basic marketing research and compiles results for projects, as assigned.</li>
<li> Maintains records and files on research projects.</li>
<li> May use computer modeling techniques.</li>
<li> Provides training in fixed income marketing area.</li>
<li> Performs other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
