<h2>Product Owner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Product Owner is responsible for developing new software product concepts, designs, and/or enhancements to existing product design that reflect market and user requirements. Conducts research and develops a good understanding of the customer's needs, new technologies, trends in the market, and competitors. Develops requirements documents, functional specifications, and mockups to clearly illustrate product ideas and concepts. Develops business plan for products and participates in all phases of the product development lifecycle, including analysis, design, testing, and integration of products as well as the introduction of products to the market.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a voice the customers on new or existing products.</li>
<li>
Highlight product features in a manner clearly understandable to the development team.</li>
<li>
Manage and develop product backlog and stories backlog for implementation.</li>
<li>
Attend sprint planning and demo meetings to give feedback on sprint achievement, as well as to scrum team.</li>
<li>
Monitor the progress of product development and plan for product release.</li>
<li>
Provide reports to update management and stakeholders on product development operations.</li>
<li>
Analyze customer opinions and feedback to determine features or components that will add good value to a product.</li>
<li>
Assess product value stream to identify areas that require improvements.</li>
<li>
Develop and implement action plans for building and releasing product features which add value to end users and increase company returns.</li>
<li>
Serve as technical product expert for sales, client support and other departments, and support the new release process.</li>
</ul>
