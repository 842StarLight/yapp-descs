<p><b>
CORPORATE COMMUNICATION ASSOCIATE/CREATIVE SERVICES
</b></p>
<p>We seek a Graphic Designer to join our Marketing &amp; Communication team in our Evanston, IL office. The Graphic Designer drives creative ideation and production for the firms biggest and most innovative global corporate communication initiatives.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
With a digital first point-of-view, this individual will lead the development of—stunning, elegant, forward-thinking design solutions across internal and external channels and so it’s essential you possess a portfolio loaded with awesome video, website, presentation, social, and event based creative</li>
<li>
We are <b>not</b> looking for a production-orientated designer although hands-on experience through every stage of a project’s lifecycle is essential</li>
<li>
We seek an inspired and motivated creative thinker who can generate and nurture fresh and innovative agency grade ideas from conception, through development, to implementation</li>
<li>
Do we want your world-class creative? Absolutely! But we also want your expertise and voice as a brand guideline’s advocate and champion of design best practices</li>
<li>
At ZS, collaboration is central to success, so you’ll need to partner and communicate with global stakeholders on a daily basis, from corporate communication and recruiting strategists to digital wizards, to business leaders and event orchestrators</li>
<li>
The Marketing &amp; Communication team is a high energy, fast paced, deadline driven environment and your ability to create and present high-quality ZS branded ideas quickly across global time zones will be critical</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A successful career at a leading global B2B or B2C creative agency or corporate brand in a graphic design role – 2-3 years’ experience required</li>
<li>
A portfolio bursting with world-class creative
</li>
<li>Exceptional conceptual thinking – we want designers with original thoughts who can take complex creative briefs and come up with fresh, innovative, and inspired digital and print design solutions that wow internal and external audiences</li>
<li>
Video production awesomeness and motion graphics magic – we need someone who can independently shoot, edit, and produce corporate communication campaign and executive leadership videos</li>
<li>
Your tool box should be stacked with Adobe Creative Cloud and Microsoft Office Apps – Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects, Premiere, Word, and PowerPoint are your preferred tools of choice</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills with the ability to clearly and concisely articulate ideas to creative leaders, marcomm collaborators, and business stakeholders at all levels of the organization</li>
<li>
A strong understanding of digital and print design best practices with a focus on video, web, social, presentation, and event experiential design</li>
<li>
The ability to work autonomously and as part of a team – you’ll need to do both at ZS, we need someone who can run with minimal direction as well someone who can collaborate across global time-zones on design projects</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience in a related field</li>
<li>
Exceptional written and oral English language skills</li>
</ul>
