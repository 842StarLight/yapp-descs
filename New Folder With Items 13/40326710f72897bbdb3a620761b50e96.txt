<p><b>Services Sales Director</b></p>
<p>
The Services Sales Director plays the critical role for setting customers up for success by prescriptively shaping and closing proposals for delivery services that ensure a customer extracts value from their Apttus software investments.<br>
<br>
The Services Sales Director serves as a trusted advisor to prospective Apttus customers by partnering with the Apttus software sales Account Executive, the Apttus Deal Desk, Professional Services team, Education Services team, Managed Services team and the customer to develop the optimal proposal.<br>
<br>
The Services Sales Director conveys the value proposition of implementation and post-implementation service offerings and then helps to close those opportunities collaboratively.<br>
<br>
For such engagements, he/she will maintain key customer relationships, ensure tight stakeholder alignment, offer proactive and prescriptive advice, all with the goal of delivering outstanding customer success. Responsibilities: Achieve ACV, services bookings, services billings and services margin targets by quarter.<br>
<br>
Lead the development of customer-specific implementation and post-implementation delivery services proposals by engaging with subject matter experts across the organization to gain consensus on the best solution for the customer. Align closely with the Apttus Software Sales teams to own pre-sales activities such as pitching, scoping, SOW development and closing Delivery services opportunities.<br>
<br>
Provide the Professional Services team with Subject Matter Expertise related to the proposed solution and customers’ needs in order to ensure successful services delivery. Your Impact, as Services Sales Director, you: Convey business value propositions of service offerings in an effective, creative manner. Are internally and externally recognized as business contributor, industry or product specialist.<br>
<br>
Have high level of knowledge of relevant technologies, emerging business/ industry trends, competitive technical direction and product direction. Develop new best practices to position, scope, price, and close services opportunities. Determine methodologies and procedures on new assignments based on general direction from senior management. Act as an advisor on complex issues and projects, exercising discretion and latitude in reaching project goals.</p>
