<p>
The Solution Architect supporting the Global Cloud Alliances, is a partner focused technical business development professional, who provides support, enablement, and guidance to CrowdStrike's Cloud Alliances team. You will be expected to take ownership of the technical design and management of strategic alliance based initiatives and programs relating to technical architecture, solutions delivery, partner training and enablement as well as working internally to represent the needs of the Sales organization. The ideal candidate will have a good mix of deep technical knowledge and business acumen in the security market place. In this position you will be leading the development and execution of technology strategies and solutions integrations to meet strategic corporate and partner needs, present and future.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Key Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
Technical leader and trusted adviser in the area of cloud computing for our partners</li>
<li>
Own the technical relationship and deliverables for one of the global system integrators</li>
<li>
Collaborate with partner and internal technical SMEs to design the proper pathways to success and go to market strategies for cloud</li>
<li>
Enable corresponding territory field SAs and SEs at both CrowdStrike and Cloud providers to position, promote and assist with demonstration environments to secure and migrate cloud workloads.</li>
<li>
Assist our alliance partners in defining the approach, tools, and end state architecture as they migrate customers to cloud platforms such as AWS, GCP, and Azure.</li>
<li>
Design and Deploy an NFR program for cloud providers to assist Cloud Solution Architects and Cloud Professional Services teams to complete CrowdStrike training and achieve certifications</li>
<li>
Shadow in live deployments that include installations, troubleshooting, and training of CrowdStrike's products running in cloud compute environments (e.g. AWS, GCP, Azure))</li>
<li>
Interface between the Sales organization and technical staff to resolve outstanding issues and drive new product adoption within cloud workloads.</li>
<li>
Provide reporting and feedback on partner field activities to Sales, Product Management and Engineering</li>
<li>
Participate in regional cloud alliances events such as security conferences, lunch &amp; learns, and alliance sponsored events.</li>
</ul>
