<div>CCB Chase Merchant Services is undertaking an expansion of its international capabilities and alternative methods of payments for the product line. As a technology platform owner you will be responsible for developing cloud based solutions to process payments for the Chase Merchant Services line of business in a global model. As the platform owner you will drive a multi-year strategy partnering with product management to build capabilities and deprecate older assets. This role will require building partnerships across the organization to provide client access and servicing. Finally, you will need the ability to be client facing for large clients to support sales and on-boarding activities to the solution.<br>
