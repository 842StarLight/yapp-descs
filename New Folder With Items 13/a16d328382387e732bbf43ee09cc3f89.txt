<p><b><i>Job Summary:
</i></b></p>
<p>Reporting to the business line head, the Business Analytics and Data Product Management Director leads the Product Analytics team, who supports key functions across the business, including Marketing, Digital and Sales organizations. He/she will be responsible for the design, implementation and ongoing optimization of all central analytics initiatives, capabilities and services to support the Consumer Bank’s businesses. This role will act as a central point for all requirements and development of analytics, data science and corresponding data management services and will be responsible for partnering with enterprise technology to develop the corresponding platforms.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Management or Supervision:</i></b> Yes</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
Major Responsibilities:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Responsible for the development and management of strategic goals and objectives for the Business Analytics &amp; Data team in alignment with the enterprise.</p></li>
<li><p>
Bridges gap between business, data science and technology by brainstorming ideas with business, and translating business needs into analyses.</p></li>
<li><p>
Share current and historical activities, impacts and other product insights which can be used to develop larger scale analyses which resonate across multiple lines of business</p></li>
<li><p>
Partners with Digital team to improve bank’s site and mobile experiences, and helps to drive product adoption via analyses on customer experiences and customer journeys.</p></li>
<li><p>
Partners with Marketing team to support analysis of measuring and optimizing marketing investments and tactics across multiple channels. Has experience applying multivariate methodologies across multiple marketing and sales channels.</p></li>
<li><p>
Partners with Data Science, Data Platforms, Reporting and Visualization teams to deliver insights to the business.</p></li>
<li><p>
Drives excellent practices and standards for establishing KPIs, OKRs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Partners with, guide and educate businesses on self-service data and analytics, and helps to create a data-savvy culture.
</p></li>
<li><p>Leads data roadmap and model discussions and development with Enterprise Architecture and Data Engineering teams
</p></li>
<li><p>Demonstrates thorough understanding of overall data models, data relationships, mapping lineage and business rules.</p></li>
<li><p>
Partners in the development of Data Management standards and capabilities.</p></li>
<li><p>
Actively participates in the research, discovery and usage of emerging technologies.
</p></li>
<li><p>Develops and maintains effective relationships to coordinate the delivery of all data-related services to clients.<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
