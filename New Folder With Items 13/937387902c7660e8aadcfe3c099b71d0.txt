<h2>Restaurant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Restaurant Manager - Beer Hall</b></div>
<div>
<br>
A Restaurant Manager in an energetic person who leads by example, sets the pace, and provides assistance where needed and contributes to building a positive team spirit.</div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work a flexible schedule based on the store's needs; must be available nights, weekends and holidays, varied hours/days, opening and closing of the store.</li>
<li>Ensures high customer-service standards.</li>
<li>Standard schedule is 45+ hour work week</li>
<li>Knowledge of equipment and software including, but not limited to: phone systems, printers/fax machines, spreadsheets, word processing, email, inventory programs, Point of Sale (POS) programs, Internet applications, forums, and social media sites.</li>
<li>Must have a strong passion for food and beer</li>
<li>The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 55 pounds.</li>
<li>3+ years minimum of restaurant management experience</li>
<li>Cicerone or Beer Server Certification a plus</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Surly started with a simple motto, “Its all about the BEER.” Well, in the Surly Beer Hall, its’s also about the food. The Restaurant Manager is responsible for managing the day-to-day execution of the Beer Hall, supporting the staff, and making the guest experience a valuable one. This position reports to the General Manager. General duties include:</div>
<ul>
<li>Ensures that each guest receives outstanding service by providing a friendly, clean, and organized environment.</li>
<li>Maintains outstanding standards, solid product knowledge (both of food and beer), and all other components of customer service.</li>
<li>Opening and Closing shifts, depending on semi rotating schedule.</li>
<li>Works with the marketing department for marketing needs</li>
<li>Assessing monthly inventory and submitting to accounting</li>
<li>Assists with social media marketing and promotional events.</li>
<li>Manages inventory levels, using inventory counts to maintain accuracy on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Supervises/advises full and part time staff under the direction of the General Manager.</li>
<li>Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with the organization's policies and applicable laws.</li>
<li>Balances registers according to accounting and security procedures.</li>
<li>Regularly monitors email for critical business information and responds in a timely fashion.</li>
<li>Brings potentially systematic problems that require immediate action to the attention of the General Manager</li>
<li>Sets the pace on the restaurant floor. Creates a positive team environment within the Beer Hall.</li>
<li>Schedules and maintains staffing plans.</li>
<li>Host continued training sessions with staff in regard to furthering beer and food knowledge.</li>
<li>Any other responsibilities as assigned by General Manager.</li>
</ul>
