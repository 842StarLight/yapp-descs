<h2>Machine Operator - Portland, OR</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div>
<p>Job ID: 159567</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
CPM, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Operates plant machinery as directed and specified while adhering to all rules and regulations mandated by safety policies and procedures to produce a superior product.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Read job specifications to determine machine adjustments and material requirements</li>
<li>
Sets stops or guides to specific lengths as indicated by scale, rule or templates</li>
<li>
Observes machine operation to detect product defects or machine malfunction</li>
<li>
Review product for inconsistencies or abnormal appearance assuring product meets quality requirements</li>
<li>
Performs routine machine maintenance</li>
<li>
Assist other operators to set up machines and any other necessary tasks to adhere to deadlines and completion of product</li>
<li>
Make sure all housekeeping is maintained in a clear and orderly fashion in assigned area</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities related to the nature of the job may be assigned on a temporary or permanent basis as needed</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skills and/or abilities required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable qualified individuals with disabilities to perform the essential duties of the job.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements/Education/Experience</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma or higher; or at least three years of related manufacturing experience in trades specific to job requirements; or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>
Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instruction, and procedure manuals</li>
<li>
Ability to be a self-starter and work independently. Proven success in leading a team</li>
<li>
Ability to add, subtract multiply and divide into all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions and decimals</li>
<li>
Have the ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out instructions furnished in written, oral or diagram form</li>
<li>
Ability to deal with problems involving several concrete variables in standardized situations</li>
<li>
Must encompass computer literacy</li>
<li>
Maintain a high level of accuracy in all aspects of work assignments</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Physical Environment</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
While performing the duties of this job the employee will be required to frequently stand on their feet for extended periods of time</li>
<li>
Must have the ability to lift and/or move up to 100 lbs</li>
<li>
Will be performing work in differing physical environments from heat to cold depending on weather</li>
<li>
Able to work a flexible schedule</li>
</ul>
