<p><b>Job Overview</b></p>
<p>
The Inside Sales Manager will have experience in overseeing and supporting an inside sales specialist team.<br>
This individual will monitor sales metrics and manage the entire sales administration process. They will collaborate with various professionals, making excellent communication skills necessary. They must be able to work in a team environment, lead and motivate the inside sales team to achieve specific goals.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
All aspects of management for the inside sales team, ensuring KPIs performance against targets.</li>
<li>
Holding regular pipeline review meetings with the team.</li>
<li>
Working on scripts with the team, making ongoing amendments as necessary.</li>
<li>
Leading and coaching a team of inside sales specialist.</li>
<li>
Set and track sales targets for the team</li>
<li>
Suggest and implement improvements in the sales administration process</li>
<li>
Coordinate department projects to meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Report on sales metrics and suggest improvements</li>
<li>
Prepare monthly, quarterly and annual sales forecasts</li>
<li>
Build an open-communication environment for your team</li>
<li>
Generating qualified leads for the organization and passing to business development team.</li>
<li>
Identifying opportunities for upselling within existing customers.</li>
<li>
Updating the CRM on a daily basis ensuring it’s up to date and relevant.</li>
<li>
Generating high quality appointments for the field sales team.</li>
<li>
Identifying target contacts within potential customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration or relevant field</li>
<li>
Proven work experience as an Inside Sales Manager</li>
<li>
Hands on experience with CRM software and MS Excel</li>
<li>
In-depth understanding of the sales administration process</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal and team management skills</li>
<li>
Strong analytical and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Numerical abilities and problem-solving attitude</li>
<li>
Previous experience managing an inside sales team</li>
<li>
Excellent people management skills and an ability to drive sales results</li>
<li>
Strong systems and CRM skills, ideally Salesforce</li>
</ul>
