<h2>Technical Sales Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Roxtec (www.roxtec.com) is the world’s largest manufacturer of modular based cable entry sealing systems. A market leader in creativity and design, Roxtec manufactures customized sealing solutions for cable and pipe penetrations, which raise the quality, safety and flexibility in working environments.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Roxtec has an immediate opening for a <b>Technical &amp; Commercial Support Representative</b> in our Tulsa headquarters. The Technical &amp; Commercial Support Representative is the primary contact, responsible for providing technical support of Roxtec products and applications to Customers and Field Sales Representatives across the US and Canada.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul><li> Provide technical support for engineered products and their applications</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrate a broad scope of product knowledge to ensure appropriate technical solution is applied</li></ul>
<ul><li> Make Product recommendations based on customer application requirements</li></ul>
<ul><li> Review and confirm customer provided specifications to ensure best product fit for application</li></ul>
<ul><li> Consult with new customers to establish industry requirements and determine best product fit</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborate with design and engineering team to create custom solutions to resolve complex technical issues that fall outside the scope of standard products.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with sales team to ensure best possible service: right product, on time, on budget, installed correctly.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop and maintain strong customer relationships across the sales pyramid at every point of the sales process.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage CRM and ERP systems to execute inquiries for RFQ’s, products sourcing, services and solutions, price discrepancies, order tracking and delivery schedules.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proactively communicate customer needs across departments to ensure positive customer relations</li></ul>
<ul><li> Attend training, sales meetings, site visits, and trade shows as necessary – 10-15% overnight travel</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Skills</b></p>
<ul><li> 2+ years inside sales or customer service experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience working with customers at all stages of the sales process from design to installation.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with Citrix based ERP platforms and CRM functions, specifically SugarCRM</li></ul>
<ul><li> Intermediate knowledge of Microsoft Excel</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent ability to multi-task in a high volume, fast-paced, dynamic work environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must be customer-focused, detail oriented, and solution-minded</li></ul>
<ul><li> Good organization and time management skills required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Capable of making independent decisions and possess the ability to work with minimal supervision</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li> High school degree or equivalent</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent communication skills both verbal and written</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong mathematical/technical/mechanical aptitude</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledgeable in the Microsoft Suite of products (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Outlook)</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Demands</b></p>
<ul><li> Regularly required to see, talk and hear.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Frequently required to sit and use hands along with fingers, to handle or feel; lift and/or move up to 50 pounds.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Occasionally required to stand, walk, reach with hands and arms, climb or balance, and stoop, kneel, crouch or crawl.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision.</li></ul>
<br>
