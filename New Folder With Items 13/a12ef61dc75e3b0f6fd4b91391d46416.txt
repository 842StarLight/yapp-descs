<h2>Technical Writer / Illustrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Fairbanks Morse, an EnPro company, is seeking a Technical Writer to join our Advertising, Marketing, and Public Relations team out of our Houston service center. The Technical Writer will mainly be responsible for preparing technical manuals, maintenance standards, service information letters, and other documentation to meet customer deliverable requirements. Additionally, the Technical Writer will review technical information included in engineering drawings and other documents and translate that into clear language understandable to customers of varying knowledge levels. The Technical Writer will prepare both written copy and clear illustrations and drawings to complement the written text, using 2D and 3D illustration software.<br>
<br>
The Technical Writer will be part of a world leader in engine technology that provides propulsion to the US Navy, US Coast Guard, and provides emergency power for nuclear power plants. Our engines weigh up to 250 tons and provide up to 33,500 horsepower to meet our customers' needs.<br>
<br>
Note: 10% travel will be required. The Technical Writer position requires access or potential access to Unclassified Naval Nuclear Propulsion Information, which is Not Releasable to Foreign Nationals (NOFORN) data. Therefore, to meet NOFORN requirement, you will have to provide proof of US Citizenship and attest that you do not have dual citizenship. Acceptable proof of US Citizenship is a US Passport, US Birth Certificate or Naturalization Certificate.<br>
<b>
</b><br>
<b>Responsibilities of the Technical Writer</b>:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare written copy and clear illustrations and drawings for technical manuals</li>
<li>
Work to meet contract commitments as scheduled, including validation activities</li>
<li>
Develop repair parts lists and illustrations using bills of material and engineering drawings</li>
<li>
Manage vendors that are providing data development resources</li>
<li>
Review specifications as they relate to technical manual deliverables and estimate effort required</li>
<li>
Read specifications and other contract documents and determine the types of technical documentation required</li>
<li>
Interact with FM Program Management, Engineering, Training, and Sales &amp; Marketing groups and determine the scope and formatting necessary</li>
<li>
Develop data for the preparation of operation and maintenance (O&amp;M) technical manuals, maintenance standards, service information letters (SILs) and other technical documents</li>
<li>
Draft text material as required for new, original technical documentation and prepare updates and revisions to existing or previously written technical documentation</li>
<li>
Prepare clear technical illustrations and graphic depictions to support the written text</li>
<li>
Prepare, organize, and deliver preliminary deliverable documentation; adjudicate and incorporate customer comments and corrections for the final deliverable documentation</li>
</ul>
