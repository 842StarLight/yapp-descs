<p><b>About Us</b><br>Conversion Path is a fast-growing digital marketing agency with a passion for helping companies grow profitable sales through specialization and innovation. Our niche is in online customer acquisition for mid to large-sized ecommerce brands like Justice, TUMI, Homage, and more. Our agency is a Google Premier Partner that’s been recognized for our leadership and innovation in the industry.</p>
<p>We’re an entrepreneurial firm in the digital advertising and ecommerce space and have achieved 50% growth in the last year. We’re looking to expand significantly over the next 18 months, so now is the time to become a pivotal piece in our business.</p>
<p>We are hiring a digital marketing analyst in our Columbus, Ohio office. We don’t require prior industry experience for candidates interested in this role. We only require a candidate to be eager to learn, develop, and become a foundational member of this exciting company in the ecommerce space.</p>
<p><b>Job Details</b><br>The digital marketing analyst plays a critical role in the day-to-day operations of the company, working on our quickly growing PPC marketing team, managing client accounts through Google, Bing and Facebook. This position requires a high level of analytical skill, attention to detail, and the ability to juggle multiple responsibilities and projects at once while meeting deadlines. This role also requires strong communication skills and the ability to discuss performance and strategy details with clients.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Managing and maintaining client budget, ROI, and sales goals</li>
<li>Working closely with Sr. Analysts to manage client accounts, create reports, and execute projects to grow accounts</li>
<li>Managing promotional calendars and processes for clients</li>
<li>Using Excel and Google Sheets for reporting and analyses for clients</li>
<li>Communicating with team members and management on account performance, projects, etc.</li>
<li>Providing support to the Sr. Analysts and Managers through the completion of ad hoc projects and requests</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>

<li>Strong writing and proofreading skills</li>
<li>Possess high levels of autonomy, self-motivation, and attention to detail</li>
<li>Display excellent analytical, organizational, project management, and time management skills</li>
</ul>
