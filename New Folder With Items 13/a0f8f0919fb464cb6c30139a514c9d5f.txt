<p><b>Ok, What Would I Be Doing?</b></p>
<p>The Garnishment Professional is responsible for setting up, remitting payments, and answering writs of garnishments for clients utilizing our full garnishment service. The position ensures we are maintaining compliance with federal, state, and local jurisdictional law for wages attachments. The Garnishment Professionals works closely with the Operations Team to provide direction and clarification assistance to clients.</p>
<ul>
<li>Professionally services clients and collaborates with other representatives.</li>
<li>Proactively identifies issues and solutions for clients to quickly address their needs.</li>
<li>Excels in customer service to ensure all clients are highly satisfied.</li>
<li>Processes and reviews federal and multi-state wage attachments daily.</li>
<li>Enters orders timely, within 2 days of receipt.</li>
<li>Monitors existing garnishments to get updated balances from agencies; starts new orders when prior orders paid in full.</li>
<li>Audits orders setup by other team members.</li>
<li>Audits and scans all garnishment and termination letters.</li>
<li>Logs agency mail and garnishment checks daily.</li>
<li>Prepares/writes termination letters.</li>
<li>Works with agencies on compliance/legal questions.</li>
<li>Audits daily reports/logs.</li>
<li>Monitors the garnishment email box.</li>
<li>Serves as a resource for garnishment questions from internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Documents new or changing procedures that affect the garnishment process.</li>
<li>Responds to all court orders within the legal timeframe set on the orders, to include correspondence with courts, branches, law offices and team members.</li>
<li>Assists with other projects as assigned.</li>
<li>Stays up-to-date on payroll regulations as to ensure compliance as well as for certification upkeep when relevant.</li>
<li>Other job duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
