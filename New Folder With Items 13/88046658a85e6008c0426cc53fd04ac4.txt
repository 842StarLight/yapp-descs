<h2>Front Desk Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>SUMMARY </b>This position is responsible for greeting guests upon arrival to access their file on the computer, check guests into either hotel rooms or condominiums in a pleasant, positive way to ensure that their experience at Burke is a positive one.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES </b>(include the following, other duties may be assigned)</p>
<ul>
<li>Welcomes guests on arrival and checks them in, accessing their reservation on the computer and advises the balance due for their stay, doing a credit card imprint or accepting cash.</li>
<li>Gives appropriate key to guests for the assigned room/condo.</li>
<li>Using an area map, indicates the location of the unit that the guest is checking into and explains how to get to room/unit.</li>
<li>Issues ski tickets during winter season to persons with packages that include skiing.</li>
<li>Orients the guest, explaining where the different service areas are located, such as Customer Service, Rental and Repair, Ski School, Cafeterias or Restaurants.</li>
<li>When required, explain how to get to stores in the area, hospital, points of interest, event locations, etc.</li>
<li>Makes dining room reservations when required.</li>
<li>May receive valuables from guests to be placed in the safe, issuing a receipt to the guest.</li>
<li>Must be well groomed and professional in appearance, wearing uniform items provided.</li>
<li>Troubleshoots problems that may arise for the guest and if necessary, consults with supervisor to resolve more difficult issues.</li>
<li>Works closely with security persons to assist guests who may have road, car or location of unit problems, that security can assist with.</li>
<li>Must be helpful and amiable toward the guest at all times. When difficulties arise you should consult a manager for advice on how to handle.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by supervisor, which could be directly related or unrelated to original position.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>This position has not supervisory responsibilities.</p>
