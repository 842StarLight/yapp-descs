<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The <b>Marketing Coordinator</b> is responsible for brand management and success throughout the organization. This person will be responsible for managing the day-to-day advertising and creative process to develop campaigns that highlight each community’s unique selling propositions and complement existing marketing collateral.</p>
<p>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze potential strategic partner relationships for Habitat</li>
<li>Oversee the marketing budget</li>
<li>Collaborate with the marketing team as well as other departments to identify strategies to better market the company</li>
<li>Oversee the planning and implementation of promotion/marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Manage and improve lead generation campaigns, measuring results and ROI</li>
<li>Monitor and report on effectiveness of marketing for properties</li>
<li>Work closely with various agencies and assist with new launches</li>
<li>Analyze potential strategic partner relationships for Habitat</li>
<li>Manage new technologies to optimize exposure</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, business or communications</li>
<li>2-3 years experience in marketing, public relations or advertising preferred</li>
<li>Strong analytical and project management skills</li>
<li>A flair for great copy and witty communication</li>
<li>Experience with brand management and corporate identity</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving skills, dynamic, effective communicator, organized with customer-oriented approach</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple projects with varied timelines</li>
<li>Excellent knowledge of MC, marketing software/programs, CRM tools, online analytics, GA</li>
<li>Plugged into social media platforms</li>
</ul>
<p>We operate in an environment where diversity is valued and individual initiative is rewarded. The scale of our portfolio offers challenges and opportunities for individuals who are looking to further their careers with an innovative company. We offer competitive compensation and benefit package along with development and growth opportunities.</p>
