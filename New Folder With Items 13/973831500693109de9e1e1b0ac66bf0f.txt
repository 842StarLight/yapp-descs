<h2>Depot Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Depot Coordinator must be self-motivated with a proven track record in computer technology. Candidate must be comfortable in the dynamic atmosphere of a technical organization with an evolving customer product base. Must possess strong presentation skills and be able to communicate professionally in written responses and when submitting reports. Candidate must be organized and analytical, able to eliminate obstacles through creative and adaptive approaches. Must possess strong degree of hardware knowledge; Point of Sale experience a plus. Project management or coordination skills a plus.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Create and maintain project plans, communication plans, and other project related tools to use while managing and directing technical implementation projects. Maintain a positive working environment, and effectively communicate project deliverables, assumptions, and status not only to the customer but internal teams and partners. Handle and resolve escalated customer service hardware issues. Provide supervision, including guidance, support and encouragement to the team. Ensures appropriate inventory levels of repair stock and advance exchange equipment. Manages customer hardware returns by coordinating with team members and the customer. Monitors and manages RMA queue to acceptable turnaround timeframe. Manage the customer depot lifecycle experience.</p>
</div></div></div>
