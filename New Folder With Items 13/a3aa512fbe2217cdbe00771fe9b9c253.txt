<b>JPMorgan Chase &amp; Co</b>. is a leading global financial services firm with assets of $2.5 trillion and operations worldwide. JPMC serves nearly half of America’s households with a broad range of financial services, including personal banking, credit cards, mortgages, auto financing, investment advice, small business loans and payment processing. Customers can choose how and where they want to bank: 5,300 branches, 16,000 ATMs, mobile, online and by phone. For more information, go to Chase.com.<br>

<div>
<br>
<b>Merchant Services</b> is the global payment processing business for JPMorgan Chase &amp; Co. Merchant Services is the world’s leading merchant acquirer with over $1 trillion in annual transaction volume in 2016. ChaseNet is a customized closed-loop proprietary payments platform, and Chase Pay is the firm’s new digital wallet that makes mobile transactions simple, safe and rewarding. Merchant Services is a leading provider of payment, fraud and data security for companies large and small and capable of authorizing transactions in more than 130 currencies.</div>

<div><div>
<p>The Solutions Marketing Manager will report to the Head of Solutions Marketing for SMB Merchant Services and will be integral to the achievement of aggressive growth goals for the Merchant Services specifically and Chase for Business broadly. The successful candidate will develop and execute SMB product/solutions marketing strategies for the US and Canada. They will reinforce and elevate Chase’s position as a leading payments/financial services provider for small business owners. Success in the role will be tied to SMB growth; greater awareness, consideration and adoption of Chase Merchant Services across target verticals and SMB life-stages.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop/refine a differentiated Chase Merchant Services value story, positioning and messaging framework grounded in small business owner needs (by vertical and business life stage) and unique Chase benefits and capabilities. Ensure appropriate positioning and messaging used by field teams (Sales and Relationship Managers) as well as in direct marketing channels.</li>
<li>Create streamlined product bundles that deliver a cohesive suite of solutions from mobile readers and terminals to tablet point-of-sale and integrated payments within software to support small business owners as they grow</li>
<li>Develop go-to-market strategies and breakthrough marketing to merchandise our solutions via in-person and digital (owned and third-party) channels</li>
<li>Inform the product commercialization activities (as a primary stakeholder) and lead the marketing launch of new products, features and benefits – guiding channel marketers, the field teams (sales and relationship managers) servicing, and cross-line of business partners on product marketing strategy</li>
<li>Drive the market and competitive research agenda, partner with those teams to deliver insights to identify or inform business opportunities and marketing strategies
</li>
<li>Partner closely with product strategy on the product development roadmap and end-to-end product experience</li>
<li>Create toolkits and enablement materials for sales and marketing, ensuring all are equipped to sell Merchant Services as a solution and specific product offerings
</li>
<li>Guide channel marketers, the field teams (sales and relationship managers) servicing, and cross-line of business partners on product marketing strategy</li>
<li>Track and measure engagement with product marketing assets; tie back to business impact and demonstrate return on investment</li>
<br>
</ul>
