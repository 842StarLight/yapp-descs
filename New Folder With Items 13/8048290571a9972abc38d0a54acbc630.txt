<h2>Director of Project Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Summary
<br>Nederman Corporation is seeking a Director of Project Management to lead a team of experienced project managers at our Charlotte, NC headquarters office. The Director of Project Management is responsible for the overall direction, completion, and financial outcome of turn-key industrial air filtration solution projects primarily based in the continental US. It is a leadership position reporting to the VP Nederman Division that will effectively manage a team of project managers to achieve both the customer and corporate goals.
<br>The successful candidate will lead a strong project management team that will keep everyone involved with the delivery and installation of the turn-key solution and ensure that nothing gets “lost in translation” between different stakeholders and departments. This requires excellent organization and communication skills along with the ability to prioritize, delegate and negotiate. The director will build an integrated team that inspires trust and confidence with both the customers and internal stakeholders. Everyone involved in the process should know exactly what is happening, when it is happening and what is expected from each stakeholder.
<br>Nederman is committed to providing our customers with leading engineering, design and turn-key air filtration solutions and services. We value employees who are highly motivated, well organized and have a desire to be part of a team-driven culture handling challenging and diverse assignments. Our ideal candidate will be an enthusiastic self-starter who thrives in a fast-paced, pro-active and growth-oriented environment that can prioritize and maintain oversight of multiple tasks simultaneously. They will be comfortable rolling up their sleeves and digging into the details as they work to broaden their business acumen and leadership skills for future personal growth and development within the corporation.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities of the position:</b><br>
Provide Active Leadership and Management of the Configured Solutions Project Management Team.
<br><ul>
<li>Assign projects to team members based on work load and project requirements. Communicates the progress of the projects and assigns resources as required to meet monthly, quarterly and yearly goals.</li>
<li>Motivate staff and provide direction and guidance to the individual team members, recognizing the role as both manager and support resource / mentor.</li>
<li>Applies extensive and comprehensive knowledge of project management principles and work processes related to the organization's solutions, products and services.</li>
<li>Estimate and maximize monthly POC (Percentage of Completion) revenue and gross margins by executing regular meetings with individual project managers and the accounting team to create goals and update status.</li>
<li>Ensure implementation, post audit review and refinement as required of formal processes to support the improvement of the project execution process and team performance.</li>
<li>Interface with key stakeholders (both internal and external) to finalize deliverables and coordinate handoff of project requirements.</li>
<li>Manage the timely submittal of project deliverables including drawings, schedules, invoices, etc. Prepare, review, and communicate information and reports related to cost control of project.</li>
<li>Manages the complete turn-key process, which includes holding initial scope review meetings, finalizing deliverables, developing subcontractor scope of work as applicable, managing the ordering, installation, start-up and commissioning.</li>
<li>Manage identification of risks which impact project delivery and ensure mitigation strategies are developed and executed when required</li>
<li>Plays a key role in the development of the annual business plan and budget requirements.</li>
</ul>
Support Sales Team in Preparation and execution of Proposal.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Plan and prepare turn-key project scope, schedule / timeline and budget. Manage and track effective utilization of project management and system design resources to achieve customer and corporate goals.</li>
<li>Facilitate sales team on development of budget costs and allocation of project management and system design hours of proposed solution.</li>
<li>Develop relationships with customer and industry contacts that promote repeat business, periodic client contacts and visits to track future work.</li>
</ul>
Lead cross-functional project teams and collaborate on best practices with other departments
<br><ul>
<li>Possesses a strong working knowledge of all project plans, specifications, subcontracts, purchase orders, daily correspondence, shop drawings, submittals, and all other project related documents necessary to maintain a complete and accurate set of as-built documents.</li>
<li>Responsible for lead project administration and daily operations. Prepares and maintains the project schedule, milestone dates, and close-out checklist.</li>
<li>Builds effective relationships with internal and external customers, suppliers, and vendors that embody company core values and requirements. In some instances, travel to the project site may be necessary to ensure successful completion of a project.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications and Skills
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor of Science in a technical degree (BSME preferred; MBA a plus)</li>
<li>3-5 years engineering, project or technical team leadership required</li>
<li>5+ years project management experience required</li>
<li>Project Management Professional (PMP) or Certified Associate of Project Management (CAPM) desired</li>
<li>Proven track record of building a solid engineering and or project team, implementing new tools and processes, leading value engineering initiatives, driving improvements and measuring performance</li>
<li>General working knowledge of industrial ventilation, filtration system design and electrical control systems is highly desired</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Project or similar, Excel, Word, Outlook, CRM, ERP Software.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills required with the ability to respond to common inquiries or complaints from clients, contractors, and other consultants. Knowledge of legal process during project execution, site supervision, and enforcement/application of those legal requirements.</li>
<li>Strong organization, problem solving, and analytical skills with attention to details</li>
<li>Must be a pro-active self-starter with the ability to work independently, learn new technology and provide leadership to project team and mentor staff.</li>
<li>Travel ~ 20% Domestic US</li>
</ul>
