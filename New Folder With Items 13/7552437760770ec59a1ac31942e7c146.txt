<h2>Enterprise Account Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Sealed Air partners with customers to solve their most critical packaging challenges with innovative solutions that leave our world, environment, and communities better than we found them. Our portfolio of widely recognized brands includes Cryovac® food packaging and Bubble Wrap® protective packaging which respectively enable a safer, more efficient food supply chain and protect valuable goods shipped around the world. Sealed Air generated $4.7 billion in sales in 2018 and has approximately 15,500 employees who serve customers in 123 countries. To learn more, visit www.sealedair.com.</div>
<div>
<div>
<div>
Summary
</div>
<div>
<div>The Enterprise Account Specialist support Enterprise Managers, Shrink Business Development Managers, Channel Directors and Cross Regional Accounts. Support is primarily related to Vendavo deals, pricing quotes/proposals and equipment orders. Assist Managers with other administrative tasks to further reduce their office time.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Responsibilities
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Create quotes and equipment proposals</li>
<li>Create and maintain accurate pricing and eliminate inactive deviations</li>
<li>Maintain Enterprise database and manual contracts</li>
<li>Assist Managers in creating and updating net cost calculators and RFQ’s</li>
<li>Upon completion of price updates, communicate new price to Sales/Distributors by request</li>
<li>Problem solve pricing issues for TBM /Channel/ Customer Experience/Rebate Team and Distributors</li>
<li>Work closely with Selling Teams, Rebate Team, Deal Desk and Marketing</li>
<li>Liaison between Enterprise Managers and Distributors</li>
<li>Assist with projects and attend conference calls as needed</li>
<li>Vendavo Deal Manager- gather information and submit deals for approval</li>
<li>Complete and collect documentation for Equipment Orders</li>
<li>SAP C4C/MDG- Customer Master maintenance for equipment customers</li>
<li>Process equipment orders in SAP-Trials, Sales, Rentals, Capital Lease</li>
<li>Process equipment transfers-equipment moved in the field from customer to customer</li>
<li>Process equipment returns</li>
<li>Instapak- submit FOC Service Agreements for approval</li>
<li>Create ad hoc sales reports (BW/Vendavo)</li>
<li>Miscellaneous - IE: Freight quotes for equipment, request expedited shipments, assist in Distributor portal documentation</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
