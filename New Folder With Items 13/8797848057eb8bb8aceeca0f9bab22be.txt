<p><b>
Job Summary</b><br>
</p>
<p>
A liaison between the community, client, other care providers, and the Services@Home agency.<br>
<br>
This professional is responsible for educating the community in services provided as well as state and federal regulations governing these services. Is accountable for generating predetermined quota for the division. Partners with agency leadership in caregiver recruitment.</p>
<div>
Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Create and provide education materials to community through regular contacts with hospitals, long term care facilities, physician offices, medical clinics, etc.
</div></li>
<li><div>Represent the location in various community groups/organizations.
</div></li>
<li><div>Coordinate all marketing and advertising in the assigned business unit under the direction of the Executive Director.
</div></li>
<li><div>Act as liaison between client, family, clinical staff and physician to ensure optimum communication and quality client care.
</div></li>
<li><div>Coordinate and facilitate communication between location and community personnel involved in client care.
</div></li>
<li><div>Schedule and coordinate community in-services and education meetings.
</div></li>
<li><div>Be available to answer care related questions.
</div></li>
<li><div>Provide ongoing input to quality improvement committee based on feedback from the community involved in the care of clients.
</div></li>
<li><div>Consistently meet and exceed predetermined referral quotas within designated sales territory.
</div></li>
<li><div>Organize time schedule to meet and exceed goals.
</div></li>
<li><div>Identify new business opportunities and contracts.
</div></li>
<li><div>Provide support in the identification and recruitment of potential employees.</div></li>
</ul>
