<h2>Director Maintenance Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview:</b></p>
<p>Tyto Athene (formerly Black Box Government Solutions) is a full service integrator focused on helping clients accelerate their ability to make decisions by providing ubiquitous and secure access to enterprise information throughout their operating environment. Tyto Athene uses a myriad of technologies, innovative thinking, and proven processes to deliver successful outcomes for its clients worldwide.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>In this position, the Director for Maintenance Services, reporting directly to the VP of Operations, plans, directs, and coordinates the tactical and strategic activities of multiple teams to include all CONUS and OCONUS onsite maintenance services contracts; has overall responsibility for the Operational and Financial performance of the Maintenance Services organization; controls organizational performance through subordinate Program Managers; coaches and counsels team managers on business, organizational, and technology strategies; provide Operations support to the Sales team within Maintenance area of responsibility to assist in growth and increased revenue; sets direction and policies for multiple locations, technologies, and/or specialty disciplines; establishes strategies and plans for the organization and sets goals to meet evolving needs; creates contingency plans to meet changes in business circumstances.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Director of Maintenance Services will also manage all technical staff and employees as part of a very large, complex team of professionals. The Director will be responsible for the overall day to day operations and guidance within his/her respective area of operations within Tyto. The Director will also work to resolve any and all employee issues/concerns in alignment with company HR policies and will foster team cohesion within the staff.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Fostering of excellent relations with the Customer and the team</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure prompt identification and resolution of service delivery and quality related issues</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure adherence to Service Level Agreements established for onsite maintenance contracts, trouble reporting and resolution, and warranty obligations</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure ongoing CPAR results for active maintenance contracts are a continual focus for exceeding satisfactory results</li></ul>
<ul><li> Continually striving to improve efficiency of services and related operations that translate into lower costs</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure accurate and timely forecast projections for programs and projects as applicable</li></ul>
<ul><li> Update and maintain data relative to operations management within Tyto applications and systems consistent with best practices</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure proper staffing levels are maintained at all sites, proactively work with HR recruiters to fulfill contractual staffing requirements</li></ul>
<ul><li> Management of subcontractors as applicable in delivery of projects and programs</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide support to proposal development and review processes when applicable</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for cost, schedule, performance and quality across the Maintenance Services area of responsibility</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The successful candidate shall have a minimum of 15+ years’ experience in the management of business, technical, and field service operations, with demonstrated program, customer service/help desk, and team management experience, or 10+ years’ experience in the above areas with a Bachelor’s degree. The successful candidate must understand delivery of services within industry best practices framework such as ITIL or DOD equivalent.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Must be able to obtain and maintain at a minimum a DOD Secret clearance</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong management skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong computer skills and familiarity with MS Office suite of applications</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with Government contracting</li></ul>
<ul><li> Familiarity with ISO quality and ITIL management standards and guidelines</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong oral skills and ability to communicate effectively to both internal and external customers</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to manage multiple personnel and functional areas</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to successfully handle multiple priorities simultaneously is required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must be a US citizen with a valid passport</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work flexible hours and travel as needed with routine customer interfacing meetings</li></ul>
<ul><li> A valid driver's license is required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prior experience with Department of Defense and Federal customers is required</li></ul>
