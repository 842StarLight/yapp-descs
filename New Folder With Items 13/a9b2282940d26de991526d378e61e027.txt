<h2>Community Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>About Meritus
</b></p>
<p>At Meritus Communities, our goal is simple: offer positive living experiences in exceptional communities at affordable prices.
</p>
<p>Meritus was founded in 2013 by a group of manufactured housing industry veterans who wanted to make a difference in the way manufactured housing communities are run and the way residents are treated. Today, we build thriving, well-run communities where residents have more than a place to live, but a place where they are able to enjoy an active lifestyle by being part of an exceptional neighborhood full of luxury amenities.
</p>
<p>Meritus Communities is one of the nation’s largest and quickest growing owner/operator of Manufactured Housing Communities (MHCs), spanning 21,000 sites across the US, and counting. Are you ready to join the team?<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>About the position
</b></p>
<p>As a Community Manager, you are responsible for the overall operations and sales at the community with a strong focus on customer service and retention.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the day-to-day operations of the community including customer service, resident relations, vendor relations, rent collections, and the community staff.
</li>
<li>Manage and increase occupancy through home sales, home leasing, site leasing, and actively marketing the community.
</li>
<li>Analyze and prepare monthly, annual, and long-term budgets. Meet or exceed all approved revenue, expense, sales, and leasing budgets.
</li>
<li>Complete monthly variance reports.
</li>
<li>Manage all third-party vendors.
</li>
<li>Develop marketing strategies based on the local market and competition.
</li>
<li>Manage all aspects of the home rental program.
</li>
<li>Maintain the physical appearance and infrastructure of the community in accordance with company standards.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<br><div>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma and 5 plus years property management experience.
</li>
<li>Must have experience creating and analyzing budgets, P&amp;L's, and financial reports.
</li>
<li>Marketing, sales, and leasing experience
</li>
<li>Strong management and operational skills including customer service; public relations; performance management.
</li>
<li>Ability to be available to resident and community needs after hours.
</li>
<li>Ability to live on-site in company provided housing.
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills including writing and verbal.
</li>
<li>Excellent computer literacy in MS Office suite of products and other software applications.
</li>
<li>Valid driver's license.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Personal characteristics
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Team oriented with an exceptionally strong work ethic and outstanding interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>Demonstrated analytical and organizational skills, problem solving abilities, and attention to detail
</li>
<li>Poised and polished professional with excellent oral and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Strong judgement and decision-making skills<br>
</li>
</ul>
