About the role
<br><br>
Syapse is looking for a Senior Director of Product Management - Data Strategy to oversee the use of data throughout Syapse offerings and services. The ideal candidate will prioritize and translate high level business requests into technical requirements, lead their team, and work with all Product Management teammates, informatics, and development teams to design and deliver successful solutions. Cross collaborative work will span the larger business and focus on a Customer First value, meeting our clients' needs, addressing the larger market as a whole, and continuing to advance the organization as an innovator and thought leader.
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities Include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Recruiting, mentoring, inspiring, and collaborating with a high caliber team of product managers and product designers.</li>
<li>Clearly defining deliverables that lead your own team, as well as illustrate for all functions what we need to deliver, so the other functions can choose how to do it.</li>
<li>Work together with informatics, senior leadership, sales, marketing, customer operations, business development, and engineering to establish and maintain alignment on the right plan to treat data as a product, or data as a business.</li>
<li>Lead the team to find that perfect balance of providing a definition of success for software development, but allowing them to choose the best technical paths to get there.</li>
<li>Establish clear, story-telling communication for customers, sales, key opinion leaders and more.</li>
<li>Collaborate cross-functionally to ensure the needs of various stakeholders are considered in data handling and processes. Communicate trade-offs so that stakeholders understand the reasons behind prioritization decisions made under time or resource constraints</li>
<li>Partner with the Data insights team to understand the long-term strategy for analytics development and processing.</li>
</ul>
