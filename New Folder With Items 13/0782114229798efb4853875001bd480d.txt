<h2>Senior Commercial Counsel</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>In-house senior attorney position with the Legal Department of a leading multinational cybersecurity company reporting to the Company's General Counsel.</li>
<li>The position requires a business enabler to provide proactive, practical and supporting advice to a sales team and procurement at a high pace environment.</li>
<li>Work both independently and as part of a diverse, highly professional in-house international legal team supporting commercial transactions with customers and vendors.</li>
<li>Interact with senior management to provide practical guidance related to the negotiation of commercial agreements.</li>
<li>Interacts cross-functionally with several departments in the organization.</li>
<li>A senior professional with extensive experience and knowledge in the high-tech industry.</li>
<li>A professional authority that is recognized both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Work on significant and unique problems at a high level of complexity.</li>
<li>As an expert in the field, uses professional concepts in developing resolution to critical issues.</li>
<li>Escalate critical issues that need to be addressed and focuses attention on these high priority issues.</li>
</ul>
<b>Job Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Graduate degree (J.D.) in Law from an accredited law school or equivalent and a member in good standing with a Bar Association.</li>
<li>Requires a minimum of 7 years of related experience reviewing, drafting and negotiating commercial agreements for the sale, license, distribution and procurement of high-tech products, systems and solutions in a multinational and multicultural environment.</li>
<li>Relevant work experience, preferably with a major law firm and in-house legal department.</li>
<li>Experience in a SaaS is a must and in cybersecurity a plus.</li>
<li>Additional experience should include negotiating contracts relating to software and integrated products; related technical support, professional services, managed services, SaaS, cloud, agency, channel, distribution, reseller, OEM, teaming, non-disclosure and other similar agreements.</li>
<li>Experience in identifying compliance issues, including trade restrictions, FCPA, and data privacy.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and creatively in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Exceptional analytical, inter-personal and verbal/written communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work comfortably with all levels of the organization and translate complicated information into clearly understood terms.</li>
<li>Solid business acumen with the ability to anticipate and identify problems to analyze strategic issues.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to influence decisions in areas outside of direct control through collaboration and teamwork.</li>
<li>Highly developed ability to manage multiple projects and balance priorities under deadlines.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of potential revenue recognition issues in the commercial contracting process.</li>
<li>Occasional travel required.</li>
</ul>
</div>
