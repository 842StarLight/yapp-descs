<h2>Service Center Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Function Summary </b><b>:
</b></p>
<p>The Service Center Supervisor provides oversight and supervision for the delivery, pickup, maintenance, cleaning, and quality processing of company assets for assigned locations. Individual will supervise the work force providing work assignments for daily activities and tracking service center metrics. Other responsibilities include compliance related activities such as self-inspections, infection control, and QC/PMI to conform to, support, and enforce all company policies and procedures. Supervises distributed site operations.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Functions </b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the organization, monitoring, scheduling and completion of the clean/QC, delivery, set-up, pick up and servicing of rental products based on set goals. Facilitate all delivery activities through a team-based approach.
</li>
<li>Ensure the assigned team performs critical care medical equipment placements, may include conducting technical in-service to medical staff on product function and use. May assist in patient transfers.
</li>
<li>Manage patient and hospital experience with company products/devices through regular and routine rounding activities including appropriate documentation of actions.
</li>
<li>Either on an individual basis or through the assigned team troubleshoot equipment and make minor repairs using hand tools. Assist with or facilitate preventative maintenance where appropriate. Ensure equipment in repair is within the allowable limits set for the service center.
</li>
<li>Leads internal and external audits of the service center and executes corrective actions on any identified audit deficiencies. Achieves 100% compliance to audit requirements. Corrective actions should be completed within 30 days of any audit findings
</li>
<li>Responsible for inventory of products and parts for the service center. Responsible for monthly asset inventories within the service center.
</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with company fleet policy and associated safety program. Ensure compliance with Corporate, State or Federal requirements for vehicles and service centers.
</li>
<li>Drive a company fleet vehicle (box truck or van) to conduct field activities as needed.
</li>
<li>Follow all Standard Operating Procedures
</li>
<li>Participate in any and all reasonable work activities as assigned by management including but not limited to participation in Arjo special initiatives and programs such as Demo and ReNu pick up / drop off activities
</li>
<li>Provides in-service training/retraining as needed on products and new processes. Supports the completion of all trainings, to include timely submission of training records
</li>
<li>Assist in completing employee annual competency evaluations. Provides input on employee performance to the TSM and assists with HR matters as needed and requested under the guidance of the TSM.
</li>
<li>Assist the TSM in determining assigned service center team schedule to ensure 24/7 coverage for the service territory. Provide coverage for service technicians (vacation, LOAs, etc.) as needed.
</li>
<li>Provide coverage for Territory Service Manager as needed. Participate in on-call and holiday work requirements as needed
</li>
<li>Demonstrate Arjo core values in daily activities.
</li>
<li>Support and facilitate training, scheduling, parts distribution and completion of all field-based product improvements to include but not limited to: TAN’s, Field Corrections, Product Upgrades, etc.
</li>
<li>Assist and / or facilitate technical support via phone to field service employees. Assist field service employees with difficult repairs onsite at customer’s location and/or service centers.
</li>
<li>Assist in preparation and distribution of required reports to assigned team.
</li>
<li>Support the delivery of preventative maintenance and repair services at customer establishments in accordance with established procedures and techniques, using proper tools, test equipment and replacement parts.
</li>
<li>Assist in the installation process at customer locations.
</li>
<li>Establish and maintain positive and collaborative working relationships with internal and external customers.
</li>
<li>Work with other internal departments to establish and grow the core business.
</li>
<li>Assist region to achieve revenue goals as it relates to the sale of company products and services.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate effective use of products and technical data provided by the company.
</li>
<li>Assess, provide cost estimates for, and complete repairs for customer-owned medical equipment or customer damaged rental assets.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Intermediate experience using Microsoft Office products such as Word, Excel and PowerPoint
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to supervise employees, manage change and multiple priorities.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated customer service skills.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to maintain confidentiality and exercise discretion in all business dealings.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to deal with employees and business contacts and convey a positive service oriented attitude.
</li>
<li>Ability to travel by commercial air service to attend scheduled business and corporate function.
</li>
<li>Ability to meet all performance aspects of the job including but not limited to: meeting all performance and safety standards.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated communication and proper business etiquette skills.
</li>
<li>Must be able to decipher data from computer-generated reports, software programs, technical manuals and written correspondence.
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Minimum Requirements </b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business Management or related degree and at least two years of service supervisory experience, or 5+ years of related strong supervisory experience accepted in lieu of degree.
</li>
<li>Must be able to complete and pass a DOT physical and ensure ongoing compliance to all DOT requirements while actively employed in this position.
</li>
<li>Must travel as required to customer sites (overnight travel amount will vary depending on the territories to be covered); must reside within the assigned territory; must be able to operate an automobile (valid driver’s licensed required).
</li>
<li>Must have a current state driver’s license in the state in which the applicant resides.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Quality Requirements </b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Build Quality into all aspects of their work by maintaining compliance to all quality requirements.
</li>
<li>Ensure compliance to all FDA and Worldwide Quality &amp; Compliance regulations (As applicable to the job function).
</li>
<li>Must have the education and experience to understand and comply with U.S. and Worldwide medical device regulations (As applicable to your job function).
</li>
<li>Attend all required Quality &amp; Compliance training at the specified interval.
</li>
<li>Adopt the “Beyond Compliance Quality Culture” in the work environment; always meet and exceed requirements.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Environmental/Safety/Physical Work Conditions:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures environmental consciousness and safe practices are exhibited in decisions.
</li>
<li>Travel requirement of approximately 25%, which may include international travel.
</li>
<li>Ability to travel by car or public transportation as needed to meet business needs.
</li>
<li>May work extended hours during peak business cycles.
</li>
<li>The individual must occasionally lift and/or move up to 25 lbs. (*NOTE* Field Sales &amp; Clinical: 35 lbs)
</li>
<li>Ability to utilize computer and telephone equipment and other related office accessories/devices to complete assignments.
</li>
<li>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is frequently required to stand, walk and sit.
</li>
<li>The employee must have use of hands to finger, handle or feel objects, tools, or controls; reach with hands and arms; speak and hear.
</li>
<li>Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, distance vision, color vision, peripheral vision, depth perception and the ability to adjust focus.<br>
</li>
</ul>
