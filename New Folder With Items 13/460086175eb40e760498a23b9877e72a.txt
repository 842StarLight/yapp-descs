<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT YOU'LL BE DOING</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Driving customer acquisition through all forms of media buying including, but not limited to: SEO, SEM, Google Shopping and Display, Re-targeting, and Social Advertising.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Establish a forecast for each channel and make recommendations on budget allocation and goals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build and optimize customer email journeys to increase engagement with the brand and drive conversion. Propose new A/B tests with the goal of improving our KPIs.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Track daily cross-channel performance data to build testable new strategies and drive actionable decisions that optimize sales, traffic and conversion.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create, implement and manage a dynamic analytics dashboard to measure our performance.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborate with key stakeholders to manage the growth and maintenance of our prospects and customer database including new programs designed to increase email capture and LTV.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Implement new customer review application and create a program to leverage reviews across the site and marketing channels.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Identify and evaluate emerging platforms and channels to lead new tests and growth initiatives.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work closely with creative team to evaluate performance of campaigns and creative assets.</p>
</li>
