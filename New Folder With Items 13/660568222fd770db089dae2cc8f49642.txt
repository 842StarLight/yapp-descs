<p>Job Posting Title:
</p>Dairy Livestock Production Specialist- Lancaster, PA Territory
<p></p>
<p>Job Description:
</p>
<p>We're hiring a Livestock Production Specialist to focus on Dairy feed sales with our partner dealer located in Paradise, PA . In this role, you will be responsible to sell feed and all related products in a manner that will optimize the dealer's market share and savings, improve the dealer’s efficiency and help achieve the dealer's goals.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Additional responsibilities include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Focus on Dairy Adult Cow and Young Animal feed sales by calling on Dairy producers while providing a consultative sales approach.
</li>
<li>Evaluate and assist animal owners with their nutritional program.
</li>
<li>Organize and conduct effective educational meetings to enhance Purina brand image and sell product.
</li>
<li>Conduct selling events to build dealer’s business in the market.
</li>
<li>Execute a high volume of farm calls per week, and annually growing sales and market share in the above categories.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience &amp; Education:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Animal Science or related field
</li>
<li>Experience providing accurate feeding and management recommendations to animal owners in the market
</li>
<li>Dairy Industry experience
</li>
<li>2-5 years of experience in feed sales, livestock nutrition, marketing, and the execution of growth plan with proven success desired
</li>
<li>Candidate must be able to lift and carry up to 50 pounds.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Competencies &amp; Other Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills are required along with strong organization and time management skills.
</li>
<li>High internal drive, a natural ability for relationship building, and leadership in a team environment.
</li>
<li>Computer skills, specifically MS Office, plus the ability to be trained on ration balancing software, etc., salesforce experience desired
</li>
<li>Requires the ability to lift and carry up to 50 pounds.
</li>
<li>On farm interaction, in all-weather types at producer facilities for a walk through, and evaluations.
</li>
<li>Ability to network in industry to put producers together to earn additional business and relationships.
</li>
<li>Strong public speaking skills.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Percentage of Travel:
</b></p>
<ul><li>5% over night travel , in addition to daily travel in the assigned geography
</li></ul>
