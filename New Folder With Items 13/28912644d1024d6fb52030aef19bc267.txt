<div>
The Ad Ops Trafficker is responsible for setting up and managing marketing campaign setups to ensure streamlined execution of our advertiser and publisher ad delivery. The ideal candidate is very organized and takes tremendous pride in a job well done. That person will be an expert with Criteo's internal tools, will leverage and maintain checklists to ensure that nothing is forgotten and will strive for zero errors. This person will work very closely with and will get requests cascaded from the client facing teams (Technical Solutions, Creative Services and Account Strategy) who transform business needs into technical configurations.</div>
