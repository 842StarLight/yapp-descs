<h2>Support Desk Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Do you have what it takes to JOIN OUR TEAM?</p>
<p>
We’re looking for a Support Desk Engineer who can join our fast-paced environment as part of our growing team in our Lincoln, Nebraska. In this full-time role, you‘ll be responsible for supporting our clients through hardware &amp; software support. Additionally, you’ll be part of the Service Delivery team which is integral for the Managed Services group of DataVizion monitoring servers, PCs and providing enterprise-grade support to clients of DataVizion.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
How to Excel at DataVizion</p>
<ul>
<li>Customer Driven – Operate as first point of contact for DataVizion’s Managed Service clients.
</li>
<li>Responsive – Resolve client’s service needs within service level agreement thresholds
</li>
<li>Attentive – Analyze customer’s environment &amp; needs for sales opportunities
</li>
<li>Communicative – Escalate issues to Account Manager or Field Services team as needed
</li>
<li>Dependable – Manage priorities, scheduled tasks, and provide project updates
</li>
<li>Collaborative – participate in meetings with Service Delivery Team, DataVizion leadership, and more
</li>
<li>Adaptable – Support multiple clients using customer service and interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>Proactive – Focus on developing industry applicable skills, attaining certifications, and improving soft skills.
</li>
<li>Accurate – Demonstrate organization skills and keen attention to detail and ensure compliance with DataVizion policies and procedures
</li>
<li>Decisive – Use critical thinking, independent judgement, and thorough understanding of company policies to make informed, customer centric decisions<br>
</li>
</ul>

<p>Job Description</p>
<ul>
<li>Respond to and Resolve customer service requests
</li>
<li>Design, Configure, and Maintain production environments for small to large scale businesses
</li>
<li>Perform service on products from multiple manufacturers and vendors
</li>
<li>Provide technical and procedural direction to both internal staff and clients
</li>
<li>Investigate and identify the root cause of service issues with a first-time-fix priority
</li>
<li>Manage and Maintain client’s IT environment by following implementation best practices
</li>
<li>Manage, Create, Close, and Update, tickets in assigned ITSM system
</li>
<li>Acquire product knowledge through classroom or on-the-job training opportunities
</li>
<li>Perform preventive maintenance and modifications on equipment<br>
</li>
</ul>
