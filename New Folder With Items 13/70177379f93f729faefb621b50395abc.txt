<h2>LEAD GENERATION SPECIALIST</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Overview</b></h4>
<p></p>
<p>The Lead Generation Specialist is responsible for prospecting, qualifying and generating new sales leads to support the business development efforts of our team at Flexera Global. Successful candidates will be highly motivated self-starters able to identify and develop new business prospects from multiple sources, including inbound marketing leads, prospect lists, social media, discovery and individual research. A dynamic personality with a drive to reach and influence decision makers is essential.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify qualified prospects, generate conversations and set up meetings, etc. via telephone and mass communication such as email and social media in order to introduce Flexera Global and our solutions and capability to appropriate buyers within the target market.</li>
<li>Follow up on leads and conduct research to identify potential prospects.</li>
<li>Conduct a needs analysis and determine prospects’ pain points to identify how Flexera Global solutions will benefit their business.</li>
<li>Build and cultivate prospect relationships by initiating communications and conducting follow-up communications in conjunction with Flexera leadership in order to move opportunities through the sales funnel.</li>
<li>Work with Flexera leaders and engineers to develop and grow the sales pipeline to consistently meet quarterly revenue goals.</li>
<li>Manage data for new and prospective clients within CRM, ensuring all communications are logged, information is accurate, and documents are attached.</li>
<li>Prepare and analyze sales pipeline reports and dashboards.</li>
<li>Communicate to customers and Flexera staff effectively.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>HS Diploma, Bachelor’s Degree preferred</li>
<li>1-3 years sales experience selling solutions to businesses.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to meet and/or exceed determined sales and activity quotas.</li>
<li>A proven track record of developing strong client relationships.</li>
<li>Excellent client service skills.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>1-3 years experience working in a team oriented or team selling environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Technical Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in MS Office products (Excel, Word, MS Outlook, MS PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce.com or another CRM Software a plus</li>
<li>Conversational knowledge of the IT and consulting industries</li>
</ul>
