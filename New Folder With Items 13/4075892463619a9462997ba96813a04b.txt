<p><b>
Job Summary</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>CNA Surety has an immediate opening for an Underwriting Manager in our Indianapolis, IN, branch office. Under technical supervision, the Underwriting Manager is responsible for underwriting risks in a book of contract (90%) and commercial (10%) business while developing and nurturing assigned agency and broker relationships. The territory will include Indiana. Candidate should demonstrate an understanding of construction and corporate commercial surety bond underwriting fundamentals and make consistent and well thought out recommendations. Individual will need to effectively utilize technical data to assist and support the recommendations.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><br>
<i>Performs a combination of duties in accordance with departmental guidelines:</i></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>1. Underwriting Expertise</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Reviews applications and financial requirements to determine acceptability of risk in accordance with CNA’s guidelines and standards
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Understands pricing components and rating methodology as well as use of the predictive modeling tools; prices risk based on financial and competitive analysis
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrates technical underwriting skills through strategic, thorough account reviews and file documentation
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepares or analyzes information on adverse underwriting decisions, rate appeals, underwriting requirements, status and declinations in order to drive results within the assigned territory
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Keeps current on state/territory issues, regulations and trends</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>2. Portfolio Management<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Manages assigned portfolio to achieve gross written premium, profitability, rate, retention, product mix, new business and portfolio management goals
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prioritizes time and resources to effectively manage and optimize producer performance
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Operates with an Enterprise perspective to identify cross sell opportunities and create growth within other lines of business and customer segments
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrates in-depth knowledge of CNA’s products and appetite while clearly communicating CNA’s position at the point of sale</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>3. Sales &amp; Distribution Management</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Develops producer relationships to build pipeline, agency intelligence, and drives performance to meet and exceed results
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develops and implements distributor specific plans, monitors performance, and adjusts plan to ensure a substantial pipeline for future profitable growth
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Drives effective relationship management and customer activities to build trust, facilitate negotiation and secure commitment with the customer
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proactively identifies cross-sell opportunities and partners with other underwriters to deliver comprehensive insurance solutions for the customer</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Reporting Relationship</b></p>
<br>
<p><br>
Director</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills Knowledge and Abilities</b></p>
<br>
<p><br>
1. Advanced knowledge of underwriting and insurance industry theories and practices.
</p>
<p>2. Demonstrated high level of technical expertise and product specific knowledge.
</p>
<p>3. Strong interpersonal communication and negotiation skills. Ability to effectively interact with all levels of CNA's internal and external business partners.
</p>
<p>4. Ability to work independently.
</p>
<p>5. Strong analytical and problem solving skills.
</p>
<p>6. Ability to exercise independent judgment and to make critical business decisions effectively.
</p>
<p>7. Knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite as well as other business-related software.
</p>
<p>8. Demonstrated leadership skills.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Education and Experience</b></p>
<br>
<p><br>
1. Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience. Professional designations and/or Accounting/Finance degree preferred.</p>
<div>2. Typically a minimum eight years of underwriting experience. Surety underwriting experience preferred.</div>
