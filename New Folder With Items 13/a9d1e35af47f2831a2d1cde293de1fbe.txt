<h2>Cosmetic Dermatology Patient Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description</p>
<p>Looking for an organized, detailed, professional, energetic and passionate coordinator for an aesthetic medicine practice. Previous experience in a Med Spa, Dermatology office, Plastic surgery office, Cosmetic/ Aesthetic or related setting is strongly preferred. Must have excellent customer service with outstanding phone and communication skills. Previous experience and knowledge with cosmetic procedures such as injectables and laser procedures are preferred.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities Include and are not limited to: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Performing multiple and diverse tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>Maintaining supplies in rooms and stocking inventory of medical supplies</li>
<li>Patient check-in and check-out</li>
<li>Comfortable using email and scheduling software applications (training will be provided for Aesthetic Pro)</li>
<li>Ability to gain knowledge of our customized services and push sales/ packages</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills</li>
<li>Dependability, trustworthy, enthusiastic, positive attitude</li>
<li>Work well with others in a team environment</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills with knowledge of grammar, spelling and medical terminology</li>
<li>Pleasant and friendly on the phone and in person</li>
<li>Neat and professional appearance</li>
<li>Monitors waiting room to ensure patients are comfortable with furnishings, drinks, and magazines are orderly, clean and appropriate</li>
<li>Posting daily cash payments accurately, generate daily report at the end of the day</li>
<li>Scheduling new and returning patients efficiently and accurately</li>
<li>Photography skills with ability to take before and after photos, as well as taking occasional videos of procedures for social media</li>
<li>Comfortable and proactive with social media (Instagram, Facebook- live features)</li>
<li>Turn over exam rooms efficiently and expeditiously, ensuring it is clean and ready for next patient.</li>
<li>Comfortable with a background check</li>
</ul>
