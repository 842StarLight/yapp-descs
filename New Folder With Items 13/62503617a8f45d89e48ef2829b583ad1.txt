<div><b>Sales Operations Manager</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
InsideView is looking for an experienced SaaS Sales Operations manager with the desire to make their mark in our growing company</b></div>
<div><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
Do you have an in-depth understanding of sales processes? Are you comfortable working independently? Do you understand the way sales people think? Do you remain calm under pressure? Do you have a high attention and enjoyment for detail? If so, we’d love to talk with you!</div>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Own the opportunity process from a sales qualified lead to close and beyond, including contracts and renewals</li>
<li>
Actively participate in Deal Desk activities to edit, review, and approve active deals</li>
<li>
Utilize your excellent communication skills to stay in close contact with the sales team to support them in their day to day activities</li>
<li>
Suggest and implement process and tool improvements to increase sales team efficiency and effectiveness</li>
<li>
Onboard, train, and guide new salespeople in the use of sales tools and details of sales processes</li>
<li>
Track and analyze data to provide stakeholders with insights and recommendations to improve performance</li>
<li>
Work with marketing to improve campaign effectiveness</li>
<li>
Partner with finance to ensure for each seller the correct book of business is set and accurate commission is calculated</li>
<li>
Contribute to the growth, improvement, and innovation of InsideView’s Sales Operations team, tools, and processes</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div>
This position is based in Austin, Texas and reports to the Director of Revenue Operations.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
About You</b></div>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
3 years minimum experience in Sales Operations, 5+ years preferred</li>
<li>
Hands-on experience with Salesforce Account, Contacts, Opportunity, Quote, Reporting, and Dashboard functionality</li>
<li>
Previous experience supporting a sales organization, and strong understanding of the DNA of salespeople</li>
<li>
Superior time and task management skills, creative and analytical problem solver</li>
<li>
Ability to handle urgent requests professionally, not easily flustered</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to work well in a team-oriented sales-driven environment but also proven successful at working cross-functionally with multiple departments</li>
<li>
Self-starter who remains curious and positive even in a rapidly changing environment</li>
<li>
Ability to self-manage, multitask, and prioritize your work in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Adaptable, fast learner able to self-regulate and remain calm under pressure</li>
<li>
Excellent judgement with the ability to lean-in to problems to solve them and the understanding of when to request input from others</li>
</ul>
