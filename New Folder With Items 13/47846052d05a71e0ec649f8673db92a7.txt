<b>
Position Objective</b>
</div>
<div>
<div>The objective of the Solutions Architect (SA) is to develop technology solutions and strategies for clients and ensure the growth of the project revenue stream by facilitating the sale of opportunities, ensuring project services through accurate proposal development and pre-sales design. The SA will help grow and expand the breadth and depth of solutions delivered to clients.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The SA is a lead technical consultant for the respective territory and acts as a solutions expert on behalf of clients and Field Engineering.</div>
</div>
