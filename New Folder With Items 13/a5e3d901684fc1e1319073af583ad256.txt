<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our client, LiveStories is a venture-backed startup building modern tools to make civic data actionable and usable. Governments, depend on LiveStories to be more data-driven, transparent, and productive. Businesses, universities, and researchers depend on LiveStories to streamline their data operations. <p>We love data and believe in its power to transform how we live, work, and play. We obsess over simplifying civic data so that government departments can gather insights to tell compelling stories.
