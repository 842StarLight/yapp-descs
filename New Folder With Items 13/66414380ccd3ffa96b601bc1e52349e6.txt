<h2>Senior Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Please review the job details below.
</b></h2>
<p></p>Maxar is currently seeking a Senior Project Manager located in Longmont, CO to support a complex mix of operational areas including project management, resource coordination, technology integration, production planning, product supportability, and supply chain management.
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responds to Sales Opportunity requests with supportability and cost estimates and providing examples of how sales opportunities can be supported through detailed statements of work.
</li>
<li>Evaluates existing and emerging DigitalGlobe product specifications for operational supportability.
</li>
<li>Coordinates with research and development (R&amp;D) team to steer technology development for factory improvements and to operationalize developing geospatial technology.
</li>
<li>Manages the operational project management aspects of projects from initiation through project closure.
</li>
<li>Supports Account Service Managers and Sales teams with customer communication and issue resolution.
</li>
<li>Routinely provides status reports to stakeholders, and assists other Project Managers with reporting requirements.
</li>
<li>Defines roles and responsibilities for project work, and coordinates with management to assign project resources for execution of project deliverables.
</li>
<li>Monitors and controls projects to ensure they stay within scope, time, cost, and quality requirements.
</li>
<li>Performs reviews of new contracts, assessing risk and operational supportability.
</li>
<li>Coordinates with research and development (R&amp;D) team to steer technology development for factory improvements and to operationalize developing geospatial technology.
</li>
<li>Works with system engineering teams to devise new methods of supporting geospatial production in the DigitalGlobe production systems.
</li>
<li>Assists in the development of quarterly geospatial production plans for SCM.
</li>
<li>Completes project management plans for larger scale projects, including summary information, product specifications and deliverables, risk registers, acceptance registers, change registers, and communications management plans.
</li>
<li>Large contributor to the Project Management Community of Practice meetings, supporting continuous improvement initiatives and lessons learned symposiums.
</li>
<li>Leader for the development and maintenance of required project management procedures and templates.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
