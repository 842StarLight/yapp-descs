<h2>E-Commerce Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Always focused on a positive culture and continuous growth, Summit Information Resources is looking for an E-Commerce Specialist. This position is the perfect opportunity for the driven, versatile marketing and E-Commerce professional.</p>
<p>
The E-Commerce Specialist is responsible for marketing and selling computer hardware equipment online. This person will be focused on driving the growth of our E-Commerce business through different platforms, while delivering excellent customer service. The E-Commerce Specialist will also work closely with all departments within the company.
</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Create and implement a strategy for E-Commerce business</li>
<li>Develop a professional posting template using photos and product info</li>
<li>Drive growth of E-Commerce business through platforms such as eBay, Amazon &amp; Newegg</li>
<li>Manage inventory and consistently keep product moving through online outlets</li>
<li>Create and run advertising campaigns as needed through E-Commerce sites</li>
<li>Analyze sales results and trends, adjusting techniques as necessary</li>
<li>Respond to all customer feedback and inquiries through email, chat and phone calls</li>
<li>Manage business relationships with E-Commerce sites</li>
<li>Work closely with Marketing Coordinator to promote products on website</li>
<li>Stay up to date on online marketplaces and recommend new business opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p>
Key Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>1-3 years’ experience in a related role</li>
<li>Strong understanding of marketing, sales and the IT industry</li>
<li>Exceptional customer service skills</li>
<li>Organized and strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and utilize all resources available to exceed goals</li>
<li>A highly focused work regimen with ability to self-manage and thrive in an entrepreneurial and team environment</li>
<li>Awareness of product market trends</li>
<li>Versatile and able to prioritize ever changing business and office demands</li>
</ul>
