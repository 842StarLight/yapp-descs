<h2>Business Development Presales Director-AX D365</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Position Summary:</b>
<p>The Microsoft Food &amp; Beverage Pre-Sales Director supports sales productivity and deal flow by securing the “technical close” in complex Microsoft Dynamics 365/AX solutions. This individual collaborates with sales and delivery resources to ensure proposed deals include technical solutions that accurately address customer needs, and are appropriately supported by key customer technical decision-makers. They are responsible for achieving a revenue quota as well as objectives comprised of the combined expectations of the sales resources, market, and/or channel supported. The sales territory will encompass pursuits across the nation with a focus on the Food &amp; Beverage industry.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li><div>Works deals assigned to the sales team supported, prioritizing effort based on maximizing total impact on team productivity and profit, or as directed by Practice Leaders or Sales Director.</div></li></ul>
<ul>
<li><div>Assess and respond to Request for Proposals, utilizing product as well as industry knowledge.</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform product demos tailoring software demonstrations to each customer/ prospect. Demonstrate products and technologies effectively to audiences of varied technical knowledge. Incorporate Full MS stack around food &amp; beverage industry and demo this collective software proficiently. Ability to demonstrate and present business solutions to customers and prospects is essential.</div></li>
<li><div>
Create and confidently deliver presentations internally and externally.</div></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><div>Proactively scopes the technical solution required to address customer requirements, assesses customers’ met and unmet needs, and recommends solutions that optimize value for both the customer and the firm.</div></li>
<li><div>
Secures input from all necessary solution stakeholders within the customer firm. Adapts solutions, as necessary, to ensure appropriate support.</div></li>
<li><div>
Help develop and maintain the company’s "first choice advisor" status with customers by leveraging the firm’s ability to meet customer needs.</div></li>
<li><div>
Coordinates closely with internal sales and delivery resources to align solution design with customers’ business requirements.</div></li>
<li><div>
Secures from customer technical staff commitments needed to ensure a deal’s “technical close.”</div></li>
<li><div>
Meets assigned targets for profitable sales growth in assigned product lines, market areas, channel, or teams supported.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provides coaching and professional development to team member sales associates in order to enhance their product knowledge, technical acumen, and technical sales skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Opportunistically pursues additional business development opportunities within clients. Collaborates with sales to ensure these opportunities are effectively covered and advanced.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain strong relationships with Microsoft channel team, partner technology specialists, and partner sales executives.</div></li>
<li><div>
Keep up with Microsoft product announcements and sales incentives and programs. Communicate important product changes to consulting and sales teams.</div></li>
<li>
<div>
Assists in development of go-to-market and sales strategies.</div>
