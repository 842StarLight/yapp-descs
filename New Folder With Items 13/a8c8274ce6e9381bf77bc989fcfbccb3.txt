<h2>STORE MANAGER & OTHER STORIES</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Function</b>: Sales - &amp; Other Stories</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Department:</b> Store</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Reports to:</b> Global &amp; Other Stories Sales Manager</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Direct Reports:</b> Assistant Store Manager, Department Manager, Visual Ambassador, Cash Office Responsible.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Overall Job Function:</b> Responsible for maximizing sales to drive profitability. Leads, manages, and organizes the store according to &amp; Other Stories shared values, standards, policies and procedures along with the &amp; Other Stories Sales Team. Serves as manager on duty, manager of a concept and support on the floor when needed in a Symbolic leader function. Is a &amp; Other Stories brand ambassador by providing excellent customer service and product knowledge at all times.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibility including but not limited to:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
People</p>
<ul>
<li>Actively work to build great teamwork with our values as the basis. Your leadership sets an example for others and you act as a role model when it comes to applying &amp; Other Stories values in your everyday work.</li>
<li>Manages, recruits, on boards and trains store teams</li>
<li>Develops teams in brand, fashion and product knowledge, customer service skills and leadership skills.</li>
<li>Provides succession planning for all staff and management team in the store</li>
<li>Follows up with management team to ensure proper training and staff assessment is being completed</li>
<li>Creates and acts on plans for future people development needs</li>
<li>Maintains an overview of all Employee Relations responsibilities in the store to ensure a positive and motivating work environment</li>
</ul>
<p>Commerciality</p>
<ul>
<li>Actively works with garments, including processing, stocking, replenishing, folding, hanging, steaming, displaying and merchandising per &amp; Other Stories guidelines to maximize selling opportunities</li>
<li>Responsible for overseeing the maintenance and upkeep of concepts</li>
<li>Maximizes sales through joint operational and commercial focus and takes action to obtain highest level of profitability for the store.</li>
<li>Manages and ensures stock level is appropriate to maximize selling</li>
<li>Responsible for pushes of high priority garments when necessary by communicating with the &amp; Other Stories merchandising team.</li>
<li>Ensures the garment presentation, garment level and visual standards are presented according to &amp; Other Stories expectations.</li>
<li>Ensures and follows up on all activities concerning events, promotions and sales activities in his/her store.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Operations</p>
<ul>
<li>Performs cash office operational functions</li>
<li>Ensures proper staff planning according to budget, takt goals and store needs</li>
<li>Establishes monitors and follows up on sales goals, sales plans and sales budgets with &amp; Other Stories Sales Team to optimize profit.</li>
<li>Executes reductions, price changes, and transfers</li>
<li>Unloads delivery truck, receives, opens and unpacks merchandise and labels merchandise with security tags</li>
<li>Follows and executes safety and security routines to prevent loss and guarantees employee and customer safety</li>
<li>Has working knowledge and oversees use of all Company systems in stores</li>
<li>Rings on the register, reports and handles all required transactions, issues receipts and packs merchandise.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Customer Service</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides excellent direct and indirect customer service according to &amp; Other Stories standards on the sales floor, in the fitting room and at the cash point.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Financial Accountability:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establishes and oversees plans, budgets and variable costs for store; works with comparable sales to last year, sales to budget and future sales trends to maximize profitability with the &amp; Other Stories Sales Team</li>
<li>Accurate recording of sales figures</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Candidate Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Graduate or equivalent.</li>
<li>At least-3 years of retail management experience or comparable management experience</li>
<li>Ability to lift in excess of 20 pounds</li>
<li>Ability to stand for long periods of time, bend, stretch, engage in repetitive motions, push, pull and carry items (mannequins, clothing, totes, torsos, etc.) for a short distance</li>
<li>Ability to climb a ladder and use a step stool</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional customer service and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Proven organizational and analytical skills</li>
<li>Consistently shows ability to be sales driven and results-oriented by taking action, relating all decisions back to store results and working to improve profit &amp; loss</li>
<li>Strong garment presentation skills with the ability to drive the business through creativity and extensive fashion knowledge</li>
<li>Ability to take own initiative and take responsibility for actions</li>
<li>Ability to work strategically, tactically and operationally</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task in a fast paced environment and prioritize effectively</li>
<li>Ability to be clear and convincing when communicating goals, information and expectations to staff</li>
<li>Ability to plan and achieve long and short term goals</li>
<li>Ability to coach and apply appropriate developmental tool for each individual</li>
<li>Experience with preparing and administrating progressive discipline process and performance management as well as succession planning</li>
<li>Ability to provide feedback in a constructive and professional way</li>
<li>Ability to handle conflict and resolve problems constructively</li>
<li>Basic computer skills, such as browser navigation, software interaction and data entry are needed</li>
<li>Open availability including evenings and weekends</li>
<li>Around 5 % travel required as necessary (Sales meetings, workshops, etc.)</li>
</ul>
