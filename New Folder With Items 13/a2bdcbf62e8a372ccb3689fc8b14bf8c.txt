The team
<br>Our Finance team plays a critical role in ensuring accurate financial reporting, optimal financial management, and efficient operations across DocuSign. The team consists of Accounting, Financial Planning &amp; Analysis, Revenue Operations, Investor Relations, and Business Operations. The Finance team partners across all organizations in DocuSign to appropriately allocate resources, inform key business decisions, and facilitate the execution of our operational plans.
<br><br>
This position
<br>The Director, Deal Desk is a high-visibility role that works on our high-risk and complex transactions. This essential role sits at the nexus of multiple functions including sales, product management, pricing strategy and legal to ensure that contracts are structured to maximize value. This role enables one to become an expert in DocuSign business practices and develop a thorough understanding of how all the parts of the business fit together. Working with senior sales leadership and executives, the Senior Manager/Director advises, structures and approves complex and high-risk deal proposals on behalf of management. Acting as a trusted advisor and financial problem solver, you will meet with stakeholders to create winning deal structures that maximize partnership opportunities. This is a new function and we are looking for an experienced professional to help build this new team from the ground up.
<br><br>
This position reports to the Sr Director of Business Practices
<br><br>
