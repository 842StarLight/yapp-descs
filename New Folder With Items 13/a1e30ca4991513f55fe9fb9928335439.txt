<div><b>Position Overview
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The technical content creation/writing strategist, is a great entry point into a wide range of roles within Siemens Digital Industries Software. A writer with an engineering or technical background that possesses both the technical aptitude and the ability to generate engaging thought-leadership content is highly sought after, no matter what career path you take in the future. This position offers great visibility within the organization and a potential career path into a variety of possible careers including: digital content writing strategist either at the divisional level or business unit level, sales support specialist, public relations specialist, or Technical Marketing Engineer (TME). Over the past five years, many employees in this role have advanced into key technical roles within two years.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities

A smaller portion of the responsibilities will focus on generating social media content across the portfolio of software products. You will gather research and data, interview subject matter experts, and translate complex information into clear and concise customer-facing content using desktop publishing tools, such as InDesign, for a variety of outbound communications including:</div>
