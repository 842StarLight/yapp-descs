<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Seattle Boat Company is looking for a hard working, motivated individual to take the lead on our Marketing Coordinator position. This person will need to have a passion for seeking new ways to engage with customers, assist in event preparation and marketing tools, have graphic capabilities and have a passion for the boating lifestyle!</p>
<p>Responsibilities Include:<br>· Create, implement and oversee an overarching marketing strategy that encompasses the dealership as a whole as well as individual products, services, programs and events;<br>· Use overall strategy to contribute to the creation of annual marketing plan and marketing calendar with specific deliverables, action items, deadlines and goals;<br>· Create lead-generating marketing messages to build leads.<br>· Develop concept, direction and deliverables for social media strategy;<br>· Provide feedback and direction to all marketing efforts, including website, newsletters, ads, promotions, and all other mediums;<br>· Create concepts for dealerships marketing messages;<br>· Monitor and use metrics from our marketing efforts to adapt and evolve programs and provide leads to the sales or service teams;<br>· Direct the creation, editing and implementation of videos;<br>· Create copy for all newsletters;<br>· Support or planning of customer events, from invitation outreach campaigns through day of event details;<br>· Develop outreach campaigns for new model year information for leads and customers;<br>· Assist and support other staff as necessary;<br>· Some travel as required;<br>· Other tasks as assigned;</p>
<p>Candidates Must:<br>· Advanced education in marketing, advertising, communications and/or retailing<br>· Experience with marketing responsibilities<br>· Ability to communicate at a high level, both verbally and in writing<br>· Be team-oriented, self-motivated, and process driven<br>· Willingness to adapt to irregular hours and a retail work environment<br>· Organized thought process and work environment<br>· Assertive, proactive and creative<br>· Desire to learn more about retailing and the understanding of consumer market trends<br>· Ability to pass a pre-employment background/drug screen<br>Benefits Include:<br>· PTO (sick, holidays and vacation)<br>· 401k Plan<br>· Health Benefits<br>· Uniforms provided</p>
<p>Enjoy working in the recreational marine industry creating Fun and Memorable boating experiences.</p>
