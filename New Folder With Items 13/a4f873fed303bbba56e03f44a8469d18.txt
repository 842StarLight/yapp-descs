<p><b>THIS IS THE CAREER OPPORTUNITY</b></p>
<p><b>Your Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>1. <b>Delivering REAL Legendary Service</b>: Maintains and owns member communication via phone, email and face to face interaction, regarding the status of the member’s mortgage loan, including but not limited to what to expect during the loan process, underwriting requirements/conditions needed to meet TDECU and secondary market guidelines. Creates a REAL Legendary Service experience for members, team members and vendors by maintaining a professional demeanor and delivering service that embodies the REAL culture.</p>
 <p>2. <b>Member communication</b>: Makes initial call to member. Confirms all information on loan application and confirms anticipated closing date. Guides and coaches members through mortgage loan process to ensure on time closing. Answers questions and recognizes issues to escalate to management to ensure a positive member experience. Keeps member applicant and other parties to the loan advised as to the loan status throughout the process. Implements and performs according to the established TDECU REAL standards. Provides updates in a timely manner and follows up on member issues to ensure a successful and efficient process.</p>
<p><b>3. Documentation administration</b>: Receives loan file from Mortgage Loan Officer and reviews file for regulatory compliance with TDECU, Federal, State and investor guidelines and all other loan program required documentation. Completes Loan Officer Accuracy Scorecard, which “grades” Mortgage Loan Officer on application and file completeness.</p>
<p>4. <b>Process administration</b>: Calculates income and reviews asset information. Sends all other required verification requests including verification of deposits, verification of employment, verification of rent, verification of mortgage, etc. Requests payoff statements as needed. Submits file to Underwriter. Communicates underwriting conditions to member and collects specified items.</p>
<p>Ensures all regulatory and underwriting conditions are met by reviewing what member or third party provides and making sure it meets what was specified. Receives clear to close.</p>
<p>Reviews insurance, title, survey, payoff statements, mortgage insurance, Desktop Underwriter and UCDP (if applicable). Makes sure all items meet TDECU and investor guidelines.</p>
<p>5. <b>Quality assurance:</b> Ensures all documentation for mortgage loans is complete and accurate to meet TDECU and secondary market guidelines. Prepares file for closing, communicating with member and closer all remaining outstanding items. Organizes loan file according to department checklists and takes accountability for each item by signing off on each checked off item. Submits a complete and accurate file to closing department. Manages and monitors deadlines to meet closing timelines.</p>
 <p>6. <b>Professional development</b>: Promotes individual professional growth; stays abreast of employment laws. Fosters teamwork and career development within the extended mortgage team. Provide continuous education to internal team members and members regarding TDECU’s Wholesale programs to expand referral pipelines to achieve projected market penetration goals.</p>
<p>7<b>. Service Standards and Best Practices</b>: Supports and maintains service standards for the TDECU Mortgage team. Participates on cross functional teams to represent and serve as an advocate for the mortgage team. Proactively communicates service level agreements and standards to external agency partners. Researches and presents best practices to the mortgage team and the organization to increase quality and efficiency. Effectively manages time and prioritizes tasks to meet all closing dates and ensure positive member experience.</p>
<p>8. <b>Audit &amp; Compliance support</b>: Reviews loan file for compliance with Federal and State regulatory guidelines as well as Investor/Insurer guidelines and credit union policy. Adheres to policy changes for all investor, secondary market, Federal Housing Administration (FHA), Veteran Affairs (VA), Premium Mortgage Insurance (PMI) and TDECU guideline changes.</p>
<p>Completes all mandatory compliance testing within designated time frame and other compliance assignments including Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC), Unusual Activity Reports (UAR), and Security processes. Implements processes as required by the Bank Secrecy Act and TDECU policies and procedures including but not limited to completing and submitting Currency Transaction Reports, being knowledgeable of signs of unusual financial activities, and reporting signs of unusual activity and/or completing reporting for Suspicious Activity Report as appropriate per procedure.</p>
<p>Stays abreast of changes within the credit union relating to services and procedures.</p>
