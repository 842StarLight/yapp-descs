<h2>Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Club Pilates offers group reformer classes in various formats and levels to accommodate everyone seeking to learn Pilates and hone their fitness expertise. Club Pilates is a well-established, nation-wide brand, committed to providing affordable and accessible Pilates to the community.</p>
<p>Club Pilates is currently established in 36 states and 2 countries. Class formats target a wide range of clients’ needs – from young to more senior and beginner to advanced. Club Pilates is one of the first studios to create a 500-hour Teacher Training Program designed to thoroughly and safely teach group Pilates apparatus classes.</p>
<p><b>POSITION: </b></p>
<p>The Operations Manager will handle day to day operations such as client accounts, scheduling and training front desk staff, maintain studio operations including but not limited to equipment maintenance, retail inventory, facility cleaning, and enforcing studio policies and procedures. Under the management of the owner, and the support of the Sales Lead and front desk team, the Operations Manager must exemplify leadership qualities in all areas of the business.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage staff schedule and supervise front desk associates</li>
<li>Implement and manage prospecting procedures</li>
<li>Develop and maintain studio operations procedures</li>
<li>Enforce Club Pilates policies and procedures</li>
<li>Manage studio budget, spending, and all reporting</li>
<li>Assist in recruiting, hiring, training, supervising and coaching all front desk staff</li>
<li>Responsible for delivering studio financial goals through personal contribution</li>
<li>Provide exceptional level of customer service at the studio to existing members via face-to-face service, phone and email.</li>
<li>Build rapport and lasting relationships with prospective and current members.</li>
<li>Resolve escalated member issues</li>
<li>Independently make decisions related to high level customer service</li>
<li>Collect outstanding dues</li>
<li>Maintain retention tracking and reporting (lost sheep)</li>
<li>Proficiency in ClubReady, to include revenue reports, attendance reports, etc.</li>
<li>Maintain equipment, cleanliness and organization of the Studio</li>
<li>Reformer maintenance</li>
<li>Ensure all forms, administrative supplies, and studio literature is stocked and visible</li>
<li>Schedule and participate in networking/community events and studio promotions</li>
<li>Maintain and schedule social media communication strategy (facebook, and instagram)</li>
<li>Any other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of retail/service sales or fitness sales experience.</li>
<li>Confident in generating personal sales and training Sales Reps in sales</li>
<li>Ability to manage and drive 4 revenue streams: memberships, retail, private training, and teacher training</li>
<li>Must be fluent in English and have excellent communication and strong interpersonal skills in person, on the telephone and via email</li>
<li>Ability to excel in a fast changing, diverse environment.</li>
<li>Ability to recognize areas of improvement and make changes using good judgement.</li>
<li>An affinity and passion for fitness.</li>
<li>Solid writing and grammar skills.</li>
<li>Highly organized, proficient in data management, ability to prioritize and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Professional, punctual, reliable and neat.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and accuracy.</li>
<li>Trustworthy and ability to handle confidential information.</li>
<li>Ability to work harmoniously with co-workers, clients and the general public.</li>
<li>Proficiency with computers and Studio software.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>COMPENSATION &amp; BENEFITS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive hourly rate and performance plus opportunity to bonus</li>
<li>Commission paid on sales</li>
<li>Complimentary pilates membership while employed</li>
<li>Employee discounts on retail merchandise</li>
</ul>
