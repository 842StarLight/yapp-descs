<div><b>Job Summary</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Pricing / Item Coordinator reports to the Wine and Beer Pricing Managers and is responsible for the administration of Supplier Pricing information inclusive of Wholesale, Purchase Allowances, FOB, and Discounts (NA’s), as well as the accurate and complete setup of new items in all warehouses that are authorized for their sale. They will manage a portfolio of suppliers and interact with internal Sales VP’s and Directors, the Supplier Rep and the Supply Chain team to insure critical New Item information is accurate, costed and priced correctly and ready for order. The Pricing Coordinator also assists in the production of price lists.<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Receive approved request for new item setup and review for completeness. Resolve any questions with the Supplier, Sales Team or Wine and Beer Pricing Leads.</li>
<li>Set up new item as per agreed to procedures and guidelines and copy into all authorized warehouses.</li>
<li>When all setup is complete including item attributes, order attributes and costing/pricing communicate to the organization via the new item email.</li>
<li>Enter new item pricing into the system as required to achieve accuracy and consistency across all databases. Assure the agreed to product cost supports the legal wholesale and retail margin at the go to market price requested by Heidelberg’s Supplier Partners and Sales Leadership.</li>
<li>Process pricing change requests as approved by Sales Management.</li>
<li>Process item attribute or description changes as they are communicated by the Supplier, Sales Team or Supply Chain.</li>
<li>Manage annual updates to supplier pricing ensuring sufficient lead time to support purchasing and chain pricing.</li>
<li>Support the analysis and implementation of needed changes to resolve pricing trouble tickets for their suppliers that have resulted from invoice variances.</li>
<li>Support Chain Pricing Specialists in making sure authorized items are available and complete in support of their duties.</li>
<li>Follow departmental best practices that maintain compliance, efficiency and timeliness.</li>
<li>Prepare needed metrics to support individual responsibilities and/or departmental metrics and report on work effort regularly to Supervisor.<br>
<br>

<br>
</li>
</ul>
