<h2>Clinical Informatics Manager (Business Analyst IV)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>A challenging and rewarding position exists for an individual with recent clinical and technical experience to work as a member of the Informatics team. The Fairfax-Falls Church CSB is looking for a dynamic and effective manager to lead CSB providers and staff in the design, implementation, adoption and integration of information systems and clinical technology to optimize patient care delivery in collaboration with operational and clinical leaders. Applies in-depth knowledge of the functional aspects of application systems and health system clinical and financial operations to optimize clinical workflow and user efficiency for the existing CSB electronic health record, "Credible". Serves as the agency wide expert in integrating, managing and coordinating complex issues and solutions among clinical, nonclinical and IT staff related to the CSB's electronic health record (EHR), "Credible". Additionally, this position requires the selected individual perform the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures accurate maintenance of clinical information in the EHR, coordinates with reimbursement services, and sets direction of training for agency clinical staff on the electronic health record.
</li>
<li>Directs and manages the development of appropriate clinical and technical business practices, utilizing process mapping techniques and integrates new and existing technology with business processes.
</li>
<li>Utilizes high level clinical knowledge, skills and abilities to improve overall quality and integrity of agency data management, and insures compliance with federal, state and local reporting requirements.
</li>
<li>Interfaces with staff responsible for CSB compliance and risk management to ensure compliance with licensure and other regulatory requirements.
</li>
<li>Provides supervision and support to business analysts in the field to improve staff support, training and problem solving.
</li>
<li>Participates on teams involved in a range of activities, including implementation of new programs and integration of such programs' business processes in the field, auditing of the electronic health record, and management of agency clinical data, and leads/manages those teams where appropriate.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates technical ability and skill to set up and maintain clinical tables/data/forms in the CSB's EHR and other agency-supported applications, including but not limited to service types for billing and diagnosis. Provides expertise and support to reimbursement team and clinical staff to correct and update technological problems that impacts revenue.
</li>
<li>Meets and maintains professional standards and federal, state, county and CSB statutes, regulations, policies and procedures
</li>
<li>Supports continuity of operations (COOP) planning requirements for disaster recovery and collaborates with other County agencies as needed.</li>
</ul>
Participates in the project planning and implementation of all clinical integration projects/tasks to align the EHR configuration to accept new or updated clinical services, allow compliant and error-free billing and detailed data reporting capability. Serves as a liaison to collaborate and coordinate between multiple agency clinical support service areas and the informatics and revenue reimbursement teams. Individual must have a strong working knowledge of setup and configuration of an EHR supporting behavioral health to include mental health services, developmental disabilities, and substance abuse. As local, state, and federal healthcare requirements constantly change, the individual in this position must be able to analyze, comprehend and understand the impact of those changes to make appropriate and reliable recommendations for EHR configuration changes that maximize the effectiveness of clinical staff to timely implement new or updated compliant programs.<br>
<br>
<b>Note:</b> To learn more about careers that make a difference, watch our video "CSB: Making a Difference in Our Community".</div>
