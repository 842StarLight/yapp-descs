<h2>Commissary Production Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>North Liberty Location</p>
<p><b>
Working Environment:</b></p>
<p>
The Commissary Production Manager oversees all facilities, team members and supervisors of a commissary style environment (bakery and kitchen) located in North Liberty, IA. This includes commercial food production equipment, mixers, ovens, forklifts, delivery vehicles etc. The operation is high volume and fast paced serving multiple locations with timely deliveries while ensuring product quality. Maintaining strong ties and positive working relationships with our retail units will be critical for success.</p>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 5 years supervisory experience - hiring, training, coaching, evaluating, leading.</li>
<li>A minimum of 5 years experience in high volume food production or manufacturing in the natural or conventional food industries.</li>
<li>Excellent problem solving, communication, internal and external customer service skills.</li>
<li>Understanding of food costing methods, experience with ChefTec software and ability to use sales data to keep pace with changing demand at multiple retail locations.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of state and local health codes, food safety, allergen and dietary restrictive issues.</li>
<li>Practical understanding of Microsoft Office suite, with intermediate to advanced understanding of MS Excel.</li>
<li>Highly organized with the ability to analyze systems and procedures, efficiently implement changes, and establish best practices.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to create and develop productive teams.</li>
<li>Ability to mentor, motivate, and inspire a diverse and creative production and culinary team.</li>
<li>Familiarity with Quality Assurance practices.</li>
<li>Flexible and adaptive to meet the scheduling requirements of a production facility.</li>
</ul>
<p>The Commissary Production Manager reports directly to the Prepared Foods Manager. This is a full time, professional, salaried position with benefits. Linn Street, Unit 2A, Iowa City, IA 52240.</p>
