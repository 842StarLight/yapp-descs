<h2>Rocky Mountain Vibes – Public Relations & Media Relations Internship (Colo. Springs, CO)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Rocky Mountain Vibes – Public Relations &amp; Media Relations Internship (Colo. Springs, CO)</b></div>
<div><b>
Start Date: Roughly April 1st, 2020</b></div>
<div><b>
End Date: Roughly September 30th, 2020</b></div>
<div><b>
Paid position: $12/hr at 40 hours per week (hours will vary in-season) and 5% commission on sales made.</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Job Description:</b></div>
<div>
The 2020 Rocky Mountain Vibes Public Relations and Media Relations Internship is designed to teach the operation of a Minor League Baseball PR department through an immediate hands-on approach. The PR intern works closely with the Director of Public Relations &amp; Media Relations in all areas and upon completion of the program should possess the skills to successfully organize and operate a Minor League Baseball team’s Public Relations program.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
The PR Intern/Assistant will be responsible for the following but not limited to:</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
April – May (Pre-Season)</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
There are many operational responsibilities during this time that the PR intern will be responsible for so time management is vital. Below is a list of various operational responsibilities the PR intern can expect in the pre-season although it does not cover everything.</div>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Organizing press box meals</li>
<li>Compiling up-to-date media contact lists</li>
<li>Soliciting and fulfilling all media credential requests</li>
<li>Writing stories for the Vibes program and website</li>
<li>Prepare all baseball card backs for potential 2020 players</li>
<li>Assisting with the editing and organization of all Vibes print materials including program and media guide</li>
<li>Training on GameDay software and becoming proficient with it</li>
<li>Become familiar with stats website, game notes, and post-game recaps</li>
<li>Become proficient in preparing stat packs</li>
<li>Research and study Vibes history and potential 2020 players</li>
<li>Assisting in sales for 2020 season</li>
<li>Assist in running the team’s social media platforms in a fun, professional manner</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
June – September (Season)</b></div>
<div>
During the season, all of our preparations are put into action and the real fun begins. Unlike most of the other areas within the ball club, the PR department is constantly at work. Our days are longer and our responsibilities continue to add up no matter where we are in the season. Most of the time we’re the first people in the office every morning and the last to leave and our department takes pride in this fact. Whether the team is on the road or in town, the PR staff is always working to put forward the most professional operation in Minor League Baseball.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Below are some of the responsibilities the PR Assistant will have during the season. As before, this is a short list and the PR Assistant should expect to handle much more than this:</div>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Cleaning and maintaining a professional atmosphere in the press box</li>
<li>Stocking the drink cooler in the press box and organizing press box meals</li>
<li>Produce or assist with daily stat packs, game notes, rosters and lineups for all games</li>
<li>Write and maintain 2020 bios for every Vibes player</li>
<li>Organize all player photos when received from team photographer</li>
<li>Act as the back-up team travel coordinator</li>
<li>Assisting the media with interview requests</li>
<li>Maintaining a positive and professional relationship with the Vibes and visiting players and coaching staff</li>
<li>Writing press releases, game recaps, and stories on a regular basis</li>
<li>Act as a back-up for the GameDay software stringer</li>
<li>Run the video board line score and press box microphone during home games</li>
<li>At conclusion of season, produce final game notes, Year in Review, player bios and other items as needed</li>
<li>Assist in running the team’s social media platforms in a fun, professional manner</li>
</ul>
