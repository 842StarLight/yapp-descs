<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Laboratory Technician is an integral part of the Laboratory Operations team and through collaboration with others is responsible for executing the objectives of the Research and Development group. This position will be involved in conducting lab experiments as assigned and be responsible to accurately document results. The Laboratory Technician may be asked to assist in preparing standard laboratory procedures, keep detailed record of experiments, and will report on project results to relevant stakeholders. The Laboratory Technician is expected to be familiar with the scientific background and literature relevant to the projects they are engaged. The work of a Laboratory Technician is directed and evaluated by a senior R&amp;D laboratory member.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<p>
Perform Laboratory Tests and Processes: Perform laboratory tests as part of the Research and Development team in order to produce reliable and precise data to support scientific investigations. These tasks must be executed accurately and following strict methodologies to carry out analyses. Techniques may include, but are not limited to, PCR analysis and nucleic acid sample preparation.</p>
<p>
Document and Present Results: Experimental procedures and subsequent results will be accurately recorded and, when possible, the Laboratory Technician may be asked to interpret results and present key findings to senior laboratory members.</p>
<p>
Product or Instrument Chemistry Validations: Conduct required chemistry test procedures for validation of instrumentation prior to sale.</p>
<p>
Assist in Creating Standard Operating Procedures: Help create, update, or maintain standard operating procedures for the laboratory. Propose new ideas to improve overall laboratory efficiency.</p>
<p>
Manage Laboratory Supplies: Assist the Laboratory Manager in maintaining inventory of laboratory supplies. This will include managing materials provided by the laboratory for internal support of other areas of the company including engineering, production, and service.</p>
<p>
Maintain Laboratory Equipment: Perform any required routine checks to keep laboratory equipment performing at a high level. This may include weekly or monthly monitoring and execution of basic instrument maintenance routines. This position will assist the Laboratory Manager in ensuring all instruments are adhering to calibration and PM schedules.</p>
<p>
Follow Laboratory Safety Practices: Adhere to laboratory safety policies and work with the Laboratory Manager and Safety Coordinator to implement new safety procedures as required.</p>
