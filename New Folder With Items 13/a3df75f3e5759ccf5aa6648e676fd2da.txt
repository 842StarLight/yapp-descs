<h2>Producer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p>
Title: Producer</p>
<p>
Location: New York, NY</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are we?</b></p>
<p>
Live Nation Entertainment is the world’s leading live entertainment company, comprised of global market leaders: Ticketmaster, Live Nation Concerts, and Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship. Ticketmaster is the global leader in event ticketing with over 500 million tickets sold annually and more than 12,000 clients worldwide. Live Nation Concerts is the largest provider of live entertainment in the world promoting more than 30,000 shows and 95+ festivals annually for nearly 4,000 artists in over 40 countries. These businesses allow Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship to create strategic music marketing programs that connect over 900 brands with the 86 million fans that attend Live Nation Entertainment events each year. For additional information, visit www.livenationentertainment.com.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Entertainment, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
The Event Producer will work with Senior Producer to advance, manage and direct successful executions of all experiential activations and/or custom events assigned. He/She will coordinate with Sales, Account Management, and Creative Solutions teams as well as liaise with relevant venue and festival contacts. Programs range from one-off events at Live Nation venues or unique locations, experiential sponsor activations at music festivals, traveling brand presence on sponsored artist tours, high-end parties for clients, music events created for content capture and more.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Liaise with client, account management team, marketing solutions and venues for each specific assignment.</li>
<li>
Lead RFP process for fabrication including evaluation and granting.</li>
<li>
Execution of budgets for events or activations (vendors, permits, labor, fabrication, etc.).</li>
<li>
Advance all production and operational elements such as power, heavy equipment, WIFI, for activation with festival staff.</li>
<li>
Procure all supplies, staffing, custom décor items, trucking, lighting, audio as per budget constraints.</li>
<li>
Manage all on site staff including Brand Ambassadors, Security, FOH staff, stagehands, etc. Ensure training as needed and onsite supervision.</li>
<li>
Must have clear understanding of logistics, fabrication, installation/tear down in multiple environments.</li>
<li>
Monitor competitive trends in the event business; identify and target emerging new marketing, and experiential trends.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA Degree required.</li>
<li>
5 - 8+ years of experience in event and experiential concepting, budgeting and production with an established record of success.</li>
<li>
Superior project and time management skills.</li>
<li>
Adept in Microsoft Office applications including Word, PowerPoint and especially Excel.</li>
<li>
Experience working with recognizable global brands.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of music and entertainment marketing preferred.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work calmly and efficiently under pressure.</li>
<li>
Must be available to work occasional weekends and evenings, with ability to travel.</li>
<li>
Highly effective at managing multiple projects at one time.</li>
<li>
Team player orientation.</li>
<li>
Ability to leverage existing industry relationships.</li>
</ul>
