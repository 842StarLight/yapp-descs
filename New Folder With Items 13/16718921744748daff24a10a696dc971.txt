<h2>Counter Manager, Bloomingdale's Norwalk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Cosmetic Counter Manager is responsible for supervising the complete operations of the cosmetics line to ensure a high level of productivity and customer satisfaction through extensive product knowledge and demonstrating exceptional Selling Skills. The associate is also responsible for fulfillment and performing other duties as necessary.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
All hourly associates are eligible to receive a quarterly bonus incentive, colleague discounts and the opportunity to be a part of an iconic brand. Certain criteria must be met to receive the quarterly bonus incentive.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Be proficient in POS and MPOS systems including Search and Send, and B*connected</li>
<li>Proactive in engaging the customer in using devices and technology to enhance the shopping experience.</li>
<li>Assist customers in all aspects of total store fulfillment and have the ability to satisfy the needs of our digital and physical customers by making appropriate partnerships when necessary.</li>
<li>Provide an exceptional customer experience by ensuring the customer is always the priority</li>
<li>Drive top line sales by consistently demonstrating exceptional Selling Skills</li>
<li>Acknowledge customers in a friendly and helpful manner upon their entry into the area</li>
<li>Assist Beauty Advisor in converting potential return into a sale through courtesy and professionalism</li>
<li>Partner with Sales Staff to complete sales and add on items to increase IPT</li>
<li>Assist in training, coaching, motivating, and developing new and incumbent associates on company policies and procedures, product knowledge, and department productivity goals</li>
<li>Review and analyze business performance of daily, weekly, monthly, seasonal, and annual sales results</li>
<li>Review advertising calendar and ensure proper execution of events</li>
<li>Ensure accuracy of promotional presentation; communicate advertising and events information to associates</li>
<li>Assist in the coordination of operational tasks as required such as, putting out new merchandise, sales &amp; event set-up &amp; damages</li>
<li>Ensure timely processing of new receipts, damages, testers, and return to vendors through delegation to self and staff</li>
<li>Develop and implement business driving events to achieve department goals</li>
<li>Supervise proper presentation, organization, storing, and replenishment of stock</li>
<li>Sell merchandise through effective customer service techniques and professional selling skills</li>
<li>Communicate regularly with vendors, planners, distributors, and buyers regarding stock needs, customer preferences, &amp; special events</li>
<li>Develop a repeat-business customer base in order to regularly communicate: upcoming events, new merchandise receipts and product replenishment to increase incremental sales</li>
<li>Participate in physical inventory process</li>
<li>Demonstrate a professional, can-do attitude</li>
<li>Maintain high customer readiness standards: deliver a clean, neat and easy-to-shop store environment</li>
<li>Adhere to Asset Protection and inventory control and compliance procedures</li>
<li>Ensure shortage prevention initiatives are executed</li>
<li>Perform these functions in an efficient manner, as directed by the Supervisor</li>
<li>Regular, dependable attendance and punctuality</li>
<li>Perform other duties as necessary</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<p><b>
Education/Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School degree or equivalent.</li>
<li>A minimum of one year of previous selling experience is required, preferably in cosmetics.</li>
<li>Previous supervisory is also preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Communication Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to interpret instructional documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Mathematical Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Basic math functions such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.</li>
<li>Able to use a calculator.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Reasoning Ability:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Self-starter</li>
<li>Able to work independently and as part of a team.</li>
<li>Must have good time management skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Physical Demands:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This position involves constant moving and standing.</li>
<li>Involves standing for at least two consecutive hours.</li>
<li>Involves lifting at least 30 lbs</li>
<li>May occasionally involve reaching above eye level, stooping, kneeling, crouching, and climbing ladders.</li>
<li>Involves close vision, color vision, depth perception, and focus adjustment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Other Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Superior organizational and time management skills.</li>
<li>Must be able to multi-task in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Must be able to build relationships and influence others.</li>
<li>Must possess a thirst for knowledge.</li>
<li>Must be able to lead by example.</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate and function as a member of a team.</li>
<li>Must possess a strong sense of urgency</li>
<li>Should be comfortable with the use of computers and frequent use of RF equipment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Work Hours:</b></p>
<ul><li>Flexible with scheduling and available to work retail hours, which may include day, evening, weekends, and/or holidays based on department and store/company needs.</li></ul>
