<h2>Planning Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY: </b>While a job description may not fully encompass every detail of a position, the purpose of the following is to provide an outline of goals, responsibilities, ability and knowledge required in order to successfully perform this job. The Planning Coordinator will work within Aquafil’s Planning team, which is responsible for production planning and scheduling. Specifically, this role will perform planning and scheduling for BCF Spinning, helping to ensure the highest possible levels of efficiency.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES &amp; DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate MRP flow by product via SAP, Fast Planner, GORV forecast and other planning tools.</li>
<li>Schedule campaigns for the BCF Spinning towers and master batch areas according to needs.</li>
<li>Maintain BCF Spinning and master capacities alongside managers (for optimal efficiency output)</li>
<li>Notify manufacturing of customer order changes, as appropriate via the schedule and e-mails</li>
<li>Support and maintain inventory turns of polymers, masters and other consumable materials</li>
<li>Review production stream lead times to ensure extruded inventory is within on time deliveries</li>
<li>Issue picking list for made to stock materials that are to be allocated to sales orders via booking.</li>
<li>Maintain excellent internal and external communication and customer service with timely responses and a sense of urgency.</li>
<li>Maintain great people and business skills with effective communication within the department and among other departments.</li>
<li>Maintain a high level of professionalism and work as a team to build solid working relationships.</li>
<li>Follow all safety rules for department and entire facility.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>MARGINAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with management in coordinating and planning production activities to improve runtime.
</li>
<li>Plan production tasks and schedules according to customer orders.
</li>
<li>Develop and review production plan with Managers for approval.
</li>
<li>Monitor the execution of production plan and resolve any potential issues in a timely fashion.
</li>
<li>Escalate complex issues to Manager for resolution.
</li>
<li>Investigate production problems, analyze root causes and help provide solutions.
</li>
<li>Communicate production status to Managers and Customers on regular basis.
</li>
<li>Analyze back orders, current orders and upcoming orders to prioritize, plan, and schedule the production operations.
</li>
<li>Coordinate with Managers in planning and implementing engineering changes in production lines.
</li>
<li>Plan inventory management processes to avoid shortages and excesses.
</li>
<li>Monitor production progress and ensure that final product meets quality standards and customer specifications.
</li>
<li>Adhere to company standards and production policies to meet company objectives.
</li>
<li>Analyze delays and interruptions and accordingly adjust production schedule to meet deadlines.
</li>
<li>Maintain reports for production planning activities and sequences.</li>
<li>Adhere to all ISO requirements to maintain compliance.</li>
<li>Assist the department to strive for excellence in fulfillment of on time deliveries and meeting customer’s expectations.</li>
<li>Assist in other duties and projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL QUALIFICATIONS, KNOWLEDGE &amp; EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent required.</li>
<li>2-5 years of experience in scheduling production.</li>
<li>Manufacturing and planning experience preferred</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office Software: Word, Excel, Access, PowerPoint, etc.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of SAP and planning software.</li>
<li>Must pass a drug screen and background check</li>
</ul>
