<h2>Staff Accountant (AR and GL)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Integrated Practice Solutions, Inc. (dba ChiroTouch), a fast-growing and leading healthcare software company, is looking for a Staff Accountant (Accounts Receivable/General Ledger) to join our Finance &amp; Accounting team. In this role, you will focus primarily on AR, but will also conduct GL and month-end duties as needed.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Review billing subscriptions for accuracy and process the provisioning or de-provisioning for each subscription</li>
<li>
Review and process refund requests promptly and in accordance with company procedure and policy</li>
<li>
Process customer cancellation agreements</li>
<li>
Work across departments to research and promptly resolve AR disputes by addressing non-standard, complex issues submitted by both customers and internal stakeholders</li>
<li>
Prepare monthly sales commission calculations</li>
<li>
Assist with the month-end close process</li>
<li>
Maintains various accounting schedules (i.e. prepaid, accrual, fixed asset schedules)</li>
<li>
Responsible for GL account reconciliations.</li>
<li>
Assist in the documentation of new and existing processes</li>
<li>
Assist with the process and preparation of annual audit and other regularly required audits</li>
<li>
Participate in special projects, as needed</li>
<li>
10% travel</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree in Accounting preferred</li>
<li>
2+ years in an accounting role (Preferred)</li>
<li>
Experience in high growth environments through organic initiatives or acquisitions a plus</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail; high level of accuracy</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office, especially MS Excel (Pivots, Vlookups)</li>
<li>
Comfortable performing ad-hoc research and analysis</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively collaborate well across business functions</li>
<li>
A self-starter with high attention to detail and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Technology savvy with experience working with ERP/financial management systems; advanced knowledge of accounting and reporting software especially SFDC, NetSuite, and Zuora</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to communicate verbally and in writing throughout all levels of the organization, both internally and externally</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task, think independently and problem solve in a result-driven team-oriented environment</li>
<li>
Previous experience with Zuora a plus</li>
<li>
Ability to work well under pressure and meet strict deadlines</li>
<li>
Ability to reprioritize and change direction as business needs change</li>
</ul>
