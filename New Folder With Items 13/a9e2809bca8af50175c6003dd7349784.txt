<h2>Digital Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About Tractor Zoom</b><br>Tractor Zoom’s mission is to take the pain out of finding and valuing heavy equipment through powerful, easy to use software solutions. AgTech and FinTech, in harmony.</p>
<p><b>We’re Looking for an ‘A’ Player</b><br>As the Digital Marketing Manager, you will be accountable for the strategy, development and execution of demand generation programs to drive awareness and demand for www.tractorzoom.com and www.ironcomps.com products and services. Through online and offline campaigns, you will directly impact our revenue goals. You will work closely with our sales leadership, oversee content strategy and help develop the Tractor Zoom and Iron Comps customer acquisition and retention strategies.</p>
<p><b>Your Responsibilities: </b><br>*</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for designing multi-channel, multi-touch demand creation programs that leverage both inbound and outbound tactics in support of revenue goals.</li>
<li>Identify, implement, test, measure and optimize new growth tactics and marketing channels to deliver revenue at or above monthly targets.</li>
<li>Forecast, measure, analyze and report on the impact of demand creation activities on sales pipeline, revenue and sales cycle length.</li>
<li>Implement testing methodologies to enhance the demand creation process, including response and conversion rates while continually improving content effectiveness, campaigns and creative strategy.</li>
<li>Work with sales to implement marketing and sales process, track and manage lead flow, and increase conversion rates at each stage of marketing and sales engagement. *</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Requirements: </b><br>*</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated track record of success directly managing demand generation campaigns.</li>
<li>Clear understanding of ROI analysis and proven analytical ability to track multiple campaigns, analyze results, and recommend changes.</li>
<li>Advanced understanding and experience with social media, online advertising,HubSpot, SEO, SEM and Google Analytics or other relevant tools.</li>
<li>Knowledge of best practices for sales and marketing operations, automation and workflow processes; understanding &amp; experience with lead management, marketing automation and demand generation with focus on success of impact/revenue.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience partnering with sales on campaigns driving revenue growth.</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of lead scoring and lead nurturing best practices.</li>
<li>Strong project management and planning skills to manage multiple projects simultaneously for seamless execution.</li>
<li>Ability to turn data into marketing intelligence and action-oriented analysis</li>
<li>Ability to think critically and communicate complex information.</li>
<li>Strong communication and written skills.</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years in demand generation or marketing operations roles, software-as- a-service marketing experience preferred</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or related field. *</li>
</ul>
