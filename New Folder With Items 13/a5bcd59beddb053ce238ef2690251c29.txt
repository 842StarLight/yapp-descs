<h2>Field Service Technician/Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div><div>
<div><b><i>Imagine . . . being part of an organization that equips those who defend our freedoms . . .<br>
</i></b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Moog </b>is a global supplier of military hardware and software integrating turret weapon solutions, system integration and precision motion control for ground defense platforms. From concept phase to prototype builds and ground testing, we support each mission as scientific, technical and operational facilitators.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>With a high demand of our products both with US Military and globally, Moog is positioned for growth in the new era of multiple weapons platform. Our team of seasoned system, mechanical and electrical engineers are dedicated to serve needs of our growing customers.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
We are looking for <b>Field Service Technician/Representative. </b>You will be responsible for technical support and on-site repair/diagnostic activities at various remote locations working in potentially harsh environmental conditions. You will train system users on operation of equipment designed and developed by Moog. When not in the field, you will perform Assembly and Test of equipment in our SLC location.<div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>
Key Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assess and responds to product critical situations in a timely, professional manner
</li>
<li>Diagnose complex technical problems, collect relevant diagnostic data, clearly communicate the issue and data to engineers, and implement solutions
</li>
<li>Train personnel in the operation of Moog hardware/systems for field operation.
</li>
<li>Responsibility for systems and components, with hardware values ranging from $5K to $1.5M
</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to travel to CONUS and OCONUS locations</li>
<li>
Perform field installations, maintenance, repairs, retrofits, and other service related activities at the customer’s facilities/field locations</li>
<li>
Inspect and verify completed field work to ensure operational and safety integrity of serviced equipment</li>
<li>
Assist in the definition and procurement of appropriate spares, tools, and equipment to support anticipated field servicing requirements of Moog products</li>
<li>
Inform Engineering and Management of potential performance, service life, misuse, and safety issues based on service history and field observations.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Key challenges:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Willingness to travel, at minimum notice, to any part of the world to perform any job related function deemed necessary by the customer or management.</li>
<li>
Work in temperature extremes for outside working conditions, such as in the desert for weeks at a time in temperatures exceeding 100 degrees.</li>
<li>
May spend in excess of 100 days in the field annually (CONUS and OCONUS).</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Detailed Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Follows through on commitments and communicates clearly with customers when solving problems</li>
<li>
Participates effectively in issue tracking and field experience information sharing</li>
<li>
Represents the organization in providing solutions to difficult technical issues associated with specific products and processes</li>
<li>
Possesses strong human relations and communication skills necessary to inter¬face with a wide range of technical and non-technical customers at all organization levels
</li>
<li>Refined interpretive, analytical, and diagnostic skills specific to systems involving electrical, mechanical, and computer interfaces.
</li>
<li>Skilled and/or knowledgeable in the use of various types of electronic and mechanical assembly, test, and measuring equipment/processes.</li>
<li>
Quickly develops a sound working knowledge of a Moog products and applications with minimal engineering documentation and short production run quantities and schedule constraints</li>
<li>
Practical working knowledge of MS Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Outlook</li>
<li>
US Citizenship is required</li>
</ul>
<div></div>

<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Ex-military</li>
<li>
Security clearance – secret level</li>
<li>
Education: Technician degree or military equivalent</li>
<li>
Broad technical background with experience that is applicable to relevant systems is important
</li>
<li>Understand the role and content of other disciplines on the performance of the system</li>
<li>
Ability to transform the root cause and corrective action into customer instructions by service bulletins</li>
</ul>
