<p><b>Business Development Agent</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Marina Landscape, Inc. is looking to hire a highly-motivated individual for the Landscape Construction Business Development Agent position for our Northern California Branch. This exempt job classification is responsible for increasing revenue and profit as a result of the development and implementation of an effective marketing campaign, sales efforts, and customer-oriented internal systems; performs other work as required.</p>
<p><b>
Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Achieves sales goals;</li>
<li>
provides short-term and long-range market forecasts and reports by generates and maintains a marketing calendar;</li>
<li>
completes job walks;</li>
<li>
prepares and reviews bid proposals, and delivers them to customers in a timely manner;</li>
<li>
attends client, pre-bid;</li>
<li>
scope review meetings;</li>
<li>
conducts market research (e.g., data collection, analysis and interpretation), and determining and evaluating market trends; creates awareness through public relations efforts;</li>
<li>
remains abreast of industry trends;</li>
<li>
performs contract negotiations;</li>
<li>
develops new business contacts;</li>
<li>
monitors and increases capture rate;</li>
<li>
tracks competitors' pricing and infrastructure;</li>
<li>
develops and executes an annual sales plan that targets a diverse and profitable portfolio;</li>
<li>
tracks all proposals, contacts, and leads;</li>
<li>
conducts sales forecasting;</li>
<li>
develops and implements marketing program, procedures, and training;</li>
<li>
prepares reports and makes formal presentations to executives, staff, and clients;</li>
<li>
utilizes computer skills, information technology, and MS Office applications effectively;</li>
<li>
provides excellent service (e.g., accurate, complete, and timely) to clients, work-site partners, and company staff in a courteous and efficient manner;</li>
<li>
fill out quarterly Commission Report</li>
</ul>
