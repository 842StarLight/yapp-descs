Position Summary<br>
Under the direction of the Associate Director of Operations, the Scheduling Coordinator provides assistance in planning, scheduling, organizing, and coordinating all events in the Santos Manuel Student Union (SMSU). Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, maintaining contact with clients, managing event contracts, scheduling and conducting pre-event meetings as needed, generating sales revenue, and coordinating efforts between SMSU Event Operations, Facilities, Audio Visual, and Building Managers. This position is the main point of contact for a large volume of walk-in traffic, phone, and electronic inquiries. Working in a fast-paced environment, this individual provides excellent, thorough assistance to students, faculty, staff, and community members to ensure quality customer service.<br>
<br>
Evaluates facility requests and determines appropriate facility and service requirements<br>
Assists clients with all aspects of event planning and conference coordination, including explanation of the reservation process and policies, facility selection, and compilation of estimated charges<br>
Makes arrangements for events including meeting facilities, audio visual equipment, tours, catering referrals, setup requirements using a web-based diagramming software, and staffing<br>
Completes all documentation related to an event including, but not limited to, invoicing, verification of certificates of liability, diagrams, University Police notifications, and parking arrangements<br>
Hires, trains, and supervises the Scheduling Office paraprofessional staff. Provides a developmental work environment with appropriately differentiated supervisory and task levels for Lead Student Assistants<br>
Serves as an expert on the SMSU Facility Use Policy and ensures adherence to the policy including contacting and tracking groups when violations occur<br>
Develops, promotes, and maintains a network of relationships with numerous CSUSB departments whose participation is essential for successful events. These areas include, but are not limited to, Special Events and Guest Services, the Office of Student Engagement, Environmental Health and Safety, Transportation Services, University Police, Campus Dining, Information Technology Services<br>
Assists in coordinating safety and security issues in collaboration with University Police, Risk Management, and Environmental Health and Safety<br>
Provides fiscal management for Scheduling budget and monitors budget as appropriate. Prepares annual budget requests and quarterly budget projections<br>
May be required to assist with setup and breakdowns which will require lifting of tables, chairs and other items<br>
Assists the Associate Director of Operations in developing and implementing a strategic sales plan for internal and external clients<br>
Contributes to the overall development of policies, procedures, and guidelines to build a successful event services program. Provides constructive modifications or suggestions for improvements in methods, techniques, offered services, and procedures<br>
Provides advice, and exercises independent judgement in resolving scheduling conflicts or grievances. Responds to customer concerns and provides appropriate resolutions to problems<br>
Conducts facilities tours and represents the department to the campus community, public, other organizations, and potential users<br>
Serves as a campus expert for EMS and coordinates training of campus clients and student organizations as needed<br>
Maintains and updates EMS scheduling software as needed<br>
Serves on campus-wide committees as assigned by the Associate Director of Operations<br>
Understands the vision of the SMSU, University and Division of Student Affairs and works towards the achievement of that vision<br>
Performs other duties as assigned<br>
<br>
Minimum Qualifications<br>
Bachelor's degree or 2 years of equivalent professional experience in a higher education setting<br>
Attentiveness to detail<br>
Excellent organizational skills<br>
Strong customer service orientation<br>
Demonstrated ability to handle multiple tasks, set priorities, and meet deadlines<br>
Effective written and oral communication<br>
Ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships with on and off campus clients<br>
<br>
Preferred Qualifications<br>
Knowledge and understanding of meeting and event planning principles, requirements, procedures, policies, and available resources<br>
Experience with Event Management System (EMS) scheduling software<br>
Experience with audio visual equipment<br>
