<h2>Senior Manager, Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>b8ta is a new company created to fix an old problem—brick-and-mortar retail. Our mission is to make retail accessible for all. We believe when makers have easy, direct access to physical retail, customers will in turn have unbiased access to what the world is creating.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As a technology company building a better retail stores and software for the world’s most innovative brands, our team is full of extraordinary thinkers, creators, builders, early adopters, and rule-breakers. We're based in San Francisco and New York with multiple flagship and small format stores across the country—and we’re just getting started. Think you can help change retail? We'd love to meet you!</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Senior Manager, Operations at b8ta is a project management superstar who is responsible for the development and execution of various, sometimes disparate, company initiatives. A key role in the success of the business, this person is persistent, curious and adept identifying customer and operational needs; develops and socializes business plans; breaks down barriers that impact performance; measures progress and corrects processes accordingly; establishes contingency plans; and champions adaptability. A successful candidate will possess outstanding attention to detail, is willing to learn and manage a multitude of products and software tools, works autonomously, and is comfortable with hands-on tasks.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Responsible for implementing technology strategy across the organization, from networking and IT to software applications to store operations (point-of-sale, analytics, content management)
</li>
<li>Oversee internal and external teams during design, construction and setup of retail outlets, ensuring timely completion of tasks and successful opening</li>
<li>
Source providers and execute pilot programs for evaluation of the latest services and solutions to maintain our best-in-class products</li>
<li>
Manage key vendors for provision of services and utilities, and procurement of fixed assets and consumable supplies for outfitting retail stores</li>
<li>
Assist with the high-level strategy of all distribution operations in support of physical retail outlets, warehouses and e-commerce businesses</li>
<li>
Coordinate supply chain stakeholders to ensure sales, inventory, profitability, and strategy goals are achieved
</li>
<li>Improve operational efficiency and shorten project life cycles by establishing a dynamic KPI program and continual feedback loop with stakeholders
</li>
<li>Proactively and reactively identify process deficiencies or constraints that will impact project execution; and provide excellent customer service to our internal clients</li>
<li>
Connect with front line operations teams, while being able to summarize complex operations and present a clear and compelling story to leadership</li>
<li>
Develop suite of reporting and associated processes to improve visibility and management of operations</li>
<li>
Drive procurement and forecast accuracy to enhance service and cost savings</li>
<li>
Perform assembly and complex merchandising consistent with store aesthetics for retail displays and demonstration equipment</li>
<li>
Maintain asset management program ensuring a high level of detail and accuracy</li>
<li>
Record and track key vendor performance</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
At least four years operations project management experience in the retail or hardware industry</li>
<li>
At least four years experience in professional analysis position</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills and the ability to work cross functionally</li>
<li>
Comfortable with large data sets and analytical models</li>
<li>
Proficient in Excel and Google Suite applications</li>
<li>
Procurement and contract management experience is a plus</li>
</ul></div>
