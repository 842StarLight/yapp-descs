<h2>Financial Services Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Summary:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Financial Services Officer serves as the senior branch customer service associate and usually works closely with the relationship managers, treasury management representative, and their own client relationships. As a front-line customer service representative they are responsible for creating a welcoming environment and good impression for our customers. Key job functions include account and service origination as well as responding to customer inquiries by telephone and in person.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures lobby coverage for walk in clients and incoming phone calls, may assist branch manager in supervising branch activity to assure clients are serviced.
</li>
<li>Serves customers in opening new accounts for commercial and consumer clients. Must have extensive knowledge of bank products and treasury services in order to make recommendations for commercial clients. Contact new clients for onboarding activities and follow up to obtain necessary signatures and documentation. Process consumer loan applications and closings and NMLS license for consumer real estate loans.
</li>
<li>Could serve back up for the teller line, be able to perform all teller duties including all client transactions, maintaining and balancing a cash drawer. Have knowledge of the ATM, Vault, Coin &amp; Cash and GL Accounts to be balanced daily. Have knowledge of all dual control procedures.
</li>
<li>Serves as a team member to accomplish company objectives and lobby and personal goals. As the experienced banker this individual would be expected to work closely with the Treasury Management Representative and the Relationship Managers to develop stronger client relationships, to provide coaching for lobby associates on sales, and assist the manager in sales reports.
</li>
<li>Provide customer service for lobby and phone requests. Research and resolve client problems, acting as the customer liaison between other bank departments. Follow up with client to assure resolution. Develop and maintain long-term relationships with clients.
</li>
<li>Actively participate in training programs to maintain and acquire additional job knowledge and skills.
</li>
<li>Complies with all department and company policies, procedures, audit guidelines, and regulations.
</li>
<li>Assists in opening and closing the branch, following procedures set by corporate security. Awareness of location and operation of surveillance equipment.
</li>
<li>Proactive calling and prospecting new clients, prepare annual reviews for existing clients, and participating in proactive partner visits with assigned partner
</li>
<li>Other duties as required.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, customer service, and sales skills.
</li>
<li>Present professional appearance and demeanor including excellent phone etiquette
</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and the ability to make sound decisions. This may include making on-the-spot decisions regarding customer transactions; weighing customer satisfaction issues with the bank exposure to loss or fraud and the ability to think through and rationalize decisions
</li>
<li>Team-oriented, possess a positive attitude and works well with others. Demonstrates diversity, flexibility, and commitment.
</li>
<li>This position requires a perceptive person who is capable of relating to individuals at all levels. Situations that present themselves may require sensitivity to both customer and bank needs and goodwill.
</li>
<li>Represent the bank in a manner that maintains the highest level of confidentiality with all information obtained.
</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize; handle multiple tasks; and work independently
</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills, detail-oriented with the ability to establish priorities and to complete forms and handle transactions with a high degree of accuracy. Must be able to handle multiple tasks and work independently.
</li>
<li>Must be able to count cash quickly and accurately, ability to work in a fast paced environment and under pressure as needed.
</li>
<li>Self-motivated and goal-oriented. Ability to meet established lobby goals.
</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of banking operations, bank products/services, and new business development.
</li>
<li>Familiar with a variety of account ownerships, both personal and business, to effectively assist customers’ needs in opening accounts and obtaining required documentation.
</li>
<li>Familiar with consumer loan applications, products, and process.
</li>
<li>Licensed NMLS.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul><li>None
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent, plus five years banking experience
</li>
<li>Bachelor degree is preferred
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Computer and Software Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Skilled in operation of a personal computer, including Microsoft Word, PowerPoint and Excel
</li>
<li>Gmail
</li>
<li>Data entry and 40 WPM typing
</li>
<li>Ability to operate standard office equipment, such as, computer work stations, typewriters, calculators, and copier
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Bankway, Image Now, Salesforce (preferred)
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Certificates, Licenses and Registrations:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Notary License
</li>
<li>NMLS – Nationwide Mortgage Licensing System
</li>
</ul>
