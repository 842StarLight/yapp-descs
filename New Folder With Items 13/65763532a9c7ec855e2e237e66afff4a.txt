<p><b>POSITION OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>The Implementation Analyst’s primary focus is to integrate new clients with Zelis Payments’ ePay solution. The IA will be expected to manage the implementation project from kick-off call to go-live. In this role you will educate clients on the ZP product suite and partner workflows, develop a thorough understanding of the client’s day to day operations, and make recommendations on implementation best practices. The IA will be expected to lead weekly client calls to ensure all assigned project tasks remain on target.</p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Deliver kick off meetings with the respective stakeholders</li>
<li>Project manage implementations and initiatives</li>
<li>Document project plans, deliverables and expectations as well as outcomes.</li>
<li>Communicate to all levels on project status, deliverables, obstacles, milestones and impacts</li>
<li>Own client relationship from sales execution through implementation to account manager hand off</li>
<li>Gather, analyze and document business and system requirements in a formalized manner</li>
<li>Perform analysis and identify gaps in functionality for system integrations</li>
<li>Facilitate training to the Clients, staff and management</li>
<li>Support and influence Client focused strategic business decisions that impact the business</li>
<li>Work with Information Technology (IT) Teams to analyze, monitor, and track innovative solutions and projects.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze and troubleshoot technical issues and offer innovative solutions.</li>
<li>Maintain awareness of and ensure adherence to Zelis Payments standards regarding privacy.</li>
</ul>
