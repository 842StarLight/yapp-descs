<h2>Teller, Sr</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Conduct transactions with speed, accuracy and efficiency while meeting or exceeding service quality standards. Safeguard bank assets assigned to them. Contribute to the growth of the bank by promoting and cross selling bank products and services.<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES<br>
1. Receives, pays out cash, handles negotiable items, loan payments, deposits, withdrawals and transfers.<br>
<br>
2. Sets up, closes and balances teller station.<br>
<br>
3. Records sales of monetary instruments.<br>
<br>
4. Assists customers with inquires in person or by telephone.<br>
<br>
5. Maintains neat and well stocked teller area.<br>
<br>
6. Cross sells bank products and services.<br>
<br>
7. Follows limits established for protection of customer accounts and bank safety and soundness.<br>
<br>
8. Adheres to bank policies and procedures.<br>
<br>
9. Lift coin bags up to 50lbs.<br>
<br>
10. Balances vault, ATM, Night Drop, Coin Machine as assigned.<br>
<br>
11. Scans bank documents and proof work.<br>
<br>
12. Exercise sound judgement when applying holds and making check cashing decisions.<br>
<br>
13. Assist other tellers with decision making within their authority limits.<br>
<br>
14. Cross train to open all standard consumer accounts.<br>
<br>
15. Adheres to work schedule. Must be flexible in availability for work schedule.<br>
<br>
16. Maintains positive, friendly and professional attitude and appearance.<br>
<br>
17. Performs miscellaneous duties as assigned.<br>
<br>
18. Completes required BSA/AML training and other compliance training as assigned. This duty is performed annually.<br>
<br>
19. The ability to work in a constant state of alertness and in a safe manner.<br>
<br>
20. Perform any other related duties as required or assigned.</p>
