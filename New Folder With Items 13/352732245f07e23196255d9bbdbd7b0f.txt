<h2>Solution Architecture Programs Administrator, San Mateo</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are looking for a <b>Solution Architecture</b> <b>Programs Administrator </b>that will be focused on supporting our Solution Architecture department. This is a full-time role that can be located in our San Mateo, CA headquarters or Roseville, CA location.<br>
Celigo's Solution Architecture (SA) function is a highly visible, strategic function that assists our sales team by helping them craft innovative cloud-based solutions using our Celigo Integration iPaaS (Integration Platform as a Service). This team requires an administrator who can manage and coordinate multiple streams of activity to enable the function &amp; team to perform effectively. This SA Administrator role is a highly interactive position that requires strong teamwork and effective communication.</p>
<p><b>
Who are we looking for:</b><br>
A resourceful, detail-oriented, quick learner that wants to grow and learn a lot in a fast-paced dynamic technology environment</p>
<ul>
<li>Effective problem solver and ability to self-manage</li>
<li>Ability to work collaboratively with different departments</li>
<li>Exceptional interpersonal skills with meticulous attention to detail</li>
<li>Driven by high standards with a strong desire to contribute and help</li>
<li>Polished and poised with excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Great energy that stimulates company culture and moral</li>
<li>Proficient in Google Apps or Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Organized, organized, organized</li>
<li>Comfortable using business software and adept at learning and understanding business/operational concepts</li>
<li>Prior administrative, operational or office management/coordination experience preferred</li>
<li>Prior experience working in a startup/fast-paced environment preferred</li>
<li>Prior NetSuite experience is a plus</li>
<li>Analytical skills are a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What would you do, if hired?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for owning &amp; coordinating the solution scoping process for services-only opportunities from the time the need is identified through the time the price is generated.</li>
<li>Coordinate directly with Solution Architects, Account Executives, Engineers and others to ensure the process and communication flows seamlessly and in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Manage the Solution Architect Scope Request process for all sales opportunities requiring scoping and coordinate with the SA team to assign a SA, prioritize the opportunities, communicate estimated delivery dates, and other related tasks.</li>
<li>Assist with administrative and operational tasks to ensure the Solution Architect function runs efficiently and effectively (including one-time and recurring tasks)</li>
<li>Help ensure data integrity by updating, tracking, auditing, and/or reporting on data in NetSuite and other business systems</li>
<li>Assist with the rollout of team initiatives</li>
<li>Update the wiki and other internal or external-facing documents, including documents used by customers</li>
<li>Assist Solution Architects with research and communication with 3rd party vendors and other data gathering activities</li>
<li>Assist with designing, vetting, and implementing new business systems or processes</li>
<li>Coordinate and/or implement special projects or initiatives</li>
<li>Other tasks as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Why You’ll Love It Here</b></p>
<ul>
<li>We are solving a really hard problem that affects almost every business on the planet—integrating cloud apps</li>
<li>You get the best of both worlds: a start-up opportunity at a company that has already established itself in the market with thousands of happy customers.</li>
<li>You’ll have the opportunity to make a significant contribution to the company as well as the iPaaS industry as a whole.</li>
<li>We’re at an exciting crossroads as we re-imagine the Integration and iPaaS space. This will take teamwork, creativity, strategic thinking and a willingness to advance the market.</li>
<li>We offer a strong benefits package, 401k, pre-tax commuter expense reimbursement, fully stocked kitchen, snacks, and many other perks.</li>
</ul>
