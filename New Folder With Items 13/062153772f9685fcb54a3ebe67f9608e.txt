<p>
This role is responsible for maintaining and developing the overall direction for the Productivity Software data analytics strategy. Working with other members of the Productivity Software product management team, customers and field-based assets.</p>
<p>
You will create solutions from existing technologies but are very much expected to bring fresh ideas based on your industry experience, customer interactions and previous services engagements.</p>
<p><b><i>
We are looking for a unique blend of business and technical savvy; a big-picture vision and the drive and ability to turn that vision into reality.</i></b></p>
<p>
You must be able to communicate with all areas of the company. You will work with an engineering counterpart to define product release requirements. You will work with marketing communications to define the go-to-market strategy, helping them understand the product positioning, key benefits, and target customer. You will also serve as the internal and external evangelist for your product offering, working with sales and key customers.</p>
<p>
Key Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li> Managing the entire product life cycle from strategic planning to tactical activities.</li>
<li> Driving the strategy, direction, investment and realization of the Productivity Software Data Analytic Solutions
</li> <li>Responsibilities also including pricing strategy and methodology, market share projection, and results tracking.</li>
<li> Specifying market requirements for current and future products by conducting market research supported by on-going visits to customers and non-customers.</li>
<li> Driving a solution set across development teams and Marketing through agile market requirements, product contract, and positioning.</li>
<li> Work with marketing to develop and implement a company-wide go-to-market plan, working with all departments to execute.</li>
<li> Managing the scheduling and product management process for all project deliverables, including working with the operations team to design and develop new services, ongoing programs and prioritizations.</li>
<li> Leading and facilitating project meetings, team coordination, issue resolution and action item tracking.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul><li>7-10 years of hands-on experience in commercial software Product Management</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to complete detailed work, organize efficiently and drive influence</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent written and oral communication skills and a track record of producing results in a team environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Deep experience in Internet of things, big data analysis, &amp; machine learning technology</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience defining business requirements and managing projects</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must enjoy managing multiple projects simultaneously and balancing priorities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience working with developers and software teams</li></ul>
