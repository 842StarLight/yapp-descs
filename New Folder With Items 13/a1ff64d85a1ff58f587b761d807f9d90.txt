<p></p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The SAP Education Consultant is responsible for delivering fundamental Project Management, SAP Activate Methodology and delivery best practices to the Ariba Customer Value Organization (CVO) Services Delivery teams and some partner delivery teams.</p>
<p>
The role of this person is not restricted to instructor-led methods but also to virtual and online training as well. In addition, the Education Consultant will need to help update, test and create new training content and/or labs in conjunction with the fundamental training batteries needed in subsequent boot camps and workshops both locally and globally. The education consultant is a key contributor to development of SAP PM/Activate learning strategies and direct development of CVO Services Delivery consultants.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
SAP Ariba CVO is transforming to meet today’s most demanding and progressive global organizations by bringing business and IT together. This means that our CVO teams need to be trained and held to a high standard when delivering/implementing SAP Ariba solutions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
EXPECTATION OF THE ROLE<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The successful candidate will have a global role, centrally orchestrating instructor led / virtual training services and programs that motivate employees to learn.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide Project Management, Activate Methodology, Consulting Skills, and Delivery Best Practices Training</li>
<li>
Ensure consistent delivery of trainings across regions
</li>
<li>Collaborate with both CVO Learning Management and Strategy &amp; Innovation Organization to determine priorities, create and review learning content.</li>
<li>
Participate in defining/revising content for training classes providing regular feedback on trainings.</li>
<li>
Manage, coordinate, and facilitate classroom learning and workshops in coordination with business priorities.</li>
<li>
Conduct ongoing enablement needs analysis and prioritize offerings in concurrence with leadership direction</li>
<li>
Foster a culture of empathy, ideation and innovation</li>
<li>
Support training needs across all regions: NA, LAC, APJ and EMEA (travel up to 50%)</li>
<li>
Manage executive escalations on the regional level</li>
<li>
International travel may be required in conjunction with scheduled requests.</li>
<li>
In addition, we place high value on relevant personal qualities: resourcefulness, results-orientation, high energy, self-confidence, empathy and exceptional team player.</li>
</ul>
