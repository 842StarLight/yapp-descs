<h2>Financial Services and Insurance Vertical Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>Facebook's mission is to give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together. Through our family of apps and services, we're building a different kind of company that connects billions of people around the world, gives them ways to share what matters most to them, and helps bring people closer together. Whether we're creating new products or helping a small business expand its reach, people at Facebook are builders at heart. Our global teams are constantly iterating, solving problems, and working together to empower people around the world to build community and connect in meaningful ways. Together, we can help people build stronger communities — we're just getting started.
</div></div>
<div>Facebook seeks an experienced Marketing Manager who is passionate about marketing the business value of Facebook’s family of apps and services to advertisers in the Financial Services and Insurance space.
<br>
<br>
The vision for this role is to inspire marketers and key decision-makers at top-tier Financial Services and Insurance companies to see Facebook as a trusted partner and indispensable advertising and business platform for achieving their most important business outcomes. This role partners with the Facebook sales team and provides marketing leadership and support as they work to grow revenue for these two verticals.
<br>
<br>
The successful candidate must have experience building, managing and executing strategic marketing plans in a fast-paced environment. The candidate must be a self-starter and be comfortable developing and executing marketing programs with internal and external stakeholders and agencies. He or she must thrive in cross-functional teams and exhibit excellent communication and prioritization skills. This is a full-time position located in New York, Chicago or Menlo Park.
</div>
