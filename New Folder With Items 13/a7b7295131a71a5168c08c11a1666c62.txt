<b>What do we expect from you?</b>
</div>
<div><div><div>
<div><div>
<p>The Development Analyst will be responsible for creating and maintaining IT solutions oriented to business needs across our different segments. These IT solutions will be focused on creating new and improving our existing developments. Management skills and team motivation that help Cosentino Group achieve its objectives as a group. This position will report to the Corporate Development Manager based in Spain.</p>
<br>
</div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
</div>
<p></p>

<div>
<b>Your Key Responsibilities will be:</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Manage and coordinate the development needs with the business and identify, document and communicate to applicable parties.</li>
<li>Plan, validate and create analysis and proposals in both the functional and technical requirements for each development.</li>
<li>Manage and coordinate the development needs in Job Tracker (projects, documentation and requirements gathering) and in the Web Apps environments for USA</li>
<li>Act as interlocutor between the Web Development and ColdFusion teams</li>
<li>Establish flows and procedures to guarantee user satisfaction, simplifying and automating processes.</li>
<li>Manage the relationship with vendors: SLA control, billing and efficient use of resources.</li>
<li>Coordinate the tests and certify compliance to move to Production and keep the processes documented and updated</li>
<li>Expand and extend the current “whitepaper” of non-SAP development environments, helping in delivering applications with added value for Cosentino</li>
<li>Manage vendors and contractors as needed based on project</li>
</ul></div>
