Today, we are expanding and looking to add a Sales Operations Manager to the team. This person will be responsible for managing, coaching and mentoring a team of Sales Operations Managers who facilitate Channel, Enterprise and Inside Sales processes to ensure timely and accurate execution of quotes and contracts. As well as manage partner relationships in pre/post-sales environment to ensure success of Nextiva's partner program and at the same time handling partner escalations.
<br><br>
<b>Ability to:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Perform quality work within deadlines with little to no direct supervision</li>
<li>Interact professionally with other employees, customers, partners and vendors</li>
<li>Effectively manage the team's contribution on all assignments and projects</li>
<li>Work independently while understanding the necessity for communicating and coordinating work efforts with other employees and organizations</li>
</ul>
<b>Position Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage Channel/ Enterprise/ Inside Sales Operations team</li>
<li>Perform team coaching</li>
<li>Holding the Channel Operations Mangers and admins to response standards</li>
<li>Help educate and direct all Channel, Enterprise, or Inside Sales related questions on process and procedures</li>
<li>Manage assignments of Channel Operation Managers and Partners</li>
<li>Verify any partner escalation is properly handled and directed towards correct department CM/CAM/IM/IC Support as needed</li>
<li>Facilitate ongoing training programs on sales portal and account creation flow</li>
<li>Train and onboard all new Channel Operations Managers on processes and procedures</li>
<li>Facilitate Onboarding process of new accounts and master accounts, as well as working with Onboarding leaders when needed</li>
<li>Ensure CM/Partners are updated on any new processes</li>
</ul>
<b>Position Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work without supervision and to make decisions that will positively affect the operation</li>
<li>Ability to assess complicated sales scenarios and recommend the most feasible solution</li>
<li>Ability to process work accurately, and with attention to detail</li>
<li>Flexible availability in work hours is required</li>
<li>Ability to execute in fast pace environment on a high performing team</li>
<li>Travel may be required up to 40%</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree (BA/BS) or 4 years of Telecom/Technology experience</li>
<li>Partner with internal organization 30%</li>
<li>Partner with Channel Managers 40%</li>
<li>Partner with Channel Partners 25%</li>
<li>Partner with Customers 5%</li>
</ul>
