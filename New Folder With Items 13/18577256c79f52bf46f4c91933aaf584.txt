AT&amp;T Strategic Account Lead 3 is considered an expert in providing wireline voice, data and wireless/mobility application and solutions to AT&amp;T Enterprise customers. As an expert, the Strategic Account Lead is successful in the development and execution of strategies that focus on meeting and exceeding the business needs of our clients.<br>
<br>
As a Strategic Account Lead 3 you’ll focus on developing sales opportunities and presenting customized solutions to business customers. You’ll support the entire branch prospecting, qualifying, and gaining access to the operational leaders within our customer base. You’ll leverage your professional network and partner closely with the AT&amp;T Client Solution Executives to gain additional access into our customer base and position AT&amp;T to offer additional core services.<br>
