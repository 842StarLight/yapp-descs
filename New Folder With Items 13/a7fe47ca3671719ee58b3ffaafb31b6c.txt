<h2>Hotel General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>The General Manager must have hands on ability to oversee all daily hotel operations.</li>
<li>Strong leadership skills and ability to lead high performance team in autonomous environment.</li>
<li>Maximizes room sales through the use and knowledge of Revenue Management systems, techniques and strategies.</li>
<li>Promotes the hotel by developing and implementing advertising, sales and publicity programs, speaking to the community and business groups, sponsoring special events at the hotel. As a General Manager you will recommend and help implement the hotels marketing plans and business plans and manage within approved plans and objectives.</li>
<li>Financial responsibilities include optimizing revenue and controlling costs to achieve budgeted Gross Operating Profit.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain tight controls over food, maintenance and room inventories.</li>
<li>Adhere to established preventative maintenance program to the hotel assets.</li>
<li>Must take personal responsibility for delivering superior hospitality service that exceeds guests expectations.</li>
<li>Leads the brand performance measurements by leading the property to the top 10' of the brand.</li>
<li>Maintains the hotels stature by inspecting guests rooms, public access areas, and outside grounds for cleanliness, safety and order.</li>
<li>Monitor and be responsive to guest feedback on social media outlets such as TripAdvisor.</li>
<li>Ability to implement company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Preserve Hampton Quality Assurance grading of acceptable or higher by reinforcing best practices in cleanliness, condition, and brand standard compliances</li>
<li>Computer skills required. Excellent communication skills.</li>
<li>Understands the value of strategic thinking, analysis, and proactive approach to the business environment.</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Fantastic opportunity to become the General Manager of a beautiful, established and recently renovated Hampton Inn property located in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina! We are looking for an energetic, highly motivated General Manager with strong initiative and desire for personal achievement to join our team. Prefer minimum 3-5 years experience as General Manager and Hampton Inn certification. Strong marketing and sales skills highly desired.</div>
