<h2>Ecosystem Manager, Development and Strategy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Welltok is a data-driven, enterprise SaaS company that delivers the healthcare industry's leading consumer activation platform. Welltok's solutions empower leading health plans, employers, providers and public entities to connect consumers with personalized health improvement resources, making it easy and rewarding for consumers to complete actions that optimize their health and wellbeing.<br>
<br>
Welltok was named one of five healthcare companies on the Forbes 2018 Cloud 100 list, ranked #1 on the 2019 Tech Tribune's Best Technology Startups in Colorado and #2 in Colorado on Deloitte's 2017 Technology Fast 500.<br>
<br>
At Welltok, we're proud of what we're accomplishing and are committed to building a company where smart, passionate people can thrive and succeed. Join us as we make healthcare more personal and rewarding!</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
Welltok is currently looking for an experienced <b>Ecosystem Manage</b>r to be part of the Ecosystem and Marketplace team who will help support the identification and management of partners aligned with customer and market needs. They will work closely with the Ecosystem Development and Strategy team to help build out the Ecosystem strategy and framework. As Ecosystem Manager, you will apply the framework that you helped to create and lead the identification, contracting and relationship management process for all ecosystem partners. The ideal candidate will possess exceptional communication and relationship building skills, detailed analytical skills and the capability to help create channel partner offerings that are mutually beneficial for partners and Welltok. You should be comfortable applying market research to identify and validate what types of partners our customers are seeking as we build narrow networks of best in class partners. You will work with Legal, Finance, Sales, the Product team and the Welltok markets segments as you take a Partner through the curation process, so its' important that you effectively manage a broad group of internal stakeholders effectively.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Gain and analyze market intelligence that will be applied to validate our strategy and selection of partners.</li>
<li>
Participate and lead the due diligence process as we identify, select and take to market partners (curation process).</li>
<li>
Work with Legal and the Connect Partner to lead the contract negotiations, especially the business terms.</li>
<li>
Work with Finance to help forecast expected Sales for new Partners.</li>
<li>
Maintain strong working relationships with partners to ensure smooth partner services and an exceptional partner experience.</li>
<li>
Establish clear value propositions for partners and the ecosystem.</li>
<li>
Coordinate with partners to identify new business opportunities for revenue generation.</li>
<li>
Help drive the internal pipeline and know what's in the pipeline to ensure the company can deliver on commitments.</li>
<li>
Help partners take advantage of Welltok's ecosystem technology to grow their business.</li>
<li>
Help ensure uniformity and scalability across our partner offerings.</li>
<li>
Know our partners well and where we do and do not have the ability to make adjustments.</li>
<li>
Educate partners on Welltok's product and service offerings and keep them up to date on new products and features to drive sales through partner referrals.</li>
<li>
Work with Operations to conduct business reviews with partners and make recommendations for improvements.</li>
<li>
Build effective collaboration and serve as an internal liaison among partners, sales and account management.</li>
<li>
Provide partner marketing support to ensure we have compelling materials for our internal sales teams and partners to drive customer demand.</li>
<li>
Develop processes, management reports and key success metrics to create transparency and influence proactive, strategic decisions.</li>
<li>
Evaluate effectiveness of partner programs and improve upon them.</li>
<li>
Participate directly in sales discussions to represent the ecosystem partners, as appropriate.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Knowledge and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Business or other applicable field; MBA, advanced degree highly preferred.</li>
<li>
Exceptional communication and relationship building skills.</li>
<li>
Confidence to act authoritatively and hold the line internally and externally.</li>
<li>
Strong financial and competitive analysis skills.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work in a dynamic cross-functional environment and provide leadership when operating in customer and partner settings.</li>
<li>
Experience in the healthcare industry, wellness programs or health insurance background preferred.</li>
<li>
Travel 20%-30%</li>
</ul>
