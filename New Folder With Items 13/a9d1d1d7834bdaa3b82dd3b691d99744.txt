<h2>2020 Spring - Customer Success - Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Workiva is searching for talented Interns to join our growing Customer Success team. This is an exciting opportunity to learn about day-to-day customer success activities spanning from the initial stages of onboarding to continued engagement and retention. During your time at Workiva you will learn what it’s like working at a high-energy and fast-paced software company.</p>
<p><b>
What You'll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create and implement customer documents using industry leading software</li>
<li>
Collaborate with internal, cross-functional teams including Product Management, Sales, Marketing, Engineering and Finance</li>
<li>
Gain exposure to some of the largest companies in the world and see how they utilize our technology to complete complex reporting</li>
<li>
See how our Customer Success Managers create a “wow” customer experience</li>
<li>
View the different phases of the customer life cycle and what role the Customer Success team plays in each phase.</li>
<li>
Have the potential to be customer facing and help lead trainings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What You'll Need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Completed second year at the undergraduate level</li>
<li>
Must be technically savvy and demonstrate a high level of competency using Excel, Word, Google Docs, etc.</li>
<li>
Strong communication and leadership skills demonstrated through leadership positions in community, school, or other programs</li>
<li>
Professional, outgoing, energetic personality with excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and take initiative</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and follow-through are required</li>
</ul>
