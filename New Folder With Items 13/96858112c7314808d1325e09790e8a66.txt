<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About the Position:</b></p>
<p>
AccuLynx is a fast-growing SaaS provider of CRM/project management software for roofing contractors. With more than 10 years in the business and impressive year-over-year revenue growth, we have quickly established ourselves as the leading software product in this multi-billion dollar industry.</p>
<p>
We are currently seeking talented <b>Sales Development Representatives</b> to join our downtown Chicago office. As a Sales Development Representative at AccuLynx, your role will be to seek new business opportunities by contacting and developing relationships with potential customers. With thousands of untapped prospects, this is an incredible opportunity for entry-level sales candidates to get hands-on experience selling in the SaaS space and make a huge impact on growing our customer base.</p>
<p>
If you want to be part of a collaborative sales team in a rapidly growing SaaS company, please read on!</p>
<p><b>
What You Will Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Contact leads through 75+ outbound cold calls and emails to set up product demonstrations</li>
<li>Schedule 3-5 daily demos for our team of Account Executives</li>
<li>Serve as a product knowledge expert</li>
<li>Track and report progress on daily, weekly, and monthly goals</li>
<li>Deliver the highest level of sales and customer service to our customers</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date with new products/services and new pricing/payment plans</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Your Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from an accredited 4-year college in business or related field</li>
<li>0-1 years of sales related work experience</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, presentation and written communication skills</li>
<li>“Hunter” mentality - you have a passion for finding ways to generate new business</li>
<li>An entrepreneurial spirit - the idea of being an active participant in a growing company must excite you</li>
<li>High level of energy, motivation, drive, persistence, initiative and commitment</li>
<li>Must be tech savvy and able to navigate a CRM</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple tasks in a fast-paced, rapidly changing environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Why AccuLynx?</b></p>
<p>
At AccuLynx we try to create the best working environment possible, comfy chairs, casual dress, and a fun environment. We believe that culture is one of the most important ingredients for success and below are some of our benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Attractive base salary and commissions</li>
<li>Generous paid time off policy and flexible work hours</li>
<li>Competitive health coverage (medical, dental, vision)</li>
<li>Free snacks and drinks</li>
<li>Gym membership (to work off the snacks and drinks)</li>
<li>401K matching and safe harbor contributions</li>
<li>Convenient Loop location close to public transportation</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Position is based in Chicago, IL, and full-time remote work is not an option at this time. Applicants must be able to work legally within the United States, and we currently do not offer H1B Visa sponsorship. Please, no 3rd party inquiries.</i></p>
