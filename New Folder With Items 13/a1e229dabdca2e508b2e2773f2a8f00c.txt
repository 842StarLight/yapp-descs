<h2>Director, Head of FX Strategy and Trading</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>MetLife Investment Management</b></p>
<div>
Based in Whippany, New Jersey, MetLife Investment Management (MIM) is one of the largest institutional investors and asset managers in the world, with over $605 billion in combined managed assets, and has consistently generated superior returns for both policyholders and shareholders. The enterprise has more than 900 dedicated professionals with extensive experience and expertise in all asset classes, with strength in public and private fixed income and private assets, including real estate.</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
MIM General Account Hedging Unit
</b></p>
<p>The General Account Hedging Unit is dedicated to creating and delivering solutions that support growth and improve the Company’s profitability through its use of derivatives strategies to solve Asset Liability Management challenges.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The General Account Hedging Unit works with many areas across the enterprise to develop, implement and manage hedging programs that improve profitability, reduce risk and capital, and increase diversification. Working with Portfolio Managers, Asset Originators, Treasury, Mergers and Acquisitions, and the Lines of Business, the team covers interest rate and currency exposure globally. Members of the team focus on rates including Governments and Swaps, Interest Rate Volatility, Cross Currency Swaps, and Foreign Exchange. The GA Hedging Unit supports MetLife globally.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
The Role</b></p>
<div>
The Director, Head of Foreign Currency Strategy and Trading, will report to the Head of General Account Hedging. He or she will manage all aspects of Foreign Currency Spot, Forward, and Options trading platform. The selected candidate will work closely with Treasury, Accounting, Risk, IT and Legal and, will also have the opportunity to work with risk management, ALM, finance, among others and gain broad exposure across the organization.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Working closely with the Variable Annuity hedging team, the Director will manage FX exposures and coordinate with the GA hedging team members to manage swapped bond programs and other initiatives. He or she will work closely within the larger Derivatives Unit and with constituents throughout the firm to develop strategy, execute and manage FX programs. The selected candidate will advise and assist on hedge program development and market our services to internal and external clients.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This leader will develop and publish strategy pieces to be distributed internally and externally. He or she will participate in committees to develop and oversee FX policies and exposures across the firm. The successful candidate will possess a superior understanding of FX markets, the derivative instruments used to manage FX risk, and the accounting and regulatory environments in insurance and asset management. He or she will work well both independently and within a group to drive new programs and maintain existing initiatives, including developing, writing, and presenting strategic views on FX that lead to actionable hedging or trading opportunities.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The FX Trading and Strategy area share a resource with the Short Term Trading unit and a team of six analysts who support the full Derivatives and Liquid Markets area.</div>
