<h2>Experienced Staff Auditor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>*THIS POSITION IS NON-EXEMPT*</b></p>
<p><b>Summary: </b></p>
<p>Experienced Staff Accountants have demonstrated an ability to capably perform the functions of a staff accountant and to take on certain more complex tasks. Semi-senior accountants are given a wider variety of diversified accounting and tax assignments under the supervision of different professionals. Performance is judged based on the quality of work, application of accounting knowledge, and ability to meet time constraints.</p>
<p><b>SALARY: $55K-$65K DOE</b></p>
<p><b>Benefits and Perks: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Health, Life, and Dental Insurance</li>
<li>Supplemental Life Insurance</li>
<li>Gym Membership Reimbursement</li>
<li>Ability to work REMOTELY!</li>
<li>Subsidized CPA examination fees &amp; spot bonus</li>
<li>Generous paid time off policies</li>
<li>Bonus payments for client or employee referrals</li>
<li>Comprehensive 401(k) matching program</li>
<li>Structured and paid volunteer and community care program</li>
<li>Discounted movie tickets</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Records transactions in ledgers, prepares trial balances and simple financial statements using computer software program.</li>
<li>Perform monthly or quarterly book-keeping and other attestation assignments while working on auditing assignments</li>
<li>Reconciles accounts to control accounts.</li>
<li>Performs schedule computations of accruals, property and equipment, and calculates depreciation expense.</li>
<li>Works effectively within given budgetary parameters; assist staff in prioritizing and managing their workload. Approaches projects with a sense of urgency and is completion orientated.</li>
<li>Performs audit or review procedures as assigned by supervisors.</li>
<li>Prepares payroll tax, sales or other local tax and certain personal income tax returns.</li>
<li>Performs other accounting, auditing and tax duties as needed in engagements and as assigned by supervisory personnel.</li>
<li>Performs and prepares presentation using power-point and other tools and applications;</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Nonessential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>May be given senior-level responsibilities of planning and scheduling engagements.</li>
<li>May be required to perform all phases of an engagement including report writing.</li>
<li>Punctually enters time and expenses to support efficient billing processes.</li>
<li>May supervise Staff accountants.</li>
<li>Such other duties as may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to organize work and projects, prioritize and meet deadlines. Ability to complete work in an efficient and accurate manner.</li>
<li>Ability to reason and multi-task.</li>
<li>Proficiency in use of computers, spreadsheet software programs, and calculator.</li>
<li>Well-rounded knowledge of accounting principles and personal income taxes.</li>
<li>Above average in utilizing power point and excel.</li>
<li>Ability to speak foreign language is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul><li>May supervise Staff Accountants during engagements.</li></ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Frequent same day travel for work at clients' offices, meetings, and seminars using a personal vehicle.</li>
<li>Occasional out-of-town travel with overnight stay for work at clients, meetings, or seminars using a personal vehicle.</li>
<li>Occasional overtime work required throughout the year. Heavy overtime work required from January 1 to April 15, may be in excess of 55 hours per week.</li>
<li>Minimal Hazards. General office working conditions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Accounting or related field.</li>
<li>Either hold a current and valid CPA's license, or be working toward obtaining the license by taking and passing the applicable state CPA exam.</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver's license.</li>
<li>At least 2+ years of experience in public accounting.</li>
<li>At least 2+ years of audit experience.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Success Factors: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proactive in reaching out to clients by having face-to-face conversation rather than e-mail and text. Same thing applies for internal communication too.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team environment.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate clearly and concisely in English.</li>
<li>Proactively seeks knowledge, new task and responsibilities.</li>
<li>Cooperative and responsive to training, supervision and constructive feedback.</li>
<li>Maintains appropriate compliance with all Firm wide and local policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
