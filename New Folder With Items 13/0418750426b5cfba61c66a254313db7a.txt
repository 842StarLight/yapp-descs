<p>Superintendent Job Descriptions 10-15-19</p>
<p>It's time to become part of a small, enjoyable work environment where you can make a big impact. We are seeking a big picture thinker that knows how to get into the details with strong jobsite supervision skills.</p>
<p>This team member will play a crucial role in setting the course for a successful project.</p>
<p><b>ABOUT US</b></p>
<p>Reel Design Build specializes in delivering turnkey solutions while providing a single point of contact to our clients. We assemble a select group of key team members to deliver new facilities that meet our clients’ needs and intended use. We focus in light to medium manufacturing facilities. Our work begins at conceptual planning and extends to initial budgeting, programming and construction project management and post construction. We construct new ground-up commercial/industrial facilities, typically with metal or tilt wall exteriors.</p>
<p>The desired candidate would be someone with a proven track record of supervision on commercial construction projects. We provide a rewarding and unique opportunity for self-motivated individuals to operate with a high degree of independence in a fast-growing company.</p>
<p><b>JOB DUTIES</b></p>
<p>You must be able to efficiently and effectively:</p>
<p>· Understand Procore PM software a plus</p>
<p>· Management the jobsite; ensuring a clean, safe and organized project</p>
<p>· Maintain daily records of all field related items using Procore (RFI’s, daily inspections/field reports, time cards, safety, etc)</p>
<p>· Communicate clearly and effectively with client and internal teammates via Procore, email, etc.</p>
<p>· Be able to provide a 3 week look-ahead and forecast the happenings on the project/schedule, etc.</p>
<p>· Some overnight travel may be required, but it is not expected to exceed 10%</p>
<p>· These positions require a minimum of 3 years of experience in commercial construction. Must be able to read plans</p>
<p>· Having a minimum of 10-hour OSHA is a plus</p>
<p>· Must recognize that safety and documentation as part of the job at all times.</p>
<p>· Maintain a clean jobsite and be able to interact with customers and subcontractors in a professional manor.</p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>· Ability to Prioritize and Self-Manage Project Management and Sales Activities</p>
<p>· Proficient with Technology and Computer Application</p>
<p>· Working knowledge of Procore is a plus</p>
<p>· Construction Experience</p>
<p>*** Bilingual (English-Spanish) is a plus ***</p>
<p><b>Additional Requirements: </b></p>
<p>- Maintain project schedules and provide 3-week look-ahead’s for your project</p>
<p>- Working knowledge and application of basic estimating and quantity take-offs; produce detailed studies that support production budgets</p>
<p>- Gather, analyze and interpret information on approval time, fabrication and delivery of all items</p>
<p>- Maintain project budget; labor management maintenance</p>
<p>- Monitor job mobilization; good technical knowledge of construction process; evaluate construction process</p>
<p>- Control and facilitate all project safety meetings</p>
<p>- Listen to, understand and effectively respond to customer needs and concerns</p>
