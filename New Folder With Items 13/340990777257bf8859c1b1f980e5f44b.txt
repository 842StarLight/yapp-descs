<h2>Marketing Graphic Design Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Come join our family at In-N-Out Burger!
</b></p>
<p>In-N-Out Burger has an excellent opportunity for a full-time <b>Marketing Graphic Design Coordinator</b> based in our Irvine, CA location. The Marketing Graphic Design Coordinator is primarily responsible for the creation and production of creative content and elements for in house and customer facing materials. Additionally, this person will be responsible for coordinating the creative process and managing project workflows with other department’s stakeholders. This position will help in the idea generation process and be relied on to take approved ideas and provide creative and branded print/digital solutions.</p>
<p><b>
General Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive the successful completion of design projects by creating materials for both digital and print applications for in house and some customer facing projects
</li>
<li>Creating branded assets including but no limited to logos, flyers, posters, web banners, invitations, web pages and online assets like banners, icons, ecomm images and email graphics/campaigns</li>
<li>
Photo and image editing as needed including but not limited to retouching/clean up, post editing, manipulation</li>
<li>
Assist with and at times handle the production of marketing materials when printing or packout vendors are not utilized</li>
<li>
Assist in brainstorming of concepts for upcoming projects</li>
<li>
Rework existing materials for new dimensions, new implementations or reprints</li>
<li>
Packing out finished pieces</li>
<li>
Various marketing program assistance as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Work Schedule + Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Full-time, Hourly, Non-Exempt
</li>
<li>Office Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 am-5:00 pm
</li>
<li>Department needs may call for occasional overtime</li>
<li>
Excellent Benefits, to include Medical, Dental, Vision, Life Insurance, Profit Sharing Plan and 401(k) Plan</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
