<h2>Parts Manager-Rockland County, NY</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>Transdev is the largest private sector operator of multiple modes of passenger transit in North America, providing bus, rail, paratransit, shuttle, sedan and taxi services. We manage over 200 transportation contracts for cities, transit authorities and airports, providing safe and sustainable mobility solutions. Our mission is to improve public transportation, to enhance quality of life and combat global warming.</p>
<p>The Parts Manager is responsible for managing inventory based on demand and cost targets. They must be able to maintain stock at a level that enables internal servicing and repair to complete work orders without delay. They must also avoid the costs of carrying excessive inventory. Finally, must have knowledge of stock-control system so that they can effectively record and analyze the movement of parts.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Inputs purchase orders into the RTA software system daily.</li>
<li>Reviews PM and work orders inputs made by the maintenance clerk.</li>
<li>Aids the Maintenance Manager in preparation of various reports.</li>
<li>Provides Maintenance Manager with month end closing reports as required.</li>
<li>Regulates the amount of inventory department carries at any given time.</li>
<li>Must also maintain accurate records of parts replaced under warranty and details of faulty parts. They must source quality parts at the right price.</li>
<li>Must be able to identify and evaluate suppliers who can offer replacement parts that match the quality of the originals.</li>
<li>Must also have good negotiating skills so they can obtain the best prices for parts.</li>
<li>Manages and coordinates subordinate staff in retail or wholesale automotive parts establishment or department of repair shop or service station.</li>
<li>Prepares work schedules and gives work assignments to subordinates.</li>
<li>Monitors inventory and requisitions new stock as needed.</li>
<li>Verifies cash receipts and keeps sales records.</li>
<li>Hires, trains, and discharges workers.</li>
<li>Inspects work in progress and completed to ensure conformance to specifications. Interprets company policy to employees and enforces company policy and practices.</li>
<li>Analyzes and resolves work problems or assists employees in solving work problems.</li>
<li>Other duties to ensure the efficient operation of the maintenance facility.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years’ maintenance, logistics, or process management experience.</li>
<li>3+ years’ experience managing a parts facility or similar detail-oriented experience.</li>
<li>Previous experience managing employees.</li>
<li>Degree in preferred.</li>
<li>Previous RTA experience required.</li>
<li>Highly organized and detailed oriented.</li>
<li>Ability to develop and manage a budget.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal communication, verbal, and written skills a must</li>
<li>Proficiency using Microsoft Office (including Word, Outlook, Excel and PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Must be able to perform complex technical and administrative work over-seeing the maintenance program to the standards set by the company and client.</li>
<li>Must be a team player yet able to work well independently.</li>
<li>Must be able to work well with a diverse group of internal and external customers.</li>
</ul>
