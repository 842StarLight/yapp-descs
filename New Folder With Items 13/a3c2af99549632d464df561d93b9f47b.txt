<div><b>
About Eaton:</b></div>
<div>
As a power management company with 2018 sales of $21.6 billion, Eaton provides energy-efficient solutions that help our customers effectively manage electrical, hydraulic and mechanical power more efficiently, safely and sustainably. We have approximately 95,000 employees across the world, and sell products to customers in more than 175 countries.</div>
<div><b>
Position Overview:</b></div>
<div>
The<b> Eaton Internship Program</b> <b>– Software</b> position will provide participants with robust and challenging assignments, designed to prepare them for careers in roles such as Embedded Software Engineer, Software Engineer and other technical roles focused on driving Eaton’s capabilities in critical technologies and trends like Internet of Things (IoT), functional safety, intelligent machines, increased diagnostics and prognostics capabilities. Participants will be located out of several different Eaton facilities, including but not limited to Gaithersburg, MD; Southfield, MI; Galesburg, MI; Eden Prairie, MN; Plymouth, MN; Grand Rapids, MI; Peachtree City, GA; Raleigh, NC; Pittsburgh PA; Menomonee Falls, WI; and Fort Worth, TX.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Example assignments include projects in areas such as enterprise software, PC-Tools development, interfacing sensors, data acquisition and communications for embedded systems to enable IoT, embedded software to enable physical products and data centric information services, creative software-centric solutions to interface and effectively manage in-building systems, and interfacing with enterprise software, mobile, and cloud based applications. Specific assignments include but are not limited to:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Our systems are constantly adding new sensing technologies to help make them more intelligent, adaptive and predictive. Some of the work will be developing next generation embedded sensing systems as well as the software that communicates with these systems. As a key member of the team, you will work with lead engineers on design principles such as defining requirements, contributing to the definition of embedded system and algorithm solutions and the external interfaces, define test plans and perform validation testing to confirm that system and functional requirements are met. In this role, you will be responsible for working with team members and your engineering mentor to estimate development effort, resources and timing to achieve the desired results.</div></li>
<li><div>
Working with an inter-disciplinary team to define new or improved diagnostics and reporting capabilities of an embedded product. This may be plausibility checks at various levels of the system, involving external and internal data or/and analysis and predictions based on internal logic or historical data logged in the device. It may also include optimizations for increased data storage and information availability.
</div></li>
<li><div>Working on defining and implementing methods to ensure embedded data security against un-authorized access over direct or remote connections within a network that includes various other local or cloud-based applications that can connect to the embedded device. Determine use cases for different authentication schemes, research existent methods, make recommendations, and implement method for an embedded device which satisfies a set of the use cases.</div></li>
<li><div>
Working on enabling our embedded products to offer selectable features and functionality based on various licensing models.
</div></li>
<li><div>As a software intern you will collaborate with our software development, architecture, QA and product management teams in all stages of Software Development Life Cycle such as gathering requirements, developing &amp; executing test cases, developing Proof of Concepts, estimating tasks and developing production level software to acquire valuable knowledge and experience in using the the Agile software development processes, latest technologies and tools to complete your assignments while expand and maintain our market leading product offerings.</div></li>
<li><div>
Research, prove and influence other software development professionals on modern software development tools and methods including the latest aspects of Agile software development, improved methods to achieve quality earlier in the development process, automated software build and testing and DevOps.
</div></li>
<li><div>For a suite of IoT applications running in Azure, determine use cases for different multi-factor authentication schemes, research MFA capabilities provided by Azure (or 3rd parties integrate with Azure), make recommendations, and develop a POC providing a custom MFA pipeline for a mobile application running on Azure which satisfies a set of the use cases.</div></li>
<li><div>
Determine how to effectively gather and store massive amounts of log data from the components of an IoT platform. The components include device/gateway software, data ingress pipeline, REST APIs and Web/Mobile Apps. Once the data is collected in a centralized repository it can be analyzed by querying or by asking right questions. You will be responsible for determining the appropriate tools and methods for gathering, storing, and efficiently querying the assembled data, including the ability to provide real-time alerts on current state/health of the platform.<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
