<p><b>Essential Functions</b><br>
The Part-Time Center Associate delivers world-class customer service to all retail customers by receiving and processing packages for courier shipment and operating copiers, fax machines, binding equipment, laminating machines, and point-of-sale devices. The Associate expertly advises our valued customers by providing accurate information on an array of products, services, and best-value options and exhibits confidence by knowing best practices related to the industry.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate must be able to work in a fast paced environment, have previous retail sales experience, strong computer and internet skills, a friendly and genuinely helpful demeanor, and a professional appearance. He or she must be a fast learner who makes an effort to learn all aspects of the business in the shortest possible time. Must be available to also work on the weekend (Saturday only).<br>
<br>
<b>Education</b><br>
High school diploma or GED<br>
<br>
<b>Physical Requirements</b><br>
Capable of lifting up to 50 pounds, bending and twisting and standing for long periods of time. Knowledge of register operation, including cash, credit cards and register receipts. Must be able to type up to or more than 45 wpm.</p>
