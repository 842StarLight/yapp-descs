<h2>Client Strategist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>To provide strategic thought leadership and creative executional support to sales personnel to ensure they can present best-in-class materials and thinking to their clients and prospects. He or she collaborates with cross functional Inmar disciplines to create data-driven, client-specific sales content in a high volume, fast-paced, deadline driven environment. He or she will develop a broad understanding of Inmar’s comprehensive suite of services and solutions. He or she will manage a variety of projects and contribute to strategic planning with the sales person, in a partnership approach. He or she will manage the proposal process for Inmar including the authoring and editing of proposals. The successful candidate will be passionate, driven and calm under pressure aligning with the Agile philosophy within a work environment. He or she will work independently and have the ability to lead or manage up in all situations, given the nature of on-time executions. The Client Strategist will be responsible to manage deadlines/project manage in both a proactive and reactive, output-based environment.
