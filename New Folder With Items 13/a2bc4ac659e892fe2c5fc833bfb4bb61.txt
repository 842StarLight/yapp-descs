<p><b>PILATES GROUP INSTRUCTOR</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Summary &amp; Reports to:</b></p>
<p>
Member of the Fitness Services Team who is responsible for delivering Pilates Group Reformer and individualized programming to our members. You will help our members improve their Fitness level and achieve their goals through education and results-based sessions. With your guidance and expertise, clients will develop an overall sense of well-being through this Mind-Body Practice. The Pilates Group Instructor exposes our members to the studio experience.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities/Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and instruct professional, innovative and comprehensive Pilates programs to ensure clients are satisfied and remain motivated to attain their personal fitness goals</li>
<li>Deliver progressive semi-private sessions, while developing a sense of community with the client base</li>
<li>Monitor and instruct clients during their Pilates training sessions on the safe and effective use of Pilates training equipment, flexibility, and postural alignment.</li>
<li>Conduct Introductory class sessions and individual assessments for new clients including pre-participation screening, medical history, lifestyle questionnaire, and goal setting to assess and recommend Pilates training programs</li>
<li>Availability to sub classes and work as a team to create a seamless, professional studio environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Sales &amp; Promotion:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Present client options and facilitate conversions to program</li>
<li>Promote all Edge Fitness programs, services, and products</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Performance:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for Lead generation and filling classes to capacity</li>
<li>Responsible for retention of client base</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to build a relationship with members</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and promotional skills; energetic, enthusiastic, and motivational</li>
<li>Ability to resolve conflicts in a professional, tactful manner</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of Pilates Practice and able to adapt to all brands of studio apparatus</li>
<li>Knowledge of safe exercise techniques</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and learn quickly</li>
<li>Excellent time management and organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to operate a computer and Microsoft Office software (Word and Excel)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Certified Pilates Instructor- Reformer certified minimum</li>
<li>One year Pilates training experience preferred</li>
<li>CPR/AED certification</li>
<li>Specialty Certifications Preferred (I.e.: Barre, TRX)</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Physical Demands:</b></p>
<p>
The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>
This position requires the ability to perform an aerobic activity for the duration of a class. Bending and moving is done frequently. Physical activity will include standing, walking, reaching with hands and arms, climbing or balancing, sitting and stooping, kneeling, crouching, or crawling. Lifting requirements are 50 pounds and occasionally up to 100 pounds. The vision requirements are close, distant, depth, and ability to adjust focus.</p>
<p><b>
