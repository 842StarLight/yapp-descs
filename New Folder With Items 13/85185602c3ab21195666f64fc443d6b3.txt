<h2>Controller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>PRIMARY OBJECTIVES:</b> To oversee the accounting and administration functions of the company assuring that:
<br>? Timely and accurate generation and distribution of financial statements, production data, labor efficiency data and project results.
<br>? Timely preparation of project invoices and collection follow-up to reduce Accounts Receivable Days Outstanding and improve Inventory Turns.
<br><br>
MAJOR AREAS OF ACCOUNTABILITY
<br>1. Full responsibility for monthly general ledger and financial statement package, including distribution of financial, production and labor results.
<br>2. Participate in the analysis and preparation of individual job statements and accuracy of job costing.
<br>3. Timely generation of monthly AIA billings using production information obtained from Project managers.
<br>4. Collection of balances owed. Identify questionable accounts to allow mechanics liens to be filed as necessary.
<br>5. Request project Insurance Certificates and Bonds as per job requirements.
<br>6. Monitor subcontract progress and payments made on subcontracts to prevent overpayments.
<br>7. Ensure timely payment of all vendors. Review all invoices and prepare checks.
<br>8. Prepare and submit sales &amp; use tax reports and other fillings as necessary.
<br>9. Oversee the maintenance and management of the office.
<br><br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITY FOR WORK OF OTHERS:</b> Accounting Staff (4), Office Administrator
<br><br>
<b>FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY:</b> Responsible for monitoring and collecting accounts receivable. Responsible for accuracy of general ledger.
<br><br>
<b>REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<b>Education:</b> 4-year accounting degree (C.P.A. certification is a plus)
<br><b>Knowledge and Skills:</b> Experience with computers and software, cost accounting, financial statement preparation and people management skills are required.
<br><b>Previous Experience:</b> 5 years of accounting experience (manufacturing experience or construction industry experience is a plus)
