<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Join the team at <b>WorldCare Pet Transport</b>, one of the world's leading pet relocation companies, as our <b>Business Development Manager</b> based in Darien, CT. In this role you will be responsible for building and maintaining our corporate accounts, knowledgeably expressing our company's offerings to decision makers in the corporate relocation industry, and actively creating opportunities to capture new business. We are seeking a highly motivated and articulate professional to help us serve the global corporate relocation industry. This position involves approximately 25-30% travel largely in the U.S.</p>
<p><b>Key responsibilities</b>:</p>
<p>* Source generation: Identifies new client opportunities through calling, networking, research and other forms of lead generation and then manages outreach along with effective follow-up. Develops new business in accordance with an agreed upon market development strategy</p>
<p>* Expands sales with client portfolio of existing accounts. Manages and builds current relationships in a B2B environment to identify growth opportunities by engaging with decision makers at our corporate accounts (which may include upper level management, team leaders and relocation counselors). Becomes one of our trusted experts on global pet relocation</p>
<p>* Creates and delivers presentations at client and prospect offices. Confidently and articulately represents our company at meetings with accounts. The key objective is to communicate our company mission, value proposition and long, successful track record.</p>
<p>* Organizes ongoing, quarterly and yearly client communication vehicles that report on our activities and results. Issues summaries and info graphics as needed</p>
<p>* Engages with accounts on a continuous basis to provide WorldCare with feedback on industry trends or important business changes</p>
<p>* Manages time efficiently for strongest results and provides regular updates to the WorldCare management team on progress</p>
<p>* Assists with completion of RFP as needed</p>
<p>* Represents company at industry events and conferences. Organizes aspects of conference participation</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<p><b>* </b>Bachelor's degree in a related field required</p>
<p>* 2-5 years of business development experience with demonstrated track record of sales achievement preferred, but will consider strong recent graduates. Experience within the corporate relocation industry is desirable but not required.</p>
<p>* Motivated, results-oriented professional with the ability to relate and adapt to different levels of decision-makers</p>
<p>* Extremely well-organized: ability to drive many projects at one time with excellent multi-tasking, prioritization and follow-up skills under strict deadlines</p>
<p>* Ability to travel, both by air and ground transportation approximately 25-30% of the time primarily in the U.S. as face-to-face business visits are integral to this role</p>
<p>* Basic knowledge of business development, economic principles and practices, as well as financial modeling</p>
<p>* Proficiency in Microsoft applications including Excel, Word, Outlook, PowerPoint. Experience with sales tracking software a plus</p>
<p>* Exceptional written, verbal and inter-personal skills necessary</p>
<p>* Driver's license required</p>
<p>* Highly professional demeanor with a superior work ethic is required. Must be comfortable in a fast-paced entrepreneurial environment and be flexible with changing business needs</p>
<p>* Passion for pets essential</p>
<p>If you have the related experience and skills and share our love for pets, we look forward to hearing from you by submitting your resume and cover letter. No phone calls, please. We are not offering relocation support currently, so we urge local candidates to apply for this position.</p>
<p>In addition to salary, we offer:</p>

<p>* Generous paid time off</p>
<p>* IRA plan</p>
<p>* An office that is in walking distance from the Darien, CT train station so commutable by train or car</p>
<p>* A casual workplace with a collaborative spirit</p>
