<h2>Full Charge Bookkeeper</h2>

<p>Immediate part-time position available for a bookkeeper at a small ecommerce company in Westlake. <p>· Filing</p>
<p>· Customer Service</p>
<p>· QB inventory Management</p>
<p>· Pay monthly bills</p>

<p>· MUST be organized and detail oriented</p>
<p>· Good communication skills</p>
<p>· AR/AP experience with QB</p>
<p>· Knowledge/experience in ecommerce is a plus</p>
