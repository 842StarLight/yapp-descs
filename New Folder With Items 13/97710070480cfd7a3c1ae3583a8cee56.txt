<br>About the role
<br>**************
<br><br>
The Deal Desk Lead will help to establish Envoy's order management function, maximize revenue, and facilitate rapid growth of Envoy's high performing Sales organization. Reporting to the Head of Sales Operations, this is a critical Sales Operations role, responsible for designing and implementing order management processes and practices, supporting sales management and sales teams, and assisting in the continued, rapid growth of Envoy and its Sales organization.
<br><br>
The successful candidate will work in a highly collaborative environment, in a very hands-on role, partnering with Sales teams, Sales Management, Product Management, Finance, Accounting and Legal.
<br><br>
--------
<br>You will
<br>--------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Create best practices around deal structures that adhere to company policies around Revenue Recognition, Discount targets, and discount approval policies. This position will be the first deal desk hire and be instrumental in building out this function.</li>
<li>Review Quotes and Order Forms for accuracy and completeness.</li>
<li>Maintain deal templates, playbooks, and best practice documentation to encourage consistent and efficient business practices globally as well as provide training to Sales team on deal structuring and best practices to increase deal velocity.</li>
<li>Provide process thought leadership on the quote-to-cash process</li>
<li>Manage the day-to-day optimization of complex non-standard deals and serve as the primary point of contact for sales, finance, and legal in these transactions.</li>
<li>Contribute to Deal Desk initiatives by leading special projects, including the creation of the playbook for non-standard deal structure models, order management automation, order management analytics and various performance metrics.</li>
<li>Work with Product Management, Finance and Sales management on new product releases to ensure a streamlined quote to cash process prior to product roll-out.</li>
<li>Maintain SLAs on deal turnaround times.</li>
<li>Assist with creating RFP assist process for Sales Operations.</li>
</ul>
--------------------
<br>You have
<br>--------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>3+ years experience driving operational improvements in a Deal Desk or Sales Operations function for a high growth B2B SaaS company. Experience operating on a team of over 80 sales reps is a plus.</li>
<li>2 years of SFDC experience</li>
<li>Strong business acumen and understanding of the Sales process (forecasting, bookings, revenue)</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce.com and contracts a must. Experience with ChargBee, a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively with necessary parties to ensure order detail and documentation is updated and entered in the system appropriately</li>
<li>Ability to work in a high-pressure environment. When month and quarter end orders come flooding in, you have the stamina to work late until the orders are in!</li>
<li>Self-motivated with a knack for being meticulous, detail oriented, and organized</li>
<li>Must have strong people/customer service skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record of process improvement for contract processing and general support to the sales organization.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and business judgment with the ability to handle projects independently end-to-end.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and critical thinking skills to understand sales policies and processes at a company and individual sales rep level.\</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills to work across the company with Sales, Finance, Legal and others.</li>
</ul>
