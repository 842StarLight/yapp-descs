<h2>Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>This is an opportunity to work in the fast-paced, data-driven world of digital advertising. You will work with clients who are not only looking to reach Morningstar audiences in North America, but also with those who want to expand their brand globally. In this role, you will manage 35+ clients by supporting the sales directors in all aspects of the sales cycle and account stewardship. You will strategize with sales directors, generate proposals and design advertising campaigns and sponsorships mainly across Morningstar.com, Morningstar Magazine and Morningstar events. You will be responsible for the success of all campaigns and satisfaction of our advertising partners. This position communicates directly with clients and advertising agencies, and internal groups at Morningstar: product managers, designers, programmers and account receivables. This position is based out of Chicago.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Key Job Responsibilities:
</p>
<p>* Provide service excellence to our partners by understanding client’s marketing initiatives and providing solutions across Morningstar’s digital and offline products
</p>
<p>* Assist in building proposals by working with the sales director and utilizing workflow and inventory management platform
</p>
<p>* Execute paperwork for contracts/scheduling, create monthly reports, optimize sales campaigns, and ensure client deliverables meet contract guidelines
</p>
<p>* Provide campaign performance reports &amp; screenshots to clients
</p>
<p>* Execute change requests and revisions to ad campaigns, creative updates, or other modifications
</p>
<p>* Function not only as the client’s day-to-day contact, but also the project manager for any custom programs sold
</p>
<p>* Work closely with ad operations team to ensure campaign delivery and pacing of program components
</p>
<p>* Partner with sales directors in business development activities such as brainstorming new packages and generating cross-selling opportunities<br>
</p>
