 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h1>
<p>Title: Account Executive</p>
<p>Job Type: Permanent</p>
<p>Location: Wayne-PA or other Sungard AS Office Location</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Opportunity:</b></p>
<p>We are looking for an Account Executive to generate prospective customers. Your #1 goal is to leverage the tools provided to meet and exceed quota. This position provides an opportunity to sell leading edge solutions for one of the premier organizations in the IT industry.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Grow Sungard AS revenue by closing net new logos.</li>
<li>Direct responsibility for the identification, contact, qualification and development of viable new accounts.</li>
<li>Business Development (BD) – Responsible for proactive, targeted, outbound prospecting; identify clear partner and account targets (ideal accounts &amp; buyers); uncover opportunities; clearly define the ask/business problem (needs identification); high-Level data gathering; qualify using pre-established criteria.</li>
<li>Manage the prospecting plan development and execution for new accounts.</li>
<li>Accurate and timely forecasting is an essential part of the account executive’s role.</li>
<li>Manage the communication and internal coordination of the Sungard AS sales support infrastructure to insure customer education and satisfaction.</li>
<li>Manage the on-going need to understand inter-related technologies that exist and keep current on new options as the business technologies expand and change.</li>
<li>Work supportively with colleagues, operating in a collegiate manner at all times and in line with the Company’s values.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 8 years of successful sales experience within a large technical account environment.</li>
<li>College Degree preferred but will consider applicants with equivalent work-related experience with a minimum educational requirement of a H.S. Diploma or GED equivalent.</li>
<li>Cloud Solution experience a plus</li>
<li>Previous proven expertise in managed services, high availability solutions, consulting and/or hardware/software solution sales.</li>
<li>An extraordinary drive to pursue opportunities both within the target client, as well as to marshal the necessary resources within Sungard to ensure strong solution creation and implementation of the strategy.</li>
<li>The ability to present to a large audience of C level decision makers persuasively and with ease is mandatory.</li>
<li>Exceptional ability to motivate and influence key contacts and decision makers.</li>
<li>Proven talent for making effective initial calls to target companies and the ability to develop relationships from a cold call or networking activity.
</li>
<li>Travel required as necessary.</li>
</ul>

<li>Employment is contingent upon successful completion of the background investigation and pre-employment drug screen.</li>
</ul>
