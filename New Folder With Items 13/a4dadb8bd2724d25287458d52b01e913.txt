This Position
<br><br>
The Proposal Writer serves as a primary writing and editing resource in the development, completion, and on-time submission of proposals and security questionnaires. Ultimately, the Proposal Writer manages the RFx response from beginning to end: from research and data collection/maintenance, to writing and editing/proofreading, to proposal document creation and revision, and (when needed) production. By working with the Proposal Manager, the Proposal Writer will respond to Request for Proposals (RFPs), Request for Quotations (RFQs), Requests for Information (RFIs), and Security Assessments. The role includes continuous review of and knowledge of the DocuSign solutions, continuous updating of boilerplate proposal documents, Master RFP Library, and Security related questions and responses.
<br><br>
This role reports to the Senior Manager of the Proposal Department.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Ownership of the RFx response assigned by the Manager. This includes planning, researching, developing, and editing complex content for proposals in response to customer solicitations – maintaining high standards around quality and accuracy of the final output</li>
<li>Serves as central point of contact on assigned proposals and leads multi-discipline or cross-functional team(s).</li>
<li>Chairs initial and ongoing status meetings to review progress on outstanding RFP content tasks/assignments</li>
<li>Writes content as needed for cover letters, executive summaries, and other materials as needed</li>
<li>Tracks roll-out processes, templates, standards, and best practices to successfully bid projects in a timely manner and providing suggestions as needed</li>
<li>Develops and maintains Proposal Content Database (Proposal Knowledge Center) to ensure the content accuracy</li>
<li>Researches and gathers corporate information, technical product information, and other standard materials. Captures any new language written for addition to the Knowledge Center</li>
<li>Identifies content gaps, then works with Sales, Technical, and Engineering experts, as well as contract &amp; legal representatives, to craft Subject Matter Expert (SME) technical responses. Manages these SME technical writing assignments to ensure that submissions are accurate, compliant with the outline and RFP instructions, and address win themes</li>
<li>Track RFx responses in Salesforce</li>
</ul>
