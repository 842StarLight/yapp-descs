Job Profile
<br><br>
As Community Relations Director, you will be responsible for meeting or exceeding occupancy and revenue rates through professional relationships, ongoing education, and community outreach opportunities. This includes facilitating the move-in process as well as community outreach opportunities, networking activities, and relationships with local Senior Centers, Chambers of Commerce, and other networking organizations. Duties include:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Giving a positive and informative first impression to all prospective residents and their families</li>
<li>Writing appropriate letters and notes as follow-up to inquiries</li>
<li>Maintaining and utilizing a system for follow-up with network sources</li>
<li>Organizing and facilitating all move-in activities while working collaboratively with the Wellness Director to coordinate smooth and timely resident move-ins</li>
<li>Conducting out-of-community resident assessments as needed</li>
<li>Meeting with the Executive Director regularly to discuss census, marketing strategies, and follow-up with referral sources.</li>
<li>Utilizing computer software system to maintain accurate and timely detailed information</li>
<li>Utilizing a computer software system to track and maintain a waitlist and future-interest list as well as track and organize vacancies, ensuring a quick and efficient move in process</li>
</ul>
