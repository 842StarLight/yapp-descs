<h2>Plumbing Instructor / Trainer / Teacher</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Do It Right Plumbers</b> of <b>Orange, CA</b> is looking to hire a <b>full-time or part-time Plumbing Instructor / Trainer / Teacher</b> to help educate and train our new team members on how assemble, install, and repair water heaters, fixtures, pipes, and fittings of fresh water and drainage systems in residential applications. As part of a dynamic and passionate team, our plumbing instructor will demonstrate technical and communicational competency to ensure that people whom have zero knowledge about the plumbing trade become competent members of the team. Are you a skilled Plumbing Instructor looking for a company with a <b>positive, supportive, and fun work culture</b> to <b>advance your career</b> with? If so, you may be perfect for this position!</p>
<p>
This is a full-time position, though we are open to it being part-time. This is a brand new position for our company and you will be responsible in creating the training program. In-field training will be required to ensure that our team members grasp the knowledge. As such, you will be the lead installer on many of the jobs with the trainees by your side. We typically work Monday through Friday as well as Saturdays when needed. This position offers a competitive pay, depending on certifications and experience. If this sounds like the opportunity that you've been looking for, please fill out our initial <b>3-minute, mobile-friendly application</b>.</p>

<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage, coach, and mentor new trainees that have no knowledge about the trade and train them to provide quality plumbing service to all customers.</li>
<li>
Conduct In-House Plumbing Instruction based upon the Nexstar Service Systems.</li>
<li>
Ensure adherence to state plumbing guidelines.</li>
<li>
Prevent or handle customer issues.</li>
<li>
Conduct work-site reviews to ensure safety measures are utilized.</li>
<li>
Monitor your team's performance standards weekly. Meet with team to review and determine areas where additional coaching and training is needed.</li>
<li>
Accompany plumbers to customer sites to observe performance and provide coaching &amp; training in various aspects including customer service, sales to include offering additional products and services, building commercial customer base, and safety/OSHA requirements.</li>
</ul>
