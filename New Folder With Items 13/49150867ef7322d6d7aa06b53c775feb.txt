<p>As a Sr. Product Solutions Engineer, you will be joining our Video Strategy and Product Growth team and acting as the technical expert to ensure a seamless and successful product launch. We are looking for an individual with ad tech experience solving technical and complex problems in programmatic advertising. The right candidate should possess the ability to work cross-functionally with internal teams and clients directly. Our ideal candidate approaches new situations with curiosity and enthusiasm, has an innate passion for learning new things and solving problems, and is highly motivated to deliver exceptional service.
