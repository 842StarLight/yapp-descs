<h2>Product Marketing Manager - Fire Safety</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>As the products, systems, and software organization for the Smart Infrastructure Division, Building Products offers total room and building solutions including fire safety. The Product Marketing Manager will be responsible for the outbound marketing activities to accelerate market adoption of the Siemens UL Fire safety portfolio. This position works collaboratively within the business segment to develop and implement strategic and tactical marketing plans. The ideal candidate will successfully synthesize market intelligence, digital inputs, and direct customer feedback to recommend shifts in the market approach and serve as a channel expert to drive go-to-market campaigns and assist in product road map and portfolio planning.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct market analysis to determine the most effective go-to-market strategies and launch appropriate campaigns to gain market share and acceptance across multiple sales channels.</li>
<li>
Partner with Marketing Communications to enhance and implement robust digital advertising, social media presence, and SEO / SEM in alignment with business segment and market channel goals.</li>
<li>
Develop and implement specific marketing plans consistent with the growth and profitability goals for product, systems and software categories.</li>
<li>
Continually gather customer feedback through various methods to assess market conditions and ensure market growth strategies are developed from Outside-In.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Product Management and Marketing Communications to develop and digitalize sales tools and marketing assets to promote awareness and drive market share growth across all sales channels.</li>
<li>
Oversee development, production, implementation and result tracking for key product go-to-market initiatives.</li>
<li>
Ensure consistency and accuracy of messaging for the segmentâ€™s offerings across all sales channels to market, including the Direct Siemens branch channel.</li>
<li>
Synthesize analytics and other market intelligence to recommend shifts in market approach and serve as in-house expert on market approach for product, systems, and software portfolio.</li>
<li>
Own definition of customer buying personas. Collaborate with product management to ensure these are integrated into product portfolio planning and management.</li>
<li>
Establish and manage the strategy for moving key leads and prospects through the adoption process to the Siemens brand.</li>
</ul>
