<h2>Transportation Technology Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>What You Will Do</b></p>
<p>Oversee the day to day management of the Client and Client’s supply chain needs, acting as the key contact for all communication and activity between the Client and RateLinx. You demonstrate complete and comprehensive understanding of Client(s) expectations and requirements and the ability to effectively coordinate execution within RateLinx. This role is a key facilitator for RateLinx, our Clients and the Carrier(s) and their operations and sales teams in terms of pricing negotiation and defining service parameters to support a Client RFP and/or annual pricing renewals. You will represent and utilize the full breadth of the RateLinx Enterprise suite to collaborate with the customer and deliver an innovative and complete solution.</p>
<p><b>Who We Are</b></p>
<p>RateLinx is a thought-leader in customized supply chain management software and logistics consulting. We are working to completely transform our customers’ supply chains. We connect all of our customers’ logistics data and deliver actionable intelligence to reduce costs, optimize strategies, and give our customers a competitive advantage. RateLinx has a values-based culture. Embracing ideas and responsiveness, we collaborate to solve our customer’s supply chain problems while achieving our goal: Being The Best.</p>
<p><b>Who You Are</b></p>
<p>You embrace our culture and share our values. You have at least a Bachelor’s Degree in supply chain or logistics related field. You bring over five years’ experience in either Logistics technology account management or senior analytics in Logistics.</p>
