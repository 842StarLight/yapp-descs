<h2>Sr. Business Systems Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Provide business analysis and process design/redesign expertise in one or more functional areas within the enterprise, specifically in the analysis of SAP SD/LE module - this is a hands on role; Maintain consistency of the Sap SD configuration area across all business units globally, Configure processes within R/3 on schedule in key areas of Sales and Distribution including but not limited to pricing, commissions, inter-company, Shipping, ATP, Outputs, IDOCs; Act as a liaison between IT and our business partners, manage functional projects through the project lifecycle as lead from start to finish for smaller projects; Monitor and report to management on the status of project efforts, anticipating/identifying issues that inhibit the attainment of project goals and implementing corrective actions; Providing accurate project estimates System configuration, unit testing, integration testing, user acceptance, and deployment on Transitioning projects to ongoing change requests, and providing post-implementation support as needed; Collaborate in planning, design, development and deployment of new applications, and enhancements to existing applications; Lead continuous improvement opportunities and appropriately challenge processes to ensure global consistency; Troubleshoot complex business and technical problems, work closely with SAP development/BI/BASIS/Change Management team as part of regular deployments and other activities and adhere to Qorvo IT SAP standards; Develop and retain relevant SAP skills – including participating in user groups, training, conferences and professional associations and cross train other members/ associates where and when appropriate; Debugging skill of 7/10 ratings due to customized nature of the landscape; Translate complex business concepts and technical terms.</p>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p>
Bachelor’s in Business Management, Computer Science, Management Information Systems or related field. 10 years industry experience to include each of the following: SAP SD/LE with an emphasis on design, configuration and troubleshooting; 4 full life cycle SAP SD implementations (incl. testing, training and documentation); Preparation of functional specifications, Configuration and testing of SD Module Integration of SD with MM, FI, Sap Intercompany settings, configuration and designing, Outputs, IDOC set up; SAP structures, configuration standards, object dependencies and user exits, security, PI, EDI; International SAP project experience; Integrating with other modules such as FI; China VAT, import/export processes.</p>
