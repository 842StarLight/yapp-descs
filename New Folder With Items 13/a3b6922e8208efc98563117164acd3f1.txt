<div><b>Function</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The jobholder is responsible for tactical and/or operational planning of components and/or finished good and/or spares and/or peripherals for a single or multiple production lines.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Manages the balance between the supply of components required for production, target inventory levels, continued/efficient production and timely delivery to customers. Plans finished goods repairs to meet the agreed turn around times with customers.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Implements and improves local supply chain master data and configuration of planning module of ERP system.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Translate the demand plan for finished goods and/or spares and/or peripherals into a supply plan and inventory plan. Assists in the preparation of the S&amp;OP process to come to an agreed long term and short term plan in line with the strategic defined KPI’s.</li>
<li>
Load up in an accurate and timely way the agreed supply plan into the Material Requirement Plan (MRP) system. Verifies the resulting purchase plan for components.</li>
<li>
Prepares the monthly production plan for a specific line(s).</li>
<li>
Translate the monthly production plan into a weekly/daily bucket and negotiate a consensus with production.</li>
<li>
Follow up on the plan realization by production and correct where necessary to meet the agreed sales schedule.</li>
<li>
Communicates component shortages and due dates for production to purchase department.</li>
<li>
Challenges production capabilities (accuracy of rooting times, tools, machine capabilities, etc).</li>
<li>
Implement changes in order priorities and analyse for the impact. As a result, implement the consensus planning.</li>
<li>
Gives feedback to sales-desk on progress of the schedule. Communicates and updates delivery schedules for the coming defined period.</li>
<li>
Create the daily materials issue plan to ensure a sufficient materials supply for the production.</li>
<li>
Assist in WIP management and WIP reduction.</li>
</ul>
