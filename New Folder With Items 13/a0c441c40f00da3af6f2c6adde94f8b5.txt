<h2>Sales Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>LE BOOK,</b> the international reference for the creative community - brings together creatives, marketers, communicators, from the worlds of technology, fashion, &amp; luxury advertising. In this highly diversified role, you will report to the General Manager of LE BOOK's NY office. You will use your experience to identify strategic opportunities that support revenue growth and product development. The LE BOOK products include our digital platform, annual CONNECTIONS creative tradeshow events in <b>NY, Chicago, Los Angeles, and San Francisco, London, Paris, Milan, Amsterdam, Berlin, Hamburg, and Geneva</b>. The Sales Representative for the NY office will focus on the U.S events and will generate revenue in 2 key areas: Event and Digital sales. This position is a terrific opportunity for an outgoing, energetic individual who wants to succeed in the dynamic world of art and technology. This full-time position includes a base salary, monthly commission, and a direct path for career growth.</p>

<ul>

<li>Open new markets, establish category leadership and expand our business</li>
<li>Ability to develop new business and manage existing business through face to face meetings.</li>
<li>Exceptional communication and presentation skills with a knack for consultative selling</li>
<li>Intelligence, positive attitude, enthusiasm, high energy, strong work ethic</li>
<li>Successfully prioritize and manage a database of prospects</li>
<li>Strong negotiation and presentation skills</li>
<li>Maintain client expectations and meet key deadlines</li>
<li>Aptitude for juggling multiple projects while constantly prioritizing</li>
<li>Affinity towards the art and technology markets a plus</li>
<li>Self-starter with willingness to roll-up-your-sleeves and work as a team player</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial and Creative!</li>
</ul>
