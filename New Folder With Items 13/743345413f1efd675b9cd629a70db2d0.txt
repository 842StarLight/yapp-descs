Job Summary: Team Leaders will manage different areas of operations in order to develop expertise in people leadership and tactical operations leadership. Leads a team of hourly non-management Partners.<br>
<br>
Essential Functions / Process Responsibilities include the following; other duties may be assigned as necessary:<br>
<ul>
<li>Provides daily supervision and support for an assigned group of hourly Partners. Maintains an environment within the facility where Partners trust and respect their Leader and work together as a team to accomplish goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Engages with Partners daily; clearly explains roles, expectations and performance measurements for each critical component that impacts business outcomes.</li>
</ul>
- Operates a team that consistently meets Customers- needs and expectations. Tracks, monitors &amp; resolves customer complaints. Responsible for service recovery efforts. Identifies trends and provides feedback.<br>
<ul>
<li>Monitors and evaluates key performance indicators for both daily and weekly team performance; measure against goals, gets team buy-in and provides feedback to the team.</li>
<li>Monitors and adjusts labor requirements to balance staffing levels and overtime costs while meeting daily facility performance expectations.</li>
<li>Takes an active role in the development of Partners. Provides leaning and growth opportunities. Addresses performance that does not meet expectations.</li>
</ul>
- Maintains a clean, safe, pest-free, and well organized facility. Follows established SOPs and ensures Partners are trained and in compliance. Documents measures through SOPs in accordance with OSHA and H-E-B guidelines.<br>
<ul>
<li>Recognizes Partner achievements</li>
<li>Majority of the time spent using independent judgment in making employment-related business decisions, or effectively recommend such decisions including but not limited to product and department related strategies, hiring, promoting, disciplining, suspending, discharging, rewarding or otherwise engaged in resolving Partner-related matters.</li>
</ul>
