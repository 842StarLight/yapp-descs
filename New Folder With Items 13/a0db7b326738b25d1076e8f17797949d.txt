<h2>Sr. Account Manager, Digital</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>GK3 Capital helps financial services companies adopt sales and marketing strategies that drive remarkable growth in a digital world. We are growing and are excited to announce that we are looking for a Sr. Account Manager with a minimum of 5 years of experience.</b></p>
<p>*<br>As a Sr. Digital Account Manager, you should have solid experience in marketing with a customer service approach. You should also have a clear idea of how digital tools add value to company strategy and performance.</p>

<ul><li>High level presentation skills; able to present complex ideas to clients in a way that produces understanding and impact</li></ul>

<ul><li>Set digital strategies for your assigned customers, considering their specific requirements</li></ul>
<ul><li>Suggest, design and implement digital projects to increase customer ROI</li></ul>
