<h2>Marketing Campaigns Manager Senior</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Purpose of Job</b></h2>
Leads the creation, facilitation and / or execution of complex marketing campaigns across multiple channels including - not limited to - media, direct, sponsorships and digital. Responsible for one or more of the following areas: ongoing management of agency resources; creating, managing and/or executing campaigns; and/or managing budgets.<br>
<br>
Collaborates and develops partnerships with various internal and external resources to develop and deliver marketing communications for one or more of the following: go-to-market plans, integrated tactical plans, project plans, milestone schedules, and/or creative strategy.<br>
<br>
We are currently seeking a talented Senior Marketing Campaigns Manager for the San Antonio Home Office.<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Requirements</b></h2>
<p><b>
Tasks:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develops and oversees overall project management of multiple, complex marketing campaigns and plans.</li>
<li>
Effectively directs Agency to ensure alignment with goals and prioritization.</li>
<li>
Coordinates and gains alignment with peers, enterprise partners and senior management.</li>
<li>
Responsible for communication of program, project and /or campaign through various reports/tool as required.</li>
<li>
Serves as a primary resource to team members on escalated matters of an unusual nature.</li>
<li>
Applies advanced knowledge of marketing principles.</li>
<li>
Applies an advanced understanding of resource tools to assess performance against marketing objectives.</li>
<li>
Begins to identify opportunities for review.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Minimum Education:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree required.</li>
<li>
4 additional years of related experience beyond the minimum required may be substituted in lieu of a degree.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Minimum Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
6 or more years of experience in a marketing, sales, advertising and/or communications function, to include at least 3 years’ experience in one or more of the following areas: direct response, sponsorships/events marketing, advertising, sales, digital or overall campaign management. Advanced knowledge of marketing industry and practices.</li>
<li>
Experience influencing business decisions, facilitating and/or participating in cross-functional teams, motivating and collaborating with key stakeholders, and implementing change management.</li>
</ul>
