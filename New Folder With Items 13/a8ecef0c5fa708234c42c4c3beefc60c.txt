<h2>Order Administration Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>Order Administration Associate – Greenville, Pennsylvania<br>
</div>
<p><br>
<b>Job</b><b> Purpose:</b>
</p>
<p>To provide the efficient and accurate processing of all purchase orders including EDI and non-EDI purchase orders. Also, coordinate and provide pricing implementation and review for all corporate sales and marketing objectives. The associate for this position should be detail oriented, customer centric and possess strong computer skills.<b>
</b></p>
<div>
<b>Key Responsibilities (Essential Job Functions):</b><b> </b>This self-motivated individual will provide support for, but not limited to, the following areas:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
 Responsible for the processing of orders including but not limited to:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
 Customer purchase orders</div>
<div>
 Show orders</div>
<div>
 Marketing Samples</div>
<div>
 Customer Samples</div>
<div>
 Reverse &amp; Rebills</div>
<div>
 Bill Only’s</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
 This function should include timely acceptance and processing of: fax orders, EDI orders, and email orders</div>
<div>
 Actively and accurately, execute all business practice updates, changes, processes, policies and procedures</div>
<div>
 This function will also include calling customers to secure accurate data needed to complete the order entry process</div>
<div>
 Goal should be to maintain an average same day order entry metric</div>
<div>
 Work directly with customers and internal resources to secure accurate data needed to facilitate all aspects of the EDI order process</div>
<div>
 Provide input and lead efforts to automate and drive best practice solutions and improvements for EDI processes &amp; procedures</div>
<div>
 Actively and accurately, execute all business practice updates, changes, processes, policies and procedures</div>
<div>
 Provide support and guidance for continued account and pricing database integrity. This includes enforcement of all internal policies &amp; procedures related to profitability and pricing regulations</div>
<div>
 Interpret, evaluate, coordinate, and communicate information for the purpose of pricing and profitability decision making</div>
<div>
 Research and provide resolution to all pricing discrepancies and errors</div>
<div>
 Actively and accurately, execute all business practice updates, changes, processes, policies and procedures</div>
<div>
 Servicing of orders, insuring that all orders requiring special attention are properly handled</div>
<div>
 Handling the splitting of orders, when necessary, from various shipping locations</div>
<div>
 Working in conjunction with the Customer Care regarding unusually large orders, special products or special requests</div>
<div>
 Assisting with customer correspondence regarding orders, delivery, status, pricing and products as directed by the Sales Administration Supervisor</div>
<div>
 Strive to automate all manual processes by identifying areas of improvement. Working closely with the IT Group to implement necessary requested changes</div>
<div>
 Actively participate in all necessary training development programs</div>
<div>
 Understand and adhere to corporate policies &amp; procedures</div>
<div>
 Participate in special projects and/or provide support to project teams regarding improvements or changes to existing programs or implementation of new programs as it relates to order processing</div>
<div>
 Proactively provide and communicate process &amp; system improvement needs</div>
<div>
 Other duties as assigned</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Experience &amp; Education:
</b></p>
<p><b>Required:</b>
 </p>
<div>One to Three years of business experience with responsibilities in business administration, sales, and project management</div>
<p>
 Bachelor’s degree in a technical or business discipline, or equivalent experience</p>
<p><b>
Desired:
 </b></p>
<p>Associate’s Degree in Business Administration or equivalent</p>
<p><b>
Computer Skills</b><b>:</b></p>
<div>
 MS Office products to include Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook and Oracle</div>
<p>
 Advanced knowledge of JDE</p>
<p><b>
Key Competencies</b><b>:</b></p>
<div>
 Strong written and oral communication skills and knowledge of multiple software and business data systems</div>
<div>
 Strong computer skills with knowledge of MS Access, Excel, and Word. Knowledge of JD Edwards or Salesforce a plus</div>
<div>
 Associate should be detail oriented and able to adapt in a fast pace environment and comprehend overall business objectives for the entire company</div>
<div>
 Must have demonstrated indirect leadership abilities and be able to adjust to changing priorities</div>
<div>
 Must have strong organizational and interpersonal skills as well as solid oral and written communication skills</div>
