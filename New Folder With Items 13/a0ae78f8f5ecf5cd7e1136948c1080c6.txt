<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
<br>
RegEd seeks a Sr. Product Manager to manage product lifecycles of SaaS-based solutions within RegEd’s Enterprise Compliance Management portfolio. As Sr. Product Manager, you will be responsible for increasing adoption, growing revenue, increasing profit and enhancing customer satisfaction. You must possess a blend of business and technical savvy, customer evangelism, pragmatic decision making, a big-picture vision and the execution talent to make that vision a reality. You will lead the product lifecycle from strategic planning and requirements definition to release management and go-to-market execution. You must enjoy spending time in the market to understand customer challenges and developing innovative solutions to drive the product roadmap. You must be able to communicate with all areas of the company, from aiding sales engagements to collaborating with implementation teams. You will work closely with development teams, representing customer requirements and rationalizing market needs to successfully deliver roadmap priorities. You will coordinate the planning and launch execution of exciting new products as well as coordinate release refreshes to enhance existing products. You should have exceptional communications skills, strong creative writing talents and a technical aptitude for enterprise solutions. The product has its roots in state and federal financial services legislation; familiarity with this space and knowledge of a SaaS-based business model will be a plus.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions/Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Manage product life cycle from strategic planning to tactical activities; Balance long term and short term road map needs, including supporting technology initiatives to scale, secure and innovate within the product line</li>
<li>Specify user and market requirements for current and future products by conducting market research supported by on-going visits to customers,non-customers, partners and conferences. Make recommendations using appropriate business filters including revenue, profit and market potential, for new market opportunities, product extensions, new products, product retirements or buy/build/partner activities</li>
<li>Develop go-to-market and marketing plans working with multiple functional groups, including Marketing, to implement.</li>
<li>Play a key role in supporting sales engagements, including prospect requirements analysis, product demonstrations, pricing, RFI responses, proposal reviews and contract reviews</li>
<li>Communicate the value proposition of the products to the sales team and help to develop the sales tools that support the selling process of your products</li>
<li>Develop sound, value-based pricing models, positioning and sales collateral strategies to maximize revenue and market penetration</li>
<li>Develop business requirements documentation, and some technical requirements documentation, for business analysts, development teams and quality assurance, and coordinate releases</li>
<li>Work with implementation and service teams to establish processes for implementation and ongoing maintenance of the product.</li>
<li>Keep current on regulatory, industry and market changes impacting the product</li>
<li>Support RegEd at trade shows, conferences, industry events and customer advisory boards</li>
<li>Manage Profit and Loss (P&amp;L) for your products. Make recommendations using appropriate business and financial metrics to improve your product’s financial health.</li>
<li>Manages multiple projects concurrently in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Contributes to situations in which workload exceeds standard work hours</li>
</ul>
<br>
Job Related Experience: 7+ years full-lifecycle product management experience or equivalent<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>7+ years’ experience in “full life cycle” product management including market research, business requirements development, competitive analysis, new product launches, including development of business requirements, development of strategic product plans, go to market plans, product launch plans, product marketing plans and sales programs for B2B software / SaaS solutions.</li>
<li>Superb verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Strong initiative and self-motivation</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills; a demonstrated ability to work collaboratively with a wide range of individuals at all levels of the organization</li>
<li>Experience conducting competitive analysis to identify weaknesses and market opportunities</li>
<li>Experience working with sales teams on RFP/RFI bid response, product section and pricing guidance.</li>
<li>Experience with managing P&amp;L of SaaS product</li>
<li>Experience with writing user and functional business requirements</li>
<li>Experience with release planning, working with development teams and using SDLC tools</li>
<li>Ability to work within a nimble, fast-paced environment</li>
