<h2>Exchange Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We're looking for an Exchange Analyst to join our New York Team. In this role, you will help create strategic stories around insights derived from a wide range of data. You will work alongside our Demand-side Business Development team to understand the business needs of DSPs, Agencies, and Agency Trading Desks and provide compelling stories, client insights, and strategy.
<br><br>
<b>Here's what we want:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Critical thinker: Logical, concise ideas around projects with a strong sense of urgency, understanding the larger story behind requests and business acumen.</li>
<li>Problem solver: Experience working with teams and collaborating across groups to achieve desired results.</li>
<li>Independent: Comfort with ambiguity and self-starter with an aptitude for defining new processes.</li>
<li>Articulate: Experience with collaborative documents, creating presentations and speaking to audiences that are not technical.</li>
<li>Experienced: minimum 4 years working experience; 0-1 years in advertising.</li>
</ul>
<b>Here's what you'll be doing:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Analyze data points and craft compelling stories around the actionable insights, producing high-level visualizations to support the story and grow the business</li>
<li>Establish business processes around teams utilizing analytics support</li>
<li>Assist internal teams with identifying key metrics to assist in their analyses</li>
<li>Ability to find and apply data and research to grow business revenue</li>
<li>Liaise with engineering, ad operations, and sales team resources to integrate proven strategies and analysis into new processes and solutions</li>
</ul>
<b>Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Comfortable knowledge of Google Suite (Sheets, Data Studio) or Microsoft Office (Excel, Power BI)</li>
<li>Resourceful.</li>
<li>Familiarity with reading or creating visualizations and presenting to audiences who may not be technically savvy</li>
<li>Knowledge of Domo or experience in practice a plus</li>
<li>Background in AdTech or knowledge of the AdTech landscape a bonus</li>
</ul>
<b>Why you'll love working here:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Our New York office serves as our strategic hub and headquarters. Located in the Flatiron District, this office houses the majority of our Client Services, Marketing, and Executive teams.</li>
<li>We offer competitive compensation, a comprehensive benefits package, 401(K), maternity/paternity leave as well as a fantastic vacation policy.</li>
<li>Perks include a fully stocked fridge/pantry/bar, catered lunches every Friday and views of the Empire State Building!</li>
</ul>
