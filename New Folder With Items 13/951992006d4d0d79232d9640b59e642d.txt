<h2>Marketing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<p>Job Opening Date: April 19, 2019 Job Closing Date: When position is filled
</p>
<p>Job Title: Marketing Associate Department: Commercial
</p>
<p>Job Location: Long Beach, Calif. Contact: Human Resources Dept.
</p>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Skills Required
 </b></p>
<p>Develop all aspects of marketing campaigns: campaign strategy, creative assets, copy writing, HTML templates,<br>
list and database management, campaign execution and reporting.
 </p>
<p>Coordinate offline and online events:
</p>
<p>o Work with sales (and marketing) leadership to book trade shows, organize collateral, reserve equipment,<br>
prepare shipment to/from tradeshow, promote event to contacts, update leads lists to database,<br>
execute follow up campaigns and report on success of event.
</p>
<p>o Work with marketing leadership to develop webinars: set up, promotion, list development, database<br>
updates and reporting.
 </p>
<p>Develop promotional materials: adverts, flyers, catalogs, etc.
 </p>
<p>Develop marketing content to drive demand and generate leads.
 </p>
<p>Update the web site to support events and other promotions.
 </p>
<p>Develop and maintain social media messaging channels.
 </p>
<p>Support global distributor network with regular communications and updates on products, pricing and events.
 </p>
<p>Manage and update marketing contacts in databases (Dynamics 365 for Sales/CRM and Marketing Automation)<br>
and develop and maintain lists to feed lead generation campaigns.
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Education and/or Work Experience
 </b></p>
<p>BA/BS with 1 – 3 years’ experience, preferably with a background in marketing communications, graphic design,<br>
video and website content management.
 </p>
<p>Experienced in marketing communications and graphic designs.
 </p>
<p>Excellent communication skills, both written and oral.
 </p>
<p>Organized and detail oriented with excellent project management skills. Be able to effectively manage different<br>
types of projects and multiple deadlines.
 </p>
<p>Produce high-quality, accurate work while managing a large number of projects.
 </p>
<p>Self-motived and efficient with proactive approach to getting things done.
 </p>
<p>Work with a cross-functional team to produce results in timely manner.
 </p>
<p>Technical knowledge and computer skills for the following software programs: Microsoft Office, Adobe<br>
PhotoShop, Publisher/InDesign, HTML and a number of other programs.
 </p>
<p>Understand complex technology and databases, including but not limited to Customer Relationship Management<br>
(CRM) tools.
 </p>
<p>Be able to work with large, complex lists and databases in Excel and other programs. Segment lists and provide<br>
A/B testing strategies for campaigns.
 </p>
<p>Be able to analyze campaign performance and publish regular reports to team with insights on how to optimize.
 </p>
<p>Video experience a plus.
 </p>
<p>Lift and carry marketing materials including catalogs, manuals, display booths, posters, and other print materials.
 </p>
<p>Lift and carry NHIC products up to 25 lbs. of weight for tradeshows and conferences.<br>
</p>
