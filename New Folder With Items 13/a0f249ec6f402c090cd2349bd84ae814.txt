<h2>Dynamics 365/AX Manufacturing/Production Business Consultant</h2>

<p>
In preparation for 2020, we’re growing and looking for a highly skilled Microsoft Dynamics 365/AX Business Consultant with expertise in Manufacturing and Production functionality to join our Delivery and Support team. You will be working with our certified Microsoft experts as well as working with other global leading partners to transform business by connecting people and processes, and by making management and operations easier.</p>
<p>
We recognize that our people are our most important resource. That’s why Avantiico believes in providing our people with professional development, offering diverse and challenging work, and providing work-life balance with less than 35% travel.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>
The Business Consultant position is responsible for providing functional guidance on customer implementations, upgrades, and support of Dynamics® 365 Finance and Operations and AX (D365/AX). Working in partnership with Avantiico’s Delivery &amp; Support team, the Business Consultant will be responsible for gathering business requirements, fit/gap analysis findings, functional design documents, test scripts, training documents, and progress updates throughout implementation lifecycle. Additionally, this position will assist in pre-sales/marketing activities such as blogs, videos, and webinars.<br>
<br>
You will require business and application expertise in Microsoft Dynamics 365/AX Manufacturing and Production functionality including production control, discrete, lean, and process manufacturing. The qualified candidate is expected to fill the role as a subject matter expert on one or multiple projects in analysis, configuration, and testing of Dynamics 365/AX modules. Acting as a solution advisor and establishing client relations is vital to the role. Additionally, the right candidate will be an individual with strong attention to detail in business process improvements and the ability to see the big picture.<br>
<br>
Avantiico is a high-performance workplace; the Manufacturing and Production Business Consultant is expected to work multiple projects with competing deadlines successfully. Avantiico is a leader in the Dynamics ERP and CRM industry and is expected to continue its significant growth, which will allow the right candidate ample opportunity for professional development.<br>
<br>
This is a full-time position based in either San Diego, Irvine, Los Angeles, Temecula, CA. This job will require up to 35% travel or more if working remotely.<br>
<br>
<b>SKILLS &amp; EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have a Bachelor’s degree, preferably in Management, Accounting, Finance, Management Information Systems or Information Technology</li>
<li>
Minimum of four years of relevant experience and one full lifecycle implementation of Dynamics 365/AX with a focus in Supply Chain and Manufacturing</li>
<li>
Deep business expertise in Manufacturing and Production functionality including production control, discrete, lean, and process manufacturing</li>
<li>
Cross-functional knowledge of other business modules and functional areas including Analytics, Supply Chain (Trade &amp; Logistics), Manufacturing, Finance, Professional Services, Service Management, Transportation, CRM, Talent, and Retail is a plus</li>
<li>
Current certification a plus or the ability to obtain after joining within 90 days of employment</li>
<li>
Possess excellent communication and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
