<h2>Business Continuity Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Functional Area:
</p>OP - Operations
<p></p>Estimated Travel Percentage (%): No Travel
<p></p>Relocation Provided: Yes
<p></p>Fortitude Group Services, Inc
<p></p>
<p>The Business Continuity Program Manager will oversee and coordinate the full life cycle of business continuity activities, to include program management, business impact analysis, plan development and maintenance, training, exercises and continual improvement. This position will interact with all levels leadership. The individual will partner with the Risk Officer on business continuity and crisis management program strategies. The program consists of the strategies required to enable rapid recovery of business services from a significant disruptive event. It also includes crisis management strategies needed to provide enterprise decision making and communications during disruptive events and mitigate risks to the firm. This is a great role for someone with an operations background or an emerging operations leader.
</p>
<p><b>DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop business recovery strategies as the organization becomes more complex and large scale.
</li>
<li>Stand up incident management team is in place at all locations.
</li>
<li>Prepare business continuity program reports to senior management committees and board of directors.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain risk assessments for lines of business, facilities and technology, identify gaps and mitigate risks.
</li>
<li>Enhance business continuity and crisis management governance documents, tools, templates and forms
</li>
<li>Consult with line of business leaders to maintain department plans that meet recovery objectives.
</li>
<li>Lead firm-wide application recovery testing with core service providers and other critical service providers.
</li>
<li>Serve as subject matter expert for third-party risk management oversight activities, including critical third-party service provider recovery strategies and objectives.
</li>
<li>Monitor and perform effective challenge of line of business resumption testing, facility management life safety, evacuation and generator testing, and information technology network and system failover, redundancy and recovery testing.
</li>
<li>Lead and maintain an enterprise-wide crisis management program and business impact analyses
</li>
<li>Engage designated responders during a crisis and coordinate response and recovery process.
</li>
<li>Coordinate management and executive management teams and responses.
</li>
<li>Reporting and modeling results
</li>
<li>Create training and awareness presentations or materials.
</li>
</ul>
