<p>As the world’s leading vendor of Cyber Security, facing the most sophisticated threats and attacks, we’ve assembled a global team of the most driven, creative and innovative people. At Check Point, our employees are redefining the security landscape by meeting our customers’ real-time needs and providing our cutting-edge technologies and services to an ever-growing customer base.</p>
<p>

<p><b>
Your role in a nutshell</b></p>
<p>
No two days for a Check Point security engineer are the same, from getting hands-on with the most advanced technologies in the market, to learning more about the customer’s most pressing security challenges and playing a crucial part in designing a solution, you’re always at the heart of fascinating projects!</p>
<p>
Reporting to the local Security Engineering Manager</p>
<p><b>
You will be responsible for</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with the sales team to identify prospects</li>
<li>Demonstrate our full portfolio of solutions that help keep organizations safe from threats and protect their operations and information</li>
<li>Educate customers, prospects and partners on the diversity of technical, operational and business challenges many organizations face and how Check Point solutions can solve them</li>
<li>Drive meetings with potential and existing customers to identify their problems and provide appropriate solutions</li>
<li>Address technical questions, offer insight into best practices and manage Proof-of-Concepts</li>
<li>Respond to RFI's &amp; RFP's</li>
<li>Engage and educate key partners and customers on the latest security technologies</li>
<li>Active participation in cybersecurity events</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<div></div>Desired Background
<p><b>What you bring to the table</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4-8 years of engineering and pre-sales experience</li>
<li>Possess strong analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Good understanding of typical customers networks</li>
<li>Ability to create an end-to-end security solution for a customer’s environment</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Relevant certifications highly preferred, e.g. CCNP / CISSP/ CCSA / CCSE</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you'll get in return</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Exposure to exciting and award-winning technologies in the Cyber Security industry</li>
<li>Meet the people behind our cutting-edge technologies during our New Hire Training held in Israel</li>
<li>Opportunity to fulfill your potential by rolling out creative ideas and projects</li>
<li>Work with and build a long-lasting relationship with a team of brilliant people</li>
</ul>
