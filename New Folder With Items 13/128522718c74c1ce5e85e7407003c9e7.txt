<h2>Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
Cova is looking for a talented and driven a driven Sales Manager to join our team. Someone with a management background that can create and execute sales strategy to gain market share in Cova's targeted markets. You would be and integral part of a senior and fast-moving leadership team, responsible for sales and revenue. With a mission of improving and extending our reach through multiple sales channels across North America's rapidly developing legal cannabis industry. As we continue to grow faster than any of our peers ever had, this role will add to our existing momentum in the market.</p>
<p><b>
Purpose of Role</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the Direct Sales Team, Sales Results and Top line Revenue</li>
<li>
Explore and expand sales channels to include: inside sales, seminar sales, telephone sales</li>
<li>
Enhance and fortify Sales Training</li>
<li>
Contribute market and competitive information to the Leadership team, including product input and guidance.</li>
<li>
Insure that the sales organization is of the highest integrity and professionalism, creating a great experience with all prospects and clients who interact with them and Cova.</li>
<li>
Continuous improvement of the Cova Sales organization.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Main Attributes for this Role</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Been on the ground floor of a young, fast growing company, wearing many hats and accountable to peers for superior performance.</li>
<li>
Software or Technology Sales – understanding of how a software company works and how SaaS/technology is sold.</li>
<li>
Made a major contribution to past companies through demonstrated success as measured by meaningful sales accomplishments: strategic accounts, new markets, innovative channel development.</li>
<li>
Motivation and drive – Career advancement in a similarly competitive industry by outperforming internal peers and outselling competitors and demonstrated performance.</li>
<li>
Running a multi-state Sales Organization – management of a remote sales force and/or working within a geographically distributed support organization.</li>
<li>
Hiring and Coaching Sales People – proficient at recruitment and managing new hires through ramp-up and to quota attainment. Ability to assist and develop sales peoples at all skill levels.</li>
<li>
Enterprise Sales – managed RFP's, engaged internal groups to assist and support sales efforts.</li>
<li>
Strategic planning – through understanding of the market, the competitors, development and positioning of the product and financial considerations, forecasting not only sales and revenue, but what can be applied to create plans.
</li>
<li>Utilization of CRM – experience in utilizing and managing a sales organization by tracking activity, communications and effectiveness through performance monitoring.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Desired Skills &amp; Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree
</li>
<li>10+ years' work experience</li>
<li>
Experience building and managing a sales organization</li>
<li>
Successful sales career</li>
<li>
Comfortable working with legal cannabis dispensaries</li>
<li>
Demonstrated leadership capabilities</li>
<li>
Proven record of success in a fast-moving environment: execution of complex plans</li>
<li>
Excellent presentation and communications skills
</li>
<li>Client recommendations</li>
<li>
Travel 50-75% within US and Canada</li>
</ul>

Application process:</b></p>
