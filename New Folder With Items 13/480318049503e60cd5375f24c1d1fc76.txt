 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About SurveyGizmo
<br>-----------------
<br><br>
Founded in 2006, SurveyGizmo is a powerful survey and data insights platform that empowers business professionals to make informed decisions. As SaaS application software, it offers user-friendly data collection tools for understanding customers, markets, and employees in real time and communicating this information across an organization. It provides data insights in over 205 countries, with 50K new surveys created and 5M responses collected every week.
<br><br>
SurveyGizmo has tremendous opportunity to continue this growth, based on current market size and the potential for more sophisticated product positioning and a robust sales and marketing engine. Details on SurveyGizmo's products and services can be found on our website (www.surveygizmo.com).

What You'll Do
<br><br>
We are seeking an Ops Rockstar looking to help take our company to the next level of growth and scale! Our ideal candidate is process focused, has experience in supporting a growing Sales &amp; Marketing organization, and is looking to make strategic and tactical contributions that will set the stage for world-class, global operations. Our ideal candidate also strives for exceptional leadership and is someone that can partner cross-functionally. Reporting to the SVP of Sales, the Sales &amp; Marketing Operations Director is a key member of the Sales Leadership team. Sales &amp; Marketing Operations is a vital link between the Business key departments supporting the Business. The Sales &amp; Marketing Ops Director will be instrumental in helping to drive and execute the sales strategy by developing policies, plans, processes, and systems for data-driven decision-making, analyses, and alignment across functions inside SurveyGizmo.
