<h2>Commercial Lines Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>ABOUT HUB:</b><br>
In a rapidly changing world, HUB advises businesses and individuals on how to prepare for the unexpected. As one of the worlds' largest insurance brokers, our focus is dedicated to providing our customers with the peace of mind that what matters most will be protected-through unrelenting advocacy and tailored insurance solutions that put our clients in control. Our growing team of professionals across North America represents a broad, deep and one-of -a kind aggregation of entrepreneurs and leaders recognized for their excellence throughout the insurance community.
<br><br>
<b>THE OPPORTUNITY:</b><br>
HUB’s Customer Service professionals provide critical support to our customers through account management, claims management, day-to-day client oversight, and underwriting services that make us a leading sales and service organization in the insurance industry. Through a robust network of resources, you will be amongst some of the industry’s top talent to help guide and support our customer’s needs and provide them with innovative solutions.
<br><br>
The Commercial Lines Assistant is responsible for providing all aspects of service support to commercial lines insurance customers. This person will work under the direction of one or more account managers or customer service agents. The Account Manager or Customer Service Agent will delegate work assignments to the Commercial Lines Assistant. If needed, account servicing, additional training and problem solving assistance will be provided by the Account Manager, Customer Service Agent or Department Manager. The Commercial Lines Assistant supports the goals and values of DMIG.
<br><br>
<b>Process policies:</b> e-file/attach agent copy and process original copy for delivery to the insured – AM/CSA will proof policy
<br><br>
Issue Evidences and Certificates of Insurance
<br><br>
Process direct bill endorsements
<br><br>
Check on policy cancellation status with carriers/premium finance companies and report back to AM/CSA
<br><br>
Order MVRs and update driver lists
<br><br>
Order loss runs
<br><br>
Order policy changes and update agency management system – when delegated to do so from AM/CSA
<br><br>
Issue auto ID cards
<br><br>
<b>Assist with back up receptionist duties including:</b> covering front desk phones, processing mail, scanning mail, routing faxes, as well as end of day kitchen duties
<br><br>
<b>Miscellaneous duties:</b> e-filing/attaching of drop filing, printing, e-filing/attaching of on-line reports, policies, endorsements, cancellation warnings, department housekeeping and all other work assignments delegated by the account manager or customer service agent
