<p><b>Business Development Associate</b></p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Kensington Sales Group was born in 2016 as an outsourced inside sales team for companies selling to Public Safety. We're maintaining a full-steam-ahead momentum and looking to bring on exceptional individuals to directly support and learn from our Co-Founders.</p>
<p><b>YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES...</b></p>
<p>We are looking for extraordinary people who want to be great at what they do. You’ll be responsible for strategically identifying and contacting prospects who best fit our ideal customers’ profiles. You’ll need a strong work ethic, exceptional communication, and interpersonal skills along with the ability to quickly develop rapport with senior executives.</p>
<ul>
<li>Working directly with our Co-Founders to support sales efforts</li>
<li>Making sales and marketing calls including cold calling, follow up calls, setting appointments, and meeting logistics</li>
<li>Initiating research of prospects and qualifying prospects</li>
<li>Establishing and building relationships with these contacts to develop leads</li>
<li>Engaging in active listening with callers, confirming or clarifying information, and addressing any questions they may have as needed</li>
<li>Utilizing software, databases, and sales tools appropriately</li>
<li>Researching, understanding, and verbally articulating a variety of topics and materials by phone, in person and in email communication</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT...</b></p>
<p>For the right person this is an awesome opportunity to be trained on the job and set yourself up for a lucrative career in sales. Our project pipeline is full and the only thing holding us back from reaching the next level is finding talented people to join our team.</p>
<p><b>WHAT WE’RE LOOKING FOR...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced and high energy environment</li>
<li>Energetic phone presence and excellent active listening skills</li>
<li>Proven success at building and maintaining relationships</li>
<li>Comfortable learning new technologies</li>
<li>Positive attitude and constantly searching for areas of improvement</li>
</ul>
