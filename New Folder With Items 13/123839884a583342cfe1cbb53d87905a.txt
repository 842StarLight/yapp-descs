<p>We have an exciting opening for a talented sales-minded individual who is interested in working for one of Seattle’s hottest startups. An award winning technology company that has been featured in the Seattle Times, GeekWire, 425 Magazine and Puget Sound Business Journal.</p>
<p>At Finagraph and we are building software and services to radically transform the lending experience for banks and businesses and to enable small businesses to eliminate cash flow as a reason for failing. If you like working in a fast-paced, rewarding and customer-first environment, that is changing the world, then Finagraph is the place.</p>
<p>We are looking for a high-energy, high-performing Account Executive (AE) to help grow and revenue for our high-powered SaaS product CashFlowTool.</p>
<p>In this role, your job will be to close deals by uncovering prospects' challenges and driving home the value of our solution. You will work closely with Sales Development Reps to advance pipeline and Partner Success Managers to ensure prospects receive the results needed to become excited, successful customers.</p>
<p>Experience you will have:</p>
<ul>
<li>Expert level pipeline management</li>
<li>Convert discovery calls set up by a Sales Development Rep into pipeline</li>
<li>Guide deals through the sales process and close business</li>
<li>Collect and maintain the data needed for effective forecasting</li>
<li>Own a concrete quarterly revenue quota target and forecast monthly progress against quota</li>
<li>Execute effective hand-offs to Partner Success Managers to ensure the continued success of the prospect</li>
<li>Provide continuous input to our ever-improving sales process to make the entire sales org better</li>
<li>Contribute to the product, marketing, success, and other teams to make Finagraph a better company</li>
<li>Have 2-5 years of selling experience (SaaS sales a plus, but not required)</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualities you posses:</p>
<ul><li>Drive and work ethic that allows you to crush goals and quotas</li></ul>
<ul>

<li>Flexibility– All deals are unique, and you can ask the right questions and use different strategies to provide value to different types of buyers</li>
<li>Fast learner – capable of grasping and mastering the CashFlowTool platform in a short time frame and able to articulate its value to different types of buyers</li>
<li>Data Driven &amp; Strategic – you can whiteboard a strategy and then build a spreadsheet that supports how the numbers work to get there</li>
