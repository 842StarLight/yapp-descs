<div>
This program offers you the opportunity to get going quickly with responsibilities that can quickly build marketing skill sets and leadership capabilities.</div>
<ul><li>
This is a three-year rotational program, empowering leaders through challenging assignments, advanced training, and mentoring. Your skills and performance are evaluated following each rotation through measurement of achievement of business objectives and results. The program includes three 1-year rotations: two at different Consumer operating companies and one in Consumer Customer Development. You will receive formalized training to develop broader marketing and leadership skills.</li></ul>
