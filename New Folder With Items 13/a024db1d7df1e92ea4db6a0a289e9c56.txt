<h2>Antenna/RF Quality Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>As a leading worldwide provider of navigation devices and wearable technology, our focus is on developing, designing and supporting superior products. Our advanced technology promotes performance, safety and ease of use in every market we serve. That makes us an engineering and technology company with a keen focus on recruiting talent in those fields. But the same vertical integration business model that keeps all design and manufacturing processes in-house also gives us the incentive to hire top performers from different backgrounds, including operations, finance and accounting, information technology, sales, marketing and communications.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Our technicians improve the quality of our products through the use of specialized equipment, devices and instrumentation. Whether verifying and calibrating equipment, tools and fixtures used to design, test or produce products our technicians are focused on keeping us ahead of the pack.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We're seeking a Quality Technician 1 at our headquarters in Olathe, KS. In this role, you'll organize and perform testing and/or calibration activities by reviewing schedules, operating equipment and producing accurate records.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Other essential functions include:</div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Follow schedules and perform tasks as assigned</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrate basic knowledge and understanding of electronics and/or mechanical theory and applications</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform basic tests and/or calibrations in accordance with plans and procedures</div></li>
</ul></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Other key responsibilities for this role include:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
EMC / RF / Antenna:</div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Review test schedules to determine which requests are ready for execution</div></li>
<li><div>
Conduct basic prioritization activities and communicate outcomes to the team</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform testing by applying the appropriate level of measurement skills, equipment operation and tool utilization</div></li>
<li><div>
Understand procedures and diagnose issues as required</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain and ensure the integrity of test equipment and records</div></li>
<li><div>
Gain familiarity with engineering test procedures</div></li>
<li><div>
Operate basic test equipment to test and troubleshoot test failures, including using equipment such as PCB milling machines, multimeters and oscilloscopes</div></li>
</ul></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<div>
Qualified candidates will possess an Associate of Arts degree in Electronics Technology or Vocational Certificate in Electronics or Engineering Technology, or substantially related field or a minimum of 80 hours towards a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics Technology or a substantially related field or a high school diploma or GED with previous or Military training or OR equivalent combination of education and relevant experience.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Other requirements include:</div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Maintain a high-level attention to detail to insure accuracy</div></li>
<li><div>
Complete other duties as assigned</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrates strong mechanical aptitude related to using tools and measurement devices (i.e. hand tools, calipers, gages, etc.)</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrates strong electrical aptitude related to operating electrical equipment and test devices (i.e. multimeters, oscilloscopes, power supplies, etc.)</div></li>
<li><div>
Previous experience using Microsoft Office - Outlook, Word, and Excel</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated strong and effective verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills</div></li>
<li><div>
Must demonstrate the ability to move work and test equipment to and from work areas</div></li>
<li><div>
Must possess the ability to lift up to 55 pounds to effectively perform the essential functions of this job description</div></li>
</ul></div>
<div>
DESIRABLE QUALIFICATIONS:</div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Previous calibration, reliability or electronics experience</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience working in a calibration lab, test lab or engineering test environment</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience with ISO9000, ISO17025, AS9100, IATF6949</div></li>
</ul></div>
