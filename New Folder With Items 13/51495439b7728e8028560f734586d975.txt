<h2>Junior Laboratory Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Analytical Laboratory-Receiving Department has an opening on 2nd shift. Additional time might be required to be worked during busy periods.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Reviews generated analytical data before assignment of official doses or analytical results.
<br><br>
Collects documentation, performs evaluations, and corrects anomalies in laboratory activities and implements preventative measures to eliminate reoccurrence of incidents.
<br><br>
Conducts surveillance of operational aspects and associated work activities of laboratory operations personnel and implements corrective actions as defined.
<br><br>
Must be able to read, write, and comprehend applicable written and/or oral instructions.
<br><br>
Required to maintain neat, accurate and complete records and files.
<br><br>
Must be able to work overtime as needed.
<br><br>
Must be physically fit to perform the above job functions.
<br><br>
Must be familiar with and have a working knowledge of analytical equipment.
<br><br>
Must be able to utilize known quantities to calibrate equipment as assigned.
<br><br>
Must be able to work under little supervision.
<br><br>
Must be able to perform basic statistical calculations (average and standard deviation) and convert data to appropriate units.
<br><br>
Must have computer knowledge and experience with various software programs (e.g., word processing and spreadsheets).
<br><br>
Very good communication skills
<br><br>
Must have successfully passed comprehensive written examinations and safety training as required.
<br><br>
Must have some mechanical aptitude and be a quick learner.
<br><br>
<b>Educational Requirements:</b><br>
High school diploma and six hours of college level mathematics, chemistry, physics, or biology related courses.
<br><br>
B.S. Degree in an Engineering Discipline—preferred
<br><br>
<b>Special Requirements:</b><br>
Prior Landauer experience and knowledge of various Landauer dosimeter types will be a considering factor
<br><br>
Candidate must maintain neat, accurate and complete records and files.
<br><br>
Candidate must also have a high degree of self-motivation.
