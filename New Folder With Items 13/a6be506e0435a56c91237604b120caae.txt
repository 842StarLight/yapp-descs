<h2>Assistant Pre-Construction Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>GENERAL DESCRIPTION</b>
</p>
<p>The Pre-Construction Manager has responsibility for delivering LS, GMP, or reimbursable proposals to meet BE&amp;K company expectations of best-in-class proposals. This includes competitive pricing that meets or exceeds client’s budget and schedule expectations in GMP or negotiated scenarios (best value provider/ good stewardship in won positions).
</p>
<p><b>PRINCIPLE RESPONSIBILITIES</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Establish a project pre-construction plan in coordination with the Project Executive including all estimating, downstream pricing, buy out strategies, deliverables scope and dates, sample deliverables, staffing requirements and other activities to insure a smooth process leading to a no-surprises GMP. With the anticipation of client scope modifications occasionally these must be organized and presented in a cogent and salable manner.
</li>
<li>Drive the successful execution of the pre-construction plan internally and externally.
</li>
<li>Effectively manage internal and external resources to deliver anticipated results.
</li>
<li>Effectively manage with the Project Executive client relationships.
</li>
<li>Continuously analyze and recommend various technology and systems enhancements for adoption.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain working relationships with local and national subcontractors
</li>
<li>Proposal Phase
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Review pricing and other pertinent information to be included in each proposal.
</li>
<li>Conduct the final review of the proposal with the top team.
</li>
<li>Participate in sales presentations as required.
</li>
<li>Perform post mortem on proposals not resulting in award.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>8. Design/Pre-Construction Phase</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with pre-project planning</li>
<li>Assist with buy-out of selected suppliers scopes of work</li>
<li>Drive all external AE and consultant activities to ensure from the outset there is a clear understanding of project objectives in terms of project scope, cost and schedule and continuously monitor the team to advance within those established parameters
</li>
<li>Supervise and assist in preparation of conceptual and downstream detailed schedules.
</li>
<li>For design build proposals, assist in the selection of A/E firms and prepare written scopes for trades
</li>
<li>Review all documentation to ensure adequacy for estimating and pricing re scope and schedule.
</li>
<li>Direct value engineering process.
</li>
<li>Prepare the list of bidders and distribute to all participants for review and comments.
</li>
<li>Direct the meeting with the Project Executive between project manager/superintendent to get buy-in of scope, approach, cost and schedule.
</li>
<li>Direct the scope and pricing strategy for all work proposed and ensure the completeness and accuracy of all proposals.
</li>
<li>Ensure that the intent/scope of the original estimate is maintained during buyout.
</li>
<li>Review deliverables for subordinates error, compliance with contracts, opportunities to enhance profitability, and risk mitigation
</li>
<li>Manage GMAX final prep and bid day procedures, set and operate bid day.
</li>
</ul>
<p>9. Construction Phase
</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with large scope change estimates</li>
<li>Ensure design scope matches matched estimated and contracted/subcontracted scope.
</li>
<li>Prepare post-bid analysis.
</li>
</ul>
<p>10. Establish and maintain good relationships with all business contacts (including architects, engineers, subcontractors and owners)
</p>
<p>11. Establish and maintain best in class estimating, scheduling, cost control and process coordination systems as well as multiple databases.
</p>
<p>12. Evaluate supplier support and performance; increase supplier support and competitive edge.
</p>
<p>13. Work closely with the PC, risk management, insurance, legal and accounting throughout the entire process.
</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</b>
</p>
<p>4 year degree in an accredited construction/estimating related curriculum or experience equivalent to a 4 year degree.
</p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE AND SKILL REQUIREMENTS</b>
</p>
<p>Advocate and proficient user of a variety of technologies...including....list Assemble Synchro BIM 360 Glue, BIM 360 Field, Revit and Navisworks, etc.
</p>
<p>Competent with personal computer based scheduling software, and any other computer software or program skills as required for the position. Experience Viewpoint Construction Software strongly preferred.
</p>
<p>Proven competence and proficiency of position’s essential job functions, duties and responsibilities.
</p>
<p>Technical skills: Planning and scheduling, Cost Control, Productivity efficiency
</p>
<p>Leadership skills: Communication, Time Management, Team Building, Managing Workforce Diversity</p>
