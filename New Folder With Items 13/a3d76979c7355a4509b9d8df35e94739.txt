<h2>Technical Support Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Role / Job Summary</p>
<ul>
<li>Answer and resolve customer technical support 24/7 calls and emails</li>
<li>Provide customer technical support at intalled sites</li>
<li>Support Delta US New Product (NPI) First Article Review</li>
</ul>
<p>Main Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Answer the 24/7 Technical Support hotline during assigned schedule</li>
<li>Respond/Resolve customers’ phone/email technical issues including system/electrical/mechanical troubleshooting.</li>
<li>Provide technical support at customers’ locations.</li>
<li>Create product troubleshooting and repair MOP’s</li>
<li>Support DEUS Sales/Customer Service with product repair/training quotes</li>
<li>Develop and provide Delta US product training for installers</li>
<li>DEUS Test/Document/Track NPI First Article status</li>
</ul>
<p>Education</p>
<ul><li>Minimum Associates degree (or equivalent) in Electronics or related discipline</li></ul>
<p>Job Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>Three to five years’ experience of customer technical support of AC and/or DC power systems</li>
<li>Telcom power system technical support and/or product repair experience</li>
</ul>
<p>Technical Skills</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated customer technical support ability to identify and resolve issues</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency with computer software including MS Office (Excel, Outlook, Powerpoint, and Word), and ERP (SAP) systems.</li>
<li>Battery backed up power system troubleshooting skills.</li>
<li>Telcom DC power system troubleshooting skills.</li>
</ul>
<p>General Skills</p>
<ul>
<li>Professional verbal and written English communication skills</li>
<li>Multi-tasking and time management organizational skills</li>
<li>IT network troubleshooting skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Travel</p>
<p>US domestic travel on a limited basis, typically less than twenty (20) days per year.</p>
<p>Job Types: Temporary, Contract</p>
<p>Contract Renewal:</p>
<ul><li>Likely</li></ul>
<p>Employment Length:</p>
<ul><li>4 - 6 months</li></ul>
