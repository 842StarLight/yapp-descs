<p><b>When looking for the right place to take your career, what's important to you?</b></p>
<p>With nearly 60 in business, Bober Markey Fedorovich is one of the region's premier accounting and advisory firms with a long history of serving leading public and private organizations in Northeast Ohio and around the country, with reach throughout North America and the world. From large, publicly held companies to smaller, family owned businesses, our clients come in all shapes and sizes.</p>
<p>That means we're big enough to serve regional, national and international clients, but small enough to foster a family atmosphere. Headquartered in Akron, with offices also in Cleveland, BMF is the perfect solution if you're looking for something a little different from the Big Four!</p>
<p>We're committed to hiring the BEST and the BRIGHTEST - and that's why we're looking for a talented, experienced Audit Professional to join our extraordinary team, and help continue building BMF's respected and long-standing reputation.</p>
<p>We are seeking an outgoing, relationship-driven CPA to join our management team - with path to partner potential! - providing oversight and management of client engagements. The ideal candidate will possess a broad base of professional skills including (but not limited to): effectively establishing workload priorities, organizing and planning individual jobs, supervising the work of others, working independently and able to analyze complex matters. In addition, the ideal candidate will possess demonstrated success with identifying prospects, building and securing new client relationships, and cultivating a strong network of professional contacts and centers of influence.</p>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p>The Audit Senior Manager is responsible for leading a profitable functional area of the practice - leading the team, building the business, and ensuring the team delivers exceptional client service.</p>
<p>This role also supports the business development and growth initiatives of the practice and is responsible
<br>
for retaining talented people through strong leadership and coaching/ mentoring relationships.</p>
<p><i>Specific responsibilities include:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Reviews and confirms engagement objectives, scope and work plan with client.</li>
<li>Identifies engagement resource requirements and ensures the most appropriate resources are assigned to specific engagement roles.</li>
<li>Anticipates, identifies, and resolves complex engagement issues.</li>
<li>Seeks regular client assessment of engagement progress and overall feedback on performance/value add.</li>
<li>Develops engagement budgets for review by the engagement Partner, monitors realization, utilization and accounts receivable progress.</li>
<li>Works with the partner group to generate leads, contacts and sales including developing and closing engagement proposals.</li>
<li>Supports the Partner team in identifying and promoting the development of new delivery capabilities and/or channels to satisfy evolving market requirements.</li>
<li>Supports the Partner team in the development and planning of practice unit strategy, objectives, and budgets.</li>
<li>Development and demonstration of industry expertise and profiling of the firm.</li>
<li>Actively participates in staff development, including as a Manager Advisor and through completion of engagement and annual performance evaluation processes.</li>
<li>Builds on strong coaching skills to provide recognition, objective and constructive feedback, and motivation to all levels of firm personnel.</li>
<li>Builds team spirit, generates excitement, and demonstrates enthusiasm and commitment to team goals.</li>
<li>Identifies and develops new initiatives to increase retention, meet resourcing needs,</li>
<li>utilization and people development.</li>
<li>Anticipates and communicates budget issues, identifies opportunities to improve engagement profitability and prepares timely billings and conducts collections work.</li>
<li>Adheres to the Firm's Mission and Core Values.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications :</b></p>
<ul>
<li>CPA Required and a minimum of 8 years of recent public accounting experience.</li>
<li>Strong business acumen.</li>
<li>Strong technical knowledge and expertise.</li>
<li>Functional/industry specialization, ideally in Construction, Manufacturing and/or Non-Profits.</li>
<li>A well-developed local/regional industry profile.</li>
<li>Strong project management and leadership skills including the ability to successfully coach and mentor talented people.</li>
<li>A thorough understanding of market trends including opportunities, global and local business initiatives and stakeholder/competitor interests.</li>
<li>Firm management: able to manage to firm/department/niche area for short and long term profitability.</li>
</ul>
