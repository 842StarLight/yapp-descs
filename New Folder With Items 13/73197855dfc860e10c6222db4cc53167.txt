<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Business Development Representative, Google
</b></h1>
<p></p>
<p>You will develop strategic marketing/product line business plans and marketing programs to support sales in designated market segments. As a Google Business Development Representative, you will be responsible for implementing Go-to-market strategy for Google Software products, training and enabling partners, and ensuring the brand has proper channel exposure and remains top-of-mind.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Get Inspired by the Work You’ll Do
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and maintain customer relationships while identifying new business opportunities
</li>
<li>Identify volume and strategic customers and develop those partnerships
</li>
<li>Analyze market data to identify trends/opportunities and create reports and/or presentations
</li>
<li>Maintain and develop strong vendor relationships
</li>
<li>Act as go-to person for supporting Sales personnel
</li>
<li>Implement and enable strategic projects such as demos, pilots, incentives, and channel promotions
</li>
<li>Lead sales trainings in-person and via webinar
</li>
<li>Represent vendor at trade shows and end user events
</li>
<li>Be an internal go-to for sales knowledge on product line including pitch, use cases, pricing, and various upsell/cross sell solutions to complement an opportunity
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your passion is sparked by:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited college or university plus two to five years of related experience and/or equivalent education and experience
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of sales processes, procedures and terminology
</li>
<li>Solid background building productive internal and external working relationships
</li>
<li>Established detail-oriented individual with proven analytical skills
</li>
<li>Demonstrated networking skills as well as solid organizational and planning skills
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications a must along with strong ability to function well in team environment through daily collaboration
</li>
<li>Proven background working problems of moderate scope that may require strategic thinking/problem solving skills
</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience exercising judgment within defined procedures and practices to determine appropriate action needed
</li>
</ul>
