<h2>Distribution Senior Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At Northwestern Mutual, we are strong, innovative and growing. We invest in our people. We care and make a positive difference.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What's the role?</b></h3>
<div>
As a Distribution Senior Data Analyst, working across our regional Distribution Performance teams (Regional VPs, Principals, Sales Directors and consultants), you will bring your ability to tell a story with data to help these teams function efficiently and drive enterprise priorities. You will work across the department and across the enterprise to provide key insights into the performance of the distribution system, while gathering feedback used to improve the systems and processes of the department. You will take an active role in identifying areas for improvement within current systems, and will be looked to as a subject matter expert within the team and department.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Bring Your Best! What this role needs:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, MIS, Mathematics, Statistics, Business or related field.</div></li>
<li><div>
5+ years of progressively responsible experience in data analysis, reporting or application development.</div></li>
<li><div>
Expertise in development and analytical tools such as SAS, SQL, MS Excel, SPSS or other tool. Visualization tooling like PowerBI or Tableau.</div></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><div>
Proven excellence in problem solving, research, quantitative analysis and analytical working techniques.</div></li>
<li><div>
Supports key Distribution Performance department platforms that enable regional team members to (1) execute on prioritized enterprise initiatives; (2) provide the enterprise with the desired transparency, input and feedback; and (3) execute on performance management responsibilities.
</div></li>
<li><div>Strong aptitude and desire for learning new platforms, development, analytic and presentation tools.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated ability to gather information systematically; consider a broad range of issues or factors and use accurate logic in analysis.</div></li>
<li><div>
Initiative to independently design and develop own deliverables while still being a team player.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to be responsive to changing business needs, manage and prioritize concurrent assignments, and handle ambiguity.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability and desire to mentor other team members through projects independently from start to finish.</div></li>
<li><div>
Creative and innovative in problem solving and formulating solutions.</div></li>
</ul>
