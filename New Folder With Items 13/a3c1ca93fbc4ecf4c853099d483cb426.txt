<p><b>
Position Objective</b>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Konica Minolta currently has an exciting opportunity for a <b>Production Systems Specialist!</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions</b>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Professionally represent the company as responsive, reliable, and customer-oriented technically proficient, cost effective and timely service of the Konica Minolta High Volume Production Line.</li>
<li>
Install, service and troubleshoot all printing and scanning issues of all production systems.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain effective relationships with customers and fellow employees.</li>
<li>
Follow company policies and procedures while maintaining the minimum calls per day, response time and other core performance metric averages.</li>
<li>
Establishes an up-to-date file of service manuals, part books and other service literature using the issued laptop and develops familiarity with contents.</li>
<li>
Maintain tool kit, supplies, and accurate inventory for assigned territory.</li>
<li>
Seek opportunities to improve territory parts budget and inventory control.</li>
<li>
Assist other technicians as needed with more complex service calls.</li>
<li>
Provides sales support as required (i.e. advising sales on problems as accounts, service leads).</li>
<li>
Installs multiple protocol and/or multiple network operating systems in conjunction with software based solutions, i.e. Barr System, AHT, TR Systems, etc.</li>
<li>
Accurate and timely completion of invoices and expense reports.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Competencies (Knowledge, Skills and Abilities)</b>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated electro-mechanical, digital electronic, and PC expertise.</li>
<li>
Ability to use appropriate technology for the position (i.e. handheld computer, phone, laptop).</li>
<li>
Must have reliable transportation and a valid driver’s license.</li>
<li>
Position requires use of personal car which is eligible for vehicle maintenance compensation program.</li>
<li>
Must be able to travel locally on a daily basis to various customer sites.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>
Must have excellent daily time management skills and be capable of prioritizing activities and schedule to obtain effective results.</li>
<li>
Ability to be work independently with very little supervision.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience, Educational Reqts and Certifications</b>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of a High School diploma or GED required. Technical School training preferred.</li>
<li>
2-3 years of experience related to technical/repair of business equipment.</li>
<li>
A+ and Net+ certification or equivalent experience.</li>
</ul>
