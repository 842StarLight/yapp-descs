<h2>Global Director Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Summary:</b> The Global Director of Product Manager for Controls works under the direction of the EVP, Global Engineering. This position is responsible for understanding and communicating the Global Controls Technologies, Product Strategies, and Roadmaps while leading/assisting the team of Core Product Managers in creating innovative component strategies and roadmaps. This role will also be responsible for cross functionally Milestone Reviews and collaborate with other Directors of Product Managers to align Global business requirements and strategies.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b> 90% • Lead Global Product Management Team to develop product category strategy to drive revenue and profitability for multiple product lines.
<br><ul>
<li>Metrix performance of Team utilizing Key Performance Indicators</li>
<li>Collaborate with other Product Management Teams and Executive Management to prioritize and align product strategies and roadmaps.</li>
<li>Assist in gathering and maintaining industry and consumer category intelligence.</li>
<li>Endure data is synthesized into facts and trends that validate company direction on product development.</li>
<li>Support team as the product knowledge expert with technical specifications and features, market drivers and the competitive landscape.</li>
<li>Present quantifiable reports and presentations for internal and external stakeholders.</li>
<li>Collaborate formally and informally across business functions.</li>
<li>Lead teams to move projects through the stage gate development process.</li>
<li>Drive activities to support key launch events or product milestones.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with the internal and external resources to understand critical intellectual property, regulatory standards, and legislation related to the product category strategy.</li>
</ul>
10% • Other duties as assigned
<br><br>
Qualifications, Knowledge, Skills, Abilities and Physical demands: Minimal Qualifications: Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent experience; Master’s Degree Preferred
<br><ul>
<li>7 - 10 years of progressive work experience in marketing, sales, product management or engineering</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Successfully managed various Product Managers with multiple Product Lines</li>
<li>Demonstrated technical skills for executing new product development projects in a manufacturing and engineering environment</li>
<li>Technical background or experience in at least one of the following areas: Power Generation, Electronics, Automatic Transfer Switches, Engines, or Alternators</li>
</ul>
<b>Knowledge:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong leadership and team alignment</li>
<li>Ability to analyze and summarize competition and competitive landscape</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills with the ability to initiate and support marketing communications and marketing services projects including advertising campaigns, packaging and merchandising development, sales collateral development, publicity programs, and trade show management</li>
<li>Ability to comprehend and communicate technical information to a wide variety of audiences</li>
<li>Strong business acumen</li>
<li>Effective organizational and prioritizing skills</li>
<li>Ability to act pro-actively and identify innovative solutions</li>
<li>Highly motivated and detail-oriented</li>
</ul>
This position requires 30% travel
