<p>
A DVP of Planning is responsible for developing and communicating Omnichannel Planning strategies to their respective Planning Team with the goal of meeting/ exceeding sales, margin and inventory productivity targets. A DVP guides Planners through the development of seasonal merchandise financial plans, Ecommerce and in-season planning. A DVP works in partnership with DMM’s to maximize overall financial performance of product categories and to develop a collaborative team culture within their area of responsibility.</p>
</div>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Direct the development of divisional, departmental and bottom-up merchandise financial plans; work with Senior Management and team to ensure reconciliation of plans back to top-side targets</li>
<li>
Partner with GMM/DMM and Group Planning Manager to develop longer range strategic goals for their area</li>
<li>
Responsible for monitoring inventory aging for business area and taking urgent action in partnership with DMM to stay within corporate standards</li>
<li>
Oversee the allocation process and communicate allocation best practices'</li>
<li>
Oversee replenishment strategies and goals with directors and managers</li>
<li>
Provide support to Buying team during product planning processes (merchandise strategy, inventory planning, assortment &amp; buy planning) with analytics and reconciliations to merchandise financial plans and Channel Plans.</li>
<li>
Partner with the GMM/DMM in the divisional open-to-buy meetings; manage opportunities and risks through the open to buy/ monthly forecast process</li>
<li>
Provide timely recommendations to react to business shifts</li>
<li>
Focus on developing and maintaining bench strength</li>
<li>
Build a high-quality planning team by developing partnerships, leading, coaching and providing constructive feedback</li>
<li>
Facilitate team environment between Buying and Planning organizations</li>
<li>
Evaluate techniques, processes and systems with management to identify improvement areas</li>
<li>
Lead initiatives to instill best practices and develop support infrastructure</li>
<li>
Partner with GMM/DMM and Group Planning Manager to develop new store plans and inventory guidelines for their area</li>
</ul>
