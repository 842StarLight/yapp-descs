<h2>Warehouse Operations Specialist I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<div><div>Responsible for performing a wide range of administrative and manual tasks supporting warehouse operations, including but not limited to picking/staging, delivering/shipping, forecasting/ordering, and receiving/stocking raw materials and/or finished goods. Responsible for providing the materials and support required for new utility construction, emergency utility repairs, and maintenance of the City's infrastructure in a timely and customer-friendly manner.
</div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<ul>
<li>Processes remote and walk-in requests, manages inventory, orders materials, and tracks projects utilizing the Automated Warehouse Management System (WMS)
</li>
<li>Utilizes Automated Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems to order, track, ship, and receive materials; creates and files system-generated documentation (e.g., pick tickets, receivers, etc.) to record all system transactions.
</li>
<li>Operates sections of the ERP autonomously to monitor inventory levels, identify obsolescence, and maintain safety stock, minimum, and maximum levels ensuring material availability; forecasts material demand based on consumption and historical sales data.
</li>
<li>Delivers interoffice mail and remote orders daily. Prioritizes orders before picking and staging materials for customers; prepares materials for delivery and performs Pick-up and Delivery (P&amp;D) functions for numerous departments across the organization; quickly responds to customer needs and modifies P&amp;D schedule/route as necessary.
</li>
<li>Assists with the performance of physical inventory cycle counts and performs cycle counts routinely; creates physical inventory documents and updates ERP as required.
</li>
<li>Operates Material Handling Equipment (MHE) such as forklifts and pickers to assemble and stage orders, load and unload vendor receipts/returns, and transfer stock; stocks material by hand as needed.
</li>
<li>Ensures materials and supplies are properly received, inspected, stored, issued, and delivered in compliance with SOP's.
</li>
<li>Manages and tracks deliveries for other departments; ensures deliveries are logged in, tracked, and picked up.
</li>
<li>Safely performs duties in fast-paced warehouse environment; follows safety policies and procedures and wears personal protective equipment as required.
</li>
<li>Performs preventative maintenance on general purpose vehicles and MHE, which includes before, during, and after operation inspections.
</li>
<li>Helps maintain physical condition and cleanliness of warehouse, offices, and common areas; performs other duties as assigned.
</li>
<li>Embraces organizational goals and City's long-term vision and understands how the Warehouse Operations Specialist role supports the growth and success of warehouse operations; adheres to policies and SOP's with minimal supervision.
</li>
<li>Builds customer confidence by focusing on customer satisfaction, exceeding achievable customer expectations, helping solve customer problems, meeting customer commitments, and responding quickly to customer requests.
</li>
<li>Seeks internal and external opportunities for career development via formal training (i.e., college courses), professional development seminars, and on-the-job training.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Additional Duties:
</b><ul><li>Performs other duties as assigned</li></ul>
</div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div><p><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b>
</p></div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<ul><li>High school diploma or GED with at least one year of work experience performing functions of shipping, receiving, and distribution operations and inventory management practices or similar duties</li></ul>
OR<ul>
<li>Any combination of related education, experience, certifications and licenses that will result in a candidate successfully performing the essential functions of the job</li>
</ul>
<b>
Core Competencies:
</b><ul>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively both verbally and in writing
</li>
<li>Ability to form and maintain effective relationships with coworkers and customers
</li>
<li>Ability to maintain regular and punctual attendance
</li>
<li>Ability to apply work experiences with the overall functions of shipping, receiving, and distribution operations and inventory management practices</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<br>
<br>
