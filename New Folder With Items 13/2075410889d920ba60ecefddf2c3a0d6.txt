<h2>Assistant Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At Bozzuto, we’ve been creating inspiring, engaging and vibrant communities for three decades.Why? Because home is where aspirations are pursued and memories unfold. We believe in the power to impact the lives of the people we touch through the delivery of extraordinary experiences.</p>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Tour Your Responsibilities:</b></h1>
<p>You’re a role model, people person, jack-of-all-trades. You’re passionate about apartment living, connecting with the local community and creating experiences for our residents that bring both of these together. You’re a quick thinker and thrive in a fast-paced environment, yet your obsession for details positions you perfectly for this leadership role.</p>
<p>As an Assistant Property Manager (APM), your primary responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Leading by example, living the standards and behaviors consistent with the core values and culture of Bozzuto</li>
<li>
Achieving financial objectives through rent collection and by managing delinquency</li>
<li>
Sales and marketing to include conducting informative and personalized tours of the community which result in new leases</li>
<li>
Building brand loyalty by striving to ensure every resident interaction is positive</li>
<li>
Ensuring that the community’s interior and exterior are impeccable, and the team appropriately represents the Bozzuto brand with a crisp, professional appearance</li>
<li>
Coaching, mentoring and inspiring team members</li>
<li>
Working weekends when prospective residents are out looking for their new home—one to two weekends per month with two days off during the week</li>
<li>
Stepping in and taking responsibility for leading when the Property Manager is not present</li>
</ul>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You Bring to Us:</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>
A college degree</li>
<li>
2+ years of experience in the property management, hospitality or retail industries</li>
<li>
Exposure to revenue, P&amp;L and budget management</li>
<li>
A kick-butt Pinterest, Twitter, Facebook or Instagram account (aka social media savvy)</li>
<li>
Outstanding communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>
Proficiency with industry software (YARDI preferred)</li>
<li>
Sales aptitude</li>
<li>
A real estate license (or willingness to obtain within 90 days of hire) where applicable</li>
</ul>
