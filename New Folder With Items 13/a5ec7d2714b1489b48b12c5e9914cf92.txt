<h2>Home Design Team Assistant REQ#5023</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>In the spirit of “making dreams come true” a Home Design Team Assistant provides organizational and administrative support to the Street of Dreams Home Design Team; helping create an unforgettable customer experience for those who have chosen to build their dream home in The Villages. This role works hand in hand with home designers, representatives from Properties of The Villages and their customers. They assist our Design Consultants with many aspects of the build process which may include pricing, drawings, filing documentation, facilitating Street of Dream tours, scheduling &amp; preparing for future home order appointments, fielding &amp; tracking telephone calls and emails, receiving and directing questions, maintaining product materials, along with other related functions that support Design Team proficiency, production and morale. Extensive software and communication skills are required. Hospitality is a must – being an enthusiastic, loyal ‘supporter’ of team initiatives. Requires strong interpersonal skills, project management and coordination, flexibility, attention to detail &amp; accuracy, sensitivity to confidential matters and the ability to produce ultimate team success while creating a customer experience second to none! This position is an integral part of the Home Design Team.
</p>
<p>Educational / Experience Requirements:
<br>
Minimum Requirements: Associate's degree (A. A.) or equivalent from a two-year college or technical school preferred; or two years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. Must have the ability to work a flexible schedule including weekends. Must be proficient in the Microsoft family of programs (excel, outlook, etc ) and be able to lift up to 20 lbs. to perform various duties and tasks.</p>
