Our technology helps our customers fulfill their mission and improve the efficiency and quality of their operations. Think about the roads and bridges you travel on, the building where you work and the home where you live, the airport runway, the parks where you relax, and the courts where you play basketball – all made from concrete, rock and asphalt. What we do best is combining next-gen technologies with over 40 years of industry expertise. We are not a “here today, gone tomorrow” software company. We have been here a long time, and like the foundation of a building that our products help build, will be for many years to come. Come build your career on that same solid foundation.</div>

<div><b>
The Role</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Primary responsibility of the Product Owner is to act as the liaison between strategy and execution. The Product Owner translates the product vision &amp; strategy established the Product Manager into actionable product releases by the product group(s).</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Product Owner also represents the needs and desires of the industry and our customers as defined by the Product Manager in the Product Roadmap through conception, definition, construction, and value delivery.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Additionally, the Product Owner serves as the “go-to” functional decision authority for agile Scrum Master and team related to product questions or design direction. The Role serves as the first source of information about product requirements for the rest of the product development team members.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Do</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Be the “front line” to ensure a quality A+ product experience for our customers.</li>
<li>
Be the “Go-to” functional decision authority (Design authority) for Scrum Master &amp; Scrum/Development Team; Customer advocate (Voice-Of-Customer)</li>
<li>
Create Requirements traceability &amp; definition (e.g. Epics, User Stories, Storyboard, Use Cases, and or Functional Requirements)</li>
<li>
Manage the Product Backlog, including development schedules, and release management/backlog</li>
<li>
Be the Product go-to-person for product backlog and defects required from services, implementations, and Investment Projects; own Sprint Planning</li>
<li>
Define success, customer satisfaction, and quality metrics for the product and then exceed them</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>

<div>
Command Alkon develops, markets, sales, implements and supports software and hardware products for heavy building contractors, haulers, and producers of aggregate, ready-mixed concrete, asphalt, cement and block.
