<h2>Solutions Engineer Manager - Federal/SLED</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Washington, DC
<br><br>
Yubico's mission is to create a safer internet for everyone. Our core invention, the YubiKey, secures logins for 9 of the top 10 internet brands, including Google and Facebook, and for millions of users in 160 countries. Collaboration and innovation are at the core of our culture, as we expand to more advanced software and services for encryption and Internet of Things (IoT). We are a fast-growing, profitable, and multinational company, offering an opportunity to bring your ideas to life with our global team.
<br><br>
Yubico is seeking a Solutions Engineer Manager who will be responsible for a team of Solutions Engineers in the region, acting as a technical subject matter expert, and responsible for positioning and selling Yubico's product in the field. An experienced Solutions Engineer Manager will have a consultative approach, extensive experience with enterprise architecture, and the ability to deliver successful proof of concepts. Successful candidates must be good communicators to a variety of business types and audiences. Candidates should have 5+ years of experience in roles that require direct technical customer interaction, such as Solutions/Sales Engineering, Sales Engineering, or Pre-sales background as well as 2+ years managing similar teams.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and develop a cohesive SE team, supporting hiring, onboarding, team competency, and career development in the region/vertical</li>
<li>Work with Solutions Engineering managers to define business and technology goals for the organization</li>
<li>Assist team through guidance and prioritization of work activities to achieve business and technology goals, translating between them to achieve optimal outcomes</li>
<li>Support team and assist in finding solutions to internal/external issues and challenges</li>
<li>Coordinate with Solutions Engineering managers globally to drive collaboration and consistency with processes and tools, while ensuring local goals are achieved</li>
<li>Establish self as resident subject matter expert on Yubico's products and ecosystem</li>
<li>Strategize with sales reps and sales managers to help achieve target sales quotas</li>
<li>Build strong relationships with prospects and establish technical credibility to help advance deals</li>
<li>Maintain relationships with existing customers and look for opportunities to expand their product usage and improve their security posture where relevant</li>
<li>Deliver successful proof-of-concepts, deployment planning, and technical consulting engagements</li>
<li>Actively communicate with engineering and marketing with respect to evolving customer requirements in the marketplace</li>
<li>Continually assist with solutions development to expand to new target markets and industries</li>
<li>Improve the customer's experience through conferences, workshops and written documentation</li>
<li>Care about contributing to an amazing work culture and environment</li>
<li>Support and be comfortable with the rapid, unpredictable nature of a young innovative company</li>
<li>Work with customers remotely and in person. Travel is expected to be 20-40%</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2 or more years managing Solutions Engineering or Pre-sales teams and 5+ years as a contributor in a similar role</li>
<li>5 or more years experience working in Federal and/or SLED</li>
<li>10 or more years experience in IT</li>
<li>Able to configure and install enterprise technologies including network infrastructure, virtualization, web applications, Windows, Mac, and Linux</li>
<li>Knowledge of commonly used security technologies and protocols including two-factor authentication, Single Sign-On, VPN, PKI, various authentication protocols (OATH, SAML, OpenID, ADFS, FIDO)</li>
<li>Experience with IAM a plus</li>
<li>Understanding of the Federal Government Budget and Procurement processes</li>
<li>Knowledge of Federal Government standards, requirements, and guidance such as:</li>
<li>NIST SP 800-63-3</li>
<li>FIPS 201-2</li>
<li>FIPS 140-2/3</li>
<li>FICAM Roadmap and Implementation Guidance</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral business communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent project management skills</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate across geographically dispersed functions</li>
</ul>
<b>Terms:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Start Date: As soon as possible</li>
<li>Location: Washington, DC</li>
<li>Salary and Benefits: Competitive Salary + Bonus + Benefits + Stock Options</li>
</ul>
