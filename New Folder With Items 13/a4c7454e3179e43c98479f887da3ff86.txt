<p><b><i>
The Big Task</i></b></p>
<p>
You will create the department from scratch and lead a staff of Customer Services Reps and Dispatchers who book and schedule service calls and do whatever is necessary to achieve department booked call and sales goals.</p>
<p><b><i>
Key Sub Tasks</i></b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead and train staff on the best ways to convert calls into booked service appointments.</li>
<li>Manage dispatch to ensure the right technicians are matched to every job.</li>
<li>Author and refine call scripts so they're clear, compelling and result in more appointments.</li>
<li>Become an expert of your market, know the customer needs and how best to engage them.</li>
<li>Monitor performance of staff, review recorded calls and advise them on how to promote sales.</li>
<li>Formalize a coaching and training platform that's cost effective and measurable.</li>
<li>Show employees how their individual contributions matter to the company's success.</li>
<li>Ensure sufficient staffing to match the business demand and seasonal spikes.</li>
</ul>
