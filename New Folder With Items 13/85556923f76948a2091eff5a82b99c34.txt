<div>
<div>
At IL Farm Bureau, the <b>Part-Time</b> <b>Advertising Sales and Marketing Representative</b> is primarily responsible for answering in-bound telephone calls and conducting some out-bound sales calls for the FarmWeek and FarmWeekNow.com classified advertising call center. Coordinates customer advertisements, billing, and payment process.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Receives and conducts calls for the placement of classified advertisements within FarmWeek and FarmWeekNow.com.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assists customers with wording, payments and publishing of the classified ads.</div></li>
<li><div>
Identifies upsell and cross-sell opportunities to increase classified advertising revenue.</div></li>
<li><div>
Determines appropriate categories for advertising and duration of ads.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assists sales team with processing of other advertisements including digital display on FarmWeekNow.com.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provides input and assistance with lead generation and marketing campaigns.</div></li>
<li><div>
Serves as backup for other in-coming advertising department telephone calls.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assists Advertising Sales Manager with miscellaneous clerical functions as needed.</div></li>
<li><div>
Tracks analytics and reports other quantitative information for FarmWeekNow.com to help measure growth in usage.</div></li>
<li><div>
Listens to podcasts of “RFD Illinois” and develop written summaries of all aspects of each show. This is done in conjunction with taking classified ads and has resulted in a special equipment headset.</div></li>
<li><div>
Performs other related work as required or requested.</div></li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
