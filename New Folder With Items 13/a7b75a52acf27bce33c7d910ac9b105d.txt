<h2>Field Account Manager - Healthcare</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>This individual will be responsible for targeted account activity within an assigned territory. Overall responsibilities include; meeting quota and revenue targets, prospecting and developing accounts and building relationships with our strategic partners.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Essential Functions:
</div>
<ul>
<li>Grow current revenue and Services while expanding the customer base.
</li>
<li>Build relationships with CDW’s strategic partners.
</li>
<li>Develop new manufacture relationships.
</li>
<li>Work closely with internal departments to ensure sales, marketing and contract programs are consistently and effectively implemented
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a high level of product knowledge on CDW’s Service offerings as well as competitors’ service offerings
</li>
<li>Present CDW’s services capabilities at seminars, tabletop shows and to user groups.
</li>
<li>Develop and execute marketing and business plans to drive revenue and profit.
</li>
<li>Report on a weekly basis, all activities to include but not be limited to; meetings, contacts, calendar, travel, updates and forecasts to the sales management.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b>
</p>
<div>Minimum Qualifications:
</div>
<ul>
<li>3 years experience in technology sales
</li>
<li>Prior experience selling technology hardware or software products
</li>
<li>Prior experience selling complex technical solutions
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Engineering, Computer Science,
</li>
<li>Management Information Systems, Business Administration, or the equivalent in experience.
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Other Required Qualifications
</div>
<ul>
<li>Excellent Communication skills
</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Preferred Qualifications
</div>
<ul>
<li>2 years experience in field customer facing sales
</li>
<li>5 years experience in hardware or software technology sales
</li>
<li>2 years experience selling complex technical solutions
</li>
<li>Cisco, EMC, Microsoft, HP and IBM Sales Certifications
</li>
<li>Prior experience selling advance technology solutions for Cisco, IBM, Microsoft, HP or EMC
</li>
<li>Experience selling to segment customers</li>
</ul>
