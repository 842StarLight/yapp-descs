Location: Pittsburgh, PA (and various client sites nationwide)</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Purpose and Objective: SAP America, Inc. seeks a Business Processes Senior Consultant at our Pittsburgh, PA office location (and various client sites nationwide) for application and process specific solution consulting (for a specific application and/or process area: creation of business blueprint, design, configure and implementation based on a blue print, creation and execution of test cases, test scheduling, and coordinate with the business/client during Integration &amp; user acceptance phases, key user training, go live support, and hyper care).</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Expectations and Tasks: Provide expert support in escalated projects. Perform feasibility studies and solution reviews. Support pre-sales activities. Take over responsibility from time to time as a sub project team leader, as a team lead, and supporting respective line manager(s) in people development tasks. Perform detailed planning of process implementation. Define detailed functional specifications for development requirements. Perform industry and corporate process implementation across all related applications (SAP/non-SAP, On-Premise and cloud-based). Develop and assure the quality of process models. Demonstrate profound knowledge of modeling standards and tools. Drive deployment of SAP Model Companies for specific area of business and project implementation based on SAP Activate methodology. Support customers digital transformation journey to S4HANA. 20% travel required to various client sites nationwide.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Education and Qualifications/Skills and Competencies: Position requires a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Management Information Systems, Business Administration, Accounting, or a related field and 6 years of experience, or alternatively, a Master's degree and 4 years of experience.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Work Experience:</p>
<p>
Experience must include 4 years of experience involving the following: design, configuration and implementation of SAP Financial Accounting (FI) and Controlling (CO); translating customer business requirements into viable technology systems; and integrating SAP FICO with other SAP solutions and modules including SAP ERP Materials Management (MM), Sales and Distribution (SD), Production Planning(PP), Human Capital Management(HCM), Transportation Management(TM), Plant Maintenance(PM), Investment Management (IM) and Project Systems (PS). Experience must involve Oil and Gas, and Chemical Industry business processes; technology implementation for Oil and Gas industry customers; customizing implementations to fulfill production and revenue accounting requirements; and certification in SAP FI and SAP CO. 20% travel required to various client sites nationwide.</p>
