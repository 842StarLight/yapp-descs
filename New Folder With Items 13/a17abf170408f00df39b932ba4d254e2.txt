<h2>Senior Product Manager for Developer Platform, Intuit Developer Group</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview</b></h2>
<div>
The Intuit Developer Group (IDG) vision is to be the global, trusted platform of choice for all developers serving small businesses and accountants, and to help our customers maximize the value of their QuickBooks Online subscriptions through additional functionality delivered by tightly integrated third-party applications and services.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As a Senior Product Manager for Developer Platform at Intuit Developer Group, you will be responsible for helping our partners develop embedded UI experiences in QuickBooks Online. You will have significant strategic impact and influence in delivering this new capability to Intuit Developer Platform, while partnering closely with engineering leaders to bring delightful experiences to Developers and Partners.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Manage all aspects of providing self-service experience for Developers and Partners building UI experiences in QuickBooks Online</li>
<li>
Be the voice and view of the customer from solution concept to market, planning, setting and managing to timelines</li>
<li>
Partner with Design and Product Development teams to develop and ship game changing features in line with your roadmap</li>
<li>
Be scrappy and entrepreneurial; figure out how to get the most business value for the least cost</li>
<li>
Research, understand and draw insights on customer needs and pain points through a strong toolkit around observing customers in a lab, in the wild, and via A/B testing</li>
<li>
Think end-to-end by connecting all aspects of Developer journey (learning concepts, exploring UI widgets, test environment, application development lifecycle and publishing an App) to ensure both tight coordination across all stakeholders and that the offering delivers world-class customer delight</li>
<li>
Deliver the key critical customer benefits and experiences while relentlessly ensuring speed, quality and operational efficiency</li>
<li>
Drive adoption of the platform and growth utilizing NPS, LTV etc. as key performance indicators while being technical enough to specify and work with engineering to build the product</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Leadership and subject matter expertise in Product Management, ideally with 7+ years building world-class SaaS-based platforms, App extension frameworks and API-based software platforms/products</li>
<li>
Expertise in developer platforms and portals required; financial technology experience a plus</li>
<li>
Excellent understanding of popular UI frameworks like ReactJS, Angular and web technologies like HTML5, JavaScript, CSS3, etc.</li>
<li>
Experience building low code/no code &amp; high productivity tools targeted for business analysts and IT administrators</li>
<li>
Proven track record of designing and building out systems in an experiment-driven, data-informed environment that enables the co-creation of value by ultimately serving end customers</li>
<li>
Exceptional project management skills; ability to plan, set and manage to reasonable timelines while delivering projects that align to the business strategy</li>
<li>
Track record of showcasing large-scale product delivery, leadership and passion that inspires greatness</li>
<li>
Can partner across organizational boundaries to deliver business results in a product ecosystem environment while communicating, collaborating, influencing and negotiating effectively though boundaryless leadership, knowing when and how to work with product engineering, sales, marketing, and others</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical and quantitative abilities; proficiency in product analytics, testing methodologies and data-backed decision processes; ability to ideate and innovate; can evangelize product vision utilizing customer research and data analysis</li>
</ul>
