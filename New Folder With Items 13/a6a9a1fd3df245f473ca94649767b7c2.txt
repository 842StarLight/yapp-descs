<h2>Fitness Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Anytime Fitness is seeking an experienced Fitness Director to oversee sales and operations in our 9 locations throughout the South Bay and Central Coast! Anytime Fitness is a 24-hour fitness center that offers personal training, small group training, and large group training for people of all different fitness levels.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal Fitness Director will be responsible for generating $500K+ in monthly training revenues (over 9 locations) and managing the entire training department including sales, staffing, member results and retention. The Fitness Director’s ultimate responsibility is to coach the trainers to success, through programing, sales, and service.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>REQUIREMENTS</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>5+ years of sales experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>1+ years of personal training experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Efficiently train Managers, Staff and Personal Trainers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to personally generate Personal Training sales.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Willingness to travel and oversee all locations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Capability to work independently and collaborate with Upper Management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication and strong interpersonal skills in person, on the telephone and via email.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to recognize areas of improvement and make changes using good judgment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>An affinity and passion for fitness.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Solid writing and grammar skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highly organized, proficient in data management, ability to prioritize and meet deadlines.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Professional, punctual, reliable and neat.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Trustworthy and able to handle confidential information.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work harmoniously with co-workers, clients and the general public.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency with computers MS Office, Google, and CRM software.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>DUTIES</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Serve as a ‘guidance counselor’ to all members of the club, ‘educator and mentor’ for all training staff, and ‘strategist’ for the Club Manager.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead generation including Social Media Marketing, Grass Roots Marketing and B2B Networking.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervise and Manage Personal Trainers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage Personal Trainer schedules.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency in ClubReady/Club OS, to include revenue reports, attendance reports, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Independently make decisions related to high-level customer service.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain cleanliness and organization of the fitness club.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enforce Anytime Fitness policies and procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strategically manage marketing campaigns to generate leads for the fitness club.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Must be available to work mornings, evenings, and occasional weekends. As a Member of the Anytime Fitness Family you will receive: Competitive salary, Benefits (Medical, Dental, Vision, Accident and life Insurance), Industry leading Bonus Opportunities and a Complimentary Membership.</p>
