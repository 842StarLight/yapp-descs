<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the team</b></h2>
We are responsible for processing and serving all Trulia’s For-Sale and Rental listings and much more. We drive the backend search experience for Trulia and provide fast access to listings as well as local data around it. Our goal is to provide new and updated listings to our users in near real-time and make the search experience amazing. We are a small team that believes in innovation, collaboration and transparency. Trulia’s culture is one of the company’s biggest strengths. At Trulia people treat each other with respect and compassion, together cultivating a culture of collaboration, learning, accountability and fun. There’s a dedication to understanding the whole person, helping to build a collegian, trusting environment.<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About the role</b></h2>
<p>
We are seeking passionate and creative machine learning engineer to lead development of our core machine learning platform for search team. You will be working on tacking challenging problems in information retrieval/ NLP domain and will be continuously innovating on areas such as NLP, Query understanding, Semantic Understanding, Intent classification, Search relevancy, Personalization, Predictive search, defining metrics and tracking, scalability to name a few.</p>
<div>
<br>
<b>
This includes a chance to:</b><br>
<ul><li>Create high-visibility, modern semantic search products and drive innovation</li></ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Be a technical leader on Search relevancy algorithms, data mining, deep learning, recommendations and NLP,NLU</li>
<li>Understands the technical aspects of our products, connect technical work to business strategy, and have the skills to implement, communicate, and influence others and Collaborate cross-functionally with other teams across the organization</li>
<li>Be a technical leader and lead a team of software engineers, applied researchers and data scientist, Define technical standard and engineering cultures, conduct design reviews</li>
<li>Chance to contribute to building semantic search capabilities ground-up by developing entity disambiguation models, knowledge graphs, NLU, recommenders and relevance models .</li>
<li>Is responsible for modeling and deploying at scale ML solutions, discovering insights, and identifying and effectively communicating opportunities based on technical expertise and business strategy.</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who you are</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Has a minimum of seven years of hands-on experience developing large scale Machine Learning (Deep learning, Reinforcement learning etc) applications.</li>
<li>
Has ability to write high performance production quality code. Proficiency in Python, Scala and other equivalent languages is a plus.</li>
<li>
