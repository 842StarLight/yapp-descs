<p><b>Job Decription</b></p>
<div><div>
<p>Bastian Software Solutions is expanding our software operations team in Kansas City, MO. We are seeking eager individuals with strong technical aptitude who want to grow in the software field. This is a full time, entry-level project management role that we call a Software Implementation Specialist. You will work directly with on our customer sites throughout the installation and commissioning of our custom software packages.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate enjoys trouble-shooting software and hardware issues, customer interaction and traveling. Software Implementation Specialist must possess strong organizational skills and the ability to work well under pressure. We understand that this is an entry level position so we will train new employees on some of the technical side.</p>
<p><b>
General Job Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Diagnose and optimize the software database structures for customer implementations.</li>
<li>Installing, starting-up and troubleshooting automation software and equipment including: Installing servers, setting up work stations, some networking</li>
<li>Onsite project coordination with Project Managers, Sales Consultants and Customers</li>
<li>Perform network diagnostics, optimization and to lead the implementation of optimization efforts with particular focus on wireless network operations.</li>
<li>Query databases for troubleshooting testing scenarios</li>
<li>Provide input into installation subcontractor selection. Occasionally for smaller projects the Site Supervisor may hire local personnel and perform the installation "in-house"</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Degree/Education Required Preferred degrees: CIS, MIS, IT or other technical related major.</li>
<li>Willing to travel 70-80% overnight within the US. Travel cost paid for by the company.</li>
<li>Excellent troubleshooting abilities and analytical skills on both software and hardware.</li>
<li>Ability to learn technical systems including networks and databases quickly. SQL knowledge is a plus.</li>
<li>Strong demonstrated sense of customer service.</li>
<li>Experience with enterprise software systems, IT or process automation a plus.</li>
<li>Must be eligible to work in the USA long term without sponsorship.</li>
</ul>
