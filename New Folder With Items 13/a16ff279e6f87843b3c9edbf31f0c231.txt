<h2>Logistics Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Title: Logistics Specialist</p>
<p>Reports To: Director of Operations</p>
<p><b>Summary of Position </b></p>
<p>Works closely with manufacturing, service, and purchasing to create a timely, cost-effective international supply chain for delivery of materials to customer locations.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for handling the technical details of international transportation—multiple modes, complex documentation, and varying customs regulations, executing distribution strategies, and building relationships with logistics intermediaries.</li>
<li>Sourcing of transportation and establishing carrier volume allocations.</li>
<li>Serves as expert in dealing with problems inherent in moving freight internationally.</li>
<li>Manages the performance of international carriers and logistics intermediaries.</li>
<li>Ensures compliance with international and U.S. laws related to import/export activity.</li>
<li>Performs planning and replenishment process for production and service materials.</li>
<li>Evaluates trade-offs between transportation costs, inventory costs, and service levels.</li>
<li>Executes logistics strategies and processes for entering new markets on a global basis.</li>
<li>Support management cost savings initiatives to reduce overall freight cost.</li>
<li>Assists with departmental projects and performs other tasks as assigned. Assist in Inventory Control.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Qualifications Required for Position </b></p>
<ul><li>BS Degree in Business Management with concentration in Logistics and Transportation or Supply</li></ul>
<p>Chain Management Desired.</p>
<ul>
<li>4–6 Years of Experience in International Logistics, Import / Export.</li>
<li>Previous experience utilizing Oracle, SAP, or other MRP software.</li>
<li>Must have knowledge of international commerce—international banking, sales and payment terms, customs regulations, and international supply chain processes.</li>
<li>Must have proven ability to handle multiple high-level and detailed tasks.</li>
<li>Must be able to show understanding of logistics and transportation optimization processes for managing efficient operations.</li>
<li>Knowledge of project modeling, supply chain processes, and business forecasting methodologies.</li>
<li>Exceptional interpersonal and communication skills, specifically in coordination with transportation intermediaries and managing personnel.</li>
<li>Strong computer skills including MS Excel, MS Access, and MS Visio.</li>
<li>Ability to perform complex spreadsheet analysis for planning.</li>
</ul>
