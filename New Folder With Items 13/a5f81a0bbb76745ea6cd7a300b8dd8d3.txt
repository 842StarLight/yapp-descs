<h2>Packaging and Shipping Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Brady Corporation Overview</b></p>
<p>
Brady Corporation is an international manufacturer and marketer of complete solutions that identify and protect people, products and places. Brady's products help customers increase safety, security, productivity and performance and include high-performance labels, signs, safety devices, printing systems and software. Founded in 1914, the Company has a diverse customer base in electronics, telecommunications, manufacturing, electrical, construction, medical, aerospace and a variety of other industries. Brady is headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin and as of July 31, 2018, employed approximately 6,200 people in its worldwide businesses. Brady's fiscal 2018 sales were approximately $1.17 billion. Brady stock trades on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol BRC. More information is available at www.bradycorp.com.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The packaging and shipping clerk processes varying types of shippable materials per customer demands; interacts with carriers, customer service, and manufacturing; maintains a high level of inventory accuracy and integrity around the shipping dock, adjacent product warehouses, and manufacturing operations.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manually picks, packs, &amp; prepares product from stock warehouses for shipment as specified in the customer order &amp; in accordance with company policies and procedures</li>
<li>Use automated conveyor for picking material, pack material per automated instructions, interact with equipment to keep workflow moving to avoid production slowdowns upstream</li>
<li>Use forklifts &amp; manual or electric pallet jacks to perform material handling duties within the facility &amp; assist loading/unloading of trucks</li>
<li>Responsible for the complete &amp; accurate shipping of all products, maintenance of finished goods warehouse</li>
<li>Support area goals for continuous improvement, by serving on ad hoc committees &amp;/or teams as required</li>
<li>Assemble &amp; label boxes</li>
<li>Palletize &amp; wrap product according to specification</li>
<li>Adhere to Brady's Attendance, Safety &amp; other Company policies</li>
<li>Restock consumable items at end of every shift &amp; as needed</li>
<li>Maintains general housekeeping, clean production areas at end of every shift, &amp; as needed</li>
<li>Provide basic support within production areas as needed &amp; other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>1 year forklift experience in a manufacturing environment</li>
<li>Maintain strong attendance</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team environment</li>
<li>Ability to train others</li>
<li>Ability to read &amp; follow verbal &amp; written instructions</li>
<li>Flexible in schedule to work overtime, nights &amp; weekends as needed</li>
<li>Ability to lift 50 lbs repeatedly &amp; stand for 100% of shift</li>
<li>Ability to bend, twist, crawl, climb &amp; reach to perform job duties</li>
<li>Must obtain forklift certification upon hire.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of manufacturing experience</li>
<li>Experience using SAP</li>
<li>Associate's degree</li>
</ul>
