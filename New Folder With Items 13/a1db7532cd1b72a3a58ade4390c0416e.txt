PRIMARY PURPOSE OF POSITION
<br>
Software Asset Coordinator supports the day-to-day activities required by the process in order to maintain license counts and asset management database under the guidance of a Lead Analyst or Manager. Works under general supervision.<br>
<br>
PRIMARY DUTIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES<br>
<ul><li>Perform, document and assist in planning work activities relating to projects, sub-projects, or processes. Assist Asset Manager in updating asset records based on approved changes. Designing and producing Asset Management reports based on specifications defined in the Asset Management process. Review requests for licensing; identify harvesting opportunities. Support audit practices with purchase and entitlement data collection. 65%</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>

<li>Maintain engagement with business and IT partners. 10%</li>
