<h2>Part-Time Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Customer Service Representative (CSR) works in a dynamic Customer Contact Center which supports the ecommerce team for Eastern Mountain Sports &amp; Bob's Stores. Whether on the phone, email or via live chat, it is imperative the CSR communicates professionally and effectively while addressing a variety of customer inquiries. In many instances, the CSR is the last point of contact for our customers, so ensuring a genuine, positive and memorable experience is very important.<br>
<br>
A variety of schedules are available to cover day, evening and weekend shifts, including holidays, between the hours of 8:00am - 8:00 pm, seven days a week.</p>
