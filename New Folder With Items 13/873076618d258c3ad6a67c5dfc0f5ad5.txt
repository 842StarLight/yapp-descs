<p>As the Marketing Automation Specialist, you will coordinate and execute on a variety of marketing production tasks to ensure successful and timely campaign management and coordination with outside sales teams. <p>Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Day-to-day tasks include set-up, testing, fulfillment, tracking, and production of marketing automation tools as they related to the automated attendants and their associated campaigns in multi-channel marketing activities;
</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with the Marketing Specialist and Manager, Revenue Marketing to review and optimize campaigns (template designs, forms, copy and graphics) to ensure quality and performance;
</li>
<li>Ensures all marketing programs are approved and tested for mobile optimization and readability across all platforms by testing and deploying through Klaviyo and ensuring proper delivery of the automated attendant's messaging;
</li>
<li>Ensuring delivery of all assigned projects in-market, including social media and email distributions as they relate to the automated attendants and their associated activities;
</li>
<li>Maintaining reports on the digital marketing KPIs that are being achieved by the automated attendants and providing actionable insights on effectiveness for stakeholders to review, such as customer profiling, segmentation studies, and retrospective measurements.
</li>
</ul>
