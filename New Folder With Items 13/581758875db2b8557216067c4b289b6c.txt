<h2>Internet of Things (IoT) Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<p>Keyfactor is expanding and looking to add an enthusiastic, professional, and goal-oriented Internet of Things (IoT) Sales Development Representative (SDR) to our team. IoT SDR’s with Keyfactor work in a high level consultative role, developing the initial interest in Keyfactor’s <i>Internet of Things (IoT) Identity Platform – Keyfactor Control –</i> with IoT specific clients. The right candidate will be highly proficient in establishing relationships with key decision makers such as Product Managers up to and including the “C” level, and have a proven ability to qualify and penetrate mid-market to enterprise commercial corporate accounts.</p>
<div><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Connect with potential Keyfactor IoT clients using the telephone, email, social media, live chat and tradeshows events.</li>
<li>Establish and schedule meetings with prospective IoT clients for our IoT Director of Sales and VP of Business Development.</li>
<li>Nurture early-stage opportunities currently in pipeline after qualification.</li>
<li>Develop new accounts while maintaining and sustaining successful long-term relationships with key decision makers up to and including the “C” level.</li>
<li>Meet and exceed qualified appointment goals for assigned territories and targeted accounts.</li>
<li>Develop product, market and competitor knowledge.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p>DESIRED SKILLS &amp; EXPERIENCE</p>
</div>
<p>Minimum of 2 years’ experience developing IT sales opportunities within mid-market and enterprise clients. Past IT sales experience should include any of the following: storage, backup/replication, virtualization, networking, security, cloud services or data center software. Candidates currently in an inside or outside technology sales role, employed with an Independent Software Vendor, Value Added Reseller or Systems Integrator are preferred.</p>
<div><b>
LOCATION</b></div>
<p>You must reside within daily commuting distance of our Independence, OH office.</p>
<div><b>
COMPENSATION</b></div>
<p>Salary will be commensurate with experience.</p>
<p>
Keyfactor is a proud equal opportunity employer.</p>
