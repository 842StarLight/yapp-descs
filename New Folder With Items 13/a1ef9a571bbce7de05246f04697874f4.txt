<h2>Business Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Inbound lead qualification and new business development in the independent pediatric practice space. Responsible for growing the sales pipeline and scheduling demos, while trying to improve overall conversion rates.</p>
<ul>
<li>Immediate response calls for all inbound lead inquiries</li>
<li>Introduce our sales team process and OP’s compelling pediatric story</li>
<li>Coordinate outbound strategy with RSM’s</li>
<li>Set up discovery appointments and calls for RSM’s</li>
<li>Schedule onsite and remote demos for qualified prospects
</li>
<li>Coordinate meetings between sales, operations, and or other vendor staff</li>
<li>Collect follow-up feedback for demos and events</li>
<li>Assist in conducting Practice Workflow Assessment</li>
<li>Move prospects through the sales cycle in agreed upon time frames</li>
<li>Weekly meetings to review pipeline strategic plan with RSM’s</li>
<li>Create ideas and plans for email and direct mail campaigns</li>
<li>Execute email and phone call campaigns</li>
<li>Help generate educational topics for webinars, email campaigns, and local events</li>
<li>Trade Show follow up, list management and de-duplication
</li>
<li>Locate reference clients and site-visit hosts, and conduct arrangements</li>
<li>Maintain OP Client Referral Rewards program</li>
<li>Gather and communicate competitive intelligence to sales and marketing leadership</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors' Degree from an accredited college or university, with a major in business, marketing, health care or related field preferred</li>
<li>1-2 years of sales experience with prospecting and phone work a plus and strongly desired.</li>
<li>Experience in medical software (EMR/PM) is a plus</li>
<li>Experience in a medical practice is a plus. Knowledge of medical practice operations, revenue models, and best practices needed</li>
<li>Must have excellent verbal &amp; written communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Effective at cold and warm calling to support sales campaigns</li>
<li>Experienced using Salesforce, GSuite, and other cloud based tools</li>
<li>Demonstrated team contributor</li>
<li>Based in PA, NJ or DE preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
