<h2>Purchasing Agent 1</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div><b>THE POSITION</b></div>
<div><p>The Infrastructure &amp; Economic Development IT Delivery Center is looking to hire a Purchasing Agent, responsible for the procurement of supplies, materials, IT hardware and software, and contractual services.<br>
<br>
<b>Work Hours</b>: Monday through Friday from 8:00 am to 4:30 pm with hour lunch.</p></div>
<div><b>DESCRIPTION OF WORK</b></div>
<div><p>This position is responsible for the daily management of all activities related to purchasing, which includes controlling and monitoring bureau procurement.<br>
<br>
Purchasing activities include but are not limited to: the acquisition of mainframe and LAN hardware and peripherals; microcomputer, mainframe and LAN software; Furniture; miscellaneous equipment; office and consumable supplies; hotels; catering; equipment maintenance; facility rentals; and miscellaneous services.<br>
<br>
Evaluates purchase requirements with the bureau directors, program managers, and IT staff, providing technical advice on commodities or services and on procurement matters, as well as advice on the suitability, individual options, needs, and cost control effectiveness. Suggests possible substitutions, alternate courses of action and procurement strategies, subsequently ensuring and/or carrying out the administrative actions to assist in obtaining available budget funds as required to facilitate and complete the purchasing/procurement actions.<br>
<br>
Processes fiscal documents for encumbrance reviews of funds availability to process contracts and purchase orders.<br>
<br>
This position is a Purchasing Cardholder for the Department and has the responsibility for ensuring that purchases made with the Purchasing Card are in compliance with the Commonwealth's and the Department's Purchasing Card rules and regulations as outlined in the current version of the Purchasing Card Manual (Pub. 363); also responsible for purchasing card acquisitions in the three bureaus and monthly reconciliation for the credit card statement.<br>
<br>
Creates, processes and maintains the bureaus Contract Purchase Orders; monitors recurring purchases and contracts taking independent action to handle renewal processing; monitors ongoing contracts and purchase orders to ensure that there are no lapses in service; and allows for adequate time to perform subsequent bids.</p></div>
<div><b>REQUIRED EXPERIENCE, TRAINING &amp; ELIGIBILITY</b></div>
<div><p>Three years of experience in purchasing work involving contact with vendors in the procurement of a variety of supplies, materials, equipment, or services; or<br>
<br>
Three years of experience in requisitioning, receiving, storing, distributing, and maintaining inventory of goods; or<br>
<br>
An equivalent combination of experience and training. Post-high school education may be substituted on a year-for-year basis for purchasing or stores experience to a maximum of two years.</p></div>
</div></div></div>
