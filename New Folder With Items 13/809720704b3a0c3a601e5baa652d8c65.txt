<h2>Business Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>JOB SUMMARY</b><br>
The Business Development Specialist is responsible for pursuing and developing relationships with developers, builders, trade associations, planning &amp; permitting offices, and other entities in an effort to generate new build leads for Charter Communications. In addition, secure, as necessary, temporary access agreements for SFU communities. Coordinate efforts for new build programs through cross-functional partnering with Construction, Technical Operations, Legal, Dispatch and Residential Sales teams. Coordination efforts include: formulating logistical details within the Technical Operations team, gathering details of necessary operations support, sales support and material resources in the field to ensure on-time property launches (both SFU &amp; MDU).<br>
<br>
<b>MAJOR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
Work across functional organizations and with industry associations, organizations or sources to identify new build projects in the market area<br>
<br>
Responsible for developing relationships with industry associations, local builders, developers, county administrators, model agents, leasing agents, etc. establishing an overall annual new build schedule in the market area<br>
<br>
Upon contract execution, take responsibility for the project management of new build through service activation<br>
<br>
Communicate with Engineering/Design, Construction departments, Internal Sales teams and public entities including:<ul>
<li>
Builders -City Planning Board</li>
<li>
Local Utility -Local County Administration</li>
<li>
Zoning Board -Dodge Report</li>
</ul>
<br>
Work with Sales teams to ensure their awareness of property launches to ensure proper planning<br>
<br>
Work across the organization to address the issues causing property concerns<br>
<br>
Maintain tracking and reporting on each assigned property; communicate progress, challenges and successes to management<br>
<br>
