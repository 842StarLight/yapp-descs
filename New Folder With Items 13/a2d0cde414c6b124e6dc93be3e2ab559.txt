<h2>AMS University Program</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Atlanta Motor Speedway is looking for college students or recent graduates who are interested in learning the skills needed to land their first job in professional sports.</div>
<div>
Experience needed for a job in minor or major league sports consists of SALES, promotions, SALES, media relations, SALES, public relations, SALES, customer service, SALES, in game entertainment, SALES....well, you get the picture.</div>
<div>
The most important experience you can acquire during an internship or entry level program is SALES experience. You must demonstrate the ability to bring people to the stadium, arena, coliseum, or speedway. That's what our 12 week program is designed for. You'll learn several different sales techniques and styles to help you personalize the methods that will make you successful in your sports career.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Answer questions concerning NASCAR weekend tickets, events, and schedules.</li>
<li>Make approximately 100 outbound calls per day to past customers and prospects to sell tickets and ancillary upsells for NASCAR weekend.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate computer records of prospects and customers within the CRM and ticketing systems.</li>
<li>Balance cash and receipt drawer at the end of each shift.</li>
<li>Take inbound sales calls.</li>
<li>Provide courteous and professional customer service.</li>
<li>Answer customer emails and chats with concise and professional answers.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Have a desire to work in minor or major league sports.</li>
<li>Be available for a 12 week program in the AMS ticket office beginning in January 2020.</li>
<li>Respond promptly to customer needs in a kind and courteous manner.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively, both orally and in writing.</li>
<li>Have a professional appearance - no visable tattoos or piercings.</li>
<li>Skilled in the use of personal computers and related software applications.</li>
<li>Ability to work evenings and weekends as required.</li>
<li>Ability to work long extended hours leading up to and during NASCAR weekend.</li>
<li>Have some knowledge of NASCAR racing.</li>
<li>Have a valid driver's license and pass all security and background checks</li>
</ul>
