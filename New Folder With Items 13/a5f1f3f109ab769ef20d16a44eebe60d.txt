<p><b>Service Technician &amp; Mechanic- Crane &amp; Heavy Equipment</b><b>ALL Crane Rental of Louisiana, LLC</b></p>
<p><b>
Geismar, LA (70734)</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
ALL Crane Rental of Louisiana, LLC. is seeking a Service Technician &amp; Mechanic- Crane &amp; Heavy Equipment. Hands-on experience with cranes and lift equipment is a definite plus but not required. Prefer local candidates or those willing to relocate. This is a full-time, non-exempt position.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for troubleshooting, maintenance, and repair on all makes of mobile cranes.</li>
<li>Diagnose, repair, adjust, and modify components as needed.</li>
<li>Reads, understands and interprets operating manuals, parts manuals, blueprints and technical drawings.</li>
<li>Positive interface with service writers, supervisor, parts department and customers.</li>
<li>Prepare proper documentation of actions taken.</li>
<li>Able to work with diagnostic equipment for troubleshooting and repairs.</li>
<li>Maintain conformity to safety requirements and other regulations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Experience Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A Class A or B CDL with an acceptable driving record is highly desored</li>
<li>Must have a strong diesel engine, hydraulic and electrical background</li>
<li>Must have basic set of mechanic’s hand tools</li>
<li>Strong initiative required; ability to work independently with minimal direct supervision</li>
<li>Ability to work safely around moving machinery; to work in conditions with marked changes in temperature &amp; humidity</li>
<li>Ability to safely operate complex construction equipment</li>
<li>Able to lift 50 lbs.</li>
</ul>
<p>Manufacturer’s training will be an integral part of this position. Applicant must be willing to learn new computer, electronic and hydraulic systems, follow safety guidelines as they evolve in our industry, and remain certified within the various portions of our field such as assembly, disassembly, and troubleshooting.</p>
