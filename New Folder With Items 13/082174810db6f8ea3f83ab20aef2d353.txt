<h2>Director, Event Technology I Sonesta Resort Hilton Head</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Job Responsibilities</b></h3>
<p><b>
Financial Management and Reporting</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Drive Results</b> by managing an efficient and profitable operation with a focused approach on revenue maximization and profitability using cost control measures in accordance with the company’s standard operating procedures.</li>
<li>
Achieve PSAV’s financial goals for the location(s) by effectively managing labor, equipment sub-rentals and other costs.</li>
<li>
Understand and utilize operational and financial reports and completes standard and ad hoc reports accurately and on time.</li>
<li>
Utilize the Company billing system to coordinate invoicing activities and makes certain that billing is reviewed and approved by clients.</li>
<li>
<b>
See the Big Picture</b> by completing the sales forecast at home location, ensuring they are accurate and submitted timely.</li>
<li>
Participate in business review presentations as needed, in collaboration with regional management.</li>
<li>
Review and manage location P &amp; L and develops action plans to address deficiencies/grow the business</li>
<li>
Confirms venue partners process all payments to PSAV in a timely basis.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Operations Management
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform daily floor operations including scheduling and equipment setups, operation, and strikes. Assist on the floor as needed.</li>
<li>
Ensure that all equipment and services are delivered on time, in good working condition and as specified by clients and the venue, and in accordance to brand standards.</li>
<li>
Anticipate equipment challenges and changes in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>
Use inventory control procedures to maintain proper inventory levels while also maintaining safety, security, and quality assurance of equipment.</li>
<li>
<b>
See the Big Picture</b> by efficiently sharing labor and equipment within the local market.</li>
<li>
Attend all operational venue meetings such as daily stand up meetings with venue operations staff.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Sales Management
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with Regional Sales Director to create effective sales strategies to maximize revenue per-event for assigned customers including upsell and cross-sell opportunities.</li>
<li>
Prepare and deliver compelling presentations that convey the value of PSAV and effectively solve customer challenges.</li>
<li>
Effectively prepare and present quotes and proposals in a timely manner to customers, including those submitted through www.psav.com, venue booking system, or other sources as assigned.</li>
<li>
Attend site visits, pre-conference planning meetings, and appropriate venue meetings as necessary to support the customer event experience.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with vendors and other departments/divisions of the company to capture and service events.</li>
<li>
Understand event cost structure and incorporate into solution designs according to established profitability guidelines.</li>
<li>
Attend customer meetings, understanding their goals and responding to their questions, concerns, and challenges.</li>
<li>
<b>
Do the Right Thing</b> by ensuring all customer information is up to date and accurate in the Customer Relationship Management system (CRM) at all times.</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledge of new product/service offerings and emerging technology supporting meetings and events</li>
<li>
Create and present information and reports, as needed, to senior management related to performance, pipeline, forecasting, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Customer Service
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Deliver World-Class Service</b> by cultivating and maintaining relationships with customers, venue personnel and various PSAV supporting functions and departments.</li>
<li>
Position will have oversight to personnel to assist with event execution.</li>
<li>
Exceed the expectations and needs of internal and external customers.</li>
<li>
Meet with guests on site to confirm that their needs are met and the equipment setup is accurate and working properly.</li>
<li>
Monitor small to medium size events and check in on customers throughout the day.</li>
<li>
Enforce the guidelines in the Employee Guide and/or venue standards for the team to portray a polished and professional image.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Technical Ability
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand and oversees the technical aspects of the job and applies basic to advanced troubleshooting and problem solving skills to guide technicians in resolving equipment and software issues in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>
Effectively utilizes applicable company computer systems.</li>
<li>
Act as on-site technical expert as needed for events.</li>
<li>
Assist on the floor with operations as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
People Development
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Value People</b> by promoting a culture of high performance, accountability and continuous improvement that values learning and a commitment to quality.</li>
<li>
Manage performance, addresses employee concerns, maintains adequate staffing levels, and facilitates team development.</li>
<li>
Manage the human resources activities including selection, performance management, and learning.</li>
<li>
Provide focused and continued coaching to develop the skills of team members.</li>
<li>
Train employees on PSAV standards for service and equipment, and verifies they are properly trained on company computer systems.</li>
<li>
Recommend team members for training opportunities, as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma is required. Bachelor’s degree is preferred.</li>
<li>
3+ years of audio visual experience</li>
<li>
1+ years of supervisory experience</li>
<li>
2+ years of customer service or hospitality experience is preferred.</li>
<li>
Sales experience is a plus.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of audio visual equipment in a live show environment</li>
<li>
Proficiency with the use of computer hardware</li>
<li>
Proficiency with computer software and programs, including the Internet and Microsoft Office</li>
<li>
Effective leadership abilities and customer satisfaction focus.</li>
<li>
A valid driver’s license is required for team members who may operate Company vehicles.</li>
</ul>
