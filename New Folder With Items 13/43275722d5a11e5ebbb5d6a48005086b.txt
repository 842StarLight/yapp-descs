<h2>Jr. Sourcing Specialist/Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Title: </b> Jr. Sourcing Specialist/Buyer</p>
<p><b>Department: </b>Sourcing</p>
<p><b>Supervisor: </b>Director of Strategic Sourcing</p>
<p><b>FLSA Status: </b>Non-Exempt</p>
<p><b>Introduction: </b></p>
<p>Microboard Processing, Inc. (MPI), located in Seymour, CT is a privately held, completely state-of-the-art Electronic Manufacturing Services (EMS) provider that has been in business for 35 years. We are relentless with our continuous improvement philosophy as well as our equipment, software and human capabilities, employing the brightest minds and the newest equipment (FUJI NXT III SMT, Automated climate controlled inventory towers, 5D X-Rays, in-line SPI’s and AOI’s, on-site Nitrogen Generation, etc.). We are constantly pursuing global certifications to maintain MPI at the leading edge of technology with a keen sensitivity to our environment (ISO 9001:2008, ISO 13485, AS91000C, ISO14001, CE, UL, ITAR.). Our dynamic growth has positioned MPI to look for the next superstar to join our Sourcing/Purchasing team.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities: </b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<p><b>Jr. Sourcing Specialist Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and maintain status reports needed by Sourcing and Supply Chain Management to determine work load and daily priorities.</li>
<li>Review customer provided documentation with Proposal Manager and appropriate MPI teams to determine customer requirements, special processes, and other data pertinent to the quote effort.</li>
<li>Format and upload customer documentation to materials quoting system.</li>
<li>Monitor open quote requests with suppliers.</li>
<li>Retrieve quote data from materials quoting system.</li>
<li>Create Costed Bill of Material (BOM), labor workbooks and supporting quote documentation for submission to customer.</li>
<li>Assist Proposal Manager with preparation of department metrics, quote reviews with executive management, and special tasks as needed.</li>
<li>Create and maintain purchase orders with suppliers.</li>
<li>Communicate supplier feedback to Purchasing and Program Management.</li>
<li>Assist Strategic Sourcing team, Purchasing team, and Supply Chain Director with special tasks and requirements.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Assistant Buyer Duties &amp; Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Program Management Team to manage the supply chain for various customer programs.</li>
<li>Purchase material and services in support of operational and production requirements.</li>
<li>Manage open Purchase Orders – confirm POs with vendor base work to expedite material shortages and maintain PO date accuracy to insure inventory levels are in balance.</li>
<li>Manage MRO Purchase Orders including consigned material cabinet.</li>
<li>Review and action non-conforming non-receivable materials.</li>
<li>Create, manage and maintain debit memos while working closely with Finance Team to ensure accuracy.</li>
<li>Manage material excess at the Program level to ensure budgets are not exceeded.</li>
<li>Pro-actively alert Program and Production Management Teams of material issues impacting production schedule.</li>
<li>Take on special projects as needed/directed.</li>
</ul>
