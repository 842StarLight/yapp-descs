 <b>General Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The position provides customer services including consulting, education, support and assistance. Leads, conducts and facilitates best linen practices, process improvement, linen utilization, and cost reduction efforts using project management tools.</p>
<p>
HHS is seeking qualified candidates for Linen Utilization Manager</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Tracks accurately and timely the usage of linen items in all areas of the hospital.</li>
<li>
Analyzes linen products for possible cost-savings alternatives for lowering costs.</li>
<li>
Recommends and implements strategic plans to reduce linen costs.</li>
<li>
Trains, monitors, supports and assists each facility with a Linen Data Management Program.</li>
<li>
Directs facilities’ staff on par level adjustments and trains nursing staff on acceptable par level use.</li>
<li>
Collaborates, consults, plans and educates linen users by designing and conducting Linen Education and training as needed.</li>
<li>
Documents linen cost reductions on a monthly, quarterly and annual basis by using project management tools.</li>
<li>
Implements a strong Reject Linen Program. Documents reject linen and works to resolve any linen quality issues identified through this program.</li>
<li>
Monitors and works to resolve any quantity, processing or distribution issues.</li>
<li>
Develops the reputation of being the “Expert” on linen distribution and utilization within the facilities.</li>
<li>
Creates and chairs a Linen Committee by getting support from the Administration and Nursing staff.</li>
<li>
Complies with and executes HHS’s utilization training program.</li>
</ul>
