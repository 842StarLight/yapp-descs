<h2>Process Improvement & Compliance Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Process Improvement &amp; Compliance Manager is responsible for researching, developing, implementing and monitoring of special projects among all departments, as directed by the VP of Retail Operations.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Functions</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</div>
<ul><li>
Utilizes project management skills for each initiative including: developing clear project charters, proper scoping, timeline management, data collection, and pre-project communication plans. Keeps projects on track and key sponsors informed. Utilizes escalation plans and countermeasures when project timelines or outcomes are at risk.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Provides subject matter expertise and advice on PI and PM training and content. Teaches improvement mindset and concepts for all levels of the organization through classroom, workshop, and project based coaching.</li>
<li>
Facilitates with some support large projects and coaches others on using improvement methods such as; Kaizen, 5S Workshops, A3s, Standard Work, Value Stream mapping, etc. Serves as a subject matter expert for such tools and concepts. Coaches and develops peers and PE staff to grow their Lean technical and consulting skills.</li>
<li>
Research and understand all aspects of assigned projects.</li>
<li>
Give clear and concise recommendations on project planning and implementation.</li>
<li>
Leads and facilitates improvement events and projects of a complex nature with some oversight, including assessment, planning, event facilitation, and follow-up/sustaining outcomes. Meets quality standards and the production of expected deliverables during all phases of the event and project life cycle.</li>
<li>
Train and develop store management personnel in the Kaizen method.</li>
<li>
Develop teaching and resource materials for the Kaizen program in collaboration with the training department.</li>
<li>
Search out and analyze opportunities that would benefit from a Kaizen Event.</li>
<li>
Analyze and monitor results from each Kaizen Event including ongoing Quality Assurance checks ensuring changes have stayed in place.</li>
<li>
Develop and deliver a presentation to management at the end of the each Kaizen Event.</li>
<li>
Facilitates improvement in operating performance through evaluation of analytical data from multiple systems in use throughout the enterprise.</li>
<li>
In conjunction with retail employees develop an effective material flow plan.</li>
<li>
Train and develop store management personnel in the Kaizen method.</li>
<li>
Develop a comprehensive manual for each system (i.e.: production methods) examined, such as: procedures, flow charts, maps, information guides for employees.</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance with all Goodwill policies, CARF standards, ISO and safety and security regulations.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Process Improvement &amp; Special Projects Manager will be a proven leader with the following:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors or Associates degree in business, marketing, or technical field required.</li>
<li>
Experience in contracts, Kaizen, ISO, manufacturing and Lean workflow process preferred.
</li>
<li>Experience in project management required.</li>
<li>
Skilled in Lean Management
</li>
<li>Certification minimum of Lean Bronze (or within 6 months of hire)</li>
<li>
Lean Silver within one year through SME</li>
<li>
Knowledge of change management principles and process improvement methodologies.</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Microsoft environments and Windows applications - Proficient Level in Word and Excel.</li>
<li>
Must be self-directed, organized, and have good communication skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task, performing several duties simultaneously without loss of composure, maintaining high quality.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates the ability to prioritize tasks, multi-task, and manage time, asking for help when needed.</li>
<li>
Able to lead and direct a group of people towards a project goal.</li>
</ul>
