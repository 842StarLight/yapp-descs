<h2>Reporting Analyst II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>The Reporting Analyst II (Business Reporting) provides services to the organization in the areas of Corporate Strategy, Compensation Reporting, and Sales Reporting and Analysis. This position also maintains global responsibilities for supporting specific global reports as assigned. Lastly, this position has the responsibility for development of applications and automation processes related to the Information topics they support.</p>
</div>

<div>

With some 29,000 people in more than 120 countries, which we’re looking to expand, we’re a great place for you to show us your worth, step up to new challenges and grow your career.</p>
</div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What does the role involve?</b></h4>
<div>
<p>As a Reporting Analyst II, you will manage the development and implementation of sales and marketing information systems, applications and tools to improve utilization of the Company’s database, including customer information, product information, trade information, and general market information; identify new opportunities where sales and marketing automation/information can be used to increase profitability. You will create reporting and applications from specification to delivery and support, using various tools and distribute reporting for sales force and specialists, using myReports portal and other portals.</p>
<p>
The Reporting Analyst II, will be responsible for the design, implementation, and maintenance of bonus calculation systems to ensure timely and accurate bonus payments for all non-Corporate bonus plans; support HR Compensation in the development of variable pay calculations to ensure bonus measurements are objective, measurable, and consistently applied according to plan documents. You will coordinate with HR Compensation to facilitate the audit and upload of payroll for bonuses and develop and support certain global market reach topics for Hilti world-wide. You will also provide guidance, training, and support for Hilti personnel who are new in position on BI and Business Objects Analysis (BOA) and other tools / applications.</p>
