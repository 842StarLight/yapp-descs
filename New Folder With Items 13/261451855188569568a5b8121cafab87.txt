<p>
We live by the Golden Rule—treating our customers the way we like to be treated—with integrity, courtesy and respect. Granger is seeking a proven project facilitator familiar with responding to proposals and administering contracts to complement our sales and service teams.</p>
<p><i>
The market development administrator provides key project management support to the sales team by efficiently facilitating the administrative details of the sales process. The individual in this position also provides support services to the entire market development team.</i></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader">

SUCCESS IN THIS ROLE</b><b>:</b>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Key Accountabilities:</b></h3>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Service Contracts and Agreements Administration</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Review and analyze requests for proposals from municipalities, communities, large commercial industrial groups, institutions and other potential and existing customers to determine:
<ul>
<li>Need to investigate new or unusual stipulations, requirements or conditions of sale and secure internal approval for agreement or counterproposal of conditions</li>
<li>Appropriate internal Granger associates to be included in the decision-making process to respond to RFP or request for service</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Facilitate the contract execution process including coordinating appropriate meetings to determine price, obstacles to providing service, required interactions with operations to execute the service</li>
<li>Manage timelines, deliverables and ownership of required tasks to submit and or respond to RFPs or request for service, and provide project management support to ensure success</li>
<li>Compile, review and submit final sales document within required deadlines, with appropriate internal final review as needed</li>
<li>Coordinates insurance and Encore renewals with attention to possible rate or service changes</li>
<li>Create and monitor an internal tracking system to stay abreast of contract end dates and timelines for renewing agreements</li>
<li>Explain terms and conditions to appropriate internal customers and ensure obligations are being met including payments and services</li>
<li>Maintain organized system of physical and digital records in Encore</li>
<li>Follow language standards and required contract content for existing and new contracts</li>
<li>Maintain an audit file for each contract which will include original contract, all correspondence, changes/deviations, amendments, clarifications, payment schedules</li>
<li>Continually monitor and evaluate performance against contract specifications and recommends appropriate remedial action as necessary</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Marketing Support</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Assist in organizing and facilitating the execution of marketing campaigns as needed</li>
<li>Support marketing with information management including compiling, formatting, and reporting information and materials as needed</li>
<li>Assist in the organizing of promotional events and traditional or digital campaigns and attend them to facilitate their success as needed</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>General Department Support</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Assist with daily administrative tasks to ensure the functionality and coordination of the department’s activities including maintaining spreadsheets and databases populated with statistical, financial and non-financial information</li>
<li>Perform miscellaneous job-related duties as assigned.</li>
<li>Assist with the distribution of outbound customer communications as needed</li>
<li>Assist team with data management needs including requesting reports from other departments</li>
</ul>
