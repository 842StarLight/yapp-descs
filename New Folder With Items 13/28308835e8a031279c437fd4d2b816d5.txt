<div>
The position of Intake Representative is the first line of contact for all services in relation with pharmacy referrals. The Intake Referral Representative is responsible for the accurate and complete input and processing of specialty pharmacy and infusion therapy referrals.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Duties</div>
<ul>
<li> Ensure that all referrals are complete, correct and within RE Pharmacy's scope of service.</li>
<li> Verify insurance is accurate and complete before to transferring to pharmacy staff.</li>
<li> Collect information for authorization for therapy and re-authorization as necessary.</li>
<li> Request and perform timely follow-up on authorizations for medical and pharmacy benefits</li>
<li> Enter patient demographic information into the operating system.</li>
<li> Determine the route and correct therapy type for the order.</li>
<li> Notify patients/referral sources regarding coverage and payment responsibilities as necessary.</li>
<li> Respond to inquiries from patients and referral sources via telephone, fax and electronic data interfaces.</li>
<li> Document in the patientâ€™s progress notes, all requests and conversations for the purposes of coordination of care.</li>
<li> Aid in the resolution of consumer/customer complaints by identifying problems and coordinating appropriate corrective action through attention to detail and taking the initiative to resolve issues before they become a problem.</li>
<li> Work with team of other pharmacists, pharmacy technicians, nurse manager and sales staff to ensure quality of care to all patients.</li>
<li> Effectively defuse customer escalations and resolve issues with their prescription benefits utilizing the available resources, databases and references to perform the required task.</li>
<li> Research and investigate orders that cannot be identified in software database obtaining any necessary missing information.</li>
<li> Follow all processes in regards to new patients, continued care and refills.</li>
<li> Partner with other departments for timely resolution of requests/inquiries to include pharmacy, shipping, customer care, finance, claims processing and eligibility.</li>
<li> Act as representative of company with outside prescribers as needed.</li>
<li> Following up on requests to ensure they were handled appropriately and in a timely manner.</li>
<li> Responsible for answering request in a timely manner as well as proper detailed keeping of all records regarding any participant/prescriber interaction.</li>
<li> Assists in other queues outside of normal work area where cross-trained.</li>
</ul>
