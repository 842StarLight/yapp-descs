<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Come Join POA's growing and dynamic IT team as a Tier III Help-desk Technician at our branch in Beaverton, OR.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Tier III Help Desk Technician is the last line of technical support for the Help Desk at Pacific Office Automation. As a Tier III staff member you are the final point of escalation for the help desk. You will be interacting with clients and 3rd party vendors to resolve highly technical issues involving all aspects of our clients IT environment. You should be able to demonstrate exceptional critical thinking, systems analysis and breadth of technology capabilities along with excellent customer service skills.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>High-Level Job Description</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work within enterprise ticketing system.</li>
<li>
Manage personal workflow to ensure efficient operations</li>
<li>
Conscientious documentation of all activities.</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot advanced software, hardware and network issues.</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot Setup new user and email accounts that did not succeed</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot advanced printer and Software issues on Client PCs</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot advanced networking issues</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot Server down issues</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot Hardware/server alerts from Infrastructure</li>
<li>
Document Resolution steps for closed tickets and notes for escalations</li>
<li>
Document new issues for Tier 2 in IT glue</li>
<li>
Monitor Backup systems and work with backup team to restore customer data as needed</li>
<li>
Escalate to Tier four as needed, continue to track and follow up on the issue until resolved.</li>
<li>
Work issues to resolution</li>
<li>
Mentor Tier 1 &amp; 2
</li>
<li>Aid in training Tier 1 &amp; 2</li>
<li>
Provide technical support over the phone or Web to end users.</li>
<li>
Use remote support software to take control of end-users' computers to troubleshoot, diagnose and resolve issues.</li>
<li>
Work with TAM/Sales as needed to resolve issues.</li>
<li>
Provide root cause analysis as needed.</li>
<li>
</li>
</ul>

5+ years MSP experience and 7+ years of IT experience</li></ul>
<p><b>
Bonus Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>

<li>
MCSE (or MCITP)</li>
<li>
CCISP/CCNA/CCNP</li>
<li>
Network+ or A+ Certifications.</li>
<li>
Knowledge and hands-on experience providing technical support to users using Windows 7, Windows 8 and other mainstream Microsoft applications</li>
</ul>
