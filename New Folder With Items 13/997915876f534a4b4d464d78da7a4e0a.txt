<h2>Senior Account Executive- Managed Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>We’re Apogee. Nice to meet you. At our core, we’re a student services organization that happens to focus on IT. We touch the lives of binge-watching, music-loving, game-playing, finals-taking, seminar-attending, class-selecting students by providing IT infrastructure that meets the needs of today’s technology-savvy generation.</p>
<p>
We work hard, celebrate success and challenge everyone in our organization to be a problem solver. If you are as passionate as we are about the intersection of technology and education, join us today.</p>
<p>
We are seeking a senior account executive to manage a territory in the midwest. You will be home-office based in Chicago. Our company is headquartered in Austin, TX.</p>
<p><b>
Who you are</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Senior sales executive with a focus on information technology, enterprise software, managed services and Infrastructure as a Service (IAAS).</li>
<li>Strategic thinker who has experience developing plans to target key accounts which includes internal collaboration with executives and engineering teams to leverage the right solution to the client.</li>
<li> Actively participate in the shaping of the Apogee footprint to be sold, financial construct and lead the sales engagement with an ability to pitch the big picture whilst being able to deep dive into details particularly during contract negotiations.</li>
<li>Experienced (10+ years) in complicated sales cycles, especially in a role that often requires knowledge of how state buying cycles and contracts work.</li>
<li>Consistent record of developing a deep network of clients within a managed services company or consultant environment.</li>
<li>Willing to travel up to 30% of the time or more as needed to achieve company goals.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you’ll do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build your territory and generate revenue.</li>
<li>Cultivate deep long-term relationships with our clients (leaders at colleges and universities) and bring multiple decision-makers together.</li>
<li>Consult and guide University executives through strategies on building a connected campus that allows their strategic mission to thrive.</li>
<li>Strengthen Apogee’s value proposition across your territory by engaging in brand building and awareness activities (attending conferences, seeking out opportunities to connect with the client).</li>
<li>Manage the complete sales cycle from prospecting to closing with new and existing clients.</li>
<li>Achieve revenue targets.</li>
<li>Update internal systems and participate in team and strategy meetings.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>You’ll love this job if this describes you:</b></p>
<p><b>
WOO.</b> WOO stands for winning others over. You enjoy meeting new people and you find that you create common ground easily. You’re a natural at striking up conversations, breaking the ice and making connections.</p>
<p><b>
Influencing.</b> You naturally turn thoughts into action and often can persuade people to join you because of your positivity and ability to bring people together.</p>
<p><b>
Simplifying the complex.</b> You are able to navigate an organization (like a university) to achieve changes that benefit the groups within it.
</p>
<p><b>Business and Financial Acumen.</b> Probably because you’ve worked since you were old enough, you have a developed sense of how businesses work and how to get things done in a professional environment. You have presented financials to executives and know how to dive into the numbers.
</p>
<p><b>Attentiveness.</b> You are dedicated to meeting the expectations of your external and internal customers. You’re able to build effective relationships, and your hard work typically gains you respect and the confidence of others.</p>
<p><b>
Presentation Skills.</b> You’re no Ricky Ricardo, but you’re effective in a variety of presentations settings: one-on-one, small and large groups, with peers and bosses (inside and outside the organization).</p>
<p><b>
Technical Aptitude.</b> Pick up on technical things quickly, and you are good at learning new products, and new technologies.</p>
<p><b>
Inherit Time Management.</b> You have a natural strength in managing your time, and it’s demonstrated in the work systems, and approach you to take with your job.</p>
<p><b>
Some important details about this job:</b></p>
<p><i>
Location.</i> This is a full-time role with Apogee. You will work from home, in either Chicago, IL or Indianapolis, IN.</p>
<p><i>
Travel. </i><i>This role will require about 30% within your region, with some travel annually to conferences, and team training events. The territory is Michigan and Indiana.</i></p>
<p><i>
Compensation.</i> We offer good pay, great benefits, and perks. The role has a strong base salary and is part of our aggressive sales incentive program. We’re a small company and you’ll soon realize you're not a badge number here. You count.</p>
