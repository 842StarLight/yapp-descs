<p><b>About MadaLuxe Group</b></p>
<p>MadaLuxe Group is an innovative global fashion leader and North America’s largest distributor of luxury fashion and accessories. Today, the group markets and distributes goods from the world’s most iconic European design houses. In close partnership with its clients, MadaLuxe Group consistently evolves its offerings to strategically drive market demand and reach the established and emerging luxury consumer. The group not only drives sales through the world’s most prestigious retailers, but also through MadaLuxe Vault, their own direct-to-consumer retail destination.</p>
<p><b>Primary Objective of Position</b></p>
<p>MadaLuxe is recruiting for an outgoing, friendly, and team oriented candidate to join our staff as a Planner. The E-Comm Retail Planner is responsible for maintaining appropriate inventory levels in stores to support sales, promotions, visual merchandising, and local trends.</p>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities include but are not limited to: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive financial results that maximize sales, inventory and profitability at the channel level</li>
<li>Develop, execute and communicate strategic merchandise financial plans and strategies to support the merchandising, marketing and financial objectives of the channel</li>
<li>Direct the development of pre-season sales, gross margin and inventory plans that support product strategies</li>
<li>Analyze historical data and current trends to identify risk and opportunities to business, recommending strategies to achieve financial goals</li>
<li>Review total financial plans with Merchandise Managers and appropriate leadership to ensure alignment of future financial and product opportunities and inventory productivity</li>
<li>Responsible for open-to-buy process and class level, top style level forecasts for each season</li>
<li>Analyze current trends to create markdown strategy</li>
<li>Analyze selling and allocate merchandise for one or more departments</li>
<li>Recap and communicate location and size selling performance</li>
<li>Execute the release of initial inventories and in-season replenishment to stores</li>
<li>Meet or exceed sales, gross margin and inventory turn goals</li>
<li>Provide buyers and planners bottoms up basic forecasts by size basic to meet in-stock targets</li>
<li>Ensure timely and accurate execution of new store openings</li>
<li>Develop knowledge of geographic and store-attribute performance and trends through data analysis and field communication</li>
<li>Conduct quarterly size and pack analysis to support the buy.</li>
<li>Review and evaluate assortment plans with the buying and planning team to ensure buyer vision is in alignment with store inventory targets. Make recommendations as needed based on product/store trend analysis.</li>
<li>Collaborate with peers to ensure consistency of information, share best practices and develop systems knowledge</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Abilities</b></p>
<p><b>Qualifications Preferred</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business, Merchandising or related field</li>
<li>5-7 years’ experience in e-comm retail merchandise planning, buying or allocation</li>
<li>Strong understanding of e-comm retail math including intricacies of open to buy, inventory turnover and gross margin</li>
<li>Familiarity with merchandise management systems and retail planning software</li>
<li>Demonstrated understanding of store profiles and how merchandise should be grouped</li>
<li>Wholesale apparel experience is preferred</li>
<li>Related project management experience strongly preferred</li>
<li>Experience as an Allocator in an apparel specialty e-comm retailer or in store management preferred.</li>
<li>Proficient in Excel</li>
<li>Develop and maintain appropriate reports to support buying needs</li>
</ul>
