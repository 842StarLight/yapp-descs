<h2>IT Practice Integration Support Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>General Summary:</b><br>
The IT Practice Support Technician is responsible for providing exemplary support for Vetsource customers in all areas of the business. This position will be support and address escalated service requests which may have initially been fielded by sales or call center teams.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for managing and responding to Practice related service requests</li>
<li>Provide first response and ultimate resolution of Practice related service requests within an agreed upon service level agreement</li>
<li>Receive and respond to inbound Practice service requests, telephone calls, and service related emails</li>
<li>Initiate outbound communications to Practice personnel requesting support</li>
<li>Contribute and maintain documentation related to support processes and procedures</li>
<li>Coordinate internal and external resources required to aid in the resolution of outstanding service requests</li>
<li>Design and document both technical and process improvement resolutions related to data integration issues</li>
</ul>
