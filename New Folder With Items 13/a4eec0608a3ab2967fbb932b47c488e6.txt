<h2>Marketing Strategist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Complex Networks</b>, formed from the acquisition of Complex Media Inc. through a joint venture by Verizon and Hearst, is a global lifestyle brand and media company and one of the most influential voices in popular culture today. <b>Complex Networks’</b> digital channels, which include Complex, First We Feast, Pigeons &amp; Planes, Sole Collector and more, is a Top 10 publisher in the U.S. for social engagement on channels like Facebook and YouTube. A diversified and profitable media business with premium distribution partners such as Netflix, Hulu, Pluto TV, Roku, and iFlix, Complex Networks’ original content exemplifies convergence culture, exploring topics that span across popular culture from music to movies, sports to video games, fashion to food.</p>
<p><b>
Complex</b> is seeking a <b>Pre-Sale Marketing Strategist </b>to join the <b>Brand Partnerships Studio (Marketing Department)</b> for our <b>New York</b> office. The candidate will be primarily responsible for conceptualizing, writing and managing integrated marketing proposals to create compelling partnership programs for a variety of advertising partners. Responsibilities also include working alongside Strategy Management team to merchandise internal editorial opportunities and produce innovative brand storytelling tools.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with Account Directors and Branded Content, Video, Social and Design Teams to develop integrated programs for blue chip brands across a variety of categories</li>
<li>Lead creative brainstorms</li>
<li>Creation of 8x -12x proposals/sales tools per week</li>
<li>Proactively produce new ideas and opportunities based on innovations in the marketplace</li>
<li>Deliver client-facing presentations alongside Account Directors</li>
<li>Interface regularly with senior management and liaise with a series of internal stakeholders</li>
<li>Develop comprehensive branded content strategies across editorial, video, social and experiential</li>
<li>Work alongside Promotions Team to identify and curate talent for creative programs</li>
<li>Provide concise art direction to Design Team members
</li>
</ul>
