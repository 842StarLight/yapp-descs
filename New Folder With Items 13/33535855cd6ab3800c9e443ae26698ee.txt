<h2>Marketing Communications Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Shift: Full time, Days</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Under the direction of the Strategic Marketing Manager, the Marketing Communications Associate will provide a full range of support services to the Marketing Communications department to meet department and organizational objectives.<br>
<br>
1. Provides full administrative support including answering department phone, ordering office supplies, managing invoices and contracts, and other administrative duties as assigned.<br>
<br>
2. Develops a variety of marketing communications materials that support business objectives within budgeted guidelines; utilizes graphic design software to develop and create letters, seminar/event flyers and posters for physician outreach and other assigned client departments.<br>
<br>
3. Works with marketing team members to maintain an up-to-date fiscal year marketing promotions calendar; maintains project production schedules and follows-up with marketing team members to support the team with meeting scheduled deadlines.<br>
<br>
4. Leads special event planning (i.e., Physician and community meetings, community events and health fairs); attends events to organize and direct support activities such as set-up, registration, etc.<br>
<br>
5. Orders, reviews and assures inventory and quality of all NCH collateral material and branded premium items. Negotiates and selects vendors. Tracks budget and ensures purchases are made within budgetary limits.<br>
<br>
6. Handles and/or participates in special projects as assigned.<br>
<br>
7. Adheres to all Northwest Community Hospital standards, policies, and procedures.<br>
</div>
<div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<div>
1. Bachelor’s Degree in English, Journalism, Marketing, Public Relations or any other Marketing or Communications related field required. 1 year of relevant business experience preferred.<br>
<br>
2. Good working knowledge of Microsoft Office, including Power Point, Outlook, Word and Excel, required. Knowledge or experience with graphic design software a plus.<br>
<br>
3. Excellent verbal communication and interpersonal skills necessary to interact effectively with all levels of the hospital staff, physicians and external customers.<br>
<br>
4. The written communication skills necessary to effectively develop Marketing communications materials including letters, flyers and posters.<br>
<br>
5. Organization skills necessary to coordinate and execute multiple projects while maintaining established deadlines.<br>
<br>
6. Availability to work evenings or weekends for special events as required.<br>
<br>
7. Ability to remain flexible as work load and priorities can change very rapidly.<br>
<br>
8. Access to an automobile and a valid Illinois driver’s license<br>
</div>
