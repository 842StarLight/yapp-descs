<h2>Configuration Management Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview:
</b></p>
<p>The individual shall perform and support to the Cybersecurity support team with configuration management of the Cybersecurity Enterprise Change Configuration Board (ECCB), support weekly auditing events and ATFP Risk Management Framework (RMF) document management.</p>
<p><b>
Primary/Major</b><b> Duties and Responsibilities (Essential Functions):</b></p>
<p>
Individual will provide contractor support work for activities including Item Management tasking under the Navy’s Anti-Terrorism Force Protection (ATFP) Ashore Program.</p>
<p>
The individual shall perform and support to the Cybersecurity support team with configuration management of the Cybersecurity Enterprise Change Configuration Board (ECCB), support weekly auditing events and ATFP Risk Management Framework (RMF) document management.</p>
<p>
Assists with a full realm of administrative support services and coordinates with internal and external customers and stakeholders. Accountable for maintaining the project tracking sheet for internal use and customer use. The individual will provide technical support involving multiple tasks/projects to include, but not limited to, the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Support and supervise documentation of configuration change management procedures.</li>
<li>Responsible for the execution of the process controls, ensuring that staff comply with process and data standards.</li>
<li>Directs, prioritizes and schedules audits; ensures that any corrective action identified in Process and/or Database audits is carried out.</li>
<li>Oversees activities relative to the purchase or sale of equipment, materials, products, or services: Examines performance requirements, delivery schedules, and estimates of cost of material, equipment, and production to ensure completeness and accuracy.</li>
<li>Support ATFP’s ECCB weekly meeting</li>
<li>Analyze and triage Engineering Change Proposals (ECPs)</li>
<li>Basic understanding of NAVFAC AT/FP policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of USN Cybersecurity principles and process.</li>
<li>Experience with a thorough knowledge in configuration management principles and use of US Navy applications.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze, maintain and recommend changes to ECCB and Navy RMF-related applications and databases.</li>
<li>Ability to conduct assessment and approval of hardware and software configurations changes via the ECP process.</li>
<li>The contractor shall support the management and transfer of Government property during system changes.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>

<li>Excellent time management skills and ability to multi-task and prioritize work</li>

</ul>
