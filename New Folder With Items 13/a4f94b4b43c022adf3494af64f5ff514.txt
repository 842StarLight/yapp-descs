PRIMARY PURPOSE OF POSITION<br>
<br>
The Director of the IT Business Office is the leader for IT Financial Management and IT Cost Management for Exelon. Responsible for related management model process and continuous improvement while driving governance, standards, and tools across IT. Directs the IT Planner Organization for effective and efficient delivery of accurate financial planning and variance analysis in coordination with Corporate Finance. Leads the IT Cost Management/Efficiency Initiatives Program Management Office or individual initiatives with business case creation and financial modeling. Responsible for IT annual benchmarking and Chart of Account activities through planning, execution and reporting, including insight and identified gaps for consideration as initiatives into Business Plan. Manages team within IT Business Office providing feedback and coaching while promoting Exelon’s core values. Manages department baseline budget and completes additional tasks as assigned by the VP of the Office of the CIO.<br>
<br>
PRIMARY DUTIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Drive disciplined financial management practices across IT in partnership with finance ensuring consistency and strong financial controls that enable financial accuracy and predictability. In addition to managing a centralized planning function consisting of 32 financial planners focused on comprehensive support and oversight of IT’s budget including monthly financial management operations, IT billing, long range planning, and optimization of baseline and project investments. 30%</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>IT Leader for Cost Management/Efficiency Initiatives delivering an effective and efficient PMO process, status reporting, dashboard/metrics, briefing to IT SLT and program management. Provide leadership and expert knowledge on business case creation and financial modeling. May be Project Manager for specific initiatives with workplan, financial and resource planning and reporting. May manage budget for PMO and any specific initiatives. 20%</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Core Functional Area Manager (CFAM) for IT Financial Management and IT Cost Management Process Areas. Lead Peer Group meetings, continuous process improvement, and maintains management model documentation. Solicits feedback from Peer Group for process gaps and Business Plan initiatives. 15%</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>IT Leader for annual benchmarking and Chart of Account analysis (service category by cost and FTE) responsible for planning, execution and reporting in coordination with Corporate Finance. Insight and gaps identified through process are considered for Business Plan initiatives. 15%</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Evaluate performance and provide coaching for IT personnel. Promote diversity and foster teamwork, collaboration, and a learning organization. Provide input on development and retention processes for IT employees. 10%</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Manage department baseline budget. 5%</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Complete other tasks as assigned by the IT VP, OCIO or as requested by IT Senior Leadership. 5%</li></ul>
