<h2>Solutions Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>***This employer does NOT offer visa sponsorship***</b></p>
<p>Futurex is seeking talented individuals with a passion for technology and an interest in the business to join our Solutions Architect team in the position of Solutions Architect. The position is based out of Futurex’s Engineering Campus in Bulverde, Texas.</p>
<p>The Solutions Architect role is a hybrid between business development and IT support. Solutions Architects are as comfortable working with technology as they are with people. The position focuses initially on developing a deep knowledge of Futurex technology by participating in our software quality assurance process, progressing into customer support and sales with Tier-1 organizations worldwide.</p>

<p>Scenic corporate campus located in Bulverde, Texas, approximately 15 minutes north of San Antonio, Texas.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze customer requirements and recommend enhancements to our cutting-edge, hardware-based cryptographic solutions</li>
<li>Provide ongoing technical support and guidance to Futurex’s global customer base</li>
<li>Test and troubleshoot new and existing hardware, software, and firmware developed for the data security and encryption industry</li>
<li>Conduct customer training on our products and data security best practices</li>
<li>Provide project management services for custom development initiatives and global Hardened Enterprise Security Platform implementations</li>
<li>Write and maintain technical documentation released to the public</li>
<li>Understand industry-specific APIs and protocols used when interfacing with external systems</li>
<li>Apply business knowledge and technical ability to create state-of-the-art solutions to help enterprises secure their sensitive data</li>
</ul>
