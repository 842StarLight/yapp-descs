<div>At Amazon, we're working to be the most Customer-centric company on earth. One customer experience we are constantly pushing to the next level is delivery speed. To do this, Amazon is rapidly building a dedicated air transportation network to exceed customers’ expectations on delivery speed. Amazon Air is seeking a Sr. Mgr, Network Capacity Planner to build scalable tools and processes to forecast and price the scheduled Air capacity available within the Amazon Air network.<br>
<br>
We are looking for a dynamic, self-starting Sr. Mgr, Network Capacity Planner to enable AIR to scale with Amazon’s growing global demand. As a Sr. Mgr, Network Capacity Planner, you will be responsible to identify, design, develop, and execute new processes, policies, goals and solutions that increase delivery speed, reduce errors, decrease costs, and improve the company’s overall transportation program, specifically relating to air transportation. To do this you will work across operations, procurement, research science, technology, scheduling, sales, engineering, and finance teams. This position requires a person with the ability to skillfully communicate with internal stakeholders, use analytics to solve complex problems, and stay hyper focused on the customers’ needs. The role provides an opportunity to shape our start-up like culture and make long term customer focused and cost effective decisions, directly impacting Amazon’s transportation network.<br>
<br>
If you want to work for a great company, have the opportunity to innovate, manage, and create new air and ground solutions and most importantly love to delight customers, then join us in making Amazon history! Amazon Air is seeking highly motivated individuals to be a part of the Amazon Air division.<br>
<br>
In this role, you will be responsible for:<br>
<ul>
<li>Partnering with internal business units including operations, ground transportation, finance, research, technology, engineering and global real estate to effectively manage expansion of air network</li>

<li>Communicating effectively with both external (Service Providers, Carriers, Vendors) and internal (Finance, Technology, Air Gateways and Hubs, Sort Centers, Fulfillment Centers) customers</li>
<li>Creating reports and process improvement initiatives to improve air and ground operations</li>
<li>Communicating goals, roles, responsibilities and desired outcomes to cross-functional project teams to gain buy-in and delivery to project schedules</li>
<li>Address barriers through problem solving, communication and active coordination with stakeholders</li>
<li>Working within time constraints to meet business critical needs (often managing multiple, time-sensitive and high priority initiatives simultaneously), while measuring and identifying activities performed and ensuring service requirements are met.</li>
<li>Able to systematically escalate problems or variances in the operating plan to the relevant owners and teams, following through to resolution to ensure they are delivered.</li>
<li>Navigating through ambiguity, managing and coordinating multiple project assignments simultaneously in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment, accepting ownership and accountability of the process and delivering on commitments</li>
