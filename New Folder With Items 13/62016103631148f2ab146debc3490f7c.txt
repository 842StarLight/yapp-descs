<p>JOB SUMMARY</p>
<p>
The Account Executive - Solutions collaborates with clients who are looking to improve workflows and streamline business processes to help them build productive and secure environments for their organization. The Account Executive - Solutions is required to maximize all solution business opportunities on behalf of EO Johnson Business Technologies. By liaising with customers, current and potential, and other departments within the organization, the Account Executive - Solutions takes all worthwhile opportunities to expand the business. This position will be responsible for the Eau Claire/Minneapolis marketing area.</p>
<p>
PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develops sales targets and strategies with the help of the Assistant Solutions Manager</li>
<li>
Grows the account base as well as closing solution sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Monitors and reports on sales progress and completing sales target goals</li>
<li>
Use initiatives to prospect for new clients, including following leads, building a referral network, visiting potential clients, making sales presentations, and closing the sale</li>
<li>
Attends conferences, community network events, trade shows and functions to promote EO Johnson solutions and other product offerings</li>
<li>
Gives advice to potential and current customers regarding consultation and software services, offering a range of solutions appropriate to the client’s needs succinctly expressing competitive advantages</li>
<li>
Demonstrates expert knowledge on key product lines and staying up-to-date on industry trends</li>
<li>
Works well under pressure, efficiently, within time constraints</li>
<li>
Self-starter with motivation to constantly improve and meet short and long term goals</li>
</ul>
<p>
MINIMUM REQUIRED EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE</p>
<ul><li>
2 years of business to business consultative sales experience selling business technology products and services</li></ul>
<p>
PREFERRED EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE</p>
<ul>
<li>
College degree in business, sales, marketing or 4 years equivalent work experience</li>
<li>
Proven record of sales success</li>
<li>
Formal sales training</li>
</ul>

ADDITIONAL ELIGIBILITY QUALIFICATIONS</p>

</ul>
