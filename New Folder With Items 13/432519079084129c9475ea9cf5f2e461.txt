<h2>Director of Marketing - Pardee Homes (Inland Empire)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Are you interested in joining an infectious company culture where accountability, top performance and teamwork are valued and rewarded? A company that is celebrating more than 95 years in the home building industry.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Pardee Homes is looking for an experienced, enthusiastic individual to join our talented group as a Director of Marketing in our Inland Empire office.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Highlights:</b> The Director of Marketing will work with the brand/division VP of Sales &amp; Marketing by planning and directing marketing efforts for the brand/division.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
 <b>Position Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Using home office templates and/or direction, modifies or produces creative pieces for advertising, collateral, sales office displays, signage, etc. ensuring adherence to company brand standards and marketing policies.</li>
<li>Purchases all local offline media, including signage programs, purchased consumer lists, etc.</li>
<li>Works with local and/or national marketing agencies and business partners to produce and maintain all marketing collateral including brochures, price sheets, fliers, and broker outreach materials.</li>
<li>Guides model merchandising direction and coordinates activities related to model and sales office installations and potentially model landscape design.</li>
<li>Participates in the selection of model options and upgrades.</li>
<li>Coordinates the closure of model complex and sales environment at community close-out.</li>
<li>Assists sales team with all consumer and broker events.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains current knowledge of competitor marketing programs, pricing and products, as well as consumer demographics and economic influences.</li>
<li>Utilizes available home office/national and local market research to develop community-specific Buyer Profiles to guide marketing activities.</li>
<li>Tracks results for all marketing initiatives, including detailed analysis of traffic sources.</li>
<li>Directs consultants on signage, model merchandising, sales office design, photography and renderings and collateral, as appropriate.</li>
<li>Participates in the development of standards and options programs for all new communities.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains marketing budgets for all communities and division as a whole. Review, code, and approves marketing-related invoices.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains division and community-specific marketing plans.</li>
<li>Assists sales team with technical support in community sales offices and models.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Position Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School or GED equivalent required. Bachelor’s degree in Marketing highly preferred.</li>
<li>A minimum of 3 years of previous Marketing experience or related industry experience required. New Home Marketing experience preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively present marketing ideas to internal teams and outside parties.</li>
<li>Superior verbal and written communication skills including grammar, spelling, and proof-reading skills.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with a focus on solving problems and developing creative solutions.</li>
<li>Ability to work both independently and collaboratively.</li>
<li>Experience and proficiency in InDesign and Photoshop is preferred.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft applications including Word, Excel, Outlook and PowerPoint.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Company Highlights:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Pardee Homes is a member of TRI Pointe Group® (NYSE: TPH), a family of premium regional homebuilders.</li>
<li>Pardee Homes Inland Empire was recognized as 2017 Builder of the Year by the Building Industry Association of Southern California – Riverside County Chapter.</li>
<li>The company was among the first to embrace master-planned community concepts, green homebuilding measures, environmentally-sensitive development practices, and customer-focused processes for new home purchases.</li>
<li>Pardee Homes is also widely known for its steadfast civic outreach, and has earned scores of honors for customer service, green and sustainable building practices, architectural design, master-planned communities, and marketing.</li>
<br>
</ul>
