<p>Vice President of US Sales</p>
<p>
As Vice President of US Sales, you will establish, develop and maintain the Netwatch US Sales function, ensuring growth, revenue and profit targets across our indirect and direct sales channels. You will recruit, develop and retain a high performing Sales team for channel sales roles. As a Senior business leader, you will develop sales strategies and tactical plans to deliver business objectives. This role will work closely with the Marketing function to build channel and end-user relationships and support business development.</p>
<p>
Key areas of responsibility:</p>
<ul>
<li>Create the Sales budget for the region at territory, industry and product level aligned to the business plan for the region</li>
<li>Develop and implement a sales management process for delivery of on-target revenue through pipeline management, territory business plans, reporting and review</li>
<li>Prepare Sales Reports and present, as required, with an analysis of results and recommendations on issues, actions and initiatives</li>
<li>Participate proactively in Sales strategy discussions and decisions of the global Sales team</li>
<li>Develop a national Sales organisation structure that meets the current and forecast needs of the business</li>
<li>Design roles that meet business needs to engage, challenge and develop team members.</li>
<li>Recruit, develop and retain a high performing Sales team</li>
<li>Manage work allocation, training and performance development to deliver a motivated and efficient team</li>
<li>Assess performance of the Sales team and promptly identify and address below-target performance</li>
<li>Develop a regional Sales strategy and tactical plans at territory level aligned to the business plan.</li>
<li>Develop initiatives to enter new markets and displace competitors</li>
<li>Train the Sales team and channel partners in the products, features, and value propositions</li>
<li>Implement continuous improvement on policies, systems, process and controls</li>
<li>Provide feedback to Product Management on customer needs, market opportunities and product roadmaps</li>
<li>Provide feedback on Sales requirements for Marketing Communications â€“ advertising and promotions campaigns, social media and website content, events and trade shows. Work closely with Marketing to ensure generation of sales leads through market research and analysis</li>
</ul>
<p>Key measurables:</p>
<ul>
<li>Achievement of target revenue</li>
<li>Achievement of target margin</li>
<li>Achievement of target market share</li>
<li>Systems, processes, analysis and reporting that meet business needs</li>
<li>On-target delivery of Sales Team KPIs</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills and experience requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in the video monitoring space, technology industry or SaaS beneficial.
</li>
<li>Proven experience in developing senior-level executive relationships with channel partners and end-user customers</li>
<li>Deep (direct and indirect) channel knowledge, development and management experience</li>
<li>Ability to balance appropriate levels of "channel conflict" necessary for the business in realizing profitable market share gains</li>
<li>Prior Private Equity group ownership experience in previous companies</li>
<li>Cross-functional teaming skills, successfully reaches target and business commitments, building credibility in their work</li>
<li>Demonstrated success in developing Customer Experience (CX) strategies and tactics that directly correlated to revenue growth and customer engagement</li>
<li>Has built and sustained a strong sales operations culture using sales a scorecard process and metrics to drive (measurable) continual improvement</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership skills in a national role with remote teams</li>
<li>A natural professional with Board Member interactions, possessing personal command of the sales function and delivers quality-based, meaningful presentations</li>
<li>Credible senior executive presence, presents with authority and confidence at all levels in the business</li>
<li>Engaged and engaging personal style, able to motivate and lead management peers and team members</li>
<li>Energetic and motivated, able to work long hours when required to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Demonstrated resilience in responding to setbacks and overcoming barriers</li>
<li>Significant business acumen demonstrated delivering results whilst managing uncertainty, competition and commercial barriers</li>
<li>Able to travel as required by the business regularly in the US, and internationally to meet business needs (infrequently).</li>
</ul>
