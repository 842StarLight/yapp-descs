<h2>Bridal Shop Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Lovely Bride – Shop Manager Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>Lovely Bride</b> is the OG Indie Bridal shop, who broke the mold of traditional bridal retail in 2010 by being the first to stock cutting-edge, indie designers in a laid-back &amp; chilled out shop environment. We are an inclusive company, that carries exclusive brands.</p>
<p>Helping brides find the one is exhilarating and rewarding, and takes energy, enthusiasm, and maturity. That is why we only hire individuals with both an eye for fashion, passion for customer service and <b>retail sales experience.</b></p>
<p><b>As a company, we stand for: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working hard while having fun</li>
<li>Empowering women and preaching body positivity</li>
<li>Being innovative and creative</li>
<li>Caring about our bride’s experience</li>
<li>Building a strong and loyal team</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute the Lovely Bride standards of customer experience</li>
<li>Help hire, train and manage team of FT + PT stylists</li>
<li>Establish and track individual stylist goals + performance</li>
<li>Meet and exceed monthly shop sales goal</li>
<li>Be an expert on all Lovely Bride product in your shop</li>
<li>Execute Lovely Bride merchandising best practices</li>
<li>Assist corporate team in building the best assortment, discovering up and coming designers and communicating product needs to corporate team</li>
<li>Assist in managing all customer issues with brides and the vendors in tune with Lovely Bride best practices</li>
<li>Sales Event scheduling and management</li>
<li>Become a systems expert in our appointment and order software to assist others in completing sales</li>
<li>Sale report management</li>
<li>Overseas operations, shipping and receiving</li>
<li>Working closely with corporate bookkeeper on invoices and billing</li>
<li>Organize and delegate to keep shop tidy and up to Lovely standards</li>
<li>Communicate your shop happenings with Management via weekly and monthly reporting and meetings</li>
<li>Network with Industry contacts, brides and local creative to drive appointments to your Lovely Bride location</li>
<li>Be a mature, positive representation of our brand to team member, industry and clientele</li>
<li>Contribute to a warm, positive and inspiring company culture</li>
</ul>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or higher</li>
<li>Retail Experience required, Bridal Experience a plus</li>
<li>Weekends required</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and a positive demeanor</li>
<li>Obsessed with customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Knack for team building</li>
<li>Sales Oriented</li>
<li>Eye for style and creative content</li>
<li>Proficient in Word, Excel, Power Point</li>
</ul>
