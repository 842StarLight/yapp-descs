<h2>Store Manager Trainee</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Brown’s Shoe Fit Co is looking for a Store Franchise Owner Trainee to fill positions in various locations in the Midwest. We are looking for honest, hard working, and outgoing people with manager qualities to work in any one of our stores located across 12 Mid-West States. We are expanding and want good people to join our team! Being a trainee gives you the opportunity to train and become a Manager in one of our other locations. <b>Opportunity for Advancement is one of the best parts of Brown's Shoe Fit Company!</b><br>
<br>
<b>Current Retail Locations we are hiring for: Bartlesville, OK</b><br>
</p>
<p>You might be a great fit with Brown's Shoe Fit Co. if you:</p>
<ul>
<li>Are looking for a full-time, "hands-on" business journey.</li>
<li>Have business leadership skills</li>
<li>Have successfully managed your finances.</li>
<li>Are a results-oriented, self-motivated individual who is interested in starting and growing a business.</li>
</ul>
<br>
You might not be a great fit with Brown's Shoe Fit Co. if you:<br>
<ul>
<li>Are looking for an easy, passive investment.</li>
<li>Are not a self-starter</li>
<li>Are not a people person</li>
</ul>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Create a business plan and strategy for operations</li>
<li>Strategize sales initiatives for high performance</li>
<li>Oversee all operations of the business from start to finish</li>
<li>Engage proactively with customers and prospects to increase sales</li>
<li>Increase brand awareness through advertising and marketing</li>
<li>Manage customer relations to improve return customers</li>
<li>Screen and interview candidates for job openings</li>
</ul>Qualifications
<ul>
<li>Proven practical experience in the same business area of operations</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of business management and operations</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and ability to build rapport</li>
<li>Good negotiation and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial mindset with a strong business acumen</li>
<li>Highly driven and self-motivated for success</li>
<li>Able to work efficiently and effectively with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Ability to work long hours at a time sitting and standing</li>
<li>Complete a drug screening and background check</li>
<li>Possess a driver's license</li>
</ul>
<br>
Our shoe store franchise needs articulate and knowledgeable individuals to take over the role of Assistant Store Manager. You’ll contribute to the high-energy atmosphere in the store, order merchandise and assist the Manager with any additional tasks that vary from day to day. We promote from within. Potential career tracks could include Store Franchise ownership after training as an Assistant Manager.<br>
<br>
Why should you work for Brown’s? We care for our employees and customers. We have been around for over 108 years. We are in the expansion process, which means more internal mobility for more qualified candidates and assistant managers. We actually have people retire with us, which means more opportunities for growth every year!</div>
