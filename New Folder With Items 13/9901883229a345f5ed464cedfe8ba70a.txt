<h2>DESIGNER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>FIRE PROTECTION DESIGNER/ESTIMATOR</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Firetrol Protection Systems</p>
<p>
Founded in 1984, Firetrol is the national leader in fire protection &amp; life safety services.
</p>
<p>Firetrol is looking for a motivated individual interested in an advanced level designer/estimator position with 3-5 years job experience. The preferred candidate would have extensive experience or knowledge with the activities listed below:
 </p>
<p>Designer Activities</p>
<ul>
<li>Reads and interprets construction documents that includes drawings, specifications and contracts and actively participates with pre-planning the overall fire protection system design approach for the following types of systems:<br>
<br>
</li>
<li>Water based fire suppression and fire extinguishing systems.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Special hazard suppression systems.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Fire alarm and mass notification systems.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Designs and lays out fire protection systems for complex buildings utilizing a CAD based software. Adheres to NFPA, FM Global, local and other national codes as appropriate.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates with other trades. Field surveys assigned project when needed.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Works with project managers and/or foreman to develop field prints and fabrication details. Supports the team with technical information as needed.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Participate in project meetings with clients and other professionals.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Participates in the BIM/CAD environment and maintains all related fire protection supporting documents.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to manage multiple design projects simultaneously depending on complexity of scope.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Perform peer reviews of systems designs and specifications.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Estimating Activities</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Examine project documents to determine bidding projects necessary systems.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide device quantities required to meet current code and bid documents.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Analyze job specification to decide the proper equipment needed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Research current adopted codes and become familiar with NFPA 72, NFPA 13, IBC, IFC, Life Safety, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support sales team members</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
