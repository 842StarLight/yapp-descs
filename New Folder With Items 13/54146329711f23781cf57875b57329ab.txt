<h2>Senior Mortgage Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>Your career search stops here! The Federal Savings Bank (“TFSB”) is a veteran-owned national bank with a focus on residential lending. Our mission is to de-mystify the mortgage process for our borrowers and help them finance their dream home. Recently named as one of Inc. 5000’s fastest growing private companies in America for two consecutive years, TFSB is actively searching for an experienced loan processing professional to assist with the timely processing of residential mortgage loans for our new<b> Frisco, TX loan production office</b></p>
<p>
At TFSB, this senior loan processing role is known as the "Underwriting Analyst". The Underwriting Analyst’s primary goal is to have a first-time approval from Underwriting with minimal conditions. It is the responsibility of the Underwriting Analyst to review, analyze, and submit loan documents to Underwriting based on agency, investor, and company guidelines with a focus on efficiency and quality.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Daily utilization of "Encompass" loan origination systems and automated underwriting systems "DU/LP"</li>
<li>
Communicate with Borrowers, Junior Processing Analysts, and third parties to obtain required loan documentation</li>
<li>
Analyze package for completeness to ensure loan file meets investor guidelines</li>
<li>
Calculate income for qualifying, including self-employed income analysis if needed</li>
<li>
Analyze credit, income, asset and collateral documents and submit files to underwriting</li>
<li>
Clear loan conditions and communicate effectively with Sales, Processing and Underwriting team</li>
<li>
Analyze Underwriting approval</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Closing Processing Analysts to review &amp; analyze title for all requirements and address issues, insurance coverage for mortgagee</li>
<li>
Set and maintain proper expectations with all parties on the loan file</li>
<li>
Effectively manage loan pipeline at various volume levels</li>
<li>
Keep abreast of all applicable State/Federal regulations to maintain loan file compliance (TFSB offers certification courses on specific loan products to aid in this effort)</li>
<li>
Assist in the seamless transition of new Mortgage Bankers and operations employees to our model</li>
<li>
Work effectively under specific time constraints and meet end-of-month deadlines</li>
<li>
Work independently and as a team</li>
<li>
Operate in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Complete TFSB customer service (S.E.E.) training and apply to everyday communications with borrowers and mortgage bankers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2 year college degree or commensurate industry experience</li>
<li>
3 + years recent Residential Mortgage Processing experience</li>
<li>
Recent Correspondent Lending experience strongly preferred</li>
<li>
Knowledge of agency &amp; investor loan programs and underwriting criteria for government and conventional offerings</li>
<li>
Experience managing a pipeline of 30+ Purchase and Refinance loans</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Encompass loan origination software preferred</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Automated Underwriting Systems; DU/LP</li>
<li>
Detail-Oriented &amp; Solution-Oriented Individual; An effective problem-solver</li>
<li>
Excellent Interpersonal, Communication, and Time Management Skills</li>
<li>
Ability to operate effectively in high-pressure situations</li>
<li>
Ability to calculate income, including self-employed</li>
<li>
Ability to work well in a team environment</li>
<li>
All applicants must be flexible with their schedules, as they may be required to work after-hours, especially during peak volume periods</li>
<li>
Bilingual or Multilingual professionals always a plus!</li>
</ul>
