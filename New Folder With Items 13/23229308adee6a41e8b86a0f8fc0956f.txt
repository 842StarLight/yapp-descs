<p><b>Solution Engineer - Healthcare</b></p>
<p>
DataBank is a growing, thriving company full of smart, motivated people. Our 650+ employees are a tightly-knit, super-focused and incredibly dedicated team. We work hard, and we're passionate about our business and customers. One of our primary goals is to provide a challenging, exciting, fair, rewarding, and growth-oriented environment for our team.</p>
<p>
At DataBank, we are on a mission to help organizations simplify their business processes. Our work is constantly changing and evolving, driven by the need to create remarkable business solutions for our customers. As North America's leading end-to-end business process solution provider, our employees are the heart of our success. Our people are committed to our customers in everything that we do. That is the DataBank difference.</p>
<p>
In our newly created position, as a <b>Solution Engineer</b>, working on a team within our <b>Healthcare</b> practice, you will be responsible for providing customers with valuable content services and management solutions that simplify their business processes. Your primary responsibilities will include solution consulting, designing, training, installing, developing, and supporting client solutions. Other responsibilities may also include pre-sales and post-sales consulting and other position-related responsibilities as defined below.</p>
<p>
Job Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Delivers efficient solutions using applicable software and technologies</li>
<li>Determines solution requirements with client by analyzing data usage, reviewing existing work processes and identifying improvement needs and system requirements</li>
<li>Assists with design and documentation prerequisites using flowcharts, diagrams, templates and user manuals</li>
<li>Participating in internal team meetings and client status calls</li>
<li>Installing software and assisting with infrastructure needs</li>
<li>Executes implementation efforts including development in multiple environments both onsite and remotely</li>
<li>Performs functional testing in controlled environment and assists client in acceptance testing utilizing business process test case scenarios</li>
<li>Provides initial support of solution after deployment and production migration</li>
<li>Promotes enhancement of existing processes and solutions</li>
<li>Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities, reading professional publications and maintaining professional networks</li>
<li>Assists in the sales process with technical solution consulting, demonstrations and support</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<p>Position qualifications and desired requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong problem solving skills</li>
<li>Excellent written, listening and verbal communication skills including interpersonal negotiation and conflict resolution abilities</li>
<li>Strong software and information technology knowledge base</li>
<li>Professional appearance</li>
<li>Strong analysis and judgement skills</li>
<li>Works well in a team and collaborative environment</li>
<li>Strong telephone presence and consulting skills</li>
</ul>
<p>
Education, Training, and Experience Desired:</p>
<ul>
<li>5 - 10 years of OnBase experience and certifications in OnBase software preferred</li>
<li>A minimum of 2 years of installation and support of content and services solutions.</li>
<li>A minimum of 2 years experience in the Enterprise Content Management (ECM) sector.</li>
<li>A minimum of 2 years experience working in a hospital/healthcare setting or healthcare vendor-related</li>
</ul>
<p>
Travel:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 20% Travel will be expected.</li>
</ul>
