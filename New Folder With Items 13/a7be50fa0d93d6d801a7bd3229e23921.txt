<h2>Senior Category Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>SUMMARY</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Meet category and overall company sales and profitability by providing field sales, management, brokers and customers with sales and marketing analysis and tools.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in sales and profitability through analysis of performance data.</li>
<li>
Analyzes and assesses category/brand performance to develop enhanced marketing and sales plans and promotions.</li>
<li>
Analyze data, make fact-based recommendations and track performance of sales and marketing plans and objectives.</li>
<li>
Utilize syndicated data, consumer data, internal data and shopper data to uncover opportunities and provide analytical recommendations.</li>
<li>
Prepare and analyze standard shipment, retail sales and panel data reports, including charts and graphs, by providing summary and detailed analysis of key strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats.</li>
<li>
Lead discussions with staff members, providing analytic results and recommendations for improved performance.</li>
<li>
Assist in developing business presentations and sales proposals.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with field sales to develop selling stories to effectively communicate brand/product performance to their customers.</li>
<li>
Assist in pre and post analysis, and provide recommendation on promotional activities and sales events.</li>
<li>
Assist in development of planograms and make shelving recommendations
</li>
<li>Perform regular, weekly, monthly, quarterly and ad hoc reporting/analysis</li>
<li>
Ensure that the retail feedback considered by attending customer appointments as needed.</li>
<li>
Participate in training and other learning opportunities for data sources/structures, analytical tools/software packages, and business functions.</li>
<li>
Other duties may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
