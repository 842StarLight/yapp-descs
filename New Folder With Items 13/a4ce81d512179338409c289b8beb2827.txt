<p><b>Job Description Summary:</b></p>
Responsible for leading market insights, strategy and commercial execution for assigned therapy line in order to improve the lives of patients and within post-acute care settings.<br>
<br>
A. Market Expertise<br>
o Collaborate across functions (primarily with clinical operations, sales and market access) to lead organizational understanding of infusion therapy for diseases and therapies. Gather information from multiple internal and external sources to create clear picture of the market, including therapy options today/future, provider specialties, site of care dynamics, payer dynamics and the infusion competitive landscape.<br>
<br>
B. Therapy Strategy<br>
o Partner with our customer facing teams along with marketing colleagues to develop a concise strategy that allows Option Care Health to meet our clinical and financial goals in each therapeutic area. Lead annual category strategy that includes customer/market segmentation, commercial goals, marketing objectives and associated KPIs and metrics.<br>
<br>
C. Commercial Execution<br>
o In coordination with marketing peers, create customer solutions and marketing campaigns that are relevant and delight our customers. Evaluate and consider mediums for deployment, including field campaigns, digital, print. Partner with sales operations to guide customer targeting and field training, as well as to report related field metrics.<p><b>
