Responsibilities* Oversee the parts sales process from start to finish.
<br><ul>
<li>Communicate with staff and customers in a friendly and professional manner.</li>
<li>Converse effectively/efficiently regarding customer parts status.</li>
<li>Read and understand the parts catalog when looking up and speaking to specific parts.</li>
<li>Answer phone calls, provide price quotes and other information to customers.</li>
<li>Review body shop estimates to verify the parts ordered are correct and all pricing is in line with the estimate.</li>
<li>Provide high level service to both internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Pull/fill orders from stock and maintain weekly bin checks to ensure accuracy of stock.</li>
<li>Notify parts manager of out-of-stock parts or shop materials that need immediate attention.</li>
<li>Locate out-of-stock parts from outside source and submit an emergency order, if necessary.</li>
<li>Make sure all internal requests for parts are billed on service repair order.</li>
<li>Receive payment from retail customers or obtains credit authorization.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications* High School Diploma or equivalent and prior auto parts sales experience (preferred)
<br><ul>
<li>Eager to improve in a dynamic work environment</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and time management skills</li>
<li>Ability to read/interpret safety and maintenance documents</li>
<li>Ability to calculate figures/amounts including; discounts, interest, commissions, proportions, %, area, circumference, and volume</li>
<li>Valid driver's license and clean driving record</li>
</ul>
