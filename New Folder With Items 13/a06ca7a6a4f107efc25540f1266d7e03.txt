<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>GoldenHome is one of the largest modern kitchen cabinetry groups in Asia. We manufacture higher quality Kitchen Cabinets, Vanity Suites and Closet Systems. We are expert in a variety of finishes including Matte and High Gloss Lacquers, Laminates, Melamine, and UV, etc.</p>
<p>GoldenHome has experienced continuous strong growth every year since founded in 1999. Sales Revenue in the Chinese domestic market alone is over $200 million annually. We have several manufacturing facilities globally, and we are currently building our newest facility here in the USA. We now produce and ship over 7,000 cabinets per day.</p>
<p>During the past 3 years GoldenHome has begun focusing on the US and Canadian frameless Cabinetry markets. We have set up 10 Regional Distribution Centers across the US and in Canada. We are continuing to expand our footprint across the US to insure coverage across all of North America.</p>
<p>Our goal is to become one of the largest, most respected higher quality frameless cabinetry brands in the US and Canadian markets and deliver better quality product solutions with higher levels of Customer Service. Additional information about GoldenHome Cabinetry can be found on our website at www.GoldenHomeKitchen.com and www.GoldenHomeCabinets.com .</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<p>GoldenHome International is seeking a Product and Technology Specialist to support our growth effort from both a Product Management perspective and an IT systems implementation perspective at our corporate office in Somerset, NJ. This is a new position created due to major business growth and expansion. We expect travel to be in the 10% to 20% range.</p>
<p>The successful candidate will report directly to the Product and Technology Manager. This is a highly visibility position with great opportunity for growth.</p>
<p>On the IT side, responsibilities will include implementation of our WMS, CRM, ERP, and Order Management Systems, as well as implementation and ongoing support for our server and phone system.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<p>1. Minimum requirements include at least 1 years’ experience in IT systems implementation with knowledge.</p>
<p>2. A Bachelor’s degree, or Associates degree on Computer Science or Engineering design.</p>
<p>3. Experience implementing, managing, and or maintaining any wholesale or retail ERP, WMS, and CRM system is highly desirable.</p>
<p>4. Must be proficient with Microsoft Office Suite, including Access.</p>
<p>5. Familiar with Sql server/Mysql/Oracle is a plus.</p>
<p>6. Working knowledge of Mandarin is necessary.</p>
