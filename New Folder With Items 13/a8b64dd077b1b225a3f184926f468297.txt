<p>Reporting to the Customer Support Manager, the <b>Technical Engineer (Engineering Support Group) </b>will provide support to clients for all existing and future Payment product
</p>
<p>offerings provided by Finastra.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities &amp; Deliverables:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Your deliverables as a <b>Technical Engineer (Engineering Support Group) </b>will include, but not limited, to the following:
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Provide professional application and technical support &amp; consultation to clients of Finastra
</li></ul>
<p>Payment products, typically banks or major corporations
</p>
<ul><li>Provide tier 2 support: act as initial escalation point for Operations &amp; Application Support
</li></ul>
<p>staff and the customer; provide guidance and training for support staff of all levels; execute
</p>
<p>escalation procedures when applicable, as defined by management
</p>
<ul><li>Work collaboratively with customers’ and Finastra technical groups for connectivity to various
</li></ul>
<p>interfaces/applications such as the Federal Reserve Bank, Depository Financial Institutions,
</p>
<p>and other third party vendors
</p>
<ul><li>Continually monitor and update all assigned cases in Salesforce CRM with timeliness,
</li></ul>
<p>accuracy, relevant information and using appropriate customer-facing communication as
</p>
<p>judged by management. Cases are accessible internally and by Finastra clients.
</p>
<ul><li>Lead installation, implementation and client coordination of testing fixes provided by
</li></ul>
<p>Development for Production related issues; document work following change control
</p>
<p>protocol
</p>
<ul><li>Create and maintain documentation pertaining to daily operations of the Payments Customer
</li></ul>
<p>Support Group
</p>
<ul><li>Install and Maintain Test Environments for all Payment Products for support of the Payments
</li></ul>
<p>Group
</p>
<ul><li>Participate in and sometimes lead infrastructure changes that relate to Payment products
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain and expand support expertise by learning, as necessary, International and Domestic
</li></ul>
<p>Payments Products and the software applications Finastra sells
</p>
<ul><li>Perform all other duties as assigned
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Experience:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree (concentration in Computer Science or Computer Engineering preferred)
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with support of technical services and must have the ability to quickly learn the
</li></ul>
<p>technical aspects needed to support the products
</p>
<ul><li>Prior experience in supporting customers of banking or financial software applications
</li></ul>
<p>preferred
</p>
<ul><li>Must have good verbal/phone presence - experience in call center or customer support is a
</li></ul>
<p>plus
</p>
<ul><li>Ability to multi-task and manage changing priorities to meet demands of clients
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated ability to contribute to an atmosphere of teamwork through collaborative
</li></ul>
<p>behavior and open communications
</p>
<ul><li>Experience interfacing with others via phone and email at all levels from senior management
</li></ul>
<p>to junior staff
</p>
<ul><li>Excellent analytical and communication (Verbal and written) skills; process-oriented
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Successful candidate must enjoy problem solving, which is a significant part of the job
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Expert technical skills in any of the following are desired: Windows Server administration &amp;
</li></ul>
<p>support; networking concepts, analysis &amp; troubleshooting; web server technologies such as
</p>
<p>IIS, Apache/Tomcat; remote access; virtualization; relational databases; Windows-centric
</p>
<p>system and application support
</p>
<p></p>

</b></p>

</b></p>
<ul><li>Willingness to work off hours and occasional long hours when necessary to achieve customer
</li></ul>
<p>and company objectives

<ul><li>Work independently and collaboratively
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work remotely to provide support, as required
