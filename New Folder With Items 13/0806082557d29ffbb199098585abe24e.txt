</p></li>
<li><p>Holman Insurance Services, a commercial and consumer insurance services company
</p></li>
<li><p>Holman Parts Distribution, a national multi-brand powertrain parts distributor;
</p></li>
<li><p>Auto Truck Group, a truck up-fitting business; and
</p></li>
<li><p>ARI, the largest privately-owned fleet leasing and management company in the world

<div>
<b>Holman Honda </b>has an outstanding opportunity for a <b>Service Advisor </b>to join the Holman family.

<p>What will you do?
</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare service estimates, sell routine maintenance/repair services and conduct follow-ups regarding unsold services and customer inquires
</li>
<li>Perform opening and closing responsibilities
</li>
<li>Schedule appointments, answer phones and collect funds for services performed
</li>
<li>Predict the time needed for completion and stay in constant communication with the customer during the repair process
</li>
<li>Assist with the coordination of alternate transportation, rental car reservations, shuttle etc.
</li>
<li>Sell needed maintenance services and related parts to customers
</li>
<li>Identify customer requests
</li>
<li>Conduct a complete vehicle walk around
</li>
<li>Present multipoint inspection results to customers
</li>
<li>Recommend needed maintenance services and related parts
</li>
<li>Conduct a detailed active delivery of each vehicle
</li>
<li>Make appropriate prospecting and follow-up contacts with Honda owners
</li>
<li>Provide outstanding customer service in accordance with dealership policies
</li>
<li>Write complete, accurate repair orders in accordance with dealership and manufacturer policies
</li>
<li>Provide estimated cost analysis of automotive repairs and routine maintenance.
</li>
<li>Provide in-depth information on original equipment manufacturer (OEM) part options
</li>
<li>Perform all other duties and special projects as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
