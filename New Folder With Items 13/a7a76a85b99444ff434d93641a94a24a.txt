Job Summary
<br>The Economic Growth Institute (EGI), is a unit within the University of Michigan Office of Research (UMOR) with a direct report to the Office of the Vice President for Government Relations. EGI empowers businesses, communities, inventors, innovators, and state and federal governments with economic and market insights to better engage and contribute to job creation, job retention, and the improvement of local, state, and federal economies. EGI is a unit within the Office of the Vice President for Government Relations and is supported by UM Office of Research.
<br><br>
EGI is seeking a Project Intermediate Manager for its Great Lakes Trade Adjustment Assistance Center (GLTAAC or Great Lakes TAAC). GLTAAC runs a federal program (Trade Adjustment Assistance for Firms, or TAAF) that helps small, distressed manufacturing firms which have been negatively impacted by imports to develop and implement business recovery plans. GLTAAC's service area is Michigan, Ohio, and Indiana.
<br><br>
This position will focus on direct outreach to potential client companies to inform them about the TAAF program and assist them with the application process. It will work closely with GLTAAC's Senior Project Manager of Sales &amp; Marketing. This is a full-time, term-limited position, which will end on April 30, 2021
<br><br>
