<h2>SALESFORCE.COM CONSULTANT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Role</b></h2>
<p>Penrod, located in the Oak Lawn neighborhood of Dallas and nationally in Milwaukee, Minneapolis, and Chicago is seeking Dallas based Salesforce.com Administrator to join our growing team. Our Salesforce Consultant will work with a team to identify, design, and execute Salesforce solutions for client application. Willingness and ability to travel up to 50% is a must. The Salesforce Administrator Certification will be required within the first eight weeks of employment.</p>
<p>
Our projects include working with advanced business process and business intelligence systems, providing solutions to ensure user adoption, and development of custom applications that enrich the user experience. We are committed to engagement and strive to “Engage More” in every aspect of our business and company culture.</p>
<ul><li>Relocation package available for the right candidate.</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h2>
<p><b>What You Must Do To Be Successful In This Position:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain validation rules, custom workflows and process, lead/opportunity pipeline management, service request and bug and feature requests, test and QA in sandbox and be the master of clean data.</li>
<li>Develop reporting and dashboard functions as well as business intelligence analytics.</li>
<li>Train/support end users with respect to their use of the Salesforce application.</li>
<li>Perform detailed analysis of business and technical requirements, in close collaboration with Product Owners and client stakeholders.</li>
<li>Participate in, and some cases lead, CRM technical architecture discussions and manage/educate stakeholders. Manage the development lifecycle to ensure the delivery of highly secure solutions that are optimized for performance and built to scale.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What Skills &amp; Experience We’d Like You To Have:</b></p>
<p>
Minimum qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree or equivalent practical experience.</li>
<li>3+ years of Salesforce.com experience.</li>
<li>Experience working on operational projects for sales, channel, marketing, and/or support organizations.</li>
<li>Superior time management, prioritization and communication skills.</li>
<li>A wide degree of flexibility and latitude is expected</li>
<li>Extremely detail oriented</li>
<li>Ability to handle stress and last-minute, urgent requests</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Salesforce Certified (Administrator and additional certification(s) preferred)</li>
<li>Experience in enterprise software</li>
<li>Strategic planning experience</li>
<li>Strong analytical, problem-solving, and organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to travel up to 50%</li>
</ul>
