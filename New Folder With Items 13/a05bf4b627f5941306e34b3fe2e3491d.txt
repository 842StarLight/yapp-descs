<h2>HVAC Project Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>C&amp;L Refrigeration is recognized as a leader in service, construction and engineering excellence &amp; innovation. We provide leading edge service, construction, installation, design and engineering services to a diverse customer base.</p>
<p>A profitable and stable service, maintenance, construction and engineering air conditioning and refrigeration firm with corporate headquarters located in Fullerton, CA is seeking a candidate for the new position of <b>HVAC Project Engineer</b>. We are a privately owned company working in CA, NV, AZ, UT, NM, TX. OR and WA. The company has over 40 years history of positive growth with a reputation for servicing, designing and building quality projects on time and under budget. We offer a competitive salary with medical, dental and matching 401K plan.</p>
<p><b>Scope of Work: </b> Provide primary project engineering and design support for heating, ventilating, air conditioning capital construction projects based on customer’s HVAC needs.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Primary duties include selection of equipment, duct and pipe sizing and routing, detail design engineering of commercial and industrial HVAC system involving heat load calculation, and preparation of drawing and specifications.</li>
<li>Able to perform HVAC heat load analysis using software.</li>
<li>Knowledge of California Title 24 Energy Codes.</li>
<li>Able to use <b>Energy Pro Software </b>a must.</li>
<li>Familiar with HVAC equipment and parts.</li>
<li>Experience with preparing mechanical drawings for permit and responding to plan check corrections.</li>
<li>Answer Project Request For Informations (RFIs) as needed.</li>
<li>Knowledge of building, mechanical, and plumbing codes for commercial HVAC installs.</li>
<li>Prior experience with HVAC estimating to help estimate HVAC projects a plus.</li>
<li>Able to perform job walks to gather field information for HVAC design.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot HVAC design problems if needed.</li>
<li>Oversee independent contractor’s execution, working with principals and sales engineers.</li>
<li>Interact directly with customer base in territory (consulting engineers, owners, architects, contractors).</li>
<li>Assist with accomplishing department goals as needed (review of new technologies, and engineering).</li>
<li>Provide product, application, pricing, and sales support as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years HVAC industry experience.</li>
<li>Engineering degree preferred.</li>
<li>HVAC design and calculation knowledge with familiarity with building and mechanical codes and ASHRAE standards.</li>
</ul>
