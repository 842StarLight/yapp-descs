<h2>Salesforce Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>EverCommerce—a company comprised of more than 35 technology subsidiaries—is looking for a Salesforce Administrator to join our Growth Engagement (GE) team. You will play a key role in consulting with subsidiaries on operational initiatives and helping them run as efficiently as possible.</p>
<p><b>
Us:</b><br>
EverCommerce is bringing together best-of-breed commerce solutions in the service sector, forming one service commerce platform that improves go-to-market strategy for technology companies and simplifies the software-buying process for business owners.</p>
<p>
Our team has extensive expertise in business operations, management, legal, finance, accounting, technology human resources, marketing and sales that, via the EverCommerce platform, we provide as a service to drive continued growth and success for service commerce companies. Our ecosystem enables companies to thrive in an environment of shared knowledge and resources with significant cost savings.</p>
<p><b>
You:</b><br>
You are an experienced <b>platform administrator</b> who has a strong background in <b>project management</b>. You are passionate about collaborating with senior business leaders on building and implementing updated processes and platforms. You have a knack for developing and monitoring key performance indicators, within a B2B SaaS operation, that produce actionable insights for functional owners. You’re a skilled and organized communicator who has no problem driving long term projects through to completion.</p>
<p><b>
Where:</b><br>
EverCommerce is located in the LoDo area of Denver. Our collaborative, open-plan office space is filled with high-energy, talented people who enjoy our weekly lunches, bubble hockey, and hanging out with their co-workers.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support the Growth Engagement team in assessing operational gaps and opportunities within individual subsidiaries and assist in building out long-term growth strategies.</li>
<li>Create and Maintain an operational project roadmap for each of your assigned EverCommerce companies.</li>
<li>Partner with EverCommerce General Management, Subsidiary Leadership and Subsidiary Functional Owners (Sales, Marketing, Customers Success, Service, etc.) as an end-to-end strategic project manager to execute key business and platform initiatives.</li>
<li>Develop best practice playbooks and frameworks around the scientific approach to improving a B2B SaaS sales and marketing funnel that can be repeatedly deployed to expand the reach of your influence over subsidiary processes.</li>
<li>Prioritize and track initiatives and their financial effects across the portfolio.</li>
<li>Communicate findings and operational results to leadership and to the broader organization by preparing and delivering presentations.</li>
<li>Provide administrative support and development bandwidth across varying platforms in the following categories:</li>
<li>CRM’s (Salesforce, Zoho, HubSpot, etc.)</li>
<li>Telephony solutions (Talkdesk, Ring Central, native CRM voice solutions, etc.)</li>
<li>Support Platforms (Salesforce, Zendesk, Zoho Desk, Freshdesk, etc.)</li>
<li>Business Intelligence Platforms (Looker, Salesforce Wave, homegrown solutions, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of project management, Salesforce.com administration, or other related work experience</li>
<li>Ability to advise and influence senior business leaders across organizational lines.</li>
<li>Comfortable in a fast-paced environment and capable of meeting short-deadline projects daily</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail and willingness to pitch in when necessary</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Management, Analytics, Operations, Economics, or related field</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits &amp; Perks:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive salary</li>
<li>Robust medical benefits &amp; 401(k)</li>
<li>Public transportation reimbursement</li>
<li>Gym stipend</li>
<li>Start with 17 vacation days plus your birthday off</li>
<li>Snacks, lunches, and happy hours</li>
</ul>
<p>We are building an exceptional company and looking for talented, energetic, and motivated people to join our unique environment located in Denver's LoDo district. If you are looking for a company that is truly focused on empowering small businesses with superior technology, come join a company that rewards authenticity and supports energy with a passion.</p>
