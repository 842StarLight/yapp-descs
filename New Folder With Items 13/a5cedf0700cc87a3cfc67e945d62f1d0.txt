<h2>Route Operations Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Company Summary: Origlio Beverage, a family company that dominates the beer industry in the 9 counties of Southeastern, PA. We are a 4th generation family owned employer, founded in 1933. We have the luxury of delivering the great taste of excellence.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Essential Functions: The Route Optimization Associate is responsible for ensuring the efficient delivery of stops and cases to customers within assigned delivery windows while following all DOT hours of service rules.</p>
<ul>
<li>Use the routing software (Roadnet) to schedule daily deliveries and pickups that meet service, efficiency and DOT standards.</li>
<li>Keep management informed of any issues surrounding Roadnet software.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Communicate when necessary with the technical support department of Roadnet.</li>
<li>Maintain Roadnet data, including geo-code map, account master data, set up new account, and other tasks to improve efficiency of routes.</li>
<li>Communicate with sales regarding delivery day changes, off day deliveries, and special customer needs.</li>
<li>Use warehouse management software (Eo Star) to virtually palletize routes for order fulfillment.</li>
<li>Review scheduling and manpower requirements as needed.</li>
<li>Provide detailed daily reports and communicate effectively to all team members in the department</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Knowledge and Competencies:</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent analytical skills are a must, including measuring and monitoring key performance metrics, maximizing efficiencies and customer service levels.</li>
<li>A flexible team player with the ability to plan, prioritize &amp; problem solve.</li>
<li>Experience within a transportation/logistics environment.</li>
<li>Familiarity with eastern Pennsylvania roadways and geography preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to be part of a cohesive team flexing to adapt in a rapidly changing environment.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Representative Machines, Tools, Equipment And/or Software Used:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience working with Routing and Transportation Management Software (i.e. Roadnet, Eo Star) is a plus.</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office, including Excel.</li>
<br>
</ul>
