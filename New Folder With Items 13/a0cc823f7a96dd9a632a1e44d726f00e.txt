<h2>Solutions Specialist Security</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>In this role, you are considered a security SME while consulting and selling complex managed security solutions and services to "C" level executives. You must have a level of business acumen to develop, lead, and close sales opportunities with our largest enterprise accounts.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Hunting within a set of accounts to identify opportunities to grow the accounts by adding Verizon security products and services.</li>
<li>
Identifying opportunities, positioning, and selling the full range of Verizon Security Services (Managed Security Services, Third Party SaaS, and Professional Security/Consulting Services).</li>
<li>
Partner to identify, develop, and close additional opportunities with existing clients and work with sales team to close the opportunities.</li>
<li>
Identify all the key technical influencers and present the Verizon value proposition to executive level clients.</li>
</ul>

Even better if you have:</p>
