 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>For Earth For Life
</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<b><i>BASIC PURPOSE AND SCOPE OF POSITION:</i></b><br>
</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>Safeguards Kubota’s assets and assesses risk exposure using financial and other reporting analytics.Collects outstanding remittances and consults on business development issues.<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<div>
<b><i>PRINCIPAL ACTIVITIES</i></b><b><i>: </i></b><b><i>This </i></b><b>position does the following in accordance with all applicable Federal, State and local laws / regulations and the Company’s policies, procedures and guidelines:</b><br>
</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li><div>Performs physical inventory auditing at the direction of the Territory Credit Manager.
</div></li>
<li><div>Completes timely collection of floorplan outstanding equipment, curtailment, etc.
</div></li>
<li><div>Assists in the preparation and submission of dealership watch list.
</div></li>
<li><div>Proactively provides data, information and communication to effectively predict dealership financial status.
</div></li>
<li><div>Ensures aged, off-inventory units are registered (R&amp;I) in accordance with Kubota’s aged-unit policies.
</div></li>
<li><div>Prepares dealership risk assessment based on financial statement data, audit and sales results.
</div></li>
<li><div>Ensures timely collection of dealership financial information
</div></li>
<li><div>Provides dealer training on company inventory programs and inventory accounting issues.
</div></li>
<li><div>Supplies historical and pro-forma financial analysis to determine dealership eligibility regarding potential new dealership points, addition of Kubota product lines, additional financial products, etc.
</div></li>
<li><div>Collects general dealership feedback from time-to-time regarding dealership financial-related training opportunities, Kubota inventory program effectiveness, etc.
</div></li>
<li><div>Resolves issues and promotes positive relationships within the assigned territory.<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
