About the Role
<br><br>
We are looking for a high energy content leader to join Qualia's growing Marketing Team and build a high quality content development powerhouse. You will focus on providing value to prospects and users through the development of educational and sales enablement content.
<br><br>
Importantly, you will be the key content partner to our Demand Gen and Sales Enablement teams, developing creative assets that help explain to our audience not only what we do and how we help them, but also be the creative backbone for their campaigns to drive meaningful engagement.
<br><br>

<br><br>

<li>Oversee and optimize Qualia's Company Blog, product newsletter, and other owned content</li>

</ul>
