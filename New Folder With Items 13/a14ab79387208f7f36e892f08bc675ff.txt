<h2>Sales Reporting Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>As Coloplast continues to grow our North America region, we are investing in our business intelligence team in order to deliver analytical insights to the organization. As part of this team, you will play a key role in providing decision makers with essential tools to drive business results.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are a fast-growing global company with an innovative culture. Curiosity is in our DNA. We seek ambitious people who want to take part in our mission to make life easier for people with intimate healthcare needs.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Function:
</b><br>
In this role, you will be responsible for preparing monthly operational and performance reports for use across the organization. You will also play a key role in identifying issues with customer sales data, making recommendations to resolve discrepancies and collaborating with internal and external stakeholders to execute on the recommendations.
<br>
<br>
To be qualified for this role, you must be able to synthesize large volumes of information and translate it into an easy-to-understand format, possesses stakeholder and project management skills, flexibility, and natural curiosity to understand how to continuously drive value through optimized reporting and streamlined processes.
<br>
<br>
<b>Major Areas of Responsibility:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute standard and ad-hoc reports for Sales, Marketing, Commercial Excellence and North America Leadership teams around product, customer and general business performance
</li>
<li>Execute on quarterly and monthly KPI performance reporting for North America and corporate leadership
</li>
<li>Provide insights on data issues through ad-hoc analyses and recommendations for process improvements
</li>
<li>Partner with global and local IT teams to develop and test reports, tools, and system enhancements
</li>
<li>Assist with sales data processing to gain a firm understanding of data and system flows
</li>
<li>Adhere to master data management policies and audit compliance within ERP and CRM systems
</li>
<li>Provide analytical support in ad-hoc and projects
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Essential Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor degree or MBA in Business or other relevant field
</li>
<li>Demonstrated strong proficiency and accuracy in data analysis
</li>
<li>Experience working with advanced analytics
</li>
<li>Proven track record with consistent high performance in data analysis and financial/sales presentations
</li>
<li>Experience with Cognos, ReportNet, Enterprise One / One World, PowerPlay and data warehouse structures
</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office platform products (Excel, Access, Outlook, PowerPoint, Word)
</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and complete tasks efficiently and independently with a high degree of accuracy to meet deadlines
</li>
<li>Strong analytical, investigative and documentation skills
</li>
<li>Strong stakeholder management skills and ability to work with all levels of the organization
</li>
<li>Self-motivated and high energy personality thriving in an entrepreneurial, fast-paced environment characterized by growth, change, and opportunity<br>
</li>
</ul>
