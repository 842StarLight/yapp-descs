<h2>Treasury Fulfillment & Service Sr Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Position located on-site in Brea, CA</p>
<p><b>
Line of Business Description:</b> The role will support the Treasury Shared Services team within Treasury Fulfillment and Servicing. Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Handling funds disbursement requests received directly from client, and/or client team.</li>
<li>
Partnering with internal business partners to obtain the necessary information to complete Inline Quality review for financial transactions.</li>
<li>
Completing quality reviews on high risk transactions such as Cashier's Checks, Internal Bank Transfers, Foreign Drafts, Pay downs and Loan Advances.</li>
<li>
Reviewing legal documents to determine if adequate documentation has been provided to meet test and controls risk requirements.</li>
<li>
Conducting outbound calls to companies to ensure authenticity of the request.</li>
<li>
Working with client team and sales partners to obtain missing information preventing the quality review from moving forward.</li>
<li>
Utilizing various bank systems to complete quality review requests.</li>
<li>
Working with leadership to complete workflow responsibilities.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
Enterprise Description/Role Overview:</i></b><b><i> </i></b><i>First point of contact for inquiries received from high revenue clients via email or phone channel. Responsible for reading, analyzing and/or determining appropriate handling and resolution of requests. Requests consist of treasury, cash management, card and/or depository products. May work with internal business partners to provide resolution to client inquiries, issues, and/or technical support.
</i></p>
<p><i>Key Responsibilities - Receive and respond to requests received by email or phone; Provides resolution for requests received via phone or email; Interacts continuously and deepens relationships with internal business partners as well as clients to further enhance client delight; Conduct limited research and respond to client and be able answer questions and inquiries; Utilize the various bank systems to support clients requests.</i></p>
