<div>Waste Management (WM), a Fortune 250 company, is the leading provider of comprehensive waste and environmental services in North America. We are strongly committed to a foundation of operating excellence, professionalism and financial strength. WM serves nearly 25 million customers in residential, commercial, industrial and municipal markets throughout North America through a network of collection operations, transfer stations, landfills, recycling facilities and waste-based energy production projects.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>To enable our business to expand our lead in a market increasingly enhanced by technology, Waste Management is undertaking a substantial technology transformation. We are seeking talented Information Technology professionals to join the Waste Management team who are motivated to help us transform the way we design, build and use technology. With your skills and experience, we look for you to combine your technical expertise with industry best practices in an effort to align information technology solutions with Waste Management business strategy.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>I. Job Summary</b></div>
<div>Responsible for coordinating end user readiness, change management, &amp; training related to the Pennies Digital Transformation effort. Change Management Coordinator will be the WM point person for all change management &amp; training activities, and will service as the face of the project both internally &amp; externally. To be successful, this role requires a constant “pulse” on the business &amp; the key stakeholders to ensure alignment, detect un-ease &amp; concern within stakeholders, and ensure that all groups are received appropriate, adequate, and timely communication related to the project. This role will partner closely with our SI (Solution Integrator) Partners to develop and implement a change management strategy, with the expectation that, after an apprenticeship period, take over full responsibility for change management &amp; training coordination.<br>
 </div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>II. Duties and Responsibilities<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each duty satisfactorily. Other ancillary duties may be assigned.</div>
<div>Under supervision, and with the assistance from SI partner(s):</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop an enterprise change impact, readiness assessment, and stakeholder assessment for the initiative – and maintain these assessments through-out the project at pre-defined intervals, including post-deployment standards of measure, follow-up planning, metrics, and milestones</li>
<li>
Develop the training strategy, contents, materials, and plans to train internal &amp; external users on the new system, including regional &amp; role based trainings as required</li>
<li>
Provide training facilitation support and/or facilitate end user trainings, as required</li>
<li>
Develop &amp; maintain a communication strategy for all stakeholder groups, which will include periodic, semi-periodic, &amp; weekly re-occurring communications to all affected stakeholders, across SBS, WM Corp, and vendors/customers</li>
<li>
Responsible for the strategy development &amp; creation of all Sales &amp; internal WM marketing materials related to Pennies Digital Transformation</li>
<li>
Serve as the point of communication for project stakeholders through-out all phases of digital transformation, including (under supervision), the creation, management, and delivery of all communications to internal stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Host and aid in the facilitation of all user group sessions, including user focus groups, requirement review sessions, Sprint review meetings, and other sessions as required</li>
<li>
Support UAT training efforts through coordinating sessions, communicating findings &amp; outputs, and attending sessions to provide a summary of change impact</li>
<li>
In support of Business Solution Architect roles, identify key stakeholders for process, business, and solution review meetings &amp; socialization</li>
<li>
Develops and defines methods and procedures for end user to address<br>
deploying technology and operational process changes</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
