<p>Are you looking for a great place to work? A place where you can use utilize your technical abilities and mechanical aptitude? A place that can offer you growth- professionally and personally? Then think about joining the Priority Envelope! Priority Envelope is a custom envelope manufacturer that is looking for a skilled <b>2nd Shift 214 Printing Press Operator</b><b> </b>to join their team in Plymouth, MN.</p>
<p><b>PRINTING PRESS OPERATOR RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Set-up, operate and troubleshoot printing press equipment to job specifications in an accurate, timely and safe manner.</li>
<li>Read, comprehend and follow all specifications on job ticket.</li>
<li>Perform set-ups and operate press equipment to meet job specifications.</li>
<li>Mix ink for color matching.</li>
<li>Thoroughly read and match samples in job ticket.</li>
<li>Collaborate with scheduling and sales departments to ensure optimal production flow.</li>
<li>Record and maintain data on production reports, job tickets and computer software.</li>
<li>Additional tasks and responsibilities as needed.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or the equivalent; basic reading, writing and math skills; computer literate.</li>
<li>Lifting 50-75 lbs. up to 30 times per hour.</li>
<li>Requires bending, twisting, turning and standing throughout the shift.</li>
<li>Hearing protection is required due to machine noise.</li>
<li>May require working with hazard materials, protection provided.</li>
<br>
</ul>
