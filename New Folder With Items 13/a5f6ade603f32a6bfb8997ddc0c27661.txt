<p>
With nearly 4 million connected vehicles today, <b>Spireon</b> is an exciting player in the growing Connected Car and Internet of Things (IoT) technology categories. We help people and businesses track and protect their most important assets with vehicle intelligence solutions that gather Big Data and provide the critical insights with easy-to-use dashboards and apps.</p>
<p><br>
<b>
This is You:</b></p>
<p>
As a <b>Regional Sales Director,</b> you possess a “hunter” mentality and thrive in building a territory. You have a proven track record selling to Franchise Car Dealers, understanding their pain points, and working with decision makers. You will utilize your in-depth knowledge of technology sales combined with Spireon product and programs, to develop a territory plan to achieve personal and business goals. You’ll work within the Spireon sales and support teams for the achievement of customer satisfaction, revenue generation, and long-term account goals in line with company vision and values.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Effectively presents and sells company products and services to current and potential clients within the specified territory.</li>
<li>
Exceeds monthly sales goals</li>
<li>
Prepare action plans and schedules to identify specific targets and to project the number of contacts to be made daily, weekly and monthly.</li>
<li>
Directly manage the prospect sales engagement, driving the sales process from lead to close</li>
<li>
Proactively generate new business from the Company’s database of identified target buyers</li>
<li>
Prepares presentations, proposals and sales contracts.</li>
<li>
Establishes and maintains current client and potential client relationships.</li>
<li>
Documents sales interactions with prospects and customers through SalesForce.com</li>
<li>
Identifies and resolves client concerns.</li>
<li>
Effectively demonstrates features related to the prospect’s pain points to help them achieve their goals and objectives.</li>
<li>
Prepares a variety of status reports, including activity, closings, follow-up, and adherence to goals.</li>
<li>
Maintains a high level of knowledge relating to current products and features.</li>
<li>
Communicates new product and service opportunities, special developments, information, or feedback gathered through field activity to appropriate company staff.</li>
<li>
Participates in marketing events such as seminars, trade shows, and telemarketing event</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b><br>
<b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years B2B field Sales experience selling to Franchise Car Dealerships</li>
<li>
BA/BS degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>
Software or Technology sales experience a must and understanding of complex and long sales cycles</li>
<li>
Must currently be in a <i>“hunter”</i> role where 75% of your position is business development</li>
<li>
3+ years’ experience working within a car dealership highly preferred; ideal roles like GM, GSM, Finance, or Fixed Ops</li>
<li>
Proficient in technology/tools to drive the sales process: SalesForce, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Zoom.com, LinkedIn, etc.</li>
<li>
Experience working in a startup environment</li>
<li>
Must be able to travel 50-75% including overnight stay</li>
<li>
Ability to map put territory and plan of attack for prospecting within multiple geographic regions</li>
<li>
Ability to overcome significant challenges and hurdles</li>
<li>
Experience with SaaS, telematics, IoT, technical sales highly preferred</li>
<li>
Consistent track record of sales results, quota attainment and raising the level of performance of those around you</li>
<li>
Ability to interact with a prospect’s decision makers to discuss and match key requirements with software capabilities.</li>
<li>
Current network and contact base of franchise car dealerships at owner or director levels</li>
<li>
Ability to thrive in a fast- paced environment, juggle multiple priorities, and be comfortable with change</li>
</ul>
