<br><b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b> Listed in order, ranking the most essential duties first. Essential functions are primary job duties that are intrinsic to the position.
<br><ul>
<li>To maintain the heating and air conditioning systems &amp; service requirements.</li>
<li>Performs multi-maintenance activities in and around the plant, including but not limited to repair, installation, and preventive maintenance. Perform any job assigned in support of production within the confines of RSC property.</li>
<li>Operates and maintains equipment, machinery, hand tools, and HVAC tools in a safe and proficient manner according to guidelines, standards and instructions.</li>
<li>Must be able to work within prescribed tolerances, be familiar with preparation of surfaces, chemical properties and other acceptable standards and have strong trouble-shooting abilities.</li>
<li>Requests, receives, delivers secures and maintains product equipment and materials as required for the job.</li>

<li>Respond in a timely manner to calls for maintenance.</li>

<li>Conform to rules.</li>

</ul>

Company Description
<br>Russell Stover Chocolates is the nation's leading manufacturer of boxed chocolates. The company's three brands – Russell Stover, Whitman's and Pangburn's - account for more than 60 percent of all boxed chocolate sales in the United States through 20+ company-owned retail stores and 70,000 wholesale accounts. After nearly 100 years in operation the company manufactures approximately 100 million pounds of chocolate candies each year in its proud tradition of being “Made in America” and “Handcrafted in Small Batches”.

This was the largest acquisition in the history of Lindt and Sprungli which has operations in over 40 countries across the globe. Russell Stover Chocolates joins sister companies Lindt USA and Ghirardelli to form a powerful confectionery triad in North America.
