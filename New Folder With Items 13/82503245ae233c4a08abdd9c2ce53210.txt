<h2>Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div>
<p><b>THEATRO IS HIRING!</b></p>
<p>
Theatro continues to grow and is looking for qualified candidates to join our Sales Engineering Team.</p>
<p>
This candidate will be focused on large retail enterprise accounts in targeted regions across the US. The person will develop a strong partnership with the sales, engineering, product management and delivery teams to execute our technology strategy within this customer base. As a Theatro Sales Engineer he/she will have every opportunity to learn and grow with our company as we enhance the value and experience of the hourly worker.
</p>
<p>This role requires a self-motivated person ready to dig into new technologies. As the tip of the spear of our technical sales team there will be a high expectation to get close to the IT teams of our most valued customers. If you have a passion for leading edge technology and a solid background in WLAN and Systems Architectures, give Theatro a try.</p>
<p>
Theatro is a fast paced, entrepreneurial and a fun place to work. Check us out at www.theatro.com</p>
<p><i>
Responsibilities Include:
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Technical Skills – WLAN, IP Networking, Server and Cloud Systems</li>
<li>Sales Skills – Partners with Account Director to develop sales strategies to unique customer needs; creates customer relationships</li>
<li>Leadership Skills – Influence others without direct reporting responsibilities</li>
<li>Market/customer Knowledge – Acts as a resource to customers based on intimate knowledge of relevant industries (Retail/Hospitality)</li>
<li>Creative Thinker – Solves complex problems which may require unique solutions</li>
<li>Level of Guidance – Able to work independently or with a team. Requires low supervision, but willingly stays connected to the group.</li>
<li>Takes Direction From – Manager and Account Directors</li>
<li>Role – Often leads semi-formal teams or significant portions of a large project</li>
<li>Level of Customer Contact – Front line customer interface representing Theatro in technical interactions</li>
<li>Main Level of Interaction – Leads negotiations of technical requirements within the customer network</li>
<li>Strategic Impact for Theatro – High in short and long-term relationships</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Job Requirements:
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors or a minimum of five years related experience working in a technical role;</li>
<li>5 + years of recent relevant work experience.</li>
</ul>
