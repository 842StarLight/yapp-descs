<h2>Auditor - Information Security (ISO 27001)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Responsible for delivering BSI’s audit services to assigned clients in accordance with all BSI, scheme and regulatory requirements to assure timely, cost effective service delivery that assures satisfaction of our customer needs. The individual will conduct various types of audits as Team Leader or Team Member.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The ideal candidate will have third party audit experience in ISO 27001 – Information Security.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Essential Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for attending any required training and following all procedures/processes/policies within BSI for management of clients, management of a home based office, use of BSI equipment and communication both internal and external to the organization.</li>
<li>
Responsible for following the training plan developed by the AVP in order to reach assessor status and then Lead assessor status through the prescribed program.</li>
<li>
Participate in additional training based on future business needs.</li>
<li>
Responsible for managing a portfolio of assigned clients based on location and a match of qualifications and client contract requirements.</li>
<li>
Responsible for contacting clients and scheduling the visits, planning the assessments, making travel plans, conducting the assessments and reporting and managing the results.</li>
<li>
Responsible for monitoring the client accounts to ensure that records, PointGlobal information, visit cycle, invoicing and other related matters are properly dealt with to assure client satisfaction is maintained.</li>
<li>
Responsible for leading teams, when necessary, and mentoring and coaching new or inexperienced colleagues as needed to meet the business needs.</li>
<li>
Any other assignments as needed to meet assessment delivery business objectives.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About You
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This great opportunity for an ambitious professional to gain visibility within the organization.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
We seek someone who meets all of the following criteria:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
2 years of verified security management experience.</div></li>
<li><div>
4 years practical experience of IT, including e-commerce applications such as: electronic banking, extranets and web-based database services.</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience of applying information security or similar standards.</div></li>
<li>
Can deliver and strive for superior performance, and who can achieve challenging objectives.</li>
<li>
Are proactive with people and can lead by example.</li>
<li>
Can demonstrate passion for the business and can inspire others.</li>
<li>
Have integrity, ensuring that business is conducted in an ethical manner.</li>
<li>
Have commercial focus, understanding customers’ needs and adding value.</li>
<li>
College degree AA or higher (or equivalent certification or work experience).</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Background and experience that would be a plus include:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Successful completion of a 5-day ISO/IEC 27001: 2013 Lead Assessor Course.</div></li>
<li><div>
Knowledge and application of Risk Assessment methodologies in the context of Information Security Management Systems.</div></li>
<li><div>
Knowledge of auditing principles and third party audit process.</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Keys to success in this job:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Knowledge and application of ISMS</li>
<li>
Key auditor traits: professional, ethical, open-minded, diplomatic, observant, perceptive, versatile, tenacious, decisive, self-reliant, collaborative, culturally sensitive, acting with fortitude, open to improvement.</li>
<li>
Risk based thinking and process approach as well as synthesizing information to prepare summary reports.</li>
</ul>
