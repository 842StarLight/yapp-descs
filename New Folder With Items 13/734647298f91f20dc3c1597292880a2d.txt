<h2>Marketing & Outreach Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>TITLE: </b>Marketing &amp; Outreach Coordinator <b>DEPARTMENT: </b>Marketing</p>
<p><b>
STATUS: </b>FT, Salary, Exempt, NBU <b>REPORTS TO: </b>Marketing &amp; Communications Manager</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PURPOSE:</b> This role administers and performs the functions associated with brand marketing and advertising, ownership services and board support.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITES</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>CUSTOMER SERVICE</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<ul>
<li>Provide internal and external customers with excellent service at all times.</li>
<li>Treat people fairly, consistently, and with respect.</li>
<li>Ensure efficient, informative, and friendly service in all areas according to established customer service vision and standards.</li>
<li>Use appropriate language, behavior, appearance, and overall conduct with or in the presence of customers, vendors, and store personnel according to BCS and store policies.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>ADVERTISING &amp; PROMOTIONS</b>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the coordination of media participation in advocating public policies consistent with the co-op’s ends.</li>
<li>Assist with valuable, engaging content for website, e-newsletter, social media and other communication avenues.</li>
<li>Coordinate and participate in regular store promotions planning (to ensure appropriate and effective strategies are used to execute the brand within the store and externally).</li>
<li>Work with Managers to coordinate advertising and other publicity for promotions. Produce publications for promotions – special flyers, coupons, recipes, and nutrition information.</li>
<li>Participate in the development and facilitation of special projects as needed to support &amp; engage the membership and customers. (Examples: member loan program, annual survey, annual meeting, tent sale)</li>
<li>Assist with positive representation of the co-op to the media and the public.</li>
<li>Participate in department meetings to plan promotions and in-store merchandising.</li>
<li>Work with Marketing Manager to coordinate advertising and other publicity for promotions and publications.</li>
<li>Provide support in marketing initiatives. (Examples: promotion metrics, tracking and monitoring)</li>
<li>Perform other tasks assigned by the Marketing Manager.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>OWNERSHIP SERVICES
</b><ul>
<li>Facilitate achievement of membership growth objectives with strategies, trainings and support materials for staff.</li>
<li>Participate in the development and implementation of owner benefits and engagement strategies such as owner meetings, celebrations and input sessions.</li>
<li>Monitor and respond appropriately to owner communications including in-person, telephone, and digital means.</li>
<li>Assist Board Administrator as needed.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>MARKETING COMMUNICATIONS</b>
<ul>
<li>Assist with and monitor timelines/promotional schedule for events, working with communications staff to ensure adequate and proper promotion</li>
<li>Manage the promotional aspects of events including, but not limited to, the following:
<ul>
<li>Develop any registration materials required for events</li>
<li>Coordinate event logistics and publicity, including public relations, advertising and collateral material design, production and distribution.</li>
<li>Review promotional materials (including advertising)</li>
<li>Write articles for internal and external publications to promote the events held, both before and after events.</li>
<li>Ensure events section of website remains current.</li>
<li>Navigate options and negotiate contracts from service providers.</li>
<li>Measure and report performance of marketing campaign ROI utilizing POS system.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Monitor and distribute customer feedback forms collected from all stores.</li>
<li>Maintain branding and communications components of the POS system.</li>
<li>Participation in co-op-sponsored events teams.</li>
<li>Assist with market research and data mining to identify customer trends</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>COMMUNICATION</b>
<ul>
<li>Attend Administrative, Departmental, and Store level meetings as needed.</li>
<li>Provide regular opportunities to receive feedback from all staff.</li>
<li>Offer constructive and fair coaching and feedback to all staff.</li>
<li>Maintain open communication links with customers to address inquiries and feedback.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul> <p></p>
<p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<ul>
<li>Associate degree in marketing, visual communications, media design or equivalent experience. Bachelor’s degree preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with social media advertising. Facebook advertising certification or equivalent preferred.</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office and Adobe Creative Cloud software. Experience in Adobe Creative Suite and Hootsuite or equivalent software preferred.</li>
<li>Experience in database management.</li>
<li>Substantial knowledge of the cooperative business model.</li>
<li>Professional and effective communication; verbally and in writing to a diverse audience.</li>
<li>Flexibility, willingness to learn/adjust/adapt to changing priorities, requirements and conditions.</li>
<li>Excellent time-management, problem prevention and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Work accurately with close attention to detail.</li>
<li>Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information.</li>
<li>Possess a work ethic that includes neatness, punctuality and accuracy.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
