<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h2>
<div><div>
<div><p>Review and resolve rejected claims timely and accurately. Contact insurance companies to follow up on unpaid claims timely &amp; accurately. Ability to appeal claim denials. Develop a deep understanding of the services provided by the assigned department in order to maximize insurance reimbursement. Analyze problems and trends, provide documentation and recommendation to Coordinator or Supervisor. Review &amp; resolve fatal and non-fatal edits timely and accurately. Submit paper claims daily with required documentation. Contribute process improvement ideas to gain efficiency and effectiveness.</p></div>
</div></div>
