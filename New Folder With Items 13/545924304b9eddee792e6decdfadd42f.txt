<p>At Microf we believe in the power of a conversation. It's how we learn, how we grow, and how ideas spread. It's at the heart of everything we do. We ask questions. We listen. That's how we help our customers succeed. So when the conversation turns to your future, your success, talk to us. We want to know how your story fits with ours and where we can go " together".</p>
<p>
We are a diversified high growth company with a 45 million asset client base with offices located in Atlanta, Albany and Roswell, Georgia.</p>
<p>
At Microf our vision is to satisfy our customers HVAC replacement needs and help them succeed financially. In this role, you will help us deliver on our vision and build lifelong relationships with our vendors and consumers. You also will demonstrate leadership by contributing to a company culture that supports customers in achieving their financial goals, team members develop their careers, and communities continue to thrive. As part of a team, you will play a vital role in living our commitment to the highest ethical standard and maintaining the valued trust of our customers.</p>
<p><b>
PURPOSE: </b>The Microf Sales Model is centered around key geographies/markets, which is led by the Regional Sales Manager (RSM). The RSM is responsible for driving new sales and net growth of their assigned geography. The success of the RSM is based on four core principles.<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Ensure that their market is growing YOY and meeting plan</li>
<li>
Sell new Enterprise accounts
</li>
<li>Align with the other internal selling organizations (Account Management, SMB Sales, etc.) to support as necessary
</li>
<li>Leverage Market Influencers – such as Distributors and Strategic Partners. RSM will work Territory Managers of the Distributors in the assigned territory and will local management.</li>
</ul>
