<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
This job is responsible for the day-to-day demands associated with Enterprise Data Governance including being the primary support for the Enterprise Data Governance Committee and associated subcommittees. Responsibilities will also include supporting MDM and the documentation associated with this program. Provides guidance and education for Associates and Intermediates. Meets individually with customers for specific projects.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide backup and support for the various data governance committees of the organization, including preparation of detail behind requests to the committee, executing follow-up tasks assigned from the meetings, and providing education and support for data governance across the organization.</li>
<li>
Under the guidance of the data governance committees of the organization and management, execute the governance requirements established for all entities across the organization.</li>
<li>
Work with data owners to establish, maintain, and distribute definitions of data elements used by analytics in conjunction with data owners throughout the organization.</li>
<li>
Lead functional efforts for projects, including guidance and education of Associates and Intermediates. Meet individually with customers for specific projects.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
