<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job description :</p>
<p>We are a growing major supplier of high quality, certified organic, gluten-free food, and bakery ingredients. Our family-owned company was established in 1973 and expanded to our USA operations in 2003. <i>Please visit our website reliableproduct.com for more details.</i></p>
<p>As a premier food ingredient supplier, we strive to help our customers to source the best quality ingredients to make their final product. We are seeking a highly motivated, responsible, hard-working and passionate individual to join us. This position will be responsible for providing outstanding customer service while managing a group of new accounts by new business development and cold calling new customers, negotiating prices and terms of the contracts and collaborating internally to meet both company and customer needs. This person will be instrumental in working closely with our clients to provide a solution for their needs. He/she should have food industry experience and work closely with the President, COO, Purchasing team to quote our customer. We are NOT looking for a pushy car salesman or someone from the auto industry.</p>
<p><b><i>We’re Looking For: </i></b></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will have effective sales skills, be comfortable putting together and delivering creative solutions packages for customers, have previous experience in the food industry or food ingredients industry IS A BIG PLUS and have a BA/BS in a related field. A positive, “can do” mentality is essential as is possessing excellent communication skills ( both verbal and written/email).</p>
<p>You will cold call and pursue new leads through a proven sales process that we provide. You will work within a phenomenal custom sales software program(CRM) Sales Force. Establish a solid business base selling core and new products to develop new diverse customers base. Maintain superior customer services, willingness to learn and understand the product thoroughly, ability to build a strong relationship with our customer and follow-through throughout the day. Be proactive in communication with both to customers as well as go the President of the company. Maintain a working knowledge of the market and growing conditions and communicate this effectively to the customer base. Communicate effectively and proactively with buyers, President and sales support staff to meet customer needs and expectations on a day to day basis. Someone who is a foodie by nature and want to learn and show interest in healthy food, gluten-free market.</p>
<p>We offer a rewarding and exciting work environment, competitive compensation and the opportunity to be a part of a growing sales force.</p>
<p><b>Core Competencies: </b></p>
<p>Warm, friendly and engaging personality. Confident and articulate by nature. Passionate about serving and being a resource to others. Strong collaborator within diverse teams, high-stress tolerance, adaptable to change, ability to listen and understand the information presented through oral communication. ability to learn fast in a fast pace environment.</p>
