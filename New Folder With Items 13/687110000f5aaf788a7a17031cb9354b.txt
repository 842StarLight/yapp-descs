<h2>Magna - Associate Director, Client Strategy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>The Associate Director of Client Strategy, Marketplace Development East will help IPG Mediabrands’ strategy and programmatic teams in NYC and the Midwest navigate the opportunities and complexities of programmatic direct investment. This role revels in the convergence of traditional publisher direct investment with audience and technology platform strategies. The Associate Director will work across all internal business units, with a focus on the Media Agency Brand Strategy and Programmatic Specialists to help evangelize MAGNA’s centralized media investment methodology and drive adoption of our marketplace discovery platform. The role requires an individual who is passionate about performance analytics, publisher investment and next generation ad technology. The Associate Director of Marketplace Development East will report directly to the Vice President of National Marketplace Development based in Los Angeles.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work collaboratively with Strategy/Investment leads and Programmatic Specialists to develop unified performance reporting which helps guide programmatic direct investment</li>
<li>Work with programmatic publisher sales teams to evangelize the adoption and use of private marketplaces</li>
<li>Work directly with the Cadreon (IPG’s ad tech unit) to document publisher investment performance reporting and optimization techniques</li>
<li>Work closely with product managers, data scientists, and platform engineers located in San Francisco to advance MAGNA’s Marketplace functionality</li>
<li>Consult with supply-side platforms like Index Exchange, DoubleClick AdExchange, TrustX, OpenX, Rubicon Project, PubMatic, SpotX, and FreeWheel Marketplace to drive publisher direct investment.</li>
<li>Consult with leading DSP platforms like The Trade Desk, DoubleClick BidManager, TubeMogul by Adobe, MediaMath, Amboee (formerly Turn), etc. to optimize PMP workflow and reporting</li>
<li>Identify and drive adoption of emerging technologies and their associated exchange technologies</li>
<li>Evangelize best practices to protect against for brand safety issues, inventory fraud, and non-human traffic exposure</li>
<li>Drive transparency initiatives and adoption of Programmatic Guaranteed</li>
<li>Setup Foundational Marketplace deals and various DSPs</li>
</ul>
