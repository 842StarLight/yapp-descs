<h2>IT Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary<br>
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<br>
<br>
The IT Business Development Manager (BDM) will report directly to the CEO. The primary focus for this position is to help expand the IT client base in the Northeastern United States. The role will be responsible for managing and growing the client base and revenue in both the middle market – where Auxis has a significant base and competitive advantage – and the Fortune 1000 market spaces where Auxis has been making significant headway and finding rapid market acceptance.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Drive Auxis’ sales process within your assigned territory to ensure high margin sales (target margins are 30+% gross margins for outsourcing and 50+% gross margins for project work)<br>
<ul>
<li>Geographic focus will be primarily the greater New York area.</li>
<li>Primary Service Lines: Information Technology (Consulting and Outsourcing)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Lead efforts to acquire new clients through direct individual prospecting and sales activities. Including but not limited to prospect identification and research, penetrating new accounts through direct prospecting activities and proper qualification of potential business opportunities.</li>
<li>
Develop high level executive relationships with true decision makers, key officers &amp; directors at new prospective clients and maintain high-level relationships with clients to facilitate recurring client revenues and referrals.</li>
<li>
Stay current on IT trends and provide credible knowledge and understanding of IT to client personnel at the executive and managerial levels.</li>
<li>
Expand awareness of Auxis’ IT outsourcing and consulting capabilities within the assigned focus area. This includes but is not limited to active involvement in professional associations, professional speaking engagements, thought leadership, etc.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Skills and Experience
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The successful candidate will be highly motivated, able to work in a challenging environment and able to work well with clients and teammates in person and remotely. Specific requirements include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of at least 7 years (preferably 10+) of overall sales experience with at least 3 years selling IT services to middle market and Fortune 1000.</li>
<li>
A demonstrated track record of individually selling at least $2.5MM+ in annual services revenue (i.e. excluding any product (hardware/software) revenue).</li>
<li>
Strong preference for candidates who have at least 3 years of IT sales experience in the Greater New York Market.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree required.
</li>
<li>Strong general knowledge of IT trends and operations (Cloud, ITIL, etc.) and be able to differentiate between nearshore Latin American outsourcing and far away off shore outsourcing in Asia. Not a “techy”, but need to understand IT and be conversant. Should be able to explain how an IT department is organized, major challenges IT organizations are facing and strong awareness of the competitive environment within the IT services industry.</li>
<li>
Proven track record of achieving sales goals. Must have had proven success in hitting sales targets on a consistent basis.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
