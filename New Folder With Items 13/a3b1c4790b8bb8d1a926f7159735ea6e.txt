<p>Staff accountant/ Information Technology Support</p>
<p>Fast growing beverage distribution company in Grand Prairie is seeking a staff accountant with a strong IT background. This is for immediate hiring.</p>
<p><b>Description: </b></p>
<p>Responsibilities include data entry and a variety of moderately complex clerical duties in the Accounting area as well as tasks required to support onsite and field personnel.</p>
<p>Time will be divided among accounting support; technical support; assisting with and learning various positions within the beverage industry.</p>
<p><b>Additional Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>This position is also the first line of support for Route Sales Representatives who may have technical and/or equipment problems in the field. Responsibilities include inbound/outbound phone support, technical troubleshooting, user training/guiding, email communications and documentation.</p>
<p>Applicant must be available to occasionally assist with phone support requests from Route Sales Representatives outside normal work hours.</p>
<p>Will be responsible for properly documenting end user reported issues as well as steps taken to implement solutions.</p>
<p>Escalate issues when necessary and ensure that critical problems are quickly communicated to your team leader.</p>
<p>.</p>
<p><b>Desired Skills: </b></p>
<p>Ability to multi-task and prioritize.</p>
<p>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.</p>
<p>Should have knowledge of commonly used concepts, procedures and practices in IT field</p>
<p>(IE,PC,desktop,laptop,Microsoft products, mobile devices.</p>
<p>Proficient in Microsoft Office especially in Excel &amp; Word</p>
<p>Comfortable dealing with numbers</p>
<p><b>Required Training and Experience: </b></p>
<p>Bachelor or Associate degree in Accounting or IT.</p>
<p>Minimum three years accounting experience and one year of technical support.</p>
<p>Proficiency in Microsoft operating systems and its office applications.</p>
