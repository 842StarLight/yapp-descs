<h2>Franchise Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Disney Parks, Experiences and Products (DPEP)’s strategic objectives include sustaining growth across our franchise portfolio and expanding a franchise management approach across all our businesses and regions. Our vision is to create an aligned Franchise voice to drive measurable value against established goals for DPEP and the enterprise. To realize this vision, we have established a DPEP Franchise function that provides clarity, alignment, consistency, and material growth across the franchise portfolio.<br>
<br>
The DPEP Franchise Manager serves as a partner to content makers and Franchise teams throughout DPEP and The Walt Disney Company (TWDC). This role is an integral member of the DPEP Franchise organization, is based in Glendale, CA, and reports to the Director, Franchise Strategy.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities :</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist in development, communication and driving of long-term strategy for DPEP globally around the applicable franchise(s) that expands audiences and builds lifelong relationships with fans</li>
<li>
Conduct analysis to inform long-term franchise planning that aligns strategies across businesses to ensure optimization of revenue streams</li>
<li>
Help identify and shepherd additional opportunities for revenue across DPEP by identifying and engaging “white space,” or untapped, opportunities for applicable franchises that can be leveraged by multiple LOBs</li>
<li>
Support the implementation of long term strategy in partnership with embedded DPEP Franchise teams on franchise efforts</li>
<li>
Serve as project manager for franchise planning, marketing implementation and sales execution, to ensure alliance with overall DPEP Franchise strategy, goals and objectives</li>
<li>
Help to define clear, actionable, relevant Franchise KPIs and ensure efforts across DPEP align with and deliver against these KPIs</li>
<li>
Facilitate communication of DPEP Franchise strategy to DPEP LOBs</li>
<li>
Support creation of presentations and other communication devices to disseminate strategies to DPEP business partners</li>
<li>
Build and foster strong relationships with content partners and Franchise teams across TWDC, as well as within DPEP globally</li>
<li>
Ensure DPEP product and storytelling experiences stay true to brand/franchise stories, characters and creative intent</li>
<li>
Promote and foster best practices and knowledge sharing across DPEP globally</li>
<li>
Partner closely with Synergy and Communications teams on Franchise efforts</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Basic Qualifications :</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Expert, engaging presentation skills (verbal and written)</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills and confident presence</li>
<li>
Strategic thinker</li>
<li>
Ability to collaborate, influence and persuade at all levels across an organization</li>
<li>
Flexible and adaptable to changing priorities with the skills to work in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Comfortable working through a matrix structure to deliver the end solution</li>
<li>
5-7 years experience in marketing or brand management roles with global mindset, working in a world-class organization</li>
<li>
Presentation experience to senior executives and large groups required</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Required Education :</b><br>
<ul><li>Bachelor of Science or Arts required; Major in consumer marketing, business, or similar fields preferred</li></ul>
