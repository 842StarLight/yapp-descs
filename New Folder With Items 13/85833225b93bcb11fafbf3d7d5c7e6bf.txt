<h2>Director, Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Reporting to the CEO, this person will serve as the lead marketing strategist for Medely Inc and Medely Solutions. The person we seek is a strategic thinker who can translate big picture marketing vision to implementable operational plans, this individual is a dot connector who must understand how all of our marketing efforts work together to build our brand and advance our goals.
<br><br>
This person will be charged with building and leading a world class marketing team whose purpose is to build best in class campaigns, drive awareness, signups, and engagement on the Medely platform.
<br><br>
<b>Who the ideal candidate will be/have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A B2B marketing leader with 5 to 10 years of experience building pipeline and delivering highly engaged prospects to sales.</li>
<li>An expert in collecting, analyzing and internally sharing competitive intelligence</li>
<li>Able to assume the role of brand storyteller and evangelist, blending positioning, messaging, and industry trends into a clear, tangible narrative</li>
<li>Deep understanding and experience about brand expression and experience and knows how to bring a brand to life through strategic marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>An experienced marketer with 5+ years spent creating and implementing brand and marketing strategies based on data-driven insights</li>
<li>Experienced in selling and providing operational solutions to small, mid, and enterprise level companies</li>
<li>Able to develop and retain a great marketing talent</li>
<li>A great collaborator as you will work across multiple teams</li>
<li>Naturally curious and enjoy asking questions, understanding how companies prosper and pushing to ensure we are operating as effectively as we can.</li>
<li>Continually focused on the improvement of the brand and how we use marketing tools to engage with our customers.</li>
<li>Customer-centric and always striving to provide a flawless customer experience. This obsession to create high quality experiences is not limited to our customers, you treat everyone including colleagues with the same hospitality.</li>
<li>Willing to roll up your sleeves and execute!</li>
</ul>
<b>What you will do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with the company founders to oversee creative execution of proposed brand strategy and positioning</li>
<li>Develop a deep understanding of our product and business strategy and customer needs, review/conduct competitive and market research, engage closely with our engineering and executive leadership team.</li>
<li>Develop the product marketing deliverable plan and build a product marketing strategy for the future of Medely's growth</li>
</ul>
<b>Within your first three months, you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work on building a fully staffed, world class marketing team</li>
<li>Design and implement a competitive response program</li>
<li>Evangelize Medely through speaking engagements, webinars, customer calls and customer outreach.</li>
<li>Deliver the content for our website updates</li>
<li>Define and implement how you will measure and report on your team's outcomes and contributions</li>
</ul>
<b>Within six months, you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Build/update a library of product marketing core assets related to buyers, competitors, and customer stories</li>
<li>Build the pitch decks and customer communications to support our renewals and upsell team</li>
<li>Be the first person in the company who the sales team goes to for messaging and competitive input and strategies</li>
<li>Arm the Sales Development Representative (SDR) and AE team with the tools and conversations to convert prospects from Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) to Sales Qualified Leads (SQLs)</li>
<li>Become an expert in our market, including knowledge of our competitors</li>
</ul>
