 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Join our team and experience Neocase Software! At Neocase, we're bringing our SaaS solution to HR Employee Relationship Management and Shared Service Centers throughout<br>North America and the World. Customers like Merck, Air France, Renault-Nissan, MGM<br>Resorts, Thales Group, Guardian Life Insurance, Société Générale, Sanofi, Baker Hughes and many more respected brands have invested in our solutions because they deliver real business results. More than 5 millions of employee use Neocase worldwide.</p>
<p>Neocase Software is actively seeking a Pre-sales Engineer associate to join our sales team.<br>You’ll support the sales effort by presenting our solutions through RFP responses, sales presentations and product demonstrations. You’ll engage with business and technical professionals on topics including HR Transformation, Customer Service, SaaS, Information<br>Security, Systems Integration and Architecture.</p>
<p><b>Specific job duties include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Delivering high-level product demonstrations to potential prospects</li>
<li>Owning the RFP process, including responding to RFP’s and RFI’s, maintaining a database of questions and answers, and requests for new product features</li>
<li>Leading discovery meetings and needs assessments with prospects and clients</li>
<li>Configuring tailored proof-of-concept demonstration scenarios based on clients’ specific requirements</li>
<li>Delivering tailored product demonstrations specific to the client’s requirements, and deepdive demonstrations in specific functional areas of the product</li>
<li>Responding to technical inquiries about the solution relative to architecture, security, privacy, performance and integration capabilities</li>
<li>Highlighting the advantages of the Neocase solution relative to competitive offerings</li>
<li>Creating and maintaining a “knowledge base” of information about key competitive offerings, relative to the Neocase solution</li>
<li>Provide other assistance to your Neocase Sales and Services teammates as needed</li>
<li>Approximately 30% travel.</li>
</ul>
