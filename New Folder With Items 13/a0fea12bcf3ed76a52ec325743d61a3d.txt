<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Are you someone who is passionate about customer service, knows how to take initiative, have excellent follow through, and truly enjoys solving problems? Then Pacific Office Automation wants to talk to you.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Come Join POA's growing and dynamic IT team</b> <b>as a Tier II Help-desk Technician at our branch in Salem, OR.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
High-Level Job Description</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Remotely troubleshoot and diagnose our customer’s network connectivity, PC, VOIP, and server issues.</li>
<li>
Message and place calls to customers for information gathering and troubleshooting.</li>
<li>
Work closely helping to resolve issues with sales representatives, customers, and VOIP engineers.</li>
<li>
Be a part of a fun, growing, and dynamic department and culture focused on providing the highest level of customer success.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Must Have Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
IT knowledge mixed with a willingness and capability to learn.</li>
<li>
Natural problem-solving ability combined with a passion for fantastic customer service.</li>
<li>
Exposure/some System Administration experience</li>
<li>
Excellent written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Bonus Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Associate’s or Bachelor’s degree.</li>
<li>
VOIP technology experience.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of networking protocols and concepts (VPN, Routing, Ports, TCP/IP, Network analysis tools).</li>
<li>
Network+ or A+ Certifications.</li>
<li>
Knowledge and hands-on experience providing technical support to users using Windows 7, Windows 8 and other mainstream Microsoft applications.</li>
</ul>
