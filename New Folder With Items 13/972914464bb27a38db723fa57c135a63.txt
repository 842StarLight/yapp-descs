<h2>Business Development Professional - DoD</h2>

<p><b>Job Requisition for </b><b>Business Developer – DoD</b><br>
</p>
<p>
Acuity Systems is an established small business that applies agile practices and a DevSecOps delivery approach to deliver large-scale IT modernization, advanced analytics, enterprise information management, and business process management solutions for federal government customers. Acuity delivers IT solutions that extent the benefits of digital transformation so that organizations and lines of business can solve more of their mission challenges.</p>
<p><b><i>
Position Description</i></b><b><i> – </i></b><b><i>Business Development Professional – DoD</i></b><br>
</p>
<p>
Acuity seeks an experienced Business Development Professional (BDP) with a strong and verifiable record of success in identifying, qualifying, and closing competitive opportunities within the U.S. Department of Defense market. Acuity is seeking a hands-on BDP who is willing and able to “roll up their sleeves” in order to deliver desired growth results. The ideal candidate will be knowledgeable about Federal technology trends and initiatives and have direct knowledge and experience with a subset of the following: IT modernization, software engineering, big data, business intelligence/analytics, enterprise information management, business process modernization and management (BPM).</p>
<p>
He/she will work collaboratively with the leadership team to achieve growth objectives, provide forecasts and identify challenges that may lie ahead, all in an effort to keep Acuity on its growth path.</p>
<p>
The BDP will report to Acuity Chief Growth Officer, who will provide primary direction regarding focus, priorities, and assignments and will assess performance. The BDP will also work closely with other Acuity leaders and subject matter experts.</p>
<p><b><i>
Critical Tasks and Responsibilities</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Guide account and opportunity focus and strategy it his/her targeted market area</li>
<li>
Identify and qualify new business opportunities using a disciplined sales process and objective qualification criteria</li>
<li>
Develop and execute an opportunity pipeline that is sufficient to support corporate growth objectives and meet his/her individual performance objectives</li>
<li>
Advocate for opportunities through corporate sales governance process.</li>
<li>
Develop and execute call plans with key customer stakeholders and influencers to influence acquisition strategy and position Acuity to win potential business opportunities</li>
<li>
Identify and develop relationships with key strategic partners</li>
<li>
Contribute as a member of Capture Teams by contributing to call plan development, setting appointments, gathering/confirming customer “hot buttons” and requirements information, providing competitive insights, and assembling teaming partners.</li>
</ul>
<b><i>
Required Skills, Competencies, Aptitudes</i></b><br>
<ul>
<li> Expertise with disciplined federal sales and capture process (such as Shipley) and supporting tools</li>
<li>
Professionalism in interacting with internal and external customers, including ability to exercise good judgment, discretion, tact, and diplomacy</li>
<li>
Excellent communicator with strong interpersonal skills and self-awareness</li>
<li>
Driven to win and excel</li>
<li>
Relationship initiator, builder, nurturer</li>
<li>
Team-oriented contributor—builds, nurtures, collaborates on teams</li>
</ul>
<b><i>
Required Minimum Experience and Education</i></b><br>
<div><ul>
<li>4+ years of experience performing in a BD/sales or capture manager role, or as a project/program manager with BD/Sales responsibilities, in the U.S. DoD market</li>
<li>
Understanding and knowledge of DoD IT budgets and individual agency spending priorities</li>
<li>
Knowledge of DoD acquisition process and sales cycle</li>
<li>
Understanding of cross-cutting DoD, agency, and enterprise level IT mission programs and data management initiatives</li>
<li>
Verifiable track record of building and executing a pipeline of qualified opportunities in his/her assigned market</li>
<li>
Achievement of at least $10M TCV in contract awards for three or more consecutive years</li>
<li>
Active and influential customer relationships with one or more of the following DoD organizations: Office of Secretary of Defense (OSD), Army, Marine Corps, Air Force, DISA.</li>
<li>
Active and influential relations within DoD contractor and vendor communities</li>
</ul></div>
