<h2>Technology Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a high energy Sales Development Representative to help grow NextCrew’s small and medium-sized (SMB) customer base. Every day you’ll be prospecting new SMB customers, responding to inbound inquiries and taking the prospect through the complete sales funnel.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proactively prospect and generate leads through research, personal emails, and other tools.</li>
<li>Respond, engage and qualify inbound leads and inquiries</li>
<li>Maintain sales funnel &amp; pipeline using CRM software</li>
<li>Schedule and run online product demonstrations</li>
<li>Run new business sales cycles from end to end</li>
<li>Provide proactive support to customers and prospects</li>
<li>Act as the subject matter expert</li>
<li>Attend tradeshows</li>
</ul>
<p><b>BASIC QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Two years of experience working for a technology sales role</li>
<li>Understanding of staffing operation and challenges</li>
<li>Ability to provide a solution to staffing challenges</li>
<li>Excellent technology skills</li>
<li>Understanding of social media profile management (LinkedIn)</li>
<li>Understanding of CRM Software (Salesforce or Zoho)</li>
<li>Excellent communication and writing skills</li>
<li>Independent, high energy &amp; highly motivated to grow</li>
<li>Understanding and experience with solution selling</li>
<li>Highly organized and have great attention to detail</li>
<li>Must be able to create engagement via targeted email and phone outreach</li>
<li>Must be very comfortable wearing multiple hats</li>
<li>Outstanding writing, presentation, and verbal skills</li>
<li>Passion for working with emerging technologies</li>
<li>BA/BS degree</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MUST-HAVE CULTURAL ATTRIBUTES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Self-motivated: You can work with a minimum of supervision and be capable of strategically prioritizing multiple tasks in a proactive manner.</li>
<li>Driven: You are a driven team player, collaborator, and relationship builder whose infectious can-do attitude inspires others and encourages great performance in a fast-moving environment.</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial: You thrive in a fast-paced, changing environment, and you’re excited by chance to play a large role.</li>
<li>Passionate: You must be passionate about online collaboration and ensuring our clients are successful; we love seeing hunger and ambition.</li>
<li>Go-getter: Thrive in a startup environment and have a “whatever it takes” approach to accomplishing goals.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>LOCATION</b></p>
