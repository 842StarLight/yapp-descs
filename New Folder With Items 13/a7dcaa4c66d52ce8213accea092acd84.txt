<h2>Senior Finance Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Senior Finance Manager, Store Operations<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Lowes is a FORTUNE® 50 home improvement retailer serving more than 18 million customers a week in the United States and Canada. With fiscal year 2018 sales of $71.3 billion, Lowe’s and its related businesses operate or service more than 2,000 home improvement and hardware stores and employ over 300,000 people.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>We are seeking a dynamic and passionate Senior Finance Manager to support the Store Operations team. Through technology, process improvement and effective workforce management, the Store Operations group is driving store simplification efforts that help our 300,000+ associates better serve our customers.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>This role will lead a team responsible for driving expense productivity within our stores as well as pulling together the total Store Operations performance story. The Senior Finance Manager will work closely with Stores Finance leadership and functions across the enterprise to help identify and drive the actions needed to meet financial commitments.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The successful candidate will be thoughtful, analytical, have a strong bias to action and the ability to collaborate across functions. This role requires a results-oriented leader that can build and develop a strong team. The candidate should also have a proven track record of managing a team with competing priorities and providing clear guidance in a fast-paced, high-energy environment.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Relocation available to those who qualify.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities include:</div>
<ul>
<li>Lead a team responsible for building processes and establishing governance mechanisms to help to drive expense productivity</li>
<li>Manage and develop key FP&amp;A processes to analyze financial performance and communicate key drivers of performance to leadership</li>
<li>Design, develop &amp; deliver insightful financial reporting, dashboards and analyses</li>
<li>Develop financial metrics to help identify expense opportunities</li>
<li>Implement process improvement initiatives such as reporting automation and process standardization</li>
</ul>
<div>
Education and Experience:</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Business, Finance, Accounting, or related analytical field</li>
<li>7+ years’ experience in a finance or analytical role</li>
<li>People leader with a demonstrated ability to build and develop high performing teams</li>
<li>Strong financial modeling and Excel skills</li>
<li>Exceptional analytical skills; highly detail oriented</li>
<li>Experience with business intelligence and reporting tools (MicroStrategy, TM1, Hyperion/Essbase, Business Objects, Cognos, Teradata, etc.)</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work independently in a fast-pace environment and manage multiple competing priorities</li>
<li>Experience with visualization and business analysis tools (e.g. Tableau, MicroStrategy, Power BI, etc.) desirable</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
