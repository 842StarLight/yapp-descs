<br><br>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
WideOrbit has an exciting opportunity for an Account Manager to oversee the relationship of some of our cable network accounts. As an Account Manager, your primary objective is to establish productive, professional relationships with key personnel in our accounts and evangelize to them how WideOrbit can help them reach their short term and long term business goals. You will be the customer's key business consultant and take ownership of projects and troubleshoot escalated issues for our clients. In order to do so successfully, you will manage expectations around software capabilities, educate customers on our various product offerings, document barriers to product use, and recommend new product features to increase future renewability and reference ability. If you have experience with WO Network or has experience in the cable network industry with a passion for taking care of customers, this could be the right job for you.
<br><br>
<b>Here is what you'll be doing:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain a deep understanding of customer's business needs, strengths and weaknesses including their workflows and long term needs. Communicate customers' needs and make recommendations on software changes to the Product Management.</li>
<li>Create trust and confidence in our customers about our products and services by demonstrating your knowledge of their business needs, provide clear and consistent communication, and act as an advocate for their needs. These actions are aimed to keep our customers' happy and satisfied with our products and services.</li>
<li>Be an advocate for customers and serve as the primary escalation path for critical customer issues while seeking out internal resources as needed to reach a solution as quickly as possible.</li>
<li>Proactively communicate project timelines to the customer, WideOrbit team members, and management.</li>
<li>Manage and own simultaneous client projects and initiatives (including software upgrades, third party integrations) while maintaining a positive, calm, and professional image to customers and WideOrbiters.</li>
<li>Coordinate software upgrades with Release Management and IT and communicate information back to relevant areas within WideOrbit.</li>
<li>Sell, negotiate, and manage Professional Service engagements.</li>
<li>Provide direct client application training onsite and/or via web-cast.</li>
<li>Optimize client satisfaction to ensure contract renewals and reference ability.</li>
<li>Document software gaps and analysis of custom code needs, where appropriate.</li>
</ul>
<b>Here is what we're looking for:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience in Cable Network Sales, Traffic, or Vendor Equivalent.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of cable network business practices.</li>
<li>1+ year of experience on WideOrbit products is strongly preferred. Advanced knowledge of Cable Network Traffic and Sales systems is required.</li>
<li>A. or B.S. in either a technical, business or media related discipline is preferred.</li>
<li>Shows confidence evangelizing and selling WideOrbit's products and services.</li>
<li>Know how to be resourceful to resolve problems and seek answers independently while raising your hand and engaging internal resources when you identify limitations about what you're able to accomplish independently.</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail and the ability to multi-task manage client initiatives to completion.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with a proven record of building strong professional relationships with internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Fluency in MS Windows, Excel, Word and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Confidence discussing and using technology with a strong aptitude to learn technical tools.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communications skills.</li>
<li>Capable of performing 100% independently, while fostering a team-based environment</li>
<li>Willingness and interest to be based on site at one of our WideOrbit customers.</li>
</ul>
