<h2>Senior Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The <b>Senior Product Manager </b>in the Kelley Blue Book International Business Development team works closely with Sr. Director of International Business Development with definitions of product strategies, vision and planning of all assigned products and then lead the products execution throughout the life cycle. This role will be responsible for analyzing existing and new technologies as well as staying in tune with current market developments and conducting competitive analysis.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
As a function of this role you will gather and prioritize product, stakeholder and customer requirements to develop specification according to current needs and support the product’s ongoing maintenance and enhancements. As a Senior Product Manager, you will also partner with software engineering team in overseeing and approving product designs, enhancements and quality. Additionally, you will interface with stakeholders in all Kelley Blue Book international markets, customers, sales and marketing to drive product direction and confirm capabilities meet market needs, to ensure revenue and customer satisfaction goals are exceeded. Key responsibilities:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Product Development &amp; Strategy</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Participates in defining product vision, strategy and product planning and execution for entire assigned products throughout the product life cycle</li>
<li>
Deliver product vision, project goals, release goals/acceptance criteria and desired business outcomes and prioritized features and products to be worked on to enable successful functional design and product development</li>
<li>
Responsible for working closely with all stakeholders (to ensure their interests are included in the release) and the team (to make sure the release is developed by the deadline and within budget).</li>
<li>
Formulate new product concepts or product enhancements to existing products to deliver on product revenue goals</li>
<li>
Builds and confirms the new product business case and vision with stakeholders</li>
<li>
Assists and leads enabling the requirements definition by Product Owners and stakeholders</li>
<li>
Measures and assesses client satisfaction with new product/project introductions</li>
<li>
Maintain a product roadmap for owned products</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Product Performance</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Monitor product financial, sales, and marketing metrics, interpret the data for trends, and formulate any corrective action</li>
<li>
Collects and analyzes customer preferences and needs to identify potential markets and factors affecting product demand. Conducts market research that will assist in driving feature prioritization.</li>
<li>
Analyze existing and new technologies as well as market developments and competition to develop product technical specifications and requirements according to needs</li>
<li>
Identify improvements and changes in process, packaging, positioning, messaging, and targeting in order to enhance product revenues and direct cost attributed to product</li>
<li>
Manage enhancement requests to current product lineup to affect product fit in the current year</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Sales and Marketing Enablement</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assists and leads getting key product information out to Sales and Marketing teams in order to enable marketing and sales activities around the assigned product</li>
<li>
May lead creating and giving sales and training presentations necessary to run campaigns and support customer visits</li>
<li>
Provides Support and Services organization with product knowledge for owned products</li>
<li>
Act as an advocate for his/her own products</li>
<li>
Recommend any process improvements and content necessary to enable Sales and Marketing of the owned products</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Product Support and Services Enablement</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Act as the subject matter expert for the owned products and provides or facilitates getting key information out to enable supporting and implementing the owned products</li>
<li>
Conduct product training, presentations and briefs as needed</li>
</ul></div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h4>
<div><b>
Required:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>BS/BA in related discipline or an equivalent combination of education and work related experience</li>
<li>7+ years’ experience in Product Management</li>
<li>Advanced interpersonal communication skills and ability to relate with and understand business audience and develop technology solutions</li>
<li>Experience delivering software and leveraging cloud-based technologies/services</li>
<li>Possess ability to relate with and understand business audience and develop technology solutions (a blend of both business and technical skills)</li>
<li>Experienced working with software engineering teams on complex products and working through all phases of development cycle in order to deliver product to market</li>
<li>Desire to work in a fast-paced environment where information must be readily digested and decisions made expeditiously</li>
<li>Proactive problem solving skills and the ability to balance multiple complex initiatives and tasks</li>
<li>Strong understanding of development processes, quality assurance, and project management methodologies</li>
<li>Must have excellent critical thinking skills - Using logic and reasoning to identify the strengths and weaknesses of alternative solutions, conclusions or approaches to problems</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written, presentation, analytical, and listening skills</li>
<li>
Proficiency in MS Excel, PowerPoint, Rally, and Visio (or other flowcharting tool)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Knowledgeable of or experience in the Automotive industry with an understanding of vehicle valuation products and service</li>
<li>
Experience working with international team</li>
<li>
Product and/or product management specific courses and certifications</li>
<li>
Experience in start-up organization</li>
</ul>
