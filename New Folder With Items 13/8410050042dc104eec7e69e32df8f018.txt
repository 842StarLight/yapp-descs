<h2>Dealership Porter / Photographer for San Mateo / Redwood City areas</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Pro-MotionPix is growing and looking for a Full Time Automotive Dealership Photographer / Porter to join our team in the San Mateo and Redwood City areas. The position is integral to our business, and will be responsible for servicing our excellent customer base by providing top-notch customer service and superior quality automotive photos. This full-time opportunity offers competitive compensation, great benefits, and a schedule of 8:30 am to 5:00 pm, Monday - Friday. It is perfect for one who enjoys life outside the confines of the cubicle.</p>
<p>Using your own reliable vehicle for transportation, job duties include following the scheduled stops, arriving at each dealership, walking all areas of the lot to find the cars on your list, moving the car to a staging area (<b>must be able to drive stick</b>), shooting a specific series of photos, and putting the car back. You will also need to talk to the manager at the dealership to let them know what was shot, what could not be shot for various reasons, etc.</p>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>

<li>Partner with dealership and company management to get the job done as efficiently as possible.</li>
<li>Represent Pro-MotionPix as the face of the company in your territory.</li>
</ul>
