<p><b>
Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Evolent Health is looking for a Senior Director, Finance to be a key member of the growing Finance team. This individual will join a small, fast-paced team of FP&amp;A professionals at a growth-oriented public company, and will play a critical role in executing Evolent Health’s mission by ensuring that the company is able to plan for growth, execute on and track performance vs. budget, as well as make informed executive decisions backed by financial and strategic analysis. Reporting initially to the VP of Finance, the Senior Director, Finance will have primary responsibility for: managing a dedicated team that provides end-to-end budgeting and forecasting processes for the company’s portfolio of Services business unit, driving improvements to unit profitability, generating actionable strategic insights from financial and operational data, and driving successful implementation of improvements.</p>
<p>
The Senior Director, Finance will work closely with the CEO of the Services business unit, Sales, Accounting, Operations, Talent, and other key executives, providing a unique breadth of exposure throughout the company. We are looking for someone with an entrepreneurial, hands-on approach who is also capable of collaborating with many different stakeholders to delve deep into operational and financial data to provide insights and inform business decision-making.</p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Be Doing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage and guide FP&amp;A analysts as they: build and maintain budget and near- and long-term forecast models, track variances against budget plans and provide operational feedback to department heads and create other financial and operational reports/analyses.</li>
<li>
Drive design and maintenance of actionable operational dashboards that enable senior leadership to fully understand the state of the business unit. Extract insights from these reports to help senior team focus on what data truly matter.</li>
<li>
Drive consistent improvement in operating margins by discovering and highlighting areas where the business can systematically improve profitability.</li>
<li>
Work with sales team to assess financial impacts of potential new client contracts.</li>
<li>
Manage the annual and quarterly budgeting and re-budgeting efforts.</li>
<li>
Carrying out other special projects as required.</li>
<li>
Manage a team of direct FP&amp;A reports, providing mentorship, guidance, and career development opportunities. Set objectives for team and delegate responsibilities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The Experience You’ll Need (Required):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Business, Finance, Accounting or related field</li>
<li>
5-10 years of experience in FP&amp;A, investment banking, consulting, or other business environment where you developed a deep understanding of business financial metrics, driving an organization toward financial goals, and the ability to discern operational issues and opportunities from financial data</li>
<li>
Experience in taking abstract business scenarios, identifying the key drivers and components, and translating into financial and operational models</li>
<li>
Exceptional oral and written communication skills, including the ability to distill complex, data-rich topics to a persuasive, broadly-understandable narrative</li>
<li>
Strong organization skills and attention to details</li>
<li>
Experience managing, coaching, and developing team members</li>
<li>
Ability to execute against multiple projects/deadlines and manage key stakeholders while moving projects forward and maintaining high work quality</li>
<li>
Strong familiarity with Excel modeling and other FP&amp;A systems (while you will have direct reports with responsibility for owning most modeling functions, you will need to be able to validate their results and coach them)</li>
<li>
Track record of success taking initiative, solving problems, and going above and beyond to get to get things done, especially under tight deadlines/time pressures</li>
</ul>
