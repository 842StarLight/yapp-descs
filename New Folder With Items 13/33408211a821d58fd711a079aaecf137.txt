<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Company is one of North America’s leading sales and marketing agencies specializing in outsourced sales, merchandising, category management and marketing services to manufacturers, suppliers and producers of food products and consumer packaged goods. The Company services a variety of trade channels including grocery, mass merchandise, specialty, convenience, drug, dollar, club, hardware, consumer electronics and home centers. We bridge the gap between manufacturers and retailers, providing consumers access to the best products available in the marketplace today.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Position Summary</b><br>
The Business Development Specialist manages financial information for Client’s key promotion trade plans. Create financial plans and forecast spend based on allotted budget for promotions. Analyze, identify, and resolve potential promotion funding issues/conflicts and communicate resolution(s) to Business Development Manager (BDM) and/or Client.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Essential Job Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
<b>Promotional Trade Plan</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Forecast, design, and conduct business and financial models for Client promotional trade plans</li>
<li>
Manage financial information including balance sheet/accounts for trade plans; prepare financial analysis for period of statements; prepare reconciliations, schedules, and account analysis</li>
<li>
Research and report on factors influencing business performance</li>
<li>
Assess financial implications of new or existing plans</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance with Company’s &amp; Client’s standards for financial management</li>
<li>
Implement annual and item(s) specific promotional plans<ul>
<li>
Financial record keeping at all local divisions</li>
<li>
Monitor spending plans to ensure that promotions are actualized and all deductions have been received</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Analyze, identify, and resolve potential promotional or funding issues or conflicts and communicate resolution to BDM and Client</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Presentation and Sales Plan</b></div>
<ul><li>
Partner with company Category Management team to build sales presentations, new items, selling promotions, pricing, and planogram changes</li></ul>
<div>
Ensure company business partners understand use of Client’s promotional fund management system to avoid and ensure conflicts do not arise with client as a result of poor performance</div>
<ul>
<li>
Effectively train company BAA and BDM in the field on use of financial system</li>
<li>
Implement new practices/protocols/procedures or revisions to existing ones for Client’s core fund management operations 60</li>
</ul>
