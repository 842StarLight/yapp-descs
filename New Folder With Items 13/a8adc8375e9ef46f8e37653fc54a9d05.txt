<h2>Assistant POS Installer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
1. Assist lead installer during installation process.<br>
2. Run CAT5e cables and terminate end of cables..<br>
3. Install hardware in ceilings and under counters..<br>
4. Troubleshoot connectivity and software problems..<br>
5. Train McDonald’s employees on system operation..<br>
6. Work on help desk when not traveling..<br>
7. Attend periodic training sessions on software, hardware, and problem solving<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>

High school diploma.<br>
Technical Training preferred, but not necessary<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
Work Ready Certificate is preferred before you apply. Please contact Kansas Works to obtain a Work Ready Certificate if you do not already have one or need a copy<br>

<b>Job Requirements</b> (specific experience, skills, knowledge, certifications, licenses, competencies, working conditions)<br>
<br>
Basic understanding of DOS commands.<br>
Basic understanding of Windows OS.<br>
Must be willing to work from a ladder.<br>
Has a good command of the English language.<br>
Possess good phone etiquette and has the ability to communicate well with others.<br>
Follows direction well.<br>
Has the customer’s wants and needs always in mind.<br>
Knows the basic art of troubleshooting.<br>
Willing to travel 2-3 weeks a month.<br>
Must have a valid drivers license.<br>
Ability to work flexible shifts.<br>
Must pass pre-employment and random drug tests.<br>
Has not been convicted of a felony in the past 10 years.<br>
Has not been charged with a DUI within the past 5 years.<br>
Must be a team player.<br>

Help Desk Support.
