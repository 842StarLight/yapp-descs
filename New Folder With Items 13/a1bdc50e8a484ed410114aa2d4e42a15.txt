<h2>Proposal Developer 1</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>Q2 Solutions is a leading clinical trial laboratory services organization with end-to-end laboratory services and secure, enterprise-wide biospecimen and consent management solutions. With a relentless focus on quality and innovation, Q2 Solutions uses its global experience and scientific expertise to transform science and data into actionable medical insights that help customers improve human health. A joint venture of IQVIA (formerly QuintilesIMS) and Quest Diagnostics, Q2 Solutions combines the best of each parent organization’s clinical trials laboratory services capabilities to fulfill its mission of treating each sample as if a life depends on it.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
PURPOSE<br>
Under general supervision, develop and prepare proposals for assigned customers, to support the Global sales team.</p>
<p>
RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>
Review Requests For Proposal (RFP). Identify information for proposal text and budget development and work closely with proposal development team to prepare and finalise proposals</li>
<li>
Prepare text and budget for proposals, rebids and related documents</li>
<li>
Provide support and assistance to other Proposal Developers in the delivery of assigned opportunities</li>
<li>
May act as Team Lead on assigned projects, to develop proposals and budgets</li>
<li>
Participate in strategy calls to discuss customer requirements and identify discrepancies in customer information</li>
<li>
Perform quality control edits on all documents and participate in the finalization of documents and distribution to the customer and internal departments</li>
<li>
Update and maintain corporate databases as required and ensure accurate information is included in tracking reports</li>
<li>
May participate in customer meetings</li>
<li>
Perform all other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>
REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>
Knowledge of CRO Industry</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Microsoft Excel, Word and understanding of costing models</li>
<li>
Ability to interpret RFPs and protocols</li>
<li>
Possess strong analytical skills Ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships with coworkers, managers and customers</li>
</ul>
<p>MINIMUM REQUIRED EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</p>
<ul><li>
Degree in Life Science, Business Management or related field and a minimum of 1 year's direct experience in Proposals; or equivalent combination of education, training and experience</li></ul>
<p>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS</p>
<ul>
<li>
Extensive use of telephone and face-to-face communication requiring accurate perception of speech</li>
<li>
Extensive use of keyboard requiring repetitive motion of fingers</li>
</ul>
