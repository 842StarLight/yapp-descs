<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>The Final Mile Business Development Manager plays a critical role that supports Final Mile field sales activities, works closely with the Vice President of Final Mile Business Development to coordinate the formulation and execution of Final Mile sales and marketing strategy, and actively drives inside sales for qualified low volume and transactional network shippers. The incumbent will be trained in all aspects of the Final Mile sales process that includes, but is not limited to: development of business opportunities that will ultimately result in deal closures; application of pricing and design strategies within the company business model that will meet the customer’s needs; and contract negotiations with customers including Fortune 500 companies seeking to outsource their transportation needs. Furthermore, the candidate will be instrumental in designing and applying process improvements and building cross-functional relationships that help to streamline the Final Mile business development process for all types of customers and J.B. Hunt operations.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p><b>The successful candidate for this role will:
</b></p>
<p><b>Lead sales operations functions for the Final Mile sales team
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize excellent oral and written communication skills to develop business opportunities and provide outsourced freight delivery services by utilizing various methods of communication and interaction including but not limited to telephone and face to face contact with potential customers. These interactions may result in assigning an opportunity to a Business Development Executive or in executing the opportunity using the Final Mile Inside Sales process.
</li>
<li>Evaluate and determine whether prospective business opportunities are prospects through a prescribed account qualification process.
</li>
<li>Communicate and demonstrate service offerings by the company by using collateral materials, PowerPoint demonstrations and the like. Work with support groups such as pricing, legal and engineering to obtain information that will provide optimal possible solutions for the prospective client
</li>
<li>Work with Business Development Executives to cultivate and prepare a pipeline of contacts and leads that can ultimately be developed into a contractual relationship.
</li>
<li>Maintain status of activity and documentation of the qualification process within the company sales activity database
 </li>
<li>Act as a sales activity system (Dynamics, Athena) Superuser: be a conduit of communications about updates, functionality, tips and troubleshooting for the Final Mile sales team
</li>
<li>Vet and route inbound leads from website, finalmile@jbhunt.com , the toll free number and enterprise and DCS sources.
</li>
<li>Represent Final Mile at the corporate office, at trade shows and industry events as deemed appropriate by the Vice President.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Provide strategic support for the Vice President, Final Mile Business Development
</b></p>
<p>Maintain updated top target lists by target industry, manage subscriptions and other industry knowledge management.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate Trade Show and Sales Market activities for the director and team, including but not limited to managing a master trade show information file and owning the relationship with trade show contacts for Final Mile.
</li>
<li>Work with cross-functional subject matter experts to build and maintain an RFP Reference folder to aid BDEs with general information about J.B. Hunt and Final Mile as they respond to bids or seek standard answers to questions about the program.
</li>
<li>Lead logistics activities for team meetings either in the field or in Lowell.
</li>
<li>Coordinate special projects related to Final Miles sales strategy as the need arises, including organizing competitive information, working with marketing on campaigns, and other items that help to drive sales growth and improve internal sales processes
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Manage Inside Sales functions for Final Mile
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with cross functional team to lead sales process engineering to create efficient, practical and profitable workflows for customers, many with less than 7000 annual stops
</li>
<li>Qualify inbound leads and convert qualified leads into Final Mile revenue following the processes developed. Partner with Final Mile Complete and Carrier Management team to provide end to end solutions until a single-order solution is deployed.
</li>
<li>Upon completion of the process build phase and formal commencement of the Inside Sales program, build a sales target list and actively solicit qualified Final Mile customers.
</li>
<li>Be able to meet or exceed quota expectations set forth by the sales director
</li>
</ul>
