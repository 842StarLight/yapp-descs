<h2>Customer Success Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p>DXC Technology (NYSE: DXC) is the world’s leading independent, end-to-end IT services company, helping clients harness the power of innovation to thrive on change. Created by the merger of CSC and the Enterprise Services business of Hewlett Packard Enterprise, DXC Technology serves nearly 6,000 private and public sector clients across 70 countries. The company’s technology independence, global talent and extensive partner alliance combine to deliver powerful next-generation IT services and solutions. DXC Technology is recognized among the best corporate citizens globally. For more information, visit www.dxc.technology.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job description</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
This role will be a key driver in the Digital Transformation of DXC’s clients.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
This Role will lead the client engagements for the full breadth of Platform DXC deployment, hybrid and client customizations, and ensure the integration of the of DXC offerings from a client perspective. They will be the face of Platform DXC to the client. They will also be the link into the DTC, and the broader Bionix team in general. They will extend this client responsibility to be engaged at the deal phase to ensure carriage of the solution from design to implementation.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The person is expected to:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Engage in Decentralized Decision-Making</li>
<li>
Enable the Continuous Delivery Pipeline and DevOps</li>
<li>
Embrace a Leadership Role</li>
<li>
Act as Change Agents</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Location</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
These teams will be linked to a DTC within a region but will spend significant time directly engaged with the clients.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
The role will have the following responsibilities:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead planning, definition, and high-level design of the solution and explore solution alternatives for client engagements</li>
<li>
Work with customers, stakeholders, and suppliers to establish high-level Solution Intent, and the solution intent information models and documentation requirements</li>
<li>
Actively participate in the Continuous Exploration process as part of the Continuous Delivery Pipeline, especially with enabler Epics</li>
<li>
Define subsystems and their interfaces, allocate responsibilities to subsystems, understand solution deployment, and communicate requirements for interactions with solution context</li>
<li>
Establish critical Non-Functional Requirements at the solution level, participate in the definition of others</li>
<li>
Operate within the Economic Framework to validate the economic impact of design decisions</li>
<li>
Work with portfolio stakeholders to develop, analyze, split, and realize the implementation of enabler epics</li>
<li>
Participate in Program Increment (PI) Planning and Pre- and Post-PI Planning, System and Solution Demos, and Inspect and Adapt events</li>
<li>
Define, explore, and support the implementation of ART and Solution Train Enablers to evolve solution intent, working directly with the DTC Agile teams to implement them</li>
<li>
Work with Product and Solution Management to determine the capacity allocation for enablement work</li>
<li>
Support technology/engineering aspects of Program and Solution Kanban</li>
<li>
Provide oversight and foster Built-In Quality and Team and Technical Agility</li>
</ul>
