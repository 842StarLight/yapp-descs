<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Job Objective(s)</b><br>
Working in a collaborative environment, show and lease rental units to prospective residents, assist and support the Community Manager in marketing and administrative assistant responsibilities as needed.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>At all times, utilize Fair Housing standards and practices as defined by state and federal law.</li>
<li>Provide tours and explanation of services to potential residents, families and friends.</li>
<li>Insure that an adequate supply of printed leasing materials and, if available, promotional give-a-ways, is/are available to be given to prospective residents.</li>
<li>Maintain up-to-date knowledge of the project’s rental criteria, unit availability, features and benefits, market rents and current leasing specials.</li>
<li>Maintain current knowledge of the property’s competitor’s rental criteria, unit availability features and benefits, market rents and current leasing specials.</li>
<li>Prepares all advertising and outreach materials with assistance from Community Manager.</li>
<li>Respond to inquiries regarding unit availability and property information while creating excitement for, and encouraging the prospect to schedule, an appointment to visit the property.</li>
<li>Maintain current prospect and leasing data on the property’s guest card and in its property management system.</li>
<li>Update and maintain weekly occupancy reports and Executive Summaries.</li>
<li>Ascertain the prospective resident’s needs, wants and financial parameters with regard to housing and conduct leasing tours of units which meet those needs, wants and financial parameters.</li>
<li>Encourage prospective residents to complete the project’s rental application and submit a security deposit to “hold” a specific apartment. Communicates with applicants by completing approved, cancel and deny letters which are to be sent the same day after submission by the applicant.</li>
<li>Submit prospect’s application to the community’s screening company and presents results to Community Manager as required.</li>
<li>Perform daily follow-up of clients, leads and files.</li>
<li>Prepare lease-related paperwork in an accurate and timely manner and obtain the signature of the Community Manager and the resident on the Residential Lease.</li>
<li>Review with new residents all lease related documents including, but not limited to, the Residential Lease, addenda and Rules and Regulations and, with the new resident, conduct a formal move-in inspection and home orientation.</li>
<li>Ensures quality of cleanliness of all vacant units and common areas.</li>
<li>Consistently meets or exceeds sales expectations.</li>
<li>Participate in continuing education programs and attend seminars to maintain awareness of marketing, sales and industry ideas.</li>
<li>Assists in coordination of marketing activities designed to expose the property to the local community.</li>
<li>Deal professionally and courteously on the phone and in person with residents, visitors, staff and all others to maintain confidentiality and sensitivity.</li>
<li>Accurately take messages to insure that urgent matters are given to the relevant staff quickly and accurately.</li>
<li>Respond to emergencies including Fire, Water (Severe &amp; Snow Storms), Power outages following all Policies and Procedures.</li>
<li>Receives and responds to all maintenance requests by write up and follow through to completion.</li>
<li>Complete miscellaneous duties as assigned in the time frame designated.</li>
<li>Perform all other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<b>Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent required.</li>
<li>Able to read, write, spell, perform basic math and speak and understand English.</li>
<li>Able to work with frequent interruptions.</li>
<li>Proven record of success in leasing and a personal commitment to customer service and Fair Housing practices.</li>
<li>Knowledge of office practices, procedures, systems and equipment including, but not limited to, Microsoft applications Word, Outlook and Excel.</li>
<li>Good interpersonal skills including the ability to communicate clearly and effectively.</li>
<li>Sensitivity while interacting with diverse groups of people.</li>
<li>The ability to self-motivate and work independently in the pursuit of personal and team goals.</li>
<li>Commitment to protect personal, property and other confidential information.</li>
<li>Possess the ability to interact tactfully with residents, staff, owners, other management personnel, corporate representatives, vendors and the general public.</li>
<li>Understand and adhere to organizational policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Maintain a neat, well-groomed and professional appearance.</li>
</ul>
