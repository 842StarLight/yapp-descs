<h2>Pricing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Pricing Coordinator will support Program Management and Sales with coordination of pricing and costing while maintaining a high level of data integrity in deadline-driven environment.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and update pricing/costing on accounts as assigned including customer specific outputs.
</li>
<li>Create and distribute time sensitive documents to internal/external customers.
</li>
<li>Interface with other departments for item sourcing needs and pricing issues as needed.
</li>
<li>Manage group mailbox for incoming documents and problem resolution as needed.
</li>
<li>Responsible for timely management of vendor increases/decreases for assigned accounts.
</li>
<li>Assign and research customers with appropriate pricing and costing.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Skills / Behaviors:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High Integrity and Work Ethic
</li>
<li>Detail Oriented
</li>
<li>Excellent Organizational Skills
</li>
<li>Strong Customer Service Skills (Friendly/Courteous/Helpful)
</li>
<li>Strong Communication Skills (Listening/Verbal/Written)
</li>
<li>Excellent follow-up skills
</li>
<li>Ability to work in team environment
</li>
<li>Maintain operational efficiency and data accuracy on pricing
</li>
<li>Exhibits level of motivation and sense of urgency
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Experience/Education:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma Required or College Degree Preferred
</li>
<li>2 years general business experience, required
</li>
<li>Proficiency in all PC software applications (MS Office Suite products) to include spreadsheets (formulas), word processing, email, etc., required
</li>
</ul>
