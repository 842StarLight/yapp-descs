<h2>Patient Care Associate (COTA/PTA)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Who we are:</p>
<p>H-Wave is leading the way in drug-free rehabilitation and pain relief for those injured at work or at play. We are well-established with a solid reputation of results since 1981. H-Wave technology is used by over 100 professional sports teams in nearly every major sport, because nothing works better for creating immediate pain relief and sustained tissue recovery. It’s a multi-functional electrical stimulation device intended to speed recovery, restore function, and manage chronic, acute or post-operative pain. It is a non-invasive drug-free alternative treatment option without harmful side effects.</p>
<p>The opportunity:</p>
<p>An H-Wave Patient Care Associate, (COTA/PTA) offers the unique ability to genuinely help patients, while enjoying the excitement and freedom that comes as a member of the outside sales team. PCA’s work regularly with clinicians and patients to provide drug-free pain relief and functional improvement for people recovering from injury, surgery, or chronic pain. This is a full-time position and a great opportunity to work in a position serving patients. The role is flexible with hours.</p>
<p>If you’re an optimistic and energetic person who enjoys meeting new people daily? Can you facilitate information while fitting an array of patients? Has your communication type been described as fast paced, extremely detailed, naturally persuasive, and extremely coordinated on present goals? If this sounds like you, then you might be our new Patient Care Associate!</p>
