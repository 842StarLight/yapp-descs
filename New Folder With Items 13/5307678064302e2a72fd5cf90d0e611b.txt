<h2>Inventory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Chick-fil-A Bellevue is a premier fast-casual restaurants, in search of a competent Inventory Manager to monitor and report on the company’s inventory. Your job is important since the efficient handling of the restaurant's products and supplies is critical for the attainment of business goals. </b></p>
<p>*<br>The successful Inventory Manager is a reliable professional with excellent record-keeping abilities. They possess great attention to detail and a business mindset. The goal is to ensure that all restaurant operations have adequate material to achieve their objectives.<br>*<br>*<br>The Inventory Manager will demonstrate ownership with inventory strategies to maximize sales, margin, and turns and play a critical role in helping Chick-fil-A Bellevue to build, manage and optimize a procurement system that continues to evolve and expand.<br>*<br>*<br>This candidate will be involved with all aspects of the Kitchen &amp; Front of House inventory planning and reporting. They must have proven successes in operational initiatives and demonstrate a passion for the customer experience. They must be fearless but thoughtful in their pursuit of results and thrive in Chick-fil-A high energy environments where tactical and strategic activities are expected to our high-level hospitality service.<br>Responsibilities*<br>*</p>
<ul><li>Devise ways to optimize inventory control procedures</li></ul>
<ul><li>Inspect the levels of restaurant supplies and raw material to identify shortages</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure product stock is adequate for all distribution channels and can cover direct demand from customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Record daily deliveries and shipments to reconcile inventory</li></ul>
<ul><li>Use software to monitor demand and document characteristics of inventory</li></ul>
<ul><li>Place orders to replenish stock avoiding insufficiency or excessive surplus</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyze data to anticipate future needs at the restaurant</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate with kitchen managers and other staff to ensure business goals are met</li></ul>
<ul><li>Report to upper management on stock levels, issues, etc.</li></ul>
<p>Qualifications<br>*<br>*<br>Strong candidates will have hands-on operations or inventory management experience and knowledge in fundamental business principles. Candidates must demonstrate the ability to succeed in managing a business efficiently, identifying problems/finding solutions, and building/improving processes. The ideal candidate is able to self-lead and maintain a customer-centric philosophy.<br>*</p>
