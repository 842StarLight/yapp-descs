<h2>Hardware Technical Product Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>Verizon Connect is guiding a connected world on the go by automating, optimizing and revolutionizing the way people, vehicles and things move through the world. Our full suite of industry-defining solutions and services put innovation, automation and connected data to work for customers and help them be safer, more efficient and more productive. As a SaaS leader, we know our talent is the most important component to our success. We hire top talent and empower them to do their best work. As a fast growing division within Verizon, we combine the fun and excitement of a start-up environment with the resources, operational excellence, and brand recognition of an established tech giant.</p>
<p>
This is a highly visible role as a Technical Product Manager / Product Owner within the hardware product team and will require you to bring a love for complex IOT systems, highly technical information, and leadership capabilities to clearly define the next generation of devices using the best from Verizon. You will influence your cross-functional teams and stakeholders to chart a new path for our intelligent devices ecosystem for our global markets</p>
<p>
Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage Product Execution through Lean or Agile Development.</li>
<li>
Manage product development lifecycle, prioritize feature development, create execution plans and identify development teams to execute the hardware &amp; devices roadmap.</li>
<li>
Work with the Product Managers, Product Marketing and UX teams as well as research teams to understand customer and market needs. Synthesize customer insights from data.</li>
<li>
Use lean approaches to experiment quickly with an aim to find product-market fit.</li>
<li>
Employ design thinking approaches to identify &amp; solve truly valuable customer problems.</li>
<li>
Partner with UX teams to create compelling experience strategies. Participate and lead user experience interviews and testing to gather continuous customer insights.</li>
<li>
Define product requirements – writing and prioritizing use cases- Write Agile-based stories that conform to best practices including acceptance criteria, positive and negative paths, etc.</li>
<li>
Incorporate feedback &amp; input from customers, partners and teams on product requirements.</li>
<li>
Work with stakeholders to actively and effectively plan work for Development and QA which balances urgent customer needs and strategic roadmap priorities.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Go to Market &amp; Assess Market performance</p>
<ul>
<li>
Ensure products and releases are launched correctly and on schedule. Gather customer feedback, run advisory session, UAT sessions and measure/manage NPS post launch.</li>
<li>
Partner with marketing, sales, and operations on defining &amp; flawless execution of GTM plans.</li>
<li>
Quickly root cause product issues and manage customer/ exec expectations. Synthesize your own analysis and succinct recommendations for a path forward.</li>
<li>
Support to Implementation, Customer &amp; App Support teams post launch stabilization activities.</li>
<li>
Lead product documentation during the Go-to-Market process for new features.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Stakeholder Management</p>
<ul>
<li>
Operates in a highly matrixed, globally distributed organization.</li>
<li>
Manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced and changing environment.</li>
<li>
Clearly &amp; effectively communicate the roadmaps, product plans, and availability to internal stakeholder teams to ensure alignment and cross-group readiness.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What we’re looking for...</b>
</p>
<p>You’ll need to have:</p>
<ul><li>
Bachelor’s degree or relevant work experience.</li></ul>
<p>
Even better if you have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
A degree or Master’s degree in engineering w/ high technical aptitude. Areas such as Industrial, Systems, Mechanical, and technology discipline. MBA/MIS/Business degree.</li>
<li>
Five or more years of experience as Product Owner/Business Analyst in a technical environment creating superior user experiences for hardware and software products.</li>
<li>
Experience operating in an agile &amp; lean /kanban product dev environment, comfortable interacting with both hardware and software development processes.</li>
<li>
Experience in managing and building consumer electronics hardware or devices.</li>
<li>
Experience managing or architecting hardware systems, IoT / industrial devices.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of modern software development technologies and languages.</li>
<li>
Experience performing data queries in Database, validate data quality and help steer API specification definition. Hands on experience of SQL, JSON and/or XML.</li>
<li>
Self-directed and collaborative &amp; cultivate relationships with teams &amp; partners.</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal, communication, presentation, and writing skills.</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel.</li>
<li>
Experience working with cross functional teams and exposure to working with UX teams.</li>
<li>
Experience working on projects involving system integration or with a focus on optimizing technical solution architecture.</li>
</ul>
