Job Description
<br>---------------
<br><br>
Vlocity Advisory Services (Program Architects) provide trusted Industry-specific business and enterprise-level technical advice to major customer organizations globally.
<br><br>
We work within the broader Vlocity Customer Success team and serve as the Customer Advocate to ensure success throughout the Customer life cycle.
<br><br>
The Vlocity Program Architect will be well versed in using the Vlocity Industry Cloud Applications and Methodologies strategically.
<br><br>
A Vlocity Program Architect will engage our Customers, from CxO to program level, to understand their business priorities, their technology architecture landscape and digital strategic aims in order to ensure effective digital transformation with great holistic success.
<br><br>
<b>We live the Vlocity values in everything we do:</b> Customers First, Integrity, People at the Core, Innovation, Speed matters, Giving Back &amp; We are all shareholders.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with customer to understand and execute against the long term strategy</li>
<li>Be the overall Vlocity customer advocate for enabling the digital transformation by actively engaging full-time to providing solution/technical guidance and demonstrating leadership</li>
<li>Take accountability for the Vlocity architecture and design. Provide design guidance/patterns to customer, other SIs and implementation team</li>
<li>Ensure you have a deep understanding of the Vertical Industry</li>
<li>Continuously provide best practices to ensure the customer receives desired returns on investment</li>
<li>Be a conduit to Vlocity product and engineering team on behalf of the customer. Align customer on future roadmap and upgrades</li>
<li>Become highly certified and recognized Vlocity platform expert</li>
<li>Participate in specific governance meetings where Vlocity needs representation</li>
<li>Build CxO, business, IT and enterprise architect level relationships within the customer team. Being able to influence leaders from customer teams is crucial</li>
<li>Contribute to the growth of PA program across Vlocity globally. Mentor newer team members on being a top performer in that role</li>
<li>Measuring Program Architect value-add to the customer and ensure high stickiness</li>
</ul>
