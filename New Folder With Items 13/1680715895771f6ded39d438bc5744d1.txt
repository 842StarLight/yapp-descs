<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Indiba USA Marketing Coordinator Job Description<br>
</b></p>
<p><br>
We seek an enthusiastic Marketing Coordinator to join our growing team! The person to land this role will ensure that all projects get the resources and attention they need to be successful. This growth-oriented position will work closely, across a number of different departments and report directly to the Sales Director. The key quality we’re looking for is an energetic, self-starter whether you’re just out of college or have a few years of marketing experience.</p>
<p>
The Marketing Coordinator creates and manages marketing content across multiple platforms and formats to drive sales, engagement, retention, leads and positive customer behavior. The position collaborates with Sales, and Operations to develop meaningful content across marketing channels including public relations, social media, web, email and video.<br>
<br>
<b>Major Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Take a leading role with Head Instructor, in developing our Indiba Activ Educational Progam.</li>
<li>Be our trade show champion! Will manage and coordinate all aspects related to our trade show events. You’ll be the face of our brand so you will have to bring your “A” Game!</li>
<li>Attend national trade shows throughout the year (30% domestic travel)</li>
<li>Coordinate trade show preparation for national and regional trade shows</li>
<li>Communicate all necessary trade show information to third party exhibit company as well as our internal booth staff</li>
<li>Staff trade show and promotional events throughout the year</li>
<li>Create, deliver, edit and optimize marketing materials across a number of platforms. Creativity and design experience is a required, high organization a must.</li>
<li>Hands-on involvement in a variety of marketing logistics; pertaining to trade shows, customer events and internal sales &amp; management meetings. Talent in PowerPoint and Excel a must.</li>
<li>Establish initial contact with organizations</li>
<li>Work with Sales Director to determine event budget and manage expenses to that budget</li>
<li>Develop schedules and assignments as they pertain to each event</li>
<li>Create and send emails to database of contacts through SalesForce</li>
<li>Assist other team members with day-to-day marketing tasks and coordinate marketing projects and activities as requested. Teamwork will be key.</li>
<li>Requires effective communication with marketing department at headquarters in Barcelona.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, management, or advertising.</li>
<li>MUST be a team player and be willing to wear many different hats!</li>
<li>Friendly, outgoing and energetic. Must be a people person.</li>
<li>Hungry to learn and adapt in a new environment. Willingness to be coached along the way.</li>
<li>Ability to cope in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Self-starter with the ability to manage projects and schedules with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Impeccable attention to detail.</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Strong time management skills are a must.</li>
<li>Design software experience is a must.</li>
<li>Experienced user of Microsoft Excel, Word, PowerPoint and Outlook.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Public Relations is a plus.</li>
<li>2+ years’ marketing and/or advertising experience is a plus.</li>
</ul>
