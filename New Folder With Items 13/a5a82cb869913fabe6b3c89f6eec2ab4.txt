<h2>Sr Product Manager-Payments</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>As a Sr. Product Manager for Payments, you will help set and execute on our strategy to enable payments and payment settling for our customers. You will help to build a more robust platform for payments orchestration, validation and processing, and you will work closely with other product teams, sales, marketing, professional services and engineering.<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Location: REMOTE</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Take ownership and accountability over the entire lifecycle of product management for our payments platform, from strategy to portfolio management to GTM to post-launch operations and monitoring</div></li>
<li><div>
Define the vision and the customer need - own and drive the customer working backwards strategy, tenets, long-term goals and documentation; including being able to write product requirements for the product development team to address functional and non-functional capabilities</div></li>
<li><div>
Use customer and market feedback, competitive analysis and business metrics to inform direction</div></li>
<li><div>
Own the one-to-two year vision for the payments product portfolio, including making decisions regarding where we prioritize our efforts</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure implementation is aligned with product goals and requirements; makes necessary adjustments</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop Go To Market (GTM) plan and deliver results that ensure the customer and business goals are met in operational launch plans</div></li>
<li><div>
Leverage data analysis skills to research difficult or ambiguous problems, leading cross-disciplinary teams where necessary and communicating recommendations to senior leadership</div></li>
</ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
7+ years of work experience as a Product Manager, Technical Product Manager or relevant experience BA/BS degree or equivalent relevant experience required, MBA preferred</li>
<li>
 Knowledge of core banking system API integration a plus Knowledge of electronic payments acquisition, host processing systems and payments regulatory requirements</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Scrum and Agile methodologies and practices preferred Knowledge of creating payments Epics, User Stories and being a subject matter experts in Payments ecosystem</li>
<li>
Successful candidate will be a tireless advocate of the needs of our customer - both our creators and payers</li>
<li>
Must have an excellent product/design sense, and you can take complex problems and craft simple experiences</li>
<li>
Understand when and how to leverage SME experts and run product proof of concepts</li>
<li>
Detail oriented and strive for operational excellence</li>
<li>
Ability to translate your product ideas into expected business impact and results Must have demonstrated ability to lead complex cross-functional projects through launch while handling an incredible number of moving pieces</li>
<li>
Experience working on a global solution with familiarity with cloud, on-premise and SaaS deployment models, APIs, multi-channel, multi-country requirements and an E2E understanding of payments processing</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned or required</li>
</ul>
