 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div>

<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Micron Technology’s vision is to transform how the world uses information to enrich life and our commitment to people, innovation, tenacity, collaboration, and customer focus allows us to fulfill our mission to be a global leader in memory and storage solutions. This means conducting business with integrity, accountability, and professionalism while supporting our global community.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Director, Business Intelligence – Sales<b> </b>will transform the Business Intelligence (BI) Tools and Analytics technology needed in the sales organization to drive visualization of data delivered to the field organization. This leader will define our data structure roadmap and work closely with all elements of the sales operation’s organization. As an industry thought leader, s/he will educate and transforming teams' abilities thru the adoption of agile practices necessary for the successful execution and delivery of the strategic vision. This role will work with cross functional teams to create analytic capabilities, providing decision makers with actionable business intelligence. You will partner closely with Business and IT functional teams to understand business processes, learn the data, and craft solutions to solve problems.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
To achieve expected outcomes, it requires that this Director has practical application and contextual understanding of BI Strategy &amp; Visualization, ML &amp; AI, Data Virtualization, Visual Analytics, Mobile-enabled BI, and BI-on-the-Cloud. S/he can successfully craft an actionable and measurable roadmap that enables sales to execute and effectively measure value and adoption for all users of solutions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This Director's background includes extensive related senior level management experience with strengths in developing and implementing policies, strategies, and initiatives in a multi-stakeholder environment. Must be highly skilled in building relationships and influencing decisions that impact diverse stakeholders and have been recognized for a broad capacity to inspire commitment to innovation and business excellence. Significant emphasis will be placed on recognizing opportunities for innovation to ensure Micron's leadership position within an increasingly competitive industry landscape. Taking all of the above into consideration, the Director of BI Analytics &amp; Technologies is able to consistently model servant leadership capabilities toward his/her team and customers that reinforces a culture of trust, communication and collaboration in the working environment - while keeping 'Fun' in the forefront!</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities include, but not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead BI Solution lifecycle through requirement gathering, analysis, design, data-modeling, testing and deployment</li>
<li>
Architect, design, prototype solutions to support business strategies and deliver business value</li>
<li>
Communicate with users to ensure BI deliverables are providing business value</li>
<li>
Work with end users and IT teams to resolve operational issues and mitigate risk</li>
<li>
Help business users use the appropriate BI techniques and tools</li>
<li>
Work with project teams to document business requirements definitions, including business opportunity, vision of solution and expected benefits</li>
<li>
Review project success criteria ensuring deliverable and key partner alignment</li>
<li>
Build relationships with business areas, understand their commitments, establish trust and dialogue on how to support those commitments</li>
<li>
Perform impact analysis and gap analysis to create data flow diagrams that support key business process and information capabilities</li>
<li>
Work with the business area subject matter experts to learn the data and understand how it is used by various areas supporting business process</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors Degree in Information Systems or Information Technology, Computer Science, or related fields of study</li>
<li>
10+ years' experience in any of the following: Business Intelligence</li>
<li>
Experience using several of the following: Agile; Data Warehousing; Microsoft BI Suite; Tableau; SAP/BW/Business Objects; SQL; Data Modeling; Data flow mapping; Source to target mapping; Requirements gathering and data analysis; Testing and data validation techniques</li>
<li>
Proficiency in reading and writing SQL queries</li>
<li>
Strong analytical, problem solving and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills (written, verbal, presentation and interpersonal)</li>
<li>
A passion for data and information</li>
<li>
The ability to work in a dynamic, fast-paced, work environment</li>
<li>
Outstanding project management skills</li>
<li>
Experience in building strong teams</li>
</ul>
