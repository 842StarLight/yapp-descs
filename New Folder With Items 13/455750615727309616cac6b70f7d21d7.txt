<h2>Planner - Commercial Chrome Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The HP Commercial Chrome team delivers products and solutions that help customers transform their business as they move to the Cloud. We are a small, fast-moving team that works in an exciting, ever-changing market developing breakthrough products for both education and business.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>As the Chrome Product Planning Manager, you will be responsible for driving business growth by understanding key customer trends and requirements and creating the right hardware product definitions to meet those requirements. The role will require managing deliverables through the product planning lifecycle - from concept planning through product planning definition closure, while driving a smooth transition to product launch and end-of-life execution and transition.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Within the Cloud Client Product Management team, the specific <b>responsibilities</b> include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Gathering and understanding key customer insights</li>
<li>
Performing analysis of technology and market trends while developing a market growth opportunity analysis within the industry</li>
<li>
Leading ideation sessions within the group to generate ideas of how to solve customer problems
</li>
<li>Documenting required features and lead feature trade-decisions based on product vision, customer needs, market trends and technology capabilities</li>
<li>
Creating and managing effective content and processes for product positioning and messaging for regional partners, sales force and channel partners</li>
<li>
Collaboration with key partners including Google, Intel, AMD and others, to understand technology opportunities and co-develop breakthrough experiences</li>
<li>
Working in partnership with downstream product management teams to ensure we have a strong story and strategy for go-to-market and external launch messaging and positioning</li>
<li>
Consistently performing competitive analysis against top industry players to ensure our lineup is differentiated and competitive</li>
<li>
Supporting growing the business plan and manage products/solutions to meet revenue and profitability goals in line with team targets</li>
<li>
Partnering cross-functionally with engineering, marketing, finance, industrial design, regions, and other product teams in consumer</li>
</ul>
