<br>
<p></p>
<p>SAP Concur is seeking a qualified person to join the SMN Business Transformation team with experience in program management, product marketing and/or project management. This person will join a dynamic team of internal strategic consultants that prioritize and lead strategic initiatives focused on simplicity and scale that will prepare the Global SMN Distribution Organization for future growth and new opportunities. As a member of this team you will work with the Senior Director to manage the execution and launch of specific projects. You will have the opportunity to work cross-functionally across the organization and manage project teams to the successful conclusion of the project that delivers the desired business impact. We are seeking someone that likes working on teams and creates team cohesion, is a tenacious problem solver and demonstrates excellent communication skills.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Reports to the VP of Global Strategy - SMN</p>
<br>
<p></p>

<ul>
<li>Conduct project planning activities and manage the execution of projects according to plan (Ideation/Strategy, Planning, Execution, Launch to Post Support).
</li>
<li>Manage and communicate a clear vision of the project’s objectives and motivate the project team to achieve them; create a project environment that enables team members to be successful.</li>
<li>Utilize excellent relationship building skills to refine cross-functional project plan and work with broad cross-functional team members to identify required tasks.
</li>
<li>Leverage strong communication and organization skills to lead regular project meetings, communicate status updates, and presents project results. Keep stakeholders informed of progress, risks and issues in order to manage expectations on all project requirements and deliverables.</li>
<li>Ensures proper governance is in place to best meet project objectives</li>
<li>Display your professional courage in identifying risks and engaging appropriate team members to resolve</li>
<li>Ability to work in an environment where answers need to be discovered and is comfortable exploring new ideas independently and as a group</li>
<li>Leverage strong analytical and process expertise to develop requirements and document related business processes, propose/understand best practices as we transform the SMN business.</li>
<li>Create and maintain comprehensive project documentation</li>
<li>Work strategically and independently with various cross-functional groups on multiple simultaneous projects</li>
<li>Perform other tasks as assigned.<br>
