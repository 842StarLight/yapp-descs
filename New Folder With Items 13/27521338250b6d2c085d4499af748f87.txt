<h2>Managing Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>Concurrency, Inc. is seeking a Managing Architect which requires experienced technology knowledge and the ability to architect Digital Workplace solutions by mapping customer challenges to solutions. Demonstrated ability to engage in senior level technology decision maker discussions related to agility, business value, and trust. Solution Architects are key enablers for other consultants and partner staff. Partnering with the Practice Manager, responsible for managing the delivery of client projects and developing people. Delivery includes the creation, assignment, and completion of technical tasks to ensure exceptional delivery of Digital Workplace solutions. This role works closely with their Practice Manager, Sales, Recruiting, and Resource areas of Concurrency to successfully run, develop and grow the practice. The role requires an individual who is resourceful and has demonstrated skill in design and deployment of solving the clients’ business problems. In addition, this role will directly manage people including career development, performance management, and billable utilization.</div></div>
