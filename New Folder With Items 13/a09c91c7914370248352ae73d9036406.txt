<h2>Millwork Administrative Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Overview:</b> A Millwork Administrative Support Specialist is responsible for reviewing manufacturing and sales reports out of the Millwork Production Software. They will be responsible to monitor daily reporting tied to Sales, Costs, Margins, Inventory, and Production. A strong belief in the mission and goals of the company are necessary to this position.<br>
</div>

Problem solving skills</li>

<li>

<li>

<div></div>

<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul><li>
System Analysis
</li></ul>
<div>This candidate's job is accomplished by maintaining system accuracy, ensuring correct data is properly reported out. Assists in configurator changes, and will work closely with the Sr. Support Specialist to trouble shoot any issues within our Millwork Production Software. Also, this candidate will be involved in training and follow-up training on all Production Software tasks. Learning the Dispatch software and systems will also be requirement of this position; and being able to support our locations in these functions.</div>
<ul><li>
 Reporting</li></ul>
<div>
Ensures that reporting is accurate and timely. Recommends improvements on reporting to improve communication and clarification.</div>
