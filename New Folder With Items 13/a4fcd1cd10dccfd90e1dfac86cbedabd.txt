<h2>Retail Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job DescriptionEdit</p>
<p><b>American Fireworks</b> is looking for individuals or non-profit organizations to partner with to operate temporary fireworks locations during the holiday season. The selling dates are approximately December 20th through January 1st.</p>
<p>This is a management/sales position. The ideal candidate will have an entrepreneurial spirit and have passion for and knowledge of fireworks. The operator will be in charge of all day to day operations. We supply nearly everything necessary for you to run it.</p>
<p>If you possess organizational skills, a desire to succeed in a small business setting, are able to work extended hours during the holiday season, and you are at least 21 years of age, then we have a great opportunity for you to make good money over a relatively short period of time. You must have adequate accommodations to be available to stay on site. (Rv,Pop-up camper, etc)</p>
<p>~ Exceptional firework products</p>
<p>~ Credit card machine, and cash drawer</p>
<p>~ Liability insurance</p>
<p>~ Advertising</p>
<p>~ Sales aides - posters, signs and price tags</p>
<p>~ Corporate support team</p>
<p>Operator would be responsible for:</p>
<p>~ Providing sufficient staff for daily sales (usually 3-4people)</p>
<p>~ Making daily cash deposits</p>
<p>~ Electricity provided at location</p>
<p>~ Moving products in and out of the storage container at the beginning and end of each selling day</p>
<p>All fireworks are on consignment, which means all un-sold items are returned to <b>American Fireworks</b> after the selling period.</p>
<p><b>The first step to begin this partnership is to contact us. Locations are being assigned fast!</b></p>
