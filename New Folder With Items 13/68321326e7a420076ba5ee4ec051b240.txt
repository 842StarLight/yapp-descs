<h2>Enterprise Software Sales Executive, Retail Banking</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>SAI provides enterprise software and consulting services to commercial, healthcare and higher ed clients across North America.
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Organizations everywhere strive to identify best practices and integrate them into their daily operations, but until now, doing so successfully has remained an elusive goal.<b> </b>SAI’s Acadia® Performance Platform enables enterprise transformation as employees use “one best way” procedures to execute recurring processes. Acadia customers leverage well designed, highly intuitive software that provides just-in-time training and procedural information to employees, anytime, anywhere, on any web-enabled device along with a simple but powerful management tool that allows for continual performance monitoring and improvement.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Following a round of customer feedback during its Early Adopter Program, Acadia has been deployed to enterprise customers in the retail banking, manufacturing and healthcare verticals and is rapidly expanding in these and other industries. Current Acadia customers include tens of thousands of users across multiple global locations and languages.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
You will be working with our sales and marketing teams, along with an experienced team of software and consulting industry professionals as we grow a significant user base among Super Regional and Regional banks across the US.</div>
</div>
