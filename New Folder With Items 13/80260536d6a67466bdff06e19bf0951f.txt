<h2>Business Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>BUSINESS DEVELOPER</p>
<p>Office Location: Atlanta, Georgia</p>
<p>Level: Mid</p>
<p>Company: Design, Engineering</p>
<p>Reports to: President - Sales &amp; Marketing</p>
<p>Territory: Southeast United States, Texas</p>
<p>Position Summary:</p>
<p>The Design and Engineering Business Developer is responsible for sourcing, cultivating, and closing business prospects related to their primary companies, Design (architecture and interior design) and Engineering (mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and fire protection), as well as cross-selling all companies within the KAI Enterprise suite of businesses. As Design and Engineering Business Developer, you will be responsible for developing new clients, maintaining existing relationships and seeking new business/project opportunities from new and existing clients specifically for architecture, interior design, mechanical, electrical and fire protection services. You will be evaluated on how effectively you create and close new profitable opportunities. Your metrics will also include cross-selling opportunities as KAI is a multi-company organization with diverse services lines that support each other.</p>
<p>A. Essential Functions</p>
<p>a) Acts according to the Company Core Values</p>
<p>b) Build and maintain relationships with new and repeat customers</p>
<p>c) Client cultivation and lead internal response to client procurement documents</p>
<p>d) Conduct calls and face-to-face meetings with clients, consultants, and partner firms</p>
<p>e) Deliver sales presentations to a range of prospective clients</p>
<p>f) Generate leads</p>
<p>g) Communicate externally and internally KAI’s market leading position and strengths to potential clients and partners</p>
<p>h) Monitor the company’s industry competitors, new products, and market conditions to understand a customer's specific needs</p>
<p>i) Educate customers on how services can benefit them</p>
<p>j) Sell the company’s services to customers within your given territory to meet or exceed sales goals</p>
<p>k) Gather pertinent information to assist and land new business</p>
<p>l) Complete Go/No Go and strategy documents</p>
<p>m) Understand and promote company programs</p>
<p>n) Maintain records of all sales leads and/or customer accounts on CRM system</p>
<p>o) Attend Weekly Sales/Marketing Team meetings to work closely with marketing department and President of Design and Engineering in setting strategic sales goals.</p>
<p>p) Attend client pre-submittal meetings</p>
<p>q) Prepare weekly and monthly reports</p>
<p>r) Travel within sales territory to meet prospects and clients, consultants, and partner firms</p>
<p>B. Desired Qualifications</p>
<p>a) BA or BS degree or equivalent in business, marketing, technical writing, or relevant technical field</p>
<p>b) 5+ years of experience in design and engineering professional service sales</p>
<p>c) 3+ years of experience in mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) sales</p>
<p>d) Understanding of the sales process and dynamics</p>
<p>e) A commitment to excellent customer service</p>
<p>f) Excellent written and verbal communication skills</p>
<p>g) Superb interpersonal skills, including the ability to quickly build rapport</p>
<p>h) Competency in Microsoft applications including Word, Excel, and Outlook</p>
<p>i) Able to work comfortably in a fast-paced growing environment</p>
<p>COMPANY PROFILE</p>
<p>KAI is an MBE-Certified AEC firm built upon a foundation of teamwork and collaboration. At KAI, our core focus is transforming communities through integrated design and construction excellence. For nearly 40 years we have provided delivery-oriented building solutions with a diverse portfolio of experience, in-house multi-discipline professionals and expertise in both design and construction delivery services. Our firm has been involved in over 45 award-winning projects providing architecture, MEP/FP engineering and construction services throughout the United States that have been instrumental in improving the quality of life and environment in communities. At KAI, we are faithful stewards, we listen to understand, give back, love one another and we hustle!</p>
