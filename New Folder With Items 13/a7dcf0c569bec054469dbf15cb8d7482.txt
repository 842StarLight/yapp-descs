<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Niche digital marketing company is seeking a motivated Marketing Strategy Account Manager for an exciting position with growth potential. The ideal candidate is an outgoing, articulate, and confident communicator, with strong attention to detail and excellent written and verbal communication skills.</p>
<p><b>Marketing Strategy/Account Manager: </b></p>
<p>Marketing Strategists are the main point of contact for all client communication after the sales process has been completed.</p>
<p>They present projects, gain approval, get revisions, and discuss internet marketing strategies.</p>
<p>They are also closely involved in the execution of internet marketing strategies such as search engine optimization (SEO), pay per click (PPC), and social media marketing.</p>
<p>Marketing Strategists are responsible for retention of the client, setting expectations and communicating with the utmost confidence in our business and concern for the client.</p>
<p>Manage blogs, directories, social media profiles.</p>
<p>Work collaboratively, but individually to grow the businesses of our clients &amp; get results.</p>
<p><b>Marketing Strategy/Account Manager Goals: </b></p>
<p>Communicate effectively with clients regarding their progress and any customer service related questions involving their campaign.</p>
<p>Learn the business’ internet marketing offerings inside and out. Manage the marketing and advertising tasks assigned monthly.</p>
<p>Work efficiently, with high attention to detail, minimizing the occurrence of costly errors.</p>
<p>Bring creativity to further the business by suggesting more effective methods and internet marketing and social media strategies.</p>
<p><b>Marketing Strategy/Account Manager Requirements: </b></p>
<p>Must be an outgoing, confident, clear communicator.</p>
<p>Must be organized and value efficiency and accuracy.</p>
<p>Highly desired – previous customer service experience.</p>
<p>Highly desired – experience in internet marketing, search engine optimization, professional writing, copywriting, graphic design, video editing, or social media marketing experience.</p>
<p>Highly desired – bachelor’s degree in marketing, advertising, or related field.</p>
