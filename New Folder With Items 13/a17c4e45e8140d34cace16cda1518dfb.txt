<h2>Agile Engagement Lead - Active Top Secret Clearance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>At Red Hat, we connect an innovative community of customers, partners, and contributors to deliver an open source stack of trusted, high-performing solutions. <h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Be the lead of an Open Innovation Labs (Labs) residency and create the catalyst for large-scale transformations the open source way. The Red Hat Labs team is looking for an Agile Engagement Lead with an active Top Secret clearance to join our North America (NA) team. In this role, you will serve as one of the experienced agile coaches who have lead agile transformations and can coach and build relationships with new product owners. You will not only serve as the agile project manager in our residencies, but also as the catalyst of these transformations. Bearing this in mind, you should feel comfortable working with C-level executives to support their business goals and equally comfortable running workshops and putting together strategic proposals that support these transformation initiatives. You'll support everything from the selling and scoping of the Labs residencies and projects to leading the delivery team's implementation and ensuring customer success. As an Agile Engagement Lead, you will manage the life cycle of a residency or project from start to closeout, including determining scope details and monitoring the project’s progress to ensure that it conforms to customer expectations and contractual agreements. Successful applicants must reside in a state where Red Hat is registered to do business.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary job responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul><li>Learn quickly, from how our customers use Red Hat’s solutions, to how to position Labs to our public sector customers; experience with our technology platform and methodology is not required</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Within 2 months, you should be prepared to lead a residency team by acquiring the following:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>

Be able to partner with customers to lead them through an agile transformation</li>
<li>
Coach and enable delivery teams on modern application design and development practices</li>
<li>

Apply your understanding of residency planning and management, team management, process management and improvement, and team building skills</li>

Develop understanding of how Red Hat platform technologies work and are positioned in the market</li>

Within 6 months, you should be ready to lead a larger Labs engagement project and support the sales process by attaining the following:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Become an agent of change for customer organizations</li>
<li>
Innovate on our current thinking and develop new approaches to change how organizations and teams deliver software and systems</li>
