<h2>Quality Control Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>PURPOSE</b><br>
This position supports the Aggregates and Asphalt businesses to ensure products meet established quality standards in the most efficient and cost effective manner.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Employee will collect samples and perform accurate quality control testing to ensure product quality. Lead Quality Control Department and staff in the area. Provides verbal and/or written test results to management and production staff in a timely manner. Assists manager with customer service issues as directed. Assists in mix design development. Certifies results of laboratory findings.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for assuring that material produced and shipped meet State and customer specifications. Failure will result in rejection of products that would produce significant negative impact to division financials and image of Lafarge in the marketplace.</li>
<li>
Provide Supervision and guidance to Tech QC</li>
<li>
Maintain DOT stock piles for DOT jobs</li>
<li>
Maintains records of test results using various databases</li>
<li>
Assists with customer service issues as required</li>
<li>
Design and prove new HMA mixes and submit same to ensure that mixes meet state specified (if applicable) and customer standards.</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot HMA and aggregate gradation problems; determine remedies. Make proper adjustments to materials at the plants to ensure compliance with specifications.
</li>
<li>Perform service, repair and calibration of lab and field testing equipment on a timely basis.
</li>
<li>Perform daily standard functions including:<br>
sampling of aggregate and asphalt<br>
visual inspection of aggregate stockpiles<br>
recordkeeping and reporting<br>
communications with lab, production and sales staff. Keep all informed of project scheduling and maintenance of inventory to ensure timely delivery of products and procuring of materials needed to make products.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
RELATIONSHIPS WITH OTHER JOBS</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Works closely with Operations and Sales</li>
<li>DOT</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS &amp; ABILITIES</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Required Education: Two years of post-secondary technical education or equivalent experience<br>
Additional Education Preferred: Bachelors / Undergraduate degree<br>
Field of Study Preferred: Chemistry or related field<br>
Required Work Experience: At least five years experience as materials QC Lab Technician or related position<br>
Required Training/Certifications: NYSDOT QCQA Certification<br>
Required Computer and Software Skills: Knowledge of processing methods and appropriate planning techniques. Proficiency with Excel, Word and PowerPoint.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Additional Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Complete understanding of standard NYSDOT and PENNDOT test procedures for aggregates and HMA (hot mix asphalt). Complete understanding of ASTM specifications.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of the operations of aggregate and asphalt plants to enable problems to be identified and resolved quickly and cost effectively</li>
<li>Comprehension of MHSA Safety Rules and their application</li>
<li>Must be able to work without direct supervision exercising discretion and independent judgment in performance of functions.</li>
<li>Valid and clean driver’s license.</li>
<li>Strong customer service orientation; solid problem solving skills; solid oral and written communication skills</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
REQUIRED LANGUAGE:<br>
Internal controls providing reasonable assurance of effective and efficient operations, including actions to prevent and detect fraud and protect Company resources</div>
