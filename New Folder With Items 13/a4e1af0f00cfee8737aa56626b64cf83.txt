<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>PRIMARY OBJECTIVE OF POSITION:</b></h1>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>This position has overall responsibility for the development, implementation and ongoing maintenance of the Site Quality Programs, conforming to the policies of Final Control Lifecycle Services. Incumbent has the capacity to contribute specialty expertise to any area of the organization where skill-set is applicable. In addition, will review and advise on documentation related to the inspection and testing phases of our products. The Regional Quality Assurance Manager is also responsible for guiding managers and employees to standards related to site procedures.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>MAJOR AREAS OF ACCOUNTABILITY:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Manage processes related to material and test certificates from internal and external sources including Nondestructive evaluation (NDE) and special process documentation</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage Inspection and Test Plans</div></li>
<li><div>
Interfacing with third party inspection agency for review &amp; acceptance of nuclear orders
</div></li>
<li><div>Management of documentation processes</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage creation of manufacturing certificates and documentation packages</div></li>
<li><div>
Customer interaction related to product quality.</div></li>
<li><div>
Review and ensure conformance to customer technical specifications, customer order requirements, code requirements and internal procedure and technical requirements</div></li>
<li><div>
Management of non-conformance, corrective action and preventive action processes</div></li>
<li><div>
Compile and analyze quality measurement data</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsibility and maintenance for the quality assurance programs and registrations.:</div></li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><div><ul>
<li><div>ISO 9001 and CSA N285.0
</div></li>
<li><div>R, S, Safety Related Appendix B &amp; ASME Code NPT</div></li>
</ul></div></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Maintain the site Qualified Supplier and Approved Supplier Lists</div></li>
<li><div>
Complete and/or facilitate supplier evaluations and audits
</div></li>
<li><div>Interface with product engineers, representatives, suppliers, 3rd party inspection agency and customers to resolve quality issues and field any questions that arise.</div></li>
<li><div>
Co-ordination of annual internal quality program, customer surveillance, third party inspection, and regulatory agency audits.
</div></li>
<li><div>Responsibility for QA requirements and leading the QA audit program</div></li>
<li><div>
Other duties as assigned</div></li>
</ul>
