Pure Storage is seeking to hire a Solutions Manager focused on Microsoft Products for the Customer Solutions Group within Marketing.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>This individual is a highly technical professional with an in-depth knowledge Microsoft products and solutions in a storage industry/product context.</li>
<li>You will work to manage the solution roadmap, including short, mid and long term strategy for all Microsoft-specific solutions, create technical content for Pure Storage solutions that are targeted to Customers, Field Sales Teams, and Partners.</li>
<li>You will also work closely with Product Management as well as other teams on the company's current and future strategies.</li>
<li>If you are a creative thinker and can independently determine what needs to be done and how to do it, we are looking for you.</li>
</ul>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>

<li>Collaborate with internal field teams as well as external resources on the customer and partner side determine and commit to solution goals and objectives leading to a solution roadmap that will help influence Pure's business.</li>
<li>Assist in technical presentations to Customers and Partners.</li>
