 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Amcor Rigid Packaging
</p>
<p>Credit Analyst
</p>
<p>Manchester, MI<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Position Overview:
</p>
<p>The position will be responsible for all customer credit reviews, new and existing. Works directly with Sales and Operational personnel to ensure clear communication and timely control of customer activity. Development and use of SAP based reports focusing on key monthly performance metrics. Responsible for the auditing of customer receivables accounts to ensure compliance with all Amcor credit management policies. Administration of the Cash on Time collections program and third party reporting services. Co-management of all customer master data within SAP.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Responsibilities and Duties:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for all customer credit reviews and recommendations.
</li>
<li>Generation and distribution of monthly A/R aging reports using SAP reporting tools.
</li>
<li>Lead administrator for Cash on Time, our internal collections software. This includes training, reporting and problem resolution, as well as, monthly balancing to SAP and maintenance of data feed files.
</li>
<li>Monthly credit utilization reviews to ensure that customer limits are aligned with actual and forecasted sales.
</li>
<li>Work directly with sales and operational personnel to provide clear updates regarding shipping holds.
</li>
<li>Utilize SAP credit and accounts receivable modules to control customer activity.
</li>
<li>Co-Lead the management of all customer master data within SAP.
</li>
<li>Enforce all corporate SOX policies regarding receivable accounts and credit reporting requirements.
</li>
<li>Assist with the construction and tracking of key working capital and cash flow information pivotal to our twelve month rolling forecast.
</li>
<li>Perform weekly cash forecasting updates and work with Treasury personnel to ensure clear communication.
</li>
<li>Develop KPI benchmarking reports to track performance trends internally and versus industry standards.
</li>
<li>Team with Collections Supervisor to perform monthly Bad Debt reviews and if required third party collections.
</li>
<li>Develop best practices to streamline work flows and improve processes.
</li>
<li>Lead contact for our third party credit review service, including incorporating data into our systems.
</li>
<li>Monthly account reconciliations for assigned accounts.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.<br>
</li>
</ul>
