<h2>Field Support Representative - Vet Tech</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>COMPANY DESCRIPTION: Heska is dedicated to quality pet care. Through innovation, invention, and strategic partnerships with worldwide leaders in diagnostics and treatments. Heska provides the benefits of latest generation technologies to pet healthcare providers. Heska’s range of complete blood diagnostic solutions include Chemistry, Hematology, Blood Gas &amp; Electrolytes, Immunodiagnostics, Heartworm, and other blood testing. Treatments and testing include Immunotherapy and complete Allergy solutions.</p>
<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY</b><b>:</b> The Field Support Representative I (FSR) position supports field sales activity and operations for several contiguous territories, with a strong emphasis on veterinary clinical instrumentation. The FSR executes hardware and accessory installation, software connectivity, detailed end-user hands-on technical and medical training for analyzers in various categories including clinical chemistry, hematology, immunoassay and blood gas analysis.
</p>
<p>Extensive travel with effective call routing, frequent clinic visits, and overnight travel will be required based on territory size and demand. Rapport development and enhancing greater Heska brand awareness in veterinary hospitals is a pivotal aspect of the role. Implementing, delivering, and executing specific training to clinic staff to properly operate and maximize use of company analyzers and other products.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p><b>Field installations, User Training, Field Support:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan, schedule, organize, document and perform chemistry, hematology, immunoassay and blood gas analyzer installations along with veterinary clinic staff training; produces high quality work in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Ensures compliance to training standards.</li>
<li>Reviews and provides suggested optimization for individual clinic sample handling, workflow, and patient results management.</li>
<li>Delivers a high level of customer service to clients to optimize customer retention.</li>
<li>Work with TSS to troubleshoot diagnostic technical issues on site.</li>
<li>Visits hospitals/clinics to train staff on basic maintenance of analyzers to ensure optimal performance, review training needs, and review inventory consumable needs.</li>
<li>Maintains efficiency of clinic coverage, accountable to maintain call coverage within set guidelines.</li>
<li>Prepares, provides and conveys diversified information, which may be of a technical or competitive nature through departmental procedures and directives.</li>
<li>Serves as an on-site liaison between customer and Heska Corporation.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate repeated clinic staff training as needed to ensure optimal customer retention, analyzer usage, and maximum analyzer up-time.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Log all customer-related activity with attention to detail and in a timely manner in the corporate customer management database.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>In-clinic visits, troubleshooting, maintenance and support:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the Technical Support Services (TSS) department to alleviate technical issues in identified clinics requiring re-training, analyzer maintenance, etc.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Work in close collaboration with Sales and TSS on an ongoing, pro-active basis to formulate solutions to various clinic and territory technical needs.
</li></ul>
<p><b>Other duties as assigned, including but not limited to:
</b></p>
