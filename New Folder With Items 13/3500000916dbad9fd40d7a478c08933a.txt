This Position
<br>Join a high-performing team that is transforming the way millions of businesses and individual professionals get business done. At DocuSign we're passionate about delighting our customers and growing our business, and we're passionate about using data – both quantitative and qualitative – to make good business decisions in a rapidly changing environment.
<br><br>
We seek a senior-level Product Data Scientist with professional experience leading reporting and analytics for product teams. As part of DocuSign's Product Insights team of expert Product Data Scientists and Researchers, you partner with the broader product development organization to understand and support product initiatives, ensure we're delivering on our KPIs, and dive into data providing deep analytics that derive impactful insights.
<br><br>
This position is an individual contributor reporting to the Director, Product Data Science.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Be the subject matter expert for driving the product data strategy</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal teams on data capture strategy that will support advanced analysis and insights</li>
<li>Define KPIs, own and manage regular reports around product use</li>
<li>Design and build automated dashboards that connect data from internal and external data sources, with visualizations and tools that allow business users to self-serve on their website data needs</li>
<li>Partner with product teams to design, administer, and analyze the results of A/B and multivariate tests</li>
<li>Deliver quick insights on ad hoc projects and analyses as necessary</li>
<li>Act with a sense of urgency and purpose, identify and resolve roadblocks, reach out to cross functional team members to solicit input and/or assist when appropriate</li>
</ul>
Basic Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>BS degree in quantitative fields (Statistics, Math, CS, Economics or Finance preferred)</li>
<li>8+ years of professional experience in a data science/analytics role</li>
<li>4+ years of experience in a web analytics role in a SaaS/Cloud industry</li>
<li>Experienced in data analytics and statistical relevancy best practices</li>
<li>Strong SQL skills</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with all the following:</li>
<li>Pulling data from external sources using APIs</li>
<li>Join data from disparate data systems</li>
<li>Distilling large data sets into actionable insights that drive business value</li>
</ul>
Preferred Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to present data in a visual format and explain the insights clearly and concisely</li>
<li>Highly motivated, organized, self-starter who thrives in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Productive individual contributor who partners well with internal teams</li>
<li>Strong communicator</li>
</ul>
