<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Marketing Manager is a key member of Stone Mountain Park’s marketing team. This position is responsible for managing and implementing marketing and promotional programs to help drive ticket sales and attendance to the Park. Additionally, this position manages the Digital Media Specialist who is responsible for developing, implementing and creating all interactive content for Stone Mountain Park’s digital communication channels including websites, email campaigns and mobile experiences. Also, this position manages the Marketing Coordinator. Management retains the discretion to change or add to the duties of this position at any time. Additional duties may be assigned.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and administer budget for Marketing department including advertising, collateral publication, promotions, production and research</li>
<li>Work with Director to determine strategic positioning for the Park and implement that position in collateral and advertisement</li>
<li>Serve as the Brand steward</li>
<li>Oversee all collateral and print design, serving as the liaison with the outside graphic design team</li>
<li>Manage all Marketing print projects to include sending out specifications, collecting bids, reviewing press proofs and distributing or shipping the printed piece as necessary</li>
<li>Administer promotional programs for the Park including coupon programs and promotions involving Park sponsors</li>
<li>Manages relationship with the outside advertising agencies</li>
<li>Work with Director to develop Revenue/Ticket Budget and pricing document</li>
<li>Responsible for creation of new tickets and leading weekly meetings</li>
<li>Manages guest communications for Snow Mountain</li>
<li>Oversee Marketing Coordinator and his/her duties as assigned</li>
<li>Oversee Digital Media Specialist and his/her duties as assigned</li>
<li>Participates in social media monitoring schedule</li>
<li>Responsible for all paid advertising for the park, including TV, radio, digital, outdoor, print, social, search and barter opportunities</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities are carried out in accordance with applicable safety policies and laws. Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Interviewing, hiring, training, and coaching staff</li>
<li>Managing department schedule and labor spending</li>
</ul>
