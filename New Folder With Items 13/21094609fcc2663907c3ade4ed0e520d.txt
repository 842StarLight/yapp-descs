<h2>Automotive Appraisal Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a family-owned group of dealerships, serving Northeast Ohio since 1919, we’re looking for people who want to deliver world class experiences to our guests and uphold our five core values- World Class Experience, Commitment to Excellence, Teamwork, Trust &amp; Respect for the individual, and Continuous Improvement!
</p>
<p>We are looking to hire an experienced Automotive Appraisal Consultant to help us keep growing. This position requires working in a fast-paced team environment, being effective as a self-starter and working individually. If you are looking for an organization to build your career that will provide you valuable training, create your own future, and a fun team-oriented environment we are looking for you!</p>
<p>
The primary purpose of this position is to help ensure the organization’s continuous improvement and growth in used vehicle acquisition, turn, and efficiency. The right candidate should be able to identify opportunities to acquire used vehicle inventory from multiple sources. This is a computer/office based position, we do not attend physical auctions to acquire our inventory. Strong computer skills and a fast pace are required for this position. This position reports to the New/Used Vehicle Director.</p>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Appraise and purchase used vehicles from guests looking to sell their current vehicle</li>
<li>
Understand used vehicle reconditioning costs and be able to explain these costs to guests when appraising their vehicle</li>
<li>
Make financially sound purchasing decisions for the company</li>
<li>
Promptly respond to inquiries from guests looking to sell their vehicle as well as follow up with guests to earn an appraisal appointment</li>
<li>
Follow the Ron Marhofer trade appraisal process and help guests understand the value of their vehicle</li>
<li>
Provide excellent internal and external customer service</li>
<li>
Maintain company's Brand</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong understanding of the retail and wholesale automobile sales process is a plus, as well as a sound understanding of current used car market and pricing.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 1+ years’ experience as a Wholesale Buyer with Auction experience</li>
<li>
Clean Driving Record (less than 4 points), Background, Professional References, and Credit History a must to be considered</li>
<li>
Tech savvy and ability to learn new software systems easily</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical and valuation skills, with a strong process orientation</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft office</li>
<li>
Quality customer service skills</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and communication skills</li>
<li>
Must have reliable transportation.</li>
<li>
Exclusivity- This is a FULL-TIME opportunity for candidates who are interested in being exclusive with our organization. We are not looking to add team members who want to keep side businesses or dealings. We are looking for new members of our family who want to represent our brand and are proud to be part of our organization.</li>
</ul>
