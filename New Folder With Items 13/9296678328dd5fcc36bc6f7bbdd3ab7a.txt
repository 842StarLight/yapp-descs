<h2>Field Application Speicalist - Lab Product Solutions - Midwest Territory</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Our products enable the biopharmaceutical industry to be at the forefront in the development and advancement of treatments for serious diseases. Earning sales revenue of more than 1.5 billion euros in 2018 our people come from over 60 nations at 50 sites in 33 countries.
<br><br>
<b>What you can look forward to accomplishing:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify customer needs and/or problems, and be knowledgeable in all IncuCyte/iQue reagents, kits and assays. Introduce solutions to needs/problems where appropriate.</li>
<li>Communicate with Service technicians to ensure IncuCyte/iQue is functioning properly</li>
<li>Product Demonstrations: Help develop detailed experimental plan, expected outcomes, and set expectations. Identify necessary reagents (cell lines, kits, etc.) and make recommendations which fit customer’s needs. Help in assay set up/train relevant lab personnel – biological, instrumentation, and software.</li>
<li>Established/New customers: Conduct On-site and remote trainings of the IncuCyte/iQue instrumentation and software. Provide support as necessary regarding assays they are currently running.</li>
<li>Help develop protocols and train on expansion into new application areas where there is interest/or need.</li>
<li>Collect product development feedback from the field</li>
<li>Identify edits in sales collateral (manuals, application notes, etc.) and product offerings and participate in their revision</li>
<li>Admin: Log cases and solutions in Salesforce.com on daily basis, maintain OPEX budget and submit expenses on monthly basis</li>
</ul>
Skills and experience that you need to bring to our company
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Microscope/image processing,flow cytometry, cell biology, cell culture,</li>
<li>Ability to understand different areas in cell biology research (e.g. neuroscience, immunology, oncology), excellent written/oral communication skills</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in life sciences, biology, or related field</li>
<li>2+ years’ field experience in a similar field application scientist/technical support role OR combination of lab experience with customer support roles (e.g. work in core lab facilities)</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Masters/PhD preferred but not required if have 2+ years lab experience</li>
</ul>
