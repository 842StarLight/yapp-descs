<div>The Customer Service Representative provides assistance to our valued customers. They are responsible for submitting quotations for customer inquiries, providing product information and technical data as required, and processing customer orders. The Customer Service Representative is also responsible for the procurement of products/services required to fulfill contract requirements. These individuals are critical to enhancing the Company’s image through their problem resolution and interpersonal skills. This position reports directly to the Inside Sales Manager.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Main Job Tasks and Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Address telephone, fax, and email inquiries from customers, vendors and co-workers</li>
<li>Generate quotes and process customer orders; communicate expected delivery dates</li>
<li>Provide trouble-shooting assistance; assist with shipment issues; resolve product related problems</li>
<li>Issue return authorizations for products that customers no longer want/need</li>
<li>Discuss product return instructions with the Shipping &amp; Receiving Coordinator and provide stocking or vendor forwarding instructions</li>
<li>Follow up with customers to ensure that issues are resolved to their satisfaction</li>
<li>Develop and maintain list of suppliers for goods and services</li>
<li>Negotiate best pricing for products/services with supplier</li>
<li>Generate purchase orders for required products/services and track scheduled delivery dates</li>
<li>Collect and distribute required purchase order documentation (ie. Material certifications, MSDS information, etc) from suppliers</li>
<li>Manage and track material returns with suppliers</li>
<li>Coordinate scheduling of labor added assemblies with Service Manager based upon contract obligations and purchase order fulfillment</li>
<li>Plan, track and coordinate delivery of purchased products against delivery date</li>
<li>Expedite purchase orders as required</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Contract Management:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Enter and maintain customer sales orders into business system</li>
<li>Assist with the clarification of contract requirements and resolution of any conflicts</li>
<li>Communicate any order issues with Accounting Department</li>
<li>Evaluate and monitor contract performance to ensure compliance</li>
<li>Perform all tasks assigned by the Inside Sales Manager</li>
<li>Work with Shipping &amp; Receiving Coordinator to ship in accordance with customer routing requirements</li>
<li>Assist with inventory cycle counts</li>
<li>Follow all Safety and Quality System policies and procedures</li>
<li>Interact with co-workers with a positive attitude to maintain a friendly and productive work environment</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Education and Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High school education, or equivalent</li>
<li>Knowledge of business and management principles and practices</li>
<li>Knowledge of project management principles and practices</li>
<li>Information technology skills</li>
</ul>
