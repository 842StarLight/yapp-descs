<h2>Security Solutions Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Our Security Solutions Architect is responsible for actively driving and managing the evaluation and prospecting stages of the sales process. This position collaborates with account executives as a subject matter expert for the services Anitian offers.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will be able to clearly articulate complex information security and compliance concepts to both business and technical prospects. This includes asking open-ended questions, gathering requirements, analyzing client need, crafting solutions, and explaining Anitian's value proposition to clients. We prefer candidates who have a background with some hands-on technical responsibilities but are also able to quickly and effectively build high-trust relationships with people of all personality types.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Build high-trust relationships with a diverse array of customers</li>
<li>
Actively network and identify opportunities for Anitian</li>
<li>
Listen to clients and assess their needs</li>
<li>
Ask appropriate, open-ended questions to incite conversation and gather intelligence</li>
<li>
Deliver clear value propositions</li>
<li>
Interpret client’s needs and translate them into solutions</li>
<li>
Collaborate with account executives to generate proposals</li>
<li>
Conduct technical presentations to clients</li>
<li>
Collaborate with multiple teams within the company to help define Anitian's products and services</li>
<li>
Collaborate with leadership to design, optimize, and augment service and product offerings</li>
<li>
Provide training and content for sales team</li>
<li>
Participate in the information security community and promote Anitian’s message</li>
<li>
Contribute to Anitian’s blog and other marketing materials</li>
<li>
Attend trade shows and conferences</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 5 years experience in a Sales Engineer role in SaaS / cloud cyber security.</li>
<li>
Outstanding communication skills (written and oral) including experience responding to RFPs</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate the solution value</li>
<li>
Strong presentation and demo skills</li>
<li>
Experience collaborating with partners.</li>
<li>
Experience working in the AWS cloud infrastructure</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of DevOps principles</li>
<li>
Cyber Security / Compliance industry experience with information security concepts, practices, and technologies</li>
<li>
Strong working knowledge of PCI, NIST, ISO, HIPAA, FedRAMP and other compliance or security frameworks</li>
<li>
Ability to eliminate sales obstacles through creative and adaptive approaches</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain a high level of productivity in a fast-paced, team environment while managing multiple competing priorities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
12+ years experience in a technical sales role, with a preference on consulting or services sales</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success with federal government agencies and SI</li>
<li>
We prefer candidates with a background that includes hands-on technology experience</li>
<li>
Entrepreneurial spirit, intellectual curiosity and a passion for learning</li>
<li>
Experience with SalesForce is a plus</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree (Computer Science or related a plus).</li>
<li>
CISSP certification or similar</li>
</ul>
