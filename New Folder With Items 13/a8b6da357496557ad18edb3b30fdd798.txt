<h2>Partnerships Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Are you collaborative, creative, a critical thinker and have a passion for culture and entertainment? Do wholeheartedly commit to supporting your team and achieving what others too easily write off as un-achievable? Are you eager to work with an agency of individuals who value innovative thinking and inspired solutions? If so, then this might be the job for you!</p>
<p><b>
AKA NYC, </b>a full-service global advertising agency specializing in theatrical and non-theatrical entertainment, is in search of a <b>Partnerships Manager</b> to be a part of its vibrant Experiential Marketing team. Candidates applying to this role must have a proven track record of proactively seeking solutions to problems and creatively leveraging available assets to achieve “1+1=3” results. An ability to meet deadlines and thrive within a fast-paced environment while remaining communicative with your team is a must. The ideal candidate will exhibit dedication, flexibility, reliability, impeccable organizational and multi-tasking skills, and feel a high degree of responsibility to the clients, the Experiential team and the agency at large. If you meet or exceed these requirements, please do apply!</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identifying, securing and implementing high level brand partnerships inclusive of event presence, sales promotions, content and social strategy integration and more</li>
<li>Cultivating, planning and managing on-site and off-site events that provide consumer engagement opportunities and reach new audiences and influencers</li>
<li>Exhibiting an expert knowledgeable about client projects, the overall campaign strategy and how your promotional activity fits in and adds value</li>
<li>Negotiating best possible value for clients</li>
<li>Attending regular client meetings to both present opportunities and feedback on activity</li>
<li>Overseeing multiple promotional campaigns simultaneously</li>
<li>Maintaining excellent relationships with clients and existing promotional partners while seeking to secure and develop new partnerships</li>
<li>Ensuring high quality of proposals and presentations</li>
<li>Working effectively with others and contributing to team success</li>
<li>Sharing contacts, opportunities and ideas within the team</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 years experience in advertising, marketing or press in the entertainment or tourism industry</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office, Adobe, PowerPoint and Social Networking platforms</li>
<li>Can work autonomously</li>
<li>Actively seeks solutions to problems</li>
<li>Understands and delivers against strategic objectives on assigned projects</li>
<li>Confidence to build and maintain solid relationships</li>
<li>Knowledge of the theatrical and tourism industry is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Duties as Assigned</b></p>
<p>
AKA is a fluid and rapidly changing organization. This position profile reflects the general nature and level of work assigned to the role. It does not restrict management’s right to reassign duties or responsibilities at any time based on business needs if the work is similar, related, or a logical assignment to the position.</p>
<p>
Please email cover letter and resume to careers@aka.nyc and include “Partnerships Manager” in the subject line. No phone calls or recruiters, please.</p>
