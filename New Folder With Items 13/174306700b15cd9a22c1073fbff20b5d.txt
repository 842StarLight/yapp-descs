<div><b>What do you get to do in this position?<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<p>He or she must have related industry knowledge; strong customer service skills, strong understanding of contracting procedures and tactics relative to such areas as project correspondence, documentation, timely notice of claims, subcontract negotiation and subcontract administration. This individual should have an advanced understanding of HVAC, DDC Building Automation Controls or other building or electronic control systems and have the ability to analyze Building Automation control system drawings and schematics as well as advanced knowledge of contract law, codes, standards and industry construction knowledge. He or she must exhibit strong leadership skills including team leading and the ability to empower others through delegating responsibility. This position plans, directs and coordinates activities of project teams to ensure that goals and objectives of the project are accomplished on time, implemented at or below the estimated cost, and billed in a timely fashion throughout the duration of the project. This position may also be responsible for directing operations personnel. Works under direct supervision of service operations manager. This project management position is based in the service department and involves managing multiple small to medium sized projects service work orders at the same time. Projects typically have a short duration and can execute through either the construction tier or direct to the end user. Responsibilities include:<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Manages resources by planning, scheduling and forecasting manpower and resource requirements<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Coordinates project in accordance with contract documents and approved sales estimate<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Manages all document control for projects, including but not limited to RFI's, meeting minutes, punch lists, transmittal log, submittal log, change order log and issues log<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Prepares project billing revenue forecasts and schedules of values<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Review all estimates and change orders for accuracy and approve all team project submittals<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Accountable for financial management on all assigned projects<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Prepares installation subcontractor bidding documents and negotiates subcontracts<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Obtain, organize, and maintain all project contract documents in accordance with Schneider Electric quality policy<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Prepares and updates project schedule and cost estimates<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Prepares and delivers final project documentation to the customer and collect payment<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Responsible for all customer-facing communication<br>
</p>
</div>
