<h2>- Personal Account Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b>: Responsible for greeting clients (in person or by telephone) and appropriately assisting them either personally or by referral to another staff member.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>include the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Takes action to connect with customers in a way that promotes 1st Security Bank.</li>
<li>Achieves monthly sales, profile and outbound call goals.</li>
<li>Processes monetary transactions and daily cashbox balancing.</li>
<li>Receives and processes name and address changes and other account information as needed.</li>
<li>Takes initiative to learn bank products, services, and procedures by utilizing their Individual Learning Plan.</li>
<li>Participates in branch operational and audit activities as assigned.</li>
<li>Follows appropriate steps to ensure branch security.</li>
<li>Provides routine information and cross-sell Bank products and services.</li>
<li>Travels to assigned 1st Security Bank locations as business needs require.</li>
<li>Adheres to 1st Security Bank policies and support management decisions and goals in a positive, professional manner and represent the bank with a high level of integrity.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>High school diploma or equivalent and at least one year work experience.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic Knowledge of all applicable Regulations that pertain to this position is required.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Knowledge of the Bank Secrecy Act and Anti-Money Laundering policies, procedures and related programs.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be bondable</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>COMPUTER SKILLS</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual should be proficient in Microsoft Office Products and other Database software.</p>
