<p>Information Technology Administrator</p>
<p>BBI (Burdette Beckmann Inc.) is an established and growing sales agency in the snack and confectionary categories seeking a skilled IT Administrator to join our Hollywood, FL team.</p>
<p>Reporting to the IT Manager, this role will focus on specific computer network, database and systems administration including installing, configuring, servicing, repairing and maintaining IT systems to ensure they are fully operational in a stand-alone and client-server environment. In addition, will participate in technical and developmental research to support continued innovation.</p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES </b></p>
<ul><li>Onboarding and training of new users by creating personalized asset sheet/password sheets and password documentation. Orienting new employees related to IT set up, login. Conducts other IT-related trainings as necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Implement, configure, deploy and support systems, including, but not limited to computing devices, servers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create, analyze, report, convert, or transfer data, using specialized applications program software.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Administer network and data security, including directory, group policy, firewalls, antivirus, email security, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Install and configure wireless networking equipment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Setup desktops, laptops, iPads and other hardware where needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Basic printer troubleshooting skills preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Basic understanding of VoIP phone systems is preferred, but not required.</li></ul>
