<h2>Sales Engineer (Graduate Program)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Hewlett Packard Enterprise advances the way people live and work. We bring together the brightest minds to create breakthrough technology solutions, helping our customers make their mark on the world.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
We’re solving the world’s most complex challenges, and our people are at the forefront of progress. In a Sales role at HPE, you’ll play a part in building the future – one big idea at a time. You’ll be selling HPE products, services, software, or solutions to customers, both directly and indirectly. Working at HPE, you’ll have the resources to develop your talent and creativity. Are you ready to unleash your potential</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Would you like to jump start your career right away? Great news! Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) is offering a unique Solution Architect Mentoring program designed specifically for recent college graduates. Join other graduates in this strategically vital training program where in one year, you will gain up to 5 years of consulting and sales engineering skills, preparing you for a position on our Presales team.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As a Solution Architect<b> </b>in our sales organization, you will have the ability to change the world. Working directly with HPE peers, sales representatives, partners, and customers, you will be able to design HPE technology-based solutions that include servers, networking, storage, software, and virtualization products to the most interesting and vexing business challenges today. The position is located in the Alpharetta, Georgia office. You will become the face of HPE’s technical sales team to our valued customers. Our Solution Architects work directly with HPE’s sales representatives providing technical expertise, solution advice, proposal creation, presentation delivery and other fun and exciting customer related communications.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
So what is this Solution Architect Mentoring program and why is HPE offering it?</p>
<p>
This program is an accelerated learning and mentoring program designed specifically to provide valuable training, mentoring and experience for recent college graduates interested in joining our innovative Presales Solution Architect team. During the mentoring program, you will be based in our primary Solution Center located in the Alpharetta suburb of Atlanta Georgia. There you will learn HPE technology via hands-on practice under the guidance of our senior architecture leads. The program is structured around a rotational schedule designed to give you hands-on experience with all HPE technologies. After completing the technical mentorship, you will be advanced to a field sales office where you will continue your mentorship working alongside one of our exceptional field-based Solution Architects. These locations are spread across the US offering numerous opportunities in many attractive cities. At the conclusion of the program, you will possess the skills and abilities to work as a professional Solution Architect.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Key activities that our Solution Architects perform today and that you, too, will one day perform include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Serving as the technical expert and valued partner with HPE sales team members.</li>
<li>
Creating solution proposals, presentations, and other customer communications.</li>
<li>
Assessing customer business needs to tailor the right solution with the right HPE products to help the customer succeed.</li>
<li>
Linking the business need to the proposed IT solution.</li>
<li>
Identifying likely problem areas that require attention and addressing those areas.</li>
<li>
Combating competitive threats in accounts and educating the customer on HPE’s key values and benefits.</li>
<li>
Anticipating potential challenges for the proposed project plan and bringing those to the customer’s attention with a resolution.</li>
<li>
Facilitating smooth transition from sales to implementation by orienting the appropriate teams to the solution design.</li>
<li>
Engaging with peers and partners on technical matters and helping educate others on HPE’s key value and benefits.</li>
</ul>
