<div><b>This Role:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
The Sales Pursuit Marketing Manager will be responsible for creating the marketing strategy and differentiated messaging for key sales opportunities across clients in North America, with specific concentration across the Midwest. This individual will work closely with stakeholders in sales and industry to identify Accenture’s competitive positioning, win themes, and overarching messaging for both sole source opportunities and RFP responses. This individual will excel at storytelling; taking complex business solutions and developing easy-to-understand stories that showcase the clients’ business challenge and Accenture’s capabilities in simple and relatable language.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>

</li>
<li>
<div>Facilitate messaging sessions with sales team</div>
</li>

<div>Take the complex and make it simple, creating client-focused messages that drive differentiation</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Create compelling stories that bring together the clients’ challenge, industry context, Accenture recommendation, and client value proposition into an easily understood and memorable narration</div>
</li>
