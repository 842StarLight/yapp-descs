<h2>Business Transformation Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>When you join Kyocera Document Solutions America, Inc. you are joining an organization that is deeply rooted in the Philosophy of “doing what is right as a human being”. Through this Philosophy, our employees are passionate about providing best in class customer service through efficiency, accountability and a sense of urgency all while putting knowledge to work to drive change.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As Business Transformation Architect, you will be a key member of a new organization that has been given the charter of driving the digital transformation of Kyocera Document Solutions of America (KDA). You are a certified Lean Six Sigma Master Black Belt or Black Belt with extensive expertise in project management and change management methodologies. You also have extensive experience with both DMAIC and Design Thinking and might also be a ScrumMaster. You will drive a core set of projects in one of five functional areas where you will be responsible for the identification and scoping of transformation initiatives, the data collection and quantification of as-is processes, the detailed analysis that will determine root causes of the most critical existing business challenges, the design and implementation of new processes and technologies that solve those challenges, and the implementation of control mechanisms to ensure solutions perform as expected over the long term. As a result of your transformation expertise, you will actively manage projects that streamline processes and operations across Sales, Marketing, customer service, advanced technical Services, finance, logistics, HR, and IT. Your work will systematically eliminate significant process defects and dramatically reduce costly variation in process outputs. You will also ensure that a superior customer experience is as core to your projects as productivity gains and revenue growth. Your projects will be grounded in strong and accurate data collection and applied statistical analytics.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Your solutions will drive change across people/processes/and systems by leveraging LEAN and Design Thinking methods. You will deliver projects on time, resolve technical issues through collaboration and sound decision making, develop aggressive project plans that are realistic and achieved, and actively controls development’s costs, scope, and scheduling. You will also ensure your projects’ statuses are well understood through accurate tracking of actual project performance relative to the plan of record. Finally, you will ensure all levels of the organization are properly informed through excellent documentation and regular communication of your strategic and tactical initiatives. Likewise, you will provide regular quantification and tracking of the business benefits of your initiatives (ROI).</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
To be successful in this position you have a winning attitude, a positive problem-solving approach, an aptitude for critical thinking, an intellectual curiosity for different business models, and enjoy working in a team environment. In this newly created role, you will have the ability to make significant and long-lasting impact on the entire KDA organization by leveraging business process management, data analytics, statistical analyze, project management disciplines, change management methods, and strategy formulation methodologies. As a result, you will be one of the critical change agents within KDA responsible for the achievement of strong organic growth and radically more efficient operations.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The chance to do something meaningful, to challenge yourself, to take others where they would not go on their own, to be a part of change in an industry, and to drive change - doesn’t come around every day!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Lead multiple business transformation initiatives that are grounded in DMAIC and Design for LSS methodology, that will streamline and implement new processes across KDA in order to accelerate revenue and reduce operating costs while dramatically improving the customer experience.</li>
<li>
Ensure good and bad work volumes are identified and quantified across Sales and Marketing, Technical Services, Service Operations, HR, IT, Finance and logistics.</li>
<li>
Develop and provide new operational understandings of people, processes and systems across KDA and quantify associated financials.</li>
<li>
Develop and share market insights and identify significant market trends as a result of new technologies and competitive actions.</li>
<li>
Provide forward thinking that is disruptive to the competition and thrilling to our customers, suppliers, and partners.</li>
<li>
Help build a portfolio of project charters that will collectively drive critical transformation across the entire KDA organization.</li>
<li>
Ensure each project has the establishment of a clear project definition, the collection of any representative samples that yield an accurate base line measurement, the establishment of root cause analysis for the project, the design and implement solutions that solve the project’s charter, and the creation of control mechanisms and reporting that ensure solutions stay in place and continue to yield the desired outcome.</li>
<li>
Ensure an optimal customer experience is a core component of every project’s solutions.</li>
<li>
Drive the development and management of our Voice of the Customer and Voice of the Employee.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain standards for project scheduling practices that ensure work is completed on time and within budget.</li>
<li>
Manage a team of that ensures work is completed on time and under budget.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain standards that ensure project activities comply with company policies, contracts, and schedule requirements.</li>
<li>
Develop, implement and administer procedures and other work controlling documents to ensure clarification of roles and responsibilities.</li>
<li>
Represent KDA, its projects and/or departments during client and project management meetings with the highest ethical standards and clear communications.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain all job reporting and logs to ensure corporate and project compliance.</li>
<li>
Educate peers across organizations on high level transformation concepts and methodologies.</li>
</ul>
