<h2>Buyer Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description:
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"></h3>
<p><b>Pentair currently has an opening for a Buyer Analyst to join our team at our Brookfield, WI location. In this role, the Buyer Analyst will provide daily, tactical management of domestic/international suppliers to the supermarket, which will ensure on time deliveries of a quality product in a cost-effective manner based on the best TCO (Total Cost Option).
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>In addition to buying, this position will also ensure the timely and accurate flow of materials from the supply base to the point of use utilizing Kanban replenishment and Pentair Material Flow System processes. This position needs to be an active participant and driver of continuous improvement as part of daily standard work.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>We will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Create career growth and rotational opportunities within various areas of the organization
</b></li>
<li><b>Pay competitively
</b></li>
<li><b>Provide on the job training and mentoring in Pentair’s proven best practices
</b></li>
<li><b>Offer health benefits &amp; coaching
</b></li>
<li><b>Support your long term retirement goals through a competitive 401K matching and stock purchase plan
</b></li>
<li><b>Offer employee discounts
</b></li>
<li><b>Protect you with life insurance and other options
</b></li>
<li><b>Tell you more about all the great benefits – just ask!
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Continuously monitor inventory dollars and turns, parts deliveries and parts shortages in relation to all planned orders for the Value Stream for constant improvement.
</b></li>
<li><b>Partner closely with production and shipping teams to meet on time delivery and inventory goals.
</b></li>
<li><b>Attend and actively participate in daily Production Review Meeting and daily production walk.
</b></li>
<li><b>Capture root causes and apply problem solving with appropriate personnel.
</b></li>
<li><b>Maintain accurate JDE parameters in the system Item Master file and for raw and finished goods components.
</b></li>
<li><b>Establish and maintain appropriate Purchase Order format to support each item in an accurate manner and in conformance with applicable procedures and policies.
</b></li>
<li><b>Work with NPD to schedule and launch new products as required while supporting downstream launches as well.
</b></li>
<li><b>Understand and apply company SIOP processes and inventory levels for respective value streams.
</b></li>
<li><b>Ensure that Kanban and Min/Max inventory staged at the supplier per the material plan is maintained and audited. Improve supplier performance to facilitate reduction in buffer inventory. Establishing and managing annual stocking agreements. Establishes and maintains On Time Delivery from the supply base at 98% +. Monitor, manage and communicate supplier performance results.
</b></li>
<li><b>Resolve invoice vs. purchase order price variance – takes corrective action against future variances.
</b></li>
<li><b>Be responsible for maintaining inventory levels, based on the material plan, within assigned commodity groups at or below targeted levels.
</b></li>
<li><b>Be responsible for the management of obsolete inventory.
</b></li>
<li><b>Work with Manufacturing, Sales and Finance on material already in inventory to determine and execute most effective plan for its elimination.
</b></li>
<li><b>Back up/support, as assigned, for other Buyer/Planners when they are out of the office.
</b></li>
<li><b>Establish the Plan For Every Part (PFEP) process for the Brookfield Material Flow System.
</b></li>
<li><b>Develop, maintain, and review inventory levels based on PFEP (Plan for Every Part) calculations and changes in demand.
</b></li>
<li><b>Plan layout of supermarket, considering usage, turnover, size, weight, and related factors of items stored.
</b></li>
<li><b>Perform regular reviews of all PFEP data and physically adjust the supermarket as required.
</b></li>
<li><b>Coordinate ECN (Engineering Change Notice) activities and the implementation of changes.
</b></li>
<li><b>Conduct business in accordance with all relevant Import/Export regulations including the International Trade Process, broker/forwarder relationships, International documents, U.S. export controls and import entry compliance.
</b></li>
<li><b>Send open order reports to suppliers and update purchase orders with revised information daily.
</b></li>
<li><b>React to past due purchase orders by getting revised date and updating our JDE software.
</b></li>
<li><b>Work with suppliers on non-conforming material and coordinate resolution (including processing debit memos if needed).
</b></li>
<li><b>Perform other duties as required
 </b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You should:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Have earned a Bachelor's Degree (B.A.,B.S.) but will consider experience.
</b></li>
<li><b>Understand Kanban replenishment systems and has demonstrated problem-solving experience.
</b></li>
<li><b>Have 5+ years purchasing and/or planning/scheduling experience preferred; lean environment desired
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>We like, but don’t require:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>JDE experience preferred
</b></li>
<li><b>Microsoft Office experience required –Excel (advanced) and Access
</b></li>
<li><b>Strong communication skills - written and verbal
</b></li>
<li><b>Knowledge and tactical experience with Lean Principles – Material flow, supermarket, PFEP, delivery applications
</b></li>
</ul>
