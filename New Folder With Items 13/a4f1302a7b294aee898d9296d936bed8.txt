<div>
The Customer Success Onboarding specialist is responsible for successful implementation, onboarding and training of Axway’s Syncplicity SaaS business in the Americas. Customer Success Onboarding at Axway facilitates effective technology deployment and training all the way through successful annual subscription. The position reports to Axway’s Sr. Director of Global SaaS Customer Success.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Responsibilities</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Take ownership of designated new customers, including trial accounts</li>
<li>
Capture potential goals and needs of the customer</li>
<li>
Work with Tier 3 Specialist to collect documentation and/or online resources to send to customers according to customer needs that will resonate with their use cases.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate the benefits of our solution within their organization and the value adds</li>
<li>
Monitor KPIs and deliver periodic reports that demonstrate the solution’s ROI</li>
<li>
Ensure customer requests are answered in a quick and timely manner</li>
<li>
Set up weekly touch-points with main account stakeholders internally and externally</li>
<li>
Create 90-day success plan for all accounts</li>
<li>
Document activities in SFDC and internal Customer Success tool</li>
<li>
Work with customers to ensure a smooth transition to a designated Customer Success Manager (CSM)</li>
<li>
Core business deliverables are:</li>
<li>
Rapid time-to-value for subscription customers</li>
<li>
Trial account customers move to a paid subscription</li>
<li>
Customer satisfaction measured through CSAT and NPS</li>
</ul>
