<p><b>TIER10</b><br>
Welcome to Tier10, where ideas reign supreme. There's no shame in being ambitious here; career growth is a priority for us – we help one another climb, we don't climb over one another. Clients aren't just customers, they're partners. Integrity and a team-driven environment serve as incubators. <p><b>COPYWRITER</b><br>
As a Copywriter, you will be responsible for crafting content for a variety of client deliverables including newspaper ads, television and radio spots, social media, websites, direct mail and more. You will work closely with the Client Services and Production teams to ensure timely delivery of creative content in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment. A strong work ethic and passion for the written word are essential.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Write copy for media, email campaigns, website, online and print advertising</li>
<li>Develop scripts and creative concepts</li>
<li>Manage pipeline workflow through internal platforms</li>
<li>Maintain constant communication with Account Teams on deliverables’ status and current work-in-progress using internal processes</li>
<li>Learn and maintain knowledge of compliance and co-op requirements for all manufacturers</li>
<li>Support Account Management team with additional tasks, as needed</li>
<li>Understand Clients’ goals and effectively organize deliverables to ensure productive campaign results</li>
<li>Partner with other team members and assist with concept development and production of marketing and sales support materials</li>
<li>Collaborate with creative team members to determine the conceptual and copy direction of branding and advertising initiatives</li>
<li>Engage in new business presentations and spec work for potential clients</li>
<li>Work ahead on client deliverables; <i>what you know in advance, do in advance</i>
</li>
<li>Know your audience! Be a student of the Industry! Hone your understanding of the automotive industry, company culture, products, services, strategic communication planning, and integrated marketing strategies</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Journalism, Creative Writing or related field</li>
<li>2 - 3 years’ professional copywriting experience</li>
<li>Retail (B2C) copywriting experience for television, print and radio scripts, <i>highly preferred</i>
</li>
<li>Ad agency experience, <i>preferred</i>
</li>
<li>Automotive advertising experience, <i>big plus!</i>
</li>
<li>Applicants should have an interest in automotive and/or a desire to learn more about the industry and our services</li>
<li>Excellent writing and editing skills in print, web, email, and social media channels</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects and work quickly to meet deadlines, and produce accurate and high-quality work</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, with minimal supervision in a team setting</li>
<li>Flexible and can adapt to quickly changing priorities and tasks</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills, as well as the ability to thoroughly proofread all of your work</li>
<li>Positive attitude and diligent worker who takes pride in their work</li>
</ul>
<p><b>COMPENSATION</b><br>
Competitive compensation commensurate on experience, as well as participation in company benefit offerings including medical, dental, vision, 401K, and wellness. This is a full-time, on-site, salaried position located at our corporate home office in Herndon, VA. Local candidates only please, no relocation assistance provided.</p>
