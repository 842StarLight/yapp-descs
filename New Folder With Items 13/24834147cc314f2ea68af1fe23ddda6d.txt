<h2>Project Administrator I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Follow all order entry procedures as defined in the appropriate docushare section.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Hold, attend and/or request contract meetings.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Gather all necessary information to complete the TWMC shop order and/or clarify information requested by the customer. Schedule drawings and revisions.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Administer the producrement of customer furnished items.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Determine hold status and follow all held shop orders to release consistent with customer requirements.</p>
<p></p>
<p>(Close contact with Order Service Representative).</p>
<p></p>
<p>Meet with customer representatives concerning orders.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Notify field sales and customer regarding shipping dates and date changes.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Review and process customer change notices. Negotiate price and delivery changes.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Coordinate with production planning on all reschedules.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Determine all software required prior to or at shipment and make sure that submittals are timely.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Meet with customer inspectors or witnesses and host their visits.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Coordinate the receipt of shipping addresses and approvals.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Coordinate with Controller and QA departments to clean up shortages and suspense items.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Train and assist Junior Project Administrators.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Regular attendance is an essential function of this position.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Compliance with applicable Company policies concerning maintaining a drug free workplace is required.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Compliance with all Company policies is required, including adherence to Company ISO 9001 Standards and compliance with Company Affirmative Action Plan and Company Safety procedures.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Incorporate the standards of excellence as outlined by TWMC (pride, desire, teamwork, attention to detail, follow-through) into daily activities in order to create a total quality environment through personal commitment to excellence.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Other functions as required (non-essential functions).</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Additional Responsibilities-Applies to positions in the Service Department:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Review customer specifications and collaborate with service engineering to develop a repair or component work scope.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Accurately develop pricing and submit to the customer.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Manage the quote through the process from creation submission to the customer, negotiations and then finalization, which includes scheduling the agreed upon service.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>SCOPE, PURPOSE, &amp; FREQUENCY OF CONTACTS:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The scope of this position includes extensive contacts with Operations, Finance, Engineering, Information Systems, Field Sales, and frequent contact with customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Advise management of potential customer concerns in a timely fashion so as to head off potential dissatisfactions.</p>
<br>

The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>

<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Bachelor's degree (Engineering and/or Business Administration preferred) from four-year college or university; and five or more years related contracts and/or motor experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>SKILLS AND ABILITIES</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>LANGUAGE SKILLS</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Ability to read, analyze, and interpret common scientific and technical journals, financial reports, and legal documents. Ability to respond to common inquiries or complaints from customers, regulatory agencies, or members of the business community. Ability to write speeches and articles for publication that conform to prescribed style and format. Ability to effectively present information to top management, public groups, and/or boards of directors.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>MATHEMATICAL SKILLS</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Ability to apply advanced mathematical concepts such as exponents, logarithms, quadratic equations, and permutations. Ability to apply mathematical operations to such tasks as frequency distribution, determination of test reliability and validity, analysis of variance, correlation techniques, sampling theory, and factor analysis.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>REASONING ABILITY</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions. Ability to interpret an extensive variety of technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several abstract and concrete variables.</p>
