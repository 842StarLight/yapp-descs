<h2>Business Intelligence Project Manager III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Manages all aspects of moderately complex projects. Leads/manages medium to large size projects, leveraging basic project management tools to deliver results. Accomplishes project objectives by planning and evaluating project activities.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manages all aspects of moderately complex projects.</li>
<li>
Leads/manages medium to large size projects, leveraging basic project management tools to deliver results.</li>
<li>
Achieves operational objectives by contributing information and recommendations to strategic plans and reviews.</li>
<li>
Prepares and completes action plans Implements production, productivity, quality, and customer-service standards.</li>
<li>
Resolves problems.</li>
<li>
Completes audits. Identifies trends.</li>
<li>
Determines system improvements and implements change.</li>
<li>
Meets financial objectives by participating in developing forecasting requirements.</li>
<li>
Participates in preparing an annual budget and scheduling expenditures.</li>
<li>
Analyzes variances and initiates corrective actions.</li>
<li>
Sets and continually manages project expectations with team members and other stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Enhances department and organization reputation by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and different requests.</li>
<li>
Explores opportunities to add value to job accomplishments.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's degree in business or related field.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Certifications:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Project Management Certification preferred.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Work Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 3 years project experience in assigned area which may include progressively responsible experience supporting a clinical system and/or data/financial analysis, etc.</li>
<li>
Experience creating and maintaining a project plan from inception through completion.</li>
<li>
Effective relationship building skills are required.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Effectively communicates with peers, peer sponsors, and leadership.</b></li>
<li><b>
Experience with project management tools (JIRA, Workfront, or MS Project)
</b></li>
<li>
<b>Lean Project Management </b>Methodologies<b>
</b>
</li>
<li>
<b>Waterfall and AGILE </b>Methodologies<b>
</b>
</li>
<li>Experience supporting data and financial analysis through project management</li>
<li>
Managing software development life cycle through project management</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manual dexterity to operate office equipment.</li>
<li>
May require periods of sitting, standing and the ability to walk to various locations throughout the health system to attend meetings.</li>
<li>
Must have normal or corrected vision and be able to clearly communicate verbally by phone or in person.</li>
<li>
Must have own transportation to travel to multiple sites.</li>
</ul>
