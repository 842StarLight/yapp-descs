<h2>Customer Service / Clerical Support Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<p>As a CSR you’ll take on a hybrid role which has a blend of customer service activities (approximately 50% of day) in combination with an administrative support role (approximately 50% of day) which benefits the marketing, sales, and logistics teams.</p>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>
Tasks</b></p>
<div>
<ul><li>Communicate with customers / homecare companies by telephone, in person, or via email to provide information about products or services, take or enter orders, provide account maintenance and updating, and resolve issues/complaints</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate with patients by telephone, in person, or via email and assist them with their specific needs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Answer incoming telephone calls and direct to appropriate party as necessary</li></ul>
<ul><li>Process invoices and billing through company’s ERP system</li></ul>
<ul><li>Utilize the company’s CRM software by daily data entry into the system</li></ul>
<ul><li>Daily business tasks that are assigned to employees on an as needed basis</li></ul>
</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Physical Demands</b></p>
<ul>
<ul><li>Regularly sitting; Occasional standing, walking</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lifting up to 20 lbs.</li></ul>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Vision</b></p>
<ul>
<ul><li>Extended viewing of computer screens on a daily basis</li></ul>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Education &amp; Experience</b></p>
<div>
<ul><li>3+ years of professional work experience with an emphasis on customer service/support</li></ul>
<ul><li>High School / GED required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Microsoft Windows (Outlook, Excel, Word, etc) experience a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li>General office/business/small business experience required</li></ul>
</div>
<p><b>All employees must pass a pre-employment drug screening</b></p>
</div>
