<p><b>ROLE DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Application specific expert consulting (for a specific application: creation of business blueprint, Implementation based on a blueprint, creation of test cases, test scheduling and execution, key user training, go live support, and post go live support)</li>
<li>Support in escalated projects
</li>
<li>Performance of feasibility studies / solution reviews</li>
<li>Support of pre-sales activities (license and consulting)</li>
<li>Identifying and applying Integrated Service Delivery options</li>
<li>Able to architect SCM solutions based on customer requirements</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>EXPECTATIONS AND TASKS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Taking over responsibility from time to time as a sub project team lead</li>
<li>Takes responsibility from time to time as team lead and supports respective line manager(s) in people development tasks</li>
<li>Detailed planning of process implementation
</li>
<li>Define detailed blueprint for development requirements
</li>
<li>Industry /corporate process implementation across all related applications (SAP/non-SAP)</li>
<li>Develop and Assure quality of process models</li>
<li>Demonstrating expert knowledge of modeling standards and tools</li>
<li>Familiar and experienced in all lifecycle phases of a standard project (Blueprint to Go live support)</li>
<li>Familiar with AGILE / SCRUM process</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>WORK EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<p><i>Functional Experience</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Experienced SCM-APO / IBP consultant with at least 7 years of module specific project experience with both APO and IBP.</li>
<li>A minimum of 3 full cycle implementations, with the ability to bring to a productive state the modules being requested: APO SNP, PPDS or gATP and IBP (any).
</li>
<li>Expereinced with the IBP planning tool in a productive environment.
</li>
<li>Strong communicator at multiple levels of the organization with strong and clear verbal and written communications; an ability to conduct presales for listed modules and the ability to conduct appropriate level training for modules listed.
</li>
<li>Must be willing to travel up to 100% as needed and be willing to travel globally as the situations arise; must be US citizen or have green card.</li>
<li>Deep understanding of industry specific business processes having worked in the business planning environment.
</li>
<li>Has performed several business cases and has managed challenging client situations</li>
<li>Understands the AGILE project methodology and has experience in a project setting.</li>
<li>Strong ECC -PP foundation, demonstrated experience.
</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Team Lead Experience</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Acted as a team lead in more than 2 projects</li>
<li>Acts as a coach or mentor for colleagues</li>
<li>Clear demonstration of leadership in a highly charged team environment; be able to demonstrate this leadership at interview.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND QUALIFICATION / SKILLS AND COMPETENCIES</b>:</p>

<li>English: Fluent</li>
