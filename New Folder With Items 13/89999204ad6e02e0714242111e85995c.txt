<h2>Customer Service Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>
This position is responsible for supporting the company's sales goals through excellent customer service and maintaining quality relations with existing accounts.</p>
<p>
This is a FT position working from 7:30 am to 4 pm.<br>
</p>
<p>This is an hourly, non-exempt position with a median wage of: $20/hour (will vary with experience)</p>
<br>

<p><b>
ABOUT YOU</b></p>
<p>
If you prefer to work independently, are an out-of-the-box thinker, and a team-player, please keep reading.</p>
<p>
Our ideal Customer Service Rep enjoys working with people in a supportive and collaborative fashion, has great communication skills, and excels at maintaining healthy working relationships. With a drive for process improvement, efficiencies and cost savings are frequently realized.</p>
<p>
This professional recognizes the legal, operational, financial, and personal impact their job performance has on their co-workers, employer, and customers and takes this seriously. Because of this, taking ownership and being accountable comes naturally to this motivated individual. Integrity and confidentiality are unquestioned attributes.</p>
<p>
This multi-tasker does not need to be micro-managed. As a forward thinker, this person can eliminate urgency due to being organized, planning ahead, and anticipating needs. However, when priorities shift, their positivity and flexibility allow them to quickly adapt to change, do what needs to be done, and always with a smile on their face.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops maximum sales potential out of assigned customers through good customer service techniques and knowledge of the company's products and services.;</li>
<li>Applies knowledge of the company's products and services to efficiently process customer orders and respond to inquiries and complaints in a diplomatic manner.
</li>
<li>Builds and maintains quality relations with assigned customers and increases the volume of sales on a consistent basis through ongoing determination of customer requirements.
</li>
<li>Contacts customers via telephone or correspondence as frequently as necessary to meet and surpass goals for the company.
</li>
<li>Maintains, updates, and organizes files on all assigned customers.
</li>
<li>Analyzes customer accounts and promptly makes corrections and/or modifications to ensure on time delivery.
</li>
<li>Contacts customers to obtain missing information or data to ensure an accurate customer database.</li>
<li>Traces and expedites orders to ensure customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
