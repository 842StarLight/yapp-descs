<h2>Center Director - Sales & Outreach</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Mathnasium will be soon opening a new location in the Morningside neighborhood of Atlanta!</p>
<p>We are seeking a pro-active, enthusiastic, sales-driven professional with customer service experience to help launch our new location. Primary responsibilities include parent communication, community involvement, sales, initial consultations,learning plan design, hiring, staff management, marketing, and school outreach.</p>
<p>This is an excellent fit for someone who has a background in education, excels in communication, has an outgoing and positive personality, feels comfortable with math, and knows how to inspire both team members and students.</p>
<p>Applicants can expect a salary of roughly $35 K/ year plus commission opportunities and full medical benefits for a job that requires roughly 35-40 hours per week. Salary increases may occur after annual performance reviews. Commission eligibility hinges on performance each month and each quarter.</p>
<p>Some key traits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Local to Midtown/ Morningside/ Virginia Highland or the metro Atlanta area</li>
<li>Strong comfort level in math through at least Geometry</li>
<li>Available from 12 PM-8 PM Monday through Thursday, and at least one weekend day for 4 hours</li>
<li>Driven to deliver excellent customer service, comfortable with conflict resolution and excellent at communicating and setting expectations with customers</li>
<li>Motivated to maintain and strengthen relationships with enrolled parents and students</li>
<li>Able to inspire a staff of young workers to deliver the best possible instruction, able to oversee and maintain the quality of math instruction</li>
<li>Able to introduce new families to the Mathnasium program with an initial consultation and assessment. While training will be provided, the candidate must feel comfortable explaining the program with great nuance and also listening intently to the needs of the customer</li>
<li>Eager to engage with parents, schools, and other local organizations to maintain Mathnasium' strong local brand awareness and seek opportunities to reach more families for enrollment</li>
<li>High comfort level with marketing, advertising, and social media</li>
<li>Possesses excellent sales skills, ability to articulate why Mathnasium delivers excellent results and how it would work specifically for any given family who might seek to enroll</li>
<li>Has strong interpersonal skills, loads of patience, ability to inspire and relate to young students while maintaining authority</li>
<li>Willing to become well-versed in the Mathnasium approach and teaching style, regardless of prior math background</li>
<li>Familiar with local Morningside/ Midtown/ Virginia Highland schools and organizations</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $33,000.00 to $37,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Customer Service: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Leadership: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
