<p></p>
<p>Do you enjoy a fast paced environment? Do you love technology and want to use your passion in a hospitality setting? Do you like to keep work flows moving forward? Then this may be the job for you!
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Encore Event Technologies is seeking an <b>Network Services </b><b>Coordinator </b>who will support the administration of all electrical, internet &amp; telephony related sales, telemarketing, and dispatch efforts in hotels where the Company provides electrical, internet, and telephony services.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>WHAT YOU'LL BE DOING:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Participates in all appropriate meetings with hotel staff, clients, and customers.
</li>
<li>Take a pro-active approach to ensure understanding and awareness of major events, clients and customers.
</li>
<li>Resolves customer sales and service issues, actions, and resolutions with the assistance of management.
</li>
<li>Coordinates and disseminates information and instructions for event specific issues and peculiarities of hotels.
</li>
<li>Assists in training Coordinators to be well versed and adaptable to event specific issues.
</li>
<li>Keep regular productive communication with the Director of Electrical and I&amp;T Services, Electrical Operations Manager, and I&amp;T Services Manager to discuss tasks, progress, and combined efforts of the E I&amp;T Department.
</li>
<li>Develop, implement, and administer standard operating procedures for the Coordinators.
</li>
<li>Ensure the E, I &amp; T Coordinator Manager is aware of any issues that would affect daily operations including, but not limited to: staffing, scheduling, assignments, exhibitors, hotel clients and billing.
</li>
<li>Process billing and contracts accurately and within established deadlines.
</li>
<li>Coordinate with the Company’s Finance Division and the Hotel’s Finance Division regarding billing and collections.
</li>
<li>Assist in cross training of all Coordinators in electrical, internet, and telephony services.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum one (1) year of experience in the tradeshow industry and/or telecommunications industry.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated working knowledge in using MS Office (Word, Excel, and PowerPoint) and customer service software (EMS and Qengine)
</li>
<li>Must have working knowledge of internet, computer products and their functions.
</li>
<li>High School Diploma or GED
</li>
<li>Associates Degree preferred
</li>
</ul>
