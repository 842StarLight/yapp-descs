<br>
<div>
The Software Quality Assurance Analyst III, in conjunction with team members, will develop and implement quality assurance standards and measures within the organization. This role is responsible for writing test plans, creating and executing both functional and understand automated tests, for tracking defects and fixes and reporting the progress of each. The Analyst will apply proven analytical and problem-solving skills to help validate IT processes through careful testing in order to maximize the benefit of business investments in IT initiatives.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Essential Functions</b><br>
<br>
<b>Strategy &amp; Planning</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Promote, foster and pro-actively implement the use of Quality Assurance best practices and standards in direct support of increased profitability and financial growth of the Sherwin-Williams Company on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Assist with the creation and development of best practices for use of our automated testing tools; including Selenium Webdriver and Java.</li>
<li>Create efficient test scripts using HP ALM and/or Jira.</li>
<li>Proactively mentor QA Professional Apprentices and teammates on QA processes, best practices, and industry standards.</li>
<li>Assist with the training of QA team members and business users on the HP and Jira Testing Tools.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<b>Acquisition &amp; Deployment</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist with the development and execution of selenium scripts that support UI Validations.</li>
<li>Support the requests of PCG customers for the creation of test scripts and automated testing.</li>
<li>Install and train personnel on software testing tools.</li>
<li>Support the QA effort on projects which includes, but is not limited to: creating test plans, writing test cases, creating data load scripts, effectively communicating statuses, coordinating meetings, creating effective reports, and performing functional, usability utilizing the HP ALM and Jira Testing Tools</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Operational Management</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Support test automation by gathering requirements from business or project team to identifying key business processes.</li>
<li>Analyze requirements, write test scripts, execute test sets, and track defects within HP Application Lifecycle Management or equivalent tool.</li>
<li>Implement efficiencies in manual script execution and the test automation process through the adaptation and use of best practices and standards.</li>
<li>Coordinate and participate in meetings and documentation as required throughout the testing process.</li>
<li>Work with and effectively communicate with QA team members, project managers, developers, and customers in both technical and non-technical terms.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Incidental Functions</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist with projects as may be required to contribute to efficiency and effectiveness of the work.</li>
<li>Assist with research into new technologies, including tools, components, and frameworks.</li>
<li>Attend management meetings on behalf of team.</li>
<li>Install software products for end users as required.</li>
</ul>
