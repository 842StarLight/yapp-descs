<p>
SMG is looking to add a VP-Customer Engagement, for our Healthcare Vertical. We are seeking a top-performing sales professional, responsible for proactively developing, implementing, and directing business development activities that will meet sales objectives as part of the overall growth strategy of the company – the proverbial “quarterback of their vertical market” at SMG. You should be a fierce competitor, but also a team player who recognizes, encourages and appreciates success in others.</p>
