<div><b>Role Summary:
</b></div>
<div>This position is focused on Information Technology Outsourcing Services for Capgemini <b>Technology, Media &amp; Telecom</b> Clients</div>
<div>
This experienced sales professional will identify, develop and manage opportunities to provide Infrastructure outsourcing services to middle market prospects within focused business sectors.</div>
<div>
Annual sales quota for this Sr. Sales position is $30 - 40MM in total contract value in Infrastructure services sales.</div>
<div>
The role is eligible for an aggressive Sales Commission Plan designed to reward top achievers performance.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<p>§ Develop deep relationships and high levels of intimacy with targeted ITO prospects in focused geographic regions.</p>
<p>§ Leverage knowledge of industry trends and client challenges to develop and deliver compelling value propositions.</p>
<p>§ Partner and work with diverse sales pursuit teams to deliver winning proposals to customers.</p>
<p>§ Develop sales strategies and unique value propositions and solutions for clients.</p>
<p>§ Develop and manage a sales pipeline of 3-4 X sales quota.</p>
<p>§ Close total contract value of $30 - 40MM in Infrastructure services annually.</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Primary Qualifications</b>:</div>
<p>§ Minimum of 8-10 years sales experience in selling large &amp; complex IT integration and/or outsourcing services contracts in the Infrastructure Managed Services market.</p>
<p>§ 5 years or greater of recent experience selling into stated enterprise business sectors.
</p>
<p>§ Experience selling complex outsourcing opportunities that include multiple integrated service towers focusing on value and business outcomes</p>
<p>§ Comprehensive understanding of targeted industry business environments, issues and trends.</p>
<p>§ Experience in building relationships with senior executives in companies with revenue ranges from $750MM to $10B+.</p>
<p>§ Proven history of successful sales of Outsourcing deals ranging from $20MM to $100MM+ total contract value in size.</p>
<p>§ Experience with the consultative sales approach in analyzing challenges of potential clients and conveying ROI and TCO concepts to CXO level management.</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Additional Qualifications:</b></div>
<p>§ High comfort level proposing and building relationships with C-suite executives</p>
<p>§ Excellent oral and written communication skills &amp; outstanding presentation skills</p>
<p>§ Significant experience in targeted account sales strategies</p>
<p>§ Ability to work in a global organizational and service delivery environment</p>
<p>§ Demonstrated commitment to stay abreast of industry trends and technical advancements within the industry sectors and enterprise markets</p>
<p>§ Experience with Sales pipeline reporting, forecasting and Salesforce.com CRM tools</p>
<p>§ Ability to travel extensively as required, primarily but not limited to the specified NA region</p>
<p>§ College degree required</p>
