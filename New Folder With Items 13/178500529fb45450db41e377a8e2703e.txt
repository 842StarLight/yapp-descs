<h2>Director, Enterprise Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>UBTECH ROBOTICS CORP is a global leading AI and humanoid robotic high-tech company engaging in R&amp;D, manufacturing, promoting as well as popularizing humanoid robotics around the globe. UBTECH has successfully developed a complete ecosystem of robots from its intelligent humanoid Alpha series to the STEAM driven JIMU robot, and intelligent service robot Cruzr and Walker. For additional information about the company, follow UBTECH ROBOTICS CORP on Facebook, Instagram, YouTube and at www.ubtrobot.com.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p>UBTECH ROBOTICS CORP is looking for a driven <b>Director of Enterprise Sales</b> to join the team in Downtown Los Angeles. The Director of Enterprise Sales will be responsible for driving the adoption of our high-tech enterprise products across all verticals.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Build strong relationships with decision makers in key accounts in order to successfully position UBTECH’s enterprise solutions.</li>
<li>Conduct accurate monthly/quarterly sales and revenue forecasts and develop plans to achieve sales targets across multiple verticals.</li>
<li>Develop long-term strategic plan to increase sales growth.</li>
<li>Understand customer pain points and requirements to align UBTECH’s enterprise offerings.</li>
<li>Own strategy, vision, and execution of Enterprise sales team across all verticals.</li>
<li>Build and maintain executive relationships with customers to influence long-term strategic direction.</li>
<li>Responsible for defining and managing individual and team KPIs.</li>
<li>Drive closing activities to meet or exceed sales targets into enterprise accounts.</li>
<li>Create opportunities at Fortune 500 companies using both your industry network and leads generated by the team.</li>
<li>Manage end to end sales process through engagement of resources such as Tech Ops, Partners, Executives,</li>
<li>Mentor, develop and lead a high-performing enterprise sales team.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree or equivalent experience required.</li>
<li>10+ years of enterprise sales experience in consumer electronics, consumer products, or high-priced tech products/solutions.</li>
<li>Experience building and leading high-performance sales teams in multiple territories (remote).</li>
<li>Proven ability to sell disruptive technology and identify opportunities in various markets.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal/written/presentation skills.</li>
<li>Ability to plan, pitch, and execute sales strategy to Fortune 500 companies.</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, constantly evolving team environment.</li>
<li>Industry knowledge and network within targeted verticals such as Shopping Malls, Retail, Real Estate, Airports).</li>
<li>Track record for consistently exceeding revenue goals.</li>
</ul>
