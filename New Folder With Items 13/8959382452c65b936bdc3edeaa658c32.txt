<h2>Jewelry Sales and Office Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Burbank Jewelry Outlet is a leading family owned retail/manufacturing jewelry store here in Burbank, CA and we are looking to grow our team.</p>
<p>We are seeking a self-driven candidate who is tech-savvy, has the ability to manage projects and provide efficient customer service. This position will be focusing on general assistant tasks such as invoicing, social media advertisement, and e-commerce administration (eBay and Etsy as well as other platforms), as well as assisting in retail sales of jewelry.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will be able to adapt and enjoy working in a growing environment. Most of all he or she must be someone who takes pride and full responsibility of their work. The candidate must have experience in the jewelry industry.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage, maintain and organize daily office operations</li>
<li>Answering and redirecting phone calls</li>
<li>Manage and add items to eBay and Etsy online stores</li>
<li>Processing job orders</li>
<li>Price quoting custom jewelry</li>
<li>Following up with customers</li>
<li>Answering questions on online platforms</li>
<li>Assisting with sales on the retail side</li>
<li>Advertising on social media platforms</li>
<li>Manage and coordinate production of fine jewelry</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>An interest in growing personally and professionally with our company.</li>
<li>Strong nature to organize and structure</li>
<li>Problem solving and solution mindset</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to calculate/estimate costs of designs before they are made.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Strong ability to multi-task and prioritize projects</li>
<li>Excellent customer service</li>
<li>Must be skilled in Microsoft Word, Excel, and Photoshop</li>
<li>Experience in the jewelry industry is a <b>must</b>
</li>
<li>Associate/Bachelor's degree in Business Administration or similar is preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>Interested candidates should submit their resume along with a cover letter.</p>
