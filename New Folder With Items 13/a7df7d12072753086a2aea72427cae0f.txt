<h2>Manager, Talent Acquisition</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are looking for a talented Manager, Talent Acquisition who is an experienced recruiter, data-driven, passionate about candidate experience and is always looking for new ways of engaging top talent. This hands-on Manager will maintain full lifecycle hiring and lead &amp; mentor a small team of talent acquisition professionals. Our Manager, Talent Acquisition will contribute to developing a talent acquisition strategy for our growing, non-tech teams, including Strategy &amp; Finance, Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, People Operations, and many other business roles. This role reports in to our Senior Director, Talent Acquisition.
<br><br>
<b>As our Manager, Talent Acquisition, you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and mentor a small team (3-4) of talent acquisition business partners</li>
<li>Manage the full cycle recruiting process to meet staffing goals across business departments</li>
<li>Partner closely with hiring managers to ensure close calibration and quick and efficient interview processes</li>
<li>Evaluate and maintain high standards for our job requirements, descriptions, and hiring processes as we scale</li>
<li>Develop and implement strategic initiatives for recruiting diverse talent</li>
<li>Source and attract active and passive candidates from various resources, including direct sourcing, referrals, social networks, networking events etc</li>
<li>Schedule interviews with hiring managers and negotiate offers</li>
<li>Create a strategy &amp; help build a pipeline of new candidates for high volume hiring</li>
<li>Participate in and host recruitment events to drive awareness of the company and brand, speaking at panels and events and learning best practices</li>
<li>Execute our vision, for our recruiting strategy, delivering results, as well as filling roles alongside colleagues on the Talent Acquisition team</li>
</ul>
<b>To be successful in this role, you'll need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>6+ years of recruiting experience, with minimum 1 year in a recruiting management role, ideally in fast-paced high growth environment</li>
<li>Direct experience managing day to day and training new team members</li>
<li>Ability to develop long-term solutions versus short-term quick fixes</li>
<li>Proven track record of diverse talent attraction, including creative sourcing solutions and programs, established relationships with hiring managers and team development</li>
<li>Deep passion for providing a great candidate experience</li>
<li>Strong tendency toward action and getting things done</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication &amp; influencing</li>
<li>Experience with Greenhouse ATS preferred</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience</li>
</ul>
