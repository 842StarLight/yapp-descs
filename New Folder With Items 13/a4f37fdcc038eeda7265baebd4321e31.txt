<h2>Senior Account Manager-Civilian</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>IronBrick established more than a decade ago with the vision to reduce the cost, risk, and effort of managing information. Today that vision is still strong, our innovative services and solutions transform IT environments by solving customer’s complex technology and business challenges.<br>
<br>
IronBrick demonstrates value by focusing on the highest priorities of our customers for IT modernization, from the data center to the cloud and from security to big data &amp; analytics. Our list of federal and enterprise customers spans the globe, and we deliver results by leveraging long-standing partnerships with leading technology companies coupled with our high performing service practice.<br>
<br>
<p>As we expand our government services footprint, we are currently looking for a DC metro-based Sr. Account Executive to join our team. <b>This is an individual contributor role.</b> The role will be responsible for driving the identification, development, and capture of IT Solution and Services opportunities in the Civilian space. Have a working knowledge of the overall Civilian organizational structure, operating environment, major IT applications, and acquisition processes is required. A demonstrated understanding of a Civilian Agencies organization, priorities, and strategic direction is essential.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will have experience supporting market assessment and strategic planning and will have played a hands-on role in new revenue attainment. The individual should have strong communication skills with the ability to consult effectively with government clients and contractor community executives.</p>
