<h2>Bookkeeper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Summary</b>: The bookkeeper position creates financial transactions and generates reports from that information. The creation of financial transactions includes posting information to accounting journals or accounting software from such source documents as invoices to customers, cash receipts, and supplier invoices. The bookkeeper also reconciles accounts to ensure their accuracy.</p>
<p><b>Principal Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Record purchases of supplies and equipment as authorized by management</li>
<li>Record and reconciled fixed asset activity to subsidiary ledger</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Record and pay supplier invoices in a timely manner</li></ul>
<ul><li>Record debt payments as they come due for payment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor debt levels and compliance with debt covenants</li></ul>
<ul><li>Record and deliver invoices to customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Record collected sales taxes from customers and remit them to the government</li></ul>
<ul><li>Record cash receipts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conduct a monthly reconciliation of every bank account (including credit card accounts where applicable)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conduct periodic reconciliations of all accounts to ensure their accuracy</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain an orderly accounting (vendors and customers) filing system</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain the chart of accounts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain the annual budget</li></ul>
<ul><li>Calculate variances from the budget and report significant issues to management</li></ul>
<ul><li>Comply with local, state, and federal government reporting requirements</li></ul>
<ul><li>Process payroll in a timely manner</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide clerical and administrative support to the office as requested</li></ul>
<ul><li>Document accounting policies and procedures</li></ul>
<p><b>Desired Qualifications</b>: The bookkeeper candidate should have an Associate's degree in accounting or business administration, or equivalent business experience, as well as a knowledge of bookkeeping and generally accepted accounting principles. Must have 3+ years experience as a full-charge bookkeeper. Preference will be given to candidates with a superior knowledge of QuickBooks. Should be very detail oriented.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $19.00 to $22.00 /hour</p>

<ul>
<li>Bookkeeping: 3 years (Required)</li>
<li>Quickbooks: 3 years (Required)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Associate (Required)</li></ul>
