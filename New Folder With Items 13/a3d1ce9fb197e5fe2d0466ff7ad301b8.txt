<h2>Digital Signage Programmer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Kollins Communications has a challenging opportunity in Ramsey, NJ for a talented individual to become a Digital Signage Programmer in our Technology department. This department supports all of the digital signage, point-of-sale electronics design, and video post-production systems in a 24/7 environment.</p>
<p>Our ideal candidate is someone who takes extreme pride in their work and wants to develop a career in the 4K-8K video industry that spans professional video production and engineered point-of-sale digital signage systems.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<p>Self-manage your on-site activity during configuration of integrated technology systems.</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide technological expertise for new system prototypes</li>
<li>Perform preventative maintenance on an assortment of video and digital signage</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to visualize the sequence of work and convert it to a measurable plan and schedule</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure, assess multiple demands, and manage priorities</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively in person and via email, phone, or text to varied audiences</li>
<li>An understanding of signal flows and control systems for distributed audio, video, and signage systems and the ability to diagnose and troubleshoot problems</li>
<li>Familiarity with BrightSign media players, programming and network is a real plus</li>
<li>Ability to read and edit PHP, ASP, JavaScript, and SQL</li>
<li>Positive can-do attitude with the ability to wear many hats</li>
<li>Flexibility to travel or work during non-normal hours as necessary</li>
<li>Work as part of a crew or independently with third party integrators</li>
<li>Commitment to work within specified deadlines and provide daily field updates</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills and the desire to work for a professional company</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $45,000.00 to $75,000.00 /year</p>
</div>
