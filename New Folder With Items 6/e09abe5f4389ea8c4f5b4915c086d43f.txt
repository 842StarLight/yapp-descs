<div><b>What is a Sales Manager In Training at Cellular Plus responsible for?</b></div>
<div>**Upon successful completion of the program, you will have the opportunity to transfer to a different Cellular Plus store location and transition into the Store Manager position. Generous relocation allowance is provided.**</div>
<ul>
<li>Inspiring your team with training and coaching to deliver the highest standard Cellular Plus guest experience.</li>
<li>Managing in-store traffic and addressing guest concerns.</li>
<li>Analyzing and addressing areas of improvement for the sales team to help meet and exceed individual sales goals and achieve store goals.</li>
<li>Ensuring smooth store operations by meeting staffing needs, making financial and sales reports, managing cash and deposits, following policies and procedures, etc.</li>
<li>Maintaining in-store visual and function by managing inventory, supplies, merchandise security, cleanliness, and maintenance.</li>
<li>Collaborating with marketing team to drive in-store traffic by performing marketing and advertising activities</li>
<li>Keeping open communication with Territory Managers, District Managers, and Human Resource to participate in acquiring, reviewing, and releasing team members.</li>
<li>Representing Cellular Plus at community events, networking functions, trade shows, etc.</li>
<li>Attending ongoing paid training to ensure your continued success as a manager.</li>
</ul>
