<h2>Food and Beverage Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job brief</p>
<p>We are looking for a professional food and beverage manager to be responsible for managing all F&amp;B operations and for delivering an excellent guest experience. The successful candidate will be able to forecast, plan and manage all F&amp;B orders, staff and finance. The goal is to maximize sales and revenue through customer satisfaction and employee engagement.<br>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage all F&amp;B and day-to-day operations within budgeted guidelines and to the highest standards</li>
<li>Preserve excellent levels of internal and external customer service</li>
<li>Design exceptional menus, purchase goods and continuously make necessary improvements</li>
<li>Identify customers needs and respond proactively to all of their concerns</li>
<li>Lead F&amp;B team by attracting, recruiting, training and appraising talented personnel</li>
<li>Establish targets, schedules, policies and procedures</li>
<li>Provide a two way communication and nurture an ownership environment with emphasis in motivation and teamwork</li>
<li>Comply with all health and safety regulations</li>
<li>Report on management regarding sales results and productivityRequirements</li>
<li>Proven food and beverage management experience</li>
<li>Working knowledge of various computer software programs (MS Office, restaurant management software, POS)</li>
<li>Ability to spot and resolve problems efficiently</li>
<li>Mastery in delegating multiple tasks</li>
<li>Communication and leadership skills</li>
<li>Up to date with food and beverages trends and best practices</li>
<li>Ability to manage personnel and meet financial targets</li>
<li>Guest-oriented and service-minded</li>
<li>Culinary school diploma or degree in food service management or related field</li>
</ul>
