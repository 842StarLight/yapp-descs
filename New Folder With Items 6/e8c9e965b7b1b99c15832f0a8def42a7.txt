<p>Profile by Sanford, provider of personalized weight loss and health coaching services and one of America’s fastest-growing franchisors, seeks an exceptional Inbound Marketing Specialist.</p>
<p>Responsible for executing lead pursuit strategies for the enterprise to increase membership inquiries and ultimately, sales. Ideal position for a highly driven marketing professional</p>
<p>who is proficient in marketing automation and has a passion for success.</p>
<p>Are you data and results-driven? Can you innovate with new methods for lead generation and customer conversions? Are you motivated by the chase of the lead? Do you have a keen eye for user engagement and conversion? Exceptional communicator? If the answer is yes, we want to hear from you!</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Grow new leads, including marketing-qualified leads, by converting site traffic through calls-to-action, landing pages, and lead generation content (including offers)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Creating, managing and executing multi-channel marketing campaigns leveraging SEO, social, inbound marketing, email, mail/print campaigns and events to drive customer acquisition with direction from the Digital Strategist</li></ul>
<ul><li>Optimize marketing automation and lead pursuit processes through email, SMS and social channels</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish closed-loop analytics with business development /operations to understand how our inbound marketing activity turns into customers and convert customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work from a rich content/editorial calendar that attracts a qualified audience (including blog posts, whitepapers, ebooks, reports, webinars,infographics, etc.) using assets created in-house and by our digital partners</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create monthly reports containing a robust description of monthly activity, suitable advice and recommendations and a clear summary of performance against the key performance indicators and metrics</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate with business owners and digital advertising agencies to deliver the best possible web product for Profile by Sanford</li></ul>
<ul><li>Propose new and improved inbound marketing campaign ideas</li></ul>
<p><b>Experience &amp; Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li>5+ years of digital marketing experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree required in marketing, public relations, journalism, communications or related field</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communicator and creative thinker, with an ability to use data to inform all decisions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with marketing automation and email software in order to generate traffic, convert visitors into leads, and then nurture them (using dynamic workflows) into converted customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>History of working with digital teams</li></ul>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Competencies</b></p>
<ul><li>Ability to think strategically and execute tactically</li></ul>
<ul><li>Evidence of staying atop market and technology trends</li></ul>
<ul><li>“Customer first” attitude and sense of urgency</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understanding of effective communication concepts, tools and techniques; ability to effectively transmit, receive, and accurately interpret ideas, information, and needs through the application of appropriate communication behaviors.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work under pressure, self-motivated to own and deliver on time, within budget</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of and the ability to monitor, measure, and optimize individual and combined utilization of hardware, software and telecommunications components.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of the organization's vision, structure, culture, philosophy, operating principles, values, and code of ethics; ability to apply this understanding appropriately to diverse situations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of effective presentation tools and techniques; ability to present information to groups with the appropriate degree of formality. Knowledge of the approaches, tools, and techniques for promoting creative, original thinking and ability to apply it to a variety of business situations.</li></ul>
