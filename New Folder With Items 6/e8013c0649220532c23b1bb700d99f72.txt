<h2>Technical Project Manager - NBC Sports</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<div>
<div>Role Purpose<br>
The Technical Project Manager (TPM) is responsible for the planning, development, and on-going operations of industry leading, live video, mobile streaming applications. The TPM guides the end to end Agile software development process including requirements development, development, testing and deployment, delivering cross-platform mobile video streaming products, with the overarching goal of shipping best in class video experiences within scope, budget and timelines.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with product, technical, and operational teams to determine business and technical requirements for new product delivery<br>
</li>
<li>Create detailed project schedules/plans, test plans, risk plans, budgets, communication plans, scope statements, etc. using generally accepted project management processes and tools<br>
</li>
<li>Develop resource plans by working with team leaders to ensure appropriate resources are in place throughout each project<br>
</li>
<li>Provide guidance to internal and vendor teams on project requirements and integrations<br>
</li>
<li>Coordinate project deliverables to ensure team is on track and communicate progress to all stakeholders in an organized and formal manner<br>
</li>
<li>Incorporate quality control processes into each project and lead QA, load testing and User Acceptance Testing<br>
</li>
<li>Manage medium-sized portfolio of projects strategically, identifying synergies amongst a series of inter-related projects to help define an optimal project roadmap<br>
</li>
<li>Proactively identify opportunities to adjust processes for efficiencies. Help develop strategies and procedures for continual improvement for project delivery.<br>
</li>
<li>Clearly and concisely articulate complex concepts for a variety of audiences.<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<p><b>Qualifications/Requirements</b></p>
<div>Basic Qualifications<br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of seven (7) years’ experience in a Project Management role managing software development of mobile, web, or connected device applications<br>
</li>
<li>BS/BA degree in a relevant field<br>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
Additional Qualifications<br>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience to carry out project management concepts, preferably PMI‘s PMBOK, Waterfall and/or Agile SDLC methodologies<br>
</li>
<li>Ability and desire to continually learn about new technologies, project management methodologies and adapt to ever-changing business and industry needs<br>
</li>
<li>Ability to understand and absorb technical requirements and translate into actionable deliverables, evaluate new ideas, manage tradeoffs, or identify risks<br>
</li>
<li>Excellent organization skills with attention to detail. Ability to work in a deadline-driven work environment and to multitask.<br>
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively organize meetings including; creating agendas, facilitating discussions, and summarizing the results, and assigning next steps<br>
</li>
<li>Experience coordinating with multiple internal departments (i.e. Business Development, Sales, Network Operations, WebOps, Analytics, Ad Sales Operations, etc.) to complete projects on time<br>
</li>
<li>Experience working with external resources, including vendors and freelancers<br>
</li>
<li>Ability to manage change in a controlled manner, identify and understand regular requests for change and quickly determine project impact on budget, schedule, scope and risk.<br>
</li>
</ul>
