<h2>Senior Business Analyst, Sales Planning</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p><b>Position Summary
</b></p>
<p>As a Business Analyst, Sales Planning you will be the technology and process / functional expert focusing on Sales &amp; Distributor planning. You will join the product management team and will collaborate with business stakeholders, distributors, product managers and Agile Teams to define and prioritize new capabilities through user stories, personas, journey maps and UX designs. You will play a critical role in bringing both business and digital thought leadership to the Agile Squads to define and execute Product Roadmaps and measure business value.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with key business stakeholders and Product Managers to understand goals, enabling capabilities and success measures for digital initiatives.
</li>
<li>Conduct focus groups, user interviews, ride-alongs, analyze behavioral usage data or 3’d party research to influence product direction or measure product effectiveness.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with other BA’s and Product Managers to define and align product roadmaps, assess TCO and build business cases to justify investment.
</li>
<li>Utilize Design Thinking and business process modeling techniques to facilitate feature / story definition or UX design. Act as the functional subject matter expert in the assigned functional domain.
</li>
<li>Ability to take a holistic product strategy and break it down into detailed requirements and acceptance criteria which can be leveraged by a cross-functional agile team.
</li>
<li>Define success metrics for key capabilities and releases; on a quarterly basis analyze how the product is performing against key objectives.
</li>
<li>Accountable for delivery of business value / outcomes.
</li>
<li>Evangelize success.
</li>
<li>Engage, educate, and support the super user network for the center of excellence for the specific applications within the defined process area.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years’ experience in information systems and/or analytical business systems; some technical experience is highly desired, but this role is not a technical role.
</li>
<li>Business process analyst experience in sales, marketing or supply chain is desired.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience implementing web / mobile solutions in an Agile environment.
</li>
<li>Experience and familiarity with Agile methodologies and tools (Rally, JIRA, CI/CD).
</li>
<li>Strong communication skills in writing, speaking, and presenting.
</li>
<li>Able to communicate technical or complex subject matter in business terms.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record of creating technology solutions to solve business problems (globally, or across multiple divisions or regions within a single company).
</li>
<li>High attention to detail is essential.
</li>
<li>Deep experience with digital – web – retail analytics.
</li>
<li>Analytical, problem solver.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education / Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business, information systems or computer science or related field with relevant work experience.
</li>
<li>5 years’ experience with digital product strategy, consulting or delivery.
</li>
<li>Experience working in high-tech, CPG or retail where there is a multi-tier distribution model is preferred.
</li>
</ul>
