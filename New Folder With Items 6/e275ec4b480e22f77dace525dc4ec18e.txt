 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About Clearwater Analytics®
<br><br>
Clearwater Analytics® is a global SaaS solution for automated investment data aggregation, reconciliation, accounting, and reporting. Clearwater helps thousands of organizations make the most of investment portfolio data with cloud-native software and client-centric servicing. Every day, investment professionals worldwide trust Clearwater to deliver timely, validated investment data and in-depth reporting.Clearwater aggregates, reconciles, and reports on more than $3 trillion in assets across thousands of accounts daily for our Fortune 500 clients.

Position Details
<br><br>
Clearwater is seeking candidates with treasury and/or accounting experience to join our Pre-Sales Team. This team is a group of experienced individuals who are responsible for assisting large deals in the sales pipeline by coordinating efforts between Sales and Marketing, creating content, and providing technical support to prospect visits and RFPs. This role requires a well-rounded individual who has excellent organizational and interpersonal skills as well as proven project management and coordination skills. A Pre-Sales Engineer will assist in the coordination of key project deliverables by helping track, communicate progress and deliver successful projects. This creative, self-starting candidate should have the ability to work well with and gain the respect of both internal and external colleagues. Travel is required for this role. Compensation will include a competitive base salary with a commission component tied to overall sales growth.
