<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>CALLING ALL CREATIVES!</p>
<p>We are looking for you. If you are talented, task-oriented, and excited for new challenges.. keep reading!</p>
<p>The Marketing Manager is a highly efficient, well-organized individual with the ability to build rapport with our clients and agents on a high level. They exercise excellent time management, and the capacity to manage multiple projects while maintaining a positive attitude. The Marketing Manager is self-motivated and continues to seek out ways to improve the process for continued growth. Most importantly they are an Ideal Team Player and are humble, hungry and smart in understanding people.</p>
<p>This person will be able to quickly identify with his/her clients and use marketing strategies effectively to position homes for sale. They will use systems and processes to improve efficiency. They will also create all marketing templates, packages, etc. for listings and for the company. They will be responsible for overall brand management and integrity, following our core values and mission statement.</p>
<p>To be chosen for this role, you must have a strong background in marketing/design or a related field. Experience using the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Premiere Pro), Mailchimp, BombBomb, Facebook Ads Manager, Google Analytics/Adwords, Hootsuite, photography, videography or similar applications is a plus.</p>
<p>Responsibilities Include:</p>
<ul><li>Work directly with leadership to drive business growth opportunities through Listings, Client Referrals, marketing, or wherever they may be found in agent conversion funnel.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Learning based attitude. Will attend ongoing training and coaching to maintain high level of agent and client satisfaction.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversee and manage all Listing Files, Listing marketing efforts including advertising campaigns.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain the constant communication and scheduling of the team Runner.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide excellent and consistent communication with clients and agents.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and maintain brand guide</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create and maintain all marketing assets for listings, marketing, social media, events, etc. and as needed by the team.</li></ul>
<p>Skills Needed:</p>
<ul><li>Demonstrated ability to think strategically about complex issues and develop thoughtful recommendations and action plans</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proactive thinking and take charge attitude</li></ul>
<ul><li>People-oriented, ability to understand how agents and consumers think and use that understanding to influence agent and consumer behavior</li></ul>
<ul><li>Curious, always thinking, motivated to find a “better way”</li></ul>
<ul><li>Skilled in learning styles with the ability to develop listing systems and how to implement them.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Energetic, enthusiastic and people-oriented. Enjoys working with different personalities and can adapt/accommodate to others working styles while achieving goals and objectives of projects and deliverables.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Service-oriented and responsive. Works well under deadlines. Can employ personal time management to prioritize projects and get work completed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborative attitude. Adept at building internal relationships/dialogue to help facilitate process, as well as generate the ‘right’ result. This includes team building and development.</li></ul>
<p>Education / Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma is required</li>
<li>A college education is a plus (specifically in hospitality, marketing, business, or Advertising/PR)</li>
<li>Experience with Google Chrome (Docs, Sheets, Calendar, Gmail) a plus</li>
<li>Customer service experience</li>
<li>Outgoing, sincere and happy attitude</li>
<li>2+ years marketing experience or equivalent education</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $30,000.00 to $60,000.00 /year</p>
