 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>

<div><div><div>Under the supervision of the Assistant Vice Chancellor for Alumni and Affiliate Relations (AAR), serve as a revenue generation specialist with a focus on generating revenue through AAR's wine, travel and business partnership programs. Also responsible for management of the UC Davis Aggie Adventures Travel program and UC Davis Alumni Wine Program.
