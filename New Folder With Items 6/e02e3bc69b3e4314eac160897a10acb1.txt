<h2>Director of Strategic Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Backed by leading venture capital investors including Sequoia, NEA, and Scale, Forter is a fast-growing, enterprise SaaS startup that has already established itself as a leader in the online fraud prevention market. Forter's fraud prevention system is designed to be consumer-centric, blocking fraud with accuracy and at the same time enabling growth by increasing approvals and ensuring an excellent customer experience. Forter's ability to increase sales and reduce chargebacks at scale has enabled the company to sign up many of the largest e-commerce merchants and online travel agencies in the world.
<br><br>
As Forter continues its rapid growth trajectory, the Director of Enterprise Sales will be responsible for driving sales for one of our Enterprise Sales Teams with holding a quota for all accounts. This role will manage a team of strategic and industry specific Enterprise Account Executives and help define strategies and account plans for growing revenue within these accounts, as well as total revenue across the team.
<br><br>
<b>Stuff you'll be doing:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Carry a team quota &amp; proven track record of meeting and/or exceed sales goals</li>
<li>Define and execute sales plans and strategies, including specific account plans, defining named accounts, establish relationships with decision makers etc.</li>
<li>Hire, recruit, mentor and develop top Enterprise sales talent across territories</li>
<li>Develop and own the sales "funnel", optimizing every step continuously</li>
<li>Define new processes to increase overall productivity</li>
<li>Provide regular reporting of pipeline and forecast</li>
<li>Keep abreast of competition, competitive issues and products</li>
<li>Communicate proactively with management, customers and support staff</li>
<li>Participate in team-building and company growth activities including strategy setting and training</li>
<li>Travel to customer locations in territory to support of sales efforts</li>
</ul>
<b>Stuff you'll need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of enterprise sales management experience is an absolute must</li>
<li>At least 5 years of Enterprise sales experience (payments or Fraud preferred)</li>
<li>Experience working for a high growth software company</li>
<li>Superb interpersonal and verbal/written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to influence, train and motivate others</li>
<li>Prior success building and leading a sales team and its corresponding revenue targets, and experience developing/executing a plan to achieve it</li>
<li>Startup background - Comfort with a fast paced environment and changing requirements.</li>
<li>Technically savvy</li>
<li>Comfortable and familiar with cross-functional collaboration</li>
<li>Loves to sell, Energetic,Enthusiastic</li>
</ul>
<b>Additional Attributes that would be helpful:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Relevant experience in e-commerce, payments, or fraud industries</li>
<li>Collaboration with internal product teams to drive new solutions that meet buyers' needs is a plus.</li>
<li>Comfort with new technologies; ability to communicate with those who are tech savvy.</li>
</ul>
