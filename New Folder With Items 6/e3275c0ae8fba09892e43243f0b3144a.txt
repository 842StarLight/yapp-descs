<b>
Your Career</b><br>
<p>At Palo Alto Networks, channel development is an extension of the territory sales team; the ultimate goal is to train and enable our channel partners to become more self-sufficient and gain leverage for the Palo Alto Networks brand. The Channel Business Manager (CBM) will be responsible for activities centered on relationship management and pipeline creation at a National Channel Partner to achieve measurable results such as increased revenue, deal registration, market share, and seller penetration.</p>
<b>
Your Impact</b><ul>
<li>
Directly manage Palo Alto Networks partner nationally with whom you will contribute to and execute annual business plans to drive all aspects of the partner relationship including sales and pipeline development, executive and field alignment, business development, sales, and technical enablement, certification plans and partner marketing.</li>
<li>
Work closely with corporate business development on embedded solutions creation, implementation, and GTM.</li>
<li>
Partner attainment against national and regional joint sales goals will become a key measurement of success.</li>
<li>
Facilitate, manage, and administer in-region sales and technical enablement for partner Sales Reps and System Engineers; presenting and promoting Palo Alto Networks value proposition</li>
<li>
Facilitate the creation, delivery, and certification of authorized training, professional services and front line support programs based on Palo Alto Networks technologies and solutions</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Field Marketing to facilitate, manage and drive demand generation activities ensuring appropriate MDF ROI and compliance</li>
<li>
Metric tracking and reporting focused on seller penetration, Territory and Account Mappings, Deal Registrations, new logos, and partner lift contribution</li>
<li>
Facilitate and participate in Quarterly Business Reviews (QBR) with cross-functional stakeholders and leaders from both organizations to measure progress against mutual business objectives</li>
<li>
Actively manage and drive Deal Registrations, pipeline development, and provide deal support to ensure orders are booked through the partner and Distributor as forecasted</li>
</ul>
<b>
Your Experience</b><ul>
<li>
Austin-based location, this position requires being on-site at Dell multiple days a week</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of Channel Operating and Partner operating models, specifically Dell GTM motions around Public Sector and SLED</li>
<li>
Strong business acumen with proven ability to engage at all levels of the partner from individual contributor up to senior leadership</li>
<li>
Proven track record of new Partner onboarding, exceeding performance objectives, and consistently growing a new vendor line 50%+ YoY</li>
<li>
Prior experience developing pipeline creation programs that demonstrated partner lift</li>
<li>
Fast-paced, high growth company experience; preferably with Cloud, Cybersecurity and/or SaaS</li>
<li>
Strong relationship building skills</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and presentation skills, especially at the executive level</li>
<li>
Solid negotiation and conflict resolution skills</li>
<li>
8-10 years of progressive channel development experience</li>
<li>
Dell experience required, preferably 3-4 years with a manufacturer calling into Dell Enterprise and/or Dell SLED</li>
<li>
University (Bachelor’s) degree or equivalent education</li>
<li>
Must have strong proficiency with various software applications including Google G-Suite and Salesforce.com</li>
</ul>
