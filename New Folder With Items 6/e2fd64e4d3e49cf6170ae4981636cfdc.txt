<h2>IT Billing and Procurement Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>CIO Technology Solutions is a Technology Solutions Provider of cloud hosting, technology support, network security and compliance, and design and solution consulting. We are committed to providing best in class service to our clients as well as helping our people grow and reach their potential. We live by the idea of work hard and play hard. We are like family and we’re passionate about what we do! If you think this is the right environment for you, we are currently seeking the newest addition to our team.</p>
<p>We’re seeking an experienced <b>IT Billing and Procurement Specialist</b>. This person is responsible for timely and accurate processing of customer’s invoices, as well as resolving discrepancies, communicating with customers regarding billing questions, and developing customer relationships. This position is also responsible for the company’s supply of products and services. They will develop vendor relationships and work to find the most cost-effective deals and suppliers. This position will interface with all areas of operation but will focus on current customer product and service fulfillment and assisting in the successful implementation of customer projects and onboarding. If you are da motivated self-starter who is customer service oriented, has strong accuracy, detail orientation and organizational skills, enjoy solving problems and helping people (not only clients but team members), then we want you to join our family!</p>
<p><b>
General Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare invoices and various reports</li>
<li>Maintain customer agreements for recurring services</li>
<li>Reconcile variance reports of monthly billing counts</li>
<li>Research and resolve billing inquiries – communicate with customer</li>
<li>Make weekly collection calls and report results to management</li>
<li>Assist in maintaining customer and vendor databases (addresses, contacts, etc)</li>
<li>Collaborate and work closely with other team members to ensure company monthly billing occurs in a timely and efficient manner</li>
<li>Obtain product pricing and availability in advance of the preparation of sales quotes</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Managing the Procurement processes in ConnectWise to achieve Just in Time delivery of products for customer projects while minimizing delivery time for break fix solutions<ul>
<li>Purchasing Approvals</li>
<li>Creating Purchase Orders</li>
<li>Placing orders with vendors</li>
<li>Receiving and shipping products</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Ensure proper and timely notification to the technical team of arriving products
</li>
<li>Reconciliation of Vendor Statements monthly
</li>
<li>Product RMAs processing with vendors (return material authorizations)</li>
<li>Assist in the development of a Standard Inventory List (internal office supplies) and establishment of normal inventory levels to be used in weekly inventory counts and inventory orders</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Provide executive and management assistance</li>
<li>Other projects and responsibilities as assigned</li>
</ul>
