<h2>Analyst, Business Systems I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Business Systems Analyst I (BSA) Solves organizational information problems and requirements by analyzing requirements; designing software programs; recommending system controls and protocols. Represent the business community and IT in prioritizing, analyzing and documenting business processes, identifying opportunities for improvement/enhancements and negotiating agreements and commitments between business partners (internal and external) and IT from requirements gathering to final implementation and maintenance. The Business System Analyst will work directly with clients/leadership at various levels within the company and with outside parties to research and identify operational business requirements with sufficient detail and clarity to allow IT staff to collaboratively develop the solution.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Act as a Business/SME liaison back to the business leadership or IT Staff to influence, manage change, and educate for priority setting to ensure business commitments and objectives are met.</li>
<li>Participate in cross-functional work sessions with both the business and IT customers to capture stakeholder needs and required system requirements/features.</li>
<li>Involved in creating the Project Charter, SLA’s and Statements of Work.</li>
<li>Partners with the IT Architect while identifying resources to accomplish the goals and objectives of the project.</li>
<li>Participate in development, documentation and analysis of detailed functional requirements via system</li>
</ul>
