<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>At Cantel, preventing infection is our business. We are dedicated to delivering high-quality, innovative solutions that help our customers improve patient care. Choosing a career with Cantel means joining a company that’s driven by an entrepreneurial spirit and the passion to shape the future of infection prevention. If you want to be inspired by your work and have a hand in shaping the future of infection prevention, we want to meet you. Come and see why people love working here.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Cantel is a leading global company dedicated to delivering innovative infection prevention products and services for patients, caregivers, and other healthcare providers which improve outcomes, enhance safety and help save lives. Our products include specialized medical device reprocessing systems for endoscopy and renal dialysis, advanced water purification equipment, sterilants, disinfectants and cleaners, sterility assurance monitoring products for hospitals and dental clinics, disposable infection control products primarily for dental and GI endoscopy markets, dialysate concentrates, hollow fiber membrane filtration and separation products. Additionally, we provide technical service for our products. Cantel medical is a leading provider of infection prevention products and services in the healthcare market, specializing in the following operating units:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Medical</b></div>
<div>
Endoscopy procedure disposables include single-use valves and irrigation tubing. Medical device reprocessing systems, disinfectants, detergents and other supplies are used to disinfect high-level endoscopes. This segment is operated through MEDIVATORS.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Life Sciences</b></div>
<div>
Water purification systems for dialysis facilities, including hemodialysis dialyzer and reprocessing systems, as well as sterilants and filtration products. This segment is operated through Mar Cor Purification, Inc. and MEDIVATORS.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Dental</b></div>
<div>
Single-use, infection prevention products used primarily in the dental market including face masks, sterilization pouches, towels and bibs, tray covers, saliva ejectors, germicidal wipes, plastic cups and disinfectants. This segment is operated through Crosstex International, Inc.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>The Director of Clinical Affairs is responsible for managing the clinical team to ensure pre-clinical and post-clinical programs for all product lines within Cantel. This position will manage the global clinical strategies for optimizing clinical studies, analyzing the results and reviewing clinical study protocols. The Director of Clinical Affairs will be the strategic clinical adviser to senior leaders and partner cross-functionally with all levels in the organization.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Main Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement clinical strategy for Cantel.
</li>
<li>Provide vision and leadership within and external to areas of responsibility. Contribute to the development of personnel within research and development. Act as a mentor within research and development to provide both career guidance and professional development.</li>
<li>
Lead and develop staff to ensure clear communication, meaningful employee development, and motivation.</li>
<li>
Manage, design and implement pre-clinical studies and post-clinical programs for all Cantel product lines.</li>
<li>
Design clinical trials and studies in collaboration with internal stakeholders to align with corporate strategies.</li>
<li>
Develop scientific podium and publication strategy for clinical data.</li>
<li>
Oversee analysis of data and composition of presentations/reports/manuscripts resulting pre-clinical and clinical studies.</li>
<li>
Synthesize literature and competitive information across product lines and relate it to Cantel clinical data.</li>
<li>
Manage customer engagement and participation in clinical studies.</li>
<li>
Lead cross-functional partnership with marketing, sales, operations, R&amp;D, RA/QA to ensure optimal use of clinical data.</li>
<li>
Maintain global policies, procedures and processes, product information and required clinical documentation.
</li>
<li>Proactively contribute to the development of the annual budget from an integrated functional prospective. Monitor progress against budget and inform management of deviations with specific correction recommendations. Define the need for incremental spending needs to achieve company goals.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate and lead by the Cantel Values and Leadership Principles.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>

<li>
Clinical/medical licensure: RN, NP, PA, MD or equivalent.
</li>
<li>15+ years’ experience working in clinical research.</li>
<li>
8+ years’ related industry experience.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills and proactive problem-solving skills are critical to success.</li>

</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
