<h2>Product Marketing Manager - Westwood, MA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Telestream specializes in products that make it possible to get video content to any audience. Our customers include the world's leading media and entertainment companies, and we are experiencing significant growth as organizations look to simplify the access, creation, and exchange of digital media. Our collaborative approach allows are employees to thrive as they learn new things. If you are looking for an industry leader where your efforts will be appreciated, Telestream is the place for you. We are currently seeking a professional Manufacturing Operations Manager to join our team.
</p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
As a Product Marketing Manager, you will be responsible for the outbound marketing activities for your products. You will craft the messaging and positioning for products. Attention to detail and an eye for quality, along with the ability to grasp and translate technical capabilities into benefits is crucial. In your role as a Product Marketing Manager you will be the expert on the customers of your products, how they buy and their buying criteria and will transfer that knowledge to the sales channel.</p>
<p><b>
Key responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop clear product positioning along with messaging that articulates the value of products and solutions in monetary terms whenever possible.</li>
<li>
Sales enablement communicate the value proposition of the products to the sales team and develop the sales tools that support the selling process of your products; develop and maintain strategic partnerships and relationships.</li>
<li>
Product launch plan the launch of new products and releases and manage the cross-functional implementation of the plan.</li>
<li>
Market intelligence be the expert on your buyers, how they buy and their buying criteria; be the expert on your competition and how to win against them; work with product team to prioritize customer-driven features.</li>
<li>
Demand generation provide input, content and review for demand generation activities.</li>
<li>
Product evangelism go-to person for telling our story to target markets by connecting with customers individually and on social channels, on stage at events, during industry-wide webcasts, in high level meetings with other players in the industry and wherever else it makes sense to go.</li>
<li>
Content creation technology overseeing and planning the technical infrastructure required to enact marketing initiatives and content creation.</li>
<li>
Training train internal teams and personnel on upcoming product features, common or ideal customer workflows and best-practices.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Location: Westwood, MA</li>
<li>
5+ years of software product marketing experience with at least 2 years experience in a field facing role</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in marketing or communications business emphasis is a plus</li>
<li>
Dynamic personality who is a compelling presenter and can tell a story in an engaging way</li>
<li>
Excellent writing and presentation skills with the ability to present technical products to both technical and non-technical audiences</li>
<li>
Ability to be both on camera and behind the camera for video content production webinars, live shows, recorded content</li>
<li>
Excellent people and management skills to interact with staff, colleagues and cross-functional teams, and third parties.</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively with deadlines and able to set priorities even when those priorities change</li>
<li>
Up to 25% travel required</li>
</ul>
