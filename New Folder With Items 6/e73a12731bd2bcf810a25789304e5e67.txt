<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>The Branch Leader is responsible for directing the overall activities of an ICS Field Branch. In this role a Branch Leader is expected to present to all levels of the enterprise, manage a full profit and loss statement, develop and lead a team, adapt to ever-changing technological developments and manage an operations strategy, all while managing a cradle-to-grave sales strategy, and understanding the sales and market trends within the industry.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Initiate growth focused activities to realize and capture market potential.
</li>
<li>Foster positive customer and contract carrier relations to retain and grow current business.
</li>
<li>Ensure customers receive superior on-time service.
</li>
<li>Hire, train and develop staff to meet branch objectives in regards to customer satisfaction, sales, and operations.
</li>
<li>Responsible for budget and profit margins to meet and exceed branch and business unit goals for sales and profitability.
</li>
<li>Prepare annual, monthly, and weekly plans to ensure each employee understands their goals in relation to the strategic objectives of the branch and ICS.
</li>
<li>Generates leads to develop new business with major accounts to grow branch revenue and maintain margin goals.
</li>
<li>Network with internal sales and marketing staff to develop and grow business.
</li>
<li>Properly manage risk and maintain financial integrity in all business matters.
</li>
<li>Ensure compliance to all local, state and federal (DOT) laws and regulations.
</li>
<li>See that all compliance issues are planned, updated or resolved daily.
</li>
<li>Foster a work environment that promotes J.B. Hunt’s values for people, performance, and ethics.
</li>
<li>Moderate travel required.
</li>
<li>Position requires full time attendance.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<p>What does it take to be considered?
</p>
<ul>
<li>A degree and 3 years of relevant <b>Flatbed </b>3PL/Brokerage/Transportation experience;
</li>
<li>OR 5 years of relevant <b>Flatbed </b>3PL/Brokerage/Transportation experience.
</li>
<li>Must understand Budget and P&amp;L management.
</li>
<li>Think strategic, but be willing to get your hands dirty.
</li>
<li>You must be able to travel and be on call 24/7.
</li>
<li><i>Leadership experience is preferred, but not required.
</i></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>So here is the job in a nutshell...
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Start your office by building your team. We encourage you to recruit experienced J.B. Hunt personnel to build your office around.
</li>
<li>Find customers for your office.
</li>
<li>Generate creative 3PL solutions to meet said customer’s supply chain needs.
</li>
<li>Leverage current assets, existing carrier relationships, or develop new carriers to deliver the solutions you crafted for your customers.
</li>
<li>Work with our existing sales force to develop addition business opportunities for your office.
</li>
<li>Continue to hire and train quality personnel and provide them a strategic vision for the future.
</li>
<li>Full P&amp;L ownership. You set your goals to drive your future.
 </li>
<li>GROW, GROW, GROW and strive for excellence daily.
</li>
</ul>
