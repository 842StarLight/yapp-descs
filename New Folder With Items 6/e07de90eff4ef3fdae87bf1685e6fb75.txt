<h2>Volkswagen General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Speedcraft Automotive Group is looking for an Automotive General Manager. If you have a proven track record, a strong work ethic and the vision it takes to lead a winning team, come grow with us!</p>
<p>Current Automotive General Managers and General Sales Managers are welcome to apply.</p>
<p>POSITION INFORMATION:</p>
<p>The General Manager is completely responsible for and will be held accountable for the successful operation of the entire dealership. S/he will develop and execute plans to ensure the dealership of a reasonable profit and the stockholders of a fair return on their investments. The General Manager will accomplish all objectives through planning, motivating, and coordinating the activities of all departments.</p>
<p>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<p>Management Related:</p>
<ul>
<li>Plan the dealership's operations for the coming year and report the plan to the Owner/CFO/EVP/COO for approval.</li>
<li>Obtain, review and recommend improvements for each department manager's monthly forecasts and plans.</li>
<li>Ensure that every department operates harmoniously and profitably. Develop and maintain a good working relationship with the factory and lending institutions.</li>
</ul>
<p>Personnel Related:</p>
<ul>
<li>Formulate policies and establish procedures for all training programs and monitor their effectiveness.</li>
<li>Handle all dealership personnel function, to include: interviewing, hiring, measuring and evaluating performance, recognizing accomplishments, and termination.</li>
<li>Review and approve the compensation plans for all employees.</li>
<li>Ensure that department managers are training and supervising employees for optimum effectiveness.</li>
<li>Communicate management's policies and procedures to all employees and ensure that they are understood and followed.</li>
<li>Complete a formal performance evaluation of each department manager once every six months.</li>
<li>Provide enthusiastic leadership to shape employees' attitudes and build morale.</li>
<li>Establish harmony and teamwork among departments.</li>
<li>Conduct managers' meetings at scheduled intervals.</li>
<li>Coordinate with the business office to ensure that records and analyses are maintained accurately.</li>
</ul>
<p>Automotive General Manager Skills/Qualifications  * * Minimum of 2yrs prior Auto Dealership Management Experience</p>

<li>High School Diploma</li>

<li>Administrative Writing Skills</li>

<li>Closing Skills</li>
<li>Customer Service</li>
<li>Selling to Customer Needs</li>
<li>Statistical Analysis</li>
</ul>
