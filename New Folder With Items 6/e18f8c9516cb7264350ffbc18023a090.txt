<h2>Racking & Conveyor Systems Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>YOU DON’T STAY #1 IN LOS ANGELES YEAR AFTER YEAR WITHOUT REACHING HIGHER.</p>
<p>Toyota Material Handling Solutions (TMHS) has been serving the material handling needs of Los Angeles county since 1973. Even though we have been #1 in Los Angeles county since 2000, we still continually strive to improve. It’s in our DNA. It’s called “kaizen” or continuous improvement. It’s the dedication to always find a better way to do things. And that commitment runs through the entire organization.</p>
<p>JOB SUMMARY:</p>
<p>Selling and managing the installation of warehouse racking and conveyor systems to create more useable warehouse space for our clients.</p>
<p>JOB RESPONSIBLITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develops new accounts</li>
<li>Secures orders from existing customers either through visiting the customer facility or contacting by phone</li>
<li>Secures opportunities to quote on customer requirements</li>
<li>Follows up (by phone or visit) on quotations submitted to customers</li>
<li>Communicates directly with new and existing customers to explain features and merits of products and/or services offered</li>
<li>Assists with the design of Racking and Conveyor Systems with CAD vendor for all drawings and blueprints</li>
<li>Work with engineering vendor for all necessary seismic studies</li>
<li>Demonstrates products or services and provides assistance in the best application of product or services</li>
<li>Answers all customer questions concerning the products with appropriate referrals where required</li>
<li>Provides customer quotes using in-house company software</li>
<li>Closes transactions and takes orders</li>
<li>Investigates warranty claims on products and works with vendors to resolve</li>
<li>Contacts prospects and explains features and merits of products and services offered, utilizing persuasive sales techniques</li>
<li>Must keep current on all racking and conveyor product offerings</li>
<li>Write up quote for all systems jobs</li>
<li>Order product from the various vendors, obtaining the best pricing</li>
<li>Obtain all proper government permits and fees to complete all jobs</li>
<li>Schedule installation through a third party vendor, oversee all work is done to specifications and on time</li>
</ul>
<p>JOB QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>15+ years of Systems Account Manager experience.</li>
<li>Minimum of five years experience working in racking and/or conveyor systems sales.</li>
<li>Experience in the material handling industry.</li>
<li>Ability to assist with the design and read blue prints.</li>
<li>Experience working with CAD drawing and working with CAD Personnel</li>
<li>Experience with obtain work permits.</li>
<li>Must have good computer skills including the ability to use MS Office and Company in-house software; Internet searching skills necessary</li>
<li>Time management skills required</li>
<li>Self motivating personality and ability to work independently</li>
<li>Goal &amp; objective oriented</li>
<li>Strong communication skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Good driving record necessary; must have own auto and auto insurance</li>
<li>Ability to perform assigned duties with minimum personal supervision from Sales Manager</li>
</ul>
<p>We offer a comprehensive benefits package including medical, dental, and vision insurance, flexible spending account, 401K, PTO, and paid holidays.</p>
