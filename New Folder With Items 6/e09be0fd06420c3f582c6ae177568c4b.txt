<h2>Production Planning Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>MultiSource Manufacturing LLC is </b>a customer-focused contract manufacturer working to provide affordable, high quality engineering and fabrication services. We strive to be a comprehensive resource for all the services you need from hands-on project management to industry standardized installation. For precision machining to complete assembly, we are your one-stop-shop for quality, efficiency, and excellence in manufacturing.
</p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>
This newly created position will be responsible for the overall planning of production and is an integral part of inventory control and capacity analysis. Coordinates and works directly with our internal customers to meet delivery dates and expectations. Develops and executes planning processes, material flow procedures, and business analyses that will drive continuous improvement in the operations management systems. Oversee production planning at all MultiSource Manufacturing locations.</p>
<p>
Headquarter in Minnetonka, this role will serve multiple locations, primarily in the Minneapolis/St. Paul metro area. Role will lead the Production Planning team and will report to the Director of Pre-Production.<br>
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND BASIC DUTIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and implement product planning process at all MultiSource locations. Recommends procedures, systems, and method changes while ensuring compliance to quality control standards and continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Build and maintain the Demand Plan to support capacity and resource planning. Schedule demand based on the product mix, equipment and labor capacity and customer strategy. Ensure accurate commitment dates to customer service team across multiple sites. Work closely with Sales, Operations and Pre-production teams to meet customer demands.</li>
<li>Maintain inventory levels at all locations. Identify and quantify capacity of departments to assist in determining and communicating real-time lead times and assist in making determination of where to produce products when capacity becomes a constraint.</li>
<li>Proactively initiates solutions to scheduling, materials, engineering and manufacturing challenges. Provide input on issues such as Lead-time, Period of Supply, Safety stock, etc.</li>
<li>Schedule all phases of manufacturing to support on-time delivery to customers. Coordinates with site leaders and their departments with respect to production volume, cost, quality and meeting production schedules and delivery dates. Calculate internal lead times to communicate real time expectations.</li>
<li>Analyze the ERP/Excel system to ensure production planning data is accurate.
</li>
<li>Expedites and monitors the progress of jobs released to the production floor, performs time standards and updates processes when appropriate.</li>
<li>Generates, communicates, and track performance(s) to the following metrics: Schedule Attainment, Inventory Days on Hand, On-Time Delivery, and other metrics as requested.</li>
<li>Plan, organize, and direct the Production Planning team ensuring they provide the necessary technical support to sales, manufacturing, and quality to achieve success. Manages by Leadership Effectiveness (EI).</li>
<li>Monitor material shortages, stock outages while working with Supply Chain and manufacturing to meet customer demands.</li>
<li>Keeps currently informed of new and/or maintains adherence to company policies, safety standards, and good housekeeping practices.</li>
<li>Assures efficient and economical utilization of materials, improvement of methods, and elimination of wasteful practices.</li>
<li>Direct and participate in CI teams and activities.</li>
<li>Performs related work as apparent or assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

<b>REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE:</b> Computer experience and knowledge using Microsoft programs. Extensive ERP / MRP systems experience preferred. Utilizing spreadsheet and ERP-type software in managing the planning process. Standard business practices and production scheduling methodology.<br>

</li>
