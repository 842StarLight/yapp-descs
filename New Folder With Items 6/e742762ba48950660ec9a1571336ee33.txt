<h2>PLM Solutions Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<p>Aras is an innovative, global software company that provides advanced Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) software solutions to business customers worldwide including BMW, General Motors, Airbus, Microsoft, Gulfstream, Motorola, General Electric, Honda, and more. With many significant recent wins we are growing fast and need to expand our capabilities.
</p>
<p>The PLM Solutions Consultant supports the customer decision process by demonstrating products and securing the “technical close” in complex solutions. This position collaborates with community, consulting, engineering, and product management resources to ensure proposed deals include technical solutions that accurately address customer needs, and are appropriately supported by key customer technical decision-makers.</p>
<p>The PLM Solutions Consultant will be working within the Automotive Community Group at Aras based in the Detroit, MI metropolitan area and reports to the Senior Director of Automotive Community.</p>
<p> <b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proactively scope the technical solution required to address customer requirements, assess customers’ met and unmet needs, and recommend solutions that optimize value for both the customer and Aras.</li>
<li>Assist in building customized solution demonstrations that meet customer requirements.</li>
<li>Receive input from all necessary solution stakeholders via community development and adapts solution demonstrations, as necessary, to ensure a clear representation of how Aras’ solutions addresses the customers’ needs and will ultimately provide a benefit to the customer.</li>
<li>Complete required training and development objectives within the assigned time frame.</li>
<li>Provide coaching and professional development to team-member sales associates in order to enhance their product knowledge, technical acumen, and technical sales skills.</li>
<li>Opportunistically pursue additional business development opportunities within customer firms.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A proven track record in demonstrating PLM and other enterprise solutions enabling new client acquisition.</li>
<li>Experience within the automotive industry and can demonstrate knowledge in automotive processes like APQP process, PFMEA, DFMEA and PPAP processes, as well as general knowledge of automotive data management.</li>
<li>A proven track record in solution sales to both corporate and large enterprise clients.</li>
<li>Minimum of four years demonstrating Software Solutions as an Applications Engineer or in a sales role including hands-on experience with PLM systems.</li>
<li>Able to develop complete and accurate proposals with minimal need for review.</li>
<li>Excellent listening capability; analytical construction of message content; clear and concise delivery of message</li>
<li>Knowledge of consultative solution-based selling as evidenced via experience with a solutions selling methodology
</li>
<li>Hands-on skills with PLM systems</li>
<li>Deep familiarity with processes and people typical in organizations considering enterprise software purchase</li>
</ul>
