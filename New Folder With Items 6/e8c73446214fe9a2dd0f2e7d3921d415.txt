<p><b>Position Description:</b><br>
<b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b>:</p>
<p>
Per Mar Security Services, the largest family-owned, full service security company in the Midwest, is seeking a Marketing Intern. The Marketing Intern will be based out of Per Mar Centre headquarters in Davenport, Iowa.</p>
<p>
The Marketing Intern will get hands on Digital Marketing experience, and be responsible for aiding the Marketing Team in implementing its Marketing and Communication plan.</p>
<ul><li><b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities:</b></li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in writing and creating content for press releases, blogs, email newsletters, ads and social media posts</li>
<li>
Analyze website, social media, advertising and lead data to track ROI for traditional and digital marketing and sales initiatives</li>
<li>
Assist with sponsored events</li>
<li>
Work with Inside Sales on marketing initiatives</li>
<li>
Assist with implementing and maintaining consistent branding across the company</li>
</ul>
