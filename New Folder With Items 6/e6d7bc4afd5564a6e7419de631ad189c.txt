Are you passionate about solving complex business challenges with process engineering and technology? Do you thrive in environments that require cross-functional thinking, collaboration, and innovation? Do you have experience with asset management, website, and other business systems? If so, this may be the job for you.
The Beats IS&amp;T team is responsible for the technology strategy and delivery of IT systems used to enable the Beats line of business. The team ensures technology and business strategy remain tightly aligned through cross-functional partnership, knowledge of industry trends, and familiarity with the Apple corporate systems landscape. We work closely with leadership, business partners, software engineers, vendors, information security, and others to deliver systems (people, process, and technology) to meet the demands of a fast-growing organization.
You will play a leading role in the delivery and operationalization of systems utilized by Beats, especially Beats Marketing, Sales, and Operations, working closely with the Beats leadership team. You will work with cross functional teams to deliver solutions to the business and serve as an advocate of the business to internal partners. A blend of business and technical acumen coupled with project management discipline is essential to the success in this role.</div>
