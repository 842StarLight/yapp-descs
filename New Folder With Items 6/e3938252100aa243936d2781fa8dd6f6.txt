<h2>Customer Service Mailer Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Summary</b>:</p>
<p>The Customer Service Mailer Technician works under the supervision of a pharmacist to deliver excellent customer service to ensure customer satisfaction and that each customer has a positive experience. This position processes payments, packages medications and ensures all records are properly maintained.</p>
<p><b>This entry-level position offers an opportunity to gain on-the-job-training for a Pharmacy Technician career.</b></p>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Fill, package, and verify medications for delivery.</li>
<li>Organize and prioritize packages to be shipped by start date.</li>
<li>Contact patients to notify them of shipment and confirm their information.</li>
<li>Process medication(s) through point-of-sale system.</li>
<li>Process and obtain payment for each prescription as required.</li>
<li>Affix Documentation Labels to daily log sheet and include special instructions or alternate addresses.</li>
<li>Create Shipping Label with appropriate shipping software (USPS, FedEx, and UPS) following S.O.P Number 3.010 for detailed labeling and shipping instructions and processes.</li>
<li>Verify each mailer for accuracy against the mail log shipping records.</li>
<li>Properly record each mailer to be shipped out according to S.O.P Number 3.010.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education: </b></p>
<p>High school diploma or equivalent</p>
<p><b>Experience and Knowledge: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have strong communication skills to ensure excellent customer service.</li>
<li>Good mathematical, analytical and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Strong computer skills and the ability to learn and apply new software.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively perform and multi-task in a fast-paced work environment.</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret detailed instructions.</li>
<li>Basic PC skills preferred detail oriented with excellent organizational skills.</li>
<li>Experience with shipping software (USPS, FedEx and UPS) a plus.</li>
<li>Six months retail experience in a pharmacy or health care environment.</li>
<li>Willingness to work a flexible schedule.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
