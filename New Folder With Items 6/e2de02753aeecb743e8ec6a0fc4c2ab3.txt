<h2>Presales Engineer USA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>Hi, we’re Betty Blocks! Do you like Big Hairy Audacious Goals? This is ours: By 2023, anyone can build an application. Quite a statement, right? So how we are doing with our BHAG? It’s going pretty well! So well, that we actually grew by 360% in 2018. And we’re just getting started! To support our superhuman ambition, we are looking for a Sales Engineer who wants to help to share the vision of our Betty Blocks no-code application development platform.</p>
<p><b>
What will you be doing?</b></p>
<p>
As a Presales Engineer you play a vital role in our sales team. You’ll be supporting your colleagues by building POC’s, demonstrate our platform to potential clients and support our sales-experts on the technical topics of the sales cycle. Within the sales team you are the to-go-person if someone has technical questions concerning Betty Blocks. Your technical knowledge is necessary in order to convince potential clients of our solution and you are always ready to support your colleagues for small and big requests and/or questions.</p>
<p><b>
What’s important for us?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have strong analytic insights, so you’re able to quickly translate the client’s processes into a Betty Blocks solution - and take the client along in the development process;</li>
<li>You have vast experience in developing custom-made applications and demonstrable knowledge of IT and software development (e.g. HTML, CSS &amp; JavaScript).</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Why you want to work at Betty Blocks?</b></p>
<p><b>
Contribute to the vision:</b><br>
We are going to change the world of application development, and fast! You’ll want to get in on that.</p>
<p><b>
A down-to-earth organizational structure without a lot of hierarchy:</b><br>
That scary man that you should address by title only and who makes you nervous to talk to (AKA the Manager) doesn’t work here. Just be yourself, we all are!</p>
<p><b>
What do we actually offer:</b><br>
We offer health insurance coverage, a brand new macbook, 15 holidays in addition to the national holidays and lots of room for personal growth.</p>
