The account executive (AE) position will oversee some of our most important relationships in the Experian Marketing Services (EMS) Financial Services vertical, including National and Regional Banks, FinTech and other related vertical clients and prospects. You will work closely with your internal sales partners and the leadership team to develop and manage a robust pipeline within an established vertical client base and new prospects. With direct responsibility for the entire sales cycle, from lead generation to closing, you will develop and nurture strategic relationships with senior leaders and key stakeholders. Bringing thought leadership, creative solution design and compelling ROI to each opportunity uncovered, you will lead the effort to position Experian Marketing Services as a true strategic advisor, building long-term partnerships and negotiating multi-year contracts which provide market-differentiating value for our most strategic Financial Services clients.<br>
