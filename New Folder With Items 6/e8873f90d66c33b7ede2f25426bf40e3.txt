<h2>IT Audit Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>The IT Audit Manager role will oversee IT General Controls (SOX testing) and ensure successful completion of IT &amp; Security assigned audit engagements. Specifically:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage IT audit projects utilizing internal staff and co-sourcing partners</li>
<li>
Oversee IT General Controls (SOX) testing</li>
<li>
Identify IT risks and process improvement opportunities</li>
<li>
Provide technical and quality oversight regarding IT risks and controls and technologies</li>
<li>
Participate in finance/business process audits as part of integrated projects</li>
<li>
Develop close and independent working relationships with IT and other business area management as necessary</li>
<li>
Develop reports and presentations on project findings and recommendations to senior management</li>
<li>
Monitor process and policy changes throughout an organization</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Player Profile</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You are a highly technical auditor with natural technology curiosity.</li>
<li>
6+ years of IT audit experience with a Big 4 accounting/consulting environment and/or experience conducting IT Audits in a multi-national company</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Information Systems Management, Computer Science, Information Assurance, or a related field.</li>
<li>
Industry recognized certification such as Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA) or Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) preferred</li>
<li>
Advanced technical understanding of IT processes - including applications and infrastructure, security and vulnerability assessments, change control, asset management, disaster recovery, data privacy, and IT risk assessment</li>
<li>
Excellent understanding of concepts related to information systems audit, information security, general IT controls, application controls and technology risks</li>
<li>
Knowledge of external leading risk and controls frameworks such as COBIT (Control Objectives for Information and related Technology), NIST and ISO27000</li>
<li>
Ability to develop, design, and execute IT audit plans and programs using a risk-based approach</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Oracle Database and Oracle R12</li>
<li>
Good project management and organization skills; ability to multitask</li>
<li>
Excellent ability to develop and write meaningful reports and presentations</li>
<li>
Motivated entrepreneur who works well individually and in teams</li>
<li>
Ability and willingness to travel domestically and internationally (20% of the time at a minimum)</li>
</ul>
