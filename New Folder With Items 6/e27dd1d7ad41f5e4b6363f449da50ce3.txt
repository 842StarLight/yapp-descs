<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Marketing Manager owns the marketing processes and brand for the entire Holz Group organization at Keller Williams Realty. They are someone who can think big enough to create huge systems, while sweating the details that ensure those systems work. They are creative enough to develop catchy material, while strategic enough to ensure the creativity gets results. The Marketing Manager is a natural leader, able to succeed by empowering others, with a strong focus on tracking results, maintaining accountability, and increasing efficiency. They have a proven track record of integrating direct response messaging across multiple platforms, including: social media, web, email, video, direct mail, events, sales team canvassing campaigns, or advertising and sponsorship.</p>
<p>
The Marketing Manager is skilled at executing multiple marketing campaigns at the same time and segmenting the sales database to make strategic offers to varying target markets. All of these systems and campaigns work synergistically together toward a common marketing and sales goal because of the Marketing Manager’s influence. The Marketing Manager works with the Director of Sales, Director of Lead Generation and CEO to ensure sales growth continues at a high level.</p>
<b>
Compensation:</b><p>
$75,000 - $100,000 plus company profit share</p>

<li>

<li>

<li>
Write and proof copy</li>
<li>
Develop marketing materials and collateral as needed for the entire organization</li>
<li>
Create and maintain master marketing budget</li>
<li>
Prepare and provide regular reports including marketing plan summary and campaign results</li>
<li>
Negotiate with outside vendors as needed</li>
</ul>
