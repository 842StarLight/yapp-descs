<h2>Account Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Account Representative</b></p>
<p>For over 30 years, L&amp;E Meridian has been at the forefront in our industry in offering professional services specializing in high-volume data management, data processing, digital printing and mail services. We are consistently ahead of the competition in technology and service.</p>
<p>We are looking to hire an Account Representative who is positive, upbeat and an organized doer who will view working at L&amp;E as a great professional opportunity. The person who fills this position will thrive in our multi-tasking environment, be energized by staying ahead of schedule in a fast-paced setting and will work closely to leadership of our organization.</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>This position works directly with our clients and our internal staff in coordinating schedules, monitor processing, and trafficking client projects from inception to completion by performing the following duties.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serves as liaison between clients, outside vendors, and inter-departments to coordinate efficient and timely project processing.</li>
<li>Updates production schedules.</li>
<li>Coordinates job estimates with assistance from Estimating department.</li>
<li>Logs jobs and trafficking activity in database or on production schedule and maintains job files of project materials.</li>
<li>Routes materials in various stages for approval by client.</li>
<li>Proofreads and traffics copy and blue lines, setups and insertion samples; performs quality control for approvals.</li>
<li>Follows quality control procedures to ensure accuracy and quality of work performed.</li>
<li>Monitors job progress and directs production scheduling to meet client deadlines.</li>
<li>Resolves discrepancies in job progression to ensure client satisfaction.</li>
<li>Prepares material according to specifications, distributes, to appropriate media vehicles before established deadline, and tracks progress.</li>
<li>Generates client billing and routes for approval as necessary.</li>
<li>Performs other job duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS/SKILLS &amp; KNOWLEDGE REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul><li>Work requires a knowledge of business and an excellent command of the English language.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have knowledge of administrative procedures, and knowledge of use and operation of standard office equipment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have knowledge of a variety of computer software applications in word processing, spreadsheets, database and presentation software.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have high level of interpersonal skills to handle sensitive and confidential situations. Position continually requires demonstrated poise, tact and diplomacy.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Some analytical ability is required in order to gather and summarize data for reports, find solutions to various administrative problems, and prioritize work.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work requires continual attention to detail in composing, typing and proofing materials, establishing priorities and meeting deadlines.</li></ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>Bachelor's (B.A.) degree from four-year college or university; or one to two years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. Must be proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel spreadsheets, Powerpoint, and be comfortable in a Windows operating system environment.</p>
