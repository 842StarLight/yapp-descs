<h2>Customer Service Representative - Tactical Gear</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Blue Force Gear is proud to design and manufacture the world’s lightest and most cutting edge military equipment – all made here in the USA! We have a dedicated team committed to the mission of our nation’s armed service, law enforcement, prepared citizen, and sporting enthusiast. Also important to our company culture is the spirit of “Always Better” – a quest for constant improvement both in the workplace and in your personal lives. In order to enhance our long term growth, we are looking for a knowledgeable, motivated Customer Service Representative!</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answers incoming telephone calls, emails, and faxes from retail, dealers, military, and law enforcement customers via our customer service portal</li>
<li>Receives above orders and inputs orders into Quickbooks</li>
<li>Responds to shipment and inventory inquiries, confirms orders, and resolves discrepancies</li>
<li>Investigates and troubleshoot customer service issues</li>
<li>Provides end users and dealers with technical support</li>
<li>Provides prospective dealers information on becoming an Authorized Dealer</li>
<li>Responds to dealer and end user inquiries regarding pricing and availability</li>
<li>Enters and modifies Sales Orders and enters quotes for Sales Department</li>
<li>Prepares, logs, tracks and maintains all RMA’s and orders throughout the RMA process</li>
<li>Follows up on website orders</li>
<li>Enters new customer data and update changes to existing accounts in the corporate database.</li>
<li>Appropriately communicate brand identity and corporate position.</li>
<li>Assist Sales Department as needed</li>
</ul>
