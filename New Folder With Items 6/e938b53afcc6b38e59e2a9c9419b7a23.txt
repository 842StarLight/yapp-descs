<p><b>Technical Account Manager</b></p>
<p>
The Technical Account Manager (TAM) assists the sales team with issues and customer management. This dedicated team provides customer response, customer escalation, and help desk follow-up to current issues. The TAM works directly with the customer, Technical Support Management, Field Service Management, Account Executives, Automation Optimization Consultants, and other teams to help our internal/external customers become quality sites of excellence. Each TAM is assigned to a sales territory and plays a critical role in enabling the sales team to effectively manage customer satisfaction. The selected individual will need to be based out of the South Central Region ( TX, LA, AL) within a 45 minutes' drive to a major airport.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage a defined regional set of customers.</li>
<li>TAM has responsibility for the management of the customer experience.</li>
<li>Schedule regular regional calls with the internal team-Sales team to participate in planning and to understand the account plan as defined by the sales leader.</li>
<li>Properly triage customer issues</li>
<li>Temporarily escalate engagement with and manage hot customer sites that experience complex, multi-faceted issues.</li>
<li>Engage the appropriate resources to ensure issue resolution. This includes making sure responsible parties are engaged on calls/on-site meetings and when applicable.</li>
<li>Provide planning to address customer performance and/or satisfaction issues. (i.e. Customer Support Issue escalation, Service Issues, software updates (hotfix)- application/tracking)</li>
<li>Prepare issue analysis, status updates, and special reporting to management on a regular basis.</li>
<li>Establish timely and clear, task and project expectations.</li>
<li>Maintain frequent communication with all levels of client interaction (Director of Pharmacy, IT, Pharmacy, Nursing, etc) to ensure proactive, solution-oriented problem identification and resolution</li>
<li>Serve as a key consultant for Product Development and Customer Response teams.</li>
<li>Work together with the Customer Response team on getting the appropriate Omnicell updates and upgrades in place.</li>
<li>Work with the account team on engaging the appropriate internal resources to help work with the site on product improvement and training.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with Upgrade Project Managers and Customer Response Team to ensure a successful implementation and smooth transition of implementation and upgrade projects.</li>
<li>Review help desk logs post-upgrade to monitor and advocate for the customers.</li>
<li>Provide product and workflow expertise to Aesynt Customers</li>
<li>Coordinate activities on the customer’s behalf across the business to ensure requests are handled appropriately and in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Work on complex problems where analysis of situations or data requires an in-depth evaluation of multiple variables and factors.</li>
<li>Recommend/take action to direct the analysis of and solution to customer problems.</li>
<li>Exercise judgment within broadly defined practices and policies in selecting methods, techniques, and evaluation criterion for obtaining results need for customer-focused interactions.</li>
<li>Often act independently in making decisions that will positively impact customer relations and future product sales opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Knowledge and Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated Project Management skills to prioritize the needs of multiple projects in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>User knowledge of information systems, especially automated order entry, medication administration, or pharmacy information systems.</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of healthcare industry</li>
<li>Several years’ experience supporting a hospital information technology or clinical environment</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Solid knowledge of computer hardware, PC Windows and Microsoft Office, Professional.</li>
<li>Strong leadership, interpersonal and communication skills.</li>
<li>Applicable education/training in regards to technical support.</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of company products, company functions, and/or service policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Collaborative, customer-focused and able to create visible value.</li>
<li>Self-directed with excellent time management and organization skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in an Engineering or Science discipline (In lieu of a Bachelor’s degree, an Associate’s degree in an Engineering or Science discipline and a minimum of 3 years of experience working on medical equipment OR Completion of Department of Defense Biomedical Equipment training program and a minimum of 1 year experience working on medical equipment.)</li>
<li>2+ years of experience in a hospital pharmacy or clinical work setting and/or information technology, product management, account management, sales, project management, and customer service.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of experience with Pharmacy specific drug configurations and complex robotics used in the pharmaceutical compounding and testing environment</li>
<li>2+ years of experience with FDA regulated products</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience working in the healthcare arena.</li>
<li>Extensive experience handling Customer Service/Management and problem resolution.</li>
<li>Working business experience with corporate customers.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of IV Solutions technical and quality processes</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>Up to 50% domestic travel, have own transportation and occasional availability during off-hours may be required.</li>
<li>Must be able to push heavy equipment and have good dexterity for doing routine configuration tasks.</li>
<li>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to lift and/or move up to 50 pounds; stand; walk/pace; use hands to handle equipment or feel, and reach with hands and arms. The employee is occasionally required to sit, stoop, kneel, crouch, or crawl.</li>
</ul>
