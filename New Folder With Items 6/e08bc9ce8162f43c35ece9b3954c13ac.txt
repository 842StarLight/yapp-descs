<h2>Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Teledyne Wireless is a flagship business unit of Teledyne Microwave Solutions (TMS) with over 50 years of experience in the design, development, and manufacture of sophisticated microwave/RF components and integrated assemblies for the most demanding challenges.</div>
<p>
Our products and capabilities are sought after by civilian and military organizations spanning numerous global markets, including Aviation, Communication, Electronic Warfare (EW/ECM/CIED), Industrial, Missile/UAV, Radar, Satcom, Space, and Test and Measurement. Teledyne Wireless invests heavily in research and development to maintain the ability to offer the world’s most advanced MW/RF capabilities for current and emerging challenges.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary and Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Direct all aspects of multiple development and or manufacturing TWT and TWTA programs from concept to production. Primary responsibilities include:
</li>
<li>Profit and Loss responsibility of assigned product line(s)</li>
<li>
Manages relationships with major customers</li>
<li>
Production oversight – coordination with manufacturing, engineering and MC/PC teams</li>
<li>
Product quality (returned product, yield, scrap and rework)</li>
<li>
Bids and proposals for new orders</li>
<li>
Contract compliance (TWT delivery, technical specification, failure evaluation/corrective action, acceptance testing).
</li>
<li>Price of Non-Conformance (PoNC) reduction.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for managing the implementation of specific customer contracts involving complex computer-based systems and/or government contracts.</li>
<li>
Plans, directs, controls and coordinates financial as well as engineering activities for the program(s), and for achieving financial and technical objectives.</li>
<li>
Define operational direction and institute remedial action(s) in order to satisfy customer requirements while achieving project specific sales targets/goals; gross margin targets; percent delivery on-time; bookings targets/goals; and customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Monitors program from project initiation through delivery, interfacing with customer on technical matters.</li>
<li>
Organizes interdepartmental activities ensuring completion of the program on schedule and within budget constraints.</li>
<li>
Assess status and risk, generate recovery plans and meet customer expectations and business objectives.</li>
<li>
May direct subordinates to achieve assignments using established guidelines, procedures and policies by providing leadership to a unit or group of employees, assigning tasks and checking work at frequent intervals to ensure production goals are met within organization goals of budget, time requirements and high level of quality.
</li>
<li>Interacts with functional peer groups regarding exchange or presentation of information.</li>
<li>
Pursues new ways to improve the efficiency of production. Develops, plans and administers procedures. Recommends changes to company policies.</li>
<li>
Confers with department supervisors or leads to determine status of assigned projects.</li>
<li>
Prepares reports.</li>
<li>
Identifies obstacles to achieving business goals and provides solutions or seeks assistance.</li>
<li>
Works to avoid erroneous decisions or failure to achieve results that may cause scrap, rework, use of excessive overtime, and/or delays in schedules.</li>
<li>
Frequently interacts with subordinate supervisors, outside customers, and functional peer groups at various management levels. Interaction normally requires the ability to gain cooperation of others, conducting presentations of technical information concerning specific project/schedules.</li>
<li>
May be tasked to change the thinking, or gain acceptance of others in sensitive situations.
</li>
<li>Daily availability to include evenings and weekends when necessary to reach goals and deadlines.</li>
</ul>
