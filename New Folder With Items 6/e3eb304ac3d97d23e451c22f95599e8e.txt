<h2>General Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Reliable Imports &amp; RV is experiencing a RECORD year! We are currently seeking an experienced General Sales Manager to oversee Mazda-Hyundai-Subaru-Preowned-F&amp;I+</p>
<p>The <b>General Sales Manager</b> is responsible for profitability in both the new and used vehicle departments and for customer retention. To achieve this, they must effectively manage sales personnel, have a strong knowledge of the market, and an in-depth understanding of all sales departments’ financial data as well as strong customer relations skills.</p>
<p>The general sales manager is expected to uphold the highest ethical standards in every aspect of the job.</p>
<p>Job duties for a general sales manager include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Creating the annual dealership sales forecasts by estimating total vehicle sales, gross and operating profits as well as expenses for the new-and-used sales departments.</li>
<li>Meeting with vehicle sales managers to plan and implement objectives for achieving sales and gross profits.</li>
<li>Hiring and monitoring the performance of the department managers, holding weekly sales meetings and conducting sales training.</li>
<li>Overseeing standards for displaying and merchandising both new and used vehicles as well as reviewing and initialing all promotions before they are finalized.</li>
<li>Coordinating the appropriate supply of new and used vehicles and ordering/acquiring vehicle inventory accordingly.</li>
<li>Meeting with the dealership’s general manager to review forecasts and profits for each department.</li>
<li>Working directly with the general manager on making recommendations on both short and long-range advertising plans, sales promotions, staffing needs, lease promotions and compensation plans.</li>
<li>Attending to customer complaints, ensuring that a high level of customer satisfaction is obtained.</li>
<li>Auditing all appraisals of trade-in vehicles.</li>
<li>Issuing all demonstration vhttps://employers.indeed.com/j#ehicles and ensuring that appropriate dealership records are maintained.</li>
</ul>
<p>At least two years in a dealership sales environment. Additionally, needs to be comfortable managing multiple departments and people.</p>
<p>Required to maintain the profitability of the dealership while controlling expenses and maintaining customer satisfaction.</p>
<p>Required to not only understand and keep abreast of the federal, state, and local regulations that affect their operations, but must also comply with these regulations.</p>
<p>Requires strong communication skills to deal with customers, employees and vendors, as they represent the dealership.</p>
<p>Must pass background, MVR, drug screen.</p>
