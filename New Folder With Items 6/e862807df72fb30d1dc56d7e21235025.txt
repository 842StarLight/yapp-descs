<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Passion for providing excellent customer service, find joy in developing rapport and relationships with customers while accurately fulfilling customer requests – this position is for you!</b></p>
<p>Industry leading benefits package includes; Health, Dental and Vision insurance, 401K match, paid time off and bonus potential.</p>
<p>The Melvin Stone Company is celebrating it’s 100th year of providing crushed stone, sand and gravel to the communities we serve. Through the process of crushing, screening and washing, we produce an Ohio Department Of Transportation (ODOT) approved product used extensively in Asphalt, Concrete, Road Base, and Commercial/Residential construction. We also provide material for agricultural and environmental purposes.</p>
<p>Position will work primarily at our Wilmington, OH office, however could be asked to work occasionally at our locations in Fayette &amp; Pickaway County.</p>
<p>This position;</p>
<p>Provides customer service and support while processing orders from receipt to delivery to exceed customer expectations.</p>
<p>Performs administrative activities to support customers and operations with the dissemination of accurate information.</p>
<p>Builds and develops professional relationships with customers; educates customers on product availability, pricing and delivery options to support achievement of customer and company goals and objectives.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include but not limited to: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Taking/processing customer orders received via phone and email.</li>
<li>Provides general clerical and office administration support to field sales representatives</li>
<li>Performs accurate data entry</li>
<li>Scale duties</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of high school diploma / GED</li>
<li>Customer service skills / experience</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written, telephone, and communication skills</li>
<li>Working knowledge of general office procedures</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Strong Microsoft Office software skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Drug Screen and Background Check is required. EOE/M/F/Disabled/Veteran/DFSP</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Call Center: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>customer service: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
