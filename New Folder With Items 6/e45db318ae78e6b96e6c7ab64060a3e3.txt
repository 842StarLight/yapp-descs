 <b>Position Overview
 </b></p>
<p><b>This individual will work on-site at our customer location.</b></p>
<p><b>
THIS IS A 2</b><b>ND</b><b> SHIFT POSITION (3pm - 11:30pm) M-F.</b><br>
<b>(occasional weekend hours).</b></p>
<p>
Datacenter technicians are skilled, hands-on professionals who provide a variety of services to keep computer servers, storage and networks operating smoothly. They are the first line of defense in safeguarding a company‘s valuable information.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Install and decommission equipment to include physically upgrading internal system components such as memory, hard drives, fiber cables, copper, and more.</li>
<li>
Work with a wide variety of Server, Network, NAS, and SAN equipment. Follow exact installation specifications and properly label and document all installations</li>
<li>
<b>
Manage Power –</b> All power receptacles for all equipment must be setup and managed</li>
<li>
<b>
Documentation </b>- Technicians precisely document and label every aspect of installation including individual cables throughout the entire path</li>
<li>
<b>
Troubleshooting skills</b> –<ul>
<li>
Perform tests to identify and fix errors in computer networks including LAN and WAN</li>
<li>
Be the hands and eyes for remote teams. Help remote System Analysts troubleshoot a variety of Datacenter issues</li>
<li>
Respond to incidents within SLAs</li>
<li>
Be accurate. Zero unauthorized changes, Zero missed change windows, 100% reliability and responsibility for actions</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Collaborate with software and networking engineering teams to ensure proper function and protection of data.</li>
<li>
Provide support to clients and coworkers, troubleshoot problems as they occur and act as main point-person in the field.</li>
<li>
<b>
Inventory management</b> – data technicians are required to maintain accurate inventory of tools, equipment, and devices in the datacenter</li>
<li>
<b>
Hardware repair</b> – Complete basic repair of damaged or faulty network equipment and devices</li>
<li>
<b>
IT computer networking</b> – technicians are in charge of installing cabling for servers, switches, routers, monitors, and computer networking. These cables must be labeled and documented</li>
<li>
Work on incidents and project simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Mentor other data-center staff as needed.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
