<h2>Senior IT Auditor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Want to work for a company that is reimagining distribution? Join Univar Solutions, the premier global distributor of chemicals and ingredients. Our goal is to function as a direct extension of our customers’ teams; to serve as an ally in their quest of business success.<br>
</p>
<p>At Univar Solutions, we’re building on our more than 90 years of experience and drawing on deep resources – inventory and logistics experts, scientists, PhDs, procurement, customer service, sales and marketers-to deliver consultative services and an unmatched portfolio to solve customers’ formulation and operational challenges. When you work for Univar Solutions, you’ll play an important role in shaping how we deliver more than chemical and ingredients.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div><b>
Primary Purpose:</b></div>
<div>The position is responsible for facilitating the organization’s global risk management approach by leading or participating in audit projects in support of the annual audit plan and management’s requests. The Senior IT Auditor either leads or assists with the execution of all phases (planning, fieldwork, reporting, and follow-up) of Univar’s Technology Audit projects, including SOX IT General Control audits. Additionally, the Senior IT Auditor participates in the execution of Univar’s SOX program.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assist and/or plan the objectives and scope of complex IT audit assignments, execute fieldwork, and conduct opening and closing meetings with department heads and senior management</li>
<li>Assist where and as needed in the execution of the SOX program, including walkthroughs, control design and testing, identify remediation procedures, and conduct risk assessments</li>
<li>Partner with Management to effectively communicate and resolve issues identified through audits</li>
<li>Prepare audit reports of findings and recommendations to Management.</li>
<li>Work on special projects and management requests</li>
<li>Supervise auditors and contractors on a project basis.</li>
<li>Conduct investigations, including planning, fieldwork, and reporting as required by Management</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as required or requested.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Specialized Knowledge/Skills:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Able to evaluate the effectiveness of internal controls by assessing whether the controls are properly designed, implemented, and operating effectively. Able to develop effective and efficient recommendations to improve internal control</li>
<li>Experience creating flow charts, data flows, and other process documentation using the traditional tools (MS Visio, Word, and Excel)</li>
<li>Good knowledge of audit procedures and techniques</li>
<li>Previous experience with and knowledge of SAP a plus</li>
<li>Prior experience with Sarbanes-Oxley programs IT general controls, including project work and program support, test of design, test of operating effectiveness, and remediation planning.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of emerging trends and best practices in corporate governance over financial reporting, Internal controls (COSO Framework &amp; PCAOB Standards)</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience working with MS Office Suite, with strong Excel skills</li>
<li>Foreign language fluency a plus, particularly Spanish</li>
<li>May provide guidance adn mentorship to co-soource audit support staff or SOX Analyst/s as needed</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education/Experience/Training:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field; Master’s degree preferred</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years audit or technology experience (preferably with the Big 4 or national/regional firm and/or similar experience)</li>
<li>CIA and/or CISA (or willing to obtain one in the near future)</li>
<li>General office work environment</li>
<br>
</ul>
