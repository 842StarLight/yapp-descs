 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>An OLAP Specialist is an expert in multi-dimensional modeling and analytical application design. The specialist is responsible for leading the development of enterprise-wide systems, which may include the development of dimensional models, logistics planning, and end-user analysis design.
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Gather functional requirements for analytical applications and translate to technical specifications.
</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between the business community and the Data Warehouse team.
</li>
<li>Design OLAP cubes with tools such as Hyperion Essbase, DB2 OLAP, Microsoft SQL Server Analysis Services, or Cognos Powerplay.
</li>
<li>Incorporate sophisticated calculations using MDX, MDL, or Calc Scripts
</li>
<li>Recommend and implement appropriate partitioning, processing, storage, and security methods.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Experience</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelors Degree is the minimum education needed, preferably in Computer Science or Management Information Systems. MBA or CPA preferred.
</li>
<li>Candidates should be proficient in OLAP development with at least 2 years of consistent experience using Hyperion Essbase, DB2 OLAP, Microsoft SQL Server Analysis Services, or Cognos Powerplay.
</li>
<li>Solid business skills in areas such as financial, demand, and capacity planning, or sales and marketing analysis, are important.
</li>
<li>A minimum of 2 years experience in data processing or business programming as a professional in one of the following areas is preferred: Banking, Energy, Insurance, Finance, Human Resources, or Manufacturing.
</li>
<li>Familiarity with AlphaBlox, Arcplan, Brio, Business Objects, Hyperion Analyzer, or ProClarity is a plus.
</li>
<li>A candidate must have good communication skills, both oral and written.
</li>
<li>A good understanding of basic Data Warehouse methodology is essential.
</li>
<li>An ability to travel weekly to client sites is expected.</li>
</ul>
