<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
The Regional Manager (RM) reports directly to the Chief Operations Officer (COO) and works closely with regions’ Office Managers (OMs) on day-to-day operations. The primary focus of the position is to ensure each location within the assigned region is functioning at the highest level to deliver quality dental services in a patient centered environment. This involves oversight of the administrative and operational practices for each center, incorporating the organization’s balanced approach to exceptional patient satisfaction and excellent quality of care while maintaining a positive margin. Performance will be measured by successfully meeting regional and organizational goals.</p>
</div></div></div>
