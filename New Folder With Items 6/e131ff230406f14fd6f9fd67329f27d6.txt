<div><b>
Position Objective:</b></div>
<div>
This position reports to the Advanced Engineering Shop Supervisor and is responsible for performing day-to-day facilities activities such as Shipping and Receiving, Warehourse Organization, Building Maintenance, Stocking of Supplies, General facilities clean up, Snow/Ice removal, AME Shop Inventory Control.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Will be required to perform various maintenance duties on facility and equipment. Will also be required to modify, clean, disassemble, assemble, and test out machinery and equipment under the direction of the Advanced Manufacturing Engineering Shop Supervisor.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Key Responsibilites:</b></div>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<ul>
<li>
Must be able to use power tools for crate building and shipment preparation..
</li>
<li>Coordinate Logistics- Schedule pickups and receives deliveries</li>
<li>
Ensures operation of machinery and mechanical equipment by completing preventive maintenance requirements.</li>
<li>
Maintains safe and clean working environment by complying with procedures, rules, and regulations.</li>
<li>
Disassemble and assemble new or modified mechanical and electrical components for industrial machinery or testing equipment.</li>
<li>
Fabricates and assembles structural metal products, such as framework for machinery and metal parts for production machinery according to job order, verbal instructions, and/or blueprints.</li>
<li>
Could be required to operate fabricating equipment, such as welders, brakes, shears, CNC Plasma Cutters, saws, drill press, milling machine, and lathe.</li>
<li>
Set up and conduct tests of complete units and components under operational conditions to investigate proposals for improving equipment performance.</li>
<li>
Coordinate waste disposal.</li>
<li>
Perform facilities maintenance tasks including facility Air Compressor, Pump, and Dryer</li>
<li>
Assist team members with special projects as needed under supervisor discretion.<br>
</li>
</ul>
