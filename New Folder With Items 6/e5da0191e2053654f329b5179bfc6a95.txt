<h2>IT Project Manager / Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>The IT Project Manager/Business Analyst is a hands-on role managing key information technology and business systems projects as well as reviewing, analyzing, and documenting business systems, user requirements, and processes. Projects and analysis will support IT infrastructure, systems, and software development for functions such as clinical research operations, quality, finance, sales/business development, purchasing/contracts, etc. Responsibilities include the coordination and completion of projects on time, within budget and scope. Oversees all aspects of projects and develops project plans and project governance. Prepares reports for upper management regarding status of project. S(he) will align with other departments and stakeholders to ensure scope, business requirements, and expectations are met. This position reports to the Chief Information Officer. Performs duties in accordance with company’s values, policies, and procedures.</div>
<div></div>

Please note that we are able to consider local candidates only at this time.</i></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned at the company’s sole discretion.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Defines, develops, tracks, and communicates project scope, schedules, milestones, deliverables, and budget to the project stakeholders and steering committee.</li>
<li>
Monitors project progress by tracking activity, proactively resolving issues, publishing progress reports and project performance, and recommending actions.
</li>
<li>Determines operational objectives by studying business processes, functions and system capabilities, gathering information, evaluating input/output requirements, and proactively recommending process and system improvements.</li>
<li>
Constructs and documents workflow charts and diagrams, writing business and technical specifications.</li>
<li>
Performs project risk management and implements risk mitigation plans.
</li>
<li>Works with cross-functional teams to manage concurrent, complex key projects.
</li>
<li>Assists the Chief Information Officer, IT Services as needed.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Supervisory Responsibilities:
</b></div>
<div>Coordinates projects with onsite IT staff, business/super users and service providers as needed. Carries out supervisory project responsibilities in accordance with the organization's policies and applicable laws.
</div>
