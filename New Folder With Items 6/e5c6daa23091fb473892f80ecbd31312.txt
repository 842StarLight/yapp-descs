<div>
The Enterprise Reporting Project Manager/Analyst serves as a business reporting subject matter expert in support of Oracle’s fast-paced Cloud business. This individual contributor role applies knowledge of Cloud Framework quote-to-cash business processes and solutions to shape reporting requirements, serving as an active partner to many teams including Finance, Compensation, Sales Operations, Operations, and OAL. This individual stays abreast of the changes in the rapidly evolving Cloud Framework and uses business acumen and reporting experience to anticipate needs of reporting users and influence the Cloud Framework solutions to ensure that reporting needs are met. This Reporting PM/Analyst is responsible to perform data analysis, communicate requirements, test Cloud Framework solutions through existing or new reports, test new reports, validate data, share information with business partners, help users uptake new reports and trouble shoot issues.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>An inquiring and detail-oriented mind, analytical expertise, ability to anticipate pitfalls, and excellent communication skills to influence, share knowledge and drive change are critical for this position. This techno-functional position is ideally suited for a business-experienced critical thinker with a passion for data analysis and an eye for connecting the dots, as well as an ability to understand and navigate the interactions of business processes, transactional solutions and the resulting data flows that generate Reporting and Business Intelligence. Experience and knowledge of Finance and Accounting rules will be utilized in this role which involves a comfort level with revenue recognition rules and financial controls. Ability to manage multiple concurrent projects and deliverables, and flexibility to adjust rapidly to changing business requirements and strategies are also important in this dynamic business environment.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Job Responsibilities:</div>
<ul><li>Understand Cloud Framework data flows and business processes affecting data; become a subject matter expert</li></ul>
<ul><li>Use expert knowledge to influence Cloud Framework related projects in order to ensure that resulting solutions provide necessary business intelligence and a proper data set from which to expand and retain Cloud business</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform data analysis to uncover anomalies, validate reports and Cloud Framework solutions, and provide insights into business activities and trends</li></ul>
<ul><li>Uphold standards of consistency, high data quality, simplicity, and ease of use</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead requirements gathering process with Finance and other functional business units</li></ul>
<ul><li>Use a scenario-based requirements and testing approach to develop a full solution</li></ul>
<ul><li>Anticipate business needs, identify risks and employ mitigation strategies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform testing of BI solutions, data integration and reporting functions through data validation</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create training and communication slides for users to understand reporting from a variety of business processes and solutions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Train the trainers within the business teams, especially Global Process Owners</li></ul>
<ul><li>Respond to questions from and share information with business stakeholders and reporting users</li></ul>
