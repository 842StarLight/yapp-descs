<h2>Accounts Payable/Collections Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">As the Accounts Payable/Collections Specialist at SchoolMint, you will work closely with the Finance team to support our company's growth. You will provide overall support to our Controller, Accounting team, and various departments. This is an exciting opportunity to develop skills and experience that will serve as the basis for a career in Finance in the tech industry.
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
Accounts Payable
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage vendor communication, requests, and documents</li>
<li>Enter vendor invoices into accounting software and properly classify to correct accounts</li>
<li>Review and process expense reports submitted by employees</li>
<li>Reconcile credit card statements and ensure receipt of supporting documentation</li>
<li>Assist in year-end 1099 filings</li>
</ul>
Accounts Receivable/Collections
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Monitor customer accounts to ensure timely payments</li>
<li>Weekly collection calls/emails to customers regarding outstanding balances</li>
<li>Deposit and post checks received</li>
<li>Maintain records and prepare reports for all past due accounts</li>
<li>Facilitate customer payments</li>
<li>Assist with monthly sales tax calculations and filings</li>
<li>Learn how to prepare and review invoices for clients</li>
</ul>
General
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Prepare balance sheet schedules in accordance with month end close</li>
<li>Compile and organize support for year-end audit requests</li>
</ul>
Ad Hoc Support
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Participate in ad hoc accounting analysis &amp; projects for management</li>
<li>Help employees within the company better understand expenses that they are managing</li>
</ul>
QUALIFICATIONS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, Business Administration, Economics or a related field</li>
<li>2+ years of experience in prior experience in an accounting or bookkeeping role preferred</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and highly organized</li>
<li>Ability to work well in a fast paced environment with project deadlines</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets</li>
<li>Excellent communication and written skills</li>
<li>Ability to work both independently and collaboratively with different levels of employees</li>
</ul>
</div>
