<h2>Business Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>The Solve(d) Business Manager is responsible for identifying marketing analytics, data science and activation solutions with the goal of offering Solve(d) services to our clients in conjunction with their assigned FCB Health Network agency.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>While this role will be part of Solve(d), the focus will be to develop and deepen relationships at the assigned agency with the goal of optimizing existing assignments and identifying new areas for collaboration.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>A suitable candidate would be excited about the challenge of working across teams and being an integrator of people and ideas. This role will be highly social in nature and while it will require familiarity with data, analytics, and media, the majority of the work will be in building process and value between Solve(d) and the assigned agency. This position would collaborate with strategy, account and creative teams at the assigned agency, to represent, identify and deliver areas of opportunity for Solve(d). Therefore, this position would be located at the assigned agency.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>As part of the ongoing role, the Relationship Manager will help identify new business/revenue growth opportunities and participate in new business presentations to existing and prospective clients. This role will require strong communication and interpersonal skills as well as an interest in sales engineering. A positive attitude and strong curiosity in data, CRM, media and the evolving advertising space is important.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The ideal person for this role will be stimulated in a dynamic environment, at ease with collaboration, accustomed to working on diverse teams and comfortable ideating with a cross-functional team.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>JOB DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>A professional at this position level within Solve(d) has responsibilities for the following activities:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Providing high-level data and activation knowledge to team members and stakeholders</div>
<div>Formalizing a clear business proposition and working relationship between Solve(d) and the assigned agency</div>
<div>Working with stakeholders at the assigned agency to develop a growth plan for Solve(d) services that appropriately reflects the culture and style of the assigned agency</div>
<div>Designing and implementing a business operating model to streamline how Solve(d) and the assigned agency work, collaborate and manage projects together</div>
<div>Building and managing relationships on behalf of Solve(d) within the assigned agency stakeholders and clients</div>
<div>Representing and evolving Solve(d) capabilities for the assigned agency stakeholders and clients</div>
<div>Working as part of a team to provide solutions to advertising business problems in regards to marketing analytics, data science, CRM and media</div>
<div>Preparing high-quality, visually compelling, client-ready documents</div>
