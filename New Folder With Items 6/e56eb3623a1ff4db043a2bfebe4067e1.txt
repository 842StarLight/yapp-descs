<h2>Senior Consulting Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>Coordinates and oversees the execution and completion of all aspects of a product or service offering project for a client, from inception to installation.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Installations may involve acceptance of capitalized equipment or enterprise software systems, or system integration or consulting projects/engagements</li>
<li>
May work at client site from time to time</li>
<li>
Typically involves extensive interaction with sales, systems engineering, product development, and other members of cross-functional teams</li>
<li>
May be required to manage people who lead projects supporting the company-client relationship, ensuring customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Projects are typically focused on the delivery of new or enhanced products to improve customer satisfaction through the use of technology</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Minimum Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>(3+ years) working in a Consulting environment including working knowledge of typical IT applications and systems currently being utilized in supported industries</li>
<li>
Working understanding of Autodesk technology and processes</li>
<li>
Ability to relate to Consulting customers based on past experience</li>
<li>
Project Management</li>
<ul>
<li>
IT Project Management (4+ years) – demonstrated experience with leading complex project implementations</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to lead a team to achieve project objectives and deliver projects that ultimately go into production within cost/schedule constraints</li>
<li>
Working experience with software implementation project management methodologies throughout software development project lifecycle</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to estimate solutions delivery projects</li>
</ul>
<li>
Working experience consulting with customers to architect and design solutions based on business requirements</li>
<li>
Experience with delivering software and/or projects to customers for acceptance</li>
<li>
Experience with delivering solutions to customers with the following technologies</li>
<ul>
<li>
Autodesk Infrastructure
</li>
<li>Non-Autodesk technologies (RDBMS, PDM, ERP/MRP etc.)</li>
</ul>
<li>
Writing skills – demonstrated ability to review and write scopes of work, project-related documentation, and develop and present presentations</li>
<li>
Ability to travel 30% of time depending on assigned projects and project requirements</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Preferred Qualifications</b><br>
<ul><li>PMP Certification</li></ul>
<br>
<b>
The Ideal Candidate</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Would have implemented 3-5 large projects using the proven Systems Development Life Cycle Management methodology</li>
<li>
Must understand how to implement systems using the methodology</li>
<li>
Have worked in consulting organizations, systems integrators or hardware vendors who implement projects for large customers. These organizations would include: Accenture, Price Waterhouse, IBM, Hitachi, Unisys, and/or Hewlett Packard</li>
<li>
Experience with Business Process Reengineering would be preferable, as would the ability to work with clients to document work flows within an organization</li>
<li>
Ability to understand Return on Investment concepts and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Would have Interpersonal skills</li>
<ul>
<li>
Team work – demonstrated ability to work in a team of technical personnel, sales people, subject matter experts, and other project managers</li>
<li>
Listening skills</li>
<li>
Communication skills – verbal and written in English</li>
</ul>
<li>
Would have the demonstrated the ability to manage multiple priorities</li>
<ul>
<li>
Managing/influencing at multiple organizational levels</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to manage through difficult situations (technical and interpersonal)</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to meet deadlines</li>
</ul>
</ul>
