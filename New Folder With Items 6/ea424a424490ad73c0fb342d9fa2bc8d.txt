<p><b>Essential Job Functions/Accountabilities:</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Electrical Engineer will use software such as ANSYS HFSS and SIWave to complete the following:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>1. Technical Support - Provide Support to end users of ANSYS EMAG products by phone, e-mail, or in person.
</p>
<p>2. Pre-sales Support - Work with our account managers to determine the customer's software needs, answer customer questions during the sales process and provide software demos/presentations on a variety of subjects.
</p>
<p>3. Consulting</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with customers to determine their analysis requirements and expectations.
</li>
<li>Develop quotes/proposals by determining scopes, costs and schedules
</li>
<li>Work with customers to propose alternative design ideas
</li>
<li>Write analysis reports and other technical documents requested by the customer
</li>
</ul>
<p>4. Technical Training - Teach ANSYS classes at client sites, on-line, and in SimuTech offices across the U.S. and Canada</p>
