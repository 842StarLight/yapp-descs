<h2>Program Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Snap-on is seeking a Program Coordinator for the Kenosha, Wisconsin Equipment Solutions team. This role is responsible for assisting multiple program managers with normal program management functions. Duties may include processing orders, managing distributions, managing inventory, billing customers, generating quotes, providing international shipping expertise. All functions are critical for driving growth, improving root/cause/corrective action processes and operational excellence initiatives. A key responsibility will be to assist with global and strategic implementation of methodologies to support the development of a world-class operation. This role will require the individual to help with management of the programs initiatives assigned and assist in monitoring (qualitatively and quantitatively) and reporting on performance of implemented activities.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Process customer orders according to established department policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Customer invoicing, credits and rebills as necessary.</li>
<li>Assist with the viability, functionality, and effectiveness of essential tools, specialty tools, equipment, OEM dealer’s distributions and programs, warranty, etc...</li>
<li>Quoting customers – Domestic and International.</li>
<li>Order entry – Domestic and International.</li>
<li>Coordinate ship dates with vendors.</li>
<li>Work closely with procurement and distribution for inventory management and reorder points for appropriate levels.</li>
<li>Maintain Backorder status report.</li>
<li>Maintain Hold Sales invoice Report.</li>
<li>Interface between other departments (Marketing, warehouse, product management, sales, procurement, distribution, operations, finance).</li>
<li>Recognize and resolves process conflicts when possible, and escalate key issues requiring decisions or approval as they occur. Troubleshoot issues with orders, invoices, inventory, etc…</li>
<li>Track sales dollars and margins in excel when applicable.</li>
<li>Assist in RCI efforts towards the improvement of programs assigned.</li>
<li>Assist in monitoring customer concerns, implementation of corrective and preventive actions for quality related issues.</li>
<li>Report project status for the OEM’s and Account Management.</li>
<li>Operate in accordance to the company safety guidelines</li>
<li>Responsible for the maintenance of Sarbanes Oxley compliancy for assigned work.</li>
</ul>
