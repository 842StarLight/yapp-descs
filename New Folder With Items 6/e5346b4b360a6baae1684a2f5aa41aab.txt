<h2>Enterprise Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Amplitude is the fastest growing digital analytics company in the world. With amazing investors (Sequoia, Benchmark, YCombinator), thousands of companies using our product and more than 400% YoY revenue growth, we are expanding the Go-To-Market function quickly!
<br><br>
To fuel the sales fire, we are looking for Sales Development Representatives who are hungry to learn enterprise software sales and launch (or re-launch) their careers. We want the best of the best and make sure that top performers are rewarded.
<br><br>
<b>About the job:</b><br>
<b>First 30 Days:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Get trained on Amplitude's sales process. Learn how to properly qualify, source and engage cold leads through outbound selling efforts</li>
<li>Start making outbound customer calls to find new business and accounts</li>
<li>Shadow fellow Sales Development Representatives</li>
<li>Attend trainings to learn about the product and industry</li>
</ul>
<b>60 Days:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Account Executives to build out account plans for your assigned strategic accounts</li>
<li>Strengthen your product and industry knowledge through 1:1s</li>
<li>Understand how to recognize and uncover larger opportunities</li>
</ul>
<b>90 Days:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Have a great understanding of Amplitude's sales model</li>
<li>Be able to help train and onboard new hires</li>
<li>Be proficient in basic product demos as you work towards a closing role</li>
</ul>
<b>You will be successful if:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You want to win all the time and you find motivation in temporary setbacks</li>
<li>You don't believe in settling, you find ways to overachieve and keep improving</li>
<li>Your ability to learn is off the charts and you are hungry for more</li>
<li>You love connecting with people online, on the phone, and face-to-face</li>
<li>You've done some selling in the past and want to learn how to be great at software sales (extra points for candidates with &gt; 1 year of SaaS sales experience)</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS at 4-year accredited college: preferably business degree, or equivalent life experience.</li>
</ul>
