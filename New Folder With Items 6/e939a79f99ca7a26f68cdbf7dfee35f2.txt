<h2>Accounting Specialist (Purchasing)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>Perform various and diverse purchasing and accounting functions to support the Finance department. Will require a self-starter who can prioritize, multi-task and work well with vendors and CHG staff.</p>
<p><b>COMPLIANCE WITH REGULATIONS</b></p>
<p>Works closely with all departments necessary to ensure that the processes, programs and services are accomplished in a timely and efficient manner in accordance with CHG policies and procedures and in compliance with applicable state and federal regulations including CMS and/or Medicare Part D, DHCS and DMHC.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>Performs purchasing functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>Completes sales purchases, including equipment, machines, goods, and services for CHG</li>
<li>Maintain an inventory of office supplies</li>
<li>Verifies specifications and price of purchase requests from various departments by corresponding with suppliers for quotations; obtaining recommendations from suppliers for substitute items</li>
<li>Obtains approval from requisitioning department, prepares purchase orders and forwarding them to suppliers.</li>
<li>Performing buying duties and monitors orders to ensure supplies are received.</li>
<li>Obtains multiple bids as required,</li>
<li>Verifies bills from suppliers against bids and purchase orders; verifying receipt of items by verifying items received to items ordered;</li>
<li>Assigns tags to new capital assets</li>
<li>Manages the inventory of all company cell phones, IPADS, and WIFI devices. Performs general accounting functions:</li>
</ul>
<p>*</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepares and records weekly deposits for all bank accounts</li>
<li>Prepares monthly reconciliations and recurring journal entries</li>
<li>Maintains various spreadsheets such as Repairs and Maintenance and Provider Refund Log</li>
<li>Reviews monthly cellular bill to ensure costs are accurate and allocated to the correct cost center.</li>
<li>Maintains inventory of check stock for all bank accounts.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
