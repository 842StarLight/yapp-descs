<h2>Property Manager- Full Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are seeking a qualified Self Storage Property Manager to join our team to run one of our properties. If you are self-motivated, reliable, and have a sales/ customer service background we would like to hear from you.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist customers with selecting the correct size storage unit.</li>
<li>Responsible for following up on all rentals, leads, addressing all questions promoting the property's features and benefits to convert leads to rentals</li>
<li>Complete all documents including leases and reports per the company's policy.</li>
<li>Responsible for the timely and accurate processing of customer payments and depositing at the local bank.</li>
<li>Develop relationships within the community and establish a marketing plan. * Responsible for light property maintenance needs. (example: changing light bulbs)</li>
<li>Other duties as deemed necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Rental management software experience preferred, but not required</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Word, Excel and Outlook</li>
<li>Punctual and dependable * Able to work with little to no supervision</li>
<li>Professional phone and customer service skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated and able to work independently.</li>
<li>Able to meet deadlines and learn from constructive development</li>
<li>.Employment is contingent upon successful completion of a background screening including criminal.</li>
<li>Reliable transportation with valid driver's license and current auto insurance required.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Property Management: 1 year (Required)</li></ul>
