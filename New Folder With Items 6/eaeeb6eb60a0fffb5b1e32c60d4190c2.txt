<h2>Digital Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p><b>POSITION PROFILE</b></p>
<p>
The Digital Campaign Specialist will be responsible for support and growth enablement for RICOH campaigns and executing digital initiatives that will continue to drive brand awareness, high-quality leads, and build pipeline. This role requires hands-on experience managing paid digital channels across multiple channels, cross-organizational collaboration and demonstrated project management expertise.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support the efforts of RICOH's campaign team to maximize the reach, influence and effectiveness and own the execution of digital campaign strategies for our digital channels, including SEM, paid social media, retargeting, content syndication and display advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Support campaigns via Ricoh USA's online corporate presence by creating and updating relevant campaign pages</li>
<li>Manage digital marketing execution (PPC, Search, Social)</li>
<li>Manage and execute email communications from inception to completion:</li>
<li>Plan and maintain the email campaign calendar</li>
<li>Gather email content assets from various campaign managers</li>
<li>Build, test and deploy email communications using a web-based email marketing system</li>
<li>Track and report performance metrics, always optimizing, testing, iterating to maximize and improve results</li>
<li>Work with cross-functional internal teams (marketing operations, website, product marketing, content, creative, social media) to develop campaign content that ensure all visuals and copy are conversion optimized, compelling and on-brand</li>
<li>Analyze In-depth campaign performance data that identifies opportunities for conversion optimization, continuously improving on lead conversion, investment mix and program ROI</li>
<li>Maintain a data-first approach through continual tracking, reporting, optimization and experimentation (A/B or multivariate testing) to meet or exceed company goals</li>
<li>Collaborate with Web/SEO to maximize engagement and conversion</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS (Education, Experience and Certifications)</b></p>
<p><i>
Typically Requires:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>BS degree in Computer Science, Interactive Communications Design or related field required</li>
<li>2-year experience required in web content management and administration</li>
<li>A quick learner with outstanding interpersonal and communication skills, strong analytical and problem-solving skills, and an understanding of software, databases and online services</li>
<li>Required knowledge of HTML, HTML5, CSS, Javascript and PhotoShop</li>
<li>Experience managing performance-based paid social, search, display and ABM campaigns at a B2B company</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Email marketing service providers (e.g. Emma, MailChimp, aWeber, etc.)</li>
<li>HTML and CSS (specifically for Email)</li>
<li>MS Excel for .csv imports</li>
<li>Google Analytics</li>
<li>SiteCore or similar CMS</li>
<li>Advanced experience managing Google Search, GDN, Bing, Adroll, ABM Tools, and LinkedIn Ads</li>
<li>Salesforce, Marketo experience preferred</li>
<li>Bizible campaign performance reporting preferred</li>
<li>Google Adwords certified preferred</li>
<li>Pluses: Experience executing email A/B tests; Web conversion tracking experience; Google Ad Manager (fka DFP)Strong Google Analytics skill set - building reports, goals, reporting on conversion metrics, testing, and conversion hand-off to sales across paid campaigns</li>
<br>
</ul>
