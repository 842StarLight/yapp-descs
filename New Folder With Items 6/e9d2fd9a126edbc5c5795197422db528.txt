<p>Dillardâ€™s Store Systems team is responsible for delivering software to solve the business needs of the retail environment. <p><b>Roles and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluates and tests new or modified software applications to verify they function according to user requirements and conform to established guidelines</li>
<li>Writes, revises, and verifies quality standards for software testing procedures</li>
<li>Communicate with 3rd party POS software vendor regarding new projects and issues</li>
<li>Some of the qualities of a QA analyst is creativiteness, openness to ideas, willingness to take on any task or role, and a constant view of the big picture. Good testers have an instinct and understanding for where and how software might fail, and how to track down failures.</li>
<li>Participate in full life cycle development from the requirements gathering and analysis phase through deployment</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with developers, analysts, and other testers to drive testing activities across the development life cycle</li>
<li>Use strategic thinking when creating a testing strategy/plan</li>
<li>Hands-on tester with an aptitude for functional testing across the application. Includes manual, automated, and exploratory testing</li>
<li>Use scripting to create automated tests with an implementation language and performance/load tests</li>
<li>Create/maintain test data for all types of testing</li>
<li>Prioritize, maintain, and refactor tests</li>
<li>Triage failures, identify root-cause, provide re-creatable steps and define corrective actions</li>
<li>Manage defects and collaborate with team to get fixes scheduled in development cycle</li>
<li>Manage deployment across both test and production environments</li>
<li>Maintain server build environments</li>
<li>Ensure Quality Assurance policies are followed. Maintain documentation and participate in audits</li>
<li>On-call rotation</li>
</ul>
