 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Assistant Director of Development, College of Business</p>
<p><b>BASIC FUNCTION AND RESPONSIBILITY: </b></p>
<p>In this role, the Assistant Director of Development works on identification, qualification, cultivation, solicitation and stewardship of annual and corporate/foundation gift prospects to meet or exceed established performance metrics and fundraising goals, documenting and tracking progress on software systems. The Assistant Director of Development, in collaboration with the Sr. Director of Development, manages special fundraising projects at the College of Business from inception to completion, and ensures good customer service to internal and external constituencies.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains a robust portfolio of 150-200 leadership annual giving level donors ($1,000 - $25,000)</li>
<li>Conducts at least 120 face to face donor visits annually in Montana and, on occasion, nationally</li>
<li>With the Sr. Director of Development and Dean of the College of Business, establishes a three-year strategy to substantially grow contributions at the annual giving level through all channels to the College increasing the number and amount of gifts the College receives annually</li>
<li>Remains current on programs and faculty/research/student initiatives in the College of Business and at the University as a whole</li>
<li>Coordinates donor membership and event sponsorship solicitations, tracking and reporting to specified college or school administrators</li>
<li>Manages communication and interaction with donors regarding scheduling, meeting follow-up, gift and pledge agreements, events and requests for information</li>
<li>Coordinates with Prospect Research and Management team to build and maintain a pipeline and deliver new prospects to the Sr. Director of Development; participates in prospect tracking and strategy meetings</li>
<li>Initiates direct donor contact for annual giving solicitations, including event sponsorships, annual membership gifts, and unrestricted Impact Fund solicitations</li>
<li>Coordinates with Gift and Fund Administration to draft and review gift and pledge agreements; sends agreements to appropriate signers for review and approval</li>
<li>Coordinates with Stewardship to ensure development and execution of appropriate stewardship plans for annual giving donors to the College of Business</li>
<li>Interfaces with Marketing and Communications to ensure development of needed proposals and collateral</li>
<li>Develops grant applications when required by constituents</li>
<li>Produces development materials including proposals, collateral, unit letters, etc.</li>
</ul>
