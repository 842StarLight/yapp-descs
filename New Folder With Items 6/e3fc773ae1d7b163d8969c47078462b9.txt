<h2>Sr. Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>Supports VP sales Director with selling and sales support of Private label and Haggar product. Will be responsible for sales reporting, working closely with planning to insure accurate forecasting as well as customer fulfillment teams. This associate will be very involved with VMI or vendor managed inventory. Provides direction to RMA in-store efforts. Key contact for internal and external personnel for all product issues.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>SCOPE:</b></p>
<p>This individual must be able to effectively manage multiple projects simultaneously and be able to prioritize them in accordance with the challenges of a rapidly changing environment. This position requires coordination with multiple levels of the organization requiring the individual to be comfortable dealing with individuals of all levels of Haggar’s organization. This position reports to the VP of Sales.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul><li> Works directly with the Assistant Buyers on item setups, promotional calendars, and monthly recap analysis. Works with Haggar Merch teams on all new item sets for customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Organizes seasonal pre-lines and market week appointments with accounts and internal personnel</li></ul>
<ul><li> Coordinates and communicates with planning and customer service on weekly order flow</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provides samples to accounts for their internal meetings and ecom needs and other duties as assigned.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain an outstanding relationship and assist in managing day-to-day communication with key merchant offices, merchandise presentation and marketing to ensure seamless execution at all levels.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Partners with our digital team to help drive web sales and marketing initiatives.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Liaison between customer and Haggar RMA team leaders as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Coordinate all marketing and advertising efforts/timelines including initiating annual marketing calendars, ordering/delivering product samples to ensure both internal/external deadlines are met, strategically/proactively managing co-op marketing budgets.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Discern/communicate new marketing opportunities providing sales metrics to validate</li></ul>
<ul><li> Build, prepare and analyze weekly ales reports, identify product trends and opportunities down to the color and size level, track test programs. Establish product life cycles to increase profitability for B&amp;M</li></ul>
<ul><li> Produce/maintain roadmaps as an external selling tool, internal roadmap for product development and working document for RMA’s as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for understanding the competitive landscape and providing detailed industry and market recaps for merchandising and design teams, participate in store visits to asses competitive product and POS initiatives.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Order and coordinate all selling samples; maintaining showroom samples and ensuring all tools are present for client meetings.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Help schedule and coordinate all market and pre-line appointments</li></ul>
<ul><li> Analyze sales and stock levels to ensure that reorder recommendations made by the system reflect the sales trend</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain seasonal pricing form</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for NOC’s, LPA, CSP and other forms</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collects, analyzes and interprets data from various sources in order to solve problems which require a professional level of knowledge. Designs relatively complex and robust reporting systems in excel.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with Planning to learn Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI) system, assist in the analysis and reporting.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for maintaining monthly WIP reports for UPL (PL) customers that requires knowledge of Logility system for accessing data.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain collaborative partnership with internal departments &amp; external customers</li></ul>
<ul><li> Identify sales trends and collaborate with Planning for consensus forecasts</li></ul>
<p>o Understand the importance of data integrity and takes action when problems are identified.</p>
<p>o Identify systems barriers and communicates ideas for improvements.</p>
<ul><li> Performs complex and highly detailed work with accuracy and precision.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supports Sales Planning by analyzing size detail information.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p><b>Relevant Work Experience</b></p>
<ul><li> 2-4 years sales or buying experience</li></ul>
<p><b>Computer Skills</b></p>
<ul><li> Intermediate/Advance use of Excel, Word and PowerPoint</li></ul>
