<h2>CSR/New Accounts Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>JOB DESCRIPTION</p>
<p><b>Job Title: </b> New Account Representative/Teller<br><b>Department: </b> Branch Operations<br><b>Reports To: </b>Branch Manager, Assistant Branch Manager, Teller Manager<br><b>Supervises: </b> None<br><b>Indirect Supervision<i>: </i></b> Tellers<br><b>Exempt Status: </b>Non-exempt</p>
<p><b>Character of Position: </b></p>
<p>The New Account Representative/Teller position is responsible for assisting the Branch Manager to achieve sales goals by prospecting for new sales and service opportunities, making product recommendations, opening new accounts, completing account maintenance, and providing customer service after the sale. Focused primarily on deposit products and services (both retail and commercial), the position is also responsible for referring loan opportunities to appropriate personnel. Based on experience and need, the position may be assigned to take consumer loan applications.</p>
<p>The position involves line supervision as needed similar to the responsibilities of a Lead Teller in order to coordinate Teller activities and ensure timely completion of work.</p>
<p>This position is well suited to those seeking an expanded sales role within the branch.</p>
<p><b>Job Duties: </b></p>
<p><b>1. Sales and New Account Duties </b><br>a. Prospect for and cross-sell new deposit accounts, safe deposit boxes, debit cards,<br>Online Banking, and related services to achieve branch goals. b. Serve to champion sales initiatives within the branch. c. Demonstrate proficiency with respect to complex titling of accounts (i.e., trusts, estates), complex account ownership structures (i.e., business accounts), complex account types (i.e., tax-advantaged accounts such as IRA or HSA), and/or handling of sensitive customers situations (i.e., deceased accounts). d. Open deposit accounts and related services on the system; complete appropriate documentation. e. Perform account maintenance and process address changes. f. Admit customers to safe deposit area in accordance with Safe Deposit Box Policy. g. Answer customer questions and resolve problems in a positive manner utilizing the full range of available resources; project a positive image of the bank.</p>
<p><b>2. Teller Duties </b><br>a. Receive checks and cash for deposit, verify amount and examine checks for endorsement.</p>
<p>b. Cash checks and pay out money in accordance with established policy. c. Place holds as necessary in accordance with Funds Availability guidelines. d. Enter all teller transactions into computer to record transactions, and issue computer generated receipts. e. Remove and record deposits from the night depository under dual control; process transactions. f. Assist with ATM balancing and transactions as assigned. g. Maintain cash drawer balances in accordance with established limits; adhere to security procedures. h. Reconcile cash daily to teller system totals; acknowledge and accept teller differences daily. i. Prove and image transactions. j. Operate coin sorting/wrapping equipment.</p>
<p><b>3. Supervisory Override Duties </b><br>a. Evaluate large or unusual transactions for tellers, reviewing for proper endorsement and recourse as well as AML, BSA, and fraud red flags. b. Provide approval and override on the teller system as appropriate or make decline decision. c. Ensure that holds, CTR, SAR, and SAR referrals are completed as required.</p>
<p><b>4. Official Check Signing Duties </b><br>a. Sign Official Checks in accordance with the Official Check Policy. b. Complete the check accurately; have a second employee review and initial. c. Ensure that guaranteed funds are accepted for purchase. d. Ensure that the purchaser is a Bank customer. e. Monitor for regulatory compliance; complete an OFAC check on the payee; record the transaction in the Monetary Instruments Log. f. Observe limits and handle exceptions in accordance with established procedures.</p>
<p><b>5. Wire Transfer Duties </b><br>a. Accept wire request from customer, completing wire transfer form in accordance with<br>Wire Policy. b. Ensure collected funds are available. c. Properly identify the customer. d. Fax wire request to the Wire Department and follow instructions as directed.</p>
<p><b>6. Opening and Closing Duties </b></p>
<p>a. Perform opening and closing duties in accordance with Physical Security Policy; follow branch all clear procedures, acting as Lookout or Sweeper as assigned; observe the safe exit of employees at closing. b. Arm or disarm alarms.</p>
<p>c. Access vault/cash safe/night depository/ATM in the presence of another employee as designated; update logs. d. Secure vault/cash safe/night depository/ATM at the end of the day. Ensure time locks, where applicable, are properly set under dual control. Update logs. e. Upon closing, sweep to make sure no customers are present, all money is secured, all combinations have been properly set, all sensitive customer information has been secured, alarms set, and all doors locked. f. Direct activities of Tellers in the absence of the Branch Manager, Assistant Branch<br>Manager, or Teller Manager.</p>
<p><b>7. Vault Duties (as directed) </b><br>a. Maintain and control vault cash to ensure an adequate supply in accordance with established limits; place weekly orders; prepare weekly shipments. b. Buy excess cash from tellers; verify and strap currency. c. Sell cash to tellers. d. Balance and replenish ATM. e. Balance coin sorter. f. Balance vault daily; acknowledge and accept differences daily. g. Maintain accurate vault cash records, including shipment and daily balancing records, as well as the Official Check and Monetary Instrument logs.</p>
<p><b>8. Other duties as assigned. </b></p>
<p><b>Organization: </b><br>Organize basic functions for a pleasant and functional working environment within the branch.</p>
<p><b>Relationships: </b><br>Responsible to the branch manager, assistant branch manager, or teller manager<br>Will assist department managers, officers and staff in their respective functions associated with the areas of direct responsibility</p>
<p><b>Compliance: </b><br>The employee has the responsibility to acquire knowledge of State and Federal banking regulations and policies and those regulations and policies inherent to position requirements.</p>
<p><b>Physical Requirements: </b><br>May have to stand for long periods of time and must be able to lift bags of coin</p>
<p><b>Job Requirements: </b><br>The employee should have at least a high school diploma or GED equivalent. The employee should have excellent customer service skills along with strong oral and written communication skills.</p>
<p><b>Software Used: </b></p>
