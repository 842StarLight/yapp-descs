<h2>Sales Director, Healthcare</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>We are seeking an experienced and motivated Sales Director to join our dynamic and growing company. This position is accountable for the development, implementation, and support of strategies and tactics to effectively acquire, manage, and profitably grow the sales of our domestic payment support services. This person will convert client conversations into qualified, developed, proposed, and closed sales opportunities.</p>
<p>
Ideally, this position will be fulfilled remotely from the East Coast US with travel to Chicago/Boston occasionally as needed.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
“Hunting” role that is focused on acquiring net new clients</li>
<li>
Identifying and leading opportunities using a consultative selling approach throughout all phases of the sales process (e.g. identification, qualification, development, proposal, closing)</li>
<li>
Developing strategic opportunity plans that define a differentiated solution and win strategy</li>
<li>
Initiating, nurturing, and sustaining trusted executive client relationships targeted at the “C” suite</li>
<li>
Supporting RFP and proposal generation process</li>
<li>
Maintaining accurate and timely administrative responsibilities relevant to the position</li>
<li>
Adhering to company policies and procedures for proper execution of compliance, training and regulatory duties</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p><b>Minimum FlyMate Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS degree or higher</li>
<li>
Experience successfully selling revenue cycle technology solutions or services</li>
<li>
Minimum of 5 years of experience in a SaaS, technology or service sales position in the healthcare market preferably within the revenue cycle</li>
<li>
A track record of consistently achieving or exceeding sales targets</li>
<li>
Ability to sell a solution that solves the client’s business problems</li>
<li>
Outstanding written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Familiarity with CRM applications; Salesforce preferred</li>
</ul>
