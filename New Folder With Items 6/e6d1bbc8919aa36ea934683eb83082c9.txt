<h2>Senior Manager, Social Media</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Cohesity ( https://www.cohesity.com/ ) is redefining data management from the ground up to solve a critical challenge facing businesses today: mass data fragmentation. Most enterprise data — backups, archives, file shares, object stores, and data used for test/dev and analytics — sits in fragmented infrastructure silos that
<br><br>
make it hard to protect, expensive to manage, and difficult to analyze. Cohesity consolidates silos onto one web-scale platform, spanning on-premises, cloud, and the edge, and uniquely empowers organizations to run apps on that platform — making it easier than ever to back up and extract insights from data. We're a values-led company looking for great people who love challenges, are collaborative and curious, unafraid to try, and passionate about customer success.
<br><br>
The Senior Manager, Social Media will be responsible for planning, implementing, and optimizing new social media marketing programs to drive global awareness and engagement, create compelling digital conversations with our prospects, customers and influencers, and drive lead generation through social media channels.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate is passionate about, and already engaged deeply in, social media with a strong background in customer acquisition strategies, building creative social media campaigns, and data analysis. The candidate should also be familiar with Enterprise B2B sales cycles and have a solid understanding of driving engagement and lead generation through social media. This role will define and execute on a strategy to increase brand awareness, accelerate demand and increase revenue opportunities.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Directly manage and/or govern the execution of all social media activities for Cohesity.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with the Marketing team as well as other groups to effectively increase awareness, engagement ad preference of Cohesity's brand and solutions.</li>
<li>Promote relevant thought leadership and promotional content daily while monitoring relevant conversations and identifying appropriate areas for ongoing engagement.</li>
<li>Create and manage the editorial calendar including drafting, editing and sourcing creative for all posts. Leverage search engine traffic, social media data and campaign results to make editorial decisions.</li>
<li>Strategically leverage social media channels as a key element of integrated marketing programs, in collaboration with Demand Generation, Web, Brand, and PR teams.</li>
<li>Monitor conversations across social media and address important mentions in real-time through engagement, escalation, or documentation</li>
<li>Manage and maintain the analytics systems to track social media engagement and results. Create monthly social media reports and aggregate data into actionable insights to inform marketing.</li>
<li>Drive paid social campaigns including ideation, goals, creative, execution, optimization and reporting</li>
<li>Create and monitor effective benchmarks for measuring impact of social media programs. Analyze, review and report on effectiveness of campaigns.</li>
<li>Communicate in a professional, but unique Cohesity social media "voice" directly aligned with our thought leadership and content.</li>
<li>Educate and enable Cohesity teams to strategically use social channels</li>
</ul>
Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated social media experience for business purposes, including in-depth knowledge and understanding of social media platforms and their respective participants.</li>
<li>Experience developing and executing global social campaign strategies.</li>
<li>5-7 years of professional level social media experience. Must be able to demonstrate knowledge and immersion in social media and provide examples of your work.</li>
<li>Experience with social media marketing for B2B companies is required.</li>
<li>Proficiency in writing for various platforms.</li>
<li>Familiar with Search Marketing and SEO/SEM best practices.</li>
<li>Proficiency in commonly used social media monitoring and online engagement tools.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience with blogging ecosystem and platform.</li>
<li>A driving passion for social media, building community, interacting with various audiences</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, time and self-management skills</li>
<li>Strong sense of initiative and ability to work under pressure on multiple projects</li>
<li>Work independently and as part of a team</li>
<li>Motivated, energetic self-starter with strong problem-solving skills</li>
</ul>
