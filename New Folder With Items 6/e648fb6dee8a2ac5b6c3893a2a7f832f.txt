<p><b>LEGENDS
</b></p>
<p>Founded in 2008 as a hospitality management company, Legends has transformed into a holistic experiential services agency with more than 1,200 full-time and 30,000 seasonal team members globally. Legends has three core divisions operating worldwide: Global Planning, Global Sales and Hospitality, offering clients and partners a 360-degree platform to elevate their brand and execute their vision. Currently, Legends works with marquee clients across business verticals including professional sports; collegiate; attractions; entertainment; and conventions and leisure. We are the industry leaders in designing, planning and realizing exceptional experiences in sports and entertainment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
THE ROLE</b><br>
Legends is seeking a Graphic Designer to join our fast-paced in-house creative team to develop outstanding visual communications. Candidate will work with the Marketing + Creative team on a project by project basis to execute a wide range of creative materials for our corporate brand and our partners.</p>
<p>
Design and layout of marketing communication materials including presentation decks, fliers, brochures, digital ads, magazines, splash pages, trade show materials, logos, banners and more.</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with design team and Creative Director to provide creative services support to multiple departments within Legends in addition to our sports, entertainment and attractions partners</li>
<li>Perform other design related duties as assigned</li>
<li>Experience in professional sports and entertainment a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design preferred</li>
<li>Minimum 3-5 years of design experience in a fast-paced, quick turn-around environment</li>
<li>Must be able to demonstrate creative experience through portfolio of work samples</li>
<li>Ability to meet deadlines without compromising creativity and quality</li>
<li>High-level of attention to detail</li>
<li>Required expertise in various graphic design software packages Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, etc.) as well as Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel) on Mac/PC Platform</li>
</ul>
<p>Applicants MUST provide portfolio of work.</p>
<p>
We are an Equal Opportunity Employer seeking diverse, qualified candidates to join our team.</p>
