<b>Position Overview</b><br>
<br>
The Design Manager is responsible for the accurate design of roof/floor trusses and walls components for single family, multi-family and commercial projects. He/she is highly motivated with exceptional design skills, managerial abilities and an absolute "pride for excellence."<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Designs metal plate connected wood roof/floor trusses and wall panels utilizing MiTek Sapphire software</li>
<li>Optimizes designs for maximum efficiency</li>
<li>Provides layouts and performs engineering using ability to understand blueprints and requirements for projects.</li>
<li>Reviews orders, layouts and data files for accuracy</li>
<li>Resolves architectural and structural design conflicts; understands loading conditions.</li>
<li>Ensures economical/proper use of materials per current inventory levels and material costs</li>
<li>Monitors departmental output to ensure efficient resource utilization and initiates corrective action as required</li>
<li>Demonstrates a strong knowledge of factory equipment to produce highly cost effective and production friendly designs.</li>
<li>Staffs, trains, develops and coaches design team, evaluates performance and initiates corrective action as required</li>
<li>Co-ordinates/assists with sales, estimating, production, engineering and architects to achieve customer schedules and cost objectives.</li>
<li>Performs job site inspections and resolves customer service issues</li>
<li>Performs other duties as required</li>
</ul>
