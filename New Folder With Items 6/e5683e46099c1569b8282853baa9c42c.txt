<p><b>About Gill Landscape Nursery</b></p>
<p>Gill’s is a locally owned Garden Center &amp; Landscape Company celebrating 41 years in business. We are based in Corpus Christi and serve the entire coastal bend and South Texas area. Gill’s started as a landscape company in 1978 and continues to provide high quality landscape design and installation. We have a team of dedicated landscape designers who work with clients to turn their outdoor spaces into beautiful and functional works of art. We design and install residential, commercial, and ranch country landscapes including patios, walkways, drainage solutions, lighting, outdoor kitchens, shade structures, water features, and more. Most importantly, we make our customers happy!</p>
<p><b>About the Landscape Designer Position</b></p>
<p>This is a great opportunity to make a long lasting career with a reputable company. The successful Landscape Designer at Gill's is equal parts designer, salesperson, and project manager. The Landscape Designer leads all aspects of landscape design and installation projects including sales, client communication, design, preparing estimates, staffing and scheduling, supervising installation, and billing. In addition to being creative and efficient with design, the Landscape Designer must be detail oriented, highly organized, and comfortable working with numbers and people.</p>
