<p><b>Position Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
Maintain and sustain a proactive channel of communication with management regarding purchasing and inventory decisions impacting their business. Generate performance reports for underperforming product lines, and initiate resolution plan of action.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul><li>Evaluates daily purchasing reports, prioritizes purchase orders by critical need; and ensures orders meet the optimal levels to maximize profit and sales. Obtains confirmations, verifies pricing and quantities, follows up with vendors to correct issues or re-request confirmations, attaches confirmations to POs, and manages confirmations record. Performs all purchase transactions for assigned categories.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Obtains confirmations, verifies pricing and quantities, follows up with vendors to correct issues or re-request confirmations, attaches confirmations to POs, and manages confirmations record.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Works the Back Order Queue and Expedite Queue and keeps ship dates accurate for the field. Works with vendors for updated information regarding shipments.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conducts analysis and correction of price discrepancies utilizing accounting business partnerships.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Leverages management input to ensure target freight levels are achieved to maximize profit margins.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Evaluates and manages dead stock materials, no calc, and aged inventory. Manages inventory /fill rates for assigned categories.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Negotiates prices with vendors. Proactively seeks out products/vendors in order to obtain best pricing as appropriate.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Handles some commodity purchasing under the general direction of the Purchasing leader.
</li></ul>

<ul><li>Broad-based procurement and inventory experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Product experience.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>3+ years of relevant work experience.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>BS or BA preferred.</li></ul>
