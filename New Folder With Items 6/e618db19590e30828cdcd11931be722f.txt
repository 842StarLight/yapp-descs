<p><b>COMPANY OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>BluSky is a full service General and Restoration Contractor specializing in the clean up and reconstruction following all types of natural and man-made disasters. BluSky is an award-winning company; recognized on the Denver Business Journal's list of Fastest Growing Private Companies for four years in a row. They have been named on the Inc. 500/5000 list of Fastest Growing Private Companies since 2009 and they have been recognized by Colorado Biz Magazine as one of the Best Companies to Work for in Colorado. Additionally, BluSky has also received recognition with the Centennial Entrepreneur of the Year Award, the South Metro Denver Small Business of the Year Award, and was a finalist in the Ernst and Young Entrepreneur of the Year. BluSky has also been featured in a number of articles in the Denver Business Journal highlighting their services and the innovation of their rapidly growing company.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>We consider this role a critical part of the accounting team. The firm is in transition from a medium to large sized business. It is an exciting environment. Our accounts payable manager works directly with our operations team and other business leaders and has a meaningful impact on the company. The best candidate will be someone who likes to get involved in and support the business.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBLITIES</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Lead an AP team of 3-4 staff members in accurate and timely processing of invoices</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide exceptional customer service</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage unapproved invoices</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process checks</li></ul>
<ul><li> Review subcontractor packets</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure W-9 verification</li></ul>
<ul><li> Track certificate of insurance information</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead the transition towards utilizing purchase orders and subcontracts in our ERP system</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul><li> BA/BS Degree in Accounting preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li> Well versed in Microsoft Office products, specifically Excel</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with Viewpoint Accounting Software a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li> Outstanding written, verbal, and analytical skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Detail oriented</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supervisory experience preferred</li></ul>
<br>
