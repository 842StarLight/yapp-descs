<h2>Centralized Servicing Representative I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>Lendmark Financial Services is a consumer finance company that specializes in providing direct and indirect personal loans, automobile loans, debt consolidation loans, and merchant retail sales financing services. Lendmark operates over 320 branch locations throughout Georgia, Tennessee, Virginia, Maryland, Florida, North Carolina, South Carolina, Kentucky, West Virginia, Pennsylvania, Alabama, Mississippi, Delaware, Arizona, California, Colorado, Idaho, Ohio, Texas and Washington.</p>
<p><b>We are an Equal Opportunity and eVerify participating employer</b>.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>Provides servicing support to the branch network.</p>
<p><b>Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Efficiently and accurately handles system related "help desk" tickets for the branch network</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring all customer complaints for the company are handled and resolved in an efficient and timely manner utilizing our complaint management software; must ensure all supporting documents are received and stored in the evidence locker for audit/compliance reviews</li>
<li>Post all repo payments and complete charge-off of accounts</li>
<li>Maintaining an accurate file manifest for the accounts being transferred into branch 22 and responsible for file maintenance and file requests</li>
<li>Processing charge off transactions on all 180 day delinquent accounts</li>
<li>Processing paid out contracts and releasing liens in a timely manner</li>
<li>Posting bankrupt trustee payments accurately according to the confirmed plan</li>
<li>Posting all other payments accurately</li>
<li>Proper documentation of all correspondence, return mail, returned check letters and bankrupt notifications in our loan servicing system</li>
<li>Filing</li>
<li>Answering the phone and handling customer inquires</li>
<li>Process all deferments for branches and centralized operations</li>
<li>Process all modifications and account maintenance for branches and centralized operations</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
