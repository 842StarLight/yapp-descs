<h2>Director of Marketing, Value-Based Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><div>Direct Value-Based Solutions portfolio with a common marketing, branding and communications approach for PreferredOne (Health Plan), ClearScript (PBM), Direct to Employer, Direct to Consumer, ACO and Fairview-Centric Networks products Business Process Outsourcing services and any other related products or services.
</div></div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities/Job Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Develop, implement and execute marketing, branding, internal and external relation’s strategies. Includes developing an executable strategy to increase the potential customers awareness of the Value-Based Solutions portfolio.</li>
<li>
Responsible for marketing materials including: printed materials, electronic and social media, and direct mail; advertising, internal and external marketing communication activities.</li>
<li>
Responsible for leveraging the skills and assets of the Fairview enterprise marketing for branding, advertising, media buying, social media, press enquiries, etc. to assure a unified marketing approach for the enterprise. Responsible for meeting the marketing needs of the product line leaders (e.g., Chief Commercial Officer for the health plan) to support meeting sales and retention goals.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with key stakeholders on Marketing Internet strategy.</li>
<li>
Oversee and evaluate market research to recommend and implement marketing strategies to meet changing and competitive conditions.</li>
<li>
Responsible for marketing budget.</li>
<li>
Leverages Fairview enterprise Marketing resources and internal Value-Based Solutions Marketing resources to accomplish objectives.</li>
<li>
Build and foster collaborative relations with operational departments to stay informed of new initiatives, products and services and to maintain a collaborate team atmosphere.</li>
<li>
Plan, develop, organize, implement, direct and evaluate the department’s function and performance.</li>
<li>
Develop and execute both short and long-range plans and programs to achieve the organization’s strategic goals within budget and within the timeline specified.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Supervisory Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Supervise, direct, and support Proposal and Sales Communication Manager, Sr. Marketing Associate, Graphic Designer and any other marketing staff within the Value-Based Solutions function.</li>
<li>
Coach and motivate staff in order to achieve department objectives as well as assist in the career and personal development of staff.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates a professional leadership style that provides vision, inspiration, respectfulness and positively advocates the Company’s direction and focus.</li>
<li>
Responsible for hiring, training and retaining staff to achieve departmental goals.</li>
<li>
Conduct thorough and timely performance reviews to ensure feedback is provided to employees. Supervisors are expected to provide performance feedback regularly throughout the year, not just at the annual performance review.</li>
</ul>
