<p><b>DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>Division Overview:</p>
<p>Our team is a diverse and agile group responsible for supporting the Sales teams in planning and executing the digital advertising campaigns for CBS products.</p>
<p>Role Details:</p>
<p>The Digital Campaign Analyst will be responsible for supporting the sales team in executing, monitoring, and optimizing the digital advertising campaigns of CBS Interactive media clients. This individual will work with a designated team to ensure flawless campaign execution as well as provide optimal client service support for assigned Advertisers and Agencies. The Digital Campaign Analyst will serve as the primary point of contact for client inquiries and requests pertaining to their advertising programs post campaign launch. This includes tracking impression delivery, reporting, assisting with optimization, and reconciling delivery discrepancies.</p>
<p>Please note that this position is not eligible for relocation bonus assistance.</p>
<p>Your Day-to-Day:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide clients/sales with any necessary campaign reporting</li>
<li>Effectively work with functional groups such as Sales, Yield, Campaign Managers to optimize campaigns to meet performance objectives</li>
<li>Proactively monitor campaign performance and delivery goals, escalate delivery issues as necessary</li>
<li>Generate reports to troubleshoot/resolve third-party reporting discrepancies or campaign/creative issues</li>
<li>Compile weekly reports for sales summarizing any potential revenue-at-risk.</li>
<li>Execute Insertion Order revisions in Operative One order management system, including budget revisions, incremental spend, reallocations and cancellations</li>
<li>Create "wrap decks" to summarize campaign performance after completion</li>
<li>Processes and submits invoice adjustments to Accounting and ensure campaigns are invoiced in a timely and accurate manner</li>
<li>Works with Finance team and Agencies to resolve billing discrepancies and ensure timely payment of invoices</li>
</ul>
