<h2>Senior Financial Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Your Role:</b></p>
<p>
The Senior Financial Analyst will assist in building and maintaining a corporate financial model that details the entire company’s operations and projects future performance</p>
<p><b>
Your Opportunity:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide management with accurate and timely financial reports and their group’s performance of established goals and objectives.</li>
<li>
Perform quantitative analysis of operational and financial data</li>
<li>
Build and manage planning databases by organizing and analyzing a wide range of data sources</li>
<li>
Build budgets and forecasts of future company performance</li>
<li>
Responsible for creating and validating content for executive level presentations and reviews</li>
<li>
Performs ad hoc analysis for executives as required</li>
<li>
Work hand in hand with Sales Operations in building the company’s annual bottoms up sales plan (rep level quotas, achievement rates, churn rates, etc.)</li>
<li>
Build a detailed order plan by product, theater, sales channel that ties to planned bookings</li>
<li>
Compile, analyze and lead monthly budget vs. actual reviews with department heads</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you'll need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in finance or accounting preferred</li>
<li>
3-5 years of experience, that has encompassed the fields of finance and accounting</li>
<li>
Operations and results- centric, working with multiple business teams throughout the organization to streamline financial operations and metrics (providing KPIs, monitoring and forecasting in a timely manner)</li>
<li>
Experience working in public company with a diversified/global organization is also preferable</li>
<li>
Basic to advanced understanding of a three-statement financial model</li>
<li>
Advanced Excel modeling and PowerPoint skills necessary</li>
</ul>
<b>
And Ideally:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience either working with or directly working in Sales Operations preferred</li>
<li>
Experience in the below BI/Planning/Sales software strongly desired<ul>
<li>
Tableau</li>
<li>
Adaptive Insights</li>
<li>
Anaplan</li>
<li>
Netsuite</li>
<li>
Salesforce</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
The personality of someone who would thrive in a financial planning and analysis role at Tenable often has the following character traits:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Analytical</li>
<li>
Inquisitive</li>
<li>
Detail oriented</li>
<li>
Self-motivated</li>
<li>
Good at managing internal stakeholders</li>
<li>
Strong communicator</li>
<li>
Enjoys using Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>
High attention to detail</li>
</ul>
