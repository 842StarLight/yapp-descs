<div><b>
 Inviting applications for the role of Technical Writer within the riskCanvas Financial Crimes Technology and Risk Consulting practice.</b></div>
<div>
 riskCanvas is a fast-growing technology and consulting startup within Genpact that uses cutting edge big data technologies to detect and deter financial crimes in major international financial institutions. We also provide consulting expertise in the areas of financial crimes, technology, and risk. We are a growing team looking to implement groundbreaking technical and consulting solutions to complex real-world problems. We are currently looking for a Technical Writer to create and maintain end user and technical documentation for our risk Canvas Analytics Suite, an End-to-End AML and Fraud solution. The role will also enhance sales efforts through the creation of engaging technical focused marketing literature and to provide a voice for our products to prospective clients.</div>
<div>
 Key Responsibilities:</div>
<div>
<div>
 Lead creation of user interface, system architecture, and API documentation for our products within the risk Canvas Analytics Suite, including collecting material, creating content, seeking feedback, and maintaining documentation through ongoing development cycles.</div>
<div>
 Managing the organization and publishing of content to internal sites, for the risk Canvas team, and our client Support Portal in multiple formats such as full product documentation and articles.</div>
<div>
 Creation of technical sales literature aimed to both inform and engage client technical leadership on cutting-edge solutions in-order to drive innovation and transformation.</div>
<div>
 Engage prospective clients through responses to technical requests, in a single voice, to address clients’ security, architecture, performance and functional needs.</div>
</div>
<div>
 Qualifications:</div>
<div>
<div>
 Bachelor is in English, Engineering, Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field. Master’s degree is a plus.</div>
<div>
 Technical writing experience in product and/or system development teams, which leverage modern, Big Data technologies such as Hadoop, AWS, RDBMS, Virtualization, or Distributed processing, with financial industry, cyber security, or sales experience a plus.</div>
<div>
 Related work experience in support of development teams operating with Agile Methodologies.</div>
<div>
 Ability to convey technical and functional content that is both concise and easy to read.</div>
<div>
 Excellent verbal communication, including experience interfacing directly with client executive and senior management, as well as developers.</div>
<div>
 Experience with Atlassian products such as Jira and Confluence, or similar.</div>
<div>
 Experience with online support sites such as Zen desk, Salesforce, Fresh desk or online publishing through Medium, WordPress, or similar platforms.</div>
<div>
 Proficient in Microsoft publishing applications, with Adobe software a plus.</div>
<div>
 Ability to be flexible and adapt to changing responsibilities, situations, tools, and requirements, as well as the manner of obtaining information from subject matter experts.</div>
<div>
 An excitement and desire to continuously learn and grow; seeking new techniques and tools to address team and client needs</div>
<div>
 Ability to work and/or travel as needed.</div>
