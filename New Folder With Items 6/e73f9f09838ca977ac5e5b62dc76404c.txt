<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Broadvoice is a next generation telecommunications company, servicing customers across North America and is driven by its mission of enabling individuals and organizations new and enduring ways to connect with what’s important to them. Broadvoice has been recognized by Deloitte Technology Fast 500 and Inc. 5000 Fastest Growing Private Companies. Headquartered in Northridge, CA, we are a fast-growing company seeking talented candidates to join our team. Our employees enjoy a comprehensive benefits packages including paid vacation and holidays, 401k matching, medical, dental and vision. Additionally, our career path planning, and continuing education will assist you with your professional goals.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b> The Broadvoice Project Manager will collaborate with team leaders to understand their business objectives and effectively provide support to a wide range of functions within the company focusing on projects across our product, development, and business teams. Acting as a single point of contact, the Project Manager will execute project plans by mapping deliverables and work streams, preparing status reports, and coordinating tasks and responsibilities. We are looking for someone with strong capabilities across all phases of project management, leadership and the ability to identify, clarify and problem solve issues to ensure completion of projects on time and within budget.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
The essential functions include, but are not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the roadmap of complex projects, including approval, planning, communications, execution, and closeout to ensure results for cross-functional teams including marketing and sales departments</li>
<li>Use formal processes such as internal PM systems to develop and maintain detailed project plans and status reports, identifying key milestones and project resource needs to ensure completed work meets all specifications</li>
<li>Monitor and create project deliverables, leading overall prioritization and direction of work streams and managing performance to meet target goals</li>
<li>Build consensus, anticipate and solve problems by escalating competing priorities to the team and identifying process improvements, breaking down barriers to the team’s delivery</li>
<li>Facilitate project meetings and provide feedback to project team members by sharing insights and recommendations</li>
<li>Assist leadership in the development and design of new projects</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications (Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 years of experience in project management with knowledge of the program management lifecycle, PMP Certification a plus</li>
<li>At least 2 years of experience in telecommunications industry or project management experience for a SaaS company</li>
<li>Experience with project management software and other tools for tracking, reporting and analyzing progress</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills with a strong track record of achieving goals and objectives within tight time frames</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects of any scope with limited direct supervision</li>
<li>Self-starter with excellent written and verbal communication and interpersonal skills to motivate and lead a team</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team, receive feedback, and execute accordingly</li>
<li>Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment and culture</li>
</ul>
