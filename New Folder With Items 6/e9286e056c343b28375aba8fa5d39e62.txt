<h2>Assistant Managers</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Oversee and coordinate the planning, organizing, training and leadership necessary to achieve stated objectives in
<br>sales, costs, Employee Partner retention, guest service and satisfaction, food quality, cleanliness and sanitation.
<br>Duties &amp; Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Understand completely all policies, procedures, standards, specifications, guidelines and training programs.</li>
<li>Ensure that all guests feel welcome and are given responsive, friendly and courteous service at all times.</li>
<li>Ensure that all food products are consistently prepared and served according to the restaurant’s recipes, portioning,</li>
</ul>
cooking and serving standards.
<br><ul>
<li>Achieve pizzaBOGO’s objectives in sales, service, quality, appearance of facility, including sanitation and cleanliness,</li>
</ul>
through the training of Employee Partners, and create a positive, productive working environment.
<br><ul>
<li>Control cash and other receipts by adhering to cash handling and reconciliation procedures in accordance with</li>
</ul>
restaurant policies and procedures.
<br><ul>
<li>Make employment and termination decisions.</li>
<li>Fill in where needed to ensure optimal guest service standards and efficient operations.</li>
<li>Continually strive to develop staff in all areas of managerial and professional development.</li>
<li>Prepare all required paperwork, including forms, reports and schedules, in an organized and timely manner.</li>
<li>Ensure that all equipment is kept clean and in excellent working condition through personal inspection and by</li>
</ul>
following the restaurant’s preventative maintenance programs.
<br><ul>
<li>Ensure that all products are received in correct unit count and condition and deliveries are performed in accordance</li>
</ul>
with the restaurant’s receiving policies and procedures.
<br><ul>
<li>Oversee and ensure that restaurant policies on Employee Partner performance appraisals are followed and</li>
</ul>
completed in a timely manner.
<br><ul>
<li>Schedule labor as required by anticipated business activity while ensuring that all positions are staffed as needed</li>
</ul>
and labor cost objectives are met.
<br><ul>
<li>Be knowledgeable of restaurant policies regarding personnel and administer prompt, fair and consistent corrective</li>
</ul>
action for any and all violations of pizzaBOGO policies, rules and procedures.
<br><ul>
<li>Fully understand and comply with all federal, state, county and municipal regulations that pertain to health, safety</li>
</ul>
and labor requirements of the restaurant, Employee Partners and guests.
<br><ul>
<li>Develop, plan and carry out restaurant marketing, advertising and promotional activities and campaigns.</li>
<li>At least 21 years of age.</li>
<li>Able to communicate and understand the predominant language(s) of the restaurant’s trading area.</li>
<li>Have knowledge of the service, food and beverage industry, generally involving at least three years</li>
</ul>
of operations and/or assistant management positions.
<br><ul>
<li>Possess excellent basic math skills and have the ability to operate a cash register or POS system.</li>
<li>Knowledge of the basic function of Microsoft Excel.</li>
<li>Able to work in a standing position for long periods of time (up to five hours).</li>
<li>Able to reach, bend, stoop and frequently lift up to 50 pounds.</li>
<li>Have the stamina to work 50 to 60 hours per week.</li>
</ul>
</div>
