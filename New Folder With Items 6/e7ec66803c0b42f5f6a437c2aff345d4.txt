<h2>Senior Vice President - Business Operations, TD Garden</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Our Exciting Work Environment<p>
This position is located at TD Garden, which is owned and operated by Delaware North, and located in Boston, MA. As New England’s largest sports and entertainment arena, TD Garden is the home of the storied NHL’s Boston Bruins and NBA’s Boston Celtics franchises and hosts over 3.5 million people a year at its world-renowned concerts, sporting events, family shows, wrestling, and ice shows. Please visit www.tdgarden.com to learn more.</p>
The Opportunity<p>
The SVP, Business Operations is responsible for developing and managing all operational facility, event scheduling and management, operational functions and personnel related to ensure client satisfaction, retention and maximization of guest experience and profitability.</p>
<p>
This includes Operations (Guest Services, Facilities and Engineering, North Station Garage, Security, Events, and Housekeeping), Event Booking, Technology, eBusiness, Sportservice relationship oversight, and serves as key contact for Hub on Causeway project.</p>
<p>
The SVP, Business Operations will promote and embody the TD Garden as the premier entertainment venue in the industry and represent TD Garden to optimize community stature and public awareness.</p>
<p>
The SVP, Business Operations serves as a thought partner with the President around operational efficiencies and exemplary guest service and satisfaction at all TD Garden events.</p>
