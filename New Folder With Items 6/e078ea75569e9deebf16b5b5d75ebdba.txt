<h2>Territory Manager - Southeast</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The <b>Territory Manager</b> is an exciting and challenging opportunity to evangelize our solutions within your assigned territory. Not only will you be responsible for new client prospecting and acquisition, you will also work with and manage a network of Valued Business Partners and lead your territory team in maintaining and expanding your established client relationships. This position requires 60-80% travel throughout your territory.
</p>
<p>This TM’s territory is the Southeast. That includes Florida, Georgia, Alabama, Mississippi, and Louisiana. The Territory Manager may be located in any part of the territory.</p>

<p><b>Who are we?</b></p>
<p>Since 2001, Status Solutions has been the pioneering provider of situational awareness technologies. Our mission is to defeat ignorance-based loss in order to achieve true situational awareness within an organization. Our customized software solutions ensure the right information reaches the right people at the right time, because RIGHT NOW matters most.
</p>
<p>At Status Solutions, we believe that ignorance isn’t bliss – it’s dangerous. Having access to information makes you better equipped to protect life, property, business, and convenience. We intend to have our awareness solutions implemented on every network, and we need the right kind of people to help us achieve that.
</p>
<p>Our ultimate purpose is to help people, so if you thrive in a small, entrepreneurial environment, where you will be making life-changing contributions to society, we want to talk to you.</p>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<p>
We are interested in talking to you if you have 3-5 years of experience walking a beat, strategically working with businesses in an outside sales capacity.
