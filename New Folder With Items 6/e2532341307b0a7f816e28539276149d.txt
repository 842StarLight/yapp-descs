<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>The Senior Leader, Data Governance is responsible for the development and implementation of the Data Governance Framework and will report to the Executive of Enterprise Data Management. This leader will be responsible for developing the strategy, processes and roadmap to continually elevate data quality, integrity and accuracy to enable business outcomes. They will partner with executive-level business stakeholders to understand their key objectives, identify where data quality and governance are critical to success then implement the policies, standards and metrics that drive measurable value. This role will also serve as the point of escalation for governance issues and ensure positive outcomes by driving key decisions and remediation plans which are actionable.
<br>
<br>
</p>
<ul><li>
<ul><li>Provide strategic vision and management to drive optimization of roadmaps in the area of data governance by domain (Property, Consumer and Lending lifecycles). Strong communication, business relationships and cross-functional collaboration skills are vital to successful outcomes.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and maintain standards for managing data as a strategic asset for data sources, MDM/Trusted data models and big data platforms while ensuring the integrity across the supply chain. Partner with the analytics team to ensure there are clear roles and responsible from data exploration to commercialization (model reviews). Have oversight for data certification and audits.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate projects that involve multiple team members and oversee the gathering of analyses into a timely, accurate, comprehensive and understandable format. This includes working across business stakeholders to identify key metadata and content subject matter experts who are the stewards across multiple business lines and functions.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead and engage team of content experts and enterprise data stewards who have oversight for data quality, industry research, content optimization and permissible use resulting in accessible, transparent, and compliant data sets to drive sustained value.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drive increase awareness and value from our diverse data portfolio/brand value to improve the improve the business impact data governance will bring understanding higher level of data curation and quality are critical to continued business growth.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead development and management of business and technical metadata development and sharing tools and practices. Accountable for the adoption and maturity of the Collibra Data Governance Platform and business process workflows.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform schedule audits and metrics on the effectiveness of a data governance program. Work across the value chain and with regulatory/compliance on developing programs to facilitate common approaches to new regulatory requirements, permissible use, access and policy enforcement.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage the algorithm logic for derived fields, reference data and inferred data techniques supporting the advanced analytics team of a high level of curation excellence.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Scale and optimize the data governance council cadence and deliverables at the various level across business lines, working, tactical and strategic. Manage data governance and quality presentations, metrics, dashboards and overall progress at the strategic level.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Formalize accountability for data documentation and work with responsible stewards to make sure logical and physical data dictionaries for individual data assets are sufficiently comprehensive, tagged, accurate, accessible and searchable in the common data catalog to promote self-service and data sharing.
</li></ul>
</li></ul>
