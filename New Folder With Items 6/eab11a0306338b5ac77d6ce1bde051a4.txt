<h2>Affiliate Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>The Affiliate Manager is responsible for managing the day-to-day activities involved with affiliate publisher of nam/offers advertising campaigns. He/she will work closely with the Sales Director, Sales Team, and Management to ensure success for both the affiliate and advertiser.</p>
</div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
</div></div>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Recruit new affiliates through research, referrals and other avenues</li>
<li>Manage numerous clients through daily email/Skype/phone interactions</li>
<li>Maintain high level of communication with affiliates, recommend new campaigns affiliates and provide performance feedback to advertiser team</li>
<li>Supply creative packages, links and reporting to affiliates as needed</li>
<li>Monitor affiliate activity, analyze performance, identify areas of improvement, and recommend ways to increase affiliate-generated revenues</li>
<li>Communicate and monitor compliance or brand guidelines in affiliate channel</li>
<li>Proactively monitor traffic sources on all campaigns</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h3>
</div></div>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing, Business or related field</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years hands on sales/account representative experience</li>
<li>Familiarity with different affiliate network platforms, such as HasOffers, CAKE, LinkTrust, HitPath, Commission Junction, and Impact Radius</li>
<li>1-3+ years directly related experience in the Performance Marketing space</li>
<li>Experience with various deal structures including CPA, CPL, CPI, CPM, CPC, etc</li>
<li>Proven ability to accommodate evolving responsibilities and last minute changes</li>
<li>Readily engages others and enjoys building and developing relationships</li>
<li>Strong decision-making, recruitment, negotiation, and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Ability to work well independently and within a team environment</li>
<li>Strong desire to succeed and grow and aptitude to learn</li>
<li>Excellent time-management skills</li>
<li>PC proficiency and solid working knowledge of Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc</li>
<li>Experience utilizing web analytics programs such as Google Analytics</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Working Conditions</b></h3>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>The affiliate manager is a remote (work from home) position – you are expected to have a stable internet connection, quick computer, and access to skype.</p>
</div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Compensation</b></h3>
</div></div>
<div><div><div>
<p>The salary for the affiliate manager is pure commission – 20-30% pending on qualifications.</p>
<p>
The position is full time (40 hours / week) to start.</p>
</div></div></div>
