<h2>Applications Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>The Business Development Associate works in support of the sales team to leverage industry experience and knowledge of automated material handling systems that provide value for prospective clients. He/she will be a part of a multi-disciplined team that involves mechanical, controls, software, analytics, conceptual development and estimating functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>KEY TASKS AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul><li> Analyze customers’ hard and soft objectives with the sales team</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead or participate in the development of winning and creative solutions</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with internal and external resources to develop associated sub system costs such as mechanical equipment, controls, software, and installation</li></ul>
<ul><li> Drive overall proposal cost summaries</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop proposal schedules that fit customer requirements and company abilities</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist in generating proposal documents</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist sales in presenting solutions</li></ul>
<ul><li> Participate and/or lead concept review meetings, pricing reviews with management, and project kickoffs on successful quote opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li> Stays current with technology within the company as well as the overall industry for distribution and fulfillment</li></ul>
<ul><li> Perform other duties as necessary</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in engineering</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficient in using MS Office Suite (Excel, Word, MS Project) as well as ACAD</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong customer presence is a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must be willing to travel as required</li></ul>
<ul><li> 3-5+ years’ experience in automated material handling systems</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>SKILLS/ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul><li> Must possess excellent verbal/written communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must be able to work in a fast-paced dynamic environment and balance multiple priorities to help drive top line results within the assigned regional business unit</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT</b>: This job operates in a professional office environment. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, phones, etc.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>TRAVEL</b>: Generally, this position requires less than 25% travel to various sites.</p>
