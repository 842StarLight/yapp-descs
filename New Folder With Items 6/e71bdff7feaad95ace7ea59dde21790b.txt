YOUR MISSION
<br><br>
As a Solutions Architect at tray, you will have a deep passion for solving customer problems with software integrations. This could be to help make teams more efficient, send smarter marketing campaigns, or to improve top level business analytics.
<br><br>
Our Customer Success team takes responsibility for helping teams just starting to use tray get off to the right start and then get the most out of the product over time. We're looking for individuals who thrive on teaching and helping others adopt new technology, new habits and new skills.
<br><br>
Equipped with the knowledge about what it takes for teams to succeed, you'll provide quantitative and qualitative feedback to the product and operations teams to improve the tray experience.
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Be a technical expert on all aspects of Tray.io</li>
<li>Deploy Tray.io following best practices, including ensuring knowledge transfer so that customers are properly enabled and are able to extend the capabilities of Tray.io on their own</li>
<li>Work hands-on with customers to demonstrate and communicate implementation best practices on Tray's technology</li>
<li>Maintain deep understanding of competitive and complementary technologies and vendors and how to position Tray.io in relation to them</li>
<li>Work with CSM's at a deep technical level to successfully position and deploy Tray.io in customer environments</li>
<li>Provide guidance on how to resolve customer-specific technical challenges</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product Management, Engineering, and Marketing to continuously improve Tray's products and marketing.</li>
</ul>
Our ideal Solutions Architect will have
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3+ years of experience working with customers in a pre-sales or post-sales technical role</li>
<li>University degree in computer science, engineering, mathematics or related fields, or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Outstanding skills presenting to both technical and executive audiences, whether impromptu on a whiteboard or using presentations and demos</li>
<li>Proficient in writing JavaScript and building API integrations</li>
<li>Experience and track record of success building solutions for enterprise customers; includes proven skills identifying key stakeholders, winning value propositions, and compelling events</li>
</ul>
