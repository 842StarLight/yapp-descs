<p><b>Description:</b></p>
<p><i>Penske Truck Leasing has an exciting opportunity to join our IT Development team! <p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Software Engineer is responsible for designing, developing, and implementing complex software systems within Penske and will be the technical expert on a development team.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li>Use your expertise to help design applications and assist in technology research.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist with the architectural design of the application</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support the Enterprise Architecture team in formulating standards and best practices for designing and coding applications.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Will be part of small to medium sized cross functional teams and are will demonstrate a consultative approach in working with process owners and IT teams to understand business requirements and translate them into technical solutions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist the development team in the creation of technical specifications and development.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Will be called upon to assist with resolution of system issues.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other projects as assigned by the Manager</li></ul>

<p><b>Required:</b></p>

<ul><li>Must be proficient in SQL</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with Design, Development and Implementation complex software systems</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with at least 1-2 mission critical business applications</li></ul>

<ul><li>Written and oral interpersonal communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong analytical skills and attention to detail required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Critical thinking &amp; problem solving ability</li></ul>
