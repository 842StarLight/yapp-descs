<h2>Content Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

WideOrbit ( http://www.wideorbit.com/ ) is the technology platform for connecting audiences and ads, everywhere. We simplify ad transactions by focusing on innovation, customer delight, and value creation. Chances are if there's an ad on your local TV or radio station, we helped put it there. Our customers include NBCUniversal, AMC Networks, Entercom Communications, Tribune Media, Grey Television, TEGNA, and many other top media companies around the world.
<br><br>
WideOrbiters are truly empowered to innovate at every level, in every function. We hire high energy activators, that aren't afraid to leverage their passion and expertise. And we're agile, which means you can make a difference with your career, while learning, growing and having fun!
<br><br>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
As Content Marketing Manager, you will take ownership of WideOrbit's content strategy across the company to drive measurable business results. You will shape our voice across all channels — blog posts, whitepapers, social media, paid ads, email, and more – creating engaging content to attract, retain and delight WideOrbit customers and prospects. We are looking for a creative storyteller with incredible writing and organizational skills.
<br><br>
<b>Here is what you'll be doing:</b><br>
Content &amp; Promotion
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Create/curate/re-purpose high-quality, valuable content to attract, inform, and engage an audience, while also promoting the brand itself</li>
<li>Write and edit blog posts, case studies, video scripts, whitepapers, one-pagers, landing pages and emails</li>
<li>Serve as the editor-in-chief by coordinating with internal and external teams to develop, copyedit, and optimize content for its appropriate placement</li>
<li>Work with designers and developers to bring content to life</li>
<li>Help Marketing operations to promote content</li>
<li>Help manage social media campaigns and surveys</li>
<li>Produce written thought leadership pieces working with WideOrbit Execs and Product Marketing</li>
</ul>
Program Management
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Own the communication strategy to support product launches and improvements</li>
<li>Collaborate with key stakeholders across Marketing, Sales, Product and other teams</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to repurpose existing marketing assets into other forms of content, updating and modifying them as needed</li>
</ul>
Analysis &amp; Optimization
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with Marketing operations to optimize content for SEO, lead conversion, and engagement</li>
<li>Measure and report on the effectiveness of all content marketing efforts, continually fine-tune the editorial calendar and identify fresh, engaging tactics</li>
<li>Plan and implement content distribution campaigns across various channels</li>
</ul>
<b>Here is what we're looking for:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years Content Marketing, Advertising, Social Media or Digital Marketing experience</li>
<li>BS/BA in Marketing, Journalism, PR, or Communications preferred</li>
<li>Exceptionally sharp writing and editing skills, ability to turn ideas into compelling stories</li>
<li>Track record of creating content of different formats; experience working with designers and developers</li>
<li>Familiarity with SEO/SEM practices; experience working with digital marketing channels</li>
<li>Experience with social media platforms, including methodologies for social posting for different platforms</li>
<li>Ability to review analytics and optimize for maximum results, experience with A/B tests is a plus</li>
<li>A self-starter and a solid team player who owns his or her success metrics</li>
<li>A hard working, nice, talented person who wants to work with other hardworking, nice and talented co-workers</li>
<li>A strong sense of initiative and ability to work under pressure on multiple concurrent projects</li>
<li>Experience with modern marketing strategy, tools, technologies and performance metrics; experience with WordPress and Google Analytics or other analytics reporting systems is a plus</li>
<li>A concise, compelling writer who writes copy that people want to read</li>
</ul>
