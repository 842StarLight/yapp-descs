<p><b>JanSport and Eagle Creek Graphic Designer: Become the Newest Member of the JanSport and Eagle Creek family.
</b></p>
<p>In this role, you will develop and apply the marketing strategy. It is your responsibility to help formulate concepts, execute and design against them for ad campaigns in print, film, outdoor and social. To deliver high quality performance, intimate working knowledge of the brand and market(s) is expected. You will bring expertise in graphic design skill sets such as creating visual concepts and storytelling, using computer software or by hand, to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, and engage with our consumers. You will be instrumental in developing aesthetics for all categories and branding for events globally. On a regular basis, you will work with your counterparts in Product Design, translating the approved concepts to retail and packaging.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>How You Will Make a Difference:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design eye catching deliverables to support marketing campaigns and initiatives, ranging from point of sale, trade ads, display signage, digital signage, retail merchandising, catalogs, and packaging.
</li>
<li>Support marketing initiatives and work within brand guidelines.
</li>
<li>Maintain creative excellence while seeking ways to improve innovation and scalability.
</li>
<li>Plan, analyze, and create visual solutions to marketing and communication challenges, finding the most effective way to get messages across in all brand touchpoints (digital, consumer and trade facing).
</li>
<li>Collaborate with other members of the Creative and Brand Teams to ensure the graphic design needs are being met, while adhering to brand guidelines.
</li>
<li>Edit longform video cuts into smaller digital and social deliverables.
</li>
<li>Design motion graphics such as gifs and small videos for email, onsite and advertising needs.
</li>
<li>Expert with industry trends and software techniques as you will give guidance as how to target the Eagle Creek and JanSport and reach them in a meaningful way.
</li>
<li>Support the Art Directors in the design and creation of original artwork such as logos, layouts, tech drawings, event apparel, set design, studio photography product styling and direction and retouching.
</li>
</ul>
