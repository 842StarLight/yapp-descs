<h2>Senior IT Contract Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>General Summary:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Senior IT Contract Specialist role is to provide support within IT Compliance function to efficiently and effectively review and negotiate customer contracts, particularly dealing with IT related requirements including controls, cybersecurity, risk assessments, audits etc. This position requires an understanding of IT risk, controls, and frameworks to perform contract redlining and to suggest updates to contract language. Areas of required knowledge include risk assessments, IT policies/procedures, security technologies, logical access controls, operating systems and databases, backup and recovery procedures, change controls, encryption, network/firewall concepts, confidential information handling, and general knowledge of regulatory requirements such as ISO, COBIT, NIST, and NERC CIP. Desired experience for this position should include exposure to two or more of the following areas: performing contract reviews for IT/Cyber/Security requirements, IT audits, regulatory compliance, developing policies and procedures, designing and implementing framework controls, risk assessments, risk questionnaires etc. The position will handle agreements sourced globally (mostly US &amp; Canada) and will work closely with Legal and business stakeholders as Compliance representative to ensure that capabilities are in place to comply, coordinate responses from relevant team and suggest compensating measure.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Job responsibilities/Essential Functions, including but not limited to the following:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Evaluate and assist Legal with negotiating / redlining contracts from a cybersecurity and IT audit perspective</li>
<li>
Provide contract-related issue resolution, both internally and externally</li>
<li>
Receive, review, track and provide reporting on contract review requests from Operating Units throughout the company</li>
<li>
Communicate contract-related information and status updates to all stakeholders</li>
<li>
Interact with key business stakeholders and customers to discuss contractual requirements and provide general advice</li>
<li>
Maintain and update all contract templates</li>
<li>
Work with team members and Legal personnel to identify areas of improvement in the contract process and implement necessary changes</li>
<li>
Responsibility in contract negotiation, administration, and management</li>
<li>
Ability to understand technology and associated controls and audit requirements to identify issues</li>
<li>
Responsible for the monitoring of overall adherence to the contractual requirements through regularly scheduled reviews of in-scope technical areas</li>
<li>
Work with IT to close issues through oversight and review of remediation plans and accompanying evidence</li>
<li>
Engage with IT control owners, including management, to review audit testing results and influence decisions</li>
<li>
Review and provide guidance from compliance perspective across areas such as application controls, logical access controls, operating systems and databases, backup and recovery procedures, change controls, pre and post deployment assessments, perimeter security, network/application architecture and selected configuration management controls on technical platforms such as VPNs, VMWare, Windows Server 20XX, AIX-UNIX, Linux and Cisco firewalls</li>
</ul>
