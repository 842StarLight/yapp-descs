<h2>Electrical and Controls Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Company Overview<br>
Delta T Systems, Inc. is a leading manufacturer of energy efficient water and oil circulating temperature control equipment and portable chillers located in the greater Milwaukee area. For almost 30 years, the company provides high quality, American made equipment to manufacturers in the Plastics, Pharmaceutical, Rubber, Food &amp; Beverages and recently the Cannabis industries.<br>
The company has been growing steadily over the last few years. We have developed and continue to introduce exciting new products and continue are path towards being the first choice for our customers.<br>
To accommodate our growth, we are seeking ambitious individuals to work in a rewarding, small team environment with a wide variety of duties. This is a long-term career opportunity; our retention rate is nearly 100%. We provide the right candidate with extensive on the job training.<br>
<br>
Job Summary<br>
This position will be a key role in leading electrical engineering and controls development for industrial process cooling, heating, and fluid handling equipment by researching and implementing the latest technologies. In addition, act as technical support for the sales, manufacturing, and service departments promoting a team environment.<br>
<br>
Job Duties
<ul>
<li>Research the latest technologies in programable controls, HMI’s, VFD’s, electromechanical devices, sensors, and switches to develop the best solutions for our products</li>
<li>Design the entire electrical system for all of our products from the power distribution side to the motor starters, safety devices, sensors, and controls following the latest codes and standards as required for the application and customer’s requirements.</li>
<li>Program controls and HMIs for standard and custom products to meet market specifications and customer specifications.</li>
<li>Prepare electrical schematics and electrical subpanel layouts using CAD software.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement testing plans as required.</li>
<li>Develop standards for drawings and documentation.</li>
<li>Build bill of materials structures for standard product designs.
</li>
<li>Provide manufacturing support for the building of customer units, and engineering support for product maintenance.</li>
<li>Interface directly with customers to ensure that the product meets expectations regarding performance, quality and appearance.</li>
<li>Implement continuous cost reduction and product improvements.</li>
<li>Provide technical support for Sales, Purchasing, and Service departments.</li>
<li>Interface with vendors as required.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Candidate Requirements
<ul>
<li>BS degree in Electrical Engineering.</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years’ experience in an electrical engineering field.</li>
<li>Proficient with PLC and microprocessor programming, embedded systems, cloud based remote monitoring, and data acquisition hardware and software.</li>
<li>Experience with communication protocols including Ethernet and Modbus.</li>
<li>Proficient with UL/CSA/ESA/CE standards.</li>
<li>Competent in presenting complex engineering solutions to customers with our sales team.</li>
<li>Must be legally authorized to work in United States for any employer.</li>
</ul>
