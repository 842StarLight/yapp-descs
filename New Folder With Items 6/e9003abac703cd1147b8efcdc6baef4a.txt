<h2>Director, Provider Solutions, Value-Based Care</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p>At HCSC, building a talent engine for the future is a key priority because we recognize that as our business evolves to reflect the changing world, we must adapt our workforce to meet this challenge. Additionally, with "excellence" one of our core values, we are focused on improving our success measures and delivering the best possible customer experience.</p>
<p>
Job Purpose:<br>
This position is responsible for providing overall strategic leadership over the management and development of enhancements to programs and capabilities that enable existing capitated solutions and/or new programs for the Enterprise; assuming role of strategist for assigned solution, managing development process from inception to product launch; ensuring solution and/or capability enhancements and/or new programs meet company business objectives and operational requirements; and coordinating development activities across corporate areas to ensure development and implementation initiatives meet assigned deadlines. This position will develop, coach and mentor managers and staff, and provide feedback in direct and matrixed reporting structures. This position works closely with all departments within the Enterprise Network Solutions team in addition to all operational business areas in the enterprise that impact the client experience.</p>
<div>
Required Job Qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree and 8 years experience in health care, health insurance, or strategic consulting environment OR 12 years experience in health care, health insurance, or strategic consulting environment</li>
<li>5 years management experience.</li>
<li>Inspirational/Values Based Leadership Experience.</li>
<li>Talent leadership experience.</li>
<li>Project management skills.</li>
<li>Health care operations experience.</li>
<li>Customer service experience and acumen.</li>
<li>Verbal and written communications skills to direct management staff and operations, build relationships across the organization and with external key stakeholders, build consensus and issue resolution</li>
<li>Enterprise-wide project coordination, project management, facilitation, presentation, and leadership experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of field sales operations and business strategies in the insurance industry.</li>
<li>Experience leading and mentoring staff.</li>
<li>Experience interacting successfully with senior management and staff across multiple departments.</li>
<li>Organizational skills, including ability to prioritize tasks.</li>
<li>Creativity and innovation, risk analysis, analytical rigor, and implementation expertise.</li>
<li>Business acumen and project/process integration skills</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
Preferred Job Qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor degree in Business or Health Administration</li>
<li>Master degree in Business Administration, Communications or Marketing</li>
<li>Knowledge of health plan operations and systems and association interfaces with enterprise-wide HCSC systems.</li>
<li>Knowledge of corporate marketing practices, product lines and Par Plan operations.</li>
</ul>
