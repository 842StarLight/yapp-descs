<h2>Service Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Introduces customers to servicing options with an emphasis on automated services to handle transactional needs. Responsible for operations of the branch by supervising employees in all phases of retail sales and operations. Handles and resolves any customer issues or complex inquiries. Maintains the branch workflow and assigns coverage as needed. Ensures that all bank policies and procedures are followed.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Bachelor’s Degree in Business and/or equivalent work experience.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>2-3 years’ experience in bank operations and/or cross-trained to perform supervisory duties.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be pleasant and tactful when interacting with customers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Good oral and interpersonal communication skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Attention to detail and ability to use judgment and discretion regarding customer transactions and confidential information.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to work in a changing, challenging and fast-paced environment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to make decisions using prudent judgment with FHB policy and procedures.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to multi-task using multiple software systems, multiple monitors and audio/video technology.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strong PC skills including familiarity with Microsoft Office Suite required.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Must be proficient in Microsoft Windows applications.</p></li>
</ul>
