<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>THE ROLE:</b></p>
<p>The AMD Server Platform Design Team is seeking an experienced engineer for PCB-based logic design centered on current and future PC architectures; specifically, hardware for debug, validation and testing of AMD microprocessors and chipsets. You will also be responsible for debug, productization, and support of our designs. Additionally, you will be a key contributor for supporting customer system designs. The Server Design Team fosters and encourages technical innovation as well as facilitates continuous career development.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support product engineering in resolving customer problems which require system-level expertise</li>
<li>Provide debug, applications support, and design assistance to customers, field applications, and sales</li>
<li>Develop comprehensive specifications for board-level and system-level products, based on a clear understanding of the function to be demonstrated</li>
<li>Design board-level products using industry-standard CAD tools</li>
<li>Debug prototypes in order to achieve production-worthy products</li>
<li>Oversee the manufacturing process at outside vendors</li>
<li>Write and review detailed technical documentation</li>
<li>Provide technical expertise for less experienced engineers</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>IDEAL CANDIDATE WILL HAVE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A technology related Bachelor's degree plus 7 years or equivalent combination of training and experience</li>
<li>Experience in a customer environment, knowledge of specific customer applications and demonstrated technical expertise in the area of system-level design</li>
<li>Excellent debugging skills at the SoC and system level</li>
<li>Extensive experience with engineering lab equipment, oscilloscopes, signal generators, DC power supplies, etc.</li>
<li>Must have excellent written and verbal communication skills for both internal and customer facing interaction</li>
<br>
</ul>
