<h2>Analytics Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>Westbrook, ME Technology</div></div>
<br><div><div>
<p>IDEXX Laboratories has an opening for a talented individual to serve as an <b>Analytics</b> <b>Project Manager</b> within the Business Operations Analytics Team. This role will offer the right candidate the opportunity to work on visible projects as part of a collaborative team. The ideal candidate will be highly organized with experience as a Project Manager, a history of managing business intelligence projects, and ideally exposure to agile development methodologies.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Duties include, but not limited to:</b></p>
<p><b>
PM Focus</b></p>
<div>
<div>
Technical Project Management. Manage multiple technical projects including scope definition, planning and execution. This includes assessment of submitted requirements, creation and communication of end-to-end project plans, clearly outlining dependencies and ownership.</div>
<div>
Engage with commercial systems teams and other departments, to stay up to date on initiatives and updates that will impact our reporting; ensuring coordination occurs early and often.</div>
<div>
Master Planner: Partner with execution teams to build project/work plans across multiple dependencies.</div>
<div>
Prioritization. Work with the Sales Analytics Manager to prioritize work and allocate resources</div>
<div>
Task Master. Continuous tracking and follow up on the different efforts and tasks.</div>
<div>
Rigorously manages scope</div>
<div>
Support creation of routine updates to stakeholders</div>
<div>
Facilitate continuous improvement of processes</div>
<div>
Foster a highly collaborative and communicative environment</div>
</div>

</i></b></p>
