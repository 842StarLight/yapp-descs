<h2>Senior Power Electronics Lead Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role Summary:</b></h2>
This Senior Power Electronics Lead Engineer will join a world-class engineering team and will provide technical oversight for internally-funded research and development programs working closely with business development on bids &amp; proposals and assist the technical sales teams to define requirements. You will be translating customer's long term needs into viable products. You will work on designing new converters/inverters, support existing designs and execute from prototype to production.<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<br>
<li>
Work with business development team to translate customer requests into a set of requirements for programs<br>
</li>
<li>Implement high level customer requirements into prototypes to be tested in house and given to the customers for their evaluation<br>
</li>
<li>Lead and manage new projects in area of power electronics<br>
</li>
<li>Be responsible for team deliverables, assign tasks, monitor progress and support of project(s) execution<br>
</li>
<li>Support project schedule development with sub-system and component task definition<br>
</li>
<li>Meet project schedule commitments (schedule &amp; cost)<br>
</li>
<li>Develop component, subsystem and system level transient models in order to enhance the team’s ability to accelerate design, development and testing activities<br>
</li>
<li>Innovate new technologies into product architectures<br>
</li>
<li>Design, analyze and document new / existing converters and inverters<br>
</li>
<li>Design and analyze analog circuitry, digital signals, PWMs, SiC Devices, and GaN devices<br>
</li>
<li>Design power stages including estimation of losses and efficiency<br>
</li>
<li>Gate drive architectures and circuit design for Si IGBT, GaN and SiC devices<br>
</li>
<li>Design and analyze high frequency magnetics<br>
</li>
<li>EMI Filters design, analysis / simulation, and test at both sub-system and component levels<br>
</li>
<li>Maintain documentation of all designs<br>
</li>
<li>Develop schematics and supervise board layouts<br>
</li>
<li>Collaborate with engineers to perform functional tasks such as control, testing, layout, etc.<br>
</li>
<li>Troubleshoot new / existing converters and inverters<br>
</li>
<li>Work directly with Engineering Program Managers to ensure program commitments are met<br>
</li>
<li>Support Cleanroom development activities<br>
</li>
<li>Provide sufficient documentation to enable conversion to production<br>
