<h2>Human Resources Manager- Greensboro, NC Friendly Center</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Human Resources Manager reports to the Store Manager and ensures successful performance of HR functions in the store. Exercises discretion to ensure accomplishment of Division HR objectives.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist the Store Manager to meet or exceed store sales and profit plans through the training, coaching &amp; managing of the HR team to support the store sales associates</li>
<li>Provide training &amp; communication regarding meeting or exceeding Customer Experience goals to store sales associates</li>
<li>Communicate with Store Manager and Division HR regarding issues and needs</li>
<li>Proper execution of all existing systems and procedures; ownership of new system implementations and processes</li>
<li>Training HR team on HR systems and procedures</li>
<li>Identify HR needs in the store and drive the solution</li>
<li>Support store Omni functions as needed</li>
<li>Adhere to annual expense budgets for store to achieve profit goals</li>
<li>Monitor store payroll and non-payroll expenses to achieve annual expense % goals and make recommendations where appropriate</li>
<li>Take timely corrective action on expense budget variances</li>
<li>Schedule staffing in all departments to meet customer service expectations</li>
<li>Administer current pay plan</li>
<li>Maintain a safe shopping and working environment</li>
<li>Drive HR Compliance. Train, coach HR team on HR compliance</li>
<li>Monitoring and controlling cash overages/shortages</li>
<li>Conduct timely reviews, communicate development needs, set goals and identify areas for improvement</li>
<li>Recruit, interview, select and retain quality associates and ensure all positions are filled in a timely manner</li>
<li>Maintain high store morale. Provide efficient, timely communications of policies and other information. Take ownership of concerns or problems that develop within the store</li>
<li>Provide effective coaching to HR team, store leader and store associates in order to improve performance of all associates</li>
<li>Develop and promote a diverse store team</li>
<li>Communicate with associates on possible career paths and advancement opportunities</li>
<li>Ensure all associates receive and complete all necessary paperwork</li>
</ul>

<li>Experience in retail management</li>
<li>Excellent leadership, supervisory, analytical and reasoning skills</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Education &amp; Experience:</b><br>
