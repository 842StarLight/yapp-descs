<h2>Customer Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><p><b>Customer Support Specialist</b></p></div>
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div><div><div><b>
</b></div></div></div></div></div></div>
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><b>
Join ABB and work in a team that is dedicated to creating a future where innovative digital technologies allow greater access to cleaner energy.</b></div></div>
<div><div><div>
</div></div></div>
<div><div><div>ABB is seeking a Customer Support Specialist for it Kansas City, Kansas location.The Customer Support Specialist will be responsible for developing and maintaining productive working relationships with assigned channel partners, at all levels of their organization.</div></div></div>
<div><div><p>
Your responsibilities</p></div></div>
<div></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Plan and implement sales strategies to maximize market share and margin with channel partners.</li>
<li>Create and manage SPA's for strategic partners and end users.</li>
<li>Develop selling strategies to negotiate and close deals.</li>
<li>Develop trust with customers to be the first escalation point when they need assistance.</li>
<li>Generate cross-selling leads and make it easier to do business with ABB.</li>
<li>Identify and track upcoming sales opportunities and influence customer specifications to favor ABB.</li>
<li>Conduct presentations to create awareness of complete ABB solutions.</li>
<li>Introduce new products to existing customer base and identify target customers.</li>
<li>Create strategic account plans for key customers.</li>
<li>Meet or exceed assigned volume measurements.</li>
<li>Develop cost effective and creative proposals based on drawings and specifications; provide value engineering suggestions; provide full packages to differentiate from competition.</li>
<li>Understand market pricing and utilize to successfully close opportunities.</li>
<li>Provide market feedback, competitive intelligence and hit-rate information to business and sales leadership.</li>
<li>Track sales leads, wins and losses in Customer Response Management (CRM) system; perform analysis on hit-rate data.</li>
<li>Create customer marketing programs New Product Introduction (NPI) launch, training to promote ABB products and services.</li>
<li>Identify new customers and create customer profiles to understand ABB’s position.</li>
<li>Participate in industry and local organizations to build relationships.</li>
</ul></div>
<div><p>
Your background</p></div>
<div></div>
<div><div>
Basic Qualification:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree and 2+ years of experience OR Associate’s Degree and 4+ years of experience OR High School Diploma/GED and 6+ years of experience</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years of experience in Sales</li>
<li>Candidates must already have a work authorization that would permit them to work for ABB in the US</li>
</ul>
<br>
Eligibility Requirements<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Ability and willingness to possess and maintain a valid driver's license</li></ul>
<br>
Preferred Qualifications:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Engineering/Industrial Distribution</li>
<li>Experience with complex contact negotiations</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of electrical industry and ABB products</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and leadership skills</li>
<li>Experience working with contractors &amp; channel partners</li>
<li>Strong technical application, take-off, and proposal development skills</li>
<li>Customer-focused mindset with proven ability to respond quickly to customer needs</li>
<li>Strong coordination and influencing skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of customer financial drivers / needs</li>
<li>Six Sigma or equivalent Quality training</li>
<li>Ability to utilize analytical software and sales tools</li>
<li>Strong oral, written communication skills and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
