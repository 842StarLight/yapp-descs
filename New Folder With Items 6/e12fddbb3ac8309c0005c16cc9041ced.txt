<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Check printer upon badging in for work in the morning and continually check throughout the day at least every half hour</li>
<li>Separate orders based on priority and label carts accordingly</li>
<li>Ensure accuracy of parts being picked on each sales order and which cart they are to be placed on for shipping accuracy</li>
<li>Make scannable labels and place on parts for shipping crew to be able to scan into the system</li>
<li>Make sure carts get to shipping as soon as they are full or if at the end of a shift all carts are brought to shipping making sure there is time to process the overnights</li>
<li>Assist in Logspares when orders are caught up or if staffing requires, as well as taking care of product on receiving line to keep it clear at all times</li>
<li>Make a bail as needed or if the receiving clerk is unavailable and assist with unloading of trucks as needed</li>
<li>Maintain 5S in all areas of receiving, including supply. Ensure scannable barcodes are on all parts and locations. Ensure shrink wrap is not hanging and all product is forward facing. Empty garbage and recycling at the end of each shift or as needed</li>
<li>Assist with picking orders if needed based on staffing or work load necessity</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by group leader/team leader or manager</li>
</ul>
