<h2>Staffing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Staffing AssociateWe are looking for Dynamic Staffing Associates who have a passion for both sales and people! Work for the largest privately held agency in North America that provides a great opportunity for growth and who will invest in your success. If you are sales driven and looking to make a difference in your community by serving as an advocate for the unemployed, then apply today to our Staffing Associate position!
<br>
<br>
<b>Objective of Position:</b>
<br>
Qualify, acquire, service, maintain and expand client base; obtain job orders (temp, temp to full-time and full-time) solicited from the business community; recruit, screen, evaluate and match qualified candidates to the clients specific job order requirements to ensure client retention; contribute to the professional standing of AppleOne as a personnel staffing service by supplying clients and applicants with outstanding customer service and results; continually meet the needs of our clients and customers (applicants); develop, coach, counsel and recommend applicants as required.
<br>
<br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Continuously develop applicant base through recruiting, reactivating and referrals.
</li>
<li>Maintain performance standards and increase sales for temporary billing and full-time cash-in. Performance standards to be determined by Branch Manager.
</li>
<li>Interface with clients to identify needs, present AppleOne programs and to obtain job orders either through Reference to Sales calls, responses to requests or from referrals.
</li>
<li>Develop client leads and obtain premise visit appointments for branchs selling associates.
</li>
<li>Maintain credit and collection standards in accordance with AppleOne policies.
</li>
<li>Interview, evaluate and reference all candidates to determine their skill levels and their suitability for employment and/or placement by AppleOne.
</li>
<li>Coach, counsel and recommend applicants as required. Determine applicants qualification for placement and assignment removal.
</li>
<li>Maximize gross margin dollars and full-time cash-in by utilizing AppleOnes business philosophy and sales techniques.
</li>
<li>Effectively manage all collections and aging of own accounts referring to manager when necessary.
</li>
<li>Recruit, screen, evaluate and match qualified candidates to the clients specific job order requirements to ensure client retention.
</li>
<li>Interface with Manager, Regional Manager and Key Accounts when appropriate on Key or A accounts.
</li>
</ul>
