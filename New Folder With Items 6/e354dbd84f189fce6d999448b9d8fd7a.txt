<h2>Event Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Company Introduction:<br>
Founded in San Francisco in 2010, Off the Grid is a leading mobile food innovator changing where and how food is consumed and created by transforming underutilized and temporary spaces into community gathering places. Through public markets, corporate dining services and catering options, Off the Grid brings communities together around food while increasing economic opportunity for mobile food entrepreneurs. Acclaimed for reinvigorating the Bay Area street food scene through 60 public events that feed more than 100,000 people weekly, Off the Grid also offers a suite of B2B products and services, including Off the Grid Catering, Off the Grid at Work, and Cubert, a modular mobile kitchen.<br>
<br>
Overview:<br>
Off the Grid is in search of energetic people who have a passion for bringing communities together around food.<br>
<br>
Desired Applicants:<br>
You are a natural blend of Operations and Hospitality experience, and you enjoy engaging with our guests and creators, as much as you like running the ins and outs of our weekly \Market\ events. The ideal candidate is proactive, attentive, and sincerely enjoys making connections and engaging with the community. Additionally, you love an unconventional work space and thrive in an outdoor work environment, and have a desire and interest to learn about food trucks and street food, while sharing what you learn with others!<br>
<br>
Details:<br>
The Event Supervisor is the key to our day to day operations within any event or activated space. This person is responsible for transporting the event assets, setting up and preparing the space for guests, coordinating with our creators, and being the hospitality presence for guests, creators, and stakeholders on site. This role delivers world class hospitality and creates memorable experiences for everyone attending our events. The Event Supervisor creates a positive learning environment for their team, always striving to improve those around them.<br>
<br>
The Event Supervisor will also function as the liaison between day to day operations and the \Market\ Manager who oversees our weekly event portfolio. Through proactive communication and being empowered to make decisions in the moment, this role has the opportunity to recognize opportunities and improve the overall experience within their market. The Event Supervisor will work side by side with the Market Manager to offer insight into each market’s persona and opportunities to better connect within the specific community.<br>
<br>
This is a full-time, non-exempt position which requires nights and weekends to regularly oversee, support and improve operational needs. This position is primarily based in San Francisco with travel to our events as needed throughout the Bay Area.<br>
<br>
Compensation: $21.50 per hour + (DOE)<br>
