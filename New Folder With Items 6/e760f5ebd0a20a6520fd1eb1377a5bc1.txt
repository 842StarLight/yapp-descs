<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who We’re Looking For:
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>An Order Fulfillment Associate to join our growing team. The Order Fulfillment Associate will be responsible for Proactively processing orders in the ERP and in an efficient, error free manner. The Order Fulfillment Associate is the front-line communicator with our supplier base and is expected to build strong working relationships with them.
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You’ll Do:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for forecasting, scheduling, and maintaining lead times of material (cables, antennas, accessories).
</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining safety stock and min-max levels, re-order points, MRP forecasted demand in order to reduce lead times while at the same time reducing excess inventory exposure.
</li>
<li>Entering and generating sales orders into the system and creating pick sheets for local distribution centers
</li>
<li>Creating and processing customer orders, work orders, shipping orders, and/or requisitions to determine items to be moved, gathered, or distributed and/or shipped for fulfillment of orders
</li>
<li>Analyzes new orders, and schedules / promised ship dates, and also provides timely accurate procurement reports
</li>
<li>Leverage dash boarding report to aid in managing Materials &amp; Forecast
</li>
<li>Interact directly with other regional sourcing/supply chain Managers in development and management of enterprise wide sourcing and supply chain strategies.
</li>
<li>Periodic air travel will be required
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Qualities You Possess:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Superior communication skills are required for frequent interaction with senior management. This includes the ability to communicate at appropriate level to non-technical/operations managers.
</li>
<li>Strategic thinker that is able to both analyze and summarize to management levels.
</li>
<li>Ability to work autonomously with a heavy workload and meet deadlines.
</li>
<li>Ability to establish and maintain effective interpersonal working relationships with internal customers, internal/external suppliers and fellow team members at all levels.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Skills and Background You’ll Bring:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in supply chain or similar degree is preferred
</li>
<li>0-2 years of experience in Order Management / planning role within telecom
</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency with Excel.<br>
</li>
</ul>
