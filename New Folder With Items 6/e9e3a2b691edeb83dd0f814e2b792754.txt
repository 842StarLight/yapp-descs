<h2>Principal Consultant - IOT Smart Venues</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>A Verizon Smart Venues Principal Consultant is someone that does not simply see the problems of today, but rather the challenges of tomorrow and the impact these challenges will have on enterprise customers, cities, municipality, society, and the world. They are the consultants of the future. They see how technology 2-5 years from now will change the way venues and people connect and engage with each other and critical services such as transportation, utilities, law enforcement, manufacturing, retail, healthcare, and education.</p>
<p>
A Smart Venues Principal Consultant is a broad thinker and a self-motivator. They are someone that has the creativity to develop ideas on their own, and then work collaboratively to evolve these ideas into reality. They are a leader and a consensus builder.</p>
<p>
The role is simple; lead Verizon and our customers into the future. To do this a Smart Venues Principal Consultant must be the foremost expert in Smart Venues solutions and technologies (i.e. Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality, Artificial Intelligence, and Analytics), how these attributes impact a customer’s ability to achieve desired business and financial outcomes, and how to translate discovered business requirements into technical solutions. Then to evolve the technical solutions into business and technical roadmaps. They must demonstrate this expertise through individual actions as well as an ability to collaborate with and lead cross-functional internal and external teams.</p>
