<h2>Sr. SQL Database Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>SCOPE</b></p>
<p>
Working as part of a team of Database Administrators within the Business Intelligence group, this position will be key in supporting the database environment for multiple high volume sales and back office applications. The Sr. Database Administrator will be responsible for collecting, understanding, modeling, documenting and communicating the data requirements necessary to build and integrate data models for business processes, including performing data rationalization, defining data element standards and collaborating with functional SMEs, information stewards, data analysts, business analysts and product owners.</p>
<p>
To succeed in this role the incumbent will need to marshal resources to identify and resolve database related issues in a timely manner and recommend monitoring and process changes to prevent reoccurrence.</p>
<p>
This role will collaborate with developers and analysts on database design and best practices in the development of new functionality and maintaining and improving existing systems. Experience clustered SQL Server 2012/14/16 environments is essential; as is experience with data modeling for reporting and analytics.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ACCOUNTABILITIES</b></p>
<p>
Designing, developing and delivering Reporting Services &amp; Analysis Services, Schema modeling, stored procedures, SSIS packages, triggers and views.</p>
<p>
Building and implementing enterprise data models, application relational data models, and data warehouse dimensional data models.</p>
<p>
Installing and configuring SQL Server, implementing high availability and clustering, applying patches and fixes and monitoring, troubleshooting and optimizing performance with limited direction.</p>
<p>
Creating backup plans, developing and testing disaster recovery scenarios, managing security, server sizing, and performance tuning.</p>
<p>
Proactively communicate with business users, stakeholders, technical peers and project teams.</p>
<p>
Maintaining contemporary knowledge of industry trends, concepts, and techniques.</p>
