<h2>VMware Senior Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div><div><div><div><div>
<p>As an August Schell VMware Senior Consultant, you will provide customers with technical leadership in the design, installation, upgrade, configuration, and implementation of VMware products. Responsibilities include: Independently conducting enterprise consulting engagements with customers to deliver solutions utilizing one or many VMware software-defined data center products. Maintaining current competency and knowledge of at least two of the following VMware products, including vSphere (ESXi and vCenter) [required], vRealize Suite, Workspace ONE/VDI, AirWatch, NSX or vSAN. You will also be responsible for providing documentation on best practices in designing, deploying and using these VMware solutions. You will participate in pre-sales meetings, discussions and conference calls with our customers to understand business and technical objectives and product requirements, in order to develop effective solutions for our customers. This job at August Schell is for a traveling professional services consultant.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The candidate must have:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>6+ years of relevant industry experience, including customer-facing IT consulting experience</li>
<li>A current VMware Certification (preferably a VCP6-DTM or VCP7-DTM or VCAP6-DTM or VCAP7-DTM)</li>
<li>Direct experience installing, configuring and managing VMware software products</li>
<li>Strong, demonstrable experience with Linux/Unix system administration</li>
<li>Strong, demonstrable scripting experience (e.g. Unix Shell, Python, Perl, Ruby, JavaScript)</li>
<li>Basic understanding of storage architectures, including peripheral interfaces (SCSI, RAID, Ethernet) and multi-processor designs (SMP)</li>
<li>Basic understanding of data center networking (TCP/IP, switching/routing, ports and protocols, firewall concepts, load-balancing)</li>
<li>Demonstrated excellent communications and interpersonal skills, including presentations</li>
<li>Excellent English language writing and speaking skills</li>
<li>BS./B.A./M.S. degree or equivalent technical training or job experience</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Pluses (not required):</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Active DoD/IC Clearance</li>
<li>Experience and proficiency with Microsoft Windows operating systems administration</li>
<li>Experience with data center automation tools, services and processes</li>
<li>Experience with AWS, GCP, Azure or other public cloud services</li>
<li>Experience with data and threat analytics products (Splunk, CarbonBlack, and others)</li>
</ul>
