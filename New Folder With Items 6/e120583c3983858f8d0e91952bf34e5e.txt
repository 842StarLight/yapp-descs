<h2>Receptionist & Facilities Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<b>Position Overview:</b><br>
The Receptionist role serves as the first point of contact for office visitors and outside callers, and is located in the central hub of CNC Software’s corporate headquarters. Great interpersonal skills, organization, timeliness and dependability are required for this role. The person in this role will have the opportunity to support multiple departments including sales, customer service, facilities management, and human resources. This person will work closely with the Administrative Services team members to remain apprised of company events, visitors and travel. Responsibilities will also include general upkeep for the business common areas and kitchens, ensuring cleanliness, organization and a full inventory of stocked supplies.<br>
<br>
The Administrative Services Department shares a unifying vision to add efficiency and increase productivity of the organization using knowledge, judgement and communication. We commit to being pro-active, solutions driven, and lessening the administrative burden in any area that we can assist at CNC Software, Inc. We encourage professionalism and provide quality support to maintain high standards of excellence. The Department’s values are dependability, kindness and helpfulness.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b><br>
<b><i>Receptionist (70% of position):</i></b><br>
<ul>
<li>Answers incoming telephone calls on the CNC switchboard, and directs calls to appropriate departments or individuals. Checks the general voice mailbox regularly, directing messages to appropriate individuals.</li>
<li>Welcomes CNC visitors and connects them with the individuals with whom they are meeting.</li>
<li>Maintains awareness for security measure protocols, to include signing in visitors, unlocking and locking doors, assisting with firedrill procedures, and notifying upper management of any issues.</li>
<li>Receives, sorts and distributes incoming mail and Company packages. When required, signs for packages or certified mail and connects mail with recipient.</li>
<li>Receives faxes, sorts and distributes accordingly.</li>
<li>Maintains inventory for office supplies and other supplies for common areas of the corporate office building.</li>
<li>Assists Sales and Customer Service with database upkeep, such as new addresses/information as it is received.</li>
<li>Assists Customer Service with order processing, USB duplication, or packaging related tasks.</li>
<li>Assist other departs as needed, as instructed by the Chief Administrative Officer.</li>
<li>Keeps front desk area and lobby clean and neat.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b><i>
Facilities Clerk (30% of position):</i></b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ensure all kitchen equipment and appliances are cleaned thoroughly. Check and clean microwaves, stoves and refrigerators, wiping down surfaces and sanitizing tabletops and countertops daily.</li>
<li>Unload, fill, and run dishwashers daily. Redistribute dishes and utensils to the appropriate kitchens.</li>
<li>Make certain that all the coffee, Latte Lounge machines and hot water kettles are clean and filled.</li>
<li>Responsible for restocking supplies in all kitchens and communicating low inventory levels the appropriate contacts.</li>
<li>Maintain and update floor plans and fire drill attendance lists.</li>
<li>Learn and share security procedures.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Minimum Requirements &amp; Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>Minimum one year’s previous front desk receptionist experience with responsibility for answering/directing calls and greeting visitors in a professional setting. Past experience in Foodservice or Hospitality a plus.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel and PowerPoint) as well as calculator, telephone systems and company network.</li>
<li>The ability to work independently, with self-direction, to complete tasks and projects in a pro-active manner.</li>
<li>Proven ability to multi-task and to manage several projects/tasks at once while projecting a positive outward demeanor.</li>
<li>Proven time management skills and a track record of working in a team environment successfully supporting others.</li>
<li>Above average communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability to provide accurate and timely information in a succinct and easy to understand manner.</li>
<li>A positive approach to work.</li>
<li>Must be personable, professional and outgoing.</li>
<li>Well-organized and able to multi-task.</li>
</ul>
