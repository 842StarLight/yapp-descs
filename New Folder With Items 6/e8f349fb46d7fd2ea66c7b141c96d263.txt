<h2>Customer Supply Chain Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div>
<div>Job Advert
</div>
<div>
<p>The Customer Supply Chain Analyst (CSCA) will play in integral part in leading the Customer Supply relationship, developing a strong relationship with the customer to drive collaborative strategies and engagement. Utilizing customer systems and joint business targets, the CSCA will analyze data trends to support the business planning and execution processes to drive superior Customer Service. This role will be also responsible for building relationships with internal supply contacts including Demand Planning, Transportation, Warehousing and several other functions.</p>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Operate under limited to no supervision, with responsibilities for the relationship management of key revenue-generating accounts</li>
<li>Understand fast-paced complex supply chains and Customer unique requirements</li>
<li>Understand sales forecast process and ensure collaborative support of NAM’s (sales) business plan with keen focus on promotional and item life cycle events</li>
<li>Responsible for the resolution of issues arising from customers, sales, and brokers regarding orders and order status, order cuts, and potential issues, with the goal to resolve issues effectively and efficiently -ensuring the coordination and communication of action plans across the business</li>
<li>Full ownership of customer KPIs, including in-stocks, fill rate, on\-time delivery and customer inventory levels, etc</li>
<li>Responsible for continuous improvement activities on assigned accounts, including but not limited to: data management and governance, analysis, recommendation of actions to improve distribution and fulfillment practices, appointment/want date adherence, lead-time variability, and inventory levels/issues
</li>
<li>Create and develop ad hoc, and situation-based action plans in order to mitigate service related issues to meet operational and financial metrics</li>
<li>Lead the communication of critical product supply situations, internally and externally
</li>
<li>Internally coordinate with all necessary departments (e.g. Demand Planning, Logistics, Credit, Sales, Transportation) to ensure optimal customer service delivery
</li>
<li>Participate and contribute to projects as directed by the Customer SC Team Lead</li>
</ul></div></div>
