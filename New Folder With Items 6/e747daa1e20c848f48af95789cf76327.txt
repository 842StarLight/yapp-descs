<h2>Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Are you motivation DRIVEN and everybody puts a limit to your GROWTH?</p>
<p>We are looking for a self-motivated salesperson to help us grow our janitorial services division. (not even a sky is the limit) Using your sales experience and creativity, you will seek out new opportunities to provide facility maintenance services to commercial clients. This is a true self-starter role. Your primary job responsibility will be calling prospects (outbound calls B2B) and setting appointments, We also offer advancement opportunities for the right individuals! We will train the right candidate.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospecting for potential clients to set your own appointments, cold calling &amp; canvassing territory</li>
<li>Going on appointments.</li>
<li>Meet with prospective clients to discuss their cleaning needs, conduct facility inspections, gathering necessary data required to generate a bid proposal.</li>
<li>Delivering / Presenting bid proposals to potential clients.</li>
<li>Conduct customer follow-ups in a consistent manor and document database with detailed notes.</li>
<li>Attend weekly sales meeting</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>5 Year minimum prior successful business to business sales experience.</li>
<li>Must be highly motivated, solution oriented and confident.</li>
<li>Possess proven sales ability as well as basic business skills.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, and a professional appearance.</li>
<li>Ability to provide valid Driver’s license and proof of insurance coverage Aptitude to work toward goals and targets</li>
<li>Curiosity, resiliency and diligent follow up skills</li>
<li>Ability to travel as needed to client locations during work days</li>
<li>Exceptional hunter – fearless new business skills</li>
<li>Strong phone skills – setting new appointments and handling known objections</li>
<li>Must have an aggressive personality and documented success with strategic selling</li>
<li>Must have the ability to persuade and influence others, to develop and deliver presentations, and to create, compose, and edit written materials.</li>
<li>Proven ability to be resourceful at networking events, shows and gaining referrals</li>
<li>Solid knowledge of advertising and sales promotion techniques.</li>
<li>Consistent professional appearance and demeanor.</li>
</ul>
