<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee day-to-day operations.</li>
<li>Design strategy and set goals for growth.</li>
<li>Follow existing franchise systems and communicate with franchisor personnel.</li>
<li>Understand completely all policies, procedures, standards, specifications, guidelines and training programs.</li>
<li>Ensure that all guests feel welcome and are given responsive, friendly and courteous service at all times.</li>
<li>Ensure that all food and products are consistently prepared and served according to the restaurant's recipes, portioning, cooking and serving standards.</li>
<li>Achieve company objectives in sales, service, quality, appearance of facility and sanitation and cleanliness through training of employees and creating a positive, productive working environment.</li>
<li>Control cash and other receipts by adhering to cash handling and reconciliation procedures in accordance with restaurant policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Make employment and termination decisions.</li>
<li>Fill in where needed to ensure guest service standards and efficient operations.</li>
<li>Continually strive to develop your staff in all areas of managerial and professional development.</li>
<li>Prepare all required paperwork, including forms, reports and schedules in an organized and timely manner.</li>
<li>Ensure that all equipment is kept clean and kept in excellent working condition through personal inspection and by following the restaurant's preventive maintenance programs.</li>
<li>Ensure that all products are received in correct unit count and condition and deliveries are performed in accordance with the restaurant's receiving policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Schedule labor as required by anticipated business activity while ensuring that all positions are staffed when and as needed and labor cost objectives are met.</li>
<li>Fully understand and comply with all federal, state, county and municipal regulations that pertain to health, safety and labor requirements of the restaurant, employees and guests.</li>
<li>Develop, plan and carry out restaurant marketing, advertising and promotional activities and campaigns.</li>
