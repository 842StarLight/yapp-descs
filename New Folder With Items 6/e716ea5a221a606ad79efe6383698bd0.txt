<h2>Skip Tracer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for locating Bank &amp; Finance customers and vehicles subject to repossession nationwide. This is solely an office position, no field work. This is not a collections or sales job, however this position does involve heavy phone contact with customers, field contractors, bank employees, and skip tracing. We are seeking experienced skip tracers but will consider applicants with successful collections experience and light skip experience.</p>
<p><b>Top asset location investigators are earning six figures. This position is for money motivated applicants.</b></p>
<p>If you are seeking only an hourly job please do not apply.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Daily duties may include some or all of the following:</li>
<li>Gather, analyze, or evaluate information from a variety of sources, such as court records, credit header data, people location software.</li>
<li>Use various location databases and software packages to locate vehicles subject to repossession (automatic license plate recognition software)</li>
<li>Work closely with repossession agents to coordinate activities</li>
<li>Manage the repossession process for bank clients</li>
<li>Work closely with bank employees</li>
<li>Perform in depth people/vehicle location searches</li>
<li>Proactively monitor accounts and address a variety of issues</li>
<li>Make phone contact with customers, field agents, and while skip tracing</li>
</ul>
