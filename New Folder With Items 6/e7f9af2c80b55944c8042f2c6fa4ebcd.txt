<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Responsible for analyzing, developing and implementing initiatives resulting in the creation and maintenance of a comprehensive business suite of reports and dashboards. From design and development through deployment and support, this position will standardize and draw upon a variety of databases across the enterprise. Responsible for working with all functional areas in the Company to analyze gaps in business processes and to formulate information technology driven solutions. Define business requirements, monitor timelines, and ensure projects are completed in a timely manner. The position requires an intimate knowledge and understanding of the organization's processes, procedures and workflows and will touch every part of the business from web design, intranet development, database administration, network support, ERP support, and integration support among a wide variety of tasks.</p>
</div></div>
<p></p>
