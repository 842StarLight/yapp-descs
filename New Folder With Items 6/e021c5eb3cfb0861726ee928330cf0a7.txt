<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b>GENERAL RESPONSIBILTIES</b></p>
</div>
<p>The Leasing Associate duties and responsibilities may include but are not limited to the following:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains a detailed knowledge of the apartment community, amenities and community.</li>
<li>Provides a daily inspection of show apartments and model appearance.</li>
<li>Handles all aspects of leasing apartments, including greeting prospective residents, touring the community, closing the sale, processing applications in a timely manner, typing leases, following up with prospects, obtains signatures for completion of leases/paperwork prior to move-in.</li>
<li>Consistently documents prospect phone and email contacts; accurately completes the market survey.</li>
<li>Maintains an acceptable closing ratio.</li>
<li>Collects and secures rental payments.</li>
<li>Produces reports in a timely, accurate and complete manner, including the monthly market survey, locator log and commission sheets.</li>
<li>Assist in marketing; Monitors the accuracy of advertisements and takes appropriate action to correct; accurately and consistently documents traffic marketing sources; Assist in leasing events and promotions; Assists in outreach community marketing weekly.</li>
<li>Posts to advertising sources daily as directed.
</li>
<li>Maintains organized community office files.</li>
<li>Maintains working knowledge of Fair Housing laws, rules, and regulations concerning apartment leasing and management.</li>
<li>Assists in the inspections of vacant apartments and takes appropriate action to prepare them for leasing.</li>
<li>Promotes resident retention by assisting with the renewal program.</li>
<li>Reports any observed maintenance problem.</li>
<li>Must adhere to and comply with company safety policies and rules and utilize safety appliances, as required.</li>
<li>Must always be courteous and helpful to residents, prospective residents and co-workers.</li>
<li>Must be available for overtime, weekend, holiday and evening work.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
