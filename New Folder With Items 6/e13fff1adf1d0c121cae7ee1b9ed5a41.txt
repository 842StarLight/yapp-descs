<h2>Lead Application Engineer, Digital Twin</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>The Lead Application Engineer is responsible for leveraging their experience, subject matter expertise and customer-relationship skills to assist in growing our software business by proactively helping customers deploy ANSYS technology to deliver on their key business initiatives. The Lead Application Engineer is responsible for executing and leading business development projects that deliver business value for ANSYS at the Sector/Country or Division level.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul><li>Collaborate with Sales to create Digital Twin opportunities and solutions to complex customers problems that drive software revenue</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for articulating ANSYS’ Digital Twin value proposition which may encompass its entire suite of products; the presentations must affirm the sales message, differentiate ANSYS, and leave a strong and positive impression with Executive level audiences that ANSYS’ technology can help deliver on their key business initiatives</li></ul>
<ul><li>Architect, execute and lead multiple high-profile Digital Twin business development projects and analysis of complex technical problems that deliver business value in asset operation and MRO space for the client as well as a positive impact for ANSYS at the North America level</li></ul>
<ul><li>A focus on designing and building scalable multiphysics system simulation-based Digital Twin based on customers’ technical needs, requirements, and state of the customers’ current infrastructure, operations, and engineering workflows</li></ul>
<ul><li>Integrate Digital twins with partners’ and customers’ IIoT platforms</li></ul>
<ul><li>Draw from your simulation experience and expertise to provide Digital Twin best practices that will demonstrate a clear path for customers to drive more value from ANSYS; collaborate effectively to transfer product knowledge and best practices to customers and the ACE team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work closely with R&amp;D and Business Units, conduct product and solution testing, and communicate customer needs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in internal corporate initiatives to further enhance the Digital Twin solutions, products, consulting and training offerings, presales/sales enablement and professional growth; actively participate in the development and mentoring of team members</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS</b></div>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in engineering or other technical discipline
</li></ul>
<ul><li>A minimum of 7 years of experience, including post-BS academic experience, in an engineering environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>A minimum of 2 years manager-level or Senior Engineer level work experience in application engineer, customer support, customer consulting services, or related customer facing activities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Is considered a subject matter expert and recognized within North America in at least one relevant discipline within our business and is sought out for counsel and advice by other engineers within ANSYS and/or by industry experts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated understanding of engineering practices and product development, technology and how these tools are used by our customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong knowledge of Reduced Order Models, validation and calibration methods</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with system modeling and simulation: Simplorer, Dymola, Amesim</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in scripting/programming languages: python, C/C++</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with at least one of the ANSYS flagship 3D physics simulation products (e.g. Fluent, Mechanical)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to manage cross-functional project teams and multiple projects which are complex in nature and coordinate colleagues across diverse teams and locations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to facilitate customer requirements process and then work with customer to build out those requirements</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong leadership and mentoring skills</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></div>
<ul><li>MS or PhD degree preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>A minimum of 8 years of experience in an engineering environment is preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have passion and drive for personal technical excellence and high performance</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have customer service aptitude and maintain customer focus</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to travel up to 60%</li></ul>
