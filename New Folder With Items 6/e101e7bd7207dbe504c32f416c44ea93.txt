<h2>Pilates Instructor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Club Pilates Bellaire, Texas, will offer group reformer classes in various formats and levels to accommodate everyone seeking to learn Pilates and hone their fitness expertise.
<br><br>
Club Pilates is a well-established, nation-wide brand, committed to providing affordable and accessible Pilates to the community. Club Pilates is currently established in 28 states and 2 countries. Class formats target a wide range of clients’ needs – from young to more senior and beginner to advanced. Club Pilates was one of the first studios to create a 500-hour Teacher Training Program designed to thoroughly and safely teach group Pilates apparatus classes. Club Pilates has already sold 400 territories throughout the United States and Canada. Its 450+ instructors provide over 6 million workouts a year to tens of thousands of members.

For more information, visit https://www.clubpilates.com/bellaire/

<b>POSITION:</b><br>
Pilates Instructor at Club Pilates Bellaire, Texas
<br><br>
<b>Status:</b> Base Salary + Bonus + Commission
<br><br>
<b>Purpose:</b> The purpose of the Pilates Instructor is to provide safe, effective group Pilates instruction in accordance with all Club Pilates guidelines and expectations.
<br><br>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>450-hour comprehensive Pilates education (Minimum education to include; Mat, Reformer, Chair, and Springboard)</li>
<li>Up to date CPR training or willingness to obtain</li>
<li>Demonstrate anatomical knowledge and focus on safety</li>
<li>Pleasant demeanor and excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>Experience teaching 6 or more participants in an apparatus-based group class</li>
<li>Maintain open availability for a minimum of 6 hours (2 shifts) per week</li>
<li>Demonstrated group reformer instruction abilities</li>
<li>Professional and effective communication skills</li>
<li>Proven public speaking skills</li>
<li>Proven Sales Abilities</li>
<li>In depth understanding of the benefits of Pilates and what sets Club Pilates apart from other Pilates studios</li>
<li>Willingness to evolve with new developments in the fitness industry, specifically Pilates</li>
<li>Comfortable adding TRX, Barre, and Trigger Point modalities to teaching repertoire</li>
<li>Team player</li>
<li>Passion for learning and willingness to attend continued education trainings and seminars</li>
<li>Punctual and Reliable</li>
<li>Ability to implement and streamline new procedures in a diverse environment</li>
<li>Ability to recognize areas of improvement and pro-actively communicate with management</li>
<li>Consistently uses excellent judgment</li>
<li>Ability to work harmoniously with the public, management and instructors</li>
<li>Professional and neat</li>
<li>Trustworthy and maintains confidentiality</li>
<li>Strong problem-solver</li>
<li>Proficient in (or ability to become) ClubReady software for basic use</li>
</ul>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide Pilates instruction to include demo classes, group apparatus classes, private and semi-private training as scheduled by a management and pursuant to Club Pilates standards</li>
<li>Must be skilled at proper and safe use of a variety of equipment during each class, including, but not limited to, hand weights, Exo-Chair, Reformer, Springboard, TRX, Magic Circle and other equipment as needed to ensure a safe yet creative group experience</li>
<li>Visually inspect the equipment prior to the beginning of class to ensure equipment is in good working order and safe to use</li>
<li>Responsible for studio tidiness and returning equipment to standards set by studio management</li>
<li>Must arrive at a set time prior to the beginning of each scheduled class or private/semi-private session</li>
<li>Participate in networking/community events as needed and studio promotions to generate new business</li>
<li>Stay up to date with and implement Club Pilates education standards at all times</li>
<li>Maintain honesty and integrity</li>
<li>Any other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
