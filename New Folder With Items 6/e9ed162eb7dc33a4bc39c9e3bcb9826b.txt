<h2>Brand and Advertising Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>THE COLLECTION</b>, South Florida’s foremost luxury automotive dealership, and home to the world’s foremost luxury automotive brands (Audi, Porsche, Ferrari, Maserati, Alfa Romeo, Aston Martin, Jaguar and McLaren), boasts a full-service, in-house Marketing Department and is currently seeking a Brand and Advertising Manager.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>
THE COLLECTION is currently seeking a rounded individual with Advertising experience, who is highly-motivated, proactive and forward thinking with excellent written and oral communication skills; and an elevated level of organization and creativity.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The individual will have the responsibility of immersing himself/herself in the dealership's customer base and the South Florida automotive retail market to develop effective programs for generating sales for THE COLLECTION’s eight luxury franchises (and growing).</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the dealership’s (8) eight franchise Google Adwords accounts</li>
<li>
Manage digital agency and all paid online advertising</li>
<li>
Oversee ad creation management – online, print, outdoor, TV, radio and mobile</li>
<li>
Traffic all advertising assets and maintain all deadlines</li>
<li>
Secure assets from all manufacturer (including still imagery and video files)</li>
<li>
Maintain close working relationship with media vendors</li>
<li>
Evaluate and respond to solicited media opportunities</li>
<li>
Maintain monthly media schedule</li>
<li>
Maintain monthly media expense reports/budget</li>
<li>
Maintain detailed internal and external marketing plans
</li>
<li>Study and adhere to brand guidelines for the THE COLLECTION and its manufacturers Study and adhere to manufacturer Co-Op program guidelines</li>
<li>
Populate graphics, content and special offers found on all dealership websites
</li>
<li>Work closely with sales and service managers to develop initiatives and collaterals that promote sales growth and customer retention</li>
<li>
Seek out and use industry sources and consumer information to architect cutting-edge marketing techniques and new technologies</li>
<li>
Attend marketing/sales promotion meetings conducted by the manufacturer and dealer associations</li>
<li>
Prepare marketing reports for dealership management</li>
<li>
Prepare marketing plans for the dealership’s manufacturers</li>
<li>
Video production vendor management including supervising on-site photography and video capture at events</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS OF THE POSITION:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree in Communications, Advertising or Marketing</li>
<li>
3 plus years of Advertising/Marketing experience</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Google Ad Words, prefer Google Ad Words Certification</li>
<li>
Knowledge of SEO, SEM and other digital platforms</li>
<li>
Multi-disciplined in digital, print and broadcast mediums and media</li>
<li>
Strong MS Office skills including Word, Excel, PowerPoint</li>
<li>
Proficient in Mac OS X and Adobe Suite</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills; Spanish a plus</li>
<li>
Relevant product and luxury industry knowledge</li>
<li>
Experience in developing and executing marketing strategies</li>
<li>
Ability to evaluate and suggest new media and technologies</li>
<li>
Fluency in design and video production processes
</li>
<li>Considerable contacts within the industry</li>
<li>
Formal presentation skills</li>
<li>
High attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
High sense of urgency</li>
<li>
Ability to ensure that tasks are completed accurately and efficiently</li>
<li>
Organizational and planning skills</li>
<li>
Conflict management skills</li>
<li>
Problem analysis and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Leadership Skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul><li><div>
Ability to work in an office environment within a cubicle, sitting at a terminal for an extended periods of time throughout the work day.</div></li></ul>
