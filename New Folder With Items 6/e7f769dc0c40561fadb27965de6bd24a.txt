<p></p>
<p>
In this role you will be responsible for selling the Micro Focus Cobol products into new and existing customers in an assigned geographic region. You’ll do this primarily on the phone with occasional trips within the territory.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Job</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prospecting within the assigned territory to discover new opportunities</li>
<li>
Identify, nurture and close opportunities with both new and existing customers</li>
<li>
Manage forecasts on a quarterly and annual basis</li>
<li>
Work closely with internal and external partners to improve the pace of business and create solutions to best serve customer needs</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a strong working knowledge of Micro Focus technologies and solutions</li>
<li>
Interface with customer prospects. Provide product information and appropriate follow-up</li>
<li>
Generate quotes and proposals that address customer needs</li>
<li>
Create demand for products and services through focused call campaigns</li>
<li>
Monitor leads from multiple online sources</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Candidates must possess the following skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Highly motivated self-starter who is articulate, persistent and outgoing with a proficient phone demeanor and able to represent the company professionally at client meetings</li>
<li>
Capacity to explore and understand customer business needs rather than just customer IT requirements, handle constructive and critical feedback to improve processes</li>
<li>
Team player who can quickly establish working relationships with a wide range of staff including representatives from field sales, marketing and product support</li>
<li>
Must have fluency in English and, if required of the territory, the relevant second language</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Candidates must possess the following experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong sales experience, ideally with a revenue target, able to provide track record of consistently meeting or exceeding quota</li>
<li>
Provide track record of consistently achieving or exceeding quota</li>
<li>
3+ years of demonstrated experience selling business or enterprise software is a huge plus</li>
</ul>
