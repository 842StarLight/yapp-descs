<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>OUTFRONT Media is looking for a successful Local Account Executive that is a high-energy hunter focused on building relationships at all levels. The Local Account Executive is motivated by identifying and solving advertising problems on behalf of the customer. They must be a strategic thinker with exceptional prospecting skills, high energy and a structured solution selling process. They are natural leaders with a desire to win in a collaborative, team environment.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify, uncover, and develop account opportunities.
</li>
<li>Up selling and leveraging business from new and established customer relationships.
</li>
<li>Excellent ability to properly qualify projects to maximize an efficient sales cycle.
</li>
<li>Develop and deliver custom sales presentations and demonstrations.
</li>
<li>Choreograph and conduct on-site assessments of customer requirements and needs.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal creative department and clients to assist in development of effective advertising campaigns.
</li>
<li>Partner with Charting, Creative, Operations and Administrative staff to ensure on time delivery of advertisements in agreed upon locations.
</li>
<li>Assist in the resolution of issues related to billing and accounts.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge &amp; Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to successfully work under tight project deadlines.
</li>
<li>Ability to understand existing business processes as well as investigate and troubleshoot issues in a time critical environment.
</li>
<li>Ability to link vision and strategy with overall business objectives and communicate rationale in a compelling, concise manner.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate a repertoire of skills such as scheduling, prioritizing, organizing, planning, problem solving, decision-making, financial savvy, and attention to detail, and follow through.
</li>
<li>Maintain a big picture, strategic outlook with the ability to iterate from company strategy and planning
</li>
<li>Self-motivated, standout colleague, action-and-results oriented.
</li>
<li>Well organized, exceptional communication and presentation skills- effective communicator/presenter to groups of all sizes and levels of management
</li>
<li>Extraordinary selling capability
</li>
<li>Ability to sell at all levels including C-Level positions
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred qualifications/Education:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated relationship building skills that extend to internal team members in pre/post-sales.
</li>
<li>Proficiency in all aspects of the sales cycle, including qualifying accounts.
</li>
<li>Experience selling mobile mobile/digital advertising ecosystem a plus.
</li>
<li>Ability to understand products and effectively identify how it can provide value to a prospective customer.
</li>
<li>Ability to work within a dynamic and fast-paced environment.
</li>
<li>College degree or equivalent practical experience.
