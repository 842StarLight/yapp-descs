<p>IDEMIA is the global leader in identity and security. Our mission is to create a safe and simple future where identity verification is indisputable, and only you can assert your identity. We are a distributed company leveraging the latest technologies to deliver world-class products in the private and public sectors of finance, telecom, identity, security, retail, sports entertainment, commercial, government, and IoT. We use a variety of technologies and approaches to deliver quality product and services to government agencies and technology companies. IDEMIA is a made up of a group of 14,000 diverse people from different nationalities, speaking over 20 different languages. Together, our solutions impact the everyday lives of citizens and nations. In this ever-changing world, protecting your identity is paramount. Join the team that is ensuring one person- one identity.</p>
<p>
The Customer Experience Account Manager position is a client-facing role tasked with ensuring high levels of customer satisfaction and account coordination in order to retain and grow existing client relationships. The Customer Experience Account Manager will manage the full relationship between IDEMIA Financial Institutions Business Line and its most important customers. The incumbent will be responsible for building strong relationships with strategic customers and engage in a variety of tasks including project management, order coordination and relationship management.</p>
<p><b>
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Operate as the lead point of contact for assigned customer(s), developing while managing and solving key issues</li>
<li>Acquiring a thorough understanding of key customer needs and requirements</li>
<li>Project manage the initial implementation of IDEMIA Financial Institutions solutions for assigned client accounts, ensuring timely and accurate delivery of services in line with client contracts and statements of work.</li>
<li>Lead kick off calls and status calls with clients in conjunction with technical personnel to facilitate.</li>
<li>Ensure that operations delivered all requirements according client SLA’s</li>
<li>Work with both internal and external stakeholders to examine delivery trends and improve communication and turnaround efficiencies, serving as link of communication between key customers and internal teams</li>
<li>Expand relationships with existing customers by continuously proposing solutions that meet their objectives, maximizing sales and profitability</li>
<li>Review customer contract to ensure that the customer is being charged for all products and services</li>
<li>Identify new sales opportunities, both depth and breadth, within assigned accounts</li>
<li>Resolve any issues and problems faced by customers and deal with complaints to maintain trust</li>
<li>Ensures that all orders received are executed and released to production with clear communication to the customer</li>
<li>Ensures all customers invoicing are accurate and timely processed</li>
<li>Ensure the timely and successful delivery of our solutions according to customer needs and objectives</li>
<li>Clearly communicate progress of monthly/quarterly initiatives to internal and external stakeholders</li>
</ul>
