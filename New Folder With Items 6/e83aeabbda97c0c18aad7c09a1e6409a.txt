<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h3>
<p><b>Cloudera is looking for a passionate and technically savvy Candidate Development Recruiter (Sales Sourcer) with a proven track record of delivering results in the very competitive enterprise software marketplace. As a member of the Talent team, you will be responsible for full-cycle activities working in conjunction with our Sourcing team.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your role will include engaging with top caliber candidates on their journey through our highly successful "Candidate Experience" process.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Additional responsibilities include consistent utilization of our applicant tracking system, driving best practices, building on our successful employment brand, and partnering with hiring managers to ensure Cloudera has the ability to deliver top level talent.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities </b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Sourcing: in partnership with our Sourcing Team, ability to apply innovative recruiting techniques to actively identify and engage top talent across a variety of technical and non-technical roles, with a focus on the field of enterprise sales.
</b></li>
<li><b>Managing the Process: desire to drive the recruiting process from start to finish. Functions efficiently, familiar with hiring best practices, constantly strives to improve results.
</b></li>
<li><b>Candidate Satisfaction: focused on building rapport and trust with candidates, passionate about employment brand.
</b></li>
<li><b>Partnering: works with hiring managers to develop, implement and manage recruiting strategies to consistently achieve against hiring goals.
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements </b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Expertise in passive sourcing, internet tools and applicant tracking systems (we use Workday).
</b></li>
<li><b>Demonstrated success recruiting top talent for a high-growth enterprise technology company or start-up.
</b></li>
<li><b>Self motivated, strong time management.
</b></li>
<li><b>Highly organized, attention to detail.
</b></li>
<li><b>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Pluses:
</b></p>
<ul><li><b>Proven track record or success in sourcing/recruiting for enterprise sales positions.</b></li></ul>
