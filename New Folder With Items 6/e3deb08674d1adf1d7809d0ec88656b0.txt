<h2>ALON Marketing Group is looking for a Project Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>A tourism marketing and business development consulting company in Farmingdale, NY, is seeking a Project Coordinator. We are a short walk from the Farmingdale train station. Ideally, the candidate would be from the surrounding area.</p>
<p><b>ABOUT: </b> ALON Marketing Group is a leading tourism business development consulting, sales &amp; marketing and tourism training organization. We strive to enable destinations and individual businesses to master and achieve their tourism business goals. We partner together with Destinations of New York State to offer events to build B2B relationships and we also partner with NYC &amp; Company and International Inbound Travel Association to provide tourism training in New York City and across the country.</p>
<p>ALON’s company culture is fast, furious and fun! We encourage participation in a respectful and inclusive manner. We are a driven boutique company working strategically with our resources and always managing our time efficiently by prioritizing tasks to keep moving forward. We are looking for anew team member who is motivated to work in a small company with big projects.We need someone who is self-motivated and passionate about the work they do. We also need someone who is independent, knows how to be a team-player, and brings new ideas and initiatives, along with the skills and determination to implement and achieve them. We hope to meet someone who is a fast learner, resourceful and can contribute, because to work for ALON is to be challenged, to grow and to make a real impact! We contribute towards health benefits, retirement savings, and we have a great team for supporting you and providing a pleasant work environment!</p>
<p><b>OVERVIEW: </b>This position is a full-time, challenging and exciting opportunity to excel in time management, prioritizing skills, as well as, gain new skills in project coordination and execution. The successful candidate will be an energetic individual able to work in a hands-on, fast-paced, multi-tasking environment and will seek to assist the team and President with developing and day-to-day initiatives. The candidate will contribute to the success and completion of all company projects by helping the team as well as managing projects of your own. This position requires enthusiasm to roll up your sleeves and learn all aspects of running a business because we hire with an intention to advance you towards a position that best matches your skills! The ideal candidate should have experience and knowledge about business administration, marketing and project management basics.Knowledge and skills in graphic design and copy writing is a plus.</p>
<p>Please submit a cover letter and take the opportunity to show your applicable experience and motivation for this job. In addition, culture fit is a major factor to become part of the ALON team. Therefore,use your cover letter to tell us more about yourself, your dreams, visions,hobbies and anything else you think would allow us to get the best sense of who you are.</p>
