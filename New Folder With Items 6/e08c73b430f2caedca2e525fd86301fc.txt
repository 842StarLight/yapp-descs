<h2>Senior Financial Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Los Angeles based Internet Brands is currently seeking a Senior Financial Analyst with a strong background in financial analysis to join our corporate finance team.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Work with operation managers within the organization to develop a comprehensive evaluation of financial results, focusing on key metrics.</li>
<li>Partner with operations to develop modeling for strategic initiatives.</li>
<li>Develop financial and operational presentations for our executive team and investors.</li>
<li>Develop acquisition-related metrics, assist in post-acquisition integration, and execute financial modeling.</li>
<li>Spearhead special projects: perform analyses to drive revenue opportunities, cost rationalization analyses, streamline operations.</li>
<li>Review and interpret monthly profit and loss statements.</li>
<li>Prepare monthly forecasts and annual budgets along with variance analysis.</li>
<li>Manage and develop sales performance and compensation plans.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Qualifications:</b><ul>
<li>3 or more years relevant experience.</li>
<li>Undergraduate degree in Accounting, Finance, Economics or similar.</li>
<li>Highest degree of proficiency in financial modeling and analysis; skills should include not only Excel expertise, but also abilities to design, communicate, and adjust models to fit business needs and changes.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate clearly and effectively with all individuals across all functions and levels in order to present findings and drive positive change throughout the company.</li>
<li>Ability to clearly define problems, identify solutions, and independently partner with operations to execute performance-improvement initiatives.</li>
<li>Experience working with SaaS, online media, or vertical software a plus.</li>
<li>SQ, BI experience a plus.</li>
</ul>
