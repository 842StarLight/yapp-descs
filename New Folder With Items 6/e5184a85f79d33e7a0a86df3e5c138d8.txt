<b>ABOUT THE POSITION</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<div>This position is located in the Avante Health Solutions-Charlotte facility located at 1040-A Derita Road in Concord, NC. The operations in this facility support Diagnostic Imaging, Oncology, and Ultrasound equipment sales, service, repair, parts, refurbishing, training, and installation for the Avante Health Solutions organization.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>As the Service Contracts Coordinator</b>, you will be responsible for creating and maintaining all aspects of Avante Ultrasound's service contract agreements to ensure the process is efficient, maximizes financial and operational performance, and increases customer retention and renewal rates. In addition, you will serve as a backup to Customer Service Coordinators (CSCs) and assist the Operations Coordinator as needed. In doing so, you will be part of a winning team that puts customer service and our customers' patients and their families at the top of the priority list.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Set-up new customers via the Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software system (Epicor-E10).</li>
<li>Work directly with customers to collect and process credit line assessment, Terms and Conditions sign-off, and any other necessary information.</li>
<li>Communicate with Sales and Service teams regarding quoting new service contract agreements and verifying equipment coverage.</li>
<li>Draft contract agreements based on strategy discussions with the Manager of Customer Service Operations, Director of Field Service Operations, Sales Representatives, and the National Sales Manager.</li>
<li>Ensure quotes and contract agreements are entered and tracked through the ERP system, while also documenting and communicating contract requirements to affected departments.</li>
<li>Convert won quotes into service contracts and service calls.</li>
<li>Document and agree on changes and amendments that may arise during an active contract period.</li>
<li>Generate, communicate, and process renewal quotes on upcoming expiring contracts.</li>
<li>Assist in ensuring compliance with Terms and Conditions of each contract agreement.</li>
<li>Work closely with accounting department on billing and payments.</li>
<li>Schedule and coordinate service calls including Preventative Maintenance (PM) and Pre-Contract Inspections (PCI) with Field Service Engineers (FSEs) and customers.</li>
<li>Answer incoming customer service calls, provide professional and quality responses and recommendations.</li>
<li>Work with FSEs, in-house technicians, and CSCs to move service tickets to resolution.</li>
<li>Create and disperse quotes to customers based on information provided by FSE, internal technical support, or customer.</li>
<li>Process declined probe repair quotes, and process repair shipments.</li>
<li>Generate and send probe repair quotes, and process repair shipments.</li>
<li>Identify areas to reduce costs and escalate to management as needed.</li>
</ul></div>
