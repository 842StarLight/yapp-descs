<h2>Construction Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Come join a large, growing Program and Construction Management division of one of the nation's top consulting firms. Our team manages some of the largest and most critical transit/rail projects and programs across multiple government agencies. RK&amp;K is seeking a dedicated and strong Construction Project Manager to work from our <b>Maryland, DC or Virginia Office</b>.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Basic Responsibilities/Functions</b></p>
<ul><li> Review, negotiate and approve transit/rail documents (e.g., schedules, submittals and change orders)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Facilitate internal and client meetings, presentations and approval of plans</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrate thorough understanding of contractor means, methods, measurements and materials</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrate thorough understanding of QA/QC development and execution process</li></ul>
<ul><li> Familiarity with interpreting contract specifications, standards, etc.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Technical and Functional Skills</b></p>
<ul><li> Minimum ten years’ experience managing transit/rail related projects and programs</li></ul>
<ul><li> Passion for quality work and client service</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with major urban commuter heavy and/or light rail system and/or facility renovations/new construction required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Capital financial strategic planning experience</li></ul>
<ul><li> RFI and Submittal Processes experience</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with project meetings and regularly supporting clients</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience using Procore or similar document management software</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficiency in MS Office software, P6 or other CPM scheduling software</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Experience and Education</b></p>
<ul><li> CCM</li></ul>
<ul><li> PMP</li></ul>
<ul><li> PE</li></ul>
<ul><li> Freight rail and bus rapid transit experience</li></ul>
<ul><li> WMATA, MDOT and local public agency experience</li></ul>
<br>
