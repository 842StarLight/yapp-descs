<h2>Facilities/Maintenance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Facilities/Maintenance position works in support of the store leadership team to receive, store, stock, and arrange merchandise displays to attract customers. In addition, the Facilities/Maintenance position facilitates the completion of all store level tasks and performs a variety of tasks related to different areas of the store including customer service, general housekeeping, and other related functions. The Facilities/Maintenance position ensures an exceptional customer experience for all Royal Farms customers.
<br><br>
Duties and responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Ensure every customer receives outstanding service by providing a friendly environment (greeting and acknowledging customers, maintaining outstanding standards, product knowledge, and all other components of customer service)</li>
<li>Support and follow all safety and security initiatives</li>
<li>Handles all grocery products in a safe and sanitary manner</li>
<li>Coordinate with supervisor and report all damages in products</li>
<li>Design and set up advertising signs and displays of merchandise on shelves, counters, or tables to attract customers and promote sales</li>
<li>Replenish products and supplies to ensure the store is stocked at all times and maintain track of all shelf life for various goods</li>
<li>Answer customers' questions about merchandise and advise customers on merchandise selection</li>
<li>Clean display cases, shelves, and aisles</li>
<li>Adhere to the execution of established Royal Farms rules, policies, procedures, and systems</li>
<li>Complete all side work as required (cleaning, dusting, sweeping, mopping, emptying trash, etc.)</li>
<li>Replenish products and supplies to ensure the store is stocked at all times</li>
<li>Communicate with the leadership team regarding customer requests and vendor-related concerns</li>
<li>Ensuring the compliance of Royal Farms’ policies, procedures, and systems (people, safety, assets, etc.)</li>
<li>Complete other tasks as assigned</li>
</ul>
The ideal candidate for the Facilities/Maintenance position must be at least 17 years old and will:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Understand that their team’s success depends on their accomplishments</li>
<li>Possess strong written, verbal, and interpersonal communication skills</li>
<li>Possess organizational skills and decision-making skills</li>
<li>Have earned a high school diploma or GED (preferred)</li>
<li>Utilize basic math skills and basic computer skills</li>
<li>Exhibit acceptance of authority</li>
<li>Be able to lift and carry 50 lbs</li>
<li>Be able to lift, bend, and stand as many as eight hours per day</li>
<li>No experience required</li>
<li>Have thorough knowledge of and be committed to Royal Farms culture and values</li>
</ul>
</div>
