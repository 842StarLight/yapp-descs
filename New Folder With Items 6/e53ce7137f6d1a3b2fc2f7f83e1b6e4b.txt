<h2>Business Product Marketing Manager, Growth, North America</h2>

<div>Facebook seeks an experienced advertising and product marketing professional with a strong background in marketing and project management to lead go-to-market strategy for our highest priority advertising solutions in North America. They must thrive with cross-functional teams and exhibit excellent critical thinking and digital marketing skills. They must also have experience influencing senior stakeholders in a highly metric organization. This role is to be based in our New York City or Austin offices.</div>
