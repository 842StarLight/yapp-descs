<h2>VAULT-Claims Analyst– Homeowners Property/Fine Arts and Collections</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Investigate, evaluate, and resolve claims made against the Company’s high net worth homeowner policies. Engage in collaborative interactions in support of other areas of the company, including underwriting, finance and accounting, actuarial, sales and marketing, risk management, operations, and technology. Provide superior service to all customers, whether internal or external.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
RESPONSIBILITIES</div>
<ul><li> Manage a vigorous load of claims involving high net worth homeowner lines. Analyze coverage and manage first party property losses including litigated and non-litigated claims. Effectively interact with insureds, brokers, insured’s personal and/or legal representatives, defense counsel and other parties as required. Inspect property losses in the field, scope losses, estimate damages, investigate subrogation opportunities and assist our clients as their individual and unique needs dictate. Draft and issue reservation of rights and denial letters, retain and direct counsel, evaluate exposure and establish reserves, manage emergency response and repair vendors, manage various experts including; engineers, accountants, and rebuilding consultants, report internally and externally as required and negotiate settlements. Represent Company in the resolution of complex claims and participate in legal proceedings, including mediations. Participate in underwriting decision making, including renewal risk assessment and communication with program agent.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with other areas of the company including underwriting, finance and accounting, actuarial, sales and marketing, risk management, operations, and technology on projects as requested. Prepare claim summaries and other reports as necessary for management. Present severity claims to senior management in large loss meetings.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Meet with existing or prospective clients and brokers. Attend relevant industry conferences/meetings.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist in training of claims associates.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Comply with internal and external reporting.</li></ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<ul>
<li><p>A minimum of 5 years’ experience handling high dollar homeowner and/or fine arts and personal collections claims or related relevant experience. Experience in handling high net worth clients is preferred.
</p></li>
<li><p>Four-year college degree is required; strong clinical background is preferred.</p></li>
<li><p>Substantial homeowner field experience is required and well versed in estimating software, i.e. Xactimate or Symbility. Knowledge of issues related to homeowner and fine arts and collections claims<i>.
</i></p></li>
<li><p>Excellent negotiation and communication skills. Technical writing experience.
</p></li>
<li><p>Proficient in Microsoft Office products.</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to analyze coverage; instruct and collaborate with vendors and experts, defense counsel regarding litigation strategy and claim resolution; resolve claims; and otherwise act within the scope of delegated authority.</p></li>
<li><p>Work is primarily in the field inspecting and investigation losses at the client’s property. Work may also require responding to <i>catastrophe claims</i> in impacted areas. Work may also include responding to emergency situations after core business hours including nights, weekends, and holidays.<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
