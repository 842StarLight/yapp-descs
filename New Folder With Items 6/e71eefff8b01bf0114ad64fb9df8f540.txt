About the role
<br><br>
As a Tulip Strategic Account Manager (SAM), you are on the front lines working with large strategic customer accounts. You will represent Tulip among top-level F500 executives to front-line manufacturing operators. Mapping and navigating complex relationships with multiple stakeholders each with individual interests and responsibilities will be key to your success. You’ll be responsible for building, managing and closing opportunities within a pipeline of proof of concept and expansion deals. Your hard work will inspire manufacturers to adopt Tulip worldwide and will drive sales revenue for Tulip.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<b>In order to succeed at Tulip, you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Break into and drive large, complex deals through lead to close. Experience navigating large company procurement processes.</li>
<li>Drive account strategies and coordinate selling efforts with channel partners to execute a sales process and win deals.</li>
<li>Manage multiple customer opportunities with multiple partners at once while maintaining a high level of attention to detail.</li>
<li>Build accurate forecasting and an operational cadence with management</li>
<li>Drive referenceable customer satisfaction in your accounts</li>
<li>Expertly demonstrate Tulip technology to end users and execute opportunities with channel partners.</li>
<li>Have passion for technology and speak fluently about current trends related to SaaS, PaaS, IoT, and cloud solutions.</li>
<li>Develop champions, stakeholder mind-share, and close at the VP/C-level.</li>
<li>Leverage and collaborate with internal/external resources as a team player; Customer Team, Sales Engineering, Marketing, Channel Partners, Operations, Finance and Customer References etc.</li>
</ul>
