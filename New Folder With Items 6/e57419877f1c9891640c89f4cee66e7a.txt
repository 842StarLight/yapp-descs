<p><b>About Postal Source</b></p>
<p><b>----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------</b></p>
<p>Postal Source has grown and expanded its scope of solutions and services to customers. At its inception, Postal Source provided primarily mailing finishing systems, but over time, we have come to provide enterprise‐level solutions which manage every single communication flowing through a particular organization. In addition, we have assisted clients with comprehensive planning and acquisitions such as setting up an in‐plant Print &amp; Mail production facility or analyzing business workflow design. We engage customers with solutions that help them deliver communications via USPS mail and through integrated solutions. We are looking for an individual to coordinate our marketing, social media, and customer experience.</p>

<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><i>As our organization has continued to grow there is overwhelming needs to enhance and maintain our customer communication and brand recognition. More and more of our clients are looking at our social media presents along with our website. They want the ability to communicate with us through those avenues for service, supplies, customer service questions, and more. This position will report to directly to the CEO, while working with all mangers to enhance our internal and external message. The role of this position will be the execution and maintenance of programs. Better utilization of Sales Force, Share Point, our website, and customer communication through follow up.</i></p>
