<h2>Strategic Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">At Anaplan, we are looking for a Strategic Account Executive based in the North Central area to join one of the fastest growing cloud vendors and make your mark on the industry. You will take your proven track record and sell an incredibly versatile solution that is helping people and companies around the world every day to make better-informed plans and decisions.
<br><br>
In this role, you will be a key contributor to Anaplan's revenue growth while driving change as a market disruptor. Reporting directly to the RVP (Regional Vice President), you will work with our Sales Development Reps, Pre-sales Consultants, Marketing, Marketing Development, Customer Success and Partner groups to build customer value and drive new business onto the Anaplan platform.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll be doing:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Prospecting into a list of targeted accounts (in your local territory) to identify broken business processes and selling Anaplan's unique ability to solve the problem</li>
<li>Building and defending Anaplan's business value throughout the selling engagement. Navigating complex prospect environments to align the prospect around the Anaplan solution</li>
<li>Building and maintaining a pipeline of high-quality opportunities</li>
<li>Utilizing Anaplan's value-based selling methodology and Salesforce.com to manage sales processes and accurately forecast business</li>
<li>Recruiting and leveraging partners and existing customers in your territory to build your Anaplan "franchise"</li>
<li>Landing and expanding the Anaplan roadmap within accounts</li>
</ul>
<b>You have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>8-15 years' successful selling into hyper growth and/or big company space</li>
<li>Experience selling SaaS solutions in directly</li>
<li>Strong network in your local territory with both customers and partners</li>
<li>Consultative &amp; relationship selling skills</li>
<li>Ability to understand and navigate through complex and fast paced company environments</li>
<li>History of meeting/exceeding quotas</li>
<li>A curious nature, hunter mentality, persevering attitude and build relationships with ease</li>
<li>BS/BA degree</li>
</ul>
<b>Bonus points:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>EPM, BI or ERP cloud software sales experience</li>
<li>Passion for a fast paced, high growth environment</li>
<li>Success selling into Finance, Supply Chain &amp; Sales/Sales Operations</li>
<li>Loves white boarding with prospects and creative selling</li>
</ul>
