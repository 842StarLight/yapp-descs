<h2>Systems Engineering Technical Business Analyst - Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Business Analyst/Project Management - Manager<br>
Are you a talented professional that focuses on the planning, execution, and monitoring of project initiatives? Are you a resourceful problem solver that thrives in the identification and definition of creative, effective technology solutions? As a Project Management/Functional Manager, you help clients set-up, manage, and operate their most complex projects, define functional requirements for technology solutions, and design and derive transformative approaches for technology systems.<br>
<br>
Work you’ll do<br>
As a Manager you will bring considerable Functional and Project Management solutions to clients.<br>
<ul>
<li>Manage day to day interactions with executive clients and sponsors</li>
<li>Manage and deliver components of client engagements that identify, design, and implement technology and creative business solutions for large companies.</li>
<li>Responsibilities include, among others, managing teams in the identification of business requirements, functional design, process design (including scenario design, flow mapping), prototyping, testing, training, defining support procedures.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain contact with top decision makers at key clients; participate and lead aspects of the proposal development process; contribute to the development of proposal pricing strategies</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>

The team<br>
Systems Integration<br>
At Deloitte Consulting LLP, our systems integration specialists help clients manage the complexity inherent with technology change, from requirements planning to architecture, testing to deployment, and beyond, helping to link their IT programs to organizational goals. And we offer a complete range of industry-leading services, including systems development, solution and platform integration. Learn more about our Systems Integration practice.</div>
<div>
<br>
Project Management Managers are required to have:</div>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of relevant consulting or industry experience managing and delivering systems</li>
<li>5+ years planning and managing project initiatives in a Project Management Office or similar team environment</li>
<li>3+ years of experience with Project Management Tools such as Microsoft Project and Microsoft SharePoint</li>
<li>5+ years of experience with Requirements Management and Traceability tools such as the IBM Rational suite and HP Quality Center</li>
<li>5+ years of experience translating requirements into optimized designs and identifying alternatives</li>
<li>A willingness to travel 80-100% of the time</li>
<li>Must be legally authorized to work in the United States without the need for employer sponsorship, now, or at any time in the future</li>
</ul>
<br>
In addition, successful Managers will have the following preferred background:<br>
<ul>
<li>Experience of projects involving solution development based on cloud (SaaS, PaaS and IaaS), web or mobile application architecture</li>
<li>Experience of leading fit gap analysis for migrating applications to cloud platform such as Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud or Force.com</li>
<li>Experience or passion for leading testing of applications on Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, Force.com and/or Google Cloud Platform</li>
<li>Experience leading teams in a collaborative environment</li>
<li>Experience managing work planning, monitoring, and reporting</li>
<li>Experience managing requirements processes including conducting interviews and documenting requirements</li>
<li>Experience managing design activities including design sessions and defining future state processes</li>
<li>Experience with industry standard System Development Life Cycle methodologies</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and plan and manage multiple assignments</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>A Bachelor’s Degree in a relevant field</li>
<li>An advanced degree in an area of specialization</li>
</ul>
