<p>I. Job Summary</p>
<p>The Inside Account Manager is responsible for proactive maintenance and revenue growth of current Waste Management customers within a book of business, ensuring that all assigned customers have signed service agreements, in initial term and on file.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>II. Essential Duties and Responsibilities</p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform all essential duties confidently. Additional minor duties may be assigned as needed.</p>
<ul><li>Increase account penetration with strategic product sales</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate proactively with all customers quarterly, to strengthen and expand relationships</li></ul>
<ul><li>Persuade current customers to sign written agreements by providing ongoing education of contract details and use of strong negotiation skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Update and renew customer service agreements</li></ul>
<ul><li>Resolve service escalations within the defined territory</li></ul>
<ul><li>Generate revenue growth by utilizing consultative selling skills in conjunction with value propositions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate rates, fees and service strategies with conviction</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand and use customer incentives and concessions within appropriate profit targets and level of authority</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish and maintain a high level of customer satisfaction</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate and collaborate with the Inside Sales Manager to resolve customer issues</li></ul>
<ul><li>Align Waste Management products and services with customer needs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Engage additional Waste Management business opportunities - referring internally as appropriate</li></ul>
<ul><li>Use Waste Management sales productivity software tools accurately and consistently (i.e. Customer Relationship Management and Pricing Tools)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Propose customer solutions that are compliant with appropriate local, state and federal regulations</li></ul>
