Description</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Assumes engineering design authority for internal or external (outsourced) development activities by collaborating with Pall associates or third-party vendors that may be located on different continents. Ensures that product performance meets all specified requirements including regulatory (UL, ROHS, CE, USP VI, etc.)</li>
<li>
Design and development of single-use, consumable products and associated hardware systems for biopharmaceutical manufacturing processes</li>
<li>
Build CAD models and detailed manufacturing drawings of new products (Single-use assemblies, Hardware systems, Mixing devices, Storage tanks, etc.).</li>
<li>
Drive good aesthetic and ergonomic designs</li>
<li>
Present design concepts to Engineering, Marketing, and Sales through sketches, renderings, models, controlled drawings and reports</li>
<li>
Assess the technical feasibility of concept designs through building and testing of prototypes or theoretical calculations and/or simulations. Writes test protocols to verify or evaluate design performance. Generates reports and presentations related to design activities</li>
<li>
Documentation of engineering drawings, technical reports, and revision history, etc. using PLM (ECO, ECR) (follow good drafting standards, geometric tolerances, etc.)</li>
<li>
Uses tolerance analysis and stress calculations for design analysis</li>
<li>
Provides regular technical updates and project summaries to R&amp;D management and other key partners</li>
<li>
Ensures a safe and balanced work environment by following all company safety policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Finishes the mandatory training assignments (Ethics, Intellectual Property, etc.)</li>
<li>
Travel to vendor and customer sites for FAT/SAT support as projects require, but typically averaging 10% or less</li>
<li>
Helps develop project plans, stage gate work, conducts design reviews, and anticipates project road blocks, delays and risks</li>
<li>
Utilizes critical issue and communication techniques to tackle problems</li>
</ul>

</ul>

<div>
Qualifications</div>
<div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Basic Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors Degree in Engineering</li>
<li>
Minimum of 3 years of experience as a design or mechanical engineer involved in new product development environment</li>
<li>
Experienced in 3D CAD modelling. Experience with design of complex assemblies (For example, Sheet metal, plastic, electromechanical systems)</li>
<li>
Experience working with external vendors (subcontractors) such as mechanical fabricators, material suppliers, etc.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of common and specialty engineering materials</li>
<li>
Proficiency in MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Project).</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Master’s Degree in Engineering or related field.</li>
<li>
5+ years’ experience as a design or mechanical engineer in a new product development environment.</li>
<li>
Experience with design and development of bioprocessing or food processing equipment.</li>
<li>
Knowledge and understanding of the current industry guidelines for designing, manufacturing, testing and documenting biopharmaceutical process equipment. (ASME BPE, GAMP, cGMP, ISPE, etc.)</li>
<li>
Experience in design and development of electronics and controls for electromechanical systems.</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge or experience with software development.</li>
<li>
Experience in qualification activities (FAT/SAT) for process equipment in the biopharmaceutical industry.</li>
<li>
Collaboration skills in a fast-paced, goal-driven matrix organization.</li>
</ul>
