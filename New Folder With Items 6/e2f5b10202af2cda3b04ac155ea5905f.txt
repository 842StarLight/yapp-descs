<h2>Education Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Who is Promethean?</b></p>
<p><br>
Promethean is a leading global education technologies company who is re-inventing the way learning is being delivered. Our mission? To create immersive learning solutions that enable teachers to engage and energize students across the world! Based out of Seattle Washington, with regional centers of gravity in Atlanta (GA), Blackburn (UK), Shenzhen (China) and offices in 10 countries, our aim, is to foster a global community of practice where tools, resources, “how-to” knowledge, experience, and the wisdom to use them intelligently are all available to use and share.<br>
</p>
<p>While Promethean is an established – well capitalized – and recognized EdTech brand, it feels like a startup with a fun, entrepreneurial energy and spirit! This is leading edge development in a fun and challenging atmosphere!</p>
<p><br>
You’ll work alongside the best and the brightest engineering talent in the industry. The Promethean software development group has opportunities in a wide range of areas including development, design, search, platform, test, quality, big data, front end and back-end. As a core participant of your team, you’ll estimate engineering efforts, prioritize projects, plan implementations and triage production issues. You need to be dynamic, collaborative and curious as we build new experiences, improve existing products and develop distributed systems powering the best-in-class interactive flat panels at a scale few companies can match.<br>
<br>
<b>A day in your life as an Education Consultant for Promethean might include:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Providing Professional Development (PD) sessions for Renton School District on Promethean hardware and software (as well as other technologies within the district)</li>
<li>
Mastering all courses in the Promethean professional development catalog / provide feedback for development of future courses / assist with development of customized PD coursework with Renton School District</li>
<li>
Working closely to build relationships with Renton School District and support their training and implementations of Promethean technology</li>
<li>
Building long term relationships and securing future Hardware and Software sales in Renton School District through effective PD and ongoing support</li>
<li>
Interacting with all levels of Renton School District administration and leadership to maintain communication</li>
<li>
Raising awareness in Renton School District of Promethean products and Promethean professional development offerings</li>
</ul>
<b>
To be an amazing Education Consultant for Promethean, you should have or be able to:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>This position is a 24-month full-time position</li>
<li>
Must live locally to Renton School District to travel in/out of schools daily</li>
<li>
5 - 10 years of teaching experience in a US classroom</li>
<li>
Promethean/Interactive Flat Panel experience in both training and content/lesson development</li>
<li>
Strong presentation skills and the ability to excite/relate to educators at all levels in Renton School District</li>
<li>
Excellent public speaking skills with the ability to speak in front of large groups of educators is essential</li>
<li>
Firm understanding of the pedagogy of teaching and learning – shown through the ability to develop / design / deliver exceptional professional development</li>
<li>
Understanding of the business side of education to support the pre-sales organization</li>
<li>
High levels of knowledge on all aspects of educational technology, technology integration, and current education technology trends</li>
<li>
A demonstrated understanding of and experience with research-based approaches to the teaching of reading, writing, mathematics and Common Core</li>
<li>
Minimal travel required for regional/national meetings</li>
<li>
Results driven, well organized, flexible, and have strong follow-up skills</li>
</ul>
