<h2>SALES INTERN</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><p>The Seattle Thunderbirds Hockey Club accepts applications for its Sales Intern Program year round. A T-Birds sales intern will:
<br>
<br>
• Generate new season ticket and group sales leads
<br>
• Learn all aspects of the sales process in regards to season tickets, group tickets, suite sales and advertising packages.
<br>
• Responsible for specific sales-driven projects
<br>
• Assisting with ticket mailings and season ticket holder outreach
<br>
• Other special projects and administrative duties, as assigned
<br>
<br>
Pursuit of Sport Management, Marketing, Sales or related career or degree is beneficial.
<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate must have great communication skills, be professional and be able to multi-task. Applicant must have proficient computer skills including Microsoft Word, Excel, Power Point, and Outlook. Any experience working for a team, athletic department, in sales or with a sporting event is beneficial.
<br>
<br>
The Sales Intern program takes place in the hockey off-season. In the past sales interns have been promoted from the program to a full time Account Executive position with the team.
