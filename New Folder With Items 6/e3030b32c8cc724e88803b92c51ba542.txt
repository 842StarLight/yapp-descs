<p><b>SUCCESS FACTORS</b></p>

<li>Establish a critical path for performance in sales, marketing, and business development by creating a framework for and adhering to specific deliverables, timeframes and metrics to ensure expedited progress and successful outcomes;</li>
<li>Take an aggressive approach in leading his/her team in all sales activities; develop and implement sales, distribution and major account strategies and tactics and solution-oriented selling methodologies to expand the engagmentment with existing customers and acquire new customers; deploy a fit to purpose sales process enabled by salesforce.com tools and drive sales success, increase revenue velocity while maintaining accountability;</li>

Doctor Evidence (DRE) is pleased to offer an exciting opportunity to join a dynamic and rapidly-growing company based in Santa Monica, California at a senior level position. DRE operates in a fast-paced and creative environment where you will be empowered to act on your initiative with the ultimate goal of selling our SaaS solutions into our client base .
