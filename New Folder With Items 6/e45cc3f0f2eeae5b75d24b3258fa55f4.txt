<h2>Budtender</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>Budtenders provide product knowledge to customers and patients by explaining the benefits of cannabis products. They deliver exceptional customer service and drive sales while assuring satisfaction. Ideal candidates are passionate about the cannabis industry and regularly sharpen their skills by researching products and trends.</p>
<p><b>What You’ll Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Cleanliness: </b> maintain an organized and tidy retail sales area.</li>
<li>
<b>Communication: </b> answer phones quickly and take messages.</li>
<li>
<b>Compliance: </b> ensure activities are in accordance with all applicable rules and regulations.</li>
<li>
<b>Consultation: </b> recommend products that best fit the consumer’s needs.</li>
<li>
<b>Coordination: </b> ensure front room, sales floor, and back room are well-stocked.</li>
<li>
<b>Greeting: </b> check shoppers in with a friendly attitude.</li>
<li>
<b>Input: </b> accurately create and process orders in the point of sale system.</li>
<li>
<b>Positivity: </b> promote a cheerful work environment.</li>
<li>
<b>Promotion: </b> inspire confidence and loyalty to our brand.</li>
<li>
<b>Service: </b> exceed expectations by providing outstanding care.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Who You Are</b></p>
<ul>
<li>An MED Support Badge holder. MED Key Badge preferred.</li>
<li>Adaptable and accommodating of weekend, evening, and holiday schedules across multiple locations.</li>
<li>Comfortable multi-tasking in a dynamic, regularly-changing environment.</li>
<li>Dependable and timely for all scheduled shifts.</li>
<li>Detailed, accountable, and pro-active.</li>
<li>Efficient, professional, and organized.</li>
<li>Experienced with customer service and/or budtending ( &gt;1 Year).</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in point of sale software, cash management, and other basic computer functions ( &gt;1 Year).</li>
<li>Physically able to perform some manual labor tasks.</li>
<li>Prepared to undergo a background check.</li>
<li>Service and team-oriented.</li>
<li>Willing to take direction and provide appropriate feedback.</li>
</ul>
