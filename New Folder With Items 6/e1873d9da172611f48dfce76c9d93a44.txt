<div>Founded in 1866, The Sherwin-Williams Company is a global leader in the manufacture, development, distribution, and sale of paints, coatings and related products to professional, industrial, commercial, and retail customers. The company manufactures products under well-known brands such as Sherwin-Williams®, Valspar®, HGTV HOME® by Sherwin-Williams, Dutch Boy®, Krylon®, Minwax®, Thompson’s® Water Seal®, Cabot® and many more. Sherwin-Williams® branded products are sold exclusively through a chain of more than 4,100 company-operated stores and facilities, while the company’s other brands are sold through leading mass merchandisers, home centers, independent paint dealers, hardware stores, automotive retailers, and industrial distributors. The company supplies a broad range of highly-engineered industrial and OEM coatings for wood and general industrial, coil, packaging, protective and marine, and transportation applications worldwide. Our 60,000 employees are diverse, innovative and passionate. With a variety of rewarding and challenging opportunities, Sherwin-Williams is a great place to find a career that takes you places.<br>

<div>The Senior Oracle Applications Analyst -Supply Chain Management position combines business knowledge and an understanding of the inner-workings and configuration options of a decentralized Oracle implementation of EBS and Value Chain Planning (VCP) instances which includes modules like Purchasing, Order Management, Oracle Process Manufacturing, Inventory, Warehouse Management, Oracle Advanced Supply Chain Planning (ASCP) and Demantra. Understand business problems and opportunities in the context of the requirements and recommends solutions that enable the organization to achieve its goals.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions</b><br>
<br>
<b>Strategy &amp; Planning</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with management to generate quarterly work plans.</li>
<li>Recommend improvements for the business analysis process.</li>
<li>Provide recommendations to address and resolve business issues for a specific business group.</li>
<li>Research tools to identify those that can best help serve the needs of clients.</li>
<li>Mentor other team members and share knowledge on system functionalities and settings.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Acquisition &amp; Deployment</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Gather requirements, create design documents, and perform impact analysis for application changes.</li>
<li>Plan and organize tasks, report progress, and manage and coordinate changes with other groups and fellow team members.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Operational Management</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop solutions to leverage Oracle applications functionality for the planning areas including Advanced Supply Chain Planning (ASCP), Purchasing, Manufacturing, Inventory Planning Methods (e.g., Min-Max Planning) and Demantra.</li>
<li>Lead user sessions for requirement and testing.</li>
<li>Guides technical team in the development of reports, conversions, interfaces, and extensions for Oracle Applications.</li>
<li>Understand and modify the Application Configuration as required (includes system setups, flex-fields, workflow builder, custom library, profile options, etc.).</li>
<li>Assist users with problems and resolve issues independently.</li>
<li>Create test plans, test cases, test scripts and performs functional testing.</li>
<li>Create and maintain system documentation.</li>
<li>Work with existing systems to track and manage requirements and issues.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Incidental Functions</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist with projects as may be required to contribute to efficiency and effectiveness of the work.</li>
<li>Attend management meetings on behalf of team.</li>
<li>Participate in hiring activities and fulfilling affirmative action obligations and ensuring compliance with the equal employment opportunity policy.</li>
</ul>

<b>
Position Requirements</b><br>
<br>
<b>Formal Education &amp; Certification</b><br>
<ul><li>Bachelor degree or foreign equivalent in related field or equivalent experience.</li></ul>
<br>
<b>
Knowledge &amp; Experience</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of IT experience.</li>
<li>5+ years Oracle applications experience with various planning, manufacturing and distribution modules like Oracle Advanced Supply Chain Planning (ASCP), Oracle Purchasing(PO), Oracle Process Manufacturing (OPM), Inventory (INV).</li>
<li>Expertise with configuration and setups for ASCP and Inventory Planning Methods like Min-Max Planning, Reorder Point Planning etc.</li>
<li>3+ years’ experience in Production support and maintenance of Oracle applications – Supply chain management modules like Oracle Advanced Supply Chain Planning (ASCP), Inventory, Order Management, Purchasing, OPM etc.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of software development and support methodologies.</li>
<li>Demonstrated skill in preparation and maintenance of implementation documents.</li>
<li>Knowledge and familiarity in underlying Oracle ERP applications database architecture, as well as Oracle database and development tools (Toad, PL/SQL Developer, SQL Plus, Forms, Reports).</li>
</ul>
