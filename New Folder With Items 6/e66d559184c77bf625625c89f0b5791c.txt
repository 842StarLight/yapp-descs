<h2>Fair and Festival Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>PROJECT OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p><b>@the Grounds (ATG)</b> is non-profit organization, responsible for the management of the <b>@the Grounds</b> campus located in Roseville, CA. The campus is comprised of 60 acres and home to the Placer County Fair, the Annual City of Roseville 4th of July Celebration, All American Speedway Racing and several public and private events including trade shows, festivals, corporate events, concerts, graduations and weddings. Opening in February 2020, a brand new 32-million-dollar Event Center. This 160,000 square foot Event Center is capable of hosting meetings of 5,000, 12 basketball courts or 24 volleyball courts, and multiple types sports tournaments and competitions making <b>@the Grounds</b> the premier meeting place in the Sacramento Area.</p>
<p>This is an exciting opportunity for an individual that is innovative, entrepreneurial, diligent and business savvy who want to be an important part of building this new operation.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>MISSION</b></p>
<p>To ensure the sustainable operation and management of <b>@the Grounds</b> as a premier sporting, meeting, event, festival, fair, concert and celebration destination with a focus both on local rentals and events that achieve specific hotel room night goals.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>AREA</b></p>
<p>Population of about 250,000 in the Placer Valley region part of the Sacramento Metroplex. It is a beautiful area with significant growth especially in our immediate area. There are great parks and sites, with a heavy emphasis on family. Just a short drive away you can enjoy skiing in Lake Tahoe, sightseeing in San Francisco or wine tasting in the famous Napa Valley.
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>Responsible for the successful planning, sales, marketing, operation and growth of ATG owned events and as well as assisting our team with facility rental events as needed. Lead the successful implementation of ATG owned events, create and plan new festivals and events. Coordinate and liaison with the other ATG department to ensure successful, well regarded and growing events. Must be a motivated professional team player ensuring excellent customer service to position <b>@the Grounds</b> as “the place” in South Placer County and the Sacramento region for events, fairs, festivals and celebrations.</p>
<ul>
<li>Operate and maintain ATG owned events in compliance with all State, Federal and local laws
</li>
<li>Develops and adheres to approved event budget for each ATG owned event</li>
<li>Develops and implements marketing plans for each ATG owned event</li>
<li>In charge of all operations of ATG owned events, coordinates and communicates with other ATG departments for each event</li>
<li>In coordination with Human Resources and other ATG departments helps recruit and train ATG event staff</li>
<li>Ensure employees are trained to perform their duties and follow all safety procedures</li>
<li>Plan and supervise daily workload of all employees for ATG owned events, interfaces with grounds maintenance, finance and program administration through the appropriate department heads</li>
<li>Creates policy, procedures, timelines and responsibilities for ATG owned events</li>
<li>Develop and administer budget for each ATG owned event to the satisfaction of the CEO and Board, with monthly updates and forecasts for revenues and expenditures</li>
<li>Monitor, recommend, implement, and communicate fee schedule changes as needed</li>
<li>Ensures maximization of site usage and revenue streams from ATG owned events</li>
<li>Oversee an aggressive marketing/sales plan for sponsors, partners and attendees for ATG owned events</li>
<li>Oversee development and operation of ATG owned events including annual Placer County Fair, Fourth of July Celebration and future festivals and promotional events</li>
<li>Resolve issues and/or conflicts as they arise</li>
<li>Ensures well run events through planning and attention to detail and communication</li>
</ul>
