<h2>Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>As a rapidly growing global provider of IT solutions, we’ve expanded from Seattle to Sydney and beyond – with reach in over 80 countries around the world. Today’s top brands count on Zones and our world-class partners – Microsoft, Apple, Cisco, Dell, Lenovo, and more – to transform their enterprises. To support this transformation, Zones offers products and services across four Solution Environments: Workplace Modernization, Network Optimization, Data Center Transformation, and Security Fortification.<br>
<br>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Pre-Sales / Solutioning Analyst will be a key member of the Sales team in support of the both the Sales &amp; Services organization. As an Analyst you will assist the Pre-Sales and Sales team with detailed, prescriptive analysis of the Services business pipeline. Specifically lead all opportunity data analysis, tracking of probability &amp; status, demand planning requirements and revenue forecasting (bookings and revenue) for the entire Services pipeline.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This role requires strong analytical capabilities, presence to partner with Sales managers, excellent follow through, and the ability to meet time sensitive deadlines. By aligning directly alongside all departments involved in the sales cycle, this role will create and leverage effective lines of communication for clearer understanding and clarity of all opportunities that will drive higher visibility into the Services revenue stream and demand planning processes in partnership with Services Finance team.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Functions/Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive the data analytics related to CRM opportunities through day to day management of all Services opportunities.</li>
<li>
Analyze account/services opportunities within top accounts and develop cadence with Sales managers to understand deal specific s including; types of Service, expected close dates, contract terms, estimated revenue “burn” rates provide actionable recommendations to Sales &amp; Finance organizations.</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing analysis of lead conversion metrics from opportunity creation through to deal closure</li>
<li>
Represent Pre-Sales and Solutioning organizational requirements/enhancements throughout the lifecycle of system projects both in CRM, PowerBI and Tenrox</li>
<li>
Communicate at the executive level with a consistent, strong voice as a key part of the Solutioning and Sales team</li>
<li>
Liase with Services Operational &amp; Finance teams take ownership of items including opportunity tracking and revenue “burn” forecast, Services demand planning reporting and analytics and reporting</li>
<li>
Aggregate, compare, analyze and summarize large amounts of data for executive consumption</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Desired Skills and Experience</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Degree in Informatics, Accounting, Finance, Business Administration, Economics or equivalent</li>
<li>
4-6 yrs. of experience in collaborating with Sales and Finance. IT Services experience highly preferred</li>
<li>
Proven track record of analyzing sales data to support business success, data fidelity and pipeline visibility</li>
<li>
Strong presence with the ability to communicate with Sales &amp; Finance staff. Present results and solutions to senior leaders</li>
<li>
Experience with Dynamics CRM and other sales tools</li>
<li>
Proven experience with Power BI using various datasets (CRM, SharePoint Lists, Excel)</li>
<li>
Possesses strong Microsoft Office skills, including Excel, Word and Power Point</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple, time-sensitive projects simultaneously</li>
<li>
Strong time management and prioritization skills with aptitude to manage multiple stakeholders in a given project</li>
<li>
Self-starter and ability to execute day-to-day with limited supervision</li>
<li>
Bias towards action, anticipation, and taking the next step and asking the next question</li>
</ul>
