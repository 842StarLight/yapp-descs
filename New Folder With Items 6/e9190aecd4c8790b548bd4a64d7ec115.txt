<h2>Inside Sales Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Denver Post, in partnership with Adtaxi, is a full-service media company, reaching more Denver-area adults every day of the week than any other medium in the market. The Denver Post’s diverse portfolio of digital, mobile, print and social media products includes Colorado’s largest daily newspaper, the state’s most-visited news website, DenverPost.com, and the largest network of websites, which includes DailyCamera.com and ColoradoDaily.com.</p>
<div>
Adtaxi is a client-centric digital organization that brings scale, precision, and sophistication to digital marketing. Leveraging the belief that people matter as much as technology, Adtaxi helps advertisers solve complex marketing challenges with custom, performance-driven solutions.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<div>
As an <b>Inside Sales Account Executive</b>, you’ll achieves success and drive revenue by utilizing professional sales skills and applying knowledge of the Denver Market, competing media and the customer’s business, in order to negotiate sales and provide effective advertising solutions.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain and service current accounts by evaluating customers needs, utilize applicable research, make presentations, prepare written proposals, and close sales</li>
<li>
Develop new business via prospecting</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain thorough knowledge of complete portfolio of sales solutions for current customers and new business</li>
<li>
Answering an average of 30 inbound calls per day, with a minimal abandoned rate.</li>
<li>
Aggressively sell against competing media</li>
<li>
Assist in the development of effective ad copy and design layout</li>
<li>
Manage all paperwork and necessary details, including accurately ordering ad insertions and adhering to production requirements; timely processing of credit adjustments and maintaining account files by following accounting/credit procedures; and preparing accurate monthly revenue projections</li>
<li>
Ensure good customer relations by handling all customer requests, questions and complaints, and facilitating their resolution through personal action and intervention</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
