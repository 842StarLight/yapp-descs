<div>Ritchie Bros. Auctioneers has an immediate opening for a full-time <b>Auction Coordinator</b> in <b>Tipton, California</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<b>
The Opportunity:</b><br>
<div></div>
<div>The Auction Coordinator serves as a lead in the communication, coordination and support to all internal departments (e.g. office, yard, sales, etc.) related to the all auction processes and the Detailed Equipment Information (DEI) Program.</div>
<br>
<div>
<i>
In this role you'll:</i><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Manage the information collection and verification processes (e.g. equipment year, sequencing, equipment pricing, etc.) related to the DEI Program.</li>
<li>Responsible for the preparation of the advertising material for upcoming sales (e.g.brochure and catalogue), in accordance with established policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Work in conjunction with the Equipment Inspector to assess equipment and compose photos for the DEI Program.</li>
<li>Responsible for auction sequencing and catalogue proofing activities.</li>
<li>
Responsible for resolving equipment contract and DEI related issues including any unresolved issues that occur on sale day.</li>
<li>Assist the Customer Service Manager (CSM) with sale day and post sale duties,customer service and administrative support as required.</li>
<li>Promote a culture where the health and safety of our employees and customers is top priority for all.</li>
<li>Assist the Sales team with appraisals and write-ups in the field between peak periods.</li>
<li>Support the local operations team with sale day duties (e.g. proxy bids, eClerking,bid catching, etc.).</li>
</ul>
<b>
Here's what you'll bring:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You have at least 2 years experience in customer facing roles.</li>
<li>High school graduate with some post-secondary education is considered an asset, such as a Finance Diploma.</li>
<li>Intermediate proficiency level in current MS Office Suite products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).</li>
<li>Proficiency using technology such as computers and digital cameras.</li>
<li>An aptitude for data/numbers and experience creating and working with spreadsheets.</li>
<li>Strong written, spoken, listening, and interpersonal communication skills in English.</li>
<li>Other additional languages would be considered an asset.</li>
<li>A solid knowledge or desire to gain knowledge of construction equipment and trucks.</li>
<li>Strong customer service orientation. You have a natural ease dealing with customers.</li>
<li>You're a resourceful, “think-on-your feet” person with drive, initiative, and a real commitment to getting the job done.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills, you’re the type that leaves no detail unattended.</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills, the ability to multi-task multiple projects and initiatives effectively and prioritize work are essential.</li>
<li>Highly capable of meeting strict deadlines in a high-pressure, ever-changing environment.</li>
<li>
You must also have the ability to adapt to shifting priorities, demands and timelines.</li>
<li>Highly motivated, goal-oriented and self directed. Energetic, can-do attitude!</li>
<li>Experience working independently and collaboratively in a team-oriented environment.</li>
<li>
You thrive working as part of a team and pitch-in wherever needed.</li>
<li>You have a strong work ethic without sacrificing your sense of humor or your ability to have fun on the job.</li>
</ul>
<i>
This job will require you to:</i>
<ul>
<li>Have a valid, clean driver’s license.</li>
<li>Climb on and off large equipment and trucks.</li>
<li>Work both indoors and outdoors year-round.</li>
<li>Work regular overtime, including weekend hours, at the auction sales.</li>
<li>
You can expect to work extra hours for two weeks prior to each sale, during each sale, and for one week after each sale.</li>
<li>Travel to other auction sites if necessary.</li>
</ul>
