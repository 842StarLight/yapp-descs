<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This role works with the marketing team and cross-functional partners across the organization to execute on the delivery of the company’s overall strategic marketing plan specifically within the Conventional vertical market. This position is responsible for ensuring the efficient orchestration of timelines, schedules and workflow in a high-volume, fast-changing environment, and requires strong organization, communication and presentation skills.<br>
<br>
Excellent project management skills are necessary to produce integrated marketing campaigns including email, print, digital, video, event and social components. Must be able to report on the status of projects and results to executive level leadership. Must have good planning and time-management skills and be adept at balancing multiple assignments and deadlines. Must be a self-starter who can build a good rapport with internal partners and peers.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<br>
PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES<br>
<br>
Responsibilities for this position include but are not limited to the following:<br>
 Build out yearly go-to-market plans that include market positioning and messaging along<br>
 Report on results of program effectiveness and go-to-market initiatives<br>
 Ability to define communication strategy and define customer problems,<br>
 Assist in development of competitive assessment and win/loss analysis, and assist in building buyer and user personas<br>
 Define the scope of project(s) in collaboration with internal marketing team, product, sales and other key internal and external stakeholders<br>
 Consistently monitor schedules and deliverables to ensure jobs are on track, and elevate issues when jobs are at risk, and plan contingencies<br>
 Drives project launch dates and ensures kick-off meetings and the appropriate status meetings are in place for timely planning and execution<br>
 Create detailed work plans: timelines, schedules and deliverables needed to successfully complete the project deliverables<br>
 Lead meetings, check-ins and stand-ups, distributing key information in advance, keeping the team aligned and on track to achieving goals and outlining next steps<br>
 Facilitate collaborative relationships with creative, social, video, digital, automation, and production teams to deliver integrated campaigns on deadline<br>
 Manage the content/creative development, review, edit and approval process for individual campaign assets</div>
</div></div></div>
