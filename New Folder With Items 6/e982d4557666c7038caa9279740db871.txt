<h2>Enterprise Sales Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>In this position, you will primarily develop business in new accounts, which will include our entire scope of offerings. You will also be responsible for developing and implementing action plans for penetration into our existing account base which is made up of a diverse set of vertical industries.</p>
<p>
 Our sales role is open-ended and exposes you to several career paths that enthusiastic high achievers can take to move into leadership positions within our company. All sales are Business-to-Business outside sales.</p>
<p>
 Skills:</p>
<p>
 New Business Development</p>
<ul>
<li>Client/Prospect Relationship Development</li>
<li>Cold Calling</li>

<li>Problem Solving, Decision Making. Listening and Creative Thinking Capabilities</li>
<li>Develop a business plan for accounts to meet or exceed sales, marketing and customer satisfaction objectives.</li>
<li>Ability to manage proposal responses</li>

<li>Assess competitor's strategy and neutralize with a competitive counter strategy.</li>
