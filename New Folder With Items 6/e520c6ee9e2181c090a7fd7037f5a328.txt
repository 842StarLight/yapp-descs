<h2>Account Executive Assistant – Commercial Team</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Summary:</b>The Account Executive Assistant position provides processing and clerical support to assigned Account Executive/Account Manager.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Functions:</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Issue certificates of insurance and evidences of property insurance as required under direction of AE/AM.</li>
<li>Run Motor Vehicle Reports as required under direction of AE/AM.</li>
<li>Monitor cancellations and keep Account Executive appraised of status.</li>
<li>File claims with carriers.</li>
<li>Process endorsements, to include change requests, suspense, invoicing and mailing.</li>
<li>Assist in renewal process by ordering loss runs and monitoring receipt.</li>
<li>Assist in preparing Loss Summary for marketing submissions; provide Loss Updates to AE/AM and to Agent if requested.</li>
<li>Assist in renewal process by gathering information and updating applications.</li>
<li>Assist on completing policy checks and confirming that all items are scanned into automation system.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Planning/organizing</b> – the individual must be able to prioritize and plan work activities and use time efficiently.</li>
<li>
<b>Interpersonal skills</b> – the individual must maintain confidentiality and be a team player.</li>
<li>
<b>Oral communication</b> – the individual must be capable of speaking clearly and persuasively in positive or negative situations.</li>
<li>
<b>Adaptability</b> – the individual must be able to adapt to changes in the work environment and/or responsibilities and manage competing demands.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>This job description is not meant to be an all inclusive statement of the duties of the position listed above.</b></p>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>Required Skills</div>
<div>
<p><b>Required</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills, as well as organizational and prioritizing skills</li>
<li>Professional demeanor, positive attitude</li>
<li>Experience with personal computers and related software (Microsoft Word, Excel)</li>
<li>Ability to juggle multiple tasks, projects, and people.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred</b></p>
<ul>
<li>One to three years insurance experience preferred</li>
<li>4-40 Customer Service Representative License or 2-20 Property and Casualty License</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Requirement Necessary on a Regular Basis:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manual dexterity, arm and upper body range of motion sufficient for use of a keyboard, mouse and telephone 7-8 hours per day.</li>
<li>Speech and hearing sufficient for in-person and telephone communication 7-8 hours per day.</li>
<li>Vision sufficient for use of a computer monitor.</li>
<li>Ability to sit at a desk 7-8 hours per day.</li>
</ul>
