<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>Reporting to Head of Data Technology and Automation within EDG, Senior Leader, Data Engineering serves as the thought leader as well as oversight to the Data Engineering organization. In partnership with other EDG leaders and peers, this individual is responsible for building, managing a team of experts focusing on Data Engineering initiatives supporting products and solutions for data onboarding. This is a hands on role which can play role of technical SME.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibility
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to communicate with project teams and Senior EDG/ Business Leaders.
</li>
<li>Possesses strong negotiation skills, influencing skills and builds relationship with peers/ clients/ partners.
</li>
<li>Ability to have difficult conversations tactfully and drive decisions in interest of the organization with speed and agility.
</li>
<li>Ability to understand and analyze complex process, build strategies to improve and automate process and ensures it is in alignment with EDG vision.
</li>
<li>Responsible to own and engage in-depth assessments to validate that the process, technology and architecture is appropriate for current state or recommend solutions for future state in partnership with peers within EDG and other departments.
</li>
<li>Manage day to day activities of VIPER, DOC/ DSF and the team of Data Engineers supporting those products.
</li>
<li>Liaison for Tax Services, TaxRoll, MetroScan, CoreLogic Australia businesses for data onboarding. Responsible for providing thought leadership to execute data engineering projects and respective change management.
</li>
<li>Ensures project portfolio is well documented, communicated and has alignment on schedules and resources. With the broader team provides SME support to key initiatives (SDP, Tax2020) while providing support to TaxRoll, MetroScan, REM, Cap Market etc. Monitors industry trends and directions, suggests new technologies and adapts to changing needs.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 12 years of experience required in area of data engineering, data processing systems and processes in large complex environments. At least 7 years experience in managing teams of architects, senior developers.
</li>
<li>Experienced in establishing teams.
</li>
<li>Strong communication skills required.
</li>
<li>Experienced in managing stakeholder expectations, manage budget, resource allocation, vendor management aspects.
</li>
<li>Good understanding of tools, processes, data engineering concepts and various automation techniques.
</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of design and integration principles for complex, heterogeneous systems.
</li>
<li>Experienced in various integration techniques and ability to understand complex integration requirements and drive actionable results.
</li>
<li>Ability to develop presentations that speaks to both EDG and business value.
</li>
<li>Good experience with Project Life Cycle and Software Development Lifecycle methodology required.
</li>
<li>Strong collaboration, influencing, and interpersonal skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to understand and speak technical language with developers and motivate them.
</li>
<li>Experienced with a few of SQL, Java, Spring, Angular.JS, Hibernate, Big Data (Hive, Hadoop), GreenPlum desired.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Bachelor's degree or equivalent; Master's degree is preferred.
</p>
