<h2>Project Manager, Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>ob Description</p>
<p>ClinEdge is a leader in patient recruitment for clinical trials and we’re looking for another strong Marketing Project Manager to add to our team. Are you looking for a role in a fast paced environment where you’re making a difference every day? Let us know!</p>
<p><b>Job purpose: </b></p>
<p>ClinEdge is currently seeking to hire a Marketing Project Manager with a strong focus in project management, patient recruitment, advertising and who will assist in the execution of our external clients’ overall patient recruitment strategy. This individual will be an integral member of the marketing team, working with sales, design, enrollment and clients on all elements of patient recruitment campaigns and overall marketing strategies. The focus of our campaigns is to drive new potential patient interest for upcoming and ongoing clinical trials, which in turn supports the advancement of treatments and medicine for future generations.</p>
<p><b>Duties and responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>This role requires someone who is web-savvy, understands paid advertising, has strong client service skills, and enjoys leading both a project and project team. The Marketing Project Manager will work alongside a strong and dedicated marketing team to implement advertising tactics for clients that may range from online advertising, traditional advertising, grassroots efforts, design, and prescreening patient referrals. This role is focused on the management of patient recruitment projects, developing strong content and implementing advertising tactics, understanding how to gauge campaign success for ROI (cost per click, cost per lead, cost per impression, etc.), and building strong rapport with clients to ensure retention and overall satisfaction. You will be responsible for leading projects that may encompass a wide array of tactics or they may be hyper focused in one specific area depending on the client needs.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate is a highly organized, self-starter, can operate well under tight deadlines and competing priorities, and is committed to delivering quality service at all times. Excellent communication skills and organization are essential. The candidate should be a team player who enjoys and is proactive in solving problems.</p>
<p>The marketing team is an established part of the company and there will be a great deal of opportunity for those who show strong initiative.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for managing the complete life cycle of highly complex projects, including planning, execution, and closeout to ensure planned results are achieved on time and budget</li>
<li>Work with clients, vendors and team to establish and achieve project goals and continually identify projects issues, gaps, conflicts, and propose solutions</li>
<li>Work as the liaison between clients, business leads, functional leads, SME’s (subject matter experts), developers and other technical team members to assist and document solutions</li>
<li>Main point for execution and management of Google and Facebook paid advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Set up and proactively manage individual campaigns to ensure all are delivering in full with the best possible performance; responsible for campaign analysis, reporting and driving optimization</li>
<li>Oversee client delivery and performance, analyze potential campaign issues, and pre-empt issues with recommendations</li>
<li>Communicate ad specs and deadlines to clients and insure all creative assets and third party tracking tags are gathered and working properly</li>
<li>Support sales team in all day to day digital activities including client requests, account management, campaign reporting/analysis, and acquiring creative assets</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales reps in frequent client communication for both pre and post-sale execution; manage communication regarding clients and campaigns with team members</li>
<li>Retain clients through exceptional client service</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills: </b></p>

<li>Bachelor’s degree in communications, management, or other relevant field</li>
<li>3-4 years’ experience in project management and marketing, ideally with a focus in patient recruitment or clinical research</li>
<li>Customer service oriented with a proactive attitude</li>
<li>Strong written, verbal, presentation, and people skills</li>
<li>Ability to lead and manage a project team in order to ensure client goals are met</li>
<li>Demonstrated organizational skills and attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to meet tight deadlines, be a problem solver, and juggle numerous projects and/or priorities simultaneously</li>
<li>Creative mindset with the ability to clearly express your ideas to cross-departmental teams and clients alike</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Additional Skills: </b></p>

<li>Experience leading and/or working alongside a call center team</li>
<li>Basic understanding of landing pages/websites, tracking mechanisms, email marketing, design</li>
<li>Healthcare industry and/or clinical research experience a plus</li>
</ul>
