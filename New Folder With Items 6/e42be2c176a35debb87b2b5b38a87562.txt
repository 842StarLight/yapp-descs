</p>
<p></p>
<p>Become part of a growing team focused on the development of the systems requirements and design for aviators and crew with all levels of the training fidelity for the missions that they perform. This impact is felt both domestically and abroad with trainers for both our US government and for foreign governments through Foreign Military Sales. Examples of programs that will be worked are the E-2 Hawkeye, B-52 and C-130 to name a few.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Simulator &amp; Training Systems (STS) Fixed Wing and Mission Systems Engineering department seeks a Senior Systems Engineer to work on the development of new and exciting simulation systems and products. The Senior System Engineer will have tasks relating to all parts of the systems development life cycle, design, development and verification.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Tasks include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Leading and participating in technical interchange meetings and design working group meetings
</p></li>
<li><p>Interacting with customers to define/capture operational concepts and translating those concepts into system requirements
</p></li>
<li><p>Decomposing requirements and developing system level architectures including system level modeling and functional allocation of requirements</p></li>
<li><p>
Contributing to the preparation and delivery of technical briefings to customers and upper management.
</p></li>
<li><p>Providing risk analysis regarding systems development decisions on meeting technical, schedule, and cost baseline requirements
</p></li>
<li><p>Estimating, tracking, and reporting project costs</p></li>
<li><p>
Interfacing with and supporting software engineers, architects, and domain leads through requirements capture, functional &amp; integration testing, and delivery support.</p></li>
<li><p>
Coordinating, documenting, reviewing, and testing of simulations, trainers and system interfaces.
</p></li>
<li><p>Performing System level Verification and Validation activities</p></li>
</ul>
