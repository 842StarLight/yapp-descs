<p><b>Pricing Analyst - Signing &amp; Execution</b></p>
<p><b>
J.C. Penney Company, Inc.</b></p>
<p><b>
Plano, Texas</b></p>
<p>
The <b>Pricing Analyst, Signing &amp; Execution </b>will support business objectives and company initiatives for pricing and signing. In this role the analyst will be responsible for analytics and operational tasks. This role’s daily tasks will be flexible and adaptable based on the needs of the team. The type of work that will be done can vary from day to day and from project to project.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Learn business process from end to end for pricing and signing</li>
<li>
Pilot new pricing and signing processes and projects
</li>
<li>Assist in developing process improvements, and develop documentation to support process improvements</li>
<li>
Create implementation plans for new pricing and signing programs and projects
</li>
<li>Assist in developing tools to assist the team in executing pricing and signing more efficiently
</li>
<li>Assist in developing project management tools and timelines
</li>
<li>Assists in pulling data from multiple data resources, analyze data and provide leadership with recommendations based off data
</li>
<li>Communicate data points, build presentations, and assist in delivery when needed
</li>
<li>Produce reporting for team and leadership as needed to drive initiatives
</li>
<li>Research competitive initiatives for pricing and signing
</li>
<li>Consistently meets established performance standards for the role, including (but not limited to) product and service sales, customer service, profit, productivity, and attendance</li>
<li>
Have knowledge of price strategies, the legal guidelines associated with each strategy, as well as the pricing and signing process needed to execute</li>
<li>
Work across multiple business, including but not exclusive too, the merchant teams, marketing, procurement, information technology, and finance.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Core Competencies &amp; Accomplishments:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree with 1 - 2+ years of experience building predictive models, data mining</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Fluent in excel, word processing programs, power point, and other MS office programs
</li>
<li>Knowledge with SQL and MS Access preferred
</li>
<li>Ability to build relationships across business lines</li>
</ul>
