<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></h2>
<p>Accounts Payable (A/P) Specialist is responsible for all payments of goods and services necessary for the basic operation of the Philadelphia Convention and Visitors Bureau.</p>
<p><b>PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Process and audit all invoices, expense reports, check requisitions, coding, previous payments, discounts, and sales/use tax accrual (if applicable)</li>
<li>Enter all invoices/payables into Accounting Software for payment</li>
<li>Ensure all appropriate approvals have been received for disbursements</li>
<li>Ensure all corporate American Express statements are received on time for prompt disbursement</li>
<li>Correspond with vendors to resolve invoice discrepancies
</li>
<li>Maintain orderly vendor files of all paid invoices</li>
<li>Assist all departments as to invoice status</li>
<li>Open relevant mail and distribute daily to proper department</li>
<li>Process all incoming payments of credit cards and maintain a settlement report for each batch and submit to receivables for processing</li>
<li>Communicate with vendors and respond to all inquiries and statements in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Communicate with co-workers, management, clients, and others in a courteous and professional manner</li>
</ul>
