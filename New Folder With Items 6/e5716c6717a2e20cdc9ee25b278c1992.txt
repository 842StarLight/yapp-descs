<h2>Financial Control Processor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The Financial Control Processor is responsible for the processing of earnings adjustments, check requests and manages daily reconciliations for NRT. The processor handles and resolves transaction exceptions that cause a variance across the Local Operating Companies (LOCs). The processor will also be expected to support the Central Processing team through consistent response times and prompt execution on all requests.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:<br>
<br>
Processing:<ul>
<li>
Complete daily sales reconciliation for multiple LOCs. Identify any variances and take prompt corrective action.</li>
<li>
Review daily Lockbox exceptions and error file. Correct issues quickly and notify any effected parties.</li>
<li>
Review check requests and enter accurately into internal systems for agent/vendor payment.</li>
<li>
Receive notification of payment issues (ie. Direct Deposit kickback) and notify affected parties promptly.
</li>
<li>Review and process miscellaneous requests from central processing.</li>
<li>
Identify any ongoing issue trends and bring possible solutions to management.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Support for the Central Processing Staff:<ul>
<li>
Provide feedback to management regarding LOC specific issues.</li>
<li>
Provide feedback to the Commission/Audit Supervision regarding incorrect deal submissions and work to resolve.</li>
<li>
Provide prompt response times to all e-mail and phone call inquiries.</li>
<li>
Be able to calculate and explain commission payouts.</li>
<li>
Assist team members in processing of transactions by daily deadlines as needed.</li>
<li>
Provide outstanding customer service including a positive attitude and constant willingness to assist both branch office staff and team members.</li>
<li>
Assist in yearly 1099 process. Resolve 1099 inquiries from agents, branch office staff, and central processing staff.<br>
</li>
</ul>
