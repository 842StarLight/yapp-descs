<h2>Team Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Tempur-Pedic© is now hiring Team Leaders! This is a great opportunity to join an experienced and passionate team of people selling The most highly recommended bed in America™! We are looking for career driven people who will be the face of our premium brand and offer superior service.</p>
<p>
*Great benefits *Competitive wages with bonus potential *Flexible schedule*</p>
<p>
As a Team Leader you will be responsible for assisting the management and leadership of one of our retail stores. This will include driving sales performance as well as ensuring standards are met across all areas of the store, from the customer experience, maintaining operational standards, to supporting the necessary elements of building and developing our retail concept.</p>
<p>
Essential Role Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Support store leadership in overall store leadership and supervision</li>
<li>Serve as an example in providing premium customer experience and build relationships with our consumers.</li>
<li>Support the store leadership in maintaining high standards of visual merchandising and brand presentation</li>
<li>In the absence of the Store Manager, provides the leadership necessary to ensure the store consistently operates to Tempur-Pedic standards</li>
<li>Foster and support a collaborative and open environment across all areas of the company</li>
</ul>
