Vortex® is seeking a detail-oriented individual who enjoys creating order out of chaos. This role will help manage the entire lifecycle of creative and marketing assets as well as develop effective strategies for organizing, sharing, searching, and archiving assets.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Create and enforce naming taxonomies, metadata schema, and asset file standards.</li>
<li>Bring existing assets up to appropriate standards, including reorganization of file directories within the server and metadata cleanup projects.</li>
<li>Provide quality control for all metadata to assure accuracy and consistency business wide.</li>
<li>Communicate to customers when assets are available, downloaded assets have been versioned, and as assets are expired.</li>
<li>Improve internal and external user experience by assisting in the regular evaluation of metadata model for adjustments and additions as business evolves.</li>
<li>Maintain an updated selection of the assets that is easily accessible and curated into collections upon request.</li>
<li>Intake and catalog large volumes of creative assets from influencers and photographers.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Experience and Skills Needed
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree and 2+ years relevant experience (or an equivalent combination) pertaining to data migration, content storage systems, or asset management.</li>
<li>Proven ability to maximize taxonomy and metadata standards to assist in the enhancement of search functionality.</li>
<li>Direct experience using digital asset management software.</li>
<li>Basic working skills in Microsoft and Adobe programs. Understanding of graphic file formats and able to create appropriate versions (high vs low resolution, CMYK vs RGB, .jpg, .psd, .pdf, etc.).</li>
<li>Additional coursework in Library Science or cataloging is a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to process raw imagery and retouch photos a plus.</li>
<li>Must be comfortable around dogs and guns.</li>
</ul>
