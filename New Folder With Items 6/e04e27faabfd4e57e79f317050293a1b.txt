<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>The Sales and Leasing Consultant is responsible for showing and leasing apartments, duplexes, and homes to prospective residents.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Interview prospective residents while communicating effectively and professionally to ascertain their needs and persuade them to view the rental property.</li>
<li>Use effective communication skills including in person communication, phone, email, and professional written communication.</li>
<li>Shows the apartment and community while using good sales techniques to discuss the layout of rooms, available facilities; such as club house, workout room and equipment, swimming pool and spas, community grounds, location of shopping centers and services in the area, and discusses the terms of the lease.</li>
<li>Effectively close and ask for the sale from the prospect.</li>
<li>Completes lease application and agreements with the prospective resident and collects any rental deposit and/or application fee.</li>
<li>Maintains the listings of the available and unavailable rental properties and communicates this to the appropriate party.</li>
<li>Inspects condition of the rental locations and premises and coordinates maintenance or cleaning services.</li>
<li>Communicates effectively and professionally with all residents and coworkers while working in a team environment.</li>
<li>Effectively communicates with third party locators and corporate housing specialists to drive traffic to the community.</li>
<li>Maintains strong market knowledge to offer insight into pricing, concessions, marketing collateral and online advertising.</li>
<li>Assists the Assistant Resident Manager, and Property Manager in daily operations.</li>
<li>Absorbs and complies with laws/policies regarding Federal, State and Municipal Fair Housing Laws, The Tenant Landlord Act, and the Federal and State Safety Regulations.</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED); or one to three years of related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Windows Operating Systems, Microsoft Office and Chrome</li>
</ul>

</ul>
