<br>
<p></p>
<p>Are you a passionate and driven sales executive in the early years of your career and want to accelerate your quota carrying sales skills and experiences? Would you thrive in a customer-facing, fast paced, technology space for one of the world’s most valuable brands?</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The SAP Sales Academy is a world-class training program that develops our next generation sales force by delivering an energizing and inspiring experience within a globally diverse environment. This nine-month program facilitates multi-dimensional, experiential learning with a focus on: digital transformation, global intelligence, human skills, business and technology acumen, solution/industry knowledge, and strategy/tools/process.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>During the program, you will receive onboarding in your local market, travel to California for two 6-week segments of classroom learning alongside your global peers in our world-class training center, then apply this learning in your local market while supporting your assigned sales mentor in the field. Upon successful program completion, you will move into a direct customer-facing sales role in your market and continue to receive mentoring and coaching support to accelerate your growth.</p>
<p>Join us for the opportunity of a lifetime, to develop a global peer network, partner and engage with customers to help solve their unique business challenges, and leveraging world-class cloud-based products and services, all while earning a great salary and benefits.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND WORK EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Successful completion of a Bachelor or Master’s degree in Business Administration or related subject</li>
<li>Fluent in English and local language, written and spoken</li>
<li>Maximum of 3 years’ work experience in a sales environment preferably in a technology company or in a role with significant exposure to software/technology solutions.</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of business processes and/or industries (such as Manufacturing, Consumer Products, Professional Services, Retail etc.)</li>
<li>Proven record of customer facing sales success and direct quota-carrying experience is strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
