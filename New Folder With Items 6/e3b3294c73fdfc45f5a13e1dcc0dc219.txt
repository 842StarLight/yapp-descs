<p>
<b>The company: </b>Roscoe Properties is a forward thinking full-service property management company with the best leaders in the industry.</p>
<p>
<b>The position: </b>We’re looking for a Regional Manager in Austin, Texas.</p>
<p>
The Regional Manager will assist in the development and monitoring of company policies and procedures related to property management; recruit, train, develop and supervise Community Managers, and share responsibility for maintaining the physical asset and performance of assigned properties. This position also includes assisting VP and other supervisors with special projects as needed.</p>
<p>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Train, evaluate and supervise all on-site staff</li>
<li>Monitor compliance to all Roscoe Properties policies and procedures, laws and ordinances, and safety procedures</li>
<li>Improve the overall financial performance of all properties within the portfolio by increasing revenues and controlling expenses</li>
<li>Responsible for all day to day property operations and maximize resident retention</li>
<li>Develop and implement marketing plans catered to the needs of each Community</li>
<li>Promptly inform Vice President or Regional Manager of resident issues involving safety, loss of income or damage to property</li>
<li>Communicate effectively and on a regular basis with Senior Management and our Clients or Owners to provide updates on Community operations and issues</li>
<li>Optimize the use of personnel resources by hiring, training, empowering and motivating on-site Community Managers and team members</li>
<li>Prepare and monitor annual budgets, goals and objectives</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4 years Property Management experience, 2 years in a Regional or multi-site manager role</li>
<li>3rd party management experience</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications</li>
<li>A valid Driver’s License is required</li>
<li>Experience with Yardi Software a plus</li>
<li>Strong work ethic</li>
<li>Works well with a team</li>
</ul>
