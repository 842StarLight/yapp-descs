<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>Successful candidate must: be at least 21 years of age; possess a current driver’s license; maintain current auto insurance coverage; possess excellent verbal communication, written communication, listening and negotiating skills; have the ability to work in a fast-paced environment and be a team player who can work well with minimal supervision; and demonstrate the ability to multi-task and respond quickly to demanding situations. Candidate must be a self-starter, highly motivated, and possess the ability to manage time effectively and service customer accounts.</p>
<p>A qualified candidate will be required to take a creative approach to sales and the ability to plan and execute sales strategies both independently and as part of our team. This position requires the availability to work flexible hours and weekends, when necessary, for special events.</p>
<p>This will be considered a full-time, salaried position with a commission incentive program. The position assumes an average of 45 hours per week on an annual basis.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES</b></p>
<p>Include the following but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Developing and maintaining positive relationships with clients.</li>
<li>Acquire and demonstrate wine knowledge and effectively present Chankaska products and manage growth of accounts and expansion of business to meet goals</li>
<li>Expand customer base through cold calling and strategic/ targeted efforts</li>
<li>Collection of customer payments, when necessary.</li>
<li>Delivering products to customers when required</li>
<li>Supporting clients at promotional events including evenings and weekends</li>
<li>Ensuring that sales goals are met</li>
<li>Demonstrate integrity and professionalism in all actions, adhering to the values of the company.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct distribution sales to liquor stores and restaurants in an assigned territory maintaining 100+ accounts in such a way that an appropriate sales volume will be achieved.</li>
<li>Gain knowledge of competitive sales trends and pricing in order to maintain specific pricing structures on all company products.</li>
<li>Know the sales trends in each account, understand the business strategies and practices of each decision maker.</li>
<li>Demonstrate entrepreneurial spirit for seeking out new accounts.</li>
<li>Listen carefully to needs and preferences of retail decision makers.</li>
<li>Overcome objections raised during a sales presentation.</li>
<li>Effectively handle special in-store/ restaurant problems and reporting that might occur from time to time.</li>
<li>Project a professional appearance while being resourceful, friendly and persistent with retail decision makers.</li>
<li>Maintain a cooperative attitude with management and provides reports that are accurate and on time.</li>
<li>Practice safe work and driving habits and, if accountable for others, ensure their understanding and compliance of such habits.</li>
</ul>
<p>This job description reflects management’s assignments of essential functions; it does not prescribe or restrict the tasks that may be assigned.</p>
