<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview

<div><div><div>
<div>Come be part of an award-winning company! We are looking for enthusiastic, customer-service focused individuals to be a part of our award-winning team. So if you're looking for a rewarding career opportunity with the top property management organization in the country, we'd like to hear from you!</div>
<div></div>
<div>

We are looking for the right candidate to join our operations team.You will have the option to sit in either our Denver, CO or Bellevue, WA office. This position supports our communities in Washington and Colorado. This role will be accountable for assisting our property operations team sustain and maximize technology. This will support AvalonBay’s goals to achieve more efficient on-site operations, enhanced accountability, improved community performance and associate satisfaction and retention.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The Role
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Specific responsibilities are as follows:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain subject matter expert knowledge for all software system applications.</li>
<li>
Provide one on one on-site software system training.</li>
<li>
Analyze and monitor reporting for all systems to pro-actively ensure standard operating practices are being met.</li>
<li>
Assist with implementation of new technology initiatives.</li>
<li>
Provide support on policy and procedures within region.</li>
<li>
Assist with the maintenance of systems as needed.</li>
<li>
Provide set-up and training for newly acquired properties.</li>
</ul>
