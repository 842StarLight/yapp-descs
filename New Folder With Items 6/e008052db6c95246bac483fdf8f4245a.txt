<h2>Sourcing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Can you find information on the Internet that no one else can locate? Are you just as comfortable navigating the Internet as you are the streets of your neighborhood? You may be the perfect candidate for our Sourcing Specialist position.</p>
<p>We need an enthusiastic, motivated individual to fill sourcing specialist role in our Springfield, IL office. you will use a variety of sources to find technical, sales and management talent that are a great fit for one of our positions at LRS.</p>
<p>You will support our full-life cycle corporate recruiters source for the positions that are high-priority for them; and you’ll be expected to work independently to find passive candidates that might just be the purple squirrel we are seeking for our harder to fill positions. You will also support other LRS companies such as Central Illinois Security and Diversified Yacht Services with their recruiting needs. And you’ll reach out to different groups and organizations to educate them about LRS’ opportunities and track our diversity hiring efforts. You’ll need to be able to set priorities and work on multiple tasks at a time.
</p>
<p>You will be working for the Corporate Recruiting team at LRS, a global information technology company with a well-earned reputation for hiring only the best available talent. Talent like yours.</p>
<p>This is a full-time, salaried position with full benefits. Since candidates cannot always talk during business hours, you will need to accommodate occasional appointments in the evenings and on the weekends.</p>
<p>As Sourcing Specialist at LRS your responsibilities will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Learning and understanding recruiting search strategies and techniques.</li>
<li>Learning and utilizing advanced sourcing strategies.</li>
<li>Mining resumes from multiple resume banks (Monster, LinkedIn etc.).</li>
<li>Sourcing potential passive candidates through internet searches, direct sourcing, and social media websites.</li>
<li>Navigating and utilizing an advanced applicant tracking system for candidate maintenance and uploading candidate resumes.</li>
<li>Pre-screening potential candidates for current openings.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Applicant Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 1 year of sales experience or 1 year of recruiting/sourcing experience.</li>
<li>You must have permanent authorization to work in the USA for any employer. No visa sponsorships are available.</li>
<li>Must be able to demonstrate the ability to navigate effectively through the internet to conduct online research.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and complete multiple tasks simultaneously.</li>
<li>Possesses outstanding organizational and multi-tasking skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work well independently.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Stronger Candidates will have the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience working with resume databases and internet sourcing tools.</li>
<li>Experience working with applicant tracking systems and related software applications.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Organization Structure and Interfaces:</b></p>
<p>You will be part of the 5-person corporate recruiting team and you will report to the Director, Marketing &amp; Corporate Recruiting who reports to the SRVP of Corporate Administration. You will interact regularly with LRS’ corporate and consulting recruiters.</p>
