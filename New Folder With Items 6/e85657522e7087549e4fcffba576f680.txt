<h2>Senior Application Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></h3>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary/Objective</b></h2>
<p>An <b>Applications Engineer II</b> uses their knowledge of custom automation and precision machining alongside a team of sales professionals, managers, designers and builders to develop an accurate concept and budget that outlines key concept points for automation systems. These machines are uniquely designed assembly, inspection, and test systems for industries that demand precision assembly for their components. Some of the industries we serve include automotive, aerospace, defense, medical equipment, pharmaceutical and industrial component manufacturers.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Duties</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>An <b>Applications Engineer II</b> will work with customers, sales, project management, and team partners to develop an accurate concept, proposal, and budget for custom automation assembly systems
<ul>
<li>Typical custom automation projects range in size from $500,000 to 10 million dollars</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Establish and communicate customers’ goals and objectives, including but not limited to:
<ul>
<li>Functional specification, cycle time, schedule, budget, resources, required process, and part tolerances</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Review requests from the customer or salesperson:
<ul>
<li>Facilitate concept and quote review meetings with the support of the Level III Applications Engineer and/management</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Develop preliminary projects schedules independently</li>
<li>Prepare/develop a concept supported by a budget that meets the customer’s specifications
<ul>
<li>Prepare Risk Assessments</li>
<li>Produce CAD drawings, as required using Solidworks</li>
<li>Produce concept simulations, as required using Visual Components software</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Prepare project proposals and presentations:
<ul>
<li>Communicate proposal information to customers, managers, sales, project management, and internal project team members independently</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Maintain constant contact with the sales team</li>
<li>Maintain AE related documentation in company record systems</li>
<li>Provide design assistance to the project team throughout the design phase of the project, attend internal design &amp; brainstorming review meetings</li>
<li>Traveling independently will be required (national and international)
<ul>
<li>10-15% per year estimated travel</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
