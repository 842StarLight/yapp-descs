<h2>Sales Assistant to Top Financial Advisor (Bilingual/Chinese)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>
The Sales Assistant position provides direct administrative support to the Managing Director through partnering with the appropriate enterprise department. The Sales Assistant is responsible for processing new business applications and manage new and existing client relations in a timely and efficient manner.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Filter all Managing Director's incoming correspondence/phone calls/emails</li>
<li>
Organize Managing Director's calendar and coordinate client appointments, firm meetings, events, and contest deadlines</li>
<li>
Review all applications for accuracy and completeness in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Perform application data entry</li>
<li>
Order relevant sales materials and promotional items for seminars and event planning</li>
<li>
Daily follow up on pending business</li>
<li>
Ensure timely follow-up throughout the New Business processing cycle, from submission to reporting of the policy</li>
<li>
Manage both new and existing client relationships to ensure customer service satisfaction</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge/Skills/Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Understanding of the Firm's functions, workflows and operations</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively communicate via verbal and written communications at all levels of the organization</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal relationship skills and the ability to work effectively with attention to detail</li>
<li>
Customer focused and business oriented</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Associate's degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>
Proficient use of computers</li>
<li>
Good interpersonal communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong organizational abilities</li>
<li>
Detail oriented</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Chinese (Mandarin or Cantonese)</li>
</ul>
