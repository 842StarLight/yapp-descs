<h2>Editorial Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Prime Publishing LLC is seeking Editorial Interns for our content sites in the areas of crafting (general, knitting, beading, quilting, crochet, sewing, jewelry) and/or cooking (general recipes, healthy recipes, dessert recipes, slow cooker recipes). Each intern will be mentored by a full-time Editor. We are a fast-growing company creating quality content and engaging consumers.</p>
<p>
The internship is set up as a hands-on 12-week learning experience. This internship is designed to accommodate the intern’s academic commitments by corresponding to the academic calendar with flexible start dates and/or schedules. Interns will learn about digital publishing, including online writing, social media, blogger outreach, analytics, and content publishing. Our goal is to not only teach skills but also provide opportunities to work on real projects.</p>
<p>
Interns will work on our Site Content team and also learn about the following areas:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Site Content Editor – </b>Monitor, create, and update content for the websites to increase site traffic monthly.</li>
<li>
<b>Newsletter Editor – </b>Create and analyze newsletter content in order to maximize site traffic, newsletter performance, consumer engagement, and advertising opportunities.</li>
<li>
<b>Community Editor – </b>Monitor and create content across social media networks and blogs in order to reach a wider audience while implementing strategic goals.</li>
<li>
<b>Promotions Editor – </b>Secure product(s) for site reviews and giveaways in order to increase traffic, engage consumers, and network with companies and publishers while working closely with sales for new revenue streams.</li>
</ul>
<p>The company will determine where an intern will be placed based upon intern qualification and company needs.</p>
<p>
Should there be mutual interest, we will share with you specifics of the internship program and the responsibilities entailed during an in-person interview.</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b><br>
– College student, preferably with English or journalism concentration or equivalent experience.<br>
– Basic understanding of HTML and online media.<br>
– Strong writing, editing, research, communication, and management skills.<br>
– Detail-oriented with the ability to meet deadlines effectively and multitask.<br>
– Interest in and/or experience with women’s lifestyle content.<br>
– Winter, spring, summer and fall programs available.<br>
– For summer interns, we prefer current juniors who will be entering their senior year in college.<br>
– Graduate students who will be in school at least one more semester/quarter are also welcome to apply.<br>
– Coordination with college or university for credit is available as determined by college or university.</p>
<p><b>
To Apply:</b></p>
<p>
Northbrook, IL location. Minutes from I-94 (Edens) and I-294 (Tollway). All positions are based in our office; no telecommuting options are available</p>
<p>
Interns typically work in our office, 8:30AM- 5:30PM (with one hour for lunch). Flex time and half-day Friday summer hour programs available. We prefer interns who can work 5 days per week (full time). Candidates who prefer to work 3 or 4 days per week may also apply and indicate days available.</p>
<p>
Coordination with college or university for credit is required as determined by college or university. ONLY apply if this will give you college credit or qualify as a work study/independent learning type program. Only interns who can provide college or university letter or documentation will be interviewed.</p>
<p>
NOTE: Only current students who can demonstrate that our internship program will provide approved integrated coursework or the receipt of academic credit are eligible for our internship program. All applicants will be required to submit proof of their eligibility and college/university approval in order to be considered for an interview. In addition to approved integrated coursework or the receipt of academic credit, interns will receive a $1,000 stipend to cover expenses.</p>
<p>
To apply for this position, please send a resume, cover letter, and writing samples to career.prime@primecp.com. We prefer an in-person interview, so please indicate first dates available for an in-person interview (i.e. upcoming winter break, spring break, after finals end, etc.). Interview by Skype is also an option.</p>
<p>
If you have special needs and require assistance please email us at career.prime@primecp.com or call us at (847)-205-9375.</p>

Pre-Employment background check required. EOE/M/F/D/V</p>
