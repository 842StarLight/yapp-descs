<p>
Associate Project Managers will work with our highly talented and experienced VMware Professional Services (PS) Team to deliver projects to our customers. In this role, you will be responsible for leading PS projects that are of low to medium complexity, from initiation to close out. The Associate Project Manager will adhere to the VMware Project Management Organization (PMO) methodology and deliver these projects to mutually agreed upon scope, schedule and budget. You will have the opportunity to work directly with the client and other team members on the day-to-day tasks and act as the primary interface to our customer (internal and external) to manage the full life cycle of projects or programs. We're looking for people who love to service our customers, have strong business ethics and great communication skills. VMware does not sponsor immigration for this role.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the execution of the project plan and the detailed Statement of Work (SOW) developed for the customer.</li>
<li>
Manage the goals and scope of a project to ensure VMware’s commitments to the customer are achieved.</li>
<li>
Prepare and maintain clear and concise activity/progress/time recording reports in accordance with VMware’s standards and submits to customers, management and Sales.</li>
<li>
Manage the implementation and daily activities, which are coordinated with VMware and/or partner resources.</li>
<li>
Communicate project budget and resource allocation to sales, customers and delivery personnel.</li>
<li>
Ensure projects are delivered on time, within budget and to the satisfaction of the customer and account team.</li>
<li>
Communicate to all stakeholders and concerned parties any existing or potential customer escalation issues</li>
<li>
Create and manage the VMware Project Change process as needed.</li>
<li>
Stay informed of current industry trends and new technology through professional associations, continued professional development, VMware required quarterly training, trade journals, networking, and associated training and seminars.</li>
<li>
Articulate and evangelize the value of the VMware Solutions and Services business benefit to the customer.</li>
<li>
Provide a single point of contact during the project lifecycle for all aspects of the project.</li>
<li>
Maintain financial management and the processes for following and executing of revenue recognition rules and policies.</li>
<li>
Manage and validate weekly revenue forecast.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as required.</li>
</ul>
