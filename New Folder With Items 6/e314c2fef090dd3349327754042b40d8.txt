</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Understanding the digital space and preparing for what’s next is at our core. We help clients reimagine how profits are made, how relationships are created and managed; we reshape how work gets done, and rewire the competitive fabric of entire industries.<br>

<div><b>Work you’ll do</b></div>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>As a Manager , you will bring considerable technical, functional, and product expertise to deliver effective solutions to clients.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage day to day interactions with executive clients and sponsors<br>
</li>
<li>Manage and deliver components of client engagements that identify, design, and implement technology and creative business solutions for large companies.<br>
</li>
<li>Responsibilities include, among others, managing teams in the identification of business requirements, functional design, process design (including scenario design, flow mapping), prototyping, testing, training, defining support procedures.<br>
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain contact with top decision makers at key clients; participate and lead aspects of the proposal development process; contribute to the development of proposal pricing strategies<br>
</li>
</ul>

<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Required</div>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<br>
<li>Minimum 8 years relevant work experience in advertising sales, marketing, consulting, and/or media<br>
</li>
<li>years of relevant technology consulting or industry experience<br>
</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of AEM/Adobe CQ/Digital Marketing Suite<br>
</li>
<li>Experience designing modules and components<br>
</li>
<li>Experience in end to end implementations of AEM<br>
</li>
<li>Experience leading large sized teams and technology engagements, including client and consulting staff<br>
</li>
<li>Experience conceptualizing, designing, building, and deploying Enterprise Content Management solutions<br>
</li>
<li>Willingness to travel 80 – 100% of the time</li>
<li>
Limited immigration sponsorship may be available.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>Preferred</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors or advanced degree in Statistics, Economics, Business, Math, or Advertising is preferred, or equivalent work experience<br>
</li>
<li>Strong understanding of SDLC methodologies (Agile, SCRUM, RUP, other)<br>
</li>
<li>A strong desire to learn and passion for digital marketing, research and analytics<br>
</li>
<li>Strong ability to navigate ambiguity and manage competing priorities.<br>
</li>
<li>Excellent written, oral and presentation abilities-must be able to communicate complex ideas in both technical and user-friend language<br>
</li>
<li>Ability to foster collaborative relationships with other cross-functional teams<br>
</li>
