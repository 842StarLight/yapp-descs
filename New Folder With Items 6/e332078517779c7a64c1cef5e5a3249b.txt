<h2>Entry-Level Tax Profesional</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Job Description<br>
At Jackson Hewitt, we are working hard for the hardest working and we are on a mission to completely change the way our customers engage with their personal finances. Jackson Hewitt combines the fun, fast-paced culture of a startup with the scale and reputation of one of the nation’s largest retailers (over 6,000 locations).<br>
<br>
We have a passion for our employees and our customers. We recognize and appreciate that our team members are our single greatest competitive advantage. Being an industry leader requires a consistent focus on quality, innovation, performance and results.<br>
<br>
We’re always looking for top performers to add value &amp; make a difference in people’s lives.<br>
<br>
We offer flexible work schedules and pay based on performance.<br>
Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Presents the Company’s value proposition to clients concerning various company products and services and uses prescribed selling techniques.</li>
<li>Conducts a thorough in-person interview with potential clients, using the company’s propriety tax software application.</li>
<li>Delivers exceptional customer service by anticipating customer needs and considers the impact of all decisions/actions on the customer.</li>
<li>Answering client calls via our national call center routing system.</li>
<li>Completes all related tax forms in accordance with policies, and in compliance with legislation and regulations. Furnishes taxpayers with sufficient information and advice to ensure correct tax form completion.</li>
<li>Consults tax law reference materials to determine procedures for preparation of atypical returns.</li>
<li>Answers questions and provide future tax planning to clients.</li>
<li>Reviews financial records such as income statements and documentation of expenditures to determine forms needed to prepare tax returns.</li>
<li>Researches tax related questions and issues, and responds to clients appropriately and within a timely manner.</li>
<li>Resolves client complaints, or refers situations to supervisor (as appropriate) for resolution</li>
<li>Audits all tax return forms for accuracy and completeness (i.e., client signatures).</li>
<li>This position has access to and regularly works with information of a sensitive, highly confidential nature, and regularly deals with diverse matters which require a working knowledge of the business.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Required Qualifications<br>
<ul>
<li>Top candidates for this position will have retail experience providing exceptional client service and performing multiple tasks in a fast paced environment.</li>
<li>Hands-on marketing with surrounding businesses, including office visits to promote brand awareness and new clientele</li>
<li>High School Degree or equivalent</li>
<li>Experience preferably in sales, service and tax preparation</li>
<li>PTIN (Preparer Tax Identification Number) required</li>
<li>Good communication, interpersonal and customer services skills</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of computer functions and math required.</li>
<li>Ability to lift a maximum of 25 lbs</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure, in a fast-paced working environment</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Field Instructor, Remote Support and Call Center certified</li>
<li>1+ years of experience preferably in sales, service and or tax preparation</li>
<li>Remote Support or Call Center ready certified</li>
<li>Enrolled Agent (EA) certification</li>
</ul>
