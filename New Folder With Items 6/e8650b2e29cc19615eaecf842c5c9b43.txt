<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>Responsible for the operating/financial performance success of all assigned projects and achievement of program management team and organizational goals and objectives. The project manager will provide specific support to all organizational initiatives and ensure that all projects meet and/or exceed client expectations and adhere to established budgets.</p>
<p><b>Day-to-Day Responsibilities </b></p>
<ul>
<li>In-depth knowledge of skills specific to content and accuracy of reports and returns</li>
<li>Focus on meeting and/or exceeding client level of expectations</li>
<li>Working knowledge specific to time management processes and the capability to identify complex problems, evaluate and execute solutions</li>
<li>Exceptional level of knowledge specific to all software programs including but not limited to Microsoft Office, Made2Manage, Outlook and Smartsheet</li>
<li>Exceptional level of written, non-written and verbal communications</li>
<li>Comprehensive understanding of the Sales to final billing work flow and management control points</li>
<li>Comprehensive understanding of the current program and non-program pricing standards</li>
<li>Willingness to travel by air and ground. Some overnight travel may be required</li>
<li>Coordinate all activities specific to assigned projects as documented in the Atlas Sign Industries standard Business process in order to exceed customer expectations and achieve profitability goals</li>
<li>Support and actively participate with the Director of Program Management in executing strategic planning and tactical initiatives</li>
<li>Utilize and maintain current status reports and ensure the accuracy of sales and job order data input</li>
<li>Utilize standard billing package process and procedure to ensure timely and accurate submission of all closed projects</li>
<li>Coordinate and control specific activities/tasks with line of business partners.</li>
<li>Responsible for tracking and documenting the overall progress of all orders and to communicate status of those orders to clients, line of business partners and management by way of established programs and reports</li>
<li>Coordinate with Production, Service/Installation and Field Service partners the delivery and installation of final product</li>
<li>Coordinate and maintain professional liaisons with clients, line of business 3rd partners and local government representatives</li>
<li>Actively participate with all line of business partners in the identification and resolution of all project issues.</li>
<li>Utilize standard M2M reports including but not limited to Sales Report, On-Hold Report to ensure on time processes</li>
<li>Achieves and maintain a working knowledge of all business process functions and reports in order to maximize efficiencies</li>
<li>Provide backup support to Director and team program managers as needed</li>
<li>Provide assistance to Director of Program Management specific to other assigned tasks</li>
<li>Maintain professional and consistent communications between Sales, Permit, Design, Pre-Production,Product Development, Manufacturing, Customer Service, Field Service,Service/Installation, and Finance throughout the product production,installation and post completion process.</li>
</ul>
