<h2>Business Development Assistant Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Job Description</b></div>
<div>Business Development Assistant Administrator<b>:</b>
</div>
<div>The Assistant Administrator reports to the Assistant Manager and is responsible for coordinating and fulfilling the daily and weekly activities that occur within an assigned area.</div>
<div><b>Company Description:</b></div>
<div>NEX Operations Departments operate in a warehouse or a manufacturing environment and Associates are subject to moderate amounts of dust, dirt, and odors, primarily produced by packaging materials and the operation of propane-powered forklifts as well as noise primarily produced by production equipment. Temperatures during the year will vary. Frequently operate a computer, read, write and interact with others in person, over the phone and via other devices. They move about within the facility and may travel to other locations.</div>
<div>Business Development Assistant Administrator Functions<b>:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Review space, manpower requirements, process flows, and other information to understand methods and activities in manufacturing and services.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Identify, recommend and implement improvements in warehouse/ plant performance to optimize product volume, yields and quality in accordance with business needs.</li>
<li>Gather and evaluate appropriate data, utilizing statistical techniques, to improve process capability and/or identify root causes of problems, and implement improvement opportunities.</li>
<li>Develop and implement equipment, procedure, contracts and process changes including design and implementation of improvement projects.</li>
<li>Provide technical advice and support to resolve unusual problems using root cause analysis.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Work with department managers to develop warehouse/ plant-wide Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) for process and equipment operation quoted by Customer Service.</li>
<li>Confer with management and other staff regarding process capabilities and other considerations to facilitate processes</li>
<li>Provide technical assistance to the warehouse/ plant in relation to process parameters and standard manufacturing methods, work instructions and processing</li>
<li>Assist in the design, procurement, and installation of new equipment.</li>
<li>Ensure all process changes are implemented and in line with company vision</li>
<li>Support and implement new product and process runs and prepare relevant documentation.</li>
<li>Work with Quality personnel on various projects.</li>
<li>Communicate regularly with management to facilitate continuous improvement and ongoing organizational learning.</li>
<li>Research new equipment and new manufacturing processes.</li>
<li>Work with ISO, 5S and Management personnel on ensuring that all processes follow relevant guidelines</li>
<li>Other duties as determined by management.</li>
<li>Ability to work flexible hours and or shifts to accommodate business needs.</li>
</ul>
