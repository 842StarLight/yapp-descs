<p><b>POSITION OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>
Provide comprehensive technical support on Telit’s Cellular IoT module products to customers during pre-sale, design and development, certification, deployment, and post-sale activities. Work closely with customers’ design teams, as well as their OEM end-customers. You will become a technical expert on Telit’s product portfolio of Cellular IoT modules.</p>
<p><b>
BACKGROUND &amp; EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>
The ideal candidate has worked in the wireless industry with Cellular/m2m/IoT experience.<br>
</p>

<ul>
<li>Cellular 4G LTE technologies</li>

<li>USB, RS232, SPI, I2C, and AT command interfaces</li>
<li>Driver setup and configuration</li>
<li>Linux operating system</li>
<li>Embedded systems, firmware, development tools, microcontrollers, antenna design and power management.</li>
<li>Logging and analysis tools (Qualcomm QXDM, Linux logging tools, Wireshark, etc.)</li>
<li>Software development/debugging in C/C++</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Requirements:</b></p>

<li>Cellular carrier certification experience is highly desired</li>

<li>Available to travel up to 15%</li>
