 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div>
<p>The Veeam Global Demand Generation team is hiring an experienced campaign manager to build and manage marketing programs targeted at the Technical Decision Maker (TDM) buyers to drive demand for Veeam’s business. These programs are comprised of a set of tactics including digital media, SEO, social media, email marketing, regional activities and events that take a buyer through a journey from educating to converting into business.</p>
<p>
In this role, you will work with internal and external cross-functional teams to build campaign assets, execute programs, and measure and report on campaign effectiveness.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop go-to-market campaigns and collaborate with stakeholders across many different functions</li>
<li>
Design end-to-end campaigns in conjunction with various marketing functions including Product Marketing, PR, digital, corporate communications, as well as maintain alignment with sales team</li>
<li>
Understand the buyer – pain points, demographics, and how they buy</li>
<li>
Develop program content and tactic roadmap based on the buyer’s journey and best practices</li>
<li>
Write, edit and proofread copy for promotional materials within marketing program</li>
<li>
Brief and manage the work of internal and external execution teams, including creative, third party writers, digital advertising, SEO, social media, regional activities and events to build out and execute programs</li>
<li>
Determine and manage program budgets</li>
<li>
Analyze data to monitor the success of programs and optimize activities based on data-driven findings</li>
<li>
Align to the needs of regional marketing and field teams at a global level without creating conflicts with other regional initiatives</li>
<li>
Work with appropriate partners, including field marketing and alliance partners, in campaign execution and response management</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
