<p><b>National Account Executive</b></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This position is responsible for managing the sales relationships of large, national, multi-ship point customers</li>
<li>Meet the sales targets</li>
<li>Coordinate with pricing, operations, and customer service teams to ensure National Account customers demand and needs are met successfully within the company operating model</li>
<li>Devise strategies to overcome obstacles and strong problem solving skills</li>
<li>Partner with customers to find and develop new opportunities for all service offerings</li>
<li>Expand National Account growth through all services (Truckload, Brokerage, Plus Power, Dedicated, Intermodal, and Mexico)</li>
<li>Maintain and grow existing National Account customer accounts and also adding new National Account customers</li>
<li>Conduct QBRs with all National Account customers and develop strong business relationships with all levels of their management team</li>
<li>Travels as necessary to meet company and customer needs, requiring overnight stays</li>
<li>Delivers exceptional service through positive engagement and patience</li>
<li>Work in a team environment to continuously improve results</li>
<li>Promote a culture of integrity, trust and safety</li>
<li>Provide assistance and expert advice upon request of Business Units, for example with complex EAR, ITAR, and HTS classifications, license applications, special trade programs</li>
<li>Train US employees on customs, ITAR, EAR, FTR, OFAC, AML and FCPA regulations</li>
<li>Assist and guide the BU’s to understand where US trade regulations might apply outside the US</li>
<li>Highly engaged, hands on and result oriented</li>
<li>Travel domestically as well as internationally, as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or combination of education and experience</li>
<li>Prior National Account management</li>
<li>5 – 10 years proven ability in high level transportation sales, including: Asset based Truckload, and value added sales such as brokerage, dedicated, and intermodal.</li>
<li>Experience securing and working with Fortune 1000 companies</li>
<li>Astute negotiation skills and ability to build strong business relationships with all levels, including C-level executives.</li>
<li>Strong verbal, written skills and analytical skills</li>
</ul>
