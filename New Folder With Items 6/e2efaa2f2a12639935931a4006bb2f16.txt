<h2>Paid Advertising Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Sprinklr is looking for a highly experienced and well-trained professional in Social Media Advertising to provide strategic expertise and empower our new and existing clientele to use our technology to meet their goals. This role will help to meet our expanding need for experts who can ensure that our internal teams, customers, and prospects have access to expert knowledge of the advertising industry and Sprinklr’s Advertising module.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate for this position will be a mix of technical expert, able to discuss product feature implementation in detail; advertising and media thought-leader, able to discuss ad strategy with the biggest brands and agencies in the world; an effective product trainer, able to effortlessly teach different levels of users on the Sprinklr platform; and a top-tier evangelist, able to captivate audiences by telling the Sprinklr story.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Main Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide expert advertising consultancy to Sprinklr prospects, including training, strategic discussion, and support</li>
<li>
Create, develop, and maintain advertising training and knowledge enablement resources</li>
<li>
Provide support, coaching, and training to Sprinklr staff on Sprinklr’s Advertising module, from the very technical to telling the Sprinklr story</li>
<li>
Work directly with the Solutions Consultant team to empower and support team efforts – you are the expert’s expert</li>
<li>
Work with Sales team to showcase Sprinklr’s Ads module to prospects and existing clients</li>
<li>
Develop and execute revenue driving initiatives at scale</li>
<li>
Contribute to development of Advertising products and processes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Desired Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of experience with paid social media, including operations and strategy roles with a platform (e.g. Facebook or Twitter), agency or major social media advertiser</li>
<li>
Demonstrate experience and a passion for the paid social media technology universe</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong technical background with advanced computer skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and as a member of a team</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 50% of the time</li>
<li>
Extremely strong communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively under tight deadlines and juggle several assignments simultaneously</li>
<li>
Passion for solving client challenges and commitment to client delight</li>
<li>
College first degree</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Technical Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of social advertising platforms, including but not limited to Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn, Snapchat, Pinterest, and Yahoo Gemini, including both native publishing tools (e.g. Power Editor) and third party paid media publishing tools</li>
<li>
Ad tracking technologies e.g. DCM or similar</li>
<li>
Web analytics e.g. Omniture or similar</li>
<li>
Knowledge of web usability best practices, website navigation design and flow, content classification, and content management methods and systems</li>
</ul>
