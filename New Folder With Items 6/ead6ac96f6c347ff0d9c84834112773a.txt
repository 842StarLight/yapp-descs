<h2>Field Service Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Field Service Manager</b><br>
<br>
PSI Water Technologies, Inc. (“PSI”), a UGSI Solutions, Inc. company, is a water industry leader in water disinfection and mixing process technologies. UGSI Solutions, Inc. is one of the most dynamic and fastest growing companies in the water infrastructure market with a portfolio of proprietary and patented technologies targeting water and wastewater infrastructure improvement.<br>
<br>
This position will be responsible for resource planning, on-site equipment start-up, customer training, customer satisfaction, cost control, internal technical and product training, and sales of parts and services. This role requires a balance between technical and managerial business skills. The successful candidate will be a self-starter with customer-centric personality, strong organization and communication skills, and MS Office proficiency. This position can be based anywhere in the continental United States and will report directly to PSI’s Vice President &amp; General Manager.<br>
<br>
Essential Job Functions:</div>
<ul>
<li>Manage the day-to-day activities of field service technicians to ensure high quality of project management execution;</li>
<li>Recruit and train field service technicians;</li>
<li>Provide field service technicians with regular performance feedback, regular performance appraisals, and on an as-required basis, performance improvement initiatives and disciplinary action;</li>
<li>Provide leadership and training on environmental, health and safety issues related to the performance of services provided at customer sites and in PSI facilities;</li>
<li>Schedule and oversee all aspects of field service, including start-up, customer training, warranty and other service visits;</li>
<li>Work with internal personnel, customers/agents and construction companies to ensure successful service support and visits;</li>
<li>Compliance with PSI’s safety and personal protective equipment policies since customer sites may allow exposure to dangerous or hazardous conditions;</li>
<li>Review and manage customer requirements: constraints of cost, time, scope and quality;</li>
<li>Provide customer satisfaction and reporting structures;</li>
<li>Modify and/or create processes to improve customer satisfaction as it relates to the field service department;</li>
<li>Create metrics to measure effectiveness and customer satisfaction of the field service department; and</li>
<li>Ensure effective communications and relationships between customer(s) and field service personnel are maintained.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
Job Qualifications:</div>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in Word, Excel, MS Projects;</li>
<li>Ability to interpret construction drawings, including mechanical, process, and electrical;</li>
<li>Review and select materials and equipment that comply with component specification documents;</li>
<li>Two (2) or more years’ experience managing field service personnel;</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in construction management, chemical or mechanical engineering, or closely related field from an accredited college or university; and</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 25% of the time to locations within the U.S.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
Physical Demands:</div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to sit for long periods of time;</li>
<li>Regularly required to stand, walk, sit, reach with hands and arms, climb or balance, stoop or kneel, talk and hear, and use fingers and hands to feel objects, tools or controls;</li>
<li>Must have manual dexterity to operate the equipment and tools required for this position;</li>
<li>Must be able to quickly respond (with the hand, finger or foot) to a signal (sound, light, picture) when it responds;</li>
<li>Ability to perform work under a variety of weather conditions;</li>
<li>Specific vision abilities include close vision and the ability to adjust focus;</li>
<li>Ability to look at computer monitors for much of the workday; and</li>
<li>Ability to occasionally lift and/or move up to 75 Lbs.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
Preferred Qualifications:</div>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with 2D AutoCAD and ERP Software;</li>
<li>Familiarity with PLC based platforms SCADA and industrial communication systems;</li>
<li>Experience in technical support for similar product lines in the municipal water and/or wastewater industries; and</li>
<li>Prior experience working in applications engineering, project engineering, or project management with other closely related technologies.</li>
</ul>
