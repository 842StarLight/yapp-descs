<h2>Vice President of Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>GENERAL POSITION SUMMARY</b> <b>:</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are in search of an experienced, enthusiastic, and engaging Vice President of Marketing to lead and motivate our internal teams and external partners. The VP Marketing will oversee the organization’s marketing strategies to help ensure the long-term relevancy of our branded experiences. In this role, you will focus on growing attendance and engagement by increasing awareness and igniting curiosity of our experiences. You will work with an engaged team to elevate the visitor experience by increasing the value, audience engagement, talk-ability, and social sharing of the experience. You will drive innovation and adaptability through market research, deep customer insights, and actionable data-driven dashboards. You will develop channel marketing strategies to increase affiliate and affinity groups and develop tourism channel marketing opportunities. You will help amplify our visitors’ experiences by increasing social engagement, curating and encouraging fan-curated content, and connecting with niche communities. You will work with a team to create signature events and programs to continue to build cultural relevancy within our market, develop opportunities with key travel partners, create opportunities to package Seattle experiences, and participate in greater destination marketing initiatives. You will help protect and project the status and reputation of Seattle’s premier tourist and cultural experiences to connect tourists to the Puget Sound and the best of the Northwest.<br>
</p>

</li></ul>
