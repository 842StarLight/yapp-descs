<br>
<p><b>
Leasing Consultant: </b>As a Leasing Consultant you would be responsible for handling all aspects of leasing apartment units in accordance with Company and Fair Housing guidelines. The Leasing Consultant position is responsible for all leasing activities including but not limited to: touring apartments with clients, lease and or pre-lease apartments, complete lease/renewal paperwork, explain lease and all paperwork to the client, operating property software systems, walk apartments and community and assist with resident relations. This position requires an individual who is sales and service driven, assertive with a professional image as well as having good organizational and polished communication skills. <b><i>The ability to close the sale is a must</i></b>.</p>
