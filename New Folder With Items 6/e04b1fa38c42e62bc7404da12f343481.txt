<p>Job Description Summary:
</p>We are looking for a talented, fashionable, edgy, high energy individual to be the first impression of a high-end showroom for a Luxury Brand located in Midtown Manhattan. This is a great opportunity for a creative person who likes to think outside the box, loves NYC, knows fashion, and understands luxury.
<p></p>
<p>Job Description:
</p>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES
</p>
<ul><li>Be the “first face” of the JENNAIR A&amp;D Flagship Showroom and set the tone for all trade, executive and consumer visits.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain high energy and greet and assist all guests upon arrival.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Data Intake for lead generation/contacts into Sales-Force.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Answer inquiries about facility services, events and directions in a professional, polite, and thoughtful manner.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create a positive and memorable experience for our guests by developing a rapport and guiding them through the brand experience center before passing off the experience to training manager.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain the upkeep and appearance of the showroom, notifying appropriate team members of service requirements.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Owning the calendar of events for the showroom. Showroom Trainings + Conference room.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in event reporting including compiling a monthly report that consists of consumer feedback, capturing photos, reviewing post-event survey responses and tracking key learnings.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead correspondence for all special or private event inquiries and generate event proposals.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Engage the trade, designers and influencers with in depth knowledge of the JENNAIR Brand as well as engage in developing collaborations with outside partners.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop relationships with designers and influencers to help drive the JENNAIR Brand conversion rate/ROI for the showroom.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS
</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
</li>
<li>Able to speak in front of large groups of people.
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Excellent organizational skills.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to stand, walk and move about for extended periods of time.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to use a computer and required software properly and efficiently.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>College Degree preferred (Hospitality/Business Management).
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of the Luxury space, I.E Fashion, Art, Interior Design.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experiential marketing or event planning/production experience preferred.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum 1-2 years hospitality or customer/guest service experience required.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be at least 21 years of age Minimum.</li></ul>
