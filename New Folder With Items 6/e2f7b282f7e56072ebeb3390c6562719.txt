<h2>ERP Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>As the go-to person for our Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems, you will assist users at all levels to navigate through the systems in order to achieve their business objectives. You will be a key member of the application support team, helping to design, develop, test, deploy and maintain data processing solutions to enhance the ERP systems.</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Job</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Provide in-person, telephone, or remote technical assistance to ERP system users.
</li>
<li>Answer questions and resolve problems through the IT ticketing system. Triage inbound support tickets.</li>
<li>
Identify problems, research answers, and guide users through corrective steps.</li>
<li>
Analyze and troubleshoot issues reported by end users.</li>
<li>
Create or update ERP system support and business process documentation.
</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you bring</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
A passion for applying technical solutions to business needs.</li>
<li>
The patience and enthusiasm to work with users across multiple departments at all skill levels. Excellent communication skills are a must.</li>
<li>
An understanding of business processes and ERP security subsystem.
</li>
<li>A High School diploma and one year of IT support experience, or an equivalent combination of education and experience.
</li>
<li>Proficiency in standard office software tools (Microsoft Office and Mac).</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize and work on multiple projects simultaneously with a high degree of accuracy.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What we offer</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
A collaborative teamwork environment where learning is constant and performance is rewarded.</li>
<li>
The opportunity to be part of the team whose technology is revolutionizing the treatment of some of the world's most devastating diseases.</li>
<li>
A generous benefits package that includes medical, dental, vision, and life insurance; a 401(k) match; and an Employee Stock Purchase Plan.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>Penumbra, Inc., headquartered in Alameda, California, is a global healthcare company focused on innovative therapies. Penumbra designs, develops, manufactures and markets innovative products and has a broad portfolio that addresses challenging medical conditions and significant clinical needs across two major markets, neuro and vascular. Penumbra sells its products to hospitals primarily through its direct sales organization in the United States, most of Europe, Canada and Australia, and through distributors in select international markets.
