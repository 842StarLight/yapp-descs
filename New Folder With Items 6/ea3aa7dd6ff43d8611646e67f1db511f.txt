<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Role and Responsibilities</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>
VMware’s Federal Team is expanding and looking for an experienced sales specialist for our NSX specialist sales team supporting the Federal Healthcare and Federal System Integrators customers</p>
<p></p>
<p>
VMware NSX is a network virtualization platform that delivers the operational model of a virtual machine for the network. Virtual networks reproduce the network model in software, allowing complex multi-tier network topologies to be created and provisioned programmatically in seconds. NSX includes a library of logical networking services – logical switches, logical routers, logical firewalls, logical load balancers, logical VPN, QoS, and distributed security. The NSX virtualization platform is helping hundreds of customers realize the full potential of a Software Defined Data Center. This is an opportunity to join an exciting and rapidly growing business within VMware.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Sales Specialist within the Network and Security Business Unit (NSBU) is responsible for a coordinated sales approach with VMware’s NSX internal and external sales team. This extended team drives toward a common goal within a set number of Federal and FSI accounts. The mission of this group is to broaden the reach of these accounts in order to ensure the VMware NSX foothold across the assigned regions. Our solution set is viewed as a ‘platform’ within an account. It changes the role that IT plays within that account from being considered a cost burden to a strategic deployment.</p>
<p></p>
