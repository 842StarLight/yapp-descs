<h2>Pricing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description:
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"></h3>
<p><b>Pentair currently has an opening for a Pricing Analyst to join our Finance team at our Cary, NC location. In this role, the Pricing Analyst will be responsible for activities related to discount management and pricing analytics. This Pricing Analyst will also assist in the forecasting and analysis to support cross-functional areas.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>We will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Create career growth and rotational opportunities within various areas of the organization
</b></li>
<li><b>Pay competitively
</b></li>
<li><b>Provide on the job training and mentoring in Pentair’s proven best practices
</b></li>
<li><b>Offer health benefits &amp; coaching
</b></li>
<li><b>Support your long term retirement goals through a competitive 401K matching and stock purchase plan
</b></li>
<li><b>Offer employee discounts
</b></li>
<li><b>Protect you with life insurance and other options
</b></li>
<li><b>Tell you more about all the great benefits – just ask!
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Support the development and implementation of pricing systems, processes, and guidelines to ensure the business achieves price realization goals.
</b></li>
<li><b>Facilitate the discount management process to highlight impact of price changes.
</b></li>
<li><b>Analyze business intelligence reports to identify key issues and resolve price discrepancies.
</b></li>
<li><b>Become proficient in Pentair’s ERP system and reporting software.
</b></li>
<li><b>Create ad hoc reports and generate insights from transaction-level sales data.
</b></li>
<li><b>Provide daily support with price requests for continuous operations as well as new business.
</b></li>
<li><b>Work closely with marketing to roll out price changes across multiple channels and all other discount structures (standard/non-standard promotions and other rebates).
</b></li>
<li><b>Assist in the monthly / quarterly forecasting and annual budgeting processes and analysis.<br>
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You should:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Have earned a Bachelor's degree (B.A., B.S.) or equivalent in Marketing, Business Management or Finance.
</b></li>
<li><b>Have 3+ years of experience in marketing or similar analyst role (pricing, sales, or finance).
</b></li>
<li><b>Be able to interpret data and draw conclusions.
</b></li>
<li><b>Have strong computer skills and knowledge of MS Office tools.
</b></li>
<li><b>Be an advanced Microsoft Excel user (i.e. PivotTables, VLOOKUP, custom formulas).
</b></li>
<li><b>Be able to thrive in a team environment, build rapport and influence peers.
</b></li>
<li><b>Have the ability to perform ad-hoc analysis at the request of various levels of management as needed.
</b></li>
<li><b>Have QAD ERP system experience.
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>We like, but don’t require:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Business intelligence software and tools (Cognos, Applix / TM1, OneStream)
</b></li>
<li><b>Customer first mindset – highly responsive to customer needs (internal and external)
</b></li>
<li><b>Clearly expressing oneself orally and in writing
</b></li>
<li><b>A high level of personal enthusiasm and self-motivation
</b></li>
<li><b>Ability to effectively analyze situations and problems
</b></li>
<li><b>Process-oriented with an improvement mindset
</b></li>
<li><b>Drive for results
</b></li>
<li><b>Solid command of basic business principles
</b></li>
<li><b>Sense of urgency and a demonstrated ability to meet deadlines
</b></li>
<li><b>High attention to detail and the ability to summarize and reconcile large quantities of data.
</b></li>
</ul>
<ul><li><b>Engages, inspires, motivates and influences others
</b></li></ul>
