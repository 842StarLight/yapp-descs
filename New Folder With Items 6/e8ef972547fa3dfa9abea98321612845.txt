<div><b>
About Us</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
AppDynamics is an application performance monitoring solution that uses machine learning and artificial intelligence (AI) to provide real-time visibility and insight into IT environments. With our unique AIOps solution, you can take the right action at exactly the right time with automated anomaly detection, rapid root-cause analysis, and a unified view of your entire application ecosystem, including private and public clouds. Using AppDynamics, you’ll finally align IT, DevOps, and the business around the information that helps you protect your bottom line and deliver flawless customer experiences at scale.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
About You</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This Education Customer Engagement Manager will be responsible for developing and executing the marketing and customer retention strategy for our enterprise software customer education program. Through a combination of customer interaction, cross-functional collaboration, and targeted marketing campaigns, this individual will drive maximum customer engagement and exceptional renewal rates for the AppDynamics education product offerings. Think of this role as a split between ‘marketing communications’ and ‘customer success manager’ for our education offerings. Through your subject-matter expertise of this specialized market segment, you will create targeted programs in alignment with business objectives and customer needs. You operate with a high sense of urgency and have a bias towards data-driven decisions.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
About this role</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The charter for this role is to scale our Education P&amp;L and optimize renewal rates through a combination of strategic customer communications and post-sales support. Day to day responsibilities will include:</div>
<ul>
<li><p>
Create and run email marketing campaigns to impact registrations, active utilization, and renewals of our customer education subscriptions; lead creation of all collateral and define optimal cadence of marketing campaigns</p></li>
<li><div>
Own the metrics around utilization and renewals of the Education product portfolio.</div></li>
<li><div>
Initiate customer-facing programs designed to drive further adoption of Education among our customers; operationalize the process of gathering and incorporating market and customer feedback to improve our Education product strategy</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop go-to-market strategies that drive awareness and demand generation campaigns for customer consumption of Education offerings.</div></li>
<li><div>
Partner with Education, Services, Customer Success, Sales, and Marketing on driving Education consumption.</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Candidate Qualifications</b></div>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
2+ years experience building, running, and optimizing email marketing campaigns for enterprise software customers; demand generation campaign experience related to enterprise software education offerings strongly desired.</p></li>
<li><div>
Experience interacting with and supporting enterprise level customers in some capacity, whether account management, customer support, marketing communications, customer success, or related functions.</div></li>
<li><div>
Internal stakeholder management experience across groups such as education, services, customer success, sales, and marketing.</div></li>
<li><div>
Customer Relationship Management (CRM) experience, ideally Salesforce.</div></li>
<li><p>
Experience leveraging Learning Management System (LMS) solutions strongly preferred</p></li>
</ul>
