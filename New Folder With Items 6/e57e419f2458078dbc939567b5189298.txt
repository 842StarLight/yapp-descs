<h2>Manager Business Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>As the Program and Project Manager you will be responsible for the collection and use of actionable data as well as review and improvement of current network equipment sparing processes. You will be responsible for data analytics and creating project plans to improve sparing effectiveness.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Interpret equipment forecasts for maintenance related equipment demand.</li>
<li>
Use and improve existing tools and databases to enable automatic gathering of data to ensure adequate inventory supply.</li>
<li>
Partner with cross functional teams on spares management strategy (typically engineering teams) to create effective strategy to increase service levels and optimize inventory.</li>
<li>
Identify optimal managed product mix and appropriate program structure.</li>
<li>
Lead the development of new business processes for network spares.</li>
<li>
In the event of capacity shortfalls, coordinate response and recovery with the vendor, supply planning and engineering teams.</li>
<li>
Develop reporting to better track, trace, and utilize inventory and partner with IT to develop automation in these processes.</li>
<li>
Track vendor performance and equipment delivery intervals and drive compliance.</li>
<li>
Coordinate with supply planning, procurement and Engineering to address equipment backorders. Provide fulfillment data and pursues needed improvements.</li>
<li>
Present recommendations for process improvement ideas to management to create efficiencies in the overall analysis and management of the planning process to include system enhancements and taking into consideration input and output variables.</li>
<li>
Manage End-of-Life / Discontinuance activities, including inventory obsolescence and working with engineering to assign replacement items.</li>
<li>
Minimize inventory levels to values that ensure the most efficient use of spares. Investigates opportunities to utilize excess equipment to reduce overall inventory values and CAPEX spend.</li>
<li>
Partner with engineering teams on decommissioning and excess equipment harvesting.</li>
<li>
Analyze forecast needs for inventory, purchasing, and engineering systems and works with to improve visibility across organization.</li>
<li>
Drive data validation and forecast results, as well as other KPIs.</li>
<li>
Data Mining in large databases to locate information related to inventory optimization.</li>
<li>
Use Visualization to communicate trends and issues.</li>
<li>
Projects:<ul>
<li>
Create automated and streamlined solutions for inventory management.</li>
<li>
Support cross-functional and cross-organizational programs related to spare inventory strategies.</li>
<li>
Continually improve processes to reduce inventory levels and minimize customer down time.</li>
<li>
Assist in defining requirements for RFI and RFP for different areas within the business.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
