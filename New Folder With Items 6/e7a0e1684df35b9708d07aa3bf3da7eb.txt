<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>
Job Summary</b><b>
</b>
</h1>
<p>The Inventory Control Coordinator III will function under the direction of the Warehouse Manager. The position will be responsible for developing, maintaining and verifying inventory processing and reconciliation functions.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ensure all authorized inventory adjustment activity for Imlay City is valid, approved and processed in a timely manner to maintain up to date system inventory balances and audit trails. The adjustments include activity resulting from plant production, receipts, shipments, hold management, product damage, customer returns, sample sales, outside purchases, donations, salvage sales and physical inventory gains and losses.</li>
<li>
Coordinate the finished goods cycle counting program to ensure all product locations for hold and shippable inventory are counted and reconciled as required. Process approved cycle count adjustments in a timely manner to maintain accurate book inventory records.</li>
<li>
In accordance with company policy, maintain procedures to ensure 100% of product on hold at the Imlay City facility is controlled and cycle counted to prevent unauthorized release.</li>
<li>
As directed, identify and communicate with corporate management to coordinate the disposition of any potential overage finished product inventory (SLOB).</li>
<li>
Monitor and coordinate all CHEP pallet activity for the Imlay City facility and any assigned third party locations and ensure the activity is accurately transmitted to CHEP USA to update their records.</li>
<li>
In coordination with Corporate Customer Service, Inventory Deployment and warehouse operations coordinate the activity required for the shipment of customer and interplant finished goods orders. This includes accessing and processing system information for pick tickets and to ensure adequate inventory availability. Communicate with the appropriate personnel the status of any customer service related issues.</li>
<li>
In coordination with corporate transportation and customer service, coordinate the appointments of tendered carriers in the transportation management system and with Imlay City warehouse operations to ensure customer and interplant shipments are picked up on time.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and process required documentation for export customer and interplant orders for finished goods.</li>
<li>
In accordance with company policy, coordinate and process any authorized request for finished goods product traceability information from the Imlay City facility or third party distribution centers.</li>
<li>
Maintain system and manual files to document inventory and operational activity. Calculate and document metrics reporting for warehouse operations as required by plant and corporate management.</li>
<li>
Coordinate the administration of processing system purchase orders in accordance with company policies for the purchase of warehouse pallets and operational supplies.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and process documentation for all inbound materials ensuring that all required quality and financial requirements are completed.</li>
<li>
Perform other related tasks or duties as required by Imlay City plant or warehouse management.</li>
</ul>
