<h2>RED Product Support Agent | Tier 2</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>General Product Support Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide after-sales support and direct communication to RED customers</li>
<li>
Maintain understanding of all current RED policies, procedures and processes</li>
<li>
Maintain and exceed service-level agreement performance benchmarks</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledge of all current and legacy RED products, specifications, technology, programs and announcements</li>
<li>
Manage a high volume of inbound and outbound emails, tickets and calls</li>
</ul>
<p>
Technical Support Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Facilitate and maintain cross-functional communication with various teams (i.e. Product Support, Sales, Engineering, Product Management, etc.)</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot advanced technical issues with customers</li>
<li>
Fill out standardized intake forms to ensure data going to Repair team is complete and all necessary items are requested up front to minimize cases where Repair has to stop and request additional information/items</li>
<li>
Filter out simple requests so more time can be spent handling field issues</li>
<li>
Proactively search online forums for reported issues, reach out to customers, and resolve the issue</li>
<li>
Handle all assigned customer issues thoroughly and thoughtfully within the stated Service Level Agreements</li>
</ul>
<p>
RMA Support Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>
Issue RMAs, Service Quotes, and provide status updates to the customer.</li></ul>
