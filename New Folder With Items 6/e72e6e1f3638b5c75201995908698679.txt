<p>Multimedia Sales Position</p>
<p>If you have an outgoing personality, an energetic get-it-done approach, and enjoy helping clients build their business, then you may be a perfect fit for a sales position with the Rochelle News-Leader and Ogle County Life.</p>
<p>As a multimedia sales consultant, you will service current accounts and also cultivate new clients in the Ogle County region of northern Illinois, helping them market their products and services through a variety of highly visible, popular and effective local media vehicles.</p>
<p>If you have a passion to get out of the office on a regular basis to help clients grow their audience and reach new customers, then we would like to talk to you.</p>
<p>Applicants should possess excellent communication and organizational skills with a positive personality and team-player approach. Previous media sales or related marketing experience is a plus, including print, digital and social media advertising.</p>
<p>Sky is the limit on earning potential and career development in this position. We offer a competitive base salary plus commission, paid vacation, paid sick days, paid holidays, excellent medical benefit plans, and 401-K options.</p>
