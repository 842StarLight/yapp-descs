<h2>Senior Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>About this Opportunity</b><br>
<div>Under the supervision of Marketing &amp; Communications management, the incumbent is responsible for supporting the development and execution of integrated, multi-channel marketing programs aimed at raising visibility in the marketplace and generating sales interest</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<b>
Business Unit: </b><b>Chief Client Office</b><br>
The Chief Client Office (CCO) is focused on providing a consistent Client Experience across all business lines and geographies, enabling enhanced Client Satisfaction, Revenue Growth and Business Line Success. The CCO function includes Sales, Partners, Relationship Management, Integration, Marketing &amp; Communications and Sales Analytics &amp; Operations.<div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Department: </b>Marketing and Communications</div>
<p>
The Marketing &amp; Communications team enhances the image and reputation of the DTCC brand and drives the growth of its businesses by creating integrated outreach programs to disseminate company news and information in communication strategies aligned with key corporate and business goals targeted to internal and external stakeholders globally.</p>
<div>
<br>
<b>
What You'll Do</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li> Under the supervision of the Director of this team, this person supports the development and execution of marketing programs/initiatives aimed at raising industry awareness and generating sales leads for the Repository &amp; Derivatives Services, Collateral and Data Services businesses. Specific tactics include but are not limited to: asset development, webinars, email communications, direct mail, advertising, social media, budget maintenance, etc.).</li>
<li> Requires the individual to liaise with assigned business contacts and other key internal and external stakeholders.
</li>
<li> Maintains detailed, updated knowledge of relevant DTCC products and services, especially as it relates to the business assigned.
</li>
<li> Understands market drivers, competitive landscape and target clients.
</li>
<li> Collaborates with sales/RM and product management to ensure alignment on all initiatives</li>
<li> Partners with regional marketing counterparts on relevant product marketing initiatives by region</li>
<li> Coordinates with direct team as well as broader M&amp;C team to ensure brand alignment and collaboration on cross promotional opportunities</li>
<li> Updates various performance metrics; produces and distributes reports to stakeholders</li>
<li> Project management including planning and scheduling, issue identification/resolution/escalation, communication</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li> Mitigates risk by following established procedures and monitoring controls, spotting key errors and demonstrating strong ethical behavior.</li></ul>
