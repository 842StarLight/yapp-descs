<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Data Analyst - Altamonte Springs, FL</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>We are currently looking for a Data Analyst. The position includes conducting regular deep-dive data analysis to identify and report upon internal/external trends/workflows that may lead to potential revenue and efficiency gains. In turn, the Data Analyst will be expected to identify root causes of issues and bring policy and procedure recommendations to the Senior Leadership team to reconcile the issue and increase productivity and efficiency.</p>
<p>The candidate is expected to be a critical thinker and thought leader. Additionally, the candidate must have superior interpersonal and communication skills as they will interface with all levels of the organization regularly.</p>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE AND EDUCATION:</b></p>
<p><b>Required Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>Proven ability to effectively communicate with a team while maintaining a high degree of professionalism</li>
<li>Candidate has a strong sense for prioritization and management to meet critical timelines</li>
<li>Detail-oriented, quick learner, motivated, strong analytical skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Master’s degree</li>
<li>Possess working knowledge of mortgage banking industry and loan workflow</li>
<li>Experience with MS Office Suite, SQL, HTML, CSS, XML</li>
</ul>
<p><b>DAILY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b> Included but are not limited to the following; other duties may be assigned as deemed necessary and reasonable by management.</p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct deep-dive data analysis and leverage findings to help devise a strategy that can enhance sales opportunities and marketing plan within the company</li>
<li>Meet regularly with departments and end-users of Business Intelligence system to determine software specifications and preferences, and based on their preferences, customize and modify software to their department and individual preferences to optimize efficiency gains and ease of use</li>
<li>Automate all levels of reporting via the use of Business Intelligence system and turn into reports, Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), notifications or a combination of the three.
<ul>
<li>This includes, but is not limited to, gathering, documenting and designing business requirements that will satisfy the business need of the department and/or user</li>
<li>Prior to rolling out end products to the users, Data Analyst will have had to document, build prototype, test, modify and retest software against business requirements to ensure accuracy and complete mapping of requirements and customer satisfaction</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Help drive end-user adoption of Business Intelligence technology within the company</li>
<li>Expected to become a subject matter expert in Business Intelligence system used at (client name)</li>
<li>Identify opportunities in the marketplace to guide overall marketing strategies and assist in the development of these strategies</li>
<li>Use data-driven analysis to develop methods for improving marketing practices and operations on a company-wide basis</li>
</ul>
