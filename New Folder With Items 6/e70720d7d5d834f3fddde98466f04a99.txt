<h2>Senior UX Designer (Mobile focused)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Are you interested in working on a cutting-edge business that understands and values your work and sees user experience, customer experience, and service design as key strategic priorities? Join us as an iOS- and Android-focused designer working on Microsoft PowerApps, the cloud business application platform that empowers anyone to quickly build and deliver mobile- and web-based business apps for use throughout their organizations.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>You'll design configurable interfaces and UX systems for Microsoft's business application platform, as well as the tools makers use to build on it. As companies digitally transform their businesses with the Microsoft Power Platform and Dynamics 365, your deep expertise with native app design patterns on the iOS and Android platforms will help us deliver intuitive and productive mobile experiences to workers. The role’s broad scope will give you ample room to flex your design muscles—both on projects that have immediate impact on the PowerApps business through to long-term explorations that shape our long-term strategy, vision and UX direction.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
PowerApps is part of the Microsoft Power Platform, which enables professional and citizen developers to improve productivity and leverage their business data with an integrated low-code/no-code application platform. Microsoft Dynamics 365 business applications are built on PowerApps, so you'll also be enabling core experiences for one of the worlds top cloud brands. Your work will provide the core mobile user experiences for business applications used by companies all over the world, empowering their employees to do their best work.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Other success factors for designers in our agile environment:<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Thoughtfulness—You’re thoughtful and deliberate in your choices and able to articulate that on the fly across a cross-functional team, and occasionally, conflicting priorities and objectives.
</li>
<li>Sales intelligence + persuasion skills—You’ve got the social intelligence and sales skills to understand (or explain) what needs to be changed, and why. You use criticism to spur insightful conversations, not a reason to be defensive or shut down.
</li>
<li>A translator of tradeoffs—There’s no perfect interface and no right answer. You’re able to create different options and translate the tradeoffs between them in the context of business objectives.
</li>
<li>A facilitator—You know how to collaborate with others and let them put their concerns on the table.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>About the team
</b></div>
<div>Your new UX group is led by an industry hire Group Creative Director who is reshaping the way digital experiences unfold in the universe of Microsoft’s Fluent design system for the developer and IT professional space. The team is comprised of widely talented and diverse individuals with interesting backgrounds and sometimes perplexing passions. It’s an environment where creativity and innovation thrive, and where you can solve hard problems and make a positive impact every day. From our perspective—this is the moment to join, we are ready to grow, positioned to win and will be the next big design team that people wish they were part of.
</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div><b>
Portfolio
</b></div>
<div>A strong portfolio is absolutely critical for this role, both in terms of demonstrating your experience and as an example of your ability to create a compelling design presentation. Showcase your experience in UX and interaction design with a focus on work that demonstrates your understanding of iOS and Android conventions and design best practices. We’d like to see a diversity of projects, but be careful that your portfolio doesn't get scattered or overly long—focus on curation and quality to choose a solid collection of outcomes and design intermediaries and deliverables (especially flows, mockups, and prototypes), and be sure the portfolio demonstrates the design and thought process behind each image.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Design degree or equivalent experience plus 3+ years’ industry experience in interactive design, visual design, and/or information architecture.
</li>
<li>Strong familiarity with design best practices for iOS and Android apps</li>
<li>Demonstrated breadth across IA, usability, interaction, and visual design
</li>
<li>Ability to create highly polished design prototypes, mockups, and other communication artifacts
</li>
<li>The ability to quickly turn around rigorous wireframes and mockups quickly in a fast-paced, iterative environment
</li>
<li>The ability to scope and estimate efforts accurately, prioritize tasks and goals independently, and rapidly produce deliverables
</li>
<li>Proficiency with design tools<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Desirable skills &amp; experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Experience designing user experiences for enterprise software/services
</li>
<li>Experience with UI design principles and patterns</li>
<li>Experience with design systems</li>
</ul>
