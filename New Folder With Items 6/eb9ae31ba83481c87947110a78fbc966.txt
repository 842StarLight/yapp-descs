<div><b>Job Description</b></div>
<div>
Drawing upon their previous experiences in industry and/or consulting, our Industry X.O. consulting professionals work closely with our clients to design, build and implement strategies that can help enhance business performance within engineering and product development, which requires our consulting professional to understand how business, technology and operations intersect. Our deep understanding of our clients’ industry and our global perspective on progressive business ideas and emerging technologies enables us to drive and deliver transformational change.</div>
