<h2>Greeter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Responsible for greeting, directing and logging customers in dealership showroom floor and outdoor sales lots.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities: </b>This job has no direct supervisory responsibilities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b><b>include the following:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Log all customers on the sales lot and showroom floor by identifying factors and the Salesperson responsible for the customer.
</li>
<li>Observe and track Sales Managers “5 minute customer contact rule” and track appropriately
</li>
<li>Ensure Salespersons turn in the “Up Card” for every customer. If not, check “no Information collected.”
</li>
<li>Greet all customers that come inside the dealership in a friendly, prompt, and professional manner.
</li>
<li>Account for all appointments on appointment sheet by name. Provide a “No-Show” report to BDC Manager immediately for a “Missed Appointment” call.
</li>
<li>Rotate through Pre-Owned to pick up “Red Cards” and observe activity, speak to Pre-Owned Managers about traffic.
</li>
<li>Enhance customer experience in any way possible for both New and Pre-Owned Departments. Ensure customers do not fall between the cracks or get abandoned during process.
</li>
<li>Walk all customers to stated destination (i.e. Parts Department, Service Department, Finance Department).
</li>
<li>Maintains CSI at or above Company standards
</li>
<li>Maintains an organized, clean and safe work area
</li>
<li>Participates in required training
</li>
<li>Records all hours worked accurately in company timekeeping system
</li>
<li>Follows Safeguards rules and regulations.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates the Company’s Core Values
</li>
<li>Complies with Company policies and procedures
</li>
<li>Observes all Federal, State, Local and Company safety rules and regulations in the performance of duties.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
