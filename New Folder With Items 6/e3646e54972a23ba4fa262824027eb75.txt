<div><b>
Summary</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The main function of this position is to ensure operational coverage and lead the regional scheduling team by reviewing, monitoring and ensuring completion of work orders for the region while maintaining coordination responsibilities for a designated territory. This includes oversight of each schedulers workload and providing guidance and handling escalated scheduling / service issues.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li><div><div><div>
Responsible for scheduling and dispatching all service events between Customer Engineers or Service Partners for an assigned territory (refer to the Operations Coordinator job description for specific duties).</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Be willing to lead by example – be accountable and dependable, maintain a positive attitude, inspire others to go the extra mile to take care of Customers (internal/external) and achieve Operational Key Results (OKR).</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Continuously monitor the scheduling efforts of the Regional scheduling team and report team statuses and action plans weekly to the Scheduling Operations Manager.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Act as a coach/mentor when communicating or addressing performance issues with the team.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Demonstrate the ability to remain calm and even-tempered during stressful situations.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Close coordination with the Scheduling Operations Manager of any behavioral or operational related issues. Present recovery plan(s) with multiple options that will allow the Scheduling Manager to make informed decisions.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Actively seek and participate in continuous improvements initiatives that impact customer care and coordination of work.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Assist the Scheduling Operations Manager in the implementation of new processes/policies and systems.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Meet with scheduling team on a regular basis to review, coach and train on various Customer Service concerns and opportunities.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Handle escalated scheduling issues within the region, including but not limited to sales inquiries and assisting Customer Success Management and Coordinated Services scheduling.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Generate standard and custom reports to monitor workload.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Proactively identify schedule risks and present options to resolve critical potential issues to the local team and Scheduling Manager.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Ensure all billable work is being scheduled and executed and tickets are closed out in a timely basis.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Where practical, empower and direct the regional team to strategically schedule preventive maintenance visits to minimize/ cover costs for the company (take over Customer Engineer Scheduling or large account scheduling).</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Ensure the team is accurately documenting all scheduling correspondence in tickets.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Maintain regional PTO schedule and ensure responsibilities are covered during absences.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Ensure every Coordinator is maintaining their performance results in BetterWorks on a monthly basis.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Provide content and assist Scheduling Manager in quarterly BetterWorks one-on-one conversations related to job performance.</div></div></div></li>
<li><div><div><div>
Promote recognition and submit positive feedback in BetterWorks and to the Scheduling Operations Manager.</div></div></div></li>
</ul></div></div>
