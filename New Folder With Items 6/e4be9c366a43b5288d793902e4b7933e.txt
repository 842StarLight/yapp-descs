<p>The Field Service Manager is responsible for coordinating all aspects of the installation projects between the customers, vendors and our company for multiple projects at once in teh assigned territory.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinate installation project with customer.</li>
<li>
Approve and submit subcontractor invoices/draws for payment.</li>
<li>
Anticipate and resolve customer issues.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain constructive relationships with customers, sales, and administration.</li>
<li>
Maintain strong visibility on job site</li>
</ul>

<li>
An understanding of carpentry, building, and contracting is preferred.</li>
<li>
Self-motivated with strong interpersonal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Problem solving and organizational skills.</li>
<li>
Great customer service.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in MS software.</li>
<li>
Project management and planning</li>
<li>
Flexibility in approach to different types of business problems and a willingness to change course to avoid project setbacks</li>
<li>
Able to create and establish structure and process across cross-functional teams</li>
<li>
Experience working independently</li>
<li>
Collaborative, Adaptable, Creative, Honest, Positive</li>
<li>
Represent Republic Elite with pride and integrity.</li>
<li>
Provide a favorable customer experience from initial contact through installation.</li>
<li>
Promote and enable constructive communication between departments.</li>
<li>
Follow all safety and environment regulations.</li>
<li>
Valid driver’s license</li>
<li>
College degree required.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 50%.</li>
</ul>
