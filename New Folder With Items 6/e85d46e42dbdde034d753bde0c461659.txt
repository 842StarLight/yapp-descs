<h2>Real Estate Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for a talented <b>Real Estate</b> <b>Assistant</b> in San Francisco, CA to manage listings and provide exceptional customer service to clients in this high-volume, fast-paced real estate team. This is a great opportunity to be the glue that holds everything together. If you thrive on moments where you can say, <i>“I got this!</i>” this is the job for you.</p>
<p>
A top producer and founder of Mosaik Real Estate, Michelle Kim has earned a reputation among her clients as a hardworking, knowledgeable, and trustworthy agent — a triple threat that is often hard to find in a salesperson. With many years of experience in sales and a background in the title and escrow field, you can count on Michelle to provide a full-service experience that leaves both buyers and sellers totally satisfied.</p>
<p>
We are looking for someone who has an eye for details and takes pride in completing tasks accurately. Your responsibilities will play an important role in clearing away distractions so the agent can focus on generating new business and closing sales. Your role is multi-dimensional; you will support the agent, provide concierge-level service to clients, and maintain impeccable listing coordination for the team. Real Estate is your jam, and you enjoy being the support that keeps it all together.</p>
<p><b><i>
Real Estate Assistants with prior real estate experience in an Operations, TC and/or similar role are encouraged to apply. Applicants seeking a position as a stepping-stone into sales—this is not the job for you</i></b></p>
<p><b>
Areas of Focus:</b></p>
<ul><li>Prepare listing presentations, attend listing appointments with lead agents, manage listing paperwork, handle signs and lockboxes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Liaise with title, escrow, lenders, and the TC for a smooth transaction</li></ul>
<ul><li>Schedule photography, stager, and various other listing vendors</li></ul>
<ul><li>Own showing schedules and provide clients with agent feedback</li></ul>
<ul><li>Serve as the key contact person for clients by providing a concierge-level of service</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain impeccable organizational and communication skills with Agent at all times</li></ul>
<p><b>
Required Experience and Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul><li>2+ years of experience assisting a Real Estate Agent, office, or team</li></ul>
<ul><li><b>Real estate license is required</b></li></ul>
<p><b>
We are looking for talent. Does this describe you?</b></p>
<ul><li>You’ll never say<i>, </i>that’s not my job; you are all about turning transactions into relationships</li></ul>
<ul><li>You’re smart, dependable and reliable; we can trust you, nothing falls through the cracks</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highly organized and love creating and using systems for efficiency</li></ul>
<ul><li>Resourceful and a self-starter</li></ul>
<ul><li>You stay cool under pressure and don't get flustered</li></ul>
<ul><li>You prefer to "own" your job, and not just wait to be told what to do</li></ul>
<ul><li>You love working in the office and out in the field</li></ul>
<ul><li>You are a great communicator and keep your team in the loop</li></ul>
<ul><li>You maintain a sense of urgency and you love working in a rapidly changing environment</li></ul>
<p><b>
Key Software Proficiency
</b></p>
<ul><li>Google Suite of Services</li></ul>
<ul><li>Real Estate Software: MLS, Dotloop, Zipforms, DocuSign, Dropbox</li></ul>
<ul><li>CRM experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Quickbooks</li></ul>
