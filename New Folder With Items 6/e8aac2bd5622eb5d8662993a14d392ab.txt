<h2>Automotive Mechanic</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are Western Automotive Ventures, Inc., the largest franchisee owner of Big O Tires,a full-service automotive center with 448 stores nationally. We are positioned to be the leader in the automobile service industry. If you believe in Empowering Ownership, Giving Legendary Guest Service and are committed to Operational Excellence, we want you to join our team! <p><b>Automotive Mechanic Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>Perform service and installation activities associated with store products and services in an accurate and timely manner. Must have a proven track record as a successful mechanic.</p>
<p><b>Automotive Mechanic Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates good customer service skills.</li>
<li>Drive vehicles with automatic and manual transmissions.</li>
<li>Services customer's vehicles, in customer service, repairing functions such as checking and filling fluids, checking and replacing filters, lubricating vehicles, oil changes, inspecting/servicing vehicles brake systems, component parts, tires, tire services, battery replacement, lights, steering and suspension, etc.,</li>
<li>Tires -- Definitions, sizing, sidewall markings, inspection, repair, balancing, inventory, tire ancillary services, TPMS.</li>
<li>VIP, vehicle inspections, Workflow process, parking lot, bay area navigation, work orders.</li>
<li>Check and replace various filters and fluids</li>
<li>Follow all shop safety policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Explain services and products to customers.</li>
<li>Look up information for various applications.</li>
<li>Operate point of sale hardware and software.</li>
<li>Prepare and inspect work orders, VIP Checklist and Vehicle Inspection Form (Health Check) for completeness.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Automotive Mechanic Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalency certificate is preferred.</li>
<li>Must have a proven track record as a successful mechanic.</li>
<li>Ability to speak read and write English is preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals.</li>
<li>Ability to speak effectively with customers and employees.</li>
<li>Good computer skills. Ability to use the company hardware and software.</li>
</ul>
