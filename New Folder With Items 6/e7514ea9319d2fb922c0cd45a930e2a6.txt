<p><b>
Who You’ll Work with:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Production Artist will work closely with other members of the Production team, as well as Account Executives, Creative and Production Designers and Copywriters. Work is typically performed under minimal daily supervision, receiving guidance from Manager and Senior Production Artists about the work that needs to be completed.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Your Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
The Production Artist is responsible for building and manipulating files to incorporate into the final layouts, while ensuring quality control and efficient production in a timely manner. This role incorporates branding guidelines (e.g. photography, text, legal requirements, etc.) into artwork layout (e.g. circulars, etc.) per client’s technical specifications.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and produce page layouts that promote the client’s brand and sales objectives</li>
<li>Work with pagination and product mix to develop page layouts and final page production</li>
<li>Executes production of creative and graphic concepts from design team</li>
<li>Produces technically correct, print-ready mechanicals for release</li>
<li>Produce four-color and black and white page versions</li>
<li>Prepare final files for print and digital production</li>
<li>Maintains and enhances the client’s identity by working within the corporate brand standards and guidelines</li>
<li>Effectively use project management system to track time on all projects</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Ability to work under the pressure of daily or hourly deadlines.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years’ experience in an agency environment</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite (primarily InDesign)</li>
<li>Maintain a basic understanding of pre-press industry</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of CMYK, RGB, color modes, conversion, tone structure, memory colors, flesh t ones and multi-tone files</li>
<li>Ability to typeset and create layouts for print</li>
<li>Ability to work under tight deadlines with a keen eye for detail</li>
<li>Ability to prepare projects for print is essential</li>
<li>Ability to build strong, technically tight, problem-free mechanicals</li>
<li>Ability to set up and organize clean template files that can be adapted to many uses and streamline the production process</li>
<li>Ability to work independently or in a team environment with a focus on achieving client initiatives</li>
<li>Willingness to learn new techniques and software and implement standard directives according to style guides</li>
<li>Per channel-add applicable software knowledge-Adobe Creative Suite, etc.</li>
</ul>
