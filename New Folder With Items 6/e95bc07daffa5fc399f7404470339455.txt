<h2>Senior Mechanical Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About our company: </b></p>
<p>Boston Semi Equipment (BSE) is a worldwide test automation products and services company. Our automation products include gravity and pick-and-place handlers capable of handling a wide range of IC packages and applications. We also develop custom automations solutions to meet your exact equipment requirements.</p>
<p>BSE is a great place to work for a software/controls engineer. It is a small company with a talented team of engineers working in an environment of quick decision making and little bureaucracy. We are developing an innovative product for our industry packed with new technology that involves robots, precise x-y-z motions, vision, complex algorithms, and HMI/GUI interfaces. We are looking for bright, talented, energetic engineers to join our team!</p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>Under minimal direction of an assigned lead or manager, designs, develops and documents mechanical assemblies or subassemblies for automated systems.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions/Major Responsibilities</b><b>: </b></p>
<p>1. Participate in all phases of projects, including requirement definition, design, testing and integration, as required.</p>
<p>2. Follow established engineering procedures and specifications for design.</p>
<p>3. Assist in developing and implementing design procedures for new device packages or modules.</p>
<p>4. Research, select and test vendor products.</p>
<p>5. Research and generate solutions for ECO’s and assist in all functions required to generate and process ECO’s.</p>
<p>6. Support manufacturing floor, Engineering Lab, and machine shop as required.</p>
<p>7. Interface with customers and sales on projects as required.</p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibility</b><b>: </b> May provide work leadership and training to lower level technical or professional employees.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b><b>: </b></p>
<p>Ø <b>Education: </b> Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from an accredited college or university. M.S. degree preferred.</p>
<p>Ø <b>Experience: </b> Minimum of eight years’ experience in mechanical design with a Bachelor’s degree, or five years with a Master’s degree.</p>
<p>Ø <b>Skills/Technical Requirements</b>: Proficiency with SolidWorks (latest release), PC software, and practical experience with strong documentation skills. Base knowledge of semiconductor packages and manufacturing processes. Excellent oral and written communication skills; ability to give direction to Engineers, Designers and Drafters.</p>
<p><b>Job Conditions</b><b>: </b> Some Domestic and international travel required. Works in lab environment with constant or intermittent noise.</p>
<p><b>Physical Demands/Strengths</b>: Prolonged periods of sitting at the computer terminal. Ability to use keyboard. Ability to lift up to 20 lbs., occasional exertion.</p>
<p><b>Protective Devices Required</b>: In required areas.</p>
