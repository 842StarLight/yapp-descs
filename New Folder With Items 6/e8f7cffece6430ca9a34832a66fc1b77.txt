<h2>Requirement & Business Analysis Senior Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>General Dynamics IT (GDIT) is looking for its next passionate, highly skilled, self-motivated, experienced <b>Requirements/Business Analysis Senior Manager </b>to join our team as we continue to partner with New York State of Health (NYSoH) to provide comprehensive health coverage to more than 4.5 million New Yorkers.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Our GDIT State &amp; Local LLC team provides NYS with innovative and agile health benefit exchange (HBE) solutions that deliver the functionality required to comply by the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act and provide the flexibility needed to easily scale up to support future requirements</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Here's where you come in:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
You’ll lead our talented Requirements/Business Analysis team. Our project is a large scale Java web services environment. It is a three-tiered application (web, application, and database server) with multiple levels of security between each tier. Our software is written with <b>Java</b>, utilizing <b>Spring</b> 5 Framework, a very heavy dose of JavaScript and UI moving towards Angular. Redesigning code to smaller bases with Microservices and REST APIs. Other technologies are in play for rules management, DROOLS, and BPM workflow, Activiti</p>
<p>
This position provides leadership of the IT Requirements/Business Analysis team to develop technology solutions that support business requirements, improve processes, as well as align resources to support our client. The primary focus includes: developing the competency and growth of the team by coaching and educating on business analysis skills, contributing to continuous improvements, resource performance management and demand management to ensure that the right resources are available to support projects.<br>
<br>
The Senior Manager supervises and coordinates the daily activities of staff, which includes assessing and monitoring staff productivity through standards and metrics to optimize performance and increase efficiency. They also monitor the activities of the business analysis team to ensure all projects’ progress is on schedule and within budget</p>
