<h2>Corporate Counsel</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>Algolia is looking to add a corporate counsel to our growing legal and compliance team! You will play a key role in structuring, drafting, and negotiating a variety of commercial agreements, as well as advising on legal issues spanning the breadth of Algolia’s products and services. You’ll support our growing revenue team, counsel product development and negotiate vendor agreements. This is a great opportunity for a dynamic lawyer who has a passion for helping scale and shape commercial contracting processes and policies, training team members to accomplish the same, impact the development and lifecycle of products and learn and take on new areas of the law.</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Draft, review, and negotiate master subscription agreements with enterprise customers and work closely with our Sales organization to close revenue deals</li>
<li>
Draft, review, and negotiate a wide range of other commercial agreements, including as related to software licenses, partnerships, consultants, resellers, referrers, and non-disclosure</li>
<li>
Partner cross-functionally with global teams on a variety of commercial matters</li>
<li>
Provide counseling to Product, Engineering, Marketing and Security teams on issues related to new and existing products and documentation</li>
<li>
Provide counseling in intellectual-property and privacy law Help identify and implement new policies, processes and procedures to manage legal and business risks in a growing organization</li>
<li>
Develop training materials and conduct training sessions for business teams</li>
<li>
Manage outside counsel for quality and expenses as needed</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
JD and member of the CA Bar in good standing or Registered In-House Counsel in CA</li>
<li>
At least 4 years of law firm experience with training in corporate law and 2+ years of in-house relevant work experience in a growing technology company</li>
<li>
Passion for partnering with Sales and closing deals</li>
<li>
Experience with drafting and negotiating complex enterprise SaaS agreements</li>
<li>
Strong communication and analytical skills and attention to detail</li>
<li>
The position will require some travel (mainly to our Paris office)</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Nice to Have:
</b></h3>
<div><ul><li>Expertise in Privacy, particularly EU data protection laws and CCPA</li></ul></div>
</div></div>
