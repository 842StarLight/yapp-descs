<h2>Lead Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>Overall Purpose: Design, develop and implement launch strategy and programs for products and services within the AT&amp;T portfolio.<br>
<br>
<b>Key Roles and Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the success of a product or group of products through development and execution of comprehensive marketing plan.</li>
<li>
Typically viewed as product owner throughout the life cycle. Conducts multi-year product planning to determine product life-cycle decisions, ensure consistency with overall marketing and corporate strategy, and achieve product performance, revenue, market share, and profitability objectives.</li>
<li>
Identifies, monitors and analyzes internal (e.g. financials, movement, revenue, cost, sales) and external (e.g., technology, market share, competitive) performance parameters to position, modify features and identify new uses for the product.</li>
<li>
Provides continuing product surveillance and management of established product to attain financial objectives. Accountable for revenue attainment and profitability.</li>
<li>
Leads cross functional teams in presentation and resolution of marketing deliverables as needed.</li>
<li>
Provides readout to leadership level regarding products and plans as assigned.</li>
<li>
Acts as team lead as needed to provide leadership to less experienced professionals. Considered an expert within the discipline.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Principal Functional Skills / Competencies associated with this Title:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Business case development</li>
<li>
Competitive environment assessment</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Cybersecurity market, especially Endpoint Security</li>
<li>
Knowledge of sales channels and segments</li>
<li>
Marketing strategy and positioning</li>
<li>
Product development strategy</li>
<li>
Vendor relationship management</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Education: Typically requires a bachelor's degree.</b></div>
<br>
<p></p>
<div><b>
Required Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Five years product management experience</li>
<li>
Must be comfortable with conveying the value of new and emerging products, technologies or applications to senior level management and across a diverse product team</li>
<li>
Product launch and lifecycle management experience</li>
<li>
Previous experience leading a cross-functional organization in executing projects from ideation to completion</li>
<li>
Strong analytic and fact-based decision-making skills</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and operate in a fast-paced, high-growth professional environment</li>
<li>
Must possess both outstanding written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>
Must be proficient using Microsoft Office products including Word, Power Point, Excel and Outlook.</li>
<li>
AT&amp;T will consider for employment qualified applicants in a manner consistent with the requirements of federal, state ad local laws</li>
<li>
The successful candidate will be able to perform the following with or without reasonable accommodation:<br>
<ul><li>Ability to operate a personal computer, wireless equipment, copier and fax</li></ul>
</li>
</ul>

Desired Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>

Six or more years product management experience, preferably in consumer new product development</li>

</ul>
