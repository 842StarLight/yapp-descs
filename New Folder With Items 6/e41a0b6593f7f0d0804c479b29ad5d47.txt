<p><b>Project Engineer </b></p>
<p><i>Description: </i></p>
<p>Growing commercial construction company in the Frisco, TX area looking to fill an entry-level position. This position has great potential for advancement in the company. Will work directly with the sales department on many pre-construction activities to prepare projects for construction.</p>
<p><i>Responsibilities: </i></p>
<p><b>Procore Set-up: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create job in Procore- our construction management software</li>
<li>Upload relevant plans, specs &amp; documents<b>Project Submittals through Procore: </b>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Identify submittal &amp; sample/mock-up procedures</li>
<li>Request submittal packages from subs and vendors</li>
<li>Submit packages through Procore for approval</li>
<li>Revise &amp; resubmit submittal packages as required<b>Project mock-ups &amp; samples: </b>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Create Selections Sheets template for job</li>
<li>Work with procurement to help purchase required materials for samples/mock-ups</li>
<li>Work with PM to help build required mock-ups</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Project buy-out: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Request new quotes from subs &amp; vendors</li>
<li>Perform new material take-offs as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Skills required: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>ProCore experience- preferred but not required</li>
<li>Submittal experience- preferred but not required</li>
<li>PlanSwift experience for material take-offs- preferred but not required</li>
<li>Ability to read construction plans- required</li>
<li>Organized</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
<li>Great communication skills</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Software proficiency: </i></p>
