<p><b>POSITION DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>TITLE: Designer</p>
<p>SUPERVISOR: Design &amp; Engineering Manager</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>· Create preliminary solar designs using remote shading software and/or site survey information</p>
<p>· Develop array layouts, structural drawings and electrical schematics</p>
<p>· Assist the Commercial Project Manager on commercial projects</p>
<p>· Review site survey information to ensure the accuracy and completeness of all information to be used for designs</p>
<p>· Interact with structural engineers, <i>sales team</i>, field managers and other staff to optimize design decisions</p>
<p>· Review all design documents for design efficacy, construction feasibility, accuracy, consistency, and fidelity to contracts</p>
<p>· Assist with the development and implementation of sales designs</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p>· Fluent in AutoCAD</p>
<p>· Familiar with principles of building construction and/or electrical systems</p>
<p>· Able to learn and adapt quickly</p>
<p>· Able to work independently, as well as manage and prioritize tasks</p>
<p>· Detail oriented</p>
