<h2>Customer Service Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Fora Financial, located in NYC, is a leading financial services company providing working capital to small and mid-size businesses nationwide.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Established in 2008, we support small businesses in need of financing to sustain or grow their enterprise by providing innovative, customized, and flexible working capital solutions. We operate through a consultative approach, listening to customers and providing what best meets their needs in an ever-changing financial market. To date, Fora Financial has funded over 22,000 individual transactions to more than 15,000 unique customers. The company has funded over $750 million in working capital solutions for small businesses since its inception.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>A Day in the Life of a Customer Service Manager:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Telephone and email outreach to current customers
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain business relationships with other companies
</li>
<li>Review reports for accounts requiring attention with regards to collections
</li>
<li>Inbound and outbound customer contact to resolve customer inquiries
</li>
<li>Use of various databases and software programs to obtain and communicate information
</li>
<li>Manage a Customer Service Representative
</li>
<li>Data entry of customer information into templates
</li>
<li>Assist Sales Representatives and Accounting with trouble shooting
</li>
<li>Attend meetings with management to review the status of collection accounts
</li>
<li>Prioritize tasks, assignments and customer inquiries in an effective and efficient manner
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>What You Have:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Attention to detail
</li>
<li>Results driven and persistent
</li>
<li>Efficient time management skills
</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively in a high volume, fast paced environment
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and communication skills
</li>
<li>Outstanding phone and email etiquette
</li>
<li>Ability to handle tough situations calmly and professionally</li>
</ul>
