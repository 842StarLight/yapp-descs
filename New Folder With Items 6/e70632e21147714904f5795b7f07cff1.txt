<h2>Key Accounts Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b>
</p>
<p>Responsible for representing Steffes in the promotion and sales of Steffes products. Responsible for maintaining a factory representative image in the designated market region.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Steffes Core Values</b>
</p>
<p><b>Trust:</b> We promote growth and long-term success by making wise decisions with trust as an essential component.
</p>
<p><b>Respect:</b> We unleash creative talents of our diverse workforce by treating each other the way we would like to be treated.
</p>
<p><b>Teamwork:</b> We believe people achieve much more by working together.
</p>
<p><b>Integrity:</b> We follow through and keep our word while being open, honest, ethical, and fair.
</p>
<p><b>Safety:</b> We view our co-workers as family members of our business and strive to ensure safety for all.
</p>
<p><b>Humility:</b> We recognize and appreciate strengths and contributions beyond one’s self. We are modest, genuine, and authentic.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Establish contact with existing accounts and understand and grow their product business with Steffes.
</li>
<li>Work with new customers to establish relationships and increase business for Steffes.
</li>
<li>Establish appropriate field management in sales territory.
</li>
<li>Establish contacts, promote and sell Steffes products, and follow up as needed.
</li>
<li>Maintain customer master files and follow up records.
</li>
<li>Participate in regional trade shows demonstrating the Steffes product line.
</li>
<li>Assist in the development of promotional materials.
</li>
<li>Assist in the development and implementation of a marketing program for assigned region.
</li>
<li>Stay abreast of market trends.
</li>
<li>Work with engineers regarding new products and/or changes.
</li>
<li>Responsible for obtaining the knowledge needed to understand the sale, application, and use of Steffes products.
</li>
<li>Responsible for projecting future expenditures for the fiscal budget.
</li>
<li>Participate in the Steffes product training agenda, as required.
</li>
<li>Submit updates of activity in area (quotes, sales, etc.) and weekly sales reports.
</li>
<li>Provide sales projections for assigned region.
</li>
<li>Maintain a positive work atmosphere by acting and communicating in collaborative manner with customers, suppliers, co-workers, and management.
</li>
<li>Helps company meet its Strategic, Quality, and Safety objectives.
</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Core Expectations</b>
</p>
<p><b><i>Customer Service</i></b><b> - </b>Demonstrate excellent customer service and display a positive attitude and behavior consistent with the Steffes Code of Conduct and Core Values.
</p>
<p><b><i>Quality </i></b><b>- </b>Actively support and participate in Quality Improvement.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Take immediate action in unsafe situations.
</li>
<li>Adhere to all safety standards; thus, promoting safe practices to ensure a safe environment.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Conduct </i></b><b>- </b>Demonstrate and support the Steffes Code of Conduct.
</p>
<p><b><i>Policies and Procedures </i></b><b>- </b>Demonstrate and support Steffes Policies and Procedures by:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Using tact, sound judgement, and maintaining a professional attitude in communication and relationships with others.
</li>
<li>Striving to deliver the best quality in daily work.
</li>
<li>Promoting a positive image of Steffes and its products.
</li>
<li>Being timely and punctual in attendance and following all policies and procedures.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Requirements/Qualifications</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in business administration or marketing
</li>
<li>5+ years of outside sales experience
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of personal computers and software including word processing and spreadsheets.
</li>
<li>Important personal attributes: good telephone etiquette, thorough, patience, well organized, and service oriented.
</li>
</ul>
<p>-OR-
</p>
<ul><li>An acceptable combination of education and experience
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Demands of Position</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>This position requires walking, sitting, or standing to a significant degree
</li>
<li>Must have the ability to lift 50 pounds from floor to waist, carry 50 pounds, and push and/or pull up to 20 pounds
</li>
<li>Must have the ability to climb ladders and/or stairs
</li>
<li>Stooping, crouching, kneeling, and bending
</li>
<li>Handling, feeling, and gripping would be required for some activities
</li>
<li>This role may be subject to internal and external environmental conditions</li>
</ul>
