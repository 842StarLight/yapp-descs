<p>THIS ROLE:
</p>
<p>We are looking for a smart and consultative colleague for the role of <b>Director, Business Development.</b> This position is responsible for establishing sales targets to meet company objectives and developing strategic sales plans for developing business and achieving sales goals.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>THE IDEAL CANDIDATE WILL HAVE/BE ABLE TO:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Existing client relationships with large ecommerce and retail advertisers spending over $100k monthly on Facebook media</li>
<li>At least 8 years of relevant experience (advertising technology and/or agency experience, and Facebook Platform familiarity)</li>
<li>You’ve confidently sold C-level executives, ability to build relationships quickly.</li>
<li>Track record of closing B2B enterprise deals over $1M in a timely manner</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, excellent listener</li>
<li>Consultative sales style, trusted advisor; you’re curious and like to learn and talk about other businesses.</li>
<li>Experience defining sales processes that drive desired sales outcomes
</li>
<li>Provide detailed and accurate sales forecasting. Compile information and data related to customer and prospect interactions</li>
<li>Monitor customer, market and competitor activity and provide feedback as needed.</li>
<li>Experience developing and managing channel partnerships.
</li>
<li>Put in place infrastructure and systems to support the success of the sales function.
</li>
<li>You enjoy driving the sales and business development process from start to finish</li>
<br>
</ul>
