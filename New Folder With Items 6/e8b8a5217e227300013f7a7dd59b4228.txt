<h2>Director of Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Digital Guardian is looking for a Director of Product Management to join the team at our headquarters in Waltham, MA! This is a new position created to support our DLP and EDR Managed Services Offering. The successful candidate will play a key role in accelerating profitable growth in what is already a rapidly growing segment for the company. Success will be defined by measured improvements in ARR, margin and customer satisfaction.</p>
<p>
For more than 10 years, Digital Guardian has enabled data-rich organizations to protect their most valuable assets with an on premises deployment or an outsourced managed security program (MSP). Our corporate office is located in Waltham, MA with locations in Reston, VA, Santa Clara, CA, Lehi, UT, London, Tokyo and Hyderabad. DG operates in more than 60 countries while servicing seven of the top ten global patent holders and include seven of the ten largest global automobile manufacturers as our customers!</p>
<p><b>
Your responsibilities will include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify process improvements, AWS cost reductions and DevOps automation opportunities that will improve MSP margin and productivity.</li>
<li>Standardize base offering and optional add-ons. Determine which capabilities should be unique to the service, vs. which should be a base product capability.</li>
<li>Create customer success measurements and identify improvements to optimize customer outcomes.</li>
<li>Understand customer value drivers and recommend changes to better align MSP resources to deliver value and drive incremental ARR.</li>
<li>Work with MSP Operations Team, Sales and Marketing to structure product and service pricing.</li>
<li>Perform or coordinate market/product research to gain insight into industry and market needs, competitive advantage and product perception.</li>
<li>Stay abreast of industry and market developments to maintain product/service competitive advantage.</li>
<li>Develop and/or improve product positioning and literature, including product descriptions and white papers, to shape market perception.</li>
<li>Provide support to the sales force by communicating product strategies and performing sales training, if necessary.</li>
<li>Participate in sales calls with strategic customers. Brief strategic accounts as necessary. Work closely with renewals team to secure MSP contract renewals.</li>
<li>Funnel new product requirements to other members of the product management team and work closely with MSP Operations as the “early adopter” for those features.</li>
</ul>
