Position Overview:<br>
<br>
Siemens is seeking an Associate Project Manager to support our construction business in the Tampa Area. This role is responsible for planning, directing, coordinating, and budgeting activities and financial reporting relating to new onstruction or big retrofit projects. The Associate Project Manager participates in the conceptual development of a project and oversees its organization, scheduling, and implementation.<br>

Responsibilities:<br>
<br>
While under the supervision of the Operations Manager, the Associate Project Manager must be able to effectively and accurately perform the following duties:<br>
<ul>
<li>Work with external parties and customers’ Superintendents, Foremen, and Journeymen to plan, organize, and direct activities concerned with assigned projects.</li>
<li>Establish project objectives, policies, procedures, and performance standards within boundaries of company policy and contract specifications. Confer with engineering and specialists’ supervisors to discuss such matters as work procedures, scope, and scheduling, as well as work sequence, project-specific safety plans, etc.</li>
<li>Initiate and maintain relationships with owners and other contacts to facilitate project activities.</li>
<li>Monitor and control project through administrative direction of on-site union personnel to ensure project is completed on schedule and within budget.</li>
<li>Investigate potentially serious situations and implement corrective measures.</li>
<li>Represent the company in project meetings and attend strategy meetings.</li>
<li>Work with our internal Financial Business Administration (FBA) team to manage financial aspects of contracts to protect company’s interest and simultaneously maintain good relationships with the customers.</li>
<li>Manage the requisition of supplies and materials to complete project.</li>
<li>Interpret and explain plans and contract terms to administrative staff, workers, and clients.</li>
<li>Formulate reports on work progress, costs, and scheduling.</li>
<li>Work with engineers to produce shop drawings and all project deliverables.</li>
<li>Manage projects to ensure the established budgets are met and ensure that products are manufactured to customer specifications.</li>
<li>Communicate the company's core value of safety and project-specific safety requirements, goals, and objectives.</li>
<li>Develop project schedules in collaboration with the Superintendent and in compliance with customer requirements, deadlines, and/or milestones; develop mechanisms to track schedule progress in the field and update the schedule as needed.</li>
<li>Collaborate with our sales team and the customers to identify any variances from the baseline and determine appropriate course of action to remedy any schedule slippage.</li>
<li>Communicate schedule updates and any schedule slippage remedies to the customers and gain remedy approvals including any compensation component.</li>
<li>Provide forecasts to the customer and participate in forecast/budget meetings as required.</li>
<li>Develop project budget based on the estimate/proposal including the budget breakdown by task and appropriate tracking units; develop cost codes for tasks/units.</li>
<li>Run reports on projects, tracking cost and billing.</li>
<li>Collaborate with our Project Procurement Team to manage RFPs for subcontractors, review subcontractor proposals for completeness and scope coverage, and furnish information to complete subcontract agreements including scope of work, pricing, and customer-specific requirements.</li>
<li>Maintain open communication with the customer and discuss potential future projects.</li>
<li>Negotiate and approve subcontract scope changes in accordance with all customer contract requirements (the prime contract conditions which flow though the subcontract).</li>
<li>Break down units and complete take-offs.</li>
<li>Manage Profit &amp; Loss in assigned projects and monthly financial review meetings.</li>
<li>Perform accurate estimates.</li>
<li>Participate in department meetings as requested.</li>
<li>Perform additional assignments as requested and maintain a teamwork attitude.</li>
</ul>
