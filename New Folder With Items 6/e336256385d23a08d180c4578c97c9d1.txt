<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>1. Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>The SDR (Sales Development Representative) is responsible for filling the top of the outbound funnel:</p>
<p>Research potential clients based on our ICPs (Ideal Customer Profile)</p>
<ul>
<li>Connect with and educate prospects on our technology outsourcing services</li>
<li>Act as the subject matter expert on our offerings/solutions</li>
<li>Respond, engage and qualify leads before handing them off to the Account Executive</li>
<li>Schedule appointments and demos for Account Executive</li>
</ul>
<p><b>2. Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive, Grit, Team Oriented: proven record of strong desire to compete and win within a team.</li>
<li>Profound desire to continuously learn and strong capacity to be coached</li>
<li>Capability of understanding customer pain points, requirements and connecting potential business to services that can be provided by our team.</li>
<li>Profound integrity and impeccable work ethic</li>
<li>Strong and professional communication skills -- written, verbal, presentation</li>
<li>Aptitude to manage numerous requests and time demands concurrently, while achieving production goals from assigned territory</li>
<li>Promotes a strong sense of urgency for reaching goals and key deliverables. Acts without being told what to do.</li>
<li>Some experience is a plus with sales tools such as: Salesforce, LinkedIn, Tableau, Google Apps, Microsoft Office, DiscoverOrg, Marketo.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>3. Training</b></p>
<p>You will receive training and learn about:</p>
<ul>
<li>Our Software Development as Service solution for SaaS, e-commerce and web platforms. You will visit our operations in Romania.</li>
<li>Our other services: #1 Digital Product Innovation, #2 User Experience as a Service, #3 Growth Marketing as a Service.</li>
<li>Salesforce and Marketo</li>
<li>Predictable Sales Funnel</li>
</ul>
