<div>The Role</div>
<div>
</div>
<p>The <b>Data Acquisition (DAQ), Control, and Real-Time Test Applications Engineering Team </b>in the Americas Support organization has an immediate opening for a <b>Senior Condition Monitoring Applications Engineer</b>. Applications Engineering is a highly collaborative role, working with business owners in Sales, the Business Units, Product Planning, Product R&amp;D, and Marketing to drive account growth and proactively invest in new industries and applications. Applications Engineering is the ideal career for engineers who enjoy working with customers in a technical role while continuing to grow as technical and business leaders.
</p>
<div></div>
<div>Key Responsibilities</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>NI Condition Monitoring Applications Engineers spend most of their time:</div>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li> Consulting with customers as they collect, interpret and refine their technical requirements</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li> Supporting customers during specification and design of their hardware and software architectures</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li> Identifying and mitigating high risk items throughout the customer engagement process</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li> Collaborating with strategy and product owners to proactively invest in condition monitoring application areas</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li> Partnering with Sales and Business Development Managers on account strategies and planning to drive revenue growth</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li> Providing customer feedback to influencers throughout the company</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li> Coaching and developing peers in application knowledge and best practices</li></ul>

</div>
