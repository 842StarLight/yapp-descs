<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>RB American Group</b></h3>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Area Director</b></h3>
<p>
REPORTS TO: Director of Operations</p>
<p>
Each Arby's Area Director will be responsible for ensuring that all the units in the district achieve excellent performance by reaching and maintaining agreed-upon objectives. An Area Director's responsibilities include:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
P &amp; L ACCOUNTABILITY</b></p>
<p>
Maintains profitability of units by monitoring performance, forecasting expenses, planning for and authorizing expenditures, controlling costs and reviewing results with unit managers.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Coaches unit manager to recognize variances.</li>
<li>
Monitors financial performance and assists unit managers in the development of corrective action plans.</li>
<li>
Assists unit managers in developing financial and sales forecasts.</li>
<li>
Monitors compliance with inventory controls and assists with days on hand analysis.</li>
<li>
Authorizes expenditures within policy limits.</li>
<li>
Reviews daily, weekly and monthly reporting and reacts to the needs of the district.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
RESTAURANT OPERATIONS</b></p>
<p>
Enforces consistent company standards, systems and procedures; evaluates product quality; implements new systems; oversees the delivery of positive customer service; supervises new product introductions and monitors unit management activities.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Enforces quality, service, cleanliness and safety standards.</li>
<li>
Assures unit managers and employees understand recipes and production procedures.</li>
<li>
Monitors the effective use of our scheduling tool and maintains a full staff of quality EEs.</li>
<li>
Coaches unit managers in recognizing and solving operational issues.</li>
<li>
Supervises the development and implementation of unit operational projects focused on addressing operational deficiencies.</li>
<li>
Supervises the gathering of operational data for review and analysis.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
MARKETING PROMOTIONS</b></p>
<p>
Follows marketing and sales promotion plans, prepares units for promotional programs and encourages collection of information on customers and the competitive market.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Supervises new product introduction and EE training.</li>
<li>
Develops unit managers' awareness of customer preferences.</li>
<li>
Monitors in-store advertising programs and promotional plans.</li>
<li>
Works with marketing team on any LSM opportunities.</li>
<li>
Helps unit managers develop community relations programs.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
FACILITIES AND SAFETY MANAGEMENT</b></p>
<p>
Supervises the overall condition of unit facilities to ensure operational acceptability and competitive readiness and establishes safety management programs.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Monitors security and safety procedures.</li>
<li>
Supervises preventive maintenance programs.</li>
<li>
Coaches unit managers in recognizing facilities and safety issues.</li>
<li>
Approves low-cost improvements to facilities and recommends more costly improvements.</li>
<li>
Manages and controls repair and maintenance budget within district.</li>
<li>
Conducts cost benefit analysis for repair and maintenance proposals.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
HUMAN RESOURCE MANAGEMENT</b></p>
<p>
Supervises effective employment orientation, training and management of employees. Teaches unit managers how to manage people. Provides quality feedback and develops promotable managers.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Serves as a resource and provides feedback to unit managers.</li>
<li>
Supervises the execution and implementation of in-unit managers.</li>
<li>
Coaches and motivates both managers and employees and takes disciplinary action when necessary.</li>
<li>
Identifies and prepares subordinates for promotion.</li>
<li>
Supervises and conducts formal management performance evaluations.</li>
<li>
Models effective supervisory behavior.</li>
<li>
Reinforces and rewards the training and development of employees.</li>
<li>
Oversees district-wide analysis of personnel needs and develops staffing plans with individual unit managers.</li>
<li>
Ensures personnel practices are in compliance with all regulations.</li>
<li>
Active in recruiting for management and crew.</li>
<li>
Responsible to ensure that staffing/recruiting needs for each restaurant are met.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
LEGAL RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>
Ensures compliance with all applicable federal, state, and local regulations.</p>
