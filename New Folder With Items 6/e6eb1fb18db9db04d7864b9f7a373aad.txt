<h2>Information Systems Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Aureus Group has partnered with a large, well-established, and growing organization to find a Information Systems Analyst to join their team in Grand Island, Nebraska. The Information Systems Analyst will primarily be responsible for enterprise-level software systems such as incident management, loyalty, retail pricing, and perpetual inventory. This position will require enhancing, maintaining, and supporting vendor-purchased and internally developed applications.<br>
<br>
Duties &amp; Responsibilities of the Information Systems Analyst:<br>
<ul>
<li>Recommend, schedule, and perform application improvements, upgrades, and repairs.</li>
<li>Deploy new applications and enhancements, including planning, configuration, testing, and support.</li>
<li>Design and support system APIs and integrations.</li>
<li>Keep all information and data confidential.</li>
<li>Work closely with the Service Desk and communicate directly with business unit leaders.</li>
<li>Enforce all company software and hardware standards.</li>
<li>Develop and deliver reports in support of business objectives and trends.</li>
<li>Support development and implementation of new enterprise-level software systems and suites.</li>
<li>Document project plans, business requirements, functional diagrams, test scripts, and troubleshooting guides.</li>
<li>Conduct research, evaluate, and recommend software products for purchase.</li>
<li>Comply with all Department and Company Policies.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Education &amp; Experience of the Information Systems Analyst:<br>
<ul>
<li>Experience with NCR StorePoint HQC or other corporate pricing and point of sale systems.</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of Microsoft SQL Server and related services.</li>
<li>College diploma or university degree, preferably in an information systems field and/or 2 years equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>Experience with Cherwell Incident Management or other service management systems.</li>
<li>Administration experience with iManage document management or similar systems.</li>
<li>Experience with the MaddenCo Tire Dealer System.</li>
<li>Experience with web services, APIs, and system integrations.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of customer loyalty systems.</li>
<li>Technical knowledge of perpetual inventory systems and processes.</li>
<li>Hands-on with business intelligence, analytics, or other reporting systems.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Minimum Requirements of the Information Systems Analyst:<br>
<ul>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of standard business, accounting, and inventory management principles.</li>
<li>Experience or training in administering service management platforms.</li>
<li>Working technical knowledge of current network protocols, operating systems, and standards, including TCP/IP.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively prioritize and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment.</li>
<li>Extensive support experience with Windows-based servers.</li>
<li>Excellent technical knowledge of software design and architecture.</li>
<li>Knowledge of database engines and design principles.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team-oriented, collaborative environment.</li>
<li>Ability to identify, create, and document user procedures.</li>
<li>Ability to read and understand technical manuals, procedural documentation, and OEM guides.</li>
</ul>
<br>

</ul>
