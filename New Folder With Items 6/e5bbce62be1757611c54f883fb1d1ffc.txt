<h2>Estimator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Gravitec Systems, Inc. has been an industry leader in the field of fall protection for over 30 years. Working with owners, architects, and contractors, we provide innovative turn-key engineering and training solutions for our clients' fall protection needs worldwide.</p>
<p><b>Join us!</b></p>
<p>We are looking for an Estimator to join our Engineering Division. This position will primarily focus on complete conceptual estimating and total bid pricing for engineering projects.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare estimates and bids based on plans and specifications.</li>
<li>Develop conceptual estimates when information is limited.</li>
<li>Identify labor, material and time requirements.</li>
<li>Compute cost by analyzing labor, materials and time requirements.</li>
<li>Proficient in reading A/E drawings and specifications.</li>
<li>Full cycle estimate/bid management.</li>
<li>Assist with maintaining integrity of contacts in CRM.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain estimate/bid standards.</li>
<li>Provide change request pricing as needed to support Project Managers and Engineers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 years of recent construction estimating experience and/or project management.</li>
<li>Ability to develop and maintain strong working relationships with project consultants.</li>
<li>Proficient working knowledge with Microsoft Office Suite and construction estimating software.</li>
<li>Strong time management skills, ability to operate with a sense of urgency.</li>
<li>Strong organizational and mathematical skills.</li>
<li>Above average written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure with a calm demeanor.</li>
<li>Ability to plan ahead and forecast.</li>
<li>Previous experience with fall protection a plus but not required.</li>
<li>Prior experience with SAP a plus.</li>
<li>Must be able to pass drug screening.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive Salary</li>
<li>Medical / Dental / Vision / Life Insurance</li>
<li>Paid vacation, sick time and holidays.</li>
<li>401(k) savings opportunity.</li>
</ul>
<p>Reports to: Engineering Sales Supervisor</p>
<p>FLSA Status: Non-Exempt / Full Time</p>
