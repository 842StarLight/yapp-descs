<h2>Receptionist - Front Desk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Safran </b>is an international high-technology group, operating in the aircraft propulsion and equipment, space and defense markets. Safran has a global presence, with more than 58,000 employees and sales of 16.5 billion euros in 2017. Working alone or in partnership, Safran holds world or European leadership positions in its core markets. Safran is listed on the Euronext Paris stock exchange, and is part of the CAC 40 and Euro Stoxx 50 indices.
</p>
<p>In February 2018, Safran took control of Zodiac Aerospace, significantly expanding its aircraft equipment activities. Together with Zodiac Aerospace, Safran has more than 91,000 employees and would have around EUR 21 billion in adjusted revenue (pro forma 2016).
</p>
<p><b>Safran </b>is ranked among the Top 100 Global Innovators by Thomson Reuters and is featured on the "Happy at work" rankings. The Group places fourth on the Universum ranking for the favorite companies of newly-qualified engineers in France.
</p>
<p><b>Safran Seats </b>is a world leader in aircraft passenger and crew seats. One million seats made by the company are now in service worldwide.
</p>
<p><b>Occupational Summary: </b><b>.<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Receptionist is the first impression for Safran Seats USA. The Receptionist welcomes visitors by greeting them, in person or on the phone. The Receptionist coordinates front-desk activities, including distributing correspondence and redirecting phone calls. The Receptionistâ€™s duties include offering administrative support across the organization. To be successful as a Receptionist, you should have a pleasant personality, as this is also a customer service role.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities (essential functions):<br>
</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Enforces Visitor Policy
</li>
<li>Continuously monitor visitor requests from internal employees.
</li>
<li>Pre-Screen pending visitors using Export on Demand/Amber Road.
</li>
<li>Update and continuously maintain Master Visitor Calendar.
</li>
<li>Sign in all visitors in Raptor upon arrival and connect with hosting employee.
</li>
<li>Courteously and promptly answers multi-line telephones and directs calls to proper extensions.
</li>
<li>Enter Terminated employees into Raptor
</li>
<li>Update and maintain internal directory
</li>
<li>Sorts and routes incoming/outgoing mail.
</li>
<li>Keeps a clean and neat work area.
</li>
<li>Maintains refreshments and keeps lobby area in professional and clean appearance.
</li>
<li>Maintains meeting room areas which may include stocking and cleaning after Executive Meetings
</li>
<li>Responsible for ordering office name plates and managing standard formats.
</li>
<li>Responsible for ordering business cards and managing standard templates.
</li>
<li>Order Office Supplies for Executive Floor
</li>
<li>Maintain and Control Company Vehicle Check In/Out Log
</li>
<li>Assists with company event coordination.
</li>
<li>Provides general administrative and clerical support.
</li>
<li>Assists other departments when needed.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.<br>
</li>
</ul>
