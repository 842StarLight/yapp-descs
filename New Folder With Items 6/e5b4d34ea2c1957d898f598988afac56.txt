<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>As a Cargo Claims Intern, you’ll learning from industry professionals to gain an understanding of the workings of cargo claims and its role within the transportation industry. You will gain experience in communicating issues in a professional manor while sharpening your critical thinking and problem-solving skills. You will also be given special short-term projects to be completed during your tenure.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>The successful candidate for this role will:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Train and work with our Cargo Claims team daily
</li>
<li>Communicate issues with internal and external customers
</li>
<li>Gain a broad overview of transportation industry
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal internship candidate will:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Be a Junior or Senior currently enrolled in an accredited institution
</li>
<li>Possess strong written and oral communication skills
</li>
<li>Be proficient in business-related computing applications (e.g. Microsoft Office applications), particularly Excel
</li>
<li>Have the ability to work in teams or individually
</li>
<li>Be available for 20-25 hours per week
</li>
</ul>
