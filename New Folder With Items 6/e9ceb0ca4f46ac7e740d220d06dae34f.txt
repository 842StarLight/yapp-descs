<div><b>Global Distribution Manager</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Position Overview:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This position is responsible for Entrust Datacard’s Global Distribution Center network that includes Hubs in Europe, India, Hong Kong, Australia, Brazil and Japan. The Manager of Global Distribution will work closely with Senior Level Regional Sales leaders around the world to ensure seamless Entrust Datacard customer order fulfillment. This role will partner with Customer Service Operations, Purchasing/Planning and Global 3PL partners to optimize fulfillment rates and reduce global warehousing cost all while increasing customer satisfaction across the network. This position will also assist with implementations of any new Distribution Hubs around the world.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Develop Global Distribution Hub operational objectives and execute plans to achieve</li>
<li>
Establish and achieve Distribution Hub budgets and KPI metrics</li>
<li>
Manage network of Global 3PL teams that operate EDC Distribution Centers located in Hong Kong, Europe, India, Australia, Brazil and potentially Japan</li>
<li>
Responsible for Inventory Control and accuracy for all Outside US Warehousing operations
</li>
<li>Utilize data analysis and KPI’s to maintain world class order fulfillment from the Global DC’s
</li>
<li>Using data analytics, looking for opportunities to decrease warehouse cycle times and cost reductions and improve quality</li>
<li>
Work closely with Global Customer Service Operations and Supply Chain to ensure customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Assist global 3PL Hub service provider selections through KPI metrics, RFP’s, RFQ’s and data analysis</li>
<li>
Assists in cross functional project teams during implementation of new 3PL Hub locations</li>
<li>
Leads Distribution Center continuous improvement projects using Lean Six Sigma methodology</li>
<li>
Organizes and runs quarterly business reviews with 3PL and Entrust Datacard teams</li>
<li>
Acts as primary liaison with Finance for year-end wall to wall physicals as needed and inventory auditing</li>
<li>
Work closely with global Transportation and Trade Compliance leaders to improve overall customer order cycle time</li>
<li>
Implement and manage all Value-Add or postponement activities that take place in the Distribution Centers</li>
<li>
Monitor VAT process as applicable at the Distribution Hubs</li>
</ul>
