<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The Retail Manager is responsible for overseeing the coordinated operation of all retail functions and retail locations for the Council (including but not limited to ecommerce site, mobile shop events, supporting resident camp trading post functions, etc.) across the nine counties we serve. This position works collaboratively with the Director of Retail and Customer Care to establish sales goals, develop marketing plans, and ensure strategic goals are met.
</p>
<p>This results-driven leader monitors and maximizes operational performance, develops financial (sales, inventory, etc.) projections, analyzes operational reports, developments and implements business strategies, aims to maximize sales opportunities wherever possible and motivates team members to set and achieve collective retail sales goals.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Retail Operations:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Oversee all retail operations processes and procedures; ensure appropriate standards, including those relating to customer experience, are outlined and manage the execution thereof;</li>
<li>
Define and communicate customer standards; set goals and benchmark results;</li>
<li>
Partner with Marketing Department and Director of Retail and Customer Care to create annual marketing plan, effectively utilize social media and mass marketing communications, and help develop and implement successful advertising and promotional campaigns;</li>
<li>
Develop and deploy inventory control system, conduct periodic and annual inventories, and maintain accurate inventory controls to meet fluctuating needs of Council and customers;</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Establish high-level of proficiency and comfort utilizing Retail IT systems, including implementation and troubleshooting of the RMS Point of Sale, RMS Manager, OpSuite and NetSuite, etc.;</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Council leadership and retail team management to define, develop, communicate, set and track retail sales goals.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Customer Service:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide quality customer service to internal and external customers, resolve concerns in a timely manner;</li>
<li>
Oversee customer escalation process and quality control by managing complex and escalated customer service questions.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Management:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Plan, prioritize, and delegate work tasks effectively to manage workflow across retail team;</li>
<li>
Train, coach, schedule and supervise retail staff;</li>
<li>
Manage team customer service functionality in Salesforce system, appropriately assigning, tracking and documenting incoming communications in accordance with GSWNY and GSYSA policies and procedures;</li>
<li>
Monitor accuracy of all reporting and database information;</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Support the Council’s strategic goals and mission as an active and engaged member of the GSWNY team.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Other duties may be assigned as needed. GSWNY maintains the right to add, change or alter the functions of this at any time.<br>
