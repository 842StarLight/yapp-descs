<h2>Renewals Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Role:</b> Renewals Specialist</div>
<div>
<b>Location:</b> Minneapolis, MN</div>
<div></div>
<div>Perforce develops DevOps tools that improve software quality and security as well as team productivity for several of the world’s leading companies, such as PIXAR, CD Projekt Red, NASA, Verizon, Honda, NVIDIA, and Johns Hopkins.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position Summary:</b></div>
The Renewals Specialist is a key member of the Sales Operations team and is responsible for managing all aspects of the annual renewal process. The position focuses on serving and retaining SLA’s for an existing customer base. The position is primarily responsible for managing the renewal process for an assigned sales territory as well as license management, quoting, order fulfillment, post-sales activities, and maintaining the customer database.
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Owns the renewal opportunity sales cycle from quote to close, including but not limited to:
<ul>
<li>Proactively contacting customers in the interest of selling the value of our products and services surrounding support and maintenance renewals.</li>
<li>Processing orders from sales staff of direct customer contact.</li>
<li>Giving price quotations, completing order sheets and verifying order information.</li>
<li>Responding to customer inquiries using established procedures and maintaining the customer database.</li>
<li>Providing customers with a personalized level of service that reinforces the importance of Perforce customer satisfaction.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Regularly provides detailed information to other departments such as order status, revenue recognition dates, pricing, etc.</li>
<li>Supports the sales team in managing the renewal process and assists with operational duties.</li>
<li>Appropriately ascertains and incorporates up-to-date client delivery schedules and operational changes into revenue forecasts and revenue timelines.</li>
<li>Promotes company products and/or services via telephone and email to existing customers and prospects.</li>
<li>Directly contributes to the sales region by closing sales and securing renewals for their designated accounts.</li>
<li>Meets quarterly productivity requirements as outlined in renewals forecast and maintains pipeline renewal opportunities.</li>
<li>Uses in depth knowledge of the organizations’ various products and services to sell Perforce support to customers.</li>
<li>Educates and informs customers on Perforce’s business practices and associated contractual obligations and implications.</li>
<li>Maintains an effective relationship with account team and transfers leads as appropriate.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<div><b>Requirements:</b></div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree, preferably in Business Administration, or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Previous software or technology experience</li>
<li>Two or more years in a customer service support role or order processing environment.</li>
<li>CRM experience preferred, Salesforce experience highly desirable.</li>
<li>Exception customer service and diplomacy skills.</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage a large case load and multi-task.</li>
<li>Outstanding abilities in the execution of sales strategies.</li>
</ul>
