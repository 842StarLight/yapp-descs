<p>Job Profile: <b>Account Executive
</b><br>
Job Title: <b>Advertising Specialist
</b></p>
<p>Vertical: Internet Marketing
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Ads Specialist is responsible for solving incoming ads tickets, fielding ads calls and communicating with customers on updates, changes &amp; advertising issues. This role also assists in work overflow and internal 97 Display advertising support while contributing to department projects with help from the Advertising director.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee Zendesk and gathering/delegating/completion of all tickets in a timely manner (12 business hours). Oversee delegation of tickets to interns, development team and graphic designer- ensures completion.
</li>
<li>Create and manage Facebook and Google advertising campaigns.
</li>
<li>Oversee phone lines and returning correspondence.
</li>
<li>Continuous research on best practices to create B2B sales opportunities.
</li>
<li>Finds creative solutions for client desires/needs
</li>
<li>Troubleshoot client issues and resolves in a timely manner<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB QUALIFICATIONS:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Bachelor’s Degree (related work experience can be substituted for educational requirement), Marketing background preferred.
</p></li>
<li><p>1 Year of Facebook Advertising and/or Google AdWords experience
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience in digital marketing, such as marketing automation, SEO, Google Analytics
</p></li>
<li><p>Demonstrated ability to manage various projects with strong attention to detail
</p></li>
<li><p>Interpersonal Skills - Ability to communicate effectively and in a professional manner with clients and co-workers
</p></li>
<li><p>Self-motivated - Able to independently solve problems, set and monitor personal performance goals, and work with little or no supervision
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to maintain a positive attitude while operating under pressure and in a fast paced environment
</p></li>
<li><p>Analytical thinker with very strong organizational, problem solving, and decision-making skills
</p></li>
<li><p>Customer focused – Acting with customer in mind, establishing and maintaining relations, gaining trust and respect through proactive efforts
</p></li>
<li><p>Possesses a drive for results and is constantly working to find new, innovative solutions to problems
</p></li>
<li><p>Excellent written and verbal communication skills - Effectively convey messages with desired results
</p></li>
<li><p>Google Certified - Pass Google AdWords Exam after 90 days of employment<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
