<h2>Director of Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Our client, a multi-state Cannabis operator with assets in Michigan, Illinois, Massachusetts and Ohio, is looking for a Director of Operations to oversee the seed to sale operations of their Cultivation and Manufacturing facility in Lansing, MI.
<br><br>
They are looking to build a world class team of people who are inspired by their vision and looking to take a leadership role with them as they expand nationally.
<br><br>
The Director of Operations is expected to execute the following roles and responsibilities in order to deliver consistent, positive, industry leading customer experiences and drive profitable top line sales.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Build Talent Attract, hire, develop, inspire and retain a team of 25+ employees in all verticals of the industry.</li>
<li>Drive Operational Excellence Analyze the business and create / communicate clear action plans that optimize results and ensure effective execution of all operations activities.</li>
<li>Drive Compliance Lead a team that adheres to all cannabis state-specific rules, regulations and laws.</li>
</ul>
Reporting directly to the CPO, the Director of Operations will be responsible for leading all of the Facility Operational Departments and its Managers, policing cross department communication, meeting company client deliveries, and upholding key compliance functions. The Director of Operations must have proven experience, be polished, organized, accountable, and have high attention to detail.
<br><br>
<b>Job Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage Departmental Teams Directly responsible for managing, overseeing, administering, policing, and controlling departmental heads and departments on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Facility Activity Reporting - Manage, update and report daily facility activity using Google Sheets reporting system; this includes cannabis and non-cannabis inventory levels.</li>
<li>Auditing Logs Deliveries for Accuracy - Lead daily auditing practices, enforce team in tracking reporting activity.</li>
<li>Coordination with Departmental Teams Facilitate the operational flow across departments with product development, orders, production, compliance, distribution, accounting and inventory divisions.</li>
<li>Facility Systems Management - Police facility systems and maintain a controlled setting.</li>
<li>Facility Advancement Lead the Facility Advancement, be accountable for the department growth, recommend software solutions innovations, design SOPs, methods, policies procedures to advance the department to ensure it meets the needs of the company. Identify cost-effective strategies and efficiencies to improve the department. and maintain trust relationships</li>
<li>Report to Senior Executive Team Report to Senior management on a daily, weekly and monthly basis communicating the status of the Inventory Department ensuring business objectives are met.</li>
</ul>
<b>Education and Experience Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree required, MBA a plus</li>
<li>5-10 years proven experience working in a comparable facility and/or setting in a highly regulated industry. (Food, CPG, or Pharma)</li>
<li>5-10 years proven experience working as a Director or Manager of operations in a manufacturing, packaging and/or cultivation company.</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of cannabis products is required</li>
<li>Must Pass DHS Live Scan</li>
<li>Proficient with Google Suite - Drive, Sheets, Gmail, etc.</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office - Word, Excel is a must</li>
<li>Effective departmental leadership skills</li>
<li>Strong focus on efficiency and effectiveness</li>
<li>Analytical mind with strong math skills</li>
<li>Reliable and trustworthy</li>
</ul>
<b>Compensation:</b> $100-120K Based on Experience
