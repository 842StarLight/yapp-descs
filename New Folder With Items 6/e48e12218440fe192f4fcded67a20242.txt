<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities (Other duties may be assigned.)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the start-up, development, and coordination of local programs for a specific portfolio of Spirits. Is responsible for working closely with market Sales Management including market VPs and Division Managers.</li>
<li>Develop incentive plans utilizing the state and local incentive budgets to ensure local market sales and profit objectives are maximized. Review and approve local market incentive payouts to ensure accuracy and effectiveness. Manages local accruals for incentives and credits.</li>
<li>Prepare, distribute, and execute all organizational aspects of VAPs, gift sets, and special orders for the local market.</li>
<li>Responsible for the coordination and time of all special packaging for warehouse management.</li>
<li>Updates all sales personnel to applicable marketing directions from suppliers. Submits updates on all current programs to sales management.</li>
<li>Obligates company funds within the guidelines of the department budget. Programs all brands in the local market within the expenditures afforded to the department. Responsible for all expenditures pertaining to sales contests and the Marketing Department.</li>
<li>Educates sales teams on brand-specific opportunities, marketing activities, and sales levers.</li>
<li>Responsible for local inventory forecasting with a monthly trend analysis with reference to incentives, fiscal goals, and yearly goals. Provides daily communication with statewide marketing/purchasing/sales department. Inventory Management – provides weekly analysis including communication on velocity to marketing, purchasing and sales. Handles transfer requests from other markets and recommends strategy to executive staff on inventory levels.</li>
<li>Build local market business plan with suppliers and ensure communication and execution within local sales team</li>
<li>Ensures that details, goals, and profitability align with focus of market on incentives before sending to VP for approval.</li>
<li>Manages local market special pricing with VP and negotiates depletion allowance support with suppliers. Makes recommendations on executive programs with statewide marketing and market VP.</li>
<li>Reviews and analyzes brands for opportunities of sales and profits. Creates and maintains local marketing calendar for major brands in regards to surveys, pricing, incentives and special market conditions. Attends supplier reviews.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of supplier funds and balances.</li>
<li>Possession of and operating a motor vehicle to access customer accounts for delivery, promotion, and efficient and prompt sales and other key activities.</li>
</ul>
