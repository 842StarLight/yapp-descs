<p>
KPMG is currently seeking an Alliance Director I Oracle to join our Business Development Organization.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Oversee the day to day alliance relationship and activities with Oracle; direct the alliance management, alliance enablement, and co-sell pipeline, and support the solution development, sales, and marketing required by the alliance</li>
<li>
Manage the firm's participation in Oracle's formal program; including training, certifications, awards, software usage rights, opportunity registration, conferences (Open World, Collaborate, and Modern Business) and joint marketing</li>
<li>
Lead development, management and approval of a joint business plans, support risk and legal contracts, and gain commitment from interested and affected individuals; provide the service networks with access to product management, development, support and training resources from Oracle</li>
<li>
Educate and align Oracle field sellers / marketers with KPMG's Oracle practice and sales teams</li>
<li>
Provide stakeholders with updates on Oracle's organization and manage the firm's alliance relationship mapping and drive progress on multiple leads; thereby increasing the size of the Alliance Influenced Pipeline</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of seven years of experience in services or consultative business development with at least two years of experience managing a strategic alliance/sales with Oracle</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree from an accredited college/university in Business or a related field or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in building and nurturing win-win alliance relationships; Strong networking and influence management skills that translate into building commitment and driving actions across organizational boundaries</li>
<li>
Highly credible communicator with excellent organization and project management skills and track record of delivering results</li>
<li>
Travel up to forty percent</li>
<li>
Applicants must be currently authorized to work in the United States without the need for visa sponsorship now or in the future</li>
</ul>
