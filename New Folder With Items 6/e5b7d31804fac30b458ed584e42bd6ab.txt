<p>POSITION SUMMARY
<br>
Reporting to the Chief Financial Officer, the Finance &amp; Accounting Manager will oversee and manage the day-to-day accounting functions and financial reporting of the Business Office. The accounting functions of the Business Office include the areas of accounts payable, cash disbursements, payroll, accounts receivable, billing, cash receipts, and general ledger. S/he will also partner with the Chief Financial Officer on accomplishing the goals of Village Shalom.</p>

<p>PRIMARY/ESSENTIAL DUTIES
<br>
1. Oversee staff in Business Office including managing day-to-day accounting functions, hiring, training, annual reviews, and employee relations and recognition. Meets with staff on an ongoing basis to review performance and provide feedback and guidance.
<br>
2. Ensure timely and accurate reporting of all financial information, including monthly financial package and variance analysis.
<br>
3. Be familiar with Medicare, Medicaid and other third party billing, reimbursement, and regulatory guidelines.
<br>
4. Maintenance and oversight of development functions with respect to proper classification, and timing and release of temporarily restricted.
<br>
5. Plan, coordinate and oversee the year-end financial and 403(b) audits, completion and filing of required sales tax, use tax, payroll and Federal tax returns, and Medicare/Medicaid cost report preparation.
<br>
6. Assist the Chief Financial Officer in coordinating and managing the annual Operating and Capital Budget process in cooperation with management staff.
<br>
7. Maintenance and oversight of The Assistance Plan (TAP) program in accordance with established guidelines.
<br>
8. Develops &amp; implements departmental policies &amp; procedures.
<br>
9. Evaluate and audit accounting and internal control systems on an ongoing basis to ensure the integrity of financial reporting and to ensure performance in accordance with policy, procedures and established internal control structure.
<br>
10. Assists with special projects as required by Chief Financial Officer.
<br>
11. Participates in applicable committees and meetings as required.
<br>
12. Completes in-service requirements annually.</p>
