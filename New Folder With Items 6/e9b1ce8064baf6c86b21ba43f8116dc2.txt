<h2>Exhibits Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The American Urological Association (AUA), the premier urologic association providing support to the urologic community, is currently searching for an energetic, detail-oriented candidate for our Exhibits Coordinator position in the Industry Relations department.</p>
<p><b>In General</b></p>
<p>This position coordinates all projects and activities related to our annual meeting exhibition as well as year-round educational course exhibits. A heavy focus on marketing and sales strategies.</p>
<p><b>Typically</b></p>
<p>The individual in this position will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides, develops and improves customer services and information for annual meeting exhibitors.</li>
<li>Plays an active role in obtaining industry prospects while qualifying sales leads for updates to our exhibitor contacts database.</li>
<li>In conjunction with the Exhibits Manager and the Convention Operations Manager, develops and updates the Exhibitor Service Kit, Exhibitor Directory, Exhibitor Prospectus, internal sales reports, exhibitor bulletins and other assigned communications Provides administrative, technical and computer support for department using Personify, a2z, ZoHo and other software.</li>
<li>Assists exhibitors in connecting with AUA exhibit vendors to secure services.
</li>
<li>Participates in Exhibitor Advisory Committee, open exhibitor meetings and future city site visits.</li>
<li>Oversees booking, collection and reporting of exhibit revenue to internal AUA stakeholders – Possibly in charge of booking and collections.</li>
<li>Works with Exhibits Manager and the Convention Operations Manager to provide any necessary Exhibitor material related to the Annual Meeting. (i.e. Exhibitor Service Kit, Prospectus, Exhibitor Short book, Exhibitor bag, etc.)</li>
<li>Coordinates development and placement of various Industry Relations and Exhibits on-site signage with GES vendor, Exhibits Manager and Convention Operations Manager</li>
<li>Collects company information for contact database as well as company descriptions in the Exhibitor Directory</li>
<li>Works with Exhibits Manager and Convention Operations Manager to create effective communications including monthly exhibitor bulletins designed to assist exhibitors with meeting deadlines, and making them aware of exhibitor services and advertising and sponsorship opportunities.</li>
<li>Works with Exhibits Manager and Convention Operations Manager to plan and execute the Exhibit Hall Networking Event. Coordinates effective marketing communications for the exhibit sales cycle for traditional exhibits as well as ExpoSuites, Skills Enhancement Workshops, Business Bistro and Skills Challenges.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>To be Successful</b></p>
<p>Candidates must be able to provide superior service delivery while working in a fast-paced, detail-oriented environment. It is essential that the individual in this role have strong organizational, database and communication skills. In addition:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree required or equivalent experience</li>
<li>2-4 years of exhibit and customer service support</li>
<li>Marketing experience preferred but not required</li>
<li>Strong written, verbal, and interpersonal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to organize and prioritize work to complete multiple tasks on time</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, utilize available resources and demonstrate initiative</li>
<li>Budgeting and accounting knowledge preferred</li>
<li>Excellent written and communication skills</li>
<li>Strong word processing and database skills including use of Microsoft Office software required: Excel, PowerPoint and Word (intermediate level)</li>
<li>Travel required for 7 to 8 days for Annual Meeting</li>
<li>Travel required periodically to accompany Exhibits Manager on site visits and/or other medical conferences</li>
<br>
</ul>
