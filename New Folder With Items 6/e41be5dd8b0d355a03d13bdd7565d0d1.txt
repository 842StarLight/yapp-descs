Healow Sales and Business Development Associate<br>
<br>
Position Overview:<br>
<br>
Responsible to develop business opportunities for patient engagement products by strategically presenting the products of Healow open access and Healow Televisits to identify and demonstrate value to current and potential clients<br>
Locations:<br>
Open to all offices<br>
<br>
Job Functions / Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Identify sales prospects and contact these and other leads as assigned</li>
<li>Work toward achieving weekly, monthly and yearly targets for leads generated and contracts signed
</li>
<li>Maintain, evaluate and update materials used for presentations</li>
<li>Prepare and perform presentations to executive members at enterprise practices in a group setting</li>
<li>Communicate new product and service opportunities and special developments</li>
<li>Develop an understanding of the products, issues and needs of practices and businesses based on specialty and geographical area(s) in order to provide feedback on how to better serve them
</li>
<li>Prepare action plans and schedules to identify specific targets and to project the number of contacts to be made</li>
</ul>
</div>

<li>Outgoing and persistent with client follow ups</li>
