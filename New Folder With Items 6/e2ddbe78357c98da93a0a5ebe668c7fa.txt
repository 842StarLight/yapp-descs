Position Overview</div>
<div>
This role is part of Retail/In-Venue Engineering team and responsible for deploying and supporting technology solutions to improve store operations and fan/customer experience. This individual will be execution and results focused, with emphasis on quality, on-time delivery, ability to structure complex work streams and work independently and with teams.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This role will have 1-2 direct reports.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Drive requirements, design, build, test, deploy, support for all software applications in retail domain.</li>
<li>Provide technical expertise in design, build and roll-out of new systems/enhancements/features/reports</li>
<li>Partner with Enterprise Architecture, Product Management and Business Stakeholders to implement technology solutions.</li>
<li>Define the processes needed to achieve and sustain operational excellence, including project delivery and systems reliability.</li>
<li>Manage vendor delivery, effort and cost estimation</li>
<li>Provide production support as needed</li>
<li>Issue, Change, and Risk Management</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Qualifications</div>
<div>
Bachelors degree and/or applicable experience in the information technology field.</div>
<ul>
<li>Sound understanding of Retail and Store processes preferably in Apparel/Footwear retail environment</li>
<li>Experience enabling omni-channel experiences like BOPIS</li>
<li>8+ years of experience in Technology. 4+ years of experience in retail technologies like Point Of Sale, Store back-office, Planogram, Reflexis Task Management, RFID. Experience with cloud-based POS systems like OpSuite will be a plus.</li>
<li>4+ years of hands-on experience in programming languages like Java, C#, Go, Node JS, Javascript, Python. AWS experience will be a plus.</li>
<li>4+ years of experience working with SQL or NoSQL database management systems like SQL Server, My SQL, Oracle, Mongo DB</li>
<li>3+ years of experience implementing large-scale, high availability systems</li>
<li>3+ years people-management experience</li>
<li>Experience with Saas-based POS systems</li>
<li>Good understanding of testing automation</li>
<li>Desire and aptitude to learn new technologies</li>
<li>Experience with Agile/Kanban methodology</li>
<li>Technical troubleshooting and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills with a high degree of professionalism in working collaboratively at all levels inside/outside the organization</li>
</ul>
