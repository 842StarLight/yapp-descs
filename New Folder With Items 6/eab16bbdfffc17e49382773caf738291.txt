<h2>Senior Financial Analyst I, Financial Reporting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>The Senior Financial Analyst I is responsible for analyzing the financial performance for CDW’s sales and gross profit margin. This role performs analysis for executive-level management to support the impacts to gross profit margin from changes in business trends, customer and product mixes, as well as understanding the impacts of specific deals on gross profit margin.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
Key Areas of Responsibility
</div>
<ul>
<li>Proactively provide timely, insightful and accurate financial reporting and analysis for Finance executives and the Executive Committee.</li>
<li>
Analyze monthly and quarterly results against prior periods to ensure the reasons for the variances are fully understood.</li>
<li>
Works collaboratively with the business and other finance teams globally to understand the business drivers behind the changes to gross profit margin.</li>
<li>
Provides quarterly Investor Relations support packages for earnings releases and earnings call scripts.</li>
<li>
Build and reviews operational financial reports for the business.</li>
<li>
Participates in cross-functional special projects as required.</li>
<li>
Provide recommendations for process improvements in reporting and analysis.</li>
<li>
Responsible for understanding the data measures, identifying any issues with the measures and communicating to the broader business.</li>
<li>
Provide system support.<br>
</li>
</ul>
