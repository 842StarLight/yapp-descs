<h2>VP of Enterprise Sales, Healthcare Practice</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Overview</b></h2>
<p>DataArt is looking for an experienced VP of Enterprise Sales to lead targeted sales efforts and develop relationships with new mid-to-large enterprise-level clients across multiple sectors but with a specific focus on Healthcare opportunities. The candidate must be able to communicate DataArt’s unique capabilities to deliver complex and customized software solutions. Core responsibilities include creating, developing, and winning a portfolio of midsize and enterprise-level client engagements with a focus on healthcare and specific experience in health insurance (payers), medical billing and encoding, EHR, practice management, and other common use cases.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Build strong, long-lasting relationships with mid-to-large enterprise level clients, with a remit across industries and a focus on healthcare.
</li>
<li>Plan, implement, and manage sales activities and pipelines to meet annual revenue targets and business goals
</li>
<li>Originate and nurture business relationships resulting in engagement opportunities
</li>
<li>Scope projects, shape engagements, and help define business solutions that best satisfy clients’ needs and achieve their desired business outcomes
</li>
<li>Present DataArt’s unique value proposition to the top-level executives of clients
</li>
<li>Plan resources, organize and mobilize teams (including solution design, UX, technical, engagement management and delivery teams) throughout the sales lifecycle
</li>
<li>Coordinate with DataArt’s Healthcare and Life Sciences practice, ensuring adequate industry coverage and opportunity focus
</li>
<li>Collaborate with DataArt’s engagement managers to ensure maximum client satisfaction and optimized revenue for DataArt
</li>
<li>Understand and assist in managing and mitigating key risks through tactical plans and actions
</li>
<li>Evangelize DataArt’s unique capabilities to deliver complex, leading edge, industry-specific custom software solutions through presentations and discussions, participation at events, speaking engagements, and tradeshows</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required Skills and Experience</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience selling professional IT or software services.
</li>
<li>Experience selling technology consultations and services
</li>
<li>A demonstrable history of meeting and exceeding expectations (consistent top 20% performance)
</li>
<li>Excellent understanding of the industry, including key players, strategies, client structures, business models, sub-verticals, needs, challenges, and key trends
</li>
<li>A high level of effectiveness in shortening the traditional enterprise sales cycle
</li>
<li>Experience and comfortable communicating with potential client firms at all management levels, from “C” level executives, to product management, to IT professionals
</li>
<li>A team player who is also highly self-reliant (self-managed) and self-motivated
</li>
<li>Must have a strong understanding of the software systems and data that are widely used in the industry
</li>
<li>Knowledge of project management processes including scope definition, project plans, resource plans, budgets, and milestone identification
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills with the ability to think, write, and present in a logical, structured, and polished manner
</li>
<li>Possess a demonstrable strategic, creative, and analytical sales approach with a focus on initiating and building client relationships</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Additional Competencies</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Experience working with multinational distributed teams
</li>
<li>Understanding of modern IT technologies and tools
</li>
<li>Business consulting experience</li>
</ul>
