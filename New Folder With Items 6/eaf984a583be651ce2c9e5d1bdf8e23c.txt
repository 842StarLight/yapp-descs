<h2>Associate Recruiter</h2>

<p><b>SEP</b> is a leading staffing provider of tech professionals on a full-time, temporary, and contract basis. We work with awesome local companies who are pushing the envelope in the tech space and specialize in placing exceptional software developers who are actively or passively searching for the next step in their careers!</p>
<p>To meet the growing market demand, we are looking for an energetic, sharp, and performance-oriented individual to join our collaborative team of recruiters! This is the perfect opportunity to build a solid skillset in business development and climb a well-defined career ladder to a management position!</p>
<p>This individual will build sustainable relationships with local hiring managers and software engineers! Our brand is predicated on teamwork, so we operate on a collaborative basis. If you’re intrinsically motivated, have excellent communication skills, and have a passion for building relationships/offering viable solutions, we’d love to hear from you!</p>
<p>A <b>Tech Recruiter </b>youwill engage in :</p>

<p>o <b>Candidate Recruiting </b>Actively working alongside candidates from the initial point of contact all the way to the job offer phase. This includes sourcing, screening, and qualifying tech professionals utilizing an applicant tracking system - Hosting initial exploratory conversations in which candidate career objectives are identified – Conducting comprehensive interviews in which candidates are thoroughly qualified and prepared to meet with clients</p>
<p>o <b>Learning Program </b>Participating in our ongoing training program to obtain technical knowledge and the skillsets needed to succeed</p>
