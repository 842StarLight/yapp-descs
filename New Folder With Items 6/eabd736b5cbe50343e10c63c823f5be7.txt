<h2>Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>K-tech Kleening Systems Inc. has been one of the top property restoration companies in Central and Northern Wisconsin since 1975. K-tech provides restoration and cleaning services to local home and business owners who have suffered property damage resulting from water, fire, mold, wind or other catastrophic related events. Utilizing the most innovative tools of the trade, partnered with experience and sincere empathy toward each situation encountered makes K-tech the top choice for most customers.</p>
<p>
K-tech Kleening Systems Inc. is seeking an Operations Manager with experience overseeing and leading a team of restoration and cleaning professionals with a focus on increasing production efficiency, completing projects on time, maintaining budgets, and exceeding customer’s expectations.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary / Purpose</b></p>
<p>
Facilitate the timely and profitable delivery of all services while maintaining company standards for quality and customer satisfaction. Effectively manage the Operations function of the business which includes: staffing, training and development, documentation of work performed, inventory control, equipment purchases and maintenance, record keeping, housekeeping and employee relations.</p>
<p>
Assist in maintaining market leadership through rigorous recruiting and hiring, staff development and business process improvement.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Principle Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create repeat customers by exceeding their expectations for quality and delivery of disaster restoration and cleaning services</li>
<li>Exceed company targets for client / customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Exceed company targets for quality ratings</li>
<li>Exceed company targets for COGS and Direct Labor cost performance</li>
<li>Exceed company targets for cash flow</li>
<li>Maintain production capacity through adequate staffing, equipment and supplies management</li>
<li>Prepare the annual Operations budget as part of the business planning process</li>
<li>Maintain efficient and accurate flow of production documentation from the field to administration</li>
<li>Development of strategic goals and business objectives for the Operations function</li>
<li>Hire, train, develop, discipline and discharge Operations employees</li>
<li>Determine requirements and execute the evaluation process for subcontractors</li>
<li>Track the financial, and overall performance of individuals, jobs and production departments</li>
<li>Maintain appropriate inventory of production supplies and materials</li>
<li>Schedule and oversee maintenance and repairs to all production equipment and vehicles</li>
<li>Facilitate the smooth transition of management succession within the Operations function through assessment, training and development of operations staff</li>
<li>Provide seamless implementation of appropriate new technology as it pertains to the delivery of services</li>
<li>Assign new projects to Estimators, Project Supervisors and Project Coordinators</li>
<li>Negotiate with subcontractors and suppliers</li>
<li>Develop profitable relationships with customers that bring value to both parties</li>
<li>Foster a team oriented atmosphere consistent with the Vision, Mission and Core Values of the company</li>
<li>Ensure the continued growth of the company by developing existing people and by assisting in recruiting and selection of new, talented people into positions of leadership within the organization</li>
<li>Assist in estimating and project management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 10 years of experience in management; construction or restoration preferred</li>
<li>Broad business experience across multiple functions: Sales, Operations, Administration, Accounting and Human Resources</li>
<li>Proficient in Xactimate software a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge of Dash, Time &amp; Materials, and other industry software is preferred</li>
<li>Experience with preferred vendor programs (Alacrity, Contractor Connection, Nexxus, Sedgwick, etc) a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge of construction and understanding of building code a plus</li>
<li>Strong written and communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to analyze financial reports and use the information to efficiently manage operations</li>
<li>Flexible schedule to support team, especially during CAT events such as hurricanes or regional storms</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver’s license</li>
<li>IICRC certifications preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits &amp; Perks</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive salary with bonus based on performance matrix</li>
<li>Medical, Dental and Vision Insurance</li>
<li>401K Match with employer match</li>
<li>Ongoing training and development</li>
<li>PTO / Paid Holidays</li>
</ul>
