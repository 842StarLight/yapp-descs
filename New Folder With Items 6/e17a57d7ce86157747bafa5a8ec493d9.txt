<h2>Social Media and Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Ozwest, Inc.</b></p>
<p><b>Job Title: </b> Social Media and Marketing Coordinator</p>
<p><b>Location: </b> Portland, OR</p>
<p><b>Description: </b>Ozwest, Inc. is a growing Portland-based company that distributes innovative toys for imagination and play in every area of life. Our brand, Zing, is an award-winning industry leader, offering everything from blast-off rockets to creating your own stop motion movies.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b>The Social Media and Marketing Coordinator is responsible for supporting various marketing activities, including social media, advertising, trade shows, and research, among others.</p>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Posting social media content to YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter on schedule.</li>
<li>Obtain samples and distribute as necessary.</li>
<li>Coordinate media events to promote the brand and work with media and influencers.</li>
<li>Work with POS data supplier to organize and retrieve data (Target and Walmart)</li>
<li>Supply requested brand assets, including graphics and videos to Sales and PR.</li>
<li>Assist Marketing and Conent Manager with web design.</li>
<li>Manage brand activations.</li>
<li>Fill out social reports and present to Marketing Manager.</li>
<li>Post company news on LinkedIn.</li>
<li>Work with Ecommerce Manager and Lead Graphic Designer on Amazon A+ page setup.</li>
<li>Manage Brand ambassadors.</li>
<li>Report sell-thru data weekly.</li>
<li>Manage market research program.</li>
<li>Coordinate with HK graphics team to create USA sales presentations for product pitches.</li>
<li>Create a bi-monthly company newsletter.</li>
<li>Determine, set, and achieve social media goals.</li>
<li>Develop, execute, and monitor social media strategies for international customers.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain key relationship with influencers, management companies, and social media companies.</li>
<li>Develop social media strategies for new and existing products, brands and product line extensions.</li>
<li>Determine and manage influencer packaging.</li>
<li>Update product copy changes for online listings for Mass retailers.</li>
<li>Create checklists, templates, timelines, etc. as needed.</li>
<li>Set-up timelines for product pitches and product launches.</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Skills, Experience, and Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree (B. A.) from four-year college or university; or related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.  </li>
<li>Minimum experience of two years working in a marketing role.</li>
<li>Moderate ability to use Adobe Creative Suite.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite.</li>
<li>Ability to maintain a friendly and professional attitude when corresponding with customers and co-workers.</li>
<li>Proficiency in written, verbal, and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and in groups.</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain trusting relationships with customers and colleagues.</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze, and interpret common scientific and technical journals, financial reports, and legal documents.  </li>
<li>Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions.  </li>
<li>While performing the duties of this job, this position requires regular use of sitting, using hands, talking, and listening to eachother. This job duty is occasionally required to stand, walk, and reach with hands and arms. The position may also require lifting and moving of up to 25 lbs.</li>
</ul>
