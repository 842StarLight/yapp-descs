<h2>Junior Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Summary
<br>-----------
<br><br>
We're seeking a Jr. Graphic Designer that can contribute to our fast-paced creative environment, working on a variety of products, including marketing, social media, print, banner ads, advertising, websites, packaging, and displays. The Jr. Graphic Designer will work under the supervision of our Director of Graphic Design to produce quality work. In this role, you should be highly creative and have a sharp eye for visual detail. Your goal will be to create impactful designs that capture and sell our company vision side by side with an innovative, collaborative team including our marketing team, advertisers, web developers, copywriters, and photographers.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br>----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Design a variety of given projects within specified brand standards and adjust to recommended revisions and comments.</li>
<li>Create campaign assets, including social media, email, ads, website</li>
<li>Design and create email blasts into ESP and send out for test approval</li>
<li>Photo retouch selects from photoshoots</li>
<li>Create assets in various crop sizes for marketing, print and digital</li>
<li>Manage organization of asset database</li>
<li>Create collateral for Marketing/Sales (handouts, signage, etc)</li>
<li>Proofreading and preparing designs to be sent to print</li>
<li>Respond to asset requests from various departments and outside vendors</li>
<li>Design decks and ensuring uniformity across all departments</li>
<li>Assist with maintenance of ecommerce website</li>
<li>Assist with packaging and various product development needs</li>
<li>Additional duties as needed</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br>------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Portfolio demonstration of strong design skills and technique is required.</li>
<li>Preferred education degree in art, design, or related field</li>
<li>Preferred 2 or more years of professional art, design, or related role</li>
<li>Professional experience with social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube, Pinterest</li>
<li>Video editing and motion graphics experience highly preferred</li>
<li>Strong proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite, primarily Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign</li>
<li>Strong creative and visual design skills with major attention to detail</li>
<li>Working knowledge of HTML and CSS</li>
<li>Experience in packaging, displays and print advertisements a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to take direction from others on concepts and design, and implement them to final output.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task simultaneous ongoing projects with strong time management skills and aggressive deadlines.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with the ability to express design concepts to others.</li>
</ul>
Why work at Ember?
<br>------------------
<br><br>
Ember Technologies, Inc. is a design-led temperature control brand whose mission is to revolutionize the way people eat and drink. Founded by inventor and serial entrepreneur Clay Alexander, Ember creates, designs and develops household products that offer consumers complete customization of their food and beverage temperature. Ember's Travel Mug and Ceramic Mug are the most advanced coffee mugs on the market, allowing individuals to set and maintain their preferred drinking temperature for hot beverages. Ember's world-class team consists of product designers, engineers and executives formerly with Apple, Microsoft, Nokia, Under Armour, Skechers, Beats by Dre and Amazon Labs.
