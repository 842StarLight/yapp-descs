<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details
</b></h2>
<p><b>Job Title: </b>Director, Enablement - Sales Leadership
</p>
<p><b>Location: Remote
</b></p>
<p><b>DEPARTMENT DESCRIPTION:
</b></p>
<p><b>Field Enablement ensures sales and sales leaders are equipped with the right content, skillset and productivity tools to maximize sales growth and customer success. Field Enablement solves business problems and opportunities with high impact enablement solutions. Whether we are enabling new or tenured sales professionals, we strive to develop the #1 sales organization in the high-tech industry that exudes the Aloha spirit.
</b></p>
<p><b>ROLE DESCRIPTION:
</b></p>
<p><b>As the Director for Salesforce’s Business to Consumer (B2C) Sales Leaders, you will be responsible for creating, owning, and executing the leader enablement plan for our B2C AMER regional Sales Leaders. You will partner with senior sales stakeholders to determine enablement priorities. This role will own the end-to-end enablement program lifecycle from research, to build through measurement. You will lead the creation of sales leader competencies and curriculum for the career progression path of Sales Leaders (1st, 2nd and 3rd line managers). The ideal candidate has a solid understanding of selling enterprise solutions, developing behavioral outcome-based leadership training, understanding leading sales methodologies and a proven track record in translating business needs into scalable and impactful enablement.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>YOUR IMPACT:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>Partner with sales leadership and product, strategic business operations &amp; enablement teams to create the annual Sales Leader Enablement Plan with quarterly deliverables
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Define, build, deliver and measure sales leadership enablement programs
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Drive high engagement of leadership program through influence and reinforcement
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Establish a network of sales sponsors so that every sales enablement initiative has participation from the business in its planning, development, sign off, and release
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Partner with the Sales Onboarding team in creating and maintaining a Sales Leader onboarding experience for leaders as well as onboarding their own newAccount Executive new hires.
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Partner with Sales Strategy on reporting and measurement to ensure that all Sales Leader enablement programs are tracked and measured<br>
</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>BASIC REQUIREMENTS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>2 - 3 years of proven track record in sales leadership training (has already done the job)
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>5-10 years experience successfully creating and delivering sales enablement with measurable results in a fast-paced environment.
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>BA/BS degree or equivalent combination of education, training, and experience in enablement or talent development.
</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>PREFERRED REQUIREMENTS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>Strong Communicator </b>: Exceptional written and verbal communication skills, as well as active listening skills. Strong ability to represent concepts, as well as summarize and communicate complex ideas into solutions specialist curriculum, with a sense of how sales professionals think, operate, and learn and apply training.
</p></li>
<li><p><b>Problem Solver </b>: Ability to identify business challenges and enablement solutions.
</p></li>
<li><p><b>Collaborative </b>: Adept at project management and cross-functional collaboration. Excellent attention to detail, oral, written and presentation skills, confident communicator with a flair for storytelling.
</p></li>
<li><p><b>Agility </b>: Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, unpredictable environment.
</p></li>
<li><p><b>Influential </b>: Ability to connect the dots organizationally, network effortlessly, and drive collaboration and program excellence in developing industry enablement content and curriculum with the GTM teams and other key stakeholders.
</p></li>
<li><p><b>Data-Driven </b>: Ability to use data to design and measure enablement.
</p></li>
<li><p><b>Effective Prioritization </b>: Ability to align to business and shifting sales priorities.
</p></li>
<li><p><b>Visionary </b>: Must be able to innovate and strategically look ahead
</p></li>
<li><p><b>Thick Skinned </b>: Ability to let go of previous expectations or feedback and move forward. Coach sales leaders and being open to varying perspectives.
</p></li>
<li><p><b>Creativity </b>: Ability to make enablement fun, engaging and experiential in the virtual and in-person setting
</p></li>
<li><p><b>Leadership </b>: Ability to gracefully work under pressure, highly adaptable and well organized.
</p></li>
<li><p><b>SaaS: </b>Strong knowledge of SaaS sales cycles, sales process, and methodologies.
</p></li>
<li><p><b>Knowledge of the Salesforce product portfolio
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>We are a team that stands by our values. We have a “get it done” attitude with a strong sense of team spirit and making an impact. We are growth-focused, collaborative, metrics-driven, and have a strong sense of urgency. To be successful, you’ll share those values.
</b></p></li>
</ul>
