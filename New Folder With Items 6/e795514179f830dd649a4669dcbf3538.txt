<h2>Performance Marketing Specialist for a FinTech Company</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Seeking SEM Specialist responsible for building and managing SEM campaigns. We’re a fast growing start-up, so we’re looking for someone with a can-do, team-oriented mentality.</p>
<p>
Camino Financial is a mission-driven, online finance company offering loans to underbanked small businesses. Camino employs a team of 50+ experienced <i>doers</i> across technology, marketing, sales, and finance; representing 6 countries and speaking 7 languages. Our company and its founders have been featured on CNN, Discovery Channel, and Univision. Check our Facebook page to learn more and engage: https://www.facebook.com/caminofinancial/</p>
<p><b>
Key Attributes We’re Seeking</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
PPC Campaign Management.</b> Management and maintenance of daily and monthly PPC spends</li>
<li>
<b>
Data Driven.</b> You make decisions based on data, cutting through data in several different ways before drawing a conclusion</li>
<li>
<b>
Advanced Analytical Skills.</b> You are an advanced user of excel and/or google sheet. Able to run SQL queries is a plus!</li>
<li>
<b>
KPI Management and Reporting.</b> Reporting, analysis and optimization of campaigns based on target KPIs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You’ll Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Set-up and management of campaigns in FB Manager, AdWords, Bing, etc</li>
<li>
Keyword research and planning</li>
<li>
Creation and implementation of the tracking pixels with intention</li>
<li>
Collaborative with content team to develop ad copy as needed</li>
<li>
Conducting of A/B testing to achieve best performance possible</li>
<li>
Compiling of reports and analysis</li>
<li>
Develop a deep understanding for user journey, leveraging a variety of tools with first and third-party data</li>
<li>
Assist in contributing research, insights, and strategy recommendations</li>
<li>
Work in limited product management role to set-up tracking and relevant APIs</li>
<li>
Assist in the creation of monthly reports and analysis for senior management and investors</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2 years or more of google, bing, and/or gemini campaign management experience using AdManager or equivalent 3rd party ad tool</li>
<li>
2 years or more of Facebook Advertising campaign management experience using AdManager or equivalent 3rd party ad tool</li>
<li>
Experience managing paid advertising accounts with average monthly spend above 20k+</li>
<li>
Proficient skills in Excel and Google Sheets</li>
<li>
Working understanding of Google Tag Manager, URL strings, Facebook Pixels, Feeds, Mobile Optimization, and Attribution is preferred</li>
<li>
Bachelor degree from an accredited college or university — Marketing or Business degree a plus</li>
<li>
Google AdWords Certification</li>
<li>
Experience in any other aspects of digital marketing also a plus, but not a requirement</li>
<li>
Spanish is a plus</li>
</ul>
