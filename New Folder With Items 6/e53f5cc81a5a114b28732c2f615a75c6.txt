<h2>Project Manager/Customer Service</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description</p>
<p>Summary: Provide post-sales customer support and project management in a print/manufacturing environment for order fulfillment of print, specialty items, and print-related services. Execute orders from customers received through DSF orders (online orders), sales reps, or through direct contact with the customer.</p>
<ul>
<li>Process orders</li>
<li>Enter estimate requests/generate quote letters.</li>
<li>Coordinate all corrections and/or changes that occur during a live job through written and/or verbal</li>
<li>instructions.</li>
<li>Data entry responsibilities</li>
<li>Review and manage multiple projects in a fast-paced environment.</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Skills</p>
<ul>
<li>Must have very good oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Requires very good organizational skills (e.g. ability to coordinate/prioritize multiple tasks, meet deadlines).</li>
<li>Knowledge of general administrative/manufacturing operations</li>
<li>General knowledge of technology and ability to work comfortably with computers and software programs such</li>
<li>as MS Office word processing, spreadsheets or other programs specific to the job in order to complete duties successfully.</li>
<li>Requires good knowledge of multiple functions or areas of the print business in which there is frequent contact with multiple departments while completing daily work assignments of the job.</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>HS diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>2 years of commercial printing experience</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Customer Service: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>Commercial Printing: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>

<li>Paid time off</li>
</ul>

</ul>
