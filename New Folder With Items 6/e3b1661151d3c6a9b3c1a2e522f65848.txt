<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>The sales position requires the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Handling and responding to incoming sales inquiries that are generated but not limited to advertising, direct mail, or referrals. These incoming inquires can be via incoming telephone calls, e-mails, faxes or regular mail.</li>
<li>Updating the company database to reflect all incoming inquiries and responses to these inquiries.</li>
<li>Determining the merit of each inquiry and taking the appropriate action. Discuss the prospects specific application and determine which piece of equipment, if any, will meet the requirements. Furnishing recommendations in the form of price quotations to prospective customers.</li>
<li>Making unsolicited outgoing calls to generate new business. Outgoing calls to existing and new prospects from the company database, rented mailing lists, published directories, trade shows, and off the internet.</li>
<li>Handling and writing up of both machine and parts orders. These orders can be called, faxed, e-mailed, or mailed in by our customers.</li>
<li>Attending professional trade shows from time to time.</li>
<li>Participating in monthly product training sessions. Product knowledge will be formally tested.</li>
</ul>
