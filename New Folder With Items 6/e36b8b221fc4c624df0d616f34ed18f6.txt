<p><b>The Challenge
</b></p>
<p>The purpose of the new position of Regional Learning Administrator is to support the broad scope of learning activities in the regions with background on the business, site and region, speak the local language and is available in the customers time zone.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Position<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<p>HRSS creates, executes and refines the operational part of the HR processes, tooling, products and services with the aim to execute them within agreed service levels, with continuous improvements and cost competitive. Operating the global harmonized processes with country specific compliance requirements, the execution is done by a centralized team in China and regional support in the regions/countries.
</p>
<p>Talent Suite Learning is a SAAS service within HRSS with a centralized operating model. There is a need to limit the number of users that have access, centralize activities to create expert knowledge which will increase the quality of the system and help to reduce pollution with a cost- effective setup. It is recommended to have a regional organization close to operations.<br>
</p>
</div></div>
<br>
</div></div>
<div></div>
<p>Learning Program Owners are accountable for all activities related to the learning items they own (e.g., add/change items, define curricula, define assignment profiles, add scheduled offerings, etc.).
</p>
<p>For all site specific training, the ownership is delegated and centralized into a TSL site contact, who is also on the Trusted Source List, and acts on behalf of all the owners of the site.
</p>
<p>HR Shared Services is responsible to process the changes in the system and to ensure the set up is according to the global guidelines and requests from the learning program owner.<br>
</p>
