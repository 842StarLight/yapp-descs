<b>Position Description</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a location sales manager in our Enterprise Cabling and Security Solutions (ECS) business who can drive sales results and strategic initiatives, as well as manage key vendor and customer relationships within the territory. Anixter is committed to bringing leading product offerings coupled with worldwide distribution capabilities and technical expertise to ensure data, voice, video and security networks can meet the network demands of businesses today and in the future. Anixter serves a variety of markets and specializes in delivering the right infrastructure solutions around the world.</p>
<p>
The location manager will lead a team of inside and outside sales representatives to aggressively prospect and drive new sales within the local area. This position will provide strategic leadership for the group, and will ensure effective coordination of team efforts. The location sales manager will have responsibility for the location meeting its revenue and expense plan.
</p>
