<h2>Market Research Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><i>We power the world’s most trusted automotive marketplaces through innovation, technology and people.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><i>KAR Auction Services (NYSE: KAR) provides sellers and buyers across the global wholesale used vehicle industry with innovative, technology-driven remarketing solutions. KAR’s unique end-to-end platform supports whole car, financing, logistics and other ancillary and related services, including the sale of approximately 3.4 million units valued at more than $40 billion through our auctions in 2018. Our integrated physical, online and mobile marketplaces reduce risk, improve transparency and streamline transactions for customers in 76 countries. Headquartered in Carmel, Ind., KAR has more than 15,000 employees across the United States, Canada, Mexico and Europe. For more information go to </i><i>www.karauctionservices.com. </i><i>For the latest KAR news follow us on Twitter @KARspeaks.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p>Reporting to the Director of Analytical Services, our <b>Market Research Analyst </b>will support the Company’s sales and marketing initiatives by collecting and analyzing data to evaluate existing and potential product/service markets in an effort to maintain and increase market share (profits). You will identify and monitor competitors and research market conditions or changes in the industry that may impact sales. You will also assist in the preparation of reports and presentations for both internal (e.g. ADESA Sales Team) and external (e.g. vehicle manufactures, industry analysts, etc.) customers.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Responsibilities and Duties:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist on requests for customized analysis from ADESA sales team and clients, i.e. national, regional and auction specific analysis of vehicle sales used to support the sales team efforts, or analysis of ancillary services provided such as reconditioning to identify revenue opportunities.
</li>
<li>Assist on requests for chargeable analytical services such as custom analysis of remarketing clients’ vehicle sales at auction (both general market and portfolio specific), presentations at client meetings or production of auction dealer surveys and other targeted surveys.
</li>
<li>Perform all functions related to the Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) decoder process. Ensure all VIN tables are maintained and kept current that feed ADESA’s proprietary auction system.
</li>
<li>Research all VIN products within the market place to determine the best solution for all ADESA holdings. Determine requirements for ADESA and work with various internal departments to implement the VIN product and processes. Automate as much of the process as possible to reduce data errors and costs.
</li>
<li>Provide monthly updates of departmental spreadsheets based on querying/researching departmental databases and other available resources. Databases include AuctionNet, AMS, Business Intelligence and LiveBlock.
</li>
<li>Research and identify macro and micro economic and demographic conditions that may impact company performance.
</li>
<li>Provide customized maps using mapping software.
</li>
<li>Assist in the completion of company publications (Global Vehicle Remarketing and Pulse) through the completion of research, writing and review of the documents. Act as liaison with printing and production companies.
</li>
<li>Interface with IT personnel in order to efficiently obtain needed data and tools.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Educational Requirements and </b><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Economics preferred but similar business degree will be considered.
</li>
<li>Minimum of one (1) to three (3) years analytical experience and proficiency with spreadsheet, graphics and database software required.
</li>
</ul>
