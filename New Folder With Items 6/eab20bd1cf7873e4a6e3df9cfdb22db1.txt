<p>We have an exciting opportunity for an Account Project Manager to join our rapidly growing TBA Outdoors team. The Account Project Manager is responsible for building and maintaining a working relationship and communicating daily with one or more client contacts. This person will be the main contact for the client at the agency. They also act as the primary point of contact between the client and the internal departments, developing relationships with internal teams and working closely with them to understand their capabilities. Lastly the Account Project Manager plays a lead role in creating content for the client's and agency needs, which includes writing for social media, PR, landing pages, blog posts, etc.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities for this role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Acts as the primary client contact for assigned projects, developing relationships with clients and working closely with them to learn their business and communication needs.</li>
<li>
Develops and deliver content for the client and agency, including but not limited to blogs for the client and company website.</li>
<li>
Works closely with all agency department staff to develop and implement strategic project plans.</li>
<li>
Has a thorough understanding of the planning, creative and production processes.</li>
<li>
Proactively gathers the necessary information to plan and implement each client project.</li>
<li>
Provides timely issuance of all documentation: proposals, project initiations, creative briefs, estimates, timelines, meeting summaries, and status and budget reports.</li>
<li>
Manages time and financial resources to ensure client projects are on time and on budget.</li>
<li>
Handles monthly billing and reconciliation of all projects for assigned clients; maintains ongoing accounting status reports for clients.</li>
<li>
Maintains working knowledge of pertinent software and peripherals needed to complete work.</li>
