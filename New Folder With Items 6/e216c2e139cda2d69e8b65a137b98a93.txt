Job Title

Test and Failure Analysis Support Technician
<br><br>
Reports To
<br><br>
Director of Quality
<br><br>
Position Summary
<br><br>
ChargePoint's Quality team is seeking a senior level technician to provide test engineering support and to analyze factory and field failures. The best candidate will be highly self-directed, experienced in both electrical and mechanical systems, and able to provide technical guidance to assembly and test technicians. This position will report into the Test Engineering group at ChargePoint.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Build short term mechanical and electrical fixtures and test setups to support reliability and validation testing, including specifying parts and working with custom mechanical part vendors</li>
<li>Set up and perform various reliability and validation testing using both standard test equipment and chambers and specialized test setups</li>
<li>Document the results of reliability/validation testing and failure analysis operations</li>
<li>Perform PCBA and system level failure analysis on failures during reliability testing and on factory and field failures</li>
<li>Create debug guides and train factory technicians in debug procedures</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications and Desired Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors or associates degree in engineering or related technical field or extensive industry experience</li>
<li>Ability to read schematics and drawings to debug PCBAs and electro-mechanical systems</li>
<li>Ability to work with electronic test equipment, such as logic analyzer, multi-meter, oscilloscope and custom test systems</li>
<li>Experience using optical microscopes, thermal cameras, PCBA dye / pry, and cross-sectioning techniques for failure analysis.</li>
<li>Experience building fixtures and test ups, including mechanical fabrication and assembly, wiring and cable assembly</li>
<li>Experienced with hand and power tools, and PCBA soldering operations</li>
<li>Experience working in mfg. or repair environments required</li>
<li>Experience with 208 and 480AC voltage systems and high-power DC a plus</li>
<li>Written and spoken English proficiency required. Fluency in Spanish and/or Vietnamese a plus</li>
<li>Proficient in MS office applications</li>
<li>Able to lift 30 lbs.</li>
</ul>
