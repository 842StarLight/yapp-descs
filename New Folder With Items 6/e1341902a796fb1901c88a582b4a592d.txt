<h2>Social Media Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SINGULAR PURPOSE:</b><br>
Develop social media content and manage communities for platforms including but not limited to Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, Pinterest and YouTube.<br>
<br>
<b>HOW WE GET IT DONE:</b><br>
<br>
<b>Content Creator</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan, build and manage clients’ social media presence</li>
<li>Create shareable content that aligns with clients’ business goals, creating moments of brand love</li>
<li>Ensure brand voice and consistency are carried out across all channels</li>
<li>Consistently find new ways to pique readers’ curiosity and create engagement through education and entertainment</li>
<li>Listen and engage in relevant social discussion about clients, competitors and/or industry</li>
<li>Oversee clients’ social media design, from cover photos to post graphics, collaborating with the creative team</li>
<li>Suggest new ways to attract prospective customers, like promotions, sweepstakes and live broadcasts</li>
<li>Work with clients and client teams for a consistent user experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Community Manager</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Day-to-day management of client social media</li>
<li>Develop an effective posting schedule, considering audience activity, web traffic and customer engagement metrics</li>
<li>Schedule and post content for consistent customer interaction</li>
<li>Communicate with followers as a representative of the client, building brand reputation and relationships</li>
<li>Respond to comments, messages, questions and reviews in a timely manner</li>
<li>Curate crowd-sourced assets and manage published content</li>
<li>Collaborate with client teams to create FAQ documents for consistent and efficient fan responses</li>
<li>Conduct regular social listening, reporting on feedback from fans</li>
<li>Follow a crisis management plan to respond to negative feedback, if needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Social Advertiser and Analyst</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Extend content reach by creating and executing social advertising plans</li>
<li>Drive consistent, relevant traffic and leads from clients’ social networks</li>
<li>Create, test and refine target audiences for each client</li>
<li>Design, create and manage advertising campaigns, working closely with social media and digital marketing leads</li>
<li>Set specific objectives, report on performance/ROI and watch for opportunities for campaign pivots</li>
<li>Effectively communicate with clients about social strategy, pivots and performance</li>
</ul>
<p><b>POSITION SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Displays in-depth understanding of social media platforms for business, when to activate and how they work together, including but not limited to Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn Twitter, YouTube and Pinterest</li>
<li>Exceptional writing, editing and visual communication skills</li>
<li>Customer service mindset</li>
<li>Ability to jump from creative to analytical thinking</li>
<li>Skilled in adjusting tone, voice and strategy based on target audience</li>
<li>Familiar with platform advertising capabilities</li>
<li>Adept at moving easily between brands and calmly handle delicate situations</li>
<li>Highly organized, multi-tasker</li>
</ul>
<p>Knowledgeable about social media:</p>
<ul>
<li>Channels and trends</li>
<li>Content development</li>
<li>Community management</li>
<li>Advertising</li>
<li>Audience targeting</li>
<li>Metrics</li>
<li>User experience</li>
<li>Live broadcasting</li>
<li>Search engine optimization</li>
<li>Employee activation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>POSITION MINDSET</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>I believe social media success is a combination of great content, smart. advertising and authentic, helpful community management.</li>
<li>I believe there is always a story to tell and a new way to tell it.</li>
<li>I believe magic can be made from existing assets.</li>
<li>I believe there are no random acts of content.</li>
<li>I believe each social channel has a specific purpose.</li>
<li>I believe social channels are not standalone marketing efforts, but they are vehicles to help tell/connect the brand story.</li>
<li>I believe visual and content are equally important.</li>
<li>I strive to make ongoing adjustments based on results.</li>
<li>I see each customer comment as a conversation starter and sales lead.</li>
<li>I put myself in the client’s, their customer’s and my team’s shoes.</li>
<li>I am an organization ninja, deadline driver and project management master.</li>
<li>I am empathetic to my audience needs.</li>
<li>I am willing to take risks.</li>
<li>I am a communicator.</li>
<li>I am proactive.</li>
<li>I love learning about and testing new technologies and techniques.</li>
</ul>
