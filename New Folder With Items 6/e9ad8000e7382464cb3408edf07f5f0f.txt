<h2>Creative Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We’re looking for an intuitive artist/designer to elevate our brand look and feel. This creative and organized individual will help ensure all content created strikes a balance between creative authenticity and corporate brand standards to positively reflect our vision and mission. You will need experience in creative/design platforms, have excellent analytical and project management skills and be able to multi-task and prioritize projects under tight deadlines.</p>
<p>
 In this high visibility role, you will work closely with the marketing, sales and clinical teams to create high impact material, including videos, web sites, educational material, collateral and multi-faceted advertising campaigns. Your exceptional written and verbal communication skills will allow you to present concepts and ideas to both internal and external audiences to ensure successful implementation of projects.</p>
<p>
 <b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic Design, Art or similar field</li>
<li>Four or more years of experience of relevant experience</li>
<li>Experience with knowledge of software such as PowerPoint, Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Flash and/or InDesign, Final Cut Pro, Premier Pro, Audacity or other media editing software</li>
<li>Experience with web design and content management systems</li>
</ul> <p>If you think you have what it takes to join the dynamic team at ivWatch, please complete the form at the bottom of the page. You can also email your information to hr@ivwatch.com.</p>
