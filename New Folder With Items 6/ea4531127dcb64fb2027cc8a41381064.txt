<h2>Finance and Accounting Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for an organized and detail-oriented Finance/Accounting Manager with a sense of urgency to manage bookkeeping, Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, and Aki’s daily financial operations. Reporting to our COO, this person will work in close partnership with multiple leaders and teams within Aki. This role requires having a “yes” mentality, a sense of humor, and an accounting and finance background ideally in tech/startups.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>
Oversee AP, AR, and payroll (bill pay/billing/collections) in a player/coach capacity (with one direct report)</li>
<li>
Mid-month, month-end, and yearly closing (bank, credit card and balance sheet recons)</li>
<li>
Quarterly commission calculations for Aki’s sales people</li>
<li>
Assist external accounting firm with year end taxes</li>
<li>
Manage cash flow on weekly basis</li>
<li>
Prepare monthly financials</li>
<li>
Provide financial reports for Aki’s leadership team</li>
<li>
Analyze costs, pricing, variable contributions, sales results and the company’s actual performance compared to the business plans</li>
<li>
Conduct reviews and evaluations for cost-reduction opportunities</li>
<li>
Liase with auditors to ensure appropriate monitoring of company finances is maintained</li>
<li>
Work with legal counsel to prepare stock option agreements upon board approvals</li>
<li>
Organize and maintain records of all investor and advisory documents-</li>
<li>
Manage expense reports platform (Zoho), including new user set up, approval work flow and trouble shooting issues</li>
<li>
Audit billing reports to ensure billing accuracy</li>
<li>
Maintain and file all vendor contract</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<p>At least 3 years of experience as an Accounting/Finance Manager; at least 5 years of progressively responsible accounting experience prior to that</p>
<p>
Experience in a startup</p>
<p>
Proficient user of finance and accounting software</p>
<p>
Strong interpersonal and communication skills</p>
<p>
Able to manage, guide and lead employees to ensure appropriate financial processes are being used</p>
<p>
A solid understanding of financial statistics and accounting principles</p>
<p>
Working knowledge of all statutory legislation and regulations</p>
<p>
BS/MA degree in Finance, Accounting or Economics</p>
<p>
Professional qualification such as CFA/CPA or similar a plus</p>
