<h2>Leasing Agent - Huntington Woods</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Are you outgoing, customer-focused, and enjoy working in a fast-paced high-energy workplace? If so, this may be the ideal opportunity for you!!!</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Our Leasing Professionals are the first impression of the property and must present a level of professionalism.</div>
<div>and unparalleled customer service. Your objective is to positively guide potential residents through the entire</div>
<div>move-in process.</div>
<div>This is accomplished through answering phones and persuading prospective residents to visit the property</div>
<div>in a friendly and professional manner, as well as, consistent and timely follow-up calls and emails to prospective</div>
<div>and/or current residents, conducting property tours and working with the potential resident to complete their application.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Essential Duties</b>:</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>To show and lease apartment units to potential tenants with a high degree of customer service and meet monthly lease quotas as assigned.</li>
<li>Completes lease form or agreement and collects rental deposit.</li>
<li>Responsible for insuring the “model” apartments and target apartments are ready for show; responsible for maintaining work areas and office in clean and orderly manner; need to have an awareness of the “curb appeal” of the property.</li>
<li>Advance knowledge of lease terms, specifications and all community policies and the ability to train others.</li>
<li>Advance knowledge of floor plans and their differences and the ability to train others.</li>
<li>Must be knowledgeable of required telephone and sales techniques.</li>
<li>Must be knowledgeable of current market conditionsUnderstand and be fully informed of current rental rates, sizes, locations and all amenities of property and the ability to train others.</li>
<li>Must develop full knowledge of application information required, screening processes and policies regarding rentals, and the ability to transfer the information into the company computer system.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain an on-going resident retention program under the direction of Property Manager.</li>
<li>Responsible to play an active role in the renewal process and meet renewal quotas.</li>
<li>Responsible for proper maintenance of all resident and property files.</li>
<li>Efficient and timely processing of all required administrative forms, reports and related information and have them entered in the company system.</li>
<li>Responsible for reporting unusual or extraordinary circumstances regarding the property or residents.</li>
<li>Courteous, efficient handling of resident requests and complaints with a high degree of customer service.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining a professional appearance and attitude at all times.</li>
<li>Must reach a closing ratio of at least 50%.</li>
<li>Other administrative duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Job Requirements:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>2 - 4 years of experience as a leasing consultant or similar job functions in the Property Management or Real Estate Industry</li>
<li>A 2-year degree in business, sales, marketing, or real estate with two (2) or more years of related experience</li>
<li>Knowledge of OneSite or any other property management software is required</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret documents such as lease agreements, safety rules, and other related documents</li>
<li>Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, interest, commissions, proportions, percentages</li>
<li>Ability to communicate efficiently and effectively with all levels of employees and customers</li>
<li>Advance knowledge of Fair Housing laws and regulations and the ability to enforce them</li>
<li>Intermediate computer knowledge of Microsoft products</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and exert excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to pass a criminal and/or credit background check</li>
</ul>
<div>As the first impression of the community, it is important for the Leasing Consultant to understand the value of providing strong customer service, effective sales techniques and how to overcome objections.</div>

<div>High school or equivalent</div>
