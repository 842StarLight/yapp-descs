<h2>Part-Time Center Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Part-time Center Associate is responsible for performing the daily tasks of shipment processing, packaging, printing, faxing, Notary, and mailroom management while providing outstanding service and solutions to our customers at this retail location. The Associate expertly advises our valued customers by providing accurate information on our array of products, services, and best-value options. The Associate exhibits confidence by knowing best practices as related to the industry. This is a part time position, 25-30 hours per week during a 6 day work week Monday through Saturday (7 day work week between Thanksgiving and Christmas), eligible for overtime pay. The ideal Center Associate candidate will have retail sales experience, strong computer and internet skills, a high school diploma/GED, a friendly and genuinely helpful demeanor, a professional appearance, and will be a fast learner who makes an effort to learn all aspects of the business in the shortest possible time.<br>
</p>
<br>
<br>
<p></p>
