<h2>QC Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Growing e-commerce company seeks full-time QC/production associate to join a highly-motivated team.</p>
<p>Are you looking for a position on a fast-growing team and are a smart, detail-oriented, hard-working self-starter who care about the work that they do and want to be part of a team that works hard every day to do it better than the competition?</p>
<p><b>Schedule</b> Mon. - Fri. 8 hours per day<br><b>Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Compare finished goods to specifications, spot and categorize defects</li>
<li>Locate and compile orders from multiple bins</li>
<li>Work with multiple departments to ensure job completed correctly</li>
<li>Maintain documentation related to job functions including purchase orders, sales orders, and packing slips.</li>
<li>Assist production and warehouse personnel as required</li>
<li>Additional duties as directed by supervisor</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in Quality Control preferred</li>
<li>Must be able to lift 50 pounds, operate a hand truck, and operate a manual pallet jack (training provided if needed), operate a box cutter and perform other manual duties as required</li>
<li>QC is in the warehouse; must be able to work in warm temperatures during the summer months</li>
<li>Must be able to stand for duration on shift</li>
<li>Must be proficient in written and spoken English</li>
<li>Must have high school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Must be proficient with email, the Internet, and able to learn to use commercial software applications</li>
<li>Must be eligible to work in the United States without employer sponsorship</li>
<li>Must be focused, fast, detail-oriented and motivated to hit the ground running every day</li>
<li>Must have reliable transportation to I-10 &amp;Elliot</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation and Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>$13.00 per hour, review at 90 days</li>
<li>The employer offers group health/dental/vision insurance and a 401(k) program</li>
</ul>
