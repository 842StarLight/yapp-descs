The Role
<br>At Fusion, the Advisory Consultant is a resource with strong consulting service delivery skills. The Advisory Consulatant can autonomously deliver multiple client engagements, while also identifying future pre-sales opportunities. They are highly competent or experienced in one or more designated solution focus areas and are capable of managing complex solutions. Through sound techniques, strong technical skills, and exceptional client relationships, the Advisory Consultant will establish a value-add relationship with the client and the Fusion Risk Management team.
<br><br>

<ul>

<li>1-3 years’ experience coordinating, participating, and/supporting BC/DR consulting engagements including scheduling and overall engagement management</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree and/or relevant experience in Business, Risk Management, Emergency Response, Business Continuity Management, and/or Crisis Management</li>
