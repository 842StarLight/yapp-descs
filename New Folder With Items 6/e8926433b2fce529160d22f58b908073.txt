<h2>Strategic Account Manager - Mid Market</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>ABOUT AGILIS SYSTEMS, LLC</b></p>
<p>Agilis Systems is a fast-growing SaaS provider who develops location-based software solutions for businesses and consumers. Our GPS fleet management solutions serve the needs of thousands of small business customers. We market our services through highly recognized brands such as Linxup.com and MOTOsafety.com.</p>
<p><b>ABOUT THE POSITION </b></p>
<p>We are currently looking to hire a Strategic Account Manager – Mid Market who is focused on building relationships and managing accounts. Our Strategic Account Management – Mid Market team works with our valued customers to grow their business using Agilis Systems’ telematics software. This role partners with our customer support, sales and product teams to deeply understand the opportunities available within our existing customer base - and in turn, deliver organic growth to Agilis Systems. If you have the skills and the entrepreneurial spirit to achieve the responsibilities of this opportunity, we would love to hear from you!</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS – RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and grow a book of business at the direction of the Strategic Marketing Manager</li>
<li>Build a business plan to grow book of business and be responsible for revenue growth Facilitate successful onboarding of new customers</li>
<li>Ensure customers are leveraging our products and services regularly and successfully</li>
<li>Contact customers through, but not limited to, phone calls, email and webinars to communicate opportunities to extend initiatives with the company</li>
<li>Act as the liaison between key customers and internal teams</li>
<li>Identify at-risk accounts to minimize monthly and annual churn rates</li>
<li>Gather requirements and features from customers and their communities to augment Sales and business growth</li>
<li>Work cross-functionality to identify the biggest sales opportunities and drive execution leading to real business results</li>
<li>Establish a trusted/strategic advisor relationship with each assigned client and drive continued value of our products and services</li>
<li>Leverage client referrals, associations, and networking</li>
<li>Monitor and manage assigned customer accounts to ensure they are healthy and churn is mitigated</li>
<li>Work with clients to establish critical goals, or other key performance indicators and aid the customer in achieving their goals.</li>
<li>Advocate customer needs/issues cross-departmentally.</li>
<li>Conduct Monthly/Quarterly Business Reviews</li>
<li>Complete customer profiling work as needed within Salesforce CRM</li>
<li>Additional duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
