<p>
Education Operations is a dynamic, global team responsible for supporting the day-to-day operations of VMware’s global Education Sales and Delivery Teams. Reporting to the Senior Director of Professional Services Operations, the successful candidate will be the key Business Partner to the Vice President of Global Education primarily focused on the continued growth and success of the Education business. In addition to strong business partnership, this Director will be a transformation leader who collaborates cross functionally to drive innovation and efficiencies that support the business strategy in alignment with Company priorities.</p>
