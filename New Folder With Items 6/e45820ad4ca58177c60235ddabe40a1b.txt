<h2>Marketing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Director of Marketing will develop strategy and tactics that optimizes global sales execution across our business units; promotes brand identity and awareness within our market segments, employment sources and the community at large; and the pursuit and development of new and existing market opportunities. The requisite skill sets required are market research and analysis, print and digital media promotion expertise, social media savviness, public relations abilities, strong verbal and written communication skills, quantitative and statistical applications skills that underpin marketing strategies, a working knowledge of relevant software/App tools and web development concepts.</p>
<p>The core personal characteristics of the Director should include, excellent creativity; work seamlessly under tight time lines within cross functional teams and outside vendors; an entrepreneurial and professionally curious demeanor about our markets; a professional presence and attractive personal appearance that is commensurate with our Brand Values, an ability to use excellent interpersonal skills that builds cross functional teamwork within our business units.
</p>
<p><b>Duties of the Marketing Director include but are not limited to</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Continuously improve upon existing marketing processes and collateral that utilizes media, events, and web site social media platforms that support our growing family of businesses.</li>
<li>Ability to expeditiously perform or coordinate with outside sources ad hoc market research that will provide recommended action steps that may optimize existing and potential new business opportunities. The Director will also utilize and further develop internal CEG client data bases and sales activities reporting that may leverage client behavior, motivations and service offerings.</li>
<li>Negotiate with third party vendors as needed to provide goods and services that professionally and economically support our global marketing requirements.</li>
<li>Work with our web site and other tech providers that maintain our sales and marketing data driven assets.</li>
<li>Work with Executive management to develop an annual effective marketing strategy, tactics and budgets. Ability to pull it all together for discussion, analysis, approval and execution.</li>
<li>Work with Leadership Team and Ownership to take creative IDEAS to a professionally and economically finished product.
</li>
<li>Represent Senior Management on all press inquiries within all mediums.</li>
<li>Oversee internal and external marketing/promotional events on behalf of Senior Management.</li>
<li>Responsible for identifying mutually beneficial community outreach opportunities within the Greater Philadelphia Area and beyond.
</li>
<li>Supervise interns and marketing staff as assigned.</li>
<li>This position will have a flexible schedule with some nights and weekends required to best serve the needs of CEG and its clients.
