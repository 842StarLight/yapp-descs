<p><b>Have you ever wanted to be a part of something truly amazing in Asheville? The Asheville Museum of Science is hiring a Visitor Experiences – Museum Admission and Retail Team Member! </b></p>
<p><b>Visitor Experiences – Museum Admission and Retail </b></p>
<p>The Asheville Museum of Science (AMOS) is the center for expert STEAM learning in WNC. We strive to spark imagination and foster lifelong curiosity in the museum and through both in-house and outreach STEAM education. These include field trips, in-school and after-school programs, intensive science camps, Saturday STEAM education, and involvement in city and county events that serve our community. We prioritize our work around ensuring all love science by making it fun and exciting and growing leaders in STEM.</p>
<p>AMOS is currently seeking a highly organized and collaborative team member with strong initiative, customer service, and retail skills. This Visitor Service team member is the face of AMOS and is critical to the museums mission: to be Western North Carolina’s home for experiential science learning, discovery, and exploration. Our team members welcome and provide information to visitors through communicating our mission, sparking interest in our exhibits, and facilitating science learning experiences. Reporting to the Operations Director, this position will be one of the versatile team members responsible for carrying out the duties in the front of the house of the museum to include Admission and Retail.</p>
<p><b>Basic Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Operate the Museum’s front desk and provide positive customer service experiences to guests of all ages. This includes opening and closing procedures.</li>
<li>Create meaningful connections and experiences for guests in a warm, receptive manner at all times</li>
<li>Process admissions, program, and membership fees, and various payments through a Point of Sale System and Quickbooks</li>
<li>Close cash drawer at EOD and process daily deposit reports</li>
<li>Answer museum’s main phone line and e-mail providing information or directing/referring customers appropriately</li>
<li>Capture accurate survey information during transactions</li>
<li>Merchandise and re-stock gift shop/retail area</li>
<li>Administrative and logistical support to all museum departments, as needed</li>
<li>Operational support in the form of exhibit maintenance (minor), cleaning, and set-up, maintenance and supply tracking, and regular inspection of exhibits, safety equipment, and tools.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required skills and experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent required</li>
<li>Minimum one-year retail and customer service experience, including payment handling</li>
<li>Must be able to stand for long periods of time and to walk around the museum, as needed</li>
<li>Must be able to handle average-weight objects up to 25 lbs.</li>
<li>Ability to work weekends and occasional evenings beneficial, although currently primarily looking to fill weekday spots.</li>
<li>Experience using Microsoft products such as Excel and cloud-based POS systems</li>
<li>Must be able to pass a background check</li>
<li>Must be legally authorized to work in the United States without sponsorship</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred skills and experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with merchandising and window display design/implementation</li>
<li>CPR/First Aid/AED certification</li>
<li>Experience working with children</li>
<li>Keyholder experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position criteria: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Passion for science literacy and hands-on learning</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and desire to communicate with visitors in a professional manner</li>
<li>Comfort with technology and ability to learn new software systems, including Blackbaud’s Altru</li>
<li>Must be able to work well with others and be a versatile member of a small team</li>
<li>Ability to quickly respond to needs in the museum, including accidents and messes</li>
<li>Strong organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Calm demeanor under pressure</li>
<li>Ability to maintain confidentiality where appropriate</li>
<li>Ability to be punctual and reliable</li>
</ul>
