<h2>Solar Commercial Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Solar Optimum is an award winning solar company in the Southern California area.</p>
<p>We are looking for a long-term <b>*EXPERIENCED* COMMERCIAL SOLAR PROJECT MANAGER </b>to join a rapidly growing team and have the ability to handle solar projects in a fast-paced, team-oriented setting.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support pre-sales with all preliminary due diligence on projects.</li>
<li>Oversee all design engineering work and ensure contractual obligations are met and implemented into design.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing every aspect of projects-from the early development stage after project assignment from sales and through to the final stage.</li>
<li>Oversee each phase of a project, including but not limited to coordination and assignment of tasks for internal Project Teams.</li>
<li>Administrative oversight of dealings with office employees, installers, engineers, and electricians.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing Contract scope content as well as negotiating contracts with Sub-Contractors.</li>
<li>Support permitting process as needed on projects.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-3 years of experience as a Project Manager in the Solar Industry handling commercial projects from beginning to end.</li>
<li>Proficiency handling legal contracts and leases, and familiarity as the customer interface.</li>
<li>Self-starter, able to work independently with the ability to quickly resolve key project issues with little oversight.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>High proficiency with MS Excel, MS Project and/or other PM software.</li>
<li>Familiar with Salesforce and Dropbox.</li>
</ul>
