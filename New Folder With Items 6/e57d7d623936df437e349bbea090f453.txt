<h2>Sales Support Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i>Insight Investments, LLC helps North American companies better control their technology acquisition, leasing and management requirements. The company has provided a wide array of solutions designed to fit specific customer needs including new and used equipment acquisition, custom leasing and financing, asset management, off-lease portfolio management, excess equipment disposal and the integration of best-of-breed technology for data management and security solutions.</i></p>
<p><i>Headquartered in Costa Mesa, CA and with locations throughout the United States, Insight has been in business since 1987, but has the feel of an entrepreneurial environment. The company currently employs approximately 250+ employees and generates more than $300 million in annual revenues.</i></p>
<p><i>We currently have an immediate opportunity for a Sales Support Associate.</i></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Red8 Support for Sales Executive(s)</li>
<li>Creating and updating opportunities in Salesforce</li>
<li>Work with Sales Executives to create and confirm accurate configurations</li>
<li>Create and track opportunity registrations with partners</li>
<li>Request and track vendor quotes</li>
<li>Create and track customer proposal templates</li>
<li>Create and track Master Sales and Service Agreements</li>
<li>Request and track special pricing requests through partners</li>
<li>Work with Sales representative / Customer on orders with missing, incorrect or damaged equipment</li>
<li>Work with Sales Representative on any specific issues that may arise</li>
<li>Red8 Operations Support</li>
<li>Salesforce “done deal sheets”</li>
<li>Great Plains software including the ability to prepare sales orders, purchase orders, inquiries, smart lists, Inventory Item set-up, Vendor Set-up, Customer Set-up</li>
<li>Working with Credit for establishing credit and extensions, when applicable</li>
<li>Credit Apps and Resale certificates</li>
<li>Working with Systems Engineers to approve orders prior to booking</li>
<li>Booking orders with partners which includes understanding each partner's requirements and processes</li>
<li>Update Salesforce.com w/ PO and SO details along with Book Dates, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to link PO's to SO's in Great Plains</li>
<li>Working with Lease Admin on lease orders including Red8/IFS Lease Invoices</li>
<li>Tracking order status with partners</li>
<li>Scanning all backup in GP and attaching to SO (and PO for lease transactions)</li>
<li>Transferring orders to accounting once shipped</li>
<li>Working with partner/distributor on any missing or broken equipment including RMA’s</li>
<li>Working with accounting on any vendor invoices received that don’t match our PO</li>
<li>Weekly &amp; Monthly reports for management</li>
<li>Accurate and timely month end closing</li>
<li>Support dealing with manufacturers and reseller programs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications / Education: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ideal candidate will have a minimum of five years experience within a VAR or direct manufacturer’s sales organization</li>
<li>Five plus years in enterprise storage and networking is preferred</li>
<li>Expert proficiency with MS Word, MS Excel, MS Power Point, MS Outlook and task management, Microsoft’s Great Plains and Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Excellent communication, interpersonal, and supervisory abilities. Strong project management skills, solid leadership abilities, organizational and analytical skills</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in related field or equivalent experience and training</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability and experience in analyzing sales information</li>
<li>Outstanding written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational and analytical skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated capacity for developing and understanding sales strategies and support infrastructures</li>
<li>Ability to work on a variety of projects and research related challenges</li>
</ul>
