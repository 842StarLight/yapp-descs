<h2>Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Snapbox Self Storage is a privately-held real estate company dedicated to providing quick friendly service to our customers. We optimize our properties through quality management and services to provide tremendous value to our customers and partners.</p>
<p>The Property Manager must be sales driven, detail oriented, and a team player with a positive attitude who demonstrates flexibility and strong work ethic. The ideal candidate will have the ability to act and operate within a team setting to accomplish objectives.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide exceptional service to our Customers and Sales leads over the phone as well as to walk-in Customers.</li>
<li>Recommend appropriate unit sizes and services based on individual Customers' needs.</li>
<li>Understand and explain the leasing process to Customers.</li>
<li>Accurately manage cash and balance receipts, pursue collections, post payments and process daily bank deposits accurately and timely</li>
<li>Sell merchandise including packing / moving supplies and protection plan to protect our Customers' goods.</li>
<li>Understand and efficiently operate our Property Management Software to complete leases, take payments and run reports.</li>
<li>Conduct daily visual and space audits of entire facility as well as perform lock checks.</li>
<li>Submit all property invoices for payment through our A/P system.</li>
<li>Make collection calls as needed on delinquent accounts.</li>
<li>Initiate and coordinate online auctions on a monthly or as-needed basis.</li>
<li>Provide coverage to other facilities when necessary.</li>
<li>Perform property maintenance both inside the facility and on the grounds including; sweeping, changing light bulbs and removing debris in units. Some facilities also require light landscaping.</li>
<li>Conduct local marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as assigned by management.</li>
<li>Travel to other locations as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Specific Knowledge, Skills and Abilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, written, and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>Excellent listening skills and the ability to ask probing questions, understand concerns, and overcome objections.</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and self-starter.</li>
<li>Must possess and professional and friendly attitude and be able to quickly develop a rapport with customers in person and over the phone.</li>
<li>Must possess and be able to demonstrate strong influencing and closing skills.</li>
<li>Ability to learn and navigate new software quickly.</li>
<li>Prior experience in retail sales and customer service.</li>
<li>Basic Computer Skills.</li>
<li>Proficient in the basic operations of Microsoft Office.</li>
<li>Self-starter with a “can-do” attitude.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $11.00 to $16.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Property Management: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>Retail: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Customer Service: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Required)</li></ul>
<p>License:</p>
<ul><li>Driver's License (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Paid time off</li>
<li>Retirement plan</li>
<li>Vision insurance</li>
<li>Dental insurance</li>
<li>Health insurance</li>
</ul>
