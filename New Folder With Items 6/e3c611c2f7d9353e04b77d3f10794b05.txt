<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>At AreYouHouseReady.com, we help prospective and existing homeowners connect with local experts in the real estate industry. We are a startup company and we’re eager to share our platform with the South Florida region. We’re searching for talented full-time Sales Representatives who can help us continue our growth. The primary responsibility of the Sale Representative is to acquire valuable members for our network of local experts.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Become proficient in utilizing a CRM software and other supplied tools</li>
<li>Be able to prepare and deliver sales presentations using online conferencing software, presentations, etc.</li>
<li>Execute billing and marketing requirements in coordination across company departments</li>
<li>Assist account management to retain clients for the long term</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with management to drive revenue by contributing ideas and enhancing the sales process.</li>
<li>Continually research prospective prospects in order to keep a flowing pipeline of sales via cold calling and other methods</li>
<li>Learn and follow our documented sales processes as it pertains to onboarding new clients</li>
<li>Assist in promoting the brands at industry tradeshows while prospecting show attendees or exhibitors</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Education Requirements: </b></p>
<p>Bachelors in business preferred, related degree <b>or</b> relevant work experience.</p>
<p><b>Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>3 years minimum experience with sales (B2B sales preferred)</li>
<li>Active experience utilizing CRM systems</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, very high activity sales role</li>
<li>Demonstrate top-notch communication and presentation skills with the ability to creatively sell to all decision-making levels</li>
<li>Superior organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to construct professional written sales communication</li>
<li>Ability to maintain a full pipeline of prospect while exceeding sales goals</li>
</ul>
