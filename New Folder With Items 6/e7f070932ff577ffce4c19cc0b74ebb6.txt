<h2>Business Administration Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<p>Tietronix Software seeks an experienced Business Administration Manager, to provide the administrative support and back office operations in Houston, Texas. This individual must be a self-starter, detail oriented, have meticulous follow through, excellent writing skills, ability to communicate clearly and effectively with people on all levels, and be an expert multi-tasker. Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage general office tasks and duties</li>
<li>Monitor office supplies and place orders</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge and awareness of all projects, assignments, and progress</li>
<li>HR representative: Assist with new hires, time sheets, vacation schedules, paychecks, and general HR functions</li>
<li>Process payroll</li>
<li>Accounts payable – full cycle – review and input invoices and work with vendors</li>
<li>Work with vendors to reconcile accounts</li>
<li>Respond to inquiries from vendors and staff</li>
<li>Provide a high level of customer service with all issues related to suppliers and vendors</li>
<li>Receive, review, and process vendor invoices for compliance with financial policies, procedures, and contractual requirements</li>
<li>Work with vendors to resolve all issues</li>
<li>Code invoices to applicable charge codes</li>
<li>Act as the Facility Security Officer – processing all NASA JSC badges</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Minimum five years experience of varied and progressive office administrative responsibilities in: budget &amp; expenses tracking, process management, standards development, process improvement, inventory control, report writing</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication</li>
<li>Ability to follow oral and written instructions</li>
<li>Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (e.g. Office 365)</li>
<li>U.S. Citizen</li>
</ul>

Please submit your resume via email to hr@tietronix.com</p>
