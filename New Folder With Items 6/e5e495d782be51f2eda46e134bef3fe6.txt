<h2>Special Product Distribution Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Key role on the Specialty Product Team and important member of the Product Innovation Organization. Will act as primary Relationship Manager for key vendor and delegate partners for Vision, Acupuncture and Exercise Rewards, Health Savings Accounts, and Telemedicine. Will act as product Subject Matter Expert for sales and account management team for these capabilities and will ensure marketing materials are regularly updated and available in partnership with Marketing and Communications. Will lead the development and ongoing management of Supplemental offerings including Fund based solutions (HSA, HRA, FSA), Life and Disability, Critical Illness and Accident, and other supplemental offerings as needed. This individual will also work with key partners across the organization to ensure alignment on product commitments to current and prospective customers and alignment with regard to supplemental product development strategies and timelines.</div>
