<p>About This Opportunity
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>InComm is hiring an Application Security Engineer to join our team in our Salt Lake City office! In this multifaceted role, you will be working closely with the Development team and Application Owners to develop more secure, less risky applications. We are looking for someone with the creative ability to find vulnerabilities by digging intot the software to the extent that you are able to discover things our tools don't detect. You will be a key member in determining and driving the long term strategy for offensive security.</div>
</div></div></div>
<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>35% Integrating security into the software development lifecycle (SDLC) including threat modeling and providing security oversight and design guidance to the DevOps process specifically with CI/CD pipeline<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>35% Perform application SAST/DAST security testing on web-based and mobile-based applications, including writing scripts and programming</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
20% Providing guidance and evaluating authentication and authorization implementation, improving metrics that measure the application security program through key performance indicators (KPI)<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>10% Providing strategic guidance and expertise to a bug bounty program
</div>
</div></div></div>
