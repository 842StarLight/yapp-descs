<p></p>
<p><b>What you’ll do</b></p>
<p>
As a <b>Data Quality Technical Consultant</b> within Global Hosting and US Professional Services you will lead SAS services delivery projects, including solution definition, implementation, and closeout at customer sites. You’ll possess an expert level of knowledge of Enterprise Data Management, Data Quality, Master Data Management, and Cloud Data architecture and tools, and ensure the highest levels of customer satisfaction.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
You will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead delivery projects directed at the implementation of SAS Data Management technologies, including, but not limited to: supporting project team delivery efforts on Data Quality and Master Data Management engagements by architecting and developing common MDM application objects and data integration routines.</li>
<li>
Conduct project assessments to determine overall scope, work breakdown, and cost of engagements.</li>
<li>
Analyze all information gathered during the sales cycle to translate sales technical vision into a detailed technical implementation proposal.
</li>
<li>Perform advanced or complex prototyping for SAS Data Management and/or Data Quality technologies.</li>
<li>
Understand, utilize and communicate best practice methodologies and industry standards internally and externally.
</li>
<li>Assist in the pre-sales process including:<ul>
<li>
Responding to RFI/RFPs utilizing the RFP Repository and crafting thorough and compelling answers.</li>
<li>
Delivering and\or directing pre-sales engagements to prove functional capabilities (POC’s or POV’s), including on-site support as required.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Interface with clients and project teams to manage / complete client work in accordance with established budgets, timeframes, and quality standards.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<p><b>
What we’re looking for</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You’re curious, passionate, authentic, and accountable. These are our values and influence everything we do.</li>
<li>
You have a bachelor’s degree in a technical and/or quantitative field of study.</li>
<li>
5+ years of technical consulting, systems engineering, applications development and/or customer support experience.</li>
<li>
Strong applied knowledge of Data Quality, Master Data Management, Data Warehousing and Data Modeling fundamentals, with a focus on Dimensional Data Modeling.
</li>
<li>Direct hands on experience designing and developing Data Quality solutions, including cleansing, matching, standardization, and survivorship routines.</li>
<li>
Exposure to building Dimensional Data Warehouses and deep knowledge of areas such as change data capture, slowly changing dimensions, data staging and loading techniques.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of at least one programming language: SAS, Python, Java, JavaScript, C.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
The nice to haves</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Good understanding of the SAS9 Platform including architecture, security and administration.
</li>
<li>Hands on expertise with Data Quality solutions such as Master Data Management, Data Standardization, Cleansing, and House Holding.</li>
<li>
Exposure to implementing ETL solutions using SAS Data Management or other ETL tools.</li>
<li>
Understanding of Hadoop Data Architectures and native Hadoop Data Management tools and techniques.</li>
<li>
Experience with Cloud Data Architectures and Cloud vendors (AWS, Azure, GCP).</li>
<li>
Strong experience and proficiency in at least one relational database such as Oracle, DB2 or Teradata.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of multiple operating systems such as Windows, Linux / UNIX system administration, performance monitoring and evaluation.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Other knowledge, skills, and abilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Superior facilitation, problem solving, organizational, decision-making, written, oral and interpersonal skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to conceptualize clients' needs and translate into specific implementation strategies.
</li>
<li>Experience to organize, lead, and mentor team members on large delivery efforts.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills.
</li>
<li>Travel as business needs dictate (up to 50% at times).<br>
</li>
</ul>
