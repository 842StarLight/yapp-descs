<h2>Senior Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<p>Smarter, Faster, and as always - First. That's our philosophy. Bloomberg Media empowers global business leaders with breaking news, expert opinion and proprietary data distributed on every platform, across every time zone. Like our products and solutions- our Sales teams are agile, dynamic, and innovative. We work hard, and we work fast - while keeping up the quality and accuracy for which we're known. It's what keeps us inventing and reinventing, all the time.<br>
</p>
<p>We’re looking for a client- facing, senior sales professional to represent Bloomberg's complete multi-platform media offerings. We’ll trust you to build and maintain relationships with new and existing clients. To have expertise in the Corporate vertical including Infrastructure, Energy, Investors Relations/Reputation, Healthcare &amp; Recruitment. You’ll partner with our internal teams and build custom ad experiences. You are a creative, seasoned sales professional. You’ll harness the power of our data and resources to maximize revenue and drive growth for our business. Are you up for the challenge?<br>
</p>
<p>You’ll need to have:</p>
 <ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li> 3+ years of recent/relevant media sales experience</li>
<li> Experience with the following desired: Programmatic sales; Cross platform selling; Strong digital video sales experience; Analytics; Media campaigns</li>
<li> Significant and strong client relationships at all levels</li>
<li> Track record of total revenue and platform revenue growth</li>
<li> Creative ideas that generates renewable and successful partnerships with the client</li>
<li> Coverage of the entire client and agency relationship</li>
<li> Internal and external communication at a highly productive and seamless level</li>
<li> Ability to prospect and develop new business</li>
<li> Deep understanding of media advertising landscape</li>
<li> Desire to partner with multidisciplinary teams</li>
<li> Ability to multitask in a dynamic environment</li>
<li> Real passion for media, advertising and business</li>
<li> Willingness for business travel as needed</li>
</ul>
<p>Does this sound like you?</p>
<p>Apply if you think we’re a good match. We’ll get in touch with you to let you know the next steps.<br>
