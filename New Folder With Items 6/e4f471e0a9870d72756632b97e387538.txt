<h2>Automotive Account Representative</h2>

<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree or equivalent experience with equivalent being 5+ years sales experience in eCommerce, Internet, SaaS, or a related industry</li>
<li>Proven track record of presenting to/negotiating with Executive Leadership (both internal/external)</li>
<li>History of meeting sales targets using a solutions-focused approach</li>

<br>
<div>Amazon’s growing Business Development team is seeking an ambitious Account Representative to drive success and growth within the Automotive Marketplace team. This Account Representative will be responsible for developing and managing a sales pipeline comprised of both high value strategic, and transactional accounts within the Automotive and Powersports category. They will develop and execute sales strategies to exceed account acquisition and output goals. This individual will be responsible for identifying and building relationships with key influencers and decision-makers within the senior management and executive teams of these accounts and be their primary point of contact. They will show the prospective account the value of selling on Amazon, help them onboard onto the Amazon Marketplace and provide limited account management afterwards. Additionally, they will collaborate with internal stakeholders and cross-functional teams to create solutions that exceed customer expectations.<br>
<br>
Amazon’s North American Seller Services organization is uniquely influential by coordinating across Amazon Customers, Amazon Category teams and Amazon Sellers. As a business development organization we drive growth for over 2MM Amazon Sellers through business intelligence, cross-selling efforts and integrated Account Management. We are successful by focusing on growth for our Sellers’ businesses.<br>
<br>
Core Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Identify, qualify, acquire and grow seller commitment to the Selling on Amazon program.</li>
<li>Analyze customer data and make recommendations to maximize Seller success. Execute successfully on the recommended plan.</li>
<li>Implement and track metrics for recording the success and quality of the sellers in your territory. Use these metrics to guide your work and uncover hidden areas of opportunity.</li>
<li>Manage complex contract negotiations and serve as a liaison to the legal team.</li>
<li>Prospect and close business to achieve quota targets for both the quantity and quality of sellers recruited.</li>
<li>Understand and utilize Salesforce.com CRM tools to track all pertinent account information and sales progress as well as forecast and prioritize to achieve quarterly quota goals.</li>
<li>Develop a thorough understanding of the e-commerce industry and competitive environment including knowledge of competitive product offerings.</li>
<li>Prioritize and complete additional projects while maintaining current book of business such as mentorship, competitive analysis, and sales analysis.</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver business reviews regarding progress and state of health for the respective territory.</li>
<li>Meet or exceed revenue targets and operational metrics.</li>
<li>Manage numerous opportunities concurrently and strategically.</li>
<li>Create and articulate compelling value propositions around the Selling on Amazon product.</li>
<li>Engage with merchants to understand their needs and gauge fit with the Amazon Services.</li>
<li>Develop a clear understanding of the Selling on Amazon products along with the features and functionalities.</li>
<li>Assist internal partners to drive change, remove roadblocks and close business.</li>
</ul>
