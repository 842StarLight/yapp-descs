<br>
<p>Power System Engineering (PSE) Regional Engineer works under the direction of a Principal Engineer and/or Area Manager and is responsible for leading and executing engineering work in their area. They are responsible to ensure that the work performed is consistent with the statement of work quoted to the customer and the customer’s needs and expectations.
</p>
<p><b>What do you get in this position?<br>
</b></p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>The Regional Engineer will be expected to develop relationships with key customers within their area, and work to become the trusted advisor for these customers in order to facilitate a partnership and drive incremental business. The Regional Engineer must develop their proficiency in all of the PSE offerings. This includes power system studies, electrical distribution design, Protection, Control and Automation (PCA), equipment assessments, power quality and grounding assessments. The Regional Engineer must continuously improve their knowledge of Schneider Electric offerings in order to ensure that the solutions they offer meets all customer expectations as well as PSE standards.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div>This role encompasses not only engineering execution but also support of business development. Regional Engineers meet with customers to evaluate their challenges and goals and ensure that our solution solves these challenges and meets the customer's goals. They ensure that the work executed under their responsible charge, whether performed independently or by or with the support of others within PSE, is performed within specifications and the specified time.<br>
</div>
