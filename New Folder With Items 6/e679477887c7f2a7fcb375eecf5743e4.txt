<h2>Assistant Plant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Ingram Concrete LLC, has an immediate opportunity for anyone seeking employment as an Assistant Plant Manager. This is a full-time position with varied hours in a fast-paced environment in Midland. We are seeking candidates with a strong work ethic, general construction background, and a minimum of one year operations experience. Bachelor’s degree preferred, bi-lingual a plus. As a stable and growing manufacturing company, we offer competitive wages, greater than average benefits as well as advancement opportunities. Compensation based on verifiable experience.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>The Assistant Plant Manager is responsible for assisting the Plant Manger in all aspects associated with their respective concrete plant in order to maximize profitability. This includes assistance with managing production and operation activities and all employees assigned to their plant/area (plant supervisors, equipment operators, mechanics and drivers), and equipment, in such a manner as to optimize production and efficiency in strict accordance with company guidelines and policies.</p>
<p><b>Major Functions: </b></p>
<p>Including, but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with managing production and daily operations of concrete plants, including, but not limited to, daily schedules, inventory reports and processing environmental forms</li>
<li>Know and understand all required aspects of the order entry and batching software.</li>
<li>Assist with all required documentation accurately, timely, and in the required format, not limited to, but including Daily Schedules, Inventory Reports, Quarterly Reports, environmental forms, etc.</li>
<li>Take orders and schedule deliveries to provide unsurpassed service and to maximize efficiency and profitability under the guidance and discretion of your supervisor.</li>
<li>Order and schedule deliveries of cementitious materials and aggregates through Trucking Manager as needed under the guidance and discretion of your supervisor.</li>
<li>Inform supervisor when subordinate disciplinary action is needed.</li>
<li>Solicit new business.</li>
<li>Interact with Sales Department daily. Communicate and coordinate all orders, jobsite conditions, customer status, any inquiry calls, job scheduling and changes on a regular basis.</li>
<li>Provide guidance and lead by example.</li>
<li>Ensure that all concrete is in compliance with job specifications and/or company policy.</li>
<li>Operate plant loader or mixer truck when needed.</li>
<li>Assist supervisor in all duties and responsibilities as directed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Pre-Requisites: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Possess extensive product knowledge.</li>
<li>Know geographic service area.</li>
<li>Extremely organized and detail oriented.</li>
<li>Good interpersonal skills and solid problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Good communication skills, both written and verbal. Sound judgment with the ability to analyze and formulate solutions.</li>
<li>Be familiar with the use of personal computers and general office equipment.</li>
<li>Valid Commercial Driver’s License.</li>
</ul>
