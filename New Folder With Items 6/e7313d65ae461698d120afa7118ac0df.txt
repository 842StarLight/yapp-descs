<p><i>Robertson Media Group, a Custom Publishing and Integrated Marketing Firm located in Franklin, TN, is hiring immediately for the position of Community Manager. <h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Community Manager – Sumner County, TN</b></h3>
<p>This position will be located in the Sumner County area of Tennessee, working remotely from a home office but with weekly meetings and responsibilities to be conducted in company office located in Franklin, TN.</p>
<p>
We are looking for the FACE of our company and magazine for the Sumner County market and <i>YOUR Sumner</i> and yoursumner.com print and digital magazines. This person should be a marketing, public relations and sales SUPERSTAR. The Community Manager will help develop the market for sales, content and partnerships. They will be a networking master, attend events, work with community organizations and leaders and help to integrate the brand in Sumner County!</p>
<p>
This person, ideally, will be capable of performing the following tasks: strategize and prospect for sales leads, cold call and develop advertising relationships and partnerships; work with community organizations to market our magazines and brand, and to create ongoing partnerships for event sponsorships and business promotions; attend events and provide photography of social pictures as needed; identify and cultivate relationships with writers including both community writers and contributors; ability to write is a major PLUS! This person will represent our company and our brand in all things and will meet all guidelines to do so.</p>
<p>
Candidate MUST live in Sumner County, TN. Candidate MUST possess reliable transportation, cell phone and personal laptop. Camera for shooting events a plus. This person MUST attend events both during the day and evening as well as weekends as required by job. This person MUST be able to attend at least one weekly meeting in Franklin office. As well as be present for production/editing process of magazine monthly in Franklin office. This person will work in tandem with magazine staff to develop territory and grow advertising and content base.</p>
<p>
This is a part-full time role with hourly or salary base plus commission compensation package. NO BENEFITS are provided.</p>
<p>
PRIMARY SKILLS REQUIRED:</p>
<ul>
<li>BASIC KNOWLEDGE OF CREATIVE SUITE SOFTWARE</li>
<li>HIGH CAPABILITY OF WORKING KNOWLEDGE OF MICROSFT OFFICE – WORD, EXCEL AND OUTLOOK</li>
<li>MUST BE SOCIAL MEDIA SAVVY WITH ABILITY TO POST AND INTERACT VIA FACEBOOK, INSTAGRAM, TWITTER AND LINKED-IN</li>
<li>MUST BE CAPABLE OF WRITING AND EDITING</li>
<li>SIMPLE PHOTOGRAPHY SKILLS NEEDED</li>
<li>MUST BE WILLING TO WORK NIGHTS AND WEEKENDS</li>
<li>MUST BE A SELF STARTER WITH HOME OFFICE SET- UP AND ABILITY TO WORK WITHOUT DIRECT SUPERVISION</li>
</ul>
