<h2>Sr. Product Manager, Platform - 190396</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Ellie Mae is the leading cloud-based platform provider for the mortgage finance industry. Ellie Mae’s technology solutions enable lenders to originate more loans, reduce origination costs, and reduce the time to close, all while ensuring the highest levels of compliance, quality and efficiency. Visit ‪ EllieMae.com to learn more.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Senior Product Manager is responsible for the product planning and execution throughout the product lifecycle, including: gathering and prioritizing product and customer requirements, and working closely with product, development, sales, marketing and support to ensure customer satisfaction, quality, and revenue goals are met.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary of Responsibilities
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Drive new platform functionality, working with internal and external parties to ensure solution success</li>
<li>
Maintain, communicate and execute on the business plan, road-map, product requirements and positioning for the solution</li>
<li>
Communicate with customers, prospects, business development, sales and marketing to understand / document business requirements and identify highest priority areas for future product investment</li>
<li>
Manage product scope and deliverable s for releases, including the prioritization of customer and internal requests into a multi-year road map</li>
<li>
Drive “whole” product delivery across all functional teams including business, compliance, development, QA, support, training, marketing and documentation</li>
<li>
Develop positioning to facilitate the development of product marketing communications, education and sales tools relating to ROI, benefits, features and functions of the product</li>
<li>
Review / evaluate deliverable's on a continual basis to ensure that business requirements are being addressed</li>
<li>
Research industry metrics, demographics, market share, trends and drivers</li>
<li>
Assess competitors and their relative positioning within the market</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
