<h2>Director, Technology Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Wolters Kluwer Health’s Clinical Effectiveness (CE) organization is a fast-growing and innovation-driven healthcare information technology (HIT) provider working on the front lines of clinical care. Our talented team of clinical editors, technologists, and product visionaries collaborate to provide advanced clinical decision support solutions that measurably improve clinical effectiveness by helping healthcare professionals provide optimal care for their patients. Our solutions are designed to be used in both clinical and commercial healthcare settings, with content geared for physicians, pharmacists, nurses and advanced practice nurses, dental professionals, healthcare administrators, and healthcare business professionals. We provide professionals the tools they need to research, double-check, and inform their decisions. As a mission-based organization committed to getting things right, we’re investing in cutting edge healthcare technology solutions to meet the strategic needs of our customers.
</p>
<p>This position will be responsible for the strategic technology roadmap of CE’s content editorial platforms across all product lines.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
</p>
<ul>
<li>Become the company expert on workflows, features, functionality, and requirements related to our editorial platform technology and operations processes
</li>
<li>Develop and drive a platform and technology vision through a comprehensive understanding of requirements from editorial, engineering, innovation, and external product sources
</li>
<li>Assemble platform requirements from multiple sources and communicate requirements to product development to achieve rapid deployment and adoption of the software
</li>
<li>Manage a small team of business analysts
</li>
<li>Analyze, model, optimize, document and implement business processes and process flows, information and information flows and business rules
</li>
<li>Work with stakeholders to identify gaps and to analyze application/system benefits to business
</li>
<li>Ensures that requirements are implemented in the final product
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communications skills with a demonstrated ability to interact with high and low level non-technical stakeholders as well as technical development teams in a global environment
</li>
<li>Write high quality requirements documents, data models, functional requirements, and user stories
</li>
<li>Write high quality user personas, use cases, and problem statements
</li>
</ul>
