<h2>Developer Relations Account Manager - Federal Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>NVIDIA is the world leader in Visual Computing and in the last few years, with its unparalleled GPU based computers, we have been driving much of the worlds growth in Deep Learning and AI research, accelerated analytics, and autonomous vehicles and robots. NVIDIA's customers include global tech-titans and span multiple verticals such as Manufacturing, Automotive, Aerospace, and Healthcare. NVIDIA is well positioned as the top ‘AI Computing Company’.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are rapidly growing the Federal business and looking to grow our team with a Senior Developer Relations account manager. You will help NVIDIA create and execute a strategy around a key Federal use case. The key use case areas are Cyber, Predictive Maintenance (PdM), and Humanitarian Assistance &amp; Disaster Relief (HA/DR). As an NVIDIA DevRel, you will lead our developer engagements within the Federal community, evangelize Nvidia technologies and accelerate their adoption. You will have strong technical competence and be a leader who can function effectively and independently in a matrixed environment.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Do you enjoy being involved in multiple, diverse technical projects at the same time and appreciate a constantly changing and challenging work experience? We are looking for a DevRel that will innovate and curate the design and deployment of high performance GPU accelerated data analytics workflows. You will need a good background in dynamic areas such as machine learning, graph algorithms, high performance computing and the data analytics software ecosystem.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing:
</b></p>
<p>Your main responsibility will be to develop a business strategy to win GPU and NVIDIA platforms' adoption for one of the key use cases within the Federal market: Cyber, PdM, HA/DR. You will need to establish relationships and influence the technical leaders, developers, and software partners within the relevant ecosystem. You will evangelize and develop our leadership position in this market.
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Collaborate with the field team to select and participate in targeted customer and industry developer events and activities
</p></li>
<li><p>Create technical and engineering activities spanning product divisions and sales geographies, particularly with solution architects and engineering resources
</p></li>
<li><p>Contribute towards our technical strategy and development of our value messaging for your partner companies. Be their key advisor to how we are differentiated.
</p></li>
<li><p>You will become an NVIDIA technology mentor and focal point for the software developer community that you engage with.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What we need to see:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Bachelor's Degree in computer science or information technology, Master's Degree is preferred. Intellectual curiosity and passion for innovation.
</p></li>
<li><p>Deep experience and understanding of applied information processing workflows within Cyber, PdM or HA/DR, in the Government market, including at least 7+ years related experience with the developers and engineering organizations, and at least 3 years in developer relations or architecture advisory role.
</p></li>
<li><p>Demonstrate that you have an established ability to influence, evangelize and persuade at both operational and executive level to achieve a targeted outcome.
</p></li>
<li><p>Show that you are able to communicate clearly and concisely to accelerate strategic decisions.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to effectively deliver a value proposition to assist both NVIDIA and its partner organizations.
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience deploying large scale data analytics or high performance computing infrastructure.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Ways to stand out from the crowd:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Multi year experience with leading AI, Deep Learning, Cyber, Graph and GPU technologies related to analytics
</li>
<li>The ability to hold a US DOD security clearance.
</li>
<li>Excellent social, organizational, planning, and prioritization skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to work under stress, interruptions and around tight deadlines.
</li>
<li>You should demonstrate superb communication skills (both verbal and written) and evidence of your ability to represent NVIDIA externally.
</li>
<li>You're a self starter with a mindset for growth and continuous learning and a passion to coach others.
</li>
</ul>
