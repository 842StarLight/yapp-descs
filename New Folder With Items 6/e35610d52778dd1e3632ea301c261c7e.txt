<h2>Director of Sales - Candlewood Suites, Thornton, CO</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>At the Candlewood Suites, Thornton, Colorado hotel our purpose is to make guests feel at home, at their best, and productive while working away from home. That’s where our employees come in. When you’re part of the Candlewood Suites brand you’re more than just a job title.</p>
<p>At the Candlewood Suites, we look for people who are down to earth and friendly, who are considerate and reliable, and who are always on hand when guests need us. With 83 suites, this extended stay hotel is looking for a Director of Sales to join our friendly and reliable team.</p>
<p><b>Purpose of Position</b></p>
<p>The Director of Sales is responsible for soliciting new or continuing business for Spirit Hospitality hotels. They will be in charge of ensuring their facility meets revenue goals in areas such as food, room rentals, and beverages; this typically includes overseeing not just guest rooms, but a banquet or rent-able meeting space as well.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for:</p>
<ul><li>maximizing the potential sales volume of all segments of the market.</li></ul>
<ul><li>the accuracy of all information on websites, collateral and advertising that promote the hotel.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintaining and updating the branded website content and working with Marketing to update the hotel’s profile, current locations, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Developing and implementing strategic marketing and sales plans.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Developing and managing sales/marketing operating budgets.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Overseeing and evaluating market research and adjusting marketing strategy to meet changing market and competitive conditions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitoring competitor products, sales, and marketing activities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establishing and maintaining a consistent corporate image throughout all product lines, promotional materials, and events.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Representing the company at trade association meetings to promote Spirit Hospitality, LLC.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Meeting with key clients, maintaining relationships and negotiating/closing deals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Preparing periodic sales reports showing sales volume, potential sales, and areas of proposed client base expansion.</li></ul>
<p>. Reviewing and analyzing sales performances against programs, quotes, and plans to determine effectiveness.</p>
