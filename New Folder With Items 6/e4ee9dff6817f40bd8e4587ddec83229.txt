<p><b>
The Role:
</b></p>
<p>As Product Manager, you will drive revenues by developing ideas based on your experience and your contact with customers and prospects into products used by leading businesses around the world. You must possess a unique blend of business and technical savvy; a big-picture vision, and the drive to make that vision a reality. You must enjoy spending time in the market to understand customer problems, and find innovative solutions for the broader market. You will be able to build and leverage a customer advisory group to understand the complete problem. You must be able to communicate with all areas of the company. You will work with other Product Managers to identify synergistic opportunities and multi-product solutions that benefit customers. You will work with an engineering counterpart to define product release requirements. You will work with marketing communications to define the go-to-market strategy, helping them understand the product positioning, key benefits, and target customer. You will also serve as the internal and external evangelist for your product offering, working with the sales channel and key customers. This is your chance to bring your passion for solving business problems to a fast-growing, dynamic, entrepreneurial team that is focused on delivering a complete Supply Chain Design and Decision-Making platform.</p>
<p><b>
Job Description:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the entire product life cycle from strategic planning to tactical activities
</li>
<li>Identify market &amp; customer needs through research, market data, customer advisory boards, voice of customer initiatives etc.</li>
<li>
Provide market evidence on feature prioritization and a clear sustainable long-term differentiation from the new proposed features along with the customer value &amp; revenue associated with them</li>
<li>
Manage the roadmap, prioritize product opportunities, refine feature ideas and maintain existing features to drive business goals</li>
<li>
Develop longer term product strategy and propose new ideas for market viability based on a market driven approach</li>
<li>
Developing and implementing a company-wide go-to-market plan, working with all departments to execute</li>
<li>
Ensure on-going product performance related to overall product quality and achieving product revenue goals</li>
<li>
Lead Product Development and Applied Research teams through market requirements, product definition, and positioning to deliver important product capabilities that solve critical customer problems
</li>
<li>Work with product marketing as part of release updates, product positioning and being the SME for product collateral &amp; thought leadership</li>
<li>
Support sales on strategic roadmap discussions and priorities</li>
<li>
Know why we are winning and losing in the market, and drive changes to our product and processes to create improvement.</li>
<li>
Maintains market and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops; reviewing professional publications; establishing personal networks; participating in conferences</li>
</ul>
