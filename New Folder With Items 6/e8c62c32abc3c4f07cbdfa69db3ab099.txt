<p>Job Description</p>
<p><b>Required skills: </b></p>
<p><i>Please do not waste your time or ours if you don’t meet at least 3 of the top 4 skills.</i></p>
<p>_If you don’t meet all the required skills, don’t expect to be offered the top of the salary range._</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to install, configure and maintain Windows Server 2008-2019 (including setting up DHCP, DNS, Active Directory, GPO, DFS, shared folders and printers, Remote Desktop applications)</li>
<li>Ability to install, configure and maintain Exchange Server 2010-2019 (including setting up correct autodiscover and MX DNS records, OWA, SSL certificates and distribution lists)</li>
<li>Ability to install &amp;, configure and maintain Microsoft SQL Server (including setting up backup jobs, configuring data locations and moving databases, shrinking logs and files, and running SELECT queries)</li>
<li>Extensive experience with networking equipment setup and configuration (firewalls, routers, switches, port forwarding, IPsec VPN tunnels and PPTP VPN dial-up, etc.)</li>
<li>Extensive experience with desktop and server hardware (install, configure and troubleshoot)</li>
<li>Extensive experience with desktop software &amp; operating systems (Examples: Outlook, Word, Excel, Windows 7, 8 and 10, accounting software like Quickbooks or Peachtree)</li>
<li>Excellent understanding of practical TCP/IP concepts (source ports, destination ports, external and internal DNS, sending email through telnet)</li>
<li>Familiarity with malware removal tools and software</li>
<li>Ability to travel through San Antonio and neighboring cities with your own vehicle</li>
<li>Ability to lift servers and computers, mount them on racks by yourself when safe to do so, and climb ladders to reach equipment mounted on walls</li>
<li>Ability to learn continuously, you will be expected to fill any knowledge gaps within 90 days</li>
</ul>

<p><b>What you will do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Complete customer trouble tickets in order of priority</li>
<li>Work remotely on customers’ networks when possible</li>
<li>Drive to customers’ locations when completing a ticket remotely is not possible</li>
<li>Communicate promptly with customers as the first point of contact through phone and email</li>
<li>Deploy equipment on customers’ networks</li>
<li>Assist the sales team in identifying the best solution for customers</li>
<li>Be on-call 24/7 to assist with any emergencies (time taken away from family is compensated in the form of additional time off)</li>
<li>Propose, plan and execute upgrades and migrations for customers with zero downtime</li>
<li>Communicate with a team where every member may be in a different location every day</li>
<li>Think critically about improvements to customer and local network to maximize the benefit of technology in supporting business goals (suggest tools and/or methods to save time, save money or simplify processes)</li>
<li>Spend all day at a customer’s office when the project demands it</li>
</ul>
