<p><b>About Keter Group</b></p>
<p>The Keter Group is a global enterprise that provides an extensive range of furniture, storage and organization solutions. Sold under a number of leading brand names including such as Keter and Curver, each and every item is thoughtfully engineered to combine durability and stylish design.</p>
<p>Since its establishment by the Sagol family 70 years ago, new product development has been at the heart of the Keter Group’s success. With the Group’s focus on continuously developing exceptional products, the Keter name has become synonymous with innovation and trend setting.</p>
<p>The Keter Group, owned by BC partners since 2016, operates 23 factories in eleven countries, employs more than 4,500 people, and currently sells to over 100 countries, maintaining sales offices in leading markets.</p>
<p>With a significant global production footprint and broad global sales support, the Keter Group is able to offer flexible logistics, faster time to market and greater responsiveness in customer service. All told, these advantages have led to annual sales amounting in €1 billion. The Keter Group distributes to 25,000 retail outlets globally, including nearly all of the top 250 retailers worldwide, with many of these relationships dating back more than 25 years.</p>
<p>The North America market is one of the most important growth engines for the overall group and is poised for rapid growth in coming years.</p>
<p>Come join the Keter team and realize your career potential in our growing, innovative organization and help write the next chapter in our success story.</p>
<p><b>Amazon Business Manager</b></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>The Amazon Business Manager is responsible for the overall sales and profitability of Keter’s Amazon Business. The Amazon Business Manager is the main point of contact for Keter’s Amazon account and is responsible to build and maintain excellent working relationships with all Amazon contacts.</p>
<p>This position will be based in the Boca Raton area, near the Keter North America office and reports to the Director of E-commerce.</p>
<p><b>GENERAL RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p>Annually / Quarterly:</p>
<ul>
<li>Builds annual / quarterly budgets to achieve longer term vision for E-Commerce growth</li>
<li>Develops a 3 year road-map to achieve growth objectives, in collaboration with the product category teams (domestic and import business)</li>
</ul>
<p>Monthly / Ongoing:</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for hitting Net Sales and Direct Contribution target for Amazon Business in all categories Keter has presence, which currently includes Lawn and Garden, Outdoor Storage and Furniture, Hardware &amp; Home.</li>
<li>Responsible for forecast accuracy and works closely with the Demand Planner to monitor POS and forecast shipments</li>
<li>Builds/maintains positive relationships with Senior Vendor Managers, In-stock Managers, Amazon support and brand specialists</li>
<li>Works with E-Commerce merchandising and marketing to ensure products have best in class product experience</li>
<li>Develops monthly promotional calendar, tracks results and adjusts future promotions to incorporate learning</li>
<li>Manage AMS spending to ensure optimized levels of spending against existing / new products; measure ROI on marketing / merchandising</li>
</ul>
<p>Product Maintenance:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides feedback to product categories on reviews, opportunities for improvements and new product ideas</li>
<li>Coordinates with category product managers to ensure new products are launched on time</li>
<li>Leads resolutions of any logistics, operations or finance issues within product portfolio</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCE: </b></p>
<ul><li>BS/BA degree in Marketing, Business or related field</li></ul>
<ul><li>3-5 years Amazon account management experience is required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in the lawn and garden category strongly preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven experience with Customer relations, managing customer concerns to satisfactory resolution required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have the ability to define and successfully execute a promotions strategy</li></ul>
<p><b>SKILLS AND KNOWLEDGE</b>:</p>
<ul><li>Would like someone who responds daily to ever-changing needs on E-commerce and continuously looks for ways to optimize sales (short term &amp; longer term)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Creative thinking / challenges the status quo to grow the business</li></ul>
<ul><li>Entrepreneurial</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analytical and shows drive for results</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to effectively handle high degree of multiple tasks simultaneously.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven ability to work under tight deadlines (24 hours or less)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong problem-solving skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong negotiating, collaboration, solution selling and relationship building capabilities in an internal and customer facing role.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong communication skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strategic thinking, ability to develop and drive a concept through to completion.</li></ul>
