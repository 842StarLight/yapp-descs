<p><b>
Position</b> <b>Title</b> – Contract Admin Specialist - 5E</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position</b> <b>Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Crown Castle Fiber is seeking a dedicated and motivated individual to help support the administration of all the National Operating agreements relative to fiber. This will include collaboration with the Regional leadership to ensure all new agreements as well as renewals are negotiated and executed using our internal protocol while promoting visibility and transparency to the overall process. This position will oversee all agreements needed for CCF to be successful to operate in the Public Right of Way (ROW) while also championing the effort to gain closure and hit critical dates needed for the business.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be an effective communicator while sharing relevant details and critical timelines to ensure the business moves forward without delay. This will include working with all individuals within CCF who many be affected or impacted as the business continues to take down new agreements in jurisdictions so we can continue to build, operate and maintain our physical fiber plant while providing paramount customer services to our customers.</li>
<li>Experience working with Federal, State and Local jurisdictions relating to contract negotiation and execution. Building relationships with our vendors while promoting CCF as a National telecommunications provider. Seeking all necessary approvals required to move forward while ensuring we follow adopted rules for Signing Authority.</li>
<li>Caring for all of the administration functions relative to our operating agreements which will include but is not limited too overseeing payment processing for our agreements, working with cross functional teams on changes with our vendors, condensing and combining agreements under one master CCF contract from all previous fiber acquisitions, reaching out to our vendors prospectively to manage renewals and any associated fees.</li>
<li>Have experience managing escalations with vendors. Engaging Legal when necessary to review contracts as well as managing edits/redlines to agreements in an effort to collectively get closure while meeting our internal requests. Managing to our National standards to ensure we have a common baseline across all Regions/Districts for speed, scale and simplicity.</li>
<li>Work with cross functional teams within Crown including NRE, Legal, Finance and Sales to ensure all expectations are being met within our contract provisions for CCF to be successful for the duration of the contract.</li>
<li>Manage the contract database within CRM to ensure all contracts have been entered and abstracted for Management reporting. This will include specific actions such as marking agreements either as active, expired, terminated or pending renewal.</li>
<li>Preparing monthly reports that speaks to; a) the overall contract base as well as any changes to the base for renewals and new agreements that were recently closed and executed b) how many contracts are currently in negotiation, with a break out on new and renewals c) fees we pay in due course and the occurrence of those fees (monthly, quarterly, annual).</li>
<li>Validating the business need for each renewal or new request for a contract. This will include mitigating fees when necessary as well as outreach to the local teams to ensure there still is a business need for that particular service type.</li>
<br>
</ul>
