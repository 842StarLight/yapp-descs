<h2>Branch Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>POSITION PURPOSE:</b></div>
<div>This position acts as the primary representative of the Parts Department in a specific branch of Highway Equipment Company. This is a very hands-on position that requires someone who works well with customers, is knowledgeable about construction equipment and parts, and is self-motivated.<br>
</div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assist with phone, email, and walk-in customer inquiries, acting as a first point of contact for the branch</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales and service departments to ensure that they have parts available when needed</li>
<li>Analyze parts operations at the branch and suggest ways to improve the department and increase profitability</li>
<li>Find and build relationships with new customers and maintain relations with current customers</li>
<li>Procure parts as needed to run the branch effectively</li>
<li>Invoice customers accurately and quickly, answering any questions they have about costs</li>
<li>Live by Highway’s values; advance and support a culture of leadership, responsibility, accountability, continual improvement, and fun among all employees</li>
<li>Contribute to Highway’s overall success by performing additional duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Must have experience working in a parts department that is directly or indirectly involved in one or more industry segments related to the industries served by Highway Equipment, its customers, suppliers, or competitors</li>
<li>Possesses knowledge of construction equipment and parts, with a strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and manage time well</li>
<li>Exhibits ability to understand and interpret equipment terminology</li>
<li>Demonstrates strong interpersonal and customer service skills, and an ability to communicate clearly and effectively with a wide range of people</li>
<li>Must be comfortable using a computer and computer software, CDK database knowledge is preferred</li>
<li>Maintains professional and friendly demeanor; exhibits ability to remain calm and polite when working with customers</li>
<li>Must be able to pass a background check</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
