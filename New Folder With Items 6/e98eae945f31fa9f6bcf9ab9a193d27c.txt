<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Position summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
The Solution Designer consults our customers on how best to implement our chatbot and live chat technologies into their digital platforms. This includes reviewing and analyzing our customers’ websites, mobile apps and other digital channels and applying design and user experience expertise to architect the optimal implementation strategy.<br>
<br>
The Solution Designer plays a critical role within our Professional Services organization, serving as a strategic advisor to our customers. He / she partners with our customers from contract signature to go-live, working to design the solution architecture and strategy behind the program.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Principal duties and responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Partner with the client and sales team to assist in providing structure for the launch process and objectives for each project</li>
<li>Lead requirements-gathering sessions with client teams</li>
<li>Build strategic vision for each program’s design and provide leadership to cross-functional launch teams, internal &amp; external stakeholders including project managers, graphic designers, deployment engineers, QA professionals, operations, and client teams to deliver the solution architecture/design</li>
<li>Perform in depth user experience analysis of the client site and provide actionable next steps to solving identified issues</li>
<li>Conduct web analytics analysis of the clients site metrics and operational insights to understand user behavior and operational areas of opportunity</li>
<li>Present analytics findings, user experience insights, and solution design business strategy to the client</li>
<li>Using analytical insights to help other departments troubleshoot technical problems post-launch</li>
<li>Perform tasks related to securing and keeping the products, tools, and processes that you are responsible for securing</li>
</ul>
