<h2>Senior Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p><b>Summary:</b></p>
<p>
As a more experienced member of the sales team, a Senior Account Executive is a liaison between FRSecure and its clients, recognizing opportunities and turning leads into long-lasting strategic relationships. Senior Account Executives communicate directly with clients and prospects to understand their business needs and recommend information security services that maximize value. Candidates for this role should demonstrate advanced communication and negotiation skills, a proactive approach to identifying clientsâ€™ needs, and a drive for accelerating business growth.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Consistently attain and exceed monthly sales quotas</li>
<li>
Manage key accounts and act as the point of contact for clients</li>
<li>
Establish and develop relationships with new and existing clients to expand sales and prioritize activity to generate maximum revenue and create a better client experience</li>
<li>
Help to ensure a collaborative working relationship between clients and their Client Success Manager</li>
<li>
Develop and deliver sales presentations in a professional and effective manner</li>
<li>
Participate in onsite visits whenever possible to build greater rapport with prospects and large existing clients</li>
<li>
Develop sales strategies designed to demonstrate the differentiating value and benefits of our services to achieve increased sales and profitability</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the operations group to evaluate project performance and to recommend refinements and improvements of all components of projects</li>
<li>
Maintain a database of contact information.</li>
</ul>
