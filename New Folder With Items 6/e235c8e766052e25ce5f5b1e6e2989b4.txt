<h2>eCommerce & Digital Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The eBusiness team at Zep is looking for a super star analyst with the passion and drive to build the capabilities to grow in eCommerce and digital marketing. This will be a great opportunity to learn and develop a career in the exciting eCommerce and Digital Marketing space by applying analytical knowledge while fulfilling the needs of millions of customers with awesome products.
 </p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Provide analytical support to the eBusiness team. This includes mining data, uncovering insights, and providing concise reports and presentations. The expectation for the team is to go beyond traditional analytics reporting and provide actionable insights that drive business outcomes. These include areas such as:
<ul>
<li>Sales and Profitability
</li>
<li>Customer Reviews
</li>
<li>Customer and Order Data
</li>
<li>Digital Content (e.g. Digital Scorecard)
</li>
<li>Competitive Intelligence
</li>
<li>Digital Advertising and ROI maximization (SEO/SEM)
</li>
<li>Other (tax, regulatory, etc.)
</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead online marketplace (e.g. Amazon, eBay, etc.) activities such as:
<ul>
<li>Pricing and “Buy Box” monitoring
</li>
<li>Inventory Management and updating
</li>
<li>Support and update marketing content, which includes key words, tagging, enhanced content, videos, etc.
</li>
<li>Brand protection and reseller management
</li>
<li>Issue resolution
</li>
<li>Competitive and category activity
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Evaluate current processes and identify and implement improvements
</li>
<li>Manage “owned” website properties including www.zep.com , zepbrew.com, etc. including content updates, and assistance in building and optimizing
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul><li>
<ul><li>Excellent analytical and presentation skills – Advanced proficiency with Excel (macros, pivot tables) and advanced knowledge of PowerPoint is required.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>eCommerce or digital experience, including navigating and managing marketplace portals, is strongly preferred.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong business acumen, and ease with “big picture” understanding of business (supply chain, marketing, financials, etc.).
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Cleaning products and consumer products experience is a plus.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Self-starter with excellent follow through – Need to drive projects with minimal guidance, and requires minimal reminders.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborative work ethic and a highly entrepreneurial attitude.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong communicator - Need to effectively synthesize, visualize, and communicate ideas to others.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated passion for the customer.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>High energy, enthusiasm, and an ability to succeed in a dynamic work environment.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to partner with cross functional groups and utilize their time efficiently.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication skills to diverse audiences including senior leadership, customers, strategic partners, etc.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Willingness to embrace change, and to thrive in a highly changing and dynamic environment.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Flexibility for occasional travel (10% - 25%).
</li></ul>

</li></ul>
