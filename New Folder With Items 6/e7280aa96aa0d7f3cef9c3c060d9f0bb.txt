<h2>Senior Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Current employees and contingent workers click here to apply and search by the Job Posting Title.
</p>
<p></p>Clear Channel Outdoor<br>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Job Summary:
</b></p>Generate advertising revenue through the creation and renewal of sales contracts by working alongside Account Executives and mentoring an Associate Account Executive.<br>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drives for revenue goal attainment, on a monthly, quarterly and annual basis.
</li>
<li>Prospects and develops new customer relationships; persuasively communicates strategies for meeting customer needs.
</li>
<li>Mentors and Coaches an Associate Account Executive to build new business and manage accounts growing revenue for the Branch.
</li>
<li>Ensures customer satisfaction by facilitating all aspects of the customer's account in cooperation with creative, operations, marketing and finance staff.
</li>
<li>Determines project pricing based on guidelines from management.
</li>
<li>Negotiates fees or services with clients with management approval.
</li>
<li>Oversees project from start to finish and ensure client satisfaction.
</li>
<li>Delivers proof of performance (POP) to clients.
</li>
<li>Drives clients around the market to show outdoor media sites.
</li>
<li>Communicates and coordinates with Charting and other internal departments.
</li>
<li>Completes other duties as assigned or requested.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of progressive sales experience in new business development where lasting customer relations are built.
</li>
<li>Skilled in working closely with customers to develop and cultivate client relationships in order to grow accounts.
</li>
<li>Sales achievement with experience in consultative or “needs” based selling techniques.
</li>
<li>Record of customer service orientation and strong work ethic.
</li>
<li>Proven track record of meeting or exceeding targets.
</li>
<li>Media sales experience preferred.
</li>
<li>Residency within the territory is preferred.
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications skills including acumen in delivering presentations.
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft software applications (i.e., Word, Excel, Outlook, and PowerPoint).
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education
</b></p>
<ul><li>4-year college degree, BA/BS or equivalent.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coaching the performance of others to help them achieve agree upon sales results.
</li>
<li>Strong organizational / time management skills, and be detailed oriented.
</li>
<li>Skilled in working closely with customers to develop and cultivate client relationships in order to grow accounts.
</li>
<li>Sales achievement with experience in consultative or “needs” based selling techniques.
</li>
<li>Possess a proven track record of meeting or exceeding targets.
</li>
<li>Have a collaborative and professional style with the objective of building strong relationships with diverse customer groups and vendors.
</li>
<li>Be a self-starter with a diligent work ethic and demonstrated flexibility.
</li>
<li>Able to multi-task and stay calm under pressure.
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications skills including delivering effective presentations.
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft software applications (i.e., Word, Excel, Outlook, and PowerPoint), CRM and social media platforms.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Competencies
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Getting Work Done Through Others – </b>Manages people well; gets the most and best out of the people he/she has; sets and communicates guiding goals; measures accomplishments, holds people accountable, and gives useful feedback; delegates and develops.
</li>
<li>
<b>Customer Focus </b>– Is dedicated to meeting the expectations and requirements of internal and external customers.
</li>
<li>
<b>Negotiating </b>– Can negotiate skillfully in tough situations with both internal and external groups.
</li>
<li>
<b>Interpersonal Savvy </b>– Relates well to all kinds of people; builds appropriate rapport.
</li>
<li>
<b>Focusing on the Bottom Line </b>– Attacks everything with drive and energy with an eye on the bottom line; not afraid to initiate action before all the facts are known.
</li>
<li>
<b>Dealing with Ambiguity </b>– Can effectively cope with change; can shift gears comfortably.
</li>
<li>
<b>Presentation Skills </b>– Effective presentation skills inside and outside an organization on both hot and cool topics; commands attention and can manage group process during the presentation.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Other Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Able to travel outside of the office at least 50% of the time for meetings and industry events.
</li>
<li>Access to a reliable vehicle and a valid driver’s license<br>
</li>
</ul>
