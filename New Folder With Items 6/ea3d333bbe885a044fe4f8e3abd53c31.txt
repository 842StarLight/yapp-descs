<h2>Accounts Receivable Specialist (Hotel Experience Preferred)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<p>The Accounts Receivable Clerk is responsible for processing payment of invoices and strict adherence to all TPG Hotels and Resorts accounting policies and procedures. <b>This position will only be needed Monday-Thursday and work 30 hours maximum.</b></p>
<p>Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Enter and retrieve information contained in computer databases using a keyboard, mouse, or trackball to update records, files, reservations, and answer inquiries from guests.</li>
<li>Transmit information or documents using a computer, mail, or facsimile machine.</li>
<li>Operate standard office equipment other than computers.</li>
<li>Prepare letters, memos, and other documents using word processing, spreadsheet, database, or presentation software.</li>
<li>Handle incoming and outgoing mail, including date stamping and distributing incoming mail.</li>
<li>Create and maintain computer- and paper-based filing and organization systems for records, reports, documents, etc. Compile, copy, sort, and file records of office activities, business transactions, and other activities.</li>
<li>Enter and locate work-related information using computers and/or point of sale systems.</li>
<li>Follow all company policies and procedures, ensure uniform and personal appearance are clean and professional, maintain confidentiality of proprietary information, and protect company assets.</li>
<li>Speak with others using clear and professional language, prepare and review written documents accurately and completely, and answer telephones using appropriate etiquette.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain positive working relationships with others, support team to reach common goals, and listen and respond appropriately to the concerns of other employees.</li>
<li>Move, lift, carry, push, pull, and place objects weighing less than or equal to 10 pounds without assistance. Perform other reasonable job duties as requested by Supervisors.</li>
</ul>
