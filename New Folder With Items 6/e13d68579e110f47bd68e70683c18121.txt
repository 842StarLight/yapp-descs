<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>Efficiency, sustainability &amp; reliability all differentiate J.B. Hunt Intermodal. When it comes to reducing the carbon footprint and transportation costs, Intermodal leads the way. Since 1989, we have acquired over 70,000 containers and have become the largest intermodal provider in the United States, with the most extensive rail network in North America. Capacity matters!
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>Our Intermodal Management Trainees receive a unique blend of classroom and hands on instruction, sharpening the skills that move them into full fleet management roles within 3-6 months. This position will support our Intermodal Business Segment giving you hands on experience and exposure to the partnerships we have within the rail networks.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The environment is fast-paced, and you will have the ability to make decisions that matter! This experience will be both rewarding yet challenging, however you will have a management team to help support the enrichment and growth in your career.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Here’s what your day will look like:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You will learn how to dispatch one or more groups of drivers delivering containers within our Intermodal network.
</li>
<li>Safety is our top priority and we expect our managers to promote our company safety culture by monitoring and training our professional drivers. To effectively do this, you will receive training on compliance and DOT regulations.
</li>
<li>At J.B. Hunt, we have a goal for our drivers to have 100% on-time service. We will ensure this by tracking the drivers progress throughout the day.
</li>
<li>One of our top priorities is the retention of our professional drivers, and you will accomplish this by focusing on the driver’s needs, what their motivations are, and building relationships with each of them.
</li>
<li>You will be responsible for overseeing and managing the maintenance of all equipment.
</li>
<li>You will have other responsibilities and tasks assigned as needed.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What will you get in return?
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This is a rare opportunity for you to use your leadership, organizational and supervisory skills to help manage J.B. Hunt’s largest revenue generating business segment.
</li>
<li>You will learn the ins and outs of JB Hunt’s processes and software as you become an expert in the logistics field.
</li>
<li>Real and achievable advancement opportunities within our Intermodal business segment.
</li>
<li>On-going training classes and continuous leadership training to help facilitate your professional growth.
</li>
<li>You will have the opportunity to collaborate with your team to uncover issues that may affect your location or the organization.
</li>
<li>Our drivers are the driving force for our organization and we want to fully understand the challenges that they might face while on the road. To give you a better insight on what their needs entails, you will have the opportunity to ride along with one of our professional drivers.
</li>
<li>You will gain valuable experience and industry knowledge that will help prepare you for the next step in your career.
</li>
</ul>
