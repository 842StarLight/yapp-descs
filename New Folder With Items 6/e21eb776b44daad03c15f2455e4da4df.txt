<p><b>Mission to Our Employees:</b></p>
<p>
Mission to Our Employees: Teladoc Health is the global virtual care leader, offering the only comprehensive virtual care solution spanning telehealth, expert medical, and licensed platform services. Teladoc Health serves the world's leading insurers, employers, and health systems and helps millions of people around the world resolve their healthcare needs with confidence. Above all, Employees will be provided the same concern, respect, values and caring attitude within the organization that they are expected to share with our clients.</p>
<p><b>
What We Are Looking For:</b></p>
<p>
The Client Integration Coordinator will be a key critical role in ensuring the operational mapping and eligibility needs when integrating a payer and their clients are completed successfully. This role will represent Teladoc in communications with the payer and internal departments and will report to the AVP, Client Integration Services. This role will be responsible to work with internal service partners to ensure the proper information is completed appropriately in order for the member to receive necessary communication pieces.</p>
<p><b>
What You Will Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
This role will be responsible for processing mail files received from clients for member communications such as Welcome Kits.</li>
<li>
This role will be responsible for ensuring the mapping of external group codes are cross walked to Teladoc internal language</li>
<li>
This role will work to document requirements and implement change in systems and processes in support of eligibility mapping and mail files.</li>
<li>
The Client Integration Coordinator will work collaboratively with leads from the following Teladoc teams to assist in the success of the Client Integration team.</li>
<ul>
<li>
Sales and Account Management</li>
<li>
Client Implementation Specialist</li>
<li>
Client Integration Configuration</li>
<li>
Data Transfer</li>
<li>
Client Services</li>
</ul>
<li>
The Client Integration Coordinator will be pulled into other Client Integration job responsibilities as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirement:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Degree or years of experience with proficiency in analytics and written communications.</li>
<li>
5 years of experience with at least 3 years in a healthcare setting</li>
<li>
Strong analytical</li>
<li>
Experience working collaboratively with business users, technical teams as well as payers.</li>
<li>
Expertise in relevant business software (Excel, PPT, Word, Visio) is required.</li>
<li>
Individual must be an organized and motivated self-starter who has experience working with remote teams.</li>
<li>
Experience in a Telehealth is a bonus</li>
</ul>
