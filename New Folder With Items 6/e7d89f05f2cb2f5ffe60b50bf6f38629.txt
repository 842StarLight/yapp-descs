<h2>Analyst, Customer Engagement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Job Overview:</b><br>
<br>
As part of the Consumer Insights team, the Customer Engagement team plays a critical role in advocating for the customer voice through the development and presentation of insights to drive actionable change throughout Macy’s Inc. The Customer Engagement team partners with senior leaders across Macy’s Inc enterprise, the store leadership team, and the digital and product teams to make certain that the voice of the customer is considered when making critical business decisions. Perform other duties as assigned.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Be an advocate for the Macy’s customer, seeking to understand their wants and needs.</div></li>
<li><div>
Proactively identify areas of opportunity to improve the customer experience, and seeking to find root causes for customer-facing issues.</div></li>
<li><div>
Lead analysis on ad hoc requests, turning insights into compelling stories by synthesizing research across multiple sources (satisfaction surveys, website experience feedback, etc.).</div></li>
<li><div>
Act as a liaison across many different functional areas including stores, digital, Macy’s Backstage, Furniture &amp; Bedding, Macy’s Credit Card Services and Wedding &amp; Gift Registry.</div></li>
<li><div>
Create and communicate all standard reports, and regularly partner with different business areas to ensure reports meet their needs and provide relevant and timely insight.</div></li>
<li><div>
Identify opportunities for survey enhancements for all of the area-specific programs from internal cross-functional parties.</div></li>
<li><div>
Support efforts to continually innovate around survey/feedback methodology to adjust to changing customer preferences.</div></li>
<li><div>
Digest data and conduct analysis through Excel, SPSS, and other tools to interpret statistical and correlational relationships between key performance indicators and overall customer satisfaction.</div></li>
<li><div>
Regular, dependable attendance &amp; punctuality.</div></li>
</ul>
