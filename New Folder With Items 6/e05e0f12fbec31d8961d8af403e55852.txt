</b></h2>
<div><div><div>

</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>As an Analyst with Hines, you will provide research and analytical support associated with acquisitions, development projects and/or asset management of operating properties. The Analyst will be exposed to various aspects of real estate investment management including market research, leasing strategies, underwriting of acquisitions, financial modeling and administration of investor requirements. Responsibilities may include but are not limited to:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Perform financial analysis of acquisitions and development opportunities via financial modeling, analysis of cash flows and application of valuation methods using a combination of Argus and Excel software</li>
<li>
Assist with due diligence process associated with acquisitions by performing document control, reading lease abstracts, verifying projections or others duties as assigned</li>
<li>
Participate in the asset management of existing properties including market research, leasing, property sales and administration of investor requirements. Duties may include reviewing leases, property financial statements or determining feasibility of proposed capital projects</li>
<li>
Perform market research and analyze business proposals to support leasing strategies and decisions as well as the underwriting of acquisition and development opportunities</li>
<li>
Source, maintain and update sales and lease comparable databases</li>
<li>
Prepare presentation materials and proposals with responsibility for written and graphic requirements</li>
<li>
Participate in the management of development projects including feasibility analysis, entitlement processes, response to proposals, financing, design, contracting, construction management and leasing support</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Closing
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Hines is a privately owned global real estate investment firm founded in 1957 with a presence in 214 cities in 24 countries. Hines has approximately $120.6 billion of assets under management, including $66.5 billion for which Hines provides fiduciary investment management services, and $54.1 billion for which Hines provides third-party property-level services. The firm has 128 developments currently underway around the world. Historically, Hines has developed, redeveloped or acquired 1,348 properties, totaling over 444 million square feet. The firm’s current property and asset management portfolio includes 512 properties, representing over 223 million square feet. With extensive experience in investments across the risk spectrum and all property types, and a pioneering commitment to sustainability, Hines is one of the largest and most-respected real estate organizations in the world. Visit www.hines.com for more information.</div>
