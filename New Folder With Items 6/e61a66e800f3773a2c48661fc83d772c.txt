<h2>Admissions Counselor - Adult Degree Completion/Graduate/Online</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>MAJOR PURPOSE: </b></p>
<p>William Jessup University seeks a highly motivated individual for the position of Admissions Counselor. The Admissions Counselor is responsible for the recruitment of undergraduate adult degree completion students through inquiry generation, immediately responding to inquiries within the same business day (preferably within one hour) and using value propositions to convert inquiries into enrolled students. The focus will be for adult degree completion programs both on-campus and online but this position will be cross-trained to support other programs within the department. We seek applicants with a strong background in sales performance and recruiting in higher education. We prefer applicants with strong interpersonal skills, ability to network with others and possess outstanding communication skills.<br><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; FUNCTIONS: </b><br>1. Generate inquiries through recruiting activities including attending fairs, attending community events, networking with churches and local businesses.</p>
<p>2. Respond to inquiries immediately – preferably within 2 hours for the purpose of converting qualified inquiries into enrolled students.</p>
<p>3. Consistently keep a personal database of past inquiries utilizing the office CRM and creating opportunities for converting those inquiries into enrolled students for the current term.</p>
<p>4. Manage all inquiries, record all interactions and create recruiting opportunities with inquiries through the usage of the office CRM</p>
<p>5. Collaborate with other staff and faculty, communicating on a regular and timely basis concerning the status of recruits and applicants.</p>
<p>6. Expertly and efficiently move inquires through the enrollment funnel including acceptance of inquiries, confirmation of accepted student for enrollment and ensuring all confirmed students become enrolled students</p>
<p>7. Participate in all on campus recruiting events including information session, campus tours and orientation for newly enrolled students.</p>
<p>8. Support Graduate Programs and Online degree programs in the admissions cycle as needed.</p>
<p>9. Perform other duties as assigned<br><b>QUALIFICATIONS: EDUCATION/CERTIFICATIONS: </b><br>· Bachelor’s degree required<br><b>REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE/EXPERIENCE: </b><br>· Successful candidate will have a strong background in modern sales performance skills (B2C experience preferred) and a demonstrated track record of consistently meeting and exceeding sales goals.</p>
<p>· Strong preference towards a candidate who has familiarity with higher education recruiting practices, usage of higher education specific CRM software and working within a university system with multiple departments</p>
<p>· Must be a problem solver and able to deal with changing circumstances and have shown the ability to overcome obstacles.</p>
<p>· Experienced in providing excellent customer service – especially in the area of providing customers with tailored solutions that directly lead to sales</p>
<p>· Possesses strong writing, interpersonal and communication skills especially as it relates to prospective students</p>
<p>· Must be willing to travel for recruiting purposes, work evenings and weekends to meet designated goals.</p>
<p>· Have the ability to work independently within a team structure and effectively with a diverse constituency.<br><b>SKILLS/ABILITIES: </b><br>· Commitment to work in a Christian liberal arts institution</p>
<p>· Demonstrated proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite and Google Apps</p>
<p>· Highly motivated self-starter who is able to prioritize their work strategically and efficiently</p>
<p><b>WORKING ENVIRONMENT: </b></p>
<p>The employee agrees to promote the values and mission of WJU as a private Christ- centered University and live a life consistent with biblical principles. The employee shall conduct himself/herself in a manner consistent with the Community Covenant and Statement of Faith. Additionally, the employee may play a formative role in the spiritual lives of students in a manner consistent with the Community Covenant and Statement of Faith. This may include leading devotions and/or spiritual formation groups and seeking other opportunities consistent with a biblical spiritual formation role.</p>
