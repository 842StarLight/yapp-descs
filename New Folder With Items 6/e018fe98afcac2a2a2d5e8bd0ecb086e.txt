<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Opportunity Overview</b></h2>
<b>
What part will you play?</b><br>
Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment seeks a Senior Manager, Brand Marketing for the Marketing department. The Senior Manager, Brand Marketing will oversee the development, management and execution of the global marketing strategies and games-as-a-service plans for console games.<br>
<br>
This role will be responsible for the product marketing strategy, global positioning, development of worldwide creative materials, partner and agency relationships, and the execution of North America marketing plans. This role will lead the go-to-market planning and have a strong emphasis in digital consumer marketing and live service planning.<ul>
<li>
Develop, orchestrate and execute comprehensive global multi-year marketing plans for a portfolio of console games.</li>
<li>
Establish global positioning and creative strategy and ensure alignment with key stakeholders across North America, Europe and development studios.</li>
<li>
Manage the development of advertising, promotions, media plans, and direct marketing on assigned titles.</li>
<li>
Partner with consumer research team to gather actionable insights to guide product strategy.</li>
<li>
Lead development of the live plan strategy, work with studio and product teams on execution, monitor performance, and optimize programs based on results.</li>
<li>
Manage North American product marketing budgets to ensure adherence to approved levels.</li>
<li>
Partner cross-functionally to ensure product development, consumer marketing, digital marketing, PR, research, trade and sales execution is aligned with strategic and financial goals.</li>
<li>
Manage relationships with key external partners such as agencies, first party, and promotional partners.</li>
<li>
Liaison with functional teams across Warner Bros. (e.g. DC Entertainment, Warner Home Video, TV, Theatrical and WB Consumer Products) and other key partners to develop and execute on cross-promotional programs.</li>
</ul>
