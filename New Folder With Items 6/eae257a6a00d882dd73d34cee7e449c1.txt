<h2>Senior Financial Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our large, privately held manufacturing client is seeking a Senior Financial Analyst to join their finance function. The Senior Financial Analyst will support the entire consolidated enterprise by providing budgeting, forecasting, and reporting as primary responsibilities. The Senior Financial Analyst will be responsible for, but not limited to, the following:</p>
<p>· ﻿Support senior and executive management by analyzing financial results and providing analysis on potential business opportunities and strategies</p>
<p>· Participate in the annual budget process along with strategic planning and monthly/annual forecasting</p>
<p>· Prepare reports and presentations for Sales and Operations regarding business objectives, opportunities, and issues</p>
<p>· Partner with the sales function and prepare budgets and sales plans and provide analytical support</p>
<p>· Work with senior management within finance and sales to implement efficient processes and identify operational areas to improve</p>
<ul><li>Provide new business case support and ad hoc analysis and lead projects as needed﻿</li></ul>
<p>The qualified Senior Financial Analyst will possess:</p>
<p>· A Bachelor’s Degree in Finance, Accounting, or other related area with approximately 5+ years in corporate finance focused on forecasting, budgeting, reporting, and analytics.</p>
<p>· Solid understanding of fundamental finance principles – such as NPV, IRR, Payback – as well as accounting principles – such as accruals, revenue recognition, and capitalization</p>
<p>· Strong Excel skills and prior experience with an ERP system (preferably JD Edwards) is highly preferred as well as data visualization software exposure such as Tableau or Power BI</p>
<p>· Cost accounting and/or manufacturing industry experience preferred</p>
<p>· MBA preferred</p>
<p>· Excellent analytical skills and the ability to assess the impact of operations on financial results.</p>
<p>· Strong cognitive, communication, and leadership skills.</p>
<p>· The ability to work independently and cross functionally at all levels.</p>
