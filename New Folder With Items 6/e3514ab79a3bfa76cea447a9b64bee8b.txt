<h2>Accounting Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Join the Habitat team as an Accounting Clerk! You’ll work hand-in-hand with Habitat’s Finance Director on accounting and compliance issues for Habitat for Humanity of Dane County and Habitat ReStore. Primary duties include accounts payable, sales/cash receipts, bank reconciliations, homeowner mortgage and escrow payments, and month and year-end close. To learn more about Habitat go to www.habitatdane.org.</p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p><b>TODAY’S DATE: </b> September 17, 2019</p>
<p><b>POSITION: </b> Accounting Clerk</p>
<p><b>REPORTS TO: </b> Finance Director</p>
<p><b>POSITION PURPOSE</b></p>
<p>The Accounting Clerk assists the Finance Director with the accounting and compliance issues of the organization; serves as backup for Loan Service Specialist, Program Manager and/or Program Assistant if required.</p>
<p><b>PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul><li><b>Accounts Payable</b></li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Process vendor invoices, including posting to the general ledger software program, preparing checks and following-up with vendor issues.</li>
<li>Maintains vendor files.</li>
<li>Completes vendor credit applications and sales tax exemption forms.</li>
<li><b>Employee Credit Card Purchases</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Posts credit card charges to general ledger.</li>
<li>Serves as liaison with credit card companies to resolve disputes.</li>
<li><b>Sales/Cash Receipts</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Reviews daily Affiliate deposits to determine chart of account and class; posts to general ledger.</li>
<li>Obtains PayPal and Blackbaud credit card receipts reports and posts to general ledger.</li>
<li>Posts daily ReStore sales to general ledger.</li>
<li>Reconciles any discrepancies between the deposit reports and the bank.</li>
<li>Sends letters for any NSF’s.</li>
<li>Maintains deposit and sales files.</li>
<li>Posts gift in kind donations to general ledger.</li>
<li><b>Homeowner Mortgage/Escrow Payments</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Posts payments to general ledger and mortgage software; reconciles totals daily</li>
<li>Calculates mortgage discount; prepare journal entries for home sales; sets up new homeowners in mortgage software.</li>
<li>Runs monthly homeowner reports and reconcile to the general ledger (mortgages receivable, mortgage discount and escrow accounts); sets up new homeowner files.</li>
<li>Assists with preparation of year-end homeowner summaries.</li>
<li><b>Month-End Accounting</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Posts Campus Chapter activity.</li>
<li>Prepare bank reconciliations.</li>
<li>Maintains fixed asset schedules; posts monthly depreciation entry.</li>
<li><b>Year-End Audit &amp; Tax Returns</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assists Finance Director in preparation of audit schedules.</li>
<li><b>Annual Budget</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Enters budgets into general ledger software program at beginning of each fiscal year.</li>
<li><b>Other</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assist with general office administration duties.</li>
<li>Prepares special reports as needed.</li>
<li>Provides support for special events.</li>
<li>File maintenance.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>Position is non-exempt (hourly), part-time, but may occasionally require up to 40 hours per week. The individual must be detail-oriented, analytical, organized and able to work independently. Position may require flexibility during peak busy periods or special occasions.</p>
<p>Successful candidate will fit with our organizational values of working hard, valuing teamwork and being accountable, with professionalism and integrity.</p>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>Minimum of five years of accounting experience required. Nonprofit experience preferred. Qualified candidates should have working knowledge of QuickBooks and MS Office products (Word, Excel and Outlook).</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION</b></p>
<p>Requires the equivalent of a four-year high school diploma; associate or bachelor’s degree in accounting preferred.</p>
<p><b>CERTIFICATES, LICENSES AND REGISTRATIONS</b></p>
