<p><b>Client Relationship Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>Navesink Mortgage Services LLC is seeking an Addison-based Client Relationship Manager ("CRM") who can directly oversee the implementation and utilization of the LoanBeam product within a portfolio of clients. This candidate’s responsibility will focus on (but may not be limited to): (1) cross-selling; (2) project management; (3) customer experience; and (4) accounting. Cross-selling will consist of expanding a clients’ existing relationship into additional products and services offered by LoanBeam. Project management will be required to manage multiple complex implementation projects. Excellent customer experience skills will be required to foster relationships with senior- and C-level stakeholders. Accounting skills will be required to manage and collect accounts receivable for their respective portfolio.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>The primary responsibilities include:</p>
<p><b>Account Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Directly oversee the implementation process of every new client within their respective portfolio</li>
<li>Organize client training initiatives</li>
<li>Create positive and supportive relationship with client to ensure success</li>
<li>Communicate regularly to remain aware of changes in the client's environment</li>
<li>Provide guidance on the installation and configuration of the software to fit the client's business needs</li>
<li>Instruct administrative users on all administrative functions in the software</li>
<li>Service existing accounts, fulfill orders, and organize daily work schedule to call on existing or potential clients</li>
<li>Oversee the satisfaction and success of assigned client relationships, framing expectations and proactively sharing production metrics</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Sales</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Seek opportunities to expand relationships for organic growth while probing for existing or potential barriers in the use of LoanBeam</li>
<li>Collaborate with LoanBeam's Support team to determine issues that could be limiting growth.</li>
<li>Strategize with direct manager around client performance, client communication, and expansion opportunities</li>
<li>Identify and drive cost efficiencies and improvements to enhance client value, credibility, and loyalty</li>
<li>Handle inbound inquiries to properly qualify a prospect</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Administrative</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Learn the tools necessary to perform responsibilities</li>
<li>Manage project milestones across multiple implementations</li>
<li>Manage client data in Salesforce</li>
<li>Attend staff meetings as needed</li>
<li>Collect and log AR aging for respective client list</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Goal oriented and self-starter</li>
<li>Deep understanding of consumer mortgage originations</li>
<li>Ability to organize and maintain multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Excellent written, oral, and presentation skills are a must</li>
<li>Ability to manage a project from start to finish and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills, and teamwork attitude</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>Comfortable leading collection conversations (i.e., collecting customers outstanding invoices.)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 3 years’ experience exceeding client development expectations or company sales quotas</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years’ experience creating and fostering interpersonal relationships to influence B2B sales</li>
<li>History of using consultative selling and problem solving to provide excellent customer experience</li>
<li>High School Diploma</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Experience</b></p>

<li>Experience working in start-ups</li>
<li>Experience in mortgage underwriting</li>
<li>Experience in accounting</li>
<li>Experience in collections or work-outs</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
