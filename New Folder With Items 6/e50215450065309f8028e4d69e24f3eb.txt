<h2>Manager of Special Events</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Manager, Special Events is a member of the Special Events team raising funds for the Westchester Medical Center Foundation. This position is responsible for the development, planning, budget management, logistics and production of assigned fundraising events including researching viable sponsors, producing ad journals, auction catalogues and soliciting ticket/tables sales, seating charts, ad sales, auction and raffle items. Reporting directly to the Director of Special Events and Corporate Relations, the Manager of Special Events will lead the execution and growth year over year on the Golf Tournaments, Girls Night Out, Go the Distance Walk, Gala, Trauma Mud Run, Healing Half, Wines of the World, volunteers and also assist with other internal events such as reunions and conferences. This individual will implement procedures to expand fund-raising in support of Westchester Medical Center Health Network through maximization of net revenues, while continuing to build awareness of the programs and services it provides for the community.</p>
