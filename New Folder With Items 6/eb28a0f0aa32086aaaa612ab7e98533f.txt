<h2>Brokerage Administrator/Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>
<b>Marcus &amp; Millichap,</b> the nation’s largest real estate investment brokerage firm, is looking for a <b>Brokerage Administrator/Marketing Coordinator</b> to provide professional support to the Boston office. Candidates must be extremely dependable, organized, detail oriented, flexible, creative, and able to work independently. Additionally, the candidate must have exceptional follow-through, be highly motivated, and possess exceptional PC skills, as well as written/verbal communication skills. The ability to multitask under pressure is a must.</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li><b>
Administrative:</b></li>
<li>
Answer phones, greet visitors in a courteous, professional manner</li>
<li>
Maintain cleanliness and organization of the reception and conference room areas</li>
<li>
Keep monthly inventory on all office and kitchen supplies and place necessary supply orders on a regular basis, keeping costs in check</li>
<li>
Process new listings; to include proofing and editing marketing package for superior quality and use of grammar</li>
<li>
Assist with the processing of internal real estate transaction paperwork</li>
<li>
Learn various software programs and company applications and conduct training sessions for agents when needed</li>
<li>
Perform other various administrative duties as required to support office</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<li><b>
Marketing:</b></li>
<li>
Create and maintain office marketing material and sales aids
</li>
<li>Prepare marketing packages for agents
</li>
<li>Create print and digital ads for publications</li>
<li>
Coordinate office events such as holiday party and summer outing</li>
<li>
Assist with the coordination of client symposiums, recruiting events, and sales contests</li>
<li>
Circulate press releases and other promotional materials utilizing the M&amp;M editorial style guide and branding</li>
<li>
Update social media sites for promotion of Boston Office</li>
<li>
Liaise with firm’s communications department as necessary</li>
<li>
Work with outside publications to promote Boston Office</li>
