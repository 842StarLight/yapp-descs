<h2>Presales Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Anexinet, based in Blue Bell, PA, specializes in digital business transformation. We empower our clients to grow their customer base and improve workforce efficiency by envisioning, developing, and operating next generation technology solutions. Our core expertise is in digital applications, analytics, and hybrid IT, enabling businesses to rapidly transform. Our clients partner with Anexinet to support the full lifecycle of your next generation digital business. Our people come and stay at Anexinet because we are a technology focused, team-oriented organization with a culture that is second to none as reflected by our 9 consecutive Best Place to Work awards from the Philadelphia Business Journal.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>We are looking to add a <b>Presales Solution Architect </b>with broad<b> application development </b>expertise<b> </b>to provide consultative technical expertise during the sales process.<br>
<br>
The <b>Presales Solution Architect</b> will work side by side with our Sales and Delivery leadership teams, interface with prospects, and provide technical expertise throughout the sales process. A Presales Solution Architect career at Anexinet is a great opportunity for those who are passionate about technology, enjoy working with a team helping to uncover and scope client challenges and want to develop their competencies at the business level. The activities performed by an Anexinet Presales Solution Architect are diverse - one day you might be assisting with a high-level pitch of our capabilities to a client’s or prospect’s CIO, and the next day you might be working hands-on to develop a small proof of concept.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Work as an IT expert and evangelist while supporting customers in their technical evaluation process by delivering high quality presentations and workshops</li>
<li>Analyze the customers' functional and technical requirements in order to design technical solutions</li>
<li>Work closely with the Sales team in the creation of proposals that present compelling technical solutions that adjust to the customers' needs and requirements</li>
<li>Understand the customers' IT architecture in order to clarify the strategic requirements and ensure our capabilities align with their IT ecosystem</li>
<li>Work together with the Anexinet delivery team in the delivery of successful Proofs of Concept (PoCs)</li>
<li>Work with Anexinet partners to develop technical capabilities and competencies to sell and deliver Anexinet solutions and technologies</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>7-10 years related experience with an information technology consulting or software company</li>
<li>BS/BA in computer science or related technical degree</li>
<li>Prior presales experience preferred but not required for someone with the broad technical expertise and a passion for evangelizing technology solutions</li>
<li>Expertise in <b>web</b> or <b>mobile application development</b>, business modeling, database concepts and project management - preferably using Agile Development Methodologies</li>
<li>Good oral, written and presentation skills, with ability to organize and present technical material in an easily understood manner</li>
<li>Deep and broad insight on leading edge technical architectures, IT products and product positioning</li>
<li>Strong business acumen is a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Interested and qualified candidates should respond to this posting or send a resume to Mike Campo, Vice President of Human Resources and Talent Acquisition to mcampo@anexinet.com.</p>
