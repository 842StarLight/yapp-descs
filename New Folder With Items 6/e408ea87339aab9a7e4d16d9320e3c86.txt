<h2>Regional Director of Revenue Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Grow RevPAR and RevPAR Index.</li>
<li>Daily room inventory management</li>
<li>Monitor hotel and competitor rates, availability, strategies, and seasonality and adjust pricing accordingly.</li>
<li>Review unconstrained demand and provide recommendations for the optimal mix of business.</li>
<li>Establish target pricing for Group and Transient and by segment.</li>
<li>Analyze local events and project their impact.</li>
<li>Prepare weekly and monthly forecasts.</li>
<li>Lead the weekly strategy meeting; reviewing prior week's decisions to evaluate effectiveness; following up on assigned tasks of others; critiquing year over year variances by segment; etc.</li>
<li>Conduct displacement analysis with group or contract.</li>
<li>Monitor online reviews.</li>
<li>Review STR reports and provide analysis of hotel performance against their comp set.</li>
<li>Utilize systems and resources including One Yield, MRDW, HPP, and CI/TY.</li>
<li>Review additional tools such as Hotelligence or Demand Positioning reports.</li>
<li>Assist with the preparation of the annual rooms revenue budget and business plans.</li>
<li>Ensure distribution channels have accurate content and pricing.</li>
<li>Develop relationships with the Online Travel Agencies (OTA) or Third Party Intermediaries (TPI) to maximize opportunities through these channels.</li>
<li>Assist with internet marketing campaigns, GDS advertising, etc.</li>
<li>Assist in developing the hotel managers so that they become proficient with revenue management.</li>
<li>Review prior days results and opportunities e.g. did the hotel sell out? Did overbooking have the desired effect? Did the hotel have the desired mix of business?</li>
<li>Monitor group blocks and cut off dates.</li>
<li>Work with the hotel to ensure coding and tracking is performed correctly.</li>
<li>Review production of negotiated accounts with hotel's sales leader.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Hotel Management, Business and/or related degree preferred</li>
<li>Minimum of two (3) years revenue management experience with Marriott &amp; Hilton with a strong preference for <b>Marriott</b> based on company growth primarily with these chains</li>
<li>Experience with hotel revenue management &amp; sales software such as CI/TY preferred, OneYield, HPP &amp; MRDW</li>
<li>Preferred Full Service Experience and strong food and beverage knowledge</li>
<li>Preferred Full Service Marriott Experience</li>
<li>Preferred strong presentation skills and ability to confidently present to capital partners and Schulte Executive Leadership</li>
<li>Preferred tier one market and big box hotel (+400 key) experience</li>
<li>Preferred experience with TravelClick, A360 &amp; D360 tools</li>
<li>Ability to forecast topline revenues within 3%</li>
<li>Contribute/Understanding to/of Business Plans and RFP Process</li>
<li>Effectively manage property director of revenue to perform optimally</li>
<li>Preferred experience of managing a region with $150 million or more</li>
<li>Profitsword/Profitsage experience highly preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Exceptional service and customer relations skills with ability to foster effective relationships with staff, management and clientele.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills and ability to communicate ideas and concepts.</li>
<li>Highly proficient in computers including, but not limited to Internet, Microsoft Office programs and hotel sales related programs.</li>
<li>Strong facilitation and presentation skills.</li>
</ul>
