<h2>Procurement Finance Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<div><div>About The Opportunity</div></div>
<div><div><div>
<p>This role will serve as the cross functional point person for reporting, analyzing, tracking, and handling activities involving tariffs between manufacturing, finance, sales and purchasing and the external consultant as needed.</p>
</div></div></div>
<div><div>Key Responsibilities</div></div>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Communication and interface with outside consultant on matters pertaining to tariffs</li>
<li>Communication and preparation in relation of the 232, 301 and potential tariffs to come in USA</li>
<li>HTS code understanding and assigning together with the brokers we use in GKN</li>
<li>Preparation of exclusions and creation of the arguments required for this documents. Coordinate collection of needed information.</li>
<li>Interface between Finance, Procurement, Commercial and other areas:
<ul>
<li>Manage the process between functions of collecting and filling necessary documents in central storage</li>
<li>Coordinate process of proper reporting of tariff cost in finance – reconcile to purchasing dashboard</li>
<li>Oversee maintaining Tariff database (design and implement process between purchasing and finance)</li>
<li>Coordinate collecting adequate data from suppliers/finance to enable recovery from customers</li>
<li>Report of the Tariff spend and recovery</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements: Related Degree or equivalent</p>
<div>
3-5 years of experience working with international trading/customs (in particular US/Mexico). Knowledge of the current tariffs and NAFTA/USMCA regulations. Ability to work independently to manage tariff responsibilities across functions to drive results in a large global matrix organization. Proficient in Microsoft Office. Excellent written and verbal communication skills. Must be able to travel independently. 10% travel. Must be fluent in English. Must be a US Citizen or permanent resident.</div>
<div></div>

<div><div>About Us</div></div>
<div><div><div>
<p>GKN Automotive is the world’s largest supplier of driveline technologies to the automotive industry. Our engineering puts driveline innovation into series production. Our systems integration expertise and software calibration capabilities make us a strong and trusted partner for electrification, all-wheel drive programs and new vehicle concepts.</p>
<p>
Our global network of production and engineering centres gives the company a presence in 21 countries. Its 54 manufacturing facilities and 5 technology centres are strategically located to ensure customers benefit from local supply chains and world-class technical support.</p>
<p>
With a comprehensive global footprint, we design, develop, manufacture and integrate an extensive range of driveline technologies for over 90% of the world’s automotive manufacturers.</p>
