<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>MyWebGrocer is the leading enterprise software and digital media company connecting Retail Grocers, Consumer Packaged Goods and shoppers through its Digital Experience Platform and holistic set of integrated service offerings. The MWG platform powers every interaction to engage, transact and retain grocery shoppers through digital offerings ranging from planning and shopping platforms to mobile and social tools.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The company also offers opportunities for Consumer Packaged Goods brands looking to reach consumers with relevant advertising, promotions and offers throughout their grocery path to purchase. Founded in 1999, MyWebGrocer manages digital solutions for more than 60 retailers across the globe, representing more than 12,000 stores, and 200+ major consumer packaged goods brands.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
MWG is recruiting for an exceptional digital media sales executive to join our expanding Sales team. This position will focus on driving revenue and building strategic relationships directly with CPG clients, and top agency executives. The ideal candidate must have proven success in driving revenue and meeting/exceeding revenue goals in a fast paced, entrepreneurial environment. We are looking for a consultative sales professional, preferably with CPG experience that can make a direct and immediate impact. You will be supported by our operations team and benefit from working alongside the sales leadership team.
</div>
</div></div></div>
