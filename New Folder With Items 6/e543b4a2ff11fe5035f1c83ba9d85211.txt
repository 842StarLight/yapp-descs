<div><b>
Description</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>To purchase components and services required by Tescom. Selects suppliers for those goods and services within established total quality standards. Assures coordinated efforts in purchasing group by uniform processes, procedures and supplier relations. Manage supplier relationships.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
<b>
</b>Sourcing - Locates sources for critical materials, parts and services. Supplier selection process to be in conformance with division supplier certification policy. Negotiates supplier contracts to minimize overall investment while supporting Tescom’s need.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
<b>Procurement</b> - Performs the following procurement functions:</div>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Purchase Orders – Issues purchase orders to qualified suppliers selected for their capabilities to meet Tescom’s needs.</div></li>
<li><div>
Accurate Database – Assures accurate database by current cost, changes to accounting, quotations, supplier history, and supplier lead-times.</div></li>
<li><div>
Expedite Orders – Expedites at any point in the order and manufacturing process to the degree required to meet customer requirements and communicates situations that will impact delivery.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assure Availability – Assures material availability to support the manufacturing process by placing sub-contract orders and purchase orders to qualified suppliers in response to MRP action notices.</div></li>
<li><div>
Discrepant Material &amp; Certification Requirements – Coordinates discrepant material and certification requirements consistent with policy and delivery schedules.</div></li>
<li><div>
Achieve Cost Reductions – Investigates new methods or products that could benefit cost reduction and product design/redesign.</div></li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div>
<b>Supplier Liaison - </b>Performs supplier liaison functions.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Negotiating</b> - Negotiates contracts to meet Quality requirements (includes cost delivery and certification requirements).</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Supplier Management</b> – Follow-up with suppliers as necessary, to resolve delivery problems, cost changes, or quality issues.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Maintain Files</b> - Maintains accurate files on supplier lead times, part costs, and quotations.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Monitor Supplier Performance</b> – Monitors supplier lead-times, on-time delivery, and quality performance.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Communication</b> – Must have excellent written and verbal communication skills. Should be able to communicate using various types of software (Outlook, MS Word, MS Excel). Must be comfortable communicating at all levels of organizations here at Tescom and at the supplier.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Customer Service</b> – Consistently works towards a 100% customer service level goal.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Satisfy Customer Demand</b> - Works with Sales, Marketing and Manufacturing to satisfy customer demand.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Participate on Teams</b> - Actively participates on cross-function teams.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Advise Management</b> – Advises management of pending supplier strikes, material shortages, capacity constraints or other trends that may affect procurement or profitability of Tescom.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Continuous Improvement</b> – Assists in the development of marketing/manufacturing strategies and objectives including customer service objectives, backlog/lead-time objectives and inventory levels. Actively involves the supplier in continuous improvement projects.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Total Quality</b> - Uses total quality techniques, which include team participation to improve personal and department effectiveness.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Continuing Education</b> - Preserves professional and technical competence by pursuing related continuing education.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Apparent or Assigned</b> - Performs related work as apparent or assigned.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Requirements</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Bachelor’s degree</div></li>
<li><div>
Buying/planning/inventory control experience in a manufacturing environment is preferred.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated ability to communicate (verbal/written) effectively with customers, suppliers, and all employees.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated ability to work in a cooperative and team-oriented environment.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated ability to read part and assembly drawings.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated analytical and planning skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
According to Government Regulations all employees in this job classification must be a US Citizen or permanent resident of the United States.<b>
</b>
</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>Competencies Required</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Action Oriented- Enjoys working hard; is action oriented and full of energy for the things he/she sees as challenging; not fearful of acting with a minimum of planning; seizes more opportunities than others.</div>
<br>
<br>
<div>Dealing with Ambiguity – Can effectively cope with change; can shift gears comfortably; can decide and act without having the total picture; isn’t upset when things are up in the air; doesn’t have to finish things before moving on’ can comfortably handle risk and uncertainty.</div>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>Interpersonal Savvy – Relates well to all kinds of people – up, down, and sideways, inside and outside the organization; builds appropriate rapport; builds constructive and effective relationships; uses diplomacy and tact; can diffuse even high-tension situations comfortably.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Negotiating – Can negotiate skillfully in tough situations with both internal and external groups; can settle differences with minimum noise; can win concessions without damaging relationships; can be both direct and forceful as well as diplomatic; gains trust quickly of other parties to the negotiations; has a good sense of timing.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Priority Setting – Spends his/her time and the time of others on what’s important; quickly zeros in on the critical few and puts the trivial many asides; can quickly sense what will help or hinder accomplishing a goal; eliminates roadblocks; creates focus.</div>
