<h2>Product (Procurement) Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Master Electronics</b> has an exciting career opportunity for a <b>Product (Procurement) Manager </b>that will report to the Director of Product Management in Phoenix, AZ.<br>
<br>
Headquartered in Phoenix, AZ, <b>Master Electronics</b> is a leading global authorized distributor of electronic components. For more than half a century, our family-owned company has remained focused on strong relationships, responsive service and added value. This is how <b>Master Electronics</b> has grown to serve hundreds of thousands of customers in partnership with hundreds of world-class suppliers.<br>
<br>
We’re seeking a candidate who is excited about the opportunity to make a difference as the company evolves and enhances our Product Management team by coordinating and expediting the procurement of components and/or raw materials for our customers. In this job, you will confer with our Sales Department and our suppliers to determine optimal product inventory to fulfill our customer orders. As such, this position is ideal for an individual who is passionate about his/her detail-oriented skills and finds considerable satisfaction with the job environment being more about “creating” vs. “maintaining”.<br>
<br>
<b>How you will spend your time:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Determines the Sales Department’s (and ultimately the end customers’) product demand needs ensuring that the correlating product inventory is available</li>
<li>
Negotiates with suppliers, analyzes purchase trend data and recommends timing and quantity of purchases</li>
<li>
Monitors inventory cycle time/turnover and product availability and adjusts inventory accordingly.</li>
<li>
Creates, submits, and expedites purchase orders that align with business objectives (e.g., availability of product in relation to competitors, gross profit, etc.)</li>
<li>
Responds to inquiries regarding inventory availability and/or associated replenishment lead time questions and requests from Sales team members</li>
<li>
Manages multiple supplier/product lines concurrently and successfully adjusts to changing demand priorities</li>
<li>
Prepare short-term and/or long term product inventory forecasts</li>
<li>
Evaluates the ongoing potential to expand existing supplier relationships for new product to distribute</li>
<li>
Compiles and analyzes reports from proprietary software (i.e. WindX)</li>
<li>
Builds effective relationships with suppliers via targeted strategies (e.g., site visits to suppliers’ facilities, hosting suppliers at Master Electronics distributions sites, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b><br>
<b>We’re excited if you have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 2+ years of buyer, procurement, or materials management experience</li>
<li>
Proficient in the use of Microsoft (MS) Office, with focus on demonstrated working MS Excel experience</li>
<li>
Proficient with navigating proprietary business software applications (e.g., sales, inventory management, etc.)</li>
<li>
Ability to access vendor history data for reporting purposes</li>
<li>
Ability to apply creative problem solving techniques to situations using sound business judgment</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task in an effective, timely and professional manner</li>
<li>
Proven ability to apply attention to detail, role-related accuracy and task follow-through</li>
<li>
Team-oriented operating style with effective interpersonal skills that allow successful business interactions with individuals of all leadership styles, personalities, and career levels (e.g. sales, accounting, logistics, warehouses)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b><br>
<b>We would prefer if you have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of an Associates degree (e.g. A.A., A.S., etc.)</li>
<li>
Previous buying/procurement experience in the electronics/electrical component industry</li>
<li>
Sales career experience successfully achieving pre-assigned quotas</li>
<li>
A strong literacy in electronic/electrical components</li>
</ul>
