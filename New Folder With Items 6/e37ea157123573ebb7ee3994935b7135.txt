<h2>concierge</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">your role
<br>-
<br><br>
Reporting directly to the senior operations manager, you will be tailoring the guest experience from beginning to end to make sure our guest stays are everything they imagined and more. We're looking for well-rounded folks who can create amazing arrival experiences, handle guests requests, and be the friendly face of The Guild!
<br><br>
Although the position is oriented towards Front Desk Operations, our lean corporate structure allows you to work on projects spanning many functions, like general operations, Field Guide tasks and Sales. By getting into the startup game early, this role offers uncapped opportunities to grow.
<br><br>
what you'll do
<br>-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Handle all guest interactions with the highest level of hospitality and professionalism, accommodating special requests whenever possible.</li>
<li>Resolve customer complaints, assist customers with all inquiries in connection with hotel services, hours of operations, key hotel personnel, in-house events, directions, etc.</li>
<li>Respond to all guest requests with accuracy and in a timely manner, making recommendations based on local knowledge.</li>
<li>Work harmoniously and professionally with co-workers and supervisors.</li>
<li>Assure that desk collateral and information for restaurants, museums, attractions, maps, and other local attractions are updated and current.</li>
<li>Possessing knowledge of what activities are available in the city (theatre, sports, concerts, shows, special exhibits, sightseeing) and establish close contact with vendors in these areas in order to provide information, ticketing, and reservations for guests.</li>
<li>The physical ability to store and retrieve guest luggage and packages.</li>
<li>The ability to utilize a variety of software programs to accurately input special arrangements the guest has made and to assure proper billing.</li>
</ul>
