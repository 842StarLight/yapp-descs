<h2>Affiliate and Influencer Recruiting Specialist (Part Time)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Company:</b> Tactical Marketing Co. is an online marketing agency that specializes in AFFILIATE MANAGEMENT. We find affiliates and influencers to create content and post links to our client’s websites and products.
 </p>
<p><b>What is Affiliate Marketing?</b></p>
<p>
A marketing arrangement by which an online retailer pays commission to an external website for traffic or sales generated from its referrals.</p>
<p>
Our ideal candidate is someone who enjoys digital marketing and has excellent written communication skills. A lot of time spent will be using various online tools to identify good partners for our clients and reaching out to these website owners and influencers to join that clients’ affiliate program.</p>
<p><b>
The Position</b>: This role will consist of research good partners for our clients and reaching out to them (via email) to join an affiliate program.
</p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Killer communication skills, ability to talk about a wide variety of products and interests via email.
</li>
<li>Organized. This position will be responsible for keeping and updating a lot of lists.
</li>
<li>Comfort working with online software tools and basic software apps like Excel.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li>Find new affiliate partners for our clients and keeping a solid line of communication open with them at all times.
</li></ul>
