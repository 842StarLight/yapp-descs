<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h1>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Title</b><b>:</b> Commercial Relationship Manager</p>
<p><b>Division</b><b>:</b> Commercial Lending</p>
<p><b>Reports Directly To</b><b>:</b> Regional/Community President<br>
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>BASIC PURPOSE:</b></h2>
<p>This position is responsible for, but not limited to, originating CRE loans and for cross-selling CenterState products to commercial accounts. Incumbents are expected to meet or exceed production goals.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></h2>
<p>This position is expected to perform the specific duties, tasks, and responsibilities as outlined below:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Interviews loan applicants, and collects and analyzes financial and related data to determine the general creditworthiness of the prospect and the merits of the specific loan request.</p></li>
<li><p>
Establishes, and negotiates terms under which credit will be extended, including the costs, repayment method, and schedule and collateral requirements.</p></li>
<li><p>
Collects and analyzes information that reflects the current creditworthiness of customers and the current merits of existing loans. May obtain information through direct inspection of the applicant’s business and/or collateral, review of interim financial reports, personal interview, and other means.
</p></li>
<li><p>Presents loan requests to the executive loan committee when the proposed loan dollar volume exceeds the department’s lending authority.</p></li>
<li><p>
Authorizes or rejects overdrafts in depository account within established authorities.</p></li>
<li><p>
Monitors loan repayment activities and takes necessary action to collect from past-due accounts.</p></li>
<li><p>
Advise customers, where possible, concerning business management and financial matters.
</p></li>
<li><p>Serves as a contact officer for non-borrowing accounts and loan customers for matters relating to other bank services.</p></li>
<li><p>
Develops new business by contacting prospects and customers, cross-sells bank services.</p></li>
<li><p>
Prepares lending activity reports for department management.</p></li>
<li><p>
Keeps abreast of local and national economic, financial, political and legislative events and climates which could have an impact on corporate and commercial lending activities.
</p></li>
<li><p>Participates in community affairs to increase the bank’s visibility and to enhance new business opportunities.</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintains professional and community relations to increase the bank’s visibility and new business opportunities, and to further personal development.</p></li>
<li><p>
Performs other specifically assigned duties.</p></li>
