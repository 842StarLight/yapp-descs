<h2>General Manager (GM) - Air Compressors</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>Sector: Machinery / Air Compressors<br>Title: General Manager<br>Status: Regular, Full-Time<br>Territory: US</p>
<p>Time: By end of August, early September</p>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>Air compressor company is looking to hire a motivated General Manager for a growing family business. Main business consists of recurring maintenance on customer’s compressed air systems. Work on compressors is done at customer’s location and/or our shop. Well established small business running for 29 years in central Omaha.</p>
<p>Normal business hours Monday – Friday 8AM to 5PM.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>Territory Management:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and manage the sales strategic plan annually keeping in mind the ability to maximize the market potential and ability to attain goals set forth.</li>
<li>Increase market share through products sales and service.</li>
<li>Manage day-to-day operations in sales office</li>
<li>Motivate staff to meet or exceed company expectations.</li>
<li>Deliver quality experiences at all levels to customers.</li>
<li>Set up and manage service appointments and record data from customer outreach in Quickbooks Management Software Suite</li>
<li>Create and maintain databases which capture appropriate statistics to manage the market side of our business</li>
<li>Collaborate with Service Manager to support the technicians out in the field</li>
<li>Protect and manage the financial resources of the company.</li>
<li>Communicate the needs of the customers &amp; staff to the appropriate department within the company who are responsible for follow-up action.</li>
</ul>
<p>Sales Team Communication and Accountability:</p>
<ul>
<li>Hold 15 minute meetings with sales staff daily to keep team abreast of latest information.</li>
<li>Participate in weekly team meeting on Fridays</li>
<li>Present market share data and competitive trends.</li>
</ul>
<p>Sales Training</p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct training</li>
<li>Team up with new sales and service specialists, orientating them to the company and assist in securing sales.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirement</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering, Business Administration, Marketing, or related field, or equivalent related experience preferred</li>
<li>Experience with commercial marketing, air compressor industry, selling or maintaining air compressor products strongly encouraged</li>
<li>Experience with territory and sales management.</li>
<li>Construction, agricultural equipment, auto, and other machinery experience preferred.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Self-motivated / Self-Starter</li>
<li>2 &gt;5 years of experience preferred</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Goal oriented</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Proficient or can learn Microsoft Office, Quickbooks and Smart Phone Apps</li>
<li>Can learn product knowledge quickly (reciprocating, scroll, rotary screw, vane compressors, and more).</li>
<li>Willingness to continue to learn and grow</li>
<li>Travel required: Little</li>
<li>Insurance and vacation benefits</li>
<li>Standard American Holidays</li>
</ul>
