<h2>Customer Success Manager - Enterprise</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Our mission is to help people everywhere find a job and company they love. We are disrupting an industry by changing how people search for jobs and how companies recruit top talent.
<br>
<br>
The Customer Success Manager (CSM) is an energetic customer facing professional who will engage, retain and drive our customers' understanding of Glassdoor product offerings, as well as, the growing Employer Branding and Online Recruiting space. The CSM is measured on the retention of a book of business within the portfolio of Glassdoor's customers. The ideal candidate will be as passionate about Glassdoor's solutions as they are about providing an exceptional experience for every customer.
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b>:
<ul>
<li>Act as a trusted advisor, thought leader, and subject matter expert to customers in relation to the full suite of Glassdoor's products</li>
<li>Onboard and educate customers about Glassdoor's products</li>
<li>Produce and conduct Executive Business Reviews, engaging with client counterparts at various levels of the organization as well as potential Agency partners</li>
<li>Monitor and manage employer content and recruitment advertising campaigns including pay-per-click (PPC), job slots management, display advertising, and profile branding to ensure consistent product performance and delivery</li>
<li>Partner and work very closely with partners in Sales to define and achieve customers' success criteria, demonstrate ROI, and ensure customer renewal with Glassdoor product suite.</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between internal teams to ensure customer needs are being met; coordinating amongst implementation, sales, marketing, finance, and product teams</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a deep understanding of customers' business drivers and recruiting goals, and steer program activities to align with those initiatives</li>
</ul>

<b>Requirements:</b>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>3-5 years of experience in B2B environments with preference for customer success, account management, sales or consulting background</li>
<li>Previous experience in Pay-per-Click (PPC) advertising campaigns or online recruiting highly beneficial. Basic understanding of Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and/or HTML, XML, advertising tracking mechanisms a plus</li>
<li>Enthusiasm for online advertising/media, marketing, and/or talent acquisition space a must!</li>
<li>Experience using SFDC (or similar CRM) and Customer Success Analytics platforms such as Gainsight or Totango are a plus</li>
<li>Occasional travel for in-person customer meetings</li>
<li>Commitment to Glassdoor's mission and values</li>
<li>Proven record of successfully being measured to retention goals is a plus</li>
