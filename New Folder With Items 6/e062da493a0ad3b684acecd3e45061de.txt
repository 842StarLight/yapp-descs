<p>Advertising Sales Representative – New York City</p>
<p>We’re looking for talented, passionate go-getters looking for a position in sales and marketing.</p>
<p>We offer a base salary, bonuses, uncapped commissions, cell phone allowances, 401K and healthcare.</p>
<p>Superior products, great personnel and commitment to provide our clients with the very best are the fundamentals that have allowed Vector to grow exponentially over the past few years. If reading this gets you excited to learn more about who we are and where we’re headed, apply NOW!</p>
<p>Position Overview:</p>
<p>Vector Media has an immediate opening for a full time Account Executive in the NYC area. Applicants must possess excellent communication skills and be highly organized. Applicant must be capable of working in a deadline-oriented environment handling daily tasks including but not limited to prospecting and developing new customer relationships, persuasively communicating strategies for meeting customer needs with the benefits of outdoor advertising products, and ensuring customer satisfaction by facilitating all aspects of the customer’s account in cooperation with creative, operations, marketing and finance staff.</p>
<p>Required Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years media sales experience is a must</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of the NYC area and its potential for revenue</li>
<li>Candidate must be detail oriented, articulate and with strong communication skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated professional sales presentation skills.</li>
<li>Responsible for revenue generation and meeting sales targets.</li>
<li>Establish, maintain and grow relationships with clients and agencies.</li>
<li>Develop new leads by cold calling and making appointments.</li>
<li>Prepare compelling and competitive proposals and presentations.</li>
<li>May negotiate fees or services with clients with management approval.</li>
<li>Communicate sale to chartering and other internal departments.</li>
<li>Develop posting instructions and distribute to internal teams.</li>
<li>Oversee project from start to finish and ensure clients satisfaction.</li>
<li>Delivery proof of posting (POP) to clients.</li>
<li>Enhanced computer skills</li>
<li>May ride along with clients to show outdoor media sites.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned or requested.</li>
</ul>
