<b>The goal of the role:</b><br>
Our B2B Business Development Executive is responsible for hitting sales quotas targeting mid to large market B2B companies ($20Mil - $1Bil Annual Sales). This includes leveraging existing network, lead generation leveraging various prospecting strategies &amp; various lead channels to develop and manage a healthy pipeline. This job will play a key role in assisting the B2B Senior Sales Director and B2B Division attaining growth goals. Additionally this job will work with the other sales support teams (Prospect Development, Partner Sales &amp; Integrated Sales) as well as other support teams within the B2B division.
<br>What will you be doing?
<br><ul>
<li>Ensuring the successful performance and profit generation of the company</li>
<li>Developing effective sales strategy and ensure its timely implementation</li>
<li>Communicating effectively in both verbal and written formats with individuals or groups</li>
<li>Handling and completing multiple assignments, working in a fast-paced environment, and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Making outbound sales calls to potential referral partners to establish interest in becoming an active referral partner of EVO</li>
<li>Soliciting referral agreements and productivity commitments for merchant account referrals for merchant processing services</li>
<li>Coordinating with supervisors to negotiate terms of referral agreements for the purpose of establishing a mutually beneficial partnership</li>
<li>If necessary, coordinating efforts of the certification process for POS software products as they relate to referral partners</li>
<li>Providing education, training and relationship management services to existing referral partners in order to increase referral activity</li>
<li>Working with channel sales managers to assign referral partners to various EVO sales channels for ongoing referral sourcing and service</li>
<li>Monitoring activity and service levels of referral partners with regular interaction with assigned EVO sales representatives</li>
<li>Maintaining referral partner assignment lists and reporting</li>
<li>Logging all interactions with partners in internal database such as Salesforce</li>
<li>Identifying and providing input to supervisor on any potential referral partner concerns, risks and competitive information including market trends</li>
<li>Conducting sales strategy meetings with referral partners in order to strengthen relationship and increase qualified referral activity</li>
<li>Creating and executing email sales marketing campaigns for referral partner channels</li>
<li>Maintaining thorough knowledge of card industry regulations, integrated card payment devices and applications and internal procedures</li>
<li>Maintaining current knowledge of EVO products, services and processes</li>
<li>Attending all new product and service training sessions as assigned by supervisor</li>
<li>Assisting Sales Account Executives as needed with related questions as it pertain to referral partners that are integrated with EVO</li>
<li>Utilizing personal computer, telephone, facsimile, copier, calculator, and other general office equipment</li>
<li>Exercising discretion and independent judgment in making decisions</li>
<li>Interacting effectively and positively with all levels of personnel, partners, and vendors</li>
<li>Handling confidential material appropriately</li>
<li>Meeting company assigned goals and quotas</li>
<li>Assisting in recruiting and training sales employees for effective delivery</li>
</ul>
