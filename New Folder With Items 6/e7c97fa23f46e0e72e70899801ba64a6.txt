<b>SUMMARY:</b> The CSR responsibilities include the processing of customer orders received either in person, via telephone, fax or internet. The CSR will assist the Branch Manager/Branch Operations Manager and other branch associates as needed. The CSR will also assist in daily task performance including invoicing; receiving and locating inventory, etc.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Operations</div>
<ul>
<li>Accurately perform order entry process, to include product, quantity and price verification</li>
<li>Confirm orders placed by customers via phone/fax/internet</li>
<li>Perform stock and price checks for customers and other branches</li>
<li>Provide freight/shipment information (cost/tracking/etc) to customers/branches in a timely and accurate manner</li>
<li>Apply freight and handling expenses to orders so as to be able to effectively invoice customers for related expenses</li>
<li>Regularly follow up with customers as to the status of their order(s)</li>
<li>Make collection (A/R) calls/contacts as directed by Branch Manager/Branch Operations Manager</li>
<li>Perform other daily tasks as directed by Branch Manager/Branch Operations Manager</li>
</ul>
<div>Sales Related Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li>Promote add-on sales at the time of order entry via the promotion of sundries and other related items</li>
<li>Assist trade and showroom customers in the selection of products</li>
<li>Acquire a high level of product knowledge about the company’s products and be able to effectively respond to inquiries as they relate to the recommend (or restricted) use</li>
<li>Maintain the CSR work area in an organized manner that is conducive to sales and service</li>
<li>Provide support to the OSR/BCSS and Design Sales Consultant in the performance of their duties. Assist as directed by Branch Manager/Branch Operations Manager</li>
<li>Additional duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Time Management: the ability to organize and manage multiple priorities at once.</li>
<li>Customer service experience with a manufacturer of building products preferred.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>SAP software experience, CMS experiences a plus.</li>
<li>High School diploma or GED required.</li>
<li>Computer proficiency in Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Team player with commitment to company values.</li>
</ul>
