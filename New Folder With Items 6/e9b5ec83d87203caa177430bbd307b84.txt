<h2>Business Intelligence Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Our Business Intelligence department is looking for a <b>Business Intelligence Analyst</b> to join their team. The Business Intelligence Analyst is responsible for providing critical management dynamic dashboards, reporting, and predictive data analysis for business units within the organization <b>.<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Business Intelligence Analyst</b> <b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Creates business intelligence reporting, dashboards, data visualizations, data mining, and predictive models developed per use specifications to help drive strategic decision making and to measure improvements in all key performance indicators and operational metrics.
</li>
<li>Develops and analyzes meaningful periodic and ad-hoc reports by extracting data from a variety of database systems used in all aspects of business operations.</li>
<li>Develops interactive and dynamic dashboards and data visualizations to promote end user self-service for data analysis needed.</li>
<li>Develops databases for gathering statistics that are currently handled manually. Works with IT or external vendors to automate functionality when possible to improve the efficiency in gathering and disseminating data and reports to business units.</li>
<li>Interfaces with managers, verbally and in writing, to understand the business needs and develops/updates reporting based on changing business environment.
</li>
<li>Assists in the development of metrics as well as the forecasting of sales trends and expenses related to loan origination for budget purposes, and in tracking and forecasting product profitability and pricing.
</li>
<li>Verifies data integrity on data analysis and logic. Identifies issues with data integrity and works collaboratively with data owners to resolve.</li>
<li>Prepares financial, regulatory, and Board/Committee Level reporting as needed.</li>
<li>Evaluates economic, market, and competitor trends to provide additional context to reports and recommendations.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>Business Intelligence Analyst</b> <b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Two or four year degree in computer science, information technology, finance, mathematics, or other related field, or equivalent combination of education and relevant experience.</li>
<li>Minimum 3-5 years’ experience in predictive analytics, reporting/analysis, and Microsoft SQL Server and/or Oracle database experience in an enterprise environment. Equivalent coursework may be given consideration, but experience as described above is preferred.</li>
<li>Professional proficiency in Microsoft SQL Server, SSRS, SSAS, and Oracle database environments.</li>
<li>Expert proficiency in Power BI and other 3rd party visualization packages.</li>
<li>Professional proficiency in PL-SQL and T-SQL query languages
</li>
<li>Excellent analytical skills including trend analysis and data modeling.</li>
<li>General knowledge of finance and accounting as related to loan and deposit products.</li>
<li>Ability to understand business needs and translate them into application functionality.
</li>
<li>Outstanding verbal and written communications skills as well as presentation skills.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work independently, take initiative, problem solve, handle multiple tasks/priorities with ease.
</li>
<li>Proven ability to work collaboratively in a team-oriented environment.
</li>
</ul>
