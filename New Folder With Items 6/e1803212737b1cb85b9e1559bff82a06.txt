<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<div><div>
<div>The Digital Product Manager (DPM) is the IT Lead accountable for delivering Digital Products, working closely with the Project Manager and Product Owner throughout the product lifecycle. Responsibilities include co-creating product vision, executing discovery and customer research, accelerated delivery from concept to first cash and aligning product roadmap with the Application Delivery Lead to facilitate scaling within the target portfolio. The DPM will have full accountability managing the relevant stakeholders, budget, resources, technology roadmap, strategic dependencies, execution, maintenance of the product and business alignment. To fill this technology leadership role we’re looking for someone who can inspire Product Owners on the art of the possible when it comes to shaping our go-to-market strategy.<br>
<br>
You’ll own product design and development through to releases of minimum viable products and lead strong team members including Product Managers, Business Analysts and multiple Suppliers who, together, provide full-stack capability. By transforming delivery from Agile to DevOps, you’ll help them to respond more swiftly to new business demands, to improve our speed to market and to better harness the expertise of our Competence Centre.<br>
<br>
Clearly, there’s huge scope to innovate and make an individual impression. Ultimately, you’ll be key to realising our business vision for Digital Payments, Loyalty, and Convenience Retail offerings, helping us to increase market penetration, customer sales and brand loyalty both in existing markets and by exploiting growth opportunities.<br>
<br>
<b>DEPARTMENT PURPOSE:</b><br>
<br>
Shell Retail Global Marketing IT Competence Centre are the driving force behind an omni-channel experience that includes our flagship Shell App (Mobile) as well as Web, Connected Car, Connected Store and API touchpoints. Here, you’ll be instrumental to the delivery management of a complex, heterogenous and highly integrated technology landscape with a multi-million-dollar annual investment and spanning Single Sign On (SSO), Mobile technology, Web technologies, eCommerce, Web Analytics, API Management, PaaS and SaaS services.<br>
<br>
<b>MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Partnering with Product Owner(s) to co-create product vision, roadmap, scope and prioritize product backlog based on hypotheses, customer needs, research, maturity of product, dependencies etc..</li>
<li>
Own full product lifecycle including product discovery, agile delivery and operations.</li>
<li>
Serve as a thought leader to the Product Owner on the art of the possible and establishing/maintaining product roadmap, to MVP and beyond.</li>
<li>
Fully embed customer experience design, design thinking, Agile delivery and dev ops through relevant practices and metrics.</li>
<li>
Relentless prioritization to maintain focus on racing from concept to proof of value, whilst balancing technical debt with scalability and product roadmap.</li>
<li>
Ensure secure, reliable and efficient operations for the product, aligned with product roadmap objectives.</li>
<li>
Provide vision and direction to the agile development team and stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Manage the Digital Product through the investment cycle driven by value generating KPIs (taking a similar approach to a venture capital firm funding a start-up).</li>
<li>
Proactively manage the formation, evolution and succession planning of the cross-functional product team.</li>
<li>
Provide an active role in mitigating impediments impacting successful team completion of Release/Sprint Goals.</li>
<li>
Collaborate and work with Vendors and Suppliers for the delivery of Sprint goals.</li>
<li>
Partner closely with the ADL to align with the overall portfolio demand, capacity, business road map and value.</li>
<li>
Establish transition plan to move product from incubation to target portfolio, taking into account scaling and operational readiness needs within a single product backlog to ensure holistic prioritization and dependency management.</li>
</ul>

WHAT DOES SUCCESS LOOK LIKE IN THIS JOB?</b><br>
</div>

Speed from concept to proof of value, enabled by Agile delivery and effective risk management.</li>

Alignment with target portfolio and Product Owner to transition from Incubation to scaled roll-out after proof of value and aligned with product roadmap.</li>

Close monitoring of all delivery aspects (Budget/Resources/Stakeholders/Governance) associated to the development and operation of the product.</li>

Maintain product investment and delivery hygiene (cost and resource management).</li>

<p><b>Company Description</b></p>
<div>Shell is a global group of energy and petrochemical companies with about 84,000 employees across more than 70 countries. We aim to meet the world’s growing need for more and cleaner energy solutions in ways that are economically, environmentally and socially responsible. We have expertise in exploration, production, refining and marketing of oil and natural gas, and the manufacturing and marketing of chemicals. As a global energy company operating in a challenging world, we set high standards of performance and ethical behaviors. We are judged by how we act and how we live up to our core values of honesty, integrity and respect for people. Our Business Principles are based on these. They promote trust, openness, teamwork and professionalism, as well as pride in what we do and how we conduct business.Building on our core values, we aspire to sustain a diverse and inclusive culture where everyone feels respected and valued, from our employees to our customers and partners. A diverse workforce and an inclusive work environment are vital to our success, leading to greater innovation and better energy solutions.</div>
