<p>Strategic Account Manager</p>
<p>Join a team recognized for leadership, innovation and diversity</p>
<p>We don’t just sell things. We offer solutions to tomorrow’s challenges.
</p>
<p>Our sales approach begins by identifying customer demands before they become challenges. We’re committed to delivering customer success through our comprehensive expertise in software and technology.</p>
<p>
Why You Should Join Us? We are a $40 billion diversified Fortune 100 leader with more than 131,000 employees in 100 countries worldwide. We invent, build and manufacture technologies that address some of the world’s toughest challenges. Honeywell is the world leader in energy &amp; NOC Services utilizing our cloud platform software for building device to enterprise solutions serving the markets for intelligent buildings, energy information systems, and IoT (Internet of Things) applications. We are changing the way the world connects smart devices into the operation of their businesses, helping our customers achieve superior operations for less money and in less time. If you desire an exciting, challenging opportunity with extraordinary earning potential, then we invite you to apply. We believe our people make Honeywell a special company and are a key competitive advantage. We are looking for a dynamic Strategic Account Manager for the Midwest. As an Account Manager, you will be responsible for leading and growing your account base and territory. You will champion client satisfaction by maintaining regular customer contact and handling customer expectations. You will drive business growth by discovering new opportunities, clients, and customers. You will deliver value by forging new strategic relationships. You will maintain, and provide reports and opportunity status using our customer relationship management system. You will grow your knowledge of Honeywell products in a team-based culture focused on innovation and customer satisfaction.</p>
<p>
Help drive integrated solutions sales that bring together both hardware and software products</p>
<p>
Contribute to the larger team through the application of new products, principles, theories and concepts in your field of expertise to develop unique solutions for customers.</p>
<p>
Be a zealot for growth by driving continuous improvement within Solutions Sales to ensure customer’s top choice is Honeywell.</p>
<p>
We expect you to be comfortable with up to 60% travel, including overnights. We can offer you incredible career progression, as well as an attractive remuneration package.</p>
