<h2>New Sales Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Company
<br><br>
Worldpay is a global payments leader powering international commerce with deep fintech expertise and a shared passion for our customers. Whether in-store, online, or on a mobile device, we process over 40 billion transactions annually and offer more than 300 payment methods supporting 126 currencies across 146 countries. It's the perfect place for exceptional people to take their careers to the next level.
<br><br>
The Opportunity
<br><br>
Vantiv's growth is on the backs of every single team member. Building relationships with our end-users also entails listening to their needs to understand what other products and services may resolve their issues. By building a team focused on sales to our customers, we can continue to grow through channels we already have open. Further, by addressing hurdles for our current customers, we can continue to build trust and assure our future as their choice in a competitive market.
<br><br>
The Enterprise New Sales Representative will be dedicated to developing new accounts and expanding accounts with clients and partners already on board with Vantiv. By focusing in on an established geographic region, he/she will be able to target areas for growth by reviewing and analyzing business trends in the location. He/she will also make an impact on Vantiv's growth by working with established customers and clients to find new solutions for their evolving needs in our catalog of products and services.

The Day-to-Day
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for new account development and/or expanding existing accounts within an established geographic territory.</li>
<li>Exhibits a level of professional expertise and applies company policies and procedures to resolve a variety of issues.</li>
<li>Has working knowledge of company products and services.</li>
<li>Responsible for selling the company's products or services as well as developing new accounts and/or expanding existing accounts.</li>
<li>Sells through a specific channel with his/her own accounts, or working on a team.</li>
<li>Responsible for small- to medium-sized accounts or have a small- or medium-sized quota/territory.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Typically requires a minimum of 1 year field sales experience. Usually 2-5 years of field sales experience.</li>
</ul>
