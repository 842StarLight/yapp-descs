<h2>Personal Lines Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>REMOTE Personal Lines Account Manager</b></p>
<p><b>SUMMARY </b></p>
<p>This position will provide support to the customers, andProducers and Account Managers in the Personal Lines Department. CustomerService will consist of discussing coverage, recommending coverage, requestingand making policy changes, handing billing inquiries, verifying accountinformation, answering customers’ questions, taking claims information, andgenerating appropriate documents. Sales support will consist of developing newaccounts, quoting new business, maintaining existing accounts, cross-sellingexisting account, and forwarding submission packages to the appropriatecarrier.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES ANDRESPONSIBILITIES </b></p>
<p>· Accurately maintain customer information in theagency management system (AMS360).</p>
<p>· Assist the Account Managers with the followingtasks:</p>
<p>· Process daily mail consisting of paymentnotices, and company claim information.</p>
<p>· Issue auto ID cards, certificates of insurance,and evidences of property insurance. (Obtain the correct information from thecustomer in order to issue above documents).</p>
<p>· Order MVRs and financial reports perinstructions from Account Manager or Producer.</p>
<p>· Process and follow up on underwriting memos</p>
<p>· Follow up on policies that cancel.</p>
<p>· Assist customers with billing questions andprocess carrier payments, as needed.</p>
<p>· Assist with the collection of premiums andreconciliation of accounts as needed.</p>
<p>· Service all existing personal lines accountsby:</p>
<p>· Speaking with customer and company personnel onthe phone and responding to all written correspondence.</p>
<p>· Answering coverage questions, recommendingcoverages, processing policy changes, taking claim information, collectingpremiums, submitting documents to the carriers and verifying that all documentsreceived from the carriers are correct</p>
<p>· Resolving customer problems and complains (withassistance/direction from Account Managers as needed).</p>
<p>· Learn the various company products, underwritingrequirements, binding authority, policy forms, rating software programs (upload&amp; download), and competitiveness in the marketplace.</p>
<p>· Process daily download from carriers, printappropriate reports and verify download policy information accuracy.</p>
<p>· Meet with Company marketing representatives tolearn of new products or changes.</p>
<p>· Participate in reviewing, developing, rewriting,and implementing of agency procedures and marketing plans.</p>
<p>· Participate in community activities as an agencyrepresentative.</p>
<p>· Promote and enhance the reputation of theagency.</p>
<p>· Quality check all work</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS </b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be ableto perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed beloware representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonableaccommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform theessential functions.</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/orEXPERIENCE </b></p>
<p>Education: Minimum High School Diploma or GED</p>
<p>Experience: 2 years insurance experience.</p>
<p>Ability to apply common sense understanding, interpret, andcarry out instructions furnished in written or oral form to insurance andgeneral office situations. Must be able to work with minimal supervision. Theability to recognize and solve customer problem that are related to theirinsurance policies is required.</p>
