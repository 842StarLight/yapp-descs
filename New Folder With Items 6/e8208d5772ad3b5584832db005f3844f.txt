What you'll do
<br><br>
DoubleVerify is seeking a highly motivated Senior Revenue Operations Coordinator. We are looking for a hands on, self-starter who thrives in a fast-paced environment.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage the daily upload of invoices from our Sales Force CRM System to our NetSuite Financial System.</li>
<li>Reconcile Salesforce data to NetSuite Invoiced Data.</li>
<li>Understanding of Global billing/Multi Currency Billing and Client Contract Language.</li>
<li>Respond to a high volume of daily e-mails and client inquiries for missing monthly invoices, discrepancies etc. (Ability to flag e-mails and follow up for resolution)</li>
<li>Review daily priorities to ensure that scheduled responsibilities are addressed.</li>
<li>Resolve billing discrepancies by forwarding on to the appropriate teams for resolution and/or possible adjustments.</li>
<li>Review, prioritize and address email inquiries from clients and internal DV teams.</li>
<li>Assist clients with daily needs such as missing invoices, change of address requests, change of customer terms, and campaign spend information, etc.</li>
<li>Generate ad-hoc reports for clients and our internal teams to confirm final revenue by customer/advertiser/campaigns etc.</li>
<li>Check Sales Force Dashboards for unbilled invoices that are ready to bill, special clients, and adjustments.</li>
<li>Assist with the client monthly PO process and submission of invoices into our customer portals.</li>
<li>Manage the invoicing of consolidated invoices to select clients</li>
<li>Manage and delegate the processing of adjustments mass adjustments (High Volume Adjustments)</li>
<li>Provide support to the AR Team with respect to updating invoice data and re-sending invoice to the customer.</li>
<li>Assist with annual audit</li>
<li>Assist in testing of our system enhancements and validating data when new functionality rolls out.</li>
</ul>
Who you are
<br><br>

<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>

<li>An understanding of accounting and billing principles</li>
<li>Familiar with multi-currency invoicing</li>
<li>Proficiency in Excel</li>
<li>Knowledge of media billing a plus</li>
<li>Salesforce &amp; NetSuite experience a plus</li>
<li>Excellent attention to details</li>
<li>Willing and eager to learn with a positive attitude</li>
<li>Experienced with high volume billing &amp; client accounts</li>
</ul>
