<h2>CBX ( Cyclebar Experience Associate) (Cedar Park)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are currently seeking high energy, passion filled, and sales motivated individuals who are fitness minded and have a love for community, and our brand! <br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent sales, communication, and customer service skills required</li>
<li>Must have genuine hospitality and passion for people</li>
<li>Goal-oriented with an ability to achieve sales in memberships and retail</li>
<li>Ability to learn and use the Club Ready software system</li>
<li>Must be fluent in English and have excellent communication skills via in person, phone and email</li>
<li>Must be able to work under pressure and meet tight deadlines</li>
<li>Must have proficient computer skills</li>

</ul>

<li>Must be willing to clean CycleTheater, Bathrooms, Lobby, etc.</li>
<li>Must be able to lift up to 30 lbs</li>
</ul>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>

<li>Maintain brand standards and initiatives</li>
</ul>
<b>Benefits:</b><br>
