<h2>Production Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Position Overview:</b><br>
We're a leading global fitness company seeking a talented, passionate production designer to join our internal marketing team. Under the direction of the graphic production manager, the production designer is responsible for developing final mechanical files from creative layouts for print and digital production on assigned projects. You will work on a wide range of integrated marketing materials across multiple brands; execute feedback and changes while building final mechanical print or digital files for release to our global teams and vendors including catalogs, banners, ads, product launches, brand platforms, style guides, and web assets/deliverables.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><b>Design Process:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Organizes and refines the visual design of projects</li>
<li>Facilitates the production of design solutions from creative team</li>
<li>Teach/enable design team to produce quality release files</li>
</ul>
<b>Collateral Creation:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Works with designers and production manager to determine appropriate production handoff points</li>
<li>Works with marketing managers to determine appropriate final deliverables needed for projects</li>
<li>Ensures rounds and final mechanicals are accurate and error free, including file construction, organization and prepping of files for printing or digital output</li>
<li>Attends press checks as needed; helps approve printer proofs</li>
</ul>
Digital Design
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Assists in providing web optimized graphic support for all digital properties</li>
<li>Works with marketing managers and user experience team to produce effective final content for our global brands and relevant ecommerce initiatives</li>
</ul>
<b>Project Management:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Project management responsibilities including planning, evaluating, organizing, and integrating projects</li>
<li>Relies on experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals</li>
<li>Assists graphics team as needed in overseeing projects and project management</li>
</ul>
<b>Develop Industry Knowledge and Network:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Stays up to date on industry trends and styles</li>
<li>Participates with peers in professional development, classes or associations</li>
<li>Strives to constantly improve skill sets: production best practices, layout, printing processes and communication</li>
</ul>
<b>Project Management and Leadership Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Engages in the typical responsibilities of planning, evaluating, organizing and integrating a project</li>
<li>Encourages designers, marketing managers, product managers and copywriter to communicate and provide accurate content for purposes of production efficiency</li>
</ul>
<b>Marginal Job Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Completes special projects as required</li>
<li>Enforces the routing and approving of artwork samples</li>
<li>Assists marketing/sales teams with image/digital requests.</li>
</ul>
