<h2>Membership Salesperson</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Gym Membership Sales</b></h3>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Major Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Promote gym attendance via flyers and membership raffle entries.</li>
<li>Convince prospective guests to have urgency to begin a fitness journey.</li>
<li>Follow up with members and guests using current Gold’s Gym tools.</li>
<li>Conduct gym tours using established Gold’s Gym sales systems.</li>
<li>Build and continually develop referral generation sources through member and community engagement.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>All Staff</b><b> Expectations</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Build and maintain a positive relationship with all staff, club members and guests.</li>
<li>Participate in training and development. Seek management assistance as needed.</li>
<li>Maintain professional appearance and work habits.</li>
<li>Be prompt and ready to work each day.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Successful Salespeople will possess many of the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>PASSIONATE about Fitness!</li>
<li>Highly charismatic, convincing and energetic.</li>
<li>Approachable and sociable. Be comfortable and confident conversing with strangers.</li>
<li>Maintains personal health and fitness habits/choices.</li>
<li>Eager to continuously learn and seeking long term growth.</li>
<li>Technologically savvy, capable of utilizing basic software.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Benefits May Include:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>401K with Company Matching.</li>
<li>Health and Dental Insurance.</li>
<li>Paid Time Off</li>
<li>Free Gym Membership (All Club Access)</li>
<li>Promotion opportunities within the company.</li>
</ul>
