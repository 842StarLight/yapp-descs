<p>Pilot Freight Services has an immediate career opening for a Sales Account Executive.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with District Manager and sales team to develop the territory, account and coverage plans for existing and new accounts;</li>
<li>Develop and maintain functional knowledge of the products, service and operations offered and actively participate in product training sessions;</li>
<li>Interact regularly with local management and develop close ad cooperative working relationship with operations staff to ensure the customer's needs are met;</li>
<li>Work cooperatively with other sales and operational staff to support a team-selling environment;</li>
<li>Ensure incoming and outgoing sales leads and routing orders are followed-up on;</li>
<li>Assist sales team by identifying opportunities, generating interest, qualifying prospects, educating and promoting awareness and developing new sales leads;</li>
<li>Develop relationships with selected clients and prospects in order to set appointments and expand the company's brand awareness by accurate need identification and solution positioning;</li>
<li>Follow-up with new clients to ensure customer satisfaction with the serviced being performed;</li>
<li>Assist the sales team by identifying opportunities, generating interest, qualifying prospects, educating and promoting awareness and developing new sales leads;</li>
<li>Schedule and conduct a pre-determined number of face-to-face sales calls as directed by regional or local management team;</li>
<li>Input sales call information into designated sales call software and provide sales reports as directed by regional or local management team;</li>
<li>Maintain a pre-determined number of target accounts and demonstrate active attention to those accounts and progress toward closing;</li>
<li>Meet sales threshold/quota as defined;</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
