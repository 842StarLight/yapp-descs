<p>LumenData has an opportunity for an experienced Informatica MDM Senior Developer to become a part of our Enterprise Data Management practice. The MDM Senior Developer is responsible for leading the technical direction, configuration, project development, and ongoing operations of Informatica MDM solutions. The successful candidate will partner with technical and business team to deliver MDM solutions, strategies, support the Informatica MDM platform through engagement with external partners and internal experts, and drive technical data strategy initiatives. The candidate must be results driven, customer focused, a technical expert, and skilled at building internal and external relationships.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a key contributor to a team of IT professionals focused on Master Data Management technology, technical project delivery, day-to-day operational support, and communication of results to leadership</li>
<li>Provide expertise within the MDM environment using technologies such as Informatica Data Director, Informatica Data Quality, and Informatica Multi-Domain MDM</li>
<li>Strong understanding of MDM, BI, ETL, and data warehousing concepts</li>
<li>Partner with leadership to drive and apply continuous improvements to the client’s data strategy</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a strong relationship with business and IT teams</li>
<li>Ensure MDM deliverables meet or exceed service-level expectations for quality, timeliness, performance, and budget</li>
<li>Work collaboratively, effectively, and efficiently with stakeholders to ensure alignment and prioritization of goals and resources across multiple stakeholders, project priorities, and organizational initiatives</li>
<li>Creates and maintains system &amp; process documentation according to the IT methodology (design, test specifications, and other deliverables as necessary) for MDM development and administration</li>
