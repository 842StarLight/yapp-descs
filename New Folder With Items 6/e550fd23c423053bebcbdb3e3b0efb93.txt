ABOUT WMBF
<br>WMBF-TV is the NBC Network affiliate for the Grand Strand and Pee Dee regions of Northeastern South Carolina and parts of southeastern North Carolina. This Gray Television owned station was launched in 2008 in Myrtle Beach, SC and was built as a state-of-the art High Definition broadcast facility.
<br>We broadcast 36.5 hours of live local news and weather along with NBC programming to 8 counties along the Grand Strand beaches westward into Florence, SC. We also use digital media platforms to deliver your Live, Local, Late Breaking news coverage to an ever-growing audience in the 95th Nielsen market.
<br>Myrtle Beach is a family vacation destination for millions of visitors each year due to our white sandy beaches, more than a hundred golf courses, amusement parks and mild weather. It is a pleasure to work in a city where so many people want to visit.

JOB SUMMARY
<br>The Digital Sales Manager is responsible for achieving the station's digital advertising revenue goals and works closely with the entire WMBF sales team to prospect, develop, close and maintain digital advertising accounts. The DSM works in partnership with the sales team to identify clients’ needs and to recommend effective marketing solutions, integrating traditional television advertising with non-traditional digital tools and new media platforms. Growing existing clients and new business development are key areas of focus. The DSM reports to the General Sales Manager.
