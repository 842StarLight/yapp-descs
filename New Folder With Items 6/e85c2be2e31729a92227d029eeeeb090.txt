<p><b>
We are</b><b> currently seeking a </b><b>Regional Estimating Manager</b><b> as a member of our Project Controls Group, located in our Boston, MA</b> <b>office</b><b>.
</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This position will be in a leadership role for the geographical region and manage the local estimating team, coordinate estimating efforts with design and construction management teams, assure adherence to STV estimating processes and procedures and review estimates for accuracy of pricing and scope.</li>
<li>The Regional Estimating Manager will report directly to the STV Chief Estimator and the role will be a working role where he/she will also develop a full range of construction cost estimates from Conceptual Design through Final Design bid documents in support of our wide range of project types and divisions inclusive of Transportation &amp; Infrastructure, Buildings &amp; Facilities, Energy and Construction Management.</li>
<li>Reviews construction design drawings &amp; specifications, gathers all material and data necessary to organize a construction cost estimate in CSI, UNIFORMAT and other appropriate Work Breakdown Structures.</li>
<li>Prepares a Basis of Estimate that outlines the scope, exceptions, allowance, format, etc. in accordance with client requirements.</li>
<li>Prepares physical take-offs and documents unit line item quantities manually or using appropriate software.</li>
<li>Researches unit price requirements and prepares estimates using appropriate unit price, labor productivity and equipment rates.</li>
<li>Analyzes bids prepared by contractors and prepares post-award change order estimates as required for construction phase change order negotiations.</li>
<li>Prepares and delivers cost reporting to clients, performs value engineering and administers constructability as part of the estimating process.</li>
<li>The candidate should be knowledgeable of all scopes of work with the ability to develop estimates across all CSI divisions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 8 years of experience with 4 year Bachelor’s degree in Architecture, Engineering, Construction Management or other related field</li>
<li>Must be able to handle multiple projects/tasks at the same time</li>
<li>Ability to meet critical project deadlines and willingness to coordinate and work as a team member</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and local knowledge of the construction industry</li>
<li>Strong communication skills both written and spoken</li>
<li>Ability to manage people and assign tasks</li>
</ul>
