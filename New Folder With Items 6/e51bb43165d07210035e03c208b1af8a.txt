<br><br>
ECI Software Solutions is searching for a Director of Professional Services. The successful candidate will provide leadership for ECi’s consulting and project implementation work in our Building &amp; Construction LBMH group. The position requires a person who models the behaviors that result in quality outcomes for clients and works to develop the skills and confidence of the Professional Services team members.
<br><br>
The successful candidate will be responsible for defining the client needs, developing a proposal to meet those needs, as well as overseeing the implementation of the complete project solution. The Director will perform in both a people management capacity and a technical leadership capacity serving on the division senior leadership team.
<br><br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manages the implementation service delivery team providing management and oversight of platform implementation. Supervise and support Professional Services team members responsible for implementation delivery including Implementation Consultants, 3rd party Implementation Consultants, Project Managers, Post Go-Live Team and Learning and Development resources</li>
<li>Offer successful programs and processes to optimize customer satisfaction and retention to new and existing customers</li>
<li>Analyze projects and recommend opportunities to increase revenue, reduce costs, improve process and Net Promoter Scores (NPS)</li>
<li>Assign appropriate resources to projects to deliver implementations on time, within budget, and ensure customer satisfaction/success</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales and Sales Operations to complete project estimates, compose statements of work, and other documents or tasks as needed to shorten/close the sales cycle and grow non-recurring revenue</li>
<li>Responsible for team management including hiring, training, performance management and career engagement</li>
<li>Partner with the Sales team and assist in project pre-qualification and scoping to ensure that high quality deals are sold that will lead to customer success. Evaluate the roadmap for a customer’s long-term vision, to identify solutions for each client's unique needs.</li>
<li>Partner with Customer Experience and Product team to evaluate customer journey, customer satisfaction, and product road map to increase customer success and retention</li>
<li>Develop strong relationships with key customers and partners including planning input, support, and participation in annual user’s conference</li>
<li>Maintain reporting on utilization, revenue, team capacity, volume of project work, and outlook to advise strategic direction of department.</li>
<li>Develops a program delivery schedule that provides training as needed and in locations that work for the participants.</li>
<li>Helps with customers Account Receivables (outstanding collections)</li>
<li>Approves new training techniques and suggests enhancements to existing training programs.</li>
<li>Oversees and establishes relationships with vendors to provide additional outside training programs. Manages and develops training professionals</li>
<li>Ensures a culture of constant refinement and uniformity in the implementation syllabus to for all go-forward products that allows consistent quality outcomes</li>
<li>Supports the culture of ECi and overall vision and direction of the B&amp;C LBMH Group</li>
</ul>
Knowledge, Skills, Ability, Education &amp; Experience
<br><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a related field (e.g., Information Systems, Computer Science, Business Administration) or equivalent work experience with implementing software technology</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of demonstrated management and leadership experience, preferably in a professional services environment</li>
<li>Experience with all phases of a client software implementation lifecycle, including initial needs analysis, project administration, software installation, onsite training, systems troubleshooting, and ongoing account management</li>
<li>Strong operational management, project management and communication/presentation skills required</li>
<li>Ability to translate customer business requirements into business solutions</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to prioritize and manage workflow to meet deadlines. Ability to adapt to change and proactively take charge of tasks and priorities is required.</li>
<li>Willing, able and eligible to travel domestically and internationally 25% of the time with peak periods of travel at 50% or greater when necessary</li>
<li>Strong teamwork and leadership skills to lead collaboration across functions throughout the organization</li>
<li>Ability to create a strong personal brand of excellence, business acumen, big-picture vision and adaptability in an ever-changing environment of strong business growth</li>
</ul>
