<h2>Accounting Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Responsible for accurately maintaining Agent profiles in the sales database, monitoring of sales entered into the sales database for adherence to Company policies, and timely and accurate processing of Agent Receivables and monthly account reconciliation.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Billing and collection of expenses chargeable to sales agents.</li>
<li>Issue monthly invoices to agents; provide clarification or adjustment if necessary.</li>
<li>Process credit card payments from agents.</li>
<li>Record agent receivable payments in database.</li>
<li>Manage annual billing and collection of agent Errors &amp; Omissions Insurance.</li>
<li>Monitor and apply expenses to applicable marketing credits.</li>
<li>Create Miscellaneous Sales for draws based on existing schedule. Monitor balances and provide reconciliation to agents as needed.</li>
<li>Monitor sales data entry for adherence to policies.</li>
<li>Utilize automated emails to review selected sales for listing commission minimums.</li>
<li>Adjust sales commission payable to agent as appropriate and notify appropriate individuals detailing changes.</li>
<li>Adjust sales (pending and closed) as needed.</li>
<li>Correct payouts to agents and co-brokers as needed on closed sales.</li>
<li>Complete special projects and miscellaneous assignments as required.</li>
</ul>

<li>Ability to work in a cooperative, collegial manner with other department personnel to accomplish departmental goals and objectives.</li>
