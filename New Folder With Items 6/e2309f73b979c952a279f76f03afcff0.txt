<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description:</b><br>
<br>
The Account Executive position is responsible for the growth and retention of clients and revenue. You will work to build relationship and serve our customers by understanding their expectations and then communicating and coordinating our P3MC capabilities and resources to achieve the desired outcome. The Account Executive will primarily be responsible for achieving customer satisfaction by efficiently managing: requests for pricing/estimating, order entry, implementing change orders, project consultation, monitoring production and due dates (project management), reporting, and invoice review / approvals. The Account Executive<br>
will be the main point of contact for the account(s) they manage and responsible for reporting, problem solving, quoting, and paperwork required.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Additional responsibilities include sourcing, data entry, timely follow up to all quotes and client requests, and general administrative responsibilities. Candidate must have tremendous relationship building skills and be comfortable communicating directly with clients. Candidate must work well in a very fast paced, deadline oriented environment; have strong organizational and problem solving skills; must have very good attention to detail; and must be able to communicate effectively and work well with others in a team environment. Candidate will be required to provide client consultation, pricing, ongoing account maintenance, and have expert knowledge of all Phase 3 products and services. Candidate must have flexible working hours. Position reports to Sales Manager or VP, Sales</p>
