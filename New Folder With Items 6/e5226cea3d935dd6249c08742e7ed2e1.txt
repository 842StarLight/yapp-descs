<h2>Financial and Operations Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>SUMMARY
</b></p>
<p>Reporting under the VP Finance and directly to the Senior Financial Planning and Reporting Manager, the Financial and Operations Analyst provides support to the VP Finance and to the VP Operations by performing duties outlined in the essential functions below. Distribution of time for this position is expected to be 90% in the office.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist the VP of Finance and the VP of Operations to analyze operational and financial transactions and associated metrics to identify revenue and cost trends
</li>
<li>Analyze historical transactions by operating regions and compare revenue and cost trends to identify best practices, revenue optimization opportunities and margin improvement opportunities (cost-savings)
</li>
<li>Utilize financial and other analytical tools and other internal reports for analyses
</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to automate reporting that will provide relevant data to Operations and Financial management
</li>
<li>Work with IT, Finance and Operations to develop automated reports and analytical tools as required
</li>
<li>Prepare reports and presentations to communicate results of analyses, conclusions and recommendations
</li>
<li>Assist in the preparation of monthly KPI reporting packages for monthly business review for all regions
</li>
<li>Assist Regional Operations Managers in the analysis of their regional financials
</li>
<li>Work closely with Finance, Operations and FRC (Fleet Reliability Center) to identify key trends and associations between financial data and relevant operational metrics coming through remote equipment monitoring<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions
</p>
<ul><li>Must be reliable, dependable, and able to work overtime if required
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must work well in a team-oriented environment
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have a strong understanding of operations
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have strong analytical skills and be able to analyze data to identify operational trends
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must possess excellent telephone, written, verbal, and organizational skills
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Aptitude for communicating with operations and financial management and staff
</li>
<li>Must have a desire to continually grow, be willing to continually learn, and seek out training opportunities
</li>
<li>Must be dependable and highly motivated
</li>
<li>Must be process oriented, able to prioritize, organize, and work on multiple projects simultaneously
</li>
<li>Must be able to work independently to provide accurate analyses of operational activity and trends
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to meet deadlines
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Must have a thorough understanding of Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel, Microsoft PowerPoint, Microsoft Access, Microsoft Outlook, and proven ability to quickly learn, utilize, and comprehend other software
</li>
<li>Must be able to learn thoroughly and become proficient on the sales and service sessions of Oracle JDE
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Must have a general understanding of the gas compression business<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>

</b></p>

</p>

</p>

</b></p>

</p>
<p></p>

</b></p>
