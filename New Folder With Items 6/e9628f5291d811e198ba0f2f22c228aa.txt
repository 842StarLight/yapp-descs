<h2>Accounting Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Our positions are fast-paced and involve handling cash, account reconciliations, and guest inquiries. Our office provides an opportunity to learn several different computer systems used at the resort, and many of our previous seasonal employees have transitioned into full-time year around employment at Big Sky. Our positions offer a great opportunity to add business skills to a business oriented resume while providing a flexible working schedules for employees to hit the slopes on a daily basis.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Daily morning processing of cash sales from resort outlets</li>
<li>
Prepare bank deposits in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Answer telephone inquiries</li>
<li>
Assist with guest issue research</li>
<li>
Make ACCURATE cash change for outlets</li>
<li>
Daily reporting and reconciliation of accounts assigned</li>
<li>
Assist Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable teams as needed</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Detail oriented, with organizational skills</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new software systems</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Excel a MUST</li>
<li>
Cash handling experience preferred (but not required)</li>
<li>
Prior experience with hotel folios is definitely a plus</li>
<li>
Data entry experience preferred (but not required)</li>
<li>
Sustained focus in a busy, high volume office</li>
</ul>

Please submit a resume with your application (highly appreciated!)</i></div>
