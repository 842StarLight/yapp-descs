<h2>Advertising Strategy - Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">USA TODAY is in search of talented college students in the Chicago metropolitan area looking for an enriching paid internship. Join a global digital media company of more than 100 media properties across the country. As a Fall 2019 Intern Program participant, you will develop and apply your experience and skills in a fast-paced, real-time digital media environment. The purpose of the internship experience is to provide opportunities where undergraduate and graduate students can develop, grow and become the next generation of talent for the USA TODAY Network.
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
Increase Competition
<br><br>
Develop competitive reporting process and surface competitive advertising spend in two ways with weekly reports and presentation of these reports to management and team:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Monitor competitive web sites and reporting tools (e.g. Media Radar) to report on current advertiser activity on competitive sites</li>
<li>Monitor and report on non- USA TODAY advertisers on competitive sites; advertising, sponsorship, branded content</li>
</ul>
Sales Content Strategy
<br><br>
Research/discover targeted USA TODAY NETWORK editorial/video/social content for presentations to specific clients and specific business categories
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Participate in the creation of pitch decks by researching and identifying content in supports of strategic sales goals</li>
<li>Content surfaced will be used directly in presentation with clients</li>
<li>Report findings directly to sale sand work closely with client solutions to incorporate content into pitch decks</li>
</ul>
HOW TO APPLY
<br><br>
We are eager to learn more about you and how you fit this role. When you apply, don’t limit your upload to a resume; show us what you’ve done. To do so, put together a single document file that includes the following, in this order:
<br><br>
1. Your resume – one to two pages.
<br>2. If you add a cover letter, tell us what makes your work stand out.
<br><br>
It is important that these items be assembled in a single document and uploaded in PDF format. Completing these steps will ensure that your application receives the highest consideration.
