<h2>Accounting Office Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Accounting Office Clerk</b></p>
<p><b>
Murdock Hyundai Lindon, the highest volume Hyundai dealership in Utah, is seeking an Accounting Office Clerk for handling Sales Deals and paperwork.</b></p>
<p><b>
Duties will be focused on Deal Booking Paperwork to transfer to the Title Clerk and will be Full-Time hours with benefits.</b></p>
<p><b>
Flexible day time schedules can be structured around school hours or home responsibilities with End-of-Month work higher volume; Office experience is preferred!</b><br>
<br>
<b>Position summary:
</b></p>
<p>Represent the Murdock Auto Team brand in managing tasks supporting the Accounting Office processes to help the organization stand in a league of our own in the automotive industry by delivering on our No Regrets promise through enthusiasm in service opportunities, guest experience, and continual innovation and growth.</p>
<p><b>
Tasks:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Communicates with the team in the Accounting Office to enthusiastically engage the Sales Team and concerns in regards to the accurate Deal Booking process: managing exceptions, communicating problems, scanning documents for digital storage, and some accounting processes accurately, and other basic needs for accounting office.</li>
<li>
Organize daily operational tasks for managing paperwork to align with Murdock Auto Team's core values and enthusiastically engaging guests with questions.</li>
<li>
Enthusiastically work through the process for other tasks assigned such as Accounts Receivable needs per company policy and state law.</li>
<li>
Develop teamwork among the employees who work in the accounting area and partner with other Murdock employees outside of accounting to create the best guest experience in the automotive industry.</li>
<li>
Problem solve with little supervision to handle unique issues that may arise as title exceptions for correct processing and improve guest experience through unforeseen problems with their car title.</li>
<li>
Enhances Murdock Auto Team's reputation by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and different tasks as defined by Murdock Auto Team Management and working as a valued team member with other departments as directed.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Skills:</b></p>
<p>
Organization, problem solving, digital scanner knowledge, basic computer and spreadsheet management, customer service, teamwork, communication with managers, engaging others, soft people skills, ability to learn in a fast-paced, small office, leadership, and adaptability to manage through change.</p>
<p><b>
Experience And Education: </b>Must be able to work as a team member in a fast-paced environment with computers and basic communication software on computers. Must be a minimum of 18 years old and authorized to work in the United States with current driver's license and good driver record.</p>
<p>
Must be able to read and comprehend employee handbooks, warranty processes, and other automotive materials. Ability to learn quickly and take direction from dealership leaders.</p>
<p>
Experience in basic customer service skills preferred.</p>
<p>
Previous experience in managing people in an office setting preferred.</p>
<p><b>
Physical Requirements:
</b></p>
<p>Ability to walk; sit; use hands, handle tools, or feel; reach with hands and arms and talk or hear. Need ability to move around the dealership. Manipulate cameras and computer. The employee may be required to lift up to 20 pounds. Work in the Lot and Dealership with other employees in all weather conditions including excessive heat and inclement weather.</p>
