<p><b>Role and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p>Process orders of increasing range and complexity, ensuring customers receive exactly what they ordered in a timely manner.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Support the Sales Team with the following Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul><li> Coordinate service and/or sales orders by communicating with sales representative, customer, vendor(s) and third party installers to ensure that customers receive order according to their specific needs</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process order requests in order management system for organization and reporting purposes</li></ul>
<ul><li> Place and track orders with multiple vendors</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare completed order and communicate with accounting for customer billing purposes</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare completed order and communicate with accounting for payment processing to vendors</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with vendors and third party installers to obtain accurate freight quote for pending orders</li></ul>
<ul><li> Problem solve with customer, vendors, and third party installers when unexpected events arise during the order process</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with sales representative on special requests to meet customerâ€™s tailored needs</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process warranty requests from customers with vendors</li></ul>
<ul><li> Organize order files for reference during order process, accounting reference, and long term documentation</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain customer records with current information and provide customers/vendors with documentation they may request from Foundation Fitness</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain accurate electronic inventory</li></ul>
<ul><li> Communicate vendor news and lead times to sales team</li></ul>
<ul><li> Support sales representatives with bids and other long term projects</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain daily office tasks including answering phones, responding to voicemail, ordering supplies, distributing mail, and any other tasks that may arise</li></ul>
<ul><li> Other tasks and projects as requested</li></ul>

<ul><li> Ability to read and comprehend instructions, short correspondence, and memos. Ability to write email correspondence. Ability to effectively present information in one-on-one and small group situations, to customers, clients, and other employees of the organization.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists. Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.</li></ul>
