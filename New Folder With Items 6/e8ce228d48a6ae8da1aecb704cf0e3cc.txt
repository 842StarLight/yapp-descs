<h2>Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>Obtain materials and/or services from suppliers at the lowest cost consistent with considerations of quality, reliability of source, and urgency of need. Study market trends, research and interview vendors, and recommend sources of supply. Issue solicitations, analyze quotations received, select or recommend suppliers and deliveries. Prepare purchase documentation, including purchase orders and contract documents and expedite delivery. Develop and maintain necessary records and files in accordance with requirements.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Prepares and manages acquisition processes in a timely manner (generally under $100,000) in accordance with SORTA, State of Ohio and Federal Transit Administration (FTA) requirements, utilizing appropriate solicitation methods, including but not limited to Micro Purchases, Requests for Quotations, Invitations for Bids and Requests for Proposals.</li>
<li>Aquires the highest quality goods and services at fair and reasonable prices using competitive processes whenever possible.</li>
<li>Works with project managers to develop comprehensive work scopes, pricing schedules and evaluation criteria.</li>
<li>Reviews scopes, specifications and independent cost estimates for compliance with SORTA, State of Ohio and FTA requirements. Verifies justification for non-competitive procurements.</li>
<li>Researches potential vendor sources and ensures that the greatest feasible extent of competition is obtained.</li>
<li>Prepares solicitation packages, contracts, purchase orders and related documents, utilizing standard SORTA forms and procedures.</li>
<li>Conducts pre-bid and pre-proposal meetings as appropriate.</li>
<li>Responds to potential bidder/proposer questions in writing; amends solicitation documents as appropriate.</li>
<li>Receives quotes, bids and proposals and conducts evaluations of responsiveness and responsibility.</li>
<li>Chairs proposal evaluation teams and ensures that evaluations are conducted in accordance with established SORTA and FTA requirements.</li>
<li>Receives and evaluates protests and makes recommendations for their resolution.</li>
<li>Conducts interviews and leads negotiations with proposers.</li>
<li>Prepares award documents, obtains vendor and SORTA contract signatures.</li>
<li>Monitors contract performance; in conjunction with the project manager; prepares, negotiates and issues contract modifications; verifies actions required for contract close-out.</li>
<li>Participates in negotiating and resolving contractor claims and disputes.</li>
<li>Maintains familiarity with changes to applicable Federal, State, and local laws and regulations and industry conditions.</li>
<li>Maintains good communications with internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Ensures that contract files are complete and compliant with SORTA, State and FTA audit requirements.</li>
<li>Facilitates the development of strategies to meet or exceed organizational and department performance goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Provides a positive work environment that does not discriminate based on race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, marital status, amnesty or status as a covered veteran.</li>
<li>Complies with Metro's safety and security policies.</li>
<li>Participates in departmental and agency-wide task forces and working groups as directed.</li>
<li>Trains designated employees on Procurement policies and procedures and helps to develop online procurement training resources for the organization.</li>
<li>Assists with administrative tasks within the department.</li>
<li>Performs additional duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>

<b>POSITION QUALIFICATIONS</b>
</div>
<div><b>Competency Statements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Communications - Excellent verbal, writing and non-verbal skills.Persuasive, consensus builder.</li>
<li>Customer Focus - Excellent problem-solving skills and a desire to exceed customer expectations.</li>
<li>Employee Development - Accurately identifies and measures own skills and knows when to ask for help in completion of tasks and projects. Seeks out and participates in opportunities (internal and external) for professional training.</li>
<li>Leadership - Energetic, innovative self-starter, committed to continuous improvement and creative problem-solving.</li>
<li>Professional Integrity - Exhibits and values commitment, leadership, accountability, diversity, honesty, fiscal responsibility, and the ability to maximize resources.</li>
<li>Proficiency - Able to multi-task, plan and measure results, create and analyze data, excellent computer skills, accuracy and attention to detail.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Education</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma required; two or four-year degree in related field preferred</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Five (5) years of related experience preferred.A viable combination of education, training and experience may be considered.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Skills and Abilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office (or similar) suite, including but not limited to Excel and Word; GroupWise or similar email and calendaring software; and Maximo or similar inventory management and purchasing software; must have the ability to learn new computer programs (software and applications).</li>
<li>Familiarity with standard public-sector procurement procedures and accounting practices.</li>
<li>Excellent human relations and organizational skills and excellent written and verbal communication.</li>
</ul>
