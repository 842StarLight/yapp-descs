<h2>Customer Service Support Tech</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Under the direction of the Technical Service Manager, the Service Support Tech will be responsible for responding to all incoming phone, email, and website inquiries in order to effectively manage and service customer accounts by conducting the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Interact with suppliers on warranty issues, return goods and service issues</li>
<li>Process service invoices</li>
<li>Track field service requests and issues using spreadsheets and company call center software</li>
<li>Place follow up calls on service issues</li>
<li>Utilize company software systems to; input invoices for payment, process back charges, retrieve sales orders, check order/warranty status, and recommend revisions as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
This position requires a High School diploma or equivalent and three plus years of experience. Excellent proficiency in MS Office platform preferred as well as strong time management skills with the ability to prioritize work to meet daily deadlines. Previous experience in customer service and/or manufacturing environment/supermarket refrigeration knowledge is a plus. Candidate must be proficient using Windows Office, and ERP software.</p>
