<h2>Enterprise Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>MedBridge was awarded Seattle Magazine's 2018 Leaders in Health Care Awards and the 2017 Tech Impact Awards. We were also named one of Seattle's top companies by Inc. 5000 2018!</p>
<p>
Are you looking for a job that has a positive impact on the world - somewhere you can grow your skills while also making a real difference in people's lives? Have you been told that you should be in sales? We are looking for a passionate Enterprise Sales Development who wants to make a big impact while experiencing the excitement of a fast-growing company at the intersection point of healthcare and technology.</p>
<p>
Come join us for a fun, fast-paced environment with a great culture. We look for people that are curious, competitive, coachable, and tenacious who can work well in a collaborative team environment.</p>
<p><b>
This Position:</b></p>
<p>
This position is a combination of making outbound calls, qualifying leads over the phone, sending email campaigns, social selling, and creating valuable sales opportunities for the Account Executives. Every day will be different, and the person in this role needs to be somebody who is a hunter and 'makes things happen'.</p>
<p>
This position is located in our Seattle office and reports directly to our Sales Development Representative Manager. The Enterprise Sales Development Representative role is the first line for the MedBridge sales team and will work with Marketing, Product, Customer Success, partners and customers to sell the MedBridge solution and contribute to the growth of the business.</p>
<p><b>
The Task-at-Hand:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Making outbound calls</li>
<li>
Managing prospecting status, data integrity, and opportunity creation in Salesforce.com</li>
<li>
Schedule appointments and demos for new logo acquisitions and expansion into the existing customer base</li>
<li>
Hold intelligent and engaging conversations over the phone and email using the Consultative Telephone Selling methodology</li>
<li>
Act as the subject matter expert on the MedBridge Solution</li>
<li>
Achieve weekly outbound communications goals</li>
<li>
Creating and maintaining a list/database of prospect clients</li>
<li>
Tracking weekly, monthly, and quarterly performance and sales metrics</li>
<li>
Building pipelines with channel partners and team members to close sales</li>
<li>
Work cross-functionally with other teams/departments</li>
<li>
Achieve monthly and quarterly KPI's</li>
<li>
Tasked with creating and driving social awareness and selling activities</li>
</ul>
