<h2>IL licensed Facial Acupuncturist min 1 yr experience PT/Contract position</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our Chicago based skin studio is a holistic, high end space located in <b>Ravenswood/Uptown</b>. We are looking for a driven, organized, and diligent service professional to provide facial acupuncture for clients. We specialize in technology-based treatments and focus on the prevention of acne, hyperpigmentation, scarring, and anti-aging. We provide long-term strategies and solutions to changing the structure of our clients’ skins, so the professional must have exceptional relationship building skills. Other responsibilities include client education as well as working the point of sale software (checking in/taking payment, entering client intake data, etc.).</p>
<p>The <b>facial acupuncturist</b> position is contract based, part-time position to start, with the potential for future full time work. We have a very positive and friendly environment while providing excellent care to our clients. The prospective candidate must be licensed in the State of IL.</p>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES of this position include independent facial acupuncture, client management and also client education of the benefits of facial acupuncture. Some clients may also want to book full body acupuncture and knowledge of such treatments is a plus.</p>
<p>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:<br>1. Experience in facial acupuncture.<br>2. IL license in good standing.</p>
<p>ESSENTIAL SKILLS AND ABILITIES:<br>1. Excellent communication skills in person and over the phone.<br>2. Warm and engaging personality.<br>3. Understanding of skin anatomy, or willingness to learn.<br>4. Good problem-solving and analytical skills.<br>5. Well-organized with attention to detail.<br>6. Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.<br>7. Empathetic with client needs and concerns.<br>8. Ability to anticipate provider needs in regard to client treatment.</p>
