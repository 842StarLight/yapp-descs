<h2>Lead Generation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Atlassian is looking for a Lead Generation Specialist to help drive top of the funnel demand for our target customer accounts. Working on the lifecycle marketing team, this position will enable lead generation via advertising, content syndication and localization of marketing programs utilizing a portfolio of agencies &amp; vendors. Key responsibilities include the optimization and performance of channels that successfully navigate GDPR requirements by growing campaign engagement while building marketing consent. If you are passionate about creative solutions to complex problems, you'll love it here.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The ideal candidate is highly proficient in funnel analytics with expertise optimizing CPL, MQL and conversion rates. You love the science of marketing as much as the art and enjoy building, implementing and measuring new systems, processes and campaigns. This exciting opportunity will grant exposure to international markets and cross functional teams throughout the marketing and sales department. You will work with exceptional products including Jira Software, Confluence, Opsgenie and new emerging businesses within Atlassian.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Sound like you? Read on...</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>In this role, you'll get to</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Recommend and deploy marketing channels performant to regions with varied and complex regulatory environments.</li>
<li>
Report on results, build optimization roadmaps and determine the mix of channels, translation and localization necessary to get results.
</li>
<li>Partner closely with fellow demand generation and product marketers to ensure accurate messaging, timing and execution of one-off and always on marketing promotions across online advertising and events.</li>
<li>
Expand the footprint our marketable database, with a focus on EMEA &amp; Canada, experimenting and learning efficient lead gen channels and process for varied customer preferences using agencies, vendors and in-house solutions.</li>
<li>
Understand Atlassian's products and customer journeys</li>
<li>
Measure, analyze, and report campaign performance and deliver weekly campaign performance briefs to marketing and sales leadership.
</li>
<li>Use marketing automation tools and related ad tech to measure results.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>On the first day, we'll expect you to have
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>3-5 years of experience in B2B demand generation.</li>
<li>
Online advertising hands-on experience running &amp; optimizing campaigns</li>
<li>
Worked for or with lead generation agencies (not required but a big plus)</li>
<li>
Desire to learn how to navigate lead generation within GDPR or similar regulatory frameworks</li>
<li>
Comprehensive understanding of enterprise lead generation standard methodologies and industry trends.</li>
<li>
Proficient in methods for tracking &amp; measuring lead generation metrics (CPC, CPM, CPL ROAS etc).</li>
<li>
Outstanding program management and interpersonal skills along with experience working across sales, marketing and technology teams.</li>
<li>
A passion for metrics. You live for the data.</li>
<li>
The desire to roll up your sleeves and do the dirty work to get the job done.</li>
<li>
Some travel required</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>

We run our organization like an agile team. The top priority items are easily identifiable and everyone on the team rallies around those deliverables, and each other, on a regular basis.</div>
