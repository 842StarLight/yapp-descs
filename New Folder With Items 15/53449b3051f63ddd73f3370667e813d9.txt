The Opportunity
<br>
<br>
The Global Director of Product Marketing is a new and exciting role at Samsung Ads. He/she will grow a global division dedicated to identifying and defining scalable product solutions that drive revenue for Samsung Ads products across Data, Targeting, Inventory, TV Plus and Programmatic initiatives. He/she will lead a team that supports business across US, Canada, LATAM, EU and APAC. In alignment with Sales, the Global Director of Product Marketing will define sales solutions for brands and entertainment, looking across vertical messaging and product based offerings.
<br>
<br>
Candidate must have a strong knowledge base of AdTech and media, with programmatic and connected TV space as a plus. The Global Director of Product Marketing will report directly to the Global Head of Marketing &amp; Analytics and will be based in the NYC office with both domestic and international travel.
