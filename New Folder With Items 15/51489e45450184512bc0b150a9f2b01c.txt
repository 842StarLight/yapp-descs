<h2>Senior Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Sales engineers are client-facing and are a primary resource for the Cox Automotive Account Directors regarding sales support, sales solutions, product knowledge, cross-company integrations, project coordination, sales cycle management, and assessing/optimizing product performance for our clients. They are responsible for navigating contacts within Cox Automotive to manage daily internal/external client requests while also executing on new selling solutions and coordinating product implementations for our clients and various Cox Automotive business units. They work in conjunction with the Account Director as a key solutions advisor and product advocate for Cox Automotive.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Sales Engineer is responsible for managing and executing on opportunities for assigned accounts. They will need to articulate all product and technology requests to both business and technical users and clients. They must be able to identify all issues of assigned accounts to assure complete client satisfaction through all stages of the sales process. They can also be assigned special projects by NCSS Leadership and be responsible for completing those projects in a timely manner.<br>
</div>
<div></div>

<div>

</div>
<div>o Ensure client needs and expectations are met and/or exceeded</div>
