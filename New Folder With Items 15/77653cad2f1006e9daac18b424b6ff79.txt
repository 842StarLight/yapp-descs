<h2>Marketing Assistant</h2>

<p>Job Description</p>
<p><b>TITLE: </b> Marketing Assistant</p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION: </b></p>
<p>Our passion at Parallel 44 and Door 44 Wineries is producing award winning wines from cold-hardy grape varietals grown right here in our own vineyard in Northeast Wisconsin (just 15 minutes east of Green Bay) or grapes purchased from other Wisconsin vineyards. It is our mission to produce wines that are easy drinking, approachable and allow people to “Explore Their Taste” all while enjoying a life better lived.</p>
<p>As the Marketing Assistant, you will report directly to the Marketing Manager and will be responsible for taking on a variety of tasks that allow for the successful completion of the inbound and outbound marketing goals of Parallel 44 and Door 44 Wineries.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the implementation of Parallel 44 and Door 44’s marketing strategy.</li>
<li>Develop original and engaging content for social media platforms while staying true to brand</li>
<li>Works with the Marketing Manager to conceptualize, film, edit, publish, and promote weekly “Wine Talk” Videos</li>
<li>Assist with the promotion and marketing of the Wineries’ Wine Club</li>
<li>Create and send out weekly newsletter</li>
<li>Assist with the marketing, and organization of major public and private events held at the wineries</li>
<li>Analyze digital and social media performance and report results to Marketing Manager</li>
<li>Perform customer and market research and communicate findings with Marketing Manager</li>
<li>Update and make edits to website including updating weekly blog as directed by the Marketing Manager</li>
<li>Update Monthly Working Plan/Marketing Calendar</li>
<li>Attend all meetings and any other training that may be deemed necessary</li>
<li>Although the primary function of this position will be Marketing, it will also require occasionally working in the tasting room, which requires the following:</li>
<li>Educate customers on the wineries history, brand and operations to increase brand allegiance and consequently tasting room sales.</li>
<li>Educate customers on wine club membership benefits to promote customer’s joining club and staying in club and thereby achieving wine club sales goals.</li>
<li>Drive tasting room sales by promoting current sales initiatives</li>
<li>Provide exceptional customer service.</li>
<li>Maintain a clean and organized tasting room.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Minimum</b> <b>Qualifications: </b>
</li>
<li>Associates Degree in Marketing or related field</li>
<li>Minimum of one year related marketing experience.</li>
<li>Proven experience with Social Media platforms (Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, and Snapchat)</li>
<li>Detail oriented and organized, with the ability to multi-task and prioritize</li>
<li>Capability to work independently or as a part of a team</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Complete Responsible Beverage Servers training course as required by law.</li>
<li>Position requires some evening and weekend hours due to special events.</li>
<li><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of Adobe programs including Photoshop and Premier Pro</li>
<li>Familiarity with Google analytics, Website Content Management systems</li>
<li>Understanding of email campaign management software such as Constant Contact, and SMS text message marketing software</li>
<li>Experience with videography and digital film editing</li>
<li>Basic Knowledge of Photography</li>
</ul>
