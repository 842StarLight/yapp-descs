<h2>IT Business Analyst</h2>

<div>
<p><b>Major Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
SAP<ul>
<li>
Update and maintain system security to ensure there are no improper delegations of authority.</li>
<li>
Keeping track of all SAP program customizations and customized reports and developing a process to control and maintain them.</li>
<li>
Develop simple queries and direct and coordinate the development of more complex queries with our external applications development partner Soft Engine.<ul><li>
Examples of simple queries would be edit reports, and check balance reports</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Maintain training guides and procedures.</li>
<li>
Coordinate troubleshooting with our external applications development partner (Soft Engine).</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
IT Operations<ul>
<li>
Network<ul>
<li>
Ensure our external hardware support partner (TMS) is performing as expected. Develop and monitor performance against KPI’s.,<ul><li>
This will involve regular monitoring of their activities on a monthly basis.</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot hardware issues and coordinate responses with our external hardware support partner or address easily remedied problems directly.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Projects<ul>
<li>
Prepare documentation for projects (e.g. documentation for testing)</li>
<li>
Possibly provide set up for testing</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Website support for ecommerce<ul>
<li>
Secure Domain names</li>
<li>
Secure Mailboxes</li>
<li>
Coordinate with 3rd party solutions</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Training<ul><li>
Develop and conduct training for various applications with the related user base.</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Organizational improvement<ul><li>
Work with Director of IT to identify and evaluate researched and recommended IT solutions</li></ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

<br>
<p>
Specific Measures of Job Performance</p>
<ul>
<li>
Ensuring adequate segregation of duties is maintained in the SAP environment</li>
<li>
Develop accurate records of all customizations and ensuring the impact of any systems changes and or upgrades results in the timely release of these customizations in the new environment.</li>
<li>
Ensuring that all documentation developed is accurate and updated regularly.</li>
<li>
Ensuring the userbase is satisfied with their support from TMS</li>
<li>
Ensure all items requested to support websites and ecommerce are delivered timely and accurately.</li>
<li>
Ensure that users are properly and regularly trained in the applications they use.</li>
<li>
Bring solutions to the table which meaningfully improve quality and efficiency of the organization.</li>
</ul>

Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Consumer products industry experience across multiple companies</li>
<li>
Understanding of controls and the need for segregation of duties in an ERP system.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in multi-tasking on several major initiatives concurrently</li>
<li>
Strong written and documentation skills, including writing software user guidelines</li>
<li>
Willing to work on a variety of business platforms and solutions</li>
<li>
Experience working in $100M to $300M+ sized entrepreneurial company</li>
<li>
Exceptional strong work ethic with ability to do what it takes to get the job done</li>
<li>
Outstanding communication abilities</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Skill</b> <b>Sets</b></p>
<ul>

Prior experience working in a consumer goods distribution organization</li>
<li>
Broad knowledge of business processes including sales and distribution, supply chain management, procurement, general ledger, inventory management, and accounts payables/accounts receivables.</li>
<li>
Experience in implementing and supporting a supply chain management system</li>

Strong familiarity with B2B and B2C and e-commerce systems such as AWS, Shopify, Magento, Channel Advisor</li>

Five (5) years’ solid ERP experience, preferably with SAP Business One</li>

Three (3) years of Crystal Reports and/or Business Intelligence experience</li>
<li>
Experience with collaboration, productivity, and workflow-based solutions</li>
<li>
Exposure to X12 EDI and various service provider portals such as SPS Commerce, True Commerce</li>
