<h2>Senior Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Senior Product Manager</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>NantHealth is seeking a highly motivated Product Manager to join its Connected Care product management team.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Reporting to the Head of Product Management and Strategy, this individual will be responsible for working with marketing, product management, sales, and product development teams to define and drive a clear product strategy for NantHealth’s Connected Care Medical Device Integration (MDI) solutions. The Product Manager will leverage his or her skills and business acumen to manage and deliver the product roadmap through the development of a set of descriptive requirements captured as user stories and specified as detailed solution requirements. In this position, some travel will be required (less than 25%).</div>
<div></div>
<div>The ideal candidate has experience with MDI solutions and has good understanding of hospital data systems. He or she is an excellent communicator, possesses strong facilitation and execution skills, and is a capable and respected cross-functional team leader.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Position can be based in Panama City, FL | Philadelphia, PA | Raleigh, NC</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>The Basics</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Seamlessly integrate and align of perspectives from a broad set of stakeholders: Product Managers, Architects, Development/Engineers, Client Services, Sales, Business Development, etc.</li>
<li>Demonstrate business acumen, know/stay abreast of latest industry trends; act as internal expert on the product as well as the associated competitive landscape</li>
<li>Partner with cross-functional teams to deliver technical guidance towards the development and management of the product roadmap and strategy</li>
<li>Own and manage the backlog; continuously create, groom and prioritize to ensure that 1-2 sprints/iterations of backlog are always ready</li>
<li>Collaborate with UX and architects in design decisions, demonstrating deep understanding of technology stack and impact on final product</li>
<li>Perform workflow analysis studies and document processes and workflow</li>
<li>Develop requirement specifications and user interactions; write complex functional design documents including use cases, workflow and system requirements</li>
<li>Conduct customer and stakeholder interviews; analyze market requirements</li>
<li>Conduct functional testing and usability testing</li>
<li>Conduct and/or participate in design reviews and product demos as required</li>
<li>Ensure adherence to and compliance with established SOP’s and Departmental Work Instructions</li>
<li>Prioritize multiple tasks intelligently, speak up when you see issues and other interesting tasks as needed</li>
</ul>
<div><b>What You Bring</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Information Technology or related field; Master’s Degree preferred</li>
<li>10+ years’ experience working as a Product Manager on web-based, SaaS or cloud products</li>
<li>3+ years’ experience in designing software Medical Device applications and specifications</li>
<li>Experience working with biomedical, IT, and clinical engineering users in hospitals to identify, design, delivery and scale technology solutions successfully</li>
<li>Experience in developing business cases</li>
<li>Working knowledge of relevant Quality, Regulatory, Quality and Risk standards related to medical devices</li>
<li>Experience with established Healthcare data interoperability standards such as HL7, FHIR, CCDA, etc.; familiarity with medical device integration and EMRs</li>
<li>Knowledge of healthcare security solutions</li>
<li>Expertise in Agile Methodology; translate unstructured or ambiguous work requests into actionable user stories and work units through problem decomposition and planning</li>
<li>Technical capability to engage daily with engineering and technical teams</li>
<li>Demonstrates superior emotional intelligence and influencing skills that ensure alignment between customer, customer-facing internal teams, product manager, and engineering team</li>
<li>Success as a cross-team influencer, with a proven track record of establishing relationships and managing expectations both internally within an organization and externally with customers</li>
<li>Superior ability to anticipate technical problems and develop effective solutions</li>
<li>Exceptional capability to build positive relationships across departments, clients and vendors</li>
<li>Outstanding ability to prioritize and execute tasks to meet contract/client deadlines</li>
<li>Working knowledge of the design and development of software Medical Devices</li>
<li>Knowledge of the relevant quality and regulatory standards</li>
<li>Expert use of productivity and design tools</li>
<li>Must be willing and able to be hands-on to meet team/client goals</li>
<li>Established capacity to work well independently and as part of a team</li>
<li>Demonstrates the initiative to explore alternate technology and approaches to solving problems.</li>
<li>Skilled in breaking down problems, documenting problem statements and estimating efforts.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze impact of technology choices.</li>
<li>Demonstrates knowledge on technical topics, such as caching, APIs, data transfer, scalability, and security.</li>
<li>Skilled in negotiation to align stakeholders and communicate a single synthesized perspective to the scrum team. Balances value propositions for competing stakeholders.</li>
<li>Ability to collect and write requirements in an Agile software development environment</li>
<li>Influences through others; builds direct and "behind the scenes" support for ideas.</li>
<li>Proactively identifies and removes project obstacles or barriers on behalf of the team.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Competencies We Seek</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates clarity of thinking to work through limited information and vague problem definitions.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and the ability to interface with senior leadership with confidence and clarity.</li>
<li>Ability to work well with global teams, including time-zone flexibility.</li>
<li>Skilled in providing oversight and mentoring team members. Shows ability to effectively delegate work.</li>
<li>High degree of interest in collaborative work environment.</li>
<li>Self-motivated and quick-learner</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Able to work under minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Strategic, analytical, innovative and creative thinker.</li>
<li>Able to successfully seek out and properly manage crucial conversations.</li>
<li>Positive in nature, pleasure to work and collaborate with.</li>
<li>Able to build strong, trusting relationships with colleagues.</li>
</ul>
