<h2>Business Development Representative - IT Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We have career opportunities for a <b>Business Development Representative</b>. In this position you will proactively target organizations to find new sales opportunities for the Agile Data Sites managed and cloud information technology services. You will work as part of our sales team and be responsible for managing the sales process and acquiring new revenue for the company. Responsibilities include attending trade shows, webinars, seminars and other corporate marketing events.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides quality internal and external customer service surrounding the Company values.</li>
<li>Outbound phone calls and emails to marketing leads and target accounts.</li>
<li>Lead qualification to determine whether prospect is a good fit for our products and services.</li>
<li>Identification of buying process and business drivers.</li>
<li>Web research on target companies including business focus, financial performance and corporate goals.</li>
<li>Excellent communication, organization, and interpersonal skills; candidates need to be very comfortable on the telephone and be ready to talk to prospects and customers.</li>
<li>Ability to identify and qualify the key aspects of a potential lead, including need, budget, time frame, decision-maker and presenting the appropriate solution.</li>
<li>Having a general knowledge of the IT industry, with the capacity to learn about individual systems and products quickly, and accurately.</li>
<li>Be the engine that drives pipeline, new logos and growth for Agile.</li>
<li>Create and prioritize strategic account lists within a defined territory.</li>
<li>Support attendance to all field marketing events.</li>
<li>Work with partners for maximum effectiveness and territory expansion.</li>
<li>Build a robust prospect network in assigned markets via event attendance, building professional relationships and leveraging your existing contacts.</li>
<li>Be open to new prospecting strategies and sales philosophies in order to successfully find qualified opportunities.</li>
<li>Candidate must be comfortable going outside of their comfort zone in prospecting activities to find the proper organization contact at target accounts.</li>
<li>Ideal candidate must be adept at meeting weekly goals on a consistent basis including: Activity metrics, meeting set and qualified sales conversions.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications/Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Marketing, relevant field or commensurate work experience</li>
<li>2-4 years’ sales &amp; business development experience</li>
<li>Excellent Diagnostic and Troubleshooting Skills</li>
<li>A Driven, : Positive, Upbeat Attitude.</li>
<li>Ability to work collaboratively with multiple departments across the organization.</li>
<li>Familiarity and commitment to daily activity tracking in corporate CRM tools.</li>
<li>Background with advanced sales training systems is advantageous.</li>
<li>Self-motivated and have a strong work ethic are required.</li>
<li>We’re looking for a competitive contributor who envisions themselves elevating their career with Agile.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5 years’ business development experience within the Information Technology field.</li>
<li>Excellent lead qualification skill.</li>
<li>Advanced working knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>General knowledge of the IT industry and leading products strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Excel: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Microsoft Word: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Communication Skills: 1 year (Preferred)</li>

</ul>
