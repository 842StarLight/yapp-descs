<h2>Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The Sales Manager ensures the sales department meets sales quotas through expert management and leadership of the sales team. He or she must forecast sales, maintain proper inventory levels in terms of vehicle types and amounts, and motivate the sales team. The Sales Manager hires, trains, and monitors team member performance, prepares reports, and ensures legal selling and advertising techniques are being followed.
<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate has a high school diploma or GED and two or more years in a large volume dealership with supervisory or management experience. In addition, he or she has a positive attitude, a confident and outgoing personality, a professional appearance, strong business acumen and sales aptitude, great communication skills, and the ability to deliver world class customer service.<br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b><ul>
<li>Manage sales activities of establishment:
</li>
<li>Direct and manage a team of sales staff.
</li>
<li>Recruit, hire and retain sales team.
</li>
<li>Develop, train and motivate sales team each day.
</li>
<li>Ensure each sales person is meeting minimum sales quotes.
</li>
<li>Ensure each sales person is meeting minimum CSI standards.
</li>
<li>Analyze sales statistics to assist dealers in promoting sales.
</li>
<li>Conduct monthly and annual reviews for each salesperson on team.
</li>
<li>Prepare periodic sales reports showing sales volume and potential sales.
</li>
<li>Control inventory by product mix and equipment, if applicable.
</li>
<li>Establish, implement and monitor only legal selling techniques and advertising.
</li>
<li>Meet or exceed monthly minimum standards.
</li>
<li>Performs other tasks and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS</b><ul>
<li>Two years of relevant experience, automotive industry preferred
</li>
<li>Experience managing a sales team
</li>
<li>Knowledge of budgeting, forecasting, staffing, and scheduling
</li>
<li>Neat, clean, and professional appearance
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills to interact professionally with customers, vendors, and staff
</li>
<li>Excellent customer service focus
</li>
<li>Strong computer and internet skills
</li>
<li>Able to operate, move, and re-position vehicles
</li>
<li>Able to stand, walk, sit, stoop, kneel, crouch, lift and carry up to 20 pounds, climb, balance, crawl, twist/turn trunk, reach, and handle office tools and equipment</li>
</ul>
