<h2>Manager of Real Estate and Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Nouria is a multi-branded fuel wholesaler/retailer, convenience store and car wash operator in New England. Our sites are identified with the major fuel brands Shell, Irving, Sunoco and Gulf, along with Dunkin Donuts, McDonald's and Subway. We also take pride in our private brands such as Xpress Stop, Lil Mart, J&amp;S Oil, FL Roberts and Golden Nozzle. We operate over 200 retail locations and supply branded and unbranded fuels to 300+ dealers throughout New England. For over 30 years, Nouria Energy has been dedicated to providing our customers with superior-quality service.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>JOB SUMMARY</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The position will be primarily responsible for managing the organization's real estate portfolio, including its relationships with landlords and tenants, and for identifying, evaluating and recommending new convenience store, car wash and/or retail gas sites. The position manages and advises on all aspects of real estate, including related contracts, permitting and zoning, and single and multi-site acquisitions. This position will work closely with multiple departments, including Legal, Operations, Construction/Development, Finance/Accounting, Environmental and Risk Management. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record as a team player and real estate project leader.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and cultivate relationships with tenants, landlords and brokers and other related service providers.</li>
<li>
Negotiate, review and advise on commercial leases and related documents.
</li>
<li>Identify and evaluate potential sites in target markets. Due diligence and research, including market mapping, competition, population density and trends, demographics, activity generators and area development trends, site availability, development viability and economic development incentives, ownership and zoning.
</li>
<li>Negotiate and advise on contracts for purchase and sale of commercial real estate and related documents.
</li>
<li>Manage real estate contract database and lease abstracting. Track and negotiate lease renewals.
</li>
<li>Coordinate activities and communications between internal and external stakeholders to complete diligence, permitting and financing within appropriate timeframes.</li>
<li>
Manage site plan approval and permitting process for all new sites, including analyzing local ordinances and zoning codes and attending municipal hearings.</li>
<li>
Develop work scope, budget and schedule throughout duration of projects.
</li>
<li>Prepare site approval package for submission, review and approval.
</li>
<li>Manage divestment of vacant or non-producing properties.</li>
<li>
Manage additional projects and provide analysis as needed.<br>
</li>
</ul>
