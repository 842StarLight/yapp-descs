<div><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: (additional responsibilities may be assigned)</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develops, trains and directs sales reps to achieve sales goals, and placement objectives and to ensure company standards are met.</li>
<li>May conduct strategic sales negotiations with key accounts.</li>
<li>Sets up schedules and appointments to obtain new accounts and to service existing accounts.</li>
<li>Surveys market area to detect business trends and opportunities for new products or new applications for existing products.</li>
<li>Ensures execution of team quotas and orders.</li>
<li>Assists in the development of sales forecasts. Plans for sales activities by forecasting conditions, defining objectives and strategies and securing resources.</li>
<li>Controls and manages sales performance by measuring and reporting results, evaluating and correcting performance and administering policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Organizes team by structuring the territories, delegating work, and staffing positions.</li>
<li>Provides leadership in making decisions, developing personnel, communicating with and motivating staff.</li>
<li>Plans and executes sales and promotional activities that effectively counter competitive trends by establishing goals for individual territories, and distribution standards for brands.</li>
<li>Monitors and evaluates the activities and products of competition.</li>
<li>Conducts weekly sales meetings for the purpose of disseminating supplier information, product and sales training and to obtain feedback on the effectiveness, efficiency and relevance of the sales and promotional activities.</li>
<li>Prepares and maintains required paperwork, reports and records.</li>
<li>Manages selling expenses to authorized levels.</li>
<li>Manages territory when a sales rep is on vacation.</li>
<li>Prepares and executes appropriate promotions.</li>
<li>Job duties include working nights and weekends on promotional activities and other account activities.</li>
<li>Possession of and operating a motor vehicle to access customer accounts for delivery, promotion, and efficient and prompt sales and other key activities.</li>
</ul>
