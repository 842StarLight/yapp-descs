<p><b>Implementation Consultant (B2B)<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Reporting to the Manager of Professional Services, the Implementation Consultant will be responsible for delivering of a set of business solution design recommendations based on PROS customers’ implementation requirements. The Implementation Consultant is a trusted advisor to our customers and partners as an industry and PROS product expert. You will work across the PROS organizations as a subject matter expert on PROS software functionality and implementations.<br>
</p>
<p></p>

<p>PROS Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: PRO) is leading the shift to modern commerce, helping competitive enterprises create a personalized and frictionless experience for their customers. Powered with Dynamic Pricing Science, PROS solutions make it possible for companies to price, configure and sell their products and services with speed, precision and consistency across all sales channels. Our customers lead their markets across more than 10 sectors, and benefit from 30 years of accumulated knowledge and data science infused into our purpose-built solutions. </b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Deliver system configuration and documentation
</li>
<li>Act as application/training/support resource during implementation and post go-live
</li>
<li>Identify and deliver quantifiable business value that is consistent with the customer’s objectives
</li>
<li>Contribute to customer satisfaction by providing timely and functional solutions
</li>
<li>Work alongside functional, industry, and technical specialists to assist with the implementation and integration of innovative solutions including methods, techniques, tools, change management, and training

<li>Must be able to work alongside multiple disciplines within PROS, such as business development, product management, and development
</li>
<li>Accountable for making sure product configuration aligns with the solution design recommendations<br>
</li>
</ul>
