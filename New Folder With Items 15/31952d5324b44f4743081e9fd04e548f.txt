<p><b>Current Role &amp; Growth Opportunity:</b></p>
<p>
Act as the “CEO” of the MedeAnalytics UK product line by defining the overall product strategy and managing the product life-cycle in an agile environment. Collaborate with internal stakeholders and clients to create and evaluate product concepts, plan road maps, generate product requirements, and steward them through the commercialization process to successfully deliver them to market.</p>
<p>
This is a chance to be a part of a growing company and work on innovative, big-data products designed to lower the costs of healthcare in the UK. The ideal candidate has product management experience and is excited about making products that help clients across the healthcare industry put action plans in place by harnessing the power of their data within a performance management tool.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Reporting to the Senior Product Director (UK):</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the “MedeAnalytics UK” product line strategy, roadmap, and ongoing market research.</li>
<li>Managing the entire product or services lifecycle or from strategic planning to tactical activities.</li>
<li>Creating business cases including financial models to support the development of the product or services.</li>
<li>Establishing customer persona and market profiles.</li>
<li>Evaluating and making Buy/Build/Partner decisions on bringing solutions to market.</li>
<li>Specifying market problems and requirements for current and future products or services by conducting market analysis, supported by attending conferences, meetings with customers and prospects and key internal resources.</li>
<li>Defining and inputting features into agile project management software.</li>
<li>Work closely with Product Owners to ensure that market requriements for new initiatives are being properly developed.</li>
<li>Management of new products within assigned product family throughout the readiness process to ensure efficient market launch of new initiatives.</li>
<li>Ongoing assessment of entire product family and individual within product family.</li>
<li>Working to construct technology partnerships and contracts and maintainign existing partnerships that support our product offering.</li>
<li>Providing tactical support to other internal initiatives such as sales training, collateral development, and product performance.</li>
<li>Overseeing the commercialization process of initiatives to ensure that solutions are brought to market with appropriate readiness levels to sell, deliver and support effectively.</li>
</ul>
