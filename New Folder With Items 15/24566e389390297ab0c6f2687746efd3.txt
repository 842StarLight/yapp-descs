<h2>Sous Chef</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Austin Proper Hotel &amp; Residences is looking for an experienced Sous Chef for the Landmark Hotel. This 32-story modern high-rise will include 244 rooms and a limited collection of 99 branded luxury residences and penthouse for sale. Amenities include two rooftop pools, wellness center and spa and three food &amp; beverage destinations driven by McGuire Moorman Hospitality.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will have an excellent record of kitchen and staff management. Applicants must be able to accurately and efficiently work full service shifts as well as prepare and portion food products prior to cooking.
<br><br>
Job Overview
<br><ul>
<li>Run the line and ensuring quality service</li>
<li>Help in the preparation and design of all food and drinks menus</li>
<li>Manage and train kitchen staff, establish working schedule and assess staff’s performance</li>
<li>Order supplies to stock inventory appropriately</li>
<li>Prep work and maintenance of quality standards</li>
<li>Comply with and enforce sanitation regulations and safety standards</li>
<li>Communicate clearly with managers, kitchen and front of house staff</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><ul>
<li>1+ years of experience as a Sous Chef in a full-service restaurant</li>
<li>Understanding of various cooking methods, ingredients, equipment and procedures</li>
<li>Excellent record of kitchen and staff management</li>
<li>Accuracy and speed in handling emergency situations and providing solutions</li>
<li>Working knowledge of various computer software programs (MS Office, restaurant management software, POS)</li>
<li>Flexible schedule - Willing and able to work shift duties that may include evenings, weekends and holidays</li>
</ul>
Education
<br>Culinary degree or equivalent preferred.
