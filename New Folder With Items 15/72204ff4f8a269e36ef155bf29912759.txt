Job Description<br>
The Sr Manager, Engineering (Sales Go-To-Market), will be responsible for the resource and budge management for VMware’s Go-To-Market initiatives. S/he will be leading the design, development, and customization and be involved in the implementation of Salesforce.com and custom application development for VMware. The candidate should have exceptional technical skills in developing mission critical applications for Sales and have excellent communication and interpersonal skills. In this role you will embrace and adhere to IT standards, processes and methodologies.<br>
