<h2>Marketing Communications Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>As Marketing &amp; Communications Coordinator at Galls, you’ll be tasked with communication efforts via multiple channels to shape customer relationships and represent Galls internally and externally. Your role is pivotal organizationally help strategize, plan, and execute campaigns disseminating information to our end-customers.<br>
<br>
<b>WHAT YOU’LL DO</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work collaboratively with multi-departmental teams in the production of creative projects</li>
<li>Drive marketing projects forward with project management</li>
<li>Assist in monitoring and reporting on customer and employer review activity on search engines</li>
<li>Manage PR initiatives; including special projects and giveaways</li>
<li>Assist in engaging leadership to continuously drive customer-focused service</li>
<li>Aid in event planning and venue logistics</li>
<li>Own the sourcing and requesting of project quotes, and third-party vendor management</li>
<li>Assist in forecasting of workflows &amp; marketing assets</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
WHAT YOU BRING</b><br>
<ul>
<li>An undergraduate degree in Marketing, Communications, and/or related field</li>
<li>At least 1-3 years of related marketing experience</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office suite and Google Docs</li>
<li>Adobe Creative Suite strongly desired</li>
<li>Prior Project Management software a plus</li>
<li>Ownership (you can take a project and run with it)</li>
<li>Creativity and enthusiasm to produce unique and exciting content</li>
<li>Collaborative and innovative spirit</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills (you’re comfortable talking to creatives, as well as C-levels)</li>
<li>Resourcefulness (you may not always have the answers – but you know where to find them)</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
WHAT TO SEND OUR WAY</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Your resume, highlighting your education, experience, and skills</li>
<li>Why you want to be a part of the Galls family</li>
</ul>
