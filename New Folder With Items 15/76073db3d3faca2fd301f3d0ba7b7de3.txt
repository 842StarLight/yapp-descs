<h2>Inside Advisory Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Inside Services Sales Manager will work on a global team that fixates on strategic Infrastructure and cloud solution sales from a professional services vantage and provides valuable &amp; proactive input into potential repeatable offerings. The candidate will possess knowledge of the solution sales process and services outcome through the origination and closing of services sales opportunities for use cases including, but not limited to Enterprise Cloud, Enterprise applications, end-user computing, datacenter re-platforming, migrations, lifecycle services, devops, and operational readiness. The Inside Services Sales Manager is expected to bring a strong foundation in enterprise Infrastructure knowledge while working together with the field Advisory Services Sales Manager’s and customer sales teams.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Inside Services Sales Manager shall:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Work with the channel, inside sales, and field sales to position volume offerings or SKU to territories
</li>
<li>Provide support to field Advisory in presales activities and booking services business</li>
<li>
Mine Client data for sold pre-paid services (FlexCredits), and work with the Client, field sales or advisory team or Project Managers to consume FlexCredits in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Review sales forecasts and data and provide regular reports to field and Advisory management</li>
<li>
Follow up on short-duration services engagements with the Client for follow on services and solution offerings and assist in creating a feedback loop for up-sell opportunities</li>
<li>
Be responsible for processing SOW legal contracts, and PO monitoring</li>
<li>
Provide weekly sales forecasts, regular reporting, imagine creative ways to provide customer value, and stay abreast of status/details for each campaign and opportunity.</li>
<li>
Analyze delivery trend data to work to develop sales strategy campaigns playbooks to increase services attach-rates to support emerging application areas</li>
<li>
Assist in scoping services delivery Level of Effort, leveraging delivery toolkits and identifying gaps for repeatable practices</li>
<li>
Understand legal and finance workflow compliance to expedite sign-off of custom services engagements</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Education Requirements</b></p>
<p>
Basic Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 2 years of experience in an OEM or channel partner (VAR) or equivalent in managing these relationships</li>
<li>
Mandatory legal document (SOW or otherwise) process knowledge.</li>
<li>
Quickly respond to tactical requests for proposals balancing the needs of the organization, along with an ability to triage small to mid-sized projects with repeatable technical proposals</li>
<li>
Have worked in a presales services or software product sales environment with direct selling responsibility</li>
<li>
Have experience leveraging developed Level of Effort toolkits to assist in technical scoping</li>
<li>
Package unstructured work streams into an outlined solution</li>
<li>
Proficiency in spoken English and English contract written language.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Spanish, French, or Portuguese in addition to English is a plus.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Understanding of hyperconvergence products, and their impact on the customer’s IT journey</li>
<li>
Salesforce and PSA (Professional Services Automation) tools usage and forecasting</li>
<li>
PMP, ISO, or other process training and certifications.</li>
<li>
Hands-on experience in crafting responses to RFP/RFIs</li>
<li>
Natural leadership abilities, ability to thrive in an independent manner combining exceptional interpersonal communication, virtual team building, and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
