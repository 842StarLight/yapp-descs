<p></p>Hardware Product Manager

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary
</b></h2>
<p></p>We are looking for an experienced Product Manager with a passion for defining and collaborating with commercial and development teams on executing the product strategy. This role will be at the core of executing a broad-based product strategy encompassing a three to five-year detailed product roadmap and vision. The Product Manager will interact with senior management in all disciplines within the organization to lead and champion product planning and manage new and existing hardware (HW) product rollouts. The Product Manager will be responsible for the complete lifecycle of the assigned product line, ensuring product requirements and deadlines are consistently and reliably met.
<br>
<br>
This position also entails market research, launch planning, and achieving growth and revenue targets in conjunction with the commercial team. The individual will work closely with customers, regulatory agencies, sales and marketing organizations to ensure that market requirements and customer needs are built into the products. The Product Manager will be at the forefront of Aristocrat’s continuing mission to maintain a dominant position in the market through effective and creative product evolution and strategy.
<p></p>
