<b>Job Description</b><br>

<p>
At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.<br>
<br>
Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p>
The Enterprise Complaints Management Office is actively searching for qualified candidates to join an exciting new leadership team. This position, ECMO Delivery Management Leader will lead a team responsible for resourcing the software development lifecycle teams (Agile, Waterfall, Kanban] for all ECMO programs and will report to the Head of ECMO Change Delivery under the ECMO Shared Services.</p>
<p>
This new leader position will partner with the ECMO Portfolio &amp; Product Development Leader and be responsible for support services and resources for ECMO Release Trains. The leader will also be responsible ensuring compliance with EPMT/EPMO policy and PMQA as well as any actions that come forth from audit, controls or decision logs. The teams managed will be responsible for Delivery Coordination, Delivery Support Services, and Resourcing Scrum Masters, Project Managers, and Business Analysts.</p>
<p>
He/She will partner and collaborate with other leaders across the ECMO and lines of business to ensure the business best serves customers and our line of business partners. The leader will leverage voice of the customer and team member feedback to help drive results and priorities.</p>
<p>
 Responsibilities Include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Delivery Coordination</li>
 <li>Define, publish and maintain all ECMO status reporting required by policy and regulatory compliance</li>
<li>Provide full transparency and reporting in coordination with ECMO Portfolio &amp; Product Development leader to the ECMO leaders when appropriate</li>
<li>Accountable for all ECMO change delivery strategy and execution ceremonies/meetings and their maturity</li>
<li>Support ECMO Portfolio &amp; Product development leader on dashboards</li>
<li>Maintains all retrospectives</li>
<li>Manages portfolio ceremony tasks, minutes, and logistics</li>
<li>Manages sharepoint site strategy and compliance as well as any share drives or email boxes and distribution lists</li>
<li>Delivery Support Services</li>
<li>Primary accountable leader for collaborating with technology on DevOps and continuous delivery pipeline, and technical agility, including: exploration, integration, deployment, and release.</li>
<li>Continuous Exploration: Arriving at MVP for a release, architecture articulation, and features scored and prioritized by teams/release trains</li>
<li>Continuous Integration: works with Testing leader to build quality into sprints so that end to end testing is completed prior to staging</li>
<li>Continuous Deployment: take MVPs from staging to deployment so that they can ship when business is ready</li>
<li>Continuous Release on Demand: enable MVP in production when business is ready or roll-back when necessary, manage to KPIs if working as expected</li>
<li>Ensure all work is in compliance with SDLC, EPMT, EPMO, ISO policy as well as challenge and surface enterprise delivery opportunities to mature</li>
<li>Accountable for all Audit logs and routines, Decision logs, or any other managed task lists</li>
<li>Accountable and main partner with technology in making the release trains successful by clearing the runway (environments, security, RANBIs, supply chain, horizontal impact reviews or BAR toolchain)</li>
<li>Responsible for working with technology to secure appropriate testing environments when needed for Proof of Concepts, Proof of Technology or Innovation Pilots</li>
<li>Maintain ECMO data structure management</li>
<li>Maintain ECMO platform rules management</li>
<li>Ensure seamless connectivity and integration from Product Development through production support coordinating with each leader and domain</li>
<li>Support the Agile Portfolio and Release Train Engineers for each program on mitigating risks</li>
<li>Accountable for coordinating with the Testing &amp; Production Support Leader on all SHRPs, Corrective Actions, and risk management</li>
<li>Accountable for removing impediments or other milestone obstacles for Release Trains</li>
<li>Resource Management</li>
<li>Accountable for growing the discipline and talent development of Scrum Master roles, Project Manager and Business Analysis disciplines</li>
<li>Accountable for obtaining any non-dedicated shared services team members in DevOps</li>
<li>Hire and manage performance of Scrum Masters, Project Managers, and Business Analysts and supplying the right talent level to teams</li>
<li>Advance the Scrum Master role and maturity in coordination with the Agile Transformation office</li>
</ul>
<p>
The Candidate</p>
<p>
A mature leader with proven critical thinking skills and demonstrated operational credibility will be determining factors in selection. A successful candidates will be articulate and possess a demonstrated leadership capability; they will be impressive in their inter-personal effectiveness and with regard to their accomplishments. The candidate will have strong relationship management, influencing, vision, operational and execution skills. A key differentiator will be the ability to understand and operate successfully in a complex, heavily matrixed corporate environment. The candidate operates with a bias with a sense of urgency, passion for results, human-centered, and personal accountability for driving results.<br>
<br>
Specifically, we seek:</p>
<ul>
<li>Leadership: Proven ability to establish and gain buy-in for an idea or vision, define stretch goals that are attainable and directly support business goals, and garner enthusiastic support across the organization to accomplish the goals. Proven they demonstrate critical, board thinking skills.</li>
<li>Problem Solving: Ability to visualize, articulate, and solve problems. Accurately analyzes complex issues, ensuring identification of underlying root causes. Demonstrates the ability to solve problems under conditions of uncertainty and ambiguity. Generates alternative solutions and expertly handles obstacles.</li>
<li>Professional Skills: Capable prolific communicator that is comfortable working with a wide variety of corporate executives, sales, marketing, engineering, operations, customers and business partners. Proven teamwork skills; excellent interpersonal, written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>Creativity and Innovation: Able to think creatively and critically to identify opportunities to uniquely and effectively meet customer needs. Strong ability to assess market and competitive conditions and to develop creative, break-through approaches supporting improved customer experiences and operational efficiencies.</li>
<li>Analytical Thinking: Obtains and uses data necessary to identify opportunities or understand problems; uses quantitative techniques to make it actionable.</li>
</ul>
<p>
As a Team Member Manager, you are expected to achieve success by leading yourself, your team, and the business. Specifically you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead your team with integrity and create an environment where your team members feel included, valued, and supported to do work that energizes them.</li>
<li>Accomplish management responsibilities which include sourcing and hiring talented team members, providing ongoing coaching and feedback, recognizing and developing team members, identifying and managing risks, and completing daily management tasks.</li>
</ul>
