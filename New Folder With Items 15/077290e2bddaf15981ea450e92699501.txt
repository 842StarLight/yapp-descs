<h2>Luxury Assistant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Tempur-Pedic Team Lead’s primary responsibility is to provide excellence in customer service while selling and marketing Tempur-Pedic products. In addition, this individual will serve as a member of management when the Store Manager and Associate Store Manager are not available. This could include opening and/or closing the store as well as covering the management duties.</p>
<p><b>Essential Role Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>* Support management in overall store leadership and supervision</p>
<p>* Drive brand awareness and brand advocacy to exceed projected store goals to include teaming up with partners in local market to raise awareness across the region</p>
<p>* Aides recruiting, hiring, and monitors development of the store associates</p>
<p>* Serve as an example in providing premium customer experience and build relationships with our consumers.</p>
<p>* Support management in maintaining high standards of visual merchandising and brand presentation</p>
<p>* Ensure financial objectives and controls are consistently and correctly managed</p>
<p>* Foster and support a collaborative and open environment across all areas of the company</p>
<p><b>Role Requirements: </b></p>
<p>* Bachelor’s degree preferred</p>
<p>* 1-3 years retail management experience (or equivalent combination of education and experience) with high-end luxury and/or custom products</p>
<p>* Strong sales skills in a consultative environment</p>
<p>* Demonstrated ability to effectively lead, direct, and train others in a store setting</p>
<p>* Skilled at current best practice retail methods, procedures, and standards</p>
<p>* Flexibility in work schedule reflecting the needs and patterns of store hours</p>
<p>* Fluency with current retail software / computer systems</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
