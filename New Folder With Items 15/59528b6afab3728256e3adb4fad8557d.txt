<h2>Sales Development Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Fresh off our Series B round (https://venturebeat.com/2019/03/05/zinier-raises-22-million-from-accel-qualcomm-others-to-automate-field-service-management/) led by Accel, along with Founders Fund, Qualcomm Ventures, Nokia-backed NGP Capital, and Newfund, we're growing our team across all functions and regions!</p>
<p>Zinier's modern field service management platform helps companies with large and complex field service teams deliver an unparalleled experience to customers.</p>
<p>From matching the right work to the right people at the right time to creating step-by-step mobile workflows guiding technicians, or even automatically creating work orders to kick off the next series of steps in an installation process, Zinier is your complete, end-to-end solution for field service delivery.</p>
<p>Our customers include large telecom companies with hundreds, even thousands of technicians who install and repair equipment on a daily basis, to leading satellite companies that provide complex tracking of ship fleets. Any company that needs to install, maintain, and repair equipment out in the field can use Zinier to supercharge their teams.</p>
<p>We're a global company headquartered in San Mateo, California, with offices in Mexico City, Singapore, and Bengaluru.<br>
<br>
<b>What you'll be doing</b><br>
</p>
<p>As our first Global Sales Director, you will lead a fast-growing, high-energy SDR team of 7+ SDRs across the US and Singapore. This team is instrumental in supporting Zinier's mission of transforming field service through AI.</p>
<p>This is a great opportunity for an experienced and highly motivated SDR leader who is able to think strategically, build a team, coach &amp; inspire, design scalable processes, relentlessly measure, and effectively collaborate across our revenue functions.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead and scale a team of global SDRs across the US &amp; Canada, Asia Pacific, and Latin America</li>
<li>
Own Inbound and Outbound functions with SDRs primarily focused on outbounding</li>
<li>
Design career paths and develop SDRs for career growth within the Sales organization</li>
<li>
Define key success drivers and appropriate measurements for the team</li>
<li>
Report on sales metrics and forecast to senior sales management</li>
<li>
Motivate and inspire the team to exceed objectives through coaching and mentorship</li>
<li>
Actively use Salesforce and Salesloft to manage the sales process and set standards for performance metrics</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience managing a Sales Development team</li>
<li>
Proven record of sales success with B2B SaaS technologies</li>
<li>
Highly skilled in cold calling and outreach strategies</li>
<li>
Ability to hire and train new SDRs</li>
<li>
Experience successfully managing people to achieve performance goals</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>
Detailed knowledge of SaaS tech stack including sourcing, prospecting, and outreach technologies</li>
<li>
Bonus: Go-to-market experience (Sales, Marketing, Sales Ops, Sales Strategy)</li>
</ul>
