Description</b></div>
<div></div>
<p>Key contributor of the Channel Account team responsible for selling and growing the sales of the Vertiv IT Solutions portfolio.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<p>As part of the Vertiv Channel Account sales team, this position will be responsible for working with the Vertiv field sales organization, Vertiv regional channel management organization and others within Channel Sales and PPM (Product and Partner Marketing) to drive sales growth of the Vertiv product portfolio through an assigned Solution Reseller.</p>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul><li><div><ul>
<li>In conjunction with the Vertiv Channel Account team, execute on our strategic plan by successfully implementing and completing actions to drive sales results to achieve and exceed our goals</li>
<li>
Develop necessary relationships within the Solution Reseller and PPM Management to drive results to meet our growth targets</li>
<li>Conduct training, lunch and learns and other activities to develop both product knowledge and mindshare within the Solution Reseller sales force</li>
<li>Work in conjunction with Vertiv Partner Marketing team to develop and implement marketing programs to assist in sales growth</li>
<li>Develop and implement a quarterly sales plan that spells out key actions and associated dates. Review progress with manager monthly</li>
<li>Act as a Reseller advocate within Vertiv to ensure necessary buy in and support by our organization including but not limited to sales</li>
<li>In collaboration with the Vertiv Channel Account team, develop and implement sales incentives for the Solution Reseller sales teams to promote specific sales initiatives</li>
</ul></div></li></ul>
<div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>
Requirements</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of Solution Reseller processes, managers, sales and product marketing</li>
<li>Prefer past coverage or employment by Solution Reseller</li>
<li>Four year college degree (B.S.) degree or equivalent</li>
<li>5 years industry experience selling complex IT hardware/software and/or power solutions for the data center</li>
<li>Track record of sales success</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills</li>
<li>Strong references and industry contacts within the Territory</li>
<li>Self-motivated and self-sufficient</li>
<li>Product application and technical awareness in our product range</li>
<li>Experienced in sales forecasting, business case preparation, quota achievement, account, solution selling, and activity reporting via a CRM system</li>
</ul>
