<h2>Senior Product Manager, eSourcing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><p>The Senior Product Manager for e-sourcing platforms will drive the effort to build a best-in-class procurement electronic sourcing platform to the Corcentric Cloud Platform. Corcentric’s SaaS P2P platform provides a modular solution to businesses of all sizes. This role will focus on the Request for Information, Request for Proposal, Request for Quotation, and Reverse Auction modules; and will also include the integration levers between other modules and third-party systems. This person will work closely with other product managers, professional services teams, customers, developers, sales teams and cross functional members to provide direction, guidance and support to deliver a best-in-class platform. The role requires developing in-depth product knowledge, subject matter expertise in strategic sourcing, and an understanding of underlying functional and technical relationships inclusive of the end-user’s usage of the platform. This position shall be comfortable in both internal facing communications and customer-facing onsite meetings as necessary.
</p></div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Drive strategy and execution of the transition from independent products into a shared platform model</li>
<li>
Decompose functionality of existing procurement, sourcing, and reverse auction products and document specifications to create a common universe of capabilities
</li>
<li>Organize collateral, tools, solutions, and information in order to create a repeatable product management process and train new resources</li>
<li>
Inventory and survey end-users, delivery teams, customers, and sales channels to document and understand desired present and future-state functionality required in the platform</li>
<li>
Document, groom, prioritize and maintain Product Backlog for the Platform
</li>
<li>Create and update product roadmaps and release schedules</li>
<li>
Create and drive the evolution of the e-sourcing platform; including RFX, RFI, RFQ and Reverse Auction functionalities</li>
<li>
Develop and document specifications of new features and functionalities that can be interpreted by the software development teams</li>
<li>
Work with the development team on UI design/ validation, sprint reviews and UAT sign off
</li>
<li>Work with and interview the internal Corcentric strategic sourcing consulting team and document best practices in conducting strategic sourcing events utilizing a cloud-based electronic sourcing system</li>
<li>
Write release documentation and support the creation of product materials to train customers</li>
<li>
Drive cross-organizational readiness for new releases including supporting the development of a product marketing roadmap for communication of incremental and long-term product releases</li>
<li>
Support sales calls and assist the software product help desk as the subject matter expert</li>
<li>
Be the cross-functional leader &amp; motivator to remove organizational roadblocks</li>
<li>
Identify and manage vendors, partners, and integration channels to achieve our vision of the best SaaS e-sourcing solution</li>
<li>
Help others have fun at work</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
