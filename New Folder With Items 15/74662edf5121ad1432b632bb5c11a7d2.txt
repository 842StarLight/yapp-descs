<h2>Control Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>John Henry Foster Company is a Full-Line fluid power distributor and service provider and is the Midwest leader in fluid power sales. We have been serving our customers for over 75 years!</p>
<p>We are looking for a highly motivated, career-minded Controls Engineer to join our team!</p>
<p><b>Benefits: </b></p>
<p>We offer a competitive salary with excellent benefits which include: medical, dental, vision, life, 401(k) with company contribution, Employee Stock Ownership Plan, tuition reimbursement, paid time off, paid holidays, and much more!</p>
<p><b>As an Engineer at JHF you will: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meet with customers to analyze and understand requirements; recommend methods to achieve design requirements</li>
<li>Design control system functionality to address or improve the customer process</li>
<li>Specify hardware and software system components that will support the recommended system; Prepare wiring schematics and control panel layouts</li>
<li>Conceive programmable logic controller (PLC) ladder and other source codes such as Visual Basic or other language.</li>
<li>Write PLC/man-machine interface and other source codes; Simulate and debug written code; Start up and debug code and system functionality at customers’ facilities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Your skills include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Engineering</li>
<li>Excellent communication and customer service skills</li>
<li>5+ years related experience</li>
<li>Familiarity with Allen Bradley (Logix 500/5000), VB.net, HMI Programming, SQL, AutoCAD preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>As a fast growing engineering focused organization we are looking for a uniquely talented Controls Engineer to fill a critical role. Are you an independent technical problem solver? Can you plan and prioritize your day without being micromanaged? Are you confident your work will be done accurately and correctly each and every time?</p>
<p>Do you enjoy being challenged? Are you fast-paced? Do you thrive while executing on multiple initiatives in a variety of environments?</p>
<p>Delivering outstanding service and providing unique customer solutions is our most crucial differentiator. If you excel at designing and delivering technical solutions that provide high impact results this is a great career opportunity for you.</p>
