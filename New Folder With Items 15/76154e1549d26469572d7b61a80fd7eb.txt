<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Full Time</div>
<div>
<p>
Job description</p>
<p>The primary responsibility of the Account Executive is to drive and grow new business revenue from advertisers and their agencies by aligning their marketing objectives with a niche and highly engaged audience. This is a high-velocity, client-facing sales role requiring deep industry expertise, relationships and a proven ability for closing digital advertising deals.</p>
<p>The ideal candidates are the ones who have ethnic background, i.e. Hispanic Americans, African Americans or Asian Americans, or professionals who had the similar work experience in the past.</p>
<p>The right candidate is aggressive, creative, and resourceful and must enjoy working in a highly entrepreneurial environment. The position requires outstanding sales and communication skills to develop and maintain advertising relationships.</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the sale of marketing packages to ethnic groups.
</li>
<li>Aggressively pitch new business – Client direct and their digital agencies</li>
<li>Understand the challenges that clients face and demonstrate how the company’s marketing products can help to solve them.</li>
<li>Actively engage in business development activities, including attending trade shows, sales meetings and workshops.</li>
<li>Prepare proposals and respond to RFPs</li>
<li>Maintain pipeline of forecasted opportunities.</li>
<li>Meet or exceed defined sales quota.</li>
<li>Work with Advertising Operations to ensure the client's needs are met</li>
<li>Work with Finance team to ensure client payments are remitted</li>
<li>Stay current with online advertising trends and innovations</li>
Base salary with aggressive commission structure (5~10% commission of gross sales)
</ul>
<br>
<p>Desired Skills and Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>More than a year's of successful digital sales experience within diverse marketing field, selling to ethnic markets in the assigned industries.</li>
<li>Strong consultative sales skills, relationship building and customer services skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of banner advertising, mobile advertising, video advertising and dedicated email marketing (eDM), their creative units/executions and current competitive landscape</li>
</ul>
<p>Submit your resume</p>
<p>If you are interested in a position at Ad Connects, please email your resume to: hr@adconnects.com.</p>
