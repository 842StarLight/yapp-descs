<div>Do you want to be a key part of a team focused on increasing the reach of cloud computing across stakeholders in Academic Medicine? Do you have the industry background, technical depth, and sales skills needed to help further establish Amazon as the leader in the market segment? Academic Medical Centers are under pressure to reduce costs, innovate at scale and improve operational performance, and they are looking to the Cloud to modernize their operations. AWS is looking for a sales specialist who understands business trends impacting the industry and is passionate and capable of driving growth across R1 University Healthcare in the Western US for AWS.<br>
<br>
Your responsibilities will include engaging with large strategic School of Medicine, Research Community and health system customers and their existing AWS account teams to qualify and pursue sales opportunities. You will engage with our business development teams, solutions architects, partner and professional services organizations to drive large opportunities to closure.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate will have a business, pre-sales, or consulting background with experience working with customers in Academic Medicine, and demonstrated experience interacting at the CxO/VP level with a good understanding of healthcare services and Health IT systems. He/she should have a demonstrated ability to think strategically and analytically about business, product, and technical challenges, with the ability to build and convey compelling value propositions, and work cross-organizationally to build consensus. A keen sense of ownership, drive, and scrappiness is a must.<br>
