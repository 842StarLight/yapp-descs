<p><b>Ready to bring passion into your career?</b></p>
<p>
Clarins Group is not only a fast-growing, leading beauty company, we’re also a committed, family-owned enterprise with a fiercely independent and pioneering spirit. As one of the top skin care companies worldwide, with a strong position in Europe and the Americas and growth accelerating in Asia, we’re looking for exceptional talent in part time retail sales to represent our beautiful line in Ulta in Culver City, Ca.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The role:</b></p>
<p>
You will represent the Clarins Brand and drive the business through acquisition and retention of customers, while providing superior levels of customer service.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What you’ll do:</b></p>
<p>
By fulfilling the main mission of this role, you will contribute to all of the 5 pillars of the Group strategy: people-centric, brand-builder, consumer-obsessed, digitally minded, and commitment to responsible beauty.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Work to make each guest’s visit a delightful and memorable experience by providing friendly, caring, knowledgeable and personalized service.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Recruit new customers and create excitement at the counter.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Perform facial treatments and makeup applications and have the ability to close a sale.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Consistently meet and exceed sales goals and expectations</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are:</b></p>
<ul><li> Customer-focused, results-oriented and brand addicted</li></ul>
<ul><li> Comfortable demonstrating products on the customer</li></ul>
<ul><li> Customer service oriented</li></ul>
<ul><li> Confident, positive and self-motivated</li></ul>

<p></p>
<p><b>

<ul><li> Skincare experience needed/ Ulta experience preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong sales ability and interpersonal skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work well with others, as well as part of a team</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to create activity and animation</li></ul>
