<div>Lighthouse is the leading retention marketing and communication software service in the medical industry. We help practices succeed by effectively engaging patients with state of the art communication and personalization, using email communications, text messaging and online services. Lighthouse combines an award-winning communications platform and best-in-class reputation with an easy-to-use patient communication system that helps practices grow and succeed. We're dedicated to solving the unique challenges that practices face and work closely with clients and industry experts to create simple, innovative, and impactful solutions.<br>

We're looking for enthusiastic Software as a Service Sales Representatives ready to crush the next challenge. Top SaaS Sales Representatives should be a quick study and great on the phone. You are bright, energetic sales professional with outstanding communication and interpersonal skills who is ready to join a dynamic sales team. You're ready to compete and contribute to the team's success. You thrive in an environment that is constantly changing and updating to be on the forefront of technology. Most importantly, you are positive, professional, and you won't take NO for an answer, and are interested in the potential to make a lot of money and move up within our company. Ready to win?<br>
