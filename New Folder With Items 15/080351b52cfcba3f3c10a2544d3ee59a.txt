<h2>Shipping Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary </b><br>The primary role of the Shipping Specialist is to prepare merchandise for shipment, receive incoming merchandise shipments, monitor and maintain inventory, communicate and coordinate with outside service vendors, and keep records concerning these transactions.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities </b></p>
<ul><li>Adhere to the Quality Management System and documented processes</li></ul>
<p>Shipping</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain professional, clean, orderly, and safe shipping and inventory areas</li>
<li>Ensure Customer Requirements are met for all shipments</li>
<li>Ensure packages are prepared for pickup and/or delivery</li>
<li>Communicate with vendors as needed to confirm priorities and lead times</li>
<li>Compare the quantity and identification numbers of product against orders and/or traveler</li>
<li>Select and/or assemble appropriate packaging per customer specifications</li>
<li>Ensure products are packaged carefully and protectively within customer’s guidelines</li>
<li>Ensure that identifying information and shipping instructions are on containers</li>
<li>Prepare records of product shipped</li>
</ul>
<p>Receiving</p>
<ul>
<li>Trace missing or misplaced shipments</li>
<li>Verify receipt of shipments against packing lists and purchase orders in ERP system</li>
<li>Perform visual inspections of OSV jobs received</li>
<li>Reject damaged merchandise and report to the Buyer email group</li>
<li>Report count discrepancies</li>
<li>Receive goods in the ERP system (move physically, if required)</li>
<li>Notify appropriate department of goods received, including maintenance parts, media and other non-material shipments</li>
</ul>
<p>Quality and Safety</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure SDS/proper GHS labeling of all products purchased/received</li>
<li>Complete accurate, lot-traceable transactions in and out of inventory</li>
<li>Work independently and as part of a team on all shipping, receiving, and inventory activities</li>
<li>Ensure Quality standards/Company Metrics are communicated/met in all shipping tasks</li>
</ul>
<p>Communications</p>
<ul>
<li>Send/receive all requests or general communications via email groups</li>
<li>Ensure all requests (related or unrelated to shipping tasks) made to individual shipping staff or the shipping team are routed through the Shipping email group by Shipping staff or requester</li>
<li>Ensure daily entries in Shipping sign-in/out logs are accurate and complete</li>
<li>Assist with review and correction of paper and electronic shipping and sales order records to resolve data errors</li>
<li>Add expedite notes and shipping changes to jobs</li>
</ul>
<p>Other duties</p>
<ul>
<li>Backup for Materials Specialist and Materials Department</li>
<li>Review assignments to adjust work priorities as needed</li>
<li>Adhere to Company policies as defined in the Employee Handbook</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>Inventory (as requested)</p>
<p>Tool Crib (as requested)</p>
