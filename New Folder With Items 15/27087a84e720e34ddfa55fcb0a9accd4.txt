<h2>Senior Director, API Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are seeking a Senior Director, API Product Management where you will help lead a global API Product Management Team in the identification, definition and delivery a set of industry leading API products. You will be focused on enabling each of Equifax’s business units in an API First approach, bring consistency across the company, and manage the API product catalog and capabilities.</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who is Equifax?
</b></h2>
<p>Equifax is a global information solutions company that uses trusted unique data, creative analytics, technology and industry expertise to power organizations and individuals around the world by transforming knowledge into insights that help make more informed business and personal decisions.</p>
<p>
Regardless of location or role, the individual and collective work of our people makes a difference in our business.</p>
<p>
We are looking for individuals who can help us disrupt the marketplace. You will do this by delivering leading-edge technology to build and deliver unparalleled customized insights that enrich both the performance of businesses and the lives of consumers.</p>
<p>
We will give you the opportunity to drive innovation and automation across the enterprise. This will include tool and process integrations across all business units within Equifax globally.</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and execute the Product Strategy for APIs enabling access to valued EFX data and insights to our customers globally.</li>
<li>
Understand strategic and competitive position in the marketplace through customer interaction and market research.</li>
<li>
Lead prioritization efforts for API Products and Platform</li>
<li>
Publish API Product Standards and Frameworks</li>
<li>
Rollout API Product Training, Coaching, and facilitation</li>
<li>
Track global adoption, roadmap transformation activities and API product migrations</li>
<li>
Analyze landscape to identify business opportunities and make product/partner recommendations.</li>
<li>
Define product roadmap including authentication, API framework, governance and regulatory standards, monitoring, sandbox, and developer portal</li>
<li>
Champion APIs as a Product and partner with peers from across the business to align efforts towards an API First approach</li>
<li>
Develop pricing strategy and operational framework for internal and external integration</li>
<li>
Partner with Technology to drive both technical architecture and delivery of product roadmap</li>
<li>
Drive sales enablement in partnership with Marketing and Sales</li>
<li>
Drive the development and approval process of required business case(s) in support of the strategy.</li>
<li>
Leads efforts in developing product and product line strategies, directions and recommendations as input to overall company planning process.</li>
<li>
Provides business planning guidance and counsel to senior management to support overall company objectives and communicates with other internal groups.</li>
<li>
Define, monitor and analyze metrics that inform the success of APIs.</li>
</ul>
