<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Daikin Applied in Faribault, MN is now taking applications for full time Punch Shear Operators in our new facility in Faribault, MN.</p>
<p>
Seeking candidates with excellent work ethic and previous fabrication experience.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Follows all safety rules and instructions, maintain housekeeping standards of work area and performs other functions on and off production line as required.</li>
<li>Sets up and operates punch and shear machine in accordance with outlined methods and procedures to properly fabricate various parts.</li>
<li>Properly sorts and labels parts.</li>
<li>Adjusts and performs minor maintenance on machine as required.</li>
<li>Utilizes Night-Train storage and retrieval system.</li>
<li>Performs preventive maintenance on a daily, weekly and monthly basis.</li>
<li>Competently operates Siemens Control software/programs and can adjust tooling</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The ability to understand and follow standard operating procedures (SOPs), read blueprints and use various measuring instruments such as scale, gauges, etc.</li>
<li>Minimum of two (2) years' experience with right-angle shearing, turret press operation and demand flow technology (DFT) processes that are set in place.</li>
<li>Ability to troubleshoot and edit NC codes.</li>
<li>Siemens control turret experience, preferred.</li>
<br>
</ul>
