Oldcastle Coastal, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. <p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This role is responsible for ensuring proper electrical functioning of plant equipment, tools and machinery and to troubleshoot any electrical malfunctioning and complete appropriate repairs and maintenance.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Install, adjust, and repair production equipment and special purpose machines as assigned</li>
<li>
Operate a variety of hand and power tools to repair, grease, weld, cut, change parts and complete tasks as assigned</li>
<li>
Weld and use cutting torch as required</li>
<li>
Perform general repairs to plant structures</li>
<li>
Test and troubleshoot malfunctioning machinery, determine repairs and discuss with head mechanic for approval and repair as needed</li>
<li>
Replace faulty parts, electrical wires, motors, controls, and circuit boards</li>
<li>
Install equipment and electrical components and test to ensure proper functioning</li>
<li>
Maintain and repair all existing electrical equipment in the production areas</li>
<li>
Install power supply wiring and conduit for newly installed machines and equipment</li>
<li>
Read blueprints, wiring diagrams, process sheets, and assembly schematic drawings</li>
<li>
Calibrate and adjust all electrical/electronic equipment according to manufacturer’s specifications</li>
<li>
Do minor programming and maintenance for programmable logic controller based equipment</li>
<li>
Dismantle equipment to gain access to and remove defective parts using hoists, cranes, hand tools and power tools</li>
<li>
Lubricate and clean parts and equipment to ensure proper operation</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

Qualifications</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>

<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements / Education / Experience</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Associate’s degree or completion of Technical School and Apprenticeship program or equivalent in training, experience, and education</li>
<li>
At least 5 years of industrial experience in electrical support and maintenance functions</li>
<li>
Must have been employed as an electrician or electrician apprentice within the past 18 months</li>
<li>
Must be knowledgeable of OSHA safety requirements, especially those related to electrical operations and components</li>
<li>
Knowledge of hydraulics, mechanics, electricity, carpentry or related field preferred</li>
<li>
Able to read and understand electrical schematics and blueprints</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of three-phase motors, programmable controls, circuits, and National Electrical Code</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Physical Environment</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to lift, push or pull up to 50 lbs.</li>
<li>
Requires often climbing, lifting, bending, pushing, pulling, stooping, and twisting</li>
<li>
Able to use tools and equipment to repair and provide maintenance while standing, crouching, stretching, extending extremities and under extreme work environments</li>
</ul>
