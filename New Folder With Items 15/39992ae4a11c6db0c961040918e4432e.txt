<h2>Newspaper/Media Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for energetic, engaged individuals who are excellent communicators in person, over the phone and via email to join our award-winning sales team. Ideal candidates must have the keen ability to listen to our advertising clients' needs while also possessing the gift of gab and ability to successfully close sales.</p>
<p>Previous newspaper/media sales experience is a plus, but we always offer ongoing training and are willing to train the right candidate(s) to help equip them with the skills necessary for success.</p>
<p>This is an excellent opportunity for pending or recent graduates with a sales background who are wanting to jump-start a career. Those re-entering the workforce or looking for a change of pace, especially those who are fed up with the demands of a retail-based schedule, also are encouraged to apply. This position is 8-5 Monday through Friday working in a professional office environment in downtown Nashua, with a portion of the day spent out in local sales territories visiting business decision-makers in person.</p>
<p>Ideal candidates are self-motivators who are are able to multitask and possess a sharp attention to detail. Strong computer, communication and phone skills are a must, as is the ability to successfully present marketing plans in person before anywhere from one person to a group of individuals.</p>
<p>This position offers a strong base salary plus unlimited commission and bonus incentives for meeting sales goals. Mileage reimbursement is paid bi-weekly, and reliable transportation along with a valid driver's license are required.</p>
