<b>Job Description
</b>
</h2>
<ul>
<li>Post sale activities to include imaging of federal division purchase orders, modifications, service contracts, and any additional add-on activity that is received.
</li>
<li>This is a fundamental activity that ensures contractual compliance with federal government requirements and is a requirement for our ISO certification.
</li>
<li>Individual will be responsible for Item Unique Identification (UID) which facilitates item tracking in DOD business systems and provides reliable and accurate data for management, finance, accountability, and asset management.
</li>
<li>Position includes managing order exceptions with the assistance of sales and other available channels until a resolution is found that is satisfactory to the customer. Types of exceptions include pricing discrepancies, lack of supplemental data, and technical configuration errors. Position requires attention to detail and strong organizational skills.
</li>
</ul>
