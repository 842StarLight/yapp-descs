<h2>Provider Relationship Manager Specialist, Vendor Management Office (VMO)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">An integral part of the VMO, the Provider Relationship Manager Specialist is responsible to support their team to help ensure McDonald’s Global Technology’s strategic providers are meeting/exceeding the performance levels specified in their respective contracts. They also manage the overall relationship for a sub-set of our provider portfolio; those providing commodity products and services to McDonald’s. In doing so, they assure the delivery of intended value from the provider relationship(s) they manage.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Help establish new and optimize existing provider relationships to ensure the successful delivery of Global Technology’s initiatives to McDonald’s</li>
<li>Support our Provider Relationship Managers in working with McDonald’s business units, Global Sourcing, and Legal partner teams</li>
<li>Help facilitate the execution of Customer Satisfaction Surveys</li>
<li>Help implement VMO training initiatives for both our internal team and external business partners</li>
<li>Report VMO processes and performance reporting to the applicable governance bodies</li>
</ul>
<b>Other Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provider Data Analysis &amp; Reporting – Support our Provider Relationship Managers in analyzing provider performance and financial data</li>
<li>Process Mapping and Design – Help facilitate VMO process enhancements and standardization</li>
<li>Presentation Support – Assist team in creating VMO presentation documents (Business Reviews, Stakeholder Presentations, etc.)</li>
<li>Provider Contract Changes – Support team members in executing contractual changes with engagement from key stakeholders</li>
<li>Provider Performance Scorecard - Assist in the development, facilitation, and reporting of the provider performance</li>
<li>Provider Performance Issue Resolution/Mitigation – Help team in resolving chronic, systemic and/or critical contract or performance issues/disputes</li>
<li>Stakeholder Communications – Support VMO to ensure McDonald’s stakeholders understand our contractual obligations and monitoring our compliance to them</li>
</ul>
Minimum Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>1 – 3 years of relevant experience in IT/Technology related provider management</li>
<li>Project management experience on large-scale hardware / software rollouts</li>
<li>Good conceptual and creative thinking with ability to communicate complex issues and information to all levels of the organization</li>
<li>Self-driven, detail oriented individual who actively works to remove barriers to achieving results</li>
<li>Ability to work in a global fast-paced and agile environment</li>
<li>Adapts to changes in priorities with ease and comfortable working “in the gray”</li>
<li>Excellent presentation, facilitation, and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Team-oriented, collaborative leadership skills and ability to lead cross-functional colleagues and provider teams</li>
<li>Demonstrated and strong ability to remain focused under pressure</li>
<li>Adaptable and flexible while working in a busy global team</li>
<li>Willingness to travel as required – 10%</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree is required in IT or related discipline</li>
</ul>
