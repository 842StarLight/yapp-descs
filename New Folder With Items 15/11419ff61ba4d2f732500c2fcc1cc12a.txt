<h2>Community Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">:
<br><br>
<b>Introduction:</b><br>
HCF believes each employee makes a significant contribution to the quality of life of our residents and the success of the organization. Employee contributions are not limited by assigned job responsibilities. Therefore, this job description is designed to outline essential functions, qualifications and job scope, but not limit the employee to the responsibilities identified. It is HCF’s expectation that each individual will be flexible in offering their services to ensure the highest quality care to our residents.
<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
Performs a variety of hospitality, marketing, customer relations, and assist with admission paperwork.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Because HCF exists to care for our residents, regular attendance and punctuality are essential to provide quality care.</li>
<li>Leadership: Ability to take action and achieve results through others.</li>
<li>Commitment to HCF mission, values, philosophy and Service Excellence Initiative.</li>
<li>Develop care community census.</li>
<li>Must be able to complete performance requirements independently while using good judgement as well as have the ability to harmoniously work with others.</li>
<li>Must be able to read, write and understand the English language.</li>
<li>Must be able to travel to conduct outside marketing activities.</li>
</ul>
<b>Position Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must follow HCF Policy and Procedures.</li>
<li>Communicate and involve Care Community leadership in all marketing and sales activity related to the operation.</li>
<li>Builds and maintains positive internal and external customer relations.</li>
<li>Drive referral process and conversion.</li>
<li>Completes hospitality process by conducting resident discharge surveys and maintaining relationships with residents post-discharge.</li>
<li>Establishes strong daily communication with resident and families to ensure successful coordination of services are delivered to residents.</li>
<li>Prepare and maintain monthly marketing plan.</li>
<li>Manage marketing budget and Return of Investment on all marketing related expenses.</li>
<li>Coordinates and implements events, media, networking activities and promotional programs.</li>
<li>Understand market conditions and opportunities to grow census and revenue.</li>
<li>Communicates and works with Regional Director of Business Development on all marketing campaigns to maximize the overall Return of Investment.</li>
<li>Coordinate all marketing, advertising, and social media campaigns to align with overall marketing strategy.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to enhance customer service and patient /family satisfaction.</li>
<li>Communicate with admission team on leads and potential admissions.</li>
<li>Enhance care coordination and the care continuum through streamlining admission and discharge process.</li>
<li>Maintains required reports</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>Knowledge, Skills and abilities:</b><br>
An Associate’s Degree in Communications/Marketing and/or prior related job experience.</div>
