<h2>Client Service Representative I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>For more than 60 years, Western Growers Assurance Trust (WGAT) has been providing members of Western Growers, an association representing employers in the agriculture industry, with an affordable health benefit solution to keep their staff and businesses thriving. When you work for WGAT, you will join a team of caring professionals who are committed to serving our clients in a variety of capacities including claims management, customer service, account management, enrollment and eligibility, finance, reporting and analytics and more.</p>
<p><b>
But wait, there’s more!</b></p>
<p>
As a WGAT employee, your focus will always be to ultimately serve our valued clients. For your efforts, we offer career stability, a competitive salary, friendly staff and a pleasant work environment. You will be part of the dynamic health benefits industry and work alongside other compassionate hardworking people who are committed to applying their talents to serving our clients. As a team member of WGAT, you will also be part of an even larger organization – the Western Growers Family of Companies. This organization encompasses companies representing a number of industries such as finance, insurance, pharmacy benefit management and health management.<br>
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description Summary</b></h1>
<p>
The Client Service Representative I reports to the Supervisor of Client Services and is responsible for the enrollment, implementation and education of Western Growers Assurance Trust (WGAT) and Pinnacle Claims Management. Inc. (Pinnacle) health benefit plan participants, as well as interfacing with third party vendors as they relate to employee health benefits. This includes the investigation of problems/issues to determine source of the problem, evaluation of investigation results and determination of necessary corrective action for problem/issue resolution using established procedures and protocol.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>
High school education or equivalent and a minimum one year of customer service experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Spanish bilingual skills, oral and written preferred.</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge of computer software programs to access and/or update customer records.</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge in personal computer software skills in Word, Excel and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>
Strong verbal, written and presentation communication skills in both English and Spanish.</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize tasks and manage time effectively, with excellent follow-up skills.</li>
<li>
Verifiable, clean DMV record and the ability to travel to various locations throughout the U.S. up to 40% of time.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Duties And Responsibilities</b></h1>
<p><i>
Education Services</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Educate newly enrolled and existing plan participants on health plan and ancillary benefits at group’s work site locations, via phone or skype.</li>
<li>
Interface with third party vendors/providers to communicate and educate them on benefits and programs specific to WGAT plan participants.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
Member Services</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist with enrollment of Trust participants’, screen all enrollment paperwork for accuracy and completion.</li>
<li>
Work with Client Service Analyst to organize assigned group service meetings and open enrollment for the most economical and effective use of time.</li>
<li>
Research and respond to WGAT participant claim and administration questions utilizing the WGAT Health Care Processing system and the FSR web based tool(s).</li>
<li>
Determine necessary corrective action for problem/issue resolution using established documentation procedures and protocol.</li>
<li>
Request Benefits Information Pamphlet and Benefit Summaries/SBCs for health benefit information specific to each WGAT account.</li>
<li>
Support Regional Sales Manager/Account Manager/Broker with plan comparisons as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
Data Collection</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Compile and track data pertaining to problems/issues, action taken and resolutions using the FSR web based tool.</li>
<li>
Document all field services to include; enrollments, service meetings, wellness and courtesy calls and visits utilizing the FSR web based tool.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
Other
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize all capabilities to satisfy one mission — to enhance the competitiveness and profitability of our members. Do everything possible to help members succeed by being curious and striving to understand what others are trying to achieve, planning and executing work in a helpful and collaborative manner, being willing to adjust efforts to ensure that work and attitude are helpful to others, being self-accountable, creating positive impact, and being diligent in delivering results.</li>
<li>
Maintain a clean DMV record and the ability to travel to locations throughout the US (mainly California and Arizona) up to 40% of the time.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
