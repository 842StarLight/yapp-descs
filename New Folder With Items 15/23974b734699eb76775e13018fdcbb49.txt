<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SALES DIRECTOR</b></h1>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>THESE ARE THE TASKS WE TRUST IN YOU:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Establishes sales objectives by forecasting and developing annual sales budgets for markets and territories in cooperation with the sales team.</li>
<li>Develops and implements strategies to leverage national sales plans, territory plans and sales process to promote growth together with the sales team.</li>
<li>Maintains sales volume, product mix, and selling price by keeping current with changing trends, economic indicators and competitors.</li>
<li>Maintains sales staff by recruiting, selecting, orienting, supporting and training employees.Coordinates consistent training on products and sales tools in an effort to ensure current techniques</li>
<li>Ensure sales results by motivating, counseling and discipline (as needed). (Includes planning, monitoring and appraising job results).</li>
<li>Maintains professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops; seminars, trainings: reviewing professional publications; establishing personal networks; participating in professional societies.</li>
<li>Meet product margins by identifying market strategies, analyzing industry trends, understand customer needs and providing the customer solution.</li>
<li>Involved in GKA management, and participates in conferences, trainings, etc. to keep awareness of the current market</li>
<li>Researches pricing and strategy, including network cost/pricing and competitor analysis while evaluating the economic value of products.</li>
<li>Accesses customer needs and suggests appropriate products, services and/or solutions in order to meet sales targets/support the growth strategy</li>
<li>Develops medium to long-term sales plans and prepares strategies to protect, grow and diversify the relationship with targeted customers.</li>
<li>Resolves problems while ensuring customers receive high quality customer service.</li>
</ul>
