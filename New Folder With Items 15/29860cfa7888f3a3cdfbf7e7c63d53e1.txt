<br>Our Opening and Your Responsibilities
<br>We have one of the largest global service and sales organizations among precision instrumentation companies, and we need to add to this team immediately in Indianapolis, IN. We are currently hiring an on -site Laboratory Field Service Technician to service one of our larger customers in Indianapolis. In this role, you will perform installations, preventive maintenance, certifications, troubleshooting, diagnosing, and repairing Mettler Toledo laboratory equipment. Also within this role, you will: * Install new equipment in customer's facility. * Set up standard and special purpose measurement equipment in customers' facility. * Disassemble instruments and equipment and inspect components for defects. * Align, repair, replace and balance component parts and circuitry. * Travel overnight to attend occasional training (minimal) * Lift and carry 50+ pounds occasionally * Stand and squat for long periods of time * Pass a pre-employment physical, drug screen and criminal background check
<br>What You Need to Succeed
<br><ul>
<li>An Associate's Degree or equivalent from a two-year college or technical school with a focus in electronics or a combination of degree. * Experience working in field service or have related experience in an engineering or manufacturing environment * The ability to identify the root causes of service issues the first time using your keen knowledge of the products and your comfort with hand tools and calibration equipment * Excellent customer service skills, detail orientation, and great verbal and written communication skills * Familiarity with OSHA regulations and safe working practices. * The discipline to update service records and complete service reports on time and ensure data integrity * The desire and ability to promote and sell services and products, generating sales leads * Technical training beyond high school and basic knowledge of mechanical, electrical maintenance, and electronics * Basic comfort with laptop computers and Microsoft Office software; SAP knowledge/experience preferred * A valid driver's license and a safe driving history</li>
</ul>
