<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Customer Service Representative is responsible for assisting company sales efforts to meet and/or exceed company sales revenue and profit objectives.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES OF CUSTOMER SERVICE REPRESENTATIVE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate sales leads by contacting prospective customers by phone or in person</li>
<li>Provide quotations for products, including technical and engineered products</li>
<li>Analyze and assess customer needs</li>
<li>Work in conjunction with Account Manager and Customer in solutions selling and implementing cost savings opportunities</li>
<li>Monitor inventory levels to fulfill the needs of customers</li>
<li>Communicate with employees, customers, and suppliers to resolve issues</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
<li>Perform all work in accordance with ISO processes and procedures</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have excellent knowledge of industrial products and services</li>
<li>Must have the ability to work on multiple projects with a high degree of accuracy</li>
<li>Must be proficient at verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Must be ability to maintain positive relationships with employees, customers, and suppliers</li>
<li>Must be proficient with basic computer operation and knowledgeable about position- specific software programs</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of industrial products and services</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma required</li>
<li>2+ years previous customer service experience required</li>
<li>Industrial sales and application product knowledge required</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>BENEFITS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>401K Plan and Company Match</li>
<li>Medical, Dental and Vision Insurance</li>
<li>Company paid Long Term and Short Term Disability</li>
<li>Life Insurance</li>
<li>Paid Time Off</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>BlackHawk Industrial is an Equal Opportunity Employer</li></ul>
<br>
