<br><br>
We are currently recruiting for a Data Science Analyst located in our Support Center (Global Headquarters) located in Irving, TX.
<br><br>
What will you be doing?
<br><br>
The Data Science Analyst will manage, measure, and provide insights on corporate-level tests. This Team Member will proactively mine data and produce analytical output. This individual will work with large scale and complex traditional and non-traditional data sources to identify opportunities that enhance performance and efficiency of marketing initiatives.
<br><br>
How will you do it?
<br><br>
Work with large scale and complex traditional and non-traditional data sources to identify opportunities that enhance performance and efficiency of marketing initiatives
<br>Demonstrate strong expertise in leveraging quantitative tools and approaches to transforming raw data into insights, such as data mining, dimension reduction, segmentation, response modeling, and designing of experiments.
<br>Analyze marketing, customer, and sales data to measure and monitor performance by channel
<br>Create and automate reporting and the dashboards that support business partners across the company
<br>Manage, measure, and provide insights on corporate level tests
<br>Assist in the creation of compelling presentations that provide actionable insights and recommendations
<br>QA/QC data and report output to ensure accuracy
<br>Appropriately account for the timeliness and quality of all assignments
<br>Manage multiple projects concurrently
<br>Identify and generate ad hoc analysis to drive short term strategies
<br>Other duties as assigned
<br><br>
<b>Job Requirements:</b><br>
What Skills you will need to do your job?
<br><br>
Bachelor’s degree in relevant quantitative discipline (statistics, economics, mathematics, computer science, electrical engineering, or a related scientific discipline)
<br><b>Fluent in data fundamentals:</b> SQL, data manipulation using a procedural language (R, Python), statistics, experimentation, and modeling
<br>Advanced Excel with higher level math and logic skills
<br>Well versed in data mining (databases, querying languages, SQL)
<br>A minimum of 1-2 years of relevant work experience in decision sciences
<br>Demonstrated skill communicating complex analysis to non-experts
<br>Strong analytical thinker in an unstructured/ whitespace environment
<br>Ability to work in a fast-paced team environment, multi-task, and prioritize accordingly to deliver on multiple projects while maintaining a service-oriented, customer–centric focus
<br>Strong written and verbal communication skills, comfortable communicating with senior leadership
<br>High level of accuracy and attention to detail required
<br>Ability to interpret various sources of business data, synthesize and deliver actionable insights to leadership team</div>
