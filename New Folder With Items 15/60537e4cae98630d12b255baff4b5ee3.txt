<h2>Sales Support & Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Missouri Great Dane is a leading transportation solution company that specializes in offering quality-built trailers at a competitive price. We’re poised for exponential growth with an expansion project underway adding on over 4 acres of yard space, doubling our shop capacity, and increasing office space. That’s where you come in!</p>
<p>We’re actively seeking a new team member to join our sales staff as a Sales Support &amp; Marketing Specialist. Our industry is fast-paced, highly specialized and rewarding! We maintain a rental fleet of over 200+ semi trailers, design custom trailers, and sell traditional refrigerated cargo &amp; dry van trailers.</p>
<p>The Sales Support &amp; Marketing Specialist will spend time advertising by taking &amp; editing pictures, organizing pictures for ease of access, updating our website &amp; advertising venues along with some social media management. The majority of this role will be sales support assisting our current sales staff with walk-in traffic, handling paperwork &amp; deal transactions, coordinating with our Parts &amp; Service departments, Organizational communication, and staging trailers on our lot for presentation &amp; pick up.</p>
<p>Our ideal candidate is someone who desires to grow with us. Our intent for the Sales Support &amp; Marketing Specialist is to move into a full-time sales role in the future. The candidate spend time learning the industry in their hired role, building knowledge &amp; polishing sales skills by having the ability to sell our stock trailers – meaning opportunities for you to increase your take home salary pay with commission every month!</p>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<p>-4-year college/university experience: preferred majors in marketing, business, or communications.</p>
<p>-Proven Marketing Experience: bring &amp; show us some examples! Showcase your literacy in web design &amp; description of products if selected for the in-person interview process.</p>
<p>-Personable &amp; relationally driven: we’re a family run business and desire to create long-lasting relationships with each other &amp; clients. We work hard together and enjoy laughing – the “Work Hard &amp; Play Hard” mentality.</p>
<p>-Patience to listen and gather information with the ability to accurately repeat that information.</p>
<p>-Willingness to learn and be taught by industry veterans and molded into a future go-to salesman.</p>
<p>-Ability to operate a forklift and drive a pickup to maneuver trailers.</p>
<p>-Strong, professional communication skills: written (email), verbal (phone), and in person.</p>
<p>-Sales experience not required, but fast-paced, customer service-oriented industry experience highly valued</p>
<p>-Strong organizational and time-management skills</p>
<p>-Occasional ability to lift up to 80 lbs</p>
