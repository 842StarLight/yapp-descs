<h2>Accounting Manager/Controller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>PRIMARY OBJECTIVES: To oversee the accounting and administration functions of the company assuring that:</p>
<ul>
<li>Timely and accurate generation and distribution of financial statements, production data, labor efficiency data and project results.</li>
<li>Timely preparation of project invoices and collection follow-up to reduce Accounts Receivable Days Outstanding and improve Inventory Turns.</li>
</ul>
<p>MAJOR AREAS OF ACCOUNTABILITY<br>1. Full responsibility for monthly general ledger and financial statement package, including distribution of financial, production and labor results.<br>2. Participate in the analysis and preparation of individual job statements and accuracy of job costing.<br>3. Timely generation of monthly AIA billings using production information obtained from Project managers.<br>4. Collection of balances owed. Identify questionable accounts to allow mechanics liens to be filed as necessary.<br>5. Request project Insurance Certificates and Bonds as per job requirements.<br>6. Monitor subcontract progress and payments made on subcontracts to prevent overpayments.<br>7. Ensure timely payment of all vendors. Review all invoices and prepare checks.<br>8. Prepare and submit sales &amp; use tax reports and other fillings as necessary.<br>9. Oversee the maintenance and management of the office.</p>
<p>RESPONSIBILITY FOR WORK OF OTHERS: Accounting Staff (4), Office Administrator</p>
<p>FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY: Responsible for monitoring and collecting accounts receivable. Responsible for accuracy of general ledger.</p>
<p>REQUIREMENTS:<br>Education: 4-year accounting degree (C.P.A. certification is a plus)<br>Knowledge and Skills: Experience with computers and software, cost accounting, financial statement preparation and people management skills are required.<br>Previous Experience: 5 years of accounting experience (manufacturing experience or construction industry experience is a plus)</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
