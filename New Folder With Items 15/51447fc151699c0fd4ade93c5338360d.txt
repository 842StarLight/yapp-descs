<h2>Sales Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Sales Account Executive (SAE) is responsiblefor new business acquisition. This is a Hunter Role that includes cold calling,email solicitation, and networking. The SAE will be responsible for the fullsales cycle from lead development through closing each sales opportunity. The SAEwill also focus on securing new partnerships, account management, and deployingstrategic engagement activities. This role manages client objectives andprovides updates on key drivers regularly. This position is based at OES’headquarters in San Antonio, TX.</p>
<p>Optech Enterprise Solutions, LLC (OES), is an Aftermarket Solutions and SupplyChain Management provider. OES provides aftermarket cost-effective, streamlinedsolutions for the telecommunications industry with growth plans to launch intoother vertical markets. OES specializes in aftermarket services inclusive ofrefurbishment, resurfacing, and testing, parts harvesting, enterprise inventorymanagement, VAR solutions, and IT/IS services. OES provides expert, high-valueadded, cost effective solutions to their clients with integrity, transparency,and at the highest ethical and professional standards. OES’ number one priorityis to help their clients improve their business, with a focus on drivinggrowth, lowering costs and improving their clients’ profitability.</p>

<p>· Ownership of the end-to-end sales process</p>
<p>· Establish strong relationships with senior executives and decision-makers</p>
<p>· Foster new sales relationships through strategic engagement approaches</p>
<p>· Develop and implement effective sales strategies</p>
<p>· Research prospective clients and trade organizations</p>
<p>· Initiate, negotiate, and close new contracts with customers</p>
<p>· Analyze sales and operations data to monitor sales progress and develops corrective plans to improve sales force effectiveness/efficiency</p>

<p>· Prepare monthly, quarterly, and annual business updates</p>
<p>Demonstrates a firm grasp of industry trends, understand market conditions, and develop strategies to stay ahead of the competition</p>
