<p>Our direct client is looking for a Software Engineering Team Lead. They have a proven track record of attracting and retaining talented employees is pivotal to our ongoing success. The atmosphere is fast-paced, and we are looking for someone who likes challenges, is a self-starter, and would enjoy working in an entrepreneurial, multi-faceted environment. <p><b><i>Job Description</i></b></p>
<p>The Software Engineering Lead will work with the VP of Engineering and Product Managers to provide technology leadership and direction within SCRUM teams. In this role, the right candidate will design and write software to enable the business for growth based on internal and external business requirements. Working in an Agile environment, the candidate will deliver quality software through code reviews, participating and leading software design sessions, and working with business leaders to ensure software is built to product specifications.</p>
<p>The right candidate must display strong leadership skills, work well in a team environment, have experience leading Software Engineers in a fast-paced organization and enjoy building consensus. Candidate must be extremely detailed oriented, technically proficient, and enjoy handling a large workload while maintaining the highest quality standards for the organization.</p>
