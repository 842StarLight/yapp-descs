<h2>Manager, Sales and Marketing Automation - Business Applications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Glassdoor is looking for experienced and motivated technologists hands on Manager who possess a unique balance of technical depth, marketing and Salesforce background, strong multi-functional communication, and good interpersonal skills. You will partner with various business units and regional needs to help evolve the marketing technology stack so that it can be highly scalable, flexible and adapt to more resilient cloud architectures. Your role and work will help address customer business problems, grow highly visible and measurable engagement, and evolve marketing technology fabric so that it can help scale and accelerate growth both domestic and international.
<br><br>
You will collaborate with core IT, business analyst, regional sales managers, sales ops, marketing ops, and marketing to assist in driving growth across global customers as well as identified growth segments.
<br><br>
As a trusted customer advocate, we are looking for someone to help our organization and multi-functional teams understand standard methodologies while advocating lean and advanced cloud-based solutions. Align our businesses in supporting an expandable and well led funnel for strategic growth. Compose, model, integrate and drive a roadmap combined with data insights made available to evolve integration points, insight tools, upgrade infrastructure, engagement enhancements, and buyer journey optimizations.
<br><br>
The ability to connect technology with measurable performance indicators and business value is critical to a solutions architect. You should also have a demonstrated ability to think strategically about business, products, and technical challenges.
<br><br>
You will enjoy keeping your existing technical skills honed and developing new ones, so you can make strong contributions to deep, holistic, and broad architecture discussions. Take part in evangelizing and communicating and documenting deep-dive education and design exercises to create best-in-class solutions.
<br><br>
Roles &amp; Responsibilities
<br>----------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Marketing, Sales, Sales Operations, IT, and Engineering teams to formulate tactics and pivot to execute a strategy</li>
<li>Ensure success in building and migrating applications, software &amp; services for Marketo, marketing tools, integration connectors, IPaaS, and the Salesforce CRM platform and ecosystem.</li>
<li>Collaborate with B2B marketing team to maintain and expand MQL (Marketing Qualified Leads), SQL (Sales Qualified Leads), and Lead scoring processes and related lead attribution systems</li>
<li>Lead, own, and deliver marketing demand generation specific technology related projects with minimal guidance that cover Prospect/Lead to Opportunity</li>
<li>Educate internal customers/vendors/partners on the value proposition of Glassdoor's MarketingTech solutions, and participate in architectural discussions to ensure solutions are designed optimally</li>
<li>Conduct one-to-few and one-to-many communication/training sessions to transfer knowledge to internal customers to help shape marketing programs and marketing campaigns or IT initiatives</li>
<li>Heal current MarTech architecture discrepancies and advance Marketing and Salesforce architectural solutions so they align to scalability and growth needs for the business for Lead to Opportunity</li>
</ul>
