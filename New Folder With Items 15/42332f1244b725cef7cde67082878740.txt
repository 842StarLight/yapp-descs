<h2>Finance Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Finance Manager will be responsible for supporting initiatives across Finance and Accounting in the Tax &amp; Accounting Division. This position reports directly to the Director, FP&amp;A and works closely with executive leadership across cross-functional teams to support the strategic plan, financial goals, and operational objectives of the business. The Division is approximately $1.5B in revenue with an operating expense budget of approximately $1B. The Finance Manager is responsible for driving improvements in efficiency, accuracy and cost across finance and accounting processes and activities around the division. Direct responsibilities include contributing to the annual business planning calendar &amp; process for TAA Finance, monthly business reporting and forecasting, and reporting on and driving critical strategic, operational and efficiency initiatives. This role is directly accountable for improving and adding structure to financial operations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will have strong accounting and finance experience and a proven track record in delivering results in operational excellence, cost savings initiatives, process design &amp; re-engineering, back office improvement and transformation initiatives as well as best practices in measuring, monitoring and evaluating effective execution across the business. This individual must be able to work effectively with global finance and accounting leaders within TAA as well as senior business leaders and teams in the companies shared services organizations. This successful individual has demonstrated experience with business transformation, delivering on large and small operational efficiency projects, and successfully managing relationships and initiatives at both the local and enterprise level. This is a role for a highly articulate Operations leader who can work across an organization to drive operational efficiency and readiness.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Works with the Divisional finance leadership team to support and improve the accounting and financial reporting processes, structure, and programs
</li>
<li>Serves as a key liaison to critical shared services functions, including but not limited to, accounts receivable, billing &amp; collections (part of TAA/GBS), sales operations, Service Excellence (matrixed to TAA NA), GBS (Global Business Services), and key groups and functions within TAA as it relates to shared platforms, resources and products
</li>
<li>Serves as an operational leader with internal partners supporting cross functional initiatives (finance, accounting, the business GMs)
</li>
<li>Responsible for identifying and leading key initiatives that drive efficiencies, savings, and process reliability within the finance and accounting functions
</li>
<li>Responsible for developing and working with the business to ensure reporting of meaningful operating KPIs that can be analyzed in order to identify issues and lead to creation of actionable plans to improve the business
</li>
<li>Responsible for supporting and improving key finance system change initiatives, including SAP and CPM, bringing to bear past experience/learnings from previous initiatives similar in nature
</li>
<li>Responsible for recommending and monitoring governance processes, including annual business planning calendar &amp; process, balanced scorecard and monthly business reporting, etc.
</li>
<li>Works with business units and accounting organizations to support compliance with new accounting standards and regulations
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Works cross-segment and cross-business unit to develop inter-business governance, resource allocation, and execution processes to ensure alignment between Small Firm priorities/plans and those of mid-large, GBS, TAA SVE, and NA Engineering.
</li>
<li>Supports the finance integration programs for any acquisitions, working with the acquiring business unit
</li>
</ul>
