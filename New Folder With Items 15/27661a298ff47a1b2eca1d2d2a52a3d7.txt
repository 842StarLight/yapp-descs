<p>We are looking to recruit a talented<b> Photographer</b> to join our Sales function. As a member of our <b>Chicago </b>team, you will be responsible for all our apartment photo shoots happening in the city. This position is an exciting opportunity for someone who’s highly ambitious and passionate about interior photography while being on the forefront of tech and advertising.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Main responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Shoot still photography &amp; 3D tour on our apartments to meet the Blueground standards</li>
<li>Organise, plan and shoot the amenities and common areas of the buildings</li>
<li>Get involved in creative projects for social media</li>
<li>Assist in the setup of the decoratives before the photoshoot</li>
<li>Continuously find ways to improve photo-shooting methods &amp; team processes through workshops etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we are looking for:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent photography skills with at least 1-year photography experience</li>
<li>Real estate photography experience will be considered as a plus</li>
<li>Eager to learn new techs and provide feedback</li>
<li>Multi-task oriented and excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Self- motivated with attention to detail</li>
</ul>
