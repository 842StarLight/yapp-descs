<h2>Global Enterprise Segment Legal Counsel</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Do</b></h2>
<p>
This is primarily a sales support position. You will support of a fixed portfolio of named customer accounts within the Global Enterprise Segment and you will negotiate with our customers a variety of complex engagements: MSAs, Enterprise Agreement, cross-architectural agreements and SaaS engagements. <b>It is meaningful, fast-paced exhausting and incredibly rewarding but you have to have the right attitude, love your clients and be passionate about their deals.</b></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who You'll Work With</b></h2>
<p>
The Global Enterprise Segment (GES) Legal team provides legal support for approximately 500 U.S-based GES accounts. We are a small team <b>with a big attitude</b>, passionate about driving our clients’ business, solving our customers’ problems, and accelerating Cisco’s transformation. <b>We take incredible pride in our ability to do more for less and be agents of change for our clients</b>.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who You Are</b></h2>
<p>
You are articulate, remain calm under fire, and possess outstanding communication, negotiation, people and time management skills. You are comfortable and confident dealing directly with customers, in-house and outside counsel, and all levels of management. <b>You work well independently and have a sense of ownership of your work but are quick to jump in the trenches and help your colleagues.</b> You love to collaborate with a high energy, talented, and fun team of professionals. You are open-minded and eager to learn to do things differently. <b>You are fun</b>.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
You will structure, draft, and negotiate contracts of major impact for Cisco with GES
</li>
<li>You will work cross-functionally to support and lead all aspects of the legal sales relationship with your GES customers</li>
<li>
You will set or influence strategic policies and initiatives, to ensure consistency and alignment with business objectives.</li>
<li>
You will establish "trusted advisor" relationships with the sales leadership teams. You will participate as a member of the extended sales leadership team by attending staff calls, providing input on business and contracting strategies, being a first point of contact on new opportunities and all legal issues while providing ongoing business advice and counsel.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Minimum Requirements:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
A minimum of 6 years of experience practicing law or in contract management/negotiation role with strong sales contract, drafting and negotiation skills.</li>
<li>
Direct experience negotiating complex business and licensing transactions.</li>
<li>
Solid experience doing SaaS and/or XaaS transactions. Outsourcing experience is a major plus, but not required.</li>
<li>
Strong communications and "soft" skills, including the ability to present complex issues in a clear and concise manner.</li>
<li>
Understanding of, and relevant experience in, intellectual property law, commercial contracts and business law.</li>
<li>
Creativity and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Strong leadership skills and willingness to collaborate, share knowledge, respond quickly to urgent matters and execute proactively.</li>
</ul>
