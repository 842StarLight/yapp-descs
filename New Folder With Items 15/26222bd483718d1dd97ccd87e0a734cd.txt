<h2>Engagement Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<div>
<div>The Information Technology division is responsible for global technology strategy and applications, corporate systems, infrastructure and operations for the company’s worldwide media portfolio. The IT portfolio includes technology for Broadcast and Cable TV, Universal Pictures, News, Parks and Resorts, Sports and Olympics, Digital, Media Labs, and Sales, Research and Analytics, and NBCUniversal’s International group. The division is comprised of a team of technologists who are responsible for partnering with the lines of business to drive their individual technology strategies, as well as maintaining a solid core technical foundation, enabling the company to manage financial &amp; HR systems, email, network, data and technical service centers, etc.<br>
<br>
The <b>Enterprise Business Engagement Manager</b> will serve as the liaison and trusted adviser between NBCU’s Enterprise Applications Business Technology Groups both inside and outside of the IT organization and Enterprise Technology (ET) which provides IT Infrastructure (network, compute, storage, telecom, workplace, and operations) services. This role is the primary point of contact for Enterprise Applications leadership and will represent ET strategies, services, and capabilities to each BTG along with executing on key projects.<br>
<br>
This position requires strong communication, influencing, and project management skills. Also important is the ability to establish and maintain key relationships both within ET and Enterprise Applications as well as interact with diverse stakeholders and partners with a “business relationship management” mindset. While a strong technical foundation is essential for this role, the Enterprise Technology Business Engagement Manager will also be expected quickly to develop deep business acumen and understand the strategic objectives, major events, and pressures for Enterprise Applications and business unit.<br>
<br>
The Business Engagement Manager will partner with a dedicated engagement team comprised of other Business Engagement Leaders, Program/Project Managers, and/or Business/Systems Analysts, who will work closely with Enterprise Applications to provide consultation, expertise, and services from the broader ET organization.<br>
<br>
Job Duties:
<ul>
<li>Develop a working knowledge of Enterprise Applications business, strategy, and key priorities</li>
<li>
Partner closely with Enterprise Applications by actively participating in regular staff/team meetings
</li>
<li>Provide guidance and navigate the ET services catalog and processes on behalf of Enterprise Applications</li>
<li>
Manage the prioritization, resource allocations, and budgeting for projects approved by Enterprise Applications and ET Leadership</li>
<li>
Strictly adhere to the NBCU Project Management Methodology and reporting requirements</li>
<li>
Partner with engagement team to ensure delivery against business expectations</li>
<li>
Enable Enterprise Applications to better understand the scope of ET offerings and the related business value and efficiencies</li>
<li>
Communicate Enterprise Applications infrastructure needs and demand to each of the ET pillars: Network, Client Services, Operations, and Hosting Services</li>
<li>
Manage delivery of programs across all key stakeholders within Enterprise Applications, ET, and third-party vendors. Closely manage communications and expectations across all groups</li>
<li>
Act as an escalation point for all BE related matters, including non-business hours outage calls, and operational activities</li>
<li>
Maintain extensive collaboration and communication between ET and Enterprise Applications to ensure the on-going alignment of BTG priorities, expectations, resource availability and service delivery
</li>
<li>Enables Enterprise Applications to better understand the scope of ET offerings and the related business value and efficiencies they provide</li>
<li>
Partner with Enterprise Applications leaders to identify technology needs and develop technical strategies, internal IT assessments, competitor benchmarking, and industry technology offerings.</li>
<li>
Participate in regular service reviews, both internally and externally, and initiate improvements within an overall Service Improvement Plan</li>
<li>
Act as a change agent by anticipating technology opportunities, proposing viable solutions, and enabling process/technical innovation.</li>
<li>
Actively solicit feedback, reviews overall engagement performance and looks to drive continue improvement</li>
<li>
Participate in budgeting and proposal efforts by completing project scoping, cost-benefit analysis, and level of effort assessments</li>
<li>
Collaborate effectively across IT and Enterprise Applications to share/leverage learnings, minimize duplication of effort, and implement streamlined solutions</li>
<li>
Provide hands-on direction and leadership in operational activities related to Incident Management, Change Management, and Problem Resolution that impact Parks &amp; Resort’s application portfolio or workflows</li>
<li>
Facilitate change management process to ensure awareness of operational events and prevent disruption to Parks &amp; Resort’s activities</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance of all IT governance and SOX related documentation by required due dates</li>
</ul>
