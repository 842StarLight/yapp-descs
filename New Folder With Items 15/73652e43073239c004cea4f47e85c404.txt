<p><b>Culture Engagement Manager</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>REFRESH your career by joining our exceptional Public Affairs team! Coca-Cola Consolidated, the largest bottler in the U.S., is looking for a Culture Engagement Manager!<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Things you should know about working as a Culture Engagement Manager:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Culture Engagement Manager co-leads Consolidated's efforts to cultivate a culture of serving others in our Field locations to support The Company's Purpose. The regional position serves as a liaison between Market Unit leadership, Field Engagement Committees, the Corporate Engagement Review Committee, and Community Partners to serve our teammates, their families, our customers, consumers and suppliers. The position is responsible for event activation and execution, relationship building, program management and measurement.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>What would success look like?</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide guidance to local culture committees on the types of organizations, programs, and events to support locally.</li>
<li>Execute enterprise-wide teammate engagement events (Holidays, teammate appreciation, Veteran's Appreciation, etc.)</li>
<li>Recommend events and programs that are teammate specific for each facility with an emphasis on fulfilling local needs. Promote and attend as many events as possible.</li>
<li>Work in partnership with HR team as well as corporate chaplains to advocate chaplaincy to teammates.</li>
<li>Inspire, recruit and provide direction to Culture Committee volunteers at all locations to champion our Purpose by addressing the spiritual, emotional, and physical needs of our teammates.</li>
<li>Provide input to assist in managing an appropriate budget for teammates' programs within the specified location.</li>
<li>With strategic direction from the Corporate Engagement team, plan and execute local events that include teammate volunteer opportunities both at the facility and off-site.</li>
<li>Regularly communicate with PACC and Market Unit leadership on strategic initiatives such as Message in a Bottle, Big Hearts, and Sustainability to engage teammates, customers, community partners and other stakeholders.</li>
<li>Serve as liaison between the business (create new sales opportunities or support existing customers) and the Corporate Engagement team</li>
<li>Partner with local leadership to help deepen community and customer relationships through local programming.</li>
<li>Measure and evaluate the success of programs, incorporate feedback into future planning and report on results.</li>
<li>Extensive travel is required.</li>
<li>Will require working some non-traditional business hours and weekends.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Prior experience with event planning and community engagement</li>
<li>Prioritize and manage multiple workstreams that may require coordination/collaboration with several departments and external partners</li>
<li>Excellent critical reasoning skills</li>
<li>Understanding of local, state, and federal government structure is preferable</li>
<li>External relationships with community stakeholders such as local charitable organizations</li>
<li>Must have local relationships and knowledge of community as well as solid understanding of local government and political landscape</li>
<br>
</ul>
