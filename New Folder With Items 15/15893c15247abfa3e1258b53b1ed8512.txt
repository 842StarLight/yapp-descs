<h2>Customer Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Main Purpose: </b></p>
<p>Account executives serve as liaisons between RPS Solutions and itsclients, representing RPS to clients and advocating the needs of clients toRPS. The goal is to build strong relationships at every level of each client’s organization(buyers, HSE, operations, and corporate leadership), with the end results ofcustomer retention and information that will help RPS accurately forecast.</p>
<p><b>Major Areas of Responsibility: </b></p>
<p>· Proactively builds positive relationships withdistrict managers, HSE managers, buyers, and rig managers, primarily throughphone contact, secondarily through e-mail, and occasionally through face-to-facecontact and mail.</p>
<p>· Gathers information from clients about plans forfuture orders and communicates with other departments in order to facilitateaccurate forecasting and efficient production of products.</p>
<p>· Leads client projects, such as new-productdevelopment and web-ordering setup, using team meetings and whatever meansnecessary to initiate and complete projects</p>
<p>· Writes and distributes both pre and post reportsfor client meetings or internal operations meetings.</p>
<p>· Partner with AC on facilitating orders fromclients through email and websites, checking for discrepancies, incompleteinformation, and other potentially problematic aspects and clarifying withclients before forwarding to Data Entry department.</p>
<p>· Provides data, such as inventory stock status,sales history, and delivery performance, to clients and RPS departments as neededusing Micro Accounting Solutions (MAS) and Excel software.</p>
<p>· Attends and contributes to interdepartmental forecastingand production meetings.</p>
<p>· Contributes to the ongoing quality improvementof Customer Relations, assisting Senior AE and CCO in developing and improving systems,strategies, and techniques.</p>
<p>· Participates enthusiastically, as assigned, inteam activities and projects related to evaluating, developing, preparing, andmonitoring client activity, team success measurements, and other specialprojects.</p>
<p>· Assists owners and sales staff as requested witha variety of projects and tasks.</p>
<p><b>Occasional Duties: </b></p>
<p>· Travels by air and car to attend clientmeetings, trade shows, and factory orientations as requested.</p>
<p>· Assists end users of the products with orderingand inquiries as requested.</p>
<p>· With other team members, answers outside callsand transfers to appropriate parties.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree required; two years’ relatedexperience preferred.</p>
<p>· Demonstration of excellent oral and writtencommunication skills. Testing may be administered.</p>
<p>· Previous work in a diverse, multi-departmentaloffice environment.</p>
<p>· Outstanding organization and self-managementskills.</p>
<p>· Ability to work on several projectssimultaneously with thorough follow-through on all projects.</p>
<p>· Professional and friendly demeanor in dealingwith clients, co-workers, and vendors.</p>
<p>· Good writing skills, especially for e-mail,including grammar, spelling, punctuation, clarity, and appropriate tone for avariety of situations.</p>
<p>· Excellent speaking and phone skills, includinggrammar, clarity, and appropriate tone for a variety of situations.</p>
<p>· Superb initiative and drive.</p>
<p>· Innovative problem-solving skills.</p>
<p>· GRIT</p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>· Two years’ experience in client relations or accountservice, preferably handling multiple accounts simultaneously.</p>
<p>· Knowledge of Excel software, data management,and project management</p>
<p><b>Physical Demands: </b></p>
<p>· Extended computer use.</p>
<p>· Hearing, speaking, writing, and data entry.</p>
<p>· Moving, lifting, and setting up display/field trialmaterials.</p>
