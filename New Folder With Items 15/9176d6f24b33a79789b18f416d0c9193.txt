<h2>Seasonal Sales Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SEASONAL SALES ASSOCIATE</p>
<p>You’ve got to…</p>
<p>o Make the best first impression – for us, that’s: smile, welcome and speak to each customer about a promo message or score item.</p>
<p>o Love us – everything about us: the clothes, accessories, shoes, styling, stores and above all, our customers. That passion must be evident in your actions, every hour, every day, every week.</p>
<p>o Bring your best to everything you do and achieve your goals, particularly daily sales and assigned KPIs.</p>
<p>o Always be on, up for anything and ready to have fun along the way.</p>
<p>o Look under rocks, be curious, ask questions and use your smarts to think boldly and do the right thing.</p>
<p>o Juggle – think many balls in the air: you’ll work with one customer, then 4 at a time, then shift to tasks (when customers are not around). It’s all about making the most of every moment and multi-tasking.</p>
<p>o Be technologically savvy, while also knowing that devices don’t dominate the dialogue.</p>
<p>o Build productive relationships with everyone on the team and always respect each other.We’ll want you to…</p>
<p>o Ace training, use product knowledge tools, participate in fit sessions and put those experiences to use.</p>
<p>o Drive sales by meeting or exceeding J.Crew | Make It Count expectations.</p>
<p>o Provide an engaging and efficient checkout experience.</p>
<p>o Meet or exceed J.Crew Card goals.</p>
<p>o Achieve Enterprise Selling targets by adhering to Standard Operating Procedures.</p>
<p>o Achieve or exceed task-specific time standards.</p>
<p>o Learn our systems and gadgets and use them effectively.</p>
<p>o Assist in processing and replenishing – our customers should always see us at our best.</p>
<p>Oh, and by the way, you…</p>
<p>o Are at least 18 years old.</p>
<p>o Communicate clearly and succinctly.</p>
<p>o Are available when we ‘re busy, including: nights, weekends and holidays.</p>
<p>o Can bend, reach, stretch as well as lift, carry and move at least 40 pounds |18 kilos.</p>
<p>o Can regularly move around all store areas and be accessible to customers.</p>
