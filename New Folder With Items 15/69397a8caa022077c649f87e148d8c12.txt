<h2>Warehouse Receiver</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>General Summary of Job Duties</b></p>
<p>Receives incoming shipments and distributes to the correct location. Orders Food and Bar cost of sales products and supplies. Maintains stock levels of all standard items and reorders as necessary. Compiles monthly food and beverage inventories and updates inventory control on a timely basis.</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain a high quality of guest service according to Jacobs Entertainment, Inc. Mission Statement and F.A.S.T. Track Values Statement.</li>
<li>Receives inbound shipments delivered to Receiving Dock.
</li>
<li>Inspects and insures accuracy and quality of all goods received and notes exceptions on invoice and/or packing slip.
</li>
<li>Signature on receiving document certifies all delivered goods were received as indicated.</li>
<li>Inventories food stores and creates requests for food and supply replenishment on assigned days and transmit orders to authorized vendors utilizing electronic means or email.</li>
<li>Places orders for other departments as required.</li>
<li>Keeps receiving and dock areas clean and free of debris.</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of applicable Company and departmental policies and procedures as well as the willingness to learn and follow any policy or procedure that may be introduced in the future.</li>
<li>Adhere to all appearance and uniform standards.
</li>
<li>Maintain an open line of communication with Management.</li>
<li>Maintain a positive and professional demeanor during all interactions with guest, fellow Employees, and Vendors.</li>
<li>Ability to accept performance feedback in a professional manner.</li>
<li>Regular attendance and arriving on time to all scheduled shifts and mandatory meetings.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>General Job Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<p>To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following F.A.S.T. Track Principles:
</p>
<p><b>Friendliness</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Offer a warm greeting to everyone you encounter with a smile and eye contact.</li>
<li>Practice mutual respect by dealing honestly in all interactions.</li>
<li>Use genuine listening skills and offer sincere responses.</li>
<li>Promote positive interactions with both external and internal guests through sincere greeting and communications.</li>
<li>Be approachable, listen and lead by example.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Accountability</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be proactive and positive.</li>
<li>If I don’t know, I’ll find out and if we don’t have it, I’ll try to get it.</li>
<li>Support and practice the F.A.S.T. Track Principles from the executive level to the front line staff level.</li>
<li>Acquire knowledge, training and education. Communicate it to all employees.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Service Excellence</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Anticipate our guests’ needs before they ask.</li>
<li>Contribute ideas and solutions to improve customer service.</li>
<li>Escort our guests rather than point.</li>
<li>Take personal pride in the quality of your work.</li>
<li>Actively seek feedback from our guests concerning our service and atmosphere.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Team Work</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Share and explain information.</li>
<li>Great teams consist of great performers.</li>
<li>Create a culture of two-way communication with employees and guests.</li>
<li>Be courteous, kind and patient with each other.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills, Education and Other Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be fluent in English and have good verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Must be able to push or pull up to 100 pounds.</li>
<li>Previous Microsoft Excel, Word and Outlook software experience preferred.</li>
<li>A valid Nevada Driver’s License with an acceptable driving record</li>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent work experience required.</li>
<li>Previous work experience in warehouse, receiving and food purchasing is desired.</li>
</ul>
