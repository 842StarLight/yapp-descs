<h2>Research Consulting Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Title: Research Consulting Manager</b></p>
<p>Holleran is a recognized thought leader in the field of engagement research, needs assessment and consulting for senior living communities and community health organizations. We are located in Wrightsville, PA, in a beautiful setting overlooking the Susquehanna River. We believe in healthy work-life equilibrium and, while we are driven professionals, we still know how to have fun at work!</p>
<p>Our Research Consulting Manager insures a best-in-class client experience by leading our research and project management teams. You are a dynamic, people-focused leader who guides teams and culture to professional excellence. Salary range: $70,000 - $85,000, dependent on career history, skills and experience.</p>
<p><b>Skills and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated success as a project lead to: implement new processes, conceive and develop new products, improve operations and/or improve business results</li>
<li>Successful track record in project management; skilled planner who can run multiple projects from conception to completion efficiently</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to consistently meet deadlines and budgets and to hold individuals accountable to the same</li>
<li>Successful work experience managing high level client relationships and delivering client presentations</li>
<li>Experience in leading culture shift</li>
<li>Relevant education: research, public health, human services, and/or statistics</li>
<li>Successful work experience in the field of aging services, market and/or opinion research, human services consulting and/or public health</li>
<li>Hand-on experience with research technology software, database platforms, internet research and tools for analytics</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to visualize the power of technology and to implement new technology to improve business outcomes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Attributes</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead by example – open and transparent</li>
<li>Decisive; makes good decisions based on due diligence and the assessment of options</li>
<li>Confident, but open to learning and personal change; recognizes the strength of humility</li>
<li>Optimistic</li>
<li>Communication excellence; both verbal and written</li>
<li>Committed to the organization</li>
<li>Creative, with a drive to bring ideas to fruition</li>
<li>Equally skilled at seeing the big picture and focusing on the details</li>
<li>Encourages autonomy; does not micromanage</li>
<li>Focused on productivity and results</li>
<li>Support and empower others, while upholding the standards of results and excellence</li>
</ul>
<p><b>A Day in the Life</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee research and client services operations; responsible for deadlines and successful project outcomes</li>
<li>Work with Sales to bring new products to fruition</li>
<li>Work with Operations to enhance product pricing and client proposals</li>
<li>Work with those charged with the oversight of client services standards and work process to insure project integrity, delivery excellence and business efficiency</li>
<li>Bring a client services perspective to special projects; working collaboratively with all stakeholders to develop the project deliverables and to secure the necessary resources for successful outcomes</li>
<li>Presentations and consulting with complex clients</li>
<li>Monitor team adherence to standards and process by establishing and communicating the ‘bumpers’ of team autonomy</li>
<li>Serve as staff manager for all client services staff</li>
<li>Member of the Leadership Team</li>
</ul>
<p>This is an onsite, fulltime, exempt position. We offer a group health plan, contributions to an HSA account, 401(k) match, disability and life insurance, available dental and vision, generous paid time off and a collaborative, growth-focused team of colleagues.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $70,000.00 to $85,000.00 /year</p>
