<h2>Parts/Service Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>POSITION INFORMATION</b></p>
<p>Under the direct supervision of the Parts and Service managers this position sells and schedules needed service work in the service department. This position performs a variety of customer service, in store marketing, promoting advertising and stocking duties related to the receiving, sale and delivery of parts and accessories that contribute to the efficient, safe and profitable operation of the dealership's parts department. This role also maintains a clean, cordial and professional atmosphere for service reception, work scheduling, job assignments and general correspondence with customers and technicians.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>BASIC RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain customer goodwill by greeting and acknowledge all customers he/she comes in contact with</li>
<li>Sells product and/or services to repair the problem and meet the customers needs</li>
<li>Assist in keeping parts department clean and orderly</li>
<li>Provide service technicians with parts as required</li>
<li>Performs routine housekeeping tasks that maintain and enhances the cleanliness of products and the retail sales area</li>
<li>Answer telephones, receive and schedule appointments</li>
<li>Obtain customer information, vehicle data, work requested and signature work order</li>
<li>Open repair orders with clear information and instructions about work to be done</li>
<li>Handle telephone inquiries regarding progress</li>
<li>When working the parts counter, follows prescribed cash, credit and check processing procedures of the dealership. Processes cores, warranties and new return merchandise as required at a point of sale</li>
<li>Keeps themselves groomed and clothed per dealership guidelines</li>
<li>Attend training as prescribed by management to maintain proficiency</li>
<li>Treats customers fairly and with honesty and demonstrate our commitment to superior customer service and ethical business practices</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma</li>
<li>Basic computer skills</li>
<li>Ability to effectively listen</li>
<li>Good written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Previous experience with diesel truck parts and repair knowledge preferred</li>
<li>Ability to speak effectively one on one</li>
</ul>
<p>WORKING CONDITIONS</p>
<p>This is a physically demanding position. Will stand eight or so hours per shift. Will lift parts weighing in excess of 115 pounds several times during each shift. Will be exposed to noise, vibration, dust, exhaust fumes and other hazardous and non-hazardous materials.<br>
