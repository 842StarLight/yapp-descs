<h2>Marketing Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Swyft is the world’s leading provider of automated retail solutions for brands and retailers. Our proven and innovative end-to-end solutions include the hardware, software and managed services needed to effectively deploy and operate automated retail machines (SwyftStores) in high-traffic locations such as airports, malls, military bases, hotels, casinos, and universities. Our clients include some of the most recognized brands and retailers in the world such as Best Buy, CVS, Pokémon, UNIQLO, Proactiv, Nespresso, and Benefit Cosmetics.</p>
<p>
Our headquarters are in downtown San Francisco, close to BART and a short walk from other major transit centers. We are a global company, operating over 1,200 automated stores throughout the United States, Canada, and Europe.</p>
<p>
This is an excellent opportunity for an outstanding individual to join a growing company with competitive compensation.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position overview:</b></p>
<p>
This full-time position is set to start in October, 2019 and will be based out of Swyft’s San Francisco HQ.</p>
<p>
The position as Marketing Executive will be responsible for leading multiple marketing programs including our annual trade show participation and budget, email and digital marketing, and drive content generation for messaging,</p>
<p>
The successful applicant will work directly with the VP of Marketing and C-level executives, external vendors, brand partners and members of the press. This role involves domestic travel.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key responsabilities:
</b></p>
<p>The successful applicant will drive the following marketing projects:</p>
<ul>
<li>Trade Show Management &amp; Events: The successful applicant will organize all of Swyft’s trade shows including design, messaging, logistics and messaging to achieve corporate objectives. Applicant will also be responsible to organize smaller office (internal) and networking events (external).</li>
<li>Website development: The successful applicant will drive Swyft’s website development and content creation. It is the applicant's job to generate content and build the website as the company and product portfolio expands.</li>
<li>Marketing &amp; sales collateral: The candidate will support the Sales team by driving a unified sales message and supporting collateral (brochures, presentations).</li>
<li>Social media: The candidate will drive Swyft’s social media presence including Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, and YouTube.</li>
<li>PR &amp; Awards: The applicant will handle relationships with press, including the creation of press release, interviews with Swyft’s Senior Management, publications and online presence.</li>
<li>Email marketing: Drive the automation of email marketing campaigns (B2C/B2B) for brands or our sales team</li>
<li>Product Marketing projects: Support product marketing projects with expertise, marketing and design</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Education requirement:
</b></p>
<p>Minimum requirements for this position is a B.S. in Business Administration, Marketing, Design, or related fields.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Required skills:
</b></p>
<p>Applicants must:</p>
<ul>
<li>Have a minimum of 2 years work experience in the retail industry</li>
<li>Have a minimum of 1 year work experience in a start-up environment</li>
<li>Be highly organized and must be able to manage multiple tasks at once</li>
<li>Be able to quickly adapt to fast-changing environments and drive decision making under pressure</li>
<li>Demonstrate experience with email marketing campaigns and digital advertising</li>
<li>Have strong creative writing skills to lead the content creation for press releases, event managing, email and online marketing campaigns, social media</li>
<li>Be experienced with Software tools such as: Salesforce, Marketing Cloud, Salesloft, and has the ability to quickly learn new ones as the need arises.</li>
<li>Have a strategic mindset and a proactive working attitude</li>
<li>Must have a professional attitude to communicate and negotiate with Senior Management, members of the press, external vendors, partners and our brands</li>
<br>
</ul>
