<h2>Head of Freemium (Director/Sr. Director/AVP)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Head of Freemium (Director/Sr. Director/AVP)</b><br>
<b>Job type: Full-time</b><br>
<b>Location: Belmont</b><br>
<b>Department: Collaboration</b></p>
<p>
Job Description:</p>
<p>
RingCentral is growing rapidly as businesses of all sizes discover the value of delivering a cloud-based unified communication and collaboration experience to their customers and colleagues. We’ve established a leadership position as recognized in the 2019 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Unified Communications as a Service (UCaaS) worldwide and are accelerating the rollout of our multi-product portfolio.</p>
<p>
To expand the addressable market for these industry-leading products, we are adding an experienced viral growth expert to ramp our freemum programs. In this role, you will work cross-functionally across Product Marketing, Product Management, Marketing, and Sales to bring compelling offers to market and drive them to scale.</p>
<p>
You must be able to connect the dots from a handful of signups within a company, to a workgroup with increasing adoption, to an enterprise-wide sale.</p>
<p>
You must be comfortable performing a wide variety of tasks and working with multiple systems, teams and KPIs in a fast-paced environment. We need a strategist who is not afraid to roll up their sleeves and do what it takes to get the job done.</p>
<p>
What you get to do in this role:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Research, analyze, and specify compelling freemium offers across the RingCentral product portfolio.</li>
<li>
Scope, design, and develop viral growth programs leveraging existing customer experiences and product usage to maximize signups.</li>
<li>
Launch and execute multi-channel marketing programs that accelerate growth through advertising, social, and PR.</li>
<li>
Partner with Product Management to develop, launch, and refine the right products to solve key customer problems and drive company growth.</li>
<li>
Partner with Product Marketing to drive synergy with broader GTM strategies and motions.</li>
<li>
Partner with Marketing on campaigns to maximize market awareness, account penetration, and usage.</li>
<li>
Partner with Upsell and Sales to identify and pursue timely cross-sell and upsell initiatives.</li>
<li>
Partner with Analytics to measure effectiveness and adjust campaign tactics as needed.</li>
<li>
Communicate, educate, and update program health to inform key executives and stakeholders on results, plans, and strategies.</li>
<li>
Build a team of cross functional freemium experts to support the companies freemium efforts</li>
<li>
Stand up the right tools and processes required to automate and improve the output of the team</li>
</ul>
<p>
The following qualifications and skills required:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree, Master’s preferred</li>
<li>
10+ years of digital Product Marketing or Product Management experience at a strategic-level</li>
<li>
Ability to scale cloud/SaaS products and solutions targeting business customers</li>
<li>
Success in leveraging freemium, viral, and/or bottom’s up growth strategies</li>
<li>
Presence to present strategies and results to C-level audiences</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a cross-functional role and direct the focus of projects outside your immediate reporting structure</li>
<li>
Experience acting as a liaison between marketing, product, and sales teams</li>
<li>
Strong writing and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Excellent attention to detail and strong organizational skills</li>
<li>
Goals and results driven. The numbers will speak for themselves</li>
</ul>
