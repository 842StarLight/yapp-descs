<h2>Product Manager (Outbound) - Chicago</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Do you have a passion for creating IoT solutions for enterprises in an ever-changing market? Do you want to create innovative proposals that will transform our customer’s business? At relayr, we are obsessed with helping traditional industrial businesses stay relevant. We focus on delivering business outcomes enabled by our technology.</p>
<p>
Relayr is the fastest growing and one of the most successful Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) organizations. With around 250 employees and 5 years of company history we are successfully helping companies in industrial markets to navigate disruption and stay relevant. Our unique combination of applying world-class technology, powerful financial and insurance offerings, with dedicated professional services allows us to deliver guaranteed business outcomes to customers.</p>
<p>
We are looking for a strategic, talented and highly motivated out-bound focused <b>Product Manager </b>to be based in our <b>Chicago</b> office.</p>
<p>
Product Managers in relayr will be true “quarterbacks” for the product/technology - acting like the General Manager of the products and solutions and owning its Market Growth. Relayr always partners with industrial mid-market customers to keep them relevant with a focus on Business Outcomes. The responsibility of the Product Manager would be to enable and educate the market and the sales teams on how technology can help with the Business Outcomes. This individual will also help with product/solution strategy to impact roadmap, drive engineering innovation with real market feedback and pulse, sales traction and enablement, solution positioning and package creation as well as own the “Route to Market” with broad support from the other functional teams.</p>
<p><b>
Key responsibilities include:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop the Positioning and Messaging framework for the solutions/technology and communicate this internally, closely aligned with sales, to external market and partners.</li>
<li>Collaborate with and act as a focal point for Field Marketing, Sales &amp; Sales Engineering, PR, Executives, Customers, Partners related to the value proposition, positioning, messaging, and go-to-market activities.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Marketing team to create all marketing deliverables for the relayr technology including sales presentations, training materials, webinars, data sheets and white papers, internal collateral such as competitive documents, conference materials, case studies etc.</li>
<li>Represent the technology/solution at trade shows, user group conferences, key customer engagements, partners etc.</li>
<li>Launch technology/product packages and features as well as new capabilities and vertical specific solutions both internally to all relayr stakeholders and externally to customers and partners.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Inbound Product Management team with buyer research and real customer feedback on relayr technology to influence roadmap and strategic direction of the product; by being the pulse of the market and the buyer.</li>
<li>Buyer research and personal validation and articulation for refining the positioning statement as well as feature prioritization for the Product/Engineering teams within relayr.</li>
<li>Join customer meetings to create innovative solutions that were not previously considered, and have a passion for helping customers to understand how our product can help their business.</li>
<li>Remain visionary, look for the bigger opportunity and right product solution to make the customer successful.</li>
</ul>

<li>BS/BA/BE with at least 10+ years of work experience</li>
