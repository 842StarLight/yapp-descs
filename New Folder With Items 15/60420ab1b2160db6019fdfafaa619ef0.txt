<h2>Enterprise Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Are you looking for an incredible opportunity to work with a high-growth SaaS company bringing market-leading products to Enterprise-level clients?

<li>Prospect for new qualified Mid-Market leads</li>
<li>Present and demonstrate the value of LeanData products and services to prospective clients</li>
<li>Bring in new business to LeanData, achieving monthly revenue goals</li>

<ul>

<li>3 + years of experience in Enterprise sales</li>
<li>Working knowledge of CRM and or Marketing Automation Software</li>
<li>Excellent presentation skills</li>
<li>Strong communication and listening skills to effectively communicate with senior management and C-level executives</li>
<li>High energy and positive attitude</li>
</ul>
<b>About you (we’d like you to have):</b><br>
