<h2>Shipping Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>JD Machine Corp. was established in 1979 and is a world-class machine shop located in Ogden, Utah. We are looking for detail-oriented, self-motivated individuals to join our JD Family. The main responsibilities of this job include packaging parts for shipment using methods to prevent damage during shipment, visually inspect parts for damage/non-conformities while packaging, and preparing shipping labels.</p>
<p><b>
This position is best suited for individuals who have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Attention to detail</li>
<li>Basic computer &amp; mathematical skills'Sending emails</li>
<li>Measuring experience preferred (tape measure, calipers)</li>
<li>Previous experience with ERP system preferred</li>
<li>Previous receiving experience preferred</li>
<li>Inventory experience a plus</li>
<li>Lean Manufacturing experience a plus</li>
<li>Ability to work well in a team environment</li>
<li>Ability to pass a pre-employment drug test and a criminal background check</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Primary Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Unloading trucks and moving product using forklift, pallet jack and dolley.</li>
<li>Counting product received.</li>
<li>Scanning and saving paperwork.</li>
<li>Un-packaging products and verifying against PO requirements.</li>
<li>Use M1 brand ERP software to document receipt transactions</li>
<li>Move materials and products to correct locations throughout the shop</li>
<li>Using calipers and measuring tape with different units of measure to verify product</li>
<li>Working and communicating with Purchasing, AP, Production, Sales, Quality, and other teams.</li>
<li>Housekeeping of area and 5S</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Climate controlled new building</li>
<li>Noise from machinery</li>
<li>Indoor/outdoor when unloading delivery vehicles</li>
<li>Personal Protective Equipment required'safety glasses, safety toe shoes</li>
<li>Standing for long periods of time</li>
<li>Ability to lift up to 40 lbs unassisted</li>
</ul>
<p>We are a family-owned company, and work to strengthen our people, our company, our customers and our community. We offer a great team-orientated, clean work environment in a modern facility with state of the art equipment and competitive wages and benefits including health insurance, dental insurance, 401(k) with matching contributions, paid time off, life insurance, long-term disability insurance, and job-related education assistance.</p>
