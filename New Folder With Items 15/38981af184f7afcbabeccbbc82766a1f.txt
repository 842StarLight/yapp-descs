<h2>Volume Control Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>**Position Description:</b> **
<br><br>
<b>Department Overview:</b> Transportation Services department is a small dynamic team that manages a unique customer portfolio, producing 9% of the revenue and 67% of volume for ONG annually. The department supports more than 5,300 transportation customers and 35 third party suppliers each month. In addition, the department is responsible for executing the monthly billing/invoicing process, volume control pipeline management and balancing analysis, all facets of external customer service including collections, and setup/monitoring the billing of negotiated contract terms.
<br><br>
Performs duties related to allocating volumes and billing based on nominations to transportation customers and gas marketing companies.
<br><br>
Prepare and monitor daily/monthly scheduling, allocations, confirmations, posting of capacity releases and dispatching of gas volumes.
<br><br>
Analyze, monitor, and assist in resolving volume discrepancies, imbalances, settlements, allocations, measurement issues and capacity issues.
<br><br>
Interact with producers, transportation customers, employees of gas marketing and pipeline companies and others responding to inquiries related to nomination/scheduling issues, balancing, inquires, and/or flow issues.
<br><br>
Provide guidance and information to company employees, transportation customers, marketers, and interconnecting pipeline companies on matters related to gas volume nominations, allocations, and capacity releases.
<br><br>
Maintain and/or prepare information and correspondence including but not limited to:
<br><br>
Imbalances/settlements
<br><br>
Invoices
<br><br>
Allocation statements
<br><br>
Volume reports
<br><br>
Operational balancing agreements
<br><br>
Prior Period
<br><br>
External user access
<br><br>
Training documents for web users
<br><br>
Bulletin Board Posting
<br><br>
Tariff rates and general terms and conditions
<br><br>
Desk procedures
