<h2>Insurance Producer - Commercial Lines - Rigg Darlington Group</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Rigg Darlington Group in partnership with Acrisure, is among the fastest growing insurance brokerage firms in the U.S. Our unique ability to offer an established, local service model along with best in class carriers, resources and knowledge allows us to greatly differentiate ourselves in our industry. We are a committed network of entrepreneurs dedicated to providing specialty insurance programs, risk management services and unique products for an ever-growing market. Our team is comprised of innovative, talented individuals who are the driving force of the success we have experienced and continue to achieve in this fast-paced industry.</p>
<p>
We are currently looking to add our team with an exciting opportunity as a Commercial Lines Producer. This position will responsible for the production of new business and as well as the management of an existing book of business with the support of an established and knowledgeable service team.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Achieve objectives established in annual producer sales plan</li>
<li>
Prospect for new business from existing clients and identified target groups</li>
<li>
Contact prospects for the purpose of providing a quote and making sales appointments</li>
<li>
Survey prospects to determine insurance needs, inspecting current insurance policies, risk management plans, property, products and records</li>
<li>
Manage and retain existing book of small commercial business</li>
<li>
Collect detailed risk and underwriting information including survey data and loss history</li>
<li>
Develop and deliver formal proposals of insurance including details of coverages, limits, deductibles and other pertinent information</li>
<li>
Work with service person or marketer to complete company submissions and selection of potential markets</li>
<li>
Conduct client and prospect appointments communicating proposals and renewals, explaining details and communicating desire to conduct business</li>
<li>
Introduce service person to clients and request that service calls be directed to them and forward all service related calls to assigned service person</li>
<li>
Stay informed of all claims or service issues and become involved in resolution when required</li>
<li>
Participate in renewals, when appropriate, by reviewing expiration listing with service person to determine appropriate action and collection of information to prepare renewal submissions</li>
<li>
Maintain production reports and attend all sales meetings as required</li>
<li>
Promote agency and insurance industry in the community</li>
<li>
Keep informed of industry developments through review of trade press and by attending insurance carrier training meetings on new products, etc.</li>
<li>
Perform other specific duties and projects as assigned by agency management</li>
<li>
Contribute to Annual Agency plan and support its execution</li>
</ul>
<p>
This description is not meant to be all-inclusive and may be modified from time to time at the discretion of management.
</p>
