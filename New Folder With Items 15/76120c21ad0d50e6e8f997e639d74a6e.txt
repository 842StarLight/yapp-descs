<h2>Regional Director of Public Policy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Looking for an opportunity with a growing, exciting company? The Gotcha Group is an innovative mobility and media company looking for a motivated (and dare we say, fun) individual to join our team as a Regional Director of Public Policy – West Coast in our Los Angeles, CA office.
<br><br>
<b>Position overview:</b><br>
Gotcha is looking for highly motivated government relations specialist to join the team. As we expand our new mobility options and work to improve access to transit, Gotcha has an increased presence and interaction with local and state governments. The Regional Director of Public Policy (West Coast) is a full-time, salaried position (with other benefits described below) who will work closely the National Director of Public Policy, other policy team members and our legal team in the continued development and improvement of our government relations strategy and regulatory behavior.
<br><br>
This role will require a diversity of skill-sets including strong communication, project management, and organizational skills. You must be a team player, who is able to manage cross-functional stakeholders and adhere to tight deadlines. As a critical member of the team, you will work with the National Director of Policy and other team members to be the expert in Gotcha’s new mobility products and tasked with conveying Gotcha’s vision for the future of shared mobility and sustainable transportation alternatives.
<br><br>
If you think you're a fit, we want to hear from you! Please submit a resume along with a cover letter explaining why you want to work with The Gotcha Group, as well as how your experience could be an asset to our growing company.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>The Regional Director of Public Policy for Gotcha will help solve regulatory challenges related to entering and operating in municipal markets in the west coast region with an immediate focus in California</li>
<li>Participate effectively and engage with the national government relations team</li>
<li>Work across departments, including Operations, Marketing, Communications as well as with external consultants, to ensure smooth alignment.</li>
<li>Monitor legislative developments and ensure appropriate government engagement in the west coast region and support other regions as needed.</li>
<li>Work with the National Policy Director and other team members to develop creative strategies and tactics to drive Gotcha’s regulatory objectives .</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree required; advanced degree preferred</li>
<li>5+ years’ work experience in government, political or management consulting, lobbying, in-house government relations, political communications</li>
<li>Experience in one or more of the following areas: city, state or federal government; legislative affairs; urban mobility; politics/campaigns; advocacy</li>
<li>Experience working in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment</li>
<li>Understanding of both the regulatory processes and politics involved at the federal, state and local level</li>
<li>Experienced public speaker, comfortable communicating your message and responding to tough questions</li>
<li>Ability to negotiate with city and/or legislative staff effectively, while maintaining positive relationships</li>
<li>Be a self-starter, with the ability to own and drive priorities within your portfolio</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze, process and convey regulatory language and parameters</li>
<li>Ability to meet deadlines without exceptions</li>
<li>Advanced skills in Microsoft Office software</li>
<li>Ability to build positive internal relationships</li>
<li>Able to work autonomously and self-motivated</li>
<li>Sense of urgency with a high attention to detail</li>
<li>Sales and Management experience preferred</li>
<li>Ability to travel as needed</li>
</ul>
Why join us?

At Gotcha, our mission is to improve the daily lives of people in the spaces and events most important to them. Built on the core belief that there is nothing we cannot do, Gotcha strives daily to expand our footprint, positively impacting as many people, cities and university campuses as possible.
