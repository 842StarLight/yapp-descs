<h2>Associate Marketing Manager - Equine</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Supports the execution of the brand plan to meet business objectives. Has developed knowledge and skills to identify and solve issues related to the implementation of the brand plan. Works with moderate guidance in own department and leverages cross-functional support</div>
<div></div>
<div>As an employee of Boehringer Ingelheim, you will actively contribute to the discovery, development and delivery of our products to our patients and customers. Our global presence provides opportunity for all employees to collaborate internationally, offering visibility and opportunity to directly contribute to the companies' success. We realize that our strength and competitive advantage lie with our people. We support our employees in a number of ways to foster a healthy working environment, meaningful work, diversity and inclusion, mobility, networking and work-life balance. Our competitive compensation and benefit programs reflect Boehringer Ingelheim's high regard for our employees.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assists in the development, implementation and execution of strategic plans that optimize sales, market share and revenue growth for a brand or set of brands in the short and long term.
</li>
<li>To support consistent execution of product strategies with internal teams.</li>
<li>To participate in the identification of insights using market research, competitive intelligence, and understanding of therapeutic areas.</li>
<li>Demonstrates understanding of the financial goals and considerations that impact the organizations overall financial performance by managing their brand budgets.</li>
<li>Integrates information from multiple sources to develop messages for target audiences by utilizing appropriate segmentation methodologies.</li>
<li>Proposes optimal promotional mix for an individual brand determines the tasks involved in completing projects and identifies the resources needed.</li>
<li>Keeps up-to-date on business practices and market trends that may impact the company's financial performance to allow development of a robust sales and product supply forecast.</li>
<li>To have close liaison with the field force and listens directly to customers to gain an understanding of customers' needs and perceptions.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required plus three (3) to five (5) years or more previous product marketing, field sales or relevant work experience.
</li>
<li>Master's degree preferred.</li>
<li>Exhibit strong written &amp; verbal communication skills along with excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated organization, planning &amp; project management skills.</li>
<li>Proven ability to work on multiple projects &amp; prioritize effectively.</li>
<li>Demonstrated strategic thinking, initiative, and creativity.</li>
<li>Develop strategic &amp; tactical elements based on research, data &amp; industry trends.</li>
<li>Demonstrated problem solving and analytical skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work with cross functional stakeholders OR demonstrated ability to work in a highly matrixed organization.</li>
<li>Proven track record of achieving goals OR proven track record of meeting financial and other quantitative goals.</li>
<li>Demonstrated success working in a team environment.</li>
<li>Performs all Company business in accordance with all regulations (e.g. EEO, FDA, OSHA etc.) and Company policies and procedures. When violations are noted/observed they are to be immediately reported to management.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates high ethical and professional standards with all business contacts in order to main BIVI’s excellent reputation within the community.</li>
<li>Successfully completes Compliance Modules assigned annually by the Company and develops challenging yet compliant advertising messages while avoiding regulatory agency warning letters.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Eligibility Requirements:

<li>Must be willing to submit to a background investigation, including verification of your past employment, criminal history, and educational background</li>
<li>Must be willing to take a drug test and post-offer physical (if required)</li>

</ul>
