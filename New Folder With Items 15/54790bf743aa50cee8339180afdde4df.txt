<h2>Partner Senior Sales Engineer - Federal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Appian provides a software development platform that combines intelligent automation and enterprise low-code development to rapidly deliver powerful business applications. Many of the world's largest organizations use Appian applications to improve customer experience, achieve operational excellence, and simplify global risk and compliance.
<br><br>
As a Partner Sales Engineer, you will educate Appian partners on the value of our platform in conjunction with the sales team as the key technical advisor and product advocate. You must be able to engage and articulate product positioning to both business and technical users, and will play a critical role in partner enablement.
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with Partner Directors to evangelize the Appian Platform to our partner ecosystem</li>
<li>Assist in the production of RFPs and other proposals to partners</li>
<li>Propose technical and creative business solutions in support of sales activities</li>
<li>Design and deliver high-quality technical Proof of Concepts for partners and prospective customers</li>
<li>Present technical and business aspects of Appian to a C-Level audience</li>
<li>Represent Appian's product to prospects at field events such as conferences, summits, seminars, etc</li>
<li>Assist with the radiation strategy within the partner ecosystem by building relationships</li>
<li>Work with colleagues across the organization to receive the support needed to accomplish the technical goals</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Math, Business Management, Computer Science</li>
<li>5+ years as a successful Pre-Sales Engineer OR 5+ years in a Professional Services/Consulting role that is client facing, partner engaging and involves sales scenarios in Federal space. 10+ years overall government IT experience</li>
<li>Have a clear understanding of the mission of the agency you're serving and their responsibility to their constituents (even if they're not directly servicing citizens it's important to understand who they serve—I.e. shared services, Office of Inspector General, etc.)</li>
<li>Experience on federal contracting process and how the government buys is a plus</li>
<li>Experience with the major contracts and contract vehicles along with the key players. For example, certain integrators embedded within organizations</li>
<li>Able to thrive in a unique culture where thinking outside of the box is required daily and creative input is necessary to solve business problems</li>
<li>Technical depth to respond to all functional and technical elements of RFIs/RFPs</li>
<li>Experience architecting software for scalable and distributed systems</li>
<li>Experience working with Global Partners as well as regional, Original Equipment manufacturer (OEM) and distributors</li>
<li>Demonstrate persistence and patience on working with large scale opportunities that scale multiple years</li>
<li>Working with partner enablement and partner practice assistance</li>
<li>Familiarity with Business Process Management Platforms/Frameworks is a plus: Appian, IBM, Oracle, Mendix, OpenSource (e.g. jBPM), etc.</li>
<li>Recent experience programming in one or more of the following: Java (JNDI, JDBC, Web Services), JSON, XML, JavaScript, SQL</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills, customer interaction, requirements analysis, presentations, and system design</li>
<li>Willing to travel 50% to customer locations throughout the assigned region</li>
<li>Excellent relationship building skills</li>
<li>Affinity to both sales and technology functions</li>
<li>Bi-lingual required (English mandatory) -Multi-lingual preferred</li>
<li>Appian Skill is a plus - BPM/ Case management, software platform experience is preferred</li>
</ul>
