<h2>Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p><b>Great Dane</b> is now hiring for a <b>Branch Manager</b> at our <b>Richmond, Virginia </b>Branch location.</p>
<p>
Directly accountable for a specified sales force with the responsibility to provide support and oversight for new and existing accounts for the company’s existing product line of new and used trailers and Great Dane Aftermarket Parts &amp; Services as well as other products offered by the company. The position facilitates and promotes all facets of sales and operations to ultimately ensure customer satisfaction.</p>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Create and implement a strategic plan to identify all potential customers and effectively organize sales territory.</li>
<li>Strategically develop, execute and manage sales plans with customers that exceed GDAP sales goals.</li>
<li>Manage customer relationships and serve as a conduit between customers GDAP</li>
<li>Analyze market trends and implement corporate marketing strategies.</li>
<li>Establish and monitor sales initiatives of sales personnel, utilizing trends, economic indicators and company needs.</li>
<li>Researches region as to buying habits, specific needs, pricing criterion and potential forecast.</li>
<li>Assist sales personnel to identify &amp; maintain accounts</li>
<li>Communicate with corporate sales team any trending economic or industry indicators.</li>
<li>Provide company leadership with the actual forecast for the upcoming year.</li>
<li>Provide real time voice of customer feedback to senior management.</li>
<li>Proactively work with entire GDAP team to provide proactive improvement that drives customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Lead the team through performance management, coaching, mentoring, hiring and career development.</li>
<li>Protect and maintain all assets of Great Dane branch facilities.</li>
<li>Monitor service department performance.</li>
<li>Ensure safety compliance at branch locations.</li>
<li>Develop and monitor budgets for branch operations.</li>
<li>Accomplish all managerial responsibilities associated with personnel</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Competencies:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Customer Focus: Is dedicated to meeting the expectations and requirements of internal and external customers; gets firsthand customer information and uses it for improvements in products and services; acts with customers in mind; establishes and maintains effective relationships with customers and gains their trust and respect</li>
<li>Action Oriented: Enjoys working hard; is action oriented and full of energy for the things he/she sees as challenging; not fearful of acting with a minimum of planning; seizes more opportunities than others</li>
<li>Functional/Technical Skills: Has the functional and technical knowledge and skills to do the job at a high level of accomplishment</li>
<li>Ethics &amp; Values: Adheres to an appropriate and effective set of core values and beliefs during both good and bad times; acts in line with those values; rewards the right values and disapproves of others; practices what he/she preaches</li>
<li>Organizational Agility: Knowledgeable about how organizations work; knows how to get things done both through formal channels and the informal network; understands the origin and reasoning behind key policies, practices, and procedures; understands the cultures of organizations</li>
<li>Command Skills: Relishes leading, takes unpopular stands if necessary; encourages direct and tough debate but isn’t afraid to end it and move on; is looked to for direction in a crisis; faces adversity head on; energized by tough challenges</li>
<li>Decision Making: Makes good decisions based upon a mixture of analysis, wisdom, experience, and judgment; makes decisions in a timely manner, sometimes with incomplete information and under tight deadlines and pressure; most of his/her solutions and suggestions turn out to be correct and accurate when judged over time; sought out by others for advice and solutions</li>
<li>Motivating Others: Creates a climate in which people want to do their best; can motivate many kinds of direct reports and team or project members; can assess each person’s hot button and use it to get the best out of him/her; pushes tasks and decisions down; empowers others; invites input from each person and shares ownership and visibility; makes each individual feel his/her work is important; is someone people like working for and with</li>
<li>Approachability: Is easy to approach and talk to; spends the extra effort to put others at ease; can be warm, pleasant, and gracious; is sensitive to and patient with the interpersonal anxieties of others; builds rapport well; is a good listener; is an early knower, getting informal and incomplete information in time to do something about it.</li>
<li>Conflict Management: Steps up to conflicts, seeing them as opportunities; reads situations quickly; good at focused listening; can hammer out tough agreements and settle disputes equitably; can find common ground and get cooperation with minimum noise.</li>
<li>Negotiation: Can negotiate skillfully in tough situations with both internal and external groups; can settle differences with minimum noise; can win concessions without damaging relationships; can be both direct and forceful as well as diplomatic; gains trust quickly of other parties to the negotiations; has a good sense of timing.</li>
<li>Perspective: Looks toward the broadest possible view of an issue/challenge; has broad-ranging personal and business interests and pursuits; can easily pose future scenarios; can think globally; can discuss multiple aspects and impacts of issues and project them into the future.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<div>
<b>Job Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Recruit and train new sales personnel.</li>
<li>Deliver ongoing sales education to sales personnel.</li>
<li>Review weekly, monthly and annual sales progress, expenses and cost structures.</li>
<li>Make calls with sales personnel, providing coaching and mentoring as appropriate.</li>
<li>Be able to sell customer the value of Great Dane Aftermarket parts and services</li>
<li>Develop and manage the local web presence and advertising methodologies.</li>
<li>Maintain up-do-date knowledge of any regulatory processes.</li>
<li>Be able to review and understand budget information</li>
<li>Be able to use and analyze data</li>
<li>Review weekly and monthly sales reports and compilations.</li>
<li>Attend and participate in local and national trade shows</li>
</ul>
