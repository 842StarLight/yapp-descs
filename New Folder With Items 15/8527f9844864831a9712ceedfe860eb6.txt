<p>We are currently hiring for our next National Key Accounts Manager, based in Phoenix, AZ. To be considered for this position, please visit the careers tab on our website and apply directly. www.eco3dusa.com/careers</p>
<p>The National Account Manager’s primary responsibility is to identify, maintain, and grow revenue with national enterprise level clients, in a specific market vertical for short and long-term value to the Company. The goal is to create recurring revenue opportunities that last many years.</p>
<p>You'll create a master list of key accounts all within a specific vertical market and develop long term relationships that translate to billable project opportunities maximizing the Company’s service offerings.</p>

<ul>

<li>Multi-site locations and/or projects</li>
<li>Annual revenue opportunity of $500k +</li>
<li>Value a relationship-based business approach versus low-bidder philosophy</li>
</ul>
