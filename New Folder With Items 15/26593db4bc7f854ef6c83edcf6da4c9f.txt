<h2>Field Service Representative, Heavy Equipment (New England)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>After-Sales Support Manager – New England</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>YANMAR America Corporation, located in Adairsville, GA, is the regional headquarters of YANMAR Co., Ltd. a global company based in Osaka, Japan. YANMAR is a recognized leader in the design and manufacturing of advanced performance diesel engines and diesel-powered equipment for a multitude of market segments including agricultural and construction equipment. YANMAR Co. Ltd., was established in 1912 and in 1933 became the world's first manufacturer to develop a practical small diesel engine. Over the last 80 years, YANMAR has been a manufacturer of world-renowned engines and equipment. YANMAR America Corporation is located at 101 International Parkway, Adairsville, GA 30103. For more information, please visit us.YANMAR.com.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Position Summary:</div>
<div></div>
<div>YANMAR America is seeking an After-Sales Support Manager for the New England Territory. The After-Sales Support Manager is responsible for continuously evaluating and improving the quality of YANMAR’s service network and serving as a technical subject matter expert on YANMAR products.After-Sales Support Managers assist YANMAR dealers to resolve customer issues and provide field service knowledge and experience back to YANMAR’s EVO//Center training development team to improve the technical training curriculum offered to our dealer network.Responsible for documenting customer visits and reporting results through CRM system or other report formats.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Duties and Responsibilities:</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Customer Technical Support</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Support distributors, engine centers and authorized parts &amp; service dealers by providing expertise and guidance in investigating, diagnosing and resolving service-related product malfunctions and issues.</li>
<li>Provide technical support at trade shows, and other customer-facing events to answer technical/user questions and to assist in resolving customer issues correctly and efficiently.</li>
<li>Assist distributors and dealers with new engine installations at OEM’s. Perform “zero hour” repairs at OEM’s.</li>
<li>Assist YANMAR Quality Assurance Department by conducting additional engine evaluations and system performance tests in the field and providing confirmation of operating conditions as an input to failure analysis and warranty claim determination.</li>
<li>Review the status of all Product Improvement Programs (PIP’s) with dealer service managers and technicians to develop dealer action and communication plans to increase PIP completion rates.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Parts Sales Support</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Meet and exceed Parts Sales Goals for achieving territory sales revenue.</li>
<li>Answer questions, provide assistance and onsite training to dealership staff regarding the YANMAR Dealer System (YDS), as well as the E-Claims warranty system.</li>
<li>Actively provide information and educate dealers on parts programs and promotions. Assist PSA’s by consulting with dealers to merchandise YANMAR parts and equipment to assist in growing retail sales.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Dealer Development</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Objectively evaluate dealership customer support capabilities and performance, identifying development areas, and working collaboratively with dealers to create a Dealer Service Development Plan to upgrade their service of YANMAR equipment and providing ultimate customer satisfaction in a profitable manner.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Regional Sales Managers to improved YANMAR’s sales &amp; service capability by filling open markets and evaluating and possibly replacing current dealers based on dealer service development plan / survey information.</li>
<li>Develop, communicate and execute a plan for developing and improving the effectiveness of the YANMAR Industrial Engine Service &amp; Parts Dealer network in the assigned territory.</li>
<li>Ensures that new and existing dealers meet existing requirements for service tools, parts, training, and that authorized YANMAR service signage is implemented as intended.</li>
<li>Initiate and complete contract terminations when dealers fail to achieve agreed-upon targets in the Customer Support Development plans.</li>
<li>Based on skill gaps identified in Customer Support Development Plans, enroll Dealership customer support personnel in appropriate YANMAR Academy learning paths.</li>
<li>Supplement YANMAR Academy technical training by delivering YANMAR Academy-approved onsite technical update seminars.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Administration</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Document customer visits by providing visit details, issues and identifying action items/tasks to resolve issues. Maintain accurate customer contact information for customer’s parts and service departments in the CRM system.</li>
<li>Submit expense reports, and update travel itineraries on a timely basis (weekly).</li>
<li>Prepare and present reports for monthly department meetings.</li>
<li>Collect, evaluate and share competitor product information (literature, pricing and programs).</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Knowledge, Skills, &amp; Abilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of Yanmar products.</li>
<li>Fluent in diesel engines, construction and/or agriculture equipment design, repair, maintenance, processes, electronic and hydraulic controls.</li>
<li>Ability to read and understand electrical and hydraulic schematics.</li>
<li>Excellent command of Microsoft Office Suite; especially Excel, PowerPoint and Word.</li>
<li>Ability to work with business systems and diagnostic software.</li>
<li>Able to type at least 35 words per minute.</li>
<li>Ability to travel, domestic and international up to 75-80%.</li>
</ul>
<div>Job Requirements:</div>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree and/or technical preferred and two (2+) years directly related technical field work experience with an engine or equipment manufacturer; or four (4) years of experience with increasing responsibility with diesel engine, construction equipment, transmission diagnostics and repair.</li>
<li>Must be able to travel to assist OEM’s/Distributors/Dealers; sometimes on very short notice.</li>
<li>Excellent oral, written communication, presentation and persuasion skills.</li>
<li>Must be able to demonstrate territorial management skills, as a remote manager in service &amp; parts sales.</li>
<li>Must have a valid passport and a driver’s license with an impeccable driving record.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>*This position is full time and based remotely in an appropriate location for effective access to the region of service. *</b></div>
