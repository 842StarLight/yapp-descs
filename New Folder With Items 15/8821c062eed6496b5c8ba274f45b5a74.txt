<h2>Damper Team Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview</b><br>
The Damper Team Supervisor will provide hands on leadership for the Damper Inspectors on a daily basis. The supervisor is responsible for building and strengthening each team member through proper training and guidance.<br>
</p>
<p>The Damper Team Supervisor will also be responsible for providing the Damper Manager with a daily detailed report on current project status as well as host daily/weekly field team meetings.<br>
</p>
<div>
<b>Role and Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Coach, mentor, motivate technicians, and influence them to take positive action and accountability for their assigned work</li>
<li>Train all technicians for proper installation of each product prior to starting projects</li>
<li>Train technicians to inspect for quality control</li>
<li>Monitor weekly manpower schedules</li>
<li>Set expectations of daily productivity and quality on the job</li>
<li>Review Safety Procedures and enforce standards</li>
<li>Utilize approved submittals, Spec-Data sheets, etc. to review the materials being installed</li>
<li>Drive performance so that each project is completed under budget and on schedule</li>
<li>Define project success criteria and disseminate to involved parties throughout project life cycle</li>
<li>Communicate and hit all project milestones, ensuring timely execution of projects according to plan</li>
<li>Completion of paperwork required on a typical project (i.e. electronic daily reports, safety tool box talks, etc.) providing accurate documentation for customers code compliance</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales and Operations Management in discussions to outline the scope of work including expectations and specifications</li>
<li>Proactively identify risks and issues and work to develop creative, client-centric, and cost-effective solutions</li>
<li>Document repairs that canâ€™t be completed, obtain and record specifications for materials to be ordered</li>
<li>Provide guidance and coordination with multiple departments around approaches solutions, and best practices</li>
<li>Interact professionally using excellent communication skills with a diverse group of managers</li>
<li>Document specific project issues and resolutions for cumulative lessons learned</li>
<li>Assist in the development, documentation and communication of policies and standards</li>
<li>Maintaining strict accountability of all equipment and tools</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>Requirements</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Previous damper inspection and compliance experience preferred</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of NFPA 80, NFPA 105 and the IBC standards</li>
<li>Good record keeping and tracking of facilities repair work</li>
<li>Ability to interpret life safety drawings</li>
</ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Use common hand tools such as knives, scissors, saws, and staple gun</li>
<li>Properly maintain equipment and tools</li>
<li>Extensive experience with Microsoft Office Suite, including Outlook, Word, Excel and Calendar.</li>
<li>Maintain professional, timely, communication with customers and coworkers</li>
<li>Professional presentation and verbal and written communication/instruction skills</li>
<li>Excellent collaboration skills both internally and externally</li>
<li>Excellent written/verbal communication and organizational/attention to detail skills</li>
<li>Positive attitude and strong work ethic</li>
<li>Self-starter, excellent listener and completes tasks independently and efficiently with limited direction</li>
<li>Strategic and critical thinking abilities in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Excellent organization, analytical, and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize tasks and meet deadlines on a daily basis</li>
<li>Self-directed and an ability to work with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Ability to handle stress and remain productive</li>
<li>Ability to learn other software systems used throughout our company</li>
<li>Team player with a positive personality</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and within a team on special, nonrecurring and/or ongoing projects</li>
<li>Professional appearance and demeanor</li>
<li>Willing to embrace change, redirection, and constructive criticism</li>
<li>Must be able to work overtime as required</li>
</ul>
