<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
BASIC PURPOSE</b></p>
<p>
Serves as an analytic and category management expert using the category management process as a decision platform for all initiatives designed to optimize distribution, shelving, price and promotional mix for Mars Wrigley Confectionery brands and the category. Provide category management assistance and fact-based selling to help meet and exceed volume, market share and other performance objectives. Deliver superior consumer &amp; shopper insights to support key Category initiatives as well as Mars Wrigley Confectionery's brand strategies.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b> (each item should account for 10% or more)</p>
<ul>
<li>
Primary point of contact for retailer's Seasonal Candy buying team regarding assortment, merchandising, pricing, and shelving.</li>
<li>
Maintain and increase influence through robust insights and actionable recommendations that drive Seasonal Candy growth.</li>
<li>
Assist sales team in developing fact and consumer based selling stories on new items, promotions, assortment, shelving optimization, and pricing initiatives.</li>
<li>
Network and drive penetration across various functions at the retailer (i.e. insight and testing team, merchandising, store operations).</li>
<li>
Work with the Category Team Lead, Category Director, Sales Team Lead, and Sales Director, and retailer to create strategic business plans that impact sales and profit.
</li>
<li>Think enterprise wide; work with the Mars Wrigley Confectionery Category Leadership Center of Excellence and Shopper Insights teams to leverage the latest insights and drive expansion of best practices across the enterprise.</li>
<li>
Partner with Shopper Marketing &amp; other internal teams to maximize promotional efficiencies for Mars Wrigley Confectionery and the retailer. Advanced analytical, project management, and written/oral communication skills required; ability to think strategically.</li>
<li>
Strong Microsoft Office skills to manipulate data and present findings in a quick and easy to read format.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
DECISION MAKING AUTHORITY, RECOMMENDATIONS AND IMPACT</b></p>
<p>
The Category Manager will have the authority to develop and lead key Category Management strategies and initiatives in support of the Seasonal Candy category. The work from this role will heavily influence category and brand strategies for Mars Wrigley Confectionery. Business building recommendations should be geared towards not only impacting Mars Wrigley Confectionery's business, but also focus on driving positive growth for the broader Confectionery category. Strategy development sourced from this Category Manager should play a key role in influencing in-store merchandising, promotional activity, pricing, and assortment. Driving Category growth is primary goal and should be leveraged to position Mars Wrigley Confectionery as a powerful Category Management leader.<br>
</p>
