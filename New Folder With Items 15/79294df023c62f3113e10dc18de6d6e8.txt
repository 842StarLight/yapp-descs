<h2>Digital Advertising Campaign Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Ifyou are looking for an amazing opportunity to use your eCommerce marketingexperience to drive growth for an extraordinary jewelry company, this newlycreated role is designed just for you! Ross-Simons,America’s favorite jeweler, is looking for a <b>Digital Advertising Campaign Manager</b>. As a key member of our expandingeCommerce business team, we’re looking for someone who can dig into the worldof Amazon and truly make a difference in our advertising and sales trajectory. Reporting directly to the VP of Marketing &amp;eCommerce, you’ll be an integral part of our strategy development andexecution.</p>
<p>The<b>Digital Advertising Campaign Manager</b>is responsible for setting up, optimizing, and reporting on digital advertisingcampaigns, primarily focused on paid search ads (Sponsored Products, SponsoredBrands, and Product Display Ads) within the Amazon Advertising Platform.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>· Create and manage Amazon advertisingcampaigns through the Amazon Advertising platform (AMS) including recommendationson overall advertising strategy.</p>
<p>· Utilize the AMS platform to administercampaigns while maintaining effective cost per acquisition (CPA), return on adspend (ROAS), or Advertising Cost of Sale (ACoS) metrics inclusive of keywordstrategy, optimizations, budget management and weekly reporting.</p>
<p>· Analyze and optimize campaigns froma variety of perspectives including conversion rates, keyword bids, impressionshares, seasonal/geographical trends, search queries, keyword and ad copyresearch &amp; development, product pages / landing pages, conversion funnel,quality score, competition, distribution channels, etc. to achieve maximum ROI.</p>
<p>· Manage the day-to-day business onAMS to grow sales by increasing product ranking, visibility and paid media strategy.</p>
<p>· Perform in-depth full-funnelanalysis and identify trends to optimize customer acquisition and conversionincluding deep dives into platform data to form the basis of efficient campaignstrategies.</p>
<p>· Manage &amp; report on ad budget,conforming to overall ACoS targets and other financial measures.</p>
<p>· Work in partnership with Merchandisingcounterparts to create an optimized advertising strategy.</p>
<p>· Develop and implement ad campaignsto support promotional strategies around key Amazon event including Prime Day, Holidays,Cyber Monday, etc.</p>
<p>· Identify new opportunities on AmazonMarketing platforms to increase demand and AOV.</p>
<p>· Develop forecasts, ROI targets, andsales plans for Amazon paid media campaigns.</p>
<p>· Ongoing understanding ofAmazon/online marketplaces, Amazon sales acceleration strategy, industrytrends, client successes, market activities, and competitors.</p>

<p>· Knowledge of product listingoptimization techniques</p>
<p>· Experience within a Direct toConsumer business model</p>
<p>· Proactive, detail-oriented, highlyanalytical and organized with strong media planning and analytical skills.</p>
<p>· Able to prioritize, multitask, organizework and accomplish tasks while managing multiple projects in a deadline drivenenvironment</p>
