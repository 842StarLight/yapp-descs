<h2>Client Partner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Reporting to our Head of Global Sales and working out of our San Francisco location, we are looking for an action-oriented relationship builder. We need a Client Partner with exceptional situational presence, intellect, ethics, and judgment that engender credibility and trust; someone with a deeply strategic approach to problem solving, and an interest in the long game. This is an opportunity with uncapped earning potential for a Client Partner, with banking, financial services or FinTech network connections, to build out an untapped territory in our western region.</p>
<p><b>
Who we are</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
As a BlueChip venture-backed, Silicon Valley Fintech, Nomis Solutions is growing at 30% year over year. We are fundamentally changing the way banks do business through science and software; working with big data and deep analytics, and building serverless products. Our mission is to partner with customers on their journey to best in class pricing technology and analytics so that they deliver more value to their customers, employees and shareholders. To date, we have over 100 implementations and 10,000 customers.</p>
<p><b>
Who you are</b></p>
<p>
You are a Sales Executive with an entrepreneurial style that combines quick, decision-making with long term planning, a well-thought-out enterprise process and a do-what-it-takes, go-getter approach.</p>
<p><b>
What's in it for you.</b></p>
<p><i>
We win as a team.</i> You will be part of a highly collaborative group, challenging the status quo.</p>
<p><i>
We delight our customers.</i> You will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Participate in our full Sales training program. You will gain insights into our value proposition, what sets Nomis apart and why we do things the way we do.</li>
<li>
Build on your reputation as a trusted advisor, nurture long-term relationships with customers and recommend tailored solutions that are aligned to business goals.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
We execute and deliver on promises. </i>You will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive the sales process and clearly present the unique value of our solutions to all stakeholders, including senior and C-level executives.</li>
<li>
Forecast sales activity in salesforce.com and achieve annual quota, while creating referenceable, satisfied customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
We thrive on growth.</i> You will:</p>
<ul><li>
Be part of a funded, scaling company with the stability of a 15+ year existence and the responsiveness of an Agile startup; a place where we support your continued career development.</li></ul>
<p><b>
What you bring</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Passion. You are genuinely interested in new ideas and new technology. SaaS ales is where you want to be and repeated success is how you plan on growing your career.</li>
<li>
Expertise. You have 7+ years of experience selling enterprise services and solutions, ideally analytics-based. You have what it takes to successfully build long-term relationships and sell into banking and financial services. You get an adrenaline rush every time you close the deal. Your achievements go beyond quotas, extending into highly satisfied clients who trust your expertise.</li>
<li>
Motivation and drive. You are determined to find the leads, make the connections and get in the door. You get a thrill from pursuing the close which also means you are calm under pressure, confident in our solutions and unendingly patient.</li>
<li>
Analytical and innovative. You can take seemingly random puzzle pieces and assemble them to form robust solutions. You can make sound decisions quickly.</li>
<li>
Sales methodology. You believe in combining the proven Challenger process with an instinct for building relationships and understanding your client's needs. You are a trusted advisor that is sought for insights and best practices.</li>
<li>
Communication. You have outstanding presentation skills. Your consulting approach in meetings gains the trust of those listening to you. You have a strong business acumen with the ability to speak knowledgeably to sophisticated buyers. Your written and verbal one-on-one communication skills - including the ability to listen and provide a true solution to a client's unique issues - are why you consistently exceed quota.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you need to know</b></p>
<p>
We call ourselves Nomistas and new team members are Newmistas. We're a company with not-quite 150 people yet working with the biggest financial institutions in the world. We are leveraging cutting-edge behavioral data science to deliver market-leading cloud-based Pricing &amp; Profitability Management solutions and insights - this is a product that can't fail.</p>
<p>
We have all of the startup excitement and rewards; and, a culture ready to be built. We are committed to getting things done, following our gut and failing fast to learn faster. If you want to help define who we are, this is an exciting chance to take your career path in a new direction. If you want to have your voice heard, we are the people who are going to listen.</p>
<p>
Interested but not sure you meet all of the qualifications? We believe that skills and experience are acquired in different ways; if you have 70% of what we are looking for, go ahead and submit your resume - we promise to take a look. Express your interest here or follow us on Twitter or LinkedIn for future opportunities.</p>
