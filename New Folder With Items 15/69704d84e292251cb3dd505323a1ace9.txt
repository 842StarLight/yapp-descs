<h2>Real Estate Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Inspirato, a leading hospitality company based in Denver, has a unique opportunity for a smart, motivated and team-oriented person to join the Real Estate development team. This is a deal support position which will provide the right candidate with an excellent learning opportunity and high level of exposure to a fast growing company at the epicenter of a highly dynamic and competitive operating environment. The ideal candidate will have a high degree of integrity, business judgement and work ethic.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage all incoming new property leads, prospects and new initiatives for your dedicated portfolio. Maintain comprehensive inputs to lead tracking database for all lead correspondence and property research in a timely and organized fashion.</li>
<li>Draft and negotiate prospective legal contacts, including initial external communication with landlords, and internal communication with all departments</li>
<li>Underwrite prospective properties combining analysis in revenue management, market supply, revenue and cost projections and service delivery model.</li>
<li>Assist team with market development initiatives including sourcing new properties, strategic assessment of inventory and market conditions.</li>
<li>Work closely with sales team representatives and personal vacation advisors to stay abreast of prospect and member demand for new locations or property types.</li>
<li>Work closely with portfolio management and revenue management teams to ensure quality control of property underwriting.</li>
<li>Lead and manage new property releases, in coordination with other company teams, including collecting all available destination and property marketing content. Develop training materials for sales and Member Services teams on new properties and destinations.</li>
<li>Participate in day-to-day operations of real estate acquisitions, interacting directly with internal groups as well as the destination team. Assist with transition and onboarding of properties to portfolio management and operations teams.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Required Skills: </b>To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following competencies:</div>
<ul>
<li>Personal Strengths – Positive attitude, organized, self-motivated, willingness to learn, and flexibility to adapt to ever-changing responsibilities and work environment are musts.</li>
<li>Analytical Skills – must have some experience financial modeling and be knowledgeable and passionate about real estate and hospitality operations.</li>
<li>Attention to Detail – Must be able to consistently demonstrate a strong attention to detail for all lead generation, financial analysis interdepartmental collaboration and contract terms.</li>
<li>Communication Skills - Must be able to communicate effectively and in a concise manner, both verbally and in writing, to both internal and external audiences.</li>
<li>Process Management - Must be able to manage multiple tasks simultaneously, ensuring that all components of each task are properly presented, managed and reported. Identify and recommend opportunities for process improvement.</li>
<li>Problem Solving – Must be able to solve problems in a timely manner; gather and analyze information skillfully and efficiently; and work well in group problem solving situations.</li>
<li>Software Expertise - Proven skills in Excel, Microsoft Word and PowerPoint. Experience with Salesforce.com is a plus.</li>
<li>Presentation Skills- Able to demonstrate a clear ability to create and deliver presentations that succinctly demonstrate the essence of the material being presented.</li>
<li>Adaptability - Adapt to changes in the work environment; prioritize competing demands; changes approach or method to best fit the situation; able to deal with frequent change, delays, or unexpected events, appreciates opportunity to adjust and adapt with growing business.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Qualifications: Candidate must meet the following qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor of Arts or Science in Business, Finance, Real Estate or Hospitality from leading university</li>
<li>1-2 years of experience preferred</li>
</ul>
