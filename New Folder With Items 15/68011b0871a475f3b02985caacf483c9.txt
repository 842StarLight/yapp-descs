<div>What you will be doing<div>
</div>
<div>Under moderate supervision of the Finance Director and Manager, performs various financial analyses for Drug Company distribution center operations as well as generates revenue reports for the sales &amp; business unit functions. Provides financial reporting to management to ensure revenues and expenses are monitored and operational issues relative to finance are highlighted and addressed.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<li>Analyzes monthly business unit and functional financial statements and operating expenses.<br>
</li>
<div></div>
<br>
<li>Prepares and presents monthly financial analysis and reports to Senior Leadership.<br>
</li>
<div></div>
<br>
<li>Generates monthly forecast, annual budget, and long-range plan to share with Business Leads and partners.</li>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<li>Analyze customer and product trends to provide business partners with insight on changes within the business.</li>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<li>Create and maintain Excel financial models for monitoring business performance</li>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<li>Develops, coordinates and prepares business unit operating expenses for financial plans.<br>
</li>
<div></div>
<br>
<li>Assists sales management in generating reports for the purpose of acquiring new business, calculates customer profitability and tracks gross profit initiatives.<br>
</li>
<div></div>
<br>
<li>Develops and prepares productivity and efficiency analysis for business segments.</li>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<li>Interacts and partners with business segments teams to gather and compile data needed to conduct analyses, identify trends and/or address senior management requests.</li>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<li>Creates and presents training documentation for new associates and the broader Finance group as required.</li>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<li>Generates ad hoc analysis reports as needed.<br>
</li>
<div></div>
<br>
<li>Performs related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
