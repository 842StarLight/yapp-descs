<h2>Account Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>Our Account Directors are key members of the sales team at a respected Industrial Transformation focused research and advisory firm located in Kendall Square, Cambridge ,MA. The territory of this role is focused on 20 or fewer named accounts in one of our coverage areas IIoT Platform, Industrial Analytics, APM or Factory of the Future and driving deals to closure.
</p>
<p>The primary targets for our services are Industrial leaders and strategic industrial solution and service providers who are aligned to your territory. As a key member of the sales team, you will work with Sales Managers and Research team members to reach and exceed the territory quota, create detailed account plans including mapping and nurturing the right buyer personas. A core responsibility for our Account Directors is building and maintaining our advisory relationship clients such as SAP, Oracle, Rockwell Automation, Siemens, Whirlpool, Exxon, Coca-Cola, Cargill to name a few.</p>
<p>
We're looking for the right candidate who is skilled in closing consultative deals, enjoys working in a high growth environment with an uncapped opportunity to exceed quotas and share in the overall financial success of the company.
 </p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Directing strategy for large, global accounts including driving and coordinating executed selling and relationship activities</li>
<li>Detailed understanding of large, global account client business strategy, drivers, goals and initiatives and translating these into selling opportunities</li>
<li>Establish and maintain executive relationships with clients to become the trusted advisor.</li>
<li>Working with and coordinating activities with Sales Managers, , Client Success and Research with the intended outcome of increased customer satisfaction, increase in retention rates, &amp; account growth</li>
<li>High-level understanding of the trends impacting the industrial environment in your coverage areas of focus i.e.: IIoT, Digital Transformation, Industry 4.0 etc.</li>
<li>Quota responsibility in line with revenue projections for territory</li>
<li>Mastery and consistent execution of our sales methodology</li>
<li>Proficient in global account planning and understanding of territory management</li>
<li>Manage forecast accuracy on a monthly/quarterly/annual basis utilizing CRM (HubSpot)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven consultative/relationship-based sales abilities with experience in manufacturing technology (services, software or hardware), with evidence of prior success in sales</li>
<li>Ability to prospect and manage C-level and senior level relationships within large multi-national companies ($1BB+)</li>
<li>Strong demonstration of intellect, drive, executive presence, and sales acumen</li>
<li>Proven experience building excellent client relationships, offering value added, insightful and strategic insight into their business</li>
<li>Strong computer proficiency in MS Office products (PowerPoint), CRM, web conferencing services and efficient in email and internet applications</li>
<li>High attention to detail and excellent written and oral presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to develop and conduct effective virtual presentations with contract decision makers C-level).</li>
<li>Knowledge of the full life cycle of the sales process from prospecting to close</li>
<li>Positive attitude, high performance and high activity individual</li>
<li>Self-motivated, possess an excellent work ethic, and have a commitment to growth in a sales career</li>
<li>Winning, team-based attitude</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> 2+ years’ experience in Industrial Software Experience in the Industrial Manufacturing sector</li>
<li>Proven experience selling Industrial software solutions (Manufacturing Execution System, Asset Performance Management, Enterprise Quality Management, Industrial IoT solution etc.)</li>
<li>Successful track record of surpassing year quota in transactional sales environment</li>
<li>Proven ability to nurture and maintain long term strategic relationship</li>
<li>Average Deal Size $20-$60k</li>
<li>Local Candidates Strongly Preferred (no agencies)</li>
<li>Minimum of Bachelor’s degree with Master’s or advanced degree preferred</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and balance multiple projects</li>
<li>Up to 40% Domestic and/ or International Travel may be required</li>
<br>
</ul>
