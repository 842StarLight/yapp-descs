<p><b>Job Title</b>: Front Desk Customer Service Representative</p>
<p><b>
Reports to</b>: General Manager/Asst General Manager</p>
<p><b>
FLSA Status</b>: Non-Exempt</p>
<p><b>
Summary:</b> Promotes quality customer service and processes sales and registration transactions. Acts as the first point of contact for potential and current Goldfish customers, and is responsible for presenting a positive image for the company.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answers telephones, responds to inquiries, takes messages and screens and directs phone calls in a professional manner.</li>
<li>Processes student registrations using effective sales/customer service techniques.</li>
<li>Schedules and manages birthday/pool parties. Supervises private party staff.</li>
<li>Resolves customer concerns/complaints using a professional approach.</li>
<li>Assists members with purchases of merchandise and vending.</li>
<li>Greets parents and students as they report to the front desk.</li>
<li>Checks in students on the attendance tracking system.</li>
<li>Maintains cleanliness of the front desk area, Snack Shack area, changing areas, restrooms and observation area.</li>
<li>Monitors Snack Shack area and provides guidance to Snack Shack staff.</li>
<li>Checks voicemail and email correspondence and responds in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Updates informational displays with accurate and timely promotions and literature.</li>
<li>Provides occasional administrative support for management personnel, including the General Manager and Assistant General Manager.</li>
<li>Makes collection calls to resolve open account problems.</li>
<li>Prepares twice daily student lesson schedules for use by instructors and management staff.</li>
<li>Enforces safety rules and regulations to prevent accidents. Administers first aid when necessary.</li>
<li>Fulfills other duties and responsibilities as assigned by the Employer.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education/Experience:</b> High school diploma or GED required. Some college preferred. Two years previous customer service and/or administrative office experience required. Intermediate level computer skills required using Word, Excel and other software systems.</p>
<p><b>
Certifications and licenses</b>: CPR, First Aid and AED certification required.</p>
<p><b>
Work Environment:</b> While performing this job, the employee is exposed to heat and humidity occasionally. Noise level is usually moderate.</p>
