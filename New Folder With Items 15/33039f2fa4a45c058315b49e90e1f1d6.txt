<b>The Job</b><br>
The Senior Manager of Entertainment Sales Research will assist the Vice President and team in managing all custom research programs and initiatives on behalf of TBS and TNT ad sales<br>
This includes a qualitative/quantitative research focus in support of ad effectiveness, ad format innovation, audience profiling, and evaluation of shifting industry trends<br>
<b>The Day-to-Day</b><ul>
<li>
Duties and Responsibilities Leads project management on campaign measurement studies and ad effectiveness, including brand lift, attribution-based, and social/branded content research efforts
</li>
<li>Collaborates closely with Sales, Content Partnerships, and client teams to align measurement solution with client objectives and campaign composition</li>
<li>
Manages end-to-end process including research proposal, study design, vendor management, analysis, and presentation</li>
<li>
Works with Vice President to manage and advance Custom Team initiatives
</li>
<li>May manage Managers, Analysts and/or Senior Analysts to include their development, providing coaching and feedback, performance management and providing day to day oversight including directing, assigning and prioritizing work</li>
<li>
Applies working knowledge of the media industry to develop and test new metrics and concepts affecting the business</li>
<li>
Stays abreast of industry vendors and methodologies to maintain best in class research and measurement solutions<br>
</li>
</ul>
