<p>
DUTIES and RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides a high level interface between the IT Applications Development team and functional groups.</li>
<li>Actively participates in cross-functional team/task forces working closely with the user community applying an IT/Business perspective to identify and prioritize opportunities for improvement.</li>
<li>Assesses problems and solutions from a business perspective with a focus on what the customer really needs.</li>
<li>Supports and enhances existing systems, as well as developing new ones, defining business requirements, providing accurate estimates, executing configuration tasks, creating/executing system test plans and training plans, creating user documentation and implementing and supporting high quality IT solutions.</li>
<li>Liaises with and ensures that the IT Technical Development team provides cost effective, high quality solutions to meet the defined business requirements.</li>
<li>Provides accurate timeline estimates for proposed solutions and takes ownership of those estimates.</li>
<li>Addresses daily support issues with urgency and ensure that resolutions are meant to solve the root of the problem.</li>
<li>Actively participates in cross-functional team/task forces working closely with the user community applying an IT/Business perspective to identify and prioritize opportunities for improvement.</li>
<li>Fosters the MSC Culture in the department and throughout the company to ensure fulfillment of MSC’s vision and unity of purpose.</li>
<li>Participates in special projects and performs additional duties as required.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<p>EDUCATION and EXPERIENCE:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or the equivalent years of business experience is required.</li>
<li>Minimum of two years formal system development experience is required.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p>SKILLS:</p>
<ul>

<li>General business experience including sales, distribution and warehouse is preferred.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, oral and written communication and project management skills are required.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of MS Excel, Word, Project and PowerPoint is required.</li>
<li>Experience with packaged software, custom software, SaaS and other cloud solutions preferred.</li>
