The Role
<br><br>
The Solution Architect (SA) is a strategic position for our clients and within the Global Services organization. The SA is responsible for understanding client marketing, business, and technical requirements and architecting a solution to deliver against those requirements. SA resources are experienced client consultants within strong interpersonal skills and broad business, technical, and digital marketing industry knowledge.
<br><br>
A successful Solution Architect is someone who has excellent business consulting skills while driving to an efficient technical solution that meets both business and functional requirements of the client.
<br><br>
--------------------
<br>What You'll Do
<br>--------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for attaining client business; technical requirements and designing; delivering a comprehensive solution</li>
<li>Lead consultative engagements with clients on the technical and business challenges related to their digital marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Lead Design Workshops and Business Requirements Definition and Creation</li>
<li>Provide insight to solution design impacts to delivery and make recommendations for ease of delivery and support</li>
<li>Understand product capabilities/limitations and applies them to the solution design</li>
<li>Responsible for Solution Discovery, Analysis, Synthesis and Design</li>
<li>Present high-level overviews of Cheetah digital architecture and integration capabilities</li>
<li>Interacts with both technical and non-technical clients, attains relevant technical and business requirements, analyzes information and designs comprehensive solutions</li>
<li>Create, manage and present an actionable Business Requirements Document that aligns a client's business needs to the Cheetah Digital Solution</li>
<li>Conducts solutions presentations and obtains client acceptance of solution design</li>
<li>Identify project issues / risks and present alternatives to alleviate or resolve</li>
<li>Validates solution components and provide solution walkthrough with client\</li>
<li>Guides client through solution deployment</li>
<li>Managing multiple client engagements and work with associated team members</li>
<li>Responsible for the consulting and design for new client implementations, migrations and client projects</li>
<li>Support the Sales and Success teams with sales opportunities by validating solutions</li>
<li>Travels to client sites for onsite discovery, workshops and solutions presentations as required</li>
</ul>
----------------------------
<br>What We're Looking For
<br>----------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>University degree (Business, Computer Information Systems, or similar degree preferred) or equivalent experience with demonstrated proficiency</li>
<li>Experience documenting client problems and software solutions using BRDs\FSDs, wireframes, diagrams, workflows, and mockups.</li>
<li>Experience leading client facing discovery sessions to conceptualize and design complex business solutions</li>
<li>Excellent presentation and verbal skills with technical, non-technical, end-user, and executive audiences</li>
<li>Past experience developing SQL with a RDBMS such as MySQL, Oracle, or MS SQL Server</li>
<li>Familiarity with HTML, XML/XSL, CSS and JavaScript</li>
<li>Familiarity with SOAP APIs, REST APIs and integration techniques</li>
<li>Familiarity with SDLC methodologies including requirements, design, implementation, testing and maintenance</li>
<li>Understanding of sales process and methodologies</li>
<li>Experience in the digital marketing industry</li>
</ul>
