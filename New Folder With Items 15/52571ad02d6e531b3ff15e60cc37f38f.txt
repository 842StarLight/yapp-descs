<h2>Senior Member Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Senior Member Services Representative performs a wide variety of teller, administrative, branch operations and clerical duties. Duties include a high level of external and internal public contact and management of various records and file systems. Confidentiality is involved, and sensitive situations may be handled. This position is located in the HOPE branches in their various locations where volume and customer traffic warrants the presence of a Sr. Member Services Representative position.</p>
<p><b>
Primary </b><b>and Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish and maintain member accounts</li>
<li>
Open new accounts</li>
<li>
Advise new and existing members on HOPE products and services</li>
<li>
Prepare and process teller transactions efficiently and effectively</li>
<li>
Adhere to established cash handling and balancing procedures</li>
<li>
Prepare corporate deposits daily</li>
<li>
Perform a variety of administrative duties including but not limited to answering telephones, data entry, filing and assisting with loan applications and loan file processing</li>
<li>
Interact with community businesses and assist in developing outreach events</li>
<li>
May train and coach MSR staff as needed and approve teller transactions as appropriate</li>
<li>
Work under moderate to minimal supervision and has some latitude for decision-making</li>
<li>
Resolve most questions and problems from other MSRs through knowledge of established policies and refers only the most complex issues to higher levels</li>
<li>
Provide excellent customer service</li>
<li>
Adhere to all branch procedures</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Secondary Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Perform general administrative duties such as attending meetings, report production,</li>
<li>
Assist branch personnel with special projects as needed</li>
<li>
Manage and organize outreach activities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Competencies/Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to handle monetary instruments and make transactions accurately</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of word processor, spreadsheet, other PC applications, or mainframe applications to produce non-standard, creative products</li>
<li>
Ability to read and interpret documents, write routine reports and correspondence</li>
<li>
Ability to speak effectively before groups of customers and employees of the organization</li>
<li>
Ability to add, subtract, multiply and divide in all units measure, using whole numbers, common fractions, and decimals</li>
<li>
Ability to compute rate, ratio, percent, and interpret data</li>
<li>
Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out detailed but basic written or oral instructions</li>
<li>
Ability to deal with problems involving a few concrete variables in standardized</li>
<li>
Excellent customer service and communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to train and coach MSRs on teller and new account tasks</li>
<li>
Excellent knowledge of teller, new account, and credit union policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Strong sales skills for selling HOPE products and services</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Desired Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Associate's or Bachelor's degree or equivalent education and experience</li>
<li>
Three plus years MSR or teller experience in a credit union or bank</li>
<li>
Strong cash handling and customer service experience</li>
<li>
Consumer lending experience</li>
<li>
Fluency in English and Spanish preferred</li>
<li>
Previous sales experience</li>
</ul>
