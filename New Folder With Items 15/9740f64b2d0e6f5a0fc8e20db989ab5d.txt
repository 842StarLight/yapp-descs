<h2>Technical Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Are you equal parts problem solver, creative thinker, and technologist? Are you one of those people who seeks out a need, breaks down the solution into clear steps, and communicates the plan to all kinds of different groups?
<br><br>
We are looking for a Technical Product Manager to join the Trimble Cloud product management team. This position is a permanent position located at the Trimble Inc. office in Westminster, Colorado.
<br><br>
Our team takes a cloud-first approach to deliver shared web services that are used by Trimble and Trimble partners. Our platform-as-a-service is used by hundreds of application product teams to expedite their time to market. We focus on building services used by developers that are simple, scalable, and secure. You will represent the product and communicate and measure its value to Trimble and our customers.
<br><br>
Your products are APIs, and your customers are software developers. Our web services are the backbone of Trimble's application access and are used by application teams across Trimble's variety of business areas. Our data services are built with scale, security, and availability in mind. Your products are mission-critical to the success of Trimble's cloud strategy.
<br><br>
As a Technical Product Manager you’ll be a key part of the Trimble Product Management team responsible for integrating new ideas, technology, and customer requirements into our offerings. You will deliver and evolve innovative solutions that continue to drive Trimble’s business success.
<br><br>
Our Product Managers play a critical role in the organization - working with many parts of the business to ensure our products are top notch. You will be responsible for the product planning and execution throughout the product lifecycle, including:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Helping to define and evangelize the vision for Trimble's web services platform and build momentum around that vision with Trimble’s software product teams and business leadership</li>
<li>Developing a strong understanding of the target customers and their needs</li>
<li>Gathering, defining and prioritizing product development in a strategic roadmap that is supported by a well-researched business case</li>
<li>Working closely with engineering and your peers in Product Management to ensure value is delivered in a timely and cost-effective way</li>
</ul>
Role Objectives
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Own the business outcome and commercial success of your product</li>
<li>Be an expert in our market and solutions, and learn everything about the competitors, opportunities, and users</li>
<li>Validate market opportunity for problems, solutions, and ideas with direct user feedback using a variety of lean development and market research techniques</li>
<li>Act as the Agile product owner and communicate regularly with other key stakeholders in the business including Engineering, Client Success, Sales, Channel, and Marketing</li>
<li>Story writing, capacity planning, grooming, and planning throughout the discovery and development process</li>
<li>Work at both strategic and tactical levels by managing the entire product life cycle from strategic planning to tactical activities</li>
<li>Conduct market research, competitive analysis, product concept validation, and prototype testing</li>
<li>Work in a hands-on, fast-paced, creative, entrepreneurial environment, in a cross-functional capacity</li>
<li>Be customer-focused, and back your recommendations with strong supporting evidence</li>
</ul>
Skills &amp; Experience for Success
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>2-5 years of experience as a product manager or equivalent experience managing the process of defining and building software products or web services</li>
<li>Experience with APIs and web services technology including REST/JSON</li>
<li>Experience leading product discovery and software integrations</li>
<li>Experience working in platform-as-a-service or software-as-a-service businesses, B2C or B2B</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>Willingness to ask questions when you don't know</li>
</ul>
