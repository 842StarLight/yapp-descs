<b>Position Overview:</b><br>
In the Enterprise Sales Director role, you will drive net new business at scale for Amobee in specific vertical markets as our expert. Additionally, the Enterprise Sales Director will work with under penetrated accounts that have the opportunity to scale. You will effectively grow relationships at all levels, coordinate internal and external stakeholders during the life cycle of the deal, and be an Amobee ambassador and evangelist in the market. The right individual will have extensive experience selling complex enterprise level deals while bringing thought leadership to our company.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
