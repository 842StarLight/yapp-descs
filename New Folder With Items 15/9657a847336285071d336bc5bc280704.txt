<h2>Actuarial Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">At Ameritas, fulfilling life is what we do daily. We continuously strive to help our customers and employees enjoy life at its very best by reducing uncertainty, helping grow assets and protecting what is most cherished. We're here to help people put worry behind and the future ahead and help enable a life that's rich in family, happiness, health and financial security. When lives are fulfilled, our mission is fulfilled.
<br><br>
Ameritas Life Insurance Corp is seeking an Actuarial Assistant to drive the business by allowing you to work with a variety of existing and new products. In addition, this role will interact with wide range of associates within our various business lines as well as our shared services areas.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Job Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provides actuarial expertise to various areas of the company to assure high quality service.</li>
<li>Provide timely and accurate work products.</li>
<li>Review and implement business requirements.</li>
<li>Assist in developing and implementing procedures to comply with new regulatory, control or governance requirements.</li>
<li>Develop new procedures to improve on existing processes.</li>
<li>Work on special projects as directed by the managing actuary or the department head.</li>
<li>Document decisions made, processes put into place and other work performed.</li>
<li>Make progress toward attaining the Society of Actuaries' FSA designation through passing exams and/or completing modules.</li>
</ul>
<b>Potential Job Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Pricing support for products. Pricing includes analysis on a statutory, GAAP and value added basis. This may include the development of tools, and the review of advertising and sales material. Also, this may include support for the filing of products with state insurance departments.</li>
<li>Provide support for the company's financial plan as well as support for reconciling actual actuarial values to the plan.</li>
<li>Support customer service with technical matters or speaking directly with internal and external sales associates.</li>
<li>Analyze sales results to identify trends in business mix.</li>
<li>Assist in the analysis and review of new or revised product specifications to implement product changes in the modeling and/or valuation software. Includes identification of new data requirements for new product features.</li>
<li>Assist the managing actuary in responding to valuation and modeling-related questions and requests for information from internal product and risk-analysis actuaries, accountants, auditors, management and other associates, as well as external rating agencies, auditors and regulators.</li>
<li>Set up, test and maintain actuarial models for the specified actuarial purpose.</li>
<li>Support actuarial risk management project runs as requested.</li>
<li>Assist in producing company experience studies as requested.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Credit for a minimum of two of the following Society of Actuaries requirements needed to obtain the Associate of the Society of Actuaries designation: all 3 VEE Subjects, Exam P, Exam FM, Exam IFM, Exam LTAM, Exam STAM, Exam SRM, Exam PA, or the FAP Interim Assessment, and FAP Final Assessment.</li>
<li>Bachelor degree in Actuarial Science, Mathematics, Statistics or a related field.</li>
<li>Ability to accurately perform detailed calculations with a general understanding of how those calculations impact higher-level values and the company's financial statements.</li>
<li>Ability to clearly and thoroughly document assumptions, decisions, processes and other work performed.</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of insurance products and concepts, actuarial theory and techniques, and regulation of the insurance industry.</li>
<li>Understanding of advanced mathematical and statistical concepts.</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively both independently and as part of a team.</li>
<li>Strong analytical, problem solving, written and oral communication, and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Office.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and switch back and forth between projects as needed.</li>
<li>Willingness to learn new software tools and packages.</li>
<li>Willingness to work a flexible schedule as needed.</li>
</ul>
