<div><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>Responsible for preparing all event documentation and coordinates with Sales, property departments and customer to ensure consistent, high level service throughout pre-event, event and post-event phases of property events. This position primarily handles events of average complexity. Ensures their property events have a seamless turnover from sales to service back to sales. Recognizes opportunities to maximize revenue opportunities by up-selling and offering enhancements to create outstanding events.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>
CANDIDATE PROFILE
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education and Experience</b></div>
<ul><li>High school diploma or GED; experienced (1 – 2 years of experience) in the event management or related professional area.</li></ul>
<div>
OR</div>
<ul><li>2-year degree from an accredited university in Hotel and Restaurant Management, Hospitality, Business Administration, or related major; at least 1 year experience in the event management or related professional area required.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
CORE WORK ACTIVITIES</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Managing Event Logistics and Operations</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Ensures that events progress seamlessly by following established procedures, collaborating with other employees, and ensuring accuracy.</li>
<li>Greets customer during the event phase and hands-off to the Event Operations team for the execution of details.</li>
<li>Adheres to all standards, policies, and procedures.</li>
<li>Ensures billing accuracy and conducts bill reviews with the clients prior to processing the final bill.</li>
<li>Manages group room blocks and meeting space for average to large-sized assigned groups.</li>
<li>Identifies operational challenges associated with his/her group and determines how to best work with the property staff and customer to solve these challenges\ and/or develop alternative solutions.</li>
<li>Uses his/her judgment to integrate current trends in event management and event design.</li>
<li>Acts as liaison between field sales person and customer throughout the event process (pre-event, event, post-event).</li>
<li>Participates in customer site inspections and assists with the sales process as necessary.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned to meet business needs.
</li>
<li>Solicits feedback from the property departments to identify areas for improvement to enhance the Event Planner’s experience.</li>
</ul>
<div><b><br>
</b></div>
<div><b>Ensuring and Providing Exceptional Customer Service
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Delivers excellent customer service throughout the customer experience and encourages the same from other employees.</li>
<li>Empowers employees to provide excellent customer service.</li>
<li>Sets a positive example for guest relations.</li>
<li>Coordinates and communicates event details both verbally and in writing to the customer and property operations.</li>
<li>Makes presence known to customer at all times during this process.</li>
<li>Oversees his/her customer experiences from file turnover through the post event phase until turnover back to sales.</li>
<li>Follows up with customer post-event.</li>
<li>Responds to and handles guest problems and complaints.</li>
<li>Uses personal judgment and expertise to enhance the customer experience.</li>
<li>Stays available to solve problems and/or suggest alternatives to previous arrangements.</li>
<li>Works to continually improve customer service by integrating obtained feedback and personal judgment into action plans.</li>
<li>Emphasizes guest satisfaction during all departmental meetings and focuses on continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Interacts with guests to obtain feedback on product quality and service levels.</li>
<li>Ensures hourly employees understand expectations and parameters for event activities.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Leading Event Management Teams</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Conducts formal pre- and post-event meetings as required to review/communicate group needs and feedback.</li>
<li>Leads formal pre-event and post-event meetings for average to large-sized assigned groups.</li>
<li>Facilitates various meetings as he/she perceives necessary (Banquet Event Order meeting, block review, etc).</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Supporting and Coordinating with the Sales and Marketing Function</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assists in the sales process and revenue forecasting for customer groups.</li>
<li>Up-sells products and services throughout the event process.</li>
<li>Forecasts group sleeping rooms and event revenue (catering and audio visual) for his/her groups.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Conducting Human Resources Activities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Reviews comment cards and guest satisfaction results with employees.</li>
<li>Observes service behaviors of employees and provides feedback to individuals and/or managers.</li>
<li>Assists in the development and implementation of corrective action plans.</li>
<li>Take initiative to use his/her experience to improve service performance according to his/her evaluation of the issue and resolution.</li>
<li>Works with the property staff and customers to address operational challenges associated with his/her group.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned to meet business needs.
</li>
</ul>
