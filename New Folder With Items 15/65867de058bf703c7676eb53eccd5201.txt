<h2>Pharmacy Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Oklahoma Arthritis Center is proud to have been voted "Top Work Place" by our employees for 6 years!</b></p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<p>
§ Accurately prepares medication orders for dispensing by the pharmacist by retrieving, counting, pouring, weighing, labeling and mixing medications.</p>
<p>
§ Coordinates billing and obtains cost approval.</p>
<p>
§ Enters patient and medication order information into the pharmacy system.</p>
<p>
§ Processes and submits insurance claims.</p>
<p>
§ Assists patients and prescribers with the Prior Authorization process.</p>
<p>
§ Provides quality customer service to patients and other healthcare providers.</p>
<p>
§ Processes new and refill prescriptions in a timely manner.</p>
<p>
§ Promotes patients' drug adherence.</p>
<p>
§ Coordinates responses and resolutions with appropriate internal and external parties.</p>
<p>
§ Performs all tasks in a safe manner that is consistent with APS policies and as well as state and federal laws.</p>
<p>
§ Assists the pharmacist with inventory of drugs.</p>
<p>
§ Ensures security of pharmacy and medications.</p>
<p>
§ Verifies the information on the prescription is complete and accurate before submitting claim to insurance.</p>
<p>
§ Receives written prescription or refill requests and verify that information is complete and accurate.</p>
<p>
§ Maintains proper storage and security conditions for drugs.</p>
<p>
§ Answers telephones in a timely manner, responding to questions or requests.</p>
<p>
§ Oversees inventory and cleanliness of Over the Counter medications and supplies for sale, including snacks and drinks.</p>
<p>
§ Assists customers by answering simple questions, locating items or referring them to the pharmacist for medication information.</p>
<p>
§ Assists pharmacist with monitoring and increasing patient compliance.</p>
<p>
§ Operates cash registers to accept payment from customers.</p>
<p>
§ Files prescription hard copies that have been filled.</p>
<p>
§ Cleans, and helps maintain, equipment and work areas, according to prescribed methods.</p>
<p>
§ Establishes and maintains patient profiles, including lists of medications taken by individual patients.</p>
<p>
§ Receives and stores incoming supplies, verifies quantities against invoices, and informs supervisors of stock needs and shortages.</p>

<p><b>Performance Requirements:</b></p>
<p><b>
 </b><i>Knowledge:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of Oklahoma State Pharmacy Board rules and regulations as it pertains to Arthritis Pharmacy Solutions.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of brand/generic conversions and proficient in pharmacy math calculating dose or day supply.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Pharmacy chain of command and workflow.</li>
</ul> <p><i>Skills:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Skill in customer service.</li>
<li>Skill in effectively interacting with patients, visitors, providers, administration and all staff members.
</li>
<li>Skill in written and verbal communication.</li>
<li>Skill in computer programs including Microsoft Office suite including Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Word and Microsoft Outlook, Pharmacy Prescription management software, and point of sales software.
 </li>
</ul>
<p><i>Abilities:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to assume responsibility and exercise authority over assigned work functions.
</li>
<li>Ability to recognize, evaluate, solve problems, and correct errors.
</li>
<li>Ability to establish priorities and coordinate work activities.
 </li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements (Education, Experience):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Current Pharmacy Technician certification and license</li>
<li>2+ years experience as Pharmacy Technician</li>
<li>Associates degree required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
§ Ability to work effectively in a fast paced environment. Physical ability to sit, perform data entry and view computer screen for long periods at a time.</p>
<p>
§ Ability to lift and carry moderate loads up to 40 lbs.
</p>
<p>§ Involves walking or standing approximately 90 percent of the day, sitting the remainder.</p>
<p><b>
Scheduled Working Hours:</b></p>
<p>
§ Full time, 40+ hours per week.</p>
<p>
§ Open hours will be 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Monday through Thursday, and 8 a.m. to 12 p.m. on Friday, but hours may vary depending on the needs of the pharmacy.</p>
