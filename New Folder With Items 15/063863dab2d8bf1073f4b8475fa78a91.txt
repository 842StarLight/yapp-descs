<h2>Managing Director - Southeast, USA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>It’s been a high velocity year expanding our team from around 80 people in our original Prague, Amsterdam and London offices to nearly 300 employees globally. With our recent $33 million Series B funding round led by Silicon Valley based VC Battery Ventures, we have ambitious plans to add regional teams in NYC, LA and Miami over the coming months. And that’s just the start...</p>
<p>
Mews makes future-proof technology that's shaking up the hospitality industry, and we’re looking for a Managing Director to lead the hospitality tech revolution in the US-Southeast region, as well as the further diversification and growth of our business.</p>
<p><b>
Your Mission:</b></p>
<p>
Hiring, nurturing and leading a high performing, cross functional team that will ultimately be based in the Miami area.</p>
<p>
Building a plan that capitalises on opportunities in the Southeast region and makes the best use of your team’s talents and resources.</p>
<p>
Creating a strong culture within the Southeast office – championing the Mews values of ambition, agility, grit, playfulness and kindness.<br>
</p>
<p>
Working across the business to make things happen – forming strong relationships with Finance, Marketing, Commercial and HR to ensure your team are set up for success.</p>
<p>
Providing clarity on key objectives and the direction of the organisation, ensuring this translates into the day to day activity of your teams.</p>
<p>
Analysing data to further inform your strategy and contribute to the organisations success.</p>
<p>
Taking ownership of the regional P&amp;L and budget, using your financial acumen to make sound decisions on the management of these.</p>
<p>
Acting as a true Mews ambassador at various events and meetings with clients when needed.</p>
<p>
Focusing on growth opportunities for the business both within your region and the wider business.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What you can expect:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Autonomy, with the opportunity to lead the effective set-up and day to day operations of the Southeast region</li>
<li>An environment that encourages diversity of thought, new ideas and innovation</li>
<li>The opportunity to be part of a significant period of growth in the organisation, and the opportunity to work on rewarding projects outside of your role.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience within a high growth SaaS organisation</li>
<li>Significant leadership experience of cross-functional teams</li>
<li>Understanding of SaaS sales models/cycles and a client focused mind-set</li>
<li>Experience of managing and leading others through change</li>
<li>High energy with an entrepreneurial mind-set</li>
<li>The ability to operate within an ever-changing business</li>
<li>Proven experience of managing financials – ownership of P&amp;L, budgets and making financial decisions with a commercial approach</li>
<li>Self-awareness and a strong focus on continuous learning for yourself and your team</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Open, collaborative and innovative culture</li>
<li>Opportunities for learning, development and advancement</li>
<li>Competitive compensation package</li>
<li>Mews library</li>
<li>Mews travel program</li>
</ul>
