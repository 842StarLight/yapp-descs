<h2>EDI Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Summary</b><br>
An EDI Analyst supports stakeholders such as Operations, Sales, IT, Accounting and external business partners. Responsible for monitoring and managing EDI file exchange processes. This is not a remote role. The EDI Analyst must live in the Tampa Bay area.<br>
<br>
<b>Summary of Essential Job Functions</b>
<ul>
<li>Support various standard EDI transactions</li>
<li>Ensure interfaces are performing as designed</li>
<li>Collaborate with payer, clearinghouses and/or trading partners to successfully maintain the EDI processes</li>
<li>Ensuring data integrity</li>
<li>Solve EDI incidents/problems and actively managing and communicating issues</li>
<li>Manage the resolution process as needed</li>
<li>Exchange information and share knowledge with the team to maximize understanding and the effectiveness</li>
<li>Escalate prolonged EDI issues to Information Technology management</li>
<li>Work proactively to implement new EDI partners and file exchanges by working with clients, client third party administrators and other external entities</li>
<li>Maintain excellent relationships with 3rd party vendors and clients</li>
<li>Additional duties and responsibilities as required and/or assigned</li>
</ul>
