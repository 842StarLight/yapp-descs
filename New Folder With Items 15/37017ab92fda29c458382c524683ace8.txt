<div><b>
Overview
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Criteo is in search of an organized, self-motivated Senior Account Strategist to help grow our CPG Vertical on the Large Customer Account team for the Sponsored Products platform. You will be responsible for maintaining trusted advisor status for large CPG brands and agencies in order to maintain and grow a dedicated book of business. The Large Customer Account team is responsible for our largest and most sophisticated advertisers in the retail vertical.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What will you be doing?
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Provide best in class account management to Criteo’s Large Customer advertisers
</li>
<li>Grow revenue and optimize accounts to improve performance for the customer
</li>
<li>Responsible for revenue, pitching and up-selling the accounts residing in your book of business
</li>
<li>Regular client meetings at different levels in the advertiser’s organization
</li>
<li>Define, implement and monitor both strategic and tactical plans for your clients
</li>
<li>Constant monitoring and analysis of retailer’s campaign performance
</li>
<li>Define a short, medium and long term business plan with the client
</li>
<li>Manage and empower specialist support teams to achieve these objectives</li>
<li>
Identify and suggest new processes that can be implemented to improve Criteo’s service
</li>
<li>Encourage feedback from customers to enhance Criteo’s products and services
</li>
<li>Track and maintain contact, event and deal information in Salesforce CRM</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What do we NEED?
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Must have at least 4-6 years of experience in online performance media (search preferred).</li>
<li>
Good knowledge of display, brands, sponsored/featured products, and full- funnel initiatives preferred.</li>
<li>
Management experience is a plus.</li>
<li>
Proven experience in direct client relationships. Experience working with retail clients is a plus.</li>
<li>
Negotiation, influencing skills and an ability to persuade at all levels of an agency or client</li>
<li>
Excellent knowledge of the local online, retail and e-commerce market
</li>
<li>Strong track record of success in offering outstanding customer service and innovation leading to sustained revenue growth and customer retention
</li>
<li>Ability to assess and resolve advertiser issues
</li>
<li>Degree qualification
</li>
<li>Ability to deliver in a time-sensitive environment
</li>
<li>Passionate about online and technology</li>
<li>
Strong project management and organizational skills</li>
</ul></div>
