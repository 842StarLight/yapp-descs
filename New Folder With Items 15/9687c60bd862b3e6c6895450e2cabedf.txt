<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p><br>
</p>
<p>Primary responsibility is to quickly resolve restaurant property issues, including determining maintenance responsibility, security and liability concerns, landlord and tenant property maintenance violations and disruptions to restaurant operations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your Key Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Review and analysis of lease/contract obligations and real estate title work.</p></li>
<li><p>
Research government records pertaining to property ownership, and regulations and ordinances pertaining to property maintenance and restaurant operation.</p></li>
<li><p>
Detailed review and analysis of site plans and surveys.</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage notices and violations from landlords, governmental agencies, coordinate resolution and response before deadlines and negotiate reduction of any fines and penalties.</p></li>
<li><p>
Work with landlords and third parties to ensure they perform their maintenance responsibilities.</p></li>
<li><p>
Preparation of correspondence, notices, summaries, and various form legal documents and contracts.</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintaining logs and database records, including tracking and monitoring issues and critical deadlines.</p></li>
<li><p>
Preparation of miscellaneous reports primarily using Excel and Lease Administration Database.</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain a positive public image of Brinker International and foster a continued positive rapport with landlords, property management companies, developers, other tenants, trade groups and public entities.</p></li>
<li><p>
Collaborate and coordinate with various restaurant management teams, including facility managers, property development, construction and design teams, risk management and property accounting to jointly ensure properties are properly maintained or timely repaired, costs property allocated to third parties, violations resolved and liabilities mitigated.
</p></li>
</ul>
