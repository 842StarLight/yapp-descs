<h2>Director, eCommerce - Bell + Giro</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Join our talented team. Employees at Vista Outdoor are passionate and committed to delivering quality products to our customers. Our culture centers on an engaged and accountable workforce. Our goal is to attract and retain a diverse workforce: rich in talent, background, ideas and experience.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Qualifications
</b></p>
<p>Each company at Vista Outdoor has its own unique DNA. We are united by a dedication to superior quality, performance-driven products and a love of all things outdoors. Bell exists to inspire &amp; enable the next generation of Boundary Breakers; Giro focuses on making gear for our customers that helps them get faster, bigger and better, sweating the details. With these unique perspectives, the Director of e-Commerce is responsible for the vision, strategy, execution and performance of the e-Commerce business these websites: Bell Helmets: https://www.bellhelmets.com/ Giro: https://www.giro.com/en/
</p>
<p></p>
<p>In this role, the person will lead the effort to move the brands (Bell, Giro and more) from a small overall contribution in revenue to double digits percentage. The person will work closely with the marketing, brand, merchandising, IT and Vista Outdoor digital marketing and eCommerce headquarters function to create a friction-less online shopping experience. The Director is also responsible for leading the digital marketing and eCommerce team to ensure successful integration and program development is coordinated into the site for deeper consumer engagement and conversion. The candidate must be creative, collaborative and consultative, with a strong ability to work well across the organization both within the marketing department and across the enterprise. The Director must be focused on profitable eCommerce sales. The Director must have a proven track record in managing the full P&amp;L of a successful eCommerce business with a rapid growth trajectory as well as building and growing a digital performance marketing team.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Own and grow the online P&amp;L for Bell + Giro
</li>
<li>Develop, communicate and champion the guiding vision for the eCommerce sales channel at all levels of the organization.
</li>
<li>Manage in-house team and external vendors in order to achieve sales plan and brand goals.
</li>
<li>Direct the day-to-day online operations relating to eCommerce with oversight responsibility for operating metrics and key performance drivers such as: traffic, average order value (AOV), conversion, opt-ins, new buyers, repeat buyers, sales, and units per order.
</li>
<li>Must work closely and collaboratively with Sales Team and planning group to set and forecast sales goals to be measured by marketing performance and planning goals for the eCommerce team.
</li>
<li>Build and manage the online assortment, promotions, price management and overall product presentation.
</li>
<li>Create and manage best of breed branded online retail consumer experiences.
</li>
<li>Ensure products are communicated effectively online to maximize item sales and increase profitability.
</li>
<li>Lead and manage a high performing performance online sales/marketing team with a strong sense for considering customer needs, incorporating company goals, and creating an experience that inspires usage and motivates completion of the online purchasing process.
</li>
<li>Partner with marketing categories to enhance product stories, increase relevancy regarding product efficacy and consumer target.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record of 5-plus years in eCommerce and online, direct and database marketing including at least five years in eCommerce.
</li>

</li>

</li>
<li>Salesforce Commerce Cloud

<li>Salesforce Marketing Cloud

</li>

</li>
<li>Strong skills in strategy and planning, budget and P&amp;L management, merchandising and marketing, and vendor selection and management.
</li>
<li>Proven leadership and team management skills derived from having direct responsibility for building and managing a cohesive, high-performing online eCommerce team.
</li>
<li>Ability to identify, communicate and inspire an organization around the next generation of eCommerce experience to dramatically grow the business, and be able to work through tough issues and overcome challenges while maintaining good relationships.
</li>
<li>Candidate must have keen business acumen plus the ability to function as a senior strategist with key members of the executive management team.
</li>

As a result, it will be critical for this new executive to understand intimately Bell, Giro and Blackburn Design methodologies and priorities in order to develop the company’s overall digital strategy. To be successful the Director of eCommerce will demonstrate the following:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Prove to be a quick study and an individual of high intellectual horsepower who can assess the growth opportunity for Bell, Giro and Blackburn Design through its people, products and processes
</li>
<li>Translate this into an operating plan that is clearly communicated and directly executed. Key to the success of both the role and the business unit.
</li>
<li>The Director of eCommerce will ensure that this plan is supported and championed by key constituents in the larger corporate structure.
</li>
<li>An ability to attract online the relevant consumer segments and ensure revenue per user is constantly increasing through the effective use of products, data analysis and technology.
</li>
<li>An ability to maintain openness for experimentation with new ideas that can yield breakthrough performance.
