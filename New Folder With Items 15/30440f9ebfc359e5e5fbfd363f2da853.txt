<h2>Solutions Architect - II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Description
<br><br>
Serve as a Solutions Architect for the C-HIT COO assisting in development and coordination of the C-HIT Solution Programs.?
<br><br>
Proactively seek, develop, and recommend methods and techniques that will improve the process of delivering solutions to C-HIT customers.?
<br><br>
Prepare and deliver training, PowerPoint presentations, and technical briefings to C-HIT customers and program delivery staff.?
<br><br>
Participate in industry conferences, professional associations, and product demonstrations.?
<br><br>
Exhibit exceptional communication skills (verbal and written), document complex problems and solutions in a clear and concise manner.?
<br><br>
Attend/participate in all Company activities as assigned.
<br><br>
Required Skills
<br><br>
C++Java/J2EE, including SOAP, RESTful, .NET and related technologies (JSON, XML, etc), Common frameworks such as struts, spring, hibernate, etc.
<br><br>
Database technologies (Oracle, MySQL, JDBC, SQL, PL/SQL, stored procedures), Big Data (Hadoop), Marklogic
<br><br>
Cloud (Amazon AWS, Salesforce.com, Azure, etc)
<br><br>
Agile (Scrum), Waterfall project methodology and practices
<br><br>
CRM software (Siebel, Clarify, Vantive, Peoplesoft, NetSuite, etc.)
<br><br>
Required Experience
<br><br>
College degree or equivalent work experience.?
<br><br>
Must have 15 years demonstrable IT experience in the US Federal space.?
<br><br>
Must have practical hands on experience in the design and implementation of complex solutions in a pre-sales and post-sales support role.?
<br><br>
Experience with assisting the Business Development team in identifying and qualifying potential government opportunities and participating in RFI, RFQ, and RFP responses, providing technical solutions, recommendations, and feedback.?
<br><br>
Experience analyzing, defining, developing and articulating solutions that address customer requirements.?
<br><br>
Must have excellent project management, technical, communication, and writing skills.</div>
