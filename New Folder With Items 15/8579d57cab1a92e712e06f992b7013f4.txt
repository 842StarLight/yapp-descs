<h2>Construction Project Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>DPI Solar is a local family business with a culture of growth, integrity, and community. We offer a dynamic range of solar installations from ground-mounted systems, to variable roof type installations, and battery / off-grid systems in the greater Portland and surrounding areas.<br>
<br>
Project Coordinator for DPI Solar - Responsible for the coordination and completion of all residential and some commercial project related work outlined in customized CRM. The Project Coordinator must have the ability to work both independently and as a member of a team. The Project Coordinator will work directly with customers, sales consultants, installers, engineers, permitting offices and inspectors.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Job Duties/Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Apply for and obtain all necessary permits for solar installations</li>
<li>Assist with and manage the flow of construction through pre-planning phase to final punch list items</li>
<li>Follow up to ensure tasks and requests are completed according to CRM</li>
<li>Coordinate project activities, resources, and information</li>
<li>Ensure projects move forward in a timely, cost-effective manner, making adjustments as necessary</li>
<li>Set expectations and timelines for customers as well as installers</li>
<li>Liaise with subcontractors to identify and define requirements, scope and objectives as needed</li>
<li>Coordinate with customers to schedule and oversee pre-construction site audit with DPI crew</li>
<li>Coordinate with customers and vendors to schedule parts deliveries and installations</li>
<li>Ensure that customer needs are met as projects evolve</li>
<li>Monitor project progress and handle any issues that arise</li>
<li>Act as the point of contact to communicate project status to the appropriate personnel &amp; departments</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p><b>Qualification Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Proven work experience in construction</b></li>
<li><b>Proven experience as a Project Manager or similar role</b></li>
<li><b>Highly Organized Personality with the ability to multi-task</b></li>
<li>Effective verbal, listening and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and manage time well</li>
<li>Demonstrates a sense of urgency and ability to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to work independently or as a team member</li>
<li>Strong client-facing and teamwork skills</li>
<li>An ability to prepare and interpret flow charts, schedules, and step-by-step action plans</li>
<li>Familiarity with risk management and quality assurance &amp; control</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with CRM software and MS Office</li>
<li>Familiarity with Adobe PDF’s to read and manipulate.</li>
<li>Familiarity and experience reading schematics</li>
<li>Valid Drivers’ License in good standing, issued by the resident state</li>
</ul>
