<h2>Software Account Manager. No Cold Calling.</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Siber Systems is looking for entry and mid-level Account Managers to join our growing team. No cold calling. Our sales force deals with inbound leads and we need motivated, communicative, hardworking, and positive team players to turn those leads into happy customers.</p>
<p>Who We Are:</p>
<p>Siber Systems is a privately-held company, headquartered in Fairfax, Virginia. Our flagship products, RoboForm and GoodSync, are used by millions of people worldwide. Our customers range from individual users and small businesses, to government agencies and leading Fortune 500 companies.</p>
<p><b>Account Manager Key Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Follow-up on and convert inbound leads into happy customers.</li>
<li>Proactively assist and expand existing accounts</li>
<li>Drive renewals of existing accounts with annual plans</li>
<li>Expand existing accounts by developing opportunities to expand usage.</li>
<li>Earn customer trust and loyalty by understanding their needs and answering questions, demonstrating the product, and recommending best practices.</li>
<li>Learn, implement, and follow structured sales and account management processes.</li>
<li>Achieve performance metrics and goals set by management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications for Account Executive: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>0 - 3 years of work experience in B2B software sales</li>
<li>Experience with CRM solutions is a plus</li>
<li>Four-year college degree is a plus</li>
<li>Excellent phone and email communication skills</li>
<li>Integrity, energy, and genuine desire to understand and solve customer pain points</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we Offer: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Base salary per your experience plus performance-based compensation (total $40,000 to $60,000).</li>
<li>Health Benefits</li>
<li>15 days paid time off and Holidays schedule</li>
<li>A culture that values creativity, integrity, and passion for delivering an awesome customer experience</li>
<li>Fun and vibrant office with outdoor patio</li>
<li>Unlimited coffee, tea, and snacks</li>
</ul>
