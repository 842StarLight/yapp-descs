<h2>NCR Level 3 Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h4>
<div>
<div><b>Overview:</b></div>
<div>
This position is a top tier support and professional services opportunity. It will entail leading and teaching L1 and L2 support personnel, working with customers on supporting existing software, hardware, management of projects, upgrades. It also will involve creative thinking to help solve issues and provide solutions to opportunities for customers, both internal and external. This will be a customer facing solution requiring some travel and creative work schedules. Ability to work from an office or home are available.</div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Participates as an active engaged member of the NCR L3 team.</li>
<li>Works at escalation point for L1 and L2 support teams.</li>
<li>Coordinates and designs solutions for customer issues</li>
<li>Works with internal teams to streamline and improve support processes</li>
<li>Coordinates, designs, and implements software upgrades for customers.</li>
<li>Consults with customers to provide solutions and offer expertise and advice</li>
<li>Contributes to strategic direction for teams</li>
<li>Independently resolves highly complex technical issues within a given technical area</li>
<li>Works with development, sales, and other teams to draft and manage statements of works for customers</li>
<li>Communicates with customers and internal teams on ongoing projects, solutions, or issues</li>
<li>Assumes ownership of escalations from lower support levels and sees them through to resolution, providing documentation of fixes for potential reoccurrences</li>
<li>Proactively searches for issues and provides solutions to prevent problems from occurring in area of responsibility</li>
<li>Understands and help to access risk to systems and solutions.</li>
<li>Provides mentoring and guidance to other technicians or developers</li>
<li>Assigns work to others. Technical leadership of assigned project</li>
<li>Provide time / resource estimates for assigned projects</li>
<li>Works with internal and external project managers</li>
<li>Leads internal and external meetings and solution demos</li>
<li>Documents solutions, upgrades, enhancements.</li>
<li>Manages own time and productivity</li>
<li>Handling of sensitive data such as electronic payments information or health information</li>
<li>Provides remote and onsite support for installations, upgrades, special projects, and other areas as needed.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Knowledge and Skills Required:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong experience with infrastructure environments (e.g. operating system, hardware, security, network, SQL databases)</li>
<li>Experience with Windows operating systems</li>
<li>Neat and professional appearance in both personal hygiene and dress required</li>
<li>Ability to converse and interact with business leaders from the beginner IT levels to C-Suite level</li>
<li>Experience with business Point of Sale and Back Office applications desired</li>
<li>Experience in scripting and understanding of programming languages</li>
<li>Strong experience with implementation, problem analysis and solutioning of infrastructure and business application environments</li>
<li>Strong problem solving, priority setting, and collaboration skills</li>
<li>Good organizational and logical troubleshooting skills</li>
<li>Possess creative ability, consulting skills, leadership qualities, credibility and self confidence</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively in a geographically dispersed team</li>
<li>Demonstrated technical leadership skills</li>
<li>Ability to translate business needs into technical requirements and solutions</li>
<li>Proficiency in software such as Microsoft Excel and proficiency with the Microsoft Office suite, to include Word and PowerPoint</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Education and Experience Required:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree or equivalent experience in Management Information Systems / Computer Science and/or a minimum of 3 years of related experience in POS support environment</li>
<li>IT project management experience using various Microsoft tools</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience in managing information technology services</li>
</ul>
