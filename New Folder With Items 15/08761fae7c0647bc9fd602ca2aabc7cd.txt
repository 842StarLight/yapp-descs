<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>The Quality Control Technician is responsible for the inspection and minor repair of 2nd day out shipped pieces. These include, but are not limited to: 2nd attempt priority pieces, top delivery problem pieces, exchange pieces, and pieces with visually damaged packaging received at the depot prior to the day of delivery.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Inspect and touch up merchandise prior to delivery.
</li>
<li>Repair any <b>minor cosmetic </b>issues discovered during the inspection.
</li>
<li>Report significant damage
</li>
<li>Track all inspected and repaired pieces.
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently
</li>
<li>Strong eye for detail
</li>
<li>Good verbal and written skills
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Minimum of a High School Diploma or equivalent.
</b></li>
<li>Experience fixing minor damage on case goods and upholstery furniture preferred.
</li>
<li>Experience working in a cross-dock warehouse preferred.
</li>
<li>Experience delivering merchandise in-home to customers is preferred.
</li>
</ul>
