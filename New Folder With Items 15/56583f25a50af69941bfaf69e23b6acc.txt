<h2>Purchasing Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Purpose </b></p>
<p>The Frozen Purchasing Assistant provides essential support to the Frozen Purchasing Manager and vital information to the sales team.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions and Basic Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate with overseas suppliers &amp; HFC Asia Production Team</li>
<li>Prepare purchase orders</li>
<li>Review and verify shipping documentation</li>
<li>Evaluate overseas inventory and make purchasing decisions accordingly</li>
<li>Labeling/packaging of products</li>
<li>Product specification</li>
<li>Import/export Federal requirements</li>
<li>Private labeling projects for key customers</li>
<li>New product development</li>
<li>Work with customs brokers, truckers, cold storage facilities, and shipping lines. Provide necessary documents to each as requested</li>
<li>Set up private lab testing for shipments</li>
<li>Updates manager and sales on status of shipments, current and incoming inventory, pricing, cost of goods, new products, landed costs, availability, packaging and samples</li>
<li>Follow up on samples sent to sales team and customers</li>
<li>Work with our Philippine office personnel to verify receiving of imported products.</li>
<li>Maintain inventory and on order reports</li>
<li>Work with Controller/Accounting Dept. on payments to suppliers.</li>
<li>Approve invoices/billings</li>
<li>Other duties as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualification Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate’s degree</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of a computer, Microsoft Office and other required software.</li>
<li>Familiar with and ability to use general office equipment such as copy machines, scanners, fax machines, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Marketing or Communications</li>
<li>Previous purchasing experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions</b></p>
<p>Normal office conditions</p>
<p><b>Work Hours</b></p>
<p>Monday through Friday, plus one Saturday per month, 45 hours/week.</p>
<p>Must also be able to occasionally work irregular or longer hours as needed.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Purchasing: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Associate (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Location:</p>
<ul><li>Hilo, HI (Preferred)</li></ul>
