With more than $2 billion in revenues, CDK Global is a leading global provider of integrated information technology and digital marketing solutions to the automotive retail and adjacent industries. <div>
The Digital Marketing Consultant leads the education and implementation of website and directory listing optimization for engagement marketing techniques, SEO and Paid Search strategies, Social Media strategies, Lead Management processes, Analytics/Reporting and Digital retailing with OEM- Automotive Retail Dealers and Wholesale Personnel through proven best practices and improvement plans. They also educate dealers and field personnel on the use of necessary reporting tools. Known as an OEM Digital SME, this role strives to achieve program and aligned Regional goals, maintaining assigned Regional, Zone and dealership relationships. Analyzes current Digital Marketing state, including Google Analytics, and consults with Zone Teams and dealership personnel regarding strategy, tactics, roles and responsibilities.</div>

Responsibilities:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Maintains thought leadership of Interactive Marketing and sales industry trends by staying current via continuous learning, attending conferences and engaging with Industry Associations portals.</li>
<li>
Grows strong OEM relationships with Corporate and assigned Zone personnel, managing to ensure positive feedback from dealers, Regional and Zone Management/travelers, developing trust and value.</li>
<li>
Establishes themselves as the Digital SME to Area/Regional personnel and Dealership executive management, driving acceptance of Digital Marketing and Lead Management best practices within assigned Regions, Areas, Districts and dealerships.</li>
<li>
Provide training on CDK products, program specifics and associated OEM Digital Marketing products to Dealers, Zones, Areas, Regions and peers.</li>
<li>
Travel with new DMCs and OEM DMS's to train and provide program features and benefits understanding.</li>
<li>
Become familiar with OEM Digital Marketing related programs to be able to confidently review the benefits and features for dealership management assessment, providing feedback for program improvements.</li>
<li>
Communicate pro-actively with Region and dealers on program progress through e-mail, phone and in-store visits providing feedback and recommendations relative to product performance.</li>
<li>
Regularly attends Area/Regional dealer and field staff meetings, delivering presentations at OEM sponsored meetings and seminars, as requested, utilizing effective presentation and facilitation skills.</li>
<li>
Provide reports as directed by Program Manager, submitting internal reports, expenses, and timecard entries prior to deadlines, accountable for accurate and timely record keeping and data delivery for all dealers and initiatives within assigned Region.</li>
<li>
Consistently meets all assigned program objectives.</li>
