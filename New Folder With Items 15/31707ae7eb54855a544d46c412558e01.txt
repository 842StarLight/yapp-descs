<h2>Client Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>For over 30 years, Rewards Network has helped restaurants increase their revenue, traffic, and customer engagement through our innovative financial and marketing services and premier dining rewards programs. Our 6 million members include frequent flyers, corporate cardholders, and a pool of highly motivated diners who spend hundreds of millions of dollars annually at participating restaurants.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
The <b>Client Success Manager</b> is responsible for reviewing client accounts, maintaining client relationships, responding to questions and resolving issues. The individual in this role will ensure that client needs are addressed quickly with responses delivered in a timely manner. He/she will communicate with various departments to find resolutions to more challenging questions or issues that may arise. This individual will be responsible for client accounts and assist the Sales team with any questions they may have relating to merchant accounts, policies and documents.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Manage clients throughout their lifecycle and encourage them to take full advantage of product benefits to ensure the value is recognized</li>
<li> Collaborate with Sales to deepen client relationships and identify and resolve client needs, as well as new product opportunities</li>
<li> Handle calls and emails from clients and members of our remote sales team.</li>
<li> Analyze client account issues, troubleshoot and negotiate resolutions in a timely manner</li>
<li> Assist client accounts by explaining and clarifying billing statements, processes and terms and conditions of their agreement with Rewards Network.</li>
<li> Serves as subject matter expert supporting the sales team on policies and procedures related to contracts, the deletion policy, and related issues. Communicates, explains, and ensures enforcement of these policies and procedures.</li>
<li> Processes account changes including any adjustments or requests including but not limited to banking change, reimbursement, email address change, etc.</li>
<li> Performs related job duties as needed and required.</li>
</ul>
