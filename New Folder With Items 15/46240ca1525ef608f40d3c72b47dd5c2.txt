<b>About the Opportunity</b><p></p>
<p>
A services company in New Jersey is actively seeking an experienced professional to join their team as their new Senior IT Buyer. In this role, the Senior IT Buyer will be responsible for conducting vendor research and providing information and guidance to internal/external business partners regarding supplier selection. Apply today!</p>
<p></p>
<b>
Company Description</b><p></p>
<p>
Services Company</p>
<p></p>
<b>
Job Description</b><p></p>
<p>
The Senior IT Buyer will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Process a queue of national and international requisitions and convert to Purchase Orders on a daily basis</li>
<li>Collect written proposals, project, or turnaround purchases and potential bundles from vendors, as needed</li>
<li>Conduct negotiations of pricing, terms &amp; conditions, Statements of Work, and other contractual material</li>
<li>Effectively partner with the Law Department to ensure successful negotiations that will result in effective agreements which have properly mitigated risk, established KPIs and associated SLAs</li>
<li>Focus negotiation on the complete fulfillment of internal stakeholder requirements through cost reductions, cost avoidance, and process improvements</li>
<li>Address invoice discrepancies from PO or goods receipt</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<b>Required Skills</b><p></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of significant experience of IT SS&amp;P, Contract Negotiation, Competitive Price Analysis, Supplier Relationship Management, and Performance Management</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in a related field</li>
<li>Experience managing Preferred Vendor Management programs</li>
<li>Deep expertise in the IT category spend, including, but not limited to software, hardware, professional services, cloud solutions, etc.</li>
<li>Strong working understanding of and experience in contract law, and negotiations in both legal and business terms in legal agreements</li>
<li>Strong financial analysis skills with the ability analyze and explain pricing models</li>
<li>Strong understanding of legal concepts as related to contract negotiations</li>
<li>Microsoft Office/Suite proficient</li>
<li>Solid problem solving and time management skills</li>
<li>Great interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (written and verbal)</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Highly organized</li>
</ul>
