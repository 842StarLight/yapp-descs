<h2>Service Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>We're looking for a Service Project Manager to join our Las Vegas team!</b></p>
<p>ACCO Engineered Systems has been annually recognized by a national industry magazine as one of the top-10 specialty contractors in the United States, and the leader in its mechanical contracting field for the Western United States. We have a diverse environment of skilled professionals - field construction workers, engineering, project managers, service technicians, and supporting staff - who collaborate together and make ACCO an engaging place to work.</p>
<p>Under the direction of the Regional Sales Manager, this position will be primarily responsible for prospecting, estimating and closing maintenance contracts, repairs of all sizes and retrofit projects in the Existing Building Market. Successful performers in this position will have the ability to develop, maintain and enhance ongoing relationships were Service Customers.
</p>
<p><b>SO WHAT WILL YOU BE DOING?</b></p>
<p><b>1. Prepare and Deliver Customer Proposals
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Includes retrofit projects, repairs of all sizes and maintenance contracts.
</p></li>
<li><p>This will include clearly defining scope, exclusions and general conditions in writing.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Negotiate scope and pricing with customer to assure a solution for the customer and a profit for ACCO.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>2. Survey Jobsites, develop Job Cost Estimates and Interface with ACCO Engineering</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Visit jobsites or obtain information from field technicians to prepare job cost estimates and alternatives solutions for customer problems.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be able to obtain equipment pricing from appropriate vendors and analyze the differences between the various features for each line of equipment.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Use the multiple software packages provided by the Company to prepare estimates for a variety of work including sheetmetal, piping and equipment/parts replacements.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Utilize internal Engineering assets to develop conceptual solutions, system sizing and equipment selection to assure proper design and installation drawings. Project Manager remains responsible for final Engineering and Constructability of our solution.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>3. Prepare Ductwork and Piping takeoffs</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Estimating job costs and developing scope for proposals requires takeoff skills.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be able to read construction drawings.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Strong math and analytical skills are required.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>4. Load Calculations
</b></p>
<ul><li>
<p>Using third party software, prepare or review (if prepared by others) load calculations to determine cooling and heating requirements.</p>
</li></ul>
<p><b>5. Selling Maintenance Contracts
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Develop an understanding of the scope for <i>ACCOCare</i>, <i>ACCOGuard </i>and <i>ACCOServe</i> maintenance contracts.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Review all <i>ACCOGuard</i> and <i>ACCOServe </i>work orders to determine if work performed is covered by the contract or is billable.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Determine appropriate tasking for a maintenance contract and prepare booking documentation.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>6. Develop and Maintain Customer Relationships</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Prospect for future customers continuously</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Close sales transactions and keep customers happy.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Make sure customers are aware of all services provided by ACCO.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>7. Communicating with Field Personnel</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Clearly conveying the scope of work for sold jobs to the field.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be available to assist the field in problem solving in the middle of jobs</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in pre-job meetings</p>
</li>
<li><p>Develop variance reports to analyze the outcome of jobs that go over budget and discuss the results with field personnel and Division Management.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>8. Business Ethics and Professional Conduct
</b></p>
<ul><li>
<p>At all times external and internal customers, vendors and fellow employees must be treated fairly, honestly and with the intent of maintaining a mutually beneficial long-term relationship.</p>
</li></ul>
<p><b>9. Organizational Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Allocate time and maintain a realistic schedule to provide service and solutions to existing customers before taking on new business.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learn and utilize internal documentation tools to completely and timely record project and billing information.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Thoroughly document repair and project work in files, which can be shared and understood other ACCO employees in your absence.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>10. Managing ACCO’s Resources</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Prepare necessary documentation to properly initiate a project awarded to ACCO. This will include communicating with Purchasing for Equipment Orders and releasing Subcontractors in a timely fashion and with proper ACCO approved documents.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Conducting Prejob Conferences with all appropriate attendees to communicate all relevant issues for a secured job. Documentation should be distributed in this meeting.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Analyzing ACCO’s internal cost reports and keeping Division Management posted on the forecasted outcome of the project on a monthly basis.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Involve Division Management in a prebid meeting to discuss whether of not the Company should propose on this work, agree on technical approach and pricing. The Project Manager may be required to involve other resources or people in the estimating, proposal writing and pricing strategy of the project proposal and execution of the project if ACCO is awarded the project.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Extra Work Proposals must be priced using the internal Extra Work Proposal estimated documents and submitted to the customer in writing in a timely manner.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The Project Manager must assist the Company in resolving collection issues.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WHAT DO YOU NEED TO BE A PART OF THIS ALL-STAR TEAM?</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Minimum of 7 to 10 years in the HVAC Service Industry. Education requirements are either an Associates degree in HVAC or a Bachelors Degree, preferably in Engineering. Must have an advanced level understanding of HVAC and Control Systems.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to relate well with a variety of personality types.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Good organizational and time management skills are a required in order to handle multiple issues
</p></li>
<li><p>Able to participate in customer relations issues and find solutions to solve customer disputes
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Strong technical knowledge in HVAC equipment, ductwork and piping.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Understand the necessity of maximizing the productivity of our technician workforce.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Good verbal, written, listening and computer skills.</p>
</li>
</ul>
