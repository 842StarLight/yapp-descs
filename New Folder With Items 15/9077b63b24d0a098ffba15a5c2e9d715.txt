<h2>Textiles Sales Representative - HBF</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h3>
<div>
<i>Position at HBF</i>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>HBF,</b> an industry leader in office furniture design and manufacturing, and a top brand in the <b>HNI Corporation </b>enterprise, is in search of a <b>Textiles Sales Representative - HBF</b> to join our team in Hickory, NC!</p>
<p><i>
Some of the essential aspects of the position include:</i></p>
<ul><li>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
This role is responsible for achieving HBF Textile sales objectives, maximizing profitable growth, and client objectives for the HBF Textiles Brand. In addition, they will create, retain and service sales opportunities through the architectural and design communities and client end users. Initiate National purchasing agreements and secure Fortune 500 accounts with standardized HBF Textile programs as well as being an actively involved member in the design community by participating in events within professional organizations, such as IIDA, IFMA and AIA.</p>
</li></ul>
<p><i>
What it takes:</i></p>
<ul><li>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Will develop, plan, and execute HBF Textile sales and marketing strategies directed at mid to large design firms, commercial and assigned dealer base. Responsible for the development and maintaining of relationships with designers, key influencers, and mid to large end users.</li>
<li>
Will prepare an annual business plan specific to assigned territory, design firm’s responsibilities and be prepared to present updates at semi-annual market reviews.</li>
<li>
Manage, oversee, and generate sales activities within region of responsibility. Identify, track, and manage potential opportunities.</li>
<li>
Grow national firms business through consultative selling, focused and customized product launch activities, and principal/direct level relationship building.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Participate in the development of opportunity and account strategies and identifies what types of activities will best increase the mindshare while consistently communicating the HBF Textiles message to multiple offices of the same firm(s).</li>
<li>
Participate in launch strategy and roll-out activities for new products, new knowledge, new capabilities (network, website redo, webinars to national firm librarians, events in a box, etc.).</li>
<li>
Create and execute plan for increasing opportunities and growing share in vertical markets (healthcare, government, higher education).</li>
<li>
Maintain and create relationships within architecture and design firms, by consistently presenting the HBF Textile products.</li>
<li>
Deliver new learning’s and possible product enhancements/updates to target firms, e.g., CEUs/Workplace programming and other tools.</li>
<li>
Develop data on wins/losses to be used internally while also shared externally with target firms and other intel that informs our relationships with those firms.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Develop or supports development and continuous improvement of processes and tools and collateral material for A &amp; D team and other field sales members and other internal users for product launches, client engagements and presentations.</li>
<li>
<ul>
<li>
Contribute to the development/improvement of new tools and materials to aid in A &amp; D selling activities and expand HBF Textiles capabilities in breadth and depth.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other HBF Textiles functions to support sales tools development and consultative selling training development and delivery for sales team members.</li>
<li>
Contribute to the periodic sharing of new competitive information and feedback from the field regarding possible product enhancements/updates between team and with Product Development.</li>
<li>
Contribute to the on-going population and quality of the typical/image library based on RFPs received and other best practice intelligence maintained by HBF Textiles.</li>
<li>
Provide input to development of product marketing materials, e.g. planning/applications guide(s), brochures, websites to drive more use by A &amp; D community.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Contribute to the development of new product categories.</li>
<li>
Oversee and assists with end-user and design support activities within channel.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Meet/exceed all revenue and growth goals assigned.</li>
<li>
Assist in the gathering of information and feedback from large A &amp; D firms located in various markets for Product Development team.</li>
<li>
Support sales training activities related to product design/application and positioning.</li>
<li>
Maintain and advances own knowledge of architecture and design and related topics.</li>
<li>
Service Design firms, end users, distribution network by maintaining binders, sample and pricing request.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><b>
Experience/Education Required</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors Degree in Marketing, Interior Design, or related field.</li>
<li>
Professional certifications preferred.</li>
<li>
Significant experience in sales (Textile) and a proven ability to develop and maintain relationships. Knowledge of textile industry and general business trends.</li>
<li>
Ability to identify and positively influence key decision makers and influencers.</li>
<li>
Positive “can-do” attitude and a commitment to professionalism, integrity and sound judgment.</li>
<li>
Ability to successfully interact across business functions, from HBF | HBF Textiles to the field sales members and independent dealers.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to lead change, handle multiple projects in a fast-paced environment, and ability to enhance culture and member capabilities.</li>
</ul>
</li>

Why HNI Corporation?</i></p>
<p><b><i>
HNI Corporation</i></b> (NYSE: HNI), is a $2.3 billion-dollar, global office furniture and hearth product enterprise. Our furniture brands are among the strongest, most widely known in the industry, including HON, Allsteel, Gunlocke, HBF, Maxon, and OFM. The depth and breadth of our brands, the scale and capability of our manufacturing, and the strength of our distribution enables us to provide the best office furniture solutions to meet the needs of every customer.</p>
