<div>Job Summary:
<p><b>I. </b><b>Position Summary</b></p>
The role of the marketing specialist is to focus on aligning our online capabilities to support and enhance our marketing and sales efforts. This will require a passion and vision of how this media format enhances revenue generation in a Business to Business manufacturing operation. The position will require working closely with every Department in the organization to identify opportunities as well as to provide guidance as to how those concepts can assist with the success of the company. The person will be well versed in web development, its maintenance and optimization. In addition, a good working knowledge of the use of other formats of online media should be essential skill sets. There is potential for this role to mature into product line responsibility functions for our core HVAC products in which analyzing, developing and promoting to increase revenue generation would be a key component.<b>
</b><p><b>II. </b><b>Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<div><ul>
<li><b>
Be responsible for creating and driving the company's path forward for our online presence.</b></li>
<li><b>
Take ownership of the website, sales literature and documentation requirements for organization (creating, managing, updating, etc.).</b></li>
<li><b>
Upgrades website by updating content and graphics; monitoring performance and results; identifying and evaluating improvement options; introducing new technology; maintaining links.</b></li>
<li><b>
Use SEO (search engine optimization) techniques to improve search ranking for the public website.</b></li>
<li><b>
Work with the Rep organizations and internal sales staff to improve the functionality of the public and Rep websites.</b></li>
<li><b>
Work with the Marketing and Sales team to develop online training tools, presentations and sales tools.</b></li>
<li><b>
Collect, analyze and communicate a range of market data from customers, competition and the general industry.</b></li>
<li><b>
Proactively identify and/or create sales tools that will be used by Sales, Marketing and Technical Service Departments primarily driven through the website.</b></li>
<li><b>
Identify, develop and present training sessions on various software applications that will establish force multipliers for internal company staff.
</b></li>
<li><b>Other market analysis, product line oversight and general activities as needed to support website development.</b></li>
<li><b>
Take ownership and develop social media presence for the company.</b></li>
<li><b>
Have the desire and abilities to take on product line oversight activities within 1 - 2 years.</b></li>
</ul></div>
