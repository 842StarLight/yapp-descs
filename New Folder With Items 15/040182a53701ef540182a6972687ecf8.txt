<h2>Fruit Expert</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Fruit Expert® - Customer Service Associate ensures Edible® service standards are met and surpassed by providing customers with prompt service, delivering a WOW experience
</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates a strong understanding of product knowledge and quality standards</li>
<li>Welcomes and connects with every customer to provide excellent customer service</li>
<li>Discovers customer needs and appropriately suggests products with every customer to enhance service and meet sales goals</li>
<li>Assists customers in the selection and purchase of Edible® products</li>
<li>Uses effective sales approaches and displays strong knowledge of product line</li>
<li>Effectively converts customer inquiries into sales</li>
<li>Builds long-term relationships and builds the trust and loyalty of customers</li>
<li>Responds to customer needs</li>
<li>Maintains customer service area and equipment in a clean and appealing manner</li>
<li>Manages difficult customer situations in a calm and professional manner</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Maintains Quality Store Operations</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepares a variety of Edible® fruit arrangements and treats according to our brand standards</li>
<li>Follows store policies and procedures for operational flow at each station</li>
<li>Performs cleaning tasks in accordance with the cleaning standards and works as a store team player</li>
<li>Presents oneself professionally and demonstrates clear communication with all customers and co-workers</li>
<li>Follows standards for merchandising, stocking, rotating and storing all products</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Financial Contribution</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Follows cash handling and register policies</li>
<li>Follows inventory stocking and recording guidelines</li>
<li>Takes responsibility to learn all aspects of the position</li>
<li>Learns and demonstrates creating the Edible® Experience</li>
<li>Learns and performs duties of customer Service, production, and/or delivery driver as needed</li>
<li>Learns and demonstrates all performance standards by completing in-store training and utilizing digital training portal on an ongoing basis</li>
</ul> <p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent preferred</li>
<li>Previous experience working in customer Service or food service establishment preferred</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and communication skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to sell and up-sell products</li>
<li>Good math and money handling skills</li>
<li>Positive attitude</li>
<li>Enjoys working within a team</li>
<li>Enjoys working in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Drive and determination</li>
<li>Desire for personal and professional growth</li>
<li>Valid state driver’s license and a clean driving record (if also delivering)</li>
<li>Computer skills including Microsoft software and register skills</li>
<li>Ability to lift, carry and/or push objects weighing up to 50 lbs.</li>
<li>Ability to stand, bend, reach and scoop throughout assigned shift</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
