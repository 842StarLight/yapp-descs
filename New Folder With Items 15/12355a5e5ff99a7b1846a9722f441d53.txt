About the role
<br><br>
Punchh is looking for experienced enterprise SaaS sales executives who will act as an individual contributor.
<br><br>
What You'll Do
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead client pursuits to achieve sales quotas while maximizing profitability and account penetration within an assigned vertical, including leading the development and presentation of the Punchh value proposition</li>
<li>Negotiate pricing and contractual terms to close sale as required</li>
<li>Identify, qualify and manage leads through market research and collaboration with internal lead generation</li>
<li>Utilize Salesforce to build, manage and grow a healthy pipeline</li>
<li>Strong business acumen to understand customer's business drivers and adapt the Punchh value proposition for each prospect</li>
<li>Manage the end-to-end sales process for each targeted prospect, collaborate internally to assess prospects' needs and define the optimal solution</li>
<li>Must be able to quickly learn and understand Punchh's product offerings and competitive landscape to develop solution proposals encompassing all aspects of Punchh platform</li>
<li>Present and negotiate deals with the CxO and their direct reports at F1000 enterprises</li>
<li>Develop tailored proposals that educate clients on Punchh Platform's superiority over competing offerings</li>
</ul>
What You'll Need
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Successful sales track record of meeting/exceeding annual quotas &gt;$2M in enterprise software sales</li>
<li>5 or more years of SaaS sales experience</li>
<li>Excellent communication, negotiating, and closing skills</li>
<li>Possess strong marketing skills</li>
<li>Experience selling into both IT and Marketing functions in F1000 enterprises</li>
<li>Experience negotiating complex contracts</li>
<li>Experience working remote with ability to travel up to 60% throughout the US</li>
<li>Knowledge of point of sale (POS) systems, payment providers &amp; processors, online ordering/eCommerce</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
</ul>
