<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
The Financial Advisor Development Program Performance Manager (FPM) supports management to develop, execute and achieve the advisor development goals of the market. The FPM is responsible for overseeing the day-to-day management of the Financial Advisor Development Program (FADPs) participants within the market, ensuring a consistent experience that is strictly aligned to the national program. The FPM partners with leadership to deliver coaching, training and performance assessments that support the development and success of all FADPs.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>
Advisor Development &amp; Training</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Under the direction of the Market Executives and the Division FADP Performance Executives, and in partnership with the FADP Coordinators, ensure FADPs are meeting and achieving hurdles</li>
<li>
Assess skills, knowledge and capabilities gaps through coaching and analysis; develop customized developmental plans to address</li>
<li>
In alignment with national program, lead training and develop activities, tools and routines to help enable FADP productivity</li>
<li>
Personal involvement with FADPs who require heightened support to achieve success</li>
<li>
Help reinforce compliance, supervision, and provide risk management support of FADPs and their activity for market leadership</li>
</ul>
