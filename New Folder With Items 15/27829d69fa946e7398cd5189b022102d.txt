<h2>Full Time Sr Customer Assistance Representative Fallon</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Customer Assistance Representative Sr (Sr CAR) will provide a high level of customer service by assisting both internal and external customers, primarily face-to-face, supporting their branch and rental needs. The Sr CAR will gain knowledge through in branch training and hands-on experience to provide administrative support, service customers and sales.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position is Full Time which requires approximately 40-45 hours per week.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>This position is located in Fallon, NV.</div>
<div>
This position pays $12.50/hr.</div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Take incoming calls- reservations, rate quotes, general questions and answers, provide information and resolution for customers, other branches, insurance companies, dealerships, repair shops and other vendors
</li>
<li>Manage outgoing calls for callback management, A/Rs and miscellaneous calls as assigned</li>
<li>
Provide a high level of customer service by assisting customers and assessing their rental needs in person and over the phone</li>
<li>
Meet and greet customers and accounts in a friendly and timely manner</li>
<li>
Provide directions and general assistance</li>
<li>
Assist to assess condition of rental upon return</li>
<li>
Process returns, check-ins transactions</li>
<li>
Effectively market the company while picking up customers up and/or dropping off customer in a safe and courteous manner and assisting customers as needed</li>
<li>
Understand and communicate rental terms and conditions, vehicle features and other services</li>
<li>
Sell optional protection products, upgrades, fuel options and other additional equipment</li>
<li>
Responsible for notifying Management of any known vehicle problems and any required vehicle maintenance</li>
<li>
Clean vehicle interior and exterior by hand or by operating washing equipment when needed</li>
<li>
Perform various administrative and basic accounting functions such as: research and billing support tasks, accounts receivables, transfer and key logs, run miscellaneous reports, supply maintenance, process customer billing</li>
<li>
Perform miscellaneous and backup duties job-related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
Equal Opportunity Employer/Disability/Veterans</i></p>
<br>
<b>
Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li><div>
<div>Must have at least 6 months of relevant work experience (can be non-concurrent) in sales, customer service (i.e. retail sales, commission/incentive sales, restaurant, serving/bartending or similar), or management/supervisory/leadership.</div>
<ul><li>
In lieu of work experience, we will consider organization involvement at a leadership level (i.e. organizations, clubs, military, community involvement, professional or collegiate athlete)</li></ul>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Must have a current and valid driver's license with no more than 2 moving violations and/or at fault accidents on driving record within the past 3 years, and no drug or alcohol related convictions on driving record within the past 5 years (i.e. DUI, DWI)</div></li>
<li>
Must be proficient in English, written and verbal comprehension.</li>
<li>
Must be authorized to work in the United States and not require work authorization sponsorship by our company for this position now or in the future.</li>
<li>
Must be at least 18 years of age.</li>
<li>
Some college preferred</li>
<li>
Must be flexible with hours</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
