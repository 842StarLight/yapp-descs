<b>
Description</b><br>
The HR Co-ops will support current initiatives and improvement projects for Emerson Climate Group and Sidney Operations Human Resources departments. They will be assigned a variety of tasks, primarily supporting Employment, Benefits, and Employee Relations functions. The opportunity to support projects pertaining to Compensation, Training and Development, and Safety may also arise.<br>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Assist with Co-op, Salaried, and/or Hourly Recruiting, opportunity to attend Career Fairs
</li>
<li>Resume review and assisting managers with the recruitment process
</li>
<li>Sit in on Co-op and Full-time interviews with the opportunity to conduct interviews as needed
</li>
<li>Assist with new hire orientation and paperwork
</li>
<li>Support communications plans
</li>
<li>Support Benefits Department in audits, pensions, and retirement documentation processes
</li>
<li>Assist with employee and labor relations, morale events, and recognition programs
</li>
<li>Conduct Co-op exit interviews<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
