<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p>The <b>Enterprise Architect</b> will possess deep domain knowledge spanning multiple technology architectures inclusive of Enterprise Networking, Hosted and On-Premises Collaboration and Data Center platforms. Enterprise Architects are the technical leadership within the Professional Services Group and peer with PreSale Solution Architecture teams. The Enterprise Architect is responsible to establish technical delivery standards, mentor junior engineering resources, collaborate with project management and partner with pre-sale management teams.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES- MAY INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING: OTHER DUTIES MAY BE ASSIGNED.
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops repeatable Enterprise solution deployment methods related to new and existing network collaboration and data center environments.</li>
<li>Participates in pre-booking technical solution development and validation to ensure delivery feasibility in terms of technical deliverables and economy.</li>
<li>Designs enterprise-class solutions with Microsoft Visio, NETFORMX or similar graphing product.</li>
<li>Design | Deploy | Test: Enterprise IP networks inclusive of:
<ul>
<li>Layer-3: BGP, OSPF, EIGRP, RIP, IS-IS, LISP.</li>

<li>Access, Distribution, and Core Redundancy Architectures</li>
<li>Cisco ISR/ASR Routers, Catalyst &amp; Nexus Switches</li>
<li>DNA Center, Cisco Software Defined Strategies &amp; Products including SD-WAN, ACI, and APIC-EM</li>
<li>Cisco and Meraki Wireless Controllers and Access Points</li>
<li>Cisco Security suite including: ASA, Firepower, ISE, AMP, NGFW, Cisco</li>
<li>Cisco UCS B and C Series Server platforms, Hyperflex, UCS Manager, VMware ESXi, vCenter.</li>
