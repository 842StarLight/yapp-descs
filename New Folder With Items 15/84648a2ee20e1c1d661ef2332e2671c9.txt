<h2>Patient Advocacy Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><div>A Patient Advocacy Representative will respond to patient, physician, insurance, customer services, and sales representative inquiries for specified clients. Troubleshoot case issues and manage resolution to completion. Act as a patient advocate by providing information and education on health insurance benefits, insurance billing, and the reimbursement process to patients.
</div></div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Answer incoming telephone and email inquiries, providing an outstanding customer experience.
</li>
<li>Review case history and insurance notes to provide appropriate information to callers.
</li>
<li>Make outbound calls to Physicians, Patients, Parents/Spouse.</li>
<li>
Call physicians to obtain information or provide information pertaining to a case or issue.
</li>
<li>Communicate results of a benefit investigation to patient and physicians.
</li>
<li>Pre-qualify patients for any available financial aid or clearly communicate out of pocket costs.
</li>
<li>Respond to inquiries and complete worklist tasks in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Initiate appeals or patient billing process when needed.</li>
<li>
Report all insurance company trend changes to the Call Center Supervisor or Manager.</li>
<li>
Participate in team meetings by sharing the details of cases worked.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by Supervisor or Manager.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>High School diploma or GED</li>
<li>
Previous customer service experience required</li>
<li>
Medical billing knowledge preferred</li>
<li>
Possess and demonstrate superior customer service skills</li>
<li>
Ability to listen and work well with others</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain confidentiality</li>
<li>
Detail oriented</li>
<li>
Possess excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to establish priorities, work independently, and proceed with objective without supervision</li>
<li>
Proficient in using Microsoft Excel, Word, and ability to quickly learn and master a variety of computer based software applications</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
