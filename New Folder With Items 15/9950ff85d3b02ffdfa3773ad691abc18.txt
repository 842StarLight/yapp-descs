<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Influential, an A.I. influencer technology and developer partner of IBM Watson, is looking for an experienced Account Executive to leverage high level relationships and become a champion of our product and offering. Based out of New York, the ideal candidate will have a desire to learn about our product, sales positioning and approach, and work collaboratively with our operations team.</p>
<p>
We offer competitive compensation, 100% company paid insurance for the employee with options to upgrade and add dependents at a discount, and equity options for all employees. We also have an awesome collaborative work environment and a diverse team.
</p>
<p><b>Here’s what you’ll be doing:</b></p>
<p>
Our Account Executive will be focused on expanding our regional presence and bringing our offering to a growing book of leading agencies and Fortune 1000 brands. You will leverage personal relationships, product knowledge, and best practices to clearly communicate the differentiators of our offering in the market. You’ll report directly to our Head of East Coast Sales, becoming an integral part of the sales team that will lead us into our next stage of growth.</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive the sales cycle: effectively conduct needs analysis, succinctly communicate Influential’s vision in the market, and proactively negotiate deals to close.</li>
<li>Collect insights into a client’s organizational structure, online and offline marketing strategy, and ideal demographic.</li>
<li>Participate in weekly meetings, sales and product trainings, and 1:1s</li>
<li>Identify market opportunities, process improvements, and facilitate market feedback</li>
<li>Closely track internal KPIs, be mindful of team objectives and key results, and follow best practices for automated performance reporting</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Here's what we'd like to see:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4-6 years of sales experience</li>
<li>Relevant client relationships in the New York market</li>
<li>You have have previously engaged with leaders at agencies and Fortune 1000 companies</li>
<li>Proven record of exceeding quarterly objectives and attention to key metrics</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce and other productivity related SaaS products</li>
<li>Selling experience in the social/media space preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Bonus points if:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You’re passionate about social media and/or influencer marketing</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About Influential</b></p>
<p>
Influential is an AI social data and conversion technology and a Developer Partner of IBM Watson. Utilizing a network of over 300,000 social media influencers as a tactic for distribution, Influential runs both native and paid campaigns on Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, Twitter, and YouTube for Fortune 500 brands including Walmart, McDonald’s, Pepsi, Nestlé, General Mills, Toyota, Samsung, Sony Pictures and many more. Influential is the only company that can track deterministic one-to-one in-store foot traffic and sales attribution from paid influencer posts, as well as TV tune-in lift and attribution. Influential is a strategic partner of WME and Oracle’s Moat and has offices in Beverly Hills, New York City, and Las Vegas. (www.influential.co)</p>
