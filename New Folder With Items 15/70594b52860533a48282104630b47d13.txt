 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p>Profiles is a creative and marketing staffing agency looking to internally hire a <b>Recruitment Marketing Account Manager</b> for the <b>Washington D.C.</b> office. The ideal Recruitment Marketing Account Manager must have experience pitching creative and digital marketing services. Project examples range from analytics, content strategy, copywriting, design, development, digital marketing, and account management. Agency experience a plus.</p>
<p><b>Recruitment Marketing Account Manager Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand prospect and existing clients creative, marketing, and web project needs to consult managed service solutions or project-based work. Project examples include but are not limited to analytics, content strategy, copywriting, design, development, digital marketing, and account management.</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal creative recruitment teams who will recruit a team of digital consultants to execute the clients long term project.</li>
<li>Consult and nurture existing clients with frequent touchpoints to exceed client expectations and satisfaction.</li>
<li>Establish, develop, and grow new business relationships with enterprise creative and marketing decision-makers.</li>
<li>Maximize territory potential through targeting prospects, qualifying, conducting customer meetings and conveying the value of Profiles Managed Services and project-based work.</li>
<li>Close new business deals by developing and negotiating contracts and integrating contract requirements with business operations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Recruitment Marketing Account Manager Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A passion for the brand, marketing, or the digital landscape. Agency experience a plus.</li>
<li>Experience pitching creative, marketing, and web services to leadership and decision-makers of Enterprise brands.</li>
<li>B2B industry sales, lead generation, prospecting, and cold calling.</li>
<li>Excellent project management, organization and communication skills are required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Profiles Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive base salary.</li>
<li>Uncapped commission plan.</li>
<li>Medical, Dental, Prescription, and Vision Insurance.</li>
<li>401k Retirement plan with a match.</li>
<li>Paid vacation, sick days, and holidays.</li>
<li>Casual dress attire, monthly cell phone allowance, mileage reimbursement, and discounted commuter plan.</li>
</ul>
