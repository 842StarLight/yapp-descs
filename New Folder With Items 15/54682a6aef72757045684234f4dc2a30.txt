<h2>Application Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Summary:</b><br>
Under the direction of Application Engineering Manager, the application Engineer serves as the technical lead for projects in the North America. The Application Engineer is responsible for technical bidding support, fully defined &amp; compliant product development prior to contract signing, and electrical engineering and interconnection support for the project engineering team during the execution phase of the project. The Application Engineer acts as the interface between Sales, R&amp;D, and project engineering. Providing specialized technical support between the project engineer, project manager and customer’s BOP designers will be an essential requirement to be successful in the role.</p>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities/Duties include:</b><br>
<ul><li><b>Regular bidding support:</b></li></ul>
Ensure bid requirements are understood, initiate and manage Goldwind bidding process and assure offers comply with requirements.<br>
Work with sales and other technical functions to systematically discover deal breaker based on GW<br>
product offerings, optimize performance &amp; cost variable to maximize customer’s value.<br>
Support sales and business development team in formulating Requests for Non-Standard Products to propose a site-specific solution not currently available within Goldwind portfolio<br>
Research and collect Input of market requirements and regional regulatory to product design and training of Sales / Customer with new product developments<br>
<ul><li><b>TSA negotiation:</b></li></ul>
Participate in contract negotiations and project meetings with regard to wind turbine electrical issues, take full ownership of interconnection contract exhibits<br>
Review and refine technical documents to maintain high quality and proper applicability<br>
Coordinate all technical support in the US and HQ of influence, according to process guidelines, minimize and manage technical risk throughout project life cycle onward.<br>
Review utility interconnection requirements to ensure wind park compliance with grid transmission operators<br>
<ul><li><b>Pre-construction:</b></li></ul>
Prepare and manage model simulation packages and associated support to system operators and customers using self-developed communication strategy<br>
Support and review grid simulation study reports and support customers to optimize projects using the simulation study results<br>
Participate and support as key interface for electrical-related work with customer and customer’s BOP designers, collection of all electrical data and customer/site/grid requirements for project interconnection studies, and clarify turbine electrical characteristics to external parties, as well as review diagrams and drawings</div>
</div>
<br>

</ul>
