<p><b>About the Company: </b></p>
<p>Traffic Plan is a high growth, nationally certified woman business enterprise operating in New Jersey, Pennsylvania, and Virginia. The company provides traffic safety services and products to utility companies, private contractors, and specialty firms.</p>
<p>Our core services include physically setting up and maintaining safety zones, complete with qualified flaggers and safety equipment around our customers’ construction and event sites. Another core service is the sale and rental of traffic control equipment including attenuator trucks, message and arrow boards, light towers, traffic drums, signs, barricades, barrels, and cones. Traffic Plan’s management team is mostly comprised of retired law enforcement officials. We also employ skilled field technicians that are members of a nationally recognized union.</p>
<p><b>Position Requirements: </b></p>
<p>Traffic Plan is seeking a full time Entry Level Recruiter with one year experience in high volume recruiting. The qualified candidate must possess strong organizational and communication skills. The qualified candidate must possess the ability to work independently and exercise complete confidentiality with all of the work content.</p>
