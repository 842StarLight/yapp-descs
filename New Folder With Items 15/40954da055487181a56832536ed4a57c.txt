<h2>Infrastructure/Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>10ZiG Career Opportunity – Infrastructure/Sales Engineer</b></p>
<p>About Us:</p>
<p>10ZiG Technology is a leading provider in Thin and Zero Clients for the latest virtual desktop solutions. 10ZiG has long-standing partnerships with industry leaders such as Citrix, VMware and Microsoft. With headquarters in Phoenix, AZ and offices in Europe and Asia Pacific, 10ZiG has a wide reach within the Thin and Zero Client worldwide marketplace.</p>
<p>Overview:</p>
<p>We are currently looking to add an Infrastructure/Sales Engineer to join our US team in our Phoenix, AZ headquarters.</p>
<p>The Infrastructure/Sales Engineer provides support for the internal 10ZiG network, ensuring that all systems are functional and appropriately configured for test, development, and technical support needs in support of 10ZiG products. This includes, but is not limited to Active Directory, VMware ESXi, VMware Horizon, Citrix XenApp/Xendesktop and Microsoft Remote Desktop Services.</p>
<p>The Infrastructure/Sales Engineer role also provides pre-sales technical support to internal and external sales teams during the sales process, working directly with partners and customers as needed during the process. Accountable for the technical validity and interoperability of solution and the direct relationship with the customers’ strategic business plans.</p>
<p>Responsibilities include a combination of pre-sales consultation services, performing technical product demonstrations, project managing, proof of concept engagements, and thin computing initiatives. Qualified candidates will work with mid-sized &amp; enterprise customers to shorten the sales cycle by overcoming technical hurdles within specified accounts. The Infrastructure/Sales Engineer must demonstrate solid communication skills, technical expertise and working both independently and within a team. They must also be able to communicate both to a technical audience as well as management and c-level executives. The Infrastructure/Sales Engineer must be able to work in a stressful yet results oriented environment, often switching different priorities at intervals as and where required.</p>
<p>Reporting into the Head of Technical Services, the role is primarily Phoenix office based but may involve some travel both within the state of Arizona and to sites in the continental US.</p>
<p>Role Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain, update and configure internal 10ZiG network in response to changing business objectives, ensuring uptime of business-critical components</li>
<li>Recommend technology updates and changes to compliment required functionality</li>
<li>Support internal 10ZiG users and external sales teams’ IT needs</li>
<li>Work with sales team for pre-sales customers and partners</li>
<li>Work with technical support team on handover of completed projects and tasks</li>
<li>Match products and solutions to customer technical needs</li>
<li>Describe key technical features, benefits and how these features differ from the competition</li>
<li>Overcome common technical objections</li>
<li>Reproduce customer issues and file articulate bug reports to development</li>
<li>Create training and presentation material as and where required</li>
<li>Present at tradeshows, partner and customer events</li>
<li>Study and learn new technology both complementary and competing to our own portfolio</li>
<li>Expert product knowledge of the entire 10ZiG portfolio</li>
<li>Understand and articulate virtualization solution including VMware, Citrix and MS RDS (Citrix Virtual Apps and Virtual Desktops experience (CCEA a plus), VMware Horizon experience (VCP a plus), Microsoft Server OS and Windows Desktop Operating Systems (MCSE a plus).</li>
<li>Understand and articulate management software options and deployment processes</li>
<li>Understand and articulate all models of Thin and Zero clients and their features</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of IT experience in a Vendor, Professional Services, IT Consulting, Systems Integration, or end-user environment</li>
<li>2+ years, technical pre-sales experience, or technical business-related experience</li>
<li>Citrix XenApp and Xendesktop experience (CCEA a plus), VMware View experience (VCP a plus), Microsoft Server OS 2012/2016/2019, and Windows 7/10 experience (MCSE a plus)</li>
<li>Computer Science or related Degree. Associates, Bachelors or higher preferred</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, customer service and communication skills</li>
<li>Professional presentation skills</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with building servers and setting up networks</li>
<li>Self-starter that manages time and activities with little day-to-day guidance or interaction from management</li>
</ul>
<p>Rewards:</p>

<li>Health Coverage and 401K</li>

</ul>
