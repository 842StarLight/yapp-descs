<h2>Shipping and Fulfillment Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About Beepods. </b></p>
<p>At Beepods, we work to raise awareness for the need for sustainability through beekeeping. We provide innovative solutions and tools to assist researchers in finding answers and encouraging the beekeeping community to implement responsible strategies to benefit the bees, the world, and all who live in it.</p>
<p>We have a strong focus on education, believing that educating the youth will have the greatest impact on the future. This position will be vital in our upcoming campaign focused on implementing beekeeping programs in schools.</p>
<p>Objective:</p>
<p>We are looking for a highly efficient individual who has the ability to adapt to a variety of situations and responsibilities, as needed. This role will work out of our warehouse location and will be required to work part time, as needed. This role could move into a full-time role as director of fulfillment in the future with job goals being executed very well.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Print Daily sales receipts and cross-check with CRM and Purchase orders to ensure accuracy</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare and print shipping labels corresponding to shipping needs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assemble wooden ware systems for QA and Personal Deliveries by our team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assemble and fill boxes, packages and other cartons that need to be shipped</li></ul>
<ul><li>Affix proper postage to packages in preparation for shipping</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track inventory and report shortages to supervisor</li></ul>
<ul><li>Process orders based on instructions and processes ( you may have to help design some of these processes)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contact carrier and schedule shipping</li></ul>
<ul><li>Fill out necessary forms/paperwork as needed by supervision to help maintain inventory and records</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain a clean and safe work area and update shipping supplies as needed</li></ul>
<p>Qualifications and Skills</p>
<ul><li>High School diploma/GED required, Associates Degree Preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>1+ years experience in e-commerce or retail</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proof of simple woodworking assembly</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent organizational and time management skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency in using Google-Suite software and tools</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with Wordpress (WooCommerce fulfillment) are a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work well in a fast-paced environment with frequently changing dynamics</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to have flexible scheduling for busy seasons</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to lift at least 50 lbs with ease</li></ul>
<ul><li>Able to follow instructions and communicate process improvements via written or drawn instructions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sales and customer service skills are a plus</li></ul>
<p>This role will be critical for the team going forward and will have an opportunity in taking ownership of the position to make it their own. There is a lot of room for career growth and input on the Beepods Team and will have opportunities to experience other roles in the company if desired.</p>
<p>***Note, these are just the types of things we are looking for. Anyone can learn a new skill, but it takes drive to reach the goals we have set for ourselves. We will teach you. We will connect you. Most importantly, if you want something, we promise to help you get there.</p>
<p>Why should I apply for this position?</p>
<p>If you have ever wanted to work for a startup company on the rise that makes a direct impact in the world around us, this is your chance. Beepods focuses on sustainability as our mission, but also in our business practices and personal development. You will be part of setting the tone and culture necessary for us to actually change the world for the better.</p>
<p>You will be working directly with the CEO of Beepods. He has worked with over 30 different organizations with a focus on leadership, team development and growth. Beepods will give you access to a variety of resources to grow your skill set, network and personal brand. Beepods is a fast-paced organization focused on building a brand, achieving a mission and developing tomorrow's leaders in sustainability for the earth and the economy.</p>
<p>Initially, this is a part-time role with the potential to move to a full-time position. As long as the position helps to speed up the operations of Beepods, there will be plenty of opportunity to pick up extra hours and responsibilities. The more versatile your skill set and the more willing you are to wear many hats and help out where needed, the faster you will be able to gain hours.</p>
