<p></p>
<p>REFRESH your career by joining our exceptional team! Coca-Cola Consolidated, the largest bottler in the U.S., is looking for an Account Developer!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Things you should know about working as an Account Developer:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Account Developer serves as the primary contact between our large store or small store managers/store owners. The position is responsible for exceptional customer relationships that are both befitting of the Coca-Cola brand and promote the needed trust to result in increased business through value-added placements and sales.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>What would success look like?</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Educate and excite customers about our leading portfolio of beverages</li></ul>
<ul><li>Consultative selling - analyze and understand our customer needs and trends to help them grow their profitability and our own resulting success by using our proprietary data to match and leverage brands with the specific account consumer demographics</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sell incremental displays, equipment placements, and promotional programs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain appropriate inventory levels, company assets and promotional ad signage (point of sale) and displays</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure each account meets Company merchandising standards, determine storesâ€™ product needs and transmit appropriate replenishment orders</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate and collaborate account activities to appropriate business partners</li></ul>
<ul><li>Utilize sales force automation tools</li></ul>
<ul><li>Transport, replace and maintain point of sale advertising as appropriate for each account</li></ul>
<br>
