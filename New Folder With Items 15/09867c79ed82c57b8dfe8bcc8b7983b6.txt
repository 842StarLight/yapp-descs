The RM Specialist will be responsible for the translation of tactical plans into day-to-day execution, project management, and supporting the execution of marketing campaigns within the Relationship Marketing department. This role will support the RM Sr.Specialists. Key responsibilities are included but not limited to building, proofing and deploying email marketing campaigns, and working with partners in key departments to develop and coordinate go-to-market plans.</p>
