<p><b>Client Service Representative</b></p>
<p>
Twenty job-seekers are needed for a<b><i> </i></b><b><i>Client Service Representative</i></b><b><i> </i></b>positions in <b><i>Plymouth Meeting, PA! </i></b>As the Health Assistant, you will primarily be responsible for responsible for providing clients with a differentiated member services experience. These temporary positions offer $15 an hour.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities of the </b><b>Client Service Representative</b><b> include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answering calls in a high volume, fast paced environment, from clients regarding their benefits, provider network, claims, invoices, billing and other health plan related questions</li>
<li>Probing for information to understand the client’s questions or issue in order to ensure first call resolution</li>
<li>Navigating and engaging multiple systems, and pulling the puzzle pieces together to resolve the customer’s questions, complex issues and requests</li>
<li>Providing guidance to customers regarding their coverage, provider network, claims and premium payments</li>
<li>Educating and empowering members to use the tools and resources available to them so they can self-service and become more savvy health care consumers</li>
<li>Navigating and providing input to policies, systems, methods, and procedures for the effective management and control of key business processes</li>
<li>Performing other relevant tasks, as needed.</li>
</ul>

<b>Requirements of the </b><b>Client Service Representative:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 1 year of customer service experience in an insurance setting (Health Plan preferably) dealing with claims, benefits and eligibility issues</li>
<li>College Degree Preferred (not required)</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills; strong verbal and written communication</li>
<li>Ability to respond emphatically</li>
<li>Experience resolving benefits and provider network questions in a fast paced, telephonic environment</li>
<li>Ability to answer the unasked question providing first call resolution</li>
<li>Ability to navigate multiple systems and multi-task</li>
<li>Ability to de-escalate tense situations and turn them into productive, resolve oriented conversations</li>
<li>Ability to navigate basic math scenarios including percentages, premiums/payments and out of pocket expenses (deductibles/coinsurance/copays)</li>
<li>Quick thinker</li>
<li>Flexibility and adaptability</li>
<li>Initiative, independence and a willingness to learn</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work as a team and collaborate</li>
<li>Bi-lingual preferred</li>
<li>Available to work 40 hours a week between 8am and 8pm</li>
</ul>
