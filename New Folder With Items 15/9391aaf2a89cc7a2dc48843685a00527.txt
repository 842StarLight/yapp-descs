<h2>Customer Success Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>If you are a good listener and enjoy helping people learn and achieve their goals and extract value from software, you might be just the person we are looking for.</p>
<p>
Contract Logix is seeking a Customer Success Associate (CSA) to join our growing Customer Success team. As a CSA, you will work closely with our customers, developing relationships and driving product adoption and positive outcomes leading to renewals, expansion, and overall satisfaction. Day to day, you will lead customers through onboarding, end-user adoption, and ongoing feedback as a trusted partner. As the voice of the customer, you will also work closely with the Customer Success, Sales, Product, and Technology teams promoting a customer-centric culture within Contract Logix</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assigned customer onboarding, including kickoff meetings, coaching, and user adoption</li>
<li>
Establish a trusted advisor relationship with customers</li>
<li>
Serve as a first level response to customer support inquiries by phone and email</li>
<li>
Promote customer satisfaction and loyalty by demonstrating an understanding of their business needs and helping them achieve their objectives using Contract Logix solutions</li>
<li>
Educate customers on the use and benefits of Contract Logix solutions</li>
<li>
Provide customers with information and assistance regarding product updates and new features</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
4 Year College Degree</p>
<p>
Excellent written and verbal skills</p>
<p>
Strong phone and conference call skills with active listening</p>
<p>
Passion for learning new technology and evangelizing to others</p>
<p>
Excellent multi-tasking and project management skills with high attention to detail</p>
<p>
An outgoing personality and an empathetic, positive attitude</p>
<p>
Experience in Customer Success, Account Management, or another client-facing roles</p>
<p>
This position is located at our headquarters in Lowell, MA.</p>
