<h2>Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>iDeals Solutions Group is an international B2B SaaS company that has been providing virtual data room services since 2008. We have always created the best experience for customers looking for a solution that would allow them to store and share sensitive data securely. Over 2,000 clients – LG, PWC, HP, EY, Deloitte, Barclays, Rothschild to name a few – inspire us to grow and become even better every day.</p>
<p>Transparency, teamwork, taking responsibility, critical thinking and over delivery are our fundamental corporate principles. They empower our company to achieve the ultimate goal – to exceed customer expectations and deliver a product that surpasses the one they were expecting to get. We are a team of 150 colleagues who speak ten languages.</p>
<p>We’re looking for a remote <b>Customer Success Manager</b>, who is a tech savvy individual with strong analytical mind who can quickly understand and communicate complex issues in a simple manner. Someone who puts customer first and holds own ground when negotiating with product or finance teams. Our business runs on numbers, and we are looking for somebody who is comfortable in such environment and can draw valuable conclusions from various data sources. The role includes obtaining excellent knowledge of the product that we offer to our clients and the specifics of client’s business processes.<br><b>Your Expected Outcomes</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conducting kick-off (onboarding) trainings for new clients</li>
<li>Handling upgrades and renewals</li>
<li>Engaging with clients through their product journey to increase adoption and retention</li>
<li>Handling incoming correspondence from clients, sales managers</li>
<li>Writing content and implementing new tools to support users including populating new in-app help center, short video tutorials, newsletters and finding new ways to drive product adoption</li>
<li>Driving referral program (increase reviews of the product and testimonials)</li>
<li>Any other activities which improve customer experience (we're open to your ideas too)</li>
</ul>
