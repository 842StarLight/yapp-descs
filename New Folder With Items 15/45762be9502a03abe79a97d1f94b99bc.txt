<h2>VP, Mid-Market Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>VP, Mid-Market Sales</b><br>
<br>
Frontier® Communications provides communications services to urban, suburban, and rural communities in 29 states. Frontier offers a variety of services to residential customers over its FiOS® and Vantage™ fiber-optic and copper networks, including video, high-speed internet, advanced voice, and Frontier Secure® digital protection solutions. Frontier Business™ offers communications solutions to small, medium, and enterprise businesses.<br>
<br>
As a critical leader in the B2B sales organization, the <i>Vice President</i>, Mid-Market Sales is responsible for maintaining, growing and modernizing Frontier’s existing Mid-Market B2B Accounts through a premier inside sales team.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The <i>Vice President</i>, Mid-Market Sales will achieve this objective through setting out clear account management parameters and sales plays for the segment, working closely with the Product and Marketing team, and recruiting, developing and leading the talent required to execute the account management and sales plays.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
It will be critical for this leader to be able to set the vision for the organization, and provide strategic leadership as well as tactically assist inside sales associates and create an environment in which they can thrive. This leader will also need to work collaboratively and cross-functionally with marketing, product management, client support, service delivery and other key stakeholders as well as other channels to achieve the sales and revenue goals for this segment.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Frontier offers its mid-market B2B customers a broad portfolio of products and solutions that are constantly being upgraded and improved. Mid-Market Sales is a new initiative to help ensure Frontier is able to deploy those solutions to target customers. As such, it is critical that <i>Vice President</i>, Mid-Market Sales is motivated and excited about the prospect of setting up a new business.</p>
<p><b>
Roles and responsibilities</b></p>
<p><i>
Overall:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage revenue of mid-market customer base, through account management and sales plays executed by the inside sales team</li>
<li>
Collaborate with peers and senior management across the organization to continually improve the sales organization and sales plays</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
Strategy/Direction:
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and evolve clear processes for the management of mid-market accounts based on limiting churn and growing the account relationship</li>
<li>
Effectively understand and respond to the customer segment needs, and connect the mid-market customer needs to Frontier offerings and solutions</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
People:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop a team of inside sales associates that fosters a high performance environment, utilizes best in class sales practices, and emphasizes development</li>
<li>
Set aspirational goals for the inside sales team, provide leadership to the team, provide tactical coaching and lead the performance management process</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
Metrics:</i></p>
<ul><li>
Measure salesforce effectiveness through performance metrics, share outcomes with sales leadership, and drive continuous improvement on the team</li></ul>
<p><b>
Requirements / Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
At least 10+ years of sales experience at a telecommunications or technology company; 5+ years of sales management experience, particularly in building and growing a high tech B2B inside sales teams</li>
<li>
Demonstrated, deep base of market and industry knowledge and ability to recognize changing market trends</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to link corporate strategic goals to customer facing solutions and to scale solutions within a customer base</li>
<li>
Proven record of meeting/exceeding established goals</li>
<li>
Track record of designing and managing disciplined sales processes</li>
<li>
Strong collaboration skills and ability to work alongside multiple team members</li>
<li>
Experience coaching and developing sales talent</li>
<li>
Experience with managing and reporting of sales data using CRM systems</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree, preferably in Business, Sales, or Marketing-related fields</li>
</ul>
