<p><b>Position Overview</b></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p>The Executive Assistant at Columbia Care provides professional administrative support to three C-Suite Executives (the Executive Chairman, the Chief Operating Officer, and the Chief Marketing Officer). Working as a trusted partner, the position will support these executives with calendar management, travel, expense management, document preparation, presentation preparation, event planning, purchasing, and other important administrative portions of key projects, as well as ad hoc tasks.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Reporting Relationship</b></p>
<p>
The Executive Assistant supports the Executive Chairman, the Chief Operating Officer, and the Chief Marketing Officer (the C-Suite Officers).</p>
<br>
</div></div>
<div>
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Major Areas of Responsibility</b></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p>Working for and under the direction of the C-Suite Officers, the Executive Assistant will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Managing the calendars of the executives by scheduling calls and meetings, sending calendar invitations, and coordinating logistical details for teleconference/video conference meetings, as well as all details relating to in-person meetings.</li>
<li>Auditing documents and presentations for errors by proofreading and alerts management of any found errors or issues. Revising presentations to ensure consistent formatting.</li>
<li>Booking complex travel and lodging arrangement for the executives.</li>
<li>Leading, managing or participating on cross-functional project and event planning teams.</li>
<li>Composing and editing correspondence, presentations, reports and other related documents.</li>
<li>Assisting in editing/refining confidential documents.</li>
<li>Overseeing the review and evaluation of work content for the purpose of developing effective administrative practices that contribute to the improvement of services provided.</li>
<li>Providing back-up administrative support to other C-Suite executives, as needed.</li>
<li>Completing other projects that may be assigned.</li>
<li>Demonstrating commitment to the Company's core values (we are patient-centered, caring, collaborative, honorable, accountable, innovative, and grateful).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Travel and Location</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The role is to be based in Manhattan.</li>
<li>Travel is estimated to be less than a 10% requirement for the position.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
</div></div>
<ul>
<li>Associate’s degree required; Bachelor’s degree preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum of 8 years’ experience as an Administrative Assistant required; experience as an Executive Assistant supporting C-Suite executives strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Administrative assistant certifications preferred (i.e. IAAP, CAP)</li>
<li>Demonstrated advanced-ability in calendar management, word processing, crafting presentations, and spreadsheet software programs (including Microsoft Office products, PowerPoint, Excel, Outlook, etc.).</li>
<li>Experienced in exercising sound judgment and making decisions in a manner consistent with confident leadership.</li>
<li>Ability to maintain flexibility and solve routine problems.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, both verbally and in writing.</li>
<li>Highly organized and effective at prioritize work.</li>
<li>Ability to book complex travel and lodging and prepare itineraries.</li>
