<h2>Marketing Support Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Kelly Services is seeking a <b>Marketing Support Manager </b>for a global, integrated healthcare services and products company located in <b>Dublin, OH.</b></p>
<p><b>Job Title: Marketing Support Manager for Connected Care</b><br>
 Location: Dublin, OH 43017<br>
 Type: Temp-To-Hire<br>
 Length: August 2019 – February 2020<br>
 Hours: 40 hours/week, 8 hours/day<br>
 Pay Rate: $50 per hour</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Description of Work:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be the Dublin-based Marketing coordinator for the newly formed Connected Care platform.</li>
<li>This will involve marketing efforts for the 3 lines of business that make up Connected Care</li>
<li>Activities may include email campaign management, SEO, pay-per-click advertising, all of which are focused on lead generation and lead nurturing sales efforts.</li>
<li>There may also be communication efforts to educate our internal sales teams on the mechanics of these technology-based programs and how they may complement our Retail Independents.</li>
<li>This could include the development of sales/marketing materials and webinars.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills/Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Digital marketing experience is a must.</li>
<li>Communications/ Agency experience would be great.</li>
<li>Healthcare experience and/ or B2B experience in some capacity is also considered a must.<br>
</li>
</ul>
