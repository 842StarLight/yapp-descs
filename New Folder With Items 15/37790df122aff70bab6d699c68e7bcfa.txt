<p><b>
Job Summary:
</b></p>
<p>The NRG Retail Regulatory team is seeking an ambitious and organized Paralegal, Regulatory Compliance to work in a fast paced and quickly growing business that participates in electricity and natural gas markets. This position is responsible for preparing and submitting the governmental compliance filings for the NRG Retail affiliates across the U.S. and Canada. The position reports to the Regulatory Compliance Manager. The position is based in Philadelphia, PA.</p>
<p><br>
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties/Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepare and file documents necessary to comply with licensing and reporting requirements for 18 states, 2 provinces, federal agencies and regional power grids (Independent System Operators).</li>
<li>
Work with contacts across the business to gather data, documents and information for regulatory filings and license renewals. Including: Senior management, wholesale, finance, marketing, sales, legal, etc.</li>
<li>
Communicate with state regulatory agency staff and utility company personnel on licensing, reporting and other regulatory matters; attend state regulatory commission meetings as needed.</li>
<li>
Stay current with relevant regulatory reporting requirements and changing of rules on a state by state level.</li>
<li>
Administer and maintain the regulatory compliance management system using Intelex software and provide training for new users.</li>
<li>
Monitor state regulatory agency docket filings of interest to the business; alert responsible government-regulatory associates.</li>
<li>
Maintain and organize an archive of regulatory filings and documents.</li>
<li>
Provide document research and retrieval support to the business.</li>
</ul>
