<h2>Sr. Network Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Lewan Technology believes in building strong professional relationships from the inside out as the best way to serve our employees and customers alike. We share a passion for working together in helping our clients by managing their technology so they can focus on managing their business. If you value hard work and accountability, have a collaborative spirit, a passion for serving others, and want to contribute to our cause, call us to discuss an exciting career opportunity in office technology. Lewan Technology, A Xerox Company, is seeking a Network Engineer to join our growing team.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
As a <b>Lewan Senior Network Engineer </b>within the Consulting Services organization, you will ensure successful outcomes for our clients by providing a world class customer experience. You'll work closely with our clients, sales team, and peers to design, implement, and support networks based on Cisco LAN, WAN, wireless and security solutions. A combination of deep technical expertise, a strong sense of personal integrity, a professional demeanor, and a focus on building lasting client relationships will be critical to ensuring ongoing success for our organization and that of our clients.</p>
<p><b>
Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Design, install, configure, troubleshoot, upgrade, and support extensive Cisco LAN/WAN network infrastructure with clients</li>
<li>Design, install, configure, troubleshoot, upgrade and support servers/appliances that provide key network services including remote access <b>VPN </b>appliances, Firewall/AMP/Stealthwatch, DNS/DHCP services, etc.)</li>
<li>Design, install, configure and support network management/monitoring solutions such as Cisco Prime Infrastructure.</li>
<li>Use packet capture tools (wired and wireless) to identify and resolve user or network communications issues.</li>
<li>Work with users, clients, vendors, and ISPs to troubleshoot and resolve issues related to LAN/WAN communications, point-to-point links, site-to-site VPNS, Internet access, etc.</li>
<li>Create documents pertaining to network changes and activities.</li>
<li>Ensure the confidentiality, integrity and availability of the data residing on or transmitted to/from/through enterprise networks.</li>
<li>Provide technical support and troubleshooting for network infrastructure</li>
<li>Design and install daily monitoring systems for Cisco equipment.</li>
<li>Ensure positive outcomes through a fanatical focus on client satisfaction</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) in Routing and Switching, Wireless, Datacenter, or Security</li>
<li>5+ years of networking experience.</li>
<li>Proficiency using Microsoft Office including Visio.</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver's license</li>
<li>Must maintain car insurance with company acceptable limits</li>
<li>Ability to travel to client sites using own vehicle, several times each week</li>
<li>Willingness to travel up to 50% <b>Compensation and Benefits:</b>
</li>
<li>Competitive salary.</li>
</ul>
<ul>

<li>401(k) with company match.</li>

</ul>
