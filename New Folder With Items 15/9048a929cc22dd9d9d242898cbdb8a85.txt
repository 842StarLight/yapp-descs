<p><b>About the Role</b></p>
<p>
The Merchandise Planning Manager DTC is responsible for delivering a best-in-class customer experience through the strategic management of inventory for a division within the DTC Channel. This role will partner with the Merchandising, Sourcing, and E-commerce teams to maximize sales and in-stocks, optimize inventory turn, and drive profitable growth. This position reports into an Omni Planning Director, leads a team of planners, and is responsible for meeting divisional sales, inventory and margin goals as outlined in the financial budget.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You're excited about this opportunity because you will...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Pre-Season Planning</li>
<ul>
<li>Assist Director in developing the annual, tops-down merchandise plans for the division.</li>
<li>Oversee the creation of pre-season, item-level sales plans that align with product rankings, merchandising strategies and tops-down financial goals.</li>
<li>Support team in designing inventory strategies that balance the composition of new and core, carry-over merchandise and that support in-stock and turn objectives.</li>
<li>Manage divisional open-to-buy. Ensure that all departments fit within the bounds of the division's receipt budget and allocated DC capacity.</li>
<li>Leverage historical data to identify key learnings around product, pricing, and inventory and apply learnings to future seasons.</li>
</ul>
<li>In-Season Planning</li>
<ul>
<li>Lead monthly open-to-buy activities for the division. Identify and execute near term actions to achieve sales goals and budgeted inventory EOMs.</li>
<li>Oversee in-season, item level forecasting and ad-hoc analysis to identify inventory-related risks and opportunities for chase. Ensure alignment with tops-down, monthly forecast.</li>
<li>Recommend divisional strategies around promotional pricing and end-of-life disposition. Ensure that departmental actions maximize sales and margin and balances inventory for total division.</li>
<li>Identify business trends that warrant analytical research provide framework for teams to investigate.</li>
</ul>
<li>Leadership &amp; Collaboration</li>
<ul>
<li>Play a leadership role in creating a people-first culture that is innovative, results-oriented, and that celebrates success.</li>
<li>Oversee the career and skill development of a team of Merchandise Planners with a focus on training, retention, and building bench strength.</li>
<li>Play an active role in recruiting, hiring, and onboarding of new team members.</li>
<li>Balance and prioritize team's workload to ensure they are set up for success.</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-channel planning partner to create a seamless brand experience and ensure customer is at the center of everything we do.</li>
<li>Develop strong cross-functional partnerships with Merchandising, Sourcing, and E-commerce teams.</li>
<li>Participate in the development of scalable planning tools, processes, and reporting and encourage sharing of best-practice to ensure consistency.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</ul>
