<p>Scientific Applications Specialist –Akoya Biosciences, Inc. Located in Menlo Park, CA and
</p>
<p>Hopkinton, MA, Akoya is a well-funded and growing company with industry leading
</p>
<p>technologies for tissue analysis. Akoya was founded in 2015 to commercialize the CODEX®
</p>
<p>multiparameter tissue analysis technology developed in the lab of Dr. Garry Nolan at Stanford
</p>
<p>University. In addition, the company recently announced their acquisition of PerkinElmer’s
</p>
<p>Phenoptics portfolio. This acquisition and the combination of the two product portfolios and
</p>
<p>teams establishes Akoya as a leading provider of quantitative tissue biomarker evaluation, from
</p>
<p>discovery through translational and clinical research. The company is in the midst of rapid
</p>
<p>commercial growth with immediate need for a Scientific Applications Specialist. This position
</p>
<p>will be headquartered out of our Menlo Park office.

<p>As the commercial representative of the CODEX Applications team you will be responsible for

</p>
<p>areas of immune-oncology, immunology and other research areas. A successful candidate will
</p>
<p>bring a strong passion for pioneering methods in next-generation tools for quantitative
</p>
<p>multiplexed imaging analysis, strong business acumen and the ability to perform in a rapidly
</p>
<p>moving environment.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Strong collaborative skills—</b>Drive key collaborations with the R&amp;D team in demonstrating the

<p>utility of Akoya’s technology culminating in strong scientific demonstration
</p>
<p><b>Scientific content creation</b> –Serve as primary developer of customer facing scientific content
</p>

</p>
<p>the sales collateral

<p><b>Clear presentation capabilities</b> -Serve as the scientific representative in customer

<p><b>Commercial Team Engagement—</b>Engage directly with commercial representatives including
