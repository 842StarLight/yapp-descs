<div>As the Jordan Retail Brand Manager - territories, you’re a critical component to the team. You will partner with and work across a matrix organization including: (Jordan &amp; Nike) Brand, Brand Design, Digital Brand and Brand Communications &amp; Integrated Retail Marketing teams.<br>

<div>Your mission is to create epic consumer experiences at retail across our different distribution channels that will both elevate our brand and drive commercial success. You will collaborate cross-functionally with Brand, Merchandising, Commerce, and Integrated territory teams to create a seamless retail experience for the Jordan consumer.<br>

<div></div>
<br>
<div>You will execute against the Jordan North America retail brand strategy and oversee execution of the Jordan Brand consumer experience at retail, including in-store environment, sales associate training, seasonal marketing campaign executions, as well as focused territory activations &amp; events.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>

You will lead an integrated territory approach and process that leads with Jumpman Retail, Direct to Consumer, serves North America wholesale and includes in-store, digital, social, outbound communication, and grass-root / sport moment events.<br>

<div>You are a Manager that drives a flawless execution across the territory with your teammates. You will work on and across category and cross-functional teams to support Jordan Brand initiatives.
