<h2>Editor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Running Portfolio <b>Editor</b> is responsible for the strategic development development and creation of content supporting Running Insight Magazine, The Running Event, and Best Running Stores. The Editor will become the voice in the specialty run industry and is expected to work independently to create/source relevant content as well as work in collaboration with sales and marketing and the rest of the Running Portfolio team. The Editor is also responsible for sourcing and managing freelance and contract contributors and working within a budget to meet financial goals.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop an expertise in the products’ audience (running specialty stores and race directors) and its customers (the brands that seek to influence the running stores), as well as ancillary partners (associations, racing groups, etc.)</li>
<li>Leverage expertise to source, write, edit, publish content for the products, specifically:
<ul>
<li>Tracks, sessions, workshops, topics and speakers for all events</li>
<li>All copy published within Running Insight Magazine</li>
<li>Webinars, podcasts, white papers, etc.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop content strategies based around popular industry topics, trends and keywords</li>
<li>Strategically plan and manage the editorial content calendar to create and maintain synergy across the Running brand family and supporting marketing and sales efforts to expand the audience and increase revenue</li>
<li>Connect with the brands to produce sponsored content as contracted by the sales team, which can be in support of their presence at the TRE/BRS or as part of a dedicated digital sponsorship</li>
<li>Work jointly with Web services and Marketing Manager to capture and analyze page view analytics to identify market trends, audience needs, and advertising opportunities</li>
<li>Source and maintain positive professional relationships with authors, industry experts, and other internal and external contacts. Conduct interviews and publish articles on same</li>
<li>Continuously build freelance staff to broaden bench strength of experts (experienced writers in running industry).</li>
<li>Work closely with the Event Director on execution improvement and business development creating product and revenue generating ideas to achieve financial growth goals</li>
<li>Represent TRE/RI at our own events and other industry events</li>
<li>Cultivate the social media presence of the Running Portfolio engaging with the community on various platforms</li>
<li>Collaborate with the entire team to pass along relevant insights and updates as they pertain to industry trends and opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge: </b>Bachelor’s Degree or higher in related field, 5+ years of industry experience. Applicants need to be self-starters who have excellent communication skills and are open to collaboration. Expectations will be to track the competitive landscape to identify trends and opportunities for new content, potential sponsorships and marketing initiatives.</p>
<p><b>
Skills:</b> Excellent copywriting, editing, presentation and networking skills are required. Applicants need to be proficient in MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook. Knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite products is preferred.</p>
<p><b>
Abilities: </b>Motivated, deadline-driven, self-starter. Understands principles of content strategy and data analysis, able to understand and translate industry observations into achievable goals for content development, digital revenue, and conference and event attendance.</p>
<p><b>
Supervisory Responsibility:</b> While there are no direct reports to this position, the Editor will be responsible for sourcing and managing freelance and contract contributors including providing editorial direction and managing payments.</p>
<p><b>
Travel: </b>Applicants will need to attend TRE and BRS events and other industry gatherings as assigned; publish event coverage during and after the events. Applicants should be prepared to speak and present at these events as needed or requested by management and/or organizers.</p>
</div></div>
