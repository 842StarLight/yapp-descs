<h2>Product Owner, Interactions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Invesco is one of the world's leading global investment managers, entrusted with managing $1.2 trillion* in assets on behalf of clients worldwide. We are the 6th largest US retail asset manager and the 13th largest investment manager globally, and our more than 8,000 employees worldwide are dedicated to delivering an investment experience that helps people get more out of life. We are purely focused on managing a comprehensive range of active, passive and alternative investment capabilities, which we draw on to provide customized solutions aligned to client needs, our most important benchmark. (*As of May 31,2019)</div>
<br>
<div>
<b>Job Purpose (Job Summary)</b><b>:
</b><br>
The Product Owner - Interactions is part of Invesco's Insights, Analytics, and Marketing organization working across Invesco's retail and institutional businesses in the Americas. The Product Owner - Interactions is within the Insights &amp; Analytics and Client Journey Management team, developing a deep understanding of our various client touchpoints to drive our platforms and capabilities across analytics, sales, marketing and other enterprise functions. The Product Owner - Interactions will deliver on our roadmap for client touchpoint data. They will partner with a technology scrum team, data management and data governance to ensure data is structured and curated to leverage across the enterprise.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This individual:</div>
<p></p>
<ul>

Must be comfortable working in an agile development environment.</li>

Have an ability to handle multiple requests from varied stakeholders, in a way that maintains clear priority, and ability to adjust when needed.</li>

Have an inquisitive and innovative mindset with a demonstrated ability to recognize opportunities to create distinctive value.</li>

Demonstrate an ability to build relationships, collaborate, mentor, motivate, and influence internal and external teams.</li>

<div>
<b>Key Responsibilities / Duties</b><b>:
</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Manages a product backlog for the Agile team</li>
<li>Works with key stakeholders to establish an ordered product backlog</li>
<li>Ensures backlog items are clearly understood by the scrum team</li>
<li>Optimizes the value of the work delivered by the scrum team</li>
<li>Ensures the product backlog is visible, transparent and clear to all stakeholders</li>
<li>Identifies and assigns business value to work in the product backlog</li>
<li>Ensures risks and issues are addressed in a clear and transparent manner</li>
<li>Serves as a liaison between stakeholders and Agile team:</li>
<li>Builds strong relationships with business, data management and technology partners</li>
<li>Drives the discovery of high value business deliverables</li>
<li>Develops, maintains and grows knowledge of customer’s business</li>
<li>Serves as a point of escalation for all stakeholders (business, data management and technology)</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains strong cross-organizational partnerships both internally and externally with third-party vendors, partners and clients</li>
<br>
</ul>
