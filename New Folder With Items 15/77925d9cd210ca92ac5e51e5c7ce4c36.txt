<div><b>Job Description:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The Contracts Process Leader role takes a holistic view of the NAAC business and its evolving contracting ecosystem to recommend, develop, build, and maintain contracting templates, policies, and practices designed to meet the evolving needs of the business. Key to success is the ability to understand the needs of the business to drive quality, speed, and consistency into our contracting framework, and to translate the understanding into value add knowledge capital contributions for the benefit of the business. The job requires the ability to partner and work collaboratively with a diverse cross functional team of stakeholders, including Solution Architects, Delivery resources, Sellers, Deal Desk analysts, Deal Management, Legal, GRM, and Global Business Practices among others, to create and deliver contributions that drive speed and value for the business. The role also supports a variety of activities across the NAAC, including support for advising on approvals, quoting, and other critical process areas that enable the effective operation of our business and the efficient and responsible completion of deals. The role will support activities such as contract template improvement, establishment of processes and documentation to support more effective engagement with our partner ecosystem, changes to the CAM to improve approval quality and speed, streamlining contract approval processes, etc. and associated training on work product for the benefit of the business.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Contracts Process Leader is responsible for the exercise of independent judgement in making key contracting and sales process improvement decisions in coordination with NAAC management that impact both our internal business and that of our customers. This includes making informed decisions based on experience, subject matter expertise, outputs from toolsets, OCS / Oracle policy, and various customer specific factors. These decisions impact overall customer contracting strategy that drives the profitability line for NAAC and the ability to efficiently and responsibly close business, and requires consideration of items such as contractual terms and conditions, contract template structure, approval workflow and processes, etc. The Contracts Process Leader is required to serve as an independent decision maker to protect the contractual interests of the business and to securing buy-in and support for the applicable processes on the part of business users.</div>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This remote-based position works collaboratively with the NAAC team as well as other Oracle organizations to improve and drive quality into our contracting and sales processes. Candidates must be based in the North American Region.</p>
