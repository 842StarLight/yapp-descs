<div>
<p><b>Who are we?<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Founded in 1924, Irving Oil is a family-owned company with operations throughout Eastern Canada, New England and Ireland with a focus on providing exceptional service and high-quality products to our customers. Every day, we strive to put our good energy in action to bring families together, support the environment and local wildlife, and to create learning opportunities that empower the next generation.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Here's what you can expect from us:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>When you're a part of our team, you'll see how we bring good energy to our business and our employees. Together, we will support our corporate strategy, high standards and the communities where we live and work. Our collaborative approach, commitment to diversity and inclusion along with our safety-first culture helps reinforce our internal brand position where People Matter. That's why we're dedicated to the development of our employees, so that they can reach their career goals.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our Retail POS Team is growing and we are currently seeking a Field Project Lead. As a customer service oriented Point of Sale (POS) Specialist you will be providing technical support to users in an efficient and accurate manner. You will assist in solving basic to advanced technical problems and provide support for all assigned areas within the Help Desk department. The goal is to make sure that customer value is maintained to the standards set forth by the company.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are seeking candidates that want to grow as professionals, but have a solid technology knowledge base and positive attitudes. Background in point of sale technology and/or software training is a plus. The successful candidate will interact with people frequently, through phone, email and face-to-face meetings.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This role will be based out of Portsmouth, New Hampshire, and requires some travel.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and support field implementation of new POS technology.
</li>
<li>Train site personnel on the use of the new technology.
</li>
<li>Provide install and technical support to technicians and employees.
</li>
<li>Build and maintain fully operational technology labs to facilitate product release, troubleshooting, and training.
</li>
<li>Log, track, troubleshoot, and resolve open incidents through incident management tools.
</li>
<li>Provide timely communications around product release.
</li>
<li>Manage software licensing, hardware deployment, and associated upgrades.
</li>
<li>Troubleshoot network, hardware and software issues.
</li>
<li>Work with vendors and third parties to resolve hardware, networking, and software issues.
</li>
<li>Create, implement and manage assigned field service projects to include associated resources.
</li>
<li>Develop installation process documentation
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and Experience
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or other IT Technical discipline
</li>
<li>Experience with POS technology an asset.
</li>
<li>Experience with loyalty software an asset.
</li>
<li>Retail IT experience preferred.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Additional Job Description
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Training and practical understanding of network solutions, cyber security and site level integration of technology solutions
</li>
<li>Functional knowledge of project management disciplines
</li>
<li>Desire to work in a customer facing environment with a strong emphasis on customer satisfaction and relationship management
</li>
<li>Prior practical application of knowledge would be considered a plus
</li>
<li>Prior experience and/or knowledge within Petroleum point-of-sale systems or Retail IT would be extremely advantageous
</li>
</ul>
