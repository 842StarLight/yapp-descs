<h2>District Manager - Western Division</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>At PGA TOUR Superstore, we’re always looking for enthusiastic, self-motivated, flexible individuals who will share our passion for helping transform our business. As one of the fastest growing specialty retailers, we’re dedicated to hiring selfless team players from different backgrounds to influence the growth of our organization.
</p>
<p>Part of the Arthur M. Blank Family of Businesses, PGA TOUR Superstore continuously strives to create a family culture for our Associates – driven by our vision to inspire people through golf and tennis.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The District Manager is responsible for the implementation and sustainability of all programs directed through store operations. Follows up on the implementation of operating programs and procedures consistent with PGATSS broad policies and objectives and insures adequate execution. Appraises and evaluates the results of store operations for their region regularly and systematically. Ensures process improvements are in place to enhance customer service levels while supporting organizational profit targets.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>The District Manager leads their store operational activities of the organization in accordance with policies and goals established by the President/C.E.O. Inspects execution of programs crossing over the following functions and/or business units: operations, loss prevention, labor management, store services/golf instruction, new store deployment/set up and associate training and development.
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Ensures company standards are uniformly met and adhered to across all stores within the assigned district.
</li>
<li>Lead, coach, develop and retain PGATSS store teams with an emphasis on developing capacity in strategic analysis, identifying opportunities and developing plans and programs to achieve organizational goals.
</li>
<li>Provides guidance and feedback to General Managers based on analysis of implementation of processes, weekly payroll reporting, and information relative to sales reporting.
</li>
<li>Directs General Managers to achieve budgeted results and other financial criteria, and to preserve the capital funds invested in the enterprise.
</li>
<li>Directs implementation of organizational procedures and controls, to promote communication and prompt information flow, thereby solidify management control and direction of the enterprise.
</li>
<li>Participates in the development and preparation of short-term plans and annual store sales and payroll budgets.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills Required:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Education: The knowledge, skills, and abilities typically acquired through the completion of a G.E.D. or High School diploma. BS/BA degree – preferred
</li>
<li>Experience: 5 years of experience in a management role and a strong track record in developing talent, implementing programs and prioritizing resources.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong leadership with the aptitude to develop and mentor associates to grow to support organizational needs. Ability to execute organizational direction by communicating inspiring goals to associates and following up on execution of programs and initiatives.
</li>
<li>Ability to organize multiple priorities to ensure that key processes and procedures are implemented and that resources are properly allocated to meet organizational goals.
</li>
<li>Analytic and decisive decision maker with the ability to prioritize and communicate to staff key objectives and tactics necessary to achieve organizational goals.
</li>
<li>Background in profit and loss management and ability to identify business trends and develop strategies to address opportunities.
</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills; a persuasive and passionate communicator with excellent public speaking skills.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Working Conditions and Physical Demands:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>The District Manager frequently travels to store location in their market and must be able to stand for extended periods of time while visiting.
</li>
<li>Must be able to accommodate a typical retail schedule, work a flexible work week, and work nights, weekends, and holidays.
</li>
</ul>
