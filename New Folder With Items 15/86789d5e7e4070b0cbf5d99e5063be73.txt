<p><b>SOCIAL MEDIA AND GRAPHIC DESIGN INTERN </b></p>
<p>Cinderella Bridez is seeking a Social Media and Graphic Design Intern to contribute to our marketing strategy team. The intern will assist in creating for our social media platforms; creating relevant, well-branded content, interacting with followers, engaging with like-minded organizations, and reporting analytics. The intern will make recommendations regarding ways that Cinderella Bridez can leverage our social media platforms, including designing new campaigns and discussing ways to partner with other organizations. The intern will also assist in the design and creation of printed materials, website graphics, and promotional advertising using his/her knowledge of graphic design.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate is someone who has a heart for Beauty &amp; Fashion and has studied or has experience in the realm of marketing, social media, and/or graphic design. The candidate should have a strong understanding of how organizations utilize social media to engage with followers and increase awareness and sales, as well as an eye for detail in regards to graphic design work.</p>
<p>Required:</p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of social media marketing</li>
<li>2+ years experience in Graphic Design</li>
<li>Strong communication skills both verbal and written</li>
<li>Strong people and organizational skills</li>
<li>Highly organized and detail-oriented</li>
<li>Must be able to travel on site and/or work virtually</li>
<li>Eye for photography a plus.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Fashion &amp; Beauty a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p>*Internship is unpaid.</p>
