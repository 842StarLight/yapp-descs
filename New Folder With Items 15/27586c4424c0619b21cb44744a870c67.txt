<h2>Applications Systems Analyst</h2>

<div><div>Responsible for analyzing, designing, configuring, supporting, maintaining and modifying all programming systems to support the company’s application systems. Responsibility includes the integration of software and hardware across multiple supported systems. Works closely with the IS Department to maintain a competitive edge with leading technologies and execute goals identified in line with our company goals.
<br>
<br>
<b>WHAT YOU GET TO DO</b>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Provide system level support on the company’s multi user operating systems, software, hardware and networks
</li>
<li>Maintain, review, analyze, modify, document and support all programming systems to support the company’s application systems
</li>
<li>Responsible for defining business requirements and designing programs/applications and processes to meet users’ needs
</li>
<li>Test, debug, document, install and implement all new modified applications and systems
</li>
<li>Familiar with relational databases and client server concepts
</li>
<li>Familiar with a wide variety of the field’s concepts, practices and procedures in information systems
</li>
<li>Suggest short and long term new technologies based on company goals
</li>
<li>May be required to flex hours to support all areas of the business
</li>
<li>Additional duties as assigned by management
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>WHAT WE VALUE</b>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Advanced computer skills
</li>
<li>Must be able to work well independently and in the context of team environments
</li>
<li>Ability to organize and manage multiple tasks
</li>
<li>Must have the ability to make recommendations to effectively resolve problems or issues, by using judgment that is consistent with company policies, practices and within the law
</li>
<li>Must possess professional presentation skills
</li>
<li>Effective at working cross functionally
</li>
<li>Advanced verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>WHAT WE EXPECT FROM YOU</b>
<br>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, related field or equivalent experience required
</li>
<li>Experience of one (1) to three (3) years of information systems experience required

<li>Experience in a distribution and/or sales environment preferred</li>
</ul>
