<h2>Marketing Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>We are hiring for a Marketing Operations Manager to help build the backbone of our multi-channel marketing engine. The role entails three broad themes: Systems, Analytics, and Lead Management. Reporting to the Head of Marketing you will manage and maintain our marketing automation platform, (HubSpot) ensuring all business, technical, and reporting/analysis requirements are met for inbound marketing programs-from email nurture to website responses to the chat bot experience. You will work closely with sales operations to monitor, measure, and make recommendations that improve lead progression, velocity, and pipeline creation.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
What Success Looks Like:</b></div>
<div><b>
In 3 months…</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Help design and execute on a comprehensive, multi-channel, multi-segment, marketing strategy to drive demand generation initiatives, track success and find ways to optimize performance.</li>
<li>Manage marketing automation software (Hubspot), develop workflows, CTAs, landing pages, campaigns (outbound emails, webinar invites, event support email, customer emails), and multi-segment nurture tracks.</li>
<li>Partner with Sales Ops to ensure lead flow from top of funnel to sales is effective and efficient by measuring the success of marketing programs from lead creation to pipeline to closed won stages.</li>
<li>Define key marketing metrics and manage the process of packaging these measurements into reports and dashboards.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
In 6 months…</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Build an attribution model that tracks and analyzes which marketing activities turn MQLs into Sales Opportunities, with the best return on investment.</li>
<li>Utilize A/B testing and optimize marketing automation, landing page, and lead nurturing concept to continually refine and improve conversion.</li>
<li>Working with sales ops, build best-practices such as data cleanliness, naming conventions, core program templates, and core program channels and tags.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
In 12 months…</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Run monthly/quarterly/annual marketing metrics reviews, program ROI, pipeline attribution and creation.</li>
<li>Drive collaboration across the Marketing Operations, Business Systems and Business Intelligence teams to identify inefficient processes and apply automation technology and best practices to solve them.</li>
<li>Own Marketing data quality (including data append and deduplication) through systematic database cleansing, leveraging data collection best practices and vendor engagements.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
What You Need:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years experience in Marketing Operations preferably working alongside Demand Generation teams</li>
<li>Must possess expert knowledge in the HubSpot Marketing Hub platform; HubSpot Certification in CRM, Campaigns, Email, Forms, Landing Pages, and Reports strongly preferred</li>
<li>Familiarity with Salesforce creating reports and dashboards</li>
<li>Experience collaborating with Sales teams and Sales Operations to build automated lead flow and lead management processes including Lead Nurturing, Reporting, and Lead Routing.</li>
<li>Experience building out operational processes to support Account Based Marketing (ABM)</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree...Concentration in Marketing a plus</li>
</ul>
