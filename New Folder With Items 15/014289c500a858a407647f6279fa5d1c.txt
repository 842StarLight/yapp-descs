<h2>Customer Care Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>JOB TITLE: Customer Care Specialist DEPARTMENT: Customer Care REPORTS TO: Customer Care Manager TERMS: Full Time</p>
<p>General Function: The primary function of the Customer Care Specialist is to provide information and assistance in response to a variety of customer inquiries, tactfully resolve customer concerns and contribute to maintaining Western CPE Customer Care as a competitive advantage. The success of this primary function is dependent on a CCS’s ability to demonstrate active listening and build relationships, knowledge of products and services, understanding of customer expectations and belief in the long term vision of Western CPE.</p>
<p>The CCS position requires the ability to effectively communicate with customers through incoming calls, outbound campaigns, email and live chat. Successful CCS’s are able to impact customers, and the company as a whole, by identifying opportunities, using available resources to solve problems and applying policies in a practical way.</p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities:  * Conduct outbound calls for service and revenue initiatives  * Answer incoming phone calls, live chats and emails in compliance with service standards  * Contribute to customer retention by providing exceptional, memorable and personal service  * Effectively use available resources to solve problems, answer questions and address complaints  * Place orders, complete registrations and provide technical assistance  * Assist customers in the navigation of our website and other administrative tasks  * Effectively balance multiple, and often competing, priorities  * Secure and maintain a full understanding of Western CPE services and correctly utilize Western CPE promotional codes, sales tools, and forms  * Document all customer contact in company database  * Work collaboratively with all staff and departments within WCPE to ensure proper delivery of products and services  * Participate in additional duties and responsibilities as assigned by Customer Care Manager  * Adhere to Western CPE policies as outlined in Employee Manual and operational procedures set forth by management  * Actively participate in team meetings and departmental planning  * Maintain professional demeanor and contribute to a positive work environment  * Demonstrate good judgement and decision-making in customer interactions  * Provide feedback to management team to promote constant growth and improvement in the department  * Support the production department as needed.  * Other duties as assigned</p>
<p>Qualifications:  * Minimum Experience: 1+ years of experience in a similar customer service role  * Minimum Education: High School diploma with college level education in business or education, or related experience.  * Knowledge/Skills/Abilities: Proficient in computer systems and data processing software; detail oriented, data entry accuracy and database management. Must be energetic, organized, flexible, ability to manage multiple projects, ability to understand general financial data, ability to adapt knowledge to various industries and development scenarios, ability to coordinate project team members, and excellent written/verbal communication skills.  * Other Skills/Abilities: Enjoys interacting with people; ability to contribute positively to a team and to encourage firm development efforts; effectively communicate division vision. Must be reliable with a positive attitude.</p>
<p>This job description in no way states or implies that these are the only duties to be performed by the employee in this position. Employee will be required to follow any other job-related instructions and to perform any other job-related duties requested by any person authorized to give instructions or assignments.</p>
