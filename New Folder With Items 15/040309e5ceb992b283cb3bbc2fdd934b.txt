<p><b>
ABOUT THE POSITION</b></p>
<p>
The Mechanical Designer is responsible for learning and designing mechanical systems from conceptual sketches<br>
through full design and construction, with oversight and training from intermediate and senior staff.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES &amp; SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Production of drawings based on markups from intermediate/senior design staff</li>
<li>
Gain knowledge and ability to read drawings for all disciplines</li>
<li>
Work with manufacturer's sales representatives to get equipment selections based on engineering criteria</li>
<li>
provided by intermediate/senior design staff</li>
<li>
Implement BIM standards and ensure they are being followed throughout drawing production</li>
<li>
Assist intermediate design staff in compiling content of design reports and presentations</li>
<li>
Layout of piping and ductwork based on guidance and oversight from intermediate/senior design staff</li>
<li>
Coordinate mechanical requirements with other trades with assistance provided by intermediate/senior staff</li>
<li>
Provide construction administration services as directed and supervised by senior staff</li>
<li>
Customize equipment and installation details to project needs with oversight from intermediate design staff</li>
<li>
Perform engineering calculations necessary to support the design of HVAC and plumbing systems with guidance</li>
<li>
and oversight from intermediate/senior design staff</li>
<li>
Gain understanding of building geometries, envelope characteristics, and internal load profiles for input into load</li>
<li>
calculation software</li>
<li>
Initial review of construction submittals and RFIs</li>
<li>
Perform site visits with intermediate/senior design staff for documentation of existing systems and conditions</li>
<li>
Perform site visits with intermediate/senior design staff for review of system installation compliance with</li>
<li>
contract documents</li>
<li>
Detailed check of work prior to returning completed tasks to intermediate/senior design staff</li>
</ul>

QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BS Architectural Engineering, BS Mechanical Engineering degree required; or equivalent combination of relevant coursework and minimum<b> 2+ years' work experience required</b>
</li>
<li>
Revit experience is an asset</li>
<li>
Able to read Architectural, Structural, Mechanical, Civil and Electrical drawings with supervision</li>
<li>
Familiarity with load calculation software, such as Trace 700</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
LEED credential is desired</li>
<li>
EIT credential is preferred</li>
</ul>
