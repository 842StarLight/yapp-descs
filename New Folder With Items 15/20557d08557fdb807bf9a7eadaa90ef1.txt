<p><b>
Here's the Scoop:</b></p>
<p>
This role within our Talent Attraction team focused on growing our long term pipelines of talent. Our University Coordinator will support a regional territory of our operations, implement and measure our candidate attraction strategies focused on Nurse pipeline initiatives. This person will implement and execute internal and external employment brand activities primarily designed for student nurses and other strategic student populations.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Our University Coordinator will be working on:</b></p>
<p>
Campus to Careers:</p>
<p>
Identifying and developing nursing school partnerships, both regional and national relationships that add value to DaVita, the school and the students. Collaborating with field operators and recruiters on local market strategies. Executing campus events including coordinating, scheduling, staffing, ordering supplies, advertising, prepping and debriefing our Teammates to host these sessions. Our goal in Campus to Careers is not only to create awareness of nephrology as a career option, but to yield hires. We track and report out on our partnership progress, number of campus to career presentations set and hires on a monthly basis. This is a critical pipeline of talent as these are the hires of tomorrow.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Global RN Program Welcome and Belonging:</b></p>
<p>
This is a long term pipeline of dialysis experienced RN talent from around the Globe. Your role will be to help notify the local leader when a Global RN has arrived in country and help them execute the welcoming and belonging playbook for a positive teammate experience. So, you’ll not only help with educating student nurses on the front end, you’ll get to experience the beauty of placing long term arrivals on the other end. Its fulfilling work.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Other initiatives the University Coordinator will be working on include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Executing and implementing targeted sourcing methods for Student Nurses, sharing your expertise with the greater recruiting team.</li>
<li>
Aid in the curation of a social platform and/or community for Student Nurses. Your innovative ideas and creative strategy will help make this come to life.</li>
<li>
Tracking and measuring School of Nursing engagements and new nurse hires. Both your own and the recruiting team so we can improve our methods,</li>
<li>
One of the ways your success will be measured will be based on the quality and quantity of your campus efforts monthly. Success measures will include number of campus visits scheduled, number of offers extended as result of events and number of long term hires made in pipeline.</li>
<li>
Partner with a Corporate Recruiting location and business line to host campus hiring events for Corporate roles throughout the year.</li>
<li>
Supporting long term pipelines of talent for PCT roles, as needed.</li>
</ul>
