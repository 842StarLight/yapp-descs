<h2>Project Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<div>We are looking for an exceptionally disciplined and organized <b>Project Coordinator</b> that can provide critical operational support to our Onboarding team. You should have exceptional project management skills and a track record of managing complex projects with many moving parts and owners. E-learning or LMS experience is preferred, but not required.</div>
<br>
<div>As a Project Coordinator, you will be responsible for supporting our Onboarding Success Managers to deliver a highly rated onboarding experience to help our customers launch their learning products on our platform ahead of any critical launch dates while delivering on any signed Statements of Work (SOWs).</div>
<br>
 <div></div>
<div><b>You will specifically be expected to:</b></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Help our Onboarding Success Managers create strong project plans with our customers to ensure goals are clearly defined, key stakeholders are identified, critical events and milestones are captured, and a launch dates are agreed to prior to kickof</li>
<li>Prepare cross functionally to deliver and execute a successful customer kickoff</li>
<li>Create, maintain, and manage a number of detailed customer onboarding project plans and training schedules to ensure our newest customers successfully launch and meet any critical milestones</li>
<li>Respond to all incoming communications from assigned customers in a timely manner</li>
<li>Ensure project plans align with the contracted project scope and account for any unique professional services or custom development SOWs</li>
<li>Manage time tracking to ensure customers are staying within their contracted number of project support hours</li>
<li>Help Onboarding Success Managers run successful customer meetings by sending meeting agendas to customers in advance, managing calendars, taking meeting minutes, and updating project plans on a consistent basis</li>
<li>Learn and remain current on the Thought Industries platform to support internal and external stakeholder communication</li>
<li>Develop a solid understanding of a customer’s core business use case, focusing on customer impact and customer business outcomes</li>
<li>Help enforce customers’ completion of project tasks to ensure timely launch</li>
<li>Help Onboarding Success Managers resolve customer escalations</li>
<li>Engage with cross functional teams with varying levels of seniority and experience to support various programs and client needs</li>
<li>Maintain a detailed, organized record of all customer conversations and meetings</li>
</ul>
 <div></div>
<div><b>You will be evaluated and measured on your ability to:</b></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Help our customers launch within 90-120 days</li>
<li>Drive high NPS and customer onboarding satisfaction scores</li>
<li>Limit customer kickbacks and escalations (customer churn)</li>
<li>Help create referenceable customers</li>
<li>Communicate in a timely and effective manner with internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>Anticipate and manage onboarding, project, and other churn risks</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with engineering, product, sales, and marketing to support our customers</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Desired Experience:</b></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of work experience in a customer-facing or supporting project management role at a technology company, preferably a SaaS and/or start up environment where business dynamics change quickly</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with an industry standard web-based project management system such as Jira, SmartSheets, BaseCamp, Asana etc.</li>
<li>Ability to receive and understand direction, ask questions when appropriate, and manage time to work effectively and independently</li>
<li>Tech-savvy, adaptable, and comfortable operating in a constantly changing work environment</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to efficiently and effectively respond to a large volume emails and Slack messages</li>
<li>Proven ability to establish and build effective working relationships, including successfully bringing calm to a tense situation</li>
<li>Knowledge of Salesforce, SmartSheets, Trello, Airtable and Slack required</li>
<li>E-learning experience a plus</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div></div>
