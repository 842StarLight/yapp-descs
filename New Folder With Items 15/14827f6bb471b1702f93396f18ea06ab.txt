<h2>CRM Product Manager Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">CRM Offering Lead- Director- 9521
<br><br>
<b>About our structure:</b><br>
Market Units are Avanade's GTM Model and are the areas clients most frequently seek our guidance during their digital transformation journey. Within our Market Units or MU's we have core Offerings, which are the solutions we deliver to our clients. By specializing in and formalizing these select Offerings, we can deepen our relationships with our clients across their entire business helping them compete in new areas, which in turn helps us to grow our market share.
<br><br>
As a Director, Regional Offering Lead for CRM, your growth opportunity is to set and execute the overall strategy, performance and business growth of our Digital Sales and Services Offerings offerings within your assigned geography. You’ll partner closely with both the Regional and Area Market Unit Lead to implement a strategy for offering creation and selection of investments. In this key role, you’ll focus on driving the offering every single day; serving as the “face” of the DSS offering internally and externally in your Region. You rally alongside Direct sales teams from education to ideation, to creation to delivery. The Digital Sales and Services Offering delivers a modern sales/service platform with improved end-to-end support for sales and service processes across office and mobile devices and of course are typically powered by Microsoft Dynamics CRM and key ISV partners.
<br><br>
In this role, you will manage full-spectrum, cross-offering solutions delivery for clients. You will partner with Talent Community Lead and Regional Capability Lead on resourcing and skill needs to maintain effective business operations, project outcomes, using the Global Delivery Network (GDN) as needed, to prepare our organization for market changes.
<br><br>
<b>Day-to-day, you will:</b><br>
Build and run your own business. You take pride in ownership of customized DSS solutions that result in measurable business value
<br>Support quality and client delivery issues for offerings and solutions in your Region
<br>Create &amp; run budget against assigned Area/Region metrics; provide forecasting and sales pipeline analysis to support processes
<br>Effectively position offering and cross-offering offerings within the Region
<br>Develop strong, effective relationships collaborating across the region and area
<br>You reliably and consistently collaborate to create/drive complex CRM offerings to improve business outcomes
<br>Partner with the global Center of Excellence (CoE) to nurture growth of your CRM offering and further advancing Avanade asset development
<br>You rally beside, educate and support Your direct selling teams from creating CRM solutions through proposal and deal closing
<br>About you
<br><br>
You love success and bring a collaborative leadership track record in developing, building long term, high value Direct clients. You’re gifted communicating within the matrix and your influencing and negotiation capabilities have helped you establish and grow solutions for key clients. Internally, you love advising leadership teams in the development and production of creative DSS solution offerings.
<br><br>
<b>In addition, your professional skillset will include:</b><br>
Experience and success with sales principles and practices, including business development, marketing, education/evangelism and complex channel management
<br>Effectively collaborate with peers in a large, matrixed organizations, including significant experience with off-shore teams and delivery
<br>Experience delivering solutions for client organizations through complex and creative shaping of projects
<br>You're an active listener always looking for ways to resolve more business problems by nurturing client partnerships. You strive to find the best and the right solution mix, not just yesterday's CRM solution.
<br>You excel at external client facing and project-based consulting. Your knowledge includes assessing and handling the opportunities and risks associated with large scale, complex projects for your enterprise clients
<br>You likely possess a Bachelor's degree in Business or technology-related field, or an equivalent combination of education and experience. An advanced degree or MBA is strongly preferred, particularly technical (IT, engineering, or related). You likely have about 7 years of relevant technology business leadership experience, including solution sales, creation, delivery and business management, and more than 5 years of proven people and/or business leadership experience, particularly within a consultancy.
