<div><b>What You Need To Know</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Assist in building a sales culture at the community level</li>
<li>Work with team to develop sales and marketing goals</li>
<li>Work with team to implement lead generation tactics</li>
<li>Monitor sales closing ratios and provide coaching to improve performance</li>
<li>Motivate the sales staff</li>
<li>Prepare and present report on sales and occupancy progress</li>
<li>Monitor sales and marketing plan execution</li>
<li>Active involvement in community outreach activities and events</li>
<li>Oversee the community advertising and promotional activities</li>
<li>Communicate with primary advertising agency on ongoing campaigns for assigned communities</li>
<li>Active participation in sales presentations to assist staff and community performance in direct sales whenever needed</li>
<li>Work cooperatively with operations staff to develop, implement and execute all areas pertaining to marketing and sales</li>
</ul></div>
<div>
<div><b>What We Are Looking For
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor’s Degree in Sales, Marketing, or a relevant discipline</li>
<li>A minimum of 3-5 years’ experience in the Senior Living industry including Rental Independent Living, Assisted Living and/or Memory Support</li>
<li>Marketing and sales oversight at a minimum of 3 communities.</li>
<li>Direct work experience that encompasses the entire sales and marketing process</li>
<li>Proven results in occupancy development</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Even Better</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Must demonstrate excellent verbal and written communication skills and have the ability to communicate and present in person, in writing, and on the telephone</li>
<li>Previous experience monitoring sales and marketing plans</li>
<li>Previous experience successfully achieving developed sales and marketing goals</li>
</ul>
