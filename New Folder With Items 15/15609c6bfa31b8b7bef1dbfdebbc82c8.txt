<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>
Dimension &amp; Scope:</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>This position is responsible for providing a full range of accounting support for the FAO clients. The incumbent will be responsible for timely and accurate preparation of customer invoicing, preparation of journal entries and reconciliation of assigned balance sheet accounts, execution of control and compliance activities, and ensuring on-time processing of payments and distribution of daily settlement advice statements to customers.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Principal Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Invoice revenues based on contract terms and ensure collection of funds</li>
<li><div>
Calculate and record monthly journal entries for revenues, cost of goods sold, and/or variable expenses</div></li>
<li><div>
Address customer inquiries and work closely with various Fuel Sales &amp; Marketing teams Commercial, Legal, and Field Personnel; research and resolve volumetric and pricing discrepancies</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist managers and Senior Accountants on various projects/tasks</div></li>
<li><div>
Works with accounting team to prepare monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reports/statements</div></li>
<li><div>
Analyzes income statement accounts and prepares monthly balance sheet reconciliations</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepares monthly journal entries</div></li>
</ul>
