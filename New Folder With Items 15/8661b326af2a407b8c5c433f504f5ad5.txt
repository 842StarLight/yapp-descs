<h2>Director of Product and Program Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Wyndham Destinations</b> is seeking a <b>Director of Product and Program Development</b> in Orlando, Florida. The Director, Product and Program Development works with multiple departments across Wyndham Vacation Clubs and Wyndham Destinations to strategize, lead and facilitate the launch of and enhancements to products and programs developed to support the company’s strategic pillars of customer obsession and best-in-class sales and marketing.<br>
<br>
This role will direct the overall strategy, development, design, and implementation of programs and products and partner with product strategy teams made up of participants from all areas of the business to achieve the desired results. This includes the management of product integration plans to ensure each department is driving for a consistent and timely integration for sales, marketing, servicing, and operations. This role will also partner closely with the project management office for execution and integration support of products and programs that require IT development and enterprise support.<br>
<br>
The director will be responsible for making recommendations and key business decisions on issues and timelines involving ongoing program benefits and making recommendations to senior management about future opportunities to maximize products/programs efficiencies. Responsible for the performance and oversight of new and ongoing programs while developing strong relationships within and outside of the organization to maximize results and meet the strategic pillars set forth by the company.<br>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Job responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Strategy and Identification</li>
<ul>
<li>
Develops the strategy and management of new timeshare products and/or programs, as well as enhancements or redevelopment of existing products or programs.</li>
<li>
Research opportunities and develop ideas to enrich product and/or programs by working with internal or external partners to build programs that will generate interest for our associates, guests and owners.</li>
<li>
Oversight of all product or program aspects – including but not limited to product or program communication plan, sales and marketing positioning, supporting collateral materials, customer servicing and the on-going management of the product or program. Develops program rollout strategy and ensures training materials are provided to marketing, sales, resort operations and servicing teams. Partner with the brand and publicity teams as relevant for external communication opportunities.</li>
<li>
Establishes integration plans and resource requirements (together with the project management office as relevant) for the products and/or programs and works with executive sponsors and senior leadership team to provide progress and program status. Ensures product teams work closely with legal, accounting, capital development, finance, training and development, brand, IT, consumer finance, sales operations, owner care, corporate communications and other functions.</li>
</ul>
<li>
Implementation</li>
<ul>
<li>
Facilitates and leads teams to achieve business outcomes.</li>
<li>
Leadership and oversight of activities and tasks among global program team members, other internal departments and client or vendor/subcontractor organizations as needed to meet product and program goals and ensure task completion is on schedule, within budget constraints, and of the appropriate quality standards for the product or program.</li>
<li>
Provide updates and timely communication to stakeholders and teams affected by program changes and project launches.</li>
<li>
Review and/or develop product and program concepts, campaign schedules and manage the review and approval process with internal and/or third party partners.</li>
<li>
Responsible for development, continued improvement, and implementation of all products and/or programs related print and digital sales and marketing materials.</li>
<li>
Responsible for partnership with Owner Services and Sales Development &amp; Training team to ensure consistent training, certification, testing and products and/or programs is administered to all functions who may communicate or deliver the product or program. Ensure regular updates of training materials.</li>
</ul>
<li>
Performance</li>
<ul>
<li>
Forecast and manage existing product or program budget. Develop a business case for new opportunities through the investment committee, senior leadership and finance.</li>
<li>
Oversight of performance evaluation of product/program results, operational costs and lessons learned at the appropriate level of detail for all levels of the organization, including key stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Distribute status and performance reports to senior leaders to ensure key milestones are being met.</li>
<li>
Implement identified strategies and opportunities based on lessons learned with the initial launch on future launches or rollouts.</li>
</ul>
</ul></div>
