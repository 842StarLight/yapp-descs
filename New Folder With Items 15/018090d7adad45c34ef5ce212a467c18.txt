<p><b>
Application Engineer (Display Signage)</b></p>
<p>
Job Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide extensive product sustaining engineering support to direct sales, channel partners and end customers.</li>
<li>Assist business development and sales team in creating customer specific solutions. This includes (but not limited to) pre-sales and post-sales technical assessments, feasibility testing, technical response to customer bids, RMA verification and on-site technical training. Support activities also include communication support of field problems and interaction with field sales personnel.</li>
<li>Work closely with R&amp;D, Product Management and Quality teams as necessary to resolve any technical issues that arise from the field.</li>
<li>Construct hardware and software demonstration prototypes to aid sales and marketing efforts. This includes rapid hardware prototype development, high-level application software coding and system integration.</li>
<li>Build and Maintain Engineering bug tracking system.</li>
<li>Travel: Approximately 20%. Participate in trade shows.</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or above in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering or equivalent field</li>
<li>2+ years experience for technical support of display products such as LCD panels</li>
<li>Ability to use a multi-meter and oscilloscope along with schematics in troubleshooting hardware problems.</li>
<li>Electronic skills to understand and contribute to design and debugging</li>
<li>Experience installing and solving problems related to X86 architecture systems</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills. Must be a self starter.</li>
<li>Fluent English</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years experience in hardware design or technical support in PC or related field</li>
<li>Experience and knowledge of LED components, thermal design considerations, optical designs, and lighting standards</li>
<li>3+ years field application engineer experience including 2+ years experience in display panels</li>
<li>Experience in engineering bug tracking system such as Redmine and Bugzilla</li>
<li>Experience in PC application coding using industry standard languages such as Visual Basic, C++ and assembly</li>
</ul>
<p>Work Authorization:</p>
<ul>
<li>To conform to U.S. Government export regulations (ITAR), applicant must be a U.S. citizen, lawful permanent resident of the U.S., protected individual as defined by 8. U.S.C. 1324b(a)(3), or eligible to obtain the required authorizations from the U.S. Department of State. More information can be found at https://www.pmddtc.state.gov/regulations_laws/itar.html</li>
</ul>
