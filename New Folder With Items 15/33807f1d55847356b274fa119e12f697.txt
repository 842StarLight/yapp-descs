<h2>Project Manager - PM</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>AV Chicago/Edge ShowTek is a full-service production, rental, and staging company. We are seeking an experienced Project Manager. This is a salaried position which may involve evening and weekend <i>work</i> as projects dictate.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate <i>for</i> this role is experienced in audiovisual project management, or an experienced audio/video engineer who feels confident enough to manage numerous events simultaneously, interact <i>with</i> clients, and provide technical and creative support to AVC/Edge’s sales team. The Project Manager position is very social and involves constant communication <i>with</i> producers, clients, venues, contract employees, and vendors.</p>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years experience in AV events industry</li>
<li>Excellent technical aptitude and understanding of industry technologies and trends</li>
<li>Proficient in audio, video, lighting, rigging and set design</li>
<li>Very strong organization skills</li>
<li>Excellent communication and client interaction abilities</li>
<li>Business attire</li>
<li>Drafting capabilities (especially Vectorworks) are recommended</li>
<li>3D rendering capabilities (i.e. 3ds Max) are recommended</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Sales Support:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Providing technical support and design to sales team during quoting process</li>
<li>Creating room layouts and diagrams</li>
<li>Contacting prospective venues to determine rules and requirements <i>for</i> outside vendors</li>
<li>Maintaining well-organized folders of information <i>about</i> various <i>jobs</i>
</li>
<li>Assisting sales staff to keep projects on budget</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Operations Support:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identifying potential problems in event plans and recommending solutions</li>
<li>Scouting venues</li>
<li>Using project management software (Podio) to maintain information <i>about</i> events</li>
<li>Developing production schedules</li>
<li>Assisting operations staff to schedule personnel</li>
</ul>
</div>
