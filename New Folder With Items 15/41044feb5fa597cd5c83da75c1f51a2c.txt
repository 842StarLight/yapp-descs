<div><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></div>
<div>Competitive Inside Sales Representative position that plays a fundamental role in achieving ambitious customer acquisition and revenue growth objects for Verst Logistics’ Packaging division as well as;</div>
<div></div>
<div>Develops and closes new business opportunities through lead generation and customer development. Will be assigned annual packaging revenue goals with the expectation to meet or exceed sales quotas. May occasionally sell other Verst services including transportation, fulfillment, and warehousing as part of the packaging turnkey solution. Develops new customer relationships while also selling into existing customer relationships.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Prospects for new business by making multiple outbound telephone calls daily in order to sell the services of Verst Packaging.</li>
<li>Researches accounts, identifies key players, generates interest and develops relationships.</li>
<li>Conducts follow up from web form fills, advertising inquires or from internal referrals.</li>
<li>Maintains Dynamics CRM database.</li>
<li>Creates, follows and documents a call cadence to assure prospects get regular contact.</li>
<li>Prospects by working with channel partners, executing marketing campaigns, following up on email campaigns, qualifying prospects, and closing deals with new and existing customers.</li>
<li>Ensures all required management reports, bid packages and customer requests are submitted on time and with complete information.</li>
<li>Provides prospects with financial quotes and information relative to projects secured.</li>
<li>Works closely with other internal departments and maintains relationships with internal decision-makers.</li>
<li>Ability to learn the value proposition for Verst Packaging services and position with prospects.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>JOB SPECIFICATIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>College degree in Business or a related field, required</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years’ experience with inside or outside sales or related experience dealing with selling or interacting with customers in a business industry, required. Experience selling services/solutions, preferred.</li>
<li>Good written and verbal communication skills and strong phone communication and phone etiquette skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to gather data from multiple sources and manage projects to drive results.</li>
<li>Must be able to manage and organize multiple priorities in a fast-paced, high volume environment.</li>
<li>Proficient computer skills using Microsoft Office and software applications, and accurate typing skills (25 WMP minimum).</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience following a sales process, making numerous phone calls per day, and achieving or exceeding sales quotas in a fast-paced, high volume environment.</li>
<li>Demonstrated time management and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Able to learn new platforms and technical aspects of the Packaging business, as well as, develop technical knowledge in the areas of Logistics, Fulfillment, and Packaging.</li>
<li>Requires strong customer orientation and excellent interpersonal and communication skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated bias for action, ability to make a compelling business case, and ability to close sales.</li>
<li>Ability to utilize and learn all relevant business software and reporting tools.</li>
</ul>
