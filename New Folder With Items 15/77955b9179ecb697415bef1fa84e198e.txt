<h2>External Mortgage Loan Originator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>EXTERNAL MORTGAGE LOAN ORIGINATOR</b></p>
<div><b>Role</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Originates first and second mortgages. Solicits new mortgage business from external sources such as realtors, builders and other referral sources. Maintains outside working relationships, and promotes the organization's products and services.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Major Duties and Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Originates mortgage loans in applicable software system, provides accurate disclosures and obtains proper signatures following regulatory timeline requirements. Attends title company closing, as needed, or closes loans directly with the member.</li>
<li>Identifies cross-sell opportunities and educates members on credit union products and services. Meets required annual cross sales expectations on an ongoing basis. Follows up on internal referrals. Maintains and strengthens relationships with existing member base. Follows up with members after closing to identify additional needs or to answer questions. Returns member phone calls and emails the same day, but no later than the next business day, after the message is received. Keeps members and realtors up to date on the status of their loan application.</li>
<li>Mortgage leads to come from external sources which include realtors, builders and other referral sources. This position is responsible for lead generation from outside calling on realtors, builders and referral sources and is expected to maintain membership in outside networking associations such as ECBOR, BAEC and others.</li>
<li>Obtains all necessary financial information from each member at applications. Performs initial review of the application for credit, cash-flow and ratio qualifications. Discloses and re-discloses loan documentation according to regulatory rules. Initiates required adverse action letters, when applicable.</li>
<li>Works with mortgage processor, underwriter and closer, as needed, to validate the underwriting worksheet includes aggregate borrowing totals, purpose, cash flows and ratios. Submits loans to processing oval to Underwriting staff. Provides assistance in the documentation, processing and closing of mortgage loans.</li>
<li>Must comply with all company policies and procedures, applicable laws and regulations, including but not limited to, the Bank Secrecy Act, the Patriot Act, and the Office of Foreign Assets Control.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div><div>
<div><b>Experience</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Three years to five years of similar or related experience, including preparatory experience.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education/Certifications/Licenses</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>A two year college degree or bachelors degree is preferred. A combination of education and experience will be considered. Incumbents are required to maintain their registration active, and current, with the National Mortgage Licensing System throughout their employment.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Interpersonal Skills</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The ability to motivate or influence internal or external senior level professionals is a critical part of the job, requiring a significant level of influence and trust. Obtaining cooperation and agreement on important outcomes via frequently complex, senior level dialogues, as well as a professional level of written communication skills are essential to the position.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Other Skills</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Is responsible for a width of information including but not limited to: the organizations current mortgage loan interest rates, commitment/rate lock/relock fee procedures, consumer loan products/rates/guidelines that are used in conjunction with Mortgage Lending.</div>
