<h2>Producing Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Nations Lending Corporation™ is a Cleveland, Ohio-based national direct mortgage lender and servicer, licensed in 47 states plus the District of Columbia. With 50+ branches in 20 states, our company and culture continue to grow. We empower our employees to pursue their career goals by supporting their individual and creative ideas, and our mission is to treat every customer like a person, not a number. We want you to Join the Nation!
</p>
<p></p>The Branch Manager will be responsible for all efficient daily operations of the branch including lending, product sales, customer service, employee supervision, and ensuring that safety and compliance are in line with the company’s standards. The Branch Manager will also facilitate new loan business by providing a superior level of staff training through guidance and leadership.
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain communication between the branch and the corporate managers by creating daily, weekly and monthly report s on the branches operational activities.
</li>
<li><p>Ensure that the branch is in compliance with federal laws and regulations set forth by state and federal regulatory agencies.
</p></li>
<li>Train staff to achieve branch goals.
</li>
<li>Meet target office metrics including: number of transactions, sales volume, loan volume, etc.
</li>
<li>Recruit new employees; Assist in hiring process and onboarding of new employees .
</li>
<li>Monitor quality of service provided by employees to maximize their opportunities to sell products and maintain quality production standards .
</li>
<li>Deliver clear and consistent feedback to all branch employees.
</li>
<li>Manage branch employee daily performance and provide frequent feedback .
</li>
<li>Advise and educate consumers about the home purchase or refinance process, and how to better manage their mortgages by discussing loan terms, rates, fees, and other costs.
</li>
<li>Gather consumer information and apply skill and experience to put clients in the best financial status possible.
</li>
<li>Take and receive applications for credit.
</li>
<li>Initiate the loan process and manage application from file start to closing using various systems.
</li>
<li>Assist and communicate with consumers throughout the loan process.
</li>
<li>Confer and meet with customers during various stages of the loan process as needed.
</li>
<li>Utilize materials, reference tools, and other resources to provide accurate and up-to-date loan program/policy information to customers and business partners.
</li>
<li>Provide complete loan applications and support materials to fulfillment center partners.<br>
</li>
</ul>
