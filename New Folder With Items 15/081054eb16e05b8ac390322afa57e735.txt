Summary:<br>
The Manager, Global Brand Protection Services, Johnson &amp; Johnson Group, reports to the Director, Global Brand Protection, Medical Devices, and is primarily responsible for developing, managing and implementing an Enterprise Customs program to prevent illicit trade from crossing country borders thereby reducing exposure to patients/customers and our business.
<br>
<br>
Key responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Partnering with GBP segment and regional leads to align on and drive focus on priority brands, countries and key ports of import/export entry. Support regional administrative requests to facilitate processing of recordings of trademarks with Customs.
</li>
<li>Engaging with Brand Protection (BP) Technology team to develop and share actionable import and export intelligence with GBP segment and region leads to target efforts</li>
<li>Prepare enterprise standard Customs training materials for law enforcement in partnership with GBP segment, regional leads and the business. Develop and refresh global customs training plan</li>
<li>Execute training and collaboration with North America Law Enforcement, Judicial and Customs/Mail Facility personnel</li>
<li>First point of contact to respond to North America customs notifications</li>
<li>Establishes and aligns procedural and response expectations with Business Quality</li>
<li>Create GBP Enterprise Customs SOP and playbook</li>
<li>Provide reports, statements and affidavits to support various product investigation, enforcement and legal proceedings</li>
<li>Management of global recordation portfolio to ensure complete understanding of all trademarks recorded, and manage renewal with the regions and REACT</li>
<li>Manage REACT and other third parties supporting the Customs program</li>
<li>Partner with North American Global Security to provide support for Custom’s law enforcement actions</li>
<li>Manage Custom’s petitions and resolves complex issues related to these requests</li>
<li>Analyze Custom’s information and provide intelligence leads to support investigations in partnership with Global Security and the business</li>
<li>Provide technical expertise to support enforcement operations</li>
<li>Develop and share global metrics to track key performance indicators and effectiveness of Custom’s program to help guide focus</li>
<li>Performs other duties as directed</li>
</ul>
