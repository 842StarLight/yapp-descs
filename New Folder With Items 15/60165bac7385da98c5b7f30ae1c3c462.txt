<b>Administrative Assistant - White Plains, NY ( Job Number:</b> ANA0104C ) Primary Location : US-NY-White Plains
<br>Description
<br>Arcadis is seeking an Administrative Assistant to join our team in White Plains, NY.
<br><br>
Our ideal candidate will be detail-oriented, proactive, resourceful, flexible, demonstrate a high level of integrity, and deliver high quality work products. The successful candidate will also have superior organization and communication skills and the ability to multi-task working in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision.
<br><br>
Performs a wide range of responsibilities including, but not limited to:
<br>Coordinate client and internal meetings, including logistics, lunches and meeting minutes
<br>Arrange domestic travel
<br>Prepare expense reports, time sheets, and coordinate client invoices.
<br>Develop written communications including business letters, e-mails, etc.
<br>Assist with the development of internal and client presentations
<br>Proofread documents to ensure content consistency
<br>Conduct ad-hoc market research
<br>Assist with the establishment and maintenance of electronic information repositories for ease of access and retrieval (MS SharePoint, MS Teams, or similar)
<br>Internal research to collect and analyze sales data and other operational data from standard internal sources
<br><br>
<b>Key Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills (both written and verbal)</li>
<li>Self-motivated</li>
<li>Exceptional multi-tasker</li>
<li>Extremely organized and detail-oriented</li>
<li>Highly anticipatory with the innate ability to catch what others miss and anticipate needs in advance</li>
<li>Excellent time and calendar management skills</li>
<li>Flexible personality who is great with last minute changes</li>
<li>“Can do” attitude - no job is too big or too small</li>
<li>Self-starter who shows initiative and makes things happen</li>
<li>Loyal and trustworthy with a demonstrated high level of confidentiality and professionalism</li>
<li>High energy and enjoys a fast-paced and collaborative work environment</li>
<li>Resilient and extremely reliable</li>
<li>Ability to learn new software applications quickly, as needed</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><ul>
<li>Minimum 1-year work experience providing administrative support to an individual or team</li>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office 365 Suite, particularly Outlook, Excel, PowerPoint, Word, and other Office 365 productivity tools such as Skype for Business and SharePoint</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2-4 years of experience preferred</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration, Marketing or Communications</li>
<li>Advanced MS Excel skills: pivot tables, formula functions, audit/analyze worksheet data, charts/graphs and formatting</li>
<li>Advanced MS PowerPoint skills: develop a presentation that can include text, pictures, graphics, and spreadsheet tables starting from scratch or working with custom slides and templates, animations</li>
</ul>
