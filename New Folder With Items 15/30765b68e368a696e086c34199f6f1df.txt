<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>As a Senior Payment Consultant, you will act as an advisor towards customers by performing chargeable work through report writing, testing, and providing other required deliverables. Provides expert technical expertise within transaction security customer base. Provides lead generation support and assists in developing new services. Assists in the training and coaching of new employees.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Performs chargeable work through report writing, defining and/ or evaluation of specifications, technical requirements, information analysis, testing, test management, (pre)auditing and other advisory services.</li>
<li>
Provides high-level technical support to customers and internal staff. This may include test planning, logistics, updating test procedures, training and quality issues.</li>
<li>
Supports lead generation and chargeable work and assists with sales opportunities.</li>
<li>
Contributes in the training and coaching of new employees.</li>
<li>
Meets customer demands and delivery requirements in all respects.</li>
<li>
Acts as a representative in the customer environment.</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as directed.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>University Degree (Equivalent to a Bachelors degree), preferably in Computer Science, Information Technology, Mathematics or a technically related discipline plus generally five years of related experience</li>
<li>
Knowledge of ISO 8583 and ISO 7816 is a plus</li>
<li>
Knowledge and experience with EMV testing and certification</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Mobile Payments, NFC protocols, Tokenization and EMV 3DS is a plus</li>
<li>
Proven experience in customer-facing advisory services, technical expertise, and communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal, written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 80% (Domestic)</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
