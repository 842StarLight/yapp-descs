<h2>Account Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>
<div><div>
<div>As a member of our Dynamic Supply Chain Team, you will play a critical role in helping us supply our products to a global customer base. In this role, you will be responsible for providing an accurate and timely domestic and/or export order fulfillment process for customers. During a typical day, you may receive orders, check product availability, coordinate secure product transportation and delivery, and ensure accurate invoicing.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>This position is located at our North American Headquarters in Downtown Houston. The position offers a competitive base salary, bonus, 401K matching, generous benefits package, pension plan and employee discounts.</div>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Roles &amp; Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Manage the domestic and/or export order fulfillment process.</li>
<li>Assure accuracy and timeliness of all fulfillment master data in SAP and extended systems including e-commerce tools.</li>
<li>Act as the main point of contact with customers for all customer-related issues.</li>
<li>Maintain effective communication between sales, marketing, operations, and transportation to effectively provide answers to inquiries regarding price, product availability, and transportation requirements, communicate shipping schedules and details to plant/storage location personnel and business management as needed.</li>
<li>Participate as team member on SAP system improvements and customer fulfillment special projects.</li>
<li>Operate with a high degree of knowledge regarding customers, products, manufacturing, distribution systems, and industry trends.</li>
<li>Maintain intermediate to advanced knowledge of the Enterprise system support tools and assists customers/vendors in full utilization of system tools.</li>
<li>Model strong core competencies with internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Demonstrate the ability to work well in a team environment.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Min. Qualifications</b>
</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>This position necessitates excellent communication and analytical skills needed to resolve problems and make decisions.</li>
<li>8+ years related experience in lieu of degree.
</li>
<li>The ideal candidate will have 2-4 years’ experience in the order fulfillment process. In addition, the selected candidate must become knowledgeable of company, products, inventory and usage, as well as product end-use markets.</li>
<br>
</ul>
