<div><b>
Summary</b></div>
<div>
The primary responsibility of a stone &amp; sand dispatcher is to control the flow of equipment and manpower at their respective plants and efficiently manage those assets. This requires taking responsibility for driver activity and equipment utilization from the start of the day up to when the driver has been either released to go home.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for own personal safety at all times.</li>
<li>
Provide cheerful, positive internal and external customer service at all times.</li>
<li>
Monitor and track Hired Haulers from the start of the order until it has finished and continually make sound decisions that will maintain service levels</li>
<li>
Make sure that drivers are present and ready to load before ticketing and note directions, load size, extra products.</li>
<li>
Flag and identify disruptions in service delivery.</li>
<li>
Verify the status of Will-Call Orders</li>
<li>
Monitor individual plant scheduling screens so that trucks can be moved efficiently between plants
</li>
<li>Monitor rejected loads.
</li>
<li>Keep sales, operations and management informed of problems or concerns throughout the day that may require their knowledge or attention</li>
<li>
Notify the customer of late or interrupted service and of all statuses that exceed pre-programmed parameters by 10 minutes or more</li>
<li>
Accommodate customers if they request specific drivers on their order. If unable, explain other options</li>
<li>
Control the number of drivers needed throughout the day and make any necessary adjustments that will maintain the goals set by the company</li>
<li>
Work with the Plant Operators on plant breakdowns and computer breakdowns
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Adhere to and follow all safety rules and regulations of OSHA/MSHA and Pioneer Materials</li>
<li>
Conduct daily safety assignments (area inspections, pre-shifts, JSA’s, TRACK, etc).</li>
</ul>
