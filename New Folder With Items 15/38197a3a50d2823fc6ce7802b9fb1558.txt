<div></div>
<div><b>** NOTE THAT THIS IS A MARKETING, NOT PRODUCT STRATEGY ROLE**</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>This role reports to the Chief Marketing Officer of IngenioRx and will lead the marketing team responsible for the commercialization of new product offerings, as well as the ongoing promotion of existing products, to advance the growth agenda for IngenioRx. As the leader of the product marketing team you will partner closely with IngenioRx’s product and solutions management leaders to understand the product roadmap and will be accountable for developing and executing against the marketing programs required to support new product development, the launch/commercialization of new products, and the ongoing adoption of products within the existing product portfolio.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>This is a <i>strategic marketing role</i> and, as such, demands a strong understanding of marketplace trends and changes, the role of analytics voice of the customer, and voice of market programs, creating business value through marketing, marketing campaign orchestration and integration, and product/solution positioning and messaging.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Specific areas of focus for this role will be:</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Planning and executing product/solution marketing strategy, programs, including product content, positioning, messaging, core benefits, competitive differentiators, and clear and memorable value propositions to internal and external stakeholders.</li>
<li>Developing deep product/solution knowledge to help evangelize the key go-to-market benefits to internal and external stakeholders. Act as the voice of the customer within the organization to provide knowledge and relevance for all product/solution activities.</li>
<li>Operating as both an individual contributor as well as a team leader to provide day-to-day direction to team members and service partners working on shared projects.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Positioning/Messaging</b>: Develop product positioning and messaging that differentiates IngenioRx's products in the market.</li>
<li>
<b>Voice of Customer/Voice of Market: </b>Support the product development agenda by bringing the voice of customer/voice of market into the process. Partner with enterprise market research team to develop a learning agenda for the product management team.</li>
<li>
<b>Product Launch</b>: Create the product launch strategy for new products including internal and external rollout plans and development of appropriate sales tools and product materials.
</li>
<li>
<b>Product Content</b>: Create the product marketing materials, website content, and tools necessary to help customers make educated purchasing decisions around the entire suite of IngenioRx’s products. This includes brochures, datasheets, product guides, videos, e.g., thought leadership, testimonials, etc.</li>
<li>
<b>Demand Generation</b>: Work to integrate new product marketing materials into the overall demand generation strategy. Strategically place materials across multiple channels (web, social, direct, etc.) to drive increased demand for new products.</li>
<li>
<b>Measure and continuously improve: </b>Procure activity reports, e.g., how our experience designs are changing member behavior, and feed them back into segmentation, product development, and engagement strategies.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>BACKGROUND/EXPERIENCE</b></div>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of strategic marketing/brand marketing experience in the B2B space – preferably in the healthcare industry.</li>
<li>A minimum of 2 years of specific product marketing experience with a demonstrated track record of contributing to the product development process and bringing new products to market.</li>
<li>Have strong interpersonal skills and the ability to work independently and in a team environment</li>
<li>Must demonstrate strong project management skills: multi-tasking priorities and facilitating/executing multiple marketing programs</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Proven ability to collaborate across teams</li>
<li>Intermediate analytic skills</li>
<br>
</ul>

</ul>
