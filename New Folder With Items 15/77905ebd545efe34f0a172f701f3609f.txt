<h2>CREDIT/COLLECTIONS MANAGER</h2>

We collaborate with some of the most widely recognized brand and retail partners in the world. We are seeking skilled candidates who are excited to join this amazing team!</p>
<p><b>
We Believe in Great Brands:</b> Our goal is to translate the brand story into product that is as innovative and distinctive as the brand itself.<br>
<b>Creative Culture: </b>Our team brings to our brand partners a unique mix of creative product design paired with merchandising expertise. We believe innovation comes from a diversity of creative ideas.<br>

<p>
We are currently seeking a Credit/Collections Manager to join our team of outstanding employees!</p>
<p><b>
POSITION OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>
Bioworld is seeking an experienced Credit/Collections Manager to direct the daily credit/collections processes and procedures. This position will directly report to the Assistant Controller.</p>
<p><b>
JOB DUTIES</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Daily
<ul>
<li>Oversee the Remittances, Invoices, and AR Inquiry mailboxes</li>
<li>Oversee the cash application process to ensure that all cash receipts have been posted daily</li>
<li>Review unapplied cash and follow-up with Chargeback and cash application teams to resolve balances</li>
<li>Problem-solve and handle escalated issues from the data processing team in India</li>
<li>Respond to inquiries from internal/external customers and assist with issue resolution</li>
<li>Manage the data entry team in India to ensure accurate &amp; timely coding and processing</li>
<li>Continuously review the AR aging/Unapplied Cash to identify any potential issues and resolve before they are aged</li>
<li>Oversee and ensure the accuracy of customer master data</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Credit Risk Management/CIT/Collections
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with CIT to establish accounts for new customers by reviewing and researching credit payment history and obtain further information if necessary</li>
<li>Work closely with the finance and sales department to review and adjust credit lines on a regular basis or when requested for existing accounts</li>
<li>Prepare and remit monthly reporting to CIT</li>
<li>Maintain bad debt and bad debt recovery records</li>
<li>Prepare and recommend bad debt reserves to management (GL 12001)</li>
<li>Initiate credits and adjustments to customer accounts within company policy limits</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>E-Commerce
<ul>
<li>Oversee cash receipts and application for all e-commerce related receivables (Dumbgood/D365)</li>
<li>Manage month-end for Dumbgood/D365 AR and assist with system development and troubleshooting</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Month-End Close
<ul>
<li>Monitor the 945 and Open Orders reports to ensure all invoices have been properly posted</li>
<li>Monitor the AR Check Clearing (GL 12006) and Sales Clearing Accounts (GL 12510) and reconcile to a zero balance at month-end</li>
<li>Close the AR module once all data has been entered for the month.</li>
<li>Assist with the reconciliation of AR-Other (GL 12500)</li>
<li>Reconcile the AR Aging to the general ledger (12000)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Team Management/Other
<ul>
<li>Hold weekly team meetings with AR team in India</li>
<li>Review all manuals, policies/procedures, and AR SharePoint files quarterly for accuracy, update as necessary</li>
<li>Review all government agency forms annually to ensure only current forms are being used</li>
<li>Stay current on all regulatory changes that may affect the way the company transacts AR</li>
<li>Continuously look for ways to streamline the efficiency of the department and follow best practices</li>
<li>Document issues and resolution ideas to bring to senior management before initiation</li>
<li>Maintain documented communication with all internal and external customers on any changes to procedures, and train staff and customers as necessary.</li>
<li>Pull audit support related to receivables as requested.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
