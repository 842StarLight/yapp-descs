<h2>Marketing Communications Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Are you looking for an opportunity to drive demand generation for a global manufacturing leader in the music industry? Do you have experience in omni-channel campaigns, PR &amp; Publicity, event and influencer marketing? Do you thrive in a fast-paced environment and embrace change? Do you love data and KPI’s? If so, Gator would like to hear from you.</div>
<div></div>
<div>At Gator, we are best known for guitar cases, bags and accessories. But we offer so much more than that and are looking for a Marketing and Communications Coordinator to grow our business. Our industry spans from all musicians, DJ’s, audio/visual, gaming to outdoor and industrial. The Gator family of brands includes Gator Cases, Gator Frameworks, Levy’s, Slappa and OEM.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Description</b></div>
<div>The Marketing and Communications Coordinator will support the execution of all demand creation, generation, acquisition, and marketing strategies to drive lift and loyalty of the Gator Co. product family. Your mission will be to build the Gator brand and position in the market to further our reputation as an innovative design and solution provider, be passionate and share our passion about our products, customers and the markets we serve.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Duties Include:</div>
<ul><li>Reporting to the VP Marketing &amp; Business Development, support the implementation of the global, multi-channel, integrated marketing roadmap (digital/social, event, product, media) for Gator products and brands
<ul>
<li>Support product management and marketing teams on product roadmap initiatives</li>
<li>Support and execute precision marketing plans to increase loyalty, conversion and tribalism such as email marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Event planning, including tradeshows, customer and sales events, customer experience and outreach initiatives to increase Gator brand awareness, drive product marketing strategy and sales</li>
<li>Market research and monitor trends to make effective, data-driven decisions around media partnerships, influencer marketing, sponsorships, campaigns and advertising</li>
<li>Partner with Sales on B2B sales and marketing opportunities</li>
<li>Generate positive publicity by proactively researching and pitching story angles, writing stories, press releases and message points for global consumption</li>
<li>Collaborate with industry partners and organizations on global media and marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Marketing performance reporting to review progress against business goals and success metrics</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<div><b>Requirements</b></div>
<div><b>Candidates must have:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>2 years of experience in integrated marketing, communications and PR strategies</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience in product marketing campaigns and promotions, using multiple marketing tactics</li>
<li>Project management skills specific to marketing plans and budgets</li>
<li>Demonstrated understanding of marketing funnel from top-of-funnel awareness to mid-/bottom-of-funnel tactics</li>
<li>Cross-functional collaborative experience</li>
<li>Strong communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Strong understanding and working knowledge of music industry and audio/visual products (gear, accessories, equipment, instruments, etc.)</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing or Communications</li>
<li>Microsoft Office experience</li>
</ul>
