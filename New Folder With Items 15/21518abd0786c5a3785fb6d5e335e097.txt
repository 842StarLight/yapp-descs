<h2>Event Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>At Quest Oracle Community, you’ll join an exciting team dedicated to creating a digital community and online experience that is changing the way users of Oracle enterprise software solutions connect, share and learn. Take a look at this position and if you’re ready to join one of the best employers in Lexington, KY - apply below!</div>
<div></div>
<div>As the event marketing coordinator, you’ll have a major impact on developing and executing marketing collateral for Quest face-to-face and digital events for the JD Edwards, PeopleSoft, Cloud and Database Communities. In this role, you’ll work hand in hand with our marketing strategy, content development, vendor, and customer engagement teams to develop print and digital materials to attract prospects and to enhance the attendee experience through event collateral. You’ll be responsible for drafting basic copy, working with a designer to develop creative assets, compiling and optimizing content provided by team members, leading the draft and review process, and ensuring clear, consistent branding and messaging to the target audience across all materials.</div>
<div></div>
<div>You’ll work in a fast-paced environment managing multiple projects at once. You should have a love of learning, and a propensity to roll up your sleeves and dive in, feeling comfortable with ambiguity as you figure out how to move your piece of the business forward. This is a key team role that will give you fantastic experience in a fast growing, customer-centric, digital-forward company, right here in Lexington!</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate, develop, and execute print marketing collateral, including event guides, flyers, direct mail, etc.</li>
<li>Coordinate, develop, and execute digital marketing collateral, including marketing kits, PPT templates, etc.</li>
<li>Proofread materials for clarity, grammar and accuracy of copy.</li>
<li>Develop and disseminate event surveys.</li>
<li>Draft copy for event scripts.</li>
<li>Manage signage development and review process.</li>
<li>Manage event promotional item inventory and ordering process.</li>
<li>Review and update automated email campaign copy.</li>
<li>Generate solutions for continual process improvement.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS REQUIRED</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Be experienced – BA/BS required, with 2-5 years of progressive marketing experience (print and digital experience preferred).</li>
<li>Possess a dual-minded approach: You’re highly creative and an excellent writer but can also be process-driven, think scale, and rely on data to make decisions.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Be self-directed and have strong initiative - you can take a clear goal or objective and build an impactful tactical plan to deliver the desired results.</li>
<li>Be a strong communicator and facilitator- you have the ability to manage a project across multiple stakeholders with varying communication and work styles.</li>
<li>Be well organized with the ability to manage multiple projects at once in a fast-paced environment, and understand how to re-prioritize based on new feedback from teams.</li>
<li>Be a problem solver- you are able to identify areas of improvement and bring solutions to the table.</li>
<li>Be adaptable and resourceful- you can navigate unexpected challenges and utilize available resources to get the job done well.</li>
<li>Be detail-oriented- you’re a strong proofreader and thorough reviewer.</li>
<li>Someone who enjoys people and a collaborative, team focused way of working. A positive attitude, high energy and enthusiasm for what you do is a plus!</li>
</ul>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS DESIRED</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Experience with third-party management</li>
<li>Familiarity with print production</li>
</ul>
