<h2>Client Support Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Do you love working in a dynamic, fast-paced environment with cutting edge technology? Are you looking for an opportunity to bridge the gap between technology, engineering, business, and product?
<br><br>
As a Client Support Engineer (CSE) at Curalate, you will work directly with over 1,000 of the world's most loved brands to troubleshoot and resolve complex technical issues, consult clients on a wide range of technical questions, and carry out operational work requests on their behalf. Client Support Engineers work cross-functionally with internal teams to drive strategic initiatives within our organization! This is a great opportunity to work hands on with clients and bridge the gap from end-user to product while flexing your technical muscle.
<br><br>
A CSE is not only the face of our organization, but is a critical thinker and subject matter expert - providing not only answers to questions, but educating users on best practices and workarounds on all Curalate functionality from troubleshooting to simple and advanced workflows.
<br><br>
Our team is quick to identify trends &amp; patterns with the overall end-user experience and provide data driven feedback to our Product Development teams. You'll provide continuous updates to our Help Center/Knowledge Base content to streamline repeat questions and provide best in class support to our clients.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
-----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Respond to client support tickets in a timely manner, prioritizing and tracking work-requests against target SLA's</li>
<li>Act as a technical consultant for clients on software bugs, general support and best practices</li>
<li>Work with clients to implement technical solutions (product feeds, small to complex queries, data analysis, etc).</li>
<li>Collaborate cross-functionally with Product, Development, Client Solutions and Sales teams on the implementation of new features and design decisions, relaying data-driven feedback to all teams</li>
<li>Collaborate and communicate said challenges with other support &amp; software engineers to generate strategic solutions and update client resources through daily standups, team retrospectives, etc</li>
<li>Answer client questions in an insightful and informative way that helps marketers excel at their jobs and become smarter about our platform</li>
</ul>
<b>About You:</b><br>
----------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>You have a minimum of 1-3 years of experience in a client-facing support position</li>
<li>BS/BA Degree in a related field</li>
<li>You excel at problem solving both from a technical and strategic perspective</li>
<li>You've used JIRA, Freshdesk, Zendesk or other ticketing tools</li>
<li>You have experience with relational databases such as MySQL</li>
<li>You have a basic understanding of how the web works (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc.)</li>
<li>You're scrappy, think on your feet, get tech, and communicate effectively</li>
</ul>
<b>Gold Stars:</b><br>
-----------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>You have experience with Amazon Web Service, Regex, and Google Analytics</li>
</ul>
