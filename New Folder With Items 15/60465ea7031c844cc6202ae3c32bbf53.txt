<h2>Director, Security Operations (West)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div><div><div><div>
<p><b><i>Summary:</i></b></p>
<div>
This position is responsible for driving Core Commercial Security Install and Service efficiencies within the region. Serves as coach and mentor to Operations Managers and install team managers to determine best practices are consistently followed. Directs and examines studies and current business practices to determine operational effectiveness and revises programs to increase quality, productivity and customer retention. This opportunity requires management of multiple projects in developing solutions that support the business. Must have the ability to understand POC and CC accounting principles.<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Duties and Responsibilities:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Escalations related to Core Commercial Security installation and service, fulfilled by district offices for install/service teams. (NA escalation point, local commercial escalation point for all things install and service related)</li>
<li>Developing/Mentoring local ops personnel (newly hired ops personnel training/vetting, weekly touch points to understand training opportunities)</li>
<li>Tracking/Reporting on key deliverables, in standard, service backlog/age, installation forecasting to budget</li>
<li>Partner with Operations Managers and Install/Service Team Managers to ensure Technicians are trained and proper capacity levels are maintained to deliver to customer expectations and company targets.</li>
<li>Ensure integration and implementation of projects and initiatives that achieve the business objectives to enhance customer satisfaction, optimize profitability, develop long-term customer loyalty. Adjust project scope, timing, and budget, as needed, based on business needs.</li>
<li>Communicates current installation and service policies and procedures, techniques, demonstration of equipment, new products and services, sales promotions and area advertising.</li>
<li>Serves as the primary contact for the district operations work force to provide input and feedback related to Corporate Support Functions. Ensures consistency of field operations activities - inspection, service and installation.</li>
<li>Trains and supports consistent and accurate Installation forecasting with the Operations Managers and Install Team Managers.</li>
<li>Consistently partner with National Account Installation Department to ensure timely execution and an excellent Customer Experience for all National Account Customers</li>
<li>Utilizes available data to create best practices and implement new processes in Region. Develop program strategy, direction and changes to business leadership.</li>
<li>Coaches and Mentors install and service team managers on skills to drive customer and employee retention/satisfaction.</li>
<li>Champion of EHS&amp;W policies and compliance</li>
</ul>
