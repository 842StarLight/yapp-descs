<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What we're looking for:</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>
A curious, driven, and passionate Supplier Master Data Analyst. You are looking for a chance to support the compliance function of supplier master data management. You have 3 to 5 years of experience working in a Master Data / Systems Role. It is also preferred that you have worked in a direct/indirect procurement or financial environment where you obtained working knowledge of SAP ECC Supplier Master, SAP Ariba, and the Ariba Network.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
You have an eye for details to identify discrepancies in the supplier master records, and then collaborate with others to correct the data.. You have the curiosity to develop an understanding of the wider impact of supplier master record changes across multiple databases where supplier master records are maintained including systems, policies, processes, and procedures.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Supplier Master Data Analyst maintains and executes the processes necessary for the maintenance of the supplier master data across all impacted Procure to Pay systems, i.e. SAP ECC, Ariba On Demand, and the Ariba Network. The Supplier Master Data Analyst is accountable for the integrity of the supplier records within SAP as well as the SaaS solution via the Ariba Network. Responsibilities include data analysis to ensure the supplier master records are complete, consistent, accurate and compliant with internal policies and SOX controls.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Validate approved requests from authorized requestors for creating, changing, extending, blocking/unblocking or deleting supplier master data records in SAP, the Ariba Network, and all Ariba modules</li>
<li>
Identify discrepancies in the supplier master records and then work with Procurement and Accounts Payable as requestors to correct them, e.g. obsolete suppliers, duplicate accounts, and incomplete or inconsistent supplier master data</li>
<li>
Engage and train all Citrix suppliers to follow the necessary steps needed to be setup, transact, and invoice with Citrix on the Ariba Network</li>
<li>
Assist with the design, testing, and implementation of new process and/or enhancements to the systems that support master data. Carry out UAT testing as required for new functionalities to identify potential issues prior to project go-live</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2 to 3 years’ experience being part of global, enterprise-wide programs with one or more of the following Ariba modules P2P, Supplier Information &amp; Performance Management, PO/Invoice Automation and ERP integration with Ariba Network preferred</li>
<li>
Broad knowledge of the importance of supplier master data in the source to pay process.</li>
<li>
Experience with Microsoft products – High Excel proficiency (formulas, pivots, v-lookup, etc.)</li>
<li>
English Language skills required – Other languages, e.g. Chinese, Japanese, a plus</li>
</ul>
