<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Founded in 1963, Tecomet is the market leading provider of manufacturing solutions for complex, high-precision products and services for the Medical Device and Aerospace &amp; Defense markets. Tecomet operates seventeen (17) global manufacturing facilities in five countries around the world and employs over 2500 people.</p>
<p>JOB SUMMARY:</p>
<p>As a member of the Customer Service Team, the CSR will act as the primary point of contact for the customer. The CSR will be required to interact with all areas of the company to effectively champion the customer demand while meeting internal company goals and targets.</p>
<p>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain customer relations via telecommunications and in person visits with a goal of building the customer rapport, which is essential in growing the business</li>
<li>Exhibit proactive approach to customer service by following up on customer requests in a timely manner without waiting/delaying responses</li>
<li>Support planning, finance, quality and sales by providing information that is necessary to meet objectives</li>
<li>Support engineering by providing information that is necessary to meet launch requirements and to grow the business</li>
<li>Works with costing, sales, engineering &amp; finance and supports activities related to quote maintenance for new products (programs), modifications to existing products (programs) and for expired quotes</li>
<li>Works with customers, engineering, quality, accounting and sales to support activities related to contract review process</li>
<li>The CSR has complete responsibility for all activities related to Sales Order Entry in accordance with the Contract Review Procedure and other organizational requirements, which include but are not limited to:</li>
<li>Entry and maintenance of customer part number and itâ€™s alignment with the Tecomet part number, quantity, pricing, revision levels (all in accordance with the Contract Review Procedure), lot coding and serialization information, sales order dates, date codes, and order codes, which are the primary source of information for accurate execution and reporting of:</li>
<li>Customer commitments, which include but are not limited to; order confirmations, change notifications, open order reporting, customer conference calls and in person meetings</li>
<li>Execution of a production schedule and other planning and scheduling activities</li>
<li>Delivery Performance tracking and reporting</li>
<li>Lot coding and serialization information that is transferred to work orders (by planning)</li>
<li>Pending and in process contract review activities</li>
<li>VMI program management</li>
<li>Resource planning and execution</li>
<li>Delivery performance</li>
<li>Perform activities related to RMA Processing and Resolution, which includes but are not limited to:</li>
<li>Customer communication</li>
<li>Data research (on discrepant product), on part detail, price detail, invoice detail, lot information, and complaint information. This data is needed by quality, planning and accounting and helps facilitate the corrective actions and resolution</li>
<li>RMA Entry and maintenance in the ERP System and related documentation</li>
<li>Replacement Sales Order Entry</li>
<li>Additional duties as requested by management</li>
<li>Maintains the confidentiality of the department</li>
</ul>
