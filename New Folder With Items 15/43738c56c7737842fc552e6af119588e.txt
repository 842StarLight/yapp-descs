<h2>(Mortgage) Loan Originator, Sr NE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Job Summary</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Under the direction of the Branch and/or Production Manager, performs office or nonmanual work directly related to the management or general business operations of</div>
<div>
PrimeLending and its customers and markets and promotes PrimeLending products and services to its client base of realtors, builders, bank officers and potential and past clients.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Essential Functions</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li><div>Markets and promotes PrimeLending’s financial products;</div></li>
<li><div>
Originators are expected to meet the Company’s minimum production standards of 3 funded units per month on an ongoing basis, by taking complete and accurate loan applications, using PrimeLending’s product set.</div></li>
<li><div>
Establishes and maintains relationships with realtors, bank officers, builders and new home sales representatives in order to develop and retain existing business; and by visiting subdivisions as required.</div></li>
<li><div>
Monitors overall customer interaction and interfaces with all parties involved on each individual loan from application to closing, including but not limited to counseling and pre-qualifying potential home buyers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Collects and analyzes information regarding the customer’s income, assets, investments or debts.</div></li>
<li><div>
Evaluates PrimeLending’s and other loan products and services and determines which financial products best meet the customer’s needs and financial circumstances.</div></li>
<li><div>
Advises the customer regarding the advantages and disadvantages of different loan products.</div></li>
<li><div>
Services existing customers and obtains all necessary support documents along with the appropriate fees and lock in information.</div></li>
<li><div>
Oversees the loan process by monitoring loan status and ensures conformity with terms.</div></li>
<li><div>
Communicates loan status to all interested parties, and obtains loan documentation after closing.</div></li>
<li><div>
Negotiates terms and conditions of loans with customers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Stays abreast of trends and developments in the local real estate market and competitive environment, as well as, changes in rules and regulations pertaining to private and government insured mortgages.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintains a professional image and adheres to standards consistent with company policies and procedures.</div></li>
<li><div>
May supervise and direct the activities of other loan officer(s), loan officer assistant(s), or loan processor(s).</div></li>
<li><div>
Other duties as assigned.</div></li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Job Requirements</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li><div>High school diploma or equivalent.</div></li>
<li><div>
Appropriate licenses, if State required.</div></li>
<li><div>
A minimum of 12 months loan originator experience required for Loan Originator; minimum of 5 years loan originator experience required for Senior Loan Originator.</div></li>
<li><div>
Interacts with regional &amp; corporate departments, as well as realtors, sales agents, builders, appraisers, etc.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated judgment and decision making ability. Excellent and effective presentation and communication skills, both verbal and written.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to quickly assess and process large amounts of data and use of appropriate AU software technology.</div></li>
<li><div>
Must be active in the community and in those job-related organizations to enhance the network of beneficial contacts.</div></li>
<li><div>
Continue to expand knowledge of issues influencing the industry and the organization.</div></li>
<li><div>
Valid driver’s license and current automobile liability insurance.</div></li>
</ul></div></div></div>
</div></div>
