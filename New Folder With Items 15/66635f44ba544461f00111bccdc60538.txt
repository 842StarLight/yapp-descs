Client Success Team
<br><br>
The Client Success Team is a key part of the Business Development Group at Subsplash. Their primary focus is on partnering with clients to dream big, develop, and implement strategies that will help them successfully leverage our platform to engage their audience and maximize the impact of their mission. This team is made up of individuals who are high-energy, relationship-based, enthusiastic, creative, part super-sleuth, part sales professional, and part evangelist that have clear goals and incentives linked to team and company success.
<br><br>
About the Role
<br><br>
As a Client Success Manager, you will report to the Manager of the Client Success Team. The CSM's primary goals are to delight clients, increase retention, and increase recurring revenue through the adoption of Subsplash services by strategically consulting, strengthening relationships, staying informed and educating clients on industry knowledge, and helping clients maximize their investment in Subsplash through fuller utilization of our platform. If you like collaborating with awesome people, making their day better, and quoting episodes of The Office, this role is probably the right fit for you!
<br><br>
Your Priorities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Build relationships with targeted clients via phone and email communication</li>
<li>Inform clients of exciting new products and features and value of Subsplash platform</li>
<li>Develop innovative solutions catered to client needs</li>
<li>Help clients succeed in their utilization and implementation of Subsplash products</li>
<li>Stay educated and be able to educate clients on industry information</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>2-3 years of Sales and/or Account Management experience</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce or other CRM tool (preferred)</li>
<li>College degree</li>
<li>Experience in a phone or other communication-based position</li>
</ul>
