<h2>Marketing Manager, Bikes and Scooters - Boston</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Lyft's mission is to improve people's lives with the world's best transportation. We care deeply about the people we work with and the communities we serve. To create the world's best transportation, we start with our own team by creating an open, inclusive, and diverse organization.
<br><br>
We are seeking a talented leader for Bluebikes marketing with big ideas and a track record of delivering marketing results. As Bluebikes expands, its popularity continues to surge. The system has broken its own ridership records year over year, setting new ridership records in both June and July.
<br><br>
If you are a results-oriented leader who is seeking a mission-driven organization that's having a real impact on health, transportation, and the environment, we want to hear from you. You will be responsible for developing the overall annual marketing strategy for Bluebikes, increasing ridership, including new member acquisitions, member retention, casual pass sales, and reduced fare riders. You will oversee a passionate team of marketers focused 100% on Bluebikes growth and reaching key demographics. You will be supported by a corporate team that will provide creative services, business intelligence, and product development.
<br><br>
If you share our vision for sustainable transportation that empowers local communities, reduces our environmental footprint and brings people together, we'd love to meet you!
