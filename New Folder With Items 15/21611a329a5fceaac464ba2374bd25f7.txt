<h2>Team Lead, Technical Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><div>
<div>Responsible for providing direction and guidance for the day to day activities of the Field Service Technicians OR manages logistical and technical support activities for the field service team. Primary responsibility is to ensure that all departmental activities are carried out in a manner to optimize customer satisfaction and brand loyalty, and the achievement of department goals and objectives. This may also include providing necessary coaching and monitoring of departmental support specialists.<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>Principal Accountabilities</b><br>
In addition to the following Epson's policies and procedures, principle accountabilities include, but are not limited to:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Call Center</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Works with department management to create, monitor, and adjust Service and Support schedules to ensure optimal customer service levels. This may include creating daily work schedules and/managing work assignment allocation</li>
<li>Participates in monitoring team activities and deliverable to ensure proper communication and technical accuracy. Ensures confidential product information is not released and accurate data entry is entered into customer call logs and databases</li>
<li>Resolves escalated technical issues and/or customer concerns</li>
<li>Escalates product issues to functional management and/or appropriate department</li>
<li>Promotes a sales environment through technical support calls or visits, selling consumables, extended warranties and hardware</li>
<li>Monitors data and creates reports on daily and monthly call center metrics or field team activities</li>
<li>Prepares ad-hoc reporting and analysis as requested by management</li>
<li>Ensures team members time card data is accurate</li>
<li>Takes support phone calls and answers customer e-mails as needed</li>
<li>Prepares ad-hoc reporting and analysis as requested by management</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<div><b>Product Knowledge</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Maintains expert knowledge of product specifications, operations, and target markets (e.g., pre-press, photo, etc.). Reviews prints and determines if quality meets product guidelines</li>
<li>Collaborates with Advance Product Support representatives and management on escalated technical issues</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Relationship Management</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Provides coaching and mentoring to team members</li>
<li>Advises management and provides recommendations on personnel and problem issues ( that require escalation)</li>
<li>Builds strong relationships with peers and Senior Managers to best meet company goals and objectives</li>
<li>Manages and leverages relationships with vendors for a specific product/service (best price, best product, etc)</li>
<li>Maintains professional relationships with peers in other corporations and outside organization</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Required Skills and Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>2-5 years of experience in a similar role</li>
<li>Completion of program at a community college, technical or commercial school or equivalent experience (minimum of a 2-year program)</li>
<li>Ability to troubleshoot computer hardware and software problems</li>
<br>
</ul>
