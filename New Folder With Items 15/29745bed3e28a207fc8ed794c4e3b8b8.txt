<h2>Accounts Receivable Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Occupational Summary:</b><br>
Oversees and administers accounts receivable process of collecting payments from customers and to maintain accounts in a timely and accurate manner.
<br><br>
Facilitates staff and customer understanding of process and policies through excellent communication and customer service.
<br><br>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
Minimum 2 year accounting degree or 5 years related experience
<br><br>
Ability to work with confidential information
<br><br>
Must maintain current knowledge of computer system and all related software, along with Microsoft Excel
<br><br>
Must maintain basic knowledge of all applicable Federal and State regulations
<br><br>
<b>Physical Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Job requires the following physical activities:</b> sitting, standing, walking, reaching,
<br><br>
lifting, bending, stooping, kneeling, finger dexterity, repetitive motions. The work
<br><br>
is performed primarily indoors.
<br><br>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
Produce accurate and timely monthly statements for customers
<br><br>
Ensure timely collection of outstanding balances and make collection calls for accounts over 60 days
<br><br>
Follow up with managers and customers on NSF checks and credit card disputes
<br><br>
Timely reporting of aged accounts receivable
<br><br>
Perform credit checks on customers and credit references as needed
<br><br>
Monitor and resolve issues with all credit card machines
<br><br>
Manage staff and ensure accounting procedures are followed
<br><br>
Assist cashiers with accounting issues and questions
<br><br>
Verify and balance deposits from all sites daily
<br><br>
Ready deposits for remote check scanning
<br><br>
Post and maintain all receipts daily
<br><br>
Review and balance unpaid service and parts sales daily
<br><br>
Post any other incoming payments daily
<br><br>
Maintain schedules for gift certificates; LOF packages; insurance claims
<br><br>
Propose recommendations to improve quality of internal processes and collection procedures
<br><br>
From time to time all employees are asked to perform reasonable administrative functions outside of this job description. In addition, all employees are expected to be cross-trained on other job descriptions within the department to facilitate vacations, medical leaves, etc.</div>
