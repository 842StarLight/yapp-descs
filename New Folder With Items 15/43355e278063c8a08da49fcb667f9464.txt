<h2>Regional Sales Executive, SE Region</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">CS Disco Inc. is hiring regional sales executives to close transactional eDiscovery deals at litigation boutiques and AmLaw 200 firms and grow our share of business within existing accounts. This Regional Sales Executive will cover the territory of Tennessee, Alabama, and Mississippi.
