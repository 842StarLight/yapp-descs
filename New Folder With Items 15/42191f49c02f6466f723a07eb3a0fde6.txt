<div><b>ABOUT THE ROLE</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As the<b> Ad Operations Manager,</b> you will be responsible for managing the creation and successful implementation of campaign activities across a wide range of digital platforms. You will play a key role in revenue operations providing exceptional support to sales professionals within Inboxlab, further strengthening the client experience and Inboxlab brand. Responsibilities include optimizing sales campaigns across multiple brands and channels, processing digital sales orders, uploading data into CRM, and monitoring programs to make sure campaigns are running properly.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Advertising Operations Manager will be client centric and driven by a competitive sales environment. You’ll offer a can do attitude and take a positive approach when supporting all sales initiatives.</div>
</div>
