<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h4>
<p></p>
<p>YMCA Safe Place Services seeks a Director of Development to help build and advance the mission of the YMCA and Safe Place Services in our community. The ideal candidate is a passionate philanthropic leader with a record for building strong relationships between donors and important causes.
</p>
<p>The Director of Development is responsible for developing, implementing, and directing a strategic funding plan to support the mission of the YMCA Greater Louisville and Safe Place Services. The Director of Development will work in partnership with the Executive Director, Staff Development Team, Board of Directors, and Financial Development Committee to maximize the impact YMCA Safe Place programs have in the community.
</p>
<p><b>STATUS:</b> Full Time, Exempt</p>
<p><b>
SALARY RANGE:</b> $50,000+</p>
<p>
(Based on Experience)
