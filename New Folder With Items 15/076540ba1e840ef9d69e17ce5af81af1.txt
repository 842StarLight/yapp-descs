 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div><b>ABOUT THE ROLE</b></div>
Senior Account Executives at Upserve are responsible for analyzing a customer’s business objectives, and advising customers on the value of Upserve’s platform as it pertains to their restaurant operations. Additionally, they provide consultations on how to best support our cloud-based Point of Sale and the Upserve HQ analytics product by designing a solution that integrates all technology, networking, hardware, and 3rd party applications into the platform. This will be a remote position.<br>
<div></div>
<p>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Consulting with prospective customers on their technology and operations needs</li>
<li>Designing a technical and operational solution for single and multi-location restaurants</li>
<li>Engages in technical discussions with potential clients through demonstrations and presentations</li>
<li>Analyzing the costs currently paid by a customer, and projecting costs and savings with Upserve</li>
<li>Forecasting weekly and monthly revenue projections and maintaining customer records in SalesForce</li>
</ul>
<div><b>ABOUT YOU</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>
Qualifications:</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in related field, such as business management</li>
<li>2 to 5 years of progressive experience advising SMB operations through use of SaaS/technology</li>
<li>Proficiency with revenue forecasting and data reporting; Salesforce experience highly preferred</li>
<li>Prior experience in the hospitality industry, and using restaurant technology such as POS, highly preferred</li>
