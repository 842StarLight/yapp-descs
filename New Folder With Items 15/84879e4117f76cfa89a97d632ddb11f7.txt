<div>
<br>

<div><b>
Overview</b></div>
<div>
We are seeking a highly motivated Retail Customer Success Manager, on Criteo’s Retail Media business team. The ideal candidate should have deep knowledge of both advertising technology and the retail landscape. This is an amazing opportunity for an experienced online media and technology professional to join the world’s leading performance display advertising company.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Retailers are at the core of our business and it is up to the Customer Success Manager to ensure that we are able to develop a strong partnership. The CSM is essential as they are tasked with coordinating with the various internal partners (Technical Services, Analytics, Finance, Brand Sales etc.) and client facing partners to ensure a smooth and successful program that allows retailers to monetize their site, drive deeper brand relationships and drive revenue. The individual will be asked to use their business and analytical skills to grow and retain revenue with existing retail partners. The candidate will need to work strategically with partners to implement creative deals impacting our largest region. They will need to monitor performance and identify opportunities for each retailer to upsell existing partners, and interact with day to day contacts at some of e-commerce world’s leading companies.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This involves managing and growing a large existing book of e-commerce retail businesses, deepening relationships within organizations and coordinating across internal teams to ensure that all facets of the program are running smoothly. Overall management of the program includes implementation of new placements, revenue monitoring and troubleshooting, leveraging data for upselling and strategic discussions, emphasizing the continued benefits of Criteo as a platform and monetization partner.</div>
</div>

<div><ul>

</li>

Analyzing program performance and client needs to optimize delivery of services and solutions</li>
<li>

<li>
Understand client needs to create custom workflow and process</li>

<li>
Partner with a broad range of internal teams (marketing, legal, brand sales, products, R&amp;D, technical Solutions) to support sales efforts</li>
<li>
Serving as an internal voice of the client; gathering and documenting business needs</li>
<li>
Travel up to 25%</li>
</ul></div>
