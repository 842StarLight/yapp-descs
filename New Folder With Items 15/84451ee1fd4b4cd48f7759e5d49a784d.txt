<h2>Part time mini bar attendant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Provide guests with afully-stocked and well-maintained mini-bar on a consistent basis throughouttheir stay. Stock the mini-bar cart with the necessary items in order tomaintain the par level on a daily basis.</p>
<p>Clean and defrost themini-bars regularly and consistently. Maintain a fully stocked inventory of allmini-bar items. If par level is low, order the necessary items from thestoreroom. Inventory all guest mini-bars on a daily basis and post theappropriate charges to the room prior to guest departure.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p>1. Raising of checks and maintaining Mini bar Register.</p>
<p>2. Authorized to enter in all guest rooms pertaining to Mini bar atany given point of time.</p>
<p>3. Stock and replace missing items, inspect, move and clean themini-bars in accordance with departmental procedures and policies.</p>
<p>4. Maintain, track and complete daily worksheet of mini bar stock.</p>
<p>5. Responsible for replenishment of Mini bar items in rooms.</p>
<p>6. Responsible for maintaining the Mini bar Pantry in terms of parstock, stacking and cleanliness.</p>
<p>7. Ensure products in Mini-Bar are not expired and take the properprocedure to remove those products as necessary.</p>
<p>8. Maintain assigned Mini-Bar carts.</p>
<p>9. Raise store requisition and obtain stock from warehouse andstore in the assigned storerooms.</p>
<p>10.Maintain and complete assigned floors and rooms requestingMini-Bar service.</p>
<p>11.Maintain the highest level of customer service and displayprofessionalism at all times.</p>
<p>12.Follows all required safety and standard operating procedures.</p>
<p>13.Ensure rotation of all mini-bar products.</p>
<p>14.Properly complete, maintain and track inventory in assignedstorerooms.</p>
<p>15.Ensure all duties found in the refreshment centerinspection/attendant evaluation checklist are followed including completingassigned floors, cleanliness of assigned mini-bars, menus are present,inventory is complete, and refill reports are accurately filled out.</p>
<p>16.Display appropriate two-way radio etiquette at all times.</p>
<p>17.Report all mini-bar concerns to management including any guestissues.</p>
<p>18.Perform any other duties deemed necessary for the success of thedepartment.</p>
<p>19.Perform other job-related duties as assigned.</p>
<p>20.Knowledge of Point of sale (POS) systems, Handheld POS,inventory and store requisition software and reporting tools.</p>
<p>21.Previous experience in hotel industry.</p>
<p><b>PREREQUISITES: </b></p>
<p>Ability to work well under pressure in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<p>Ability to work cohesively with fellow Colleagues withoutconstant supervision.</p>
