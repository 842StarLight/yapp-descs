<h2>Senior Counsel - Transactions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>The Senior Counsel – Transactions will spend a significant amount of his/her time supporting this S&amp;P 500 company in ordinary course commercial transactional matters,<br>
OEM/direct sourcing transactions and strategic transaction, as well as providing support and assistance with integration and M&amp;A activities.<br>
The Senior Counsel – Transactions will take an active role in reviewing and negotiating end user software license agreements, distribution agreements,<br>
enterprise agreements, consulting agreements, vendor agreements, non-disclosure agreements and related contracts schedules and forms.<br>
This role will also work closely with the Associate General Counsel – Technology and Intellectual Property and her team, the business units,<br>
and partner team with respect to OEM/direct sourcing transactions, LNDAs and partner agreements, as well as the Senior Counsel – M&amp;A and Strategic Transactions<br>
to support legal due diligence and post-acquisition integration.
</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul><li>Interacts with sales and account managers and distributors throughout North America and South America, playing a key role in identifying potential obstacles</li></ul>
and in helping move each ordinary course commercial transaction opportunities forward.<br>
<ul><li>Drafts and negotiates software licenses, service agreements, nondisclosure agreements, distribution agreements, and other technology agreements with customers</li></ul>
and corporate partners.<br>
<ul>
<li>Drafts and negotiates complex sourcing, partner, service, strategic transaction agreements and other technology agreements.</li>
<li>Works closely with internal legal team to ensure that all ordinary course commercial transaction agreements, OEM/direct sourcing agreements and</li>
</ul>
partner agreements protect the intellectual property rights of ANSYS.<br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborates with the internal legal team on M&amp;A due diligence and legal integration activities</li>
<li>Assists in risk mitigation arising under local, state, and federal laws in North America.</li>
<li>Will provide education and training of sales, ACE, and other staff on any commercial legal policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS</b>
<ul>
<li>Law degree from an accredited law school and admitted to practice law in the United States.</li>
<li>Minimum of seven years of experience practicing as an attorney with a national or international law firm and/or in-house legal department
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least two years of in-house experience</li>
<li>In-house experience at a software or technology company</li>
<li>Extensive experience with contract drafting and negotiation</li>
<li>Working knowledge of anti-trust, intellectual property, and commercial law.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and superior intuition. Strong ability to communicate effectively with a wide variety of audiences,</li>
</ul>
including senior management and key external stakeholders and proven ability to engage, influence and align stakeholders<br>
<ul><li>Capacity to think strategically to identify issues and develop long-term solutions</li></ul>
<br>
