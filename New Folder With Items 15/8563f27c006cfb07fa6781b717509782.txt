<h2>Senior Product Marketing Manager, AI API</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We're looking for a product marketing manager who is motivated by owning and driving their product business and connecting with users and customers. We are in search of a product marketing manager who will wow and inspire developers with possibilities of using AI and automated machine learning. In this newly created role, you'll be the marketing lead for our self-service AI API products, and be responsible for driving the strategy and activities around market impact, which will include but are not limited to launches, demand generation strategy, and influencing AR and PR activities for our AI API line of business.

You'll interact with Marketing, Sales, and Product Management regularly as you review anticipated features and develop the associated marketing strategies. The role requires a combination of keen interest in building a self-service business from the ground-up, breaking into the developer market, AI and machine learning, content creation. This is a rare opportunity to build the product line.
