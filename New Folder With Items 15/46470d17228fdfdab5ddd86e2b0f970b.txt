<p>Job Summary</p>
<p>
The Senior Partnership Marketing Manager II oversees a team responsible for delivering best in class, branded marketing and loyalty programs across channels leveraging data-driven insights and vertical expertise to drive profitable portfolios that deliver value for our cardmembers, brand partners and our organization. Guides strategy of differentiated marketing activities that support our ability to drive strong, mutually beneficial growth in New Accounts, Accounts Receivable, Credit Sales and Tender Share for a specific vertical of clients. Collaborates with senior leadership to shape Marketing decisions and strategies. Works closely with Account team and Field team senior leadership to support the enhancement of our competitive position and maximize profitability. Demonstrates deep industry and vertical knowledge to optimize the overall program marketing planning for book of clients, across products and channels. Other duties as assigned to meet function/business needs</p>
<p>
Essential Job Functions</p>
<p>
Account Marketing Planning - Leads a team of marketing professionals that provide expertise and oversee marketing planning (annual, seasonal, long range) relevant to specific vertical and product set, optimizing program performance, delivers on Critical Few Initiative (CFI) expectations and achieves/exceeds budget targets. Guides marketing planning for assigned clients as part of the Vertical Marketing Team (VMT) strategy and assessment process. Guides team to optimize marketing fund allocation in support of delivering mutually beneficial growth for key client partners and our organization.</p>
<p>
Marketing Best/Next Practices - Is accountable for ensuring marketing best practices are in place for specific client programs across channels based on program vertical/maturity. Contributes to process that identifies and champions next practices for ongoing performance and differentiation. Leverages industry benchmarks, a test and learn approach and partners internally with Product and Capability owners and with other LOBs on emerging strategies and technologies to identify relevant opportunities for specific vertical client set. Guides team on developing and executing marketing best practices for customer capability adoption. Demonstrates deep knowledge of the particular vertical and applies learnings from industry insights and marketing best practices to provide value to our cardmembers, clients, and Account Team partners. Leads enterprise wide initiatives to further the Know More Sell More® brand promise.</p>
<p>
Program Performance - Partners closely with Client Partnership leaders and matrixed team to influence P and L performance, optimizing customer segmentation and lifecycle strategy and managing investment decisions of marketing capital to maximize return on investments and positively impact portfolio KPIs and deliver on CFI expectations. Partners closely with various analytics and research partners to identify key customer segments and respective behaviors and aligns on the predictive modeling and targeting approaches to drive program revenue and return, including test strategies</p>
<p>
Relationship Management - Supports client satisfaction and relationship through participation in key meetings (Op Comm, Seasonal planning, weekly marketing planning meetings) as determined and ensuring team provides strong ongoing support of client relationships and execution of the marketing plan in partnership with client teams and matrixed support teams</p>
<p>
Talent Development - Provides coaching to Senior Partnership Marketing Manager I and Partnership Marketing Manager I/II and creates mentoring opportunities for learning and development. Conducts touch bases with direct reports on a regular basis to cascade information.Works with associates to create development plans based on their goals and level of performance. Guides associate behavior by leading with and demonstrating attitudinal leadership. Demonstrates leadership by training of peers and/or direct reports and being viewed as a knowledge resource within the department.</p>
<p>
Preferred Education/Certifications</p>
<p>
Bachelor's Degree in Marketing, Finance or related field. MBA Preferred.</p>
<p>
Preferred Work Experience</p>
<p>
5+ years experience in Marketing, Partnership Management. Proven experience building marketing roadmaps and strategies for retail, brand, consumer or credit cards. Highly skilled at building connections internally and externally to facilitate program growth. Strong understanding of financial concepts, retail, loyalty and credit programs, consumer behavior, and marketing analytics</p>
<p>
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</p>
<p>
Strong interpersonal skills and proven track record for driving results</p>
<ul>

<li>Excellent verbal and presentation skills. Able to convey point of view for marketing strategy and analytics that drives loyalty in a manner that engenders confidence and credibility.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a matrixed organization.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with the ability to understand financial drivers of profitability and ROI maximization of marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>Experience building and leading high-performing teams</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Powerpoint</li>
