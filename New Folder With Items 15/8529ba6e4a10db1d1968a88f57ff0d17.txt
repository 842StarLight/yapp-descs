<h2>Modern Workplace Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Headquartered in Spartanburg, South Carolina, Milliken &amp; Company is a global, innovative, industrial diversified manufacturing company that has been exploring, discovering, and creating ways to enhance people’s lives since 1865. With expertise across a breadth of disciplines including specialty chemical, floor covering, and performance materials, the company works around the world every day to add true value to people’s lives, improve health and safety, and help make our world more sustainable. Milliken has long led the way for "knowledge-based" investment, employing over 100 PhDs, and has accumulated over 2,200 U.S. patents – and more than 5,000 patents worldwide. Milliken is widely acknowledged as an international leader in research technology, innovation, and customer service. With over 35 manufacturing facilities located in the U.S., U.K., Belgium, France, China, India, Mexico, and Australia, and other sales and service operations throughout the Americas, Europe and Asia, Milliken's more than 7,000 associates work to deliver innovations that do good for the world, create new experiences, and build for the future.
<br>Information Services is looking for a candidate for a key position on our Modern Workplace Center of Excellence team. This team will assist in leading Milliken’s deployment of modern, digital tools and processes throughout the enterprise. The most qualified candidate will possess strong communication skills with the ability to communicate effectively with all levels of the organization. They must be able to translate business requirements into technology opportunities. The candidate should also have a high level of technical competence with an emphasis on modern web tools and cloud-based software. This role will require a self-motivated individual that is comfortable being part of a team but is also able to act independently once direction is set. The team will be challenged to move quickly and will be the source of significant change within the company. Familiarity with the following technologies will be considered a plus: Office 365, Microsoft Azure, Microsoft SharePoint, Google Apps, Modern Development Tools, and Modern Social Media Tools.
<br><br>
<b>As a Modern Workplace Architect you will:</b><br>
Provide guidance, training and consulting for the deployment of modern tools and processes through the enterprise.
<br><ul>
<li>Communicate with business leaders at all levels of the company to help them identify their business requirements and translate those into technical opportunities.</li>
<li>Develop and deliver training materials related to modern workplace tools and processes.</li>
<li>Deploy modern workplace solutions using tools such as SharePoint Online, Microsoft Teams, Microsoft Azure, and Office 365.</li>
</ul>
Be a key member of a center of excellence team that will focus on long term alignment and digital growth within the company.
<br><ul>
<li>Work with other members of the IS team to identify and develop strategies, policies and guidelines for the modern workplace at Milliken.</li>
<li>Work well with others on the team and be able to work independently after the team has set the direction.</li>
<li>Thrive in a fast paced and innovative culture.</li>
</ul>
Be a social influencer.
<br><ul>
<li>A key part of the modern workplace strategy involves embracing the role of social media within an enterprise.</li>
<li>Bring existing knowledge of social media tools and practices to the job.</li>
<li>Be a change agent.</li>
</ul>
Minimum Requirements
<br><ul>
<li>3 years of related work experience</li>
<li>Strong effective communication skills</li>
<li>Technical Competence with Modern Web/Cloud Based Software</li>
<li>BS/BA Degree</li>
</ul>
