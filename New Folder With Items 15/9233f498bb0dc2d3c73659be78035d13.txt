<h2>Sr. Manager, Animation Home Video Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The Senior Manager, Animation Home Video Marketing works closely with the Sr. Director, Marketing in setting the strategic direction for VIZ Media’s home video product line, including both physical and digital products. This position is responsible for building and leading multi-channel comprehensive marketing plans, including optimizing all key marketing vehicles such as partnerships, websites, email newsletters, social media, media buys (online &amp; offline), and PR strategies. The right candidate will lead in the identification of industry trends, tracking and analyzing competitive products, and leading the retailer marketing relationships.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Works with key P&amp;L owners to develop, create, and execute comprehensive marketing plans for VIZ Media’s animation properties and key initiatives</li>
<li>
Partners with the sales team in identifying retailer offerings; including price points and features</li>
<li>
Researches and identifies competitive offerings to better shape the VIZ point of differences and identify areas for growth</li>
<li>
Assesses issues and challenges in the current marketing and product business model (and its organizational capabilities) and recommends necessary changes for improvement</li>
<li>
Maintains clear &amp; actionable results-driven goals for the Animation Marketing team including quarterly and annual marketing planning calendars</li>
<li>
Translates marketing strategy into digestible, implementable and prioritized project plans</li>
<li>
Acts as a focal point of on-going communication among cross-functional project teams, including, but not limited to: Sales &amp; Retail Development, Animation Production, Licensing, Business Development, Partners and Vendors</li>
<li>
Conducts regular audits of ongoing Marketing Campaigns, including customer behavior analyses, and incorporates the results into the improvement of all marketing campaigns and reports findings to the business leadership</li>
</ul>
<p>
 In addition to these responsibilities, performs other activities as assigned</p>
