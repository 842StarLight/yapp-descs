<h2>Inside Sales Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Inbound Sales Representative meets our customers’ business or personal needs by providing the right advertising product or technical assistance with our online services. This individual should have a competitive spirit and a strong customer focus with the ability to complete workload through inbound calls, fax and email while providing professional, courteous customer service to our advertising customers while meeting deadlines in a highly accurate environment.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Provide accurate notice placement while offering superb customer service</li>
<li>
Responsible for placing approximately $70K in revenue per period</li>
<li>
Answer inbound calls, faxes and emails across multiple markets by quickly toggling between applications &amp; confidently navigate an ad order entry system
</li>
<li>Develop professional relationships with our customers
</li>
<li>Assist customers in placing product orders
</li>
<li>Recommend new product offerings
</li>
<li>Work with customers by answering questions, researching/resolving issues, and trouble-shooting
</li>
<li>Ensure that customers enjoy the time they spend working with you
</li>
<li>Help customers locate ads online and in print
</li>
<li>Document all customer transactions in accordance with company policy</li>
<li>
Meet and maintain all individual and team standards as set by the department
</li>
<li>Attend product and system training events
</li>
<li>Share ideas to improve business practices
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
Confident demeanor, competitive drive, and goal-oriented nature
</li>
<li>Excellent communications skills (oral and written), courteous and clear telephone voice
</li>
<li>Willing to invest time and effort into learning about Tribune products
</li>
<li>Ability to retain knowledge of products
</li>
<li>Positive, people oriented and energetic
</li>
<li>Be motivated to meet customer needs
</li>
<li>Be analytical, creative, and innovative in solving problems</li>
<li>
Call center experience in customer service or sales preferred</li>
<li>
Bilingual skills (English-Spanish) a plus
</li>
<li>In-depth experience with Internet (Web and email)
</li>
<li>Experience with computers (MS Office)
</li>
<li>Typing skills; Minimum 45 wpm</li>
<li>
Ability to collaborate and work respectfully with coworkers and leaders is essential</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
