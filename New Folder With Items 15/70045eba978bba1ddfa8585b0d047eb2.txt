<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you looking for your next sales challenge with a well established (15+ years), global company that is 2 years into its launch in the United States? Are you a motivated, highly driven, self-starter with business-to-business sales experience? Would you like to help change the lives of your customers by presenting a solution that turns their good business into a great business? Then we would like to hear from you!</p>
<p><b>
About us:</b></p>
<p>
simPRO Software is a SaaS company that develops business management cloud solutions for the trade contractor industry. Our products are designed to optimize business workflow to refine process, improve productivity and increase profitability. simPRO Software is a global software house with operations in Australia, New Zealand, the United Kingdom and now the US.</p>
<p><b>
The role:</b></p>
<p>
You are a self-motivated, articulate and well-presented sales professional who thrives on selling a solution. You are highly energetic and have a sense of urgency in everything you do - particularly sales. You are passionate about the brands you represent and are looking to continue to grow and develop your career. You may have existing contacts you can bring to the role. You enjoy networking and nurturing key stakeholder relationships across all the markets and territories you cover. You are trusted by your customers. You have experience in facilitating complex sales demonstrations both online or in person. You are an expert at positioning a compelling value proposition to single decision-makers right up to large buyer groups. You seamlessly handle objections and close sales. You are a confident and experienced negotiator.</p>
<p>
simPRO's client base is increasing, and as a result, we require the skills of a Business Development Manager that can expand and continue to grow with our new focus on the NorthEast Region.</p>
<p>
This position is based in Washington DC or Maryland in an autonomous role and requires the successful candidate to work from a home office and report directly to the Head of Sales based in Colorado. There will be an initial, several weeks of training and onboarding, with time required at our US HQ in Broomfield, CO. The successful applicant will also possess a current full drivers licence as well as have reliable transportation.</p>
<p>
This is a unique and exciting opportunity to join an established, employee-friendly, global company with a new US presence that started in 2017 in Colorado and will grow exponentially across the United States in the next year.</p>
<p><b>
Role and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The primary purpose of the Business Development Manager is to be responsible for ensuring they proactively develop new business and achieve the sales targets for their region. Transactions are small in value ($600-$2,000 MRR) but high in numbers (5-7 per month).</li>
<li>
Understand how trade contracting and field service businesses work and be persuasive in promoting product benefits given specific circumstances and needs.</li>
<li>
Articulating and appreciating the workflows associated with trade contracting businesses is critical.</li>
<li>
Understand simPRO's software product range and associated add-ons and services at an expert level.</li>
<li>
Delivery of compelling and entertaining sales presentations to all types of buyers and buying groups involved in organizations both large and small.</li>
<li>
Develop and execute a sales plan with monthly and quarterly activity updates to expand business into new accounts throughout the region.</li>
<li>
Participate in weekly and monthly one-on-one and team forecast and pipeline reviews, meetings and training sessions to ensure ongoing improvement and best-practice sharing.</li>
<li>
Display a thorough understanding of opportunities and threats by market segment in the assigned regions and an in-depth knowledge of the strengths and weaknesses of alternative solutions.</li>
<li>
Generate referrals and qualified leads from industry trade shows, associations and regional networking events.</li>
<li>
Be available to travel through Texas and to Colorado for sales meetings, product demonstrations, trade shows, regional events, prospect affinity group gatherings plus attend, team meetings and conferences.</li>
<li>
Execute personal campaigns, activities and processes to build and nurture a pipeline of prospects, ensuring a high conversion rate to qualified lead status and close a high proportion of deals to ensure above quota results are consistently achieved.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
To be successful in this role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You have a successful, 3-5 year, selling career.</li>
<li>
You are collaborative, proactive and strategic in your approach.</li>
<li>
You have an innate 'can do' attitude and a flexibility to change.</li>
<li>
You are a team player and are prepared to roll up your sleeves and contribute, at times, outside your job requirements.</li>
<li>
You are able to generate your own leads and manage the entire pre-sales process.</li>
<li>
You can operate with autonomy.</li>
<li>
You are enthusiastic to make a difference in your customers lives by presenting the compelling business solution that simPRO offers.</li>
<li>
You are diligent and consistent in follow through and follow ups.</li>
<li>
You have advanced computing skills plus excellent written and verbal communication.</li>
<li>
You will thrive on achievement, exceeding targets and contributing to the growth of simPRO's and your customers business.</li>
<li>
As a highly articulate and well-presented individual, you represent your employer with pride.</li>
</ul>

Desirable Competencies:</b></p>

<li>
Enjoy working autonomously.</li>
