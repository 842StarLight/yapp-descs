<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></h3>
<p>
Candidate is required to operate a field service truck and perform service on Komatsu and other brands of heavy equipment in an assigned territory. Candidate will have the required ability to work independently in performing repairs and maintenance, including strong knowledge of diesel engines, hydraulics, and electronics. Individual should have strong diagnostic and repair skills.</p>

<p></p>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
DESCRIPTION IN DETAIL:</b></h5>
<ul>
<li>Perform heavy equipment inspections and repairs in the field</li>
<li>Capable of lifting 60lbs and able to climb on and off all machines</li>
<li>Diagnose, troubleshot and repair mechanical, electrical and hydraulic breakdowns onheavy equipment</li>
<li>Drive a service truck to various locations and make repairs</li>
<li>Work independently with no supervision</li>
<li>Work on Komatsu and other brands of heavy equipment</li>
<li>Must be willing to provide service above or below ground based on customerrequirements</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
EDUCATIONAL/EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS:</b></h5>
<ul>
<li>Qualified candidates must have 3+ years of successful hands-on experience with dieselengines, hydraulics, diagnostics, and be experienced with the latest electronicallycontrolled machines.</li>
<li>Must be able to fully comprehend hydraulic and electrical schematics with a history ofsuccessful troubleshooting and technical experience.</li>
<li>Must maintain daily service reports, equipment status reports, and log time worked, efficiently</li>
<li>Competent in MS Excel / Word / Outlook (email) for technical questions with equipmentmanufacturers and suppliers. We will provide training on the software (Microsoft Office)but you MUST know how and be willing to fill out daily service reports on a laptopcomputer, as this is part of the billing process.</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate professionally and in a service-oriented manner usingwritten, electronic and verbal process, to: customers, vendors, manufacturers, etc. fortechnical questions and support.</li>
<li>Successful candidate will be dispatched for repair of customer's on-site equipment asneeded.</li>
<li>Valid, clear driving record and background check are required for this position.</li>
<li>Basic tool set required for position; Company will provide some specialty tools/equipment.</li>
<br>
</ul>
