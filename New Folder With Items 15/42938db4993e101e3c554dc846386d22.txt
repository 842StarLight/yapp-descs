<h2>Senior Manager, Retail Systems</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Michael Kors is always interested in hearing from talented, globally-minded individuals with a passion for fashion.</b></h1>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
If you enjoy working in a creative, fast-paced environment, then we would love to hear from you!</b></h1>
<p></p>
<p>
This position will be responsible for strategy, design, implementation, and support of all Store Systems technology, including fixed POS workstations, mobile devices, windows workstations, RFID, and other retail store technology as required. Primary responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead and manage IT activities for store projects such as new store openings, renovations and remodels, as well as coordination and execution of deployments of all new technology in stores.<ul>
<li>
Build teams and partnerships with key business stake-holders and vendors to ensure technical project requirements and design are in-line with need.</li>
<li>
Create and present project budgets to key business stake-holders and management.</li>
<li>
Coordinate project activities for all internal IT and vendor partners as needed. Manage and maintain a schedule of deliverables and serve as primary point of contact for business, IT, PMO and vendor partners providing regular detailed project status updates.</li>
<li>
Follow Change Management procedures on all deployments.</li>
<li>
Responsible for resource management, both internal and external, to ensure project deliverables are achieved.</li>
<li>
Responsible for achieving budget and timeline goals, mitigating risks and managing business expectations.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Provide line management and leadership of the Level 2 Retail Helpdesk team, to include:<ul>
<li>
Full responsibility for the recruitment, motivation, training, and retention of staff.</li>
<li>
Manage Level 2 Helpdesk activities, including ticket/incident management, achievement of SLA target, and escalations to and monitoring of 3rd party providers.</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities to improve the store experience by partnering with internal and vendor partners as needed to identify and resolve recurring issues.</li>
<li>
Develop knowledge articles for Level 1 team to increase opportunities for first call resolution.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Partner with all business teams in support of Store Operations, Loss Prevention, Tax and Finance activities and needs.</li>
<li>
Partner with infrastructure and security teams to provide necessary support for technical updates / enhancements including design, testing and deployment.</li>
<li>
Coordinate global store systems teams on execution of all company-wide store deployments.</li>
<li>
Lead software and hardware evaluations and selections to support business requirements.</li>
<li>
Directly manage 3rd party vendor relationships in support of store projects and ongoing support activities, from identification and recommendation of vendors to partnering with Procurement on contract negotiations.</li>
<li>
Manage and track store technology inventory.</li>
<li>
Own the assigned processes from conceptualization through implementation, as well as future measurement and evolution of the process and systems.</li>
<li>
Benchmark retail industry trends to help to define and enhance the in-Store technology experience.</li>
<li>
Identify and recommend innovative solutions to streamline processes, reduce cost, implement best practices, or improve the overall in store experience.</li>
</ul>
<p>
QUALIFICATIONS</p>
<ul>
<li>
This individual must possess a strong understanding of support for in-Store Store Systems, Point of Sale, Mobile retail solutions, payment device management, inventory management tools, processes and devices, as well as networking concepts, with capacity to lead related projects and objectives.</li>
<li>
Candidate should have managed the implementation of complex Store Systems solutions within a retail space.</li>
<li>
Candidate should have a successful track record of creating and delivering effective, timely and cost-effective solutions to meet business objectives.</li>
<li>
Should be comfortable with being hands on in support of implemented solutions and work as a team player.</li>
<li>
Candidate must be able to gather information rapidly, work efficiently in a fast paced environment and be results oriented.</li>
<li>
Must be comfortable presenting and leading discovery sessions with business teams and vendors.</li>
<li>
Must possess facilitation and influencing skills to manage expectations, remove obstacles and mitigate risks.</li>
<li>
Proficient in MS Office products (Word, Excel, Power Point, Project, Visio).</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Windows Operating Systems and Apple iOS operating systems and device management.</li>
<li>
Experience with Landesk or similar remote deployment/support tools required</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience in hands on support of store technology</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience in people management in related area</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience managing vendor partners in complex retail deployment projects</li>
<li>
Experience with building, presenting and monitoring financial budgets</li>
<li>
Strong oral and written communication and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
