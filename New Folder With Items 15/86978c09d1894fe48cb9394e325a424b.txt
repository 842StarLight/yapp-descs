<h2>Customer Service Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><i>Customer Service Specialist </i>is responsible for coordinating effective execution of field service work through field service techs, 1 st Light Branches and/or third-party sub-contractors. Technical know-how to help determine need to dispatch service tech, excellent communication, desire to succeed and taking ownership are critical person characteristics needed for the position.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the primary point of contact with field service techs, 1 st Light branches and/or sub-contractors to effectively coordinate/communicate field service activities
</li>
<li>Serve as a point of contact to customers to effectively coordinate communication around field service activities and manage field service related customer inquiries and escalations
</li>
<li>Create and deliver/communicate service quotes to customers as required
</li>
<li>Create service cases and notate as necessary
</li>
<li>Update third party portal sites as necessary
</li>
<li>Manage direct customer communication and expectations
</li>
<li>Resolve field service escalations to resolution
</li>
<li>Review and approve sub-contractors quote(s)
</li>
<li>Submit quotes to third party owned system for service outside of scope
</li>
<li>Oversee service territory set up in scheduling software tool
</li>
<li>Aid in the on-boarding of new service tech and sub-contractors
</li>
<li>Deliver on-going training to service techs and sub-contractors
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p>Performs other duties and projects as assigned.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS AND WORK ENVIRONMENT:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this position. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the functions.
</li>
<li>While performing the duties of this position, the employee is regularly required to talk or hear. The employee frequently is required to use hands or finger, handle, or feel objects, tools or controls. The employee is occasionally required to stand; walk; sit; reach with hands and arms.
</li>
<li>The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 10 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this position include close vision, distance vision, color vision, peripheral vision, and the ability to adjust focus.
</li>
<li>The noise level in the work environment is usually moderate to high.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS (Education, Experience, Skills):
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED (Two years college or equivalent work experience preferred)
</li>
<li>Sales Force experience (preferred)
</li>
<li>Ability to read electrical diagrams and designs (preferred)
</li>
<li>Cheerful attitude and willingness to adapt to a fast-paced work environment
</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge of computers and Microsoft Office
</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task
</li>
<li>Demonstrated interest in renewable energy
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (oral and written) and a strong, friendly telephone presence
</li>
<li>Understanding of basic computer networking (preferred)</li>
</ul>
