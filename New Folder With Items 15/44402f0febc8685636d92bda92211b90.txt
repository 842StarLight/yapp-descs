<h2>Merchandiser (Omaha)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Merchandiser</b></p>
<p><b>OMAHA, NE</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b> Responsible for maintaining product availability and merchandising products at all designated accounts.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and maintain product displays in compliance with company standards</li>
<li>Maintain appropriate product levels in beverage sections and throughout accounts including shelf allocation and cold equipment.</li>
<li>Maintain clean and organized back-room and manage trade breakage in assigned accounts</li>
<li>Meet customer service expectations through daily communication, problem resolution, and appropriate follow up</li>
<li>Maintains professional, team relationship with co-workers and customers.</li>
<li>Transport, replace and maintain Point of Sale advertising as appropriate for account.</li>
<li>Maintain Merchandising standards while rotating products each time in designated accounts</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>No experience necessary – on the job training is performed.</li>
<li>High-School Diploma/GED</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license</li>
<li>Good driving record</li>
<li>Must be 18 years of age.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>SKILLS &amp; ABILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to operate a manual / powered pallet jack or lift product.</li>
<li>Demonstrated communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Ability to repetitively lift, pull and push 50+ pounds, reach above head height, stand, walk, kneel, bend and reach.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>WORK SCHEDULE</b>: 6:00am-Completion, 5 Day Workweek. Must have availability on weekends and all major holidays.</p>
