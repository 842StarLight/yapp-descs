<h2>Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>At Clinical Trial Media, we start every day striving to improve the patient journey and change the public perception of clinical trials. Our creative agency consists of a close-knit group of inspiring innovators, designers, self-starters, and producers. At CTM, we are passionate about improving the recruitment/retention process for patients, investigator sites and clients and we look to help sites do their best work. We are seeking an enthusiastic Sales Coordinator who is looking for an opportunity with growth potential. You will be the support for our Sales team in preparing presentations for clients and customer testimonials with success stories. You are extremely detail-oriented and tech-savvy with a strong sense of urgency.You should be an excellent communicator and comfortable managing multiple tasks. You also need to be a team player with a “can-do” attitude.
<br>
<br>
<b>Benefits of a full-time position at CTM include competitive compensation, health insurance including medical, dental and vision, 401k employer matching contributions, paid time off, life insurance, state-of-the-art office space, extensive job training, team building activities throughout the year, and a positive work environment that fosters on-going professional growth</b>.

<br>
<b>Sales Coordinator responsibilities include:</b>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the support of the sales and business development team</li>
<li>Develop all sales materials (Powerpoint presentations, case studies, client testimonials, etc.)</li>
<li>Coordinate proposal submissions</li>
<li>Maintain and coordinate Sales staff schedules</li>
<li>Produce budget grids</li>
<li>Support Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system to enable the tracking of performance metrics and produce reporting</li>
<li>Schedule meetings, coordinate logistics, attend and take notes, follow-up on action items</li>
<li>Provide support in planning, prepping, setting up, producing and breaking down all materials when attending sales conferences as part of the cross-functional events team</li>
<li>Assist marketing/creative team with social media content and postings</li>
<li>Conduct online research as necessary</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications include:</b>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience required</li>
<li>1-3 years of experience in a professional environment; experience supporting a sales department preferred</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office, especially Powerpoint, a must</li>
<li>Experience with social media posting a plus</li>
<li>Detail oriented and excellent time management</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Excellent communication and writing skills</li>
</ul>CTM is an equal opportunity employer.
