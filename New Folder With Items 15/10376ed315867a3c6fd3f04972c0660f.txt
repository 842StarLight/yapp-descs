<h2>Ticket Service Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Job Summary</b><br>
The Service and Retention Coordinator under the direction of the Vice President of Ticket Sales &amp; Service is responsible for assisting in maximizing Season Ticket Members renewal percentages by providing outstanding year-round customer service to assigned accounts by developing long-term relationships. Focus is on “Relationship Selling” by creating long lasting relationships with Season Ticket Members through interpersonal skills by using phone calls, emails, office visits and in-game visits, local events. Actively support the Mission Vison Values of the organization on a daily basis. Actively lead by example with Customer Service with all Ticket Holders, Daily Briefings, Power Hours, Snap Shots, &amp; related staff meetings.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Tasks and Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li> Handling client questions and concerns (via phone and email &amp; as well as in the Season Ticket Member Lounge).</li>
<li>Executing Season Ticket touch point program.</li>
<li>Assisting in game day responsibilities (in seat visits, Service Central &amp; Season Ticket Member Lounge).</li>
<li>General line support for sales and service calls &amp; ticket trade requests.</li>
<li>Assisting with mailings, invoices, and receipts.</li>
<li>Ticket deliveries when appropriate.</li>
<li>Introducing New Season Ticket Members to STM benefits including our Rookie STM Orientation event.</li>
<li>Reach new sales goals via Season, Group, Partial &amp; Flex Plan sales by maximizing add-ons and cross selling opportunities.</li>
<li>Assist with other tasks assigned by the Director of Special Projects &amp; Team President.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div>

<li>Quality presentation skills</li>
<li>Manage up when opportunities arise</li>
<li>Welcoming in-Arena Game Night Presence – Service with a smile</li>
<li>Team oriented</li>

</ul>
