<div><b>About the Position</b></div>
<div>You are the leader of the Reporting Professional Services (RPS) team. You will drive practice operations, have oversight for customer reporting requests, and train and develop team members. This is a client facing role, and you will be responsible for creating and executing the Statement of Work to deliver client reporting needs. This includes report writing requests and consultative report analytics to deliver BI solutions for our customers. To be successful in this role, you must possess a solid mix of experience with large data sets, client-facing professional services, managing people and parallel projects, and be ready to<b> Make A Difference</b>!</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Primary Duties</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Drive the RPS work stream to intake and deliver client business intelligence reporting requests</li>
<li>Create, manage and review reporting statement of work and change requests with customers</li>
<li>Align execution and delivery cycle times to the practice’s financial targets and forecasts</li>
<li>Provide progress dashboards, weekly status reports and monthly business reviews to the organization</li>
<li>Lead requirements discovery sessions with internal and external stakeholders, designing mock-ups, and reviews analysis artifacts with RPS team resources</li>
<li>Design, develop and validate BI solutions including engaging clients for acceptance testing</li>
<li>Provide direction and oversight to RPS activities and staff assignments</li>
<li>Mentor and provide guidance to the team, developing direct reports to ensure staff have appropriate training, skill levels, and expertise</li>
<li>Provide thought leadership to identify and develop BI best practices across the Reporting and Analytics platform</li>
<li>Make hiring decisions and conduct employee performance reviews</li>
<li>Provide reporting support to internal departments including Implementation Services, ODT, Sales, and Client Services.</li>
</ul>
