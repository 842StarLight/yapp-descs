<h2>Territory Manager - National Accounts</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
Sentry is seeking an experienced self-motivated and results driven individual to fulfill a highly skilled position as a Territory Manager for our National Accounts Division. As the Territory Manager for Sentry, you’ll work with brokers and agents in a defined territory to create Sentry awareness and generate new business submissions. You’ll be the team leader in the sales of loss sensitive casualty business to National Account clients with premiums in excess of $500,000.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<b>WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><p>
The Territory Manager will be responsible for all broker and client relationships in the territory, consisting of Kansas, Missouri, Nebraska and Arkansas. The candidate will reside in the territory, and travel regularly to meet with brokers, clients and prospects. As the team leader you’ll work closely with our Underwriting associates in order to obtain/retain large individual commercial accounts.</p>
<div>
Additionally, you’ll:<br>
<ul>
<li>Solicit accounts through selected brokers or in response to inquiries from prospective clients.</li>
<li>Work with Underwriting, Loss Control, and Claims to determine acceptability of the risk and services needed.</li>
<li>Serve as the primary contact with assigned accounts and their brokers.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the appropriate functional units to provide exceptional service to your accounts.</li>
<li>Deliver and present proposals to brokers and prospective clients and discuss Sentry advantages and differentiators.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><div>
In order to be eligible for this elite opportunity you must meet the following criteria:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree and equivalent work experience with National Accounts Casualty business.</li>
<li>Experience with Sales and Underwriting of large Risk Management type accounts is preferred.</li>
<li>5-8 years of experience with loss sensitive insurance programs, with an insurance carrier, commercial agency or brokerage preferred.</li>
<li>Previous technical insurance experience, preferably in sales and/or underwriting.</li>
<li>Highly motivated self-starter with the drive to be a top performer.</li>
<li>Exceptional communication, organization and selling skills.</li>
<li>Ability to travel extensively (5-7 nights per month) and have a valid driver’s license and ability to have and maintain an acceptable motor vehicle report.</li>
<li>Required to have primary residence within your sales territory.</li>
</ul>
</div>
