About Mars, Incorporated</b></p>
<p>
Mars is a family-owned business with more than a century of history making diverse products and offering services for people and the pets people love. With almost $35 billion in sales, the company is a global business that produces some of the world’s best-loved brands: M&amp;M’s®, SNICKERS®, TWIX®, MILKY WAY®, DOVE®, PEDIGREE®, ROYAL CANIN®, WHISKAS®, EXTRA®, ORBIT®, 5™, SKITTLES®, UNCLE BEN’S®, MARS DRINKS and COCOAVIA®. Mars also provides veterinary health services that include BANFIELD® Pet Hospitals, Blue Pearl®, VCA® and Pet Partners™. Headquartered in McLean, VA, Mars operates in more than 80 countries. The Mars Five Principles – Quality, Responsibility, Mutuality, Efficiency and Freedom – inspire its more than 100,000 Associates to create value for all its partners and deliver growth they are proud of every day.</p>

<p><b>
***Role can sit in Mount Olive, NJ or Chicago, IL***</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This job will be the a be one of the more senior project/ program managers within our Finance Transformation organisation, who are tasked with being trusted professionals, delivering IT capabilities and value-generating business change at scale. This role provides program leadership for large complex global business change programs or projects. The scope includes coordinating the organisation, direction and implementation of a dossier of project(s) and transformational activities to achieve the outcomes and realise the benefits of strategic importance to the organisation. They will be responsible for leading and managing the setting up of the program/project through to delivery of new capabilities, realisation of benefits and program/project closure. Their primary responsibility is for successful delivery of new capabilities and establishing governance.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This transformational project role is part of our overall digital acceleration program.</p>
