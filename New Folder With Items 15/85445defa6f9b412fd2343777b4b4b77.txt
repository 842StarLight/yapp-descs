<h2>Administrative Assistant II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>EnerSys is the global leader in stored energy solutions for industrial applications. We have over thirty manufacturing and assembly plants worldwide servicing over 10,000 customers in more than 100 countries. Worldwide headquarters are located in Reading, PA, USA with regional headquarters in Europe and Asia. We complement our extensive line of Motive Power and Energy Systems with a full range of integrated services and systems. With sales and service locations throughout the world, and over 100 years of battery experience, EnerSys is the power/full solution for stored DC power products. We continue to grow in our industry by ensuring all employees possess the following core competencies: customer focused, team player, engaged, ethical, strong communication skills, and works safely.</p>

<b>
Job Purpose</b>
</div>
<div><p>Scheduling customer orders and processing paperwork to meet service goals.</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Demonstrate a consistently positive, cooperative, self-motivated, courteous and professional attitude and demeanor.
</li>
<li>Regular in person attendance with on time arrival as scheduled. Punctuality and dependability are a job requirement.
</li>
<li>Proficient in computer software utilized in warehouse, shipping and receiving.
</li>
<li>Confer with customers to determine parts and or services required. Provide customers accurate information about unit prices, shipping dates, anticipated delays and other requested information.
</li>
<li>Ability to accurately enter data using electronic, paper, and other means to record information.
</li>
<li>Processes orders in the scheduled time.
</li>
<li>Verifies pricing and enters data into computer.</li>
<li>
Enter and track purchase orders.
</li>
<li>Train in all positions of customer service. Must be able to fill in in all customer service positions if needed.</li>
<li>
Create and analyze customer service reports.</li>
<li>
Enter, monitor and close production orders to schedule. Basic understanding of MRP.</li>
<li>
Participate in annual Physical Inventory.</li>
<li>
Maintain files for easy access and meet all regulations.</li>
<li>
Prepare/maintain international shipment paperwork.
 </li>
<li>Maintain Company pricing data base.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned by Supervisor</li>
</ul></div>
</div>

<b>
Minimum Qualifications:</b> A high school diploma or GED from an accredited institution and two years of administrative experience are required.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b> A four year business degree and two years of administrative experience in a high volume manufacturing based customer service facility.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
COMPETENCIES:</b></div>
<div>
1. Possess excellent written and verbal communication skills;</div>
<div>
2. Proficient computer skills in use of standard office software. Demonstrated ability to learn industry specific software and ability to create spreadsheets for analysis and tracking of various information. Negotiation skills a must for conflict resolution.</div>
<div>
3. Ability to interpret customer requests and relate to specific parts.</div>
<div>
4. Demonstrated planning and organizing, skills. Proven record of controlling parallel and multiple tasks and projects.</div>
<div>
5. Must be creative, have excellent analytical skills, possess high personal performance standards and be comfortable operating in a fast paced environment.</div>
<div>
6. Ability to verbally communicate with customers telephonically using standard business English and acceptable grammar.</div>
<div>
7. Mathematical Skills: Understand and perform business mathematical concepts such as addition, subtraction, ratios, discounts, multipliers, and data interpretation. Ability to apply concepts such as fractions, percentages, ratios, and proportions to practical situations.</div>
<div>
8. Reasoning Ability: Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations with some standardization. Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.</div>
<div>
9. Computer Skills: Proficient in the use of business software and computer hardware. Experience in utilizing different software applications.</div>
