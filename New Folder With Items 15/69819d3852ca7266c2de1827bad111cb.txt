<h2>Key Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Questel specializes in Intellectual Property + Innovation software and services. As a key account manager, you will be managing about 50 client accounts (across the south East region), while also targeting new prospects in this same region.</p>
<p>Daily tasks may consist of:</p>
<ul><li>Travelling across the designated territory (about 25-30% of the time), to develop marketing and sales activities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Providing technical support to the existing and potential customer base.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Researching and developing new methods of reaching prospective customers and targeting new markets.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborating with IP professionals, acquiring an extensive knowledge of Intellectual Property laws.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attending conferences and user meetings.</li></ul>
