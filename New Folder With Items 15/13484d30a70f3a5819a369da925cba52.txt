<h2>Systems Integration Test Eng I - Temp</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>KARL STORZ SE &amp; Co. KG based in Tuttlingen, Germany, is a family-owned, global company committed to benefiting humanity by advancing medical technology through innovation and education.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>For more than 70 years, KARL STORZ has been dedicated to earning its international reputation as a leader that designs, engineers, manufactures, and markets all its products with an emphasis on visionary design, precision craftsmanship, and clinical effectiveness.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>
System Integration Test Engineer I Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Develop custom software and/or use existing software to perform Integration test and measurement activities defined below.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide early feedback to development team (systems engineers, software architects, etc.) on robustness of system components as they are integrated.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Perform hardware and software integration and informal verification activities</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>‘White Box’ intrusive testing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>‘Black Box’ verification testing</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Debug and characterize defects, providing detailed configuration and descriptions for passing and failing tests for product evaluation reports.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Exploratory system testing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Automation of software and hardware verification.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Analyze test results to ensure existing functionality and recommend corrective action.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Consult with system and development engineers in resolution of problems.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Help develop verification test plans.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide Software Quality Metrics.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Performance metrics (CPU utilization, memory usage, etc.)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Quality metrics (bugs/release, static analysis warnings, etc.)</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support Bench-(alpha) and Market Acceptance (Beta) testing by providing and/or updating equipment required for testing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work with and assist Student Interns.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assists product development engineers in the diagnosis and replication of product failures.</p>
<p>Regular attendance is an essential job function.</p>
<p>Will spend part of his/her time as a member of continuous improvement teams undertaking projects and seeking ways to improve the quality of KSI’s products and services.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p><b>SKILLS, KNOWLEDGE, AND LICENSE OR CERTIFICATE REQUIRED:</b></p>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Must have strong analytical skills and good technical report writing skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must have good interpersonal and communication skills, both verbal and written.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Desired: experience with object oriented methods and languages: C++, C#, Java, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Desired: experience with Python programing language.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Knowledge of Linux.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Desired: familiarity with Internet technologies, communication protocols, networking, network security, and related techniques.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience with Software, Hardware and Firmware Systems Integration.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience with Integration Test and Software Testing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Self-motivated and able to produce high quality work alone or within a team.</p>
</li>
</ul>
