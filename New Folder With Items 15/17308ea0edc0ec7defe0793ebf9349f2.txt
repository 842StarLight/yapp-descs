<h2>Logistics Project Manager (Heavy Haul)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>SUMMARY</p>
<p><br>
<br>
Responsible for all aspects of logistics support from obtaining all necessary drawings and packing list for estimating projects supporting the Sales/Commercial Teams to executing all projects with the Project Management Team on time and on budget. This includes all modes of transporting, including ocean, air, rail, ground, import/export, specialized transport of over size, over weight equipment and rigging operations. This includes analyzing current and historical rates, identifying areas for improvement while meeting the service expectations of both internal and external customers. Understand and comply with import/export compliance requirements. Maintain and track shipment status for all auxiliary cargo to keep the necessary teams updated internally and at project site locations.</p>
<p><b>
MUST HAVE:</b><br>
<br>
<b>International shipping experience - import/export operations/compliance</b><br>
<b>Trucking experience - oversized, permit loads</b><br>
<b>Knowledge of ocean transport and port operations</b><br>
<b>Solid understanding of Incoterms</b><br>
<b>Contract experience - knowing Terms and Conditions, liability, etc.</b><b><br>
</b></p>
<p><br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>
Interact with Project &amp; Logistics Managers on the transportation of Gas Turbine &amp; Steam Turbine and miscellaneous equipment:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Perform Route Surveys for moving major equipment to identify best method, low risk solutions identifying obstacles and challenges to include all detailed plans and modes of transport like ocean, barge, rail, heavy haul, truck, etc.</p></li>
<li><p>
Perform risk mitigation plans and optimization opportunities to balance cost savings with on time delivery
</p></li>
<li><p>Provide up to date transport plans and schedules</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide Advance Shipment Notifications to site based on contract requirements</p></li>
<li><p>
Accumulate, consolidate and distribute packing list data (preliminary and actual) as required</p></li>
<li><p>
Support weekly delivery meetings with the customer and/or site</p></li>
<li><p>
Support the Transportation &amp; Logistics Project Manager as necessary</p></li>
<li><p>
Import/Export Operations and Compliance – receive, review and approve import/export invoicing packets</p></li>
<li><p>
Receive transport invoices and ensure compliance with contract for payment processing and payment</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain records and reports as required</p></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><p>

<li><p>
Manage any claims that result from damage, delays or charges related to the transportation of equipment.</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop and maintain a good working relationship with Site Supervisors and EPC end customers.</p></li>
<li><p>
Monitor and evaluate sub-contractor work to insure and confirm the work performed on is done in a professional manner with quality workmanship and materials.</p></li>
<li><p>
Cooperate, negotiate, and correspond with MHPSA/MHPS clients and/or any other 3rd party in a professional manner while representing MHPSA in any business related transactions.</p></li>
