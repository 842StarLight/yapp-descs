<h2>Market Product Manager II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>This is a remote opportunity ideally located in Minneapolis, MN, Houston, TX, or Stoughton, MA.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>ABOUT SICK, INC.:</p>
<p>SICK is a leading global provider of intelligent sensors, systems and services for factory, logistics and process automation applications. With more than 1,000 patents, innovation and technology are at its core. This focus on innovation and “Sensor Intelligence” have allowed SICK to develop products for every phase of production in the automotive, packaging, electronics, food and beverage, consumer goods, storage and conveyor, robotics, material handling, oil and gas, chemical, power, maritime industries and more. In addition, SICK’s focus on Sensor Intelligence allows us to make Industry 4.0, or the Industrial Internet of Things, a reality for their customers.</p>
<p></p>
<p>POSITION SUMMARY:</p>
<p>This position is responsible for the overall market success of SICK, INC.’s product portfolio within our defined focus segments throughout the entire product lifecycle. Primary responsibilities include providing the highest level of product knowledge, product positioning, and marketing expertise to customers, sales teams, and channel partners to develop and improve SICK, INC.’s competitive advantage, and market share. Create and improve SICK, INC.'s competitive advantage and process efficiencies to consistently build profitable revenue growth in assigned Business Unit(s).</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This position exhibits clear leadership in the four pillars:</p>
<ul><li> Technical - Technical excellence in assigned Business Unit(s).</li></ul>
<ul><li> Commercial, Tools - Commercial excellence in Sales &amp; customer engagement in assigned Business Unit(s) utilizing common tools (CRM, SalesPro, et cetera).</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaboration - Collaboration excellence in intra-subsidiary communication and with headquarters in assigned Business Unit(s).</li></ul>
<ul><li> Business - Business excellence in assigned Business Unit(s) with full ownership of portfolio revenue and margin, budget, and forecast commitments.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Market Product Management is involved in all six phases of the Product Management Process.</p>
<ul><li> Strategic Assessment</li></ul>
<ul><li> Midterm &amp; Budget Planning</li></ul>
<ul><li> Product Development Process</li></ul>
<ul><li> Product Launch Process</li></ul>
<ul><li> Product Lifecycle</li></ul>
<ul><li> Product Phase Out</li></ul>
