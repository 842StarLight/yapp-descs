<h2>Branch Operation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Job Duties<br>
Branch Banking Operations<br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and supervise the branch’s operations activities ensuring compliance regulations with the bank’s policies and procedures and government regulations.</li>
<li>Ensure the branch receives a satisfactory rating on internal audits.</li>
<li>Supervise the branch’s daily activities and staff to ensure operating losses are minimized.</li>
<li>Attend required monthly/quarterly operations meeting with the Retail Support Department and inform the Branch Manager and branch employees of the up-to-date policies, procedures, and system applications.</li>
<li>Assist branch manager in allocating manpower and other resources, and to analyze activities of the branch to develop optimum efficiency and productivity in the operation processes and customer service.</li>
<li>Support branch manager to maintain operating, personnel, and other expense within budget.</li>
<li>Provide recommendations for improvement of branch operations.</li>
<li>Ensure that the branch premises are secure and branch assets are protected.</li>
<li>Ensure the branch's appearance is maintained in accordance with Bank standards.</li>
<li>Ensure all branch staff successfully completes all mandatory training assigned in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Coach the branch staff on operating procedures, customer service, technical and product knowledge.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Customer Services Management<br>
- Supervise branch staff to ensure the branch exceeds customer expectations in terms of accuracy, efficiency, courtesy and professionalism. - Ensure that customer problems and complaints are managed professionally and effectively, responded to in a timely manner and maintained at a minimum level.<br>
<br>
Customer Acquisition and Development<br>
<ul>
<li>Identify and understand basic customer needs in order to complete transactions with accuracy speed, efficiency and certainty.</li>
<li>Meet with current and potential customers, to discuss their financial requirements; sell appropriate consumer and commercial financial services products to meet their needs while developing and expanding account relationships.</li>
<li>Identify cross-sell opportunities, including opportunities to refer customers to other sales representatives (CRE, C&amp;I, PCM, MLO, Primevest) through proper customer profiling and needs-based selling.</li>
<li>Open deposit accounts on bank’s system ensuring all documentation requirements, policies and procedures are adhered to.</li>
<li>Actively participate in all bank sales campaigns and promotions and exceed targets set.</li>
<li>Maintain superior and courteous service to promote products and expand customer relationships.</li>
<li>Exceed customer expectations in terms of speed, efficiency, certainty and professionalism.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Qualifications &amp; Prerequisites<br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of customer services experience in the branch banking industry or related environment, which including 3+ years of branch operations experience is preferable.</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of Branch Banking-related information technology and banking regulations and procedures.</li>
</ul>
- Knowledge of financial reports, branch banking operations, branch efficiency and productivity, audit procedures, and the Bank’s laws and regulations in operation areas. - Strong communication and inter-personal skills.<br>
<ul>
<li>Basic PC skills and Microsoft Word and Excel competency.</li>
<li>Fluent in English and Chinese (mandarin preferred) a major plus.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business or related field is required.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
