<h2>Business Support Manager - Capabilities - Wholesale</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>
<p>
Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p>
Wholesale Banking provides financial solutions to businesses across the United States and globally. Our four major business lines include Corporate &amp; Investment Banking, Commercial Banking, Commercial Real Estate, and Wells Fargo Commercial Capital. We also have groups in credit risk, group risk, finance, marketing, human relations, and the Wholesale Chief Operating Office that support our businesses.</p>
<p>
We believe that Process Excellence is made up of elements of leadership, teamwork, methods, and practices. We believe it is foundational to the repeatability of any operation, service, function or capability we utilize in the bank. When learned and embedded into a business culture, a business can transform many key attributes including quality, cost, risk and productivity. Ultimately, we want to create an environment that maximizes the talent of our team members and our knowledge of Process Excellence so that we can deliver world class solutions and services to our customer base.</p>
<p>
We are seeking an experienced professional to join us to help develop and deliver a world class Business Process Management (BPM) capability to the Wells Fargo Wholesale group. This position will lead a team of process architect, production support, process librarian and quality assurance team members in supporting Wells Fargo Wholesale BPM.</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for maintaining a current and accurate view of Consent Order Commitment Number (CCN) of processes, to include detailed audit trail of every change and the business justification and approvals wot include into key routines.</li>
<li>Develop and manage a transparent, stable and comprehensive change requests capability that satisfies all stakeholders needs and addresses all assets (BPM production, Controls in SHRP, TLD, Legacy BPRM, WIP, etc).</li>
<li>Drive adoption of new process and publish regular reports to Process Champions on status.</li>
<li>Obtain stability. Eliminate noise in the process, processing all CR since 2/28 with validate audit trail, by all LOBs and the Controls Organization Migrate to EBPM SOR.</li>
<li>Establish process inventory governance process (aligned very closely with the Change request process, where adds, removes, and other significant changes are reviewed and approved by wholesale stakeholders as defined in Wholesale BPM Change Control Procedures</li>
<li>Maintain all Process and Procedure documentation</li>
<li>Ensure that the Controls requirements are executed on we migrate to one view of all changes.</li>
<li>Educate and provide viability as needed into legacy BIKE inventory and lineage</li>
<li>Process and stabilize International changes and provide approved audit trail.</li>
<li>Build and manage a team or Process Architects that will define, validate and constantly improved value streams and processes adherence to PAT model. Ensure they are trained and consistent with EBPM standards and align to LOBs to develop SME. Partner with the Lead librarian to ensure consistency across teams and standards are met.</li>
<li>Via ongoing inventory and process model analysis , identify improvement / control opportunities within and across value streams, LOBs, WF.</li>
<li>Manage the pre-sprint factory efforts in partnership with EBPM, LOB Librarians and the WPMO.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Team members support our focus on building strong customer relationships balanced with a strong risk mitigating and compliance-driven culture which firmly establishes those disciplines as critical to the success of our customers and company. They are accountable for execution of all applicable risk programs (Credit, Market, Financial Crimes, Operational, Regulatory Compliance), which includes effectively following and adhering to applicable Wells Fargo policies and procedures, appropriately fulfilling risk and compliance obligations, timely and effective escalation and remediation of issues, and making sound risk decisions. There is emphasis on proactive monitoring, governance, risk identification and escalation, as well as making sound risk decisions commensurate with the business unit’s risk appetite and all risk and compliance program requirements.</p>
<p>
As a Team Member Manager, you are expected to achieve success by leading yourself, your team, and the business. Specifically you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead your team with integrity and create an environment where your team members feel included, valued, and supported to do work that energizes them.</li>
<li>Accomplish management responsibilities which include sourcing and hiring talented team members, providing ongoing coaching and feedback, recognizing and developing team members, identifying and managing risks, and completing daily management tasks.</li>
</ul>
