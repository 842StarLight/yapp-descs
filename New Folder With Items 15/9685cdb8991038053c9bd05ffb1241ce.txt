 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Come work at a place where innovation and teamwork come together to support the most exciting missions in the world!

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>
<p>The competition driven shift towards mobile applications and FinTech enabled APIs delivered out of the cloud is transforming how financial services organizations build applications with faster time to market and innovation. Standing up and tearing down build environments and pushing to production isn’t just about the app, but the entire end to end delivery of the app inclusive of underlying services such as the network, managing performance and security into a variety of environments. To meet greater business agility, development teams are evolving their capabilities to support greater network and security automation via robust application services as they embrace managing across private and public clouds.
</p>
<p>The Solutions Marketing team is looking for an Industry Vertical Marketing manager, who is experienced in campaign marketing to key verticals such as Financial Services, Federal Government, and others. You’ll have a solid background in campaign strategy and execution which reach and engage audiences in a compelling way. You will know which channels and content work best to get your message out for a given vertical, whether web, email, social, paid media, or others. You’ll be skilled at measuring the success of your work, tracking customer engagement leading to sales pipeline and wins. Culturally, you should espouse the benefits of agile marketing, love rapidly iterating on campaign delivery, and not be afraid to take risks and fail fast!
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Core Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<p><b>Customer Experiences
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create compelling vertical campaigns to drive increased relevance and understanding of F5’s value to Financial Services (and other verticals).
</li>
<li>Partner with peers in product marketing to curate content to engage the audience and grow their skills with thought leadership, standard methodologies, reference architectures, use cases and training resources.
</li>
<li>Build signature moments and support launches based on F5 innovations to advance the solutions in the market.
</li>
<li>Use rich customer, firmographic, demographic and content engagement data to deliver highly personalized and targeted communications to help drive the right journey path at an individual level.
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Core skills
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Build and demonstrate market intelligence of financial services needs, how they purchase, and their selection criteria
</li>
<li>Grow insight, obtain knowledge and analyze data for highly targeted and relevant communications to financial services customers especially
</li>
<li>Communicate the value and impact of your work internally with sales, channel, and partner teams via webcasts, direct meetings, marketing communications, and industry events
</li>
<li>Can prepare monthly reports which articulate business performance
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Other skills
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 4 years of experience in solution, product, or industry marketing in the enterprise IT market.
</li>
<li>Highly desired is experience in financial services marketing (or industry vertical marketing)
</li>
<li>BA/BS degree required
</li>
<li>Experience developing and leading to critical metrics and quantified performance measurements.
</li>
<li>Experience in the entire customer lifecycle from acquisition to nurture to post-purchase engagement.
</li>
<li>Good teamwork and negotiation skills, and the ability to work effectively across the organization.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of enterprise marketing and sales processes.
</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills.
</li>
<li>Very strong written, verbal and presentation communication skills including to executive audiences.
</li>
<li>Has good understanding of cloud and networking technologies
</li>
<li>May require the ability to travel via automobile or airplane, approximately 10% of the time may be spent traveling.
</li>
</ul>
