<h2>Independent Marketing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Are you a college student or a stay-at-home mom or dad with free hours each week? Are you looking for ways to supplement your full-time job income? Valpak is looking for independent marketing consultants (IMCs) in the Tampa Bay market. This is an opportunity for independent contractors only.</b></p>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<b>GREAT, SO WHAT IS AN IMC?</b><br>
They are naturally energetic and passionate people who have great networking and social skills. They are often interested in participating in their local communities by getting to know the owners of the neighborhood pizza shop, the guys at the auto garage, and the owners of the hair and nail salons.<br>
An IMC would seek out and meet owners of local businesses in an assigned territory and share with them how a Valpak advertising campaign can bring them new leads and customers. Every day is different and interesting!<br>
A successful IMC would:<br>
<ul>
<li>Attend a 1-day, no obligation introductory training to learn about Valpak’s products and advertising opportunities</li>
<li>Contact qualifying local businesses to garner interest in Valpak</li>
<li>Utilize a self-guided tool to target clients and introduce Valpak products to potential advertisers</li>
<li>Submit qualified leads to internal sales reps</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>SO, WHAT KIND OF FOLKS ARE YOU LOOKING FOR?</b><br>
We’re looking for awesome people. Bonus points if you have previous sales skills, but not a requirement. In a nutshell, this opportunity is for people who:<br>
<ul>
<li>Love interacting and connecting with people</li>
<li>Have a passionate and persuasive can-do attitude</li>
<li>Take initiative in generating new Valpak business in local neighborhoods</li>
<li>Are meticulously organized and always have a plan of action</li>
<li>Can walk into a place of business or call a business and have a focused yet friendly conversation</li>
<li>Can use laptops, tablets, and smartphones</li>
<li>Can get excited about setting a goal then meeting and exceeding that goal</li>
<li>Love what they do, and enjoy being a positive contributor to the local Valpak team</li>
</ul>
There are no sales quotas. As an independent contractor, you will have the flexibility to determine your hours, your sales targets within your assigned territory, and the methods you prefer to generate sales leads.</div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<b>HOW WILL I GET PAID?</b><br>
<ul>
<li>$50 for each qualified lead</li>
<li>Additional $100 for each qualified lead that turns into a sale</li>
</ul>
</div>
