<p><b>
Position Summary
</b></p>
<p>The Manager of Business Development will be a key new member of the Business Development team, reporting to the Vice President of Corporate Development. Based in Hoffman Estates, this global function will be responsible to manage various aspects that support Business Development (Partnerships / Strategic Alliance) activities for the company. Primary responsibilities include the development of Commercial, Reseller, and Strategic Alliance Agreements and then acting as a primary point of contact for post-deal oversight. Please note that although there will be support provided by Associates on the Corporate Development team, this will be an individual contributor manager position.</p>
<p>
The position will have heavy interaction with key senior level management of the business (ex., CEO, CFO, etc.) so highly skilled presentation capabilities are a must.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities &amp; Essential functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Identify, research, and develop future business development opportunities across CDK portfolio</li>
<li>
Source new partnering opportunities to advance CDK’s goals and bring them forward to business leaders to ensure alignment</li>
<li>
Lead and co-lead pre-deal activities including identifying opportunity, negotiating term sheet, developing business cases, preparing presentations on the opportunity for executive leadership and coordinating with legal and other departments on closing of the opportunity
</li>
<li>Key (and oftentimes primary) business contact for post-deal management including resolving contractual issues, managing and negotiating any amendments and preparing post-deal reporting to the executive team</li>
<li>
Managing revenue from partnerships including revenue risk mitigation and driving new ideas to grow revenue from existing partnership</li>
<li>
Day to day partnering with Director and Vice President level leaders across the organization from deal creation through ongoing relationship. Strong communication and partnership skills is a must.
</li>
<li>Leading quarterly partnership reviews with key internal stakeholders and drafting and publishing report to the executive team.</li>
<li>
Lead or contribute to special/ad-hoc internal projects as appropriate The Manager needs to balance multiple projects, be results oriented, and drive projects to completion according to deal timelines in a team environment</li>
</ul>
