<p><b>Experienced Salesperson Commission-Only Position (Work Remotely)</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Description: </b>Open position for a sponsorship-based, commission-only, for-profit promotion campaign with a fundraiser component for a publishing company.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Job duties:</b>Through your independent lead generation, you will find individuals, businesses and corporations to sponsor copies of one of our family-friendly books to be given to cancer patients and to raise funds for book promotion (for-profit) and to help fund research for lower-toxic cures for pediatric cancers and help families in crisis during cancer treatment (non-profit).
</p>
<p>Total minimum campaign goal is to find sponsors for 24,000 books ($600,000).</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Experience Level:</b> Experienced/Advanced</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Job Types:</b> Contract, Commission</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Salary:</b> $90,000.00 to $120,000.00 /year</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Work hours:</b> Flexible. You decide how many hours you need to work to reach your target of a minimum of 2,000 sponsored books ($50,000)/month.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Commission:</b> 15% for up to $2,000 sponsored books/month; 20% for any amount above 2,000 sponsored books in a calendar month.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Base salary: </b>None</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Job Type:</b> Commission-only, contract</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Room for growth:</b> Yes</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Location:</b> Work remotely from home.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Pay frequency:</b> You decide (weekly, semi-monthly, or monthly) via PayPal or company check.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Special</b> <b>Note:</b> This is a pilot program that is intended to be rolled into a permanent, ongoing program if the pilot program is successful. This job could turn into a full-time position if desired.</p>
