<p></p>
<p><b>Purpose and Objective:</b></p>
<p>Concur Technologies, Inc. seeks a Technical Writer (Sales Support Senior Specialist) at our Atlanta, GA location to support bid/proposal development for bilingual English-Spanish and/or Portuguese clients in response to Request for Proposals (RFPs), Tenders, Requests for Information (RFIs), Requests for Quote (RFQs), and Security/Technical Questionnaires.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Expectations and Tasks:</b></p>
<p>Be an integral part of the Proposal team, facilitating proposal content development and specialized document content. Serve as primary project manager/contributing writer/editor to proposals, and serve as the liaison to subject matter experts within the company for specialized written materials. Support all phases of the proposal development process including review and understanding of Tender/RFP opportunity requirements, project management, merging of product write-ups, drafting new content, copy editing, and rewrites. Work closely with internal subject matter experts (SMEs) to draft concise, complete, and clear proposal responses to customer requirements and/or questions in accordance with established company guidelines. Identify and communicate content gaps for SME response and further database development. Create supporting collateral for the RFP Team as well as sales self-service. Facilitate internal requests for information relating to sales RFPs &amp; other sales opportunities. Facilitate internal requests for information relating to existing contracts, including coordination with internal legal resources. 10% travel required.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and Qualifications/Skills and Competencies:</b></p>
<p>Bachelor's degree in Information Systems, Business Administration, Management, or related field and 6 years of experience required. Will accept a Master's degree and 4 years of experience.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work Experience:</b></p>
<p>Experience must involve 3 years of related experience (sales/marketing/business development) in the Cloud SaaS, business application industry. Experience must also include delivering end-to-end Proposal Management services to an enterprise sales organization, such as the Cloud Software industry, including strategy, expertise, partnering, and execution on customer bid pursuits; Working knowledge of Cloud Software architecture, privacy, compliance, and security concepts as demonstrated through experience responding to customer security questionnaires/risk assessments; Following processes and executing enablement to optimize sales productivity; Delivering winning, multi-million-dollar solutions or complex services responses to RFx; Providing clear feedback to management regarding bid projects and other team initiatives; Building relationships and internal networks with teams and individuals to facilitate aligned support and serve as a representative of the bid and proposal management team; and advising on and adhering to bid and proposal management methodology best practices. Experience must include Proposal Project Management, including: maintaining schedules, organizing resources, coordinating inputs and reviews, ensuring bid strategy<br>
implementation, ensuring compliance, resolving internal team issues, and providing process leadership. Experience must include : working closely with SMEs at all levels of the organization, to bring in functional, technical, finance, and legal expertise when required to respond to prospect/client requirements; conducting legal review of proposal terms and conditions to identify red flags, and to articulate legal concerns to the sales team and in house legal teams; assembling proposal response teams, resources and tools to<br>
generate proposals under strict deadline constraints; evaluating RFx opportunities and providing input into opportunity qualification, win strategies, and business discussions to the sales team; collaborating with the account team to understand the customer/prospect’s needs and how best to articulate software and services offerings in the Cloud to meet those needs;<br>
Leadership of kickoff calls to discuss RFx requirements, assignments and timelines; Reviewing proposal documents for ethical and legal issues to ensure the bid response is compliant with corporate policies. Experience must include business writing experience and editing abilities that result in the design and creation of proposal content for both active pursuits and team collateral for future pursuits. Experience must include facilitating the development of key messaging, win themes, differentiators and proof statements, while shaping the alignment of the RFx with the value proposition, SAP differentiators and deal structure; Reviewing content provided by SMEs to ensure it is compliant, responsive and supports the win theme and messaging that meets the customer’s requirements; Applying suitable templates, messaging, and layouts that are compliant with RFx requirements for customer proposals, presentations, while creating standard and non-standard documentation, including enclosures, appendices, etc.; Performing proofread and edit to ensure that final proposal content is consistent with corporate branding; and conducting gap analysis of existing database content to harvest appropriate re-usable content for contribution to the content management database from completed proposal projects. 10% travel required.</p>
