<h2>Outside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Blue Book Building and Construction Network is the largest, most active commercial construction network, offering products and services including free digital tools that help the industry improve its workflow – from design through post-construction.</p>
<p>We serve General Contractors, Subcontractors, Suppliers, Manufacturers, Architects, Engineers, Building Owners, Transportation and Utilities Professionals, Real Estate Developers, Facility and Property Managers and Government Agencies.</p>
<p>We are looking for an experienced sales entrepreneur for one of our <b>Colorado </b>territories covering <b>Ft. Collins, Loveland, Longmont, Greeley and Cheyenne, WY.</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct sales presentations with existing and prospective customers.</li>
<li>Meet with General Contractors in office, on job sites and at association meetings.</li>
<li>Demonstrate a strong understanding of products, promotions and market trends.</li>
<li>Properly prepare for appointments by building proposals, preparing objections.</li>
<li>Maintain current knowledge of the latest company and industry developments by attending sales meetings and training.</li>
</ul>
