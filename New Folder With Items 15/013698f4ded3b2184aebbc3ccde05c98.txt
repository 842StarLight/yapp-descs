<h2>International Logistics Specialist - Fort Myers</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>R+L Global Logistics </b>is seeking an <b>International Logistic Specialist </b>(AE) to join our Global Team. The International Logistics Specialist will both develop and operate our Air/Ocean and Import/Export service offerings.</p>
<p>
New College Graduates obtaining a degree in Business, Supply Chain, Transportation, Logistics, Marketing, International Business, Sales or other Business related majors are encouraged to apply.</p>
<p><b>
International Logistic Specialist </b><b>Summary of Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and essential job functions include, but are not limited to the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Timely preparation of rates and quotes for customers and overseas agents</li>
<li>
Coordinate the routing of import and export bookings with airline and ocean carriers</li>
<li>
Support the development of existing and new customers by working closely with your Branch Manager and the entire sales organization</li>
<li>
Collaborate and build relationships with customers, carriers, and agent representatives regarding air, ocean, import, and export products to strengthen service competitiveness</li>
<li>
Prepare all necessary shipping documentation related to international bookings and process all import/export documentation to clearing agents to include: (HAWB's, MAWB's, AES's, Manifest, Shipping Instructions, Haz Cargo Decl., Bill of Ladings, and Certificate of Origin, etc.)</li>
<li>
Dispatch pick-ups to authorized agents and drayage companies, shipping lines, airlines, trucking companies, clearing agents, warehouses, and all other parties involved in the shipment</li>
<li>
Track and trace shipments, researching and resolving any issues regarding billing or shipping discrepancies, communicate timely correspondence back to both customers and vendors</li>
<li>
Act as a liaison with Custom Brokers and agents, along with Ocean and Air Terminal Operators</li>
<li>
Support your branches quarterly metrics while obtaining if not exceeding your individual metrics</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
International Logistic Specialist </b><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to work in a blended operations and customer development role</li>
<li>
Self motivated, assertive, and responds to branch metrics</li>
<li>
Reads, analyzes and interprets highly complex international regulations and procedures</li>
<li>
Manages complex transactional or emotional customer situations promptly and professionally</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask in a fast paced environment with rapidly changing priorities</li>
<li>
Contributes to building a positive team spirit and puts success of team above own interests</li>
<li>
Ability to read, write, and speak English fluently</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
International Logistic Specialist </b><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>
Summary of </b><b>International Logistic Specialist </b><b>Experience and Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High school Diploma or GED</li>
<li>
Preferred but not required, a Bachelor's Degree in Business with emphasis in Supply Chain Management, Transportation and Logistics, Sales and Marketing, or International Business</li>
<li>
Minimum of two years International Forwarding Experience</li>
<li>
Detailed knowledge of operational procedures regarding air, ocean, import/export business</li>
<li>
Strong customer service, customer development, or business development experience</li>
<li>
Knowledge of state, federal, and international applicable laws and regulations i.e. customs, TSA, security, and all other import and export government agencies that regulate international trade</li>
<li>
Prior experience with Cargowise software system preferred but not required</li>
<li>
Experience in Microsoft Office Suite and other spreadsheet applications</li>
</ul>
