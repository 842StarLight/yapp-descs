JOB SUMMARY (Primary purpose of the position.)<br>
We are currently seeking an energetic, versatile solutioning expert to join our Application development team. This individual must be an experienced business solutions architect with a strong track record in successfully transforming business processes using IT proven tools. Someone with extensive Microsoft .NET Framework development and architecture experience and Microsoft stack technology background that includes familiarity and experience with a broad range of software development best practices, architecture patterns, technologies, frameworks and tools such as Visual Studio, SCM, CI, MS SQL Server, MVC, jQuery, CSS, Bootstrap, SaaS, Enterprise, Multi-threaded software, REST, Azure, and more. Application Engineer is responsible for designing, developing and implementing programs and applications. Also perform prototyping, application tests and code reviews to ensure product is satisfactory.<br>
