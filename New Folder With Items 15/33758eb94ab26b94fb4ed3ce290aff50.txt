<h2>Commercial Insurance Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Position Summary:</p>
<p>The Account Manager serves as atechnical resource for clients, advisors, Insurance Company Partners andprospects while providing prompt, efficient, high-quality service fordesignated accounts.</p>
<p>PrincipalResponsibilities:</p>
<p>· Responsiblefor gathering the information and risk management recommendations for renewalsand for delivering renewals and/or binders as needed.</p>
<p>· Managesall policy activity aspects of assigned accounts including endorsements andinvoicing.</p>
<p>· Presentsrisks to the insurance marketplace for quoting.</p>
<p>· Maintainsa concern for timeliness and completeness when interacting with clients, theorganization and Insurance Company Partners to minimize potential for errorsand omissions claims.</p>
<p>· Receivesphone calls and office visitors requesting quotes, changes to existing coverageand/or new policies. Determines acceptability and placement, completesapplications or endorsements, and collects premium when applicable.</p>
<p>· Reviewsexisting accounts to determine if additional lines of insurance should besolicited and does so by mail and/or phone prior to renewal.</p>
<p>· Activelyseeks referrals from current client base to solicit for new business prospects;follows up to generate new business using prospect database and automation system.</p>
<p>· Cultivatesstrong relationships with Insurance Company Partners and is responsible forknowing carrier requirements and attributes.</p>
<p>· Looksfor opportunities to improve the firm, business segment and processes. Bringsissues and discrepancies to appropriate leadership.</p>
<p>Education, Experience,Skills and Abilities Requirements:</p>
<p>· Mustpossess a current Florida 2-20 License, as required by the StateDepartment of Insurance to effectively service clients (or be willing and ableto obtain required licenses within the first 90 days of employment).</p>
<p>· ABachelor’s degree is preferred.</p>
<p>· Mustpossess at least two years’ experience and demonstrated proficiency inCommercial Account Management.</p>
<p>· Demonstrateseffective presentation skills through verbal and written communications.</p>
<p>· Exhibitsexcellent client service and problem-solving skills.</p>
<p>· Cancomfortably work in a team environment.</p>
<p>· Possessesintermediate to advanced knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel,PowerPoint, and Outlook and the ability to learn any other appropriateinsurance company and firm software programs.</p>
<p>· Demonstratesthe organization’s core values, exuding behavior that is aligned with corporateculture.</p>
<p>Special WorkingConditions:</p>
<p>Fast paced, multi-taskingenvironment.</p>
<p>Some travel may be required.</p>
