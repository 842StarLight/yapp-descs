<h2>Email Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Email Marketing Specialist will be responsible for the smooth execution and analysis of the marketing department’s outbound email and digital marketing programs. Supports the marketing team in generating leads for the internal Dedicated Defined Benefit Services sales team – helping high income clients reduce tax liabilities by making large retirement plan contributions. Manages email operations and reporting. Analyzes data and uses insights to create predictable outcomes and process improvements. Manages scheduling, implementation, and follow-up for the Continuing Education Program and other webinars for advisors and CPAs.</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Email Marketing targeted at Financial Advisors and CPAs: Manage end-to-end execution of email campaigns, including building, deployment, analyzing, and optimizing email campaigns – focusing on lead generation.</li>
<li>Support Marketing Operations manager with email marketing strategy, list management, data management, nurture streams, landing page &amp; form creation, and lead scoring.</li>
<li>Collaborate as needed with Marketing team on digital marketing campaigns (e.g. social media, advertising, paid search).</li>
<li>Coordinate with various vendors including Marketing automation (Act-On), CRM (Salesforce), and WebEx.</li>
<li>Manage monthly tracking and reporting results for online/outbound marketing programs.</li>
<li>Identify marketing funnel enhancements and process improvements with the marketing and sales team.</li>
<li>Project manager for delivery of Continuing Education WebEx webinars including scheduling, delivery, follow-up and reporting. Work closely with Sales to ensure a smooth handoff of webinar leads from marketing to sales.</li>
<li>Our I-Client service philosophy and our Core Values of People Matter, Quality First and Integrity Always® should be visible in your actions on a day-to-day basis showing your support of our organizational culture.</li>
</ul>
