<h2>Software/Firmware Project Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Manage multiple and complex New Product Development Projects and multi-disciplinary teams (Product Management, Procurement, Marketing, Electrical, Mechanical, Firmware, and Software Engineering, Manufacturing and Sales) to bring new product ideas to market in a fast-paced environment.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Provide effective team leadership and focus to meet project timeline and cost goals while managing tactical activities.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Work closely with Product Management to define the market requirements and project scope.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Work closely with Procurement to ensure effective supplier base meets component cost and delivery requirements.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Facilitates the development of detailed product specification that supports the marketing requirement.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Develops and maintains comprehensive product development and resource plans with the cross-functional team that minimizes time-to-market requirements.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Executes the product development plan following a structured, gated process.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Conducts frequently scheduled team meetings to drive the deliverables to the plan.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Manages project activities across multiple brand companies and multiple sites.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Facilitates problem resolution and conducts Risk Analysis with cross-functional team to identify potential issues and develop mitigation plans to keep projects on-schedule and on-target to goals.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Manage product development efforts to a project budget, including development of Capital Expenditure Requests (CAPEX).</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ensures that the input market requirements = the end product deliverables.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Manages the qualification of new products through all phases of testing: function, environmental, regulatory, ALPHA and BETA testing.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Communicates issues and project status reports on a timely basis to Senior Management.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Provide effective Performance Review input for team members.</div>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<div>BS Engineering or Computer Science Degree with a demonstrated technical background in Project Management position, MS or MBA a plus.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>7-10 yrs minimum experience in new product development of electro-mechanical products or related field.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Project Manager Professional (PMP) certification a PLUS.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ability to work with multi-disciplined cross-functional teams across multiple sites.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Strong English oral and written communications.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>3 years demonstrated successful Project Management.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Experience with Agile processes and methodologies is highly desirable.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Experience with software/firmware release management.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite, including MS Project or equivalent project management software. Experience with visual project management boards (Trello/Jira) a plus.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Participation or familiarity with FMEA principles a plus.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Experience with commercial product manufacturing and the ability to integrate the needs of the End-users and Manufacturing to provide optimum designs that exceed those of competitors.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Individually accountable coupled with a team perspective.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Experience working with off-shore and domestic suppliers.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Experience with a fast paced, customer driven development environment.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Strong attention to detail and follow-through on outstanding issues and corrective actions.</div>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
