<h2>Strategic Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>The Strategic Account Manager is responsible for delivering the Cargill Protein group vision and long-term goals for large, complex, strategic customers in the Protein Ingredients Channel that drive significant business and financial impact. This individual will be responsible for achieving the objectives that will drive significant profitable growth and mutual value creation across assigned customers. Targeted accounts include large multi-national branded CPG companies and associated co-manufacturers. These are the most complicated, highest value, and highest growth potential customers in our portfolio.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
The position requires significant influential leadership in order to develop, build, and maintain relationships at multiple levels (including Sr. Management/Executive Leadership)of the customer’s organization as well as within functions across the Cargill Protein business to ensure alignment. Work activities include researching and identifying customer needs to create and propose solutions, setting targets, structuring agreements, negotiating, and executing/measuring according to the defined strategy.<div>
<br>
<div><b>
Principal Accountabilities</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
60% - Core Accountability Category #1: BUILD, DEVELOP &amp; MAINTAIN EXTERNAL CUSTOMER RELATIONSHIPS</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Systematically establishes, builds, develops integrated customer relationships with Protein Ingredients strategic and core customers. Accountable for the overall success of the customer relationships by delivering significant business/financial impact within the area of scope.</li>
<li>
Establishes and oversees strategic and tactical alignment amongst senior level stakeholders, with a focus on mutually beneficial outcomes that create shared value, expectations, and goals. This includes effective facilitation across multi-functional, matrixed, or integrated connections (i.e. zippered relationships) that consist of decision makers both externally (i.e. customer leaders) and internal to Cargill (i.e. Platform/BU/Functional Leaders).</li>
<li>
Oversees/monitors the customer relationship for continuous value creation and optimization. Strategically represent the customer to the Protein Ingredients Channel of Cargill Protein. Advocates, positions, and coordinates resources/information across the business for meeting the customer’s current and future needs, requirements as well as gaining customer long term, continued commitment.</li>
<li>
Effectively engage the business, marketing, R&amp;D, innovation teams within the Protein Ingredients Channel in addition to our extended sales organizations. For the assigned accounts, this position is accountable for conveying customer strategies, enabling collaboration across Cargill specific to the customers’ needs and opportunities, and providing ongoing customer communications.</li>
<li>
Establishes strategic coordination across-multiple BUs to drive cross-selling opportunities to ultimately provide an integrated approach to meet/exceed customers current/future needs and requirements, as well as gain customer long-term/continued commitment.</li>
<li>
Develops, maintains, and implements strategic account planning delivery process that contains the strategies and action plans for maximizing the value of the account and Cargill’s potential to further develop the business relationship with the customer and broader network of internal constituents.</li>
</ul></div>
<p></p>
<div>
<div><b>15% - Core Accountability Category #2: RESEARCH &amp; IDENTIFY CUSTOMERS BUSINESS NEEDS</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Represents the voice of the customer within Cargill Protein Ingredients Channel and is well versed in the customer’s long-term/strategic goals, needs and requirements. Is an advocate for the customer inside the business.</li>
<li>
Partners and collaborates with senior level leaders/executives (i.e. Business Leaders / Commercial Leaders, Functional Leaders; Marketing / R&amp;D / Operations / Supply Chain) within Cargill Protein Ingredients channel to develop a multidisciplinary/broader understanding of Cargill’s products and services to proactively identify/match to customer/ business’s needs.</li>
<li>
Continuously develops exceptional knowledge of competitive offerings. Proactively analyzes competitor capabilities along with comparisons to Cargill capabilities to determine gaps and to position Cargill against competitive options.</li>
<li>
Utilizes industry knowledge across a broad to ensure marketing/sales strategies and tactics (i.e. Product/market segmentation, pricing, channels of distributions) utilized by the businesses are effectively aligned with the US CPG marketplace practices.</li>
<li>
Directly works with sales and business leadership to develop overall objectives and growth strategies for new and existing products. Discovers new prospective customers and qualifies new sales opportunities within existing accounts to create new opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>15% - Core Accountability Category #3: SOLUTION PROPOSAL &amp; DEVELOPMENT/ NEGOTIATION &amp; SALE EXECUTION</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Positions Cargill for leading opportunities by presenting the company’s capabilities and solutions. Designs and implements strategic sales process and overarching alignment for Strategic Accounts in accordance with our Customer Segmentation.</li>
<li>
Develops strategies and implements long-term outlook solutions with collaboration of lead developers (i.e. Marketing, R&amp;D, Innovation, etc.) and senior commercial leaders to create new products geared towards growing overall market share and promoting increasing revenues.</li>
<li>
Links Cargill’s core competencies with customer needs, takes an innovative approach to creating solutions based on a long-term and distinctive value proposition to the customer.</li>
<li>
Develops and structures highly complex sales proposals that represent maximum value optimization to customer and business. Ensures that proposal addresses customer’s key issues, needs and requirements.</li>
<li>
Participates in cross-functional collaboration to leverage innovative revenue generating solutions, including branding, pricing segmentation and product configurations. Shares cross-functional implications to the broader businesses segments and collaboratively develops and implements solutions for highly complex strategic account(s).</li>
<li>
May directly lead and coordinate multiple large, complex strategic opportunities through the pipeline by directing multiple, cross-functional teams from opportunities to closed business.<b> </b>Will close critical, complex sales transactions.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>10% - Core Accountability Category #4: PERFORMANCE TARGETS, MEASUREMENTS AND RESULTS</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Develops a long-term outlook, strategic account plans, and sales forecasts that incorporate market variations and enterprise goals.</li>
<li>
Designs and recommends improvements to the account planning process in collaboration with the Marketing leaders.</li>
<li>
Leads sales forecasting and measurement processes. Monitors customer satisfaction communicates the customers concerns to those who are servicing the customer directly. Enforces and communicates the commitment to the customer.</li>
<li>
Designs and implements improvements to the account planning process. In collaboration with the Marketing/Commercial leaders, leads execution of customer analysis for individual or groups of customers using Cargill marketing tools (i.e. customer hierarchy or customer segmentation) and within defined portfolio of products/services goals.</li>
<li>
Translates business plans and sales strategy into account specific growth plans. Develops forecast that includes complex opportunities that are transformational/strategic for the customer.</li>
<li>
Delivers on sales performance / financial targets / budgets; sales volume, prices, revenue, profitability.</li>
</ul>
