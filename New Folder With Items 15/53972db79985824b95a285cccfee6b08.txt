<p>About MapAnything, A Salesforce Company:<br>
</p>
<p><br>
MapAnything is a pioneer in Location Intelligence software for field sales and service, enabling more than 2,000 global enterprises by combining location intelligence, integrated asset tracking, and route optimization to drive productivity and better customer experiences. MapAnything is a Salesforce Premier ISV Partner who has received IDC’s Innovator Award in Field Service for Manufacturing, and G2 Crowd’s Leader for Field Service Management and Field Sales Tools. To learn more, visit www.mapanything.com .<br>
</p>
<p><br>
ROLE DESCRIPTION:<br>
</p>
<p><br>
You will be responsible for responding to customer requests that have been escalated from Tier 1 support analysts. The Global Support work environment is highly client-focused and fast paced, operating globally to provide 24/7/365 technical support. A successful agent operates efficiently in this climate, maintains composure, exhibits professionalism, and delivers the highest levels of client satisfaction at all times. You will also be asked to answer technical questions, solve technical problems and suggest appropriate workarounds related to supported applications. As a Senior Success Agent you will develop and maintain technical expertise in assigned areas of product functionality and utilise that expertise effectively to help customers. You will also be asked to create knowledge base materials dedicated towards operational efficiency while also empowering and enabling the Salesforce community.
<br>
<br>
PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS &amp; SKILLS:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor/Master Degree preferably in Computer Science
</li>
<li>2-5 years of prior experience in Customer Support
</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce and/or CRM applications.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated analysis, problem solving and skills troubleshooting expertise
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively prioritise and escalate customer issues as required
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills
</li>
<li>Solid understanding of Internet technologies: firewalls, web servers, web proxy servers, etc.
</li>
<li>Understanding of database concepts and data management (RDBMS) and SQL
</li>
<li>Salesforce Administrator
</li>
<li>Read, develop, and debug software with Java, C# or at least one modern object-oriented language
</li>
