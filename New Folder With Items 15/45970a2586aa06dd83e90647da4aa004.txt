<p>As the Director of Planning you will be responsible for the provision of planning, administrative, budgetary and direction to the Planning Department. This includes, evaluating sales forecasts against capacity &amp; maintenance event scheduling. You will establish &amp; maintain the organizational processes &amp; structure to manage maintenance event resource planning for induction, workflow, daily adjustments to workflow and real time data collection. You will report event work scope status compared to contract from pre-induction through departure &amp; post departure performance. You will identify &amp; incorporate continuous improvement opportunities to support customer requirements, increase production efficiency and increase facility capacity. This position reports to the General Manager.<br>
