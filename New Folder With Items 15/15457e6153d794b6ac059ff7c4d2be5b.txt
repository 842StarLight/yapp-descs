<h2>Continuous Improvement Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Safran </b>is an international high-technology group, operating in the aircraft propulsion and equipment, space and defense markets. Safran has a global presence, with more than 58,000 employees and sales of 16.5 billion euros in 2017. Working alone or in partnership, Safran holds world or European leadership positions in its core markets. Safran is listed on the Euronext Paris stock exchange, and is part of the CAC 40 and Euro Stoxx 50 indices.
</p>
<p>In February 2018, Safran took control of Zodiac Aerospace, significantly expanding its aircraft equipment activities. Together with Zodiac Aerospace, Safran has more than 91,000 employees and would have around EUR 21 billion in adjusted revenue (pro forma 2016).
</p>
<p><b>Safran </b>is ranked among the Top 100 Global Innovators by Thomson Reuters and is featured on the "Happy at work" rankings. The Group places fourth on the Universum ranking for the favorite companies of newly-qualified engineers in France.
</p>
<p><b>Safran Cabin </b>is the world’s leading supplier of cabin interiors for regional, narrow and wide-body, business and military aircraft.
<br>
The company's product range includes integrated cabin interiors, overhead bins, galleys, lavatories, crew rests and cargo containers.
</p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Duties:
</b></p>
<p>The Continuous Improvement Manager is responsible for supporting the CI initiative and daily management by ensuring strategies are implemented and principles and practices are incorporated in the processes under transformation. Possesses high competency of CI tools and techniques; mentors, coaches and educates in the use of CI methodology, tools and techniques to ensure timely completion of projects and expected results. Works collaboratively with process owners, site leadership and their employees to ensure CI efforts are driven throughout the organization.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This role provides leadership to a matrixed cross-functional integrated program team (IPT), uses lean Sigma tools to positively impact Cost, Quality, Delivery Performance, Cash &amp; Customer satisfaction and drives continuous improvement using company root cause and corrective action processes. This role is responsible for delivering the program objectives, managing program performance, risks, opportunities, and change control according to internal rules and processes.
</p>
<p>.
</p>
<p><b>Summary of Duties:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify, propose and manage projects and performance improvement plans related to the activities performed at the responsible site/location.
<ul>
<li>Define &amp; implement Hshin Kanri, cascade up and in line with site objectives
</li>
<li>Ensure SAFRAN+ and Lean Sigma reviews at Site and Department levels with a purpose to share/benchmark site activities
</li>
<li>Perform monthly, quarterly, bi-annual reporting, jointly with Finance
</li>
<li>Successfully lead high impact DMAIC &amp; change projects for the area of responsibility
</li>
<li>Successfully lead deployment of One SAFRAN for the site/location
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Develop and manage Improvers’ resources on-site:
<ul>
<li>Support black belts and engage them in improvement projects
</li>
<li>Ensure appropriate Improvement projects are defined, sponsored, managed and delivered, ensuring coherence be between top-down and bottom-up initiatives
</li>
<li>Plan and monitor appropriate trainings and certification of identified resources in order to staff the improvement plan with the right project managers
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Responsibility, authority and full accountability for the successful execution and customer satisfaction of Continuous Improvement plans &amp; projects
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the overall financial performance of initiatives
</li>
<li>Maintain the business case for the entire project life cycle
</li>
<li>Update program financials quarterly and determine root cause and corrective actions for variances
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Ensure consistency with SAFRAN policies; implement Group projects and report results
</li>
<li>Serve as a primary liaison with executive management
</li>
<li>Responsible for managing and controlling risks and opportunities
</li>
<li>Coach and train the site leadership team: Black Belts, Green Belts, Sponsors and Team Members on Lean methodologies
</li>
<li>Establish and monitor KPIs associated with the projects and critical KPIs for the site<br>
</li>
</ul>
