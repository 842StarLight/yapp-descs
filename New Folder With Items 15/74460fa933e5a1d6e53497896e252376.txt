<div>Amazon Publisher Services is a suite of cloud-based services that helps publishers build thriving digital business. We are seeking a technology-savvy product marketer for our Advanced TV team. In this role, you will define the go-to-market strategy for our next generation of products that will shape will shape the future of OTT advertising. You’ll need to invent new mechanisms to help build this new program. You will work closely with our broadcaster customers and across Product Management, Account Management, Business Development, Sales and PR. The ability to build simplified messaging and collateral will be critical to success.<br>

<li>Achieve key performance targets from product marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Complete customer and marketplace research to align products and how they are positioned to customers</li>
<li>Work closely with Product Management to identify needs and sharpen product offerings based on customer and marketplace feedback</li>
