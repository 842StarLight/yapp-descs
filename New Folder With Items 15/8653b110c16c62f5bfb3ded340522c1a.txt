<h2>Client Service Specialist - Property Preservation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Northsight Management is a dynamic, national property preservation company founded in 2009. We provide mortgage field services including marketable &amp; conveyance repairs, recurring field services, inspections, P&amp;P, REO property maintenance and more. Our success has been a result of our advanced technology systems, streamlined practices and knowledgeable staff. We proudly celebrate our remarkable dedication to quality and integrity.</p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY for Client Service Specialist - Repairs/Property Preservation: </b></p>
<p>Using property preservation knowledge and administrative tools, Client Service Leads evaluates properties for conveyance work including maintenance, restoration, or compliance. Responsible for managing a portfolio of pre-sale and post-sale assets including but not limited to VA, USDA, and FHA from delinquency to final dissolution.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS </b><b>for Client Service Specialist - Repairs/Property Preservation</b><b>: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyzes condition of assets and history to determine property needs for conveyance</li>
<li>Big Six Damages (hurricane, tornado, earthquake, flood, fire, and boiler explosion)</li>
<li>Identifies and resolves all code violations and citations</li>
<li>Maintains data integrity, utilizing internal systems (Salesforce)</li>
<li>Reviews contractor bids and prepare cost estimates</li>
<li>Submits bids to clients and invertors</li>
<li>Maintains clear and professional communication with client, internal staff and contractors</li>
<li>Resolves loan level issues relating to all property preservation matters</li>
<li>Reports damage claims to client</li>
<li>Identifies damages for surcharge</li>
<li>Interaction with clients (email, phone) escalate to client service lead as needed</li>
<li>Other tasks as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE </b><b>for Client Service Specialist - Repairs/Property Preservation</b><b>: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Education or equivalent</li>
<li>Experience with FHA post-sale property to conveyance</li>
<li>Knowledge of Department of Housing or Urban Development (HUD) guidelines</li>
<li>Experience with cost estimation software</li>
<li>Experience in the construction industry a plus</li>
<li>Familiarity with Microsoft Office and project management software a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KEY COMPETENCIES </b><b>for Client Service Specialist - Repairs/Property Preservation</b><b>: </b></p>

<li>Adaptability - embrace change and pivot when changes arise</li>
<li>Problem solving orientation - identify and solve problems with creative and innovative solutions</li>
<li>Perseverance - ability to see things through completion regardless of obstacles</li>
<li>Cultural steward - brings an element of fun and caring to the workplace</li>

</ul>
