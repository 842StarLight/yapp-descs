<h2>WFIU/WTIU Corporate Development Sales Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Develops proposals and presents underwriting, production support, special event, and web opportunities to businesses, professionals, and advertising agencies under the supervision of the Sales Manager. Explores prospects, signs, renews, and services new and current corporate development partners for WFIU Radio and WTIU TV programs and promotional activities.</p>
<p>
Develops prospecting and lead strategies for both stations with involvement from station managers / staff, and participates in station activities and events.</p>
<p><b>
Note: </b>This is a full-time, professional staff position eligible for all full-time IU staff benefits. Pay structure includes a base salary plus sales commission.</p>
Required Qualifications
<p><b>Bachelor’s degree in business, marketing, or telecommunications and two years of successful work in media development or sales, including experience working cooperatively with production, traffic, or programming departments.</b></p>
<p>
Combinations of education and related experience may be considered.</p>
<p>
Possess a valid driver’s license with the ability to be insured by Indiana University.
</p>
<p>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, with a superior customer service orientation, including ability to deal effectively with a variety of public and private clients and groups. Proficiency with computer software, including Microsoft Office Suite. Experience in personal and electronic contact of prospective and client companies, the utilization of media research tools, and the creation of presentation materials.</p>
Preferred Qualifications
<p>Knowledge of or willingness to learn about public broadcasting.</p>
Working Conditions / Physical Demands
<p>Position requires ability to travel, mainly in south and central Indiana. Some national training travel required.</p>
<p>
Physical requirements of this position include ability to spend long periods of time at a desk operating a computer and talking on the telephone. This position also requires face-to-face meetings with clients and couriering materials between clients (internal and external) and WFIU and WTIU production departments.</p>
Salary Range Base salary of $24,000 plus commission. Salary Plan PAE Salary Grade 2PR FLSA Exempt Work Location
<p>Bloomington, Indiana</p>
Job Category Development/Fundraising
