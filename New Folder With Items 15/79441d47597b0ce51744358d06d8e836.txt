<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you will be doing:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Build strong relationships utilizing value-based selling skills with the largest Enterprise Organizations in the United States.</li>
<li>
Use your extensive sales experience and consultative selling skills to initiate long-standing relationships with prospective customers.
</li>
<li>Cultivate mutually beneficial relationships with strategic partners.
</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and timely customer, pipeline, and forecast data.
</li>
<li>Provide value-added counsel to customers as a trusted advisor creating awareness and demand for Instructure products.
</li>
<li>Manage all internal databases and account update tools.
</li>
<li>Report out weekly to sales management on project, business and relationship status.</li>

Partner with your account development team to drive a strategy to approach your sales territory.</li>
