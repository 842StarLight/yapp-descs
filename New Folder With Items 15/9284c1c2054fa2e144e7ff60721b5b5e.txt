<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Great work you’ll do here</b>:</div>
<div>
The Patient Advocate will develop relationships with community referral base and potential referral sources to meet targeted financial objectives and annual budget. Complete a market analysis to use in developing and implementing marketing plans. Use the marketing plans to maintain and increase client/referral base.<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with the Administrator and Regional Director of Sales to develop monthly sales plan that will assist in maintaining current client base and identifying potential referral sources</li>
<li>
Initiates and facilitates contracts with referral sources</li>
<li>
Develop and implement sales plans that are consistent with market analysis and reflect current potential referral sources</li>
<li>
Meet monthly Agency targeted Medicare admission goals</li>
<li>
Maintain a weekly call plan of targeted referral sources and manage time and resources to complete the plan</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing recommended enhancements to current services and development of new service/niche offerings</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain Physician, Hospital and Assisted Living Facilities profiles including background, contact history, objectives, referral trends etc.</li>
<li>
Maintain communication with referral sources to ensure customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Research and maintain up-to-date information on market competitors including patient, census, referral source and marketing strategies</li>
<li>
Maintain open communication with the Administrator and Regional Director of Sales towards goals and challenges in the industry</li>
<li>
Act as a liaison between referral sources and agency staff to resolve service problems and maintain positive relations</li>
<li>
Participate in Weekly Sales/Operations meeting with Administrator and agency staff to discuss sales activity, potential problems and new opportunities</li>
<li>
Build relationships through networking with colleagues, clients and community organizations</li>
<li>
Promote Services through conventions, trade shows, public relations etc.</li>
<li>
Complete other assignments and duties as requested and assigned to accomplish the goals and objectives of the Company</li>
</ul>
