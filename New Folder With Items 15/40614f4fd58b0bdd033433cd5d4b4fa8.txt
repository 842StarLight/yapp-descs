<h2>Business Systems Analyst - Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The Business Systems Analyst Manager is the key liaison between high profile clients and the Merkle Technology Teams. This is an extremely visible position, as the Business Systems Analyst Senior Manager elicits and drives requirements at multiple levels of detail for Merkle clients. The Business Systems Analyst Senior Manager should have a strong attention to detail and is entrusted with accurately documenting requirements and acceptance criteria for a project. Solutions are primarily centered on creating a CRM database / data warehouse, with daily data updates in a Merkle hosted environment.</p>
<p>
Key Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
Interface with the client, third-party vendors, and multi-disciplinary teams within Merkle (Data Warehouse Developers, Project Managers, Quality Assurance, Business Intelligence, and Information Technology) to support the solution delivery process.</li>
<li>
Lead solution discovery sessions with the client stakeholders and subject matter experts as a means to illicit solution requirements, both business and functional</li>
<li>
Synthesize complex and sometimes contradictory information into concise, readable, unambiguous written requirements at multiple levels of detail</li>
<li>
Take primary ownership for written requirements specifications (Business and Functional), from initial creation, through change management and solution delivery</li>
<li>
Profile and understand various client data (e.g. Customer records, Transaction records, Web Activity records) as it relates to stated requirements and solution design</li>
<li>
Provide input to Project Management regarding schedule, scope, and cost tradeoffs</li>
<li>
Serve as the functional point of contact for the solution during Design and Development, working through data and design questions/issues as they arise</li>
<li>
Work closely with the QA discipline to define and execute a test plan, which will include internal and User Acceptance Testing</li>
<li>
Lead the delivery of the system into production by designing and supporting the formal client acceptance process, according to Merkle’s defined standards</li>
<li>
Maintain the solution in production, working with end users, and facilitating change requests with the broader team using a defined change management process</li>
</ul>
</div>
