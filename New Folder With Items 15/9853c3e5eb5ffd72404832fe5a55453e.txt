<h2>Controller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Prepare timely financial statements and conduct financial review and analysis.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage accounting operations including A/R, A/P, GL, Inventory Accounting and Revenue Recognition.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Lead ERP or other software implementation related initiatives on behalf of accounting &amp; finance.Maintain Chart of Accounts &amp; related hierarchies.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop and document business processes and accounting policies to maintain and strengthen internal controls.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Motivate and lead accounting team members by clarifying roles and providing helpful feedback.Oversee and review work.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Help prepare and oversee Inventory reconciliations and analysis; including documentation and coordination for the annual physical count.Help prepare the company to move to cycle counts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Act as the primary interface with external audit firms for the annual financial review and corporate tax returns.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage and comply with local, state, and federal government reporting requirements and tax filings. Oversee monthly sales tax liability and tax payments.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain the cap table as well as support the companies 409A valuations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Prepare monthly or quarterly reporting required under any Bank agreements.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Research technical accounting issues for compliance.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reconcile and monitor cash balances and forecasts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reconcile and review balance sheet accounts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Preparation of Cash Flow Statement.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist FP&amp;A in preparation of annual budgets and forecasts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Oversee and review payroll.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Other responsibilities and projects as assigned by management.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
