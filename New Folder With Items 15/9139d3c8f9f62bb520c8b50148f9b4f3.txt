<h2>Partner Development</h2>

<b>Position:</b> Partner Development
<br><br>
<b>Location:</b> Atlanta GA
<br><br>
Position Description Overview
<br><br>
The Partner Development Manager identifies, recruits and develops partner relationships across our market verticals and strategic initiatives working in the fast paced, ever evolving payments fintech space.
<br><br>
Through a high touch approach, the Partner Development Manager works closely with our Sales and Solutions leadership teams to secure and facilitate finding the right partners that match Fiserv’s goals and needs and develop a path forward that benefits working together.
<br><br>
Job Responsibilities
<br><br>
Research and identify partner targets based on strategic intent and vertical requirements
<br>Qualify partner for alignment and interest.
<br>Engage partner through direct effort, or third party introduction
<br>Communicate value of partnership with Fiserv; position products against competitors; articulate product and service capabilities
<br>Collaborate with technical solution teams to identify integration path
<br>Execute and manage partner negotiations to increase and secure long-term revenue on initial contract engagement and renewals
<br>Monitor partner performance; act as key point for resolution for partner issues that may have enterprise impact
<br>Advocate for partner through internal team introductions, awareness efforts; coordinate introductions and training; optimize partner performance
<br>Linkage with vertical sales leadership to for lead generation and pipeline development
<br>Establish and manage enablement, referral process; tracking and reporting of partner performance
<br>Responsible for new account selling to regional merchant chains or community banks with moderate length sales cycles. These are individual salespeople below the national account or strategic account level, but above the transactional or short cycle account level. *non-sales

<br>Job Requirements
<br><br>
Bachelor’s degree in a related field or equivalent work experience preferred
<br>Minimum of 5+ years progressive experience in partnership management, sales, business development with proven experience building partnerships that yield tangible results
<br>Payments Industry experience with understanding of partner ecosystem and economic drivers
<br>Strong negotiation skills and contract experience
<br>Works independently under minimal supervision. Relies on experience and judgment to plan and accomplish assigned tasks and goals
<br>Excellent communication skills and ability to work with both technical and business stakeholders.
<br>Present products, services and capabilities to partners, C-suite executives
<br>Comfortable in a fast paced environment that requires excellent time management and the ability to multi-task and prioritize effectively
<br>Ability to travel to on-site meetings, industry and company events
