<h2>Assistant Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILTIES:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Manage Supplier Performance (Quality, Punctuality, Lead time compliance &amp; service) and facilitate Major Non- Conformance closure (delivery and quality)</li>
<li>
Support new product introductions by working with R&amp;D; communicating new product volumes with vendors</li>
<li>
Assist buyers with vendor orders and current projects</li>
<li>
Develop close working relationship and active dialogue with internal customers and departments such as quality, supply chain, and finance</li>
<li>
Impeccable attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to work well with a team</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Able to thrive in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Must have good planning, organizational and execution skills.</li>
<li>
Food industry knowledge is a plus, but not required</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of operational purchasing and raw materials &amp; indirect goods and services is required</li>
<li>
Communicate with vendors on PO’s, delivery confirmations, and materials shortages.</li>
<li>
Review production demand, historical usage, MOQ, shelf life and lead times</li>
<li>
Monitor deliveries and work with vendors to resolve issues</li>
<li>
Communicate vendor quality issues and work with vendors to resolve claims.</li>
<li>
Maintains accurate purchasing files including: accurate pricing, supplier lead times, minimum order quantities, freight rates, supplier contact information, fees and standard cost</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 </b><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree preferred but not required</li>
<li>
Knowledge of SAP, SAP SRM or similar ERP system preferred but not required</li>
<li>
Technical skills in computing and using standard software (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Internet)</li>
</ul>
