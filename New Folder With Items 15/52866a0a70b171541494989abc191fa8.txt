<b>Responsibilities - Internet Sales Consultant:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responds to Internet and telephone customer inquiries and ensures that the dealership actualizes its maximum profit potential on Internet sales.</li>
<li>
Communicates effectively with Internet customers and prospects according to their preferred method of communication, whether by phone, by e-mail, or in person.</li>
<li>
Will be on the phone and computer all day. Will be responsible for all inbound phone calls and high volume of email correspondence.</li>
<li>
Has thorough knowledge of all dealership products and services.</li>
<li>
Satisfies the transportation needs of Internet-generated customers.</li>
<li>
Directs customers to product information resources, including those available on the Internet.</li>

Assists customers in selecting a vehicle.</li>

Researches auto-buying services on the Internet and acts as liaison with any services the dealership uses to promote its site or advertise.</li>

Maintains an ongoing customer database to capture repeat business.</li>

Other duties as assigned.</li>
