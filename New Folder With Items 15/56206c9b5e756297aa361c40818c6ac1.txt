<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>EDG and Mosaic are looking for a marketing coordinator to join our team in New York City. EDG is a cutting-edge architecture, engineering and product design firm. Mosaic is a separate company that shares an office and makes software that helps architecture and engineering companies run. Our ideal candidate is interested in every element of multi-channel marketing, including brand development, social media, PR, website, digital advertising, email, content, nurturing customer success, and more.</p>
<p>
If you an eye for design, are naturally curious, and have a passion for technology, join us.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan and execute all marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Maintain marketing information systems and channels (earned, owned, paid)</li>
<li>Coordinate industry events, public relations, proposals, and other marketing collateral</li>
<li>Manage PPC/Ads: Google Ads, Capterra, Linkedin, etc</li>
<li>Manage Social media channels</li>
<li>Manage SEO/SEM</li>
<li>Manage outside PR, events, calls and relationships</li>
<li>Manage our in-house creative team, agencies &amp; content writers</li>
<li>Support lead generation and business development campaigns</li>
<li>Hands-on involvement for planning, execution, and measurement – including email marketing, lead nurturing, website management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years’ experience</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in marketing, writing, or related field</li>
<li>Quick, hands-on learner; go-getting, brilliant time manager and meticulous doer</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication, design</li>
<li>Highly reliable, organized, with strong performance of follow-through</li>
</ul>
