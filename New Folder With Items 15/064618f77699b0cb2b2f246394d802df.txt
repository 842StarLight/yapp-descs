<b>The Road Ahead:</b><br>
Modular’s Product Management team is responsible for shaping innovative solutions to unique and challenging problems. Working with our partners around the world, you will help define and prioritize requirements to continue to compliment Modular Mining’s extensive mobile equipment interface offering for data acquisition and intercommunication.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>What a Product Specialist does at Modular Mining Systems:</b><br>
As part of Modular’s Product Management team, you will assist in managing the entire product life cycle for equipment interfaces and therefore have the opportunity to define, analyze, scope, and prioritize product requirements. You'll be tackling tough challenges that require innovative out-of-the-box thinking, an understanding of our customer’s business and operating environment, problem solving skills, and the ability to make sound business decisions. If you consider yourself someone who can apply mining domain know-how in a software development environment and contribute with practical solutions in a dynamic and team-oriented environment, this is a great opportunity to work side by side with some of the biggest innovators in the mining technology industry. Some domestic and international travel is required to attend both internal and external meetings.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
Each day will present you with new challenges and opportunities to test and grow your skills. As a Product Specialist with Modular, your responsibilities will include:</div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with Product Development to provide application and implementation guidance and direction to achieve product release and deployment</li>
<li>Assist the Product Manager in creating a competitive strategy for machine interfaces</li>
<li>Triage support issues requiring Product Development support</li>
<li>Identify and document opportunities for innovation while working closely with internal and external end users and various mine technology groups</li>
<li>Contribute to the sales and support process by providing clients and our regional sales teams with thoughtful responses, product demonstrations, product release bulletins, and value measurement tools</li>
</ul>
