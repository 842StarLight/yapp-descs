<h2>Systems Engineer - Power</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b> The role of Systems Engineer will interface daily with other departments and maintain effective contact with Consulting Engineers and Customers. Insurability and a valid driver’s license are required.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Basic Duties
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>PRINCIPAL FUNCTIONS &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES (including, but not limited to):</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Primary responsibility includes the technical marketing of Altorfer and Caterpillar power systems to the Consulting Engineer community.</li>
<li>
Create, edit and communicate product guide specifications, system one line, general outline drawings and product bulletins as required.</li>
<li>
Top-level electrical and mechanical schematic design of customized power solutions including: power distribution, protective relay &amp; control, UPS, communication, fuel, cooling, exhaust and other various systems.
</li>
<li>Develop leads, new account generation and client relationships through direct mail, group presentations, lunch &amp; learns and an annual open house for Continuing Education Credits.</li>
<li>
Assists Sales Representatives in achieving forecasted sales budgets and quotas.</li>
<li>
Quote, close and manage various opportunities as assigned by Sales Manager.</li>
<li>
Keep current on the activities of competitors and advise supervisor of those activities, which can affect the success of Company objectives.</li>
<li>
Keep current on all products policies and programs.</li>
<li>
Maintain a high degree of sales professionalism through study and attendance at Caterpillar and other training seminars.</li>
<li>
Establish contact database and maintain activities, opportunities, accounts, and contact information in Sales Link.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>EDUCATION/QUALIFICATION/EXPERIENCE:</b></div>
<div>
B.S.in Engineering or ten years of design and advanced industry experience required. Computer proficiency in Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Auto Cad and a CRM system. Key competetancy areas desired: Self-management, Planning, Time management, Influence, Vision, Problem solving, Techncal expertise.</div>
