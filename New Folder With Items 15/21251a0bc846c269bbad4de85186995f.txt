<h2>Payroll Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION TITLE: Payroll Coordinator</b></p>
<p><b>Why Join Us </b>CADimensions, Inc. is a growing company that has led the way in 3D CAD &amp; 3D printing industries in Northeast for the past decade and currently has an opening for a key individual to join our team. If you are an exceptional person with a passion for technology and business, and an eye on the future – we would like to hear from you.</p>
<p>CADimensions, Inc. has partnered with SOLIDWORKS &amp; Stratasys– the world leaders in 3D technology, and we are currently expanding our back office team. We have an immediate opportunity for a <b>Payroll Coordinator </b>based out of Syracuse, NY.
</p>
<p>This person is responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Collect, compile and enter payroll, benefit, and commission data</li>
<li>Investigate and correct payroll discrepancies and errors</li>
<li>Update payroll records by recording changes including insurance coverage and salary increases</li>
<li>Address employees’ pay-related concerns and provide accurate payroll information</li>
<li>Complete requests for pay-related documents including statements and verifications</li>
<li>Develop, manage and maintain comprehensive payroll records</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with federal and state regulations and guidelines</li>
<li>Assist with all internal and external HR related inquiries or requests.</li>
<li>Schedule meetings, interviews, HR events and maintain agendas.</li>
<li>Produce and submit reports on general HR activity.</li>
<li>Keep up-to-date with the latest HR trends and best practice.</li>
<li>Support other assigned functions.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Finance, Accounting, Business Administration or related preferred</li>
<li>2 years of experience as an HR Coordinator/Payroll</li>
<li>Exposure to Labor Law</li>
<li>Exposure to payroll practices</li>
<li>ADP payroll experiences required</li>
<li>Working knowledge of NetSuite or other CRM software desired</li>
<li>Understanding of basic accounting principles and systems</li>
<li>Knowledge of federal and state payroll regulations and requirements</li>
<li>Microsoft office experience with a focus on Excel required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>BENEFITS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>CADimensions, Inc. offers competitive wages and a total benefits package that includes medical insurance options.</li>
</ul>
