<h2>Director of Sales & Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>It's fun to work in a company where people truly BELIEVE in what they're doing!
</p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsible for the development and implementation of a comprehensive internal and external sales and marketing strategy and related plans to maximize market share, revenue, and occupancy growth. Specific responsibilities include market development, customer service and retention, market and business research and planning, strategic direction for promotion and advertising, public relations and community and industry relations. Interact with both internal and external customers to achieve brand clarity, operational consistency, and product uniformity. Work with Revenue Management, Corporate Sales, Marketing and Business Development to ensure Rate &amp; Occupancy goals are met.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements
</b></p>
<p>Five (5) or more years of management experience, in the sales and marketing arena, within the Travel Industry is required. Or an equivalent combination of education, training and/or experience highly preferred. Must have leadership skills to motivate and develop staff to ensure accomplishment of goals. Able to set priorities, plans, organize, delegate and control. Knowledge of hospitality sales, marketing and other sales management functions to include, research, planning, market positioning, market segments, advertising and promotion, internal marketing and distribution channels is required.</p>
