<h2>Mechanic</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We have an immediate need for 1 - 2 Mechanics.</p>
<p><b>Technician</b></p>
<p>Lucky’s employees many technicians with varied expertise allowing them to specialize in specific services offered to customers. While most positions capitalize on that special talent, all technicians share some of the same responsibilities. Many of these are itemized below to explain the company culture and image Lucky’s presents to its customers. Lucky’s business model is built on quality of service and fairness to customers. This quality of service brings customers back and brings in new customer from unsolicited word-of-mouth advertising.</p>
<p>A good work ethic with attention to detail will best serve this position. General mechanical knowledge and ability is a must for any technical positions.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review your job schedule one day in advance or first thing in the morning to plan your day</li>
<li>Preview each project assuring appropriate equipment, parts, time and shop space are allotted for the project</li>
<li>Prep station, tools, supplies and part for the job prior to start</li>
<li>Fully inspect the project at start to determine needs that may not be listed on the project ticket</li>
<li>Immediate reporting of unexpected needs for customer approval and parts requisition</li>
<li>Efficiently perform required work for each project</li>
<li>Properly clean up from each project</li>
<li>Properly record all time used to each condition of the project</li>
<li>Record detail story of work performed on projects</li>
<li>General shop cleanliness</li>
<li>Report project status with management for all jobs assign at the end of each day</li>
<li>Assist management and service writer with evaluation and estimates for appropriate projects when requested</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge requirement: </b></p>
<ul><li>General mechanical knowledge</li></ul>
<p><b>Supervisor: </b>Service Manager and Shop Floor Foreman</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
