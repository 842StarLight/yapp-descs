<div><div><div>
<p>Good Harbor Management LLC ("Good Harbor"), an affiliate of Alden Torch Financial LLC ("Alden"), was formed in 2016 with the purpose of providing proprietary, multifamily property management services for affordable housing assets principally owned and controlled by Alden through its numerous affiliates.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Job Description:</p>
<ul>
<li>Under the direction of the Community Manager, the Leasing Consultant is responsible for maximizing occupancy by generating qualified traffic, networking within the local community, scheduling appointments, securing rentals, promoting resident retention, and providing or coordinating exceptional customer service.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Functions</p>
<p>This primary functions for this position are:</p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct all leasing activities at the property, responding to telephone inquiries, providing tours of apartments and amenities, following up with prospects, and coordinating all move-ins</li>
<li>Maintain passing email shop, phone shop, and physical shop scores</li>
<li>Maintain closing ratios in line with Good Harbor policies and procedures</li>
<li>Maintain a working knowledge of the LIHTC program and be able to help ensure file compliance</li>
<li>Adhere to all Good Harbor prospect and resident follow up procedures including, but not limited to, sales, service, request and random follow up</li>
<li>Inspect apartments with the residents at move in/out and complete all the necessary paperwork</li>
<li>Comply with all Fair Housing regulations, Tax Compliance regulations, and other laws and company procedures as they relate to property specific guidelines and occupancy standards</li>
<li>Walk the property for curb appeal and overall property appearance</li>
<li>Assist with the monitoring of service operations to ensure service requests are completed within 24 hours and curb appeal standard are met</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Roles &amp; Responsibilities</p>
<p>This position has responsibility for:</p>
<p>Sales/Marketing</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform telephone market surveys and physical shops of competitors</li>
<li>Recommend strategies and programs for on-site marketing activity</li>
<li>Develop and monitor merchant/neighborhood referral programs for move-in packets, newsletters, and resident retention</li>
<li>Initiate sales outreach plans including but not limited to new development, community management, internal and external communication, and support</li>
<li>Recommend programs and strategies to increase qualified traffic and closing ratios</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Administrative</p>
<ul>
<li>Support the Assistant Community Manager with tracking and processing renewals, lease expirations, and notices to vacate</li>
<li>Collect and accurately process and report receipt of application fees, security deposits, rents and other fees</li>
<li>Keep files, desk, and leasing area organized</li>
<li>Inspect market ready units and models each morning, ensure models/mini-models are stacked with refreshments, lights on and show ready</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Customer Service</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in planning of resident functions</li>
<li>Provide excellent customer service and display courteous and professional attitude toward all customers</li>
<li>Attend and assist in facilitating resident functions and community affiliated events, including on weekends and evenings</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>Knowledge, Skill and Competency Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Competency is based on: education, training, skills and experience. In order to perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following knowledge, skills and competencies:</li>
<li>Assist the Community Manager and/or Assistant Community Manager in collection of delinquent rents and processing of all administrative forms and report.</li>
<li>Ability to maintain all resident and property files and play an active role in the lease renewal process including completing all lease forms and agreements.</li>
<li>Superior sales and customer service skills including helping our customers, answering all incoming calls and greeting incoming visitors, and resolving any issues with professionalism and clear communication.</li>
<li>Strong time management and organizational skills with and without direct supervision while being able to maintain professionalism under pressure/deadlines.</li>
<li>Good follow through is necessary so our customers feel their needs are recognized and met.</li>
<li>Strong adherence to ethical standards including, but not limited to, the ability to maintain confidentiality and maintain fiduciary responsibility.</li>
<li>Experience in HUD and LIHTC</li>
<br>
</ul>
