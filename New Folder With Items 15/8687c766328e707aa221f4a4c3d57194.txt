<h2>Executive Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>Provides account management services and add-on sales for employer group health benefits including medical, dental, vision, life, disability, and voluntary products; follows processes and procedures involved in evaluating an assigned book of business; leads and mentors EBC staff; and coordinates implementation, renewal negotiations, benefits coordination, detailed plan analysis, and sales presentations to clients.
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Acts as a liaison between insurance companies and clients in resolving group and individual claims problems/underwriting issues; contract and regulatory compliance procedures; verifies member coverage as required by health care providers; and related coordination of benefits.
</li>
<li>Prepares and submits carrier bid specifications for fully insured and self-funded quotes to include creating detailed spreadsheets on rates, premiums, funding scenarios, and coverage summaries; reviews responses on each carrier bid and negotiates terms with carriers; and reviews all materials with client to state recommendation for their ultimate decision.
</li>
<li>Conducts detailed contract reviews for each coverage; and consults with clients to determine the appropriate benefits program design, policy coverages, program changes, exclusions, and/or other insurance needs of the group.
</li>
<li>Prepares and delivers presentations to clients to communicate benefits plan updates/advisories with regard to annual plan renewals, census verification/evaluation, eligibility guidelines, billing processes, legislative updates, and other healthcare reform compliance issues.
</li>
<li>Solves complex employee benefit related problems; escalates coverage issues and concerns; and assists employees with proper filing of claims appeals.
</li>
<li>Assists with onboarding, training, and mentoring to Account Management staff; establishes and maintains position/training workflows; places monthly outreach calls to key clients; compiles and reviews client medical plan performance, loss ratios, and trend analysis; provides additional sales support; and leads interdepartmental activities.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have a Bachelor’s Degree in Management, Business, related field or equivalent experience.
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred.
</li>
<li>Must have more than three (3) years life and health account management experience in an insurance carrier, brokerage/agency, TPA, group employee benefits administration, or sales environment, or related experience.
</li>
<li>Must have strong knowledge of health and ancillary products.
</li>
<li>Must have familiarity and practical knowledge of quoting process and tools.
</li>
<li>Must have ability to maintain confidentiality and adhere to HIPAA regulations pertaining to PHI.
</li>
<li>Must have strong written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills
</li>
<li>Must have strong attention to detail, time management, and follow-up skills.
</li>
<li>Must be able to proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel, and other related software programs.
</li>
<li>Must have a Group I Life &amp; Health certification.
</li>
<li>Must be able to attend carrier/industry seminars to stay abreast of new laws, regulations, carrier products/pricing models that impact client benefit programs.
</li>
<li>Must be able to work in a high-pressure, fast-paced environment while maintaining the utmost poise and composure when dealing with sensitive client issues.
</li>
<li>Must be able to work extended hours to serve client needs as they arise.
</li>
<li>Must be able to stand for periods of time while performing presentations and enrollment meetings.
</li>
<li>Must be able to lift 10 – 25 lbs. of boxes, materials, and the like.
</li>
<li>Must be able to travel locally, state-wide, and nationally.<br>
</li>
</ul>
