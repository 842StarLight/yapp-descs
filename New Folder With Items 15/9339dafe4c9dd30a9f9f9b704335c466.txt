<h2>Category Management Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary:
</b></p>
<p>The Category Management Analyst is part of the internal sales department and will lead the development of VMS sales/business analytics projects and processes translating key learnings into actionable insights that will optimize and grow the business. This position reports directly to the Sr Manager of Sales Analytics.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide fact-based actionable category insights and trends for Field Sales in order to drive incremental sales at the customer level.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Perform 4P (Product, Pricing, Promotion, Placement) opportunity analysis at the customer level utilizing Nielsen / retailer POS data. IE. ACV revenue growth opportunities; data mining at the segment and SKU levels.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Act as a market intelligence provider regarding new categories and retailer trends; anticipate strategic opportunities and inform key stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop a monthly standardized category review template for Field Sales highlighting KPI’s in segment and category performance.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assist in supporting Field Sales and Customer Marketing with the annual customer planning process.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Cultivate and maintain the relationship with the 3rd party syndicated data provider (Nielsen) ensuring data integrity against internal sales data variances.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Conduct ad-hoc analysis in support of the Sales Department.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><br>
</p>
<p><b>Supplemental Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Special projects in support of the Natrol commercial teams.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Communication and Interface</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communication and interface required with Field Sales, Customer Marketing, Sales Operations, Brand Marketing and Finance as noted in the above key responsibilities. This position requires interactive collaboration across the organization.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required education and experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2-5 years of experience in CPG/health and wellness or other fast-moving consumer-oriented categories, primarily in Category Management.</li>
<li>Advanced Nielsen/IRI syndicated data skills.</li>
<li>Proficient in retailer analytics systems is a plus (Market6, KeHE Retailer Portal, etc.).</li>
<li>Proven track record of business analytics leading to strategic recommendations that optimize a business.</li>
<li>Project management skills with a bias for results.</li>
<li>A self-starter who is comfortable working autonomously.</li>
<li>Advanced Microsoft Office (PowerPoint, Excel, Word, etc.).</li>
<li>Knowledgeable to manipulate and extract information from internal software programs (i.e. SAP) is preferred.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business or related field or equivalent experience.</li>
</ul>
