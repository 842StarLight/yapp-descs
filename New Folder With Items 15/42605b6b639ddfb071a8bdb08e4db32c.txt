<h2>Center Director- STEM Enrichment Center for Kids</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for an energetic, creative, STEM loving leader to be the center director at a kids' coding center located in the Fort Mill/Tega Cay area. This is a salaried position.</p>
<p>The perfect candidate will have a strong sales, marketing and events background and be excited to join our team of dynamic, energetic, forward-thinking minds. The candidate will provide a fun and safe learning environment for children ages 7-14 years old.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include: </b></p>
<p>Overseeing daily operations of the center and employees.</p>
<p>Following up with leads, scheduling tours, and enrolling new students.</p>
<p>Working with our software to maintain communication with clients.</p>
<p>Working with parents to define children's learning needs.</p>
<p>Working with schools and local organizations to plan events and providing information about our services.</p>
<p>Engaging with children and families in the center.</p>
<p>Ensuring that parents understand how their child is learning and progressing.</p>
<p>Ensuring the center is a fun and safe learning environment for our students.</p>
<p>Upholding corporate standards with respect to center cleanliness &amp; operational standards.</p>
<p>Opening / Closing the center as needed.</p>
<p>Willingness to learn from and communicate with (super cool) hands-on owners.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b>:</p>
<p>Experience managing a team.</p>
<p>Ability to think on the fly and be perceptive to center dynamics.<br>Knowledge of business operations, sales, and team leadership.<br>Flexible schedule<br>Familiarity with Microsoft suite, social media, office equipment.<br>Passion for STEM education.<br>Must be fun to work with and enjoy working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.<br>Strong analytical and critical thinking skills.</p>
<p><b>Starting salary listed above is for training period, and is negotiable based on skill set and experience.</b> It also includes 2 weeks of paid vacation (dates pre-determined) yearly.</p>
<p>Serious inquiries only please! Computer coding and STEM experience is not necessary!</p>
