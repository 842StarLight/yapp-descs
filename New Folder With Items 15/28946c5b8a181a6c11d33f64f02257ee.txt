<h2>Segment Marketing, Senior Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Segment Marketing, Senior Manager
<p>Equinix is one of the fastest growing data center companies, growing connectivity between clients worldwide. That’s why we're always looking for creative and forward thinking people who can help us achieve our goal of global interconnection. With 200 data centers in over 24 countries spanning across 5 continents, we are home to the Cloud, supporting over 1000 Cloud and IT services companies that are directly engaged in technological innovation and development. We are passionate about further evolving the specific areas of software development, software and network architecture, network operations and complex cloud and application solutions.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>At Equinix, we make the internet work faster, better, and more reliably. We hire talented people who thrive on solving hard problems and give them opportunities to hone new skills, try new approaches, and grow in new directions. Our culture is at the heart of our success and it’s our authentic, humble, gritty people who create The Magic of Equinix. We share a real passion for winning and put the customer at the center of everything we do.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Segment Marketing Manager is a sales, customer and market focused role with the ability to teach/coach the Equinix value proposition and how to apply it to solve customer challenges. S/he must demonstrate a depth of knowledge, experience and passion for digital transformation. The candidate must possess broad expertise in digital business with a background in network &amp; cloud architectures, applications and data. S/he must possess the business acumen to identify business needs and opportunities, and the problem-solving skills to help achieve them through technological innovation.
<br>
<br>
The Segment Marketing Manager fosters relationships by working closely with account teams, solution architects, customers and industry analysts. S/he provides leadership and vision, while being a contributing, inspiring member of the team of resources focused on achieving business outcomes. S/he collaborates with account executives and technical subject matter experts to define account strategy and deliver the right approach for our clients' digital transformation. S/he is an expert on IT, including how enterprises leverage technology to achieve business goals.
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p>In order to be successful in this role, you must possess superior communication skills and experience, a technical and creative solutions background, writing &amp; presentation skills and a strong knowledge of enterprise networks. You will need to be comfortable understanding client’s pain points and latest network, architectural, and application designs within sub-segments. Candidate must possess a drive and a curiosity to automate and affect change within companies and industries by understanding latest network and technical trends including Digital, Edge, Cloud, IoT, mobile, and omni-channel development.
