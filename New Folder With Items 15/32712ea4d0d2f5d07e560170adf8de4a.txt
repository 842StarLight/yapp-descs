<b>Description:</b><br>
Full-time Office Administrator Position
<br>B. Riley Wealth Management Inc., - Houston, TX
<br><br>
Founding in Memphis in 1996 with branch locations across the U.S., B. Riley Wealth Management, Inc. (BRWM) offers comprehensive wealth management services to individuals, families, corporations and non-profit organizations. Additionally, BRWM benefits from unique access to the platform of B. Riley Financial, a publicly traded diversified provider of financial and business advisory services.
<br><br>
We are seeking a full-time Office Administrator with a minimum of 2 years related securities, operations/registered sales assistant industry experience to work in our Houston office.
<br><br>
Outstanding customer service skills are a must, as well as excellent written and verbal communication skills. The successful candidate will demonstrate the ability to work independently to organize, prioritize, and complete tasks timely and efficiently.
<br><br>
In this position you will provide operations support to senior managers and brokers. Responsible for flow of correspondence, requisition of supplies, as well as additional clerical and account-related duties. Serve as administrative point of contact and coordinator for all activities related to recruiting and personnel, operational and compliance matters. Also will be responsible for directing and coordinating office services and related activities, including maintaining branch compliance with established WSI policies and procedures. As office administrator, responsible for overall office administrative activities, including the reception, shipping and correspondence, large purchasing requests, and facilities.
<br><br>
Duties of this position include, but are not limited to the following:
<br><br>
1. Supervises and coordinates overall administrative activities for the branch office.
<br><br>
2. Manages the reception area to ensure effective telephone and mail communications both internally and externally to maintain a professional image.
<br><br>
3. Supervises the maintenance and alteration of office areas and equipment, as well as layout, arrangement and housekeeping of office facilities.
<br><br>
4. Communicates daily with clients providing trade settlement figures and coordinating the delivery and payment of securities and check disbursements and other routine requests.
<br><br>
5. Sets up new accounts and monitors account transfers.
<br><br>
6. Researches and resolves late payments, overdraft items, pending service inquiries, and communicates resolution as appropriate.
<br><br>
7. Prepares various types of correspondence using Word, Excel, and other necessary software.
<br><br>
8. Interacts maturely and effectively with WSI brokers, clients, and internal contacts.
<br><br>
9. Receives incoming calls, handle requests and directs communication to appropriate parties.
<br><br>
10. Provides necessary client services to walk-in clients, including the receipt of monies and securities.
<br><br>
11. Orders office supplies as necessary.
<br><br>
12. Performs other duties as assigned.
<br><br>
B. Riley Financial, Inc. employees enjoy competitive salaries, access to our 401(k) profit sharing retirement plan and our other benefits including paid holidays, vacation, and sick leave, voluntary group medical, dental, and vision insurance, and company paid life and disability coverage.
