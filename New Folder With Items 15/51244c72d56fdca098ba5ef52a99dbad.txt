<br>

<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<br>
<b>
What You Will Be Doing:</b>
<br>
<br>
You will be responsible for supporting our Salesforce customer relationship management (CRM) ecosystem and our vibrant user community. This means performing a range of tasks, typically focused on daily user support, data cleansing and reporting. Prompt and personalized customer care is critical because time is of the essence in a sales environment. You will also be working cross-functionally with other teams in order to effectively solve issues and support overall sales operations.
<br>
<br>
<b>More details:</b>
<br>
<br>
Specifics of day-to-day work include (but are not limited to) the following activities:
<ul>
<li>Research companies, stay apprised of all relevant developments (mergers, acquisitions, etc.) and coordinate updates to Salesforce</li>
<li>Leverage a combination of data quality software and Salesforce tools to identify and fix data discrepancies within and across records (Accounts, Contacts, Activities, etc.)</li>
<li>Review and compare external data sets to CRM database and update records accordingly</li>
<li>Update training documentation and other supporting materials, as needed</li>
<li>Provide routine support to end-users. Typical examples include the following: entry of new Accounts &amp; Contacts, data update requests, data-related questions, reports, brief demos on feature usage, etc.</li>
<li>Build and modify a range of reports &amp; dashboards for sales, marketing and other key groups</li>
<li>Analyze sales data and provide fit-for-purpose metrics that are used to make business decisions</li>
<li>Support system enhancement projects by taking responsibility for specific configuration and security-related tasks</li>
<li>Implement sales automation features to improve efficiencies and downstream intelligence</li>
<li>Test, validate, and document system changes</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h2>
<br>
<b>
What You Need to Have:</b>
<ul>
<li>Read, write and speak fluent English; fluent in host country language required.</li>
<li>Undergraduate degree (BA/BS) required.</li>
<li>At least three years of sales analyst and related systems experience</li>
<li>Strong Microsoft Excel skills (hands-on experience with formulas, comparing datasets, etc.)</li>

<li>Experience with third-party tools (e.g., data quality software) is a plus</li>

</ul>
