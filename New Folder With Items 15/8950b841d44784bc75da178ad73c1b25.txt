<h2>Digital Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
Chicago or Cleveland based position
<br>NAR's subsidiary, REALTORS® Information Network (RIN), is looking for a Digital Marketing Manager to join their team. Reporting to the Chief Operating Officer of RIN, the Digital Marketing Manager will be responsible for driving member engagement and developing innovative strategic marketing campaigns through various social media and digital marketing channels to optimize user's experience. The ideal candidate is creative with exceptional skills in digital marketing, developing strategic content for social and digital media platforms, and highly collaborative.
<br><br>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Oversees digital marketing, social media, and content strategy for Top Level Domains (TLDs)</li>
<li>Devise strategies to drive online traffic to website</li>
<li>Provide social media creative and community engagement of organic social media channels including Facebook, Twitter, and Pinterest</li>
<li>Develop and manage digital marketing campaigns utilizing a range of techniques including paid search, SEO, and PPC</li>
<li>Manage paid media partner including paid search, paid social media, and paid placement</li>
<li>Tracking conversion rates to help determine website improvements</li>
<li>Work with team to manage campaigns including drip</li>
<li>Help influence UI from a marketing perspective</li>
<li>Provide guidance on how to best optimize new content on the site and what needs to be done to improve the performance of existing site content</li>
<li>Improve the site's usability, design, content and conversion rates</li>
<li>Working with team to understand market conditions and customer research</li>
<li>Reviewing new technologies and keep the company at the forefront of developments in digital marketing</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>Minimum three years' experience in digital marketing, online and social media content development, and conceptualizing strategic brand marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Strong demonstrated understanding of current digital marketing concepts, strategies, and best practices</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in ecommerce, SEO, Email marketing, media advertising and social media</li>
<li>Experience managing affiliate programs and working with agency partners</li>
<li>Great project management, prioritization and organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to execute and analyze digital marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Adept to thinking in innovative ways to find solutions to problem</li>
<li>High level of proficiency with Microsoft Office—Outlook, Excel, Word and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills—verbal, written, and presentation</li>
<li>Strong customer relations and interpersonal skills with a high level of response to all inquiries</li>
<li>Ability to travel occasionally</li>
</ul>
