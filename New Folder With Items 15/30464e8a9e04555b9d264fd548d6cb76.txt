<p><b>Company Description:</b></p>
<p>
At Custom Residential Systems, Inc, our mission is to bring “life to your fingertips.” That’s been our core guiding principle since our founding and it has always shaped the direction of our company.</p>
<p>
Our team members make that purpose come to life in our client’s homes by providing access to and solutions for technologies which simplify individuals lives. We provide services in the following fields: structured wiring, fire/security alarm, phone, data, computer networking, home automation, CCTV and other low voltage applications.</p>
<p>
Our team consists of high quality individuals who strive for career growth, work diligently to achieve company goals and exceed client’s needs. We believe in a performance-based culture where talents, experience and effort are met with additional opportunity.</p>
<p>
We strive to provide something for everyone who wants to build a successful career. Here, you will find supportive co-workers, an inclusive and innovative environment, the tools you need to expand your skills and advance your career.</p>
<p><b>
Job Description</b></p>
<p>
As a Manager, Service and Maintenance at Custom Residential Systems, Inc. you will provide oversight of all service and maintenance requests. Your team will consist of Service Technicians that you will manage/guide to examine complaints; identify solutions; suggest improved methods and techniques; and ultimately recommend system improvements for a specific application</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Hire, mentor, train and develop Service Technicians (including leading the Annual Performance review cycle in conjunction with Senior Management)</li>
<li>Monitor and Manage support requests (from intake to final resolution) that customers request</li>
<li>Schedule and provide oversight/management of multiple (and ongoing) service requests and ensure that projects are delivered on time and at/below budget</li>
<li>Ability to install, program and troubleshoot all company offered products</li>
<li>Manage and provide solutions for customer service issues</li>
<li>Communicate job status and needs on behalf of staff to Upper Management</li>
<li>Serve as liaison between Sr. Management, Sales, Human Resources and all Business Unit Functions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Expert level knowledge of low voltage installation procedures and guidelines</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of home automation/control system programming (Control4, Savant, Elan, URC etc.), networking and surveillance</li>
<li>High School or equivalent</li>
<li>5+ Years of Experience and/or Merit Based; Prior Management Experience (Indirect or Direct)</li>
<li>Ability to carry, climb and maneuver on ladders; work with hands and power tools; ability to work in harsh temperature/weather conditions, outside, in attics/crawlspaces</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of and the ability to use Android, iOS, OSX and PC software</li>
<li>Present a positive, professional and courteous image; exceptional interpersonal skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Attain Permanent Employee Registration Card (PERC) prior to start</p>
