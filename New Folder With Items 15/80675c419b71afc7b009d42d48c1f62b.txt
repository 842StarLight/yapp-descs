<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Who are we?</b></p>
<p>BirchStreet Systems provides global enterprises with multiple locations a complete Procure-to-Pay business solution deploying a SaaS model using cloud technology. BirchStreet software focuses on improving business processes by automating eProcurement, AP Automation, Inventory Control, Recipe Management, Executive Dashboards, and Capital Budget Management modules. Established in 2002 headquartered in Newport Beach, California with offices in China, Mexico City, Singapore, India, and the UK. BirchStreet is now in hypergrowth with no signs of slowing down.</p>
<p><b>Job Overview: </b></p>
<p>In this position you will be responsible for lead generation for BrichStreet products and services. Assist in designing and recommending lead generation programs and set short and long-term strategies. Evaluate and implement appropriate prospecting techniques to increase lead volume. Based on prospect and customer feedback and industry trends, make recommendations for product or service enhancements to improve customer acquisition. In addition, you will monitor, evaluate, and report changes in sales data, competition, customer satisfaction, and customer trends.</p>
