<h2>Assistant Production Manager - UniClean</h2>

<div>Where will a UniFirst career take you?<br>
<br>
As an industry leader in the rental, lease and sale of uniforms and facility services products, UniFirst Corporation has grown to become one of the largest companies in the garment services industry. With over 14,000 employee Team Partners and more than 250 facilities throughout the United States, Canada, Mexico and Europe, we're poised to sustain continuous stability and growth for years to come. Your career will, too. Find out just how far UniFirst can take you.<br>
<br>
UniClean Cleanroom Services (a division of UniFirst Corporation) is the premium supplier of cleanroom garment services and cleanroom consumable items. From a small, start-up business in 1936 to a $1Billion industry leader, UniFirst now serves more than 200,000 customer locations in 45 states, Canada and Europe<br>
<br>
A cleanroom/contamination control background is a plus, but not necessary. If you are highly motivated, possess a strong work ethic and want to establish a career with an industry leader in a growing market, then we want to hear from you!!<br>
