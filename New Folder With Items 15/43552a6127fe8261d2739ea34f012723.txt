<h2>Project Manager III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>COMPANY:<br>
TechnipFMC plc is a UK-based company that provides complete project life cycle services (conception, feasibility study, front end engineering, detailed engineering, procurement, construction, commissioning, test runs, maintenance &amp; decommissioning) for the energy industry. TechnipFMC was ranked 23rd among world's Top 225 International Design Firms in the year 2017 by Engineering News-Record. It was formed by the merger of FMC Technologies of the United States and Technip of France that was announced in 2016 and completed in 2017. TechnipFMC acts in three distinct segments: subsea, Offshore/Onshore and surface projects.<br>
DEPARTMENT:<br>
Reports to the Manager/Supervisor, in Subsea<br>
<br>
RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
Plans, manages, coordinates and closes out assigned projects typically consisting of the schedule, technical scope and financial performance of multiple projects in order to deliver a quality service, on-time, and to customer requirements. Typically, projects will have multiple, highly complex work scopes, and significant customer collaboration. This is an advanced role with the operations management organization which looks at life of field management including, but not limited to, understanding and implementing customer maintenance philosophies and pro-actively managing service resources. Has an awareness of TechnipFMC’s portfolio of product and service solutions to customers’ arising needs. Demonstrates leadership and the ability to work within a matrix team environment and contributes significantly to team performance. Demonstrated behaviors focused on customer success.<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Typically manages specialized, complex (high-impact) single projects or multiple, but similar type projects.</li>
<li>Develops and oversees project plans, including business, production, and/or organizational priorities.</li>
<li>Produces integrated solutions to strategic issues and presents recommendations to customers.</li>
<li>Customer service focused with commitment and sense of urgency to ensure customer success.</li>
<li>Performs to meet customer needs at all times.</li>
<li>Communicates customer requirements to all internal resources: service, engineering, MMRS, rental tool, quality.</li>
<li>Adheres to communication protocols.</li>
<li>Communicates project status to customer.</li>
<li>Demonstrates proficiency using independent judgment.</li>
<li>Consistently and clearly demonstrates creativity and innovation in finding solutions to unusual problems minimizing lead times regardless of customer’s need date.</li>
<li>Able to clearly identify and manage areas where project conflict exists or has the potential to exist.</li>
<li>Builds and develops strong customer relations for assigned projects.</li>
<li>Demonstrates understanding of elevation structure internally and externally.</li>
<li>Facilitates communications between cross-company and functional teams to ensure that issues are identified and incorporated into schedules and processes.</li>
</ul>
<br>
MAIN TASKS:<br>
<ul>
<li>Serves as the single point of contact for the customer after the sale as assigned. Builds and maintains relationship with customer and other suppliers associated with the Project.</li>
<li>Provides the project overview and implements the system interface within the scope of the project.</li>
<li>Coordinates with Senior Manager to establish a realistic project schedule and monitors compliance with the defined project objectives.</li>
<li>Serves as the single point of contact with management for the Project and coordinates communication between the various functional areas and other TechnipFMC product areas involved in the project. Ensures that the project status and progress is effectively communicated to the TechnipFMC Project Team and to the customer.</li>
<li>Develops and communicates the project budget. Approves all project related expenditures as authorized and manages costs to meet budget commitments. Ensures that adequate resources are applied to the project.</li>
<li>Leads Stage Gate Process and ensure processes are implemented in all phases of project execution.</li>
<li>Identifies and manages strategic risks, interfacing with the Gate Readiness Review Committee (GRRC) and the Decision Review Board (DRB).</li>
<li>Develops integrated project team charter and leads team building, alignment, and communication processes.</li>
<li>Crafts Project Team Staffing Plan and writes Organizational Interface Plan. Defines team member responsibilities and tracks and monitors individual performance, quality of output, and the impact of output on project delivery and profitability.</li>
<li>Leads planning of project strategies including contracting, risk management, project controls, and project execution. Leads effort in developing Project Execution Plan.</li>
<li>Procures executed customer Variation Order (VO) agreement. Manages and negotiates VO’s and other changes to the contract within their scope of work. Conducts scheduled VO reviews and updates VO summary log.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains Master Issue’s list and Lessons Learned database. Conduct Previous Projects’ Lessons Learned Review.</li>
<li>Ensures that project risks are identified and managed throughout the entire life cycle of the project.</li>
<li>Develops and implements Project Closeout plan.</li>
<li>Develops final equipment disposition and storage plan.</li>
<li>Executes Customer Acceptance Notification.</li>
<li>Develops and implements Commercial Performance plan.</li>
<li>Conducts periodic financial reviews.</li>
</ul>
REQUIREMENTS:<br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS Degree in related field or equivalent years of work experience required.</li>
<li>Typically 10+ years overall experience.</li>
<li>Must have demonstrated application of Project Management techniques and standards.</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of engineering, procurement, manufacturing, testing and service processes.</li>
<li>Understanding of commercial terms and conditions.</li>
<li>Understanding of methods of cost control, risk management, project scheduling, and contract management.</li>
<li>Ability to implement product/project execution plans and clearly understand related policy and procedures.</li>
<li>Must have experience working on project teams meeting critical timelines in an effort to provide quality customer service.</li>
<li>Demonstrated abilities in Leadership and Team Building with the ability to motivate a diverse group of people to successfully complete team objectives.</li>
<li>Must have strong customer relations skills and ability interact with varying levels of management.</li>
<li>Demonstrates strong interpersonal, communication, internal and external negotiation, and planning skills.</li>
<li>Understanding of maintenance philosophies; understanding of offshore logistics; knowledge of subsea products; and understanding business management planning tools.</li>
<li>Proficient in relevant MS Office applications. Proficient in scheduling and SAP software.</li>
</ul>
<br>
WORK ENVIRONMENT: Spends 75% of time in a temperature-controlled environment (office, airport, airplane). Spends 25% of time in a field environment or in a subcontractors shop and may be exposed to possible hazards including high noise, heavy machinery and inclement weather. Travels 5 - 25% of time and may include international travel. Offshore travel may be required.<br>
<br>
PHYSICAL DEMANDS: Frequent sitting and operating a computer and meeting with customers.<br>
