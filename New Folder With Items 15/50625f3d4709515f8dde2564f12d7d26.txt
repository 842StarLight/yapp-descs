<p>
We need to hire an Inventory Manager experienced in automotive inventory control to manage the overall inventory of our company of about 1300 vehicles.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
What are the objectives of inventory management?<br>
The main objective of inventory management is to maintain inventory at appropriate level to avoid excessive or shortage of inventory because both the cases are undesirable for business.</p>
<p>
Inventory manager must have the ability to balance the following:<br>
1. To keep inventory ready for sale at sufficiently high-level no less than 600 a month.<br>
<br>
2. To minimize investment in inventory at minimum level to maximize profitability.<br>
<br>
<b>Objectives of inventory management are: -</b><br>
<br>
1. To ensure that the supply of ready vehicles for sales continuous to meet the demand of our car lots.<br>
2. To minimize carrying cost of inventory by controlling the overall inventory total.<br>
3. To keep investment in inventory at optimum level.<br>
4. To reduce the losses of theft, obsolescence &amp; wastage etc.<br>
5. Monitor and work with sales managers to make arrangement for sale of old age inventory.<br>
6. Work with buyers and buyerâ€™s manager to minimize inventory acquisition costs.</p>
<p>
7. Communicate with buyers on a regular basis the needs of inventory based on type and cost.</p>
<p>
8. Work with the service director and other management to make sure we keep our reconditioning cost under control to meet the objective set for total cost of inventory.</p>
<p>
9. Manage and control the mix of inventory by type and cost tier to ensure optimal inventory levels .</p>
<p>
10. Establish and implement internal inventory audits and report results and improvement plans to the Directors.</p>
<p>
11. Develop and execute inventory insights strategies to maximize return on investment by working very closely with the buyers.</p>
<p>
12. Provide input to future system improvements relative to inventory control and process of inventory in the make ready process.</p>
<p>
13. Identify issues as well as opportunities for improvement related to inventory or processes within the facilities. communicate with the local management team, other facilities and corporate departments to resolve any inventory issues in a timely manner. This includes opportunities to minimize inventory shortages or overages.</p>
<p>
14. Manage and oversee the wholesale process of selling 150 vehicles a month at local auctions and out of state.</p>
<p>
15. Oversea the quality control team to ensure all vehicles meet the standards and ready to be delivered for sale to the lots.</p>
