<h2>Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Founded in 2012, we have successfully built one of the most popular keyless access control systems, offering our customers a modern and more secure workplace through our cloud-based technology – and we are just getting started!</p>
<p>Join us as a Sales Manager to help us grow and make more office spaces digital, seamless and secure. You’ll be responsible for a fast-growing SaaS sales team that focuses on marketing and selling our cloud-based services around the globe.<br>
</p>
<p>We’re looking for someone with a driven, curious and humble attitude who excels in a techie-startup environment and someone who loves to work together in order to succeed. In this role, you’ll be more than just a leader; you’ll be a mentor and the glue both inside and outside of the team. People want to work with you because they feel empowered and you can motivate a team to pull together.<br>
</p>
<p>The right candidate has a passion for IoT technology and international B2B. The position is based in New York and you’ll be managing the NYC-based sales team while collaborating with our product and tech team in Stockholm, Sweden. You’ll also work closely with Kisi’s co-founders to help refine our sales strategy to attract and expand our potential customer base.<br>
</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Managing your team: </b>Leading a performant team to deliver strong results based on clear goals that are aligned with Kisi’s business values</li>
<li>
<b>
Growing your team: </b>Kisi has ambitious growth plans. You will help grow the sales team to the next stage</li>
<li>
<b>
Creating a successful revenue performance: </b>Manage the team pipeline and prioritize the right sales leads to optimize the deals closed<br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Engaging your team:</b> Coaching and mentoring your team members to drive continuous education through workshops, seminars and conferences<br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Becoming a cloud-based access control expert: </b>Stay up-to-date with our new product features and processes, as well as identify emerging markets and market shifts</li>
<li>
<b>
Helping us improve our product:</b> Work closely with our management, marketing, product and operations teams to improve the customer’s experience with Kisi</li>
</ul>

A natural drive to interact with people in a positive and professional manner</p></li>
