<h2>SR Business Development Professional</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>DAWSON is a Native Hawaiian Organization 8(a) small business that brings the Spirit of Aloha to our employees. As part of the DAWSON “Ohana”, you will be provided a best-in-class benefits program that strives to ensure our great people have peace of mind when it comes to health and wellness. For more information about DAWSON and our benefits programs, please visit www.dawson8a.com.<br>
<br>
SUMMARY: A Business Development Manager is to prospect for new clients by networking, cold calling, advertising or other means of generating interest from potential clients. They must then plan persuasive approaches and pitches that will convince potential clients to do business with the company.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>DUTIES and RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<p>Lead and drive sourcing for new opportunities across the firm’s DoD and National Security accounts to develop and curate a qualified pipeline and drive execution of the firm’s top priority Cyber opportunities. Develop bid recommendations based on a thorough analysis of the firm’s competitive position, Cyber capabilities, analysis of market drivers, and likely acquisition strategy in collaboration with key stakeholders. Develop and execute a winning capture strategy, including developing client relationships, defining the client’s objectives, challenges, and constraints, and influencing the acquisition strategy through direct client interface, whitepapers, requests for information, and media in coordination with internal resources. Maintain responsibility for working closely with the firm’s Professional and Technical Services (PTS) market teams to assist with the development and integration of intellectual capital, technical, management, past performance, and pricing solutions while maintaining leadership’s situational awareness through executive briefings.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>Establish relationships with key clients and stakeholders and be comfortable with supporting client meetings and events. Execute and manage the transition from business opportunity through proposal development to award and participation as an independent evaluator in all major proposal reviews to ensure continuity from capture to proposal delivery.<br>
<br>
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
<ul>
<li>Identifies and generates new business; develops and delivers sales presentations.</li>
<li>Develops customer relations including but not limited to sales leads, research, cold calling, qualifying leads, developing leads, and customer service.</li>
<li>Maintains and develops client relations for new and larger established existing customers, assuring all existing customer are contacted regularly to ensure customer satisfaction and develop need-based marketing relations.</li>
<li>Develops sales documentation including but not limited to spreadsheets, correspondence, sales proposals, presentations, and internal paperwork for new customer proposals and presentations.</li>
<li>Develops and implements a sales action plan with objectives and strategies to increase revenue and aggressively acquire new accounts.</li>
<li>Develops an effective sales presentation that is continually tested and used in the field.</li>
<li>Develops and implements a plan for closing business and maintaining long-term customer relationships.</li>
<li>Seeks/creates opportunities to expand business with current clients; identifies further business needs and develops and presents solutions.</li>
<li>Grows existing accounts to full potential and generates maximum revenue on a long-term basis.</li>
<li>Develops, implements, and maintains procedures that enhance the efficiency of the business development team.</li>
<li>Attends conventions, conferences, and trade shows as needed; prepares post-event reports and analysis.</li>
<li>Hires, trains, supervises, motivates, and develops business development staff; manages schedules and workflow.</li>
<li>Assigns duties and monitors quality of work; assures staff conforms to organizational policies and procedures and government regulations.</li>
<li>Provides day-to-day guidance and oversight of subordinates; actively works to promote and recognize performance.</li>
<li>Keeps up to date on overall activities of the team, identifying problem areas and taking corrective actions.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
<br>
QUALIFICATIONS:</div>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of experience with managing complex business development efforts with particular emphasis on negotiating direct Native 8a awards with the DoD and over $100M in sales value within federal government accounts, including the DoD and intelligence community (IC)</li>
<li>Experience with developing Cyber solutions for federal government accounts, including the DoD and IC</li>
<li>Experience with Cyber domain operations</li>
<li>Experience in developing and maintaining effective internal and external business relationships with clients and partners</li>
<li>Experience with the successful execution of all phases of a large, multi-year capture effort, including strategic planning, teaming, proposals, market and competitive analyses</li>
<li>Experience with the business development life cycle and solution selling methodologies</li>
<li>Ability to communicate multi-step and interdependent activities effectively to capture teams and ensure efficient execution of captured efforts</li>
<li>TS/SCI clearance required</li>
<li>BA or BS degree required</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Additional Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated experience in capture of directed native 8(a) contract awards.</li>
<li>Experience with leveraging the use of internal and external experts</li>
<li>Ability to provide existing relationships with Cyber DoD, and IC clients and decision makers, including IT and mission organizations</li>
<li>Ability to provide existing relationships with large and small businesses in the federal and commercial Cyber market, including teaming contacts</li>
<li>Ability to form partnerships with industry and academia</li>
<li>Ability to provide technical expertise in Cyber capability development to fulfill client requirements</li>
 </ul>
