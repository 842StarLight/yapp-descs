<p><b>

<p>
Property West Residential invites you to join our growing and dynamic team as a Community Manager at our 121-unit property Elan Pacifico in Encinitas. Our beautiful community is located less than 1 mile from the beach and boasts beautiful interior and exterior amenities. Explore Moonlight Beach along with a multitude of walkable nearby restaurants and love where you work!</p>
<p>
Please visit our property website at https://elanpacificoencinitasapartmenthomes.prospectportal.com/ to view more information on the community!</p>
<p>
Property West Residential promotes a healthy work life/balance and invests in its employees! We offer paid educational training, company sponsored holiday parties, and the opportunity to promote within. We are seeking energetic, self-starters who are experienced in the Property Management field. Our ideal candidate is highly organized and motivated leader with a positive attitude, self-initiative and the ability to multi-task diverse assignments and responsibilities.
</p>
<p>The Community Manager will assist with leasing, collections, resident retention, marketing, staff supervision and scheduling, preparation and service of legal documents, renewals, income projections, budgeting, expense control, accounts payable and accounts receivable and overall administration of the property.</p>
