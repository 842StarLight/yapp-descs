<h2>Outbound Call Center Agent Midland/Odessa</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Purpose</b></p>
<p>To effectively and efficiently solicit orders for services over the telephone and to schedule dental appointments for patients.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>The Outbound Call Center Agent performs many tasks requiring both interpersonal and technical skills. Primarily responsible for the solicitation of orders over the phone for services at the dental clinics.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Deliver prepared sales talks, reading from scripts that describe products or services, to persuade potential customers to purchase a product or service.</li>
<li>Contact businesses or private individuals by telephone to solicit sales for goods or services.</li>
<li>Explain products or services and prices, and answer questions from customers.</li>
<li>Obtain customer information such as name, address, and insurance coverage, and enter orders into computers.</li>
<li>Maintain records of contacts, accounts, and orders.</li>
<li>Schedule dental appointments for prospective customers for patients.
</li>
<li>Other duties as may be assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p></p>

<p><b> Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b></p>

<li>Knowledge of principles and processes for providing customer service. This includes customer needs assessment, meeting quality standards for services, and evaluation of customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Knowledge of operation of telecommunications systems.</li>
<li>Technology skills needed include use of Softphone software, and call distribution software.</li>
<li>Must be able to talk to others to convey information effectively.</li>

</ul>
<ul>
<li>Performing day-to-day administrative tasks such as maintaining information files and processing paperwork.</li>
<br>
</ul>
