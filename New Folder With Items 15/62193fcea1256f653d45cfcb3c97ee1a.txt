<h2>Enterprise Solutions Representative - Bloomington, IL</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>SumnerOne is currently focused on expanding in the <b>Illinois Marketplace</b>! We’re always looking for people who are passionate about how technology is changing the way people create, store and share ideas and who have the drive and creativity to help move companies forward with technology. At SumnerOne, we’ll give you every opportunity to succeed, along with room to let your ideas shine. We are an exciting, dynamic company where the sky is the limit.</p>
<p>
Our Enterprise Solutions Representatives are responsible for maintaining and growing relationships with our valued clients as well as expanding their local territory through new business growth. We are currently expanding our customer base further north in <b>Illinois to the Springfield, Bloomington, Normal and Peoria areas.</b></p>
<p><b>
Title: Enterprise Solutions Representative</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This is our tier 1 sales role, as your experience and proven results grow you will have the opportunity for advancement.</li>
<li>In training we will teach you about the sales process, how to build relationships with customers and prospecting (infield, through email/LinkedIn and over the phone). You will work directly with our Account Managers, Senior Sales Reps and subject matter experts in a mentorship style to gain knowledge and experience in sales and our industry.</li>
<li>This position will be a remote position within this territory. We will provide you with a laptop and other equipment necessary to complete your daily tasks.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>A successful ESR...</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Has a <b>great</b> attitude and is driven to achieve</li>
<li>Creates hardware, software, and service solutions based on the desired outcomes of our clients</li>
<li>Has a passion to help our clients be successful</li>
<li>Uses and maintains a state-of-the-art database to strategically plan and organize</li>
<li>Possesses the drive to work with a strategic sales plan and exceed goals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>General Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive and passion to succeed in a fast-paced, high-energy, goal-driven sales environment</li>
<li>High ethical and professional standards of business conduct and image</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Strong relationship building skills and passion to seek out win/win/win outcomes (client/self/company)</li>
<li>Legal US Citizen with High School Diploma or equivalent (College degree preferred)</li>
<li>Valid Driver’s License, insurability, and reliable transportation</li>
<li>General understanding and utilization of Microsoft Office programs</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>SumnerOne is one of the premier office technology dealers in the Midwest, with offices in Missouri, Kansas, Oklahoma, Arkansas and Illinois. We’re a family of respected companies united by ONE goal: to exceed our customers’ expectations by helping them get the most from their investments in print, IT and document technology. Headquartered in St. Louis, Missouri, we are proud to say we’ve grown to become one of the largest independent dealers in the nation. We have accomplished this by employing the best people, representing solutions from leading manufacturers of office technology, and by supporting our clients at the highest level. Our success is evident by our continued growth with our people being the key to that success.</p>
