<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Estimator provides quotes to customers seeking traffic control services and traffic plan design as well as the sale and rental of traffic products. This position is vital to B.A.T.S. growth and will work closely with the Business Development and Operations departments. The Estimator also proactively seeks new traffic control opportunities by searching online bid boards for upcoming projects. The Estimator submits bids to general contractors and sub-contractors based on project specifications. Someone with experience within the traffic control or construction industry is highly preferred.</p>
