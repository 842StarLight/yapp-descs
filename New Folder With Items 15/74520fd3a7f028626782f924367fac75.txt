<h2>Senior Product Manager at Leading Vertical Software Company</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Company Overview: </b></p>
<p>Assembly Software is the leading provider of case management software to plaintiff law firms. Our two products, Needles and TrialWorks, have multi-decade histories and have assembled the largest user base in our industry, with over 2,500 law firm customers and more than 40,000 users. In 2017, the business was acquired by Ridge Road Partners –long-term focused, business builders – who are transforming the business into a dynamic, fast-growth enterprise. The company’s exciting development roadmap includes transitioning the products from on-premise to web-based SaaS solutions (2019 release) and building new, high value modules for the user base.</p>
<p>Assembly Software is an <b>equal opportunity employer</b>. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.</p>
<p><b>Why You?</b></p>
<p>As Product Manager will be a part of designing, developing, and launching new legal tech products across the entire law firm enterprise. You will work closely with our sales and implementation teams, our customers, and our engineers to make these products a reality. If you're passionate about solving difficult problems with smart people and building products for a rapidly expanding user-base, this job is for you.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead the definition, design, and execution of new products</li>
<li>Work closely with members of design, engineering, and research teams to define and build the product</li>
<li>Work closely with customer-facing team members to become an expert in customer needs and requests</li>
<li>Work with customer advisory and customer focus groups to translate their needs into leading solutions</li>
<li>Understand the competitive landscape including product adjacencies</li>
<li>Develop and maintain product roadmap and translate that into clearly prioritized requirements</li>
<li>Create clearly actionable specifications for Agile development teams including acceptance criteria</li>
<li>Lead internal training and rollout processes for SaaS releases</li>
<li>Collaborate with Scrum Masters on management of sprint and portfolio level objectives</li>
<li>Support stakeholder teams during product launch cycles</li>
</ul>

<li>5+ years’ experience supporting agile development of a SaaS platform</li>
<li>UX research and design experience</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong ownership and accountability</li>
<li>Passion for solving problems</li>
<li>Great attitude and a love of teamwork</li>
<li>Customer value mindset</li>
<li>Strong communication skills to work with internal teams and our customers</li>
<li>Ability to both execute in the weeds and to zoom out and see the bigger strategic picture</li>
<li>Cross-functional communication and collaboration experience across multiple internal and external groups</li>
<li>Experience creating and coordinating successful beta rollouts, user focus groups, and product launch plans</li>
</ul>
