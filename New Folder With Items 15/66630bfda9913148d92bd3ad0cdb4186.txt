<h2>Principal Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">AECOM is seeking a Principal Solution Architect to identify, qualify and translate business growth opportunities into AECOM’s innovative, next generation technology projects. You will work with capture/business development managers for problem definition and use case discovery, and develop solution architecture in support of large proposals. You will support variety of activities including solution ideation, prototype development as well as development of white paper/RFI responses. You will report to the VP of Technology to align our technology projects with customer needs and growth objectives.
<br><br>
The successful candidate will be a senior staff member with a diverse background and extensive client relations, business development and sales experience in technology services industry. Demonstrated knowledge and a track record of successfully developing and delivering large scale, complex projects to the Federal Government is desired. You will be familiar with application of latest transformation technologies such as Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, IoT, Predictive Maintenance, OT/IT integration, cloud platforms, large scale data management and analysis platforms, and robotic process automation to solve complex business problems.
<br><br>
