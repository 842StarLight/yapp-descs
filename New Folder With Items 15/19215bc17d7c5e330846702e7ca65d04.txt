<h2>Manager, Customer Program Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Manage the development, integration and delivery of programs and services to customers through cross-functional collaboration within the <i>Services</i> organization and other <i>AEP Energy Supply</i> companies</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the development and delivery of customer focused and tailored programs and services, based on customer attributes and requirements, coupled with customer usage, energy technology, energy market, and energy rate analyses and insights generated by <i>Services</i> practice areas</li>
<li>Manage the development and response to <i>“Request for Proposals”</i>, including collaborating with various functional areas to deliver</li>
<li>Work closely with <i>AEP Energy Sales</i> organization to understand opportunities and customer dynamics, including organizational dynamics, sales &amp; procurement process dynamics, and overall deal orchestration</li>
<li>Oversee coordination with the <i>Program Administration</i> function to ensure flawless delivery of solutions</li>
<li>Develop and implement analytical tools and reports to track Program success based on adoption and profitability
</li>
<li>Stay current on competitor offerings, industry trends, and technologies to identify gaps and assist the <i>Director of Program Management</i> in developing strategy to fulfil</li>
<li>Collaborate with other functions within <i>AEP Energy Supply</i> to optimize solutions development and delivery</li>
<li>Develop personnel and add resources, as required, for the growth of the function</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge &amp; Skill Requirements:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates ability to synthesize and develop high value solutions packages that fit closely customer needs</li>
<li> Demonstrates functional knowledge and understanding of electric and gas commodity products, RTO and utility programs, renewable and sustainability offerings, behind the meter technology, energy data analytics, vendor capabilities, etc.</li>
<li>Demonstrates a high level of responsiveness and commitment to customer program success</li>
<li>Ability to build and manage relationships with cross-functional teams</li>
<li>Ability to develop and manage employees</li>
<li>Working knowledge of energy markets and load analysis</li>
<li>Familiarity in Energy Management practices such as Load Management</li>
<li>Proficiency with software tools, including Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, OneNote, etc, required to complete all tasks and responsibilities effectively</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Strong data driven mind-set with an ability to make quick decisions</li>
<li>Process driven with meticulous attention to detail</li>
<li>Problem Solver with minimum supervision</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Education &amp; Background Requirements:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required, along with 5+ years of energy industry experience in developing and delivering customer focussed solutions</li>
<li>Prior experience in developing and managing personnel preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Attributes:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Embodies AEP Energy Values.</li>
<li>Strong service and results orientation.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal/written communication skills- clear, concise, proactive.</li>
<li>Highly organized and detail oriented. Able to prioritize, multitask, and manage time and resources effectively, contributing to multiple projects.</li>
<li>High emotional IQ- superior interpersonal skills, win-win mindset, relationship-builder.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills – written, spoken, listening with ability to explain and interpret complex issues clearly, mindful of audience.</li>
<li>High-energy, drive, ambition, self-starter.</li>
</ul>
