SUMMARY OF POSITION
<br>This position supports both local and field users, including those in corporate, sales and construction offices. This position requires the deployment, replacement, repair and troubleshooting of windows desktops, notebooks and mobile devices.
<br><br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
Technical support on-site, at both General Office and remote locations
<br><br>
Set up computers for deployment by means of company approved configuration checklist.
<br><br>
Provide technical support for in-house applications such as Microsoft Office, Microsoft Lync Client, AutoCAD, Architecture, 3ds MAX, Civil 3D, VisionREZ, Buzzsaw, Design Review, Adobe Creative Suite, BlueBeam PDF Editor, Xerox AccXes Tools, and RePLOT.
<br>Participate and assist in hardware and software rollout projects
<br>Assess severity of network problems
<br>Provide new hires with basic introduction to hardware and software.
<br>Auditing and managing of computer equipment and other network related infrastructure (routers, switches, internet lines, and wireless cards)
<br><br>
Support for internet and network related issues
<br>Support for Windows, Mac, and other mobile operating systems such as iPhone, Android, and Windows Mobile.
<br>Troubleshoot connectivity between IP phones and Microsoft Lync Client and provide the users with basic user operation of the device.
<br><br>
QUALIFICATIONS
<br><br>
High School Diploma or equivalent required; Associates Degree in IT or a related field preferred.
<br>Requires 3 years of relevant IT experience.
<br>Understanding of IT Helpdesk practices with strong emphasis on customer service.
<br>Extensive knowledge of computer hardware, software, and basic networking.
<br>Understanding of network wiring and small office network configurations.
<br>Experience working with active directory managing user accounts and access.
<br>Support experience with Autodesk productions such as AutoCAD, Civil 3d, and 3ds MAX.
