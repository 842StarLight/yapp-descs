<h2>TradeUp Business Development Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Subaru TradeUp Advantage Coordinator is a full-time position working directly with customers in the service department, to determine if they are eligible for Subaru's TradeUp Program and help set up appointments to meet with our sales team. The TradeUp Advantage Coordinator will report to directly to the General Manager/Subaru Sales Manager daily.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b>(include but are not limited to)</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage data mining tool (TradeUp Advantage) in Subaru Care Connect Platform</li>
<li>Manage service lane positive/healthy negative equity customers—work with the service advisors in distributing information to customers</li>
<li>Proactively seek out new customers as well as existing customers</li>
<li>Generate custom lists of positive/healthy negative equity customers and contact via email, phone or direct mail</li>
<li>Develop and maintain relationship with customers</li>
<li>Develop and grow sales through prospecting, cold calling and relationship development</li>
<li>Keep customers loyal to dealership through retention</li>
<li>Collaborate with team members and managers alike</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Self-motivated and able to work independently</li>
<li>Upbeat and positive attitude— has to be able to “soft sell’ customers</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills, communication skills and relationship building skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of data mining tools and software</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in the automotive industry or any telecom experience in either a Sales or Internet/BDC capacity strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Prior experience in automotive sales or business development strongly preferred</li>
<li>Good phone etiquette and intermediate computer skills (Word, Outlook, Excel)</li>
<li>Understanding of CRM software</li>
<li>Strong Mathematical Skills</li>
<li>Customer service mentality</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills and the ability to multitask</li>
<li>Self-motivated with the ability to work independently</li>
<li>Ability to communicate at a high level, both verbally and in writing</li>
<li>Ability to work evenings and weekends</li>
<li>Have a valid state driver’s license</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required experience: </b>One year in a sales or customer service role</p>

</ul>
