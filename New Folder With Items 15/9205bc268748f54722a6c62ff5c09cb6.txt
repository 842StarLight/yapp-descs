<div><b>Project Manager - Enterprise SaaS Implementations</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>You love taking a vision and making it become a reality for our customers by managing timelines, setting the right expectations, and driving projects forward. You care about getting it done and getting it done right. As a project manager, you use your expertise and experience to direct the project plan and mitigate risk within each project. You facilitate project status meetings as well as steering committee meetings to make sure executives and project stakeholders are aligned and up to date on the project. After the project is done, you're constantly striving to share best practices across the team, improving the tools, processes and knowledge for next time. It’s satisfying and enjoyable, and you’ll never be bored. We think you’re going to really like it here.</div>

<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Work with new customers to understand their timelines, manage project plans, facilitate status meetings, manage project related issues, and ensure a successful deployment</li>
<li>Develop &amp; maintain relationships with key customer champions, including VP Sales Operations/Revenue Operations, VP Sales, CRO, as well as other executives and leadership</li>
<li>Facilitate a smooth hand off from the pre-sales team once a customer chooses to partner with us</li>
<li>Create project plans with milestones for each project</li>
<li>Provide regular status updates to project stakeholders</li>
<li>Facilitate status meetings and steering committee meetings</li>
<li>Manage any project related issues or escalations</li>
<li>Ensure the customer will be a positive reference to other customers/prospects</li>
<li>Facilitate a smooth hand off to the Customer Success &amp; Support teams</li>
<li>Build strong cross-functional relationships, working with Sales, Customer Success and Support, Product Management, and Engineering</li>
<li>Contribute to the growth of Clari by being a Brand Ambassador and assist in the hiring of great talent</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>4 year BS/BA degree required; Computer Science, Engineering, Math, or Management Information Systems preferred</li>
<li>2-4+ years’ experience in a customer-facing project management role for SaaS implementations</li>
<li>Successful track record managing multiple SaaS implementation projects</li>
<li>Experience in working with cross-functional teams including Customer Success, Sales, Product Management, and Engineering</li>
<li>PMP certification or similar project management certification preferred</li>
<li>Superior communication skills (presentation, written, and verbal) and demonstrated ability to communicate/present effectively at all levels of the organization including the C-suite</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving skills to resolve project related issues and escalations while navigating challenging situations in a professional manner</li>
<li>Passion for delivering outstanding customer experience; customer-focused, enthusiastic, positive and service-oriented</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize, multi-task, and perform effectively under pressure</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills, coupled with the ability to think “outside-the-box”</li>
<li>Team player, enjoys working hard, exhibits professionalism, is dedicated to meeting and exceeding expectations</li>
<li>Ability to travel to customer locations for training and events (less than 10%)</li>
</ul>
