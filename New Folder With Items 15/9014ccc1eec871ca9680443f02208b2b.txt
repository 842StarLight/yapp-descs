<h2>Customer Success Manager - Enterprise</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Your Role:</b><br>
<br>
Tenable has an immediate need for a Customer Success Manager who will be responsible for establishing and driving sales activities for our software products within a designated geography. You will be expected to penetrate and prospect named accounts for new business as well as establish and work with partners in a specified market space.</p>
<p><b>
Your Opportunity:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for growing revenue via prospecting, qualifying, selling and closing from existing enterprise client accounts</li>
<li>Responsible for Quarterly &amp; Annual Revenue Targets</li>
<li>Manage client relationship through all phases of the sales cycle Provide a consultative solutions sales process to prospects</li>
<li>Conducts one-on-one and group sales presentations</li>
<li>Provide account management to an existing territory</li>
<li>Responsible for tracking customer information, forecasts and reports</li>
<li>Develop and maintain prospect and customer list based on strategic marketing data and other sources for sales leads</li>
<li>Plan &amp; Strategize marketing activities with the Marketing and Channel teams for the end users and channels</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You'll Need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have experience selling On-Demand, SaaS (Software as a Service) solution or subscription based product to a diverse customer base</li>
<li>BA/BS degree and 3+ years of sales experience focused on growing existing business or new business</li>
<li>Large account management or project management experience</li>
<li>Proven track record for closing large and mid size companies</li>
<li>Ability to adapt, overcome objections, and learn complex solutions</li>
<li>Strong time management skills</li>
<li>Create new business opportunities through in-depth conversations and relationship building</li>
<li>Proven track record of growing business from an established client base.</li>
<li>Consistent and documented track record for achieving monthly/quarterly/annual target metrics</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills are a must</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced, team environment with revenue deadlines</li>
<li>Working knowledge of computer applications such as Microsoft Office and Salesforce.com</li>
</ul>
<b>And Ideally:</b><br>
<ul><li>Network Security background</li></ul>
