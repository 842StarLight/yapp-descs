<h2>EHS Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Home Market Foods (HMF) is a dynamic enterprise that has become the market leader in a number of prepared meat-based meal and snack categories. The company has grown substantially across its markets through excellence in innovation, manufacturing, and customer service. This success has been nurtured by its ownership group and an expanding team of world-class executives. HMF is building on this track record through complementary acquisitions and a growing number of business development initiatives. HMF's business model is poised to continue to thrive and offer greater opportunities for its customers and team.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
We are based in Norwood, Massachusetts, which is comfortably within the Boston metropolitan area. HMF's facilities include a state-of-the-art plant, and R&amp;D facility, as well as warehouse and Corporate functions. We are focused on continued growth and are executing against various initiatives that include additional product lines, new sales channels, greater penetration of our extensive customer relationships, as well as opportunistic acquisitions. Our success is directly dependent on attracting and retaining the best possible talent across our functions. To this end, we are posting for a strong Environmental Health and Safety Specialist.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
The <b>Environmental Health and Safety Specialist</b> will assist with the implementation of company-wide multi-media environmental, health and safety programs.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Works as part of an integrated team to increase staff awareness with environmental and occupational safety policies and procedures within the production environment</li>
<li>Serves as a member of Emergency, First Responder team on call 24 hour basis</li>
<li>Prepares training documents, creates and maintains training records, creates job hazard analysis</li>
<li>Analyzes safety and environmental data to produce environmental and safety compliance reports</li>
<li>Performs safety and environmental sampling and monitoring to include sampling and monitoring of storm water discharges in support of permit compliance, indoor air quality, noise assessments, non-ionizing radiation monitoring, temperature</li>
<li>Responsible for maintenance and ordering of PPE inventories</li>
<li>Assists with coordination of company medical monitoring programs as required</li>
<li>Serves as focal point of contact on sustainability initiatives</li>
<li>Integrates SDS sheets into online database, as well as assisting with GHS Hazcom compliance</li>
<li>Reviews and understands complex environmental and safety regulations related to compliance</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education and/or Experience
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors of Science degree in environmental, health and safety, biology, chemistry or related field or at least 2 years of related experience in a manufacturing facility in an EHS role</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Must be proficient the Microsoft Office Suite of products</li>
<li>Strong organizational and time management skills; ability to work in a fast-paced environment while multi-tasking priorities</li>
<li>Must be able to participate in a company lock out, tag out program, as well as a respiratory protection program
</li>
<li>Must be able to serve in an on-call capacity</li>
<li>First aid and CPR training preferred</li>
<li>Familiarity with HACCP and SQF or other Quality Systems as relating to food manufacturing required</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Physical Demands:</b></p>
<p>
Able to work on sustained basis in cold, high heat environments, ability to climb ladders. Ability to wear as needed safety equipment to include but not be limited to respirator. Ability to lift up to 25lbs</p>
