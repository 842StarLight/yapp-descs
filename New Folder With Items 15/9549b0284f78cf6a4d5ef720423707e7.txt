<p><b>Title:</b> Call Center Representative</p>
<p><b>
Reports to:</b> Call Center Manager</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
 <b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Fitness Connection is seeking Call Center Representatives that will provide excellent customer service to all Fitness Connection members and guests while assisting the company in meeting or exceeding company and personal revenue goals.</p>
<p><b>
Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Make required number of outbound calls to Fitness Connection members to collect past due balances while maintaining a positive professional and multitasking attitude</li>
<li>Answer inbound calls from Fitness Connection members and guests regarding billing, agreements, payment options and customer service</li>
<li>Aid Fitness Connection members needing assistance with policies and procedures</li>
<li>Maximize phone availability to ensure that clients experience minimal hold times in queue</li>
<li>Log all calls appropriately with details regarding member interactions</li>
<li>Deliver the highest level of customer service to all callers</li>
<li>Meet/exceed individual performance benchmarks</li>
<li>Assist the call center to meet/exceed team performance benchmarks</li>
<li>Provide general information about Fitness Connection and all Fitness Connection facilities</li>
<li>Resolve customer inquiries/concerns quickly and efficiently while upholding Fitness Connection policies and procedures</li>
<li>Handle any/all follow-up tasks such as mailing, e-mailing, and follow up information, faxing emailing etc. in a timely fashion</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Position duties require sitting for long periods of time, daily walking, lifting, bending, squatting and reaching.</li>
<li>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</li>
</ul>

<li>Strong customer service skills</li>
<li>Possess strong leadership abilities</li>
<li>Possess strong motivational, communication, team building and training skills.</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated and have strong organizational &amp; management skills.</li>

<li>Must have a high level of professionalism, honesty, integrity, and work ethic.</li>
<li>Exhibit willingness to work flexible hours.</li>
<li>Ability to complete multiple tasks</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
