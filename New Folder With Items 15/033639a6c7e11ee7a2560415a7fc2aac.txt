<h2>Director of Digital Content</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB DESCRIPTION
</h1>
<div>
<p>We’re redefining how news is produced and distributed for busy, mobile audiences, and we’re looking for a creative, innovative leader to drive these efforts. The<b> Director of Digital Content</b> for KPNX, TEGNA's NBC affiliate in Phoenix, will serve as a member of the senior management team and lead the publication and distribution of engaging, compelling content across multiple platforms. This person will play a key role in charting the station’s content and growth strategy.</p>
 <p><b>In this role, you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the digital content team and lead digital strategy to grow multi-platform content, audience and engagement</li>
<li>Be the voice of innovation in the newsroom and inspire others to follow your lead</li>
<li>Lead our newsrooms to develop workflows that deliver our content 24/7 across current and future platforms with storytelling that is optimized for each screen</li>
<li>Analyze and actively use data and metrics to inform editorial and strategic decisions and to create accountability for results</li>
<li>Actively partner with the news director and marketing director to create three-screen synergy (TV, digital, mobile) around station content and campaigns</li>
<li>Oversee all station efforts in social media including Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Snapchat, and other emerging platforms.</li>
<li>Lead the content team in identifying new content types and innovative technologies that help us reach and serve new audiences</li>
<li>Create unique, engaging, and shareable content</li>
<li>Be a resource for Sales in its content generation and digital sponsorship efforts</li>
<li>Teach, coach, and mentor both members of the digital team and the entire newsroom in best practices for writing and producing content for digital and social platforms</li>
<li>Skillfully use social media and digital tools to research, discover and distribute content</li>
<li>Collaborate with content leaders across TEGNA to maximize the sharing of our content and to leverage innovation and best practices among stations</li>
<li>Hold newsroom employees accountable to TEGNA’s ethical and editorial standards</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<br><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB REQUIREMENTS
</h1>
<div>
<p><b>Here’s what you need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in journalism, communications or equivalent experience in the field</li>
<li>5-10 years of content experience, including at least 2-3 years managing other digital journalists</li>
<li>Solid journalistic judgment and a news philosophy that emphasizes accurate, fair and unique local content, along with community engagement</li>
<li>A deep understanding of social strategy and SEO with the hunger to keep up with emerging trends</li>
<li>Ability to calmly handle live, breaking news situations and the ability to deal with the rapid pace of news</li>
<li>Knowledge of online CMS and news production systems along with social newsgathering tools like CrowdTangle and Social News Desk</li>
<li>Experience analyzing audience data and identifying behavioral trends, Google Analytics experience a plus.</li>
<li>Passion and vision for emerging media</li>
<li>Strong organizational and communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>About TEGNA:</b></p>

Across platforms, TEGNA tells empowering stories, conducts impactful investigations and delivers innovative marketing solutions. For more information, visit www.TEGNA.com.</p>
