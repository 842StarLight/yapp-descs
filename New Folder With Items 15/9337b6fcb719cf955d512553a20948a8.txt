<h2>Senior Marketing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">As a Senior Marketing Analyst you are responsible for both strategic and tactical project management of mid-sized marketing and sales campaigns. Responsibilities will include: Coordinates the development of competitive value propositions, client-centric messaging and points of view. Supports marketing team by demonstrating expertise in various areas, including advertising, social media, and direct marketing Assists with the analyses of marketing data, including campaign results, conversion rates, and online traffic in order to improve future marketing strategies and campaigns. Sets up tracking systems for marketing campaigns and online activities.
<br><br>
Helps establish goals and objectives in order to reach our customers through appropriate marketing channels (digital and traditional). Manages mid-sized marketing campaigns to ensure projects are completed on time and under budget Collaborates with both internal partners, works closely with all levels of management and builds and maintains key relationships Monitors key performance indicators, particularly those related to demand generation, sales follow-up and brand engagement. Manages digital and direct-response led marketing plans to communicate messages, engage with target audiences and capture sales leads. Manages project budgets and assists with budget forecasting and reconciliation.
<br><br>
<b>Qualifications:</b> Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Journalism, Communications or related field of study 5-10 years of experience managing marketing projects (digital and print production of marketing collateral), experience in an advertisement agency or related Ability to work in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment. Project Management experience. The ideal candidate will be organized, flexible and proven experience in a fast paced environment. Multi-tasking and time-management skills, with the ability to prioritize tasks is a must.
<br><br>
Prior Insurance or Financial Industry experience preferred Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
