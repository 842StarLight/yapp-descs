<h2>AECO Software Application Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Kelar Pacific, an Autodesk and Bluebeam Gold Partner, is looking for motivated and competent individuals to join our AECO Technical Team. The ideal candidates should be knowledgeable, dependable and enthusiastic with a desire to grow professionally.</p>
<p><b>General Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Train &amp; support Architectural, Engineering, &amp; Construction (AEC) clients in Autodesk and Bluebeam solutions.</li>
<li>Identify and provide clients with technical solutions to solve business issues.</li>
<li>Provide Building Information Modeling (BIM) project services including modeling, drafting &amp; coordination.</li>
<li>Increase consulting and product training revenues.</li>
<li>Work as part of a team to increase “specialized-vertical” CAD / BIM software sales.</li>
<li>Support Kelar Pacific sales team by providing technical online and in-person presentations.</li>
<li>Provide technical recommendation and support to ensure successful post sales solution implementation.</li>
<li>Provide industry trends workshops and blogs for Kelar Pacific team.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Marketing and Business Development team to run industry webinars.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Skills and Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Practical use of Autodesk AEC solutions including Revit Architecture / MEP, Navisworks, AutoCAD &amp; BIM360 Cloud services (Civil 3D/Infraworks a plus).</li>
<li>Knowledge of Bluebeam Revu and Studio or similar PDF mark-up solution.</li>
<li>Minimum 3-5 years of Architecture, Engineering or Construction Industry experience.</li>
<li>BS in Architecture, Engineering, or Construction or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>Strong understanding and knowledge of Building Information Modeling &amp; Virtual Design Construction.</li>
<li>Strong presentation and communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to teach minimum intermediate level class for Revit Architecture / Structure, MEP, AutoCAD &amp; Navisworks Manage. Knowledge of Bluebeam workflows a plus.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills.</li>
<li>Ability to learn and grow with new products and technology.</li>
<li>Former BIM Manager and Contract Coordinator a plus.</li>
