<h2>Supply Chain Sales Architect (Position In Hickory, NC)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description</p>
<p>The Solutions Engineer is responsible for assessing, developing and implementing logistics solutions that improve the prospective clients’ business models. Understanding the clients’ current state with the use of diagrams, process maps, and analyses is essential to the architecture of an improved future state. While being an integral part of the Transportation Insight sales process, and supporting organic solutions for existing clients (when necessary), the incumbent will be a hands-on leader, with an emphasis on strategic thought, business acumen, creative problem-solving, flexibility, and sound judgment. The Solutions Engineer will collaborate with designated employees, senior-level team members and prospective customers to validate existing processes and service gaps to drive improvements within the prospective client’s enterprise-wide supply chain.</p>
<p>The individual in this role brings demonstrated skills in consultative selling, supply chain management, TMS system implementation, project management and is able to apply these skills effectively across functional departments. He or she is able to price complex multi-mode service solutions and provide key inputs for the production of the demonstration and presentation documents to prospective clients.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Gather client requirements and understand the key drivers for value-based improvements to be delivered.</li>
<li>Assess a validated prospects’ current supply chain program by documenting the current state.</li>
<li>Coordinate the assessment process for assigned validated prospects. Hold the internal organization accountable to meet assigned timelines.</li>
<li>Understand the client's application, overall objectives and requirements to propose the best solution for their needs.</li>
<li>Work with a cross-functional delivery team to develop a solutions strategy and operational framework.</li>
<li>Participate in the solution design process by facilitating prospect strategy meetings and coordinating internal resources.</li>
<li>Document the future solution to help with the production of custom presentations and demonstration documentation.</li>
<li>Help create pricing strategies for Transportation Insight service offerings that are both competitive and attractive to prospective clients.</li>
<li>Assist sales personnel, as needed, in the Demonstration stage to close new business.</li>
<li>Develop project plans; coordinate projects; complete projects on time; communicate changes and progress.</li>
<li>Use web meetings and conduct multiple weekly conference calls with the client.</li>
<li>Act as client and project advocate at TI. Engage internal resources as needed.</li>
<li>Create a standard approach for the transitioning of clients from the sales organization to the account management organization to improve start-up and hand-off of operations.</li>
<li>Facilitate hand-off of projects to Implementation and Client Services support groups upon completion.</li>
<li>Work with Marketing to develop supporting material for use during the assessment process, demonstration, as well as internal training.</li>
<li>Develop internal programs to increase and improve performance, close ratio, and revenue growth.</li>
<li>Collect information on client's unfulfilled needs and potential service improvements and report with recommendations to the Leadership Team.</li>
<li>Analyze the results of initiatives to refine strategy: provide feedback and recommendations on a strategic and tactical level.</li>
<li>Support ongoing improvement efforts by documenting best practices.</li>
</ul>
