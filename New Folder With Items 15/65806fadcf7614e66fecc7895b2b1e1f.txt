Description</b></div>
<div>Emerson GeoFields, a leading provider of software and services for the oil and gas pipeline industry is looking for a Senior Application Architect to work in our Atlanta office. This position requires strong experience in Microsoft .NET technologies. Knowledge of GIS and Esri ArcObjects are a plus.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Architect, design, code, test, and deploy new features</div></li>
<li><div>Establish and maintain standards for application architecture, development, documentation and deployment.</div></li>
<li><div>Mentor other developers in application architecture and development of the solution
</div></li>
<li><div>Actively participate in code walkthroughs/inspections to ensure consistency and quality</div></li>
<li><div>Follow the established software development methodology</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Additional Information</b></div>
This role does not require travel.<div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>
Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>BS or MS degree in Computer Science or related technical field
</div></li>
<li><div>8+ years of .NET architecture and development experience
</div></li>
<li><div>Relational data access and persistence techniques using .NET/C#</div></li>
<li><div>Experience in ASP.NET Web API</div></li>
<li><div>Experience in enterprise software development</div></li>
<li><div>Strong problem-solving, analytical, and conceptual skills
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to collaborate with other team members in the execution of large-scale projects</div></li>
<li><div>Esri ArcGIS and ArcObjects development experience preferred</div></li>
<li><div>Xamarin Forms development experience preferred</div></li>
<li><div>Web development experience preferred</div></li>
</ul>
