<h2>Meat/Fish Clerks</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Position Priority is Customer Service and knowledge of Meat and Fish.</p>
<p>-Weighs, wraps, prices and labels poultry, meat, fish and other fresh products in the Meat Department.</p>
<ul>
<li>Organizes and rotates product in cooler, organizes storage areas and refrigerates products immediately.</li>
<li>Transports wrapped product from work area to display cases. Maintains and stocks display cases. Pulls out of code date and spoiled product. Ensures proper merchandising, signing, inventory level and eye-appeal of department.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Controls shrink and expenses by properly handling merchandise,equiptment and supplies.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Maintains and organizes meat department cooler and freezer. Ensures cleanliness and sanitation of sales floor, meat and butcher block cases, work areas, cooler, freezer, equipment and retail cases.</li>
<li>Ensures that product packaging, labeling, rotation and wrapping standards are met.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assists customers by taking and filling special orders. Makes product suggestions and engages in suggestive selling. Makes intercom announcements.</li>
<li>Answers and responds to incoming calls appropriately</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Other necessary and required duties.</li></ul>
<p><b>SKILLS AND PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to be friendly, courteous, tactful and maintain composure in dealing with customers and co-workers.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate orally and read and follow written work assignments.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Must have knowledge of product variety, standards, perishability, food safety, sanitation, labeling and department policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Frequently reaches, lifts, stacks and maneuvers objects of varying dimensions and weights up to approximately 80 lbs. Bends, stoops and reaches frequently and occasionally climbs ladders. Pushes and pulls fully loaded hand trucks, high boys and pallet jacks. Performs repetitious arm movement to package, weigh and wrap products.</li>
<li>Manual dexterity and good eye-hand coordination are necessary.</li>
<li>Mental alertness is necessary to ensure safe and accurate completion of work activities.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Operates scales, meat wrapping machine, computer software and electronic ordering devices to input price, weights, order and inventory and print labels.</li>
<li>Utilizes knives, scrapers and other cutting and wrapping equipment. Utilizes cleaning supplies and equipment. Utilizes safety cutters. Requires use of safety gloves.</li>
<li>May be required to work nights, weekends and holidays. Scheduled hours will vary. Good attendance is required.</li>
<li>Exposure to cutting equipment and other work aids necessary to perform job duties.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Hands are frequently exposed to moisture while handling and stocking poultry, meat, fish and other fresh product. Requires the ability to work, for prolonged periods of time, in an environment range of 40 to 50 Fahrenheit. Hands are frequently exposed to 30 Fahrenheit while handling product in the display cases.</li>
<li>Exposure to 28 Fahrenheit while handling products in cooler and -20 Fahrenheit in freezer.</li>
<li>Working conditions consist of a temperature controlled store environment.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Types: Full-time, Part-time</p>
<p>Salary: $9.00 to $16.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Meat/Fish: 3 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
