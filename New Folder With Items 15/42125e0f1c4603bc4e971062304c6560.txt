<p>
Oldcastle Coastal, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b><br>
</p>
<p>
Under general supervision, fills and stacks bags, while keeping the work area neat and clean. The open positions are for both day and night shift<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Operate packing machines to fill bags with cement-based products</li>
<li>
Stack bags in a predetermined fashion when necessary<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Must be able to learn and operate machines: palletizer, packer, and forklift</li>
<li>
Alert lead person to equipment malfunctions or production problems</li>
<li>
Maintain a clean and organized workspace</li>
<li>
Follow all company, safety, and general plant policies</li>
<li>
Initiative to work with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned by the supervisor</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>

Qualifications</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>

The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skills and/or abilities required. <br>
<p><b>
Education / Experience / Requirements</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Sufficient formal or informal education to ensure the ability to perform at a level required for successful job performance</li>
<li>
Able to understand and carry out written and oral instructions</li>
<li>
Able to lift up to 100 pounds</li>
<li>
Able to bend, lift, and stretch over an extended period of time</li>
<li>
Able to work in a dusty environment</li>
<li>
Follow safety procedures including wearing a respirator, gloves, safety glasses, and back support (items will be furnished by the company)</li>
<li>
Pre-employment drug test and lifting assessment required</li>
<li>
Willing to work flexible schedules/overtime as required</li>
</ul>
