<h2>Office Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We're recruiting for an <b>enthusiastic and detail-oriented</b> <b>Office Administrator </b>to provide support to our Sales and Production teams at our Tuff Shed Factory Store in <b>Phoenix</b><b>.</b></p>
<p><b>
DO YOU HAVE WHAT IT TAKES TO BE A SUCCESSFUL TUFF SHED OFFICE ADMINISTRATOR?</b></p>
<p>
Strong <b>customer service skills</b>, working directly with customers, vendors, subcontractors, and sales consultants to coordinate and schedule Tuff Shed installations; ability to predict and resolve all levels of issues.</p>
<p><b>
Attention to detail</b>, preparing the daily "packs" required to install our quality products. This includes <b>verifying orders, requesting materials, and creating purchase orders and waivers.</b></p>
<p>
Acting as the <b>first contact with our "Big Box" partner</b>, responding to queries, <b>managing the</b> <b>database</b>, and providing assistance to our sales teams.</p>
<p>
Consistently understanding and meeting the needs of our customers, <b>recognizing special needs</b> and <b>recommending appropriate upsells.</b></p>
<p><b>
Experience working in "trades/industries"</b> and or the ability to learn technical aspects related to <b>basic product design and functionality.</b></p>
<p>
Ability to assist the General Manager with <b>various front office needs as they may arise, including</b> HR processes such as pre-employment checks and documentation, benefits management and monitoring of the employee time-tracking system.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
SKILLS &amp; EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>
Successful <b>prior experience working in logistics, coordination, project management,</b> or in a similar capacity</p>
<p>
Proven experience working in a <b>fast-paced environment</b>, meeting <b>tight deadlines</b> on multiple projects</p>
<p>
Hands-on computer skills including <b>Word, Excel</b> and <b>Outlook</b>. Experience in <b>JD Edwards</b> software highly preferred</p>
<p>
Ability to take on a role with a <b>steep learning curve</b>. You'll be expected to know the details about our products and discuss offers and options with our partners and customers</p>
<p>
High school diploma or equivalent. This role will require <b>strong math and communication skills</b></p>
<p>
Must possess a current valid driver's license and a satisfactory Motor Vehicle Report</p>
<p></p>

WHAT'S IN IT FOR YOU?</b></p>
<p>
As a Tuff Shed Office Administrator, you'll have the opportunity to join a successful company, and be part of the growth team!</p>
