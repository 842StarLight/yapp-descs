<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>TVU was built to help people create and distribute broadcast quality live video content easily. TVU solutions are playing a crucial role in how live news and sports are captured, produced and distributed at many of the world’s top media companies. With over 2500 broadcast and media production customers in over 85 countries using TVU solutions, a career at TVU offers countless ways to have an impact on the media business.</p>
<p>*<br>At TVU, we take on the challenges and push the envelope. We do things others think are impossible. We work with our customers and create solutions that help our customers be more successful every day.<br>*<br>*<br>We are currently seeking a self-motivated, results-oriented team player to be part of the Product Marketing team. The ideal candidate will have a tenacious, energetic attitude, competitive nature, be driven to success, and able to work in an extremely fast-paced environment with technologies that evolve rapidly. You will also possess great interpersonal skills. The preferred individual should be located in Mountain View, CA corporate headquarters.<br>*<br>*<br>Primary Responsibilities:<br>*<br>*</p>
<ul><li>Develop and manage global Go-To-Market plans for one or more TVU product lines, from defining objectives, goals and strategies, to measuring results and ROI.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor and report product line P&amp;L to upper management</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create and execute end-to-end launch plans and process for products in the TVU product family (which includes hardware, software and services) in partnership with key stakeholders and teams across the company</li></ul>
<ul><li>Define appropriate positioning/messaging based on competitive analysis, market factors/trends and customer feedback, and work closely with the corporate marketing and communication teams to create effective marketing material.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Recommend product pricing based on customer feedback, competitive analysis and market factors</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work closely with the product management group to ensure customer and market requirements are being added to the product roadmap.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist sales teams with customer specific proposals and presentations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Represent and evangelize TVU Networks’ products and services at internal and external events.</li></ul>
<p>*<br>*<br>Requirements:<br>*<br>*</p>
<ul><li>BS or BA required. MBA preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>2-5 years of experience in the B2B technology space, ideally in broadcast or telecommunications (hardware/software products, mobile, desktop applications or SaaS)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong business mind with prior experience and a proven track record in go-to-market planning, product launches, product marketing, partnering with product and marketing teams.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Passion for technology</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong analytical skills and the ability to clearly present recommendations and results.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication skills - written, verbal and interpersonal</li></ul>
<ul><li>Willingness to travel up to 20% of the time</li></ul>
<ul><li>Driven, self-starter with a strong point of view who is able to work on multiple projects simultaneously with multiple teams across all departments</li></ul>
<ul><li>Thrives in a fast-paced, ever changing environment and doesn’t object to rolling up their sleeves.</li></ul>
