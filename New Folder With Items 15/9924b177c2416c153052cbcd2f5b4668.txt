<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary of Responsibilities</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
The Marketing Specialist creates and executes the design and production of marketing communications and analysis to support the overall Engineered Papers business units new Alternative Markets growth strategy within NAM and global markets. The specialist uses best practices in B2B and B2C marketing communications to support and accelerate initiation of new sales and to develop a strong brand in a growing, multi-billion dollar market. This position works across multiple segments, obtains input from multiple commercial and technical leaders and reports to the Sr. Director, Global Business Development, Paper.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities</b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops detailed annual media / PR plan for assigned regions and segments to support maximum exposure for the business and key products. Maximizes exposure through thought leadership and supplements with an appropriate level of paid advertising.</li>
<li>
Develops relationships with editors/journalists of key industry publications and organizers of key conferences to support execution of the thought leadership component of plan.</li>
<li>
Identify, plan and execute new social media outlets (Facebook, Linked-in, etc.) and other innovative ideas that could benefit SWM</li>
<li>
Provide accurate and reliable market intelligence considering these major axis of analysis – Consumer consumption/ growth trends; technology evolution, customer production/volume and competition<b>.
</b>
</li>
<li>Develop a network of consultants/ experts to assist in maintaining and reporting on key market intelligence. Use of 3rd party statistical services such as Datamyne, Brightfield, Digimind.</li>
<li>
Coordinate with legal and R&amp;D teams to develop simplified reporting strategy for updates and changes of the regulatory and legal environment impacting SWM alternative markets.</li>
<li>
Works within budget to manage and execute tradeshows and customer innovation events within the industry</li>
<li>
Works internally or with external vendors to maintain tradeshow assets and to develop message and image appropriate graphics for tradeshow assets.</li>
<li>
Establishes specific goals for each event, provides training for internal resources staffing the event, and calculates / tracks event ROI.</li>
<li>
Works within budget to manage and execute the development of content and the design and production of a hierarchy of branded marketing literature that communicates EP Alternative Markets product and industry capabilities.</li>
<li>
Ensures corporate and product branding is consistent across all collateral.</li>
<li>
Owns and implements process to standardize and streamline providing branded, professional product samples to customers from operations across our franchise.</li>
<li>
Ensures all marketing communications deliverables use consistent corporate branding and EP Alternative Markets product brand architecture.</li>
<li>
Plans and implements strategies to enhance lead generation from website presence, email marketing, tradeshow presence, etc.</li>
<li>
Trains sales people on how to use and maximize lead generation tools and integrate data gathered into CRM systems.</li>
<li>
Works across the organization to develop product and market specific content for SWM website; provides content to SWM Enterprise Communications Director for implementation on website.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
General</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Adheres to the SWM Code of Conduct and guiding principles</li>
<li>
Follows all SWM established methods, policies, rules, practices, and procedures.</li>
<li>
Follows internal approval process for all externally facing marcomm materials, including sign off from Director Alternative Markets</li>
<li>
Supports the other functions and team members within the Marketing &amp; Business Development team as needed: Business Development, Product Management, Commercial Directors</li>
<li>
Willing to travel up to 30%, including possible international travel</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education / Experience</b><b>:
</b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Advertising or business-related field with minimum 5-7 years’ experience in B2B marketing either at an industrial manufacturer or a marketing agency specializing in industrial/B2B marketing or a combination of education and experience.</p>
<p><b>
Required Capabilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Detail oriented, self-starter with ability to work in autonomous environment, plus a positive attitude</li>
<li>
Superior communication skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>
Excellent project management skills – capable of advanced planning, managing projects from start to finish, prioritizing critical path items</li>
<li>
Ability to meet multiple deadlines, prioritize and multi-task</li>
<li>
Tradeshow management experience</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of all social media platforms</li>
<li>
Intermediate to advanced computer skills including Excel, PowerPoint, CRM applications</li>
<li>
Ability to interact across multiple levels, time zones, and functions in the organization</li>
</ul>
