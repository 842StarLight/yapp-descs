<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
Account Manager
<br><br>
MT Business Technologies has exciting opportunities for motivated individuals with a passion for sales. We are looking for sales representatives to develop new customer relationships and maintain our excellent relationships with current customers. As a Sales Professional, your primary responsibility will be to sell our hardware and software solutions to accounts within a protected territory. This will involve daily activities to build a robust pipeline of qualified opportunities.
<br><br>
At MT Business Technologies, our mission is to provide total office solutions that satisfy the unique needs of our customer’s business. With a strong legacy of over 90 years in business, MT Business Technologies is an Ohio office solutions firm with multiple locations across the state including Toledo, Cleveland, Mansfield, and Columbus.
<br><br>
<b>Job Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Market products within your territory: initiate mailings, attend trade shows, provide product information</li>
<li>Cold call on prospective accounts</li>
<li>Attend networking events</li>
<li>Schedule and facilitate product demonstrations best suited to customer needs</li>
<li>Secure appointments with account decision makers</li>
<li>Close sales, gather all detailed information, and develop a sales plan</li>
<li>Perform business reviews with clients on a scheduled basis</li>
<li>Maintain accurate records of existing and prospective client information</li>
<li>Make proposals to expand customer service and equipment placements/upgrades</li>
<li>Maintain relationships with current customers by follow-up contact and consultation after the sale</li>
</ul>
<b>Job Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or proven track record of success in consultative sales preferred</li>
<li>1-3 years of sales experience preferred</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of the sales process with a focus on customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, prospecting, territory management, and closing skills</li>
<li>Professional appearance, articulate, and good presentation skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated, results-oriented, persistent, and self-confident</li>
<li>Good computer skills</li>
</ul>
<b>Compensation and Benefits:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Competitive base salary + uncapped monthly commissions + bonuses</li>
<li>Generous benefits package (medical insurance, life insurance, 401k, generous holiday/vacation days)</li>
<li>Protected Territory</li>
<li>Cell phone allowance</li>
<li>Mileage reimbursement</li>
</ul>
