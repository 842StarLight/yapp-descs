<h2>Regional Practice Manager - Primary Care Offices</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Position Purpose</b><br>
A member of Trinity Health, Holy Cross Hospital is a full-service, non-profit, Catholic, teaching hospital operating in the spirit of the Sisters of Mercy.<br>
<br>
This is a FT position on day shift with benefits. This position manages the day to day operations of 2 busy Primary Care locations.<br>
<b>Job Description Details:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manages day to day activities of all assigned practice locations.
</li>
<li>Assures productive, full schedules for each provider. Assists Director of Operations in finding replacement staff.
</li>
<li>Assists with employee screening and interviews. Works with the human resources department on successful on boarding of new employees.
</li>
<li>Performs time keeping reporting and monitoring.
</li>
<li>Handles all staff issues, complaints and concerns in a timely and confidential manner. Understands and applies disciplinary/discharge policy as appropriate.
</li>
<li>Evaluates team members and perform associate reviews.
</li>
<li>Ensures office adherence to OSHA, TJC and CMS standards for safety, infection control.
</li>
<li>Manages all office audits, practice reviews and reporting.
</li>
<li>Assures follow up of all billing and documentation inquiries.
</li>
<li>Communicates frequently with Director of operations on status of all practice functions.
</li>
<li>Responsible for productivity, financial, compliance reports, and sending daily, weekly and monthly practice reports.
</li>
<li>Motivates, communicates, and provides a positive environment in which all employees find job satisfaction.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>

<br>
<b>About Us</b><br>
Trinity Health is one of the largest multi-institutional Catholic health care delivery systems in the nation, serving diverse communities that include more than 30 million people across 22 states. Trinity Health includes 94 hospitals, as well as 109 continuing care locations that include PACE programs, senior living facilities, and home care and hospice services. Its continuing care programs provide nearly 2.5 million visits annually.<br>
<br>
Based in Livonia, Mich., and with annual operating revenues of $17.6 billion and assets of $24.7 billion, the organization returns $1.1 billion to its communities annually in the form of charity care and other community benefit programs. Trinity Health employs about 133,000 colleagues, including 7,800 employed physicians and clinicians.<br>
<br>
Committed to those who are poor and underserved in its communities, Trinity Health is known for its focus on the country's aging population. As a single, unified ministry, the organization is the innovator of Senior Emergency Departments, the largest not-for-profit provider of home health care services - ranked by number of visits - in the nation, as well as the nation's leading provider of PACE (Program of All Inclusive Care for the Elderly) based on the number of available programs.
