Assists external clients in the implementation of technical systems, software, or solutions. Evaluates client needs, develops configurations that support business processes, plans and executes on delivery and implementation plans, and tests and troubleshoots final system setups.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Documents customer requirements and configures applications to meet customer-specific business processes.</li>
<li>Interfaces with customers during design and deployment of business solutions.</li>
<li>Manages the solutions through deployment, training, and hand-off activities.</li>
<li>Translates client's business requirements into application definitions, workflows, business processes, and solutions.</li>
<li>Creates and maintains system-related documentation, such as business, system, and functional requirements, process flows, status reports, and configuration worksheets.</li>
<li>Identifies problems and researches alternatives through testing and consulting with peers.</li>
<li>Oversees resource schedules and coordinates schedule updates to maximize productivity and revenue capture.</li>
<li>Prepares presentations, gains consensus from client and implementation team, and implements solutions.</li>
<li>Configures and deploys enterprise application and business processes.</li>
<li>Tracks and reports key performance indicators/metrics.</li>
<li>Supports business process testing of the application prior to end-user acceptance testing.</li>
<li>Prepares hand-off and custom project training documents.</li>
<li>Ensures that all projects are delivered on time and on target of the budgets.</li>
<li>Participates in cross-functional teams to explore process improvement initiatives, measure results, and refine processes to achieve business objectives.</li>
<li>May be involved in pre-sales product demonstrations or provide assistance in scoping projects or developing proposals.</li>
<li>This position may have potential responsibility for SOC-II Controls.</li>
<li>This position may require knowledge of applicable data privacy practices and laws.</li>
<li>All employees of QSI NextGen are required to complete annual HIPPA and compliance training.</li>
<li>All employees of QSI NextGen are required to read and accept code of conduct upon hire and annually thereafter.</li>
<li>This role may have access to sensitive data to include PII and ePHI.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Active Listening - Ability to actively attend to, convey, and understand the comments and questions of others.</li>
<li>Business Acumen - Ability to grasp and understand business concepts and issues.</li>
<li>Communication, Oral - Ability to communicate effectively with others using the spoken word.</li>
<li>Communication, Written - Ability to communicate in writing clearly and concisely.</li>
<li>Customer Oriented - Ability to take care of the customers' needs while following company procedures.</li>
<li>Deductive Reasoning - Ability to apply principles of logical or scientific thinking to a wide range of intellectual and practical problems.</li>
<li>Detail Oriented - Ability to pay attention to the minute details of a project or task.</li>
<li>Problem Solving - Ability to find a solution for or to deal proactively with work-related problems.</li>
<li>Project Management - Ability to organize and direct a project to completion.</li>
<li>Time Management - Ability to utilize the available time to organize and complete work within given deadlines.</li>
<li>Working Under Pressure - Ability to complete assigned tasks under stressful situations.</li>
</ul>
<b>Skills &amp; Abilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree (four-year college or technical school) Preferred, Field of Study: Electronic data processing, information systems, systems analysis, and computer programming</li>
<li>3 plus years of experience in electronic data processing, information systems, systems analysis, and computer programming</li>
<li>Knowledge of circuit boards, processors, chips, electronic equipment, and computer hardware and software, including applications and programming</li>
</ul>
