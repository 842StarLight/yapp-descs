<h2>Chief of Staff, CPO</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Numerator is a market intelligence firm that brings together omnichannel marketing, merchandising and sales data to make pursuing new possibilities simple for brand, retail and agency clients. Owned by Vista Equity Partners, Numerator is the only company in the marketplace to connect omnichannel purchase data (powered by the InfoScout OmniPanel) and comprehensive path data to deliver an unmatched view of the consumer shopping and purchase experience.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
The ideal candidate is somebody who is very organized and makes every person on the greater team better. The role will be helping facilitate operational discipline, organizational change management and leadership support via insights and action. Responsibilities will be across the entire Product (Product Strategy, Product Development, Engineering and Operations) organization’s communication strategy, budgeting, MBO &amp; KPI tracking, liaison to the Board of Directors/executives/teams and overall execution of strategic and roadmap initiatives for the company. The role will report to the Chief Product Officer.
</p>
<p>A typical week would be to run the agenda for the Product senior leadership team, monitor progress against MBOs and KPIs, manage any budget variance requests, prepare for an internal update, analyze standard reports / dashboards that monitor the health of the product organization and handle any other projects as directed by the Chief Product Officer.
</p>
<p>The role will provide episodic, strategic updates and drive process improvement and capability development for the Product team.</p>
<p></p>
