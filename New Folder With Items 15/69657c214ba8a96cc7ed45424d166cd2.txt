<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>DESCRIPTION</b></h2>
<p><b><i>
Basic Purpose of Position: </i></b>The Service Administrator has four main functions. Serve as a backup to the Service Coordinator, open and track all Service jobs in AX, conducts AR collections for the department, and assist with billings.</p>
<ul>
<li>As a back up to the Service Coordinator<ul>
<li>Serves as the main point of contact for all service customers to ensure customer expectations are met.</li>
<li>Proactively follows up with customers after completion of service visits.</li>
<li>Handles decisions regarding the scheduling and movement of manpower and materials.</li>
<li>Schedules and dispatches field labor force to meet customer needs and expectations based on the nature of the call (Emergency, Preventive Maintenance, available resources and customer needs).</li>
<li>Supports material procurement for customer part sales (pricing part sales, processing orders and invoicing) as well as internal requests in support of Service projects.</li>
<li>Effectively utilizes Service Software in the delivery and management of service activities: opening service calls, supporting and scheduling planned preventive maintenance visits, updating customer information, creating purchase orders.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Open and track all Service jobs in AX.<ul>
<li>Works with our Sales team to collect all necessary information to be able to open new Service Jobs in AX.</li>
<li>Reviews and process contracts and POâ€™s.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>AR Collections
<ul>
<li>Reviews all open invoices and prioritizes collection efforts.</li>
<li>Works directly with our customers to collect all open and due invoices.</li>
<li>Works along with the rest of the Service Administrative team to make any necessary changes to invoice or processes to expedite invoice payments.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Billings<ul>
