<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>FM:Systems, a leading provider of Integrated Workplace Management Systems (IWMS) and the top performer in space management and workplace services software, is seeking an Account Manager to build, strengthen and grow client relationships within an assigned territory. We are looking for a results-driven individual who is passionate about maintaining and growing long term client relationships. In this role the successful candidate will play a key role in driving the revenue growth from current customers. This role is ideally based in our corporate HQ in Raleigh, NC.</p>
<p>
At FM:Systems, we have 3 guiding values around what we believe and how we behave: Do the right thing, act with urgency and cultivate a culture of excellence &amp; accountability. We aim to offer our clients an exceptional experience with every interaction, foster innovation and invest in our people. If this sounds like a company you would like to work for, apply today!</p>
<p><b>
In this role you will have the opportunity to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Cross sell and upsell an assigned installed base of customers.</li>
<li>
Engage enterprise customers with a proactive strategy.</li>
<li>
Increase customer satisfaction by understanding business needs and recommending FM:Systems solutions.</li>
<li>
Create a strong referral network.</li>
<li>
Expand current contract values through upselling additional software services.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The successful candidate for this role will have the following skills and experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree, preferably in Business.</li>
<li>
Proven track record with a minimum of 5 years’ experience growing SaaS revenue within assigned install base accounts.</li>
<li>
Strong communication, listening and writing skills.</li>
<li>
Organized and reliable – able to work independently with little direction when necessary.</li>
<li>
Self-starter with natural curiosity and technical agility.</li>
<li>
Strategic account planning discipline for enterprise application software.</li>
<li>
Work within cross-functional teams to build pipeline, progress pipeline, and close business.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 35% for this opportunity a must.</li>
</ul>
