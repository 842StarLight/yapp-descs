<h2>Supply Chain Risk Associate - Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Partner with clients to develop and execute supply chain consulting solutions that align compliance requirements with true risk management to improve the effectiveness and efficiency of operations. Assist with delivering - GRC risk management, projects and business improvement initiatives, which include consulting services that complement our supply chain auditing services, and software tools to manage risk. Assess current capabilities against best practices and identify strategic, tactical improvements and design solutions. Work with organizations to address some of their most significant business challenges. Play a role in the analysis and development of transformational business models and assist clients integrate and operate them.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Responsibilities:</b></p>
<div>
Design and develop BSI supply chain risk services, products and projects related to supply chain security, CSR/responsible sourcing, business continuity, and anti-bribery/corruption.
</div>
<ul>
<li>Promote BSI services and build network via presenting at conferences and/or webinars, and submitting articles for publication
</li>
<li>Accountable for revenue/profitability for client accounts</li>
<li>
Grow the business directly through selling through your network</li>
<li>
Grow the business indirectly by engaging with Business Development Managers to support their sales opportunities, understand client needs and identify opportunities for advisory projects in CSR/supply chain security</li>
<li>
Develop proposals and generate new sales through existing clients and client referrals in accordance with the sales plan. Introduces other products and services to clients.
</li>
<li>Scope and deliver projects and support senior Advisory team members in delivery of projects. Individual project delivery responsibilities will vary by client and project, but may include:
</li>
<li>Supply chain due diligence program development, gap/maturity assessment, and benchmarking
</li>
<li>Developing and delivering training workshops to clients and/or their suppliers</li>
<li>
Accountability for delivering on project deadlines with a high level of quality and integrity</li>
<li>
Bespoke client projects and research</li>
<li>
Supporting suppliers through management of supplier improvement programs via BSI software
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
