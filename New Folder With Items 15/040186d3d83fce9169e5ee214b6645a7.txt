<h2>Director Product Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Director Product Marketing
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>TrueCar is seeking an experienced product marketing leader to drive adoption of TrueCar’s growing portfolio of dealer solutions. The Director of Product Marketing will lead the overall go-to-market strategy, messaging, positioning and sales enablement, working closely with sales, product and marketing teams to meet revenue targets and customer satisfaction goals.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>This key role will work closely with our insights and analytics teams to deeply understand product capabilities, target markets, competitive landscape and emerging trends. This position will also guide and support our marketing communications and industry relations teams with product marketing content to ensure marketing campaigns and customer education programs align with go-to-market strategy, product capabilities and sales goals.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Director of Product Marketing must have a strong understanding of automotive marketing and retail operations, and a proven track record of revenue growth in an automotive marketing or software company. The ideal candidate will have a relentless focus on customer-centric messaging rooted in solving our client’s problems and should be skilled at distilling a complex map of features and capabilities into a clear and engaging storyline. This role reports to the SVP of Industry Marketing.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>Design, execute, and deliver on a comprehensive go-to-market strategy and develop product marketing assets supporting marketing and sales efforts, including product playbooks, sales toolkits, positioning statements, customer success stories and product FAQs.
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Leverage customer insights, market intelligence and market research to strategically position and differentiate TrueCar’s dealer solutions
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Demonstrate clear and compelling points of view including unique and actionable advice on how to scale and diversify the company’s dealer product portfolio.
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Develop deep market insights including developing competitive positioning, pricing and packaging recommendations to attract, win and grow product adoption.
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>In addition to the stated responsibilities, the Director of Product Marketing is expected to be a team player willing to take on key B2B marketing tasks and projects, contributing to TrueCar’s success.<br>
</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>Bachelor's degree in Marketing or related field, MBA preferred
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>10+ years of work experience in marketing or related field
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>5+ years of automotive industry experience at a leading marketing or software solution provider.
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Ability to simplify and present complex information with confidence to establish trust with multiple audiences - prospects, clients, sales and executive teams.
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Self sufficient and self-starter able to forge a strategic direction despite ambiguity and uncertainty.
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Agility with focus on speed-to-market of deliverables in a fast-changing environment.
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Excellent verbal, written and presentation skills
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Positive, enthusiastic attitude and a passion for quality work
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>This role is based in Austin TX
</b></p></li>
</ul>
