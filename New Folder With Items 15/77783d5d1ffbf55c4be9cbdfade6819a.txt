<h2>Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>CBRE Global Investors (the "Firm") is seeking summer interns in its Americas direct real estate investment management business to underwrite and value new and existing investments. Investments will vary by property type, investment type and risk profile.<br>
<br>
CBRE Global Investors is a wholly-owned, independently operated division of CBRE Group, Inc. (“CBRE”). The Firm, founded in 1972, has an exclusive focus on investing in real assets. The Firm maintains operations in the Americas, Europe and Asia and has over 600 institutional investors and clients with $105.5 billion in assets under management.<br>
<br>
The business philosophy is to employ a disciplined, research-based investment process executed by experienced teams whose interests are closely aligned with investors. Investments are primarily made in markets and assets judged to have above-average risk-adjusted return potential and are actively managed throughout the holding period. The Firm capitalizes on the “CBRE knowledge advantage” throughout the investment process, integrating local market intelligence provided by the CBRE platform into research, strategy development and investment execution.<br>
