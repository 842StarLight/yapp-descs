<h2>Network Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for an energetic network support specialist to work in our fast-paced operations center in Phoenix, Arizona. The ideal candidate will provide superior customer service in support of the company's network infrastructure and a passion for expanding his or her knowledge. The selected candidate must have a strong work ethic, the ability to hit deadlines, and the skills to communicate effectively.</p>
<p>Weekend and night shifts are required for this position.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical assistance to customers</li>
<li>Work with engineers, software developers and customers to identify, troubleshoot and resolve issues and improve product and network issues</li>
<li>Provide installation assistance to both customers and field service technicians</li>
<li>Coordinate tasks and manage workflow of multiple vendors to solve customer needs</li>
<li>Document all tickets with accurate and current information</li>
<li>Monitor multiple networks and proactively identify potential issues</li>
<li>Keep current with software releases, known compatibility issues, equipment configurations, and network protocols</li>
<li>Assist engineers with development and testing</li>
<li>Other tasks assigned by the NOC Manager</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 1-year networking experience. Electronic trade school and/or certifications can substitute for the required experience.</li>
<li>Understanding of payment and point-of-sale technology preferred</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of WAN/LAN and legacy protocols</li>
<li>Skilled at understanding and analyzing the designs provided by the network manager to be able to monitor the network</li>
<li>Good verbal and written communication skills along with basic understanding of technical terms</li>
<li>Excellent logical skills, mathematical skills and technical skills</li>
<li>Cisco CCNA certification a plus</li>
</ul>
