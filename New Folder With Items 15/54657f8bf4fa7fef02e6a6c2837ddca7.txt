<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Product Manager Basic Scope/Purpose: </b></p>
<p>Drive profitable growth of the product line through leadership of the product team; responsible for Product Management activities from strategic planning to tactical execution.</p>
<p><b>Principal Accountabilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify, assemble, and lead the product team to include Sales, NPD, Product Engineering, Applications Engineering, Marcom, Finance, and Operations/Supply Chain.</li>
<li>Develop and execute Strategic Product Plan.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a Product Fact Book.</li>
<li>Gather and interpret intelligence on competitors, markets, customers and regulations.</li>
<li>Develop product training materials, pricing strategies, and go-to market strategies.</li>
<li>Fill the NPD pipeline, develop product definitions, and lead product launches.</li>
<li>Develop a communications strategy to include advertising, collateral and product specific materials.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance, recommend proper inventory investments, establish customer service and quality requirements.</li>
<li>Support product supplier selection and cost reduction projects.</li>
<li>Review and approve product changes that affect Form, Fit, and Function.</li>
<li>Manage product portfolio with special attention to product life cycle management.</li>
<li>Regularly report to management on product activity and profitability.</li>
<li>Maintains and promotes Fenner Drives Values and Behaviors at all times.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
