<h2>LEAD OPERATIONS ANALYST</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><div><div>Responsible for supporting dedicated customer engagements, addressing post-sale network operations and related technical issues. Will interface with various levels of management and multiple internal work groups and organizations to lead and direct customer service improvements, resolve customer service problems and issues, and act as the primary escalation resource to the customer. Will provide a single point of contact and be accessible 24x7 when normal Project Operations fail to meet the customer's expectations.<br>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Customer Care Relationship and Communication Management:</b> Required to be an expert at identifying and utilizing resources. Must continually develop relationships within the customer base, Project Operations Team and other organizations. Will be an effective team member providing customers with a superior customer service experience through communication, coordination, and facilitation, in addition to follow up on customer satisfaction surveys.</li>
<li>
<b>Manage Service Level Requirements:</b> The goal is to reduce customer network outages, time to repair, and SLR credits. Will perform real time 24X7 escalations to help facilitate timely and accurate resolution of repair issues. Will develop service improvement plans in conjunction with their customer and the Project Operations Team. Will identify process issues, Root Cause Analysis and solutions. Responsible for ensuring Project Operations Team's customer notification databases have current and accurate customer information at all times.</li>
<li>
<b>Stewardship Reporting:</b> Works with Reporting Team to develop, analyze and distribute reports based on customer specifications. Some reports could include: Trouble History, Mean Time to Repair, % Availability, Ticket Resolution, Reason for Outages, Network Optimization, credit for Service Level Requirements details, etc. Also included are inventories, network enhancements and current organization and escalation charts.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div></div></div>

Job Description</b>

<div><div>
<b>Preferred Technical Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Engineering or a related field and/or 2-5 years of equivalent experience in sales engineering, engineering and/or sales.</li>
<li>CPE - A basic understanding of routers, switches, Voice CPE and their applications from local access to the enterprise and WAN environments.</li>
<li>WAN Protocols - A basic understanding of network protocols.</li>
<li>Products - A basic understanding of MPLS, Frame Relay, IP, ISDN, Voice Services.</li>
<li>Industry terminology - A basic understanding of the OSI model and principles, and a solid understanding of industry terminology i.e. POPs, COs, muxing, CPE, ILEC, HiCap.</li>
<li>Voice/Data network design principles - A basic understanding of network design to include the components end to end. A basic understanding of voice switching technologies and equipment, PBX interconnection, 911 networks and equipment.</li>
<li>Data Services- An understanding of IP routing protocols such as RIP, OSPF, IGRP, BGP and how they are utilized and the impacts the present in the WAN environment.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Non-Technical Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Decision making, analytical, problem solving, negotiation, communications (oral, written and listening), organizational, project management, team work, and presentation skills to lead and influence customer and cross- functional teams.</li>
<li>Ability to work autonomously on multiple projects and associated deliverables while maintaining strong customer focus.</li>
<li>Fluency with MS office product suite (EXCEL, PowerPoint).</li>
<li>Travel required. Must be able to travel nationally and possess a valid driver's license with a satisfactory driving record.</li>
</ul>
