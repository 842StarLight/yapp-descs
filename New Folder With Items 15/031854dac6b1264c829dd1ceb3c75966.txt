<p><b>
COMPANY DESCRIPTION:
</b></p>
<p>Hormel Foods Corporation, based in Austin, Minn., is a global branded food company with over $9 billion in annual revenues across more than 80 countries worldwide. Its brands include <i>SKIPPY</i><i>®</i>, <i>SPAM</i>®, <i>Hormel</i><i>®</i><i> Natural Choice</i><i>®</i><i>, Applegate</i><i>®</i><i>, Justin's</i><i>®</i><i>, Wholly Guacamole</i><i>®</i><i>, Hormel</i><i>®</i><i> Black Label</i><i>®</i><i>, Columbus</i><i>®</i> and more than 30 other beloved brands. The company is a member of the S&amp;P 500 Index and the S&amp;P 500 Dividend Aristocrats, was named one of "The 100 Best Corporate Citizens" by Corporate Responsibility Magazine for the 10th year in a row, and has received numerous other awards and accolades for its corporate responsibility and community service efforts. In 2016, the company celebrated its 125th anniversary and announced its new vision for the future - <i>Inspired People. Inspired Food.™</i> - focusing on its legacy of innovation. For more information, visit <b>www.hormelfoods.com</b> and <b>http://csr.hormelfoods.com/</b>. To learn more about Hormel Foods International Corporation, visit <b>https://www.hormelfoods.com/about/our-family/hormel-foods-international/</b></p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>
The incumbent is responsible for building, planning, executing and measuring the annual operating plans for the territory. Also has P&amp;L responsibility which requires managing top and bottom line performance based on annual plans. This position is critical in building growth strategies for the region, including but not limited to market penetration, market development, new/alternate channels, new product development and mergers and acquisitions.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><b>Business management:</b> works directly with Product Managers to develop annual plans, new product development initiatives, advertising and promotional programs to achieve plan figures. Execute against the HFIC Annual Operating Plan with related distributors, brokers and direct accounts in the market place. Develops and assesses opportunities for volume, trade spend and profitability for all commodity groups with an end result of achieving gross margin and segment profit goals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><b>Regulatory Compliance:</b> interacts with logistics and international compliance to improve lead times, comply with all legal requirements and keep product and sanitary registrations in good standing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><b>Supply Chain:</b> work closely with demand planning to effectively manage inventory and code dates for key products.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><b>In field education:</b> provides constant training to distributors and brokers to represent Hormel professionally and effectively with their own customers and accounts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><b>Financial Management:</b> this position requires a good understanding of P&amp;L management, including but not limited to understanding cost trends, setting price strategies, and determining margin targets by category and by account. Responsible for keeping expenses under control and according to AOP.</p>
