<h2>Manager – Strategic Alliances</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>As a Manager – Strategic Alliances for large OEM like SAP, Oracle, Microsoft, IBM &amp; Salesforce, you will have an exciting opportunity to deliver on our strategy to build mind-share for Wipro around Application Services business with the above-mentioned Technology OEMs.</div>
<div>
Your responsibilities within Wipro will include driving C-level and field relationships across these OEM in the region. By establishing and growing business and technical relationships, and managing the day-to-day interactions with these OEMs and Wipro field sales/practice teams.</div>
<div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The ideal candidate will possess both a business/sales background that enables them to engage at the CXO level and field level, as well as technical competency focused on the IT landscape and Digital technologies.</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Roles &amp; Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Execute the strategic business plan for partner alliance program for the region – measured by collaborative revenue (influence revenue) and joint services opportunity pipeline.</li>
<li>
Build key relationships with OEMs and Wipro field teams – measured by partner executive connects, Wipro field connects in the region</li>
<li>
Identify specific customer segments and industry verticals to approach with a joint value proposition with the OEMs</li>
<li>
Serve as a key member of the GTM team in helping to define and deliver Wipro’s overall go-to-market strategy for application services in India.</li>
<li>
Conduct Pro-active Joint Account Planning Sessions for key accounts with opportunities – measured by preparation of joint account plans and plan achievement against target</li>
<li>
Consistently message the alliance value proposition and strategic direction internally and externally – evaluated by Wipro’s choice as a preferred partner in the region and measured by joint strategic initiatives in play, executive connects in the region</li>
<li>
Own the relationship map, and drive and monitor cadence meetings, both with Partner field teams and with Wipro internal field &amp; practice teams</li>
<li>
Participate in sales and delivery escalation resolution jointly with partner organization</li>
<li>
Manage all reporting requirements and performance metrics such as pipeline and revenue</li>
<li>
Basic Qualifications</li>
<li>
8+ years of experience in world class technology company. Experience in Application Services business in India is must.</li>
<li>
3-4 years of experience in managing alliances with Oracle/SAP/IBM/Salesforce/Microsoft from Application services perspective.</li>
<li>
Good understanding in atleast one of the Technology areas around SAP/Oracle ERP, CRM, Digital Technologies (Cloud, Azure, Customer Experience (Portal, Content Management, UI/UX, Mobility), Enterprise Experience (BPM, SOA, Micro Services/API Management)) is must.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to initiate action, manage change, make high impact decisions, and complete challenging assignments.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree required; MBA a plus.</li>
<li>
Self-driven, Initiative taker, Analytical, Thought leadership, communication and collaboration skills are utmost essential</li>
</ul>
