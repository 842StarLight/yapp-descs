<b>
A Day in the Life</b><br>
The Medtronic Supply Chain (SC) Organization is driving a functional transformation focused on improving business outcomes for internal and external stakeholders. One of the key strategic focus areas for the SC functional transformation is developing and deploying integrated business planning (IBP) across Medtronic. We are seeking a Senior Supply Chain Analyst for Americas IBP &amp; Core Planning to coordinate the monthly core planning and Integrated Business Planning cadence for Americas and sub-regions. You will ensure Integrated Business Planning continually improves and aligns with industry best practice and continues to support Medtronic in building executable plans, accurate demand forecasts and communicate key decision requirements.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate Americas Regional Demand review process for IBP (S&amp;OP Coordination) and support the Americas IBP Deployment lead</li>
<li>Finalize agenda and key issues for the Americas Regional Demand review meeting each month</li>
<li>Ensure all steps are adhered to within the Demand Review process that is constructed, and information flows seamlessly from one process step to another</li>
<li>Responsible for overall meeting deck creation and Americas IBP Demand Review meeting inputs, outputs, minutes and action log</li>
<li>Track the key metrics to monitor the effectiveness of the process and report the metrics back to the Americas IBP deployment lead, Enterprise IBP process owner and the team to identify root causes for correction or process improvement</li>
<li>Generate recommendations to the IBP leadership team to ensure inventory levels are optimized, and excess inventory is dispositioned to support business objectives</li>
<li>Responsible for the system and tools maintenance and data integrity, and work together with the Enterprise team to streamline processes and enhance tools to ensure continuous improvement</li>
<li>Collaborate with business leaders and sub process representatives to ensure that the process and practices are aligned with the principles of IBP and that processes are designed to meet the requirements of the region.</li>
<li>Train local Demand Planners on tactical Demand Review processes and tools</li>
<li>Document processes, procedures, and training materials in alignment with common GSC standards</li>
</ul>
