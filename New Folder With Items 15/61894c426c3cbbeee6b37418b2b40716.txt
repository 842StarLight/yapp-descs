<h2>Sr. Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Innovate to solve the world's most important challenges</p>
<p>We’re looking for a talented, experienced product manager to lead part of Honeywell’s global, mobile computer offering. The right person for this job wants to make a noticeable impact on results while gaining valuable experience in one of the most admired companies in the world.</p>
<p>
The future is what you make it.</p>
<p>
When you join Honeywell, you become a member of our global team of thinkers, innovators, dreamers and doers who make the things that make the future.</p>
<p>
That means changing the way we fly, fueling jets in an eco-friendly way, keeping buildings smart and safe and even making it possible to breathe on Mars.</p>
<p>
Working at Honeywell isn’t just about developing cool things. That’s why all of our employees enjoy access to dynamic career opportunities across different fields and industries.<br>
Are you ready to help us make the future?</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive all aspects of the product life cycle from concept to end-of-Life</li>
<li>
Define market requirements for future products based on primary and secondary research with customers (VOC), sales, partners, and internal sources</li>
<li>
Develop and execute plans addressing product, pricing, channel, messaging and competitive positioning; work closely with global marketing teams on launch and promotion</li>
<li>
Define, promote internally and gain executive approval for roadmaps, strategies, policies and plans for the product line</li>
<li>
Work with Sales and Corporate Communications to evangelize and promote the product in achieving quarterly revenue and profit goals</li>
<li>
Work with planning, regional marketing and factory teams to produce a forecast that ensures product availability while minimizing inventory costs</li>
</ul>
<p>
Ability to travel 10% to 30% domestically and internationally.</p>
<p><b>You must have</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>
7 - 10 years of experience as a product manager of a network-connected, hardware/software product offering sold globally through multiple channels</li>
</ul>
<p>
 <b>We value:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
MBA</li>
<li>
Experience being held accountable for financial results</li>
<li>
Ability to lead, including influencing executive decisions with evidence-based, clearly-articulated arguments</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrated technical aptitude acquired through a technical degree or experience.</li>
<li>
Experience delivering technology solutions to logistics, field mobility, retail or similar markets</li>
<li>
Experience marketing solutions involving IoT, telecom or other distributed network solutions and offerings</li>
</ul>
