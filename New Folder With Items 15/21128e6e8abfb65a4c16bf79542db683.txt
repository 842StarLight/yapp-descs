<div><b>University Supply Chain Overview</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>J&amp;J Supply Chain encompasses supply chain organizations across the Johnson &amp; Johnson Family of Companies. This model enables strategic supply chain decision-making across our companies, while keeping supply chain organizations embedded in our sectors and connected to our businesses, close to our respective markets and customers. All functions that “plan, source, make and deliver” our products – are part of J&amp;J Supply Chain. Serving the needs of over 1 billion people who use J&amp;J products with our Global approach, we manufacture more than 26 billion product units per year! Process begins at raw component sourcing to end-product distribution with high quality, compliance and service performance.
</div>
<div>The Supply Chain assignment provides development in leadership, analytical, and project management skills. Cross-functional team work provides an opportunity to partner with a variety of people in various functions in the organization. We offer an interactive, team environment, where your capabilities and skills will be developed to build a strong career foundation.</div>
