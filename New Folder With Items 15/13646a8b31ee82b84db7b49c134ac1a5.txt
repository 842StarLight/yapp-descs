<h2>Associate Scientist - Technical Applications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Quanterix is a company that's digitizing biomarker analysis with the goal of advancing the science of precision health. The company's digital health solution, Simoa, has the potential to change the way in which healthcare is provided today by giving researchers the ability to closely examine the continuum from health to disease. Quanterix' technology is designed to enable much earlier disease detection, better prognoses and enhanced treatment methods to improve the quality of life and longevity of the population for generations to come. The technology is currently being used for research applications in several therapeutic areas, including oncology, neurology, cardiology, inflammation and infectious disease. The company was established in 2007 and is located in Billerica, Massachusetts. For additional Information, please visit https://www.quanterix.com ( https://www.quanterix.com/ ).
<br><br>
<b>Position Overview:</b><br>
This critical role will be part of our Technical Support team and serve as a key technical point of contact between our customers and the Quanterix internal organization with an assay and application focus. As the technical experts working side-by-side with our customers, the Technical Applications Associate Scientist will:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Professionally, efficiently and conclusively triage, troubleshoot, and resolve customer's technical questions</li>
<li>Log and manage customer issues using Quanterix's complaint management system</li>
<li>Troubleshoot a variety of issues related to Quanterix products with a focus on instruments under limited instruction</li>
<li>Strong interest in learning our Simoa technology and support our end-users of the technology</li>
<li>Collaborate with colleagues to ensure efficient and timely support for our customers</li>
<li>Assist customers with and provide analysis of customer data</li>
<li>Develop and maintain positive relationships with customers</li>
<li>Triage and dispatch issues requiring field support to Field Service Engineers and Field Applications Scientists</li>
<li>Provide technical guidance regarding the choice and use of the appropriate Quanterix product to existing and prospective customers</li>
<li>Working closely with sales and marketing, QA/QC, research and development to address customer concerns, determine corrective action and customer follow-up</li>
<li>Collaborate with colleagues and other teams to offer the level and quality of support our customers expect</li>
<li>Working hour flexibility may be required to support customers leveraging technology for translational applications or outside of EST time zone.</li>
<li>Approximate travel 15%</li>
</ul>
