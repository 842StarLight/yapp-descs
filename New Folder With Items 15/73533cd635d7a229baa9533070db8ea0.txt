<h2>Sr. Credit Controller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Company Description</b></p>
<p>Dahua Technology USA Inc. is a subsidiary of Dahua Technology, the world’s second-largest manufacturer of video surveillance equipment by market share, according to a 2018 IHS report. Dahua Technology USA brings high-value, total security solutions to the North American market by focusing on integrity and personal relationships to enhance the customer experience. Dahua Technology’s surveillance solutions, from award-winning cameras to video management software, deliver unparalleled quality, reliability, and stability. Dahua Technology USA provides sales and support throughout the U.S. and Canada.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>The Sr. Credit Controller position will work as part of the Finance department responsible for the overseeing of the collections of customer accounts through communicating between various functions within Dahua North America. This associate will handle issues related to credit assessments and improve, create, and maintain processes and accurate records of financial activity. Through this approach this associate will make great decisions that exceed company standards.</p>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Collect payments and minimize customer debt (aged/current) - maintain high customer satisfaction and loyalty</p>
<p>· Communicate efficiently with customers and internal business team</p>
<p>· Follow up on, collect and allocate payments</p>
<p>· Close monitoring of debtor balances including non-payments, delayed payments and other irregularities, manage and resolve customer disputes;</p>
<p>· Performing customer billing, collection and reporting activities according to specific deadlines</p>
<p>· Working closely with other relevant departments and maintaining strong internal relationships.</p>
<p>· Working to agreed KPIs and exceeding targets</p>
<p>· Analyze reporting and implement processes based on findings</p>
<p>· Update and retrieve information for customers credit applications</p>
<p>· Review and approve shipping according to credit exposure</p>
<p>· Reconcile all credit card related payments and resolved customer disputes</p>
<p>· Analyzing employee expenses and resolve related inquires raised by employees</p>
<p>· Maintain all finance related documents in accordance with compliance requirements</p>
<p>· Provide support for other areas of Finance when required</p>
<p><b>Competencies: </b></p>
<ul><li>Organizational Support--Follows policies and procedures; Completes administrative tasks correctly and on time; supports organization's goals and values; Benefits organization through outside activities; Supports affirmative action and respects diversity.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oral Communication--Speaks clearly and persuasively in positive or negative situations; listens and gets clarification; Responds well to questions; Demonstrates group presentation skills; Participates in meetings.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Written Communication--Writes clearly and informatively; Edits work for spelling and grammar; Varies writing style to meet needs; Presents numerical data effectively; Able to read and interpret written information.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ethics--Treats people with respect; Keeps commitments; inspires the trust of others; Works with integrity and ethically; Upholds organizational values.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Quality--Demonstrates accuracy and thoroughness; Looks for ways to improve and promote quality; Applies feedback to improve performance; Monitors own work to ensure quality.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Interpersonal Skills--Focuses on solving conflict, not blaming; Maintains confidentiality; Listens to others without interrupting; Keeps emotions under control; Remains open to others' ideas and tries new things.</li></ul>
<p><b>Required Education and Experience</b></p>

<p>· Financial management experience is desireable</p>

<p>· Bachelor’s degree or above in Accounting, Finance, or related field</p>
<p>· Excellent interpersonal, presentation and reporting skills</p>
