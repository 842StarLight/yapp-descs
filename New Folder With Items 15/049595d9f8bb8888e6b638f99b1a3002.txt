<p><b>Overview: </b></p>
<p>If you want the opportunity to earn $100,000 plus per year and you are passionate about sales we have the position for you. As a Customer Development Associate at the Kenneth Clark Co (KCC), you will be one of the reasons why we will continue to grow our customer base. High Quality Service is what we do and being the top freight service provider in the country is what we strive for. Your main objective will be to develop, build, and manage a portfolio of customer accounts within in your territory. You will be selling freight and shipping services to companies that manufacture or distribute a variety of goods throughout North America.</p>
<p>Why KCC?</p>
<p>Kenneth Clark Co (KCC) is a leader in the third-party logistics industry. Its fast-paced, energetic sales model and state-of-the-art technology has allowed us to be one of the most sought after logistics companies in North America. Motivated employees help to arrange truckload freight movements every day. Fierce dedication to the company’s winning principles – integrity, honesty and teamwork – gives KCC the edge to continually exceed the expectations of its employees, customers and carriers, and makes this a company people are proud to be associated with.</p>
