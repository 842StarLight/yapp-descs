<h2>Sales Associate - furniture and building materials</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Second Chance, Inc., a non-profit social enterprise, is hiring full-time Sales Associates to provide critical sales support to customers in a 250,000 square foot warehouse. The Sales Associate is responsible for meeting or exceeding sales goals. You will strive to create long-term customer relationships that will lead to increased sales. Ultimately responsible for creating a welcoming atmosphere and providing an exceptional customer experience. If you're interested in joining a business with a heart, we'd like to interview you.</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure that each customer receives exceptional service by providing a friendly environment - greeting and acknowledging each customer.</li>
<li>Contribute to the store's sales goals by negotiating prices within the prescribed parameters.</li>
<li>Develop sales tickets to describe sold products and appropriate price.</li>
<li>Maintain solid product knowledge of all items in the store.</li>
<li>Maintain an awareness of all promotions and advertisements.</li>
<li>Assist with merchandising display, maintenance and store cleanliness.</li>
<li>Share the company's vision and mission with our customers</li>
</ul>
<p>Minimum Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent experience preferred.</li>
<li>Competent skills in basic math and ability to measure furniture and goods for customers when needed.</li>
<li>Retail sales experience required -- sales experience in furniture, white goods, building materials or antiques a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate with people from all walks of life.</li>
<li>Ability to maneuver around sales floor, work with and around cleaning chemicals, and lift/carry up to 30 lbs.</li>
<li>Ability to work a flexible schedule to meet the needs of the business, including holidays and weekend shifts.</li>
<li>Ability to walk throughout a 250,000 square foot warehouse to assist customers.</li>
</ul>
<p>Hours of Operation: 8:45 am to 5:15 pm</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $12.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>retail: 3 years (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Required)</li></ul>
