<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>We currently have a great opportunity available for a Sales Contracts Analyst I. The Sales Contracts Analyst I will be responsible for reviewing and processing equipment sales contract packages; work together with customers and field sales to ensure proper, accurate and complete supporting documentation is received prior to processing; update internal sales systems and communicate with other departments to ensure proper contract setup resulting in accurate invoicing.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li><div>
Responsible for reviewing and processing complete sales packages. This includes review of all required documents such as equipment contract, customer purchase order, tax status form, as well as other supporting documents. Analyst will work closely with field sales and management to obtain all necessary signatures per company policy and verify all associated paperwork is accurate and complete prior to proper execution including data entry in required systems.</div></li>
<li>
Ensure all documents and relevant email communications are properly stored and retained. This includes creating an electronic folder for each customer and saving all documents associated with equipment order with proper nomenclature to appropriate systems.</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing support for basic customized proposals. Communicate relevant contract and addendum data to customers and provide support as needed.</li>
<li>
Other duties and projects as may be assigned.<br>
</li>
</ul>
