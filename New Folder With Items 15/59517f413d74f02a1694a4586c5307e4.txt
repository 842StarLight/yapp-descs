<h2>Commercial Underwriting Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Title: </b>Director</p>
<p><b>
Department: </b>Investment Advisory Services (IAS)</p>
<p><b>
Position Reports to: </b>Managing Director</p>
<div>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>
Trimont Real Estate Advisors is a commercial real estate services firm, which has been providing asset management, loan servicing, due diligence and customized advisory solutions to commercial real estate lenders and investors for over 30 years. We are a trusted partner to clients in the Americas, Europe and Asia.</p>
<p>
We are seeking a Director to assist the IAS team in the underwriting process through financial modeling, due diligence and analysis.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Principal Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Lead comprehensive and portfolio level assignments including secondary market portfolio acquisitions (e.g., b-piece, non-distressed loan pools) with limited supervision from senior team members</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in managing client relationships with limited oversight from Client Lead and/or Business Unit Head</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide employee management for team of individuals that are in a more junior position</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create and edit financial models for the valuation and cash flow projections of assets; run ARGUS audits and scenarios</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist the underwriting team in valuing office, retail, industrial, multi-family, hospitality and unimproved land assets</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work with team in collecting all required underwriting documentation and monitor receipt of information to meet target dates established for closing the transactions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create and manage the underwriting database</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provides quality control on materials prepared by more junior team members</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Lead presentations to investment / loan committees with limited to no supervision from more senior team members</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Interacting with third party consultants including attorneys, title agents, brokers and sales agents, and construction consultants</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Perform lease abstracts</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Conduct property site inspections and market research</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Required qualifications</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Thorough understanding of capital structures and positions within capital structure including b-pieces and mezzanine financing</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>General understanding of all major underwriting criteria across all property types</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Advanced knowledge and experience with Excel based financial modeling including Macro and VBA knowledge</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic knowledge of financial statement analysis, general accounting, cash flows and mortgage interest calculations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic knowledge of third party market information platforms (Costar, REIS, RCA).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Highly analytical thinking with demonstrated talent for identifying, scrutinizing, improving, and streamlining complex work processes</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Results-driven achiever with exemplary planning and organizational skills, along with a high degree of detail orientation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Exceptional listener and communicator who effectively conveys information verbally and in writing</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Resourceful team player who excels at building trusting relationships with clients and colleagues</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Applicable knowledge of Microsoft Office suite</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>College (BS/BA) degree</p>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>8 -12 years of Commercial real estate loan underwriting experience</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ARGUS DCF and AE knowledge.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Understanding of commercial real estate transactions and industry terms</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience with real estate related software applications and market data providers including Argus, CoStar, REIS and Bloomberg</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Academic background in real estate, finance, economics, accounting or business administration</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
In performing their respective tasks and duties, employees are expected to:</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Interact professionally with all other employees, customers and organizations</li>
<li>Work effectively as a team contributor on all assignments</li>
<li>Work independently while understanding the necessity for communicating and coordinating work efforts with other employees and organizations</li>
<br>
</ul>
