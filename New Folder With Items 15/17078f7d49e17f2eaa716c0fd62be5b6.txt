<h2>Brand Manager, E-Commerce</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Who We Are
<br><b>Thrasio was established in 2018 with a clear mission:</b> to become the largest, most profitable seller on Amazon. Over the past year, our growth has been dramatic; we have over $100MM in committed capital, we're sustainably profitable and by the end of 2019 will surpass 100 employees that sit across 5 global offices. Thrasio creates and sells over 1,000 best selling products on Amazon across a number of different categories; everything from household goods and pet products to fitness equipment and baby devices. We hire remarkably smart and driven individuals that thrive on building a company that is disrupting the way e-commerce is done; if this sounds like you, let's talk. At Thrasio, we're disrupting, reimagining, and innovating the consumer goods industry for the next century, join the ride.
<br><br>
The Role
<br>This is a great opportunity for a self-starter to take ownership of a portfolio of products, ensuring their success day-to-day including: content and creative on the listing, marketing tactics, financial health of the business, customer service, risk mitigation and compliance, inventory and developing strategies for growth. Your success will be based on your intimate knowledge of the ASINs you manage, ability to diagnose issues and develop action plans. Other responsibilities include:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Analyze Amazon ASIN level data (sales, conversation rate, page views, costs, etc.) to determine why certain products are trending up or down, clearly articulate observations and propose strategies to troubleshoot problems.</li>
<li>Day-to-day maintenance of keeping product listings successful on Amazon. This includes: best seller tags, monitoring metrics shifts, understanding market trends, changing customer profiles, demographics, daily reporting, competitive landscape, pricing changes, liasoning with offshore customer service teams, and inventory monitoring.</li>
<li>Develop strategies to increase sales, reduce costs and drive conversion rate of acquired products including: price optimization, PPC, packaging/inserts, coupons and deals, customer communications, creative, changing package dimensions, subscribe and save, removing unprofitable products and more.</li>
<li>Produce daily, weekly and monthly reporting for all products, coordinating with our finance team to ensure we are showing an accurate P&amp;L.</li>
<li>Proactively catch issues before they are problems and understand everything about the ecosystem in which your products are selling (e.g., competitor landscape, industry landscape, Amazon marketplace rule changes).</li>
<li>Research programs and tools on Amazon and other ecosystems to increase sales and ensure we're protecting our brand (e.g., Amazon Transparency)</li>
<li>Lead the transition effort when we acquire a business. This includes doing a deep dive of all information provided during diligence, migrating the prior seller's accounts (social media, email software, customer service management, etc.), developing a relationship with the seller to ask questions and doing an audit (and potentially an overhaul) of their entire listing and end-to-end customer process.</li>
<li>Interface with our legal team in the event of intellectual property or other legal issues, such as product suspension or identifying an illegal seller of our product.</li>
<li>Coordinate with our Customer Obsession Manager and overseas customer service team to ensure we are providing a world class customer experience for your product portfolio.</li>
<li>Partner with internal teams to develop a go-to-market strategy and facilitate successful, impactful product launches and international expansion.</li>
<li>Coordinate with our supply chain team to ensure products are always in stock, premium quality, have updated packaging, and have all appropriate required certifications.</li>
</ul>
What You Bring to the Party
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience in product management, marketing, or an analytical function, preferably at a startup or other fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>3+ years of e-commerce experience, preferably working within the Amazon ecosystem.</li>
<li>Ability to dig through data, draw conclusions and make recommendations.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills, a high level of organization, and impeccable attention to detail.</li>
<li>Ability to handle aggressive deadlines and prioritize work in a fast-paced, often ambiguous environment with fire drills coming up on a near-daily basis.</li>
<li>A highly analytical self-starter who can manage multiple projects and figure out complex problems without a lot of oversight.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to create engaging content, come up with creative ideas, and think outside the box.</li>
<li>Positive attitude, reliable, transparent and enjoy working with different types of people.</li>
<li>Strong proficiency in excel.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Business, Marketing, Data Science or a related field.</li>
</ul>
