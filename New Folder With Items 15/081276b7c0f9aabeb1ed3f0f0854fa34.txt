<div><b>Position Summary
</b></div>
<div>We are seeking a Principal Metallurgical Engineer with extensive experience in the field of flotation as the Subject Matter Expert for Worley Mining and Metals group. The candidate will be an experienced senior process engineer with experience over the range of engineering progressions from conceptual studies, various studies levels (Level 1-5), detailed engineering and commissioning. Primary responsibility will be front end study efforts which include a component of sales and business development.
</div>
<div>The candidate must have a working knowledge of extractive metallurgy processes across a range of commodities (gold, copper, nickel, lead/zinc, etc.) and various downstream related processing circuits and facilities.
</div>
<div>The Subject Matter Expert will also have the responsibility for introducing systems and procedures for the integration and long term retention of other process engineers within the Mining and Metals line of business. Responsibilities include team management across multiple locations, overall global alignment of process designs, heat and material balances, equipment specifications with conditions of service, review of equipment and mineral services RFQ packages, development of Process Flow Diagrams (PFDs) and Piping &amp; Instrument Diagrams (P&amp;IDs).
</div>
<div>Excellent writing and verbal communication skills, client interaction and organizational skills, and proficiency with process modelling software such as MetSim and excel based process modeling. Candidate must be highly motivated, customer focused, project oriented and work well in a team environment. Candidate should also have the ability to manage a heavy workload, travel for short periods of time, and support international mining projects.
</div>
