<b>Position Summary</b><br>
<b>We’re searching for a Manager, Applied Analytics</b> who will be a leader within the core Data and Analytics (DNA) team. Data and Analytics is strutuced into three specialties: Applied Analytics (AA), Data Science (DS) and Data Strategy &amp; Technology (DS&amp;T).<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Applied Analytics leaders are responsible for developing and translating the data story in meaningful ways and applying analytical insights to business challenges. This requires being a jack-of-all-trades when it comes to business and marketing performance analytics. Applied Analytics leads are responsible for the foundational elements that make our clients better able to plan, optimize, measure, and share the performance of their marketing efforts.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Tasks and responsibilities include:</b></div>
<div><ul><li> Support the development of the overall measurement strategy for clients through discovery and development of measurement plans, learning agendas, and test plans</li></ul></div>
<ul><li> Support the development of measurement frameworks and actionable insight generation</li></ul>
<ul><li> Day-to-day collaboration with key vendors and stakeholders</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborate with internal team on client communications and deliverables, helping to define the story in a simple and concise way and communicate success of client’s marketing efforts</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborate on day-to-day client deliverables</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proactively propose performance improvements for clients’ marketing activities</li></ul>
<ul><li> Through compelling data visualizations, develop, maintain and communicate insights, key metrics and reports, highlighting opportunities for improvement
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Keep internal stakeholders informed about new opportunities, issues and performance succeses.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborate with DNA leadership to develop and document case studies with process and results</li></ul>
<ul><li> Support DNA leadership in in new client acquisition and new business development</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage direct reports’ careers and provide growth opportunities, coaching and training</li></ul>
<ul><li> Foster a team environment and play an active role in building teams</li></ul>
