Conduct Risk Governance and Program Manager Job Description:<br>
The Wealth and Abbot Downing Conduct Risk Governance and Program Manager is responsible for leading a team of professionals focused on ensuring front line controls are in place to comply with all enterprise level conduct related policies and procedures, regulatory requirements, and best practices. This position will serve as a subject matter expert in working with the WIM Conduct Risk Control and Reporting &amp; Analytics team to monitor and address conduct risks.<br>
Specific responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Understand and identify front line requirements of all enterprise conduct related risk management and oversight programs. This includes Sales Practices, Ethics, Complaints, Anti-Bribery and Corruption, Reputation Risk and Insider Threat.</li>
<li>In coordination with the WIM Conduct Risk Control team, ensure that well-designed and effective programs are established to meet the conduct-related enterprise requirements on a timely basis.</li>
<li>Provide subject matter expertise to senior WM &amp; AD leaders on conduct related requirements and programs</li>
<li>Monitors conduct-related issues and corrective actions to ensure heightened attention and timely resolution.</li>
<li>Advise and challenge LOB specific controls designed to detect or prevent conduct related issues</li>
<li>Work with the Reporting and Analysis team to identify additional metrics and data sources to improve reporting and provide actionable information to WM and WIM senior leadership</li>
<li>Ensuring our vision, values, goals, and behavioral expectations are embedded into WIM culture via training and education, communication and awareness, performance management &amp; incentive compensation, sales goals, and other risk culture programs.</li>
<li>Drive WIM conduct strategy; and establish and administer long-term goals for managing conduct risk; considered a subject matter expert and strategic advisor regarding interpretation of Conduct Risk Management Programs.</li>
<li>Evaluate key conduct risk and performance indicators to identify emerging trends across the enterprise, major businesses, risk domains, and the industry.</li>
<li>Promote a strong conduct risk culture and provides leadership through leaders and senior managers and their teams.</li>
</ul>
<p>
The successful candidate will work closely with other leaders across the WIM Conduct Risk, Wholesale Control organizations and the company in the development and execution of company risk management strategies. Accordingly, critical success factors will include the ability to develop partnerships with many business and functional areas, provide insight into the economic climate and related market developments and identify trends which may present opportunities or reflect gaps in the Company’s risk management execution/direction.</p>
