<h2>Salon Assistant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Sol Sunless is expanding and we are taking applications for a high-energy team member who can be trained as an Assistant Manager. Assistant Managers will help with all store operations, included but not limited to operating spray booths, educating customers about products and aftercare, obtaining customer feedback, keep track of store inventory, train and encourage other staff members to assist in hitting a store sales goal! We are looking for upbeat, SALES DRIVEN rock stars who have a passion for helping others.</p>
<p>At Sol, we value the motivation and energy that an upbeat atmosphere provides it's employees so we provide our staff with newly renovated, modern stores that are fully equipped with state-of-the-art technology, upbeat music and amazing co-workers!!</p>
<p>Our ideal candidate is someone outgoing who has previous assistant managerial customer service and sales experience. He/she has superior listening skills to put any apprehensive first-time sprayers at ease, can give or receive accurate preparation, aftercare and maintenance information and properly execute the following tasks:</p>
<p><b>DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Greet guests upon arrival for their appointments</li>
<li>Oversee the education and execution of color consultations with first time guests</li>
<li>Give instructions on how to use our spray booths and other sunless equipment to new staff members and guests</li>
<li>Provide product knowledge training to new employees and give guests alternative suggestions for products, services, etc.</li>
<li>Receive guest feedback and make necessary adjustments</li>
<li>Post fun and informative social media updates on your store's accounts on a daily basis</li>
<li>Maintain a clean and tidy work atmosphere at all times</li>
<li>Deliver service, product and sales information to guests and employees</li>
<li>Follows proper sales and customer service etiquette</li>
<li>Trains or assists with training new staff members</li>
<li>Successful multitasking ability is a <b>must</b>
</li>
<li>Make updates to guest's personal information through computer software</li>
<li>Assist headquarters in helping your store reach it's sales goal</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE &amp; REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be reliable and always on time to work (15 mins early)</li>
<li>Previous assistant managerial experience is desired</li>
<li>Previous customer service and sales experience is desired</li>
<li>Knowledge of personal computers and scheduling softwares</li>
<li>Skills in dealing diplomatically with guests in stressful situations</li>
<li>Previous salon-related experience desired</li>
<li>Previous sunless tanning and/or skin care experience is a plus</li>
<li>Quick learner and superior attention to detail</li>
<li>Must be able to multi-task efficiently and correctly</li>
</ul>

</ul>
<p>You may be asked to attend some training shifts at some of our other locations with other managerial staff, so the ideal candidate must have reliable transportation.</p>
