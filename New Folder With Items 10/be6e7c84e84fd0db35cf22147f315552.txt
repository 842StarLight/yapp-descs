<h2>Automotive General Sales Manager - Suffolk County Location</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Atlantic Autogroup </b>is looking for a General Sales Manager who wants to shape a rewarding future with a growing leader in the automotive industry in one of our Suffolk County dealership.<br>
<br>
As the leading auto group on Long Island, our goal is to ensure that every customer, professional staff and community member feel like part of our family. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction is unmatched.<br>
<br>
Requirements<br>
<br>
You must have at least 2 years of proven auto sales management experience.<br>
Your people skills must be strong enough to be able to lead, train, manage, and direct your sales team so that they can meet goals and quotas.<br>
You must be able to combine customer service skills with a strong close.<br>
Your computer skills must also be strong, and you know your way around our CRM system.<br>
You must have a good grasp on Internet processes and know how to evaluate the effectiveness of your advertising budget.<br>
You'll need to have a good knowledge of fixed operations and other dealership departments in order to use our processes to expedite delivery and promote strong CSI.<br>
Reynolds &amp; Reynolds experience is a big plus!<br>
We'd also like you to have a comfort level with portfolio management tools such as Xtream or Auto Alert.<br>
Accountability is a must!<br>
<br>
This is a high power-position in high volume store so we are offering an excellent compensation package with benefits that commensurate with experience.<br>
<br>
Our goal is to hire the very best so you will be required to pass a background, DMV check and a drug test. Full-time employees are eligible for medical benefits after 90 days of employment.<br>
</p>
