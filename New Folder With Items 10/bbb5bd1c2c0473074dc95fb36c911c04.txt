<h2>Customer Svcs Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>This position exists to work effectively with the rich network of internal and external staff, partners and park users to effectively oversee customer service at the Thomas C. Savage Visitor Center at Fort Snelling State Park. This position plays a key role in the daily operations of the Visitor Center to enhance support for park staff and provide exceptional customer experience. At the confluence of the Mississippi and Minnesota Rivers, Fort Snelling is itself a diverse hub that brings together abundant elements of natural, cultural, historical and future significance. Efforts will focus on providing staff support, enhancing visitor experience by providing information and retail merchandise as well as assisting with meeting room reservations and daily operations at the Thomas C. Savage Visitor Center .</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li><p>Math skills sufficient to handle sales transactions and provide accurate change to customers, and count and reconcile daily revenue receipts.</p></li>
<li><p>Knowledge of point of sale software sufficient to process merchandise sales.</p></li>
<li><p>Customer service skills sufficient to remain courteous, helpful, tactful, effective, and professional in manner, while providing information to the public and other internal and external clientele.</p></li>
<li><p>Organizational and interpersonal skills sufficient to work as a productive member of a team.</p></li>
<li><p>Conflict resolution/human relation skills sufficient to negotiate, resolve issues, read situations and settle disputes equitably by finding common ground and cooperation.</p></li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>One year of retail experience.</li>
<li>One year of experience managing or leading a Visitor or Welcome Center in a recreational setting.</li>
<li>Two years' experience working with computer based programs (spreadsheets and word processing programs) and media equipment (power point and other digital media programs).</li>
<li>One year of experience hosting or setting up group meetings or events.</li>
<li>Experience in a lead worker role, helping to monitor or direct other staff members.</li>
<li>Understanding or knowledge of the Dakota cultural history at Fort Snelling State Park.</li>
<li>One to two years' experience working with partner groups.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Minnesota State Parks and Trails.</li>
<li>Inventory control procedures including, ordering of product, stocking merchandise, and developing marketing plans and new product selection.</li>
</ul>

Physical Requirements</b></h2>
