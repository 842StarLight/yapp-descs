<h2>Implementation & Delivery Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>FrogData (www.frogdata.com) is an Auto Dealer Analytics Platform designed for Dealer Principals, General Managers, and CFOs. Our platform delivers Advanced Reporting, Business Insights, and Predictive Analytics for Auto Dealers, helping them make better decisions that have positive impact on their business.</p>
<p>We are part of a diversified automotive solutions company with offices around the world, servicing thousands of dealerships worldwide. We intend to establish FrogData as a dominant player in the analytics market for the automotive industry and are investing heavily in technology and people.</p>
<p>We are rapidly expanding and are building a team of innovators and leaders. We are looking for dynamic professionals to be a part of our Implementation &amp; Delivery Team.
</p>
<p>Implementation &amp; Delivery Specialist
</p>
<p>This is a key position responsible for implementation &amp; delivery of the FrogData platform. The process for implementation requires data extraction from client DMS or CRM systems and working with internal teams to deliver the final analytics to clients.
</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive the sales process from initial inquiry to close of sale
</li>
<li>Develop and communicate strategic goals for the implementation and support of FrogData’s customers
</li>
<li>Architect implementation policies and procedures to increase efficiency and reduce errors
</li>
<li>Assist in data gathering, report writing and documentation required for data analysis by the development team
</li>
<li>Assist sales team members to present the analytics to clients and provide ongoing consulting support
</li>
<li>Provide analyses to identify opportunities and explain trends, work with business owners to help them identify areas of optimization
</li>
<li>Create monthly reports for each client/ business owner with emphasis on specific areas of business process improvement
</li>
<li>Identify and implement procedures that increase productivity and enhance performance of our customers</li>
<li>Coordinate with internal and external stakeholders on solution implementation
</li>
<li>Be a product expert and provide customer training as a part of the implementation process</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience &amp; Qualifications</p>
<p>Must have experience in implementation of complex SaaS solutions preferably in the automotive sector
</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of Auto Dealer DMS Systems. Prior experience in similar systems is preferred.</li>
<li>Must have automotive retail experience, specifically service management and shop management
</li>
<li>Experience in using Excel, Tableau and/ or other BI tools
</li>
<li>Experience in working with large quantities of raw, disorganized data
</li>
<li>Highly analytical mindset – ability to solve problems
</li>
<li>Exceptional communicator with superior presentation skills and a proven ability to build strong relationships (both internal &amp; external) across all levels of an organization
</li>
<li>Extensive experience overseeing technical processes is strongly preferred
</li>
<li>Experience working in a fast paced, high growth environment
</li>
<li>8+ years of experience in a client/customer facing role
</li>
</ul>
<p>We offer a industry standard base compensation with attractive commission plan along with health and other benefits.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time<br>
Required education: Bachelor's degree
<br>
Field Sales: 5 years</p>
