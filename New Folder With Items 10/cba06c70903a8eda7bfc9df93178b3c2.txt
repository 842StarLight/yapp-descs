<div><p><b>
JOB SUMMARY</b></p></div>
<div><div>
<br>
</div></div>
<br>
</div>
<p>
The Customer Service Manager is responsible for the processes and systems related to the servicing customers and support of sales staff. They will take orders, probe and influence customers to enhance sales of value added products during the order taking process.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Receives calls and places client orders.</li>
<li>
Sells the company and sells value added products.</li>
<li>
Probes clients for rates, maximum quantity and new business.</li>
<li>
Responsible for on-time delivery (avoids over-booking).</li>
<li>
Explain company policies; ability to say “No” to clients.</li>
<li>
Ability to get detailed information form clients including directions to job and any special instructions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma</li>
<li>
Minimum 2 years of sales and operations experience</li>
<li>
Ability to interact and clearly communicate with internal and external personnel, customers and supporting agencies.</li>
<li>
Detail-oriented</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal, analytical and computer skills.</li>
<li>
Proactive, self-motivated, organized and team focused</li>
<li>
Ability to be flexible</li>
</ul>
