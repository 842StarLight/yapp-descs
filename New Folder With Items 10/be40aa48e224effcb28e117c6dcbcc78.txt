<h2>Sr Director, Business Development - Drug Development Software</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Certara is hiring a Sr. Director, Business Development to support our Pharmacometrics Software business!</b> <b>This position will be based in our Princeton, NJ or Raleigh, NC office.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Certara’s pharmacometric drug development and informatics software portfolio is the most wide-ranging in the industry and used across the drug development life cycle. Pharmacologists, toxicologists, biostatisticians, pre-clinical scientists, chemists, geneticists and other scientists use our software to inform key safety and efficacy decisions, including target identification and optimization, first-in human dose and dosing regimen, trial design, target validation, compound triaging, comparative efficacy, and mechanistic drug performance.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>The Senior Director, Business Development is responsible for managing all aspect of the sales and business development function for the Certara Pharmacometrics Software (PMX-SW) business. This “player-coach” role is responsible for managing the existing global field and inside sales team. The incumbent will need to understand our client’s critical decisions and challenges and financial position, and then determine how Certara’s PMX-SW’s products will fit their requirements. The Senior Director, Business Development will need to understand our client business models, development program focus, and corporate objectives at a strategic level so they can strategically pursue business with new and existing clients.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>**We would prefer to hire a candidate that local to our Princeton, NJ or Raleigh, NC offices, but will also consider remote based candidates.**</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Contributes to overall PMX-SW business performance as part of the PMX-SW Leadership team.</li>
<li>Develops and leads business development initiatives with senior level business, clinical, R&amp;D and IT leadership in Biopharma companies.</li>
<li>Works closely with marketing and Product Management to provide customer and market feedback that drives overall product and marketing strategies.</li>
<li>Establishes clear expectations and priorities for the overall Business Development team.</li>
<li>Develops and executes individualized staff development plans for the sales team members.</li>
<li>Develop an understanding of Certara products and services to successfully deliver our value propositions to prospects and existing clients.</li>
<li>Provide clear direction, and actively communicate with sales team and other stakeholders within PMX-SW and across broader organization.</li>
<li>Qualify companies and contacts to understand the customer’s key needs/challenges through identification, discovery, planning and account/customer qualifications.</li>
<li>Document communication/discussions and all opportunity details in SalesForce.com to ensure a tracked record of existing correspondence and/or future next steps required.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain relationships with new and existing clients, clinical decision makers, PhD scientists, IT and senior management teams in pre-clinical, DMPK, clinical, and pharmacometricians.</li>
<li>Gather market intelligence as input to product management and product marketing to improve offerings and communication for local market needs.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with cross functional commercialization teams with a strong business orientation and ability to view issues from multiple functional perspectives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of a Bachelor’s Degree in a science related curriculum, advanced degree a plus with a demonstrated successful career path in Business Development.</li>
<li>10 years of combined business development experience in biopharmaceutical space.</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of experience managing a global sales team with multiple channels.</li>
<li>History of successfully representing software or software/services providers to the drug development space, from preclinical through Phase 4.</li>
<li>Experience selling to a variety of biopharma firms – Tier 1 to Tier 4 biopharma and CRO is preferred.</li>
<li>Proven experience in identifying, evaluating and pursuing business development opportunities within new and existing key markets for self and for team.</li>
<li>Understanding of software sales and the business implications of different models, including enterprise, SaaS/cloud.</li>
<li>A proven history of meeting or exceeding quotas, both for self and for the team.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of SalesForce.com and other sales/marketing software tools for managing a sales team against target metrics.</li>
<li>An understanding of the role of Clinical Pharmacology and Model Informed Drug Development and software tools in the drug development process is preferred, it is expected this will develop over time.</li>
<li>Understanding of pharmaceutical software and service organizations and their revenue drivers is strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Experience developing sales strategies, constructing sales territories and allocating key accounts in a logical fashion.</li>
<li>Experience designing and managing commission programs.</li>
</ul>
