<h2>Client Relationship Representative - The Center for Corporate and Professional Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<p>To conduct all sales activities, from lead generation through close, in an assigned territory. Work within the sales and support teams for the achievement of customer satisfaction, revenue generation, and long-term account goals consistent with assigned areas' vision and values. Reports to Designated Supervisor.</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Additional Basic Function – if applicable:</b></p>
</div>
<div> The Client Relationship Representative, a self-supporting position that requires entrepreneurial drive, is responsible for prospecting, lead generation, client relationship management, consultative selling, proposal development, closing the sale and contracting, along with overseeing training delivery logistics for each contract sold.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Examples of Duties:</b></p>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Duties/essential functions may include, but not be limited to, the following:</b></p>
<p>
Present and sell products/services to current and potential clients.<br>
<br>
Prepare action plans and schedules to identify specific targets and to project the number of contacts to be made; develop a database of qualified leads through referrals, telephone canvassing, face-to-face calling on business owners, direct mail, email, and networking.<br>
<br>
Follow up on new leads and referrals resulting from field activity.<br>
<br>
Oversee marketing budget, assist with the development and implementation of strategic marketing plan.<br>
<br>
Prepare a variety of status reports, including activity, closings, follow-up, and adherence to goals.<br>
<br>
Gather market and customer information to gain a clear understanding of customers' businesses and requirements.<br>
<br>
Collaborate with members of the sales and support staff to ensure goal attainment and success.<br>
<br>
Develop and make presentations to potential clients.<br>
<br>
Prepare sales proposals and lead sales meetings with clients.<br>
<br>
Prepare sales contracts; negotiate and secure agreements/contracts with clients.<br>
<br>
Maintain sales materials and current product knowledge.<br>
<br>
Serve on department, division, and University committees; participate in various professional, civic, community, or University organizations to further enhance visibility and marketing efforts.<br>
<br>
Provide functional guidance of administrative/professional employees and/or direction supervision of support staff on an ongoing basis. May provide ongoing facilitation of consecutive projects.<br>
<br>
Perform related duties as assigned.<br>
<br>
May be responsible for attending and participating in client events.</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Additional Examples of Duties – if applicable:</b></p>
</div>
<div> In-person sales and consulting meetings with HR leaders and others.
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
</div>
<div>
<p>Bachelor's degree in Marketing or relevant field. Two to three years experience in marketing or sales. A minimum of one year of experience in professional business presentations.</p>
<p><b>
License/Certification:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Knowledge Of:</b></p>
<p>
Advertising and sales promotion techniques<br>
Desktop office computing applications</p>
<p><b>
Skill In:</b></p>
<p>
Written and verbal communication with clients and sales presentations<br>
Customer relations<br>
Basic bookkeeping or accounting/budgeting</p>
<p><b>
Ability To:</b></p>
<p>
Persuade and influence others<br>
Develop and deliver effective presentations<br>
Create and edit written materials<br>
Set priorities, analyze problems, and offer solutions<br>
Provide a positive company image to the public<br>
Ensure a high quality of customer service and follow-up actions<br>
Travel locally to current and potential clients<br>
Resolve problems independently and make decisions<br>
Work non-traditional hours<br>
Establish and maintain cooperative and effective working relationships with others<br>
Remain current regarding trends in the field</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications – if applicable:</b></p>
</div>
<div>
<p>Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Training and Development, Adult Education, Marketing or related discipline, plus two to three years in business to business sales. Experience in talent development, adult learning or employee training and development and strong interpersonal skills, writing and organization skills desired. The demonstrated ability to develop prospect lists, generate leads, hold professional client meetings and close sales is also desired. Employee will be expected to travel throughout Northeast Ohio to meet with potential clients and manage program logistics, as well as work non-traditional hours.</p>
</div>
