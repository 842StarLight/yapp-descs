<h2>Project / Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Open Systems International, Inc. (OSI) www.osii.com is a fast-paced, growing, high technology company, headquartered in Medina, Minnesota. As a world leader in the Operations Technology (OT) field, OSI develops and supplies state-of-the-art energy management, optimization and control software solutions to energy utility companies worldwide.
</p>
<p>In this position you will be responsible for project execution, project profitability and customer satisfaction. You will manage complex phases of large projects. More senior positions will act as a resource to OSI Executive Management on corporate, strategic, organizational and tactical project matters. This role is a key part of our business unit and project delivery function with significant impact on successful project execution, profitability and customer satisfaction. As a Project and/or Program Manager you will have the following responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and/or oversee the development of project plans and schedules.</li>
<li>Manage the contract change process as needed.</li>
<li>Coordinate project implementation plans.</li>
<li>Ensure that projects are properly tested, approved and documented</li>
<li>Set the strategy for projects based on the business unit's priorities.</li>
<li>Lead and coach assigned project managers.</li>
<li>Manage and determine timelines, priorities and resources for the project.</li>
<li>Assist in setting up partnerships and alliances with vendors.</li>
<li>Participate in sales demos, customer meetings, trade shows and industry conferences.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Minimum</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's or Master's degree, or equivalent experience, in Business, Finance/Accounting, Electrical/Computer Engineering or related technical field.</li>
<li>3-7 years of project management experience for entry level positions and 10+ years of project management experience, managing projects of $5 million or more for senior and/or director level positions.</li>
<li>Ability to deal with highly technical and complex projects.</li>
<li>Business leadership, coaching and mentoring skills.</li>
<li>Experience with PERT/CPM and Microsoft Project.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Preferred</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Electric utility industry and/or automation/SCADA project experience.</li>
<li>An Engineering degree and experience with electrical utilities or automation systems.</li>
<li>International project management and solid understanding of international cultures and business transactions.</li>
</ul>
