<h2>Community Director - Lease-Up</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SUMMARY</b></h1>
<div>Responsible for the day-to-day operations of a brand new garden style multifamily lease up. Build a strong team(s) with common goals and objectives. Manages residential real estate properties for clients by performing the following duties personally or through subordinate employees. Duties include participation and oversight of budgeting, workplace safety, financial reporting and tracking, marketing, operations, and a property’s overall performance as a real estate asset.</div>
<div></div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>JOB DUTIES</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>By working with the construction/development team, you will be responsible for effectively managing the receiving of the units from construction.</li>
<li>Ensure that all punch list items are taken care of prior to acceptance.</li>
<li>Inspect (personally) each building at least once each week. Maintain daily records of inspections, and provide follow-up supervision to ensure corrective action is taken as needed. Inspection areas include, but are not limited to, all buildings, grounds, and other common and public areas to insure that employees are performing their assigned duties.</li>
<li>Inspect vacated apartments and decide the extent of turnover repairs/redecorating that will be required.</li>
<li>Inspect and approve vacant apartments that are ready for move in.</li>
<li>Monitor stock levels and project supplies and replacement items required for property operations and building maintenance in coordination with the service manager.</li>
<li>Ensure common areas (e.g. halls, stairways, community room, grounds, play grounds, etc.) clean and in good repair.</li>
<li>Repair unsafe items immediately, or mark so as to ensure no person is injured by the item.</li>
<li>Monitor work of contractors and report findings/concerns to the Regional Manager.</li>
<li>Continuously be aware of safety practices and procedures; Conduct monthly Safety Meetings with all staff members.</li>
<li>Assure that lease or rental agreements are being properly completed and renewed.</li>
<li>Ensure the Company’s policy regarding Fair Housing is understood and followed by all employees and contractors and that necessary Fair Housing notices are posted as required by local, state and federal regulations.</li>
<li>Assists with eviction of tenants in compliance with court order and directions from Attorneys and Regional Manager.</li>
<li>Assure that all offices are kept clean and neat.</li>
<li>Assure that equipment is kept in proper working condition and repairs are made as necessary. Replace equipment as necessary as coordinated and approved with the Regional Manager.</li>
<li>Assure that policy and procedure changes are communicated to the employees in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Assure that procedure manuals and instructional type memoranda are kept orderly and readily available to staff members.</li>
<li>Follow emergency procedures as outlined in Company manuals and policy guidelines. Notify next level of management and proper authorities immediately of emergencies (e.g. fire).</li>
<li>Immediately report claims concerning property or liability insurance as required by policy.</li>
</ul>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>EMPLOYEE RELATIONS</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Hire and train the best employees in the industry.</li>
<li>Supervise all on-site personnel (either directly or indirectly.)</li>
<li>Provide staff with leadership and supervision which motivates employees to effectively perform.</li>
<li>Constantly coach and mentor employees on individual and community goals.</li>
<li>Provide new employees with a thorough introduction of the community and the Company so that they understand what is expected of them, the importance of their contribution to the community, how they fit into the team, and the importance of working safely.</li>
<li>Ensure employees attend training classes.</li>
<li>Review employee performance and schedule training as needed.</li>
<li>Make recommendations for the advancement and promotion of above average employees.</li>
<li>Make recommendations for the termination of non-qualified employees in coordination with the Regional Manager and the VP of Human Resources.</li>
<li>Ensure employees understand the content and follow the Company’s Employee Handbook.</li>
<li>Report all on the job employee injuries to the HR department at the Main Office the same day that they occur.</li>
<li>Assure that employee bulletin board(s) are orderly and up-to-date and all necessary federal and state posters and Company notices are posted for employee information.</li>
</ul>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>MARKETING</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Oversee the daily marketing/sales/customer service effort to ensure maximum potential is reached.</li>
<li>Have proper “tools” available for the marketing effort (floor plans, area information, transportation schedules, etc.)</li>
<li>Ensure vacant units are clean and in market ready condition at all times.</li>
<li>Review first impression items monthly, e.g., entrance signs, office, professional attire, and model appearance to ensure a positive impression.</li>
<li>Review shopping reports with Office staff and provides guidance on how to improve.</li>
<li>Participate in the development of the advertising plan for the community.</li>
<li>Review and analyze market survey information and make pricing recommendations.</li>
</ul>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>RESIDENT RELATIONS</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Insure active resident relations programs are in place.</li>
<li>Aggressively pursue resident retention.</li>
<li>Ensure staff is providing superior customer service to all residents.</li>
<li>Ensure that someone on the management team meets all new residents prior to, or at, occupancy.</li>
<li>Interview residents who present challenges/problems that the Assistant Community Director or other staff employees cannot resolve and attempt to resolve them and/or make recommendations to the Regional Manager on how the matter can best be resolved.</li>
<li>Assure prompt and positive action on all resident complaints.</li>
<li>Continually stress customer service and courtesy by all staff members in their contact with residents and prospective residents.</li>
<li>Assure proper dress, neatness and personal cleanliness by the entire staff is maintained at all times, (e.g. uniforms for grounds/maintenance, proper career apparel for office employees).</li>
<li>Follow-up with residents after maintenance has been performed through phone calls, personal notes, etc.</li>
<li>Communicate with your residents about community news.</li>
</ul>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>FINANCIAL PROCEDURES</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate an ability to understand financial goals, operate asset in owners’ best interest in accordance with the Policies &amp; Procedures Manual.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate records of all community transactions and submit on timely basis (i.e., rent rolls, delinquency reports, move-in/move-outs, etc.).</li>
<li>Prepare annual budgets and income projections in a timely and accurate manner.</li>
<li>Ensure that all rents and late fees/check charges are collected, posted and deposited in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Generate necessary legal action, documents and process in accordance with State and Company guidelines.</li>
<li>Provide constant vendor/contractor communications concerning scheduling, billing, vendor relations and certificates of insurance.</li>
<li>Ensure that A/P invoices are submitted to the corporate office for payment, handle petty cash and all funds.</li>
<li>Become thoroughly familiar with all accounting procedures.</li>
</ul>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></h1>
<div>Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with Company policies and applicable laws. Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, interviewing, hiring, and training employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; coaching, rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving problems and issues.</div>

<div>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Degree preferred, but will consider CAM/IREM certification in place of a degree. Combination of education and experience may be considered.</li>
<li>Must have at least five years’ experience in the multi-family industry working with new lease-ups; preferably in a leadership position.</li>
<li>Must have at least two years’ experience managing and leading a team of multi-professionals.</li>
<li>Must have a valid state-issued driver’s license.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
