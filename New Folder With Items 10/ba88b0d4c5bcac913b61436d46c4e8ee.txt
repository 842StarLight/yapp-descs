<p><b>Pregis Job Posting </b></p>
<p><b>Position: Sussex Electro-Mechanical Technician</b></p>
<p><b>ABOUT THE POSITION: </b></p>
<p>Pregis LLC is a leading provider of innovative protective packaging materials and systems. Headquartered in Chicago and operate multiple North American manufacturing and warehousing facilities devoted to protective packaging solutions.</p>
<p>Exceptional team of packaging professionals are trained to help customers select the right protective packaging solutions for specific application requirements. Products and systems can be found in virtually every consumer and industrial segment and are supported by a comprehensive sale, customer service, technical service and distribution network. Pregis’ customer roster includes everyone from well-known brands to emerging start-ups.</p>
<p>At Pregis, we believe in continued investment in protective packaging innovation to develop solutions that help meet or exceed customer’s expectations. They take a consultative approach to every project they undertake so they can help their customers understand financial impact, as well as performance deliverables.</p>
<p><b>Role Purpose: </b></p>
<p>Researches, develops, designs, and tests electrical components, equipment, and systems by performing the following duties.</p>
<p>Listed below are the essential functions and requirements of this role:</p>
<p><b>JOB FUNCTIONS</b>:</p>
<p>1. Willingness to assist Manufacturing in solving issues that arise during the build process.</p>
<p>2. Assists, supports, and trains other members of the Machinery Division group in solving complex diagnostic problems.</p>
<p>3. A good understanding of basic electro-mechanical systems.</p>
<p>4. Able to troubleshoot electrical systems at the signal level.</p>
<p>5. Able to maintain and keep an orderly laboratory while setting up experiments.</p>
<p>6. Assist in designing test systems to investigate new controls and examine existing controls.</p>
<p>7. Gather and manipulate appropriate data from test system and present the relevant information.</p>
<p>8. Able to work with engineers in the design and set up of experiments.</p>
<p>9. Work with technical writers to ensure work instructions are readable and accurate.</p>
<p>10. Ability to assemble mechanical systems from 2-D and 3-D drawings.</p>
<p>11. Ability to wire cabinets from schematic drawings.</p>
<p>12. Good understanding of machine control logic and the systems that are used to execute machine control logic.</p>
<p>13. Helps develop new products using the latest technologies.</p>
<p>14. Works with field engineers to document design flaws and deficiencies.</p>
<p>15. Willingness to learn new software applications to enhance engineering’s ability to gather and present data.</p>
<p>16. Requires superior interpersonal and communication skills and the ability to develop and maintain good working relationships with employees, staff, management, etc.</p>
<p>17. Must possess superior organizational skills and be able to prioritize and work simultaneously on several projects.</p>
<p>18. Ability to operate within a team atmosphere.</p>
<p><b><i>Collaborative Skills</i></b></p>
<p> Works well within a small team to solve collective problems.</p>
<p> Proactively reaches out across departments to collaborate.</p>
<p> Encourages teamwork with all department and/or facility members.</p>
<p> Communicates well with fellow department and/or facility members.</p>
<p> Accepts training readily, quickly understands new concepts.</p>
<p> Shows willingness to offer training to fellow department members.</p>
<p> Performs a task with limited supervision and may require that you provide guidance to fellow employees.</p>
<p> Demonstrate the ability to work on a team to achieve departmental results.</p>
<p><b>Workforce Responsibility: </b><br>Reports to: Director of Engineering</p>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<p><b>Education / Experience: </b></p>
<p>Associate degree in engineering technology or five or more years of experience. Must have a working knowledge of Microsoft Windows operating systems and associated Microsoft Office Applications.</p>
<p>Overtime Requirements: Overtime as needed.</p>
<p><b>Language Skills: </b></p>
<p>Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintaining instructions and procedure manuals. Ability to write routine reports and correspondence. Ability to speak effectively before customers and have good interpersonal skills.</p>
<p><b>Mathematical Skills: </b></p>
<p>Ability to compute electrical engineering design calculations.</p>
<p><b>Reasoning Ability: </b></p>
<p>Ability to design new and complicated electrical assemblies along with PLC programs with a minimal of supervision. Ability to use logical thinking to be successful in these projects.</p>
<p><b>Additional Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>Walking and standing on cement floors.<br>Lifting up to 100 lbs. clean environment, moderate noise.</p>
