<h2>Account Manager (Columbus, NE)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Account Manager
</b></p>
<p>Columbus, NE</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b> Responsible for achieving sales objectives through the advance sale of products/brands and packages to Large and Small store customers. Responsible for performing product resets, rack/display placements as designated by established plan-o-grams. Management of all point-of-sale inventory for promotional activity.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Executes and closes all sales calls for assigned sales routes.</li>
<li>Maintains and sells incremental displays, as needed; and secures incremental equipment placements.</li>
<li>Sells in promotional programs and ensures dealer compliance with mandated Customer Value Promotions.</li>
<li>In connection with a sales call, maintains appropriate inventory levels, company assets and point of sale.</li>
<li>Ensures account meets Company merchandising standards.</li>
<li>Transports, replaces and maintains Point of Sale advertising as appropriate for account.</li>
<li>Receive requests for generic and local Point of Sales (POS) and rack orders</li>
<li>Receive POS, racks and coupons in facility and secure appropriately and according to internal audit standards.</li>
<li>Coordinate area-wide rack orders to minimize freight costs</li>
<li>Work in teams to perform resets of assigned stores and ensure that package, brand, and promotional goals are reflected in store sets.</li>
<li>Ability to direct the work activities of other team members that may consist of co-workers and/or persons from different beverage companies in the implementation of the reset activity.</li>
<li>Provide adequate communication to the customer on the changes to the store as a result of the reset activity.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School or GED (General Education Diploma) - Required</li>
<li>Bachelor Degree - Preferred</li>
<li>1+ years of general work experience.</li>
<li>1+ years of previous sales experience. Food &amp; Beverage/Consumer Goods Products industry experience a plus.</li>
<li>Valid Driver’s License</li>
<li>Clean driving record</li>
<li>Must be able to push/pull/lift up to 50+ pounds</li>
<li>Must be able to bend, kneel and climb periodically</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>SKILLS &amp; ABILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communication Skills</li>
<li>Dexterity Skills</li>
<li>Problem Solving</li>
<li>Interpersonal Skills</li>
<li>Must be able to read, speak, and write in English to be able to communicate with customers, coworkers, and supervisor.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>WORK SCHEDULE: Schedule – 7am – 5pm. 5-day workweek.<br>
</b></p>
