<h2>IT Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>We are an MSP Mentor Top 100 company looking for an experienced <b>IT Project Manager </b>to join our team. The successful candidate is a disciplined, self-motivated PM who enjoys working with people as much as he or she enjoys managing projects.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>IT Project Manager tasks and responsibilities</b></div>
<ul><li>PMs are responsible for all areas of Project Management throughout a project lifecycle
<ul>
<li>Initiation/Presales:
<ul>
<li>Presales responsibilities shared with SE/TAM role. A PM's part in the Initiation phase of a project is to assist the SE/TAM, in varying degrees, with: Needs Assessment, Design, Scope Definition (high level), Assumption Definition, Exception Definition, Risk Identification.</li>
<li>While planning is primarily the PMs job, PM also shares some planning responsibilities prior to sale with SE/TAM for high level task breakdown and task effort estimation, top-down.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Post-sale, PMs perform all aspects Project Planning and Management required; Schedule, Resources, Communication, Scope, Budget, Procurement, Risk, Change, Quality</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Actively participates in establishing the company's product and solution best practices.</li>
<li>Must maintain a solid understanding of all other department process, how their resources are used during a project, and what impact the project may have on those departments.</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate effectively, and in order to do so, the PM:
<ul>
<li>Must be confident in Project Management practices, well written, and well spoken</li>
<li>Must fully understand the business need and business goal of every project</li>
<li>Must fully understand the deliverables of every project</li>
<li>Must be familiar with the technology or solution involved</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Ensures organizational best practices have been met and approved for project documentation, technical configurations, and Managed Services solution configurations.</li>
<li>Must be driven to continually self-improve by attending Project Management trainings and/or seminars and any other required technology or solution trainings as required.</li>
<li>The position requires visits and in-person interaction with our clients. Travel is primarily within Eastern Massachusetts.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Required Skills &amp; Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years experience as a Project Manager in IT related fields</li>
<li>Impeccable written and oral communications skills</li>
<li>Bachelor Degree or equivalent certifications in related studies</li>
<li>A general understanding of the following is required:
<ul>
<li>Local Area Network</li>
<li>Windows Active Directory environments</li>
<li>Wide Area Network</li>
<li>Virtualization, and high availability solution</li>
<li>VDI and application virtualization (Citrix and RDS environments)</li>
<li>Wireless and Networking infrastructure</li>
<li>Microsoft Office 365 products and services, Azure services and solutions, and other cloud technologies</li>
<li>Mesaging and communication solutions, primarily Microsoft Exchange</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>The ideal candidate uses experience, knowledge, and management tools and techniques to effectively manage a project and fulfill the project objectives and requirements. Effective project managers require a balance of technical, interpersonal, and conceptual skills that help them analyze solutions and interact appropriately.</li>
<li>Must show leadership and team building qualities, and with this, be able to guide their project team towards achieving the project objectives.</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite products including Excel, Word, Outlook</li>
<li>Familiar with MS Project, PowerPoint, Visio, OneNote</li>
<li>Familiarity with PMBOK is a preferred</li>
<li>Familiarity with ConnectWise, and Kaseya solutions is a plus</li>
<li>Some experience as a network engineer or technical sales engineer is a plus</li>
</ul>
