<h2>Commission Only Sales Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Commission based advertising sales position supporting DigitalEffex Web Hosting &amp; Design, a web hosting and design firm, serving the rapidly growing small business community. The ideal candidate will possess a high energy level, outgoing personality and be results oriented, with demonstrated success in advertising sales. (Experience with small business owners, web hosting and design, and advertising is a plus)</p>
<p><b>CORE FUNCTIONS: </b></p>
<p>Promotes/sells/secures orders from existing and prospective customers through a relationship-based approach.</p>
<p>Demonstrates web design and hosting packages and services to existing/potential customers and assists them in selecting those best suited to their needs.</p>
<p><b>DETAILS OF FUNCTION: </b></p>
<p>1 or more year of successful outside commission based sales experience</p>
<p>Skilled at cold calling and securing sales from small business accounts.</p>
<p>Capable of seeking out and participating in local networking events</p>
<p>Identifies prospects, customers, and referral sources, while developing and maintaining excellent relationships.</p>
<p>Strong interpersonal and communication skills (oral, written, presentation)</p>
<p>Must be highly organized and self motivated</p>
<p>Positive and energetic attitude</p>
<p>Knowledge of Word, Outlook, Excel</p>
<p>Must be willing to work as an independent contractor.</p>
<p>High level of English skill required to contact existing customer base.Initial and on-going sales material and training are provided as needed.</p>
<p>This is a commission only based position. Hours are flexible. For each confirmed client, we will pay: 10% paid on design fee for completed website design projects. Projects can range in price from $750 to $5,000.</p>
<p><b>REPORTING</b> :</p>
<p>* Reports directly to the CEO</p>
<p>Please submit a resume and 3 references to apply. Make sure you let me know your experience, how are you planning to do this job, what you can bring to the table and jobs you have done in the past.</p>
<p><b>Required experience: </b></p>
<p>Closing sales, cold calling, strong interpersonal communication, website design and development: 1 year</p>
