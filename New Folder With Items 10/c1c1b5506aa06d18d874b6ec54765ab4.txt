<p><b>Who we are</b></p>
<p>
Dude Solutions is a leading provider of cloud based operations management software to optimize facilities, assets, and workflow. We empower our clients and our people, combining innovative technology with operational insight to transform the places we learn, live, heal, work, and play. We’re both proud and humbled to play a role in the daily operations of so many clients, and we look forward to continually drive innovation, efficiency and safety throughout the markets we serve.</p>
<p><b>
About the job</b></p>
<p>
As the Senior Director of Sales for the Sales Development Team you will be instrumental in building and executing the strategy for this newly formed team. In this role, you be responsible for leading our Sales Development Representative team with the charter of driving new opportunities into our sales pipeline. This position requires someone with strong leadership skills who can develop the team to reach their goals and meet their pipeline development quotas.</p>
<p><b>
What you’ll be doing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Achieve and exceed monthly SDR team opportunity, pipeline quotas and all activities of lead generation</li>
<li>
Lead and develop our SDR management team and overall SDR teams including target setting, training and skill development, one on one frameworks, call coaching, workshop leadership and daily motivation</li>
<li>
Build trust and credibility with internal sales leaders and business partners through regular interaction that is evidenced by feedback and proven working partnerships and results.</li>
<li>
Work with the Marketing department on the design and execution of successful demand generation campaigns.</li>
<li>
Deliver monthly business reviews through tracking program results, measuring program success, ROI, and key reporting metrics.</li>
<li>
Dive in and get your hands dirty by guiding SDR work, presenting to prospects, supporting in hiring or whatever may arise to push the team forward</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you need</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of leadership experience scaling, managing, coaching and training an inside sales leadership team</li>
<li>
8+ years of successful B2Bsales experience, preferably SaaS and/or subscription-based business sales models</li>
<li>
Must have experience managing a team to a sales quota</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree preferred, if no degree 5 years leadership experience and 10 years strategic sales experience</li>
<li>
Remarkable and consistent track record of sales success</li>
<li>
Ability to demonstrate strong leadership skills with the ability to develop and motivate</li>
<li>
Understanding of the difference between a coach and a manager</li>
<li>
Experience working in a fast paced sales sales environment</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Salesforce, Salesloft experience is a plus</li>
</ul>
