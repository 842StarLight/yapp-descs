<p><b>About ikeGPS:</b></p>
<p>The United States and Canada are in the middle of a communications boom. The drive for more bandwidth is fuelling amazing growth in the deployment of fiber and wireless networks. ikeGPS is changing the way communication companies like AT&amp;T, Verizon and Crown Castle deploy fiber and wireless networks. IKE Analyze from ikeGPS is achieving rapid adoption because we simply help communication companies deploy networks faster and safer while lower deployment costs through better efficiency. ikeGPS is seeking an experienced enterprise sales professional to join our team and help us build on our success and respond to this amazing growth opportunity.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Enterprise Sales Manager Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Enterprise Sales Manager will be a key member of the Americas sales team for the Communications Business Unit focused on developing and executing Target Account Sales Plans in pursuit of growing our business with a select list of tier-one communication companies. The position will be supported by marketing and subject matter experts so that you can focus on presenting our capabilities, managing the sales process and making deals happen. The Enterprise Sales Manager will report to the SVP responsible for the Communications Business Unit.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Enterprise Sales Manager Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><i>Leverage outstanding written and verbal communication skills to align IKE Analyze with qualified opportunities and manage the Solution Selling sales process</i>. Time management, presence, presentation and follow-up skills are a key to being successful in this position. The successful applicant will have a demonstrated ability to be a leader in the sales process.</p>
<br>
