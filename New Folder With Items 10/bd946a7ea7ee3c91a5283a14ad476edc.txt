<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Create and process all title applications, and other misc. title work needed compliant with each of the 52 states, including all notarization for the company.</li>
<li>
Files/scans all necessary paperwork daily.</li>
<li>
Processes BMV and posts all BMV checks.</li>
<li>
Refunds and/or collects monies for customer tag work.</li>
<li>
Responsible for generating and working the “Missing Title Report” and working it on a weekly basis to assure all collateral is accounted for and present.</li>
<li>
Responsible for sales tax and BMV schedule.</li>
<li>
Follows up on missing titles due.</li>
<li>
Meets all title filing deadlines.</li>
<li>
Answer’s the telephone and responds to customer and lien holder requests throughout the day.</li>
</ul>
