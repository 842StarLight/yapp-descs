<h2>Global Head of Customer Marketing Advocacy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Global Leader Customer Marketing Advocacy
<br><br>
<b>Vonage Marketing Mission:</b> We are a highly creative, energetic and results-oriented organization that produces and delivers the content Vonage needs to entice, educate, engage with and sell to customers around the world. We connect with the external marketplace through all media including print, digital and social. Our team leverages deep industry, market and customer data into actionable insights and marketing strategies. We are creative and collaborative, striving to translate how Vonage's products and services can meet the needs of our current and future customers.
<br><br>
Why this role matters
<br><br>
This is an exciting opportunity to build the Customer Marketing Advocacy practice and lead marketing initiatives that drive retention, loyalty, growth and community across Vonage's customer base. You will create a strategic vision and partner with internal stakeholders in Marketing, Sales, Product, Analytics and Customer Care to develop an end to end program that includes customer stories, testimonials, third party user reviews, up-market ABM strategies, creating on and offline communities and advisory boards as well as influencer programs to help us drive growth from our global customer base.

What you will do
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Partner with business unit leaders, Sales, and other key stakeholders to understand product capabilities and translate this understanding into consistent and compelling customer use cases (personas) and stories executed across multiple channels.</li>
<li>Deliver innovative, best-practice and data-driven customer programs to outperform the competition and significantly enhance Vonage's success and profitability.</li>
<li>Leverage data, insights and market research as well as competitive insight to identify customer needs and create multi-channel customer stories, customer testimonials, online communities and content to deliver value to our B2B buyers and developers.</li>
<li>Segment and mine the database of existing customers to identify strategic opportunities to increase share by driving loyalty and referrals.</li>
<li>Build a robust Advocacy program (vendor assessments) and Third Party User Review program to increase consideration and adoption of new SaaS offerings across our customer base.</li>
<li>Leverage market insight to build relevant, compelling and scalable content, including white papers, one-sheets, website content and case studies as well as sales enablement tools and customer-facing assets.</li>
<li>Leverage online channels including social and site experiences to drive customer loyalty.</li>
<li>Travel can be up to 20%.</li>
</ul>
What you will bring
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong understanding of customer marketing, and an ability to leverage data and insights to drive customer advocacy strategies.</li>
<li>Ability to be extremely creative, analytical and channel savvy and find new ways to engage customers and drive retention, upsell, cross-sell, and referrals.</li>
<li>Experience creating customer loyalty strategies and partnering closely with Sales, Customer Care and external vendors for advocacy and third party reviews.</li>
<li>Omni-channel knowledge that allows you to activate assets using on and offline channels.</li>
<li>Strong strategic, communications and analytical skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Salesforce, Marketing Automation, Customer Insight tools.</li>
<li>Ability to:</li>
<li>Manage to build a customer advocacy strategy from the ground up and to quickly optimize it leveraging learnings</li>
<li>Facilitate effective discussions and integrate the ideas and recommendations of diverse stakeholders</li>
<li>Translate offerings into person based use cases</li>
<li>Tailor communication approach to specific situations and audiences</li>
<li>Work closely with Sales, Product, Customer Support and Analytics</li>
</ul>
