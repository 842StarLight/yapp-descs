As a manufacturer of innovative workspace product solutions that are sold to the commercial, educational, industrial and other end markets, we are constantly developing functional products that are value priced. Under our Stand Up Desk Store brand, we create workspace furniture solutions that nurture a healthy and collaborative work environment.</div>
<div><b>
GENERAL PURPOSE:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Assure all accounting transactions are posting to the correct general ledger accounts and oversee all inventory accounting, costing and reconciliation. Perform account reconciliations and follow through on corrections and processes to assure errors do not repeat. Provide back up to the Controller specifically to lead month end close process and high data analysis.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND Responsibilities:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Functional Duties and Responsibilities:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Lead month end close procedures and record journal entries for multiple company divisions</li>
<li>Reconcile general ledger accounts and follow up on posting errors to assure they do not repeat</li>
<li>Manage inventory accounting and reconciliation for multiple warehouse locations. Monitor and explain differences within inventory balances. Support all physical inventory taking processes. Conduct inventory cost analysis</li>
<li>Work with department leaders to prepare, analyze and develop detailed, in-depth reporting as needed</li>
<li>Identify areas of process improvements and efficiencies, and bring to Controller as suggestions on business impact and how to implement</li>
<li>In absence of personnel, perform other accounting department job functions as a backup</li>
<li>Establish metrics (KPIs) for operational initiatives, develop data presentation format, and report to Controller monthly</li>
<li>Meet department deadlines as established in coordination with the Controller</li>
<li>Responsible for sales commission calculations</li>
<li>Assure company and GAAP standards are applied and adhered to</li>
<li>Ad hoc data analysis projects as required</li>
<br>
</ul>
