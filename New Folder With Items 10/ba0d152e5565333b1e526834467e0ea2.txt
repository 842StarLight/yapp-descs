<div>CEVA Logistics is currently seeking an Account Executive I. <ul>
<li>Develop and maintain functional knowledge of the products, services and operations offered by the company and actively participate in product training sessions.
</li>
<li>Interact regularly with station and operations managers, and develop close and cooperative working relationship with operational staff to ensure the customer’s needs are met.</li>
<li>Schedule and conduct a pre-determined number of face-to-face sales calls as directed by the sales or station management.
