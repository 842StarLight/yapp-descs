<h2>Business Analyst for Software Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The <b>Business Analyst for Software Development</b>is responsible for determining the requirements of a project or program, assisting to define the ideal path to bridge the business problem utilizing a technology solution, and communicating clearly to stakeholders, facilitators and peers.</p>
<p><b>Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform software requirement gathering and analysis via user groups, interviews, research, etc.</li>
<li>Elicit, organize, analyze, document and communicate business requirements.</li>
<li>Provide subject matter expertise on software functionality and business processes</li>
<li>Analyze business requirements and translate them into more detailed user stories, use cases and other functional requirement documents.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Quality Assurance to support testing efforts.</li>
<li>Create and maintain business process work flows and flowcharts.</li>
<li>Perform validation of solutions by analyzing the end product and applying the requirement specifications.</li>
<li>Assist in developing project plans, leading meetings and other related tasks to gather and coordinate activities for requirements gathering.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
