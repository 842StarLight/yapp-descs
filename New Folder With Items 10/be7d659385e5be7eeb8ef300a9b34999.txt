<h2>Senior Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>NeighborWorks America: Working Together for Stronger Communities
</b></p>
<p><b>Purpose of Division: </b>NeighborWorks America, through NeighborWorks Services Group, provides a comprehensive spectrum of courses, consulting, networking opportunities and other support to help professionals and their organizations adopt innovations, improve performance and achieve meaningful impact. We offer organizational capacity assessments, skills-based training courses, leadership-development programs and sophisticated tools for quantifying outcomes.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b>This position is focused primarily on cross-promoting our various Services Group offerings, as well as undertaking creative work (writing, graphic design) that supports effective and efficient marketing of Services Group offerings. In addition, this position is responsible for promoting all training courses provided online or in a place-based format, targeting both client organizations and prospective training participants. Finally, this position is a primary liaison point for our collaborative work with Public Relations and Resource Development.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties/Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop marketing materials to promote our online learning and place-based training products and disseminate to prospective client organizations.</li>
<li>Develop materials and approaches to market NCHEC products and services beyond Training Institutes.</li>
<li>Create and send targeted email messages promoting online learning courses and place-based training events to prospective learners to maximize course participation/revenue.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop and distribute information via social media that targets non-NeighborWorks network audiences, working with other marketing staff and Public Relations as appropriate.</li>
<li>Undertake graphic design and marketing copywriting as needed (emails, fliers, templates, webpages, annual catalog, etc.), working with fellow marketing staff as appropriate.</li>
<li>Represent NeighborWorks and the Services Group at industry events and trade shows, networking with partners and customers as appropriate, and promoting our presence.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Work with the Resource Development team to ensure that they are equipped with materials that communicate the full range of our offerings.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Network with potential partner organizations to trade event calendar listings, e-newsletter inclusions, banner ads, etc. Write content as needed.</li>
<li>Update web content on the public website for Service Group offerings as needed (e.g., news items, event updates, course and faculty highlights).</li>
<li>Explore new opportunities to better cross-promote to customers shared by our various business units and work to integrate with ongoing marketing efforts.</li>
<li>Manage the NeighborWorks mobile app for our major training events. Create basic content and design, coordinate marketing efforts, and provide assistance to end-users.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Provide on-site assistance at training events and at industry conferences and trade shows as needed.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Nature of Work Contacts:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaboration with NeighborWorks America Training Division staff as well as other NeighborWorks America staff and affiliate organization staff</li>
<li>Regular contact with printers, designers and other vendors and professional services consultants.</li>
</ul>
