<h2>VTC Engineer Tier 3 Expert (Tysons)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Tier 3 Video Engineers are expected to design, configure, install, and troubleshoot all levels and complexities of video teleconferencing and related video systems, including multi-location video distribution systems. Support for Enterprise Legacy RF CATV Distribution as well as newer QAM/DTV Solutions. Support Multicast / Unicast Desktop Video and IPTV Enterprise Network Architecture. Configuration Management of Enterprise Grade VTC Environment consisting of 3500+ Endpoints, Call Bridges, Gatekeepers and Traversal Servers (VCS/SBC) to include Polycom and Cisco Devices.<br>
Video Teleconferencing Technicians and Engineers are required to be fully versed in the operations, installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting of VTC systems from manufactured such as Polycomm and Cisco.<br>
