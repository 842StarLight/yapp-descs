<h2>Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Roostify is transforming the mortgage industry with an innovative and integrated platform that's streamlining the entire digital lending experience. We believe that home lending should be a fair, fast, and transparent experience. Our software is used by banks across the country to improve lending experiences every day. We are a team of innovative thinkers on a mission to reinvent the lending experiences so people can accelerate their future.
<br><br>
Roostify is looking for an experienced Sales Engineer with a track record of success to join our team. We're looking for someone passionate about engaging with prospects and customers as the key technical advisor and advocate to drive and manage the technology evaluation stages of the sales process. This role will be the primary customer-facing technical member of the Sales team but also cross functions with Product Management, Engineering, and Marketing in addition to being responsible for the success of prospects' technical evaluation and business value of Roostify.
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br>-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work as part of a Sales team to formulate account strategies</li>
<li>Experience with building strong relationships throughout the sales cycle with our customers' technical staff</li>
<li>Ability to articulate product positioning and competitive differentiation to both business and technical users</li>
<li>Experience with responding to functional and technical elements of RFIs/RFPs</li>
<li>Ability to travel 25-50%</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written, and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
SKILLS &amp; EXPERIENCE
<br>-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5-7+ years experience as a pre-sales or Sales Engineer supporting enterprise software solutions, preferably in an early-stage startup</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, a related discipline, or equivalent work experience as a Sales Engineer</li>
<li>Domain expertise in the mortgage lending industry; Ability to dissect complex mortgage ecosystems, identify improvement opportunities and communicate business solutions.</li>
<li>Deep knowledge of sales cycle and presales activities</li>
<li>Experience with Heroku, Amazon Web Services, and API-base solutions</li>
<li>Enterprise SaaS experience: application integration, BI, analytic applications</li>
<li>Strong understanding of UI/UX design principles</li>
</ul>
