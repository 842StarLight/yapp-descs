<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>JM Family</b> is currently hiring an<b> Investment Operations Analyst</b>. This position supports the investment team in managing JM Family’s investment portfolios (Profit Sharing, Pension, 401k, Insurance, Corporate, Non-Qualified). The position will work with internal and external associates to ensure the seamless execution of investment activities and reporting. Primary responsibilities include performance reporting, monitoring investment compliance, assisting with annual audit, cash management, and trade reconciliations.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
How You'd Spend Your Day:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Collecting and entering monthly accounting/performance data.</li>
<li>
Reconciling custodial and manager statements.</li>
<li>
Monitoring transactions and cash movements, including fee payments, capital calls, and inflows/outflows.</li>
<li>
Preparing monthly performance and attribution reports.</li>
<li>
Maintaining and updating the document management system.</li>
<li>
Interacting with various divisions of the firm to obtain information including Tax, Legal, Finance, and Accounting.</li>
</ul>
