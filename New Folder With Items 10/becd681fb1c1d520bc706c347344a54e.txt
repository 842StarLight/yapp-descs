<h2>Temporary PT Administrative Assistant - Calverton, NY</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Job ID: 154308</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Anchor, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p><br>
This position will perform general administrative duties for the department it is assigned to. The individual will perform routine clerical functions and may support more than one department simultaneously.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Perform a variety of clerical work involving standardized forms, reports, data, and procedures using established instruction/routines</li>
<li>
Prepare simple forms or reports; sort and files documentation</li>
<li>
Maintain historical records by filing documents</li>
<li>
Read and route incoming mail, prepare outgoing mail and correspondence or other printed materials</li>
<li>
Maintain postage meter and stamp outgoing mail</li>
<li>
Compose and type routine correspondence</li>
<li>
Organize and maintain file systems, and file correspondence and other records</li>
<li>
Answer and screen managers' phone calls, arrange conference calls and meetings, maintain conference room schedule</li>
<li>
Greet visitors and direct to appropriate area or person</li>
<li>
Coordinate and arrange meetings, prepare agendas, reserve and prepare facilities, record and transcribe meeting minutes</li>
<li>
Order and maintain supplies and arrange for equipment maintenance</li>
<li>
Provide support for assigned departments</li>
<li>
Develop and grow internal and external customer relationships for the purpose of improving the company's growth</li>
<li>
Conduct research and compile and type statistical reports</li>
<li>
Some A/P &amp; A/R filing and file maintenance</li>
<li>
Update and maintain company phone list</li>
<li>
Reconcile petty cash - monthly</li>
<li>
Maintain supply of promotional printed material, such as brochures, price books, color charts, binders, etc.</li>
<li>
Answer phone calls and support the receptionist as needed</li>
<li>
Assist the General Manager in the day to day office functions to ensure efficiency</li>
<li>
Assist the General Manager with Human Resources and Payroll</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities related to the nature of the job may be assigned on a temporary or permanent basis as needed<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b><br>
</p>
<p>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skills and/or abilities required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable qualified individuals with disabilities to perform the essential duties of the job.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Requirements / Education / Experience</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma or equivalent of at least 1 year of office experience or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>
Associate’s Degree preferred</li>
<li>
Knowledge of basic office equipment (phone, fax, copier, 10-key)</li>
<li>
Strong Microsoft Office/computer skills</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate with employees, peers, supervisors, vendors, and customer is an effective manner</li>
<li>
Must be detail oriented, organized, and have problem-solving and reasoning skills</li>
<li>
Ability to perform basic math calculations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, division and computing rate and ratio</li>
<li>
Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out written and oral instructions</li>
<li>
Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables with little or no structure</li>
<li>
Internal and external customer service oriented</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Physical Requirements</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to perform sedentary work, occasional walking, standing, squatting, and bending required</li>
<li>
Ability to lift up to 10 lbs. and carry small objects and occasionally lift up to 25 lbs.</li>
<li>
Prolonged sitting, repetitive use of fingers and hands for typing</li>
<li>
Available to work overtime as necessary</li>
<li>
May be required to work under stressful conditions</li>
</ul>
