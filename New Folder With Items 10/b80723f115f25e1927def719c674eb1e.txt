<b>About Us:</b><br>
<br>
<b>CribMaster™</b>, provides inventory control solutions through advanced software, innovative vending hardware, Radio-Frequency Identification (RFID) and customer services to manufacturing, distribution, government and service industries for managing indirect inventory (tools, spare parts, MRO, etc.). CribMaster (http://www.cribmaster.com/) ties fast and reliable tool crib or store room transactions to a powerful system of inventory control. The system provides critical data to manage consumable and durable inventory, reduce the risk for lost or misplaced tools, and provide critical data to business leaders to reduce risk of tools being lost or left behind, reduce costs and increase productivity.<br>
<br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
<br>
Master Data Specialist will take responsibility for the data management of our sales application Sales Force, as well as SAP and will be working with various departments to gather and manage our master datasets, develop reports, implement new data, and troubleshoot data issues. To do well in this role you need a very fine eye for detail, experience working in teams, and understanding of databases. As CribMaster’s Master Data Specialist, you will manage SAP master data, including creation, updates, and deletion and provide overall Master Data governance, ongoing maintenance and direction of master data in a manufacturing environment.<br>
