<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
This part-time position will be responsible for supporting the SCM- commodity management team in its sourcing activities. These activities will include managing the SCM-Engineering Change Order (ECO) process, publishing RFQs, quote analysis, generating Pos, invoice reconciliation, and reporting. Knowledge of basic supply chain principles, MS-Office Suite, MRP systems and engineering blueprints are required.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS INCLUDE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage SCM-ECO process using engineering software and tracking progress of same: Log ECR/ECO, prepare RFQ-pull prints, identify suppliers to quote, quote analysis; assign ECOs to other team members as required; communicate internally with different functional areas and externally with suppliers; prepare periodic progress reports and presentations.</li>
<li>
Support PPV and savings by submitting reports from the ESG Sourcing Group to ESG Finance.</li>
<li>
Support the Supplier Quality function by notifying them of PPAP sample availability, recording final results and releasing part in ECO system.
</li>
<li>Support negotiations with vendors regarding price quotes for new and special components by providing quote analyses and assistance to category/commodity management team. Must be able to collect, organize and analyze data in a meaningful way to facilitate decision-making during the negotiation process.</li>
<li>
Support category manager/Supply Chain Specialist with new product project launches, by ordering samples from prototype through Project Beta Build.</li>
<li>
During new product introduction communicate supplier requirements to planning group. Audit/verify receiving records (price and quantity), analyzing and/or adjusting releases from new or existing purchase orders, working with sales and/or engineering to identify upcoming orders and subsequent component requirements, researching any invoicing discrepancies for component/raw material purchases, etc.</li>
<li>
Must become proficient with J. D. Edwards MRP software and be able to enter and maintain purchase orders and/or release/delivery information.</li>
<li>
Maintains a detailed understanding of logistical options and associated costs to assure that material flow from suppliers to customer’s results in a high quality delivered product with an optimal cost structure. Will require make versus buy and total cost of ownership (TCO) assessment.</li>
<li>
Prepares and issues all necessary reports and/or correspondence. Must be proficient with advanced knowledge in Microsoft Office Suite (Mail Merge, Pivot Tables, VLookup, etc.) and SharePoint.</li>
<li>
Support team members in their absence – warranty, supplier quality, and meeting participation.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
