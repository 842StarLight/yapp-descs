<h2>Marketing Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description:
</b></h2>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"></h3>
<p><b>POSITION PURPOSE
</b></p>
<p><b>Marketing Intern is responsible for entry level marketing support to designated members of the Marketing Team within the Pentair Filtration Solutions group. The intern will support marketing projects that involve: planning, developing, producing, delivering and maintaining products, programs, promotional and other marketing literature, training materials, catalogs and price lists.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Support marketing projects that involve: planning, developing, producing and delivering of both print- and electronic-based internal and external communications.
</b></li>
<li><b>Support marketing services projects including, but not limited to: trade shows, photography, graphics, dealer programs, trade advertising, public relations, and newsletters.
</b></li>
<li><b>Manage and maintain website content. Prepare written materials including some marketing copy, business communications, reports and presentation scripts.
</b></li>
<li>
<b>Develop and maintain strong working relationships with Marketing, Sales and Customer Service teams, customer/dealer and vendor contacts.
</b><p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS:
</b></p>
<p><b>Pursuing a 4 year degree in Marketing or other business related field
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS
</b></p>
</li>
<li><b>Skill in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access, Outlook
</b></li>
<li><b>Basic knowledge of sales and marketing principles
</b></li>
<li><b>Detail oriented and accurate
</b></li>
<li><b>Must have demonstrated the ability to work independently with minimal supervision and complete projects on schedule and on budget
</b></li>
<li><b>Analyze, organize, and problem solve frequently and efficiently
</b></li>
<li>
<b>Ability to communicate status and actions effectively
</b><p></p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL AND MENTAL DEMANDS SPECIFIC TO THIS POSITION:
</b></p>
<p><b>Walking, standing, bending, lifting up to 50lbs, use of a computer, typing and ability to speak English.
</b></p>
</li>
</ul>
