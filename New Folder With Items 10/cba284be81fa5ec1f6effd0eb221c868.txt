<h2>Customer Success Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Title: Customer Success Executive</b><br>
<b>Location: Remote, DC</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Customer Outcomes Success Architect will be responsible for developing and maintaining C-level executive relationships and relationship management across 2-3 accounts. The over-riding objective for the Success Architect will be to drive Customer Outcomes at these managed accounts leading to client’s product adoption, renewals, and expansion of ServiceNow offerings with the accounts.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
An ideal candidate will have a achieved a senior level position and successful track record in management consulting, (professional services firms), focused on technology and organizational transformation. They will have demonstrated the ability to become a trusted advisor to C-level client leaders and facilitate customer success from strategic planning functions including business value identification, road mapping, as well as advising and defining successful execution strategies including governance frameworks and managing large enterprise programs.<br>
<br>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Servicing as the Success Architect for 2-3 target accounts</li>
<li>
Developing strong executive relationships with the CIO, CFO, CHRO and business leaders within each of the 2-3 accounts to understand the clilent’s vision and how ServiceNow plays in their digital transformation journey. By;<ul>
<li>
Understand business objectives and develop customer roadmaps to fully realize value from ServiceNow</li>
<li>
Business value definition/realization/ benchmarks</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Together with the ServiceNow account team, define and execute winning co-delivery models to achieve successful projects/programs.<ul>
<li>
Develop relationships with ecosystem partners at the account to identify opportunities to co-sell and co-deliver within the account.</li>
<li>
Work with SN teams as well as ecosystem partners to develop implementation strategies and readiness process to accelerate time to value</li>
<li>
Work with clients and SN teams to establish delivery operating model governance to ensure successful go-lives and end-user adoption</li>
<li>
Maintain account-level relationships with strategic partners serving the targeted accounts to ensure ServiceNow has a clear value proposition within the account</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Participate in Account delivery governance (including Steering Committees with participation from client business and technology leaders across their enterprise) representing the ServiceNow platform</li>
<li>
Advocate/champion ServiceNow’s advisory and expert services best practices and industry use cases with clients</li>
<li>
Contribute thought leadership (methodology and white papers) on how Advisory, Expert Services, and Co-Delivery can optimize to achieve customer outcomes.</li>
<li>
Deliver high customer sat metrics for the assigned accounts.</li>
</ul>

<div></div>
<div><b><i>

<div></div>
