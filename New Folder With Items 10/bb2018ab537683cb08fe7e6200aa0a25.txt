<h2>Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Greenlots is unlocking the possibilities of the new electric mobility future by delivering innovative software and services that empowers utilities, cities, communities and automakers to deploy EV charging infrastructure at scale. Our technology is connecting people to their destinations in a safer, cleaner, and smarter way. Headquartered in Los Angeles, CA, the company’s global footprint spans across three continents with deployments in 13 different countries.</p>
<p>
We are looking for a customer-focused and strategic thinking Sales Engineer to support Greenlots' Mid-West Region Business Development efforts. As a key member of the sales solutions team, the SE provides technical sales support, consulting during the sales process, field support during installations, as well as interfacing directly with the customer for technical questions. Typical pre-sale activities include gathering, understanding, and analyzing customers’ business and technical requirements, then formulating responses for customer quotation. The SE is responsible for architecting solutions for customers as part of the proposal and sales process. The SE is a subject matter expert on Greenlots' business model, value proposition and technical solutions.</p>
<p>
Post sales activities include site walks for EV placement, technical responses to customer questions, and on the ground technical support – including being willing to roll up your sleeves to work with the solution vendors for site restoration.</p>
<p>
The position also requires initiative, willingness to travel, on the spot creative thinking, common sense and good judgment. Candidates should have a college degree in an associated field (e.g. Information Technology, Engineering, etc.), and 5 years professional level technical/engineering experience required.</p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Requires competency in customer focus, change &amp; innovation, relationship building &amp; influencing, results focus and inspirational leadership. We want you to have a presence and quickly demonstrate credibility in front of the customer</li>
<li>Represent Greenlots on customer appointments as the subject matter expert for Greenlots technical solutions specifically and EV placement generally</li>
<li>Responsible for working with Business Development (BD) and Sales to develop customer proposals and business cases</li>
<li>Work closely with BD and Sales to drive company growth by identifying opportunities, presenting alternatives to solve prospect business needs and completing customers follow up actions</li>
<li>Responsible for developing solution specifications and feasibility assessments</li>
<li>Design and prepare all technical paperwork to provisioning team for customer response</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What We’re Looking For:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years’ professional level technical/engineering experience</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills are essential</li>
<li>Candidates should have a college degree in an associated field (e.g. Business, Information Technology, Engineering, etc.) or equivalent technical experience</li>
<li>2-3 years industry experience in solutions development</li>
<li>Familiarity with EV concepts and solution design</li>
<li>Experience with EV edge device hardware a strong plus</li>
<li>Experienced in Wireless Networks – Cellular, Wi-Fi, Mesh, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What We Offer:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Benefits: Full health-care coverage for employees (medical, dental, vision), 401K, annual discretionary bonus</li>
<li>Participation in Greenlots’ long-term incentive plan</li>
<li>Daily ownership of your role in a challenging, high-growth environment</li>
<li>Environment: You’ll get to work with and learn from some of the best and brightest in this emerging market</li>
</ul>
