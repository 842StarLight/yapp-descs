<h2>Media Strategist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Influential, an A.I. influencer technology and official partner of IBM Watson, is looking for a best-in-class Media Strategist who will be responsible for the strategy, execution, performance, and reporting of media campaigns across multiple platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Youtube, Snap, Pinterest, etc.) Reporting to the Director of Media Strategy, this role will assist in the day-to-day stewarding of the media activity on assigned campaigns for our top clients, and help take social media planning to the next level. The Media Strategist will manage the creation and execution of various media strategies, including, but not limited to: campaign designed to deliver, in-store attribution, TV tune-in, sales lift, brand awareness, etc.</p>
<p>
We offer competitive compensation, daily catered lunches, a comprehensive benefits package, and equity options for all employees. We also have an awesome collaborative work environment and a diverse team who rocks. Ready to join?</p>
<p><b>
Here’s what you’ll be doing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with the media team to build, deploy, track &amp; optimize social media campaigns (in platform) to successfully execute the defined strategy and achieve business results</li>
<li>Assist with designing A/B testing, brand lift surveys &amp; other attribution based measurement studies across social media channels to drive business results</li>
<li>Collaborate with team members to ensure agreed upon pacing and delivery of campaigns, prompt delivery of reports to clients and accurate/useful performance reports</li>
<li>Analyze campaign performance and make recommendations based on analysis</li>
<li>Develop a strong working relationship with our partners in the media sales community, to assist with the day-to-day needs and account maintenance for campaigns</li>
<li>Work with our attribution partners to develop media strategies designed at driving key business results such as: Brand Awareness, Sales Lift, Tune In, etc.</li>
<li>Develop audience strategy using campaign briefs and target audience research analysis.</li>
<li>Maintain detailed files of all pertinent records (latest media plans, performance reports, etc.) relevant to each campaign</li>
<li>Maintain awareness of new marketing platforms and techniques, as well as look for opportunities to innovate and develop new media strategies to drive clients’ advertising forward</li>
<li>Reconcile all campaigns to ensure that the client receives all planned media and is invoiced/credited properly</li>
<li>Develop media plans across multiple verticals including Automotive, Entertainment, CPG &amp; ensure their successful implementation.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Here’s what we’d like to see</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree and 3-5+ years of experience in paid social media</li>
<li>Demonstrated enthusiasm for influencer marketing / paid social and a strong desire to learn &amp; build knowledge of social media attribution, social analytics &amp; target audience strategies</li>
<li>Proactive ability to multitask, strong written and oral communication skills, excellent organizational aptitude, attention to detail, and accuracy when working with numbers</li>
<li>Proven ability to optimize media spend to drive key business results and manage large social media budgets ($150-$350k/mo)</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge and a participant in the various social channels, including Facebook, YouTube, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and more.</li>
<li>Strong Microsoft Excel experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Bonus Points:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient with 3rd party ad-serving, tracking &amp; analytic systems to be able to accurately input media plan details and launch campaigns</li>
</ul>
