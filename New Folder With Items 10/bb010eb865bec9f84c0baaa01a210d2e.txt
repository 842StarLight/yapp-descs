Reports To
<br><br>
Director, Sales Enablement

What You Will Be Doing
<br><br>
In this hybrid role of designer and trainer, you will be creating online courses, in- person curriculum, and delivering courses to partners and internal employees on the exciting future of vehicle electrification. You will be on the cutting edge of electric vehicle (EV) charging with the leader in the EV charging industry, ChargePoint.
<br>You will travel to partner sites, ride along with sales people, and design training curriculums that shorten new hire sales ramp and create the strongest sales force in the EV charging business.
<br><br>
What You Will Bring to ChargePoint
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop, deliver, and evaluate training programs to support learning objectives that align with sales organization's goal.</li>
<li>Continually work to ensure all training materials are consistent and up-to-date and in line with products and larger organizational initiatives</li>
<li>Work cross functionally with sales leadership, marketing, product, and systems teams to align training goals</li>
<li>Create and design instructor-led and on-demand learning modules such as e-learning programs, podcasts, videos, webinars, podcasts, articles, and eLearning modules.</li>
<li>Develop assessments to measure the effectiveness of the curriculum and learner performance in the workplace, integrating results into new and existing course curriculum.</li>
<li>Track training data, report on metrics, and provide feedback and recommendations on how we move forward with training for sales roles.</li>
<li>Summarize and share data through regular reports and presentations to the sales leadership team.</li>
<li>Work with internal teams to increase awareness of learning programs</li>
<li>Maintain a weekly update to the sales team on cross functional information sales people need to know.</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Three years of experience with instructional design and adult learning techniques.</li>
<li>Experience creating training curriculum for sales</li>
<li>Experience delivering training</li>
<li>Experience develop full training cycle (assess needs, plan, develop, coordinate, facilitate, monitor and evaluate).</li>
<li>Experience designing and developing learning programs across various delivery mediums, including in-person training, remote synchronous training, and online modules</li>
</ul>
