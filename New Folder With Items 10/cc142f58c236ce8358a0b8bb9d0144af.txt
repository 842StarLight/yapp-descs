<h2>IT Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
As a Consultant at Spyglass Microsoft Technology Group (MTG) you will be working on projects to develop technology solutions for a variety of clients in industries such as Financial Services, Healthcare, Life Sciences, Manufacturing and Higher Education. Your work is generally done at client locations in the Greater Boston and New England area in a team environment that consists of Spyglass MTG and Client members.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate and execute technical customer projects from inception through closure to ensure timely, cost effective delivery to maximize customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Aid the presales process by lending your project management expertise to sales pursuits and proposals.</li>
</ul>
Project Management
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop efficient project plans (and estimates) and effectively implement, drive and coordinate these plans</li>
<li>Create and update project status reports, risks / issues list, project plans</li>
<li>Schedule and facilitate meetings to provide status, obtain sign off, resolve critical issues, and work through project scheduling conflicts.</li>
<li>Capture and distribute meeting notes</li>
<li>Anticipate and effectively resolve problems and address roadblocks.</li>
<li>Implement and/or work within an established budget.</li>
<li>Effectively communicate project status to the project team, executives and clients.</li>
<li>Create professional presentations to communicate project information effectively.</li>
<li>Assist with communication and training plan creation if / as needed</li>
</ul>
Business Analysis
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Requirements gathering and documentation. Document gaps between current state and future state; writing use cases, etc.</li>
<li>Assist project QA resources with test planning and execution if / as needed</li>
<li>Work as both an individual contributor and team member on projects.</li>
<li>Must be available to travel, up to 15% of the time, to and from client sites outside of the New England region.</li>
<li>Must continuously maintain and upgrade self-knowledge of industry, market and related products/solutions and maintain valid certifications.</li>
<li>Must possess the ability to think strategically, process information logically and solve problems.</li>
<li>Must possess the ability to work independently with minimal supervision.</li>
</ul>
