<h2>Web Content Administrator / IT Systems Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>This is a front-end entry-level IT type position working with web based and 3rd party purchased applications to make our business with multiple locations run efficiently. The Web Content Administrator / IT Systems Support is responsible for project management in regards to our third-party website that houses all our dealer inventory. You are the liaison between the functionality needed of our website to the 3rd party website team that houses and maintains our website. Approximately half the job is also data entry to make our inventory robust and accurately entered online. Inventory presentation to our customers include uploading pictures, content writing and inventory features. We also need this person to have strong skills in basic office IT hardware support including installing new computers, printers and other basic office needs. We are a growing business and all of our positions must be flexible to wear different hats to get the job done.</p>
<p>****We are not looking for a web designer or developer, however experience with these skills will be considered. This is an entry level position that will include some mass amounts data entry. If you are fresh out of a web design or developer program, this may be the perfect job for you.</p>
<ul>
<li>Complete data entry tasks in order to maintain company wide inventory presentation for our customers which include uploading pictures, content writing and inventory features.</li>
<li>Complete basic IT admin needs for the office including setting up new computers, printers and basic troubleshooting.</li>
<li>Controls user access and passwords to web-based programs and 3rd party applications our dealerships use</li>
<li>Email database management with Gsuite</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail for project tracking &amp; communication while requesting changes to our 3rd party website provider.</li>
<li>Proposes and implements systems enhancements that will improve the reliability and performance of the website</li>
<li>Train personnel on any new and existing programs and applications our dealerships use</li>
<li>Troubleshoots web-based application, software and hardware issues to either hire help or resolve the problems</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Necessary Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced computer skills (DMS/CRM experience a plus)</li>
<li>Experience with Gsuite for Business strongly preferred</li>
<li>Excellent Proficiency in Microsoft Office suite</li>
<li>Comfort with fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Ability to learn and perform tasks with limited direct oversight or training</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Understanding of computer hardware preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of Wordpress, PHP, Javascript preferred but not required</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualified candidates will be contacted by P&amp;P and initially interviewed by phone after applying through Indeed. All hiring is done through the corporate office. This is a full time position requiring 40+ hours per week in a dealership environment including some Saturday work. Compensation starts at $13/hour;negotiable with experience, and benefits are available after a satisfactory probationary period.</p>
