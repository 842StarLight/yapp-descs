<p><b>About the Role</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a Product Marketer/Content Specialist to produce internal and external facing content assets, that contribute to the overall success of our sales team. You will join a team of talented individuals who work collaboratively with sales, product managers, and subject matter experts to help accelerate our sales cycle.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develops a thorough understanding of the audience and the content assets required by meeting with appropriate stakeholders
</li>
<li>Research and build knowledge about our products, services, and technology</li>
<li>Determine the clearest and most logical way to present information for an external and internal reader to easily consume and comprehend</li>
<li>Works with our creative team to create graphics and illustrations to elaborate on or complement technical concepts</li>
<li>Meets with subject matter experts in order to ensure that specialized topics are appropriately addressed and discussed</li>
<li>Collaborates with translators as necessary to convert writing into a variety of languages</li>
<li>Revises, edits, or updates content assets with product updates as necessary</li>
<li>Creates, adapts, and follows project schedules and deadlines</li>
<li>Remains up-to-date on technological and product developments
</li>
<li>Attends conferences and trade shows pertinent to the APM industry</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li> 5 + years proven working experience in product marketing and technical writing for software.
</li>
<li> Excellent grammar, punctuation, and formatting skills</li>
<li> You thrive in an agile work environment that requires flexibility and self-direction</li>
<li> You are well organized, manage your time well, and are attentive to detail, meeting deadlines and juggling priorities</li>
<li> You have the ability to quickly grasp complex technical concepts and make them easy to comprehend</li>
<li> Bachelor's Degree
</li>
</ul>
