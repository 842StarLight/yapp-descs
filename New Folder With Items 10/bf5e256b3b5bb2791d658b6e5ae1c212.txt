What You’ll Do:<br>
As an Account Manager you will focus on growing our current customer base, as well acquire new customers in the greater Dallas – Fort Worth Metroplex. You will be selling our entire portfolio of solutions that include Data Center Technologies, Cyber Security, Microsoft Solutions, Cloud Solutions, and Cisco Technologies.<br>
<ul>
<li>Meet or exceed established monthly sales goals.</li>
<li>Seek out new opportunities constantly within established region.</li>
<li>Follow up on referrals and new leads resulting after field activity.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to cross-sell and up-sell services.</li>
<li>Develop &amp; execute marketing and business plans to drive revenue and profits.</li>
<li>Sell to customers based on their business requirements using a solutions/consultative sales approach.</li>
<li>Develop partnerships with Vendor Field Sales Representatives for joint selling opportunities within the territory.</li>
<li>Build and strengthen customer relationships through follow up and pro-actively resolving customer satisfaction issues.</li>
<li>Continue to train and develop knowledge of new technologies and selling points, including enhancing expertise in data and voice application technologies.</li>
<li>Participate in all mandatory and voluntary training programs that will enhance knowledge, skills and abilities to maximize performance and skill development</li>
<li>Prepare proposals, presentations, and sales contracts.</li>
</ul>
