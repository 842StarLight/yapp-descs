<h2>Outside Sales Representative - High Point, NC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>If you consider yourself outgoing, are active within the community and are looking for a work/life balance, we have an opportunity for you.</p>
<p>
Welcome Wagon is a national advertising/marketing company that welcomes families to their new homes by providing them with introductions to local businesses, professionals and services through print, digital and social media.</p>
<p><b>
Outside Sales Representative Requirements:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
2 years outside sales experience a must</li>
<li>
Cold calling experience a plus</li>
<li>
Phone sales experience a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
This position is home based and offers the following</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Benefits - medical, dental, vision and life insurance</li>
<li>
Generous commission plan - double commission (40%) in the first 60 days</li>
<li>
Bonuses</li>
<li>
Expense reimbursement</li>
<li>
Training and support</li>
</ul>
<p>
The opportunity requires both phone and in-person sales skills (phone to set appointments, in-person to present and close). As a Sales Representative, you will sell consultative advertising including our Gift Book, Direct Mail, Digital Marketing and Social Media Programs within your local market.</p>

<p>

<p>
Hiring Full Time with benefits and Part Time without benefits.</p>

Physical Requirements</b></p>
<p>
