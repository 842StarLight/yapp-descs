<p><b>
The opportunity</b></p>
<p>
We are seeking a driven Business Optimization Manager who will be accountable for the identification and prioritization of business improvement initiatives and change management support for the US Gas Operations Business Unit. Additionally, the Business Optimization Manager will be responsible for leading the identification of key process improvements, implementing best practices across the organization, ensuring alignment across operating plans and strategic initiatives, establishing consistency in project data configuration and documentation, and leading and developing a team of Business Analysts and Project Managers. The successful candidate will have demonstrated facilitation skills, written and verbal communications skills; and ability to develop and deliver clear, concise presentations; be an adaptable team player with the ability to work well in a collaborative environment; demonstrate strong business acumen with a drive and commitment to achieve goals and business results.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This position may be filled in Houston, TX or Charleston, WV depending on the location of the successful candidate.</p>

<p><b>
About our business</b></p>
<p>
TC Energy is a leading energy infrastructure company in North America. We have three complementary businesses of natural gas pipelines, liquids (oil) pipelines, and power generation. Our operations span three countries, seven Canadian provinces, and 34 U.S. states.</p>
<p>
Our 57,500-mile natural gas pipeline network is positioned in two of the most prolific and lowest cost basins in North America – the Western Canadian Sedimentary Basin (WCSB) and the Appalachian Basin. Our systems are well placed to meet the continent’s growing demand for clean-burning natural gas by connecting the supply in these basins to important and growing markets across the continent. We supply more than 25 percent of the clean-burning natural gas consumed daily across North America.</p>
