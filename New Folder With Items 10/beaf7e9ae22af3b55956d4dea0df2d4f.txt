<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Are you a dynamic individual that is passionate about technology and looking for an IT Internship? Come join VectorUSA’s growing technical team. You will gain valuable industry knowledge and experience around both network and server environments, and learn basic troubleshooting and diagnostics skills. You will gain experience and proficiency in managing data center environments to ensure the stability of diverse client infrastructure. A successful candidate will be self-disciplined, highly motivated to learn new technologies, work well on individual tasks in a team environment, and be able to work well and communicate clearly with both technical and non-technical personnel in high-stress situations. If you are looking for a growth opportunity and a challenging but rewarding experience, consider joining the VectorUSA team.</div>

<br>
<ul>

<li>
