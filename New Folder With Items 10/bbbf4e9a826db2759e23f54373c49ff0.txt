<h2>Director, Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<div>Sparkfund is an energy technology subscription company based in Washington, D.C. We give customers access to essential building energy functions and outcomes — like cold air, light and resilience to storm events — without the constraints of ownership, enabling them to do more energy projects, faster, and with fewer resources. We strive to create positive change by deploying solutions that are good for business and good for the planet. And we’re building a diverse and collaborative culture to help us achieve all of that.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Sparkfund is hiring a Director, Business Development to drive growth for our business as part of our rapidly expanding Sales organization. You will be responsible for developing new business opportunities, directly engaging key customer stakeholders, and closing deals with large commercial clients across portfolios of buildings. You will leverage your existing networks in our strategic markets and work enterprise deals through our partners (e.g. Shell and Southern Company). While you may be located elsewhere and travel to visit your clients, we would expect you to spend a significant amount of time in DC to be a collaborative member of the team, learn the ins and outs of our business, and contribute to our company culture.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you will be doing</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Identify new opportunities with commercial customers and national accounts in our strategic market segments of commercial real estate, healthcare, hospitality, and education</li>
<li>
Secure meetings with high-level decision makers</li>
<li>
Create and facilitate client presentations</li>
<li>
Manage six- and seven-figure transactions to close</li>
<li>
Share lessons learned with larger team and provide insight into customer experience to help build out the tools and processes of a robust Sales organization</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Requirements</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree and significant sales experience in commercial real estate, asset management, and/or the financing or procurement related to advanced energy technology for commercial buildings</li>
<li>
Knack for building long-term relationships and trust</li>
<li>
Track record of exceeding quotas selling complex products/solutions to commercial clients</li>
<li>
Fearless attitude, ability to find a creative solution to every problem, and a willingness to learn by doing</li>
<li>
Dedication to what we’re building at Sparkfund, and making an impact through increasing access to energy efficiency across the economy</li>
<li>
Commitment to shared success in a collaborative team environment</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>We’d love it if you have</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Existing network of C-Suite executives, particularly COOs and CFOs</li>
<li>
Deep understanding of our customers, their pain points, and how they buy, and an ability to communicate our expertise in their businesses</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
