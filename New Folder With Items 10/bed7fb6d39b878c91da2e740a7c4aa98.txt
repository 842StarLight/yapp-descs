<h2>Chief Revenue Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Zift Solutions, a Portfolio Company of Arrowroot Capital, is looking to hire a Chief Revenue Officer (CRO). The Chief Revenue Officer will lead the entire revenue generation process including new-name sales, sales to existing customers, and channel sales. Our ideal candidate will have extensive experience in the development of current and new revenue models. The CRO will be a senior member of Zift Solutions' leadership team with accountability for achieving sales goals across multiple lines of business.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Define and maintain the company's sales methodology and revenue strategy, and<br>
design/launch revenue programs.</li>
<li>
Recruit, hire and provide sales methodology training for the sales team.</li>
<li>
Design and optimize channel sales strategy.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain strong collaborative relationships with C-level executives of key clients.</li>
<li>
Lead the sales teams, including the creation of benchmarks for analyzing managing performance.</li>
<li>
Willingness to work a flexible schedule and travel without restrictions.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Brains, personality, communication skills, commitment and accountability.</li>
<li>
10+ years executive-level experience in delivering rapid revenue growth in a SaaS environment; experience in Channel Management/Partnerships/Alliances highly preferred.</li>
<li>
Demonstrable experience designing and/or identifying repeatable and scalable<br>
operations.</li>
<li>
Experience and success structuring sales quotas and managing a team to achieve<br>
revenue expectations.</li>
<li>
Impeccable client-facing skills including verbal presentations, written documentation, and negotiating skills. Able to represent the company at key industry events.</li>
<li>
Strong collaborative team-player at the executive level and team builder.</li>
<li>
Highly organized and capable of handling several projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Must be an enthusiastic leader; you inspire others to be the best versions of themselves.</li>
<li>
Exceptional analytical ability and operational experience across multiple departments within an organization: Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, Partnerships.</li>
<li>
Experience with accurate forecasting, P&amp;L, monthly financial statement review/analysis, productivity measurements, and board-level communication of performance.</li>
<li>
Experience in strategic planning and execution. Knowledge of contracting, negotiating.</li>
<li>
Must be technically savvy and proficient with MS Office, SFDC, and corporate<br>
collaboration and productivity products.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years of experience leading Channel/alliance partnerships.</li>
<li>
Extensive understanding of SaaS-based cross-channel selling.</li>
<li>
Experience selling marketing automation, CRM, and/or channel technology products.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Compensation and Benefits:</b></p>
<ul><li>
We offer a highly competitive compensation package including base and bonus, comprehensive benefits, and equity.</li></ul>
