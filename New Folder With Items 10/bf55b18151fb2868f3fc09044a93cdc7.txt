<h2>Senior Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">SoftBank Robotics, the global leader in workforce automation, is seeking a passionate and dynamic Senior Account Executive to join a fast growing sales team that is focused on shaping the future with artificial intelligence and robotics. We believe automation to be the next big technological shift, comparable to the introduction of the smartphone.
<br><br>
Do you want to help us shape the future?
<br><br>
Our ideal candidate is intellectually curious, has a penchant for problem solving, and has an interest in being a part of a rapidly growing industry. The candidate should be a well-organized self-starter, and have a track record of exemplary performance.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Meet or exceed monthly sales targets by selling solutions into enterprise accounts</li>
<li>Manage the entire sales process to ensure delivery against key performance metrics</li>
<li>Create opportunities at F500 companies using both your industry network and leads from SDRs / Marketing</li>
<li>Manage the end to end sales process through engagement of appropriate resources such as Sales Engineers, Executives, Partners etc.</li>
<li>Manage customer inventory levels and obtain re-orders</li>
<li>Generate accurate and recurring weekly forecasts</li>
<li>Evaluate the effectiveness of the go-to-market model and identify opportunities to improve sales by working cross-functionally to develop growth plans within owned vertical as well as new business segments.</li>
<li>Design and interpret key performance metrics and craft plans to address identified opportunities</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>$10M in minimum lifetime dealsize closed</li>
<li>Experience solution selling into F500 organizations at the million-dollar deal level</li>
<li>Intellectually curious, and a successful problem solver</li>
<li>Industry knowledge / contacts within at least one of our target verticals (Real Estate, Airports, Cleaning, Retail, Facilities Management)</li>
<li>Successful negotiator</li>
<li>Strong strategic planning skills</li>
<li>Ability to lead a cross-functional team</li>
<li>Must be organized, detail oriented, and able to multitask</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written skills</li>
<li>Travel required (50% of role)</li>
</ul>
