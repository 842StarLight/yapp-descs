<h2>Accounting Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Bethesda Country Club is hiring an <b>Accounting Manager</b>. This is a great opportunity for someone looking to grow their career in the country club industry. The Accounting Manager will support the Controller with managing the day-to-day functions of the accounting department and other financial operations of the Club. The Accounting Manager will ensure that work is properly allocated and completed in a timely and accurate manner. Day-to-day operations include, but not limited to, Daily Sales, Internal Audit, Accounts Receivable, Statement Billing, Payroll, Accounts Payable, Fixed Assets, Inventory Control, General Ledger review, journal entries, month end closing and Financial Statements.</p>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Process bi-weekly payroll 10 departments, up to 230 employees</li>
<li>
Maintain employee benefits schedules</li>
<li>
Review all invoices for accuracy, coding and approval</li>
<li>
Accurately enter invoices and schedule for timely payment</li>
<li>
Respond to financial reporting inquiries from internal and external sources</li>
<li>
Maintain assigned schedules such as depreciation, fixed assets and fuel tax</li>
<li>
Reconcile assigned GL and deposit accounts</li>
<li>
Review inventories for accuracy</li>
<li>
Prepare journal entries and schedules associated with month-end close</li>
<li>
Prepare balance sheet, income statement and cost of goods sold reports</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other department managers and staff to ensure proper internal controls are in place</li>
<li>
Support Controller with special projects, reporting and analysis</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have at least 3 years of accounting experience in a hospitality environment country club preferred</li>
<li>
Must have strong interpersonal, supervisory and customer service skills</li>
<li>
Must have strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Must be well organized, detail oriented, work well under pressure and meet all required deadlines</li>
<li>
Must be PC proficient, especially with accounting software, POS systems and Microsoft Office</li>
<li>
Associate/Bachelor Degree in Accounting/Finance/Business Administration or equivalent work experience preferred</li>
</ul>
