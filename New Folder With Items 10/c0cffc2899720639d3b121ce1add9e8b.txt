<div></div>
<div><b>Role Description:</b></div>
<div>The Marketing Specialist’s role is to design, create, and deliver marketing programs to support expansion and growth of the company services and products. This role requires an in-depth knowledge of both in-bound and out-bound marketing strategies and tactics. Proficiency in graphics, web advertising, social media and web development is a must, however, this role also requires familiarity with a wide range of marketing practices that include, but not limited to, events, sponsorship activation, lead generation and campaign management. Marketing specialists rely on judgment in planning and experience to accomplish identified goals.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>What Winning in This Role Looks Like:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Developing, managing and executing layouts of communications such as presentations, newsletters, event support materials, research papers, and brochures</li>
<li>Planning, development and execution of digital programs and campaigns, including online advertising, web site strategy and design and social media campaigns</li>
<li>Interfacing with cross-functional teams to support the needs of various business units, including Recruiting, Sales and Innovation</li>
<li>Leading on-going management of digital touch points, including artisantechgroup.com, social media landing pages and other digital assets</li>
<li>Managing day-to-day production of external marketing partners, as well as campaign budgets and schedules</li>
<li>Owning and maintaining site analytics, metrics, and campaign reporting</li>
<li>Providing in depth working knowledge of SEO and adwords programs</li>
<li>Presenting key results and recommendations to senior leadership</li>
<li>Developing and coordinating multimedia packages</li>
</ul>
<div><b>You should have:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or diploma in Marketing or Business</li>
<li>2+ years of experience in management of digital marketing assets and campaigns</li>
<li>2+ years of proven marketing experience</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Core Competency Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Deep understanding of digital marketing and digital production</li>
<li>Proven experience delivering effective and innovative digital campaigns</li>
<li>Proven experience delivering successful campaigns on a variety of marketing platforms, including rich media online ads, micro sites, and social media applications</li>
<li>Solid understanding of web metrics, digital analytics, with the ability to generate, analyze and interpret data.</li>
<li>Must be able to manage priorities and deadlines with limited direction and guidance work under constant deadline pressure and</li>
<li>Must be able to manage multiple projects across multiple lines of business.</li>
<li>Strong management skills, excellent oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Experience delivering digital campaigns for mainstream products and services</li>
<li>Proficient with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, WordPress and Elegant Themes</li>
</ul>
