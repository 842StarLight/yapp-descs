<h2>Data Services Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>NetNumber is expanding! Due to the rapid growth we see in the sales of our products, NetNumber is looking to hire a Data Services Sales Representative. NetNumber Global Data Services provides a single, cost effective source for authoritative global data covering telecom, enterprise and security markets.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
We need someone who is ready to make a major difference to the company!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
This remote or telecommute role is suitable for experienced Sales Representative with experience selling to Carrier, MVNO, Enterprise, and Information services industries.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
We seek an individual that is well connected and has experience selling a range of data services and products across a wide range of customers in the US as well as internationally. Someone who understands the recurring sales model similar to SaaS products and can build a pipeline and forecast relatively quickly would be ideal.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This position carries a quota and is a remote, home-based office position, preferably in the US. This is a sales hunter position that requires a mindset of uncovering opportunities in new and existing accounts. The purpose of this position is to expand the NetNumber footprint within existing accounts and to penetrate new accounts.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
This position requires a polished, experienced Sales Representative professional with proven success working in both a direct sales model and partner-driven sales model.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p>The main responsibilities of the job are:</p>
<ul><li> Sales of NetNumber’s Data Services and related Cloud Services</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborate with NetNumber team members in Product, Technical Architecture, and GDS Sales Management to provide optimum solutions to prospective clients</li></ul>
<ul><li> Meet with Customers and Partners to ascertain the needs of who require these solutions</li></ul>
<ul><li> Meet sales quota goals</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop quotes based on those needs</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work closely with Technical Architect and Product Management in a team environment to achieve Sales goals</li></ul>
<ul><li> Promote/introduce NetNumber’s other products and services to accounts and partners</li></ul>
<ul><li> Support existing customers and partner sales, as needed</li></ul>
<ul><li> Hunt and respond to RFI’s and RFP’s in collaboration with NetNumber Bid Desk</li></ul>
<ul><li> Expand base within existing accounts through the introduction of new products/services</li></ul>
<ul><li> At certain times, act as “overlay” subject matter expert to other sales teams</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Requirements
</b></p>
<p>The successful candidate will have the following personal attributes:</p>
<ul><li> Must be a strong hunter and closer</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong history of sales success in carrier, enterprise and information services industries</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge and relationships with significant Telecom Carrier &amp; Enterprise Customers a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to quickly learn the product sets and present with confidence and knowledge</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent presentation skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proven ability to sell data services in a recurring sales model</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work in a team environment developing complex solutions to address customer needs.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to travel as needed up to 20%.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work from home position in any major city with proven ability to do so</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with a partner-driven sales model and support meetings, quotes, and other associated activities, as needed.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Experience</b></p>
<ul><li> Sales professionals with approximately 1-3 years experience in B2B Software/SaaS sales preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to grasp and learn telecom services and IT concepts</li></ul>
<ul><li> Understanding of voice and data networking technologies a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proven track record of success</li></ul>
<ul><li> Driven sales producer with an understanding of sales goal accomplishment</li></ul>
<ul><li> Forecast requirement knowledge and ability to maintain an accurate forecast</li></ul>
<ul><li> Salesforce experience</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent English skills; multi-lingual is a plus</li></ul>
