<h2>Drafter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Do you enjoy updating drawings and designs for production and prototype models? Then this entry level Drafter position is for you! Every day you will help Minco succeed by investigating product requirements, partnering with engineers on the design process, responding to customer needs, and living Minco’s <i>icare</i> values - Integrity, Communication, Accountability, Respect, &amp; Energy.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
At Minco, you can make a difference by being part of something that is evolving, growing and continually improving. Minco products can be found everywhere from on an oil rig miles under the sea to satellites miles above our heads - and in your doctor’s office! Our temperature sensing, controlling, and heating, as well as the flexible circuits that pull it all together, are in products that save people’s lives, explore the world, protect our military, and bring energy to everyone.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Work with various groups, such as sales, production, engineering, and purchasing to complete projects or respond to customer needs regarding product specifications, drawings, results, and other information</li>
<li>
Perform or assist in technical design tasks from engineering concepts including part modeling, drawing creation, circuit layout and preparation of supporting documentation using CAD software</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Design Engineers, Manufacturing Engineers, Process Engineers, Technicians, and customers on new product designs</li>
<li>
Design, output, verify and complete approvals for all manufacturing tools including specification drawings, CAM outputs, tooling drawings, electrical schematics, and fixture drawings</li>
<li>
Perform or assist in circuit/pattern design and layout to meet customer requirements and optimize product performance</li>
<li>
Participate in design reviews needed to provide functional and cost-effective fixtures for product fabrication</li>
<li>
Interact with suppliers on tooling designs
</li>
<li>Update records, documents, procedures, and system requirements regarding product introduction, maintenance and deletion, such as drawings, designs, specifications, parts, and other product requirements
</li>
<li>Identify areas of improvement, provide recommendations, and implement solutions using appropriate tools (e.g. databases, spread sheets, etc.)</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><i>Basic Qualifications:</i></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Associate degree in technically-oriented discipline (Engineering Technology, Drafting, , CAD, etc.)
 </li>
<li>3 month minimum (internships included) design/drafting experience in a manufacturing environment</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><i>
Preferred Qualifications:</i></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Knowledge of PCB circuit layout</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Altium Designer &amp; Vault
</li>
<li>Knowledge of AutoCAD,
</li>
<li>Experience with SolidWorks</li>
<li>
Knowledge of translating CAD/CAM data between different software platforms (i.e. Altium, SolidWorks, DXF, IGES, and STP, Gerber)</li>
<li>
Ability to read and understand ANSI/ASME standard mechanical drawings, schematics, and written work instructions</li>
<li>
Ability to independently interpret and apply the ASME Y14.5M – 1994 Dimensioning and Tolerancing Standards</li>
<li>
Understanding of fixture design and GD &amp; T, tolerance analysis; experience with eCAD and PLM
</li>
<li>Ability to plan and organize the complete drawing package for a design project</li>
<li>
Experience drafting specifications, drawings, artwork, (circuit design) and designs for products</li>
<li>
Experience applying drafting and operational principles, practices, and standards</li>
<li>
Ability to understand basic electrical and mechanical principles</li>
<li>
Computer experience (MS Outlook, Excel, Word, PowerPoint)<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>What Skills You Bring:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent written and oral communication skills for internal customer contact</li>
<li>
Energetic self-starter; driven, collaborative, and organized</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to effectively build relationships with employees and management</li>
<li>
Ability to respond to and resolve questions and concerns</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast-paced, dynamic and multi-cultural environment
</li>
<li>Demonstrated skill and proficiency in time management and the coordination of multiple concurrent projects and assignments with tight deadlines</li>
</ul>
<div></div>

This position is a regular full-time position located in our headquarters in Fridley, Minnesota.<br>
