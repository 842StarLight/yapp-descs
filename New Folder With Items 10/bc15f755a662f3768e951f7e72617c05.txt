<div><b>Summary:</b></div>
<div>
<div>The Strategic Account Executive is responsible for the direct sale of RF-SMART products and services to his/her assigned customer target-base. The purpose is to cultivate relationships, as well as to seek and follow new leads within the current account list in order to acquire new upsell business for RF-SMART. In calling on current customers, the Strategic Account Executive will gain access to decision makers, develop strong relationships, provide information, convey the value proposition of RF-SMART products, quote prices to customers, and shape the engagement strategy for each assigned account.</div>
</div>
