<br>
<p>
Oldcastle Coastal, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Summary</b></div>
<p></p>
<div>
<br>
<p>
This position is responsible for ensuring the highest level of customer satisfaction by providing support, guidance, proper follow-up and resolution to client inquiries or issues.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Assist customer with product questions, ordering, order tracking, complaints and problems</li>
<li>
Enter orders into system for delivery</li>
<li>
Prepare quotes</li>
<li>
Issue credits or debits accurately when applicable</li>
<li>
Answer incoming calls</li>
<li>
Assist walk-in counter customers</li>
<li>
Categorize, file, and maintain ship ticket copies for audit purposes and file all customer records</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities related to the nature of the job may be assigned on a temporary or permanent basis as needed</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
