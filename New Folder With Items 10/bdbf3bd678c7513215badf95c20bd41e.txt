<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Title: </b><b>Commercial Technical Support - Trainer II
</b>
</h2>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview:
</b></h2>
<p>Train internal and external customers, via phone and in-person, on Treasury Management services/products, including information reporting and transaction initiation to ensure their successful product usage. Provide daily post-installation/training technical support and troubleshooting assistance to external customers, typically via phone
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary Responsibilities:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Support provided includes, but is not limited to, follow-up training, web browser troubleshooting, operating system interaction with Bank applications, hardware troubleshooting and troubleshooting Bank application-specific issues to resolve identified issues and problems.
</li>
<li>Provide analytical and technical support, inclusive of web browser troubleshooting, operating system interaction with Bank applications, hardware troubleshooting and troubleshooting Bank application-specific issues, to internal departments such as Commercial Customer Service, Treasury Management Product Management and Treasury Management Sales.
</li>
<li>Complete various reports to provide a record of activity related to productivity and customer support.
</li>
<li>Complete system testing of Treasury Management product updates or implementations to ensure smooth functioning of the products for customers.
</li>
<li>Adhere to applicable compliance/operational risk controls in accordance with Company or regulatory standards and policies.
</li>
<li>Promote an environment that supports diversity and reflects the M&amp;T Bank brand.
</li>
<li>Maintain M&amp;T internal control standards, including timely implementation of internal and external audit points together with any issues raised by external regulators as applicable.
</li>
<li>Complete other related duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Minimum Qualifications Required:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or in lieu of a degree, a combined minimum of 6 years’ higher education and/or work experience including a minimum of 2 years’ proven internal and/or external customer service experience, inclusive of providing product support and training.
</li>
<li>Proficiency with personal computers as well as working knowledge of operating systems and web browsers.
</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tasks and assignments.
</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills, with ability to interact with customers and client personnel in various organizational positions.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
