<h2>Technical Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Technical Account Manager</b></p>
<p><b>New York City, NY</b></p>
<p><b>Direct Hire</b></p>
<p>Overview</p>
<p>The Technical Account Manager (TAM) works with Customers to seamlessly implement our technology. TAMs also serve as extended members of your team, continuously troubleshooting issues, clearing roadblocks and monitoring overall program health and progress.</p>
<p>Specific services delivered by the TAM include:</p>
<p>· Serving as the primary contact point for our solution questions</p>
<p>· Managing deployments for new Customers and platform updates for existing Customers</p>
<p>· Performing ongoing program health checks, support, training, and sharing of best practices</p>
<p>· Coordinating regular on-site visits</p>
<p>Our TAM team is comprised of security professionals with deep, demonstrable experience implementing a wide variety of leading security products in some of the largest, global enterprise environments.</p>
<p>Partnership</p>
<p>For the customers- TAM is your partner in achieving your implementation goals and collaborating with your team to bring the full resources of our technology to enhance your security goals. They’ll work closely with your team to understand your challenges and concerns and provide recommendations to ensure we exceed your expectations.</p>
<p>Technical Expertise</p>
<p>TAM is a security professional who has deep and demonstrable expertise implementing a wide variety of security products in some of the largest enterprise environments in the world.</p>
<p>The TAM is available, as needed, to provide customized on-site and virtual solution training to ensure you are maximizing the value of our platform.</p>
<p>Continuous Collaboration</p>
<p>Engage your TAM to discuss system performance, risks, deliverables, and progress on open issues to ensure you are on track to achieve your goals.</p>
<p>TAM responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:</p>
<p>· Engage with assigned Customer accounts through regular communication, on-site meetings and executive briefings.</p>
<p>· Lead and drive deployment of our solution within the Customer’s environment</p>
<p>· Provide proactive technical guidance to Customers to drive security operations excellence</p>
<p>· Understand &amp; assess the Technical &amp; Security Value of the Customer and act on any risks uncovered</p>
<p>· Drive best practices and minimize the probability of operational issues occurring across our strategic Customers</p>
<p>· Track &amp; manage any assigned actions for the Customer and ensure that all assigned Customers or potential Customers receive high quality support from all departments within the organization.</p>
<p>· Work closely with other departments &amp; functions within the organization to ensure that clear and concise communication is managed for the Customer.</p>
<p>· Ensure that relevant solution information is continuously communicated to other departments and ensure that the Customer receives documented updates in a timely manner.</p>
<p>· Help Customers understand how they can integrate our technology into their incident response procedures.</p>
<p>TAM responsibilities DO NOT include the following:</p>
<p>· Management and integration of systems other than our solution.</p>
<p>· Ownership of systems and/or processes out of scope of our Customer business agreement.</p>
<p>TAM Hours of Operation:</p>
<p>· TAM will be assigned to a geographical location to best provide services during Customers regular business hours</p>
<p>· Customers with 24/7 Support should work with our support outside of regular business hours in the case of urgent issues. The TAM will coordinate with our Support to take ownership and track any issues to ensure complete coordination of activities related to the Customer.</p>
<p><b>Customer Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Once the location of the TAM is agreed, the Customer will be responsible for T&amp;E/Incidentals for any TAM that are incurred as a result of travel outside of a 50-mile radius from the primary Customer location.</p>
<p>· The Customer will provide access to any systems and facilities that the TAM must use in order to properly execute the tasks they need to accomplish for the Customer</p>
