<p><b>Our Mission</b></p>
<p>
At Palo Alto Networks® everything starts and ends with our mission:</p>
<p>
Being the cybersecurity partner of choice, protecting our digital way of life.</p>
<p>
We have the vision of a world where each day is safer and more secure than the one before. These aren’t easy goals to accomplish – but we’re not here for easy. We’re here for better. We are a company built on the foundation of challenging and disrupting the way things are done, and we’re looking for innovators who are as committed to shaping the future of cybersecurity as we are.</p>
<p><b>
Your Career</b></p>
<p>
As a Systems Engineer, you will run and drive sales engagements into a set of large new enterprise accounts. You will play a key role in navigating complex accounts in order to generate and deliver winning proposals, contract bids, statements of work, and RFI/RFP responses. It is business-critical to meet and exceed sales quotas by building and implementing strategic account plans targeting enterprise-wide deployments of the Palo Alto Networks Next-Generation Security Platform.</p>
<p>
Your role is one of technical guidance – working with your account manager to build integrated solutions for our client’s environments. With your colleagues, you will displace competing technologies, and build market share within your targeted list of major accounts, and most importantly, help your client sleep at night as our product integrates to build secure digital transactions.</p>
<p><b>
Your Impact</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Understand and effectively present our security platform and solutions</li>
<li>
Create and deliver compelling demonstrations of Palo Alto Networks products and solutions</li>
<li>
Educate customers and partners on the modern network security landscape</li>
<li>
Identify and qualify new opportunities and support technical pre-sales activities to opportunity closure</li>
<li>
Expand the Palo Alto Networks install base within existing accounts</li>
<li>
Enable and leverage channel partners and work successfully in a channel-driven sales model</li>
<li>
Create evaluation test plans with customers and lead the evaluation process to a successful conclusion</li>
<li>
Differentiate from industry technologies and solution</li>
<li>
Assist customers through the support escalation process</li>
<li>
Continuous self-improvement and learning to maintain current technical knowledge, participate in technical communities of interest</li>
<li>
Identify problems in customer environments; effectively engages customers at the technical field and technical management levels</li>
<li>
Effective in customer-facing engagement, and in writing, presentation, and creative problem-solving skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Your Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BS in Computer Science or equivalent education</li>
<li>
6+ years of experience as a Systems Engineer or Systems Integrator</li>
<li>
Practical experience working with routing and switching products</li>
<li>
Strong communication and presentation skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>
Ability to analyze complex multivariate problems and use a systematic approach to gain quick resolution</li>
<li>
Experience selling, demonstrating, installing and/or solving network infrastructure security appliances including, but not limited to Firewalls, SSL/IPSec VPNs, Security Proxies and Caches</li>
<li>
Proven experience with systems installation, configuration, and administration of routers/switches, UNIX Linux and Windows-based systems (prior Active Director LDAP experience desirable)</li>
<li>
Experience that emphasizes L2-L4 Networking (L2 Switching architectures including Spanning Tree, IP routing including OSPF and BGP, L4 Load-balancing)</li>
<li>
Industry knowledge of security and network product and technology trends</li>
<li>
Knowledge of competitors solutions and selling strategies</li>
<li>
Understanding of modern datacenter and public cloud infrastructure and technology</li>
<li>
Familiarity with network forensics, incident response, and modern network threats and malware</li>
<li>
Highly skilled with automation and orchestration tools and technologies</li>
<li>
Knowledge of endpoint security technologies and products</li>
<li>
Palo Alto Networks certifications and other industry certifications is helpful</li>
</ul>
