<h2>Manager, Solutions Architecture</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div>At Red Hat, we connect an innovative community of customers, partners, and contributors to deliver an open source stack of trusted, high-performing solutions. We offer cloud, Linux, middleware, storage, and virtualization technologies, together with award-winning global customer support, consulting, and implementation services. Red Hat is a rapidly growing company supporting more than 90% of Fortune 500 companies.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Red Hat Sales team is looking for a Manager of Solutions Architecture team to join us in Plano, TX, supporting the South Central region. In this role, you will provide guidance to a team of technical presales experts responsible for positioning Red Hat’s solutions and services to end customers and partners. Your team will consist of solutions architects that support field sales across our largest customers in the South Central region. As a Manager, you will collaborate with sales management, as well as the Consulting, Engineering, and Support teams, to meet company-outlined sales objectives and assist your team in developing and delivering business-facing messaging on technical subjects. Successful applicants must reside in a state where Red Hat is registered to do business.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary job responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Manage presales technical activities for the development and implementation of Red Hat’s solutions</li>
<li>
Collaborate with sales management on customer engagement strategies and regional go-to-market strategies</li>
<li>
Select, develop, and evaluate team members</li>
<li>
Oversee the development of detailed customer installation and system integration plans</li>
<li>
Provide and oversee presales technical support in sales presentations and solutions demonstrations</li>
<li>
Work with marketing and product managers to provide feedback on Red Hat’s offerings and technologies</li>
<li>
Serve as a company point of contact on specific technical projects with customers</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required skills
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of technical experience that includes enterprise information technology (IT) architecture</li>
<li>
Experience working in or selling to large commercial entities</li>
<li>
Master's degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
Experience with large enterprise environments</li>
<li>
Broad knowledge of current industry tools, trends, and best practices</li>
<li>
Excellent consultative skills; written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work on your own in a fast-paced and growing environment</li>
<li>
Experience managing and coaching solutions architects or other customer-facing team members in best practices and processes</li>
</ul>
