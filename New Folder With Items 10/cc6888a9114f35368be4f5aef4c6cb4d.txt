<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for an experienced Marketing Manager who is driven by creating new and innovative consumer marketing programs in partnership with our retail customers. The ideal candidate will have strong category management expertise in product display, marketing, and promotions to make our products highly visible, accessible, and appealing to consumers in order to maximize velocity and grow sales.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Primary responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Lead development of an integrated shopper marketing strategy. Identify key customers, set budgets, prioritize tactical plans, and establish ROI criteria.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Execute shopper marketing programs with excellence – lead cross functional teams to deliver all components of programs on time and on budget</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Coordinate with the digital marketing team to develop strategies and execution tactics to ensure our brands have a cohesive presence and all consumer activity is aligned</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Lead sales and marketing teams in weekly/monthly calls to coordinate and share all promotional activity and communicate live calendar of events company wide</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<p></p>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b><ul>
<li>
Manages consumer promotion budget and remains accountable for delivering program Volume and ROI KPIs.
</li>
<li>Communicate with brand leaders and sales team to develop and refine innovation and product launch support materials, including sell sheets
</li>
<li>Responsible for managing channel marketing touch points including, sampling, merchandising, loyalty programs, POS execution, shopper marketing programs, and promotional activities.
</li>
<li>Develop trade market needs, such as channel strips and print ad creative, and marketing support materials.</li>
<li>
Develop opportunities of seasonal programs and strategies to achieve retail needs and maximize sell-thru.</li>
<li>
Maintain active list of distribution points on all products to support geotargeted advertising and communication campaigns</li>
<li>
Manage and track coupon and promotional discounting programs</li>
<li>
Facilitate information, image and promotional requests for sales team, brokers and customer service. Maintain library of assets on company SharePoint site.</li>
<li>
Support sales team in the commercialization of private label products. Lead communications with internal stakeholders and private label brand managers to finalize submission of all key documentation and sheparding graphics through our internal approval process for printing.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b><ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree required.
</li>
<li>At least 3 years of Brand, Integrated Marketing, Shopper Marketing and/or Category experience required
</li>
<li>Consumer packaged goods experience required, ideally in food manufacturing
</li>
<li>Previous experience working across Digital Media a plus
</li>
<li>Experience working with Nielsen data and data analysis
</li>
<li>Experience in Field Sales position a plus
</li>
<li>Previous co-marketing experience a strong plus.
</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 25%</li>
<li>
Proven ability to multi-task and organize the management of co-marketing/consumer promotion execution.
</li>
<li>High energy, strong communication, and interpersonal skills to function effectively in a team environment.
</li>
<li>Commitment to results; ability to incorporate new learning into existing plans and act decisively.
</li>
<li>Strong project management skills
</li>
<li>Strong ability to work in teams and build good working relationships
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)<br>
</li>
</ul>
