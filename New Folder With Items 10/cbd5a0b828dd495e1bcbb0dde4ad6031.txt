<h2>Media Planner/Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Pavone Marketing Group, Inc. is in search of a Media Planner/Buyer to work in our Philadelphia, PA office.
<br><br>
Necessary Qualifications
<br><ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>5-7 years of media and media planning/buying experience in both traditional and digital spaces</li>
<li>Strong mathematical abilities</li>
<li>Possess effective written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
Job Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Identify the target audience for a particular media campaign and decide how best to communicate to that audience</li>
<li>Analyze, along with media representatives, proposals and packages for digital and print advertising</li>
<li>Negotiate with sales companies to reach the greatest number of potential customers at the lowest cost to the client</li>
<li>Provide new and profitable ideas to grow clients’ business</li>
<li>Gather, organize and present competitive research and spending data</li>
<li>Provide business analysis to identify risks and opportunities related to lead generation, media optimization, conversion rates and application yield rates</li>
<li>Support execution of marketing solutions across a variety of media channels, including SEM, display, social media, email, affiliate marketing and traditional marketing</li>
<li>Determine appropriate digital or traditional media platform to use for all buys</li>
<li>Participate in client meetings (as requested) regarding media plan presentation and strategy sessions</li>
<li>Work with agency’s budget analyst and brand manager on budget planning</li>
<li>Analyze the effectiveness of the campaign and use this data to inform future campaigns</li>
</ul>
If qualified and interested, please apply with your resume and salary requirement. No phone calls, please.
