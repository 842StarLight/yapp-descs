<h2>Development Specialist I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Join an exciting team of advocates for girls and women at Rise Up, based at The Public Health Institute (PHI)! Rise Up is hiring a Development Specialist.
</p>
<p>Rise Up activates women and girls to transform their lives, families and communities for a more just and equitable world through investment in local solutions, strengthening leadership, and building movements. Since 2009, Rise Up’s powerful network of over 500 leaders has directly benefited 7 million girls, youth, and women, advocating for over 100 laws and policies impacting 115 million people in Africa, Latin America, South Asia, and the U.S. For more information, please visit https://riseuptogether.org
</p>
<p>Rise Up is based at PHI, an independent, nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting health, well-being and quality of life for people throughout California, across the nation and around the world. As one of the largest and most comprehensive public health organizations in the nation, we are at the forefront of research and innovations to improve the efficacy of public health statewide, nationally and internationally. PHI was distinguished as one of the top 50 “Best Non-profit Organizations to Work For” by the Non-Profit Times in a national search. To learn more about our meaningful work visit www.phi.org.</p>
</div>
<br><div>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Rise Up is seeking an experienced Development Specialist to play a vital role on our team at an exciting time of organizational growth and expansion. Reporting to our Development and Partnerships Manager, you will support institutional and individual fundraising efforts. You will also support Rise Up’s communications work as needed working closely with our Communications Associate.
</li>
<li>Development duties include, cultivating donor relationships, tracking and processing donations and acknowledgements, managing the donor and constituent database, coordinating fundraising events. Communications work includes assisting with email marketing, social media, and other contributions to external communications as needed. This position also includes financial duties related to donations processing and event budget management.
</li>
<li>The Specialist will be a strong systems thinker, writer and problem-solver possessing the ability to be extremely detail-oriented with stellar organizational, verbal, and written communication skills. A skilled understanding of donor relationships and fundraising are also beneficial to this position. The applicant should be interested and have experience in helping to grow Rise Up’s development and communications.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Development:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serves as the liaison to our Leadership Council and coordinates quarterly meetings, events, and LC member stewardship and recruitment.
</li>
<li>Supports set-up and build-out of a donor database (Salesforce) and provides ongoing database maintenance and reporting.
</li>
<li>Processes and tracks all donations, liaisons with fiscal sponsor on donations, and administers all acknowledgements.
</li>
<li>Coordinates logistics for fundraising events, including the Annual Breakfast.
</li>
<li>Researches and seeks out new funding sources, opportunities for donor engagement, and coordinates donor appeals.
</li>
<li>Assists in gathering relevant impact data for funder reports, proposals and communications.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Communications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supports monthly newsletter development process and provides ongoing copywriting and editing of collateral, content, and other written communications as needed.
</li>
<li>Supports set-up and build-out of a contact management in database (Salesforce) and provides ongoing contact maintenance and segmentation.
</li>
<li>Manages and maintains contact lists on email platform (Mail Chimp).
</li>
<li>Supports day-to-day social media efforts, including curating and posting content across platforms, developing and distributing social media toolkits + guides, and assisting in overall organizational social media strategy.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br><div>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required</li>
<li>At least one year of experience supporting development and communications efforts in the non-profit or global / public health sector
</li>
<li>Knowledge of and experience with Sales Force or similar CRM
</li>
<li>Strong administration and logistic coordination skills</li>
<li>Excellent writing and relationship building skills</li>
<li>Excellent communication with team members and funders</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work with and in diverse communities and cultures
</li>
<li>Ability to work well independently and on a team in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Commitment to advancing social justice for girls, youth, and women globally
</li>
<li>Advanced computer skills in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, database software
</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and analytical skills</li>
</ul>

</b></p>

</li></ul>
