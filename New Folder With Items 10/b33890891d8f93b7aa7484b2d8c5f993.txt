<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
This position will assist in all aspects of Facility Presentation, specializing as a videographer and video editor. This position will be a highly creative position that will conceptualize and execute creative content that will support the overall creative vision of projects.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for videography and video/audio editing to support the Facilities Presentation department.</li>
<li>Continuously review departmental needs and recommend upgrades, repairs, maintenance or special projects to the Director Facilities Presentation.</li>
<li>Track emerging graphic, animation, video, print and digital media trends and how they might apply to the organization.</li>
<li>Participates in projects as requested by Director Facilities Presentation.</li>
</ul>
Required Qualifications (Job Knowledge, Skills, and Education):
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>A minimum education level of: High School Diploma or its equivalency (Bachelor of Arts/Sciences Degree in art, design, animation, and video production preferred).</li>
<li>Proficiency in a range of software applications such as Adobe Premiere, After Effects and Photoshop. Experience with Adobe Illustrator and other software preferred.</li>
<li>Must be able to manage multiple tasks and projects.</li>
<li>Specific experience as it relates to video/scoreboard production in a 16,000+ seat sports and entertainment facility; organizing and directing event production requirements for sporting and entertainment arena events preferred.</li>
<li>Knowledge and previous experience working with NBA, NHL/AHL, NCAA (hockey and basketball), FELD Entertainment, AEG Live, Live Nation and other concert and family show promoters preferred.</li>
<li>Requires a flexible work schedule, which will include some evenings, weekends and holidays.</li>
<li>Motion design experience is preferred.</li>
<li>Basic knowledge and operation on all video, audio, production and lighting systems preferred.</li>
</ul>
