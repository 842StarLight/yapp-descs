The site merchandising team is responsible for leading the strategy, innovation and development of our customer experience. We're looking for a strategic, collaborative, and highly analytical individual to join site merchandising. The right candidate will demonstrate a winning attitude, be flexible, action and results oriented, analytical and self-starting. They will be responsible for creating, automating and analyzing customer and site data to help improve the fashion experience. This role is critical to ensuring that the Fashion group is powered by data-informed insights at both a tactical and strategic level. The role requires strong independent thinking skills, coupled with the ability to lead the narrative on how to approach and solve key problems the business faces daily. While strong analytical acumen is a must, the ideal candidate must also be able to communicate data and insights to business users and package ideas/results into cohesive, strategic stories. They will be passionate about fashion and creating the best in class online fashion destination.
