JOB SUMMARY
<br><br>
The Edibles Packaging Agent is responsible for full support of packaging for Cresco Labs medically infused edible products. The Agent completes tasks assigned by the Kitchen Head Chef and Packaging Supervisor. Carry out day-to-day tasks including prepping, cleaning, labeling, wrapping and operating basic production and packaging machinery in accordance with the State and standards set by Cresco Labs.
<br><br>
CORE JOB DUTIES
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Executing tasks assigned by the Kitchen Supervisor and/or Manager</li>
<li>Measuring, packaging, and labeling our infused products as directed by Kitchen Supervisor and/or Manager</li>
<li>Maintaining a sanitary work environment</li>
<li>Safely transport products from work space to storage area</li>
<li>Ensure that all functions are performed to the highest standard of quality and compliance</li>
</ul>
REQUIRED EXPERIENCE, EDUCATION, AND SKILLS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong attention to detail and ability to adhere to strict policies and procedure</li>
<li>Ability to measure, package and label in a precise manner</li>
<li>Effective time-management skills and ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, changing and challenging environment</li>
<li>Proficiency in windows-based software and point of sale applications a plus</li>
<li>Ability to work in a consistent 65-degree environment</li>
</ul>
