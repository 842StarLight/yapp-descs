<p>Tuthill Corporation is a privately held, global manufacturer of industrial goods specializing in rotating equipment such as pumps, meters, vacuum systems and blowers. Tuthill’s legacy began in 1892 when James B. Tuthill started a business to manufacture and sell common brick to Chicago construction companies. Today, the corporate office is located in Burr Ridge, IL and houses a range of roles that actively support and guide Tuthill's manufacturing lines of business. Tuthill has remained a family-owned business and now has its fifth generation of leadership in place.</p>

<p>Responsible for partnering with the Tuthill Lines of Business (LOBs), along with the other IT Teams, in support of our 14 Business Processes, moving us forward on our journey to excellence.
