<h2>RN CLINICAL SUPERVISOR</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Clinical Staff Supervisor supervises, directs and coordinates overall operations and duties of clinical staff. Position is responsible for the direct supervision of all the clinical support staff and Registered Nurse using professional skills and knowledge to provide safe and effective telephone advice, patient care instructions and direction to appropriate level of care of CCOC patients. Participates in the interviewing and evaluation process of each department. Communicates policies and procedures. Performs related clerical duties and assists in the general maintenance of equipment, supplies, and exam rooms. Assists physician with all designated aspects of office care and patient care.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS, DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<p>Includes the following but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Serves as clinical support staff supervisor coordinating daily workflow, developing staff schedules, participate in hiring and performing annual evaluations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Trains and supervises clinical support staff in processes necessary to conduct departmental activities; responsible for identifying and implementation of new clinic programs to clinical support area to create an effective patient flow and physician clinic.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Answers referred incoming calls promptly.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Uses good judgment when giving advice to patients.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Redirects calls for advice from a physician and appointment booking when necessary.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Records accurate, concise, legible documentation pertaining to the patient history and phone conversation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Triages patient phone calls that require a level of medical expertise beyond the RN Clinical Desk Clerk’s scope.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Determines the urgency of the patient request; if uncertain, checks with physician.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Handles patient advice calls, discusses test results, answers patient’s questions, provide instruction and patient education.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assists other staff as needed.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE
</b></p>
<p>Current license to practice as Registered Nurse in the State of Ohio</p>
<p>Current BLS for healthcare providers required</p>
<p>Minimum three (3) years experience preferred as a Registered Nurse with demonstrated competence in all aspects of nursing care and independent decision making.</p>
<p>Experience in supervision, telephone triage, emergency medicine, orthopaedics adult/pediatric ambulatory care, home health care, or ICU preferred.</p>

<p><b>COMMUNICATION SKILLS</b></p>
<p>Demonstrated excellent written and oral communication skills and ability to use Windows based computer software.</p>
<p>Ability to communicate effectively with administrative staff, physicians, patients, families, employees, as well as external customers.</p>
<p>Excellent communication skills required both written and verbal, with the ability to communicate effectively with administrative staff, physicians, patients, families, employees, as well as external customers</p>
<br>

<p><b>SKILLS</b></p>
