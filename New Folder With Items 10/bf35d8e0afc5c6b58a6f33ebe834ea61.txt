<p>
As a people manager, you are responsible for supporting the success of not only your direct reports, but the success of all employees within the larger team you are a part by helping to identify development opportunities and supporting team members to achieve their goals. You are expected to know about the members of your extended team and share insights with your peer managers. Look for opportunities to coach and recognize employees directly and provide just-in-time feedback so that employees can reflect on your input immediately.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Presales Director leads and manages a team of dedicated virtual Presales Specialists and/or Principals. The Presales Director provides leadership and mentoring for their team and develops the team members’ solution and sales skills to optimize team performance. A Presales Manager must possess strong solution selling and value-based selling skills. The team has expertise on the components of SAP’s Platform and Technology portfolio.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
In addition to managing a team, The Presales Director is a thought leader in virtual and digital selling techniques. The Director will be a key contributor to the vision and implementation of the operational plan for virtual and digital selling across North America Presales. The Director will closely collaborate with COEs and sales teams to define and successfully drive adoption of an agreed selling motion that translates to achievement of our business drivers and virtual selling initiative objectives.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Team Development Task:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Identify areas of development for each team member and work with each to define and execute a tailored learning plan. Foster an atmosphere of continuous learning and improvement.</p></li>
<li><p>
Monitor the development activities for your team members toward their industry and solution learning goals.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide feedback and coaching to your team members after dry-runs, demos and presentations in order to help them hone their skills.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide formal oral and written feedback to your team members during semi-annual performance management reviews.</p></li>
<li><p>
Propel all your team members toward their revenue targets. Involve each team member in at least one deal per quarter and aim for target quota attainment by most of your team members.</p></li>
<li><p>
Help develop the larger presales organization through leadership of a Solution Hub which provides enablement and a voice of the field to solution management.</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Leadership and Readiness Tasks:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Drive thought leadership on virtual and digital engagement across Presales.</p></li>
<li><p>
Act as a creative and critical thinker who thrives on experimenting and adapting to rapidly shifting markets, customer preferences and go to market strategies.</p></li>
<li><p>
Work closely with other teams (Sales, CoE, Marketing and others) to align on consistent digital approaches and strategies.</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain a high-level knowledge of SAP’s solution suite for the Platform and Technology suite.</p></li>
<li><p>
Understand the key competitors and ensure their team members understand and leverage SAP’s unique value wedges in every customer interaction.</p></li>
<li><p>
Build key relationships and maintain a network of people in the IBU through the Solution Hubs that can be leveraged for market unit/regional support when necessary.</p></li>
<li><p>
Understand fully the demo assets and enablement assets that should be leveraged in the market unit/region and making sure any gaps in needs are addressed.</p></li>
<li><p>
Engage in supporting marketing events in assigned specialization where appropriate.</p></li>
</ul>
