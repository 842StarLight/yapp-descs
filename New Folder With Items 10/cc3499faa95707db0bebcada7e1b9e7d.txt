<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>At Nth Degree, we don’t merely manage trade shows, exhibits, and events. We use them as a means to create loyal customers for our clients. Our job is to craft experiences that help build stronger brands—and we relish it. Whether we’re managing a complex trade show exhibit, driving strategy for an international event, or simply installing a 10×10 booth for one of 1,400 domestic and multinational clients, we’re passionate about what we do. From our headquarters in Atlanta, Georgia, as well as 22 satellite offices across North America and several major European cities, we serve our clients at over 13,000 event and trade show projects annually.</p>
<p>
Nth Degree is home to some of the most tenured and sought-after experts in the industry. Around here, that doesn’t translate to stuffy or unapproachable. Instead, we value transparency, connectedness, and the lives we live outside of the office. We love what we do and who we do it with—because our people are brilliant, humble, and fun!</p>
<p>
We’re seeking a results-driven Account Manager who is every client’s favorite go-to person. A goal-crushing, customer service pro who has the tireless motivation to exceed goals and the innovation to create new ways to do just that. If this sounds like your kind of challenge and we’re your kind of people, let’s talk!</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
How You’ll Add Value</b></p>
<p>
The Account Manager serves as a brand ambassador and is an instrumental player in organizational success. This role offers the opportunity to help grow the business, extend the reach of our solid reputation, and partner in executing the future vision of the business and brand.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
How You’ll Get It Done</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish and consistently meet sales and customer service goals for consulting, creative concept development, logistics management, and labor and on-site project management services</li>
<li>
Monitor sales against forecasts and participate in the determination of potential incremental business for the territory; participate in sales forecasting and planning by researching, developing and maintaining long and short-range marketing plans, attending sales meetings, and assessing, updating, and modifying projected goals to increase revenue and sales; create and review statistical reports and marketing data</li>
<li>
Manage the implementation and delivery of key clients’ activity, maximizing performance and return on investment, and identifying an opportunity to expand</li>
<li>
Build client relationships through service excellence and proactively balance campaign needs; regularly provide updates on new products and services</li>
<li>
Develop existing accounts and add additional accounts to the territory; develop Salesforce pipeline</li>
<li>
Maintain up-to-date knowledge of competitive positioning in the marketplace</li>
<li>
Prepare business plans and sales strategies to identify and qualify new sales or business expansion opportunities and market company products and services within a defined geographic area; follow up on customer inquiries for product offerings, pricing, and delivery information</li>
<li>
Schedule meetings and travel to prospective customer locations to arrange face-to-face appointments and develop excellent rapport; prepare and execute sales presentations to all levels of management</li>
<li>
Write and respond to inquiries to and from vendors, coworkers, and customers on issues relating to products, pricing, discounts, and product availability; act as a liaison with external customers to resolve conflict, address concerns, and answer questions</li>
<li>
Represent the company at industry association meetings and trade shows to promote company and product</li>
<li>
Partner with Account Executive in writing and reviewing 30 and 90 days plans for territory, and in strategically analyzing prospects and existing accounts to maximize the return of selling efforts quarter-by-quarter</li>
<li>
Partner with customer service and operations teams to ensure high customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What You’re Bringing With You</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years’ similar sales experience; event, trade show, or convention experience preferred</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Office and varied database software; familiarity with Salesforce</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in business acquisition and retention</li>
<li>
Exceptional interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Ability to solve problems with a creative, flexible, and resourceful approach</li>
<li>
Superior written and verbal communication skills; ability to produce and edit effective presentations and business copy</li>
<li>
Expert time management and organizational skills with the ability to quickly and gracefully adapt to a rapidly-changing environment</li>
<li>
Ability to travel</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What Success Looks Like</b></p>
<p>
Performance objectives for this role are designed with business acquisition and retention at front of mind. Using strategically efficient project management, sales, and customer service practices will create the opportunity for the Account Manager’s success.</p>
