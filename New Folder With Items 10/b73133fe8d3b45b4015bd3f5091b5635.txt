<h2>Parts Counter Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Bingham Equipment Company </b>is an agriculture, construction, and compact utility equipment sales and rental company currently seeking an experienced Parts Counter salesperson for our Tucson Location who performs a variety of customer service, in store, marketing, promoting, advertising and stocking duties related to the receiving, sale and delivery of parts and accessories that contribute to the efficient, safe, and profitable operation of the dealerships parts department. Ongoing responsibility for assisting with the counter parts sales and customer service responsibilities.</p>
<p><b>Major Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>Parts Department: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sells products and/or services to repair the problem and meet the customer's needs.</li>
<li>Maintain customer goodwill by greeting and acknowledge all customers he/she comes in contact with.</li>
<li>Assist in keeping parts department clean and orderly.</li>
<li>Record all sales and/or customer transactions that occur.</li>
<li>Provide service technicians with parts as required with exceptional speed.</li>
<li>Assist with Parts Manager in merchandising displays.</li>
<li>Maintains the retail sale floor and displays to meet or exceed the dealerships standards and today's customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Controls showroom inventory to ensure maximum sales and customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Performs routine housekeeping tasks that maintain and enhances the cleanliness of products and the retail sales area.</li>
<li>When working the parts counter, follows prescribed cash, credit, and check processing procedures of the dealership</li>
<li>Processes cores, warranties, and new return merchandise as required at a point of sale.</li>
<li>Attends training as prescribed by management to maintain proficiency in his/her job description.</li>
<li>Uses dealership equipment and resources safely and proficiently.</li>
<li>Performs other tasks as required by Parts Manger.</li>
<li>Keeps himself/herself groomed and clothed per the dealership guidelines.</li>
<li>Verify receiving documents.</li>
<li>Follow up on shortages and expedite by reporting to Parts Manager.</li>
<li>Should be knowledgeable in merchandising</li>
</ul>
