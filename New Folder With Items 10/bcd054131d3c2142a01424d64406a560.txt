<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div>
The US National CTO Cloud Solution Architect (CSA) Team is looking for a talented individual to lead a team of deeply technical Cloud Solution Architects (CSA) to drive growth of Azure Services for Enterprise accounts across the US in different industries.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Key responsibilities include:</div>
<div>
This CSA Manager role will directly manage full time employee CSAs that will engage prioritized Enterprise customers across the US. This is an exciting role that will help fuel Azure Customer success through customer retention and growth.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Key responsibilities include:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Hire, on-board, develop, coach, mentor and evaluate CSA. Set a clear bar for excellence. Build a high performing team that will be diverse and inclusive</li>
<li>
Must possess exceptional cross-team collaboration and communication skills. Partner with all required Microsoft stakeholders in the subsidiary to drive CSA role clarity, account coverage, project assignment and effective cross-team collaboration</li>
<li>
Coach and enable your team to accelerate Azure projects to production, including guidance on architectural design, technical recommendations, blocker escalations and technical resource orchestration
</li>
<li>Ensure your team delivers high quality production deployments that are resilient and successful</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Drive operational excellence, including timely consumption opportunity management and high-quality reporting and insights</li></ul>
