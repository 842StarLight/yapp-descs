<p><b>Title/Billing Specialist
</b></p>
<p>Ford of Boerne has an outstanding opportunity for a results-focused, highly driven and experienced Title-Billing Specialist. The purpose of the Title-Billing Specialist is to process titles and registrations as well as process car deals by verifying completeness of documentation.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Verifies data, completes state forms, makes disbursement for taxes and fees, and submits to the state</li>
<li>Processes new car and pre owned registrations on dealerâ€™s software system</li>
<li>Processes pre-owned vehicle title transfers</li>
<li>Prepares and distributes title envelopes</li>
<li>Maintains follow-up on all incoming titles</li>
<li>Maintains and reconciles schedules for tax, title and license payable and lien payoffs</li>
<li>Identifies internal control issues by reviewing existing policies, verifying changes in authorized personnel, and communicating issues to the Assistant Controller</li>
<li>Maintains error logs</li>
<li>Processes payoffs on trade-ins and floor plan, pulls contracts, and forwards to financial institutions</li>
<li>Verifies Finance and Insurance (F&amp;I) data to documents</li>
<li>Reconciles inventory schedules as assigned on a weekly and monthly basis</li>
<li>Reconciles and completes sales consultant payroll semi-monthly</li>
<li>Verifies Finance and Insurance (F&amp;I) logs to accounting data before month-end</li>
<li>Processes rebates</li>
<li>Attends weekly department meetings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience
</b></p>
<p>High School Diploma or General Education Diploma; and six months related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. Dealership experience is perffered.</p>
