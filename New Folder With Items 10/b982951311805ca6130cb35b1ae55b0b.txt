<h2>Senior Director of Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>COMPANY OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>Routeware is shaping the future of the waste industry as a leading provider of fleet automation and analytics software. Based in the Portland, Oregon metro area, our 20-year-old company continues to grow and celebrate successes in delivering high quality fleet automation management software to municipalities and companies of all sizes. Our culture is one of impact within a dynamic, collaborative environment that appreciates innovation, initiative, and creative problem solving. We provide a casual yet professional work environment with a standard of excellence, designed to bring the best out of our team members.</p>
<p><b>POSITION OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a driven and experienced Senior Director of Marketing who is passionate about delivering impactful, on-brand messaging to a wide variety of audiences across multiple channels and platforms. You will be at the center of today’s hottest technologies as our products span software, hardware, Software-as-a-Service, and Cloud technologies in a desktop and mobile environment. In joining our dynamic and fast-paced environment, you will work with cross-functional teams to design, build and roll-out products that serve the company’s vision and strategy.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>The Senior Director of Marketing will be responsible for leading and overseeing all strategic and development aspects of branding, marketing communications, lead generation, product launch, and management of the company’s image. To be successful, you will focus on pipeline and revenue outcomes (not just top of funnel), think strategically and in terms of long-running and repeatable programs. Overall, you will serve as “editor in chief” to ensure that our content is engaging, driving marketing results and propelling our customers through each stage of the sales funnel.</p>
<p><b>TASKS AND OPPORTUNITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee all web advertising content for high-traffic, lead generating inbound channels (social, email, blog, landing pages, etc.) working with direct reports responsible for day to day execution</li>
<li>Managing product management/marketing</li>
<li>Website content creation and layout ensuring all written content is on-brand</li>
<li>Develop, implement, analyze and constantly refine our customer acquisition program strategy to guarantee effective and efficient content that articulates our core benefits and points of differentiation</li>
<li>Understand and develop generation campaigns that encompass the entire buying journey</li>
<li>Establish best practices, tools, and processes for promoting and executing an integrated marketing plan that helps Routeware differentiate and stand out in the marketplace</li>
<li>Develop strategies for inbound and outbound campaigns – incorporating email marketing, SEO/ SEM, social website, display advertising, live events, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>Our ideal candidate will be a high-energy, high-output individual that possesses many of the following skills and qualities:</p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of enterprise/mid-market B2B marketing experience</li>
<li>Experience with software and SaaS companies that sell into the enterprise/mid-market</li>
<li>Experience with marketing automation (e.g. Pardot, Marketo, HubSpot, Eloqua, Act-On) knowledge and Salesforce</li>
<li>Excellent presentation skills utilizing systems including MS Office, Word, PowerPoint, and Excel</li>
<li>Prior experience managing outside PR agencies and analyst relations</li>
<li>Strong analytical and financial skills; must have budget management experience</li>
<li>Possess good interpersonal and management/leadership skills</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Passion to work in a small, entrepreneurial company</li>
<li>Strong computer and Internet skills</li>
<li>Ability to develop/edit compelling, succinct, error-free marketing materials and campaign messaging</li>
<li>An undergraduate degree in a related field is a plus and an MBA strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
