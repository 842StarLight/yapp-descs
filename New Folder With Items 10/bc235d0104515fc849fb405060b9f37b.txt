<h2>Chief Technology Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Upserve is looking for a Chief Technology Officer (CTO) to lead the company’s technology organization, with responsibility for the vision, leadership, and implementation of overall technology strategy and development and test processes. The role will require a passionate, business-oriented technology leader who can provide strategic leadership and management in the design and development of the major technical initiatives that support strategic business programs and facilitate revenue growth. The ideal candidate will be able to get into the details while providing strategic, technical, and operating expertise and guidance, and bringing creativity, vision, and passion to the technology organization. We are looking for a CTO who is a customer focused engineering leader with a sharp product sense to lead our Engineering and Product teams.</p>
<p>
About the Role:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead Upserve’s technical teams to develop and execute on a product and platform vision for the company. Identify opportunities and risks for delivering the company’s services through technology and innovation. Assess obstacles and technical hurdles to success and recommend solutions.</li>
<li>Position the company through cutting-edge software, and other enterprise-wide applications and their alignment with the strategic direction of the business.</li>
<li>Establish and implement short and long-term departmental goals, objectives, policies, and operating procedures.</li>
<li>Lead technology &amp; business planning, project management and business analysis to achieve business goals by identifying and prioritizing development initiatives and setting timetables for the evaluation, development, and deployment of all technology-based services.</li>
<li>Develop enterprise objectives and budgets to facilitate the orderly and efficient capture, storage, processing, and dissemination of information.</li>
<li>Provide leadership for infrastructure &amp; security teams ensuring high availability, security and scalability. Implement cloud solutions and evaluate solutions that expand the reach of the organization.</li>
<li>Define technical architecture &amp; set vision for software development teams to ensure projects are delivered in a timely manner with expected user functionality and reliability.</li>
<li>Identify, evaluate, and execute new and innovative solutions and technologies that have proven results in the market pertaining to profitability, competitive advantage, cost savings, and ability to positively scale the Technology department with company growth.</li>
<li>Maintain a proactive information security posture of all technology systems.</li>
<li>Responsible for the development, review, and certification of all back-up, business continuity, and disaster recovery procedures and plans.</li>
<li>Evaluate overall operations and technology functions and recommend enhancements.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Responsibilities Include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluating and implementing improvements to the current system for ongoing innovation and disruption in the industry.</li>
<li>Discover and implement new technologies that yield competitive advantage</li>
<li>Developing the company’s strategy for using technological resources</li>
<li>Ensuring technologies are used efficiently, profitably and securely</li>
<li>Supervise system infrastructure to ensure functionality and efficiency</li>
<li>Build quality assurance and data protection processes</li>
<li>Monitor KPIs and budgets to assess technological performance</li>
<li>Use stakeholders’ feedback to inform necessary improvements and adjustments to technology</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Skills &amp; Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience as a CTO or similar leadership role</li>
<li>Proven ability to conduct technological analyses and research</li>
<li>BSc/BA in Computer Science, Engineering or a related field; MBA or other relevant graduate degree is a plus</li>
<li>The ideal candidate will have functional depth in technology and product development across all core competencies and prior experience as a CTO/CIO or as a second in command.</li>
<li>Ability to lead and inspire technology professionals to succeed in a fast changing and highly competitive market, keeping the team focused on strategies and tactics to best leverage the resources of a growing company.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate and collaborate with the non-technical parts of the business to get the entire company on the same page with regard to the roadmap and any challenges that arise.</li>
<li>Maintain explicit efforts to share knowledge between functions and departments outside of Engineering/Product.</li>
<li>10+ years of proven success as a complex technological environments with increasing responsibility in product development leadership.<br>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
