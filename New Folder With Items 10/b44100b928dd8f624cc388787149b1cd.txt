<h2>Manager of Community Outreach & Strategic Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ABOUT POWERMARKET</b></h4>
<p>PowerMarket is a leader in the community solar industry, providing utilities, developers, and energy retailers the indispensable digital tools necessary to manage and administer its community solar projects and subscribers. You will be part of the clean energy revolution when you work for PowerMarket, helping our world redefine the way we interact with our energy. We are looking for team members that are passionate, humble, and hungry. You will be working in a fast-paced environment with unprecedented opportunity to grow and advance as the company scales. We work out of the Urban Future Lab, an exclusive cleantech NYC incubator located in downtown Brooklyn, with incredible views of the Manhattan skyline. We are looking for a fearless Manager of Community Outreach &amp; Strategic Partnerships to join our team and crush it.
</p>
<p>As the Manager of Community Outreach &amp; Strategic Partnerships, you will take a leading role in all facets of PowerMarket's community solar subscriber <i>services,</i> including the engagement, acquisition, and support of retail and commercial subscribers across PowerMarket's community solar portfolio. You will have the opportunity to develop strategic partnerships with mission-aligned organizations and grow our subscriber base through various marking activities and campaigns. This position is for a professional and entrepreneurial-minded team member who is looking to be challenged and shape the future of the business.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Lead and manage PowerMarket's subscriber acquisition campaigns across its community solar portfolio.</li>
<li>Effectively communicate complex solar and energy industry concepts to retail customers with expert gravitas, but in terms that customers can understand.</li>
<li>Conduct market research and analysis to optimize acquisition services.</li>
<li>Develop top-of-funnel relationships with strategic partners and collaborate on creative marketing and brand awareness.</li>
<li>Manage and support subscribers as they move through enrollment and onboarding.</li>
<li>Work in cross-functional teams as necessary to improve processes and drive strategic direction of the acquisition teams.</li>
<li>Create community solar training materials and manage social media accounts.</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Passionate about community solar and the clean energy future.</li>
<li>Proven experience in strategic partnerships, <i>marketing,</i> and/or outbound sales and support.
</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of marketing elements (including traditional and digital marketing such as SEO/social media) and market research methods.</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial spirit with a creative mindset.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in <i>marketing,</i> communications, or equivalent professional experience.</li>
<li>Experience with basic software technologies like HTML and the Internet generally.</li>
<li>Desired 3 - 5 years of relevant experience.</li>
</ul>
