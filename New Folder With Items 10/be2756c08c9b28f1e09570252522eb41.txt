<h2>Macy's Bayfair Center, San Leandro, CA: Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Job Overview:</b><br>
<div>As a Sales Manager, you will support My Macy's by driving sales and selling with focus on the Macy's customer. All activities related to presenting a clean, neat and organized shopping environment for our customers are under your direction. With training, coaching and development, a team of Associates will grow following your leadership.</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Key Accountabilities:</b><br>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Sales</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Drive and exceed sales goals by executing Macy's initiatives</li>
<li>Lead the push toward selling through coaching and recognition</li>
<li>Review and utilize reports; implement action plans focusing on deficient areas</li>
<li>Identify best sellers and key items; communicate merchandise needs to optimize the My Macy's process</li>
<li>Ensure accuracy of promotional presentation; communicate advertising and sales information to Associates</li>
<li>Partner with Merchandising team to plan and execute floor moves, merchandise placement and sales set-up</li>
<li>Review your personal Selling Area Scorecard monthly for your Associate Turnover, Sales &amp; Star Rewards results</li>
<li>Strengthen attendance and weekend hours compliance among staff</li>
<li>Motivate Associates to solicit our Star Rewards Program by reinforcing the benefits of new accounts</li>
<li>Establish high level of shortage awareness and effectiveness; ensure that all policies and procedures are implemented</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Customer</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Coach Associates to consistently deliver effective selling behaviors resulting in high customer engagement</li>
<li>Review &amp; utilize Associate Scorecards to provide recognition; develop and communicate strategies to improve results</li>
<li>Observe Associates twice daily through formal observations focusing on selling behaviors; ensure Associates are providing an outstanding shopping experience to all customers</li>
<li>Coach to Associate Scorecard metrics weekly through daily informal observations defining one focus metric and behaviors to improve an individual metric</li>
<li>Ensure optimum sales floor coverage and lead selling initiatives throughout the store as needed</li>
<li>Maintain high customer readiness standards; deliver a clean, neat and easy to shop store environment</li>
</ul>
<div><b>People</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Recruit a qualified team of selling-focused Associates; build a bench for future advancements &amp; promotions</li>
<li>Ensure all Associate training is conducted on a timely basis, ensure assigned mentors engage with new hires</li>
<li>Coach Associates on product knowledge by holding in-store product training with Vendor Representatives</li>
<li>Actively fill open positions prioritizing internal Associates in Commission, Specialist and High Level selling areas</li>
<li>Engage in Macy's recognition program; reward Associates with recognition cards</li>
<li>Meet with Associates in department weekly; identify top sellers and talk about opportunities</li>
<li>Conduct ongoing Talent Analysis of Associates and establish career progression plans for key players and positions to result in retention of best people and turnover reduction</li>
<li>Utilize review process as a tool for Associate talent development, promotion and advancement</li>
<li>Monitor and address performance issues on a timely basis</li>
<li>Address Associate concerns in a reasonable and fair manner, consistent with the values expected by the Company</li>
<li>Lead team to support giving back to the local community helping create stronger, healthier places to live and work</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Skills Summary:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 1-3 years in a leadership/supervisory position in a service-driven environment</li>
<li>Ability to empower and develop a team</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate and function as a member of a team</li>
<li>Ability to execute plans and strategies</li>
<li>Strong leadership, interpersonal and communication skills</li>
<li>Highly organized and able to adapt quickly to changing priorities</li>
<li>Ability to anticipate and solve problems, act decisively and persist in the face of obstacles</li>
<li>Commitment to exemplifying the highest integrity and professional business standards</li>
</ul>
