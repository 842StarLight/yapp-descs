<div>
At our Company, we grow People, Brands, and Businesses! We are seeking a very talented Art Director who will be a key creative team member. Responsible for supporting the creative team, taking concepts and creating tangible reality while seeing the development and execution of creative projects through completion. Accountable for delivering quality creative products that maintain the integrity of the art concept. Will interface with all levels of the internal team, outside vendors, and work closely with account, production, and copywriters.</div>

<div>The Company is one of North America’s leading sales and marketing agencies specializing in outsourced sales, merchandising, category management and marketing services to manufacturers, suppliers and producers of food products and consumer packaged goods. We bridge the gap between manufacturers and retailers, providing consumers access to the best products available in the marketplace today.</div>
