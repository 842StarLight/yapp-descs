<h2>Digital Sales Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB DESCRIPTION
</h1>
<div>
<p>KFMB Stations Group, a TEGNA Company, has an amazing opportunity for an individual that's looking to grow professionally, and join a talented team of digitally-minded marketers.
</p>
<p>The <b>Digital Sales Specialist</b> will support the Sales Department and oversee digital advertising campaigns including front-end research, pre-sales strategy, order entry/trafficking, creative management, campaign optimization and reporting.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide research and strategy recommendations for various digital solutions to Account Executives</li>
<li>Organize preliminary audience &amp; demographic data for various digital solutions including: Social Ads, Targeted Display/Video, Targeted Emails, PPC and Over-The-Top (OTT) campaigns</li>
<li>Create compelling proposals for Account Executives</li>
<li>On-board all digital campaigns, collecting assets and trafficking new and existing projects to the various fulfillment teams</li>
<li>Draft and approve insertion orders/contracts for specified campaigns and flight dates</li>
<li>Collaborate with Digital Account Managers to monitor campaign performance for: Social Ads, Targeted Display/Video, Targeted Email, PPC and OTT using multiple reporting platforms</li>
<li>Provide client-facing reports for all designated accounts</li>
<li>Work with the sales team to create client solutions that maximize results</li>
<li>Work with corporate ad operations to schedule campaigns while adhering to service level agreements (SLAs)</li>
<li>Work with clients to overcome technical, ad-related issues</li>
<li>Oversee campaign delivery and fulfillment</li>
</ul>
</div>

<p><b>About TEGNA:</b></p>
<p>TEGNA Inc. (NYSE: TGNA) is an innovative media company that serves the greater good of our communities. Murrow, George Polk, Alfred I. DuPont and Emmy Awards. TEGNA also delivers innovative and unparalleled solutions for advertisers through TEGNA Marketing Solutions (TMS). TMS is a one-stop shop that helps businesses thrive through an unmatched suite of services and solutions that reach consumers across television, email, social and over-the-top (OTT) platforms, including Premion, TEGNA’s OTT advertising service. Across platforms, TEGNA tells empowering stories, conducts impactful investigations and delivers innovative marketing solutions. For more information, visit www.TEGNA.com.</p>
