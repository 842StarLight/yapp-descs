<h2>Warehouse Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>The Warehouse Assistant will be responsible for the logistics to job sites throughout Texas and/or multiple states. The position will also be responsible for processing, organizing, and storing inventory. The Warehouse Assistant will pull, package and prepare orders for delivery to job sites as directed by Warehouse Manager. The ideal candidate is comfortable with travel, growing in a new upcoming industry, focused and experienced, and/or willing to learn the logistics, inventory software, databases and electrical systems that are used in the solar industry.</p>
<p><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Operate heavy machinery like forklifts to move or store inventory.</li>
<li>Deliver equipment and materials to job sites using large box truck. Unload truck with hydraulic jack. Obtain signatures for receipt of equipment and materials.</li>
<li>Receiving, moving, checking and storing incoming inventory/materials.</li>
<li>Accurately pull, package and prepare orders for delivery.</li>
<li>Basic competency in troubleshooting electrical systems or a willingness to learn electrical systems.</li>
<li>Ability to work with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Ability to do on-site inspections.</li>
<li>Ability to accurately depict on-site visual inspections to Warehouse Manager over the phone or by email.</li>
<li>Work with Sales, Design, and Installation Teams to ensure the logistics of equipment and materials to job sites to meet deadlines.</li>
</ul>
