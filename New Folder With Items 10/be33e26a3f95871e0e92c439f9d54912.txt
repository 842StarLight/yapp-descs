<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Role: </b></p>
<p>To be a primary contact forresidents and prospective residents by leasing apartments, coordinating resident activities and building and maintaining resident relations.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Responsible for all activities related to apartment rentals, move-ins and lease renewals, including generating and handling traffic, leasing apartments, qualifying prospects, preparing lease documentation and completing move-in procedures inaccordance with the company’s and the community’s established policies and procedures.</p>
<p>· Provide high quality resident service and convey resident service requests to the Service Manager and other service associates.</p>
<p>· Assisting the Property Manager in gathering and compiling current market and economic information and preparing competitive and market analysis and market trends.</p>
<p>· Handling telephone and on-site traffic and manage them correctly in our Lead Management Systems.</p>
<p>· Show“ready” apartments, closing the lease, qualifying prospects, completing all related paperwork and activities to ensure a timely move-in.</p>
<p>· Execute100% lease compliance accuracy</p>
<p>· Maintain 95% occupancy</p>
<p>· Trackall leads through lead processing system</p>
<p>· Complywith all state, federal and/or local laws relating to Fair Housing.</p>
<p>· Buildand maintain resident relations.</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Strong commitment to company fundamentals and values – The Berger Way</p>
<p>· Self-directed and proactive.</p>
<p>· Strong customer service skills and professional phone manner.</p>
<p>· Demonstrate excellent organizational skills with the ability to prioritize and work effectively on multiple tasks in a fast-paced, demanding environment.</p>
<p>· Be dependable, reliable and follow through on commitments, producing timely work to required or higher standard.</p>
<p>· Be attentive to detail and monitoring adherence to Company standards and policies.</p>
<p>· Positive, “can-do” attitude, professionalism and a high level ofenthusiasm</p>
<p>· Demonstrated proficiency with Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook.</p>
<p>· Must possess strong Excel and general computer skills.Property Management specific software experience of some kind required e.g.,ResMan, I love Leasing, Onesite, etc.</p>
<p>· Knowledge of Fair Housing laws a plus.</p>
<p>· High School Diploma required. Associate’s Degree or some college level courses preferred.</p>
<p>· Experience in the apartment management and leasing industry is preferred .</p>
<p>· 2-3 Saturday's per month is required.</p>
