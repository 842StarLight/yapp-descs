<h2>PUBLIC CONTRACTS & PROPERTY MANAGEMENT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>POSITION WILL BEGIN IN MARCH 2020.</b></p>
<p>Manages all property owned or leased by Forrest Health including ensuring timely payments owed and due under lease arrangements. Assist VP in real estate transactions and accounting of real property and appropriately update/tracks the FMV of relevant real property. Also serves as the Contracts and Legal Department’s (the “Department”) contract mangagement administrator and works under the direction of the Vice President and General Counsel (“VP”) to facilitate and maintain an accurate and up-to-date central repository for all contracts and legal documents that have an impact on the operations of Forrest Health. Will oversee and manage the contract management system database (software) as well as user profiles, which would include granting access, deleting users, and resetting passwords. Responsible for the daily operations of the contract management system. Assist VP in communicating with vendors and updating regulatory documents such as Business Associate Agreements and Standard Contract Addenda. Develop and maintain repository for Forrest Health’s archived agreements. Provide list of current contracts that are to be presented to, and approved by, the Hospital’s Board of Trustees and make initial determination on whether such contract is subject to, or exempt from, the Open Meetings Act. Coordinate and track Forrest Health’s DNV requirement of evaluating “all” system agreements and perform reporting functions as determined necessary by DNV upon annual audits. Assists in daily operations of Department as needed.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Performance Expectations are as follows:</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate the ability to organize and track Forrest Health’s contracts in the draft and signature process</li>
<li>Demonstrate the ability to manage Forrest Health’s collective agreements in a contract management software and stay abreast of key dates, including dates of expiration and/or notice periods</li>
<li>Demonstrate the aptitude to work with other Forrest Health leaders in the review and/or initiating action for Forrest Health contracts</li>
<li>Demonstrate the aptitude for efficiently organizing and house annual contract evaluations as required by Forrest Health’s accreditation entity</li>
<li>Demonstrate the ability to facilitate and effectuate real estate transactions, including acquisitions, sales, leases, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>Education: Bachelor degree in business, economics, or real estate preferred. Work experience may substitute for some collegiate studies. Required to be able to operate a personal computer, various software programs, and modern office equipment. Required to as well as Microsoft Office products. Required to be able to assume responsibility for daily activities and projects without direct supervision. Organizational/planning skills are required. Required to communicate daily with Forrest Health leaders, staff, and vendors to coordinate and track contract requests, contract documents, and renewal/termination of contracts.</p>
<p>Work Experience: A minimum of three years of experience working in a professional office setting utilizing computers, office equipment, and data processing systems is required. Experience working in real estate field including real property deeds, land descriptions and leases of real property.</p>
