<h2>Sales and Marketing Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary: </b></p>
<p>The Marketing/Sales Assistant supports Marketing with Advertising and Sales on Campaigns, Leads and Forms to include: marketing calendar tasks, implementing marketing/sales campaigns, social media campaigns, assistance with lead generation and on collecting and interpreting marketing and sales analytics.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with the development and maintain content and communications for Marketing and Sales campaigns, activities and events.</li>
<li>Conduct frequent research to obtain content specifically for publication and sharing across different media channels, including social media posts, email, guides, brochures, and newsletters.</li>
<li>Assist with copy-writing for blogs and/or any electronic communication as needed.</li>
<li>Support Sales and Marketing by helping to manage events and associated campaigns for any upcoming conferences.</li>
<li>Assist with the maintenance of the CRM system.</li>
<li>Qualify leads for Sales.</li>
<li>Create and post marketing event updates through internal communication channels.</li>
<li>Research relevant sources for content, such as scientific publications, industry media and social media outlets.</li>
<li>Keep up to date with industry specific trends and activities that are utilized to communicate with customers.</li>
<li>Create Corp ID in database and LOAs, LOCs, NDSs, and MSAs/Contracts.</li>
<li>Update corporate information in database utilizing Pipe Drive as a reference point.</li>
<li>Qualify Sales lead lists.</li>
<li>Record results from required tasks and surveys in SmartSheet.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent required; Bachelors of Arts/Science in Business or related field preferred.</li>
<li>2-3 years’ experience in a Sales/Marketing Admin role preferred</li>
<li>Keen ability to multitask</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills with the ability to effectively interface with all levels and departments (internal and external) on a formal, informal, written and verbal basis.</li>
<li>Strong overall business and people skills, including planning, presentation skills and business acumen.</li>
<li>Microsoft Word, Excel, Power Point along with Publisher a must.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to:</li>
<li>Flourish in a fast paced, deadline driven work environment.</li>
<li>Think creatively and act independently within scope of assigned responsibilities converse, listen and take notes simultaneously.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Windows, Word, Excel, Outlook and Internet (Microsoft Office Suite); able to PowerPoints, emails and other departmentally required documents. Willingness and ability to learn the Company’s proprietary database program.</li>
</ul>
