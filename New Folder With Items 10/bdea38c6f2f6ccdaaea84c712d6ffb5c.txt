<h2>System Engineer/Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b>Description</b><br>
Systems Engineers (pre-sales engineers) are software experts who work alongside Area Sales Managers to structure and conduct evaluations of OpenFLEX 's products by its potential customers. Systems Engineers perform a wide range of duties; from delivering technical presentations and demonstrations to managing the evaluation process, conducting detailed requirements analyses, understanding the competitive marketplace, performing technical account management, and coding/delivering proof-of-concept and prototype projects. The most desirable applicants will have strong experience with Object Oriented software, a good foundation in software implementation, and experience in working with customers. Currently successful Systems Engineers at OpenFLEX have come from positions as software engineers, post-sales consultants, product managers, technical support engineers, technical trainers, and (of course) pre-sales engineers.</p>
<p>OpenFLEX considers Systems Engineers to be critical to our company's success. Systems Engineers will own 50% of the decisions and responsibilities in the sales process. OpenFLEX SE's will be expected to take great initiative to understand customers' problems and deliver viable solutions. In exchange, they will be rewarded by working in a fast-paced, successful start-up environment to service some of the highest profile projects and customers in hi-tech today.</p>
