<h2>Enterprise Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for a talented and competitive Account Executive that is excited at the opportunity to thrive and grow at a high-growth, major VC backed startup. This AE will play a fundamental role in achieving our ambitious customer acquisition and revenue growth objectives. We love an entrepreneurial spirit and someone who wants to come in and grow our company and build their personal brand. You are competitive, want to be the best, but also a team player that makes those around you better.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Qualify new sales opportunities through inbound lead follow-up</li>
<li>Prospect cold leads to generate interest and new contacts to fill your funnel</li>
<li>Research accounts, identify key players and build relationships</li>
<li>Demonstrate our innovative Sales CRM product and align value to customers pain points</li>
<li>Close sales and achieve quarterly quotas</li>
<li>Maintain and expand your list of assigned prospects</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years relative experience</li>
<li>Track record of over-achieving quota</li>
<li>Competitive team player who wants to be the MVP and help the entire team succeed</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, prioritize, and manage time effectively</li>
<li>Desire to quickly climb the ranks and grow into a senior sales role or sales leadership position</li>
<li>Experience selling CRM SaaS a plus</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
