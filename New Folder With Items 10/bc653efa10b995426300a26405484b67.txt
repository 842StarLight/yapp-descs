<h2>Field Service Tech V, Mid-Atlantic</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About Mettler Toledo
<br>Working at Mettler Toledo means you are impacting the world in important ways. Our precision measuring devices span the world's laboratories, production facilities, and retail stores. We manufacture, sell, and service instruments that detect, measure, and weigh - from the infinitely small to the largest of vehicles or airplanes. * We help feed the world. We impact how food is produced, packaged and made safe. * We help keep you healthy. Our instruments make it possible for scientists to develop new drugs and bring life-saving medicines to the world. * Mettler Toledo products are important to the production and consistent quality of every kind of consumer product. Mettler Toledo Rainin manufactures pipettes, one of the most basic tools used in every type of chemistry laboratory and many kinds of biological laboratories. Rainin's pipettes consistently rank among the top premium pipette brands in North America, Europe, Asia and the Middle East.
<br>Our Opening and Your Responsibilities
<br>This position will be responsible for direct support of Rainin's On-Site service program. Works at customer sites within a defined geographic region servicing and calibrating Rainin and other brands of pipettes. Conducts mechanical and electronic measuring, recording, and inspection on pipettes serviced to ensure conformance to established specifications. Assists the Area Service Leader by providing front line technical and administrative support to Field Service Technicians in the assigned territory. Team Lead Responsibilities: * Providing on the job training &amp; mentoring new hires while working onsite. * Train techs assigned to them in completing timecards, expense reports, and other monthly reports. Ensuring these reports are submitted in a timely manner. * Facilitate completion &amp; timely submission of any assigned trainings by Field Service Technicians in the assigned territory. * Work with the Onsite Coordinator to ensure all equipment for team members is maintained and serviced in a timely manner. * Ensure Proficiency Testing Data for team members are completed &amp; submitted on time. * Create Snow tickets whenever necessary to address any software or hardware related issues within the team. * Respond to daily work/service related questions from team members. Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned. * Following daily service visits schedule through careful coordination with their Area Service Leader. * Drives to customer sites to provide onsite pipette service according to the service schedule, including use of personal vehicle when needed. * While on site at customer facilities, the Field Service Tech provides pipette repair and calibration services. * Representing Rainin to customer in a professional and positive manner. * Ability to work independently with minimal supervision or as part of a small team. * Completes service reports and expense reports and is responsible for maintaining service equipment and parts inventory. * Provides Rainin Sales with customer leads for potential new product purchases * When available, assists in Service Center production activities as assigned by the Area Service Leader * Travel out of local area of up to 50% of the time required to support the business, including overnight travel up to 25% of the time.
<br>What You Need to Succeed
<br><ul>
<li>Driving License with good driving record * High school diploma or general education degree (GED); minimum of two years related experience with pipette calibration or equivalent combination of education and prior work experience. * Previous knowledge or work experience with pipettes preferred. * Must have strong interpersonal and verbal (English) communication skills. * Must have good written communication skills, as there will be frequent email communication with customers and associates and daily completion of service reports. * Must have good mechanical aptitude and the ability to disassemble and reassemble small precision mechanical instruments. * Strong organizational skills to successfully develop and schedule, process, and complete multiple tasks.</li>
</ul>
