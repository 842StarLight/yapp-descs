<p>LPi is seeking qualified Advertising Sales Executives to make an impact in local communities through print and digital advertising. We are looking for a <b>bilingual sales professional </b>that will want to drive new business development and make an immediate impact on local communities.</p>

<p><b>WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Someone that loves to be the phone calling B2B prospects.</li>
<li>An individual that displays natural entrepreneurial attributes with a winning and competitive spirit.</li>
<li>Someone that has an appetite for learning and has exceptional organizational skills.</li>
<li>An individual that can meet sales goals and doesn’t take rejection personally.</li>
<li>Someone that has a commitment to a high standard of integrity and trustworthiness.</li>
<li>An individual that is hardworking, passionate and has a servant leadership mentality.</li>

<li>Proven ability to self-manage in an autonomous work environment</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, analytical, and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license, proof of insurance and reliable transportation - REQUIRED</li>
<li>Proven successful track record of phone-based sales - PREFERRED</li>
</ul>
