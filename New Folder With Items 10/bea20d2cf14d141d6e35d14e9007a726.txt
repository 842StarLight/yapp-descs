<h2>Accounting Manager (must be bilingual in Japanese/English)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>This position will manage the accounting staff and operations of the Accounting department. He/she will be responsible for the supervision of non-exempt employees who maintain general and subsidiary ledgers of the facility, prepares operating and financial statements, and reconcile bank statements. Other subordinate functions may include fixed asset control and depreciation, accrual to meet anticipated liabilities, preparation of general tax reports such as those for sales, payroll and personal property taxes, and payables/receivables.</p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Set departmental goals and objectives and measures performance against goals</li>
<li>Plan and administer departmental budget and lead annual organization-wide budget creation</li>
<li>Provide historical data and projections as required for budget development and planning</li>
<li>Monitor, review and verify accounting records and transactions for accuracy, timeliness and adequacy of supporting documentation</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with internal policies and agency rules and regulations</li>
<li>Lead coordination of all accounting and financial audits</li>
<li>Oversee account reconciliation and production of regularly scheduled summary reports, income statements and financial statements for assigned accounting operations</li>
<li>Analyze reports for accuracy prior to distribution</li>
<li>Provide financial data with explanations and interpretation as requested by top management</li>
<li>Provide direction and input for the development of accounting applications, reports, forms, records and documented procedures</li>
<li>Examine and evaluate internal methods and procedures for adequacy of controls</li>
<li>Other projects as assigned by top management</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Education and Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Accounting or related field</li>
<li>3-5 years of accounting or related experience</li>
<li>3-5 years of supervisory experience in an accounting position</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Skills:</p>
<p>· Effective oral and written communication skills</p>
<p>· Attention to detail and accuracy</p>
<p>· Strong organizational and planning skills</p>
<p>· Ability to manage others and delegate tasks</p>
<p>· Ability to work well with others</p>
<p>· Requires a high level of analytical ability to identify and evaluate problems and recommend solutions</p>
<p>· Knowledge of finance, generally accepted accounting, budgeting, and economic principles</p>
<p>· Knowledge of auditing practices and principles</p>
<p>· Knowledge of applicable laws, codes and regulations</p>
<p>· Ability to analyze financial data and prepare financial reports, statements, and projections</p>
<p>· Knowledge of and experience with financial computer applications</p>
<p>· Computer competency</p>
<p>· Bilingual in Japanese and English</p>
<p>· Demonstrated use of SAP or other ERP system</p>
<p>· Demonstrated mastery of Accounting software</p>
<p>Physical Effort / Working Conditions:</p>
<p>The work environment is an office and long periods of sitting at a computer should be expected. Occasional minimal lifting of up to 25 lbs may also be requested.</p>
