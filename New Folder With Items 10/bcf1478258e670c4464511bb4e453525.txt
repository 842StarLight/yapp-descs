<h2>Manager of Demo Engineering</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>WalkMe pioneered the Digital Adoption Platform (DAP) to transform the user experience in today’s overwhelming digital world. Using artificial intelligence, analytics, engagement, guidance, and automation, WalkMe’s transparent overlay assists users to complete tasks easily within any enterprise software, mobile application or website.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
About the role</b></div>
<div>
Here at WalkMe, our product demos are one of the most important assets, and we need a rockstar Demo Manager to oversee them. In this strategic role, you will manage a team of Demo Engineers, and work with them to set the strategy for all our standard demos. These are the demo environments used across the company to bring our product to life, through high-quality content and customer-centric value propositions. The environments and flows are used by AEs, AMs, and SCs to demo WalkMe to prospects, and used at conferences, roadshows, and more!
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>This role will report directly to the VP of Solution Consulting.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Own:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
You will establish which demo environments we should create/procure, and the best demo flows within those environments. This will require an understanding of our market, product functionality, new releases, product messaging, best practices, etc.</li>
<li>
As you will manage a team of Demo Engineers, it will be essential that you can allocate projects to the team, inspire them, and ensure that processes are running smoothly.</li>
<li>
Additionally, you should develop a data-driven approach to demo development. Find ways to collect requirements from various teams by soliciting and listening to feedback and monitoring demo use and success.</li>
<li>
Lastly, you will be responsible for ensuring that the Solution Consulting and other adjacent teams are enabled to demonstrate our product properly, and use all the great content that your team is creating.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>You will upload the four E’s of Demo Engineering:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Expertise: understand best what WalkMe does, and what it can and will do</li>
<li>
Exploration: push the boundaries of the product by testing new features and discovering new use cases</li>
<li>
Execution: bring those possibilities and future possibilities to life in demos</li>
<li>
Efficiency: make it possible, quickly and easily, for others to demonstrate those things</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You Need to Succeed:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
2+ years experience in software sales, ideally as a sales engineer or solution architect</li>
<li>
4+ years in sales or product marketing</li>
<li>
Experience with or exposure to HTML and CSS (Javascript is a bonus)</li>
<li>
Knowledge of on premise and cloud applications/platforms, across web, desktop, and mobile frameworks</li>
<li>
Deep exposure to the configuration of at least two other complex software platforms</li>
</ul></div>
