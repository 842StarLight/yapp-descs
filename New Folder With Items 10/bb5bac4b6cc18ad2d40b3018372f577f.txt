<h2>Special Events Coordinator - Van Buren (Part Time)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
Unity in Diversity</b></p>
<p>
Our team members are as diverse as our music venues. With its owned/operated/managed venues, Live Nation Clubs and Theaters, a division of Live Nation Entertainment, is wide-ranging, including the legendary Fillmore venues and the intimate House of Blues clubs throughout the United States. Live Nation Clubs and Theaters is the country’s preeminent group of intimate music venues, featuring superior sound and lighting technology in one-of-a-kind custom-designed environments aimed to bring fans and artists together in unparalleled musical environments.</p>
<p>
Our mission is to create a profitable and principled global entertainment company. To celebrate the diversity and brotherhood of world culture. To promote racial and spiritual harmony through love, peace, truth, righteousness and non-violence. Our venues bring fans as close to artists as possible and our aim is to create teams that are just as close.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Clubs &amp; Theaters, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you, then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
I. Essential Functions
</p>
<ul>
<li>Support Director of Sales in coordination of all aspects of operational set-up and break down of events (equipment needs, rental needs, and station set-up)</li>
<li>
Provides administrative support and assistance to the Director of Sales</li>
<li>
Provides sales administrative support (prospecting lists, sales system database management, floor plan creation, etc.) as directed by Director of Sales</li>
<li>
Provides marketing administrative support (email lists, creative service requests, etc) in support of Director of Sales</li>
<li>
Assists Production department all aspects needed for an event as directed by Director of Sales</li>
<li>
Supports Director of Sales liaison between client and venue Team Members throughout Event</li>
<li>
Executes admission tickets and retail requests for Special Events as directed by Director of Sales</li>
<li>
Must maintain a neat, clean and well-groomed appearance</li>
<li>
Supports Director of Sales in handling all aspects of the Special Event once the special event order (SEO) has been distributed</li>
<li>
Maintain files in proper order after the special event order has been distributed</li>
<li>
Assist Director of Sales as requested with special needs by the client</li>
<li>
Due to the nature of the hospitality industry; you may be required to work varying schedules to reflect the business needs of the venue</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
II. Accounting Functions</p>
<ul>
<li>
Verify Deposit / Payments tracking schedules with Director of Sales</li>
<li>
Proper execution and completion of all post event paperwork: Party Recap Sheet, Captain’s Report and Receipts directly supporting Director of Sales</li>
<li>
Responsible for proper settlement or payment on night of event with client and entering billing information into all systems to generate a final guest check as directed by Director of Sales</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
III. Staff Functions</p>
<ul>
<li>
Produce weekly staffing summary for all events – includes number of team members and their time to be scheduled and verify staffing was handled by Operations Managers as directed by Director of Sales</li>
<li>
Support Director of Sales in overseeing attendance according to schedules, adjust and reassign server stations, as necessary, in order to provide quality service and adherence to CM expectations during functions.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
IV. Inventory Functions</p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain and order all Linen inventory &amp; cleaning (if applicable)</li>
<li>
Order equipment, maintain inventory / par levels &amp; report deficiencies to Director of Sales</li>
<li>
Order all specialty items as sold by Sales Team and upsell to client as opportunity arises as directed by Director of Sales</li>
<li>
Ensures equipment rentals for SE are ordered and returned to vendors as directed by Director of Sales</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have knowledge of food and beverage preparations, service standards, guest relations and etiquette</li>
<li>
Knowledge of all appropriate table settings and etiquette</li>
<li>
Basic Mathematical skills</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to lift and carry objects up to 35 pounds</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a very fast paced environment with considerable noise and interruptions. Must be able to change activity frequently.</li>
<li>
High School Diploma; College degree preferred.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Restaurant &amp; Venue Operations; event planning, food preparation &amp; presentation, menu planning, food costing, computers (word processing, database spreadsheets)</li>
<li>
Entry level knowledge of legal and HR issues with regards to the hospitality industry (federal, state, and local law regarding liquor, labor and health code regulations) POS systems</li>
<li>
Exhibits positive attitudes; excellent written and verbal communication; high level of organizational and follow-through skills; communicates assertively; maintains personal integrity</li>
<li>
Ability to handle various projects at one time make good decisions; delegates; follow up; communicate with staff and guests in a positive manner</li>
</ul>
