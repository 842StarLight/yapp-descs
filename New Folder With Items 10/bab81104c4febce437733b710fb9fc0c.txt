<h2>Creative Design Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Asthe <b>Creative Design Manager (CDM) </b>you will manage the CFS Brandsgraphic design process, from conceptualization to delivery. The CDM will delegate design tasks within thedesign team and ensure on-time high-quality work.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES &amp; ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Train, mentor, supervise, and lead the graphic design team.</li>
<li>Participate in designing and direction of marketing concepts, packaging design, web design, advertisements, brochures and other forms of visual communication.</li>
<li>Develop efficient processes and procedures for production workflow, process documentation, design approvals, color management, quality control, project and budget tracking and file organization.</li>
<li>Maintain development, implementation, and monitoring of CFS Brands' identity/branding system; align the brand and sub-brands with current goals, strategies, and initiatives.</li>
<li>Maintain Graphic Standards produced internally or by contractors.</li>
<li>Complete projects on time and within budget.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION / EXPERIENCE: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Advertising or Communications or related degree</li>
<li>It is expected that the successful candidate will have a track record progression over a 5 to 7-year period of professional experience.</li>
<li>3+ years in graphic design.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS REQUIRED: </b></p>
<p>The type ofexperience required to effectively perform the essential functions andresponsibilities of this position are:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong proven ability in brand building and marketing communications functional leadership.</li>
<li>Current graphic design and copywriting skills.</li>
<li>Customer experience and outcome focused mindset.</li>
<li>Experience leading a graphic design team, preferably in an agency or an agency-like environment servicing multiple internal and external clients.</li>
<li>Fast paced rapidly changing environment where well thought out risk taking is acceptable.</li>
<li>Must have strong interpersonal skills, be goal and performance oriented and be able to set priorities, establish work plans and provide specific management feedback.</li>
<li>Must be a team oriented, creative problem solver.</li>
<li>Proven ability in art direction, typography, layout, composition, color theory, and information design; multimedia/motion graphics design experience is a plus.</li>
<li>Experienced in Adobe Creative Suite</li>
<li>Pre-press production experience preferred</li>
<li>A proven portfolio of work campaigns in a variety of media (printed, digital, environmental)</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects and develop a creative design within a tight deadline</li>
<li>
<b>Focus on the Customer</b> – Forms active working relationships with customers by anticipating, analyzing, and responding quickly to opportunities, needs, and problems while aligning with prioritized areas of focus as it pertains to the region.</li>
<li>
<b>Knows the Business</b> – Speaks and acts with up-to-date knowledge of issues relevant to the organization, and business.</li>
<li>
<b>Drives for Results</b> – Sets standards of excellence and strives for improvement. Holds him/herself and others accountable.</li>
<li>
<b>Walks the Talk</b> – Builds trust, delivers on promises, communicates directly, respects other opinions, maintains confidentiality, and treats people fairly. Demonstrates consistency between words and actions.</li>
<li>
<b>Sound Judgment and Decision Making</b> – Makes effective decisions based on logical assumptions and adequate information; takes into consideration resources, constraints, and organizational values.</li>
<li>
<b>Fosters Teamwork</b> – Works with others both as a leader and a team member towards shared goals related to business objectives (within regional team, corporate internal departments, and executive leadership). Encourages the open expression of ideas.</li>
<li>
<b>Financial Acumen</b> – this is necessary to understand the financial impact of decisions on the business as it pertains to sales growth and margin improvement.</li>
</ul>
