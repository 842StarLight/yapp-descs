<h2>Function Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Function Coordinator is responsible for contracting the use of events in our function areas. They coordinate functions which deliver the highest level of quality in conjunction with the theatre’s world-class perception. They foster positive relationships with outside vendors and community organizations and utilize the facility for additional revenue outside of the seasonal performance schedule.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS: </b></p>
<p>The following list of essential job functions is not exhaustive and may be supplemented as necessary.</p>
<p>1. Responsible for communication between all potential and contracted renters including meetings, phone calls, emails, and negotiating estimates and sales contracts.</p>
<p>2. Produce financial agreements and contracts with clients</p>
<p>3. Create and revise room layouts for each event and assist in the set-up and break down of events.</p>
<p>4. Enter function details into the booking software.</p>
<p>5. Relay necessary event information to Director of Operations such as event bookings, logistics, parking, event set-up, food and beverage needs.</p>
<p>6. Be present for the events, rehearsals, and related meetings. Coordinate staffing needs with Director of Operations.</p>
<p>7. Keep track of event finances for each event, including check requests, invoicing and reporting and submit for approval from Director of Operations.</p>
<p>8. Work with the Director of Operations in the coordination of marketing materials for rental sales, including but not limited to brochures and website information.</p>
<p>9. Create new and unique ideas to help promote theatre function rooms during days/times the theatre will not have stage performances.</p>
<p>10. Ensure all events and event details reflect the world-class image set forth by the venue.</p>
<p>11. Check voicemail and email frequently to assure quick response to potential renters.</p>
<p>12. Work in conjunction with Director of Operations for smaller, free events, and larger on-stage events such as graduations/commencements and corporate events as needed.</p>
<p><b>SKILLS AND SPECIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<p>The employee must possess the following knowledge, skills and abilities and be able to demonstrate that they can perform the above responsibilities and essential functions to a high level of competency, with or without reasonable accommodation.</p>
<p>1. Ability to work extended or irregular hours to include days, nights, weekends, and holidays is required. Must be willing to work event-based hours determined by the busy theatre schedule.</p>
<p>2. Excellent communication skills, including writing, proofreading skills, and speaking.</p>
<p>3. Proficient using the Web to conduct research, event administration and procurement as well as with the latest version of Microsoft Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint.</p>
<p>4. Ability to effectively deal with internal and external customers, donors and members with high levels of patience, tact and diplomacy.</p>
<p>5. Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with attention to detail.</p>
<p>6. Excellent organizational and time management skills.</p>
<p>7. Ability to manage multiple simultaneous projects and work assignments from a variety of staff and volunteers, while maintaining a positive and professional attitude.</p>
<p>8. Excellent interpersonal skills both in person and by phone, with high professionalism.</p>
<p>9. Fantastic customer service ethic and high expectations for quality.</p>
<p>10. Ability to move throughout the theatre and office environment and continuously perform essential functions for an extended period of time.</p>
<p>11. Ability to lift, move and/or carry up to a maximum of 50 pounds by hand.</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION &amp; QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<p>1. Bachelor’s Degree preferred, or equivalent combination of education, training, and experience.</p>
<p>2. Strong interest in performing arts.</p>
<p>3. Three years of event/function planning and sales experience.</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>sales: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>event/function planning: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
