<h2>Enterprise Territory Manager - Southeast</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Your Role:</b><br>
<p>Tenable is currently searching for an Enterprise Territory Manager, who is responsible for establishing and developing business through existing and new clients in their territory.</p>
<b>
Your Opportunity:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and develop the existing client base through marketing and direct sales activities</li>
<li>
Identify, qualify and develop new opportunities through prospecting and networking</li>
<li>
Manage and maintain your territory’s sales pipeline and target key accounts and vertical markets for development</li>
<li>
Achieve assigned monthly, quarterly and annual sales budgets and other objectives established by the Vice President of Sales</li>
<li>
Provide monthly and quarterly sales forecasts</li>
<li>
Manage the day to date activities of sales personnel dedicated to your region. This includes inside sales representatives and sales engineers</li>
<li>
Consistently achieve and/or exceed your assigned quotas and revenue objectives</li>
<li>
Utilize channel sales resources in your region as necessary</li>
<li>
Stay abreast of competitive industry and market intelligence and provide feedback in this area to executive management</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>
What You'll Need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree and 5-7 years of experience directly relating to this job</li>
<li>
Ability to take on a consultative style when presenting ideas and products</li>
<li>
Competency with sales tracking tools</li>
<li>
Outstanding oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Must be detail oriented and capable of accurate work with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
The ability to travel approximately 25% of the time</li>
</ul>
