<p>Sales and Marketing Associate for Code Ninjas - League City, TX</p>
<p>Seeking enthusiastic college graduate with interest in sales, marketing, education and technology.</p>
<p>Our company</p>
<p>Code Ninjas is a national provider of children education services for computer technology, specifically computer programming. Coding is the new literacy. We believe that we are creating the problem solvers of tomorrow. You are invited to join this new and exciting leading-edge service.</p>
<p>Along with learning critical thinking and problem-solving skills, the children have the opportunity to learn responsibility, team work and leadership. We try to foster a cooperative environment where students can build new friendships and extend their social skills.</p>
<p>Our curriculum consists of four years of content progressing through nine levels of achievement. Specifically, we teach children age targeted software programming tools, for gaming, web-development, 3-D graphics, robotics and autonomous drones.</p>

<li>Track and respond to all prospective inquires, via phone, email and social media</li>
<li>Invite prospects to tour our facility and to learn about our services</li>
<li>Conduct tours and describe program to prospective clients</li>
<li>Review client agreements, membership pricing and enrollment with prospective clients</li>

<li>Train clients on procedures of our operations</li>
<li>Coordinate flow of enrollment, registration and student visits</li>
<li>Evaluate effectiveness of programs and make recommendations for improvement</li>
</ul>
