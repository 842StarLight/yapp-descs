<h2>Sales Engineer East</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>StarLeaf is seeking an experienced Sales Engineer to work as part of the Sales &amp; Marketing Team from our brand-new office in the Newark Area. This role will suit someone who has previous experience on a technical pre-sales team, ideally for a Video Conferencing/Collaboration, UCaaS, or other cloud product sold primarily to major or enterprise customers.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Aspects of the role include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide active pre-sales support and demonstrations at key accounts nationwide (including many Fortune 500 accounts)</li>
<li>Work closely with StarLeaf RSMs to provide appropriate Sales Engineering resource for their requirements</li>
<li>Work closely with StarLeaf channel partners across the country to ensure they are well trained technically, and up to date in all StarLeaf products</li>
<li>Consult closely with the StarLeaf Product Teams to triage product feedback and feature requests from the field</li>
<li>Identify gaps in technical documentation and work closely with the Sales Enablement Team to solve them</li>
<li>Plan team meetings and summits as required</li>
<li>Become a StarLeaf product expert and be able to articulate the benefits of all products in the StarLeaf portfolio</li>
<li>Become familiar with all relevant competition and be able to explain technical differentiators between StarLeaf and alternative video solutions.</li>
<li>Responding to functional and technical questions arising from evaluation trials and RFIs/RFPs</li>
<li>Understanding of optimal StarLeaf product configurations for different customer environments</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 Pre-sales Engineering/Solutions Consulting
</li>
<li>Experience working in a start-up environment with dispersed sales teams preferred</li>
<li>Background in Video Conferencing/Unified Communications preferred.</li>
<li>Professionally presented with excellent presentation and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Must be prepared to travel 50%+ and attend training in the United Kingdom</li>
<li>Comfortable in a commercial sales environment.</li>
<li>Demonstrable passion for technology.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generous starting salary with a good commission structure.</li>
<li>Share options.</li>
<li>Medical and dental insurance, plus life and disability insurance.</li>
<li>15 days paid vacation entitlement.</li>
</ul>
