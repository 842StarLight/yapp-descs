<h2>Data Entry Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The <b>Data Entry Clerk</b> ensures that all customer issues and concerns are resolved promptly. He or she greets and engages with customers and fellow team members to ensure customers are treated in a professional and courteous manner which will ensure a 100% customer satisfaction.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The ideal candidate has some post-secondary education or training, previous experience in a similar position (administrative, clerical, call center, customer service, etc.), and a working knowledge of automotive sales/service. He or she must have a pleasant and courteous personality, strong administrative and organizational skills, strong phone and computer skills, and excellent communication and interpersonal skills.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
In addition to competitive pay, we offer our employees: <b>Health, Dental, Vision, Life, 401K, Health &amp; Wellness Program, community involvement and company paid vacation and holidays.</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Daily Data Entry</li>
<li>
Deal with customers promptly in a professional and courteous manner.</li>
<li>
Meet and greet customers promptly in a professional and courteous manner.</li>
<li>
Engage with customers waiting for their vehicles in the customer lounge when necessary.</li>
<li>
Work closely with team members to ensure things run efficiently.</li>
<li>
Make daily observations of concerns and report to Manager daily/weekly.</li>
<li>
Maintain databases in Excel.</li>
<li>
Manage customers' accounts and keep record of interactions and transactions.</li>
<li>
Prepare and distribute activity report.</li>
<li>
Communicate and coordinate with internal departments and follow up with interactions.</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
