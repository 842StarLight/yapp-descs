<b><br>
</b><p></p>
<p><b>TCF Capital Solutions is currently looking for a Data &amp; Reporting Analyst to join their team in Minnetonka, MN</b></p>
<p>As a <b>data and reporting analyst,</b> you will dive into data as a mechanism to deeply understand a business and build relationships. Plus, you will create informative, actionable, and repeatable reporting that highlights relevant business trends and needs for a variety of audiences.</p>
<p><b>What you will work on:</b></p>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Designing and developing data visualizations such as Tableau dashboards in order to distribute data</li>
<li>
Satisfying ad-hoc reporting and analysis requests</li>
<li>
Identifying, collecting, analyzing and presenting trends, visualizations, and summaries of data</li>
<li>
Collaborating with business leaders to define metrics, performance indicators and product analysis</li>
<li>
Slice and dice data to provide insight across multiple dimensions (e.g. sales rep, products, pricing, source, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<br>
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Science, or field in which strong data analysis skills are acquired</li>
<li>
5+ year of professional SQL experience handling complex queries, functions, stored procedures, and optimizing database performance</li>
<li>
Proficiency and experience with Tableau software</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of SAP Business Objects</li>
<li>
Basic understanding of Hadoop framework</li>
<li>
Experience extracting, mining, transforming, analyzing and loading complex data sets</li>
<li>
Knowledge of database design principles</li>
<li>
Additional requirements:<ul>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to think outside the box and learn new techniques/technologies in short periods of time</li>
<li>
Exceptional attention to detail, accuracy; and ability to deliver on schedule and by deadline</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to work in mixed settings (independently, collaboratively, in groups)</li>
<li>
Passion for looking for the meaning—the “why”—behind the numbers, with an insatiable drive to discover actionable insights</li>
<li>
Desire to dig deep and get lost in data, those who are most effective also have the ability to think beyond a tactical level</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
