<b>In this role, you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive adoption and usage of PandaDoc through ongoing engagement with customers</li>
<li>Identify at risk customers and help reduce churn</li>
<li>Onboard and train additional account users via demos, presentations, collateral, and webinars.</li>
<li>Proactively review key account metrics with clients and create goals for clients to hit AND help them hit those goals. Their goals are yours!</li>
<li>Work with Customer Support, Sales, and Product to articulate and monitor customer requests and feedback through the appropriate channels.</li>
<li>Automate process and customer outreach allowing scale and reach</li>
<li>Define key interaction points in the customer lifecycle</li>
</ul>
<b>About you:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2-3 years experience as an Customer success, Customer service, or other similar role, preferably with SaaS product(s).</li>
<li>Track record of developing Customer Success and Onboarding programs</li>
<li>Proven ability to smoothly on-board new customers and manager relationships on an ongoing basis.</li>
<li>Have worked with a software sales team and sold software products developed in an agile environment.</li>
<li>History of being a compassionate problem solver with great communication skills, attention to detail, and a strong passion for user experience and customer happiness.</li>
</ul>
