<h2>Electronic Cable Design Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Electronic Cable Design Engineer</b></p>
<p><b>West Brookfield, MA</b></p>
<p>This is a <b>DIRECT HIRE</b> opportunity.</p>
<p>The Cable Design Engineer will be responsible for the design of all new cable products, as well as modifications and revisions to existing products. This individual will work closely with sales, production, and provide design and technical assistance to customers.</p>
<p><b>About the Job: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design high reliability, customer cables using established methods and techniques.</li>
<li>Work independently as well as be a team player, working closely with sales and production personnel.</li>
<li>Routine customer contact relative to cable design assistance and technical support.</li>
<li>Some travel required</li>
<li>BS degree in Mechanical Engineering or Electrical Engineering preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years experience in electronic cable manufacturing environment preferred.</li>
<li>Knowledge of electronic cable design, materials and production techniques necessary to produce high performance cables. Knowledge of RoHS, REACH, and other environmental/safety standards preferred.</li>
<li>Good communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Knowledge of engineering software/CAD required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Secondary job functions and responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Help maintain company's computer network and individual work stations</li>
<li>Knowledge of Apple platform a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Pay Range: </b>$60,000 - $65,000 annually</p>
<p><b>About You: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Looking for growth and ability to expand scope of skills outside engineering.</li>
<li>Likes engineering, but isn't singularly focused</li>
<li>Eager, inquisitive, smart, able to learn new skills and research</li>
<li>Willing and able to analyze current processes and suggest or implement adjustments</li>
<li>Willing and able to get on the floor, understand process and look into what are we doing wrong or what can we differently / more efficiently</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Types: Full-time, Temporary</p>
<p>Salary: $60,000.00 to $65,000.00 /hour</p>
