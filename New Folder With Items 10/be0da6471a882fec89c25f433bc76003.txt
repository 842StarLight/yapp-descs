What will help you succeed</b></p>
<ul>

At least 10 years of relevant work experience. Expert in one or more major technology and business domains.</li>
<li>Experience with following -<ul>

<li>Angular / React</li>
<li>MongoDB, MySQL, Cassandra</li>

<li>Architecture and Design of Enterprise Systems</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Exceptional listening skills, with an ability to ask the right questions and uncover hidden insights.</li>
<li>Excellent client service abilities, including strong presentation skills.</li>
<li>Proven team leadership and management experience.</li>
<li>Engineering and software development background</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science, system engineering and / or related studies</li>
<li>Knowledge of Agile methodologies and processes, team leadership and account/client management</li>
<li>50% travel is required for this role at this time</li>

<p><b>You will get the chance to</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Shape your own path as you work with the best brands and professionals in the industry</li>
<li>Work in a flexible, team-oriented culture that’s truly Agile</li>
<li>Mentor and coach a team of Tech Leads and Architects</li>
<li>Influence a large team or multiple teams to achieve their goals</li>
<li>Overseeing technological, architecture and design decisions and recommending corrective actions if needed</li>
<li>Have the opportunity to define and overview a consistent agile development process according to the team and customer expectations</li>

<li>Complete performance reviews for his/her team members</li>
