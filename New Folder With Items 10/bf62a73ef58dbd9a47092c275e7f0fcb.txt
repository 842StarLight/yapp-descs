<h2>Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Guideline is looking for a Sales Coordinator to join the Sales team in our Portland, Maine location.
<br><br>
What will you be working on?
<br>As a member of the Guideline Sales team, the Sales Coordinator is a sales development role. This role will be responsible for streamlining the sales process as a key role in qualifying and educating prospective clients to programmatically identify sales qualified leads for Account Executives. You will own lead qualification to optimize lead handoff between sales development level and account executives. Success in this role will manifest by maximizing the Lead Velocity, which means the number of Qualified Leads per rep within a given period of time is constantly growing.
<br><br>
The ultimate goal is to expand our retirement platform technology footprint with new clients through our ecosystem partners and present Guideline's solutions to new prospects and leads, and help drive the growth of Guideline.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Support our growing team to help develop and acquire new business</li>
<li>Communicate with and learn about Guideline's prospective clients through phone calls, email, LinkedIn and other online platforms</li>
<li>Help team members follow up with their daily tasks</li>
<li>Manage, track, and report activities and results</li>
<li>Diligently update CRM to stay organized and focused on priority prospects</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
The Team
<br><br>
The Guideline Sales Team is not the traditional sales team. We are in charge of turning all inbound leads (generated by marketing) into live customers. The sales process is fast and customer-centric. Our close rate is high because we are truly offering a superior product and a solution that companies need.
<br><br>
