<h2>Marketing Communications Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Full Time</div>
<div>
<div>
<p>
Job Description:</p>
<p>
Develop and maintain brand and marketing strategies.<br>
Implement company’s rebranding program throughout the entire organization and ensure that all internal and external materials are created according to the corporate Visual Identity (VI) guidelines<br>
Conduct training about VI program in overseas locations.<br>
Research, write, edit, proof, design, layout marketing and communication pieces (newsletters, emails, social media, training materials, sell sheets, investor materials, portfolio reports, sales presentations, etc.)<br>
Maintain corporate website and social media platforms (LinkedIn and Facebook)<br>
Create, edit and publish promotional content (text, images, videos, etc.) on the corporate website and social media channels.<br>
Manage and liaison with third party consultants and agencies.<br>
Requirements:</p>
<p>
Bachelor’s Degree required, preferably with a degree in communications or marketing<br>
1-2 years of experience in marketing communications or PR roles<br>
Proficiency in MS Office software<br>
Proficiency in both English and Chinese, overseas experience is a plus<br>
Strong verbal and communication skill<br>
Attention to detail<br>
Knowledge of Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator or any design software is a plus</p>
