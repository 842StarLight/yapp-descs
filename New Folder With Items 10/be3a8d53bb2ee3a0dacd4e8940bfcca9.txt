<h2>Technical Support Team Member</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Aprilaire is an industry leading manufacturer of healthy air solutions. We focus on indoor air quality by offering innovative technology that controls humidity, temperature, air purity, and ventilation. We believe that all of these aspects are critical in creating healthy air environments for families in their homes. That’s been our goal since 1954, when Aprilaire was established as part of Research Products Corporation, our parent company. Today we’re in over 7 million homes, and we’re not stopping there. We strive to enable every family to breathe better air and feel more comfortable in a healthy home.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are growing and promoting and are seeking a technically-minded individual to fill the role of <b>Technical Support Team Member</b>. In this exciting role, you’ll play a key role in improving indoor air quality for millions of people. You’ll also be responsible for providing technical and application assistance to our customers, including installing HVAC Contractors, Engineering Professionals, and consumers.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
If you are looking for a fun work environment and a rewarding career, read on to check out the details of this position and learn why Aprilaire is a great company to work for.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>In this role you will:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Work for a stable company in a fast-paced, friendly, team-oriented department that embraces progression and new ideas.</li>
<li>
Work in our nationally recognized call center at the corporate office and have the opportunity to help solve customer’s indoor air quality problems with Aprilaire solutions.</li>
<li>
Receive incoming and place outbound calls from/to HVAC professionals helping them with questions regarding product applications and specifications.</li>
<li>
Get the opportunity to work with our National Field Sales Force and HVAC contractor network.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>If you are the type of person who can fix anything and tell someone how you did it - your new position awaits you at Aprilaire!!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
What other special qualifications will you bring to this position? The following knowledge, skills, and abilities will serve beneficial to you in being successful as a Technical Support Team Member.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Two to three years’ prior experience in technical support and application assistance of products involving low and high voltage wiring, plumbing, mechanical or related field.</li>
<li>
Experience or ability to learn wiring, mechanical and refrigeration sizing and troubleshooting.</li>
<li>
Ability to work with a top-notch team, with a combined experience of nearly 100 years.</li>
</ul>
