<h2>LMS Account Manager I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>The LMS Account Manager is responsible for overseeing the LMS on-site staff to ensure that all contracted services at a client site are being met. In addition, the LMS Account Manager is responsible for developing and maintaining the client relationship and should be the primary point of contact between LMS and the client. The LMS Account manager has HR and direct report responsibilities for the LMS on-site staff. This person is to follow direction of LMS organization management in a professional and effective manner.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>All areas of responsibility listed below are essential to the satisfactory performance of this position, with reasonable accommodation, if necessary. Any non-essential functions are assumed to be included in “other related duties as assigned”:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Manage all assigned resources and optimize their productivity.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Implement and manage all LMS production policies and procedures.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Analyze and control all production costs to produce profit for area(s) of responsibility.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Recruit, train and develop LMS Associates. Conduct One-on-One’s and Team Meetings.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Write and administer performance reviews.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Communicate and demonstrate value of LMS solution offerings with team and client.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Interface with customers, sales personnel and management as required to achieve LMS objectives.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Complete special projects as assigned utilizing internal resources as needed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Exercise good business judgment, initiative and creativity.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Personally operate various LMS equipment when necessary due to absence, in start-up situations or as required.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain a clean, safe, professional and productive work environment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop and maintain monthly reports for client monthly meetings and internal use.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure that all client and site documentation is up to date and relevant.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work closely with LMS floater staff to ensure coverage of all LMS sites.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work cohesively with Imaging counterparts to achieve both LMS account growth and new business goals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Leverage all internal and external resources to maximize LMS and Loffler value with client and employees.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Conduct all of the above activities in a timely and complete manner.</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications/Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Ability to work with minimal supervision.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Works well under pressure and achieve deadlines.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excitement for and demonstrated ability to manage people and build cooperative and effective teams.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to work with detailed information accurately.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Able to build effective and positive relationships both internally and externally.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to communicate timely and effectively both in written and oral format.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite and additional business related software as applicable.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Knowledgeable with Loffler’s current product line and software associated with the products as well as LMS solutions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstration of action as a professional to positively represent Loffler, LMS, and the position.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strong ability to work cooperatively in positive upbeat manner within a team environment towards a common goal.</p>
</li>

<br>
</li>
</ul>
