<h2>Associate Broker Services Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Great Position at Ultimus Fund Solutions for someone with experience in financial services! Promotions and 20 days PTO- take a closer look!</b></p>
<p>
The Associate Broker Services Specialist supports the functions of the Broker Services Department. The primary function is to maintain relationships with financial intermediaries who process trades, on behalf of their clients, in mutual funds serviced by Ultimus Fund Solutions.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Typical duties include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Performs daily NSCC processing. Resolves mismatches and refers error resolution to senior or higher levels.</li>
<li>Assists in the maintenance of client investment portfolios and investment reports</li>
<li>Assists in the performance of commission processing.</li>
<li>Assists with monthly and quarterly verification of accuracy for brokerage invoices processed by Ultimus on behalf of the funds and their advisors.
</li>
<li>Processes trade corrections submitted by financial intermediaries including creating and submitting loss letters to financial intermediaries.</li>
<li>Oversees and performs maintenance on accounts, brokerage companies, branches and reps resulting from requests submitted to the mutual fund families.</li>
<li>Performs and maintains all Broker On-Line access requests; set-up functions, recordkeeping, etc.</li>
<li>Communicates with the appropriate parties, performs all necessary set-up functions and maintains appropriate records.</li>
</ul>
