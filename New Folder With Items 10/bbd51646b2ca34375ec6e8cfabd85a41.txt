<h2>Product Engineer- IoT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Product Engineer is responsible for identifying, validating, developing, testing, and supporting new products as defined to meet business requirements. Works closely with OEM/ODM partners, R&amp;D, product management, applications engineering, and marketing teams worldwide to drive new product development and overall profitability.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborates with suppliers and vendors to identify new products and technologies; conducts new product evaluation, testing, and qualification.</li>
<li>Defines and executes required engineering, design, and production validation testing (EVT, DVT, and PVT) and supports release activities throughout the launch process.</li>
<li>Responsible for interpreting required safety and regulatory certification requirements and collaborating with product development and/or vendor technical teams to ensure required features/functionality is implemented along with review/approval of certification agency reports.</li>
<li>Prepares and delivers technical training to the organization supporting both technical and sales functions including pre-sales engineering and post-sales technical support.</li>
<li>Stay current with technologies and trends and incorporate this knowledge into products and solutions to solve customer problems.</li>
<li>Does competitive product analysis and testing; communicates competitive intelligence on features, value propositions, pricing to product managers.</li>
<li>Assists with creating product data sheets, brochures, application notes, use case diagrams, videos, and technical blogs.</li>
<li>Completes systems integration testing with other Black Box product lines and third party equipment.</li>
<li>Contribute to product planning and regular market technology review.</li>
<li>Acts as project manager for select subcontractors / manufacturing partners during development projects.</li>
<li>Assists with assessing customer contract quality requirements and the organization's capability to comply with requirements and ensuring that these requirements are flowed down to departments, suppliers, subcontractors and vendors.</li>
<li>Assists sales engineering and marketing with trade show product demo design, set-up, configuration and event presentations.</li>
<li>Utilize project management tools and techniques to organize priorities and document work/results</li>
<li>Provide required technical support for escalated product issues and liaise with vendors/partners as required to resolveQualifications</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills, Knowledge, Abilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated experience in computer networking, software applications, signal switching, and/or visualization technologies</li>
<li>Excellent written, verbal and listening communication skills with diverse stakeholders</li>
<li>Must be results driven and exhibit a high level of initiative, good business acumen, excellent analytical and problem solving skills, with demonstrated organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to read, interpret &amp; generate product drawings and specifications</li>
<li>Possess both commercial and technical aptitude toward qualifying and validating products &amp; processes</li>
<li>Solid teamwork traits and the ability to work collaboratively in a multidisciplinary team environment</li>
<li>Experience with new product introduction project management process and techniques</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills with ability to manage multiple priorities</li>
<li>A genuine interest in advanced technology and an on-going desire to learn</li>
<li>Travel is required, up to 10%, primarily in North America with occasional international travel</li>
</ul>
<p>Education/Experience Requirements</p>
<ul><li>3-5 years of experience and 4 year degree in Engineering, Engineering Technology or Computer Science; experience within this industry may be substituted for candidates with an Associate’s degree</li></ul>
