<h2>District Manager - Kay Chemical</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>District Managers are responsible for developing and managing a group of field associates in all field sales activities in the assigned district which includes the following: growing sales and retaining existing customers by providing excellent service; gaining new sales through new products and new customers, and providing the field associates with the proper training on food safety and sanitation to ensure they conduct high quality surveys and support the field team on maintaining our equipment.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Location: We prefer candidates currently residing within or willing to relocate to Northern California, Utah, Oregon or Washington.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Travel: Candidates must be willing to travel up to 50% of the time.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Main Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Coaches and trains a team of field associates through formal training programs and informal conversations to foster the learning and development of the field associates</li>
<li>
Considers training and developing associates as “job one”</li>
<li>
Responds to daily requests and needs of a team of field associates</li>
<li>
Recruits, hires and retains qualified associates</li>
<li>
Trains and coordinates the onboarding process for the new hires</li>
<li>
Sets high standards and models the behavior they want to see in others</li>
<li>
Complete and maintain weekly, monthly quarterly reporting</li>
<li>
Utilizes internal reports to drive business results and make district decisions</li>
<li>
Manages expenses for the district’s meetings, travel, labor, equipment and repair parts, field stock, and samples</li>
<li>
Manage assets for the district such as vehicles, tools, equipment, parts, and office equipment</li>
<li>
Accompanies field associates on field visits within territories and required intervals (minimum twice a year)</li>
<li>
Demonstrates proper procedures involved with repairing, replacing and installing equipment.</li>
<li>
Develops individual development plans with each assigned field associate</li>
<li>
Provides honest, constructive and ongoing feedback on performance with field associates</li>
<li>
Plans and assists in new store rollouts/installs as required</li>
<li>
Communicates goals, objectives, successes, competitive strategies, customer requirements and product information to the field associates</li>
<li>
Recognizes and rewards individual and team achievement effectively.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates knowledge of district objectives and the business plan to achieve goals</li>
<li>
Retains customers through creating high customer satisfaction and provides customers with the necessary resources</li>
<li>
Directs field team on how to upsell products to current customers</li>
<li>
Resolves customer issues and problems efficiently and effectively</li>
<li>
Addresses customer concerns and follows up regularly</li>
<li>
Completes store visits when territories are vacant or as needed within the district</li>
<li>
Ability to work nights and weekends as needed</li>
<li>
Ability to do overnight travel as needed</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>

<li>
21 years of age or older</li>

</ul>
