<h2>Marketing Communications Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Keen, a Scaleworks portfolio company, is seeking an ambitious and enthusiastic Marketing Communications Specialist to lead community engagement initiatives. This cross-functional role will blend the disciplines of marketing and publicity and is an exciting opportunity to blend your creative, strategic and communication chops.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Create a best in class digital presence across our owned channels (e.g., social media, blog) and industry channels (e.g., review sites, discussion forums)</li>
<li>Cultivate relationships with current customers, industry leaders, and organizations to support business growth and operations</li>
<li>Support in tradeshow and conference execution in partnership with Sales</li>
<li>Be a creative leader on the team, surveying creative needs across channels for a more holistic customer journey and driving the creation of new assets that tell our story (e.g., videos, whitepapers, content posts)</li>
<li>Investigate and test new channels to communicate with our prospective customers</li>
<li>Partner with other teams, both within Marketing and cross-functionally, to accurately track and deliver results that contribute to overall success</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Solid project management skills: strong attention to detail, organized and possess the ability to lead and collaborate with cross-functional partners, prioritize high impact activities, and keep complex projects moving forward</li>
<li>Excellent written/verbal communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Experience with digital marketing and social media channels</li>
<li>Ability to drive creative execution</li>
<li>Knowledge of Hubspot, Google Analytics, Adobe Creative Suite, HTML, CSS</li>
<li>Curiosity and eagerness to learn new marketing tools and tactics</li>
<li>Solid understanding of the marketing mix and the analytical aptitude to measure results and make improvements</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial mindset - we’re looking for someone determined and able to win</li>
<li>Event/tradeshow or field marketing experience a plus</li>
</ul>
