<p><b>Inside Sales Support Specialist</b></p>
<p><b>Seiko Instruments USA, Inc. – Torrance, CA</b></p>
<p>Seiko Instruments Inc. (represented in the U.S. by Seiko Instruments USA Inc.) is a manufacturing organization historically responsible for the design and production of timepieces that established the SEIKO brand name. Today, Seiko currently manufactures and markets a wide variety of products such as thermal printers, scientific instruments, information systems, communication products, and machine tools.</p>
<p>Our Thermal Printer Division is a pioneer in high-performance, high-reliability direct thermal printing solutions. Our business is growing, our portfolio is expanding with new and exciting products; thus our team needs to grow as well. We are currently looking for an experienced Sales Support Specialist to join our dynamic and high energy support team in providing assistance to both field sales and customers. This position will work from our U.S. headquarters, located in Torrance, California.</p>
<p>As the team’s Sales Support Specialist, you will be responsible for providing sales support functions to the division’s internal and external customer base, including sales representatives and distributors. You will be responsible for orders from receipt of quote, to the processing of the invoice. Your daily activities will be varied and will include not only order processing, but problem resolution, inventory management and processing of RMA’s, chargeback’s and sales returns. You will review website sales history and customer trends in order to suggest promotions. You will also work with the sales team on special pricing and customer discount programs.</p>
<p>Desired Experience and Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>3 to 5 years Inside Sales Support / Customer Service experience.</li>
<li>Familiarity with purchasing, forecasting and warehouse operations.</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office Suite. Above average Excel skills.</li>
<li>Proficient in SAP or similar enterprise software.</li>
<li>Experience working with reps and distributors.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal skills.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
</ul>
