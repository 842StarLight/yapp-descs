<b>Job Description:</b><br>
Why Work for Us?
<br><br>

Together the teams in our stores, distribution and manufacturing centers, and support center work together to help our customers make creativity happen. We offer career growth, benefits packages, retirement plans, tuition reimbursement, and more. Michaels Team Members also enjoy a wide variety of ways to save including discounts at our stores, select fitness and childcare centers, restaurants, hotels, and more.
<br><br>
We are currently recruiting for a Marketing Sr Business Analyst located in our Support Center (Global Headquarters) located in Irving, TX.
<br><br>
What will you be doing?
<br><br>
Th e Senior Business Analyst provides planning, reporting and strategic insights derived from sales, coupon effectiveness, customer data and competitive intelligence to drive business-critical decisions. This high visibility role has a direct impact on company results and requires exceptionally strong analytical and math skills, a focused attention to detail, and the ability to communicate across the organization.
<br><br>
How will you do it?
<br><br>
<b>Forecasting, Analysis &amp; Evaluation:</b><br>
Integral participant in the development and completion of the annual sales and coupon budgeting process and monthly forecasting process
<br>Developing insights and understanding of sales and coupon performance based on data and statistical methods
<br>Closely working with the Marketing, Operations, and Merchandising team in determining variance explanations on sales and coupon performance
<br>Comfortable communicating with and presenting analysis, results &amp; insights to the executive level on a daily basis
<br>Ability to identify and articulate actionable insights
<br><br>
Reporting, Test Development, Tracking &amp; Analysis
<br><br>
Creating and automating reporting and dashboards supporting business partners across the company
<br>Manage, measure and provide insights on corporate level tests
<br>Proactively mine data and produce analytical output
<br><br>
Ad Hoc Analysis
<br><br>
Various projects as identified by supervisor.
