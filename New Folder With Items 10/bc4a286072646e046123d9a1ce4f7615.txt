<h2>Software Product Manager – CRM / Relationship Intelligence</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>Intapp is based in Palo Alto, CA but the right candidate could be based anywh</i></b><b><i>ere in the U.S.</i></b></p>
<p>
The Intapp Relationships product is the leading relationship intelligence (ERM) solution for the world’s leading professional services firms. Intapp Relationships uses machine learning and AI technology and is revolutionizing the way firms discover and grow their client relationships. In this position you will drive roadmap prioritization, work closely with the engineering team to execute and deliver the entire product development lifecycle, and ultimately be responsible for ensuring the success of the solution in the market. You will coordinate with product managers across all of Intapp’s product portfolio and act as an internal and external product and market expert and evangelist.</p>
<p>
In this role you will have significant engagement and interaction with customers and Intapp front line teams (sales, customer success, professional services, support) and will work closely with senior level executives.</p>

Key Responsibilities:</b></h4>
<ul>

<li>

<li>
Partner with sales, customer success, support, and product marketing teams to define business requirements, product design, deployment and adoption plans.</li>
<li>
Work with engineering leads to manage the product development lifecycle with our engineering team.</li>
<li>
Identify requirements, design features and groom user stories for Intapp’s Relationships solution<b><i>.</i></b>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>

Work with Intapp’s AI and Machine Learning engineers to advance the use of AI/ML in the Relationships solution.</li>

Lead requirement discovery sessions with clients and prospects.</li>
