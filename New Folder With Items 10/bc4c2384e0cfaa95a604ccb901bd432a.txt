<b>Role Overview</b></p>
<p>
As the successful candidate, you will be required to manage multiple projects across one or more business units, key business functions and repair sites, and be responsible for delivery performance program management of repair and spares businesses which support both commercial and military customers. The Customer Management organization is currently seeking an experienced, high intensity, customer focused Program Manager to manage and align delivery performance expectations shared by our customer facing teams (Customer Sales Managers and Performance Program Managers) with our business unit(s) and Aftermarket Services repair facilities.</p>
<p>
The strategic mission of this role will serve to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Increase customer satisfaction by providing faster responses and robust recovery and action plans</li>
<li>
Reduction of burden on business unit aftermarket teams thru effective consolidation of customer issues and driving a prioritized, action-based discussion</li>
<li>
Increased responsiveness to Collins Aerospace Aftermarket Services leadership thru identification, acting on and escalation of any “help needed” issues that impact performance</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b><br>
Customer Satisfaction
</p>
<ul><li>Acting as a customer advocate, identify, document, and manage customer expectations as “voice of the customer.”</li></ul>
<p>
Program Management</p>
<ul>
<li>
Identification of systemic or unique issues impacting performance within a specific business unit(s) and/or MRO site(s).</li>
<li>
Ensure assigned business unit(s) and/or MRO site(s) have robust recovery plans with specific actions, owners and timelines for completion, and can establish credible dates for returning to enterprise performance objectives agreed to via the Aftermarket Council along with “fly-forward” plans, tracking performance against assumptions.</li>
<li>
Identification of any subject matter, business or operational expertise, best practices or resources needed from within the enterprise, which can provide guidance to a specific site location to facilitate recovery.</li>
<li>
Align with and engage Aftermarket Services Repair Operations Excellence resources where appropriate to support and drive ACE Operating System events that will drive sustained performance improvements.</li>
<li>
Identification of risk and/or escalation of any issue impacting progress towards recovery, including mitigation actions.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Communication</p>
<ul>
<li>
Create and maintain an on-going engagement forum to facilitate voice of the customer on performance related matters, including alignment of customer issues to robust action plans and sustained recovery.</li>
<li>
Share best practices and continuous improvement opportunities with other MRO sites and business units.</li>
<li>
Present/provide clear, aligned action plans for use with the customer (external-focus) and Aftermarket leadership (internal-focus).</li>
<li>
Working with the business unit and/or MRO site, provide “fly-forward” recovery plans and tracking progress to those plans.</li>
<li>
Identification of any “help needed” for support or resources to ensure recovery plans stay on track.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Champions the customer culture by being tenacious, working with a sense of urgency and a focus on results.</li>
<li>
Develops key relationships and can function effectively and collaboratively across a matrix organization</li>
<li>
Demonstrates crisp and effective communications at all levels, with an attention to detail</li>
<li>
Takes initiative and can operate in vague situations and create defined outcomes</li>
<li>
Effectively challenges status quo and “finds a way” thru seeking new/different ideas and solutions</li>
</ul>
