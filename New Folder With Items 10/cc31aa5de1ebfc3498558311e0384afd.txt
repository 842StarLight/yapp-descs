<p>Customer Service Representative</p>
<p>Frozen Food Express is looking for a Customer Service Representative for our Salt Lake City, UT location.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Support daily activities of FFE Account Manager</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for daily hands-on customer related situations, account development and customer satisfaction</li></ul>
<ul><li>Accurately and timely input all load information into the operating system</li></ul>
<ul><li>Will be responsible for communicating directly with customer’s employees to determine daily freight needs and providing status updates to them</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide exceptional customer service to FFE Customer/employees, carriers, and drivers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicating daily with carriers, the on-site customer and freight employees to ensure on-time pick-ups &amp; deliveries</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enter orders into the system</li></ul>
<ul><li>Interacting directly with customer to determine products ready to be shipped</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor and trace load so that follow up can be made to the customer regarding issues that could effect on-time pick up or delivery</li></ul>
<ul><li>Creating and disturbing freight documents (BOL’s, labels, manifest, etc)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Entering load information and tracking/tracing shipments in software system</li></ul>
<ul><li>Scheduling necessary appointments with receivers and coordinating delivers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Running all necessary reporting tools and reporting information to the appropriate departments/managers as requested</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure superior customer service, problem resolution, and effective procedures and process throughout the department</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate service issues, declined business and new business opportunities to the sale department</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other duties as assigned or as needed</li></ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul><li>3+ years of current Customer Service experience required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Transportation experience required</li></ul>
<ul><li>AS400 experience preferred, but not required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong problem solving skills and ability to multi-task</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to manage multiple priorities and exceptional follow-through abilities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong organization skills for planning, decision-making and executing.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent oral and written communications skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Individual must be flexible. As hours will be based on Customer needs, overtime or weekend shifts may be required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be professional</li></ul>
<ul><li>PC literate to include Microsoft Office products such as Word, Excel and Outlook</li></ul>
