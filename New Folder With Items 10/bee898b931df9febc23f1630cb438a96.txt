<h2>Front Desk Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are currently seeking to fill Saturday as well as evening front desk support positions.</p>
<p>Minnesota School of Cosmetology (MSC) began as the Minnesota Cosmetology Education Center and was founded in September of 2003. The school is committed to providing career-focused education that assists students in reaching their career and life goals. This position offers an excellent opportunity to join a well-established organization. Our philosophy is simple and comprehensive: We care. This drives our commitment to our employees and our community. Please visit us at www.msccollege.edu</p>
<p>The Front Desk Associate ensures smooth operation of the school by providing service to clients during school visits, encouraging the sale of retail products and services to clients and teaching students how to utilize Millennium salon software.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities of this position may include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand the procedures used in the school and perform them consistently</li>
<li>Understand retail product information and service menu information</li>
<li>Understand the importance of making the client feel welcome, comfortable, and special</li>
<li>Answer phone and set appointments using proper procedure</li>
<li>Clean desk, stock &amp; dust shelves daily/weekly depending on what is needed</li>
<li>Order office supplies</li>
<li>Organize new student kit equipment</li>
<li>Provide administrative help to the financial aid and academic offices, as needed</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<p><b>Ideal candidates possess the following qualities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Student-centered philosophy in support of academic excellence</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills, with a focus on quick response time</li>
<li>Effective problem-solving and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to learn and use necessary technology quickly and adeptly</li>
<li>Adaptability; able to succeed in a fast-paced and ever-changing work environment</li>
<li>Passion and enthusiasm for creating an amazing customer experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements of this position include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma/GED</li>
<li>Minimum of 1-2 years of customer service experience</li>
<li>Knowledge of office practices, including experience with Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Experience in the cosmetology setting a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Company History and Highlights</b></p>

We offer eligible employees the following benefits: dental insurance, life insurance, long-term disability insurance, medical insurance, a 401k plan, tuition benefits, flexible spending accounts and paid time off (PTO). Employees are encouraged to achieve personal and professional goals in a flexible, dynamic and success-oriented work environment.</p>
<p>Background screening will be conducted on final candidate as part of the hiring process.</p>
<p>Job Types: Full-time, Part-time</p>
