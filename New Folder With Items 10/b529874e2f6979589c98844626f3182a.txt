<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Basic Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
Interacts directly with prospective and current residents to achieve maximum occupancy. Responsible for the leasing process from property introduction to resident occupancy and maintains communication throughout the tenancy of the resident.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Provides information about the apartments, amenities, and community to the prospective resident and schedules visits to the community. Responsible for leasing apartments at an acceptable determined percent of total prospects.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Interview the prospective resident(s) to determine their needs and qualifications to lease.</li>
<li>
Completes a guest card on all prospective residents and enters into the property management software.</li>
<li>
Effectively explains all lease and community policies to new and current residents.</li>
<li>
Accurately and efficiently prepares and assists with all leasing paperwork (i.e. applications, lease agreements, employment, credit screening, landlord verification's and lease move-in packets).</li>
<li>
Collects security deposits, rent and all other funds associated with resident applications and move-ins.</li>
<li>
Enters service requests from residents into the property management software, closes work orders as requested by the manager.</li>
<li>
Supports the overall marketing effort and offers input and suggestions to property promotions, advertisements, pricing, etc.</li>
<li>
Understands and complies with all Fair Housing Laws and company standards.</li>
<li>
Monitors community advertisements by quantifying effectiveness of each source.</li>
<li>
Prepares monthly competitive surveys and makes recommendations on marketing based on those surveys.</li>
<li>
Monitors all current and future vacancies to proactively react to future occupancy needs.</li>
<li>
Inspects all “vacant rent ready” apartments on a regular basis to ensure they are up to company standards and ready to show to prospective residents.</li>
<li>
Works with the community manager to assure curb appeal and community appearance is acceptable.</li>
<li>
Conducts follow-up activity with all residents in order to establish positive resident relations. (ie- work order satisfaction, move in satisfaction, etc.)</li>
<li>
Maintains current resident files.</li>
<li>
Attends and participates in training seminars as needed and requested.</li>
<li>
Assists the Community Manager in implementing resident retention and renewal programs.</li>
<li>
Makes recommendations to management regarding improvements for the overall operation of the community.</li>
<li>
Attends and assists in the coordination and organization of resident relations and activities, such as keeping the company website updated, parties, etc.</li>
<li>
Represents Nexus in a professional manner at all times. Consistently maintains a professional courteous attitude when dealing with residents, co-workers and the general public.</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
