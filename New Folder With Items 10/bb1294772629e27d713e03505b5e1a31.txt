<h2>SDK Product Marketing Manager - Robotics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>NVIDIA is revolutionizing the way AI, robots and autonomous machines work together. We are looking for a <b>Product Marketing Manager </b>who is passionate about robotics to join NVIDIA’s developer product marketing group. Our team is focused on growing the developer ecosystem that uses NVIDIA's robotics software development kits. We need someone technical to build go-to-market plans and shepherd the creation of supporting marketing materials like sample applications, tutorials, white papers, and deeply technical presentations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Do you have the rare blend of both technical and marketing skills? We need hard-working and creative people who want to be working on state-of-the-art technology and are passionate about supporting the ecosystem of roboticists and developers. If so, we would love to learn more about you.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Building awareness </b>– You will educate developers on the features and capabilities of NVIDIAs Robotics SDKs to drive adoption.
</li>
<li>
<b>Creating content </b>– Generate developer-centric content, including blog posts, announcements, webinars, developer tutorials, and more to communicate the value proposition.
</li>
<li>
<b>Launching products </b>– Define the go-to-market (GTM) strategy and be responsible for the cross-functional implementation of the plan with product management, engineering, campaign marketing, PR, and social media teams
</li>
<li>
<b>Gathering market intelligence </b>– Understand your developer's needs through solid metrics, analytics and build assets to delight the developer community
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Some travel may be required for trade shows, conferences or technical sales training.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This is a public-facing role where you will evangelize and nurture the use of software development kits to grow the NVIDIA developer community.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What we need to see:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have excellent written and verbal communication and interpersonal skills with a proven ability to articulate a value proposition to technical and non-technical audiences.
</li>
<li>Experience presenting to technical audiences, executives and generating content for developers
</li>
<li>Ability to influence and drive decision-making with various stakeholders
</li>
<li>Passionate about getting things done quickly and efficiently
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently with minimal direction
</li>
<li>Please provide writing samples of public-facing technical content
</li>
<li>Product marketing or technical marketing experience with developer products
</li>
<li>Understanding of software engineering principles
</li>
<li>Previous experience with Python, C++ or other modern programming languages
</li>
<li>5+ years of meaningful work experience in a technical role at a technology company
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering, Computer Science, or similar field.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Ways to stand out from the crowd:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Masters degree in Computer Science or Computer Engineering or similar field
</li>
<li>Experience developing robotics applications with NVIDIAs Isaac SDK or ROS
</li>
<li>A good blend of strategic thinking and tactical execution
</li>
<li>Previous experience building developer ecosystems
</li>
<li>In-depth knowledge of modern deep learning software architecture and applications
</li>
<li>Experience developing AI applications such as computer vision and speech recognition
</li>
</ul>
