<h2>Audience Attendee Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The <b>Audience Attendee Development Specialist</b> manages marketing campaigns to drive attendance at CVC shows throughout the year and serves as a central point of communication in marketing for the vet group with other key departments, including editorial, show operations, digital, design, and sales. This position is key in executing our marketing programs on time and on budget and for testing and providing in depth analysis of their results in order to make data driven decisions.</p>
<br>
<div>
<p><b>PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>
Campaign Management (60%):</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates tactical promotional/marketing plans to achieve attendee goals efficiently while protecting against list fatigue within given budgets.</li>
<li>Develops data-driven marketing messaging, targeting and campaigns that are consistent with the strategy and show vision defined by senior management in assigned line of business.</li>
<li>Conducts a/b or multi-variant testing in all phases of marketing promotion plans and uses results to inform future promotion plans.</li>
<li>Coordinates marketing piece approval with show operations and editorial/creative teams to ensure the correct content is being created/promoted to deliver maximum impact.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Analytics/Reporting (30%):</p>
<ul>
<li>Completes routine database analysis to provide segmentation recommendation to meet attendee objectives.</li>
<li>Tracks and analyzes testing and reports results and creates recommendations and best practices re: tactics effectiveness.</li>
<li>Maintains an ROI report to correlate registered attendees to the marketing dollars spent to obtain them.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Other (10%):</p>
<ul>
<li>Attend CVC shows or other industry shows as necessary.</li>
<li>Seeks opportunity to advance both job-related and technical skills; is committed to increasing event marketing knowledge.</li>
<li>Remains up-to-date of competitive activities in veterinary industry and leverages insights to shape future marketing strategies.</li>
<li>Performs additional duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
JOB REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in business administration, marketing, marketing research, statistics or a related field or equivalent professional experience</li>
<li>At least one year experience in conference/show marketing, digital marketing, market research, or related position.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills (both written and verbal)</li>
<li>Strong reporting, writing, and copyediting skills</li>
<li>Strong proficiency with MS Office products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Experience with web analytics software, email deployment systems, social media platforms, and SEO/SEM preferred.
</li>
<li>Proven track record of generating quantifiable results, and working on cross-functional teams.</li>
</ul>
