<h2>Account Manager - Retention</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>WalkMe pioneered the Digital Adoption Platform (DAP) to transform the user experience in today’s overwhelming digital world. Using analytics, engagement, guidance, and automation, WalkMe’s transparent overlay assists users to complete tasks easily within any enterprise software, mobile application or website.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
WalkMe’s Renewals Managers are entrusted with the strategic relationship and commercial well-being of WalkMe’s existing startup edition customers. The Renewals Managers will ensure that the executive sponsors in their client portfolio are realizing positive results from their WalkMe license and in turn renewing or increasing their investment with WalkMe.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Do</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
A comprehensive understanding of WalkMe solutions and connect that knowledge directly to customer return on investment.</li>
<li>
Build relationships with client executive levels; make sure they are engaged and seeing value of WalkMe</li>
<li>
Maintain a high retention rate, ensure on-time renewals and create expansion opportunities (up-sell, add-on, cross-sell, etc.)</li>
<li>
Proactively identify customer risk and opportunity signals; develop comprehensive strategies to combat and/or limit</li>
<li>
Conduct regular business reviews with Customer Success Manager counterpart</li>
<li>
Understand competitive products and can speak at length to product differences that highlight WalkMe’s advantage</li>
<li>
Be an escalation point-of-contact for other relationship and commercial issues</li>
<li>
Develop strategies and coordinate diverse support to help customers maximize the value from WalkMe solutions</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities for the customer to expand their WalkMe investment, both within their existing platforms and from new ones</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Own</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
The Renewals Manager position is a quota carrying position. As an early member of the team, you'll help shape process, manage customer’s retention lifecycle, negotiate pricing, unearth expansion opportunities and close renewals to achieve annual targets.</li>
<li>
Your experience identifying business problems and working cross-functionally to provide solutions, as well as building relationships with important decision makers within the current accounts will be crucial to your success.</li>
<li>
You will direct business value assessment alongside WalkMe Customer Success Managers to ensure our customers gain the most return on investment from WalkMe.</li>
<li>
The success of this role means higher customer satisfaction, increased retention and expansion velocity.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You Need to Succeed</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
2-5 years of customer management experience at a software company [SaaS model] or management consultancy [Bain, Deloitte, McKinsey], with an emphasis on renewals and expansion</li>
<li>
2-5 years of commercial and technical knowledge, can discuss technical aspects alongside commercial negotiations</li>
<li>
Experience navigating with a wide-range of sophisticated contracts and legal discussions</li>
<li>
Ability to rapidly develop and deliver creative business solutions for complex business problems</li>
<li>
Experience with a variety of customer and commercial management tools and technologies [Salesforce, Excel, Jira, Totango, Google Apps, and Workday]</li>
<li>
Experience within a globally-dispersed, multilingual team</li>
<li>
General knowledge of cloud architecture as well as on-premise IT landscape</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong presentation skills and ability to establish credibility with C-level executives</li>
<li>
BA or BS required</li>
</ul></div>
