<h2>Sample Librarian - New York</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Phillip Jeffries is looking for a Sampling Librarian in New York City!<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Our Showroom Sales team is searching for a highly motivated Sample Librarian with a desire to learn about the various functions involved in running a luxury wallcovering showroom sample department. This position will be in our brand-new Flagship location in New York City within the D&amp;D Building.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
If you’re looking for a rapidly growing company that has the feel of a start-up and the foundation of an established business, Phillip Jeffries may be your next home!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
This position will report directly to the Operations and Sampling Lead.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What you will be doing:</b></p>
<p>
Organizational qualities to be demonstrated to the team by offering support and manage the memo process for clients
</p>
<p>Register new projects and prepare sample requests through company CRM software
</p>
<p>Support Inside/Outside Sales team by introducing him/herself to clients as an added resource while operating from the sample library station</p>
<p>
Own sample replenishment software process and exceed our team’s expectations of you your sample room abilities through our CRM software</p>
<p>
Maintain organization of the entire memo inventory</p>
<p>
Consistently organize existing sample catalogues and memos throughout the showroom
</p>
<p>Assist with seasonal roll-out of new collections</p>
<p>
Participate with special projects and showroom events as needed</p>
<p>
Acting as the owner for the library of samples for the entire library station maintaining efficiencies and suggesting improvements within your station on an ongoing basis</p>
<p>
Remaining in close contact with our sample replenishment department in NJ at our headquarters</p>
<p>
Maintains adequate levels of office supplies/order new supplies as needed</p>
<p>
Maintain a close team spirit with the operations lead by fulfilling and achieving daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly goals
</p>
<p>Additional support to be determined as needed</p>

Must have:</b></p>
<p>
Upbeat, positive, energetic, can-do and optimistic attitude</p>

Responsible and reliable</p>
