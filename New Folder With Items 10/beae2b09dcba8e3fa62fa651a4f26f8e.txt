<h2>Designer & Customer Service Contact</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>General Position Overview</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicates information among several departments (Sales, Prototype, Production) to ensure good Customer Relations and timely delivery of products</li>
<li>Designer &amp; Sales Manager act as primary customer contacts during quotation process: communicating status of project, suggested changes, impact of changes on cost and scheduling, etc. (Does not create quotation or submit quotation, but simply assist in quotation process)</li>
<li>Designs new or modified racks, pallets, and/or bins to meet customer specifications using automated design software ( SolidWorks (Required), CATIA (Preferred), etc.)</li>
<li>May evaluate, with Prototype &amp; Production Team, products, parts, or processes for cost efficiency and reliability</li>
<li>Coordinates design requests, changes, and scheduling of Prototype production with Assistant Manager, Prototype</li>
<li>Prepares bills of material (BOMs) for both, Prototype and Production requests</li>
<li>Completes customer-specific documentation processes during quotation process</li>
<li>Finalizes design for each project and issues to Production following customer review, changes, and receipt of approval</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Actions/ Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support Sales, Design, &amp; Production initiatives and activities as needed</li>
<li>Communicate customer requests, approvals, and material needs/changes to Sales, Design, Prototype, &amp; Production personnel</li>
<li>Issue bills of material, prototype drawings, and final production drawings within designated project timelines</li>
<li>Report weekly to Manager on project status, special activities, customer relations, etc.</li>
<li>Reach out to customers directly with specific questions regarding design.</li>
<li>Some Travel required</li>
<li>Must be flexible</li>
<li>Maybe requested to stay to complete assigned task</li>
</ul>
