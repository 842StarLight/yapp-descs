<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Training Manager, NA (External)
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>Essity is currently searching for a <b>Training Manager, NA (External).
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>This individual supports the Brand Activation Manager, NA in alignment with overall business strategy for continued profitable growth. This role will implement operationally efficient and strategic Customer Education training activities that drive growth for Essity HMS NA by driving Customer loyalty/retention; improving brand awareness for Essity’s products and services through clinical excellence; and driving successful utilization of Essity HMS products in care delivery.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU WILL DO:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Capture and analyze the desired Customer Education training programs and how the programs will be delivered (online, F2F, etc.) and to whom (Dealers, Directors of Nursing, Certified Nursing Assistants, Caregiving Relatives, etc).
</li>
<li>Leverage existing clinical curriculum and create customer-facing versions.
</li>
<li>Lead the development of and/or prepare respective customer education programs, processes, tools, and activities aligned to identified needs.
</li>
<li>Partner with Channel and Product Marketer (US/CA) peers to centrally develop customer education programs and events that ensure consistent activation of brand and channel GTM strategies and value propositions.
</li>
<li>Thoroughly understands salesforce business objectives and perception of the category and the brand/products and translates them into meaningful and impactful Customer experiences.
</li>
<li>Develop annual Customer Education training calendar with a focus on increasing brand awareness and preference, deepening product application knowledge, and support achieving 100% customer retention.
</li>
<li>Develop and execute methodologies for measuring and tracking A&amp;P investments against Customer utilization of programs and brand preference for clinical speakers and trainings from Essity HMS NA; leveraging scorecard practices
</li>
<li>Ensure compliance in the utilization of all clinical evidence with appropriate Regulatory, Compliance, and Legal partners.
</li>
<li>In partnership with National Clinical Directors (US/CA), owns and develops the regional (US/CA) plans and manages any necessary agency networks, driving for NA synergies and operational efficiencies wherever possible to deliver best ROI.
</li>
<li>Experience with content platforms (ACTO) for managing and delivering eLearning curriculum.
</li>
<li>Provides Train the Trainer presentations to field sales and Customers as needed.
</li>
<li>Assist the sales field by being available to address ongoing market needs
</li>
<li>Strategically and efficiently manage A&amp;P budget.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>WHO YOU ARE </b><b>:
</b></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business or marketing with a focus on clinical/customer-facing education and training.
</li>
<li>English Required (ability to work with translation company to French Canadian)
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft office suite and ability to work with graphics software - minimum Photoshop
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong clinical education program development and management experience – min 5 years
</li>
<li>Ability to activate training for all stages of B2B sales cycle.
</li>
<li>Sound understanding of the Healthcare environment. Incontinence and/or Medical Products training experience highly desired.
</li>
<li>Ability to quickly garner respect amongst peers (sales, marketing, finance, distribution and operations)
</li>
<li>Ability to identify tasks and take the initiative to take action
</li>
<li>Proven problem solver with strong ability to multi-task and handle project deadlines
</li>
<li>Demonstrable communication and collaborative skills
</li>
<li>Strong organizational and project management experience
</li>
<li>Proven ability to identify new market trends and knowledge of healthcare/government legislation and impact on customers
</li>
<li>Experience in converting technical product information to salesforce and customer relevant benefits
</li>
<li>Highly developed, demonstrated teamwork skills
</li>
</ul>
