<h2>Implementation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>We are excited to announce that Compliance Systems (CSi) is now part of CUNA Mutual Group!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Purpose</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Compliance Systems’ mission is to grow long-term sustainable recurring revenue and to achieve annual revenue goals through our Business Partners and direct sales to Financial Institutions (FI). Business Partners refers to any entity that distributes our products and services as an integrated part of their product lines or directly integrates our products and services for their own use. Our Financial Management team’s mission is to manage and preserve our recurring revenue growth and to provide exceptional support, training, and services to Clients.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Implementation Specialist I is responsible for implementing our solutions with FIs. This includes coordinating and communicating with specific members of the FI and Business Partner organizations to ensure combined solutions are implemented and the onboarding process meets the needs of our clients. Relative to other Implementation Specialist roles, generally, the person in this role has less autonomy, less familiarity with our product, and works with a lower volume of FIs and FIs with a smaller asset size. This position requires that the employee develops a deep understanding of our products and services and building a strong FI relationship throughout the implementation process. The expectation for this individual is to develop deep Compliance Systems product knowledge over time, have a recognized customer service orientation, and an ownership mentality. Also, the Implementation Specialist I must have strong intellect, results orientation, a passion for the Fintech industry, the ability to execute in a dynamic environment, with strong communication and basic project management skills. Specifically, under the full direction of the Implementation Manager, the Implementation Specialist I:</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Coordinates FI onboarding process. Ensures successful issue and process management. Learns the optimal use of Compliance Systems’ product functionality.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions and Basic Duties</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Essential functions, duties and responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned:</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Under the direction of the Implementation Manager, coordinates FI onboarding process.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Organize and manage all aspects of client implementations to ensure a successful deployment of the Compliance Systems product.</li>
<li>
Set expectations with client during kickoff to align on expected deliverables and stages of implementation.</li>
<li>
Coordinate the FI team member’s training with our CSi University team.</li>
<li>
Schedule and participate in kickoff and periodic status meetings with FI team members supporting the implementation process, addressing questions, and working through issues during user acceptance testing.</li>
<li>
Facilitate and track project issues via web-based tracking tool (e.g. Trello).</li>
<li>
Aid in the answer/resolve/triage of product and organization issues that may arise with clients during implementation. Escalate issues only after collecting all relevant information.</li>
<li>
Work closely with FI primary point of contact, Partner Implementation resources, and CSi University to ensure coordination of all areas to make every possible effort to meet the client’s desired go-live date.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Under the direction of the Implementation Manager, ensures successful issue and process management.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive process adherence and ensure day-to-day issue management using a consistent review process and our internal management tools (e.g., databases, JIRA issue tracking/CRM applications, bug-tracking tools, source controls, management reports, etc.). Activities should include the following: change process, issues process, research process, development process, and communication process.</li>
<li>
Review issues submitted via web-based tracking tool and/or discussed during UAT calls, create IR’s as directed, and communicate expectations for delivery and completion.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Under the direction of the Implementation Manager, learns the optimal use of Compliance Systems’ product functionality.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Learn the Compliance Systems’ features, services and solutions through research of client questions, recreation of reported issues, and by validating defect resolutions.</li>
<li>
Regularly ask yourself, “During training, how can the FI better understand and use our product, tools/resources and system?”</li>
</ul>
