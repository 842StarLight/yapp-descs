<h2>Banking Center Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<p><i>Are you an experienced Banker? Do you enjoy leading people and leveraging your expertise to grow business? If so, check out the Banking Center Manager role.</i></p>
<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The Banking Center Manager is responsible for managing the sales, service, profitability and operational activities of the banking center, in accordace with regulatory, compliance and procedural requirements.
</p>
<p>This includes ensuring exceptional customer service and leveraging that exceptional experience to grow the business foothold of the Bank in that particular market.</p>
<p><b>
REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent required</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree preferred</li>
<li>3 - 5 Years experience in function
</li>
<li>3 - 5 Years experience in industry
</li>
<li>NMLS registration required</li>
<li>Demonstrated success in leading, motivating and influencing; strong coaching, motivational and training skills, focused on sales, service and operations.</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge and understanding of retail products, services, regulations and compliance.
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills with the ability to deliver effective communication (verbal and written) proactively with a customer-oriented approach.</li>
<li>Results oriented, with proven success in consultative selling; ability to generate new business sales, retain existing business, and to close sales.</li>
<li>Excellent problem solving, decision making and organizational skills; strong attention to detail, customer responsiveness, and timely follow up/completion of tasks.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office and core banking software required.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p><b>
Team Leadership</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead a team of sales and service professionals to meet and exceed sales and service goal targets, ensuring an exceptional customer experience, consistent with Chemical Bank's service standards.</li>
<li>Recruit, develop and retain key talent for the banking center; ensure the training needs of the banking center team are met, including product knowledge and operational training.</li>
<li>Coach, mentor, guide, observe and provide feedback to team members; motivate, recognize and reward team members for achievement of sales and service goals.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Retail Sales Manager and HR in matters related to employee development, mentoring, compensation, recruitment, performance reviews and disciplinary action.</li>
<li>Facilitate daily, weekly, monthly banking center team meetings.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Sales/Business Development</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop, implement and manage a plan to meet personal and banking center goals, capitalizing on the unique opportunities of the banking center market, which includes generating and retaining core deposits.</li>
<li>Generate new business through prospecting, outside calling, networking and referrals.
</li>
<li>Actively call on current customers to retain, strengthen and deepen relationships.</li>
<li>Build and leverage relationships with business partners to create referral opportunities across lines of business.</li>
<li>Monitor banking center activities and results to ensure sales success, keeping Retail Sales Manager abreast of successes and challenges.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Banking Center Management/Operations</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the smooth and efficient operations of the banking center, ensuring that activities are completed in compliance with all applicable procedural and regulatory rules and guidelines.</li>
<li>As a member of the platform team, act as a sales and service consultant to the customer; conduct relationship reviews, open new accounts, process loans and operate a cash drawer when necessary.</li>
<li>Research, identify and resolve complex customer problems / issues while providing the highest levels of customer service.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance and audit related activities are successfully completed in a timely manner and in accordance with prescribed protocol; identify operational and service deficiencies and implement
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Community Immersion
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify opportunities for community involvement; lead, participate and encourage team participation in the community/Community Reinvestment Act efforts and activities.</li>
<li>Identify key sponsorship, marketing and advertising connections.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Other / Miscellaneous</b></p>
<ul><li>Provide support on sales campaigns and other initiatives.</li></ul>
