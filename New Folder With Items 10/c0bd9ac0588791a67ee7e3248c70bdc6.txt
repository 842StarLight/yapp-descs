<h2>Software (SaaS) Inside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview:
</b></p>
<p>YOU are driven, dedicated, and have what it takes to smash quotas and succeed in software sales. You’re interested in making great money and like the idea of working in a start-up environment. Maybe you even have some prior experience selling for a marketing or fundraising software company. Sound familiar? If so, come join one of DC's Hottest Companies where can you do well by doing good.</p>
<p>
Salsa Labs offers…</p>
<ul>
<li>
A dedicated training program that builds your knowledge of the nonprofit market</li>
<li>
A culture that offers snacks, a laid back dress code, and the ability to work in a meritocracy</li>
<li>
A base salary plus commission/bonus potential and plenty of room for growth</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Recruit new clients to Salsa’s Community via a mixture of cold calling and warm leads</li>
<li>
Achieve monthly, quarterly and annual goals and metrics as outlined by sales management</li>
<li>
Maintain a strong pipeline of qualified, prospective clients</li>
<li>
Update client data and communications within Salesforce CRM</li>
<li>
Conduct software product demonstrations virtually using screen share technology</li>
<li>
Develop proposals and respond to RFP’s in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Continuously educate yourself on the latest digital fundraising and advocacy best practices in the nonprofit space</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What We’re Looking For:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Driven sales performer with demonstrated ability to meet/exceed quotas</li>
<li>
Minimum 2 years successful inside sales experience selling a Software-as-a-Service solution</li>
<li>
Very comfortable with and does not shy away from making cold calls</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills, and excellent attention to detail</li>
<li>
College degree or equivalent experience (required)</li>
<li>
Salesforce CRM experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Benefits of Working at Salsa Labs:</b></p>
<p>
Aside from working with smart, passionate Salsa employees, having uncapped growth potential and an awesome work/life balance, this position includes a competitive suite of benefits. Benefits include health, dental and vision insurance, 401K with immediate enrollment, life insurance, short and long-term disability insurance, stock options, paid holidays, unlimited paid time-off and no dress code!</p>
<p><b>
Location &amp; Work Environment:</b></p>
<p>
Just a few blocks from the Bethesda Metro on the Red Line, our office is located at 7920 Norfolk Ave, Suite 550, Bethesda, MD 20814. Our culture is fast-paced, creative and innovative with a casual work environment.</p>
