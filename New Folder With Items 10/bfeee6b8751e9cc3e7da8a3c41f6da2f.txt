<h2>Digital Signal Processing (DSP) Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Are you looking for a position that provides you the environment and support to explore innovative ideas? Do you seek a position where you can apply your academic knowledge to real world applications? If this sounds like the flexibility and satisfaction you seek, consider applying today to become a <b>Digital Signal Processing (DSP) Engineer </b>with us at Vibration Research.</p>
<p>Vibration Research (VR) is a family owned and operated, global corporation headquartered in West Michigan. At VR, we lead the vibration control system industry with innovative solutions and customer service second to none.  Advanced hardware capabilities combined with powerful, user-friendly software make VR controllers the premier choice for companies and testing labs around the globe. Our diverse customer base ranges from small facilities to Fortune 500 companies. We don’t like to brag but we get to work on amazing projects with the coolest companies around.Just to name a few<i>…Boeing, US Naval Research Lab, BMW, Polaris, L3, Gentex and University of Michigan!</i>  Strong customer relationships are built on excellent products and strong technical support.</p>
<p>Currently we seek an analytical, team player who has an entrepreneurial spirit and innovative problem-solving skills to join our highly intelligent development team. As a <b>DSP Engineer</b>, you will report directly to our Sr. VP of Product Development and will serve as our subject matter expert (SME) for all things digital signal processing.</p>
<p>As a <b>DSP Engineer</b>, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and program digital signal-processing algorithms to establish, maintain, and alter digital signals for analysis and control of sound and vibration</li>
<li>Develop, document and maintain architectures, requirements, algorithms, interfaces and designs for embedded real time software systems</li>
<li>Determine operational feasibility, perform analysis, define problems, determine requirements, and develop solutions</li>
<li>Provide system and program documentation, flowcharts, layouts, diagrams, charts, and code comments as needed</li>
<li>Confirm system and program operation by conducting rigorous testing</li>
<li>Strive to improve the performance and reliability of the existing software base</li>
<li>Maintain historical records by documenting program development and revisions</li>
<li>Provide support and documentation of released software to the sales and support team</li>
<li>Keep job knowledge up to date - study state-of-the-art development tools, programming techniques, and computing equipment; participate in educational opportunities; read professional publications; maintain personal networks; participate in professional organizations</li>
</ul>
