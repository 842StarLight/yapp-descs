<h2>Accounts Receivable Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>ATI, Inc. is an Industrial Insulation, Scaffolding and Coatings Contractor, primarily working in the Industrial Construction Industry. ATI, Inc. started in 2001 and now has multiple locations in CO., WY. &amp; TX. We offer competitive pay and a full benefits package. <b>The successful candidate will be located at our Grand Junction, CO location.</b></p>
<p>
We are committed to provide our Employees a stable work environment with equal opportunity for learning and personal growth. The success of ATI, Inc. is built by committed, efficient employees who believe in our company, who gain satisfaction from their work, and who strive toward extraordinary achievement. We believe that people work together best in an environment where they are recognized for their efforts and respected as individuals.</p>
<p>
The Accounts Receivable Manager will ensure that all money owed to the company is billed and received correctly in a timely fashion, as well as ensuring that all related costs have been coded and input correctly. Reports directly to the Controller and will have indirect reporting responsibilities to other executive managers.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assign and monitor clerical and administrative responsibilities and tasks among office staff</li>
<li>
Monitor contract billing requirements and change orders</li>
<li>
Handle customer inquiries and disputes</li>
<li>
Review customer invoices and supporting documentation</li>
<li>
Allocate available resources to enable successful task performance, and monitor office staff activities to ensure maximum efficiency</li>
<li>
Review new contracts and coordinate adjustments and compliance requirements prior to signature</li>
<li>
Setup and track new jobs</li>
<li>
Follow up on and collect customer payments</li>
<li>
Perform account reconciliations and job folder audits</li>
<li>
Review and approve invoices generated by staff</li>
<li>
Any other tasks assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Personnel Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>

<li>
Train new and existing staff members</li>
<li>
Evaluate and manage staff performance</li>
<li>
Coordinate and maintain positive communication between field and administrative personnel</li>
</ul>
