<p></p>
<p><b>Catering Service Specialist
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>About the position:
</b></p>
<p>The Catering Service Specialist is an office based, customer focused position responsible for nurturing relationships with clients utilizing both verbal and digital communication through the entire catering order lifecycle – from assistance with menu questions and guidance around solutions to fit a customer’s event or budget need, to confirmation of orders, post-delivery follow up, account maintenance activities and troubleshooting issues as they arise.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The role works closely with the management team to ensure we provide the best service possible to every customer.
</p>
<ul>
<li>As a Catering Service Specialist you will have a thorough knowledge and understanding of the Panera menu
</li>
<li>Understand the needs of our customers, and provide appropriate communication to ensure satisfaction by Engaging, Exploring and Exceeding
</li>
<li>Work as a team player with a passion for continuous learning
</li>
<li>Build catering sales through strong customer relationships, providing great customer service and involvement in sales building activities
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>We’re looking for:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Great verbal and written communication and people skills
</li>
<li>Food or retail sales/service experience preferred
</li>
<li>Basic computer skills and comfort with learning to use different software applications including Outlook and SalesForce
</li>
<li>Ability to handle engaging customers by phone while entering data into a computer related to their orders
</li>
<li>Well-developed personal organization skills
</li>
<li>Strong detail orientation
</li>
<li>Team success philosophy
</li>
<li>Strong problem solving/customer resolution and time management
</li>
<li>Must have own transportation for assistance with customer deliveries on occasion and acceptable driving record
</li>
<li>Minimum of 18 years of age
 </li>
</ul>
