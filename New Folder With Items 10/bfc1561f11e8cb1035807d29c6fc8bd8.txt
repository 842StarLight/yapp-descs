<h2>Account Strategy & Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>As an integral member of the Knowledge Hub Media Sales Team, you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect and identify potential clients/customers within the enterprise software/hardware and business solutions sector.</li>
<li>Evaluate and educate potential clients/customers on the value of Knowledge Hub Media’s B2B lead generation and advertising services.</li>
<li>Take a consultative approach with existing and prospective clients to identify their marketing and lead generation needs.</li>
<li>Sell lead generation and marketing campaigns that are finely targeted, and tailored towards your client’s niche audience.</li>
<li>Track your sales pipeline and activity utilizing Salesforce.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Ideally, you will have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 2 years of B2B advertising sales experience.</li>
<li>A desire to be part of an industry changing organization.</li>
<li>A Bachelor’s Degree.</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce or similar CRM software.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Your compensation will include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Base salary plus bonuses and commission.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Location:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ideally you will be located in the Philadelphia, Pennsylvania area. However, we are also open to hiring remote sales executives who are motivated self-starters (who are comfortable working from home). Please attach your resume and a cover letter</li>
</ul>
