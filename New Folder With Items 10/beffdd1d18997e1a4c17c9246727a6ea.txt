<h2>Director of Business Planning</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Position Overview:</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a Director of Business Planning to lead Universal Audio’s business planning and analysis processes. This role will collaborate with the executive team and product groups to evaluate and influence new product and business opportunities, promotion and merchandising decisions and the overall corporate business model. The successful candidate will demonstrate strong financial, analytical and influence skills, as well as hardware and B2C software experience. This is currently an individual contributor role reporting directly to the CFO.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul><li> Collaborate and partner with multiple cross functional teams (Product/Program Management, Engineering, Sales &amp; Marketing, Finance, Operations, etc.) to help manage and drive product planning &amp; forecasting, product pricing, licensing/development agreements, new product introductions, promotions &amp; product business cases.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Analyze complex business problems and issues using data from internal and external sources to provide insight to decision-makers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Build, maintain and automate complex and dynamic financial models to provide data driven, actionable insights to drive product growth and other strategic objectives.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Identify investment requirements for development efforts, generate Business and Product Unit P&amp;L’s and automate data collection, reporting and analysis.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with business partners to understand and model customer buying behavior including initial hardware platform and follow-on software and services. Own and recommend financial impacts.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Perform projections and ROI analysis on new products, product promotions and acquisitions. Analyze actuals vs expectations and recommend fine-tuning to optimize performance.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Construct forecasts, recommendations and strategic/tactical plans based on business data and market knowledge.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Conduct various ad hoc analyses and provide general financial/data support to help business partners solve problems and understand the financial implications.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop recommendations for optimizing business and financial performance.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Create and deliver clear and effective executive-level presentations and collaborate with executive team and other stakeholders on an ongoing basis.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Create specifications for reporting and analysis based on business needs and required or available data elements.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul><li> BS/BA in finance, economics or equivalent. MBA or other Advanced Financial degree preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li> 7+ years in Business Planning, Financial Planning and Analysis or Pricing in a high technology company.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong understanding of financial concepts and metrics, research, analytical and modeling skills to enable proper business decisions.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Exceptional team player with a positive attitude and a focus on business issues.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent written, verbal, communication and influence skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Expert Excel skills including both data analysis and forward-looking models.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Detail-oriented, organized, and able to multi-task and shift priorities quickly, while maintaining control of deliverables.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with Adaptive Insights, Oracle Hyperion Planning or other forecasting/analysis platform a plus.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Highly motivated and able to thrive and think clearly under pressure and tight deadlines.</li></ul>
