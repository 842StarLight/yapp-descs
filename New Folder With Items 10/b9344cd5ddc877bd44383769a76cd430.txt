<h2>Lids District Mgr in Training (DMIT)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive sales by delivering exceptional customer service per the Operations P&amp;P Manual.</li>
<li>
Successfully complete the DMIT Training Program to enhance core skill set (ie: recruiting, developing, coaching, leading and training).</li>
<li>
Train store personnel on standard operating procedures as well as State and Federal laws and regulations.</li>
<li>
Communicate consistently with store personnel to insure understanding and compliance with standard operating procedures.</li>
<li>
Act as the liaison between the District Sales Manager and the stores under the DMIT’s direction.</li>
<li>
Take appropriate actions to insure each store maximizes sales, minimizes asset loss, and conforms to budgetary requirements.</li>
<li>
Extensive travel(up to 90%) from store-to-store, within a given geographic area</li>
<li>
Perform work of subordinates as needed.</li>
<li>
Support and adhere to all company policies, procedures, and guidelines.</li>
<li>
Communicate with employees at all levels of the company.</li>
<li>
Ability and willingness to travel overnight for training and/or business meetings.</li>
<li>
Ability to work varying days and hours, based on business needs.</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain an excellent attendance record.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Supervisory Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Carry out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with LIDS policies, procedures, and applicable laws.</li>
<li>
Supervise Store Managers through planning, assigning, and directing their overall functions.</li>
<li>
Appraise subordinates on positive performance and assist with formal performance evaluations.</li>
<li>
Recruit, select, develop, and train store personnel on proper store operations and procedures.</li>
<li>
Administer the progressive steps of discipline to include verbal and written warnings. In addition, the DMIT carries out employment terminations following approval from Human Resources and DSM.</li>
<li>
Direct compliance of store personnel with established company policies, procedures and guidelines including, but not limited to, safekeeping of company inventory, funds and property.</li>
<li>
Address complaints and problem solve when appropriate with the assistance of the DSM and or RD.</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h2>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Four year degree in business or a related field and one year relevant experience or; two year degree in a related field and at least two years of relevant experience or; three years of relevant experience.</li>
<li>
Proven supervisory skills</li>
<li>
Proven ability to perform independently with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to communicate verbally in a clear, audible, and grammatically correct manner.</li>
<li>
Ability to operate a computer, as well as maneuver relative software programs.</li>
<li>
Posses a current valid driver’s license, certificate of insurance coverage and the ability to drive an automobile.</li>
<li>
Driving required for up to 100% of the daily work schedule.</li>
<li>
Standing required for up to 90% of the daily work schedule.</li>
<li>
Relative computer hardware and software experience.</li>
<li>
Ability and willingness to travel overnight for training and/or business meetings.</li>
</ul>
