<h2>Office 365 Security & Compliance Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The <b>Office 365 Security and Compliance Consultant </b>position is primarily focused on service delivery activities that are supported and delivered by Epiq’s Information Governance practice group. The geographic scope is global with an emphasis on activity delivered within North America. The focus of the position is providing advisory and project-based offerings related to the security and compliance capabilities embedded within the Office 365 platform. The position is expected to be directly billable on an hourly or fixed project fee basis with annual billable utilization targets applied individually and practice-wide.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide advisory, administrative, and project-based services related to the security and compliance capabilities embedded within the Office 365 platform which include legal hold and preservation management, privacy compliance and data protection, and advanced eDiscovery, amongst others.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with or provide general supervision to other information governance associates or 3rd party contractors to execute on project tasks, including migration project design and best practices.
</li>
<li>Support various sales motions by advising clients on the value and efficiency of Epiq’s Information Governance service offerings as they relate to compliance challenges enabled through the use of Office 365 and adjacent technologies.
</li>
<li>Provide advisory services related to improving an enterprise’s experience in utilizing the embedded compliance features within Office 365.
</li>
<li>Provide active project support including coverage for peers.
</li>
<li>Support internal training and talent development initiatives
</li>
<li>Contribute to strategic business initiatives within the information governance practice
</li>
<li>Contribute to product assessments for emerging technology platforms and new service offerings.
</li>
<li>Perform administrative tasks that support the practice’s objectives, as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Qualifications &amp; Characteristics
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience: 3+ years of demonstrable advisory and administrative assignments related to the security and compliance functions found within Microsoft Office 365 and other related enterprise products.
</li>
<li>Education: Bachelor degree is required; professional certifications are preferred.
</li>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communication skills. Exceptional problem solving skills.
</li>
<li>Independent direction for daily tasks.
</li>
<li>Collaborative mindset and service orientation
</li>
<li>Domestic U.S. and international travel is variable and up to 35% annually
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>If you like wild growth and working with happy, enthusiastic over-achievers, you'll enjoy your career with us!
