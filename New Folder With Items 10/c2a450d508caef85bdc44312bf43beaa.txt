A day in your life as a Merchant Services Strategy Operations and Controls Manager:</div>
<div>
Under the direction of the Merchant Services Unit Manager, this position is responsible for managing the accounting, operational, reporting, audit and financial controls for the department, while assisting the Unit Manager in fulfilling fiduciary duties related to the profitability and soundness of the business. Assists in business unit’s compliance with applicable laws, regulations, regulatory requirements and Bank policies and procedures, including but not limited to those related to Fair Banking, Anti-Money Laundering laws and regulations, Bank Secrecy Act and USA PATRIOT Act.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We’ll give you these important responsibilities…</div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Responsible for the collection, review, creation and preparation of departments reporting such as QPR, compensation packages and payouts, annual Stress Test and other financial reporting. This position is responsible for assisting the Sales Officers, PRO’s and Relationship Officers with exception pricing models with focus on profitability. Updates and ongoing management of the financial matrix called the XMOD is also involved. Collaborates with the MS management team with a focus on Semi-annual Industry Card Brand Releases and their financial impact to the bank and its merchants.</div></li>
<li><div>
Supervises the daily operations and workflow of the department’s accounting and reporting to ensure the accurate and timely preparation of operational, performance and Regulatory/Association reports for the department. Creates a strategic development and marketing plan to monitor sales/retention effort and goals for the Retention team, includes unit and individual goals. Creates reporting for Retention team to measure and forecast sales/retention plan results. Provides reporting to track vendor and card brand performances.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assists the Unit Manager in preparing the department’s Capital and Operating budgets. Analyzes and conducts research to prepare budget variance responses and to assist the Unit Manager in preparing periodic financial performance reports for upper management, such as the QPR, BPR and Open and Closed reports.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assists the Unit Manager in the strategic planning process by providing and analyzing financial, departmental and industry data. This may include historical as well as pro forma analyses.</div></li>
<li><div>
Collaborates with the Controllers department and periodically evaluates the daily operations and workflow, ensuring that all transactions were conducted accurately and that cash and general ledger accounts reconcile with external funding and settlement reports in a timely manner. Completes and reports monthly budget analysis and reporting variances to the Unit Manager.</div></li>
<li><div>
Coordinates the financial activities of the department’s audits. Works with auditors to provide requested information, including department policies, procedures, industry regulations etc. Communicates audit activities and findings to the department managers and coordinates responses to citations, corrective measures and procedures to avoid repeat citations.</div></li>
<li><div>
Keeps informed and abreast of current vendor contracts, Bank policies and procedures; federal laws, regulations and Card Association’s rules and bylaws that may have financial impact to the department. This may include business enhancements, pricing changes, vendor’s operational changes and product development and performs all other miscellaneous responsibilities and duties as assigned.</div></li>
</ul></div>
