<h2>Product Owner - Solution Team</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Socrata, now the Data &amp; Insights Division of Tyler Technologies, helps governments use data more strategically and effectively in the design and delivery of their programs and missions. <b>Our data platform and cloud-based solutions support the world’s most ambitious data programs at every level of government</b><b> and a</b><b>re a cornerstone of Tyler Technologies’ Connected Communi</b><b>ties strategy.<br>
</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>We are looking for a well-rounded, data-driven <b>Product Owner</b> to become a foundational member of a cross-division Solution Development team. In this role, you will dive deep into the workflows and business problems impacting Tyler’s 21,000 local government customers throughout the United States and develop data-driven analytical and transparency solutions leveraging the Socrata data platform.</div>
<p><b>
What You’ll be Doing</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a <b>Product Owner</b> to lead the design, development, and market introduction of new, domain-specific Socrata solutions for Tyler customers in the justice, public safety, community development, and public administration verticals. You will have a great deal of scope and latitude to define and execute your product’s roadmap; work with customers and other Tyler divisions to inform our view of the market; work with engineers to prioritize and build our products; collaborate with Product Marketing to execute a successful go-to-market strategy; be a coveted resource for Sales on strategic accounts; and support our leadership team with strategic market and P&amp;L analysis.</p>
<p>
This is a new role, and the person who fills it will have a unique opportunity to help shape the vision and process for how the Socrata platform supports the delivery of <b>Tyler Technologies’ Connected Communities strategy.</b></p>
<p><i>
Candidates for this position are ideally located in our Seattle office. We may also be open to someone being located in any of our major Tyler office locations, specifically DC, Troy, Plano, Duluth, Dayton, DC, Yarmouth, or Lakewood.</i><br>
<br>
<b>On a Typical Day You Might…</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Assess the market potential of a handful of valuable use cases in a data-rich vertical such as property appraisal and tax, and pick one to develop into a solution.
</li>
<li> Work with your UX partner to develop a design prototypes that tell the story of your next release and be ready to articulate story in different ways to future customers, division leaders in our company, and engineers on your team.</li>
<li> Collaborate with partners and subject matter experts from other Tyler divisions, customers and technical staff to refine the roadmap for a data solution.</li>
<li> Manage feature tradeoff decisions across the product roadmap.</li>
<li> Collaborate with local and distributed engineering teams to serve as the voice of the customer and the market.</li>
<li> Dive deep into a government customer’s specific process to understand how data can be used to achieve a better outcome.</li>
<li> Work closely with UX researchers and designers to refine an existing capability or proposed adaptation.</li>
<li> Complete a market landscape analysis and understand how Tyler can use data and insights to enhance its competitive position.</li>
<li> Hop on the phone or in a plane to support a strategic sale, unblock an implementation or better understand a customer frustration.</li>
<li> Find a clever way to celebrate the team’s success at achieving an important milestone.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
You Have…</b></p>
<ul>
<li> A passion for (or at least latent interest in) public service, civic technology, and building the future of government.
</li>
<li> A BA, BS or advanced degree in a relevant field (Business, CS, Statistics, etc.) or equivalent certifications and real-world experience.</li>
<li> 2+ years of experience in product management, UX Design, or product development.</li>
<li> An understanding of your role as both a technical lead and a business owner, responsible for the end-to-end success of a product portfolio.</li>
<li> An iterative mindset and approach, including a strong grounding in agile methodologies and frameworks.</li>
<li> Insatiable intellectual curiosity and willingness to quickly acquire expertise about novel subjects and problems.</li>
<li> Experience or comfort working with both co-located and remote engineering teams.</li>
<li> Experience bringing a software product to market and iterating based on customer and market feedback.</li>
<li> A data-driven approach to product prioritization, and experience developing Total Addressable Markets (TAMs), pro formas, and other business planning artifacts.</li>
<li> The aptitude and appetite to develop an end-to-end understanding of the business. You need to be able to understand the needs of, and influence, engineers, marketers, sellers, customer service, and customers.
</li>
<li> Technical chops. You must be able to influence and motivate talented and brilliant engineers.
</li>
<li> Indirect leadership skills. Effectiveness in this role requires you to communicate a vision and drive multiple teams toward execution without direct authority.
</li>
<li> Strong presentation skills and experience in support of technology sales a plus.</li>
<li> Ability to develop and deliver creative business solutions for complex problems.</li>
<li> Excellent project management skills.</li>
<li> Comfortable and confident making the hard choices necessary to hit a tight deadline.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
It would be great if you also have…</b></p>
<ul>
<li> An MPA, MPP, or other degree or certification relevant to government policy and governance.</li>
<li> Direct experience building software for government clients, either as a public servant, vendor or contractor.</li>
<li> Experience in an enterprise SaaS environment, including knowledge of cloud providers and technologies.</li>
</ul>

<div>
Tyler Technologies (NYSE: TYL) is a leading provider of end-to-end information management solutions and services for local governments. Tyler partners with clients to empower the public sector - cities, counties, schools and other government entities - to become more efficient, more accessible and more responsive to the needs of their constituents. Tyler's client base includes more than 15,000 local government offices in all 50 states, Canada, the Caribbean, Australia, and other international locations. In 2017, Forbes ranked Tyler on its "Most Innovative Growth Companies" list, and Fortune included Tyler on its "100 Fastest-Growing Companies" list. Headquartered in Plano, Texas, Tyler has more than 4,200 employees in multiple divisional and regional offices across the U.S. You can learn more about Tyler Technologies at www.tylertech.com.<br>
