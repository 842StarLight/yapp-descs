<h2>OUTREACH ENROLLMENT COORDINATOR</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<p>The Online Enrollment Center (OEC) is a Chancellor and Trustee initiative, part of a new, shared services strategy for driving enrollment growth by creating a student outreach and service center for USNH that provides consolidated enrollment services for all participating USNH institutions. The OEC serves as the virtual service center for enrollment campaigns by providing best in class telephone, email, online and outreach student-centric services.</p>
<p>
Enrollment Specialists in the OEC serve as the first point of contact for prospective students. Enrollment Specialists introduce new students to the University System of New Hampshire campuses. May assist in development of articulation agreements.</p>
<p>
The OEC will provide follow-up to campus initiated campaigns and may provide essential information, answer questions, advise and provide problem resolution for current and potential students, parents/families, in the areas of registration, enrollment, financial aid, transfer programs and articulation agreements.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Duties / Responsibilities</b></h2>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>Job Duties
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Duty/Responsibility</b></div>
<p>Managing the enrollment process from uncovering opportunities, developing solutions, closing opportunities and on-going exemplary customer service. Using independent, sound, judgment, make decisions about a prospective student’s enrollment status (accepted or not) based on their qualifications, appropriate fit, transfer credit information, financial aid status, etc. Has authority to make an independent assessment of the fit for their program and institution based on criteria and policies developed by the team during the online enrollment specialists training program. Understands and applies frameworks for ethical decision making during the enrollment phase. Using policies developed and decided on by the OES team during the training phase, and including the criteria above, decide and place students into appropriate degree programs at the various USNH institutions. This may include guiding prospective students through the institutions’ enrollment processes including answering questions relating to program requirements, transfer credits, payment options, required documentation and admission criteria. It also requires managing an appropriate follow up cycle and process including verbal and written communications to ensure that students are contacted with appropriate frequency and supported throughout the enrollment process as guided by campus requirements. Maintains positive relationships with students to help support program persistence and generate referral activity. They engage with prospective and Outbound phone calls and serves as a primary point of contact for student questions and concerns including but not limited to degree requirements, programs, policies and procedures. Identify obstacles to enrollment and has authority to make decisions allowing a student to enroll as applicable.</p>
</div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>Job Duties
</div>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div><b>Duty/Responsibility</b></div>
<p>Utilizing and managing the ECM and institution’s CRM and computer/web based systems, is responsible for tracking activities and individual projects. Utilizing Salesforce, APEX reports, Banner, and other systems and social media to best reach students and document outreach efforts. Must be able to analyze and draw conclusions from the data leading to on-point delivery for registrations and advise the Enrollment Center Manager regarding the enrollment playbook, sales processes, sales pipeline and sales forecasts. Makes policy and procedure decisions to assess the data used to enroll students in the various programs. Uses independent judgment to revise policies as new information is learned to increase enrollment in USNH programs. Undergoes extensive training by campus experts to understand the complexities of degree requirements and program specifics at the various institutions and must demonstrate competence and apply knowledge of these areas, along with legal knowledge of financial aid requirements, etc. in their work. Also must be trained and competent in using advanced software applications to help in enrolling students and meeting performance goals. This position requires a high volume of outbound calling.</p>
