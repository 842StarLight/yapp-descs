<h2>Marketing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Description<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>At a Glance</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Legrand </b>has an exciting opportunity for a <b>Marketing Analyst</b> to join the Electrical Wiring Systems<b> Team</b> in <b>West Hartford, CT</b>. Working closely with Product Marketing Manager, maintains and updates product line information and marketing materials. Responds to requests for information from sales teams and customers. Coordinates sales and operations planning process for Infloor product lines. Works with internal project teams to provide accurate forecasts of future business.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What Will You Do?</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Manage data collection and analysis for monthly forecast process, work with operations and marketing director(s), to develop and deliver a purchasing and operation plan which reflects the current business needs of the organization.
</li>
<li>Evaluate processes used to gather and analyze data and apply continuous improvement process to increase forecast accuracy. Utilize forecasting tools as the foundation for building in new business as it enters our operations.
</li>
<li>Responsible for loading and tracking forecast accuracy for sales through Electrical Distribution, Retail, Intercompany, House and OEM channels.</li>
<li>
Working closely with Forecast Manager assist with development of the line item budget by channel, including the review and inclusion of new products for the upcoming year, average sell prices, price loss, and the monthly spread of the budget.
</li>
<li>Develop evaluation tools and procedures to ensure the forecast accuracy for the total channel, including new products, stocked products, and manufacturing family forecast as reported to Legrand.</li>
<li>
Performs other similar and related duties as required.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Required Skills</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors degree marketing, accounting, business</li>
<li>
3-5 years of system/data research and analysis</li>
<li>
Excel and Access Skills</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Preferred Skills: </b>:<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Requires strong organizational and time management skills, close attention to detail and be able to effectively handle multiple priorities.
</li>
<li>Exceptional computer skills, extensive knowledge of word processing, spreadsheet, presentation and data base software, proficiency with various e-mail, calendaring, meeting management and related communications software.</li>
<li>
Must be strong interpersonal skills, with the ability to interact with individuals at all levels within the organization. Must be able to work independently as well as within a variety of teams.
</li>
<li>Must have strong analytical skills and proven ability to effectively conduct in-depth research, identify trends and summarize information on a variety of subjects.
</li>

Able to create effective reports, presentations and business correspondence.<br>
</li>
</ul>
