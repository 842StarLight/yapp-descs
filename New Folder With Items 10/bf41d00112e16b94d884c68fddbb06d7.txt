<h2>Product Marketing Manager (PMM)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>VMware is a global leader in cloud infrastructure and business mobility. The mission of the VMware End User Computing (EUC) Business is to create consumer simple, enterprise secure technology that enhances the skills and lifestyles of the most valuable resource within every organization – their people. We enable individuals to pick the digital tools that empower them to do their best work so they can accomplish their ambitions and enjoy their lives. We do this by creating software that enables enterprises to deliver streamlined, simple experiences for their employees so they can use the digital tools of their choice while providing the connectivity, security and management that enterprises require to operate at scale.</p>
<p>
Join our fast-paced team that is driving VMware’s success in End User Computing and the Digital Workspace.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Our team is looking for a Product Marketing Manager (PMM) to focus on product marketing for analytics across our EUC Business Unit. As a PMM, you will be a member of the team responsible for defining, positioning (internally and externally) and driving the messaging, strategy and execution of the go-to-market plan for analytics in our digital workspace platform, Workspace ONE. You will be actively engaged in owning activities related to the enablement of our sales and partners and in ensuring our customer message for analytics is consistent across all marketing programs and channels. Your role will require an understanding of the current market for analytics across endpoints, users, apps and networks to help develop strategy to help drive our Workspace ONE messaging related to analytics. You’ll work closely with EUC product management, sales specialists, alliances and our security ecosystem of partners supporting Workspace ONE Intelligence to develop thought leadership and engagement.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You can expect to perform tasks such as these:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Deliver compelling marketing content to drive go-to-market and awareness generation efforts for Workspace ONE and Workspace ONE Intelligence, including but not limited to web updates, social media marketing, etc.</li>
<li>
Maintain an understanding of the competitive landscape to properly position and differentiate VMware’s portfolio</li>
<li>
Assess partner landscape to define future ecosystem synergies for our EUC Business Unit</li>
<li>
Understand VMware’s portfolio and participate in cross-functional marketing activities and programs</li>
<li>
Engage with customers, regional marketing and sales leaders to gather intelligence and feedback to develop relevant and compelling marketing strategies and messages</li>
<li>
Participate in launches, sales support, customer executive briefings, PR/AR, naming and pricing/packaging, working closely with technical marketing and product management</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Have a great attitude – with the willingness to learn new skills, take initiative and collaborate across teams</li>
<li>
Aptitude to quickly come up to speed on new technology concept</li>
<li>
Passion to evangelize disruptive new technologies</li>
<li>
Possess strong analytical and strategic thinking skills</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task, adapt to changing priorities rapidly and work under pressure</li>
<li>
Be a great communicator - verbally and in writing</li>
<li>
Be able to translate geeky-widget-speak into business benefits that resonate with customers and reporters</li>
<li>
Be a team player who is shares new ideas, thinks outside-the-box and takes on new challenges</li>
<li>
Deliver high quality content and be willing to take ownership of the details</li>
<li>
2-4 years of related experience in high-tech is required</li>
<li>
Background in analytics and high-tech security, including across endpoints, mobile, users and/or applications is preferred</li>
<li>
Familiarity with security markets and terms like EDR, CASB, UEBA, IAM and MTD is a plus</li>
</ul>
