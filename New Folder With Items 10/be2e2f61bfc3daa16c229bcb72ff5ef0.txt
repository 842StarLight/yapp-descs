<h2>Enterprise Sales Executive (Prospecting)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a key member of the Intellum Sales Team, the Enterprise Account Executive will be responsible for identifying, penetrating, and closing opportunities with key strategic accounts (Fortune 500, Medium-to-Large Enterprise, High-Tech, Fast-Moving Companies) within an assigned territory.
</p>
<p>The Enterprise Account Executive must be able to navigate through large, complex organizations to access Senior Management/C-Suite individuals. This individual will do so by introducing new, innovative, and disruptive concepts in the Customer &amp; Employee Education, Customer Success, and Sales arenas, ultimately positioning Intellum’s products and services as the catalyst for implementing these concepts.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Rapidly develop an understanding of the challenges our buyers face and the unique ways in which Intellum solves them</li>
<li>Master the tools and the ability to deliver relevant “use-case” demos on your own or through partnership with a Sales Engineer.</li>
<li>Create, Qualify, Develop, Manage, and Close business from within your own pipeline</li>
<li>Work qualified leads from marketing to identify and assess feasibility of potential opportunities</li>
<li>Identify stakeholders and decision makers and develop trust-based relationships that help convert opportunities to wins</li>
<li>Develop opportunity timelines and ensure that deal milestones and deadlines are met</li>
<li>Work closely with marketing to develop competitive analyses that improve the win ratio</li>
<li>Work closely with the Intellum leadership team to develop strategies for meeting sales goals</li>
<li>Manage multiple complex sales processes</li>
<li>Track daily sales activity and client interactions</li>
<li>Report on key success criteria to senior management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 7+ years of selling complex SaaS/Software solutions into the B2B space.</li>
<li>Proven sales success of achieving and/or over-achieving aggressive quotas.</li>
<li>Experience selling into Fortune 500 and/or High Tech/High Growth Organizations.</li>
<li>The ability to build relationships at all levels of the organization, especially C-Level
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>A solution &amp; value selling approach to sales and an entrepreneurial approach in all aspects of your career</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Willingness and ability to travel (25-40%)</li>
<li>Knowledge of Salesforce CRM and/or other SaaS applications</li>
<li>A mastery of sales methodologies like Solution Selling, Value Selling, SPIN, or similar.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience selling into Customer Success, Sales, Marketing, and/or Customer Support organizations</li>
<li>Experience with enterprise learning, talent management, or human capital management (HCM) systems. (preferred, not required)</li>
</ul>
