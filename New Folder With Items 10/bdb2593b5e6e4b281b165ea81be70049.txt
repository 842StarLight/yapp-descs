<h2>Community Relations Associate - Full-Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Belmont Village Senior Living is seeking a Community Relations professional with combined experience of inside sales and outside sales/business development. Responsibilities will include communicating with prospective residents as well as engaging professional referral sources, conducting community tours and successfully achieving monthly sales goals related to occupancy. This is a Full Time position with weekend availability required.

Join our dedicated team to help make Belmont Village a Great Place to Work and Live!:
<br><br>
<b>PURPOSE:</b><br>
This position promotes Belmont Village services through achieving and maintaining occupancy at or above the budgeted census level, through building strong relationships with referral sources and consistent prospect/family contact to advance each sale towards move-in.
<br><br>
<b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Knowledgeable in all features, advantages, and benefits of Assisted Living and Memory Care</li>
<li>Effectively leads community tours and presents relevant information and services to our customers.</li>
<li>Has the ability to articulate the Belmont Village services, programs, and pricing policies to its customers.</li>
<li>Maintains relationships of trust with key area physicians, medical communities, and entities which have a significant senior clientele.</li>
<li>Maintains an accurate, current, and thorough contact list using a lead management system.</li>
<li>Knows and understands the competitive environment in the local market with respect to services, programs, and price offerings.</li>
<li>Demonstrates strong team orientation within the Belmont Village community and corporate support staff.</li>
<li>Plans, organizes, and participates in marketing events and seminars to promote Belmont Village.</li>
<li>Ensures sales service fulfillment by maintaining communications with residents, families, and referral sources for future referrals</li>
<li>Researches and understands the local community’s business and medical environment.</li>
<li>Carries out community relations functions within budget.</li>
</ul>
<b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Associates Degree or equivalent</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years of prior leasing or sales experience within the senior living field</li>
<li>Proficient in written and verbal English</li>
<li>Excellent telephone and communication skills</li>
<li>Demonstration of ability to establish long-term relationships</li>
<li>Interest in working with the older population</li>
<li>Computer experience and ability to use or learn to use database software</li>
<li>Ability to travel locally to fulfill job responsibilities</li>
<li>Ability to periodically travel overnight</li>
<li>Ability to work flexible schedules in a 24 hour operation including weekends</li>
</ul>
<b>PREFERENCES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing or related field</li>
<li>More than 3 years of prior leasing or sales experience within the senior living field</li>
<li>Experience working with the older population</li>
</ul>
