<p><b>About Meritus
</b></p>
<p>At Meritus Communities, our goal is simple: offer positive living experiences in exceptional communities at affordable prices.
</p>
<p>Meritus was founded in 2013 by a group of manufactured housing industry veterans who wanted to make a difference in the way manufactured housing communities are run and the way residents are treated. Today, we build thriving, well-run communities where residents have more than a place to live, but a place where they are able to enjoy an active lifestyle by being part of an exceptional neighborhood full of luxury amenities.
</p>
<p>Meritus Communities is one of the nation’s largest and quickest growing owner/operator of Manufactured Housing Communities (MHCs), having purchased over 21,000 sites across the US since its founding. Are you ready to join the team?<br>

<p></p>
<p><b>About the position
</b></p>
<p>Office Coordinators handle the routine office work and administrative responsibilities of the community in addition to leasing, sales, and marketing for the community. They are also responsible for assisting with planning and coordinating community events and activities.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish rapport with residents and prospective residents, field community comments, suggestions, and complaints, and forward to the appropriate manager
</li>
<li>Prepare and distribute resident and community communications (i.e. rule reminders, violation notices, increase letters, delinquency notices, eviction paperwork, lease addendums and renewals)
</li>
<li>Prepare bills and statements for approval
</li>
<li>Perform administrative functions including answering phones, typing, copying, faxing, and filing
</li>
<li>Complete and maintain community records, reports, and files
</li>
<li>Collect and post resident security deposits, processing fees, rents, inspection, or other fees; record in the proper accounts and issue receipts
</li>
<li>Make collection calls for rental payments
</li>
<li>Process move-ins and move-outs
</li>
<li>Assist with the preparation of marketing materials and implementation of resident relation activities such as Craigslist, MH Village, local newspapers and any other marketing sources.
</li>
<li>Assist prospective residents by reviewing new and used home listings, showing homes, and assisting with applications
</li>
<li>Forward all customer service requests to the Community Manager
</li>
<li>Taking pictures of all homes returned to inventory and or resident purchases as well as bank repo’s.
</li>
<li>Distribute all company notices and or violations
</li>
<li>Records all traffic logs and or guest cards, telephone calls
</li>
<li>Ensure home is move in ready
</li>
<li>Immediately follow up on prospects that did not close and try to convince them to close again, if possible refer them to a sister property
</li>
<li>Lease signing and new resident orientation
</li>
<li>Accept rental payments and post in property management software program
</li>
<li>Comply with federal, state and company policies, procedures and regulations.
</li>
<li>Assist in lease transactions by typing leases, gathering applicant’s history and credit approvals.
</li>
<li>Maintain accurate monthly commission sheets on leases, renewals and delinquencies for bonus purposes.
</li>
<li>Assume Community Manager’s duties/authority in absence of immediate supervisor in accordance with company and community guidelines.
</li>
<li>Provide general clerical assistance to community office.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
