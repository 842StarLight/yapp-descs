<h2>Levelset Sales Academy Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you a self-starter, passionate about learning new skills and looking to grow into a sales career? We're currently accepting applicants for Levelset Sales Academy, the premier inside sales training program in New Orleans.</p>
<p><b>
Why join the Levelset Sales Academy?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
To build the foundation you will need to jump-start a successful and lucrative career in the tech industry</li>
<li>
To sharpen your skills as an inside sales rep by uncovering opportunities and maximizing your conversations with prospects</li>
<li>
To get exposure to the strategic design of SaaS customer acquisition models, business development strategy, and an understanding of a high-growth business</li>
</ul>
<p>
Do you think you have what it takes to succeed?</p>
<p>
After completing the 8 week Levelset Sales Academy, you will earn your Levelset Inside Sales certificate and move into a Sales Development Representative (SDR/BDR) or Account Executive (AE) role with Levelset, with full-time benefits and growth potential.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
During this 8-week, paid full time training program, you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Learn how to prospect and identify key decision-makers at target companies</li>
<li>
Work closely with tenured Account Executives to learn how to develop targeted lists, call strategies, and messaging to drive opportunities for new business</li>
<li>
Expand your communication and people skills by laying the foundation for a strong, long-lasting relationship with businesses</li>
<li>
Level up your experience and generate new business opportunities through outbound cold calling</li>
<li>
Engage with prospects using a value-oriented, consultative approach</li>
<li>
Practice listening to prospective customers and educating them on what solutions will best meet their needs</li>
<li>
Be exposed to a rapidly scaling business, with learning sessions on growth strategy, fundraising, entrepreneurship and more</li>
<li>
Pave the way for exponential learning and earning paths through a successful inside sales career</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What should be true about you:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
No direct sales experience is required, but you're comfortable and confident talking to people</li>
<li>
You're passionate about your career and your potential</li>
<li>
You're determined to win, and thrive with a challenge</li>
<li>
You are a constant learner, ready to learn new skills</li>
<li>
You care about where you'll be in your career in 5, 10, and 15 years - and you want to work with a company that cares too</li>
</ul>

About Levelset and the Levelset Sales Academy</b><br>
</p>

The Levelset Sales Academy is dedicated to expanding and training the future tech sales talent of Louisiana.</p>
