<p><b>
PRIMARY FUNCTION:</b></p>
<p>
Provides superior member service by accurately and efficiently performing all member service and teller related transactions in accordance with VACU policies and procedures. Performs thorough needs assessments on each member to strengthen and deepen member relationships while ensuring quality member service.</p>
<p><b>
JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrates appropriate knowledge of all VACU policies and procedures related to the platform, including Security and Information Security</li>
<li>
Establishes member relationships through quality service and cross selling VACU products/services to meet member needs</li>
<li>
Handles each transaction proficiently and accurately, exercising good judgment and seeking approval for any transaction exceeding assigned levels of authority</li>
<li>
Performs paying and receiving functions with accurate balancing and reporting, including but not limited to deposits, check cashing, loan payments, miscellaneous sales, cash advances, etc.</li>
<li>
Verifies and balances assigned cash drawer daily with minimal cash variances. Assists in reconciling errors or discrepancies. Maintains adequate cash and/or cash limits as required by VACU policy.</li>
<li>
Must maintain appropriate knowledge of all VACU products and services</li>
<li>
Answers the phone and respond to member phone inquiries professionally and accurately</li>
<li>
Building and retaining member relationships by performing follow – up and courtesy calls to members.</li>
<li>
Provides quality service by following all member service expectations</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring that business goals and individual member service and sales goals are consistently met.</li>
<li>
Responsible for delivering high quality service and needs based sales to members</li>
<li>
Required notary certification. Must stay informed of all notary rules and regulations.</li>
<li>
Comply with all published enterprise level policies and procedures including, but not limited to, Risk Management policies.</li>
<li>
Complete all required, ongoing enterprise level training including, but not limited to BSA, OFAC, and Information Security.</li>
<li>
Report all Risk Management Policy violations in accordance with policy.</li>
<li>
Must develop and maintain a working knowledge of all applicable federal and state regulations including corporate policies and procedures. Stay up to date on all required loan documents, systems, credit reports and related lending documents.</li>
<li>
Other duties may be required and assigned by the supervisor</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
JOB QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p><b>
Knowledge: </b>Good understanding of products and services offered by financial institutions preferred.</p>
<p><b>
Skills:</b> Satisfactory computer skills required.</p>
<p><b>
Abilities:</b> Positive and effective communication and public relations skills, cross-selling, problem solving and interpersonal skills. Sound judgment. Ability to handle difficult situations. Ability to handle multiple tasks. Professional appearance and manner.</p>
<p><b>
Minimum Education and Experience: </b>Minimum of high school diploma or equivalent. Some college education or business courses preferred. Customer/member service experience, preferably at a financial institution</p>
<p>
Must be able to be actively registered with the Nationwide Mortgage Licensing System and Registry (NMLS)</p>
<p><b>
Other Requirements: </b>Regional Utility Representatives must have transportation to all VACU assigned locations and must be able to work the varying hours of any assigned branch location.</p>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p>
This job requires the ability to sit and stand for long periods of time.</p>
<p>
This job requires occasionally lifting up to 50 pounds and/or up to 30 pounds frequently.</p>
