<p><b>Overview:<br>

<p>Americas Products IT (APIT) supports Retail Credit/Debit Card and Loyalty processing systems for all Chevron and Texaco branded fuel stations across North America. The Solution Architect is accountable for the design, technology selection, and development oversight of our Retail Card Authorization, Settlement, Point of Sale (POS), Loyalty systems.<br>
