<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description:</b></h2>
<p><b>Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Marketing Director is responsible for the marketing, communications and public relations efforts for our four brands: Ponte Winery, Ponte Vineyard Inn, BOTTAIA Winery in Temecula and Hawks View Winery in Oregon.</p>
<p>The primary goal of the department is to support the sales efforts of the brands. This is achieved by a) Understanding the 4 different psychographic segments we serve within the context of the market conditions in which the brands operate and b) Effectively communicating to these segments how the four brands serve their needs. This entails focused attention to marketing data, and the aggressive execution of a realistic marketing plan.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate with department managers and upper management to set specific marketing and sales goals for the department including measurable KPIs.</li>
<li>Analyze existing consumer/guest company data, conduct informal guest surveys and line manager interviews to understand and define the various psychographic groups and their needs.</li>
<li>Develop strategy and implement tactics to grow sales, wine club memberships, hotel occupancy, and group revenue.</li>
<li>Prepare or update the annual marketing plan, annual marketing budget and quarterly marketing reports for the Brands, including drafting presentations related to internal communication and department marketing reports.</li>
<li>Manage and execute the marketing plan including overseeing the printed and digital marketing materials for the wine club newsletter, winery, hotel, wedding, special event and outlet branded materials as applicable for all Brands.</li>
<li>Evaluate and report return on investment on the various platforms.</li>
<li>Oversee all digital advertising campaigns and make monthly adjustments based on performance.</li>
<li>Direct and support the design of all marketing collateral and ensure that they are coherent with the Brands’ desired image and target audience.</li>
<li>Oversee marketing planning and branding meetings.</li>
<li>Manage the internal marketing communication, including the Ponte monthly associate newsletter.</li>
<li>Determine website goals, oversee design and implement strategies related to traffic management.</li>
<li>Direct all marketing department vendors, including graphic designers, advertising liaisons, public relations firms, photographers, videographers and printing companies.</li>
<li>Monitor and manage the image and content of company websites and partner websites for coherence with the Brands marketing goals.</li>
<li>Set goals and strategies for targeted email marketing of all companies and outlets.</li>
<li>Produce monthly email marketing campaign schedules for each Brand to encompass product releases, value-add items, property updates, and sales promotions. Manage email campaigns through direct marketing system, Emma.</li>
<li>Develop and execute marketing promotions for all winery Wine Clubs and Tasting Rooms, and the Hotel.</li>
<li>Coordinate and execute photo and video shoots and management of photo and design files.</li>
<li>Manage and oversee all social media marketing by monitoring accounts and campaigns, determine social media goals, develop and execute strategies and tactics.</li>
<li>Oversee the design and ordering of marketing collateral.</li>
<li>Oversee charitable contribution projects to execute industry events, communication and follow-up.</li>
<li>Oversee the maintenance of the content database of all graphic design photography and print files for the marketing department.</li>
<li>Manage all social media such as Trip Advisor, Yelp, Facebook and Instagram for customer comments both positive and negative and assist in line managers’ responses to all guest comments.</li>
<li>Manage and oversee email databases.</li>
<li>Understands and adheres to the Ponte values and service standards.</li>
<li>Performs all other duties assigned.</li>
</ul>
