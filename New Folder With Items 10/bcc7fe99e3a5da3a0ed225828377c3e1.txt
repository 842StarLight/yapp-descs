<p>Job Title: Sr. Regional Engineer - HPI Louisiana</p>
<p>
Location of Position: Other LA US, LA 70032 USA</p>
<p>
Posting Number: req7034</p>
<p>
Description:</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Sr. Regional Engineer has a primary objective of business retention with profitable business expansion as the secondary goal. This person must be a good problem solver and be able to assess a customer situation from both a sales and technical point of view, develop improvement plans and then influence the sales team to insure the objectives are met.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions/Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide technical support for the commercial sales team in water treatment and / or process technology</li>
<li>
Continuous technical knowledge improvement</li>
<li>
Understanding of our waterside chemical, equipment, and service offerings</li>
<li>
Understand individual customer concerns, develop corrective action plans and communicate to the commercial team</li>
<li>
Gather industry intelligence and market trends to better shape corrective actions or new business development</li>
<li>
Direct customer management through clear communication internally and externally</li>
<li>
Document competitors’ activities and assess potential market moves.</li>
<li>
Make technical presentations to all levels of customer organization.
</li>
<li>Develop engineering and installation work scopes, schedules and cost estimates to facilitate total scope project offerings
</li>
<li>Contribute positively to Geo sales and profitability goals</li>
<li>
Be a leader who finds potential improvement opportunities and develops solutions.</li>
<li>
Be a coach and mentor for the field sales team</li>
<li>
Any job duties as assigned by supervisor or manager</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Minimum Requirements</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors degree in Engineering (Chemical, Industrial or Mechanical) or a relevant Technical degree (Biology, Chemistry) from an accredited university or college</li>
<li>
Willingness and ability to travel up to 30% of the time</li>
<li>
Minimum 10 years in industrial water treatment sales and service.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Other Eligibility Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability and willingness to travel within territory, as required</li>
<li>
Ability and willingness to reside in assigned region, or within customer proximity requirements, as required</li>
<li>
Work frequently in a heavy industrial environment requiring use of Personal Safety Equipment and exposure to noise, dust, chemicals and other similar irritants typical of those found in refinery, chemical plants, power plants, etc., as required</li>
<li>
Ability and willingness to comply with stringent drug testing and background check requirements set by customer safety requirements, as required</li>
<li>
Ability and willingness to perform active work at customer sites, often subject to weather conditions, requiring climbing, pulling, lifting, pushing, stretching, bending, and other physical activities.</li>
<li>
Ability and willingness to occasionally drive for extended periods of time</li>
<li>
Basic Computer Skills including MS Office, and other software programs within a Windows environment</li>
</ul>
<p>
Desired Characteristics:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong creative problem solving skills</li>
<li>
Customer driven/focused</li>
<li>
Demonstrated oral and written communication skills at all levels of the organization</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and leadership skills</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills</li>
<li>
Solid presentation skills</li>
<li>
Team oriented and willing to lead others</li>
<li>
High energy, detail oriented</li>
<li>
Ability to successfully manage multiple tasks within a Matrix environment</li>
<li>
Ability to prepare detailed reports and correspondence</li>
<li>
Proficiency with MS Office (Outlook, Excel, Word, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>
Must comply with all Suez and customer EHS requirements</li>
</ul>
