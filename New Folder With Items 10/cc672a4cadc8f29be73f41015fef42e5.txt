<h2>Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>We are looking for a remote based Sales Director to join our high-performing sales team in a time of exciting growth. In this role, you will be an individual contributor building a sales pipeline and driving new business revenue across a variety of industries within regional territory, by leading and managing efforts from lead generation through sales close. We are looking for a true sales hunter with knowledge and experience in market research, and a passion for technology. This person must have a proven track record for delivering on or exceeding revenue goals, highly motivated, organized, and team-oriented.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Fuel Cycle is the leading all-in-one market research cloud platform. We help the world’s most customer-centric brands such as Google, Hulu, Facebook, Church &amp; Dwight, and Viacom engage directly with their consumers and capture actionable insights through our online communities, panels, product exchanges, and more. At Fuel Cycle we’ve fostered a culture of customer obsessed individuals, working passionately to deliver extreme impact and empowering each other.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Identify new business opportunities and key stakeholders, guiding deals swiftly and diligently through our sales process from discovery to close</li>
<li>Consistently build current and next quarter pipeline through outbounding, events, target account research and other lead generation activities</li>
<li>Maintain pipeline with diligence and timeliness to support accurate pipeline reporting and forecasting for current &amp; future quarters</li>
<li>Demonstrate timely actioning of leads, proposals and prospect pitch follow-ups</li>
<li>Consistently meet quarterly and annual quota and revenue goals</li>
<li>Develop compelling pitch decks and insights from our platform and other internal sources for prospect presentations and outbounding that align with prospects business issues</li>
<li>Develop and maintain strong relationships with senior-level Account contact targets and champions</li>
<li>Partner with Marketing, BDRs and Product teams to strategically target accounts</li>
<li>Engage with internal stakeholders to collaborate on opportunities, share best practices and partner with cross-functional teams</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>4+ years of experience in technology sales (SaaS sales preferred)</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required, MBA is a plus</li>
<li>Prior experience selling Market Research, Customer Engagement or Consumer Insights tools</li>
<li>Self-starter with a record of success generating new business opportunities</li>
<li>Proven track record of excellence with year-over-year quota attainment</li>
<li>Ability to build proficiency in our product and to knowledge share in detail directly with customers</li>
<li>A skilled negotiator with a high close rate</li>
<li>A trusted advisor to executives and C-suite type individuals</li>
<li>Ability to apply insightful judgement, identify and analyze information to make decisions and solutions</li>
<li>Ability to think strategically and execute tactically</li>
<li>Functional knowledge of Salesforce.com and its use for updating accounts, opportunities, and deal stages</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
