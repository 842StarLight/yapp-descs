<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Project Manager</b></h1>
<p>Are you looking for an exciting opportunity to be a leader within a highly successful global payroll services business? Are you looking to take your career to the next level by driving success through our values of Respect, Integrity, Teamwork, Accountability, Diversity and Community? Do you have a strong work ethic, global mindset and the desire to help build a great culture?</p>
<p>
iiPay is a high growth, innovative company, based in US and UK, focused on delivering an outstanding customer experience and service levels. Our payroll managed service is underpinned by our market leading global payroll management system delivering a unique client experience and value-added services.
</p>
<p>iiPay has a unique opportunity for an experienced Project Manager lead a project team of Implementation Consultants and Payroll Specialists alongside external international partners to deliver a successful global roll out programme of the iiPAY software for our clients, and is pivotal to our goals
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role overview
</b></h1>
<p>Our highly motivated and engaged team members are at the heart of our success! This is a highly visible role among the Project Management Team at iiPay and the ideal candidate will be an autonomous self-starter who is excited to work in an environment of innovation, change and collaboration!</p>
<p>
The PM will manage all elements of the project plan from pre-contract signing through to the successful transition into Operations. The PM is responsible for managing the client relationship during this phase to ensure the objectives of the project are met and the returns are fully delivered for iiPAY.</p>
<p><b>
This role is based in Dallas
</b></p>
