<h2>IT Support Specialist, PLM</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<ul>
<li>The IT Support Specialist PLM will assist Lectra Customers and internal technical personnel on the technical aspects of Lectra solutions, in particular PLM, PDM and Enterprise solutions, received by the North American Call Center. It is the specialists responsible for responding to customer request and seeking solutions remotely.</li>
<li>This position serves as first point of contact dealing directly with the customers to trouble-shoot and resolve issues, assist with installations, and identify and communicate business opportunities.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Deliver the highest level of response to our customers service requests by phone and internet, respecting the customer contract</li>
<li>Trouble-shoot / isolate problems between OS and Lectra proprietary software or peripherals and PLM</li>
<li>Ability to resolve problems remotely and determine critical cases to escalate to Tier2</li>
<li>Follow service request until the final resolution</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, identify, and communicate business opportunities</li>
<li>Assist with installation of Lectra software solutions</li>
<li>Log all service calls and daily activities into the service monitoring software (Oracle EBS/Salesforce)</li>
<li>Candidate will possess a positive attitude and be willing to be part of a team</li>
<li>Identify business opportunities and submit information to sales teams</li>
<li>Analyze, develop and/or support customer customization needs (creation of reports, scripts, triggers, connectors, …)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Internal &amp; External Relations:</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Works directly with clients, 2nd level support and interacts frequently with Technical, Solution Implementation Team, Sales and Marketing.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><b>EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE:</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor of Science in Computer Science or related field experience.</li>
<li>At least two years of experience in software maintenance and support</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The following technical skills are essential:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with a n-tier architecture</li>
<li>Establishing a diagnosis</li>
<li>Experience with Windows: Server 2003, 2008R2 and XP, Windows 7, 8, 10, and Microsoft Office Suite</li>
<li>Java/J2EE applications and understanding of distributed system design.</li>
<li>Expereince administrating Oracle Database &amp; MongoDB</li>
<li>Knowledge of JBOSS</li>
<li>Systems Integrator Tools such as ETL – (Talend)</li>
<li>Cloud environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The following technical skills are advantageous:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of iReport</li>
<li>Application servers: JEE</li>
<li>SQL programming, ETL, XML, scripts</li>
<li>Network, domains</li>
<li>Virtual machines, Terminal Server, Citrix</li>
<li>Business and processes: styling, pattern design, textiles/Fashion industry processes</li>
<li>Must be fluent in English and Spanish; fluency in French a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><b>POSITION QUALIFICATIONS:</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>This position requires a motivated self-starter with strong communication skills (English and Spanish, French a plus) and strong technical and trouble-shooting skills.</li>
<li>Technical support experience in multi-tier applications</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills and OS knowledge</li>
<li>Knowledge of IP Networking (LAN/WAN, TCP/IP, Internet)</li>
<li>Good organizational skills</li>
<li>Good Oracle Database knowledge</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written skills in English and Spanish (French a plus)</li>
<li>Very strong customer service skills</li>
<li>Experience in the clothing industry and solutions Lectra would be a plus.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><b>TRAVEL:</b></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>The position is based in Atlanta, GA, USA</li>
<li>There will be up to 10% of traveling required to customer sites/Lectra offices; domestically and internationally.</li>
</ul>
