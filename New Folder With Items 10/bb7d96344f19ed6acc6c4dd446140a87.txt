<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Trustwave is a leading cybersecurity and managed security services provider that helps businesses fight cybercrime, protect data and reduce security risk. Offering a comprehensive portfolio of managed security services, security testing, consulting, technology solutions and cybersecurity education, Trustwave helps businesses embrace digital transformation securely. Trustwave is a Singtel company and the global security arm of Singtel, Optus and NCS, with customers in 96 countries. For more information about Trustwave, visit https://www.trustwave.com.</p>
<p>
You are a savvy security industry expert who understands the needs of IT Departments for effective, easy and affordable solutions to address a broad and constantly-changing threat environment. You are also a talented communicator who can explain complex concepts in clear, concise compelling ways to maximize the effectiveness of our 200+ sales representatives.</p>
<p>
Your extensive Product Marketing background has prepared you to take ownership of our product lines, working with Trustwave’s PR, Analyst Relations, Field Marketing and Web teams to get your compelling messaging out to the largest possible audience. At least 2 years of experience as an Enterprise Security PMM are required. You will write sales scripts, competitive briefs, data sheets, web copy and customer presentations. You will lead cross-functional teams to launch products, campaigns, webinars, and events. You will be a rock star of Product Marketing.</p>
<p>
Occasional overnight travel to other Trustwave offices, domestic and international, may be required. Travel is expected to be 10-20%. This position reports to the Director of Product Marketing.</p>
<p>
Come join our crusade to make good information security available to organizations of all sizes!</p>
<p>
Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
5-7 years of experience in product marketing, evangelizing IT and/or networking solutions.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of technology components of information security domain.</li>
<li>
Experience working effectively in a high growth environment.</li>
<li>
Excellent communications skills, to both internal and external stakeholders, both oral and written, highly skilled in content creation for digital consumption.</li>
<li>
Possess a blend of business, marketing and sales acumen.</li>
<li>
Highly organized, self-directed business style, with strong ability to prioritize.</li>
<li>
Must work well in a team, fast-paced, and deadline-driven environment.</li>
<li>
Excellent people skills to interact with colleagues, cross-functional teams, and third-parties.</li>
<li>
Highly self-motivated and driven to succeed.</li>
<li>
Team player with strong sense of urgency.</li>
<li>
Flexible and willing to do "what it takes" to get the job done.</li>
<li>
High energy and enthusiasm-eager to engage with customers and evangelize the company's offering.</li>
</ul>
