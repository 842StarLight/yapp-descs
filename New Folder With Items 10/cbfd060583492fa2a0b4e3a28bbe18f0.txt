<h2>Digital Marketing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Suzy is a human intelligence platform that helps organizations make better, more informed decisions…faster. Powered by an on-demand network of more than a million consumers, Suzy lets you target and retarget any consumer segment to ask questions and get insights in minutes. Some of the biggest brands in the world use Suzy to validate important decisions, better understand their customers, increase conversions and increase growth. Learn more at www.suzy.com.</p>
<p>
Suzy is looking for a data-driven Marketing Analyst to play a key role in the development of our website strategy. This is a great opportunity for someone with a passion for marketing to develop their analytical, project management, and marketing strategy skills.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive the measurement strategy for all marketing activities, both online (inbound marketing sites, demand generation landing pages, mini sites) and offline (direct mail, conferences, etc.).</li>
<li>
Act as the subject matter expert regarding use of our marketing automation tools, particularly in relation to data flow.</li>
<li>
Leverage a variety of data sources to derive actionable insights about website experience, advertising performance, web user behavior, and down-funnel sales success.</li>
<li>
Lead digital advertising strategy and conversion rate optimization tests, interfacing with key stakeholders on the marketing, sales, creative, production, and technology teams.</li>
<li>
Own the visualization and reporting of marketing-generated leads.</li>
<li>
Build and execute regular audits of website data quality and data collection consistency.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Preferred qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree, preferably in business, computer science, or a quantitative field; or equivalent</li>
<li>
Minimum of 3 years’ experience in digital marketing; B2B SaaS sales process, AdWords, social media advertising,</li>
<li>
Experience with MS-Excel and Google Analytics; Google Analytics Certification preferred</li>
<li>
Experience with data visualization tools, Tableau &amp; SQL a plus</li>
<li>
Understanding of digital advertising, conversion rate optimization (CRO), landing page optimization, and search engine optimization (SEO) techniques</li>
<li>
Familiarity with marketing automation concepts; Act-On experience is desired</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, presentation, writing and research skills</li>
<li>
Experience learning and discussing technical concepts related to web development and information systems</li>
<li>
Passion for emerging web marketing and technology trends</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Experience with Omniture, WebTrends, Segment, Salesforce CRM, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is desired.</li></ul>
