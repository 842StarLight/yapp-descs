<h2>Assistant General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Courtyard by Marriott, South Boston is currently looking for an experienced Assistant General Manager.<br>
<br>
The Assistant General Manager helps the General Manager with overall operational and financial performance, including effective financial management, staffing models, and customer service levels for the entire hotel. He or she helps with asset management, accounting compliance, customer satisfaction, public relations, sales and marketing, human resources, personnel training and development, and development of an annual operating budget.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b><ul>
<li>
Oversees the management and daily operations of the hotel</li>
<li>
Helps manage and mentor staff, including recruiting/hiring/training of all employees, overseeing HR/payroll, managing issues of discipline/termination, ensuring ongoing training/education, etc.</li>
<li>
Helps manage and maintain property, which includes ensuring the building/grounds are kept in a clean and safe condition, that all systems are maintained according to set schedules/procedures, etc.</li>
<li>
Ensures compliance with all applicable federal and state laws and all company policies</li>
<li>
Helps manage budget and finances</li>
<li>
Helps ensure stated goals are met</li>
<li>
Monitors sales and marketing initiatives and activities</li>
<li>
Addresses customer concerns and issues</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b><ul>
<li>
College degree in Hotel Management or a related field</li>
<li>
Three years progressively responsible management experience</li>
<li>
Previous hotel experience required</li>
<li>
Knowledge of budgeting, forecasting, staffing, and scheduling</li>
<li>
P&amp;L experience</li>
<li>
Strong computer and internet skills, including Microsoft Office suite</li>
<li>
Experienced with business applications, including payroll, scheduling, and accounting software</li>
<li>
Strong leadership and motivational skills</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, customer service, and problem solving skills, including the ability to maintain composure under stress</li>
<li>
Strong organization skills, able to multi-task, and can manage time to meet frequently changing deadlines in a fast-paced environment</li>
</ul>

BENEFITS</b><ul><li>

This is a highlight of the major benefits offered: Health and Welfare benefit plans (Medical, Dental, Vision), Retirement Savings Program (401K), and Vacation and/or Paid Time Off (PTO).</li></ul>
