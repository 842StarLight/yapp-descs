<div><b>
Job Description</b></div>
<div>
The Oracle Cloud Executive Sales Sr. Manager manages the opportunity sales process through to client delivery. The right individual will possess the ability to speak to a business audience &amp; clearly articulate Accenture’s value proposition and Solutions offerings in client/new business scenarios and, is comfortable driving deeper solution conversations, designs and workshops during project delivery.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
A professional at this position level within Accenture has the following responsibilities:</div>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Originate and develop Oracle specific opportunities, help to qualify them</li></ul>
<ul><li>Take the qualified opportunities through the sales process and help successfully close the sale</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop new Client relationships and manage those effectively</li></ul>
<ul><li>Expand our work in new areas of the client’s business through new Oracle offerings and services through approved contracting channels</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage the SOW &amp; provides status updates to clients for the incremental work sold</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drive Oracle services sales for Systems Integration and Application Outsourcing work across existing and new clients in North America</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop new client relationships and expand existing relationships to drive Application Services Sales</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage client expectations in conjunction with the Client Account Lead (CAL) or Technology Account Lead (TAL)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work closely with the client to identify business requirements, and define service requirements for new services</li></ul>
<ul><li>Leverage industry expertise to influence client in resourcing strategies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in Account planning and forecasting activities</li></ul>
<div>Travel up to 50%</div>
</div>

<br>
<div></div>

</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years Financial Services industry experience
</li>

</ul>
<div><b>Professional Qualifications:</b></div>

<ul><li>Knowledge of one or more of the Oracle EBS Applications (<b>Oracle CRM, Oracle Financials, Oracles HRMS, Oracle Logistics, Oracle Supply Chain Applications, Oracle Order Management, Oracle Transportation Management and Oracle Warehouse Management Systems</b>)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Exceptional Sales and Solution delivery leadership abilities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with C-Level client relationship building and relationship management.</li></ul>
