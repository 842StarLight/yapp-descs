<h2>Senior Clinical Lead - Clinical Operations Center Informatics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Title</b></h3>
Senior Clinical Lead - Clinical Operations Center Informatics<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h3>
<p><b>
In this role, you have the opportunity to:</b></p>
<p>
At Philips we believe the future of hospital care co-ordination will require hospitals to better combine clinical insights on patient acuity with operational insights on resource availability combined with predictive analytics to improve overall patient progression across the care continuum. Philips is uniquely positioned to integrate and analyze clinical data across our own solutions and those from third parties, both inside and outside the hospital, to generate real time actionable analytics and proactive recommendations to help ensure patients are transitioned to the most appropriate care setting at the right time.</p>
<p><b>
You are responsible for:</b></p>
<p>
Manage and Drive Clinical Solution Strategy/Engagement</p>
<ul>
<li>
Discover and define clinical and operational pain points and help convert into user stories and feature definitions for product development teams</li>
<li>
Lead clinical solution design sessions as part of product definition process</li>
<li>
Together with product management define &amp; validate clinical operations analytics strategy to enable roadmap</li>
<li>
Define customer change management / workflow policy and procedures impacted by new products</li>
<li>
Promote, share and endorse clinical best practices to achieve healthcare organization’s goals.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Support field sales in delivering sales presentations for C-level executives and customers; for all pre-sales business initiatives, sales strategies and proposals</li>
<li>
Support customer solution demonstrations</li>
<li>
Work with customer clinical and physician leads to implement clinical operations center applications (Change Management / Workflow – Policy and Procedure definition)</li>
<li>
Work with value analyst to lead the collection and baselining of KPI/ROI data and support customer change management initiatives to achieve KPI/ROI</li>
<li>
Define and execute onsite clinical deployment of the solution (training, activation)</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are a part of: </b>BG Connected Care Informatics; Clinical Operations center business category</p>
<p>
This role will initially be based in our Andover office. Philips will be relocating this office to a new site at Cambridge Crossing, a large development underway on the Cambridge-Somerville-Boston line. This is a very exciting time to be joining Philips, as this new location will be our largest North American R&amp;D innovation center. The new office is set to open in early 2020.</p>
<p><b>
To succeed in this role, you should have the following skills and experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
RN, B.S in Nursing or related degree</li>
<li>
Minimum of 5 years of related experience</li>
<li>
Experience with patient throughput, capacity management, and business process reengineering / transforming workflows critical</li>
<li>
Direct experience related to command centers and centralized care models critical</li>
<li>
Experience with inpatient care areas ( e.g. telemetry, medsurg, transfer centers, ICU, ED)</li>
<li>
Experience with optimizing patient admit / discharge methods important</li>
<li>
Experience in software product development and informatics solutions highly desirable</li>
<li>
Ability to navigate large cross-functional teams and influence direction</li>
<li>
Experience with leading assessment and clinical recommendation process for health systems / hospitals / IDN’s on operations and enterprise monitoring</li>
<li>
Strong clinical skills and knowledge of how the adoption of technology can impact workflow and influence patient progression and care coordination</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

We offer a competitive salary, outstanding benefits and flexibility in a career with a positive and supportive atmosphere in which to develop your talents further.</p>
