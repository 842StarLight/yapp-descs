<p>Principal Functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>B2B Sales</li>
<li>Building new business opportunities to grow revenue and clients through your outbound calling activities.</li>
<li>Qualify prospects and leads hence build opportunities in the sales cycle.</li>
<li>Drive Sales activity to achieve monthly and quarterly revenue and unit sales targets.</li>
<li>Heavy outbound cold calling activity targeting new clients</li>
<li>Qualify new business opportunities and conduct Zoom meetings demonstrating FleetUp’s products and services and then closing opportunities.</li>
<li>Leverage client referrals, associations and networking to build sales pipeline.</li>
<li>Identify new lead sources both outbound and inbound.</li>
<li>Update and Manage SalesForce.com CRM tool for all of your assigned accounts.</li>
</ul>
<p>Major Job Functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>Achieving all planned sales objectives, metrics, and targets monthly and quarterly by proactively selling via telephone and in person will required to prospective new customer base</li>
<li>Plan and organize your time effectively to allow an optimal frequency of outbound sales calls per day and to meet required sales metrics.</li>
<li>Compiles weekly sales reports and performance metrics as needed.</li>
<li>Manages Individual Territory with emphasis on outbound calling of 40 or more calls per day and conducting a demo per day.</li>
<li>Senior Account Executives will be required to manage and grow pipeline as well as forecast accurately to sales management, with an emphasis on outbound calling and product demonstrations will be key to success in this role.</li>
