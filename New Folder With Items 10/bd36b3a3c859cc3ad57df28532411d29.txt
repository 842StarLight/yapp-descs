<h2>Technical Product Marketing Manager - Competitive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Workfront Technical Product Marketing Manager - Competitive is part sales engineer or technical product manager, part competitive intelligence (CI) analyst. This is a highly technical position within the Workfront CI team responsible for leading deep, technical competitive analysis. This includes understanding competitors' solutions at the feature and architectural level, evaluating functional strengths and weaknesses and comparing those to Workfront. The competitive technical analysis will help the CI, product management, and product marketing teams to better understand core product differentiation to help sell against the competition as well as maintain a competitive product roadmap. The position will also serve as the central point of contact for all competitive technical requests, including feature, architectural, and platform competitive capability knowledge requests. Open to remote opportunities.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Conduct deep competitor product research to understand feature/function strengths and weaknesses and comparative advantages to Workfront.</li>
<li>Create competitive technical collateral that can be leveraged by direct sales and partners. The candidate must be able to create content that spans from a value message that an account manager can deliver to buyers and champions as well as for the sales engineer to deliver to more technical audiences.</li>
<li>Serve as a competitive technical subject matter expert to product management, sales and solutions engineers</li>
<li>Analyze and understand competitors' solutions that span across multiple product sets. The candidate must have the technical capabilities to determine the underlying technologies and architectures that are used in various enterprise networking offers.</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales and solutions engineering organizations to coordinate efforts, jointly identify competitive messaging, and gather Intelligence. The candidate must have a proven track record of working across multiple organizations and communicating clearly..</li>
<li>Present competitive data and enablement to global sales organizations. The candidate must have experience in presenting to sales organizations or to customers. Experience in presenting to both small and larger groups in person, and webinar presenting skills is preferred. Ability to communicate technical information is imperative.</li>
</ul>
Education And Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree is required</li>
<li>5+ Years of enterprise sales engineering/solutions architect role or technical product manager</li>
<li>2+ years experience analyzing competitor solutions</li>
<li>Strong understanding of the enterprise work management and underlying trends</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills; mastery in English</li>
</ul>
