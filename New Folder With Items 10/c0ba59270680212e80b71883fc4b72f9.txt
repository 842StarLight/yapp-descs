<h2>Front Desk -Customer Sales & Service Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Do you want to work with a supportive and skillful team? Come work for a well-known and successful company!<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>AAA is looking for a talented, innovative, customer-centric and driven Front Desk- Customer Sales and Service Consultants to join our team! We are looking for new talent to ensure our AAA members/customers are receiving top notch customer service by hiring candidates that are passionate about their career. The Customer Sales and Service Consultant (CSSC) will be trained on all AAA products and services that we offer so you feel confident in what you are selling. </b>The goal is to go above and beyond for all AAA member/customer every time.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>This position is located in Clarence, NY. - The hours of the Branch are </i></b><b><i>Monday through Friday </i></b><b><i>9:00 am to 6:00 pm and Saturdays 9:00 am to 3:00 pm/the hours of the position will fall within the hours of 9:00 am to 6:00 pm Monday through Friday and every other Saturday working 37.5 hours per week. </i></b><b>Schedules are determined by the needs of the business.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
BENEFITS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Health, Dental, and Life Insurance</li>
<li>Competitive PTO package</li>
<li>401K with match after 6 months!</li>
<li>Free <b>AAA</b> Membership</li>
<li>Tuition Reimbursement</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Delivers passionate and expert service to AAA members/customers in the branch and via phone.
</li>
<li>Responsible for creating and managing first impressions by welcoming all members/customers to AAA upon arrival.
</li>
<li>Manages the check-in process and member/customer wait-time experience.</li>
<li>Collaborates with manager to set individual monthly sales goals and creates a plan to achieve them.</li>
<li>Recommends and sells the appropriate level of AAA Membership to new and renewal members, based on their driving habits and individual need.</li>
<li>Promotes and sells retail products and services utilizing the AAA sales process to determine customer needs and provide the best recommendations.</li>
<li>Provides outstanding service to member/customers to create totally satisfied member experiences and loyalty to AAA.
</li>
<li>Provides Maps, Tour Books and pre-ordered TripTiks to members, and ask if they need any other trip planning services.
</li>
<li>Provides referrals to Travel and Insurance Sales Associates based on recommendations to members.
</li>
<li>Assists with processing weekly Travel Store inventory orders.
</li>
<li>Complies with federal and/or state mandated regulations and licensing.
</li>
<li>Participates in other duties and projects as required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Two-year degree or certificate from college or technical school.</li>
<li>Minimum of one year retail sales and customer service experience required.
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office including Word, Excel, and Outlook.</li>
<li>Ability to think like a customer and create positive, lasting member relationships.
</li>
<li>Ability to sell, upsell, and cross-sell a variety of products and services.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate effectively and professionally with members, fellow associates, and management team.</li>
<li>Consistently demonstrates commitment to the AAA Way: Passion &amp; Caring, Expertise, Accountability, Effective Communication, and Operational Excellence.</li>
<br>
</ul>
