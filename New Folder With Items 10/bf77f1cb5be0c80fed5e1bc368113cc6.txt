<p>Are you looking for a friendly, fast-paced workplace with an emphasis on helping customers and empowering team members? Snap Finance is a thriving leader in the financial services industry, and our team members are the foundation of our success. <p>Snap Finance is a rapidly growing FinTech company focused on digital disruption in the $1+ trillion financial service industry. Our proprietary technology platform and machine-learning decisioning algorithms are changing the face and pace of consumer retail finance. FinTech is the combination of technology and finance and is at the forefront of the next generation of emerging disruptive technologies with the ability to democratize the lending process for all consumers. Snap has a strong supportive culture and is dedicated to its customers, merchant partners, and team members.</p>
<p>Our employees are modern day shopkeepers that blend traditional steadfast commitment to service and top results with the speed and energy of today. We take ownership of our responsibilities and are accountable for our actions.

<p>At Snap, the Sr Analyst, Partner Strategy will work hand in hand with those managing strategic relationships with key partners. The successful analyst will have exceptional communication skills, a focused, can-do attitude, the ability to tackle multiple projects simultaneously, and a seasoned dependability.
</p>
<p><b>The Job...</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with strategic relationship managers and cross-functional teams to assist with reporting and analysis (ad hoc and otherwise), overall strategic operations and enablement, new merchant on-boarding, market research / marketing, training initiatives and incentives, etc.</li>
<li>Work to leverage, enhance and grow existing reporting capabilities while ensuring accuracy and training any new team members on reporting systems and capabilities</li>
<li>Produce rollout schedules, working with field team as needed, to actualize and ensure rollouts (pilots and comprehensive) progress according to plan</li>
<li>Oversee the future planning and features / functionality related to enterprise-level reporting and analytics for key partners</li>
<li>Leverage, enhance, and create new systems and processes in support of our key partners and relationships (software, tools, required capital assets, planning for partner events, etc.)</li>
<li>Work closely with strategic partnership managers to oversee, communicate and optimize around all enterprise-level integrations / onboarding projects</li>
<li>Oversee – liaising with internal project teams, sales ops, etc. as necessary – projects and project-management needs for enterprise partner success Success organization</li>
<li>Build, enhance and manage operations and operational needs related to systemization and scale for supporting the overall partnership experience for enterprise-level partners - training, marketing, mobile, ecommerce, incentives, rewards, etc.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<ul>

</li>
<li>Unyielding dependability, reliability, and work ethic

<li>Highly analytical with a strong understanding of consumer finance</li>
