<h2>Regional Manager-Facilities and Maintenance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
<b>Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</b> managing and coordinating General Building Operations &amp; Maintenance (O&amp;M) as well as managing and coordinating the Baggage Handling O&amp;M contract, and managing and coordinating various facilities design &amp; construction projects assigned by CRE management including issuing Requests for Proposals (RFPs) for both design and construction projects, design/construction management, relocation logistics, and contract execution for the following Hubs/focus cities: DTW and MSP. Communicates with all levels of Delta stakeholders from executives to entry level employees. Coordinates and manages architects, engineers, program managers, construction managers, general contractors and suppliers to complete a project. Performs a variety of oversight roles, including budgeting, master planning, defining, communicating, implementing corporate standards, and preparing for performance process improvements. Prepares budgets, defines scope of work, writes RFPs, sources and selects most qualified contractors to execute the projects and prepares project schedules, reports, timelines, briefings, business cases, and funding requests. Delivers facility projects, maintenance, and construction investments safely, on-time and on-budget. Collaboratively plans and influences airport master planning activities by defining Delta facility requirements and offering technical solutions and guidance to the airports noted above. Travel (estimate 10% of time) as required to complete projects and maintain relationships with airports noted above and internal Delta customers. Heavy interface with various internal Delta business units. Practices safety-conscious behaviors in all operational processes and procedures.
<br><br>
<b>Job Qualifications:</b><br>
A Bachelor of Science degree in Architecture, Engineering, Construction Management, Facility Management, Business or related field required. At least five to eight years professional work experience in facilities maintenance management, construction management, project management, or design. Must possess proven project management, strong technical, analytical, mathematical, and organizational, decision making, and administrative skills. Should possess strong financial, coordination and communication skills. Must be capable of handling multiple projects simultaneously. Must perform with a high level of accuracy. Must be proficient in use of software including MS Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Outlook, and capable of using AutoCAD and MS Project. Should possess excellent written and verbal skills. Should have overall knowledge of company procedures and policies. Must be able to work in a fast paced environment with minimal supervision. Must be performing satisfactorily in present position.
