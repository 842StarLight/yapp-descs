<h2>Manager, CFS Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for developing and executing best in class systems and standards that will increase revenue, help maintain department profitability and reduce time to sell and deliver a vehicle to the customer, thus improving the overall customer experience and improving shareholder value.
</p>

<div>

<div>
Corporate Teams (CFS, Sales, Legal, Information Technology, Shared Service Center)</div>
