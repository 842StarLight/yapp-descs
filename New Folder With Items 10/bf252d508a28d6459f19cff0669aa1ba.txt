<p><b>Auto Body Shop Estimator / Assistant Manager Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>The estimator inspects and analyzes collision damaged vehicles to create a repair plan and apply pricing.</li>
<li>Builds relationships with vehicle owners, repairers, and insurance personnel to negotiate costs and sell work.</li>
<li>Also known as a customer service representative, stays in contact with the customer during the entire repair process, works to address all customer concerns, and makes certain complete customer satisfaction when repairs are complete.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Auto Body Shop Estimator / Assistant Manager </b><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior estimating experience at a collision repair facility is preferable</li>
<li>Ability to write complete and accurate estimates</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of automobile repair estimating, including relevant software programs</li>
<li>Excellent sales abilities and overall communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to effectively manage multiple projects simultaneously and independently in a fast-paced work environment</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
