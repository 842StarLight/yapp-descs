<h2>Marketing Communications Specialist II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The Marketing Communications Specialist will be responsible for coordination, production and evaluation of branded marketing programs and projects in collaboration with the CFN U.S. Marketing team. This role will evaluate, select, write, edit, and manage production of content for communication vehicles, focusing on external branded websites and blogs, and internal intranet sites, and also including packaging, articles, brochures, flyers, direct mail, e-blasts, business letters, social media posts, news summaries, press releases, presentations, etc.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The person in this role will coordinate the development and revisions of sales and marketing support materials with agency or graphics department, including layout, design, proofing and printing. Manage the information and communications for brand, vendors and districts. Maintain version control system for all materials. Coordinate updates of brand external websites- language content, maintenance of search engines, sales tools, training materials, ad response and 800 phone inquiries.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Principal Accountabilities:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>50% - Digital Content Management</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Provide input in to the digital marketing strategy.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Website marketing strategy development, maintenance and execution</li></ul>
<ul><li>Blog marketing strategy development, maintenance and execution</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sources, writes, proofreads, and edits content for websites and blog efforts
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinates with writers, producers, and other contributors to brand web sites to ensure consistency in style, tone, and quality.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyzes web traffic reports, trends, and statistics and works with respective teams to develop appropriate content and response strategies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keyword &amp; SEO analysis and maintenance of websites</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide back-up support for social media content development and monitoring.</li></ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>50% - Marketing Program and Project Coordination and Brand Support</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Will serve as lead on small to mid-sized projects and support on large sized projects. Works across species and brands as needed to manage specific portions of the marketing plan including, packaging, advertising, material development, and tradeshow execution.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide brand guideline support to the Marketing’s Communications and Go to Market teams across all projects, including but not limited to: template development and approval, logo usage approval, and photography approval.</li></ul>
<br>
