<div>Are you a subject matter expert and big-picture thinker who connects the dots and thrives on anticipating the unexpected? Are you energized by helping Government and Public Services organizations prepare for challenges and overcome threats? If so, Deloitte could be the place for you! </div>

<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Organize and deliver services on a cross-section of complex projects</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Contribute to the development and cultivation of business and vendor relationships
</li></ul>
<div></div>
<ul><li>As a technical or functional advisor, work extensively with the management of the project to guide efforts that address the client’s needs</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Serve as a “Subject Matter Expert”, adding interpretive value to data presented or experienced insight into a functional process or issue</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Display leadership and business judgment in anticipating client/project needs and developing alternative solutions</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Identify opportunities for other Deloitte service offerings that can address client needs</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Responsible for managing solutions, delegating resources, and helping to ensure quality assurance principles are met across projects and deliverables
</li></ul>

<ul><li>Provide leadership and support for delivery teams and staff
</li></ul>
<div></div>

<div><b>The Team</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>Transparency, innovation, collaboration, sustainability: these are the hallmark issues shaping Government and Public Services initiatives today. Deloitte’s Government and Public Services practice is passionate about making an impact with lasting change. <div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>Required:</div>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Risk Management, Economics, Political Science, Mathematics, Engineering, Geography, Geospatial Intelligence or related field</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>7+ years of relevant experience in one or more of the following areas - risk management, geospatial analytics, crisis management, or grants management</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>3+ years in a technical or functional lead role</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Proficiency in the Esri ArcGIS suite of software (ArcGIS Desktop, ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online)</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Experience mentoring and coaching others</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Proven leadership skills demonstrating strong judgment, problem-solving, and decision-making abilities</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Experience managing senior-level client relationships</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Experience presenting to clients or other decision makers to present and sell ideas to various audiences (technical and non-technical)</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Pre-sales, proposal, and RFP experience</li></ul>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li>Must be able to obtain and maintain required clearance for this role</li></ul>

</div>
<ul><li>Graduate degree</li></ul>
<div>

<div>
</div>
