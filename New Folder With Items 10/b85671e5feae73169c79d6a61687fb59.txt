<h2>Strategic Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Account Manager will maintain and expand relationships with strategic OEM, major accounts or aftermarket retail automotive customers. They have direct responsibility for achieving assigned sales and bookings in addition to short and long-term strategic account objectives while respecting, supporting and preserving established Midtronics channel partners.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for sales growth, new business development and problem resolution paths with Auto OEM’s, Major Aftermarket Automotive retail service providers in North America markets.
</li>
<li>Develop and manage vital partnership relationships with key decision makers and influencers.</li>
<li>Cultivate and build industry experience, market knowledge and bonding relationships through active participation with key Aftermarket and industry supported organizations and boards such as ETI, AASA, AAIA, AMRA/MAP, Tire Industry Association and other related organizations.
</li>
<li>Develop and execute strategic account plans through identification of customer value/problem identification/solution analysis. Identify and work with Customer Champions and Project Approvers.
</li>
<li>Lead Midtronics internal/Customer external/3rd party influencers or program facilitators in customer solution development.
</li>
<li>Expand relationships vertically and horizontally within Major Accounts managed.</li>
<li>Complex Customer presentations, develop equipment, system and software proposals along with capex validation to achieve successful Customer approval for major programs.
</li>
<li>Quarterly and Annual budget planning, tracking and reporting on Major accounts managed for Midtronics leadership team.</li>
<li>Develop, document, track and execute account plans utilizing documented Sales Responsibilities &amp; Procedures to include maintenance of opportunity dashboards, customer road mapping, engagement trackers, and CRM customer documentation and call reports.</li>
</ul>
<div>BA/BS – MBA a Plus</div>
<div>3 - 5 Years sales or marketing of high dollar (500K -$3M) programs requiring capex of high level committee financial approval</div>
<div>Strong customer engagement and long-term relationship building experience</div>
<div>Existing C-level relationships within automotive OEM’s, retailers or major accounts is a plus</div>
<div>Strong Microsoft office, network communications protocols, android software basic understanding, verbal, and written communication skill set required</div>
<div>Experience in customer value evaluation, ROI analysis and detailed program quote generation is required</div>
<div>Strong work ethic, team building experience, ability to work well under pressure while handing multiple tasks, self-starter and learner</div>
<div>Able to travel overnight 50% of time for direct customer/industry engagement to support and develop new opportunities</div>
</div>
