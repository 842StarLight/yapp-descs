<b>Job Title:</b> EMISSIONS/SAFETY INSPECTION TECHNICIAN
<br><br>
<b>Department:</b> Operations
<br><br>
<b>Reports to:</b> Shop Supervisor, Assistant Store Manager, Store Manager
<br><br>
<b>FLSA Status:</b> Non-Exempt (Class B) Non-Managerial position
<br><br>
<b>Position Purpose:</b><br>
To ensure customer receives accurate and complete gasoline emissions inspection to his/her vehicle in a timely and professional manner and to maintain a pleasant and safe environment for customers.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide services to customers following procedures and standards as outlined by management for gasoline emissions inspections including, but not limited to:</li>
<li>Assist customers in making correct decisions for preventive maintenance needs.</li>
<li>Ensure all gasoline emissions and safety inspections are completed in accordance with state laws.</li>
<li>Advise customer of the testing results.</li>
<li>When assigned, complete sale transaction in register.</li>
<li>When assigned, assist with advertising outside of store.</li>
<li>Understand and comply with OSHA and EPA standards.</li>
<li>Clean facility as directed to maintain the cleanliness and general repair of the shop and property.</li>
<li>Follow all policies and procedures as outlined in the Employee Handbook and/or JTSS documentation.</li>
<li>Other duties as requested or assigned, including performance of Lube Technician duties.</li>
</ul>
<b>Job Requirements/Competencies:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Customer service skills to assist customers.</li>
<li>Ethics and integrity to treat customers and other employees with respect.</li>
<li>Basic computer skills to enter customer and vehicle information correctly.</li>
<li>Cash handling skills.</li>
</ul>
<b>Education/Experience:</b><br>
High school diploma or GED preferred.
<br><br>
<b>Certifications required for:</b><br>
Gasoline emissions certification; Safety inspection certification; Brake plate certification; and On the Spot certification.
