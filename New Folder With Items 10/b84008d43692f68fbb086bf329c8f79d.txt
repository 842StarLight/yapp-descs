<h2>Reservations Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The words "Southern California" conjure up an intoxicating blend of sun, sand and surf. At Loews Santa Monica Beach Hotel our guest’s enjoy the quintessential California getaway at a premier hotel near Santa Monica Pier. Welcome to Santa Monica's Exhilarating Escape.
<p></p>
<p>A Loews Hotels Group Coordinator is responsible for the proper reservations processing of all contracted group room blocks. The Group Coordinator represents their property and the Loews Hotels brand according to our “Four Diamond and More” standards. As the main point of contact for contracted groups rooming requirement/issues the position must “engage and delight” with a warm, professional and efficient manner. Group Coordinator ensures that group contracted rooming requirements are met and that requirement/needs outside of the block are met or alternatives provided based on the needs of the hotel in achieving its financial goals. Group Coordinator has direct contact with key group personnel via phone, email and in person. Position is also responsible for internal communication regarding group block status/issues/etc.
</p>
