<h2>Northeast US SLED Systems Engineering Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Nutanix Systems Engineering Manager (SEM) is a technical field position that is the technical leader for the Northeast SLED Systems Engineering teams.</p>
<p>
You will have leadership responsibility for the Systems Engineers within the territory and will be a working manager. It is expected that the SEM can perform all of the functions that a Nutanix Systems Engineer does including running Proofs of Concept. The SEM will act as the Systems Engineering resource for the Regional Sales Director.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Recruit and hire new Systems Engineers into the territory.</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintaining a passionate, upbeat and learning culture within the region; including training and mentoring new Systems Engineers and AEs on the team.</li>
<li>
HR responsibility for Systems Engineers within the region (expenses, reviews, etc).</li>
<li>
Support field SE team in complex evaluations, solving problems and challenging customer environments.</li>
<li>
Take ownership in ensuring channel enablement is ongoing.</li>
<li>
Cultivate channel partner relationships with their technical teams.</li>
<li>
Act as a floating SE and fill in for existing team members if there is a resource conflict, or vacation coverage.</li>
<li>
Provide an escalation point for pre-sales and post sales technical issues that arise in the territory.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain relationships with key customers in the territory.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Deep knowledge of the virtualization and storage and/or cloud markets as well as design capabilities.</li>
<li>
Experience with public cloud offerings, SAAS, and consumption models is desired.</li>
<li>
About 50% travel within region.</li>
<li>
Strong communication (written and verbal) and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Experience managing direct reports and associated HR items (expenses, promotions, performance reviews) is desired.</li>
<li>
'Whatever it takes' attitude and motivation to do whatever necessary to assist in growing the culture and the sub-region</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BS CS/EE or related (or equivalent work experience)</li>
<li>
5+ Years experience as a pre-sales Sr. Systems Engineer</li>
<li>
1-2 years of experience as a manager or managing a team</li>
<li>
Experience in vertical position and/or working with government entities, including state and local organizations is highly desirable</li>
<li>
Experience with RFx responses and large contract pursuit is a plus</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Must be located in the Northeast US</li></ul>
