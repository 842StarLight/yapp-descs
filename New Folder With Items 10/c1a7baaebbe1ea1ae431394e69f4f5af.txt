<h2>Morning Replenishment Assoc (Seasonal, Part-Time) STORE CLOSING</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">At Kmart, we aspire to provide our Shop Your Way (SYW) Members with excellent products and services that improve their quality of life. As an Early Morning Replenishment Associate, you will be responsible for replenishing and relocating merchandise on and off the sales floor, building and maintaining ad displays, maintaining Company standards in merchandising displays and presentations. The Early Morning Replenishment Associate will also be knowledgeable on all facets of the SYW Ecosystem.
<br><br>
<b>JOB DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Have a Happy, Helpful, and Here for You attitude for all interactions with fellow associates and Members.</li>
<li>Replenishes and relocates merchandise on and off the sales floor and practices effective stockroom rotation.</li>
<li>Builds and maintains ad displays.</li>
<li>Fills and straightens merchandise displays in compliance with layout and merchandise standards.</li>
<li>Displays safe practices in proper lifting and the use of company required box cutters.</li>
<li>Achieves corporate cartons per hour goal for unload and/or replenishment process.</li>
<li>Makes a great first and lasting impression on every customer through great Member service by offering to take the Member to the merchandise and assists in carry-outs on large items, greeting Members, and making eye contact.</li>
<li>Assists in the process of replenishing the Distribution Center (DC) and common carrier shipments and understands the importance of verifying information on DC labels during the replenishment process.</li>
<li>Demonstrates good use of Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) by applying sensors on designated items.</li>
<li>Displays strong skill sets in use of the Remote Maintenance Unit (RMU); utilizes the RMU to track merchandise in the stockroom.</li>
<li>Follows corporate guidelines relating to Merchandise Claims &amp; Control (MC&amp;C) and known loss merchandise, and open/empty packages.</li>
<li>Understands the MC&amp;C policy in the processing of damaged goods in the replenishment process.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable of the financial goals regarding sales, margin, and shrink and take steps to improve the stockroom in these areas.</li>
<li>Assists in building displays and has knowledge of the use of the Retail Enterprise Suite (RES) printer to properly sign end caps and displays.</li>
<li>Understands all register functions including how to process all transactions and forms of tender.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>REQUIRED SKILLS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>1-2 years of related experience.</li>
<li>18 years of age or older.</li>
</ul>
