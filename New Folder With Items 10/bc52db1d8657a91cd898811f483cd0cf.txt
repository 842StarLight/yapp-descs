Job Description
</div>
<div>
<p>Encompass Insurance is one of the largest personal property and casualty insurance brands sold exclusively through a network of more than 3,000 independent agents in most states. With a legacy that dates back to 1897, Encompass employees and agents are committed to delivering a superior insurance experience for our customers. Encompass is a solely owned subsidiary of The Allstate Corporation (NYSE: ALL), the nation’s largest publicly held personal lines insurer.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Encompass is growth focused, looking to expand its presence throughout the country. We are seeking enthusiastic, creative, results-oriented people who thrive in a fast-paced environment. Encompass offers an exceptional career experience for our employees, with impactful work and exciting opportunities to make a difference. Join Encompass and you’ll be part of an exciting future.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Territory Sales Consultant will profitably grow or manage Encompass premium and market share in targeted markets and consumer segments, and achieve desired business results by developing, leading, managing and implementing effective strategies, processes and programs in the Independent Agency channel.</p>
<p>
Achieve strategic objectives and business goals by leading all sales-related activity within a market, including but not limited to implementing strategic objectives by effectively consulting with assigned independent agencies, monitoring progress and making appropriate adjustments, and working effectively with peers, Regional Sales Manager and Director, and other internal business units.</p>
<p>
The Territory Sales Consultant is based in the field and reports to a Regional Sales Manager.</p>
<p>
This role is managing the Arizona territory.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with cross-functional business partners to develop and implement market-specific sales tactics designed to achieve specific growth and profit goals and targeted business results</li>
<li>
Develop new and improve or maintain relationships with assigned agencies and lead resolution efforts where needed and/or appropriate</li>
<li>
Provide insight as requested to help inform effective regional, market and/or state specific sales programs to drive results</li>
<li>
Effectively communicate with internal associates and agencies, presenting business cases to influence decision making and business results</li>
<li>
Lead the market distribution channel to maximize areas of opportunity, including identifying and appointing new agencies aligned with the Encompass strategy and ending relationships with incumbent agencies who are not aligned with the Encompass strategy</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance with state, Encompass and/or Allstate rules, regulations, programs and guidelines</li>
</ul>
