<p></p>
<p><b>
Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, written, and verbal communication skills, with the ability to interface effectively with individuals at various levels, both internal and external</li>
<li>
Analytical, with strong problem-solving abilities and creative resolution skills</li>
<li>
Self-motivated, with the ability to work on multiple projects in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Proven ability to recognize and adapt to company culture</li>
<li>
Ability to analyze, organize and integrate large amounts of complex information into clear, concise presentations</li>

<li>

<li>
Excellent analytic skills with the ability to use key tools to dive deep and provide analysis consistently on performance metrics</li>
<li>
Strong Excel capabilities preferred and capability to use Web Analytics platforms</li>

<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with DMM and buying groups to influence assortment for Omnichannel to achieve sales, merchandising margin and gross profit goals</li>
<li>
Coordinate with marketing and merchandising for messaging and cross promotional opportunities, events and deals</li>
<li>
Create, manage and execute against a marketing calendar to support enterprise initiatives as well as web specific promotional opportunities including site real estate merchandising campaigns throughout the customer journey</li>
<li>
Understand and subsequently optimize the site experience based on customer signals including customer intent, competitive insights, customer feedback, stock position, pricing, and key initiatives</li>
<li>
Leverage web analytics to understand customer interactions with categories marketing, featured placement's and general online shopping behavior to identify business opportunities and risks</li>
<li>
Collaborate with and inform the Merchandising and Marketing teams to ensure assortments, promotional activity and customer facing marketing lead to desired business results</li>
<li>
Manage regular reporting on site performance and communicate to partners in Marketing, Merchandising and Omnichannel support teams</li>
<li>
Drive improvement in the performance of the site and help drive projects and initiatives to further enhance the shopping experience</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Digital Marketing to support strategies for traffic generating channels to ensure the best possible customer experience</li>
<li>
Conduct competitive research to understand competitor trends; synthesize trends and provide recommendations based on findings to cross functional groups</li>
<li>
Develop a thorough understanding of Academy’s policies, procedures and safety rules</li>
<li>
Duties may change; team members may be required to perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
