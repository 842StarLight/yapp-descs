<p>Job ID: 155364</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Oldcastle Coastal, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Summary</b><br>
</p>
<p>
This is a general yard worker role, in which individuals will be assembling orders, wrapping completed pallets, and loading them onto customer vehicles with a forklift. Individuals will be certified to utilize a forklift by Coastal.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Receive customer order slips from supervisor, gather correct products by hand or with forklift, assemble orders for customer, leave in designated spot for pick up</li>
<li>
Safely utilize forklift to move completed pallets to staging areas, or to load pallets onto vehicles</li>
<li>
Work directly with customers in a friendly, professional, positive manner to check orders</li>
<li>
Perform basic, routine maintenance to include recharging batteries; lubricating, fueling and cleaning forklift</li>
<li>
Make sure all housekeeping is maintained in a clean and orderly fashion in assigned area</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities related to the nature of the job may be assigned on a temporary or permanent basis as needed<br>
</li>
</ul>
