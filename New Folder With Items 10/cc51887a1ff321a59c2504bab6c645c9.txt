<p><b>About the Position<br>
</b></p>
<p><br>
The Enterprise Account Executive creates, identifies and closes sales for Salesforce Marketing Cloud within a specific geographical territory. You will drive deals from first contact to close and must be a self starter who can generate a large pipeline of business within a short period of time and effectively manage complex deals through the entire sales process to closure.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></p>
<p><br>
Create and drive revenue within a specified territory
<br>
Generate business opportunities through prospecting and cold-calling
<br>
Drive brand awareness campaigns and lead generation in assigned territory
<br>
Meet or exceed all sales quotas
<br>
Own the sales cycle – from lead generation to closure
<br>
Maintain account and opportunity forecasting with our CRM
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements<br>
</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>A proven sales hunter and closer

</li>
<li>Exceptional organization, presentation, and communication skills – both verbal and written
</li>
