<div>
Description</div>
<div>
<div>
Do you enjoy leading the implementation of Continuous Improvement and Lean Initiatives? We are currently seeking a candidate with direct program management consultancy experience in an integrated delivery network health care system environment leading large-scale improvement and change management projects from development to implementation. You will be responsible for implementing and delivering on cost savings and other performance guarantees as part of customer Performance Partnership agreements.</div>
<div>
Essential duties and responsibilities</div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Build strong relationship with clients. Highly skilled in areas of customer relationship management and a proven track record in leveraging relationships to drive results in an environment where peer influence was a critical factor to success.</div></li>
<li><div>
Consult at a "Sensei" level with customers. Help customers re-engineer new or existing operations to build continuous improvement strategy. Evaluate customer operations, develop a needs analysis and construct necessary recommendations. Define linkages between strategic objectives and process improvements, using both analytical tools and consulting techniques.</div></li>
<li><div>
Lead and facilitate consultative lean events at customer locations that are focused on delivering defined results regarding waste elimination. Develop and leverage LEAN champions within customer organizations.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop and gain consensus regarding high level KPI’s. Use bowlers and other visual management tools to ensure sustainment.</div></li>
<li><div>
Successfully drive improvement strategies that include results that have impacted sustained cost savings or cost avoidance at the enterprise level in a healthcare setting.</div></li>
<li><div>
Conflict management and negotiation. Must be able to use said skills to manage results in difficult situations.</div></li>
<li><div>
Highly skilled and practiced at providing polished presentations to C-Suite level executives.</div></li>
<li><div>
<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Consult with and mentor high level BCI Sales Leadership to drive the correct logic to ensure appropriate identification for high value customers in support of current risk sharing business model. Review customer agreements to fully understand contracted deliverables and milestones, focusing on committed cost savings and performance objectives.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work with Beckman Coulter Process Improvement team to diagnose work flow impediments and provide expert counsel on applicable Lean tools to eliminate waste. Develop solutions that are aligned with planning schedules and quality objectives of the client.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work with internal customer facing BCI peer groups to identify roadblocks and develop processes which ensure peer group alignment to committed cost savings and customer performance objectives.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work with BCI Principal to establish a joint cadence of process governance. Get sign off from customer partners when goals have been achieved and/or projects are complete. Identify gaps in current deliverables and support manufacture of innovative methods to close gaps and provide desired results. Facilitate course corrections as necessary to hit milestones.</div></li>
<li><div>
Contribute to development of Beckman’s new internal DBS assessment tool and back end offering, including simulation software and the content of future performance dashboards.</div></li>
<li><div>
Support program messaging by collecting before and after data points to develop proof points &amp; marketing material to promote the success of the program.</div></li>
<li><div>
Although this is largely an external customer facing role, assistance with internal DBS activities, including training of associates will be required upon request.</div></li>
</ul></div>
