<h2>Partner Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>How would you like to work for one of the country's 50 Most Engaged Work Places? Are you a seasoned account or partner marketing manager with strong business acumen and project management experience? Do you have an entrepreneurial spirit and thrive in an environment where you have autonomy to work on online direct response marketing strategies in order drive sales into an organization?</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
World Travel Holdings is seeking an experienced Partner Marketing Business Manager for the Wilmington, MA office. In this highly visible role, you will be the key owner of your partner accounts including developing and maintaining relationships, working on online direct response marketing strategies and campaigns from inception to completion, as well as being the internal advocate for your partners in order to increase cruise sales. The idea candidate has an entrepreneurial spirit, a background in relationship account management, proven analytical ability and a strong understanding of online direct response marketing.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Account Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for all client communications, conflict resolution, and compliance on client deliverables and revenue</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain relationships with new and existing partners to increase cruise sales for managed programs</li>
<li>
Communicate the client's goals and represent the client's interests to the team</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Project Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Act as the internal advocate for your portfolio of partner accounts</li>
<li>
Conduct all necessary upfront planning for new projects including ROI analysis, creative direction, product, vendor and co-op requirements and prioritization</li>
<li>
Collaborate with internal creative team to ensure that SLA’s on brand merchandising strategy and promotions are executed efficiently and on time</li>
<li>
Provide regular two-way communication between the client and team to ensure strong team representation and set proper client expectations</li>
<li>
Work cross-functionally to stay up-to-date on projects, maximize opportunities related to the project while identifying and eliminating potential issues
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Direct Response Marketing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with partners and internal creative team to create and execute direct response marketing programs, primarily using email and online placements</li>
<li>
Actively seek new, innovative exposure opportunities for our products</li>
<li>
Ensure maximum return on exposure opportunities</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Business Acumen</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the partner marketing budget; proactively seek out co-op marketing, and other available marketing funds from partners</li>
<li>
Produce required reporting and provide thoughtful and strategic analysis to maximize the Partner Brand opportunity</li>
<li>
Utilize analytical marketing techniques to target customers and prospects. Capitalize on in-house (Cognos) reporting and external sources to analyze partner/industry data and trends to better position assigned product line</li>
<li>
Manage brand profit/loss and work closely with Finance to create sales forecasts
</li>
<li>Working with our partner, assume ownership and accountability for generating qualified online and offline leads</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree (preferably in Business or Marketing) or MBA plus 3+ years of account relationship management experience required in a fast pace, direct-to-consumer business model preferred</li>
<li>
Must possess an entrepreneurial spirit and drive for results</li>
<li>
Strong bias towards taking initiative and driving results with minimal oversight</li>
<li>
Proven analytical ability. Must be savvy and passionate about numbers, market indicators and metrics, and must have the aptitude to translate hard data into insight and action</li>
<li>
Significant online direct response marketing experience, including direct involvement with e-commerce platforms, email marketing, and PPC advertising required</li>
<li>
Proficient MS Excel skills needed to develop and analyze reports, models and templates from large data sets</li>
<li>
Experience with Cognos reporting or other business intelligence tools a plus</li>
<li>
PowerPoint and strong communication skills necessary to clearly and succinctly communicate to partners</li>
<li>
15-20% overnight travel to visit Partners is required</li>
</ul>
