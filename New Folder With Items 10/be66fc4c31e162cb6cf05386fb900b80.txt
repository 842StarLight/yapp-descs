<p><b>CUSTOMER SUCCESS MANAGER
</b></p>
<p><b>Customer Success Managers create meaningful relationships with our clients and find ways to be perpetually one step ahead of their needs. They deliver the optimal merchant experience by providing consultation and insight into payments, processing, and products. As a Customer Success Manager, you will be expected to work across the entire organization and develop deep and meaningful relationships with your merchants and internal Braintree and PayPal partners.
</b></p>
<p><b>Key Focus Areas include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>Payments: Grasping this complex, fast-moving industry and helping clients do the same.
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Processing: Providing meaningful insights and recommendations based on transaction data.
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Product: Knowing the Braintree and PayPal solutions from Control Panel to codebase and finding the right fit for merchants.
</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>This requires a dynamic individual with impeccable written and verbal communication skills, a track record of successful project execution, and an unnatural love for solving problems - all while being consultative and entrepreneurial in approach.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your day will vary depending on requests, as you’ll be the main point of contact for Braintree’s most important merchants.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>Manage relationship of existing enterprise merchants across multiple verticals, acting as a payments consultant and internal advocate for the merchant
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Form deep, trusted relationships with merchant contacts, working to uncover servicing roadblocks and find solutions
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Provide business support through incoming phone calls and emails for merchants
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Proactively review merchant’s operating environment, including competitive threats, to manage merchant health and mitigate risk
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Conduct regular outreach to merchants to deliver education, best practices and recommendations
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Drive growth of Braintree, PayPal and Venmo product footprint with merchants by identifying opportunities and generating leads
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Develop account plans, maintain opportunity pipeline, drive Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs), share roadmaps and solicit feedback from merchants to drive strong relationships
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Maintain relationships with internal cross-functional teams to ensure a seamless customer experience
</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>Organization: At any given time, you juggle multiple time-sensitive inquiries, monitor the health of your merchant portfolio, and work on long-term projects
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Analytical: You comfortably work with and interpret data sets to provide your merchants with meaningful insights and subsequent </b><b>recommendations
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Quick Learner: You thrive on curiosity and learning in formal training and situational environments but also develop your own deep understanding of how things get done
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Positive Spirit: You thrive in team settings, don’t feel like any task is “below you”, and gracefully navigate a fast-paced and ever-changing environment
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Communication: You take time to craft the best response possible as you know efficient and effective communication is critical to success but when the phone rings and there’s no time to edit a draft, you can speak crisply on complex subjects
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Customer Service: You can tell us stories about how you turned an angry customer into one that loved you again
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Sales Acumen: You have a proven track record of working with clients to identify new product opportunities, conducting effective Business Reviews and assisting with contract negotiations and renewals
</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>Minimum 3 years experience in Account Management, Customer Success and/or Sales
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Prior payments experience preferred
</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Experience with Excel, PowerPoint, Looker, Salesforce preferred
</b></p></li>
