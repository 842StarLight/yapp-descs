<h2>Additive Manufacturing Project Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>FATHOM is driven by advanced technologies that enhance and accelerate a company’s product development and manufacturing processes. Every day, industry-leading companies leverage FATHOM’s expertise to put satellites into orbit, electric cars on freeways, and a full spectrum of devices into people’s hands and homes. FATHOM’s services focus on prototype fabrication and production parts by way of uniquely blending additive technologies and materials with foundational manufacturing methods—companies go from concept to prototype to production in a way that wasn’t previously possible. This complete offering is supported by a dynamic in-house industrial design and mechanical engineering focused team. FATHOM strives to be its customers’ preferred partner for accelerated product development by providing best-in-class services and support.</p>
<p></p>
<p>FATHOM’s proven ecosystem of dynamic advanced manufacturing solutions has earned award-winning recognition. As of 2018, FATHOM has been ranked six consecutive times on Inc 500|5000’s list of fastest-growing private companies in America. FATHOM has also been recognized year-over-year on the Inner City 100 list by ICIC as published in Fortune Magazine and the San Francisco Business Times’ Fast 100 list of fastest-growing private companies in the Bay Area.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Are you passionate about additive manufacturing? Do you want to help designers and engineers make the unmakeable? Do you have what it takes to provide an exceptional customer experience? If yes to all, this is the role for you! As an Additive Manufacturing Project Coordinator, you will support our sales team and customers by gathering project details and requirements to quote their projects quickly and accurately and then support them through the ordering and delivery process.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities //</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be the face of FATHOM</li>
<li>Answer incoming calls and direct or advise on next steps</li>
<li>Respond to existing customer requests for quotes, order submissions, etc.</li>
<li>Occasionally greet and check-in customers for meetings or order pick-ups</li>
<li>Collect project and order information and details from customers and our Account Managers, such as technical requirements, CAD, payment, shipping information, etc.</li>
<li>Support the sales team in nurturing and growing new and existing customer accounts</li>
<li>Review CAD for manufacturability and communicate anything that may not meet customer expectations</li>
<li>Provide outstanding customer service throughout the quoting and order process</li>
<li>Educate our customers on the services we offer</li>
<li>Accurately enter data into our CRMs and quoting software</li>
<li>Work with a sense of urgency to meet tight timelines and get customers their quotes quickly</li>
<li>Act as a liaison across the sales and production teams</li>
<li>Identify areas for improvement in our processes and customer experience and help come up with solutions to streamline or improve them</li>
<li>Problem solve – if an issue arises, quickly and appropriately remedy the situation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills //</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>Customer facing experience</li>
<li>Interest in and basic understanding of additive manufacturing</li>
<li>Sense of urgency – we work in a fast-paced and deadline-driven environment</li>
<li>CAD – creating, manipulating, and/or reviewing</li>
<li>Excellent communication, problem-solving, time-management, and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Eagerness to exceed customer expectations</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to communicate and collaborate across teams/departments</li>
<li>Calm under pressure</li>
<li>Great organizational skills and ability to appropriately prioritize</li>
<li>Microsoft Excel</li>
 </ul>
