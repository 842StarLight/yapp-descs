<p><b>
Position Title:</b> Principal Fiber Network Architect - 8E<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Network Deployment Innovation Fiber Network Architect is responsible for the development of innovations in Product, Process, Engineering and Architecture for the Crown Castle Fiber Network. Provide technical leadership and vision based on individual’s extensive knowledge of Fiber Optic Deployment technology. The Network Architect must have considerable experience in Optical Networking and Technology, Passive and Active Optical components, Optical Multiplexing, Deployment Methodologies, and Proof of Concept Testing/Certification and Change Management.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Develop and publish standards for fiber deployment innovation to include backbone and customer projects</li>
<li> Work with other internal groups to design, develop and deploy new product offerings.</li>
<li> Research emerging technologies and equipment and develop technical specifications for new products and services.</li>
<li> Responsible for developing and conducting Proof of Concept testing of hardware, software and services</li>
<li> Responsible for aligning deployment strategy with external parties to improve cost, speed, and complexity of network deployment</li>
<li> Provide technical leadership for the Engineering and Operations organizations</li>
<li> Analyze customer requirements, equipment capabilities and services to make written recommendations and oral presentations on network architecture, equipment selection, product definitions, routing policies, etc.</li>
<li> Resolve architecture issues and provide improved design methodologies.</li>
<li> Responsible for taking a product from concept to operation in terms of network topology, deployment methodology and supporting systems.</li>
<li> Facilitate technical transfer of information to Engineering, Operations and Sales departments.</li>
<li> The individual must possess and employ the highest ethical and business standards and always conduct himself/herself with the greatest degree of professional integrity.</li>
<li> Miscellaneous other duties and responsibilities as may be requested or required by management</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Education/Certifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li> BS degree in Engineering or related field</li>
<li> Master’s degree in Engineering or related field preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Experience/Minimum Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li> A minimum 5 years’ experience in deployment innovation of preferably with an ISP</li>
<li> Experience in IP and Ethernet Tier1 ISP preferred</li>
<li> Optical DWDM/CWDM transport preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Other Skills/Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Must have a thorough understanding of fiber optic cable construction methodology, passive and active optical components, optical multiplexing, optical fiber deployment methodologies</li>
<li> Thorough knowledge of the network deployment process, from permitting through network build and turn up.</li>
<li> Must have experience working with Physical Layer, Optical Networking and Coding</li>
<li> Employee must analyze requirements from within the group, from other groups, and from outside the company and select appropriate physical, process, software and routing implementation of those requirements.</li>
<li> Must be able to diagnose and resolve Engineering and Deployment issues, and hardware and software failures.</li>
<li> Must be detailed oriented and highly motivational and the ability to work in a collaborative environment.</li>
</ul>
