<h2>Senior Product Marketing Manager, Foundation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Apttus is looking for a Senior Product Marketing Manager to guide our product strategy and tell our story to the marketplace. Reporting to the VP of Product Marketing, you will research the Quote to Cash market, anticipate market trends, be the expert on our ideal buyer persona and guide our product strategy through our next phase of growth. You will also articulate a clear, consistent, and creative value proposition for our target markets, while acting as the linchpin between our sales and product teams.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
You will have the opportunity to lead cross-functional teams across the business to develop a compelling set of go-to-market initiatives and sales enablement programs, while strengthening our product positioning and messaging. You will also have a proven track record of delivering successful results in the same or similar position at another B2B software company.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>
Know The Market: stay on top of the space we compete in and our performance in it to anticipate market trends and customer needs. Develop competitive intelligence, threat assessment, actionable insight and associated timelines.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Package/Price our Solutions: responsibility for how our product assets are packaged, priced and launched as solutions that resonate with target buyer personas</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Enable The Sales Teams: develop creative positioning and messaging that resonates with our target buyer personas and provide the sales tools and training to ensure our sales teams have what they need in order to execute sales plays in the market
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Go to Market: set and refine Sales Plays, packaging and pricing. Oversee and direct integrated marketing campaigns to generate demand and thought leadership</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Evangelize: as a trusted advisor and visible presence that positively influences our Sales, Partner, Customer and Analyst communities.<br>
</li></ul>

The Apttus Intelligent Cloud powers all of Apttus’ Quote-to-Cash solutions, including Configure-Price-Quote (CPQ), Contract Management, E-Commerce, Renewals and Revenue Management.
