Description</b><div>
</div>
<div><b>Position Overview:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Reporting to the Supervisor, Customer Care, provide pre-sale and post-sale support to distributors and end users for Welch Allyn products.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul><li> Provide telephone support to customers in an efficient manner.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure accurate processing and monitoring of customer orders, service notifications, and complaints.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Answer customer questions on products and services.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process request for Return Material Authorization (RMA) and provide RMA status information as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Complementing the efforts of our sales and marketing teams, promote Welch Allyn products, offering special promotions and engaging in up-sell and cross-sell opportunities.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Recommend changes to established processes and follows existing procedures to process phone orders, provide order status updates, look up part numbers and pricing information.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrate proficient knowledge of commonly-used concepts, practices and procedures that are vital to a customer centric organization.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work with a cross functional team.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> May be required to host a visitor and support sales reps at customer visits if needed.<br>
</li></ul>
