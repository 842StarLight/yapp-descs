<br>
<p><b>
Position Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
Performs month-end closing procedures by creating journal entries, analyzes balance sheet accounts and prepares reconciliations for assigned general ledger accounts. Assists in the preparation of detailed schedules for quarterly and annual reviews and audits. Prepares and processes monthly and quarterly sales and use tax returns and related payment requests.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements (our ideal candidates will possess):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Monthly close process - Prepares journal entries and recurring assigned GL journals</li>
<li>
Perform account reconciliations for assigned GL accounts.</li>
<li>
Prepares all sales and use tax returns and payment requests for all required states. Reports monthly sales to U.S. Census Bureau, reports and pays annual CA Tourism Assessment. Prepares annual payment for CA Environmental Fees, prepares and pays annual Nevada Commerce Tax.</li>
<li>
Assists with review of expense reports and review of A/P batches</li>
<li>
Assists in the implementation of process improvement projects in order to maximize efficiency.</li>
<li>
Assists in preparation of rent and related occupancy costs</li>
<li>
Interacts professionally and ethically, constructively participating as a collaborative team member or leader in pursuit of common goals.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience:</b></p>
<p>
Bachelors in Accounting or Finance required. Minimum 3+ years accounting experience required, preferably in multi-unit restaurant/hospitality industry. Very good working knowledge of generally accepted accounting procedures (GAAP) required as well experience with Sarbanes Oxley internal control requirements.</p>
