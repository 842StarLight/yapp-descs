<h2>Application Engineer - Philadelphia</h2>

<p><b>Exciting Career Opportunity ~ DesignPoint is looking for several Application Engineers to join our growing SolidWorks Core Technical Team</b><b> in Philadelphia, PA!</b></p>
<p>
Do you love SOLIDWORKS and want to build an engineering career around a complete state of the art 3D design software package?<b> </b><b>DesignPoint</b> provides 3D printing and 3D software solutions which allow our customers to create, simulate, publish, and manage data in order to design cool innovative products faster, better, and more cost-effectively. We are growing and are seeking a passionate, experienced engineer who enjoys engaging with customers and wants to be part of an <b>Application Engineering </b>team (located in Lancaster, PA, Clark, NJ, and Philadelphia, PA) that has been awarded #1 in Customer Satisfaction for all of North America by SOLIDWORKS Corporate for the past two years.</p>

SOME OF THE COOL THINGS YOU WILL BE DOING IN THIS ROLE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>

<li>
<b>SUPPORTING</b>: Assisting our customer's SOLIDWORKS users on our technical support hotline as they learn to best incorporate the tool in their design process</li>
<li>
<b>TRAINING</b>: Conducting structured training classes in our DP offices and at customer sites</li>
