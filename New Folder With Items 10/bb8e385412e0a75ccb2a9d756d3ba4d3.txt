Required Skills<br>
<p>The Creative Project Manager will play a key role on the IMAX Creative team. We are looking for a collaborative team player, a multi-tasker who likes to wear many hats, juggling many different projects, always with a smile. We need someone who is organized and detail-oriented with strong follow-through and interpersonal skills, someone who can manage multiple projects and collaborate with individuals with equal amounts of grace and composure.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
You will report directly to the Director, Creative and be responsible for managing creative projects for film campaigns.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
This is a 4-month temporary role. This is not a new role within IMAX Creative.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What you’ll be doing:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage Film projects that span across print, audio/visual, digital and online mediums.</li>
<li>Work with key business partners on brief intake to kick-off projects and bring clarity on key objectives, timeline, budget and final deliverables</li>
<li>Facilitate and drive project briefing process for IMAX Creative knowing when to include specific team members and when not to</li>
<li>Drive the project from a project management function working with cross-functional teams and key business partners to ensure all deliverables are on brief and delivered on time.</li>
<li>Develop and manage overall project timing plans, monitor delivery dates providing updates to all key team members on progress and raise red flags on any timing issues</li>
<li>Oversee and facilitate progress on all file deliverables</li>
<li>Act as final round of QC for all deliverables related to your project before final delivery to business partner or partner agency (i.e.: print house)</li>
<li>Traffic and deliver creative assets to key business partners and teams for review and approval</li>
<li>Work directly with internal designers, AV team and external agencies to ensure proper execution of project deliverables.</li>
<li>Act as both account manager and project manager when it comes to communicating risks and status updates</li>
<li>Assist with miscellaneous ad hoc projects (as needed)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>What we’re looking for:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred</li>
<li>5+ years of creative/advertising/marketing agency preferred</li>
<li>Experience working across several project types: print, digital, motion, audio/visual and environmental</li>
<li>Understanding of technical deliverables across the above project types</li>
<li>Understanding of creative process as it relates to design and content</li>
<li>Ability to listen, gather information, evaluate situation and find solutions that fit the problem that is truing to be solved</li>
<li>Ability to manage vendors, timelines and budgets</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and prioritize without missing deadlines and work within defined budgets</li>
<li>Understands how to build budgets, write scopes of work, develop timelines and working with project management tools like Basecamp</li>
<li>Strong account management skills to interface with key business partners</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office including Word, PowerPoint and Excel</li>
<li>Working knowledge of latest Adobe Creative Suite preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>

<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Great follow-through and organizational skills</li>
<li>Strong listening, project management and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills</li>
<li>Attention to detail is a must</li>
<br>
</ul>
