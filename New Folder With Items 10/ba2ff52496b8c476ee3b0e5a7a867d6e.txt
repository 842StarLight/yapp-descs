<p><b>Position Description:</b></p>
<p>The Field Service Technician is the site liaison with thecustomer. This role will perform troubleshooting and repair on all Flowserveproducts, with specific focus on control valves - Valtek and Kӓmmer controlproducts. The FST will also train customer/end-user on use and maintenance ofproducts. This position impacts very heavily on future sales for Flowservesince prompt, efficient service is one of the most effective sales tools.</p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Troubleshoot, repair and assist customers with service issueswith valves, Kämmer and Valtek products, in a quick, professional, and cost-effectivemanner.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work or interface with instrumentation and control engineers andelectricians on site during commissioning and start-up of Valve Actuators.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create a detailed service report clearly describing workperformed on site and including all relevant information such as order &amp;serial numbers of units worked on, pictures, control systems, details of workperformed, environmental information, parts used, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Carry out pre-inspections of all control valves destined to thespecific customer</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform all functions in a safe manner.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform other duties as required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sales and the ability to influence customers purchasingdecisions.</li></ul>
<p><b>Position Requirements:</b></p>
<ul><li>Must possess a high school diploma or GED.</li></ul>
<ul><li>5 plus years of field service experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>3 plus years of control valve experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Operate hand power tools and measurement tools.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be willing to work overtime hours, especially during plant</li></ul>
<p>outages and scheduled turnarounds on weekends &amp; holidays as required.</p>
<ul><li>Ability to work in a team atmosphere</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be a self-starter and capable of working without much</li></ul>
<p>supervision.</p>
<p><b>Preferences:</b></p>
<ul><li>Previous experience in a manufacturing environment, valve</li></ul>
<p>automation, and instrumentation.</p>
<ul><li>Ability to read/interpret pneumatic schematics to tube valves</li></ul>
<p>correctly.</p>
<ul><li>Possess basic computer skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Microsoft Office Applications Experience (i.e.: Excel, Word,</li></ul>
<p>etc.).</p>
<ul><li>Hands-on mechanical and electrical repair of valves and valve</li></ul>
<p>operators.</p>
<ul><li>Knowledge of electricity and electronics.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong troubleshooting skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to read and understand schematics, wiring diagrams, and</li></ul>
<p>overall system drawings.</p>
<ul><li>Experience with the use of Process meter/4-20mA, Loop Tester,</li></ul>
<p>Signal Generator and Power Analyzer.</p>
<ul><li>Familiarity with industrial network protocols (HART, Foundation</li></ul>
<p>Fieldbus, Profibus, DeviceNet, Modbus based protocols).</p>
