<h2>Credentialing Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>
Supervisor has direct responsibility for the administration of all credentialing processes and services. Responsible for the overall operation of the Records and Licensing Department by ensuring the credentialing and compliance processes for nurses is efficient, operationally sound, and proactively in line with the Fastaff and US Nursing guidelines. Assist with maintaining Joint Commission standards, and stay current with certification requirements and reporting.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<p>
These are functions a qualified incumbent or applicant must be able to perform with or without reasonable accommodation.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the supervision of subject matter experts</li>
<li>
Demonstrate understanding of all certifications, verification sources, compliance elements and to provide staff training on these items</li>
<li>
Determine department staffing needs to ensure proper resource levels are maintained to meet service level agreements within corporation</li>
<li>
Assisting in resolution of administrative and operational problems. Identify and report credentialing issues to appropriate leadership</li>
<li>
Manage and oversee reporting needs for the credentialing department as it pertains to tracking performance.</li>
<li>
Oversee processes and operations of in compliance with contractual obligations.
</li>
<li>Work closely with IT staff to ensure dataflow is efficient and is maintained in compliance with best business practices</li>
<li>
Works towards achieving department goals and continuous process improvement initiatives. Seek better ways to meet the needs of Fastaff/ US Nursing and client facilities</li>
<li>
Oversee credentialing audits internally</li>
<li>
Manage and maintain NJHA and WSRP audit compliance</li>
<li>
Travel as needed for Fastaff projects and USN needs</li>
</ul>
