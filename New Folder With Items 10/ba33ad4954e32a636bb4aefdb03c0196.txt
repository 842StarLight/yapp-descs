<h2>Financial Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>Are you looking to make a difference in a patient’s life? At AmerisourceBergen, you will find an innovative culture that is patient focused and dedicated to making a difference. As an organization, we are united in our responsibility to create healthier futures.<br>
</div></div>
<div></div>
<div>What you will be doing<div></div>
<div>
We are seeking a self-driven, creative Finance professional with excellent communication and interpersonal skills to develop commercial offerings that address the complex landscape of the healthcare industry. As part of the commercial underwriting finance team, the candidate will develop essential financial modeling skills and learn to collaborate with the Sales, Legal, Sourcing, and Operations teams to forecast sales trends and profitability for national healthcare providers, including our largest Health Systems segment customers. This associate will have a passion for creative problem solving and should be comfortable working both independently and collaboratively in a dynamic, fast-paced environment.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop innovative customer pricing models, integrating an overall understanding of the customer goals with AmerisourceBergen’s strategic vision
</li>
<li>Lead internal deal reviews with Finance and Sales leaders presenting the opportunity, deal structure, and profitability drivers (including all risks and opportunities)</li>
<li>
Liaise with cross-functional teams to gather customer-specific or deal-specific information</li>
<li>
Analyze customer purchasing trends to provide internal guidance on product pricing and margin initiatives</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing financial support for executed deals to drive margin and overall organizational goals</li>
<li>
Perform post-deal profitability reviews of recently signed customer accounts</li>
<li>
Generate ad hoc analysis reports as needed</li>
<li>
Perform related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>What your background should look like (minimum qualifications)<div>
Requires BA/BS in accounting, finance, or other business-related field; normally requires a minimum of two years financial experience.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>MINIMUM SKILLS, KNOWLEDGE AND ABILITY REQUIREMENTS:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Strong understanding of financial processes and systems; outstanding knowledge of data analysis, data mining tools including data warehouse applications, MS Access, MS Excel, and other query and reporting tools</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and presentation skills, both written and orally</li>
<li>
Capable of building excellent cross-functional relationships, working across functional boundaries and at all levels of the organization</li>
<li>
Focus on driving long-lasting change across the organization in support of Drug Corporation and enterprise-wide goals</li>
<li>
Ability to think creatively and critically about deal modeling, customer profitability and the needs of the customer</li>
<li>
Fundamental knowledge of profitability analysis, operational impacts of financial processes, etc.</li>
<li>
Understanding of AmerisourceBergen strategic imperatives, philosophy and goals in order to affect financial oversight in protecting and growing the organization’s assets</li>
<li>
Strong mathematical, analytical and organizational skills, including exceptional attention to detail</li>
</ul>
