<h2>Senior Content & Publicity Specialist (International)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The Senior International Content &amp; Publicity Specialist leads the execution of brand publicity and content marketing efforts across earned and owned media for the Enterprise, Alamo, and National car rental brands in the LAC and Asia. This position is responsible for managing vendor relationships, social media marketing, content creation and translation, media/influencer relations and community management, ensuring the brands' messages are effectively delivered in the external environment and appropriately within each international market.. This position will also leverage his/her writing and creative skills, along with community management skills, to bring exposure to the Enterprise Holdings' car rental brands.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Enterprise Holdings is the largest car rental provider in the world as measured by revenue and fleet. The company and its affiliate Enterprise Fleet Management – which combined offer a total transportation solution that includes extensive car rental and car-sharing services, truck rental, corporate fleet management and retail car sales – accounted for $24.1 billion in revenue and operated 2 million vehicles throughout the world in 2018. Enterprise Holdings’ annual revenues also place it near the top of the global travel industry, exceeding all other rental car companies, many airlines, and most cruise lines, hotels, tour operators and online travel agencies. Enterprise Holdings’ regional subsidiaries and Enterprise Fleet Management currently employ more than 100,000 people worldwide.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Through its integrated global network of independent regional subsidiaries and franchises, Enterprise Holdings operates the Enterprise Rent-A-Car, National Car Rental and Alamo Rent A Car brands at more than 10,000 fully staffed neighborhood and airport locations. The Enterprise Holdings global network operates in more than 90 countries and territories, including North America, Central America, South America, the Caribbean and Europe, as well as parts of Asia-Pacific and the Middle East. Today, the company’s three brands serve more than 95 percent of the worldwide car rental market.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead the vendor selection process, including social media management and listening tools</li>
<li>
Maintain relationships with appropriate journalists and bloggers; lead proactive and reactive brand media and influencer relation efforts</li>
<li>
Serve as a mentor to junior team members; develop standards, processes, and training documents for the team</li>
<li>
Create and present key and actionable metrics and make recommendations for improving engagement and content</li>
<li>
Develop content for owned LAC and Asia-specific digital and social properties including conception, creation, monitoring and reporting, working closely with search, creative, media buying and analytics</li>
<li>
Draft and/or edit brand publicity materials including messaging, tweets, Facebook/Instagram, WeChat and</li>
<li>
Line posts, press releases, pitch letters, web article content, influencer blog posts, etc.</li>
<li>
Monitor relevant cultural trends to spark online conversations and inform future content</li>
<li>
Meet with key vendors to understand what is new and evolving in social media tools globally</li>
<li>
Manage our social media communities by participating in online conversations on behalf of the brand to ensure two-way dialogue; seek ways to continually engage current and potential customers and community members, ensuring consistency with brand voice</li>
<li>
Tag content to adhere to best practices and help drive viewability</li>
<li>
Stay abreast of, and adhere to, best practices, platform policy guidelines, rules of participation etc. to remain relevant and up-to-date in a quickly changing social media landscape; help to educate internal stakeholders and employees about the social media landscape in LAC countries, Japan and China
</li>
<li>Maintain Enterprise Holdings brand standards and ensure all communications comply with these standards</li>
</ul>
