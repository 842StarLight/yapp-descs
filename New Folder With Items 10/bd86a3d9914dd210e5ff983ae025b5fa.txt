<p><b>About Marvell
</b></p>
<p>Marvell is empowering the global data economy. </p>
<p></p>
<p>At Marvell, you’ll see your ideas put to work, share in the success of our company, and achieve a healthy life-work balance in a strong culture of corporate citizenship and global semiconductor leadership. If you are ready to excel, innovate, and truly enjoy where you work, apply now for the Lead Designer, Corporate Communications position and join Marvell’s growing Global Corporate Marketing and Communications organization.
<br>
<br>
<b>The Opportunity<br>
</b></p>
<p><br>
As a Designer, your expertise lies in your flexibility, creativity, and execution abilities in a fast-paced environment. You constantly think of ways to improve the design and visual storytelling capability of the team and create compelling stories for Marvell. In this position you’ll partner closely with our marketing, sales, and product teams to build awareness of the Marvell brand.
<br>
<br>
The Designer, Corporate Communications should have:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Expertise with software such as InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Keynote and Power
</li>
<li>A diverse portfolio that exhibits excellent use of typography, color, imagery and depth of design work.
</li>
<li>Proven ability of designing broad, reusable solutions.
</li>
<li>Strong, clean visual design sense.
</li>
<li>Effective strategic thinking and the ability to rationalize your design decisions.
</li>
<li>Ability to evaluate key areas of responsibility and implement marketing improvements, working with the team to identify and execute best practices in structuring an efficient workflow.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with leadership on presentation and content development to support business needs via PowerPoint and/or InDesign.
</li>
<li>Partner with our content experts to evolve our brand across all marketing touch points.
</li>
<li>Design, document, and maintain a visual system that will help maintain consistency across all of our marketing assets.
</li>
<li>Take ideas from inception through production for projects like product pages, landing pages, emails, slide decks, case studies, event booths, etc.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with Corporate Communications team members and regional peers to ensure consistency of the Marvell brand.
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>B.A / B.S degree or equivalent experience.
</li>
<li>A minimum of 2 years of design experience – technology preferred.
</li>
<li>Proven expertise in using Adobe Creative Suite tools: Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, etc. and proficiency in Microsoft PowerPoint.
</li>
<li>Be able to exhibit strong written and oral communication skills.
</li>
<li>Meticulous attention to detail.
</li>
<li>Expectation of confidentiality on all business matters.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership skills, diplomacy, and ability to gain buy-in from diverse teams.
</li>
<li>Always do the right thing and represent Marvell with ethics and integrity.
</li>
</ul>
