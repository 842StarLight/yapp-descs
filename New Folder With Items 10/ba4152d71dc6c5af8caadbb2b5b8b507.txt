<h2>Automation and Controls Specialist - Tech Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>Established and thriving company needs Automation and Controls expertise for troubleshooting issues and answering technical pre-sales and post-sales questions received via, phone, email and/or chat. Questions may involve any product from any product line which include: PLC's/I/O/PC Control, HMI's/Communications, Drives/Motors/Motion, Process/Fluid Power, Power Distribution and Industrial Components.
<br>
<br>
<b>Requirements</b>:
<ul>
<li>Customer Support/Troubleshooting - Provide solutions to customer issues received via, phone, email and/or chat. Maintain internal database (Heat application) of call information. May conduct technical research, testing and/or re-create customer issues using actual equipment and configurations to assist in trouble-shooting.</li>
<li>Training Attend team meetings and training sessions to learn new products. When not taking calls, may also train/re-train on existing products to increase knowledge.</li>
<li>Documentation May create application notes, review product documentation and update hard-copy or online documentation as needed.</li>
<li>Point of Contact Act as a Tech Support liaison with other teams when new products are considered. This may include documentation development and review for new products as well as evaluation and/or development assistance for new products.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications</b>:
<ul>
<li>Requires 5 years experience, or Technical Degree with a minimum of 3 years experience.</li>
<li>PLC programming and troubleshooting/start-up experience.</li>
<li>Experience setting up and troubleshooting Windows to industrial Ethernet network.</li>
<li>HMI programming and troubleshooting experience.</li>
<li>Communications experience with various industry networking solutions (Devicenet, Profibus, etc.) desirable.</li>
<li>Experience with AC servo, Variable frequency drives, and stepper systems products a plus.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
