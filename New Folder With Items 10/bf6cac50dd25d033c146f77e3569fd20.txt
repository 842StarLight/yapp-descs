<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>You’ll build products that hundreds of thousands of real estate pros rely on. In fact, what you do here absolutely matters – we don’t write code that never sees the light of day or get caught up in big company bureaucracy. Our developers are at the heart of our company. And the dedicated team is full of highly seasoned senior developers that have been promoted from within. So, if you’re looking for a long-term career with a highly-profitable company, join us. We’re looking for full-stack web developers. What that means to us is that you are a well-rounded professional developer who is fluent in each of the technologies that make up our core product engine. You’re completely at home in Visual Studio writing solid C# .Net MVC code. You may not be a DBA, but you have no problem navigating a database structure in Management Studio and writing SQL queries and stored procedures. You're experienced in both consuming and developing RESTful web services. You may not be a designer or specialized front end developer, but you have strong knowledge and skills with HTML and CSS. You have a solid understanding of client-side development and are very comfortable writing JavaScript. Software development isn’t just a job for you. You’re passionate about technology and are constantly polishing your skills, learning new techniques, patterns, frameworks, etc.
</p>
