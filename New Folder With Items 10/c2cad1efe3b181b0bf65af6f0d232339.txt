<h2>Sales Excellence & Strategy Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Global Licensing Sales Excellence (GLSE) team is embedded into the Commercial Solutions Execution Team (CSET) within Worldwide Commercial Finance (WCF). CSET is accountable for the execution of our commercial / Volume Licensing business by driving Deal Execution optimization, designing Sales Motions, optimizing Deal Operations, developing Sales Models and ensuring the integration of Compliance. GLSE focuses on the enablement of our sales organization, mostly the 600+ Commercial Executives (CE), who are our customer-facing dealmakers, as well as the partnership with the three Regional Operations Centers.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<p>
This role is set-up to serve as the interface between our Corporate strategy and Field sales teams. It plays a critical part as we transform to a true modern selling approach, putting the customer and their digital transformation at the center.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The role purpose is to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive and land global and regional projects that impact the CE role. Own planning, execution and delivery of change management projects by assessing impact for the community, bringing in relevant stakeholders, orchestrating resources and activities.</li>
<li>
Regional focus will be Microsoft’s markets in the US, Canada and Latin America.</li>
<li>
Engage with Commercial Executive leadership to identify critical areas of improvement in the CE role, processes and readiness.
</li>
<li>Gather valuable customer and field feedback, sharing this business insight with related Corp teams to help further develop and adapt programs and offers to best fit with customers’ and sellers’ requirements.</li>
<li>
Enable CE role effectiveness through developing and implementing licensing sales readiness activities that allow the CEs to become top performing licensing solution negotiators.</li>
<li>
Unblock critical deals during deal processing by navigating through program policies and assessing business risk.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
As one of six globally located Sales Excellence &amp; Strategy Managers, your primary responsibilities will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Landing global and regional change management projects, influencing capacity, effectiveness and productivity of Microsoft’s Commercial Executive community.
</li>
<li>Defining readiness landing milestones and coordinating deliverables globally, together with key stakeholders (WCF, Product Marketing Groups, Segments). Ensuring the content created is targeted to the needs of the Commercial Executive Community to sell and negotiate such offers with customers</li>
<li>
Virtually leading a community of ~2405 CEs: monitor their performance against key metrics which are critical for the organization, identify drivers for any blockers, which are keeping the CE from being successful and driving change initiatives to improve performance.</li>
<li>
Integrating with the licensing leadership teams in your territory to align on initiatives and strategies.</li>
<li>
In partnership with Microsoft’s Regional Operation Centers and other resources, driving discipline in close management of most critical deals and manage deal escalations at month-end (some working hours flexibility required).</li>
<li>
Providing feedback about services, programs and offerings back to key stakeholders, ensuring continuous program development.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
You are core to the success of all key GLSE projects, activities and deliverables. In partnership with your peers, you are responsible for ensuring that critical projects are delivered on time and with high quality, strategic licensing events are coordinated and executed to the full satisfaction of its participants and executive presenters.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This is an ideal role to practice, reinforce and round your general management skills. Outstanding performers with former commercial sales / sales operations / enterprise marketing or sales excellence backgrounds will get exposed to challenging and fast paced execution. Outstanding performers and execution-driven candidates will be exposed to deep business judgment and big picture business thinking.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years relevant business experience: commercial sales / sales operations / enterprise marketing / sales excellence.</li>
<li>
Strong leadership, excellent organization, planning, engagement and business talent.</li>
<li>
Excellent cross-group collaboration.</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational, project management and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Strategic thinking ability.</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication.</li>
<li>
Proven track record of delivering timely, quality and customer focused results.</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively within all levels of the organization, from executives to individual contributors.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple tasks/projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Ability/flexibility to travel (domestic and international travel) and work across different time zones.</li>
<li>
BA required; MBA preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p>
The role requires passion for excellence, intellectual curiosity, and comfort with ambiguity, proven cross-group collaboration, and a high level of executive maturity.</p>
