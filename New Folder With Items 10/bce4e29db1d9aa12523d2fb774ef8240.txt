<h2>Assistant Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Do you have previous experience working in the consumer finance industry? Are you goal-oriented and have you helped to lead teams in the past? If so, Mariner Finance has an amazing opportunity to join our team as an <b>Assistant Branch Manager </b>in our<b> York, PA </b>branch. Assistant Branch Managers not only lead the way in new business development but, they also help to mentor their team.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Become a part of a fast-growing, dynamic company, and join a team that offers an opportunity for accelerated professional growth! If you enjoy interacting with customers and thrive in a results-oriented environment, we would love to hear from you!<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Developing and promoting additional business for the branch through community relations.</li>
<li>
Providing service to existing and prospective customers seeking loans.</li>
<li>
Originating, reviewing, and approving loan applications.</li>
<li>
Assessing approved loans for possible up-selling and denied loans for possible counteroffers.</li>
<li>
Reviewing settled loans for completeness and accuracy of documentation.</li>
<li>
Placing inside sales calls to increase customer base.</li>
<li>
Retaining and maintaining existing accounts as well as developing awareness of other sales/service activities.</li>
<li>
Resolving policy related issues with exceptional timeliness and professional courtesy.</li>
<li>
In Managers absence, coordinating functions and daily operational issues through communication with Regional Manager.</li>
<li>
Performing additional customer service, sales, and administrative responsibilities as necessary based upon on market demand and branch staffing.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Associates Degree, or at least one (1) year of college course work, in a related major required</li>
<li>
Recent successful completion of a lending training program or other closely aligned course of study within the past 3 years - May be supplemented by additional college work or lending training programs.</li>
<li>
4 years of related experience in a sales and/or financial services industry - May be supplemented by additional college work or lending training programs.</li>
<li>
Military rank of E-4, W1, or O1 and above can be substituted for degree.</li>
<li>
Excellent written communication skills to compose professional, error-free correspondence.</li>
<li>
Ability to compose correspondence and format various reports utilizing PC and MS Office software.</li>
</ul>
