<h2>Product Release Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>Visual Lease is looking for a bright, self-motivated, hands-on individual that is experienced and eager to lead our Release Management practice! This is a unique opportunity to make a real impact in an exciting and fast-growing company! This individual will play an integral role in strategizing, implementing and executing build management and release processes.</p>
</div></div></div>
<p>What You Will Do
</p>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with marketing on positioning future product initiatives and helping build marketing materials, education, and sales enablement</li>
<li>
Plan and quarterback the release windows and cycles across a number of different product lines</li>
<li>
Coordinate internal and external release webinar activities</li>
<li>
Communicate release details and schedules to different teams as required</li>
<li>
Measure and monitor progress of development and QA testing to ensure application releases are delivered on time, and that they meet or exceed expectations</li>
<li>
Coordinate customer-facing documentation and other educational support</li>
<li>
Work with deployment team to improve future deployment capabilities</li>
<li>
Work with engineers to understand impacts of branches and code merges</li>
<li>
Provide period reporting to key stakeholders on release progress and readiness</li>
<li>
Manage releases, identifying and mitigating risks to release scope, schedule, and quality</li>
<li>
Responsible for making final Go/No-Go decision</li>
<li>
Coordinate product deployments and release retrospectives</li>
<li>
Document clear and concise processes</li>
<li>
Continuously work to make improvements on the release process</li>
</ul>
