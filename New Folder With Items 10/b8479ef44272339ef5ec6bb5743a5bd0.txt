<div><b>
Position Summary:</b></div>
<div>
The Account Executive - Technology (AE-Technology) is responsible for developing new relationships and penetrating existing Technology category sales in Strategic, Large and Mid-Market accounts within a geographic territory. The AE-Technology collaborates with Staples Strategic Account Managers to achieve margin and revenue goals and builds selling relationships with clients that profitably grow Staples Technology category sales. The AE-Technology is the key point of contact and responsible to provide ongoing long term sales support processes for the technology category in their geographic market.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Responsible for identifying and closing technology opportunities within the Staples Enterprise Account Base within an assigned region</li>
<li>
Works with Staples Enterprise and Staples Technology Solutions Sales Management teams to develop go to market strategies for technology hardware and services</li>
<li>
Proactively Identifies sales opportunities within existing accounts, and work with Staples Enterprise field Sales Force to provide ongoing hardware purchasing programs developed to meet specific customer needs</li>
<li>
Identifies, through extensive product knowledge, opportunities to increase gross margin through additional technology offering and market trends</li>
<li>
Maintains contact at the customer's senior management level. Provides consultative information to Staples Enterprise Customers, Prospects, as well as, Staples Enterprise sales force in product pricing and selection</li>
<li>
Sells value and innovative solutions beyond the core products to meet customers' unique buying needs and requirements</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain ongoing relationships with all key technology suppliers</li>
<li>
Works with key technology suppliers to attain the best costs available for Technology opportunities</li>
<li>
Provides on-going training support to the entire Staples Enterprise sales team on technology hardware products and services as required by management</li>
</ul></div>
