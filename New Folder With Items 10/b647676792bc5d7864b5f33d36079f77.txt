<div>
Are you passionate about technology and interested in joining a community of collaborative colleagues who respectfully and courageously seek to challenge the status quo? If so, read on to learn more about an exciting opportunity with Deloitte’s Information Technology Services (ITS). We are curious and life-long learners focused on technology and innovation.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>Work you’ll do</b><br>
Facilitates and coordinates workstreams of account activity, advanced analytical tasks, and internal client-ready deliverables on an ongoing basis, as part of a team that is assigned to drive adoption for technology investments across the firm. Participates in setting overall strategic and tactical plans for retaining and expanding delivery to the business; and directs the day-to-day activities for fulfilling the plan, executing proposals for specific opportunities, and maintaining internal client relationships as appropriate.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Collaborates with ITS leadership and relationship managers to develop strategic plans for all portfolios and areas of focus based upon the understanding of the business and industry</div></li>
<li><div>
Coordinates and mobilizes individual workstreams, sub-teams, approaches and analyses within the overall set of account activities. Manages day-to-day activities within the assigned workstreams and conducts initial quality reviews</div></li>
<li><div>
Partners with marketing and learning colleagues to manage the creation of deliverables, proposals, and other documents for account team review.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develops and owns the overall account relationship management process.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provides cross-functional support to the account by serving as a trusted advisor with extensive account and industry knowledge.</div></li>
</ul>
