<h2>Customer Service Representative (2nd & 3rd shift)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Kreg Therapeutics, Inc. is a provider of specialty medical equipment and is looking for a <b>Customer Service Representative</b> in our Melrose Park corporate office.<br>
<br>
<b>*Must be available 2nd or 3rd Shift &amp; weekends*</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Duties include:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Answer inbound calls from Hospital and Nursing Home customers (40 per day).</li>
<li>
The entry of orders into custom software database.</li>
<li>
Research service orders for quality control.</li>
<li>
Follow up with technicians, service coordinators, management and sales team.</li>
<li>
Guarantee customers receive the best service.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and communication skills.</li>
<li>
Outstanding customer service skills.</li>
<li>
Proficient computing skills and attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Work Shift availability, for evenings and weekends.</li>
<li>
Ability to pass a criminal background check and preliminary/random drug screens.</li>
<li>
Opportunities for Growth within the Organization.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Kreg Therapeutics, Inc. offers:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Competitive Pay (DOE).</li>
<li>
Major Medical &amp; Dental Insurance.</li>
<li>
Short Term Disability and Accident Benefits.</li>
<li>
Retirement Benefits (401k).</li>
<li>
Paid Holidays.</li>
</ul>
<div>

<b>
About Kreg Therapeutics</b>
</div>
<div>
Since 1996, Kreg Therapeutics has been a provider of specialty medical equipment, focused on distributing support surfaces, bed frames, and bariatric equipment to hospitals. At Kreg Therapeutics, we are committed to providing the best service through the highest degree of quality execution, a commitment to excellence and a passion to do the right thing. The strength of the relationships we build lets us grow an attachment to our customers, drives our ability to expand, and differentiates us from others. The mutual respect and support we demonstrate for every customer, employee, and business partner enables our team to exceed expectations.</div>
