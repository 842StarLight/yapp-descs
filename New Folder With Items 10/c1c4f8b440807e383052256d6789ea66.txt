<h2>Experienced BDC Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for a highly motivated individual with excellent customer service and leadership skills to join our dealership as Business Development Manager. The Business Development Manager will manage, train, develop, and motivate a team of representatives in our Business Development Center (BDC) and ensure we have an effective team to handle all incoming sales phone calls, internet leads, and emails from customers. He/she is directly responsible and accountable for maximizing sales and profitability of the BDC to agreed levels of performance.
</p>
<p><b>Performance Objectives of the Automotive Sales BDC Manager:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Master inbound/outbound sales calls, customer contact, follow-up, and lead generation.
</li>
<li>Answer customer internet inquiries via e-mail and phone.
</li>
<li>Drive production in a fast-paced call center environment to meet department goals.
</li>
<li>Follow up with existing and potential customers to generate sales appointments.
</li>
<li>Develop, support, and mentor team members using call monitors and monthly employee performance plans.
</li>
<li>Responsible for recruiting, selecting, training, coaching, disciplining employees; administering schedules; communicating job expectations; planning, monitoring, appraising, and enforcing policies and procedures.
</li>
<li>Subject Matter Expert: Learn quickly to develop an “expert” level of understanding of all products, sales promotions, organizational procedures, and operating programs.
</li>
<li>Prepare call center performance reports by collecting, analyzing, and reconciling the CRM.
</li>
<li>Motivate and educate the team to deliver on department performance goals.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements for the Automotive Sales BDC Manager:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of overall automotive Sales/Sales BDC operations.
</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 - 5 years supervisory/management experience in a Sales BDC environment.
</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 - 5 years of sales experience in the automotive industry.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of performance evaluation and call center metrics.
</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office, Dealership Management, and CRM software.
</li>
<li>Must be able to work a flexible schedule, including weekends.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time
</p>
<p>Experience:
</p>
<ul><li>Automotive Sales BDC/Sales Management: 3 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
