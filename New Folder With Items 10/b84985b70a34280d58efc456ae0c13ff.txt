<p><b>Position Description:</b></p>
<p>The successful candidate will provide prompt, courteous, and competent insides sales support to include but are not limited to the following job duties:</p>
<ul><li>Manage high profile and complex parts orders to meet committed shipment dates and achieve a high level of customer satisfaction.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop an in-depth understanding of our ERP systems (currently SYMIX and FASTRAQ) top perform a multitude of tasks including order entry, part # creation, job and purchase order creation, order error resolution, commissions, and all other functions needed for correct order release into production for both Taneytown and Hastings parts business.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist the Commercial Operations team when product knowledge is needed for quoting purposes.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate and interact with other departments on a daily basis to support on time delivery of orders.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in and/or lead departmental Continuous Improvement and Cycle Time Reduction projects.</li></ul>
<ul><li>All other duties and projects assigned to promote the growth of the Taneytown Business Unit and overall aftermarket for Flowserve products.</li></ul>
<p><b>Position Requirements:</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor degree in business or engineering, or equivalent work experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Superior communication, interpersonal, and negotiating skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong organization skills with ability to multi-task in a fast pace environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have good computer skills including MS Office applications.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work independently with attention to detail, excellent judgment, and decision-making skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong self-motivation and willingness to learn.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to successfully work in a team oriented and CIP environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to read and understand pump bills of material and detailed part drawings.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to travel if required, approximate time average is &lt; 5%</li></ul>
