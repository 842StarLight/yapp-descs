<p><b>VP of Global SaaS Operations</b></p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
As Vice President of Global SaaS Operations, you will manage Aktana’s global production environment with services ranging in requirements from 3 nines to 4 nines. Aktana’s solutions are an integral part of global sales and marketing strategy execution and optimization for life science industries. You will manage the operating team as well as our infrastructure. At AKtana we embrace an infrastructure-as-code approach to system engineering. Our system must be self-healing and self-scaling. The ideal candidate is driven to operate a highly scalable, fault-tolerant, and easy to administer SaaS infrastructure for deploying, configuring, monitoring, maintaining, and troubleshooting Aktana services. You are pro-active and organized, diligent about documentation, can’t sleep unless Nagios has everything covered, and don’t feel a job is done until it’s automated for the next time. This is an opportunity to grow our global operations team, the process, and the way our overall infrastructure is run.</p>

<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage, motivate, lead, and retain a global team of systems operators to manage our global infrastructure.</li>
<li>Work with platform and application teams to define standards for applications to be hosted in our environment. Collaboratively design an operating environment and application architecture which can deliver four nines or more of production reliability.</li>
<li>Ensure all applications have appropriate monitoring and remediation for all foreseeable systems issues.</li>
<li>Design and maintain an incident response regime for all applications. Oversee a routine drilling plan and chaos monkey approach to ensure the team s and system s readiness to deal appropriately with any incident.</li>
<li>Complete the migration of all services to infrastructure-as-code approach.</li>
<li>Working with your team and systems, deliver operating results of the specified uptime and performance characteristics for each application.</li>
<li>Participate as a member of the engineering leadership team to foster an environment of high trust, high collaboration, and shared accomplishment on the team.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Establishing operational objectives, strategies and work plans to improve current operations as well as planning for future products and customer requirements.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Establishing and assuring adherence to budgets, schedules, work plans and performance requirements.</li>
<li>This role involves infrastructure implementation and cross-functional collaboration with Customer Success and R&amp;D to meet our business goals in a fast-pace environment.</li>
</ul>
