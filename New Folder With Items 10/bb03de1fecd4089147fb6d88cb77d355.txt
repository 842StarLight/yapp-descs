<h2>Senior Buyer, Ecommerce Make Up</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Ulta Beauty is the largest specialty beauty retailer in the United States and the place for the true beauty enthusiast who gets butterflies as she shops for beauty and experiments throughout our store. We are the only one to provide our guests prestige, mass and salon products and services under one roof – All Things Beauty, All in One Place™. We put our guests at the center of all we do, committing to offer her unrivaled ways to be beautiful in an environment that provides the thrill of exploration and delight of discovery.
</p>
<p>The Senior Buyer is the lead buyer responsible for setting department strategy to maximize sales, margin, and inventory productivity. In partnership with the retail team and merchandise planning team, they manage key business area within their division. They will also manage key departmental &amp;/or divisional projects for the entire team. They will represent and support the DMM/VP in their absence. Responsible to mentor, coach and develop team members. The position reports directly to DMM/Sr. Manager of eCommerce Merchandising depending on the department.
</p>
<p>In addition, they will monitor email and mobile marketing cadence and effectiveness. This position is a part of the ecommerce merchandising leadership team and works closely with executive leadership management, merchandising planning and inventory, digital creative, marketing and finance teams as part of a category management team.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Divisional Strategy:</b> Leads the development and execution of current and long-range ecommerce merchandising and financial strategy</li>
<li>
<b>
Financial Plans:</b> Develop and execute Brand, Department and potentially Division financial plans including sales and margin in conjunction with merchandise planning team.</li>
<li>
<b>
Marketing:</b> Develop and execute email and mobile marketing strategy to drive profitable sales and grow market share that aligns with corporate marketing strategy. Key partner with loyalty marketing to execute and grow key loyalty programs for the enterprise. Works directly with the DMM/VP on overall marketing strategy.</li>
<li>
<b>
Site Merchandising</b>: Partner with DMM/VP in leading the development of a cohesive cross-channel presentation of product assortment across Ulta.com. Partner in the development and growth of key digital merchandising programs (Beauty Breaks, Bags, Skinfatuation, etc)</li>
<li>
<b>
Reporting</b>: Pulls, prepare and analyze reports from BW, SAP, or Merchandising systems as needed</li>
<li>
<b>
Trend:</b> Proactively assess and monitor the competitive landscape and marketplace to determine potential opportunities or threats and develop strategies to address. Close eye on Ecommerce landscape and online competitors, identifying category trends and online growth opportunities.</li>
<li>
<b>
Brand Management:</b> In partnership with retail team, manage the omni and online only brand portfolio, performance, and relationships to maximize profitability and achieve financial objectives</li>
<li>
<b>
Negotiation:</b> Negotiate and challenge key brand partners for exclusivity, vendor terms (where applicable), sampling, or exits to support the financial plans and strategy</li>
<li>
<b>
Collaboration:</b> Act as the lead in the development and maintenance of internal and external relationships, including Distribution Centers, eCommerce, Marketing, Operations, Planning &amp; Allocation, Stores and brand partners..</li>
<li>
<b>
Values:</b> Exemplifies Ulta Beauty's core values within role. Ulta Values are quality and timely decision making, continuous improvement, collaboration, accountability, coaching and developing, planning and prioritizing, communication. Rallies the team to align to Ultas cultural principals.</li>
<li>
<b>
Continuous Improvement:</b> Complete on-going training and development courses supported by the HR Training Team or the Merchandise Operations Training Team.</li>
<li>
<b>
Leadership:</b> Lead, coach and develop their direct reports to achieve financial objectives and prepare them for success and broader responsibilities</li>
</ul>
