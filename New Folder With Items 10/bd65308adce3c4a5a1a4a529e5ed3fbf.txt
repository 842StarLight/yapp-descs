The CPQ Application Specialist will be responsible for implementing the Oracle Configure, Price, Quote CPQ solution including alignment with business processes, enterprise technical architecture, user experience and change enablement. Primary responsibilities will be focused providing technical subject matter expertise in client workshops and configuring the application.The candidate will need to be client-facing and can serve as a subject matter expert for key capabilities such as Product Catalog, Pricing, Quoting, Guided Selling, Discounting, Pricing Analytics, Opportunity Management, Quote to Cash, and Order Management.</div>
