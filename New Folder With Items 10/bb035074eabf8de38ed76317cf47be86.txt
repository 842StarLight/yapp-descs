<h2>Front-End Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Title: </b>Front End Supervisor</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Department: </b>Sales</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Supervisor:</b>Branch Manager</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
FLSA: </b>Non-exempt</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Supervises Cashiers, Front-End Loaders, Checkers and U-Boat Retrieval.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Supervises, trains and evaluates cashiers, front-end loaders, checkers and U-boat retrieval.</li>
<li>
Ensures friendly, efficient and professional customer service.</li>
<li>
Ensures compliance with established sales and security policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Investigates and resolves customer complaints in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>
Assists customers with product location and detailed information when needed.</li>
<li>
Plan and prepare work schedules to include lunch and vacation.</li>
<li>
Performs cashier “check-outs” and operates register, when needed.</li>
<li>
Performs Supervisor functions of voids and returns, price adjustments, IOUs (F-6) vs. invoicing and cancels transactions.</li>
<li>
Assists checkers in comparing customer receipts to items on cart to avoid losses.</li>
<li>
Efficiently uses industry specific software to resolve customer inquiries, price checks, inventory queries and price discrepancies.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Other Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Performs other work-related duties as required and assigned.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Education, Experience and Skills Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree, OR</li>
<li>
High School Diploma of GED with at least 4 years experience in customer service or related field, OR</li>
<li>
Any appropriate combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>
Ability to read, analyze and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, and technical procedures.</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from managers, clients, and general public</li>
<li>
Ability to calculate figures, and amounts such as discounts, interest, proportions, percentages, area, mass and volume.</li>
<li>
Effective oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
High level of interpersonal skills to handle sensitive situations.</li>
<li>
Commitment to company values and strong customer orientation.</li>
<li>
Computer Literacy</li>
</ul>
