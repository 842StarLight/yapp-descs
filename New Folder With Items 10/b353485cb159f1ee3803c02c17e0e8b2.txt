<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Nutanix Solutions Architect (SA) combines deep technical expertise with a keen understanding of how to address the critical business needs of enterprise customers through Nutanix products and solutions. The SA is a high-impact, high-visibility role, and is considered to be the pinnacle of the Systems Engineering career path at Nutanix.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Be the key technical leader and influencer in shaping customer decisions to buy and adopt Nutanix Files, serving as a Solutions Architect and trusted advisor for both the end customers and field sales teams</li>
<li>
Present, demonstrate and help execute proof of concepts for Nutanix Files and drive customer engagements from early conversations to decision making evaluations.</li>
<li>
Design and implement Nutanix Files solutions</li>
<li>
Influence Nutanix Files go-to-market strategies by providing feedback to sales, marketing, and engineering on current and future product requirements and sales blockers encountered</li>
<li>
Be a self-starter who can flourish in a dynamic technology company</li>
<li>
Support our sales teams on large and/or strategic opportunities</li>
<li>
Love for solving complex problems</li>
<li>
Engage with senior technical decision makers throughout the sales process</li>
<li>
Present and discuss the value proposition(s) associated with your specialization</li>
<li>
Build long-term business relationships and become a trusted advisor within select accounts to see opportunities and sell Nutanix Solutions</li>
<li>
Ability to demonstrate and improve reseller/alliance partner relationships to increase sales</li>
<li>
Build and deliver technical product and solution presentations and/or training sessions to customers, partners, and Nutanix SEs as needed</li>
<li>
Install, support, and maintain Nutanix solutions during Proof of Concept (POC) deployments</li>
<li>
Stay well ahead of the learning curve in your area of expertise and related business trends</li>
</ul>
