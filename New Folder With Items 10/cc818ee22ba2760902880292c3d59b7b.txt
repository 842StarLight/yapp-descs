<h2>Tax Manager</h2>

<p>Job Description</p>
<p>
FLSA Status: Exempt</p>
<p>
Position Summary</p>
<p>
The individual in this position prepares and reviews tax returns, standard accounting work papers and statements, and other financial reports. Responsibilities also include preparing statistical and account analyses. He/she is familiar with tax and audit standard concepts, practices and procedures. Leads and directs the work of others. Relies on extensive experience and judgment to accomplish goals. A wide degree of creativity and latitude is expected.</p>
<p>
Required Experience and Education</p>
<p>
A current and valid CPA license is required. Must be a member in good standing with the American Institute of CPAs and respective state societies. 5-7 years experience in public accounting or relevant position, demonstrating a progression in complexity, scope, and number of projects. Special consideration will be made to waive the requirement of the CPA license in lieu of comparable experience and demonstrated expertise.</p>
