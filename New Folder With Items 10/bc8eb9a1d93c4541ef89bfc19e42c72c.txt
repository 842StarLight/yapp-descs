<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p><b>Counter Assistant Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>
Principal Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>
1. Meet and greet all customers in a gracious, professional manner.</b></p>
<p><b>
2. Begin to develop customer satisfaction indices.</b></p>
<p><b>
3. Track time for repair order to develop, maintain “standards”.</b></p>
<p><b>
4. Contribute data as requested to the department operating budget.</b></p>
<p><b>
5. Strive to reduce customer wait time to 5 minutes before helping them.</b></p>
<p><b>
6. Schedule appointments on the customer appointment log.</b></p>
<p><b>
7. Take complete customer information on repair order including Email and phone number.</b></p>
<p><b>
8. Communicate with customer to determine the nature of the complaint and make specific notes on repair orders.</b></p>
<p><b>
9. Complete detailed information on customer concern and time frame before passing on to service.</b></p>
<p><b>
10. Communicate with the store manager to help schedule appointments and customers.</b></p>
<p><b>
11. Help Manager Organize daily work load.</b></p>
<p><b>
12. Answer all incoming calls and schedule or take messages for the manager to handle.</b></p>
<p><b>
13. Be willing and able to invoice out customers.</b></p>
<p><b>
14. Take responsibility for maintaining customer call back log and customer call backs.</b></p>
<p><b>
15. Sign and handle vendors invoices according to company Purchase Order Policy.</b></p>
<p><b>
16. Be willing to give customers rides home.</b></p>
<p><b>
16. Other duties assigned by Store Manager.</b></p>
</div>
