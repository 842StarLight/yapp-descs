<p>About Ideoclick:</p>
<p>

Core products and services include strategy advisory and business planning, pricing strategy, marketing, content creation and optimization, catalog management, inventory management, and reporting data and software. Our high-quality, global customer base includes businesses such as Clorox, Kellogg's, Unilever, CVS and many other top brands and retailers. Position Overview:</p>
<p>
We expect the eCommerce Client Success Coordinator to be a passionate client advocate within Ideoclick, with the ability to drive client success and satisfaction through strategic management of their business inputs. We are looking for an individual that excels at building relationships. The Client Success Coordinator will assist the eCommerce Client Success Manager in managing client relationships, inputs, and results. The ideal candidate will be a self-starter with a passion for retail and a deep curiosity about how things work. He/She is interested in creating a top-notch client experience and is able to execute well to achieve long-term growth for our clients.</p>
<p>
A successful Associate eCommerce Client Success Coordinator will be able to:</p>
<ul><li>Work with the eCommerce Client Success Manager for the accounts to deliver excellent client satisfaction through assisting with day-to-day client relationships, client communication, and managing project schedules.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in the development and execution of growth-focused joint business plans with the client, to include strategy, marketing, supply chain, and content creation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Clearly communicate and deep-dive client metrics related to growth, sales, marketing, inventory, content, marketing, and ROI.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help to coach and educate clients on key business drivers and inputs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate with cross-functional groups on execution.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify and recommend internal process improvements.</li></ul>
<br>

Qualifications:</p>
<ul><li>3+ years’ experience in client or account management, consulting, business management, online marketing/eCommerce, or sales.</li></ul>

<ul><li>Experience using data to drive decisions; comfort with Microsoft Excel/spreadsheets and doing analysis.</li></ul>

<ul><li>Willingness to roll up sleeves and dive into the details; a “can-do” attitude.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bachelor's degree or higher.</li></ul>
