<h2>Transportation Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Armstrong Transport Group, Inc. is a third party logistics firm that provides transportation services to our Independent Agents and contractors. We are currently seeking to hire a Transportation Sales Representative to join our dynamic team.
</p>
<p>The Transportation Sales Representative will lead sales initiatives for the MaxFr8 division of Armstrong Transport Group. The primary focus will be to prospect for new clients by networking, cold calling, advertising or other means of generating interest from potential clients. The ideal candidate will develop a rapport with new clients, and set personal targets for sales and provide support that will continually improve the relationship. It is also expected that this position will grow and retain existing accounts by presenting new solutions and services to clients. A successful candidate will work with mid and senior level management, marketing, and technical staff. Strategic planning is a key part of this job description, since it is the TSR's responsibility to develop the pipeline of new business coming in to the company. This requires a thorough knowledge of the market, the solutions/services the company can provide, and of the company’s competitors.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Source new sales opportunities through inbound lead follow-up and outbound cold calls and emails</li>
<li>Successfully develop and managing a plan to expand new business that are not existing clients of Armstrong
</li>
<li>Understand customer needs and requirements</li>
<li>Close sales
</li>
<li>Research accounts, identify key players and generate interest</li>
<li>Maintain and expand your database of prospects within your assigned territory</li>
<li>Team with channel partners to build pipeline and close deals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 2 years experience in the Transportation Industry</li>
<li>High School Diploma required, Bachelor's Degree preferred
</li>
<li>Experience implementing and running new sales channels</li>
<li>Creative thinking and seeking innovative solutions to complex business problems
</li>
<li>Experience working with Microsoft Excel, Word and Gmail</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect for potential new customer's and turn those relationships into business</li>
<li>Cold call as appropriate to ensure a robust pipeline of opportunities</li>
<li>Identify potential clients, and the decision makers within the client organization</li>
<li>Set up meetings between client decision makers and company’s practice leaders/Principals</li>
<li>Participate in pricing and rating with the Account Managers</li>
<li>Handle objections by clarifying, emphasizing agreements and working through differences to a positive conclusion</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<p>
Armstrong offers a robust benefits plan for our employees. The Transportation Sales Representative will receive Health, Dental, Vision, Life, Short Term Disability and Long Term Disability beginning 60 days following the first month of employment. A 401K with 3% match is available after 6 months of employment.</p>
