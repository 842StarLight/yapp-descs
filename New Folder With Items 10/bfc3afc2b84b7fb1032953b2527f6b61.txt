Job description
<br><br>
BigTime software has an opening for an Implementation Consultant with 5+ years relevant business software experience, in our downtown Chicago headquarters. In this position you will provide consulting, on-boarding and training to new and existing customers of our time billing application. Projects are done online, using online tools. You will work closely with our sales and customer support teams. We are looking for a positive minded, motivated, technical individual who is a quick learner and loves working with people, in a dynamic, growing environment.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide on boarding consulting to new customers</li>
<li>Provide group training seminars online and at our location</li>
<li>Design and optimize customer business processes</li>
<li>Provide consulting on integrating with QuickBooks</li>
<li>Develop presentations and written materials</li>
<li>Contribute content to knowledge base, blog &amp; social sites</li>
<li>Coordinate with our Sales and Support teams.</li>
<li>Suggest feature and product enhancements to our development team.</li>
<li>Help design and implement new exciting ways to better serve our customers.</li>
</ul>
<b>Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Business application consulting</li>
<li>Knowledge of business processes in professional service firms</li>
<li>QuickBooks / accounting applications consulting (ProAdvisor a plus)</li>
<li>Excellent communicator (oral &amp; in writing)</li>
<li>Relevant working knowledge of web and application technologies.</li>
</ul>
