<p><b>
Title:</b> Senior Solution Sales Executive – East (SAP Ariba)</p>
<p><b>
Organization:</b> SRM North America</p>
<p><b>
Reports To:</b> VP SAP SRM</p>
<p><b>
Location:</b> Home Office, New York, Boston, Newtown Square, or Pittsburgh preferred</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
SAP Ariba, a market leading cloud, network procurement offering is the fastest growing business for SAP serving more than 1.8 million companies, transacting over $800 Billion commerce every year. SAP Ariba is enabling friction-less commerce between companies, through the exchange of direct and indirect goods and services making SAP’s vision of Run Simple, Run Real-Time and Run Networked a reality for customers.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
A Senior Solution Sales Executive’s primary function is to sell SAP Ariba Spend Management Cloud software, solutions and related services to prospective and existing Fortune 1000 Enterprise and Mid-Market customers located in the East region.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for prospecting, qualifying, selling, and closing software/service revenue for both new and existing SAP Ariba customers</p>
<p>
Build relationships with key executives and decision makers within assigned accounts</p>
<p>
Form strategic account plans including customer profiles, targeted programs, application descriptions, forecast reports, and action items</p>
<p>
Develop and maintain technical and marketing knowledge of the entire SAP Ariba Spend Management product line.</p>
<p>
Develop, share and maintain an in-depth knowledge of all key competitors.</p>
<p>
Interact with the current sales organization and all other SAP Ariba Colleagues in pursuit of overall customer satisfaction.</p>
<p>
Assume full responsibility of quota attainment as agreed to and communicated by the Sales Vice President.</p>
<p>
Participation and involvement in applicable industry conferences both internal and external</p>
<p>
All other duties as assigned</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
Past software sales experience in the following areas including Supply Chain Management, Procurement, Sourcing/Contracts, Finance/AP, or ERP</p>
<p>
Exceptional presentation skills required</p>
<p>
Strong problem solving and analytical skill required</p>
<p>
The Ability to build relationships and quickly develop trust with C-Level executives including CFO, CPO and CEO required</p>
<p>
Highly professional written and oral communication skills required</p>
<p>
Ability to work effectively in a sales team environment</p>
<p>
The position can be Home Office based (New York, Boston Newtown Square, or Pittsburgh preferred)</p>
<p>
University degree preferred</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Basic Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
Minimum 5 or more years of successful enterprise sales experience along with a strong history of quota attainment required</p>
