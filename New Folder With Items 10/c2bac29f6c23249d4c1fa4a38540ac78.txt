<h2>Menu and Data Administration Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Main Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborates with the menu design and procurement team before menu presentations, to ensure menu design meets customer requirements as well as to leverage existing data and product catalogs. Ensures Design Chef’s presentation grid is complete with existing and new recipes and products are clearly identified.</li>
<li>Attends menu presentation with Design Chef to capture changes, take pictures, and update menu grid during the scrub session after the presentation.</li>
<li>Updates specifications (in collaboration with Menu design chefs and commercial) in Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system, including A/O equipment, instructions, pictures, and ancillary non-culinary menu items and services.</li>
<li>Liaises with menu design team to identify required recipes and updates in system.</li>
<li>Liaises with materials management and procurement team to identify correct existing materials and new materials that require Sourcing.</li>
<li>Creates and maintains master live data sets for each account in the Company’s ERP system. Live data sets include both Sales and Distribution and Production Planning Data.</li>
<li>Performs menu reads with the production units and acts as single point of contact for all specification questions in all systems.</li>
<li>Works with JIRA issue reporting system.</li>
<li>Performs continuous audits to ensure standard, consistent and quality master data in catering systems.</li>
<li>Analyzes costing of master data to validate data quality.</li>
</ul>
