<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our Sales Development program is a unique opportunity for entrepreneurial, motivated self starters. This program is targeted towards recent college graduates and those beginning their career in sales in a highly energetic and competitive start-up environment.</p>
<p>The SDR team is the key component that identifies potential customers from marketing leads and turns them into qualified opportunities for our Account Executives. This front-line group is the first point of contact for prospects - and sets the stage for all future interactions. We’re looking for people who want to help customers get the value out of our billing and recurring revenue solution - you personally make the connections between people and products - making sales possible. You’ll spend lots of time interacting with our prospective customers on the phone and via email in order to qualify opportunities, answer questions, and generate interest and excitement about RecVue.</p>
<p>We have a seasoned sales team that knows how to execute and a product that our customers love—including customers like Hertz, WWT, ACI Worldwide, ATS, UK Fuels and CrownCastle. A successful SDR will have the opportunity to grow within the RecVue sales organization as the company continues to expand its global reach.</p>
<p><b>Key responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect, educate and qualify leads to create sales-ready opportunities.</li>
<li>Interact with leads from a variety of different channels via phone, email and chat to follow-up, nurture</li>
<li>Qualify leads generated by marketing campaigns, lists, and other sources and develop relationships to identify sales opportunities</li>
<li>Keep abreast of the industry and technologies to ensure you are a trusted resource to prospects and customers</li>
<li>Follow the current RecVue pre-sales process and consistently use Salesforce.com to document prospect interaction, ensuring efficient lead management</li>
<li>Innovate and build off of the current RecVue pre-sales process to continuously develop new best practices</li>
<li>Develop and maintain daily plans to maximize phone time, including pre-call planning, adhering to metrics and customizing scripts to market segments and prospect type</li>
<li>Consistently hit qualified opportunity quotas and productivity goals</li>
<li>Provide closed-loop feedback to ensure continuous process optimization</li>
<li>Demonstrate the capability to grow and take on more responsibility—we’re looking for folks who want to grow into the next role</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Need to have</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The ability to influence others and build rapport quickly—especially over the phone</li>
<li>Mature and confident with strong interpersonal skills and a true team player</li>
<li>In addition to your educational pursuits, a history of extracurricular activities such as sports, student government, etc…</li>
<li>Strong ability to learn new technology</li>
<li>Competitive, self-starting, and personally motivated</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal skills</li>
<li>Ability to work in a high energy team sales environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Nice to have</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A self-starter with a track record of successful, credible lead follow-up and sales development</li>
<li>Experience working in a startup selling into the mid-market/enterprise space</li>
<li>Fearlessness with a willingness to try new ideas, concepts, and be wrong (but definitely learn from the experience)</li>
<li>Familiarity with cloud-based accounting software a plus</li>
</ul>
