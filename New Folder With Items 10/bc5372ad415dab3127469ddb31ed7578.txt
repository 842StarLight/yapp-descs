<div><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsible for the management and maintenance of the market database (Salesforce) of all Space Enablement teams (8 lines of business). Creates and manages lists of targets, tracks progress, and issues periodic updates. Works with Team Leads and ensures business plans, tracking reports, pursuit strategies, analysis, and various other schedule and ad-hoc reports, dashboards, and analysis are accurate and timely.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Prepares materials for and participates in business development meeting and calls. Partners with Research and Marketing in generating data for reports and supporting materials for the Space Enablement business.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Is the local Salesforce expert and go-to resource. Helps users become familiar with all functions of the tool to facilitate integration of Salesforce into the culture of the business. Responsible for comprehensive understanding of Space Enablement and CBRE platform of services.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Coordinates timely entry and processing of billing and accounts receivables invoices. Collaborates with members from Space Enablement and advisory groups on client billing and various complex AP transactions such as vendor invoices and expense reports for a local market and/or region. Performs PeopleSoft level one review and approval of various AP items to ensure proper coding, allocation, and backup for all items and expenses in PeopleSoft Financials.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Reviews and follows up on monthly AR report; resolves client questions and ensures fees are collected. Escalation point for vendor invoices and payment inquiries.
</div>
<div>Provides monthly ad-hoc reports to management to track project budgets, T&amp;E allowances, revenues,etc.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
 Compiles broker, producer and/or management pipelines for forecasting process.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Creates corresponding file(s) that is compliant with company and state requirements.</div>
