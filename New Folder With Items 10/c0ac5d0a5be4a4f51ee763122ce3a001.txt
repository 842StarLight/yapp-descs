<p><b>Sofive Soccer Centers - Youth</b> <b>Soccer Manager</b></p>
<p><i><b>Sofive is a rapidly growing startup that is developing soccer centers – modern sport complexes with small-sided soccer fields. We have 5 sites to date, with much more in the pipe.</b></i></p>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<ul>
<li>We are currently seeking an Youth Soccer Manager (full-time position) for our state-of-the-art center in Columbia, MD</li>
<li>Description: lead the youth soccer programs sales and management (leagues, tournaments, parties)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Description</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect new clubs, teams, leagues and other potential clients, and achieve or exceed individual and centers’ youth soccer sales goals</li>
<li>Sales strategy and execution: lead the process of implementing sales tactics for both youth soccer leagues and tournaments, as well as youth parties</li>
<li>Focus on customer experience and retention of current and past players: design and execute sales tactics, form personal relationships with coaches, parents and players</li>
<li>Co-lead and co-manage a team of business development representatives and managers on duty, holding them accountable for execution of best practices</li>
<li>Overview, plan, monitor and schedule all youth soccer programs, ensuring smooth and efficient operational procedures enabling Sofive center to provide the highest level of customer service</li>
<li>Participate in referees hiring, training, and performance reviews</li>
<li>Attend weekly management and staff meetings to ensure strong communication</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven leadership ability in soccer organizations with 5 years + of sales and management experience</li>
<li>Be passionate and knowledgeable about the soccer industry</li>
<li>Must be assertive, enthusiastic, energetic and punctual with an entrepreneurial drive to create, grow, operate, and manage youth soccer leagues, tournaments, and events</li>
<li>Portray strong relationship management skills, work ethic, and care for customers and products</li>
<li>Have detailed knowledge of MD soccer market</li>
<li>Communications skills required: need to build a good rapport with customers, parents, team captains</li>
<li>Ability to deliver a high level of customer service, to plan and organize the work necessary to run a successful center</li>
<li>Spanish language skills a plus</li>
<li>College degree strongly preferred</li>
<li>Mastering Microsoft Office software</li>
</ul>
