<div><b>
POSITION OVERVIEW:
</b></div>
<div>
The Warranty Operations Representative processes inquiries, incoming products and enters data for warranty commitments for repair, replacement or credit for consumer returns due to failures in material and construction for Marmot and ExOfficio products. The Warranty Operations Representative maintains department records and supports the department by performing a variety of duties including product inquires that may be received by telephone, mail, email or fax from consumers, outside sales representatives or retail store accounts. The Warranty Operations Representative interprets warranty policy eligibility to consumers in a way that projects a strong, positive impression of Marmot and ExOfficio.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Processes incoming product by effectively receiving, opening, and identifying products using department catalog reference library. Reviews consumer request for repairs or replacement. Monitors and evaluates warranty product returns relative to normal failure rates and usual life span. Resolves consumer product repairs and replacements relative to normal policies and procedures. Effectively relates problems and corrective actions to be taken to consumer. Forwards non-routine complaints to supervisor. May utilize senior product development employees when identifying products not readily recognized.
</li>
<li>Enters incoming product data that does not have a Repair Order into spreadsheet by date of arrival, including consumer last name and zip code for easy reference by other warranty service reps. Enters information in the contact software system.
</li>
<li>Effectively tags, communicates problems, potential corrective action and contacts the consumer when replacement product is warranted. Tags and relates problems, testing requirements, and corrective actions to the repairs station by date received. Ensures that time sensitive requests are processed appropriately. Forwards non-routine complaints or credit requests to supervisor.
</li>
<li>Answers incoming telephone calls or meets with on-site consumers, determines nature of inquiry and relates procedures for processing repairs, technical product usage, and/or care and cleaning instructions. Answers questions about warranty procedures and provides callers with address, return instructions, and other information.</li>
<li>
Informs consumer of unit repair costs or price differences for replacements, ship dates, anticipated delays, and any additional information needed by consumer for replacement products.
</li>
<li>Boxes, labels and ships repairs and replacement products to the consumer. Follows up to ensure delivery by specified dates to monitor timely arrivals. Communicates with shipping, warehouse, or common carrier personnel to expedite or trace missing or delayed shipments.
</li>
<li>Records incoming products according to date received when they do not have a Repair Order Number. Informs Customer/Retailer of Online Warranty System.</li>
<li>
Effectively answers warranty email inquiries from customers in a way that projects a strong, positive impression of Marmot and ExOfficio.</li>
<li>
Repairs tent poles using the electrical aluminum cut/grinding machinery. Utilizes safety goggles, machine guards and other proper safety measure as required.</li>
<li>
Maintains a clean and safe work environment. Share in responsibility for sweeping, removing debris, and stacking corrugated and other materials safely to keep aisles clear and observe fire safety procedures.<br>
</li>
</ul>
