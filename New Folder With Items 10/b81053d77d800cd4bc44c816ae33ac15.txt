<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Accounting Associate</b></h2>
<br>
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Purpose of Job</b></h3>
<p>The Accounting Associate role assists the Accounting Manager with bookkeeping, clerical duties, and account management.</p>
<p>
This role involves extensive e-mail and telephone communications with the Company’s customers, experts, and staff. The Accounting Associate is responsible for providing complex administrative support to physicians and non-physician experts. They are responsible for processing a high volume of invoicing, reconciliation and/or collections.</p>
<p>
This individual must possess excellent organizational and problem-solving skills, with a strong customer-centric attitude.</p>
<p>
The Accounting Associate must also be self-motivated and able to work independently and without excess direction or supervision. He/she must also possess advanced knowledge of Quick Books (or similar software) with strong attention to detail. This individual will need to be deadline-driven and possess the ability to work confidently within a high volume, fast paced environment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Requirements</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Maintain a full understanding of the Company’s retention agreements, processes and procedures</li>
<li>Input and submit a high volume of invoicing</li>
<li>Process and prepare deposits in accordance with GAAP</li>
<li>Create and setup vendor bills for timely payment</li>
<li>Maintain the accuracy and timeliness of all data entered in the Company’s General Ledger</li>
<li>Reconcile accounts receivable to ensure that payments are accounted for and properly posted</li>
<li>Verify discrepancies and resolve client billing issues utilizing customer-centric problem solving</li>
<li>Regularly perform follow-up with past due accounts</li>
<li>Produce financial reporting on a monthly, quarterly and annual basis</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned as needed</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Minimum Requirements</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business or related field</li>
<li>2 years’ experience in Accounts Receivable</li>
<li>&gt; 5 years’ experience working in Accounting, Finance or Administration</li>
<li>Experience in completing end of year taxes</li>
<li>Experience in customer service</li>
<li>Construct professional e-mail communication</li>
<li>Detail-oriented and have a strong ability to multitask on a continuous basis</li>
<li>Manage time effectively</li>
<li>Proven problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Intermediate to advanced 10-Key skills</li>
<li>5 years’ QuickBooks or experience working with a similar software</li>
<li>Able to create and edit Microsoft Excel spreadsheets</li>
<li>Type a minimum of 50 WPM (Typing test required)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Preferred Requirements</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with journal entries.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in working with income statement and balance sheet</li>
<li>Experience handling sales tax and franchise tax</li>
<li>Experience with 1099</li>
<li>Working knowledge of the processes and procedures associated with end of month closing/quarterly closing</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Work Schedule</b></h3>

</ul>
