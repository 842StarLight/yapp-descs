<div>This position will serve as a strategic partner to the Lead Contract Fleet Technician and assigned customer account(s) with a focus on ensuring all vehicle maintenance and repairs performed meet customer expectations and manufacturer and LBS standards and guidelines. Looking for an individual to foster, manage, and promote positive internal and external relationships through excellent communication and customer service while assisting in the development of and adherence to sound practices and processes.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Provide on-site technical assistance, examine buses to determine malfunctions, perform required repairs, and perform scheduled and unscheduled maintenance on vehicles in accordance with manufactures’ recommendations, statutory requirements, and company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Prepare buses for NYS Department of Transportation inspections and customer daily use.</li>
<li>Promote positive interdepartmental and customer relationships through effective communication.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Minimum Requirements of the position are:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>Basic diesel engine knowledge. Preferably with International and Cummins engines.</li>
<li>Computer proficiency in web-based programs and applications, Microsoft Office Suite applications and related departmental software.</li>
<li>Basic airbrake and air conditioning knowledge.</li>
<li>Familiar with DOT regulations in the State of New York.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Certifications or Licenses Required:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Valid New York driver’s license CDL class A or B with passenger endorsement and no airbrake restrictions with DOT medical certificate or obtain within first six (6) months of employment. Must be maintained throughout employment.</li>
<li>A.S.E certification in bus or heavy truck repair highly desired.</li>
</ul>
