<h2>VP (Marketing)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>MonsterCloud is the leader in ransomware removal and one of the first companies to offer cybersecurity as an outsourced service to businesses. MonsterCloud’s comprehensive cybersecurity as a service platform provides companies with both the technology and, more importantly, expertise needed to defend themselves from hackers, data breaches, ransomware, and other external cyber threats. MonsterCloud reduces reputational risk and affords companies peace-of-mind, knowing their businesses are continuously being monitored and protected by some of the world’s top cybersecurity experts.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Position Overview:</b></div>
<div>
This is an exceptional growth opportunity for one of the top companies in a fast-growing B2B technology and service market segment. The ideal candidate must be a hands-on execution artist focused on driving awareness and sales leads from online channels, such as organic traffic via SEO and paid search traffic. Additionally, the roll will play a key roll in defining core brand and product positioning and act in a business development capacity to identifying new channel and growth opportunities. While the company is in South Florida, this is a remote location opportunity. The role reports directly into the CEO.<br>
<br>
</div>

Develop customer testimonials, videos, and other core marketing content</li>
