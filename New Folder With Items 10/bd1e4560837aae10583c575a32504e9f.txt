<h2>Marketing & Program Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Why Sentons?</b></p>
<p>
Mobile devices are at the center of our lives. As device makers think about how to increase the level of interactivity making phones, tablets and other related devices even more seamless in our every day lives, there is a clear need for touch and force sensing beyond the screen. For Sentons, every surface is an opportunity for interactivity and sensing how you want to use that device. As Sentons drives to capture the opportunities in this rapidly evolving market, we seek to grow our team and extend the very goal oriented, driven, creative, collaborative and customer oriented culture we’ve created.</p>
<p>
This is a high visibility role. You will drive and manage both corporate product marketing programs as well as customer programs involving the customers’ industrial design, next generation user interface and mechanical integration of high performance sensor and processor technologies for the world’s leading phone manufacturers.</p>
<div>
<p><b>Role Description</b></p>
<p>
Lead multi-functional teams including Sales, Marketing, PR, Ultrasonics, Electrical, Mechanical, Firmware and Software Engineering to deliver market leading sensing solutions for key customers.</p>
<p>
Build customer relationships to develop understanding of customer requirements, expectations, key decision makers, potential roadblocks, structure/hierarchy, and to nurture and develop champions.<br>
Develop and communicate product positioning, value proposition,<br>
market opportunity, and strategic strengths and weaknesses with respect to<br>
competitors.</p>
<p>
Define the customer program plan/schedule/key milestones and drive program execution.</p>
<p>
Communicate with internal and customer executives to drive the decision-making process.</p>
<p>
Ensure tight team coordination and communication whilst managing development budgets and engineering builds.</p>
<p>
Effectively and consistently communicate overall program status. Identify and resolve conflicts, inter-dependencies and program issues quickly and efficiency.</p>
