<h2>Associate Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>Supports a cross functional team through a defined commercialization process. Effectively work in a fast pace environment while ensuring all product launches align with defined scope. Manage discontinued product/packaging process with excellence to minimize Wells exposure. Assist CI initiatives that drive out waste and/or increase efficiencies for products manufactured at Wells.</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Supports a cross functional team through the defined new commercialization process to deliver sustainable product launches, allowing Wells to achieve financial metrics.</div></li>
<li><div>Work in a fast pace environment with direct interface with Wells marketing, sales, and partnerships to meet corporate objectives. Maintains professional relationship demands with internal and external customers.</div></li>
<li><div>With guidance supports CI initiatives that drive out waste and/or increase efficiencies for products manufactured at Wells (ie. mix consolidation, ingredient optimization, plant feasibility).</div></li>
<li><div>Coordinate discontinued product/packaging process (run out plans, SKU optimization action, DIP and ect.) to minimize E&amp;O exposure when product life cycle is deemed complete. Work through secondary market as needed.</div></li>
<li><div>Support the Wells defined commercialization processes (strategic input from marketing) and deliver upon defined scope.</div></li>
<li><div>Effectively complete Discontinued Item Process (DIP) to ensure the reduction of E&amp;O, both raw materials (includes packaging) and finished goods.</div></li>
<li><div>Assist commercialization of corporate initiatives to achieve the overall corporate strategy, as well as coordinate the corporate run-out/transition plans to minimize our financial exposure.</div></li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
</div>

<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree required, preferably in Business or Project/Product Management.</li>
<li>1-2 years of Business experience required, or related experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of the overall WELLS business strategies in order to build better alignment cross functionally partners to commercialize products. Knowledge of WELLS policies and procedures. Demonstrates knowledge in a variety of the field's concepts, practices and procedures. Knowledge and familiarity with the commercialization tools and processes.</li>
<li>Possesses strong thinking and decision making skills. Problem solving skills, functional and technical skills, written and verbal communication skills, relationship management, interpersonal skills, analytical skills, insight generation.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work with others to complete work assignments. Ability to support and effectively communicate vision and purpose. Ability to coordinate, influence and support change. Ability to develop and execute business strategies. Ability to effectively support a cross-functional team, drive results and build relationships. Ability to communicate effectively at all levels in the organization.
</li>
</ul>
