Building on a rich tradition—but emphasizing innovation—the office shares characteristics with PitchBook and the financial technology industry itself. <br>
<b>About the Role:</b><br>
<br>
“Customers are King!" Each and every day the Customer Success Team works to ensure that are clients are maximizing the value they are receiving from PitchBook. Onboarding, training, support, business development and renewals are handled by the Customer Success team. We believe in doing whatever it takes to make our customers successful and the CS team drives that success every day.<br>
<br>
As an Account Manager for Enterprise accounts, you’ll be managing PitchBook’s largest customer relationships. Account Managers work to drive value that leads to expansion, renewals and customer satisfaction. You will be dedicated to finding additional opportunities within current client accounts by establishing lasting relationship with key decision makers.<br>
<br>
<b>Primary Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Act as primary point of contact and business partner for a dedicated portfolio of clients via in-person meetings, phone and/or email</li>
<li>
Identify growth opportunities to expand existing user base within portfolio of accounts to achieve and consistently exceed sales and retention goals</li>
<li>
Conduct client demonstration sessions designed to address the individual needs of the account and end user with a consultative approach</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with a designated Customer Success Manager to strategize on account retention and growth opportunities</li>
<li>
Go above and beyond to ensure customer satisfaction with quarterly business reviews and continual product education</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Have experience with Salesforce or other CRMs to forecast sales and maintain updated records of client interactions</li>
<li>
Have excellent verbal and written communication skills with a keen eye for detail</li>
<li>
Have an appreciation for the PE / VC marketplace and the ability to learn, comprehend, and demonstrate why and how each of our client types engages with our product and our data</li>
<li>
Have at least two (2) years of sales or account management experience</li>
<li>
Have a BA/BS degree</li>
</ul>
