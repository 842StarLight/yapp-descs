<b>About this Career Opportunity:
</b><ul>
<li>Coordinates customer-focused services to business/employers among partners in the one-stop system and center to achieve WIOA Business Services Outcomes.</li>
<li>
Brings new employers to the system, and connect employers to the one-stop service delivery system.</li>
<li>
Establishes and maintains active and frequent contact with employers. Maintains follow-up contact with employers to determine satisfaction with services and plan for quality improvement.</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains detailed knowledge of the local labor market. Provides current workforce intelligence, data and reports that can assist business and partners in workforce needs.</li>
<li>
Understand the needs of businesses and align workforce development solutions in the local and regional economy; focusing on the following sectors: Healthcare; Advanced Manufacturing; Trade/Deep Sea/Transportation/Warehouse logistics; Construction; Information Technology/Cyber Security; Military/Defense</li>
<li>
Enlists cooperation of community and business leaders to help increase awareness of available workforce services in the community.</li>
<li>
Deliver presentations to business and trade organizations regarding workforce related topics and services, in order to promote career pathways and create a talent pipeline through work-based learning opportunities.</li>
<li>
Develop customer-centric solutions-based strategies to meet recruitment needs, including hiring events, career fairs (such as JobFest, Career Day) and targeted position placements. Flexible to occasionally work outside of normal business hours for events and/or to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
