<h2>Account Manager / Customer Success</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Account Managers at EDITED are strategic thinkers who thrive in making our customers successful in our product. As a member of the team, it’ll be your direct responsibility to develop personal connections to our customers, helping them understand our product and see value in it.</p>
<p>
You'll be a true product expert and advocate - that means understanding every feature of EDITED as well as being able to explain its benefits to a wide range of users and stakeholders. You'll be speaking to our high profile retail clients every day by email, phone and in person, so you’ll need an intrinsic understanding of retail to build strategic relationships and speak knowledgeably and empathetically about the issues they face.</p>
<p>
You will provide support to a portfolio of customer accounts so they successfully use and integrate EDITED. You will take responsibility for every account, making sure you retain every customer, and they see a return on their investment. You will also help us shape the future of the software through being a strong customer advocate - listening to their views about the product and reporting them to the relevant teams internally. We expect you to be passionate about our customers, and be as excited about helping them to get the most value out of our product, as we are.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Our expectations for your position:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>You will be an outstanding collaborator and work with Retail Strategists to develop success plans for customers that outline their critical success factors, metrics for success, potential issues, and provide recommendations.</li>
<li>You are able to identify client warning signs (both in-person, and via usage data) and communicate it with the VP of Customer Success.</li>
<li>You will take responsibility for regular progress reviews with customers (e.g. quarterly business reviews/QBRs) and identify opportunities for upsells and manage processes.</li>
<li>You must be a strong advocate for our products, able to articulate positioning, use cases and benefits.</li>
<li>You have exceptional verbal and written communication skills and are able to communicate across time zones and all types of people in all sorts of roles.</li>
<li>You are organized and meticulous about documenting processes.</li>
<li>You are numerate, and data literate, and know how to identify trends in client usage and use these trends to assess levels of integration.</li>
<li>Manage executive, mid-level, and project relationships among a diverse set of customers, turning them into EDITED ambassadors.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2 years account management/customer success experience.</li>
<li>An interest or background in fashion or retailing.</li>
<li>Proven success in growing existing accounts and relationships.</li>
<li>Technical competence and enthusiasm (understanding of SaaS software or technology).</li>
<li>Excellent written, communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Loves being on a team.</li>
<li>Independent – to take initiative and own processes.</li>
<li>Highly organized. Tenacity to finish projects.</li>
<li>Proven negotiation experience.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
