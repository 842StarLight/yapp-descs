<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>

At Dewberry, we seek out exceptional talent and strive to deliver the highest quality of services to our clients. Whether you’re an experienced professional or a new graduate, you’ll have the chance to collaborate with the best and brightest and work on innovative and complex projects at the forefront of the industry. Our commitment to excellence stems from our personal integrity and from other defining attributes, which we call “Dewberry at Work,” that have inspired our employees to be successful for more than a half-century.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Dewberry is currently seeking a Proposal Manager for pursuit and proposal efforts based in our Raleigh, NC office. This position requires a marketing and business development background in the management consulting and A/E businesses; experience with building engineering (mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and structural (MEP)) services is preferable.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>The Proposal Manager will work in tandem with business development and operations personnel to determine go/no-go decisions, pursuit win strategies, craft sales messages, assemble pursuit teams, manage the proposal process, and develop proposal responses.
</li>
<li>Responsible for full life cycle of the proposal preparation process, including state and local proposals, SF330’s, design-build proposals, technical proposals, qualification packages, and oral presentations.</li>
<li>
Comply with our corporate standards to present a consistent appearance/style for all presentation graphics, proposals, resumes, and collateral materials.</li>
<li>
Write supporting sales materials including project descriptions, resumes, service line overviews, and executive summaries, etc.</li>
<li>
Have sufficient sales acumen to represent the firm appropriately with teaming partners and subcontractors, at conferences, and business networking opportunities.</li>
<li>
Support Business Development and operational personnel in market research, competitor analysis, and client intelligence.</li>
<li>
Manage multiple proposal efforts and teams simultaneously for within diverse markets and service offerings.</li>
<li>
Organize marketing material for ease of use.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required Skills &amp; Required Experience
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>B.A. or B.S. in Liberal Arts or equivalent experience required.</li>
<li>
5+ years’ experience personally producing proposals and managing proposal efforts.</li>
<li>
Extensive experience with state/local proposal formats.</li>
<li>
Must have extensive MS Word, PowerPoint and Adobe InDesign experience. Other graphics software and CRM database experience is also desired.</li>
<li>
Exceptional written and oral communications skills
</li>
<li>Effective proposal management skills, including planning, organization, problem identification and resolution, and resource management skills.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills and prior experience leading multi-disciplinary teams and work collaboratively.</li>
<li>
Must be available to work overtime as necessary in order to meet business requirements.</li>
</ul>
