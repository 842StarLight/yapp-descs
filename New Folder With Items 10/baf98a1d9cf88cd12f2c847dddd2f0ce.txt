This position is responsible for providing overall management for complex product development projects through the product concept, system requirements, engineering design, and design verification phases. Projects may be the development portion of externally funded programs or internal funded research and development programs. For all assigned projects, the Engineering Project Manager is responsible for the cost, schedule, risk management and technical performance of development projects. The Engineering Project Manager works closely with the Business Development, Engineering, and Program Management teams to develop and execute sound product development projects.<br>
<br>
Establishes program schedule to include the development of Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) and Integrated Master Schedule (IMS) and manages scope to ensure continued alignment with contract or internal requirements and budgets. Monitors and assesses program performance against plan (schedule and financial), identifies risk, develops and implements corrective action when necessary to initiate program changes.<br>
<br>
Working as a matrix manager, directs the project team to include resource allocation and management. Through expert communication and leadership skills, establishes a common understanding among team members on technical approach, scope, schedule, risk and financial objectives. For internally funded programs, works with Supply Chain team to execute to procurement plan.<br>
<br>
Controls expenditures within the limitations of the project budget and regularly reports on project budget to actual performance via the Estimate to Complete (ETC) process. Able to utilize Earned Value Management as a cost control technique.<br>
<br>
Performs as proposal manager or volume lead on technical/development aspects of bid and proposal activities. Able to prepare and present bid review briefings to senior leadership. Actively seeks new business in coordination with Business Development.<br>
<br>
Demonstrates ethical and professional behavior in accordance with company values. Performs other duties, as assigned.<br>
