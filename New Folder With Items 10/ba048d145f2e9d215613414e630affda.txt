<h2>Enterprise Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>ABOUT US</b></p>
<p>
NextCapital is the leader in enterprise digital advice. NextCapital partners with world-class institutions to deliver personalized planning and managed accounts to individual investors, whether they’re saving for retirement in a 401(k) or IRA or investing in a taxable brokerage account. Our open-architecture digital advice solution provides better outcomes for our partners and individual investors by offering integrated account aggregation, analytics, planning and portfolio management, and customizable advice methodology and fiduciary roles.</p>
<p><b>
JOIN OUR TEAM</b></p>
<p>
We're looking for a talented and highly motivated Enterprise Marketing Manager to join our team. The ideal candidate is a self-starting B2B marketing whiz who is eager to build a world class marketing discipline at a high growth company. Learn more about NextCapital at themuse.com/companies/nextcapital.</p>
<p><b>
WHAT YOU’LL DO</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Quickly ramp-up NextCapital’s go-to-market strategy, positioning, value proposition, and messaging</li>
<li>Oversee the design, creation, and ongoing execution of all sales and marketing collateral materials</li>
<li>Drive lifecycle marketing strategic planning and execution for both enterprise client and individual investor audiences</li>
<li>Serve as the primary content owner for our retail website at NextCapital.com</li>
<li>Manage public relations activities including press releases, events, content, and third party agency relationships</li>
<li>Define marketing funnel analytics and optimization efforts with support from data warehouse team</li>
<li>Work with our enterprise sales and relationship management teams to develop and deliver best practices playbooks to augment existing partner sales and marketing capabilities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
WHO WE’RE LOOKING FOR</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have a Bachelor’s degree in marketing or a related field</li>
<li>You have 5-8 years of experience in marketing enterprise technology services</li>
<li>You have experience in the financial industry</li>
<li>You’re adept at communicating effectively across multiple audiences</li>
<li>You stay calm and focused in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>You have a demonstrated ability to execute market research, develop strategies &amp; tactics from the ground up, and manage budgets</li>
<li>You favor evidence-based decision making and have experience manipulating and reporting on KPIs</li>
<li>You are a strategist and planner, but with a bias for action and an ability to quickly develop and deploy marketing programs and assets</li>
<li>You are equally comfortable leveraging a defined process or designing a new one</li>
<br>
</ul>
