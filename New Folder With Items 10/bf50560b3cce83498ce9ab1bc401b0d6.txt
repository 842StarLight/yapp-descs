<p><b>Why Scranton Gillette Communications/SGC Horizon?</b></p>
<p>
Located in Arlington Heights, a northwest suburb of Chicago and accessible via the Metra Union Pacific Northwest line with a shuttle to and from the station, Scranton Gillette Communications (SGC/SGC Horizon) is a leader in business to business communications reaching over 1 million professionals monthly through our print, digital and custom media and live &amp; virtual events.
</p>
<p>It is our mission to be the most trusted source of content for the industries we serve through a range of high-engagement digital, print and in-person experiences. We set the benchmark for our industry with leading digital and print brands, including <i>Professional Builder</i>, <i>Roads &amp; Bridges</i>, <i>Professional Remodeler,</i> <i>Water &amp; Wastes Digest, Furniture, Lighting &amp; Decor and Imaging Technology News</i>, among others. We lead in content marketing by going beyond traditional publishing to provide innovative, effective marketing solutions that help major brands communicate with their customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Description:</b></p>
<p>
We’re looking for a mid-level Marketing Project Coordinator to join our team! You will work with our Marketing Manager and Marketing Project Manager to coordinate the development, creation and execution of brand marketing efforts to drive revenue, including but not limited to research, promotion, digital and social media, webinars and events.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Work with internal teams to develop, create and execute marketing campaigns and media kits for all brands</li>
<li> Develop necessary components and deploy/manage webinars</li>
<li> Develop and execute successful publication branded events</li>
<li> Develop, deploy and measure social media or programmatic campaigns as required for the publication brands</li>
<li> Set up, execute and analyze research studies for the brands and/or their clients using the appropriate platform</li>
<li>Maintain up-to-date records of projects, materials and project status</li>
<li>Proactively engage internal teams for material updates.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>What We’re Looking for:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> 5-7 years relevant experience with: marketing campaign development and execution; event coordination; market research; sales collateral; digital and social media; email campaigns</li>
<li> General digitally savvy including a working knowledge of social media (profiles, ads, engagement practices) and programmatic ad platforms</li>
<li> Ability to lead brands through campaign milestones with the capability to influence key decision makers</li>
<li> Strong analytical skills with the ability to create Excel based reports</li>
<li> Strong project management and coordination skills.</li>
<li> Ability to work independently in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment</li>
<li> Excellent interpersonal and communication skills (verbal/written/listening)</li>
<li> Excellent attention to detail</li>
<li> Knowledge of Microsoft Word and Excel or Google Docs and Spreadsheets; Familiarity with Adobe Create Suites programs a plus. Working knowledge of how digital platform based software for research, video production, webinars, advertising operate</li>
<br>
</ul>
