<h2>Kitchen Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Company Introduction:<br>
Park Burger is a casual, family-friendly neighborhood restaurant, serving Denver’s prizewinning burgers, fries, shakes, and beer. At Park Burger, we pride ourselves on crafting the best burger using only the freshest, highest quality ingredients. We start with all natural, never frozen beef, buffalo, and house made turkey and veggie burger patties, all served on locally baked buns made exclusively for Park Burger.<br>

Overview:<br>
Summary:<br>
<br>
Park Burger Restaurant Group is searching for an energetic, professional and visionary leader for one of our restaurant concepts. Park Burger Restaurant Group is a locally owned and operated company in Denver, Colorado with a strong focus on fresh and local ingredients. All restaurant Managers are hired with the understanding that they can move in between any of our four concepts, which include Park Burger, Homegrown Tap &amp; Dough, Park &amp; Co and Birdcall. We are a fast-growing company, opening new locations and concepts each year which is creating ample opportunity for growth.<br>

Additionally, we offer an employee benefits package consisting of Medical, Dental and Vision coverage, supplemental benefits, paid vacation and sick time, fitness discounts and pet insurance.<br>

Responsibilities/Functions of Position:<br>
<br>
Essential Functions:<br>
<br>
<ul><li>The Kitchen Manager is responsible for assisting in managing the daily operations of our restaurant, including the selection, development and performance management of employees. In addition, they help oversee the inventory and ordering of food and supplies, optimize profits and ensure that guests are satisfied with their dining experience. This role primarily focuses on the back of house operations, but can require guest facing duties.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Adhere to company standards and service levels to increase sales and minimize costs, including food, beverage, supply, utility and labor costs</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Enforce sanitary practices for food handling, general cleanliness, and maintenance of kitchen and dining areas. Ensure compliance with operational standards, company policies, federal/state/local laws, and ordinances</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Responsible for ensuring consistent high quality of food preparation and service</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Maintain professional restaurant image, including restaurant cleanliness, proper uniforms, and appearance standards.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Supervise portion control and quantities of preparation to minimize waste</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Ensure positive guest service in all areas. Respond to complaints, taking any and all appropriate actions to turn dissatisfied guests into return guests</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Ensure a safe working and guest environment to reduce the risk of injury and accidents. Completes accident reports promptly in the event that a guest or employee is injured</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Manage shifts which include: daily decision making, scheduling, planning while upholding standards, product quality and cleanliness</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Investigate and resolve complaints concerning food quality and service</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Interview hourly employees. Direct hiring, supervision, development and, when necessary, termination of employees</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Conduct orientation, explain the Park Burger/Homegrown/Park and Co Philosophy, and oversee the training of new employees</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Develop employees by providing ongoing feedback, establishing performance expectations and by conducting performance reviews</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Maintain an accurate and up-to-date plan of restaurant staffing needs. Prepare schedules and ensure that the restaurant is staffed for all shifts</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Provide direction to employees regarding operational and procedural issues</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Performs other duties and responsibilities as required or requested</li></ul>
