<br>
Are you looking for a new challenge in a fast-paced, innovative company? Come make history with Amazon Advertising’s legal affairs department. We are looking for a Contracts Manager to support Amazon Advertising’s rapidly growing businesses. We are currently looking for someone who has the uncanny ability to always know where they are needed, can identify and implement process improvements, and can effectively work cross-functionally with other teams. The successful candidate will possess the ability to work efficiently and independently; manage high volumes of tasks and projects with little guidance; react with appropriate urgency to situations and events that require a quick response or turnaround; take effective action without having to know the total picture; and think creatively and be proactive in solving problems that affect people within the group or other related groups. Your responsibilities will include working with the business to review marketing collateral and other customer-facing and publicity messaging; negotiating and documenting a variety of third party contractual relationships, including sales and services agreements, licenses, NDAs, amendments, and other agreements; developing internal processes to track transactions from beginning to end; and coordinating with clients and international counsel. You must have excellent written and oral communication skills as this position involves direct contact with principal business owners.
