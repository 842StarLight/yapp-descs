<p><b>JOB SUMMARY
</b></p>
<p>The Volunteer Services Coordinator is accountable for the organization, implementation, and evaluation of Volunteer Services activities. This individual will work collaboratively with the Volunteer Services Manager to coordinate various volunteer efforts and recognition, promotion of the volunteer services program, and coordination of volunteer materials, compliance, and communications. The Volunteer Services Coordinator must be able to exercise independent judgement and communicate knowledgeably on Marshfield Clinic Health System programs.
</p>
