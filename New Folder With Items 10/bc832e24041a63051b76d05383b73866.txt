<h2>Assistant Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Garden Distribution organization is hiring an Assistant Branch Manager to work in the Fairfield, OH Pennington Seed warehouse. This position requires an enthusiastic and outgoing self-starter to manage daily warehouse functions such as shipping/receiving, order entry, and customer service. In addition, this role supports customer sales by managing 3rd party customer onsite sales.</p>
<p>
Central Garden &amp; Pet Company (NASDAQ: CENT &amp; CENTA) is a leading innovator, marketer and producer of quality branded products for the pet and lawn and garden supplies markets. For additional company information, please visit our website at www.central.com.</p>
<p><b>
MEASURES OF SUCCESS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Manage 3+ employees within counter sales, shipping, and warehouse departments</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Generate inside sales by calling on smaller local accounts from inside the office</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Operate sales counter; managing inside sales and walk-in customers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Generate sales orders, pick tickets, and route deliveries via SAP</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support outside sales reps with order entry and customer service</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage light manufacturing capabilities as well as a small private fleet with daily deliveries.Oversee drivers and maintain DOT compliance</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Follow and implement required safety procedures. Assist in training and keeping all employees trained and up to company standards</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Oversee daily warehouse activities (shipping/receiving, grass seed blending, inventory management)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>IDEAL CANDIDATE ATTRIBUTES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>BA or BS preferred (Agriculture, Agricultural Business, Turf, or Business Management)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent communication and relationship building skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proven ability to manage a team; strong teamwork ethic</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proficiency in MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be able to read, analyze, interpret and make decisions when identifying in-process problems, and able to deal with a variety of job assignments</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to multi-task and perform effectively and organized with a high level of details</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be willing to learn SAP software program functions. Previous SAP experience is a plus</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manual labor required in position (i.e. lifting 50 lb. bags materials)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Daily use of industrial equipment (i.e. forklift and other material handling equipment)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to work in a constant state of alertness and safe manner</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
