<h2>STAFF SPECIALIST (DukeCard)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The DukeCard specialist is a member of the DukeCard team and works closely with DukeCard systems analysis, financial management and program management staff. The role serves as the primary point of contact for all customer interactions and liaises with university departments regarding program activities and special projects. He or she is expected to advise management on the status of current activities, additional or expanded program requirements and make appropriate recommendations for solutions to problems. This position reports to both the team lead and the director of DukeCard.
<br><br>
<b>Duties &amp; Work Performed:</b><br>
Perform daily card production and financial management operational tasks according to documented procedures and guidelines Provide front-line support to users via telephone, email, social media or walk-in visits in a professional manner Perform a variety of non-routine, IT support functions; resolve routine questions and problems, and refer more complex issues to higher levels; may be assigned an area of specialization based on skills and experience (e.g. financial, card production, inventory management, summer conferences, etc.) Work special events such as First Year Move-in Day, Employee Appreciation and Staff Orientation Assist with testing and maintaining operational documentation such as troubleshooting, FAQ and solution procedures as assigned Train users (and peers) as needed on card production tasks Provide card printer support to Duke hospitals and the Duke University Marine Lab Coordinate documentation pickup for storage and shredding according to the university’s document retention policy Assign, remove and manage the recreation center memberships for employees, students, alumni and sponsored guests Participate in regular team meetings and training sessions Perform other duties as assigned
<br><br>
<b>Supervisory Responsibilities:</b><br>
None
<br><br>
<b>Education:</b><br>
<b>Required:</b><br>
Associates degree (Computer Science, Accounting or related field preferred) or related experience.
<br><br>
<b>Preferred:</b> BA or BS degree
<br><br>
<b>Experience &amp; Skills:</b><br>
<b>Required :</b><br>
Minimum of three years technical and customer management experience in a higher education setting. Working knowledge of basic accounting, communications and project task management.
<br><br>
<b>Preferred:</b> Knowledge of Blackboard Transact Access and Financial systems Knowledge of ServiceNow service management software Experience with using and troubleshooting card production technology such as photo upload systems, card printers and laminators; ability to manage card stock inventory Experience configuring hand-held devices such as iPod Touch, Motorola 9410 and managing device inventory Experience with Point of Sale Equipment and/or Sequoia hardware Experience with productivity tools such as Word and Excel Accounting experience is a plus
<br><br>
<b>Soft Skills:</b> Team orientation; demonstrated ability to collaborate with others to solve problems Demonstrated strong verbal and written communication skills. Ability to communicate with all levels of customers as well as other support entities Excellent customer service skills including expectation setting and customer follow up. Excellent time-management and organizational skills Self-motivated and goal-oriented; must have strong analytical skills Ability to work under minimal supervision Continuous attention to security and confidentiality issues due to exposure to sensitive data within support role
<br><br>
<b>Working Conditions:</b><br>
Fast paced office environment with constant customer contact.
