<h2>Chef - Sous</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
Who are we?</b></p>
<p>
Live Nation Entertainment is the world’s leading live entertainment and eCommerce company, comprised of four market leaders: Ticketmaster.com, Live Nation Concerts, Artist Nation and Live Nation Network. Ticketmaster.com is the global event ticketing leader and one of the world’s top five eCommerce sites, with over 27 million monthly unique visitors. Live Nation Concerts produces over 22,000 shows annually for more than 2,300 artists globally. Artist Nation is the world’s top artist management company, representing over 250 artists. These businesses power Live Nation Network, the leading provider of entertainment marketing solutions, enabling over 800 advertisers to tap into the 200 million consumers Live Nation delivers annually through its live event and digital platforms. For additional information, visit www.livenation.com/investors.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
HOB Entertainment, a division of Live Nation Entertainment, offers exceptional club venues nationwide. House of Blues Venues provide several genres of live music, from Rock, Hip Hop, Country, Rock en Español, Blues, Jazz, Reggae and Pop. House of Blues venues are also known for their southern-style cuisine, juke-joint inspired atmosphere and the World Famous Gospel Brunch.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
House of Blues opened its doors on Thanksgiving Day in 1992, serving its first meal to the local shelters. Since opening, House of Blues has been committed to giving back to the community through the International House of Blues Foundation.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Entertainment, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Responsible for all food and food related costs, procedures, quality, and all back of house labor percentage and budgets.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><b>
I. Financial Performance of Departments</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Meets or exceeds budgetary expectation</li>
<li>
Food &amp; Beverage Cost</li>
<li>
Labor Cost</li>
<li>
Operating Accounts</li>
<li>
Inventory is controlled, monitored and maintained bi-weekly</li>
<li>
Develop menu engineering program to project and monitor trend and predict cost</li>
</ul>
<p>
II. Supervision of the Department</p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist and support kitchen management in any of their job functions, as needed</li>
<li>
Work with Executive Chef to schedule shifts for kitchen staff</li>
<li>
Ensure that there is no unapproved overtime on shift</li>
<li>
Check labor daily in Labor Management system</li>
<li>
Perform all opening and closing kitchen supervisory tasks</li>
<li>
Ensure positive and creative team environment within department</li>
<li>
Facilitate open communication with the Sous Chefs, supervisors and staff</li>
<li>
Facilitate proper inter-departmental communications and organization</li>
<li>
Perform timely staff evaluations (training and annual). Set written development and performance goals for all team members, and monitor progress</li>
<li>
Assure complete and satisfactory on-going staff training per corporate training program</li>
<li>
Ensure appropriate staffing levels. Recruitment and hiring staff/supervisors, ensure that hiring/disciplinary/termination standards follow all HOB guidelines</li>
<li>
Maintains open door policy/one on one with staff</li>
<li>
Decisions are information based and follow up on efficiently</li>
<li>
Information is gathered, shared, and used appropriately</li>
<li>
Ensure adherence to safety guidelines</li>
<li>
Ensure proper execution of all corporate mandates, SOP’s and Specs</li>
<li>
Participate in weekly Kitchen Manager’s meeting</li>
</ul>
<p>
III. Sale Building</p>
<ul>
<li>
Development, with Executive Chef and Corporate Food and Beverage, of cost effective specials and menu changes to achieve maximum sales</li>
<li>
Assist Executive Chef with menu changes 2 times a year in Restaurant</li>
<li>
Create menu contest with Executive Chef to increase particular sales in designated areas</li>
<li>
Ensure food is considered excellent value for money and increases guest frequency</li>
<li>
Assist and support kitchen staff in any of their job functions, as needed</li>
<li>
Participate in outside appearances and fund raising events to increase awareness of the House of Blues</li>
</ul>
<p>
IV. Maintain Operation Supplies</p>
<ul>
<li>
All staff has supplies needed to facilitate job</li>
<li>
All staff is trained (and signed off) on equipment upon hiring</li>
<li>
All equipment is maintained in operable condition</li>
<li>
All events are organized and planned per HOB specs.</li>
<li>
All housekeeping standards are maintained in compliance with inspection guidelines / Local Health Code</li>
<li>
HOB Specs. Forms are updated and used to insure consistency</li>
</ul>
<p>
V. Maintenance and Update of Micros and Labor Systems</p>
<ul>
<li>
All employees input prior to work</li>
<li>
All employees are job classed and current wages are inputted</li>
<li>
All files are maintained and changes are submitted to Corporate Food and Beverage</li>
<li>
All menu item counts are current and maintained weekly</li>
</ul>
<p>
VI. Food</p>
<ul>
<li>
All food standards are met per HOB Specs and not deviated from without approval from Corporate Food and Beverage</li>
<li>
Food portions are monitored and revisions are recommended to Corporate Food and Beverage</li>
<li>
New items are recommended quarterly to Corporate Food and Beverage</li>
<li>
Guest and crew feedback is reported and issues resolved</li>
<li>
Recipe adherence is 100%</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Required:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years kitchen management experience and experience in management</li>
<li>
Skilled in cooking, cost controls, management, crew training and development</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Kitchen and dishwashing procedures</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Food and Beverage costs, labor costs, and product cost</li>
<li>
Computer knowledge: Excel worksheets, databases, word processing, and Micros</li>
<li>
Ability to run all kitchen operations</li>
<li>
Knowledge of operations and procedures of a multi-faceted entertainment facility</li>
<li>
College degree</li>
<li>
Able to lift at least 150 lbs.</li>
<li>
Able to stand for long periods of time (10 – 12 hours)</li>
<li>
Able to tolerate extreme heat</li>
<li>
Able to work in a fast paced environment</li>
</ul>
