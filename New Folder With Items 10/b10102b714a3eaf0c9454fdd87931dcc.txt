<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Some Challenges You’ll Tackle
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>As a Manager in our Restaurant Accounts department, this position will be leading and developing a team of Account Advisors. The Account Advisor team's focus is to expand our restaurant relationships by selling and servicing existing restaurant clients.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Lead a team of 8-10 Account Advisors who are actively improving our restaurant accounts every day</li>
<li>
Develop the team’s sales skills to and teach them to build relationships with their portfolio of restaurant accounts</li>
<li>
Work with each individual on your team to meet or exceed goals for account retention, enhancement and our online ordering products</li>
<li>
Have a one on one with each team member at least once a month to discuss pipelines, activity performance and progress to personal goals</li>
<li>
Have a team meeting at least once a month</li>
<li>
Listen to sales calls and score them for quality and development purposes</li>
<li>
Encourage team evolvement with our products and company initiatives.</li>
<li>
Contribute to cross-functional projects related to our restaurant accounts or our team’s tools and products</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
