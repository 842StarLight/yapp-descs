<h2>Customer Service Representative (Technical Support)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p>We are looking for a well-rounded, energetic Client Support Representative that serve as a voice and face of the company when interacting with customers in providing support of our product line. Desiring someone who is proficient in excel and power point, and a passion for providing outstanding customer service. This person will be on the front line so it is essential and imperative that the qualified candidate be able to express themselves clearly and concisely.</p>
<p>Job Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Help Desk Support</li>
<li>Consult with customers by telephone to provide information, answer customer questions, resolve individual problems, handle complaints, and handle other transactions</li>
<li>Invoicing</li>
<li>Update customer files and databases</li>
<li>Run sales reports</li>
<li>Build Power Point presentations</li>
<li>Importing pricing to spreadsheets for current and prospective customers</li>
<li>Flexibility to work overtime when necessary</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in use of computers, spreadsheets and putting together presentations in Power Point</li>
<li>Any experience in SAP software or willing to learn SAP software as well as additional software</li>
<li>Basic understand of learning the product line over time</li>
<li>Excellent analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Ability to meet deadlines, perform duties with accuracy</li>
<li>Flexibility and willingness to assume new duties and responsibilities</li>
<li>Ability to maintain confidential information</li>
<li>Self-reliant, ambitious, with a desire to succeed</li>
<li>A desire to provide a great customer service experience for all accounts and customers a top priority</li>
</ul>
