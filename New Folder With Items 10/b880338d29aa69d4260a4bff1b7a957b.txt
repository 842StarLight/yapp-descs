<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Avanti Residential is a rapidly growing property management company comprised of 26 apartment communities and approximately 5,500 units in the Colorado and Arizona markets. The work environment is challenging, fast-paced, multi-faceted and dynamic. We are a privately held company with our corporate offices in a downtown, professional setting poised for tremendous growth in the coming years giving a successful candidate a wide range of future career growth opportunities.</p>
<p>
JOB SUMMARY:</p>
<p>
The Leasing Consultant assists the Community Manager and Leasing Manager with showing and leasing of available apartment homes to prospective residents. This position ensures a positive working environment, quality community living, and a high standard of operations to meet Avanti Residential objectives and marketing plans.</p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES</p>
<ul>
<li>
Completes lease form or agreement and collects rental deposit.</li>
<li>
Responsible for insuring the "model" apartments and target apartments are ready for show; responsible for maintaining work area and office in clean and orderly manner; need to have an awareness of the "curb appeal" of the community.</li>
<li>
Thorough knowledge of lease terms, specifications and all community policies.</li>
<li>
Knowledgeable of required telephone and sales techniques.</li>
<li>
Knowledgeable of current market conditions.</li>
<li>
Fully informed of current rental rates, sizes, locations and all amenities of community.</li>
<li>
Maintain contact with all apartment locator services and local businesses to provide informational materials.</li>
<li>
Responsible for "shopping" competitive properties.</li>
<li>
Develop full knowledge of application information required, screening processes and policies regarding rentals.</li>
<li>
Responsible to conduct out-reach marketing at least once per week.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain an on-going resident retention program under direction of Community Manager.</li>
<li>
Responsible to play an active role in the renewal process.</li>
<li>
Responsible for proper maintenance of all resident and community lease files.</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintaining and updating Leasing and Property Handbook and Advertising Log Book.</li>
<li>
Efficient and timely processing of all required administrative reports and related information.</li>
<li>
Responsible for reporting unusual or extraordinary circumstances regarding the community or residents.</li>
<li>
Responsible for assisting Community Manager in collection of delinquent rents.</li>
<li>
Courteous, efficient handling of resident requests and complaints.</li>
<li>
Represent the company in a professional manner at all the times.</li>
<li>
Perform other related duties and responsibilities as required.</li>
</ul>
<p>
 KEY COMPETENCIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have excellent organizational and time management skills to successfully navigate frequent interruptions and changing priorities</li>
<li>
Proficient computer skills including knowledge of property management software preferably YARDI</li>
<li>
Work independently with minimal supervision and be accountable for actions, self-motivated</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills - written and verbal</li>
<li>
Demonstrated knowledge of phone and email procedures and etiquette</li>
<li>
Thrive in a fast-paced environment; Ability to prioritize and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Positive attitude, professional demeanor, team player</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE GUIDELINES - Any combination of education and experience that would likely provide the required knowledge and abilities is qualifying:</p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or GED</li>
<li>
One to two-year of Customer Service experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>
REQUIREMENTS</p>
<ul>
<li>
Possession of valid driver's license and up to date vehicle insurance OR reliable transportation</li>
<li>
Prior to employment pass criminal background screening</li>
</ul>
<p>
Salary for this full-time position will be commensurate with experience. We offer excellent benefits including medical, dental, vision and life insurance, short-and long-term disability insurance, flexible spending account and a 401(k). Housing discount is offered.</p>
