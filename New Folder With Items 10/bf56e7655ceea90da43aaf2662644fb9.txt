<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Tenstreet is a growing, fast-paced, SaaS business providing solutions for the transportation industry. Together we're disrupting the ways in which companies recruit, onboard and hire drivers. We're a team of over achievers who excel in execution, collaboration and problem solving. We offer great benefits and a casual environment. But the real perk of working at Tenstreet is being part of a community that values your work, encourages life-long learning and fosters a supportive culture.
<br>
<br>
The Account Management position is responsible for maintaining client support. This individual will be a highly motivated, self-starter, able to effectively work cross-functionally between various teams.
<br>
<br>
<b>What you'll be responsible for:</b>
<ul>
<li>Handling all phases of new product implementation and training for assigned clients.</li>
<li>Providing top level application support: Responding to &amp; resolving client requests and questions.</li>
<li>Focusing on client relationship and retention to ensure that each client is as successful as possible in using our product.</li>
<li>Serving as an expert on all operational aspects of all Tenstreet programs and software solutions.</li>
<li>Delivering a high level of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Being the interface to the client and work closely with other Tenstreet teams to implement strategic projects.</li>
<li>Working with Advisory team members to identify additional products and services that would add value for your clients.</li>
<li>Leading training sessions for both clients and internal Tenstreet employees.</li>
<li>Performing special projects as required.</li>
</ul>
<b>You may be a fit if:</b>
<ul>
<li>You write and speak clearly and concisely.</li>
<li>You naturally build strong relationships with clients over the phone.</li>
<li>You have a natural ability to handle a lot of complexity, understand what next steps are needed, then take action.</li>
<li>You're known for getting stuff done.</li>
<li>You're passionate about learning, do so quickly, and enjoy solving puzzles.</li>
<li>You have a tenacious focus on making every client as successful as possible and gain energy from making clients happy.</li>
<li>You very quickly get to the heart of the matter when discussing issues important with clients.</li>
<li>You are organized and not intimidated by (sometimes lengthy) to-do lists.</li>
</ul>
<b>What we'll provide you:</b>
<ul>
<li>A positive and generative work environment enabling you to develop your skills, collaborate with other professionals and invest in others.</li>
<li>The unique culture of working in a thriving software company right here in Tulsa.</li>
<li>Full benefits including 3 health insurance options, dental, vision, 401k, 15 PTO days, FSA/HSA accounts and other great perks.</li>
</ul>
