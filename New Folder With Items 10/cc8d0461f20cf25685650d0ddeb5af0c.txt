<h2>ISV Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>
<p>The ISV Account Executive focuses on the ISV community which is a strategic growth priority for nVoq. This role will drive recruitment of healthcare ISVs to embed HIPAA-compliant speech recognition, including both Android and iOS developers. The ISV Account Executive will be responsible for maximizing uptake and utilization of the nVoq solutions platform by targeting and developing commercially viable engagements with this ISV community.</p>
<p>
This is intended to be a virtual role for an individual with an established home office, working with a predominately virtual management team across the US; there is no relocation available to Boulder. The Boulder HQ office utilizes an office hoteling arrangement for remote employees.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute nVoq's go-to-market strategy for ISVs by identifying and engaging target vendors.</li>
<li>Manage the engagement life cycle with targeted vendors including: qualification, evaluation, contracting, onboarding and account planning including regular revenue forecasts.</li>
<li>Coordinate joint value proposition development including solutions marketing activities.</li>
<li>Stay abreast of current industry trends and developments as an active member of the nVoq platform sales team.</li>
<li>Closely monitor competitive platform activity from a commercial and functional standpoint.</li>
<li>Serve as a platform ambassador for nVoq at relevant industry events and end user conferences.</li>
<li>Collaborate with team colleagues for internal and management reporting processes and partner management infrastructure.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skill Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>B.S. required, MBA desirable</li>
<li>8+ years minimum partner account management experience, in the software industry, preferably with an OEM-centric platform vendor organization such as Salesforce, Oracle, SAP, Cisco, HP or Microsoft supporting multiple verticals. Prior experience with the Healthcare and/or contact center industry is a plus.</li>
<li>Excellent proficiency in oral/written English including executive communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Strong technical/engineering orientation – able to effectively conceptualize and communicate relevant platform synergies.</li>
<li>Confident, articulate self-starter with outstanding presentation and proposal development skills</li>
<li>“Hunter” personality and networking-savvy</li>
<li>Familiar with SaaS business models, proposal development and contracting processes</li>
<li>Team player capable of and comfortable working with a distributed virtual organization</li>
</ul>
<p>This position reports to the VP of Platform Sales based in the Upper Midwest.</p>
<p>
Travel is anticipated to be 40-50% including regular (4-6 week frequency) visits to the Boulder HQ office or other team meetings, and participation within relevant industry events.</p>
<div>
Compensation will include a significant variable component based on attainment of commercial targets and partner satisfaction objectives.</div>
