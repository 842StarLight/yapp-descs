<div>As the leader of the Channel Incubation team for Surface you will be a senior manager and leader within our Surface commercial global sales organization focusing on growing the Surface Channel ecosystem, guiding a team of senior sales professionals and working with in-country channel sales leaders along with key stakeholders in the corporate organization. You will collaborate with and guide our partner development managers and partner organizations, to support building new businesses and practices. Focus areas will include directing and developing your team to build a strong channel, mainstreaming new products through our partners and developing new markets and new channels to market.

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div><div>
<div>
Are you passionate about helping partners build practices around new products and the growing Surface business? Are you eager to lead a team of highly capable partner development professionals? Are you looking to make your own impact in one of Microsoft’s top business priorities and the same time lay foundations for future growth via incubating new products and developing our partners capabilities?<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>As the Surface Channel Incubation Manager, you will be responsible for leading the Channel Incubation team and working with partner development managers to support the growth of our Surface channel ecosystem. Your team of direct reports are located globally covering different Surface markets, so you will be experienced in managing a mature team of sales or channel professionals based remotely. You will lead your team to drive the development of the relationship between Microsoft and our partners, onboarding new products, developing increased capacity, entering new markets, introducing new ways of selling, providing assistance and guidance that drives long term growth. Reporting directly to the Surface Commercial General Manager, you will provide a team environment with diversity and inclusion that allows for the development of subject matter expert for your entire region in assisting our partners adopt and build offerings, business practices around the very newest Surface portfolio devices and business models.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
As a Surface Channel Incubation Manager, you will:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Assign core priorities to the channel incubation team to:</b></div>
<ul><li>Influence top regional partners (time zone wide) in their adoption, sales and business offerings around new Surface products throughout the Microsoft fiscal year, inclusive of the Microsoft Stores ecosystem, resellers and distributors.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure that each of the Channel Incubation Leads has an appropriate portfolio of partners that they will directly manage and engage.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enable your team to assist Partners engaged in large and complex customer activities involving new Surface products, acting as a liaison between Microsoft field sales teams, Surface Engineering and other Microsoft organizations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Integrate your team into product planning and engineering teams to prepare them for product disclosure briefings and guide partners in planning for the adoption and integration of new Surface products into their sales models.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Align the team to local Surface Channel organizations within time zone, to ensure consistent planning and continued alignment between teams</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and roll out new programs into target markets, whilst also setting the long range plan for the introduction of new Surface products into maturing markets, with the intention that all new Surface devices should be part of a partner reseller or distributors mainstream business within 12 months of launch.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain a cohesive framework from incubation phases of concept, planning, deployment, management, mainstream and transition with clear exit criteria aligned with subs including resource management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Act as the lead coaching expert for partner development roles in Surface incubation, as well as aligning with the wider team comprised of Microsoft field sellers and business leaders focused on the maturity of the Surface channel ecosystem</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide support to the subs on net new channel types like Telco, Education as a Channel, AV etc and within 12 months upskill the local PDM’s to manage the new channel types.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Engage with the One Commercial Partner organization and align on partner needs and management of incubation products</li></ul>

<ul><li>Appropriately assign leadership within your team for specific strategic partners, ownership and further development of the Surface channel partners</li></ul>
<ul><li>Be the executive sponsor for strategic Surface partners and those engaged with Surface strategic customers identified within our account lists</li></ul>
<ul><li>Mentor and develop your team, consistently improving skills and enabling them to grow and thrive in their roles.</li></ul>
