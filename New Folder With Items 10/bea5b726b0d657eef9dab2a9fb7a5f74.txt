<h2>Renewals Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div><div>The Customer Success team partners with our customers to ensure they are successfully using our service, adopt it widely and are continually deriving business value from LaunchDarkly. As a Renewals Manager, you will be responsible for planning and executing the renewals lifecycle while driving high customer satisfaction by managing retention and usage goals.</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Develop and execute a renewal strategy to retain and grow our client base</li>
<li>
Proactively manage a portfolio of client renewals identifying risks &amp; ensuring on-time customer retention and high renewal rates</li>
<li>
Maintain and report an accurate forecast of renewals; accurately maintain and update a rolling 120-day forecast of your portfolio and communicate any renewal risk to leadership to develop resolution strategies</li>
<li>
Manage the renewal process with customers, including renewal quotes, renewal opportunities, and order forms; work closely with Customer Success, Finance and Sales teams to ensure all contracts are accurate</li>
<li>
Identify customer dependencies, uncover blocking issues and demonstrate strong account management and commercial capabilities to drive renewal to on-time closure</li>
<li>
Ensure your customers have completed the renewal process prior to the renewal date by confirming paperwork and data to ensure accuracy in SalesForce and closing opportunities</li>
<li>
Discover and identify upsell/cross-sell opportunities upon contract renewal to maximize customer growth</li>
<li>
Verify contracts, data integrity of all quotes, and review terms &amp; conditions to ensure renewal subscriptions in line with company guidelines and policy</li>
<li>
Uncover trends, propose improvements and build processes that improve the renewal workflow</li>
<li>
Occasionally assist Finance with dunning and collections</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who you are:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
3 to 5 years of demonstrated success in a Sales, Operations, Renewals or Account Management</li>
<li>
Strong customer management and negotiation skills</li>
<li>
Ability to organize, prioritize, complete activities and meet deadlines on a daily basis</li>
<li>
Strong process management, financial acumen, and the ability to apply strict policies.</li>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>
Team Player</li>
<li>
Experience with contract language interpretation</li>
<li>
Sales and communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong multi-tasking and prioritization skills</li>
<li>
Attention to detail while working on various renewal opportunities</li>
<li>
Enjoys working independently and is self-directed (they will run their own show, so want to encourage that mindset I think)</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Bonus Points if:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
You have excellent customer management skills</li>
<li>
You have the ability to react and adapt to potential rapid shifts in priorities and organizational policies</li>
<li>
You have a strong track record of leading negotiations</li>
</ul></div>
