<h2>Program Specialist III Contract Specialist 8 hrs Prob Business Office</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div>The Beaverton School District is accepting applications for <b>Program Specialist III - Contracts Specialist</b> at <b>the Business Office </b>for the 2019-20 School Year.<br>
<div>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
The Contracts Specialist acts as the organization’s focal point in the development, negotiation, and maintenance of District contracts. This position is responsible for establishing and maintaining concise and enforceable contract documents to protect the interests of the Beaverton School District. Working with team members from Purchasing, Legal, Risk Management, and Finance, this position ensures that District contracts are in proper form and meet legal guidelines, statutory requirements and Board policy. The Contracts Specialist must be familiar with the various contract types including construction, professional services and general goods and services and be able to use them appropriately on behalf of the District’s schools and departments.</div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<b>Duties/Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<br>
<div>1. Responsible for drafting or reviewing, negotiating, and administrating contracts for a broad array of products and services utilized in the effective operation of all aspects of the Beaverton School District. Contract types include personal/professional services, construction, maintenance services, goods, requirements, intergovernmental agreements, and information technology.</div>
<br>
<div>2. Working with team members from Purchasing, Legal, Risk Management, and Finance, responsible for the development and implementation of consistent District-wide contract procedures and practices, including contract forms and templates.</div>
<br>
<div>3. Manages workflow for him/herself and the Senior Purchasing Specialist as this role has a higher contract approval/execution limit and is an integral part of the timely execution of contracts to ensure smooth and effective continuity of operations.</div>
<br>
<div>4. Working with Legal and Risk Management, serves as the primary contracting resource to all District schools and departments. The specialist provides professional level contracting information and options on a daily basis. The specialist informs and advises District personnel and potential suppliers about the legal requirements of public contracting including but not limited to: pre-qualification, Bureau of Labor and Industries (BOLI) wage rates, State of Oregon certified D/M/W/ESB/SDVBE which are historically underutilized enterprises, as well as State and District requirements concerning bid, performance, and payment bonds.</div>
<br>
<div>5. This position ensures proper contract performance in accordance with the contract terms and helps resolve disputes while providing updates to management and District leadership as needed.</div>
<br>
<div>6. Coordinates contract document development, review and approval by Legal, Risk Management, District Management, and District Board as prescribed by District policies.</div>
<br>
<div>7. Provides excellent customer service and maintains effective business relationships with District departments and departmental staff; he or she acts as a liaison between the contractor and District personnel in some circumstances.</div>
<br>
<div>8. Assists in presenting and training District staff on procurement and contracting procedures, both on a one-on-one basis and in a group setting.</div>
