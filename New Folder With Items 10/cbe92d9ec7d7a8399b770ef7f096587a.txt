<h2>Freelance Photographer - Washington DC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>
RealPage, Inc. is a web-based property management software solutions company that enables multifamily rental properties to manage their marketing, pricing, screening, leasing, accounting, purchasing, utilities and other property operations.</p>
<p><b>
Your Role</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for creative, organized and professional photographers to provide unique content for our site, www.mynewplace.com, one of the nation's largest apartment and home rental websites. Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Scheduling photo sessions with local clients.</li>
<li>
Photographing apartment complexes and amenities, showcasing communities in their best light.</li>
<li>
Upload unedited photographs to our photo team in a timely manner, we'll take care of the rest.</li>
<li>
Opportunities to photograph various cities and neighborhoods, showcasing unique attributes of communities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 2 years of professional photographic experience.</li>
<li>
Must own professional equipment including but not limited to: HDSLR/DSLR, 18 MP+, sharp wide angle lens (10-18 mm on cropped sensor, 16-24 mm of full), strong tripod. Acceptable camera brands, must be professional-level camera bodies with quality lens(es): Cannon, Nikon, Sony, Olympus</li>
<li>
Proficiency in capturing HDR images through bracketing, working with fill flash and ability to properly white balance images.</li>
<li>
Detail oriented and organized. Will be responsible for scheduling appointments with clients.</li>
<li>
Live within driving distance of the job posting location.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assignments are close to home.</li>
<li>
Freelance, flexibility in scheduling your own assignments.</li>
<li>
No post-production work.</li>
<li>
No sales-generation work.</li>
<li>
Build your portfolio and see your work on a high-trafficked website.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
How to get involved</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Send us your resume and a cover letter, explaining why you'd be a good fit for RealPage, Inc.</li>
<li>
REQUIRED Showcase your skills via online portfolio or website. If you do not have one, please upload HDR imagery(PDF of multiple images)</li>
<li>
If you don't have a portfolio, please send a supplemental Mini-Shoot that includes 10-15 unedited bracketed interior and exterior photographs of properties</li>
</ul>
