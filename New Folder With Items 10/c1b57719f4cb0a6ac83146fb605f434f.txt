<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>As a multimedia advertising sales expert, you will work closely with local/regional clients within the Automotive category. Responsible for assessing client's advertising needs and offering creative solutions to help reach their objectives and better engage their customers.<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Proactively prospect, qualify, grow and maintain all sales activities, from lead generation through close in an assigned territory or category</li>
<li>Possess a thorough knowledge of the digital product portfolio (features, benefits, rates, etc.); use knowledge to overcome customer objections</li>
<li>Develop prospecting leads using a variety of sources, including digital, radio, TV and direct mail</li>
<li>Understand how the competition sells their products and services, including rates and specials</li>
<li>Keep records of products sold to customers and look for ways to increase advertiser spend</li>
<li>Create marketing presentations and update as needed</li>
<li>Create proposals while working with the client’s budget and also looking for growth opportunities</li>
<li>Actively pursue and maintain an understanding of new digital trends in the media industry</li>
<li>Analyze and interpret key sales metrics and develop strategies to reach sales success</li>
<li>Work with appropriate departments to create compelling media solutions for advertisers</li>
<li>Work as a leader of the sales and support teams to ensure accuracy and client satisfaction</li>
</ul>
</div>
