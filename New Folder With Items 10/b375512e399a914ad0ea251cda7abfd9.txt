Position Title: CAD Designer 3D Modeler<br>

<br>
The CAD Designer - 3D modeler position will be responsible for working with on-highway truck manufactures and diesel engine manufacturers to assemble 3D assembly models from each manufacturer to check for fit and interference. This position will be required to interface with peers, designers, engineers, and managers to confirm proper assembly of basic components and options for marketing, sales, and manufacturing.<br>
<br>
<b>Requirements</b><br>
<br>
Basic:<br>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of on-highway class 4-8 trucks, diesel engines, and emissions aftertreatment systems and their components.
</li>
<li>Proficiency of Creo and Windchill.
</li>
<li>Able to work with 3D models of parts, sub-assemblies, and large assemblies checking for fit and interference.
</li>
<li>Proficiency of the Microsoft Suite software.</li>
<li>Ability to work with diverse internal team, global customer, and engineering team in a fast paced, goal-oriented environment.</li>
<li>2-year certificate or diploma in 3D modeling program.</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of component and assembly customer design practices.</li>
<li>Ability to read and comprehend instructions, correspondence and memorandums</li>
<li>Ability to write simple technical and informative correspondence</li>
<li>Ability to effectively receive and present information to other employees of the organization at remote locations via phone, skype, and email.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze and effectively problem solve difficult design/drawing issues either independently or collaboratively with customer and/or vendor in a diplomatic manner as necessary</li>
<li>Willingness to ask questions and secure necessary information to insure customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Ability to work efficiently and establish credibility with both customers and coworkers</li>
<li>Ability to demonstrate initiative and set a high standard of quality in the workplace</li>
<li>Self-motivated with the ability to operate independently, work proactively, and to achieve results without close supervision</li>
<li>Ability to be flexible according to workload as established by management</li>
<li>Ability to identify and handle priority and/or sensitive issues with discretion and confidentiality</li>
<br>
</ul>
