<h2>Self Storage Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Exciting and unique opportunity to be part of a new locally owned facility from the ground up. This facility will be built out in several phases. Looking for energetic, enthusiastic and professional person with the ability to work with a diversified client base. This job opening requires a minimum of two years experience as a Self Storage Manager with management and sales experience. Previous lease up experience a plus. </b></p>
<p><b>Skills Assessment will be emailed to you after applying please complete this and submit.</b></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p><b>Sales</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to convert phone and walk-in leads into rentals; understand your sites features and benefits in order to conduct a successful tour and close the sale</li>
<li>Execute lease accurately; clearly explain lease, insurance, other policies and rules to new customer</li>
<li>Know your competition; What do you have to offer that your competition doesn’t? What do reviews say about them, what specials are they offering?</li>
<li>Foster business relationships in the community to promote your facility</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Operations</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand and conduct opening and closing procedures including walk-thru, overlocks, lock walks, vacants, daily reports, posting payments, balance petty cash daily and make daily deposits</li>
<li>Maintain accurate customer records, including the lease, Protection Plan/insurance, change of address, notes, rental increases, letters or receipts</li>
<li>Process late notices, pre-lien, liens and auctions according to state laws</li>
<li>Resolve customer issues in a timely manner; know when to take an issue to the next level</li>
<li>Answer emails and return phone calls</li>
<li>Regularly conduct physical audits of the property taking note of maintenance issues such as painting, burned out lights, spills and similar needs</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with vendors on site for pest control, landscaping and repairs</li>
<li>Keep adequate supply of merchandise, forms, cleaning products and other supplies</li>
<li>Perform light maintenance, clean office, restroom(s), vacant units, golf cart, hallways and property on a daily basis</li>
<li>Process daily invoices, collection notices and make collection calls according to procedures for your site</li>
<li>Monitor security cameras, alarms and understand next steps when alarms go off after hours</li>
<li>Manage, train and motivate those who report to you</li>
<li>Know when and how to use incident reports</li>
<li>Learn and understand the chain of command; know who, what and when to report something</li>
<li>Responsible for making sure your site meets or exceeds cleanliness standards</li>
<li>Be prepared to activate any emergency procedures if necessary</li>
<li>Compile month-end reports or other reports as requested</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Revenue Growth</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage how you are using your specials</li>
<li>Send rental increase notices out in a timely manner</li>
<li>Ability to motivate your team to reach your sites goals each month</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED equivalent</li>
<li>Minimum of two years as a Self Storage Manager with management and sales experience</li>
<li>Clear and effective communication in English, both oral and written</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively on your own or as part of a team</li>
<li>Punctuality and integrity</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task; dealing frequently with both tasks and customers</li>
<li>Proficient skills in Microsoft Suite (Excel, Word, Outlook) and other email programs; the ability to quickly learn storage and related industry software</li>
<li>Basic math skills, to include the ability to add, subtract, multiply, divide and use decimals and percentages</li>
<li>Ability to understand legalities of lease information and to follow self-storage lien laws and company policies and procedures</li>
<li>Self motivated with a positive attitude and the desire to take pride in your work</li>
<li>Willingness to follow directions, work within rules and accept constructive criticism</li>
<li>Know how to use common sense and reason with customers and at the site</li>
<li>Must have valid California driver’s license</li>
<li>Successful completion of background check</li>
<li>Ability to operate golf cart and motor vehicle</li>
<li>Must complete the assessment sent by Indeed to your email after applying</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands: </b></p>
<p>Physical demands arerepresentative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully performthe essential functions of this job.</p>
<ul>
<li>Able to hear and speak clearly; answer phones and answer customer questions.</li>
<li>Ability to see; must be able to monitor security cameras, inspect property, verify customer ID, read computer screen, inventory auction items, merchandise inventory, verify work performed by vendors. Specific abilities include depth perception, peripheral vision, close and distance vision and the ability to adjust focus.</li>
<li>Use of hands to fingers, and arms are required for many of the tasks stated in this description in addition to filing paperwork, emptying trash, use tools to perform maintenance tasks, drive the golf cart, use the computer and office equipment and open storage doors.</li>
<li>Able to squat, bend, stand for up to 7-9 hours per day, lift and carry items weighing 40 lbs, climb stairs, open and close roll-up doors, hallway and office doors.</li>
<li>Able to walk to any point on the facility.</li>
</ul>
