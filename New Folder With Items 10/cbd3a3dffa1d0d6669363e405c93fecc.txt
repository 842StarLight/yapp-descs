<h2>Archer Senior Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Archer Senior Consultant</p>
<p>
Overland Park, Kansas, US</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides technical and consultative leadership for solutions opportunities on a range of complex engagements.</li>
<li>
Delivery of technical architectural design, strategies and plans for engagements.</li>
<li>
Technical expert in leading pre-sales meeting or presentation.</li>
<li>
Able to assist in creating proposals that address current and evolving client requirements.</li>
<li>
Participates in installation, customization, and integration of an Dell solution.</li>
<li>
Works on complex projects and manages multiple work streams. Defines and authors deliverables.</li>
<li>
Delivers projects with approved methodology while adhering to margin, planning and SOW requirements.</li>
<li>
Ability to supervise staff and plan/assign work on project engagements. Leads project quality assurance activities, including technical QA reviews.</li>
<li>
Understands and adheres to proper escalation and change control procedures.</li>
<li>
Validates requirements, limited prototyping and functionality design and creates proposals that address current and evolving client requirements.</li>
<li>
May provide testing process, code reviews, certification of software, and issue resolution as point of contact for technical questions.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Essential Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years’ experience</li>
<li>
Understands that travel may be required for extended periods of time.</li>
<li>
Understands customer business challenges and provides strategy that addresses long term goals.</li>
<li>
Independently analyzes large amounts of data, provide logical options, create and delivery correct documentation, sound work products and deliverable to achieve customer satisfaction.</li>
</ul>
