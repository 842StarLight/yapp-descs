<h2>Teller II, Concierge</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>No prior banking experience required!</b></p>
<p>
With a history known for stability, growth and success, Consumers Credit Union (CCU) is glad that you are interested in considering a career opportunity with us!</p>
<p><b>
What you'll do in this role:</b></p>
<p>
As a <b>Concierge Teller II</b>, you will become a master working in a hybrid role! Not only will you provide outstanding service as a Teller, but you will also be the face of the branch! Our Concierge Tellers act as the liaison between our customers and our digital bankers or digital tellers, who are waiting anxiously from our Corporate Office to assist them through our Digital Banker Offices or our Interactive Teller Machines. It's a busy job and we have lots of customers, but don't worry! You'll have a partner Concierge Teller I, so you can focus on customers who use the Digital Banker Offices.</p>
<p><b>
This work includes:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Greeting customers as they enter the branch, determining their needs, directing them accordingly, and consistently representing the credit union in a professional manner.</li>
<li>
Completing transactions (including deposits, withdrawals, payments, monetary purchases, etc.) with a high regard to accuracy and detail.</li>
<li>
Cross-selling CCU products/services in order to meet established goals/expectations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you'll need to join the team:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Education equivalent to a H.S. Diploma</li>
<li>
At least 1 year of similar experience (things like retail, service, sales, and cash handling)</li>
<li>
Must be comfortable engaging in sales oriented conversations, upselling, and promoting products/services</li>
<li>
Must be proficient with computer systems/software applications</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal, communication, and organization skills and a genuine approach to helping others is necessary</li>
<li>
Flexibility to work 40+ hours/week between the following hours:<ul><li>
Mon - Thurs 7:30am-6:30pm; Fri 7:30am - 7:30pm; Sat 7:30am-2:30pm</li></ul>
</li>
</ul>
