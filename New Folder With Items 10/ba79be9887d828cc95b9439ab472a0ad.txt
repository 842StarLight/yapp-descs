<p><b>Fuel Dispatcher</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>This position reports to the Dispatch Supervisor/manager of Logistics &amp; Distribution
 </p>
<p></p>
<p>JOB OBJECTIVE: The Dispatcher is the primary contact between the customers and the Company. The emphasis of this position is ensuring that customerâ€™s needs are met in a prompt and professional manner.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Supervising drivers, driver safety, and customer service.
</li>
<li>Responsible for monitoring fuel inventory at customer locations.
</li>
<li>Dispatch drivers to replenish fuel inventory at customer locations.
</li>
<li>Review and complete the order process, prepare schedules for all deliveries.
</li>
<li>Responsible for implementing Federal, State and local rules and regulations.
</li>
<li>Monitor fuel usage and make independent decisions in regards when and where to deliveries, including decisions regarding diverting loads, canceling deliveries and adding deliveries as needed based on market conditions.
</li>
<li>Ensure the overall customer experience and service exceeds Company standards.
</li>
<li>Plan and assign deliveries to maximize productivity, profitability and customer service.
</li>
<li>Anticipate, identify, prioritize and resolve/eliminate problems through effective delivery, planning and scheduling.
</li>
<li>Forecast and project fueling needs/deliveries based on recent or prior sales.
</li>
<li>Performs load planning activities and coordinates load assignments.
</li>
<li>Handle administrative paperwork and computer entry duties.
</li>
</ul>
