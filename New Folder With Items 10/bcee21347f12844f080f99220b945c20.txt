<h2>Account Executive - New Markets</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Localist is a first of its kind Event CMS for Marketing and HR teams. Our team thinks big, dreams big and delivers big. This is an excellent opportunity to join a high energy company with a dynamic team culture, at just the right time.</p>
<p>
Localist is looking for a high performing Account Executive that would feel at home on a top tier commercial team. Strong candidates are those that are the best at what they do and want to work with the best; they are top performers who can help us build the next great software company.
</p>
<p>It will be your responsibility to promote Localist’s Event CMS to Marketing and HR teams—which means you have a solid understanding of how to speak to HR and Marketing decision-makers at large companies. In order to be successful, you must be confident, creative and curious.
</p>
<p>Here’s what the job looks like:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the new business sales cycle with HR and Marketing executives at companies with annual revenues of $20-million+
</li>
<li>Consultative, with a Challenger-based sales approach</li>
<li>Work with a dedicated Business Development Associate as they prospect through an account-based territory and manage leads</li>
<li>Use Salesforce.com to track and manage pipeline and forecasts accurately</li>
<li>Consistent execution of Localist’s internal sales methodology and process</li>
</ul>
<p>And here’s how to know if we’ll be a good fit:</p>
<ul>
<li>You have grown to become a top performer (top 10%) with every company you have sold for</li>
<li>You’re self-motivated and feel empowered in a dynamic, fast-paced environment</li>
<li>You’re committed to our culture of positivity and high performance and you know how to contribute to and build upon it.
</li>
<li>You value feedback and thrive on constant improvement and learning</li>
<li>You can be engaging, persuasive, and effective in a sales conversation</li>
<li>You have 5 or more years of inside sales experience selling software via web demos to mid-market/enterprise companies</li>
<li>You have a deep understanding of the full sales process life cycle (from prospecting to close)
</li>
<li>You can use technology to prioritize and manage the sales process</li>
<li>You can work beyond your sales role to help our marketing and product teams refine messaging and product features (i.e. create sales/marketing alignment)</li>
</ul>
