<h2>Regional Director - US - East</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">SonicWall has been fighting the cybercriminal industry for over 26 years, defending small- and medium-sized businesses and enterprises worldwide. Backed by research from SonicWall Capture Labs and the formidable resources of over 26,000 loyal channel partners around the globe, our award-winning, real-time breach detection and prevention solutions secure more than a million business and mobile networks and their emails, applications and data.
<br><br>
Ready to do your part in the cyber arms race? SonicWall offers fun, high-energy work environments at the leading edge of technology, networking and cybersecurity. Whether you're in sales, marketing, engineering, product management, technical support or finance, you'll have real opportunities to help protect more than 500,000 organizations across the globe. If you're ready for a career that can impact change — and not just another job — join one of committed and enthusiastic SonicWall teams in offices around the world.

The mission of the Regional Sales Director (Central/Southeast Region) is lead a team of Territory Account Managers in the Central &amp; Southeast Region. He/She will be responsible to drive top line revenue growth through the delivery of comprehensive security solutions through our channel partners and direct touch organizations. The sales leader will be the driving force with a solid business development mindset along with possessing entrepreneurial type skills with a relentless passion that will help lead a team to expand and grow revenue. The leader will be expected to operate across functional lines to identify and execute on new growth opportunities through a blend of partner development, strategic partnerships, high-touch sales, and creative marketing.
