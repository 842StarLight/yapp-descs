<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Point B Communications, a boutique branding and advertising agency located in Chicago’s West Loop, is searching for an account executive to join its growing account team.</p>
<p><b>We are… </b></p>
<p>Point B is an established branding &amp; digital agency with over 40 years of experience. We were born a branding agency and to this day, branding remains our core competency—the place where we thrive and help our clients thrive as well. We believe the ultimate success of a product or service always comes back to the brand, although once our branding work is done, we’re just as keen to bring that brand to market with smart strategies and seamless execution. With that said, we don’t take ourselves too seriously. There’s always a hot espresso at hand, cold beer in the fridge, quarters in the arcade game, and plenty of like-minded teammates to keep things interesting.</p>
<p><b>You are…</b></p>
<p>The link between client and agency, and as such you need to speak both languages. You are responsible for keeping a lot of balls in the air, making sure projects get delivered on time and on budget while staying in close communication with the creative team and keeping the client happy.</p>
<p>You serve as the client’s voice when developing and executing advertising campaigns. You ensure the efforts of Point B team members align with the client's goals. You are responsible for developing new client relationships and maintaining existing ones.</p>
<p>If you think you can excel at all this, we’d love to hear from you.</p>
<p><b>Things you’ll need to do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meet and liaise with clients to discuss and identify their advertising requirements</li>
<li>Work with agency colleagues to devise advertising that meets the client's brief and budget</li>
<li>Present campaign ideas and budgets to clients</li>
<li>Work to brief media and creative staff and assist with the formulation of marketing strategies</li>
<li>Liaise with, and act as the link between, the client and the agency by maintaining regular contact with both</li>
<li>Ensure that communication flows effectively</li>
<li>Handle budgets, manage campaign costs and invoice clients</li>
<li>Write client reports</li>
<li>Monitor the effectiveness of campaigns</li>
<li>Undertake administrative tasks</li>
<li>Arrange and attend meetings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Marketing, Advertising, Public Relations, or a related field</li>
<li>Minimum three years of agency experience</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>High level of organization</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
<li>Strong work ethic</li>
<li>High competency level on computer and software skills compatible with job responsibilities</li>
</ul>
