<h2>Marketing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Title:</b> Marketing Associate</p>
<p><b>
Department:</b> New Homes</p>
<p><b>
Reports To:</b> Vice President</p>
<p><b>
FLSA Status:</b> Full Time Non-Exempt</p>
<p><b>
Location:</b> Springfield, PA
</p>
<p><b>Summary:</b> McKee Builders is seeking a talented and energetic individual to fill the role as Marketing Associate at its corporate office in Springfield, PA. The Marketing Associate will play a vital role in supporting the New Homes department and the Company as a whole.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage in conjunction with Vice President, OSC &amp; Ad agency Facebook, Houzz, Pinterest, Instagram, YouTube, Bright MLS and all online directories including but not limited to Zillow, BDX, New Home Source, 55places.com</li>
<li>
Send new advertising opportunities to the ad agency
</li>
<li>Create, Send, Gather and analyze E-blasts and reports to assist with creating and sending e-blasts that will have higher open and CTR</li>
<li>
Attend weekly marketing meetings</li>
<li>
Sporadically Travel to new homes sites to take pictures and videos of houses under production, sales meetings, giving tours of homes and homes under construction, and features highlighted by construction managers</li>
<li>
Enroll for various webinars/conferences to further knowledge
</li>
<li>Google bubble Maps where sales/prospects come from for all communities
</li>
<li>Scout Billboards</li>
<li>
Assist with transportation/delivery of sales and marketing signs from a vendor to specific sites</li>
<li>
Manage Call Rail numbers in conjunction with OSC</li>
<li>
Assist VP with reviewing various collateral for accuracy including; blogs, e-blasts, brochure inserts, website, social media posts, billboards, etc.</li>
<li>
Attend Quarterly roundtable meetings offsite</li>
<li>
Copywriting for e-blasts, blogs, PR in conjunction with ad agency</li>
<li>
Review McKee website for functionality and accuracy on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>
Dig into source reporting clarify and communicate with on-site sales and break outsources by product for communities with multiple products</li>
<li>
Ability to edit pictures minor auto affects etc. for social media, product, and events to put online especially for Quick Deliveries and making sure all pics look good not crooked, etc.</li>
<li>
Review Google Analytics with VP</li>
<li>
Assist with the small wrap up details on models and clubhouses shopping for TVs, setting up about McKee videos on model TVs, appliances and creature comforts for clubhouse</li>
</ul>
