<h2>Towers Project Manager</h2>

<p><b>About Hangar</b></p>
<p>
Hangar is a leading drone software and analytics company. We help customers digitize their operations. We scan constructions sites, mines, solar fields, telecom infrastructure – even architectural heritage. Our flight app is the most powerful drone mission planner in the world today, allowing users to effortlessly perform complex flights and accurately scan even the most challenging terrain or structures. Our analytics platform helps clients run their businesses more smoothly, providing a seamless, unified portal to view digital reconstructions of their sites and to access detailed component analysis powered by pioneering machine learning and artificial intelligence.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
About the role</b></p>
<p>
Hangar has launched a new product focused on using drones to survey cell phone towers. This is a fast growing, global market. Our clients often sign Hangar to provide both software and services, using Hangar to manage the logistics of sending technicians or external subcontractors to all of their thousands of cell towers. You will join the Hangar team and take a pivotal role in handling the operations for these clients. Your mission will be to ensure that clients and their workforce and their subcontractors can successfully scan hundreds of cell towers each week. You will help scale and manage the national and global roll-out of this product, working with clients, third party drone subcontractors, and all of our internal stakeholders.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Oversee end-to-end operational processes for all tower business, ensuring client projects are being completed on time, working with sales, operations, support, and product stakeholders along with external partners</li>
<li>
Manage internal stakeholders in operations against OKRs, including site acceptance review, post production, support, data QA</li>
<li>
Manage external partners as part of end-to-end operations and hold them accountable to defined requirements / SLAs; working specifically with drone service provider partners, and data processing partners that have been integrated into the end-to-end solution</li>
<li>
Act as the main POC with clients and partners on operational related requests and troubleshooting any issues blocking smooth operations, pulling in key stakeholders as required, and quickly bubbling up and following through with urgent issues</li>
<li>
Communicate delays to appropriate stakeholders, specifically to account manager</li>
<li>
Execute against defined national roll-out plan together with all stakeholders</li>
<li>
Identify and suggest improvements in operational processes, systems, product</li>
<li>
Keep key internal stakeholders updated on operations</li>
<li>
Work with sales account manager on setting realistic expectations and timelines on new projects</li>
<li>
Work with product managers and operations team on seamless roll-out of new features into the existing system and process</li>
<li>
Coordinate, organize and run regional on-boarding events relating to pilot on-boarding and training</li>
<li>
Suggest necessary adjustments to resources to remain on-schedule</li>
<li>
Track issues and ensure fixes are implemented</li>
<li>
Communicate status and timeline to Customer Account Manager</li>
</ul>
