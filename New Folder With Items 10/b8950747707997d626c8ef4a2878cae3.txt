<div>SUMMARY:</div>
<div>The Senior Dual Skill Designer position develops innovative, cost effective, visually appealing and structurally sound point of point of purchase displays and merchandising solutions utilizing the dual skill set of both structural and graphic design. They have a very high aptitude for overall project management and are able to lead a team effectively.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Interact with Account Executives and customers to ascertain design needs and creative objectives</li>
<li>
Perform Graphic Designer duties, as required, including developing projects using standard techniques: ideation/concept development (sketching and/or digital), 3D modeling/rendering (Strata, Form Z, etc.), graphic layout/messaging (Adobe), 2D visual capabilities, pre-press art verification (image corrections, proofing, production ready print files), produce comps</li>
<li>
Prepare graphic design files for proofing, mock-up, and final output; coordinate with customer's graphics department as needed</li>
<li>
Perform Structural Designer duties, as required, including conveying concepts with quick sketches (fast and fluid), and CAD (Artios) visualization; producing samples and mock ups using various techniques and skills such as Drafting (2D/3D), model making, various knife tools and cutting tables (Konsberg/DataTech Tables); creating promotional materials and developing Bill of Material (BOM), POG, Specifications, Palletization, Cape-Tops, ect.</li>
<li>
Responsible for project development record keeping, file naming/part numbering, utilizing project management system</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with Design Manager, Sales personnel and other designers and offices to develop customer programs; participate in or lead project review meetings with Sales, Project management and other designers</li>
<li>
Develop presentation material and assist with the presentation of abstract ideas, as well as formal presentations in a variety of settings (one-on-one, small and large groups to include peers, supervisors and clients); manage presentation process as needed</li>
<li>
Perform Technical Illustration tasks as required i.e., Plan-o-grams, Assembly Instructions, Retail Assembly Instructions, Digital Photography, Illustrator and Photoshop work</li>
<li>
Effectively prepare and present ideas, concepts and presentations to customers</li>
<li>
High-level understanding of overall project management and project flow from concept creation to production and retail execution</li>
<li>
Work collaborative with project management personnel to manage accounts and projects</li>
<li>
Travel, as required, for customer support, training, meetings, and manufacturing and assembly plant support</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
