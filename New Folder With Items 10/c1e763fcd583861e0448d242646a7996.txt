<h2>National Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Blendtec is an awesome place to work. See why!<br>
<br>
Blendtec is a growing global health company, a leader in marketing premium blenders and kitchen innovations to over 80 countries around the world. Blendtec is the first and only company to offer a commercial grade, safe, reliable high speed blender with the quietest, but most powerful motor on the market.<br>
<br>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
Our company requires a National Sales Manager with strong interpersonal and communication skills, a focus on organization, and enhanced multitasking abilities. You will be mainly responsible for building and maintaining strong relationships with key accounts. B2B sales, marketing and strategic analysis are important aspects of the position, along with exceptional negotiation skills and the ability to close deals. The ideal candidate has experience researching market trends, targeting relationships and using proven methods to develop our business strategy while retaining marketing partners.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develops and implements strategic sales plans to accommodate corporate goals.</li>
<li>
Directs sales forecasting activities and sets performance goals accordingly.</li>
<li>
Reviews market analyses to determine customer needs, price schedules, and discount rates.</li>
<li>
Implements, trains, and performs evaluations to develop and control sales programs.</li>
<li>
Advises dealers, distributors, and clients concerning sales and advertising techniques.</li>
<li>
Represents company at trade association meetings to promote product.</li>
<li>
Delivers sales presentations to key clients.</li>
<li>
Coordinates liaison between sales department and other sales related units.</li>
<li>
Analyzes expenditures of division to conform to budgetary requirements.</li>
<li>
Assists other departments within organization to prepare manuals and technical publications.</li>
<li>
Prepares sales report showing sales volume, potential sales, and areas of proposed client base expansion.</li>
<li>
Monitors and evaluates the activities and products of the competition.</li>
<li>
Frequent Domestic Travel</li>
<li>
Possible International Travel</li>
<li>
Passport required</li>
</ul>
