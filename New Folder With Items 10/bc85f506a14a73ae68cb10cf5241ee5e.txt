<p><b>Job Title: Call Center Analyst</b></p>
<p><b>Department: Member Service
</b></p>
<p><b>Classification: Exempt</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>The Call Center Analyst, under the direction and in collaboration with Altura’s Member Service leadership team, performs call center related analysis designed to maintain and improve operational performance of the call center and staff. The Call Center Analyst administers critical communication software applications, designs and optimizes work flows and provides insights and recommendations to improve service quality, sales, workforce productivity and efficiency.</p>
