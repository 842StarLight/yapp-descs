<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
As an integral part of the Lerner Residential team, the Leasing Consultant performs exemplary customer service activities at our communities. The incumbent is a sales professional who is able to identify and address the needs of potential residents and close the sale. The position does not have supervisory rights, and works under the direct supervision of the Property Manager.
</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities*</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Greet prospective residents professionally and build a rapport with them</li>
<li>Answer inquiries from prospective residents; use standard sales and marketing techniques to encourage a visit to the community. Ensure prospect data is entered timely in our database to comply with Fair Housing laws</li>
<li>Complete prospect follow-up in accordance with Lerner Residential follow-up expectations and record such activities in property management system for compliance with Fair Housing laws</li>
<li>Meet with prospective residents and check the availability of units that best fit their desires and budget; present apartments and the overall community to prospective residents; explain other amenities of living at the community; use standard sales closing methods to sell the prospective resident on available units</li>
<li>Assist assigned communities in gathering information from prospective residents after they have indicated a desire to move to the property</li>
<li>Assist assigned communities with completing market surveys and to include gathering current occupancy, rental rates and concessions
</li>
<li>Assist with marketing efforts to promote the properties</li>
<li>Maintain a positive and professional customer service attitude</li>
<li>Attend training seminars for leasing consultants to improve marketing and sales techniques</li>
</ul>
<p>Excellent attendance is required to perform the essential functions of this position. *Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of the job, as described above.</p>
