<h2>Salon Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Are you interested in experiencing the excitement of one of the hottest new segments of the beauty industry?
<br><br>
Does being part of a start-up business committed to delivering an outstanding client and employee experience appeal to you?
<br><br>
Are you excited about joining an organization that is more like a family than a business?
<br><br>
If so, then we are looking for you! THE LASH LOUNGE® at Chimney Rock in Bound Brook, NJ, is looking for a dynamic and motivated Salon Manager. We are a national franchise that is new to New Jersey. Our salon will offer a luxury experience specializing in eyelash extensions. We are committed to creating an inviting atmosphere that will WOW our clients with our service and the confidence that their beautiful new eyelashes will give them.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will be highly motivated, enjoy multi-tasking, possess excellent client service skills, have exceptional attention to detail, and be very comfortable with technology. This candidate must also have strong leadership skills and be able to provide coaching and professional development to other salon staff and will be responsible for the daily operations of the salon including business performance and the client experience.
<br><br>
Previous salon or spa management experience is preferred, but not required, for this position.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities Include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Scheduling the staff, including stylists and other front desk associates, to ensure proper salon coverage.</li>
<li>Managing the appointment scheduling for clients and ensuring incoming and outgoing calls are handled with a “Gold Standard” customer service level.</li>
<li>Managing the tracking and inputting of client records into the salon software with correct client lead source and promotional codes.</li>
<li>Managing an organized work area including the preparation and filing of the client charts, intake forms, consultation forms, after care hand-outs, package purchase agreements, and all other forms as needed.</li>
<li>Becoming an expert working and training on the POS software, the sales transactions of clients for services and retail items, and managing the end of day cash drawer reconciliation.</li>
<li>Resolving issues such as scheduling conflicts or mishaps, product returns, customer complaints, and general salon maintenance to keep everything running and looking its best.</li>
<li>Ensuring the salon's appearance is always top-notch and gives our clients and employees an outstanding environment appropriate for a luxury salon.</li>
<li>Educating clients on all services along with retail products’ key benefits.</li>
<li>Assisting with promotional and marketing events to help build the brand awareness of the salon.</li>
</ul>
Position is available immediately. We look forward to hearing from you.</div>
