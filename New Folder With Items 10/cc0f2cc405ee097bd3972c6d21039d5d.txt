<h2>Senior Field Product Manager, Public Sector Readiness: Security & Compliance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role:</b></h2>
<p>
Join us as we pursue our disruptive new vision to make machine data accessible, usable and valuable to everyone. We are a company filled with people who are passionate about our product and seek to deliver the best experience for our customers and partners. At Splunk, we’re committed to our work, customers, having fun and most importantly, to each other’s success.</p>
<p>
We are looking for a Senior Field Product Manager, Public Sector Readiness: Security &amp; Compliance who will drive expansion of Splunk products in regulated environments across the public and private sector.</p>
<p>
Field Product Managers are responsible for acting as an extension of the PM team but work closely with the field. They bring input to product owners to prioritize the right set of features for Splunk customers</p>
<p>
In this role, you’ll be embedded in the field and bring the input back to the Splunk product teams. Using this input, you will help improve the alignment of products and services with standards and frameworks required for conducting business with Public Sector customers.</p>
<p>
Along the way, you’ll get to wrestle with hard technical problems, work with developers and architects, writers, and a team of PMs who love technology.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Do you understand intricacies behind acronyms like FIPS 140-2, Common Criteria, FISMA, 508, GDPR, FedRAMP, etc?</li>
<li>
Do you know how infrastructure software has to evolve and change and integrate to meet the requirements behind those standards &amp; frameworks?</li>
<li>
Do you have a working knowledge of data, logging and analytics technologies and understand how they fit together to deliver concrete business value to regulated customers?</li>
<li>
Have you learned the lessons that only come with the experience of working with auditors to get products, solutions, or services accredited or certified?</li>
</ul>
<p>
If so, you might be well suited for this role.</p>
<p>
Preferred location This position can be based anywhere in the US but the Washington, DC Metro area is preferred</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<p>
You’ll prioritize regulatory environment requirements to expand Splunk's presence. You will collaborate with product teams across Splunk, a group of architects and engineers, and most significantly, with customers to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Identify a variety of highly regulated customer needs for the platform and architecture</li>
<li>
Prioritize customer certifications/compliance needs and requirements and collaborate with internal teams (PMs, Product Security, Cloud Services &amp; Ops, Internal Audit etc.) to ensure they are prioritized</li>
<li>
Maintain a compliance/certifications roadmap along with estimated release dates</li>
<li>
Develop &amp; execute plans to obtain &amp; maintain necessary government certifications/compliance (Section 508, CC, FedRAMP, FIPS etc.)</li>
<li>
Collaborate in the product feature planning process; ensure that the product set meets the requirements of all regulated customers who will deploy/use it, whether they are IT professionals or procurement departments.</li>
<li>
Understand the market and competition for use case support as a means of driving adoption of Splunk technology.</li>
<li>
Support planning, execution and go-to-market</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirements</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Extraordinarily strong analytical abilities and a natural predisposition toward data-based decision-making. You should nevertheless be able to make reasoned decisions in the face of uncertainty or imperfect data.</li>
<li>
Ability and prior experience working closely with sales teams and solutions architects is a requirement</li>
<li>
Understanding of compliance and certifications requirements including FedRAMP, FIPS, FISMA etc.</li>
<li>
Technical background and/or IT Architecture/Administration experience, MBA is a plus.</li>
<li>
If you’re a deep Splunk administration authority with exposure to federal environments, passionate about product management - this is a phenomenal opportunity for you</li>
<li>
Excellent written and interpersonal skills characterized by transparency and brevity</li>
<li>
Ability to influence people at all levels across a broad variety of job functions</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational skills to juggle many tasks</li>
<li>
Dynamic and engaging speaker comfortable in front of audiences of all sizes</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize projects and efforts according to business needs</li>
</ul>
