<b>
Job Description</b><p></p>
<p>
The Sales Analyst will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Developing and maintaining sales management tools</li>
<li>Assisting with the migration of sales management tools to automated solutions</li>
<li>Performing rigorous analysis of sales performance data</li>
<li>Deriving insights from data and analysis to advise sales leaders on recommendations</li>
<li>Documenting, mapping, and assisting with improvements to processes and systems</li>
<li>Assisting with the concept, design, pilot, maintenance, and automation of sales management tools</li>
<li>Developing analytical frameworks and reporting that can quickly and accurately synthesize large datasets</li>
<li>Performing other related duties as required</li>
</ul>

<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<b>Desired Skills</b><p></p>
