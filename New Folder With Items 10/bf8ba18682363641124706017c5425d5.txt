<h2>Associate Director of Development - MFH Aldersgate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>
Under the direction of the Executive Director for the Foundation, the Associate Director of Development will be responsible for Special Events, Donor Recognition and Stewardship for Methodist Family Health Foundation. This will encompass special events, promoting/maintaining the recognition societies, and stewardship plans for all Foundation donors.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Manage the Employee Giving Campaign</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Manage the Christmas Campaign.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Manage Bright Night event.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Manage Southern Silks event.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Manage the Donor Recognition and Stewardship Program.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Assist with Get Up and Give.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Assist with public speaking to churches, civic groups, etc.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Perform other related duties as assigned by the Executive Director of the Foundation.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
Requires Three (3) years’ experience in Fundraising or Sales.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Require a bachelor’s degree.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Personal integrity, character, and a strong sense of ethics</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Ability to deal effectively with a multitude of donor personalities</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Excellent verbal, writing, and organizational skills</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
An affinity for serving the public good</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
High energy level</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Deadline and detail oriented</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Knowledge of computers and donor software programs such as Raisers’ Edge.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Willingness and ability to travel</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Must be physically capable to receive verbal and written directions. Must be physically capable of sitting and standing for several hours at a time.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Must have good auditory, visual and olfactory ability. Ability to use hands and fingers to handle or feel objects, tools or controls.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Must be able to maintain effective audio, visual discrimination and perception needed for making observations, communicating with others, reading and writing, and operating office equipment and other treatment equipment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Must be able to use a telephone to communicate verbally and a computer to communicate through written means, to review information and enter/retrieve data, to see and read characters on a computer screen, chart or other treatment items.</p>

<p></p>
<p>
Must be willing and able to work with all patients of Methodist Family Health.</p>

<p></p>

Flu shot is mandatory and required for all positions (subject only to qualified exemptions).</p>
