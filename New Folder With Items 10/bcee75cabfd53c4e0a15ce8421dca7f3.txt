<h2>Sr. Manufacturing Process Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div></div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Senior Process Engineer at our Hickory, NC location supports Plant Manufacturing and Technical Operations while maintaining a strong focus on safety, flawless launch, continuous process and equipment improvement and optimization. Applies knowledge of principles, theories and concepts of acceptable industry standards and practices to design, develop, upgrade and optimize processes, methodologies, equipment, and facilities in processing or manufacturing products that meet customer expectations on quality, cost and delivery.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop, update, document and communicates manufacturing processes and technical parameters that meet product specifications and supports company goals on safety, quality, cost and delivery.</li>
<li>Evaluate and optimize processes and equipment to generate cost savings, and improve quality, efficiency and productivity</li>
<li>Prepare and validate various engineering deliverables such as manufacturing development plans, work instructions, process data sheets, equipment selection, process review and analysis reports, change management documentation, and capital improvement projects in support of operational excellence</li>
<li>Develop new processes, research emerging technologies, and execute characterization and optimization studies of current processes using design of experiments, root cause analysis, troubleshooting and decision making tools</li>
<li>24/7 support of manufacturing lines to include identifying and resolving equipment/material/training/process issues, and coordinating activities of maintenance, quality, production and other resources to execute cost effective solutions
</li>
<li>Perform product testing and measurement as it pertains to process development or improvement.</li>
<li>
Analyze test data, interpret results, formulate conclusions, and help define path forward.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assist with design and implementation of new lab or in-process test equipment.</li>
<li>Insure compliance with ISO-9001 Quality System regarding R&amp;D, Testing.</li>
<li>Provide subject matter expertise to other departments and technical mentorship to the engineering team</li>
<li>Oversee production scale-up of developmental products and train operators on all manufacturing phases and in-process control procedures.</li>
<li>Write or modify Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) when transferring new or modified technology to production.</li>
<li>Recommend safety practices with respect to material handling, processing, environmental impact, waste, etc.</li>
<li>Participate on Fiber-Line Safety Committee.
</li>
<li>Responsible for providing technical support to the sales team when applicable.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
