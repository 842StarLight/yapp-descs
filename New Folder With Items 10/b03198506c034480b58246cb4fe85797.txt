<h2>Marketing Manager/Jewelry Sales Team Member</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Marketing Manager/Jewelry Sales Team Member</p>
<p>Hamilton Hill Jewelry seeks marketing professional to create and implement its marketing plan working closely with the business owner and division managers while also being part of sales team.</p>
<p>Hamilton Hill is an independently owned, internationally respected, full-service fine jewelry boutique founded in 2001 located in historic downtown Durham, NC.</p>
<p><b>Skills, Talents, Interests Important for Success</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in digital, print, video, radio marketing strategy and execution</li>
<li>Passion for design, fashion and contemporary fine jewelry</li>
<li>Enthusiasm for sales and service</li>
<li>Experience and facility with point-of-sale databases, Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, word processing, spreadsheets</li>
<li>Talent for business and promotional writing. Excellent penmanship. Excellent spelling and grammar</li>
<li>Ability to think “on your feet” and creatively manage complex situations</li>
<li>Ability to follow instructions while being a self-starter</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Digital Marketing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan and execute all digital marketing, including SEO/SEM, email, social media and display advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Design, build and maintain social media presence</li>
<li>Measure, analyze and report the performance of all digital marketing campaigns and assess against goals. Recommend updates and refinements to strategic plan, continuous improvement and execution</li>
<li>Identify trends and insights and brainstorm new and creative growth strategies</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Website Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor performance of the website and check that all newly added inventory (POS) appears correctly</li>
<li>Periodically update homepage slider images, designer images/text, special events list, store hours, services, etc.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Print/Radio Advertising</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop annual advertising plan and maintain calendar</li>
<li>Produce print-ready ads</li>
<li>Draft and/or edit radio copy</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Printed Materials</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create brochures, flyers, postcards, and invitations</li>
<li>Produce in-store signage</li>
<li>Maintain stationery supply</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Video and photography for marketing purposes</b></p>
<ul><li>Participate as needed in writing text, staging scenes, etc., in conjunction with videographer. Coordinate clothing, make-up, hair as needed</li></ul>
<p><b>Product photography</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with designers to obtain high-res product photos and other marketing images</li>
<li>Crop, re-size and upload files to HH server</li>
<li>Photograph jewelry as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Community/PR</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Explore opportunities to build HH brand through donations, sponsorships, and business partnerships</li>
<li>Increase brand awareness through HH events (such as trunk shows) and by offering the store as an event venue for other organizations</li>
<li>Manage the process of loaning jewelry to influencers, brides, media, etc. Ensure optimization of social media opportunities associated with loans</li>
<li>Develop and maintain relationships with community and associations (e.g. Discover Durham, Downtown Durham Inc.)</li>
<li>Raise visibility of Hamilton Hill Jewelry by delivering brochures to hotels, new developments, salons, other hot spots</li>
<li>Create opportunities for public speaking, interviews, and media coverage</li>
<li>Attend Triangle Wedding Connections (FB group of wedding professionals) monthly meetings</li>
<li>Actively participate in minimum of one professional and one civic/non-profit organization</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Sales (non-commission): </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Jewelry sales in person and by phone/email/text from greetings through closing to follow up</li>
<li>Cultivate current and prospective clients</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Store/Office</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Participate in jewelry display set-up and break-down and store tidying</li>
<li>Answer phones as needed</li>
<li>Greet/assist customers and delivery people as needed</li>
<li>Assist fellow team members as needed, recognizing the needs of the team over those of the individual</li>
<li>Serve as host along with other team members when visiting designers/vendors/industry partners are on site</li>
<li>Perform periodic software updates (e.g., POS, Windows), troubleshoot with software or hardware providers in advance of calling IT professional</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other duties as requested and deemed appropriate by the owner and teammates</b></p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Fine Jewelry Sales: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Luxury Brand Marketing: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Marketing: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional Compensation:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bonuses</li>
<li>Store Discounts</li>
</ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>

<ul><li>Yes</li></ul>
