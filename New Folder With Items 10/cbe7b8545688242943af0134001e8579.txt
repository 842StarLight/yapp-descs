<h2>Client Services Representative - Oncology</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Sema4 is a next-generation health information company, spun out of the Mount Sinai Health System, that provides advanced genomic testing and merges big data analytics with clinical diagnostics. Our team creates practical tools that help patients, clinicians, and researchers better diagnose, treat, and prevent disease. We are striving to construct a more comprehensive picture of health by integrating three key elements: a wealth of clinical experience that informs the answers that patients and providers are seeking, the world-class academic research that illuminates new directions, and the pioneering information science that puts all the pieces together.</p>

<p>Operating independently, the <b>Client Services Representative</b>, reporting to the <b>Manager, Client</b> <b>Services</b>, will be accountable for end to end execution and strategic planning of client needs and solutions. Responsibilities include leading the development and execution of program strategy in collaboration with key team members and functional leadership, collection of documentation from external institutions and coordination of transfer of tissue samples, from end to end.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p><b>Program Management:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate with healthcare providers and patients each day through phone calls and email to coordinate sample procurement and order clarification.</li>
<li>Constant interaction, coordination and communication with laboratory directors, accessioning team members and the operations team.</li>
<li>Perform quality review and inspection of required materials and documents needed to submit to internal and external institutions, for release to clinicians and oncologists.</li>
<li>Partner with internal laboratory departments to ensure completeness and timeliness of all ordered testing.
</li>
<li>Develops, maintains and facilitates daily, weekly and quarterly reporting regarding specimen acquisition progress and process gap analysis.</li>
<li>Using provided guidelines, capture relevant clinical information and patient history from provided medical reports and clinical documentation, and enter it into our LIS system (Medgis).</li>
<li>Develops and maintains a collaborative working relationship with all internal and related contacts, as needed, including physicians, teammates and the healthcare team members involved in the care of the patient.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Initiatives/Process Improvements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead continuous improvement initiatives, related to oncology testing, that increases the quality of deliverables to the client and value to the client and Sema4.</li>
<li>Work with the Sales Directors to evolve and implement policies and processes.
</li>
<li>Partner across the organization to implement best practices across project teams, e.g., resource allocation, risk management, accountability, timeline development and client and/or partner management.</li>
<li>Serve as a key subject matter expert internally for the oncology testing offering of Sema4.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skill Sets and Knowledge:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills, including very good writing and proofreading capabilities.</li>
<li>Attention to detail, eye for quality and excellent organizational skills.</li>
<li>High degree of proficiency and learning agility as it relates to databases and software applications, as well as, use of Microsoft Office programs.</li>
<li>Familiarity with medical terminology. A related degree, highly preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrated problem solving skills with a mindset towards solutions, is required.</li>
<li>High level of interpersonal skills in order to handle sensitive and confidential situations.</li>
<li>Analytical ability is required to gather and summarize data for reports, find solutions to various administrative problems and to prioritize work.</li>
<li>Must be able to work on a team as well as be able to work independently, and to be self-motivated.</li>
</ul>
