About the job…..<br>
<br>
As a Property Manager at Pinnacle, you’ll put your outstanding leadership and savvy business skills to work at one of the most respected apartment companies in a management opportunity that offers real leadership, innovation and support.<br>
<br>
Our Property Managers are the cornerstone of our team. They are responsible for keeping our communities in the top-notch condition our residents have come to expect, building motivated and trustworthy teams who consistently deliver a notably higher level of service and maximizing the operating performance of our community. Be ready to be busy!</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Requirements</b></h2>
<div>Essential Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Operations. Ensuring the smooth running of our community in a fast-paced environment. Overseeing all operations including maintenance, capital improvements, lease administration, budgeting, forecasting, reporting, collections, evictions, vacancy anticipation, marketing, lease renewals, service contracts, expense control, audits, etc.</li>
<li>Customer service. Providing superior customer service and communication to our residents and prospects to enhance customer satisfaction and increase renewals, revenue, reputation and profitability.</li>
<li>People development. Developing, mentoring, leading, and managing a high-performing, cohesive team, including leasing, customer service, maintenance and management personnel, in order to maximize their engagement and minimize turnover.</li>
<li>Marketing. Driving revenues with your thorough understanding and analysis of competition and development of creative marketing programs.</li>
<li>Leading by example. Instilling, maintaining and modeling the Pinnacle mission to be the best national management company.</li>
<li>Supervise day-to-day operations of entire on-site team, ensuring that all Pinnacle policies and procedures are being followed.</li>
<li>Maintain effective on-site staff through interviewing, hiring, and terminating as necessary.</li>
<li>Maintain a positive living environment for community residents through prompt conflict resolution and consistent follow-up.</li>
<li>Manage and maintain all aspects of overall community budget and finances</li>
<li>Work with leasing staff to ensure that leasing/marketing goals are being met.</li>
<li>Maintain positive relations with all community vendors.</li>
<li>Coordinate special projects as requested by Regional Property Manager.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Personal Competencies:<br>
<ul>
<li>A competitive spirit</li>
<li>High-energy</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership and strategic thinking skills</li>

<li>Warm, friendly and service-oriented philosophy</li>
<li>High degree of flexibility and tolerance for change</li>
<li>Ability to train, develop, lead and mentor</li>
<li>Superior written and verbal communications skills</li>
<li>Extremely computer literate</li>
<li>Organized and detail-oriented</li>
<li>Customer-service driven</li>
<li>Able to multitask</li>
<li>Financials experience/experience working with a budget</li>
</ul>

Qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of a high school diploma, Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>3 years of on-site property management experience</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Experience in supervisory role and managing staff</li>
<li>Experience in writing and maintaining budgets</li>
<li>Proficient in Yardi property management software or other similar property management software.</li>
<li>General office, bookkeeping and sales skills</li>
<li>Computer literate, including Microsoft Office Suite</li>
</ul>
