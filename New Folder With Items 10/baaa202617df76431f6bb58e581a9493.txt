<h2>Commercial Lines - Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Customer Service Representative
<br><br>
Currently seeking a Commercial lines Customer Service Representative for an exciting opportunity within the P&amp;C division of a US insurance brokerage agency in Worcester, Ma. Offices are throughout New England, but this position would report to the Worcester office of Marsh Kemp Insurance. The ideal candidate will have 5-7 years of experience in Commercial lines Insurance.
<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Phone calls and office visitors requesting quotes, binders, changes to existing coverage and/or new policies. Determine acceptability and placement, complete application or endorsement and follow up within 30 days. All requests for Certificates of Insurance are to be completed by end of business day.</li>
<li>All incoming polices are to be reviewed, the computer updated, invoiced and scanned. Request endorsement if necessary. Audits, (i.e. setting appointments, negotiating disputes, billing issues) between client and company.</li>
<li>Inspection/compliance requests.</li>
<li>Receive all claims. Follow up on outstanding claims and provide assistance in their resolution, as necessary.</li>
<li>Billing inquires from clients and companies.</li>
<li>Review all terminations and cancellations to determine action to be taken. All documents are to be scanned and named.</li>
</ul>
Actively solicit increases in limits and/or coverages or rounding out accounts through sales to clients
<br><ul>
<li>Document through client notes all material conversations with insureds and/or carriers.</li>
<li>Process all transactions required for Registry of Motor Vehicles (i.e. RMV-1, RMV-3,plate returns etc.)</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Must have a valid Massachusetts Property and Casualty producer/agents license.
<br>Proficient with Applied TAM Online 2018, agency automation system and proficiency in various software programs, including but not limited to Microsoft Word and Excel. Continuing education classes are required.
<br><ul>
<li>Benefits offered: We offer a competitive benefits package: Medical, Dental, Vision, LTD, STD, Life Insurance, 401k, 17 days of PTO, 7 paid holidays.</li>
</ul>
