<h2>Channel Marketing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a member of Vistaprint's Channel Analytics Team, you will perform detailed analyses of advertising activities to optimize Marketing ROI while maintaining a great experience for our target audiences. This detail oriented professional will work closely with marketers to understand business needs, translate business needs into an actionable measurement plan and present associated findings and recommendations.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
What you’ll do all day:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Data Exploration, Visualization, and Dashboarding</b>. Identify trends and drivers, perform exploratory data analysis, and present views that will provide visibility into business performance.</li>
<li>
<b>
Testing and Measurement.</b> Design and execute sophisticated methodologies (ex. A/B and multivariate tests) to optimize the customer experience and maximize sales, then analyze the results to provide insights and recommendations to the business.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Data Analysis and Modeling.</b> Incorporate advertising data, transactional history, and website activity into analyses and statistical models to drive efficient marketing spend, improve the customer experience, acquire valuable customers, and drive repeat behavior.</p>
<p>
You’ll be measured on how well you:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Drive insights.</b> Solve challenging business problems, work through the data, and find the meaningful insights that will help improve our business.</li>
<li>
<b>
Partner and communicate.</b> Work closely with marketers, your team, and other project stakeholders to frame a learning agenda and communicate actionable findings.</li>
<li>
<b>
Deliver results.</b> Utilize strong organizational skills and attention to detail, learn new tools and techniques, and effectively manage projects supporting multiple business initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<p>
 We’re looking for someone who:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Is strongly orientated towards data and technology.</b> You are proficient in programming including experience with SQL, R, or Python with expert skills in Microsoft Excel.</li>
<li>
<b>
Is passionate about data.</b> You are comfortable with exploratory analysis and statistical techniques, and are driven to find meaningful patterns and trends in the data. Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau is a plus.</li>
<li>
<b>
Is a resourceful and driven problem solver.</b> You enjoy and have demonstrated the ability to work through complex problems, draw inferences from data, and use your skills and business acumen to identify solutions.</li>
<li>
<b>
Is consultative.</b> You know how to come up with valid conclusions and recommendations based on data. You have excellent organizational skills, strong attention to detail and ability to effectively manage projects supporting multiple business partners.</li>
<li>
<b>
Is experienced and technical. </b>You have 3-5 years of experience within Analytics and a bachelor’s in Economics, Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Engineering, or another relevant field. Master’s degree is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Here are a few examples of work we’ve done in the past:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Select the ideal location for a retail store pilot. </b>Chose the right neighborhood to open Vistaprint’s first retail store in Canada by analyzing purchase trends, customer density, and small business activity in the market.</li>
<li>
<b>
Identify drivers of customer lifetime value.</b> Leveraged internal and external data sources to understand what makes a newly acquired customer valuable.</li>
<li>
<b>
Refine new customer offers.</b> Collaborated with Product and Pricing marketers and analysts to test new customer acquisition offers and identify which ones are most successful in attracting high quality customers to the business.</li>
</ul>
<p>
And a few reasons you might love it:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
You'll grow and learn a lot.</b> You’ll be exposed to a wide range of projects in our growing company, work with amazingly talented colleagues, and tackle a diverse set of problems.</li>
<li>
<b>
You’ll have the opportunity to make a difference. </b>You’ll be working on projects that have real impact on decisions – we are a very data-driven company.</li>
<li>
<b>
You’ll love your team and colleagues.</b> You’ll work with people who are as energetic, smart, and driven as you are. Our Analytics organization is a global team of talented professionals with different specializations and backgrounds. We can’t wait to meet you!</li>
</ul>
