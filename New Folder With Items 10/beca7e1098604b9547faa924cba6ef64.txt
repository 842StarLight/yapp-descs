<h2>Digital Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Jeffers is expanding the digital marketing team! If you enjoy working as a team, being creative, learning about e-commerce and love animals, Jeffers has an exciting opportunity for you! Prior experience is preferred, but not required.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop strategies to drive online traffic and track conversion rates. Manage digital marketing campaigns utilizing paid search, SEO, and PPC.</li>
<li>Paid Advertising Campaigns (PPC): Developing and managing PPC campaigns such as Google AdWords, Bing Ads, and Facebook.</li>
<li>Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Maintain and improve onsite content as well as link building.</li>
<li>Content Management: Regularly create and update editorial content: blog, onsite content, and social media updates.</li>
<li>Monitor and evaluate search results and search performance across the major search channels using Google Analytics and BrightEdge.</li>
<li>Develop and execute e-commerce promotions calendar, working cross-functionally with Sales, Marketing, Design, and Operations.</li>
<li>A desire to continue learning and growing</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS AND ABILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Basic knowledge of HTML and CSS</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills in English</li>
<li>Ability to communicate information clearly and accurately</li>
<li>Ability to manage priorities and workflow</li>
<li>Versatility, flexibility, and a willingness to work within constantly changing priorities with enthusiasm.</li>
<li>Acute attention to detail</li>
<li>Team player who works productively with wide range of people</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree, preferably Marketing, Public Relations, Business, or Computer Science</li>
<li>or 2+ years of relevant work experience</li>
<li>A passion for creativity, numbers, and analytics.</li>
</ul>
