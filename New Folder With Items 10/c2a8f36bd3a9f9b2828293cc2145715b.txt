<h2>Transportation Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Title</b></p>
<p>Transportation Supervisor</p>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<div><div>
<div>Accountable for distribution activities and fleet maintenance. Effectively leads direct reports and works collaboratively with the Sales function to ensure that product is delivered in a safe, efficient, cost-effective manner that meets or exceeds customer expectations and Kemps budgetary requirements.</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates and schedules deliveries to meet and exceed customer expectations while optimizing loads and minimizing work hours. Communicates with dispatchers and other necessary parties regularly to give them clear direction and discuss any product shortages, customer issues, etc.
</li>
<li>Arranges and monitors trailer load size to ensure trailers are utilized in the most efficient and cost-effective manner.
</li>
<li>Effectively manages and analyzes the distribution budget and proactively seeks ways to reduce costs through continuous improvement of distribution operations.
</li>
<li>Works as a partner and coach with route drivers to manage their performance. Provides on-going performance feedback. Helps people stay focused on priorities and follows up to ensure success.
</li>
<li>Maintains a positive working relationship with the Sales function. Actively shares information with and seeks information from Sales Supervisors. Works to resolve conflicts that may arise between the Sales and Distribution functions in order to provide service to customers that is both high quality and cost-effective.
</li>
<li>Compiles and analyzes distribution and fleet information to generate appropriate reports for the Company, insurance providers, and government entities.</li>
<li>Supervises route delivery drivers to ensure distribution activities are performed in a timely and accurate manner. Establishes and holds people accountable for policies and procedures that ensure safe operations. Investigates and reports all accidents and injuries appropriately to maintain a safe work environment and comply with state and federal laws.</li>
<li>Manages payroll administration, hiring, discipline and termination of DSD or route personnel.</li>
<li>Manages driver DOT files for applicable personnel. Notifies employee of physical exams or other DOT requirements to ensure no lapse in qualifications. Submits request to appropriate agency for updating of moving violations and related information.</li>
<li>Assists in promoting and actively participates in the Company's safety program. Investigates and reports all accidents/injury incidents to appropriate personnel and to the insurance company to ensure employee safety and to comply with external reporting requirements.</li>
<li>Assists in ride-a-longs with drivers to ensure efficiency and customer service. Makes recommendations based on route observations.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p><b><i>Depending on the business needs of the branch location, the Transportation Supervisor may perform the following duties:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates the operation and maintenance of vehicles used for the delivery of product. Monitors fleet tractor maintenance in order to avoid equipment failures and resulting work delays.
</li>
<li>Monitors refrigeration equipment to include, but not limited to, School Coolers and Milk Dispensers</li>
<li>Coordinates the activities of hired haulers. May proactively negotiate freight rates.</li>
<li>Enters exception timecard data for union or non-union drivers and outside haulers into the Kronos database system. Contacts individual drivers to make any necessary adjustments.</li>
<li>Trains and supports drivers on the use of technologies including eRMS and PEOPLENET.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as determined by business needs.</li>
</ul>
