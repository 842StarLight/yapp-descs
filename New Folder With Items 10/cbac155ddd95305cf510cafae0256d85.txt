<h2>Business Analyst</h2>

<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Retirement &amp; Personal Wealth Solutions (RPWS) serves the core financial needs of individual clients and plan participants by providing best in class, integrated products in the most intuitive, efficient and client friendly way while maintaining a forward thinking mindset and risk management focus. RPWS is responsible for both the institutional and personal retirement capability set to include 401(k), Equity and Health Benefit plans for companies of all sizes as well as college savings, personal retirement planning, retirement income, insurance and estate planning for individuals. RPWS also provides tools and content that enables advisors and bankers to help their client’s live their best financial lives.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Description:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Business Analyst within the Retirement and Personal Wealth Solutions (RPWS) organization drives business performance reporting, research and analytics for the institutional segment across ERISA and non-ERISA investment solutions. In this role you will partner with many areas of Bank of America including but not limited to Merrill, Private Bank, Chief Investment Office and Investment Solutions Groups to quantify the institutional consulting business and drive business development activities. In addition to working across the enterprise, the Analyst will gain experience across multiple products and platforms such as ML One, Investment Consulting and Outsourced CIO for Defined Benefit, Defined Contribution and Philanthropic solutions.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
In addition to institutional business analytics, you will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Assisting Global Institutional Consulting (GIC) Advisors and Sales Specialists with prospects and clients</li>
<li>
Identifying and helping to develop new GIC Advisors</li>
<li>
Managing multiple strategic projects such as targeted sales campaigns</li>
<li>
Working with external industry key stakeholders such as Barron’s for Institutional Consultant Advisor rankings, Pension &amp; Investments and others to create awareness and strengthen the GIC brand</li>
<li>
Creating thought leadership and identifying business development and management activities</li>
<li>
Collaborating closely with RPWS Sales, Finance, Product, Governance and Marketing partners</li>
<li>
Creating presentations / business plans and presenting to senior leaders</li>
<li>
Managing and reviewing sales pipeline using Salesforce.com</li>
</ul>
<p>
Posting and maintaining publications on intranet site</p>
<p></p>
