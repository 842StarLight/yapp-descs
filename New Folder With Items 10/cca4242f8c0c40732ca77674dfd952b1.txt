<h2>Customer Success Manager - US</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>As a Customer Success Manager with CentriLogic Inc. you will ultimately be responsible for ensuring that our customers receive the highest level of customer satisfaction. You will be the liaison between our clients and the company. Reporting into the Chief Revenue Officer initially and then to the head of sales - US, this role will be based out of our Rochester, NY location.</p>
<p>
Candidate should have experience managing existing relationships, always display the highest integrity, and be fully capable to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal requirement for task driven supervision. This position is for a team member who has a proven record of excellent written and verbal communication skills, experience in IT and technology industry, and is a self-starter and problem solver.</p>
<p>
 <b>Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and become a trusted advisor to your portfolio of accounts including general support, contract renewals, retention and overall client satisfaction</li>
<li>Responsible for developing and managing account objectives, strategies and detailed account plan, ensuring all client tracking systems are kept up to date</li>
<li>Conduct regular client touch points via phone, email and in person meetings to review new product/service offerings, key activities, service performance, opportunities for improvement and upcoming business opportunities</li>
<li>Manage contract renewals/up-sells and work closely with the Sales team to identify cross-sell opportunities.</li>
<li>Work together with the assigned AE to further develop the customer relationship while providing strategic recommendations and best practices</li>
<li>Collaborate with the technical support teams on “who does what, when and how” before and during incident management. This includes proactively managing client communications and expectations</li>
<li>Be the internal voice for the customer within the company</li>
<li>A successful Customer Success Manager team member will demonstrate our core values of accountability, client service excellence, and innovation with a proactive approach to improving our business</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Experience &amp; Skills
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years of experience in Information Technology, Data Centers, IT managed services, Hosting and/or Software Development working closely with clients</li>
<li>College or university degree</li>
<li>Demonstrated passion for client service coupled with general business acumen</li>
<li>Excellent presentation, written, organizational, prioritization, time management, analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>A proactive, flexible and dependable team player that thrives in a fast-paced, dynamic working environment</li>
<li>Ability to demonstrate a high level of understanding and skill in dealing with complex client needs with a strong ability to influence and maintaining a positive client experience</li>
<li>Highly accountable with strong negotiation skills and the demonstrated ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships, both internally and externally</li>
<li>Experience with SalesForce and project coordination/management would be an asset
</li> </ul>
<p><b>About CentriLogic</b></p>
<p>
CentriLogic is a global provider of managed IT solutions. With a comprehensive portfolio of IT infrastructure, private connectivity, and application services, CentriLogic works with clients to develop adaptable and dependable IT solutions that enable them to achieve positive business outcomes. The company’s dedication to a client-centric philosophy has earned it the trust of some of the world’s most innovative companies and visionaries. With regional headquarters in Canada, USA, and the United Kingdom, CentriLogic delivers solutions to clients throughout North America, Europe, and Asia.</p>
