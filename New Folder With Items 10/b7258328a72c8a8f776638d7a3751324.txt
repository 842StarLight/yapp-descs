<p><b>The Role
 </b></p>
<p>We are seeking an experienced Account Executive to join our growing Middle Enterprise team. In this role you will focus on increasing market share in Middle Enterprise accounts in an assigned region. By joining our already successful team, you will be an instrumental part of our consistent year over year revenue growth!
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your day-to-day
 </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Actively engage with prospective new customers via phone, web conference and in-person to introduce Proofpoint products and our value proposition
</li>
<li>Consistently research and prospect within your assigned territory accounts to identify new potential Proofpoint customers in the Mid-Market segment
</li>
<li>Drive the entire sales process from start to finish including prospecting, opportunity identification, introductory meetings, product demonstration, customer Q&amp;A, as well as negotiation and closing
</li>
<li>Team up with our rapidly expanding channel partner network to drive both net-new and incremental revenue
</li>
<li>Interact with the management team in 1-1, team meetings and via forecasting and reporting to provide critical updates, and strategic account planning
</li>
<li>Become a perpetual student of Proofpoint products, and the ever changing CyberSecurity industry!
</li>
<li>East Coast Territory-expected to work territory hours
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you bring to the team
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 3+ years of experience selling complex enterprise class software
</li>
<li>Experience selling to either Mid-Market or Enterprise level accounts strongly preferred
</li>
<li>Track record of sustained success/over-achievement in a sales role
</li>
<li>Experience managing the entire sales cycle, from prospecting through negotiation and closing
</li>
<li>Strong phone and interpersonal communication skills (verbal and written) as well as organizational skills
</li>
<li>Great attitude, and a team player that will share their recipe(s) for success
</li>
<li>Experience working with Salesforce.com or other CRM is a plus
</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree or equivalent experience required
</li>
</ul>
