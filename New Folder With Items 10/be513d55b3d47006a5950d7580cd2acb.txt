About the role:</b></div>
<div>
We are seeking an Enterprise Account Executive to become a critical part of our long term growth and success. You will focus on increasing sales for Cobalt Robotics within a targeted region. This will be accomplished through scheduled appointments with qualified prospects and your own networking activities. You will be an instrumental part of our consistent year-over-year revenue growth and success.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you’ll do:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Work closely with our sales development manager to actively identify and create sales opportunities with prospective new customers to introduce Cobalt Robotics’ value proposition</li>
<li>
Drive the entire sales process from qualified prospect to closed sale including lead qualification, introductory meetings, product demonstrations, site visit and use case development, engineering collaboration, proposal and contracting</li>
<li>
Team up with our channel partner network to drive new sales and revenue</li>
<li>
Provide forecasting and reporting updates to the management team
</li>
<li>Responsible for strategic account planning
</li>
<li>Become a subject matter expert in Cobalt Robotics and physical security assessment</li>
<li>
Some travel to trade shows and conferences</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
