<h2>Solution Architect, Technical Publications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>
EAC seeks a Solution Architect to lead the definition and delivery of technical publication solutions to its customers.</p>
<p>
The Solution Architect’s role is to strategically design and deploy business solutions that support customer needs and achieve customer goals. The Solution Architect understands business challenges, translates them into requirements, defines an approach, and leads the project team to successfully implement the solution. Someone in this role works with business and technology teams to ensure alignment and success for customers, and also provides pre-sales and business development support. The Solution Architect provides technical leadership and guidance to customers as a subject matter expert, and provides internal leadership to project and cross-functional teams within EAC.</p>
<p>
EAC Product Development Solutions (EAC) is a complete source for product development. We provide a full suite of product development software, and offer consulting and training services to support every phase of product development. EAC is ready to offer our brightest ideas to ensure you enjoy the best solutions in the industry.</p>
<p><b>
Job functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Understand customer goals and requirements and provide technical leadership.</li>
<li>
Design solution architecture and system integrations.</li>
<li>
Design data migration procedures, tools, and strategies.</li>
<li>
Lead customer projects and project teams.</li>
<li>
Implement customer projects and solutions.</li>
<li>
Provide support to sales and business development teams.</li>
<li>
Provide technical leadership to customers.</li>
<li>
Provide leadership on cross-functional teams.</li>
</ul>
</div>
