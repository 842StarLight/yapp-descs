<p><b>Our Digital Advertising and Marketing Technology Company is</b> <b>adding to our team due to unprecedented growth! This entry-level position is great for new college grads entering the workforce, or those looking to pivot into digital marketing. </b></p>
<p>For the past two years, we have been named as Top 100 Fastest-Growing Private Companies in Washington. and Inc. 5000 Fastest-Growing Private Companies in the U.S.</p>
<p>This unprecedented growth is creating enormous opportunities for the right candidate to join our team. Client Services Coordinators have the ability to continuously add to their skills and knowledge base while handling new campaign launches, account management, asset design and creation, reporting, marketing analytics and more.</p>
<p>The Client Services Coordinator works directly within the team of other high-performing service specialists creating internal systems and programs, as well as marketing and communication strategies associated with our Live Chat and social media departments designed to grow the business at a scalable pace while ensuring five-star service programs remain the focus for our clients, colleagues, and stakeholders.</p>
<p>The day-to-day work-life of a CSC is always changing as you will have the opportunity to manage your own accounts, become a specialist in numerous marketing platforms, and assist in all areas of our rapidly growing organization. We are constantly adding new tools to increase our authority as an advertising specialist and expect the CSC to assist in the growth of this area.</p>
<p>Our culture works hard to create an environment that is open to hearing new ideas on how we can improve our processes while giving you the freedom to work creatively and make significant contributions to our organization.</p>
<p>Please fill out this quick survey in addition to your application. It helps us assess your fit within our organization: https://www.cultureindex.com/c/eQ9t1hz7s1</p>
<p>Responsibilities of a CSC include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Creating systems that ensure a seamless client experience from initial launch to final billing.</li>
<li>Creating a flow of information between Sales Team, Client, and Operations team that ensures maximum productivity and the highest level of client satisfaction.</li>
<li>Working directly with Sales Team on all new-client launches managing the customer experience.</li>
<li>Developing a workflow strategy that allows our Operations Team to meet and exceed deadlines on behalf of the Client. Creating and implementing Social Media content Strategies that are combined with digital content to market our agency.</li>
<li>Developing coherent marketing materials combined with direct advertising schedules to assist Sales in new market development.</li>
<li>Becoming trained on all Advertising systems including becoming Google Adwords certified and certified on all Display Advertising platforms.</li>
<li>Working with design and operations team on website tactics and launch of new products.</li>
<li>Working with on-site chat team in the areas of customer service and client retention.</li>
<li>Assisting the Sales Team with client reports and behind-the-scenes work on all accounts giving them the freedom to seek out new business.</li>
</ul>
