<h2>Inside Sales, Key Account Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
Gordian's Inside Sales - Key Account Representative, is a strategic role with responsibilities that include building trustful relationships, understanding customer needs, and the expansion of existing customers product portfolio. This role is ideal for someone with experience selling SaaS based solutions in both the public and private sector.
<br>For nearly 70 years, RSMeans data, North America’s leading source of construction cost estimating information, has given owners, developers, architects, engineers, contractors and others the tools to project and control the cost of both new building construction and renovation projects.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
New and existing customer acquisition
<br>Proactive outbound prospecting
<br>Effectively manage customers in assigned territory
<br>Respond to inquiries with a “customer first” attitude
<br>Position the right product and services in a timely manner
<br>Effectively articulate the RSMeans Data value proposition, determine requirements and expectations, recommend the appropriate solution and emphasize solution features and benefits.
<br>Pipeline management and effective utilization of CRM
<br>Perform at a level that exceeds all activity and sales metrics
<br>Consistently achieve sales quota on a monthly, quarterly and annual basis
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Bachelor’s Degree
<br>Enterprise sales experience
<br>Strong communication skills both verbal and written.
<br>Proven track record
<br>Possess a demonstrated ability to understand and explain complex solutions and services
<br>Awareness of the sales process with the ability to understand and interpret customer needs to identify sales opportunities
<br>Proficient in MS Office Suite and prior experience using CRM solutions and sales prospecting technologies.
<br>Travel up to 10%
<br>Fortive is a diversified industrial growth company comprised of Professional Instrumentation and Industrial Technologies businesses that are recognized leaders in attractive markets. With 2017 revenues of $6.7 billion, Fortive’s well-known brands hold leading positions in field instrumentation, transportation, sensing, product realization, automation and specialty, and franchise distribution.

Our team grows by tackling challenges that accelerate progress and further their careers. With a culture rooted in continuous improvement, the core of our company’s operating model is the Fortive Business System. For more information please visit: www.fortive.com.

Gordian is the world’s leading provider of facility and construction cost data, software and services for all phases of the building lifecycle. A pioneer of Job Order Contracting (JOC), Gordian’s offerings also include our proprietary RSMeans data and Sightlines Facility Intelligence solutions. From planning to design, procurement, construction and operations, Gordian’s solutions help clients maximize efficiency, optimize cost savings and increase building quality. Gordian is a part of the international business group Fortive, with 26,000 people employed worldwide.
