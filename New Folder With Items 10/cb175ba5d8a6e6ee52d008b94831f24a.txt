<h2>Global Market Development Manager – Digital Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>When you’re part of the team at Thermo Fisher Scientific, you’ll do important work, like helping customers in finding cures for cancer, protecting the environment or making sure our food is safe. Your work will have real-world impact, and you’ll be supported in achieving your career goals.<br>
<br>
<b>How will you make an impact?</b><br>
<br>
The Global Market Development Manager, Digital Marketing for Molecular Biology will drive the development and execution of digital marketing programs for a team of global market development managers. Reporting to the Sr. Manager of Digital Strategy, this person will join a team of Digital Marketing and Operations professionals that define and manage ongoing global marketing programs and support regional programs for Thermo Fisher Scientific Molecular Biology brands including Invitrogen, Applied Biosystems, and Thermo Scientific.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What will you do?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Own and drive a Search Engine Marketing (SEM) strategy for specific product portfolios within the Molecular Biology business, to maximize performance indicators including ROAS and Lead Generation</li>
<li>Develop and manage a program for 3rd party industry advertising opportunities to maximize the quality of traffic from the partners, to drive revenue, and generate market qualified leads for our sales team</li>
<li>Partner with division and group resources to drive comprehensive web page improvement efforts to maximize conversion, improve page performance, improve SEO, and to improve the customer’s online experience</li>
<li>Support the development and drive adoption of advanced web analytics by partnering with Market Development Managers and Divisional and Group resources to implement tactical/diagnostic-level reports that identify tangible actions towards improvement</li>
<li>Work with the team to do comprehensive scheduled reviews of the business unit’s key campaigns, and propose and drive formalized solutions to improve campaign performance with each scheduled review</li>
<li>Coordinate with global functions to ensure consistency with brand profiles and company standards.</li>
<li>This position interacts with varying levels of internal and external personnel and will require direct customer communication, public presentation, and a team-focused approach.</li>
<li>Work closely with regional marketing development managers to build and execute marketing programs within their regions.</li>
<li>May perform other related duties as required and/or assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
How will you get there?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience successfully managing digital marketing programs in a corporate environment</li>
<li>2+ years of web analytics experience using Adobe Analytics (preferred) or Google Analytics</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills as well as analytical, project management and planning skills are necessary.</li>
<li>Strong business acumen and customer focus are essential.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, digital marketing, marketing communications, or related analytical fields of study</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 15%.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>
Desired Qualifications:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Working familiarity with Adobe Ad Cloud, Google Ads, or other digital advertising platforms</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to optimize pages for search engines (SEO)</li>
<li>Proficiency in managing large scale paid search programs, with complex campaign and regional structures</li>
</ul>
