<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary</b></h4>
<p>
As the leader of naviHealth’s health plan business development team, you’ll play a critical role in leading all aspects of the organization’s growth strategy with prospective and current health plan clients including vision, strategy, process, management, pricing and contracting. The ideal candidate will have a deep understanding of the health plan market and proven success in management and execution of growth, strategic partnerships, sales, and deal execution.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>
Become an expert in naviHealth’s technologies and solutions, and use this knowledge to inform business development efforts</li>
<li>
Develop long-term relationships with prospects and clients built on credibility, trust, and deep understanding of health plans’ strategic priorities and focus areas</li>
<li>
Lead all aspects of growth for our health plan line of business, including:<ul>
<li>
Driving solution-specific sales strategy: sales process, pipeline management, customer segmentation, pricing, contracting, and execution</li>
<li>
Understanding naviHealth’s dynamic marketplace and evolving, innovative portfolio of solutions</li>
<li>
Generating leads, coordinating, and leading sales meetings with prospects</li>
<li>
Coordinating technology demonstrations and clinical model reviews with prospects</li>
<li>
Executing complex, enterprise solution sales</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Lead a team of Directors that are assigned to maintain the desired level of performance and recognizes and rewards appropriate continuous improvement as it relates to behaviors of the marketing team.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with various departments with naviHealth to execute deals and drive growth, including Analytics, Clinical, Operations, etc.</li>
<li>
Partner with the business development support team to execute necessary deliverables including development of PowerPoint presentations, sales materials, and Excel models to support sales efforts</li>
<li>
Attend industry conferences, manage a network of health plan contacts, and publicly represent naviHealth to the health plan industry</li>
<li>
Understand the competitive landscape and key market dynamics, informing sales approach and strategy around emerging trends</li>
<li>
Perform other duties and responsibilities as required, assigned, or requested</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in business, healthcare or related field; Graduate degree preferred</li>
<li>
A minimum of 6+ years’ experience in sales, business development, corporate development, strategy, or similar roles. Experience working for a top-tier consulting firm or high-growth healthcare services or technology company desired.</li>
<li>
Strong executive presence, excellent people management and influencing skills</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal, communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Experience creating professional and compelling PowerPoint presentations</li>
<li>
Ability to review and understand financial models</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask, prioritize, adapt to change, and work well under pressure in an entrepreneurial environment</li>
</ul>

<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About naviHealth</b></h4>
<p>
Being a pioneer in post-acute management and care transitions with 19 years of experience, naviHealth is uniquely positioned to manage patients, improve clinical and financial outcomes, and share risk with payors and providers. We provide clinical service support alongside proprietary technology and advisory solutions that empower health systems, health plans and post-acute providers to navigate care episodes across the continuum, with the goal of reducing waste and improving patient outcomes.</p>
