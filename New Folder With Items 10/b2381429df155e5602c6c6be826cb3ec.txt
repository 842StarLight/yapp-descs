<p><b><i>Job Summary:</i></b></p>
<p>We are looking for an energetic Outside Sales Trainee to join our team. This opportunity is a great way to establish a career in Technology Sales! In this role you will engage with prospective customers, cold call into target customer accounts by using creative techniques, work closely with marketing and proactively follow leads and referrals. A big part of this role is providing support to the Outside Sales Representatives and scheduling new client presentations. Position could lead to an Outside Sales role within 9-12 months!</p>

<p><b><i>Job Responsibilities:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and utilize account management tools and contact target accounts through cold calls and e-mail</li>
<li>Set up meetings and calls between prospective clients and Account Executives to identify new business opportunities</li>
<li>Build long-term trusting relationships with clients</li>
<li>Be familiar with new pricing and payment plans</li>
<li>Qualify leads from marketing campaigns as sales opportunities</li>
<li>Handle customer objections to close sales</li>
<li>This description applies to new Outside Sales Trainees for their first six months in this position</li>
<li>Bonus eligible as described in the Outside Sales Trainee Bonus guidelines</li>
</ul>
