<h2>Email Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Join the team shaping the future of healthcare. MedBridge is looking for a highly motivated Email Marketing Coordinator with a proven track record of creating effective marketing campaigns. Our email program is fundamental to the success of our marketing and growth of MedBridge.</p>
<p>
In this role you will be responsible for the successful execution of multiple email programs; tasks include coding HTML email campaigns, building landing pages, sending campaigns, growing lists, employing tracking and reporting to drive continuous improvements and scalability of the system.</p>
<p>
Email Creation/Lead Generation</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage email campaigns including audience segmentation, coding, and deployment</li>
<li>
Code within our current email templates while continuously making improvements to design</li>
<li>
Produce both broadcast and triggered emails as assigned</li>
<li>
Create content emails and carry out proper review processes</li>
<li>
Build new marketing landing pages for lead capture and conversion</li>
<li>
Responsible for efforts to grow and maintain email lists</li>
</ul>
<p>
Email Best Practice Management/Reporting</p>
<ul>
<li>
Monitor email deliverability and address any potential issues</li>
<li>
Research current trends and suggest improvements to elevate existing campaigns</li>
<li>
Carryout A/B testing and reporting to continuously make improvements to the program</li>
<li>
Track email performance and report on KPIs</li>
<li>
Provide ad hoc reports on channel activity as requested by key stakeholders</li>
</ul>
<p>
About You:</p>
<p>
Required</p>
<ul>
<li>
1-2 years of experience in email marketing or coding HTML emails</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of HTML, CSS, and common email-related bugs</li>
<li>
Experience deploying emails from an email service provider (i.e. MailChimp, Active Campaign, Campaign Monitor, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Pardot, etc.)</li>
<li>
Comfortable with popular code editors (e.g. Sublime Text, Atom, Dreamweaver)</li>
<li>
Basic understanding of A/B testing concepts and reporting</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail and an eye for quality are essential</li>
<li>
Self-starter that isn't afraid to roll-up their sleeves and get things done</li>
<li>
Proven track record of prioritizing activities and keeping complicated projects moving forward</li>
<li>
Knowledge of email regulations including CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and CASL</li>
</ul>
<p>
Preferred</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree preferably in Marketing, Communications, Business, or Software Engineering</li>
<li>
Experience working across multiple buyer segments: individual and enterprise</li>
<li>
Familiarity with email/contact database management in an ESP</li>
<li>
Experience using a CMS such as WordPress</li>
<li>
Experience with general Microsoft Excel skills would be desirable</li>
</ul>
