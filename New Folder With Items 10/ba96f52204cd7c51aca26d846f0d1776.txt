<h2>VP, Strategic Healthcare Technology Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Job Description:
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Role Summary/Purpose:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Reporting into SVP, Healthcare Payments Strategy, the VP of Strategic Healthcare Partnerships will be a part of a team that builds and maintains strong relationships with existing and prospective partners and will assist in designing and implementing strategic initiatives to accelerate the growth of CareCredit in existing markets as well as to facilitate the entry of CareCredit into new markets.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute CareCredit’s partnership strategy and a structured partnership program for CareCredit within the healthcare payments space.
</li>
<li>Support growth of the CareCredit Network via the development and execution of partnership campaigns. Work closely with the marketing and sales teams to maximize opportunities that come from channel partners and be responsible for the development and refinement of ongoing channel development strategy.
</li>
<li>Provide partner account management, build and nurture strategic relationships with operational and executive staff within partner organizations
</li>
<li>Effectively engage with internal partners, including sales team and provider experience team to facilitate organized teams, ensure meaningful results are achieved, and build ongoing relationships
</li>
<li>Work closely with CareCredit’s Business Development and Sales teams to outreach and manage a pipeline of prospects through partners.
</li>
<li>Lead partners through CareCredit integration. Establish implementation / engagement cadence (weekly, monthly, quarterly business reviews) to ensure results for performance and growth are met and exceeded or evolve existing business cadence to drive higher levels of performance
</li>
<li>Define what is needed across existing CareCredit teams to support this strategy, including in sales, marketing, IT, and operations. Assess their readiness to support enterprise-level requirements and work with their leaders to enhance this.
</li>
<li>Work with our partners and internal teams to identify expanded solution definitions and strategies for driving these solutions through the partner ecosystem.
</li>
<li>Communicate professionally and at multiple levels within the client’s organization; anticipate partner needs and correspond proactively.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties and/or special projects as assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications/Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in a related field (healthcare or business)
</li>
<li>7-10 years of experience dealing with healthcare technology companies required, preferably in the revenue cycle management area or EHR space - applicants without healthcare experience will not be considered
</li>
<li>Highly knowledgeable about healthcare landscape and current trends in healthcare
</li>
<li>A minimum of 3 years experience working in a client relationship and/or business development role, identifying multiple opportunities aligned to strategy
</li>
<li>Demonstrated collaboration, project management, problem-solving, quantitative and qualitative analysis skills
</li>
<li>Strong understanding of software implementation process
</li>
<li>Strong critical thinking skills with an ability to creatively develop solutions that address client needs
</li>
<li>Up to 30% travel required.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired Characteristics:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Masters in Business Administration
</b></li>
<li><b>Prior experience in financial services
</b></li>
<li>Genuine passion for and commitment to the transformation of healthcare delivery
</li>
<li>Ability to work in complex matrix organizations and drive deep relationships with all stakeholders to deliver results.
</li>
</ul>
