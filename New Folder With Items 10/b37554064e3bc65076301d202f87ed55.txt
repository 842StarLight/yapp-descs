<h2>Entry-Level Claims Representative - Auto Material Damage</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
We're a people-focused business, and we pride ourselves on the promise to deliver efficient and effective services to our customers. At Sentry, we believe people are our competitive advantage. We strive to attract, train, and motivate our employees to keep Sentry as a market leader in our industry. We are committed to hiring people with the potential to excel. As a Claims Representative for our Auto Material Damage unit, you’ll play a critical part in fulfilling Sentry’s promise to be there for our customers during their time of need.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><p>
The AMD Claims Rep will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate losses and prepare or audit damage appraisals</li>
<li>Appraise labor costs, actual cash values, parts, and repair prices, etc</li>
<li>Evaluate, negotiate, and settle total losses</li>
<li>Monitor and control rental expenses by managing the total loss process</li>
<li>Determine if subrogation opportunities exist while taking the appropriate actions to protect Sentry</li>
<li>Resolve issues with policyholders, claimants, or state insurance departments</li>
<li>Perform other job related duties requiring the same general degree of responsibility</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><ul>
<li>Associate Degree and/or 2+ years related work experience</li>
<li>Ability to obtain and maintain state adjuster/appraiser licenses</li>
<li>Knowledge or experience of automobile collision repairs beneficial</li>
<li>Body Shop/estimating or previous claims experience preferred</li>
<li>Well-developed organizational, human relations, and communication skills</li>
<li>Strong computer skills and demonstrated ability to learn new systems quickly</li>
</ul>
