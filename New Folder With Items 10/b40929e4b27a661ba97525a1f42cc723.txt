<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and manage relationships within our Commercial market segment (300 – 999 employee based companies)</li>
<li>Target, open and close new accounts in an expansive territory</li>
<li>Pitch and demo Clari to sales leaders &amp; execs – VP Sales/CRO, CFO, VP Sales Ops/Enablement</li>
<li>Manage large commercial deals from first call to close</li>
<li>Build and manage large pipeline of business</li>
<li>Achieve and exceed individual monthly set quotas</li>
<li>Execute best sales practices and technique as provided by management</li>
<li>Create satisfied, reference-able and referring customers</li>

<ul>
<li>3-5 years of strategic software selling experience to Mid-Market and/or Commercial accounts</li>
<li>Hunter - appetite for opening and closing new accounts</li>
<li>Experience managing 3-6 month sales cycles</li>
<li>Proven ability to close in the Commercial line of business</li>
<li>Understanding of SaaS and selling to LOB seller</li>
<li>Business savvy to work with Marketing, Product, and Customer Success leaders</li>
<li>Ability to establish relationships with C-level clients, business and technical buyers, and key project stakeholders.</li>
<li>Ability to clearly articulate your point of view in professional manner</li>
<li>A results-oriented approach which balances a “take charge, do-whatever-it-takes” attitude with teamwork and collaboration</li>
