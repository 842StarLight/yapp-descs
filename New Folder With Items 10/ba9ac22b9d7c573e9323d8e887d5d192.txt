<h2>Sales Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>BluHoot.com is a Rockland community/B2C based small business directory, focused solely on leveraging relationships with local, small businesses and its surrounding community to build a robust directory of small businesses and events for residents and tourists while building the brand recognition of small businesses through newsletter marketing, directory listings, website development. The sales position will be focusing heavily on lead development, conversion and retention.</p>
<p>We are looking for a self- motivated individual with a “closer” mindset to join our Sales team. This position will report directly to the owner. Initial compensation includes a commission based salary and monthly bonus plan. Quarterly reviews which yields a performances above and beyond agreed scope of work can land you in a salaried position, with commissions, optional benefits and bonuses. If you love to connect with clients and help them discover how a locally based directory can help their profit margins by scaling their marketing by utilizing print marketing, personalized websites and an SEO driven marketing listing, then BluHoot.com is the place for you to start a rewarding career!</p>
<p>What You'll Do/Who You Are:</p>
<ul>
<li>You listen to your customers and use your expertise, creativity, and passion to discover our products and inspire their confidence to market their small business with BluHoot</li>
<li>You are engaging and can leverage your personality to quickly connect with others</li>
<li>You build trust and rapport with your customers</li>
<li>You meet or exceed all targets for revenue, close/conversion rate.</li>
<li>You can simultaneously navigate multiple software applications and technologies</li>
<li>You efficiently manage your time and always put the customer experience first</li>
<li>You are able to overcome any objection and don’t take “no” for an answer</li>
<li>You believe relationships matter, love being part of a team and are focused on achieving both team/individual goals</li>
<li>You promote appropriate solutions and inspire customers to subscribe to BluHoot by seizing the opportunity and highlighting for the sale on every call</li>
<li>You play to win and have a positive outlook</li>
<li>You solve problems by thinking critically and creatively</li>
<li>You strive to always do the right thing</li>
</ul>
<p>What You'll Need:</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication and relationship building skills</li>
<li>2+ years of sales experience, preferably in a solutions-based selling environment (Retail, SaaS, Account Management, etc.)</li>
<li>You demonstrate the ability to think like an owner of the business</li>
<li>You demonstrate the ability to be teachable and open-minded to feedback</li>
<li>You demonstrate the ability to sound confident in your product recommendations</li>
<li>You are reliable and always strive to be on time</li>
</ul>
