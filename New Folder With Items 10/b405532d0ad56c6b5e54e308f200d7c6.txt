<h2>Territory Account Manager - Aloha POS - Cleveland</h2>

<p><b>About NCR
</b></p>
<p>NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a leading software- and services-led enterprise provider in the financial, retail, hospitality, telecom and technology industries. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga., with 34,000 employees and does business in 180 countries. NCR is a trademark of NCR Corporation in the United States and other countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>POSITION: Territory Account Manager - Aloha POS - Cleveland
</p>
<p>LOCATION: Cleveland, OH
</p>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>POSITION SUMMARY &amp; KEY AREAS OF RESPONSIBILITY:
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Position is responsible for selling the Hospitality solution portfolio within an assigned territory; Position sells solutions and services to secure new opportunities maximizing revenue and profitability and increasing NCR's market share
</li>
<li>Targets accounts in local geographic area, typically at ownership level, in the small to medium-sized business market. Position is responsible for prospecting, managing and bringing to closure new business opportunities within targeted area including: Table Service accounts, Quick Service accounts, and other relevant hospitality industries in a territory.
</li>
<li>As a member of the sales team, objectives are focused and performance is measured on the ability to close new business.
</li>
<li>Position executes the sales process by moving new opportunities successfully through the funnel; Position accountable for building relationships with prospective and competitive customers; Articulates solutions in terms of ROI to the client
</li>
<li>Serve as key interface in defining customer needs and NCR's ability to meet those needs through solutions; Serve as the liaison between the customer and NCR in regard to issues for new customer escalating any roadblocks to securing the business
</li>
<li>Responsible for proposing and closing deals in the assigned territory
</li>
<li>Position requires strong prospecting skills, ability to build customer relationships through consultative selling and promoting customer confidence in NCR; Must maintain focus on the development of new business opportunities and closing new accounts allowing us to aggressively penetrate competitive accounts leading to long term business relationships
</li>
<li>Work in cooperation with NCR teams to ensure a cohesive “Unified NCR” approach with all customers and minimize conflict
</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally within organization to ensure all aspects of the business work together for best results for the company and customers
</li>
<li>Structure mutually beneficial financial relationships<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Restaurant operations, sales, or management experience and/or training/certification
</li>
<li>Exceptional communication and presentation skills, both verbal and written
</li>
<li>Highly motivated and driven to succeed
</li>
<li>Strong relationship-building skills
</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving skills
</li>
<li>Objectivity and ability to make sound decisions
</li>
<li>Aptitude to learn quickly and perform well under pressure
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently yet within company guidelines
</li>
<li>High level of integrity, confidentiality and professional maturity
</li>
<li>Ability to travel within local or regional territory 50% - 100% of time.<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:
</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>1 year+ of sales or operational experience with complex software and hardware solutions and professional services or prior experience in the POS industry
</li>
<li>Sales and/or Marketing experience with emphasis on prospecting, qualifying, and closing new business
</li>
</ul>
</div>

</li></ul>
