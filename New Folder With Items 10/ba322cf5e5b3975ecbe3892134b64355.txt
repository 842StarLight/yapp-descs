<h2>Inside Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Connection, a Fortune 1000 Global Technology Solutions Provider is adding to our family of passionate individuals. We work together to connect people with technology that enhances growth, elevate productivity, and empower innovation. If you would like to discover how over the 35 years of collaboration has shaped our culture of integrity, teamwork, and mutual success, we would love to speak with you!<br>
<br>
Working under minimal supervision of the Technical Sales Manager, the Inside Solutions Architect (Inside SA) provides pre-sale technical consulting to advise and recommend technology solutions of various levels of complexity to clients, referring only exceptional problems and issues for management review or approval. The Inside SA works directly with account executives to engage with clients. The Inside SA assesses the client's needs utilizing business systems analysis and designs a solution that fits the functional requirements of the client. The Inside SA, as a solution provider, exercises discretion and independent judgment in decision making during the design of the solution, considering pricing which meets the client's budgetary requirements. The Inside SA strategizes with their assigned sales team on IT solution opportunities. The Inside SA acquires and maintains various technical certifications pertaining to their technical specialty areas.<br>
<br>
