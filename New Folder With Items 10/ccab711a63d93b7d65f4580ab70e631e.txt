<h2>New Client Setup Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Overview</b></p>
<p>A Paycom New Client Setup (NCS) Specialist serves as a single point of contact to new clients for training, payroll support, technical support and customer service as they transition to the Paycom software. The Specialist is the front line for both client retention and satisfaction. The NCS Specialist ensures that all payrolls for their assigned clients are 100% accurate, new administrators and users are appropriately trained, and requests for assistance, questions and fixes are answered in a timely manner. The NCS Specialist provides solutions to client inquiries and always does so with a smile in their voice and the client in their mind. The Specialist thrives in a collaborative work environment and takes responsibility of handling problems through to completion, thus forming a trusted relationship with their clients and colleagues. The NCS Specialist exhibits the Paycom attitude of willingness to go the extra mile to resolve a problem and takes every opportunity to learn and expand their industry and technical knowledge. When confronted with a challenging problem, the NCS Specialist empathizes with the client, takes full responsibility to resolve the issue, asks for help when needed, and understands the urgency and importance of each situation.</p>
<p>Key Performance Objectives</p>
<p>The following are the performance objectives for this position. In assessing competency and fit, all candidates will be bench marked against these standards.</p>
<ul><li>
<b>Populate the project plan, prepare for and assist with implementation team on kickoff meeting</b>.</li></ul>
<p>Work with implementation team to set final schedule, get customer acceptance and support to meet all key milestones and dates in the project plan.</p>
<ul><li>*Ensures collected customer data is complete, accurate and ready for set up.</li></ul>
<p>*Identify all combined data requirements, key data holders, and manage the process for collecting and transmitting all required data to the correct Paycom department. Troubleshoot data problems as necessary data for high-level accuracy following Paycom security standards.</p>
<ul><li>*Effectively communicates across departments to ensure accurate setup for clients.</li></ul>
<p>*Work with Implementation Specialists and Sales Representatives throughout setup process to obtain information and gain understanding of client business processes. Provide ongoing training and resolution to client inquiries for up to 45 days after initial check date (or until the customer transitions to long-term support).</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Completes all scheduled calls with the customer within dates identified during the kick off meeting.</b></li>
<li>Conduct and assist with any additional scheduled calls held with the client i including but not limited to implementation review call, perfect first payroll call and post payroll GL acceptance call.</li>
<li><b>Maintains client accounts by providing excellent client service.</b></li>
</ul>
<p>Maintains a minimum client satisfaction score of 8+. Receive unsolicited positive feedback from clients regarding the clarity of their communications, technical knowledge, patience and problem-solving abilities.</p>
<ul><li><b>Actively engages in self-learning and self-improvement.</b></li></ul>
<p>Successfully completes the 13-week training program through active participation in all role-plays, assignments and pass all testing. Become a perpetual learner and seeks to improve. Completes the annual product certification process.</p>
<ul><li><b>Processes payroll for all assigned accounts- 100% accuracy.</b></li></ul>
<p>Proactively acts on all payroll instructions, arranges payment methods, and monitors, validates and acts on any highlighted red flags in each payroll run. Communicates payroll status quickly and accurately both internally and to the client.</p>
<ul><li><b>Creatively addresses and documents complex client issues.</b></li></ul>
<p>The NCS Specialist applies their creativity to identify alternatives and workarounds to enable Paycom clients to meet their needs until a more permanent solution is found. When an issue cannot be resolved within the standard 10 minutes or on the same call, the NCS specialist documents client inquiries and complaints in a clear manner that can be traced to the source and the situation is clearly explained in the notes.</p>
<p>Strong NCS Representatives will consistently meet the following key metrics:</p>
<ul>
<li>100% on time implementation project delivery- no pushes or no-starts.</li>
<li>Ensure all deadlines are met for each client.</li>
<li>Successfully completes all milestones within 45 days of first payroll run.</li>
<li>Receives scores of 9 or 10 on all implementation surveys.</li>
<li>Consistently meets 80% weekly average phone availability.</li>
<li>100% implementation satisfaction within 45 days of check date-full transition to Paycom Service Department.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree required</li>
<li>Demonstrates proficiency with computers, specifically in Excel, web conferencing and internet search techniques.</li>
<li>Professional appearance and demeanor</li>
</ul>
