<div>Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) is an Amazon service for our sellers. The FBA team collaborates with sellers and our Amazon fulfillment centers to create a seamless experience for sellers to leverage our world-class facilities. 71% of sellers who use FBA report more than a 20% increase in unit sales after joining FBA. In a very real way, we are changing lives with the work we do. Our FBA employees wake up every day thinking about how to enable our sellers to be successful on our platform. Enabling seller success allows us to provide Amazon customers with unparalleled selection and pricing. FBA is a win for sellers and a win for customers.<br>
<br>
To create a richer end-to-end Seller experience while improving FBA profitability, FBA is looking for a Senior Product Manager to join a dynamic team at the forefront of the vision focused on the solutions targeted to expand FBA eligibility for global selling. You will be responsible for bringing a strong Customer focus to innovation and influencing a product roadmap and vision that dramatically transforms how Sellers can grow their business while reducing total supply chain costs and complexity.<br>
<br>
Successful candidates will demonstrate experience balancing data analysis, customer research, project and stakeholder management, and internal/external communication. You must have sound business judgment and the ability to think strategically with a long-term vision about this fast growing business, while being able to execute on tactical details.<br>
<br>

<ul>
<li>Enhance features for existing FBA programs and develop features for newly identified /justified business opportunities.</li>

<li>Define and manage the rollout of product features and enhancements for the product initiatives.</li>
<li>Prioritize and make trade-off decisions balancing customer experience, seller experience, and business value.</li>
<li>Manage tech development teams and global stakeholders to meet defined milestones and delivery dates. Influence external teams’ roadmaps to execute on the product vision.</li>

<li>Drive adoption of the rolled out solutions, develop mechanisms to identify and iterate on the Seller experience.</li>
<li>Set-up mechanisms to audit and perform necessary course corrections where needed to meet business objectives</li>
</ul>
