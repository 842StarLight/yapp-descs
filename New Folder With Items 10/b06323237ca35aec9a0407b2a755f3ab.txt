<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY (BRIEF):</b> The Video Producer is responsible for managing the creative planning and production of video content to support new product introductions, ongoing promotion, and post-sale support. This position will collaborate with internal team members and external agencies as appropriate to create dynamic, on-brand and on-message content.</p>
<p><b>
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Create dynamic video content suitable for multiple platforms that aligns with organizational strategies and brand messaging.</li>
<li> Collaborate with internal team members and lead the planning of content production projects.</li>
<li> Coordinate all logistics of a video shoot.</li>
<li> Produce various types of video content: how-to for operation, installation, service, and recipes, customer success stories, event promotion, employee activities, and more.</li>
<li> Ability to creatively shoot and edit to tell dynamic stories.</li>
<li> Enforce brand marketing guidelines to maintain consistency.</li>
<li> Manage outside agencies as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p>EDUCATION and EXPERIENCE:</p>
<p>
The successful candidate must possess a 4-year college degree in Marketing, Communications, Public Relations/Advertising, Journalism or related field, and have three to five years of experience.</p>
<p><b>
SKILLS and QUALIFICATIONS</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5-7 years of work experience in TV/Video/digital creative roles</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of Adobe Creative Cloud Suite, Final Cut Pro, Adobe Premier and Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of Adobe Premiere and Final Cut Pro</li>
<li>Fluent in Adobe After Effects, Illustrator and Photoshop</li>
<li>Advanced experience with video editing in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Advanced experience with audio recording and monitoring</li>
<li>Experience with exporting/delivering various Video Codecs for Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of working on DSLR cameras</li>
<li>Understands the Principles of Photography: Shutter Speed, Aperture and ISO</li>
<li>Understands the Principles of Cinematography: Composition, Lighting and Framing</li>
<li>Experience with Story Boarding, Shot List creation and Scripting</li>
<li>Maintains an organized File Management system</li>
<li>Creative thinking and ability to present ideas in large group settings and to executives</li>
<li>Willing to contribute creative ideas to the team and experiment with various cinematic styles</li>
</ul>
