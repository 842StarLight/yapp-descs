<h2>Marketing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Prominent Property Management company headquartered in Bloomfield Hills, MI seeking full time Marketing Director.</p>
<p>The role of marketing director for a privately held real estate management company includes advertising, marketing, design, communications and public relations for the company as well as the individual properties within the portfolio that it manages. The portfolio includes over 150 properties located in sixteen states. The marketing director will oversee a small group of professionals tasked with the responsibility of providing marketing services to the managers of the real estate portfolio. In addition, the marketing director will collaborate with other senior management staff to establish strategy to further improve the overall performance of the portfolio. A sample of responsibilities include the following:</p>
<p><b>Advertising. </b>Work with senior staff, regional managers, managers and external media providers to develop and manage promotions or advertising campaigns.</p>
<p><b>Website</b>/<b>Social</b> <b>Media</b>/<b>Reputation</b> <b>Management</b>. Manage company and property websites, along with corporate social media presence including Facebook, Google, Instagram, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Twitter, and YouTube.</p>
<p><b>Communications</b>. Oversee corporate communications strategy and develop innovative advertising, public relations, social media and event planning to support operations of the company and its properties.</p>
<p><b>Design</b>. Provide design and photography services to the company and its portfolio of properties, including logos, floor plans, brochures, letterhead, flyers, newsletters and signs.</p>
<p><b>Public Relations. </b>Lead public relations strategy for the company, including the implementation of strategic communications, drafting of press releases, responding to media inquiries and providing support for properties as well as key executives.</p>
<p><b>Brand Awareness/Research Analytics</b>. Build brand awareness through smart positioning of assets with target audience through market surveys, data research and analytics.</p>
<p><b>Supervisory Skills</b>. Lead a professional marketing team to provide marketing, advertising and communication services to the closely held real estate portfolio operated by the company, including making hiring decisions, supervision of employees and developing budgets.</p>
