<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Founded in 1968, Paragon Properties is family owned company based in Bingham Farms, MI – a suburb of Detroit, the most up-and-coming urban landscape in the country. Here, innovation stands on the broad shoulders of tradition. Having fun and working hard is in our DNA. Our people are the foundation to our success; we are invested in hiring talented individuals and committed to focusing on their growth and development within the company.</p>
<p>
We are looking for an energetic and assertive Sales Representative to join our team. Sales Representatives sell new, pre-owned, and convert rented homes in a specific manufactured housing community. They advertise and show homes, and facilitate the prospect through the purchasing process. Are you ready for a career that offers both professional and financial opportunity and growth?</p>
<p><b>
Don’t you want to: LOVE WHAT YOU DO, DO WHAT YOU LOVE?
</b></p>
<p><b>Job Overview:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Attract prospective buyers through targeted marketing and advertising</li>
<li>Show prospective buyers new and pre-owned homes for sale in the community</li>
<li>Assist prospective buyers through the financing and closing process</li>
<li>Be up to date on the housing market and trends</li>
<li>Lucrative commission structure up to six figure earning potential</li>
<li>Convert current renters to home owners</li>
<li>Personal gratification with every customer connection you make</li>
<li>Financial gratification with amazing closing numbers</li>
<li>Take pride in growing a manufactured community to its full occupancy potential</li>
<li>Be trained the newest marketing and Advertising techniques</li>
<li>Collaborate with Manager to select sites and home specifications on new home orders</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
