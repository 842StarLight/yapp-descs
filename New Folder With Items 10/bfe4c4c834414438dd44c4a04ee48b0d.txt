<div>

We are harnessing the power of cloud, data analytics, the Internet of Things, and user design thinking to deliver on the promise of intelligent buildings and smart cities that connect communities in ways that make people’s lives – and the world – better.</p>

<br>
<p>
We enable smart facilities powered by the IoT (Internet of Things). More and more building systems are data-enabled and connected to the web. At Johnson Controls, named “IoT Innovator of the Year for 2019” by the IoT Breakthrough Awards Program, we are partnering with visionary organizations to build the first smart buildings enabled by artificial intelligence and IoT. We help enterprises put their building data to work, helping facilities managers discover insights, find efficiencies, and create other sources of value, chief among them sustainability.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What you will do</b></p>
<p>
This position is responsible for product and portfolio management, and contributing to the aggressive growth of the business. The position will serve as the single point of accountability to provide Market Insights, Product Strategy, Product Development Priorities, Product Commercialization Deployment and Lifecycle Management within the Digital Solutions Business Unit. The position must be a quarterback, working with customers, software development, sales, marketing, training, literature, operations, and other departments and business units to implement and deliver on all aspects of product management for their assigned product lines.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
How you will do it</b></p>
<p>
Market Insights (Examine market situation in order to identify relevant market opportunities and issues):</p>
<ul>
<li> Use market intelligence to identify issues and opportunities that will direct the product management effort.</li>
<li> Understand which market-product combinations are important to address.</li>
<li> Understand and align with top-down business strategies.</li>
<li> Use voice of customer to determine and validate requirements for existing products as well as new product developments.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Product Strategy (Develop and articulate a clear product vision and strategy for sustainable and profitable growth that aligns with BU vision and strategies):</p>
<ul>
<li> Establish a vision for the product and how it will achieve profitable growth for the business</li>
<li> Maintain product roadmap for Product Line segment that is inclusive of all products within the assigned Product Line marketed or produced in all global regions.</li>
<li> Assess attractiveness and ability to compete in market segments and identify target segments</li>
<li> Define how the product will be positioned within the portfolio and against competitors and the overall strategy for the product</li>
<li> Achieve a balanced portfolio</li>
<li> Product Development (Ideate, prioritize and develop new products that meet the most urgent needs of target markets and offer advantage over competitive offerings):</li>
<li> Establish a front-end of innovation that identifies creative ways in which to solve customer pain points</li>
<li> Develop a long-term vision of product development to sustain market position</li>
<li> Implement scalable and agile product development to accelerate time-to-market and systematically reduces new product risks</li>
<li> Co-create with customers (where applicable) to bring products to market aligned with customer needs</li>
<li> Align resources against highest priorities</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Product Commercialization Deployment (Manage the multi-functional activities required to launch a new product in the market):</p>
<ul>
<li> Develop first-hand familiarity with the global sales offices and channels through which primary market needs are addressed.</li>
<li> Finalize launch strategy - who to target, how to position the new product, how to price and how to generate demand</li>
<li> Establish commercial (sales, channel and marketing) readiness with the required sales/channel enablement and market communication material</li>
<li> Prepare supply chain, operations and IT systems for and supplying products</li>
<li> Lifecycle Management (Manage the health of the product in accordance with its role in the portfolio, its performance and its stage in the product lifecycle):</li>
<li> Manage growth and profitability across the product mix</li>
<li> Understand and generate demand and match supply to forecasted demand</li>
<li> Coordinate functional activities to deliver the desired customer experience</li>
<li> Take decisions on the future of the product</li>
</ul>
</div>

<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<div><b>
What we look for</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Bachelor's degree from an accredited four-year college or university. MBA or appropriate graduate degree is desirable.</li>
<li> Relevant work experience in Product Management and analytics or comparable, preferably in data oriented and software products.</li>
<li> Experience with data query, visualization, dashboard and/or scorecard tools.</li>
<li> Experience in B2B software/services.</li>
<li> Strong understanding of behavior/predictive data analytics concepts with a proven ability to apply these concepts to generate value for our customers.</li>
<li> Ability to operate at both a strategic and tactical level when it comes to capability, process and technology analysis, design and development.</li>
<li> Strong critical thinking skills. A good combination of creativity and business acumen.</li>
<li> Strong written and oral communications skills with the ability to synthesize insights to support decision-making at all levels of the organization - senior management, team members, peers, and partners.</li>
<li> Exceptional interpersonal skills with the ability to build relationships inside and outside the organization with stakeholders, customers and colleagues.</li>
<li> To perform this job successfully, an individual should have complete working knowledge of MS Office, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Outlook, etc.</li>
<li> Must be willing to travel up to 25% of the time.</li>
</ul>
