<h2>Relationship Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Relationship Advisor</b></div>
<div><b>Fayette County Market - Polo Club Branch Opening January 2020.</b></div>
<div><b>Full Time Hourly Non Exempt</b></div>
<div><b>Level 7 - Starting rate of $16.35 per hour.</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>We are currently seeking individuals who will begin training for our New and Exciting Polo Club branch that is under construction. The qualified applicants will complete a training curriculum with our Education and Development Department and on the job training at our Central Kentucky Branches that support the Relationship Advisor model.</i></b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Work Schedule for Training: Monday - Thursday 8:00 am - 5:00 pm; Friday 8:00 am - 6:00 pm; Saturday Rotation 9:00 am - 12:00 pm. Branch hours of operation will be determined at a later date.</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>This position provides a single customer interaction in the branch, serving as new account and financial advisor while also serving as a processing agent for routine financial transactions including account servicing, check cashing, withdrawals, and deposits via a cash drawer.</b></div>
<div><b>Nothing in this job descriptions restricts management's rights to assign or reassign duties and responsibilities to this job at anytime.</b></div>
<div><b>All Kentucky Bank employees are required to adhere to the appropriate summaries of responsibilities of the BSA policy and procedures.</b></div>
<div><b>Essential Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Engages with customers to identify needs to create opportunities for the Bank</li>
<li>Identifies cross selling opportunities</li>
<li>Processes Teller/New Account/Loan and/or Operational Transactions</li>
<li>Performs general duties</li>
<li>Adheres to the rules and regulations relating to the Bank Secrecy Act (BSA)</li>
</ul> <b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of business concepts, tools, processes that are needed for making sound decisions in the context of the Bank’s business; ability to apply this knowledge appropriately to varying situations</li>
<li>Concrete understanding of Bank’s software, policies, procedures, and structure to provide the utmost in Premier Customer Service</li>
<li>Apply financial knowledge to recommend an array of banking and wealth management products</li>
<li>Utilize reports to proactively identify and successfully produce sales opportunities</li>
<li>Ability to analyze credit request to recommend appropriate product and structure. Ability to utilize software decision platform, apply bank policy and all regulation pertain to consumer credit</li>
<li>Ability to conduct themselves in a professional and positive manner while working with customers, co-workers, and other Kentucky Bank contacts</li>
<li>Knowledge of and ability to identify and engage potential opportunities. Build relationships and create opportunities</li>
<li>Work independently and as a team to create an environment of superior service, financial advisement, and relationship growth</li>
</ul>
