<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>CUMULUS MEDIA – Tri-Cities, TN/VA </b>is seeking a talented and dynamic <b>Account Executive</b> who thrives in a fast-paced working environment. We are looking for a passionate, motivated, goal-oriented and highly competitive individual with a proven track record of over-achieving sales goals. We <i>hire</i> driven, resourceful, organized, customer-focused problem solvers who have great communication skills and the ability to cultivate client relationships. In return, we offer a unique <i>career</i> opportunity: one where you are given a challenging mission, world-class tools to help you succeed, and the chance to realize your <i>full</i> potential as a sale’s professional.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Cumulus | Tri-Cities features 5 radio stations in the Tri-Cities and surrounding area. </b>Our stations include 101.5 WQUT Classic Rock, AM 910 WJCW Talk Radio, 104.9 Nash Icon WKOS, 640 AM WXSM Sports Radio and 1320 AM WGOC Business Talk radio. The cluster of 5 stations reaches thousands of listeners on a daily basis.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Account Executive identifies and develops new business <i>opportunities</i>; grows existing client relationships; offers solutions that help clients achieve their business goals; closes business and meets or exceeds set sales targets.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Able to identify client business needs <i>by</i> gaining a deep understanding of their goals, objectives and processes as well as their external environment including key market and consumer trends to proactively develop customized advertising solutions to meet client objectives</li>
<li>
Deliver effective multi-platform, integrated sales presentations (broadcast, digital, events); negotiation and closing</li>
<li>
Beyond our broadcast products, have strong familiarity and become <i>full</i>-versed in selling both Cumulus’ station digital assets (e.g. streaming, stations website and social advertising) and digital audience extension products to deliver a complete marketing solution to advertisers</li>
<li>
Excel at prospecting and aggressively seeking new clientele <i>by</i> networking, cold calling, canvassing, referrals or other means to build and maintain a <i>full</i> pipeline of sales prospects</li>
<li>
Follow all station and corporate procedures for preparing orders, resolving billing issues, submitting regular reports regarding sales, pipeline lists, forecasts and competitive analysis</li>
<li>
Stay abreast of the competitive landscape and emerging technologies to best position Cumulus in the marketplace</li>
<li>
Think creatively and generate original ideas</li>
</ul>
