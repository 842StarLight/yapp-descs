<p><b>
D</b><b>ESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>
We have an immediate opening available for an <b>Director, Account Management </b>for CDK Heavy Truck. This role will work to enhance the relationship between Truck OEM Partners, Key Truck Clients and CDK by identifying the partner/client's unique characteristic and establishing business processes to support ease of doing business. This individual will also act as the leader of all sales and relationship related activities for named large competitive accounts in order to assure new business sales opportunities are identified. Other objectives: monitor performance, achieve financial goals, develop, motivate and counsel staff, foster a collaborative environment, provide feedback information about market conditions and plan sales strategy to achieve fiscal year goals.</p>
