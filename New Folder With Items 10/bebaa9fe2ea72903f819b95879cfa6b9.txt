<h2>Solutions Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>For over 25 years, Yorktel has been the video managed services partner around the world for some of the largest business and government agencies. With over 10,000 video systems under management worldwide – more than any other video communications service provider – Yorktel has the proven expertise and global reach to deliver enterprise-quality visual communications.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Yorktel is currently seeking a Solutions Architect who will provide advisory, consultancy and support for infrastructure, managed services, cloud services, end point technology and industry related trends and information to enterprise customers. The ideal candidate will have an expert understanding of collaboration infrastructure, equipment, services and technology across varying platforms., should have referenceable experience with thought leadership and demonstrated innovation, selling conferencing services, cloud services and associated technology. A knowledge of Microsoft Lync and Skype for Business architecture and how to integrate with it at the video and audio level is required. The candidate will work with account managers and customers to provide and explain solutions and present proposed architecture designs, services and technology to customers. Must be able to identify trends, solutions and troubleshoot complex issues within the customers Collaboration environment. Candidates are expected to have the experience, maturity and skills needed to communicate effectively with corporate management at all levels, as well as technical and non technical customers.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This position reports directly to the SVP Advanced Services, who will provide direction and support to the Solution Architect in regard to the following areas; client escalations, internal and external resources, and customer service. This position will work closely with the Program Manager and other team members within solution architecture, delivery teams, engineering, and onsite staff for the development of new technology solutions. This position will also be expected to communicate internally with project, program and account managers, maintenance implementation managers, and engineering.</p>
<p></p>

<p>Documented experience with provided high-level and detailed architecture designs for video conferencing platforms, with the following expectations;
