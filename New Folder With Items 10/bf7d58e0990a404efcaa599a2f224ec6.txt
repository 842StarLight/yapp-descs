<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary &amp; Key Responsibilities<br>
</b></h2>
<p></p>Responsible for profit and growth for a specific state assignment and takes an active role in formulating and implementing state strategies through analysis and focus on competitor behaviors including product and pricing strategies, marketplace dynamics, pricing segmentation and account level analysis. Must work collaboratively with the Product Management team as well as representatives from Product Development, Actuarial, Field Sales, Underwriting, Claims and IT to accomplish goals and objectives.
<p><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the field sales team and other business partners to develop state profit and growth strategies by dissecting and managing data at a cellular level to optimize pricing, risk selection, portfolio mix, market segmentation and competitive position.
</li>
<li>Negotiate rate changes with field sales team, actuarial, product development and other business partners in order to maximize profit and growth while formulating pricing packages to document rate changes.
</li>
<li>Work closely with the field sales team, including traveling within the territory, in order to build expertise on state assignments.
</li>
<li>Take an active role in the development and refinement of pricing practices across the Product Management department eliminating redundancies and establishing consistency in processes.
</li>
<li>Recommend improvements to pricing policies, procedures and workflow, including handoffs with product development, actuarial and filing/regulatory departments.
</li>
<li>Work with product development and filings to develop, update and implement coverage forms along with filing of those forms and responding to questions from departments of insurance when they arise.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Experience/Education
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Two years' experience in commercial property-casualty insurance required
</li>
<li>Bachelors degree in a business-related field, or a combination of experience and training deemed to be the equivalent.
</li>
<li>Requires a solid understanding of business insurance pricing principles, manual rules, policy forms, and underwriting guidelines and how these components contribute to positive corporate underwriting results.
</li>
<li>Requires a solid understanding of business insurance workflow, including how policies are entered and processed. Proficiency in word processing, database software, and the creation of spreadsheets is critical for success in the position.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Communication and Collaboration Skills
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent business writing skills are necessary in order to communicate precisely and effectively with other State Auto associates, regulatory bodies, reinsurers, and other outside entities.
</li>
<li>Above-average verbal communication skills are necessary in order to communicate clearly and effectively with all constituencies.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Problem Solving
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to accurately resolve materials versioning conflicts, interpret written requirements, and translate them into action items.
</li>
<li>Must be able to make judgments based upon previous experiences and factual information and recognize when and to whom to escalate issues for resolution.
</li>
</ul>
