<h2>Accounting Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p></p>
<p>When you join us at Thermo Fisher Scientific, you’ll be part of a smart, driven team that shares your passion for exploration and discovery. With revenues of $22 billion and the largest investment in R&amp;D in the industry, we give our people the resources and opportunities to make significant contributions to the world<br>
<br>
<b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
The <b>Accounting Assistant (non-exempt)</b> will be responsible for a variety of accounting duties including, but not limited to: processing a high volume of accounts payable, which includes a three way match (purchase order, receipt, invoice) posting, general ledger coding, routing expense invoices for approval, weekly check runs, ensuring invoices are paid timely and accurately.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Accounts Payable:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides accurate and proficient data entry and processing of invoices including review for appropriate documentation and approval</li>
<li>
Follow approval authority matrix and ensure there are appropriate approvals before entering invoices for payment</li>
<li>
Ensures timely payments to avoid late charges and negative impact on early pay discounts</li>
<li>
Pays invoices by obtaining payment authorizations, matching transaction documentation and scheduling/preparing disbursements, including check runs, wires and ACH payments</li>
<li>
Researches various issues relating to invoice vs. purchase order variances.</li>
<li>
W-9 processing</li>
<li>
Review and upkeep of AP e-mail box</li>
<li>
Monitoring international supplier payments due; setting up wire payments</li>
<li>
Assist in preparation of yearend issuance of 1099s</li>
<li>
Prepare and maintain financial reports and assist with month end-closing as needed</li>
<li>
Filing and maintaining accurate file of invoices and paid items</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Accounts Receivable:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Processing of Return Authorizations/Credits to customers</li>
<li>
Preparation of customer set up files</li>
<li>
Backup for cash application</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Other General:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bank reconciliations</li>
<li>
Sales Tax Filings</li>
<li>
Miscellaneous adhoc analysis requests</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Minimum Requirements/Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Associates degree or bachelor’s degree in accounting or finance</li>
<li>
1 – 3 years of accounting experience</li>
<li>
Familiar with standard accounting concepts, practices, and procedures</li>
<li>
Must be task-oriented and able to follow standardized work instructions in a structured environment that is high-volume and fast-paced and must be flexible with changing priorities as they arise</li>
<li>
Attention to detail, allowing early detection and correction of data entry errors at time of data entry</li>
<li>
Computer literate and familiar with accounting software programs.</li>
<li>
Experience with automated accounting systems (ERP), JDE experience a plus</li>
<li>
Detail-oriented, self-motivated, team player</li>
<li>
Oral and written communication skills and ability to deal with vendors and co-workers; interact positively with all levels of associates</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Excel software and excellent data entry skills required</li>
<li>
Flexibility in scheduling due to business requirements (i.e., month-end close)</li>
<li>
Organizational skills and ability to work independently with changing priorities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Working Conditions/Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
Office work environment. Sitting for extended periods of time. Data entry using a standard computer keyboard. Lifting up to 30 pounds (typically boxes of files).
</p>
