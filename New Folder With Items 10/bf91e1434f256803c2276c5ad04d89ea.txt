<div><div>Responsibilities
</div></div>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>
<ul>
<br>
<p>The Marketing Message Strategist for our Enterprise sales group will develop global enterprise messaging strategies and supporting marketing assets to position Lexmark’s value proposition and competitive differentiation for a wide variety of offerings, services, and solutions. This role leverages market research to understand customer challenges and motivations, the competitive landscape and market trends while working closely with R&amp;D, product marketing, services marketing and industry consultants to transform technical detail into compelling value propositions.</p>
<p>
Ideal candidates will have an appreciation for the nuances of creating global content and will actively engage with colleagues across geographies to understand and adapt to local market needs. The marketing message strategist collaborates with subject matter experts, creative services, demand generation, and field marketing teams to generate content for use across multiple communication vehicles – sales enablement, web, campaigns, events, PR and social media – in alignment with corporate strategy.</p>
<p>
This role provides an outstanding opportunity to work with a diverse and dynamic team of professionals around the globe.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<p><b>
Designs Enterprise Messaging strategy:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sets content strategy in concert with corporate and path to market priorities</li>
<li>Develops Messaging Platform Documents (MPDs) to support new offer launches</li>
<li>Collaborates with creative services, subject matter experts and product teams in the development of new materials</li>
<li>Responsible for publishing and maintaining content in Elevate (sales enablement portal)</li>
<li>Supports training team in the development of sales education modules</li>
<li>Provides content to the global RFP team</li>
<li>Participates in analyst briefings and response development</li>
<li>Supports SME and peer marketing teams with messaging development</li>
<li>Provides ad hoc executive messaging support</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications Desired in a Successful Candidate:</p>
<p>
This role requires excellent critical thinking, project management and change management skills, along with the ability to influence without authority. The right candidate will be a creative, resourceful and strategic thinker with a passion for the creative marketing process.</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent field experience required</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years’ experience in developing communication strategies</li>
<li>Strong presentation, writing/positioning and editing skills</li>
<li>Confidence in engaging with executives</li>
<li>Global marketing experience - a strong plus</li>
<li>5 or more years product marketing/field marketing/sales experience desired</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Lexmark customer segments and Sales coverage (Channel and Enterprise)</li>
</ul>
<p>
Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated problem-solving skills, as well as continuous improvement process skills</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Must thrive in a fast-paced business environment and display professionalism and confidence in complex situations</li>
<li>Strong collaborator with a diplomatic disposition</li>
<li>Demonstrated strong organizational and project management skills with attention to detail, including working with cross-functional teams in global positions</li>
<li>Training/education/consulting experience</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency in the use of Microsoft Office 365 programs (PowerPoint, Word, Outlook, Excel, Dynamics, Teams, Sharepoint)</li>
</ul>
<p>
Travel:</p>
<ul>
<li>Some international travel may be required</li>
</ul>
<li>The individual hired in this role should based in/near Lexington, KY to be daily commutable to the corporate headquarters for face to face meetings.</li>
<br>
</ul>
