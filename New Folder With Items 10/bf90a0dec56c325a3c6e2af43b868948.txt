<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
immixGroup is seeking an MI Intern to join its public sector MI team. The role of the MI team at immixGroup is to understand what drives the procurement of enterprise COTS products within the government and leverage that knowledge to help software and hardware manufacturers shorten sales cycles.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As an MI Intern you will report to and work in close collaboration with the MI Manager on a variety of projects. This includes primary and secondary research to analyze and identify enterprise information technology (IT) sales opportunities on behalf of leading software and hardware manufacturers and solution providers. The intern will support the development and delivery of major presentations, training sessions, tools, market overviews, and company profiles for internal and external audiences. This is an excellent opportunity for a technology-savvy and self-motivated professional to work closely with major players in the technology industry while expanding expertise and relationships.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>

<li>
Supports MI team with special projects performing data mining, analysis, and reporting to support supplier/partner initiatives</li>
