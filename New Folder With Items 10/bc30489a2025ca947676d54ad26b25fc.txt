<h2>Private Events Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Join the Lou Malnati's Family!</b><br>
Private Events Planner</p>
<p>
Right now is a fantastic time to join the Lou Malnati’s family! We are growing quickly and have many exciting initiatives underway making us a fantastic place to work. Our company strategy is to continue growing – growing our people and our ability to bring authentic Chicago style pizza to more customers – while strengthening our company culture and people processes.</p>
<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p>Responsible for coordinating all private dining opportunities at Gold Coast, Lincolnwood, Naperville, Buffalo Grove, Lakewood, Schaumberg and Elk Grove with a focus on increasing business and profitability for all private events.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p><i>
Event Planning and Execution</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as lead point of contact for all inquiries for private events including equipment needs and/or special menus for customers</li>
<li>
Provide quotes and proposals to customer in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Streamline website, menus, contracts for all stores</li>
<li>
Create special event menus within the means of our company (traditional Lou’s menus with some/very little variation)</li>
<li>
Co-Create and implement SOP’s for events and proposal requests with your manager and Director of Sales</li>
<li>
Coordinate private parties in all restaurants using TripleSeat software</li>
<li>
Follow contact strategy for leads and sales of events</li>
<li>
Serve as liaison between stores and client for seamless execution</li>
<li>
Negotiate contracts for volume discounts, buyouts and/or to meet customers’ budgetary requirements with support from your Manager and Director of Sales</li>
<li>
Resolve customer issues in accordance with the guidelines set by Malnati Organization</li>
<li>
Ensure the customer experience is professional &amp; hospitable from start to finish.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
Increase sales in store party rooms
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Create outreach plan for overflow when our private room is already booked to keep business as DOC</li>
<li>
Utilize outreach to grow re-book events</li>
<li>
Maintain relationships with event planners to obtain more opportunities</li>
<li>
Seek out new event planners and/or personal accounts to obtain more opportunities</li>
<li>
Host on-site room views, tastings or off-site meetings, when applicable</li>
<li>
Give recommendations for areas of improvement for planning, execution and/or profitability</li>
<li>
Create survey to measure customer satisfaction, store satisfaction, likeliness to refer and probability of repeat business</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
POSITION REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Customer service and event planning experience</li>
<li>
Sales experience a plus</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple projects at one time</li>
<li>
Highly organized with attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain composure and professionalism in high-stress/high-demand situations</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint</li>
<li>
Hours may include a combination of office hours and off-site/event hours, based on need</li>
<li>
Schedule will include some night and weekend events</li>
</ul>
