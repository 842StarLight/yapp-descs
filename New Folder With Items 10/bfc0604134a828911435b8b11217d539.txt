<h2>Marketing Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>We are seeking a talented highly motivated, self-starting creative individual with a passion for connecting with current and future customers through content marketing, social media, and traditional marketing efforts.
<br>
<br>
The Marketing and Social Media Assistant will be responsible for advancing the community and customer experience through online and traditional marketing. This position is charged with accelerating the expansion and the use of social media channels as well as to bring a high degree of creativity to our existing methods. This position requires interaction with the marketing, customer service, operations, sales and technology teams to ensure that our website, social media, collateral and other communications accurately reflect our brand and to achieve overall goals.
<br>
<br>
Applicants should have a well-developed capacity for coordinating and implementing marketing communication projects with responsibilities in social media, public relations, search marketing, advertising, brand stewardship and special events management. Familiarity with standard concepts, practices, and procedures within marketing and advertising is critical, while knowledge of real estate functions is a bonus. A certain degree of creativity and latitude is required.
<br>
<br>
In addition to marketing, brand, and social media related work, this position will also share a role in tracking sales data; maintaining collateral and marketing materials inventory; maintaining databases; and preparing reports.
<br>
<br>
This position will lead our efforts to engage stakeholders with quality content, create content delivery plans, and maintain consistent, brand-appropriate content as well as oversee and administer the company's social media marketing and advertising.
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><ul>
<li>
Manage all MLS listings</li>
<li>Plan for and organize closing events and gifts</li>
<li>Executes new community launch including signage and events</li>
<li>Develop and market special events for the agency
</li>
<li>Promote all team events on social media</li>
<li>Make sure all company materials are accurate and relevant</li>
<li>Develop marketing materials and advertising campaigns from conception to completion</li>
<li>Prepares weekly sales reports</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><ul>
<li>
Experience in social media management and content writing</li>
<li>Always searching for the next best thing to reach an even broader market</li>
<li>Marketing Degree or equivalent work experience required</li>
<li>Open to new ideas and encourages co-workers input</li>
<li>Exceptional phone skills</li>
</ul>
