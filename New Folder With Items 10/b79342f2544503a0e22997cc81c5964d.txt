<h2>Physician Office Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Muskegon Surgical Associates has provided the finest surgical care along West Michigan’s lakeshore since 1972. Our surgeons deliver care in multiple specialties including general surgery, hand surgery, plastic surgery, and vascular surgery. We also have a dedicated IAC accredited vascular ultrasound lab, a medical spa, and an occupational hand therapy center.</p>
<p>The specialty practice employs approximately 100 individuals, including 15 surgeons, across two physical locations in Muskegon, MI. Most surgical procedures are performed at Mercy Health Muskegon, Muskegon Surgery Center, or North Ottawa Community Health System.</p>
<p>The Administrator is a senior staff position with broad responsibility for overall management and development of the organization’s operations. The position has authority and responsibility for decisions related to the daily operations of the practice which are defined by the Shareholder Board. The role functions as a liaison between the practice personnel and the owner-operators. We are looking for an energetic, servant leader who shares our entrepreneurial mindset and our desire to maintain our independence.</p>

<ul>
<li>Accounting and finance</li>
<li>Administrative support functions/personnel</li>
<li>Billing Manager / Revenue Cycle</li>
<li>Building and grounds</li>
<li>Health Information Management (a.k.a. Medical Records)</li>
<li>Human Resources</li>
<li>Information Technology</li>
<li>Legal</li>
<li>Marketing</li>
<li>Physician Recruitment</li>
<li>Real estate</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Hand Therapy Center operations/personnel</li>
</ul>

<ul><li>Serves as Compliance Officer</li></ul>
<p><i>Other Ancillary Services</i></p>
<ul><li>Spa Operations &amp; Sales Supervisor</li></ul>
<p>Top responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>This position functions as the administrative officer of the practice and as such includes direct supervisory responsibilities for a team of managers and senior supervisors.</li>
<li>Create and maintain a purpose-driven culture, fostering individual motivation, teamwork and high levels of performance and accountability.</li>
<li>Serves as a change agent, coach, mentor, team builder and facilitator.</li>
<li>Function as liaison between the practice and other outside vendors who offer services to the company including accountants, attorneys, bankers, consultants and others.</li>
<li>Assists the Physician President and Shareholders in the development of broader goals and objectives based on needs. Develops and implements strategies to appropriately maximize operations. Deploys the company business plan.</li>
<li>Lead the ongoing development, communication, and implementation of team concepts, programs, policies, values, strategic objectives and our Brand Promise.</li>
<li>Design, manage, and continuously improve processes, systems and policies that positively impact our stakeholders</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with DOL, DOJ, DHHS, HIPAA, OSHA, etc.</li>
<li>Target excellence in patient access; fiscal management; internal communication; service delivery; operating efficiency; stakeholder satisfaction; and staff development.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in health administration required (or comparable degree with experience).</li>
<li>Master's degree in business, health administration, public health, or public administration from an accredited program is preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years of progressively complex prior experience in medical practice management including broad responsibility for supervision of staff, both junior and senior, and for complex operations across multiple functional areas and geographic locations.</li>
<li>Specific experience in a surgical specialty is highly desirable.</li>
<li>Certification as either a CMPE or FACMPE from MGMA/ACMPE is highly desirable.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High level of proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite with strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>High level of proficiency with Electronic Health Records, Practice Management Systems, and/or Care Management Systems</li>
<li>High level of proficiency with accounting and payroll systems.</li>
<li>Competence and capability in change management.</li>
<li>Proficient with interpersonal communication and relationship excellence.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, skills in time management, organization and the ability to delegate.</li>
<li>Demonstrated conflict resolution skills, the ability to collaborate with team members and to develop cross functional teams.</li>
<li>Demonstrated effective stress management skills by prioritizing work, being self-aware, and practicing delegation.</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of general ledger structure and financial reporting.</li>
<li>Understanding of cash management and accounts receivable analysis</li>
<li>Understanding of government incentive programs and regulations including functional knowledge of Generation II Accountable Care Organizations (ACOs).</li>
<li>Understanding of generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP).</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to analyze/evaluate/recommend proposed payer contracts.</li>
<li>Functional knowledge of corporate contracts.</li>
<li>Benefits program knowledge including health, dental, vision, and STD/LTD insurance options as well as 401(k)/profit sharing plan.</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of specialty medical practice industry.</li>
<li>Understanding of processes related to credentialing, licensing, registration and certification of all providers.</li>
<li>Ability to evaluate/negotiate malpractice insurance coverage options along with development of asset protection strategies for practice.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of applicable Federal and State guidelines and laws, OSHA/safety, corporate compliance, HIPAA Privacy, HIPAA Security, HR policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
