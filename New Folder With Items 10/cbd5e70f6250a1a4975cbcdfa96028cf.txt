<p><b>The opportunity:</b>
</p>
<p>As Principal Solution Consultant within OpenText Enterprise Content Services/Life Sciences team you will work as a Vertical Solution Specialist (Pre-Sales) virtual/anywhere in US with OpenText sales team as a core member to identify and develop revenue opportunities within the OpenText Life Sciences customer and prospect base. This will involve analyzing complex customer requirements and providing a compelling, technical sales presentations and demonstrations to the customer, which leverages one or more solutions from the OpenText's solution suite. You will be responsible for helping the customer understand the value proposition of OpenText's solutions.</p>
<p><b>You are great at:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working directly with Account Executives in the field to communicate OT value propositions to the Life Sciences customer/prospects.</li>
<li>Provide innovative thought leadership, strategies, on how OpenText’s solutions can address Life Sciences business problems.</li>
<li>Applying both technical and business skills to solve problems within an account and subsequently architecting an appropriate technical solution to meet the customer’s business requirements.</li>
<li>Presenting to senior level business and technical users within a customer account.</li>
<li>Qualifying opportunities at key milestones, taking into consideration the competitive landscape, solution fit, relationship with the customer, etc.</li>
<li>Develops and delivers product demonstrations and proof of concepts against specific customer requirements.</li>
<li>Responds to RFx requests, taking the lead for the solution and technical elements of the response.</li>
<li>Searching to find ways to create new opportunities for OpenText, either by improving existing products, processes and/or use of our technology.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What it takes:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree and a minimum of 3 years relevant work experience in a solution selling\value based selling environment.</li>
<li>7+ years experience within the Life Science vertical, with a specific focus on one or more of R&amp;D/Regulatory Submission, Electronic Trial Master File and Quality and Manufacturing document management processes.</li>
<li>Expert in 21 CFR Part 11 and how technologies meet these requirements. Understanding of GxP regulations.</li>
<li>Solid skill level in at least one product area of Enterprise Information Management, such as document management, engineering document management, enterprise content management, capture, process automation, information governance, legacy system decommissioning.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Cloud computing strategies, business models, and issues.</li>
<li>Ability to work remote/virtual and travel as needed up to 25%</li>
</ul>
