<h2>ITS Project Manager/Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>ITS Project Manager/Business Analyst<br>St Joseph’s College is seeking a qualified candidate for the position of Project Manager/Business Analyst (PM/BA). The PM/BA will be responsible for assessing, developing and documenting requirements for business, and service needs along with leading the successful implementation of technical projects through effective planning and execution.<br>Responsibilities<br>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Elicit requirements using various methods such as interviews, document analysis, requirements meetings, business process analysis, use cases or user stories, scenarios, comparative product analysis, task and workflow analysis</li>
<li>Formulate test cases to ensure that functional user’s needs are correctly interpreted</li>
<li>Research and recommend software products and software development to support and implement new or improved processes and/or requirements</li>
<li>Develop and maintain project related technical documentation using visual diagrams, process models, process maps or other tools as appropriate.</li>
<li>Manage the coordination, implementation, execution, control, and completion of technology projects</li>
<li>Estimate the resources and participants needed to achieve project goals</li>
<li>Set and continually manage project expectations with team members and other stakeholders</li>
<li>Manage project activities and timelines, to ensure projects are delivered on time, within budget, adhere to high quality standards, and meet customer expectations</li>
<li>Monitor and summarize progress of project(s) and prepare status reports</li>
<li>Escalate risks, issues and road blocks</li>
<li>Lead meetings and track action items</li>
<li>Manage changes in project scope, identify potential crises, and devise contingency plans</li>
<li>Define project success criteria and disseminate them to involved parties throughout project life cycle</li>
<li>Maintain the PMO project management software; Teamdynamix</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 3 years of business analysis and project management experience</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Information Technology or a related field required</li>
<li>The Project Management Institute’s (PMI) Project Management certification is preferred</li>
<li>Experience working in higher education institution highly preferred</li>
<li>Experience with MS Visio, MS Project Professional, and Project Management software</li>
<li>Strong documentation skills (process documentation, narratives, flows, diagrams,etc)</li>
<li>Strategic thinker with strong analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Professional and personable; ability to work constructively and collaboratively with people at all levels</li>
<li>Willing to take initiative, deal with ambiguity and assume responsibility</li>
<li>Excellent oral, written, and interpersonal communication and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
<p>This position will be based in Long Island and will require some travel between the two campuses. Please email cover letter, salary requirements and résumé.</p>
