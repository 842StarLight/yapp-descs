<b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p>
The Sales Development Representative will play an integral role in driving the growth of our customer base and revenue through generating qualified leads for our Enterprise sales team.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Specific Responsibilities will include:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct outbound calls to lead campaigns consisting of warm leads and cold calls</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities, generate interest, qualify, educate, and develop new opportunities</li>
<li>
Work with field sales team counterparts to develop and build territory plan</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The Successful Candidate will need:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrated success in sales development or lead generation</li>
<li>
Prior experience with cloud and/or SaaS products preferred</li>
<li>
Experience with Salesforce.com is a plus</li>
<li>
Organizational skills and attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to understand technology and communicate it to prospects</li>
<li>
Quick learner and self-starter with an entrepreneurial work ethic</li>
<li>
Knowledge of business organizational structures and buying influences</li>
<li>
BS/BA or equivalent</li>
</ul>
