About the Function
<br><br>
The Cyxtera Lead Data Center Facilities (Electrical) is accountable for planning, maintenance, and documentation of IDC operations and associated mission critical infrastructures toward the economy and maximum utilization of facilities, equipment, and contract resources. As a member of the Facilities Team, the Lead Data Center Facilities (Electrical) will support mentoring practices of junior staff to ensure continuity of training and quality results. The Lead Data Center Facilities (Electrical) ensures effective oversight of all contractor activity and maintains fiscal responsibility of projects and required maintenance practices.
<br><br>
About the Position
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Ensure operational efficiencies and best practices toward maintaining availability / uptime of all 24X7 facilities mission critical operations and Electrical infrastructure.</li>
<li>Ensure efficient and economic utilization of corporate resources to effect budget maximization and minimize cost.</li>
<li>Standardize and document all mission critical emergency and operational procedures and maintain as-built documentation.</li>
