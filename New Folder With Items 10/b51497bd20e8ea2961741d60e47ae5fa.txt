 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div>
<p><b>Title: </b>Content Marketing Specialist (Part-time)</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Reports to: </b>Marketing Manager</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>General Purpose: </b>The purpose of the Content Marketing Specialist position is to develop and enrich CSI’s content portfolio with high quality marketing content on digital and traditional media platforms to serve CSI’s overall marketing strategy.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Develop high quality marketing content for website, blog, social media, white papers, and printed materials to engage current customers, prospective customers, and a broader audience in the higher education and international education industry.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create content to support branding and demand generation campaigns, including email, social media marketing campaigns, paid search and outreach marketing campaigns.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate with Digital Marketing Specialists on content measurement, audience engagement, customer feedback, and demand generation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate with other departments and provide marketing support for campus events and other activities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other duties assigned by the Marketing Manager.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience:
</b></p>
<ul><li>A bachelor’s degree in Journalism, English, or Communications is preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Previous marketing or SEO experience is a plus.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Language Skills:</b></p>
<ul><li>Exceptional English writing and editing skills with a focus on storytelling</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Mathematical Skills:</b> Ability to add, subtract, multiply, and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions, and decimals. Ability to</p>
<p>compute rate, ratio, and percent and to draw and interpret bar graphs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Reasoning Skills:</b> Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists. Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.</p>

<p><b>Equipment/Computer Skills:</b> Incumbent must be able to use the following equipment and software packages with proficiency:</p>
<ul><li>Microsoft Office Suite</li></ul>
