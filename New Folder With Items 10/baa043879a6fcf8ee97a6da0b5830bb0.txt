<p><b>Location</b></p>
<p>Austin, Texas</p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>The Computer User Support Specialist will collaborate with other developers, computer operators, and project managers to maintain and create new and/or improved business solutions. We are looking for an entry level individual with a flexible schedule to handle on call support, machine maintenance, and job management.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL POSITION FUNCTIONS</b>:</p>
<ul><li>Oversee daily performance of computer systems, application software and peripheral systems.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work directly with users to resolve problems with hardware or software related to the fulfillment and financial applications.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Take the initiative to examine and improve processes in order to remediate problems and streamline daily operations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work a flexible and open schedule to include some long shifts, weekends and holidays in a 24x7x365 environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversee the nightly and weekly AS400 application and system backups.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Write operational documentation of standard operating procedures and daily job submittals.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate with outside vendors to support and maintain hardware and software utilized by users and/or computer operations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure Operations Department timelines are being met in a manner in accordance with the workday schedule.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Respond to Help Desk requests related to user access on the AS400, normal report distribution, special ad hoc reports and special projects as assigned.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work under minimal supervision and act as liaison to applications and infrastructure teams. Periodically act as backup to Operations Lead.</li></ul>
<p><b>SPECIALIZED KNOWLEDGE/SKILLS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Considerable interpersonal communication skills, both oral and in writing</li>
<li>Working knowledge of AS/400 operating system, commands, job automation software, report distribution software and backup/recovery hardware and software</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office suite</li>
<li>Ability to learn new technologies as needed</li>
<li>Complex problem solving skills Using logic to identify the strengths and weaknesses of solutions</li>
<li>Effective time management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>MIS degree or working towards a degree</li>
<li>Knowledge of computer technology concepts of backups, networking and troubleshooting</li>
</ul>
