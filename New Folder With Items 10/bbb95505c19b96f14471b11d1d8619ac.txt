<p><b>JOB SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>Manages the Enterprise Applications Team, consisting of the Business Systems Technical/Functional Analyst and Systems Engineer. Responsible for overall maintenance and governance of the company-wide ERP system and integrations, and business process improvement projects and activities. Directly involved in the functional and organizational design of new business practices working collaboratively with business process stakeholders and those in authority of subject matter specialization. Works closely with the IT ERP Project Manager on strategic implementations. Systems integration is a strong focal point, including the design, testing, execution, and maintenance of processes and workflows that effectively and efficiently interconnect disparate computer systems. Leverage new information technologies, data and the internet of things to deliver innovative digital workplace solutions that are agile and scalable as business needs change.</p>
<p>Manages the Network Services Team consisting of the Infrastructure and Security Architect and Network Engineer. The Network Services team is responsible for security threat identification/mitigation, global WAN/LAN connectivity, telephony and global VMWare virtualization hosts. Additionally, the Network Services team is responsible for management of our central data center located in Phoenix.</p>
<p>This role includes bringing together discrete systems utilizing a variety of techniques such as computer networking, enterprise application integration, business process management or programming to improve practices and/or introduce new ones. Applies proven communication skills to interface with business leaders and manage change by clearly articulating required changes and the resulting benefit to the business. Responsible for new technology research and development as applies to CPI business needs. Works to define digital strategy with business functions to deploy standard and mobile applications to support CPI growth.</p>
<p>Creates, monitors, and communicates project plans and ongoing status to IT Director on all open projects.</p>
<p>Works with IT Leadership team to create department budgets and technology plan.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides consultation to line-of-business (LOB) customers in defining or designing business processes, functions and organizational structures, as well as in researching, identifying and internally marketing enabling technologies based on customer requirements.</li>
<li>Through supervision of direct reports, maintains governance of ERP system, including any requested modification, upgrades, or reporting requirements.</li>
<li>Through supervision of direct reports, supports and maintains global CPI network connectivity and IP telephony.</li>
<li>Participates in enterprise strategy development, including environmental analysis, opportunity identification, value cases and business innovation portfolio development.</li>
<li>Participates in enterprise architecture development, including business architecture, third party integrations, information architecture, application portfolio and technical architecture.</li>
<li>Seeks ways to apply new technology to, and reuse existing technology for business processes; researches and provides information on technical trends.
</li>
<li>Assesses near-term needs, using collaborative interview processes, to establish business priorities; consults with technical subject matter experts and develops alternative technical solutions; advises on options, risks, costs vs. benefits, and impact on other business processes and system priorities.</li>
<li>Collaborates with other IT functional areas to remain apprised of project status, and informs LOB customer management of progress; conversely, keeps IT's technology and service managers aware of key LOB customer issues, identifying and resolving potential problems and conflicts.</li>
<li>Facilitates development of cross-LOB (enterprise) solutions that combine knowledge of particular business processes and issues, general technological options, and process facilitation techniques.</li>
<li>Leads application support and improvement efforts collaborating with IT ERP Project manager and IT peers.</li>
<li>Works with IT Leadership to prepare for and participate in yearly budget planning and manage monthly spend.</li>
<li>Leads Business Process Mapping and Modeling to support business requirements generation to enable Business Process Improvement initiatives.</li>
<li>Supports Companywide Enterprise Information Management initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<p>Develops, motivates and directs staff to create a team environment, and enables staff to fulfill the LOB customer operating objectives</p>
