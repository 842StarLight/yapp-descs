<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your Challenge: </b>The Senior Associate, Accounts Receivable (AR) will be tasked with performing a full range of accounts receivable duties including invoicing for all types of revenue sources, cash application, revenue recognition, collections, monitoring receivables, and maintaining records for multiple entities and business units.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>The things that you will tackle:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generates and mails/e-mails invoices for HIMSS and its subsidiaries for various products and services (including exhibits and sponsorships among many others) utilizing various systems. This includes interacting with the business areas and sales teams to ensure the appropriate product/service is being billed and revenue recorded in the applicable profit center and account.</li>
<li>Monitors status of receivables collection and aging of outstanding balances. Makes accounts receivable collection phone calls/e-mails for various HIMSS products and services. Assists in keeping business areas and sales teams informed of collection status and works with them as needed.</li>
<li>Monitors and ensures compliance with established policies and procedures for receivables and revenue processing. Suggests revisions to procedures to increase efficiency and effectiveness.</li>
<li>Participates in the monthly close process related to receivables and revenue. Provides accounting support as assigned to ensure that monthly closing schedule tasks and deadlines are met and financial statements are generated on time.</li>
<li>Performs account reconciliations, most specifically related to financial transactions for smaller conferences and events. creates journal entries and/or revisions to data in sub-systems to adjust activity as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you will need to be successful:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>An Associate’s Degree or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>A minimum of five (5) years of experience in billing, accounts receivable, and general accounting handling journal entries and account reconciliations; previous experience in a professional society, trade association or healthcare management preferred.</li>
<li>PC proficiency, including MS Outlook, Word, and Excel; experience with Personify, a2z, or other billing, receivables, exhibit management, customer management software Experience with Microsoft Dynamics Great Plains desirable.</li>
<li>Outstanding analytical skills with ability to achieve results in a complex environment.</li>
<br>
</ul>
