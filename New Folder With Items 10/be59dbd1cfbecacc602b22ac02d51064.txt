<p><b>Team Lead, Managed Services</b></p>
<p><b>
Professional Services |</b></p>
<p>
Reporting to the Regional Head of Managed Service, a managed services team lead is responsible for setting the tone for their managed services team and strives for quality and excellence in all aspects of managed services.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Main Responsibilities are:</b><br>
</p>
<p>
People Management:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage and retain a team (up to 10) of high performing Managed Services Consultants working across multiple enterprise-level clients</li>
<li>
Nurture talent to focus on customer delight, increase adoption and retention of the Sprinklr platform</li>
<li>
Track and monitor the team’s billable utilization, to ensure the utilization target is attained on a monthly basis</li>
<li>
Assist and guide the team with goal setting and career pathing/development</li>
<li>
Conduct bi-weekly 1 on 1 session with all team members</li>
<li>
On-boarding new resources<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Commercial Management:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the U.S. based Managed Services book of business</li>
<li>
Accountable for Managed Services client retention percentage</li>
<li>
Increase Managed Services penetration percentage across Sprinklr’s client base</li>
<li>
Account alignment with Regional Sales Managers/ Success Managers with new up-sell opportunities</li>
<li>
Provide bi-weekly updates for client renewal to Regional Practice Lead</li>
<li>
Serve as an escalation point for customer issues ensuring quick resolution</li>
</ul>
<p>
Desired Skills and Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>
Enthusiastic and creative leader with the ability to inspire people who also has the ability to motivate and focus a team and to develop individuals</li>
<li>
2 years prior people management experience ideally in high paced SAAS, Technology, or Marketing environment</li>
<li>
3+ years of experience in enterprise software deployment management and/or implementation</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Proven ability to work effectively under tight deadlines and juggle several assignments simultaneously</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong technical background with advanced computer skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and as a member of a team</li>
<li>
Demonstrate experience and a passion for the social media technology universe</li>
<li>
Extremely strong communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Fluency in social media platforms and direct experience in working with or for social media management software is preferred</li>
<li>
Passion for solving client challenges and commitment to client delight</li>
<li>
4-year college degree (minimum)</li>
</ul>
