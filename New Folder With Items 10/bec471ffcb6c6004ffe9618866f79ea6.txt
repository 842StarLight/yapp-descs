<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>ClickNotices is searching for a high-energy marketing professional to join our growing organization! We are looking for someone who’s highly motivated and driven to help us accelerate that growth!</p>
<p>In this role, you will draw on your creativity and marketing experience to expand the ClickNotices’ brand and mission throughout digital, social media, and traditional print channels. Working closely with our sales team, you will be a powerful resource in increasing interest and adoption of ClickNotices. The ideal candidate will be eager to learn our industry and expand their knowledge of marketing methods while taking ownership of content generation channels.</p>
<p>Your Opportunity</p>
<ul>
<li>Champion the ClickNotices brand</li>
<li>Have a passion for lead generation to create a full marketing pipeline for sales</li>
<li>Create engaging social media posts and campaigns</li>
<li>Contribute to the overall content marketing strategy</li>
<li>Maintain a monthly content marketing calendar</li>
<li>Monitor, measure, and enhance paid advertising channels</li>
<li>Analyze results from digital campaigns and report regularly on the results</li>
<li>Contribute to the content creation on the website, including blogs, whitepapers, and new pages as necessary, while boosting SEO</li>
<li>Provide direction and suggestions on visual design across all marketing channels</li>
<li>Work directly on email and direct marketing campaigns, maintaining consistent and high-level design and tone</li>
<li>Have an attitude of flexibility to assist Marketing Manager in any other tasks as they arise</li>
</ul>
<p>What You’ll Need</p>
<ul>
<li>4-year college degree or equivalent experience in a content marketing or lead generation role</li>
<li>1-5 years of experience as part of a marketing team or as part of a marketing agency, preferably in a B2B environment</li>
<li>Superb written and oral communication skills with a knack for explaining concepts clearly for your audience</li>
<li>Experience using a CRM tool and related Marketing Automation tool</li>
<li>Experience using a CMS system to manage website content</li>
<li>Ability to leverage digital measurement tools such as Google Analytics</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills with the ability to follow-through</li>
<li>Experience using Adobe Suite software such as Photoshop, Illustrator, In-Design, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to thrive and adapt in a fast-paced, collaborative environment that is constantly changing</li>
<li>Consistently be able to maintain a high quality of work that is representative of the company</li>
<li>Self-starter attitude and a drive for increased success and personal growth as a professional</li>
</ul>
<p>Ideal Experiences, but Not all Necessary</p>
<ul>
<li>Salesforce, Pardot, and Wordpress experience</li>
<li>Knowledge of real estate and property management, particularly the Multi-Family housing industry</li>
<li>Previously planned or coordinated events</li>
<li>Experience with market research and creating action plans on findings</li>
<li>Previous graphic design or presentation design experience</li>
</ul>
