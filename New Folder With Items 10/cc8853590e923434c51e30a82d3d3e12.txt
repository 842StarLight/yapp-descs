<p><b>Position Overview:</b></p>
<p>
Our Concierge Initiator will provide a superior class of service for our traveling clients. This will include consultation and the assignment of concierge order to Concierge agents. This position is responsible for ensuring consistent client satisfaction through service excellence to our concierge clients. Our Concierge Initiator are the first experience customers who opted for Concierge service will have; they are the bridge between initial orders and Concierge agents in all processing offices.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<br>
<p>
Provides the highest level phone support; which includes detailed information on available services that align with requirements, assisting the customer in making informed decisions.</p>
</li>
<li><p>
Analyzes customer requests and makes recommendations based on specific travel needs, ensuring realistic expectations that promote a dedicated customer base.</p></li>
<li><p>
Takes ownership of process, assign orders to Concierge agents and proactively communicate customer’s needs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Stays abreast of all product/service enhancements, system updates, and changes to requirements; become specialized in Visa and Passport processing; maximizing efficiencies and productivity.</p></li>
<li><p>
Promotes best practices and quality control, follows policies and procedures, and upholds standards of work to ensure compliance.</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintains customer records in proprietary database, utilizing system functionality to ensure accurate submission of data that maximizes productivity. Monitors own work and maintains updated customer profile through timely submission of notes, support documentation, and communications.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provides customer service and uses sales techniques to retain customers; educates customers about added-value products that may benefit them.</p></li>
