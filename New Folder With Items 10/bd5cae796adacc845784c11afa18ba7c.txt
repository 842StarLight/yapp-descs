<h2>I.T. Help Desk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>This position involves working in a team environment by providing Point of Sale support to our remote automotive retail locations via the phone and remote desktop software. The position also entails working with our corporate office providing customer service, internal systems work, reporting, and hardware/software troubleshooting.</p>
<p>The Point of Sale system is industry specific and our team will train you on its usage and support requirements.</p>
<p>Specific Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides technical expertise to end users using easy to understand methods</li>
<li>Communicates internally and with customers effectively</li>
<li>Manages and prioritizes support requests</li>
<li>Support a Windows 7/10 and Server environment</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Access and/or SQL Server</li>
<li>Knowledge of SQL language a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge of Linux systems a plus</li>
<li>Troubleshoot/repair hardware/software problems as required</li>
<li>Work independently and with a team in a relaxed business environment</li>
<li>Assist in desktop, laptop, and network printer deployment</li>
<li>Maintain professional image</li>
<li>Display a positive attitude and willingness to work with the customer to solve their needs</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Associates Degree or relevant job experience</li>
<li>Must have a positive attitude, and willingness to do what it takes to succeed</li>
<li>Must be able to work after-hours or weekends (paid on-call) if needed</li>
<li>1-3 years experience with PC hardware and OS support</li>
<li>Good working knowledge of overall personal computing hardware and software</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (both written and oral)</li>
<li>No travel is required</li>
</ul>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<p>Upon meeting eligibility requirements, you may elect to participate in the company's Medical/Dental/Vision plans as well as 401(K) retirement plan, and paid vacations.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Microsoft Office: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Windows: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Remote Access Software: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>SQL: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>

<ul><li>Associate (Preferred)</li></ul>
