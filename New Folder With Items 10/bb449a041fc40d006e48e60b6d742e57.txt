<b>Job Summary</b>
</div>
<div><div><div><div>
<div><div>
<div>The Technical Engineering Director leads the development and maintains the technical knowledge of the department. Detailed knowledge of both the technologies being used by our clients and the technology that we need to deploy to meet our client’s needs. Leads the development of processes and procedures within their assigned discipline.</div>
</div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Mentors, trains, and develops department staff on technical skills and knowledge</li>
<li>Leads discipline training (internally and externally)</li>
<li>Provides leadership on Quality Assurance by maintaining and implementing industry standards, best practice and company procedures</li>
<li>Leads in the vetting of tools, software and outside resources specific to the discipline</li>
<li>Leads the development of the project execution plan for the discipline</li>
<li>Identifies resources needed required to carry out design and construction work in the discipline and work in collaboration with Design Management staff to assemble this talent</li>
<li>Routinely holds technical training and mentorship of the department</li>
<li>Maintains memberships in industry specific technical societies, and routinely speaks/presents at society conferences</li>
<li>Ability to absorb technical knowledge in markets outside of his/her core</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to influence internal management and customers</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate with staff across remote offices and time zone</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Must be a strong team player with an ability to build effective working relationships with individuals, department leads, and client representatives</li>
<li>Ability to mentor and train staff</li>
<li>Works to build and maintain positive and productive working relationships both internal and external</li>
<li>Provides input to the development of engineering budget and schedule to meet requirements</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
