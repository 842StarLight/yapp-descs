<b>AS THE ENTERPRISE SALES EXECUTIVE YOU WILL:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Independently identify and develop prospective clients.</li>
<li>Passionately prospect into targeted accounts and create interest in Dispatch's product offerings.</li>
<li>Develop and close multi-year SaaS and services contracts.</li>
<li>Negotiate and close contracts that result in predictable, committed revenue.</li>
<li>Elicit prospects' and customers' business, functional and technical requirements.</li>
<li>Find opportunities to expand into additional functional business units.</li>
<li>Understand and address prospect and customer questions and objections.</li>
<li>Show the value of our best-in-class field service management and customer experience software.</li>
<li>Connect, engage and motivate in conversations, presentations and written communications.</li>
</ul>
<b>WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>An experienced enterprise sales executive (enterprise SaaS preferred) with more than 4 years in the field.</li>
<li>Accustomed to selling $250K+ ARR deals.</li>
<li>Known as a high-achiever with a track record of exceeding sales targets and typically perform at the top 10% of your peers.</li>
<li>Adept at handling complex enterprise sales cycles.</li>
<li>Technically savvy enough to have a working knowledge of how software gets built.</li>
<li>Able to present to, interact with, and consult with senior management at Fortune 500 companies.</li>
<li>Excited to thrive in a fast-paced, high pressure, start-up culture that expects phenomenal things every day.</li>
</ul>
<b>SKILLS WE SEEK:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience preferred</li>
<li>4+ years of experience in SaaS or software sales (Enterprise).</li>
<li>Proven ability to build a sales pipeline through effectively hunting, prospecting, cold calling, networking and lead generation activities.</li>
<li>Natural ability to run fast while exercising sound judgment.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Salesforce (preferable).</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and sense of commitment is a requirement.</li>
<li>Motivated and comfortable working remotely in a home office.</li>
<li>Excellent communication, presentation, and customer relationship building skills.</li>
<li>Ability to leverage the extended team to succeed.</li>
</ul>
