<p>Advantech has an immediate opening within our key account sales force, as a Key Account Manager located in the Minnesota market. The ideal candidate will be aggressive and self-motivated, exhibiting the ability to manage assigned key accounts. The candidate will be required to identify and review engineering specifications, coordinate and propose product configurations, negotiate sales and service contracts, while interfacing with OEM customers and channel sales partners.</p>
