<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Full-Time</p>
<p>
Radio Mankato is seeking a new Account Executive to work in the Mankato area. This person should first and foremost be a team player with a good attitude and goal oriented. Attention to detail and excellent communication skills are also a plus. Sales experience is not necessary, but preferred. The hours for this position are typically a normal work week, Monday – Friday, 8am – 5pm with select weekend work for special events sponsored by the Radio Stations. This position involves, but is not limited to, working with local businesses to promote their brand and increase their sales. This is achieved by (1) developing and maintaining solid client relationships and (2) helping the client to create a comprehensive radio advertising campaign. Radio Mankato is a unique cluster of radio stations located in the heart of Southern Minnesota. We are a community driven and family focused radio syndicate thanks to it’s dedicated local ownership of over 65 years founded by Don Linder, succeeded by John Linder. In an age of syndicated, chain-owned radio; Radio Mankato broadcasts live, bold, and local entertainment. Our content is full of personality with the area’s most seasoned radio professionals. Delivering not only the entertainment that listeners want, but also the information they need with the best local weather, news, farm information, and sports coverage.</p>
<p>
If you meet these minimum qualifications and are interested in this position, please send a cover letter, salary requirements and resume to E-mail: jobs@radiomankato.com EOE</p>
