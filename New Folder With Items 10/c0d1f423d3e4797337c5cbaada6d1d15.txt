<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Administer all facets of accounts payable and the financial elements. Forecast and analyze related budgets, audit expenses and execute special projects.
</p>
<p>AR and AP duties and responsibilities are essential job functions and requirements and are subject to possible modification.</p>
<p>
Assist the Financial Services department in creating timely and accurate financial statements; administer PEO Franchise billing and special financial projects.
</p>
<ul><li>Process month-end balancing and journal entries.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complete the month-end process.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>General ledger analysis of accounts.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare monthly Standard Reporting Package.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Extend credit to customer and handle wire transfers.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare sales tax returns for multiple states.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Process expense reports for employees.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare weekly inventory reports for Inventory Management</li></ul>
<p><b>
Essential Functions and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Adhere to Kate Somerville quality procedures and health / safety standards</p>
<p>
The responsibilities of the staff accountant include, but are not limited to, the following:
 </p>
<ul>
<li>Daily and weekly balancing of cash.
 </li>
<li>Maintaining document retention files for all departments.
 </li>
<li>Coordinating approval process of all accounts payable invoices via department heads.
 </li>
<li>Coordinating and inputting payables into PS, batch summary report balancing and submission for approval, verification, GM approval and posting by Business Manager (BM).
 </li>
<li>Processing all payables for imaging via MG Shared Services.
 </li>
<li>Maintaining open and paid vendor files.
 </li>
<li>Coordinating new vendor setup.
 </li>
<li>Reconciling vendor statements and handling payment complaints or discrepancies.
 </li>
<li>Preparing daily deposits and cash reports and taking deposit to the bank.
 </li>
<li>Reviewing all expense reports for accuracy and proper expense disclosure and classification. Reporting any variances to BM.
 </li>
<li>Working closely with AR/payroll clerk to assure the accuracy of payroll, billing and all corresponding journal entries.
 </li>
<li>Performing monthly bank reconciliation for depository account.
 </li>
<li>Making month-end standard journal entries, including amortization of all prepaid assets.
 </li>
<li>Reviewing periodically during the month the general ledger for input accuracy and verifying that accounts have been reviewed for content.
 </li>
<li>Completing cash reconciliation and intercompany balancing report.
 </li>
<li>Reviewing outstanding check log for possible abandoned checks.
 </li>
<li>Maintaining station current and expired contract files.
 </li>
<li>Preparing month-end payroll journal entry balancing with media general intercompany entries.
 </li>
<li>Running all required month-end reports and files according to document retention guidelines
 </li>
<li>Putting together GL book each month with required backup and signatures.
 </li>
<li>Preparing Use Tax Report on a monthly basis and maintaining schedule of taxable use tax items.
 </li>
<li>Preparing Accounts Receivable Report detailing all past dues and balancing GL to AR.
 </li>
<li>Preparing month-end balance sheet review and reconciling any differences with BM.
 </li>
<li>Preparing Month-End Capital Cash Flow and PPE Statement.
 </li>
<li>Reconciling capital expenditures per COR’s.
 </li>
<li>Reviewing and reconciling petty cash vouchers to administrative assistant’s reconciliation, replenishing petty cash on an as needed basis.
 </li>
<li>Responding to inquiries from GM and department head regarding expenses, budgets, etc.
 </li>
<li>Auditing for compliance:
 </li>
<li>Preparing payroll and inputting in the absence of payroll/HR clerk.
 </li>
<li>Administer benefits in the absence of payroll/HR clerk.
 </li>
<li>Assisting BM in all budget preparation, input and balancing.
 </li>
<li>Performing other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills, Experience and Education Required</b></p>
<p>
Strong verbal and written communication skills</p>
<p>
Must be able to work independently.
</p>
<p>Must be detail oriented and organized.
</p>
<p>Must be able to work on many different projects at one time.
</p>
<p>Accounting software</p>
<p>
Bachelor's Degree in Accounting or Business preferred.</p>
