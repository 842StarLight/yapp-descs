A day in your life as a Dealer Banking Assistant:</div>
<div>
Under the direction of the Manager/Supervisor, this position is responsible for performing a variety of functions as required to meet departmental objectives. Assists in business unit’s compliance with applicable laws, regulations, regulatory requirements and Bank policies and procedures, including but not limited to those related to Fair Banking, Anti-Money Laundering laws and regulations, Bank Secrecy Act and USA PATRIOT Act.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We’ll give you these important responsibilities…</div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Performs various functional tasks including but not limited to: indirect loan or lease contract processing, indirect loan or lease documentation, dealer or customer correspondence. Handles large volumes of work quickly and accurately and manages tasks in rotational assignments.</div></li>
<li><div>
Communicates with internal/external clients in a professional manner. Identifies customer needs. Utilizes key Excellence in Sales and Service techniques and tools to meet sales objectives and financial goals and to provide the highest professional level of service to internal and external clients.</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsible for maintaining, enhancing and deepening relationships with assigned Dealer clients as well as developing new business.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provides Team Support and assists in cross training of staff.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintains full knowledge of products and services. Sells and cross-sells products and services.</div></li>
</ul></div>
