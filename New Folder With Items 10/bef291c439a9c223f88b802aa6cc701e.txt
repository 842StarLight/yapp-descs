<h2>Marketing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>GotPrint, a rapidly-expanding online printing company with multiple facilities across the United States, has an opening for an experienced Marketing Analyst who will be responsible for analyzing various aspects of our advertising and marketing campaigns, ensuring campaign objectives are being met, and carrying out quantitative and qualitative research to produce campaign evaluation reports.<br>
</p>
<p>
 From these reports we will be able to improve the effectiveness of our campaigns based on consumer performance, market needs, pricing, customer incentives, buying habits, online website traffic, conversion rates, cost-per-acquisition analysis and customer LTV/AOV fluctuations. Analyst will also help determine positioning in the marketplace by researching competitors and analyzing pricing and marketing methods. Must be able to interpret and forecast future trends based on historical data.<br>
</p>
<p>
 Additionally, the position will have a high degree of visibility across all company departments and will be responsible for ensuring all projects are delivered in a detailed, timely, and concise manner.<br>
</p>
<p>
 <b>Tasks and Responsibilities:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
 <li>Monitor and forecast marketing and sales trends</li>
 <li>Measure the effectiveness of campaigns and strategies</li>
 <li>Devise and evaluate methods for collecting data</li>
 <li>Suggest research methods appropriate to pre-defined criteria</li>
 <li>Design and use qualitative and quantitative research tools</li>
 <li>Analyze campaign performances and research results</li>
 <li>Convert complex data into understandable tables, graphs, and written reports</li>
 <li>Work with various marketing channel managers</li>
 <li>Prepare reports and present results to management</li>
 <li>Ensure deadlines are met</li>
 <li>All other duties, as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
 <b>Qualifications:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
 <li>Minimum of a Bachelor's Degree in related field</li>
 <li>5+ years professional experience in data or financial analysis, advertising, online and offline marketing, in-depth analysis, trending, forecasting and budgets.</li>
 <li>Articulate, strategic, and pragmatic problem-solver with ability to excel in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment.</li>
 <li>Excellent ability to write, speak, and use images to clearly and persuasively communicate with both technical and non-technical audiences.</li>
 <li>Proven ability in reporting and analysis.</li>
 <li>Must be familiar with Google Analytics, PPC, media buying, reporting, ROI driven strategies and general business analysis.</li>
 <li>Must be proficient with PC or MAC computer.</li>
 <li>Must be familiar with shortcuts.</li>
 <li>Strong project management skills with the ability to multi-task and deliver against strict deadlines</li>
 <li>The ability to be flexible, multi-task and work effectively in a team environment is required.</li>
 <li>Keen attention to detail</li>
<br>
</ul>
