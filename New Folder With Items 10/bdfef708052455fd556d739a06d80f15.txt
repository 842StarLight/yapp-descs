<h2>Project Coordinator - Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Department: Strategic Initiatives / Division: Enterprise Project Management Office</p>
<p>This position will provide project management support for all new projects ran through the EPMO, including new products, partners and technical solutions. The Project coordinator is a key support role to coordinate and document end-to-end operational pieces of various Sales initiatives. The position is based in the company’s Foothill Ranch, CA headquarters.</p>
<p>Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>General project management support of various sales initiatives</li>
<li>Work with Sales reps, Underwriting team and external brokers to develop and launch new products</li>
<li>Discuss, assess and document partner or product requirements</li>
<li>Create and maintain project plans reflecting current open items and progressing statuses</li>
<li>Track and communicate business needs, action items and statuses</li>
<li>Conference call and meeting coordination, including documenting agendas and minutes</li>
<li>Interact with Reporting, Finance and internal Operations teams to identify preferred system setup and business workflows</li>
<li>Document established workflows utilizing Visio</li>
<li>Coordinate technical aspects needed with the IT department and ensure development, testing and production releases are in line with desired timelines</li>
<li>Continuously document and share decisions agreed upon and pending open items with relevant parties to ensure overall seamless implementations</li>
<li>Interact with internal executives and external partners to build relationships and become a supporting contact for new partnerships and product roll-outs</li>
<li>Meet regularly with Global sales teams and senior management to prioritize sales pipeline initiatives and provide status updates</li>
<li>Engage with the Sales Portal team to be involved in user-acceptance testing and broker training of online sales portals</li>
<li>Gain a thorough understanding of the All GBG internal applications to be able to provide ad hoc support and give training to brokers and reps as needed</li>
<li>Administer new API or EDI client setups, testing and address issues</li>
<li>Work to streamline steps and timelines needed to launch new products</li>
<li>Various other Program Management initiatives as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years’ experience in a corporate setting working with multiple departments</li>
<li>Insurance Industry experience preferred</li>
<li>Project coordination or management background preferred</li>
<li>Process and detail oriented with good writing skills</li>
<li>Self-starter that has excellent time management and communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to document and share information effectively with all types of employees</li>
<li>Understanding of partner data exchanges through EDI or APIs and SFTP sites</li>
<li>Comfort level with Word, Excel, Visio and other task-tracking software</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required, a concentration in business-related field preferred</li>
<li>Work schedule flexibility to accommodate conference calls in varying time zones</li>
</ul>
