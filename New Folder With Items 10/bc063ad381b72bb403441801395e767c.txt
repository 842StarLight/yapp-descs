<p></p>iHeartMedia Stations<br>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Job Summary:
</b></p>Assists the promotions or marketing departments with daily activities that promote the station(s), clients or events.<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Coordinates and attends client meetings with sellers and sales managers as needed to plan events and event logistics.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborates with multiple departments to create and execute promotions such as remotes, events, van hits and other street team activities from start to finish.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drives promotional vehicles.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs basic office administrative functions and updates station web site.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conducts on-site promotions, and handles clients and listeners.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sets up and runs audio and other types of equipment; hangs banners and other staging elements.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Records events (i.e. photos, videos, audio and social media measures for station promotions).
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sets up, breaks down and transports promotional event equipment as required.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepares contest rules, waivers, and release forms for on air, digital, social media and other contests.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervises prize inventory and in-studio prize sheets as well as awarding of prizes at events.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>May coordinate and oversee on-site appearances, remotes and events.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>May be responsible for all winner prize fulfillment and release forms.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Advanced skills in Microsoft Office, Photoshop and social media platforms
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent organizational skills; ability to prioritize and effectively manage time
</li></ul>
<ul><li>High work standards and degree of attention to detail
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Problem solving and decision making
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Project management from start to finish; assumes responsibility &amp; accountability for assignments and tasks
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Actively listens; clearly and effectively conveys information; demonstrates effective business writing skills; shows excellent grasp of grammar
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Exhibits good interpersonal skills; collaborates with others; maintains composure when faced with difficult situations and personalities
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent driving record
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Physical ability to stand for multiple hours and lift or move 40-pound objects
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Work Experience
</p>
<ul><li>1-3 years’ experience in outdoor promotions and/or marketing and/or customer service
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Education
</p>
<ul><li>High school diploma; 4-year college degree preferred (emphasis in Communications, Advertising or Marketing)
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Certifications
</p>
<ul><li>Valid driver’s license
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proof of insurability<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Location
</b></p>Harrisburg, PA: 600 Corporate Circle, Suite 100 and 200, 17110<br>
<p>
