<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h1>
<br>
Responsibilities:
<br>
<br>
This role will report into the Solution Delivery organization. The IT Solution Delivery Customer Facing Analyst will support the applications that support the businesss Quote to Cash transnational processes, with primary focus on the BD Medication Management Solutions MMS Equipment &amp; Subscription business unit. This person will partner with the IT Business Relationship Manager, IT peers to manage the delivery of approved and prioritized projects in the customer facing portfolio; they will also work with BDs service providers and vendors to manage the delivery of operations and projects, ensuring performance metrics are achieved and projects are delivered meeting business functional expectations along with being on time, budget and a quality product the first time. In addition, this person will ensure the appropriate GxP, SOX and other legal and regulatory processes are supported, and the necessary documentation completed along with Application Life cycle Management for the suite of applications supporting the MMS customer facing business processes.
