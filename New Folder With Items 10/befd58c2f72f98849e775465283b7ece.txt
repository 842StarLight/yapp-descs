<h2>Senior Network Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>John Drake &amp; Associates is an IT consultancy, hosted and managed service provider in the Chicagoland area.</p>
<p>Senior Network Engineer responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Support design, implementation and maintenance of network infrastructures for JDA and clients</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Oversight of the administration of Cisco routers, switches, and firewalls, network monitoring tools, and anti-spam appliances</li>
<li>Oversight of the administration of network and system monitoring tools</li>
<li>Maintain network security policies and have knowledge of current security best practices and tools for a highly secure and available production environment</li>
<li>Network systems planning and diagramming (Visio) for documentation</li>
<li>Use of protocol analyzers to identify and resolve network issues and monitor network usage trends</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Work with telecom providers to manage existing telecommunication circuits, troubleshoot outages, and install service upgrades</li>
<li>Work with sales team to develop proposals and provide technical input and insight during meetings.</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Experience with LAN hardware including patch panels, Fiber Optic, Cat5e and Cat 6 wiring</li>
<li>A strong working knowledge of technologies surrounding a network infrastructure</li>
<li>Experience working with, designing, and supporting IP Telephony, Linux, Microsoft Server, VMware virtual environments, specifically datacenter networking</li>
<li>Strong experience in working with firewalls</li>
<li>Excellent documentation skills</li>
<li>Self-starters with a good work ethic, the ability to work independently or as a team and a desire to come up with the best solutions for our clients</li>

<li>Experience with wireless access points and controllers</li>
<li>Great customer service skills</li>
