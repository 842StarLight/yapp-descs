<b>Position Description:</b> This position performs a key role in the introduction of new products into the company. In addition to the sourcing and purchasing of components for new products/orders, this position creates and maintains Item Data, Bills of Materials, and assists with the analysis of component characteristics.
<br><br>
Strategic Buyer (FT, day shift)
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Obtains materials, components, equipment and services in a cost effective and timely manner. Identifies potential sources and works to qualify alternatives.</li>
<li>Analyzes maintains and executes Materials Requirement Plan (MRP). Identify and appropriately address material supply issues.</li>
<li>Maintains data integrity of MRP planning parameters. Continuously plans, schedules, and manages material supply to equal demand.</li>
<li>Supports customers’ business needs using standard processes where possible and customized solutions where necessary. Ability to present and communicate with customers in a professional manner.</li>
<li>Investigates and analyses global sources for product sourcing and availability.</li>
<li>Optimizes overall cost of materials and makes sourcing decisions based on overall total cost. Optimizes Purchase Price Variance (PPV) by reducing cost of materials and by limiting unfavorable PPV.</li>
<li>Reviews Standard costed BOM output for cost drivers, unidentified parts, and unapproved suppliers and takes the appropriate actions.</li>
<li>Quotes unidentified materials and obtains samples when necessary.</li>
<li>Transfers quote data to the appropriate buyer when a PO is placed, promoting a seamless transition.</li>
<li>Works with the Customer Quotation Specialist on setting priorities and improving the quote process.</li>
<li>Responsible for Standard cost setting, management and updates.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Requirements / Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must be a US person.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent work experience. Electronics manufacturing industry experience a plus. APICS or NAPM certification desired.</li>
<li>Experience with ERP, MRP and MRP II systems.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and analytical skills along with solid problem solving skills. Ability to work with and lead teams.</li>
<li>Computer knowledge is required such as: Microsoft Office: Word and Excel (used for manipulation of data using v-lookup, pivot tables, etc.) Database software experience is a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to perform/prioritize multiple tasks with attention to detail required.</li>
<li>Knowledge of DFMA (Design for Manufacturing and Assembly) is a plus.</li>
</ul>
