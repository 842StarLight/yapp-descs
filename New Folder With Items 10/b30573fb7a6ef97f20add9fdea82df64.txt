<h2>Finance Manager - Restaurant Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Operations Finance Manager is responsible for providing operational and financial analysis support to executives and the field management team on strategic planning, budgeting, forecasting, key performance indicators, variance analysis and other ad hoc analyses. This position also prepares weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annual management reports. This position will also interact with various functional areas, including Operations, Marketing, Accounting, and IT.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><div>
<ul><li>Create and manage financial modeling and reporting tools for various functional areas</li></ul>
<ul><li>Compare and analyze financial and other operating results against established plans and targets</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyze financial impact of marketing and operations initiatives
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create and distribute routine as well as ad hoc reports and analyses
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate results via presentations to the executive team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supports the company store annual operating plan process by developing and maintaining financial</li></ul>
<p>
 models and tools, gathering business intelligence, and partnering with operations to set store level plans</p>
<p>
 that are in line with company goals
</p>
<ul><li>Partners with Operations and other functional departments to create financial forecasts, evaluates</li></ul>
<p>
 and reports on actual versus projected performance, and identifies risk and opportunities to assist in driving results
</p>
<ul><li>Build, maintain and distribute dashboards to assist with tracking and reporting of key performance indicators</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review trends and exception reports to identify and communicate opportunities to various functional areas</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sales, Gross Profit, Labor, and Store Operating Expense modeling and reporting.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Theoretical versus actual cost of goods sold analysis</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sales and expense planning during the annual budget process.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform ad-hoc analysis and other job-related duties as needed</li></ul>
