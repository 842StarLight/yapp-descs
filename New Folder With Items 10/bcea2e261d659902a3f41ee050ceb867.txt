<h2>AMBASSADOR-MORONGO REWARDS PT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>A member of a “sales centric” team, focusing on identifying and referring high worth players to Player Development. Establish and maintain Morongo’s BEST standard through constant and direct contact with guests. Answer questions, provide assistance and educate guests on the benefits of using the Morongo Rewards card, available promotions, and general amenities while on the Casino floor.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meets, greets and delivers exceptional service to all guests.</li>
<li>Maintains relationships with known players and develop relationships with potential players.</li>
<li>Seeks out and “sells” guests on the value of signing up for a Morongo Rewards Card.</li>
<li>Issues card reprints, PIN changes and comps to players as needed.</li>
<li>Educates guests on available promotions and general casino amenities.</li>
<li>Assists all other casino departments with the issuance of players cards for established and new guests.</li>
<li>Assists guests with acquiring necessary assistance from other departments as needed, example: need slot attendant, drink service, security, EMT’s.</li>
<li>Assists with safety concerns occurring on the floor and notify appropriate department.</li>
<li>Performs other job related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements:</b></h3>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and ability required.</p>
<p>Computer skills, excellent verbal and written skills. Working knowledge of ACSC, AS400, Group Tracker and Kman software. Ability to maintain strict confidentiality of classified information. Ability to tactfully and diplomatically interact with all guests. Ability to handle high stress situations with composure. Ability to speak to large or small groups. Must possess excellent business sense and high professional ethics. Must possess a high regard for guest service. Must be self-motivated, goal and detail oriented, and possess strong organizational skills. Required to act as a sales representative and illustrate the benefits of the Morongo Rewards Card.</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<p>Casino and six (6) months of Rewards Counter experience preferred. High School diploma or GED required.</p>
<p><b>LICENSES, CERTIFICATES, REGISTRATIONS:</b></p>
<p>Must undergo and successfully pass a background investigation to obtain and maintain a gaming license issued by the Morongo Gaming Agency.</p>
<p><b>LANGUAGE SKILLS:</b></p>
<p>Must be able to read, write &amp; speak English. Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions and procedure manuals. Must have the ability to write routine reports and correspondence, and to speak effectively with guests and team members.</p>
<p><b>MATHEMATICAL SKILLS:</b></p>
<p>Ability to add, subtract, multiply and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions and decimals.</p>
<p><b>REASONING ABILITY:</b></p>
<p>Must have the ability to independently problem-solve challenging situations. Must have the ability to carry out instructions furnished in written, oral or diagram form and have the judgment to decide which procedures apply to a variety of situations. Must reason with people effectively yet cordially.</p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</b></p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the team member is regularly required to stand or walk during the duration of their shift. The team member frequently is required to reach with arms and use hands to manipulate or feel. The team member might occasionally lift and/or move up to 25 pounds. This position requires the ability to hear and speak on the telephone and vision abilities required by this job include close vision, color vision, depth perception, and ability to adjust focus.</p>
<p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT:</b></p>
<p>The duties of this position are typically performed on the casino floor. The casino environment has moderate to loud noise levels and is a smoking environment.</p>
<p><b>WORKING HOURS:</b></p>
<p>Because we are open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, most positions require flexibility in scheduling and may require team members be available for evenings, weekends, holidays and special events. A typical workweek and normal work schedules may vary based on business needs and may include scheduled shifts Monday through Sunday. Position may require overtime.</p>
<p><b>INDIAN PREFERENCE:</b></p>
<p>We are an Equal Opportunity Employer while practicing Native American preference according to law.</p>
