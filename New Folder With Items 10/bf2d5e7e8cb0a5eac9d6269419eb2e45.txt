<h2>Lead Reg Performance Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Bring your drive for excellence, team orientation and customer commitment to Independence; help us renew and reimagine our business and shape the future of health care. Our organization is looking to diversify, grow, innovate and serve, and we are looking for committed, empowered learning-oriented people to join our team. If this describes you, we want to speak with you.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Summary:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The lead position is responsible for supporting and backing up the Manager of Regulatory Performance Management. The lead will work independently and autonomously with minimal management oversight. They will own the Sales Allegation and Marketing Review processes and make recommendations to management when needed. They will also manage the compliance process and workflows for marketing material submissions and supervise Sales Allegation investigations</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Principle Duties:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<ul><li> The lead must be able to maintain productive relationships with leaders from business areas across the organization while supervising processes and workflows, delegating tasks to staff as needed.</li></ul>
<li>
 The lead will be a recognized expert in the Medicare Marketing guidelines and provide compliance recommendations to management and executive leadership.</li>
<li>
 Interpersonal skills, effective communication and ability to work independently are required.</li>
<li>
 The lead must be able to provide feedback and mentor associates to drive towards better more efficient processes and managing staff production and functions for Sales Allegations and Marketing Review</li>
<li>
 The lead will be responsible for ensuring the Sales Allegation process is a complete and thorough process allowing Independence to hold our agents to the highest standard and intents of the Medicare Marketing Guidelines. Review Sales Allegations submissions from the Appeals and Grievances area and decide if a complaint should be accepted and investigated as a Sales Allegation per the Medicare Marketing Guidelines or sent back to the business area.</li>
<li>
 Independently Determine Sales Violation category and severity.</li>
<li>
 Lead the investigations process ensure complete and thorough investigations and determine disciplinary actions and make recommendations to management.</li>
<li>
 Assigning tasks to investigators to ensure thorough and complete investigations and adherence to compliance timelines.</li>
<li>
 Provide feedback and mentoring to investigators improving the overall quality and timeliness of investigations.</li>
<li>
 Supervise product deliverables for Sales Allegation investigations.</li>
<li>
 The lead will work independently with minimal oversight to ensure the marketing review and submission processes meet all CMS expectations for timeliness and adherence to CMS rules.</li>
<li>
 Assign tasks to Senior Regulatory Coordinators to review and submit materials to CMS as needed. Independently review materials, determine acceptability to CMS and submit for approval with minimal if any rejections.</li>
<li>
 Manage project deliverables for marketing review during AEP and other periods of peak volume.</li>
<li>
 Deciding when materials should be escalated or expedited and providing recommendations to senior management.</li>
<li>
 Work with business areas providing expert advice pertaining to changes in guidance or rules and communicating with executives whenever marketing changes are requested.</li>
<li>
 Determine and deciding when sales initiatives or enterprise initiatives or member communications can be completed internally or submitted to CMS for review.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
</div>
<ul><li> Independently Design and develop Desk Level Procedures and Job Aids<br>
</li></ul>

<div></div>

<li>
Experience with the Medicare Marketing Guidelines preferred</li>
<li>
Experience supervising people, coaching and mentoring, Proofreading, Excellent verbal and written communications</li>
</ul>
