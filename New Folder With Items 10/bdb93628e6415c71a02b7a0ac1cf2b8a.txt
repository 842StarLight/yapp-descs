<h2>Communications Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB PURPOSE</b></p>
<p>Member of the marketing team, the Communications Manager is responsible for all the communication mix.</p>
<p><b>MAIN DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Define a comprehensive communication strategy, in line with the overall company and marketing strategy.</li>
<li>Implement this strategy adequately in the various elements of the communication mix (advertising, advertorials, on-line, PR, event marketing, etc.) and assign the budget accordingly.</li>
<li>Be the custodian of the Aireloom and Kluft brands (brands attributes, brands identity, key visuals, etc.) and adapt them over time to keep them relevant, desirable and up to date.</li>
<li>Act internally and externally as a brand ambassador.</li>
<li>Plan and execute the appropriate media strategy and verify post campaign effectiveness.</li>
<li>Define and execute a comprehensive digital strategy (websites, digital campaigns, social media program, etc.).</li>
<li>Support the company’s network of retailers in their own communications plans, providing them with the appropriate tools to ensure the communication of a consistent brand image.</li>
<li>Define and execute a PR program to reach effectively the company’s trade and end consumer audiences.</li>
<li>Develop the brochures and leaflets required to promote the brands’ activities (product launch, trade show, etc.).</li>
<li>Produce all brand and product imagery and copy used by the company and its partners.</li>
<li>Design and implement other consumer marketing actions such as targeted event or mailings.</li>
<li>Manage the Marketing Requests related to communications topics.</li>
<li>Manage the different agencies and other communications partners (creative, media, PR).</li>
<li>Conduct regular brand review to benchmark Aireloom and Kluft communications against their major competitors (positioning, execution, share of voice, etc.) and initiate corrective actions.</li>
<li>Manage the communications budget.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KPIs</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Net sales and gross margin.</li>
<li>Google metrics.</li>
<li>Maximizing the numbers and quality of projects within budget constraints.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>DIRECT REPORT</b></p>
<ul><li>No direct reports.</li></ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>University or technical degree.</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of relevant experience in communications (company or agency).</li>
<li>Proven experience in digital strategy and programs.</li>
<li>Excellent creative and communications skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a small and lean organization, hands-on, result oriented, team player and leadership skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WORK PLACE &amp; TRAVEL</b></p>
<ul><li>Office in Rancho Cucamonga, with trips for meetings with agencies &amp; clients and exhibitions (20%).</li></ul>
