<h2>Virtual Reality Guest Experience Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<div>
<div>Deliver superior service while connecting with our guests through interactive storytelling that brings them deeper into a fully immersive virtual reality experience.</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<div>
<p>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</p>
<p>
All associates may be considered for cross-training; some may be assigned duties in one or more areas at management discretion. General responsibilities for all positions include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Exhibit excellent guest service skills.</li>
<li>
Act as brand ambassador: share information with potential guests about this unique virtual reality experience in a manner that encourages them to take the journey that day.</li>
<li>
Comfortable interacting with technology.</li>
<li>
Present a calm demeanor that deters others from engaging in disruptive conduct, while encouraging a positive interaction with guests.</li>
<li>
Answer guest questions courteously and accurately or quickly direct them to the appropriate resource.</li>
<li>
Work effectively with supervisors and co-workers.</li>
<li>
Execute opening and closing technical procedures.</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively multitask as needed, including but not limited to greeting guests, tearing and scanning tickets, checking IDs, directing guests and answering guest questions.</li>
<li>
Perform functions related to operation of Virtual Reality (VR) experience, including use of tablet technology (Microsoft, Apple, HP).</li>
<li>
Facilitate ticketing, check-in, and other guest service needs using various sales systems.</li>
<li>
Ensure working order of attractions hardware and software.</li>
<li>
Assist guests in putting on/taking off required VR gear and describe the use during the show.</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask and effectively respond to changing environments, circumstances, and situations while operating the VR show.</li>
<li>
Ensure the proper use of safety practices and procedures while operating the VR show.</li>
<li>
Ensure the security of all cash, receipts, and tickets.</li>
<li>
Properly care for guest and show equipment devices, including maintaining equipment hygiene standards.</li>
<li>
Perform nightly custodial duties as necessary, including but not limited to vacuuming, mopping, sanitizing restroom fixtures, etc.</li>
<li>
Clean and maintain exterior and interior of facility including restrooms.</li>
<li>
Perform daily stocking and maintenance duties, as directed by management.</li>
<li>
Ability to work and meet deadlines with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>
Follow all procedures to ensure a safe work environment, as well as the safety of our guests.</li>
<li>
Follow instructions on safe use of all chemicals/cleaning materials.</li>
<li>
Uphold AMC's <i>Business Practice Standards</i> and ensure compliance with company procedures and programs.</li>
<li>
Maintain regular personal attendance for all scheduled shifts.</li>
</ul>
