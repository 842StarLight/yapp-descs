<h2>Visual Merchandiser/Sales Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Furniture Row in Rapid City, SD is looking for a full-time Visual Merchandiser/Sales Associate.</b>
</p>
<p>This role will entail the duties of a Visual Merchandiser, as well as the role of a Sales Associate.</p>
<p><b>
General Objectives</b>:<br>
After 30 years of experience in the business we know that talent comes from a variety of different experiences and backgrounds. We know that our successful candidates have a high standard of integrity, an excitement for building relationships with customers, and the commitment to success.</p>
<p>
At Furniture Row, our visual merchandiser will implement and maintain appropriate floor displays, in accordance with the Visual Merchandising Guidelines. Develop and grow accents sales (plants, pictures, lamps, mirrors, rugs, etc.)</p>
<p>
The visual merchandiser should have a strong understanding of the marketing and visual merchandising budget and work within the budget parameters. You will implement visual merchandising displays and fixture presentations. You will be involved in preparing for and presenting at retail meetings.</p>

<p><b>Tasks and requirements of the Visual Merchandiser/Sales Associate include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communication with store leadership to discuss and plan floor design, layout formats, display points, items to be displayed, and creative design flow of the store merchandise.</li>
<li>Design window or internal displays based on a theme, style/trend, and advertising components.</li>
<li>Obtain accessories in congruence with styles and trends while constructing displays to include: painting, wiring, design, layout, etc.</li>
<li>Actively optimize lighting to display merchandise to reflect style/trends as well as showcasing the presentations to maximize customer engagement.</li>
<li>Arrange ticketing and signage to enhance presentation of merchandise while reflecting advertising, promotional opportunities, and customer engagement.</li>
<li>Set-up, maintain, and remove displays based on store leadership and advertising trends.</li>
<li>Selling on the sales floor - We Will Train You!</li>
</ul>
