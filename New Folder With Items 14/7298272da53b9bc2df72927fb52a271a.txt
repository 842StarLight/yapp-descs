<h2>Financial Planning and Analysis Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Come join our family at In-N-Out Burger!
</b></p>
<p>We have an excellent opportunity for a full-time <b>Financial Planning and Analysis Manager</b> in our <b>Irvine, CA</b> office. The Financial Planning and Analysis Manager will be responsible for overseeing financial analysis, modeling, forecasting, long range planning and reporting efforts to support company management and ensure efficient financial operations.</p>
<p><b>
General Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze current and past trends in key performance indicators including all areas of revenue, cost of sales, expenses and capital expenditures.</li>
<li>
Lead company budgeting process</li>
<li>
Monitor performance indicators, highlighting trends and analyzing causes of unexpected variance.</li>
<li>
Perform reporting and analysis to support the business.</li>
<li>
Develop financial models and analyses to support strategic initiatives.</li>
<li>
Analyze complex financial information and reports to provide accurate and timely financial recommendations to management for decision making purposes.</li>
<li>
Support management team and departments heads with in-depth analysis.</li>
<li>
Perform new site analysis, new store financial forecasting, project budgeting/reporting and return on investment for Real Estate.</li>
<li>
Manage capital planning and utilization.</li>
<li>
Research industry insights and create benchmarking.</li>
<li>
Lead business case development and testing analysis.</li>
<li>
Supervise a Senior Financial Analyst.</li>
<li>
Implement and manage financial planning and reporting tools, as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Work Schedule + Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Full-time, Exempt, Benefits</li>
<li>
Office hours: M-F, 8:00 am – 5:00 pm</li>
<li>
Some weekend and holiday availability required
</li>
<li>Travel is required: Travel between corporate facilities (California: Irvine, Baldwin Park and Lathrop) as well as to satellite offices in Nevada, Arizona, and Utah
</li>
<li>The frequency and extent of travel will vary depending on the volume and nature of positions in each facility</li>
<li>
Excellent Benefits, to include Medical, Dental, Vision, Life Insurance, Profit Sharing Plan and 401(k) Plan</li>
</ul>
