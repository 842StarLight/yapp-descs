<h2>Marketing and Communications Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Goodwin House Incorporated (GHI) seeks a Marketing &amp; Communications Specialist who combines strong project management skills with an entrepreneurial spirit to join a highly collaborative marketing and communications team. Our growing organization is leading the field of senior living and health care services as we innovate and redefine aging. We have a story to tell, a voice to be heard and a passion to share.</p>
<p>As the Marketing &amp; Communications Specialist, you will tell our story, lift our voice and spread our passion. You will contribute directly to extending awareness and managing the reputation of the Goodwin House brand, products and service lines through content creation, digital marketing, social media, public relations and internal communications.</p>
<p>About GHI:</p>
<p>GHI has been enhancing the lives and expanding the possibilities of aging for older adults since 1967. Mission-driven, independent and not-for-profit, we are known for our integrity, innovation and excellence. We pride ourselves on providing high-quality service and exceptional personalized care. Our family of services includes: two life plan communities – Goodwin House Alexandria and Goodwin House Bailey’s Crossroads; an innovative continuing care at home program – Goodwin House at Home; and a growing group of specialized care services that include home healthcare, therapy, hospice and palliative care. GHI advances our mission through a 501(c)(3) supporting foundation that provides financial support to current and future residents in need and to fund programs dedicated to well-being and vibrant living as we age. GHI was recognized as a 2019 Top Workplace by The Washington Post.</p>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<p>Our rich benefits package supports employees’ career development, wellness and retirement. Core benefits include: multiple health plan options, dental plans, life and disability insurance; tuition assistance; paid time off; discounted meals; on-site fitness centers and a warm and welcoming culture. Our 401(k) has up to a 4% match, and an employer discretionary contribution to the 401(k) plan that has contributed an additional 3% annually to eligible staff members since 1999. We pride ourselves on our high levels of employee retention, high job satisfaction among employees and our designation as the 7th top workforce on the 2019 Top Area Workplace by The Washington Post.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Create and implement targeted marketing and communications efforts. Coordinate work flows across multiple platforms to reach both internal and external stakeholders.</li>
<li>Manage website content. Collaborate on the development of engaging and informative content, ensuring consistent tone and perspective.</li>
<li>Co-manage the Goodwin House blog and email marketing programs. Collaborate on campaigns and messaging. Create and maintain editorial and email calendars.</li>
<li>Collaborate across departments and throughout the organization to develop content and manage efforts to meet goals.</li>
<li>Contribute to the development and management of sponsorship and advertising opportunities for the organization as well as individual service and product lines.</li>
<li>Stay current with digital developments (e.g. Google algorithm changes, SEO best practices, etc.); media, public relations and sponsorship visibility and trends to maintain and/or improve performance in these disciplines.</li>
</ul>
<p>For this position, we are seeking the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, journalism, business, or related field.</li>
<li>Minimum four years’ experience in communications, PR, marketing or related field.</li>
<li>Experience with digital platforms, campaigns across multiple platforms, publishing software platforms and Microsoft Office.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to develop new and creative marketing and communications ideas and strategies—and evaluate their effectiveness.</li>
<li>Strong writing/editing skills; ability to gather and synthesize information for delivery via multiple digital channels; and a knack for crafting messages that are both informative and engaging using words, infographics, videos, and images.</li>
<li>Proficiency in WordPress and email platforms (MailChimp, ConstantContact, etc.). Familiarity with CRMs (Salesforces) a plus.</li>
<li>Proactive problem solver with excellent analytical/organizational skills and strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>Ability to work well under pressure and manage time effectively.</li>
</ul>
