If that sounds like you, you sound like a good fit for us. Want to be the face of our company? Become our next Claims Rep!<br>
<br>
<b>WHAT YOU'LL DO</b>
<ul>
<li>Verify that our customers have coverage</li>
<li>Investigate and gather information about claims by contacting all parties involved</li>
<li>Accurately document all decisions, correspondences, reports, and discussions</li>
<li>Gain experience in determining liability and setting reserves</li>
<li>Evaluate your investigations to make decisions about the claims</li>
<li>Learn about determining if subrogation exists and taking steps necessary for recovery</li>
<li>Resolve claims in the best interests of the insured, the claimant, and Sentry</li>
</ul>
<b>WHAT IT TAKES</b>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Related insurance or coursework beneficial</li>
<li>Knowledge of insurance policies and coverage, claim payments procedures and familiarity with medical terminology preferred</li>
<li>The desire, ability and interpersonal savvy to handle a fast-paced, high-volume work environment</li>
<li>Effective oral and written communication and human-relations skills</li>
<li>Ability to make sound decisions while using good judgment in evaluating claims</li>
<li>Effective computer skills</li>
</ul>
