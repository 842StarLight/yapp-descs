<h2>Optician & Optometric Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Looking for a friendly, motivated, team players to join our fast paced, dynamic team. Must be able to work some evening hours (until 7 pm) and a few Saturdays a month (9-2pm). Compensation is negotiable based on experience</p>
<p>. Bilingual is a plus.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<p>Responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:</p>
<p>Optician</p>
<p>*examine and fill prescriptions written by Optometrists</p>
<p>*recommend ophthalmic frames, lenses, lens coatings/ materials after considering prescriptions, occupations, hobbies, lifestyle, habits and facial features</p>
<p>*interpret prescriptions, conduct patient consultation, recommend frames and lenses and take appropriate frame styling measurements</p>
<p>*interpret and calculate insurance benefits and copays based on insurance eligibility</p>
<p>*prepare work orders for labs</p>
<p>*repair, adjust and refit broken or damaged frames</p>
<p>*troubleshoot, instruct and consult with clients about adapting and wearing or caring for eyeglasses</p>
<p>*verify, dispense and adjust glasses</p>
<p>*maintain display and inventory of optical merchandise and retail, optical sales floor area and dispensary</p>
<p>Optometric Assistant</p>
<p>* preliminary testings (AR, tonometry, lensometry, VAs,Photos, etc), maintaining efficient patient flow.</p>
<p>* scheduling patient appointments</p>
<p>* be able to handle electronic health records</p>
<p>* contact lens training (education, insertion, and removal)</p>
<p>* maintain overall cleanliness of office and exam rooms</p>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<p>- MUST have some experience with the job descriptions above</p>
<p>- speak English</p>
<p>- minimum 18 years of age</p>
<p>- type and use the computer proficiently</p>
<p>- must be team player, willing to work with others and interact with patients</p>
<p>- must be able to enter numbers and words appropriately in software programs</p>
<p>- must be able to do math (simple adding, subtracting, multiplication) using a calculator to do so</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Optical: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p>Location:</p>
<ul><li>Dallas, TX (Preferred)</li></ul>
