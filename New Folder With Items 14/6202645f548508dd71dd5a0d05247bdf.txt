<h2>Physical Medicine Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About Crossover Health</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Crossover makes remarkable health possible by bringing people, their doctors, data, and benefits together under one connected system of health. We’ve built an entirely new category of primary care providing one simple place to go for trusted care—in person, online, anytime. We are working with the most innovative employer partners to integrate disconnected health and wellness benefits with tech-enabled services which allows our partners to increase access to care, decrease spend, and deliver an unrivaled experience for employees near and far.</p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p>
The Program Manager is expected to dedicate 80% of their time to development, management, and optimization of the Physical Medicine Program and 20% of their time to clinical care at a Crossover Health clinic.</p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Models core Crossover Health values (inspire people, be fearless, design everything, be authentic, stay curious) by consistently challenging the status quo to redefine healthcare delivery</li>
<li>
Facilitates domain expertise across the Physical Medicine Program</li>
<li>
Utilizes clear communication strategies and creates a sense of community among program members</li>
<li>
Defines and regularly updates scope of services and standards of care for the program</li>
<li>
Provides documentation guidelines and oversee chart audit process</li>
<li>
Participates in recruiting, hiring, and on-boarding new program members</li>
<li>
Creates and implements training resources and supporting materials</li>
<li>
Develops program outcome metric standards and reporting mechanisms</li>
<li>
Mentors and supports program members in gaining experience, education, and exposure to new opportunities</li>
<li>
Serves as subject matter expert and provide Crossover-wide support for the following:</li>
<ul>
<li>
New clinic openings</li>
<li>
Sales opportunities and requests for proposals</li>
<li>
New product lines</li>
<li>
Technology enhancements</li>
<li>
Partnerships and third-party offerings</li>
<li>
Clinical operations</li>
<li>
Marketing programs</li>
<li>
Research and clinical trials</li>
</ul>
<li>
Uses clinical analytics and evidence based practice to guide program decisions</li>
<li>
Develops and manages the program budget</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p><b>
Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Doctorate of Physical Therapy or Doctorate of Chiropractic</li>
<li>
Minimum of 5 years experience working in your area of practice</li>
<li>
Must be a Crossover Health employee for at least one year</li>
<li>
Exceptional clinical and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Must be at least 0.8 FTE employee</li>
<li>
Must be licensed in the state of clinical practice</li>
<li>
Must be in good standing without recent disciplinary actions</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Consistently represents the Crossover mission with passion to both internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Demonstrates high learning agility and at least one cycle of success managing a program-related team project</li>
<li>
Demonstrates creativity, flexibility, and ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment that requires management of changing priorities</li>
<li>
Takes initiative, highly organized and enjoys managing projects of all scopes and sizes to completion</li>
<li>
Demonstrates leadership characteristics by unifying and positively influencing program members</li>
<li>
Communicates respectfully and effectively with cross-functional teams to solve complex problems with collaborative solutions</li>
<li>
Exhibits strong presentation skills and enjoys helping others succeed</li>
<li>
Displays strong work ethic and pride in their goal to deliver the quadruple aim of healthcare</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p><b>
Physical Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide excellent Physical Medicine care through in-person and digital communication channels including video conferencing, telephone, secure messaging, chat-based communication, and asynchronous care</li>
<li>
Comfortable with a variety of technology platforms including Google suite, video conferencing software, CRMs, EMRs, etc.</li>
<li>
Able to work on location with a care team at a designated clinical site.</li>
<li>
Able to travel for occasional leadership, program, professional, and clinical meetings</li>
</ul>
