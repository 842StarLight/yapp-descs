With more than $2 billion in revenues, CDK Global is a leading global provider of integrated information technology and digital marketing solutions to the automotive retail and adjacent industries. <p>
We have an immediate opening available for a <b>Heavy Equipment Account Relationship Manager (ARM)</b>. Create business relationships and generate sales leads with high-level contacts through face-to-face client site visits. Maintain an ongoing business relationship with current clients to facilitate future sales. Work in conjunction with a specific field sales team. Work under general supervision of the Director of Sales (DOS). High level of proficiency in communication and collaboration. Typically require 1 to 3 years of DMS (Dealer Management System) or industry experience. Functional responsibilities include, but not limited to:</p>

<p>
Call reports in Salesforce.com, such as…</p>

<li>
Proactive backlog review to find uninstalled applications – or – applications installed but not claimed</li>
<li>
Coordination with CAMs (Client Account Managers)</li>
<li>
Regular review of client application penetration report</li>
</ul>
