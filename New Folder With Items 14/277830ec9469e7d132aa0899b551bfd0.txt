<h2>Technical Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><i>Location</i>: Nashville/LaVergne, TN</p>
<p>
Position Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Provide telephone <i>based</i> technical support to customers.</li></ul>
Troubleshoot FANUC CNC and FANUC Robotics controls, and other brands in future, for external customers and internal technical support for TIE sales team.<br>
Document customer issues and provide written feedback in TIE’s customer relationship management software (Salesforce).<br>
Interface with TIE Technicians and Supervisors to help identify root cause for warranty failures.<br>
Document and maintain configuration and test procedures where needed for components.<br>
Qualification Requirements and Competencies:
<p>
2-5 years with CNC and/or Robotics <i>experience</i> (FANUC <i>experience</i> preferred).<br>
<i>Experience</i> in maintenance troubleshooting, technical support or repair services.<br>
<i>Experience</i> with non-FANUC brand CNC (Siemens, Mitsubishi, etc.) a plus.<br>
<i>Experience</i> with internal components and broad understanding of repair-ability of FANUC technology a plus.<br>
Associate’s or Bachelor’s degree in technology or electronics preferred but not required.<br>
Computer proficiency, with advanced skills in FANUC ladder, and Microsoft Office® products, including Word, Excel, Power Point, and Outlook preferred.<br>
Must have strong organization and efficiency skills, and the ability to quickly transition from one task to another in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision.<br>
Organize and coordinate projects to meet simultaneous <i>deadlines</i>.<br>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.<br>
Must be self-motivated and have the ability and desire to assume ownership of responsibilities.<br>
Able to work independently but also perform as a key team player.<br>
Ability to be flexible and adaptable.<br>
Commitment to company and individual goals.<br>
Honesty; someone whose integrity and values are above reproach with no hidden agenda or ulterior motives.<br>
If you are interested in this position, send an email to <i>employment</i>@fanucworld.com and include your cover letter and resume. Place "TECHNICAL SUPPORT" in the subject line.</p>
