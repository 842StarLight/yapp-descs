<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details

<p>Salesforce, the Customer Success Platform and world's #1 CRM, empowers companies to connect with their customers in a whole new way. The company was founded on three disruptive ideas: a new technology model in cloud computing, a pay-as-you-go business model, and a new integrated corporate philanthropy model. These founding principles have taken our company to great heights, including being named one of Forbes's "World's Most Innovative Company" five years in a row and one of Fortune's "100 Best Companies to Work For" eight years in a row. We are the fastest growing of the top 10 enterprise software companies, and this level of growth equals incredible opportunities to grow a career at Salesforce. Together, with our whole Ohana (Hawaiian for "family") made up of our employees, customers, partners and communities, we are working to improve the state of the world.
</p>

<p><b>Department and Role Description:
</b><br>
The Enterprise Corporate Sales team is closely aligned to the Enterprise Business Unit as they partner together on selling to our largest, top Fortune 500 clients across a diverse set of industries and is broken down into the following segments:
<br>
<br>
Select Accounts
<br>
Key Accounts
<br>
Strategic Accounts
</p>
<p>Health and Life Sciences
</p>
<p>Financial Services
<br>
<br>
The team is responsible for growing and managing subsidiaries within the Enterprise Parent accounts, including partnering with our Strategic Account Managers to evangelize the Salesforce vision and account strategy. Under your leadership, our ECS AE's will be selling the Customer Success Platform (#1 B2B and B2C platform) and all the amazing applications including Marketing, Commerce, Service, Communities, Sales, Collaboration, and Industries, which are all built on the Salesforce Platform, while leveraging internal resources from prospect to close.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Profile
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>• The RVP, ECS will be responsible for setting and executing Enterprise Corporate Sales strategy within the FINS vertical. This executive will assume leadership of a growing sales organization of Account Executives, to help drive complex enterprise deal transactions.
</p>
<p>• Set and execute an aggressive customer acquisition strategy to generate aggressive annual growth in revenue and bookings
</p>
<p>• Maintain key customer relationships and develop and implement strategies for expanding the company’s customer base
</p>
<p>• Provide detailed and accurate sales forecasting
</p>
<p>• Manage overall sales process, set appropriate metrics for sales funnel management
</p>
<p>• Plan and manage at both the strategic and operational levels
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Skills/Experience
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>• 2nd or 3rd line leadership experience leading teams in strategic sales in the US
</p>
<p>• Strong track record of recruiting, developing and retaining a high performing enterprise sales organization
</p>
<p>• Consistent overachievement of quota and revenue goals
</p>
<p>• 8+ years in software and/or applications sales (ideally in a CRM, ERP and/or B2B SW company), selling primarily to the CxO level
</p>
<p>• Proven expertise industries competitive to marketing cloud
</p>
<p>• Proven track record of building satisfied, loyal and reference able customers
</p>
<p>• C-suite level resources, aligned with Regional Vice Presidents, Account Executives and internal leadership teams to present a single front internally and help represent a single vision for our customers
</p>
<p>• Proven success working within a highly matrixed organization and establishing strong relationships across all functions • Strong operational and analytical abilities
</p>
<p>• Strategic enterprise sales experience and revenue achievement selling multiple enterprise software offerings
</p>
<p>• Experience selling cloud based enterprise applications is strongly preferred
</p>
<p>• Bachelor degree preferred
</p>
