The team
<br>Our Marketing team creates world-class content and data-driven marketing solutions that communicates the value of DocuSign's Agreement Cloud to reach more customers and help them understand the potential impact DocuSign can have on their business. We design creative for a new business campaign. Refine messaging around new product launches. Organize captivating customer events. Develop innovative social media campaigns. Collaborate on playbooks and training with the Sales team. Marketing is the face and voice of DocuSign and plays a key, high-impact role in how we tell our story.
<br><br>
This Position
<br>DocuSign's vision is to provide the leading apps and platform technologies for companies to modernize their systems of agreement, from preparing agreements to getting them signed to fulfilling the terms and finally, reporting on, retaining and retrieving agreements. As Senior Product Marketing Manager , you will be responsible for communicating value and increasing sales effectiveness for our flagship product, DocuSign eSignature, plus related products and modules such as eNotary, Payments, and hybrid cloud appliances. You'll drive product messaging and positioning, enable our Sales teams with content and tools aligned to customer needs in various stages of the sales cycle, and collaborate with marketing to build pipeline. You'll work cross-functionally with Product Management, Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, Business Development, and senior leadership.
<br>This position is an individual contributor and reports to the Director of Core Product Marketing.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Own the positioning, messaging, and value propositions of DocuSign's offerings related to electronic signature.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for new products, packages, and partnerships to serve unmet needs.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product Management to ensure that new products and functionality are featured, packaged, priced, launched, and communicated effectively.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales Enablement to ensure that content, tools, and other Product Marketing assets are effectively deployed to, and used by, the field.</li>
<li>Create, lead, manage, and measure product marketing and lead generation initiatives to showcase the value of DocuSign's electronic signature products for particular use cases.</li>
<li>Support strategic customer and internal events (e.g. customer/partner conferences, sales meetings) by owning development of presentations/sessions/training.</li>
<li>Represent DocuSign at third-party events.</li>
</ul>
