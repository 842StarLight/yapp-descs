<h2>Processor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Processor</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Minimum Salary:</b> $15.00 per hour</div>
<div></div>
<div>Executive Personal Computers (EPC), a premier IT equipment re-marketer, has an opportunity at our Atlanta, Georgia facility for a Processor to:</div>
<ul>
<li>Pack, wrap, palletize, load, and move IT equipment for transportation using a pallet jack following required shipment standards</li>
<li>Use software to record and remit IT equipment information and/or create a new profile in the database</li>
<li>Perform minor diagnostic tests to identify IT equipment grade</li>
<li>Clean, wipe and use vacuum cleaner to prepare IT equipment for sale/transfer</li>
<li>Identify IT devices including PCs, laptops, HDDs, printers, networking monitors, etc.</li>
<li>Maintain quality of work while meeting minimum quota standards</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Working Environment:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Warehouse temperatures can vary widely based upon local weather conditions, and may occasionally exceed 90 degrees Fahrenheit</li>
<li>Ability to work in an environment with moderate noise levels, which may grow loud at times</li>
<li>Should be open to extra hours, such as weekly overtime, in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Help maintain a clean and organized work space</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Education, Skills, &amp; Experience</b>
<ul>
<li>Basic computer skills and able to perform user-friendly computer tasks such as print, type, and data entry</li>
<li>Experience using basic computer software</li>
<li>Basic math skills to count IT equipment with speed and accuracy</li>
<li>Must be able to multi-task, have good decision-making skills, and work with minimal supervision</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Physical</b>
<ul>
<li>Must regularly lift and/or move up to 10 pounds, frequently lift and/or move up to 25 pounds, and occasionally lift and/or move up to 50 pounds with or without a reasonable accommodation</li>
<li>Must be able to work 8 hours shifts while performing the duties of this job</li>
<li>Must be able to regularly bend, twist, and reach</li>
<li>Use a pallet jack to move IT equipment from dock to work station</li>
<li>Must be able to handle IT equipment by reading and identifying serial printed letters, numbers, symbols, and model information with a small font size</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Personal Skills</b>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to use common sense when performing job tasks</li>
<li>Must be able to meet or exceed production goals, follow procedures, and adhere to quality requirements</li>
<li>Must be able to work well with others and perform in a team work environment</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<div>High school diploma or equivalent, and/or IT or relevant electronic experience or education are preferred but not required.</div>
