<h2>Meat Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Leads department and staff, including financial stewardship, customer service, department conditions, merchandising, food safety, work safety, communication, hiring, training and performance management. Ensures the department provides an exceptional customer shopping experience, with the highest level of service and product knowledge. Provides leadership in line with company values and promotes a positive and progressive work environment.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
<b>Leadership</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understands, models and instills company culture. Creates a positive and supportive work environment.</li>
<li>Promotes a collaborative team environment through recognition, leading by example and working alongside staff. Demonstrates approachability and fosters open communication. Creates an environment where staff are invested and encouraged to contribute ideas. Demonstrates a pro-active approach in resolving staff issues and conflicts.</li>
<li>Builds and sustains a high performing team. Responsible for development of team members including interviewing and hiring, training and development and performance management.</li>
<li>Seeks opportunities to train and develop staff members to take on increased responsibilities. Demonstrates ability to adapt approach to different learning styles among staff.</li>
<li>Demonstrates a whole store perspective. May work with store leadership team in providing oversight to overall store operations, including service levels and store conditions. May act as Manager On Duty as assigned.</li>
<li>Builds trust and respect through dependability, organization and follow through.</li>
<li>Demonstrates adaptability, flexibility and creativity in problem-solving and is solutions-oriented.</li>
<li>Bases decisions on analysis, experience, resources and circumstances. Prioritizes and acts decisively under pressure.</li>
<li>Demonstrates self-awareness and seeks opportunities for professional growth. Seeks and responds positively to feedback.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains effective verbal and written communication systems.</li>
<li>Oversees effective scheduling and labor allocation to meet business needs and labor budget. Ensures adequate staffing conditions, including leadership coverage.</li>
<li>Ensures department representation at all required committee meetings. Encourages and supports staff participation in other committees.</li>
<li>Prepares and delivers corrective actions and performance reviews in a timely manner. Follows documentation and compensation guidelines.</li>
<li>Communicates in a professional and constructive manner. Responds to and follows through on emails and other communications in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Demonstrates an understanding of basic Human Resources concepts; works with resources at store level to increase knowledge.</li>
<li>Acts as a steward of company resources and Vision/Mission/Values by training, modeling and promoting sustainable behaviors/operations.</li>
<li>Balances administrative tasks with presence on floor.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Specific Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages and provides oversight to all areas of the department. Assesses, prioritizes and delegates tasks and production.</li>
<li>Ensures consistency of department conditions. Ensures all areas of department are stocked, rotated, faced and adhere to merchandising standards and category schematics.</li>
<li>Responsible for in-stock conditions in department. Maintains appropriate inventory levels to achieve sales and margin goals. Trains staff to write orders consistent with department goals and standards to ensure the highest level of product availability.</li>
<li>Plans for weekly ads, events and holidays using tools and reports. Works with store management to understand and respond to current store trends.</li>
<li>Builds displays and arranges department products to make attractive presentations, highlighting featured/unique/seasonal items.</li>
<li>Maintains professional vendor relationships.</li>
<li>Ensures customer special orders are accurately placed and delivered.</li>
<li>Receives and unloads merchandise; checks quality, count and condition. Communicates any issues to management and merchandising team.</li>
<li>Stocks and rotates products with attention to product quality and expiration dates.</li>
<li>Maintains cleanliness, organization and rotation of all areas, products and supplies.</li>
<li>Plans, leads and analyzes the monthly perishable inventory.</li>
<li>Demonstrates ability to multi-task and manage own time. Adjusts priorities, productivity, efficiency and speed based on needs and conditions of department and store.</li>
<li>Observes all rules and policies as listed in the New Leaf Community Markets Staff Handbook and Operations Manual.</li>
<li>Performs other duties and tasks as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Customer Service and Merchandising</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains excellent department conditions, including appearance, cleanliness, signage, staffing and service levels. Models friendly and attentive customer service, coaches staff, recognizes accomplishments and identifies areas of opportunity.</li>
<li>Handles escalated difficult customer service situations and seeks solutions to delight the customer. Trains and coaches staff to do the same.</li>
<li>Reads and understands merchandising information. Develops and executes merchandising plans to promote peak seasonal/ad item/holiday products, drive sales and create an optimal customer shopping experience.</li>
<li>Demonstrates excellent knowledge of products in the department and general understanding of culinary terminology, product labeling and common dietary restrictions.</li>
<li>Shares product knowledge with customers and staff. Makes recommendations and suggestions based on customer needs. Establishes consistent program for communicating product information and knowledge to staff.</li>
<li>Demonstrates an awareness of trends in the industry.</li>
<li>Delivers exceptional service and demonstrates friendly and attentive behaviors with internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Engages internal and external customers in a friendly manner, shares information, assists in locating resources, and seeks opportunities to enhance the customer experience.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Financial Stewardship</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates a strong understanding of labor, sales and margin goals and factors that influence them. Meets labor and margin goals. Develops and executes a plan when results do not meet expectations.</li>
<li>Trains department staff to develop financial knowledge.</li>
<li>Ensures that department adheres to company invoice, credit, transfers and inventory procedures.</li>
<li>Coaches department to maintain appropriate, accurate and organized inventory and back stock. Handles damaged and spoiled product according to company policy.</li>
<li>Demonstrates creativity in merchandising and utilization of products in order to increase sales and minimize waste.</li>
<li>Leverages resources and labor to maximize efficiency, without compromising quality and service standards.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Permits and Licenses:</b> Varies by State</p>
<p><b>
Facilities, Sanitation, Work Safety and Food Safety</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Participates actively in the maintenance and enrichment of a workplace safety culture.</li>
<li>Understands and enforces New Leaf Community Markets work safety policies and guidelines set forth by OSHA and other regulatory agencies. Takes appropriate action on work safety concerns raised by staff and regulatory agencies.</li>
<li>Identifies and addresses factors that contribute to the functionality of store and department equipment. Follows through with service and maintenance needs.</li>
<li>Promotes the highest level of department cleanliness. Ensures general housekeeping and janitorial tasks are scheduled and conducted on a regular basis and ensures sanitation standards established by store policy and state and local health departments are practiced.</li>
<li>Understands and enforces New Leaf Community Markets food safety policies. Takes appropriate action on food safety concerns raised by staff and regulatory agencies.</li>
<li>Demonstrates proficiency with emergency procedures, safety equipment and MSDS.</li>
<li>Ensures staff members are properly trained on department equipment and safety processes.</li>
<li>Frequent handwashing is required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Tools and Equipment Used</b><br>
This position may be required to use the following tools and equipment: Office Equipment, Computer, Knives and Slicer, Band Saw, Sausage Pump, Grinder and Cuber, Industrial Food Processor, Smoker, Mixers, Blast Chiller, Heat Wrapper, Temp Gun, Scale, Cart, Hand truck, Baler, Pallet Jack, and Dishwasher</p>

<p><b>Physical Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Medium work level</li>
<li>Continuously required to be on feet with a mix of standing and walking throughout shift. Sitting occasionally required.</li>
<li>Frequently required to climb stairs, reach, grasp, stoop, bend, kneel/squat and walk.</li>
<li>Consistently required to lift up to 10 pounds without assistance.</li>
<li>Frequently required to lift up to 40 pounds without assistance.</li>
<li>Work performed frequently requires medium physical strength.</li>
<li>Consistently required to use near and peripheral vision, in addition to depth perception.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work occurs in a grocerystore environment which includes surfaces that can be wet and slippery, moderate noise level, and temperature extremes in controlled environments in the cooler and freezer, and depending on location, working outside in all temperatures.</li>
<li>There may be exposure to dust, mold, gluten, fragrances and other fumes and airborne particles, fresh fruits, vegetables, house plants, and flowers.</li>
<li>Work requires use of and working in proximity to common commercial cleaning agents.</li>
<li>This position works on or around moving machinery or moving parts.</li>
</ul>
