<h2>Senior Marketing Program Manager - Digital Products</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Careers that Change Lives</b><br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
The <b>Senior Marketing Program Manager</b> will be responsible for understanding the voice of the customer and working with cross-functional teams, particularly R&amp;D, PMO, Regulatory, Clinical, Human Factors and Quality groups to develop project plans, concepts, features, and requirements, for digital products that will grow the Advanced Insulin Management Business Unit. You will be the central conduit in ensuring that the digital product portfolio roadmap and key strategic initiatives are executed to serve customer, market and business needs, informed by strong business analytics. Given the nature of work, you will have a high degree of visibility in the organization including interactions with senior leadership. You will be a cross-functional influencer who will leverage Digital Strategy development expertise to guide development efforts for a new segment of customers, both patients and physicians.</div>

<b>
A Day in the Life</b><br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Product Strategy &amp; New Product Development</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Drive digital product development decisions for future and sustaining Intensive Insulin Management BU product lines, influencing key stakeholders such as Human Factors, PMO and R&amp;D to achieve product objectives for overall commercial viability, such as ease-of-use, comfort, appeal and consumer driven architecture.</li>
<li>
Through deep understanding of current business and future strategic objectives, be able to articulate future steps needed to continue the growth trajectory of business.</li>
<li>
Work with internal and field (i.e., outside the BU) to investigate and provide solutions to issues related to commercially released products.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Cross-Functional Team Participation &amp; Leadership</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Functional leadership responsibilities for the pipeline strategy and analytics team including recruitment &amp; selection, performance evaluation &amp; management, people development etc.</li>
<li>
Manage and coach direct reports work streams to participate in and lead cross-functional teams in optimizing product development strategy and execution.</li>
<li>
Participate in and lead cross-functional teams to optimize future Intensive Insulin Management BU product strategy around given business needs, market needs, customer needs, technical trade-offs, schedule limitations and resource constraints.</li>
<li>
Generate business plans, market requirements documents, presentations and supporting materials to align all functional groups behind a common vision.</li>
<li>
Work with other global (regional) marketing partners to understand unique customer &amp; business needs and ensure product requirements are aligned with global products and strategies.</li>
</ul>
