<h2>Consulting Systems Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Want to be at the forefront of the Computer Networking industry? With the acquisition of Meraki, Cisco formed the Cloud Networking Group and built the fastest growing group within the company. Becoming known as the most successful acquisition in Cisco's history, the Cloud Networking Group is triumphing's sales targets and continuing to win new logos.
<br><br>
We are looking for a "rising star" who is highly motivated, technically savvy and passionate to join Cisco's CNG as our next Consulting Sales Engineer. In this role, your primary responsibility will be to team up with a Product Sales Specialist and perform technical pre-sales activities for new and existing opportunities. Utilizing your in-depth knowledge of CNG's networking solutions and our competitors' offerings, you will clearly demonstrate the advantages to both prospects and partners. We highly use our pre-sales engineers, which means you have the opportunity to make excellent money while contributing to the success of the entire organization.
<br><br>
----------------
<br>Responsibilities
<br>----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>You will act as the technical subject matter authority, thoroughly understanding products, features, functions, and benefits while being able to communicate to all level of customers</li>
<li>You understand customer requirements for wireless networks and explain how Cisco Meraki will integrate with existing infrastructure, as well as service future needs</li>
<li>You will Craft product documentation for customers and perform competitive analysis regarding competing products</li>
<li>You'll perform site surveys and provide onsite deployment assistance on large deployments</li>
<li>You'll provide technical guidance to channel partners and assist them in effectively selling the Cisco Meraki solution</li>
</ul>
-------------------------------
<br>Desired Skills &amp; Experience
<br>-------------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>You will have field pre-sales experience</li>
<li>You have authoritative knowledge of 802.11a/b/g/n wireless networks, site surveys, requirements gathering, deployment challenges</li>
<li>You have an understanding of wired networks, including firewalls, content filters, routing/switching, VLAN's, etc</li>
<li>You understand wireless security methodologies, including 802.1x w/ RADIUS integration</li>
<li>You have a consistent track record of 5-10 years in a pre-sales technical role (solutions architect, systems engineer, sales engineer, etc)</li>
<li>You have knowledge of sales cycle, strong verbal and written communication skills, and validated negotiation techniques</li>
<li>You have amazing relationship building skills, tenacity, resilience and presentation skills.</li>
<li>You have strong interpersonal skills with the ability to multi-task and set priorities.</li>
<li>CCIE, CWNP, CWDP, CCNA preferred</li>
<li>Ability to travel 50+%</li>
<li>BS or MS degree in computer science, computer engineering or related discipline required</li>
</ul>
Must be authorized to work in the U.S. - Position does not provide sponsorship
