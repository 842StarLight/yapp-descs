<h2>Assistant Director of IT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Responsible for the efficient operation and maintenance of all computer systems, data and voice communications activities at property.
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure critical systems, specifically Front Office Property Management System, Call Accounting, HSIA, PABX and Food &amp; Beverage Point of Sales computer systems are available twenty-four (24) hours per day.
</li>
<li>Establish and maintain user procedures and hardware familiarization for all systems.
</li>
<li>Provide administration services, to include; contribution to technology capital and operating budgets.
</li>
<li>Provide Systems Administration, including maintaining user/group accounts, user configuration, trouble-shooting access problems and completion of on-site documentation.
</li>
<li>Assist in Server infrastructure management, performance monitoring, Operating System upgrades and troubleshooting.
</li>
<li>Ensure effective support for other systems, including back office, sales and catering and human resources applications and any other at the property.
</li>
<li>Provide system support services, including service outage diagnosis, troubleshooting and restoration of service, virus protection management.
</li>
<li>Maintain and promote Four Seasons core technology standards as outlined in the Four Seasons IT Core Standards.
</li>
<li>Assist in the co-ordination of new acquisitions and major projects as required.
</li>
<li>Participate in property-level, corporate-initiated technology implementations.
</li>
<li>Good knowledge and support for Desktop OS and applications including patches.
</li>
<li>Provide direct guest support and quality support to function rooms and conference services.
</li>
<li>Administer Lotus Notes, connection to global VPN and other communication tools.
</li>
<li>Assist in the process of systems backup and recovery procedures.
</li>
<li>Assist in management of third party vendors.
</li>

</ul>
