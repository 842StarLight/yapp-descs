<h2>Marketing Coordinator and Assistant to the Dean</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Marketing Coordinator and Assistant to the Dean is responsible for providing administrative support to the Dean of the Campus. Working with our Admissions staff, the Coordinator will assist in generating a sufficient quantity of qualified applicants to Flagler College - Tallahassee. This is accomplished by working with our Marketing partner to create and execute a Marketing plan that includes: relationship building on the TCC campus, direct student recruiting, digital and other media, and relationship building in the Tallahassee Community.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Administrative support to the Dean</li>
<li>Relationship building on the TCC campus
<ul>
<li>Faculty – class presentations</li>
<li>Academic Advisors</li>
<li>Student Clubs and Organizations</li>
<li>Newsletter</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Assist in Student Recruiting
<ul>
<li>Campus signage (some basic graphic design duties)</li>
<li>Direct mail (lead generation)</li>
<li>Telephone outreach</li>
<li>Campus events/calls to action</li>
<li>Tabling</li>
<li>E-mail</li>
<li>Class presentations</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Digital media
<ul>
<li>Develop a social media plan to include: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Update of FCT Webpages, Twitter</li>
<li>Other Media</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Guerrilla marketing</li>
<li>Relationship building in the Tallahassee Community
<ul>
<li>FCT Community Advisory Council (CAC)</li>
<li>Employers with tuition benefits</li>
<li>State Agencies</li>
<li>FCT Alumni</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Share employment/Internship leads with Department Chairs</li>
<li>Assists with graduation responsibilities (e.g. academic awards, ceremony needs)</li>
<li>Assists with academic probation, warning, and dismissal processes]</li>
<li>Assists with proof-reading and formatting documents</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Review of applications will begin immediately and continue until the position is filled.</i></p>

<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors in related field</li>
<li>Strong creativity</li>
<li>Sales, marketing, and/or recruiting experience</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of Flagler College policies and procedures</li>
<li>Maintaining confidentiality and to use good judgment in making decisions concurrent with college policy</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively verbally (in person and by phone) and in writing with Flagler College administrative staff, faculty, prospective students, and members of their families as appropriate</li>
<li>Ability to use Microsoft Office software and the Flagler College software</li>
<li>Ability to plan, organize, prioritize, and coordinate work assignments with the Admission’s staff, student interns, and Alumni Ambassadors</li>
</ul>
