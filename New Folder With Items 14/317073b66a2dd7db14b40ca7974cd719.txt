<p><b>Director</b><b>, Global Systems Integrators (GSI)</b><b> - Americas</b></p>
<p>
The Director of Global Systems Integrators (GSI) for Americas has a key role in managing a team of regional GSI managers. The GSI Team at Nutanix bridges the relationships between our GSI partners and our regional sellers. This is a unique opportunity to join one of the fastest growing businesses at Nutanix, within a rapidly expanding company. We are looking for someone who has a strong track record working with GSI partners, has managed a sales team, and has established trusted advisor status with their key decision makers.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Build and manage a team of 4+ regional GSI Managers, recruit and hire</li>
<li>Define, build &amp; execute GSI joint business plans</li>
<li>Track/create pipeline, and close deals by partnering with the GSI partner field &amp; Nutanix field</li>
<li>Educate the GSI partners on Nutanix’s strategy, value proposition and key messages</li>
<li>Establish &amp; maintain relationships with the key decision makers at our GSI partners (e.g. Delivery Head, Practice Head, Head of Infrastructure, etc.)</li>
<li>Thought leadership within Nutanix. Work with all of levels of sales, marketing along with our Business Units to drive the global GSI partner priorities.</li>
</ul>
<div>
The ideal candidate will demonstrate:<br>
<ul>
<li>How to build a 2-3 year business strategy with clear annual execution oriented objectives.</li>
<li>Strong sales expertise especially in the G2K</li>
<li>Interaction with large companies and building joint go-to-market plans</li>
<li>A keen financial acumen</li>
</ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Understanding the team dynamics and how to grow and motivate a team</li></ul>
<br>

Experience / Education / Requirements:</b>
