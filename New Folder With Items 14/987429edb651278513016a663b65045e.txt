<h2>Sales Associate (PT) - Orlando Vineland Premium Outlets</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Citizen Watch America </b>represents the sales and marketing for the Citizen Group within the U.S., Caribbean and Latin American markets. The brands included within Citizen Watch America are Citizen, Bulova, Frederique Constant, Alpina, and Ateliers deMonaco. Each individual brand builds upon their very distinctive identities in the market with their unique, differentiated advertising, marketing and brand campaigns. As a combined company, however, the brands can assist each other and leverage the power that a larger unified entity brings.</p>
<p>Our vision is to lead the affordable luxury watch market by offering the most compelling collection of brands and products that are loved, favored, and respected by citizens of the world.</p>
<p>We will continue to leverage our heritage and expertise to enrich the lives of our current and future customers and Employees through unparalleled excellence in creativity, design, craftsmanship, innovation, technology, value, and service.</p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Demonstrate merchandise, totals purchase, and accepts payment for customers in retail store by performing the following duties. Inspires customers to buy, creating a lasting positive impression of the Citizen Brand . Meets Sales goals. Opens and closes store as designated by General Manager or designee.</p>
<p>This position is located at our Orlando Vineland Premium Outlets location.</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Greets customers and welcomes them to store</li>
<li>Appropriately stocks cases, counters, or tables with merchandise and point of sale materials</li>
<li>Sets up advertising displays and arranges merchandise according to corporate guidelines to promote sales.</li>
<li>Obtains merchandise from stock as requested by customer and stocks merchandise received for sale in store.</li>
<li>Answers customer's questions concerning features and functions of watch products, demonstrating such as requested.</li>
<li>Totals purchase price and tax on merchandise purchased by customer to determine bill.</li>
<li>Accepts payment and prepares receipt of purchase.</li>
<li>May operate bar code scanner, register, or other point of sale equipment.</li>
<li>Wraps or bags merchandise for customers.</li>
<li>Periodically cleans shelves, counters, or displays.</li>
<li>Assists in taking of inventory of stock, and reordering merchandise.</li>
<li>Maintaining good housekeeping standards</li>
<li>Adheres to Loss Prevention and inventory control and compliance procedures</li>
<li>Meets regular, predictable, dependable attendance and punctuality</li>
<li>Available to work a variety of hours, which may include early mornings, evenings, weekends, and overnight shifts.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED preferred</li>
<li>1-3 years of related experience</li>
<li>Retail jewelry and watch experience highly desirable</li>
</ul>
<p>EEOE</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Additional Compensation:</p>
<ul><li>Commission</li></ul>
