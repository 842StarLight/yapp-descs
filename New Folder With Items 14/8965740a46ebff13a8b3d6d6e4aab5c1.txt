<h2>Social Community Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>“We are innovators who make purpose-built gear for life's most demanding missions.”</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Our ethos defines our culture – Trusted, Responsive, Accountable and Committed.</i></b></p>
<p>
5.11 Tactical has established itself as the fastest-growing and most well-known brand of tactical apparel, gear and functional products in the world. Through a commitment to operator-led product innovation, quality and compelling value, 5.11 has developed a loyal following among some of the most demanding and discerning end-users of apparel and gear.</p>
<p>
At our Operations Center in Manteca and Development Center in Irvine, we leverage our deep relationships with Law Enforcement, Special Forces Operators and First Responders to gain unique insights. The results are products that break new ground combining ruggedness, speed, range of motion and ultimate comfort in professional duty, tactical training, adventure, women’s tactical, fitness and industrial professionals.</p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a Social Community Specialist with a customer-first mindset to help build, grow, and manage our customer online communities and relationships. This person will be responsible for answering customer questions, responding to positive and negative reviews, providing product recommendations, and joining relevant conversations on behalf of the brand.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Actively engage with consumers across multiple platforms (Google, Yelp, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, etc.) in accordance with brand guidelines and business policies.</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities to positively engage with customers online.</li>
<li>
Establish best practices for community engagement and identifies opportunity to optimize answers online.</li>
<li>
Facilitate online conversations with customers and respond to queries and/or escalate complaints.</li>
<li>
Thoroughly research customer issues, complaints, and comments, to provide complete and timely resolution to achieve satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Receives and processes order adjustments as requested by the customer.</li>
<li>
Provide customers with information about products, services, as well as offer product recommendations.</li>
<li>
Ability to up-sell and clearly explain promotions, pending sales offers, etc.</li>
<li>
Manage multiple online feedback systems.</li>
<li>
Provide internal and external community training as necessary.</li>
<li>
Maintains expert knowledge of products and services offered.</li>
<li>
Maintains communication between brand marketing and customer service teams</li>
<li>
Track and report customer feedback trends to stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Assist with customer order fulfillment for marketing department as needed</li>
<li>
Monitors the social email account</li>
<li>
Other duties may be assigned to accomplish company or department objectives</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
 </b><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma.</li>
<li>
Previous Customer service experience or social moderation a plus.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of social media platforms and/or customer service software a plus.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates the ability to engage customers with a positive and friendly attitude.</li>
<li>
Comfortable working with multiple software systems.</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask and switch between multiple screens and systems.</li>
<li>
Exceptional attention to detail</li>
<li>
Strong time management and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Self-starter, highly collaborative, true team-player</li>
<li>
Enjoys working with people and social/online communities</li>
<li>
Schedule flexibility to include occasional evenings and/or weekends</li>
<li>
Values the tactical lifestyle, an outdoor enthusiast, and/or is a person who pushes the limits of personal endurance and strength is a plus!</li>
<li>
Higher education with a focus in English, Communications, Public Relations, Journalism or Marketing preferred but not required</li>
</ul>
