<h2>Field Engagement Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Job Summary</div>
<p>
The Field Engagement Manager is accountable to the execution of the strategic roadmap in order to deliver results in acquisition and credit sales for assigned client(s)/territories. The Field Engagement Manager will partner with internal leadership and cross functional partners to ensure strategies are aligned to the goals of Alliance Data and assigned client(s).</p>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<p>Reports to</p>
<p>
Sr Manager</p>
<p>
Essential Job Functions</p>
<p>
Drive Results – Accountable to achieving assigned performance metrics, including applications, tender share, activation, and other KPIs based upon the engagement structure. Own the relationship with assigned partners and influence the behaviors by understanding and leveraging business insights, reporting, and key measures to identify opportunities for growth.</p>
<p>
Develop Talent – Responsible for personal development and contributes to the overall development and growth of the larger field team.</p>
<p>
Grow Profitability – Effectively manages to financial budgets including travel and other expenses</p>
<p>
Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<div>
Education/Certifications</div>
<p>
Bachelor's Degree in Marketing or related field, or equivalent education and/or experience</p>
<p>
Work Experience</p>
<p>
3 - 4 years of experience in a sales driven culture and responsible for individual budget and expenses.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</div>
<p>
Strong vertical / industry leadership experience</p>
<p>
Ability to provide effective feedback</p>
<p>
Presentation Skills</p>
<p>
Ability to build and maintain partner relationships</p>
<p>
Ability to influence behavior of partners to support growth and profitability</p>
<p>
Working Conditions/ Physical Requirements</p>
<p>
Up to 75% travel required, including air travel.</p>
<p>
Direct Reports</p>
<p>
None</p>
