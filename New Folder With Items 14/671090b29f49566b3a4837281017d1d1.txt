<h2>Systems Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Imagine a company that values your entrepreneurial spirit, gives you the ability to drive your own success, and allows you the freedom to explore creative solutions. Revionics – voted one of the Top Work Places in 2015 by the Austin American-Statesman – is looking to add new talent to our team. Immerse yourself in a company culture that prizes individuality, self-motivation, and creative thinking.
<br><br>
At Revionics, the Systems Engineer will be part of a team that owns infrastructure technologies and responsibility for installing new components and maintaining configurations. You'll be part of a small but fast-paced highly collaborative team with low egos and high output. Rock and roll as part of a 24/7 on-call rotation responding to issues and users with a global footprint and prototyping new technologies to increase operation efficiency and effectiveness.
<br><br>
<b>Who You Are:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Background in architecture, implementation, and delivery of large-scale systems in real-world situations</li>
<li>Ability to walk up the cloud stack (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS) model with modern terminology and relevance</li>
<li>Broad knowledge of IT market and trends</li>
<li>Knowledge of Cloud providers (AWS and GCP) and surrounding tools (Kubernetes, Docker, etc.)</li>
<li>Detailed knowledge of architectural approaches including consolidation, cloud computing, virtualization, information management, Big Data, AI, Machine Learning, etc.</li>
</ul>
<b>What You Have/Can Do as a Minimum:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Configuration management tools (especially Terraform and Puppet);</li>
<li>Script languages (including Python, Ruby, JavaScript);</li>
<li>Awareness of DevOps toolchains (e.g. Jenkins, Gitlab)</li>
<li>Cloud infrastructure and container-based infrastructures.</li>
</ul>
<b>What You Can Do to Stand Out:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Cloud Architecture Development, Deployment and Migration</li>
<li>Experience with CI/CD build and release automation tools</li>
<li>Able to utilize REST and other API interfaces in applications and systems to accomplish tasks</li>
<li>Knowledge of deploying servers and applications in on-premise virtualized environments</li>
</ul>
<b>What's in Your Tool Box:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Able to work flexible hours to include nights and weekends (24X7 environment/on a rotation)</li>
<li>Personable professional with a desire to work independently in collaborative and fast-paced teams.</li>
<li>Low-Ego, Action-Oriented, and Intellectually-Curious personalities highly desired.</li>
</ul>
<b>Who We Are:</b><br>
Predictive. Prescriptive. Profitable Retailing.
<br><br>
We provide SaaS-based pricing, promotion, markdown and space solutions. Retailers in all segments across the world adopt our self-funding model to improve top-line sales, demand and margin. Our customers gain that competitive edge and improve their value proposition while outmaneuvering competitor price aggressiveness.
<br><br>
During the days of first-generation price optimization solutions, at a time where science in retail was viewed as voodoo, our founder Jeff Smith nurtured the concept that there could be a better way. He went on to form Revionics around that new-generation vision, and to this day we remain committed to his goal: To help retail businesses and everyday users solve complex pricing challenges leveraging the latest machine learning science with a completely transparent process, usable in an intuitive way that fits into retailers’ normal business flows.

</ul>
