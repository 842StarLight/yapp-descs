<h2>Vitality Bowls Assistant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
 <b>Vitality Bowls Assistant Manager</b></p>
<p>
We are a health minded, nutritionally conscious, energetic company, focused on the customer experience. Be a part of a fun, dynamic, growing team committed to bringing healthy food to the community!</p>
<p>
As an Assistant Manager of Vitality Bowls, you will be responsible for the overall shift-level operations including assisting in growing sales, managing financials, operational excellence and exhibiting amazing customer service skills. In the absence of the Store Manager, the Assistant Manager is effectively the store leader.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Assistant Store Manager Responsibilities include</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Managing food orders, speed of service and ensuring quality food production</li>
<li>Following all company policies pertaining to daily operations and ensuring all employees do the same</li>
<li>Establishing strong, transparent lines of communication with upper management, customers, Shift Supervisors and Team Members</li>
<li>Actively coaching, training and quizzing your team about their knowledge of the company, our products and allergen safety</li>
<li>Following all training plans thoroughly</li>
<li>Recognizing outstanding work performance while providing necessary constructive feedback</li>
<li>Participating in interviewing and hiring of Team Members</li>
<li>Adhering to all labor laws</li>
<li>Holding self and employees accountable to company policies regarding time and attendance</li>
<li>Consistently exhibits and holds team accountable to customer service standards</li>
<li>Complying to all company policies, procedures and expectations, ensuring all employees do the same</li>
<li>Showing exemplary knowledge of Vitality Bowls brand and products</li>
<li>Consistently educating and engaging customers and employees on all Vitality Bowls products and services</li>
<li>Managing inventory levels daily, assisting in performing operational procedures such as inventory counts, placing orders, accepting deliveries, etc.</li>
<li>Assisting in creating schedules based on the needs of the business</li>
<li>Observing the needs of customers and team members and communicating those observations to upper management</li>
<li>Meeting all business and productivity metrics daily during shifts, including sales and budgets</li>
<li>Identifying performance opportunities and collaborating with upper management to create strategies to improve overall store and team member productivity</li>
<li>Following all company food safety, allergen safety, cash handling, cleanliness and operational policies and procedures, and ensuring that all employees are in compliance as well</li>
<li>Leading the team by example</li>
<li>Promoting a positive, uplifting and motivating in-store culture that thrives on team work</li>
<li>Having a growth mindset and looking to create, plan and realize goals pertaining to self and store performance</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by upper management based on business needs</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Assistant Store Manager Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have reliable transportation</li>
<li>Minimum of 1 year supervisor experience</li>
<li>Available to work a minimum of 35 hours per week and all day-parts including mornings, nights and weekends</li>
<li>Proficiency with technology such as POS software and emails</li>
<li>Ability to stand for long periods of time, bend and regularly lift up to 40 pounds</li>
<li>Maintain a clean and work appropriate personal appearance</li>
<li>Restaurant or food experience preferred</li>
<li>A positive and uplifting attitude!</li>
<li>Has a food safety certification or is willing to become food safety certified</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Assistant Store Manager Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bonus Program</li>
<li>50% off meals while working</li>
<li>15% family discount</li>
<li>Career Advancement Opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>

<p><b>
About Vitality Bowls</b></p>
<p>
Vitality Bowls is a Superfood Café focused on delivering top quality organic Açaí, Acerola, Pitaya and Graviola Bowls®, smoothie, fresh juices, soups, salads, and panini. Vitality Bowls® are enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack, or dessert.</p>
<p>
This company was created because of our strong commitment to bring health and wellness to every community across the country through fresh, high-quality superfoods. This philosophy has guided everything we do and we strive to instill the same health-minded values to our employees and customers.</p>
