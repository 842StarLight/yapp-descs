<h2>Marketing & Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Slalom is a purpose-driven consulting firm that helps companies solve business problems and build for the future, with solutions spanning business advisory, customer experience, technology, and analytics. We partner with companies to push the boundaries of what’s possible—together.</p>
<p>
Founded in 2001 and headquartered in Seattle, WA, Slalom has organically grown to over 6,000 employees who love their work and life. We were named one of Fortune’s 100 Best Companies to Work For in 2018 and are regularly recognized by our employees as a "<i>best place to work</i>". You can find us in 27 cities across the U.S., U.K., and Canada.</p>
<p>
We are looking for a strategic, self-directed and results-oriented marketer to join our Silicon Valley team. You will collaborate with our leadership team to set the vision and strategy for Slalom Silicon Valley’s alliance marketing and marketing operations, aligning with local business objectives. In this hands-on role, you will be leading a portfolio of marketing activities such as Demand Gen campaigns, partner marketing and events from inception through completion to help drive the tremendous growth that is in front of us. With your passion for marketing program operations development, data, testing, and optimization, you will help design, build and lead the marketing programs for services that inspire customers to act.
</p>
<p>You love mixing marketing and technology every single day for the advancement of the brand you represent? We are marketing devotees and we love that too: your key responsibility is to ensure that all campaign operations run smoothly.
</p>
<p>We want someone with a consistent record and understanding of partnership/alliance marketing, direct marketing and execution of corporate events; as well as experience in the high-tech industry. Demonstrated dedication to excellence, flexibility, poise, confidence and the ability to work with clients in a professional manner are a requirement.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
This marketing executive will be responsible for helping the leadership team define the strategy and execute on key marketing initiatives, events and campaigns (alliance related, client related, value proposition related), with a tactical focus:</p>
<p>
§ Alliance Marketing: focused primarily on Tier 1 alliance partners such as AWS, GCP, Salesforce and Tableau, you will craft, own and execute overall partner marketing engagement with Tier 1 partners, including partner events, relationships management with key marketing executives in the partners organization, securing Alliance Partners’ Marketing funds, executing Partner Campaigns, developing joint collateral &amp; announcements.</p>
<p>
§ Direct Marketing: covering all local segments, you will orchestrate various direct marketing programs, such as, advertising, sponsorships, external events management, messaging, campaigns, event management, storytelling, account-based marketing and sales enablement.</p>
<p>
§ Build <i>Electronic Direct Mailing</i> engines and run campaigns in Pardot (for direct marketing or alliance/partner marketing). You will also run social media marketing tools, networking groups and subscriptions.</p>
<p>
§ External Event management: you will collaborate with leaders, recruiters and alliance managers who are implementing local marketing programs to help ideate, plan, and execute impactful external client events from intimate client experiences to large scale events.</p>
<p>
§ You will develop and administrate relevant dashboards and reports for measurement and analytics all marketing campaigns and activities, to deliver returns against expected business results and critical metrics and advise on future campaigns, events.</p>
<p>
§ Localize campaigns and branding initiatives built by the national marketing team.</p>
<p><b>

<p>
§ Bachelor’s degree or higher, preferably in Business, Marketing, or Communications.</p>
<p>
§ Minimum 5-7 years of alliance and/or direct marketing experience.</p>
<p>
§ Real passion for crafting and implementing memorable experiences, especially events and campaigns that feed relationship building — we love ideas that are outside of the box!
</p>
<p>§ Consistent record of driving return on investment, customer acquisition, and deep executive engagements through creative marketing programs.
</p>
<p>§ Demonstrated experience in developing, executing and maintaining marketing strategies to meet organizational objectives.</p>
<p>
§ Experience working end-to-end in mass marketing tools such as Pardot, Social Studio, Salesforce, LinkedIn and Tweeter.</p>
<p>
§ Experience handling contract negotiations with Marketing vendors.</p>
<p>
§ Excellent interpersonal, verbal and written communication skills.</p>
<p>
§ Knowledgeable about the Silicon Valley market, venues and vendors.</p>
<p>
§ Experience handling budgets and complex schedules.</p>
<p>
§ Ability to set priorities and work both independently and collaboratively.</p>
