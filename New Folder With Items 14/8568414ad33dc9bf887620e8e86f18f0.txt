<div><b>About FX Services</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>JPMorgan’s FX Services business offers automated foreign exchange (FX) services across a number of use cases, such as corporate or retail clients making international payments, custodians funding international securities trades or eCommerce merchants wanting to price their products in multiple currencies.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The business is supported by global the FX Services technology team with contributors in Houston, New York, Bournemouth, London, Mumbai, Bangalore and Singapore. The team is organized into Agile development teams supported by QA and Product Delivery Teams.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key Responsibilities</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The successful candidate will be involved in the business analysis and management of software development processes for FX Services trading/sales software. Specifically you will:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Business Analysis: Interact with external clients, internal stakeholders and regulators to gather and document requirements.</div></li>
<li><div>
Analyze, design and implement new efficient processes for our current businesses.
</div></li>
<li><div>Analyze, design and implement solutions for clients.
</div></li>
<li><div>Product Delivery and Management: Manage projects against aggressive client commitments and deadlines. Ensure ordered and well documented product backlogs. Remove blockers to development progress. Manage stakeholder expectations.</div></li>
<li><div>
Produce high quality documentation for internal users and external clients.</div></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Expectation management for various stake holders with competing interests, for limited resources.</li>
<li>QA test definition, oversight and review of automated acceptance and integration tests. Challenge development teams to ensure technical solutions are sound.</li>
<li>Be commercial aware and mindful of costs vs benefits in everything we do.</li>
<br>
</ul>
