<h2>Revenue Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description:</p>
<ul><li>Review and analyze market conditions for each hotel in region. This process included an analysis of competitors’ rates by market segment for weekday and weekend, a review of local market conditions and special events, and a forecast of occupancy and rate.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Control rooms inventory by market segment as a result of analyzing each hotel’s business mixes and stay patterns. Adjust inventory by market segment based on a review of hotel performance.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Set rates for each market segment for each hotel and adjust rates based on a review of hotel performance.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review ALL reports (STR, Hotelligence, Market Vision, etc.) to better understand market and property performance.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attend ALL brand Revenue Management calls to provide input and analysis of current conditions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review group and volume accounts, and set prices to achieve maximum revenue for this type of business. Analyze special account requests and provide rates that are consistent with hotel’s revenue strategy for sales accounts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyze each hotel’s revenue performance and identify opportunities to increase revenue. Present revenue strategy recommendation and supporting documentation to VP of Revenue Management, Vice President of Operations and General Managers, Director of Sales and coordinate the implementation of the strategy.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyze each hotel’s use of PMS (Opera/Perform, etc.)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review market opportunities that require price point advertising and set rates that are consistent with hotel’s revenue strategy.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prefer a minimum 2 years hotel industry experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum of 2 years’ hotel revenue management experience</li></ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul><li>Must have excellent oral and written communication skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have good analytical skills and decision-making ability.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to work independently and multi-task, prioritizing as appropriate.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent problem solving skills and ability to develop detailed plans.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Requires working knowledge and experience using Microsoft Office products including Word, Excel, Outlook</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to simultaneously manage several objectives and reassign priorities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to collect and analyze information to identify trends, cause and effect, and to make forecast.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Problem solving skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to communicate technical and analytical information to persons with non-technical backgrounds.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to use analytical data to support proposed action plans and programs</li></ul>
