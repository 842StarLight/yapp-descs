<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Here’s a little more about this opportunity for the right candidate:
</div>
<ul>
<li>Excellent team / culture
</li>
<li>In-house design, video and interactive capabilities</li>
<li>
Successful, innovative company willing to invest in big ideas</li>
<li>
Continued opportunity for financial reward and advancement</li>
</ul>
<div>
The Community Marketing Specialist works directly with the Director of Marketing to assist communities in meeting marketing, sales and revenue objectives. This individual acts as the day-to-day liaison between regional support teams, communities and the creative services department.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Lead the project management of all jobs through all stages of the project to ensure deadlines are being met and workloads for the creative team are reasonable.</li>
<li>
Proactively and responsively communicate with communities to understand their marketing and sales needs and develop and communicate appropriate solutions to achieve desired goals. Act as a customer advocate as it pertains to messaging and brand experience.
</li>
<li>Open and manage new jobs and schedule dates for creative development and fulfillment.
</li>
<li>Proof creative before sending onto client/community.</li>
<li>
Review jobs and resolve issues; Place jobs on-hold that require additional information or approval, acquire the information needed to remove the job from hold, and adjust the final due date (if necessary) when hold is released.</li>
<li>
Approve (with manager) final artwork prior to print.</li>
<li>
Research community advertising requests, handle insertion orders and relate documents for media placement. Follow internal guidelines for procedures.</li>
<li>
Process print and media invoices and reports spending in established documentation.</li>
<li>
Process billing and other financial documentation monthly.</li>
<li>
Develop project creative briefs to inform strategic and creative solutions.
</li>
<li>Communicate with internal team – managers, traffic, and creative – to ensure appropriate solutions are developed to meet communities’ marketing and sales goals.
</li>
<li>Research inquiry and lead generation data from CRM and community insight.</li>
<li>
May perform other duties as needed and/or assigned.<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
