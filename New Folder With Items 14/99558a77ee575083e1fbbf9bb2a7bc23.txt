<h2>Trade Show Operations Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
ViewSonic is looking for an energetic individual with a solid understanding of trade show logistics. Under the direction of the Trade Show Manager safeguarding flawless execution of trade show booth and equipment setup, take down. The candidate will work closely with product marketing, sales and channel marketing departments to ensure trade shows booth are built on time, functional, and safe to operate.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with trade show department, channel, sales, resellers, partners and show management to ensure products are shipped, delivered and returned.</li>
<li>Travel to trade show locations to perform on-site execution, coordinate trade shows tasks including set up, tear down.</li>
<li> Assist with product inventory including booth properties, promotional materials and marketing literature.
</li>
<li>Build third party relationships with show management to execute successful events.</li>
<li>Manage and distribute show leads and reports.</li>
<li>Continually assess and deliver detailed analysis of trade show effectiveness and make recommendations for improvements.</li>
<li>Ensure corporate brand compliance.</li>
<li>Perform all other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
 </p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma required, 2-year associate degree preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum 2 year’s trade show experience preferable.</li>
<li>Must have working knowledge of trade show union labor rules &amp; regulations.</li>
<li>Team player with strong problem solving and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Ability to self-direct and work independently.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal communication, organizational and time management skills.
</li>
<li>Keen attention to detail is a must.</li>
<li>PC proficiency with MS Word, Excel and Power Point required.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Windows, Mac, Android and iOS, including firmware and software upgrade.</li>
<li>Understanding of display products</li>
<li>Prior experience of traveling to various events and trade shows preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Travel: up to 50% travel to attend shows and events nationwide</li>
<li>Environment: While traveling, work can take place during weekends and evenings as dictated by the trade show schedule; normal office and warehouse environment when not traveling to attend shows.
</li>
<li>Physical Effort: Position requires ability to set up trade show booths, involves heavy lifting 40 –50 pounds unassisted for booth set up. A continuous and rigorous pace is to be expected during business travels.
</li>
<li>Hazards: Negligible, little or no exposure to hazards.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>The above statements are intended to describe the general nature and level of work being performed by people assigned to this job. They are not intended to be an exhaustive list of all responsibilities, duties, and requirements.</li></ul>
