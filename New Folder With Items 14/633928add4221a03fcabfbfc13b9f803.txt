<p><b>ABOUT THE OPPORTUNITY</b></p>
<p>
Based in Walpole, MA and reporting to our Director of Engineering, your primary role will be to work with the engineering developers and help them test the functionality of their various designs. You will be testing hardware, software and their combined functionality. In this key role, you will help the engineering team debug new products and product changes prior to passing the products on to the Design Verification (QA) group for final pre-release testing.</p>
<p><b>
PRINCIPAL RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop test plans for new products as well as significant product modifications and enhancements.</li>
<li>
Perform the tests as developed and report the results to the appropriate design engineer(s).</li>
<li>
Work with the designers to resolve any issues or bugs that may be identified</li>
<li>
Work with our Design Verification (QA) group to help them get their final pre-release test plans created.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
C/C++ programming experience</li>
<li>
PC based test equipment development (including GPIB/serial devices)</li>
<li>
Solid understanding of A/D, D/A, amplifiers and related circuitry</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communications skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>BENEFICIAL KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
DAQ system experience</li>
<li>
Ethernet, CAN, Serial and Avionics bus test experience</li>
<li>
LabVIEW programming experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BSEE or equivalent</li>
<li>
3 or more years experience in a test engineering role</li>
</ul>
<p><b>JOB LOCATION</b>: Walpole, MA</p>
