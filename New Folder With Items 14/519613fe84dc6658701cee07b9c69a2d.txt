<h2>Asset Team Member</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>General responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Optimization of implant and instrument placement at accounts, warehouses for SISO and utilization of Central Inventory.</li>
<li>Includes analyzing, ordering, and building trays/kits/SMS Tree for new product rollouts.</li>
<li>Includes analysis and reporting on validating instrument trays and implant kits.</li>
<li>Evaluation &amp; ordering of new set (implants &amp; instruments) requests outlining business justification per Zimmer Corporate Policy.</li>
<li>Evaluation and processing of lost/wasted items.</li>
<li>Facilitating implant/instrument exception scenarios to and from the field and corporate</li>
<li>Includes Product Experience Reports, Recalls, Expired product, and Worn Instruments</li>
<li>Partner with Sales teams &amp; hospitals personnel to develop continuous improvement programs to enhance case service &amp; optimal asset utilization.</li>
<li>Work closely with Zimmer Corporate to implement processes &amp; systems to meet asset turn rates &amp; Cost-To-Serve targets.</li>
<li>Administer cycle count &amp; audit program to obtain 99.99% inventory accuracy by location. May include warehouse or account locations.</li>
<li>Determine root cause of variances and develop corrective action plans.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by supervisor</li>
</ul>
