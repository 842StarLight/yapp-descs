<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">At Kenzan, we create elegant software solutions that capture the eye and imagination. If you are interested in joining our community of passionate and creative people, we'd like to hear from you.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate for this role enjoys driving projects from concept to completion, is creative, a master presentation writer, strong communicator and eager to build something special. If you are looking for an opportunity to make a big impact on a growing organization, this role is for you.
<br><br>
If you have a lot of these skills, but not all of them, please still apply. We love to work with those who are willing to learn.
<br><br>
What we'll expect from you. You will work across the organization to drive Kenzan's marketing efforts. Here are some specific tasks:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive strategic lead generation programs - manage and nurture leads, and drive acquisition and engagement of new clients</li>
<li>Develop go-to-market materials, own and evolve messaging for Kenzan services and solutions</li>
<li>Spearhead content creation process and work with technical subject matter experts to create tangible offerings and campaigns that showcase Kenzan's capabilities and expertise</li>
<li>Collaborate and develop relationships with corporate marketing to support Amdocs marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Manage communication channels including: company website, tech blog, social media presence, press and other publicity</li>
<li>Write engaging and highly polished presentations for meetings</li>
<li>Oversee Kenzan involvement with conferences and other industry events</li>
<li>Seek out and maximize exposure and publicity opportunities</li>
<li>Work with senior leadership to facilitate the writing of case studies and/or white papers for public distribution</li>
<li>Identify new business opportunities</li>
</ul>
Some skills we require.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience taking highly technical concepts and translating them into business values for a services-led organization</li>
<li>Experience leading marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Knowledge of sales process</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communications skills</li>
<li>Proficiency with design using the Adobe suite</li>
<li>Familiarity with Google Ads and similar tools</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial attitude with a creative and collaborative approach</li>
</ul>
What you can expect from us. We offer competitive salary, health benefits, and a 401K plan. Join our collaborative working environment with lots of natural light, and everything you need to be your best.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Mentorship, and opportunities to grow and learn</li>
<li>Collaboration with experienced professionals</li>
<li>A supportive and non-competitive community</li>
<li>Best-of-breed tools including your choice of machine (most of us use MacBook Pros)</li>
<li>Exposure to cutting edge technologies and custom business solutions</li>
<li>A commitment to your professional growth, including coursework and professional development resources</li>
</ul>
More about us. Kenzan ( http://www.kenzan.com/ ) is a professional services company owned by Amdocs ( http://www.amdocs.com/ ) that specializes in digital transformation, application development, and architecture consulting. Combining leadership with technical expertise, Kenzan works with partners and clients to craft solutions that leverage cutting-edge technology, from ideation to development and delivery. We are highly collaborative, exceptionally detail-oriented, and love to be challenged. We use the latest technology including cloud infrastructure, containerization, and continuous integration and delivery tools.</div>
