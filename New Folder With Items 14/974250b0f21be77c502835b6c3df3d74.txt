<p>We are currently looking to fill the following position based out of our Denver office:</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Enterprise Account Executive</b></h3>
<p>Up to a new challenge in one of the most attractive Direct Sales roles in this country? The Enterprise Account Executive will report directly to the VP, Sales and will be an integral part of Valen’s dynamic sales team. The successful candidate must have an outstanding track record of new account sales of complex technology, data and/or analytics preferably into P&amp;C insurance carriers.</p>
<p>The Enterprise Account Executive must have a strong understanding of predictive analytics as applied to the insurance industry plus a passion to sell the products in compelling ways. You must be able to effectively communicate these new technologies and approaches in ways that are understandable and compelling. The successful candidate will coordinate all Valen activities and resources necessary to effectively close deals. You will be responsible to contribute input into sales materials, collateral and presentations.</p>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate new account sales of Valen’s products and associated services for P&amp;C insurance carriers as well as upgrade and renewal opportunities with existing clients</li>
<li>
Manage entire sales process from initial contact through opportunity and contract closing</li>
<li>
Build and maintain an active and robust pipeline that yields on-target or better sales productivity</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you’ll need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Managing multi-party/multi-constituency selling environments, preferably insurance</li>
<li>
Experience selling to senior executives / C-suite</li>
<li>
Latent needs selling and ability to create ‘budget’ vs. spending ‘budget’</li>
<li>
‘Business case’ selling (building clear value proposition and ROI based on business impact) with expertise in ‘Pilot to Production’ sales cycles</li>
<li>
Prospecting / Hunting to discover client needs</li>
<li>
Proven abilities in both solution and consultative selling – selling consulting services AND technology/software</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics, Engineering, IT/Computer Sciences or Business</li>
<li>
Proficient in CRM (preferably SalesForce.com)</li>
<li>
Strong written and visual presentation skills</li>
<li>
Ability to travel (approximately 35%)</li>
</ul>
