<p>About the Job:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Mobile Technologies, Inc. (MTI) is seeking candidates to become a Tier 2 Field Technician. In this position, you will install, maintain, and / or repair interactive displays, within retail, commercial and municipal environments. We are looking for responsible, self-motivated, service-oriented people with good technical aptitude, who are willing to perform variable field-based assignments at client locations. All training will be provided and paid by MTI. We offer a highly competitive hourly wage and mileage reimbursement.<br>
</p>
