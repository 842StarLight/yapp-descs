<h2>Career Services Placement Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Title: Career Services Placement Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Reports To: Director of Career Services</b></p>
<p><b>Department: Career Services</b></p>
<p><b>FLSA Status: Non-Exempt</b></p>
<p>MyComputerCareer owns and operates private accredited colleges across the United States as well as via the Internet from our corporate headquarters near Raleigh, North Carolina. Our schools serve the needs of thousands of students while giving employers an educated and skilled workforce in the markets where we are located.</p>
<p>We provide educational opportunities, including high demand certifications, for one aspiring to become an Information Technology professional. Our programs are designed to prepare students for direct entry into the job market and provide hands-on instruction. It is our goal to specialize in those areas for which the job growth potential is highest. Our hallmark is personalized, practical, real-world instruction that prepares the student for his or her future while meeting the needs of employers. And, we pledge to accomplish that in an encouraging, resourceful, and enabling atmosphere.</p>
<p>To learn more about our company, please click here: www.mycomputercareer.edu</p>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>In addition to being able to perform the functions and duties of the Career Services Specialist, Placement Managers will take on assisting the department in a leadership role by providing additional guidance for Career Services Placement Team Members, and support for the Executive Director of Career Services through quality assurance, training opportunities and a focus in facilitating continuous improvement with the evolving needs of our students.</p>
<p>They are action-oriented, forward thinking, upbeat, and driven. A track record of success in not only building strong employer relationships, but proven success in expanding employer outreach. The selected candidate will build a personal rapport with the Career Services Placement Team and support them in transforming our students and graduates into expert job seekers and productive employees.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties</b></p>
<p><b>And Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Career Services placement team members will directly report to the Placement Manager</li>
<li>Drive the Placement Team to create new relationships and build on existing relationships with employers to maintain a steady flow of job leads in the technology industry to meet weekly and monthly goals is essential.</li>
<li>Effectively lead the department to deliver exceptional career counseling services to our students that support the core values and the mission of MyComputerCareer.</li>
<li>Coach and develop a high functioning team to execute day-to-day operations of core student services including the ability to foster effective communication and a positive work environment.</li>
<li>Conduct daily observations &amp; provide coaching on the Placement Team’s ability to readily assess the talents of the individual students they speak with and their ability to match and market them with the pipeline of job leads available.</li>
<li>Assist in training and driving the implementation of our new Career Services Management (CSM) system, MyCareerConnect.</li>
<li>Ability to exercise discretion and apply independent judgement to address employee problems.</li>
<li>Ability to resolve potential problems from a broad perspective to identify the underlying root cause.</li>
<li>Ability to articulate high-level interpersonal situations.</li>
<li>Work closely with the Director of Professional Development &amp; Eligibility to oversee Career Fairs and streamline Advisory Board meetings on campus’</li>
<li>Facilitate Weekly Team Meetings and bi-weekly 1-on-1 meetings with all team members.</li>
<li>Ensure consistency of communication and documentation, providing constructive criticism, weekly audits of documentation, including PGEVFs, TWC forms, etc.</li>
<li>Facilitate Onboarding &amp; New Hire Training</li>
<li>Create and maintain all Policies &amp; Procedures related to Career Services Placement</li>
<li>Manage and provide professional development plans for Career Services Placement Team</li>
<li>Identify departmental areas of concern and perform other related duties as required</li>
<li>Miscellaneous job duties as needed</li>
</ul>

<li>Effective verbal, presentation and listening communications skills</li>
</ul>
