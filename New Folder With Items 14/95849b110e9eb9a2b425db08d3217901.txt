<h2>Packaging Designer - Dallas/Ft Worth, TX</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Sealed Air partners with customers to solve their most critical packaging challenges with innovative solutions that leave our world, environment, and communities better than we found them. Our portfolio of widely recognized brands includes Cryovac® food packaging and Bubble Wrap® protective packaging which respectively enable a safer, more efficient food supply chain and protect valuable goods shipped around the world. Sealed Air generated $4.7 billion in sales in 2018 and has approximately 15,500 employees who serve customers in 123 countries. To learn more, visit www.sealedair.com.</div>

Job Description
</div>
<div>
<div>Develop and design new custom packaging or modify existing designs to provide functional and cost effective packaging solutions for AFP customers. More specifically:</div>
<ul>
<li>Confer with production, sales and customers to establish and evaluate design concepts for packaging products. Evaluate feasibility of design ideas, based on factors such as appearance, safety, function, serviceability, budget, production costs/methods. Present designs and reports to customers for approval, and discuss need for modification.</li>
<li>Fabricate or coordinate the fabrication of samples in wood, plastic, corrugated, or other materials using hand or power tools. Modify and refine designs, using working samples, to conform with customer specifications, or production limitations. Analyze product failure data and laboratory test results in order to determine causes of problems and develop solutions</li>
<li>Research production specifications, costs, production materials and manufacturing methods, and provide cost estimates and itemized production requirements. Recommend improvements to work methods or procedures.</li>
<li>Use computer aided drafting or design (CAD) software for designing packaging systems or other engineering tasks.</li>
<li>Prepare technical reports and related documentation.</li>
</ul>
