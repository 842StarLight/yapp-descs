<h2>IBMi / AS/400 Programmer</h2>

<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong AS/400 programmer/analysts needed for a legacy application system maintenance support , modification of existing RPG/RPGLE 400 and Cl programs</li>
<li>Know how to map EDI documents (810,844,845,849,850,856,820) and generate/manage EDI source files in TrustedLink/Inovis/OpenText. XML knowledge.</li>
<li>Design analyze, modify, test, and document computer programs according to program specifications and IT standards.</li>
<li>Comprehensive understanding of iSeries (IBMi / AS/400) system architecture, and operating system utility, CL, DBU, SQL, back up, and recover</li>
<li>Good communication and analytical skills. Should be able to document design and code to customer standards.</li>
<li>Excellent in teamwork, interpersonal, and written and verbal communication skills with different functional department users.</li>
<li>Must be adept at learning, evaluating and applying emerging technologies</li>
<li>Maintain professional and positive attitude with coworkers</li>
<li>Have knowledge on application of INOVIS, BIZ-CONNECTION,VAN/AS2 connectivity, MARKMAGIC, Application or other label printing application, ERP manufacturing &amp; financial administration &amp; inventory &amp; sales, is preferred</li>
</ul>
