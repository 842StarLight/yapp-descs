<br><br>
<b>Summary:</b><br>
The Ticketing Director is responsible for overseeing the development of the box office, ticket sales, tracking of ticket sales, and the management of show inventory. This position will assess, develop, implement and evaluate new policies and procedures to make ticketing processes more efficient. Additionally, the Ticketing Director will develop and maintain a rapport with the venue and ticketing contacts while monitoring sales, opens, and holds after on-sales. They will also collaborate with the accounting department to settle the financials for each event.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Oversee ticketing for all shows, which include confirmation of ticket sales, supervision the on-sale information, the scaling of presale packages, and management all Box Office operations and personnel.</li>
<li>Review and approve events prior to tickets going on sale at the box office. Advise on ticket pricing and scaling.</li>
<li>Monitor sales, opens and holds after on-sale and up until show day, including updated sold maps and provisions of audits as needed. Review expense reports and vacation requests for ticketing staff.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the accounting department to settle the financials for each event.</li>
<li>Supervise the placement of ticket holds including fan club tickets and preparing ticket confirmation sheet.</li>
<li>Oversee show build for Premium Ticketing department, if applicable, and prepare ticketing information sheet report and send to operations.</li>
<li>Manage operation of various ticketing platforms and advise on troubleshooting issues as they arise. May also be sent to new venues acquired by AEG to get them on board with our system of accounting and facilitate training in AXS.</li>
<li>Manage customer service issues via email and at the box office. Collaborate with ticketing companies to continue expanding current software capabilities as well as instruct them on how to manage AEG's customer issues.</li>
<li>Manage staff by assigning festival, show and one-off ticketing projects and help troubleshoot any issues that arise.</li>
</ul>
