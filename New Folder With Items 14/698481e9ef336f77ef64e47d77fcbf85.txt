<h2>Pharmacist in Charge/Dispensary Pharmacist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position: </b>Pharmacist-In-Charge, Dispensary Pharmacist, Pharm. D.</p>
<p><b>Our Story</b></p>
<p>We are a family-owned operator of medical-only cannabis pharmacies throughout the country looking to hire a Pharmacist-in-Charge for our upcoming location in Utah. We aspire to professionalize the medical cannabis industry and become a true resource for our patients. We actively engage with the communities we serve and employ socially responsible business practices reflective of our stewardship as licensees in highly regulated state medical cannabis programs. Our proven history of setting the standard of care ensures full regulatory compliance and long-term business success.</p>
<p><b>Summary of Position</b></p>
<p>The Pharmacist-In-Charge will serve as the Leader of the Dispensary/Pharmacy most responsible for the patient care experience. Recent experience (within the last 5 years) with patient-oriented drug delivery systems and a passion for patient care and service delivery. Familiarity with computers.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>· The Dispensary Pharmacist ensures compliance with all Utah Department of Health Medical Cannabis Program rules and regulations governing the Act, and directly supervises the activity within the dispensary to include dispensary staff training and education.</p>
<p>· The Dispensary Pharmacist provides management and leadership in the Cannabis Pharmacy to include monitoring all point of sale transactions.</p>
<p>· The Dispensary Pharmacist manages patient specific logs as required, the receipt, storage and auditing of all inventory, and is responsible for ordering new inventory and supplies, maintaining accurate records using the Cannabis Pharmacy inventory tracking software program.</p>
<p>· As the responsible party at the Dispensing Facility, this position resolves all inventory discrepancies and patient-staff conflicts.</p>
<p>· The Dispensary Pharmacist also serves as the Privacy Officer, responsible for the maintaining of the Dispensing Facility’s privacy policies and procedures, the security and confidentiality of all patient information, as well as overall compliance with HIPAA.</p>
<p>· This position acts as a liaison internally between all staff, dispensary management team and the General Manager, and externally between the Dispensing Facility and law enforcement, Commissioner of Health, and the local community.</p>
<p>· Directs and monitors department managers to accomplish goals of the plan, consistent with established and safety procedures.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>· Qualified applicants must be full-time residents of the State of Utah, no exceptions.</p>
<p>· Must have a genuine passion for helping others to feel better.</p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree in the pharmaceutical sciences, Pharm. D. preferred. An active license in good standing without restrictions.</p>
<p>· Must be a United States Citizen or authorized to work in the United States.</p>
<p>· Must be able to pass an extensive local &amp; national criminal background check.</p>
<p>· Must live within 45 minutes of the Dispensary/Pharmacy.</p>
<p>· Must be willing to take the state-required 4-Hour training course for certification.</p>
<p>· <b>Priority will be given to Pharm. D’s with compounding experience and/or extensive continuing education certifications.</b></p>
<p><b>Experience/Skills</b></p>
<p>· Knowledge of marijuana strains, (indicas, sativas, hybrids), edibles, extracts, and current products on the market or have a willingness to learn.</p>
<p>· Dispensary-related experience a plus.</p>
<p>· Must be friendly, outgoing, trustworthy, dependable, and hardworking.</p>
<p>· Must be able to provide compassionate patient service.</p>
