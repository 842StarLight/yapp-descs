<h2>Regional HR Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>For more than a century, Alsco has been providing industry-leading linen and uniform services to customers in many industries, including restaurants, health care, automotive and industrial operations. Currently, we operate 80 commercial processing centers in North America and have 20,000 employees in 13 countries. We’re looking for the next generation of ambitious visionaries and pioneers. We are committed to attracting high-caliber talent and fostering an environment of employee achievement. Alsco is a high-performing workplace with an exciting future. Join our team and build your career with one of the largest linen and uniform rental service companies today!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Classification: </b>Exempt</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Regional Human Resources Manager executes the planning, coordination, facilitation, and oversight of HR activities for 11 union and non-union production facilities in Las Vegas, NV, Yuma, AZ, St. George, UT, Honolulu, HI and the following 7 locations in California: San Diego, Anaheim, Los Angeles, San Jose, San Francisco, Santa Rosa, and Sacramento. This position supports a workforce of about 1,500 employees.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul><li> Develop branch HR competency by training, advising, and consulting with branch HR, office managers and line managers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage and implement a variety of human resources strategies at the plant and sales regions, balancing business partner and corporate HR initiatives.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Engage leadership in understanding HR initiatives, policies, procedures and processes.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Know and understand talent at all levels and recommend and implement succession and development strategies.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage OFCCP / AA / EEOC activities, compliance, and audits for the region.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Promote and maintain optimal labor / management relations.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>
1. Multi-location, multi-state experience, strong verbal and written communication.</p>
<p>
2. At least 7 years HR generalist experience.</p>
<p>
3. Solid computer skills including Excel, PowerPoint and Word. Experience with Ultimate Software a plus.</p>
<p>
4. Experienced in legal compliance with various laws and regulations covering areas such as EEOC, OFCCP, FMLA, FLSA, workers’ compensation, etc. Must demonstrate a mastery of California-specific labor law.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education:</b></p>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s Degree in HR or related field; Master's Degree preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li> HR certification preferred.</li></ul>
