Job Description
<br>---------------
<br><br>
Vlocity, Inc. is recruiting a Senior Demo Engineer – CPQ &amp; Product Modeling to help build Vlocity product demonstration capabilities for Vlocity Solution Consultants. The key to successfully delivering many of Vlocity's demos is the creation of sales catalogs and technical catalogs. The Senior Demo Engineer - CPQ &amp; Product Modeling will design and implement product modeling, catalog creation, and CPQ capabilities for our demos. The goal is to enable our Solution Consultants to deliver repeatable, reliable and reusable demo experiences that speak to customer workflows, process flows and business requirements.
<br><br>
This role will be part of the Demo Engineering Team. We work within the broader Vlocity Solution Consulting organization and our goal is to provide a trusted single source of truth regarding the latest demo environments – so that our Solution Consultants can confidently demo the latest, stable product capabilities to customers. This role will report to the VP Demo Engineering, and will be part of Vlocity's first dedicated Demo Engineering team – helping Soution Consultants deliver faster and more accurate demos – directly contributing to the hyper-growth of our company.
<br><br>
We seek a talented Senior Demo Engineer - CPQ &amp; Product Modeling with experience in working with – and serving as a trusted advisor to - internal stakeholders including Solution Consulting and Product Development, to design and shape unique and extensible Enterprise level experiences.
<br><br>
