<p>At Xandr, data analysis and forecasting are our bread and butter – we process, inspect and analyze billions of ad events nightly, to provide unparalleled forecasting to our publishers. Our platform must constantly evolve, and we’re searching for engineers to contribute to solving these Internet-scale problems. You will need to keep abreast of the latest technologies to handle large amounts of data and quickly determine their applicability.</p>
