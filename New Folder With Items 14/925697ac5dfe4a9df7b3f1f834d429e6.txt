<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who We Need:</b></h2>
<p>The Office Coordinator position is responsible for creating a warm and inviting reception to all employees, guests and vendors. The Office Coordinator will handle all administrative and some HR-related duties for the business. The ideal candidate will have a positive attitude, strong organizational skills, the ability to multi-task and thrive on working in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What You Will Do:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Manage phone at front desk and greet incoming clients/visitors.</li>
<li>Maintain calendars of conference rooms and support executive team in scheduling trainings and/or meetings.</li>
<li>Plan and coordinate the selection and delivery of food for meetings and trainings.</li>
<li>Support the planning of company-wide events and manage all internal and external vendor relationships.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing an office supply inventory and order supplies as needed.</li>
<li>Coordinate Office Fob Access security and maintain communication with property management on needs/feedback. Regularly audit employee fob access.</li>
<li>Sort and deliver incoming USPS, UPS and FedEx deliveries; sort and ship outgoing USPS and/or FedEx mail.</li>
<li>Manage new hire Welcome Box preparation and placement/shipping.</li>
<li>Audit monthly benefit invoices and provide HR with findings for approval.</li>
<li>Responsible for drafting and sending weekly communication to the Charlotte office.</li>
<li>Audit employee access in Skyprep, the company’s Learning Management System.</li>
<li>Assist the Recruiting team with initiating Wonderlic assessments for candidates.</li>
<li>Coordinate and schedule NMLS licensing visits for new Loan Originators.</li>
<li>Regularly stock the break room with necessary supplies.</li>
<li>Offer support to departments on team member engagement.</li>
<li>Fulfill supply requests for employees as needed.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You Need:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma or GED.</li>
<li>BS/BA in Business, or related area preferred.</li>
<li>1+ years of Office Administration experience.</li>
<li>Advanced computer skills, including Microsoft Office and Google Apps.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Exceptional decision making, problem-solving and strategic thinking skills.</li>
<li>Ability to strive in a fast-paced environment and take constructive criticism, coaching and feedback on a consistent basis.</li>
<li>Highly detail orientated and organized with the ability to multi-task many projects across business lines.</li>
<li>Ability to exercise discretion on sensitive and confidential matters.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, as well as the ability to interact successfully and collaboratively across business lines.</li>
<li>Ability to forage and maintain excellent working relationships with all members of the Cardinal Financial team.</li>
</ul>
