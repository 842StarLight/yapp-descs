<h2>Chief Marketing Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Now we're looking for a great and skilled marketing lead. Our ideal candidate must do everything possible to make marketing operations effective in all markets. We will be happy to work together with a person who has experience in the field of information publishing and e-commerce.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Strategy + Execution - Effectively develop marketing strategy and plan that includes demand generation, brand, product marketing, customer marketing, content marketing, and any other necessary activities.</li>
<li>
Build, maintain and push the company brand, including messaging, tone, design, and public and analyst relations to drive heightened awareness and brand loyalty.</li>
<li>
Money Distribution - Assign and plan marketing budgets.</li>
<li>
Team Leading and Building - Lead, coach, and grow the Marketing team as you create alignment and set priorities, provide guidance &amp; administrative support to members of the team.</li>
<li>
Marketing Analytics - Establish clear KPIs and processes for reporting on the effectiveness of marketing efforts.</li>
<li>
Teamwork: Maintain strong communication and collaboration with Sales and Product teams to develop overarching digital marketing strategy and approach, including social media, SEO/SEM, email marketing and web development.</li>
<li>
Other Tasks (not less important) - Monitor promotions, advertising, media planning, consumer research, multi-tiered communications, traditional and social media tools, and all other relevant marketing activities to reach various target audiences in the most effective manner possible.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Necessary Skills:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
7+ Years in Marketing including leading a team.</li>
<li>
Strong leadership ability, including mentorship, development, and growth of a team.</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills in English with demonstrated ability to communicate professionally at all levels.</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of marketing instruments.</li>
<li>
You know how to develop, evolve, and execute the marketing strategy.</li>
<li>
MBA is a great plus.</li>
<li>
The real story of developing, inspiring, retaining, and coaching a marketing team.</li>
</ul>
