<div><b>Global FP&amp;A and Strategic Director
</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Reporting to the VP of Finance, the individual will provide key strategic, business and financial leadership and acts as a business partner to senior leadership in NI. Additionally, the Director will centrally manage all financial planning and analysis activities for the business, sales, marketing, R&amp;D and G&amp;A functions, partnering with the Finance leadership and supervising the network of FP&amp;A professionals around the world to understand performance and drive execution. Included in these efforts is the management of ongoing transformation initiatives to improve efficiencies, capabilities and controls + simplify reporting across the global enterprise. The Global FP&amp;A director also needs to develop and retain key talent in the FP&amp;A organization on a global basis. Underlying the role is the need to act as a change agent within FP&amp;A, encouraging an environment that employs accountability, collaboration and trust while fostering team work and execution.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Core Responsibilities</b>:</div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to support alignment between strategy and finance across the organization, partnering with leadership to develop and implement strategic business plans and operating initiatives, through a strong business partnership with Corporate Strategy, Corporate Development team and the Business Unit GM’s.</li>
<li>
 Maintain the multi-year strategic financial plan and business model.</li>
<li>
Add financial insights to the planning and execution of our company strategy driving cross-functional alignment through the annual strategy sessions, operational reviews, and acquisition pipeline management activities, etc.</li>
<li>
Consolidate and provide insights from market and competitive guidance to help shape strategic direction to support company ability to meet/exceed operating model results. Maintain/Provide Competitor financial analyses &amp; aggregated market share.</li>
<li>
Drive financial process discipline for strategic analysis and investments</li>
<li>
Provide M&amp;A financial due diligence and integration guidance that will allow NI to make winning moves
</li>
<li>Develop / support standardized strategic management reporting, including market share, other financial KPI’s, dashboards, business trends, competitive analysis and performance vs plan. Support Management Reporting: BvA’s, Headcount, CFO board updates - Support the creation of analyses, commentary and presentation materials for Board meetings.</li>
<li>
Review of Hyperion Management Adjustments</li>
<li>
Coordinate the Annual Budget Process in alignment with RVP’s and BU’s in developing Sales Plan and CM’s</li>
<li>
Improve P&amp;L predictability through improved Forecasting</li>
<li>
Run the Monthly Financial Forecasting (currently: OPEX, future: P&amp;L’s); provide basis for guidance and quarter-end decision making.</li>
<li>
Investor Relations support: earnings, conference prep, individual investor conferences/meetings and external financial messaging</li>
<li>
Provide financial modeling and analysis for various ad-hoc financial projects, particularly for strategic decision-making at the VP+ level.</li>
<li>
Team management: goals, performance reviews, salary planning, development</li>
<li>
Provide Business Partnering / Business Enablement for Financial perspectives &amp; analyses for business units, global sales, marketing, R&amp;D and G&amp;A functions</li>
<li>
Ability to provide strategic M&amp;A analysis to support BU’s maximize financial growth</li>
</ul>
