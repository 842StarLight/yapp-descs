<h2>Account Manager, Economic Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>Emsi is the nation’s leading provider of workforce and economic analytics, delivering software and consulting services to help communities prosper. We are looking for a highly organized and motivated Account Manager to join our team. The position requires striving to know our economic development customers at every level of their organization (from the decision-makers to the researchers) to ensure they are equipped with the knowledge, data, and tools they need to promote economic prosperity in their communities. Emsi’s Account Managers are on the front lines, proactively building relationships with our hundreds of clients. We are looking for people who are passionate about living out our chief tenet: bless the customer.</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Duties and Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Provide data assistance and technical support on Emsi software via phone, email, and chat
</li>
<li>Negotiate, document, and invoice renewals with clients</li>
<li>
Coordinate the implementation of new SaaS subscriptions</li>
<li>
Make meaningful contact with every client throughout the organization
</li>
<li>Schedule strategic client visits and onsite trainings (e.g., travel to a metro area and meet with all 10 Emsi clients in the region)</li>
<li>
Establish client-specific goals for every account you manage</li>
<li>
Report metrics to Customer Service Manager</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
