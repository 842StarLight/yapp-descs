<b>ABOUT PHOENIX ENERGY TECHNOLOGIES, INC.:</b><br>
Founded in 2004, Phoenix Energy Technologies is a leading provider of Enterprise Energy Ma nagement Software and Enterprise Energy
<br>Management Services. PhoenixET works with retail , grocery and healthcare customers to discover opportunities and implement
<br>strategies which reduce energy consumption and lower facilities spend. Through real-time measurement and verification of energy use,
<br>related costs and contextual data (such as weather, occupancy, and schedules) PhoenixET helps its customers get a "full view" of energy
<br>consumption and costs to drive efficiencies, reduce energy spend, and deliver maximum ROI.
<br>In addition to its SaaS software services, PhoenixET also provides professional monitoring and analysis AaaS services to its customers.
<br>PhoenixET has taken an innovative approach to alarm and data management with a focus on built-in intelligence. By leveraging the
<br>PhoenixET technology platform, together with a robust and innovative services arm, and proactive analytics tools, PhoenixET Customers
