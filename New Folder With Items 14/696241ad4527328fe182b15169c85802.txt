<h2>Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Property Manager with National Development at Woodland Station Apartments</b></p>
<p><b>The Opportunity: </b></p>
<p>National Development is looking for an experienced Property Manager for Woodland Station Apartments in Newton.</p>
<p><b>As a Property Manager, your primary responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Leading by example and being a role model for the standards and behaviors consistent with the core values and culture of National Development</li>
<li>Inspiring the team to effectively execute sales, marketing and operational activities that achieve budgeted occupancy and customer retention goals</li>
<li>Managing the financial performance of the property in accordance with the established budget</li>
<li>Effectively coaching all team members to understand how they contribute to the big picture</li>
<li>Creating and delivering timely and accurate financial and operational performance reports to clients and ownership groups that clearly explain operational effectiveness, trends and variances</li>
<li>Cultivating and maintaining strong, loyal relationships with all constituents—owners, residents, employees and vendors</li>
<li>Being an expert on the competitive marketplace and effectively coaching the team to proficiency in differentiating the community and National Development from competition</li>
<li>Partnering with the internal marketing group to develop strategies which position the community as a destination for people seeking engaged and enriched living experiences</li>
<li>Creating an operating environment that assures consistent guest satisfaction and yields outstanding customer satisfaction ratings and community reviews</li>
<li>Inspiring the professional growth and development of all team members by encouraging knowledge sharing and collaboration</li>
<li>Supporting participation in training and educational opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>You Create Value by: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Building a motivating environment that encourages your team to give their very best</li>
<li>Coaching in the moment and providing team members with opportunities to develop their skills while simultaneously developing your own</li>
<li>Proactively managing the performance of all team members through performance reviews, regular constructive feedback and development/growth plans</li>
<li>Connecting with people by engaging in meaningful conversations that demonstrate your concern and genuine desire to make them feel at home</li>
<li>Taking responsibility for customers’ problems, making sure they’re solved and treating them like you would your best friend</li>
<li>You play an active role in making certain the community is impeccable</li>
<li>You roll up your sleeves and assist team members—no job is “below your pay grade”</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Bring to Us: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in the property management</li>
<li>A track record of success building, developing and retaining high-performing teams</li>
<li>A track record of building and maintaining strong relationships with clients, investors, ownership groups and vendors</li>
<li>Experience managing a budget and/or P&amp;L</li>
<li>Experience with a Revenue Management software platform (YieldStar preferred)</li>
<li>Proficiency with industry software (YARDI), apps and computer programs</li>
<li>Outstanding communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
</ul>
