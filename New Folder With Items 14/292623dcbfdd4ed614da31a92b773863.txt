<p><b>Company Overview:</b></p>
<p>
25% of the Fortune 50 trust Samasource to deliver secure, high-quality training data and validation for the technology teams driving humanity forward. From self-driving cars to smart hardware, Samasource fuels AI. Our staff are driven by a mission to expand opportunity for low-income people through the digital economy, and our social business model has helped lift over 50,000 people out of poverty. <b>Check us out on the BBC!</b></p>

About You:</b></p>

We're committed to hiring a high-caliber, hands-on, entrepreneurial-thinker to strategically lead Finance. We're looking for someone who is:</p>

<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Reporting to the COO, the VP of Finance will partner with fellow Samasource executive team members and lead us as our financial advisor. <i>This position can be based in our San Francisco HQ </i><b><i>or</i></b><b><i> </i></b><i>our NY office.</i></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Own the company's financial plan including regular updates and adjustments to the financial model and communication of key elements to the management team.</li>
<li>
Drive the quarterly/annual budgeting and planning process, as well as long-range financial planning. Plan for the downside and find opportunities for the upside.</li>
<li>
Complete monthly/quarterly budget vs. actual variance analysis and drive accountability across the company.</li>
<li>
Oversee external firms (accounting/tax/audit) and ensure accuracy and timeliness of the monthly accounting close process.</li>
<li>
Communicate and present our financial results to relevant audiences including company updates, investor updates, board updates and materials.</li>
<li>
Lead strategic analysis of major initiatives and products to assess ROI, Payback Period, etc. Evaluate the risks/rewards and provide the data to enable better business decisions.</li>
<li>
Advise on compensation strategy including equity and bonus planning.</li>
<li>
Optimize cash flow and handle treasury management.</li>
<li>
Assist the sales cycle on price negotiations with large enterprise customers.</li>
<li>
Streamline the billing process, including selection of financial systems that will support billing and financial operations.</li>
<li>
Build and develop the Finance and Accounting Team.</li>
<li>
Embody our core values and uphold our unique mission-driven culture. We value diversity and inclusivity, and encourage team members to be their authentic selves at work.</li>
<li>
Collaborate and share financial best practices. We are continuous learners.</li>
<li>
Deliver meaningful feedback to your teammates. We believe in radical candor and promote active transparency.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
10+ years of progressive finance leadership experience.</li>
<li>
Experience at a venture-backed SaaS company (ideally post Series B), with Sr. Dir./VP responsibility for financial planning, analysis, accounting, and audited financials &amp; fluent in SaaS metrics.</li>
<li>
Track record as a trusted advisor and business partner - driving business insights, actions, and accountability across departments with a customer service-oriented attitude.</li>
<li>
Accomplished leader and coach - ready to manage and build an all-star team.</li>
<li>
Thrives in a dynamic, fast-paced work environment.</li>
<li>
Exhibits strong written and oral communication skills and can influence across the organization. You inspire confidence and can garner support for strategic initiatives.</li>
<li>
Adept at change management and defining new processes.</li>
<li>
Experienced in developing and presenting business plans, models, and financial forecasts to management and the investment community.</li>
<li>
Able to select and implement billing and ERP systems.</li>
<li>
Diligent, with an eye for details and strive for accuracy and operational excellence.</li>
<li>
Scrappy, can show your grit, and willing to pitch in where needed.</li>
<li>
Applies good judgment and can maintain confidentiality of sensitive customer data.</li>
<li>
Bonus Points: Previously raised VC financing or assisted with M&amp;A.</li>
</ul>
