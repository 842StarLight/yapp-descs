<h2>Product Marketing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Product Marketing Consultant will have a dual role in the organization. They will create new and revise life insurance marketing collateral, both digital and written. They will also have responsibility for life insurance competitive support for both external and internal audiences.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES MAY INCLUDE, BUT ARE NOT LIMITED TO, THE FOLLOWING:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinating life product research on top industry designs, pricing, dividend and interest crediting, compensation, marketing programs and underwriting practices
</li>
<li>Conducting in-depth analysis on research to determine where Ohio National's life product portfolio fits within the competitive landscape and identifying opportunities and threats to product positioning
</li>
<li>Facilitating discussions and conducting presentations of analysis to primary business stakeholders that will influence strategic decisions on product development and positioning
</li>
<li>Creating, printing and distributing life insurance marketing content for various audiences, media and applications in conjunction with graphic designers
</li>
<li>Providing assessment, tracking and recommendations for new programs and for enhancing existing marketing efforts
</li>
<li>Providing marketing consultation, recommendations and strategies aligned with corporate goals
</li>
<li>Educating home office support teams, agents, distribution managers and Regional Officers through a variety of presentations, meetings and memos focused on industry sales and product trends, competition analysis and highlighting the core strengths of the company's life products
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>THE IDEAL CANDIDATE WILL HAVE THE FOLLOWING:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>A bachelor’s degree or equivalent in English, communications, journalism, marketing, public relations, advertising or similar field
</li>
<li>At least five years of experience in a sales, marketing or research role in the financial services or insurance industry
</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, along with experience writing marketing content, with command of grammar, style, punctuation and proof reading and able to follow corporate/AP style
</li>
<li>Creativity, flexibility, adaptability and ability to problem solve
</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, project management, coordination and detail skills
</li>
<li>Customer service mindset and be able to work and thrive in a collaborative environment
</li>
<li>Able to work independently, prioritize effectively and meet deadlines
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT:</b> This job operates in an office setting. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, phones, photocopiers, filing cabinets and fax machines. This is largely a sedentary role; however, some filing is required. This position requires the ability to lift files, open filing cabinets and bend or stand as necessary.<br>
