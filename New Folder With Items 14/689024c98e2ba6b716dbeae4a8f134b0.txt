About the Job</b></div>
<div>
The HomeX portfolio operations team partners with HomeX executives and our portfolio of service companies to generate long-term value. The team designs and implements strategies to unlock growth and strengthen operations across our rapidly growing portfolio.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The HomeX portfolio operations team is comprised of experienced consultants and executives who partner with our investment company executives to generate long-term value. The team works to develop plans that unlock growth and strengthen operations via scalable and repeatable processes and methodologies.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We are searching for a Senior Manager of Portfolio Operations who will lead our go-to-market and activation efforts. As a lead, the individual will work closely with the executive teams within the portfolio to drive improvements opportunities such as sales and operations productivity, in addition to developing best practices and strengthening connections across the HomeX ecosystem.</div>
