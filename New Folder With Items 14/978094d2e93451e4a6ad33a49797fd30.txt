<h2>Pharmacy Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Supporting Community Healthcare is a rewarding role. LifeLong Medical Care is looking for a <b>Pharmacy Technician</b><b> </b>to work at one of our sites in Berkeley. The <b>Pharmacy Technician</b> works with licensed clinicians and clinic support staff to direct patients to appropriate resources for medications. Assists providers to expedite prescription refills, assists with pharmacy information requests, manages in-clinic dispensing, and is responsible for ordering, receiving, and managing medications for the clinic dispensary.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This is a full time, benefit eligible position. <b>Valid California Pharmacy Technician certification </b>required.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><i>
LifeLong Medical Care is a large, multi-site, Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC) with a rich history of providing innovative healthcare and social services to a wonderfully diverse patient community. Our patient-centered health home is a dynamic place to work, practice, and grow. We have 16 primary care health centers and deliver integrated services including psychosocial, referrals, chronic disease management, dental, health education, home visits, and much, much more. LifeLong Medical Care is an Equal Opportunity Employer.</i></div>
<div></div>

Benefits</i></b></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Essential Functions </b><b>- </b><b>«««««</b><b> </b>(Highest time requirements)</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
General</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Provides LifeLong patients with medication resource support, information, and management.
</li>
<li>Helps patients determine the most appropriate sources for their prescription medications and referring patients to appropriate programs and resources— discount pharmacies, Patient Assistance Programs, Dispensary, or Lifelong contract pharmacies.
</li>
<li>Assist providers with prescribing, including formulary expertise and Prior Authorization technical assistance.</li>
<li>
Acts as internal technical expert regarding medication management and purchasing.</li>
<li>
Provides clinic-level 340B compliance and expertise.</li>
<li>
Maintains clinic drug management processes and records in accordance with CA Board of Pharmacy regulations, DEA, and FDA requirements.</li>
<li>
Helps manage clinic medication expenditures.</li>
<li>
Assists with regulatory audits</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>
Manages Refill requests </b>«««««</div>
<ul>
<li>
Reviews incoming patient meds refill requests and makes recommendations regarding required patient care and refills based on written protocols.</li>
<li>
Refers refill requests to providers or RNs according to protocols.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>
Medication Access Support:</b> ««««</div>
<ul>
<li>
Assists Health PAC/FSFS/Medicare and uninsured patients including donut hole and patients whose meds are not covered despite other coverage to access Medication Programs:.</li>
<li>
Works with uninsured patients to determine the most appropriate sources for their prescription medications and referring patients to appropriate programs and resources—Patient Assistance Programs, discount pharmacies, Dispensary, or Lifelong contract pharmacy. This activity is for all patients, insured or uninsured.
</li>
<li>Determines Lifelong Health PAC formulary medications to be dispensed directly to patient from clinic by analyzing provider prescribing patterns</li>
<li>
Verifies eligibility for medication programs and updates/maintaining pharmacy data base with Lifelong contract pharmacies</li>
<li>
Works with providers to ensure adherence to Lifelong Health PAC formulary</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>
Resolves pharmacy eligibility and medication issues: </b>«««</div>
<ul>
<li>
Assists patients, providers, and nursing staff in researching and resolving non-refill related -pharmacy issues, such as insurance issues, claims processing, adjudication, and formulary coverage.</li>
<li>
Interfaces with community pharmacies</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>
Dispensary Management </b><b>«««</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Assists Providers with dispensing and RNs with refills</li>
<li>
Keeps appropriate on hand supplies of all medications<ul>
<li>
Ordering and receiving dispensary medications from multiple sources</li>
<li>
Managing samples</li>
<li>
Ordering and maintaining Direct Relief and other donated meds</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Tracking all categories of medications—purchased 340B meds, DAP, Samples, Direct Relief and other donated meds</li>
<li>
Reports monthly of utilization, inventory, and expenditures</li>
<li>
Completes monthly dispensary inventories and annual end of fiscal year drug inventories</li>
<li>
Disposes of expired/outdated medications according to state and federal requirements</li>
<li>
Environmental maintenance— refrigerator, room, software</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>
Prior Authorizations (Provides technical expertise for Pas)</b><b>«««</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Researches patient prescription plan and demographic information</li>
<li>
Researches prescription plan formulary alternatives (formulary lookups and alternative resources)</li>
<li>
Initiates paperwork and forwarding to prescriber for consideration</li>
<li>
Documents and tracks completed Prior Authorization request forms, approvals or denials of requests via EHR</li>
<li>
Contacts insurance/pharmacy and/or patient for any necessary follow-up</li>
<li>
Transfers Medicare Part D prior authorizations each insurance plan year</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>
Drug Assistance Programs: </b>««</div>
<ul>
<li>
Assists patients with DAP program applications and meds</li>
<li>
Initiating DAP applications</li>
<li>
Receiving medications</li>
<li>
Tracking patient eligibility</li>
<li>
Ordering refills for existing DAP patients</li>
<li>
Serve as patient advocate to Patient Assistance Program</li>
<li>
Maintains records in accordance with CA Board of Pharmacy regulations and other regulatory entities</li>
<li>
Monthly reporting of financial savings to clinic</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Drug recalls:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Monitors dispensary and clinic stock meds inventory for drug recalls, completing required activities and paperwork</li>
<li>
Serve as patient advocate to Patient Assistance Programs</li>
<li>
Maintains records in accordance with CA Board of Pharmacy regulations and other regulatory entities</li>
<li>
Monthly reporting of financial savings to clinic</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Drug Rep liaison:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Point of contact for pharmaceutical sales reps</li>
<li>
Schedules presentations, events, and distributes information and pertinent communications to providers</li>
<li>
Receives, stores, and helps dispense medication samples</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Manage Secure Rx paper/pads/labels: (varies by clinic)</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Orders, receives and stores secure Rx paper/pads</li>
<li>
Distributes secure Rx pads to providers and tracks provider usage</li>
<li>
Maintains documentation including signature log books and documents use or destruction of secure Rx paper/pads</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Coordinates Medicare Part D program outreach and assistance (all clinics)</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Assist patients in comparing Medicare Part D prescription plans and assisting patients in enrollment process.</li>
<li>
Coordinate with University of Pacific, University of California San Francisco, and Touro University College of Pharmacy preceptors and student coordinators dates, times, and location of Open Enrollment outreach events.<ul>
<li>
Manages Lifelong staff and volunteer availability</li>
<li>
Reserve facilities, maintenance, and set-up and cleaning</li>
<li>
Initiate outreach calls, mailings, flyers, and other documentation, marketing, and promotional activities</li>
<li>
Assist patients in comparing and enrolling in Medicare Part D plans</li>
<li>
Check-in patients attending outreach events and assigning either Pharmacy student group or Lifelong staff volunteer to patient during event</li>
<li>
Follow up on any issues that were not able to be resolved during outreach visit</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
