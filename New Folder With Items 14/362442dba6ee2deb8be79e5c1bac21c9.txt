Manager</p>

<p></p>
<p>Summary</p>
<p>This<br>
position is a member of the management team. Manages the organ’s efforts to Retain,<br>
recruit, and expand relationships with Select Employee Groups (SEGs) and<br>
community organizations. Coordinate and execute RGCU corporate events. Achieve<br>
specific business development goals, document activities and prepare reports as<br>
needed.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Duties and<br>
Responsibilities include the following: (Other duties may be<br>
assigned). This job description in no way implies that the duties listed here<br>
are the only ones that the employee can be required to perform. The employee is<br>
expected to perform other tasks, duties and trainings as dictated by their<br>
supervisors/manager.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Manages</li></ul>
and ensures activities and results directly support Rio Grande Credit Union’s<br>
strategic operations, sales, and service goals, efforts, and vision.
<ul><li> Create,</li></ul>
develop, support, and refine the SEG program through communication and coordination<br>
of periodic events, incentives and defined standards.
<ul><li> Serve</li></ul>
as primary liaison between RGCU and employer groups to maintain and expand<br>
existing relationships.
<ul><li> Promote</li></ul>
RGCU through visible participation in community activities and raising funds<br>
for community events.
<ul><li> Work</li></ul>
with management team and key representatives of membership groups to sponsor<br>
events, provide presentations, and engage in on-site activities that introduce<br>
Rio Grande Credit Union to potential members, while providing sales and service<br>
to existing members.
<ul><li> Provide,</li></ul>
support, and participate in community organizations, including but not limited<br>
to chamber of commerce, community committees, CDFI development, and networking<br>
organizations.
<ul><li> Acts</li></ul>
as liaison for strategic partnerships with community partners and community<br>
development endeavors. Develops and coordinates<br>
criteria for strategic partnerships as well as initial implementation.
<ul><li> Direct</li></ul>
and coordinate activities for volunteers and employees in community events.
<ul><li> Develop,</li></ul>
prioritizes, and implement tactical project plans, including member promotions<br>
programs, press events, trade shows, benefits fairs, on-site visits, sales<br>
events, credit union events and community fundraisers.
<ul><li> Develops,</li></ul>
coordinates and deploys appropriate social media posts. Coordinates responses<br>
to public reviews.
<ul><li> Manage</li></ul>
theme and exhibit space layout development for events.
<ul><li> Coordinate</li></ul>
registration and payment procedures, promotional advertising and mailings, and<br>
corporate sponsorship activities.
<ul><li> Maintain</li></ul>
relationships and service level agreements with preferred vendors.
<ul><li> Assists</li></ul>
in other areas as directed by the VP of Marketing and Member Experience and<br>
President/CEO.<br>
<p></p>
<p>Supervisory<br>
Responsibilities</p>
<p>Directly supervise employees responsible for<br>
community outreach and events and SEG development. Carries out supervisory<br>
responsibilities in accordance with the organization’s policies and applicable<br>
laws. Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring and training employees;<br>
planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; rewarding and<br>
disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving problems.</p>
