<div>Position Description</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>Hours: Monday - Friday 8:00 am - 5:00 pm<br>
<br>
Responsible for providing effective and compliant delivery of Treasury Management products including ACH, RDC, Wires, Positive Pay, Merchant Services, etc.; works with account officers on expanding/prospecting existing relationships, maintaining current customer relationships, and growing new customer relationships.<br>
<br>
Provide secure, accurate, responsive, and courteous customer service to both internal and external customers at all times. Maintain strict confidentiality of customer and banking relationships.<br>
<br>
Primary objective of this position is to be a trusted advisor to every internal and external customer. Inspires confidence and builds lasting relationships by consistently exhibiting each of the trusted advisor skills in order to help our customers identify and achieve their financial goals.<br>
<br>
Essential Job Functions:<br>
<br>
1. Provide extraordinary front end phone support for Treasury Management/E-Banking Services to both internal and external customers with 90% or greater observation results.<br>
2. Responsible for growing Treasury Management banking relationships by reviewing prospect logs and working with other officers/prospective clients on suitable products.<br>
3. Initiate Sales, Implementation, and Support of all Treasury Management/E-Banking services.<br>
4. Primary contact for business development of new Treasury Management customers.<br>
5. Assists with the maintenance of Treasury Management systems, inventory, and Database.<br>
6. Maintain, organize, and distribute, data for Treasury Management customer prospect files to be used in prospect meetings.<br>
<br>
Other Functions:<br>
<br>
1. Assists with the verification of Treasury Management contracts completed by additional Treasury Management staff for accuracy as needed.<br>
2. Assists in ongoing monitoring of risks of Treasury Management customers.<br>
3. Responsible for prioritizing and delegating Treasury Management customer support requests to T3 staff as needed.<br>
4. General knowledge of all banking products for potential cross-selling opportunities.<br>
5. Other duties as requested by their supervisor or other management representatives of the bank.<br>
6. Must comply with all bank policies and procedures that are job related and/or consistent with business necessity.</div>
