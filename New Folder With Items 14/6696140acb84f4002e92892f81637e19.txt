<h2>Program Manager, Business Strategy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Catchpoint provides a Digital Experience Monitoring (DEM) SaaS solution for medium to large enterprises, helping them to better understand and improve the performance of their online systems. As a Business Strategy Program Manager, you will play a critical role in ensuring the success of projects within Catchpoint. You will work closely with the CEO and executive team, leading all aspects of project information including timelines, budgets, resource allocation, scope and definition.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive project implementations and delivery of key project deliverables, working collaboratively with all project partners.</li>
<li>
Manage the highly detailed scheduled of operational activities related to new product launches</li>
<li>
Develop and track project implementation plans. Provide detailed reports of progress against timeline and plan.</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and follow through. Provide work direction and general project management including scope, schedule and resource planning. Review of projects to ensure that the project is meeting technical and process specifications as outlined.</li>
<li>
Determine the complexity level of new product/project initiatives and proactively engage the right cross functional teams to develop a successful commercialization plan.</li>
<li>
Develop credibility to influence &amp; collaborate across all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>
Highlight and solve for risks to the launch timeline or customer experience</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>+5 years of commercialization program management, preferably for a SaaS or Software organization.</li>
<li>
Able to work independently, analyze problems and make decisions with minimal management intervention.</li>
<li>
Ability to focus on the big picture and manage timelines by: connecting all dots and understand key cross-functional dependencies, uncovering roadblocks &amp; defining critical paths, and anticipating problems and creating contingency plans</li>
<li>
Ability to escalate with clarity and urgency, effectively communicating (written or verbally) across the entire organization.</li>
<li>
Possess strong emotional intelligence, facilitative leadership and communication skills: model calm, poised leadership in entrepreneurial, fast-paced environment, able to take ambiguous situations and add structure and clarity for the team</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>

OVERVIEW:</b></p>
<p>
Catchpoint is revolutionizing end-user experience monitoring to help companies deliver amazing digital experiences. We are proud to partner with digital innovators like Google, L'Oréal, Verizon, Oracle, LinkedIn, Honeywell, Priceline, and Qualtrics, who trust Catchpoint to improve their brand experience and drive their business success.</p>
