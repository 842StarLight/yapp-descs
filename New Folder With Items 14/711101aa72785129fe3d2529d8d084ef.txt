<h2>Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Medscape, a division of WebMD, develops and hosts physician portals and related mobile applications that make it easier for physicians and healthcare professionals to access clinical reference sources, stay abreast of the latest clinical information, learn about new treatment options, earn continuing medical education credits and communicate with peers.<br>
<br>
<b>All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability, or veteran status.</b></p>
<p>
WebMD is searching for a Data Analyst to join our team!</p>
<p><b>
What You Will Be Doing at WebMD</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Using SQL and analytic skills, analyze website engagement, clinical interests and client supplied targets data to create profiles, segmentation, and trend analyses identify members relevant to a program based on customer requirements</li>
<li>
Managing defined targets through the life of a campaign from Sales to Reporting</li>
<li>
Creating and maintaining documentation and user guides</li>
<li>
Creating standard and ad-hoc reports provided daily/weekly/monthly with insights into emerging patterns aligned with business initiatives that may be driving the changes</li>
<li>
Performing advanced analytics to understand and measure the success of programs, including click through analysis, retention, re-engagement and A/B testing.</li>
<li>
Providing historical performance, trending and benchmarking analysis</li>
<li>
Investigating changes in traffic, engagement, retention and click through and assisting in identifying root causes.</li>
<li>
Providing counsel on email and site tagging to facilitate tracking and reporting.</li>
</ul>
