<h2>Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>RECRUITER<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Career Description</b></p>
<p>
This positon will accelerate the growth of the firm by sourcing candidates for the Financial Advisor Role. The primary responsibility is to build high level referral sources with the financial representatives, staff and centers of influence within the community in order to obtain referrals and promote the Northwestern Mutual Financial Advisor career.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p>He or she builds high-level quality relationships in the community and within the office to promote the Northwestern Mutual (“NM”) career, generate referrals and develop centers of influence (“COI”). Creativity, exceptional communication skills, achievement drive are critical prerequisites for this career opportunity.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Recruiting
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute the firms recruiting plan to reach activity and productivity goals</li>
<li>Develop referral source networks through:</li>
<li>Office endorsements from Financial Advisors/ co-workers/ current and former interns</li>
<li>Develop community centers of influence (COI) and community connections</li>
<li>Social media and internet sourcing</li>
<li>Colleges (career center, career fairs, campus organizations, athletic programs faculty, alumni)</li>
<li>Northwestern Mutual Colleagues (Financial Advisors, co-workers, current and former interns)</li>
<li>Develop and coordinate marketing and recruiting</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Selection</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Master information and language regarding all aspects of the Financial representative career and each step of the selection process</li>
<li>Review resumes and schedule initial interviews</li>
<li>Conduct pre-screen and initial interviews of all candidates entering the process; Administer selection profiles</li>
<li>Direct all candidates through the selection process and series of interviews with management</li>
<li>Maintain integrity of the selection process to ensure proper candidates are selected</li>
<li>Effectively communicate with candidates</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Accountability and Activity Tracking</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Report weekly activity to the leadership team</li>
<li>Coordinate regular (weekly, monthly &amp; quarterly) meetings with the leadership team</li>
<li>Coordinate monthly meetings with FR’s to discuss potential referral opportunities</li>
<li>Develop proficiency and use of recruiting software to maintain candidate databases and accurate records of prospects to ensure efficiency of the selection process</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Miscellaneous</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain sufficient inventory of all recruiting materials &amp; supplies needed</li>
<li>Develop programs/contests as needed to enhance recruiting results</li>
<li>Back-up contracting and licensing responsibilities</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualification Criteria</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required</li>
<li>Three years progressively responsible work experience, preferably in sales, recruiting or financial services industry preferred</li>
<li>Strong work ethic, integrity, effective communication skills required with the ability to build rapport and influence others</li>
</ul>
