<h2>Sales Director, Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Analytics Sales Director will drive Analytics services revenue and order booking in the region. This will include the below technology areas:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Advanced Analytics</li>
<li>
Data warehouse/Marts</li>
<li>
Business Intelligence</li>
<li>
Data Modelling</li>
<li>
Big Data Analytics (Lake, Ingestion, Mining, Analytics)</li>
<li>
ETL/Data Migration</li>
<li>
Master Data Management/ Data Governance / Data Quality</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Create Leads and positions Analytics solutions with clients.</li>
<li>
Strike domain led conversions using Analytics use cases that would address customer scenarios/requirements and also business pains.</li>
<li>
Influence RFPs and spec them to our advantage.</li>
<li>
Work with internal stakeholders seamlessly and follow up to close proposals with the right commercial elements, if need be to negotiate with relevant stakeholders internally to create the right commercial constructs for scope identified.</li>
<li>
Negotiate and close commercial and contract terms with customer.</li>
<li>
Participate in events and present POVs, and represent Wipro Analytics.</li>
<li>
Work with partners/OEMs to come out with GTM (Go to market) plans.</li>
</ul></div>
<div>

<div><ul><li>

<div><b>
Experience:</b></div>
<div><ul>

<li>
Should have 5-7 years Sales or sales enablement experience in enterprise accounts, with a good ability to farm these accounts.</li>
</ul></div>

Behavioral Competencies:</b></div>
