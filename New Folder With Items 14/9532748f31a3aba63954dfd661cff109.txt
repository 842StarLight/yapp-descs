<h2>Programs Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>The Pinnacle Leadership Foundation</b> provides a unique setting for groups to focus on team building, leadership development, strategy and planning. Throughout the 400+ acre property found in Kensington, NH, you will find a variety of open air and enclosed meeting spaces where participants can interact, learn, reflect, challenge themselves, and grow. We also offer a challenging ropes course, miles of hiking trails, and reflective open water spaces.</p>
<p>This person will be detail-oriented, organized and flexible when supporting all the activities occurring on the property. This position is full-time and occasional weekend work is required. The Programs Administrative Assistant person will report directly to the Events Manager.</p>
<p>*Local applicants apply only.</p>
<p><b>Duties to include but not limited to: </b></p>
<p>Enter event details into event management software</p>
<p>Act as liaison between client and PLF to finalize program details</p>
<p>Coordinate or assist with arrangements for program activities such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>Setting up meeting space</li>
<li>Compiling offsite deliverable's</li>
<li>Preparing for student programming</li>
<li>Grocery shopping to stock overnight cabins</li>
<li>Mailings</li>
<li>Inventory of building needs (office supplies, refill water in fridge, easel paper/markers, etc.)</li>
<li>Opening and closing cabins for overnight groups, coordinate cleaning of all buildings on property</li>
<li>Other programmatic tasks as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>To be successful in this position you have the following skills and traits: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must excel at attention to the details, strong organizational skills and accurate record keeping;</li>
<li>Ability to work within a team, and also work independently;</li>
<li>Ability to set priorities and meet deadlines; able to respond to changes in priorities and can multi-task;</li>
<li>Ability to anticipate and take initiative;</li>
<li>Must hold valid driver’s license;</li>
<li>Must be able to exhibit good judgment, discretion and confidentiality;</li>
<li>Team player and resourceful in problem solving, reliable and flexible;</li>
<li>Must be open to our culture and values – risk-taking and open &amp; courageous communication;</li>
<li>Commitment to community and dedication to change people’s lives</li>
<li>Familiarity with Tripleseat sales and event management software a plus</li>
<li>Successful employment will require a background check</li>
</ul>
