<h2>Partner Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Description</b></p>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p>Domo is changing the way Executives manage their business. Pivotal within the most innovative business management platform, Domo’s Partner Relationship Manager(PRM)<b> </b>team is a game changer in managing strategic partner relationships. Domo is in need of experienced PRM’s who have experience managing SaaS based Partner relationships. A successful candidate will have demonstrated sustained exceptional relationship management, innovation, creativity, insight, and good judgment.</p>
<p><b>Team Culture:
</b></p>
<p><b>Work - </b>A PRM on the Partner Services team at Domo works in partnership with a Partner Sales Manager (PSM) and is responsible for enabling the execution of a partner’s go to market (GTM) strategy, advocating for partner’s needs internally, holding ongoing performance reviews, and coordinating professional services escalations.</p>
<p><b>Fight - </b>We fight to satisfy all Domo customers and partners. Our goal is to do everything possible to make a partner’s interactions with Domo a smooth, timely, and positive experience for the customer.
</p>
<p><b>Innovate – </b>We aren’t just overseeing a partner’s relationship with Domo. We are leading strategic conversations around how a partner can continually evolve the way Domo’s platform and data can transform the way customers’ run their business.
</p>
<p><b>Create – </b>Our Domo PRM’s need to be able to create long lasting relationships with our partners and customers. We need to create trust, so our partners know we will deliver on our promises and deadlines.
</p>
<p><b>Serve - </b>We serve the partner and customer. In short, we enable our partner ecosystem to provide unparalleled quality and value to our collective customer base.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The Partner Relationship Manager(PRM) owns the enablement of a partner’s go to market (GTM) strategy</li>
<li>Works with partners on certification failures and remediation</li>
<li>Works with partners and PSM’s on assignment of services work both pre and post sales</li>
<li>Performs performance reviews on a reoccurring basis with partners</li>
<li>Exception handling of daily project and/or deal escalations, and coordinates with all internal teams to drive a timely and efficient resolution</li>
<li>Works with partners, PSMs, support, consulting, product, CSMs, and Partner Services to resolve any blockers to the fulfillment of service obligations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The successful candidate will have a minimum of 5 years (or equivalent) relationship management experience in a software product and/or industry.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated excellence at handling complex escalation topics to both executive and technical personnel</li>
<li>Working knowledge of specialized BI tools and solution providers</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and manage multiple task assignments</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills, including presentation skills (MS Visio, MS PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Strong problem solving and troubleshooting skills with the ability to exercise mature judgment</li>
<li>Experience enabling strategic partner roadmaps, meeting with business owners, and performing current state analysis</li>
<li>Experience understanding conceptual and technical designs, including the use of object-oriented analysis and design techniques</li>
<li>Experience in recognizing business problems and recommending/developing solutions</li>
<li>College degree in related field and/or equivalent experience and education</li>
</ul>
