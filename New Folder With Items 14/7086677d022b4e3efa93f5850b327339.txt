With an extensive breadth of products and growing digital marketing innovation, this is an exciting time to be part of Honeywell Aerospace's Customer Marketing group. (Read more about Honeywell Aerospace at aerospace.honeywell.com.)</p>
 <p>Reporting to the director of global marketing, the analytics manager will uncover actionable account and customer insights to increase revenue, lead generation and brand awareness. As a subject matter expert in customer relationship management platforms, marketing automation, website analytics and advertising dashboards, you will stitch data together for better comprehension and visualization.</p>
 <p>Honeywell Aerospace has a fast-paced environment and the optimal candidate will be goal-oriented, ambitious and not afraid to recommend new ways to report. In this role, you will pitch in to help your team in analytics and lead qualification while trying to automate as much as possible and drive accuracy.</p>
 <p>We value a “win and have fun” approach and adhere strictly to our code of conduct, valuing our eight behaviors at Honeywell. We are the #futureshapers of the Aerospace customer decision journey.</p>
 <p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
 <ul>
<li>Automate reporting across multiple systems</li>
<li>
Effectively aggregate data by account, market, business or region, as needed</li>
<li>
Evaluate effectiveness of global customer marketing campaigns</li>
<li>
Analyze online user behavior and advise on optimization of the customer journey to increase leads and/or revenue</li>
<li>
Meet monthly deadlines for Aerospace reporting needs, as required</li>
<li>
Matrix manage and mentor marketing data scientists and lead qualification specialists</li>
<li>

Provide proactive and reactive analysis of customer traffic and opportunities</li>
<li>
Identify and resolve gaps in website tagging and tracking</li>
<li>
Improve current digital dashboards as needed</li>
</ul>
