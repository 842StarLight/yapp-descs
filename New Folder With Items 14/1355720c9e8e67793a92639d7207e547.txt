<div><b>What We Do:</b></div>
<div>
<b>The Sales Capture Director</b> Responsible for leading and driving sales of Accenture Security services to target G2000 clients through both direct and high touch channel sales motions.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key responsibilities include:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Direct Client Relationship Development: Develops new and leverage existing relationships with potential buyers in the assigned region. Leads all sales activities with assigned direct accounts.
</li>
<li>High Touch Channel Management: Works with and supports assigned Accenture LLP client teams to sell Accenture Security services – as both standalone solutions as well as part of broader transformation sales.
</li>
<li>Demand Stimulation and Opportunity Generation: Consistently identifies opportunities to increase market awareness of Accenture Security capabilities and leads RFP response activities.
</li>
<li>Sales Process Leadership: Constructs and executes strategic opportunity plans, including win themes, win tactics &amp; action plan, relationship plan, deal shaping and price-to-win. Reviews solution plans, cost models and written proposals as appropriate to ensure alignment with sales strategy, win themes, and price-to-win.</li>
</ul>
