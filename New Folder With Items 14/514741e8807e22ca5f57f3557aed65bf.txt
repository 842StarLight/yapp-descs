<br>
<p>
Oldcastle Enclosure Solutions (OES), a CRH company, has united the Carson®, Christy®, and Synertech® brands to form the largest manufacturer of plastic, polymer, and concrete utility enclosure products serving the municipal water, irrigation, electric, communications, and transportation markets. OES has 11 manufacturing and distribution centers strategically located across the U.S. and Canada.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Description</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Region Sales Manager (RSM) – will be responsible for building and leading a network of independent sales agencies to achieve the organization’s sales growth and customer satisfaction objectives in Northern California. The RSM will lead and coordinate the customer centric activity at key end user accounts, contractors and our distribution partners.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Region Sales Manager is a high energy self-starter who is driven to succeed, can energize others to achieve team success, has effective written and verbal communication skills across all levels of an organization, and is effective at executing across the sales opportunity lifecycle. The Ideal candidate will have strong relationship building skills; working knowledge of the constructioin market including end users, engineering and EPC firms, distributors and other market participants; experience working cross functionally to effectively balance customer and business needs; and an outside in, customer centric mindset.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Function</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Region Sales Manager (RSM) – will have responsibility for generating sales in the defined territory for Oldcastle Enclosure Solutions and in support of Oldcastle Infrastructure.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Duties</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Generate profitable sales of OES products in each market segment.</li>
<li>
Develop sales and marketing plans and strategies for long term business growth while achieving the company’s annual sales growth goals.</li>
<li>
Develop and manage a network of Independent Sales Representative to provide market coverage and generate sales.</li>
<li>
Lead and execute the sales process from prospecting through proposal, order and post order activities to drive desired sales outcomes</li>
<li>
Manage key customer relationships and lead the development and closing of all strategic customer opportunities.</li>
<li>
Understand customer needs and expectations to develop our value proposition.</li>
<li>
Assimilate and communicate market segment research and competitor analysis.</li>
<li>
Provide detailed and accurate sales forecasting.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>

<li>
Demonstrated customer champion with a strong aptitude for understanding customer needs.</li>
<li>
Effective cross functional collaborator who can balance external and internal objectives.</li>
<li>
High initiative and results driven; a self-starter who operates with speed, simplicity, and passion to succeed.</li>
