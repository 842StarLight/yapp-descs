<h2>Sales and Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>General Description: </b></p>
<p>The Sales and Marketing Specialist is responsible for planning, developing, and implementing all the Organization’s marketing, corporate image, product image, public relationship, and direct online platform strategies as well as activities both external and internal to enhance company value and yearly revenue.</p>
<p><b>Job Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for overseeing the development of product packages, product review, press release, advertisement, gift boxes, trade shows, web design, online advertisement in the most cost-effective and efficient way.</li>
<li>In charge and support the eCommerce sales platform by strongly establish digital marketing, social media, content enrichment, online traffic counts, conversion rate and SEO marketing by control of the best ROI.</li>
<li>Photograph product images to be used in websites, catalogs, and social media.</li>
<li>Develop, film, production, and post-production editing of video projects.</li>
<li>Develop new and innovative ways to increase sales and drive traffic leveraging social marketing initiatives, third party marketing tools, and promotions.</li>
<li>Utilize third party portal and dashboard tools to analyze sales trends for forecasting purposes and to seek sales opportunities.</li>
<li>In charge of making sure the Company stays competitive and relevant by staying up to date and prioritizing on trends.</li>
<li>Work closely with the sales team to ensure that the best and most current product content is at the forefront of the sales priority list.</li>
<li>Plan and conduct market research to clearly identify opportunity to increase sales with existing and potential customer channels.</li>
<li>Analyze and adjust marketing budget, strategies and programs to increase effectiveness.</li>
<li>Communicate with outside advertising agencies and ongoing campaigns negotiate contract terms.</li>
<li>Analyze marketing programs and adjusts strategy and tactics to increase effectiveness.</li>
<li>Provide post-event reports, analysis and regular status reports on marketing programs.</li>
<li>Plan and conduct market research to identify opportunity to increase sales.</li>
<li>Develop brand advertisement strategies in order to grow sales.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain consistent corporate image throughout product lines, promotional materials and events.</li>
<li>Maintain and ensure good care of all marketing equipment, facility and product samples. Well organize product related files, instruction files, graphic images and video.</li>
<li>Manage and organize trade shows including planning, booth design, booth set up, logistics, coordination and marketing materials etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Necessary Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage SEO, marketing database, social media and advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Strong proficiency with standard IT applications, such as Photoshop, Illustrator, MS Office, Google Adwords etc.</li>
<li>Excellent writing and communication skills, copywriting experience a plus</li>
<li>Proven ability in photography, videography, video and photo editing</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze, and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures, or government regulations. Ability to write reports, business correspondence, and procedure manuals. Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers, and the general public.</li>
<li>Ability to add, subtract, multiply, and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions and decimals. Ability to compute rate, ratio, and percent and to draw and interpret bar graphs.</li>
<li>Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists. Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral diagram, or schedule form.</li>
</ul>
