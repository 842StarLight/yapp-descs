<p>
Oldcastle Infrastructure, a CRH company, is the leading manufacturer of precast concrete, polymer concrete, and plastic products in the United States. Our products range from utility vaults, reinforced concrete pipe, catch basins, drainage and septic tanks, to retaining walls, storm shelters, wall panels, concrete barriers, a variety of prestressed concrete products and more. With more than 80 locations nationwide and 3,000 plus employees, Oldcastle Infrastructure is committed to upholding core values of reliability, quality, and service in cutting edge ways.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The primary role of the Regional Sales Manager is to provide leadership and direction for a sales team that meets and exceeds customer expectations, develop and execute sales strategies to exceed the team financial (top line sales, margin expectations, and EBITDA) and non-financial goals. The Sales Manager is charged with assembling and leading a team to identify and capitalizing on opportunities, retaining and servicing existing customers, and supporting the primary focus of growing our financial success through world class customer service, highly accurate cost estimating and quoting, and superior market knowledge and awareness. We have manufacturing plants located in the Chesapeake Region – Fredericksburg, VA and Edgewood, MD.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Lead a team of Outside Sales and Estimators to drive new business and grow existing accounts in the respective market</li>
<li>
Implements corporate commercial initiatives and builds and develops a sales team through KPI tracking, coaching, and leadership</li>
<li>
Develops and implements strategic sales plans to accommodate sales goals.</li>
<li>
Manage the budget and forecast for the territory and individual sales representatives</li>
<li>
Proactively call and visit general contractors, sub-contractors, and municipal engineers to follow-up on quoted business, establish and build relationships that lead to profitable growth.</li>
<li>
Drive total Oldcastle Infrastructure message by recognizing opportunities and collaborating with other Oldcastle business units including but not limited to working closely with the internal storm water and enclosures teams to cross sell and support strategic initiatives.</li>
<li>
Work with the accounting team and sales team for tracking down accounts receivable issues.</li>
<li>
Reviews market analyses and responds to customer’s needs in a focused and service-oriented manner, quickly addressing issues to provide world class results.</li>
<li>
Implements and manages systems that provide complete, accurate quotes that are consistently formatted and provided in a timely manner tailored to the customer’s project needs.</li>
<li>
Provides market based pricing based on commercial tools, CRM software, dashboards, real market feedback, and industry knowledge.</li>
<li>
Review and comment on all contracts, and take the lead on resolving customer service issues and contractual issues.</li>
<li>
Works closely with other managers</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
