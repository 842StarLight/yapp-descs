<h2>Marketing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>What You’ll Be Doing:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and track the company’s marketing data points that drive bottom line growth, focusing primarily, but not limited to, email marketing and customer data</li>
<li>Optimize automated flows that promote growth and engage customers throughout the life cycle of purchasing conversion</li>
<li>Test, analyze, and optimize email and digital marketing content strategies to achieve growth and achievement</li>
<li>Identify, create and develop ongoing content strategies to identified customer segments</li>
<li>Track trends in our digital marketing assets, advertising, and sales funnels</li>
<li>Work collaboratively to dream up new ways to more effectively reach consumers and drive customer growth</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>
Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Authorized to work in the US</li>
<li>5+ years of analyst experience, preferably in marketing</li>
<li>3+ years experience in using data to develop and measure the effectiveness of content marketing
</li>
<li>Experience in analyzing and tracking many data points, while coming to actionable conclusions based upon the data</li>
<li>Experience in A/B testing
</li>
<li>Experience with Excel and Access Database (all applicants subject to assessment)</li>
<li>Experience with email marketing platforms a plus</li>
<li>Experience with writing SQL a plus</li>
<li>Experience working in ecommerce a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>What You’ll Have:</p>
<ul>
<li>A passion for data analysis and a natural curiosity</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Self-directed and self-starter</li>
<li>Ability to work well under pressure</li>
<li>Strong work ethic combined with a "team-first" mentality</li>
<li>Ability to manage time effectively and to drive multiple projects forward at once</li>
</ul>
