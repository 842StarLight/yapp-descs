<div>Lucid is a market research platform that provides access to authentic, first-party data in over 90 countries. Our products and services enable anyone, in any industry, to ask questions of targeted audiences and find the answers they need – fast. These answers can be used to uncover consumer motivations, increase revenue, and measure the impact of digital advertising. Founded in 2010, Lucid is headquartered in New Orleans, LA with offices in Dallas, New York, London, Sydney, Singapore, Gurgaon, Prague, and Hamburg.</div>

The Opportunity</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Lucid seeks an experienced Account Manager to create long-term, trusting relationships with our customers and grow existing accounts. The Account Manager’s role is to oversee a portfolio of customers, develop new business from existing clients and actively seek new sales opportunities.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Develop strong relationships with customers, connecting with key business stakeholders to activate and scale existing customers</li>
<li>
Serve as the lead point of contact for all customer account management matters</li>
<li>
Handle both strategic high level management and transactional sales activity including creating project customer bids</li>
<li>
Answer customer queries and identify new business opportunities</li>
<li>
Handle both strategic high level management and transactional sales activity including creating project customer bids</li>
<li>
Work closely with cross-functional internal teams including Customer Success Managers and Project Success teams to improve the entire customer experience</li>
<li>

<li>
Forecast and track key account metrics</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
