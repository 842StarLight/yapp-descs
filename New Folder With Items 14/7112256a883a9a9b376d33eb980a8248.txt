<h2>Digital Marketing Manager-Vanity Fair Brands</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Description/Job Summary</b></p>
<b>
Fruit of the Loom, </b>an independent, wholly owned subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway Inc. &amp; leading international basic apparel company, has an immediate opening for <b>a Digital Marketing Manager </b>to support Vanity Fair intimate apparel at its corporate headquarters in Bowling Green, KY.<br>
<br>
The Digital Marketing Manager has responsibility and accountability for managing the marketing efforts related to Vanity Fair Brand digital communities to ultimately drive business unit growth and profitability (P&amp;L responsibility). Responsible for translating marketing brand strategy into short and long-range digital brand strategies inclusive of pricing, product mix, brand communication, consumer segmentation and targeting, distribution, etc. The Manager represents the business unit across the organization, acts as the digital liaison to and from the Marketing team, and works cross-functionally to achieve goals.<br>
<p><b>Responsibilities/Duties</b></p>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Brand / Business Strategy</b><br>
Collaborate with Marketing team to develop and execute short and long-term retailer digital strategies. Execute global retailer.com digital content to support the marketing strategy, drive brand recognition and promote compelling messaging resulting in increased sales activity and leads. Represent the voice of the Marketing team to the Digital / Ecommerce team.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<b>Website Content Load</b><br>
Partner with Marketing and Digital / Ecommerce teams to load content (copy, pdp, imagery, alternate images, video, etc.) on retailer websites by established deadlines. Provide Marketing and Digital / Ecommerce team with deadlines when content is due.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div><div>
<b>Project Management:</b><br>
Manage indirect partnership with cross-functional teams and multiple marketing projects at various stages to a successful and timely execution. Requires strong coordination and communication across the various teams to ensure business objectives are met.
<ul>
<li>Manage the completion of marketing initiatives in partnership with cross-functional teams and potential external partners.
</li>
<li>Identify and work with the Digital / Ecommerce team on projects to add or enhance content on the retailer.com channels to increase relevance, engagement, and ultimately goal completion.
</li>
<li>Maintain an updated status report on project elements with clear action items and deliverables and follow-up with cross-functional team members and agencies as needed to ensure project elements are on track.
</li>
<li>Report out results of marketing initiatives. Advise marketing of the ROI on tactics and make recommendations for refinements to digital strategy and tactics.
</li>
<li>Deliver all aspects of projects on time.
</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<b>Budget Management</b><br>
Responsible for assigned brand(s) budget. Report on financial expenditures related to initiatives. Collaborate with business unit leader to ensure the digital spend is within objectives and budget.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div><div>
<b>Pricing / Trade Promotions</b><br>
Collaborate with Marketing, Sales and Digital / Ecommerce teams to provide recommendations to the business unit for trade and consumer promotional strategies. Provide pricing, promotional and media buy guidelines for retailer.com that reflects marketing's strategy and considers retailer promotional pricing and their strategies. For unique digital product, partner with Digital / Ecommerce team to develop pricing recommendations by analyzing cost, list price and suggested retail price to achieve brand and retailer target margins.<br>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Communications / Presentation</b><br>
Presentation creation and delivery across internal teams and external partners. Ensure team is providing appropriate messaging for the target audience. Facilitate meaningful discussion around feedback to move a project forward. As a digital marketing subject matter expert, serve as a point of contact for advice/opinions/education across the organization, especially to the Marketing team.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<b>Business Analytics &amp; Reporting</b><br>
Routine and ad hoc analysis and reporting. Track and measure metrics to support goals and strategic imperatives from various initiatives and communicate to appropriate management. Analyze and report on retailer.com results. Take corrective action or make recommended improvements as needed to optimize spending versus business goals.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<b>Retailer.com /Brand.com Product Portfolio Management</b><br>
Based on dynamic business needs, partner with Marketing, Sales and Digital / Ecommerce teams to develop and implement unique new product strategy for retailer.com and brand.com</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<b>Team Management</b><br>
Provide updates to members of Marketing team. Assist in training of department staff on digital marketing process and procedures. Responsible for day-to-day management of assigned staff. Manage performance and provide timely feedback. Support the continuous improvement of Marketing team process and performance.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<b>Inventory Management &amp; Forecasting</b><br>
Monitor retail sales and consumer demand trends to support the Sales team as they manage the forecast and maximize inventory levels.</div>
</div>
<br>
</div></div>
