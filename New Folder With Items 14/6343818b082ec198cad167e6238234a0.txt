<div><b>
Overview:</b></div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
The Media Artist is responsible for using visualization tools such as animation software and video game engines to create pre-visualizations of concerts and events. The media artist position supports sales, marketing, project design, and integration by creating visualizations that are both beautiful and physically accurate. Some of the deliverables a successful candidate will produce are still renderings, photo composites, 3D animations, 360 VR animations, and real time rendering experiences</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Create 3D architectural renderings from existing CAD geometry to support sales and design</li>
<li>
Use Cinema 4D to model fabrication ready elements for a show. Support these elements with animations and still renderings.</li>
<li>
Create real time visualizations from a mix of pre-existing CAD geometry and elements modeled in Cinema 4D.</li>
<li>
Present visualizations to Clients for final approval</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
