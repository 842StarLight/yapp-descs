<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The <b>Service Technician</b> reports to the <b>Service Manager(s).</b> The Service Technician will provide an exceptional customer service experience by providing quality service repairs, installations and preventative maintenance for our FoodService Division clientele. Additionally, the <b>Service Technician</b> will be responsible for:</div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Customer Relations</b><ul>
<li>
Establish a relationship with the customer so that the customer is satisfied with Royal Cup’s products and services.</li>
<li>
Represent Royal Cup in a professional manner at all times in all forms of customer communication and service.</li>
<li>
Represent Royal Cup’s professional image by following the dress code policy and by wearing company issued name tag at all times when on duty.</li>
<li>
Maintain a positive attitude at all times by taking ownership of own responsibility and actions.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>
Service Commitment</b><ul>
<li>
Equipment Repair</li>
<li>
Call customer within 2 hours of receiving call to assess the issue and give an E.T.A.</li>
<li>
Call customer if prior commitments cannot be met.</li>
<li>
Inform manager if any call is open more than forty eight hours.</li>
<li>
Repair Equipment in a timely manner, minimizing the customer’s service interruption.</li>
<li>
Make good equipment decisions (ie. when to swap equipment rather than repair).</li>
<li>
Manage equipment ordering through proper procedures.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>
Equipment Installation</b><ul>
<li>
Install all types of equipment in a manner that meets the customer’s requirements.</li>
<li>
Perform any pre-work, as required, to prepare for proper equipment installation. This pre-work may include connecting water lines, drilling holes, setting up drain lines and hooking up basic electrical components where “local codes” allow.</li>
<li>
Install and swap outs may require heavy lifting up to 75lbs.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>
Preventative Maintenance</b><ul>
<li>
Maintain equipment at optimal functionality by performing routine cleaning, necessary maintenance, and repairs in an organized and efficient manner, minimizing and/or preventing any interruption of service for the customer.</li>
<li>
Perform assigned National Accounts inspections within allotted time.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Customer Retention &amp; Revenue Growth
</b><ul>
<li>Maintain up to date product knowledge; be prepared to answer customer questions related to Royal Cup products and services.</li>
<li>
Maintain customer awareness and focus; while visiting an account, be alert and take notice of any “red flags” that signal dissatisfaction with our products or service. For example, noticing a competitor’s equipment or products being used and/or negative comments toward the company.</li>
<li>
Maintain a sales and service mentality; be aware of things that may lead to new account sales opportunities, such as new construction or referrals from customers.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>
Administrative Responsibilities</b><ul>
<li>
Accurate completion &amp; timely submission of all paperwork:<ul>
<li>
Equipment Loan Agreements, Transfers &amp; Receipts.</li>
<li>
Service Invoices tracking all service activities.</li>
<li>
Completed overtime sheets at the end of “B” &amp; “D” weeks to receive timely payment.</li>
<li>
Period Inventory Data
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Communication Standards:<ul>
<li>
Check voice mail and customer service messages at least twice daily.</li>
<li>
Respond to all voice, text and email messages promptly.</li>
<li>
Maintain current customer data programmed in the handheld device at all times.
</li>
<li>Tele-communication of handheld device data on a daily basis.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>
Asset / Expense Control
</b><ul>
<li>Inventory Controls:<ul>
<li>
Perform Period Inventories for equipment &amp; parts, keeping variances less than 10%.</li>
<li>
Keep parts inventory at par level without over-stocking.</li>
<li>
Return all defective parts and equipment under warranty for credit where applicable.
</li>
<li>Recycle parts and equipment where appropriate.
</li>
<li>Maintain appropriate equipment inventory to meet area demands.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Provide guidance to Territory Managers when ordering parts.</li>
<li>
Control vehicle expenses through proper maintenance and defensive driving.</li>
<li>
Follow all company safety procedures.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>Policy Responsibility</b><ul>
<li>
Always be mindful of and adhere to GMP’s, Quality and Food Safety requirements.</li>
<li>
Adhere to all company policies and procedures and guidelines.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
