<div><b>
Department Overview – Construction Management</b></div>
<div>
RCM is primarily responsible for construction cost management, building system evaluation, building department expediting, bidding, contract administration and construction. Related’s strong relationships with local quality contractors and union trade groups have enabled Related to develop one of the nation’s largest and most complex developments, including Time Warner Center, and the Hudson Yards. RCM works with both internal and external stakeholders including design, development, finance, marketing and sales teams. We are committed to the highest standards of excellence and strive to always exceed customer expectations and be the best in our industry.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div>
<div>The Related Companies is seeking a construction professional to join its construction management team (“RCM”) as a project manager (“PM”). As a PM you will be responsible for managing and directing the construction of Related’s commercial building(s). The successful candidate must possess the requisite education and experience that will permit them to manage the construction process and on larger projects, collaborate with senior construction management executives within the firm. The successful candidate must possess the interpersonal skills that will allow them to collaborate with Related’s in-house design, development, finance, marketing and sales teams.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the day-to-day operational aspects of a project or portion thereof.</li>
<li>
Serve as RCM’s primary liaison between general contractor, project consultants and internal development team.</li>
<li>
The ability to make decisions that consider underwriting models.</li>
<li>
Effectively communicates relevant project information to superiors.</li>
<li>
Resolves and/or escalates issues in a timely fashion.</li>
<li>
Understands how to communicate difficult/sensitive information tactfully.</li>
<li>
Formulate and executes project schedule.</li>
<li>
Administer document control procedures for all construction related documents.</li>
<li>
Manages the scope review process.</li>
<li>
Negotiate change orders.</li>
<li>
Manages the permit process and related governmental requirements.</li>
<li>
Manages and updates project budget.</li>
<li>
Holds regular status meetings with project team.</li>
<li>
Manage tenant fit-out process</li>
<li>
Perform daily on-site walk-thru’s to ensure contract performance, safety compliance, and quality control with the intent of delivering a best in class product.</li>
</ul>
</div>
