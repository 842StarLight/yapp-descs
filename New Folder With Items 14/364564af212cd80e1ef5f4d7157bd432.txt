<h2>Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>CHEERS!</b> Deutsch Family Wine &amp; Spirits is searching for an Item Master Analyst to join our team in Stamford, CT. If you are a Data Geek who loves to analyze and work with numbers, this is the position for you.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This role is an integral part of the broader Supply Chain team, responsible for working with our Order Fulfillment, Purchasing, Inventory, Financial Planning &amp; Analytics, Sales, Marketing and IT teams to ensure all item master data is executed timely and accurately, while continuously improving Supply Chain's processes and systems, resulting in the highest value possible. In addition to managing the Item Master in JDE, this role will be responsible for the execution of pricing across all platforms including various State Liquor Control Boards. The Item Master Analyst will support various departments, analyze and investigate supply chain issues, provide reporting upon request, coordinate projects and perform other tasks as requested.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Who Are You?</b></p>
<ul><li> Internship or 0 - 2 years’ experience in supply chain</li></ul>
<ul><li> JDE ERP experience preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent oral &amp; written communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to exercise judgment &amp; recommend solutions</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to research &amp; interpret information</li></ul>
<ul><li> Computer competencies (Word, Excel, Outlook, SharePoint, etc.)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Detail oriented</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to perform duties with minimal supervision</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to plan and organize heavy workload and work within tight deadlines</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to flourish in a team-oriented environment</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop and maintain productive working relationships with key members of other departments</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work through ambiguity and uncertainty</li></ul>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s Degree preferred</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>What Will You Do?</b></p>
<ul><li> Manage the Item Master data accurately across all DFWS and vendor platforms</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work closely with our Compliance team to ensure item integrity and federal, state, export and distributor compliance is met</li></ul>
<ul><li> Identify opportunities in the current processes and develop improved ways of working following master data best practices</li></ul>
<ul><li> Partner with FP&amp;A to efficiently manage the execution of fiscal year pricing plan across all DFWS companies</li></ul>
<ul><li> Generate and submit monthly price filings and control state quotes according to the fiscal year pricing plan</li></ul>
<ul><li> Coordinate with FP&amp;A and Marketing on all pricing announcements in accordance to our contractual distributor obligations</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage correspondence between various partners involved in the pricing process Keep management informed of project status and any related issues</li></ul>
