<p>As a Senior Mortgage Loan Officer at Fairport Savings Bank, your primary focus will be to:</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Provide Excellent Customer Service to each and every customer through each and every customer interaction</b></li>
<li><b>Drive sales results and increase FSB’s loan volume</b></li>
<li><b>Build your own book of business by strengthening and deepening relationships with referral sources</b></li>
<li>
<b>Maximize productivity</b> <b>by producing quality mortgage applications</b>
</li>
<li><b>Grow your own capabilities by participating in community and partner events</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Comprehends the various mortgage products offered through the Bank and the mortgage flow chart from application to closing</li>
<li>Identifies potential business primarily from referral sources such as realtors, builders, professional, and personal contacts and promotes FSB’s products and mortgage department</li>
<li>Serves existing customers’ mortgage needs and follows up on referrals from branch staff, telephone inquiries, and walk-in business</li>
<li>Follows up with pre-qualified customers and First Home Club customers to assess their progress and keep them engaged in the sales cycle</li>
<li>Develops and maintains relationships with external sources through sales calls and other marketing efforts</li>
<li>Schedules and conducts mortgage and home equity interviews at times convenient for the loan customer</li>
<li>Enters data in the computer and uses Calyx reports to manage pipeline, including loan status and rate lock expiration date</li>
<li>Evaluates preliminary documentation to determine if the loan application meets FSB, agency, and investor guidelines</li>
<li>Utilizes LP, DU, and GUS to ensure the viability for a sold loan</li>
<li>Works closely with loan processing to properly structure various loan scenarios</li>
<li>Coordinates loans with non-profit agencies (i.e. Pathstone, Neighborworks) to be used in conjunction with 1st mortgages</li>
<li>Conducts weekly mortgage status calls to borrowers and referral sources (i.e. realtors and builders)</li>
<li>Serves as primary contact with customers, realtors, builders, attorneys, and other parties related to the transaction as needed; follows up with attorney’s to ensure timely delivery of title reports; follows up with borrowers concerning any missing documentation required for file submission</li>
<li>Works with loan processing and underwriting staff to build a complete mortgage file. Follows throughout the process to ensure timely commitment and closing</li>
<li>Sends follow up mailings to closed borrowers, thanking them for their business and asking for referrals</li>
<li>Active cross sells other Bank products and services</li>
<li>Meets established annual loan production goals</li>
<li>Represents the Bank at various professional functions (i.e. realtor picnics, etc.) and always serves as an ambassador for Fairport Savings Bank’s mortgage department</li>
<li>Must be available for evening and weekend appointments</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Education:</b> Associates’ degree or equivalent</li>
<li>
<b>Experience:</b> Five or more years of Mortgage Loan Officer experience with established referral sources</li>
<li>
<b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b>: Thorough knowledge of credit application process and familiarity with underwriting criteria and guidelines. Ability to develop and implement marketing tools. Sales and goal oriented. Strong verbal and written communication with ability to interact effectively with people from diverse backgrounds. Ability to maintain confidentiality. Detail oriented to review income, asset verification, and loan parameters. Flexibility and problem solving skills. Computer literate and able to use laptop. Knowledge of loan origination software and all documentation necessary to process a loan from pre-qualification through closing.</li>
</ul>
