<h2>Inventory Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Lolli and Pops is a high-end purveyor of sweetness and confectionary treats offering over 2000 unique items of indulgence and every reason to let your inner child come out and play. Shop, snack and learn through the exploration of each of Lolli and Pops’ unique candy-themed rooms.</p>
<p><b>Role Description</b></p>
<p>The Inventory Analyst will be responsible for end to end sales and inventory planning for Lolli and Pops.</p>
<p><b>Inventory Analyst responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage inventory tracking from vendors to stores to provide visibility to multiple stakeholders and ensure data integrity</li>
<li>Optimize allocations and identify potential MOS risks in areas of responsibility to all stores to maximize sales, margin, and sell-through and minimize waste</li>
<li>Be proactive and engaged in non-traditional planning tasks like vendor communication for deliveries, floor set review, managing allocations and specific store management</li>
<li>Actively participate in pushing forward with new processes and optimizations across planning and merchandising.</li>
<li>Oversee appropriate system parameters, profiles, safety stocks, display minimums, etc. to achieve store/item in-stock goals and objectives</li>
<li>Partner cross-functionally within operations, merchandising and inventory control to share information to achieve area goals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office, with strong capabilities in Excel</li>
<li>1-2 years retail planning, distribution or stores experience</li>
<li>Comfort with computer systems and technology. You don’t need to be a computer scientist, but you need to be really comfortable learning and understanding systems, from Windows to various software packages.</li>
<li>Proactive by nature. This job will have a high degree of autonomy, but equally high expectations that you will take the ball and run with it.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and prioritize workload in a high pace environment</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required</li>
</ul>
<p>* This Job Description captures the essential responsibilities of the position. It is not intended to record all duties and expectations of the position as may be communicated and/or assigned.</p>
