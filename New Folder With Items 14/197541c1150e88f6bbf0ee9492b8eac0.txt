<p><b>Personal Trainer / Team Coach:</b></p>
<p>
An Anytime Fitness Team Borden Coach focuses primarily on helping all members “get to a healthier place. by leading and building our small group training program. Our coaches must possess the knowledge, skills, and abilities to create and conduct safe and effective exercise for a wide variety of participants. In addition, he/she must be comfortable and confident with fitness program design, program instruction, and influencing behavior change. The PT does this by servicing Fitness Consultations, Traditional (1-on-1) Training, Small Group Training, Virtual Training and additional programming opportunities as deemed appropriate by the individual club. Beyond the traditional roles described, the coach provides guidance, education, and encouragement and most importantly, develops relationships with all members to improve retention. Team Borden expects coaches to possess above average customer service skills and be on a mission to provide a “surprisingly personable experience” with everyone he/she meets. Coaches are responsible for the general upkeep of the club (equipment, restrooms, floor cleanliness), sales and marketing for programming, and proactive outreach to inactive members to assist in retention efforts.</p>
<p><b>
What we offer:</b></p>

<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Motivate and coach clients in a team format, small group format, and 1:1.</li>
<li>Match Anytime Fitness personal training programs - in conjunction with the Personal Trainer’s expertise - with the member’s goals and abilities using our functional movement screen.</li>
<li>Conduct fitness consultations and goal assessments to ensure the member’s goals are registered</li>
<li>Track, monitor and report each member’s progress while working to continue an ongoing personal training regimen with each client</li>
<li>Successful Personal Trainers continue education of each member and create long-term relationships</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Current CPR, AED and personal training certification(s)<b> is required </b>and must be a nationally recognized certification such as NASM or ACE</li>
<li>Previous PT experience is not required</li>
<li>Previous experience with fitness club management software (Club OS, Club Ready, Mindbody, apps like My Fitness Pal all a plus)</li>
<li>Superior communication skills (verbal &amp; written) with the ability to connect with people while motivating them to achieve their goals. Clear understanding of teamwork is a must</li>
<li>Desire to continually learn new principles in the areas of training, overall fitness, health and nutrition</li>
<li>Hands on training and experience in areas such as corrective exercise, post-rehab, and injury prevention, are a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>

This position begins at $18/hr after the required 90 day paid internship period ($15/hr). Bonus opportunities are offered after 90 days.</b></p>
