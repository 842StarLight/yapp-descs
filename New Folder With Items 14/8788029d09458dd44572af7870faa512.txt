<h2>Dispatcher</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Dispatcher is responsible for assigning shipments to 20+ trucks and drivers, scheduling of routes and material handling activity using BisTrack (WMS) technology and arranging back-hauls/credits. Provide excellent customer service by meeting customer’s needs.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b>:</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for determining the most efficient and economical methods of shipment for company products.</li>
<li>Regularly interacts with operations manager, product managers, sales, drivers, material handlers, other department team leaders and human resources.</li>
<li>Resolve issues with lost or damaged product. Document delays or possible delays and all other pertinent information about critical shipments.</li>
<li>Facilitates cooperation and communication within delivery department team and company.</li>
<li>Gathers all information necessary to insure smooth daily operations. Records dispatch time, perform call outs, time of arrival, time of departure, time of delivery, proof of delivery, tracking information, and other time critical information.</li>
<li>Promote continuous process improvement.</li>
<li>Meet or exceed assigned daily delivery goals.</li>
<li>Instructs team members regarding delivery schedule and department responsibilities.</li>
<li>Ensuring work is completed in a timely and safe manner.</li>
<li>General knowledge on all of Zeeland Lumber’s products</li>
<li>Learns, understands and works with BisTrack/WMS/ERP systems</li>
<li>Proactive monitoring and tracking critical shipments to ensure timely delivery of equipment.</li>
<li>Demonstrate equipment operations and work and safety procedures to new employees, or assign employees to experienced workers for training.</li>
<li>Confer with management or subordinates to resolve worker problems or complaints.</li>
<li>Ensure team compliance with the quality system and applicable policy, procedure and practice.</li>
<li>Enforce safety regulations.</li>
<li>Follow DOT regulations and monitor driver log sheets</li>
<li>Dispatching multiple geographies in Michigan, Indiana, Ohio, Illinois. Geography experience and map reading capability a must.</li>
<li>Tracks weather and traffic issues and other events that may cause delays. Communicates and documents events proactively.</li>
<li>All other responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Qualifications / Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>3 years of dispatch experience preferred.</li>
<li>Strong technological capabilities using:
<ul>
<li>Enterprise ERP understanding (WMS/BisTrack) or similar software</li>
<li>Internet (GPS tracking/maps) or similar software</li>
<li>Outlook/Word/Excel</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong analytical and cognitive skills.</li>
<li>Energetic, forward-thinking and creative individual with high ethical standards and an appropriate professional image.</li>
<li>Ability to follow basic written and verbal instructions, such as bills of materials and shipping documents, or equivalent. Ability to read shipping documents.</li>
<li>Outstanding interpersonal and communication skills.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education / Training / Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED Equivalent.</li>
<li>3 years of related training and experience, or equivalent, within the shipping/transportation industry</li>
<li>Experience in the Building Industry preferred</li>
</ul>
