<h2>Senior Research Chemist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Clariant is a focused and innovative specialty chemical company based near Basel in Switzerland. Last year the company recorded CHF 6.6 billion in sales harnessing the talents of its 18 000 employees across 53 countries.
</p>
<p>Clariant’s portfolio is optimized for future growth, with a focus in four business areas: Care Chemicals, Catalysts, Natural Resources, and Plastics &amp; Coatings.
</p>
<p>We believe that our growth is all about our customers and their success. Investment in innovation capabilities and developing inspiring environments for courageous and determined minds are therefore key to reaching our goal.
</p>
<p>Join us and help create sustainable value through chemistry for customers all over the globe.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Clariant is seeking a Senior Research Chemist/Engineer for its Louisville Kentucky location. This position will focus on research and development of fuel processing catalysts for fuel cell applications.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Improvement of existing products and development of new products.
</li>
<li>Execute technical service, production support and R&amp;D projects.
</li>
<li>Drive defined work streams and projects forward with creativity, endurance and team spirit to meet milestones and objectives.
</li>
<li>Participate in development of new methods for catalyst preparation, characterization, and performance testing.
</li>
<li>Prepare and test catalyst samples for technical service, production support and R&amp;D projects. Operate bench scale test units, conduct gas chromatography (GC) analysis.
</li>
<li>Organize and analyze data to formulate conclusions and next steps to drive projects forward.
</li>
<li>Troubleshoot test unit and GC operations and resolve problems with assistance from other group members and maintenance.
</li>
<li>Maintain a very organized and detailed laboratory notebook. Write reports and make presentations as needed.
</li>
<li>Maintain assigned equipment and troubleshoot problems.
</li>
<li>Maintain a clean and safe work environment.
</li>
<li>Performs other functions as needed.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced degree (Master’s or Ph. D.) in Chemistry, Chemical Engineering or related field.
</li>
<li>Must be familiar with catalyst preparation and evaluation, GC operation and data analysis.
</li>
<li>Ability to analyze and correlate data obtained in catalyst preparation, characterization, and testing.
</li>
<li>Strong desire to work with members from various functions within the organization (sales, production, R&amp;D, etc.)
</li>
<li>Knowledge of data acquisition and control hardware/software is a plus.
</li>
<li>Strong mechanical aptitude and ability to be very hands on, flexible, and thrive in a team-oriented environment.
</li>
<li>Ability to conduct experiments in a safe and environmentally responsible manner.
</li>
<li>Very strong analytical, critical thinking and troubleshooting skills.
</li>
<li>Must have excellent communication and technical skills.
</li>
<li>Must be able to effectively handle multiple projects simultaneously.
</li>
<li>Must be flexible and able to move between projects as needed.<br>
</li>
</ul>
