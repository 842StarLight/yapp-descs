<p><b>Key responsibilities and accountabilities</b></p>
<p>
The Client Executive is market-facing role designed to achieve the Company’s revenue, margin and market share goals in assigned segments of the life sciences and biotech industry. This role will require a strong blend of executive skills including pipeline management, client discovery, scientific acumen, product knowledge, process automation, team selling, communications, negotiations, financial experience, and major account management. This position will be required to collaborate with clients, prospective clients and various internal resources including scientific operations, application support, product management, business development, operations, production, service, marketing, engineering and other corporate professionals in the sales process.</p>
<p>
Position requires participation at meetings and events in the evenings and during weekends periodically throughout the year.</p>
