<h2>Technology Project Manager/Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>This position is part of our Investment Technology team and will work with technology and business stakeholders to design and implement scalable technology solutions to meet investment and reporting needs. Primary responsibilities include analysis, development, and ongoing support of technologies and analytics driven solutions, serving as a liaison between technology and business stakeholders, managing projects, analyzing user requirements and translating them into implementation plans, identifying project issues and developing solutions, and managing vendor relationships. The successful candidate must possess strong knowledge of equity data (e.g., Capital IQ, Bloomberg, Thomson Reuters), have strong programing experience (SQL, Python, C++), project management experience, including developing and implementing business processes and technology solutions. MS degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field required.<br>
<div>
<div>
<br>
We are seeking a Technology Project Manager/Business Analyst to join our Investment Technology team. The team develops and implements the software required to support the firm’s trading, quantitative research, and other operations. The Technology Project Manager/Business Analyst will work with technology and business stakeholders to design and implement scalable technology solutions to meet investment and reporting needs.<br>
<br>
The successful candidate will have a strong understanding of financial equity data and will be responsible for project management, requirements analysis, infrastructure, development, quality assurance, and operations support within the Technology department.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Analyzing, developing, and ongoing support of technologies and analytics driven solutions, and serving as a liaison between technology and business stakeholders at all levels</li>
<li>Managing projects, leading meetings, developing consensus, establishing implementation plans, managing project schedules, and applying best practices of project management</li>
<li>Analyzing portfolio manager requirements and translating them into implementation plans to update optimization, trading, and analytical reporting systems</li>
<li>Identifying project issues and developing solutions collaboratively with business stakeholders; understanding and mitigating technical and operational risks</li>
<li>Providing oversight for the various phases of testing and user acceptance of technical implementations</li>
<li>Evaluating and managing relationships with data vendors and technology providers</li>
</ul>
<br>
Requirements include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Master’s degree in a technical discipline (e.g., Computer Science, Engineering, Statistics)</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of equity data (Capital IQ, Bloomberg, Thomson Reuters, etc.) and front/middle/back office operations</li>
<li>Programming experience (SQL, Python, C/C++, Java)</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in project management or technical leadership role at a financial firm—analyzing, designing, developing and implementing business processes and supporting implementation of technology solutions</li>
<li>An advanced level of mathematics and statistics</li>
<li>Strong leadership skills</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and the ability to translate technical terms and issues for non-technical stakeholders</li>
</ul>
