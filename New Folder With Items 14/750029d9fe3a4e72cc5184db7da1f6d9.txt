<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Patient Care Coordinator at</b> <b>HearingLife</b><i> </i><b><i>–</i></b><b> Sioux City, IA</b></p>
<p>
HearingLife is currently seeking a Patient Care Coordinator (PCC) in our <b>Sioux City</b> office to drive growth in a competitive market. In this role, you'll have the opportunity to make a significant contribution to HearingLife’s successful history of creating hearing solutions for a rising marketplace.</p>
<p><b>
Salary &amp; Benefits:</b></p>
<p>
We offer a competitive compensation based on experience. Total compensation, which is an hourly rate plus monthly performance bonuses, is expected to average between $15-17 per hour. Benefits (Medical, Dental, LTD, Life Insurance) &amp; Paid Time-off; eligibility according to insurance qualifications. Professional, fast paced and challenging work environment.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The ideal candidate will work with HearingLife’s local Clinician to perform certain hearing and hearing aid related services for our patients.</p>
<p><b>
**The PCC's primary responsibility is to solicit appointments through outbound telemarketing techniques**</b></p>
<p>
The PCC will also provide professional patient care as directed by their employer. The PCC shall perform these daily office duties, including but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Data entry and appointment scheduling using company scheduling protocols and internal scheduling database software.</li>
<li>
Order and maintain appropriate/approved office supplies.</li>
<li>
Manage petty cash account, execute routine bank deposits and credit transactions.</li>
<li>
Submit sales reports</li>
<li>
Package and ship supplies, ear molds to clinics &amp; main office.</li>
<li>
Greet all patients and obtain potential referrals.</li>
<li>
Understand current marketing promotions.</li>
<li>
Work directly with insurance team to determine patient specific coverage and understand current HIPAA laws.</li>
<li>
Conduct credit application calls with vendors as needed.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
