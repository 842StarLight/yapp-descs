<h2>Supervisor, Warehouse</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Responsible for assisting the Warehouse manager by supervising all, functions or specific functions of the warehouse, including shipping, receiving, and returns, as well as maintaining a high level of productivity and ensuring that all orders and returns are processed accurately and on-time on a daily basis.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Accountable for directing all activities in the warehouse to ensure accurate, timely and efficient operations including shipping of sales orders, receiving of inventory, and returns processing.</li>
<li>Responsible for hiring, training, developing and motivating staff; scheduling, delegating work assignments; monitoring individual and group performance to ensure high level of productivity; and for reviewing all distribution processes and procedures in order to continuously improve efficiency.</li>
<li>Provides prompt assistance to Order Management, Sales personnel, and customers in the resolution of shipping questions or issues.</li>
<li>Maintains positive, partner relationships with all carriers, including assisting with selection and price negotiations for new vendors; resolves shipping problems, initiates tracers and files claims when necessary.</li>
<li>Determines effective layout and utilization of warehouse space for efficient operations.</li>
<li>Responsible for safeguarding of inventories, facilities and staff.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Requires 5 years of related experience and management of two or more regular full-time employees; bachelor's degree in a relevant field preferred.<br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 years of leadership experience with a track record of quality, attention to detail and an aptitude at leadership.</li>
<li>Effective written and oral communications.</li>
<li>Excellent PC skills required. Excel and Microsoft Word.</li>
<li>Experience supervising in a dynamic and multifaceted environment.</li>
<li>Must be able to lift 50lbs.
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
