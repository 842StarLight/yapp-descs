<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details
</b></h2>
<p>The Customer Success Group (CSG) at Salesforce is a 7,000+ strong organization responsible for managing customer relationships post-sales, and ensuring successful customer outcomes and customer retention. This includes Success Management, Support, Professional Services, and Renewals functions.
<br>
<br>
CSG is looking for a Senior Director who is highly motivated and passionate about delivering business insights and enabling timely decision making using analytics and data. The ideal candidate has a demonstrated track-record of exceptional strategic, operational, executive communication and interpersonal skills along with deep expertise in data management and business analytics. This role will lead a team responsible for defining, objectively reporting, and analyzing critical measures of customer health and will report up to the CSG COO organization.
<br>
<br>
The Senior Director will establish and communicate an innovative vision for scaling and optimizing our advanced analytical capabilities in order to provide deep insight into key drivers of success and business value for our customers and increase customer retention. In addition, the Senior Director will lead the development of multi-level and trusted reporting and analytical models in partnership with Operations, Finance, Customer Success and Renewals leaders. This role a critical business partner to our leaders within Success and Renewals teams and plays a key role in informing key strategic choices for the business and driving those to implementation.<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish an innovative vision to evolve CSGâ€™s analytics capabilities with an eye to automation, integration and scale. Communicate and generate support for this vision among business leadership and key stakeholders. Own and execute on a multi-year road-map to implement this vision.
</li>
<li>Engage with customer facing teams and build trusted, collaborative relationships with key business stakeholders, especially within the Success Management and Renewals communities.
</li>
<li>Lead, mentor, motivate and grow a global team of data and advanced analytics professionals who are responsible for delivering business insights and reporting. Set the standard and enhance business analytics and technical analytics skills within the team.

</li>
<li>Define and maintain pulse of key business KPIs and flag when leadership action is required. Work with team to perform root cause analysis, and provide key insights leading to actionable recommendations.
</li>
<li>Guide/assist team in the preparation of data and insights to support executive-level business reviews, meetings with Sales leaders, and senior management team meetings. Present to senior leadership.
</li>
<li>Assess and prioritize ad hoc analytic project requests and determine the best approach and team members to support. Build mechanism/data infrastructure that reduces lead time for supporting such requests.
</li>
<li>Create and execute on a vision for self-service analytics and insights. Drive the development of new and refine/extend existing dashboards supporting all levels of the CSG organization. Lead exec level requirements gathering and provide ideas on how to innovate in order to address needs and solve key business questions. Participate in the cross-functional development of shared / cross-company dashboards.
</li>
<li>Lead development of a plan to enable field and business partner teams in effectively consuming available data and analytics assets; be a thought leader in advocating and establishing a data-driven culture within the organization.
</li>
<li>Advise Operations functions on data requirements necessary to perform key analyses and assess business performance; participate in the development of process improvements required to track desired data.
</li>
