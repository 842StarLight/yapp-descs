<h2>Event Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Marriott Park City is looking for a dynamic individual to join our Sales/Events team as an Event Manager. Must have prior Event Management experience, hotel experience preferred.
</p>
<p>Below are the Core Responsibilities that make up the priority functions and most important responsibilities of this position. These Core Responsibilities are divided into two categories, Job Function and Professionalism and Style Expectations.
 </p>
<p>Following the Core Responsibilities is a Physical Requirements category that is also expected of all positions within Driftwood Hospitality Management, LLC.</p>
<p>
 Each responsibility of this position is assigned a weight that indicates the percentage of the overall job it represents. For performance appraisal purposes, the Core Responsibilities what the job is; the substance of the job account for 90% of the overall appraisal score. The other 10% is based on the Professionalism and Style category how the individual goes about doing the job.</p>
