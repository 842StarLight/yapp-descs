<h2>Senior Functional Consultant - CRM and Power BI</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Dynamics 365 Sales Senior Functional Consultant's main responsibility is to integrate the Customer with the Technical teams, providing guidance and ensuring the technical capabilities of our software customizations match agreed on requirements. You will be responsible for providing high-quality software implementation expertise to Flintfox customers and support the customers either on-site or remotely during the customization, installation and integration process.</p>
<p><b>
 Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for determining the functional solutions for the areas related to Business Operations to ensure the solution is of the highest standard possible within the scope, timeline and budget for the project</li>
<li>Leads discussions with the business process owners and subject matter experts providing leadership in business process management activities</li>
<li>Partners with the Functional Solutions Architect to ensure the solutions for the finance, accounting and related business operations are sound and work well with the solutions being implemented throughout the project as a whole</li>
<li>Utilizes the Flintfox Project Methodologies and Accelerators to ensure the project is done in a repeatable manner and of the expected quality</li>
<li>Work with business leadership, end users and IT staff to identify, design and configure business solutions</li>
<li>Educate end users on ERP functionality</li>
<li>Understand the requirements of customers within the project and strives to deliver these consistently</li>
<li>Work with customers to achieve a successful result</li>
<li>Gather requirements and produce quality functional design documentation</li>
<li>Train software users</li>
<li>Act as a conduit between Customers and Technical teams</li>
<li>Work on multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Provide solution architecture support and technical guidance</li>
<li>Maintain a high-level overview of technical competence and domain knowledge of Dynamics ERP products</li>
<li>Work closely with the engagement team to ensure we meet and exceed customer requirements and expectations</li>
</ul> <p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of previous experience in software implementation, in the capacity of a Functional Consultant</li>
<li>Microsoft Certified Professional Dynamics Certifications.</li>
<li>5+ years of Functional knowledge, using Microsoft Dynamics CRM 2011</li>
<li>Project management skills and experience</li>
<li>In-depth expertise on Sales Management Cycle, including processes from Sales Order creation to Invoicing</li>
<li>Expertise in Master Planning</li>
<li>Expertise in Forecast management and deployment with Master Planning</li>
<li>Hands-on deployment experience in Process Manufacturing</li>
<li>Hands-on experience in Advanced warehouse management</li>
<li>Understanding of financial terminology and processes
 </li>
</ul>
