<h2>Marketing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Conduct market research to identify new opportunities</li>
<li>Gather and analyze consumer behavior data</li>
<li>Monitor competitors' marketing activities</li>
<li>Coordinate with the Marketing Design and Content teams to create advertising material</li>
<li>Prepare regular sales forecasting reports</li>
<li>Keep organized records of marketing metrics and results of past campaigns</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications &amp; Skills</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in marketing, business or related field</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with a goal-oriented attitude</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Solid computer skills, including MS Excel and web analytics</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively within a team and independently</li>
<li>Good organization skills</li>
<li>Related job and internship experience</li>
</ul>
