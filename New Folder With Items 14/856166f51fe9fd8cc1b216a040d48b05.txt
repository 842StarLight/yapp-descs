<h2>Onsite Application Support Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Liveperson is an expert based culture, built on the drive and passion of smart people! Our business is growing and we are looking for people like you!
<br><br>
LivePerson is the global leader in conversational commerce solutions and we aim to make life easier by transforming how people communicate with brands. Our 18,000 customers, including leading brands like Citibank, HSBC, Orange, Virgin Atlantic, RBS and Liberty Global, use our conversational commerce solutions to orchestrate humans and AI, at scale, and create a convenient, deeply personal relationship — a conversational relationship — with their millions of consumers.
<br><br>
Over the next three years, our goal is to transform the 268 billion analogue phone calls between a brand and its consumers to digital on the LiveEngage platform. By doing this, we enable consumers to get back time and experience a more connected relationship with the brand in which sales, service, marketing, branches, stores, and contact centre's become a unified experience.
<br><br>
The successful candidate has an opportunity to join a highly outstanding team within a fast-paced and successful organisation.
<br><br>
We are looking for a dedicated, ambitious and self-driven Technical Account Manager.
<br><br>
As a Technical Account Manager, you will work closely with our customers, build relationships with them and help them achieve their technical goals and overcome technical challenges.
<br><br>
<b>In this role you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist customers' technical requests and take care of any technical issues, responsible for all customer engagements from initiation to resolution through ensuring progress by SLA and escalation.</li>
<li>Build strong customer relationships, especially with key customer stakeholders and sponsors</li>
<li>Always strive to provide an exceptional customer experience</li>
<li>Manage customer expectations and lead them to customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Make sure all deliverables are on time, on quality and fulfill customers' requirements</li>
<li>Keep track of key account support metrics</li>
<li>Take initiatives in identifying growth opportunities</li>
<li>Provide constant transparency to an open engagement status/progress while communicating progress to both internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>Pre / Post-release deployment management</li>
<li>Customer Advocacy</li>
<li>Known Limitations Management</li>
</ul>
<b>You should be an expert in:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A proven track record of Technical Account Management or other relevant experience for a SaaS or web company</li>
<li>Experience in managing multiple stakeholders and projects</li>
<li>A listener who is customer-oriented and attentive to their needs</li>
<li>Critical thinker and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Good understanding of web technologies and the associated support teams/processes</li>
<li>Team player with the ability to work with multiple stakeholders and cross-organizational efforts with a number of different virtual teams concurrently</li>
<li>Good time-management skills</li>
<li>Great interpersonal and communication skills</li>
<li>BSc/BA degree or equivalent</li>
<li>Experience in using software for sales management such as Salesforce, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure and to tight deadlines.</li>
<li>Availability for off-hours escalations</li>
<li>Availability to travel to customer sites as required (up to 15%)</li>
</ul>
