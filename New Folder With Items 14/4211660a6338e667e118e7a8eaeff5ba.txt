<h2>Front Desk Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The overall objective and purpose of the Front Desk Agent is to provide a warm welcome and hospitable service in order to create an exceptional guest experience</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties / Responsibilities / Tasks:</b></p>
<p>Primary duties, responsibilities, and tasks include, but are not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Greet and interact with internal and external guests in a warm and friendly manner, using professional verbiage</li>
<li>Process check-ins, check-outs and room assignments</li>
<li>Post guest charges and payments, process no-shows and adjust disputed charges</li>
<li>Run daily reports and contingency lists. Conduct bucket checks to ensure accuracy</li>
<li>Maintain the integrity of the guests’ privacy, including confidentiality of personal information and key control</li>
<li>Verify rates, codes and make appropriate adjustments on guests’ invoices</li>
<li>Operate the telephone system, answering calls within 3 rings, using the correct salutation and telephone etiquette</li>
<li>Process and respond to wake-up calls according to the standard operating procedure</li>
<li>Notify guests of messages and record them legibly and completely</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of room types, hotel amenities and features, hours of operation and area attractions</li>
<li>Respond promptly to questions, concerns and special requests. Follow up to ensure the guest is satisfied</li>
<li>Log guest requests and concerns according to procedures. Communicate with departments in a timely manner</li>
<li>Proficiently solve problems</li>
<li>Maintain a neat and organized work area</li>
<li>Maintain integrity of the Company proprietary information and protect Company assets</li>
<li>Maintain complete knowledge in the use of all office equipment, property management systems and access according to specifications</li>
<li>Block and unblock rooms according to the hotel’s needs</li>
<li>Demonstrate effective sales techniques to upsell rooms, amenities and products</li>
<li>Maintain complete knowledge and comply with company policies and procedures</li>
<li>Maintain neat, clean and professional appearance according to the standards</li>
<li>Maintain a safe work environment. Report accidents, injuries and unsafe conditions</li>
<li>Welcome and acknowledge guests with disabilities</li>
<li>Develop and maintain positive working relationships and support the team to achieve our goals</li>
<li>Comply with quality assurance expectations and standards</li>
<li>Attend required training and meetings</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned, planned or un-planned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications (Confirmable Formal Education, Certifications, Work/Industry Experience)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or General Education Degree - required</li>
<li>Minimum of 6 months hospitality or related industry experience – required</li>
<li>Student or graduate of hotel management school - preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KSA’s (Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities to be discovered via interview &amp; reference checks)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to perform all tasks at the front desk and proficiently use job-related software and property management programs</li>
<li>Possess strong oral, written and interpersonal skills as well as the ability to effectively communicate in the English language</li>
<li>Ability to remain calm in various situations and use sound judgment</li>
<li>Possess a guest service demeanor and outgoing personality with the ability to relate to diverse guests</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules and procedural manuals</li>
<li>Ability to demonstrate cash handling procedures and calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, commissions, upgrades and percentages</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and multi-task</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>While performing the duties of this job, the incumbent is regularly required to stand, sit, stoop, bend, walk and crouch</li>
<li>Push, pull, and lift up to 10lbs without assistance</li>
<li>Reach with hands and arms, use fingers to handle or feel</li>
<li>See, hear and communicate in the English language</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Environment/Conditions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The majority of time will be spent indoors</li>
<li>Candidate must be comfortable working in a shared space, with constant noise, without the use of a private office.</li>
<li>Overtime is frequently required with or without notice.</li>
<li>Schedules may vary from week to week based on business demands</li>
</ul>
