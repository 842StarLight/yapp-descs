<div>
<p>Design &amp; Integration, a leading audiovisual technology company in Baltimore, is currently seeking a Control System Programmer with a strong desire for personal growth and reward.</p>
<p>
This is an ideal position for a recent graduate with Computer Science or Engineering Degree, with knowledge of C++, C-Sharp, JavaScript programming languages. The selected candidate will follow a 1 year career track that will develop their technical capabilities and industry understanding. Opportunities for advancement include project engineer, project management and system programming.</p>
</div>
</div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Functions</b></h4>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Control Systems Programming: Develop source code and touch panel files for systems control based on systems line diagrams and project scope of work.</li>
<li>Engineering, Sales and Service support: Provide technical support in the design and functionality of systems control in the sales, engineering and service departments.</li>
<li>Systems Testing: Assist both In-House and Field testing of systems with integrated control.</li>
<li>User Guides: Develop control systems user guides for programmed systems.</li>
<li>Control Systems Training: Train the end users of the system on the touch panel interface. This task is shared with Production Engineer.</li>
<li>Product Development: Work to develop standardizations for touch panel interfaces and control code development to help streamline programming costs. Report/document any problematic products to sales and engineering.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain knowledge of current and new technologies that company and related manufacturers offer (i.e. AMX, Crestron, Extron, etc.)</li>
</ul></div>
<div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Qualifications</b></h4>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering or related field.</li>
<li>Ability to read and understand blue prints, wiring diagrams, and other AV and architectural drawings.</li>
<li>Basic office and computer skills; proficiency in spelling, punctuation, grammar, and oral communication skills. Knowledgeable of Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook.</li>
<li>Planning and organizational skills. Ability to multitask and work on multiple projects concurrently.</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of Audio/Visual Systems.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of control systems programming from various manufactures.</li>
<li>Understanding of different control methods and how to implement them.</li>
<li>Expertise in troubleshooting and diagnosing malfunctioning systems.</li>
<li>Ability to initiate and maintain positive working relationships with DI employees and other trades. Manage team members and interact with customers and project team members professionally.</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated, detailed oriented, prideful in his/her work, and have a strong desire to succeed.</li>
</ul></div>
