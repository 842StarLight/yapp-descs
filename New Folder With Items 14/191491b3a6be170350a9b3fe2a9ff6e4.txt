Nextiva is seeking a Director, Compensation. A leader of compensation who has a passion for data and supplying insights that can help drive meaningful solutions in our organization. You will have a hands-on role that collaborates with Finance, HR, and business leadership, be responsible for compensation program design and manage day-to-day compensation administration for a global employee population. You will build on your experience to help develop innovative solutions to ensure we align salaries and variable compensation with talent, stay competitive and, through it all, keep it simple.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead a small team of compensation professionals and build out an organization to support the global needs of the company.</li>
<li>Own the global pay structures and job architecture including methodology for job evaluations and leveling. As the company scales globally, this role will be responsible for ensuring that appropriate structures are implemented and maintained.</li>
<li>Responsible for the leading the annual cycle processes.</li>
<li>Partner with the Organizational Design /Talent Management team to build a career framework and work with HR/leaders to identify, build, and modify job families, leveling and salary grades to align with our comp philosophy and business strategy.</li>
<li>Develop recommendations for salary budgets, adjustments, structure changes and program changes based on market analyses and a review of internal equity.</li>
<li>Responsible for evaluating executive compensation and creation and updating of compensation committee materials.</li>
<li>Build strong partnerships with HR Business Partners, Recruiters, and leaders on pay decisions, policy and guideline interpretation.</li>
<li>Partner with business leadership to provide analytical support to assess competitiveness of existing compensation programs.</li>
<li>Overall accountability for all programs and processes being in compliance with existing internal/SOX controls and identify opportunities to reduce risk.</li>
<li>Design and build compensation reports and tools to streamline program administration, reduce risk, and to assist stakeholders with compensation decision making.</li>
<li>Develop ongoing education/training materials about compensation, including executive compensation.</li>
<li>Drive analysis for pay parity and partner across HR to support related initiatives to support long term pay equality.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent</li>
<li>10+ years of increasing responsibility and experience in global compensation, including executive compensation and sales compensation</li>
<li>Proven experience in leading a team of compensation professionals</li>
<li>Proven ability to build cross-functional relationships and partner with leaders to deliver solutions that are aligned to strategy, support business objectives, and meet all legal requirements</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal and written presentation skills, including ability to prepare materials for audiences at all levels of the organization</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge and experience with Excel, Word, and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Deep expertise with compensation analytics, practices, and trends</li>
</ul>
