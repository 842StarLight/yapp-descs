<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Posting Title</b></h2>
Senior Commercial Policies Specialist<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The Challenge</b></h2>
The Senior Commercial Policies Specialist administers the GE Appliances, a Haier company, co-operative advertising policy and procedures including day to day operations and management of the policy.<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What you will Do</b></h2>
<p><b>
Essential Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage and administer GEA’s Cooperative Advertising (MAP) and UMRP policies and procedures; identify ads that are non-conforming/inconsistent with GEA policies and take appropriate action as communicated within GEA policies.</li>
<li>
Own Cooperative Advertising and Logo Guidelines for local marketplaces, protecting brands while driving impressions.</li>
<li>
Support House of Brand (“HOB”) strategies at the local/reseller advertising level, driving consistency and lift for the National Advertising team.</li>
<li>
Oversee the evolution of Commercial policies based on emerging technology and industry developments.</li>
<li>
Lead Council for Customer Advertising consisting of leaders from Finance, Legal, Marketing and Sales, to coordinate and execute the strategy for cooperative advertising; partner with leadership to manage escalation issues.</li>
<li>
Execute projects from conception to completion while ensuring appropriate teams are involved in the decision-making process.</li>
<li>
Manage multiple key, integrated vendor partners, with daily oversight and course-correction as needed, making decisions quickly and consistently.</li>
<li>
Improve efficiency by identifying and leading simplification and process improvements, as well as contributing to cross-functional strategies to continually improve efficiency.</li>
<li>
Support business-wide Oracle transition as related to internal commercial policy tools, processes and any direct impact on vendors’ crucial data feeds or tools.</li>
<li>
Focus on improving reseller advertising experience and driving cooperative advertising fund utilization.</li>
<li>
Monitor GEA product advertisements on third-party (3P) websites and identify unauthorized resellers; partner with vendors, Legal and Security teams to address unauthorized resellers.</li>
<li>
Define, analyze and provide key metrics and data to leadership teams.</li>
<li>
Communicate and train new Sales employees on commercial policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Manage co-marketing relationships, supporting the National Advertising team; execute sponsorship strategies to drive media and consumer impressions of the GEA HOB and products.</li>
</ul>
