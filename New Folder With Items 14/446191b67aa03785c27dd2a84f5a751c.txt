<h2>Enterprise Account Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job brief:</b></p>
<p>
Enterprise Account Executives (EAE) are responsible for winning new customers via strategic prospecting, advanced sales skills and expert industry knowledge to meet sales quotas. An EAE is directly responsible for identifying business opportunities with larger shippers and deploying the appropriate strategies to win customers. An EAE should strive to be industry experts. They utilize expertise in securing and retaining key customers. An EAE also shares what they know and learn with teammates to improve the overall effectiveness of the team. They are expected to be a reliable professional who balances a results-driven sales focus with a priority of maintaining an outstanding customer experience.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Roles and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Achieve assigned sales goals for revenue and profitability</li>
<li>Achieve goals for new customer wins</li>
<li>Retain and grow assigned existing customers with proactive approach to customer needs</li>
<li>Seek win-win relationships to grow sales and resolve issues utilizing expert skills and knowledge</li>
<li>Maintain accurate sales records, sales reports and customer information in the sales database</li>
<li>Develop and remain current on industry and market trends with objective of industry expertise</li>
<li>Develop and remain current on prospecting techniques, sale skills, and best in class customer service.</li>
<li>Demonstrate professionalism in the workplace</li>
<li>Contribute to the management of day to day operations by striving to ensure customers have the following experience with our service:</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The Right Product · In the Right Condition · At the Right Place · For the Right Price<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills and Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5 to 7 years successful sales experience in logistics market</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills - written and verbal</li>
<li>Driven to achieve outstanding results</li>
<li>Demonstrable analytical skills to correctly determine customer needs and identify potential issues</li>
<li>Ability to think and act creatively to implement strategies</li>
<li>Demonstrable business acumen and a deep understanding of industry sales process</li>
<li>4-year degree from accredited university</li>
</ul>
<p>Roles are managed through specific KPI’s based upon business requirements as deemed appropriate.</p>
