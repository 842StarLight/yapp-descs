<h2>Implementation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Off Duty Management provides a comprehensive solution for law enforcement agencies to better manage their off-duty programs. This solution includes a web and app-based system that is supported by an operations and back office team.</p>
<p><b>Job Purpose: </b></p>
<ul><li>The Implementation Manager is responsible for leading the implementation phase for ODM with new Law Enforcement agencies. This includes developing timelines, conducting training and coordinating all aspects of the onboarding process with new agencies. This position will serve as the main point of contact for the new onboarding agencies and is responsible for the overall strategic design/completion of project timeline to include the Agency's "Go Live" date. The Implementation manager will work closely with Operations team and Law Enforcement and will be accountable for managing implementations to deliver high quality solutions on time and within budget. Secondary responsibility includes technology innovation support as needed.</li></ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Leads the implementation process for new agencies onto the Off-Duty Management system</li>
<li>Conducts analysis of agency requirements and implements those requirements</li>
<li>Develops timelines and deliverables for all responsible parties on the projects</li>
<li>Develops and provides training to officers and agencies on the OfficerTRAK® software</li>
<li>Assists in the agency specific configuration of the OfficerTRAK® software</li>
<li>Creates and customizes communications</li>
<li>Works closely with sales and operations teams to ensure smooth implementation of agency</li>
<li>Adheres to all company policies, procedures and business ethics codes and ensures that they are communicated and implemented within the team</li>
<li>Define project scope, develop project plan, determine necessary resources and establish project timeline</li>
<li>Provide strategic direction and guidance to both internal operational functions and agency to mitigate project risks to all parties</li>
<li>Able to understand RFP language and demonstrate to agency how their objectives will be met through the implementation phase</li>
<li>Ensure quality and service standards are met; including agency contractual deliverables related to the implementation process</li>
<li>Meet regularly with agency to determine service requirements and expectations for overall satisfaction</li>
<li>Ability to manage and host webinars for Agencies, if needed, during the onboarding process</li>
<li>Identify both successful and unsuccessful project elements and establish best practices for project execution</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to standardize, document and define procedures</li>
<li>Identify process inefficiencies and provide innovative solutions to solve for gaps</li>
<li>Complete special projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills/Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High level of organization and reliability</li>
<li>Experience dealing with customers</li>
<li>Able to function independently and as a part of a team</li>
<li>Highly proficient level computer skills including MS Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Outlook</li>
<li>Experience with software and mobile app systems</li>
<li>Travel up to 50%</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience &amp; Education: </b></p>

<li>Minimum 2 years’ experience managing projects and complex tasks</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years’ customer service experience</li>
<li>Law Enforcement or security experience preferred</li>
</ul>
