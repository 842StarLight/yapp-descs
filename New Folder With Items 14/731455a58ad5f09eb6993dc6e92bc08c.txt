<h2>Entry Level Lead Generation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Career Opportunity: </b>Entry LevelLead Generation Specialist</p>
<p>Practical Data Solutions (PDS) is seeking a Lead Generation Specialist to make outbound calls, email and written follow-up to targeted businesses, generate interest in PDS’ services and set up presentations. An ideal candidate would be assertive, motivated and will be very comfortable talking on the phone. Must have the gift of being able to communicate effectively how our company can help the potential client. Being able to relay confidence over the phone is key to this role.</p>
<p>This is a fantastic opportunity for the right individual who wants to be part of a dynamic and experienced team to reach their full potential. This is an entry level position that provides training and the opportunity to advance. Candidate must possess excellent verbal and written communication skills to assist in identifying targets, promote value and gain agreement for potential customers to look at PDS software products and services demonstrations. Opportunities for advancement abound for goal driven individuals.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to identify potential new clients and target accounts</li>
<li>Leverage Social Media platforms as a business tool</li>
<li>Identify key individuals in the target accounts</li>
<li>Leverage marketing efforts and systems to gain interest in PDS’ products and software services</li>
<li>Arrange demonstrations and meetings with prospective clients</li>
<li>Conduct appropriate follow-up</li>
<li>Starting salary range: $42,000-46,000, plus bonuses</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Contact healthcare organizations by phone in order to provide information about PDS</li>
<li>Appropriate professional follow-up via email and written communication</li>
<li>Update and Documentation of Prospect info into PDS’ Customer Database</li>
<li>Adjust sales scripts to better target the needs and interests of prospective clients</li>
<li>Adapt and tune marketing and client communications as appropriate</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>A bachelor’s degree preferred (or equivalent experience) in business, marketing, sales or a related area</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with great listening skills</li>
<li>Outstanding computer skills and the ability to master new software quickly</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Outlook)</li>
<li>Understand technology, software and technology concepts</li>
<li>Be precise, and detail oriented</li>
<li>Be outgoing and friendly with a high energy level</li>
<li>Be highly motivated and a natural self-starter</li>
</ul>
