<p>Production Scheduler</p>
<p><b><i>Summary</i></b></p>
<p>The Production Scheduler has the primary responsibility of creating weekly production schedules for a manufacturing environment consisting of multiple production lines in a 24/7 facility. The qualified individual will ensure that the production schedules are satisfying customer demand while supporting plant safety and efficiency goals. This role also manages various operational aspects of external co-manufacturing engagements. Strong collaboration with Operations, Supply Chain, Procurement, and Finance will be critical to meeting key goals for this role.<br>
</p>
<p><b><i>Primary Responsibilities</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops a weekly manufacturing schedule to establish and maintain finished goods inventory at target levels</li>
<li>Evaluates daily production data and adjusts future production schedules as required</li>
<li>Setup &amp; maintain scheduling system parameters</li>
<li>Reviews sales forecasts, open orders, backorders, future dated orders on a daily basis and adjusts production schedules accordingly</li>
<li>Smartly coordinates sequencing of manufacturing runs to optimize equipment utilization</li>
<li>Works with Procurement to ensure material availability for scheduled production</li>
<li>Works with R&amp;D and other functions to schedule, coordinate &amp; manage test runs as necessary</li>
<li>Actively previews, communicates and coordinates the production schedule with Operations to ensure effective and efficient manufacturing execution</li>
<li>Proactively resolves issues that might prevent schedules from being completed to plan</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Reports schedule attainment</li>
<li>Manages co-packer engagements inclusive of supplying forecasts, issuing purchase orders, managing finished goods inventory levels, managing inventory/flow of any supplied materials, issue resolution</li>
</ul>
