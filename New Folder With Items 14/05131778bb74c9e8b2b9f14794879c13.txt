<h2>Paid Advertising Analyst</h2>

<p><b>Company: </b></p>
<p>Sunvalleytek is a fast growing, private company in Fremont, CA whose world-class staff are disrupting a multi-billion dollar global industry for consumer electronics. Our founder created the company with a passion to provide leading edge consumer electronics including power banks, desk lamps, Bluetooth speakers, headphones, and much more. Today, millions of global customers enjoy products from Sunvalleytek’s consumer facing brands including: RAVPower, TaoTronics, HooToo, VAVA and Anjou. We currently sell the majority of our products through e-commerce, focusing on Amazon.com and major brick and mortar stores including Best Buy, Walmart, and more.</p>
<p><b>About the Role: </b></p>
<p>We are looking for a self-motivated and enthusiastic Paid Advertising Analyst to join our team. The Paid Advertising Analyst will help manage the paid social media campaigns from analysing to launching in order to generate traffic and sales for our social media pages and increase our brand awareness. The ideal candidate would be familiar with managing and optimizing paid social campaigns to generate traffic and brand awareness. They should be high interest and desire to stay on top of the latest trends in paid search. The candidate should also have some engineering/computer science and data analysis background.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul><li>Manage paid social media campaigns for our innovative consumer tech products across multiple platforms (e.g. Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube etc.)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and execute ad campaigns strategies to optimize user acquisition campaigns</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyze the performance of campaigns and adjust campaigns’ budget in order to achieve revenue target.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop test and manage a/b testing result analyses for campaign optimization purpose</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborating cross-functionally with our internal marketing, brand, and product teams to ensure best practice is being applied</li></ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's degree in data analysis/statistics/engineering/computer science related field</li></ul>
<ul><li>3+ years relevant experience in paid social media advertising experiences</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum 1 year management experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experiences with consumer electronic products preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>In-depth knowledge of processes and technologies in running social media paid campaigns</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with running A/B testing and experiment design</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience working within MS Excel (pivot tables, vlookups etc.).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong analytical and critical thinking skills with attention to details</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong written and oral communications skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Goal oriented with positive attitude and high energy</li></ul>
<p><b>The following skills/experience is a huge plus!</b></p>
