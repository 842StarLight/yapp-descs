<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Are you looking for a career in cloud software sales?
<br><br>
Anaplan is hiring energetic, persistent SALES DEVELOPMENT REPRESENTATIVE who love to win!
<br><br>
The SDR at Anaplan is typically the first voice a prospective Anaplan client will hear.
<br><br>
You will deliver a message of an easier way to do business; planning, forecasting, modeling and budgeting. You will follow-up on and create qualified leads, learn how to sell using Account Based Marketing techniques, build rapport, nurture relationships and schedule initial meetings and demonstrations for the direct sales force with enterprise companies around the world.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll be doing:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and generate Sales Qualified Leads by following up on marketing-generated leads and inbound requests, by speedy follow-up with event attendees, and by outbound cold-calling, email correspondence and other forms of digital prospecting to targeted prospects in our Target Market</li>
<li>Learn how to have value-based discussions with prospects</li>
<li>Work with field sales to develop and execute Account Based Sales Development strategies targeting the right person at the right time, with the right message.</li>
<li>Challenge themselves to meet and exceed weekly and monthly sales metrics, forecasts, meeting and call objectives</li>
</ul>
<b>You have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years Inside Sales/Business Development/Sales Development experience, preferably at an enterprise software company selling SaaS / Cloud applications</li>
<li>Consistent track record for meeting and exceeding lead generation, forecasting and sales objectives</li>
<li>Experience calling into Fortune 2000 accounts at CXO level</li>
<li>High level of energy, drive, enthusiasm, initiative, commitment and professionalism</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Discipline to maintain high call volumes</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
</ul>
Our Minneapolis office is located downtown at 300 North 1st Avenue, Suite 500 ( https://goo.gl/maps/YgtadKozojD2 ). We have just moved into newly renovated office space which encourages collaboration, creativity, innovation in a fun and relaxing working environment.
