<h2>Field Technical Support Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Agility Fuel Solutions is the leading global provider of highly-engineered and cost-effective compressed natural gas, liquid natural gas, propane, and hydrogen fuel systems and Type 4 composite cylinders for medium- and heavy-duty commercial vehicles. Our portfolio of products also includes the production of high-performance, modular battery packs for commercial vehicles. Our solutions enable the safe and effective use of natural gas, propane, and hydrogen as a transportation fuel. These clean fuels reduce greenhouse gas and other air emissions and save money for fleet operators and their customers. To learn more about Agility Fuel Solutions, please visit our website at www.AgilityFuelSolutions.com</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>We are currently seeking a <b>Field Technical Support Representative </b>for the Eastern Region (East of the Mississippi River) This position will be home office with considerable travel.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Overview:</b></p>
<p>The position will be supporting Regional Sales Managers to help service and promote the sale of products and programs to existing and new customers and is responsible for the day-to-day management of the service model within the specific region, including: parts and service programs, technical product support, training and product validation support. The individual will be the primary technical interface between Agility and the customer in the field assuring an exceptional customer experience.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li> Support Sales Reps in servicing accounts of customers and prospects including attending meetings and presentations when required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide exceptional customer service and technical support of products</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maximize fuel system uptime through timely response, service provider coordination, and/or repair of products to ensure the highest levels of customer satisfaction</li></ul>
<ul><li> Communicate and interact with customers, sales and other service counterparts in a positive, courteous and professional manner to earn long-term partnerships</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop a thorough understanding of Agility’s products</li></ul>
<ul><li> Complete required contact and service reports in a timely fashion including, call reports, performance reports along with product analysis and weekly expense and activity reports</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide effective “hands-on” training classes related to product installation, safety, use, inspection and maintenance, as required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain current working knowledge of local and national codes where applicable</li></ul>
<ul><li> Take inbound calls related to product and/or installation and provide technical expertise and field service coordination</li></ul>
<ul><li> In conjunction with Sales, conduct regular customer fleet audits to identify/refine strategic field service network and parts stocking programs</li></ul>
<ul><li> Effectively utilize, manage and maintain the elements of the “CRM” program</li></ul>
<ul><li> Capture Voice of Customer data whenever possible to help improve products/processes</li></ul>
<ul><li> Grow the Authorized Service Provider (ASP) network, selectively and strategically and monitor/adjust to ensure a robust, responsive and responsible network is maintained</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist and support Trade Show activities</li></ul>
<ul><li> Travel approximately 50% to 70% of time to provide service support in the field</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead creation of Technical Service Bulletins and work with Engineering on product engineering solutions to eliminate field issues</li></ul>
<ul><li> Support the implementation of product configuration tools to ensure seamless customer installation and parts support at time of order and through life of products</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills and qualifications</b></p>
<ul><li> SAE automotive certification or equivalent technical training</li></ul>
<ul><li> ~ 3-5 years of relevant work experience, preferably in natural gas or transportation applications</li></ul>
<ul><li> ASE Mechanic Certifications a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work independently and collaboratively, be a self-starter</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent communication skills with ability to write clear and concise correspondence</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent time management and organizational skills to maximize effectiveness</li></ul>
<ul><li> Good problem solver</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to read and interpret Engineering drawings</li></ul>
<ul><li> Computer skills to include Word, PowerPoint, Excel</li></ul>
