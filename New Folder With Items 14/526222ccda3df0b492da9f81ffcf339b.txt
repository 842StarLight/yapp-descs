<h2>Manager, Product Data</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><p>If you live and breathe product data and you are a visionary leader in this exciting space, the Product Data Manager position at Panduit may be perfect for you! We are seeking a data steward to manage business-critical enterprise information to create value for our organization and our channel partners. If you have expertise in managing PIM data, building strategies that position product data as an asset, and in championing product data quality as a means to ensure e-commerce success, we are interested in meeting you!</p></div>
<br><div>
<p>As the Product Data Manager, based in our world headquarters in Tinley Park, IL, you will partner with various teams across Digital Business, Product Management, Marketing and Sales to shape product data effectiveness across our website’s and channel partners’ digital shelf. We will rely on you and your team to evangelize product data as an asset, govern Panduit's taxonomy process, transform/load data, create and edit standard operating procedures (SOP), enforce policies, and deliver process improvement. The successful Product Data Manager manages good working relations with our product management teams to enrich product data with attributes and media that are demanded by today’s dynamic B2B digital channels and that will ensure outstanding business outcomes. In this highly visible opportunity, you will lead a team of six to eight professionals, ensuring that their performance meets our goals and exceeds customer expectations. Equally critical to your success will be your ability to create and track key performance indicators (KPIs) and use those metrics to improve the customer experience, refine our strategic direction, and provide a real impact to the organization.
</p>
<p>Based on your past success, you have demonstrated the ability to define, develop, and manage the implementation of product data and merchandising strategies that drive sales and product effectiveness. You have driven operational innovation by staying on top of innovations in PIM management, data automation and syndication systems; creating business cases for these updates; and effectively onboarding these advanced technologies. You have created a positive brand recognition and customer experience. You have demonstrated excellent oral, written, and presentation skills to convert highly strategic concepts into deliverable results. With your track record of leadership, you will empower your team to continuously drive improvements related to our online experience with our customers.
</p>
<p>The success candidate will exhibit deep knowledge of cutting-edge online merchandising and ecommerce management, combined with analytical training in a B2B organization, and a clear understanding of customer behaviors. If you are energized by the idea of developing and leading the product data quality strategy for an innovative industry leader that offers amazing benefits in this highly visible role - then apply today!
</p>
<p>Strategy
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develops and maintains an annual product data optimization and syndication strategy that is in alignment with the overall business strategy and is based on a deep understanding of best practices in data management.
</li>
<li>Utilizes analytics to identify opportunities for testing and optimization.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Partnerships
</p>
<ul><li>Creates partnerships with business units and works with key stakeholders to coordinate product data initiatives across the company and ensure support for execution of the digital strategy.
</li></ul>
<p>Coaching Team and Development
</p>
<ul>
<li>Plans, directs, and controls the activities and staff involved in operating an online business through high-value product data.
</li>
<li>Performs a full range of managerial responsibilities which includes interviewing, coaching and developing employees; planning, assigning and directing work; performance management.
</li>
<li>Leverages existing tools and teams to continuously improves the ability of our customers to find our products on Panduit.com as well as the sites of our key distributors globally. Experience with a PIM system (Product Information Management) is a must, Informatica brand preferred.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Data Maintenance
</p>
<ul>
<li>Analyzes performance of existing online initiatives and makes recommendations to improve data quality, quantity and process to syndicate to our distributors.
</li>
<li>Develops and directs the implementation of methods to track effectiveness, and monitors progress and costs.
</li>
<li>Ensures data maintenance reflects best in class practices.</li>
</ul>
