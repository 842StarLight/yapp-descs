<h2>Cloud Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>We are looking for a highly motivated and passionate Cloud Solution Architect for Cloud Applications and/or Infrastructure solutions to drive high priority customer initiatives on the Microsoft Azure Platform in collaboration with customers and the Microsoft field in the enterprise accounts segment of our business. This is a customer facing role, owning the overall technical relationship between the customer and Microsoft Azure Infrastructure and Applications Development Platform.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
You will own the Cloud Infrastructure and/or Azure Applications Development technical customer engagements (State &amp; Local Government) including architectural design sessions, specific implementation projects and/or pilots. The ideal candidate will have experience in customer-facing roles and success leading deep technical architecture discussions with senior customer executives, Enterprise Architects, IT Management and Developers.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Key responsibilities include:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Understand customers' overall applications portfolio, IT and business priorities and success measures to design implementation architectures and solutions (Microsoft and 3rd party solutions) using PaaS, DevOps &amp; Advanced Application coding</li>
<li>
Apply technical knowledge and customer insights to create a modernization roadmap. Architect solutions to meet business and IT needs, ensuring technical viability of new projects and successful deployments, while orchestrating key resources and infusing key Infrastructure technologies (e.g. Windows and Linux IaaS, Security, Networking, etc.), and Application Development and DevOps technologies (e.g. App Service, containers, serverless, cloud native, etc.) as appropriate</li>
<li>
Ensure all solutions exhibit high levels of performance, security, scalability, maintainability, and appropriate reusability and reliability upon deployment</li>
<li>
Develop deep relationships with key customer IT decision makers, who drive long-term cloud adoption within their company to enable them to be cloud advocate</li>
<li>
Be the Voice of the Customer; Share insights and best practices, and connect with Engineering teams to remove key blockers</li>
<li>
Assess the Customers' knowledge of Azure platform and overall cloud readiness to support customers through a structured learning plan and ensure its delivery through partners</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other Cloud Solution Architects in developing complex end-to-end Enterprise solutions on the Microsoft Azure platform</li>
<li>
Maintain technical skills and knowledge of market trends and competitive insights; collaborate and share with the technical community
</li>
<li>Be an Azure Platform evangelist with customers, partners and external communities</li>
</ul>
