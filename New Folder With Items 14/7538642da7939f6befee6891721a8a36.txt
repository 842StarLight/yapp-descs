<h2>Real Estate Market Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>RPRG is currently seeking a full-time Real Estate Market Analyst for its Columbia, Maryland or Atlanta, Georgia location. The successful candidate will conduct comprehensive market analyses, focusing on rental markets.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Prepare market feasibility studies as part of a lender’s application for Section 221(d)(4) mortgage insurance</li>
<li>Prepare rental market studies for submission to lenders and state agencies for Low Income Housing Tax Credit allocations.</li>
<li>Market and Product advisory analysis of existing markets, product and pricing recommendations for residential and commercial developers and land owners</li>
<li>Studies of for sale communities</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>An undergraduate or graduate degree in urban planning, real estate finance, geography, economics, or related field</li>
<li>Two or more years of experience working in the field of real estate research, market analysis, economic analysis, and/or planning</li>
<li>An ability to communicate clearly and effectively through the use of narrative text, statistical tables, graphs, and maps</li>
<li>Intermediate or advanced modeling skills using Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Access, and/or GIS software</li>
<li>Aptitude in the areas of quantitative analysis, written communication, and problem-solving</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Salary:</b></h4>
<p>Salary is commensurate with experience. Individuals with two to four years’ experience can expect to earn a salary in the range of $50,000 to $80,000.</p>
<p>The firm offers a complete benefits package, including health insurance, disability insurance, and 401(k) plan.</p>
<p>Please forward resume to: MJustice@rprg.net</p>
