<p>Are you interested in working for an organization dedicated to innovation, customer intimacy, and improving healthcare for everyone? Do you enjoy working in an environment filled with respect and integrity? Are you driven by a will to win? You’re in the right place!</p>
<p>
Since 1992, Omnicell has been inspired to create safer and more efficient ways to manage medications across all care settings. As a leading provider of solutions across the continuum of care, we offer a path to fully automated medication management infrastructure, powered by a cloud data platform that supports improved patient care, fewer errors, enhanced safety, and new opportunities for growth.</p>
<p>
Our vision for the Autonomous Pharmacy integrates a comprehensive set of solutions powered by the Omnicell Cloud Data Platform across three key areas: Automation – solutions designed to digitize and streamline workflows; Intelligence – actionable insights to better understand medication usage and improve pharmacy supply chain management; and Work – expert services that serve as an extension of pharmacy operations to support improved efficiency, regulatory compliance, and patient outcomes.</p>
<p>
Over 5,500 facilities worldwide use Omnicell automation and analytics solutions to increase operational efficiency, reduce medication errors, deliver actionable intelligence, and improve patient safety. And more than 40,000 institutional and retail pharmacies across North America and the United Kingdom leverage Omnicell’s innovative medication adherence solutions to improve patient engagement and adherence to prescriptions, helping to reduce costly hospital readmissions.</p>
<p>
Come join our team and be inspired by care!</p>

<p><b>Senior IV Pharmacist Consultant</b></p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish ongoing clinical advisor relationship with RIIS customers and support them in achieving their goals for the RIIS program
</li>
<li>Lead clinical discovery and implementation support of IVX for assigned accounts.
</li>
<li>Provide clinical support for internal RIIS management and IVX implementation teams</li>
<li>Assist in development and implementation of strategic sales and operational tools and programs for IV solutions
</li>
<li>Assist the Product Marketing Manager and the marketing team in development of customer facing programs, materials and publications that highlight and advance the use of Omnicell IV automation products</li>
<li>Support and participate in the risk management process for the IV solutions and serve as a member of the risk management team</li>
<li>Serve as liason between customers and R&amp;D to provide input on enhancements and new product developments. Serve as clinical advisor for improvements to existing platforms (IV2) and new IV platforms in development (IV3)
</li>
<li>Support and collaborate with other customer support staff, field engineers and the R&amp;D staff on special projects and customer initiatives to improve customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Develop financial and clinical tools for preparing business plans and ROIs for customer acquistion of IV automation and management of RIIS accounts
</li>
<li>Develop IV training and educational programs for Omnicell staff and sales personnel
</li>
<li>Provide clinical expertise and insight to internal teams regarding sterile compounding and the sterile compounding regulatory environment</li>
<li>Conduct industry research and guide internal studies on IV automation.
</li>
<li>Develop and present lectures to industry leaders and associated professionals about IV automation and the compounding environment in order to increase awareness and knowledge about the benefits of IV automation</li>
<li>Conduct clinical assessments of customers’ current IV processes and procedures to identify potential IV automation solutions to solve challenges and support their fiscal initiatives.</li>
<li>Formulate customer specific IV program business plans to outline the operational and financial impact of IV automation technology at client sites</li>
<li>Formally present outcomes and recommendations to hosptial and industry executives at client sites.</li>
<li>Educate and advise hospital and compounder site staff on re-engineered operations to improve safety and efficiency of IV compounding.</li>
<li>Actively participate in sales team strategy calls and sales business reviews</li>
<li>Support sales initiatives by providing direct clinical support at customer meetings conference calls, trade shows and other customer facing venues</li>
<li>Work with sales staff and strategic marketing team to identify product/market trends and to develop and implement effective sales and marketing strategies.
</li>
<li>Maintain fiscal responsibility for all Company-related business expenses.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Required Knowledge and Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of medication management processes in acute and non-acute care settings</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal communication skills with the ability to work well with customers and with associates at various levels.</li>
<li>Ability to present in front of small to large audiences at all levels in the health care system
</li>
<li>Documented experience developing and presenting high-level cost-benefit models to senior level executives</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of IV automation systems used in hospitals and compounding facilities. Working knowledge of hospital medication automation systems, inventory software, analytics and informatics systems.</li>
<li>Deep knowledge of IV compounding and regulatory environment throughout the US and ROW including USP, ISO, FDA requirements</li>
<li>Current working knowledge of hospital pharmacy and hospital operations</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of hospital finance and healthcare equipment acquisition models</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Basic Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS Pharmacy</li>
<li>8 or more years of hospital pharmacy practice with 5 or more years of experience managing inpatient pharmacy operations including IV programs preferably in an Integrated Health Network or Health System</li>
<li>2 or more years of experience in pharmacy informatics and data analytics</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Master Degree or pharmacy degree</li>
<li>8 or more years pharmacy or senior healthcare management experience (Director Pharmacy or VP Hospital Operations)</li>
<li>Six Sigma/Lean trained</li>
<li>Supply chain experience (including pharmacy) in health care organizations</li>
<li>Technical product sales experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Certificate/Licensure</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Registered Pharmacist (RPh)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Travel up to 75% of work time based upon demand</li>
</ul>
