<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The <b>Staff Accountant</b> is primarily responsible for supporting the Director of Accounting in carrying out the responsibilities of the Accounting Department for event(s) and day-to-day operations.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Maintain month-end close calendar and prepare deliverables for Director of Accounting review and signoff ahead of each due date</li>
<li>Support internal customers in the financial side of their businesses operations including, but not limited to, the areas of financial systems; budget and forecast development and management and staff scheduling and time reporting</li>
<li>Ensure that financial records are accurate and documented in accordance with the policies of Richmond Raceway and International Speedway Corporation</li>
<li>Perform and prepare reconciliations of balance sheet accounts monthly and ensures that all reconciliations are ready for final review by the Director of Accounting according to department guidelines and times but always prior to GL Close Day</li>
<li>Prepare applicable corporate reports monthly in conjunction with corporate deadlines</li>
<li>Prepare and file monthly sales and use tax return including preparation of all supporting workpapers for review by the Director of Accounting</li>
<li>Prepare and post journal entries (both general ledger and forecast entries) for Director of Accounting review including preparation and assembly of all supporting journal detail and confirmation of correct posting of journal entries</li>
<li>Review financial reports and identify variances from budget and forecast and document these observations to discuss with the Director of Accounting</li>
<li>Attend monthly close meetings and participate in the discussions with department heads to review budget variances and financial trends.</li>
<li>Aid in event treasury management, payroll, and reporting responsibilities including conducting of fieldwork related to the event audit; workpaper preparation including documentation of observations and results from signage, display, suite, hospitality, and program advertising audits.</li>
<li>Participate in the finance and accounting onboarding of new employees at Richmond Raceway including orientation to expense reports and review of travel policy, WebNow, ABI, FanTracker, orientation to department budget and product codes commonly used at Richmond, and other department specific finance and accounting onboarding.</li>
<li>Responsible for the monitoring and managing of all past-due customer accounts receivable</li>
<li>Serves as the alternate gatekeeper for all departments in WebNow for review and coding of accounts payable invoices</li>
<li>Collaborate with all departments to ensure proper accounting for purchases and billing</li>
<li>Assist the Director of Accounting with budget and forecast preparation including conducting account analysis, inputting data into budget worksheets and uploading budget documents to PeopleSoft</li>
<li>Perform special finance projects as requested by the Director of Accounting</li>
<li>Prepare monthly and weekly reports as required to provide management with current operating data and performance.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
 <b>Education and/or Experience</b>
</div>
<ul><li>A Bachelor’s degree in accounting, or a Bachelor’s degree in business with emphasis in accounting from a four-year college or university, is preferred.</li></ul>
