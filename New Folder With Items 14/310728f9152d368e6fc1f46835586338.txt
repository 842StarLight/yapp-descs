<h2>Copywriter- Enablement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Become a member of our talented Enablement, where we use creativity to fuel revenue growth, increase customer retention and deliver a world class customer experience. MINDBODY is looking for a Copywriter in our San Luis Obispo or Scottsdale office who will cultivate our brand voice and advocate for our customers through a variety of sales content and campaign assets.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Driven by details, Copywriters use words to translate complex ideas into relatable and intuitive user experiences. They are naturally curious, constructively critical, and passionately meticulous. Copywriters have a strong command of the written word and draw upon their experience, skill, and intuition to conceptualize solutions across our web and mobile products.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
You should be an enthusiastic individual who has a strong command of the written word. You will demonstrate attention to detail and problem-solving skills by writing new assets and modifying existing ones. You are talented and driven and strive to create ground-breaking work. You are also passionate about people, customer-obsessed, and able to collaborate across disciplines on a daily basis while maintaining the highest standards.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS AND REQUIREMENTS:
</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree in English, Journalism, Advertising, Marketing, technical certification or work equivalent.</li>
<li>1-2 years working in a digital copywriting role, or other related field.</li>
<li>A well-crafted online portfolio and available clips.</li>
<li>Professional knowledge of English grammar and strong familiarity with various forms of style and composition.</li>
<li>Ability to make creative decisions and draft original, compelling copy that is on brand and adheres to strategy.</li>
<li>Ability to take on responsibility, collaborate effectively, and work comfortably across disciplines.</li>
<li>Highly effective self starter who can manage commitments, schedules and scope of work</li>
<li>A solid intuition of best practices used in user-centered interaction design.</li>
<li>Past experience serving on teams that have created new products as well as improved existing ones.</li>
<li>UX know-how (preferred, not required).</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with members of the Revenue Enablement, Sales leadership and Marketing teams to align copy writing with target audience needs, brand positioning, and team resources.</li>
<li>Apply fundamental concepts, practices, and procedures of copywriting.</li>
<li>Able to manage project, timelines and deliverables without direct supervision.
</li>
<li>Ensure consistency across all phases of the customer journey by creating standards, style guides, and processes.</li>
<li>Participate in the brainstorming and creation of concepts for best-in-class user experiences.</li>
<li>Set challenging goals and demonstrate persistence in accomplishing them.</li>
<li>Identify key decision-makers and the people that influence them.</li>
<li>Work independently but also show an ability to be nimble and apply existing guidelines when problem solving.</li>
<li>Uphold the company values and culture.</li>
<li>Clearly and accurately express ideas and convey information with customers.</li>
</ul>
