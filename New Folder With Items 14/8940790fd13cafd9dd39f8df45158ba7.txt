<h2>Director, Public Sector Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">As Director, Public Sector Sales, you will be responsible for determining strategy, building the marketplace awareness, and growing our revenue in the public sector in order to extend Teletrac Navman’s market leading SaaS offerings to the government. Your primary focus will be the Federal Government with a secondary focus on State and Local markets. The position includes supporting our FedRAMP authorization efforts, refining our public sector go-to-market strategy, and optimizing our contracting approach. You will engage in helping to direct marketing resources and efforts to build awareness for Teletrac Navman in the government space, coordinating initiatives with our Customer Success, Product Management, and Support functions. The end goal is to lead our Public Sector Sales team and their efforts to substantially growth in the Federal and State and Local constituencies.
