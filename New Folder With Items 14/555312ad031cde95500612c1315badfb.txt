<h2>Interior Sensing Camera Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This position is for the Driver Information – Interior Sensing (DI-IS) Camera Engineer. The Camera Engineer is working with customers, the DI-IS team, and the Customer Regional Engineering teams and suppliers. The goal is to understand needs; create standardized, scalable and cost efficient camera designs; analyze gaps and propose solutions. This leads to the founding of a complete camera team (Optics, Mechanics, Electronics, Image Quality IQ, Lab, Software, …) The Camera Engineer is also technology scout and performing competitive analysis.</p>
<p><b>

<p>

<p><b>
The Role</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Define camera sub systems for Interior Sensing (IS).</li>
<li>Durability: perform simulations and measurements in order to evaluate aging, thermal influence and optical performance.</li>
<li>Create, coordinate and track work packages.</li>
<li>Support DI-IS with the integration of the camera sub-system.</li>
<li>RFQs: Read all related information and analyze.</li>
<li>Create summary and discuss in team. Point out “Reuse, Adapt, New” vs. existing standard or road map.</li>
<li>Identify risks.</li>
<li>Estimate efforts.</li>
<li>Lead reviews with customers (open questions follow-up, conformity matrix etc.) and suppliers.</li>
<li>Support meetings with core team, CRE team.</li>
<li>Technology: Scout for new technologies as well as new ways to apply existing technologies.</li>
<li>Create timing and deliverables plans.</li>
<li>Develop manufacturing concepts (DFM), e.g. related to alignment, validation.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The Requirements</b></p>
<p>
To be a good fit for Interior Sensing Camera Engineer you will have:</p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years in the field.</li>
<li>Bachelor of Science in Optics, Physics or Engineering.</li>
<li>A strong technical background in all fields involved in camera development: Optics and Image Quality, Lens, Coatings, Filters, CFM, Imagers, Integrated Circuits and Micros, Companion Chips, Data Transfer, Communication, ISP, Physics. DI-IS Cam.Eng(.Mgr) must be able to understand multiple disciplines and be open to expand her/his horizon.</li>
<li>Evidence of hands-on and successful work.</li>
<li>Experience with reading customer RFQs, and distilling key information is key.</li>
<li>Evidence of high work output, and ability to meet tight deadlines will be needed.</li>
<li>Experience and/or Versatility in using computer based tools, e.g. CAD, simulation (optics, FoV, mechanical, endurance, thermal).</li>
<li>Experience in video processing.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>7+ years experience in the field.</li>
<li>Masters degree in Optics, Physics or Engineering.</li>
<li>Desirable is experience in DMS/OMS including control of illuminators (LEDs).</li>
</ul>
