<h2>Director – Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<div><div>
<b>Role</b><br>
Moody's Analytics, a wholly owned subsidiary of Moody’s Corporation, offers leading-edge software, advisory services and research for credit risk management. This product management role is within the Risk and Finance Analytics line of business in the Enterprise Risk Solutions division – one of the largest divisions of Moody’s Analytics.<br>
<br>
We are looking for a Director to serve as business lead for customer success. In this role you will work with our users to identify which business applications are relevant and help drive usage of our tools. As the lead in the customer success role you will drive retention by working with our research, professional services, and product development teams to bring these customers the solutions they need.<br>
<br>
In addition, you will be responsible for helping the rest of our customers improve their practices by leveraging the experience and use cases of our top customers. This will be achieved through a combination of translating these best practices to the appropriate level of sophistication for other customers as well as helping to prioritize future product initiatives for the rest of our market.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><ul>
<li>
Determine each client’s needs for the portfolio products roadmap and identify opportunities and potential issues for Moody’s Analytics.
</li>
<li>Educate clients to use products in line with industry best practices.</li>
<li>
Understand each client’s priorities and help position our solutions to meet their needs.</li>
<li>
Leverage feedback from top tier clients to help develop new product and service offerings.</li>
<li>
Work with sales to map the client organization and identify the key stakeholders
</li>
<li>Provide feedback to key Enterprise Risk Solutions stakeholders
</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<div><ul>
<li>Minimum of 10 years’ experience in a client facing role in Sales or Consulting within the financial services industry – or experience working at a bank or life insurer.
</li>
<li>Advanced experience with RiskFrontier or other Credit VaR / Economic Capital platforms.
</li>
<li>Proven history in managing key stakeholder expectations.
</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of the capital and credit markets and financial products.
</li>
<li>Broad understanding of BASEL II, BASEL III, CCAR, ICAAP, and regulatory capital.</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel - approximately 30% of the time.
</li>
<li>B.S. in a quantitative field (Finance, Economics, Accounting, Mathematics, Statistics, Operations Research, Computer Science). Masters or MBA preferred.</li>
<li>
FRM or PRM or CFA designation a plus.</li>
<li>
Good understanding and appreciation of Moody’s Analytics products and services.</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills, including effective presentation skills to multiple levels of management and associates.</li>
</ul></div>

<p><b>Working at Moody's</b></p>
<div>Moody's is an essential component of the global capital markets, providing credit ratings, research, tools and analysis that contribute to transparent and integrated financial markets. Moody's Corporation (NYSE: MCO) is the parent company of Moody's Investors Service, which provides credit ratings and research covering debt instruments and securities, and Moody's Analytics, which offers leading-edge software, advisory services and research for credit and economic analysis and financial risk management. The Corporation, which reported revenue of $4.4 billion in 2018, employs approximately 13,100 people worldwide and maintains a presence in 42 countries. Further information is available at www.moodys.com.</div>
