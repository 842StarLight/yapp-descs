<h2>Restaurant Manager - Full-Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Founded in 1964, Benihana is the leading Japanese Restaurant in the dining Industry. The key to our success is simple: Our ability and passion for hiring the right people, investing in their training and development and keeping them happy! We recognize that growth and development is a process that never ends, that relationships need constant nurturing and that improvement is always possible.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are always open to individuals that have the passion and commitment to grow with the Benihana Family and share in our Success!</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Responsibilities</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Guest Related</div>
<ul>
<li>
Seeks out and uses service-related guest feedback and takes appropriate action to continually improve the level of service, drive overall guest satisfaction, increase return visits and reduce guest complaints.</li>
<li>
Develops and implements plans to continually improve guest satisfaction in the areas of server attentiveness and table cleanliness.</li>
<li>
Concentrates on training service personnel, to continually improve the guest experience.</li>
<li>
Ensures safety, sanitation and security guidelines are followed at all times and immediately resolves any items identified in inspections.</li>
<li>
Reinforces suggestive selling behaviors by ensuring that the guest is invited to enjoy sushi, side orders, beverages and desserts as part of the dining experience.</li>
<li>
Is responsible for maintaining the Benihana standards of products, services and quality.</li>
<li>
Creates and fosters a culture to make wine and sake an integral part of every guest’s dining experience.</li>
<li>
Creates an atmosphere that insures guests to feel welcome and wish to return.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Employee Related</div>
<ul>
<li>
Sources, recruits and selects diverse, highly skilled service and front of house employees.</li>
<li>
Guarantees commitment to dress/grooming and personal hygiene standards.</li>
<li>
Ensures steps of service are consistently executed by training, developing and holding employees accountable.</li>
<li>
Supports other managers in food and beverage training to develop the front of house employees.</li>
<li>
Delivers timely performance feedback in the form of active coaching, counseling and recognition.</li>
<li>
Prepares fair and consistent front of house schedules in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Ensures appropriate staffing levels to deliver a great guest dining experience.</li>
<li>
Takes necessary disciplinary action, including dismissal for unacceptable behavior or performance decisively.</li>
<li>
Conducts an all staff meetings at least once every four weeks.</li>
<li>
Is able to effectively address employee relations issues, appropriately problem-solve and liaison with the Human Resource department as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Investor Related</div>
<ul>
<li>
Develops and implements service plans that outline specific initiatives to increase return visits.</li>
<li>
Inspires teamwork among managers to build guest counts, sales and earnings through providing a superior dining experience for the guest.</li>
<li>
Manages beverage costs and monitors to ensure that all beverages are properly pre-checked and accounted for.</li>
<li>
Is responsible for protecting the company’s assets and interests from damage and theft. Keeps equipment functional.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Leadership Related</div>
<ul>
<li>
Takes a leadership role in initiating service standards that support the goal of delighting the guest.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates the appropriate level of knowledge and problem-solving and decision-making skills needed to drive service initiatives.</li>
<li>
Holds employees accountable for excellent service-related results and fosters an environment of open, clear, two-way communication.</li>
<li>
Gives feedback and offers retraining or redirection to raise performance.</li>
<li>
Quickly takes the necessary and appropriate action when performance falls below standards.</li>
<li>
Acts as an ambassador and enforcer of company regulations, policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Has general knowledge of Japanese culture relating to the Benihana Concept.</li>
<li>
Must have thorough working knowledge of restaurant operational procedures in all phases including equipment.</li>
<li>
Has full knowledge of corporate regulations, policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Has general knowledge of laws (both federal and local) that govern the restaurant industry.</li>
<li>
Is able to tabulate, compute and analyze business statistics of the Benihana Restaurant.</li>
<li>
Is able to use a personal computer or laptop computer provided by the Company for tasks given by the Company.</li>
<li>
Is able to operate and teach employees the P.O.S System.</li>
</ul>
