<h2>Director Integrated Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>REVOLT TV is focused on expertly curating the best in Hip Hop culture and engaging youth in social conversation. This unapologetically Hip Hop, multi-platform network offers original programming, videos, artist interviews, exclusive performances and live events.<br>
<br>
REVOLT TV is looking for a Director of Integrated Marketing to support the Ad Sales Integrated Marketing teams in NY and LA.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>What You’ll Do</b>:<br>
<br>
We are looking for an experienced, solutions-oriented Director of Integrated Marketing to develop premiere multi-screen sponsorship programs, branded content concepts, and compelling onsite activations for REVOLT’s advertising partners. Responsibilities include the creation, presentation, and execution of advertiser initiatives along with recaps and reporting on core results and KPI’s.<br>
<br>
This strategic thinker and diligent team-player will utilize REVOLT’s linear TV, digital, social content and on-the-ground events as the foundation for creative ideas that are compelling to advertisers while remaining authentic to REVOLT’s brand and audience.<br>
<br>
The Director will work with the Sales team to understand client needs and develop relevant solutions through collaboration with internal partners including Programming, Talent, and Social/Digital teams. They will manage their project’s efforts independently with support from leadership.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate will be passionate about Hip Hop Culture, possess strong interpersonal and creative skills, along with a strong work ethic, positive attitude, organized thinking and solutions-oriented approach. They will do well working in a dynamic, fast-paced environment independently, while knowing how to keep management in the loop with timely, key updates.<br>
<br>
<b>Your Day-to-Day</b>:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Pro-actively develop and deliver best-in-class multiplatform marketing solutions that support REVOLT’s priority initiatives</li>
<li>Review client RFP’s and brand brief’s with Sales executives and attend client meetings</li>
<li>Strategize, imaginatively conceptualize, and creatively write sales deck’s and marketing presentations</li>
<li>Manage the execution of sold campaigns from launch to completion, working in lockstep with Production Management and external vendors</li>
<li>Collaborate with our Insights team and Research partner to gain insights for clients for both pre and post sale reports/recaps.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Who You Are</b>:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Strong affinity/passion for Hip Hop Culture (Music, Fashion, Art, Social Justice, etc.)</li>
<li>Savvy in events, linear, digital marketing across video, display, mobile and social preferably within the TV industry, at an ad agency, media company or digital company with ad-supported revenue</li>
<li>A motivated individual with a solutions-based mindset, great attention to detail, succinct communications, organization skills and timeliness</li>
<li>Influence strong interpersonal skills across cross-functional teams, building productive relationships across REVOLT with various levels of management</li>
<li>Excellent PowerPoint and superlative creative writing deck skills</li>
<li>Capability to interpret RFP’s with the capacity to communicate complex concepts succinctly</li>
<li>Thrives in a fast-paced environment and changing business priorities</li>
<li>Able to implement multiple projects simultaneously within time and budget constraints, independently</li>
<li>Ability to travel domestically and work weekends and beyond standard office hours</li>
<li>Proactive self-starter with strong client service focus</li>
</ul>
