<h2>Business Development Manager - SAAS</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description </b></p>
<p>We are currently seeking ‘ Business Development Manager - SAAS’ to drive and expand sales of our ERP Software and Services into major accounts and SMB. Specifically, you will be responsible for opening new doors and driving the sales process from initial interest development through Cold Calling, Prospecting, Need Analysis, Product Positioning, Software Demos into Proposal and Closing.</p>
<p><b>About us </b></p>
<p>At 2nd Foundation Software, we empower fashion businesses to manage and control operations thru our flagship ERP Software called -Axiom. Since our inception in 2009, we have earned a sound reputation of a Customer Centric company and take great pride in our accomplishments.</p>
<p><b>Your Responsibilities </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Open new doors &amp; Identify leads through a variety of methods including a significant amount of cold calling, emailing, web research, relationship and referral marketing.</li>
<li>Create and maintain robust pipeline through aggressive outbound prospecting efforts.</li>
<li>Use sales cycle and selling skills to educate and qualify prospects.</li>
<li>Schedule and conduct presentations and product demonstrations.</li>
<li>Create proposals, negotiate and close sales.</li>
<li>Produce accurate and timely forecasts, develop account strategy and meet monthly, quarterly goals.</li>
<li>Maintain up-to-date prospect information in CRM</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent real-world experience.</li>
<li>At least 2 year of experience in selling Software As A Service - SAAS.</li>
<li>Experience in discovery processes, qualification, pre-call planning, call control, account development, and time management</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to meet and exceed sales targets on a consistent basis</li>
<li>Self-motivated individual, with strong follow up skills, who enjoys customer contact and problem solving.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Salary/Compensation</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Base salary with excellent sales commission.</li>
<li>Performance bonus every month you meet or exceed sales targets.</li>
</ul>
