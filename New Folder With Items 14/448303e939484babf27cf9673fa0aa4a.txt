<h2>Finance Manager- Verticals</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university in business, finance, engineering, economics, or related fields and a minimum of 7 years of finance experience, OR master’s degree and 5 years of experience, or an equivalent combination of education and experience.

<p>Amazon.com seeks a Finance Manager to be a key partner for Amazon’s Performance Advertising business, a high-growth Advertising business. We are breaking fresh ground, pioneering a crucial program for future Amazon growth, and our products are among the fastest growing at Amazon. We are entrepreneurial and have a bias for action with a broad mandate to experiment and innovate.<br>
<br>
Our self-service advertising products provide low-latency ad serving for a performance-based advertising program with large ad inventories, a unique problem space within Amazon advertising. We are looking for a top analytical mind capable of understanding the complex interaction of advertisers participating via a “pay-per-click” business model. You can learn more about Advertising products at https://advertising.amazon.com/<br>
<br>
As a Finance Manager in the team, you will be leading a team that is responsible for advertising for specific Verticals such as Books and Apparel. You will provide support through business performance management, analytics and financial modelling. This role requires an individual with excellent business, communication, and technical skills, enabling collaboration with various functions, including economists, product managers, software engineers, finance leaders, as well as senior business leadership. The successful candidate will be a self-starter comfortable with ambiguity, with strong attention to detail, an ability to work in a fast-paced, high-energy and ever-changing environment.</p>
