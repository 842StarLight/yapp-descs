<b><i>
What is the Treasury Accounting Cash Control / Fund Distribution and Performance team responsible for?</i></b><br>
</div>
<div>
<br>
We work closely with our business teams to understand their business needs and define the technology vision/roadmap. Our partners trust us because we have a shared vision and we deliver on our commitments. We are passionate about what we do and help each other grow to our greatest potential. TACC/Fund Distribution &amp; Performance Team provides quality technology support and solutions for various treasury accounting and cash control related applications like Check Management System, Frontier, Global Payment System, TOAR (Treasury Operational Accounting and Reporting), and publishing the Fund NAV and performance to internal/external clients using Unity.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>What are the ongoing responsibilities of a Software Engineer?</i></b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The primary focus of this position is to provide support to the vendor product Unity and other applications, design/development, customization within the products, code reviews/audits and identifying improvement/automation opportunities. The role is also responsible for ensuring adherence to technical standards. The individual will be expected to apply their experience to help in advancing the technical landscape, while closely working with the business users. Successful candidate will be working closely with senior team members to assess the feasibility of technical solutions, and measuring the success of deployments. The candidate will work in a highly diverse, decentralized and global environment. This is a full-time position and will be located in our Rancho Cordova office.</div>
<br>
 <div></div>
<b>Production Support</b>
<div>Applies understanding of the business domain to triage support issues. Correlates environmental changes to production issues. Schedules and monitors production jobs. Monitors capacity metrics. Collaborates with vendors to resolve issues. Supports Disaster Recovery activities. Configures and supports vendor products.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Software Engineering</b></div>
<div>Develops new code and configuration for complete features. Reviews source code of more junior engineers. Provides oversight for junior engineers work product by incorporating basic business domain knowledge. Develops and maintains automatable test cases. Develops automated build and deployment methods. Self-educates to gain a comprehensive understanding of relevant development languages, runtimes, frameworks, database platforms, and tooling.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Documentation</b><br>
Analyzes requirements, proposes high level solutions, and documents new code and configurations for complete features. Documents solutions and configurations for vendor products or developed systems. Reviews effort estimates of more junior engineers. Contributes to project plans. Understands vendor product releases. Documents production support issue resolutions, Disaster Recovery steps, and responses to audits. (Required)</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Technical Leadership
</b></div>
<div>Plans and coordinates cutover and implementation activities. Builds and maintains effective working relationships with other technical teams. (Required)</div>
</div>
