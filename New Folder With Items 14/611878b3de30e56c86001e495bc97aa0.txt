<h2>Strategic Account Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>

</p>

</p>

<div>
<div>Short Description
</div>
<div><p>The Strategic Account Representative is an outside sales position primarily responsible for managing and growing industrial segment revenue within a geographic market. Qualified candidates will be able to develop business relationships with a select group of companies and individuals within the industrial segment. This is an excellent opportunity for motivated self-starters who want to enhance their skills with a company whose reputation speaks for itself.
</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>Responsibilities
</div>
<div>
<p>Generate revenue with a select group of companies within the industrial segment (Oil/Gas, Manufacturing, Power, Mining, etc.)
</p>
<p>Daily account management and revenue growth through visits to various customer offices, facilities, and job sites.
</p>
<p>Develop, plan and organize sales strategies to achieve desired results/goals.
</p>
<p>Effective implementation of industrial services concepts, products and services.
</p>
<p>Identify customer’s needs and react appropriately while understanding the market conditions and local competitors pricing.
</p>
<p>Resolve customer issues and concerns.
</p>
<p>Assist branches in keeping abreast with industrial customer changes and job site problems.
</p>
<p>Support all team members.
</p>
</div>
</div>
