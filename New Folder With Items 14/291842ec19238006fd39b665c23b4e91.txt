<h2>Buyer/Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>The Buyer/Planner has the primary responsibility of supporting Operations for both released products and new product introduction. Other responsibilities include customer acknowledgements, material planning and procurement for both direct and indirect materials, drive daily activities and priorities using metrics as well as incorporating input from multiple departments.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate request for Quotes, Requisitions, and Purchase Orders.</li>
<li>Expedite all open Purchase Orders to cover requirements for assigned products, goods, and services.</li>
<li>Ensure availability of materials to meet production schedules.</li>
<li>Follow up with open orders and NPI/Production expediting.</li>
<li>Responsible for ERP transactions.</li>
<li>Process MRB/RMA Transactions back to contract manufacturer.</li>
<li>Prepare Internal Sales Orders for suppliers.</li>
<li>Open purchase orders, review, and follow up including FBC open purchase order report reconciliation.</li>
<li>Create Travelers – RFID Software.</li>
<li>Responsible for work order transactions.</li>
<li>In support of others:</li>
</ul>
<p>¨ Submit and follow up vendor qualification packages.</p>
<p>¨ Update customer tracker/send backlog reports.</p>
<p>¨ Resolve invoice problems.</p>
<p>¨ Assist with cycle count.</p>
<p>¨ Create excel reports.</p>
<p>¨ Assist with semi-annual full physical inventory count.</p>
<p>¨ Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul><li>BS or BA in Business or Supply Chain preferred.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years related experience in a Buyer/Planner role and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>Proficient with MS Office Suite – advanced skills with MS Excel.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience in a manufacturing environment preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to work in cross-functional teams.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Results oriented work ethic.</li></ul>
