<h2>Mortgage President</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Bank of American Fork, one of Utah's premier community banks, is hiring a Mortgage President based in our Orem office.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Directs and coordinates all Mortgage Loan Department activities</li>
<li>Participates in the management and strategic planning of the Bank Mortgage Division</li>
<li>Directs daily operations of the Mortgage Loan Department and supervises employees; monitors production levels, profit and loss, maintains a highly qualified staff, strong knowledge of all mortgage products and rates both with the bank and the industry as a whole, sufficient to meet the daily lending demands for customers</li>
<li>Maintains loan delinquency control, and reporting to regulatory agencies and investors as required; calls on delinquent customers as needed, develops and maintains rapport with buyers in the secondary market and oversees the servicing portfolio and investor reporting</li>
<li>Monitors market conditions to maximize profitability on sale of loans, hedging risk if necessary</li>
<li>Oversees and evaluates products and procedures currently in place and implements changes as required</li>
<li>Works with marketing staff in developing mortgage loan promotions</li>
<li>Assist Chief Risk Officer in CRA compliance</li>
<li>Insure that all lending activity is within established policies and regulations</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Five to eight years of similar or related experience</li>
<li>A college degree (BS or BA in a relevant field)</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of real estate lending practices and procedures, and related areas</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of bank policy and procedures</li>
<li>Strong managerial, communication and public relations skills</li>
<li>Ability to run spreadsheet and word processing software</li>
</ul>
<p>Schedule: Monday-Friday, 8-5.</p>
<p>
We offer competitive pay based on experience, excellent full benefit package, paid time off and holiday pay.</p>
<p>
Equal Opportunity Employer/Veterans/Disabled</p>
