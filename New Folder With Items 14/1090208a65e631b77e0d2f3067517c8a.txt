<h2>Demand Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>
Plans and prepares production schedules for manufacture of industrial or commercial products by performing the following duties.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Plan, schedule, release, distribute and close work orders and purchase requisitions.</li>
<li>
Monitor the schedule and plan production departments to support dynamic customer demand and service levels.</li>
<li>
Identify and resolve issues to ensure production continuity and adherence to customer delivery schedules.</li>
<li>
Create master schedule to establish sequence and lead time of each operation to meet shipping dates according to sales forecasts or customer orders.</li>
<li>
Analyzes production specifications and plant capacity data and performs mathematical calculations to determine manufacturing processes, tools, and human resource requirements.</li>
<li>
Plans and schedules workflow for each department and operation according to previously established manufacturing sequences and lead times.</li>
<li>
Collaborating/communicating with Production, Sourcing, Quality, Engineering, Customer Service and other departments in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>
Support implementation of new ERP System to forecast production demand, manage customer forecast, safety stock requirements, and maintain desired inventory levels.</li>
<li>
Performs administrative activities associated with the effective management of shop operations, including compiling, storing, and retrieving production data for reports.</li>
<li>
Support development and facilitation of S&amp;OP, Sales and Operations Planning meetings and process.</li>
<li>
Monitor slow moving inventory and obsolescence and drive improvements to achieve inventory goals and reduce obsolescence.</li>
<li>
Support development of cycle counting process and ongoing physical counts and system transactions and yearly physical inventory.</li>
<li>
Work with operations, supply chain, engineering and support departments to improve production efficiencies.</li>
<li>
Participate in lean events and the support of continuous improvement initiatives and projects.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and lead daily production meetings.</li>
<li>
Support development and maintenance of key production metrics.</li>
<li>
Serve as back up support for operations and production leadership team as needed.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p><b>
Education and/or Experience</b></p>
<p>
Bachelor's degree from four-year college or university; or three to five years planning experience; inventory management and analysis experience in a manufacturing environment.</p>
<p><b>
Language Skills</b></p>
<p>
Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals. Ability to write routine reports and correspondence. Ability to speak effectively before groups of customers or employees of organization.</p>
<p><b>
Mathematical Skills</b></p>
<p>
Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, interest, proportions, percentages, area, circumference, and volume. Ability to apply concepts of basic algebra and geometry.</p>
<p><b>
Reasoning Ability</b></p>
<p>
Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out instructions furnished in written, oral, or diagram form. Ability to deal with problems involving several concrete variables in standardized situations.</p>
<p><b>
Computer Skills</b></p>
<p>
To perform this job successfully, an individual should have knowledge of ERP System, Microsoft XA Preferred, Word Processing software; Excel Spreadsheet software; Manufacturing software; Order processing systems and Inventory software.</p>

Certificates, Licenses, Registrations</b></p>

APICS certification a plus.</p>
