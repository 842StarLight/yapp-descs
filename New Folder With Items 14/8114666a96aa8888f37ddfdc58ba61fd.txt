<p><b>Assistant Store Manager</b></p>
<p>Stop N Stor, a multi-facility storage chain in the Greater Savannah areas, is looking for an Assistant Manager in the Pooler area to help manage the day-to-day operations of a state-of-the-art self storage facility.</p>
<p>The Storage Facility Assistant Manager must have excellent communication &amp; customer service skills, be able to convert walk-in and web reservations, and perform light facility maintenance.</p>
<p><b>Sales Focus: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong sales skills with interacting with potential new tenants/customers</li>
<li>Maximizing conversion of telephone and walk-in inquiries</li>
<li>Highly motivated to achieve business objectives</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Customer Service: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain a friendly and cheerful representation of the Stop N Stor brand to potential customers, tenants, and staff</li>
<li>Professional appearance and positive attitude</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Executing new leases</li>
<li>Maintaining accurate records for each customer</li>
<li>Taking daily payments</li>
<li>Reporting EOD results</li>
<li>Performing collections for delinquent accounts</li>
<li>Strong accountability</li>
<li>Maintaining property appearance</li>
<li>Light cleaning of the property</li>
<li>Work weekends</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Office Suite (i.e. Word, Excel, Outlook)</li>
<li>Storage Facility Software / Point of Sale Software knowledge is a plus</li>
<li>High school degree preferred</li>
<li>Retail or sales experience preferred</li>
<li>Previous storage facility experience is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation &amp; Benefits: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Some bonus opportunities</li>
<li>Competitive Compensation &amp; Benefits Package (health, dental, vision, life)</li>
<li>Company is in the process of building a 401k with tenure based match</li>
<li>Potential after time period of success to move in to a managerial role</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Please note that only those candidates that we are interested in pursuing for an interview will be contacted.</i></p>
<p>Job Types: Full-time, Part-time</p>
<p>Salary: $10.00 to $12.00 /hour</p>

<ul>
<li>Sales: 2 years (Required)</li>
<li>Customer Service: 2 years (Required)</li>
</ul>
