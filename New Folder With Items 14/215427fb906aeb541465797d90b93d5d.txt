<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Marketing Coordinator</b><br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Job Summary:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Lead PSC Industries marketing strategies, materials and multiple platform online presence. Create and update focused marketing materials for targeted markets, including flyers, email templates, PowerPoint templates, trade show materials, and website updates based on Management’s direction.</li>
<li>
Tracking sales data; maintaining promotional materials inventory; planning meetings and trade shows; maintaining databases; preparing reports. Also will support Human Resources with recruiting materials for job fairs and employee retention programs through focus on employee recognition via online platforms and formal communication with employees.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Main Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Implements marketing and advertising campaigns by assembling and analyzing sales forecasts; preparing marketing and advertising strategies, plans, and objectives; planning and organizing promotional presentations; updating calendars.</li>
<li>
Tracks product line sales and costs by analyzing and entering sales, expense, and new business data.</li>
<li>
Prepares marketing reports by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing sales data.</li>
<li>
Keeps promotional materials ready by coordinating requirements with graphics department; inventorying stock; placing orders; verifying receipt.</li>
<li>
Supports sales staff by providing sales data, market trends, forecasts, account analyses, new product information; relaying customer services requests.</li>
<li>
Receives competitive product information, evaluates product characteristics, market share, pricing, and advertising; maintaining research databases.</li>
<li>
Plans meetings and trade shows by identifying, assembling, and coordinating requirements; establishing contacts; developing schedules and assignments; coordinating mailing lists.</li>
<li>
Monitors budgets by comparing and analyzing actual results with plans and forecasts.</li>
<li>
Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities; reading trade publications.</li>
<li>
Accomplishes organization goals by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and different requests; exploring opportunities to add value to job accomplishments.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Required Qualifications &amp; Experiences:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Direct Marketing, Market Segmentation, Marketing Research, Coordination, Project Management, Reporting Research Results, Understanding the Customer, Process Improvement, Initiative, Planning, Financial Skills</li>
<li>
2+ years working in Marketing (Online and/or consumer products a plus, but not required)</li>
<li>
Associated degree in marketing or business.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate ability to think creatively</li>
<li>
Outstanding communication skills in written and verbal forms including technical writing</li>
<li>
Proficient in Adobe Photoshop &amp; Illustrator</li>
<li>
Proficient in Wordpress editing and content management</li>
<li>
“Basic” HTML coding capable</li>
<li>
Pass the drug screen and background check.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
