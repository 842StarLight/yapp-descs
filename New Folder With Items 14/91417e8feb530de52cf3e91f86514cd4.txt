<p>Headquartered in Congers, NY with offices in East Hartford, CT. Goosetown Communications are one of the largest mobile voice &amp; wireless enterprise communications companies in the Northeast and United States. We provide field communications and service to many of the public safety, commercial and industrial users in our areas. These include but not limited to Police, Fire, County, School Districts, School Bus &amp; Transportation companies. Due to a recent acquisition we are seeking additional outside account executives to interface with current customers and new prospects through telephone, and face-to-face meetings to sell assigned products and services in the East Hartford, CT greater business area.</p>
