<div><b>
About the Role</b></div>
<div>
Performs a broad range of duties for an executive or executive team, as assigned. Manages appointment scheduling and meeting/event logistics; maintains assigned calendars. Responsible for preparing agendas and materials for regular and special meetings; secures requested information and compiles various reports. Handles telephone calls and emails as requested, routing them to others or dealing with them directly, as appropriate. Prepares a variety of communications including emails, memos, and meeting minutes as needed. Processes expense reports. Book travel arrangements including pre-authorizations.</div>
</div>
<div><div>

What you will do</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Provide support and coordination on projects, with the ability to quickly re-prioritize in order to meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Coordinate and schedule meetings based on executive/group availability as needed. Includes scheduling the conference room and all related technology as well as provisions</li>
<li>
Ensure event logistics are planned appropriately and in advance of the event</li>
<li>
Manage travel arrangements, as needed, including pre-authorizations</li>
<li>
Distribute and track documents, ensure proper routing/approvals and deliver or file when finalized</li>
<li>
Audit and process expense reports and route for approval</li>
<li>
Prepare presentations, emails, memos and other written communication pieces</li>
<li>
Administer, organize and maintain project and department files, both electronically and in hard copy</li>
<li>
Attend project / community meetings and prepare meeting minutes as assigned</li>
<li>
Perform other related duties as assigned</li>
<li>
Submit and process invoices/check requests</li>
<li>
Maintain and order office supplies as needed</li>
<li>
Onboard and set up new hires</li>
<li>
Handle calls and emails as appropriate</li>
<li>
Support reception coverage</li>
<li>
Provide customer service, troubleshoot and documenting phone call/experience and routing to appropriate department</li>
<li>
Additional duties as required</li>
