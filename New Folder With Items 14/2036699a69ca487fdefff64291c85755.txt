<h2>Front Desk - Fitness Club</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Join theRow House team providing the perfect environment for our members to come to meet their fitness goals, connect with other members, and to be given the best customer service in the industry! We area new national franchised fitness studio based upon rowing.</p>
<p>The purpose of the <b>front desk service personell</b> is to assist the General Manager with new membership sales, booking prospects into Intro Classes, and providing excellent customer service to our existing members. Fitness knowledge or background is preferred but not required.</p>
<p>REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Fluent in English and have excellent communication skills via in person, phone, and email</li>
<li>Very friendly and outgoing, in all situations, with all client types</li>
<li>Must be able to work under pressure at times</li>
<li>Must have proficient computer skills - being able to learn/use customer software tools</li>
</ul>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist the GM with the sales process</li>
<li>Book and confirm prospects into classes</li>
<li>Greet and check-in clients and prospects</li>
<li>Conduct tours of the facility, establishing a good customer relationship</li>
<li>Present available services and retail to current or prospective members</li>
<li>Ensure studio is clean and stocked.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>COMPENSATION &amp; PERKS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive base pay based on experience</li>
<li>Free or discounted studio memberships</li>
<li>Commission paid on membership and retail sales</li>
<li>Significant opportunities for growth within the studios, including additional sales, coaching, and management opportunities</li>
</ul>
