<h2>Sr Software Engineer - Front End Web Developer (UI)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you passionate about building the best possible user experience with a team of people who really care about their craft? Do you excel in an environment of continuous self-improvement and learning? Do you dream in responsive containers and swoon over flexbox's possibilities?</p>
<p>
We are looking for someone who is motivated to build truly great web applications to be our team's lead frontend engineer. The apps we build are used by Splunk’s sales &amp; customer success organizations and our partners to help prospects and customers understand the value they can derive from Splunk, and provide actionable plans to achieve this. These apps have a measurable effect on Splunk’s bottom line.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Your Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Create amazing applications in a modern single-page JavaScript framework (React).</li>
<li>
Collaborate with UI experts and designers to implement responsive interfaces across multiple platforms, including web and mobile.</li>
<li>
Build close relationships with teammates, product managers, and quality engineers.</li>
<li>
Break down feature requests into actionable plans and assist when needed with the creation and execution of test plans.</li>
<li>
Put your head together with key architects and managers to establish and implement standard methodologies for delivery of high quality software.</li>
<li>
Continually strive to improve the efficiency and capabilities of the full-stack developers in your team.</li>
<li>
Ensure development projects fulfill business requirements and delight end-users.</li>
<li>
Engage in hands-on, in-depth analysis, review, design, development and debugging of your applications.</li>
<li>
Lead your team to UI greatness!</li>
</ul>
