<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Front Office / Guest Service Agent is responsible for providing attentive, courteous and efficient service to all guests during check-in and checkout and throughout their stay, in the hotel's continuing effort to deliver outstanding guest service and financial profitability.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Greet, check-in and checkout guests approaching the Front Desk in a friendly, efficient and courteous manner and in accordance with company, hotel and brand standards.</li>
<li>
Answer guest inquiries about hotel services, facilities, restaurants, transportation, entertainment and hours of operation in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Resolves guest challenges and takes appropriate action to ensure 100% guest satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Build rapport with rewards members, distinguished visitors; escort guests to rooms, make welfare calls, encourage feedback throughout their stay.</li>
<li>
Identify, solicit and sign up potential reward members (if applicable).</li>
<li>
Maintain and market promotions and guest programs.</li>
<li>
Obtain all necessary information when taking room reservations and follow the rate-quoting scenario.</li>
<li>
Follow all cash handling and credit policies.</li>
<li>
Ensure logging and delivery of packages, mail and messages to guests and meeting rooms.</li>
<li>
Use proper two-way radio and phone etiquette at all times when communicating with other employees.</li>
<li>
Fully comprehend and be able to operate all relevant aspects of the Front Desk computer system.</li>
<li>
Be able to perform and complete all tasks and duties on the shift checklist in a timely and efficient manner.</li>
<li>
Process sales for guest marketplace, dry cleaning, postage and other miscellaneous transactions.</li>
<li>
Complete a bucket check, room rate verification report, and housekeeping report.</li>
<li>
Balance and prepare individual paperwork for closing of shift according to hotel standards.</li>
<li>
Maintain a clean work area.</li>
<li>
Assist guests with safe deposit boxes.</li>
<li>
Review Front Office log daily.</li>
<li>
Be aware of all rates, packages, special promotions and closed out/restricted dates as listed.</li>
<li>
Be familiar with all in-house groups.</li>
<li>
Fill in and support coworkers and team as needed to ensure efficient operation.</li>
<li>
Have knowledge of emergency procedures and assist when needed.</li>
<li>
Perform duties, special assignments and projects as requested by management.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
