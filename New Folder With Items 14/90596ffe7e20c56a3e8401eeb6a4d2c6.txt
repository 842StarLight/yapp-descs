<h2>Safety Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>LifeSafety Technician – Route Sales &amp; Service</b></p>
<p>Perform installation, inspection,testing, service, and repair required on fire extinguishers, exit/emergencylights, and kitchen hood suppression systems, for compliance with applicable fireand life-safety requirements as mandated by standards and codes, manufacturersrequirements, federal, state and local ordinances, customer requirements, andthe expectations and requirements of the company.</p>
<p><b>PrimaryDuties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform inspection, testing, service, and repairs as required for fire extinguishers, exit/emergency lights, and kitchen hood suppression systems, such as six-year maintenance, hydrotest, recharge, battery replacement, bulb replacement and other services and repairs as mandated by Standards and codes, manufacturers requirements, federal, state and local ordinances, and procedures, customer requirements, and expectations and requirements of CertaSite.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and perform service and repair of fire extinguishers, exit/emergency lights, and kitchen hood suppression systems.</li>
<li>Evaluate customer site for additional equipment and services that customer may require.</li>
<li>Look for opportunities to expand and/or cross-sell services.</li>
<li>Discuss deficiencies noted with customer, explaining the problem and its resolution.</li>
<li>Record and document all inspections, repairs, discrepancies, and recommendations, either electronically or manually.</li>
<li>Maintain a good positive relationship with the customer and pass on information to the appropriate personnel for follow up, quotes, questions, or concerns.</li>
<li>Maintain vehicle in clean and orderly fashion. Keep vehicle stocked with parts, equipment, and supplies, necessary to perform the job.</li>
<li>Maintain equipment and tools necessary to safely perform job.</li>
<li>Work in compliance with the Company's safety program.</li>
<li>Some situations may require work on nights, weekends, and/or holidays. For some locations, these demands will be more frequent or required than others. This will be discussed with your Manager.</li>
<li>Must be comfortable with heights, working on ladders or lifts, or in confined spaces.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with electrical wiring and circuitry, desired.</li>
<li>Must possess the ability to calculate basic math including fractions, decimals and percentages.</li>
<li>Must possess the knowledge and ability to understand and operate basic computer functions, operating systems and software applications such as Windows, Word, Apple IOS, Android and Outlook. In addition, required to learn and use industry specific devices and software.</li>
<li>Must currently have a valid U.S. driver's license.</li>
<li>State license or NICET certifications for the state in which you are applying is desired. In addition, employees may not have prior experience but have the potential and willingness to learn. The training and testing required to obtain a license or certifications is provided and paid for by the company.</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills needed:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Strong mechanical and technical aptitude</li>
<li>Highly organized and detail-oriented</li>
<li>Problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication and interpersonal skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Overtime may be required and the number of hours may vary based on work location.</li>
<li>May be exposed to all kinds of work environments, varying from hot to cold temperatures and exposure to dust.</li>
<li>Occasional travel overnight to a customer location.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must successfully pass a lift test up to 50 pounds.</li>
<li>Frequent lifting and carrying of toolboxes, equipment, parts, tools, equipment, cabling, and/or wiring.</li>
<li>Frequent ladder climbing and checking, pulling wire, etc.</li>
<li>Walking, stooping, bending, crouching, kneeling, and raising hands overhead.</li>
<li>Driving is required and the amount of time driving may vary.</li>
<li>Required to wear Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) for this position in accordance with safety standards.</li>
</ul>
