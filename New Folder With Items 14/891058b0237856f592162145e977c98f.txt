<h2>Junior Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">A pioneer in insurance technology, Insureon operates the largest online insurance marketplace for small businesses in the U.S. Our proprietary technology platform enables business owners to easily compare and purchase the insurance they need. We also license this platform to select industry participants who are looking to more effectively service their own clients. We are integrated with every major U.S. carrier, manage over ~$400M in premium annually and are licensed in all 50 states. For more information, visit https://www.insureon.com
<br><br>
The company employs approximately 175 people nationally, with its Corporate location in the heart of the financial district in the Chicago Loop.
<br><br>
We place a high value on having fun at work, personal and company integrity and teamwork, and we believe our success is truly a team sport. We foster a service mentality inside and outside our company and our fast-paced team is driven by results and is energetic about servicing our clients.
<br><br>
<b>Summary of the Role:</b><br>
<b>Objective:</b> To provide assistance and support to the AM (Account Management) Team Leads daily job functions as well as growing knowledge of the AM desk and products to gain experience to eventually service own book of business as an Account Manager. The priority function of this role will be to assist the AM Leads of a specific Desk, followed by the rest of the AMs.
<br><br>
This role is viewed as a developmental position with the objective of obtaining the skills, proficiencies and sustained performance necessary to migrate to an Account Managers role over the course of time.
<br><br>
Essential Functions and Responsibilities;
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Approved State Licenses needed</li>
<li>Prepare and submit renewal applications</li>
<li>Identify and execute cross sell opportunities</li>
<li>Broker the terms and conditions of insurance contracts</li>
<li>Coordinate and monitor account servicing</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>Additional or Daily Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist the AM team members to act on alerts in the agency management system.</li>
<li>Follow up with Carrier/ Client on new business quotes and renewals.</li>
<li>Review renewal data and assist AMs to maximizing our book retention rate.</li>
<li>Support policy cancellation process.</li>
<li>Assist with customer servicing requests from clients by working with internal teams and carrier service centers.</li>
<li>Deliver excellent customer service to delight our customers in every online and offline interaction.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years of relevant experience.</li>
<li>Familiar with Customer Account Servicing in regulated/financial services experience preferred.</li>
</ul>
<b>Education/Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High School Degree Required</li>
<li>4 Year Degree Preferred</li>
<li>Approved State Licenses needed</li>
</ul>
<b>Computer Skills:</b> Word processing, spreadsheets, email software and other related programs. Comfortable working with 3rd Party or Agency management systems, Various Web Applications, Client or Insureon Agent portals and other related online applications.
<br><br>
<b>Physical Demands or Requirements:</b> This role requires sitting at a workstation for extended periods of time, while working in an office environment.
<br><br>
<b>Travel Requirements:</b><br>
This an office environment and workstation-based assignment, no travel is required to complete the responsibilities of this assignment.
