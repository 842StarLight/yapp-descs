<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h1>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Nutrition Program Coordinator (NPC) serves as the primary driver for all products and services considered part of Life Time Training’s Nutrition, Metabolism and Weight Management departments. They will ensure all team members are educated in Life Time's nutrition philosophy and programs. They will mentor a team of nutrition coaches and ensure they can deliver artistry level nutrition coaching services.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Performs member outreach and connectivity for nutrition products and services</li>
<li>
Promotes and assists Life Time Training initiatives such as the 90 day Challenge, Alpha events and Black Friday offers</li>
<li>
Provides monthly education to PT staff on any of the Nutrition products or services offered</li>
<li>
Maintains monthly calendar of events and communicates to PT team and Regional Lead weekly</li>
<li>
Completes and submits a monthly business review to PT Department Head and regional Nutrition Lead</li>
<li>
Performs 1:1, partner or group nutrition coaching for members</li>
<li>
Provides a variety of services such as grocery tours, healthy living classes, lab testing and nutrition coaching</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3 months or greater tenure with Life Time Training</li>
<li>
High School Diploma or GED</li>
<li>
Registered Dietitian, CISSN, Certified Nutrition Consultant or other considered certification</li>
<li>
Personal Training Certification</li>
<li>
Knowledge of fitness, cardiovascular training, nutrition and program design</li>
<li>
Ability to operate a computer and office software, Microsoft Word and Excel</li>
<li>
1 client success story submission to myStory or LifeTime-WeightLoss.com</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Nutrition, Dietetics, Kinesiology or Exercise Science</li>
<li>
Experience with outpatient nutrition counseling in a fitness industry</li>
<li>
Experience in product or service sales and marketing</li>
</ul>
