<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>Job Title: Barista</b></p>
<p><b>Reports To</b></p>
<p>Barista Supervisor/operations manager</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Promotes coffee sales by educating customers, selling coffee, coffee grinding and brewing equipment, accessories and supplies; preparing and serving a variety of coffee drinks, along with pastries and cookies, accurately posting sales to our point of sale system, and maintaining a high level of energy and customer satisfaction.</p>
<p><b>Activities &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>Primary</b></p>
<p>Welcomes customers by determining their coffee interests and needs</p>
<p>Educates customers by presenting and explaining the coffee drink menu and answering any questions.</p>
<p>Sells coffees and coffee grinding and brewing equipment by explaining differences in coffee beans and coffee preparation machines; demonstrating how brewing equipment operates.</p>
<p>Prepares and sells coffee drinks by following prescribed recipes and preparation techniques for coffee drinks such as espresso, espresso lungo, caf√© latte, and cappuccino.</p>
<p>Generates revenues by attracting new customers and defining new and expanded services and products.</p>
<p>Maintains inventories by replenishing coffee bean supply, stocking coffee brewing equipment, and maintaining supplies, pastries, and cookies for the coffee bar.</p>
<p>Keeps equipment operating by following operating instructions, troubleshooting breakdowns, maintaining coffee supplies, performing preventative maintenance, and calling for repairs.</p>
<p>Maintains a safe and healthy work environment by following organization standards and sanitation regulations.</p>
<p>Improves quality results by studying, evaluating, and re-designing process, implementing changes, maintaining and improving the appearance of the store and coffee bar.</p>
<p>Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities, reading publications on coffee, retail trade, and food service, and maintaining personal networks.</p>
<p>Enhances coffee shop reputation by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and unique requests and exploring opportunities to add value to job accomplishments.</p>
<p><b>Supplemental</b></p>
<p>Bar code reader equipment</p>
<p>Cash registers</p>
<p>Credit card processing machines</p>
<p>Point-of-sale receipt printers</p>
<p>Point-of-sale terminals and workstations</p>
<p>Touch screen monitors</p>
<p>Point-of-sale software</p>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<p>Basic knowledge of coffee shop service procedures and functions</p>
<p>Basic mathematical skills</p>
<p>Able to understand and speak using the predominant language(s) of guests</p>
<p>Able to handle money and operate a point-of-sale system</p>
<p>Able to work in a standing position for long periods of time (up to 5 hours)</p>
<p>Able to safely lift and easily maneuver bags, boxes, and containers weighing up to 25 pounds</p>
