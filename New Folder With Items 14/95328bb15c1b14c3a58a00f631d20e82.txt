<p><b>Account Executive – New Horizons Learning Center </b></p>
<p>Location Anaheim, CA</p>
<p>Employment Full time</p>
<p><b>New Horizons</b> <b>Learning Centers </b>started in 1982 and grew to become one of the world’s largest IT, Applications, and Leadership skills training provider. Offering the most relevant and timely content delivered by the best instructors, we provide customers around the world with their choice of convenient class times, delivery methods and formats to accelerate their success. With thousands of courses spanning from foundational training to specialized certifications, our core IT training is focused on technology partners such as Microsoft, Cisco, Citrix, IBM, and VMware. We offer comprehensive professional development for technologies like big data, cloud, cybersecurity and networking.</p>
<p>Since 1982, New Horizons has trained over 15 million students. New Horizons has over 250 centers operating in 61 countries.</p>
<p>Every year, companies spend millions of dollars upgrading their IT hardware and software infrastructure to make their business run more efficiently. The best way to make use of those upgrades is to also upgrade the skillset of the people who install, manage and create this technology. That is where <b>New Horizons</b> <b>Learning Centers</b> come in.</p>
<p>We are looking for an Account Executive out of our Anaheim office, who is self-motivated, a team player, goal orientated, and has excellent communication skills.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES –</b></p>
<p>The Account Executive will be responsible for achieving targeted sales goals by means of account development, executed through gaining incremental revenue within existing accounts as well as penetration into new accounts. These accounts will be a combination of small, medium, and large accounts in all business sectors. Selling New Horizons training within this environment requires consistent sales activity, strong presentation skills and the ability to engage the customer utilizing a consultative sales approach, along with the ability to establish personal relationships with key decision-makers within the targeted account base.</p>
<p><b>Compensation</b>: Base Salary + Commission</p>
<p><b>Benefits: </b> medical, dental, vision, 401k option and paid time off.</p>
