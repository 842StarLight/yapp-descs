<b>Job Summary</b>
<br>
<br>
The Help Desk Technician reports to the Help Desk Manager and will be required to provide exceptional IT Help Desk support services to all Employees and Business Partners of alphabroder.
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities and Duties</b>
<br>
<br>
<ul><li>
<b>General Responsibilities</b>
<ul>
<li>Provides first and second level Help Desk support which includes receiving calls, receiving emails, instant messaging, opening and logging Help Desk tickets</li>
<li>Address and perform initial troubleshooting on issues reported to Help Desk Team and if needed reassign issues/tickets to other appropriate IT Teams or Business Units</li>
<li>Identify, diagnose, resolve or escalate end-user network or local printer problems, PC hardware problems, e-mail, Internet, VPN and local area network access problems</li>
<li>Support remote Outside Sales Team members and users working remotely</li>
<li>Assist and work with other Teams within IT Department and other Business Units to provide support of the day-to-day technology operations of the company</li>
<li>Place repair tickets and manage communication with third party vendors for support</li>
<li>Work with and manage relationships with external vendors and service providers</li>
<li>Provide one-on-one end-user problem resolution for Company approved items as described: Desktop Computers; Monitors; Laptops; Printers; Copiers; Fax Machines; Mobile Phones; Avaya Digital Phones; Avaya IP Phones (VOIP); Windows devices; MAC devices; Computer Peripherals</li>
<li>Perform Imaging of Desktops and Laptops using imaging software with updated customized company desktop image</li>
<li>Tag, deliver, set up and assist in the configuration of end-user desktop hardware, software and peripherals to include toner for copiers, printers, faxes and Distribution Center Operations technology equipment i.e. RF Scanners.</li>
<li>Send defective Desktop Hardware and other computer devices to Vendor for repairs</li>
<li>Carefully scrutinize then submit Purchase Requests for Desktop Hardware and Software needs on behalf of end users for Help Desk Manager approval.</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li><ul>
<li><b>Support users’ Telephony and Mobile Services needs</b></li>
<li><b>Monitor Solarwinds alerts, validate alerts with local users affected, troubleshoot issue and escalate to appropriate IT Teams if needed</b></li>
<li>Maintain Company Inventory of hardware and subscriptions</li>
<li>
<b>Use company Help Desk ticketing system, Track-It to manage assigned work orders</b>
</li>
<li>Use applications, software tools and utilities provided by IT Department</li>
<li>Provide support for IT projects tasks assigned by Help Desk Manager or IT Project Managers</li>
<li>Escalate Critical and urgent issues to Help Desk Manager or other management as appropriate</li>
<li>Perform and complete additional tasks assigned by Help Desk Manager or management as appropriate</li>
</ul></li></ul>
</div>
</div></div>
<div><div>Qualifications</div></div>
<div><div>
<div>
<b>REQUIREMENTS AND EXPERIENCE</b>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of help desk experience</li>
<li>3+ years’ experience with Microsoft Windows 7 pro, Windows 10 Pro, Microsoft Office Suite 2010 and 2016</li>
<li>1+ years’ experience with Microsoft Office 365 Cloud Service</li>
<li>3+ years of technical experience with building, installing, configuring, troubleshooting and supporting desktop hardware.</li>
<li>3+years’ experience with building, installing, configuring Apple OS X and Mac hardware</li>
<li>Experience using desktop imaging or ghosting software</li>
<li>Experience with IT asset inventory tracking</li>
<li>Ability to manage and maintain IT inventory.</li>
<li>Experience performing identity management and access administration</li>
<li>Smart phone experience helpful</li>
<li>Exposure to network administration</li>
<li>Working knowledge of BMC Track IT Help Desk Software or other Help Desk Management System</li>
<li>Basic experience navigating Linux/UNIX CLI</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work independently</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work well in a team environment</li>
<li>Ability to adapt and apply knowledge/skill to new technologies</li>
<li>Ability to work remotely and support end user using remote control tools</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously; must be able to prioritize work</li>
<li>Good interpersonal skills; ability to work effectively with all levels and departments.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate verbally; provide excellent customer service and work with people comfortably side-by-side of varying technical acumen; ability to handle constantly changing flow of traffic; ability to problem-solve; exercise patience and professionalism during stressful situations</li>
<li>Ability to work at night, off-hours, holidays, and weekends to support various support requirements</li>
<li>Self-starting; willingness to take personal initiative and lead others</li>
<li>Highly effective written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Need to have the ability to travel to any of alphabroder Distribution Center (DC) Locations and Offices for onsite Help Desk support or IT Project Support.</li>
<li>Travel can be schedule for multiple consecutive days.</li>
<li>Need a valid driver’s license</li>
<li><b>Participate in emergency afterhours on-call rotation</b></li>
</ul>
<b>EDUCATION</b>
<ul>
<li>AAS degree in computer science or other related technical discipline; or equivalent vocational training and experience</li>
<li>Certification in A+, Net+ and Microsoft MCP a plus</li>
</ul>
