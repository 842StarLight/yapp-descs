<h2>Project Manager - Sales Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We’re looking for a hard-working, operations experienced individual to help advise our customers in Sales &amp; Marketing Operations. We work with customers ranging from start-up to later growth stages, including these industries: Software/Saas, Professional Services, eCommerce, and others.</p>
<p>This individual will work with our Principal Consultants as well as third-party system experts (HubSpot, Salesforce, Zapier, etc.) to develop, document, and optimize processes; implement and optimize third-party software tools; establish metrics and reporting for management teams; and enable change management within our customer’s teams to ensure project success.</p>
<p>As part of our customer-focused, results-driven organization you’ll be responsible for ensuring all projects are completed within expectations and the customer’s experience meets our high standards.</p>
<p><b>Those applying need to be able to: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work directly with customers, serving as the main point of contact and ensuring a successful project</li>
<li>Collect and review project (ex. CRM) requirements with clients and work directly with team and clients to determine, design and implement the software solutions</li>
<li>Manage simultaneous software implementations for multiple clients, engaging customers in regular strategic calls to ensure project milestones are met</li>
<li>Keep well-documented requirements changes gathered from clients on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>Research and troubleshoot issues with System specialists and develop solutions for customer requirements</li>
<li>Develop training materials/guides for customers and educate customers to ensure adoption of the software</li>
<li>Review, manage, and train System Specialists who are assigned to your projects</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>4+ Years experience in either Sales or Marketing operation departments</li>
<li>Project management experience, including the requirement to organize due dates and deliverables, then execute to those timelines</li>
<li>Customer-facing experience is a plus; however all candidates should have experience managing a software implementation project</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of the sales cycle and how CRM plays a role as a sales solution</li>
<li>Preferred experience in SaaS, Software, and Professional Service industries</li>
<li>Expert experience using HubSpot and/or Salesforce</li>
<li>Experience in other third-party cloud-based tools such as SalesLoft, Pardot, Zapier, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>We’re looking for someone to start out on a project basis, but there is a strong potential for a future full-time role. Each of our customer’s needs are very different, so please only apply if you are flexible and a problem-solver! Customer-centric attitudes and focus are a must!</p>
