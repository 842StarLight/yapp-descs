<h2>Strategic Alliances Director - Technology Alliances</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Director – Technology Alliances, will be responsible for managing the overall alliance/partner strategy and driving to sales targets with a set of key technology partners including Palo Alto Networks, Check Point, Arista Networks, Juniper Networks, etc.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop a comprehensive business plan and go-to-market strategy that drives incremental revenue for both Forescout and the assigned Technology alliance partners.</li>
<li>
Develop and manage relationships with assigned technology alliances across functional areas including sales, product/service development, marketing and business development.</li>
<li>
Integrate Forescout products and solutions as part of standard, repeatable solutions and global offerings for assigned technology alliances.</li>
<li>
Evangelize Forescout products and solutions and educate the services, sales, marketing and technical field teams of assigned technology alliances.</li>
<li>
Build and execute broad and deep enablement plans and strategies driving adoption and integration of Forescout products and solutions within partner portfolio</li>
<li>
Lead and facilitate executive QBRs, joint executive alignment, and overall engagement</li>
<li>
Work with the GSIs’s leadership teams as the primary interface to Forescout</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Desired Skills and Qualities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have prior experience managing technology alliances relationships.</li>
<li>
Prefer experience in the security space</li>
<li>
Must possess a quantifiable record of success in enterprise technology sales, with a demonstrated ability to meet or exceed sales objectives and the ability to generate new business through GSI partners.</li>
<li>
Must understand complex sales process, build consensus and be adept at developing relationships at multiple levels.</li>
<li>
Must be able to rally internal cross-functional resources to enable partner success.</li>
<li>
Must be comfortable engaging with a variety of roles ranging from developers to marketing to sales to senior executives.</li>
<li>
Ability to set priorities, focus on details and motivate internal and external teams to deliver agreed-upon goals.</li>
<li>
Ability to lead teams and projects through influence rather than direction.</li>
<li>
Stand out communications skills and high energy level.</li>
<li>
Strong problem solving skills.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Experience &amp; Education:</p>
<ul>
<li>
10+ years of enterprise software sales, business development, product management and/or alliance management experience required, with demonstrated increasing levels of responsibility.</li>
<li>
Experience working both in start-up and larger company environments, and with partners of different sizes.</li>
<li>
Understanding of network, cloud &amp; IoT security software business.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Target Account Selling or other established sales methodology a plus.</li>
<li>
Technical competence strongly preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience working with security vendors including Palo Alto Networks, Check Point, Arista, Juniper Networks preferred.</li>
</ul>
