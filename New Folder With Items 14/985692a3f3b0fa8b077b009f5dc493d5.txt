<p><b>GeneralSummary</b></p>
<p>The Account Executive positionis a consultative sales position. Individuals in this position are responsiblefor PracticeManagement, Medical Billing, and Electronic Healthcare Records softwaresales within the Business to Business client demographic segment.</p>
<p><b>PrincipalDuties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops and implements sales plans for assigned sales channel, including clearly defined strategies that maximize revenue opportunities of new clients</li>
<li>Responsible for the account management and up-sell of "named accounts" as assigned</li>
<li>Understands the business needs of enterprise clients and proposes solutions to meet those complex business needs</li>
<li>Prepares and presents professional sales proposals and presentations that align with client business goals and objectives</li>
<li>Maintains all sales databases necessary to report sales activity and client information</li>
<li>Self-generates leads through industry networking, vertical trade shows and working defined account lists</li>
</ul>
<p>· Works with the Onboarding team to ensure timely implementation of sold accountsand ongoing support</p>
<p>· Works in conjunction withother business support groups, including Onboarding,Client Relations, and Marketing personnel</p>
<p>· Attends all sales meetingsand training sessions as required by management</p>
<p>· Achieves monthly and quarterly sales quotas.</p>
<p>· Maintains relationships with current clients by follow-up, andconsultation after the sale.</p>
<p>· Identifies current and future clients’ needsby establishing personal rapport with them, and other persons in aposition to understand product and service requirements.</p>
<p>· Keeps detailed and accurate notes of all client activities in CRMsystems.</p>
