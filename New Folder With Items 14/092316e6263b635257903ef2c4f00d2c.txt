<h6 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>EXECUTIVE HEALTH PHYSICAL COORDINATOR-LGH CORPORATE-FULL TIME</b><b> (JOB ID: 1847)</b>
</h6>
<p></p>
<p>
Location: LGMC<br>
Employment Status: FT<br>
</p>
<p><b>
SUMMARY:</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>The Executive Health Physicals Coordinator will carry out responsibilities and duties including but not limited to scheduling, performing clinical nursing assessments, assisting physicians with procedures, and post physical paperwork and follow-up with the patient and physician. The Coordinator maintains a close working relationship with patients, physicians and internal departments to insure the program delivers high quality, well-coordinated patient care. Additionally, this role assists in marketing the program to businesses and individuals to meet and/or exceed program budget and performance. This position routinely conducts biometric screenings and results consultations for corporate and community events.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li> The Licensed Practical Nurse works under the direction of the Corporate Health Manager &amp; Medical Director
</li>
<li> Initiates patient registration; has patient complete appropriate forms, enter demographics and insurance/billing information and appropriately scans into EMR/computer system maintaining a 95% accuracy level</li>
<li> Assists in identifying new clients and attends appointments with key decision makers through various methods including but not limited to, out-bound telephone sales, attending meetings, health fairs, trade shows, and sending program materials</li>
<li> Maintains a clear understanding of HIPAA Privacy Guidelines to ensure participant and corporate client confidentiality</li>
<li> Accurately performs current practice standards and performs basic nursing skills in a competent manner</li>
<li> Maintains current competency skills necessary to perform biometric screening, fitness testing, EKG, venous draw</li>
<li> Documents accurately and completely in the EMR and other forms related to the examination, evaluation, treatment and follow-up visits.</li>
<li> Maintains proficiency in utilizing electronic medical records through proper scanning, documentation, scheduling, and</li>
</ul>
<p>
attends educational training as necessary</p>
<ul>
<li> Manages and documents patient calls, medication requests, exam related questions, through critical decision making skills, effectively triaging coordinated care with MDs/practitioners to deliver patient care and instructions</li>
<li> Track and calculate EHP clinic specific volume &amp; stats on electronic spreadsheet to be reviewed monthly with one-up</li>
<li> Successfully uses relationship-based strategies to engage participants in care and elicit compliance</li>
<li> Ensures the appropriate release forms are completed prior to assisting participants with referrals and/or appointments</li>
<li> Responsible for providing charges, accepting payments for services rendered, and records payment in software</li>
<li> Interacts with patient accounts and ensures patients are billed properly</li>
<li> Routinely conducts biometric screening for corporate and community events including but not limited to, finger stick blood draw, blood pressure, height/weight, anthropometric measurements, and result consultations</li>
<li> Provides individualized health and fitness consultations for biometric screening participants identifying areas of concern, recommending lifestyle modification opportunities and establishing attainable short/long term goals</li>
<li> Performs high risk consultations offering recommendations and assist with scheduling appointments and post screening follow-up</li>
<li> Administers vaccinations (such as influenza) to age appropriate clients</li>
<li> In conjunction with the Business Development Specialist, serves as a marketing representative including development of marketing strategies, preparing sales packets and presenting the EHP program to local businesses and individuals</li>
<li> Responsible for scheduling initial and annual services along with any requested imaging services with the performing physician and imaging center for new and existing EHP accounts</li>
<li> Responsible for maintaining and developing client relationships and proactively assisting in their healthcare needs</li>
<li> Coordinates and facilitates activities on the day of services</li>
<li> Performs clinical duties and patient education as part of the physical, including drawing blood for lab work, obtaining vital signs and assisting the physician with the exercise stress test</li>
<li> Ensures all abnormal services are followed up appropriately</li>
<li> Works with the performing physician to create a comprehensive post visit EHP summary report to be provided to the client</li>
<li> Delivers comprehensive summary report to clients offering recommendations for additional services based on data analysis</li>
<li> Maintains office and clinical supplies at par levels</li>
<li> Maintains and sanitizes office and medical equipment according to state and federal guidelines</li>
<li> Cleans exam rooms after each patient and prepares rooms for the next patient</li>
<li> Assists with other duties as needed</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
EDUCATION/TRAINING/EXPERIENCE:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li> Maintains current licensure as Licensed Practical Nurse in the State of Louisiana</li>
<li> BLS – Healthcare Provider</li>
<li> Experience with assisting in the performance of exercise stress test</li>
<li> Minimum of three years’ experience required in health related field</li>
<li> Customer friendly – Presents well to public and can interact with patients on a one-to-one basis. Able to multi-task while maintaining professionalism and courtesy</li>
<li> Ability to accurately assess vital signs (blood pressure, pulse), perform blood work for lipid panels and various labs</li>
<li> Ability to accurately counsel patients ranging from adolescent to geriatric on both an individual and group level in relationship to health risks and lifestyle modifications</li>
<li> Proficient computer skills, including but not limited to: Microsoft Office, Internet Explorer, Outlook and biometric screening software</li>
<li> Demonstrates ability to work effectively with physicians, medical professionals, employers and brokers/third party administrators</li>
<li> Experience in care coordination services (appointments, referrals, and communication/fax, EMR) preferred</li>
</ul>
