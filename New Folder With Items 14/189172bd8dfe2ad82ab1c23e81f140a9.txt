<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Bilingual required (Spanish&amp;English)</b><br>
<br>
Account managers are responsible for advising and creating marketing strategies in order to help launch their clients' products and services effectively, while also collaborating with their clients' advertising and public relations departments so as to create successful and unified plans.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Roles and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Establishes and maintains a high level of customer satisfaction. Communicates to and works with Local Sales Manager and/or VP of Sales to resolve unique customer issues.</li>
<li>
Effectively manages prospects by developing marketing plans and maintaining key information in the prospect database.</li>
<li>
Maintains sales pipeline to achieve goals for new sales.</li>
<li>
Matches Norsan Media’s portfolio with customer needs by demonstrating knowledge of customers, pricing and competition; effectively communicates pricing and service strategies;</li>
<li>
Effectively uses Norsan Media sales productivity software tools (i.e. Sugar CRM database, Proposal Program, Pricing Tools, Norsan Cloud, etc.).</li>
<li>
Uses a variety of prospecting tools to generate business and reach potential clients</li>
<li>
Service existing accounts, obtains orders, and establishes new accounts by planning and organizing daily work schedule to call on existing or potential sales outlets and other trade factors.</li>
<li>
Responsible of explaining the terms of the sale and making any necessary arrangements, such as collecting a check, cash or credit card, getting a signature on a contract or filling out a credit application for a payment plan</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring customer satisfaction after the sale is made</li>
<li>
Will make themselves available for any further questions a customer or prospect might have regarding the product(s)</li>
<li>
Submits orders by referring to price lists and product literature.</li>
<li>
Recommends changes in products, service, and policy by evaluating results and competitive developments.</li>
<li>
Resolves customer complaints by investigating problems; developing solutions; preparing reports; making recommendations to management.</li>
<li>
Contributes to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed.</li>
<li>
Other minor duties may be assigned</li>
</ul>
