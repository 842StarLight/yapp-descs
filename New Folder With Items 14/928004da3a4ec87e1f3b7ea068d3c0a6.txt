<p><b>It's an exciting time at Metrohm USA!</b></p>
<p>We are the North American division of world-renown Metrohm AG (Herisau, Switzerland), an industry leader in laboratory instruments and accessories for analytical testing/research. We are looking for a Applications Specialist to join our team in Riverview, Florida. It's your chance to be a part of a dynamic team and make an immediate impact!</p>
<p></p>
<p>This position is office-based; however, the ideal candidate can be located within commuting distance to our Fountain Valley, CA or Houston, TX offices.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The primary responsibility of this role is to support the sales process by developing analysis methods for the characterization of customer submitted samples. This support typically begins with defining and negotiating the project scope, followed by detailed planning and execution of lab experiments with customer submitted samples, and culminates with the reporting and presenting of results at the customer site or through a remote meeting platform such as TeamViewer or Skype for Business.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Principal Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and document user-friendly solutions for the analysis of customer samples as part of pre-sale feasibility studies.</li>
<li>Work with Product Specialists to define/develop applications for competitive positioning of Metrohm USA products</li>
<li>Provide VOC feedback to Product Management teams</li>
<li>Provide applications assistance in response to customer inquiries (both internal and external), including education, training and on-site visits.</li>
<li>Write technical documentation, analytical reports, and presentations for customers and the Metrohm USA Product Team(s).</li>
<li>When necessary, provide post-sale applications support by assisting the technical support team and participating in the Escalations process.</li>
<li>Performs or assists in training sessions, both in house and in customersâ€™ laboratories. Training can include class room lecture and hands on instrument training,</li>
<li>Requires up to 20% travel including international.</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>Education and Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>Master of Science or Ph.D. Degree in Chemistry, Physics or related field with four years of technical experience in Ion Chromatography; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</p>

<p></p>
<p><b>Additional Qualifications</b>:</p>
