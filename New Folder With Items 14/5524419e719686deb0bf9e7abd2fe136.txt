<h2>Specification Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>ASSA ABLOY Door Security Solutions provides end-users (healthcare facilities, schools, universities, military, commercial buildings, etc.) with unsurpassed security and life-safety solutions and the essential support services (LEED consultation, code compliance, access control system integration, product research and selection, specification writing services, technical support, etc.) to ensure well-functioning doorway systems in the commercial building industry. This is all accomplished by combining the strengths of industry-leading door and hardware brands. Visit www.assaabloydss.com for more information.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>The Specification Consultant is under the direct supervision of a Regional Architectural Manager with the skills necessary to effectively write Division 08 door and hardware specifications</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul><li>Provide support for the team's architectural and specification writing activities and services with the objective of increasing the market awareness and penetration of related ASSA ABLOY products and innovations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for team member and customer technical and special application questions and requests for mechanical hardware.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conduct or assist with value added Educational Programs currently registered with AIA/CES and CSI/CEN to strategic architectural firms, association events, and any other approved location with multiple design team decision makers and influencers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Facilitate project coordination meetings with architectural firm, end user, security consultants, integrator, construction manager/general contractor, and distributor if appropriate.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Preparation of Door Hardware, Steel Door &amp; Frame, Wood door, Integrated Door Opening Assemblies, Security Specifications, catalog cuts, and other related opening specification sections from targeted architectural firms, contract hardware distributors, and end user customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate and assist with "Product Substitution and Approval" procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Routinely interact and communicate with local DSS agencies concerning architectural sales calls, specification bid coverage, end user specification guides and design standards, project tracking (CRM, McGraw/Hill Dodge, Team Meetings), sales growth objectives, and related marketplace strategies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Actively participate and network within local industry professional organizations including AIA, CSI, DHI, IIDA, USGBC, and any other approved associations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Meet agreed upon goals and objectives that include strategically focused markets for secured opening leads and or generated specification documents, targeted 'Solution' specification opportunities, architectural calls, and presentations.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS and SKILLS</b></div>
<ul><li>College bachelor or Technical College associate degree in an industry related program (i.e. Architectural Drafting and CAD; Architectural Engineering; Architectural Technology; Construction and Building Management; Electronics Engineering) is a requirement.</li></ul>
<ul><li>AHC/CDC, CDT, LEED GA or AP credentials preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong communication skills and a professional presentation demeanor are highly desired.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Professional experience in builders or door hardware project management and shop drawing production, architectural computer aided design and drawings, construction management, or a similar industry related field is a highly preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>A technical aptitude enhancing the ability to read, interpret and review architectural construction documents including door schedules, floor plans, elevations, submittals, shop drawings and wiring diagrams.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working knowledge of architectural construction documents including CSI MasterFormat and related Division 08 "Openings" specification sections is a plus.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Above average computer skills and working knowledge of major Microsoft "Office Suite" software programs (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint) required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Detail orientated with strong organizational skills and follow up habits.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to meet multiple project deadlines and exercise effective time management.</li></ul>
