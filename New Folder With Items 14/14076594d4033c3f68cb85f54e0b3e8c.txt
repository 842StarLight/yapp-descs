<h2>Distribution Manager - Coleman</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Title:</b> Distribution Manager - Coleman</div>
<div>
<b>Location:</b> Wichita, KS</div>
<div>
<b>Reports To:</b> Director US Distribution &amp; Transportation</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Summary:
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Distribution Supervisor is responsible for the direct functions of The Coleman Company facilities in North America. The Distribution Manager ensures that all operating facilities function according to the Coleman business plan thus fulfilling customer requirements.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Functions:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage and direct distribution and related activities within Coleman’s North America Distribution Centers by supervising and counseling direct reports across multiple shifts to ensure efficient delivery of product to customers.</li>
<li>
Determine optimum space requirements and proper storage media within the facility.</li>
<li>
Develop and implement Distribution strategies, both short-term and long-term strategies based on analyses of essential conditions such as type of product, imported vs. manufactured, consolidated warehousing vs. shipping warehousing, and customer requirements.</li>
<li>
Provide expertise to customers, Sales, Marketing, Customer Service and Manufacturing on distribution and packaging issues.</li>
<li>
Negotiate with vendors for the “best deal” on supplies and equipment such as stock locator hardware and material handling equipment.</li>
<li>
Receive returned products and process based on factors such as quantity and whether new or defective. When defective, spot trends and determine if salvageable. Educate Sales on problems with receiving returns.</li>
<li>
Review customer programs by providing costs, processes and industry practices to ensure company margins are protected and customer compliance is accomplished.</li>
<li>
Other duties may include, but not limited to:</li>
<ul>
<li>
Local Fleet operations.</li>
<li>
Outbound shipping activity.</li>
<li>
Value added services.</li>
<li>
Container receiving.</li>
<li>
Security.</li>
<li>
Information Technology.</li>
<li>
Material handling systems.</li>
<li>
Inventory control and accuracy.</li>
</ul>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<div><b>Basic Qualifications:</b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor’s degree, preferably in logistics.</li>
<li>
A minimum of 7 years of experience in distribution management.</li>
<li>
Experience with proven hands-on leadership managing a full P&amp;L in distribution roles.</li>
<li>
Experience with SAP.</li>
<li>
Excellent inventory control, product packaging, records maintenance and cost pricing methodology skills.</li>
<li>
A demonstrated ability to successfully coordinate and interact with customer service and traffic.</li>
<li>
Critical thinking ability in providing ROI and root-cause analysis.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of DOT/Hazmat regulations.</li>
<li>
A valid Driver’s license and the ability to drive a Company vehicle.</li>
<li>
A demonstrated track record of mentoring and developing direct reports and subordinates.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Distribution experience in a manufacturing/CPG company.</li>
<li>
Experience in Continuous Improvement, 6 Sigma, Lean.</li>
</ul>
