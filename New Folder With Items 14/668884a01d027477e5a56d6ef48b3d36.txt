<h2>Media Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>This position is the point person for our Automotive Account both nationally and locally. The ideal candidate will have an excellent working knowledge of Automotive marketing and be able to develop meaningful results-based relationships with our clientele.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Digital Marketing Experience </b>- it is essential to have real in-store digital experience.</p>
<p><b>
Presentation Work</b> –Building excellent presentations is a huge part of this position. PowerPoint skills are highly desired.</p>
<p><b>
Reporting</b> - Generate excellent reporting presentation materials to help recap and review the prior month for our Key Automotive Clientele.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Successful digital automotive marketing experience is a must</li>
<li>
Acquisition – our TV stations bring us great prospects that they want help in presenting to</li>
<li>
Automotive Marketing Manager will work with our media and account managers to develop excellent presentations and will lead the presentation to these prospects</li>
<li>
Pipeline Management – Utilize CRM software to log every opportunity</li>
<li>
Effective management of this process will help maximize sales and deliver more accurate forecasting</li>
<li>
Market Awareness - Feeding back market analysis into the wider team to make sure that we understand the competitive landscape, commercial models, and competitor price points</li>
<li>
Organized, driven, proactive, and self-motivated – Managing the end to end sales/reporting process is the position. Organization, time management, and attention to detail will be critical to delivering success</li>
<li>
Clearly and concisely present our Auto media solutions. Ability to develop professional written documents and client presentations especially in PowerPoint</li>
<li>
May need to provide regular sales forecasting, sales plans, and other required reporting to sales management as requested</li>
<li>
Implement a consultative sales process by gaining a deep understanding of each customer’s campaign strategy, message, and goals, and determining where we can achieve maximum impact</li>
<li>
Must be a self-starter; strong entrepreneurial orientation required</li>
<li>
Experience in building real results-based client relationships</li>
<li>
Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Should have a strong working knowledge of Auto marketing services including social and search engine marketing</li>
<li>
Strong working knowledge of Google Analytics and preferred Google Certified</li>
<li>
CRM software experience or aptitude to learn - this will be very important to the job function</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
