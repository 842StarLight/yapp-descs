<h2>Install Account Executive - Majors</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Why We Need You:</b><br>
The Procore Account Executive, Expansion will be responsible for applying an understanding of Procore's products, sales methodology, processes, prospecting techniques and customer base while working closely with the Customer Success team to ensure we maintain our client base. In this role you will focus on collaborating across Customer Success, Sales and Product to ensure our clients renew and expand.
<br><br>
This position is based in our office in Austin, TX. We're looking for someone to join our team immediately.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage a book of business of existing clients to effectively renew and expand accounts usage</li>
<li>Develop Renewal Plans with Customer Success to maximize expansion, help introduce new usage, tools, products to current client</li>
<li>Run Product Demonstrations</li>
<li>Research accounts, identify key champions, generate interest and obtain business requirements</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and up-to-date forecasts</li>
<li>Provide sales management with reports on sales activities and projects as requested</li>
<li>Pursue and increase knowledge of key competitors to ensure that our value proposition is effectively communicated to customers</li>
<li>Manage and maintain accurate leads, opportunities, and account information within Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Achieve or exceed monthly and quarterly targets</li>
<li>Obtain repeat business, referrals, and references by applying understanding of the unique requirements of your customers</li>
<li>Networking, relationship building, coordinating internal resources to solve client's issues and execution of service agreements</li>
</ul>
<b>What we're looking for:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS or equivalent experience preferred</li>
<li>2+ years of demonstrated successful software sales, preferably B2B</li>
<li>Experience using a consultative, solution based sales methodology desired</li>
<li>Proven record of success in an inside sales and or outside sales based selling model</li>
<li>Proven ability to communicate effectively via telephone and email with customers</li>
<li>Ability and resilience to work in a fast paced sales environment</li>
<li>Ability to develop trusted relationships</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office products and online collaboration tools</li>
<li>Experience with CRM and opportunity management systems, preferably Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Proven ability to develop and manage pipeline and forecasting</li>
</ul>
