<h2>Senior Deductions Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>This role is responsible for researching and reconciling customer disputes, payments, credits, invoices, and contracts to reflect the proper status and balance of the customer's account receivable and ensure payment recovery on invalid short payments/claims. Disputes are researched utilizing HighRadius (cloud-based system), QAD (ERP), Customer and third-party websites and external and internal departments. Position handles significant high volumes which requires fast decision making and a self-starter requiring minimal supervision. Analyze historical customer payments and disputes to derive issues, trends and root cause analysis using multiple systems and customer supplied information.
<p></p>
<p><b>PRINCIPAL RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor and track the daily analysis of customer disputed invoices/short-payments on a very high volume-based activity.
</li>
<li>Perform analysis of customer accounts based on the customer’s purchases, payment history, credit worthiness, credit limit exposure and our business strategy.
</li>
<li>Track and evaluate past due A/R accounts to reduce the risk of credit exposure
</li>
<li>Evaluate credit worthiness of accounts and determine proper account order hold or releases
</li>
<li>Perform offsetting adjustments to ensure customer account balances are properly stated
</li>
<li>Contact customers to secure payment on overdue balances, issues resolution on open items
</li>
<li>Provide guidance and support to Sales and Operations Managers, Logistics, Common carrier disputes, outside Distribution operations about the release of orders to accounts that have aged accounts receivable.
</li>
<li>Maintain a strong cross functional communication with Field Sales, Agents, Logistics, and Management regarding identified account risks, potential credit problems, disputes and customer comments. Identify and escalate as necessary.
</li>
<li>Provide monthly and ad-hoc reports and updates to key stakeholders
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Systems </b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and manage all data related to customer discounts, allowances and rebates in business ERP and Deductions Management ERP
</li>
<li>Track work case creation issues (Reason Codes) in High Radius in no more than 15 days from the date a case is generated, unless the issue is resolved
</li>
<li>Responsible for assisting in the implementation of IT process improvements in Deductions Management application and internal ERP process improvements.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Compliance </b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Following SOP business rules, and through case creation, generate a credit memo where applicable to resolve customer financial disputes.
</li>
<li>Ensures GAAP, SOX and Watts policies and procedures are adhered to and the ongoing operation, maintenance and controls of the AR system;
</li>
<li>Ensure proper SOX documentation on customer accounts, credit memo issuance and approvals
</li>
<li>Support the monthly audits of deductions, payments and write-offs
</li>
<li>Identify and assist in the preparation of accounts for placement with collection agencies or 3rd Party activity.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Financial Responsibilities
</p>
<ul>
<li>Financial recovery of invalid disputed customer account receivables balances
</li>
<li>Negotiate and structure payment plans
</li>
<li>Ensure customer accounts are accurately stated in adherence to US GAAP
</li>
<li>Recommend write offs for uncollectable open invoices and disputed balances
</li>
</ul>
