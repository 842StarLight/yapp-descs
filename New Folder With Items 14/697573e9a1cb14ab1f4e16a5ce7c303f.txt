<p><b>Position Description:</b></p>
<p>The purpose of this role is to lead and direct the materials function for a highly engineered product in a value stream environment.</p>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Provide long-term planning to support plant objectives for on-time delivery.</p></li>
<li><p>
Effectively manage the materials organization to meet enterprise inventory, OTD, and lead time targets.</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible for creating and managing a high performance materials team (Master Scheduler, Warehouse Supervisor/Team and Planning, Expeditor and Buyers for each Value Stream.</p></li>
<li><p>
Establish team goals and action plans to achieve department objectives in alignment with site and enterprise objectives.</p></li>
<li><p>
Direct the activities of shipping, warehousing, receiving and support value stream leaders to ensure appropriate training and development of personnel to meet operational needs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Plan and Execute through Long, Medium, and Short term planning cycles against requirement dates.</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop and maintain the facilities material forecast at item master level.</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop business processes within plant ERP and help drive master data governance.</p></li>
<li><p>
Create/implement/maintain an inventory controls program (e.g. cycle counting) and warehousing management strategy.</p></li>
<li><p>
Focus on master data attributes and master production schedule accuracy.</p></li>
<li><p>
Support Value Stream leadership to implement processes to ensure accuracy of inventory transactions throughout the operation.</p></li>
<li><p>
Work with the organization on improving the sales and operations planning (S&amp;OP) process.</p></li>
<li><p>
Must maintain strong training skills and desire to develop systems approach, good problem solving and action planning skills.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide overall management strategy and develop robust systems for plant inventory levels including WIP and finished goods to meet inventory goals and support demand requirements.</p></li>
<li><p>
Support and encourage a lean culture.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>B.S. Degree, preferably in Materials Management, Supply Chain, Engineering or Business Management</p></li>
<li><p>
Six Sigma Certified, preferred</p></li>
<li><p>
APICS Certification, preferred</p></li>
<li><p>
Experience in Supply chain, procurement, production controls or similar field, preferred</p></li>
<li><p>
Experience with demand planning and/or supply planning as it relates to S&amp;OP is a plus</p></li>
<li><p>
Experience with an ERP/MRP system required.</p></li>
<li><p>
Hands on experience with SAP is preferred</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferences:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>MBA preferred</li>
</ul>
