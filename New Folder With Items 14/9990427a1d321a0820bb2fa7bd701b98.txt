<h2>Account Executive - Pinellas County</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Overview
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>This position will cover Pinellas County.</b></div>
<div>
<b>
Summary of Essential Functions for the Position:</b><br>
</div>
<div>
The Account Executive is responsible for successfully marketing to doctors’ offices, hospitals, nursing facilities,<br>
ALF’s (Assisted Living Facilities) and various communities and organizations to achieve the Company’s<br>
financial and market share goals. Identify and educate potential customers and referral sources about healthcare<br>
services provided by the Company. Establish trusted relationships with referral sources and in-house staff.<br>
Continually prospect for new account, as well as maintain current accounts, and comply with all sales related<br>
policies and procedures.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Qualifications / Licensure / Certification / Knowledge / Skills / Abilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Two (2) to three (3) years of home care or healthcare experience preferred, with proven success in sales a</li></ul>
 plus.<br>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of health care services strongly desired.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple tasks, work effectively and independently with all levels of clinical and</li>
</ul>
 administrative staff.<br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to represent the Company effectively in a variety of settings and with diverse communities.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both oral and written with excellent interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Must be well organized and demonstrate attention to detail.</li>
<li>Ability to work within a team environment and with deadlines.</li>
<li>Must demonstrate exceptional customer relations skills and a commitment to providing quality service.</li>
<li>Acceptance and ability to demonstrate and support the core values and goals of Agency.</li>
<li>Proficient with a computer and Microsoft Word, Excel and Outlook software.</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license, automobile liability insurance and reliable transportation required.</li>
<li>100% travel may be required.</li>
</ul>
