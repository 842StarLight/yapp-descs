<h2>Lead Senior Auditor - SOX</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>PURPOSE OF ROLE:</b><br>
The Senior SOX Auditor II performs a variety of audit and assurance activities related to evaluating and supporting the Company's governance and business risk management processes, financial accounting, IT and operational internal control processes and procedures, and regulatory compliance requirements. Particular areas of focus include Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) 404 compliance (walk throughs and controls testing), external auditor assistance with flexibility and capability for other financial, operational and IT audit and compliance coverage. This role is the day-to-day leader of project and is responsible for interpreting test results and signing off on communication and deliverables for audit engagements. In addition, this role is responsible for delivering oral or written reports and communications to clients and assists in training and supervision less experiences auditors. This position will require travel 10-20%.<br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITY STATEMENTS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Plan, perform, evaluate, summarize and communicate assigned risk assessments and audit activities in a professional, competent, independent and objective manner in accordance with ICS Department standards, including: Audit operational areas and processes, including sales, distribution and manufacturing facilities.</li>
<li>Assist and support the Company's SOX 404 assessment, including assessment and testing of internal controls over financial reporting</li>
<li>Assist and support the Company's external auditor’s quarterly reviews and annual audit procedures, including process walk throughs, controls testing and substantive testing</li>
<li>Demonstrates effective project management by discussing project expectations with the team, maintaining consistent communication throughout the audit engagement and identifies development opportunities for staff auditors. Recommends performance coaching opportunities for staff auditors</li>
<li>Prepare and provide written and oral reports, communications and/or presentations to management presenting observations and recommendations for internal control improvement and facilitating management action plans</li>
<li>Manages client relationships with open communication throughout the audit process, keeping the client aware of potential concerns and the Audit Manager aware of project status or issues that need to be addressed</li>
<li>Respond to and support management requests regarding internal control assessments, process and procedure evaluations, special investigations and internal control education</li>
<li>Utilize data analysis techniques and audit software to evaluate and monitor financial and operational risks and to support department continuous auditing development objectives</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
REQUIRED EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree with experience in internal/external audit or relevant business experience</li>
<li>Minimum of 4+ years audit experience</li>
<li>CPA, CIA, CISA or related certification required</li>
<li>Public accounting and/or prior SOX 404 experience</li>
<li>Retail business experience preferred</li>
<li>Experienced with risk assessments, control design evaluation, testing procedures and documentation</li>
<li>Experienced with SOX 404 controls requirements, external auditing support for control walkthroughs and substantive audit testing</li>
<li>Flexibility and capability for financial accounting, IT and operational audit and compliance coverage, including audits of sales, distribution and manufacturing facilities</li>
<li>Ability to analyze and identify root causes of internal control issues and make appropriate recommendations</li>
<li>Good communication and interpersonal skills necessary with ability and experience in interviewing and collaborating with management and supervisors</li>
<li>Ability to manage assignments independently and in a team environment</li>
<li>Ability to prepare and facilitate presentations related to audit and assurance activities as well as internal control education</li>
<li>Experience with data analysis utilizing MS Excel or audit software preferred</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 10 - 20%</li>
</ul>
