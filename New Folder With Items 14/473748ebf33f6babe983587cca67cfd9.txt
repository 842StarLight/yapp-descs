<h2>Product Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>American Products</b> is currently seeking an <b>Product Designer</b> to join our fast-paced team! Come join us as we continue on our mission to provide superior design and quality enclosures with world-class service! We are growing rapidly – and are looking for a dedicated, hard-working individual seeking a career opportunity. We offer on-the-job training for most positions, and have generous paid time off and benefits plans.</p>
<p>
 Positive energy is a MUST. The most effective members of our team do everything they do with a high level of honesty and integrity while continuously focusing on a positive environment and the highest quality products possible.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
The right candidate for this position will be able to successfully:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design equipment using Autodesk Inventor, Autodesk Vault, and AutoCAD.</li>
<li>
Update production designs to meet customer needs and internal standards.</li>
<li>
Develop Bills of Material (BOMs), customer drawings, manufacturing drawings, and other related engineering documents.</li>
<li>
Must be able to solve problems through collaboration and creativity.</li>
<li>
Engage with and develop positive relationships with the Sales and Production teams and our Customers.</li>
<li>
May perform other duties as assigned to help the rest of the American Products team.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p><b>
Interested? Here’s what we would like to see:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate’s degree in Computer-aided Drafting and Design (CAD) or a Mechanical Engineering degree.</li>
<li>
Solid proficiency in CAD.</li>
<li>
At least 1 year of experience with the use of ERP software (i.e. Syteline).</li>
<li>
Minimum of 2 years’ experience in industrial mechanical design with an emphasis in sheet metal design. Equivalent combination of related education and experience may be acceptable.</li>
<li>
Must be a self-starter, able to take a vision and create a solution.</li>
<li>
Must be detail-oriented, with the ability to successfully follow through on assignments.</li>
<li>
Understanding of safety standards is required; including the use of personal protective equipment (PPE).</li>
<li>
Must be trustworthy, dependable and able to work well with other team members.</li>
</ul>
If you meet the above qualifications, we would love to speak with you!<br>
<br>
American Products is an Equal Opportunity Employer.</div></div>
