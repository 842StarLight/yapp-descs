<p><b>
WHAT DOES A DATABASE DESIGNER DO?</b></p>
<p>
LEAP combines world-leading technology with the provision of quality content, which enables our clients to produce legal documentation both quickly and accurately.</p>
<p>
A Legal Database Designer drives our business to the next level. You will help differentiate our company from all others. In this role, you will be working with legal researchers to design our database, user interface, calculators, and automated forms, working in a LEAP proprietary database design platform. You will join a dedicated team who are responsible for a comprehensive library of over 4,000 forms.
</p>
<p>This is the perfect position for someone with a database design background who is looking to take their career in a new direction and work with the latest technology.</p>
