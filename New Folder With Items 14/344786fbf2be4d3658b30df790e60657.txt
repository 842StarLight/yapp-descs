<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<div><div><div>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>We're looking for an Account Manager to join Team Snapchat! Within our Advertising Solutions organization, the Emerging sales team focuses on challenger brands, disruptive business models and high-growth, high opportunity performance advertisers, ranging from some of Snap’s largest customers to early-stage startups. Working from our New York, NY office, you will partner with an Account Executive to deepen relationships and increase revenue across a core group of clients within a specific client category or advertising vertical.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
What you’ll do:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Build, manage, and grow relationships and advertising spend with key clients and advertising partners by helping them achieve their performance goals</div></li>
<li><div>
Partner with Account Executives, Creative Strategists and cross functional partners to develop innovative, insight-driven digital campaigns on Snapchat, bringing measurable ROI for your clients</div></li>
<li><div>
Become a master of Snap’s world class internal advertising tools, diving deep into campaign performance data; guiding KPI-driven measurement strategies, identifying performance trends, optimizing campaigns to achieve results, and providing recommendations for upsell opportunities</div></li>
<li><div>
Work on first to market creative executions, combining Snap’s array of advertising products across augmented reality, direct response and brand to achieve the performance objectives of your clients</div></li>
<li><div>
Serve as the Snap product expert by educating clients and ad partners on Snap’s advertising product suite and influencing Snap’s ad product roadmap by owning your client’s participation in Alphas and Betas</div></li>
<li><div>
Present campaign analysis and resolve client objections in face to face client meetings as well as bi-weekly conference calls</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage projects involving complex work streams and cross-functional collaboration (internally and externally)</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
Minimum qualifications:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Bachelor’s degree in business, communications, marketing, or another related area of study</div></li>
<li><div>
3+ years experience in the digital media industry, with a focus on performance and brand marketing, ad-tech, programmatic in real-time bidding marketplaces, or a related field</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
Preferred qualifications:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Understanding of Direct Response performance advertising metrics and Snap’s competitive platforms.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong Excel and PowerPoint skills, as well as experience with analyzing datasets and delivering actionable insights</div></li>
<li><div>
Understanding of media management within a biddable auction advertising environment and demonstrated skill in educating others (e.g., advertisers, agencies)</div></li>
<li><div>
Proficient in CRM and data analysis tools such as Salesforce and Looker</div></li>
<li><div>
Pre-existing relationships with client decision-makers</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams and all levels of management (internally and externally)</div></li>
<li><div>
Proven track record of delivering on quota and obtaining positive reviews</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to manage multiple projects with strong attention to detail</div></li>
<li><div>
Capable of working in a fast paced environment and adapt to changes</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong presentation and communication skills</div></li>
<li><div>
A passion for Snapchat as a user and knowledge of our ad products</div></li>
</ul>
