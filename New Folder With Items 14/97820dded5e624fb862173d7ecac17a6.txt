<b>
You are:</b><br>
A Forward thinking Contributor who can establish data driven residual values. You are a Loyal individual who has knowledge of the residual setting process and the risk associated to the business<br>
<br>
<b>You will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Set Residuals, conduct portfolio assessment on market and residual trends and conduct regular analysis on concentration of collateral in the portfolio.</li>
<li>
Conduct Loss given defaults on all collaterals</li>
<li>
Report all Asset Management's metrics monthly</li>
<li>
Collect data from multiple sources in an effort to build a data base of historic values in various collaterals</li>
<li>
Partner with our risk management &amp; legal team on supporting execution, deal workout, recovery and liquidationsPartner with Secondary Remarketers to remarket returned and repossessed equipment</li>
<li>
Research various equipment types to project present and future equipment values to provide residual values to the sales team and/or credit team in a timely and thorough manner</li>
<li>
Ability to develop and implement policy and procedures within Asset Management</li>
<li>
Understand components of equipment leasing to ensure prudent equipment covenants are incorporated into lease/loan documentation to protect equipment value and mitigate risk</li>
<li>
Monitor the various equipment markets for technology changes, market influences and regulatory changes that affect value of leased equipment and residual pricing</li>
<li>
Perform and coordinate portfolio and administration activities as assigned and required by policy</li>
<li>
Schedule equipment inspections as needed and arrange outside third party inspections and appraisals as requested</li>
<li>
Perform other job-related duties as may be required and assigned</li>
</ul>
