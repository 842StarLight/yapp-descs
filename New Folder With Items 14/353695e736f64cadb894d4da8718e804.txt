<h2>Associate Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>General Overview:</b></p>
<p>
Develops the merchandise assortments that drive sales by focusing on product selection, product development, department trends and international sourcing. Collaborates closely with Senior Buyer, Planning and Distribution, Visual Merchandising, Store Operations and the Distribution Centers. Responsible for the professional development of an Assistant Buyer.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Principal Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and acquire merchandise through foreign and domestic travel</li>
<li>
Achieve or exceed department sales, margin and turnover objectives</li>
<li>
Strategic planning following the retail merchandising calendar</li>
<li>
Manage vendor and agents to ensure the best possible pricing</li>
<li>
Identify trends and maximize growth opportunities</li>
<li>
Determine advertising proposals and projections for multi media and in store promotions</li>
<li>
Partner with Visual Merchandising on schematic guidelines</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the Planning and Distribution team on financial projections, including sales and markdowns</li>
<li>
Maintain communication with the stores</li>
<li>
Manage the professional development of an Assistant Buyer</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 3 -5 years in a retail merchandising position with a multi unit retailer</li>
<li>
Bachelors degree required</li>
<li>
Excellent planning and negotiation skills</li>
<li>
Ability to identify, develop and manage existing business and spot future trends</li>
<li>
Creative problem solving and risk taking skills</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical skills; trend forecasting and item management</li>
<li>
Experience with Merchandising software systems; AS400, Brio</li>
<li>
Strong PC skills including Microsoft Excel and Windows</li>
<li>
Strong leadership and team collaboration skills</li>
<li>
Proven ability to develop subordinates</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Lifting and moving boxes of samples – 1-3 hours per week</li>
<li>
Operation of a PC – 25-30 hours per week</li>
<li>
Branching and working in the stores (stocking the sales floor)</li>
</ul>
