Job Description:</b></p>
<p>Alford Home Solutions, a regional leader in the Pest Control and Home Improvement Industry, is seeking to hire a driven, hardworking Universal Pest Technician to join its team.</p>
<p>
Alford provides its employees with top quality tools, products, and resources. Further, employees are trained and educated on our state-of-the-art Pest Technology. We offer access to a company vehicle with low windshield time, industry top pay, and a dedicated route of customers</p>
<p><b>
Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul><li>Responsible for all Termite and Pest Control sales and service for the customer</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contacts potential leads in a timely manner</li></ul>
<ul><li>Schedules leads effectively and efficiently</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides a thorough inspection and analysis of the property as it relates to pest problems</li></ul>
<ul><li>Formulates a solution for the customer to address all identified Pest problems</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicates treatment options and pricing to the customer</li></ul>
<ul><li>Executes customer contract and subsequent treatment plan</li></ul>
<ul><li>Updates customer file in pest software on a regular basis</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide customers with an exceptional experience</li></ul>
<p><b>
Skills and Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul><li>Active state issued driver’s license</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lifting, standing, kneeling, crawling</li></ul>
<ul><li>General math skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication skills, verbal and written</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understanding of Pest Control Sales</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to operate a computer, tablet, and smart phone</li></ul>
<ul><li>Desire to learn and excel in the Pest Control Industry</li></ul>
