<h2>Business Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">SUMMARY
<br><br>
Responsible for the financial functions of the facility.
<br><br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
Recruits, hires, provides orientation/training, processes new hire paperwork, assists with benefits administration, processes payroll, processes accounts payable, responsible for private billing and collections, manages resident trust, performs facility banking and cash handling duties, manages qualified business office staff and ensures that employee performance meets or exceeds expectations. Maintains employee files according to state legal requirements.
<br><br>
Works with the admissions department to assess patient eligibility for Medicare, Medicare, and Insurance. Processed Medicaid applications and councils patients and their families with regard to the coordination of benefits and meeting eligibility requirements. Works with the MDS and Therapy departments to ensure billing accuracy. Ensures proper accounting procedures and controls are in place and followed. Ensure that all state mandated records are maintained according to law. Maintains resident files according to state legal requirements.
<br><br>
ENTRY QUALIFICATIONS
<br><br>
Mininum of three (3) years experience as a business office manager with supervisory responsibility.
<br><br>
Proficient in the use of a personal computer and job related software packages.
<br><br>
Point Click Care and RFMS experience is a plus.
<br><br>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
<br><br>
Solid customer service skills.
<br><br>
WHAT WE OFFER
<br><br>
Compensation package commensurate with experience and proven results.
<br><br>
Medical, dental, vision,and life insurance benefits.
<br><br>
Paid vacation days, sick days, and holidays.</div>
