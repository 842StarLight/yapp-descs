<h2>Advertising Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>DFWChild sales team is looking for an Advertising Coordinator. This position is responsible for collecting ad materials; both in print and digital, trafficking and reporting among other operational responsibilities. The Coordinator will assist with issue close, page creation and final page approval prior to press. This position requires extreme attention to detail, heavy customer contact, the ability to multi-task and meet deadlines. The Candidate for this position must be organized, diligent, outgoing and can effectively communicate with the sales team and an extensive client list.</p>
<p><b>Key Functions</b></p>
<p>Assist sales team in all aspects of client services</p>
<p>Assist in the preparation of proposals and media plans as directed</p>
<p>Cultivate positive business relationships with clients and sales, ad operations.</p>
<p>Asset collection of print and digital materials</p>
<p>Understanding of print and digital specs to effectively communicate changes to clients</p>
<p>Management of Digital Campaigns</p>
<p>Provide operational support to Production department as well as the Sales team</p>
<p>Assist with distribution</p>
<p>General administration tasks</p>
<p><b>Qualifications and Experience</b></p>
<p>Bachelors Degree</p>
<p>Strong computer skills</p>
<p>Strong attention to detail</p>
<p>Motivated, positive attitude</p>
<p>Strong written and verbal communication skills</p>
<p>Ability to work both independently and in a team-oriented environment</p>
<p>Excellent time management skills and the ability to work efficiently under tight deadlines</p>
<p>Previous sales and publishing experience is helpful, but not required</p>
<p>Punctual and flexibility</p>
<p>Great job for a recent college graduate looking to enter sales/marketing field</p>
