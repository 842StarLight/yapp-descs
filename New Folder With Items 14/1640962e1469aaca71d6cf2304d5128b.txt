<p><b>Job Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Translate business, market, and customer needs (collaborating with the Business Leads) into product strategy and requirements (e.g., functional and technical design documents).
</li>
<li>Works on problems of moderate scope where analysis of situations or data requires a review of a variety of factors.
</li>
<li>Analyzes market data to identify trends/opportunities, develops strategic direction from market information, and creates compelling market analysis presentations.
</li>
<li>Assist Business Leads with overall solution business cases.
</li>
<li>Create internal training, marketing content (as needed), assist with implementation methodology.
</li>
<li>Oversees the development of strategic market/product line business plans and marketing programs to support sales in designated market segments
</li>
<li>Work closely with sales and account management to educate potential customers on product offering and aid with customer on-boarding
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in discussing solutions with potential customers and gathering feedback for product development.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate technical proficiency and effectiveness working with Software Engineers, Architects and QA.
</li>
<li>Develop and use design research methodologies and insights to drive decision making
</li>
<li>Interpret research results, derive implications, perform analysis and gather strategic intelligence to provide insight regarding the market, trends and our customer needs, pain points and behaviors
</li>
<li>Work with product managers, designers, developers and partners to ensure product and service design is informed by user insights
</li>
<li>Contribute to the wider development of user insights repository in the synthesis and creation of Customer Success Criteria, user workflows and end-to-end journey maps
</li>
<li>Ensure overall quality of project solutions throughout the research and design process.
</li>
<li>Acts independently to determine methods and procedures on new assignments.
</li>
<li>Often acts as a facilitator and team leader.
</li>
<li>Must be able to travel up to 40%.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Additional Knowledge &amp; Skills:
</b></p>

<li>Strong communication skills including written and verbal are necessary.
</li>
<li>Strong problem solving and analytical skills required.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of healthcare revenue cycle is strongly desired
</li>
<li>Comfortable with taking initiative and driving toward a positive outcome for stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Masterful at uncovering insights, and able to inspire others in acting upon them
</li>
</ul>
