<h2>Energy, Resources, & Industrials (ER&I) - Industrial Products & Construction (IP&C) SAP – Senior Manager (FICO / Supply Chain / PP & PM)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Energy, Resources, &amp; Industrials (ER&amp;I) - </b><b>Industrial Products &amp; Construction (IP&amp;C) SAP – Senior Manager (FICO / Supply Chain / PP &amp; PM)</b></p>
<p>Are you an SAP professional experienced with clients in Industrial Products or Engineering &amp; Construction? Our practice is undergoing heavy growth and high demand for SAP talent, while giving our talent the chance to work with world-class organizations solving key business issues in a team environment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work you’ll do</b></p>
<ul><li> Manage Industrial Products &amp; Construction SAP projects, including oversight of team members, budgets, and project timelines</li></ul>
<ul><li> Interface with Deloitte clients regarding project expectations and future business opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead and support SAP consulting sales pursuits and IP&amp;C practice development initiatives</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop your knowledge of and gain project experience in SAP’s new S4 HANA and future solutions</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborate on innovative solutions to support the future of SAP and IP&amp;C</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Qualifications</b></p>
<ul><li> Functional/technical proficiency with at least 4 + full life-cycle SAP implementations, including SAP implementation project management experience with Industrial Products and/or Engineering &amp; Construction companies</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>Must have at least 8 years </b><b>industry experience in the Industrial Products &amp; Construction</b> area, such as Manufacturing, Aerospace, and/or Engineering firms and experience implementing SAP. Applications include SAP S/4HANA and Cloud (Hybris, Ariba, IBP, SuccessFactors, and SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must have the following module experience: FICO or Supply Chain or PP &amp; PM</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience defining systems strategy, developing systems requirements, designing and prototyping, testing, training, defining support procedures and implementing practical business solutions under multiple deadlines</li></ul>
<ul><li> Adept at designing and implementing technology-enabled business solutions for clients as part of a high-talent team and as a team lead or Project Manager on at least 4 full cycle SAP implementations</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong current hands-on configuration and design skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Able to collaborate with clients, identify engagement follow-on opportunities and have a strong desire to excel and be committed to gaining exposure to multiple industries while further developing your career</li></ul>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s degree or equivalent</li></ul>
<ul>
<li> No Sponsorship Restrictions Ability to travel 80-100% is REQUIRED</li>
<li> No Sponsorship Restrictions</li>
</ul>

<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Master’s degree preferred</li></ul>

You will be surrounded by equally trained and enthusiastic SAP practitioners, mentoring and coaching team members, and perfecting your project leadership and PMO skills across the following capabilities:</p>
