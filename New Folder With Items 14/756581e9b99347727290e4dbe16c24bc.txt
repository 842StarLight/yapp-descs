<h2>2020 Logistics Analyst- Arctic Cat</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. is a leading global manufacturer of golf cars, utility and personal transportation vehicles, snowmobiles, side-by-sides, all-terrain vehicles, professional turf-care equipment, and ground support equipment. Textron Specialized Vehicles markets products under several different brands. Its vehicles are found in environments ranging from golf courses to factories, airports to planned communities, and theme parks to hunting preserves.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
 Responsibilities:</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Efficiently plan and execute truck load, LTL, and intermodal shipments of vehicles to customers, hub locations, branches, distributors, and dealers in support of Textron Specialized Vehicles logistics, financial and customer service goals.</li>
<li>Use Transportation Management System (TMS) and Warehouse Management System (WMS) to maximize efficient use of outbound conveyances.</li>
<li>Communicate planning, shipping, and delivery information to internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Optimize the use of all available resources by ensuring trailers and containers are cubed efficiently and by coordinating available backhauls of used vehicles.</li>
<li>Manage overall carrier relationships and secure appropriate number of carriers to support shipment volume.</li>
<li>Ensure on-time and complete deliveries and communicate activity to internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Route outbound shipments to maximize freight revenue and cost savings.</li>
<li>Coordinate schedules, crating requirements, and shipment preparation with Logistics warehouse and shipping personnel.</li>
<li>Provide daily updates in sufficient detail to all internal stakeholders as required.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
