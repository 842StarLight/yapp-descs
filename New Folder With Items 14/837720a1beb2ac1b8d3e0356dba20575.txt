The Role
<br><br>
We are seeking a Commercial Real Estate Broker to help lead our sales efforts in the Los Angeles market and collaboratively across our 10 markets. In this role, you will work closely with the Chief Sales Offices and will be part of our growing Sales and Business Development division. The successful candidate will have a proven track record in creating opportunities and closing deals and will be responsible for prospecting, outreaching and developing new leads within their assigned markets. In addition, the entrepreneurial candidate will work closely with our greater Sales, Real Estate (Transactions) and Operations teams to ensure client needs are being met.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will have 3-5 years of sales experience, and is a self-motivated, collaborative team-player.
<br><br>
As a Commercial Real Estate Broker (Account Executive), you will:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Achieve and exceed monthly, quarterly and annual quotas</li>
<li>Prioritize and manage your sales pipeline in Salesforce</li>
<li>Cultivate and maintain business relationships with key decision makers</li>
<li>Utilize innovative sales tactics/methodologies to perform at a high level</li>
<li>Accompany and lead space tours with qualified prospects to close deals</li>
<li>Track phone calls, emails and overall activity using Salesforce</li>
<li>Provide monthly forecasts on the sales pipeline</li>
</ul>
Helpful Skills &amp; Experience You'll Need to Succeed
<br><br>

<li>Excellent communication, interpersonal, and presentation skills</li>

<li>Experience with Salesforce a plus</li>
<li>Commercial real estate sales experience a strong plus</li>

</ul>
