<h2>Technical Marketing Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The <b>Technical Marketing Engineer</b> is a business development position looking for a Sales Engineer with a technical background and sales experience. This is a permanent position with a growing company in the electronic power systems industry. Work with clients to arrange sale and deliver of medium to large scale electronic power supplies and systems. This is a permanent hire position with an excellent base salary, bonus and benefits package.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p><br>
<i>Design – in / Design Win:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement in cooperation with the Business development a common strategy for Design in / Design WIN for maximizing: Sales, Market share and Margins.</li>
<li>Suggest and propose the most appropriate technical solutions for the customers’ needs concurring to the identification of key projects and setting priorities for them.</li>
<li>Work closely with Business Development Managers and Strategic Account</li>
<li>Managers in sales efforts</li>
<li>Execute the Sales strategies, build and maintain the highest level of customer engagement with the main objective of meeting or exceeding profitable sales growth targets in alignment with Business Development Team and Marketing Management.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Current Portfolio /New Products / Roadmap:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Collect all the needs and demands for new standard products projects identification</li>
<li>Contribute to the definition of development strategy for standard products</li>
<li>Define new product specifications, price positioning and expected margins</li>
<li>Responsible for the life cycle and for the margin of the assigned products, managing phase-in and phase out logic and ensuring follow-up and fixed objectives for standard products (i.e.: time-to-market)</li>
<li>Responsible for New Product Initiatives, KPI definition, monitoring and achievement</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Internal / External Interface:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Meet the sales force to present the product families and associated roadmaps.</li>
<li>Support Sales on a call Base for strategic customers initiatives</li>
<li>Ensure the release to market and the technical promotion of new products, creating and delivering training to sales, customers and distributors,</li>
<li>Ensures customer issues are directed to appropriate groups for effective resolution</li>
<li>Responsible for the developing of the marketing pack (press release, articles and product presentations) to support the release to market promotional activities and campaigns</li>
<li>Engage Application Engineering/Technical Support as required to influence specifications and develop solutions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor or Master’s degree in Electronic Engineering with a strong technical background</li>
<li>5 years of successful experience as Sales Engineer developed preferably in Power Supplies/Power Conversion or semiconductor industries</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience in AC-DC Power Supply and Electronic Power Conversion (100W up to 3KW sweet spot).</li>
<li>Knowledge in programmable and dimmable Constant Current &amp; Constant Voltage LED Drivers/LED Power Sources (15W-250W sweet spot)</li>
<li>Knowledge in Software and Firmware; digital protocols (ex. I2C, DMX, DALI), digital communication between Power Supply and LED Drivers and the application).</li>
<li>Effective in communication with presentation skills</li>
<li>Strong decision making and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Be results driven and customer focused</li>
<li>Highly organized, detail-oriented</li>
<li>Fluent in oral and written English language</li>
<li>Strong skills and extensive experience in performing technical analysis in laboratory: compatibility, trouble shooting, failure analysis.</li>
<li>Availability and willingness to travel supporting Customer &amp; Distributor visits</li>
<li>Willingness to actively attend to trade shows and specialized Conferences.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
