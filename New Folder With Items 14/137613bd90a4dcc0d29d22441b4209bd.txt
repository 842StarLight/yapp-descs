<p>Ready to join a fast-paced work environment that demands an individual with savvy organizational skills and a passion for advertising? As Virtual Sales and Service, you'll share in the responsibility of performing duties central to how our expert advertising department conducts business each day (such as managing extensive advertising campaigns and processing critical ad materials). You'll also play a key role in keeping our high-profile clients satisfied with expert customer service (including identifying potential sales opportunities and educating clients on media products and services). It's a position in which you'll work side by side with, sales representatives and Management to strengthen and enhance our reputation as a powerful resource for local and national advertisers.</p>
<p>
Altice is looking for someone to Support the Political and Public Affairs sales teams and our various platforms including: onboarding &amp; launch of private deals, working with both internal and external participants Ensure all campaigns are properly booked in our systems; test creative assets &amp; run quality assurance checks Provide deal health analysis, delivery management, and troubleshooting with buyers Weekly deal stewardship and account tracking for revenue and delivery Campaign Analysis</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<p>
Perform duties including entering ad orders, managing ad campaigns, processing materials, setting up accounts and responding to billing inquiries</p>
<ul>
<li>
Educate customers on media products and services, identify potential sales opportunities and refer leads</li>
<li>
Problem solve ad production issues</li>
<li>
Create, maintain and distribute sales and service productivity reports</li>
<li>
Maintain constant communication with sales reps and managers on account statuses</li>
<li>
Assess team operations and recommend opportunities for process improvement</li>
<li>
Assist in orienting new team members</li>
</ul>
