<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The primary job responsibility of the Retail Category Specialist, Bakery is to act as the intermediary between the customer, vendors and the fresh sales team. It is the responsibility of the RCS to make sure the customers’ needs are met by the company and in doing so increase sales on a consistent basis. To do this communication skills must be very strong to both our external and internal customer. The external customer is the business owner, manager, buyer, vendor or category manager. The internal customer is everyone at Kehe, Purchasing, Operations, Transportation and Finance.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Functions
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Growth in identified Strategic Accounts is expected, either through better account penetration, new programs etc.</li>
<li>
All KeHE Fresh customers identified as Strategic and new customers identified as Strategic fall into your responsibility.</li>
<li>
The RCS must insure all promotional allowances are in ours and the customer's system accurately and with correct start and end dates.</li>
<li>
The RCS will actively work to insure promotional activity is always happening in your accounts by working directly with Manufacturers and Brokers on your customer's behalf.</li>
<li>
The RCS will be responsible for inventory committed to by his customer and will work on overstock product until it is brought in line or has a resolution.</li>
<li>
The RCS will work with the Broker and Vendor community to grow our mutual business by presenting new items to the customer. These can be new items to Monterrey or existing items that Monterrey already carries for other customers.</li>
<li>
The RCS will present new items to their customers that are brought in to Monterrey for other accounts. If it is in our warehouse we want to sell it to everyone! The exception would be private label or exclusive items to a customer in a region.</li>
<li>
The RCS will return all phone calls and emails within a reasonable amount of time, no more than 24 hours is the expectation.</li>
<li>
The RCS will be financially conservative and a good steward of company funds and resources.</li>
<li>
A target list of new accounts you will be working on will be submitted to the Director of Marketing by the 19th and updated by the second Monday of the Month, each month with results of your efforts.</li>
<li>
Daily call sheets will need to be entered prior to 10 am the following work day.</li>
<li>
An itinerary will be provided to the Sr. Director of Strategic Accounts weekly by Friday 5pm for the following week.</li>
<li>
Travel for this position is required. Send in travel forms and requests and within budget.</li>
<li>
All presentations will require a recap by the following work day at 10 am. Travel percentage is 50%</li>
<li>
Supporting our customers, marketing and sales team on case by case basis.</li>
<li>
Quarterly review of category sales will be provided. Details on each account will need to be reviewed and returned to the Sr. Director Strategic accounts within 7 days.</li>
</ul>
