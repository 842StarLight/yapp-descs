<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Position Summary:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
This position will perform daily customer service duties for the Avitum Division. Handle direct customer phone calls and emails, processing orders through SAP order entry system, customer correspondence; purchase order confirmations, credits, debits, material returns, invoices, etc.), and providing miscellaneous follow-up information to customers. The Customer Service Representative will ensure that all the needs of our customers are met, including contract pricing, equipment service agreements and assist in meeting division goals and objectives.</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities: Essential Duties
</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Answer and address Avitum Customer Service phone calls and emails.</div></li>
<li><div>
Process orders through SAP order entry system including verbal, fax and emails. Issue order confirmations and, when requested, copies of product certifications.</div></li>
<li><div>
Interface with production scheduling, purchasing, document control, quality assurance, and logistics regarding issues relating to completion and delivery of customers orders.</div></li>
<li><div>
When needed, follow-up with correspondence to customers to satisfy their expectation for a high level of customer service.
</div></li>
<li><div>Enter Avitum Capital Equipment customer purchase orders and equipment Service Agreements in SAP order entry system and follow through with all details related to the processing of capital equipment and service agreements.</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsible for customer contract pricing entry into SAP, distribution of contract to appropriate sales field personnel and follow up for signed agreement and expiration date.</div></li>
<li><div>
Monitor customer problems with billing, product availability, shipment tracking and troubleshoot as needed.</div></li>
<li><div>
Support Avitum field personnel; including Sales Representatives, Customer Engineers, and Clinical Specialists.
</div></li>
<li><div>Process and initiate RGA’s, PIR’s, debits and credits for authorization and approval by Customer Service Supervisor.</div></li>
<li><div>
Backup for service max field service billing.</div></li>
<li>
Support various Avitum sales and marketing customer initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Expertise: Knowledge &amp; Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Knowledge of daily customer service duties and responsibilities and SAP system.</li>
<li>
Basic product knowledge necessary.</li>
<li>
Understanding of the Global structure of the network of B. Braun companies.</li>
<li>
Computer knowledge of various software programs.</li>
<li>
Computer skills in Word, Excel, and communications software.</li>
<li>
Must possess excellent interpersonal, organizational and communication skills.</li>
<li>
Must possess mathematical skills to calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, commissions, percentages, etc.</li>
<li>
Multi-lingual language skills a plus.</li>
<li>
Must be able to communicate effectively (both oral and written) with others both internally and externally with multi-cultural customer base.</li>
<li>
Must be organized with efficient time management skills and be able to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram or schedule form.</li>
<li>
Must be able to reason and solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists.</li>
</ul>
