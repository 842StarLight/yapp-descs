Control Management (CM) maintains a strong and consistent control environment across the firm. With Control Managers appointed for each Line of Business, Function and Region, there is a comprehensive coverage and joint accountability model with the business executive that promotes early operational risk identification and assessment, effective design and evaluation of controls and sustainable solutions to mitigate operational risk.</div>

</div>
<div></div>
<div>3) Control breaks and resolution</div>
<div></div>

<p>
4) Control governance &amp; reporting<br>

<div></div>
<div>This Control Data Manager will be responsible for partnering with the Control Managers and the Business partners in developing MIS and automation to continuously monitor the control environment within the Chase Auto and Business Banking lines of business and CCB HR function. The role will include developing and executing the strategy to incorporate data analytic sources and tools into control evaluation for real-time assessment of the operational health of the business. This individual will work with internal functional areas as well business partners to develop robust metrics-based approach to ensure sustainable risk mitigation.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Analyze Chase Auto and Business Banking risk and control universe for trigger-based and automated monitoring opportunities
</div></li>
<li><div>Leverage basic and complex coding techniques coupled with other technical tools to develop measureable and sustainable triggers which appropriately monitor risk
</div></li>
<li><div>Manage data integration into control evaluation to migrate from traditional sampling methodology to automated approach for comprehensive monitoring</div></li>
</ul>
