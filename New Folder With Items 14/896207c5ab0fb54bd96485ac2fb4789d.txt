<p>Spectrum Retail Sales Associate (Part-Time)<br>Posting Job Description : <b>Spectrum</b> is America’s fastest-growing TV, internet and voice provider. Our organization is one filled with a diverse group of hardworking people. They’re committed to helping us grow, and we’re committed to growing with them. <ul>

<li>Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental and vision</li>
<li>401(k) with company match</li>
<li>Paid training</li>
<li>Career advancement opportunities</li>

<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>

</ul>

<li>Telecommunication experience</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>

<li>Read, write and speak the English language to effectively communicate with employees, customers, and suppliers in person, on the phone and by written communications</li>
<li>Lifting up to 35 lbs.</li>
<li>Standing for prolonged periods of time</li>
<li>Wearing a required uniform</li>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent</li>
</ul>
