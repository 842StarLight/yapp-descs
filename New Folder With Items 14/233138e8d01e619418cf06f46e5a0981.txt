Anchor, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. <div><b>
Job Description Summary</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Check vehicles to ensure that mechanical, safety and emergency equipment is in good working order</li>
<li>
Maneuver trucks into loading or unloading positions, following signals from loading crew and checking that vehicle and loading equipment, are properly positioned</li>
<li>
Operate forklift to load/unload and move material to designated areas</li>
<li>
Collect delivery instructions from appropriate sources, verifying instructions and routes</li>
<li>
Maintain logs of working hours using On Board Computer (OBC) system</li>
<li>
Maintain driver vehicle inspection report (DVIR) for vehicle service or repair status, following applicable state and federal regulations</li>
<li>
Report vehicle defects, accidents, traffic violations, or damage to the vehicles</li>
<li>
Secure cargo for transport, using ropes, blocks, chain, binders, or covers</li>
<li>
Drive trucks to weigh stations before and after loading and along routes to document weights and to comply with state regulations</li>
<li>
Drive trucks with capacities greater than 3 tons, including tractor-trailer combinations, to transport and deliver products or other materials</li>
<li>
Obtain receipts or signatures for delivered goods for services when required</li>
<li>
Inventory and inspect goods to be moved to determine quantities and conditions</li>
<li>
Inspect truck equipment and supplies such as tires, lights, brakes, fuel, oil, and water to ensure items are in proper regulation and/or working order<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements/Skills</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
At least 3 years of driving experience - flatbed is a plus</li>
<li>
Must have completed a licensed truck driver training program</li>
<li>
Must possess a valid Class-A CDL license</li>
<li>
Must be in compliance with all DOT requirements</li>
<li>
Clean MVR over the last 3 years with no tickets or accidents</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Work Environment</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Perform general physical activities requiring considerable use of your arms, legs and moving your whole body, such as climbing, lifting, balancing, walking, stooping, and handling of materials</li>
<li>
Other physical demands include sitting, walking, standing, bending, stretching, using hands and fingers to hold, grasp, push, and lift items</li>
<li>
A portion of work time is spent in a tractor trailer truck which requires extended amounts of sitting and operating a vehicle according to DOT guidelines</li>
<li>
Position will also require working in warehouse and production areas where temperatures can range from cool to hot and noise level can be moderate to high</li>
<li>
Lifting requirements are generally up to 80 pounds with assistance if needed</li>
</ul>
