<div></div>
<div><b>Principal Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>Provides ongoing sales and service to long-term clients</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Articulates and influences clients regarding Communicorp’s marketing solutions value proposition and translates the influence into revenue increases</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Develops strategies to penetrate accounts with the key decision makers</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Grows revenue, both organic and new, at a high rate through development of strategic plans that meet or exceed clients’ requirements</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Manages sales cycle from prospecting target accounts and contacts to closing and renewing accounts; gathers and uses data to inform management of customer activity identifying customer requirements, competitive trends and changing environments</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Collaborates and communicates effectively with internal teams to create strategic solutions that promote customer objectives; listens to the needs of the market and shares with the product and marketing teams</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Prepares sales forecast reports of open opportunities as directed by management; participates in annual sales budget process; provides customer service before, during and after the order cycle by</div>
<div>acting as the company liaison between the customer and all departments within Communicorp</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Works closely with management to develop new sales strategies, pricing plans and product/service recommendations; works independently managing assigned customer base, expanding and developing accounts; conducts sales and account presentations</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Performs other duties as required</div>
