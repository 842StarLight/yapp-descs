<h2>Digital Marketing Technologist</h2>

<p><b>JOB SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
The Primary responsibilities of this role will be to manage the technology operations of MW Industriesâ€™ digital marketing ecosystems with an emphasis on managing assets within our cloud environment. This is inclusive of supporting the current architecture as well as developing optimized and cost-effective architecture for both our AWS and hosted web environments in a growing and changing landscape. This role will serve as the primary liaison between our outsourced platform providers, outsourced user experience providers, and internal digital marketing and IT departments to deliver a secure and optimal end user experience.</p>
<p><b>
REPORTING TO:</b><br>
VP of Information Technology - Strong indirect relation to VP of marketing and sales</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and maintain cloud infrastructure to support current and future digital marketing requirements</li>
<li>
Assist with aligning marketing technology to business goals, helping to create an end-to-end integrated marketing stack</li>
<li>
Work closely with internal Marketing and IT departments, along with outsourced providers to develop and maintain an optimized user experience on all web platforms</li>
<li>
Own and maintain all domains, inclusive of certificate renewal and the like</li>
<li>
Manage Linux operating environment</li>
<li>
Maintain website backups to ensure disaster recovery requirements are met</li>
<li>
Work with outsourced and internal IT to ensure application and server security is maintained and monitored</li>
<li>
Develop, test, and support functionality for digital marketing applications</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
SKILLS and ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to manage web application servers in an AWS or Azure environment and Linux operating system</li>
<li>
Strong grasp of Apache and understanding of NGINX for load balancing</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of SQL, specifically MySQL database; with a Familiarity with Amazon Aurora</li>
<li>
Proficiency with CSS, HTML/HTML5, JavaScript, AJAX, jQuery, PHP</li>
<li>
Familiarity with API integration</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of web application security and remediation best practices</li>
<li>
WordPress and/or WooCommerce experience a plus</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>
Self-starter with attention to detail</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
EDUCATION and EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS degree required</li>
<li>
Minimum 8-10 years of progressive experience in digital marketing ecosystems</li>
<li>
Experience with SEM, SEO and management of digital marketing KPIs</li>
<li>
Prior experience with version control and change management procedures</li>
</ul>
