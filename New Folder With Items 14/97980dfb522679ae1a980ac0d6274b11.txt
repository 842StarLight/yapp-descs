<h2>Greeter and Sales Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
The role of a Greeter and Sales Advisor is to welcome and educate our guests along with selling them services and wash memberships.
<br><br>
The Greeter and Sales Advisor role is a critical and important role at our dealership. You're directly responsible for our guests experience and their vehicles, assuring they are taken care of and informed. Additionally, your role is important as a team member that can assist in many areas around the dealership campus as needed. This role will give you exposure to many areas of our business and is a great spring board to other opportunities across our enterprise.
<br><br>
Energy, Effort and Enthusiasm are everything to a successful team member in this role. Don't expect to be lazy, bored or feel unappreciated. The role of Greeter and Sales Advisor is a critical one that will keep you busy, engaged and exposed to all facets of a dealership operation.
<br><br>
The Greeter and Sales Advisor role works 5 days a week, including some weekends. We offer very competitive pay, benefits such as health and dental insurance, matching 401k retirement program, great company culture and much more.
<br><br>
Come experience was it means to be part of a growing, thriving dealership environment where we put our customers and team members first.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Job Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a expert on the benefits behind all of our services</li>
<li>Accessing guests’ needs by listening, observing and recommending services and programs</li>
<li>Build guest relationships and deliver VIP service through product knowledge, trust and service to drive repeat business</li>
<li>Services Coordinator and site supervisor during high volume periods</li>
<li>Understand emergency exiting and gate release procedures</li>
<li>Follow safety guidelines to promote a safe workplace for guests and team members</li>
<li>Housekeeping duties such as sweeping, mopping, cleaning bathrooms, emptying trash, refilling supplies is expected throughout the day during down time</li>
<li>Maintain a calm demeanor during periods of high volume or unusual events to keep the location operating to its standards and to set a positive example to guests and team members</li>
<li>Treat guests and fellow team members with honesty, integrity and commitment</li>
<li>Adherence to our uniform and grooming standards</li>
<li>Punctual, responsible and accountable</li>
<li>Be a team player!</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent and enthusiastic customer service skills to ensure optimum customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Sales or customer service experience preferred</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (listening, verbal and written)</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and “hustle” while following instructions and procedures</li>
<li>Ability to stand for extended periods of time and work outdoors throughout the year</li>
<li>Ability to work flexible hours, weekends and holidays</li>
<li>Basic computer skills required to operate point-of-sale software and Auto Sentry pay stations</li>
<li>High school diploma (or pending graduation date), GED, or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Adaptability, multi-tasking and sound decision-making abilities are required and essential to the success of this role</li>
<li>Willingness to comply with all company policies and uphold standards for service quality</li>
</ul>
Working Conditions / Equipment Used
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Work outdoors in all seasons and types of weather</li>
<li>Ability to quality inspect your work as well as that of other team members</li>
<li>Constant standing, walking and bending; up to 4 consecutive hours of standing</li>
</ul>
