<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Position Summary:</div>
<div>
KSVI-KHMT - The TV and digital platform operated by Nexstar Broadcasting, Inc. in Billings, MT is searching for a dynamic, aggressive sales executive. In this position, you will work within the established Sales Department guidelines to increase sales volume for both TV and digital revenue by executing plans and strategies set forth by Sales Management. Applicants must be driven, self-motivated, creative professionals looking to take the next step in their career. Team player individuals with a passion for winning, a great attitude and a competitive spirit are the ideal candidates for this position. Experience preferred but not required.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Implement strategies to consistently grow revenue and exceed revenue goals.</li>
<li>
Establish credible relationships within our business community.</li>
<li>
Make sales calls on prospective clients and develop new business.</li>
<li>
Maintains assigned accounts and develops new accounts.</li>
<li>
Prepare and deliver sales presentations to clients</li>
<li>

Develop and maintain a strong knowledge of market trends and the media landscape for Billings and surrounding areas.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
