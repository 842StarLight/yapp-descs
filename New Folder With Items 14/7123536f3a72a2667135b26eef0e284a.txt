Job Summary
</div>
<div>
<p>We are seeking an experienced <b>Research Scientist I (Analytical Chemistry) </b>for our <b>Safety Assessment</b> site located in <b>Shrewsbury, MA</b>.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The <b>Research Scientist 1</b><b> (Analytical Chemistry) </b>will provide scientific support for nonclinical research or laboratory-based studies; perform basic study management tasks and interpretation and reporting of study data/conclusions. This person will also oversee routine study start-up procedures requiring cross-departmental interaction with all key study personnel. They will support assay development, assay transfer, assay validation, and study execution, and is involved in data review and acceptance (i.e. - small molecule, protein, nucleic acid, etc.) emphasizing HPLC and A280 assays.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The following are minimum requirements related to the <b>Research Scientist 1 (Analytical Chemistry) </b>position:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree (B.S./B.A.) or equivalent in a scientific related discipline.</li>
<li>Minimum 6 years related experience in chemistry/pharmacy, or similar laboratory environment, including 1-year related experience in the contract research or pharmaceutical industry in an analytical development role.</li>
<li>Experience with A280 assays and use of SoftMax Pro software is a plus.</li>
<li>Dose formulation analysis procedures and stability experience preferred but not required.</li>
<li>An equivalent combination of education and experience may be accepted as a satisfactory substitute for the specific education and experience listed above.</li>
<li>Effective written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple projects, prioritize work and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Proficiency in the use of standard software including Microsoft® Excel, Word, PowerPoint, etc. and with standard laboratory calculations.</li>
<br>
</ul>
