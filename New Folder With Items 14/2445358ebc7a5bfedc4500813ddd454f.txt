<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
The Channel Manager (CM) wins, maintains, and expands relationships with partners to sell services through TBI. Primary sales activity will be focused on acquisition of new revenue from the partners able to increase our referral and call center partner programs. The CM will provide additional support to these partners on an ongoing basis to develop the business. The CM will also help develop strategic business plans to help drive revenue through the partners. He/she will be responsible for ongoing training and development of partners and will be required to stay current on all emerging technological advances in the marketplace.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Recruits, trains and collaborates with partners to achieve recruitment and sales goals</li>
<li>Consistently works with partners on business plans and reviews performance quarterly for all selling partners</li>
<li>Meets with the Director weekly to review sales metrics, and strategic opportunities for partners to be successful</li>
<li>Consistently performs at 100% of monthly quota</li>
<li>Acts as the primary contact for partner escalations</li>
<li>Provides information for on-going training related to industry trends and vendor offerings</li>
<li>Reports on progress of each newly recruited partner for quotes and orders</li>
<li>Attends trainings for new products, services and technology</li>
<li>Attends TBI sales meetings and training functions</li>
<li>Travels approximately 20% of the time</li>
</ul>
