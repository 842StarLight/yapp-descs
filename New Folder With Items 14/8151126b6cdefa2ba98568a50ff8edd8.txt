<b>Position Summary</b><br>
The role of the Business Manager is to provide administrative and clerical support to the Director and ensure effective and efficient operations of the Program.<br>
<b>Essential Job Duties</b>
<ul>
<li>Proactive participant providing administrative support in the following areas of the Program’s operations:
<ul>
<li>Accounting functions and reporting in accordance with the generally accepted standard accounting principles and regulatory requirements,</li>
<li>Banking responsibilities as related to Program needs (cash ledgers, cash flow, deposits, withdrawals, checks etc.),</li>
<li>Monitor monetary functions to comply with IRS and reporting regulatory requirements,</li>
<li>Effectively manage and follow recruiting, pre-employment and new hire process,</li>
<li>Personnel administration, maintain and update staff files and training documentation,</li>
<li>Payroll reporting through appropriate systems and in partnership with Human Resources and Payroll,</li>
<li>Establish a schedule and monitor related to Program reporting requirements to internal and external clients,</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Support Billing Manager with network contracting and enrollment tasks; and Program and staff credentialing and monitoring for renewal,</li>
<li>Ensure eligibility authorization and claims information is accurate and up to date,</li>
<li>Interact and follow-up with external parties as needed for coordination related to credentialing and billing,</li>
<li>Establish processes to manage and pass audit and inspections,</li>
<li>Assist with the preparation and maintenance of annual budget items/tasks,
</li>
<li>Maintain Program contracts, cooperative agreements, letters of support, leases, corporate bylaws, etc.</li>
<li>Update Program organizational chart,</li>
<li>Track Program property inventory and advise Director of any discrepancies,</li>
<li>Establish and maintain effective open communication with internal and external parties,</li>
<li>Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information,</li>
<li>Assist with special projects and other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Minimum Education, Training and Experience</b>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree,</li>
<li>A minimum of 4 years experience as an Administrative Assistant role preferred with experience in business administration, bookkeeping, accounting or budget management experience,
</li>
<li>Microsoft Office proficiency (Excel, Word, Outlook and PowerPoint),</li>
<li>Experience with medical billing and electronic health care software preferred,</li>
<li>Effective communication skills (verbal and written) and public speaking skills,</li>
<li>May be required to obtain and maintain current CPR and First Aid Certification by nationally recognized organization,</li>
<li>Must possess and maintain valid driver’s license in the state of employment and have a driving record that meets the standards of the AMIkids Transportation Policy (CDL may be required based on role).</li>
</ul>As well as an opportunity to make an exceptional difference in kids' lives, AMIKids offers a comprehensive benefits package including Medical, Dental, Vision, Life and STD Insurance, Retirement Opportunities, Holidays &amp; PTO, Education Assistance, EAP, Disability Programs and Credit Union Membership.
