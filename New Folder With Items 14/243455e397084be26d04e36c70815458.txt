<p><b>Email Marketing Specialist - Rooftop Digital
</b></p>
<p>Rooftop Digital: We have the spirit of a startup and are passionate about delivering new costumers for some of the largest national brands in the home-services, auto and financial verticals. As a leader in the online advertising space, we’re looking for varsity players to come grow our team. We’re an employer who offers competitive compensation, great benefits and miscellaneous perks like a fully-stocked fridge loaded with free snacks and drinks. Together we’ll build a strong future for our company and your career.</p>
<p><b>
What Will I Be Doing?</b></p>
<p>
The position will report to our Email Marketing Manager. In your role as an Email Marketing Specialist, you will be part of our success by:</p>
<ul><li>Email Campaign Management to increase engagement with consumers through autoresponder campaigns, triggered and transactional emails, re-engagement campaigns, and other marketing automation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>You’ll be responsible for the planning and execution of email marketing, promotional programs, analysis of sales and response data to facilitate segmented campaigns.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Developing weekly email performance reports across multiple brands, containing key performance indicators to be shared with major shareholders.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Managing weekly email campaign schedules to ensure deadlines are met.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Brainstorm and implement contact strategies aimed at new customers to communicate value brand propositioning and at high-value customers to communicate their worth.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Segmenting lists based on data and behaviors such as past email engagement, website interactions, subscriber interests, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working alongside other team members within our marketing department.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyzing daily statistics &amp; performance across all campaigns.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working with Content &amp; Creative teams on graphics, verbiage, content, etc.</li></ul>
<p><b>
What Do I Need to Demonstrate?</b></p>
<p><b>
Required:</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in General Management, Marketing, or other degrees that show ability of high proficiency for detail, general marketing knowledge, communication, and analytics</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highly analytical and capable of deriving meaning from A/B testing data and email optimization</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to multi-task and thrive in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment</li></ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Experience &amp; Skills:</b></p>

<p><b>
Characteristics Necessary for Success:</b></p>
<ul><li>Organized and self-starting ability to work independently, execute quickly, move to the next task and follow through without constant oversight</li></ul>
