<h2>Worldwide Partner Programs Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Role and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Our team seeks an innovative Worldwide Partner Program Manager to manage and support how and where we drive partners through the lifecycle via stakeholder engagement efforts, applying change management methodologies. This individual has knowledge in and passion for channel strategy and execution, instructional design, stakeholder engagement, and communication strategy and execution. The successful candidate will be responsible for driving sound decisions, strategic planning and communication efforts across the VMware teams that work with partners such as resellers, cloud, service-only and strategic partners. Must have an unflinching desire to radically simplify our partners’ end-to-end program experience to unleash partner productivity and profitability.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Be willing to learn all details of VMware’s global partner programs</li>
<li>
Align and tune program communication efforts and stakeholder learning to continually support VMware go-to-market initiatives, strategies, and priorities</li>
<li>
Lead or contribute to partner process development and channel program strategy on a global and/or business-wide level</li>
<li>
Drive strategy into execution by creating strategic engagement plans and developing communication processes and holistic story structure</li>
<li>
Provide project, program, and resource management support to program/strategic projects and activities</li>
<li>
Proactively own and execute ongoing communication efforts to maintain global Partner Programs<ul>
<li>
maintain internal and external stakeholder matrices, impact assessments, communication plans</li>
<li>
execute field and partner communications</li>
<li>
maintain and improve program assets and documentation</li>
<li>
initiate and implement process improvements to support all VMware global partner programs
</li>
<li>collaborate and communicate frequently with internal stakeholders who support channels: geo channel leaders, partner programs management, sales management, partner operations, partner incentives, go-to-market, etc.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
