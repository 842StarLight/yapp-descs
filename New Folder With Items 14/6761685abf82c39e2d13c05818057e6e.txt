<br>
<p>The Amazon Devices Planning team is looking for an energetic, focused and skilled Technical Program Manager (TPM) to lead programs within the Amazon devices space. If you are a Technical Program Manager with an analytical flare, strong problem-solving skills, excellent communication skills, ability to influence business and technical leaders in a dynamic environment and the desire to be an individual contributor to Amazon's next generation machine learning and big data technology, we want to talk with you. Joining our team provides the unique challenge and opportunity to build machine leaning solutions that directly impact the marketing and sales of Amazon devices around the globe.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate will draw upon exemplary program / project management, critical thinking, and problem solving skills, and a passion for creating reliable and maintainable systems. The candidate should be open to new challenges, extremely good at multi-tasking, innovative, creative, self-directed and a great team player. Candidate should drive continuous process improvement, and collaborate effectively with aggressive cross-functional business and software development teams to solve problems and implement new solutions.<br>
<br>
The team is just getting started and is growing rapidly. This is a great time to get involved an area that is still considered Day 1, and is both exciting and of great benefit to Amazon.</p>
