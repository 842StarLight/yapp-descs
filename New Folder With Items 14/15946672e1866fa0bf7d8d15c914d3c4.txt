<b>About Gray Television:</b><br>
Gray Television supports local community interests by providing quality television broadcasting and exceptional service in each market we serve. Gray owns and/or operates television stations and leading digital properties in 93 television markets including the first or second highest rated television station in 85 markets. We are an unmatched broadcast pioneer that keeps getting bigger and better.
<br><br>
We constantly strive for excellence. Through upgrading to the latest technology and seeking new ways to stay on top in our markets, we focus on training and development of the best and brightest employees in the business.
<br><br>
<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
The successful candidate will be well-versed in digital display advertising, mobile advertising, video advertising, native digital advertising, as well as 3rd party serving. He/she will be able to identify which platforms would be best to advertise on given the brand’s objectives and requirements as well as inventory available to maximize the impact of the campaign.
<br><br>
The Digital Ad Operations Specialist will be on his/her way to becoming an expert in the field of digital media, have a strong understanding of media consumption habits and audience profiles, and can independently our TV stations and clients. He/she can also easily navigate reporting and provide impactful takeaways to strengthen campaign’s delivery. Always an enthusiastic contributor, the Digital Ad Ops Specialist will add value to the team in both the pre-sale and post-sale phase of a campaign.
<br><br>
<b>Key Traits:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strategic: The ability to align the digital campaign plan with the organization’s strategic objectives is paramount to success in this role</li>
<li>Analytical: Interpreting data and being capable of adjusting the digital strategy based on analytics is a plus</li>
<li>Tactical: Capable of handling tactical aspects of digital campaigns; ability to solve technical problems related to website, content, advertising, marketing efforts</li>
<li>A Creator: Technically savvy combined with creative vision -- Digital Marketing is partly science but, make no mistake, it takes a creative mind to fill this role</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>Provide customer service and act as point of contact for assigned stations and clients</li>

<li>Monitor the scheduling, pacing, and implementation of all advertising campaigns to ensure timely, accurate implementation, and full delivery</li>
<li>Communicate effectively and closely with local sales and support teams including vendors and external teams</li>
<li>Ensure that campaign revisions are fully communicated and executed with 100% accuracy</li>
<li>Confirm all ad orders adhere to tech and creative specifications and support creative/technical related inquiries from clients</li>
<li>Manage advertising specs and guidelines as new partners are added and as the company and product offerings evolve</li>
<li>Manage relationships with distribution channels, outside vendors, agencies, etc. and ad hoc requests from sales team</li>
<li>Partner with staff from across departments as a liaison on projects, new procedures, new systems, workflows, best practices and campaign, or creative issues as requested</li>
<li>Maintain Ad Ops processes and best practices and provide suggestions to leadership on improvement opportunities</li>
<li>Assist the development team in identifying and resolving ad-related technical issues</li>
