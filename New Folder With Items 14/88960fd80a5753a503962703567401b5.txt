<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Emplify is on a mission to help all people achieve their true potential at work. We help leaders make sense of the many dynamics at play in their workforce so they can engage and retain top talent. Our employee engagement measurement platform fuses advanced survey technology and proven methodology with human strategists, to identify the specific actions business leaders and managers can take to continually improve employee engagement and performance.</p>
<p>
The Account Executive (AE) creates, manages and closes opportunities. Our AE’s sell value instead of price and educate first-time buyers. Our AE’s are self-starters and driven to exceed their potential. Amazing opportunity to join a high-growth startup that generously rewards high performers. The AE role is located in our Fishers, IN HQ office.</p>
<p><b>
A Day in the Life</b></p>
<ul>
<li>10 minute rapid “stand-up” with the rest of the team recapping the wins from yesterday and sharing your goals for today</li>
<li>Grab some caffeine and maybe some chocolate almonds to fuel your energy</li>
<li>Begin digging into your pipeline and prep for calls by working with the sales development reps</li>
<li>Uncover cultural and engagement issues with a VP of HR or CEO, show them how our tool can help solve their challenges</li>
<li>Pause to celebrate a team member’s sales win (maybe play a little Pop-A-Shot)</li>
<li>Close the day with a CEO who is looking for a tool to scientifically measure their engagement</li>
</ul>

</li>
