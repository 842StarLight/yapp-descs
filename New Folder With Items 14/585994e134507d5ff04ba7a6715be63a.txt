<h2>Yard Helper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Position Summary</p>
<p>
Dawes Rigging and Crane Rental, Inc.-Milwaukee is seeking an entry level team member to take responsibility for the maintenance of the Grounds and Service Department. This is a full-time, non-exempt position.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Functions</p>
<p>
Miscellaneous tasks around the yard: maintenance and clean-up of equipment, buildings and general area<br>
Responsible for cleaning and pressure washing of cranes and heavy trucks<br>
Assist in loading/unloading cranes and crane components<br>
Provide physical labor at construction sites and assist other craft workers as directed<br>
Operate hand and power tools and a variety of other equipment and instruments<br>
Maintain conformity to safety requirements and other regulations</p>
<p></p>
<p>Skills and Experience Requirements</p>
<p>
Must have valid driver’s license with an acceptable MVR<br>
Strong initiative required; ability to work independently with minimal direct supervision<br>
Ability to work safely around moving machinery; to work in conditions with marked changes in temperature &amp; humidity<br>
Ability to safely operate complex construction equipment<br>
Able to lift 50 lbs</p>
