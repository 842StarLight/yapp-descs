ESSENTIAL DUTIES
<br><br>
The New-vehicle Product Specialist advises customers on vehicle platforms, option packages, and
<br><br>
powertrain choices with special attention to features, benefits, functionality, and information.
<br><br>
Essential Duties include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
<br><br>
Greets each customer promptly upon arrival at dealership.
<br><br>
Stays informed of current vehicle inventory and location.
<br><br>
Tracks changing vehicle market conditions and incentives.
<br><br>
Is familiar with the appraisal process and sells the appraisal.
<br><br>
Attends sales meetings and special events as scheduled.
<br><br>
Is familiar with departmental sales objectives.
<br><br>

<br><br>
Works on service drive contacting customers for test drive opportunities.
<br><br>
Ensures relevant content on all social media.
<br><br>
Monitor customer comments and feedback and ensure reputation management policies are in place.
<br><br>
Attends all sales and service meetings to gather information on daily, weekly, and monthly customer
<br><br>
goals.
<br><br>
Stays informed on all department specials and incentives and ensures that expired offers are promptly
<br><br>
removed from sites.
<br><br>
Plans weekly and monthly enthusiasm events and employee team-building events and places on
<br><br>
proper media platform.
<br><br>
Works with all departments and outside media to ensure proper placement on sites.
<br><br>
Is proactive in looking for opportunities to promote dealership awareness and community involvement
<br><br>
on social media sites.
<br><br>
Communicates effectively with Internet customers and prospects according to their preferred method
<br><br>

<br><br>
Has thorough knowledge of all dealership products and services.
<br><br>
Satisfies the transportation needs of Internet-generated customers.
<br><br>
Directs customers to product information resources, including those available on the Internet.
<br><br>
Assists customers in selecting vehicles.
<br><br>
Understands the psychology of Internet customers and works with them accordingly to draw them into
<br><br>
the store for proper vehicle demonstration and to close the transaction.
<br><br>
Checks email frequently and responds to inquiries immediately. Delivers messages intended for other
<br><br>
departments promptly. Notifies customers that their messages have been forwarded to the
<br><br>
appropriate personnel for prompt attention.
<br><br>
Handles all Internet sales inquiries personally.
<br><br>
Coordinates Internet promotions with other dealership sales promotions and advertising.
<br><br>
Ensures that the website address is prominently displayed throughout the dealership, e.g., on
<br><br>
business cards, flyers, pens, and all dealership advertising.
<br><br>
Researches auto-buying services on the Internet and acts as liaison with any services the dealership
<br><br>
uses to promote its site or advertise.
<br><br>
Works with webmaster to update the website frequently to attract new and repeat visitors.
<br><br>
Takes photos of dealership inventory for use on the dealership site.
<br><br>
Maintains an ongoing customer database to capture repeat business.
<br><br>
Knows and understands the federal, state, and local laws that govern retail automobile sales.
<br><br>
Establishes personal income goals that are consistent with dealership standards of productivity, and
<br><br>
devises a strategy to meet those goals.
<br><br>
Keeps abreast of new products, features, accessories, etc., and their benefits to customers.
