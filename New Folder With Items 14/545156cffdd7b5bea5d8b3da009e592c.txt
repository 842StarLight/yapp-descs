<p><b>
Customer Service Goal:</b></p>
<p>
To support Tundra's goal of providing customers the best product experience and offering through support of product information and marketable presentation.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b><b>: </b>Position will drive the processing and collection of information needed to support product expansion as well as maintenance initiatives as well as evolving with standards</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Job functions</b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Systematic approach to reviewing and optimizing product information and marketing details to increase appeal as well as order value. Prioritizing and making enhancements as a result of reviews. Independent identification of opportunities based on industry insight</li>
<li>Work with vendors to implement product expansions, review vendor progress monthly to improve trends</li>
<li>Grow product awareness for sales team and customers by working with marketing</li>
<li>Creation of growth and performance metrics for products as well as support of identification of new opportunities for the assortment</li>
<li>Identification and implementation of automation opportunities to support the product databases</li>
<li>Creating and maintaining audit queries to ensure availability and support of products to all sales channels and adjusting to evolving standards and requirements</li>
<li>Creation and maintenance of team documentation to support ongoing standard requirement changes</li>
<li>Corrections made as necessary to databases, enhancing existing product information to support differentiated details and attributes. Support ERP and Web databases &amp; product maintenance activities</li>
<li>Support of item upload process to gather item specs, and other data driven details necessary for our ERP system as well as new product launches</li>
<li>Processing cost changes and preparing in excel format for review as needed</li>
<li>Resolving product ticket issues as assigned within Web and ERP databases</li>
<li>proofing as necessary</li>
<br>
</ul>
