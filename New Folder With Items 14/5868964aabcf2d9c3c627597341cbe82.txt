<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Financial Analysts ensure that Google makes sound financial decisions. As a Financial Analyst, your work, whether it's modeling business scenarios or tracking performance metrics, is used by our leaders to make strategic company decisions. While working on multiple projects at a time, you are focused on the details while finding creative ways to solve big picture challenges.</p>
<p>Google’s Ads Finance team ensures that Google makes sound financial decisions. We balance the roles of trusted advisor and guardian to reveal opportunity, manage risk, and optimize investment across industries and products.<br>
<br>
We are strategic partners to business leaders across Sales and Product and drive impact through a grounding in hard data, an independent point of view, a bias to action, a collaborative approach, and a focus on the development of our people. Acting as mini-CFO’s and financial consultants, the Ads Finance Team is focused on the details while finding creative ways to solve big-picture challenges.</p>
<p>Ads Finance is part of Google’s Financial Planning &amp; Analysis (FP&amp;A) organization, providing financial and analytical support to Google’s business organization. Our work is integral to the operations and strategic decision-making of the leadership team across all our advertising products. In this role, you will be the trusted advisor to the business leaders, you will be the guardian of financials driving decisions around resource allocation, business growth, and revenue management.<br>
<br>
As a Strategic Finance Analyst, you'll contribute analytical rigor and strategic thinking to drive our business forward. This is an exciting role that provides full finance support to business partners as well as offering scope for leadership of individual analytical projects. You will work in close partnership with highly talented finance colleagues from across the region and consult on planning, reporting and decision support for the wide range of activities that acquire, delight and retain our users, advertisers and employees.<br>
<br>
You will be an analyst who wants to make your mark on a successful and growing team, and who wants to further develop already effective business partnering skills. You are an excellent communicator who enjoys collaborating with people who possess a diverse set of talents. Additionally, you posses excellent time management skills and the ability to prioritize effectively while creating space for original thinking and thoughtful analysis to support our executives.</p>
<p>
The name Google came from "googol," a mathematical term for the number 1 followed by 100 zeros. And nobody at Google loves big numbers like the Finance team when providing in depth analysis on all manner of strategic decisions across Google products. From developing forward-thinking analysis to generating management reports to scaling our automated financial processes, the Finance organization is an important partner and advisor to the business.</p>
