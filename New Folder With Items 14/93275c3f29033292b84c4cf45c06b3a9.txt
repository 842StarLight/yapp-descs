<b>
INTERNSHIP POSITION OVERVIEW:</b><br>
<br>
Under supervision, interns are expected to develop a broad knowledge of Less Than Truck Load (LTL) operations, tools, processes and procedures working alongside senior professionals, managers and leadership in areas of operations and sales.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL JOB DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<br>
1. Gain knowledge of LTL industry principles, terms, policies and procedures as defined by company<br>
2. Develop competency and understanding of processes and systems used in operations and sales<br>
3. Gain understanding of analytical approach and procedures to measure operations objectives<br>
4. Develop through understanding of intent and control measures of reports for processes that post results<br>
5. Develop and communicate ad hoc reporting<br>
6. Assist with various assignments and projects as assigned<br>
7. Comply with all applicable laws/regulations, as well as company policies/procedures<br>
8. Perform other duties as required<br>
<br>
