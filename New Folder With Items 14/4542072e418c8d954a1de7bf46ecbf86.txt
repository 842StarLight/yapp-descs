<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibility</b></h3>
<div>AkzoNobel has a passion for paint. We’re experts in the proud craft of making paints and coatings, setting the standard in color and protection since 1792. Our world class portfolio of brands – including International, Interpon, Sikkens and Chemcraft – is trusted by customers around the globe. Headquartered in the Netherlands, we are active in over 150 countries and employ around 35,000 talented people who are passionate about delivering the high-performance products and services our customers expect. In North America, we’ve been producing innovative, high-performance coatings since 1888. Today, you can find AkzoNobel’s products supporting industries ranging from aerospace to consumer electronics, infrastructure, transportation, home décor, furniture and marine vessels. With a regional headquarters in Nashville, Tennessee, AkzoNobel’s North America operation includes 3,500 employees at more than 30 manufacturing sites, technology labs and business offices. For more information please visit www.akzonobel.com<br>
In North America, AkzoNobel provides performance coatings products to customers through four operating business units: Powder Coatings, Automotive &amp; Special Coatings, Industrial Coatings and Marine, Protective &amp; Yacht Coatings.<br>
The Sr. Chemist will evaluate, select, and apply standard scientific techniques, processes, and criteria in the development, adaptation, and modification of new and existing products and technologies. This position represents significant professional work knowledge and experience.<br>
<ul>
<li>Conduct research and development of new products, new technologies, and updates of existing products using innovative ideas, materials, and test methods.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable of materials, products, and technologies commonly encountered within the business area.</li>
<li>Ensure robustness in all phases of research and development through the use of accepted techniques including DOE, FMEA, SPC, and others as required.</li>
<li>Provide training and supervision for Technicians by identifying development needs, mentoring, and planning/scheduling activities such as experiments and projects.</li>
<li>Provide technical assistance and support to Production, Sales, and Marketing in support of product scale-up, quality control, application testing, and other cross-functional activities.</li>
<li>Know and observe company and location health and safety requirements.</li>
<li>Maintains detailed technical records in the form of a Laboratory Notebook and Databases.</li>
<li>Provide progress reports, monthly and quarterly reports, technical plans, and project summaries to ensure appropriate communication of project status and needs.</li>
<li>Maintain and develop professional contacts including company personnel, vendors, and customers.</li>
<li>Maintain a safe and clean work environment in accordance of company safety rules to ensure compliance with federal, state, and local environments and safety regulations.</li>
<li>Oversee specific functions and/or locations, as required.</li>
<li>Travel requirements are light, typically 0-4 trips annually.</li>
<li>Participate in Special Teams including safety, HSE, housekeeping, and others as required.</li>
<li>Performs additional duties as assigned and required.</li>
</ul>
