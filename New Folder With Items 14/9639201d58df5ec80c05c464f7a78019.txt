<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>WalkMe pioneered the Digital Adoption Platform (DAP) to transform the user experience in today’s overwhelming digital world. Using artificial intelligence, analytics, engagement, guidance, and automation, WalkMe’s transparent overlay assists users to complete tasks easily within any enterprise software, mobile application or website.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As a Business Development Manager, you will manage a team of Business Development Representatives (BDR's) in our growing Raleigh office and report to the Director of Business Development in San Francisco . Your team of BDRs will provide lead generation, business development and qualification activities for our Account Executives by setting qualified meetings with prospective new logo customers and by going deeper and wider within existing enterprise customers.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Own</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Help us build a new team of BDRs in our Raleigh office</li>
<li>
Inspire and develop the team through 1:1 and group training sessions. Coach the team through a variety of activities to improve their skills.</li>
<li>
Ensure your team of BDRs meets call, email and social media activities and targets to hit ongoing quotas</li>
<li>
Know our services, sales techniques, pricing and marketing programs and understand sales patterns and strategies</li>
<li>
Collaborate closely with our Account Executives to create new opportunities</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Need to Succeed</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Four plus years of experience in sales management, or sales development management preferably in SaaS or similar enterprise applications, with a history of exceeding quota expectations</li>
<li>
A team-player with the ability to motivate and inspire to help BDRs exceed goals</li>
<li>
Experience developing productive business relationships with all levels of our client management</li>
<li>
Organize sales campaigns that may include support from multiple teams</li>
<li>
CRM experience or comparable salesforce automation tool</li>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree or equivalent work experience</li>
</ul></div>
