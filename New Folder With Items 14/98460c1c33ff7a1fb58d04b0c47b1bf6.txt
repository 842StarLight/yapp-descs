<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p><i>Salt Lake</i> magazine- the magazine for Utah, also the publishers of <i>Utah Style &amp; Design</i> and <i>Utah Bride &amp; Groom</i>, is seeking to hire an Account Executive for the Park City market that is highly dedicated, outgoing, aggressive, determined, punctual, creative, energetic, and loves a fun environment where ideas are always welcomed.</p>
<p><b>WORK SCHEDULE</b></p>
<p>Full-time Monday through Friday 8:00-5:00.</p>
<p><b>HIRING COMPENSATION</b></p>
<p>Strong base pay + lucrative commission structure.</p>
<p><b>BENEFITS</b></p>
<p>Monthly expense reimbursements, medical/dental/vision insurance, 3 weeks PTO after 1 year, paid holidays, 1 week off after Christmas.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>Account Executive will be responsible for growing and cultivating a book of business through structuring effective advertising campaigns for clients. Building strong relationships with clients will be crucial. Ability to work under pressure and hard deadlines. Ability to be flexible, balance many tasks, and work in a fast-paced environment. Account Executive will be responsible for qualifying leads that are a strong fit for our niche publications and closing new business. Account Executives will be responsible for hitting personal revenue goals. Individual must have strong time-management and self-discipline skills, as well as being welcoming to leadership guidance.</p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>Bachelors Degree in Mass Communication, Marketing, PR, Advertising, or related field. Must have a reliable vehicle for transportation. Must be computer savvy for creating proposals, contracts, constant emailing, client research, calendar scheduling, and frequent utilization of a CRM tool. Passion for helping other businesses to flourish. Must have previous experience in leadership and/or management. Must have a resume that shows consistent previous employment.</p>
<p><b>PREFERENCES THAT ARE AN ADDED PLUS</b></p>
<p>2+ years strong sales experience.</p>
<p>Management experience.</p>
<p>Experience on the phone handling client management.</p>
<p>Knowledge about CRM tools.</p>
<p>Previous marketing-related employment.</p>
<p>Candidate that lives near or in Park City.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $30,000.00 to $90,000.00 /year</p>
