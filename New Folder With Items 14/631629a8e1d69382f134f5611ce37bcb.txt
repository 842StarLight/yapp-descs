<h2>Account Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Overview:</b><br>
The Account Development representatives specializes in acquiring customers through inbound leads and qualified prospects. This role is responsible for providing customers with product solutions based on facts and understanding of customers' business needs and strategy. This ADR owns opportunities through the full sales cycle from lead to close.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Receives well qualified leads and prospects to sell integrated solutions based on customer need.</li>
<li>
Lead sales process for all Rackspace solutions</li>
<li>
Meet virtually with qualified leads to better understand customer needs and provide proposals</li>
<li>
Identifies opportunities to grow customer proposals, expand initial footprint and solution offerings based on customer need</li>
<li>
Lead efforts to create proposal for solution to prove value add</li>
<li>
Leads the negotiation, closure, and documentation of customer contracts and renewals (for customers with growth potential)</li>
<li>
Collaborate with prospective customers to select the best solution to meet their needs out of the full product portfolio.</li>
</ul>
