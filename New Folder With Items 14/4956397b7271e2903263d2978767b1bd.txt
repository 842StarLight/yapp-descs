<h2>Team Member Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>
The Team Member Development Manager is a leader who is passionate about the hiring, growth and development of fitness professionals. This leader owns all aspects of the onboarding, development and succession of The Life Time Fitness Professional. The T.D.M. will lead and manage a cohort of fitness professionals as they are on-boarded into the Fitness and Nutrition team, as well as, guide their development and succession desires throughout their career within their given location.</p>

Major Duties and Responsibilities</b></h3>
<p>
Member Experience</p>

<li>
Conducts fitness professional file audits each month.</li>
<li>
Follow-up client call for quality assurance.</li>
<li>
Conducts shadows of member experience interactions to assure a quality experience.</li>
<li>
Reassigns clientele when a trainer is termed</li>
</ul>
