<h2>New Home Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Use customer-focused sales skills to convert online (and phone) leads from various sources to neighborhood appointments by investigating needs and providing relevant product and neighborhood information.</li>
<li>Tracking and updating of lead data including, but not limited to, contact information, home buying preferences, neighborhoods of interest, marketing sources through sales software.</li>
<li>Report monthly lead activity and results to Corporate and Regional Leadership (total leads, appointments set and held, sales conversion, etc.)</li>
<li>Assist Neighborhood Sales Managers (NSMs) in identifying and updating accurate StanleyMartin.com neighborhood information</li>
<li>Continue to engage and nurture unresponsive leads to appointment and sale conversion in accordance with the Stanley Martin cadence and Rules of Engagement.</li>
<li>Work with NSMs to follow up on lead appointments and assist in closing and/or problem solving when necessary.</li>
<li>Participate in re-engagement efforts (surveys, phone-a-thons, text-a-thons, etc.) for both leads and stalled neighborhood traffic units</li>
<li>Work with marketing and divisional sales to stay up to date on neighborhoods, products, and promotions</li>
<li>Audit and work with Corporate marketing to improve the website listings for every neighborhood and product</li>
<li>Attend monthly sales meetings and conduct bi-weekly calls with sales leadership to review lead feedback and conversion information.</li>
<li>Represent the company professionally in all interactions with customers and home buyers</li>
<li>Complete all other duties as assigned by manager</li>
<li>Adhere to company safety standards and help promote a safe working environment for office and field warranty employees, subcontractors and homeowners</li>
<li>Adhere to and promote the Mission, Vision, and Values of Stanley Martin</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Position Standards:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Team player with a positive attitude</li>
<li>Attention to Detail</li>
<li>Knowledge of MS Office (CRM experience a plus)</li>
<li>Organizational Skills</li>
<li>Positive Attitude</li>
<li>Self Starter</li>
<li>Strong Communication Skills (written and verbal)</li>
<li>Strong understanding of the sales process</li>
<li>Knows how to respond to objections and turn them into a sale</li>
<li>Problem-solving skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Position Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
College degree preferred; 3+ years of Sales experience, residential preferred; lead management, online sales, SEO knowledge preferred</p>
