<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT YOU WILL DO:</b></h2>
<p>Responsible for developing and maintaining a partnership between the Regional Marketing stakeholders and Product Development teams, in order to define/communicate product positioning and customer vales to facilitate successful market introductions of products produced by PUM.<br>
<br>
<b>Product Promotion</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Support the market introduction process (MIP) by being the key contact for PUM/PUN products for global marketing and sales collateral.
</li>
<li>Provide product positioning training and materials for sales departments and customer service.</li>
<li>
Develop standardized sales presentations, proposals, scripts, etc. to communicate to global sales and marketing teams the features, benefits and values of products.</li>
<li>
Design and implement product launches for offline and online channels.</li>
<li>
Collaborate cross functionally, with Product and Market Development Managers, on market development strategies, sales opportunities and field communication solutions.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with regional sales teams on advertising and promotion to ensure product marketing, positioning, accuracy and effectiveness are achieved.
</li>
<li>Track customer care dashboards, metrics, consumer feedback and customer satisfaction to measure performance and employment of existing and new functionalities and features.</li>
<li>
Monitor and compose commercialized product information for web backend in conjunction with Regional Marketing and the DEA (Design/Engineering/Administration) group.
</li>
</ul>
<b>Regional Market Planning:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with the Product Manager and Market Developer to determine customer or user requirements to incorporate value propositions for regional sales and marketing teams</li>
<li>
Create and enhance marketing collateral (i.e. case studies, data sheets, whitepapers, FAQ, feature or benefit sheets)
</li>
<li>Develop market specific global problem statements, using business cases, scenarios, sharing recommendations and results with senior managers to facilitate product roadmap definition.</li>
<li>
Review training communication and mediums to provide input for improving communication effectiveness with regional sales team, dealers and customers.
</li>
<li>Review current product sales and market feedback of current product for feature/messaging integration into new product specifications and collaboration with Product Managers.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Other:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist product management in the development of corporate strategies.</li>
<li>
Complete competitive analysis and provide insights to product managers to be used in product strategy development.</li>
<li>
Support channel partners with sales collateral, best practices, and training.</li>
<li>
Aid in development of market requirements document for creating product business case(s).</li>
</ul>
