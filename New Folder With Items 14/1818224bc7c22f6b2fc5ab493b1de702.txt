<h2>CNC Programmer/Setup Machinist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Dover Flexo Electronics (DFE) is located in the seacoast region of New Hampshire. We are an established manufacturing company looking for a motivated self-starter to help us improve our manufacturing and develop/implement new and modern techniques to help us grow. This is an opportunity for the right person to make a difference as we execute our growth strategy.</p>
<p>We are looking for a CNC Programmer/Setup Machinist who will be primarily responsible for the programing and setup of milling and turning centers in our newly acquire machine shop. The right candidate will be involved in development of the manufacturing process and may include researching new tooling, programming and operating CNC machines, and choosing proper equipment to support the process. You will also provide some support for the design and development of new products.</p>
<p><b>Position Specific Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop programs and work with engineering to develop fixturing and tooling.</li>
<li>Setup machines for jobs with 1st piece inspection.</li>
<li>Help train and mentor new machinist and machine operators.</li>
<li>Maintain technical knowledge by attending educational workshops, reviewing professional publications, and establishing personal networks; apply technical knowledge to improve processes.</li>
<li>Continually improve daily work and practices in terms of quality and quantity.</li>
<li>Coordinate programming priority to support the shop schedules.</li>
<li>Communicate process and product developments across business units.</li>
<li>Work with Product Development Engineers and sales department to provide estimates of programming time and manufacturing time for new jobs.</li>
<li>Upkeep of records and paperwork.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Requirements </b></p>
<ul><li>Must have 5+ years’ experience in an machine shop or manufacturing environment.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Must have 5+ years’ experience using CAM software required, CAMworks preferred.</li>
<li>Strong math and trigonometry skills.</li>
<li>Six Sigma experience preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to read prints, understanding of ASME Y14.5M Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing, and familiarization of inspection equipment and gages.</li>
<li>Ability to present technical information effectively.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, communication and organization skills.</li>
<li>Moderate physical activity requiring agility and manual dexterity, including walking, standing, or bending as well as lifting or moving objects.</li>
<li>Requires occasional lifting and carrying of 25 lbs. or more and standing for extended periods of time.</li>
</ul>
