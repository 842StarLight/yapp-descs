<h2>ALD Brand Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Description</b><br>
<div><b>Position Summary:</b></div>
<div>
Position responsible for increasing velocity of brands in a set universe of accounts within a specific geography. Incumbent builds relationships with account level key influencers &amp; decision makers and is responsible for sell-in of strategic initiatives at key accounts. The position focuses on getting the PRUSA portfolio of brands in the best position to be ordered by consumers – this includes educating staff, developing marketing initiatives (features), and utilizing all resources to build brand affinity in their respective geography.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>
Improve merchandising and visibility of brands in accounts, including back bar placement and POS utilizationIdentify each account’s velocity drivers and ensure PRUSA brands are included in the key velocity driversEducate Account Staff on key items with particular emphasis on new items as they are sold inIdentify account opportunities/distribution gaps and initiate the sell in of new itemsResponsible for selling-in and in some cases executing strategic initiatives at key accountsResponsible for menu and promotional tracking of PRUSA and competitive brandsResponsible for performing surveys during account visits (Briefcase)Schedule and administer on site product promotions and signageBuilds relationships with key account gatekeepers (owners, bartenders, customers) and conducts staff trainings to build brand loyalty and knowledgeMonitors and maintains up-to-date awareness on product information, company initiatives and promotions, advertising, and industry trendsDevelops account detailed “Call List” with key contact and call scheduleResponsible for daily Pre-Plan of activities and daily/weekly recap of results and accomplishments to team managerManage robust budget, taking time to evaluate ROI’s and communicating successes/learningsProposes new ideas/methods on how to improve performance, maintains a positive, can-do attitude and influences others to gain support for an idea or causeResponsible for product distribution and placement in conjunction with SWS sales teamWorks closely with sales team to achieve PRUSA strategic goals and implement initiatives, innovations and programsCollaborate and communicate store level plans with brand agents, Sales People, PRUSA MM’s, and Sales Mgmt. to ensure proper resource allocationWork flexible hours which include early morning (i.e. 4:00a.m. -6:00a.m. start times), evenings, and/or weekends. Overnight travel as required. Additional hours required during peak season of October, November and December and June and in accordance with the event schedule.Perform other work related duties and special projects as assigned.Be aware of federal, State and local laws affecting the beverage/alcohol industry with account responsibility.Project a positive, professional image at all times.Ability to use and understand Supplier specific application.</div>
