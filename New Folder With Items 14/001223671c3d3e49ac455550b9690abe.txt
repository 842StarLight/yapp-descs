<h2>Financial Center Manager- South MoPac</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The Financial Center (“FC”) Manager performs the overall administrative supervision of all branch office activities. The FC Manager directs daily operations and personnel activities to assure convenient and efficient operation of the branch within the framework of federal and state laws, NCUA regulation, and UFCU policies and procedures.</p>
<p>
Location: South MoPac Financial Center</p>
<p>
5033 W Hwy 290, Austin, TX 78735-6751</p>

<p><b>Financial Center Management</b></p>
