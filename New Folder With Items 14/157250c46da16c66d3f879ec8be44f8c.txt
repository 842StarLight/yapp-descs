<h2>FF&E Procurement Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>YOUR ROLE:</b></p>
<p>As the Procurement Leader, you will work with Interior Designers, Practice Leaders, and the Construction Services Manager to oversee the furniture, fixtures and equipment (FF&amp;E) procurement process to deliver successful and profitable procurement projects on budget and on time within the firm. You will work closely with the Interior Designers leading each FF&amp;E procurement project to help define the scope, scheduling needs, budget and profit margins of their procurement projects in coordination with the Construction Services Manager.</p>
<p>You have strong <b>business acumen</b> and <b>customer focus</b>, and are <b>widely trusted</b>, seen as direct and truthful, with <b>strong integrity</b> and <b>understanding of others</b>.</p>
<p>Through strong <b>strategic agility</b>, <b>negotiating</b>, <b>composure</b>, and <b>presentation skills</b>, you will work with Practice Leaders and Marketing and Business Development in creating strategies to market and sell Progressive AE?s FF&amp;E procurement services, while executing highly effective and extensive outbound efforts to develop relationships with existing and new clients that will lead to new business.</p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU WILL DO:</b></p>
<p>Leadership</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate the knowledge and expertise to develop and execute a strategic plan for the growth of Procurement at Progressive AE</li>
<li>Possess the knowledge and skills needed to develop business initiatives, effectively interview, negotiate, and close deals.</li>
<li>Work with other Practice Leaders to increase consistency, expand knowledge, share experiences to improve collaboration and to encourage cross-selling to meet business development objectives across targetted markets.</li>
<li>Act as an ambassador for companywide values and ethical standards of practice.</li>
<li>Assist the team to proactively track and then successfully achieve meeting the procurement and installation schedule and overall project costs.</li>
<li>Identify and solve problems before they become issues that cost additional time and money.</li>
<li>Understand, promote, and utilize Progressive?s established standards for process delivery.</li>
<li>Maintain positive relationships with employees, clients, contractors, and suppliers.</li>
<li>Model positive behaviors, values, mutual respect, collaboration, and ethical standards of the practice. Display high level of energy, enthusiasm, and responsiveness.</li>
<li>Consistently utilize excellent judgement and problem-solving skills related to independent decision making.</li>
</ul>
<p>Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Create project work scopes with Project Manager.</li>
<li>Create and maintain an overall procurement schedule utilizing Microsoft Project or similar software. Coordinate with Owner and Contractor. Prepare and execute project work plans. Monitor and alert the project team when changes occur in the project scope, and update schedules promptly.</li>
<li>When necessary, assist with the installation of procurement products at project site on final installation date.</li>
<li>Coordinate logistics and warehousing. Follow up and resolve any delivery issues that might arise.</li>
<li>Arrange shipping, receiving, and inspection. Follow up and resolve any issues related to items damaged during delivery or product errors.</li>
<li>Review all Procurement Specifications on a regular basis with all notes regarding production and delivery of products to stay informed.</li>
<li>Track orders and inform team of any changes or delays.</li>
<li>Review and approve invoices.</li>
<li>Coordinate pre-installation meeting with Owner, Contractor and Install team to review building accessibility, loading and staging area, debris removal, and building restraints.</li>
<li>Manage and supervise installation.</li>
</ul>
<p>Staff Development/Retention</p>
<ul>
<li>Support, encourage, and challenge your team members that are engaged on their projects, so they remain enthusiastic about their roles on the project and within the company.</li>
<li>Identify staff in your team who may show some business acumen and interest toward business development. Work with the Construction Services Manager to provide these identified individuals with opportunities to grow their skills to enhance future market expansion and possibly succession.</li>
<li>Meet with Interior Designers on a monthly basis to review any current issues and to discuss potential means/methods to improve all phases of the procurement process.</li>
<li>Foster and maintain a collaborative professional relationship with the entire Project Team, including Architectural Designers, Project Architects, Project Managers, Interior Designers and Engineers.</li>
</ul>
<p>Project Quality</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain processes and forms for purchasing furniture, artwork, accessories and signage, including quotes, project trackers, owner acceptance letters, procurement packages, and other necessary systems.</li>
<li>Exhibit initiative, eagerness to learn and take on new responsibilities, respond well to mentorship, management, and training, and continuously show professional growth through performance contributions.</li>
<li>Develop good communication with internal team members, clients, and external partners:
<ul>
<li>Effectively articulate procurement ideas to the client and project team.</li>
<li>Maintain contact and clear communication with the client at various levels of project work.</li>
<li>Report schedule and budget deviations to the Construction Services Manager and client on a timely basis.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Present a professional image through appropriate business attire, personal appearance, and ability to carry and project yourself in a professional manner.</li>
<li>Perform a quality control check on all Purchase Orders and Specification Sheets prior to being issued outside the firm.</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of current trends and introductions of furnishings, artwork, accessories and signage.</li>
<li>Follow Progressive?s Project Process standards and best practices.</li>
<li>Act as an ambassador for company-wide values and ethical standards of practice.</li>
<li>Attend professional development activities including ?Lessons Learned? sessions.</li>
</ul>
<p>Financial</p>
<ul>
<li>Seek out new opportunities and contact manufacturers to improve profit margins on wholesale products for improved profits.</li>
<li>Inform Accounting of potential requests for credit applications and list of vendors, if known.</li>
<li>Inform Accounting of potential cash request amounts that will be coming up. Give timelines and amounts of cash requests when known.</li>
<li>Approve all deposit requests prior to check requests to Accounting.</li>
<li>Analyze costs regarding delivery/installation services to determine need to utilize external services or to create a new service in-house to perform the required services.</li>
<li>Track all procurement projects on a weekly basis to assure budget/profit goals are being maintained.</li>
<li>Report all budget/schedule concerns to the Construction Services Manager in a timely fashion to mitigate any losses.</li>
</ul>
<p>Commitment to Client Service</p>
<ul>
<li>Understand the need for professionalism and exhibit a commitment to external and internal client service.</li>
<li>Effectively understand and translate the client?s needs, goals, and important timelines for delivery and final installation of procurement projects.</li>
<li>Stay committed to maintaining your current clients and staying in touch with team members to ensure that clients? needs are being met during the process of your projects.</li>
<li>Provide senior client leadership and project oversight as appropriate, including:
<ul>
<li>Display an excellent understanding of the client?s business drivers, capital planning process, and key performance measures as evidenced by the proven ability to be a strong internal client advocate and strategic business partner.</li>
<li>Display advanced skills at building value by tactfully opening clients? perspectives to unseen risks, missed opportunities, and alternative creative ideas and concepts.</li>
<li>Demonstrate advanced consultative skills and consistently enhance credibility with clients by being an insightful problem-solver and a key resource for achieving client business objectives.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Business Development/Marketing</p>
<ul><li>Provide outstanding client services to Practice Leaders and end user clients, demonstrating marketplace value and instilling confidence with internal business development colleagues to unleash the power of cross-selling procurement services. With this in mind, key activities to support business development include:
<ul>
<li>Maintain contact with Practice Leaders to make sure overall business and profit goals are being met.</li>
<li>Work with Practice Leaders, Construction Services Manager, and Marketing to create a plan and materials for sales opportunities and more global marketing efforts.</li>
<li>Communicate our Procurement capacity and process to other staff including Interior Designers, Architectural Designers, and Project Architects.</li>
<li>Maintain contact with Practice Leaders to ensure that Procurement services are being offered on most or all of projects sold.</li>
<li>Collaborate in early involvement in proposals and contract negotiations to establish scope of work, schedule, and budget.</li>
<li>Ensure prompt and timely response to all proposal requests.</li>
<li>Actively and creatively identify new business opportunities and develop new client relationships along with maintaining established relationships. Listen for and seek out new projects which may exist or are being formulated.</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
