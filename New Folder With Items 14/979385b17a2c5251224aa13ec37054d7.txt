<p></p>MaritzCX is looking for a Marketing Communications Specialist to work part-time (20-28 hours per week, Monday-Friday) in our Lehi, Utah office.
<br>
<br>
The communications specialist supports the Marketing team and company initiatives and will be provided the opportunity to learn about Customer Experience in a fun, friendly environment.
<p></p>
<p>Required Capabilities include:
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>An Excellent Writer: </b>The ability to create clear, concise and dynamic content to be used in various channels: Maritzcx.com, blog posts, client stories, emails, webinar invitations, collateral and enablement material.
</li>
<li>
<b>A Passionate Story Teller: </b>Passionate about, telling a story, and understands how to use a specific tone of voice when communicating a brand. Passionate about storyboarding and presenting a message- not afraid to dive in and learn.
</li>
<li>
<b>A Fast Mover &amp; Team Player: </b>Someone that can contribute to the conversation and brainstorm sessions, interview subject matter experts, dig in and do research about a topic, bring together a million minutia to make sure we’re creating helpful, creative content. Someone that helps drive our marketing efforts around thought leadership, demand generation, and industry events.
</li>
<li>
<b>A Visual Thinker: </b>A person with a creative mind who enjoys creating a good PowerPoint deck, loves to brainstorm new content ideas, and loves to be active on social media.
</li>
</ul>
