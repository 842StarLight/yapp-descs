<h2>Leasing Consultant 3106/3107</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Arlington Properties, a progressive and growing property management company headquartered in Birmingham, Alabama, is seeking a Leasing Consultant for a 408-unit multifamily community in Hoover, Alabama. We pride ourselves on providing our residents with best-in-class service through impeccable property management and emphasis on resident relations. We are seeking a self-motivated, goal-oriented multifamily professional with proven success in sales and marketing to join our team; prior leasing and/or real estate sales experience is desirable, but not required. As the key liaison between management and our current and prospective residents, this individual must be friendly, outgoing and service-oriented. If you are a results-driven professional who would enjoy the challenge of this fast-paced environment, we want to hear from you! Some weekend work is required.</p>
<p><b>Job Overview</b></p>
<p>Title: Leasing Consultant</p>
<p>Department: Property Management</p>
<p>Reports To: Community Manager</p>
<p>Supervises: None</p>
<p>Job Classification: Hourly, Non-Exempt</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>The Leasing Consultant acts as the Company’s representative during interactions with prospective and current residents to achieve maximum occupancy and NOI, and is responsible for the leasing process from introduction to the actual occupancy of the resident and maintains communication throughout the tenancy of the resident.</p>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops positive rapport with current and prospective residents.</li>
<li>Provides information about the apartments and the community to prospective residents and schedules visits to the community.</li>
<li>Answers leads via telephone and internet, and greets visitors in person to determine whether the community meets the client’s qualifications.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining an acceptable closing ratio.</li>
<li>Effectively explains all community policies and lease terms to new and current residents.</li>
<li>Accurately and efficiently prepares and assists with all leasing paperwork (applications, lease agreements and addenda, employment and credit checks, and leasing move-in packets.)</li>
<li>Collects security deposits, rent and other funds associated with resident move-ins and tenancy.</li>
<li>Supports overall marketing and advertising efforts; monitors advertising and effectiveness of results.</li>
<li>Understands and complies with federal, state and local landlord-tenant and Fair Housing laws.</li>
<li>Inspects amenities, common areas, and vacant apartments regularly to ensure that the are ready to show to prospective residents.</li>
<li>Attends and assists in the coordination and organization of resident relations functions and events.</li>
<li>Assists the Community Manager in implementing resident retention and renewal programs.</li>
<li>Drafts service requests and communicates them to the maintenance department as directed.</li>
<li>Performs other tasks as assigned.</li>
<li>Works effectively and efficiently under productivity deadlines; communicates professionally, respectfully, and courteously with supervisors, colleagues, vendors, and customers.</li>
<li>Must be regular and predictable in attendance and available for weekend work.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>A high school diploma or equivalent is required. A college degree or related coursework in sales, marketing, communications, or property management is desirable. Leasing or hospitality experience preferred. Customer service experience is desirable. Excellent communication and organization skills are necessary. Basic arithmetic skills and computer literacy are required.</p>
