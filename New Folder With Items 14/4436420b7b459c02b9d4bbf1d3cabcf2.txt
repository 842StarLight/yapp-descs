<h2>Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About Avison Young</b></p>
<p>Avison Young is the world’s fastest-growing commercial real estate services firm. Headquartered in Toronto, Canada, Avison Young is a collaborative, global firm owned and operated by its principals. Founded in 1978, the company provides value-added, client-centric investment sales, leasing, advisory, management, financing and mortgage placement services to owners and occupiers of office, retail, industrial, multi-family and hospitality properties.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>This Graphic Designer will be responsible for the design, style and look of multiple types of marketing materials. Reporting to the Senior Director, Tri-State Marketing, the graphic designer will create proposals, presentations, custom property flyers, offering memoranda, mini-websites, print and electronic materials. Candidates must be a creative self-starter with a strong work ethic, who thrives in a fast-paced, deadline driven environment.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide graphical support and assistance in the coordination of any aspect of the internal and external marketing of Avison Young and its professionals throughout the region</li>
<li>Create and produce business development materials and tools, including presentations, flyers, pitches, brochures, proposals, and offering memoranda</li>
<li>Create and assemble collateral materials for clients and prospects</li>
<li>Work with other members of the Tri-State region marketing team to produce and create materials</li>
<li>Utilize graphic and web-based design tools that include, but are not limited to, Adobe Creative Suite and Microsoft Suite to produce innovative materials, presentations and collateral</li>
<li>Produce marketing materials for assignments, including advertising campaigns and marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Update existing customized websites for property marketing</li>
<li>Strive to innovate within brand standard guidelines and established operational procedures</li>
<li>Take on full ownership of assigned projects</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent relevant experience</li>
<li>At least 3-5+ years of experience in a related field, commercial real estate experience a plus</li>
<li>Excellent Adobe Creative Suite skills (InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, etc.) and Microsoft Office (specifically Power Point)</li>
<li>Outstanding written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills with the ability to multi-task with multiple projects and deadlines</li>
<li>Work independently with minimal direct supervision as well as in a team environment</li>
<li>Ability to exercise judgment, tact, and diplomacy</li>
<li>Ability to communicate expectations in order to meet deadlines</li>
<li>An outstanding customer service attitude</li>
</ul>
