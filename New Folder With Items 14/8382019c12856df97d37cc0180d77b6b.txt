<p>ABOUT SNP<br>
Founded in 1992, SNP is a leadership communications company. Our mission is to<br>
make our customer’s message clear and memorable. We accomplish this through<br>
<i>content </i>support, communications <i>coaching, </i>and <i>creative </i>development. We recognize<br>
that no two customers are the same and our approach can’t be either. This means that<br>
SNP’ers are adaptable and creative, and must thrive in our fast-paced, collaborative<br>
work environment.
</p>
<p>ABOUT THE ROLE
</p>
<p>As a NYC-based Account Director, you’ll be responsible for sales growth within<br>
existing customer accounts, generating new business, and leveraging your<br>
established network of connections with companies relevant to our space (from early<br>
start-ups through multinational enterprise).
</p>
<p>You must be service-oriented at the core and appreciate the importance of a<br>
consultative, human-approach to sales.
</p>
<p>You will work directly with the VP of Sales who is based in San Francisco. You are<br>
inspired by the idea of a team-approach to scaling our business and bringing the SNP<br>
values and products to every customer relationship.
</p>
<p>You’re responsible for:
</p>
<ul><li>Taking a strategic approach to developing the East Coast market, with a</li></ul>
focus on NYC.
<ul><li>Qualifying inbound leads through detailed discovery and nurturing them</li></ul>
through the sales process to a successful close.
<ul><li>Partnering with our Strategic Account Managers to identify new</li></ul>
opportunities within existing accounts.
<ul><li>Seeking out new sources of revenue through a creative outbound approach.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Generating proposals and presenting them to prospective customers, which</li></ul>
requires a thorough understanding of customer needs.
<ul><li>Meeting and exceeding quarterly targets.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Good CRM hygiene to drive sales insights and keep the business informed.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partnering with senior leadership to design our program offerings in new</li></ul>
ways that continue to enhance our “best of the best” standards.
<ul><li>Working with Creative and Operations teams to ensure clear communication</li></ul>
and seamless delivery of services to the customer.</div></div>
