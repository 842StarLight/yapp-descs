<h2>Franchise Recruiter</h2>

<p><b>PIRTEK – B2B Industrial Based Franchise</b></p>
<p>PIRTEK is a unique business-to-business franchise and is one of the few industrial franchise business opportunities available. Wherever there’s industry, there’s demand for onsite hydraulic and industrial hose replacement services by PIRTEK. With over 30 years of franchising experience, the PIRTEK service-based business focuses on reducing equipment downtime by repairing and maintaining hydraulic and pneumatic powered machines.</p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>We're seeking an experienced, energetic, self-motivated sales associate with strong communication skills to assist in our expansion throughout the U.S. This role would communicate with potential franchisees (mostly by phone) to gather important information and help qualify leads.</p>
<p><b>Roles &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand and articulate the systems and programs for each of our two franchise tiers.</li>
<li>Lead candidates through a multi-stage process to educate them about our franchise opportunities and obtain approval as franchisees.</li>
<li>Assist existing franchisees in recruiting qualified buyers for their locations.</li>
<li>Give guidance to existing franchisees looking to sell their businesses.</li>
<li>Work with independent business brokers.</li>
<li>Primary point of contact for approved franchisees and work with internal departments to complete transactions after initial franchise approval.</li>
<li>Exceed aggressive sales goals to support the growth of our franchise business.</li>
<li>Maintain and update sales pipeline daily.</li>
<li>Educate the prospects about the brand, culture, vision and values · Diligently respond and follow-up with all franchise inquires</li>
<li>Effectively close sales and meet company sales targets</li>
<li>Assist with developing marketing initiatives that drive qualified prospects · Provide relevant feedback to executive management on sales processes, forecasting and tools</li>
<li>Identify, interview and qualify interested Franchise candidates (new and existing) within an approved market to achieve the current fiscal year’s business plans.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications </b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 3 years’ experience in franchise sales, business brokerage, or related field is required.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business administration preferred but not required.</li>
<li>Extensive sales experience.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>High comfort level interacting with franchisees and franchise candidates via telephone and email.</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Office Suite.</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce, ACT, FranConnect, or similar lead tracking software (CRM).</li>
<li>Good understanding of start-up operations and financials.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to collaborate with other members in the team.</li>
</ul>
