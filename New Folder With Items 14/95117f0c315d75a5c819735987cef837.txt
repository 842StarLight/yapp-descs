<h2>Sales Manager-Codefuel</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>About the Job</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Your knowledge of online media combined with your communication skills contribute greatly to CodeFuel's sales team. Using your influencing and relationship-building skills, you'll provide excellent client service, research and market analysis. You anticipate how decisions are made, persistently explore and uncover the business needs of CodeFuel's key clients and understand how our product can grow their business. Working with them, you set the vision and the strategy for how their advertising can reach users.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
At CodeFuel, you will take initiative in fine-tuning small details, without losing sight of the big picture. You'll get things done and you'll view obstacles as an exciting challenge that demand a creative solution. Above all else, you will take pride in your work and be inspired and motivated by your role in improving the way people drive worldwide.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are looking for individuals who are familiar with the world of monetization and who are self-starters, team players and ready to be part of a dynamic environment.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Generate business plans to deliver growth expectations of an assigned territory. Develop defined strategy/tactics and needs analysis in order to execute</li>
<li>
Analyze, define, and manage business growth requirements while working closely with peers in Sales team</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with teams to drive growth with new and existing customers while working to prospect, qualify, grow, and maintain a territory account list proactively</li>
<li>
Identify client business needs and proactively develop customized marketing solutions to meet client objectives</li>
<li>
Maintain awareness of relevant market knowledge and trends, including but not limited to; evolving technologies and their management, client needs, expectations and opportunities, shifts in the competitive landscape, and operational efficiencies</li>
<li>
Develop and grow relationships and build presence with agency executives and clients</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Minimum Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Experience in sales across a range of industry verticals</li>
<li>
Experience working and selling to companies in the hi-tech, web publishing, mobile applications and/or software field required</li>
<li>
Experience presenting and selling media solutions to clients and agencies</li>
<li>
Compelling presentation skills with a natural ability to hold strategic conversations and analyze audiences to change tactics</li>
<li>
Experience in presenting to C-level executives</li>
<li>
Must have a distinguished track record of sales success</li>
<li>
Able to work effectively and smoothly with a strong international team</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Experience and interest in being part of a high growth business</li>
<li>
Experience in marketing, brand advertising, and storytelling</li>
<li>
Established relationships and presence within the marketplace</li>
<li>
Ability to work towards strategic goals and business objectives</li>
<li>
Ability to drive and implement ideas in a changing environment</li>
<li>
Excellent business judgment, creative problem-solving, and decision-making skills</li>
<li>
Excellent project management, communications, presentation, interpersonal, and organizational skills</li>
</ul>
