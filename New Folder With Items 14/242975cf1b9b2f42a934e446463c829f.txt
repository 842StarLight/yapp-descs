<h2>Senior Learning & Development Specialist - Business Emphasis</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>PRIMARY FUNCTION:</b></p>
<p>
This role is responsible for designing, developing and delivering engaging training that supports VACU’s Mission and growth. This position will work with employees and internal business units to provide learning solutions that support key initiatives, identify knowledge gaps and strengthen employee skillsets. Member Business Service training topics will be a primary focus for this role.</p>

<li>
Promotional training</li>

<p><b>
JOB QUALIFICATIONS:
 </b></p>
<p><b>Knowledge:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Broad understanding of techniques and philosophies of adult learning</li>
<li>
Comprehensive knowledge of business service deposit and loan products and processes</li>
<li>
Comprehensive knowledge of financial institution functions, philosophy, products, services and delivery channels</li>
<li>
In-depth knowledge of a financial institution operations preferred</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong PC skills to include proficiency in Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint</li>
<li>
Experience with financial software applications and loan systems preferred</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, both written, oral, and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Self-motivated with strong multi-tasking, organizational, and time management skills</li>
<li>
Works independently and interdependently</li>
<li>
Works effectively with all levels of employees and exercises good business judgment</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrates an aptitude for conducting training courses covering a wide array of operational and employee development topics</li>
<li>
Demonstrates adaptability and strong problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Exhibits self-motivation and being a team player</li>
<li>
Builds strong working relationships, internal and external to the organization</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Minimum Education and Experience:
</b></p>
<p>Prefer four year college degree in education, business or HRD development. Prior training experience with formal classroom training is preferred. Prior experience with business services, to include comprehensive knowledge of business deposit and loan products and processes is required. Experience with financial institution operations preferred.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p>
This job requires sitting and standing for long periods of time. This job requires infrequent lifting or carrying of objects up to 10 pounds.</p>
