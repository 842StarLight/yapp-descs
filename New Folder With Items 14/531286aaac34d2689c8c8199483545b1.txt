<p><b>Company Background</b></p>
<p>
Paul Davis Restoration, Inc. is a national organization that provides property damage emergency services, restoration and reconstruction services for residential and commercial properties due to water, fire, mold, storm and other disasters. The Paul Davis Network is made up of more than 350 franchises and Company Owned Operations across the US and Canada. Since 1966, we have helped more than 2 million property owners restore their properties and lives. Paul Davis Restoration, Inc. is owned by First Service Corporation, a global leader in the rapidly growing real estate services sector. Visit www.pauldavis.com for more information.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties/Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p><i>A Business Development Representative will be responsible for growing awareness of Paul Davis and building relationships in the community with organizations that utilize restoration services.</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Build key relationships within the construction and restoration community to generate business leads</li>
<li>Cold call prospective business by email, phone call and in person introductions</li>
<li>Possess understanding of product positioning and marketing</li>
<li>Maintain a sense of urgency in creating and following up with leads</li>
<li>Communicate the various service offerings to potential customers</li>
<li>Attend business related events within the community</li>
<li>Serve as the lead for special campaigns and assignments</li>
<li>Compile and communicate progress reports</li>
<li>Coordinate and participate in seminars, tradeshows and other industry events</li>
<li>Develop a business plan in partnership with Paul Davis leadership to achieve profitability goals</li>
<li>Submit and maintain a log of weekly activities and meetings scheduled</li>
<li>Participate in company meetings, trainings and conferences</li>
</ul>
