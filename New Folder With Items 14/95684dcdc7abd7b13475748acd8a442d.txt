<h2>Solution Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h3>
<p></p>
<p>With more than $150M in funding, 100% year over year growth for the past five years and a recent billion-dollar valuation, Seismic is the leader in the enterprise sales enablement software space. Using leading edge cloud technologies and web enabled solutions, we are innovating and solving complex problems to help the world’s top companies improve collaboration and content delivery between marketing and sales. Headquartered in San Diego and with more than 600 employees across the globe, Seismic is privately held by its executive team and investment firms General Atlantic, JMI Equity, Jackson Square Ventures, Lightspeed Venture Partners, and T. Rowe Price.</p>
<br>
<b>
Solution Consultant Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Partner with clients to help them understand Seismic’s software and implementation best practices</li>
<li>Consult with the client to develop solutions that leverage Seismic’s Content Management System and LiveDoc capabilities</li>
<li>Review and analyze client content and collateral to determine organizational methodologies</li>
<li>Understand business and content-related problems to identify and present solutions</li>
<li>Lead the creation of production dynamic document templates for new and existing clients</li>
<li>Provide support and ongoing services for existing clients in accordance with SLAs</li>
<li>Assist with conducting on-site and virtual training with clients as necessary</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Solution Consultant Requirements</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to understand a client’s existing content structure and use-cases to help guide the client through implementation</li>
<li>Extensive experience with organization methodologies for large volumes of content including folder architectures and metadata</li>
<li>Critical thinking, analysis, troubleshooting, and problem solving expertise</li>
<li>Ability to architect solutions based on customer requirements using the capabilities of Seismic</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency with the entire Microsoft Office Suite, with an emphasis on Excel, Word and PowerPoint</li>
<li>3+ years of experience in management consulting, asset management marketing, sales operations, or a related field</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills with proven ability to initiate and manage relationships with customers at all levels</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of data systems and structures</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Proficiency with Salesforce.com, and a solid understanding of Salesforce Object structure a plus</li>
<li>Prior experience with SaaS a plus</li>
</ul>
