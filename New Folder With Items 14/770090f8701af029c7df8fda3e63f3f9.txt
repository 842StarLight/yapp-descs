<h2>Federal VMware Inside Product Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Dell is hiring and we are looking to grow our Dell VMware Team for Federal VMware Inside Sales Product Specialist (VMware Software Solutions). Position is available in Round Rock, TX</p>
<p></p>
<p>
If you have a strong sales and technical background in building relationships and a true passion for selling and acquiring new accounts to provide best solution for our customers…We would love to hear from you!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Description</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Effectively articulates the value proposition associated with dell and VMware’s products and services to Federal customers.</li>
<li>
Recommends business solutions considering customer needs and Dell interests.</li>
<li>
Builds relationships with customers based on knowledge of Dell’s technology, products, and services.</li>
<li>
Stays abreast of current industry trends and how Dell’s customers are being impacted to help solve specific market challenges in targeted industries.</li>
<li>
Leverages cross-functional resources to achieve results/meet customer needs.</li>
<li>
Effectively prioritizes multiple demands while ensuring customer needs are met.</li>
<li>
Makes decision based on an appropriate amount of information/data analysis<p></p>

Principal Accountabilities</b></p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Provides product or BU specific expertise.</li>
<li>
Responsible for selling a defined set of products and services.</li>
<li>
May own account sets and/or be called into accounts as opportunities are identified.</li>
<li>
Acts as a technical resource to support the sales organization to meet and/or exceed their objectives.</li>
<li>
Identifies customer needs/requirements, and recommends the appropriate products and solutions.</li>
<li>
Remains up-to-date on new product, technology, and services offerings.</li>
<li>
Stays current on industry and vertical solutions as well.</li>
<li>
Assists in the on-going technical education and development of the broader team.</li>

</li>
