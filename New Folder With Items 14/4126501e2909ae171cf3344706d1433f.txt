<p><b>1 SCOPE
</b></p>
<p>The Manufacturing Engineer I position provides support and assistance to manufacturing
</p>
<p>engineering. This is an entry level position in manufacturing engineering. This is a salaried and
</p>
<p>exempt position.
</p>
<p><b>1.1 Reporting
</b></p>
<p>This position reports to the Manufacturing Engineering Manager.
</p>
<p><b>2 RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<p>The Manufacturing Engineer I is responsible for supporting various manufacturing-engineering
</p>
<p>functions within the corporation, including process design, manufacturing documentation,
</p>
<p>production tooling design, failure analyses, process improvement, and sales-quotation support.
</p>
<p><b>2.1 Duties
 </b></p>
<p>Read, interpret, and clarify a wide variety of customer documentation including drawings,<br>
work instructions, and miscellaneous specifications.
 </p>
<p>Create manufacturing processes to effect optimal efficiency and precision of finished goods.<br>
 Create and update manufacturing documentation, which is clear and concise and can be
</p>
<p>utilized by typical manufacturing personnel.
 </p>
<p>Evaluate customer returned material (RMA) and create written evaluation reports.<br>
 Assist with identification and implementation of processes improvements to for the sake of
</p>
<p>increased efficiency and/or improved product performance and specifications.
 </p>
<p>Implement solutions to manufacturing problems and manufacturability problems. Suggest<br>
design changes when applicable.
 </p>
<p>Prepare quality planning documentation<br>
 Redline drawings for accuracy as needed<br>
 Prepare technical reports and responses to corrective action requests and customer returned
</p>
<p>material.
 </p>
<p>Evaluate non-conformances and proposed resolutions<br>
 Modify or create work instructions for products based on observations of the product build.<br>
 Perform and/or coordinate internal audit activities for the quality management system<br>
 Other duties as assigned
</p>
<p><b>3 QUALITY &amp; PERFORMANCE
</b></p>
<p>Performance metrics for this position are based on accuracy, thoroughness, and productivity. In
</p>
<p>addition, the ability to manage multiple priorities to timely completion is a critical element.
</p>
<p><b>4 REQUIRED SKILLS &amp; EDUCATION
 </b></p>
<p>Proven troubleshooting, corrective action and analytical skills and abilities relating to non-<br>
conforming material and quality issues.
 </p>
<p>Proven ability with troubleshooting and analyzing Complex processes.
 <br>
2 year Engineering degree or equivalent experience<br>
 Excellent written and verbal communication skills<br>
 Ability to clearly communicate and resolve issues with a wide variety of people and demands.<br>
 Must demonstrate excellent problem-solving and decision-making skills
</p>
