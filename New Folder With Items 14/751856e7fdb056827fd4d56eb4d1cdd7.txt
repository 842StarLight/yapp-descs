<h2>Account Executive, Growth</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">JW Player is looking to add a talented, self-starting, and high energy Growth Account Executive to our team to apply their skills and experience to drive revenue growth in our New York Sales team. The ideal candidate is a strong communicator who will leverage their exceptional prospecting skills to identify opportunities to generate pipeline while managing the entire sales cycle to bring those opportunities to a successful close.
<br><br>
The Growth Account Executive position offers an opportunity to learn from other successful JW Player Account Executives and is a direct path to next-level Account Executive roles that close larger deals.
<br><br>
We value creative, dedicated and intelligent employees. JW Player demands a lot and requires top-notch performers that can deliver quality results.
<br><br>
<b>In this role you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Confidently convey JW Player's value proposition and be able to react to competitive questions and FAQ's</li>
<li>Perform prospecting research and prospecting outreach (phone calls, emails, etc.) that result in closed deals</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales and marketing leadership to develop and execute on a prospecting plan of attack, including call scripts, audience segmentation and approach</li>
<li>Maintain and improve CRM data within Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Actively manage leads pipeline and campaigns in order to meet goals and quarterly objectives</li>
<li>Collaborate with all areas of the organization to optimize team productivity</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS or equivalent experience</li>
<li>1+ years experience generating new SaaS sales or other customer-facing roles within the B2B, Technology and/or SaaS selling experience</li>
<li>1-2 years experience as a Sales Development Representative or Junior Account Executive, or similar</li>
<li>High-volume prospecting experience via email, cold calls, etc.</li>
<li>Outstanding communication, time management, and organizational skills</li>
<li>Passion for new innovative technology</li>
<li>Results-oriented with a sense of urgency and passion for success</li>
<li>Ability to deliver expected results in driving pipeline growth for SaaS-based deals</li>
<li>Comfortable analyzing sales data and utilizing sales tools, including Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>High ethical standards and level of integrity</li>
</ul>
<b>Bonus Points:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience in media technology and/or video industry</li>
</ul>
