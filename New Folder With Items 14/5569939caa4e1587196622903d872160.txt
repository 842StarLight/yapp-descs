We are growing at a very rapid pace, have a great culture and everyone works together as a team. We have developed an atmosphere and platform where motivated, success driven professionals looking for a long-term career have an opportunity to thrive.</p>

<p>
As a result of our recent transition from NetSuite to Salesforce, we are aggressively pursuing at least one Salesforce Developer to join our Internal Information Systems team. This role would work closely with various key internal stakeholders across Sales and Customer Support, Marketing, Technology, Analytics and Executive teams. You would also play a key role in transitioning out of the implementation phase and working with out external partner to manage the process. As we have transitioned to Salesforce from NetSuite, previous experience with NetSuite is highly preferred.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Functional knowledge of Salesforce data structure and understanding of how to leverage the tool to meet complex process and reporting requirements</li>
<li>
Deliver support and solutions for break/fix issues</li>
<li>
Communicate and collaborate with other technical resources and stakeholders regarding status, technical issues and creative solutions</li>
<li>
Perform reviews of data to develop customized solutions within the Salesforce platform to support critical business functions and meet project objectives, client requirements and company goals</li>
<li>
Manage daily support and maintenance of internal Salesforce instance, and conduct long-term improvement operations to ensure compatibility with evolving mission requirements</li>
<li>
Collaborate and follow-up with various internal departments, including marketing, product development and operations, to ensure Salesforce environment supports internal needs relating to functionality and performance</li>
<li>
Lead testing and implementation of software develop efforts including coding, configuration, maintenance, installation, testing, and debugging</li>
<li>
Assist in data import/export/updates, system customization</li>
<li>
Experience documenting and analyzing processes, procedures, and/or policies</li>
<li>
Customize fields, page layouts, record types, reports and dashboards</li>
<li>
Design, configure, and develop custom code/objects</li>
</ul>
