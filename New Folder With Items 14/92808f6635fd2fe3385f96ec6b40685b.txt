<h2>Staff Accountant/AP</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Who We Are:</p>
<p>
At Signiant our intelligent file movement software helps the world’s top content creators and distributors ensure fast, secure delivery of large files over public and private networks. Built on Signiant’s patented technology, the company’s on-premises software and SaaS solutions move petabytes of high-value data every day between users, applications and systems with proven ease.</p>
<p>
Why We Need You:</p>
<p>
We are growing and looking for an experienced Staff Accountant with General Ledger and Accounts Payable experience. This role reports to the Assistant Controller. The ideal candidate will possess a high level of attention to detail as well as the ability to multi-task and juggle priorities. The position requires someone who is articulate, professional, and who understands the ins and outs of wearing multiple hats at a growing organization.</p>
<p>
What You Get:</p>
<p>
Opportunity to work with small team and gain exposure to many facets of accounting/finance. Be ready to roll up your sleeves and help grow the accounting/finance team.</p>
<p>
What You’ll Do:</p>
<ul>
<li>Full-cycle AP: key invoices, process payments, maintain vendor records, vendor inquiries</li>
<li>Month end close process; including creation and posting of journal entries and account reconciliations</li>
<li>Identify, code, and manage fixed assets</li>
<li>Administer expense report system (Expensify) ensuring conformity with company policies</li>
<li>Credit Card processing; including working with card holders at month end to record expenses accurately</li>
<li>Purchase Order tracking</li>
<li>Record and amortize prepaid expenses</li>
<li>Compliance and regulatory filings, e.g., sales/use tax, 1099s, etc.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul> <p>What You’ll Bring:</p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years Accounts Payable or accounting experience desired</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Accounting or Finance;
</li>
<li>Analytical thinker and problem solver who can multitask and adapt to changing priorities</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal, organizational, and meticulous analytical/documentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and be resourceful in obtaining information when it is not readily available</li>
</ul>
