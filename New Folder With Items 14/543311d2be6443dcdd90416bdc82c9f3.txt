<h2>CSR - Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Vogue Cleaners is looking for experienced Customer Service Representatives to join our team. Candidates for these positions should posses strong communication skills, and a firm understanding of what it takes to provide exceptional customer service.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Interacting with customers for dropping off &amp; picking up of garments.</li>
<li>Managing phone call inquiries, &amp; in person customer requests.</li>
<li>Accurately tagging, &amp; describing garments to be cleaned &amp; pressed.</li>
<li>Utilizing our point of sale software to process transactions.</li>
<li>Accurately assembling &amp; racking garments for customer pickup.</li>
<li>Maintaining the organization and cleanliness of work areas.</li>
<li>Working with management to stay updated on garment knowledge &amp; company policies.</li>
<li>Opening &amp; or closing of the store.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to remain professional &amp; courteous with customers at all times.</li>
<li>Strong verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze &amp; trouble shoot.</li>
<li>Consistent &amp; reliable form of transportation.</li>
<li>Effective time management skills.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Types: Full-time, Part-time</p>
