<b>Mortgage Originator Associate</b>
<br>
<br>
<b>Elmira Savings Bank</b> is seeking a<b> Mortgage Originator Associate</b> to support our Mortgage Originations team in the Ithaca, NY area. The Primary role of the Mortgage Originator Associate is to support the Mortgage Originators by helping customers during the mortgage process - performing administrative and relationship building tasks.
<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate for this role is a recent college graduate or entry level professional with previous mortgage, banking, or administrative experience and a strong desire to become successful in mortgage operations or mortgage sales.
<br>
<br>
<b>Mortgage Originator Associate</b> <b>Essential Duties</b>
<ul>
<li><i>Track referrals, update data base and maintain contact with customers, Realtors and Attorneys.</i></li>
<li><i>Attend various events with Originators including; open houses, homebuyer classes, client closings, networking and community events, etc.</i></li>
<li><i>Obtain necessary information and update applicants during the mortgage process.</i></li>
<li>
I<i>nput loan data, scan and upload files to credit department for processing and review..</i>
</li>
<li><i>Manage Client and Real Estate Agent Portals.</i></li>
<li><i>Process loan information for credit and automated underwriting.</i></li>
<li><i>Assist with coordination of client meetings, various presentations and data collection.</i></li>
<li><i>Coordinate the Mortgage Originators schedule.</i></li>
<li>
<i>Provide coverage for processors and Mortgage Originators as needed.</i>
</li>
</ul>
<b>Basic Qualifications</b>
<ul>
<li>Must have ability to effectively communicate mortgage requirements to clients, processors, and referral partners.</li>
<li>Must be eligible and willing to become NMLS licensed within the first six months of employment.</li>
<li>The Mortgage Originator Associate must have excellent customer service skills, positive attitude and the ability to work independently as well as in a team environment are required.</li>
<li>Previous lending experience is preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with Mortgage Builder software is desirable.</li>
<li>Previous experience with Conventional, FHA, VA, USDA, Construction and SONYMA mortgages is desirable.</li>
<li>Must be pro-active and initiate action to correct problems as necessary.</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented and have the ability to prioritize tasks in a logical manner.</li>
</ul>
