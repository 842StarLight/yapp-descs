<p><b>
RingCentral, Inc., </b>a leading provider of Unified Communications as a Service (UCaaS), fosters a dynamic, success-driven, and team-oriented environment committed to delivering simple and delightful products for our customers. We are looking for a candidate with an entrepreneurial spark and a passion for redefining how people communicate.</p>
<p>
The RingCentral environment is dynamic, success-driven, team-oriented and committed to providing world class service for its customers. Do you have the ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment? We are looking for candidates with an entrepreneurial spark!</p>

<p><b>
Job Description</b></p>
<p>
We’ve got the best product in the market. Can you help our millions of users get the most out of it? We’re looking for an expert in all aspects of retention marketing to join our team. Our ideal candidate is a creative, analytical, and curious self-starter who can multitask and lead. You’ll need to balance strategic thinking, tactical execution, and a love of the customer journey to drive targeted in-app, email, and rep-centric programs to increase adoption and retention. Can you pull people and ideas together to help our most valuable asset, our amazing customers, get the most out of their investment in RingCentral?</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maximize our customers’ return on investment in RingCentral solutions by enabling them to utilize all the benefits of being a RingCentral customer</li>
<li>
Own the strategy, development, and execution of targeted marketing programs to increase app and feature adoption</li>
<li>
Take a holistic view of the customer journey to drive automated activation, nurture, and growth initiatives to improve retention and reduce churn</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Own automated dashboards to measure and report on campaign progress and effectiveness.</li>
<li>
Analyze project performance, derive insights, and recommend actions to continuously improve performance.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Lead and influence a cross-functional teams consisting of sales, marketing, professional services, customer success and product</li>
<li>
Regularly present to internal leaders on strategy and program experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>

You support what is right for the customer while driving significant financial contribution to the company. You’re comfortable navigating data and measuring success, and have the following skills and experience:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>

Bachelor’s degree, Master’s degree a plus</li>
<li>
At least 7 years of customer marketing and/or customer success experience. B2B and SaaS experience a plus</li>
<li>

<li>
Strong writing and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Results driven with a keen analytical ability to turn insights into action</li>
<li>
Highly motivated with a sense of urgency and willingness to adjust in a fast-paced, team environment</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask, prioritize and problem solve. Excellent time management skills</li>
<li>
Creative self-starter with a can-do attitude. Can work independently, self-reliant, and self-motivated</li>
<li>
Demonstrated leadership ability with experience in hiring and developing talent</li>
<li>
Extensive experience working with Sales, Professional Services, Customer Success, Marketing and Creative teams.</li>
<li>
A strong track record of driving scalable and impactful programs to increase adoption and utilization</li>
