<h2>Salesforce Administrator/Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Amgen is committed to unlocking the potential of biology for patients suffering from serious illnesses by developing innovative human therapeutics. Amgen focuses on areas of high unmet medical need and strives for solutions that improve health outcomes and dramatically improve people’s lives. A biotechnology pioneer since 1980, Amgen has grown to be one of the world’s leading independent biotechnology companies, has reached millions of patients around the world and is developing a pipeline of medicines with breakaway potential. At Amgen, our mission is simple: to serve patients.
<br><br>
As part of this mission, the Amgen information technology team is seeking a Salesforce Administrator/Analyst who will be based onsite in Tampa, FL. to help build out our new Salesforce Platform team. This is a unique opportunity to help Amgen build a global Salesforce platform supporting all business functions across the enterprise. This groundbreaking team will help drive our Salesforce strategy and framework to provide enablement, adoption and technical expertise which accelerate Amgen's core business.
<br><br>
This team presents an amazing opportunity to use your existing Salesforce skills and gain new ones through hands on application. As an organization dedicated to improving the quality of life for people, Amgen fosters an inclusive environment of diverse, ethical, committed people who respect each other but compete intensely to win. Together, we live the Amgen values as we continue advancing science to serve patients.
<br><br>
The Salesforce Administrator will report to the Director Information Systems based at Amgen’s Capability Center in Tampa, FL. At Amgen, our mission is simple: to serve patients. Our new Tampa Capability Center provides essential services that enable us to better pursue this mission. This state-of-the-art center serves as a base for finance, information systems, and human resources professionals to make a meaningful impact at one of the world’s leading biotechnology companies.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
Contribute to the platform vision advising stakeholders on the use of Salesforce technologies
<br><br>
Work in an agile team to mature platform capabilities though backlog grooming, sprint planning and other agile ceremonies
<br><br>
Provide guidance to cross-functional teams on the platform methods and best practices such as: application and integration development methodologies, architecture standards, functional and technical solutions, environment management, testing, and platform education
<br><br>
Solve key implementation issues and demonstrate ability to work towards successful resolution
<br><br>
Lead the evaluation of business and technical requirements and provide guidance on solution architecture that aligns with the overall Salesforce Platform strategy
<br><br>
Review releases and roadmaps from Salesforce and evaluate the impacts to current applications, orgs, and solutions
<br><br>
Develop and support platform admin activities including data integrity, user account maintenance, reports &amp; dashboards, and workflows
<br><br>
Negotiate solutions to complex problems with both internal teams and third-party service providers
<br><br>
Build relationships and work with cross-functional teams; contribute to broader goals and growth beyond the scope of a single role
<br><br>
Communicate and lead platform changes including upstream and downstream impact (from Salesforce) to appropriate partners such as project teams and user groups
<br><br>
Deploy applications into a secure and scalable production environment and help teams understand production landscape, integrations, and release processes
<br><br>
