<b>Business Analyst</b><br>
<br>
PioneerRx Pharmacy Software in Irving, Tx is seeking candidates for the position of Business Analyst. The Business Analyst must be a highly motivated individual who is eager and ready to accept additional responsibilities when needed. The ideal candidate has a friendly demeanor, the ability to learn new technology, can multitask, and complete projects in a timely manner. This person should demonstrate exceptional customer service and problem-solving skills.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>At PioneerRx you will:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Track sales goals, monitor the sales process for data integrity, and recommend improvements for the customer journey for two sales teams.</li>
<li>Build relationships and own the follow up/tracking of special projects with our external business partners and internal initiatives with the VP of Business Development.</li>
<li>Participate in meetings with customers, vendors, and strategic partners along with the executive team.</li>
<li>Schedule and coordinate conference calls, meetings, and webinars with internal executive team and business partners in order to keep initiatives on track.</li>
<li>Review implementation goals and pending go-live customers with the implementation manager and coordination team.</li>
<li>Take pride and initiative in delivering consistent, high-quality work. Exhibit a high level of awareness, attention to detail and follow-through.</li>
<li>Balance multiple priorities, meet deadlines and problem-solve quickly, in a fast-paced environment. Anticipate needs without instruction.</li>
<li>Build collaborative relationships internally and externally. Communicate appropriately and effectively across all levels, always demonstrating the highest professionalism and poise.</li>
<li>Be agile and responsive, demonstrates the resourcefulness, drive, and commitment to supporting the needs of the business and the team.</li>
</ul>
