<p><b>
Job Scope:</b></p>
<p>
</p>
<p>
Operate vehicles (driving, parking and staging) in support of sale day events and general auction operations. Includes movement of vehicles to, from and on auction property as well as local car dealerships if necessary.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Locate, prepare and operate vehicles (driving, parking, and staging) in support of sale day events and general operations.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Operate and park all vehicles in a safe manner in compliance with all applicable company policies</li>
<li>Line up and park vehicles in the correct order as requested and locate “lost” vehicles</li>
<li>Place labels on vehicles as needed
</li>
<li>Coordinate assistance when a vehicle is inoperable or needs service or gas
</li>
<li>Assist customers and coworkers as needed</li>
<li>Drive assigned vehicles through auction lanes on sale day</li>
<li>Drive vehicles to and from auto auctions and dealerships as needed
</li>
<li>Assist with getting vehicles operating as needed including inflating tires or jump-starting vehicles using a battery box and air tank when necessary
</li>
<li>Assist with maintaining lot area when necessary</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned by supervisor</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Valid driver’s license for a minimum of one year and safe driving record required.</li>
<li>Successful completion of background check, motor vehicle history check and drug test will be required</li>
<li>Successful completion of a driving skills test</li>
<li>Ability to drive vehicles with standard and automatic transmission preferred but not required</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced and team-oriented environment.</li>
<li>Ability to walk and stand for long periods of time, ability to lift up to 30 lbs.
</li>
<li>Ability to enter and exit vehicles repeatedly
</li>
<li>18+ years of age; 20+ years for Transport Driver (driving vehicles off property) roles</li>
<li>Good problem-solving and organizational skills<br>
</li>
</ul>
