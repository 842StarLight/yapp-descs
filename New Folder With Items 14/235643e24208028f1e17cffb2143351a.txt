<br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Estimate food consumption, place orders with suppliers.</li>
<li>
Resolve customer complaints about food quality or service.</li>
<li>
 Direct cleaning of kitchen and dining areas to maintain sanitation standards, and keep appropriate records.</li>
<li>
Monitor actions of staff and customers to ensure that health and safety standards and liquor regulations are obeyed.</li>
<li>
May use computer software to monitor inventory, track staff schedules and perform other record keeping tasks.</li>
<li>
Check quality of deliveries of fresh food and baked goods.</li>
<li>
 Arrange for maintenance and repair of equipment and other services.</li>

Total receipts and balance against sales, deposit receipts, and lock facility at end of day.</li>

Recruit, hire, and oversee training for crew.</li>
<li>
Prepare or oversee preparation of weekly/daily scheduled work hours.</li>
<li>
Monitor food preparation and methods.</li>
<li>
Identify and estimate quantities of foods, beverages, and supplies to be ordered.</li>
<li>
Maintain relationships with customers and staff.</li>
<li>
Update and use job-related knowledge.</li>
<li>
Schedule staff work hours and activities.</li>
<li>
Evaluate health and safety practices against standards.</li>
<li>
 Organize, plan and prioritize.</li>
<li>
Make decisions and solve problems concerning menus and staff.</li>
<li>
Judge the quality of food, preparation and job applicants.</li>
<li>
 Process and analyze information when scheduling and budgeting.</li>
<li>
Record information about inventory and health practices.</li>
<li>
Handle food, utensils and bookkeeping materials.</li>
<li>
Monitor food preparation and cleaning methods.</li>
<li>
Get information from customers, employees and inventory records.</li>
<li>
Inspect equipment and food deliveries.</li>
<li>
Monitor and oversee purchases, menus, staff and payroll.</li>
<li>
Guide, direct and train staff.</li>
<li>
Coordinate the work and activities of staff.</li>
<li>
Perform administrative activities such as scheduling, budgeting and payroll.</li>
<li>
Communicate with customers, sales reps and suppliers.</li>
