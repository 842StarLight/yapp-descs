<div>
Hillrom is a $2.8B leading worldwide manufacturer and provider of medical technologies and related services for the health care industry, including patient support systems, safe mobility and handling solutions, non-invasive therapeutic products for a variety of acute and chronic medical conditions, medical equipment rentals, surgical products and information technology solutions. Hillrom's comprehensive product and service offerings are used by health care providers across the health care continuum and around the world in hospitals, extended care facilities and home care settings to enhance the safety and quality of patient care.<div>

<b>
Description</b><div>
</div>
<div><b>
JOB SUMMARY:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Reporting to the Executive Director of Patient Monitoring and Cardiology, this role will lead the US Service and Advance Connectivity solutions to customers. This individual contributor role will help lead a cross-functional team that partners with the Strategic Business Unit (SBU) and US Patient Monitoring and Cardiology marketing teams to incorporate service, customer, and market feedback into new service plans and service connectivity projects and support execution of marketing programs targeted to hospitals and IDNs. This will help deliver service connectivity solutions with Hillrom’s strategic partners and drive the sale of Welch Allyn connectable devices, service, and software.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>This role will re-build and position Patient Monitoring and Diagnostic Cardiology service offerings for warranty, service plans, bio-med training, clinical education, and remote device management in the US market.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>This individual will gain understanding of how connectable service products work as part of a larger customer solution for end to end device connectivity and execute Go-to Market (GTM) strategies for new connected service solutions.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position will work closely with the Upstream Marketing, Service Contracts, Service Engineering and Service Delivery teams to deliver team to define next generation connectivity commercial and technical service strategies.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position will collaborate closely with the US Sales team, end customers, and partners in pre and post sales conversations to understand the customers’ value drivers and articulate how our products will work in their environment to meet their business needs. This role will be the subject matter expert for the connected service solution and will be responsible for supporting customer service connectivity projects for strategic accounts.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
You will act as the marketing lead for partner specific service connectivity projects and will lead the Go-to-Market commercialization activities of new partner solutions. This role will train sales, field delivery, and customer service on the options for each service offering.</div>
