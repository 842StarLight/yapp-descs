<h2>Program Underwriter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>AmWINS Special Risk Underwriters (SRU)</b>, an AmWINS Group Company, is seeking Underwriters to join our team in <b>Atlanta</b>.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate will be able to function independently and think outside the box. This position requires self-direction and motivation to learn the job and continue to seek better ways to complete the job as an ongoing process. The individual in this role should have the capacity to understand concepts and the drive to succeed in a team environment.<br>
<br>
As an Underwriter, you will have the opportunity to grow your career with one of the largest independent wholesale insurance brokers and general agencies in the U.S. insurance industry.<br>
<br>
<b>Underwriter duties:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Underwrites new and renewal program business for acceptability based on guidelines established by company and SRU; reviews applications, coverages, classifications, exposures, financial condition and losses, claims and engineering reports; discusses pricing with broker</li>
<li>Submits accounts above authority to company with suggested pricing; negotiates pricing with company</li>
<li>Re-rates quotations as necessary</li>
<li>Prepares and sends out proposals</li>
<li>Completes Account Summary for each account</li>
<li>Completes status of account form</li>
<li>Reviews bound account information</li>
<li>Serves as primary contact for AmWins brokers</li>
<li>Maintains premium and exposure information in company proprietary software (AmLink, OnBase) including comments as necessary</li>
<li>Maintains account logs for control of outstanding items</li>
<li>Orders loss control in accordance with company guidelines, reviews completed reports and takes appropriate action, as required</li>
<li>Coordinates with other department personnel; consults engineering, claims and sales personnel to obtain necessary information in the handling of an account</li>
<li>Responds to phone calls, e-mails, faxes in accordance with service standards</li>
<li>Acts as departmental resource; answers technical and general questions from others in department</li>
<li>Approves endorsements within authority</li>
<li>Completes Rate Change Reports</li>
<li>QA policies, proposals and binding acknowledgements</li>
<li>Performs Quarterly QA Self Assessments and carrier Self Audits on peer files</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
