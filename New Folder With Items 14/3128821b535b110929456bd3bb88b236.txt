<b>POSITION PURPOSE</b><br>
The Home Service Coordinator must be bilingual, fluent in both Spanish and English. This position will operate as a sales and customer support expert that help build, manage and facilitate an active and diverse community of local Service Professionals across many trades. They will be expected to educate consumers and trade professionals about the value of using and partnering with THD/Pro Referral. Home Service Specialist will on-board Service Professionals; ensuring they have a complete understanding of all tools, resources, and process to achieve a high rate of customer satisfaction. This position will also interact directly with consumers to facilitate quotes and close sales.<br>
<br>
<b>MAJOR TASKS, RESPONSIBILITIES AND KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES</b><br>
90%- Consumer &amp; Professional Trade Businesses (Pro) customer support via inbound/outbound calls, email and chat systems.<br>
10%- Document customer interactions using web-based software tools and ensure end-to-end support, research and follow up are completed to ensure best effort in matches between professional trade businesses and consumers. Other activities as assigned by leadership<br>
<br>
<b>NATURE AND SCOPE</b><br>
This position reports to a Supervisor, Customer Care.<br>
This position has no direct reports.<br>
