<h2>Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Winter Haven Management Services, LLC provides accounting and administrative support services to a variety of Real Estate related businesses including land developers, hotels, HOA management firms and commercial and residential asset owning and operating companies.</p>
<p>The company is seeking a full time Accountant to join our team.</p>
<p><b>Primary Duties of the position: </b></p>
<p>· Process weekly Accounts Payable</p>
<p>· Communicate with vendors to resolve any problems</p>
<p>· Record daily deposits</p>
<p>· Maintain bank balance sheets and excel worksheets</p>
<p>· Prepare monthly bank reconciliations</p>
<p>· Prepare journal entries, EFTs, wires, etc.</p>
<p>· Prepare month end journal entries and schedules</p>
<p>· Prepare and pay FL Sales &amp; Use Tax, Tourist Tax, Communications Tax</p>
<p>· Record job closings on G/L from HUD statements</p>
<p>· Prepare financial statements</p>
<p>· Update fixed assets additions/deletions and balance schedules to G/L</p>
<p>· Prepare yearly audit schedules and 1099s</p>
<p>· Assist other accountants as needed</p>
<p><b>Prior Experience: </b></p>
<p>Required: At least 4 years working as an accountant</p>
<p>Preferred: At least 6 years working as an accountant</p>
<p>Acceptable: 3 to 4 years working in an accounting position</p>
