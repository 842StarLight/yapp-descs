<h2>Business Development Center Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Automotive BDC Representative – Crest Automotive Group</b></p>
<p>Are you looking for a fast-paced, rewarding career that stretches your creativity? Are you ready to jump into a new career opportunity with the mindset of a motivated entrepreneur? All automotive consumers begin the buying process online so it’s crucial that we have team members that are willing and able to provide an unforgettable car-buying experience online. We are seeking energetic individuals to join our unique and fast-paced Internet Sales team in our Business Development Center.</p>
<p>In this role, you will be the link between the buyer and their future vehicle. You will assist the customer with questions regarding the company, prospective vehicles, and their ability to purchase. You will learn about all of the newest vehicles and technologies on the market. We have an in-house training team dedicated to your success along with offering online and in-store training programs with our manufacturers. Your success will be dependent on your ability to market yourself creatively using social media platforms and other avenues. You will also need to be an entrepreneur who works on themselves every day to improve skill sets to reach required performance metrics.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor and respond to all Internet leads (referral services and website)</li>
<li>Develop client prospecting file upon lead receipt</li>
<li>Quote pricing, rates and inventory availability</li>
<li>Set appointments</li>
<li>Keep in constant contact with team members and management</li>
<li>Remain up-to-date on products, market trends and certifications</li>
<li>Understand and implement dealership sales and purchase process</li>
<li>Maintain Customer Service Index (CSI), meet and/or exceed monthly goals</li>
<li>Must be familiar with Client Relationship Management (CRM) software</li>
</ul>
