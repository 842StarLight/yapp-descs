<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SVP, Global MarTech Product Management Lead</b></h2>
<div>
<b>Locations:</b> Irving, Texas</div>
<div>

<div><div><div>
<p>Global Analytics &amp; Information Management (AIM) provides central capabilities globally across Citi’s Global Consumer Bank P&amp;Ls, which includes Credit Cards, Retail Bank, Retail Services and Mortgage to stand up enterprise level analytics capabilities and platforms. This central organization is focused on driving programs to deliver an Omni-channel and customer focused marketing and service experience across Citi relationships for our clients. This group has a transformational mandate.</p>
<p><b>
Job Description:</b></p>
<p>
The AIM organization is looking for an individual to drive the transformation of its marketing technology for the Global Consumer Bank. We need someone who is excited about reimagining the future of customer marketing, establishing decision infrastructure and providing data driven leadership.
</p>
<p>This role offers the chance to be a part of a team building cutting-edge technology, data and machine learning applications across the global marketing ecosystem. The modernization of Citi’s capabilities will deliver a real and sustainable source of competitive advantage and establish Citi as a leader in omni-channel marketing.</p>
<p>
The role’s purpose is to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive the strategy, design and execution of sophisticated marketing platform and related decision components in the martech ecosystem.</li>
<li>
Enable analytically-driven campaign offers across channels and P&amp;Ls leveraging internal and external data.</li>
<li>
Integrate data, decision, and delivery capabilities to enable Omni-channel orchestration of offers, messaging, content and experience across channels.</li>
<li>
Drive omni-channel personalization and customer experience by revamping the global martech stack.</li>
</ul>
<p>
The individual will need to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Champion and drive capability changes ensuring full buy in from all stakeholders in the business.</li>
<li>
Collaborate across the enterprise, building relationships with key stakeholders both internally and externally. This person will have extensive experience as a people leader, investing and growing talent.</li>
<li>
Work with multiple global business stakeholders to drive tangible use cases and with technology teams to deploy capabilities.<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:</b></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Lead and coordinate activities with business and technology teams to design and deploy martech capabilities – real time decision engine, customer data management, data management platforms, automated journey and campaign execution.</li>
<li>
Drive the implementation and integration of cloud based marketing solutions.</li>
<li>
Deploy sophisticated, analytically driven offers in the digital channel using internal and external data.</li>
<li>
Focus on and improve marketing capabilities and campaign management process.</li>
<li>
Proactively collaborate with technology teams to determine the best technical implementation method for enhancements.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with business teams and senior executives to help define roadmap for aspects of data, decision, and delivery platforms.</li>
<li>
Manage all aspects of configuration and technical/functional capabilities, including all changes and potential system implications related to the major release upgrades</li>
<li>
Communicate regularly with the Business, IT systems leadership, Steering Committee and other stakeholders to ensure functionality meets field needs and to develop ongoing project plan.</li>
<li>
Maintain familiarity with other IT system /business application capabilities and scope definition in order to make recommendations about potential upstream/downstream integration or functionality intersection points.</li>
<li>
Maintain system integration initiatives within the larger product roadmap.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain user group as a sounding board for any new features or initiatives and overall feedback.</li>
<li>
Consistently and persuasively champion the team's vision to create differentiated experiences that address the requested and unarticulated needs of customers across varied geographies.</li>
<li>
Provide leadership over the strategic direction of a set/domain of martech capabilities, across all touch points, including strategy, overall product roadmap, annual &amp; quarterly planning and the communication of the plan to the greater team and senior leadership.</li>
<li>
Work with executive stakeholders across business, and technology to balance competing interests and needs across the organization to arrive at a clear vision, communicating effectively and aligning various partners along the way.</li>
<li>
Work to establish a mature martech capabilities management practice in the group, consistently looking for opportunities to optimize and improve our overall organization, tools and processes.</li>
<li>
Stay up-to-date with the rapidly evolving digital landscape and provide leadership over advanced and emerging concepts, to help both educate leaders across the company, as well as inform our forward looking roadmap.</li>
<li>
Be full versed in our technology stack, how things are being delivered to the customer, the performance against established goals, and why it was created in the first place.</li>
<li>
Communicate and present well, with experience preparing and giving presentations to various senior stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Help build customer adoption to new tools and technologies.</li>
<li>
Work in a fast-paced environment and will be required to lead not only at the strategic level, but also tactically day-to-day by diving deeply into business, product, and technical domains.</li>
<li>
Have experience with structured, hypothesis-driven product improvements, rigorous metrics analysis, consumer insights and design experimentation.</li>
<li>
Connect the experience together with various measurements including sales, engagement and brand loyalty.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Education: Bachelors or Master Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or Information Technology</li>
<li>
10+ years of experience, including working with technology to deploy systems in either Risk, Marketing, Collections, or Operations</li>
<li>
Salesforce Marketing Cloud experience required</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability in deploying sophisticated, analytically driven offers in the digital channels leveraging internal and external data</li>
<li>
Strong marketing technology background</li>
<li>
Familiarity with digital marketing platforms and solutions including 3rd party hosts (Acxiom/Epsilon/Merkle), as well as internally deployed solutions such as FICO/Blaze/Triad</li>
<li>
Understanding of inner workings of digital ecosystems and know the differences between industry acronyms like DSP, PPC, SSP, CDP, DMP, MTA, etc.</li>
<li>
Experience using a DMP</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Personal Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong stakeholder influence and buy-in skills</li>
<li>
Excellent written and communication</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate analytic results to non-technical partners</li>
<li>
Able to be independent and a self-starter, comfortable in a fast-paced matrixed environment</li>
<li>
Strong execution skills and results oriented approach</li>
<li>
Act with curiosity and takes the initiative to identify problems, offer creative solutions and resolve issues</li>
</ul>
