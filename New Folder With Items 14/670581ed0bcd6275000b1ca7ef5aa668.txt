<p><b>Sr. Project Manager
</b></p>
<p>We are seeking an experienced project manager to lead a data integration and transformation project within an established PMO.</p>
<p>
The project this will relate to integrating standardized P&amp;C policy and claim data feeds into a single common platform with a shared set of processes and data validation. This will also enable integration of our partner data. This position will report into the PMO.</p>
<p>
A typical day will involve issue management, team meetings, schedule maintenance and proactive monitoring of work packages/tasks. Communication to various stakeholders will also occur daily and working with leadership to determine any additional project needs. Time and attention will need to be spent firming up and managing a reliable project schedule.</p>
