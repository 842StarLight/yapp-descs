<h2>Customer Relationship Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Park Place Communities is a fast-growing company with an open, supportive culture, and we are looking for talented individuals to join our growing team.</p>
<p>
We are a real estate investment and management company that purchases, renovates and manages affordable housing communities around the US.</p>
<p>
The Customer Relationship Administrator role will function as a key component of the Four Peaks Capital Partners (FPCP) investor team as new processes emerge and existing systems evolve to better execute the mission. We are seeking an individual that has a sales (or sales support) background with excellent communication and organizational skills, who can multi-task while maintaining accuracy and deadlines. Commitment to quality work is required. The ideal candidate is dependable, detail oriented, and enjoys interacting with people. This person is an innovative, independent, achievement-oriented self-starter who thrives in a high-pressure environment.
</p>
<p>This is a new and evolving role to help create, manage, expand, retain and develop the FPCP investor client base.
</p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>The majority of the Customer Relationship Administrator role is based around constant communication with new and existing investors including setting investor appointments for the Managing Partner, replying to inquiries and emails, answering questions and follow up.</li>
<li>Professionally represent the organization internally, and external events that provide opportunities for promoting organizational goals or soliciting new investors.</li>
<li>Support the development and execution of the investor relations and fundraising strategies.</li>
<li>Identify and respond to Investor targets-of-opportunity.</li>
<li>Investor communication including scheduling calls, disseminating information, creating files and onboarding for investors (PPMs, contact information, investments, etc.).</li>
<li>Maintains client confidence and protects operations by keeping information confidential.</li>
<li>Investor research.</li>
<li>Recruit new investors and where possible, increase levels of investment from existing investors.</li>
<li>Ensure investors and potential investors are informed and engaged.</li>
<li>Support the investor relations effort to find and encourage prospective investors by broadening awareness and interest through conferences, podcasts, presentations, meetings, media, and mailings.</li>
<li>Measure project and team performance using strategic tools and techniques, analyze results and report data to appropriate partners.</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of experience in a sales role or supporting sales role.</li>
<li>Excellent with communication and writing skills.</li>
<li>Ability to use Microsoft Office products, along with investor tracking software.</li>
<li>Strong organizational, analytical and decision-making skills.</li>
<li>Excellent communication, management, and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Some travel for meetings and events may be needed.</li>
</ul> <p>EXPERIENCED ONLY APPLY. You must be:</p>
<ul>
<li>Extremely detailed oriented</li>
<li>Able to organize every element and document systems</li>
<li>Motivated, positive team builder</li>
<li>A commitment to excellence in all that you do</li>
<li>Flexible, Adaptable, and Trainable</li>
<li>Passionate about technology and enjoys using it to become more efficient.</li>
<li>Comfortable with tight deadlines; organized, efficient, detail-oriented and able to multitask</li>
</ul>
<p>Compensation competitive and DOE - position includes benefits such as PTO, sick leave, medical, dental and vision coverage and more after intro period.</p>
