<h2>Service Writer for Material Handling Dealership</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Working at Atlantic Forklift Services, LLC gives you the opportunity to be part of a growing local business creating innovative customer solutions, focused on driving customer satisfaction. We'd like to offer you:</p>
<ul>
<li>The opportunity to grow in your career as the organization grows</li>
<li>Comprehensive benefits package</li>
<li>Competitive pay scale and 401K with employer match</li>
<li>A fulfilling career where you can relate to and engage with an incredible team</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Purpose:</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for providing overall support for the sales and service teams by generating and following up on service quotes to obtain purchase orders. Other activities include billing service and PM jobs, shipping and receiving of parts, assisting customers by answering phone calls and other general administrative items to ensure that Atlantic Forklift Services is recognized as the best customer service provider in the Carolina's for material handling users.</p>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain excellent customer relations through organization and communication skills</li>
<li>Generate and follow up on all service-related quotes to obtain customer purchase orders</li>
<li>Utilize technician recommendations to create and complete repair quotations through to invoicing</li>
<li>Work with Service Manager to provide warranty validation &amp; claims coverage's including computation of charges, submission and follow-up</li>
<li>Utilize order entry and job management software to track all customer activity</li>
<li>Support service team to complete daily invoicing as needed</li>
<li>Support parts team to assist with parts ordering, shipping and receiving</li>
<li>Assist customers and service team by answering phone calls and capturing critical customer data in job management software</li>
</ul>
<p>This position is responsible for ensuring that technician recommendations are quoted within 24 hours and follow up completed within 48 hours. Managing this process is critical in order to meet the 70% close rate goal, which will also help to ensure that the service and parts team revenue goals are met each month.</p>
<p><b>Skills/Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must demonstrate exceptional computer skills, including Microsoft Office, with the ability to use technology with little direction</li>
<li>Must have the ability to clearly communicate via written and verbal skills with customers and a team of professionals</li>
<li>High School Diploma required</li>
<li>Must be able to pass a pre-hire drug screening and background check</li>
<li>This position requires long hours sitting and using office equipment and computers</li>
<li>This position will also require light lifting of supplies and materials less than 25 lbs</li>
</ul>
