<div><b>
Description</b></div>
<div>Emerson Actuation Technologies is looking for an experienced software leader responsible for managing a global engineering team focused around the development of software, firmware, and connectivity solutions for electrically driven valve actuators. The team is responsible for development of new products, sustaining existing products and customer support. The Software Engineering Manager will also be coordinating and directing engineering activities at other locations located both in the US and overseas.</div>
<div></div>
<div>The successful candidate will be a natural leader with strong communications skills and customer focus who enjoys developing and working in a collaborative and fast paced work environment. The role is a great opportunity to gain experience managing multiple technologies including hardware interfaces, desktop applications, web applications, network protocols, and more.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>Manage large or significant projects from requirements through development, testing and release to the field</div></li>
<li><div>Manage day to day work for the team, including request tracking, task assignment, and backlog management</div></li>
<li>Manage software related aspects of cross functional projects to ensure quality and schedule goals are met</li>
<li>Coach and assist team members with problem solving, estimation, and other aspects of the software development lifecycle</li>
<li>Coordinate with marketing to define technical direction and estimate engineering developments
</li>
<li>Provide direction for our overseas development teams and advocate for their needs</li>
<li>Remove barriers preventing the group’s global teams from being successful</li>
<li>Assist the team in initiating and driving continuous improvement efforts</li>
<li>Work with other organizational leaders to set project priorities, select projects for initiation and assign team members</li>
<li>Coach and assist team members in the research and assessment of new technologies to achieve greater quality and efficiency</li>
<li><div>Mentor and provide career guidance for direct reports</div></li>
<li><div>Provide resolution to field issues working with the service organization
</div></li>
<li><div>Perform other duties as assigned</div></li>
</ul></div>
<div>
</div>

<div><b>
Requirements</b></div>
<div><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Bachelor’s Degree in Electrical Engineering or Computer Science.
</div></li>
<li><div>Minimum of 4 years of experience working in roles related to software or firmware development</div></li>
<li><div>Minimum of 2 years of project or team leadership experience using agile practices</div></li>
<li><div>Demonstrated experience implementing processes to improve quality, efficiency and timeliness</div></li>
<li><div>Strong written and verbal communication skills
</div></li>
<li>
<div>Proficient in the use of Microsoft Office application software</div>
<div></div>
<div>Preferred Qualifications:
</div>
</li>
<li><div>People management experience</div></li>
<li><div>Experience in the process or oil and gas industry</div></li>
<li><div>Familiarity with the valve and actuator industry
</div></li>
<li><div>Familiarity with software development, object-oriented programming and associated design tools C#, .NET, WPF, XML, SQLCE.
</div></li>
<li><div>Familiarity with process industry protocols of Modbus, Foundation Fieldbus, Profibus DP, AS-i, and HART.</div></li>
<li><div>Familiarity with Microsoft Team Foundation Server and Visual Studio tools.
</div></li>
</ul>
