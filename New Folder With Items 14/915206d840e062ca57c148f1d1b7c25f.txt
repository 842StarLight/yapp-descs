<h2>Onboarding Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>As a driven and ambitious Onboarding Specialist, you'll use your knowledge of customer retention and growth strategy to successfully onboard new customers onto the Curate platform and drive continued adoption throughout each company. You will be responsible for developing and deepening customer relationships through teaching and supporting our Curators through their initial onboarding process, resulting in lower customer churn (approx. 10% lower). You will also be responsible for growth in each customer’s lifetime value (approx. 10% lifetime value growth expected in the customer’s first 90 days).</p>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p><b>Day-To-Day:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Contact new customers and rigorously ensure onboarding compliance.</p></li>
<li><p>
Perform onboardings for new customers and develop relationships that lead to opportunities for product growth.</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain records of customer activity to ensure proper call frequency and follow-up for customer education and retention.</p></li>
<li><p>
Meet and exceed assigned churn and growth goals</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide input on sales training, automation, account load management, and strategy implementation plans for new products</p></li>
<li><p>
Assist the consultation team and other Curate teams with customer feedback, technical issues, or customer needed features.</p></li>
<li><p>
Execute on previously established guidelines, processes, and company principles</p></li>
</ul>
