<h2>Senior Brand Manager, Remington</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Division Information</b><br>
<p>Spectrum Home &amp; Personal Care (HPC) Division participates on a Global basis in small electric appliances, shave/groom appliances and beauty appliances. Our well- known brands include George Foreman®, Black+Decker®, Farberware®, Breadman®, Juiceman®, Russell Hobbs® and Remington®. Our products appeal to consumer trends for healthy eating, stay-at-home cooking, creating today's new hair style and much more. We distinguish our product offerings from the competition by employing forward thinking, innovative technologies matched with a strong understanding of consumer needs to bring to market high quality, superior products that delight the end user.</p>
<br>
<b>
Job Summary</b><br>
<p>This role is a leadership position within the organization with strategic ownership of multiple brands. As a leader, the incumbent must have the skillsets &amp; experience necessary to make sound business decisions, competently manage and develop direct reports, set strategic direction and influence internal/external stakeholders to follow that direction.</p>
<br>
<b>
Primary Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><br>
<p>The Senior Brand Manager will have responsibility for optimizing short and long term volume and profit performance for a brand/product portfolio by developing, championing &amp; executing the strategic vision</p>
<ul>
<li>Ownership of P&amp;L for assigned brand(s)/product line(s) – accountable for performance against plan</li>
<li>Lead overall vision for product pipeline &amp; development strategies</li>
<li>Manage overall product portfolio &amp; mix</li>
<li>Develop cross-channel pricing strategies</li>
<li>Set strategic direction and oversee execution for consumer/cross-channel advertising &amp; promotion plans</li>
<li>Maintain up-to-date competitive benchmarking, in partnership with Market Insights</li>
<li>Develop and direct consumer research strategies, and execute in partnership with Market Insights</li>
<li>Manage &amp; develop direct report(s)</li>
<li>Serve as the lead “voice” for assigned brand/product portfolio for all key constituencies, including sales, R&amp;D, regulatory, operations and finance</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Education and Experience Profile</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree required, MBA strongly preferred.</li>
<li>6+ years of packaged goods brand/product management experience with a record of increasing responsibility and delivering proven results</li>
<li>4+ years of profit and loss responsibility, including marketing budget management</li>
<li>Experience managing and developing direct reports</li>
<li>Has managed all four key areas of marketing responsibility (the four “p’s”): product development, pricing, promotion &amp; advertising, and place (channel/distribution strategy)</li>
<li>Experience leveraging internal/external business performance data to inform strategic decisions</li>
<li>Has led development and delivery of key account sales presentations</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Required Skills</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Must exhibit strategic thinking skills (ability to see the big picture)</li>
<li>Must be able to balance strategic planning (“creating the future”) with tactical execution (“running the business”)</li>
<li>Must have ability to analyze complex data and situations and develop a range of simple solutions</li>
<li>Must be able to utilize quantitative, qualitative and syndicated research to develop brand/product plans</li>
<li>Must be an excellent presenter, with strong presence and an ability to influence key internal and external customers</li>
<li>Solid understanding of traditional business software applications (MS Office, etc.)</li>
<li>Must be able to manage &amp; develop direct reports to maximize their potential</li>
</ul>

Working conditions are normal for an office environment.</p>
