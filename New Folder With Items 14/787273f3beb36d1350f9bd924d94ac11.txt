<h2>Assistant Bookkeeper / Assistant Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Entertainment Business Management Firm (Accounting Firm) is looking for an Assistant Bookkeeper to handle multiple high-net worth client accounts in the entertainment industry under Account Managers supervision. The firm takes pride in mentoring and developing staff internally towards technical growth and promotion. Offers excellent stability and very competitive benefits.</p>
<p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Handle bookkeeping for high-net worth individuals in the entertainment industry</li>
<li>Book investment accounts</li>
<li>High volume accounts payable and receivable</li>
<li>Prepare multiple bank reconciliations</li>
<li>Investment reconciliations</li>
<li>Process payroll, responsible for payroll taxes, quarterly returns and issuing</li>
<li>Prepare annual 1099</li>
<li>Vendor relationship</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Highly preferred - B.S. in Business Administration or completed some accounting courses</li>
<li>Prior experience in the Entertainment Business Management, CPA Firm or Property Accounting is a plus or ideally 1+ years of bookkeeping experience (A/P, A/R, Bank Reconciliation, P/R, Sales Tax, Business License, etc)</li>
<li>Datafaction, Zenith, QuickBooks, Yardi or other Accounting software experience is ideal</li>
<li>Prepares clear and complete work papers</li>
<li>Experience with processing cash receipts, vendor invoices, payroll taxes, quarterly returns, and bank reconciliation process</li>
<li>Excellent phone and communication skills</li>
<li>Able to meet multiple deadlines</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $50,000.00 to $58,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Bookkeeping: 1 year (Required)</li>
</ul>
