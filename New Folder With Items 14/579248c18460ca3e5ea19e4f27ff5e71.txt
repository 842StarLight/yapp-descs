<h2>Application Engineer Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Analyzes and designs new routine products and improves existing products.
</li>
<li>Designs and/or improves routine components and products with guidance on manufacturability, quality and cost with a strong understanding of design tools and methods.</li>
<li>Optimizes components and products with guidance on manufacturability, quality and cost with a strong understanding of analysis methods.
</li>
<li>Solves problems with a solid understanding of problem solving process and ability to apply to routine design problems.
</li>
<li>Assists in capturing voice of customer and development of product specifications.
</li>
<li>Translates customer needs to manufacturing requirements.
</li>
<li>Creates test plans and develops new test protocol for products or components, with a basic understanding of reliability and failure mechanisms.</li>
<li>Responsibility within the Commercial Air Solutions Technology Team for supporting Nicotra Gebhardt ECM and fan system applications including: site commissioning and support, documentation of design application, and production/quality support. Specific responsibilities include: Ownership of Fan Filter Units (FFU) site commissioning and other Nicotra Gebhardt application support</li>
<li>Design, debug, and testing of FFU applications and installation.</li>
<li>Documentation of installation site setups and requirements using CAD software and other methods</li>
<li>Translation of customers requirements into actionable installations and FFU package sales.</li>
<li>Debugs and supports software and hardware development, testing, production and quality teams
</li>
<li>Works with European teams to meet new customer/system requirements related to Nicotra Gebhardt advanced products.
</li>
<li>Responsible to respond to technical inquiries from customers in North America.</li>
<li>Significant travel will be required.</li>
</ul>

Minimum Qualifications</b></div>

<li>Experience with Microsoft Office tools (Word, Excel, etc)</li>
<li>Skilled in organizing, prioritizing and coordinating working relationships with all levels of personnel within and outside of the organization.</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills and the ability to coordinate multiple projects simultaneously.
</li>
