<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Classified Advertising Sales Representative</b></h2>
<p><b>Department:</b> Advertising Sales<br>
<b>Location:</b> Philadelphia, PA Office</p>
<p>The Classified Advertising Sales Representative is responsible for selling physician recruitment and classified advertisements in ACP’s publications, associated electronic newsletters, and the ACP Career Connection website. The successful candidate will be accountable for prospecting, marketing, selling, and developing existing and new accounts in an assigned territory.</p>
<p>In this role, you will actively maintain sales contacts via email, telephone, and networking at industry functions with written proposals and presentations to physician recruiters, advertising agencies and other potential clients</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain existing relationships and establish new client relationships through regular contact by phone, email, and attending conferences and ACP’s annual meeting.</li>
<li>Make cold calls on potential clients listed in the contact management database.</li>
<li>Provide written proposals, quotes for individuals ads and discuss options based on the client’s needs and budget.</li>
<li>Generate new business by researching leads in competitor publications and websites.</li>
<li>Follow up on ad renewals and expired renewals with established clients to encourage clients to rerun ads.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Communications, Advertising or Marketing preferred.</li>
<li>Proven success in the sale of print and digital recruitment ads preferably in the physician and healthcare industries.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Goldmine or other advertising contact management database.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage multiple and simultaneous deadlines.</li>
</ul>
