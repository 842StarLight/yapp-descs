Job Summary</b><br>
 </div>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>We are seeking a <b>Sample Processing and Materials Technician</b> for our Biologics Testing Solutions site located in <b>Malvern, PA.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Responsible for performing a wide variety of tasks involved in sample processing and inventory management of critical components, critical laboratory supplies, specialty reagents, client-supplied raw materials and internal and external cell banks. Performs activities related to log in, sample processing, storage, shipping of stored materials and client support.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p> The following are responsibilities related to the <b>Sample Processing and Materials Technician.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform daily tasks including sample receipt, log in, release and distribution.</li>
<li>Responsible for the execution of sample storage, updating, and maintaining the storage database to ensure it is complete and without error.</li>
<li>Create, updated, and monitor client storage files and internal seed bank files.</li>
<li>Make detailed observations and maintain clear, accurate and timely documentation of all laboratory work.</li>
</ul>

<p>The following are minimum requirements related to the <b>Sample Processing and Materials Technician</b> position.</p>

<li>

<li>

Ability to interact appropriately with all levels of employees. Ability to work effectively as a member of a team. The employee must regularly lift and/or move up to 50 pounds.</li>
