<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Our Account Executives bring on new clients with support from a solid inbound lead pool, partnering with a Business Development Representative to setup new buyer conversations, and most importantly possessing the outbound prospecting skill-set to build your own pipeline.</li>
<li>
The majority of new business is created over phone/ meetings and web demonstrations but going onsite strategically for key opportunities is encouraged.</li>
<li>
The ideal candidate is able to understand and analyze a prospect’s challenges, and can formulate and present a strategic plan to address their specific needs.</li>
<li>
Drive and own the entire sales process:</li>
<li>
Planning: target upper end SMB, Mid-Market, and lower end Enterprise accounts with C-Level Executives in Marketing, Digital Marketing, and eCommerce.</li>
<li>
Prospecting: outbound personalized calling and e-mailing rising above the noise from the hundreds of other Sales professionals calling on our potential buyers to secure buyer conversations.</li>
<li>
Presenting: qualify to ensure our activity will lead to potential revenue, build and demonstrate value conducting software demonstrations.</li>
<li>
Closing: own the entire sales process gaining an agreement on what must take place in working towards a business relationship.</li>
<li>
Exceed monthly and quarterly revenue targets.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
