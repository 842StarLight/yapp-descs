<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h1>
<p>Title: Business Development Representative</p>
<p>Job Type: Permanent</p>
<p>Location: Wayne-PA</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Opportunity:</b></p>
<p>The Business Development Representative is a sales role designed to support the revenue generation in a high-performance sales environment. We are seeking a professional that has a strong work ethic, high aptitude for learning and who is seeking opportunity for growth and expansion of sales responsibility. The Business Development Representative will engage prospective clients via telephone, email, and social media to qualify their interest and fit for Sungard Availability Services, LP solutions and pass qualified leads to the sales team. It is expected that this individual will leverage all accessible tools, resources and support structure provided to meet and exceed all KPIs and revenue sales quota. This position provides an opportunity to sell leading edge solutions for one of the premier organizations in the industry while providing opportunity for future growth and advancement.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute business development programs as defined in Regional Go-to-Market strategy.</li>
<li>Maintain desired outbound selling activities to increase Market awareness.</li>
<li>Utilize knowledge of Sungard Availability Services products and services to define entry points into prospect accounts.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Enterprise Account Executives on selling strategies and Account Plans.</li>
<li>Research and gather information about target Accounts in support of Key account plans while building prospect database.</li>
<li>Ensure all customer facing activity is recorded and maintained in Salesforce.com to provide the business with market data as well as pipeline growth.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to effectively learn technical products, services, and solutions and readily apply new knowledge is required.</li>
<li>Proven ability to listen, extrapolate information, and leverage resources to effectively provide solutions to customer.
</li>
<li>Possess excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>College degree in Business Administration, Management, or Marketing preferred, but will consider applicants with a minimum educational requirement of a H.S. diploma or GED equivalent.</li>
<li>One year of experience in a sales-based environment preferred.</li>
</ul>
