<h2>Leasing Specialist - Boulevard Apartments</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p>We are looking for our next great sales and leasing professional for Boulevard Apartments!
</p>
<p>
If you are passionate about helping others and knowledgeable of multifamily residential real estate, we have the right opportunity for you! At Sherman, we pride ourselves on hiring resident-focused and skilled property management talent who strive to lead community operations. We believe we can offer a challenging, fast-paced and rewarding experience that is unique to supporting our mission, vision, and values.
</p>
<p><b>
Where will this role work?</b></p>
<p>
Boulevard Apartments, a 235 unit affordable tax credit residential community in Milwaukee, WI.
</p>
<p><b>
Core Business hours:</b></p>
<ul><li>
This is a full-time position that will average 35-40 hours per week. Core working hours will be Monday-Friday 9 AM - 5 PM with occasional evenings and weekends as required.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Core Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Customer/Client Focus</li>
<li>
Communication Proficiency</li>
<li>
Resident Event Coordination</li>
<li>
Business Acumen</li>
<li>
Results Driven</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ensure customer needs are met; provide directions, suggestions for restaurants, post on active building local events occurring.</li>
<li>
Assist with welcome packet creation / move-in gift tailored to site marketing and branding.</li>
<li>
Lead resident event coordination, planning and execution.</li>
<li>
Patrol and manage property esthetics both interior and exterior.</li>
<li>
Attend networking relevant function opportunities.</li>
<li>
Assist with resident retention program development.</li>
<li>
Promote active resident engagement within the assigned community.</li>
<li>
Maintains customer service and excellent relations with residents, resident cooperatives, commercial tenants, outside agencies or vendors and organizations.</li>
<li>
Works on the last day and first day of each month until responsibilities completed; some overtime may be required.</li>
<li>
Manages prospects by answering incoming calls and internet leads. All inquiry calls and emails must be returned promptly (within 24 hours).</li>
<li>
Makes appointments, shows apartments, screens applicants, and verifies applications for completion.</li>
<li>
Assists applicants with the application process by explaining all applicable forms. Ensures applications are accurate and complete.</li>
<li>
Contacts and offers units to approved applicants.</li>
<li>
Schedules and conducts orientation for all new move-ins. Updates turnover information on a daily basis to include gathering of move-out and move-in times.</li>
<li>
Shops comparable projects in the market place monthly.</li>
<li>
Develops community contacts to maximize the general awareness of the housing community and its advantages.</li>
<li>
Contact and offer units to current residents for approved unit transfers. Prepare all required transfer paperwork and complete transfers with residents.</li>
<li>
Updates all applicable referral agencies.</li>
<li>
Inputs all traffic and applications in the property management system regularly.</li>
<li>
Other tasks &amp; duties as assigned based on business need.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Required Experience:<br>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years of industry experience within residential real estate; or an equivalent number of years of education and customer service experience.</li>
<li>
Additional education in real estate, sales and/or business management is preferred.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal, written and presentation skills; Ability to communicate effectively with community tenants, vendors / external parties, and across corporate divisions.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Real Page or other related Property Management software (Yardi, Lead2Lease, OneSite Accounting, etc.) strongly preferred.</li>
<li>
A passion for the mission, vision, and values of Sherman Associates.</li>
<li>
Office hours may vary due to the specific needs of the portfolio.</li>
</ul>
