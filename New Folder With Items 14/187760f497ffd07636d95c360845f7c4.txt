<p><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>
AEG and Ontario Reign seek a Manager of Game Presentation and Events. This individual will be responsible for overseeing all in-arena, on-ice, and event activities/promotions/productions/operations. The individual will manage and oversee all game and event operations staff. The goal of this position is to enhance the experiences of the Ontario Reign fans, build relationships with vendors, building/event personnel, and clients.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Producer for all Ontario Reign home games and events including: scripting, rundowns, timing sheets, in-game inventory, pre-production, live production, operations. Controls creative, special effects, special presentations, ceremonies, promotions</li>
<li>Maintain effective communication with and serve as liaison between, Reign Game Presentation, AHL, SMG Arena staff, and Front Office.</li>
<li>Serve as Project manager/producer on events for the Reign as well as events/projects in cooperation with other departments, including: Broadcasting, Corporate, Marketing, Ticketing, Community Relations, and Hockey Development.</li>
<li>Oversee part time stage managers, Reign Ice Crew and event staff, including: hiring, payroll, training, assessing performance, and scheduling</li>
<li>Work with the LA Kings Game Presentation team to align Brand and entertainment value with Kings to ensure a comparable experience across the two connected brands</li>
<li>Work with multiple departments on implementation of in-arena group experiences and in game assets, including (but not limited to): flag team, anthem singers, on-ice/concourse/stage performances, group recognition</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring all graphic &amp; video content (including required sponsorship and/or marketing content) is delivered and loaded into all corresponding machinery, tested, and working properly prior to all games/events.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SPECIFIC JOB KNOWLEDGE, SKILL AND ABILITY:</b></p>
<p>
The Manager, Game Presentation and Events must possess the following knowledge, skills and abilities and be able to explain and demonstrate that he or she can perform the essential functions of the job, with or without reasonable accommodation, using some other combination of skill and abilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience with scriptwriting and production timing/rundown execution</li>
<li>Extensive Software knowledge – Office, Adobe, Premiere/Final Cut Pro, Avid,</li>
<li>Prior experience managing a team</li>
<li>Highly organized and detail oriented with strong follow-up skills &amp; ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Experience creating still and/or motion graphics.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, Premiere and/or After Effects</li>
</ul>
