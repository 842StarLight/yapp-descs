<h2>Buyer II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Summary: Purchases production materials, components, equipment, tooling, MRO items, and services utilizing new and/or existing suppliers in support of the company’s operations, and manages quality, cost, and on-time delivery of goods and services of assigned supply base.</p>
<p>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:<br>1. Responsible for strategic sourcing of assigned commodity parts.<br>2. Manage business relationship with suppliers through supporting activities including ordering, communication of demand / volume / delivery time changes, issue resolution, sharing of end-user feedback, provide update of order status via the past due orders report, runs open Purchase Order Reports<br>3. Analyzes, negotiates, and issues RFQ’s to suppliers for new and existing materials, supply contracts<br>and services<br>4. Develop a strong understanding and knowledge of suppliers and capabilities<br>5. Drafts sourcing recommendations for award summarizing the bid history and justifying the selection of awarded bidder<br>6. Develops and executes sourcing strategies and prepares monthly reports to track and show sourcing strategy and progress, including achievement of spend reduction targets (PPV)<br>7. Studies market trends, interviews vendors, and identifies new domestic and global suppliers<br>8. Works with Supplier Quality Engineering (SQE) for source selection<br>9. Performs problem solving activities with suppliers, and establishes continuous improvement targets<br>10. Gathers and prepares information for ECM presentations<br>11. Identifies and works with suppliers and internal resources to resolve delivery problems<br>12. Releases requisitions, prints out orders, and transmits orders electronically to suppliers<br>13. Working with stakeholders and management teams to implement and enforce policies that increase spend visibility and compliance.<br>14. Works closely with Finance teams to expedite receiving and AP processes<br>15. Acts on own initiative, takes responsibility for results<br>16. Uses a methodical and systematic approach; plans ahead, defines clear priorities and allocates resources effectively<br>17. Quickly understands and analyzes complex issues and problem; comes up with sound rational judgement<br>18. Communicates in a clear, precise, and structured way; speaks with authority and conviction; presents effectively<br>19. Cooperates well with others; shares knowledge, experience and information; supports others in the pursuit of team goals<br>20. Adheres to company rules and procedures; executes plans with commitment and determination; achieves high quality results.<br>21. Communicates with suppliers on EDI problems, testing, etc.<br>22. Provides information to freight provider regarding all expedites (other than supplier past due).</p>
<p>23. Supports Corporate Purchasing Department monthly, quarterly and annual goals and objectives<br>24. Supports new program launch activities with new/existing materials/suppliers<br>25. Learns and keeps current on manufacturing processes, market trends, and technical development<br>26. Performs other duties as assigned</p>
<p>Requirements:<br>27. BA/BS in Business or a related discipline<br>(Degree in Engineering or Supply Chain Management preferred)<br>28. 3 to 5 years of professional experience within direct purchasing, negotiating with suppliers and managing the supply base post-negotiations.<br>29. Prior purchasing experience involving spend in excess of $30 million is beneficial. Must have<br>experience with global or regional sourcing processes / decision making<br>30. Must have demonstrated performance in working cross-functionally and/or within a matrixed organization and working independently to obtain results.<br>31. Must have prior experience preparing and delivering presentations to senior management as well as experience presenting information to suppliers and internal customers.<br>32. Must be able to independently troubleshoot issues/concerns and determine course of action.<br>33. Strong knowledge, skills, and abilities in the following areas:<br>a. Knowledge of TS-16949 requirements<br>b. Excellent written and verbal communications and presentation skills<br>c. Problem solving and analytical skills with attention to detail<br>d. MS Office and related business software packages<br>e. Organizational and planning skills</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>

<ul><li>Bachelor's (Required)</li></ul>
