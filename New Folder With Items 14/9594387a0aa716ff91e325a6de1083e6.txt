Maxim's Audio Business Unit is seeking an experienced Senior Business Manager to grow our revenue, customer base and market share in consumer market. <ul>

Define and implement initiatives and grow revenue from existing products, while diversifying the customer base across multiple applications.</p></li>
<li><p>
Lead the discovery phase of new product development - Research new technology, market trends, and customer needs, analyze the competition, drive product-development strategies, new user experience, and roadmaps, guide product definition, conduct preliminary business case analysis and prioritize projects that have the strongest business case and strategic value.</p></li>
<li><p>
Guide the product development through the new product development phase, drive silicon verification and validation, track the development of your new products and resolve problems to keep them on schedule</p></li>
<li><p>
Lead the market introduction phase of new product development and drive development of EV Kits, reference designs, application notes, software device drivers and marketing collateral. Train Maxim sale, FAEs and distributors and work with field personnel to develop and execute account penetration strategies.</p></li>
<li><p>
Closely work with local sales and technical team for project design win, pricing, demand and supply, quality, customer relationship management and etc.</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop strong connections with leading customers, key processor vendors, 3rd party partners decision in close loop</p></li>
</ul>
