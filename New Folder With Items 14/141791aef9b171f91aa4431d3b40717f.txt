<p>Technical Support Agent</p>
<p>Location: Austin TX</p>
<p><b>ClearDATA</b> is seeking an enthusiastic inside sales professional with a deep knowledge of healthcare technology to truly make a difference in Healthcare! If you’re passionate about creating a new category of technology that will fundamentally make healthcare better, come join our team. As a key member of the sales team, you’ll work closely with your Marketing counterparts to qualify and further develop warm leads by performing custom, end-user requirements analyses. These are complex, competitive sales that require a high degree of cloud computing knowledge, sales acumen, and tenacity. The ISE's first responsibility is to meet, or exceed, monthly quota expectations, by delivering an exceptional prospect buying experience, thorough knowledge of the product portfolio, and a well-orchestrated sales process.</p>
<p><b>What You'll Do:</b></p>
<ul><li>managing inbound and/or outbound calls following various communication scripts when speaking to a caller</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help identify caller as a customer and help them create a Support ticket if needed</li></ul>
<ul><li>identifying customer needs, answering questions and providing solutions/alternatives to issues</li></ul>
<ul><li>first line of support for our ClearData customers answering basic cloud questions to assist our customers</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>What makes a good Technical Support Agent:</b></p>
<ul><li>Use customer service skills to put customers at ease and help them solve their technical problems</li></ul>
<ul><li>Having empathy can help greatly enhance the customer experience when they call in.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Having agents who connect with callers, rather than making the customers feel like they're talking to a brick wall</li></ul>
<ul><li>Can help build brand loyalty and callers will likely have a more positive experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Needs excellent listening and verbal communication skills to understand customer complaints and clearly relay solutions to technical problems</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>What You Need:</b></p>
<ul><li>Customer service experience or technical background</li></ul>
<ul><li>Basic understanding and application of Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery concepts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Self-motivated and self-directed, ability to translate technical direction into functional solutions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to put requirements into operational scenarios and communicate effectively with management and customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong and passionate desire to learn</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>What Will Make You Stand Out:</b></p>
<ul><li>1+ years of technical experience with Cloud Technologies (AWS, Azure, or GCP)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Windows and/or Linux Server Administration</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience working in the healthcare industry.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience working in a high growth VC backed startup.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Have or pursuing a CS, CIS, or other technical degree</li></ul>
