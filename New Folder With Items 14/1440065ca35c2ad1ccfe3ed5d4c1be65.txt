<p><i>
Come join our growing team of Assistant Community Directors!</i></p>
<p>
We are looking for someone who is passionate about Property Management and desires the opportunity to expand their Management skills with our growing company. As an Assistant Community Director, you would have a vital role in the success of your property. You would be responsible for overseeing the property and staff in absence of the Community Director, assisting with various reporting functions, leasing and collection of rents.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
About IRET: </b>IRET was founded in Minot, North Dakota, in 1970. You can find our corporate offices located in Minneapolis, Minnesota and in Minot North Dakota, along with onsite operations in eight states. From Denver, CO to Minneapolis, MN, our company continues to grow throughout the Midwest, proudly providing apartment homes to thousands of residents, extending our vision to be the premier provider of apartment homes in vibrant communities by focusing on integrity and serving others. IRET is built around our employees. Our Mission and Vision unites our employees as one team.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Summary</b><br>
Under the direction of, and together with, the Community Director, the primary responsibilities include ensuring that the community generates a satisfactory cash flow and that the property is maintained in accordance with IRET standards. Assures customer satisfaction. Duties will be performed following Company procedures and in accordance with EHO and EEO regulations and guidelines.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Expectations</b><br>
Requires little supervision. Self-directed and self-motivated. Prioritizes and sets deadlines. May assist others in prioritizing, setting deadlines, problem solving, etc. May direct the work of others and acts as the Community Director in the absence of the Community Director.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><br>
include the following: To perform the job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform these essential functions.</p>
<p><b>
Operational:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Participates in weekly pricing and renewal calls and recommends strategy to achieve goals.</li>
<li>
Coordinates resident events and volunteer events.</li>
<li>
Serves as a direct contact for residents answering questions and resolving issues.</li>
<li>
Handles eviction processing.</li>
<li>
May assist with resident inspections.</li>
<li>
Monitors renter’s insurance compliance.</li>
<li>
Leasing Consultant responsibilities and duties.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Financial:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for rent collections and managing delinquency.</li>
<li>
May assist with processing statements of deposit accounting within 7 days of move out.</li>
<li>
Completes SODAs within seven days of move out.</li>
<li>
May assist Community Director with purchase order and invoice support.</li>
<li>
Reviews balances and credits to ensure proper accounting of charges and payments are applied</li>
<li>
Prepares for month end closing.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b><b> </b><b>Leadership:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides supervisory support to resolve resident issues.</li>
<li>
Collaborates with Asset Management and the Community Director to prepare renewal plans.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
Responsibilities are 30% marketing &amp; sales, 30% customer service, 30% business admin, 10% property maintenance.</i></p>
<p><b>
Supervisory Responsibilities</b><br>
This role has no supervisory responsibilities.</p>
