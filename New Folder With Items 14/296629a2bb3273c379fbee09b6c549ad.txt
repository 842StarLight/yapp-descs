<h2>Product Marketing Manager, Data</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>***CHARLESTON RELOCATION***<br>
<br>
Benefitfocus (NASDAQ: BNFT) unifies the entire U.S. benefits industry on a single technology platform to protect consumers' health, wealth, property and lifestyle. We believe employers should be able to access a richer standard of benefits for their employees to thrive, brokers should be empowered as trusted advisors, and carriers and product suppliers should be able to connect more people, more easily, with the right products. With fresh thinking and a view towards what’s next, we’re committed to evolving our platform to deliver a world-class consumer benefits experience that is powered by artificial intelligence and enables better decision making across the entire ecosystem. The marketing team at Benefitfocus is a fundamental part of our future success and growth strategy.<br>
<br>
As a Product Marketing Manager, Data, you will be part of the larger product leadership team at Benefitfocus. You are responsible for the outbound marketing activities of your solutions, including market messaging and positioning. You will be the expert in buyers, how they buy and their buying criteria and will transfer that knowledge to the sales channel. You'll conceive and develop innovative marketing programs that drive demand with attention to detail and an analytical lens. This position plays a critical role in the broad business planning, working closely with senior executives to develop business targets and resource requirements, influences our long-term technical and business strategy and enables us to deliver innovative new solutions rapidly. You will be seen as the subject matter expert for your area of focus within Benefitfocus Product Data and AI.<br>
<br>
A successful candidate will bring a passion for technology services, strong business acumen and judgment, ability to define visionary, ground breaking products, desire to have an industry wide impact and ability to work within a fast moving environment in a large company to rapidly deliver services that have a broad business impact. You will manage and be responsible for understanding customer needs and delivering some of our most strategic technical projects, and have a significant bottom-line impact on our business and competitive position.<br>
<br>
<b>Your Responsibilities Will Include</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop product positioning and messaging that differentiates your products in the market</li>
<li>
Execution of Product Planning and Development – Including customer goals and business requirements for product release, ensuring implementation is aligned with product goals and requirements, and ownership of product positioning.</li>
<li>
Expand use cases for yield optimization that can be resolved via Automation or AI/Machine Learning and ensure feedback loop from reps and customers to identify pain points and improvement opportunities</li>
<li>
Evangelize Automation/AI across the organization, drive integration with customers and partners and ensure desired positive impact is achieved</li>
<li>
Partner closely with Analytics and Operations teams to ensure proper measurement instrumentation, monitoring and reporting of product success post launch</li>
<li>
Lead Product Launch – plan the launch of new products and releases and manage the cross-functional implementation of the plan. Own the go-to-market plan to deliver results that ensure the customer and business goals are met in operational launch plans.</li>
<li>
Sales enablement – communicate the value proposition of the products to the sales team and develop the sales tools that support the selling process of your products</li>
<li>
Lead interaction with Technical Team – Including helping the technical team make trade-offs based on customer requirements, QA/testing of the product.</li>
<li>
Pricing - You will be responsible for pricing research and analysis. Work with product management, engineering, sales and finance to develop competitive pricing strategies that drive growth and profitability and support our business strategy</li>
<li>
Market intelligence – be the expert on your buyers, how they buy and their buying criteria; be the expert on your competition and how to crush them</li>
<li>
Demand generation – develop the strategy and manage the marketing programs that drive demand for your products</li>
<li>
Lead Product Definition – Own and drive the customer working backwards strategy, tenets, long-term goals and working backwards documents (press release, FAQ) including customer and market feedback, competitive analysis and business metrics to inform direction.</li>
<li>
Define Product Vision – Including all aspects to future roadmap, investment, innovation and experimentation.</li>
<li>
Lead and mentor technical product managers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul>

Product marketing in the Technology sector (SaaS preferred)</li>
<li>
Experience or exposure to the human resource, healthcare, or employee benefits markets and ability to find the intersection of HR Benefits + technology</li>
<li>
3-5 years in product management in an Agile environment, with a record of launching successful software products to market.</li>
<li>
Direct experience leading a product related to Data Automation applied to yield optimization. APIs as a product experience a strong plus</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of deep learning, machine learning and statistics</li>
<li>
You possess superior leadership qualities, with a track-record of coaching cross-functional teams in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
You are a skilled public speaker with experience presenting to senior executives and the ability to engage and capture your audience</li>
<li>
You are a pragmatic and practical self-starter; an outcomes oriented person who can juggle multiple work streams, loves to problem-solve and can efficiently drive a collaborative process</li>
<li>
You thrive on challenge, and take pride in being an excellent teammate and embody the Benefitfocus core values; anticipatory service, respect the individual, working together and celebrating success</li>
<li>
You are willing to travel intermittently to support our sales team and clients</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Information Systems or related field, or equivalent experience (MBA preferred)</li>
<li>
Strong track record of shaping business strategy for technical products or services</li>
<li>
Superior analytical and quantitative skills; experience in data collection, model building, testing and validation.</li>
<li>
Experience translating customer needs into innovative and detailed product requirements for customer-facing online and mobile products/services</li>
<li>
Experience working with customers, technical teams, and management to collect requirements, describe software product features, and technical designs</li>
<li>
Exceptional attention to detail, organizational, and project management skills</li>
<li>
Must have shipped a customer- or developer-facing product or service</li>
</ul>
