<h2>Application Support and Training Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>Work Schedule: </b>Monday - Friday, 8:00 am - 5:00 pm</p>
<p>Actively looking for an experienced Application Support and Training Analyst to provide premier quality customer service, support, and training to the company’s valued corporate product clients. The successful candidate must have strong written and verbal communication skills, technical skills, and analytic skills to work closely with the clients, product management, and sales team in support of their efforts. As a team player, you will assist with the proprietary software client and server installations, configurations, and Tier I, II, and III application troubleshooting for enterprise clients. In addition to these dynamic, mission-critical responsibilities, you will train to be a software trainer who will have the opportunity to travel to customer locations around the globe to provide on-site application instruction to new and existing users.</p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Using available knowledge and resources, provide quality client technical support by analyzing application, database, and operating systems symptoms to identify and resolve issues.</li>
<li>Document client’s issue and process of resolution.</li>
<li>Provide training to clients in the use of system and applications.</li>
<li>Support client database and installations in production and test environments.</li>
<li>Contribute to training manuals, knowledge base, and tutorials as needed.</li>
<li>Escalate complex problems to Development and monitor the output to keep the customer informed.</li>
<li>Provide technical support to the sales team during client presentations.</li>
<li>Create and follow-up on customer retention efforts.</li>
<li>Provide application training to new and existing customers upon request.</li>
<li>Domestic and International Travel approximately 25% of the time.</li>
<li>Other duties and projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills and Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Troubleshooting and problem-solving</li>
<li>High attention to detail</li>
<li>Professional attitude and desire to resolve issues with patience and understanding</li>
<li>Knowledge of software and hardware system principles, theories, concepts, and technologies</li>
<li>Familiarity with the following: SQL, Oracle, MS Access, MS Office, CRM systems</li>
<li>Organized with excellent English verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Customer service, technical, and analytical skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and in a team environment</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in technical discipline or equivalent experience</li>
</ul>
