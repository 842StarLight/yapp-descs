<h2>Client Solutions Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a Client Solutions Manager (i.e. Account Manager), you'll establish and maintain long-term client relationships and manage complex digital marketing campaigns. The ideal candidate will have top-notch client-facing account management experience and some knowledge of programmatic marketing or paid media from an agency.</p>
<p>
Mogo Interactive, a Learfield IMG College company, works with a variety of clients in verticals including e-commerce, collegiate sports, higher education, finance/banking, as well as over 150 nonprofit arts organizations across North America.</p>
<p>
Mogo's highly skilled digital marketers consistently deliver measurable, market-leading results for our clients by artfully leveraging a comprehensive suite of state-of-the-art programmatic tools that are generally not accessible to most organizations in-house.</p>
<p>
This role can be based in either our Sacramento, New York, or Corte Madera offices.</p>
<p>
WHAT YOU’LL BE DOING:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Lead day to day strategic management of a variety of client accounts including: media planning, account onboarding, campaign analysis, optimization, project manage troubleshooting, reporting, etc</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Address client questions, communicate campaign progress, proactively identify potential issues and develop client-centric solutions to maintain and strengthen business relationships</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborate with Sales, Display, Search, and Social teams to create and present media recommendations to prospective and existing clients</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create and maintain documentation associated with each project (tasks, schedules, SOWs, budgets, project briefs, IOs, etc.)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Deliver effective oral presentations (over the phone and in-person) with professionalism and business maturity</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support the team to recruit, onboard, coach, and motivate a team of Client Solutions representatives</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Travel as needed</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>WHAT SUCCESS IN THIS ROLE LOOKS LIKE:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Clear, consistent and effective communication with clients, prospects, and internal teams over email, on the phone, and in-person</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrate deep understanding of agency process, departments and how to effectively get work done on time and on budget</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proactively problem-solve to resourcefully seek the best solution to challenges</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A focus on continually evolving the product offerings, content and results we deliver to clients and prospects</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>WHAT WE’RE LOOKING FOR:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Bachelor’s degree (B.A.), or equivalent</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>2+ years of marketing experience, agency/media experience preferred</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>1 year of programmatic advertising experience</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Understanding of performance analytics and the ability to interpret data into meaningful insights and actionable next steps</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to communicate directly with clients to address questions and resolve concerns with diplomacy and acumen</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strong organizational skills and ability to maintain accuracy and meet deadlines in a high-volume, fast-paced environment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent interdepartmental coordination, collaboration, and project management skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proficient in Microsoft Office / Google Suite</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Knowledge of the following platforms is a plus (DV 360, FB Ad Manager, Search 360, Google Analytics, Datorama, etc)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Google Analytics certified is a plus</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>WHAT WE OFFER:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Healthcare, 401(k), PTO, flexible work environment, fun team outings and company events, challenging work and professional development, and the Corte Madera location is dog-friendly with free parking</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>We are excited to work with anyone who shares our values regardless of race, color, national origin, citizenship, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, marital status, disability, or veteran status.</p>
