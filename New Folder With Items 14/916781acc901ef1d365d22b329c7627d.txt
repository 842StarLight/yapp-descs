<h2>Senior Manager Vehicle Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>This position serves as the onsite operational leader for the emerging Mobility business. Responsible for operations of the mechanical, body, and lot operations, including: scheduling, directing work assignments, vehicle logistics, placement of vehicles, vehicle movement and vehicle transport. Helps drive process creation and improvement in fluid operational environment. Partners with client and sales teams to provide excellent wholistic client experience.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<p>Job Responsibilities:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Own and manage all lot operations on a daily basis to ensure efficient operations for all fleet mobility clients
</li>
<li>Partner with client and operational leadership to create, evolve, and execute operational business plans, analyzing operational expenses, and implementing procedures to improve efficiencies.</li>
<li>
Coordinate Reconditioning, Mechanical, Lot Ops, Detail, and other departments to schedule and prepare vehicles for client delivery. Effectively manage all aspects of service operations, ensuring all vehicles are properly staged and efficiently delivered per client specifications and SLAs.
</li>
<li>Understand and implement standard operating procedures for multiple clients while monitoring and meetings client SLAs for services required
</li>
<li>Learn, understand, and manage core operational systems(AS400, RPP, AMT) and the required systems of clients
</li>
<li>Maintain positive customer relations through strong communications and responsiveness
</li>
<li>Supervise and develop mechanical and lot operations staff, their professional and technical knowledge by: recruiting, selecting, orienting, training employees and by providing educational opportunities.
</li>
<li>Manage work and relationships with onsite 3rd party providers
</li>
<li>Provide coaching and training for: lot drivers, mechanics, others operational team members, and administrative staff.
</li>
<li>Counsel and discipline employees as necessary. Plan, monitor, evaluate job performance and conduct performance appraisals.
</li>
<li>Monitor lot damage, meet with general management on a regular basis to review and resolve potential lot operation problems.
</li>
<li>Work with Lot Coordinators to ensure all vehicles are located, delivered to the lot, properly accounted for and parked.
</li>
<li>Ensure all company assets used in operations are properly maintained and accounted for. Maintain/supervise maintenance of equipment including radios, PT scanners, service trucks, wreckers, fuel trucks/equipment, jump boxes, lot vans, golf carts, and lift trucks.
</li>
<li>Maintain all lot areas and sale lanes to ensure operational efficiency.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain effective schedules for all operational personnel.
</li>
<li>Visibly demonstrate safety commitment by following, inspecting, and educating on all safety and health procedures and modeling the behaviors related to such. Work in cooperation with safety leadership in support of all safety activities aligned with Safety Excellence.
</li>
<li>Provide assistance and timely feedback to customer inquiries
</li>
<li>Maintain operational requirements by initiating, coordinating and enforcing production and control standards and procedures. Ensure all established costs, quality, and delivery commitments are met.</li>
<li>
Review work volumes, plan and continuously monitor staffing levels to ensure efficiency, quality work product, and effective customer service; review departmental performance against key performance indicators and metrics and develop and execute strategies for improvement.
</li>
<li>Effectively leads the team at the location by setting an example in behavior, championing Cox/Manheim values and ensuring that all employees are treated with respect.</li>
<li>
Enforce all company policies and procedures related to employee and customer conduct.
</li>
<li>Partner with various market level support teams (i.e. Finance, HR, MFS, Recon, Safety, Security, Sales) to ensure effective and efficient operational processes that align with company objectives and strategies and high quality customer service and support.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned by manager or supervisor.</li>
<li>
Occasional exposure to weather conditions, fumes, and noise.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<p></p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications:</b></h4>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 5 years operations experience required</li>
<li>
Experience in the automotive industry or auction business</li>
<li>
Effective management and decision making skills required</li>
<li>
Reconditioning and/or body shop experience preferred</li>
<li>
Comfortable working in variable and unpredictable work environments
</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interaction skills required
</li>
<li>Effective management, decision-making, and organizational skills required</li>
<li>
Good computer and software knowledge essential</li>
<li>
AS400 experience preferred</li>
<li>
Physical Demands:
</li>
<li>Ability to sit, stand and walk for prolonged periods of time. Vision abilities required include close, distance, color, and depth perception.</li>
</ul>
