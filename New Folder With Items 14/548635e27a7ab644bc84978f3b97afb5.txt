<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Are you that “go-to” resource because of your deep knowledge and expertise? Our Project Manager holds the key to our customers fully experiencing the value of our fuel management software solutions and generating revenue for our company. Our customer-obsessed implementation team works on projects for our customers from the planning stage to executing the goals of the contract.
<br><br>
<b>In this role:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for managing projects critical to the success of the customer software as a service (Saas) and services solution implementations</li>
<li>Develop and maintain project plans</li>
<li>Drive and control scope, timelines and budget of customer implementations</li>
<li>Prepare Statement of Work (SOW) estimations</li>
<li>Work with various products to gain functional and technical knowledge to assist with customer implementations</li>
<li>Serve as the liaison between the customer and cross-functional teams, i.e. systems, integration, and operations to ensure that all internal dependencies are in line with customer timelines and objectives</li>
</ul>
<b>On your first day, we’ll expect that you will have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Successfully lead revenue generating implementation projects on schedule with high quality</li>
<li>Ability to communicate with leaders at all levels within the company and customers throughout the implementation process</li>
<li>Engaged and inspired cross-functional teams throughout the design, development, support and product management process</li>
<li>Familiarity with the software development lifecycle</li>
<li>PMP preferred</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science field preferred or equivalent experience</li>
</ul>

<br><ul>

</ul>
