<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h4>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<p>Laird is seeking an experienced antenna engineer with management background to lead a team in the development of advanced antennas for commercial markets. The ideal candidate must thrive in a fast paced environment, be a team player, and must be able to deliver against deadlines.</p>
Responsibilities
<ul>
<li>Lead a team of engineers in the design and development of antennas from 50 MHz to 10 GHz+ from initial concept stages through production launch.</li>
<li>Design antennas using computer simulation software HFSS.</li>
<li>Evaluate antenna performance using network analyzer and antenna chambers.</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver antenna concept reports and final antenna performance reports internally and to leading OEM customers.</li>
<li>Work as part of an engineering design team with mechanical engineers, the antenna lab, project managers, product managers and sales managers.</li>
<li>Research trade publications, patents, technical papers and existing products to determine the “state of the art" of antenna technology.</li>
</ul>Requirements
<ul>
<li>Experience designing multi-polarized antenna elements and arrays from 1.5 GHz through 6.5 GHz for Telecom applications.</li>
<li>Expert in simulating and optimizing antenna performance in HFSS or CST.</li>
<li>Experience in design, test, and production launch challenges.</li>
<li>Experience managing development engineering personnel.</li>
<li>Experience in designing and testing low PIM antennas.</li>
<li>Hands on experience in using Network analyzers for evaluating S-Parameters using gating in time domain.</li>
<li>Fluent in matching techniques and use of the Smith chart.</li>
<li>Hands on experience in using Anechoic chambers.</li>
<li>Hands on experience with Matlab.</li>
<li>Experience in Low cost antenna designs.</li>
<li>Proven Track record of antennas on the market.</li>
<li>Ability to complete antenna projects from initial concept to product launch with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Works well in a Team environment and guides the team as the “expert”.</li>
</ul>Education &amp; Experience Requested
<ul>
<li>Minimum Bachelor, M.Sc. degree preferred in Electrical Engineering or a related field.</li>
<li>10 or more years experience.</li>
