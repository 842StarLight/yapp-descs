<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Title
</b></h3>Project Executive
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h3>
<p>As a subsidiary of Toyota Industries (TICO), Vanderlande North America is enjoying rapid growth and extraordinary market demand. Vanderlande is a global market leader in material handling system manufacture, design, installation, and service. Vanderlande systems employ the latest in robotic, mechanical, and software technology. Vanderlande’s customers are household names in e-commerce, package delivery, and air transportation.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Project Executive:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Project Executive is responsible for the successful delivery of our Material Handling Systems at major distribution hubs and Warehousing facilities throughout North America. This Project Executive must have a proven track record with demonstrable positive results as the responsible manager in charge in the delivery of large (greater than $100M) and complex projects or programs in the Material Handling, General Construction, or similar project-driven industries.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Builds and organizes multi-disciplined team(s) of Project Manager(s), Site Managers, Engineers, Planners, Administrators, and others as required to lead delivery of large and complex projects in excess of $100M or groups of projects comprising multi-year programs at select distribution hubs in North America.
</li>
<li>Leading project teams through sales, pre-construction (systems engineering), execution, and closedown phases.
</li>
<li>Leads the administrative, commercial, technical and financial management and control of all assigned projects.
<ul>
<li>Establishes team expectations based upon corporate objectives
</li>
<li>Puts mechanisms and processes in place to ensure expectations are fulfilled
</li>
<li>Creates and maintains continuous improvement loop to monitor performance and improvement opportunities.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Coordinates with external stakeholders from middle management to C-Level stakeholders to deliver program requirements while protecting Vanderlande interests.
</li>
<li>Coordinates with Government and Authority Having Jurisdiction including but not limited to Building Department, Building Permit Contractor, OSHA, FMI, TSA, Fire Department, EPA, DOL, Port Authority, Airport Authority; as well as city, state, and federal governments to fully comply with the law.
</li>
<li>Develops and maintains appropriate relationships with labor, including local unions, to ensure successful project delivery.
</li>
<li>Puts organization in place to address requirements of internal stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Serves as a proactive leader and problem solver to address complex issues and situations as they arise.
</li>
<li>Implements and leads program level Risk Management process.
</li>
<li>Mentors project team members.
</li>
<li>Sufficient understanding to contract law to successfully negotiate and execute prime contracts, subcontracts, and change orders.
</li>
<li>Leads training, development, and evaluation of the performance of all reporting employees. Works with other department managers in reference to the needs and performance of members on the project teams.
</li>
<li>Directly supports sales activities as required.
 </li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Qualifications and Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS in Engineering, Construction Management, or similar technical field.
</li>
<li>Experience working with Amazon Transportation and Logistics
</li>
<li>Minimum 10 years Project Management experience of increasing responsibility leading teams in delivery of large, complex projects with demonstrable positive results; including at least 7 years of experience as the responsible person in charge.
</li>
<li>Delivery of projects in Fixed Price and Cost Reimbursable methods.
</li>
<li>Direct experience with government contracting requirements for project delivery.
</li>
<li>Exceptional and demonstrable leadership capabilities in creating unified and motivated project teams.
</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills – must have command presence.
</li>
<li>Customer facing – Ability to deal with challenging and rapidly changing customer requirements
</li>
<li>Strong, independent decision-making ability.
</li>
<li>Solid and demonstrable negotiating skills.
</li>
<li>Travel, as required for position.
</li>
<li>Strong business acumen who understands “Thinks Big Picture”.
</li>
<li>Experience leading delivery execution of complex projects such as material handling systems, institutional or manufacturing facilities, or similarly complex projects.
 </li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired </b><b>Qualifications and Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Professional Engineer or similar credential.
</li>
<li>Project Management Professional (PMP) or similar credential.
</li>
<li>LEED AP Accredited Professional.
</li>
<li>Graduate Degree (technical or MBA)
</li>
<li>Exposure to European and American cultures.
</li>
<li>Pre-existing relationships with Amazon, DHL, UPS, FedEx, or similar.
</li>
<li>Ability to coordinate tasks in a small team.
</li>
<li>Ability to work in an entrepreneurial international organization, especially working within a project organization, where cooperating with others and being a team member is a natural.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Type
</b></p>
<p>This is a full-time position
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Travel:
</b></p>
<p>This position requires up to 50% travel.</p>
