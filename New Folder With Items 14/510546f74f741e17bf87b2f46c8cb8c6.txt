<h2>Business Intelligence Internship</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Team: Business Intelligenc</b></div>
<div><b>
Job Description:</b></div>
<div>
The Business Intelligence Internship position will successfully support all Business Intelligence activities related to data structure/ reporting, business development, sales, marketing and emerging technology. These duties will require working with all teams that support the overall sales process, which include ticketing, sponsorship, marketing, finance and creative. This role will assist with the planning and execution of essential tasks such as data capture, email marketing, target account research, reporting, lead management, event support, and business development campaigns. The ideal candidate should have a positive attitude and be highly motivated to multitask. This role will report to the Director of Business Intelligence.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the creation of department reports, surveys and other data centric measuring tools to help the decision-making process.</li>
<li>Assist with preparing and loading data for analysis, reporting and dashboards.</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal stakeholders to come up with creative solutions to client challenges.</li>
<li>Assist with the preparation of presentations including customer deliverables and internal documentation.</li>
<li>Assist with the development of email campaigns and workflows, including template design, message development, email setup, and performance tracking.</li>
<li>Identify partnership opportunities within specific industries. This will include market research, data mining, and studying the companies and trends within the national category targets identified by our internal team.</li>
<li>Assist with incubating and piloting the next wave of emerging technologies for clients and the BKSE enterprise based on applied research and development.</li>
<li>Collect, construct, and summarize data for project-specific needs.</li>
<li>Perform all other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Skills and Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Completed or pursuing a degree in Business Administration or Marketing is preferred, but not required.</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Dynamics or other CRM platforms preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with Marketo or other marketing automation software preferred.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and writing skills.</li>
<li>Highly task motivated with particular attention to detail.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills and advanced business knowledge.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint required.</li>
<li>Ability to stay focused and operate under pressure to meet tight deadlines.</li>
<li>Self-motivated to work alone, yet energetic in team situations.</li>
<li>Able to work non-traditional hours, in non-traditional settings.</li>
<li>Must be dependable, flexible and able to adapt to a variety of situations.</li>
</ul>
