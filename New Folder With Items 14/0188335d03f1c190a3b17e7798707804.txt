<h2>Membership Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Membership Representative, New York City </b>(Upper Manhattan and the Bronx)</p>
<p><b>Competitive Base Salary, Generous Commission Structure, Excellent Benefits </b>(estimated total salary range of $55,000 to $85,000 per year)</p>
<p><b>If you have experience as a sales professional and interested in working in an industry that is fun and the backbone of every town and city – then we have the job for you. </b></p>
<p><b>Position Overview: </b> A great opportunity with the original trade association for NYS restaurants - the New York State Restaurant Association (NYSRA). Founded in 1935, we work on behalf of restaurateurs and have an engaged and supportive membership and are looking to significantly expand membership in New York City. NYSRA provides restaurateurs with a platform for statewide cooperation and leadership on advocacy issues, along with powerful resources and support through education and events, staff training, and cost saving programs. The successful candidate will have the opportunity to manage a region with big upside potential for membership growth.</p>
<p><b>Position Profile: </b> The primary responsibility of the Membership Representative is to grow membership in the New York City market. The Membership Representative must understand and effectively communicate the overall value that the NYS Restaurant Association delivers to members, along with the benefits and features of each support service offered. The position also requires assistance with member retention, Association events in your region, and other projects in support of membership as needed.</p>
<p><b>Principal Responsibilities: </b> Become expert in the advantages and benefits of membership. In addition, develop comprehensive knowledge of association history, advocacy issue positions, and any other information necessary to understand and confidently present the membership opportunity to prospective members.</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and qualify leads through meetings with members, networking, trade shows, inbound inquiries, and other leads provided by the Association in order to build a consistent pipeline of member prospects.</li>
<li>Utilize restaurant visits, telephone calls, email and written correspondence, referrals, and other appropriate communication means to secure meetings with prospects.</li>
<li>Be informed about the challenges faced by restaurant owners and aware of current restaurant trends.</li>
<li>Make compelling presentations to prospective members outlining the value of membership and the features, advantages and benefits of our support and services offering, and answer any questions regarding membership.</li>
<li>Manage and overcome sales objections pertaining to membership, and confident in closing the sale.</li>
<li>Follow up with prospects in a respectful manner until sale is made or prospect has clearly indicated they are not interested in membership at this time.</li>
<li>Represent NYSRA at networking events (sometimes evenings), trade shows and/or other events where recruitment efforts can be maximized.</li>
<li>Maintain database of prospects and activities through CRM software and report weekly activity to Director of Membership.</li>
<li>As needed, contact members who have not renewed membership and attempt to resolve any issues and retain membership.</li>
</ul>
<p>K<b>nowledge/Skills/Abilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Natural ability to quickly build rapport with prospective members.</li>
<li>Have a hospitable and service-oriented attitude.</li>
<li>Possess the skills required to present in a clear and compelling manner.</li>
<li>Have the drive and motivation to achieve and help our members be successful.</li>
<li>Be a team player who can work with peers and staff to successfully deliver the information and services our prospects and members need.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 years of professional sales experience.</li>
<li>Associates or undergraduate degree preferred.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work in Microsoft Office, CRM software.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>Regular Work Week: Monday – Friday: 9:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m.</li>
<li>Love Your Weekends and Evenings? We rarely work weekends, holidays or evenings because that is when restaurants are busiest.</li>
<li>Other Work Hours: On occasion, you may need to attend meetings or networking events outside of regular work week hours.</li>
</ul>
