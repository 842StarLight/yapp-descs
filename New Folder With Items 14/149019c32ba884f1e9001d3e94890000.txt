<h2>Commercial Leadership Program (CLP) - GE Power (2020)</h2>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role Summary:</b></h2>
The GE Power Commercial Leadership Program (CLP) is the premier program for developing entry-level talent into future commercial leaders. This challenging two-year rotational program is focused on the accelerated development of sales talent at GE. CLP offers a core curriculum that fosters the development of commercial skills and techniques that are critical to success in all GE businesses.<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<div>
As a Commerical Leadership Program - CLP member, you will accelerate the growth and<br>
development of exceptional talent through a dynamic sales curriculum that promotes the<br>
specialized knowledge and skills inherent to GE’s future sales leaders. You will leverage GE’s<br>
boundaryless selling environment; offering a framework where members can harness their<br>
outstanding communication, leadership, and technical skills. As a member, you will develop and<br>
demonstrate an understanding of quality customer partnerships. You will partake in three to four<br>
six-month rotational assignments advanced sales training, business and leadership courses,<br>
mentoring and career planning during two years.</div>
