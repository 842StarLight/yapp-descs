<p><b><i>
About Nuspire Networks:</i></b><br>
At Nuspire our mission is simple – keep our customers safe from cyber-attacks with best-in-class security services, so they can focus on managing their business. We've been protecting organizations from cyber-attacks for 20 years and we pioneered the distributed, managed security model within the enterprise and franchise market</p>
<div><b><i>
Position Description:<br>
</i></b></div>
<div></div>
<p>Nuspire is one of the fastest growing Managed Security companies in the industry targeting the industrial sector and end location businesses in North America, Europe and Latin America. One key driver to our growth is executing outbound inside sales activities to our target market.</p>
<p>
As a result of our tremendous growth, Nuspire is looking for entry-level inside sales professionals. Position description desires motivated and goal-oriented individuals focused on finding, contacting, and cultivating business opportunities within our program sales team. The sales representative will target end locations for large organizations across North America. Position is based in Commerce Twp., Michigan.</p>

<br>

<br>
<p><b><i>
Responsibilities:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Learn and immerse yourself in cybersecurity technology and current events.</li>
<li>
Learn and articulate Nuspire's benefits and outcomes to our target market.</li>
<li>
Uncover opportunities and leads via outbound calling, email, and digital activities</li>
<li>
Receive continued mentorship, coaching and training in proven sales techniques and systems</li>
<li>
Understand clearly our target market's business and information security environments, challenges and pain-points</li>
<li>
Work with our franchise clients to promote our managed security solutions.</li>
<li>
Increase sales and average order size by working with the client to determine their specific business needs.</li>
<li>
Follow up on requests for information and leads from email, phone calls, web chat, community and other sources.</li>
<li>
Make at least 80 outbound calls per day on warm leads.</li>
<li>
Make follow up calls to new clients to ensure satisfaction and encourage conversion.</li>
<li>
Working closely with team members as well as other departments to increase knowledge of services, with the goal of increasing sales and improving processes.</li>
<li>
Follow up on requests and orders in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>
May be expected to perform other duties as assigned by manager.</li>
</ul>
