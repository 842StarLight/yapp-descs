<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>– WHO WE ARE –</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>We at McKinley Equipment Corporation strive to be the most trusted sales and service distributor in our marketplace. We accomplish this mission by being the best in our industry when it comes to installing and servicing loading dock and warehouse equipment.</p>
<p></p>
<p>As a family-owned company, we’re always innovating, expanding, and taking our industry into unprecedented territory. With our advanced systems and proprietary technology, it’s no wonder why three of America’s top 10 retailers and dozens of top builders / general contractors trust us for installing and servicing their warehouse equipment. So join us if you want to be a part of something bigger than yourself, because while most people are here to play the game—we're here to change it.</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>– JOB DESCRIPTION –</b></h2>
<p>As a full-time McKinley Equipment Technician, you are responsible for the most important part of our company: servicing our customers’ equipment. <b>You’ll handle everything from material lifts and loading dock equipment, to commercial doors, balers, and compactors.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>As the person with the most face-to-face interaction with our customers, you represent the McKinley name in every job you complete, which means<b> your mechanical, electrical, welding, pneumatic, and hydraulic expertise coupled with your ability</b> <b>to troubleshoot problems quickly, all while providing the best customer service,</b> is the key to your success.</p>
<p></p>
<p>McKinley has thrived for over 70 years because of our extremely high-quality technicians who are both experts and professionals. Does this sound like you? Then join our team!</p>
