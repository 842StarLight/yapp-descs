<h2>Claims Tech - CDA (Part-Time)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
We're currently looking for an individual to join our Claims Data Administration team to assist with routine administrative duties. In this role, you’ll perform document identification, data entry and validation functions, and additional miscellaneous administrative duties.</p>
<p><b>
This position is required to work onsite in Stevens Point, WI.</b></p>
<p>
To meet the needs of our department, we’re looking for part-time associates with availability during the following times:</p>
<ul>
<li>Monday: 7:00am - 7:00pm</li>
<li>Tuesday – Friday: 7:00am – 6:00pm</li>
<li>Saturday: 7:30am – 4:00pm</li>
</ul>
<p>
Schedules are based on your availability and business needs and are created two weeks in advance. Shift may vary between 3 hours to 8 hours.</p>
<b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><p>
As a member of our Claims Data Administration team, you’ll:</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform various activities as they relate to the identification, data entry, and verification of claims related mail; this includes processes related to the handling of unidentified mail and items which are not conducive to scanning</li>
<li>Perform data entry related to mail processing; including medical bills</li>
<li>Responsible for monitoring various systems and reports to ensure accuracy within the overall mail transaction process</li>
<li>Perform quality control checks on scanned documents to ensure images have a high degree of clarity and readability</li>
<li>Search for and identify mail within our claims system and determine if mail is for an existing claim or First Notice of Loss (FNOL)</li>
</ul>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>0-2 years of related work experience</li>
<li>Basic PC knowledge and skills</li>
<li>Ability to accurately and efficiently enter and verify data on a PC</li>
<li>Previous data entry experience beneficial</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
<li>Good organizational and communication skills</li>
</ul>
