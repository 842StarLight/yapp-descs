<h2>Business Development and Account Support Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>Verizon Connect is guiding a connected world on the go by automating, optimizing and revolutionizing the way people, vehicles and things move through the world. Our full suite of industry-defining solutions and services put innovation, automation and connected data to work for customers and help them be safer, more efficient and more productive. With more than 3,500 dedicated employees in 15 countries, we deliver leading mobile technology platforms and solutions.</p>
<p>
As a SaaS leader, we know our talent is the most important component to our success. We hire top talent and empower them to do their best work. As a division of Verizon, we combine the fun and excitement of a start-up environment with the resources, operational excellence, and brand recognition of an established tech giant.</p>
<p>
The Business Development and Account Support Manager is responsible for helping to grow revenue and achieve business results with Verizon Connect’s OEM partners, as well as identifying and implementing new logo opportunities. As a Business Development and Account Support Manager, you will work with diverse groups of stakeholders – both within Verizon and our OEM partners – to establish connected vehicle strategies and business plans, and to support the business to growth and performance expectations. Business Development and Account Support Managers have a deep understanding of the connected vehicle ecosystem, and work with their OEM partners to identify value-creation opportunities, propose new services and business models, and work with internal teams to launch new business initiatives.</p>
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li><p>
Provide support to existing OEM accounts across consumer and commercial TSP, and wireless businesses</p></li></ul>
<ul><li><p>
Help achieve targeted revenue from OEM accounts by proposing, nurturing and contracting for additional opportunities and developing creative, profitable business models.</p></li></ul>
<ul><li><p>
Grow revenue from the existing OEM customer base by working with Marketing, Sales, and other internal teams to improve business performance through promotions, incentive programs, outreach and other tactics.</p></li></ul>
<ul><li><p>
Identify land and expand opportunities with new logos.</p></li></ul>
<ul><li><p>
Define, submit and manage the product initiatives necessary to grow the business.</p></li></ul>
<ul><li><p>
Serve as point of contact for OEM partners in managing developing relationships.</p></li></ul>
<ul><li><p>
Provide leadership to the teams of people responsible for making the OEM the businesses successful.</p></li></ul>
