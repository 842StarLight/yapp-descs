<h2>Clinical Specialist, Cardiac Rhythm Heart Failure, Houston TX</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p>Provide technical, educational, operational and sales support to assist the district in meeting CRHF sales and customer service objectives. CRHF seeks candidates who will meet our customer expectations by striving without reserve for the greatest possible reliability and quality in our products, processes and systems by being accountable, having a voice and taking action.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
A DAY IN THE LIFE: POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul><li> Technical Support</li></ul>
<p>
o Successfully completes CRHF Field Technical Training – including online and field-based training</p>
<p>
o Performs checks/interrogations of all CRHF medical device systems post sign-off</p>
<p>
o Supports implants of all CRHF products post sign-off</p>
<p>
o Provides on-call support as needed on evenings and weekends post sign-off</p>
<p>
o Provide troubleshooting support as requested</p>
<ul><li> Operational Support</li></ul>
<p>
o Assures completion of patient registration and any other required hospital documentation</p>
<p>
o Helps manage consignment inventory and trunk inventory once obtained</p>
<p>
o Provides additional inventory support to meet customer needs</p>
<p>
o Maintains software on programmers throughout the district as directed</p>
<p>
o Assists with managing daily coverage logistics as needed</p>
<ul><li> Educational Support</li></ul>
<p>
o Educates and trains physicians, hospital personnel and office staff on products or solutions for which training has been completed</p>
<p>
o Assists with educating and training new Clinical Specialists and Sales Representatives post sign-off</p>
<ul><li> Sales Support</li></ul>
<p>
o Partners with DSM, Sales Reps and DM to learn market dynamics and local customer motivations and needs; has a basic understanding of how Medtronic CRHF products and solutions offer value to the customer</p>
<p>
o Assists with obtaining customer POs throughout the quarter</p>
<p>
o Has a basic understanding of the competitive landscape</p>
<p>
o Independently identifies customer needs and opportunities and provides feedback to Sales Rep(s) to support sales objectives</p>
<ul><li> Ongoing Technical Development</li></ul>
<p>
o Stays up to date on new products, solutions and patient management offerings</p>
<p>
o Completes all assigned training in a timely manner</p>
<ul><li> Performs other related duties as assigned</li></ul>
</div>

<p><b>MUST HAVE - BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p><i>
IN ORDER TO BE CONSIDERED FOR THIS POSITION, THE FOLLOWING BASIC QUALIFICATIONS MUST BE EVIDENT ON YOUR RESUME</i></p>
<ul>
<li> A High School Diploma PLUS a minimum 7 years of work experience in the healthcare or engineering field, OR</li>
<li> An Associate’s Degree PLUS a minimum 5 years of work experience in the healthcare or engineering field, OR</li>
<li> A Bachelor’s Degree PLUS a minimum of 3 years of work experience in the healthcare or engineering field, OR</li>
<li> A Bachelor's Degree and currently enrolled in a certificate program from a pacing school like ATI or PrepMD and 3 years of prior professional experience</li>
</ul>

PHYSICAL JOB REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
