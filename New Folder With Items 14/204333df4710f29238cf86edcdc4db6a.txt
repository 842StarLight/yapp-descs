<h2>Automation Engineer - Erie, PA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About Premier Automation:</b></p>
<p>
Premier Automation is an engineered solutions company that specializes in providing automation, drive and robotic control solutions and services to industrial markets. Premier Automation offers custom industry specific technology solutions to help our customers increase their industrial productivity, efficiency and overall systems performance. We take the time to understand their unique process and requirements, and then work closely with them to develop the best control solution for their need.</p>
<p><b>
Job Profile:</b></p>
<p>
As an Automation Engineer at Premier Automation, you will be interfacing with customers to meet their various custom specifications. To create these innovative solutions, you will be designing and programming PLC and HMI hardware with support from Automation team members.</p>
<p><b>
Job Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Some travel required (25-50%)</li>
<li>
Lead/Participate in the design, implementation, testing, commissioning, and evaluation of automation systems. This includes the responsibility to manage projects and other resources.</li>
<li>
Develop industrial automation programming projects from start to finish. This includes creation and maintenance of software for a given project, PLC and HMI programming, and field service commissioning.</li>
<li>
 Professionally communicate with customers both verbally and in writing concerning project status, program needs and reports</li>
<li>
 Interact with internal staff and shop floor personnel, as required, in the performance of job responsibilities.</li>
<li>
Formulate and perform factory acceptance test plans for completed systems.</li>
<li>
Work with customer to finalize system design, controls package, and operator interface.</li>
<li>
Complete Factory Acceptance Test and Site Acceptance Test.</li>
<li>
Provide PLC and HMI programming field support.</li>
<li>
Generating Bill of Materials</li>
<li>
PLC Hardware design and drawings</li>
<li>
Interfacing with customers to meet expectations</li>
<li>
Provide expert technical advice on PLC's and HMI's to customers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Desired Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
4-year Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering or related degree.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 3 years of PLC and HMI programming experience</li>
<li>
High level of technical knowledge</li>
<li>
Start up and commissioning experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge, skills &amp; abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
 </b>Ability to effectively communicate both verbal and written with internal and external cusomers at all levels.</li>
<li>
Complexity and Problem-Solving skills. Ability to function as a technical expert to equiptment and systems regarding troubleshooting and operations.</li>
<li>
Strong troubleshooting skills with automation systems, ladder logic programming experience, and experience with SCADA systems</li>
</ul>
