<h2>Enterprise Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Backed by leading venture capital investors including Sequoia, NEA, and Scale, Forter is a fast-growing, enterprise SaaS startup that has already established itself as a leader in the online fraud prevention market. Forter's fraud prevention system is designed to be consumer-centric, blocking fraud with accuracy and at the same time enabling growth by increasing approvals and ensuring an excellent customer experience. Forter's ability to increase sales and reduce chargebacks at scale has enabled the company to sign up many of the largest e-commerce merchants and online travel agencies in the world.
<br><br>
We are looking for an extraordinarily talented individual to help build a world-class sales team. The Enterprise Account Executive will be a foundational member of the team responsible for driving new customer acquisition and establishing and nurturing a strong pipeline. The Enterprise Account Executive must be highly driven - having a key entrepreneurial role in establishing Forter's presence in the US. We are looking for a self-starter with exceptional executive presence. You have to be able to manage a broad territory and be able to skillfully run the entire sales process supported by a team of the top fraud experts in the business.
