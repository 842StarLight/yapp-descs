<h2>Executive Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Multi-unit Pure Barre fitness studio ownership is looking for a highly-motivated, smart, and enthusiastic Bookkeeper/Executive Assistant to provide secretarial and administrative support. This position will support the owners in keeping the company organized, goal-oriented, and future-focused. Both in-person office work and remote work will be required.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This is a full-time position, with hours fairly consistent with traditional 9a-6p Monday-Friday “office hours.” However, some hours may from time to time become irregular due to larger projects, events, and major sales. To apply, please provide us with a cover letter telling us about yourself and why you think you’ll be a good fit for the position.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Bookkeeping Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Performs full-charge bookkeeping and maintenance of the company’s Quickbooks file</li>
<li>
Prepares financial statements, analysis, and detailed reports on a monthly basis and as requested</li>
<li>
Maintains detailed and accurate records for accounts payable and expense reporting</li>
<li>
Works as a liaison between vendors to streamline communication and information</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Executive Assistant Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Aid owner in marketing campaigns &amp; social media.</li>
<li>
Maintain schedules for front sales staff.</li>
<li>
Maintain owners’ schedule including internal and external meetings, 1-on-1s, and various team meetings.</li>
<li>
Identify, anticipate and prepare information and other requirements for the owner before meetings, appointments, presentations, etc… and follow-up with requests for information, outstanding reports, and correspondence.</li>
<li>
Head up and/or assist the owner in special projects, such as work with retail vendors, collaborations, events, etc.</li>
<li>
Enter new retail inventory as well as maintain inventory stock levels.</li>
<li>
Maintain and manage the sharing of google drive files</li>
<li>
Sort and distribute incoming email, faxes, and postal mail/packages.</li>
<li>
Monitor CRM system and scheduling software to ensure correctness and completeness.</li>
<li>
Aid owner and lead instructor in planning in and out of studio events and special activities.</li>
<li>
Audit paid digital marketing leads.</li>
<li>
Be willing to serve as an occasional runner for business-related errands for the owner as requested to support the reduction of owner's workload.</li>
<li>
Any other duties as self-assigned or assigned by the owner.<br>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years of bookkeeping experience</li>
<li>
2+ years of administrative experience</li>
<li>
Strong skills in Quickbooks, Google Apps, and MS Office (especially excel and google sheets)</li>
<li>
Experience in fitness studio management software (Mindbody/ClubReady/etc) is a plus</li>
<li>
Willing and able to work at the fitness studio/office locations, from home, and remote locations.</li>
<li>
Highly self-motivated, able to move through many parts of the work with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>
Strong customer service phone skills</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Outstanding organizational and time management skills.</li>
<li>
Positive “can-do” attitude.</li>
<li>
Ability to use discretion and maintain confidentiality.</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask and independently prioritize daily workload.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of office systems and procedures</li>
<li>
Enthusiasm for the boutique fitness industry is a plus.</li>
<li>
Experience in calendar management, travel arrangements, expense claims, and good telephone manner.</li>
<li>
Be able to work as part of a team and have the desire to be part of a growing company</li>
<li>
Must have a valid California driver’s license.<br>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
