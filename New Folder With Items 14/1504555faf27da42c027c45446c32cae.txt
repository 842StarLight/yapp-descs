<h2>Project Manager, Hydraulics Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are looking to fill an open Project Manager, Hydraulic Engineering position.</p>
<p><b>
POSITION DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Design custom Hydraulics &amp; Pneumatics presses for thermoset material applications.</li>
<li>
Determine project objectives by reviewing proposals and customer requirements.</li>
<li>
Provide correct drawings and bills of materials to manufacturing in a timely fashion on sales orders.</li>
<li>
Provide correct drawings and manuals to the end customer in a timely fashion on sales orders.</li>
<li>
Provide technical assistance to sales to help sell more machinery.</li>
<li>
Provide technical assistance to manufacturing to insure on time delivery within the cost estimate.</li>
<li>
Provide technical assistance to customer service to answer customer related problems.</li>
<li>
Provide technical assistance to end customers installing and starting up our machinery.</li>
<li>
Refine existing designs to reduce cost and improve performance.</li>
<li>
Incorporate assembly and field requested improvements in a prompt fashion.</li>
<li>
Control engineering costs within the projected budget.</li>
<li>
Follow all ISO 9001 procedures and work instructions and participate in Q100 quality efforts.</li>
<li>
Other duties may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS:</b> Proficient with SolidWorks, AutoCAD, SolidWorks, Simulation and Microsoft Office (main focus in Excel).</p>
<p><b>
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES: </b>This job has no supervisory responsibilities.</p>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b> To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p><b>
EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE: </b>Bachelor’s degree in Engineering (BSME), 5+ years design experience of custom capital equipment and machinery.</p>
<p><b>
OTHER QUALIFICATIONS: </b>Willingness to travel to customer locations, generally 10-15% of the time, but rarely would exceed 30% of the time. Knowledgeable in computer assisted engineering and design software and PLC’s.</p>
