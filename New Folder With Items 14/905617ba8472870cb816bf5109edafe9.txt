<h2>Business Development Representative (Outbound)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Company Overview:</b><br>
ARCHIBUS, global provider of an integrated workplace management system (IWMS) platform, and Serraview, a cloud-based provider of space optimization and workforce enablement software, recently announced that they have merged to create a leading provider of solutions for managing the modern built environment. This powerful business combination unites the ARCHIBUS comprehensive suite of real estate, infrastructure, and facilities management solutions with Serraview's cloud-first, employee-centric space management software to help organizations optimize the "Workplace of the Future." The combined company will offer thousands of customers around the world an integrated IoT powered, user-friendly platform to effectively manage their real estate facilities, infrastructure, and workplace assets, and enable employees.
<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
As a part of the BDR team, you will use your skills and training to build interest and create new opportunities for the Account Executives. If you work hard and produce results in this role, the opportunity for growth is unparalleled!
<br><br>
Top qualities for an individual successful in the BDR role include being competitive, tenacious, results driven, energetic and highly motivated to succeed. Displaying strong time management skills while in a fast-paced, changing environment is also imperative. A strong candidate must demonstrate behaviors associated with a high-performance sales culture in order to deliver consistent results against a sales quota.
<br><br>
<b>Day to Day Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strategically identify, prospect, and qualify leads based on ideal customer profile</li>
<li>Manage pipeline through a high volume of strategic calls, emails, social completing 75+ activities a day</li>
<li>Demonstrate the value of our offering through phone call, email, LinkedIn and other social mediums</li>
<li>Leverage resources effectively to create targeted messaging and educate prospective clients, highlight key features and benefits, overcomes objections, and gain buy in for solutions</li>
<li>Conduct the research required to understand prospect's business, technology and product requirements and develop a compelling value proposition.</li>
<li>Contact prospective companies to gather key qualification data and qualify opportunities for Account Executive team</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and detailed activity records in Salesforce CRM</li>
<li>Work with Account Executive team to identify ongoing strategic targets</li>
</ul>
<b>You'll love this job if you:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Have 1-3 years outbound sales development experience, preferably in the SaaS industry, prospecting, cold calling</li>
<li>Are energetic, competitive, tenacious, and results driven</li>
<li>Have clear and concise communication skills, oral and written</li>
<li>Know how to manage you time efficiently and are disciplined with your daily approach in fast paced environment</li>
<li>Are eager to learn and constantly develop product &amp; market industry knowledge</li>
<li>Are hungry for feedback and passionate about developing your sales skills</li>
<li>Are familiar with Salesforce.com &amp; Microsoft Office, LinkedIn (and/or other prospecting tools)</li>
</ul>
