<h2>Relationship Manager III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary:
</b></p>
<p>Proactively grows bank revenue by focusing on prospective companies to offer an appropriate suite of products and services in response to the client's needs. Build a portfolio of clients by performing the following duties:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Identifies and solicits prospective banking relationships meeting the Bank's client profile and evaluates creditworthiness while ensuring compliance with Bank's credit policy.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>Effectively manage, develop and retain quality business relationships by providing clients with outstanding service and solutions.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Earn the client's full banking relationship by leveraging and cross-selling the Bank's suite of products based on client's needs.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor client portfolio including overdrafts, past due loan payments, credit and collateral exceptions and loan grades. Identify any deteriorating credit situations and elevate as necessary to credit executives.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Desired Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<p>
Minimum of seven years proven and progressive business development and credit experience</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree - finance or accounting preferred</li>
<li>Strong sales, analytical, negotiation, communication and teamwork skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated and driven to succeed in organically growing new business</li>
<li>Computer knowledge to include spreadsheets, Internet software, E-mail, and word processing.</li>
</ul>
