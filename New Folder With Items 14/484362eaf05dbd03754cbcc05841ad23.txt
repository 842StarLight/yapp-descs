<h2>Senior Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Senior Account Executive
<p>Equinix is one of the fastest growing data center companies, growing connectivity between clients worldwide. That’s why we're always looking for creative and forward thinking people who can help us achieve our goal of global interconnection. With 200 data centers in over 24 countries spanning across 5 continents, we are home to the Cloud, supporting over 1000 Cloud and IT services companies that are directly engaged in technological innovation and development. We are passionate about further evolving the specific areas of software development, software and network architecture, network operations and complex cloud and application solutions.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>At Equinix, we make the internet work faster, better, and more reliably. We hire talented people who thrive on solving hard problems and give them opportunities to hone new skills, try new approaches, and grow in new directions. Our culture is at the heart of our success and it’s our authentic, humble, gritty people who build The Magic of Equinix. We share a real passion for winning and put the customer at the center of everything we do.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Plans, builds, and maintains relationships with key partners in assigned accounts/prospects
</p></li>
<li>You facilitate strong customer relationships to ensure timely resolution of customer issues
</li>
<li>Conducts quarterly business reviews with customers to identify and develop new selling opportunities
</li>
<li>Leads Executive Briefings
</li>
<li>Leads and drives coordinated sales approach with the extended sales team (Sales Engineers, Solutions Architect, Customer Care, SSA, Commercial Solutions, Sales Operations, etc.) and external partners (Reseller, Strategic Alliance, etc.)
</li>
<li>Collaborates with global sales team to sell the Equinix platform
</li>
<li>Demonstrates consistent cross region exports
</li>
<li>Understands business drivers of accounts and leverages strategic alliances and reseller partners to penetrate accounts
</li>
<li>Researches and documents detailed understanding of customer business and organizational landscape
</li>
<li>Drives strategic, global, multi-line of business global account plans with Equinix top customers globally
</li>
<li>Drives coordinated selling approach and account planning with global counterparts
</li>
<li>Identifies customer’s business needs, challenges, and technical requirements to match to Equinix solutions
</li>
<li>You lead the delivery of compelling pitches, in partnership with SE/SAs where appropriate; customizes pitch to customer and persona
</li>
<li>You have proven proficiency of the Equinix product set and solutions
</li>
<li>Sells full suite of Equinix offerings to include global footprint and achieves exports
</li>
<li>Uses external partners to drive solution development in new areas/prospects
</li>
<li>You actively address high churn risk customers leveraging internal resources and external partners when needed
</li>
<li>Facilitates customer contract renewals and negotiations to protect revenue
</li>
<li>You partner closely with internal resources to understand customers contractual obligations around notice periods, renew terms, Equinix exposure
</li>
<li>Actively monitors and maintains status of opportunities in SFDC, following the principles of forecasting
</li>
<li>Identify at risk accounts, expiring contracts and forecasts churn
</li>
<li>Prioritizes list of accounts/prospects for short and long-term pursuit to achieve assigned sales objectives
</li>
<li>Provides accurate forecasts
</li>
<li>May focus on particular vertical or sub-vertical within a dedicated sector
</li>
<li>Coordinates with Opportunity Development Team to strategize lead &amp; sales opportunity qualification
</li>
<li>You pursues the highest propensity prospects, filling the funnel with opportunities; pitching prospects primarily at C-level; utilizing your industry contacts and partners
</li>
<li>Actively prospects account base to sell global platform and achieves outside of country
</li>
<li>Utilizes available tools to prospect and use account strategy plans
</li>
<li>Leads commercial offer and contract negotiations, using internal resources as needed to obtain the best commercial terms possible
</li>
<li>Understands commercial levers and problem solves to make initial recommendations on deal structure; overcome objections and position and engage and senior levels in account
</li>
<li>Partners with sales leadership to present at regional deal review
</li>
<li>Mentors Account Executives or other sales professionals; leads special projects, provides mentorship to team on new products/processes/best practices
</li>
</ul>
