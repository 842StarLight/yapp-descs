<h2>Manufacturing Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><p>Manufacturing Engineer / Programmer
<br>
<br>
<b>Position Purpose:
</b><br>
<br>
Write programs for CNC manufacturing equipment and create shop routers. Develop and implement cost-effective manufacturing processes and methods in accordance with product specifications and quality standards. Recommend and implement improvements to production processes, methods and controls. Coordinate manufacturing launch for new products.
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:
</b><br>
<br>
1. Create manufacturing parts routers and work instructions from engineering drawings and specifications. Select equipment and establish processes and associated time standards to be employed during production.
<br>
2. Use CAD/CAM software to generate accurate and highly efficient programs for turning, drilling, milling, punching and burning operations.
<br>
3. Create nests for optimal utilization of burn tables.
<br>
4. Propose and implement manufacturing cost reduction and quality improvement ideas. Provide feedback and recommended solutions to Engineering, Sales, and Costing departments on any identified issues or opportunities for improvement.
<br>
5. Work with suppliers and production personnel to analyze and select the most cost effective production speeds, feeds, tooling, jigs, fixtures and software to be used during production.
<br>
6. Coordinate the manufacturing launch of new products and train others as required.
<br>
7. Research, recommend and provide cost justification for various tools, machinery and equipment.
<br>
8. Analyze and plan work flow, space requirements and equipment layout.
<br>
9. Lead or participate in assigned projects as required.
<br>
<br>
<b>Skills/Competencies:
</b><br>
<br>
1. Action/Results Oriented/Execution
<br>
2. Analytical Thinking/Problem Solving
<br>
3. Technical Functional Skills
<br>
4. Short Term Execution
<br>
5. Communication
<br>
6. Planning and Organization
<br>
<br>
<b>Education and Experience:
</b><br>
<br>
1. Bachelor’s degree in technical field or equivalent combination of education and experience preferred. Associate degree required.
<br>
2. At least 2 years’ manufacturing experience with knowledge of machine tools.
<br>
3. Must be able to read drawings.
<br>
4. Proficient in computer assisted manufacturing (CAM).
<br>
<br>
<b>Job Success Factors:
</b><br>
<br>
1. Ability to accurately and efficiently create cost-effective CNC programs for equipment.
<br>
2. Ability to independently assess and implement manufacturing cost reduction opportunities.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Physical Requirements:
</b><br>
<br>
1. Must be able to travel throughout the plant on a regular basis.
<br>
2. Must be able to effectively use a computer with all its components.
<br>
3. Must be able to effectively utilize various office equipment (phone, computer, fax machine, printer, copier, filing cabinet).
<br>
<br>
Travel:
<br>
<br>
1. Minimal travel required.</p></div></div>
