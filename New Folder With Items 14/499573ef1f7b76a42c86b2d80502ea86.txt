<h2>HR Generalist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job ID</b>: 2127<br>
<b>Position:</b> HR Generalist<br>
<b>Location:</b> San Mateo, CA HQ</p>
<p>
Aryaka is looking for an HR Generalist to join our team in our San Mateo, HQ office. Our ideal candidate will be an experienced professional responsible for providing comprehensive, proactive HR services in a variety of areas: benefits compensation, HRIS, HR Metrics, performance management, on-boarding, ensuring legal compliance, but also have a passion for all things people. This person must possess the ability to multitask and adapt in a fast paced start-up environment.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with the HR team in completion of company-wide processes such as performance reviews, merits and bonuses, updating job descriptions, benefits communication and enrollment, and rewards and recognition programs.</li>
<li>Develop strategy and content for Human Resources communications.</li>
<li>Develop business acumen and demonstrate advanced knowledge of HR metrics.</li>
<li>Develop and analyze data report findings and trends and ask critical questions needed to evaluate data.</li>
<li>Communicate the impact on organization strategy of relevant and important findings from data analysis.</li>
<li>Maintain data integrity of various HR systems (e.g., HRIS, benefits, Performance Management) and works to identify root cause when issues arise.</li>
<li>Create accurate regular and ad-hoc HR metrics and reports with strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>Recommend and support changes to assure a strong and productive culture and work environment.</li>
<li>Evaluate critical activities in terms of value added, impact and utility derived from cost-benefit analysis.</li>
<li>Provide continuous process improvement, and suggestions to assist function in meeting departmental goals.</li>
<li>Work with HR team members and external resources to drive timely completion of HRIS, Benefits, exit interviews and other HR projects as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experienced background in the high-tech industry.</li>
<li>Ability to exercise good judgment and discretion while maintaining confidentiality.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively support diverse functional client groups including commercial and technical managers and employees.</li>
<li>Strong persuasive and relationship-building skills.</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of employment law, with an emphasis in California employment law.</li>
<li>Expert-level knowledge in at least one or two functional areas of HR.</li>
<li>Strongly demonstrated organizational and project management skills with the ability to multi-task.</li>
<li>Excellently demonstrated verbal and written communication, including presentation skills.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).</li>
<li>Experience with US payroll.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business field of study.</li>
<li>Five plus (5+) years progressively responsible diversified experience as Human Resources Generalist including experience in a global business environment</li>
<li>Experience using HRIS systems, running reports, maintaining data integrity</li>
<li>Experience in using other HR tools such as ATS and LMS.</li>
<li>Experience in utilization of sound investigation, and reporting practices</li>
</ul>
