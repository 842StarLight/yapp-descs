<h2>HVAC Service Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>The Role</i></b>
</p>
<p>We are looking for an exceptional service manager to lead our team of talented technicians.
</p>
<p>As a service manager, you will work as part of a dynamic and passionate team to maximize capacity, efficiency and profitability. You will manage staff and exceed customer expectations by accomplishing the highest quality service calls, every time.
</p>
<p>By working closely with our CSR's and Dispatch, you will match the right technician to every job, helping achieve the company's financial goals.
</p>
<p>You will routinely ride along with the technicians throughout the market while having a locally based office.
</p>
<p>We are willing to train technical and industry experience.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Responsibilities</i></b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Integrate sales strategies across other departments to drive profitable results.
</li>
<li>Show technicians how to establish customer rapport to sell the right products and services.
</li>
<li>Understand sales criteria and hold your team accountable for exceeding revenue goals.
</li>
<li>Work alongside customer service and dispatch to ensure overall success of the business.
</li>
<li>Monitor performance of field technicians and advise them on how to improve.
</li>
<li>Manage team schedule to meet ongoing customer demand and seasonal spikes.
</li>
<li>Make sure incentive programs are equitable and properly documented.
</li>
<li>Review and approve invoices, time cards, curbside feedback and option sheets.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Requirements</i></b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record in service, with 3 years in management.
</li>
<li>
<b>Ability to inspire and lead others to attain company goals.</b>
</li>
<li>Good presentation skills; able to convey confident and decisive messages.
</li>
<li>Multi-tasker who enjoys working in the field with diverse teams on varied projects.
</li>
<li>Detail oriented and highly organized with exceptional follow-through abilities.
</li>
<li>Top performer who can instill pride of workmanship in self and others.
</li>
<li>Passionate about Acosta and loves the brand.
</li>
<li>High integrity with advanced social skills and ability to make solid connections.
</li>
<li>Strong desires to motivate technicians to be better tomorrow than they are today.
</li>
<li>IT competent, previous experience with Microsoft Office and the latest sales applications.
</li>
<li>Outgoing personality that blends well with a fast-paced, goal-driven environment.
</li>
<li>Highly motivated, self-starter who's flexible and has a great attitude on life.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</p>
<ul>
<li>Medical Benefits Available: health, dental, vision, life insurance, short-term and long-term disability
</li>
<li>New technology, including iPhone, iPad &amp; access to integrated software
</li>
<li>401k Plan with a company match
</li>
<li>A family. This is last on the list because it’s most important. We care about our team, and expect you to bring that same caring when you join. We do a lot more than just work together. You’ll come to love our company outings, and you’ll build life-long friendships at Acosta.<br>
</li>
</ul>
