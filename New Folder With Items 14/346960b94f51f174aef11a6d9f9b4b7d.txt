<p>Job Description Summary:
</p>Sr. Salesforce Developer Lead
<br>
<br>
The global sales operations team supports the functions essential to the global sales force. These include planning, sales training and on-boarding, process optimization, compensation design and administration, sales technology management and administration, sales and partner reporting, rev share and account management.
<br>
The Sr Salesforce Developer Lead will lead large-scale, highly complex technical initiatives and/or projects requiring integration of cross-functional systems. Provides technical guidance in evaluating applications systems or evaluating requests for proposals. This person will be managing a team of developers and working directly with key stakeholders globally including; business leads, solution/technical architect, business analyst and quality leads to help analyze and design solutions for business requirements. Sr Salesforce Developer Lead will not only be responsible to take on the development work but also work with offshore development teams to meet the deadlines and deliver quality work.
<p></p>
<p>Job Description:
</p>
<div>Responsibilities:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Participate in all phases of software development life cycle including working with architects in understanding the design of requirements, prototyping, coding, testing, deployment and support
</li>
<li>Develop high quality and scalable application logic with Apex and SOQL
</li>
<li>Develop highly interactive UI using Lightning Components and JQuery technologies
</li>
<li>Develop Salesforce Marketing Cloud applications using JourneyBuilder, Salesforce Sync etc.
</li>
<li>Develop custom applications using Force.com and integrate salesforce.com with other systems
</li>
<li>Apply best practices and design patterns of best of breed applications developed on the Force.com platform
</li>
<li>Design and generate Salesforce dashboards, reports and statistics that support better decision making and process improvement within the organization, including sales forecasting, win/loss analysis, and market trend analysis, etc.
</li>
<li>Perform administrator functions such as user management, profiles, roles, permissions, rules, assignment rules, queues, licenses, capacity and storage management
</li>
<li>Assist with database de-duping and clean up procedures, including data migrations and/or mass data uploads
</li>
<li>Coordinate and manage developers in the team, architects, business analysts, and project managers on evaluation, scope and new development requests
</li>
<li>Work closely with project leads and administrators on system upgrades and regular internal system audits
</li>
<li>Develop process frameworks, deployments and automation in salesforce and related salesforce tools, such as Apttus and Seismic,
</li>
</ul>
