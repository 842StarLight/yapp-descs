<h2>Inside Sales / Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Purpose And Responsibility:</b></p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will support the Expert TA’s sales and marketing teams and will directly engage with many of our potential clients. When individual instructors, or entire departments, decide to use the Expert TA system, the account executive would function to help establish instructor accounts, customize the program as possible to suit any particular needs, and offer training to help an instructor or entire department get started with using the system.</p>
<p>
Employees are responsible for maintaining the highest level of customer service in support of internal teams and client relationships.</p>
<p>
At Expert TA you would:<br>
Work with sales, marketing, and product delivery teams to support the company’s internal needs as well as the clients.<br>
Work with and become part of the sales team to make sure all client relationships are being cultivated and assist where needed.<br>
Build strong relationships with faculty and university leadership in order to become a trusted adviser.<br>
Become an expert with our software so that you can support the needs of clients.<br>
Be involved with the development and execution of marketing efforts.</p>
<p><b>
Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Account Executive – As Expert TA grows and expands into new disciplines, we will need more and more account executives who will need training, oversight, and guidance from experienced individuals.</li>
<li>Inside Sales – The sales responsibilities will involve learning the Expert TA system in detail and engaging directly with clients to provide support and offer training sessions. The skills and knowledge would allow for a natural transition into a key account and enterprise level sales role.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you need for this position:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Previous Sales experience</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>Strong computer skills and ability to quickly learn and apply new technologies</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to follow through independently, executing well-defined objectives</li>
<li>Strong aptitude in Math and Sciences</li>
<li>Good interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Customer service and account management experience</li>
<li>Technology consulting</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Additional Company Information:</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Our salaries are competitive and commensurate with experience. We are a performance-based culture and have goal-oriented incentive programs. At Expert TA we believe each employee, regardless of their responsibility, can make a difference for our clients, the software program and for themselves. Expert TA is built on a strong foundation of integrity, community and leadership. We are an equal opportunity employer.</i></b></p>
