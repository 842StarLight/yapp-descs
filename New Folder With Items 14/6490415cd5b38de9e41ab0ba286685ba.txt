<h2>PPC Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div><p>This is an exciting career opportunity for an experienced PPC campaign specialist to join a reputable digital marketing agency that’s based in Colorado with a great team of individuals who are passionate about the work they do.</p></div>
<div><p>
The ideal candidate is hard working, ethical, a natural problem solver, self-motivated, and emotionally balanced professional that loves to help businesses succeed and can promote digital marketing solutions to support their business efforts.</p></div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b></h4>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Lead accounts in the area of planning, strategy, and execution</li>
<li>
Build and manage campaigns on Google Ads</li>
<li>
Set up new Pay Per Click (PPC) accounts.</li>
<li>
General management of PPC accounts, including, budget management, bid management, ad copy testing, landing page optimization, etc.</li>
<li>
Analyze data to develop effective strategies to help partners reach their goals.</li>
<li>
Research and implement new PPC updates and strategies. * Develop an understanding of client products, services, verticals, competition, and business models</li>
<li>
Conduct keyword research using multiple tools</li>
<li>
Conduct competitor research where appropriate</li>
<li>
Work with content and marketing teams to drive SEO in content creation and promotion</li>
<li>
Make recommendations to both the size, scale, and effectiveness of your advertising efforts to both sales staff and current and potential clients</li>
<li>
Manage campaign expenses, staying on budget, estimating monthly costs and reconciling discrepancies</li>
<li>
Deliver weekly and monthly KPI reports to communicate performance trends and developments</li>
</ul>
