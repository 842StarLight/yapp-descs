<p><b>Accenture Microsoft Business Group – CMT Senior Manager</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>What we are looking for?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Technology Advisors, individuals who not only understand the Core Microsoft platforms but how it affects their area of the business; trends associated with the technology; functional area and industries and the value propositions for clients to rotate to the new</li>
<li>Individuals who have a desire and experience in client-facing roles and who possess skills in communicating to clients about how the Microsoft platforms can help them achieve their initiatives</li>
<li>This is a fast-paced, dynamic career path so we are seeking individuals who come from or will be comfortable in an "up or onward" model where learning, growth and development are a constant essential part of becoming a successful consultant</li>
</ul>
<p>People in our <b>Client &amp; Market</b> career track drive profitable growth by developing market-relevant insights to increase market share or create new markets. They progress through required promotion into market-facing roles that have a direct impact on sales.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>A professional at this position level within Accenture has the following responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Adapts existing methods and procedures to create possible alternative solutions to moderately complex problems.</li>
<li>Understands the strategic direction set by senior management as it relates to team goals.</li>
<li>Uses considerable judgment to determine solution and seeks guidance on complex problems.</li>
<li>Primary upward interaction is with direct supervisor. May interact with peers and/or management levels at a client and/or within Accenture.</li>
<li>Determines methods and procedures on new assignments with guidance.</li>
<li>Decisions often impact the team in which they reside.</li>
<li>Manages small teams and/or work efforts (if in an individual contributor role) at a client or within Accenture.</li>
</ul>
<p>It is our intention for our people to live where they work and work where they live. However, in the context of our profession and serving our clients there may be times when you will need to travel.</p>

<p><b>Basic Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 3 years of experience with implementing infrastructure and/or enterprise software architectures as part of Technology transformation programs</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of either Infrastructure, Microsoft Azure, O365, Modern Workplace, or Dynamics project experience</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of experience with Infrastructure Platforms and/or Microsoft in the Communications, Media, Technology or Manufacturing Industries</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>Microsoft Azure certification or similar Infrastructure credentials/certification</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Enterprise Infrastructure Platform and/or Microsoft Platform client facing sales and service experience</li>
<li>Ability to define and communicate the “big picture” in terms of the overall integrated solution (both functional and technical components) and break it down into smaller manageable work units</li>
<li>Deep knowledge of Microsoft’s enterprise technology platforms</li>
<li>Journey to Cloud strategy and implementation experience</li>
<li>Experience in working with onshore and offshore teams</li>
<li>Proven ability to work independently as well as with a team</li>
<br>
</ul>
