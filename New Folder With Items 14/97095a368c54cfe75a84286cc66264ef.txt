<p><b>Overview:</b></p>
<p>The Distribution Center Manager has responsibility for the coordination of all distribution functions. Reporting to the Plant or Materials Manager, the Distribution Center Manager is responsible for coordinating all shipping, receiving, late point packaging, customer service functions, traffic, inventory control, storage and handling of both raw materials and finished goods. This position is responsible for managing the department/facility budget. The Distribution Center Manager has supervisory responsibility for distribution personnel.</p>
<p><b>Principal Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead the DC Teams to support company objectives in cost, quality, service and safety.
<ul>
<li>Cost – manage the DC budget and drive productivity by reducing the volume-adjusted cost to operate year over year.</li>
<li>Quality – maintain inventory accuracy at 98% through cycle counting. Reduce shipping errors by 10% year over year.</li>
<li>Service – ship 98% finished goods material within business team specific shipping window targets.</li>
<li>Safety – provide a safe working environment as measured by ORIR.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Provide responsive customer service to all customers, both internal and external. Interact frequently with corporate sales team.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain an effective system of operation and implementation of written procedures in the form of Standard Operating Procedures, ISO 9001 and Technical Operating Instructions.</li>
<li>Establish, implement, and maintain effective training programs and safety programs.</li>
<li>Manage the traffic functions within corporate guidelines including the use of approved carriers, the Transportation Management System and routing guide.</li>
<li>Support transportation cost reduction initiatives.</li>
<li>Provide an environment for LeanSigma culture, techniques and principles to flourish within the DC.</li>
<li>Work with the Plant Manager to establish goals and objectives for the department.</li>
<li>Responsible for the control and repair of all returnable reels.</li>
</ul>
