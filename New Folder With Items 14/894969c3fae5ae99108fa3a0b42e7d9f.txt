<p><b>Shipping and Stock Associate</b></p>
<p><b>Position Description</b></p>
<p>A Shippingand Stock Associate receives shipments, packs and ships orders, checks forshipment accuracy, stocks items, and returns any defective merchandise.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities of a Shipping and StockAssociate</b></p>
<ul>
<li>assists inunloading shipments to the receiving area</li>
<li>unpacks inventory</li>
<li>places allocatedinventory in its designated location in the stockroom and expands the stockroomwith orderly allocation for new parts</li>
<li>organizesinventory in the stockroom by placing items in their designatedlocations</li>
<li>uses forklifts totransport bulk shipments or heavy items</li>
<li>pulls stock listedon BOM’s and places them in designated locations in assembly area the firstthing each morning</li>
<li>answers sales Associatequestions about available items in stock</li>
<li>retrieves itemsfrom the stockroom at their manager’s request</li>
<li>conductsinventory as required</li>
<li>uses inventorycontrol software to log information; experience with Sage Pro ERP is a plus,but not required</li>
<li>assists managersand Associates in preventing theft and maintaining proper inventory</li>
<li>pulls defectiveitems from shelves at the manager’s request</li>
<li>packs defectiveitems or overstock and arranges the return shipment</li>
<li>observes propersafety procedures in the stockroom, particularly when lifting and moving heavyitems</li>
<li>reports issues orproblems to inventory manager</li>
<li>keeps a clean andorganized work area during shifts and ensures their work-area is tidy afterclosing</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>possesses physical strength necessary to regularlyunload, lift, and carry boxes and equipment from, roughly, 30-40 lbs</li>
<li>must regularly use tools to lift objects that areheavier than 50 lbs and be mindful of their own maximum lifting capacity</li>
<li>exhibits strong organizational skills</li>
<li>will be required to learn shipping software</li>
<li>pays close attention to detail</li>
<li>demonstrates strong interpersonal and customer serviceskills</li>
<li>exhibits willingness to learn the range of thecompany’s products</li>
<li>works well with a team</li>
<li>communicates clearly and effectively</li>
<li>demonstrates the ability to read and interpret invoicesand packing slips</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience</b></p>
<p>A Shippingand Stock Associate typically holds a high school diploma or GED. Good computerskills are required, as you will be required to learn how to use ourstock-management software. You will also be required to learn how to operate aforklift.</p>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<p>TheShipping and Stock Associate will arrange and oversee company inventory in threeprimary areas of our facility: the shipping and receiving dock, production area,and stockroom. A typical workweek isapproximately 40 hours.</p>
