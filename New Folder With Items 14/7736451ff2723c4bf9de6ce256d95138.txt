<h2>Head of Procurement & Negotiation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About Us
<br><br>
MediaMath is a leading independent advertising technology company, working with brands and agencies. <br><br>
We need talent like you to fuel this next-generation ecosystem.
<br><br>
Key Responsibilities
<br><br>
We are looking for a senior executive to structure and drive "win-win" arrangements with our clients, partners and vendors. Reporting directly to the Chief Finance Officer, you will bring expertise to stand up and drive a best in class procurement function within our company to include optimization and management of MediaMath's partner &amp; supplier agreements.
<br><br>
The Head of Procurement &amp; Negotiation will not only develop overall cost reduction targets and oversee execution of these initiatives across the business – they will train key stakeholders on negotiation techniques to ensure all parties feel confident engaging in complex and critical negotiations designed to optimize results for all sides.
<br><br>
<b>You will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Create and execute a new global procurement process for MediaMath designed to create efficiency, deliver economic savings and maximize beneficial outcomes for all stakeholders –</li>
<li>Manage MediaMath's terms of agreement with our Media, Tech and Data supply chain partners</li>
<li>Negotiate/renegotiate terms of agreement with vendors which support all areas of the organization</li>
<li>Develop and implement cost reduction targets across MediaMath</li>
<li>Share procurement best practices with the business to help stakeholders drive discussions around pricing and procurement in an efficient and optimal manner</li>
<li>Build and maintain strong relationships with strategic clients, partners and vendors</li>
</ul>
<b>You are:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A self-starter, with the ability to build the roadmap and drive delivery through operational excellence</li>
<li>A relationship builder, excited by the opportunity to partner with team members cross-functionally</li>
<li>Able to challenge our "ways of working" to maximize our resources and enhance our success</li>
<li>Able to influence and facilitate groups with diverse perspectives and bring teams to consensus/alignment</li>
<li>Able to demonstrate prudent judgment when making important decisions by utilizing knowledge of industry best practices and current trends</li>
</ul>
<b>You have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A bachelor's degree</li>
<li>A minimum 10 years demonstrated procurement and strategic negotiation experience within an Adtech/SAAS environment</li>
<li>Experience leading negotiations with Fortune 500 companies</li>
<li>Achieved significant cost savings and optimal non-economic terms through the negotiation of key economic contractual and other terms</li>
<li>Believe in the value of "win-win" arrangements</li>
</ul>
