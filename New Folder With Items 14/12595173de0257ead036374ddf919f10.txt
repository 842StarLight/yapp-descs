<h2>Production Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Supervises product assembly and repair teams to ensure targets for quality/service/costs are achieved. Supervises resources for product assembly and repair operations. Primary responsibility is to ensure assembly and repair outputs are maintained to strict timetables, to standard operating procedures and quality assurance standards. Monitors and reports assembly and repair output and timelines and predicts potential problems such as machine breakdowns or material shortages. Assists with special projects assigned by the Production and Repair Center Manager.</p>
<p>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND BASIC DUTIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Plans resources to meet productivity requirements</li>
<li>Supervises staff to ensure company safety procedures, policies and administrative procedures are implemented and followed.</li>
<li>Reviews current work practices to identify areas in which quality or productivity could be improved.</li>
<li>Ensures all quality and standard operating procedures are being followed.</li>
<li>Liaises with sales support, customer support, warehousing and quality assurance to ensure prompt shipment of products.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining legible and accurate records including quality and assembly and other procedures to comply with regulatory requirements; Quality Systems Regulations and Standard Operating Procedures.</li>
<li>Provides leadership, support and direction to direct reports by following best practices.</li>
<li>Contributes to performance evaluation of direct reports.</li>
<li>Participates in talent acquisition and development.</li>
<li>Encourages an environment based on teamwork and continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Maintains health and safety standards for the workplace and individuals.</li>
<li>Performs additional duties, and completes projects as required.</li>
<li>Schedules and manages installations, preventative maintenance, repairs, and field service upgrades</li>
<li>Develops/maintains equipment service training materials for service engineers</li>
</ul>
<p>PERFORMANCE MEASUREMENTS</p>
<p>1. Policies are implemented effectively and are in accordance with quality initiatives and goals<br>2. Staff understands and complies with established policies and procedures<br>3. Timetables and deadlines are met or exceeded<br>4. Staff is effective and well trained</p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS</p>
<p>EDUCATION/CERTIFICATION: Associates Degree in related discipline or equivalent experience and education</p>
<p>REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE:</p>
<p>Knowledge of policy and procedure writing and implementation<br>Familiarity with quality systems and initiatives<br>Advanced computer skills including networking, server, SQL Databases and Citrix software<br>BME service skills including troubleshooting and electrical testing<br>Demonstrated leadership and mentorship skills</p>
<p>EXPERIENCE REQUIRED: Minimum of 6 years of related experience in similar role</p>
<p>SKILLS/ABILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Analytical Skills</li>
<li>Computer Literacy</li>
<li>Able to work in and manage teams</li>
<li>Good communication with written skills appropriate to all levels of the organization</li>
<li>Able to adopt a methodical approach, paying attention to detail</li>
<li>Planning and scheduling skills</li>
</ul>
