<div>Evergreen FS is farmer-owned cooperative serving the needs of customers in McLean, Woodford, Livingston, Dewitt and Macon counties. Providing a full range of farm supplies and services including agronomy, seed, fertilizer, fuel, structures, custom turf, financing, marketing advice and products. Specializing in the production of corn and soybeans and operate several grain elevators as part of our operations. Evergreen FS offers a full line of energy products including gasoline, ethanol, biodiesel, diesel fuel, propane and lubricants to our customers at competitive prices. Evergreen FS is a member of the GROWMARK system.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>PURPOSE AND SUMMARY STATEMENT</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Responsible for assisting in the direct operations of Farmtown retail store to ensure profitable sales, customer satisfaction, business retention, and company growth.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b></div>
<div>Assists customers with purchases, product information, product availability,information handouts, and items in the store or warehouse.
</div>
<div>Assists with operation of the store cash register, product checkout of purchases, and carrying out purchases.</div>
<div>Stocks shelves/bins, organize stock, cleans up and refills display items, and rotates and restocks all items in the store, warehouse, or external property areas.</div>
<div>Performs necessary housekeeping duties which may include sweeping, mopping, emptying of trash, cleaning of restrooms, cleaning the store equipment, and clear exterior sidewalks in order to maintain a clean, organized, and safe work area.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>OTHER JOB FUNCTIONS</b></div>
<div>May be required to exchange propane cylinders for customer empty cylinders,.
</div>
<div>Attend company and department meetings and training programs.</div>
<div>Follows Code of Conduct and Corporate Compliance Program, Environmental Health and Safety, OSHA and DOT policies and procedures, as they apply.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Performs all other duties as assigned.</div>
<br>
