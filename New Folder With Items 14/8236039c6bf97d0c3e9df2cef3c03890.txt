<h2>DATABASE ANALYST/ADVANCEMENT COORDINATOR</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Database Analyst/Advancement Coordinator will provide database management, analysis, and administrative support to Louisville’s Advancement Department.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Essential Job Functions:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Database Analyst/Advancement Coordinator's main responsibility is to maintain our Raiser’s Edge (RE) database and mine the data to help the Advancement Office reach its growth objectives. Specific database responsibilities include:</div>
<ul>
<li>Accurately and consistently input donor, gift and financial information</li>
<li>Maintain the integration between the school’s primary database (Core) and RE; and RE and Financial Edge (FE)</li>
<li>Add information into RE to make it a more robust database that can be mined for specific needs (includes student activities information, wealth management, constituent codes, relationship data, etc.)</li>
<li>Build queries to help identify donor potential, build guest lists, pull giving reports, create our annual financial report, etc.</li>
<li>Provide weekly donor reports for President and Director of Advancement</li>
</ul>
<div>The Database Analyst/Advancement Coordinator will also be responsible for managing other Advancement event software and performing the following:</div>
<ul>
<li>Input data, oversee auctions and run event software the night/day of event</li>
<li>Be present at all major fundraising events (3) and special events (2-3) throughout the year</li>
<li>Manage matching pledges and gifts through third-party corporate matching portals</li>
<li>Confirm receipt of funds and reconcile monthly bank statements through merchant account portals</li>
<li>Build event registration forms, monitor guest response and sales, generate reports and handle simple website updates through school Blackbaud website</li>
</ul>
<div>The Database Analyst/Advancement Coordinator will also be responsible for leading or assisting in mailings:</div>
<ul>
<li>Generate tax receipts and gift acknowledgments</li>
<li>Create recipient lists and assist with preparation of large mailings</li>
<li>Lead segmentation, letter generation and mailing process for annual fund appeal</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>At least two (2) years experience with relational databases, preferably Raiser's Edge; and/or a bachelor's degree in Information Science/Computer Science or related field; and/or comparable work experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of nonprofit institution operations and IRS requirements in posting and acknowledging gifts is preferred</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail and highly organized</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of accounting/bookkeeping</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple complex projects and tasks simultaneously and effectively</li>
<li>Strong writing and analytical skills</li>
<li>Able to work effectively in a collaborative setting</li>
<li>Ability to handle sensitive and confidential information in a manner that complies with government regulations and professional fundraising standards</li>
<li>Able to lift heavy objects (up to 25 lbs.) in preparation for events</li>
<br>
</ul>
