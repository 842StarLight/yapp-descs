<b>What we're looking for:</b><br>
The Mid Market Solutions Consultant will play a critical role in growing our client base of customers who have up to 500M in annual revenue across the United States and Canada. Do you have a strong curiosity to understand client business needs and technical requirements? Do you command a deep understanding of API centric architectures? You will help qualify technical requirements, demonstrate our capabilities and assist in solution design for our prospective clients. You will also have a deep understanding of how environments integrate between Zipwhip's platform, and 3rd party software partners.
<br><br>
Externally, you are driven to ensure an extraordinary solution design and experience for Zipwhip's enterprise customers. You will own defining the technical solution design required to win the hearts and minds of customers. Are you ready to combine your deep technical knowledge with your excellent interpersonal skills to confidently present to end users as well as Sr Executives? Although the role is primarily pre-sales, there may be a requirement to assist in implementation, deployment and post-sales optimization for select accounts.
<br><br>
Internally, you partner closely with sales and are a highly respected teammate. You will also establish trusted relationships with Engineering, Product Development, Product Marketing, and Customer Success. You take pride in championing the technical needs of our enterprise customers. You will be relentless problem solving and mobilizing resources to quickly resolve issues. At times you will write up detailed documentation of product feature improvement requirements. Your effort plays an influential role in building the enterprise business.
