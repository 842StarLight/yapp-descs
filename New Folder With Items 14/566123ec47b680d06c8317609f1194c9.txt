<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Business Development / SDR – Base + Commission</b></h2>
<p>Ready to join a growing and thriving inside sales team? If you've got a passion for selling and the ability to think on your feet, self-motivate and hunt for new opportunities, then you will thrive at TeamDynamix. We are hiring a Sales Development Representative (SDR) to drive new business by partnering with our outside sales and marketing teams. You will be supported by a fully integrated marketing program that consists of multi-channel activities including events, content distribution, webinars, and a strong digital backbone.</p>
<p>
The company leverages marketing automation and behavioral scoring to identify ‘warm leads’ and ideal customers. As an SDR you will be leveraging this technology along with multiple data sources to identify sales ready opportunities. Your sales team will also work with you to develop a strategic territory plan that you can use to uncover new opportunities in high impact areas.</p>
<p>
This is a position that includes ongoing learning and exposure to sales techniques and methodology aimed at giving you a foundation to eventually move into an enterprise sales role. We are looking for hunters with a quick wit and a strong will to win.</p>
<p>
TeamDynamix offers Service and Project Management together on a single cloud-based platform. The solution transforms IT from order taker to strategic innovator. Organizations in the public sector, education, and healthcare leverage the solution to improve IT maturity, optimize resources, and deliver enhanced end-user service. The platform delivers IT Service Management (ITSM), Project Portfolio Management (PPM), and Enterprise Service Management (ESM) together in one solution. More at TeamDynamix.com, @TDXBuzz, LinkedIn.</p>
