Job Description:</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>The GE Tier 1 Technical Support Agent provides heroic customer service by resolving product or services issues by clarifying the customer's concerns determining the cause of the problem; selecting and explaining the best solution to solve the problem; expediting correction or adjustment; and following up to ensure resolution.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Handle pre-sales, technical support, and order management inquiries from customers related to C by GE products and services.</li>
<li>Handle questions via telephone, email, and chat addressing hardware, software and app related issues</li>
<li>Maintain clear and thorough documentation of support emails, calls, and chats for existing products</li>
<li>Maintain ownership of cases through final resolution</li>
<li>Multitask through multiple systems and multiple tools while troubleshooting with customers</li>
<li>Escalate appropriate issues to the Subject Matter Expert(s) or to the client contact</li>
<li>Evaluate and take action on requests for exceptions to client support policies</li>
<li>Act as the last point of contact for the customer</li>
<li>Effectively communicate with team members and customers</li>
<li>Proactively identify ways to avoid recurring issues by recommending new and updated processes and technical articles</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Internal applicants performance and attendance must be in good standing, with no history of NCNS in the previous 90 days.</li>
<li>Internal applicants must not be on any issued documented coaching (verbal or written) or Performance Improvement Plan.</li>
<li>High School Diploma or Equivalent</li>
<li>A passion for exciting and new technology</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>Strong written, verbal, and organization skills</li>
<li>Ability to use critical thinking and logic skills to isolate and resolve technical issues in a timely manner</li>
<li>Time management and prioritization skills</li>
<li>Ability to navigate in a Windows and OSX environments, as well as Android and iOS</li>
<li>Comprehensive understanding of wireless, Bluetooth, and wired networks</li>
<li>Familiarity with mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets</li>
<li>Must act independently and be self-motivated</li>
<li>Ability to work any shift in a 24/7 operating queue, including weekends and holidays</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Candidate</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Previous experience supporting smart home and lighting products</li>
<li>Strong technical background in networking and network troubleshooting</li>
<li>Experience with case management CRM such as Zendesk or SalesForce</li>
</ul>
