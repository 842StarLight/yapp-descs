<h2>Purchasing/Inventory Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Who we are . . .</b></p>
<p>
RUG DOCTOR LLC is the worldwide leader of high-quality solutions designed to enhance the lives of our customers in the manufacturing, sales, and service of carpet cleaning equipment and products. Rug Doctor strives to create a positive and diverse work environment that values people, performance, customer commitment, quality and integrity. For more information on Rug Doctor visit our website at www.rugdoctor.com.</p>
<p><b>
Who we are looking for . . .</b></p>
<p>
Rug Doctor is currently seeking a full-time <b>Purchasing/inventory Clerk</b> located at our Fenton, MO facility that will be responsible for providing support to the Purchasing Department on an as needed basis and performing inventory cycle counts.
</p>
<p><b>What you will be doing (not all inclusive) . </b><b>. .</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide support to Purchasing Department by following up with suppliers as needed to confirm orders</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Track orders and ensure timely delivery</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Update internal databases with order details</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Scan and file documents as needed</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Perform cycle counts on a daily, weekly, monthly basis</li>
<li>Log counts on Excel spreadsheet and present to Inventory Control team</li>
<li>Document and report all inventory errors throughout the course of work performed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>
Education &amp; Talent we’re looking for (not all inclusive) . . .</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of JDE business software is ideal</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented with good basic mathematical aptitude, as much of the work requires thoroughness and accuracy</li>
<li>Good interpersonal/communications skills are necessary</li>
<li>Ability to work well in a high pressure team environment</li>
<li>The ability to learn quickly along with good memory retention is highly desirable</li>
<li>Exhibit team player qualities of cooperation and coordination; professional interaction in all business contacts, all day, every day</li>
<li>Present for work when scheduled is a mandatory function</li>
<li>Comfortable working in non-climate controlled environment</li>
<li>Comfortable walking through warehouse for entire shift including upstairs and downstairs</li>
<li>Previous inventory experience</li>
</ul>
