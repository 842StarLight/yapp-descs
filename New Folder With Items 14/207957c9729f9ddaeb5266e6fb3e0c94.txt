<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Plans, designs, develops, and executes organization’s marketing plan for assigned product by establishing effective strategies. Provides territory analysis of competition and potential Sales and Marketing Management.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage a marketing plan and corresponding budget:</li>
<li>
Ensure effective control of marketing results, and take corrective action to guarantee that achievement of product marketing objectives falls within designated budgets.</li>
<li>
Continuous concept and content renewal based on relevance to end-users, business goals and general segment needs</li>
<li>
Perform necessary competitive marketing audit to ensure plans are relevant and effective</li>
<li>
Create ongoing, strategic dialogue with Product Development (HS/SS) to ensure the following:</li>
<li>
Product information is being updated and distributed in a consistent format, to all strategic stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Create ongoing, strategic dialogue with Marketing Services (HS/SS) to develop and or implement the following:</li>
<li>
Coordinate advertising and public relations campaigns, including events, sponsorships and relevant trade shows.</li>
<li>
Create ongoing, strategic dialogue with Mohawk Interactive Group to ensure the following:</li>
<li>
All segment branded sites adequately represent the strategic business goals.</li>
<li>
Metrics are developed and monitored to ensure site effectiveness</li>
<li>
Site content is accurate and relevant to the needs of the segment and corresponding user community</li>
<li>
New content, applications and technologies are being leveraged to meet the strategic goals of the segment</li>
<li>
Oversee content development with Mohawk Creative to ensure the following:<ul>
<li>
Strategic consistency between segment brand standards Content is developed and leveraged within a consistent, accurate, integrated strategy across all media</li>
<li>
Ensures all product marketing materials are accurate and up-to-date.</li>
<li>
Establishes and maintains a consistent corporate image throughout the product lines, promotional materials, and events.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as necessary.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
