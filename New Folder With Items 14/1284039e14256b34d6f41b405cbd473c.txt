<h2>Sales Development Executive CPG</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Sales Development Executive is responsible for meeting and exceeding revenue objectives and sales goals through new business acquisition in the CPG category.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities </b>:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Make sales calls for the purpose of: Identifying and capturing all revenue opportunities, expanding knowledge base of customer business/objectives and requirements, building meaningful value-added relationships, selling the value of Valassis products/services and solutions and work toward establishing partnerships.
<ul>
<li>Penetrate and close new business opportunities
</li>
<li>Build and cultivate relationships to maintain and grow revenue in all accounts
</li>
<li>Use data, research and insights to create constructive tension with clients and lead them toward a Valassis solution
</li>
<li>Understand how Customers’ consumer marketing, advertising and trade/sales dollars are being spent to include: Competing for and securing every order or reporting it as competitive activity; create cross-selling and up-selling opportunities.
</li>
<li>Obtain and understand Customers’ marketing and advertising requirements and objectives
</li>
<li>Create demand for all Valassis Products, Services and Solutions.<br>
</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Effectively work through internal processes and procedures to support optimization of Reporting and Sales:
<ul>
<li>Maintain accurate and up-to-date forecasts and pipelines through effective utilization of Salesforce.com
</li>
<li>Manage customer information/intelligence in accordance with company policy<br>
</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education &amp; Knowledge </b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent combination of education and experience
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office and ability to learn new systems<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience / Skills and Competencies </b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2 - 5+ years of successful outside sales experience; preferably with CPG accounts.
</li>
<li>Proven sales experience including lead generation, solution selling and closing sales
</li>
<li>Experience in (highly preferred) and grows proficiency in:
<ul>
<li>Industry: Marketing, Media, Advertising background
</li>
<li>Digital products
</li>
<li>Selling multiple / integrated products
</li>
<li>Selling to the assigned vertical
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Strong skills in: qualification, overcoming objections, initiative, conceptual selling, presentation delivery and negotiation
</li>
<li>Tenacious hunter focused and skilled at prospecting and closing sales
</li>
<li>Ability to interpret marketplace needs and translate them into products/services<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Travel of 25% - 50% required<br>
</p>
