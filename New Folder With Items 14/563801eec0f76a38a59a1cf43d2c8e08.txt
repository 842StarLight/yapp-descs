<p>
As part of Wells Fargo Auto, the Auto Relationship Manager will be responsible for cultivating and maintaining relationships with medium to large and complex and/or high producing auto dealer accounts within the assigned market territory. This role will develop and execute strategy, proposals, and initiatives to generate additional revenues through optimizing business opportunities. Assesses the needs of the auto dealer to ensure satisfaction. Understands the profitability and risk of ongoing relationship. Develops and implements procedures to improve relationships if needed while also maintaining profitable relationships. In addition, responsible for securing any money owed to WF Auto (i.e. booksheet discrepancies, rebatables, etc.). Assists in clearing any customer generated complaints through the LOB or Enterprise complaints office. Ensures processes within the market territory support corporate objectives.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Requires travel within an assigned territory. Must live in assigned territory.</li>
<li>Uphold, support, foster and engage in Wells Fargo Vision and Values</li>
<li>Be able to assess a dealer’s needs and provide solutions to help them succeed financially</li>
<li>Train and educate dealers around Wells Fargo products and services</li>
<li>Maintain accurate dealer contact information</li>
<li>Responsible for partnering with other LOB within Wells Fargo</li>
<li>Ability to prospect dealer opportunity within an assigned market</li>
<li>Responsible for driving in applications from active dealerships</li>
<li>Complete annual dealer needs assessment</li>
<li>Ability to build relationships at all levels of the dealership</li>
<li>Meet pre-defined expectations for calling activity with accurate documentation in SOR both pre-call and post-call</li>
</ul>
