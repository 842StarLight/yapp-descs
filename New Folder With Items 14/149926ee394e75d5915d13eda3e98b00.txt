<h2>Project Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Project Administrator is responsible for coordinating project activities, including simple tasks and large plans. This position manages schedules and workflows, arranges assignments and communicates progress to all team members.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collect draw submissions for bank review to include General Contractors pay applications and lien waivers</li>
<li>Manage a construction web-based software that allows project stakeholders to work and collaborate from anywhere on construction documents</li>
<li>Manage accounting system to include invoice approval, contract/change order entry</li>
<li>Create and issue construction documents to include contracts, change orders, master service agreements, task orders and manage RFIs, submittals, and certificates of insurance</li>
<li>Manage the sales force system for project data to include creating and maintaining project data electronically through all stages of the project</li>
<li>Receive visitors at the front desk, answer and channel incoming calls, arrange travel, coordinate meetings, and all other administrative tasks assigned.</li>
</ul>
