<h2>Mortgage Financing Advisor - Riverview, FL</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div><div><div><div>Primarily responsible for developing and implementing strategic business initiatives for Pulte Mortgage, supporting the Builder, supporting Mortgage Operations, supporting Buyers and following all state and regulatory requirements.<br>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Having availability to meet with customers in order to provide mortgage financing expertise</li>
<li>Working with Buyers and the Sales Team in order to "capture" as many mortgage sales as possible in order to give the Builder predictability and visibility into their backlog</li>
<li>Pulte Homes sales training and building strong relationships</li>
<li>Backlog / Pipeline Management performance as measured by closing homes on-time &amp; providing extraordinary Customer Service</li>
<li>Branch operations performance as measured by, however not limited to, Capture, Customer Service, Compliance and Helping to Sell More Homes.</li>
<li>Professional development</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 - 5 years previous related experience in mortgage lending preferred</li>
<li>At least one year of Mortgage origination experience required
</li>
<li>3-5 years successful sales and/or business development background</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills along with strong computer proficiency with Microsoft Office applications, particularly Excel</li>
<li>Previous Mortgage experience is highly recommended</li>
<li>Must have high energy and be self-motivated to set and achieve sales goals</li>
<li>Must have excellent customer service and presentation skills</li>
<li>Incumbent must have the ability to work in a fast-paced, high volume environment</li>
<li>Must possess excellent time management skills</li>
<li>Must have the ability to manage multiple priorities and possess strong analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Must possess strong math aptitude</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>

</ul>
