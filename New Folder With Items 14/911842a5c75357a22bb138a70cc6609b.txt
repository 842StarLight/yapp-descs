<h2>Digital Experience Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Alcon, the world leader in ophthalmology, is looking for an experienced Digital Experience Designer to join an exciting new team focused on driving a novel digital business innovation forward.
</li>
<li>The Digital Experience Designer is playing a critical role in the commercialization, support, and ongoing development of this innovative venture.</li>
<li>This position will serve as a technical knowledge expert to provide ongoing in-market support for both the sales teams and escalated customer needs.</li>
<li>A successful candidate will be highly motivated, entrepreneurial and digitally savvy with a passion for people, digital innovation and creating digital converts.</li>
<li>Product Design.</li>
<li>Support every step of the user experience from concept to execution with design thinking, sketching, prototyping, testing, learning, polished execution and iteration, working closely with the Product &amp; Marketing teams.</li>
<li>Hands on delivery of concepts, personas, user journeys, storyboards, system maps, user flows, wireframes, visual design comps, and prototypes with Sketch, Adobe CS, InVision, and other tools.</li>
<li>Identify user pain points and design problems and devise elegant solutions.</li>
<li>Participate in, and over time begin to lead, concept sessions leveraging ethnographic research and design thinking.</li>
<li>Balance consumer/customer frictions and needs with business goals, market viability and technical feasibility.</li>
<li>Translate consumer/customer insights into concepts, personas, and user journeys &amp; illustrate design ideas using storyboards, process flows and system maps.</li>
<li>Incorporate user research and usability testing to iterate on and improve product solutions.</li>
<li>Create visual design deliverables based on the brand style guide (e.g. images, sketches and tables).</li>
<li>Deliver design assets in a format that can easily be consumed, and embedded by our engineering team for the various layouts and screen sizes Cross-Functional Cooperation.</li>
<li>Collaborate effectively with multi-disciplinary teams including, Product Managers, Engineers, Marketers, Customer Service and Content Strategists to gather and evaluate user requirements and translate into product design.</li>
<li>Partner with product and engineering to ensure designs are properly implemented.</li>
<li>Collaborate with brand/ marketing team to develop the brand vision and supporting assets to deploy in market for user engagement and acquisition.</li>
<li>Work with marketing in the development of visual collateral for marketing initiatives and develop brand guidelines, online advertising, and websites.</li>
</ul>
