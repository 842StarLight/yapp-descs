<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h2>
<p>MacB, an Alion company, has an immediate need for a Business Development (BD) Manager in Austin, TX. The BD Manager will lead account strategy development and be responsible for understanding customer needs, identifying and building critical relationships, and prospecting for new and unpublished opportunities. The BD Manager will identify and expand our presence within the Army Futures Command, Combat Capability Development Command (CCDC) Aviation and Missile Center, and U.S. Army Space and Missile Defense Command / Army Forces Strategic Command and other Federal Government agencies. The BD Manager's primary focus will be on developing business opportunities that can be executed on GSA contracts, including the OASIS Contract, as well as Defense Technical Information Center contracts. The BD Manager work closely with technical SMEs, program managers, and account managers to develop and deliver an actionable account strategy informed by critical relationships and client perception.</p>
<p>
Within the Advanced Technology Group and the Weapon System Solution Operation, Business Development Managers work with Account/Line managers and Solution Architects to develop strategies for growth and identify new market opportunities primarily for GSA and DTIC contract vehicles. BD Managers will represent Alion to customers, vendors, and teaming partners, making strategic assessments on capabilities and leading opportunity capture. Alion BD managers represent the voice of the customer to Alion account managers and participate in the entire capture process, from identification and qualification to proposal submittal.</p>
