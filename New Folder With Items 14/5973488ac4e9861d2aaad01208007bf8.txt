<h2>Wifi Systems Engineer III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Leads the design/delivery of technical solutions to customers, based on Ixia Wi-Fi products and Hawkeye Active monitoring solutions.
</li>
<li>Keeps abreast of market trends and competitive products, and identifies competitive differentiators.
</li>
<li>Engaged throughout the sales process, including requirements analysis, feature demonstrations, benchmarking, competitive positioning, and execution. Owns the deal’s technical closure.
</li>
<li>Understands customer’s technology, products, test requirements to recommend solutions to meet their needs, and ensures customer satisfaction.
</li>
<li>May build proof of concept test setup based on customer requirements to demonstrate feasibility of the solution, often requiring rapid prototyping.
</li>
<li>Integrates Ixia products and services at the system/solutions level into customer’s environment and ensures interoperability within the customer environment.
</li>
<li>Acts as the customer voice to influence future product design to meet customer needs.
</li>
<li>May deliver customer training classes and consulting related to installed solution and provide post-sales support when needed.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div>
<div>
Job Qualifications<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div><ul>
<li>BS Computer/Electrical Engineering
</li>
<li>Typically, 5+ years practical experience
</li>
<li>Minimum 3-5 years of experience as a Pre-Sales SE for a network vendor</li>
<li>
Strong hands-on experience with IP networking configuration and deployment</li>
<li>
Switching: Understanding of Campus Switching Designs, 2 and 3-tier models, STP avoidance, etc.</li>
<li>
Routing: OSPF, BGP, VLAN, etc.</li>
<li>
Automation Technologies and scripting skills: TCL, Python literacy</li>
<li>
General command line Linux skills</li>
<li>
Strong hands on troubleshooting skills with firewalls and IP networking</li>
<li>
Must have strong background in Networking and WiFi technologies
</li>
<li>Working WiFi experience</li>
<li>
Security: FW Rule Design, IPsec VPN</li>
<li>
Possess a solid understanding of network protocols (IP, UDP, TCP)</li>
<li>
Excellent initiative &amp; ability to align broad strategic and tactical goals</li>
<li>
Great customer facing skills; builds credibility and confidence in Ixia’s service offerings</li>
<li>
Collaborative and cross functional style - works with sales, development and marketing teams to aid in growing sales business.</li>
<li>
Ability to troubleshoot and solve problems</li>
<li>
Clear communicator with customers and peers</li>
<li>
Ability to live with and resolve ambiguity</li>
<li>
Travel required (minimum 50%)<br>
<br>
Relocation is NOT available for this position</li>
</ul></div>
