<p><b><i>Sales Support Associate</i></b></p>
<p>Quirch Foods; established in 1967, is a distributor and exporter of food products to the food retail and food service industries. The company is wholly owned and managed by the Quirch family, and is a certified Minority Business Enterprise. Quirch Foods is one of the largest distributors of food products in the Southeastern U.S. and the Caribbean, with a customer list that includes independent and chain supermarkets, food service distributors, processors, manufacturers, cruise lines, and restaurants. The company has been ranked as the largest exporter among the top 50 exporters in the U.S, and was ranked #5 in the Hispanic Business magazine by measure of revenue amongst Hispanic businesses in the U.S. Quirch Foods enjoys a rich past, a successful present, and looks forward to an exciting future. Our Passion, Your Joy.</p>
<p><b><i>Position Summary: </i></b></p>
<p>Quirch Foods is seeking an experienced Sales Support Associate to join our Sales team. The Sales Support Associate undertakes administrative job duties as it pertains to the sales department. Meets with sales employees to make new plans regarding sales or how to improve the job. Follows up with customers, completes orders, and processes sales orders.</p>
<p><b><i>Essential duties and Responsibilities: </i></b></p>
<p>The role will be exposed and impact the complete supply chain process with our customers:</p>
<p>· Improves customer service experience, engages customers and facilitates organic growth</p>
<p>· Keeps accurate records and documents customer service actions and discussions</p>
<p>· Coordinates travel arrangements, meetings, conferences, and events</p>
<p>· Assists customers with existing and new orders, account statuses and relevant problems</p>
<p>· Collaborates with internal departments for order coordination and delivery</p>
<p>· Provides any necessary data or reports to the sales team</p>
<p>· Other duties as assigned</p>
<p><b><i>Qualifications and Requirements: </i></b></p>
<p>· High School Degree or GED preferred</p>
<p>· Proficient in the use of Microsoft Office including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook</p>
<p>· Bilingual: English and Spanish preferred</p>
<p>· Ability to travel: 10 – 30%</p>
