<h2>Field Service Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>At Epredia, we improve lives by enabling better cancer diagnostics. We are a team of talented individuals with diverse backgrounds and expertise, working together to develop precision diagnostic solutions for our customers. We are a fast growing global company that drives innovation in diagnostics to create better outcomes for patients. Joining Epredia is much more than a job- it is an opportunity to be part of something with a purpose. Find out how you can improve lives while advancing your career.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Remotely based, in New Orleans, LA. Only, the Field Service Engineer is responsible for customer support activities, with a focus on preventative and corrective maintenance of instrumentation.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p>Utilize proven troubleshooting processes to diagnose and resolve technical issues on a variety of electromechanical instruments. Manage service call scheduling, parts inventory and administrative responsibilities with minimal supervision. The Field Service Engineer will be responsible for customer relationship management, written documentation of service activities, and effective interaction and communication with internal technical support personnel, manufacturing, and sales. The Field Service Engineer will represent the company in a positive, professional manner in all dealings with Epredia customers, both external and internal. Provide periodic reports to management including compilations of customer complaints, trend analyses, and suggestions for improvement. Provide on-site product installation and customer training, on an as-needed basis. Review available technical literature to maintain an awareness of current laboratory practices and procedures.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements/Qualifications
</b></p>
<p>= Associate’s Degree with applicable related experience.
</p>
<p>= 2-3 years of field service experience
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, Abilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to read, interpret and analyze schematics, assembly drawings and wiring diagrams
</li>
<li>Must possess basic software skills and computer literacy
</li>
<li>Good written and verbal communication skills, self-motivated and an effective team player
</li>
<li>Physical requirements include the ability to lift 50lbs
</li>
<li>Up to 90% travel may be required to meet customer needs
</li>
<li>Must possess a valid driver’s license
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS Degree in Biomedical, Mechanical or Electrical Engineering and a minimum of 2 years experience
</li>
<li>Familiarity with Anatomical Pathology Instrumentation, optical instrumentation, networking instrumentation and Laboratory Information Systems is preferred
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Non-Negotiable Hiring Criteria

<p>Associate’s Degree with applicable related experience.
