<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
CMM Operator</b></h2>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Your tasks:</b></h3>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Perform and record applicable testing of incoming raw material samples (resin, paint, etc) and disposition in SAP.</li>
<li>
Update and maintain material spec sheets.</li>
<li>
Perform and record results of various laboratory testing as needed</li>
<li>
Coordinate on site and third party calibration of gages</li>
<li>
Maintain gages and gage calibration</li>
<li>
Provide feedback of receiving quality issues to supplier development using SAP quality notifications</li>
<li>
Program CMM for part measurements</li>
<li>
Maintain CMM and related tools</li>
<li>
Measure parts and analyze layout data</li>
<li>
Perform Annual Layout per Customer specific requirements.</li>
<li>
Support PPAP approvals from Customer.</li>
<li>
Prepare and analyze MSA for gages</li>
<li>
Download CAD from company and supplier websites</li>
<li>
Provide CAD models for engineering changes, gages, and part modifications</li>
<li>
Analyze engineering drawings and schematics for gage and part dimensioning, review, and problem resolution</li>
<li>
Program part layouts in "Metrolog X4" software</li>
<li>
Interpreting of GD&amp;T and program the software accordingly</li>
<li>
Other duties as needed</li>
<li>
Each employee is responsible for the quality of his/her personal performance and how said performance impacts the environment, safety, and data security. This is applicable to the departments of development, production, management, logistics, and after market services, as well as to contact with customers and suppliers. Managers and supervisors are also responsible for the quality of his/her team's performance and how said performance impacts the environment, safety, and data security.</li>
<li>
This principle of comprehensive quality management is developed and furthered through training (on the job), continuing/ extended education, as well as continued supply of information and updates, and should be exemplified by supervisors on every level.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of customer specific requirements (CSR) necessary through advanced training, or work experience or a combination of the two.</li>
</ul>
