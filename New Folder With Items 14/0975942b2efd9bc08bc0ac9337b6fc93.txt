<h2>CIP Automotive Product Mgmt Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Intern Program begins June 1, 2020. Successful candidates must be currently enrolled in a college program with a targeted graduation of 2020 or 2021.</p>
<p></p>
<div>
Primary role:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Supports portfolio and product development that enable the planning and execution of automotive marketing and advertising strategies, including packaging, pricing, value position, and competitive benchmarking.</li>
<li>Leverages existing portfolio of data and analytic assets to uncover opportunities for automotive brands to improve their marketing and advertising performance</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
Job Complexity:<br>
<ul>
<li>Typically supports pre-existing products and assists on new product introductions.</li>
<li>Interfaces most closely with the product and solution management team and media sales team</li>
</ul>
<br>
Market Problems, Market Definition, Competitive Landscape: Supporting Engagement<br>
<ul><li>Supports research in core markets to drive understanding of unmet needs, market segmentation, competitive landscape, and market potential.</li></ul>
Product Strategy &amp; Roadmap: Supports<br>
<ul><li>Supports maintenance of existing product strategies and roadmaps based on Product Management guidance. Supports development of product strategy through collection of information that prioritizes enhancements taking into account customer needs, market trends and competition pressures.</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
Operational Leadership and Profitability: Supports<br>
<ul>
<li>Supports product financial performance for one or more products to ensure go-to-market and operational execution are delivering targeted results and promotes continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Assists in the preparation of team, management, and customer presentations</li>
</ul>
<br>
Product Requirements: Supports Market Collection<br>
<ul><li>Participates in the collection and collation of customer and market needs.</li></ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
Intern candidates should possess:<br>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated ability to independently manage multiple product initiatives in a matrix organization. Strong project management skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated written communications skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated verbal communication and presentation skills.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Strong analytical skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to identify, analyze, evaluate alternatives and formulate responses to resolve to business and customer problems</li>
<li>Quickly analyzes, incorporates and applies new information and concepts, and is able to make a effective decisions.</li>
<li>Proficient with PC and related software, including Microsoft Office suite.</li>
</ul>
<div>
Education:<br>
<ul><li>Seeking a junior or senior level student enrolled in a Bachelor of Science program focused on Business, Marketing, Economics, or related field, or equivalent combination of education and experience in industry/workflow.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to physically perform general office requirements.</li>
<li>Must be able to perform essential responsibilities with or without reasonable accommodations.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>
2020 Summer Intern Requirements:</b><br>
To be eligible to apply for a place on our intern program, you must be able to answer ‘yes’ to the following questions:<br>
Are you legally eligible to work for the duration of the intern program in the country for which you have applied?<br>
Will you have completed your studies in either 2020 or 2021? This program is open to current students only. If you do not reside in the IHS Markit office location for which you are applying, are you able to self-relocate in time for the program start date?</p>
