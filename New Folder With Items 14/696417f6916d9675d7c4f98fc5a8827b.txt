<h2>Senior Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Overview</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The primary focus of this role with be to support all reporting and analytical needs of the Go-To-Market (Sales, Marketing, Channel) functions at Mimecast. More specifically, this role will primarily be supporting Marketing and the Demand Generation Funnel. You’ll work very closely with senor leadership across Marketing to identify high priority, strategic needs, capture those needs as requirements and ultimately deliver effective solutions that drive growth. This role will require strong business acumen, a great deal of self-sufficiency, outstanding communication skills and the ability to work effectively with co-workers across the US and around the world.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate is someone looking to make an impact, driving business decisions through data and analytics. You have the intellectual curiosity to gain a full understanding of Mimecast’s business, along with the inner workings of the GTM organizations. You will provide the analytical horsepower to tackle complex cross functional challenges by using advanced modeling and analytical skills, with the ability to convert data into action.
</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Responsibilities</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Own the data (KPIs and metrics), analytics, and reporting to empower the Marketing function</li>
<li>
Prepare professional, polished presentations and present key insights to Senior Leadership (C-suite included)
</li>
<li>Manage multiple tasks and projects simultaneously, own deliverables end to end, and establish a consultative partnership with key stakeholders to achieve their goals</li>
<li>
Identify and adopt best practices in reporting and analysis: data integrity, test design, analysis, validation, and documentation
</li>
<li>Continually improve ongoing reporting and analysis processes, automating or simplifying self-service support for customers.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Qualifications</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Requirements for this position include strong expertise in requirements gathering, database/systems, and project management. Qualified candidate must be able to deliver on multiple projects over multiple timelines in a fast-paced environment.<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree; Master’s is a plus</li>
<li>
5+ years of proven Business Analyst experience, ideally with background supporting Marketing
</li>
<li>4+ years report and dashboard development experience using Tableau (or similar BI tools)</li>
<li>
Proficient in Alteryx</li>
<li>
Knowledge of, or experience with Sirius</li>
<li>
SaaS background a must</li>
<li>
Hands-on experience working closely with CRM systems, ideally Salesforce.com
</li>
<li>Experience working directly with data engineers and developers</li>
<li>
Stellar communication and presentation skills: You’re articulate and concise – both verbally and in writing</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively in a highly demanding environment with multiple competing priorities both independently and as part of a team. You will be confident partnering with other departments, and leaders within the business to achieve your objectives.</li>
</ul>
