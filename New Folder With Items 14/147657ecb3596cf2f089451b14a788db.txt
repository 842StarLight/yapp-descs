Description
<br><br>
M&amp;L Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram is seeking a motivated Sales Associate.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Greet and guide customer as they appear on the lot to proceed into the sales process</li>
<li>Assist the customers to find a vehicle that meets their needs by using the current inventory information</li>
<li>Enhance the sales process by demonstrating the vehicles features on the lot</li>
<li>Assist customers in the completion of their sales applications</li>
<li>Assist management to increase sales by inspecting the lot’s vehicles daily, reporting any problems to management</li>
<li>Assist management in ensuring the lot is merchandised correctly to maximize sales</li>
</ul>

<br><br>
<ul>

<li>Outgoing and friendly, especially while handling objections</li>
<li>Quality customer service skills and sales track record</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communications, in-person and over the phone</li>
<li>Persuasive and able to overcome customer objections during the sales process</li>
<li>Proficient in basic business math including percentages</li>
<li>Capable of learning and using Microsoft Office, accounting software, and proprietary sales programs</li>
<li>Persistent, competitive and good work ethic</li>
<li>Focuses on the customer’s needs to enhance dealership and personal sales</li>
</ul>
