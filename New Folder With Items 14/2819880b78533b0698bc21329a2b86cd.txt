<h2>Technical Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you a project manager with a technical bent? A relationship-builder who happens to be pretty technical as well? If so, you're the profile we're looking for in a Technical Account Manager. Our TAMs are responsible for building and maintaining the post sales relationship with our largest and most complex customers, and they usually have specific technical requests and needs that you will be critical in assisting with.</p>
<p>
Sound good? Read on to see if you'd make a great addition to the Storable team</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What you'll do everyday:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for building and maintaining post sales relationships with our largest and most complex customers.</li>
<li>
Provides account and project management and high-level technical support to project teams and customers</li>
<li>
Serve as a trusted advisor, ensuring successful deployment and adoption</li>
<li>
Provide advice and guidance as the Storable subject matter expert to ensure successful ongoing usage and adoption</li>
<li>
Design and develop innovative solutions to customer requirements</li>
<li>
Document technical requirements, schedules and client interactions.</li>
<li>
Test, conduct quality assurance and validate for technical feasibility and product launch.</li>
<li>
Deliver web based or onsite training to assigned Enterprise accounts to support organizational adoption</li>
<li>
Undertake discovery and education activities to identify opportunities for usage across organizational functions and processes</li>
<li>
Function as a frontline technical resource for "best practice" and informal customer questions</li>
<li>
Engaging with customer support as a customer advocate to ensure speedy resolution of customer issues</li>
<li>
Hold regular review meetings with customers to discuss any issues or problems and provide reports to the other members of the account team</li>
<li>
Engaging with Product management as the customer advocate on product roadmap discussions</li>
<li>
Analyze customers' support requirements and identify areas where the company can offer improved service or reduce support costs.</li>
<li>
Coordinate engineering and product teams, partners, and stakeholders, leading the strategy, execution, and troubleshooting of our largest customers.</li>
<li>
Build strong cross-functional working relationships with Sales, Engineering, and Product Management.</li>
<li>
Provide escalation management on critical cases by working closely with Support, Product, and/or Engineering to provide regular status updates to the customer.</li>
<li>
Conducts quarterly reporting and case reviews to spot trends, proactively identify potential issues, and offer tailored release recommendations to meet stability, performance, and new feature requirements</li>
<li>
Last but not the least, deliver and exceed on all performance targets.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What You Need to Bring to the Table</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years of experience in a related technical function is required.</li>
<li>
Direct customer advocacy and engagement experience in post-sales or professional services functions in Fortune 1000, mid-tier, and/or start-up companies.</li>
<li>
Experience working with large customers in a consulting or technical evangelist capacity, especially in the software or developmental tools industry</li>
<li>
Successfully managed customer engagements to completion and customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical skills &amp; problem solving skills, combined with the ability to provide quick resolution to problems.</li>
<li>
Interest in working creatively with customers understand technical and business requirements and implement solutions, including change management and technical engagement</li>
<li>
Exceptional verbal and written organizational, presentation, and communication skills.</li>
<li>
Must be customer service oriented and believe in teamwork, collaboration, adaptability &amp; initiative.</li>
<li>
Demonstrable success in thinking strategically, executing tactically while providing consistent and high levels of customer satisfaction and retention in a fast paced environment.</li>
<li>
Competitive &amp; competitively aware. Proficient at explaining strengths &amp; vulnerabilities.</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel periodically based on customer and business need</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience.</li>
</ul>
