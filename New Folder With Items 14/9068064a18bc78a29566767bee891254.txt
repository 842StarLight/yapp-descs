<h2>Point of Sale (POS) IT Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>We have an exciting opportunity for a Point of Sale (POS) Administrator to join our team providing daily support of POS operations across our retail stores in the Denver area. In this role, you will be the main point of contact working out at our Denver location off Federal Boulevard. You will implement configuration requests to ensure proper performance and utilization of up-to-date features to help drive store performance. The Administrator will work closely with store personnel and retail management regularly to verify that systems are functioning as intended. Travel will be approximately 20% of the time.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We offer a competitive benefits package that includes health, dental, vacation pay, 403b Retirement options and more!</div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential functions include:</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain and configure all store POS workstations, peripherals and printers</li>
<li>
Participate in project activities by providing technical expertise in system administration, configuration, design and deployment of related POS technologies</li>
<li>
Execute and coordinate with retail operations for sales, markdowns, and promotions in POS system</li>
<li>
Provide technical problem resolution to POS technology related issues within the retail environments</li>
<li>
Plan capacity needs for technical environment and meets with stakeholders to determine ongoing configuration requirements</li>
<li>
Assess the need for system reconfigurations based on request trends; make recommendations to IT and retail teams</li>
<li>
Work with varoius departments including vendors, IT teams, and retail employees on technical issues and problems resolution
</li>
<li>Rapidly respond to incoming incident tickets and resolve crucial and high incidents raised; escalate as appropirate</li>
<li>
Perform system maintenance
</li>
<li>Draft instructions for frequently asked questions</li>
<li>
Work with IT and retail locations to maintain PCI compliance</li>
</ul>
