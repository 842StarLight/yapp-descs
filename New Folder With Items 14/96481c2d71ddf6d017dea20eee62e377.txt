<div><b>Job Description</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Accenture Emerging Technology practice is seeking an experienced Sales Performance Management (SPM) Analyst/Consultant/Manager implementing SPM related products with enterprise clients in our SF and Emerging Business. The ideal candidate will work closely with our clients, the vendor team, and be part of our delivery teams in developing robust, scalable and flexible solutions for various customers<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities as an Anaplan Model Builder</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Write business requirements, help with functional design, prototyping, process design (including scenario design, flow mapping) and testing within the Anaplan platform
</li>
<li>Work with multiple functional teams to understand existing excel models and translate them into efficiently built Anaplan models
</li>
<li>Ability to configure and maintain Anaplan lists, modules, dashboards, actions and other model settings
</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain data models with complex relationships
</li>
<li>Ability to modify existing data models in order to incorporate new functionality</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Responsibilities as part of the SPM Practice</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Help define solutions on SPM tools, including Anaplan, Xactly, Salesforce and other sales performance tools in the Salesforce/SFDC ecosystem for our clients and across industries
</li>
<li>Supporting the successful delivery of Sales Performance Management client solutions and delivery.
</li>
<li>Developing Sales Performance strategy, solutions and implementation plans for clients.
</li>
<li>Disseminating best practices on solution positioning and delivery.
</li>
<li>Document client specific SPM requirements, processes and design for post-production reference and knowledge transfer
</li>
<li>Strong understanding of SPM component design concepts and principles to offer practical alternatives and design appropriate solutions
</li>
<li>Proactively manage and deliver assignments and tasks in a timely and accurate fashion
</li>
<li>Grow strong relationships with clients and other team members to accurately understand business needs and develop viable solutions
</li>
<li>Participate in the training and mentoring of new colleagues
</li>
<li>Driving business development including: supporting pre-sales activities, participating in sales calls and developing proposals with your teams and vendor counter-parts</li>
</ul>
</div>
