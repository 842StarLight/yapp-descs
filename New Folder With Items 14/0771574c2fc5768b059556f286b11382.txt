<h2>Director of Food & Beverage-BOK</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Under the Assistant General Manager's supervision the Director of Food &amp; Beverage is responsible for direction and coordination of concessions, bars, and premium suite food and beverage operations (including warehouse for those areas) at the BOK Center, including Branded Concepts and Retail Merchandising, Sponsorships, and ensuring company standards are maintained. This individual will help to facilitate optimum efficiency, maximize profits, and provide exceptional customer service by performing the following duties individually or through subordinate supervisors. This position oversees all arena bar, suites, and concessions food and beverage operations including banquets, concerts, various shows, and special functions. Oversight of all serving of any alcoholic beverage in all areas.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Negotiate contracts and agreements for necessary activities and services at the facility.</li>
<li>Direct and administer the execution of operating and marketing financial plans and documents; to include, operating revenue and expense budgets; capital expense plans and budgets.</li>
<li>Evaluate facility practices and recommend improvements to better reflect the needs of the client and the facility and/or to improve the efficiency and safety of operations, in compliance with organization.</li>
<li>Oversee the development of work plans and assigns appropriate duties to subordinates; makes hiring decisions.</li>
<li>Oversee Food &amp; Beverage operations as noted in position summary during events to ensure client satisfaction and quality control standards.</li>
<li>Analyze information concerning facility operations such as daily food sales, patron attendance, and labor costs to prepare budget and to maintain cost control of facility operations.</li>
<li>Inspect food service facilities to ensure equipment and building meet state, local health laws and internal regulations</li>
<li>Maintain cost and quality control standards and reviews inventory controls.</li>
<li>Inspect and sample prepared foods to maintain quality standards and sanitation regulations.</li>
<li>Assist Special Events department in developing creative solutions for maximizing profits and enhancing logistics of indoor and outdoor SMG Tulsa Special Events.</li>
<li>Evaluate existing venue food &amp; beverage and alcohol-related sponsorship programs at venues and work with Director of Business Development to enhance the visibility of those sponsors; find new potential sponsorship and revenue-generating opportunities and partnerships within the BOK Center.</li>
<li>Coordinate with Director of Marketing to generate more community awareness of department success stories and develop new public relations opportunities to promote food &amp; beverage capabilities.</li>
<li>Manage relationships with building tenants and respond to challenges, requests, and issues from general managers and team ownership.</li>
<li>Provide the highest customer service excellence.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with all Savor F&amp;B policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Required to speak and understand basic English well enough to converse with customers, supervisors, and employees.</li>
<li>Maintain a professional work ethic.</li>
<li>Enforce all sanitation procedures including safety guidelines and OSHA requirements.</li>
<li>Follows all rules and regulations which apply to the food and beverage operations and to SMG Food and Beverage Division.</li>
<li>Maintain a cooperative, team-like attitude in working with supervisors and fellow employees (including other departments).</li>
<li>This position requires the ability to work flexible, sometimes long hours which include nights, weekends, and holidays.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
