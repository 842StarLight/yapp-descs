<h2>Bingo Attendant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Sell all bingo games, related products, and suggest additional items to each customer not only in the bingo cage, but also on the floor.</li>
<li>
Attendants will be required to cross train in all essential areas of bingo, including but not limited to; cashier sales, floor sales, prep, paymaster, and caller duties.</li>
<li>
Maintains accurate sales and cash transactions.</li>
<li>
Verify’s bingo’s, accurately pays winnings, record bingo winners and amounts paid.</li>
<li>
Responsible for securing inventory and maintaining accurate inventory records. Prepares bingo supply orders on a bi-monthly basis.</li>
<li>
Must learn electronic Bingo machines in order to correct errors when needed.</li>
<li>
Call games as required.</li>
<li>
Provides quality customer services and maintains customer rapport by answering questions and explaining the rules and how to play Bingo.</li>
<li>
Maintains a clean and organized work area. Keeps the customer area well stocked with flyers, keeps the tables clean, and empties ashtrays before, during, and after each session.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Spirit</b><b> Mountain</b><b> Standards:</b></p>
<ul>
