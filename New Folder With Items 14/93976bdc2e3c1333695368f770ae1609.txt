<div><b>
The Role:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Collective Medical is looking for a Regional Vice President of Hospital Sales to focus on growing our Hospital Network. This role is critical to the future success of Collective Medical and is a great opportunity to help drive our mission forward as we continue to build a world class company. The successful candidate will be a member of the Sales Leadership Team and will help develop, coach, manage and oversee our mid-market hospital sales team. This is a remote position with our ideal candidate located in the eastern United States region.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Who you’ll work with:</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
This position will report to the Sr. Vice President of Sales and will supervise our hospital sales team. They will be deeply involved with our strategy, product and marketing team.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
What you’ll be doing day to day:</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop sales strategies, in cooperation with the SVP of Sales, specific to sales of Collective to U.S. acute care hospitals</li>
<li>Lead and motivate a team of direct sellers to achieve individual and team revenue goals and professional growth</li>
<li>Work closely with key stakeholders across the business to identify and implement sales strategies and products</li>
<li>Responsible for building and developing mid-market Hospital sales and achieving target bookings and revenue plan</li>
<li>Coaching and developing a team of high performance field sales reps</li>
<li>Ensure adequate training, competency, and proficiency of field sales reps</li>
<li>Develop, administer, and manage compliance with hospital sales processes and methods including CRM and other sales tools compliance across your team</li>
<li>Develop and manage forecasts and forecast process for the Hospital field sales segment</li>
<li>Develop and manage key performance metrics indicators for the Hospital field sales team and reps</li>
<li>Work directly with SVP of Sales and executive leadership on key accounts</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Skills and experience we’re looking for:</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>7+ years experience in a healthcare IT sales role to hospitals, preferably a SaaS company</li>
<li>3+ years in a Sales Team management capacity with a routine track record of meeting team sales objectives</li>
<li>Knowledge of the US Hospital landscape, specifically independent community hospitals</li>
<li>Excellent coaching and sales performance management skills</li>
<li>Proficient with CRM tools such as Salesforce and solution sales methodologies</li>
<li>Established relationships within the healthcare industry</li>
<li>Ability to identify and build relationships with a variety of stakeholders, CMO's, CFO's, Clinical Leadership, CIO’s, CTO’s, etc.</li>
<li>Broad healthcare knowledge with a passion for making the system better</li>
</ul>
