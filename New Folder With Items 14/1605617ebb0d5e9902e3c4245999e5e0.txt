<b>
Description</b><div>
</div>
<div>
The Grey Midwest Art Director is a seasoned visionary creative force who leads design teams in shaping customer experiences in all current and emerging interactive media. The Art Director implements creative deliverables on projects from brief to final build and manages/directs junior-level team members.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Creates and leads original designs from concepts; able direct those efforts in others through completion</li>
<li>
Creates and communicates concepts that intrigue and inspire both peers and clients with smart rationale to back them up</li>
<li>
Collaborates with Copy Director to ensure that both the copy and visuals are in perfect sync to make the concept as powerful, vivid, and compelling as possible</li>
<li>
Effectively manages a team through project completion
</li>
<li>Participates in client presentations/reviews, supports pitches and sales efforts</li>
<li>
Actively trains, mentors, and provides guidance to junior-level designers</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
6 + years of industry experience in a well-established, creative role with a proven track record
</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Advertising or equivalent</li>
<li>
Agency experience working across all channels</li>
<li>
Strong creative portfolio highlighting relevant/category experience</li>
<li>
Solid verbal communication skills, with experience presenting to all levels of clients</li>
<li>
Experience working with large teams in client-facing role</li>
<li>
Excellent written and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Able to gracefully handle multiple projects in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign</li>
</ul>
