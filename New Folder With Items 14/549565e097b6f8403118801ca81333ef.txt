<div><b>POSITION OVERVIEW</b></div>
<div><b>Applications Engineer, Semiconductor
</b></div>
<div>Within the Support organization, Applications Engineers are responsible for Industries and Applications targeted for investment by the Sales, Business Units and R&amp;D organizations. Successful AEs enjoy working with customers on a variety of challenging technical assignments that ensure project success and enable growth within targeted areas of our business. AEs form trusted technical partnerships with our customers and create mutual value by guiding system design, development and deployment decisions. As internal NI system users, AEs are in a unique position to recommend application improvements that strengthen the value NI delivers to our customers.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>As a Staff Semiconductor Applications Engineer, you will work with senior AEs to enable Semiconductor Automated Product Test (APT) customer success. Within the first 6 months, you will build application knowledge and software engineering skills as you work with a cross-functional team on a customer project. Within a year, you will acquire NI product certifications and develop industry acumen that will enable you to continue building your career.</div>
