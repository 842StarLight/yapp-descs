<h2>Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b><b>: </b></p>
<p>Responsible for providing administrative support to the Mortgage Sales Manager and other office personnel as instructed.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS (RESPONSIBILITIES): </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answer phones and route callers to appropriate office personnel and greet incoming visitors and direct them to the appropriate waiting area.</li>
<li>File, copy and scan documents and organize and maintain filing systems for various reports and documents.</li>
<li>Assist mortgage center personnel with gathering documents and/ordering loan documents.</li>
<li>Orders office supplies and receives and distributes incoming deliveries and mail.</li>
<li>Perform related responsibilities as required or assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<p>Knowledge/Skills/Abilities</p>
<p>Equivalent Education/Experience</p>
<p>· Ability to interact effectively with mortgage center, regional and corporate personnel, as well as with clients and vendors</p>
<p>· Proficient in PC software, including PowerPoint, Word, Excel, Adobe, and Outlook; demonstrated ability to learn additional programs as required</p>
<p>· Excellent verbal and written communication skills</p>
<p>· High School Diploma</p>
<p>· At least 1 year of related office experience</p>
<p><b>SPECIAL JOB DIMENSIONS: </b></p>
<p>Work requires sitting for prolonged periods during 8 hour work day.</p>

Major changes in areas of responsibility may occur, as directed by management, and will require revision of this job description.</p>
