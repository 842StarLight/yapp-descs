<h2>Senior Global GAAP/STAT Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role Summary:</b></h2>
The Senior Global Financial Analyst is a dynamic role that plays a critical part on the controllership team as the strategic and technical lead for the multiple international legal entities of a US GAAP reporting legal entity.<p></p>
<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Responsibilities:</b></h2>
As the Senior Global Financial Analyst you will:<br>
<ul>
<li>Oversees STAT reporting and US GAAP reporting for 12 international legal entities</li>
<li>
Technical expert for all STAT reporting for the business</li>
<li>
Strategic controllership project lead for international locations and provides support to the global controllership team</li>
<li>
Project leader for work transitioning from 3rd party into local business controllership
</li>
<li>Technical expert for all statutory accounting for multiple international countries
</li>
<li>Ensure timely reporting of STAT financials with reporting of STAT to GAAP and STAT to Tax and value added tax filings</li>
<li>
Ensure appropriate financial close procedures are in place including account reconciliations for international legal entities</li>
<li>
Overall ownership of international legal entities balance sheets and income statements
</li>
<li>Ownership and accountability, Cash management, Accounts receivable, Accounts payable, Payroll, Pension, IBS, Variance analysis, and Internal controls for international locations</li>
<li>
Ownership of any statutory reporting including Tax and liaise with the auditors
</li>
<li>Ownership of global legal entities balance sheet and income statements</li>
<li>
Provides best practice and insights to the team over all international processes and accounting
</li>
<li>Ownership and continuous review of financial controls and reporting systems within the international locations looking for improvements in process
</li>
<li>Develop financial close functions that will help streamline, standardize and strengthen efficiencies
</li>
<li>Interact with Global Tax Team with Sales Tax and Value Added Tax compliance</li>
<li>
Review and complete Account Reconciliations as a member of the controllership team</li>
<li>
Quarterly record to report and financial close functions
</li>
<li>Completion of controllership audit request for KPMG and Corporate Audit Staff</li>
<li>
Overall ownership of issues identified with KPMG and Corporate Audit Staff to resolve any financial reporting issues identified during the audit for international entities</li>
<li>
Assist in executing and testing business requirements related to Corporate Enterprise Standards compliance</li>
<li>
Work with Digital team to test enhancements made to financial processes</li>
<li>
Provides best practice and insights/leadership over all international processes and accounting
</li>
<li>Develop and provide training, as necessary</li>
<li>
Contribute to department and organization special projects as assigned</li>
<li>
Document policies, procedures and workflow for assigned areas of responsibility</li>
<li>
Drive process and issue resolution and push for simplification</li>
<li>
Enforce Controllership in accordance with GE and GEH Policies and Procedures</li>
<li>
Ensure all systems and processes adhere to GE General Accounting Procedures (GAP) and Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) and recommend and implement enhancements as changes warrant</li>
</ul>
