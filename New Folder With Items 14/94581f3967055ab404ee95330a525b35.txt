<b>Staff Accountant, Job Listing | Guns Dot Com, LLC | September 2019</b>
<br>
<br>
Since 2011, Guns.com has grown into the most trusted source of information about guns online. We have published thousands of articles and reviews, keeping gun owners in the know about the issues they care about. Now, in addition to being your most trusted source for news, we have evolved into an online marketplace that celebrates gun ownership.
<br>
<br>
Over the years, the Guns.com team talked to thousands of gun enthusiasts, store owners, manufacturers, and organizations. It became apparent that the process of buying a gun online was too cumbersome, slow, and confusing. We set about to change that. And so, we built a better way to buy guns online - bringing ease and savings to customers, while protecting and growing local gun store owner revenue.
<br>
<br>
Our mission is to Keep It Local, Make It Easy, Serve Our Customers, and Take Pride in Ownership. For more information, please visit: https://www.guns.com/
<br>
<br>
Guns.com is searching for a qualified, professional <b>Staff</b> <b>Accountant</b> for a position in our Burnsville, MN Headquarters.
<br>
<br>
This role provides a strong candidate the opportunity for training and acquiring the necessary hands-on experience from working with a team of experienced professionals in a dynamic, fast-paced e-commerce retailing environment.
<br>
<br>
A successful candidate will maintain a positive attitude, be a good team player, and be prepared to provide whatever assistance that may be required from all levels of management in the organization.
