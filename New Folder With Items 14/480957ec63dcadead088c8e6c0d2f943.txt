<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Strong people skills are the key to success, as management responsibilities include, hiring, retention, performance management, and staff development. Store Manager responsibilities also include resource planning and scheduling, budget/P&amp;L management, training and facility management relative to meeting defined sales and service objectives.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
With quality installations and superior customer service as one of the company’s core values, the Store Manager will be accountable for ensuring the proper use of materials and safety equipment, and ongoing safety education and training.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Additionally, the Store Manager will work closely with the regional operations and sales team to develop initiatives to further expand business opportunities. Will also be expected to do installations when necessary.</div>
</div></div></div>
