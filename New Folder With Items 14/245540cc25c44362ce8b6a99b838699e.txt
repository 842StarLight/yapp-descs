<h2>Router</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Southern Glazer's Wine &amp; Spirits is hiring a Router!!!</p>
<ul><li>Route/dispatch an average of 55+ truck routes on a daily basis.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate driver's routes according to customer requests, geographic considerations and in compliance with DOT regulations and company policies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Notify drivers and warehouse supervision/management of schedules/assignments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Operate an automated routing computer system (i.e. RoadNet) and track schedules, changes in assignments/routes, and maintain follow-up information.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track and input driver mileage and hours within the recordkeeping system.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate with various locations/depots on a daily basis regarding shipping status and projected/confirmed deliveries.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partner with the sales force and customer assistance to resolve shipping/delivery discrepancies or concerns regarding product arrival status.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Make on-demand decisions to resolve issues efficiently with the least disruption to the dispatched route and customer.</li></ul>
<ul><li>The scheduled shift is Monday - Thursday from 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM.</li></ul>
<ul><li>The position may require flexible hours which include early morning (i.e., 4:00 a.m. - 6:00 a.m. start times), evenings, and/or weekends.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Additional hours required during the peak season of October, November and December.</li></ul>
<p>Minimum Qualifications:</p>
<ul><li>High School Diploma/GED</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum of 2 years of routing/dispatch experience in a fast paced, distribution/trucking industry.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be proficient in working with computer hardware and software, specifically Microsoft Office products and automated routing software.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Familiar with local metropolitan area and extensive understanding of local market's geography.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Logistics understanding and skill.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Capable of managing stressful and high pressure situations as there is an extreme sense of urgency to complete dispatch procedures in a timely and accurate manner.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adaptable and able to assist a variety of internal customer groups and varying personality types.</li></ul>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul><li>Associate's Degree in Business or related field.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bilingual in English and Spanish is desired.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Familiarity with beverage and or consumer packaged goods distributor/manufacturing environment.</li></ul>
