<h2>A/P Accounting Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Citizen Watch America</b> represents the sales and marketing for the Citizen Group within the U.S., Caribbean and Latin American markets. The brands included within Citizen Watch America are Citizen, Bulova, Frederique Constant, Alpina, and Ateliers deMonaco. Each individual brand builds upon their very distinctive identities in the market with their unique, differentiated advertising, marketing and brand campaigns. As a combined company, however, the brands can assist each other and leverage the power that a larger unified entity brings.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our vision is to lead the affordable luxury watch market by offering the most compelling collection of brands and products that are loved, favored, and respected by citizens of the world.</p>
<p></p>
<p>We will continue to leverage our heritage and expertise to enrich the lives of our current and future customers and Employees through unparalleled excellence in creativity, design, craftsmanship, innovation, technology, value, and service.<br>
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Report to and collaborate with the Accounts Payable Lead
</li>
<li>Process daily accounts payables, including ensuring all purchase invoices are coded accurately and appropriately
</li>
<li>Timely production of checks/ACH batches on a weekly basis
</li>
<li>Process expense reports, including:
</li>
<ul>
<li>extract Reporting from Concur Expense Reporting System to load and interface to NAV Accounts Payable system
</li>
<li>review for accuracy and reasonableness and adherence to expense policy
</li>
<li>process reimbursement checks/ACH to employee
</li>
</ul>
<li>Process American Express Statements, including:
</li>
<ul>
<li>obtain AMEX file load from departments to interface load into NAV Accounts Payable system
</li>
<li>review for accuracy and reasonableness and adherence to expense policy
</li>
<li>process payment to AMEX
</li>
<li>manage and maintain accurate Vendor data to accommodate AMEX vendor history
</li>
</ul>
<li>Respond to and resolve employee/vendor queries and issues
</li>
<li>Assist and collaborate on ad hoc accounting projects
</li>
<li>Maintain accurate vendor data and W-9’s “soft” copies in NAV Vendor Card Subsystem
</li>
<li>Assist with compilation of 1099 MISC IRS tax forms
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years high volume experience in an accounting department performing accounts payable functions
</li>
<li>Thoughtful, attention-to-detail, multi-tasker who is extremely hands on and flexible
</li>
<li>Microsoft Dynamics Navision (NAV) experience a definite PLUS
</li>
</ul>
