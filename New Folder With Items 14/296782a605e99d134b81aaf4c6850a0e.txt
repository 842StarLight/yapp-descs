<p>About You:
</p>
<ul>
<li>You have significant experience selling SaaS/Cloud based ERP/HCM/Financial planning solutions to the C level in Large Enterprise prospect accounts with successful revenue attainment
</li>
<li>You are passionate about consultative selling and have a drive to win new business/initiate long-standing relationships with one of the most transformational, disruptive cloud companies on the planet!
</li>
<li>You will initiate and manage complex, strategic, sales cycles by orchestrating internal teams of pre-sales, value management, bid management, inside sales, marketing and sales support
</li>
<li>You understand the fundamental differences and benefits of an on-demand/Cloud/SaaS architecture as an alternative to legacy ERP solutions
</li>
<li>You have experience cultivating mutually beneficial relationships with strategic partners and alliances
</li>
<li>You understand the competitive landscape and customer needs so that you can effectively position Workday solutions within Workday's prospects
</li>
<li>You maintain timely and accurate pipeline, customer, and forecast data
</li>
<li>You are an excellent communicator, boasting excellent verbal and written communication skills
</li>
</ul>
