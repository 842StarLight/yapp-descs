<p>
About DSTI</p>
<p>
We are a locally-owned global manufacturing company that designs and produces high-performance custom fluid sealing solutions for a <b>variety of industries</b> including medical, aerospace, oil and gas, food packaging, and alternative energy. We have minimal competition; it is typically a decision to make or buy for our customers. We take pride in our <b>clean and modern state-of-the-art manufacturing facility</b>. This position offers the <b>opportunity to travel</b> (domestically and abroad) 10-25% of the time. However, the majority of the time will be spent in our Andover, MN location. Due to our highly collaborative sales approach, we do not employ remote sale representatives so please only apply if you are willing to work in our Andover office.</p>
<p>
In order to attract and retain the best in the industry who share our values to trust, communicate, collaborate, and evolve, we work to create a desirable company culture. We offer a <b>casual work environment</b>, a <b>great deal of flexibility</b>, a <b>concern for safety and wellness</b>, <b>special events and perks including sporting event tickets to enjoy with the family</b>, <b>fun team events during work hours</b>, <b>family events and parties</b>, and <b>opportunities to give back</b>. And, Penny the Australian Shepherd, our <b>canine chief morale officer</b>, works on Fridays to ensure that all is well!</p>
<p>
A Day in the Life as a Sales Engineer
</p>
<p>As a Sales Engineer, you use your <b>exceptional communication skills</b> to understand customer requirements and propose solutions. Ultimately, you are responsible for ensuring sales volume <b>meets or</b> <b>exceeds targets</b>. You do this through <b>leveraging incoming leads</b> from our digital marketing team and also by <b>developing relationships</b> to deepen <b>partnerships </b>in existing accounts.</p>
<p>
You divide your time between managing incoming leads and <b>proactively identifying new strategic opportunities</b>. You work with internal teams to understand projects status and appropriately <b>communicate key details</b> to the customer. You establish and maintain contacts in the industry to gather necessary intelligence. You actively participate in the <b>sales forecasting and planning process</b> and collaborate with internal teams to meet targets. You consistently use our exceptional <b>custom technology tools</b> to track your leads and open opportunities. You partner with Engineering and Project Management teams frequently for quoting and status updates.
</p>
<p>Due to the global nature of our business, the majority of your time is spent engaging with customers via phone or email. There is an <b>opportunity to travel</b> a few times a year (not to exceed 25%) both domestically and internationally. Your customers also enjoy visiting DSTI so you have the opportunity to showcase our <b>state-of-the-art facilities</b>.</p>

Are you a quick learner with a high aptitude for software and technology? Are you both team-oriented and able to work independently? Do you enjoy a collaborative environment? If so, then you might just be perfect for this position!</p>
