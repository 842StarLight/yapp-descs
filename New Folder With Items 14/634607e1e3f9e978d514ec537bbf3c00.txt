 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Zapproved is hiring an Associate Customer Success Manager (CSM) to join our fast-growing software company headquartered in Portland, Oregon. This position works to create and build customer relationships from the signing of the first contract through the entire lifecycle of the account for our Small to Midsize Customer base. We are a fast growing team and need team members who are able to complete their defined roles as well as seek out other opportunities to contribute.</div>

<div><b>
What You’ll Be Doing:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Prioritize and manage your account base to proactively identify opportunities for growth and work closely with Account Executives and the Customer Success Operations Manager to drive expansion (both cross-sell and upsell) of this account base.</li>
<li>
Serve as the primary point of contact for customers after implementation for Zapproved’s SMB Accounts (Small to Midsize Businesses), an account base of 200+ customers</li>
<li>
Engage with customers reactively to drive product adoption and ensure they are leveraging the solution to achieve full business value</li>
<li>
Keep up to date with the most recent changes in the ZSuite and be able to demo these capabilities to prepare and educate customers and internal stakeholders on new features and releases</li>
<li>
Leverage Gainsight heavily to respond to adoption and utilization trends and partner with Customer Success Operations Manager to execute campaigns that generate demand for new sales opportunities, promote general adoption best practices, and offer periodic customer business reviews as needed</li>
<li>
Identify renewal risks and collaborate with internal teams to remediate and ensure a successful renewal</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What You’ll Need:</b></div>
<div>
To be a team player who is technically proficient and has the patience and passion to guide customers and stakeholders through all the capabilities of our products and solutions. It’s essential that you can embrace the importance of teamwork, collaborate with a variety of people in a diverse environment and be adaptable to changing work requirements.</div>
