<h2>Data Products Activation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About Us
<br><br>
MediaMath's mission is to help innovative marketers delight their customers and drive real business outcomes. Over 9,500 marketers in 42 countries use our enterprise solution to reach the right user with the right message for the right price, wherever they are. These are the leading brands and agencies in the world who rely on MediaMath to provide technology, services and leadership to help them continue to transform their marketing practices and compete in a quickly changing industry. Founded in 2007 as a pioneer in "programmatic" advertising, MediaMath has been recognized as a leader in the space in four independent evaluations over the last two years. MediaMath has offices in 16 cities worldwide and is headquartered in New York City.
<br><br>
Key Responsibilities
<br><br>
The Manager, Audience Activation is responsible for driving revenue growth through the successful activation of the MediaMath Audience product set. This person will possess a deep knowledge of the audiences and data activation, and gain market insights to help continuously improve MediaMath Audiences. As the subject matter expert, it will be this person's responsibility to work closely with the sales and engagement teams to identify new opportunities where data can improve performance to help grow overall budgets.
<br><br>
<b>You will:</b><br>
Sales &amp; Client Engagement
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Ensure that MediaMath Audiences are properly activated and adopted by clients – work closely with the platform solutions team to help maximize results and expand campaign budgets</li>
<li>Work with MediaMath account team to ensure ongoing client satisfaction and usage of MediaMath Audiences</li>
<li>Support in educating and guiding clients on audience best practices</li>
<li>Work closely with Clients, Commercial teams, and Product team identify new ways to promote, package, and scale the usage of audiences to clients</li>
<li>Provide user feedback and insights to Product team related to new features</li>
</ul>
<b>Performance Mindset:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Understand the benefits of programmatic and how data can enhance targeting, scale and performance</li>
<li>Work closely with delivery teams to troubleshoot and optimize campaigns to ensure maximized usage and performance</li>
<li>Develop comprehensive strategies to enable product users to optimize results</li>
</ul>
<b>Product Expertise:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Gain deep knowledge of MediaMath Audiences, team structures, end users and activation processes</li>
<li>Gather market feedback and propose appropriate product improvements to Product Management team</li>
<li>Advocate for optimal product roadmap, based on client interaction</li>
</ul>
<b>You are:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Results Driven: Proven ability to exceed goals in budget and on-time in a fast-paced environment; maintaining a keen attention to detail despite multiple and often conflicting priorities</li>
<li>Customer Focus: Track record of building strong relationships internally and externally with ability to set and manage expectations</li>
<li>Strong Communicator: Excellent written and oral communication skills, including presentation skills</li>
<li>Analytically Oriented: Ability to conduct qualitative and quantitative analysis; providing subsequent insights and recommendations for strategic and/or tactical next steps</li>
<li>New Product Activation: Demonstrated aptitude with solutions selling for untested and new products – ability to iterate quickly without a rule-book but within the bounds of our capability</li>
</ul>
<b>You have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 4 years' experience in the industry</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree required; MBA or advanced degree preferred</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years' experience working in data activation preferred</li>
<li>Qualified candidates will have strong knowledge of multi-channel marketing and digital advertising</li>
</ul>
