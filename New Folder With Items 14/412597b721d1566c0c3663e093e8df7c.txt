<h2>Systems Design Engineer - Piping</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>The North American HQ of a major international manufacturing company has an opportunity for a progressive and creative Engineer to join their prestigious Product Development team. This Engineering driven firm can offer you a strong salary, cutting edge technical challenges and multiple avenues for professional growth and advancement.
</p>
<p><b>You will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review customer system design requirements and develop product solutions for piping motion control needs, i.e. thermal movement accommodation, seismic movement, and vibration attenuation.
</li>
<li>Assist customers with product selection and engineering calculations for piping motion control solutions.
</li>
<li>Identify and assist in the development of new piping motion control products.
</li>
<li>Identify and obtain testing, certifications, approvals to improve product acceptance or competitive advantage in the market- place.
</li>
<li>Participate in major industry organization committees and codes and standards committees for seismic and/or vibration attenuation.
</li>
<li>Investigate field installations as to product usage and performance. Keep Sales Management, Product Directors and Engineers up to date on trends or current issues.
</li>
<li>Provide general support to sales organization including creating, organizing and conducting technical seminars.
</li>
<li>Perform technical literature creation and review related to piping motion control, seismic applications and/or vibration attenuation.
</li>
<li>Perform additional related projects as assigned by the Manager of Engineering Services.
</li>
<li>Prepare code-compliant piping system designs and design drawings using customer-compatible software such as Autodesk, RevIT or AutoCAD
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS in Mechanical Engineering and PE (Professional Engineer) preferred. EIT will be considered
</li>
<li>5+ years experience in the development of mechanical products including piping, piping systems or other similar industrial components
</li>
<li>EPC background preferred (design, build)
</li>
<li>Experience ANSYS or other similar stress analysis and knowledge of piping motion control
</li>
<li>Willingness to travel to customer sites on a regular basis
</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Keywords </i></b>: Stress Analysis, Ansys, Caesar, AutoPipe, Caepipe, PE, EIT Piping,
</p>
<p>Full Relocation available for qualified applicant</p>
