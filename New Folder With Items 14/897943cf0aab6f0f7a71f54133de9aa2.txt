<h2>Engineer, Data Science</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>As a member of the AdSmart Product Engineering Team, the Engineer, Data Science, AdSmart will be directly responsible for data science engineering as part of building out the necessary platform and products for NBCUniversal’s AdSmart product suite. NBCUniversal’s AdSmart products will enable NBCUniversal to better understand it's brand’s audiences such as NBC News, Bravo, The Tonight Show, Saturday Night Live, and USA Network as well as audiences that cross brands. The goal is to ensure we know who is watching what, where and when. In turn, enabling NBCUniversal’s sales teams to properly align our audiences with the market advertisements that can benefit them the most.<br>
<br>
You’re a big thinker who can analyze and evangelize a long-range opportunity, architect a groundbreaking solution and roll-up your sleeves to get code out the door when needed. You are data-driven and analytical. You understand the concept of a value proposition and evaluation criteria, and you know how to align them with low-level milestones to get the work done. You can apply domain knowledge from one technical subject, in order to quickly ramp and deliver on a new one. You know how to learn from failure until you succeed, and you are able to articulate and quantify the reasons for your decisions.<br>
<br>
You will be part of the AdSmart Engineering team, participating in the data science that will drive both current and future data science initiatives within NBCUniversal’s AdSmart group.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><ul>
<li>
Serve as an engineer data science for AdSmart products.</li>
<li>
Participate in, and execute, a 12-36 month product roadmap with input from the delivery team, stakeholders, and leadership team</li>
<li>
Develop and code the data science services that are core to Audience Studio, under the leadership and direction of the VP/Chief Architecture</li>
<li>
Analyze and report results and adjust the overall engineering strategy accordingly with engineering leadership</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Qualifications
</b><ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field</li>
<li>
At least 2+ years of software development experience</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of data structures, algorithms</li>
<li>
Ability to apply data science theory to effectively to advertising &amp; audience data</li>
<li>
Fluency in at least 1 of the following programming languages (R, Python, or Scala)</li>
<li>
Understanding of basic statistical concepts</li>
<li>
Experience in forecasting, numerical optimization, natural language processing, or machine learning techniques.</li>
<li>
1+ years of experience with both relational database design (SQL), non-relational (NoSQL), big data, real-time technologies]</li>
<li>
Experience in web application development and associated skills (REST, HTTP, web services)</li>
<li>
Able to easily transition between high-level strategy and day-to-day implementation</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Desired Qualification:
</b><ul>
<li>Self-directed, ability to multi-task, sharp analytical abilities, excellent communication skills, capable of working effectively in a dynamic environment</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Excellent teamwork and collaboration skills</li>
<li>
Results-oriented, high energy, self-motivated</li>
</ul>
