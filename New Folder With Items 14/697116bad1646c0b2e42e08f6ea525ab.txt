<h2>Senior Business Technology Operations Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>The Senior Business Technology Operations Analyst role serves as a subject matter expert for Bazaarvoice business processes and the accompanying systems. This dynamic position blends strategic project and operational activities that drive company growth as well as operational excellence. This analyst will be responsible for requirements development and documentation, data management( including queue maintenance) process mapping, solution development, and</div>
<div>
implementing/monitoring improvements, Specific workflow processes include but are not limited to data governance, SKU creation, eCommerce, services, and marketing support. Areas of focus will vary according to specific needs of the business road-map.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Become an expert in cross functional process related to Bazaarvoice systems &amp; drive system and process changes resulting in improvements in system usage, internal efficiency, and data accuracy</li>
<li>
Works with business leaders and individual contributors to elicit and document functional requirements for new business processes and supporting information technology systems and measure</li>
<li>
Investigate best practices and make recommendations for continuous improvement – prioritized recommendations may result in project work
</li>
<li>Documents as-is and to-be business processes and communicates business implications and solutions to issues/challenges in a manner that can be leveraged by business leaders to understand the change management efforts required to implement changes</li>
<li>
Complete required tactical and operational activities as needed – monthly hands on testing with IT releases
</li>
<li>Subject Matter Expert - become an expert in cross functional process related to Bazaarvoice business systems. Drive system and process changes resulting in improvements in system usage, internal efficiency, and data accuracy</li>
<li>
Follow and develop process standards for documentation and communication with the Business Analyst team
</li>
<li>Develop metrics to establish or measure improvement success</li>
<li>
Lead, communicate, and train users on system or process changes</li>
<li>
Definition and maintenance of data standards. Educating user community on data standards. Keeping abreast of business changes and adapting standards to meet new needs as they arise</li>
<li>
Data Governance support, system, and queue maintenance</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Skills and Experience:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
<b>
6 – 10 years </b>of business experience with a management consulting focus and working through complex project initiatives from marketing through fulfillment and services including IT system implementations and SKU deployments</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience working with an enterprise Salesforce solution– must be able to pull reports, create dashboards, complete imports, exports and data transformation as required; admin and data migration experience is a plus
</li>
<li>Proven success developing and documenting requirements and seeing these IT requirements through deployment and testing
</li>
<li>Advanced to expert skills in Microsoft Visio, Excel, and PowerPoint. Experience creating dashboards, reports, graphs, charts as well as developing work instructions</li>
<li>
Proven expertise in process definition and improvement methodologies; Lean/Six Sigma experience and certification preferred</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively with a diverse team at a high pace; Must have exceptional problem-solving skills and communication skills
</li>
<li>Service oriented team player with a “can do” attitude and approach to supporting our partners</li>
<li>
Salesforce.com, JIRA, OpenAir, CPQ, NetSuite and related app proficiency or other experience with systems related to our services organization a plus</li>
</ul></div>

<div>About Bazaarvoice</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Bazaarvoice connects brands and retailers to consumers, so that every shopping experience feels personal. From search and discovery to purchase and advocacy, Bazaarvoice’s solutions reach in-market shoppers, personalize their experiences, and give them the confidence to buy. Each month in the Bazaarvoice Network, more than a billion consumers view and share authentic content including reviews, questions and answers, and social photos across 6,000 brand and retail websites. Across the network, Bazaarvoice captures billions of shopper signals monthly - data that powers high-efficiency digital advertising and personalization with unmatched relevance.</div>
