<h2>Digital Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Digital Sales Coordinator is responsible for provide general support and account maintenance for the Digital Account Specialists/Senior Digital Account Specialists and Digital Account Executives as directed by Director Digital Revenue &amp; Planning. This position’s primary focus will be campaign stewardship, client reporting, act as a primary liaison with Digital Ad Operations / Tech teams and post campaign handle billing and assist in performance recaps for clients and agencies.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><div>1. Maintain campaigns within contract management systems.<br>
2. Interface with Digital Account Specialists/Senior Digital Account Specialists and Ad Operations team(s) regarding campaign scheduling, optimizations, and re-allocations.<br>
3. Schedule flight changes, makegoods and monitor campaign delivery across Discovery Digital websites, social platforms and TVE / VOD for assigned accounts.<br>
4. Gather campaign delivery data weekly and monthly leveraging internal and external reporting tools.<br>
5. Complete client facing reporting templates.<br>
6. Partner with and shadow Digital Account Specialists/Senior Account Specialists to gather campaign assets from clients, agencies and internal teams.<br>
7. Maintain files of campaign screen shots / proofs of campaign launches for all placements.<br>
8. Complete monthly billing and reporting requests as assigned.<br>
9. Submit post-sale research/benchmark requests for wrap reports.<br>
10. Shadow Digital Account Specialists on building Digital campaigns and aiding Account Executives in request for proposal responses with internal departments.<br>
11. Participate in special projects and assignments.
</div></div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a related field of study (advertising, communication, marketing, business, etc.</li>
<li>Previous internship experience in Media, Advertising Agency or Digital / Technology space highly preferred. Digital Media / advertising sales experience a plus but not required.</li>
<li>Strong problem solving / analytical skills and computer literacy (specifically Microsoft Excel and Power Point).</li>
<li>Written and verbal communication skills are required.</li>
<li>Detail-oriented; ability to complete large volume of work in a timely manner; ability to multi-task.</li>
<li>Ability to work both independently and cooperatively within a team environment.</li>
<li>Proven track record of exceptional performance, high productivity and meeting deadlines.</li>
<li>Must have the legal right to work in the United States.</li>
</ul>
