<h2>Technical Data Analyst (Marketing)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Overview</div>
<p>PROLIM (www.prolim.com) is currently seeking <b>Technical Data Analyst (Marketing) </b>for Location <b>Livonia, MI </b>for one of our top Client.</p>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p>This role is a blend of client service, strategic consulting, and analytics. It requires strong analytic skills, a passion for continuous improvement, and a keen interest in direct marketing. Senior Direct Marketing Data Analysts build strong consultative relationships with internal and external clients, demonstrate strong business/financial modeling capability with tools such as R, Python, Excel, and SQL, and are able to apply statistical analysis principles to business challenges.
</p>
<p><b>Top Skills
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Business, Math or Statistics and MBA or Master’s degree in Marketing, Statistics, related discipline required
</li>
<li>Expert user of Tableau, SQL, and Excel
</li>
<li>5 years of professional experience in an analytical role
</li>
<li>Proficient in analyzing and interpreting data from multiple sources via R or Python</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze direct marketing data to evaluate marketing effectiveness and sales performance across multiple dimensions (campaigns, applications, sales, etc.). Make campaign optimization recommendations to business partners and teams to improve performance.
</li>
<li>Proactively seek out new direct marketing opportunities. Analyze potential financial impact, determine stakeholders, develop execution plans, lead meetings, and launch campaigns.
</li>
<li>Develop automated, easy to understand reports and ad hoc analyses that help us understand direct marketing patterns, identify differences among our customer groups and recognize opportunities for improvement.
</li>
<li>Serve as a consultative partner on planning, strategy, measurement, and optimization opportunities for multiple clients, which include marketing channel owners and others throughout the organization. Champion data-driven decision making within the organization.
</li>
<li>Regularly present to partners, internal departments, and vendors to communicate performance.
</li>
<li>Assist business partners with identifying meaningful and measurable marketing objectives for interactive marketing campaigns.
</li>
<li>Conduct independent research and analysis and develop reporting based on the needs of the business.
</li>
<li>Contribute to marketing testing roadmap by proposing testing opportunities, designing test plans, and executing in accordance with the testing roadmap.
</li>
<li>Develop and execute processes to leverage statistical models to optimize marketing initiative outcomes. Work with Data Scientists to identify and prioritize new modeling opportunities.
</li>
<li>Contribute to sales forecast and metric creation and maintenance.
</li>
</ul>
