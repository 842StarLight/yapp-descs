<h2>SaaS Business Development/ Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Requires: 4 - 6 years of sales experience successfully selling SaaS tools or digital B2B solutions into brands</p>
<p>The company provides cutting-edge data and AI technology to solve multiple marketing challenges for its clients. The Business Development executive builds relationships with marketers, selling SaaS subscriptions of a new, revolutionary service that delivers marketing insights for their products and services. The position is largely self-directed and reports to SVP of Sales. What's in it for you:</p>
<p>Base plus uncapped commission. Benefits, Flexible vacation policy, Fun, dynamic colleagues, Social outings, Days of service, WeWork office with tons of perks. Be part of something special!</p>
<p>Candidates should be conversant in marketing research language and process, and have a proven track record of closing annual subscription deals and consistently exceeding sales quotas. The role requires use of established and self-sourced contacts in marketing as well as inbound leads to build a pipeline and drive prospects through the sales funnel to closure.</p>
<p>SaaS Business Development/Sales Manager Responsibilities:</p>
<p>· Aggressively source leads via web, social media, and existing contacts</p>
<p>· Open doors with new prospects, map the organizations and find the decision maker(s)</p>
<p>· Convert inbound leads into opportunities and sales</p>
<p>· Consultatively sell by questioning and understanding prospect use case(s) that the company addresses, linking the features of the company solution through to benefits and business payoff</p>
<p>· Confidently price, incent and close deals, exceed quota</p>
<p>· Build relationships and brand advocates at your accounts</p>
<p>· Document all sales efforts, progress and results in CRM</p>
<p>· Provide internal feedback about product opportunities and unmet needs</p>
<p>· Work with client success managers to ensure 100% customer satisfaction</p>
<p>SaaS Business Development/Sales Manager Qualifications:</p>
<p>· 4 - 6 years of sales experience successfully selling SaaS tools or digital B2B solutions into brands, Martech or Adtech sales to the marketing or research functions and Subscription sales experience</p>
<p>· Strong understanding of marketing strategy.</p>
<p>· Experience successfully utilizing a consultative sales method a plus (Formal training a plus, e.g., Challenger, Sandler, SPIN etc.)</p>
<p>· Experience successfully uncovering needs, exploring pain points, introducing multiple solutions with follow through to business results. Genuine, active listener. Enjoys building relationships.</p>
<p>· A proven quota track record of achievement and goal setting</p>
<p>· Experience closing business in a new budget, multi-decision maker and influencer sale</p>
<p>· Consistently and accurately uses sales technology tools like CRM, web share, social platforms, etc.</p>
<p>· Self-motivated; thrives in a fast-paced environment and loves a results-driven culture</p>
<p>· College degree or equivalent; continual learner.</p>
<p>Other titles: Sales Manager, Adtech Sales, Adtech Business Development, Martech Sales, Martech Business Development, SAAS Sales, SAAS Business Development, Individual Contributor Role, Account Executive</p>
<p>Account Executive SAAS or Martech or Adtech.</p>
