<b>The opportunity:</b><br>
As a Packaging Designer in our Corrugated Packaging plant in Dayton, NJ, you will act as a creative strategist leading the design of products and packaging from concept to completion. The Packaging Designer will gather project data from WestRock sales, project managers and customers, create designs in the Computer Assisted Design (CAD) program, solicit and incorporate customer feedback, and create prototypes for customer approval.
<br><br>
<b>How you will impact WestRock:</b><br>
Develop new, innovative and appealing designs for packaging and point of purchase displays
<br>Develop designs to enhance customer products, improve margins, and reduce packaging costs
<br>Challenging the status quo, and developing winning solutions for our customers
<br>Receive new design requests directly from customers, sales partners, customer service reps, or business unit resource manager and gather key specification data
<br>Translate requirements and specification data to conceptualize and create packaging that meets customer’s needs and expectations
<br>Produce product design documentation such as concept sketches, technical drawings, and illustrations that define and communicate design intent
<br>Leverage expertise and knowledge of corrugated facility and machinery, board combinations, retailers’ wants and needs, and customer’s capabilities to inform product design
<br>Collaborate with manufacturing stakeholders to review and discuss new product designs, answer design questions, ensure that products are manufacturable, and incorporate revisions to the design based on feedback from the manufacturing team
<br>Support customer discussions and presentations by supplying all design documentation, leading product design overviews, and answering all customer questions related to the product design
<br>Manage multiple projects simultaneously, ensuring critical milestones are met and all hours and related resources expended in the support of the work done to complete assignments are tracked and documented
<br>Consistently act as a key design point of contact with customers and account managers through open and ongoing communications
<br><br>
<b>What you need to succeed:</b><br>
Bachelor’s degree in Packaging Science, Industrial Design, or similar, OR at least 3 years’ experience designing packaging and/or point of purchase displays
<br>Must have a high school diploma or equivalent
<br>Demonstrated experience with design software including ArtiosCAD or similiar enterprise Computer Assisted Design and 3D software
<br>Knowledge of Adobe Illustrator and pallet layout programs
<br>Knowledge of corrugated packaging plant machinery and tooling
<br>Familiarity with retailer specifications, print production, and manufacturing process
<br>Proven track record of critical thinking and problem-solving skills
<br>Excellent communication skills with ability to effectively present design ideas to clients, WestRock sales representatives, plant production teams, and business unit management
<br>Creative thinking and ability to convey abstract ideas/concepts verbally or with quick sketches
<br>Demonstrated ability to drive multiple projects in a fast-paced deadline driven environment
<br><br>
