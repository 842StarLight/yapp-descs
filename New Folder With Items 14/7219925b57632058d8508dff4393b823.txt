<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>CMT</b> <b>Industry</b> professionals design, implement and deploy packaged software solutions that have been developed to accommodate unique Communications, Media and Technology industry business and management processes, regulatory requirements and other business needs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>CMT Aerospace and Defense Industry Applications</b> professionals analyze, design, build, test and deploy innovative industry-specific solutions related to Aerospace and Defense.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The <b>Aerospace &amp; Defense Senior Manager </b>will work closely with Aerospace and Defense clients to identify opportunities, design, and deploy solutions in core operational areas of their businesses such as Engineering, Product Lifecycle Management (PLM), and Supply Chain execution.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>A professional at this position level within Accenture has the following responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides solutions to<b> complex business problems</b> for area(s) of responsibility where analysis of situations requires an in depth knowledge of organizational objectives.</li>
<li>Involved in setting <b>strategic direction </b>to establish near term goals for area of responsibility.</li>
<li>Interacts with senior management levels at a client and/or within Accenture, which involves negotiating or influencing on significant matters.</li>
<li>Has<b> latitude </b>in decision-making and determining objectives and approaches to <b>critical assignments</b>.</li>
<li>Decisions have a lasting impact on <b>area of responsibility </b>with the potential to<b> impact outside area of responsibility.</b>
</li>
<li>
<b>Manages </b>large teams and/or work efforts <b>(if in an individual contributor role) at a client or within Accenture.</b>
</li>
</ul>
