<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company description:</b></h4>
<p>Healthchat provides Patients, Doctors and Medical staff a fast, personal and easy-to-use platform to communicate in the easiest possible way, second only to face-to-face conversation. Our cloud-based, HIPAA-compliant communications platform makes it possible for medical professionals to collaborate seamlessly and securely with each other, resulting in better outcomes for patients through efficient diagnosis, treatment and follow-up.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Position Overview:</b></h4>
<p>The Business Development Executive for Healthchat is a Self-Starter responsible for maximizing relationships with Physicians and Hospital Systems. The ideal candidate is an externally focused product owner, who will gain experience in delivering Healthchat’s product solutions for physicians &amp; health systems while leveraging the voice of the customer to drive solution design and delivery.</p>
<p>
The BDE will report directly to the Vice President of Business Development and will be primarily responsible for the introduction and successful adoption of Healthchat’s communication solutions while increasing maximum brand awareness among patients.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Specifically:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Research healthcare organizations to develop prospecting lists</li>
<li>Network within existing partners to expand our visibility and identify new opportunities</li>
<li>Create Healthchat leads through proactive outreach (e-mail and phone) to achieve monthly sales goals</li>
<li>Respond to incoming Healthchat leads and information requests</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with VP Business Development to follow up on leads, provide follow up materials as needed</li>
<li>Support the VP Business Development to ensure prospective Healthchat projects and partnerships are moving through the pipeline</li>
<li>Perform online presentations to engage Healthchat prospects, develop relationships and provide valuable information to the market</li>
<li>Provide feedback to Healthchat Marketing team in the development of innovative campaigns to support success</li>
<br>
</ul>
