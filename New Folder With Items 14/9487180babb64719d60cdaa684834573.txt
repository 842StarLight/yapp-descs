<b>Job Description Summary</b><br>
<br>
Responsible for contributing to unit's growth and profitability by providing marketing, portfolio management and credit support for senior relationship managers and relationship managers. May manage a portfolio of less complex relationships. Generally targets companies with sales of less than $50 million<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Job Functions</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provides historical financial analysis for CRs and other purposes.<br>
</li>
<li>Maintains credit compliance including monitoring documentation and providing regular financial analysis. Identifies potential problem loans.<br>
</li>
<li>Prepares CRs.<br>
</li>
<li>Prepares all internal documentation required.<br>
</li>
<li>Prepares financial models including obtaining and analyzing all necessary financial data using specialized commercial credit analysis software.<br>
</li>
<li>Researches market for prospective clients. May prepare business development plan. Makes joint calls on customers and prospects.<br>
</li>
<li>Prepares marketing materials and participates in presentation.<br>
</li>
<li>May manage a portfolio of less complex commercial banking relationships providing consultative advice and products and services to meet customers’ needs and enhance and expand banking relationships.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Other Job Duties</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Promotes good customer relations and identifies opportunities to refer customers and/or potential customers to other units of the bank to provide cross-sell opportunities that meet the Bank’s and customer’s needs.<br>
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Required Experience</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Requires advanced knowledge of job area typically obtained through advanced education combined with experience.<br>
</li>
<li>May have practical knowledge of project management.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Education</b><br>
<ul><li>Bachelor's Degree</li></ul>
<br>
<b>
Skills</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Some experience in commercial lending.<br>
</li>
<li>Some knowledge of commercial lending policies, procedures, practices and documentation.<br>
</li>
<li>Good knowledge of accounting, financial statement and cash flow analysis, and financial model analysis and presentation.<br>
</li>
<li>Fundamental knowledge of financial services used by commercial customers.<br>
</li>
<li>Limited knowledge of capital markets.<br>
</li>
<li>Documentation knowledge.<br>
</li>
<li>Financial analysis skills.<br>
</li>
<li>Written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Bilingual in Chinese preferred.</li>
</ul>
