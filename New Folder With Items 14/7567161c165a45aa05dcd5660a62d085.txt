<p><b>Job Title: </b>Accounting Manager</p>
<p><b>
Department: </b>Accounting/ Finance</p>
<p><b>
Reports to: </b>Controller</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b> The Accounting Manager will oversee the activities of the Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable, ensuring the accurate and timely processing of AP and AR and maintaining accurate records. Position involves direct supervision of up to 15 A/P and A/R Associates.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Directly supervise team members in Accounting/Accounts Payable/Accounts Receivable Department, to include enforcement of company policies and applicable laws, interviewing, hiring, planning, assigning and directing work flow, providing performance feedback, rewarding and coaching, addressing complaints and resolving problems</li>
<li>Ensure all existing accounts payables and receivables related internal controls are being followed, with regular testing and updating as necessary</li>
<li>Ensure all periodic accounts payable &amp; account receivable deadlines (data entry, posting, filing, accruals, etc.) are met</li>
<li>Reconcile balance sheet accounts on a period-end close basis</li>
<li>Perform various period-end close expense analyses and accruals</li>
<li>Assist with the accurate and timely dissemination of financial management reports including, but not limited to, internal and external monthly financial statements and periodic audits</li>
<li>Assist with accounting training and associated accounting set-up for new store locations</li>
<li>Document and perform internal control validating testing for other accounting functions within the organization.</li>
<li>Ensure that all documentation complies with company's regulations and professional generally accepted accounting standards.</li>
<li>Respond to Controller, VP of Finance, and/or CFO as assigned with accurate and timely work to facilitate financial reporting needs.</li>
<li>Participate in a wide variety of special projects and compile a variety of special reports.</li>
<li>Communicate with co-workers, management, vendors, customers, and others in a customer service oriented and professional manner.</li>
<li>Conform with and abide by all regulations, policies, work procedures, and instructions. and performs other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
