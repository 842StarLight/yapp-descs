<h2>BDC Call Center Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Are you energetic, engaging and hard-working? Do you love working with customers? Are you competitive and self-motivated? We are seeking enthusiastic and driven individuals to join our unique and fast-paced Business Development Team, as a Business Development Coordinator.
<br><br>
You will be given training in sales processes, sales techniques and product knowledge. You will be paid a base hourly rate plus commission and bonuses with no upper limit. Good Coordinators will average $15-$22 (or more) per hour.
<br><br>
This role is a breeding ground for our wider sales and management organization with tremendous growth opportunities. We are seeking individuals who are interested in a career, not just a job. If you have a background in sales, or have always thought that you could be successful in a sales role, this is a great opportunity to start your career in retail automotive and familiarize yourself with the entire dealership experience.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Generate sales appointments by working with prospective customers who have requested assistance or information via internet, email, chat or phone call inquiries (No cold calling!)</li>
<li>Generate sales appointments by selling the value of our store, our high level of service and track record of integrity and customer care</li>
<li>Interact with customers to identify their individual needs and opportunities for vehicle sales presentations</li>
<li>Rigorously follow up with customers by completing phone calls, emails, text messages and video chats (100+/day) as assigned by the CRM system and/or management</li>
<li>Work closely with sales management to communicate customer expectations and ensure a fantastic customer experience</li>
<li>Present in a professional and well-spoken manner at all times</li>
<li>Log all activities and take extensive detailed notes in a CRM</li>
<li>Meet and exceed goals each month and quarter</li>
<li>Stay informed about new products, features, accessories etc. and their benefits to customers</li>
<li>Attend sales and product training as well as sales meetings as assigned</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong verbal and written communications skills</li>
<li>Tech-savvy and experience with a CRM, or the ability to quickly learn new software</li>
<li>Time management, prioritization and multitasking skills with the ability to manage a high workload</li>
<li>Ability to interpret and follow instructions shared in written, oral, diagram or schedule form</li>
<li>Team player attitude</li>
<li>Willing to submit to a pre-employment background check &amp; drug screen</li>
<li>Able to work some nights (until 8PM) and some Saturdays</li>
<li>Clean driving record and valid drivers license</li>
</ul>
