<div>Job Description<br>

<p>High Times Cannabis Cup Advertisers and Vendors come in all shapes, sizes and market caps, and no one Cannabis Cup Advertising solution works for all. Your knowledge of sales, combined with your communication skills and energy will make this a perfect fit. Using your influencing and relationship-building skills, you provide our clients unique opportunities to show case and advertise their products and services like no other. You will anticipate how decisions are made, persistently explore and uncover the business needs of Cannabis cup clients and understand how our range of product offerings can grow their business. Working with them, you set the vision and the strategy for how their advertising can reach thousands of customers at our events. You will be responsible for prospecting, engaging, qualifying and closing new partnerships with brand advertisers for event advertising, and, or other relevant advertising solutions.</p>
