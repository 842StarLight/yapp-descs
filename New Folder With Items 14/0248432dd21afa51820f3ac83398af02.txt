<h2>Sr Financial Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Extreme Networks is hiring for a Financial Analyst! You will work closely with the CIO and other IT management to develop and monitor the department’s budget and forecast goals. You'll report to FP&amp;A Manager and be responsible for supporting worldwide department budgeting and forecasting efforts, provide operating cost and capital project analysis and adhere to fiscal procurement and spend corporate policies.<br>
<br>
<b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop near and long term operational and capital spend targets and measure progress against the targets.</li>
<li>
Coordinate capital requisition requests and track ongoing capital projects while providing capital budget and forecast updates.</li>
<li>
Prepare month end accrual journal entries and account reconciliations.</li>
<li>
Support annual operating plan efforts.</li>
<li>
Establish effective partnerships with business unit leaders and finance peers.</li>
<li>
Provide ad hoc analysis and financial support in addition to recurring accountabilities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Secondary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create and maintain financial models to improve the predictability of results. Support the development of long-range economic models with alignment to strategic and operating plans. Analyze financial performance and key performance drivers.</li>
<li>
Evolve forward looking metrics and forecasts to more accurately predict future financial performance, including key supporting analysis to include sensitivity analysis and scenario planning.</li>
<li>
Partner with Accounting, Operations and other functional support units to manage operational initiatives and processes.</li>
<li>
Increase financial transparency, awareness and understanding of accounting policies and financial measurements.</li>
<li>
Provide financial analysis and reporting on the effectiveness of department programs and key operational metrics</li>
<li>
Research and report on industry benchmarks; analyze and compare EXTRs results to best practices. Identify and lead improvements to systems and process that impact sales</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 7 years of related finance experience</li>
<li>
Undergraduate degree required in Finance or Accounting, CPA desirable</li>
<li>
Experience working with complex and dynamic IT organizations.</li>
<li>
Experience with Oracle Hyperion, ERP and BI a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 Core Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Problem solving - the ability to solve complex problems</li>
<li>
Finance - Solid financial skills</li>
<li>
Communications – Ability to communicate and influence up and down the organizational matrix</li>
<li>
Performance Measures:<br>
Achievement of IT financial objectives<br>
Forecast accuracy<br>
Speed of delivery ~ Accounting close to published results<br>
On-time delivery of budgets, forecast and other corporate deliverables<br>
Qualitative feedback from business unit leadership</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
<b>LOCATION:</b> Position is based at our Salem, NH site (near Boston) or at our South San Jose, CA headquarters. It is <b>not</b> budgeted for relocation. Qualified local candidates living in either of those areas are encouraged to apply.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Extreme Networks, Inc. Wired to wireless, desktop to datacenter, we go to extreme measures for our 20,000-plus customers in more than 80 countries, delivering 100% insourced support to organizations large and small, including some of the world’s leading names in business, education, government, healthcare, manufacturing and hospitality. Founded in 1996, Extreme is headquartered in San Jose, California.</b><br>
