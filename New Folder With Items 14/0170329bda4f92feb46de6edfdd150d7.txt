<p>Founded in 1921 Newmont Mining Corp. (www.newmont.com) is one of the largest gold companies in the world. Headquartered in Denver, Colorado, the company has approximately 24,000 employees and contractors, with the majority working at Newmont's core operations in the United States, Australia, Ghana, Peru and Suriname.<br>
Newmont operations in South America are trough two different companies, Newmont Peru SRL and Minera Yanacocha SRL.</p>
<p>
Newmont Peru SRL was founded in 1983 as an exploration mining company whose main objective is obtain new gold reserves.</p>
<p>
Minera Yanacoca SRL start their operations in 1992 in Cajamarca City partnership with Buenaventura, is the largest gold mine in South America.</p>

<div>Purpose
</div>
<div><p>To develop, implement, continuously improve and execute Newmont Goldcorp’s procurement strategy, policies and guidelines to drive shared goals and objectives across the entire supply chain and specifically for goods and service-oriented categories and contracts across the South America region the Lifecycle Procurement and Contract Management program while being the business process expert for the stage gate process</p></div>

<div>
<div>
Essential Duties
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Sourcing and Procurement Strategy</b>
</p>
<ul><li>Develop and execute regional sourcing and procurement strategy for sites, regional operations, exploration in order to achieve successful execution of budget plans and investment via the procurement lifecycle.
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop, implement, continuously improve and execute Newmont Goldcorp’s procurement strategy, policies and guidelines to drive shared goals and objectives across the entire supply chain and specifically for goods and service-oriented categories and contracts across the region.</li>
<li>
Develop sourcing and procurement vision for the region in alignment with Corporate and Regional objectives and builds strong working relationships with internal stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Enhance Newmont’s overall procurement capability through continuous improvement and development of the organization.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Regional Category Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Own the Regional Sourcing and Procurement Portfolio.</li>
<li>
Drive the execution of the categories within the portfolio.</li>
<li>
Validate and record all savings and cost avoidance.</li>
<li>
Drive alignment with transactional procurement to ensure consistent use of Newmont Goldcorp procurement standards.</li>
<li>
Develop capability for end users regarding regional category management.
</li>
<li>Accountable for the execution of Lifecycle procurement and contract management process.</li>
<li>
Provide active leadership and support across the region to enable effective team performance.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
People Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead the team of contracts managers and contract administrators in executing the strategy</li>
<li>
Identifying and capturing new enterprise spend areas to add value and drive cost savings.</li>
<li>
Have direct people management responsibility for all sourcing and procurement personnel in the region excluding the Local-Local team.</li>
<li>
Champion knowledge sharing across team and ensuring SC team continues to develop and consistently implement best in class tools and techniques.
</li>
<li>Practice appropriate leadership and management disciplines with indirect reports to achieve Business goals.
</li>
<li>Support and contribute to team building events and team dynamics through personally displaying leadership and team attributes.</li>
</ul>
