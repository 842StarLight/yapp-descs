<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Details:</b><br>
Zylo is the leading SaaS management provider for the enterprise. With cloud investment soaring and decentralized SaaS spend proliferating, enterprises struggle to effectively manage their subscription software. Zylo discovers all SaaS purchased by the business: Overall spend, utilization, renewals, and more are visible in one SaaS system of record. By overcoming the challenges of modern IT leaders and adapting to the needs of its users, with Zylo, IT can manage, measure, and govern their SaaS investment from a central location and empower business leaders to manage their cornerstone apps.
<br><br>
To Learn More about Zylo's Values and Culture check out our blog post here: Zylo's Culture and Values ( https://zylo.com/zylife/zylo-values-2019/ )
<br><br>
<b>Overview:</b><br>
Zylo is looking for a dynamic Business Development Representative to help triple sales pipeline to achieve audacious goals with some of the best customers in the world. We dream big and aren't afraid of the challenge to create and brand Zylo as the world's largest independent SaaS management platform. You will have a direct impact on our annual recurring revenue and our growth and pipeline of our company. Simultaneously, over time you will work to improve and create processes to improve the sales cycle internally and externally. We already have some of the best companies as customers, including Eli Lilly, Slack and more. Now it's your turn to generate more opportunities to expand our impact to help companies be able to discover, track, manage, and govern all of their SaaS applications in one place.
<br><br>
