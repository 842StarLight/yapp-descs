<div><div>
<p>At OneAmerica, we deliver on promises when customers need us most. We believe the best way to serve our customers is to know that every individual, employee, family and business we work with has unique personal and financial goals. We keep our promises, so we can help them achieve their goals and realize their definition of financial success.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The OneAmerica Retirement Services (RS) division serves America’s retirement plan needs. The RS division is committed to earning and maintaining clients’ needs by providing exceptional service and expertise to plan sponsors, participants and our industry’s best advisors.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Leader of Client Management Services is responsible for managing the teams that are responsible for the day to day administration of Qualified Retirement Plans. These teams are in multiple locations across the country with this role based in Indianapolis. This position will have direct responsibility for the delivery of the administrative services provided to the Retirement Services clients. The role interfaces and partners with other groups within OneAmerica to ensure client expectations are met.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Leader of CMS, a Sr. Director level position, requires a deep understanding of Defined Contribution best practices around plan design, administration, and corporate actions. This person is the strategic lead who is responsible for the ensuring the service satisfaction and retention of our Defined Contribution clients.<br>
<br>
The CMS leader will interface frequently with senior members of internal leadership team as well as our client contacts and their financial advisors. A successful candidate must have excellent interpersonal skills, proven negotiating skills, a demonstrated administration knowledge of Defined Contribution and Non-Qualified Deferred Compensation plans, and an appreciation for the challenges that face today's plan sponsors.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
This position will be part of the leadership team in the Client Administration group and will help execute the strategic vision and business plan of RS, as well as impact the RS strategic vision of change management. The incumbent will be responsible for developing and implementing the OneAmerica way for RS Client Administration planning and execution. This role will drive our strategy and capabilities while demonstrating OneAmerica’s ASPIRE values and EWA beliefs.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>In collaboration with other RS team members and enterprise functions, develops, drives and supports key elements of RS’s long-term strategy:
<ul>
<li>Improving our Operating leverage</li>
<li>Deliver on our relationship strategy.</li>
<li>Improve sponsor</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Leads all product, business development, service model and participant services functions to drive growth, sales and client retention results across the RS division.</li>
<li>Influences and drives development of process improvement strategies and objectives for Client Management Services within RS Client Administration.</li>
<li>Accountable for overseeing the portfolio of projects and initiatives to mature RS capabilities in the areas of sales, service and operations.</li>
<li>Develops, manages and optimizes the execution of the strategic plan for the CMS group.</li>
<li>Champions the development of key relationship partnerships within the RS division, as well as across the enterprise.</li>
<li>Develops and Grows associates and leaders within the CMS organization</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree;</li>
<li>15+ years of Retirement Plan Administration experience, including working knowledge of Testing/5500/Prototype Plan document/VCP/Defined Contribution Consulting and Plan Rules</li>
<li>Proven experience in achieving business results through strategic leadership and capability development</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written and presentation skills</li>
<li>Structured, organized individual who is capable of handling multiple tasks and driving results</li>
<li>Embraces change and knows how to lead change through influence</li>
<li>Strong collaborator and relationship builder with a demonstrated ability to partner with others for successful execution</li>
<li>Adept at applying best practices and frameworks in a practical way</li>
</ul>
