<p>The Copy Manager reporting to the Creative Director, develops content strategy for the brand voice as well as manages a team of writers and freelancers who write for our leisure brands. This includes, but is not limited to, copy and content for B-to-B and B-to-C in advertising, marketing and content for digital and print mediums which promotes sales and educates customers on multiple brands within the Flight Centre Travel Group USA Leisure family. The ideal candidate is passionate about travel, and stays current on industry trends and events. They will also be up-to-date on web (SEO) best practices.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
CORE FUNCTIONS:</p>
<ul><li>Act as the voice for the brands, driving the written expression of the brand in all marketing and original content</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strengthen and develop our brand voices along with creating brand strategy documents, positioning and providing guidelines to ensure consistency.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop effective strategy, with the creative team, that clearly distinguishes and positions the brands within the marketplace</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop a strategy to curate UGC (user generated content) for display on web and marketing materials</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage Workplace by Facebook content and integration strategy</li></ul>

<ul><li>Deliver creative copy that is on brief, on time, and effectively conveys the brand in all marketing communications</li></ul>
<ul><li>Mentor and Inspire the copy team as well as a being a contributing writer</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage writers who are responsible for:</li></ul>
<p>
o Advertising Copy for sales campaigns, newsletters, product descriptions, direct mailers, web copy, emails and video</p>
