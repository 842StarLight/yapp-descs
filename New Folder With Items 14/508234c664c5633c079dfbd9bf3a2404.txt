<h2>Client Solution Specialist</h2>

<b>Position Overview</b><br>
<br>
At PNC, our people are our greatest differentiator and competitive advantage in the markets we serve. We are all united in delivering the best experience for our customers. As a Client Solutions Specialist - Corporate Travel Agent within PNC's Supply Chain Management organization, you will be based in Louisville KY. PNC Travel is responsible for providing both general and in-depth business specific customer service, to answer questions, and resolve issues. You will be an integral member of PNC as you interact daily with one of our most important assets, our employees. You will respond to routine customer service/service partner inquiries, and also reply to issues via telephone and e-mail by using airline systems, scripts and procedures. During your active and highly productive workday, you will navigate a computerized system for information gathering and booking travel for employees. Through teamwork and integrity, you will direct travelers to published materials, secondary sources, or senior level CSRs for more complex inquiries. Working with all levels of PNC employees and travel suppliers is required.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Profile</b><br>
<br>
<ul>

<li>
Acts as a point of contact for clients, client teams or service partners and may act as an escalation point for client implementation and servicing issues.</li>
<li>
Initiates, updates and verifies client, account, or transaction details in relevant systems/applications. Provides reports as needed.</li>
<li>
May perform general administrative support.</li>
</ul>
