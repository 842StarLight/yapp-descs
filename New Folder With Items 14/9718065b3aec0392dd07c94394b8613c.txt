<h2>Manager, FP&A - Enterprise Technology</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Role</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
We are looking for an FP&amp;A Manager to lead the Finance team for the Enterprise Technology business unit. As part of the Corporate FP&amp;A team, this role will serve as the Finance Partner for the recently created Enterprise Technology business unit. This is an opportunity to join a start-up within the larger start-up and be a key member of the company’s Technology and Finance organizations. Working closely with functional leaders across the business, this individual will take the lead in developing financial plans and laying the financial infrastructure to achieve our near and long-term growth objectives. Reporting to the Finance Partner for WeWork’s Technology organization, this position will apply her/his knowledge and expertise in Technology Finance, including budgeting, strategic planning, reporting, and analytics to create and implement a scalable financial infrastructure for this new business unit. The FP&amp;A Manager will be an integral part of a high-profile team with ongoing exposure to functional and finance partners worldwide. The ideal candidate will have exceptional financial and strategic planning skills, a great attitude, refined communication skills, a proven ability to be resourceful, and will be eager to dive into the data.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Build the foundation for financial reporting, including: annual budget development and quarterly forecasts, long-range planning, and on-going reporting versus plan.</p></li>
<li><p>
Support the monthly close process through ownership of core models and templates. Partner with accounting team to ensure entries are booked timely and accurately.</p></li>
<li><p>
Analyze monthly and quarterly business results, KPIs, trends, and synthesize results / critical business insights for leadership.</p></li>
<li><p>
Create, manage, and automate operational reports and dashboards to monitor the performance of the business.</p></li>
<li><p>
Build team’s reputation as a step-change thinker, action-driver, and value-creator by providing action-oriented solutions to complex questions.</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
4-6 years of experience in financial planning and analysis, investment banking, or related field</p></li>
<li><p>
Expertise in SaaS or other subscription business model economics (CAC, LTV, churn, sales compensation, etc.)</p></li>
<li><p>
BA/BS degree with a major in finance, accounting, or related field</p></li>
<li><p>
MBA preferred</p></li>
<li><p>
Strong capabilities in financial modeling, Excel, Google Sheets, PowerPoint</p></li>
<li><p>
Knowledge of Workday, Essbase, and/or Tableau a plus</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Must be detail-oriented and keen to dig into numbers and variances to propose recommendations and drive improved economic outcomes</p></li>
<li><p>
Demonstrated track record of leading and influencing teams with strong interpersonal and team-building skills</p></li>
<li><p>
Strong written and verbal communication skills</p></li>
<li><p>
Authenticity, resilience, and empathy are all key attributes for success</p></li>
</ul>
