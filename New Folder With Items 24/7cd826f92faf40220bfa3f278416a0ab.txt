<h2>Ecommerce Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Ecommerce Business Analyst</b><br>
<br>
<b>Your Opportunity:</b><br>
You will be the primary liaison between IT and our eCommerce stakeholders (Marketing, Merchandising, and Sales), turning ideas, concepts and requirements into production-ready features and functionalities. You will evaluate, design, and document business requirements and collaborate with teams to ensure successful implementation/resolution of defects, functional improvements, and new features and capabilities.<br>
<br>
<b>Pay range:</b> $70,000 - $95,000 annually<br>
<br>
<b>What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with business users to understand business needs and objectives.</li>
<li>
Document business requirements, functional specifications, solution design, system requirements, and process/data flows for all assigned development efforts.</li>
<li>
Prepare and lead coordination meetings between developers, marketing, business users, and vendors.</li>
<li>
Manage all aspects of ecommerce vendor relationships.
</li>
<li>Work with developers to review and clarify requirements to ensure technical specifications and design satisfy the business needs.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the QA team for successful testing and code deployment.</li>
<li>
Validate final solution/product meets signed business requirements documentation.</li>
<li>
Manage system issues through triage and resolution.</li>
<li>
Monitor and manage issue logs with prioritization of critical issues.</li>
<li>
Create progress reports on issue resolution.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What You’ll Need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Information Systems, Marketing, Business, or a related field.</li>
<li>
5 years of experience in a technical Senior Business Analyst role supporting a high-volume eCommerce platform.</li>
<li>
Exceptional skills in creating project documentation and communication.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple assignments concurrently in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>
Excellent time management and attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, presentation and interpersonal skills, both written and verbal.</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively communicate with technical, non-technical, and executive teams on issues, tasks, and status.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to develop and foster highly effective working relationships across IT, Marketing, Merchandising, and Sales.
</li>
<li>Strong self-motivation and self-direction.</li>
<li>
Exceptional organizational and problem-solving skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience with off the shelf Content Management Systems (CMS), ecommerce platforms, or SAP.
</li>
<li>Project management within established Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC), SCRUM/Agile certification, or PMI certification.</li>
</ul>
