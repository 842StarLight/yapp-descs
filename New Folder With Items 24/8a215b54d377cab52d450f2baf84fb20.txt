<h2>Billing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>To accurately maintain inventory records and record profit from sale of vehicles
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities: </b>This job has no direct supervisory responsibilities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b><b>include the following:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain inventory schedules
</li>
<li>Records profit from sale of vehicles
</li>
<li>Records salesperson commission
</li>
<li>Verify and ensure prompt payment of factory incentives
</li>
<li>Post all incentive payments
</li>
<li>Post all retail, loaner, and fleet deals, &amp; add to deal log, correct deal log
</li>
<li>Receipt daily ACH contract fundings
</li>
<li>Floor plan payoffs
</li>
<li>Balance gross profit with manager’s monthly front end
</li>
<li>Challenge and apply for all old incentives not paid
</li>
<li>Trade Payoff Checks – Ensure Timely Receipt of Trade Titles
</li>
<li>First Look Water Report
</li>
<li>Assist Bank with Floor Plan Audits
</li>
<li>Build relationships and communication with dealership personnel, accounting managers, and HAG.
</li>
<li>Maintain the following Schedules: Contracts in Transit Schedule, We Owe Schedule, Incentives Schedule, Trade Pay off Schedule, Lien Pay off Schedule
</li>
<li>Inventory Reconciliation Schedule
</li>
<li>Maintains CSI at or above Company standards
</li>
<li>Maintains an organized, clean and safe work area
</li>
<li>Participates in required training
</li>
<li>Records all hours worked accurately in company timekeeping system
</li>
<li>Follows Safeguards rules and regulations.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates the Company’s Core Values
</li>
<li>Complies with Company policies and procedures
</li>
<li>Observes all Federal, State, Local and Company safety rules and regulations in the performance of duties.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
