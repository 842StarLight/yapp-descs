<h2>vAuto Performance Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>THIS IS A REMOTE POSITION, WE ARE ACCEPTING APPLICANTS FROM ANYWHERE IN THE UNITED STATES. THIS IS NOT A REGIONAL BASED POSITION.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As the vAuto Performance Manager, you will be responsible for client retention, harvesting additional accounts, and upselling the vAuto suite of products. The vAuto Performance Manager must have a strong focus and experience in retail automotive management. To be successful in the role of vAuto Performance Manager, one must be able to work effectively in a fluid, fast-paced agile environment, handle a high account load, and be willing to travel on a regular basis.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Technology we use:</b></div>
<div><ul><li>
Customer Relationship Management Systems, MS Office (Excel, Word, Powerpoint)</li></ul></div>
<div><b>
Your Role:</b></div>
<div>
<ul><li>
Working REMOTELY, effectively manage the progress and results of accounts assigned, while engaging in high-profile activity across strategic client groups</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Build and develop strong working relationships with customers and all users at all levels from Dealer/Owner/Decision Maker on down</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Work with 80-110 accounts, as well as ‘at risk’ clients and determine the proper action plan</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Monitor and analyze account performance and trends to determine adjustments to dealer expectations, goals, and objectives in order to achieve results</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Generate additional revenue by harvesting existing accounts for additional stores and products, while continually working on turning around cancelled accounts</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Determine needs for additional resources in order to maintain continued success with all clients</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Assist in generating high client attendance to product workshops</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Actively participate in product development/enhancement review sessions to continue in development as a vAuto ‘knowledge master’</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Travel to clients, allowing for maximum time with all clients and maximum client reach</li></ul>
