<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Founded in 1980, Samson Technologies is a leading manufacturer of audio products for performers, fitness instructors, educators and content creators. Our family of brands includes Samson Wireless and Audio products, Hartke bass amplifiers and speakers and Michael Kelly Guitars. Headquartered in Long Island, New York, Samson manufactures, distributes and sells products that can be found in more than 90 countries around the world. Samson is a place for people who are passionate about products and take the initiative to make things happen, all while still having fun. We look for people with talent and vision, take chances and embrace challenges.</p>
<p><b>Description: </b></p>
<p>We are looking for a Marketing Coordinator to participate in our company’s marketing efforts. The Marketing Coordinator will administer the market department’s calendars ensuring deadlines met, identifying all the tasks that need to be completed, and delegating those tasks to the appropriate team members considering resource allocation. The Marketing Coordinator will plan and organize attended trade shows and events. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills, well organized and methodical. The Marketing Coordinator will ensure our company’s marketing efforts help us achieve our immediate and long-term business goals.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage marketing department project calendars, including product launch and content schedules.</li>
<li>Support marketing and advertising campaigns by assembling and analyzing sales data, planning and organizing presentations.</li>
<li>Keeps promotional materials ready by coordinating requirements with graphics department; inventorying stock; placing orders; verifying receipt.</li>
<li>Supports staff by providing sales data, market trends, forecasts, account analyses, new product information; relaying customer services requests.</li>
<li>Plans meetings and trade shows by assembling and coordinating requirements; developing schedules and assignments; booking travel and overseeing logistics.</li>
<li>Monitors budgets by comparing and analyzing actual results with plans and forecasts.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Writing, editing, communication, organizational management skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite</li>
<li>Knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite a plus.</li>
<li>Detail oriented with the ability to multi-task and manage projects across multiple departments.</li>
<li>Friendly and outgoing personality.</li>
<li>A team player who takes the initiative to help others.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Time management skills and the ability to prioritize and work with multiple tasks.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work self-sufficiently.</li>
</ul>
