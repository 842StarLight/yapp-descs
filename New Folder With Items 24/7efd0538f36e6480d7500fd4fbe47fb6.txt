 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h3>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
The incumbent will report directly to the Vice President, Marketing and will provide project management as well as administrative support to marketing professionals within the Commercial Team. This role will assist in managing and monitoring a marketing budget and will coordinate multiple assignments, projects and provide support to the Marketing team. This role will facilitate and track on an ongoing basis contracts, purchase orders and statement of work processes. This role will manage administrative operations for the commercial leadership team and organization ensuring optimal efficiency. This role will assist in commercial project planning and other commercial activities.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties/Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Oversee and coordinate day-to-day operations of all marketing activities, providing comprehensive administrative support, and streamlining workflow for the VP of Marketing/ other members of the marketing team.</li>
<li>
Manage Statement of Work (SOW), Purchase Order (PO) and vendor set up processes for commercial team, including tracking of all open POs and contracts</li>
<li>
Interface with commercial and other cross functional teams to facilitate meeting planning and organization, preparedness, and correspondence.</li>
<li>
Facilitate meetings by creating structured agendas, preparing PowerPoint slides, preparing and distributing meeting materials, coordinating A/V and telecom meeting equipment, ensuring connectivity of remote participants, and taking minutes as needed.</li>
<li>
Track promotional materials across marketing, digital, print and other mediums.</li>
<li>
Manage inventory of marketing collateral and ordering platform for the team.</li>
<li>
Manage and collaborate with both internal and with external third parties to ensure the success of all commercial initiatives through project planning, tracking and facilitation.</li>
<li>
Develop tracking mechanisms to monitor and report on cost budgets as required by projects</li>
<li>
Serve as liaison with IT and facilities on behalf of the commercial team including promotional material review process and software</li>
<li>
Coordinate budget management and accrual process including the submission and routing of key internal documents as required for the proper allocation and payments of funds</li>
<li>
Maintain database of Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs) and industry physician and other contacts</li>
<li>
Manage internal and marketing meeting logistics, including registration and attendee tracking, presentation and materials support and pre- and post-event activities</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with other executive administrative staff to ensure commercial calendar alignment and optimization across full team</li>
<li>
Identify facility, exhibit set-up, equipment and catering requirements for meetings</li>
<li>
Manage and reconcile corporate credit card accounts, senior team travel activity and expense reports for the VP of Marketing and members of the marketing team.</li>
<li>
Compose internal and external correspondence, organizational updates, and routine communications, as needed.</li>
<li>
Provide general office support, including filing, photocopying, scanning, faxing, and preparing and scheduling overnight shipping/delivery services.</li>
</ul>
