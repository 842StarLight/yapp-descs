<h2>Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>At Blue Skies, the Data Analyst is primarily responsible for the successful completion of project activities as directed by their Project Manager. These project activities include understanding the customers’ business in order to complete requirements analysis and definition, design of the solution, prototypes and testing, deployment of the solution and post go-live support. The Data Analyst works under the direction of the engagement Project Manager but may also be required to lead small to medium engagements.</p>
<p>The Data Analyst’s responsibilities fall into three major categories: Delivery, Practice Development, and Sales &amp; Marketing.</p>
<p>Delivery</p>
<ul>
<li>Sustain an average billable utilization of 100%</li>
<li>Maintain a high level of Customer Satisfaction by exceeding client expectations</li>
<li>Self-Manage the completion of assigned activities</li>
</ul>
<p>Practice Development</p>
<ul>
<li>Participate proactively in corporate initiatives</li>
<li>Provide operational support to Blue Skies with the creation and refinement of deliverables and processes (focused on project delivery)</li>
</ul>
<p>Sales / Sales Collaboration &amp; Marketing</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide Sales &amp; Business Development Support when assigned</li>
<li>Maintain expected levels of activity within the Blue Skies’ “Relationship Sales Program”</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>Blue Skies expects the Data Analyst to plan, research and analyze the business requirements of the customer; make reasoned decisions after assessing the details, and then provide recommendations along with the risks and issues involved with each alternative.</p>
<ul>
<li>Project Initiation – participate in the project onboarding process, assimilate client information and project contractual obligations, understand assigned tasks</li>
<li>Project Planning – to assist the Project Manager with the detailed planning of assigned activities</li>
<li>Project Execution – successfully complete all assigned individual and team project tasks throughout the project, meeting timelines and deliverable quality standards</li>
<li>Project Closure – complete assigned project closure tasks including Blue Skies’ documentation, templates and marketing activities</li>
<li>Corporate Growth – participate in corporate initiatives in support of Blue Skies’ Journey to Excellence mission</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills and Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Management Consulting experience desired</li>
<li>Master Data Management (MDM) software implementation experience (Informatica, Reltio, SAP MDG, Talend, Teradata, etc.)</li>
<li>Strong Analytical, Problem Solving skills and keen Attention to Detail</li>
<li>Ability to collect &amp; interpret large data sets to identify patterns &amp; trends</li>
<li>Ability to analyze data and report the results back to the relevant members of the business</li>
<li>Ability to analyze large datasets &amp; write comprehensive reports</li>
<li>Ability to translate business requirements into data requirements</li>
<li>Defining new data collection and analysis processes</li>
<li>General Business Process and Operations understanding (i.e., Finance, Supply Chain, etc.)</li>
<li>Understanding of Core Data objects (Product, Customer, Vendor) and cross-functional impact of each</li>
<li>Understanding of data gathering, inspecting, cleansing, transforming, and modeling/diagramming techniques</li>
<li>Understanding of data integration, data conversion, data profiling and data life cycle</li>
<li>Implementation Methodology Lifecycle understanding and execution</li>
<li>Ability to Self-Manage effectively</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate effectively on a team</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to learn new concepts and approaches</li>
<li>MS Excel and SQL proficiency to support data analysis and reporting activities</li>
<li>MS PowerPoint content development and creativity skills</li>
<li>Friendly, respectful, courteous, selfless and modest</li>
<li>Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Mathematics, Computer Science, Information Management or Statistics</li>
</ul>
