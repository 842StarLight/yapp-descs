<h2>Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>ABOUT US</b></p>
<p>
Solutran, based in Plymouth, Minnesota, is an innovative financial technology industry leader in the high-volume payments processing space with a focus on creating game-changing, customer friendly technologies. We serve the nation's largest health insurers, retailers, state governments, and consumer product manufacturers in a unique blend of participants on an innovative payment network. We are a market leader in each of our current business lines building market share with new products that administer over-the-counter benefits to Medicare Advantage participants and others, support food insecure individuals and families through government WIC EBT and SNAP programs and deliver state-of-the-art technology that incentivizes healthier eating via our Healthy Savings product.
</p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
At Solutran, our culture is one where we strive to understand our prospects goals first, such that we can lead them to success. Our programs provide immense long-term benefits to our customers. As such, this requires the sales leaders and their teams to be relentlessly focused on creating material value for our customers and seeing each program through to a successful launch. This results in high customer satisfaction and recurring revenue for the business, while creating additional growth opportunities for the entire organization into the future.</p>
<p>
This position will be responsible for identifying, executing, and supporting sales initiatives across this unique blend of solution and network constituents. This includes the S3 promotion and payment platform that runs on the S3 network as well as the Visa processing capabilities. This role will directly manage the healthcare sector sales team working to bring these healthy living programs to sponsors. This position will also have direct ownership over our largest key healthcare accounts. Finally, the role also includes management of the team responsible for onboarding and managing the S3 retailer network.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide direct sales and sales leadership to procure benefits and rewards programs with the top one hundred healthcare companies.
</li>
<li>Align with key retail and program manager partners to drive jointly coordinated sales activities, as well as complex implementation and operational alignment.
</li>
<li>Responsible for the strategic relationship management across all healthcare client and partner accounts.
</li>
<li>Directly manage the healthcare sales and retail network sales teams.</li>
<li>
Adopt and help evolve the Solutran sales strategies with a focus on driving financial results.
</li>
<li>Foster a positive, fair and results-oriented sales culture in which teamwork and collaboration with the rest of the organization are the key tenants.</li>
<li>
Identify, prioritize, and secure business based on a deep understanding of our program and the business strategies of our customers.</li>
<li>
Plan and prepare sales pitches and presentation materials focused on target companies.</li>
<li>
Present fact-based business proposals that lead to successful opportunities.</li>
<li>
Develop strong working relationships with key contacts within each company.</li>
<li>
Maintain a CRM and business tracking process focused on up to date data and team member task ownership.</li>
<li>
Partner with other cross-functional teams to meet customer goals; assist with contract management and implementations.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate an existing knowledge base of the health insurance industry or an ability to quickly learn it. This includes an emphasis on government-funded plans.
</li>
<li>Exercise enterprise solution selling capabilities and strong leadership intuition.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p>An individual in this position must be able to successfully perform the essential duties and responsibilities listed above. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of this position.</p>
