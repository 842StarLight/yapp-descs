<h2>Employment Skills Trainer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
To provide supervision and production training to Extended Employment Services participants assigned to various training sites.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential duties include the following. Other duties within the scope of this position may be assigned.<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Assigns program participants to different tasks such as hanging, sorting, sales floor tasks or other appropriate duties.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Trains, supervises and assists with program participant work site activities/breaks as needed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Monitors and records all production data for each program participant.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Uses software / case management systems (GoodPoint, Microsoft Word, Outlook, etc.) to input program participant data and communicate with team.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develops methods to improve quality and quantity of work performance.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Partners with retail management to ensure that productivity requirements are met.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provides information to the Case Manager regarding program participant production, behavior and progress, if applicable.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensures participants are swiping in and out of KRONOS on a daily basis.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Completes case narratives in a comprehensive and accurate manner.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Conducts monthly safety meetings</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participates in case staffing/service planning meeting.</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
