<h2>Digital Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p>If you are knowledgeable and passionate about Digital Marketing, we want to talk to you! Auto Loan Acceptance Corp is looking for an experienced, hands on, Digital Marketing Manager to develop, implement, track and optimize our digital marketing campaigns across all digital channels for our multi-state, growing car companies. Duties include planning digital marketing campaigns, including web, SEM, email, social media and display advertising. Maintaining our social media presence across all digital channels. Measuring and reporting on the performance of all digital marketing campaigns with a heavy focus on developing leads and tracking conversions and return on investment.</p>
<p>Candidate should have a strong grasp of current marketing tools and strategies and be able to lead integrated digital marketing campaigns from concept to execution. Our Digital Marketing Manager will work with our sales department and vendors to launch campaigns on time and on budget. We have a sales and finance division and are a multi-state company. Top pay for the right experience with a full benefit package. Looking for someone local in the Sioux Falls or surrounding area. Confidential interviews. EOE</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Plan and execute all digital marketing, including SEM, marketing database, email, social media and display advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Design, build and maintain our social media presence</li>
<li>Measure and report performance of all digital marketing campaigns, and assess against goals (ROI and KPIs)</li>
<li>Identify trends and insights, and optimize spend and performance based on the insights</li>
<li>Brainstorm new and creative growth strategies</li>
<li>Plan, execute, and measure experiments and conversion tests</li>
<li>Create landing pages and optimize user experience</li>
<li>Keep websites fresh and up to date</li>
<li>Utilize strong analytical ability to evaluate end-to-end customer experience across multiple channels and customer touch points</li>
<li>Collaborate with agencies and other vendor partners</li>
<li>Evaluate emerging technologies. Provide thought leadership and perspective for adoption where appropriate</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>BS/MS degree in marketing or a related field or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Proven working experience in digital marketing. Hands on experience required</li>
<li>Demonstrable experience leading and managing SEM, marketing database, email, social media and/or display advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Excellent analytical and project management skills</li>
<li>An ability to multitask and perform under tight deadlines</li>
<li>Highly creative with experience in identifying target audiences and devising digital campaigns that engage, inform, motivate and ultimately convert to a lead</li>
<li>Experience in optimizing landing pages and user funnels</li>
<li>Experience with A/B and multivariate experiments</li>
<li>Solid knowledge of website analytics tools and working knowledge of ad serving tools</li>
<li>Experience and certified in setting up and optimizing Google AdWords campaigns</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and data-driven thinking</li>
<li>Up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices in online marketing and measurements</li>
</ul>
<p>Benefits</p>
<ul>
<li>PTO</li>
<li>Vision and Dental Insurance</li>
<li>Health Insurance</li>
<li>401K Program</li>
</ul>
