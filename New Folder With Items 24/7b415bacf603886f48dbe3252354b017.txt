<h2>Client Success Manager- Data License</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About the Company</b></p>
<p>
PlaceIQ is a leading data and technology provider that powers critical business and marketing decisions with location data, analytics and insights. An early industry pioneer, PlaceIQ has become the standard for fueling better decisions by marketers, analysts and publishers through powerful, location-based consumer insights, real-world measurement and attribution.</p>
<p>
With PlaceIQ, companies can uncover opportunities within the consumer journey by learning about and connecting with location-based audiences, measuring real-world ROI and applying insights that drive intelligent marketing and successful business outcomes.</p>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
As a Client Success Manager (CSM) for Data License customers, you will work directly with sophisticated clients as well as product, engineering, operations and account leads to ensure clients are realizing the most value from licensing PlaceIQ data. The CSM owns success for each data license customer, measured as renewal and variable revenue production. They proactively drive the execution of use cases leveraging PlaceIQ data across a client’s multi-disciplinary teams to ensure achievement of client objectives.</p>
<p>
This role involves interfacing with a broad spectrum of customer roles, from executive to operational, engineering, analytic and business leads so that workstreams used to integrate and make use of PlaceIQ data can be properly managed. You will be required to go deep on addressing technical, operational problem-solving, addressing geospatial methodological questions and drive account engagements with customer’s clients. Ideal candidates demonstrate an ability to manage/lead complex, large accounts across disciplines and organizations; are process-oriented in how you work; and able to think through complex problems and clearly communicate requirements into PlaceIQ data science, engineering and operational personnel.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead post-sales engagement with data license clients and partners to ensure successful use and value creation from PlaceIQ data to fulfill each customer’s use case needs</li>
<li>
Drive cross-functional teams with customers spanning operations, data science, engineering, analytics, marketing and business disciplines that are managing workstreams executing successful deployment of PlaceIQ data within customer’s enterprise</li>
<li>
Project manage tasks between customers and PlaceIQ internal resources in a process-oriented way to ensure efficient and effective execution of work between customers and PlaceIQ operational teams</li>
<li>
Execute cadenced review sessions with customers to ensure we are achieving customer objectives &amp; expectations, including weekly, monthly and quarterly reviews as required</li>
<li>
Be the Trusted Advisor for the client on all things geospatially related, methodologies utilized by PlaceIQ to ensure high levels of consultative engagement to solve customer problems</li>
<li>
Interface with C-Level and tactical customer personnel with a high level of professionalism that drives credibility and rapport across the customer’s enterprise</li>
<li>
Engage with customer sales &amp; account teams to help them sell-through solutions leveraging PlaceIQ data, including presentations to end clients articulating our value proposition</li>
<li>
Responsible for driving client upsell opportunities and securing ongoing renewals</li>
<li>
You become an expert in geospatial methodologies and have a full understanding of PlaceIQ technology &amp; techniques to support deep questions from client data science teams</li>
<li>
Maintain and update documentation processes for PlaceIQ data license solutions</li>
<li>
Manage internal ticketing process with attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Steward through contract redline iterations customers and internal counsel</li>
</ul>
