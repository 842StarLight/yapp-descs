<h2>Senior Manager Finance, American Standard Brand</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Reporting to the VP, FP&amp;A this role is the primary finance business partner to the VP of Marketing for the American Standard brand. This role is responsible for partnering and influencing cross-functional teams in Marketing, Sales, Supply Chain and Engineering to complete financial analysis/make recommendations based on findings, in an effort to drive sales growth, profitability and return on investments. The position requires strong analytical experience and proven communication skill.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Work extensively with the American Standard brand marketing team to build robust category and brand strategies</li>
<li>
Drive financial analysis of overall brand profit and provide insight in the following areas:<ul>
<li>
Net Sales and Margin health</li>
<li>
New product performance</li>
<li>
POS market trends and competitor comps</li>
<li>
Return on key investments (product development, promotions, advertising)</li>
<li>
Conduct extensive review of transactional-related pricing and make recommendations on process improvements and savings initiatives</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Support the New Product Stage Gate process<ul>
<li>
Develop robust sales and profit models</li>
<li>
Work with Engineering and Supply Chain to validate product cost assumptions and identify optimal sourcing options</li>
<li>
Drive adherence to sales and margin hurdle rates and guidelines, ensuring that estimates are not overstated for the sake of getting the project approved</li>
<li>
Challenge the cross-functional team to consider all risks and opportunities and the implications to the project (project timing delays and impact on customer display reset windows, marketing investment needed to ensure success, etc.)</li>
<li>
Perform post-audit to leverage learnings in future Stage Gates</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Drive SKU rationalization process for the brand by reviewing sales and margin data and making recommendations for pruning underperforming SKUs</li>
<li>
Participate in Annual Operating Plans, Strategic Plans and Monthly Forecasting of new product sales, SG&amp;A and CAPEX
</li>
<li>Assist to develop sales targets by product category/customer, with specific linkage to overall sales goals and new product commitments</li>
<li>
Participate in SOIP demand meetings to ensure linkage of latest sales forecast to production planning, with specific emphasis on new products and promotions. Identify to Management any risks and opportunities</li>
<li>
Guide professional development of direct report (Manager Finance, AS Brand) including goal setting performance evaluation, training, mentoring, understanding of other functional areas and linkage to the Company’s overall long-term strategy</li>
<li>
Work closely with the FP&amp;A team and the GROHE/DXV finance leader to ensure harmonization across brands where applicable</li>
<li>
Ad hoc projects as directed by management to drive strategic initiatives</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>A bachelor’s degree is required, preferably in Business/Finance/Accounting; an MBA is desirable</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge of accounting principles, CPA or CFA desired but not mandatory</li>
<li>
The successful candidate must have at least 7-10 years of professional experience, including meaningful experience as a key member of a world-class financial function; experience in brand or product finance a plus</li>
<li>
He/she must have significant planning and financial management experience, ideally in a global manufacturing company environment, where data analysis is valued.</li>
<li>
Strong systems skills and experience with simplifying processes</li>
<li>
Excel proficiency, working with large volumes of data and pivot table modeling</li>
<li>
Knowledge of SAP or similar ERP systems</li>
<li>
He/she will be able to relate to operational issues as well as understand, create, and present financial information to senior management</li>
<li>
The Sr. Manager of Brand Finance will possess superior technical financial skills balanced with sound business judgment</li>
<li>
He/she will have the ability to effectively balance financial and operational factors and to analyze complex business problems and develop appropriate solutions in a timely and responsive manner</li>
<li>
Capability to assume broader roles in the future</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational, communication and presentation skills; ability to thrive while being tested and pushed by the financial leaders and senior management</li>
<li>
Entrepreneurial spirit, with a hands-on, roll-up-the-sleeves mentality and a structured and pragmatic approach</li>
<li>
Demonstrated analytical thinker with a long-term value-creation orientation</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills; ability to build relationships with superiors, peers and subordinates across a large and complex organization</li>
<li>
Unquestionable ethics, integrity and business judgment</li>
<li>
Strong strategic thinker and general manager mindset; capable of translating business objectives into tactical action plans and milestones</li>
<li>
Driven, with a winning mentality, combined with a sense of humor and genuine interest in people.
</li>
<li>Tactful and diplomatic professional with unquestionable integrity and personal values.</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
