<h2>IT Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary: </b>This position is responsible for the hands-on management and completion of Information Technology Department projects at Union Supply locations as well as customer locations. This position configures, monitors, and provides support on designated Information Systems and manages assigned IT projects in Server Room, Data Center and Warehouse environments as required. Participates in discussions and IT Department meetings regarding the upgrade and support of Warehouse Management Systems and IT system management and security applications. Prepare and maintain all essential project documentation such as Project Plans, Charters, Network Diagrams, Rack Elevations, Test Plans, correspondence, etc.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the deployment, monitoring and support of Information Systems that are used for Union Supply off-site warehouses and related technologies required for order and inventory management, and the fulfillment of sales orders from a warehouse environment.</li>
<li>Identify, understand and satisfy customer IT needs related to the fulfillment of sales orders via off-site warehouses and the processing of credits payable to Union Supply customers.</li>
<li>Prepare and manage project budgets and schedules to achieve financial objectives and project timelines.</li>
<li>Effectively manage all project communications for all assigned IT related projects including Project Charters, Statements of Work, internal memos and Project Status Reports.</li>
<li>Review project tasks with stakeholders and take action to reduce the risks and minimize the effects of unforeseen changes or events.</li>
<li>Provide direction to technical personnel and contracted technicians for both the installation and support of Information System and Warehouse Management software solutions.</li>
<li>Report to and/or advise senior management regarding Information Systems project plans, project risks, security risks and concerns, customer requests for scope changes, etc.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned by the Director or Technical Services.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>5 years of technical project management related of commercial off the shelf and/or custom built information systems or solutions.</li>
<li>3-5 years of experience providing support to end users, troubleshooting hardware/software issues and installing desktops, laptops and peripheral equipment.</li>
<li>2-3 years of related experience deploying computer hardware and equipment in server room or co-location facilities; installing and configuring software applications; and testing or quality control of project implementations.</li>
<li>A minimum of 2-4 years desktop PC/laptop support, technology support and/or Help desk experience.</li>
<li>Proficiency using Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, PC hardware and printers.</li>
<li>Possess excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Very strong system and performance troubleshooting experience.</li>
<li>Must have superior customer service skills and experience and able to interact with customers regarding contract requirements and support issues.</li>
</ul>
