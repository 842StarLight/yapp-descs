<h2>North America Channel Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>As a Regional Channel Director at Moogsoft, you will be responsible for developing a strategy to generate incremental pipeline, increase sales productivity, and close business leveraging value added resellers across North America. You will be sales expert for your partners’ journey into the world of Moogsoft AIOps. You will be at the forefront of a next generation approach to IT Operations and Analytics driven by real-time machine learning. In this role you will guide partners through successfully qualifying and navigating Moogsoft AIOps opportunities with real world issues faced by IT Operations undergoing digital transformations.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Moogsoft is the creator of Moogsoft AIOps - a next generation approach to IT Operations and Analytics driven by real-time machine learning. Moogsoft AIOps helps Enterprises &amp; Service Providers deliver consistently excellent customer experiences, regardless of the underlying complexity or dynamic nature of the supporting IT infrastructure and clouds.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Regional Channel Director creates, maintains, and expands relationships with target channel partners across North America. The Regional Channel Director will work with field sales and partners to identify new business sales opportunities, cross-selling opportunities and assist in building revenue-focused partner ecosystem and channel sales organization. The Regional Channel Director will add value in building a world class channel program to enable Moogsoft to achieve its strategic and revenue goals.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We’re looking for committed, high energy candidates who are looking to make a big impact and who want to work on a great team. The perfect candidate will thrive on challenges and will drive programs through excellent execution and tenacious follow up. The Regional Channel Director will be an evangelist of new technologies such as AIOps to channel partners and customers. The right person will make a significant contribution to the Company’s success.</div>
</div>
