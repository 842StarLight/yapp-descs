<h2>Sales Performance Events Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Mission
<br><br>
As a member of the Sales Performance team, you will help organize, plan, and execute best-in-class events for a global sales organization. We not only enable the field, we create and sustain a high performance culture in sales through enablement, reporting and analytics, content creation, as well as company events. The Sales Performance Events Specialist will contribute to the team by organizing, planning, and owning internal sales events including New Hire Trainings, QBRs, sales meetings, and partner enablement events.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate has a background with event planning, enterprise sales, and marketing. The Events Specialist will work with outside vendors and collaborate internally with teams to host effective and engaging events.
<br><br>
Outcomes
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Plan cost effective events working with hotels, restaurants, and other outside vendors</li>
<li>Support Marketing Events Team on events as needed (SKO, Tech Summit, etc.)</li>
<li>Organize, and coordinate the sales events calendar within Sales and with other groups</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with cross functional groups like Marketing, Sales Management, and Sales Operations</li>
<li>Manage and administer the monthly cadence of events, trainings, QBRs, etc.</li>
<li>Work within Events Team standards (budget, negotiation, legal, etc.)</li>
</ul>
Your success is measured by the overall business impact of your ability to create effective events, collaborate across, and support field teams to reach sales goals.
<br><br>
Competencies
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Project management and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Interpersonal and strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Experience working in events cross-functionally with various departments</li>
<li>Ability to negotiate favorable prices for hotels, restaurants, etc.</li>
<li>Excellent presentation skills (oral and written), as well as ability to motivate, teach, empower, and inspire</li>
</ul>
Benefits
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Medical, dental, vision</li>
<li>401k Retirement Plan</li>
<li>Unlimited Paid Time Off</li>
<li>Catered lunch (everyday), snacks, and drinks</li>
<li>Gym reimbursement</li>
<li>Employee referral bonus program</li>
<li>Awesome coworkers</li>
<li>Maternity and paternity plans</li>
</ul>
