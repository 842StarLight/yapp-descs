<p>Job Description</p>
<p>Full-time or Part- time Jewelry, Gold, Diamond, Watch, Coin &amp; Silver Buyer. We are looking for a self motivated enthusiastic individual who works well in a professional environment and is able to work independently from direct supervision. Looking for courteous, friendly, honest, trustworthy and outgoing individuals with sales experience. Applicants must be able to think on their feet, adapt to new situations, and grow along side the company.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<p>Responsibilities - Total daily operations of store including but not limited to: Opening/Closing of store, processing transactions with the public (purchasing, ect...), providing excellent customer service, punctual, jewelry sales, cash handling, inventory management, and properly maintaining store appearance. This position offers compensation for commission as well as a competitive salary depending on experience. Employment Testing/Assessment, Drug Screen and Criminal Background Check Required *Must be able to provide current drivers license and US citizen.</p>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<p>Preferred Experience: Office Management Sales &amp; Purchasing Advertising Cash Handling Customer Service Knowledge in Jewelry &amp; Watches GIA Certification Diamond Knowledge Watch Repair Jewelry Polishing Ebay Sales Bilingual Skills: Communication skills Microsoft office MAC proficient Secretarial skills Education: High School Diploma or Higher Education GIA Graduate Gemologist Diamond Certified Business Management *Must be able to provide current drivers license. This will be a full or part time position 3-5 days a week depending on full or part time shift. Store hours are Monday-Friday 10am-6pm &amp; Saturday 10am-3pm. Closed major holidays. Compensation based on experience. Bonus given based on performance and sales. Will train the right individual.*</p>
