<div><p><b>About the Team</b>

<div><p>You will be part of the Enterprise Support and Reliability Engineering - eCommerce Support group, supporting our mission of providing frictionless, always-on eCommerce services to the public, as well as supporting custom business workflow tooling. <div><p><b>About the Role:</b>
</p></div>
<div><p>As a Team Lead, Site Reliability Engineer at Williams-Sonoma you will support eCommerce production operations on the eCommerce Support team as an engineer who serves as an example to their team on how to solve problems at root cause. You already enjoy solving problems with code, which is why you're a balanced engineer, at home both in application code written by others, as well as designing your own app from scratch. Your documentation and communication skills are above par and you have prior experience working in a production support capacity as a full-stack, Site Reliability, Production Operations, or similar engineer.
</p></div>
<div><p>Your primary focus will be on the resilience, operability, stability, and scalability of the Williams-Sonoma family of brands' eCommerce websites and to ensure that the websites maintain optimal availability for our users. You will collaborate with a full-stack team of peers to monitor, detect, and investigate site behaviors, correct and enhance code already in production, deliver features, and facilitate more efficient software business process.
</p></div>
