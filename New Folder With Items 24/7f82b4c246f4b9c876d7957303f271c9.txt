<div>
The Global Senior Financial Analyst is responsible for assisting the Director of FP&amp;A in supporting Executive Management with timely and effective financial analysis and reporting.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Monthly consolidation, analysis, and publication of regional financial results and forecasts, including internal and external reporting
</div></li>
<li>
<div>Coordinate and prepare monthly, quarterly, and yearly presentations / exhibits for reviews with Business Unit Executive Management</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Prepare product line and business segment profitability reporting and analysis</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Work and coordinate with site finance personnel to understand financial results and variances</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Analyze P&amp;L, working capital, and balance sheet positions</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Special projects and ad-hoc analysis on an as needed basis</div>
</li>
<li><div>Provide on-the-job training to less experienced Analysts</div></li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>
Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Bachelor’s degree in Accounting or Finance required</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>3 to 5 year financial reporting experience</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>CPA and/or MA Degree, preferred</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>HFM experience preferred</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Mastery of systems, analytical tools, and data management: Demonstrates a mastery usage of analytical software (excel, HFM, Hyperion Planning, etc.) for descriptive modeling of performance drivers. In addition, he/she encourages proper use of the HFM global data warehouse to enable integrated, enterprise-wide planning and performance management. Capable of designing reports and analysis business managers will use.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Insatiable desire to solve business puzzles: Possesses a powerful, innate need to discover and illuminate the underlying drivers of and obstacles to performance. Exhibits strong problem-solving skills.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Excellent in Excel and Power Point proficiency</div>
</li>
<li><div>Outstanding oral and written communication skills; comfortable with interacting/presenting to all levels in organization</div></li>
</ul>
