<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Provides overall strategy and direction of the SMG Technology Organization and platform to ensure the effective, efficient and secure operation of all automated data processing systems.
</li>
<li>Ensures appropriate processes, structures, systems, and people’s competencies required to run the Technology organization for a business-to-business technology and services insights provider.
</li>
<li>Aligns strategy, services, investment decisions and delivery structures and processes with the strategic direction of the enterprise.</li>
<li>
Ensures services meet the needs of the clients, and takes responsibility for improving processes that are negatively affecting customer satisfaction and relationships.
</li>
<li>Oversees the development of corporate standards, technology architecture, technology evaluation and transfer.
</li>
<li>Identifies, develops, implements and maintains security-related processes that reduce the organization's operational risks.
</li>
<li>Partners closely with Product Management, Data Science, Client Insights, Marketing and Sales to develop strategically-focused, high-quality technology solutions clients want to buy.
</li>
<li>Develops, monitors, and manages the department budgets. Closely monitors performance and develops strategies for future technologies and programs to accommodate business needs.
</li>
<li>Coordinates with external entities to design, develop, and implement new products.
</li>
<li>Ensures the technology leadership team selects and develops strong teams of people responsible for developing and delivering solutions for the business and customers.
</li>
<li>Fosters staff communication and collaboration with various departments across the company.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>This position reports to the Chief Operating Officer
</li></ul>
</div></div></div>
