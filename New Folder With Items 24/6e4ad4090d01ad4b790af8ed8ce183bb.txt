<h2>Manager of Customer Success - Duo Security</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Duo Security, now a part of Cisco, is the leading provider of Trusted Access security and multi-factor authentication delivered through the cloud.</div>
<div>
Duo’s mission is to make security simple for everyone. We were born from a hacker ethos and a desire to make the Internet a secure place. We believe in empowering people to follow their passions inside and outside of the office and enable every employee to bring their whole self to work.</div>
<div>
Our team is our secret weapon. We run the spectrum from artists to analysts, low-key to high energy, and bring together a diversity of skill sets, experiences, and perspectives to solve what we consider to be the world's most pressing geopolitical challenge — transforming the security industry as we know it. Together we build solutions that are easy, effective, trustworthy, and enduring. And that’s why we are the most loved and trusted company in security.</div>
<p>Customer Success advocates for and provides support to Duo users by delivering superior experiences that are timely, accurate and professional. Their efforts have culminated in a Net Promoter Score of 68, placing Duo well beyond the industry standard and in the company of some of the most popular brands in tech.</p>
<p><b>As a Manager of Customer Success you will...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be passionate about customer advocacy and will play a critical role in ensuring that our customers have the best possible experience with Duo.</li>
<li>Direct a team of CSMs to build trust with our customers, and further solidify Duo’s standing as the most loved company in security.</li>
<li>Cultivate positive working relationships in the Sales organization as well as with other department leaders to align activity, support customer retention and adoption, and enable account growth.</li>
<li>As a leader, you are emotionally intelligent and have compassion for our customers, your team and partners in the organization.</li>
<li>Help to create outstanding customer engagement; monitor key performance indicators and ensure delivery against targets</li>
<li>Act as a trusted advisor and support escalation point internally and with Duo’s customers.</li>
<li>Work with peers in Customer Success leadership to continually improve processes and align on department and company goals.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills you have…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years experience in building and leading highly successful customer-facing teams.</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in a customer-facing role such as customer success, account management or strategic consulting. SaaS experience a plus.</li>
<li>Self-motivated, dedicated teammate with innovative ideas to inspire customer loyalty and adoption.</li>
<li>Strong social skills and experience building healthy internal and external relationships. Able to project a professional image and credibility.</li>
<li>Track record of success in collaborating with Sales to drive account expansion.</li>
<li>Team-oriented, versatile leadership qualities to guide a team in achieving success metrics and building potential career growth.</li>
<li>Familiarity with IT security concepts and industry trends.</li>
<li>Relevant Bachelor’s degree; strong preference for computer science, information security or related degree.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>3 Reasons why you should apply…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You are passionate about customer advocacy and are exhilarated by helping others to succeed</li>
<li>You are energized by the rapid pace of a start-up in growth mode and the change that can occur quickly</li>
<li>You thrive as part of a team, always assume positive intent and are an eager participant</li>
</ul>
