<h2>Associate Marketing Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Summary
</b></p>As the Associate Marketing Account Manager, you will provide day-to-day marketing planning, development and execution for a variety of clients, while building and maintaining relationships with our internal program and marketing teams, to help us continue our growth as a nimble, swift and impactful marketing agency.
<br>
Reports to: Marketing Account Manager or Sr. Marketing Account Manager
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>Associate Marketing Account Managers support our marketing account management teams in all aspects of client marketing, including:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement client marketing plans
</li>
<li>Draft creative briefs for new creative projects
</li>
<li>Work with creative Resource Manager and Producers to forecast and manage creative projects
</li>
<li>Manage production and execution of client marketing activities, including, but not limited to campaigns, sales enablement tools, events, web updates, email campaigns and social media content
</li>
<li>Prepare presentations for internal team and client meetings
</li>
<li>Draft monthly client reports
</li>
<li>Manage client &amp; internal budgets, forecasting, invoicing, and reporting
</li>
<li>Tracking of AP invoices &amp; creating of AR invoices
</li>
<li>Maintain strong working relationships with internal teams and clients
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Consummate collaborator and team player
</li>
<li>Active listener and problem solver
</li>
<li>Strong and succinct written and verbal communicator
</li>
<li>Born multitasker who’s deadline driven
</li>
<li>Highly organized and process-oriented with strong attention to detail
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and solve problems creatively
</li>
<li>Thrives through change
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>Hard-working and determined
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in marketing, advertising or communications preferred
</li>
<li>One to three years of in-house marketing and/or agency experience
</li>
<li>Intermediate level experience in PowerPoint, Excel and Word
</li>
</ul>
