<h2>Process Engineer Manager 3 - Human Resources - Business Process Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>
<p>
Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p><b>
Human Resources</b> develops and implements people strategies to support Wells Fargo’s business objectives. This group manages compensation and benefits, human resource service centers, systems and payroll, finance, team member relations and assistance, talent management, learning and development, and recruiting. We work together as One HR to make Wells Fargo an employer of choice. Our operating foundation is based on our ability to operate efficiently, develop HR team members, strengthen controls, and scale technology infrastructure.</p>
<p>
We believe that Process Excellence is made up of elements of leadership, teamwork, methods, and practices. We believe it is foundational to the repeatability of any operation, service, function or capability we utilize in the bank. When learned and embedded into a business culture, a business can transform many key attributes including quality, cost, risk and productivity. Ultimately, we want to create an environment that maximizes the talent of our team members and our knowledge of Process Excellence so that we can deliver world class solutions and services to our customer base.</p>
<p>
We are seeking an experienced professional to join us to help develop and deliver a world class Business Process Management (BPM) capability to the Wells Fargo Human Resources group. This role will have the unique opportunity to shape Human Resources Business Process Management function and contribute to Wells Fargo's goal of achieving process-excellence, a top transformational change effort. A key partner to HR's senior leaders, this role will manage a team of process engineers for the purpose of improving/optimizing HR's process performance and creating a compelling experience for Wells Fargo's team members. This role will report to the Functional Leader of the HR Group Business Process Management Team.</p>
<p><b>
Key responsibilities include:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage individuals and matrixed teams focused on process engineering initiatives, including developing business processes models</li>
<li>Evaluates team members work to determine gaps, risks and opportunities in order to prioritize design efforts and achieve maximum efficiency for the company</li>
<li>Provides clear and concise direction and coaching of direct reports and matrixed staff</li>
<li>Responsible for the successful execution of the process definition, measurement and improvement framework and methodology</li>
<li>Provide leadership on the development and advancement of our common enterprise Operational and Process Excellence framework, methods, standards, techniques, tools, training and language</li>
<li>Drive business awareness of our common enterprise Operational and Process Excellence methods, techniques, tools, etc.
</li>
<li>Provides consultation on the use of process definition measurement and improvement techniques and tools to increase efficiency and effectiveness</li>
<li>Provide coaching and mentoring to sprint teams and LOB leadership in process definition standards, processes, tools and techniques</li>
<li>Lead collaboration and partnering opportunities with other enterprise programs or initiatives to help advance the goals of those efforts, while advancing the culture of operational excellence</li>
<li>Develop and maintain the process engineering team into a pool of highly skilled expert resources for the businesses to utilize</li>
<li>Provide thought leadership in the areas of process definition, process management and process improvement</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring Enterprise process modeling standards and quality assurance processes are followed</li>
</ul>
<p>
As a <b>Team Member Manager</b>, you are expected to achieve success by leading yourself, your team, and the business. Specifically you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead your team with integrity and create an environment where your team members feel included, valued, and supported to do work that energizes them.</li>
<li>Accomplish management responsibilities which include sourcing and hiring talented team members, providing ongoing coaching and feedback, recognizing and developing team members, identifying and managing risks, and completing daily management tasks.</li>
</ul>
