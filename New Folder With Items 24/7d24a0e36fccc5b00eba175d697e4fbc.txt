<h2>Assistant Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
This position works closely with the Branch Manager to attain branch/bank established goals. Manages a team responsible for exceptional customer service standards along with operational soundness and generating revenue for the branch/bank.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Acts as back up to personal banking team to open new accounts, receive loan requests and gather credit-related information</li>
<li>Ensures the branch is operationally sound</li>
<li>Ensures that the tellers are trained and competent in explaining all products and services offered</li>
<li>Monitors sales and service goals</li>
<li>Processes and oversees technical tasks such as opening and closing of the branch, overdraft monitoring, wire transfers and collections, stop payments and monitoring branch controls</li>
<li>Routinely and consistently provides guidance and training to branch team</li>
<li>Resolves conflicts with minimal help</li>
<li>May back up new accounts</li>
<li>May back up teller line</li>
<li>Ability to pass personal banking training</li>
<li>Responsible for satisfactory audits</li>
<li>Actively contributes to employee management and training</li>
<li>Services complex accounts often</li>
<li>Actively coaches and mentors team to deepen customer relations</li>
<li>Works with Branch Manager to identify business development opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MINIMUM JOB REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma or GED</li>
<li>2+ years retail banking experience</li>
<li>Bilingual preferred</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree preferred</li>
<li>Some management experience</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office suite and any banking software used in the branch</li>
<li>Training and mentoring skills</li>
<li>Customer focused service and sales skills</li>
<li>Understands consumer and small business lending</li>
<li>Overall knowledge of bank operations</li>
<li>Previous Teller experience</li>
<li>Knowledge of policies and procedures</li>
</ul>
<p><b>OUR CORE BEHAVIORS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Be Genuine</b>. Conduct yourself in a consistent, honest and compassionate manner at all times</li>
<li>
<b>Work Ethic</b>. Be willing to go the extra mile for the customer, whether internal or external</li>
<li>
<b>Influence</b>. Be an active player in participating, building and contributing to service</li>
<li>
<b>Continuous Learning</b>. Commit to gaining knowledge, skills and experience on an ongoing basis in order to better serve the customer, increase personal satisfaction and improve Columbia Bank</li>
<li>
<b>Team Play</b>. We win together. Individuality is encouraged to advance and enrich the work of the team</li>
<li>
<b>Problem Solving</b>. Bring creative, problem-solving mentality to every situation. See alternatives, take initiative and assume responsibility for your actions</li>
<li>
<b>Communication</b>. Keep others informed and up-to-date. Actively listen and learn from each other</li>
<li>
<b>Have a sense of community: </b>We firmly believe that we're only as strong as the communities we serve. Your involvement is an important part of who we are.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL AND MENTAL REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to sit at a computer monitor for extended periods of time</li>
<li>Ability to perform repetitive finger, hand, and arm movements</li>
<li>Ability to lift up to 15lbs</li>
<li>Ability to effectively discern information and formulate appropriate action</li>
<li>Ability to reach, squat, bend, and manually manipulate standard office equipment</li>
<li>Ability to drive to various locations</li>
<li>Ability to think critically and provide appropriate solutions</li>
</ul>
