<h2>Part Time Customer Experience Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>At eyebobs, we are determined to live out loud and break through all barriers to help our customers frame their personalities and empower them to be their true and authentic selves. We see our Customer Experience Associates as the most critical tool in achieving this mission which is ultimately realized by providing world-class communication to and from our customers.</p>
<p>
As a CEA you will be the voice of eyebobs, providing outstanding service to our customers through every channel in which we interact with them. You will significantly impact eyebobs’ brand by making every customer interaction a delightful one. We are looking for a strong communicator – someone who is confident and comes across on the phone, in email, and on chat with genuine warmth and personality. When confronted with an issue or a frustrated customer, you listen carefully to understand the problem and are creative and resourceful in finding the best and fastest solution.</p>
<p>
Being the main point of contact for customers, our Customer Experience Associates are essential team members at eyebobs. Responsibilities and daily tasks include:</p>
<p><b>
Core Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Expert handling of incoming and outgoing customer care calls, emails, chats, and escalations</li>
<li>Processing sales transactions over the phone, tracking orders and responding to customers within SLA requirements</li>
<li>Multi-tasking using various in-house and off the shelf software programs (i.e Netsuite, Slack, etc.)</li>
<li>Offering value-added solutions and promote customer loyalty by providing exceptional customer support</li>
<li>Updating internal documents and customer records daily</li>
<li>Collaborating with our QA, Product and Marketing teams to solve customer concerns</li>
<li>Working closely with our Logistics and Website developers to troubleshoot issues</li>
<li>Providing feedback on potential issues and follow up processes</li>
<li>Additional project work and assigned tasks when required</li>
<li>Gaining an in-depth knowledge of eyebobs products</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 1 -2 years of Customer service experience preferred plus an ability to think quickly on one's feet</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills - you excel at being concise, clear and you read your audience to determine the appropriate tone to use</li>
<li>Proven problem-solving skills - our customers appreciate efficiency and speedy resolutions</li>
<li>Ability to remain calm and de-escalate potentially contentious situations</li>
<li>Keen attention to detail and strong time management skills</li>
<li>A professional, positive, take-charge attitude</li>
<li>A passion for continuously improving</li>
<li>A team player attitude, also capable of working independently</li>
<li>Proficiency using Windows operating system, basic knowledge of Word, Excel and G-suite</li>
<li>Some flexibility required- Current hours are 8am - 6pm Monday through Friday and 8am – 4pm Saturday</li>
<li>High School Diploma</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits:</b></p>
<p>
We want eyebobs to be the best place to work in the Twin Cities, and we do our part to take care of our team. In addition to a competitive salary, we provide:</p>
<ul>
<li>You’ll get all the tools you need to get your job done well.</li>
<li>We believe in the power of a great office. Eyebobs headquarters comes stacked with complimentary snacks and beverages, comfy lounge spaces for breaks and a beautiful retail store…psst, the discount is fabulous!</li>
<li>Bring your dog to work days!</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About eyebobs:</b></p>
<p>
At eyebobs, we believe the right pair of glasses can change your life. Your personality comes through your eyes. Every day you express who you are. Sometimes loudly, boldly and sometimes more subtly but when you wake up in the morning, we believe that the most important part of your wardrobe starts with our glasses. No matter who you are, where you are from or what you want to say, we are building eyebobs to help you be your true and authentic self. Our frames are available through three channels: direct to consumer, our eyebobs retail stores and from our retail partners through our wholesale business.</p>
