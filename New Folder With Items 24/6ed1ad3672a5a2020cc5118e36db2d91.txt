<div>
<p><b>Social Media Associate</b></p>
<p>
Tough Mudder’s mission is to grow a global tribe that lives courage, personal accomplishment, teamwork, and fun. From day one, we’ve built a culture to reflect that philosophy, bringing on quirky people who are great at what they do and passionate about our events and customers.</p>
<p>
Our ideal candidate wants more than a few lines on their CV – they want to help build a company that changes the lives of millions of people for the better. This role will give you the opportunity to push your boundaries and find out what you’re capable of, to work with and learn from incredibly smart people, and, most of all, to enjoy the journey of helping to market a world-class organization.</p>
<p>
Sound like something you can get on board with?</p>
<p><b>
The Job:</b></p>
<p>
Reporting to the Senior Social &amp; Content Marketing Manager, the Social Media Associate will be responsible for developing and executing content strategy on Tough Mudder’s social media channels. This individual must be an analytical and strategic thinker who is able to manage complex projects and deliverables while staying informed on new social media tools, trends, and channels. This person must have experience writing copy for social media, show previous success in managing social media channels, and a deep understanding of the Tough Mudder community. Some domestic travel and weekend work required.</p>
<p><b>
Develop and Execute Social Media Strategy</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop social media editorial strategy across Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat, Instagram Stories,Youtube and Pinterest</li>
<li>
Execute scheduling and publishing social content across channels</li>
<li>
Create engaging social media content that aligns with overall campaign strategy in order to enhance brand awareness and community</li>
<li>
Responsible for weekly and monthly reporting, analysis of KPI’s and developing strategic recommendations to optimize content and social strategy</li>
<li>
Formulate and execute plan for repurposing user-generated-content</li>
<li>
Run tests to ensure effectiveness of social media strategy</li>
<li>
Contribute in depth knowledge of emerging social media platforms</li>
<li>
Develop strategy for channel growth via content optimization and cross promotion of platforms - Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, Pinterest, Twitter + Blog</li>
<li>
Collaborate with international teams, serving as key 'global expert' in social media</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Leverage social channels to drive promotions and sales activities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Determine most effective means by which to promote ticket sales, additional event revenue content, merchandise sales</li>
<li>
Conduct a/b testing to confirm most effective channels for business activations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Deliver Effective Community Management on all Social Channels</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Interact with community on a daily basis across all channels</li>
<li>
Collaborate with brand ambassadors and influencers to maximize content distribution</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Plan and Deliver Event by Event Social Media Plan</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work closely with live events team to deliver engaging, up to date content for each event- pre, during, and post</li>
<li>
Develop clear strategy for community growth and engagement surrounding each event location and event format - Tough Mudder Classic, 5K, Tougher, Toughest, and World’s Toughest Mudder</li>
<li>
Deliver on site live coverage across social media platforms at 5-8 events per season</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The Traits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong interest in consuming content across social channels and interest in staying on top of what’s trending</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal/written communication; strong writing and copy-editing skills a must</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to successfully manage social media channels and communities</li>
<li>
Entrepreneurial spirit who can initiate projects and implement changes to optimize content across all channels</li>
<li>
Organized, attentive to detail, and able to prioritize well</li>
<li>
Strategic thinker and efficient executor</li>
<li>
Passion for outdoor/athletic events and a strong interest in travel</li>
<li>
Experience with Adobe Photoshop and/or video editing software is a plu</li>
<li>
Invested in Tough Mudder values of Pride, Accountability, Continuous Improvement and Teamwork</li>
<li>
Intuitive feel for the Tough Mudder brand and mission</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3 - 4 years of relevant social media marketing experience across a variety of channels. Experiential marketing a plus.</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of social media marketing landscape (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube best practices)</li>
<li>
Proven track record of success in producing engaging and innovative digital content in sports/events</li>
</ul>
