<br><b>Summary:</b> ? Responsible for the successful day-to-day management of partner marketing activities across Cognizant’s eight Strategic Enterprise partnerships to help drive co-marketing efforts that align to each partnership’s Go-To-Market objectives. ? Collaborate with SPG’s Alliance leaders and Field Marketing organization to understand market challenges and opportunities and develop partner-marketing programs that build awareness and drive measurable results. ? Work hand in hand with Field Marketing to oversee the development and delivery of key partner events and campaigns that increase brand awareness and generate Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs). ? Nurture close relationships with BU stakeholders, Field Marketing, Corporate Marketing, and the Cognizant Growth Community to align strategic partner marketing strategy and programs to corporate brand positioning and the Cognizant way to market ? Track the effectiveness and ROI of partner marketing programs leveraging marketing automation (Pardot), Salesforce tools and social/digital platforms ? Own internal partner communications for SPG; key channels include bi-monthly newsletter, monthly partner briefing series, winwires, partner portal and internal social platforms such as Chatter. Continuously improve the way we market ourselves internally by bringing forward innovative ideas that will enhance our existing programs.
<br><b>General Responsibilities:</b> ? Develop, drive and manage day-to-day execution of partner marketing plans by collaborating with Alliances Managers, Partner Marketing organizations, and other key stakeholders across the business. ? Manage overall SPG investment spend against budget and report on partner marketing performance by tracking Pardot metrics for each partner marketing activity. ? Create and execute partner-marketing plans for key alliances in portfolio focused on generating awareness and marketing qualified leads (MQLs) for SPG/partner-led marketing programs with the support of Cognizant Field Marketing. ? Support Field Marketing led partner-marketing programs in collaboration with Cognizant Field Marketing and other Cognizant shared services marketing functions. ? Collaborate with key stakeholders, including BU, industry, sales leadership, Corporate Marketing (thought leadership, advisory, AR, PR, social media, website teams), Field Marketing, and Digital Business peers, to formulate plans and execute programs. ? Work effectively with agencies, contractors, and in-sourced and offshore resources, as needed, to ensure delivery of consistent deliverables aligned with Cognizant standards ? Lead the development of joint partner messaging, collateral and sales enablement assets while managing content for Cognizant and Partner websites as well as internal and external partner communications. Content Management: ? Re-launch and maintain an internal winwire program to showcase key partner wins across NA, UK, Europe and APAC; ensure wins are being shared through all appropriate channels on both sides (Cognizant and partner). ? Demonstrate familiarity and experience with social media platforms such as LinkedIn and Twitter to support key partner events and programs; ability to report back on metrics and performance of key posts and announcements shared. ? Develop joint partner messaging and collateral for market as well as sales enablement assets and tools for field. Sponsored &amp; Hosted Events: ? Manage and execute partner events including executive roundtables, webinars, sponsored events and conferences. ? Play a key role in developing and delivering pre, during, and post event partner marketing programs that engage and influence footfall at events. Metrics, Data &amp; Insights: ? Track the effectiveness and ROI of marketing programs leveraging marketing automation (Pardot), Salesforce tools and social/digital platforms. ? Adept a measuring success and impact within pre-defined KPIs and ability to suggest new measurements of success ? Ability to report on all partner related marketing programs and campaigns through metrics captured in Pardot; provide recommendations based on findings.
<br><b>Required Experience:</b> ? 10+ years of experience and proven success in partner marketing in IT and track record of establishing new and innovative ways to market ? Prior experience developing and executing partner-marketing programs leveraging email, internet, social media, event and direct marketing channels ? Excellent project management skills and proven ability to lead cross-functional, cross-regional teams to complete objectives on time and within budget ? Previous experience managing multiple stakeholders in a highly matrixed model ? Strong written and verbal communication skills, including at executive level ? Ability to align tactical execution with strategic vision ? Previous experience with Accenture, Deloitte, IBM, TCS, or similarly focused technology services or consulting firms ? B.A. or B.S. degree (MBA bonus)
<br><b>Key Traits and Competencies:</b> ? Self-driven, high-capacity manager, who possesses the ability to flourish in a startup environment and the diplomacy and navigational skills needed in a large, Fortune 195 organization. ? Innovative, out of the box thinker who can demonstrate creativity in employing content and tools to drive brand activation. ? Data driven, able to track and report on measurable activity directly connected to marketing and promotions. Must be comfortable to employ analytics to understand and tune strategy and tactics. ? Mission driven with a sense of urgency: take ownership of your role in the company’s overall success. ? Thoughtful, with a strategic acumen, creative thinking and detailed analytical problem solving skills. ? Collaborative with an adaptable style and open and inclusive approach to drawing out other’s viewpoints. ? Excellent work ethic, able to actively demonstrate commitment to the company’s objectives. ? An outstanding relationship builder, adept at influencing others at all levels of the organization. ? Able to drive end-to-end marketing activities from planning to execution with a high degree of autonomy. ? Determined, resilient, confident, versatile and unpretentious, with a sense of humor. ? Able to organize and manage information and time effectively, have a process orientation and attention to detail.
