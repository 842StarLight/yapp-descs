<h2>Receptionist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Broad Function: Answer the telephones in a courteous and friendly manner. Greet visitors to the facility. Create invoices from sales orders when the product ships. Entering accounts payable invoices.</div>
<div>Hours 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM, Mon - Fri.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Principal Duties and Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Answer the telephones in a courteous and friendly manner.</li>
<li>Greet visitors ensuring a positive first impression for potential/current customers.</li>
<li>Maintain visitor/temporary badge log ensuring ITAR compliance at all times.</li>
<li>Maintain the conference room schedules.</li>
<li>Invoice sales orders from the daily ship list. Verify traveler packets for shipment accuracy. Compare shipped quantities, ship method, serial numbers, etc. on both the traveler and the chain of custody.</li>
<li>Verification of all sales orders before shipment. Compare purchase order requirements against the sales order for accuracy.</li>
<li>Maintain an invoice/shipment spreadsheet and communicate daily shipment numbers.</li>
<li>Attach invoices to check stubs once payment is received and has been posted. Verify amounts and record differences.</li>
<li>Daily input of accounts payable invoices. Verify invoices for accuracy and schedule payments.</li>
<li>Maintain Usage Tax spreadsheet on a monthly basis.</li>
<li>Open and sort mail.</li>
<li>Filing.</li>
<li>Ordering lunches as needed.</li>
<li>Other work as assigned by Finance and HR staff.</li>
</ul>
<div>Job Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge and ability to use computer and office equipment.</li>
<li>Ability to use industry specific software with limited training.</li>
<li>Ability to answer multi-line phone system.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and complete daily projects.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<div>Required Education: High School Degree</div>
<div>Required Work Experience: 0-2 Years Relevant Experience</div>
<div>Previous accounts payable experience. Minimum of 1 year experience in a receptionist role.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>Must be eligible to work on defense and satellite programs subject to ITAR Regulations. Therefore, candidates must be a U.S. Citizen or a U.S. Permanent Resident ('Green Card').</i></b></div>
<div>Must comply with TTM Export Control Policies and Procedures and all applicable laws including ITAR, EAR and OFAC including but not limited to: a) being able to identify ITAR product on the manufacturing floor and understand that access to these products and related technical data is restricted to only US Citizens and US Permanent Residents; b) recognition of Foreign Person visitors by badge differentiation; c) understand and follow authorization procedures for bringing foreign visitors into facilities (VAL); d) understand the Export and ITAR requirements for shipments leaving the US; e) manage vendor approvals for ITAR manufacturing and services.</div>
