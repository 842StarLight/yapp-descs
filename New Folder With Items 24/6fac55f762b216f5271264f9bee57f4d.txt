<h2>Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Legacy Properties, </b>a high-end Apartment Community, is seeking a full-time experienced <b>Property Manager</b>. Must be willing to relocate to Northern Minnesota.</p>
<p>The Property Manager is responsible for overseeing the day to day operations and financial functions of the Community.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES include, but are not limited to: </b></p>
<p><i>Financial Management: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Posting all payments and income received from applicants, current residents, and non-current residents</li>
<li>Processing and reconciling daily all account receivables</li>
<li>Monitor bad debt and prepare late notices, contact Residents and Guarantors and file evictions</li>
<li>Responsible for posting of all charges to Resident Ledgers, including bill backs and misc. fines when applicable</li>
<li>Responsible for coding and processing property related invoices</li>
<li>Responsible for end of month closing procedures and reporting</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and organized records, auditing Resident files, misc. tracking reports</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Personnel Management: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure effectiveness of staff through ongoing training, coaching, counseling, and guidance</li>
<li>Manage and lead existing team members on daily, weekly, monthly, annual staff responsibilities and goals</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Leasing Management: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure policies and procedures are followed for obtaining lease signing closure success, prospect follow up, signing/documentation of leases and report systems are accurate and up to date</li>
<li>Assist in Resident complaints, concerns and ensure Resident satisfaction is met.</li>
<li>Develop and implement creative Resident retention programs and monthly events (ex. Resident functions, special promotions, monthly newsletter, etc.)</li>
<li>Effectively show, lease and move-in prospective Residents</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Administrative Management: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Inspect and maintain property appearance and ensure repairs are noted and completed on a timely basis</li>
<li>Assist in and obtain competitive bid process when related to property maintenance contracts and resources.</li>
<li>Assist with marketing efforts, including but not limited to social media, general marketing collateral, and all ILS platforms.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills both written and verbal.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills.</li>
<li>Willingness to work non-traditional hours,including early evenings, weekends, and some holidays.</li>
<li>Experience in real estate leasing.</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Sales experience preferred</li>
<li>Property Management software knowledge preferred</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of Fair Housing Laws</li>
</ul>
<p><i><b>To apply, please submit your resume and references.</b></i></p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>property management: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
