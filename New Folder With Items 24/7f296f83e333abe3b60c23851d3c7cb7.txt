<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Description</b></h2>

<div>
<div><b>Company Description</b></div>
Construction Journal is a provider of construction project information delivered through the cloud. We introduced our first online service in 1997 and have since expanded our service offering to over 30 states. Our core mission is to help our customers increase their revenue by empowering them with actionable intelligence.
<br>
<br>
Our SaaS platform provides comprehensive and time sensitive information on construction projects and service contracts. Our service offering is intuitive and easy to use. We deliver our service offering via major internet browsers. We sell to businesses of all sizes that have an interest in construction project information on a subscription basis primarily through our direct sales efforts.
</div>
<div>
<div><b>Role Description</b></div>
We are seeking a skilled Research Associate to join our team. This position is suited for a strong purpose driven leader who strives to be part of a dynamic and ambitious team. The ideal candidate should have experience in customer service and strong communication skills. This is a fast-paced, multi-tasked position. If you like to be challenged and work independently then this could be the role for you.
<br>
<br>
As a member of our Research Associate team you will communicate with architects, engineers, and contractors by phone and internet to obtain timely and accurate information on a variety of new and existing construction projects. You will be part of an elite team of Research Associates that are focused on identifying, gathering, and analyzing construction project data.
<br>
<br>
Our goal is to build an organization of smart, ambitious Research Associates, committed to our mission and focused on winning, but are able to balance this with a respectful, healthy environment and lifestyle. At Construction Journal, integrity and reliability are as important as talent and effort.
</div>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Developing relationships with Construction Managers, architects, engineers to obtain notice of construction projects in conception, and planning phases.
</li>
<li>Analyzing information provided from public offices of zoning notices, site plan approvals, newspaper articles for potential construction projects being considered.
</li>
<li>Outbound calling to owners, architects, engineers and general contractors to obtain specific construction project information.
</li>
<li>Obtaining, plans, specifications, and bidders/plan holders list on out to bid projects.</li>
</ul>
</div>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>
