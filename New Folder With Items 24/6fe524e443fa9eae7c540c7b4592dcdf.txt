<p></p>
<p>Gate City Bank is now hiring for a <b>full-time Personal Loan Officer </b>position at our new <b>Sauk Rapids </b>office! This position is responsible for prospecting, establishing, and maintaining relationships with customers by providing personal lending product and service expertise to assist customers in the decision-making process, while staying within established lending limits and ensuring loan quality and deposit growth for the Bank.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Interviews customers to obtain financial history. Analyzes information and sells customers on the products and services that best meet their needs.
</li>
<li>Prepares personal lending loan applications completely, accurately and in compliance with Federal guidelines and the Bank’s policies and procedures.
</li>
<li>Approves loans within approval limits, and if appropriate, forwards higher values for approval.
</li>
<li>Investigates and closes all approved loans in accordance with Federal guidelines and the Bank’s policies and procedures.
</li>
<li>Consistently informs and sells customers on credit life and disability insurance and GAP insurance.
</li>
<li>Responds to customer inquiries regarding all aspects of personal loans.
</li>
<li>Maintains awareness of all current policies and procedures pertaining to personal loans and all interrelated services and functions.
</li>
<li>Meets established personal production goals and assists in the achievement of all the Bank’s annual sales goals.
</li>
<li>Expected to assist in securing liens on mortgages, titles and UCCs.
</li>
<li>Build, seize, and recognize relationship building opportunities with all areas of the bank, including but not limited to deposit, mortgage, business banking, financial planning, and insurance.
</li>
<li>Exhibit ‘Brilliant at the Basics’ customer service skills and the ability to develop and maintain productive relationships with colleagues, management, and clients.
</li>
<li>In order to provide expected levels of service to Gate City Bank customers, co-workers, and vendors, prompt and reliable attendance is an essential function of this position.
</li>
<li>Must comply with all Gate City Bank policies, procedures, applicable laws, and regulations.
</li>
<li>Must be detailed-oriented and maintain high degree of accuracy in processing transactions.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>COMPUTER AND EQUIPMENT SKILLS:
</b></p>
<p>Proficient working knowledge of PC for Windows, Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, and PowerPoint), Internet, Email and Gate City Bank software systems applicable to the position. Understanding of how to use the following equipment: computers, fax machines, copiers, scanners and printers.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:
