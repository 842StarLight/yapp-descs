<h2>Metro Area Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Metro Area Manager will manage Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge and Ram dealerships within multiple states surrounding one of our regional Business Centers. The Area Manager is responsible for maximizing retail sales, vehicle production, performance, revenue and achieving established targets in his/her district, composed of 10-15 higher volume dealers in a major market. This role will serve as a liaison between the dealers' and FCA to assist in all areas of retail development including forecasting retail sales objectives by vehicle line ensuring the dealer orders meet market demand and managing monthly vehicle allocation and scheduling to ensure maximum production capability within the district.
<br><br>
Additional responsibilities will include, but not limited to, reviewing minimum sales responsibility (MSR) and provide consultation on improvement opportunities, as well as reviewing and analyzing dealer financial statements and consult with dealers to improve their operations and retail business. The Manager will ensure that advertising and marketing initiatives are consistent with the corporate message and branding guidelines, as well as ensuring proper levels of sales satisfaction are present at all dealerships while identifying areas for improvement.
<br><br>
The Metro Area Manager will motivate and lead independent entrepreneurs to higher levels of performance consistent with marketing plans, merchandising, and financial statement reviews. Training dealership personnel on new products is another critical responsibility of the Metro Area Manager.
<br>The candidate will reside in the Baltimore, MD or Washington, DC area.
<br><br>
