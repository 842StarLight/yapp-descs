<p><br>
IndraSoft, Inc. is seeking a SAP Functional Business Analyst to join the Defense Logistics Agency’s Enterprise Business Systems program which provides systems integration, systems engineering and application management services to the agency’s enterprise systems. The IndraSoft team is currently supporting the integration and sustainment phase of the agency’s SAP-based modernization efforts to enhance supply chain operations. The DLA is responsible for providing food, fuel, medical supplies, clothing/textiles, construction and equipment to US service men and women in 126 nations. The agency averages 54,000 requisitions and 8,200 contracts per day and manages 520,000 shipments annually. The Enterprise Business Systems effort has enabled DLA to re-direct shipments in-flight, all within one system with real-time monitoring of the processes and transactions.</p>
