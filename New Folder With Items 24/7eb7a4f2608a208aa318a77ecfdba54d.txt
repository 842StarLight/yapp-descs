<h2>Area Vice President of Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>About Starry:</b><br>
Starry is reinventing the way people connect to the internet. Our mission focuses on two things. First, we're making the experience of accessing the internet simple, transparent, and delightful. Second, we're bringing that experience to underserved communities around the world. We approach our mission with a cutting-edge wireless technology, customer service designed to delight, and a culture of innovation and intellectual curiosity.
<br><br>
<b>Who we're looking for:</b><br>
The Area Vice President of Sales will lead a team of Real Estate focused sales professionals and Account Executives who are responsible for delivering sales growth for Starry's services in the NYC/NJ market. The ideal candidate will exceed company objectives by developing strategic sales plans that promote Starry's "Customer First" mission statement. You must be a strong leader, and will utilize your excellent analytical, critical thinking, and problem solving skills to effectively manage the sales teams operational cadence.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Partner with the General Manager to define the sales strategy and operational plan for the sales team</li>
<li>Develop enterprise account penetration strategies and successfully execute them</li>
<li>Build, develop, and manage a sales team capable of delivering our initiatives</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally and maintain clear communication across teams</li>
<li>Establish competitive Sales Commission programs</li>
<li>Collaborate on various Sales &amp; Marketing initiatives to enhance branding efforts</li>
</ul>
<b>Minimum Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing or related discipline, Master's or MBA preferred</li>
<li>10 years of experience in SaaS or Real Estate Tech sales, with at least 5 years experience leading a team</li>
<li>Experience launching new markets</li>
<li>Experience sourcing, structuring, communicating, and negotiating sales terms/agreements</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills; able to communicate effectively to varying audiences</li>
<li>Skilled in collaborating with legal partners and negotiating contracts</li>
<li>Ability to deal with the ambiguity typical of a new company</li>
<li>A willingness to thoughtfully push initiatives forward</li>
<li>An innate ability to balance early stage scrappiness with professionalism</li>
<li>Outstanding written and verbal communication, with great interpersonal skills</li>
<li>You have passionate feelings about Starry and our mission</li>
<li>Highly motivated; intellectually curious</li>
</ul>
We work hard, so we take care of each other and try to enjoy ourselves along the way. We have:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Premium medical, dental, and vision coverage with no employee contribution required</li>
<li>12 weeks of paid parental leave</li>
<li>Catered meals on a weekly basis</li>
<li>Groups for skiing, biking, running, climbing, stretching, shuffleboard, darts, and more</li>
</ul>
Happy Interneting!
