<h2>Private Dining Event Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Fort Restaurant is looking for a Restaurant Private Dining Sales Manager. This position is responsible for all restaurant private dining sales, coordination and execution as well as a key member of the restaurant leadership and dining room management team. The Restaurant Private Dining Sales Manager will be the active manager on duty in the restaurant 50% of the time and work as a sales manager selling, giving site visits to prospective groups, and detailing events 50% of the time.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS</b>: To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p>The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE</b>: Prior experience required. Depending on work experience, a degree in hospitality or restaurant management may be required, if there is less than three years’ experience. ServeSafe or equal training is a plus. CPR and First aid certified is a plus. AV knowledge using wireless microphone, lavalier, projector, screens, Sonos, Pandora for Business and more is a plus.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>LANGUAGE SKILLS:</b> Ability to read, write, and verbally communicate effectively and professionally with Executive and Sous Chef, other business departments, guests, and vendors. Ability to diplomatically deal with difficult situations and people, while exhibiting a consistent level of professionalism.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS</b>: While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to stand, walk in a fast pace environment during business hours and events.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Employee is required to use hands/fingers often at the computer and using the telephone. The employee must regularly lift and/or move up to 10-25 pounds and frequently lift and/or move up to 50 pounds. Often outside year round.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>COMPENSATION</b>: Base salary of $50,000 plus commission on each party completed, paid on the 5th and 20th of each month.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>HOURS- </b>minimum of 40 hours per week, normally Tuesdays through Saturdays 2-10pm or may be closing manager 2-3 nights per week. Schedule is based on needs of the restaurant and events along with scheduled site tours and tastings.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Fort is a dinner only restaurant open 4:30pm-8:00pm, unless there are private events in the daytime such as weddings, rehearsal dinners, and corporate events. This position will typically offer two consecutive days off per week.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>INTERNET/COMPUTER SOFTWARE SKILLS</b>: Must be Proficient in Microsoft Office and Microsoft Excel and a plus if you know Reserve, Aloha, Quickbooks and Eventbrite software.<br>
</p>
