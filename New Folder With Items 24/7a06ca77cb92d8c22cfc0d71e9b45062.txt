<h2>IT Service Desk Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>With our strong investment in research and development and our culture of continuous improvement, Hexcel is the industry leader in the manufacturing of composite materials and industrial fabrics. We invite you to join the Hexcel team at various manufacturing sites, sales offices and R&amp;T centers around the globe. Become a part of the “strength within.”
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Hexcel is currently seeking an <b>IT Service Desk Analyst </b>for the <b>Salt Lake City, UT, USA </b>location.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The selected individual will be responsible for but not limited to the following obligations:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Record and resolve Service Desk queries within SLA targets.
</li>
<li>Day to day monitoring and updating the Service Desk system, including escalation of tickets where necessary to Senior Analysts, other IT teams, and the IT Service Desk Manager.
</li>
<li>Set-up and installation of new PC hardware &amp; software, using departmental procedures and standards.
</li>
<li>Carrying out routine scheduled IT activities, such as server backups.
</li>
<li>Carrying out administration duties such as adding and removing network user ids and email accounts in line with departmental procedures and standards.
</li>
<li>Recording hardware faults and liaising with third-party maintenance companies to assess whether repairs meet economic justification.
</li>
<li>Undertaking routine hardware maintenance.
</li>
<li>Liaising with Service Desk personnel to ensure effective coverage of regional/local issues and effective response and handoff of priority incidents to staff members.
</li>
<li>Delivering IT technology training through individual meetings, online, or scheduled classroom training.
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently.
</li>
<li>Service Desk support duties as assigned.
</li>
<li>Periodically covering after-hours and on-call shifts
</li>
<li>Occasional travel to other offices when IT presence is required
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED required; Associate’s or Bachelor’s degrees preferred
</li>
<li>Minimum of 1- 5 years of IT support experience
</li>
<li>Knowledge of PC hardware technology
</li>
<li>Knowledge of maintenance and repair of PC hardware
</li>
<li>Knowledge of current Windows OS applications, servers and workstations
</li>
<li>Knowledge and working experience of current MS Office product Suite
</li>
<li>Knowledge of PC network security issues, including virus protection and prevention
</li>
<li>Personable and effective communication skills, able to impart instructions clearly and concisely
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate training and support needs
</li>
<li>Effective, logical problem solving abilities, capable of using initiative to provide a solution to recurring problems
</li>
<li>Must have the ability to work effectively as part of a team
</li>
<li>Spanish, French or German language proficiency is a plus
</li>
<li>Microsoft Server Manager Console experience preferred
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Eligible candidate must be: U.S. citizen, U.S. national, a person lawfully admitted for permanent residence, a temporary resident under sections 210(a) or 245(A) of the Act, a person admitted in refugee status, a person granted asylum. Hexcel (NYSE: HXL) is a global leader in advanced composites technology, a leading producer of carbon fiber, and the world leader in honeycomb manufacturing for the commercial aerospace industry.
</p>
