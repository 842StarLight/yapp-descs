<h2>Senior Account Executive (Cloud Security Sales)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Anitian is expanding and is adding experienced account executives to our Anitian Compliance Automation division. Anitian offers automated compliance solutions and is a Security AWS partner.</p>
<p>
We are currently searching for a new Senior Account Executive with strong connections to the New England area.</p>
<p>
Our ideal candidate will have an entrepreneurial spirit with expert skills at networking and building high-trust relationships. The income potential with this position is virtually unlimited. A background in SaaS or cloud sales is a must for consideration.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Learn the product and services Anitian offers and be able to discuss them in detail with prospective clients</li>
<li>
Network and build sustainable, high-value relationships with decision-makers</li>
<li>
Track all sales activity in our Salesforce CRM</li>
<li>
Execute the full sales cycle from prospecting to close</li>
<li>
While this is a largely a hunting role, the successful candidate may be required to manage existing accounts; namely those which are closed by you as the candidate</li>
<li>
Pro-actively network and sell to multiple contacts within an organization, including c-level executives</li>
<li>
Develop customer relationships by way of a consultative sales approach; an approach that provides for the highest level of service</li>
<li>
Must have a proven ability to ask appropriate open-ended questions to qualify and build trust with potential clients</li>
<li>
Present solutions and work with customers to tailor those solutions to their needs</li>
<li>
Must have a proven ability to overcome objections and differentiate Anitian Compliance Automation</li>
<li>
Must be able to participate in regional marketing events</li>
<li>
Network with peers by way of attending community and industry meetings to network with peers</li>
