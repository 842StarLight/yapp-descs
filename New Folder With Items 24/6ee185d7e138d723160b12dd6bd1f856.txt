<h2>Project Leader/AME - NPI</h2>

<b>Division Information</b><br>
<p>Hardware and Home Improvement (HHI) is a major manufacturer and supplier of residential locksets, residential builders' hardware, faucets, and commercial locksets and doors, with a portfolio of renowned brands, including Kwikset®, Weiser®, Baldwin®, National Hardware®, Stanley®, FANAL®, Pfister™, EZSET®, and Tell. HHI is a leader in its key markets with #1 positions in U.S. residential locksets (Kwikset), Canada residential locksets (Weiser), U.S. luxury locksets (Baldwin), and U.S. builders' hardware (Stanley-National Hardware), #2 in Mexico residential hardware (Fanal), #3 in U.S. retail plumbing (Pfister), and #1 in U.S. retail commercial locksets. Headquartered in Orange County, California, HHI has a global sales force and operates manufacturing and distribution facilities in the U.S., Canada, Mexico and Asia. HHI is a division of Spectrum Brands Holdings, Inc.</p>
<br>
<b>
Job Summary</b><br>
<p>Project lead new product introductions (NPI) and facilitate product innovations and changes from conception to launch. Provide leadership working with cross-functional team members with emphasis on meeting key metrics.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<b>
Primary Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>(100%) Project Leader – New Product Introductions
<ul>
<li>Oversee and lead projects with global cross functional team, working towards the common goal of introducing new or changing products with focus on meeting key metrics including but not limited to cost, quality and timing.</li>
<li>Support adherence to global new product roadmap, project milestone process and metrics.</li>
<li>Report out at monthly to Senior Management at Product Development Reviews.</li>
<li>Coordinate the activities of documentation, material planners, supply chain logistics and manufacturing operations with respect to assigned projects.</li>
<li>Manages/reports out on projects in all areas including funding, product costs, product quality, productivity, safety, ergonomic, environmental, space and timing limitations.</li>
<li>Utilize Microsoft Project or equivalent software to manage all phases of projects.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
