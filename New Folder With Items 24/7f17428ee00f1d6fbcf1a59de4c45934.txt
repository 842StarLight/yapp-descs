Caterpillar is the world's leading manufacturer of construction and mining equipment, diesel and natural gas engines, industrial gas turbines and diesel-electric locomotives. Our products have evolved from simple mechanical workhorses to sophisticated, electronically controlled work-site solutions. This transformation, along with our smart factories and our integrated dealer network, has provided a wealth of Big Data that is mined and analyzed across various segments such as product development, manufacturing, supply chain, marketing, sales and customer support.<br>

Come join a world class organization and play a key role in Caterpillar’s digital transformation.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p>
Cat Digital’s Analytics team is looking for a talented and motivated Sr. Data Scientist to develop and deliver analytics models to support product development for the enterprise as well as condition monitoring for customers. As a data scientist, you will use machine learning and other analysis techniques to integrate time-series machine sensor data and physics-based simulation models to improve design validation and on-board condition monitoring for machines operating in every corner of the globe.
</p>
<div>Typical problems include but are not limited to: creating reduced order or surrogate models of physics-based simulations to enable design exploration and uncertainty quantification; fusion of telematics data and simulation models to develop digital twins; statistical modeling for calibration and validation; sensitivity analysis and optimization.<br>
</div>
