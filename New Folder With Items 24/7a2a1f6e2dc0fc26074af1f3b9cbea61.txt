<h2>Warehouse Worker</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>The Warehouse Worker position will assist Floor Associates, Cashiers and Production crew to complete all necessary tasks. This is a regular full-time, non-exempt position located at San Fernando Valley (North Hollywood), reporting to the SFV Security &amp; Dispatch Supervisor. Compensation is commensurate with skills and experience. This position is eligible for overtime and benefits according to applicable guidelines.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Maintain cleanliness and sanitary conditions of site.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Clean and organize production area.</li></ul>
<p>• Close production and staging areas. •</p>
<p>Maintain organization and cleanliness of store, offices, bathrooms, parking lots, etc</p>
<ul><li>Front area support (hangers, bagging, customer service for carry out).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Unload trucks when necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Operate forklift.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform ‘floater’ and ‘unloader’ duties (trash removal, barrel removal, assists sorters).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Unload and sort donation carts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Separate salable items from recycle, baler and trash.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist preparer when necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track carts and time on board and with pricer for production report.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum requirement of 30 minutes per hard goods carts &amp; 15 minutes per clothing carts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Put out remaining merchandise onto sales floor.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain organization and cleanliness of production and warehouse including parking lot and front entrance of store.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Load textiles and e-waste.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Other Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Ensure task checklist is completed daily.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform daily forklift inspection.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain MSDS inventory.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure all chemicals and supplies are in stock.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure all recyclables are labeled and in compliance with EPA.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Check to make sure all electrical appliances are turned off at end of shift.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Load and unload dump truck.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Promote RMA Safety Program in alignment with the Injury and Illness Prevention Program (IIPP) and model safe work practices.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities</b> There are no supervisory responsibilities to this position.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul><li>High school diploma, GED or equivalent.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated work ethic with consistent attendance and punctuality.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Valid Driver’s License and insurable under RMA</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills</b></p>
<ul><li>Must be able to demonstrate excellent customer service, and be able to multi-task.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Forklift operating experience.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Software </b>There are no software requirements for this position.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Demands</b></p>
<ul><li>Must be able to lift 60 lbs. work on feet for long periods of time.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain physical condition appropriate to the performance of assigned duties</li></ul>
<ul><li>Hands-on position, including moving boxes, lifting merchandise, etc.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<p>This position will function in a thrift store and warehouse environment, will also be working in proximity with an at-risk population where errant behavior and disease risk may be present. Appropriate safety considerations will be required to avoid illness or injury.</p>
