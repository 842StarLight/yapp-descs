<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB</b> <b>TITLE: </b>LEASING CONSULTANT</p>
<p><b>GENERAL PURPOSE</b>:</p>
<p>Learn and adhere to Company policies and procedures as set forth in Company manuals. Provide prospective residents with a positive overview of the property. Show available apartments to prospective residents. Perform necessary paperwork to process new residents. Provide general office assistance and report to Leasing Manager.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b>:</p>
<p>Tour property and show apartments to prospective residents, including facilities, grounds, and other amenities.</p>
<p>Complete guest card information and follow up with prospective residents both by mail, e-mail and telephone.</p>
<p>Process rental applications for Leasing Manager approval, prepare lease documents, including new leases and renewals, and process notices to vacate premises. Set up and maintain resident lease files.</p>
<p>Provide assistance to residents regarding complaints or problem resolution. Prepare service requests from residents via telephone, e-mail or office visits. Conduct follow up calls in relation to status of service request performed.</p>
<p>Shop, visit, and compile information for all competing communities and surrounding sub-market in relation to pricing, availability and concessions. Inform Assistant General Manager and/or General Manager of findings.</p>
<p>Monitor Internet web-sites for property to ensure accuracy and competitiveness by reviewing competitionâ€™s advertising.</p>
<p>Promote property in a positive fashion via Internet advertising, Preferred Employer Program (PEP), off-site marketing, resident newsletters, and promotional campaigns. Assist with planning and attend community activities/ functions sponsored by the property.</p>
<p>Communicate with residents and co-workers with courtesy and efficiency to ensure resident satisfaction and smooth operation of office.</p>
<p>Remain current with regard to Landlord / Tenant and Fair Housing laws.</p>
<p><b>NON-ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS: </b></p>
<p>Pick up garbage found on property grounds.</p>
<p>Freshen and inspect vacant apartments, models, and tour route daily prior to viewing with prospective residents.</p>
<p>Daily use of balloons, helium, or other promotional items.</p>
<p>Off Site marketing.</p>
<p>Other duties as assigned by General Manager.</p>
<p>Attend staff meetings and training sessions.</p>
<p><b>JOB SPECIFICATIONS</b>:</p>
<p>Must possess the ability to read, comprehend, and follow simple to moderately complicated verbal and/or written instructions to perform bookkeeping tasks assigned and maintain simple records. Must possess basic arithmetical calculation skills (addition, subtractions, multiplication, division, percentages, etc.). This level of proficiency is normally achieved through completion of a high school education or its equivalent.</p>
<p>Be able to effectively communicate with residents, staff and other suppliers and vendors to ensure smooth and efficient activities.</p>
<p>Work with multiple deadlines, maintaining efficiency and control over projects assigned within time frames allowed and changing priorities.</p>
<p>Must have a basic knowledge of sales/marketing and customer service techniques. Must be able to work with various kinds of people, maintaining a professional, positive tone. Basic computer knowledge of Microsoft Word, Outlook/Gmail and Excel. This knowledge may be acquired through high school sales/marketing, computer courses or equivalent experience (three to six months).</p>
<p>Possess a basic knowledge of lease agreements and terminology. Understand basic landlord/tenant and fair housing laws. This knowledge may be acquired through education or equivalent experience (three to six months).</p>
<p><b>WORKING CONDITIONS</b>:</p>
<p>Typical working environment is in an office setting on a property, working with a personal computer at a desk and a telephone. May also involve greeting and taking prospective residents to vacant apartments in a multi-level apartment community. May be subject to outdoor weather conditions ranging from rain or snow to excessive heat during seasonal months.</p>
