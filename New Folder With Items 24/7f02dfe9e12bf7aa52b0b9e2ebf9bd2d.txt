<h2>Creator Publishing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>As a Publishing Manager, you will use your project management know‐how, multitasking abilities, and YouTube knowledge to optimize a client video pipeline that distributes thousands of videos every year across multiple platforms. The right person for this role has a passion for and knowledge of YouTube, Facebook and creators, plus experience with operational workflows. The ideal candidate will be organized, self‐motivated, able to work independently, and asks the right questions to get the job done correctly and efficiently. You will be able to communicate with internal and external teams quickly and concisely.</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilties</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Publish all content to social channels per client needs (YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Snpachat, Twitter)</li>
<li>
Ability to find workflow efficiencies across multiple different accounts, from entertainment creators to publishers</li>
<li>
Learn and experiment with new and surprising ways in which to solve clients’ problems</li>
<li>
Manage Publishing Coordinators and operational team</li>
<li>
Stay informed of platform updates as they relate to publishing</li>
<li>
Work with creators and talent managers to execute strategies surrounding creative and business objectives.</li>
<li>
Assist in pulling content performance and analytics across all social platforms for client facing reports.</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
2‐4 years relevant experience in a client‐facing role</li>
<li>
YouTube Certified within the last year and/or extremely familiar with current best practices for SEO and discoverability on YouTube</li>
<li>
History of managing online video channels, especially YouTube</li>
<li>
Experience with overseeing channel development strategies from building and presenting decks to execution</li>
<li>
Eye for detail in order to oversee brand design and channel best practices</li>
<li>
Analyze and interpret data to provide channel recommendations</li>
<li>
Create, present, and be able to continually speak to YouTube platform changes</li>
<li>
Work and cross‐function with sales, business development, and account teams when applicableExperience working and delivering to multiple stakeholders from internal and external teams</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Google products (Drive, Docs, Sheets, etc)</li>
<li>
Prior Experience with YouTube backend (or other CMS)</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
