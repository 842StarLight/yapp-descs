<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SPECIFIC DUTIES:</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>
Administer Corporate Hazard Communication programs (SDS’s, product labels)
</li>
<li>Global responsibility for the creation of product SDS’s and other safety related documents covering all commercial and experimental products and sold under various companies owned or affiliated with Troy Corporation worldwide. Manager additional staff in the development of SDS’s, product labels and other safety related documents.</li>
<li>
Manage software for multilingual SDS/Label development and maintenance
</li>
<li>Direct the activities associated with the current informational database in Intelligent Authoring (IA) for all raw materials, formulations, and finished products including establishment of procedures for organization, updating, and communicating changes to global users. Review and evaluate all new and updated supplier SDS’s and coordinate the input of relevant date into the software database and the current Excel database.</li>
<li>
Global Design and management of label formats utilizing IA capabilities for all manufacturing regions and integration of product labels from current word-based system into IA (Pesticide labels, as .pdf files, all other labels designed and generated within IA). In conjunction with processes developed by IT department, develop label formats to allow for integration of bar codes, Item Codes, and product weights as part of the printing process. Develop maintenance procedures for updating of product labels for all manufacturing locations.
</li>
<li>Provide Product Safety support to all Troy Departments, agents and customers</li>
<li>
Create and maintain Inter/Intranet Safety Documents</li>
<li>
Manage all SDS’s and labels in designated formats on the Troy network for use in Troy’s Website and internal requirements. Evaluate and implement regional needs for additional SDS and label format availability based on regulatory and sales requirements.
</li>
<li>Maintain electronic and paper files of all raw material and finished product SDS’s for all Troy locations and provide internal notification of updated information.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain raw material and product databases for internal informational and external regulatory reporting purposesMaintain database and reporting program to prepare Right-to-Know information for each of the facilities that require annual reporting (Troy chemical Corporation – Newark, Troy Corp – Florham Park, FTI Corp – Florham Park). Prepare and file the annual reports.</li>
<li>
Maintain Hazmat compliance for transportation regulations worldwideEvaluate and classify all Troy products for Hazmat classification for all models of transport including International Maritime Dangerous Goods (IMDG) segregation classification. Maintain all Hazmat information in BPCS to print out on shipping Bill of Lading (BL).</li>
<li>
Performs all other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
