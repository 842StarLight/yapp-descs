<h2>Customer Service Administrator - Spanish</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Communicates directly with customers, taking telephone orders, responding to price and delivery questions, and answering product related questions.
<br>Promise Dates -- Verifies production dates and enters information into the scheduling system.
<br>Enters, maintains, and processes customer orders into the Order Entry system (keyboard entry).
<br>Edits all orders, checking information such as addresses, transportation methods, quantities and weights, dimensions and insurance charges.
<br>Ensures we have the right product for the customer and that the order makes sense.
<br>Enters MRP requirements for customers as required.
<br>Maintains customer master lists and forecast reports.
<br>May generate and distribute various reports on a daily basis.
<br>Files documents such as drawings and customer orders.
<br>Types various documents as required.
<br>Forwards Order Confirmation to customers.
<br>Performs duties in accordance with quality standards
<br>Maintains work area in accordance with established housekeeping procedures to ensure a safe and clean work environment.
<br>Performs such similar, comparable or related duties as may be required, such as providing occasional back-up for reception duties as needed.
<br><br>
Technical/Associates Degree or equivalent required.
<br>Must be proficient in understanding, speaking, and writing in both Spanish and English.
<br>Two years of previous experience in Sales, Customer Service, or Order Entry in manufacturing.
<br>Aptitude to enter data accurately and rapidly.
<br>Good oral and written communication skills.
<br>Problem solving and negotiation skills.
<br>Ability to organize and prioritize multiple tasks.
<br>Ability to work well with all levels of personnel within the company, and outside of the company.
<br>Strong ability to use office equipment and computer software including Microsoft applications and telephone equipment.
