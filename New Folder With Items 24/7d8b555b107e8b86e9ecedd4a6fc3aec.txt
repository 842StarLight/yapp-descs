We are looking for people who feels that Customer Success comes from the heart. We are looking for someone who has the best customer management and business consultancy skills. People who are passionate about engaging their customers and expanding their productivity and performance. If You have awesome relational skills and can create win/win environments for all parties that you work with. If this describes you, we want you to join us and help leading our customers to success.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop direct communications and relationships with assigned accounts to maintain high satisfaction while assisting them to achieve their business goals.</li>
<li>Develop Ground plans with accounts to drive growth by leveraging all innovations, existing tools and integrate all relevant business functions such as marketing, business development, financial consultant, services, etc.</li>
<li>Identify and promote cross sale opportunities such software options and value-added services.</li>
<li>Develop intimate knowledge of the accounts’ environment to recommend operational excellence activities to increase efficiency, uptime and market addressability.</li>
<li>Support where needed with all customer inquiries regarding supplies/service price increase, invoices and other non-sales related issue</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Qualifications / Requirements:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years of proven experience in customer success, customer facing or Account management, while working with organizations of all sizes and with multiple levels of stakeholders including operational, senior management, and executive contacts.</li>
<li>Deep technical background with marketing automation tools, e-mail marketing, personalization and other related products and services</li>
<li>Impeccable written and verbal communication skills as well as Presentation capabilities.</li>
<li>Detail oriented and analytical.</li>
<li>Strong team player, but still a self-starter and self-driven Thrives in a multi – tasking environment and can adjust priorities on the fly.</li>
<li>Ability to travel 30-35% to visit customer sites.</li>
</ul>
