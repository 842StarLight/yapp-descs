<h2>Enterprise Account Executive - 20344</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p>Profiles, an award-winning Creative &amp; Marketing staffing agency is seeking an <b>Enterprise Account Executive </b>to sit in<b> Baltimore, MD or Washington, DC, or Philadelphia, PA. </b>The Enterprise Account Executive must have a proven track record in Enterprise sales and account management and be subject matter expert in creative and digital services.</p>
<p>This is sales director level role will maintain and expand relationships with creative and marketing decision-makers of Enterprise organizations. The Enterprise Account Executive will strategically work with the CEO to identify large accounts for prospecting and penetration in targeted growth areas.</p>
<p><b>Enterprise Account Executive Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish productive, professional relationships with key Senior Management C-Level personnel in assigned customer accounts.</li>
<li>Target accounts to land large RFP's and managed service agreements in support of creative, digital, and marketing project needs.</li>
<li>Coordinate the involvement of the Business Development team, including support, service, and management resources, in order to meet account performance objectives and customers' expectations.</li>
<li>Meet the target goal for profitable sales volume and strategic objectives in assigned accounts.</li>
<li>Proactively lead a joint company-strategic account planning process that develops mutual performance objectives, financial targets, and critical milestones for a one-year period.</li>
<li>Proactively deliver an exceptional client service experience to nurture and grow existing accounts.</li>
<li>Lead the project or hiring solution development efforts that best address customer needs while coordinating the involvement of all necessary company personnel.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Enterprise Account Executive Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Four-year college degree.</li>
<li>Minimum seven (7) years strategic sales experience in a business-to-business environment. Enterprise sales preferred.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of pitching creative, digital, or technology projects and services. Managed Services or digital agency experience a plus.</li>
<li>This position requires travel to all corporate markets and prospective markets. Baltimore, MD, Washington, DC, Philadelphia, PA, and Richmond, VA.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Senior Leadership team at Profiles to develop yearly sales and account strategies.</li>
<li>Closely coordinates company executive involvement with customer management.</li>
<li>This position may have direct report staff assigned to support responsibilities in specific customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Profiles Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive base salary.</li>
<li>Uncapped commission plan.</li>
<li>Bonus plan.</li>
<li>Medical, Dental, Prescription, and Vision Insurance.</li>
<li>401k Retirement plan with a match.</li>
<li>Paid vacation, sick days, and holidays.</li>
<li>Casual dress attire, monthly cell phone allowance, mileage reimbursement, and discounted commuter plan.</li>
</ul>
