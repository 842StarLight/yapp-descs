<div>
The SoftwareOne Tax Manager for North America (including additional satellite countries) is a newly created position due to the growth of the company and the growing complexity surrounding direct and indirect taxation in an international environment. This position is located in Waukesha, WI and reports to the Finance Director with very strong dotted line reporting to Group Tax (in Switzerland).<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Tax Manager has a great opportunity to serve as the tax expert and own all tax related functions for the U.S., and Canada, and to a lesser extent for Trinidad, Jamaica and Puerto Rico. This person will be responsible for building out the function for these regions. The position will be a blend of compliance and strategic tax planning work.<br>
</div>
</div></div></div>
<div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Prepare federal, state and local tax return workpapers and work with external provider to timely file all direct and indirect tax returns.</li>
<li>
Calculate and pay federal and state income and franchise tax estimates for the U.S. and Canada.
</li>
<li>Prepare U.S. and Canadian income tax provisions, monthly tax accounting entries, and annual provision-to-return adjustments.</li>
<li>
Manage audits for federal and state income tax, personal property, and sales &amp; use tax with support from Accounting Team.
</li>
<li>Recommend most effective tax structure for foreign and domestic acquisitions in conjunction with Group Tax.</li>
<li>
Work with Group Tax team to implement global tax strategies to improve cash flow and reduce global ETR.</li>
<li>
Identify and manage annual tax savings projects.</li>
<li>
Review U.S. and Canadian tax depreciation and disposals schedules.</li>
<li>
Prepare and review other non-income tax filings, including annual reports, business license applications, personal property tax returns and sales &amp; use tax returns with support from Accounting Team.
</li>
<li>Prepare all BEA and Census reports on quarterly an annual basis.</li>
<li>
Perform state and local tax research and maintain state exemption certificates.</li>
<li>
Perform contract review and consultation related to customer tax matters.</li>
<li>
Serve as main contact for withholding tax questions and research, especially for sales organization in complex cross border deals.</li>
<li>
Review, implement and maintain T&amp;E and payroll policies to ensure alignment with IRS standards and rules.</li>
<li>
Perform special projects as requested.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
