<div><b>Hyperion Senior Manager</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Are you ready to enhance your skills and build your career in a rapidly evolving business climate? Are you looking for a career where professional development is embedded in its core culture? If so, Deloitte could be the place for you! Join our team of Government and Public Service Financial specialists who support large scale financial transformations by leveraging cutting edge technology and take your career to the next level!</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Work you’ll do</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>As a Senior Manager within the Finance team, you will:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Organize and deliver financial consulting services on a cross-section of complex projects</div></li>
<li><div>Responsible for the development of business and vendor relationships
</div></li>
<li><div>Lead pursuit teams focused on new opportunities and lead major aspects of the proposal development process</div></li>
<li><div>Responsible for project(s) financials including development of financial plans
</div></li>
<li><div>Lead day-to-day interactions with executive clients and Deloitte team</div></li>
<li><div>Establish relationships in strategic client positions; negotiate, lead, and facilitate these relationships</div></li>
<li><div>Display both breadth and depth of knowledge regarding functional and technical issues</div></li>
<li><div>Provide counseling/coaching, leadership and support for delivery teams and staff
</div></li>
<li><div>Actively participate in staff recruitment and retention activities providing input and guidance into the staffing process</div></li>
<li><div>Focus on managed revenue targets as well as achieving quarterly or yearly sales objectives for new business</div></li>
<li><div>Lead business development initiatives to grow the Finance team and the Federal practice</div></li>
<li><div>Manage resources and budget on client projects</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide full lifecycle financial analytic systems management from requirements, design, development, and deployment</div></li>
<li><div>
Client-based travel, up to 80-100%</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>The Team</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<p>Finance &amp; Enterprise Performance</p>
<p>Our Finance &amp; Enterprise Performance team works as a trusted advisor to CFOs, COOs, CIOs, and key executives. We advise, implement and deliver on premise and as-a-service technology solutions, helping our clients develop strategies and capabilities to support enterprise-wide decision-making, improve finance operations, and create impact within their organization.</p>
</div>

<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Required:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Experience with Planning and Budgeting Systems and/or Experience with Financial Reporting Systems</li>
<li><div>
Bachelor's degree in accounting, business, finance or other business-related field</div></li>
<li><div>7+ years of relevant consulting or industry experience in financial analysis, transformation or strategic initiatives</div></li>
<li><div>3+ years in a technical or functional leadership role</div></li>
<li><div>Experience mentoring and coaching others</div></li>
<li><div>Proven leadership skills demonstrating strong judgment, problem-solving, and decision-making abilities</div></li>
<li><div>Experience managing senior-level client relationships</div></li>
<li><div>Experience presenting to clients or other decision makers to present and sell ideas to various audiences (technical and non-technical)</div></li>
<li><div>Pre-sales, proposal, and RFP experience</div></li>
<li><div>Proven track record of consistently attaining managed revenue targets as well as achieving quarterly or yearly sales objectives for new business</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to obtain and maintain the required Clearance for this role</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Preferred:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Masters of Business Administration (MBA) degree</div></li>
<li><div>Prior professional services or Government consulting experience</div></li>
<li><div>
Deep functional knowledge around financial systems and processes</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience implementing Oracle Hyperion</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience with Oracle Business Intelligence (OBIEE) or similar enterprise reporting Tools</div></li>
<li><div>CDFM, CGFM and/or PMP certification</div></li>
</ul>
