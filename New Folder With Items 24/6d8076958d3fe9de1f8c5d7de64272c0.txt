<h2>Manager, Partnership & Occasion Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Summary:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>This is one of the coolest &amp; rarest opportunities at The Hershey Company! It is a rare opportunity to touch The Hershey Company’s brands – Hershey’s, Reese’s, Kit Kat, Twizzler’s &amp; Ice Breakers - and learn the inner workings of the its award-winning marketing department. You will build our biggest brands &amp; businesses by planning &amp; executing our key ’20 &amp; ‘21 marketing partnerships &amp; activation programs. We are looking for a fast start to join Partnership &amp; Occasions Marketing (POM) team to drive brand love &amp; frequency of our core brands.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key responsibilities include (and their percentage of estimated time allocated):</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Partnership Activation &amp; Marketing Execution: bring scale partnership marketing programs to life, specifically leading the commercialization, merchandising, promotions, point of-sale, experiential, PR and earned media endeavors (60%)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Marketing &amp; Business Planning: lead, in partnership with brand marketing &amp; sales, annual and long-term marketing &amp; business plans which drive core growth of our portfolio of confection brands (25%)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Identification of Strategic Partners &amp; Partner Management: based upon business priorities identify new partnership opportunities to accelerate brand growth &amp; equity as well as mange &amp; growth existing partnerships (15%)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>This position is part of POM, which is within the US Marketing Organization, and reports to the Director of POM. This is a marketing role for a seasoned marketer on a general management path. It is a great opportunity for someone with marketing experience to learn more about the company’s iconic brands.</div>
<br>
