<h2>Customer Event Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Sealed Air partners with customers to solve their most critical packaging challenges with innovative solutions that leave our world, environment, and communities better than we found them. Our portfolio of widely recognized brands includes Cryovac® food packaging and Bubble Wrap® protective packaging which respectively enable a safer, more efficient food supply chain and protect valuable goods shipped around the world. Sealed Air generated $4.7 billion in sales in 2018 and has approximately 15,500 employees who serve customers in 123 countries. To learn more, visit www.sealedair.com.</div>
<div>

<b>
Responsibilities</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The Sealed Air Packaging Innovation Center is a $1.5mm investment and a key lead generation vehicle for product care. The Sales Development Manager will manage all aspects of the facility and conduct approximately 250 customer events per year where 20% will be end users with C suite participation. This is a high impact position requiring disciplined preparation and follow up. Business development is achieved through specific end user activities, and the training and education for Sealed Air, UPS and Channel Partner teams.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Lead all activities that enable new revenue streams as a result of this high tech, collaborative space</li>
<li>Schedule and conduct approximately 250 public, private and client tours in conjunction with UPS resources</li>
<li>Lead customer visit pre-calls via phone / Webex and conduct post visit follow up</li>
<li>Manage the facility space, equipment and day to day needs including any supporting personnel</li>
<li>Work with UPS to market the space internally to Sealed Air and UPS, externally to the public and prospects</li>
<li>Follow up on all customer opportunities, facilitating new sales with the expectation of delivering a ”first call” qualified, next step handoff” to the Product Care Enterprise and Field sales teams</li>
<li>Act as a liaison among onsite UPS Packaging Engineers, Sealed Air Designers, Sealed Air Fulfillment area, CTP Program and Sealed Air Fast Radius Additive manufacturing</li>
<li>Foster an environment of collaboration with Sealed Air marketing, portfolio and communication teams for continuous improvement and contemporary messaging</li>
</ul>
