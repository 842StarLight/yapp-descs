<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>VIE – Marketing Cloud &amp; Data Specialist</b></h2>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About fifty-five</b></h3>
<p>Part of You &amp; Mr Jones, the world’s first brandtech group, fifty-five is a data company helping brands collect, analyse and activate their data across paid, earned and owned channels to increase their marketing ROI and improve customer acquisition and retention.</p>
<p>
Headquartered in Paris with offices in London, Hong Kong, New York and Shanghai, the data company was named by Deloitte as one of the fastest-growing tech firms in Europe, thanks to its unique technology approach combining talent with software and service expertise.</p>
<p>
As a Marketing Cloud and Data specialist, you will be in charge of helping the US consulting team design datalakes and Cloud-based platforms for the purpose of improving client’s marketing performance. You will translate the client’s business requirements (marketing &amp; IT) into technical specifications. You will facilitate the production of technical design documents in close collaboration with 55’s Engineering, Data Quality experts, Data Science and Consultant teams. You will be the first point of contact in the US team to answer technical questions from the 55 consultants and you support them for client workshops and training. The position is based in NY, but short travel periods are expected for client delivery.<br>
</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Skills and Experience</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 years of experience</li>
<li>Initial experience on a cloud-based project (as a technical project manager, developer or architect)</li>
<li>Deep understanding of public cloud products, especially GCP and AWS</li>
<li>Significant experience on leveraging webanalytics, advertising and CRM data in the Cloud for activation or measurement purpose</li>
<li>Strong project management skills in the field of data architecture and datalake design</li>
<li>Great communication skills and at ease with both marketing and IT teams</li>
<li>Client-facing experience for detailed technical specifications discussions</li>
<li>Fluent English</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Education and Experience Required</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Master’s degree (business, engineering) or equivalent diploma from a top tier university</li>
<li>2+ years experience in consulting firms or digital/media agency</li>
<li>Solid experience in project management in an international context with both business and technology stakeholders</li>
<br>
</ul>
