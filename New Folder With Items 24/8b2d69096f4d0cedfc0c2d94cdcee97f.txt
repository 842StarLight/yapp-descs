EPICA is a pioneering technology venture that gives companies unprecedented decision-making power by analyzing data in real-time through large scale data processing. <br><br>
ABOUT THE JOB
<br><br>
The CSM will be involved in all aspects of support, account management, product demos, educating customers and more. The CSM will work with a set number of strategic EPICA SaaS clients to:
<br><ul>
<li>Serve as the primary contact for the onboarding of new customers, training the end-users, and provide post-go-live support.</li>
<li>Conduct executive business reviews to understand current deployment, business relationships, and general issues.</li>
<li>Empower customers to connect their goals and challenges to solutions on our platform.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the engineering and development teams to set up o our software platform per customers’ requirements and troubleshoot technical issues.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales to support pilot customers, renewals, and expansion opportunities.</li>
<li>Represent the voice of the customer to provide input into every core product, marketing, and sales process.</li>
</ul>
The end goal of the CSM endeavor is to retain and grow business relationships in perpetuity,” Customers for Life” as measured by Net Retention, Usage, and ultimately Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).
<br><br>
Key Success Criteria
<br><ul>

<li>Drive Customer References &amp; Case Study Generation</li>
</ul>
