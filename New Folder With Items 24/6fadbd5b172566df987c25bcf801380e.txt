<p>Start or continue to build your career with a top ranked mortgage company!<br>
<br>
Waterstone Mortgage was recently named the <b>#2 Best Company to Work For by Mortgage Executive Magazine!</b> Our company culture is second to none; to learn more about our exceptional work/life balance and employee engagement, visit GlassDoor. We pride ourselves on offering a full benefits package, including competitive pay, 401k match and PTO.</p>
<br>
<p>
The primary function of the New Client Coordinator is to manage the Loan Officer’s pipeline and to make certain that all loans are ready for submission to the Processing Department. This position is also responsible for communicating with referral partners, current and past clients, listing and selling agents, title companies, lenders and Underwriters to solidify relationships and garner future business. Assist Loan Originator in taking applications, scheduling appointments, and locking loans.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Screen calls and book appointments for Loan Originator.</li>
<li>Track leads on a daily basis for Loan Originator.</li>
<li>Responsible for taking applications and scheduling appointments for the mortgage team.</li>
<li>Assist Loan Originator in gathering all necessary forms and paperwork.</li>
<li>Responsible for taking and submitting complete loan applications for each customer file.</li>
<li>As part of the sales team, this individual is responsible for requesting/obtaining referrals from all WMC clients, current and potential along with sales partners.</li>
<li>Ensure the CRM is updated with new listing agents, referring agents, title companies, and builders.</li>
<li>Track all requested documents in Encompass.</li>
<li>Respond to customer inquiries and other related parties on transactions as needed.</li>
<li>Responsible for tracking all leads until an appointment is scheduled on the calendar.</li>
<li>Maintain Loan Originator calendar. This includes scheduling, updating and confirming appointments.<br>
<br>
<b>ADDITIONAL DUTIES:</b>
</li>
<li><i>Work week: Friday-Thursday. Possibility of working from home during weekend hours</i></li>
<li>Complete any additional duties assigned by management</li>
<li>Complete regulatory and compliance training, as required</li>
<li>Assist WMC employees and departments as needed</li>
<li>Responsible for partnering with Corporate Marketing to create and/or order marketing</li>
<li>Oversee and manage event planning for client events<br>
<br>
<i>Individuals in this position shall not engage in loan origination during the course of his/her employment with WMC. Loan origination is defined as (1) taking a residential mortgage loan application; or (2) offering or negotiating terms of a residential mortgage loan for compensation or gain; (3) advertising or communicating to the public that you can perform loan origination services. Please see the Loan Originator Compensation Policy for more details on the definition of loan origination.</i>
</li>
</ul>
