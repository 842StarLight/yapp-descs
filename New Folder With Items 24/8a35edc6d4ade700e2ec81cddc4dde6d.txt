VMware – Contracts Manager – Commercial Legal Group (CLG), Customer Infrastructure Team<br>
<br>
Position Overview:<br>
<br>
VMware seeks to add a highly talented individual to its Commercial Legal Group, supporting the company’s commercial sales business. This Contracts Manager position will be based in Palo Alto, California. While the primary job responsibility is helping to develop and update contracting documentation and terms for cloud sales offerings, you will form part of a new and growing team of Contract Negotiators working collaboratively with our CLG Attorneys, Sales organization, Deal Management Team, Finance, and the various Business Units in the U.S. and globally to ensure best-in-class, seamless support to our customers. All tasks will be performed under the supervision of a VMware attorney.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>You will help VMware Legal teammates to develop documentation to support VMware’s worldwide customer sales models, with a primary focus on VMware’s cloud services offerings, and to advise on legal, finance and business considerations associated with those commercial transactions.</li>
<li>You will help to develop and maintain appropriate contract terms for new cloud service offerings as the company releases or acquires them, and help update contract terms for existing cloud services offerings, to ensure alignment with current VMware policies and practices.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li>You will help update negotiation processes and tools that align with the company’s sales terms, including playbooks and training.</li>
<li>You will collaborate and partner effectively with business stakeholders in contracting processes, including the Business Units, Sales organization, Finance and Deal Management Team.</li>
<li>You will participate in special projects within the Legal Department as needed, including developing processes and policies that improve transaction velocity, efficiency and standardization without material increase in risk, and assisting others with transactions and the development of templates, playbooks and training across the company’s many technologies and sales businesses.</li>
<li>You will perform other projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree; law degree is a plus but not necessary.
</li></ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li>5 or more years of relevant experience with a US-based high-tech company; substantial experience with a cloud services company, and with its terms of service, commercial contracts, and processes for sales and services, is strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Proven skills in drafting commercial transaction documentation, including cloud service offerings, and/or software licensing, support, and professional services, and in working effectively with business teams on the legal aspects of sales contracting. Strong influencing skills are a plus, as is experience with negotiating agreements.</li>
<li>Strong business acumen, including ability to exercise good judgment about sensible business risks.</li>
<li>Knowledge of the IT industry, data protection, information security, privacy, and the latest trends in cloud services is also a plus.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience and skills in managing a volume of large, worldwide, and/or strategic agreements and suite of agreement templates through collaboration tools.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication, interpersonal and leadership skills.</li>
<li>A self-starter who operates well in a fast-paced business that strongly values teamwork and excellence, as well as execution, customers, community, and utmost integrity.</li>
<li>Track record of excellence and leadership in past jobs and academics, and of enthusiasm for learning.</li>
</ul>
