<h2>Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Chicago-based EZLinks Golf LLC, a PGA TOUR affiliated company, provides leading technology and services to the golf industry including tee sheets, point of sale, and reservation services, with a focus on driving rounds of golf. EZLinks also powers TeeOff.com by PGA TOUR, a leading online retail site, helping golfers book tee times at more than 3,000 courses worldwide.</p>
<p>We are growing and are looking for people who want to be a part of our growth story. EZLinks offers a dynamic and casual environment, competitive benefits, and a chance to change the golf industry.</p>
<p><b>Position:</b><br>
We are seeking a full-time <b>Customer Success Manager</b> to cover the eastern seaboard. This role will require approximately 25% of time driving to accounts and/or travel to other areas to participate in related company events. This position will be responsible for managing a portfolio of multiple accounts for a select territory, with the purpose of maximizing client satisfaction, account retention, and increasing use and satisfaction of EZLinks products and services on a per-client basis. This position will report to our Director of Customer Success.</p>
<div>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the on-going assessment and review of the needs of assigned golf courses and management companies</li>
<li>Accountable for all aspects of relationship management of assigned accounts with the result of maximizing client satisfaction and product usage across all product and service lines, including client retention/renewals and revenue generation</li>
<li>Perform annual client reviews resulting in both in person and/or over the phone facilitation of on-site or remote (ie via Webinar) product and service consultations and demonstrations</li>
<li>Ability to leverage marketing materials to promote the benefits of EZLinks products and services</li>
<li>Attend nation-wide industry conferences, regional meetings, and trade shows to promote the brand</li>
<li>Gather and share client success stories, case studies and product usage data to promote smart practices across teams</li>
<li>Review performance on the consumer portal (TeeOff.com) and other partner distribution channels. Recommend pricing and yield strategies to drive more rounds of golf and increase revenue</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 3 years’ experience in a sales function responsible for client retention and overall customer service</li>
<li>Advanced, working knowledge of MS Windows 7 and XP OS, as well as full MS Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, and Access)</li>
<li>Display of exceptional communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Undergraduate degree in Business, a related field, or equivalent is required</li>
<li>Must be able and willing to travel by car at least 25% of the time</li>
<li>Must be able and willing to work remotely and have a conducive place to manage business needs</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years’ of experience in sales including managing a portfolio of accounts</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years’ of experience being accountable for client retention, consultation and/or sales training</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years’ of experience managing projects and successfully meeting deadlines</li>
<li>Proven abilities to communicate and listen effectively to clients to understand current and future needs, and suggest ways to provide solutions</li>
<li>Prior work experience in software training is preferred</li>
<li>Proven experience working in a dynamic work culture with the ability to integrate new ideas to continually improve sales operations is needed</li>
<li>Experience working effectively with colleagues within sales, marketing and other functions, and at varying levels is required</li>
<li>Experience within the golf industry is strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
</div>
