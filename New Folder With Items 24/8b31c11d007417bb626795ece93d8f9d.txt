<h2>Marketing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Cape Cod Broadcasting Media</b> (99.9 WQRC, Cape Country 104, WFCC Cape Classical 107.5, Ocean 104.7 &amp; CapeCod.com) is looking for an experienced Marketing Director well versed in media, advertising, PR, promotions, event planning and social media and responsible for the external and internal perception of our company and five brands to listeners/users, clients and staff.</p>
<p>
The position requires creative talent, execution skills, and brand centric concepts to promote engagement and participation to attract and retain listeners/users and encourage client participation and co-branding opportunities.</p>
<p>
The right individual will be highly organized, reliable, professional in and out of the building; will work with the Program Director and Digital Strategy Director to create annual marketing campaigns for 4 radio stations/websites and Capecod.com; will work with the General Sales Manger to develop and produce solution-based promotions to meet client goals and enhance our company and five brands on site at client events.</p>
