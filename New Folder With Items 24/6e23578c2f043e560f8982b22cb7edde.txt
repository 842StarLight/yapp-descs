 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>
<p>
Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p>
The Enterprise Complaints Management Office (ECMO) plays an integral role in having an enterprise view of our customer concerns, identifying risks in a timely manner, providing enhanced reporting capabilities at the line of business and Enterprise levels, and delivering a variety of benefits to our business. This Office includes four main functions: Executive Complaint Operations, Data Analytics and Reporting, Quality Assurance, and Complaints Shared Services. Together these teams focus on capturing, researching, and resolving customer dissatisfaction; executing deep root cause analysis; and proactively identifying continuous opportunities that are engineered to improve our customers’ experience and drive out dissatisfaction.</p>
<p>
The Shared services Operations Support team is actively searching for a Business Support Consultant 1 (BSC1) to join an exciting new leadership team. This position will be reside within ECMO Shared Services and play a critical role supporting Business Continuity Planning. As the teams come together in a centralized fashion from across the organization to build ECMO, business continuity is critical to ensure a positive experience for our customers. The team member in this position will partner with various business partners inside and outside of ECMO to build robust, flexible, and effective continuity plans.</p>
<p>
An individual contributor with proven critical thinking, analytical skills, and demonstrated operational credibility will be determining factors in selection. Successful candidates will be articulate and possess a demonstrated leadership capability; they will be impressive in their inter-personal effectiveness and with regard to their accomplishments. It requires strong relationship management, influencing, vision, operational, analytical and execution skills. A key differentiator will be the ability to anticipate changes and influence leaders across ECMO to meet Wells Fargo policy regarding BCP. The role requires a sense of urgency, passion for results, people skills and personal accountability for driving results.</p>
<div>
Specifically, we seek:<br>
<ul>
<li>Leadership: Proven ability to establish and gain buy-in for an idea or vision, and garner enthusiastic support across the organization to accomplish agreed-upon goals. Ability to influence business partners and key contributors that do not report directly to the BSC1.</li>
<li>Problem Solving: Ability to visualize, articulate, and solve problems. Accurately analyzes complex issues, ensuring identification of underlying root causes. Demonstrates the ability to solve problems under conditions of uncertainty and ambiguity. Generates alternative solutions and expertly handles obstacles.</li>
<li>Professional Skills: Capable prolific communicator that is comfortable working with a wide variety of corporate executives, sales, marketing, engineering, operations, customers and business partners. Proven teamwork skills; excellent interpersonal, written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>Creativity and Innovation: Able to think creatively and critically to identify opportunities to uniquely and effectively meet customer needs. Strong ability to assess market and competitive conditions and to develop creative, break-through approaches supporting improved customer experiences and operational efficiencies.</li>
<li>Analytical Thinking: Obtains and uses data necessary to identify opportunities or understand problems; uses quantitative techniques to make it actionable.</li>
</ul>
</div>
