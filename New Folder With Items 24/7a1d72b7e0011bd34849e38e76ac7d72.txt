<h2>Garage Door Apprentice</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
A1 GARAGE DOOR SERVICES
<br>The industry leader in garage door installation and repair, is seeking great people to become a residential and custom Garage Door Apprentice to join our growing family-oriented organization.
<br><br>
The Garage Door Apprentice is responsible for assisting and learning the installation of residential and custom garage doors. We are looking for driven and self-motivated individuals seeking a trade they can grow in to a career. The right candidate will be mechanically and technically inclined and possesses the interpersonal skills to thrive in this customer service focused position.
<br><br>
<b>To the right Garage Door Apprentice, we offer:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Consistent work</li>
<li>Health, dental, life, and Vision insurance</li>
<li>Company vehicle and tablet</li>
<li>Paid training</li>
<li>Administrative support and helpful management/technical assistance as necessary</li>
<li>Highly competitive career Path</li>
</ul>
The apprentice position consists of learning in the classroom along with 3 months of in the field training with one of our senior installers. Upon completion of your apprenticeship, you would be place on the schedule as a full time junior installer.
<br><br>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities for the Garage Door Apprentice:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Report to training manager daily for tasks</li>
<li>Report to Senior Installer for field training</li>
<li>Learn the A1 procedures and policies</li>
<li>Familiarize yourself with A1 software and tech.</li>
<li>Use a variety of hand and power tools</li>
<li>Learn to inspect job sites both pre and post install</li>
</ul>
Compensation
<br>Apprenticeship will last approximately 3 months. During this time you will receive a $15.00 hour pay. Upon completion of the program you will graduate to a Installer and or a Sales and Service Technician . Installers are paid commission and piece work per job and average between $1000-$2000 a week.

<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Requirements for the Garage Door Apprentice:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Great Attitude</li>
<li>Mechanical aptitude, including skill in using a variety of hand and power tools, accurately reading a tape measure, and interpreting equipment manuals</li>
<li>Commitment to customer satisfaction and high-quality standards</li>
<li>Ability to learn a new software and be comfortable working on a tablet</li>
<li>Physically able to move items weighing 80-150 lbs. and the ability to bend, climb, reach, kneel, etc. for extended periods</li>
</ul>
