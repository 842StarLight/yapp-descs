<h2>Small Progressive Clinic - Medical Office Manager Position</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>***NOTE: Please read through the ad and send us a COVER LETTER if you're really interested. Applications without a cover letter will not be considered.</p>
<p>Do you like working in small business settings? Interested in making a difference in people's lives daily? Would you like to be part of an amazing, positive team where you feel valued?</p>
<p>We've built a dynamic team of doctors and admin staff and are looking for your help to become even more organized and efficient. We are an integrative medicine clinic and our goal is to help people fundamentally change their lives to live happy and symptom-free.</p>
<p>The team is fun and light-hearted with a very positive office environment. We like to keep communication open and support personal and professional growth with coaching opportunities and regular meetings.</p>
<p>The position is full-time, Monday through Friday, with healthcare benefits, paid time off, discounts on services, and bonuses.</p>
<p><b>Primary </b><b>responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Office reception: Answering phones, scheduling office visits, and checking patients in and out for their appointments</li>
<li>Managing flow of patients in front office and treatment rooms</li>
<li>Managing inventory of supplements, test kits, office supplies, and medical supplies</li>
<li>Prospectively identifying gaps in patient service or office flow and suggesting improvements</li>
<li>Tracking patient retention and managing the recall process</li>
<li>Managing facility, including phones and tech support needs</li>
<li>Working with Clinical Director to refine processes and practice policy</li>
<li>Reporting and tracking sales to refine marketing strategies</li>
<li>Producing financial reports for contractor payments, budgeting, and forecasting</li>
<li>Managing front desk and back office teams</li>
<li>Organizing marketing events and networking with other businesses</li>
<li>Keeping social media accounts up to date</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excited to work with kind, compassionate doctors who genuinely care about the staff and their patients.</li>
<li>Very tech savvy and able to troubleshoot most issues that come up with computers, bluetooth speakers, etc.</li>
<li>5 + yrs. of experience in a medical setting with at least 1 year of experience managing a team.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills as well as a professional appearance and demeanor</li>
<li>Experience with medical software and electronic medical records</li>
<li>Immaculate record-keeping and organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent multi-tasking and critical thinking skills</li>
<li>Understanding of HIPAA and basic coding for insurance reimbursement</li>
<li>Comfortable with MS Office suite, Mac OS, peripherals</li>
</ul>
