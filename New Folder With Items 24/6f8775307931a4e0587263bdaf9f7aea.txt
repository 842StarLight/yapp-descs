<p>
KPMG is currently seeking a Manager in Enterprise Solutions for our Consulting practice.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead and participate in the design, development and deployment of implementation of SAP Hybris to support client business initiatives, including architecting, designing and building the Hybris solution from the ground up as well as the customization, extension, and implementation of third party software products which integrate with it</li>
<li>
Possess technical ownership of multiple applications and will work closely with the functional resources to ensure that the applications are fulfilling client requirements</li>
<li>
Accountable for translating the functional specifications into an application architecture that is conducive to IT's technical processes and standards</li>
<li>
Provide leadership to others and interface with senior management to provide and obtain information to build consensus regarding project direction</li>
<li>
Independently manage project teams for complete engagements, following leading practices and coordinating technical and functional resources</li>
<li>
Participate in business development by identifying new opportunities and assisting with proposal development; Manage and provide leadership to teams of staff level professionals and become actively involved in staff development and practice management
</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
A minimum of five years of SAP Hybris related experience with a professional services firm and experience leading and delivering full life cycle implementations</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree from an accredited college/university; MBA or MIS from an accredited college/university is preferred
</li>
<li>Hands on Experience on SAP Hybris Commerce and proficient in JEE Architecture (Spring, JSP, Servlets, JMS, JNDI, ORM, EJB) as well as integrating Hybris with SAP ECC/HANA, Adobe Experience Manager</li>
<li>
Experience in E2E Hybris Project delivery on SAP and other cloud platforms and experience in Order to cash process, sales promotions, subscription, content management and consent management</li>
<li>
Functional knowledge on SAP Sales and Distribution, IS Retail and CRM</li>
<li>
U.S. based; travel may be up to eighty to one hundred percent of the time</li>
<li>
Applicants must be currently authorized to work in the Unites States without the need for visa sponsorship now or in the future</li>
</ul>
