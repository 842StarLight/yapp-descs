<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Jump-start your corporate sales career with one of the fastest growing companies in the travel industry, Safe Harbors Business Travel (a division of WorldVia Travel Group). Our team has the stability and reputation of an established company, combined with an exciting startup atmosphere. We are in the heart of our growth story and looking to expand our sales capabilities while expanding our team’s knowledge in the world of corporate travel management. Do you want to be a part of a company committed to monster growth, but thinks people first, profits second? Safe Harbors Business Travel has a passionate culture for people and the places they travel.</p>
<p>
WorldVia Travel Group is a travel sales and media company with five distinct and proven lines of business:</p>
<ul>
<li>Travel Quest Network, Independent Travel Agent Hosting</li>
<li>Main Street Travel/Travel Leaders, Retail Travel Sales</li>
<li>CruiseWire.com, Cruise Vacation Sales (launching this summer)</li>
<li>Safe Harbors Business Travel, Corporate Travel Management</li>
<li>Ministry Travel, Humanitarian/Mission Travel Air Sales</li>
</ul>
<p>WorldVia Travel Group has grown 65% PER YEAR since our current leadership team joined in 2015, but it’s time to crank up our growth. Great talent is at the heart of our expansion strategy and we need rock stars to join our Corporate Sales team who want to propel their career forward and share our passion for our clients and their travel needs.</p>
<p>
We are looking for a competent Account Executive to find business opportunities and manage customer relationships. You’ll be directly responsible for the preservation and expansion of our customer base. The ideal candidate will have talent in sales and experience in customer service. We expect you to be able to achieve balance between customer orientation and a results-driven approach. The goal is to find opportunities and turn them in long-term profitable relationships based on trust and mutual satisfaction.<br>
</p>
<p>
In this role, you will have the:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Opportunity to learn from a forward-thinking team who are experts in the travel industry</li>
<li>Enthusiastic support and a systematic approach for building your sales and corporate travel knowledge, with an unlimited commission plan</li>
<li>Chance to make your mark as our company expands, while enhancing our reputation in the travel community</li>
<li>Ability to drive your career to the next level as part of our Training &amp; Development Program</li>
<li>Opportunity to earn uncapped commission</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the entire sales cycle from finding a client to securing a deal</li>
<li>Unearth new sales opportunities through networking and turn them into long term partnerships</li>
<li>Make winning presentations to prospective clients</li>
<li>Provide professional after-sales support to enhance the client experience</li>
<li>Maintain relationships with won clients to maintain a current view of client needs</li>
<li>Negotiate very large sales agreements and maintain records of sales activity data</li>
<li>Coordinate and follow up with the Sales Development Representatives regarding the qualified leads that are generated and share leads with vendor partners</li>
<li>Partner with the leadership team to develop innovative ideas to increase sales and improve client experience</li>
<li>Travel to and participate in vendor events and trade shows
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience as a successful Account Executive, or Sales Representative</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of market research, sales and negotiating principles and have the ability to close deals</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Outstanding knowledge of MS Office (with an emphasis on Excel and PowerPoint); knowledge of CRM software (eg. Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.) is preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Excellent communication and presentation skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Can work both independently and as a team</li></ul>
<ul><li>
An ability to build long-lasting business relationships</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Overcome obstacles and challenges with rewarding solutions</li></ul>

Benefits</b></p>
