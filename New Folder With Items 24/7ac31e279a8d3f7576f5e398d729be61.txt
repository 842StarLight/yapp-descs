<h2>Center Store Field Merchandiser</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h4>
<p></p>
<p>The primary purpose of this position is to oversee the team of Center Store Specialists to insure Center Store departments are meeting merchandising expectations and achieving sales and profitability goals in Grocery, Frozen, Dairy, HBC/GM, Adult Beverage and Fuel Stations. Oversight includes but is not limited to Center Store merchandising execution, space planning and execution process, CGO compliance, and DSD support and execution. Frequent independent judgments are essential. The incumbent is also required to perform all tasks in a safe manner consistent with corporate policies and applicable laws.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Uphold the standards and maintain execution of all Center Store merchandising plans and initiatives to meet or exceed the sales and profit objectives of the Company.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Schedule and audit the weekly responsibilities of the Center Store Specialists, providing direction and setting priorities for resolution of action plan issues and stores needing assistance.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develops Center Store merchandising plan in coordination with the Director, Center Store and Merchandising Communication to insure fulfillment of contractual obligations as well as meet customer demand based on weekly and ongoing promotions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborates with the Director, Center Store and Merchandising Communications to ensure effective communication of promotional plans as well as supporting merchandising execution plans to Operations Team via Sales Program, Sales Memorandum, Hot Sheet and various other communication vehicles.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain relationships with local DSD vendors to uphold contractual obligations and maintain store service standards.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Evaluate and report to management an overview on in-stock conditions, CGO effectiveness, plan-o-gram compliance, reset activity and quality and freshness of products in Center Store for the Company.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Oversee the completion of compliance checks assigned department in each store within the district.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Adhere to and enforce all physical safety and food safety regulations; promote goodwill with respect to government agencies and officials.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Evaluate and make recommendations on sales floor and backroom cooler/freezer inventory levels, safety issues and overall conditions to reduce out-of-code and shrink.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide Center Store Specialist resources to District Managers, HR Specialist and Store Managers in the selection and development of Center Store Department Managers, trainees and bench strength candidates within the assigned department.</p>
</li>
</ul>
