<h2>Order Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Order Specialist Job Summary:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As an Order Specialist, you'll be the main purchase order coordinator responsible for order management including analyzing and inputting data, solving problems, and collaborating with business partners in order to ensure order details are communicated promptly and accurately. You'll play a pivotal role in helping secure quality purchases in a timely fashion in support of sales to drive positive results.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Daily Order Entry - Order Processing</li>
<li>
Track Orders</li>
<li>
Order Maintenance</li>
<li>
Order Management and process
</li>
<li>Vendor Outreach
</li>
<li>Generate Return Authorizations/Credit Memos/Replacement Orders</li>
<li>
Update information on Purchase Orders</li>
<li>
Fast passed entry of customers purchase orders</li>
<li>
Work on the phone, email with customers and vendors</li>
<li>
Prepare and sort documents for data entry</li>
<li>
Enter data into database software and checking to ensure the accuracy of the data that has been inputted</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned by Senior Management</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Required Skills:</b></div>
<ul>

Must have 2 years of experience working in an office environment</li>

Strong Data Entry Skills</li>
<li>
Self Starter</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and communication skills with excellent relationship building skills</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Analytical, multi-tasking and detail-oriented</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills</li>
</ul>
