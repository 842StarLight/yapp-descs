<p><b>Auto Truck Group has an outstanding opportunity for a </b><b><i>Production Planner/Scheduler.
</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Production Planner/Scheduler balances complex, diverse stakeholder communication along with in-depth analysis and data management in order to plan, schedule and support production processes ranging from manufacturing, assembly, material handling, inspection and shipping. They serve a pivotal role in ensuring customer orders are processed and shipped in accordance with planned schedules, job specifications, and established quality standards.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What will you do?
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create &amp; Maintain production schedule in order to efficiently balance the use of resources, satisfy customer requirements, and effectively manage risks to production.
</li>
<li>Work closely with production teams to monitor plant performance &amp; status, assist with solving problems, and provide critical input on the identification of resource bottlenecks that impact production.
</li>
<li>Work closely with external stakeholders (sales &amp; account management) to maintain a clear understanding of customer expectations &amp; priorities as well as upcoming sales opportunities. Incorporate these into the schedule while providing advance notice to stakeholders on delivery concerns.
</li>
<li>Monitor status of current &amp; upcoming work to ensure progress according to plan while addressing variances and contingencies in real time.
</li>
<li>In conjunction with Supply Chain team, determine raw material requirements and status of necessary components to support production schedule.
</li>
<li>Generate reporting, visuals and statistics necessary to intuitively communicate performance and production status to production team, sales, and customers while being prepared to educate stakeholders on the use &amp; interpretation of this info.
</li>
<li>Work with internal and external customers to plan first-article, demo, or pilot vehicles and the related customer meetings into the overall schedule.
</li>
<li>Participate in daily production meetings or project meetings to ensure scheduling information and project responsibilities are communicated appropriately to all stakeholders, being prepared to lead meetings as needed.
</li>
<li>Participate &amp; Lead Operations Continuous Improvement Projects to continuously enhance performance.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What are we looking for?
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA or BS degree plus 2 years of planning and/or scheduling experience in an industrial production environment.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated competency in computer software to include databases, spreadsheets, and word processing and MRP/ERP systems.
</li>
<li>Minimum of 2+ years of experience as a Program Planner/Scheduler.
</li>
<li>Strong understanding of Project Management concepts and best practices.
</li>
<li>Advanced capability and experience with MS Office, especially Excel.
</li>
<li>Experience with resource loading and capacity planning theories and concepts.
</li>
</ul>
