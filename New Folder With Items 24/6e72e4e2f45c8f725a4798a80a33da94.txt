<h2>Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Overview:</p>
<p>
Office Manager is a key position at LNS Research responsible for being the go-to person to support all team members. This position is the glue to assist in keeping the team organized and focused to do their best work. The role is perfect for someone who likes variety, fast-paced environment and can think fast on their feet to shift priorities at the blink of an eye. This position is high visibility and will work with all teams including supporting the company Co-Founders.
</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Sales Operations</li>
<li>Manage metrics for individual and team performance including creating tracking systems and reporting to the team</li>
<li>Establish, track and monitor KPIs for deal size, renewal rates, growth, and other overall metrics to monitor the health of the business.</li>
<li>Administrator for HubSpot CRM system including configuration and updates to overall Sales Dashboard, Deal, Contact and other properties, as required.</li>
<li>Assist in Contract signature and creation process including the use of eSignature system.</li>
<li>Creating projects in a project management system for closed contracts and assigning tasks to appropriate team members</li>
<li>Tracking and support of Account Development Plans for Sales team.</li>
<li>Office Administration</li>
<li>Organize company social outings for Cambridge based staff and larger events during company and team off-site meetings</li>
<li>Process office supply orders for Cambridge based staff</li>
<li>Make travel reservations at the request of team members</li>
<li>Event logistics lead for coordinating hotels, meeting space, IT needs and other support services for LNS held client events, including financial analysis and proposals.</li>
<li>Schedule company wide meetings and maintain holiday schedule</li>
<li>Presentation review and creation, when requested</li>
<li>Schedule meetings for co-Founders, when requested</li>
<li>Process company postal mail and bank deposits</li>
<li>Order business cards for new hires and others when needed.</li>
<li>Internal IT</li>
<li>Assist in maintaining software vendor relationships.</li>
<li>Administrator for internal IT systems and provide appropriate access to identified employees, contractors, vendors and customers, as needed.</li>
<li>Responsible for oversight of IT help desk services and working with Leadership Team on related procurement, repair, and replacement of hardware.</li>
<li>Facilities Management</li>
<li>Oversees the coordination of building space allocation and layout, communication services and facility expansion.</li>
<li>Responsible for coordinating receiving, maintenance needs, parking, and cleaning services.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain positive relationships with vendors, contractors, and other outsourced functions.</li>
<li>Manage a replacement plan and purchasing for equipment and furniture needs, as needed.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Minimum Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma with 4+ years of relevant experience.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, self-directed team environment.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and balance multiple projects.</li>
<li>Competent in MS Office products, web conferencing services and efficient in email and internet applications.</li>
</ul>
