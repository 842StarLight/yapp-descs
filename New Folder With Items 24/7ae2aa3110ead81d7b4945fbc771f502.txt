<b>Position Overview</b>
<br>
<br>
The Microapps Pre-Sales Specialist possesses deep technical expertise in a targeted technology domain and drives business for their given areas of specialization. The Microapps Specialist aligns with supporting sales professionals and pre-sales engineers throughout the Americas and are responsible for identifying and matching technology opportunities with the customers business issues, objectives, and outcomes to drive product sales. If you are a self-starting, problem solver with a knack for sales and a passion for technology, then this is the role for you. If you are passionate about combining your technical expertise with your understanding of the customers business needs to solve complex problems. You have at least 5+ years pre-sales experience in enterprise technology, ideally in Virtualization, Security, Digital Workspaces, Citrix Workspace or SaaS. This is a remote-based position with travel to customer locations or corporate meetings, as needed. You will cover all accounts across the Americas. You will be the face of Citrix for our customers while serving as the face of our customers to our product teams, and you will help propel Citrix by driving the companys revenue and growth.
