<h2>HVAC Controls Project Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Don t miss the opportunity to join a growing, full service contractor and supplier of Building Automation Controls Systems (DDC) in beautiful Casper, WY!</b> Our client seeks energetic team members who strive to succeed and make a difference in a hands on, customer focused environment. Their greatest asset is their team of dedicated, talented employees who work hard to provide unmatched services to their clients. They are seeking an experienced BAS/DDC Technician with experience overseeing HVAC Control system installations, programming and startup/commissioning. <b>Relocation candidates welcome to apply, and assistance will be provided!</b></p>
<p>
Duties may include visiting customer sites to program and troubleshoot building automation systems, developing drawing packages for projects, and commissioning systems. Additional responsibilities may include supporting engineers in researching part numbers and manufacturers, and assisting the Sales Team to develop system drawings in support of technical proposals.</p>
<p><b>
Compensation: </b>Competitive compensation package includes base salary starting at $75,000.</p>
<p><b>
Basic Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Three or more years experience in project engineering/programming and commissioning HVAC control systems</li>
<li>Experience Implementing software code and managing all aspects of the software code delivery and commissioning for commercial Building Automation System (BAS) projects.</li>
<li>Ability to program projects with various levels of complexity.</li>
<li>Capable of performing network troubleshooting, database troubleshooting and repair programming.</li>
<li>Should be proficient with BACnet, Lon and Modbus with BAS integration experience.</li>
<li>Tridium/Niagara experience desired.</li>
<li>The ability to work independently, as well as on a project team.</li>
</ul>
<p>Company offers an outstanding compensation package that includes a competitive salary, company vehicle, full benefits and retirement plan. Pay is commensurate with experience. You will be required to complete a background check, drug test and have a valid driver s license.</p>
