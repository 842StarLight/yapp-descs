<div><b>About the Role</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Our Senior Managers of Enterprise Sales will build and manage experienced acquisition and expansion sales teams focused on partnering with large global enterprise shippers to solve their logistics and freight challenges. The candidate should have experience building and managing world-class enterprise sales teams and be familiar with modern selling methodologies.</div>
<div></div>
<br>

They are motivated by building the future, are a proven manager and leader, can dig deep into the data, drive their team to deliver, and have a true entrepreneurial spirit.</div>

 <b>What You’ll Do</b>
</div>
<ul>
