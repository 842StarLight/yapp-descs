<p><b>
Job Summary</b><br>
</p>
<p>
This position is responsible for the dispatch function, coordinating with the sales department, shipping, receiving, customer, delivery truck services and the logistics operations.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Inspect all loads as a last and final check</li>
<li>
Assist the Transportation Manager in performing all tasks necessary to ensure that all vehicles and drivers are safe and in compliance with all legal and regulatory agencies</li>
<li>
Verify specials</li>
<li>
Receive/verify incoming materials for facility</li>
<li>
Serve as the will call expediter</li>
<li>
Maintain the yard in an orderly fashion</li>
<li>
Schedule workload</li>
<li>
Work closely with the production to the team to determine items ready for delivery</li>
<li>
Coordinate daily delivery schedules based on sale’s request for product and product availability</li>
<li>
Operating heavy equipment as needed</li>
<li>
Other duties may be assigned as needed</li>
</ul>
