<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Responsible for acting as a contact person for banquet functions. Also responsible for supervising banquet staff, ensuring that functions are set correctly, helping with paperwork, linen inventory and training of staff.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Job Overview</div>
<div>
The purpose of this position is to manage, plan and direct the banquet operations including service, and set-up of service. The position will report to the Banquet Manager and will partner closely with the Catering/Sales and Culinary departments to ensure the banquet operations can meet the demands of the clients successfully. General Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li>Oversees all banquet service operations and ensures that all banquet service details are executed. Assist in ensuring that the events are on time. Make adjustments as needed and relay that information to the appropriate personnel.</li>
<li>Review Banquet Event Orders (BEOâ€™s) at the beginning of each shift to ensure room are set as contracted. Confirm and setup A/V equipment needs to contract for the event.</li>
<li>Responsible for the performance of the banquet service staff, including direct supervision of the banquet staff. Provides leadership to the staff in the absence of the Banquet Manager</li>
<li>Collaborate with Catering/Sales, Food &amp; Beverage managers and the Culinary team to ensure that quality service is provided for all meeting and banquet guests.</li>
<li>Manage the cost and quality control of banquet services.</li>
<li>Be an active liaison between the guests and other hotel staff to ensure outstanding customer service while maintaining company procedures and complying with company policies</li>
<li>Maintain all equipment and facilities in an organized, clean, safe environment.</li>
<li>Communicate any areas of need, problems, and concerns from guest to Banquet Manager</li>
<li>Perform monthly inventories and order supplies as needed in collaboration with the Banquet Manager</li>
<li>Work closely with Guest Services and Maintenance to schedule and perform transportation of supplies and equipment as needed throughout the Resort</li>
<li>Complete administrative tasks as assigned by Banquet Manager</li>
</ul>
