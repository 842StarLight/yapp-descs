<h2>Director of Product Marketing, Cloud & Application Security (AMMK602)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Overview</b>
</p>
<div>This Director of Product Marketing role will be responsible for key outbound marketing activities for Fortinet’s Mail and cloud security solutions, which covers security for private cloud, public cloud and SaaS applications. The successful individual will drive product and solution messaging that positions Fortinet as a market and thought leader in key enterprise segments.<br>
</div>
<div>
<br>
Fortinet sees Cloud Security as one of its main growth opportunities that will accelerate its consistent growth even further, Mail is a key component of enterprises moving away from managing their own mail infrastructure. By crafting and driving the mail &amp; cloud stories, The Product Marketing Director will have the opportunity to make a difference and play a key role in the company’s future growth.</div>
<div>
<br>
Responsibilities include product messaging and positioning, launching new product and solutions, writing and developing whitepapers and collateral, presenting at key industry events, developing sales and partner enablement toolkits, developing GTM strategy and pricing, implementing sales incentives, training sales and partners, contributing articles, blogs and thought leadership pieces, and other product-related outbound marketing activities. The position requires the ability to develop strongly differentiated product, platform and solutions positioning and messaging.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>REQUIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>10+ years experience in Product Management, Product Marketing, Solution Marketing or related roles</li>
<li>At least 3-5 years experience with web application security and cloud-related technologies - such as v, infrastructure-as-a-service, SaaS – required, familiarity with key vendors and platforms such as AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud, VMware NSX, and Cisco ACI.
</li>
<li>Experience with information Security products, Mail Security, NGFW, WAF, CASB, Sandbox.</li>
<li>Be results driven and have the ability to manage projects horizontally throughout the organization.</li>
<li>Demonstrates creative energy and strong problem solving skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal communication and writing proficiency</li>
<li>The Ability to conceive, initiate, develop and sell new ideas in a team environment</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to credibly engage with technical and sales communities</li>
<li>Strong internal/external presentation skills with energetic presence</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or a Technical field is required, MBA preferred</li>
</ul>
</div>
