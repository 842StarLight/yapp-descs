<div>
Microsoft’s <b>Consumer and Device Sales (CDS)</b> organization works with ecosystem partners to build, market and sell a winning portfolio that delights customers and earns fans around the world. One of the largest and most profitable business at Microsoft, the CDS organization is charged with leading Windows consumer &amp; commercial, Xbox gaming, Surface, Office, new business Growth &amp; IoT. If you’re passionate about impacting the global ecosystem of partners and influencing the customer experience on the hundreds of millions of devices that ship every year, then this is the team for you.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The<b> CDS Internet of Things (IoT) Sales Team</b> is sitting at the intersect of the Intelligent Edge and Intelligent Cloud, enabling Microsoft Partners to leverage all of Microsoft’s assets and platforms in the transformation of their businesses and industries through Intelligent Edge-to-Cloud solutions. We are the Intelligent Edge specialist team, driving scale impact through partner enablement, across System Integrators, ISVs, OEMs and Solution Aggregators and more. From Windows IoT, Azure Sphere, Mixed Reality to Azure IoT, we drive partner success from inception, through solution creation to solution co-sell.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Make history at Microsoft by joining the world’s first <b>Defense &amp; Intelligence Mixed Reality sales organization</b>. With the success of HoloLens V1, combined with leading 1st party SaaS, PaaS &amp; 3rd party ISV &amp; SI solutions, we’re taking Mixed Reality interactive experiences to a whole new level for mission critical LoB with HoloLens 2, Azure Kinect and the entire portfolio of Intelligent Edge solutions for Defense &amp; Intelligence customers and partners.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We’re looking for a passionate, competent <b>Mixed Reality Specialist</b> with a proven successful track record of sales success selling into US and/or International Defense and Intelligence entities. Top candidates will have strong solution sales experience in business applications and enterprise device sales. All candidates must have deep relevant industry line of business sales within the Defense sector. The ideal candidate has a deep understanding of the highly relevant use cases with experience selling and creating customer success with augmented reality and possess a good general understanding of how this category applies to the unique customer requirements in this sector. Leading candidates will also understand how technologies like IoT, AI &amp; Mixed Reality fundamentally change how mission execution finds a whole new realm of success through cutting edge digitally transformation. All candidates must also possess deep experience building and managing a large enterprise customer pipeline collaborating deeply with account managers and other solution and technical specialists accurately identifying and qualifying high value opportunities.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Mixed Reality Defense Specialist has an established track record of building tremendously positive rapport across their direct team and can quickly &amp; easily build respect with everyone from customer &amp; internal business unit executives to CxO leaders to IT professionals. Successful MR Specialists can get everyone on the same page across disparate teams demonstrating customer obsession through leadership by example.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As a seasoned solution sales professional, the ideal candidate is also demonstrably capable of consistently helping their team find the right 1st and 3rd party solutions that map to the specific customer needs with every sale and can speak fluently about our cloud SaaS &amp; PaaS offerings paired with ground breaking devices such as HoloLens 2 coupled with the right Defense SI or ISV fit.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Show us you’re the one by providing concise examples of your sales and sales management skill explaining a product and platform strategy as an industry grade solution sale in Mixed Reality. Demonstrate how you work fluidly with both technical and business leaders sharing examples of credible technical conversations you’ve had with other Defense customers to prepare them for important technology investments. This role also provides engineering and business groups a direct channel to customer requirements with a highly nuanced understanding of the specific mission requirements of this customer with VR &amp; AR technologies and maintain a comprehensive inventory of established and emerging competitors, partners and developers. You must be able to show us relevant experience and explain your plan to learn everything you need to be successful in this critical paradigm shift in technology.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The ideal candidate does the right things, the right way, efficiently collaborating across our enterprise sales organization and customer success units persistently while:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Understanding the technical and line of business Solution Selling strategies required to reach line of business decision makers and know what it means to qualify an opportunity the right way, the first time</li>
<li>
Making others great across a diverse team of high capacity professionals inclusively while preparing to scale the business for significantly broader growth and coverage</li>
<li>
Knowing how important it is to network with the right people on the account team, engineering, services &amp; product management both directly and indirectly across multiple stakeholders at both line and leadership levels consistently</li>
<li>
Demonstrating clear leadership by example thru deep customer obsession where your actions speak louder than words with borderless collaboration and always with a growth mindset</li>
<li>
Removing technical &amp; business sales blockers identified by your team with engineering and business groups.</li>
<li>
Driving a strategic portfolio of showcase opportunities thru the team from proof-of-concept to pilot to enterprise deployment while prioritizing your precious time working across some of the largest and most complex Defense organizations in the world in close partnership with your direct and extended account team members</li>
<li>
Working side-by-side with early adopter customers and partners whom you’ve matched to capture feedback and insights to help inform the product groups plans for broad distribution</li>
<li>
Serving as both technical &amp; business advisor for your direct and indirect teams for Microsoft’s 1st &amp; 3rd party Mixed Reality solutions across all relevant field communities within Microsoft</li>
