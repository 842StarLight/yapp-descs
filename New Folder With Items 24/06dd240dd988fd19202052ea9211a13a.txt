<p><b>TRUMARK FINANCIAL CREDIT UNION</b><br>
<b>JOB DESCRIPTION<br>
</b></p>
<p><br>
<b>Job Title:</b> Marketing Communications Coordinator<br>
<b>Department:</b> Marketing<br>
<b>Reports To:</b> AVP, Marketing<b>
</b><br>
<b>FLSA Status:</b> Exempt MT 40<br>
<b>Prepared Date:</b> September 2019<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>ACCOUNTABILITY/OBJECTIVE:</b><br>
Responsible for creating, coordinating, and executing marketing initiatives in order to achieve<br>
credit union goals and build awareness of TruMark Financial in the communities served.<br>
Responsible for maintaining a clear message and a consistent brand image across all media.<br>
<b>
</b><br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
</p>
<p>1. Coordinate activities with agencies, media-buying firms, direct mail firms, telemarketing<br>
firms, designers, photographers, A/V houses, printers, computer programmers, and<br>
directly, if needed, with various sources of advertising media, etc.
</p>
<p>2. Coordinate tactics to enhance the credit union’s brand awareness in the marketplace.<br>
3. Track and report on campaign performance.<br>
4. Perform complete design process from conceptualization to delivery for internal and
</p>
<p>external communications using Adobe Creative Suite and/or other design applications
<br>
5. Implement traditional marketing campaigns (i.e., direct mail, email, newspaper) and
</p>
<p>surveys and report results
<br>
6. Researches, writes, and edits promotional messaging<br>
7. Coordinates all direct mail efforts including: design, list, print, scheduling, and tracking
<br>
8. Assist with branch-opening promotions and other special events.<br>
9. Develops, maintains, and manages external website and promotional landing pages
</p>
<p>including copywriting, content development, design layout, basic HTML editing, and site<br>
map management.
</p>
<p>10. Collaborates with the credit union’s Digital Marketing Coordinator to enhance the<br>
website’s search engine optimization
</p>
<p>11. Researches, develops, and implements new marketing campaigns and other various<br>
projects which impact the marketing department, the credit union, and/or its members.
</p>
<p>12. Manages the work of graphic designers to ensure the quality of creative content and<br>
timeliness.
</p>
<p>13. Maintain working knowledge of financial products and services.<br>
14. Reviews and edits other departments’ and employees recurring or mass communications
</p>
<p>to ensure quality, effective communications, and consistency with trademark and style<br>
guidelines including notification letters and regulatory communiqués.
</p>
<p>15. Obtains, summarizes, and provides research on variety of projects which impact on the<br>
marketing department, the credit union, or its members.
</p>
<p>16. Remains active in credit union training programs. Complete the position’s present<br>
curriculum as outlined by completing the following – credit union annual required
</p>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<p>compliance training and the supplemental courses listed within the prescribed<br>
time. Follows policy and procedure daily to ensure compliant with current regulations.
</p>
<p>17. Completes all other tasks, assignments or functions as assigned and/or required.<br>
18. Communicate marketing initiatives and relation operational procedures to internal
</p>
<p>constituents in a clear and timely manner.<br>
<b>
