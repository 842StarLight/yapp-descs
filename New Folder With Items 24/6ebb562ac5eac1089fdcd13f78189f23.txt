<h2>Senior Project Manager/Market Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>EnviroVantage Inc. Epping, NH</b></p>
<p>
As the leading New England Environmental Specialty &amp; Demolition Contractor with over 30 years of experience, EnviroVantage is recognized as the trusted authority for commercial, industrial, government and residential projects across New England and the Mid-Atlantic. The highly trained staff at EnviroVantage is looking to add to our team as we continue to grow.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Reports to:</b></p>
<p>
COO &amp; CIO</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
JOB DESCRIPTION</p>
<p>
Essential Functions</p>
<ul>
<li>
Project management of large abatement/demolition/environmental remediation projects.
</li>
<li>Preparation and management of project budgets, schedules, labor, disposal, cost accounting requirements and quality controls.
</li>
<li>Preparation of technical proposals, reports and evaluations.</li>
<li>
Responsible for managing projects in their entirety including spearheading all communication with the client, subcontractors, regulators, and ensuring daily/weekly progress reporting on production is accurate and reported back to the EnviroVantage leadership team</li>
<li>
Read, analyze and interpret drawings, blueprints and project documents</li>
<li>
Ability to adhere to all OSHA; EPA; as well as other federal agency, state and company hazardous materials laws and policies.
</li>
<li>Be a strong team leader/player with a positive attitude who is hard working and motivated every day</li>
<li>
Build Relationships with existing and new customers</li>
<li>
Seek out and pursue new project opportunities within EV's existing Business Development and Sales strategy</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITY</p>
<p>
This position is responsible for managing large abatement/demolition/environmental remediation projects, supervising subcontractors, and managing supervisory staff.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
POSITION TYPE</p>
<p>
This is a salary exempt position.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
TRAVEL</p>
<p>
Travel within New England<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE
</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 10 15 years working as a Project Manager in the construction and environmental remediation fields required.</li>
<li>
Experience with all OSHA; EPA; as well as other federal agency, state and company hazardous materials laws and policies required
</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree required</li>
<li>
Microsoft Office, Word, Excel and Projects proficiency required</li>
<li>
Exemplary written and oral communication skills required</li>
<li>
Valid driver's license and clean driving record required</li>
<li>
Security clearance to gain access to US Department of Defense DOD and other federal facilities required</li>
<li>
Estimating software experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
CERTIFICATIONS
</p>
<ul>
<li>OSHA 40-Hour Hazardous Waste Operations and Emergency Response HAZWOPER training, OSHA 29 CFR 1910.120 required</li>
<li>
OSHA 8-Hour HAZWOPER Training Refresher if necessary, OSHA 29 CFR 1910 required</li>
<li>
OSHA 30-Hour Construction Training, OSHA 29 CFR 1926 required</li>
</ul>
