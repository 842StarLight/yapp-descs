Tasks
<br><br>
The following list includes a sample of the tasks a person in this role would need to perform.
<br><br>
Calibrates radiation detection equipment using ionizing radiation sources/techniques
<br><br>
Assures customer satisfaction and on time delivery of calibrated devices
<br><br>
Able to meet production rates
<br><br>
Reviews generated analytical data before assignment of official calibration certificate
<br><br>
Provides training to other laboratory personnel as required.
<br><br>
Collects documentation, performs evaluations, and corrects anomalies in calibration laboratory activities and under guidance implements preventative measures to eliminate reoccurrence of incidents.
<br><br>
Conducts surveillance of operational aspects and associated work activities of laboratory operations personnel and implements corrective actions as defined.
<br><br>
Required to maintain neat, accurate and complete records and files.
<br><br>
Must be able to work overtime as needed.
<br><br>
Must be able to utilize known quantities to calibrate equipment as assigned.
<br><br>
Must be able to work under general supervision and independently
<br><br>
Must have general computer knowledge and experience with various software programs (e.g., word processing and spreadsheets).
<br><br>
Bachelor degree in life sciences or comparable qualification.
<br><br>
<b>Experience:</b><br>
Two years of relevant experience as a laboratory technician
