We have an exciting leadership opportunity on the global IT Security team at TJX!<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The <b>Vice President, Security Services </b>will work alongside our Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) to build up the service capability within the IT Security function at TJX. We are excited to have the opportunity to bring on an operator with a deep understanding of the technology transformation occurring in the Cyber Security landscape; a leader capable of helping to build the strategic framework that guides Security governance and risk management decisions across the organization.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Lead a team responsible for understanding the business &amp; technology needs of various verticals throughout TJX, and ensure those needs are met in a secure and compliant way</li>
<li>
Manage the IT Security Product Portfolio for delivery of the IT Security Strategy, core security services, and Security Consulting</li>
<li>
Provide a link between various IT security groups and business stakeholder to ensure that IT Security is prepared for the foreseeable demands on the team</li>
<li>
Assume a key role in developing, implementing, and monitoring strategies to achieve the organization’s long-term goals and objectives.</li>
<li>
Develop, track, and assess key performance measures across the IT Security Organization. Develop management reports.</li>
<li>
Support company-wide, board/executive level, and internal / external communication and presentation requests.</li>
<li>
Overseeing general day-to-day operations of the IT Security Organization.</li>
</ul>
