<h2>Supply Chain Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position overview:</b></h2>
<p>This role requires a high level of analysis, reporting and hands-on application within each department of operations to ensure supply chain excellence. This position provides leadership and support to key positions within the operations team. This includes, but is not limited to: shipping, inventory, planning and procurement as needed.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Support Operations, customer Service, and sales team members in all aspects of day-to-day operations to ensure that all supply chain needs are met.</li>
<li>Ensure all material requisition orders, sales orders, and transfer warehouse orders are fulfilled on a day to day basis.</li>
<li>Understand and execute all supply chain activities such as stock take, physical inventory count, inventory accuracy in allocation and consumption, material demand, and purchase order entry as needed.</li>
<li>Maintain our work order management system</li>
<li>Keep an updated status of production work orders and material commitment</li>
<li>Assess and manage inventory levels to ensure supply will meet forecasted needs</li>
<li>Work with project managers, product development, customer service, procurement, and sales to provide delivery dates and shipping information</li>
<li>Manage shipping and receiving department</li>
<li>Maintain and update ERP system with accurate data entry.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years of supply chain and operations experience</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years demonstrated experience in inventory management</li>
<li>Familiarity with Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software, and/or Material Requirements Planning (MRP) software.</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with logistics, inventory management, and procurement.</li>
<li>Purchasing experience</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Traits &amp; Abilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>The 4 H’s: honest, humble, hungry, happy</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and highly detailed-oriented</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Self-starting multi-tasker</li>
<li>Appetite for problem solving</li>
<li>Dependable</li>
<li>Team player</li>
<li>Continuous improvement mindset</li>
<li>Eagerness to learn</li>
</ul>
