<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What will you be doing?
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Build and manage a pipeline of mobile app prospects based in the <i>East/West</i> region.</li>
<li>
Ensure detailed and accurate qualification of advertisers.</li>
<li>
Present, educate and evangelize Criteo’s App Advertising products with the aim of achieving significant revenue growth.</li>
<li>
Deliver against an established set of performance KPI’s including IO’s, Revenue and advertiser performance goals.</li>
<li>
Develop long standing customer relationships through consultative sales and effective account management at all levels of the client’s organization including VP, Marketing, UA and Growth Managers.</li>
<li>
Partner with a broad range of internal teams (marketing, account strategy, legal, tech solutions, creative and analytics) to support the sales efforts.</li>
<li>
Work strategically to meet and exceed customer and organizational goals.
</li>
</ul></div>

</li>
