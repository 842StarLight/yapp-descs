<h2>Director, Audience Development & Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Condé Nast is a global media company producing the highest quality content with a footprint of more than 1 billion consumers in 32 territories through print, digital, video and social platforms. The company’s portfolio includes many of the world’s most respected and influential media properties including Vogue, Vanity Fair, Glamour, Self, GQ, The New Yorker, Condé Nast Traveler/Traveller, Allure, AD, Bon Appétit and Wired, among others. Condé Nast Entertainment was launched in 2011 to develop film, television and premium digital video programming.
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h1>
<p>Come and work for Vogue, one of the most respected music brands in the world. The Director of Audience Development &amp; Analytics will partner with site leads to craft initiatives that introduce our content to new readers and build loyalty with our existing audience. She or he will be responsible for developing and implementing a content distribution strategy that will extend the reach and amplification across newsletters, multiple social networks, search and more. Using analytics, research, and insights, the Director will also inform the content and editorial strategy across all aspects of Vogue. Finally, this person should also have an eye for revenue, with an understanding of how display, subscription, e-commerce, and partnerships come together to move the business forward.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will be someone who deeply understands the digital media industry, is highly analytical, and is nimble and creative with the ability to incorporate data and testing into the decision-making process. We need people who have an entrepreneurial spirit, an inherent understanding of the web, and an eye for strategy. Curiosity, motivation and rigor are a must, and a love for music is of course a plus.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Primary Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Collaborate with brand stakeholders to develop and execute data-driven audience growth plans across all content distribution platforms
</p></li>
<li><p>Lead brand analytics team and define measurement and testing strategy
</p></li>
<li><p>Lead brand social strategy and staff, applying best practices to content strategy that strengthen and optimize both the website and social media footprint
</p></li>
<li><p>Liaise with Managing Editor on packaging of special projects and digital cover stories, work on editorial social strategy
</p></li>
<li><p>Work closely with digital leadership to determine and meet growth KPIs and ensure they align with revenue strategy
</p></li>
<li><p>Work cross-functionally with Editorial leads, Business Development, Marketing, Sales, Product, Research, and other central strategy groups to identify opportunities, execute brand initiatives, and maximize customer monetization
</p></li>
<li><p>Represent Vogue as needed at product, branded, and marketing meetings
</p></li>
<li><p>Liaise with senior editors weekly on growth opportunities in editorial tied to targeted trends, topics, and initiatives
</p></li>
<li><p>Give regular morning meeting updates to the staff on trending topics and other data-driven content opportunities
</p></li>
<li><p>Identify new growth opportunities and best practices based on emerging digital trends, competitive business intelligence, and consumer insight
</p></li>
<li><p>Develop and experiment with new content executions onsite and in emerging distribution platforms
 </p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Required Skills:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Undergraduate degree in related field (marketing, analytics, communications, science, business, finance, accounting, or statistics) required. Graduate degree a plus.
</p></li>
<li><p>7+ years of experience in the media and entertainment industry, digital advertising agency or online marketing, advertising, or publishing organization, with a thorough understanding of online business models and strategies.
</p></li>
<li><p>2+ years of experience managing teams.
</p></li>
<li><p>Detailed knowledge of major web analytics tool such as Google Analytics, Omniture, Parsely, and/or Chartbeat
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience with a multivariate testing tool (e.g. Adobe Test &amp; Target, Optimizely) to optimize marketing and user experience initiatives
</p></li>
<li><p>Solid understanding of social and mobile channels, and deep familiarity with social analytics tools
</p></li>
<li><p>Deep understanding of all audience development channels - SEO, social, newsletter, and partnerships
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to clearly articulate and present analyses to all levels of management, including c-suite executives and Editorial leadership
</p></li>
<li><p>Entrepreneurial, digitally savvy, with exceptional analytical, quantitative, problem- solving, and critical thinking skills
</p></li>
</ul>
