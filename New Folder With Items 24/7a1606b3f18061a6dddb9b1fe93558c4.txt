<h2>Sales Executive, Senior</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>What does a great Senior Sales Executive, Dovetail, do?</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Fiserv’s Dovetail segment is a leading provider of bank payments and liquidity management solutions. Dovetail offers financial institutions around the world to transform their payments infrastructure to meet the evolving needs of wholesale, commercial and retail customers. Dovetail enables banks to differentiate their payments offerings and reduce their costs. Fiserv’s acquisition of Dovetail in 2018 has since contributed to the product’s rapid growth over the past two years.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a Regional Sales Executives to be based in North America. We are looking for individuals who can engage directly with C level executives in a named territory to ensure a successful sales campaign of complex multi-million-dollar solutions.</p>
<p><br>
In this role they will need to understand prospect's business and technical needs, prepare and give company and product presentations aimed at driving demand for Dovetail's products. The Sales Executive will be responsible for providing prospects with business and product advice, preparing RFI/ RFP response and proposals as needed, and closing business. Internally at Dovetail the Sales Executive is responsible for the coordination of sales campaigns using resources from both within the sales organization, and from other internal departments.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key activities expected to be performed include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Build relationships with the key executives at assigned prospects across the operations, technology and product management functions.</li>
<li>
Understand the bank's capabilities, needs and plans from both a technical and business perspectives and business justifications for making payment systems investments</li>
<li>
Communicate Dovetail solutions in a benefit-oriented way focusing on client needs</li>
<li>
Personally, call on every assigned bank in the territory</li>
<li>
Maintain accuracy and up to date forecasts using Fiserv CRM system including documented needs analysis and sales campaign decision analysis and planning</li>
<li>
Liaison with other departments within Dovetail (e.g. Product Management, Implementation, Marketing, Development, etc.) to lead a virtual account team</li>
<li>
Travel as required to prospect and customer locations in the region on a frequent basis</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience.</li></ul>
<p></p>

Ability to develop proposals and presentations for customers in a way that adds value to the sales process; strong written and verbal communication skills are a mandatory requirement</li>
