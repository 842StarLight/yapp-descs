<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job description</b></h2>
<div>
<p>
As an Account Executive, you will report to our VP Sales North America</p>
<div>
<b>
Here are your tasks:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ownership of the full sales cycle from lead to close</li>
<li>Initiate, negotiate and close new and renewal contracts with customers</li>
<li>Establish lasting relationships with senior executives and decision-makers</li>
<li>Navigate key decision makers to build Simplifield awareness within organizations</li>
<li>Foster new relationships for Simplifield through outbound efforts</li>
<li>Identify customer needs and collaborate with Simplifield teammates to ensure customer success</li>
<li>Serve as the voice of the customer and collect feedback to drive continuous improvement across all areas including product</li>
</ul>
</div>
