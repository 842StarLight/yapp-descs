<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Summary</b></h1>
<p>
This is a skilled position requiring the employee to perform a wide variety of credit and customer service functions for the accounting department.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>
The position will be required to contact customers for payments, negotiate acceptable payment arrangements, and collect past due balances and meet company DSO expectations.</li>
<li>
Position will interact with dispatch, sales, and billing to resolve customer issues as they may arise.</li>
<li>
The position will draft pre-lien notices research and file mechanic’s liens, complete lien waivers and provide assistance to the Credit Specialist in credit approval process.</li>
<li>
Position will require the employee to research accounts with disputes and make the appropriate adjustments.</li>
</ul>
