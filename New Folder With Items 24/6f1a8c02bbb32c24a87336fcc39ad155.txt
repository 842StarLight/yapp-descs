<h2>Contract Negotiator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>The Contracts Negotiator will provide negotiation support for customer agreements in assigned territories/verticals. This role will provide vital legal, commercial, and operational support to our Sales, Services, Cloud and Sales Operations teams during mainly customer negotiations. In this role you will have an opportunity to influence and participate in the growth of the business.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>This person is responsible for negotiating, drafting changes, reviewing, structuring, and administrating agreements that establish business relationships with customers and/or partners</li>
<li>This person will be responsible for analyzing sales transactions to ensure compliance to company policy (including the Kronos finance principles), applicable laws/regulations and other established guidelines and requirements, as well as resolving customer and Field Sales inquiries to achieve high-level customer satisfaction in a fast-paced deadline driven environment</li>
<li>This person is flexible to work on “ad hoc” projects and other responsibilities within the legal department
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5-7+ years prior experience negotiating terms and conditions, drafting contractual provisions, preparing contracts for review and ensuring compliance with company policy as well as applicable laws/regulations</li>
<li>Experience with procurement processes, such as RFP (Request for Proposal) process, negotiation of contracts, drafting contractual clauses and use of alternative contracting vehicles preferred (through partner or re-sellers)</li>
<li>Experience negotiating and drafting software, Software as a Service (SaaS), or services contracts is preferred</li>
<li>Ability to utilize time efficiently to meet multiple deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to work in an independent yet collaborative environment</li>
</ul>
