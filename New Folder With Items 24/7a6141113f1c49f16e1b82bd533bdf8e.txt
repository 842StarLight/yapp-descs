<p>Online Sales Specialist (Marysville)</p>
<p>Looking for an Inside Sales Associate to manage our online sales and marketing initiatives. This position responds to, qualifies, and manages leads and customer requests for new homes, and reports directly to our Sales Manager.</p>
<p>A Training and mentoring program will be provided.</p>
<p>Primary Job Responsibilities:</p>
<p>Respond to all email and phone leads immediately<br>Qualify the leads based on needs<br>Provide consistent follow-up to online prospects with the goal of setting an appointment with the Onsite Sales Agent<br>Provide information and marketing material to prospects<br>Respond to the sales line and voicemail for leads<br>Work with the prospect to determine the best community and floor plan<br>Utilize lead-management software<br>Create and deliver ongoing email campaigns<br>Track and report on lead activity and sales conversion ratios<br>Monitor third-party websites and online advertising<br>Continually research the competition and their online activities<br>Additional responsibilities as needed</p>
<p>Job Qualifications:</p>
<p>Two years of customer service experience<br>Customer focused and personable<br>Proven email and phone communication skills<br>Sales contact management database experience<br>Solid organizational, planning and managing skills<br>Maintain focus while working in an unstructured environment with nominal supervision<br>Ability to multi-task and manage time well<br>Must be able to learn and follow proven sales process</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 2 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
