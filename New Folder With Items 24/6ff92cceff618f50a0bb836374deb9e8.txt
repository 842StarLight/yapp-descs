<b>The role:</b><br>
DISQO is hiring a Lead PHP Engineer to manage a team of Engineers on implementing state of the art technologies; laying the foundation for all future development and scalability. This role reports into the Director of Engineering and will be responsible for DISQO's main application, Survey Junkie.
<br><br>
Through Survey Junkie, DISQO gathers attitudinal and behavioral data, conducting 1.5 MM+ surveys each month. With hundreds of thousands of users, every update has an immediate impact on our users. Survey Junkie users are based in the U.S., Australia and Canada.
<br><br>
Working closely with Product, this role will be responsible for applying best practices to ensure our users have an amazing in app experience, introduce improved features, reduce cycle time and enable the marketing and sales teams to be more effective.
