<p><b>Position Description:</b><br>
</p>
<p>An ideal SOA tester would have the analytical skills and Kroger awareness to validate the engine's results. SOA tester will provide the support in developing SOA/integration interface test plans and estimates, automated test script development, executing service test scripts, reporting/tracking defects. The tester may also contribute to functional testing as needed by the team. The Quality Engineer is responsible for managing quality and test related process execution with high impact to the business. This includes providing feedback on artifacts developed during requirements and analysis phase within EDGE and other R&amp;D projects, authoring test plans and test automation, and performing test execution for both project and support related activities. The tester should be able to work on different protocals and API's</p>
