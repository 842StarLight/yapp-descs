<h2>ACCOUNT MANAGER, COMMERCIAL PROPERTY CASUALTY INSURANCE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you a Commercial Property &amp; Casualty insurance professional passionate about serving your customers? Have you ever worked for a company whose motto is “Work hard, play hard, and love who you do it with”; who’s passionate about challenging you to think differently; and who cares about YOU the employee?</p>
<p>
This describes Holmes Murphy to a “T” as well as one of our latest job openings! We are looking for candidates that would be the perfect fit to join our team as an <b>Account Manager</b> in the Property Casualty department of our Kansas City, Missouri office. If this sounds like your dream job, keep reading!</p>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Prepares certificates, binders, auto ID cards and accident kits.</li>
<li>Processes endorsements by checking coverages, invoices and updates computer systems.</li>
<li>Orders and suspends policy changes.</li>
<li>Follows established procedures for tracking policies to be checked and sent to clients.</li>
<li>Participates in renewal planning with team.</li>
<li>Maintains follow-up system for receipt of audits.</li>
<li>Reviews, analyzes and summarizes various types of data including audits, using standardized formats.</li>
<li>Reviews, analyzes and checks experience mods using available software (modmaster, NCCI.)</li>
<li>Investigates and corrects omitted items and commission differences on audits and endorsements.</li>
<li>Maintains monthly reporting forms and follows up with Consultants, as needed.</li>
<li>Assists or prepares client renewal PSR document and updates application, as needed.</li>
<li>Prepares client renewal review documents to include copies of the PSR, applications, experience mod worksheet, loss runs and summary, schedules, and any other pertinent information.</li>
<li>Invoices new and renewal policies, monthly installments, direct bill, and agency bill, and ensure the accuracy of this invoicing.</li>
<li>Accountable for account accuracy by ensuring that on-line customer information as well as items received by carriers are accurate and current, including coverage schedules, policy formats, policy endorsements, premium and the follow-up on pending changes.</li>
<li>Knows and uses various carrier websites for quoting purposes.</li>
<li>Prepares policies for delivery to customers and for our office records.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains positive work relationships with team members to include: participating in team meetings, providing back up when necessary, communicating information and effectively problem-solving as situations arise.</li>
<li>Orders loss runs.</li>
<li>Can prepare loss summaries.</li>
<li>Receives and processes carrier downloads.</li>
</ul> <p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> General knowledge of property casualty coverages and the ability to apply that knowledge in the performance of the job duties.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and the ability to apply these skills in either verbal or written form when interacting with various clients, company personnel, and team members.</li>
<li>Experience with computer data entry required; working familiarity with Sagitta, Excel and Word.</li>
<li>Ability to learn and use carrier websites, and other programs such as Zywave, Modmaster, and others as required for the position.</li>
<li>High level of organizational ability; able to handle and prioritize multiple tasks.</li>
<li>Work both independently as well as in a team environment to attain team and Agency goals.</li>
<li>Detail orientation and problem-solving ability.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills, with ability to perform simple to complex mathematics computations.</li>
<li>Maintain confidentiality.</li>
<li>Willingness to consistently participate in internal and external educational opportunities to enhance knowledge of current insurance topics or relevant system improvements.</li>
<li>Ability to use a variety of computer programs and software (Word and Excel).</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills, excellent verbal/written communication skills, and ability to complete duties in a professional and timely manner.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma required; college degree preferred.</li>
<li>Active state specific Property/Casualty Insurance license, or ability to acquire license within three months of hire.</li>
<li>Two to Four years’ administrative/clerical experience, preferably in the insurance industry.</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to pursue a technical designation/education: CISR (recommended).</li>
</ul>
