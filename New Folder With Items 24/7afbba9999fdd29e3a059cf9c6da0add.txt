<h2>Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Property Manager Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist in setting market rents, conducting market research, defining leasing goals and running renewal process</li>
<li>Supervise turnover including obtaining bids from subcontractors, supervising turnover process, hiring staff for turnover, and managing small renovation projects</li>
<li>Handle resident accounting tasks such as rent collection, posting rent and preparing deposits</li>
<li>Handle daily operations including: answering calls, scheduling appointments, greeting visitors, showing residences to prospective residents and any necessary paperwork, filing, etc.</li>
<li>Become knowledgeable on our properties versus competition and effectively communicate features to prospective residents</li>
<li>Apply established sales and marketing strategies to increase property traffic, maintain closing ratios and meet or exceed budgeted occupancy</li>
<li>Run and approve credit checks and lease applications</li>
<li>Prepare leases, tenant files, move-in paperwork, confirm residences are move-in ready and perform move-in orientations with new residents</li>
<li>Maintain good rapport with current and prospective residents</li>
<li>Process maintenance requests and follow up with residents to confirm completion</li>
<li>Ensure resident files are properly maintained</li>
<li>Have a thorough knowledge of lease terms, specifications and policies</li>
<li>Learn, audit and ensure team’s compliance with all Fair Housing Laws including local, state and federal housing regulations</li>
<li>Oversee any on-site staff and the relevant departments (leasing and maintenance), approve timesheets, and set staff schedules .</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Property Manager Skills and Knowledge Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>At least five years property management experience</li>
<li>Previous direct customer service experience required</li>
<li>Proven record of successfully leading teams</li>
<li>Comfort using a variety of computer applications required (e.g. Microsoft Office Suite)</li>
<li>Experience with property management software required (e.g. Buildium, Yardi, Propertyware)</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Superior attention to detail, high energy and positive attitude</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and also to work closely with other team members</li>
<li>Ethical and professional conduct and integrity beyond reproach</li>
</ul>
<b>Property Manager Education Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Undergraduate Degree</li>
</ul>
<b>Property Manager Additional Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Valid driver’s license and dependable vehicle. Limited in-town usage of personal vehicle for errands and property visits required, any out-of-County personal mileage reimbursed at the Federal rate</li>
<li>Availability to work Saturdays during prime leasing season</li>
<li>Must be able to perform the physical functions of the position, not limited to: ability to walk properties, complete physical inspections, deliver resident communications and show properties</li>
</ul>
</div>
