<h2>Regulatory Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our West Los Angeles based client, a nutritional supplement company, are searching for an individual to fill their Regulatory Specialist to provide regulatory and quality assurance support for dietary supplements and healthy food brands. This role will be responsible for providing regulatory guidance, document control and product label review support to various areas of the company including Product Development, Marketing, Sales, Graphics, Operations and Legal.</p>
<p><b>Main Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide domestic and international regulatory guidance on product development and product label creation.</li>
<li>Communicate with regulatory agencies on administrative and routine matters.</li>
<li>Perform detail review of product formulas to ensure accuracy of raw material ingredients.</li>
<li>Manage International registration projects as needed by customers.</li>
<li>Develop Formula Specifications and Supplement Facts in collaboration with Product Development, Marketing, Contract Manufacturers, and Quality Assurance departments.</li>
<li>Review and approve final product packaging based on compliance with U.S. labeling regulations.</li>
<li>Assist with management of Engineering Change Order program.</li>
<li>Assist with acquiring necessary State and Federal regulatory licenses as needed.</li>
<li>Maintain external references, lists of national and international regulation standards and FDA guidance documents that affect the products and operations.</li>
<li>Provide technical assistance on various department tasks and projects as needed.</li>
<li>Assist with writing and reviewing department standard operating procedures.</li>
<li>Assist with managing document control activities within the department.</li>
<li>Assist with acquiring technical data and information from contract manufacturers and maintain technical database.</li>
</ul>
