<p><b>Business Development Representative - NETSCOUT Systems</b></p>
<p>
NETSCOUT SYSTEMS, INC. (NASDAQ: NTCT) is a market leader in real-time service assurance and cybersecurity solutions for today’s most demanding service provider, enterprise and government networks. We collect, correlate, organize, and analyze traffic data from all corners of your environment.</p>
<p>
NETSCOUT is always searching for top talent! We are currently searching for a Business Development Representative in our Westford, MA Sales office.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Business Development Representative will develop qualified leads for our US based customers. This role is responsible for developing and implementing strategic sales plans and initiatives in support of the Field Sales Team in order to achieve penetration goals corporate revenue goals for an assigned territory.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and develops new opportunities within assigned region</li>
<li>Establish qualified leads through cold calling, email, social and other prospecting techniques</li>
<li>Penetrate field designated target list of accounts that NETSCOUT is not currently engaged in</li>
<li>Follow up with Marketing Leads within region</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for business expansion at selected existing target accounts</li>
<li>Re-engage with stagnant/lost customers</li>
<li>Targets competitive win-back campaigns</li>
<li>Work with field team in a coordinated fashion driving and supporting selected initiatives</li>
<li>Support and drive attendance at selected field events</li>
<li>Utilize data resources such as Rain King, Install Base, past leads, LinkedIn, etc.</li>
<li>Analyze market data to identify opportunities</li>
<li>Maintain thorough knowledge of company products/services</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>B.S. / B.A or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of experience in an Inside Sales or Business Development role</li>
<li>Previous experience selling computer software or related networking products</li>
<li>3 + years’ experience calling on Fortune1000 Accounts</li>
<li>Knowledge of Service Provider Industry and Technologies a plus</li>
<li>Ability to articulate the NETSCOUT value proposition</li>
<li>Results oriented, Self-Starting, and highly motivated</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communications skills</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team environment</li>
<li>SFDC and LinkedIn experience a plus</li>
<li>Advance level database skills and solid working knowledge of MS Office Tools including Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands: </b>While performing the duties of this job, you will regularly be required to talk or hear. The employee frequently is required to stand; walk; use hands to type, dial, or handle; and reach with hands and arms.</p>
