<h2>Automotive Account Field Rep- Entry Level</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are Dealer Specialties / Dominion Dealer Solutions and we are changing the way people shop for vehicles online!</p>
<p>
We are adding individuals to our team that believe what we believe and that’s giving the online shopper what they are looking for. Our customers are automobile dealerships who rely on us to collect and distribute their vehicle inventory information across the Internet. Our field service technicians visit dealerships daily on assigned routes to collect vehicle data and take high quality photographs of each dealer's inventory. The work you do impacts100% of a dealer’s online shoppers. The ability to build relationships with all sorts of personalities quickly is an absolute must. The field service technician position is paid commission on the amount of vehicles you photograph, so the harder you work the more you make.</p>
<p>
Field Service Technician essential functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Service designated route as designed</li>
<li>
Take high quality photographs of dealer’s inventory</li>
<li>
Collect standard features and options of each vehicle</li>
<li>
Communicate with dealership management daily regarding which vehicles are photographed and online</li>
<li>
Upload data and photographs collected to home office for distribution via proprietary software</li>
<li>
Some product upselling and new business prospecting in some areas</li>
</ul>
<p>
Field Service Technician requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Enjoy working outside at least 85% of the time</li>
<li>
Valid driver’s license, reliable transportation and auto insurance</li>
<li>
Clean driving record and background</li>
<li>
Ability to identify vehicle makes, models and features</li>
<li>
Computer savvy and technical problem solving skills is a plus</li>
<li>
Available to work Saturdays when needed</li>
</ul>
<p>
Field Service Technician benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Flexibility</li>
<li>
Rewarding pay</li>
<li>
Paid vacations</li>
<li>
Great benefits (401K, Insurance, Paid Holidays)</li>
</ul>
<p>
We offer a dynamic environment, excellent growth opportunities, competitive earnings and a comprehensive benefits package that includes a generous 401(k).</p>
