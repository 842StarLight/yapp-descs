<p><b>10X Customer Service Representative</b></p>
<p>
RPM Freight Systems is looking for the ultimate team player who will create value in any situation. You understand customer engagement and what a huge piece that is to future success of the company and your career. Our Customer Service Representatives are our direct link to our customers AND carriers. They provide top notch service for RPM Freight Systems over the phone, chat and email.</p>
<p>
Our Customer Service Representative are personable, patient, and most of all, detailed!</p>
<p>
You also understand that good ideas can come from anywhere and are open minded when it comes to ideas from other RPM family members. You are a flexible multi-tasker who communicates naturally, passionately, and collaboratively.</p>
<p></p>

<p>
THE 10X TASKS AT HAND:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Gathers information from the Carrier to provide accurate and timely updates to Customer and Carrier Sales Reps</li>
<li>
Get and share detailed information about each shipment the customer is moving</li>
<li>
Care for customer needs, helping them get their products from one point to another</li>
<li>
Growing the business through an exceptional customer experience</li>
<li>
Resolving problems to maintain trusting relationships in the most difficult of situations</li>
<li>
You're expected to be as much of a friendly teacher as proficient doer</li>
</ul>
