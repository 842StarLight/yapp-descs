<p></p>Orthofix Spine Operations &amp; Strategy Analyst
<p></p>
<p><b>How you'll make a difference?
</b></p>
<p>Develops and supports analytic and strategic approach for Orthofix Spine Sales, Payer Development, Inventory Reserves and Operations. Focus on analytical and tactical needs to drive business commitmentswhich include revenue, operational service levels, and sales leadership support. Develops new operational metrics and analytical approaches to help Orthofix Spine understand business drivers and identify areas of opportunity. Support leadership within business cycle rhythm.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What will your Duties and Responsibilities be?
</b></p>
<p><i>Nothing in this job description restricts management’s right to assign or reassign duties and responsibilities to this job at any time.
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive sales, payer development, inventory reserves, operational analysis to establish and mature processes within Orthofix Spine organization
</li>
<li>Focus on driving a continuous improvement mindset within all stakeholders within the Orthofix Spine group
</li>
<li>Support overall operational revenue targets to ensure they are achieved
</li>
<li>Develop and execute approach to understand performance leading and lag metrics and relationships
</li>
<li>Act as primary liaison between the reporting IT department and operations department heads
</li>
<li>Research, analyze and resolve problems with supporting data and provide implementable solutions
</li>
<li>Collaborate within the analytics team and with operations to create real business solutions in line with Orthofix Spine initiatives
</li>
<li>Create baseline and trending metrics to support high level targets and year end goals
</li>
<li>Blend internal and externally purchased market data to support marketing and growth strategies
</li>
<li>Support operations process improvement project work
</li>
<li>This position is very visible to all leadership levels of Orthofix and must set a good example by constantly and consistently exceeding all minimum acceptable performance standards in areas such as quality, attendance, attitude, leadership and productivity
</li>
<li>Other projects as assigned
</li>
<li>Regular attendance required
</li>
</ul>
