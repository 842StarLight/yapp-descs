<h2>Marketing Automation Manager, Chicago, IL</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The National Restaurant Association has dynamic new role for a Marketing Automation Manager, who is equal parts creative, collaborative and a skilled communicator, who has mastered the art of delivering the right message at the right time.</p>
<p>
An integral member of our Enterprise Marketing team, you’ll work in close collaboration with departmental stakeholders, to plan, develop, and execute a wide range of email marketing programs, including membership, lead generation, communications, marketing campaigns, internal communications and newsletters, with a foucs on segmentation, data quality and enrichment, and reporting and analysis.</p>
<p>
The role requires deep knowledge of customer (member) acquisition, marketing automation, and list segmentation, and ability to identify a high volume of quality contacts that align with our membership growth and event attendance goals.</p>
<p>
Ideal candidates bring experience in Membership Marketing, preferably from Association industry, as well as e-commerce. Must be digitally-savvy with strong marketing sensibilities, and comprehensive understanding of the nuances of digital audiences and deliverables. Role requires proven proficiency in working with Marketo.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as email campaign manager and email communication expert for The National Restaurant Association and in accordance with Enterprise Editorial Calendar.</li>
<li>
Manage or support email campaigns end-to-end, including planning, targeting, set-up, testing, execution, reporting and optimization</li>
<li>
Manage newsletter strategy under the direction of the Enterprise Marketing Director, in partnership with constituents across the enterprise.</li>
<li>
Works to establish a governance of customer information and list management in partnership with the Data and Membership teams.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with our creative services, member engagement and success teams, sponsorship, sales, consultants, and other members of the Enterprise Marketing team and Company to advise, design and deploy email campaign content that is compelling, accurate, and audience appropriate.</li>
<li>
Build and support campaigns to drive qualified leads, content engagement and sponsored marketing content programs in Marketo/Salesforce.</li>
<li>
Write email content for lead generation, drip campaigns, and customer communications.</li>
<li>
Craft, analyze, and communicate digital marketing metrics, KPIs, intelligence and optimization strategies.</li>
<li>
Manager of list strategy, development and segmentation, list aggregation, email campaign performance analysis, and forecasting and reporting as it relates to business planning.</li>
<li>
Develop strategies and content to grow our newsletter and subscriber base.</li>
<li>
Identify, develop and implement marketing automation platform/CRM together with key stakeholders and business units.</li>
<li>
Think creatively, stay up-to-date on relevant trends in digital marketing, and bring fresh, new ideas to marketing programs.</li>
<li>
Oversee SEM, mobile strategies, display ads and other web based marketing programs.</li>
<li>
Develop, evaluate and oversee the implementation of A/B testing protocols and procedures.</li>
<li>
Set the strategy for online marketing and manage landing page conversion optimization.</li>
<li>
Conduct keyword research and create lists of targeted search terms in partnership with Web Content Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Marketing/Digital Marketing, or related area of study.
</li>
<li>5+ years of experience, or combined education and experience, in B2B content, in-bound and/or digital marketing.
</li>
<li>Proven experience using Marketo and developing lead generating programs through digital campaigns.</li>
<li>
Experience in membership marketing, preferably from association industry, and e-commerce.</li>
<li>
Clear understanding of the nuances of digital audiences and deliverables.</li>
<li>
Able to manage multiple projects and adjust priorities to meet business needs, while maintaining a strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>
A willingness to grow professionally, seek out opportunities to improve, and respond positively to feedback.</li>
<li>
Comfortable in a collaborative team and working with employees and contractors in remote locations.
</li>
<li>Able to extrapolate action items from marketing metrics and analytics to refine content and strategies.</li>
<li>
Experience with, or show aptitude and willingness to learn, web-based platforms for media management including Google Analytics, Salesforce, or others.</li>
<li>
Ability to work both independently and as a team member.</li>
<li>
An understanding of the principles of visual design.</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail and accuracy, project management, and interpersonal skills required.</li>
<li>
Strong research skills with the ability to identify and vet sources of information.</li>
<li>
Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite, including Word, PowerPoint, Outlook and Excel, as well as Adobe Creative Cloud.</li>
<li>
Experience in using the following:<br>
<ul>
<li>HTML, PHP, CMS, CSS implementation and improvement</li>
<li>SEO/ SEM, Google Analytics, Google AdWords and Google Tag Manager</li>
<li>All Social Media platforms (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn etc)</li>
<li>Testing platforms such as Optimizely or Litmus experience are a plus</li>
<li>Photoshop, Kentico, Diners, Salesforce.com or IMIS experience are a plus</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
