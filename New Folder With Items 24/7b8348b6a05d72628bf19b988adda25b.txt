<h2>Customer Support Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Darwin Automotive, a cutting edge software company, is seeking candidates with technical capacity, computer proficiency and a strong customer focus to join our technical support team. In this role, you’ll contribute to the achievement of organizational goals by providing effective and timely technical support. Previous experience is not necessary, but candidates must be self-motivated, demonstrate strong communication skills and be adept at building relationships. The ideal candidate will have previous experience in the automotive market or F&amp;I experience. This role requires candidates to be able to work 10-6 and occasional Saturdays. This will be a remote position.</p>
<p>The role requires close collaboration with external and internal members of the organization to develop and implement effective support strategies. Deliver service and support to various groups including customers, sales team, &amp; implementation team</p>
<p>Pluses include (100% paid medical, 401K profit sharing, rapid advancement opportunities).</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>

<p>Skills</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Telephone skills</li>
<li>Professional, friendly and pleasant demeanor</li>
<li>Computer skills</li>
<li>Organizational skills</li>
<li>Active listening skills</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities</p>
<ul>

<li>Log into our phone system to manage incoming calls</li>

<li>Provide technical assistance and support for incoming concerns and issues related to the Darwin product</li>
<li>Handle a range of technical cases at any one time at all different complexities</li>
<li>Document all correspondence via our CRM system</li>

</ul>
