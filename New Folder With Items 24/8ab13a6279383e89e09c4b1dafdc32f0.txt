<h2>General Sales Manager Automotive Tri State Area</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>FAMILY OWNED AUTOMOTIVE ENTERPRISE WITH MULTIPLE DEALERSHIPS IN THE TRI-STATE AREA IS SEEKING A SALES MANAGER TO LEAD ONE OF ITS BRAND FACILITIES<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>This is a growing enterprise in the Tri-State area. We are seeking an experienced General Sales Manager who can put an action plan in play that addresses the value proposition of retaining New Car/Used Car Front &amp; Back End Gross.
</b></p>
<p><b>Job Description: </b><b>The Sales Manager is a Key role in leading the entire dealership’s operation and will be held accountable for the successful operation of the entire dealership.
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Develop and execute plans to ensure that maximum dealership profitability is achieved.
</b></li>
<li>Responsible for the realization of the defined company targets as well as the overall sales and market share, marketing, sales and fixed operations objectives
</li>
<li><b>Ability to forecast and present business performance metrics.
</b></li>
<li><b>Highly skilled in leading and developing high performance teams throughout the dealership
</b></li>
<li>Accomplish all objectives through leadership, analysis, planning, motivating, coaching and coordinating the activities of all departments as a whole business unit.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage Leads and follow up with customers
</li>
<li>Maintain inventory pricing
</li>
<li>Ensure that sales department operates harmoniously and profitably. Discuss/implement course corrections as needed to achieve goals
</li>
<li>Formulate policies and establish procedures for all training programs and monitor their effectiveness.
</li>
<li>Review and approve the compensation plans for sales employees with department managers for alignment to budgets.
</li>
<li>Supervising employees for optimum effectiveness.
</li>
<li>Provide enthusiastic leadership to shape and inspire employees’ attitudes, build morale and fosters creativity and innovation.
</li>
<li>Conduct dealership Sales meetings at scheduled intervals.
</li>
<li>Coordinate with the business office to ensure that records, reports and analysis are maintained accurately.
</li>
<li>Execute a Code of Ethics that fosters proper business decorum, empathy, and compassion, while ensuring compliance with all corporate and governmental mandates.
</li>
<li>Develop merchandising strategies and assist in creating an effective, cost-efficient advertising and marketing plan.
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Resolve any customer complaints that departmental management has not been able to resolve.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Qualifications:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5-10 years of dealership management capacities with a proven track record of success, experience preferred.
</li>
<li>Ability to lead, motivate and train staff at various levels while moving at a rapid-pace in a dynamic work environment.
</li>
<li>Competent multi-tasker with exceptional organizational and analytical skills, as well as an aptitude for information technology to maximize efficiency.
</li>
<li>Effective Communicator in person, on the telephone, and in writing.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Highly skilled in customer negations, customer service and customer satisfaction – strong closer with CSI focus
</li>
<li>Previous employee management experience including hiring, training, mentoring, counseling and development
</li>
<li>Experience in increasing retail facility business and market share by developing and meeting aggressive volume sales goals from both existing and new customers
</li>
<li>Demonstrates stability of performance under pressure. Stays focused during times of ambiguity and/or adversity and seeks the information/support needed to move forward and manage risk/impacts.
</li>
<li><b>ADP experience preferred.<br>
</b></li>
</ul>
