<h2>Public Sector Strategic Account Leader - Civilian (VA and SSA)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">This position is remote, based in the Washington DC metro area.
<br><br>
----------------
<br>Responsibilities
<br>----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Strategic Account Leader will report to an Area Sales Manager or Regional Director.</li>
<li>Act as a primary point of contact and the face of GitLab for our strategic and large ( ../../gitlab-com/www-gitlab-com/master/handbook/sales#market-segmentation ) prospects.</li>
<li>Contribute to root cause analysis on wins/losses.</li>
<li>Communicate lessons learned to the team, including account managers, the marketing team, and the technical team.</li>
<li>Take ownership of your book of business</li>
<li>document the buying criteria</li>
<li>document the buying process</li>
<li>document next steps and owners</li>
<li>ensure pipeline accuracy based on evidence and not hope</li>
<li>Contribute to documenting improvements in our sales handbook ( ../../gitlab-com/www-gitlab-com/master/handbook/sales ).</li>
<li>Provide account leadership and direction in the pre- and post-sales process</li>
<li>Conduct sales activities including prospecting and developing opportunities in large/strategic accounts</li>
<li>Ensure the successful rollout and adoption of Gitlab products through strong account management activities and coordination with pre-and-post sales engineering and support resources</li>
<li>Travel as necessary to accounts in order to develop relationships and close large opportunities.</li>
<li>Generate qualified leads and develop new customers in conjunction with our strategic channel partners in exceeding quota.</li>
<li>Expand knowledge of industry as well as the competitive posture of the company</li>
<li>Prepare activity and forecast reports as requested</li>
<li>Update and maintain Sales' database as appropriate</li>
<li>Assist sales management in conveying customer needs to product managers, and technical support staff</li>
<li>Utilizing a consultative approach, discuss business issues with prospect and develop a formal quote, a written sales proposal or a formal sales presentation addressing their business needs.</li>
<li>Respond to RFP's and follow up with prospects.</li>
<li>Develop an account plan to sell to customers based on their business needs.</li>
<li>Build and strengthen the business relationship with current accounts and new prospects.</li>
<li>Recommend marketing strategies.</li>
</ul>
------------
<br>Requirements
<br>------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>You share our values ( https://about.gitlab.com/handbook/values ), and work in accordance with those values.</li>
<li>Minimum 5+ years of experience selling into US Dept of Transportation and/or US Dept of State with a comprehensive understanding of the local Public Sector landscape and associated business processes.</li>
<li>Able to provide high degree of major account management and control</li>
<li>Work under minimal supervision on complex projects.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and ability to excel in a team oriented atmosphere</li>
<li>Strong written/verbal communications skills</li>
<li>5+ years successful quota attainment in the Public Sector Space</li>
<li>Very motivated and goal-oriented</li>
<li>Must want a career-oriented environment that is both fun and professional.</li>
<li>Strong customer service orientation and ability to develop and maintain relationships</li>
<li>Preferred experience with Git, Software Development Tools, Application Lifecycle Management</li>
</ul>
