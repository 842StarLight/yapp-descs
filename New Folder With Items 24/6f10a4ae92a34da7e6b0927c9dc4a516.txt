<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About LionDesk: </b></p>
<p>LionDesk is a fast growing and thriving startup that provides an integrated CRM platform to make it easy and affordable for sales and marketing professionals to connect, communicate with, and close more deals leveraging today's most sophisticated technology.</p>
<p>LionDesk has an open API development platform that integrates with 100s of the best in class tools so professionals can run their operations from a single centralized hub. For more information visit www.liondesk.com</p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>Our new team member will join an established technology company who is continuing to grow, they will have the opportunity to learn innovative skills within the real estate technology industry.</p>
<p>As an Inside Sales Representative, you will learn our product from the ground up and support our clients by phone and email. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills and experience documenting issues and resolutions. This candidate is a product expert with radical accountability for driving awareness of feature enhancements.</p>
<p>Successful candidates must be sales driven, team-oriented; speak on the phone comfortably, with authority and concern to each client’s needs; and manage time efficiently and prioritize their workday accordingly.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to consult with real estate professionals and provide recommendations on online real estate marketing best practices and strategies to maximize value.</li>
<li>Nurture all assigned leads to convert them to subscribed users.</li>
<li>Continues to maintain or exceed minimum sales goals. </li>
<li>Perform all scheduled appointments on-time.</li>
<li>Perform required internal client-related follow-up activities as needed (Support tickets, Billing inquiries, assistance from management, etc.)</li>
<li>Provide comprehensive training via phone and email throughout the duration of the client marketing process.</li>
<li>Reply promptly to all emails and answer all incoming phone calls.</li>
<li>Responsiveness to clients is extremely important. This correspondence includes answering questions about their specific projects, training on the platform, aiding them while navigating the website, communicating best practices, offering advice and suggestions, and fielding technical questions.</li>
<li>Having intimate knowledge of new product roll outs, ability to adequately test site features and workflow, and effectively communicate these enhancements and train clients.</li>
<li>Demonstrate superior time-management by adhering to scheduled shifts, appointments, breaks, meetings, Power Dialer time, etc.</li>
<li>Strive to continually develop your sales ability &amp; become a Subject Matter Expert.</li>
<li>Maintain personal and professional integrity at all times.</li>
<li>Assists all team members by answering questions and sharing/suggesting best practices. </li>
<li>Provide superior customer service, contribute to a positive work environment, and embody the company culture.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1 – 3 years of account management/sales experience.</li>
<li>Proven ability to interact with clients in a fast-paced, high-volume customer serving setting.</li>
<li>Possession of strong organizational and account management skills.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail while meeting strict deadlines.</li>
<li>Experience on boarding to and providing continual training of SaaS platforms.</li>
<li>Be a positive team player able to work with varying departments in the best interest of the company. Management experience a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently upon receiving instruction.</li>
<li>Highly efficient with the ability to multi-task and re-prioritize as needed; outstanding time management skills</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills and experience working with multiple client personas</li>
<li>Friendly and personable to create long-lasting relationships with our valued clients.</li>
<li>Familiarity with commercial real estate and loan sale transactions a plus.</li>
<li>Experience with Zoom Meeting or similar call-in training programs a plus.</li>
</ul>
