ABOUT THE ROLE</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
VTS is looking for an intelligent, highly motivated sales professional to join our team as a key contributor. Comfortable navigating large enterprise organizations you will identify, develop and manage executive level relationships with complex key accounts of strategic significance to the organization. Possessing strong financial and business acumen you will build strategic account-based business plans that increase revenue by sourcing expansion business opportunities and executing on-time renewals while delivering exceptional client outcomes. Responsible for the entire sales process you skillfully build consensus, handle objections, negotiate large deals and successfully close on time.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Well versed in solution selling our Account Development Managers possesses a passion for technology, innovative business solutions and commercial real estate. You are a fast learner, naturally curious, skilled at working cross departmentally and possess a consultative, diagnostic approach to client relationships. If this describes you and being at the forefront of innovation in the world’s second largest asset class is exciting, then we want you on our team!</div>
