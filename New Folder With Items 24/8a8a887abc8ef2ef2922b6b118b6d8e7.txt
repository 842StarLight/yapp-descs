<h2>Sr. Dynamics Financial AX Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This<b> Sr. AX/D365F&amp;O Consultant </b>will provide in-depth knowledge of the Microsoft Dynamics AX application in evaluating the customers’ requirements, business processes and current issues to develop models and provide effective solutions.</p>
<p><b>Open to Location: </b>This position can work remote and reside anywhere in the US.</p>
<p> <b>Responsibilities of this Dynamics AX/D365F&amp;O Consultant include</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Participate in discovery meetings with the client to understand current business processes that need to be incorporated into AX</li>
<li>Work with the client to understand legacy data components and identify the appropriate location for such data in AX. Document mapping of that data for conversion by technical staff.</li>
<li>Validation of business practices and processes in Dynamics AX/D365 F&amp;O based on discovery analysis</li>
<li>Conducting GAP analyses to determine difference between application and business processes.</li>
<li>Identify creative solutions for processes that aren’t fully accomplished by standard AX/D365F&amp;O</li>
<li>Develops and delivers presentations to customers by outlining findings and recommendations to guide the implementation of the identified solution.</li>
<li>Translates functional business objectives and critical success factors into Functional documents</li>
<li>Configure application as appropriate to conform to business process definition.</li>
<li>Train users in a hands-on methodology and develop training documentation.</li>
<li>Provide on-going support to end users, assisting with trouble shooting or additional training via email, phone or site visits.</li>
<li>Maintaining and expanding client relationships through delivery of high quality services.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b>:</p>
<p>Minimum:</p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS in MIS, Computer Science, or Accounting preferred</li>
<li>Significant industry experience in accounting, manufacturing or distribution.</li>
<li>3+ years of Dynamics AX functional implementation experience required</li>
<li>Microsoft certifications in Dynamics AX/D365F&amp;O</li>
<li>DIEF experience</li>
<li>Experience in Product creation and management, Trade agreements, Procurement's and Sales</li>
<li>Experience with intercompany activities</li>
<li>Experience with multiple legal entries management</li>
<li>Ability to guide Workflow procurement process</li>
<li>Experience with associated tools and technologies such as SQL Reporting Services or SharePoint are highly desirable.</li>
<li>Exceptional interpersonal, communication and relationship building skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and effectively meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Excellent demonstration and presentation skills</li>
<li>A passion for client success and enthusiasm for the products we implement</li>
<li>Ability to travel to provide services to clients; and nationally up to 75% of your time.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Warehouse manager R3</li>
<li>Experience in Manufacturing and or Retail</li>
<li>Desire to constantly assess and incorporate new technologies and software into their skill set</li>
<li>Strong project implementation skills (planning, data conversion, module setups, training, project management, reporting)</li>
<li>Ability to recognize and incorporate client needs at a worker level.</li>
<li>Proactive approach in communicating project status and project risks to all parties, internal and external</li>
</ul>
