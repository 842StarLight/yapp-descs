<h2>Director of Statistical Healthcare Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Director of Statistical Healthcare Analytics provides growth,strategic, and operational leadership in the area of healthcare provider and payer analytics. As well as provide tool recommendations to enable analytics capabilities for data scientists and analytics users. This position will report to the Chief Executive Officer.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop statistical modeling for new and ongoing product lines.</li>
<li>On-going monitoring of product lines including but not limited to the analytical tools and dashboards required for executive decision-making.</li>
<li>Develop analytical insights for the areas clinical and quality and payer analysis</li>
<li>Ensure the accurate and timely preparation of management tools for analyzing clinical, and operational results using enterprise tools.</li>
<li>Serve as point of contact and resource for analytics.</li>
<li>Provide data-driven recommendations of growth projects in the pipeline of Division Senior Strategic and Planning leadership.</li>
<li>Prepare on-demand analyses and ad-hoc reports and insights.</li>
<li>Research and keep current knowledge of competitive dynamics in the market.</li>
<li>Provide sales with sales support as needed in the areas of analysis.</li>
<li>Present reports and findings to customer and conduct and lead client facing discussions and meetings as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree in business, economics, statistics, mathematics, actuarial science, public health, health informatics, healthcare administration, finance or related field.Master's degree preferred.</p>
<p>· 7+ years of experience in healthcare analytics, leading an organization, creating strategic plans, and operationalizing functional teams in direct relation to meeting business needs or realizing goals tied to strategic objectives.</p>
<p>· Experience in strategic planning and solutioning preferred. Broad exposure and understanding of statistical, analytical, or data mining techniques and appropriate applicationof these capabilities in a business performance environment preferred.</p>
<p>· Working knowledge of analytical tools, including R, Python, SAS, ArcGIS, QGIS, Microstrategy,Tableau, Hadoop, or related tools preferred.</p>
<p>· Experience in provider contracting, claims pricing, financial reporting/analysis, data modeling, statistical modeling or data analysis preferred.</p>
<p>· Experience with report/dashboard development, data/reportautomation, self-service capabilities, data design and integration, or data quality and governance preferred.</p>
<p>This is a remote position but may require some travel.</p>
