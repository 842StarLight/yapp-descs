<h2>STORE MANAGER TRAINEE- Flying J @ Wye</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>To train with store manager and learn overall operation of store. Areas to be trained include the following: customer service; learning to make supervisory decisions that are consistent with Town Pump standards, policies, and procedures; understanding controllable expenses, sales increases, inside gross margin, shortage controls, and promotions programs; setting the proper example for co-workers in terms of professionalism, attitude, and teamwork.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p>Under the direction of the Training Manager, the Manager in Training will perform the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Customer service to include communicating with customers, resolve complaints and inquiries.</li>
<li>
Daily bookwork using computer to record employee information, inventory and sale information.</li>
<li>
Make bank deposit.</li>
<li>
Fill in for absent employee.</li>
<li>
Order supplies and merchandise.</li>
<li>
Answer telephone and screen calls.</li>
<li>
Light maintenance and cleaning of store and restrooms.</li>
<li>
Stock cooler and shelves.</li>
<li>
Effectively prioritize, organize, delegate, and follow up on store tasks to be accomplished.</li>
<li>
Security of all store assets. Control of cash, fuel, and inventories. Control of store merchandise and labor hours.</li>
<li>
New employee hiring, orientation, and training.</li>
<li>
Merchandising, to include receiving, pricing, stocking, displaying, and rotating of bar inventories.</li>
<li>
Store maintenance and basic repairs.</li>
<li>
Inventory of merchandise.</li>
<li>
Key daily sales.</li>
<li>
Proper entry of merchandise invoices.</li>
<li>
Disciplining and/or terminating problem employees.</li>
<li>
Enforcing all company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Supervisory decisions that are consistent with Town Pump Inc. standards, policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
