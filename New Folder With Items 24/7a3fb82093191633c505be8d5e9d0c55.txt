<p><b>Has your paper napkin business plan turned into a billion-dollar reality? Ours has and that sketch on a napkin is now our world-class software platform which combines cutting-edge technology with outstanding client service and premier wealth management capabilities. Shareworks by Morgan Stanley is designed to simplify the complexities of equity plan management, while helping employees realize the full potential of their benefits.</b></p>
<p>
Shareworks by Morgan Stanley is looking for an experienced <b>Product Manager</b> to join our growing product team. Shareworks is our industry leading equity plan administration SaaS platform. We are disrupting this niche of the FinTech world one region at a time. Our Product Managers are embedded with our engineers to guide products from conception to launch. As part of the product management team, you bridge the technical and business worlds as you design enhancements that our customers love. You'll be the hub between partners, customers, development, marketing, support, implementation consulting, operations, and sales for your product area. You have a bias toward action and can break down complex problems into steps that drive product development in our agile environment. Your cross-functional product team will include UX designers, developers, quality analysts, and business analysts.</p>
<p><b>
Things you will do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Use our Shareworks Academy certification program to achieve proficiency and then mastery of our platform</li>
<li>Help to define the product vision and strategy for Shareworks</li>
<li>Work with customers, potential customers, and subject matter experts to discover what problems we should be solving</li>
<li>Work with UX to validate design ideas prior to development</li>
<li>Define high-level features and guide their decomposition into detailed requirements</li>
<li>Join client implementation project teams when complex integration or customizations are required</li>
<li>Support QA analysts to ensure test case coverage</li>
<li>Enforce UX standards and consistency</li>
<li>Help your development team understand the business needs, problems, and desired outcome as you work on projects</li>
<li>Understand the global context for new functionality while designing enhancements that align with our overall product strategy</li>
<li>Participate in the development process from definition, through design, build, test, release, and maintenance</li>
<li>Capture details during internal stakeholder and external customer meetings</li>
<li>Contribute to the design and evolution of our equity plan administration web application<br>
