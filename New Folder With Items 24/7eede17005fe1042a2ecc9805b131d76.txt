<b>Director of Sales (Microsoft Azure Cloud Services &amp; Power BI Data Analytics)</b><br>
<br>
TwoConnect is seeking a <b>Director of Sales</b>. The perfect candidate for this position has about 5 years of direct IT cloud services sales experience. You will be responsible for lead generation, qualification, and closing sales for our SaaS, PaaS Azure Cloud integration and software development services.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>You will be based from home but traveling to customer locations.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>If you just got excited, keep reading!<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>What will you do?</b><br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Build the market pipeline:</b></div>
<ul><li>
Right now, customers have requirements around Microsoft’s Azure cloud services. We satisfy these requirements with our award-winning team of Microsoft Azure experts. We believe that we are the best in the market at what we do. But, we haven’t been chasing this work outside of already established relationships—and, we know that has to change. This will include inbound and output sales oversight, client facing meetings, and relationship building with customers as well as internal stakeholders.</li></ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Engineer opportunities based on customer need:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
We’re looking for someone who can do more than just book out our consultants and sell our Microsoft Azure consulting services. We want someone who listens to customer needs, understands the technology, and can work with customers and internal teams to uncover new opportunities that increase sales and profits while providing the best value to our customers.</li>
<li>
We want someone who knows how to sell software development services in the cloud. You will need to use good ole-fashion elbow grease to drive the exceeding of goals and objectives. You can expect to make lots of outbound phone calls and emails. You love to hunt!</li>
<li>
Create and develop strong long-term relationships with CEOs, COOs, Director of HR, CFOs of large and mid-size multinationals.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
What are we looking for?</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Any experience with Microsoft Azure or Google Cloud Services is a HUGE advantage. Experience selling Cloud solutions or enterprise consulting and software development services.</li>
<li>
Affinity with technology, especially Microsoft or Google Cloud.</li>
<li>
Team player, but also comfortable working without close supervision.</li>
<li>
Motivated and proactive, accurate, organized and reliable.</li>
<li>
Driven with an undeniable ‘can-do’ attitude.</li>
<li>
You will be based from home but travel to customer locations may be required.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Your Goals</b>:</div>
<ul>
<li>
This is a hunting sales position specifically targeting national and multinational organizations with regional making.</li>
<li>
Required to sell the full Microsoft Azure &amp; Google Cloud development and integration portfolio including Hybrid Cloud, IaaS, PaaS, and associated managed services.</li>
<li>
You will work to drive customer adoption helping to fuel the growth of our managed services and on premise software development and cloud integration business.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Your Responsibilities Also Include</b>:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Identify direct customer leads and opportunities to expand the pipeline.</li>
<li>
Close sales by building rapport with potential Partners and Customers; explaining product and service capabilities; overcoming objections; preparing contracts.</li>
<li>
Proactively reach out and assist customers in the successful deployment of their Cloud services solution.</li>
<li>
Coordinate pre-sales assistance in the delivery of effective proposals to the prospects and Customers.</li>
<li>
Initiate sales process by scheduling appointments; making initial presentation; understanding opportunity requirements.</li>
<li>
Expands sales in existing accounts by introducing new Microsoft &amp; Google cloud consulting and development services.</li>
</ul>

Presentation Skills, Internal Communications, Excellent Verbal Communication, Closing Skills, Motivation for Sales, Sales Planning, Territory Management, Prospecting Skills, Persistence, Meeting Sales Goals.</li>
