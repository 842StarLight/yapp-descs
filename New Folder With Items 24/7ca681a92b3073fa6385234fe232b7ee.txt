<h2>Director of Business Development, K-12 Schools</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Director of K-12 Business Development will work closely with business unit leadership of Tyler’s K-12 portfolio of products in order to align strategy in all aspects of the business units resulting in increased overall sales to School districts. The ideal candidate will embrace the challenge for annual year-over-year sales growth while having no direct authority over the business units themselves.</p>
<p>The Director will work across independently operated school product business units to facilitate a collaborative and unified direction. The Director will work with sales and marketing leadership to ensure a unified sales approach and messaging that will set Tyler’s K-12 portfolio apart from the competition and drive cross-selling of the various point solutions. Additionally, the Director will work across the independent business units with an ear toward alignment in strategic direction, product roadmaps and operational delivery. The Director will bring visibility to all aspects of the evolving K-12 market to ensure business decisions are being made based upon available data and that supports an overarching K-12 strategy and mission for Tyler.</p>
<p>Work with our integrated district management solutions that help school districts bridge information gaps across all departments. From school ERP to student information and transportation, you can help districts of all sizes achieve more by improving transparency between parents and schools, advancing operations, and enabling data-driven decisions.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<p>The following represents a sampling of key initial duties and is intended as a model that will grow over time.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Pricing.</b> Ensure a deep understanding of current Tyler and competitive pricing structures and how those align with the value provided by Tyler’s solutions. Ensure pricing is packaged in meaningful ways that resonate with a K-12 entity (i.e. per student pricing, etc.).</li>
<li>
<b>Marketing.</b> Work with corporate marketing to develop a marketing strategy based upon Tyler’s collection of School applications versus our current approach of point solution sales.</li>
<li>
<b>Contracts.</b> Work with Legal to develop a School District contract which can leverage any of Tyler’s school solutions at any point in the future.</li>
<li>
<b>Nexus &amp; Product Prioritization.</b> Define specific application integration points that will differentiate Tyler’s solutions and work with Development to schedule, monitor, and deliver these features across Tyler’s applications. Ensure product roadmaps are approached with synergy across the Tyler K-12 business units.</li>
<li>
<b>Research.</b> Conduct regular research on Tyler’s addressable market and whitespace opportunities and educate senior leadership as trends develop. Ideate on establishment of new revenue streams specific to the K-12 market.</li>
<li>
<b>Sales Channel.</b> Research current sales channels and make recommendations to senior leadership on potential territory and or organizational changes which would help Tyler compete more effectively in the school marketplace</li>
<li>
<b>Training.</b> Teach Tyler Sales representatives how to effectively sell each Tyler School application: SIS, Transportation, ERP.</li>
<li>
<b>Sales Assistance.</b> Help Tyler Sales representatives throughout the sales process (Pre-RFP, RFP, Demonstration, Contract Negotiation, Statement of Work, and Transition to Implementation). This will include travel to prospects as needed.</li>
<li>
<b>Tyler Enterprise Application Strategy.</b> Work as a liaison with other Tyler Enterprise Applications (i.e. Socrata, MyCivic, etc.) to drive and prioritize a K-12 strategy as appropriate.</li>
</ul>
</div>
