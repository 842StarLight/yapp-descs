<h2>Staff Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This position will act as the (Staff Consultant) Business Analyst for UHY’s internal ERP Implementation Project. Upon completion of the implementation, the Business Analyst will be considered to remain at UHY to provide ongoing support of the ERP system, or other firm functions.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Support the successful execution of internal ERP/digital transformation project.</li>
<li>
Lead work packages within project with mixed teams of consultants, vendors and internal business partners.</li>
<li>
Support the management communications between clients, groups within company and third parties.</li>
<li>
Support project quality assurance reviews by ensuring alignment with firm methodologies and leading practices and by assisting to validate that the designed solution will meet clients’ business requirements.</li>
<li>
Ensure high quality output and professionalism.<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Tasks
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>ness BesBusiness Unit Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Primary responsibility supporting internal project through business process optimization, change management, and software implementation
</li>
<li>Act as an internal business analyst documenting processes and participating in optimizing, designing, implementing and testing new business processes including, but not limited to, Sales, Quote-to-Cash, Procure-to-Pay, and Record-to-Report</li>
<li>
Communicate functional requirements and configuration to testing, training, and technical leads</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintaining and updating the testing approach and plan. Writes testing scripts and sets up the testing environment for the testers. Executes the test plan with the testers. Tracks testing issues and fixes against plan.</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintaining and updating the training approach and plan, in concert with the vendor/implementer</li>
<li>
Create training materials and set up the training environment for the users. Execute the training plan with the users.</li>
<li>
Support the implementation of software by ensuring business requirements are configured in the new software product</li>
<li>
Support planning and executing change management strategies to ensure user adoption</li>
<li>
Support the data migration activities by carrying out data improvement activities, verifying data mappings between legacy and target systems, and verifying data accuracy post-migration</li>
<li>
Support the data integration activities by identifying integration use cases, data flows, and data mappings across systems</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Skills
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Exceptional and articulate interpersonal and business communication skills (verbal, written and listening).</li>
<li>
Ability to write, present, listen, question, and facilitate meetings in an effective manner.</li>
<li>
Strategic thinking and action skills with the ability to identify opportunities for continuous improvement.
</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of Microsoft Office products including Microsoft Project, Office, Word, Excel, Visio, PowerPoint and other project management tools.</li>
<li>
Must be willing and able to travel up to 25% domestically with some higher periods of travel as needed.
</li>
<li>Consultative skills including assessing needs, building rapport, and handling objections.
</li>
<li>Flexibility and excellent organization and time management skills with the ability to work in a deadline driven environment.
</li>
<li>A positive approach to proactively problem solving while maintaining composure and attention to detail.
</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
