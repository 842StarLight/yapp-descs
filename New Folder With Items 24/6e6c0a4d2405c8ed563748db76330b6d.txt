<h2>Enterprise Sales Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>The Job: </b>Enterprise Sales Lead | Top SaaS seller to Federal Logos, preferred.</div>
<br>
<br>
We are seeking a strong analytical mind that understands business organizations and operations, likes talking with people, is service-oriented, likes doing a variety of activities. Must thrive in a fast-paced, high-growth environment, willing to wear multiple hats and do the job that needs doing.<br>
<br>
<br>
Growing at more than 30% year over year, Agiloft is looking for a Technical Sales Lead to join our expanding national team. You will develop new software sales for our award-winning Contract Lifecycle Management and other business software solutions. This is an outstanding role for somebody that is looking to advance their business development career with a fast-growing company.<br>
<br>
<br>
In addition to industry competitive base and sales incentive compensation, the position offers the opportunity to earn company stock options. Full medical and dental benefits as well as matching 401K retirement plan are also provided.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Primary Responsibilities include, but not limited to:</b>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Selling the product!</li>
<li>Develop and lead enterprise sales opportunities from inception to close</li>
<li>Be the expert that articulates the company’s capabilities in the context of customer needs</li>
<li>Manage and lead proof of concept evaluations</li>
<li>Interact directly with prospects to address any technical questions and represent the technical expertise of the company’s Engineering and Success teams</li>
<li>Provide technical demonstrations of the product configured to a Client’s individual use case</li>
<li>Prepare quotations for prospective clients</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>We need someone who can bring the following to the table:</b>
<div>
<ul>
<li>5-10 years of software sales or consulting experience in Contract Lifecycle Management, ITIL Service Desk, Business Process Management, Document Management, ERP or related enterprise systems</li>
<li>BS/BA or related industry field sales experience</li>
<li>Enterprise Cloud software/SaaS full sales-cycle experience</li>
<li>Experience with value selling and other leading solution sales techniques</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, communication, persuasion, presentation and writing skills</li>
<li>Experience scoping, managing and executing customer pilots and Proof of Concepts</li>
<li>Outstanding problem-solving skills, including the ability to meet a business requirement with a technical solution</li>
<li>Passion and people skills</li>
<li>Ability to work unsupervised from home office as required</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Willingness to travel (up to 20%)</li>
</ul>
