<br>Technical Product Marketing Manager - Platform
<br>**********************************************
<br><br>
Reporting to the VP of Product Marketing, this role will provide strategic planning and tactical execution of content development, sales enablement and customer engagement for Sumo Logic (across both IT Ops/DevOps and Security Analytics markets). The successful candidate will bring a few years of Analytics domain expertise, to gain credibility quickly with Sales &amp; Customers as the Subject matter expert.
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop marketing plans through deep engagement with product management, sales and other marketing teams</li>
<li>Design and develop deeply technical sales and marketing collateral including presentations, benchmarks, blogs, white papers, videos and field training courses</li>
<li>Support customer meetings and training</li>
<li>Deliver enablement to sales, sales engineering and partners</li>
<li>Develop competitive assessments, and creative collateral to position Sumo uniquely in the minds of Analytics practitioners</li>
<li>Evangelize Sumo Logic in ecosystem/partnering activities</li>
<li>Drive integrated communications initiatives with other businesses and teams across internal and partner ecosystem</li>
</ul>
<b>Desired Qualifications, Skills and Experience::</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 years experience in product management or product marketing or sales engineering or technical marketing roles with a complex SaaS offering geared to IT Ops / DevOps, Security or Analytics</li>
<li>Exceptional written and oral communication skills to evangelize how Sumo excels at offerings for the various IT Operations persona both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Highly technical to be able to independently develop demos, webinars, videos, technical collateral, ROI/value oriented assessments, etc.</li>
<li>Highly collaborative work style to learn from others and build upon their ideas.</li>
<li>Passion for driving product innovation and to effectively communicate cross functionally within Product Development, Engineering, Marketing, and Sales.</li>
<li>Curiosity to learn the intricacies of Sumo Logic's business and adapt past experiences to Sumo Logic's current opportunities.</li>
<li>Technical undergraduate degree</li>
</ul>
