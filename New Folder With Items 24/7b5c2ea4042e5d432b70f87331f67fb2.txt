<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
As the Director of Mid-Market Sales (MMS), E-commerce, you will be a thought leader for MMS’ highest touch business growth programs within these sectors, across both heritage and digitally-native companies. You’ll set the vision, strategy and operationalize how the team approaches the market and how it better understands and transforms customers and their businesses. You will own C-level relationships with the highest tier customers driving our growth. You enable your team to reach for ambitious performance goals. Your knowledge of digital media, combined with your sales skills and analytical abilities, will shape your team’s strategy.
</p>
<p>With a rapidly growing array of sales teams, it's important for us to have experienced, charismatic leaders. You'll lead and coach high-performing account teams that use consultative sales skills to understand our advertisers' needs and deliver measurable solutions that grow their businesses. You will also work with the sales leadership team to set strategic objectives and run the day-to-day operations for the business. You are an excellent communicator with a proven ability to train and motivate a large team, and you take an analytical approach to sales management.</p>
<p>
The Mid-Market Sales team partners with the largest accounts in the small and medium business segment. We are focused on providing scaled solutions to our partners that solve their marketing challenges and provide insight into their marketplace opportunity. In addition, this Mid-Market US team partners internally across multiple functions and regions.</p>

Responsibilities</b></h2>
