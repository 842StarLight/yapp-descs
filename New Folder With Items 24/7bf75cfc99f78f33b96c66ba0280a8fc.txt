What You Will Be Doing
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Research, design and develop contemporary processor, networking, user interface, sensor, safety, and charging electronics for ChargePoint's industry-leading products.</li>
<li>Deliver designs optimized for multiple dimensions including cost, reliability and feature richness.</li>
<li>Develop from the lowest levels of circuit design to full-scale system design and see those systems all the way through to volume manufacturing and field deployment.</li>
</ul>
What You Will Bring to ChargePoint
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong background in electrical/electronic design and understanding of system level engineering tradeoffs.</li>
<li>Experience designing, interfacing to and controlling peripheral devices and sensors.</li>
<li>Desire to innovate and create new technologies in vehicle charging</li>
<li>Must be inventive, curious, and creative as well as fanatical about cost, quality, and building market leading products.</li>
<li>Able to drive solutions across software, mechanical, power and thermal boundaries to assure great results.</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience in developing complex products moving into production.</li>
<li>Analog and digital circuit design experience</li>
<li>2+ years of experience developing electronic products.</li>
<li>Coding experience - C++ or assembly.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or higher in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science or related disciplines.</li>
</ul>
