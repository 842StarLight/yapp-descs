<h2>MARKETING CONSULTANT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Join one of the West Virginia’s fastest growing and most advanced media groups!</p>
<p>
West Virginia Radio Corporation has a rare opportunity for an aggressive, experienced, confident and competitive marketing profession to represent and sell broadcast and digital marketing products and services.</p>
<p>
Specific products include advertising and marketing products with WJLS radio stations and Pikewood Digital. A background of success in the sales marketing, advertising and communication fields is preferred.</p>
<p>
Main duties include establishing and developing long-term business relationships; identifying and securing new business opportunities; presenting campaign proposals; generating creative ideas and solutions; tracking and reporting on business successes.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide advertising and marketing solutions for clients that achieve their objectives, increase business and foster strong working relationships.</li>
<li>Seek out prospective customers/business opportunities with new and existing customers.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain customer relations in order to grow profitable sales.</li>
<li>Present products and services of West Virginia Radio Corporation including radio and digital marketing options.</li>
<li>Follow-up on all generated business to assure customer is being serviced and to document the outcome of the proposed solutions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree is desirable.</li>
<li>Prior media sales experience is an advantage, but no necessary.</li>
<li>Must have reliable transportation.</li>
<li>Must present and communicate in a professional manner.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Must be organized with good time management skills.</li>
<li>Possess proven analytical problem solving solutions for the customer and the company.</li>
<li>Computer proficiency in Windows and Microsoft applications.</li>
<li>Possess strong presentation, negotiation, and closing skills.</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated and able to work independently to meet or exceed goals.</li>
<li>Be able to think creatively and generate original ideas.</li>
<li>Able to pay close attention to detail.</li>
<li>Be able to work calmly and effectively under pressure, react quickly, and meet tight deadlines.</li>
<li>Be able to handle rejection.</li>
<li>Have the confidence and determination to pursue leads, overcome obstacles and secure deals.</li>
</ul>
<p>WV Radio offers a competitive salary, plus health benefits and a 401K program. WV Radio Corporation is an Equal Opportunity Employer.</p>
<p>
Please send Cover Letter and Resume to psnuffer@wvradio.com or click the button to apply!</p>
