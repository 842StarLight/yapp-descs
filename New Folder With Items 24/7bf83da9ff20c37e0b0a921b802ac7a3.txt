<h2>Business Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Q2 is seeking a <b>Business Consultant </b>to work closely with Implementation Engineers, Project Managers, and Client Project teams. This individual will be reporting to an Premier Services Delivery Manager and will engage on multiple on-line banking implementations concurrently. Business Consultants provide subject matter expertise while working within various Q2 implementation project teams. You will develop, deliver and train best practices in both technical and non-technical aspects of a project. The Business Consultant inspects for gaps in training, work aids and execution detail so that both the client and Q2 teams meet and exceed delivery expectations. Typically involves extensive interaction with sales, systems engineering, product development, and other members of cross-functional teams including various client disciplines. May communicate in various forms to Q2 and Client Executive level staff. These types of communication can include emails, presentations, formal and informal ad hoc meetings. These communication events can include consultative recommendations, setting expectations, developing requirements, negotiating and facilitating conflict resolution.
</p>
