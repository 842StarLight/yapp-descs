<h2>Director of Sales, Americas</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The position will lead and manage the sale of teleSys solutions and services to network equipment providers and telecom service providers; cloud service providers such as IaaS, PaaS and SaaS companies in the Americas. Key responsibilities include achieving targeted sales bookings &amp; revenue quotas and displaying account growth within the region. The Sales Director will generate and grow revenue through existing accounts; targeting new accounts for development and revenue generation; and exploring new leads and opportunities. Development of account strategy and management plans are expected, including sales forecasting with online tools, with focus on multi-year forecasting and sales growth.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities : </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Managing Regional Sales activities within allocated region with existing and new accounts.</li>
<li>Defining and executing Targeted Account Sales plans and growth strategies</li>
<li>Leveraging partnerships to develop and execute sales plans</li>
<li>Investigating and driving new business opportunities</li>
<li>Helping to prepare proposals and responses to the RFIs, RFPs</li>
<li>Generating opportunities and revenue within assigned territories and uncovering new opportunities within the territory through both direct and partner sales activities</li>
<li>Maintaining strong customer relationships with all buying influencers and decision makers</li>
<li>Presenting to Customers and Prospects a complete product &amp; solution range using both standard material and/or developing customized material that might be required</li>
<li>Must work closely with Product Line Management, teleSys senior management, and Sales Operations on all aspects of the sales process and customer/market needs and drivers</li>
<li>Driving internal teams (SE, PLM, Service, Finance, Legal) in developing technical and commercial proposals and bids that result in competitive business closure</li>
<li>Produce an activity report on a weekly basis and provide relevant &amp; critical information/documentation in timely manner</li>
<li>Keeping abreast of competition, competitive issues and products</li>
<li>Assist in coordinating all commercial and technical issues relating to pilots, tests, field trials, and other types of evaluations</li>
<li>Candidate must be self sufficient and comfortable working in a startup-type environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements : </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required</li>
<li>A minimum of 10 years direct sales experience involving high level consultative telecom software &amp; solution sales within large national/global Service Providers - Tier-1 and Ter-2 fixed-line and mobile operators, Cloud communication SP’s, and larger Enterprises accounts.</li>
<li>Background &amp; experience in selling Next Generation Carrier Network infrastructure: STP's HLR's, Diatmer Controllers, SBC's, Media Servers, VoIP, IMS/VoLTE, signaling and Media Gateways products &amp; technologies.</li>
<li>Background &amp; experience in selling Value Added Services (Voicemail, Unified Communication, Conferencing, Video, IVR, mobile clients), and/or other telco/web application software solutions</li>
<li>Familiarity with NFV/SDN and other telecom cloud/virtualization and digital transformation trends &amp; requirements</li>
<li>Ideal vendor experience &amp; Knowledge includes GenBand/Sonus(Ribbon), Radisys. ACME, Nokia/Siemens, Alcatel/Lucent, Huawei, Ericsson, Mavenir, Metaswitch, Cisco, Dialogic</li>
<li>Skilled at creating and executing prospecting strategies for discovering new accounts.</li>
<li>Skilled in contract negotiation and ability to effectively manage sales activates from initial contact through closing of the business.</li>
<li>Excellent presentation skills and a persuasive communications style.</li>
<li>Presenting and discussing solutions with C-level Executives and other decision-makers.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills in building and maintaining collaborative relationships with cross functional &amp; cross-cultural teams.</li>
<li>A track record of success in achieving and exceeding quotas.</li>
<li>Flexibility to travel frequently to customer meetings and corporate events</li>
</ul>
