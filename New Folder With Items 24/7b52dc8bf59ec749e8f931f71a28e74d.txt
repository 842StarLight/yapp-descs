<h2>Principal Network Engineer - Aruba Software</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The job family spans across multiple engineering disciplines. Work consists of designing, developing, analyzing, troubleshooting and debugging systems, software and solutions for research and/or research development of product, services, and solutions for company’s portfolio. Requires a broad knowledge and application of engineering disciplines, methodologies and tools.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Providing pre and post sales technical support to the sales teams, customers and partners for various technologies and solutions related to Aruba products.</li>
<li>
Work on complex problems requiring in-depth fault analysis and troubleshooting.</li>
<li>
Generate documentation in the form of case notes, tech tips, best practices, RCA reports, and case reviews</li>
<li>
Formulate technical action plans for analyzing and resolving problems.</li>
<li>
Simulate customer issues in lab.</li>
<li>
Participate in solutions for knowledge base.</li>
<li>
Provide technical cross-training to relevant groups as needed.</li>
<li>
Participate in serviceability enhancement requirement reviews.</li>
<li>
Work effectively in a team environment including colleagues, QA, development engineers, and the Aruba field organization.</li>
<li>
Occasionally required to travel to customer sites for troubleshooting, training purposes, business reviews.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor degree in Computer Engineering/Electrical Engineering or equivalent is required.</li>
<li>
Minimum eight (5 + ) years of experience in customer facing remote or onsite Technical Support preferably with 5+ years in L3 escalation role</li>
<li>
Extensive experience with or knowledge of Network Management/Monitoring Suites (Zabbix, Nagios, Aruba AirWave) and Network Access Control technologies (Microsoft NPS, Cisco ISE, Aruba ClearPass).</li>
<li>
Familiarity with system administration and operation. Basic system performance analysis, Linux shell, and Windows Active Directory skills a plus.</li>
<li>
Must be a flexible self-starter who can ramp up with new technologies quickly and can adapt to a growing and evolving team.</li>
<li>
Strong work ethic and a “can-do/will do” attitude.</li>
<li>
Comprehensive understanding of networking technology and the ability to quickly acquire deep technical knowledge.</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of LAN and WAN networking protocols and technologies.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational and time management skills.</li>
<li>
Must be customer focused and have the ability to manage and resolve complex technical issues.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills, outstanding presentation and interpersonal skills, and ability to confidently engage with the customer at all staff levels.</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask and work independently in a fast paced environment towards set business goals and objectives</li>
<li>
Well recognized industry certification like CCNA/CCNP is required, CCIE/CWNP preferred</li>
<li>
Understanding of 802.11 and other wireless technologies is highly desirable but not mandatory</li>
<li>
Experience with design and implementation of Aruba Networks products and solutions. ACMP, ACMX, ACDX, or ACCX certification is an added bonus</li>
<li>
Extensive experience in Docker Swarm, Kubernetes container technology</li>
<li>
Intermediate exposure to SIEM (Splunk, Qradar, Nitro, or Arcsight)</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of JSON, Yaml data formats used for log management</li>
<li>
Minimum 2 years of experience in the security industry and security threat landscape</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience in BigData (Hortonworks, Cloudera, Hadoop, Kafka) platforms</li>
<li>
Working knowledge in debugging issues with any of the following: ElasticSearch, Hbase, Zookeeper, and HDFS</li>
<li>
Proactive interaction with other teams to drive resolution of hardware / software deficiencies / bugs</li>
<li>
Intermediate to Expert level knowledge with Phyton and bash scripting</li>
</ul>
