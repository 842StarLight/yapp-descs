<h2>Client Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Global provider of market leading solutions. Dynamic team culture. Competitive bonus programs. Collaborative cross-functional teams. World class benefits. Challenging and Rewarding work.</p>
<p>
At Aderant, we power the world’s leading law and professional services firms. We focus on delivering world class products and services which are developed, marketed, sold, and supported by superior talent. The expertise and talent of our people have made us the largest independent provider of law firm technology in the world, and our people will drive our success in the future.</p>
<p>
We are looking for an Account Manager who will partner with and ensure the long-term success of our customers. You will be responsible for developing long-term relationships with your portfolio of assigned clients. You will liaise between clients and cross-functional internal teams to drive the adoption, retention and successful delivery of our solutions according to client needs.</p>
<p>
<b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage relationships with assigned clients with a focus on; adoption, retention, and satisfaction.</li>
<li>Work effectively with Account Executive and coordinate involvement with other multidisciplinary personnel, including Support, Service, and Product Management, in order to meet account objectives and client expectations.</li>
<li>Create a trusted/strategic advisor relationship with key personnel in assigned client accounts and maintain strong, long-lasting customer relationships.</li>
<li>Partner with clients to establish goals and key performance indicators and aid the customer in achieving their goals (includes building roadmaps, sharing best practices, product reviews and health check consulting).</li>
<li>Advocate customer needs/issues cross-departmentally.</li>
<li>Drive the adoption and successful delivery of our solutions according to customer needs and objectives.</li>
<li>Assist with high severity requests or issue escalations, as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Impeccable written, verbal communication / presentation skills.</li>
<li>Detail oriented and analytical. Strong team player but still a self-starter.</li>
<li>Thrives in a multi-tasking environment and can adjust priorities on-the-fly Requirements</li>
<li>1-2+ years of relevant experience.</li>
<li>Finance, Accounting or CPA background</li>
<li>Law Firm familiarity required</li>
<li>You’re driven: No one needs to push you to excel; it’s just who you are.</li>
<li>Eager to learn, adapt and perfect your work; you seek out help and put it to good use.</li>
<li>Deliver top service to our customers.</li>
<li>50% Travel</li>
</ul>
