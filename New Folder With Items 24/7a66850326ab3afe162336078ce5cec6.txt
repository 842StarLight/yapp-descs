<h2>Sr. Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Insight Catastrophe Group (ICG)</b> has an exciting opportunity to join our Operating Analytics team in the Atlanta, Georgia area. The Senior Data Analyst position is responsible for gathering, analyzing, and transforming data from many different sources. You will partner with Information Management and other teams, Sales primarily, to execute data analytics and operational initiatives.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>What you’ll be doing:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Supporting sales staff by providing sales data, market trends, forecasts, account analyses and new services information</li>
<li>
Proactively producing and analyzing data to develop insights about drivers of performance</li>
<li>
Building digital measurement solutions within Google Analytics and in-house reporting tools to provide valuable insights and recommendations to achieve targets</li>
<li>
Analyzing detailed web metrics to determine trends, successes, site issues, and opportunities</li>
<li>
Conducting actionable data analytics on critical projects</li>
<li>
Maintaining the reporting tools and dashboards to support day-to-day productivity management</li>
<li>
Taking an ownership role for data accuracy and integrity across the reporting interfaces</li>
<li>
Leveraging data from a variety of sources to analyze performance, identify areas of opportunity, and provide actionable insights to operations leaders and front-line team members</li>
<li>
Designing and conducting extensive analysis of performance trends and communicating the findings</li>
<li>
Designing and conducting Ad-hoc analysis when required</li>
<li>
Other job-related duties as requested</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
We’re looking for someone who:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
is experienced in data assimilation and transforming gathered data into meaningful insight for internal or external stakeholders</li>
<li>
is a self-starter that adapts well to change in a fast-paced, dynamic workplace and works well in cross-functional teams/projects</li>
<li>
has strong verbal and written communication skills with all levels of management</li>
<li>
has the ability to communicate complex information in a clear and concise manner</li>
<li>
has advanced Excel skills including experience with Pivot tables and complex functions</li>
<li>
exhibits strong organization skills with attention to detail</li>
<li>
is familiar with database interface tools, SQL and programming concepts</li>
<li>
has Demonstrated the ability to work with large datasets and tell a story using the data</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Qualified candidates have:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or related field experience preferably in the insurance industry</li>
<li>
Advanced proficiency in SQL, Excel, relational databases and building decision support models</li>
<li>
Experience with Tableau, Microsoft Power BI or other visualization tools</li>
<li>
3+ years of experience in analytical role (analysis, information science, data visualization or other relevant quantitative fields)</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Highly preferred candidates have:</b></div>
<ul><li>
1 or more years of experience with Salesforce or similar CRM systems</li></ul>
