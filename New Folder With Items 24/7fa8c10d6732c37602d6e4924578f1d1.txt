<p></p>
<p>KAR is seeking a <b>Legal Administrative Assistant </b>to join its legal team in Carmel, Indiana. The Legal Assistant must understand business priorities, demonstrate strong prioritization and multi-tasking, proactively anticipate needs and drive improvements for the team. The ideal candidate will be professional, highly energetic and personable with exceptional attention to detail.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide administrative support to the legal department, including but not limited to maintaining files and file systems, preparing expense reports, managing incoming mail and correspondence, monitoring service of process, and planning and scheduling meetings, conferences and travel.
</li>
<li>Support the implementation and maintenance of the legal department’s document management system, including identifying and implementing process improvements within the legal department and the enterprise-wide contracts management system.
</li>
<li>Assist with matter intake, including creating, organizing and maintaining various legal files and systems.
</li>
<li>Monitor and process legal expenditures and invoices, ensuring that they are timely received, reviewed, approved and paid.
</li>
<li>Facilitate the execution of signature requests from colleagues and business partners globally, including obtaining signatures (both electronic and hardcopy) for agreements and providing guidance on authorized signatories.
</li>
<li>Assist with managing corporate governance and entity management for KAR and its subsidiaries, including preparing written consents, resignations, minutes and resolutions and maintaining entity minute books and global entity organizational charts.
</li>
<li>Assist with regulatory licensing and compliance matters, including assembling necessary applications for various entities and filing annual reports.
</li>
<li>Prepare routine and non-routine documents and reports, including gathering and summarizing data.
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Educational Requirements and Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s or Associate’s degree preferred.
</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in a law firm or in-house legal department (law firm experience preferred).
</li>
<li>Must be able to manage a heavy workload in a fast-paced environment, including the ability to prioritize and handle multiple tasks with frequent interruptions.
</li>
<li>Must be detail-oriented and organized with excellent communications skills (written and verbal) and interpersonal skills in dealing both inside and outside the organization.
</li>
<li>Must demonstrate high level of integrity and discretion in handling confidential information as well as professionalism in dealings inside and outside the company.
</li>
<li>Must be proficient with the Microsoft Office suite of products and have the ability to learn all applicable business and matter management systems, including CSC Matter Management, NetDocuments, DocuSign, SpringCM and Salesforce.
</li>
</ul>
