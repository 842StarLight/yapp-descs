<h2>Manager, Marketing and Communications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Responsible for all aspects of RGB Spectrum's traditional, content and digital marketing programs.</p>
<p><b>Work Performed</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute integrated campaign strategies to build awareness and drive engagement in target vertical markets for specific buyer personas</li>
<li>Create and execute demand generation programs to grow leads and fill Sales pipeline funnel</li>
<li>Create and execute comprehensive marketing strategies across all channels: digital, social, print, PR, e-mail and events</li>
<li>Oversee the planning, creation, and production of marketing materials including website, social media, advertising, newsletters, videos, and other product collateral</li>
<li>Work closely with product managers to translate technical product differentiators into customer and end-user friendly messaging</li>
<li>Manage press engagement/coverage in concert with external public relations company</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and knowledge required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills, with proven expertise adapting messaging to target multiple audiences</li>
<li>Experience marketing technology products and strong aptitude to position them to a non-technical target audience</li>
<li>Working knowledge of SEO, social media, web metric measurement tools such as Google Analytics.</li>
<li>Experience using CRM tools to manage and analyze campaigns</li>
<li>Working knowledge of PPC online advertising and sponsored content platforms, Ex. Google Ads, LinkedIn</li>
<li>Experience leveraging and managing 3rd party resources such as agencies, writers and graphic designers</li>
<li>Good planning, organizational, and time management skills. Able to manage multiple simultaneous projects.</li>
<li>Background and knowledge of the professional audio and video market is preferred</li>
<li>Background in the IT channel is desired</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of related marketing experience</li>
<li>Competency with Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel) and Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, Premier)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and training:</b></p>
<ul><li>
<p>College degree in English, Business Administration, Marketing, Technical Communications, or similar</p>
</li></ul>

Vision abilities required by this job include being able to use a computer and printouts of documents and spreadsheets.</p>
<p>The noise level in the work environment is usually quiet to moderate.</p>

<p><b>About RGB Spectrum</b></p>
<p>Founded in 1987, RGB Spectrum provides innovative solutions for the display, recording, and transmission of computer and video signals. Company offerings include multiviewers, video wall processors, matrix switchers, codecs, recorders, and integrated solutions for control room management. RGB Spectrum's products are preferred by major global organizations in industrial, security, medical, educational, commercial and military markets.</p>
<p>The company provides a first class, modern building in Alameda, with quick access to all East Bay freeways. Our central location is convenient for shopping, restaurants, parks, and beaches, and also puts you just minutes from San Francisco and Berkeley.</p>
<p>RGB Spectrum offers an attractive compensation package with a range of benefits including 401(k), medical/dental/vision, FSA, HSA, tuition reimbursement, and commuter benefits.</p>
<p>We offer all the advantages of working in a smaller, more personalized environment; each employee can make a difference and directly contribute to the success of the company.</p>
<p>At RGB Spectrum, you will join a growing team of talented professionals, and be a part of an industry-leading organization that has a long history of innovation.</p>
