<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Do you love how it feels to create a genuine community? Do you have a passion for building relationships and a deep curiosity around the rapidly evolving social media landscape? Zyper is seeking a highly qualified and motivated Account Manager to be the face of our platform and manage direct relationships with our brand partners. You will be directly responsible for delighting our customers and driving them to renew year after year. This candidate should be seeking the challenge of helping fuel the growth of an exciting, dynamic organization.
<br>
<br>
<b>Company Overview:</b>
<br>
Zyper is a consumer facing software tool at the forefront of the dramatic landscape shift in the advertising industry. We’re putting the power back into the hands of the people- building communities and using brands as a vehicle for creativity, connections and innovation. We are obsessed with consumer and customer connections; and fascinated by people's online identities and interactions. Zyper's technology uses a combination of natural language processing and computer vision to identify highly engaged friendship groups and their brand affinities. We enable brands to identify their top 1% of fans and turn them into an active community of brand advocates.
<br>
<br>
<b>The Role:</b>
<br>
Reporting directly to the Executive Director of Sales, the Account Manager will own the day to day of Zyper’s client relationships. This position plays a key role in making sure our clients understand the full value of the platform and constantly iterate their use cases in order to grow with Zyper. This role is responsible for executing against client strategic initiatives and ensuring clients see results. This key team member is integral in delighting clients and bringing their objectives to life. This role will also work closely with sales to maximize strategic up-sell opportunities while providing insight to increase client retention.
<br>
<br>
<b>Your Responsibilities:</b>
<br>
Own the day to day relationship with clients, empowering them to use Zyper on a daily basis
<br>
Collaborate with Sales, Client Services, and Community teams to transition clients from the onboarding to the ongoing phase of the partnership, bringing initial objectives to life and actively managing expectations
<br>
Maintain active conversation via status calls and delightful emails to assure clients understand the full value of the tool
<br>
Refresh community/campaign guidelines on a regular basis in accord with the client’s marketing calendar
<br>
Deliver weekly reports via Zyper’s analytics dashboard
<br>
Proactively analyze platform usage and share insights that drive clients to extend community content &amp; conversation to their other marketing channels
<br>
Educate clients around new product releases and monitor feedback
<br>
Lead quarterly/annual business reviews to prove value delivered around the partnership
<br>
Document common best practices and challenges across clients, constantly learning in order to create scalable processes
<br>
