<h2>Education Community Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Community Manager, Education
<br>Job Description
<br><br>
WeVideo is on the hunt for a Community Manager with a passion for education and a talent for organization, process, and details. We are looking for an all-star who has passion for the K-12 education community, can handle technical tools, and has a knack for details while keeping an eye on our overarching vision. WeVideo is the market leader in video creation and storytelling tools for the education market. Become our community-building expert to help us continue to grow and make our digital creativity platform available in classrooms everywhere.
<br><br>
As a Community Manager, you will coordinate between our education users (classroom teachers, instructional coaches, school leaders), sales and marketing teams, and WeVideo Ambassadors to ensure our customers are fully engaged with WeVideo. Through our educator community, you will develop creative and popular programs, activities, and initiatives which transform our engaged users into active advocates and evangelists, helping us meet business objectives for increased awareness and new business growth in K-12.
<br><br>
If you are solution-driven, people-focused, passionate about K-12 education and looking for an opportunity to make an impact, we are looking for you.
<br><br>
