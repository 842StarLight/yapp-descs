<h2>Senior Software Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Kaliburn Inc., a subsidiary of Lincoln Electric, engineers and manufactures plasma cutting systems, plasma torches, plasma consumables and control system units for high-tech cutting applications. Building new products that constantly raise the bar in the industry is possible through a strong foundation of engineering talent such as mechanical, fluid dynamics, heat transfer and software engineers.</p>
<p>
The Senior Software Engineer will design, modify, develop, write, and implement software programming applications.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Complete full life cycle development using structured design methodologies including test driven development, unit testing, code reviews and scrums</li>
<li>
Design, develop, test and maintain Windows services and applications</li>
<li>
Diagnose, resolve software defects, and write unit tests to validate solution</li>
<li>
Work closely with test analysts to develop regression and automated test systems</li>
<li>
Complete manager assigned tasks across concurrent projects delivering on team commitments</li>
<li>
Validate products in cutting lab and, occasionally, at third party sites</li>
<li>
Communicate an accurate state of work to cross functional team
</li>
<li>Deliver high quality, reliable, testable, reusable software that aligns to requirements</li>
<li>
Effectively communicate risk with full transparency
</li>
<li>Work effectively within a cross-functional team to deliver quality products on time</li>
<li>
Follow software repository management policy</li>
<li>
Estimate task duration based on functional requirements</li>
<li>
Participate in design and code reviews</li>
<li>
Develop architecture and write well designed, testable, reusable code</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
REQUIRED SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics or an Accredited Bachelors of Science Degree in an engineering program focused on software development</li>
<li>
Proven 5+ years’ experience developing commercial software</li>
<li>
Ability to plan and estimate task duration</li>
<li>
Strong and current commercial programming experience with C# or similar object-oriented language</li>
<li>
Object Orientation analysis, design and programming</li>
<li>
Strong analytical &amp; reasoning skills with ability to visualize solutions and outcome</li>
<li>
Passion for solving problems
</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of multi-threaded parallel processing methodologies</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of real time systems</li>
<li>
Deep understanding of design patterns and software design methodologies</li>
<li>
Excellent knowledge of rationale databases, SQL and ORM technologies</li>
<li>
Technical, persistent mindset with strong attention to detail<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
PREFERRED SKILLS</b></p>

Knowledge and application of graph theory</li>
<li>
Interfaces to material management systems</li>

WPF and MVVM design pattern</li>
<li>
Entity Framework or similar persistence framework</li>

<li>
Ability to read and understand electronic schematics<br>
