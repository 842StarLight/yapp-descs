 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div>
<p>The Business Development Executive is responsible for selling national and regional HVAC Preventative Maintenance and HVAC Planned Unit Replacements Programs.</p>
<p>
Target markets include multi-location retail, fitness, financial, telecommunications, healthcare and distribution centers across the United States and Canada.</p>
<p>
The sales role includes cultivating relationships and building trus, asking questions and qualifying propects, problem solving and devloping solutions,
</p>
<p>along with closing sales and mobilizing new business in a competitive, fast paced environment.</p>

<ul>
<li>Create strong relationships with key client stakeholders at both senior and mid-management levels</li>
<li>

<li>
Understand the competitive landscape and market trends</li>
<li>

<li>
Desire to own projects and exceed expectations, with ability to find solutions and deliver results within a rapidly changing, entrepreneurial, technology-driven culture</li>
<li>
Ability to identify and solve client issues strategically</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal skills, with the ability to communicate effectively with management and cross-functional teams, for both technical and non-technical audiences</li>
