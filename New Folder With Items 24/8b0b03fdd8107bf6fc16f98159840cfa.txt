<h2>Senior Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h3>
<p>
This is a full or part-time marketing position with a focus on a broad marketing and leadership skill set, including marketing strategy, advertising, client relationships, and general problem-solving. This is an ideal position for a self-motivated individual who also has a passion for working with a small team, learning about a wide selection of technologies, tools, and ideas, and helping clients grow their business.</p>
<p>
Most of all, as a small team collaboration is a strong focus for our culture. We will work together to succeed and give guidance along the way. You will work directly with our team in a virtual environment with a select group of &amp;Marketing clients to create and execute marketing projects. This is a part or full-time role with plenty of room for growth as individual and company performance warrants.</p>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>
15+ years of traditional marketing background - solid strategy, marketing and/or sales experience<br>
Experience in entrepreneurial, startup, or mid-size companies</p>
<ul><li><p>
Consulting, marketing agency, VC, or PE experience a plus</p></li></ul>
</li>
<li><p>
Experience in coaching or teaching within organizations</p></li>
<li>
<p>
Self-Starter and “Roll Up Your Sleeves” Attitude</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Able to Work Independently with a remote team, but travel occasionally on an as needed basis</p></li>
<li><p>
Low tolerance for bureaucracy, and bias for speed</p></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><p>
Must work remotely in one of the following geographic areas: Greater Philadelphia, Central Indiana, Greater Chicago</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Lead senior level of client relationship to deliver outsourced marketing services as "account manager' with full accountability for client satisfaction and meeting their KPIs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Work with a small team of peers and junior level personnel to develop marketing strategy and complete marketing execution deliverables under your direction (digital marketing, research, project management, content development, social media, SEO / PPC advertising, PR, events, etc) - with full accountability for internal metrics.</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible for growing the business - either through existing customers if appropriate, or new client acquisition, and improving the organization's culture and internal work processes.</p></li>
</ul>
