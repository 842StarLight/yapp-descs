<h2>Manager, Key Accounts</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>View’s mission is to <i>create delightful human environments</i>.<br>
<br>
In the great tradition of challenging the way we think about everyday essentials, the team at View has reinvented something that hasn't changed for centuries – the simple window – and in doing so, is transforming the largest industry in the world: real estate.<br>
<br>
We manufacture a new generation of smart windows that let in natural light and views, and enhance mental and physical wellbeing by significantly reducing headaches, eyestrain and drowsiness. In addition, View’s windows reduce glare and heat, improving the energy efficiency of buildings by 20 percent. View’s windows are digital, cloud connected, and can be controlled from anywhere, including your phone. We are also leveraging advances in AI to develop smart building and IoT applications that make buildings healthier, smarter and more productive than anyone ever imagined.<br>
<br>
View is already installed in over 50 million square feet and is growing rapidly. If you dream of changing the world to make it a better place – join us.<br>
<br>
POSITION SUMMARY</p>
<p>
The Key Accounts Manager will be responsible for growing View’s business in our target verticals (commercial property developers, equity investors, &amp; corporate offices healthcare, and/or higher education) and establishing View as the design-in/preferred choice in these verticals. The manager will establish strong executive and decision-maker relationships at target accounts in each vertical, with an ultimate goal to drive portfolio deals and establish View as the default choice at these accounts. The manager and team will work closely with the View sales team to close on-going sales opportunities, and also identify and develop new sales opportunities from their customer executive relationships to drive these to successful wins. Each vertical manager in the team will develop the content expertise, relationships, record of successful wins, and public profile to be considered a thought leader in his/her vertical by both customers and internal View employees (executives and account/sales executives).</p>
<p>
Key deliverables</p>
<ul>
<li>
Total business won in each vertical, and specifically wins that are attributable to the Key Accounts team’s origination and/or significant contributions.</li>
<li>
Total business volume, significant wins and market share at target key accounts in each vertical.</li>
<li>
Repeat business at each key account and in each vertical.</li>
<li>
Thought leadership, as exhibited by speaking engagements at conferences and industry events.</li>
<li>
Strong executive relationships at key accounts in each vertical.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Key Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience in and ability to successfully drive and close wins at large, enterprise accounts. Must have the ability to navigate large, complex organizations, proactively identify and resolve roadblocks, and develop an effective account and vertical strategies.</li>
<li>
Strong influencing and persuasion skills towards potential customers, View account executives and View executives. Must be politely relentless – Smart Glass is still a new product category so market need and a sale is not automatic. The Key Accounts team needs to convince customers of the benefits of adopting View across their portfolios and organizations.</li>
<li>
Strong executive presence; ability to build relationships at different levels of customer organizations, from CxO to mid-level managers.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical and strategic skills – necessary to formulate and then execute the strategy for each vertical. The Manager needs to guide the vertical owners to formulate, articulate and get executive buy-in for on strategy (e.g., market segments and accounts to target in each vertical, rationale, and path to achieve the best outcomes), and then drive successful execution. There is a strong bias towards measurable results.</li>
<li>
Ability to hold team accountable for delivering results, coach team members, and recognize when team members need help and roll up sleeves to execute.</li>
<li>
Adaptability: View is in an exciting, hyper-growth phase, and the needs of the company will continue to evolve. The Manager must drive execution today, but also have a clear vision of the company needs in 2 and 3 years, and build the team and skills to achieve these.</li>
<li>
Humility, ability to provide and receive candid feedback, and ability to learn.</li>
<li>
Success factors: being relentless, self-driven, possesses an ownership mindset, and a strong blend of strategic and execution skills. Key account and BD experience, intelligence, and high motivation level and drive are table stakes.</li>
</ul>
