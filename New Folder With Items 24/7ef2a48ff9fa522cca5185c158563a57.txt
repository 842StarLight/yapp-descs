<div><b>SUMMARY
</b></div>
<p>Responsible for retrofit and repair of environmental-comfort systems, utilizing knowledge of air conditioning theory, pipe fitting, and mechanical layouts</p>
<div></div>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Other functions may be assigned.</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Responsible for servicing products and equipment on assigned projects and ensuring customer satisfaction</p></li>
<li><p>
Uses a variety of hand-tools, following blueprints or engineering specifications, to diagnose and repair units.
</p></li>
<li><p>Identifies, analyzes, diagnoses, and repairs systems and products at customer's location.</p></li>
<li><p>
Performs preventative maintenance, site surveys, replacement, and modifications as need or requested by customers.</p></li>
<li><p>
Prepares for on-site installation and repairs by examining building layout, anticipating difficulties, gathering materials and coordinating on-site work, as necessary.</p></li>
<li><p>
Inspects vehicles by checking vehicle condition and literature supplies</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintains vehicles by keeping records of service manuals, scheduling, and complete repairs and maintenance service</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintains equipment by inspecting for signs of war.</p></li>
<li><p>
Documents work by completing paperwork on each job and maintaining files</p></li>
<li><p>
Represents company by serving as a direct customer contact.
</p></li>
<li><p>Provides re-training and follow-up training at customer site.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provides sales leads to Project Manager</p></li>
<li><p>
Determines parts to order for repairs and timeliness of need</p></li>
<li><p>
Documents work by completing paperwork on each job, including daily time, progress, and duration; and maintaining files.</p></li>
<li><p>
Records parts, material, labor, subs and other cost data per assignment and returns unused resources.</p></li>
<li><p>
Turns in all required paperwork and reports in a timely manner.</p></li>
<li><p>
Keeps current on all Trane products concerning installation, operation, maintenance, service and repair</p></li>
<li><p>
Read and interpret product specifications and experience in working with building specifications, drawings and designs</p></li>
<li><p>
Provides technical support to customers</p></li>
<li><p>
Flexibility to work overtime/weekends as necessary
</p></li>
<li>
<div>Regular travel requirements with some overnight travel</div>
