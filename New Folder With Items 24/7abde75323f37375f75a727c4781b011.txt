<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY: Contract-to-Hire / Possible Direct Hire for PERFECT Candidate</b></p>
<p>The Senior Project Manager / Business Analyst / Certified SCRUM Master / Process Engineer will work directly with the lines of business to gather detailed requirements and participate in the end to end development of projects, epics and user stories. Ensures active participation from both the Technology Department and the business through Product Owners, and manages budgets, effort, scope, estimates, and project schedules. Works as an independent resource, interacting daily with all levels of management within all the lines of business and within the Technology Department, providing strong leadership and an open channel of communication. Partners with the Director, Digital Strategy and Analytics to communicate a crisp strategic vision for the Technology Department and for the Institute in all matters related to innovation and technology solutions.</p>
<p>A key responsibility is to work closely with Product Owners as part of the Scrum Team to develop User Stories for the lines of business at ANAB/ANSI. This may mean having to supervise additional Business Analysts and review/approve their work.</p>
<p>This role is also be responsible for providing oversight over ANAB.org rebuild to combine it with legacy ANSI.org accreditation content, including design refresh and search integration with backend systems that provide accreditation status and other key and critical pieces of information. Support activity and initiatives related to ANAB infrastructure integration and AUDEEE Integration with ANAB.org. Management and/or assistance with budgeting, resource planning, roadmap maintenance, operation of agile process. Working collaboratively with the Director, Agile Software Development, the SENIOR BA/PM / Certified SCRUM Master / Process Engineer ensures that proper staffing and excellent delivery is provided across all development efforts. Ensures that ANSI’s software development practices and quality standards are followed, and that the quality standards are met or exceeded by all solutions. Works closely with the DevOps, Agile, and Release Management leads to ensure that methodologies, technologies and automation are implemented for all development efforts.</p>
