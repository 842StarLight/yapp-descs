<h2>Deductions Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Huffy Corporation is looking for a Deductions Analyst. Our Deductions Analyst is responsible for all customer deductions / chargebacks for Huffy Corporation. This includes the clearance of authorized deductions and the collection of unauthorized deductions by Huffy’s customers. Responsible for the timely flow of customer deductions data between departments and the maintenance of all related records.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Plans and maintains a program for systematic follow-up and collection of delinquent deductions, including monthly reporting and collection of invalid deductions from customers.
</li>
<li>Maintain documentation file and match proper paperwork. Analyze and determine whether the deductions are valid and chargeable to the appropriate general ledger and account number.
</li>
<li>Insure all deductions carry proper approval according to company policies.
</li>
<li>Communicate with customers, sales representatives and other Huffy constituents to resolve deduction problems.
</li>
<li>Analyze and measure the validity of deductions and their proper accounting within Huffy systems. Proactively research to control the outstanding deduction balance.
</li>
<li>Identify and track all licenses promotional claims, including defectives, on a regular basis. Create quarterly spreadsheets for all data collected.
</li>
<li>Communicate on a regular basis to assure all Huffy departments are working within the guidelines of their agreements on issues such as pricing, shipping terms, vendor compliance, etc. including promo / advertising (overbill) programs.
</li>
<li>Responsible for monitoring Sales Representatives’ promotional allowance programs (Huffy Overbill) making sure customers are not taking claims outside of what is allowed within their promotional program. Ensure JDE Customer accrual set-up and Non-Standards are correct and up-to-date. Month-end reconciliation and distribution of reports to Sales Department and others. Collect back any unauthorized claims taken by Customers.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
