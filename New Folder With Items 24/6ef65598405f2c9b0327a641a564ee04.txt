<b>
Position Summary</b><br>
Responsible for planning and conducting company's load research studies and performing related load research and pricing analyses. Responsible for modeling complex business problems, discovering insights from load research data through the use of statistical, algorithmic, mining and visualization techniques. Provide technical and regulatory expertise in the development and presentation of major forecasting and risk assessment proposals/projects and regulatory filings. Provide strategic project leadership to provide timely and accurate load and gas research analysis.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Design load research studies applying appropriate sampling technique and theory. Be an advocate for integrity of internal historical data, subscription data, and forecast data. Perform company's load research studies for use by various departments consistent with industry standards.</li>
<li>Plan, design, and implement strategic load research projects. Prepare strategic statistical analysis, reports, and actionable recommendations on the electric and gas load characteristics within all state service territories as input to rate cases, system planning, and compliance. Ensure that results of load studies are an accurate key input to rate class cost of service studies, pricing design, peak forecasting, DSM/load management program planning, transmission and distribution system planning, and power purchases.</li>
<li>Coordinate with business units and provide them expert technical, statistical, and regulatory advice in meeting their business needs. Help develop forecasting and risk assessment proposals and analyses to support various business unit objectives. Respond to requests for analyses by business units and for regulatory filings. Conduct ad hoc analysis and simulations upon request. Participate in and lead load research projects.</li>
<li>Defend load research analyses in contested regulatory proceedings through written and/or oral testimony. Support the requests of internal organizations that request expert load research consulting services. Assist cross-departmental efforts to develop, collect, and analyze information and data required to support programs that meet corporate goals.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Minimum Requirements</b>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's of Science or Bachelor's of Arts degree in economics, engineering, mathematics, physics, quantitative analysis, business or a related discipline, or equivalent experience (two years professional related experience equivalent to one year of college), in addition to 8 or more years of electric utility or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>Quantitative graduate degree preferred.</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of SAS and/or similar statistical software or programming languages.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of database design and use of SQL Server/Developer and/or similar database software.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication including presentation skills with internal and external stakeholders, including a demonstrated ability to present results to audiences with varying levels of technical expertise.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and project management skills.</li>
<li>Able to handle multiple assignments with a high level of attention to detail.</li>
<br>
</ul>
