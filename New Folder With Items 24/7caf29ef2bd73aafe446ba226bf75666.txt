<h2>Accounts Receivable Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Brand Junkie was founded in 1997 as a promotional marketing company. We specialize in embroidery, screen printing, and promotional products for corporations, schools, and organizations of all sizes. Our company sets the standard with our creative ability, customer service, and quality.</p>
<p>We are looking for an energetic &amp; motivated individual to join our New Orleans team!</p>
<p>The perfect candidate will have the following attributes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Enjoys working in a fast-paced and dynamic work environment.</li>
<li>Accountability</li>
<li>Integrity</li>
<li>Respectfulness</li>
<li>Eagerness to learn new skills and advance within the organization</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review unbilled sales and create invoices accordingly</li>
<li>Performing day to day financial transactions, including verifying, classifying, computing, posting and recording accounts receivables’ data.</li>
<li>Process and apply customer payments.</li>
<li>Facilitate payment of invoices due by sending bill reminders and contacting clients.</li>
<li>Reconcile the accounts receivable ledger to ensure that all payments and invoices are accounted for and properly posted.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Detail oriented with strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Strong data entry skills</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of Microsoft Office programs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in finance, accounting, business administration or related field and/or 1 year work experience in a similar position</li>
<li>Proficiency in QuickBooks Enterprise software or similar software</li>
</ul>
