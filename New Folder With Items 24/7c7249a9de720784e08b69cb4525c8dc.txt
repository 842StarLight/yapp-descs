<h2>Senior Manager Business Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>At Discover, be part of a culture where diversity, teamwork and collaboration reign. Join a company that is just as employee-focused as it is on its customers and is consistently awarded for both. We’re all about people, and our employees are why Discover is a great place to work. Be the reason we help millions of consumers build a brighter financial future and achieve yours along the way with a rewarding career.</p>
<p>
Responsible for managing a team of professionals that leads the development of infrastructure systems, network releases, and network off-cycle projects.</p>
<p>
Join our team as a Senior Manager within the Discover Global Network business Operations area, helping to lead the build of our next generation of Platform capabilities and help Discover not just achieve our vision, but who are keen to learn new technologies, grow their skills and, most importantly, drive the evolution of growing a highly skilled workforce.</p>
<p>
We’re looking for passionate, collaborative individuals that can help shape the evolution our payment networks and position Discover Financial Services as the leading direct bank and payments partner who is responsible for the delivery &amp; support of multiple value streams while effectively managing a team of Business Analysts, including hiring, directing, mentoring, and assigning work.</p>
<p>
As a key member of the Payment Network leadership team within Operations the individual will be expected to collaborate with key business and technology partners in helping to define the strategy and execution of our application development and architecture.</p>
<p>
We embrace change and evolution, both in our staff and systems. We are building new payment systems for our networks that allow us to utilize modern technologies (APIs , Cloud, Big Data, Open Source, Event driven real time processing) and approaches to delivery (Scaled Agile, CI/CD, TDD + BDD) that will enable us to produce software and systems to support our business and adapt to change within the fast paced FinTech world.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Analyzes and develops requirements for data, workflow, logical processes, and system interfaces.</li>
<li>Plans, implements, and executes projects aligning to the appropriate release cycles.</li>
<li>Communicates project issues and achievements to the business and management.</li>
<li>Manages a team including hiring, performance, coaching, and development decisions.</li>
<li>Translates business goals of projects and directs the team in developing solutions that result in positive customer experiences.</li>
<li>Coaches and mentors team members. Supports individuals to achieve their individual development goals. Utilizes Agile and Lean practices to identify and solve systemic issues.</li>
<li>Utilizes tools to support data-driven decision making. Identifies and removes blockers to enable the team to meet business goals.</li>
<li>Lead a team of business analysists &amp; product owners responsible for the creation and elaboration of our payments network capabilities.</li>
<li>Oversee delivery of software through continuously deployed solutions via the right tools; i.e. SAFe/Scrum/Kanban agile methodologies.</li>
<li>Be a strong people manager, establishing goals for the team, evaluating performance, ensuring focus and motivation and providing coaching and guidance for team members.</li>
<li>Be reliable, self-motivated and willing to accept responsibility and accountability.</li>
<li>Be flexible and adaptable to change in an ever changing marketplace</li>
<li>Drive a culture of quality, innovation and best practice within your teams</li>
<li>Coach and develop our future talent</li>
<li>Conceptualize and communicate strategic approaches</li>
<li>Promote a risk aware culture within the team to ensure efficient and effective risk compliance management practices by adhering to required standards and processes that support the DFS approach to audit and compliance.</li>
</ul>
<p>Minimum Qualifications</p>
<p>
At a minimum, here’s what we need from you:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Networking or IT, or related field</li>
<li>6+ years of experience in Networking, IT, or related field</li>
<li>In lieu of a degree, 8+ years of Networking, IT, or related field</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Qualifications</p>
<p>
If we had our say, we’d also look for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Master’s Degree in Networking, IT, or related field</li>
<li>8+ years of experience in Networking, IT, or related field</li>
<li>Proven track record of delivering quality in an Agile environment</li>
<li>Proven track record of turning concepts and ideas into tangible solutions</li>
<li>Strong communication skills with proven ability to articulate vision and inspire teams</li>
<li>Self-motivated, Commercial understanding of the Payments domain</li>
<li>Strong knowledge and understanding of emerging trends in software engineering</li>
<li>Proven experience in coaching teams #LI- BG1
</li>
</ul>
