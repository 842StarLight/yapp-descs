<p>Primary Function
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are seeking a talented <b>Thermal Engineer </b>to join our team at A. O. Smith. This position is located at our newly opened Lloyd R. Smith Corporate Technology Center; a 42,700-square-foot research and development lab located in Milwaukee, WI. In this role, you will use your previous experience working in a thermal lab, setting up, designing, running, and analyzing lab projects.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Set up, design, run and analyze lab projects in a thermal lab setting</li>
<li>
Work with industry and universities to develop new technologies and standards</li>
<li>
Acts as technical leader to others within the company and provides technical direction to co-workers in area of expertise</li>
<li>
Collaborate with others on writing patents, papers, or trade secrets</li>
<li>
Direct use of specific CAE software such as Solidworks, MATLAB, LabView, Minitab, EES, CFD, FEA, etc. and interprets results</li>
<li>
Use and apply advanced engineering principles within the field of thermal sciences</li>
<li>
Participates in regular collaboration with product design and manufacturing teams; may lead functional best practice meetings, and has a recognized reputation of contributions to operations</li>
<li>
Effectively communicates technical material to internal business units</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering or related field</li>
<li>
Must have previous experience working in thermal lab setting up, designing, running, and analyzing lab projects</li>
<li>
Demonstrated expertise and accomplishments in the field of thermal sciences (heat transfer, fluid mechanics, thermodynamics)</li>
<li>
Experience with CAE tools such as Solidworks, MATLAB, LabView, Minitab, EES, CFD or FEA</li>
</ul>
