<p><b>
About the GreenLake Program Management for strategic initiatives and alliances</b></p>
<p>
HPE Pointnext services organization is currently seeking a Global Program Manager for its GreenLake Flex Capacity (FC) product management organization focusing on<b> </b>strategic initiatives and alliances. With GreenLake, Pointnext creates a consumption based pay-as-you-go service-based offering for customers, combining servers, storage, networking, software and services. This provides our customers with the highly desirable option of paying for the usage of their infrastructure on a variable basis monthly, with no upfront cost for the solution.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Flex Capacity Worldwide Portfolio team is responsible for developing and maintaining the most innovative data center on premise, private, and hybrid cloud offers in the market today, and continues to innovate the portfolio to maintain HPE’s substantial lead on our competition.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
We are seeking a Program Manager to support and lead teams through the process and implementation of company’s High Touch Services through new tools, route’s to market, business models deployments and go to market programs.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Manage activities, resource capability, schedules, budgets, and ensure cross company communications to facilitate product completion on schedule within budget. Work with Services management to identify and improve process and program efficiencies. Work can involve external parties such as software vendors, service providers and partners, etc.</p>
