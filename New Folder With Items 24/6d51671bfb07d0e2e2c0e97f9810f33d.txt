<p>Company Overview</p>
<p>
B&amp;H Mechanical Services (Indianapolis, IN) is a joint venture between BMWC Group (Indianapolis, IN) and The Hill Group (Chicago, IL) and is fully supported by both firms. B&amp;H Mechanical Services provides design and commercial construction services for the building, healthcare, manufacturing and pharmaceutical markets. B&amp;H Mechanical Services provides full service commercial HVAC, plumbing, sheet-metal, hydronics, process/utility piping, pipe and sheet-metal fabrication, modularization, medical gas, service, maintenance and provide all engineering, BIM/CAD and preconstruction services to support these service offerings.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The primary responsibility of this position is to maintain operational and administrative control of projects to which he/she is assigned, and to develop personnel, markets and technology in such a manner as to gain optimum profit and growth on a short term and long term basis.</p>
