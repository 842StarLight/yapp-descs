<p>
Oldcastle Infrastructure, a CRH company, is the leading manufacturer of precast concrete, polymer concrete, and plastic products in the United States. Our products range from utility vaults, reinforced concrete pipe, catch basins, drainage and septic tanks, to retaining walls, storm shelters, wall panels, concrete barriers, a variety of prestressed concrete products and more. With more than 80 locations nationwide and 3,000 plus employees, Oldcastle Infrastructure is committed to upholding core values of reliability, quality, and service in cutting edge ways.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Create and manage a production plan based on sales demand, forecasts, and inventory requirements to meet customer service goals for PA facilities.
</li>
<li>Create and provide weekly schedule based off the demand plan to manufacturing floor leads/supervisors, communicating product specifics, quantities required, and expectations on priorities.</li>
<li>
Schedule plant utilization, as well as mold/insert changes required to maximize efficient runs while meeting the demand plan. Enter/review/adjust production orders into ERP system as required.</li>
<li>
Resolve issues stemming from manufacturing delays promptly, communicating with the departments and customers affected with action plans being taken to minimize any negative impact.</li>
<li>
Review, analyze, and modify plans based on actual production activities and capacity utilization, and adjust short term plan to optimize service and inventory turns.</li>
<li>
Manage standard stock list at plants to balance service levels vs inventory cost, ensuring proper turns for different category items.</li>
<li>
Help identify and correct inventory variances stemming from production errors, mis-scans or any other discrepancies.</li>
<li>
Statistical analysis of sales data history to identify trends, sourcing improvement opportunities, and ability to level load production.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Sales and Marketing in coordinating new product launch, transition and/or discontinuation of products.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Project Leads and Transportation teams to address issues through continuous improvement in the order fulfillment process.</li>
<li>
Maintain key performance metrics surrounding production planning and plan attainment, as well as keep up to date various key data fields within the ERP system that drive master planning.</li>
<li>
Manage mold inventory across the Area, prioritize plant need.</li>
<li>
 Link sales and production priorities, which will drive Engineering priorities</li>
</ul>
