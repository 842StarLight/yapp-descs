<h2>Sales Manager - Natural Gas Software</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p><b>Sales Manager</b></p>
<p>Blackstone Technology Group is hiring a permanent <b>Sales Manager </b>who will drive NET new business within large accounts for our software solution Trellis Energy, Blackstone Technology Group’s natural gas pipeline management product.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Trellis Energy Background</b>:</p>
<p>Trellis Energy (trellisenergy.com) is an innovative San Francisco-based technology company bringing energy companies into the 21st century through advanced technology. Our Trellis ETMS product and solutions support the following Natural Gas transaction management domains:</p>
<ul>
<li>Interstate &amp; Intrastate pipelines</li>
<li>Gathering</li>
<li>Storage</li>
<li>LDC Gas Supply &amp; Customer Choice</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and drive strategy to hunt for qualified prospects through existing energy industry network or lead generation</li>
<li>Customer relationship management, customer calls, meetings, product demonstrations, prepare/ present proposal and drive the sales process to successful closing</li>
<li>Focus on driving NET NEW business for Trellis</li>
<li>Developing a sales pipeline to ensure continued meeting of revenue</li>
<li>Building strong relationships with key senior clients and helping closing key deals as necessary</li>
<li>Working with Marketing to drive lead generation</li>
<li>Maintaining in depth awareness of market and industry trends</li>
<li>Maintain accurate records of contacts and sales process</li>
<li>Produce and provide business plans quarterly to Blackstone senior management</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>10 + years of sales and client management experience selling Software (SaaS) based solutions</li>
<li>Energy/Oil and Gas/Power industry experience required; natural gas/pipeline experience highly preferred</li>
<li>Ability to provide strong client and management references</li>
<li>Proven track record of effectively communicating and presenting to the C-suite</li>
<li>Ability to strategically sell and lead all aspects of complex deals</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record of delivering against quota and excelling in a highly competitive market</li>
<li>Excellent knowledge of industry trends, technologies and pricing models</li>
<li>Passion for generating new business, hitting sales goals, and developing and building deep client relationships</li>
<li>Customer-focused leadership with excellent communication, networking, and demonstrated negotiation and consultative solution selling skills</li>
<li>Excellent listening and presentation skills</li>
<li>Strong time management skills; ability to prioritize and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of Microsoft Office and CRM software (PowerPoint, Word, Excel)</li>
<li>Confidently able to build both internal and external relationships</li>
<li>Documentation of formal sales and negotiation training a huge plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
