<h2>Digital Marketing Sales Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Reynolds and Reynolds develops and supports software for automotive retailers, featuring the industry’s only Retail Management System. As a Digital Marketing Sales Consultant, you will be responsible for selling our marketing, advertising, and digital media solutions to car dealerships within your territory.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the subject matter expert for our Digital Marketing Solutions</li>
<li>Be responsible for building and maintaining relationships with our current customers as well as finding new accounts</li>
<li>Give product demonstrations</li>
<li>Plan and implement a sales strategy within your region</li>
<li>Training consists of on-the-job training with a mentor in the field, online training courses, and classroom instruction at our headquarters (Dayton, OH)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>1+ years of sales experience</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to build strong customer relationships</li>
<li>Must be a self-starter with good time management skills</li>
<li>Sales, advertising, or marketing experience preferred</li>
<li>Ability to travel overnight 5 nights/month</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Company car for business and personal use</li>
<li>iPad, iPhone, laptop and home office equipment</li>
<li>Medical, dental, vision, and life insurance</li>
<li>401(k) with up to 6% matching</li>
<li>Professional development and training</li>
<li>Promotion from within</li>
<li>Paid vacation and sick days</li>
<li>Paid holidays</li>
<li>Referral bonuses</li>
<li>Discounts for cell phones, cars, computers, entertainment, and much more</li>
</ul>
<p>Reynolds and Reynolds promotes a healthy lifestyle by providing a non-smoking environment. We encourage applicants of all ages and experience to apply, as we do not discriminate on the basis of age</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $90,000.00 /year</p>
