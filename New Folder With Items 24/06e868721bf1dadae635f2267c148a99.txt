<h2>Shipping Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Caring Transitions of Charleston gives families peace of mind by managing all the details and work that go into their relocation. We also specialize in downsizing, senior relocation, clean-out services, online auctions, and estate sales. The quality of our services truly depends upon the caring professionals who are a part of our team. Each employee is important to our success!</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a <b>Shipping Supervisor/Material Coordinator</b> to manage daily shipping and distribution operations. You will be the one to ensure complete orders are shipped and delivered in a timely manner.</p>
<p>A successful shipping Supervisor is analytical, well-organized and excels in communication. They must be comfortable with paperwork and information systems and have great knowledge of shipping and receiving procedures.</p>
<p>The goal is to ensure that the shipping processes are running smoothly and effectively.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities : </b><i>(<b>computer skills are required</b>)</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Use software to send invoices and manage all shipping payments.</li>
<li>Direct the flow of packages from preparation to shipment.</li>
<li>Inspect labels, barcodes and other features of completed orders.</li>
<li>Collaborate with warehouse, purchasing and other managers to optimize processes.</li>
<li>Resolve issues regarding shipped orders.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance to legal regulations and company policies.</li>
<li>Report to management on activities and issues.</li>
</ul>
<p>*<br>Ensure that shipping is packed properly.</p>
<p><i>Other: </i></p>
<p>*<br>Deliver moving/ packing material to appropriate jobs.</p>
<p>-May require occasional heavy lifting (up to 50 pounds), Saturday shifts, and early morning shifts.</p>
