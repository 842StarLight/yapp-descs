<b>About you:</b><br>
We are seeking a Director of Sales to create and execute a sales strategy to drive growth for our rapidly growing sales organization. The ideal candidate is passionate about staff development, coaching, performance management and growing out a team. The right candidate has domain expertise in Inside sales and Enterprise sales. We are looking for someone who is data driven and analytical, and strong leadership skills are essential. You will be reporting to and working closely with our SVP of Financial Advisor Revenue. As a key leader on the Sales leadership team, you will have the opportunity to shape a nascent yet thriving sales organization, as well as its processes and strategies.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Execute SmartAsset's go-to-market strategy for the flagship Smartadvisor product</li>
<li>Recruit top sales talent</li>
<li>Run monthly game plans</li>
<li>Oversee a high producing sales team</li>
<li>Coach, motivate, and develop a management team to build a high performing sales team</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a strong understanding of market trends and customer opportunities</li>
<li>Work closely with senior leadership across Sales, Product, Marketing, Operations to execute SmartAdvisor's strategy</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Skills and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of leadership experience managing, training, and scaling a high-performance sales team in a high-growth environment</li>
<li>3+ years managing a team of inside sales managers</li>
<li>Client facing, able to jump in on client meetings to scale the business</li>
<li>Demonstrated success forecasting, setting objectives &amp; goals and analyzing sales performance data to improve sales effectiveness</li>
<li>Experience coaching, motivating and training an inside sales team, transactional, enterprise</li>
<li>Financial technology or technology expertise is preferred</li>
<li>Experience selling into the wealth management space is preferred</li>
<li>Ability to communicate, motivate and train team members</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree required</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, team environment where you love being in a hands-on role</li>
<li>Ability to analyze data on Salesforce and structure how to optimize performance</li>
<li>Ability to manage and drive KPIs to maximize performance</li>
<li>Work closely with sales training on the creation and execution of new hire on-boarding, as well as continuing education</li>
</ul>
