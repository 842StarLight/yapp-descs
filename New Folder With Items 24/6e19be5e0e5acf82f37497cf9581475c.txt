<h2>Contact Center Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>TCM BANK, NA</b></p>
<p>
TCM Bank, N.A., a subsidiary of ICBA Bancard, serves as a trusted advisor to community banks, helping them build and strengthen customer relationships with fully branded consumer and business credit card programs. TCM's services include credit card program consulting, portfolio acquisition, product development and agent bank sponsorship choices.<br>
<br>
Like our parent organization, ICBA Bancard, TCM Bank is committed to Main Street America. We base our operations in the U.S., including our Customer Contact Center. We believe the cardholders we serve are first and foremost our agent banks' customers. Our priority is making sure our customers receive the same personal attention they get when they walk into a community bank branch.<br>
<br>
In addition, a portion of our profits go back into programs that benefit our community banks. This is what sets TCM Bank apart from the other agent programs in the marketplace.</p>
<p><b>
Contact Center Associate (Call Center)</b></p>
<p><b>
SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
Our Associates provide the best red carpet customer service while assisting customers with their accounts. This is a non-sales position <i>with a monthly bonus incentive</i>. Calls received by phone and/or written correspondence are answered timely and always in a professional manner. Our Contact Center Associates treat customers with the utmost respect and kindness. We hire associates that enjoy working with people and solving problems in a fun and rewarding position. <b>Currently this position provides up to a $300.00 sign on bonus. Our benefits package is second to none. We provide premium health, dental, vision and life insurance at zero cost to the employee. This is a spectacular opportunity - Come Join Our Team!</b></p>
<p><b>
GENERAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Answer phones and respond to customer requests in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>
Identify, research, and resolve customer issues using available resources and systems.</li>
<li>
Process orders, forms and applications.</li>
<li>
Identify and escalate priority issues.</li>
<li>
Transfer customer calls to appropriate resource.</li>
<li>
Follow-up on customer inquiries not immediately resolved.</li>
<li>
Recognize, document and alert the supervisor of trends in customer calls.</li>
<li>
Meet departmental goals and position expectations as established.</li>
<li>
Recommend process improvements.</li>
<li>
Responsible for annual completion of all assigned training, including Bank Secrecy Act related training.</li>
<li>
Responsible for all applicable compliance requirements, with a duty to immediately report to Management all suspicious activity.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p>
High School Diploma or GED.</p>
<p><b>
GENERAL KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS &amp; ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Knowledge of customer service principles and practices.</li>
<li>
Computer literate with the ability to learn customer service software applications.</li>
<li>
Must have good listening and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively in a multi-task, high-volume environment.</li>
<li>
Duties require professional verbal and written communication skills and the ability to type fluently.</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and results-oriented.</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational skills.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of credit card terms and conditions preferred.</li>
<li>
Six months or more experience in a Contact Center environment preferred, but not required.</li>
</ul>

Principals Only - No Agencies Please</i></b></p>
