<h2>Product Manager - Data Products</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive roadmap for products you own end-to-end, from idea to rollout. This may include regular client conversations, market research driven by platform and partner usage data, competitor research, and other sources to determine product needs, prioritize ideas, build and own the core product roadmap and work with engineering, marketing, sales and account teams success to ensure successful rollout across multiple global markets.</li>
<li>Own, maintain, and improve key business metrics and goals</li>
<li>Coordinate between multiple stakeholders and teams to prioritize highest impact items</li>
<li>Help us build and scale our product organization and architect how we build</li>
<li>This role will also hold ownership for a number of internal products</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of professional work experience ideally in Business, Engineering or Product roles</li>
<li>2+ years of work experience in Product Management at a company focusing on programmatic advertising, digital data, and/or SaaS technology</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent with a record of high performance</li>
<li>Strong written, verbal, and collaboration skills</li>
<li>Proficiency in product planning, wireframing and/or rapid prototyping tools</li>
<li>Experience working in an Agile development environment, and project management tools (e.g. JIRA)</li>
<li>Experience working with global teams across multiple timezones (we have hub offices in Singapore, London, Sydney, and San Francisco and operate in most markets worldwide)</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 40% including internationally</li>
</ul>
