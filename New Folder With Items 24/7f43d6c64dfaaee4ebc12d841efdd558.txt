<h2>Digital Media Sales Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description/Responsibilities: This position will be responsible for prospecting and communicating with major brands and digital marketing agencies.</p>
<ul><li>Work with Fortune 500 Companies and their agency partners to educate them on the merits of digital and influencer marketing.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Apply creativity, demonstrate accountability and maintain a deep understanding of ShopHer Media’s services and business model.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Apply a consultative sales approach that focuses on client objectives, but also maintain a sense of urgency on closing deals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Build a book of business from the ground-up, mentor team members, and prepare for additional leadership as we scale.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Leverage ShopHer Media’s large, existing relationship database - as well as research and identify new potential clients using prospecting tools in order to move opportunities through the sales funnel.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage data for new and prospective clients in CRM, ensuring all communications are logged in accurately and in a timely manner.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help develop internal marketing/sales strategies and collateral.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Respond to RFPs and generate proposals that meet client objectives/KPI’s.</li></ul>
<p>Qualifications/Requirements:</p>
<ul><li>B.A./B.S. or equivalent degree and a minimum 2-4+ years’ prior experience in media sales required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in developing relationships and selling digital media (CPA, CPM, CPC, CPL flat rate sponsorship) to major brands and agencies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Deep understanding of the advertising industry--including client-direct and key agency relationships</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven track record of reaching prospecting, sales and revenue goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Superior written and verbal communication skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to engage, build relationships, and converse with people with ease in order to pitch and close our products and services to major brands and agencies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent presentation/pitch, public speaking, problem solving and negotiating skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong knowledge of CRM's software, prospecting tools and Microsoft Office products – Word, Excel, &amp; PowerPoint.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Interested in growing with a successful firm that will embrace your personal and professional goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enthusiastic about a long-term, meaningful career in digital media sales and business development.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Can have fun while working hard!</li></ul>
