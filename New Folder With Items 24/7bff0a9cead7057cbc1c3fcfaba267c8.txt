<h2>Billing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>Responsible for managing the organization of various support required for billing for a high volume and fast paced environment. Serve as the liaison between 3rd party transportation, operations, customer service, billing, etc. to help facilitate the resolution of billing support and issues. Will also assist with processing invoices during peak times and other ad hoc reporting and special projects.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for maintaining and managing the Open Sales Order Report daily</li>
<li>Follow up with Vendors and in-house fleet operations to ensure all delivery paperwork have been submitted and deemed complete</li>
<li>Prepare invoices ensuring that pricing data, freight charges and taxes are calculated accurately</li>
<li>Trouble-shoot billing errors and various delivery related fees</li>
<li>Maintain invoice records and files</li>
<li>Potential to cross train in all billing positions to serve as a back-up when needed to cover PTO/Vacations/Illness</li>
<li>Preparations of reports to include gross profit reports, freight rates and pricing</li>
<li>Balance truck inventory and account for variances</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>Ability to reconcile customer accounts, Mathematical skills to manually compute and review billing details, ability to convert amounts into per gallon amounts quickly and accurately using calculators or accounting software. Organization skills, Data Entry Skills, Financial Software, Analyzing Information, Attention to Detail, Thoroughness, Verbal/Written Communication. This position requires phone calls, emails and face-to-face interaction with various parties and the initiative/drive to get the job done. We are looking for someone with strong interpersonal skills and ability to build rapport quickly as well as have a motivated and go-getter attitude.</p>
