<b>What We Are Looking For:</b><br>
As the Manager, Strategic Sales Development Team, you will need to lead a highly-motivated team of Strategic Sales Development Reps. If this is you, we'd love to talk! You'll work with both Sales and Marketing to help penetrate accounts and ultimately revenue.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage a team of up to 10 Strategic Business Development Representatives</li>
<li>Work with the Bandwidth's Sales Trainer to curate training topics for the team</li>
<li>Work collaboratively and cohesively with all Marketing team members for campaigns, strategic gifting, account based selling, and trade shows</li>
<li>Align with Senior Sales Leaders on monthly goals and targets</li>
<li>Drive enterprise sales opportunities with the goal of beating monthly targets</li>
<li>Increase conversion rates for accepted leads, opportunities generated and closed/won deals</li>
<li>Ability to provide insight and analysis of Account Based Selling techniques and strategies</li>
<li>Build and maintain strong relationships with others in Sales, Marketing, Product and Technology Teams</li>
</ul>
<b>What You Need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>3 – 5 years of sales experience with 1- 2 years spent managing a team</li>
<li>Deep understanding of sales tools such as Salesforce, SalesLoft, ZoomInfo and LinkedIn.</li>
<li>A strong understanding of sales process and methodology</li>
<li>Bachelors degree is required</li>
</ul>
