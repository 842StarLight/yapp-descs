<h2>After Hours On Call Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are currently looking to expand our after hours on call staff! We are a local/travel healthcare staffing firm. <b>This is a part-time position allowing the ideal candidate to work from home in the evening/night (2-3 per week) &amp; up to 2 weekends with rotating holidays. Required working hours are 4pm - 8am during the week &amp; weekend is Friday 4pm until Monday @ 8am</b></p>
<p>The Staffing Coordinator is to supervise all field staff during the shift. The Staffing Coordinator is the main point of contact for the clients to meet their demands for staffing related issues during non-business hours. They are also the main point of contact for all employees with staffing related issues. The position allows the right person to work 2-4 shifts a week during non-business hours (4pm-8am) and also includes weekends. The position is paid per shift and requires the ability to work the above-listed shifts. <b>Perfect for a “stay at home” position or well suited for someone who already works from home. This would NOT work for someone who currently works full time outside of the home. </b>Training is three days located in Overland Park Kansas during regular office hours.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Resolve any customer service issues quickly and efficiently to maintain the highest level of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Through consistent communication develop manageable relationships with employees and all clients at every level.</li>
<li>Maintain a positive image of the company through all communicative processes.</li>
<li>Uncover additional opportunities through contact with candidates on assignment and clients to grow our business and job opportunities for our candidates.</li>
<li>Exceptional written/ oral communication skills.</li>
<li>Intense focus to detail.</li>
<li>Ability to juggle many activities under constant and changing demands.</li>
<li>Absolutely must have an innate sense of urgency to meet client and employee needs and requests.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Basic knowledge of healthcare terms, specialties, and skills (preferred but not required)</li>
<li>1-year experience in client relations or sales exposure</li>
<li>Computer knowledge and Internet access.</li>
<li>Ability to work Holidays, Weekends, and fill in for full-time staff during normal business hours &amp; the office located in Overland Park, Kansas</li>
<li>Basic working knowledge of Microsoft applications, complex staffing software, and phone system handling.</li>
<li>Knowledge/ reference to the region you are assigned</li>
<li>Well organized &amp; a multi tasking genius</li>
<li>Take charge personality with well tuned problem solving skills &amp; the ability to think "outside of the box" immediately</li>
<li><i><b>Work from home in the evenings, overnights and weekends</b></i></li>
</ul>
