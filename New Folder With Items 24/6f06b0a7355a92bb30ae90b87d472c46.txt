<h2>Customized Training Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Customized Training Representative (CTR) is responsible for assessing, developing, implementing, managing and evaluating training programs for businesses, professional associations, non-profit organizations, and workforce centers. Incumbent will be responsible for making connections with existing accounts as well as developing new business leads to create long-term client relationships.</p>
<p>The CTR is responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Meeting the customized training revenue goals established in the annual budget for the Workforce Development Solutions (WDS) department through selling customized training contracts to businesses and organizations;</li>
<li>Managing client engagement and training contract fulfillment, including the operational processes necessary for contract fulfillment;</li>
<li>Managing grant implementation in collaboration with the Business Office; and</li>
<li>Providing research and development by delivering training programs to clients that may lead to new academic awards for degree-seeking students.</li>
</ul>
<p>Tasks/ Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Generate leads for contracted sales or referral to other customized training representatives;</li>
<li>Meet with clients to assess professional development and training needs;</li>
<li>Write proposals and negotiate contracts with clients to provide services;</li>
<li>Manage multiple training programs effectively and professionally, including: planning, organizing, tracking all training work, schedules, and deadlines. In addition to submitting invoices and maintaining the client database;</li>
<li>Market training and services;</li>
<li>Manage and oversee licensed and non-licensed program faculty and adjunct instructors.</li>
<li>Develop program budget and meet financial objectives to achieve annual revenue goals and gross margin;</li>
<li>Provide superior customer service and create long-term client relationships.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Associate degree and a minimum of 2-3 years of relevant work experience.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience with sales and marketing of products and/or services;</li>
<li>Customer service skills sufficient to actively listen to and understand customer needs and provide accurate information as appropriate;</li>
<li>Practical professional experience in a business/industry environment;</li>
<li>Proven record of problem-solving experience;</li>
<li>Experience with employee training and development;</li>
<li>Proficiency in interpersonal communications, curriculum development, and leadership skills;</li>
<li>Evidence of professional ethics;</li>
<li>Evidence of computer literacy; Microsoft office software applications;</li>
<li>Evidence of orientation to detail and organizational skills;</li>
</ul>
