<p><b>
General Job Description</b></p>
<div>
Reporting to the Manager of Advanced Services, this is a highly technical role, providing post-sales remote and on-site support of Junipers Network Products.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>This is a full-time position</li>
<li>The Resident Engineer will support varying projects, sometimes fully dedicated to a short-term project and at other times assigned to multiple part time engagements</li>
<li>Support may vary and may be provided remotely and/or on-site at a customer location</li>
<li>Travel may be required</li>
<li>Must hold a valid passport</li>
<li>May occasionally require long work hours</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
The Resident Engineer should have:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
