Business and Auxiliary Services is seeking a qualified Customer Care Specialist to provide direct customer service and support to Panther Central. Duties associated with this position include but are not limited to:<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Serve as first point of contact to public via front counter (assist with questions, form completion, card production, etc.) and telephone</li>
<li>Assist with administrative functions as required and/or requested.</li>
<li>Responsible for data entry, data queries, and updating data in all applicable University systems.</li>
<li>Explain policies and procedures of housing, food services, and ID card to students, parents, faculty, staff and other visitors.</li>
<li>Responsible for monitoring and reporting problems regarding lobby area to appropriate staff.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
