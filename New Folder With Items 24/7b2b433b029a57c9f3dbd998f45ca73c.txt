<p><b>COMPANY DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>
SAP is the global market leader for business software and related services, and SAP National Security Services Inc.® (SAP NS2®) is an independent U.S. subsidiary, offering SAP solutions with specialized levels of security and support to meet the requirements of U.S. national security and critical infrastructure customers.</p>
<p>
Must be a U.S. Person; NS2 does not offer Visa sponsorships for this role
</p>
<p>All internals must have manager’s approval to transfer
</p>
<p>The Project Manager will support the Cloud Line of Business at our Herndon, VA location as a member of the Cloud PMO Team. The project manager will support the planning, monitoring and controlling, and reporting of highly technical projects. The Project Manager will work with SAP &amp; SAP NS2 customers, technical staff, functional managers, and sr. leadership to gain understanding of project requirements, track and report project progress, as well as track and manage risk mitigation. The Project Manager is responsible to ensure that all activities are performed in adherence to the contract, SAP Methodology, quality standards, reporting and governance frameworks.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage large scale technical projects covering cross functional teams, including technical project planning, monitoring and controlling the project performance</li>
<li>
Leading the project management efforts for multiple simultaneous projects, including tracking project objectives and organizing multiple work streams
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Communicate project status, issues, and risks timely to all stakeholders, cross-functional teams and managers.</li>
<li>
Help manage Change Control, Issues and Risks to ensure timely implementation of projects, to include carrying out risk assessments, identifying project roadblocks, and driving mitigations / solutions</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the management of communication between and among customers, partners, and engineering staff in order to ensure that project objectives are successfully met on time, in scope, and within budget.
</li>
<li>Work with technical and non-technical teams, including Customer Support, Professional Services, Product Management, and QA teams for efficient and effective resolution of project issues and escalations</li>
<li>
Contribute to development and maintenance of PMO standards, processes, and best practices</li>
<li>
Support the contracts and sales organizations in the development of customer facing services contracts based on requirements analysis.<br>
</li>
</ul>
