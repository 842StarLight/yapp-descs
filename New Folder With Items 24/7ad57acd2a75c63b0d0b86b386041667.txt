<h2>Field Service Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>Performs client-site servicing, repair and/or installation of company product(s). This job may include any aspect of product field support, including system hardware and software, medical or laboratory devices and equipment, and systems networking. Provides technical support to customers on operational or maintenance aspects of system equipment. Serves as customer contact on technical and service related problems. Diagnoses mechanical, hardware, software and systems failures using established procedures. Determines most cost effective repair/resolution to minimize customer downtime. Prepares reports for analysis of product failure trends and service ability issues.
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Abides by all safety procedures and practices applicable to location and position; participates in appropriate safety training; demonstrates commitment to and support of EH&amp;S principles and values; champions safety as necessary
</li>
<li>Performs other duties assigned by management that fall within the generally expected scope of this position
</li>
<li>Provides routine service support to Sales Account Representatives in strategic accounts to execute agreed upon customer system assurance plans
</li>
<li>Assists Sales Account Representatives with start-ups of new applications in both existing customers and in new accounts
</li>
<li>Provides technical support to customers; identifying and resolving customer pain points, escalating as required
</li>
<li>Services new and existing values and provides specialized sealants to pipeline equipment.
</li>
<li>Provides actuator services ranging from maintenance to full repairs on existing and new equipment.</li>
</ul>
</div>
