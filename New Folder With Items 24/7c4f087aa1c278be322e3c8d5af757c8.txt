<p>
FedEx Solutions information<br>
The FedEx Solutions team supports and enables the FedEx Salesforce. FedEx Solutions consists of three organizations; Customer Solutions, Global Sales Operations (GSO), and Sales Enablement.<br>
<br>
– The Customer Solutions team within FedEx Solutions is focused on supply chain consultation, solutions design, new customer implementations, supply chain analysis and modeling, customer bid responses, content development and web development.<br>
<br>
– The FedEx Global Sales Operations (GSO) team supports the global sales force with core sales operations systems and processes and directly supports the FedEx Services Sales team with planning and execution. GSO defines customers and matches them to Sales professionals, creates compensation strategies and executes compensation processes, and builds tools and resources to make the FedEx Sales team more effective.<br>
<br>
– The FedEx Sales Enablement team includes Sales education, the Go-to-Sales team which increase Sales’ efficiency and effectiveness through demand management and sales enablement, and the Sales Support &amp; Content Management team which improves Sales’ effectiveness by optimizing the post-sale support experience, actively managing Sales content, and supporting improvements to content delivery systems.<br>
<br>
Position Details<br>
Full-time internship opportunity available in the Memphis, TN area from June 2020 to August 2020.<br>
<br>
Under close supervision, the successful candidate may assist with the development of projects, programs, and applications for the FedEx Sales teams as well as work across departments to assist with current initiatives to increase Sales efficiency. The successful candidate may assist with research, analysis, recommendations, and prioritization of Corporate Sales initiatives including education, communication, events, programs, and processes. Responsibilities may include project management, research and report development, analytics, documenting processes/procedures and recommending process improvements.<br>
