Description<div>
</div>
<div><div><b>
This position can be in either Providence, RI, Eden Prairie, MN, Denver, CO or Rocklin, CA.</b></div></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Position Purpose:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>Manages the Demand Planning function and process. Coordinates all customer and UNFI event, new item and promotional volume planning activities to ensure achievement of all department, distribution center, and company financial objectives. Ensures that the highest standards of professional integrity are maintained throughout the assigned department.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Provides overall department goal setting and direction.</li>
<li>
Coordinates departmental activities with other departments including; Sales, Procurement, Customer Care, Merchandising, Supplier Management, etc. to ensure optimum results.</li>
<li>
Monitors department’s results and performance compared to planned future projections, identifies solutions and recommends corrective actions.</li>
<li>
Analyzes and troubleshoots demand plan related issues in a timely and accurate manner.</li>
<li>
Continually monitors and identifies areas to improve the demand planning process and the accuracy of the event forecasts and projections.</li>
<li>
Attends various meetings to maintain awareness of programs and issues providing relevant department information in support of programs.</li>
<li>
Participates in Supply Chain and other projects by conducting analysis and providing relevant event planning data.</li>
<li>
Supports and implements Corporate initiatives.</li>
<li>
Initiates and follows through on personnel actions such as: hiring, terminations, layoff, promotions and salary increases.</li>
<li>
Coordinates, and evaluates formal training activities and on-the-job training programs.</li>
<li>
Maintains formal and informal channels of upward communication; encourages the expression of new ideas, suggestions, and complaints.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Job Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred. A degree in business or related field.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated knowledge and experience in forecasting and demand planning (food industry preferred).</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of procurement, logistics, inventory management, marketing, accounting and financial management practices.</li>
<li>
Understanding of promotional and new item planning from a corporate, customer and supplier perspectives.</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills. Ability to make appropriate decisions and present analysis findings.</li>
<li>
Strong Interpersonal and customer relationship skills to effectively develop and maintain business relationships at all levels of the company and with the outside business community.</li>
<li>
Strong written &amp; verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Strong planning, organizing, and negotiating skills.</li>
<li>
Proven supervisory and management skills.</li>
<li>
Proficient / Expert in the use of computers and related office software programs, especially Excel, Word, PowerPoint and Access.</li>
<li>
Position may require some travel.</li>
</ul>
