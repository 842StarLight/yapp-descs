<h2>MilCloud Account Executive – Defense Agencies</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p><b>Roles &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Build the MilCloud 2.0 Services business in the Department of Defense (DoD)</li>
<li>
Focused on cloud transformation and adoption of MilCloud 2.0 Services by engaging with key DoD customers, Mission Partners, and subcontract providers who are reinventing their IT strategy by adopting and delivering cloud computing solutions</li>
<li>
As an Account Executive for MilCloud 2.0, you will have the exciting opportunity to help shape and deliver on a strategy to build mind share and broad use of MilCloud 2.0’s cloud services.
</li>
<li>In this role, you will: • Develop a growing book of business across assigned accounts within DoD.
</li>
<li>Drive business and technical relationships and close business at a rapid rate by helping to define, identify, and pursue key opportunities.</li>
<li>
Establish deep business and technical relationships through your knowledge of the customer s mission and the environment.</li>
<li>
Have day-to-day interactions with these agencies. Have both a business background that enables them to drive an engagement and interact at senior military and civilian levels.</li>
<li>
Have a technical background that enables them to easily interact with staff officers, contracting officials, software developers, and architects.
</li>
<li>Have a demonstrated ability to think strategically about the mission, product, and technical challenges, with the ability to build and convey compelling value propositions.
</li>
<li>Be able to travel as necessary.
</li>
<li>Utilize salesforce and other internal GDIT systems to track key performance metrics.</li>
</ul>
