<h2>SEO Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The SEO analyst is responsible for executing Search Engine Optimization (SEO) strategies to improve Lowes.com visibility in all major search engines (Google, Yahoo, Bing, etc.) to drive increased traffic from search engines to Lowe's. The SEO Analyst has assigned responsibility for analysis and performance of organic traffic to Lowe's in owned areas of the business. Supports the implementation of SEO strategies, including data analysis, on-page optimization, content gap analysis, competitive research, technical recommendations and reporting.<br>
<br>
Works with stakeholders such as digital merchandising, onsite search &amp; navigation, UX, production, business operations and other teams to ensure successful growth of organic traffic to Lowe's. Also works with enterprise teams such as merchandising, marketing, data science and analytics to address opportunities to improve Lowe's performance. This individual demonstrates the ability to be a self-starter to complete assigned projects from start to finish. An obsession with consumer motivations, media and competitive trends along with informed recommendations to key stakeholders around how to drive traffic and deliver total company KPIs.<br>
<br>

About Lowe's:<br>
Lowe’s Companies, Inc. (NYSE: LOW) is a FORTUNE® 50 home improvement company serving more than 18 million customers a week in the United States, Canada and Mexico. With fiscal year 2018 sales of $71.3 billion, Lowe’s and its related businesses operate or service more than 2,200 home improvement and hardware stores and employ approximately 300,000 associates. Founded in 1946 and based in Mooresville, N.C., Lowe’s supports its hometown Charlotte region and all communities it serves through programs focused on safe, affordable housing and careers in the skilled trades.<br>
