<h2>Business Systems Analyst / Quality Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>The Business Systems Analyst / Quality Specialist evaluates existing Omni Channel business systems and provides solutions to optimize the user/ customer experience, ensures the quality of all Omni Channel applications, and achieves business goals. Documents and analyzes business systems defects, determines scope, objectives, and proposes strategy to implement improvements.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Documents and tracks user / customer feedback from multiple sources, including, incidents, defects, concerns and business needs.</li>
<li>Partners with stakeholders to lead the defect triage and update relevant status, and target cycle.</li>
<li>Monitors, analyzes and identifies patterns, trends, conflicting requirements and root cause analysis; documents and categorizes findings in coordination with the help desk.</li>
<li>Participates in design process with Omni Channel design / development teams to assist in the definition, testing and validation in staging environment of functional requirements for defects.</li>
<li>Ensures requirements related to defects are documented, actionable, measurable, testable and related to identified business needs or opportunities.</li>
<li>Defines business quality standards, related to entry criteria for Staging &amp; Production environments.</li>
<li>Responsible for building relationships with IT software development and QA teams.</li>
<li>Responsible for the business approval of software changes into the Staging and Production environments</li>
<li>Summarizes findings, including priority, scope, objectives, and proposed strategy to stakeholders.</li>
<li>Provides input into Omni Channel messaging, including but not limited to updates and workarounds, in collaboration with the Communications team.</li>
<li>Acts as backup to Point of Sale (POS) Configuration Analyst.</li>
<li>Stays current on the latest industry technologies, trends and strategies.</li>
<li>Assists employees, vendors and other customers by answering questions related to outstanding defects for Omni Channel applications.</li>
<li>Completes work in a timely and accurate manner, does not miss deadlines, while providing exceptional customer service.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Requirements</b></h2>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This position requires a minimum of five years of information technology, business analysis or related experience.</li>
<li>Previous experience with UAT and regression testing required.</li>
<li>Previous experience with Retail Systems required; One View Commerce experience preferred.</li>
<li>SAP/Hybris experience preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of multiple software development life cycle methodologies is needed.</li>
<li>Advanced level computer experience, including skills in Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Visio is necessary.</li>
<li>Detail oriented and organized.</li>
<li>Ability to identify complex problems, review information to develop and evaluate options, then implement solutions is essential.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate across all levels of the organization and to clearly articulate technical ideas to a non-technical audience both verbally and in writing is required.</li>
<li>The ability to work efficiently under pressure, accurately meet deadlines, present a professional demeanor and work well independently is essential.</li>
<li>Customer service skills including the ability to manage and respond to different customer situations while maintaining a positive and friendly attitude is essential.</li>
<li>In addition, troubleshooting, organizational and problem solving skills with a can-do attitude and the ability to adjust to changing requirements are essential.</li>
<li>Maintaining confidentiality, treating others with respect and upholding Company values are key attributes.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Educational Requirements:</b></p>
<p>This position requires a bachelor's degree in information technology, business or related field, or equivalent experience.</p>
