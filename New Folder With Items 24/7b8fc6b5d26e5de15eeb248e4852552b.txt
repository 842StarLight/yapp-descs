<h2>Senior Director, Merchants Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The Sr. Director, Merchants Solutions will be responsible for championing merchant client needs directly into the CyberSource product portfolio, strategy, business planning process and roadmap to meet regional and global needs to grow.</p>
<p>
Solution Managers are key to our segment and business line strategies, accountable for the alignment of sales, customer success, marketing and product teams to execute a plan to achieve P&amp;L targets. They drive the vision and segment strategy of the product line and its delivery and execution across the organization to provide integrated merchant acceptance solutions to merchants of all sizes around the globe.</p>
</div>
