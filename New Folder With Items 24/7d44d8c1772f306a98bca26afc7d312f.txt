<h2>Account Executive, Cloud Applications – Boston, MA</h2>

<p><b>About NCR
</b></p>
<p>NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a leading software- and services-led enterprise provider in the financial, retail, hospitality, telecom and technology industries. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga., with 34,000 employees and does business in 180 countries. NCR is a trademark of NCR Corporation in the United States and other countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>POSITION <b>: </b><b>Account Executive, Cloud Applications – Boston, MA
</b></p>
<p>LOCATION <b>: </b><b>Boston, MA
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>YOU ARE:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>A motivated self-starter with a positive attitude, a passion for helping small businesses succeed, who adapts to a fast-changing environment and who LOVES cutting edge technology.
</p>
<p>We’re NCR, one of the world’s first tech companies, and we’re adding teammates to our cloud-based, hospitality-focused sales team. You will be helping independent restaurant operators stay ahead, and adapt to a fast-changing world (along with the technology running it), increase their bottom line and decrease their operating expenses using our innovative tools.
</p>
<p>When it comes to communicating, you’re as clear as you are convincing. You’re also usually the most technologically up-to-date person in the room, and you care about keeping small business in the game.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><br>
<b>YOU CAN EXPECT TO </b>:
<br>
<br>
Usher in the future. Your love of technology, paired with your in-depth knowledge of our products and services, will make you a natural at generating new business. Your hard work will pay off, too—you’ll earn commission on top of a competitive base salary.
<br>
<br>
Keep a sharp lookout in all directions for new account opportunities. You’ll manage the entire sales cycle from initial prospecting and qualification to deal closing. Some days you’ll deliver presentations and proposals, while on others you’ll obtain contracts and maintain forecasting discipline.
<br>
<br>
Be a key member of several teams at once. This is where your personality shines. You’ll cultivate and maintain positive relationships with business owners, management, and local business groups simultaneously.
<br>
<br>
Gather interesting and insightful information to assist your team in marketing activities. Other responsibilities? If it supports the goals of the sales team, you’re game.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
Deliver great work from the office and field alike. You’ll maintain a working home office environment because you’re not one to miss a beat—or a deadline.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PEOPLE DESCRIBE YOU AS:
</b><br>
<br>
Driven and totally self-motivated. And you have a great attitude, too, even when the going gets tough.
<br>
<br>
Someone who seems to develop great relationships easily. You love figuring people out, and what they need, so that you can help.
<br>
<br>
A stickler for details. You’re not only motivated by results, but you have a high-energy demeanor, and are focused on accomplishing what you set out to achieve.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>YOU HAVE:
</b><br>
<br>
Two or more years of sales or operational experience with complex software and hardware solutions and professional services
</p>
<p></p>
<p>A Bachelor’s degree (preferred) with solid Microsoft Office skills—especially Excel and Word.
<br>
<br>
A knack for working with others. Speaking clearly and confidently (on the phone and in person) comes effortlessly to you.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Restaurant operations, sales, or management experience and/or training/certification
</p>
<p>3+ years sales and/or Marketing experience within Restaurant/Hospitality industry, with emphasis on prospecting, qualifying, and closing new business<br>
</p>

Visit our careers site for a list of the benefits offered in your region in addition to a competitive base salary and strong work/family programs.<br>
