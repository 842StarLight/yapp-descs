<h2>Product Management Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Product Management Leader has functional and personnel responsibilities for leading T-System's product management organization(s). This leadership position will be accountable for ensuring the success of the product management objectives through the development and oversight of effective product management processes, project oversight and building an environment that enables team members to be successful in exceeding T-System client and business objectives.
<br><br>
<b>PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Oversees all activities of a product management department, which includes overseeing and ensuring the processes and functions of the Product Managers are efficiently and effectively designed, monitored and well executed</li>
<li>Serves as leader in defining the corporate product management processes and ensuring compliance</li>
<li>Provides a disciplined project management structure to establish goal for deliverables, continuously monitor progress vs. product plans and reports on progress to stakeholders (e.g. GM, ITG, ELT)</li>
<li>Objectively measures the success of the product management function through key performance indicators and objective result measurements</li>
<li>Collaborates with leaders of engineering, marketing, sales and client support to translate the product needs into requirements and to drive consensus within the product departments, which ultimately promotes the achievement of the product goals</li>
<li>Expected to objectively evaluate strategies and proposals generated by Product Managers</li>
<li>Responsible for building a market understanding and cultivating in the departments' team members a product's vision supported by business cases and market analysis (competitive, technology) as well as compiling the product planning material necessary for driving product initiatives further with clarity and consensus</li>
<li>In collaboration with Product Managers, responsible for developing, defining and overseeing the product's roadmap and managing it as required for the purpose of meeting deliverables for the business - in this capacity, the leader also incorporates the existing development backlog and makes priorities as determined by the business, consumer and market needs</li>
<li>Responsible for overseeing the "voice of the client" processes and personally engaging with clients to gain insights on the client and market feedback that aids T-System in a deeper understanding of current and future product needs</li>
<li>Works intimately with Product Managers and the marketing department to drive marketing, lead generation and sales strategies</li>
<li>Partners with sales leadership to ensure alignment between product plans and the go-to-market sales approach, as well as sales support strategies</li>
<li>Represents the business in promoting the product by advocating for its value and benefits at executive briefings, marketing events, online consumer platforms and so forth</li>
<li>In the absence of a Product Manager, the Product Management Leader will be expected to personally fulfill those responsibilities which include, but are not limited to:</li>
<li>Executes processes to understand product(s) opportunities, client needs, competitive forces, technology trends and quantitative market research</li>
<li>Identifies product directions from market analysis and develops the supporting business cases for recommended market opportunities</li>
<li>Sets product strategy, creates the product roadmap, go-to-market plans</li>
<li>Provides project transparency to ITG processes and other management processes</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team environment</li>
<li>Complies with all internal policies and procedures</li>
<li>Ensure individual compliance with all privacy and security rules and regulations; commits to the protections of all Company confidential information, including but not limited to, Personal Health Information</li>
<li>Other duties as required or assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS AND REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5-7 years of demonstrated experience in multiple dimensions of product management and knowledge of relevant markets</li>
<li>Must demonstrate an understanding of the marketplace(s) in which the product is positioned</li>
<li>Must demonstrate an aptitude for product marketing and sales support</li>
<li>Strong aptitude for software development processes</li>
<li>Understanding of product management processes</li>
<li>Ability to work with senior executives, marketing professionals, sales professionals, software developers and other related IT professionals</li>
<li>Outstanding communication (written and verbal) and effective negotiating skills</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree (Master's degree preferred)</li>
</ul>
<b>TRAVEL:</b> Limited travel anticipated, approximately 2-5 days per month
<br><br>
<b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</b> Must be able to communicate effectively with clients and team members, both verbally and written. While performing the duties of this job, the employee must be able to travel independently and will frequently be required stand and sit for long periods of time.
