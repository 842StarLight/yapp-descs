<h2>Sales Account Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<i>Position at Allsteel Inc.</i>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>Allsteel,</b> an industry leader in office furniture design and manufacturing, and a top brand in the <b>HNI Corporation </b>enterprise, is in search of a <b>Sales Account Specialist</b> to join our team in Muscatine, IA!<br>
</p>
<p>As a Sales Account Specialist (SAS), you will be responsible for achieving challenging sales, profit, and mindshare targets in Allsteel dealer accounts. The SAS is a link between the Allsteel Field Sales team, the independent dealer/NSD commercial channels, and Allsteel headquarters. The SAS operates primarily via a virtual selling approach, working directly with Allsteel dealer partners in collaboration with the Market Development Managers. Activities are focused in the area of selling execution and professional development.</p>
<p><i>
Some of the essential aspects of the</i> <i>position include:</i><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with the Allsteel field sales team to achieve sales and profitability goals at assigned dealer partners. Primary delivery methods will be virtual, including phone, email, and webinars.</li>
<li>
Measure and track account selling activity and share results with leadership.</li>
<li>
Work as a business partner and consultant to increase Allsteel’s sales volume.</li>
<li>
Creates and implements account growth plans to achieve assigned revenue, profit, and mindshare objectives including networking, lead generation, deal flow, RFP’s, and win/loss data.</li>
<li>
Executes cold calling activities to drive new customer acquisition.</li>
<li>
Drive new product sales and program utilization to assigned accounts. Train accounts on product, price, programs, and workplace trends. Training targets will include dealer leadership, sales members, and design staff.</li>
<li>
Partner with Allsteel field sales team and dealer account team members on joint in-field selling efforts.</li>
<li>
Provide market intelligence, and timely market feedback to management on competition, competitive trends, and make recommendations on product and program enhancements to improve Allsteel’s competitive position.</li>
<li>
Provide dealers with guidance on bid activity and pricing strategies.</li>
<li>
Assists in the writing and presentation of proposals to dealers as needed.</li>
<li>
Work with Design Applications and Proposals Department to assist in the development of responses for dealer driven sales opportunities.</li>
<li>
Work with key departments at Headquarters to optimize the dealer experience with Allsteel.</li>
<li>
Complete robust onboarding activities and formal training events to develop a strong understanding of selling deal flow tactics, product and programs, financial impact of deals, and resources required to execute at a high level.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>What it takes:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Marketing or related field.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated excellence in communication, negotiation, and relationship skills. Sharp business acumen.</li>
<li>
Outstanding presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Prior experience in a sales or marketing role preferred.</li>
<li>
Strong writing and negotiation skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent decision-making skills.</li>
<li>
Strong virtual selling skills.</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite; CRM experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Headquartered Based</li>
<li>
Travel required</li>
<li>
Willing to relocate to targeted geographic locations (outside of Iowa) in a sales role after demonstrating full sales competency.</li>
</ul>
