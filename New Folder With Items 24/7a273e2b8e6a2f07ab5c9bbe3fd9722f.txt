<p><b>IT Support Technician</b></p>
<p><b>Unistar-Sparco Computers, Inc</b>. is a Millington based IT Solutions Provider. We are currently looking for an entry level IT Support technician. The IT Support technician will be responsible for managing and maintaining our internal infrastructure as well as our external client’s networks. Ensuring client (internal and external) satisfaction and quieting their networks are essential in this position.</p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and/or Abilities Required: </b> To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.</p>
<p>· Ability to work on multiple priorities and/or projects simultaneously.</p>
<p>· Excellent listening and communications skills, both verbal and written.</p>
<p>· Strong customer relationship skills.</p>
<p>· Organized, detail oriented and self-motivated.</p>
<p>· Ability to provide and maintain detailed documentation on each job.</p>
<p>· Ability to remain in contact with the client thru the completion of the incident.</p>
<p>· Problem-solving abilities and ability to meet reasonable deadlines.</p>
<p>· Ability to build positive and collaborative relationships.</p>
<p>· Willingness to develop professionally.</p>
<p>· Ability to work with little supervision</p>
<p>· Strong computer skills and the ability to effectively communicate through e-mail.</p>
<p>· Knowledge in basic networking configurations.</p>
<p>· Basic understanding/troubleshooting of DNS, DHCP, and TCP/IP required</p>
<p>· Experience in Windows OS environment.</p>
<p>· Experience working in an Active Directory Domain environment.</p>
<p>· Basic understanding/troubleshooting of VOIP Systems.</p>
<p><b>Expected Outcomes: </b></p>
<p>· Success in the IT support position will be based on your ability to meet or exceed our client’s expectations and by exceeding the SLA (Service Level Agreements) put in place for the various agreements.</p>
<p>· The team will be graded on the profitability of the client agreements and on the number of incidents that are able to be resolved before affecting the clients.</p>
<p>· Achievements of quickly resolving incidents that our clients’ open tickets for and by constantly monitoring the alerts generated by our tools to resolve incidents before our clients are aware.</p>
<p><b>Educational/Vocational/Previous Experience Recommendations: </b></p>
<p>· A+ certification or equivalent is required.</p>
<p>· Network+ Certification is preferred.</p>
<p>· Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP) is preferred.</p>
<p>· 1+ years’ experience in computer and network management is required</p>
<p>· Experience in managing end client networks is preferred.</p>
