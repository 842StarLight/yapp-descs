<h2>Principal Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>At Red Hat, we connect an innovative community of customers, partners, and contributors to deliver an open source stack of trusted, high-performing solutions. We offer cloud, Linux, middleware, storage, and virtualization technologies, together with award-winning global customer support, consulting, and implementation services. Red Hat is a rapidly growing company supporting more than 90% of Fortune 500 companies.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Red Hat Ansible Automation Product Marketing team is looking for a Principal Product Marketing Manager to join us in Durham, NC. In this role, you will create content to help the global Red Hat Field Sales and Partnering teams further accelerate their success in one of the most innovative technology offerings in the market. You’ll also establish a clear understanding of the target customers, competition, business drivers, and selling motions of your sales channels and work across teams to design innovative marketing programs to guide customer and channel demand. You’ll develop marketing collateral and sales tools that will be used by Red Hat's Sales team and partners. You will work cross-functionally and globally with numerous teams, including the Portfolio Marketing, Product Management, Campaigns Marketing, Technical Marketing, Partner Management, Field Marketing, Channel Marketing, Engineering, Sales, and Services teams. You’ll conduct research, craft market strategies, and develop content to support demand generation and enable field sales and Red Hat’s channel partners. You’ll be in charge of developing, communicating, and implementing activities aligned with the business plan. As a Principal Product Marketing Manager, you’ll need to be able to understand customer needs and develop compelling messaging, as you will spend time communicating with both customers and partners. Qualified remote candidates based in the U.S. will also be considered. Successful applicants must reside in a state where Red Hat is registered to do business.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary job responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Help us tell stories that resonate with the field and with our customers to help propel the business forward</li>
<li>
Create effective marketing assets and content, including presentations, case studies, solutions briefs, web content, data sheets, and sales kits that can be used globally</li>
<li>
Jointly create technical marketing collateral with technical marketing managers, engineers, and field marketing teams, including technical presentations, whitepapers, and case studies</li>
<li>
Collaborate with product management teams to ensure that marketing plans align with the directions and support offering roadmaps and releases</li>
<li>
Create concise and compelling core messaging for our offerings that will embody the key attributes of an offering, including value proposition, use cases, and differentiation</li>
<li>
Participate in technical aspects of the go-to-market engagements like roadshows, workshops, webinars, solutions demos, and industry and partner events</li>
<li>
Support engagements where required in high-touch accounts worldwide by collaborating with field sales and services teams</li>
<li>
Work with alliance and ecosystem partners to develop joint-value propositions</li>
<li>
Manage asset repository for the Red Hat Ansible Automation solutions-related content</li>
<li>
Develop and deliver enablement and training activities for the sales team</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other product teams to create multi-offering and multi-solution marketing materials that address customer use cases and challenges</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
