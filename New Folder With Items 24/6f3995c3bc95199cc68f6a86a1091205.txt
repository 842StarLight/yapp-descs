<b>Job Title:</b> Store Manager in Training
<br><br>
<b>Reports To:</b> Store Manager
<br><br>
<b>Job Location:</b> Store Location
<br><br>
<b>FLSA Status:</b> Salaried Exempt
<br><br>
<b>Exempt Classification:</b> Executive
<br><br>
JOB SUMMARY
<br><br>
The Store Manager in Training are being trained to become future Store Managers. The primary role of the Store Manager in Training is to develop knowledge and understanding of the day-to-day store operations, customer service, and merchandising under the supervision and direction of a Store Manager. This position requires strong leadership skills and the ability to motivate and supervise associates. Also included are establishing an interactive sales approach, keen awareness of safety &amp; loss prevention, maintenance of store cleanliness and appearance, excellent customer service, and a positive attitude.
<br><br>
JOB DUTIES AND ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Direct the operations of the assigned store</li>
<li>Direct all store personnel to build revenue and meet sales goals</li>
<li>Ensure attractive, safe, and accurately priced merchandise displays and floor plans</li>
<li>Recruit, train, &amp; develop personnel</li>
<li>Take a hands-on approach with directing daily goals for the Assistant Store Managers</li>
<li>Be a positive and professional “role model” for the team</li>
<li>Find solutions to associate and customer questions and concerns</li>
<li>Responsible for the maintenance and upkeep of the building and grounds</li>
<li>Develop the product knowledge and sales techniques of the team</li>
<li>Demonstrate effective and consistent communications and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Demonstrate effective problem-solving and analytical skills</li>
<li>Become an expert with standard concepts and practices within the retail environment</li>
<li>Maximize inventory, cash control, and loss prevention techniques</li>
<li>Establish and preserve a positive work atmosphere</li>
<li>Use general office equipment such as telephone, copy machine, fax machine, calculator, computer</li>
<li>Operate Telxon gun, forklift, pallet jack, ladder, and other retail equipment</li>
<li>·May be required to cross train and perform other duties</li>
</ul>

<br><br>

<li>Ability to relocate within the mid-west for a Store Manager position</li>

<li>Proficient User of computer software systems</li>
<li>·Ability to interact with the team in a fast-paced environment, remaining flexible, proactive, resourceful and efficient</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and coordinate, analyze, observe, make decisions, and meet deadlines in a detail-oriented manner</li>

</ul>
