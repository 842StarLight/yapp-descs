<h2>Vice President, Sales West</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Titus is the global leader in protecting and classifying sensitive data. Acquired by Blackstone in 2017, Titus is fast growing and profitable. The world's best companies use Titus including financial services, global military and government agencies and numerous Global 2000 companies.</p>
<p>
The Vice President, Sales West is responsible for driving the region to exceed company objectives and delight our enterprise customers. Titus operates a 100% channel sales model and aligns with strategic partners including Netscope, Forcepoint, Palo Alto Networks, Virtu, DataGuise and McAfee to build an eco-system of channel partners.</p>

What You Will Be Doing</b></p>
<ul>

Leading a team of sales reps and recruit open territories to drive revenue from new and existing customers</li>
<li>
Managing the region as a business to forecast accurately and consistently meet/exceed sales quotas</li>
<li>
Building a quality sales pipeline by leveraging an eco-system of strategic and channel partners</li>
<li>
Following a consistent management operating rhythm: forecast, pipeline and opportunity reviews</li>
<li>
Building a network of trusted partner relationships at the management and executive level</li>

Driving account strategies and coordinate team selling efforts with partners to close business on a quarterly and annual basis</li>
