<h2>Director Implementation Engineering</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Director of Implementation Engineering (DIE) is responsible for leading a team of distributed highly skilled technical Engineers in the coordination of provisioning TierPoint Services. The DIE will provide leadership, coordination and operational management of TierPoint’s Client Provisioning team. Further responsibilities include: improving the process under which we execute deployment of products, maintaining a high level of customer satisfaction throughout the provisioning process, ensure consistency of delivered solutions, ensure expediency of delivered solutions, review staff performance with intent to mentor where appropriate and recommend change where required, and to perform in a manner consistent and necessary to meet our cultural and financial goals.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Director of Implementation Engineering reports to the Vice President, Managed Services and Implementations. The ideal candidate will be a key contributor to TierPoint’s focus on culture and employee/customer satisfaction.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Provides quality internal and external customer service surrounding the Company values.</li>
<li>
Oversee the Client Provisioning Managers representing multiple IT Engineering skill sets on a day-to-day operational basis</li>
<li>
Formulate strategies to ensure the delivery of solutions are done expediently and consistently with attention to quality.</li>
<li>
Establish or expand upon key performance indicators for continually measuring and improving the performance of TierPoint’s process, resources, and staff</li>
<li>
Create employee performance goals and objectives, manage and measure performance, provide on-going coaching and feedback for your managers, and assist managers in creating career development plans with their IT Engineering professionals</li>
<li>
Act as an escalation point for both internal and external concerns including conflict resolution.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with internal and external leaders from whom support for success is necessary</li>
<li>
Drive efficiencies and make recommendations to improve overall cost effectiveness of staffing model</li>
<li>
Participate in the creation and implementation of policies and procedures to ensure consistency with company goals, industry best practices, and regulatory requirements</li>
<li>
Participate with sales, engineering and service teams to provide input and insight into customer solutions that are not standard product offerings</li>
<li>
Attend daily, week, monthly meetings, etc. as scheduled</li>
<li>
Performs salary administration, conducts interviews and makes recommendations for new hires, consultants and/or replacement person</li>
</ul>
