<h2>Customer Service Rental Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>As a Customer Service- Rental Agent, you will be the person representing our company. Our main goal is to make a good first impression, be professional and knowledgeable about the rental process. You will be responsible for completing all required paperwork with the renter, and making sure all the proper documenting procedures are followed (license, registration, insurance). You will be working closely with insurance companies to verify the renter’s insurance in order to determine if the renter has the appropriate coverage for the requested rental. Other duties of a rental agent include: </b></p>
<p><b>-Writing and explaining the rental agreement with the client</b></p>
<p><b>-Taking phone calls from customers, insurance companies, and body shops</b></p>
<p><b>-Checking car conditions, including assessing damage, safety, and cleanliness</b></p>
<p><b>-*</b>Provide outstanding customer service, and respond to customer needs and complaints in a timely fashion.*</p>
<p><b>-Handle multiple phone lines to answer customer questions</b><br><b>-inspect returns for damages, process a complaint for damages, if any</b><br><b>-Update inventory of vehicles that are in use or in maintenance</b><br><b>-Responsible for answering all inquiries, including price quotes and telephone calls</b><br><b>-Act as liaison between showroom and maintenance team for rental vehicles</b><br><b>-Have a good knowledge/learn the computer software systems used</b><br><b>-Interact with customers, while upholding company standards and positive reputation</b><br><b>-Explain terms of rental agreements.</b><br><b>-Show customers to their vehicles, pointing out damage on vehicle before renting</b><br><b>-Check mileage and inspect for damage when vehicles are returned</b><br><b>-Provide ongoing service and sales support.</b></p>
<p><b>Must be able to multi task and handle a busy environment. </b><b>Salary will be established at interview depending on experience. </b><b>Customer service and customer satisfaction is our first priority</b></p>
