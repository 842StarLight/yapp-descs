<p><b>Who We’re Looking For</b><br>
<b>Data Analyst – Salt Lake City</b><br>
<br>
We understand the power of (quality) data. It elevates the user experience, reveals patterns, and provides valuable insights that have a direct impact on our bottom line.</p>
<p>
And that’s why we’re looking to increase data’s role in our current strategy.</p>
<p>
If your idea of a good time includes combing through brand payment reconciliations, analyzing large data sets, and discovering gaps/issues/opportunities in sales and payment info, we want you on our team.<br>
<br>
As a Data Analyst at Clearlink, you’ll work with the Data Warehouse team to define units and revenue. You’ll take on ad hoc reporting assignments from other departments to help improve our processes. And you’ll develop a keen understanding of our operations and communicate your findings far and wide.</p>
<p><b>
Breaking Down Your Day-to-Day</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reconcile payments from brand partners</li>
<li>Run and analyze reports to find pay gaps, issues, and opportunities</li>
<li>Define units and revenue alongside the Data Warehouse team</li>
<li>Communicate findings to brand partners and across the organization</li>
<li>Analyze and work with large data sets in Excel</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>What You Need</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Operational experience</li>
<li>Strong Microsoft Excel skills</li>
<li>Analytical/financial skills, such as SQL, Macros, Data Warehouse</li>
<li>General understanding of the sales funnel</li>
<li>Strong critical thinking and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Capacity to self-manage and self-motivate</li>
<li>Exceptional attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize multiple projects</li>
<li>Desire to collaborate</li>
</ul>
