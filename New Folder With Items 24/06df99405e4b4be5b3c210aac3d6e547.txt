<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Account Manager (Technical) (Burlington, Vermont)</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who We Are</b></p>
<p>
We are a client-focused, values-driven company that provides comprehensive technical support for organizations of all sizes and types here in New England and beyond. We support a wide variety of technologies for our clients and prioritize our clients’ needs and best interests over our own sales numbers. As such, we are not a sales-driven organization, thus do not incentivize on sales with commissions. Rather, we let sound and reasonable decision-making drive our bottom line. This translates into long-term relationships with our clients that are rewarding on many levels, not the least of which is a high degree of personal interaction that enhances the enjoyment of our work and our success.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Ideal Candidate</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for an enthusiastic, empathetic, personable, self-driven individual undeterred by the challenges that come with working in a small but growing tech company. We need you to take ownership of our clients, be effective at communicating their needs to our engineering/support teams and have finesse to liaise questions and requirements back to the client on projects. This role is for a tech-savvy professional, able to explain technical details and requirements to a non-technical audience. Besides building strong relationships with each client, you will need to understand the desires and goals of potential future clients and use that to introduce Open Approach's products and services.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Account Manager’s primary responsibility is to manage our clients and their businesses with the goal of achieving high client satisfaction and retention. As a part of this management, they are responsible for providing a business and escalation point of contact for the client. Client satisfaction must still be juggled to ensure our company policy, procedures and standards are followed and maintained to keep us scalable.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be knowledgeable of the products and services stack that is available to the client.</li>
<li>Match clients’ needs to projects or other services that we can provide.</li>
<li>Effectively communicate with business owners in a meaningful, value-driven way.</li>
<li>Maintain a deep and current understanding of each client’s business situation, business needs and IT environment, translating those needs into service and product requirements.</li>
<li>Remain knowledgeable of market and industry trends, competitors, and leading client strategies.</li>
<li>Track all account activity in the Professional Services Automation (PSA-ConnectWise) system.</li>
<li>Identify and take action on renewals of products and software.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the appropriate departments to develop and maintain a technology plan that supports clients’ needs.</li>
<li>Identify trends and develop strategies to support these trends.</li>
<li>Create opportunities, when appropriate, and follow-up activities.</li>
<li>Utilize our proposal tools to initiate proposals to clients.</li>
<li>Proactively conduct both strategic and tactical sales for clients, or when received directly from the client.</li>
<li>Know how to identify technology risks with the client, and proactively engage with them to mitigate those risks.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively collaborate with the engineering and technical support teams, developing solutions to ensure client satisfaction.</li>
<li>Use project management methodology to thoroughly document, prioritize, track and drive client requirements and technical solutions for small projects.</li>
<li>Full accountability for the client and internal relationship.</li>
<li>Initiative to keep up on current and future technologies on a broad level.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal skills are absolutely imperative.</li>
</ul>
<p>Core competencies of a successful Account Manager include project management, technical understanding, excellent client service, and having the finesse to match client requirements with technical solutions. We don’t expect you to create the solution, just know how it fits with our clients, while allowing for scalability.</p>
<p>
A successful candidate for this position, above all, will closely reflect the values that we have built this company upon: honesty, integrity, and an ambition to continually improve every aspect of our work.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Experience:</p>
<p>
At least 5 years of account relationship experience is preferred, especially in a technical organization. Bachelors or equivalent experience.</p>
<p>
Compensation is negotiable depending on experience.</p>
