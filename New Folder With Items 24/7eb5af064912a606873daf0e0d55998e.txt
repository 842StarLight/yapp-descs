<h2>Security Compliance Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Essential Functions</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop, maintain and monitor internal controls best practices initiatives by proactively exploring control deficiencies associated with systems and processes throughout the Company.</li>
<li>Oversee and ensure an effective internal controls and regulatory compliance across Epsilon is being met following a risk-based approach in accordance with established company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Improve methods of capturing and presenting status of key compliance requirements in order to provide leadership with clear, concise data to enable appropriate decision making. Coordinate audit-related tasks to ensure the readiness of managers and their teams for audit testing and facilitating the timely resolution of any audit findings.</li>
<li>Support SSAE-18, PCI-DSS, ISO27001, HIPAA, NIST-CSF, and risk assessment activities across multiple office locations</li>
<li>Provide advisory role during new client onboarding or acquisition projects to assess security requirements and controls to ensure that security and compliance controls are implemented as planned.</li>
<li>Report and prepare presentations on the levels of IT compliance risk and control effectiveness to key stakeholders such as IT-business unit management, senior management and internal/external auditors.</li>
<li>Promote, support and implement solutions that reduce the total cost of internal controls and ongoing compliance activity.</li>
<li>Recognizes and identifies potential areas where existing policies, standards and procedures require change.</li>
<li>Makes recommendations regarding scope, timeline, budget changes and/or improvements.</li>
<li>Occasional travel is required to support compliance activity across multiple locations.</li>
<li>Maintains currency of knowledge with respect to relevant state-of-the-art technology, equipment, systems, regulations and/or standards</li>
<li>Performs miscellaneous job-related duties as assigned.</li>
<li>Basic project management skills.</li>
<li>Strong communication and customer interaction skills.</li>
<li>Leads general and application control reviews for simple to complex computer information systems.</li>
<li>Leads information control reviews to include system development standards, operating procedures, system security, programming controls, communication controls, backup and disaster recovery, and system maintenance.</li>
<li>Leads and/or performs reviews of internal control procedures and security for systems under development and/or enhancements to current systems.</li>
<li>Prepares audit finding memoranda and working papers to ensure that adequate documentation exists to support the completed audit and conclusions.</li>
<li>Prepares and presents written and oral reports and other technical information in a pertinent, concise, and accurate manner for distribution to management.</li>
<li>Consults with and advises associates and management on various operational issues related to information systems, and on general business operations as needed.</li>
<li>Follows up on audit findings to ensure that management has taken corrective action(s).</li>
<li>Coordinates and interacts with external auditors, associates, and management as appropriate;</li>
<li>Assists in answering customer questions and prospect questions in support of sales</li>
<li>Assists with improving information security awareness throughout the organization</li>
<li>Conducts risk assessments for both corporate and customer environments</li>
<li>Serves as a trusted security advisor in answering associates, client or customer questions</li>
<li>Performs and supports initial technical evaluation of risk acceptance requests, working with key stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Knowledge and Skills</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong working expertise with Information Security, Compliance &amp; IT Management Standards; ISO27001, PCI-DSS, SSAE-16 SOC1 &amp; SOC2, SOX, HIPAA, and NIST-CSF</li>
<li>Experience supporting security controls, compliance and audit activity within a service provider organization with multiple technologies and architectures; Windows, Unix/Linux, VMWare, Oracle, SQL, IPS/IDS, DLP, and other security technologies.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office software, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Visio and SharePoint.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Experience and Education</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Requires 5-7 years of previous related experience.</li>
<li>Associate’s or Bachelor’s degree preferred.</li>
<li>This role requires at least one active certification in any of CISSP, CRISC, CISA, or other relevant information security, compliance or audit certifications</li>
</ul>
