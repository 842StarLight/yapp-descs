<p><b>WHO WE ARE</b></p>
<p>
 We are a South-East market-leading mortgage lending operation. We're a family-friendly company known for our incredible customer service and our philosophy of going above and beyond to keep our customers happy. We understand that our people make the difference that makes us successful.</p>
<p>
Job Description:</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate for this role will ensure that all processes are completed so that loans are available for the customer on the day of the loan disbursement.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities and Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Figure &amp; create wire amount for every loan closing.
</li>
<li>Order each wire through warehouse systems.
</li>
<li>Coordinate with closers and title companies/closing attorneys regarding funding documents, wire amounts, funding requirements &amp; return funds.
</li>
<li>Coordinate with the secondary department regarding closed loans for loan sales.
</li>
<li>Manage closing spreadsheet to keep records for wires that have been ordered, funding docs received, files completed &amp; any notes regarding docs needed.
</li>
<li>Coordinate with accounting regarding funds sent, funds to receive, and invoices to include for payment.
</li>
<li>Ordering wires for loan closings</li>
<li>Corresponding with title companies for approval to disburse the wire</li>
<li>Receiving and examining the closing documents for any issues</li>
<li>Corresponding with both in-house employees and outside title companies to obtain any missing documentation</li>
<li>Documenting all needed information into company software interfaces</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma</li>
<li>Knowledge of real estate and mortgage banking is helpful</li>
<li>Organizational skills for efficiently completing tasks in a time sensitive environment</li>
<li>Good, professional phone / email communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to handle stressful, periodic deadlines and workloads</li>
<li>Excellent problem solving skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Software Knowledge:</p>
<p>
A working knowledge of Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Word, Adobe Acobat, and Gmail is preferred.</p>

Quantitative Requirements:</p>
