<h2>Solution Analyst Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Essential Job Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify Revenue. Partner with the Sales, Customer Relationship Manager, and the Customer Configuration Manager to identify and qualify leads on new features, enhancements, and/or full end-to-end solutions.<ul>
<li>
Transform the customer’s objectives or challenging problems into creative solutions and opportunities through use of CSG’s product suite or custom development solutions with PSG.</li>
<li>
Assist in the creation of new features &amp; solutions that meet the customer requirements, fit within the OSS/BSS infrastructure, and maximize CSG’s product offerings.</li>
<li>
Participate in the development of sales proposals and other key documentation that are leveraged during the sales process.</li>
<li>
Participate in the solution selection and decision-making process and provide the necessary information to enable the customer to make an informed decision.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Consult and Advise: Bridge the gap between CSG and the customer to identify and qualify business solutions and opportunities in alignment with customers’ business plans. Gather resource requirements and cost/benefits analysis for conceptual solution models.<ul>
<li>
Collaborate with sales, relationship management, and customer care teams to identify and prioritize business requirements for new features and/or complex changes. Ensure business requirements are clearly defined, variable, and scalable globally across the company.</li>
<li>
Consult internally and externally on technical opportunities inherent in the existing product roadmap</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Leverage a logically structured, data-driven approach with critical thinking and disciplined problem solving tools to arrive at solutions that are mutually satisfactory for CSG and the customer.<ul>
<li>
Gather requirements using interviews, document analysis, workshops, surveys, site visits, business process descriptions, use cases, scenarios, business analysis, task, and workflow analysis</li>
<li>
Collaborate with developers, programmers, and designers in conceptualizing and developing product enhancements and/or new features.</li>
<li>
Participate in design sessions in prototyping new solutions for the purpose of enhancing product portfolio and increase user adoption of CSG’s product portfolio.</li>
<li>
Ensure technical viability of solutions as defined in the Statement of Work and subsequent change requests.</li>
<li>
Provide input on the creation of comprehensive solution architecture and requirements documents.</li>
<li>
May serve as a subject-matter-expert and participate in the resolution of system and product issues.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Provide technical guidance to cross-functional teams through the design, build, test, and delivery process.</li>
<li>
Generate thought leadership around our product assets and overall solutions.<ul>
<li>
Engage in knowledge transfer with colleagues on other teams.</li>
<li>
Continue to educate self on business to continue to create efficiencies and/or mitigate risk via technology changes for the business.</li>
<li>
Keep up to date on industry and product technology trends to ensure the ability to work on variety of different solutions for the business</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Professional Working Behavior</b></p>
<p>
This role is accountable for professional working behavior to include; building and maintaining constructive working client relationships, implementing proactive and concise communication, acting as a resource to colleagues, and engaging in collaborative thinking and problem solving while demonstrating CSG’s Competencies and Core Values.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
This role will likely require maintaining on on-site presence at CSG Customer’s location, and at a minimum will require a candidate comfortable with direct and regular client visits and interactions in a professional office setting.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Travel</b></p>
<p>
Must be able to travel internationally and/or domestically (dependent upon location of the role) up to and exceeding 25% of the time</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Minimum Requirements:</b></p>
<p><b>
Education</b></p>
<p>
Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Computer Science, and/or a related field</p>
<p><b>
Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Typically 5-7 years of relevant work experience in the area of product management, business analysis, solution design, and customer consulting in a comparable environment<ul>
<li>
Experience in creating and/or participating in the planning and estimating of the full Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) activities. Experience with Lean and Agile methods desired.</li>
<li>
Experience working with Agile planning and management tools, e.g. Atlassian Suite - JIRA &amp; Confluence desired</li>
<li>
Understanding of Service Oriented Architectures (SOA), complex integration strategies, and working with APIs desired</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of SQL and experience with RDBMS desired (e.g. Oracle, SQL Server, or Postgres)</li>
<li>
Knowledge of CSG applications preferred.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Must be able to take a holistic view of the overall business and produce solutions, in relation to the commercial impact of the business and translate into detailed functional and/or technical specifications</li>
<li>
Strong issue management skills; analytical, technical, and problem solving skills to identify and facilitate solution development in an effective and efficient manner</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to build relationships at all levels in the organization; both internal and external</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication skills with the ability to present solutions and the associated business impact in a clear and concise manner</li>
<li>
Process-oriented and logical thinker with the ability and flexibility and adaptability to respond to changes</li>
<li>
Ability to manage projects from small to medium scale</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to develop alternative solutions to technical &amp; business related problems and recommend the best solutions to the business and project team</li>
<li>
Ability to leverage effective facilitation and negotiation techniques to gather requirements from a diverse range of stakeholders and group size</li>
</ul>
