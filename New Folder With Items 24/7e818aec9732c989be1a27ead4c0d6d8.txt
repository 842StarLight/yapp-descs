<h2>Electronics Engineer - Software</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We at <b>Leggett &amp; Platt Automotive</b> are looking for a <b>Electrical Engineer - Software</b> to join our rapidly growing Automotive Team in the Detroit, MI area. We are a multi-billion-dollar globally diversified company that designs and manufactures various engineered components and products. Our growing automotive business unit, which exceeds one billion dollars in sales, is comprised of four main product segments with operations on three continents. Our customers include OEM and Tier 1 automotive companies.</p>
<p>
In this role, you will report to the Group Manager Electronics Engineering and be responsible for the achievement of excellence in electrical software engineering. This role will design and develop exciting new products with a dynamic team, finding solutions to interesting challenges.</p>
<p>
If you are looking for a challenging, rewarding opportunity to showcase your expertise, we want you!</p>
<p><b>
What you will be doing?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Plan, develop and validate embedded Software code</li>
<li>
Support project in compliance to Automotive SPICE up to level 3</li>
<li>
Develop suitable validation and test methods for embedded electronics modules</li>
<li>
Develop breadboard or first run proto type circuits for product feasibility evaluation</li>
<li>
Develop software for embedded systems</li>
<li>
Design, develop, built testers as needed for design evaluation of electronics</li>
<li>
Apply suitable engineering tools for the design analysis of electrical and electronic circuits</li>
<li>
Coordinate with applications engineering to handoff new products for production</li>
<li>
Contribute in the innovation of new concepts, designs, and novel new products, which may include the use of new and improved processes and operational techniques</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain adequate records of technical information, process methods / std’s., design and program details/info., and records to support/procure reliable patent actions</li>
<li>
Participate with Marketing and Sales in the identification of new product opportunities and provides guidance as to their technical and economic feasibility</li>
<li>
Assist Purchasing in supplier selection and development</li>
<li>
Actively participate in the requirements to retain ISO9001, IATF16949 certification</li>
<li>
Responsible for knowing and upholding the H&amp;S Policy and for working in a safe manner and reporting all unsafe or unhealthy conditions or acts</li>
<li>
Participates in continuous improvement activities using the Kaizen philosophy</li>
<li>
Maintains professional status through personal education, training and development</li>
<li>
Must work as a team player and maintain good employee relations</li>
<li>
Maintains confidentiality of all information received from the customer/company</li>
<li>
Adheres to all company policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Completes any other assignments, duties and projects that may be assigned</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
We’re looking for someone with:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
University degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering or equivalent</li>
<li>
2 to 5 years of experience in Automotive Embedded Software
</li>
<li>Experience with SW validation and respective tools</li>
<li>
Static analysis (ex: QAC, mathworks, etc.)</li>
<li>
Unit Testing (ex: Tessy, VectorCast, etc.)</li>
<li>
System Testing (ex: Vector Cast, etc.)</li>
<li>
Familiar with CMMI or Automotive SPICE processes.</li>
<li>
Experience with Electronic CAD (Ex: Cadence, Altium and/or Mentor suite experience an asset)</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Suite of Products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Project, Outlook)</li>
<li>
Strong electrical design background or electrical /electronic.</li>
<li>
Proven track record for innovation, creativity, and practicality.</li>
<li>
Tenacious problem solving and troubleshooting a must.</li>
<li>
Confident, self-starter with excellent communication skills, both written &amp; oral</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain strong pace and extended effort in resolution of programs, projects, and schedules.
</li>
<li>Well organized including the ability to schedule and maintain multiple activities.</li>
<li>
Able to function in demanding environment.</li>
</ul>
