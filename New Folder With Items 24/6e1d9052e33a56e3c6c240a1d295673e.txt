<p><b>What will you contribute?
</b></p>
<p>As a key member of the CIO leadership team, you will oversee Service Delivery leaders for all Finastra business lines and partner organizations. In this position you will be accountable for understanding and representing the LOBs needs, partnering with lines of business to develop IT service delivery roadmaps, ensuring IT delivers fit-for-purpose services, fostering and building productive relationships, facilitating communications with partner organizations and / developing / driving improvement plans. This role reports directly into the Chief Information Officer (CIO).
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities &amp; Deliverables:
</b></p>
<p>Your deliverables as a VP, IT Client Delivery Management will include, but not limited to the following:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with key leaders across Finastra’s Lines of Business (LOBs) and partner organizations to understand their Technology needs and collaboratively develop Technology strategy
</li>
<li>Serve as senior advisor and single point of accountability for IT Services provided to LOBs and partner organizations.
</li>
<li>Recommend, deliver and support technologies and services that meet current needs, and evolve strategies to meet future needs
</li>
<li>Foster and build productive relationships with partner organizations and LOBs, facilitating bidirectional communication and driving improved collaboration
</li>
<li>Track and communicate performance of all IT services delivered, from projects to daily operations
</li>
<li>Drive process improvement to support delivery excellence
</li>
<li>Assist IT with prioritizing incoming workload and effective utilization of resources assigned to LOB projects
</li>
<li>Applies in-depth business acumen and commercial expertise to differentiate Finastra or the business segment from the competition
</li>
<li>Integrates functional and business knowledge to anticipate changing business direction.
</li>
<li>Leads diverse activities through multiple levels of managers; has accountability for major deployment of business segment or enterprise-wide operations
</li>
<li>Contributes to enterprise function or business segment long-term direction and strategy
</li>
<li>May serve as a member of a Functional Leadership or business segment management team, influencing strategy through scope and scale of areas managed
</li>
<li>Establishes global or business segment-wide standards, policies and practices
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 10 years large-scale program management experience
</li>
<li>Minimum 10 years experience managing large technical teams in mission-critical environments
</li>
<li>Proven track record in driving transformation programs in the financial services / software sector
</li>
<li>Demonstrated success in implementation of large, complex programs
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal written and verbal communication skills required. C andidate must be able to effectively communicate at all levels of the organization.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in providing thought leadership in strategy development
</li>
<li>Experience with business process improvement and business process reengineering • Strong strategic thinker, yet able to deliver tactical deliverables
</li>
</ul>
