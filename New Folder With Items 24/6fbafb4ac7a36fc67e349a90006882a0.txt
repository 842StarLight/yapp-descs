<h2>ABM Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Terminus is leading the B2B marketing revolution. We do this through our industry-leading Account-Based Marketing (ABM) platform, designed to empower marketers to drive quality growth for their companies by targeting the right accounts, engaging them across multiple channels, activating their sales and success teams at the right moment, and accurately measuring their total influence on pipeline, deal velocity, and real revenue.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Terminus is looking for an ABM Consultant to join Professional Services. The ABM Consultant focuses on marketing, business, and Terminus platform strategy. As an ABM Consultant, you play a critical role by becoming a trusted resource, partner, and extension of our customers’ marketing teams. The ABM Consultant engages with a portfolio of customers across multiple verticals. This role is responsible for leading both onsite and remote engagements that define the customer’s business/marketing goals, identify areas of opportunity, and provide both one time and ongoing consultations. Terminus customers rely on their ABM Consultants to provide the day to day strategy, recommendations, and reporting analysis necessary to be best in class ABM marketers while maximizing return on investment (ROI).</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The ABM Consultant will advocate on behalf of the customer and enable a truly strategic partnership with Terminus. By managing to measurement, the ABM Consultant will develop roadmaps for the assigned customers and quantify impact produced along the way. Typically, ABM Consultants run and manage single-threaded engagements where they are the sole practitioner. Additionally, the ABM Consultant can serve as the single point of contact into the Professional Services organization, incorporating and leveraging subject matter experts, Terminus partners and other applicable resources where necessary.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This is a rare opportunity to join a fast-growing company with an open and collaborative work environment, where you can be directly responsible for the success and direction of our enterprise customers.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Be the definitive expert in operationalizing ABM for our customers and drive their success with Terminus as a trusted advisor, partner, and extension of their marketing team.</li>
<li>
Manage a diverse portfolio of customers within a variety of industries at different levels of sophistication.</li>
<li>
Proactively manage client expectations and deliver exemplary customer service. Meet or exceed the customer’s expectations of knowledge and professionalism. Escalate any issues that impact successful execution of the work before the customer is negatively impacted.</li>
<li>
Be an expert on campaign management, B2B advertising, and best practices of account-based marketing strategy.</li>
<li>
Become an expert on the Terminus platform and have a thorough working knowledge of partner technologies that customers leverage in their ABM/MarTech stack.</li>
<li>
Understand and translate customer business goals into successful campaign plans in order to achieve them using Terminus technology</li>
<li>
When required, provide detailed reporting and analysis to customers with prescriptive recommendations on next best action.</li>
<li>
Have a thorough understanding of setting up and running mobile, social, video, and display advertising campaigns.</li>
<li>
Advocate and assist in the prioritization of new customer features and product enhancements, influencing the Terminus product roadmap and collaborating with the Product team to identify short-term solutions.</li>
<li>
Track and maintain accurate client records and hit key engagement milestones using Terminus project management and professional services automation software.</li>
<li>
Manage both internal and client-side teams against immediate short-term projects and/or campaigns, with an understanding of long-term planning requirements and ensured scalability.</li>
<li>
Design, produce and present reviews and recommendations on a monthly, quarterly, annual and ad hoc basis.</li>
<li>
Work with our sales department to promote the value of consulting to new and existing customers. Assist with proposals, RFP responses, and pitches. Have the ability to sell the value of professional services and consulting.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
