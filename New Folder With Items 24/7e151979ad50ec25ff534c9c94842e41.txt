<h2>Accounts Payable Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>CORE Business Technologies is a software, networking and imaging solutions integrator specializing in systems and web integration and digital workgroup printing systems. We are located in East Providence, RI. Our commitment to innovation, growth and challenge has helped satisfied clients compete successfully since 1983. </b></p>
<p><b>We are looking for a reliable and detailed professional to help us properly maintain all operations of our AP and Cash Management department.</b></p>
<p><b>Accounts Payable Coordinator and Cash Management Job Description: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review all invoices, expense reports and check requests for appropriate documentation and approval prior to payment</li>
<li>Responsibilities will include matching invoices and entering all invoices into the financial system</li>
<li>Issue stop payment request</li>
<li>Schedule and process daily/weekly check runs</li>
<li>Match invoices to checks, obtain all signatures for checks and distribute checks accordingly</li>
<li>Prepare and report all appropriate 1099 vendors at year-end.</li>
<li>Respond to all vendor inquiries</li>
<li>Reconcile vendor statements, research and correct discrepancies</li>
<li>Assist in month end closing</li>
<li>Process journal entries</li>
<li>Maintain files and documentation thoroughly and accurately</li>
<li>Disburse petty cash and maintain documentation</li>
<li>Calculate and submit sales taxes on paid invoices and customer billings.</li>
<li>Monitor and reconcile the flow of cash coming into and going out of the organization; provide Senior Management with daily cash flow reports</li>
<li>Work with bank on any updates or changes needed</li>
<li>Process outgoing mail and cover switchboard on a rotating schedule or as needed</li>
<li>Assist with other projects as needed</li>
</ul>
