<h2>Demand Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>Sugar Foods is a privately held food manufacturer, packager, importer and sales and marketing organization. Since 1948, we’ve built lasting relationships throughout the food industry and are consistently recognized as a Top Supplier by our foodservice distributor and national chain account customers. In Retail, our products are found in supermarkets throughout the country in both brand and private brand formats, including our brand Fresh Gourmet (#1 brand of salad toppings in America).</p>
<p><b>Summary: </b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for forecasting / demand planning for the Retail organization as it relates to the commercialization of new products and modification of existing products. Develops and maintains a twelve-month rolling forecast of the Companies’ new and existing Retail product lines. Timeliness and accuracy are critical to the success of this position.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates with the sales team to maintain a rolling 12-month demand forecast for the Retail Division.</li>
<li>Reviews historical sales trends, research demand drivers, prepare forecast data, evaluate statistical forecast models, and promote an agreed to forecast result.</li>
<li>Coordinates cross-functional research activities to reconcile significant variances and refine the forecast to reflect updated sales and marketing assumptions.</li>
<li>Utilizes a collaborative approach with Sales, Marketing, Operations and Supply Chain to obtain current and accurate information for demand forecasts.</li>
<li>Provides input to the Supply Chain organization for developing inventory strategies on existing items, new products, and product phase-outs.</li>
<li>Closely coordinates and communicates customer action plans with supply planning.</li>
<li>Using best methods (history, statistical models, customer information, and software tools) in creating forecasts targets.</li>
<li>Conducts monthly forecast meetings with each sales person to review and update their customer forecasts.</li>
<li>Coordinates of all retail promotional activity to include forecasts, component requirements and run-out dates. Provide direction and disposition of leftover finished goods and raw materials / packaging.</li>
<li>Monitor potential obsolesces risk by ongoing review of code date report and work with Sales, Distribution and Customer Care to provide recommended disposition for all potential rotation and/or short code issues.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications - Education and/or</b> <b>Experience </b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree in logistics, statistics, business, supply chain or other related fields.</li>
<li>3 – 5 years relevant job experience</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with other team members.</li>
<li>Ability to utilize advanced forecasting and analysis techniques to anticipate consumer demand and market trends.</li>
<li>Able to provide technical expertise and mentoring about the demand planning function.</li>
<li>Excellent analytical, statistical, communication, presentation and system skills with thorough attention to detail.</li>
<li>Experience with any forecasting application software.</li>
<li>Demand Planning certification through SCC, APICS, or similar Supply Chain organization.</li>
</ul>
