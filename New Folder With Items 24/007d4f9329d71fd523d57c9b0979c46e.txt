<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Role and Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<p>
As the Partner Programs Performance &amp; Analytics Manager, you would be responsible for developing a Partner Connect dashboard, in collaboration with Partner Sales and Program leadership and our GEO partners.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
In this role, you will be building the worldwide partner program performance modeling, across the key program elements of Partner Connect: IT priority, market and solution maturity, licensing model. Additionally, you will work hand in hand with our GEO teams to establish models for local partner program performance goals. Your goal will be to ensure the right outcomes are achieved for both VMware and our Partners, based on the Partner Connect relationship.</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Build and manage the partner programs performance model and dashboard, across various criteria, e.g., program tier, route to market, etc.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Programs, Finance, Operations, and GEO’s to build the partner program performance model</li>
<li>
Translate the needs of the Partner Program Office, and GEO Partner Sales leads into meaningful and effective data models</li>
<li>
Partner with Operations and IT to build and automate dashboards and reporting to ensure success</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Program Strategy and Design lead with performance models as Partner Connect evolves</li>
<li>
Track and measure Master Services Competency program adoption, as part of overall Partner Connect success</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Required Skills:</b></h2>
<p>
This is a highly strategic position and the ideal candidate will have experience identifying and measuring all elements of a Partner Program, as well as providing insight and guidance based on the performance analytics outcomes.</p>
<p>
This role requires a balance between strategy, analysis and rigorous execution against our partner program and revenue goals. Experience with financial modeling, partner sales, partner program frameworks, and ability to model performance-based systems are required.</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
5-8 years of experience in the IT software industry required</li>
<li>
A minimum of 3 years’ experience in sales, sales enablement, and/or partner programs analysis</li>
<li>
Ability to translate strategic objectives into a clearly defined and executable analytical model</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of data analytics software tools</li>
<li>
Conduct deep business analytics and forecasting for partner program performance, during a development cycle and in post-implementation</li>
<li>
Optimize a points-based calculation by key criteria, e.g., IT priority, market maturity</li>
<li>
Establish cadence for and conduct on-going sensitivity tests, determining partner distribution by tier</li>
<li>
Provide Program and GEO leadership with guidance on recommended changes to Partner Connect Program based on performance results</li>
<li>
Proven track record of consistently meeting or exceeding assigned goals and targets</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Preferred Skills:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Understanding of a partner go-to-market model</li>
<li>
Strong problem-solving and troubleshooting skills to provide creative solutions for partner performance issues and to drive joint VMware and partner business opportunities</li>
<li>
Ability to collaborate across groups for successful, shared outcomes</li>
<li>
High level of interpersonal awareness, communication and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
