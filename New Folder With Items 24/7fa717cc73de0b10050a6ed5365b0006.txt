<h2>Marketing Support Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>
We’re seeking someone who is comfortable meeting new people, who has excellent communication skills, and who is a serious multi-tasker. If you are self-motivated and have superb interpersonal skills, then you’ll thrive in this work environment. Our idea of the ultimate candidate is one who is proactive, is experienced, truly enjoys providing superior service, and loves taking ownership. Joining our team makes a difference every day in people's lives.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Roles/Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide sales route administration and database management</li>
<li>Assist with ERP (Emergency Ready Program) file completion and database management</li>
<li>Provide marketing administration including referral source follow-up</li>
<li>Maintain Franchise web and social media sites</li>
<li>COI (center of influence) and facility research</li>
<li>Maintain key account target list and provide research</li>
<li>Provide brand and marketing coordination, including advertisement placement and tracking</li>
<li>Provide newsletters and e-blast coordination</li>
<li>Coordinate public relations programs</li>
<li>Events coordination</li>
<li>Maintain sales and marketing materials</li>
<li>Perform other office duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Necessary Experience/Skill Set</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Two years’ experience with sales and marketing support desirable</li>
<li>Superb customer service, administrative, and verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Experience in the commercial cleaning and restoration or insurance industry is desired</li>
<li>Working knowledge of current business software technologies is required</li>
<li>Ability to remain calm and professional during tense or stressful situations</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills and strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Very self-motivated and goal-oriented</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Associate’s degree in marketing or business or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Ability to successfully complete a background check subject to applicable law</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Hours</b></p>
<ul>
<li>20 hours/week, flexible to work additional time when required</li>
<li>Vary between 7 a.m. and 5 p.m.</li>
</ul>
