<h2>Director of Channels - International</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Director of Channels - International manages all business aspects of a defined portfolio of partner and channel relationships and provides leadership to a team of channel managers. With a primary focus on sales revenue and lead generation, the role also balances sales support needs, partner relationship management, and sales training. In addition, the role is responsible for identifying and recruiting new partners, as well as ensuring a positive onboarding experience for the partner.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Meeting and exceeding set sales quotas and partner recruitment goals while adhering to the Company's sales rules of engagement</li>
<li>Aggressively driving partners to maximize sales through best practices and efficient use of company's partner enablement resources and ensuring appropriate training and support</li>
<li>Communicating with partners about new products and service offerings</li>
<li>Providing leadership to a team of channel managers</li>
<li>Working with marketing to drive programs and events through the channel</li>
<li>Working collaboratively to define and execute partners' sales plans</li>
<li>Demonstrating industry knowledge, keeping abreast of competition, competitive issues and products</li>
<li>Attending and participating in sales meetings, product seminars, and trade shows</li>
<li>Collaborating with peers in different business units and worldwide theaters</li>
<li>Training and supporting partners</li>
<li>Travel up to 75% of the time</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS / REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong entrepreneurial spirit and a willingness to do what it takes to get the job done</li>
<li>Exceptional interpersonal skills, customer-focused, strong communication and organizational skills, goal oriented, and self-motivated team player</li>
<li>Ability to quickly learn, use, and understand technology applications</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION:</b> Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience.
</p>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE:</b> 15 years related experience in a high-tech company managing a portfolio of channel partners; experience working in international environments and managing business development initiatives in culturally diverse regions; evidence of having attained and exceeded goals and quotas in previous positions; experience working with software integration and ERP solutions is key.
</p>
<p>KNOWLEDGE/SKILLS/ABILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledgeable about channel sales initiatives and partner engagement best practices
</li>
<li>Knowledge of marketing and sales processes
</li>
<li>Extensive experience managing channel partners, VARs, VADs, SIs, and alliance partners
</li>
<li>Self-motivated and innovative, possessing the ability to motivate and influence partners
</li>
<li>Strong capacity to build relationships at both executive and operational levels
</li>
<li>Knowledgeable about transportation and logistics software
</li>
<li>Superior verbal and written communications skills
</li>
<li>Superior interpersonal and organizational skills
</li>
<li>Persistently demonstrates a positive and enthusiastic attitude toward the company, the work, the vision, and the people
</li>
<li>Ability to consistently drive and deliver outstanding results
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively and consistently engage a cross functional team
</li>
<li>Ability to organize and prioritize
</li>
<li>Ability to lead teams to success
</li>
<li>Ability to give and receive direction
</li>
<li>Ability to influence and create excitement with 3rd party entities</li>
<li>Ability to travel, domestically and internationally</li>
<br>
</ul>
