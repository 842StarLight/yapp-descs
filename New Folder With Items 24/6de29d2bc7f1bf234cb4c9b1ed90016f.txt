<h2>Consumer Loan Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY
</b></p>
<p>The <b>Consumer Loan Consultant</b> demonstrates a highly motivated, positive, outgoing, influential personality with a strong emphasis on sales and results orientation to ensure the credit union maximizes lending and sales opportunities with Members who apply for credit over the phone. This role demonstrates an unsurpassed commitment to exceptional Member service and teamwork that ensures a Raving Fans experience with each encounter. Is fully knowledgeable of all credit union products and services and is able to confidently offer appropriate solutions to fit Members’ needs. <b>This role will work at our Anaheim Hills office.</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ANTICIPATED WORK SCHEDULE
</b></p>
<p>The ideal candidate must be available to work 40 hours per week Monday-Friday 9:00am – 6:00pm.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Views situations from the Member’s perspective to effectively respond to their needs and concerns. Follows up to make sure the Member’s expectations have been met or exceeded. Explains recommendations, products and services in ways Members can easily understand. Demonstrates patience, respect and empathy when dealing with Members or their complaints.
</li>
<li>Uses strong interpersonal skills and influence to consistently achieve sales goals. Overcomes objections with persuasion and persistence. Displays confidence in presenting appropriate solutions to fit Members’ needs. Maximizes opportunities to sell products and services to benefit Member and enhance quality and depth of Member relationship and profitability.
</li>
<li>Using credit reports, Member data, and other information, actively seeks opportunities to increase loan volume. This includes contacting Members with pending loan applications and referring Members for non-consumer loan products when appropriate.
</li>
<li>Uses clear and simple language to communicate information and decisions to internal and external Members. Explains recommendations, products and services in ways Members can easily understand.
</li>
<li>Actively participates in and completes all ongoing required loan and sales training. Willingly adapts to changes in work environment.
</li>
<li>Maintains current knowledge of loan insurance and assists Members in making insurance selections.
</li>
<li>Books out the consumer secured collateral that the Member is interested in purchasing.
</li>
<li>Takes quality consumer loan applications in real-time on loan origination software.
</li>
<li>Pulls credit report, reviews it with the Member and engages in conversations that allow you to offer appropriate loan products with reasons why they will benefit the Member.
</li>
<li>Reviews the loans on a Member’s credit report so as to determine what payments should be included in the Member’s total and unsecured debt ratios.
</li>
<li>Writes thorough and clear notes to the centralized underwriter to eliminate confusion.
</li>
<li>Cross sells appropriate loans and account services.
</li>
<li>Sends the application electronically to the centralized underwriter.
</li>
<li>Carefully communicates all aspects of the underwriter’s decision to the Member (both approvals and denials).
</li>
<li>Proactively makes calls to Members to follow up on pending loan applications and applications that have been approved but have not yet funded.
</li>
<li>Performs related duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION REQUIREMENTS
</b></p>
<p>These specifications are general guidelines based on the minimum experience normally considered essential to the satisfactory performance of this job. Individual abilities may result in some deviation from these guidelines.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>To perform effectively in this position, the incumbent must have:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced working knowledge of consumer lending and processing.
</li>
<li>Advanced working knowledge of consumer loan laws and regulations.
</li>
<li>Advanced working knowledge of DMV procedures and tax return analysis.
</li>
<li>Advanced working knowledge of Visa.
</li>
<li>Advanced oral and written communications skills.
</li>
<li>Basic accounting and ten-key skills.
</li>
<li>Experience in on-line data processing.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Typically, these skills are the result of one or more years experience in making and processing consumer loans and completion of courses or seminars in the field. The incumbent must be able to perform this job safely, without endangering the health or safety of him/herself or others. The management of the credit union may change this position description at any time, according to business needs.<br>
