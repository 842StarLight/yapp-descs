<b>Purpose &amp; Summary:</b> The Account Manager II is responsible for the overall satisfaction of our current and future customer base, and meeting or exceeding their needs. Responsibilities include, but are not limited to: indirect training, settlements (billing and payments), logistics coordination, general inquiries, data-integrity, reporting, and customer care. Your service-driven approach when managing our current customer relationships will build a foundation of trust while working through sales proposals for current and new accounts. Day-to-Day responsibilities may include:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Training and coaching new team members in account management</li>
<li>Fielding customer inquiries with a high degree of responsiveness and professionalism</li>
<li>Managing the transition of contact from sales to account management for new accounts</li>
<li>Reviewing and ensuring that all payments &amp; invoices are timely</li>
<li>Guiding the operations team to execute agreements with customers</li>
<li>Executing the inbound &amp; outbound order creation</li>
<li>Completing settlement paperwork and the reconciliation of accounts</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements &amp; Qualifications:</b> For consideration as an Account Manager II at Dynamic, candidates must have at least one year of relevant experience in customer service or account management. Additional qualifications include:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>CRM and ERP software experience</li>
<li>Proficient Microsoft Office Suite, Outlook, and Quickbooks utilization</li>
<li>Customer Service Best Practices</li>
<li>Superior written &amp; verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Strong organization and time management skills</li>
<li>Natural relationship builder with conflict resolution skills</li>
<li>Thrive in a fast-paced environment and accepts change</li>
</ul>
