<h2>Security Project Engineer I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Summary Description</p>
<p>
Responsible for all phases of the design and programming of security systems. Provides support to security technicians.</p>
<p>
Duties and Responsibilities</p>
<p>
Essential Functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>Assists in creating/editing customer graphics, schedules, reports, and front end related programs for user GUI.</li>
<li>Installs customer graphics at site as required.</li>
<li>Assembles and tests front end for customer as required.</li>
<li>Performs customer and employee training as required.</li>
<li>Performs all engineering tasks on security projects, including, CAD design, sequence of operations, controller software, front-end hardware set up, all front-end software creation and set up, system functional testing, customer training and job commissioning.</li>
<li>Assists in performing job reconciliation of security projects.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains excellent working relationships with all customers.</li>
<li>Completes all system software setup before the beginning of system checkout.</li>
<li>Ensures that response times on projects meet established Tri-M turnaround times.</li>
<li>Supports service department with warranty and post-warranty customer support, and in after-hours support.</li>
<li>Performs or assists with job estimates/takeoffs as required.</li>
<li>Assists in performing security sales demonstrations and in supporting sales professionals in the sales cycle.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Job Qualifications</p>
<p>
Skills/Educational Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in mechanical or electrical engineering or equivalent technical training</li>
<li>Minimum of 4 years’ experience as a technician and/or engineer in the security industry, including experience with access control systems, CCTV, burg systems, and other building security systems.</li>
<li>Excellent working knowledge of Microsoft Windows applications: Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access.</li>
<li>Excellent working knowledge of drafting software (AutoCAD)</li>
<li>Good programming ability in common computer languages</li>
<li>Ability to handle and independently prioritize multiple tasks while meeting project deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to think independently and work under pressure</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
<li>Excellent human relations skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and communication skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Physical Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Working a minimum of 40 hours/week</li>
<li>Sitting 6 to 8 hours/day.</li>
<li>Lifting weights of 0 to 30 lbs. from floor to above shoulders.</li>
<li>Repetitive use of hands for writing, typing, data entry, and filing.</li>
<li>Vision adequate for computer use and reading.</li>
<li>Hearing adequate for phone use.</li>
</ul>
