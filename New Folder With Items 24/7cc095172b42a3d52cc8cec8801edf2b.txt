<p></p>Has ownership and accountability for local agency operations. Support Regional Agency Director by planning and directing operations of local agency to ensure maximum profitability, meeting needs of insureds and maintaining high customer service quality.
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>:
<br>
The following is a summary of the essential functions for this job. Other duties may be performed, both major and minor, which are not mentioned below. Specific activities may change from time to time.
<br>
1. Develop and carry out income and expense plan for local agency.
<br>
2. Develop and maintain strong client relationships.
<br>
3. Coordinate all training for personnel and maintain a highly trained professional staff.
<br>
4. Keep abreast of current trends and marketing conditions in the insurance industry, and pursue continuing education.
<br>
5. Deliver responsive service, advice and professional counsel to insureds and personnel.
<br>
6. Ensure that all payments for services are collected to minimize delinquency of accounts.
<br>
7. Maintain relationship with various insurance carriers to ensure availability and competitiveness of markets.
<br>
8. Direct all functions of agency to ensure efficient work flow is accomplished on a timely basis in a professional manner.
<br>
9. Periodically evaluate associatesâ€™ performance and take appropriate action to ensure maximum productivity.
<br>
10. Through an active personal marketing plan and directing the sales efforts of others, be the sales leader of the agency.
<br>
11. Total commission revenue of the agency will be approximately $250,000 or less.
</p>
