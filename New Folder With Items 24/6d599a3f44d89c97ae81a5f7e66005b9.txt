JOB SUMMARY
<br><br>
The purpose of the Replenishment Specialist is to drive sales through optimization of replenishment and allocations programs all while staying aligned with the companyâ€™s financial objectives.
<br><br>
JOB DUTIES AND ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for analyzing sales and inventory levels of your product category to decide store allocation of import and domestic product across all stores</li>
<li>Autonomy to purchase category product without limit and accountable to staying aligned with budgetary objectives</li>
<li>Review recommendations from Inventory Planner and determine direction and appropriate action regarding replenishment ordering</li>
<li>Identify poor performing items and categories and collaborate with Category Manager on recommended adjustments of inventory levels and pricing</li>
<li>Key initial orders for seasonal and/or basic programs as well as place, track, and adjust all new store orders</li>
<li>Generate and leverage reports for effective decision making</li>
<li>Monitor Purchase Orders and Special Orders for on-time shipping and fulfillment percentage</li>
<li>Partner with logistics and DC to recommend shipment changes to align with inventory goals</li>
<li>Set up and maintain item information in Eagle/ERP System</li>
<li>Assist in the product selection and minimum presentation stock level</li>
<li>Drive clear communication with strategic partners both inside and outside the organization</li>
<li>Assist Category Managers in vendor meetings and the line review process</li>
<li>Ensure accuracy of print advertising and product in stock to 98.5%</li>
<li>Participate in store travel to review stock performance on planogram, off planogram, and stockroom</li>
<li>May be required to cross train and perform other duties</li>
</ul>
