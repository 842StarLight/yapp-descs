<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Long Lines LLC, a wholly owned subsidiary of Schurz Communications, Inc., is seeking a motivated, detail-oriented part time employee to join our team. This individual will provide exceptional service to our clients while promoting and selling new products and services. If you are energetic and have a passion for excellence, Long Lines could be your ideal fit!</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Adapting, learning and delivering the best customer service our industry can give.</li>
<li>Sell and promote company products and services</li>
<li>Research customer complaints, processing payments and billing adjustment</li>
<li>Achieve sales goals by soliciting new and or up sell services</li>
<li>Follow up on and close sales leads</li>
<li>Resolve customer account issues</li>
<li>Maintain and organize records of sales / solicitation activity</li>
<li>Perform other duties as may be requested by supervisor</li>
<li>Maintain an appropriate and professional image of the company through appearance, demeanor, and interactions with customers at all times</li>
<li>Follow all safety rules and comply with company policies and specifications</li>
<li>Speak and understand English language proficiently</li>
<li>Speak and understand Spanish is a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>You will need to have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED equivalent</li>
<li>Ability to lift up to 25 lbs.</li>
<li>Ability to work while seated for prolonged periods of time</li>
<li>Ability to wear telephone head set for prolonged periods of time</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and organize effectively</li>
<li>Ability to use a 10-key adding machine</li>
<li>Ability to utilize problem solving techniques with high end cable TV equipment</li>
<li>Ability to use personal computer and software applications (i.e. word processing, spreadsheet, billing system)</li>
<li>Ability to work independently</li>
<li>Knowledge and ability to use the following office equipment: computer, telephone, copier, fax, calculator, and stapler</li>
<li>Knowledge of basic mathematics</li>
<li>Knowledge of cable television, internet, and phone products and services</li>
<li>Knowledge of common phone etiquette and de-escalation techniquesVision ability: close vision, peripheral vision, and ability to adjust focus</li>
<li>Able to communicate clearly, educate customers and promote/sell company services</li>
<li>Able to work independently and plan accordingly to complete daily assignments</li>
<br>
</ul>
