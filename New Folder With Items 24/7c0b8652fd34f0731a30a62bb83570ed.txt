Job Summary
<br>Direct the strategy and projects, people or innovation streams. Supporting the development of research staff members. Direct the construction of a variety of statistical analyses including data mining, predictive modeling and/or actuarial techniques. Review analyses and provide direction and insight. Work within broad limits and authority on complex assignments requiring specialized knowledge in breadth and/or depth in area of expertise which may be on or more of the following: Advanced statistical analyses. Actuarial and/or research methods. Broad business knowledge. In-depth Line of Business knowledge. Broader awareness of enterprise Analytics &amp; Research community, Directly manage staff for large complex projects and/or direct complex statistical, operational or technical problems.
<br><br>
Marketing Description
<br>This is a highly visible role in Small Commercial Analytics, leading a team in developing and maintaining various models that influence Small Commercial business strategy. The business analytics team in Small Commercial Analytics is focused on taking a data driven approach to addressing questions in different business partners, including product management, underwriting, business process effectiveness, distribution and sales, business center, etc.
<br><br>
Primary Job Duties &amp; Responsibilities
<br>Direct and/or perform a variety of advanced statistical analyses including data mining and actuarial research techniques. Identify &amp; creatively use conventional and non-conventional data sources independently. Direct multiple complex research projects simultaneously as well as large and complex initiatives. Analyze/interpret data, draw business inferences and clearly articulate findings for target audience. Direct the construction and present analyses and recommendations to target audience. Lead in-depth efforts in applying, validating and implementing predictive models. Proactively stay abreast of industry, developments, changing trends and jurisdictional issues. Direct industry analysis and benchmarking studies. Adjust strategy as appropriate to anticipate, respond to, and manage trends. Develop and maintain effective relationships with business partners. Direct the development of new approaches to business problems. Partner with peers across the company to ensure consistency and alignment where appropriate.
<br><br>
Minimum Qualifications
<br>PhD STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) degree with 4 years experience or Masters STEM degree with 7 years experience or Bachelor's STEM degree with 9 yrs experience required. Advanced working knowledge of modeling/research/analytics or actuarial required. Relevant statistical analysis work experience required.
<br><br>
Education, Work Experience &amp; Knowledge
<br>Relevant work experience in directing and performing research and/or advanced analytic work (e.g. predictive modeling) in the insurance industry preferred.
