<h2>Senior Account Manager, Strategic Accounts - Tradeshows</h2>

<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Slate360 is seeking an experienced tradeshow/event marketing Senior Account Manager to join our growing organization. In this position, you will be responsible for managing and creating long-term, trusting relationships with our clients.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities and Qualifications</b></p>
<p>· Maintain strong relationships with existing clients and seek avenues for growth with both existing and new clients</p>
<p>· Direct experience within the tradeshow and event marketing industry</p>
<p>· Strong skills in strategy, prioritization, and logistics planning with multiple clients</p>
<p>· Ability to manage and maintain large program accounts (consisting of several conferences per year)</p>
<p>· Establish timelines, weekly meeting agendas, internal and external task lists, etc.</p>
<p>· Establish and oversee internal budgets within the company and external budgets with the client</p>
<p>· Proven results of delivering client solutions and meeting sales goals</p>
<p>· Experience delivering client-focused solutions to customer needs</p>
<p>· Excellent communication skills: must be a listener, a presenter, and a people-person</p>
<p>· Strong negotiation skills, with the ability to follow through on client contracts</p>
<p>· Proven ability to juggle multiple account management projects at a time, while maintaining sharp attention to detail</p>
<p>· Strong verbal and written communication skills</p>
<p>· Ability to read set-up prints and floorplans</p>
<p>· Ability to work a flexible and demanding schedule</p>
<p>· Capable of analytical thinking and problem solving</p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree in marketing, business administration, sales, or relevant field</p>
<p>· Previous work experience in sales, management, account management, or relevant experience</p>
<p>· Basic computer skills and experience with CRM software and the Microsoft Office Suite, with emphasis on superior Excel skills</p>
<p>· Ability to travel up to 30% of the time, including internationally</p>
<p><b>Education</b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent work experience</p>
