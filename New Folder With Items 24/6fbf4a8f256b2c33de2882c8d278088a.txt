<p>
ROLE SUMMARY<br>
The Kaiser Managed Care Specialist will be responsible for the relationships and business opportunities with HCPs in Kaiser clinics, hospitals, and facilities to drive customer value and overall product results across multiple therapeutic areas. They will leverage a high level of product, competitive, customer, and territory knowledge with effective selling techniques and marketing strategies to deliver customer service and education as well as support the execution of promotional programs for their assigned therapeutic areas and products.</p>
<p>
The Managed Care Specialist will be the “eyes and ears” with the HCPs. They will identify customer insights, opportunities, and tactics at the local level which they will share and discuss with the account team and other key stakeholders in managing the Kaiser account strategies and plan. They will develop and execute effective territory business plans to enhance key relationships and drive territory results that align with the overall Kaiser account plan objectives and strategies.</p>
<div>
<br>
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES<br>
<ul>
<li>Execute sales and marketing strategies within the territory, conducting sales calls to HCPs and other key customers to achieve sales and market share objectives</li>
<li>Support the implementation of Sanofi and Kaiser tailored programs and brand specific strategies in Kaiser clinics, hospitals, pharmacies, and other major medical facilities in their assigned geography in order to expand business and brand loyalty</li>
<li>Establish relationships with key clients and thought leaders in assigned territory, developing and coordinating physician advocates for promoted products</li>
<li>Conduct effective product presentations to customers communicating appropriate technical, therapeutic, disease state, and product information</li>
<li>Plan, coordinate, and facilitate "speaker programs"</li>
<li>Collaborate and coordinate with other members of the Kaiser account team (Account Lead, Account Executives, other Managed Care Specialists) to share insights and learnings, triage issues and questions, develop strategies and tactics, and identify opportunities and new offerings</li>
<li>Create and implement local strategic business plans, leveraging customer insights and opportunities, to enhance and grow key relationships and territory business in line with regional Kaiser business plan and strategy</li>
<li>Conduct various administrative duties including entering all calls into the company computer with relevant post call notes, weekly reports, plans of action, expense reports</li>
<li>Attend all company and Kaiser meetings as well as medical education programs, which can occur during evening hours and/or weekends</li>
<li>Complete and maintain training requirements for responsible therapeutic areas, selling skills, compliance, etc</li>
<li>Complete all fleet safety training and maintain an acceptable driving record regarding accidents and incidents</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
QUALIFICATIONS<br>
Skills and Experiences<br>
<ul>
<li>Fully accomplished as current sales professionals with 3+ years successful pharmaceutical / medical sales experience acting as leaders, mentors and role models within their district and territory selling team</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to establish long-term relationships with key customers for their designated therapeutic area and may have had responsibility for building and maintaining formulary availability, ensuring product availability, and organizing resources for symposia</li>
<li>Considered technical expert of specific company products, specific therapeutic classes, and specific patient needs</li>
<li>Strong business acumen including excellent and persuasive business communication, ability to analyze and address territory opportunities, and develop and execute local business plans specific to the needs of their customers</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage territory budget</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license with a clean driving record and ability to pass a complete background check</li>
<li>Computer Skills: proficiency in business software (MS Outlook, Word, Excel), comfortable with current sales force automation system, and embraces technological change</li>
</ul>
