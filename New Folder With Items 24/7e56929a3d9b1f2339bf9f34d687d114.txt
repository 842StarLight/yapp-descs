<p><b>About Arteza:</b></p>
<p><b>
Arteza</b> is revolutionizing the Art Supply industry as one of the fastest growing brands and e-commerce retailers in the country. We offer a convenient way to shop for art supplies coupled with a highly innovative product offering. Headquartered next to the beaches of Miami, we are quickly expanding the team. If you are interested in working in a fast paced, start-up environment with highly motivated entrepreneurs, this is the place for you.</p>
<p><b>
Our opportunity:</b></p>
<p><b>
Arteza</b> is seeking a motivated, goal oriented, results driven <b>Supply Chain - Forecast Analyst </b>to join our exciting start-up in North Miami Beach, FL. Our team is growing and if you are passionate about e-commerce and career growth, an opportunity at Arteza may be a great match! In this position, your responsibilities will include creating and managing the forecasting for both North America and Europe. Being in a startup environment, you will not only be responsible for your role but will also have the opportunity to learn all aspects of Supply Chain including Supply Planning, Inventory Management, Fulfillment, Inbound and Outbound Logistics. You will work side by side with intelligent, passionate, highly motivated entrepreneurs. We’re looking for someone who has the personality, passion, excitement and drive to succeed!</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><div><b>
Manage item-channel forecast using statistical modeling software. Analyze and validate data and present findings to Sales and Marketing as well as Management.</b></div></li>
<li><div>
Communicate and coordinate with Sales, Operations and Marketing teams to discuss planned product promotions in order to produce more accurate forecasts.</div></li>
<li><div><b>
Utilize the data warehouse to conduct analysis, produce reports for sales forecast at channel and network levels.</b></div></li>
<li><div>
Support management with risk assessments and mitigation activities.</div></li>
<li><div>
Propose and implement solutions to improve demand forecast accuracy.</div></li>
<li><div>
Monitor and report on important changes in sales forecasts, budgets, and business strategies.</div></li>
<li><div>
Conduct daily forecast maintenance.</div></li>
<li><p>
Address demand related issues in a timely and effective manner.</p></li>
</ul>
