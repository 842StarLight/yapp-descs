<h2>Paralegal/Specialist - Corporate Legal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We’re changing the world. Let us start with yours.
<br><br>
First Solar’s legal department seeks candidates who believe in our mission of providing cost-advantaged solar technology through innovation, customer engagement, industry leadership and operational excellence. First Solar’s legal team is an international team of trusted advisors who provide first-class legal advisory services across the entire business and operations of the company. The department’s goal is to enable scale and support the company’s growth through timely and efficient legal support across a wide spectrum of legal projects and matters, while mitigating the company’s risks positions and enabling compliance.
<br><br>
The legal team looks for individuals who enjoy contributing to an innovative, forward-thinking team, are effective problem solvers and continuously look for ways to improve processes to help us lead the world’s sustainable energy future.
<br><br>
<b>Basic Job Functions:</b><br>
The Corporate Legal Specialist is responsible for providing support to First Solar’s Global Legal Department, with a broad range of transactional, corporate secretarial, record-keeping, database-related, board-related, international and commercial focused projects of varying complexity.
<br><br>
The Corporate Legal Specialist will work closely with Tax, Treasury, Compliance, Human Resources, Business and Project Development, as well as all levels of Executive Management with respect to the preparation, execution, notarization and legalization of a wide variety of transactional and corporate documentation.
<br><br>
The Corporate Legal Specialist will also serve as the sole Serengeti E-Billing Tracker Coordinator, responsible for all systems maintenance as well as the daily processing of legal invoices, matter creation, billing policy formation and implementation, attorney support, invoice accruals and reporting.
<br><br>

<b>Essential Responsibilities:</b><br>
Provide executive-level support with preparation, drafting, review, and distribution of Board Materials, including minutes, written consents, resolutions and other documentation, with independent interaction with Board members and all levels of management as needed
<br><br>
Manage activities related to contract/document/records management functions and interface within all areas of the legal department as well as cross-functionally with First Solar departments and third party service providers
<br><br>
Support litigation management with discovery; consistently and independently interface with appropriate First Solar departments and outside counsel
<br><br>
Provide drafting, research and organizational assistance to in-house attorneys and outside counsel during the negotiation period and at the closing of complex transactions (i.e. mergers/acquisitions, project acquisitions, divestitures, financings, product sales, etc.)
<br><br>
Work collaboratively with other specialists, attorneys and other functions as part of a global process for creating legal entities and ensure all subsidiaries are qualified to do business in all jurisdictions as necessary. Draft and maintain corresponding subsidiary documentation. Maintain database to track subsidiaries, both globally and domestically; establish and lead working relationships with non-US administrators of subsidiary housekeeping
<br><br>
Responsible for serving as the sole Serengeti tracker coordinator for payment of all legal invoices as well and running the systems back end, creating invoice approval routes, fixing accounts payable errors and coordinating quarterly accruals for finance
<br><br>
Responsible for writing User Guides when implementing new systems (e.g., Serengeti)
<br><br>
Support the management of the Legal Budget; includes tracking and submitting invoices on behalf of the legal team, quarterly accruals and invoice troubleshooting
<br><br>
Responsible for multitude of SoX controls on a monthly and quarterly basis on Movaris for audit purposes
<br><br>
Assist EPC department in obtaining state professional licenses (construction, engineering) as requested and keeping licenses current
<br><br>
Supervise execution and distribution of legal and transactional documents; help ensure First Solar policies are complied with as part of this process
<br><br>
Utilize computer skills and technical knowledge to handle and assist with the creation of designs and/or development of intranet websites (hCue, Serengeti and Sharepoint)
<br><br>
Prepare and maintain electronic and manual data rooms for due diligence; organize and file closing documents once transaction is complete
<br><br>
Responsible for drafting Board meeting agendas and maintaining minute books
<br><br>
Perform other associated responsibilities and special projects (ex. M&amp;A, insurance bonds, etc.)
<br><br>
Responsible for receiving and parceling out all litigation based Service of Process through the Company’s registered agent CT Corporation
<br><br>
Responsible for the training and onboarding of new paralegals
<br><br>
Organize and attend paralegal-only meetings and coordination efforts across the multi-discipline-focused paralegal team
<br><br>
Other duties as assigned
