<h2>Drop Ship Coord-Perf & Service</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>Job Description Summary
<br>
Under the direction of the Senior Manager Drop Ship, the Coordinator is responsible for the timely and accurate readiness in QVC’s systems of vendors, merchandise, and inventory in order to support the sales and fulfillment of QVC’s Drop Ship business. This individual oversees item setup, inventory analysis and maintenance, reporting, and feedback to QVC’s Drop Ship vendors in the effort to assure accurate, compliant, and available inventory is available for sale and fulfillment.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description Details
</b></p>
<div><div>Job Description
</div></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>Job Description
</p>
<p>The Coordinator is responsible for integrity and quality of data, and providing clients with appropriate information &amp; recommendations. The individual uses an independent, proactive approach to the Drop Ship business and the setup and maintenance related to vendor and item creation as well as the management of inventory.
</p>
<ul>
<li>The Coordinator uses discretion to assess the viability of a current or potential Drop Ship vendor with respect to management of inventory, ability to support the required mix of carriers and services
</li>
<li>The individual ensures that all vendors are delivering the Brand experience according to company policies and procedures
</li>
<li>This individual will use discretion and provide advice to QVC management regarding the readiness and ability of vendors to use specific carriers
</li>
<li>This individual will work independently in configuring systems that control the available inventory for sale, the ship methods used for packages, and the customer experience in tracking and visibility post-purchase
</li>
</ul>
