<h2>Inside Sales Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Whether you are starting your career or are a seasoned professional you can be a part of a great sales team at Connection. We are looking for Inside Sales Account Managers to bring their passion for customer service, consultative sales, and problem solving to our sales teams in Wilmington, OH. Our Inside Sales Account Managers are professionals providing the exact solutions that our customers want, specifically working to uncover IT needs for State and Local Governments, K-12, and Higher Education (SLED).<br>
<br>
Your career begins with an 8 week paid training to learn the systems, best practices, pre and post-sales support resources, and valid contract vehicles that allow Connection to make immediate impacts with public sector customers nationwide. Your internal training opportunities continue throughout your career to provide you with ongoing professional development, resources, and tools to be building a successful, challenging and rewarding career. Your success is our number one priority.<br>
<br>
Join our team of Inside Sales Account Managers at Connection today! We will reward you with a generous compensation package, medical and dental plans, 401K with match, vacation time from day one, and wellness benefits. Right from the start you will enjoy a great environment to build your career. We look forward to supporting you for the long term.<br>
<br>
<b>Duties include:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Cold call and prospect to establish new accounts which maintaining established book of business</li>
<li>
Maintain and grow business with existing accounts</li>
<li>
Plan and organize daily work schedule</li>
<li>
Demonstrates exceptional follow-through, personal drive and the desire to make a difference</li>
<li>
Presents key selling points, features, and benefits while focusing on the customer's needs and expectations</li>
<li>
Maintains a track record of sales growth (even during slow times)</li>
<li>
Uses a consultative sales approach</li>
<li>
Maintains company values and demands the highest standards of conduct from self and others</li>
<li>
Listens attentively and responds effectively to customer concerns</li>
<li>
Negotiates or mediates discussions competently</li>
<li>
Demonstrates ability to close sales and overcome customer's objections</li>
</ul>
