<h2>Manager Aggregates Plant .2M ST - .9M ST</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Job Summary</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>Managing daily operation of sand mine; including supervision of salaried supervisor(s) and hourly employees. Responsible for budgeting; mine planning; permitting; working as member of plant management team to achieve operational goals and objectives; managing contracts and contractors; and community relations.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Job Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Achieve and/or maintain Zero4Life
<ul>
<li>Utilize VFL program and support the SIC and SBO programs</li>
<li>Promote a healthy work force</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Track cost and maintain costs in the sand mine operation.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a safe and environmentally compliant operation with the assistance of the Safety and Environmental Representatives.</li>
<li>Develop new operational methods to reduce costs and improve operational efficiency.</li>
<li>Interface with Corporate Mine Planning staff as well as local QC staff to develop the short term and long - term mining plan.</li>
<li>Direct production to meet production goals.</li>
<li>Minimize overtime used, schedule vacations, etc.</li>
<li>Maintain equipment with assistance of maintenance personnel through coordination and scheduling.</li>
<li>Maintain purchasing of regular materials for the operation and work with Procurement to purchase routine materials.</li>
<li>Maintains operational compliance with zoning and related permits</li>
<li>Develop annual budget for the operation.</li>
<li>Responsible for meeting all MSHA and local safety requirements as well as maintaining environmental issues at this sensitively located operation.</li>
<li>Be a Company Representative with the local community and interface with them.</li>
<li>Understand basic financial reporting related to aggregate operations</li>
<li>Utilize Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to help guide production and identify areas for improvement</li>
<li>Coordinate with sales to plan for future inventory requirements</li>
<li>Adhere to all CEMEX, MSHA, and OSHA rules and regulations at all times and wear proper safety equipment while on plant grounds</li>
<li>Meet or exceed company safety standards</li>
<li>Perform other job responsibilities as assigned by management</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree with a focus in Mining, Civil Engineering or Mechanical Engineering preferred</li>
<li>3 Years of mine supervision experience required</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills</li>
<li>Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access) experience required</li>
<li>AutoCAD experience a plus with desire and ability to learn more detailed mining software (Surpac Quarry)</li>
<li>Ability to work in an outdoor environment with some physical labor at times</li>
<li>A Self-motivated Team player with a focus on mission accomplishment</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Working Conditions</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Capable to work extended hours as needed</li>
<li>Ability to participate in required overnight travel as needed</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Physical Requirements</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Requires stationary position, traverse, move, transport, and the ability to significant degree to operate, activate, use, prepare, inspect, place, detect, or position</li>
<li>Exerting up to 50 pounds of force occasionally, and/or a negligible amount of force frequently. Job involves sitting most of the time, but also involves walking or standing for brief periods of time</li>
<li>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to talk and hear, in order to communicate to employees/visitors</li>
</ul></div>
