<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Codal is a user experience design and development agency with a focus on blending an Agile process with the latest technologies. Our clientele has ranged from small business to the Fortune 100, but our philosophy has always remained the same: to empower brand visibility and deliver the most elegant web and mobile solutions possible.</p>
<p><b>Roles &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>Codal is searching for an energetic and highly skilled account executive, a salesperson who excels in a client-facing role and has the ability to manage and grow Codal's operations across a global market.</p>
<p>With veritable experience in selling enterprise-level software solutions, mobile, or development services, our ideal candidate will hunt and generate leads by tapping into a variety of channels and sources, whether that's cold calls, networking events, or simply striking up conversation. Other responsibilities include:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Facilitating and fully executing the end-to-end business development process, from lead generation to closure</li>
<li>Establishing quantifiable, concrete monthly sales goals and meeting or exceeding them consistently</li>
<li>Being an active participant in ensuring sales conversion and actively supporting Codal's brand</li>
<li>Driving strategic deals and managing the needs of multiple accounts simultaneously</li>
<li>Generating leads by tapping into a variety of sources and channels</li>
<li>Leading post-sale efforts for our customers, as well as identifying and pursuing areas for new business with existing clientele</li>
<li>Assisting in the authoring of sales documentation including quotations, scope-of-work definitions, and proposals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements / Must Haves</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Education: Bachelor's Degree in IT, Computer Science, Engineering, Business or a related field is required</li>
<li>A minimum of 4 years of experience in sales, with a background in enterprise-level software solutions, mobile, or development services highly preferred</li>
<li>Familiarity with CRM systems and marketing automation platforms such as Hubspot and Salesforce</li>
<li>Demonstrate high levels of integrity, initiative, honesty, and leadership</li>
<li>Unmatched marketing, communication, and presentation skills</li>
<li>A team player who can easily adapt to a rapidly changing environment.</li>
<li>Experience leading multi-disciplinary teams</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Why Work For Codal</b></p>
<p>As a Codal employee, you're a member of a dedicated &amp; driven organization, composed of industry visionaries and auteurs. We take the utmost pride in our work and are truly passionate about the services we provide. As a world-class agency, we offer all of the benefits of an enterprise company, in a friendlier, tight-knit office community.</p>
