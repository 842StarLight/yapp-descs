<p><b>Detailed Description:</b></p>
<p>
The role of Business Intelligence Consultant will work with project teams to identify, specify, and deliver business intelligence solutions for clients. These clients are primarily in the water and wastewater industry and the consultant will use a variety of tools and process to collect, organize, and display visual information originating from a variety of source systems. Deep understanding of databases, data structures, SQL programming, and specific tools (especially Microsoft’s PowerBI) are important to successfully performing in this role.</p>
<p>
In addition to the technical skills described, consultants at BC are also expected to work effectively in a team-based project delivery environment, and maintain interpersonal skills suitable to working with clients. While not a primary objective of this position, the ability to support teams in developing and delivering proposals and other marketing and sales materials projects with BI components is a strong plus.</p>
<p>
The successful candidate will serve as a member of the Solutions Delivery Enterprise group based in one of the offices listed above. This is an exciting opportunity to work in a growth-oriented professional consulting team mentored by nationally recognized, senior technical staff on various projects within our Business Consulting Practice. Typical projects could involve:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Deployment of field data collection tools and management of large field datasets</li>
<li>
Development of BI dashboards to visualize project data for analysis and presentation</li>
<li>
Supporting the generation of reports and technical memoranda</li>
<li>
Supporting efforts in the storage, reformatting, extraction and consolidation of large amounts of data</li>
<li>
Development of different prototypes for Smart Utility use cases</li>
<li>
Development of prototypes for visualizing information on GIS and other BI platforms for Smart Utility use cases</li>
<li>
Manipulation and analysis of data using spreadsheets and GIS</li>
<li>
Travel to project sites for data collection, solution configuration and client meetings</li>
<li>
Participate in client development activities</li>
<li>
Assisting in the collaboration with other company subject matter experts and technology vendors on new field technology in flow, pressure and water quality measurement including Internet-of-Things smart sensors, data loggers and systems to support storage and display of this data.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Required Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience automating tasks using scripting and machine learning tools (e.g., Python, R)</li>
<li>
Experience with accessing data in an RDBMS (SQL Server, Oracle) via SQL</li>
<li>
Integration of data from business systems (e.g., GIS, CMMS, hydraulic modeling, condition assessment) using ETL tools (e.g., SSIS, Power BI, SQL manipulation)</li>
<li>
Strong aptitude for researching and solving technical problems</li>
<li>
Technical writing expertise preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Additional Desired Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience in data management and relational database fundamentals required</li>
<li>
Experience in data presentation and analytics using Microsoft PowerBI and/or Tableau</li>
<li>
Experience in deploying Microsoft SQL server is a plus</li>
<li>
Experience in deploying Microsoft SharePoint is a plus</li>
<li>
Experience with developing data dashboards/visualizations, especially with Microsoft’s Power BI.</li>
<li>
Experience with geospatial data collection and data management using Esri ArcGIS software</li>
<li>
Experience with deploying field data collection processes and tools for water/sewer infrastructure inventory &amp; condition assessment.</li>
<li>
Basic working knowledge of water, wastewater and stormwater infrastructure hydraulics and operation</li>
</ul>
