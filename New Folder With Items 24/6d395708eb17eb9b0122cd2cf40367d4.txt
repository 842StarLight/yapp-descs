<p>L3Harris is dedicated to recruiting and developing diverse, high-performing talent who are passionate about what they do. Our employees are unified in a shared dedication to our customers’ mission and quest for professional growth. L3Harris provides an inclusive, engaging environment designed to empower employees and promote work-life success. Fundamental to our culture is an unwavering focus on values, dedication to our communities, and commitment to excellence in everything we do.
</p>
<p>L3Harris Technologies is an agile global aerospace and defense technology innovator, delivering end-to-end solutions that meet customers’ mission-critical needs. The company provides advanced defense and commercial technologies across air, land, sea, space and cyber domains. L3Harris has approximately $17 billion in annual revenue, 50,000 employees and customers in more than 130 countries. L3Harris.com
</p>
<p>The Sr. Manager, HR Business Partner will lead the HR team in support of the Services Division in the Maritime Sector of the Integrated Mission Systems segment of L3Harris Technologies. This role will directly report to the Sr. Director, Human Resources for the Maritime Sector and partner closely with the GM and leadership team within the Services Division.
</p>
<ul>
<li><div>Deliver Human Resources leadership and support to the organization to facilitate meeting business and strategic growth objectives
</div></li>
<li><div>Establish and execute the client HR strategy, exercise thought leadership, exercise good judgment, and make difficult decisions
</div></li>
<li><div>Collaborate with functional leaders to create workforce and staffing plans to ensure availability of human capital necessary to accomplish business results
</div></li>
<li><div>Use metrics/analytics to drive performance. Champion employee engagement, retention and diversity and inclusion.
</div></li>
<li><div>Oversee Talent Management activity including Performance Management, Employee Relations, Organizational Development, Employee Development, and with a relentless focus on enhancing diversity representation.
</div></li>
<li><div>Build and maintain effective working relationships with management and employees and provide advice and counsel to both on employee-related and organizational matters.
</div></li>
<li><p>Drive L3Harris business operating system (“e3,” L3Harris’s continuous improvement operating model) principles and processes with a focus on increasing administrative excellence and process improvement across the business units and within the HR function.<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
