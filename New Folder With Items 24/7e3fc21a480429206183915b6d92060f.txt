<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Credit Memo Specialist issues credit memos to customer, collections and sales representative requests.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Functions
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Receives and issues credit requests by fax, email, and primarily by phone within set guidelines utilizing the appropriate credit type codes.</li>
<li>
Compares the customer credit request with actual listing of product returned, making appropriate adjustments and releasing credit memos.</li>
<li>
Escalates issues regarding credit memos to A/R Management, Transportation, Warehouse and Sales.</li>
<li>
Assists customers in identifying when credits were released and how they were used.</li>
<li>
Prepares report of memos that are on hold for sales organization.</li>
<li>
Execute queries to extract credit memo information.</li>
<li>
Must maintain good attendance.</li>
<li>
Maintain safe work practices.</li>
<li>
Maintain safeguards of confidential company information.</li>
<li>
Other duties and special projects as requested.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
