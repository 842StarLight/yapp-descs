<p><b>
Your Career</b></p>
<p>
As a Director of Product Marketing at Palo Alto Networks, you will play a key role in planning and executing the product marketing strategy for our SaaS Security and Data Loss Prevention products. You will own positioning, messaging, content creation and sales enablement for the products that you are responsible for.</p>
<p>
You will work closely with stakeholders from product management, sales, field marketing, and technology partners.</p>
<p><b>
Your Impact</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Author compelling, high quality content, both at a business and a technical level, in a variety of formats including print, web, social media, and video.</li>
<li>
Establish the unique value proposition of a product, with a clear understanding of the target markets and buyer personas.</li>
<li>
Launch products and collaborate with field teams to execute marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>
Effectively work at building and executing plans that cross different functional groups, including product management, business development and sales.</li>
<li>
Represent the company at conferences to evangelize a point of view</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Your Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of product marketing experience is required.</li>
<li>
Experience working in either the Cloud Access Security Broker (CASB) or Data Loss Prevention (DLP) technology domains is required.</li>
<li>
Outstanding verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Comfortable with creating and delivering presentations in a range of environments, from industry conferences to customer briefings.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree required, MBA is desirable</li>
</ul>

Your Team</b></p>
