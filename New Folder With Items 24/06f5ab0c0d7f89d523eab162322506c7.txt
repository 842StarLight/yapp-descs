<p><b>What’s the role?</b></p>
<p>You’ll be developing and communicating high level supply plans that convey Oracle liability to suppliers. Interfacing regularly with marketing new products, teams, and finance planning teams to assure plans are as accurate as possible. Analyzing historical volumes and integrating learnings into plans. Query writing and/or analysis is often needed.
</p>
<p>Facilitate the Consensus Demand Planning Process and agreement of the final CDP Judgement for Services ARR/unit demand plans. Develops and communicates services demand plans which drive data center capacity plans and hardware demand plans generating Oracle liability with suppliers. Interfaces regularly with Cloud Sales, Finance, Product Management, &amp; Capacity planning teams to assure plans are as accurate as possible for Organic, Inorganic, Project, and Internal Demands.</p>
<p>Monitor hardware spend / forecast to ensure they are aligned to the hardware capacity plans &amp; Capex budgets required to support the Services ARR/unit demand plans for assigned Services &amp; Capacity Pools. Interfaces regularly with Cloud Sales, Finance, Product Management, &amp; Capacity planning teams to assure plans are as accurate as possible for Organic, Inorganic, Project, and Internal Demands.
</p>
<p>Monitor forecast vs. actual metrics for all sources of Demand at the services layer (organic, Inorganic, Project, &amp; Internal demand) as well as for the hardware demand plans. Monitors consumption at all levels and update plans as needed to diagnose consumption issues and drive resolution with the appropriate functions. Maintains all planning item attributes. Ensures plans flowing out to supplier portals are accurate; performs plan validations and extract commitments from the supply. Base judge the quality of those commitments and escalate as needed. Supports new product teams, end of life teams and excess and obsolete inventory teams waterfalls and other management reports as needed.</p>
<p>Serve as Business Operations Point of Contact between assigned Cloud LOB and Operations / GP&amp;F organizations. Ensure required weekly / monthly deliverables are fulfilled as a part of the Capacity Interlocks.</p>
<p>Manage the development and implementation of a Data Science based statistical forecast into the Cloud Services CDP process. Understanding of advanced Statistical Models and Algorithms often required. Manages regional demand splits for demand plans.</p>
<p>Coordinate Vendor commits and provide Operations Rack level commitments to LOB for assigned products. Work with suppliers for assigned products to understand the weekly plans and drivers behind material changes.</p>
<p>Develop plans for inventory and safety stock of assigned products and manage inventory to ensure targets for inventory levels, E&amp;O and delivery SLAs are met.</p>
<p>Monitor the Excess &amp; Obsolete Inventory, and reports performance as well as Risks and Opportunities to Cloud Lines of Business. Drive demand mitigation efforts for assigned products, ensure unfavorable P&amp;L hits are within goals for assigned products.</p>

<b>
Detailed Description and Job Requirements</b><div>
</div>
Interfaces with sales, business units and suppliers to develop product forecasts, create inventory strategies and resolve supply-demand issues impacting revenue, customer delivery and margin.<br>
<br>
Independently works to develop product forecasts, create inventory strategies and resolve supply-demand issues for assigned products. Leads cross-functional supply chain teams to solve excess inventory and supply shortage problems impacting revenue, customer delivery and margin. Drives forecast accuracy improvement. Assesses forward-looking supply chain revenue capability for senior leadership. When necessary, develops and coordinates supply allocations for internal and supplier teams to optimize supply chain performance. Presents business results and planning recommendations to cross-functional teams in sales, finance and business units. May be assigned to assist in the design, development and evaluation of new planning processes and systems. Provides direction, specialist knowledge and training to junior planners for their activities. Assists senior planners and management teams in their activities on an ad-hoc basis. Builds constructive stakeholder relationships which involve direct interaction and influence with different Oracle lines-of-business and Oracle suppliers.<br>
<br>
Job duties are varied and complex and the planner will frequently need to exercise independent judgment. Leading contributor individually and as a team member, providing direction and mentoring to others. Able to operate in a fast-paced, ambiguous environment. Key skills and abilities include complex/statistical data analysis, influencing, presentation, communication, process improvement, problem solving, and cross-functional team leadership. Key knowledge areas include expertise in supply chain planning, in-depth understanding other supply chain disciplines and product/business strategy, and strong overall business acumen. Prefer 5 years relevant experience and BA/BS degree.<br>
