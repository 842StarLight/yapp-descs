<h2>Technical Assistant, Sales Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Stop Loss Insurance Services</b> (SLIS), an AmWINS Group Company, is looking for a <b>Technical Assistant (Sales Support)</b> to join our Worcester, MA office. This non-exempt position will work closely with an Account Manager, Sales Executive and Claims Manager, assisting in various vital functions to the team. The position will support an existing team of sales professionals serving a growing client base.<br>
<br>
This is not a remote position, nor is relocation assistance available. The candidate must be able to commute to a centrally located office in Worcester, MA for normal office hours.<br>
<br>
<b>The ideal candidate will have:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Some college preferred but not required</li>
<li>Solid analytical, verbal, and written skills</li>
<li>Above average Outlook and Excel skills</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and communicate in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple groups and deadlines</li>
<li>Knowledge of group health benefits is preferred but not required</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Administrative Assistant/Sales Support Assistant duties include:</b></div>
<ul><li>Assist in preparing requests for Stop Loss Insurance to our carrier partners and obtaining competitive policy options for current and prospective clients. This will include:
<ul>
<li>Assist in analyzing requests for proposals (RFPs) as received by email</li>
<li>Email requests for additional information to broker/administrator</li>
<li>Follow up for additional information at the request of Account Manager or Claims Manager</li>
<li>Prepare standard RFP package and submit to carriers selected in collaboration with Account Manager</li>
<li>Enter information into an online enterprise management system (AmLINK) and Excel forms.</li>
<li>Update as needed throughout process</li>
<li>Follow up with underwriters to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Review responses received for accuracy and ensure that all requested options are included</li>
<li>Assist Account Manager with sending updates and/or requests for additional options</li>
<li>Assist Account Manager with sold/renewal case paperwork</li>
<li>Advise Account Manager of any underwriting changes Assist Claims Manager with sold case paperwork, tracking receipt of claims reports</li>
<li>Processing Claims reimbursement checks</li>
<li>Prepare invoices and send to proper contact</li>
<li>Send out and track Business Associate Agreements as well as Broker Appointment paperwork</li>
<li>Manage Vendor Payable</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<div><b>To the qualified candidate we offer:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Compensation includes competitive hourly wages, with occasional overtime and bonus plan</li>
<li>Full benefit package including: Medical, Dental, Life, Disability and 401(k)</li>
<li>Collaborative, continuing education focused work environment</li>
<li>Entry level position with opportunity for advancement in growing office</li>
<li>Generous Paid Time Off (PTO) with Vacation and Personal days</li>
<li>Position will report to Operations Manager</li>
</ul>
