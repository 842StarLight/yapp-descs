<h2>Business Analyst/Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ECS is seeking a <b><i>Business Analyst/Project Manager</i></b> to work in our <b><i>Rosslyn, VA</i></b> office. <b><i>Please Note: This position is contingent upon contract award.</i></b></p>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops, coordinates, tracks, and manages projects for a variety of simultaneous initiatives of</li>
<li>varying sizes</li>
<li>Supports cross‐functional project teams consisting of application development, IT operations,</li>
<li>and information security teams</li>
<li>Works with subject matter experts to document user acceptance test plans and coordinates the</li>
<li>execution of user acceptance testing</li>
<li>Documents and tracks project changes, risks, and issues through resolution</li>
<li>Regularly meets with stakeholders to identify and document business, functional, and technical</li>
<li>requirements</li>
<li>Maintains and updates all requirements documentation</li>
<li>Communicates changes to requirements and projects to all stakeholders</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b><i>Must have a Bachelors Degree</i></b></li>
<li>2‐3 years of business analysis experience</li>
<li>3‐5 years of project management experience</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communications skills</li>
<li>Ability to translate conceptual requirements into business and functional requirements</li>
<li>Experience gathering and documenting business and functional requirements</li>
<li>Experience managing projects across development lifecycles (analysis through implementation)</li>
<li>Track record of successfully managing multiple projects of varying scope, size, and complexity</li>
<li>Familiar with software or systems development lifecycles</li>
<li>Experience with both Waterfall and Agile project methodologies</li>
<li>Ability to multi‐task and effectively prioritize competing requirements</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b><i>Degree/certification in either Business Analysis or Project Management is a plus</i></b></li>
<li>Self‐starter who can quickly acclimate to a fast‐paced environment</li>
<li>Multi‐task effectively between business analysis and project management activities</li>
</ul>
