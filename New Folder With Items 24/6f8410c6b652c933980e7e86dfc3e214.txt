<p>Responsibilities</p>
<p></p>
<p>What you will do on any given day:</p>
<br>
<p>
Like any high performing team member, a Business Development Representative is measured by effective outreach activity and qualification resulting in opportunity generation, so in partnership with our marketing and sales teams, you will be finding and engaging new prospects through inbound lead follow up, outbound prospecting and social selling.<br>
An average day will consist of research, numerous phone calls and email outreach to identify key stake holders and potential initiatives, as well as database management (data hygiene, data intelligence and append) for key accounts.<br>
You will help improve market intelligence and maximize opportunities for new products and services through extensive account research, database enhancement and strategic outreach.<br>
Gain deeper market and account penetration with major accounts by mapping potential opportunities (services, solutions, etc.)<br>
Effectively identify and communicate with C-level decision makers, demonstrate an understanding of their challenges, their unique market dynamics and the specific needs of various account influencers<br>
Uncover additional opportunities by working with our Account Executives and marketing teams to develop and execute targeted plans of action going beyond current contacts/installed products.<br>
