<h2>Digital Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About the Role</b></p>
<p>As Digital Marketing Manager, you will be responsible for the development of strategy and execution of CynergisTek’s digital marketing plan. You will also serve as a hands-on contributor in the support development of digital marketing campaigns and implementation, optimization, and management of digital marketing platforms and related tools. You will combine your knowledge of integrated marketing with your business acumen to bring greater brand awareness, increase lead generation, and increase customer engagement.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Ideal Candidate</b></p>
<p>Someone who has knowledge of design software such as Adobe Creative Suite. Serve as the primary administrator for the Marketo instance, oversee team’s Marketo activities, and own Marketo reporting with experience in B2B marketing in the tech and/or cybersecurity industry a plus.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, communication, or related field required</li>
<li>5+ years’ experience in a marketing role</li>
<li>In-depth experience with Marketo</li>
<li>Working knowledge of web design principles, best practices, and WordPress</li>
<li>Familiarity with Salesforce</li>
<li>Solid understanding of social media, PPC, SEO, and SEM</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to develop and execute integrated sales and digital marketing strategies</li>
<li>Familiarity with analytical tools such as Google Analytics, Crazy Egg, etc.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>About CynergisTek</b></p>
<p>CynergisTek is a top-ranked cybersecurity and information management consulting firm dedicated to serving the healthcare industry. CynergisTek offers specialized services and solutions to help organizations achieve privacy, security, compliance, and document output goals. Since 2004, the company has served as a partner to hundreds of healthcare organizations and is dedicated to supporting and educating the industry by contributing to relevant industry associations</p>
