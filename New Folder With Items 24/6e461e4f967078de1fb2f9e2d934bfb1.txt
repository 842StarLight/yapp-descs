<div><b>
The Role:</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Collective Medical is seeking a highly motivated, determined, results-oriented Hospital Sales Executive that will focus on health systems and medium and large independent hospitals representing Collective Medical’s hospital specific solutions. You will be helping these organizations with solutions to better manage their highest-risk, highest-cost and most vulnerable patient populations. We are seeking a candidate with hunger, a desire to grow, excellent communication skills and an interest in accelerating their career in healthcare IT sales. The Hospital Sales Executive will be responsible for direct sales to hospitals, promoting Collective Medical’s care collaboration network and applications. This role is a remote position; candidates must live in the Southeast.</div>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<div>
 <b>Who you’ll work with:</b>
</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
This position will report directly to the Regional Vice President of Hospital Sales and will work closely with our Inside Sales, Sales Development, and Marketing department.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
What you’ll be doing day to day:</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Developing hospital territory plan including mapping of hospitals and health systems, identification of key client prospects, and account penetration strategy</li>
<li>Identify and target prospective tart roles and titles that are key stakeholders in adopting and participating in the Collective Medical care collaboration network</li>
</ul>
<div>
- Actively call on accounts within your territory - qualifying account needs and priorities relative to Collective solutions.</div>
<ul>
<li>Drive accounts through the Collective sales process from qualification thru closed contracting</li>
<li>Deliver comprehensive, accurate and compelling sales presentations using all marketing and sales materials to key stakeholders involved in the buying process·</li>
<li>Complete all sales meeting preparation and follow-up in an effective and timely manner</li>
<li>Collaborate with other functions (i.e. Clinical, Success / Delivery, etc.) to develop and deliver the most compelling solution relative to customers identified needs</li>
<li>Demonstrate comprehensive knowledge of relevant healthcare trends, priorities, challenges and opportunities and how Collective Medical’s solutions align and support</li>
<li>Complete administrative and sales documentation responsibilities as required, including maintaining an update to CRM with accurate activity and opportunity depiction</li>
<li>Build a pipeline of qualified hospital opportunities through direct selling of Collective Medical’s high-value network and applications and advance them through your pipeline through closure</li>
<li>Achieve all sales activity goals and sales growth objectives for your defined territory</li>
</ul>
