JOB PURPOSE:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Responsible for the overall management of the mechanical engineering department, including both administrative and technical aspects. Serve on project teams as a lead engineer. Manage engineering and design teams, must possess strong leadership and communication skills.</div>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>PRINCIPAL RESPONSIBILITIES:</div>
<ul>
<li>On time design according to local norms / regulations and according to the contract specifications and budgetary limitations.</li>
<li>Resource planning and Management.</li>
<li>Reviews project Technical specifications and clarifies as needed all requirements with Sales, Project Manager and Estimating</li>
<li>Participate in Kick off meetings with Project, Sales and Estimating</li>
<li>Heads Design Review Meeting with Design Team and informs scope and schedule, assist with interpreting the project specification.
</li>
<li>Recruit, select, orient, and train; communicate job expectations; plan, monitor, appraise, and review employee performance. Enforce policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Contribute mechanical engineering information and recommendations; resolve problems; identify trends; determine administrative system improvements; implement process changes.</li>
<li>Design Change Management, manage and coordinates changes.</li>
<li>Support and Train new hires in mechanical design fundamentals, design standards, mechanical and structural calculations.</li>
<li>Supports other departments as needed such as Project Management, Offer Engineering, and Sales</li>
<li>Updates job knowledge by remaining current on codes and requirements; participating in educational opportunities; reading professional publications; maintaining personal networks; participating in professional organizations.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div>EDUCATION: Bachelor’s degree in Engineering is required.</div>
<p>EXPERIENCE: Minimum of 5 years of experience in mechanical, application or sales engineering in an industrial environment. Strong written and verbal communication skills. Must be a team player. Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines. Attention to detail is a must. Well-developed PC skills and experience in mechanical drawings using CAD software is required. Must have good knowledge of mechanical engineering discipline.</p>
