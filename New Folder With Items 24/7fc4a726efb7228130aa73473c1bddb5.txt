<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Program Manager, Store Systems serves as the product owner and Subject Matter Expert (SME) for point-of-sale and other store systems and owns applicable product roadmaps and releases. This role works with business stakeholders to ensure our systems’ overall alignment with corporate strategy, develops requirements to help achieve strategic goals, and works with application development teams to extend our systems as needed.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Works closely with business stakeholders to align product roadmaps with business strategy.</li>
<li>
Establishes, documents and prioritizes product requirements.</li>
<li>
Supports the product development effort by clarifying requirements and providing functional insight throughout the development process.</li>
<li>
Establishes, maintains and communicates the product roadmaps for our store systems, including the product release schedule, and works with other departments (training, legal, operations, etc.) to proactively mitigate any associated business impact of upcoming changes.</li>
<li>
Works with Application Development and Quality Assurance to establish test plans and product acceptance criteria.</li>
<li>
Ensures that Support Services is both aware of product changes, and capable of supporting these changes.</li>
<li>
Works with Training and Communications teams to ensure that training content and associated communications are created in a quality and timely manner.</li>
<li>
Provides additional triage as needed for L3 support issues that impact our business.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
