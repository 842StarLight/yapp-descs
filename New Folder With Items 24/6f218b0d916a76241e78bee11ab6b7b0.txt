<h2>QA Documentation & Data Spec</h2>

<p></p>
<div>
<p>Responsible for creating and managing the master data files. Responsible for making decisions regarding set-up changes, workflow assignments in the PLM system, and system design changes.
</p>
<p>Responsible for preparing, implementing and updating Wayne Farms, LLC and customer specifications, to include updating existing specifications and creating new specifications.
</p>
<p>Responsible for managing within the ERP/PLM system the master data and spec tasks for all new projects. Responsible for providing training to other functional teams on how to use the PLM/ERP system.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES AND TASKS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Set-up and maintenance of Adage and Optiva systems for master data and specs including: attaching items, item packs, products to warehouses, updating calendars, thru-puts, yields, specs set-up, etc.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with other Master Data personnel to complete new item set-ups and maintain Master Data systems (Adage and Optiva) (e.g. changes to existing items).
</li>
<li>Prepare and modify all aspects of customer and Wayne Farms LLC specifications, both fresh and further processing products.
</li>
<li>Update existing specifications and create new specifications (with customers or as part of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) or Optiva processes).
</li>
<li>Provide feedback to other PLM system stakeholders on most efficient/effective way to set-up new projects.
</li>
<li>Provide training to other PLM system users on how to use the system.
</li>
<li>Provide recommendations and support for PLM/ERP system improvements.
</li>
<li>Work with IT to maintain and improve the specification database/reporting system and master data systems.
</li>
<li>Interface with Regulatory, Sales, Research and Development (R&amp;D), Product Management, Quality Assurance (QA) and Operations on an on-going basis ensuring first-time accuracy on new specifications and master data set-ups.
</li>
<li>Act as primary contact for customers, plants, QA, R&amp;D, Sales, and Product Specialists for specification changes.
</li>
<li>Verify and ensure accuracy of all master data information in all company systems.
</li>
<li>Work with Purchasing, Regulatory, Sales, R&amp;D, Product Management and vendors to get all necessary information entered into specifications (i.e. ingredient line, formulation, nutritional and allergen information) and in the PLM system.
</li>
<li>Manage change requests process and deviation systems and database (i.e. creation, monitoring and resolution of spec change and deviation requests).
</li>
<li>Assist Regulatory Affairs, and other Corporate QA functions in a back-up role.
</li>
<li>Support SQF compliance and program requirements as needed.
</li>
<li>Support maintenance of QA SourceSync data for FSE Net.
</li>
<li>Complete customer questionnaires and requests for information.
</li>
<li>Champion alignment of customer specs, expectations and process capabilities, with Wayne Farms, LLC production specs.
</li>
<li>Review formula routing process to verify accuracy of data.
</li>
<li>Communicate to Adage/Optiva usersâ€™ set-up status.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>

</b></p>

</li>
<li>2+ years relevant experience in food processing industry (e.g. product development and plant scale up on food items, Master Data, Quality Assurance/Control, production/scheduling or Regulatory Affairs).
</li>
