<p>
Meadow Burke, a CRH company, is a premier manufacturer and distributor for the concrete construction industry. For more than seven decades, we have served architects, engineers, and contractors with a superior line of concrete reinforcing products, concrete forming accessories, road and bridge products, and industry leading products for precast and tilt-up construction.</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Meadow Burke is searching for an aggressive, results-oriented District Sales Manager to drive sales (NV, AZ, NM, CA). This is a home-based position reporting to the Central / Western Regional Sales Manager, in this critical role you will service and grow our sales with new and existing distributor and rebar fabricator customers. You will partner with engineers, contractors and Meadow Burke’s Product Managers to increase demand and specification of our concrete accessories products. In this fast-paced, growing organization those that are highly motivated and driven by results will have the opportunity to succeed and advance.</p>
<p></p>
<br>

Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
360° partner with demonstrated history of building and maintaining strong relationships with customers, vendors, and internal associates</li>
<li>
5+ years of high-performance sales experience in the construction industry (experience with rebar fabricators, distributors and concrete contractors preferred)</li>
<li>
Excellent communicator with the ability to coach and educate customers, dealers, or inside sales team on products</li>
<li>
Innovative spirit to work cross-functionally in developing organizational growth concepts and ideas</li>
<li>
Business or Engineering degree preferred</li>
<li>
Amicable, strategic, creative, confident, organized, and analytical</li>
