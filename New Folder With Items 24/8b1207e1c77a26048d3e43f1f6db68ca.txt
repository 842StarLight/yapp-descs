<p><b>Director of Employee Enablement NA Mid-Market Sales - Tempe</b></p>
<p><b>PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES
</b></p>
<p>The Organization:</p>
<p>The enablement team of the People Team of the Global Mid-Market Sales Organization owns and drives training, enablement and competency development programs and activities for specific target audiences, such as Mid-Market (General Business and Mid-Market) Sales, as well as virtual live classroom offerings. Key elements are definition, alignment and execution of a high-quality and high-impact enablement portfolio, jointly with global and regional stakeholders. This is an innovative organization that also serves as central point of contact for all aspects of training and enablement for the organization.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Role:</p>
<p>Core responsibility of Director of Enablement NA Mid-Market Sales role is the roll out, promotion and fine tuning of all globally lead Sales enablement programs, such as the black belt program, solution and industry programs and onboarding programs.</p>
<p>Additionally, to lead and drive the adoption of the Digital Sales Motion by the Mid-Market Sales Teams in the region and to understand and support the local enablement needs of the Mid-Market Sales reps.</p>
<p>This is done in close alignment with the regional Commercial Sales global leadership as well as the Mid-Market Sales team by leveraging and executing key training- and enablement programs, providing continuous learning to sustain skills and offering coaching to individuals and groups. In addition, the role will act in a supporting manner for opportunities in order to help drive deal execution and closure through coaching, mentoring and support.</p>
<p><b>EXPECTATIONS &amp; TASKS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Take on responsibility for adopting global strategic enablement programs and leading regional adoption within Mid-Market Sales and all the different MUs in close alignment with Mid-Market Sales Leadership.</li>
<li>Work with the Mid-Market Sales teams to develop and execute enablement programs to help drive pipeline and close deals on SAP Solutions.</li>
<li>Identify needs and gaps and prioritize key learning goals jointly with the business.</li>
<li>Define, build, execute and measure prioritized enablement programs and activities that drive adoption of DSM as well as productivity of the Mid-Market Sales teams.</li>
<li>Act as evangelist for DSM in the business, pro-actively establish and continuously facilitate network and build further ambassadors.</li>
<li>Define roadmaps and recommendations in order to provide guidance to target audience in terms of up-skilling and education.</li>
<li>Provide mentoring and coaching to individuals or groups in order to support their ongoing skill development as well as concrete deal execution.</li>
<li>Conduct frequent reporting of executed training activities with the clear goal to create real business impact and measure this according to defined business KPI’s and goals.</li>
<li>Facilitate and moderate on-site as well as virtual trainings.</li>
<li>Conduct review and quality assurance for enablement and training.</li>
<li>Drive innovation of learning concepts and foster a continuous learning culture.</li>
<li>Drive sharing of experiences and best-practices in the enablement community.</li>
<br>
</ul>
