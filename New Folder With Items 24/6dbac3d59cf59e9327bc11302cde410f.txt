<h2>Sales Business Analyst - Customer Data</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><p>General Mills is reshaping the future of food. We believe food makes us better. It nourishes our bodies, brings us joy and connects us to each other. As one of the world's leading food companies, General Mills operates in more than 100 countries and markets more than 100 consumer brands, including Cheerios, Nature Valley, Betty Crocker, Yoplait, Annie's Homegrown, Old El Paso, Epic Provisions, Blue Buffalo and more. Are you passionate about the future of food? You've come to the right table. We want the very best talent to help lead something big.
</p></div>
<div>
<p>Do you have a passion for data? Are you willing to get your hands dirty to ensure data is clean to help our team sell more effectively? Are you interesting in supporting and shaping cutting edge sales capabilities? If so, the Business Analyst - Customer Data role might be right for you!<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>As a member of the Convenience &amp; Foodservice (C&amp;F) Data Governance team you’ll be responsible for most aspects of the Customer Data Domain within the business unit. As we continue to evolve our data-driven capabilities, you will play a vital role in helping us accelerate access to additional customer data and build processes to harmonize, maintain and surface insights to key stakeholders to help Field Sales sell more efficiently.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES
</b></p>
<p><b>Data Source Acceleration
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with sales, vendors and customers to create pipeline for quality customer data sources that are fully vetted and ready for internal ingestion.
</li>
<li>“Prep” data sources by harmonizing with internal data and establishing an ongoing ingestion process<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Owns Customer Lifecycle
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Works with Sales to create new customers and ensure they are accurate, harmonized and accessible through all consuming tools and systems.
</li>
<li>Uses data quality reporting to identify and remediate data quality issues… no one like data duplicates!<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Salesforce Frontline
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve on the front line to support our field sales team as they learn and surface questions about our new Salesforce CRM solution.
</li>
<li>Help educate and research/resolve issues identified with the data within the tool<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualified Leads
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assists Analytics team in creating qualified sales leads using a variety of internal and external data sources.
</li>
<li>Help create targets to sell more!
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>
</ul>

</li>

</ul>
