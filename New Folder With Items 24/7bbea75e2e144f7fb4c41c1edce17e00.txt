<h2>Business Development Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Group Sales Department is seeking a professional and self-motivated Business Development Executive for a sales territory within Southern California. Operating from a Sales Office in Valencia, the role of this Development Executive will be to seek new business opportunities by identifying, soliciting and selling to new and existing accounts with an employee/membership base of 100 or more. The position will also be responsible for identifying and analyzing the territory’s sales trends and understanding those results in the context of the total department’s performance. In this position, the Development Executive will be a driving force for the company by boosting sales and contributing to its long-term business growth.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Set and execute on a cadence of phone, email, and social communications to build a pipeline of qualified opportunities within large enterprise organizations to achieve sales quotas.</li>
<li>
Manage the top for the sales funnel to ensure sales has a pipeline to support goals.</li>
<li>
Prospect and identify key executives at target companies.</li>
<li>
Promptly follow-up with all leads.</li>
<li>
Prioritize strategic target account lists within a defined territory.</li>
<li>
Research and build new and existing accounts.</li>
<li>
Run discovery calls with prospects to qualify opportunities.</li>
<li>
Maintain consistent levels of activity to reach assigned goals.</li>
<li>
Manage, track, and report sales activities and results using internal CRM.</li>
<li>
Report on trends and information discovered during the prospecting and qualification process.</li>
<li>
Be available to work flexible hours at nights and on weekends.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Ideal qualifications and experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A minimum of 2 years of sales experience</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of CRM cloud applications (hands on experience with Salesforce)</li>
<li>
Enterprise prospecting experience is a plus</li>
<li>
Excellent communications skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>
Strong working knowledge of Windows with main attention to Word, Excel, Power Point and Outlook</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to meet and exceed sales quotas</li>
<li>
Must be extremely organized and detail oriented</li>
<li>
Must be a self-starter and possess the ability to work with minimal daily direct supervision</li>
<li>
Must demonstrate outgoing personality and professional appearance</li>
</ul>
