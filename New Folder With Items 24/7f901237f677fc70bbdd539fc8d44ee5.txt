<h2>Property Manager</h2>

<p></p>
<p>We are searching for Property Manager professionals to join our team! We have an immediate need for an experienced Manager to join the team at Gardens of Annapolis, one of United Groupâ€™s professionally managed Senior communities.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Property Manager is responsible for maintaining the physical asset and maximizing the financial returns from that asset in accordance with the Owner's objectives. Responsible for all computer operations and upkeep of accounting books/records; supervising staff including maintenance team; ensures performance of duties on a timely basis; conducts all inspections of the physical asset; assists with special projects and administrative tasks as defined by the Regional Manager.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Professional designation, i.e. Certified Professional Manager (CPM), Accredited Residential Manager (ARM), or equivalent industry designation preferred with experience with YARDI, and a minimum of 3 years of property management experience.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:
</b></h5>
<p>The activities listed below are not all inclusive. However, they are indicative of activities normally performed by the Property Manager:
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Resident Management</i></b>
</p>
<ul><li>Oversees and implements rent collection programs
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manages resident complaint log
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervises and assists with move-ins and move-outs
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Personnel Management</i></b>
</p>
<ul><li>Ensures Company policies and procedures are followed
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervises on-site personnel and all activities relating to personnel management, i.e. scheduling, payroll, progressive discipline, etc.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manages employee training and development
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Hires and terminates employees in accordance with established guidelines
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conducts formal employee performance evaluations and recommendations for promotions and/or salary increases for all site personnel
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Marketing and Leasing</i></b>
</p>
<ul><li>Prepares advertising, marketing materials, and events
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepares regular market surveys
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepares weekly and monthly reports
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reviews all rental applications and lease forms for accuracy and compliance with resident policy
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Makes recommendations to improve marketing and leasing programs
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Maintaining Physical Asset</i></b>
</p>
<ul><li>Regularly performs inspections of maintenance work
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs regular inspections of common areas, community rooms, common hallways, stairwells, elevators, apartments, grounds, exterior of building, perimeter of property, etc.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervises all vacant apartment make-ready procedures; ensures productivity of staff through routine inspections, view of work in progress
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Makes recommendations for physical repairs, replacements, and/or improvements when approved
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Makes recommendations for supplies, materials, and equipment and orders when approved
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Evaluates maintenance operations periodically to determine cost efficiency
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Financial Reporting and Control</i></b>
</p>
<ul><li>Preparation of the property's annual budget - works directly with Regional Manager and Property Accountant
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitors budget performance and reports variances from budgets
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists in preparation of monthly financial accounting, reporting, and explanation of variances
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepares purchase orders, inputs all invoices into Yardi
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reports payroll information to the Corporate office / Human Resources on a timely basis
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervises inventory of all equipment and supplies
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Management of property's Petty Cash
</li></ul>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications:
</b></h5>
<ul><li>Bachelors degree, or related experience in Property Management, Sales, or Leasing
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum five (5) years managerial experience supervising three (3) or more subordinates
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong organizational, management, and teamwork skills
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Professional image compatible with United Group - positive attitude, energetic, assertive, and ability to serve as a role model for subordinates
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Solid administrative skills - knowledge of Microsoft Office products, i.e. Excel, Outlook, Word, PowerPoint, Publisher
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to handle finances and work within a budget; attention to detail
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrates integrity on personal and professional level
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Willingness to work respectfully with people of diverse backgrounds required
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to solve problems involving residents, personnel, finances, equipment failure, emergency situations, etc.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability and willingness to substitute for any position at the site<br>
</li></ul>

</li></ul>

</li></ul>
