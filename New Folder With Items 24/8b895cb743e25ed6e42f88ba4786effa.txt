<h2>Area Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Pennrose is an industry leader in the development and management of Affordable Housing. We are excited to be hiring for an Area Manager for our Baltimore, MD properties.
<br><br>
The Area Manager (AM) is a traveling position that is responsible for providing leadership when there is a vacant property manager position within the portfolio. The Area Manager reports to the Vice President of Operations directly, and assists the Regional Manager. This position is also involved in working on-site to assist the Vice President in completing special projects, such as recertifications during mass recertification periods, or helping properties with recertifications when they are behind schedule. While providing coverage for vacant property manager positions, the AM will oversee the overall operation of the property. They will provide leadership and assume the responsibilities of the Property Manager for each of the properties they are filling in at.
<br><br>
The AM will recruit, interview, train and mentor new Property Managers, Assistant Property Managers, and Leasing Consultants, in collaboration with the Training and Development team within their portfolio.
