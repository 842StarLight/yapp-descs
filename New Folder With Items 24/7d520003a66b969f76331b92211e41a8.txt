<h2>PT Store Clerk - Owensboro, ky</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary/Objective</b> The retail sales clerk is responsible for obtaining or receiving merchandise, totaling bills, accepting payments and making change for customers in retail store.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b> Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>Stock shelves, counters or tables with merchandise.</li>
<li>Set up advertising displays or arrange merchandise on counters or tables to promote sales.</li>
<li>Stamp, mark or tag prices on merchandise.</li>
<li>Obtain merchandise requested by customer or receive merchandise selected by customer.</li>
<li>Answer customer questions concerning location, price and use of merchandise.</li>
<li>Total price and tax on merchandise purchased by customer to determine bill.</li>
<li>Accept payment and make change. Wrap or bag merchandise for customers.</li>
<li>Remove and record amount of cash in register at end of shift.</li>
<li>Calculate sales discount to determine price.</li>
<li>Keep record of sales, prepare inventory of stock and order merchandise.</li>
<li>Keep store clean and orderly.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer/Client Focus.</li>
<li>Initiative.</li>
<li>Teamwork Orientation.</li>
<li>Communication Proficiency.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibility</b> This position has no supervisory responsibilities.</p>
<p><b>Work Environment</b> This job operates in a retail business environment. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, phones, photocopiers, filing cabinets and fax machines.</p>
<p><b>Physical Demands</b></p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to talk and hear. This position is very active and requires standing, walking, bending, kneeling, stooping, crouching, crawling and climbing all day. The employee must frequently lift and/or move items that weigh up to 50 pounds.</p>
<p><b>Position Type and Expected Hours of Work</b></p>
<p>This is a part-time position. Days and hours of work vary by schedule. Evening and weekend work may be required as schedule demands.</p>
<p><b>Travel</b></p>
<p>No travel is expected for this position.</p>
<p><b>Preferred Education and Experience (But not required)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or G.E.D.</li>
<li>Previous retail or customer service experience.</li>
</ul>
