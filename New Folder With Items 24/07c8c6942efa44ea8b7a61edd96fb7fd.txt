<div><b>Position Summary:</b></div>
<br>
</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div>Wayne Fueling Systems, a global leader in fuel dispensing, payment processing and point fuel management technology, is seeking a Sales Support Specialist and System Analyst. The Sales Support Specialist and System Analyst is responsible for performing a wide range of sales support and order management system functions in addition to testing and validating multiple levels in DoverCom; a new web-based sales order management system.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The sales support function includes guiding customers through an order management tool with some level of complexity. They update customer records and communicate details of orders, inquiries and deliveries to representatives and sales managers. They also keep sales team members informed of any problems or issues that customers might raise during an email or sales call, such as late deliveries or quality defects. This position will aid in supporting, identifying, developing and communicating sales account needs for the department, also supports company plans for growth through various channels in the retail fuel industry.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>System analyst’s duties include testing new model configurations, troubleshooting UI issues, reporting and logging system errors to an engineering team, visibility and prioritization to open tasks, and continual communications across cross-functional departments.
</div>
<div>This position reports to the Manager of Sales Support.</div>
<br>
</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div><b>Position Responsibilities:</b></div>
<br>
</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for providing initial response and subsequent follow ups to customer’s emails and phone calls</li>
<li>Primary support for customers placing orders and requesting changes through Wayne processes
</li>
<li>Work closely with Sales Managers and Customers with quotations, price exceptions, delivery methods, graphic requests, and quote conversions.</li>
<li>Guiding customers through product choices based on site requirements.</li>
<li>Responsible for implementing, tracking and documenting customer claim requests for missing, damaged or incorrectly shipped items
</li>
<li>Develop a thorough understanding of systemic ordering tools and product literature location and dissemination for order entry and customer response activities</li>
<li>Assist the sales team with minimal reporting requests to identify trends and volumes within various regions or channels</li>
<li>General communication with Sales managers for outstanding items requiring their attention or assistance</li>
<li>Communicate order details to Sales managers and customers from acknowledgement to delivery</li>
<li>Cross collaborate with other departments to meet or exceed customer expectations</li>
<li>Testing, logging and tracking new equipment model configurations in CPQ.</li>
<li>Troubleshooting UI issues with customers, both internal and external, and logging and tracking these activities.</li>
<li>Attend and affectively contribute to CPQ daily meetings in addition to delivering timely responses to actions item assignments.<br>
