<h2>Donor Services Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Donor Services Director leads the Donor Services team to support the development and fundraising efforts of the entire Development department. The Donor Services Director proactively leads and identifies opportunities to improve the processes related to data capture and management to ensure accurate content, gift processing and reporting, donor identification, research, cultivation and stewardship efforts. The Donor Services Director and the Donor Services team provide support to the Vice President of Philanthropy and Major Gift Officers for fundraising and donor engagement for successful achievement of revenue and engagement goals.</div>
</div></div></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you'll be doing
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b><i>Essential Job Functions:</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for complex reporting and analysis of donations and donor database activity to identify trends, monitor results and make data driven recommendations on the strategy / approach in order to meet the needs of the business</li>
<li>
Oversight of all data management, donor cultivation, donor support and ensuring excellence in stewardship.</li>
<li>
Responsible for setting / maintaining department budgets and partnering with finance to ensure that all gift revenues are properly recorded; provides year to date and year-end gifts reconciliation.</li>
<li>
Responsible for vendor management / relationship ensuring contractual obligations / service levels are maintained.</li>
<li>
Responsible for staying abreast of industry trends, relationship building across the non-profit and IDD industry and proactively identifying opportunities for enhancements and future growth.</li>
<li>
Responsible for developing annual stewardship strategy in partnership with key stakeholders as well as monitoring progress / making course corrections as needed based on performance.</li>
<li>
In collaboration with marketing and external consultants develops the overarching strategy for external communication campaigns and engagement strategies with measurable ROI.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Directs the maintenance and operation of the donor database, including oversight of database security, quality of processes, and proactively identifying areas to improve operational effectiveness</li>
<li>
Oversight of the donor research process ensuring quality / in-depth briefs are developed in advance of donor meetings.</li>
