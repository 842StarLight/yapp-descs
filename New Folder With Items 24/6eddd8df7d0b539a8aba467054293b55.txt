<h2>Technical Product Resolution Expert</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>A post-sales function responsible for ensuring ConMed offers "best-in-class" technical product support to both our internal business partners and external customers. As the Technical Product Support Export, you will have a high technical aptitude, combined with the passion and drive for taking care of our customers; love problem solving, thrive in a cross-functional working environment and have a strong desire to work with multiple stakeholders (e.g. Engineering, Sales, Training, Marketing, Operations, Quality, etc.).<b>Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Primary customer contact for very complex technical product support troubleshooting related issues, i.e. emergency situations (going into surgery) or already in surgery "patient on the table"</li>
<li>Responds to very complex customer technical product inquiries via telephone, text, email or chat sessions.</li>
<li>Resolves very complex customer technical product concerns raised during installation, operation, maintenance or product application or compatibility matters.</li>
<li>Troubleshoots very complex technical problems with malfunctioning electro/mechanical equipment or software applications and recommends corrective action.</li>
<li>Leverage excellent communication skills to diagnose very complex technical problems and instruct end-users through appropriate maintenance or troubleshooting procedures.</li>
<li>Utilize CRM to record ALL customer interactions, technical support issue reported, technical resolution recommended to provide 3600 visibility pertaining to ALL customer communications; in addition to removing the need for the customer to restate information/troubleshooting steps attempted should they call back or speak to another specialist.</li>
<li>Submits service requests resulting from support telephone calls, email, text or chat sessions (as needed).</li>
<li>Files internal &amp; external customer complaints arising from troubleshooting support provided via telephone, text, email or chat sessions are filed within 24 hour or less - i.e. malfunctioning, defective, out of box failures or recurring product issues (as needed).</li>
<li>Documents customer information and recurring technical issues to support product quality programs and product development.</li>
<li>Establishes, updates &amp; maintains systems that provide answers ranging from common to very complex troubleshooting questions and problems.</li>
<li>Effectively represent customer position and requirements for developing support related offerings and/or initiatives</li>
<li>Develop and maintain expert level knowledge of ConMed assigned product portfolio.</li>
<li>Attends assigned product portfolio procedures at customer locations to maintain real world troubleshooting product knowledge and understanding of customer product needs.</li>
<li>Develop, maintain &amp; update training curriculum necessary for onboarding new TPS hires and to update/maintain TPS team product troubleshooting knowledge as new products are upgraded, acquired or released.</li>
<li>Train and onboard new technical product support hires, ensure specialists are properly trained when new products are upgraded/improved, added or released.</li>
<li>Serve as an internal resource to assist sales with new product launch training (as needed).</li>
<li>Serve as an internal resource to assist sales representatives and partner with other functions when necessary.</li>
<li>Must be available to provide occasional, on-call support to cover nights and weekends on a rotating basis.</li>
<li>Other duties as directed by the Technical Product Support Supervisor or Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Enjoy daily contact with our internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Progressively successful customer service track record.</li>
<li>Ability to work both independently and in a collaborative team environment</li>
<li>Ability to balance multiple projects and see them through to completion.</li>
<li>Ability to balance and prioritize competing demands and create practical solutions to business problems</li>
<li>Comfortable in front of higher-level leadership and able to easily develop and leverage key business relationships</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in related discipline or minimum of 8+ years of related experience OR 6 years and a Master's degree OR a PhD with 3 year OR equivalent experience</li>
<li>Advanced level proficiency in the use of MS Office and ERP/CRM systems</li>
<li>Must possess a strong customer focus, expert technical product knowledge critical to responding to daily customer-centric troubleshooting activities.</li>
<li>Detail oriented with excellent interpersonal skills, including excellent verbal/written communication skills and the ability to effectively interface with all departments.</li>
</ul>
