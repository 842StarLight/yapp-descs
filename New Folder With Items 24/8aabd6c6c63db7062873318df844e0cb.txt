<h2>Recruiter (Contract-to-Hire)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>JAGGAER</b> is the world's leading provider of comprehensive source-to-pay solutions. Some of the largest Commercial, Manufacturing and Life Sciences companies in the world trust JAGGAER with billions of dollars of annual spend. Additionally, JAGGAER is the leading procure-to-pay provider in the Higher Education and Government sectors. JAGGAER eProcurement and strategic sourcing customers across the globe have gained access to the best suppliers, with the best terms, on our scalable, customizable, user-friendly platform. Our SaaS-based, source-to-settle solution provides unparalleled visibility, insights and recommendations to procurement leaders and suppliers. The result is a fluid supply chain driven by powerful spend analysis, comprehensive contract management and efficient accounts payable solutions.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The<b> Recruiter</b> is responsible for the day-to-day, full-cycle recruiting operations for the Human Resources team. The Recruiter carries out responsibilities in all areas of recruiting including: sourcing, networking, working directly with hiring managers, extending offers and coordinating the onboarding process. The work requires dealing professionally with highly confidential information and providing strong customer service to internal and external customers.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Principal Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for recruiting strategy to ensure the hiring of quality employees in a cost effective and timely manner.</li>
<li>
Source, screen, interview, recommend, and hire internal and external candidates for open positions.</li>
<li>
Evaluate candidates' strengths compared with position requirements. Conduct initial phone screens or in-person interviews as applicable. Coordinate interviewing schedules and feedback sessions with hiring managers. Negotiate offers and gain commitment from candidates for current and future job requirements.</li>
<li>
Build and drive effective relationships through solid communication and focused attention to brand and culture.</li>
<li>
Continuously improve processes.</li>
<li>
Ensure timely and accurate entering and processing of relative data in Human Resource Information System including processing new hires and terms.</li>
<li>
Provide routine and ad hoc reports via business objectives in support of compensation planning, compliance, staffing and other processes.</li>
<li>
Collect, compile and analyze data and prepare a variety of statistical and other reports for the department; analyze comparative data related to salaries and benefits, make recommendations regarding proper classification of positions.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Requirements
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Superior verbal and written communication skills at all levels, including interpersonal skills, phone skills, documentation and interviewing skills.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 3 years of recruiting and sourcing experience across multiple disciplines including technology and sales.</li>
<li>
Experience training managers on hiring processes and associated best practices, including interviewing guidelines.</li>
<li>
Must have experience utilizing non-traditional and traditional sourcing and talent identification means.</li>
<li>
Familiar with employment law and all legal facets of hiring policies and practices.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and to change course when needed.</li>
<li>
Excellent ability to manage multiple priorities and a high volume of open positions; ability to communicate accordingly when business priorities shift.</li>
<li>
Strong drive for results and ability to get tasks/projects completed on time</li>
<li>
Experience with applicant tracking systems, such as iCIMS preferred</li>
<li>
Experience recruiting for Affirmative Action employers subject to OFCCP compliance</li>
</ul>
