<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
</div>
<div>
<p>VBS offers custom consulting services, training, seminars, publications, and other related products to our clients. Our clients also enjoy unlimited access, through phone or email, to our in-house human resources experts. Our team of experts answers phone and email inquiries regarding a wide range of HR-related topics, questions, and issues.<br>
<br>
As our newest <b>Senior HR Consultant</b>, your mission is to assist members over the phone (and via email) with HR-related issues and requests, making sure to communicate the information in an easy-to-understand and accessible way, allowing them to apply the laws and regulations to their respective situations.</p>
</div>
