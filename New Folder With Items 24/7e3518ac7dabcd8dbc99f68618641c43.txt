<h2>Administrative Assistant (Part-Time or Full-Time, Entry Level)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<p>Do you want to be part of a fast-growing and exciting technology company? Accelerated Information Systems is an award-winning provider of world-class enterprise content management solutions. Our mission is to solve business challenges through the use of technology, expert consulting, and customized software solutions.</p>
<p>
We are seeking an Administrative Assistant to work with our rapidly expanding software company. Our ideal candidate is someone with exceptional organization skills, great energy, a positive attitude, and a passion for technology. This position will include customer outreach, scheduling meetings for our executives, assisting in document and proposal preparation, and administrative tasks around the office, such as greeting guests, and accepting and distributing letters and packages. We are looking for a bright, eager individual who shows potential to grow within our organization. This position has flexible hours between 9am and 5pm.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Answer phones and make calls to customers as directed</li>
<li>Collect mail and packages</li>
<li>Greet and direct office visitors</li>
<li>Coordinate with property management personnel as required</li>
<li>Manage scheduling and coordinating use of conference room</li>
<li>Maintain inventory and reorder office supplies when necessary</li>
<li>Prepare coffee in the morning and light maintenance tasks</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent organization and communication skills</li>
<li>Professional phone etiquette</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Outlook, Word, and Excel</li>
<li>Experience with outbound calling (sales, fundraising, marketing) a plus</li>
<li>Experience with Quickbooks Online a plus</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Must be authorized to work in the US</li>
</ul>
<p>Accelerated Information Systems has a comfortable, business casual office with a friendly atmosphere. We work to maintain the best possible environment for our employees where people can learn and grow with the company. We strive to provide a collaborative, creative environment where each person feels encouraged to contribute to our processes, decisions, planning and culture. The core business hours are 9 AM to 5 PM. We are located in central Long Island directly adjacent to the Hicksville LIRR station.</p>
<p>
Compensation: Hourly, commensurate with experience.</p>
