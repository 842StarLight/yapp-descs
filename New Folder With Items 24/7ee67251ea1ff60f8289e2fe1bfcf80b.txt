<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Google is proud to provide services to billions of Internet users around the world. While currently there are over 3 billion Internet users globally, more than 4 billion people are still not connected to the Internet and have not experienced the wonderful things that the Internet can bring to their lives. Ensuring quality internet access is foundational to serving the Next Billion Users (NBU), and Google Station is our platform for increasing access via high-quality, free public WiFi. Launched in 2016, Google Station is currently live in seven countries (India, Mexico, Thailand, Nigeria, Indonesia, Brazil, Philippines), with ambitions to scale rapidly to bring high-quality WiFi access to more people. NBU Monetization team is searching for a Solutions Consultant to help build the next generation of Monetization products to support Google Station. You will work closely with cross-functional teams such to architect technical solutions and run/manage campaigns.</p>
