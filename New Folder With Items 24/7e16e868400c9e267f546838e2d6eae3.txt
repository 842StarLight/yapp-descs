<h2>Chief Marketing Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Chief Marketing Officer - CMO
<br>
Mobile Social App
<br>
<br>
Vrenetic is expanding the ways people perceive the world through immersive social streaming video experiences. Founded and started by Roland Emmerich and Marco Weber in early 2017, we have launched our flagship social application, Vresh, and are now taking things to the next level. We are a rapidly growing and well-funded start up that is showing the world social media in a new space.
<br>
<br>
As Chief Marketing Officer, you are a proven marketing leader in all aspects of digital marketing including Growth (paid and organic), Brand Awareness, Publicity, Engagement and Retention. We are seeking an exceptional team builder and executive to hold this high-profile position, reporting directly to the CEO. The Chief Marketing Officer will oversee global marketing efforts and bring results across all marketing channels of the organization. As a key executive within the company, you will manage teams in U.S., Europe, and Asia and interface closely with other stakeholders of Marketing, Sales, Product, Content, and Analytics to ensure integrated success. You should have an exceptional command of brand campaigns, paid social advertising, social media marketing, influencer marketing, PR, audience targeting, organic growth methods, and revenue generation. Ability to analyze and use metrics to create data-driven marketing strategies is also core to this role.
<br>
<br>
We would like you to be someone who has either worked for a startup technology company, or understands the time frame and fluidity of situations at a startup to be able to thrive on the energy and flow.
<br>
<br>
You must have a passion for the work you do and for building team success.
