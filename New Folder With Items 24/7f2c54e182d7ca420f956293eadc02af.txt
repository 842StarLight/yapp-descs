<h2>Director, Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Director, Marketing</p>
<p>
Manlius, NY</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
As the Director of Marketing, you will be responsible for providing vision and business leadership in support of Stickley's strategy, enabling our customers across multiple channels and showcasing a thriving brand. As a hands on leader you will plan and direct all activities of a diverse team across advertising, communications, digital and creative.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Develop an omni-channel marketing approach, allowing customers to engage Stickley regardless of technical platform or physical venue, while supporting organizational strategy and objectives</li>
<li>
Develop and implement digital, social media and public relations plans which enhances brand recognition and maximizes connections to key stakeholders</li>
<li>
Lead research activities which frame the approach for maximizing sales, growth, product development and customer service</li>
<li>
Direct efforts and outcomes of marketing agency, ensuring targets and outcomes are met</li>
<li>
Direct implementation of marketing and advertising policies and practices</li>
<li>
Lead all marketing communications in coordination with sales and retail leadership while meeting all budget requirements</li>
<li>
Provide leadership and support for the design, development and implementation of all marketing and advertising products</li>
<li>
Build, develop and lead a team capable of carrying out the necessary marketing strategies and tactics</li>
<li>
Deliver marketing metrics which track current performance and impact future planning and actions</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS AND QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's and/or Master's degree in Marketing, Business Management or related field</li>
<li>
10+ years of progressive leadership experience in marketing/advertising and related functions, preferably in home furnishing or related industry</li>
<li>
Ability to plan and manage at both strategic and operational levels</li>
<li>
Track record of developing and implementing results-oriented, financially responsible marketing plans</li>
<li>
Ability to work collaboratively with colleagues and staff to create a results-driven, team-oriented environment</li>
<li>
Excellent time-management, project management and organizational skills</li>
<li>
An inspiring and motivating leader capable of demonstrating strong communication with excellent written, presentation, oral communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Possess excellent judgment and be able to work in a changing environment</li>
</ul>

BENEFITS:</b></p>

<li>
Paid vacation</li>
<li>
Group health, prescription drug, dental insurance coverage</li>
<li>
Company paid life insurance</li>
<li>
401k retirement plan</li>
<li>
Flexible spending account</li>
<li>
Generous employee discount</li>
</ul>
