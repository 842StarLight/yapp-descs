<h2>Engineering Group Leader (Civil/Structural Engineering)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p><b>Summary Of Position</b>:</p>
<p>
The Engineering Group Leader is accountable for technical planning, forming projects, managing multiple projects and may manage engineering teams consisting of Technicians and Senior Technicians. He/she provides strong leadership, technical direction and ensures that team members are continually trained in Doka formwork systems. Ensures that the team represents the proper use of Doka concrete forming equipment for customers. He/she provides technical assistance to the sales department from the project design stage to project inception and completion. This position will support Doka's Midwest Regional Engineering Support Team located in Channahon, Illinois.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Determines and plans technical solutions for all types of forming projects.</li>
<li>
Partners with customers, contractors, sales and operations departments while managing jobs from preliminary project planning stages to project completion.</li>
<li>
Supports Sales by determining appropriate formwork solutions that meet the customerâ€™s needs (economically, and in compliance with safety and regulatory guidelines).</li>
<li>
Works with sales to produce AutoCAD drawings during the proposal stage and finalizes working drawings for Doka forming systems and provides material lists for quotations and shipments to the site.</li>
<li>
Relied upon to provide guidance to customers regarding forming solutions, service job sites and respond to questions concerning equipment usage, etc.</li>
<li>
Motivates team and effectively delegates work insuring team/branch/company objectives are met.</li>
<li>
Produces and ensures that the drawings indicate the proper use of Doka forming equipment and that the drawings represent safe, economical and efficient designs.</li>
<li>
Provides analysis and design/price calculations as required (based upon level of experience and education).</li>
<li>
Ensures that the policies, procedures, programs, standards of performance and compliance are adhered to.</li>
<li>
Monitors conformance with contractual guidelines.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Other Responsibilities/Peripheral Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Visits job sites as required, identifies any improper implementation and takes action to ensure technical solutions are immediately implemented.</li>
<li>
Service the job site for the correct use of formwork systems in the absence of a service advisor.</li>
<li>
Ensures safe working practices and a safe working environment.</li>
<li>
Other duties/projects as assigned, such as participating in company committees and teams.</li>
</ul>
