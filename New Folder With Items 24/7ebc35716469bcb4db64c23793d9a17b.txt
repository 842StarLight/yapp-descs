<p>
Dell provides the technology that transforms the way we all work and live. But we are more than a technology company — we are a people company. We inspire, challenge and respect every one of our over 100,000 employees. We also provide them with unparalleled growth and development opportunities. We can’t wait for you to discover this for yourself as a <b>Senior Advisor Project/Program Management in Round Rock, TX.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
From developing brand new accounts to driving expansion across existing accounts, our Direct Sales team transforms opportunity into action every day. Whether focusing on a geographical area, product line, industry, channel or market segment, their goal is the same: to develop relationships with the end-users of the organization and generate meaningful sales. They are the direct line between our ground-breaking portfolio of product solutions and the desks of satisfied customers.<br>
Not even ground-breaking products sell themselves. That’s the job of our inside sales professionals. They take to market a product portfolio that includes PCs, electronics, accessories, mobility products, enterprise solutions, services and packaged software. Inside Sales Management is all about managing, inspiring and motivating professionals at all levels to deliver against targets. Beyond day-to-day people management, our team also shapes, executes and communicates specific sales strategies.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
Manages projects, typically from initiation through delivery. Coordinates cross-functional teams through meetings and progress measurement activity, which bring distinct, specific projects to completion on time and within budget.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Understanding strategic priorities across our 10 Sales enablement Priorities Pillars.</li>
<li>
Align with business teams to support their effort specific to program/project life-cycles and Value realization.</li>
<li>
Identifying opportunity areas and best practices processes, tools, and related controls.</li>
<li>
Developing business cases, ROI analytics, impact analysis and prioritization requirements for departmental initiatives translating opportunities to process steps, organizational design, and tool requirements.</li>
<li>
Coordinating across organizations to determine impacts from implementation of future state solutions.</li>
<li>
Develop enablement roadmap, communications, governance) plans for programs that will impact our sales organizations.</li>
<li>
Partnering with various organizations to ensure alignment.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong and demonstrable program management/process design and delivery in an Operational environment.</li>
<li>
Strategic thinking with focus on finding and developing unique opportunities.</li>
<li>
Proven ability in complex program design and implementation; ability to prioritize effectively.</li>
<li>
Proven ability in stakeholder management with excellent influencing skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent data management and analysis skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent PowerPoint development and presentation skills.</li>
</ul>

Education and Experience</b></p>
