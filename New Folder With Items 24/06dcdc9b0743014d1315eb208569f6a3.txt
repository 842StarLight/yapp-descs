<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Marketing Associate</b></h3>
<br>
<b>Job Summary</b><br>
<br>
The Marketing Associate’s primary responsibilities are the development of creative internal and external marketing materials for print and web; development of multimedia campaigns to engage dealers as well as consumers; building brand awareness across the board while adhering to consistent brand identity. This position will work closely with management and sales staff to execute successful B2B and B2C product marketing, while constantly evolving and learning to stay abreast of the latest industry trends.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities &amp; Duties</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Conceptualize and design marketing materials for multiple corporate divisions (i.e. print and digital ads, newsletter content, website graphics, tradeshow materials, national publication ads, etc.)</li>
<li>Assist management and sales team to promote product.</li>
<li>Lead product photography/miscellaneous photography shoots.</li>
<li>Manage digital assets.</li>
<li>Manage social media accounts (i.e. Instagram, Facebook, Twitter).</li>
<li>Create eNewsletter campaigns.</li>
<li>Understand and execute website administration fundamentals within a CMS.</li>
<li>Collaborate with vendor marketing teams to ensure quality product marketing.</li>
<li>Collaborate with external printers, advertising reps, etc. to execute projects as assigned.</li>
<li>Stay current on the latest software, design trends, and industry best practices.</li>
</ul>
<br>

<li>College degree in Visual Communications, Graphic Design, Marketing, or related field.</li>
<li>2 years of professional experience preferred.</li>
<li>Proficient in Adobe Creative Cloud Suite.</li>
<li>Proficient in Mac OS or PC.</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office Suite.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of design principles (must submit recent portfolio with application).</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Additional Skills &amp; Abilities</b>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Warehouse Associate</b></h3>
<div>
<b>Job Summary</b><br>
<br>
MGE Wholesale is looking to add to our warehouse team! If you thrive in a fast-paced environment and desire a career with an organization that provides a stable and supportive environment for its associates, then MGE Wholesale may be the place for you!<br>
<br>
<b>Summary/Objective</b><br>
The Warehouse Associate will perform an array of functions that may include receiving and processing incoming stock and materials, picking and filling orders from stock, packing and shipping orders, and managing, organizing and retrieving stock in the warehouse. If you have familiarity and experience with warehousing, shipping and receiving, coupled with a commitment to great customer service, apply today!<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions</b>
<ul>
<li>Prepare shipments by processing order requests; pulling materials; packing boxes; placing orders in the shipping area</li>
<li>Sort and place materials or items on racks or in bins according to organizational standards</li>
<li>Load and unload packages onto or from delivery vehicles; lift, carry, push or pull packages</li>
<li>Maintain inventory controls by recording amounts of materials or items received or distributed through a computer. May also assist in counting of physical inventory</li>
<li>Prepare parcels for shipment according to delivery instructions; utilizing most cost effective methods</li>
<li>Maintain safe and clean work environment by organizing warehouse; keeping shelves, pallet area, and work stations neat</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Requirements</b>
<ul>
<li>Ability to understand and follow instructions regarding work duties and safety methods</li>
<li>Ability to discern numbers and information in order to sort and ship packages correctly</li>
<li>General computer skills for basic data entry</li>
<li>Forklift Operator experience preferred or ability to become certified</li>
<li>Ability to use basic tools and equipment such as pallet jack, forklift, dock carts, hand-held scanner, shrink wrap rollers, scales and conveyors</li>
<li>Ability to lift/move heavy objects</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Perform other duties as required</li>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Benefits</b>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Inside Sales Representative</b></h3>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
At MGE Wholesale we value our customers; we work hard to earn our reputation for quality and integrity every day. Our Wholesale Sales Team seeks committed, service-oriented individuals who are passionate about educating and providing our customers with the products we sell. We offer excellent opportunities for professional growth and development.</p>
<p>
Join our Wholesale Sales Team where you will find supportive leadership, a committed staff and an engaging work culture.</p>
<p>
The role of Inside Wholesale Salesperson contributes to the success of MGE Wholesale by maintaining a working knowledge of firearms, cutlery and accessories and using this knowledge to sell products to our customers. The Inside Wholesale Salesperson will be responsible for achieving inside sales revenue goals by obtaining and up-selling orders, creating customer satisfaction, and adding value to the customer’s buying experience.</p>
<br>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Obtain orders by answering phone calls, verifying and entering items, shepherding orders to fulfillment, explaining stock-outs and expected delivery dates.</li>
<li>Increase orders by suggesting related items, explaining features, and checking customer’s buying history.</li>
<li>Build own customer list and open customer accounts by obtaining customer agreement and collecting customer information via callouts, cold calls and internet.</li>
<li>Keep up-to-date with new products.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for continuous improvement of processes.</li>
<li>Prepare, generate and follow up on verbal or written quotations to secure orders or determine reason for loss of order.</li>
<li>Review open customer order reports and takes action on open items, specifically items that may be at risk in meeting the customer’s promised delivery date.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as required.</li>
</ul> <b>Qualifications and Skills</b>
<ul>
<li>Previous sales and customer service experience preferred.</li>
<li>Effective communication skills.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of firearms.</li>
<li>Willingness to work in a team environment and stay objective and fair when dealing with sensitive situations.</li>
</ul> <b>Benefits</b>
<ul>
<li>Paid Time Off (Vacation/Sick).</li>
<li>Paid Holidays.</li>
<li>401K With Employer Match.</li>
<li>Choice of Medical Plans (Including Health Savings Account).</li>
<li>Vision &amp; Life Insurance.</li>
<li>Clean, Climate Controlled Environment.</li>
<li>Competitive bonus / incentive program</li>
<li>Generous gear and merchandise discount</li>
</ul> </div>
