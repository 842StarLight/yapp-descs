<h2>LOT ATTENDANT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The Lot Attendant is responsible for the proper maintenance of the physical lot and building and all vehicle inventory.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Make certain all inventory vehicles are clean and presentable to the public.<br>
</li>
<li>Inform management of any issues pertaining to the physical nature of any vehicle that might prohibit its sale.<br>
</li>
<li>Confirm that dealership plate frames are affixed to the rear of all inventory vehicles.<br>
</li>
<li>Responsible for making certain dealership advertising plates are affixed to the front of all inventory vehicles.<br>
</li>
<li>Responsible for making certain that all wholesale vehicles are in the designated areas in the additional inventory lot.<br>
</li>
<li>Confirm that all used vehicles on payoff are in the specified area in the additional inventory lot.<br>
</li>
<li>Make certain that all vehicles awaiting title are in the specified area and clearly marked "waiting for title".<br>
</li>
<li>Confirm that all other vehicles are displayed in the appropriate areas: vehicles waiting for "works check",<br>
</li>
<li>vehicles waiting to be cleaned, vehicles for stock.<br>
</li>
<li>Responsible for making certain the additional lot is maintained and organized according to Ira sales management specifications.<br>
</li>
<li>Immediately replace the sold vehicles or empty spaces with fresh inventory.<br>
</li>
<li>Confirm that if open spaces are being filled that the most marketable inventory is being used first.<br>
</li>
<li>Make certain all new vehicles have factory stickers displayed and addendum stickers if applicable.<br>
</li>
<li>Confirm that all used vehicles in inventory are displayed with Limited Warranty information, Buyer's Guide information and a vehicle selling price.<br>
</li>
<li>Proper monitoring and maintenance of all keys, both new and used, is a function of this position.<br>
</li>
<li>Responsible for the proper check in of all new vehicle inventory from the manufacturer.<br>
</li>
<li>Note any and all damage to any new vehicle being delivered by carrier according to the guidelines and specifications as related by Ira and manufacturer.<br>
</li>
<li>Services relative to interior maintenance and/or "odd jobs" may be required on occasion.<br>
</li>
<li>Responsible for the proper check in of all used vehicle inventory from any given automobile auction, or any other source.<br>
</li>
<li>Make certain that the vehicle lot is properly maintained and cleaned at all times.<br>
</li>
<li>Follows federal, state and local law as well as company policy about safeguarding all information.<br>
</li>
<li>Reports on time to work and follows schedule that is given. Calls supervisor if he/she cannot be at work on time.<br>
</li>
<li>Keeps current with annual HR training along with any other training that might be required for this position.<br>
</li>
<li>Follows all company policies and procedures. Informs supervisor of any illegal activity.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
