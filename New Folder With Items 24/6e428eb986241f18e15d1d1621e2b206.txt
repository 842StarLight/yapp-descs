<h2>Applications Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>JOB SUMMARY:</p>
<ul><li>The Applications Engineer will review and analyze RFQs to determine the most efficient and cost effective method of manufacture. They will responsible for determining material costs, processes, delivery times, etc. They will also be responsible for communicating directly with customers regarding quotes. They must be able to model parts using 3D CAD and develop CAM programs to determine the most efficient method of manufacture.</li></ul>
<p>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze and interpret complex blueprints, CAD models, specifications, etc.</li>
<li>Evaluate design, process and quality requirements to determine most economical manufacturing methods required to produce a component</li>
<li>Communicate directly with customers to discuss quote details to ensure quote clarity and accuracy</li>
<li>Consult with vendors and subcontractors to obtain necessary information to provide accurate cost estimates</li>
<li>Calculate costs based on materials, labor, shop rates, etc.</li>
<li>Work with engineering to solve DFM concerns</li>
<li>Model parts using 3D CAD</li>
<li>Identify and suggest cost savings ideas</li>
<li>Other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 year engineering degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years of experience in CNC machining</li>
<li>CNC machining cost estimation, quote development and/or sales</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Strong math skills (shop math, trigonometry, etc.)</li>
<li>Experience with 3D CAD/CAM software (e.g. Autodesk Inventor, Fusion, Solidworks, Mastercam, Esprit, etc.)</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of GD&amp;T</li>
<li>Machinist experience preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of metal cutting tooling (e.g. inserts, toolbars, drills, end mills, taps, etc.)</li>
<li>Knowledge of appropriate speeds and feeds for various materials (e.g. steels, stainless steels, sintered metals, titanium, nickel alloys, etc.)</li>
<li>Knowledge of 3-axis CNC milling, 5-axis is a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge of CNC turning, Y-axis and live tooling is a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge of automated manufacturing (e.g. robotic cells, gantry loaded lathes, etc.)</li>
<li>Local candidates preferred</li>
</ul>

<li>Medical Benefits</li>

<li>Paid Vacation</li>
<li>Paid Holidays</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
