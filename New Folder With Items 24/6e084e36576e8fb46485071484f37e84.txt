<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>As a Sales Development Representative (SDR) you’re the first point of contact for prospects and a key contributor to the development of the sales pipeline. You set the tone for our clients’ experience and nurture those relationships until they’re qualified leads.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What You'll Do</b></h3>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop new opportunities via inbound chat, phone, and email. This is not a cold calling role.</li>
<li>
Qualify prospects based on how well SnapEngage can meet their needs.</li>
<li>
Keep things moving forward by scheduling meetings between prospects and solution consultants.</li>
<li>
Maintain an organized pipeline and document prospect interactions in our CRM.</li>
<li>
Conduct weekly forecasting with the team on expected Sales Qualified Leads.</li>
<li>
Develop and leverage consultative selling skills to help first understand and then meet client needs.</li>
<li>
Track your progress and work towards goals, such as conversion numbers, etc.</li>
<li>
Shape and evolve your messaging and pitch based on team input and client feedback.</li>
<li>
Contribute to the ultimate success of the team by constantly searching for opportunities to improve.</li>
<li>
Research prospects and identify stakeholders in order to generate interest and establish rapport.</li>
<li>
Be a force for good and positively contribute to the culture.</li>
<li>
Take on other tasks as needed.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About You</b></h3>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
At least one year of experience in sales-oriented role.</li>
<li>
An eagerness to learn and develop a career in Software as a Service (SaaS) sales.</li>
<li>
Quick to adapt to new technologies and processes.</li>
<li>
Superior active listening skills and a sincere desire to understand and meet prospective clients’ needs.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills and the ability to clearly explain technical issues and propose solutions.</li>
<li>
Ability to organize leads and effectively multitask.</li>
<li>
Adaptability and a readiness to flex with a growing, changing business.</li>
</ul>
