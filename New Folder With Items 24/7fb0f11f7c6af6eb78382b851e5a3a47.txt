<h2>Business Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>We are seeking highly motivated and outgoing individuals to join our Business Development Team, to help continue our business success, expand our market share, credibility and customer diversity, serve as a valuable source of information for customers and the public and service current customer accounts. As a Business Developer with Tri State Restorations, you will contribute valuable sales and marketing information and strategic recommendations to achieve operational objectives.</p>
<p><b>Company Overview: </b></p>
<p>Tri State Restorations specializes in the restoration of commercial buildings following a property loss. Our service suite is designed to quickly restore business to companies impacted by disaster, minimize recovery time, mitigate loss and bring peace of mind. We know that a disaster can strike at any time; we are here to help 24 hours a day, seven days a week. We ease our clients through their traumatic time by providing them with outstanding customer service and personal attention. Our commitment to this priority has bolstered our reputation as a company that is compassionate, reliable and thorough.</p>
<p>For more information about who we are and what we do, please visit our website at tristaterestores.com</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>As a Business Developer with Tri State Restorations, you will help develop, implement and evaluate our commercial service program. You will also help us develop outside sales action plans to grow our market share and success. We are headquartered in Clarksburg, MD, but 95% of work will take place within set territory: Fairfax / Loudoun (VA), Montgomery / Frederick (MD) or Howard / Baltimore (MD)</p>
<p>Additional tasks of the Business Development position include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Working with current and potential customers in set territory</li>
<li>Developing and maintaining new relationships with commercial properties and key decision-makers</li>
<li>Assisting in the implementation of production, productivity, quality and customer service standards</li>
<li>Solving problems, identifying trends and assisting in determining new inroads for growth</li>
<li>Working with the company executive and staff to determine annual and gross profit plans by assisting in the forecasting and development of annual quotas for specific areas</li>
<li>Projecting expected sales volume and profit for existing and new opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional responsibilities of the Business Development position include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Assisting in new marketing opportunities by identifying consumer requirements.</li>
<li>Participate in regular conference calls and report weekly on sales metrics</li>
<li>Forecasting projected business.</li>
<li>Establishing targeted market share.</li>
<li>Staying very involved in the community through participation in specific trade associations.</li>
<li>Acting on revenue goals and contributing to new ones.</li>
<li>Taking the lead on setting up and creating relevant programs, classes, and events for our clients.</li>
<li>Assist in staff rotating on-call responsibilities</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<p>An ideal candidate for Business Developer with Tri State will have exceptional verbal, written and interpersonal communication skills, creativity and an entrepreneurial mindset. You must be able to develop strong customer relationships and close sales effectively. You must also be looking to commit to a long-term career with a financially-solid, swiftly-growing and employee-friendly company.</p>
<p>Additional requirements for the Business Development position include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 1-2 year sales or marketing experience. College graduates welcome with bachelors degree or better with marketing studies.</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of marketing concepts, positioning, sales planning, competitive analysis and product development</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Salesforce or equivalent software program</li>
<li>Outside sales and/or customer service experience a plus</li>
<li>Working knowledge of social media and emerging technologies a plus</li>
<li>Experience with Trade Shows (1 or 2 a year) is a PLUS</li>
<li>Easy-going, compassionate towards people, with outstanding customer service skills.</li>
</ul>
