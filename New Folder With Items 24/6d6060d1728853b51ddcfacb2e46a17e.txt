<p><b>About the Position:</b></p>
<p>The Digital Marketing Coordinator will assist in developing and optimizing the digital customer experience at First Internet Bank, which includes marketing automation platforms, corporate website, landing pages, social media, SEO and direct marketing efforts. They will be responsible for collaborating with the marketing team to develop and launch acquisition campaign strategies to ensure Bank goals are met. Additionally the ideal candidate will provide support for internal communications and events. This role requires the use of data analytics as well as a strong focus to collaborate and partner across the organization. The Digital Marketing Coordinator will have a high level of initiative, one who is comfortable working in a fast-paced environment. This individual must be tech-savvy with excellent written and social media communication skills along with strong project management skills.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Help develop, execute and optimize the Bank’s digital customer experience and optimize sales funnel using A/B testing and other conversion rate optimization tactics</li>
<li>Work with Marketing team to develop and implement digital marketing programs that acquire new customers for the Bank
</li>
<li>Assist in the implementation of tracking across all digital marketing campaigns
</li>
<li>Create campaign reports on a regular basis for all levels of management</li>
<li>Perform day-to-day operations in the Bank’s content management system, including creation of web pages, landing pages and other necessary website functions</li>
<li>Evaluate emerging technologies, provide thought leadership and perspective for adoption where appropriate</li>
<li>Perform regular analysis on competitor social media, websites etc. to understand competitive landscape
</li>
<li>Provide logistical support for internal and external events</li>
<li>Work across the organization to ensure cohesive communication approach Bank-wide</li>
<li>Manage marketing promotional collateral</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>This position requires a college degree in marketing, business, or a related field and at least one to three years digital marketing experience with hands on-experience with marketing automation software, social media platforms, website analytics tool and content management systems.</p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of and experience with current and evolving digital marketing trends
</li>
<li>Experience with marketing automation software (SalesForce Marketing Cloud, Hubspot etc.)</li>
<li>Experience with search engine optimization and content marketing strategies</li>
<li>Understanding of social media channels that include but are not limited to: Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube</li>
<li>Experience with Google Analytics or other website analytics tools</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication, including strong editing, grammar, and proof reading skills</li>
<li>Basic understanding of web technologies, including HTML and CSS preferred.</li>
<li>Knowledge of A/B testing best practices</li>
<li>Strong coordination and organizational skills</li>
<li>Proactive with excellent troubleshooting, problem resolution and follow-through skills</li>
<li>Ability to learn new software and analytics programs as necessary</li>
<li>Previous experience in marketing financial services a plus</li>
</ul>
