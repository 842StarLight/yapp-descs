<h2>Research/Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>The role:</b><br>
The Research/Data Analyst position will help to guide all aspects of the local data platform for office, industrial, retail and multifamily data collected and analyzed for the Northern California area. This position will build and continue to elevate a best-in-class research program that differentiates JLL and drives a competitive advantage in the marketplace through market expertise, analysis and insight. This team member will be responsible for achieving data excellence, to enhance our offerings in the areas of business intelligence, data visualization, and graphical output.
<br><br>
The analyst will have primary responsibility for collecting and analyzing data, maintaining and constantly elevating the quality of our data, creating client-facing content and presenting it in a compelling way. They will work with the research manager to interpret client needs, find the information that addresses the needs, and present the information in the best format possible. They will assist in tracking and analyzing all aspects of the local commercial real estate market and will leverage that knowledge proactively for research products, client projects and business development efforts.
<br><br>
This position will have direct responsibility for the Northern California commercial real estate market and will work closely with JLL research leadership in the San Francisco office, as well as colleagues throughout the Americas research group.
<br><br>
<b>Key responsibilities:</b><br>
Primary responsibility for the collection and auditing of JLL’s proprietary database of commercial real estate properties, lease and sale comps for office, industrial, retail and multi-family properties.
<br>Create compelling visual communications, integrating JLL’s proprietary research data, for the purposes of business development, external distribution, and client requests.
<br>Produce interactive maps, reports, and presentations that bring research content and real estate data to life.
<br>Create tableau dashboards that identify CRE market trends and allow deep market analysis.
<br>Collaborate at a high level with local market leadership and sales force to execute the research plan
<br>Maintain intimate and extensive knowledge of the macro and micro factors affecting the marketplace and proactively position research to leverage this knowledge for the business and for clients
<br>Expand and improve the research platform, program, output that demonstrates the firm’s market expertise
<br>Contribute to and execute the production of all local office research reports and collateral
<br>Develop and share best practice standards for the region and the Americas Research Group
<br>Develop strong relationships with internal and external clients and facilitate the use of research as a differentiator including being personally client-facing and externally focused on new business efforts
<br>Work with marketing teams and business leaders to position research to contribute to all business development efforts
<br><br>
<b>Specific responsibilities:</b><br>
Manage the statistical and analytical program for the Northern California real estate market
<br>Develop and implement a plan and timeline for covering specific, current geographic and analytical gaps in office and economic research coverage
<br>Maintain an acceptable level of data completeness to include data validation and audit levels.
<br>Present regularly to internal teams and clients and present compelling trend and analysis
<br>Analyze and report on the local and regional economy with respect to employment statistics, major company activity, industry trends and demographics that affect the local real estate market
<br>Contribute to special research reports and white papers on unique market economic or industry trends
<br><br>
<b>Experience and qualifications:</b><br>
Strong organization, data collection, validation and auditing skills.
<br>Up to one year in commercial real estate research, economic and demographic analysis or closely related field
<br>Command of local markets and business, with good understanding of macro trends and historical perspective
<br>High level analytical skills and problem solving ability and written and oral communication skills
<br>Experience managing workload, report productions and programs
<br>Ability to effectively and professionally meet client expectations and positively represent the firm
<br>Ability to multi-task, succeed under pressure and effectively manage time and workload
<br>Proactive and strategic thinking and ability to align research with the business and innovate research output
<br>Command of requisite technology including Excel, PowerPoint, and Word and database systems
<br>Comfort with visualization tools (such as Tableau, Power View) and basic presentation and mapping skills
<br>Bachelor’s degree in business, economics or related field required
<br>Motivated and self-starting with a desire to succeed and contribute to the success of a team
<br>A desire to work within a diverse, collaborative, and driven professional environment
<br><br>
<b>About JLL Research:</b><br>
JLL Research consists of 400 full time professionals globally and is the largest research group in the real estate industry and among the best of any world-wide professional services firm. Our mission is "To create clear competitive advantage for our clients through the strategic use of accurate data, market intelligence and innovative thinking from around the world". Our research activities include maintaining comprehensive property market databases, analyzing current market conditions and future trends, understanding the factors that drive property performance and occupancy trends, and providing research-led strategy and advice to investors, developers and occupiers around the world. We are committed to developing new metrics and methodological tools to capture and understand the factors driving office, retail and warehousing/logistics real estate markets across the world and bring insight to the future for our clients.</div>
