<h2>Marketing Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Spirion, headquartered in St. Petersburg, FL, is the leading provider of enterprise data management software to help businesses reduce their sensitive data footprint and proactively minimize the risks, costs and reputational damage of successful cyberattacks. Spirion helps organizations avoid costly data breaches by discovering, classifying, monitoring and protecting personal information, medical records, credit card numbers, and intellectual property stored across the enterprise, within e-mail, and in the cloud.</p>
<p>Spirion specializes in the high-precision search and automated classification of unstructured data using its AnyFind™ engine’s unparalleled accuracy when analyzing human-generated text and images. Spirion has thousands of customers among leading firms in the healthcare, public sector, retail, education, financial services, energy, industrial, and entertainment markets</p>
<p>Spirion is actively seeking a <b>Marketing Operations Manager</b> to join our market-leading cybersecurity product and marketing teams.</p>
<p>The Marketing Operations Manager will be responsible for executing the marketing automation strategy, developing marketing analytics and intelligence, and providing marketing KPI forecasting and reporting. The MOPs manager will own the digital ecosystem that enables demand generation, campaign performance management, lead qualification and delivery, as well as the systematic optimization of marketing processes. The role will align with IT Business Systems, Operations, and Finance to increase organizational transparency and accountability.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Own and manage the marketing automation platform (MAP) including maintenance, program design and optimization</li>
<li>Manage other marketing systems that generate, distribute, and report on leads</li>
<li>Analyze results, set-up testing protocols, and work to optimize systems and programs in order to achieve the highest and fastest revenue results</li>
<li>Assess the impact of marketing campaigns on the bottom line and recommend program changes based on data analysis</li>
<li>Manage the marketing automation process executing demand generation campaigns, lead nurturing, and customer marketing</li>
<li>Establish scalable processes that ensure campaign and lead management best practices</li>
<li>Forecast, establish, and report monthly KPI targets for marketing lead sources and sales region destinations</li>
<li>Evaluate new technologies and applications to enhance the digital ecosystem</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications and Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree required with a strong preference for Marketing, Advertising, Communications, or Business major.</li>
<li>Experience using Salesforce &amp; Marketo automation platform, certification preferred.</li>
<li>5+ years of digital marketing experience</li>
<li>Understanding and experience with implementing enterprise level digital architectures including web, search, CRM, acquisition databases, and email marketing and applying digital marketing best practices to support strategy</li>
<li>Great attitude and desire to be a key member of the Digital Marketing team</li>
<li>Willingness to innovate and challenge the status-quo</li>
<li>Ability to remain flexible in his/her approach and a willingness to take creative input from a variety of stakeholders</li>
<li>Strong communicator and presenter who is very comfortable interacting within a team environment and cross-functionally across an organization, as well as with senior management</li>
<li>Comfortable synthesizing response data and making sound, actionable recommendations</li>
<li>Must be detailed oriented, team player and technologically savvy</li>
<li>Cybersecurity software industry experience a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>An individual must be able to perform each Essential Function of the job satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities, who are otherwise qualified, to perform the Essential Functions.</p>
