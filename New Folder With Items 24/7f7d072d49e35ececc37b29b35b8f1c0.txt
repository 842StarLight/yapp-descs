<p><b>Proposal Manager/Technical Writer</b></p>
<p>
Become a part of our First Student team as a Proposal Manager/Technical Writer. We hire individuals who make safety and customer service their top priorities. If you have the drive, confidence, and determination to succeed, we are looking for you!</p>
<p><b>
At First Student, we are proud to offer:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Tremendous career advancement opportunities due to a strong presence across North America.</li>
<li>A comprehensive benefits package with paid holidays &amp; vacation, medical, vision, and dental coverage, and 401(k) savings plan.</li>
<li>A great work environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Proposal Manager/Technical Writer responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates with Operations, Marketing and Business Development to write and develop proposals,best-and-final offer, interview presentations and other required documents.</li>
<li>Compiles a Table of Compliance and Win-Theme that outlines the production specifications and section details as a guideline for development of the proposal.</li>
<li>With input from Directors of Business Development and other corporate sales support staff, develop a compelling proposal with the intent to win the business. Author, proofread, edit, print, compile and ensure accuracy of the final proposal document.</li>
<li>Ensures proposal compliance with requirements specified in the Request for Proposal (RFP).</li>
<li>Complete all required forms and obtains all necessary signatures;includes bonding and insurance requests</li>
<li>Schedules, coordinates and tracks proposal delivery before the specified deadline.</li>
<li>Support the Business Development Team in sales efforts, including developing PowerPoint presentations, documents, reports, communications, etc.</li>
<li>Maintain and update proposal boilerplate, current corporate information, location profiles, resumes, bios, customer references and templates as needed.</li>
<li>Drive prospective and continuous improvement in the proposal development process and materials by identifying opportunities, building organizational support for new ideas and collaborating with sales, marketing and communications to plan and execute.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Proposal Manager/Technical Writer requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, English,Communications, Journalism or related field required.</li>
<li>3-5 years of technical writing experience. RFP response writing a plus.</li>
<li>Excellent writing skills, with a strong orientation to detail and service.</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of work processing, spreadsheet and presentation software</li>
<li>Proposal writing, management experience strongly preferred</li>
<li>Ability to exercise independent thinking with a high level of prioritization skills</li>
<li>Strong customer relation skills</li>
<li>Ability to generate creative ideas, meet multiple deadlines and work independently</li>
<li>Knowledge of business and management principles.</li>
<li>Some travel may be required.</li>
</ul>

As a huge company with 120,000 staff and over 2.5 billion customers worldwide, you’ll also enjoy security, a brilliant future, and excellent training.</p>
