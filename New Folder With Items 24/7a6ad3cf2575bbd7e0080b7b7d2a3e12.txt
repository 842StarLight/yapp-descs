<h2>Head of Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Marketing Evolution provides the most powerful marketing measurement and optimization solutions that increase campaign performance, sales, and engagement. Leveraging patented technology and data from hundreds of sources, our breakthrough person-centric approach provides marketers with actionable insights to prospectively recommend media, message, and budget allocation. Forward-looking brands rely on Marketing Evolution to deliver accurate unified marketing measurement across both online and offline channels, while maximizing their media spend, marketing Return on Investment (ROI), and brand impact.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
About the Role</b></div>
<div>
Reporting to the Chief Customer Officer, the Head of Operations will define and implement enterprise-wide operations methods and standards, including metric tracking and reporting for further refinement. Working closely with executives and stakeholders across departments, they will quickly absorb existing strategy, priorities and customer requirements, as well as current product functionality and associated business processes. Using this knowledge, they will design and deploy appropriate business processes to ensure consistent, high quality, on-time delivery to customers.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Design, deploy, measure and fine-tune an operational delivery process that includes:Job scheduling (internal and customer-initiated)</li>
<li>
On-time job delivery</li>
<li>
Status information and updates for internal and customer users</li>
<li>
Issue/gap investigation and resolution</li>
<li>
Build out a centralized support team to identify and resolve internal and customer-reported delivery risks or issues:</li>
<li>
Source, vet and implement the appropriate tool suite for ticket/case management</li>
<li>
Define support responsibilities and required candidate skills</li>
<li>
Hire, train and deploy support staff</li>
<li>
Define, deploy, measure and improve time to resolve statistics</li>
<li>
Work with product team to identify new features or improvements to address common support issues, more rapidly diagnose issues and move some required functions to the customers where appropriate</li>
<li>
Manage and evolve the new customer on-boarding/configuration team</li>
<li>
Train or upgrade Technical Implementation managers to support 3 – 4 parallel new customer implementations</li>
<li>
Work with product team to reduce new customer on-boarding and configuration time below 90 days by evolving the product functionality and/or UI</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
