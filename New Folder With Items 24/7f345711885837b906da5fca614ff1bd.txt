<h2>Director of Marketing, Enterprise Automation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Director of Marketing, Enterprise Automation will be responsible for lead generation and pipeline creation to support the growth of our leading enterprise document conversion technologies. The ideal candidate will have proven success managing a variety of channels, including SEO, PPC (Google Ads), marketing automation, email marketing, social media, event marketing, content strategy, and other critical marketing functions from creative ideation to execution. Diligence, organization, and laser-focused time management are essential qualities that the stand-out candidate will possess.</p>
<p>In this role, you will manage a tight, in-house marketing team to execute on your objectives. In addition, you will have the support of a global team of designers, coders, and marketing specialists to assist you in your efforts. The ideal candidate will enjoy working independently and be able to collaborate and communicate effectively with sales, management, marketing, IT, and external partners and vendors. You will manage a global marketing budget and report directly to the President of the business unit.</p>
<p>This position offers highly competitive pay as well as great benefits including medical insurance, dental, vision, a generous commute stipend, annual bonus opportunities, and more. It is a great fit for someone looking to take the next step into a senior management role within a team environment that works hard but values and appreciates its employees.</p>
<p>About CVISION</p>
<p>CVISION Technologies is a digital media software company based in Queens, New York. Our industry-leading document conversion solutions help enterprises email, share, archive, and manage their documents much more efficiently using automation and server-level technology.</p>
<p>CVISION is a business of Foxit Software. Foxit is a leading provider of solutions for converting, reading, editing, creating, organizing, and securing PDF documents. Foxit has sold to over 100,000 customers worldwide, located in over 200 countries on 6 continents, and has integrations with Google Chrome, Gmail, Amazon Kindle, and more.</p>
