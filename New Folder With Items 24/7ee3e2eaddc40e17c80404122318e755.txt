<h2>Territory Manager- Mississippi & Louisiana</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>“We are innovators who make purpose-built gear for life's most demanding missions.”</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Our ethos defines our culture – Trusted, Responsive, Accountable and Committed.</i></b></p>
<p>
5.11 Tactical has established itself as the fastest-growing and most well-known brand of tactical apparel, gear and functional products in the world. Through a commitment to operator-led product innovation, quality and compelling value, 5.11 has developed a loyal following among some of the most demanding and discerning end-users of apparel and gear.</p>
<p>
At our Operations Center in Manteca and Development Center in Irvine, we leverage our deep relationships with Law Enforcement, Special Forces Operators and First Responders to gain unique insights. The results are products that break new ground combining ruggedness, speed, range of motion and ultimate comfort in professional duty, tactical training, adventure, women’s tactical, fitness and industrial professionals.</p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p><b>
The Territory Manager</b> is a champion of the brand through a high-level of communication and relationship-building across the organization and marketplace and is responsible for pipeline development in Public Safety Departments. This includes new Department business, increasing existing Department sales, and retrieving lost Department business. The ideal candidate will live within the assigned Indianapolis Territory.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Call on Public Safety Departments to create a pipeline of business by demonstrating, promoting, and selling 5.11 Tactical products.</li>
<li>Develop strong professional relationships with major Public Safety departments and within the territory</li>
<li>Develop, implement and execute business development strategies and plans to development new department business. Additionally create plan to increase existing department sales and retrieve lost department business.</li>
<li>Manage and keep current account information, performance and forecasts through the use of Salesforce</li>
<li>Actively participate in local grass-roots industry-related events</li>
<li>Experience working in fast-paced, rapidly changing environments</li>
<li>Coordinate and attend regional and national trade shows</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Have 3 to 5 years of proven Sales experience in outside sales</li>
<li>Associate’s degree or above preferred</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written and presentation skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, prioritization and follow up skills</li>
<li>Be proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (MS Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, etc)</li>
<li>Strong knowledge and handling of tactical equipment and gear preferred</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce or other CRM software</li>
<li>Have a proven track record of strong leadership and relationship building experience</li>
<li>Be available for extensive travel</li>
</ul>
