<h2>Warehouse Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Carrier Enterprise (CE) is a subsidiary of the largest independent HVAC/R distribution company in the world, WATSCO, Inc. CE proudly distributes the Carrier, Bryant, Payne and Gree brands of HVAC equipment, as well as a full-line of aftermarket parts, supplies and accessories. CE has over 150 locations throughout 28 states, Puerto Rico, the Caribbean, and Latin America, with over 1600 employees ready to serve you, providing the best products with best-in-class customer service.</div>
<div>We currently have an exciting opportunity for a <b>Warehouse Associate at our Richmond, VA location.
</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Stores materials/equipment</li>
<li>Upholds environmental health and safety standards
</li>
<li>Works closely with internal customers (Store Associates and Sales Managers)
</li>
<li>Provides excellent customer service to external customers (mechanical contractors; dealers, owners)
</li>
<li>Requires forklift driving and handling to load and unload product</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Job Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma or GED required</li>
<li>Minimum 2-3 years of warehouse operational experience
</li>
<li>HVAC experience is preferred, but not required
</li>
<li>Good communication (written and oral) and interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>Basic computer skills</li>
<li>Proven commitment to customer service and a safe work environment
</li>
<li>Must be team-oriented
</li>
</ul>
