DWFritz

Your Purchasing Team at DWFritz
<br><br>
At DWFritz, the Purchasing team is fast-paced and very attentive to detail. This team maintains flexibility in order to be prepared for abrupt changes based on the needs of the customer all while keeping costs and lead times down. The positive, can-do attitudes of these team members assist the interaction between vendors and other internal teams, facilitating the creation of brilliant products. The Purchasing team handles a large volume of projects and consistently finds innovative solutions to every-day tasks.
<br><br>
Your Challenge
<br><br>
To provide cost effective and timely materials in support of our projects to deliver machines we can be proud of to our clients.
<br><br>
Your Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage RFQ’s, purchase orders, and expedites from approved vendors per assigned commodities.</li>
<li>Analyze quotations received, select or recommend suppliers with whom orders are to be placed.</li>
<li>Negotiate better pricing to reduce project costs.</li>
<li>Help to set up and/or qualify new vendors, as needed.</li>
<li>Participate in cross-functional teams for category specific projects delivering savings and efficiencies.</li>
<li>Participate in procurement/supply chain improvement initiatives, as assigned.</li>
<li>Maintain positive vendor relationships.</li>
<li>Maintain a positive, productive relationship with project teams.</li>
<li>Follow department policy and procedures.</li>
</ul>
Your Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Relationship Building: Seeks out, cultivates and maintains positive and productive business relationships internally and externally. Is able to motivate and work well with others through their optimistic attitude and constructive approach to solving problems or dealing with conflict.</li>
<li>Drive &amp; Energy: Shows initiative and takes personal responsibility while taking an overall proactive approach. Helps to resolve issues without excessive guidance and appropriately engages necessary resources to accomplish tasks. Presents an energetic demeanor while dealing with multiple projects or tasks.</li>
<li>Organizing &amp; Planning: Drives towards overall business needs by prioritizing multiple competing tasks, and effectively managing time. Utilizes available systems and resources effectively within the scope of the work being performed.</li>
<li>Communication: Recognizes value of continuous information exchange. Actively seeks and disseminates information both in person and through use of technology. Demonstrates active listening and shares ideas, opinions, and feedback in a diplomatic and constructive manner. Raises issues and offers potential solutions that take into account all functions.</li>
<li>Demonstrated skill in negotiating.</li>
<li>Knowledge of procurement in a manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>Proficiency with MRP/ERP software and systems.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills.</li>
<li>Problem solving, system and methods improvement skills.</li>

<li>Proficient in Microsoft Excel and Word.</li>
