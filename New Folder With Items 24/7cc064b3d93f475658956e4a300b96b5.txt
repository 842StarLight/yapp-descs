<h2>Development Analyst (New York)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>The Development team manages the development process from the inception of transaction through the construction and delivery of each project. Development team members specialize in land use, design and construction management and employ these skills across a wide range of asset types in all the diverse geographies in which CIM operates.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Development Analyst will assist Senior Development Staff with the day-to-day management duties from acquisition through pre-development, pre-construction, construction, closeout and pre-operation phases of the project(s) assigned.
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Working with the Investment Deal Team(s) to provide due diligence information and feasibility analysis of potential acquisitions
</li>
<li>Assist in negotiating and drafting contract terms and scope with consultants and contractors
</li>
<li>Assist in management and oversight of consultants, contractors and JV partners
</li>
<li>Assist in developing and maintaining project timelines, ensuring milestones are met on time and within budget
</li>
<li>Provide reports related to project status to Investments Team and other stakeholders
</li>
<li>Prepare and track development budget related to assigned projects
</li>
<li>Assist Senior Development Staff in securing the appropriate approvals, certifications, permits, etc. from city and other government agencies as required
</li>
<li>Responsible for completing and executing project contracts in accordance with department requirements</li>
<li>
Manage project documents including discretionary and non-discretionary approvals, agency releases/declarations/determinations, easements, covenants, tract maps, construction documents, A/E design and engineering plans and specifications, contract/change orders, closeout documentation
</li>
<li>Responsible for sharing and uploading documents and communication on GC’s web based project management site
</li>
<li>Tracking and reporting project status to investors, lenders, joint ventures, quasi-public partnerships and the like
</li>
<li>Assist with monthly construction progress reporting including but not limited to schedules, applications for payment, etc.
</li>
<li>Assist in the development of sales and marketing campaigns
</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS: (including certification, licenses, etc.)
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree and equivalent work experience in a field related to building design and construction. Advanced Degree preferred
</li>
<li>Experience in reading and understanding Construction-related documents
</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></h3>
<div><ul><li>Oversight of design team and consultants, contractors, subcontractors and vendors
</li></ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>TECHNICAL SKILL REQUIREMENTS:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in Microsoft Office applications
</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in using Sharepoint or other document control software.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in using Project Management software
</li>
</ul></div>
