<h2>Recruiter</h2>

<p>Thrasher is seeking a highly motivated Recruiter to help us redefine our industry by constantly striving to surpass the status quo. This role will have the primary duty of managing the company’s recruiting program and exercising discretion and independent judgment with respect to matters of significance related to recruiting and other human resources issues. In addition, this individual will provide high-level tactical support to every employee and function of the human resources department. If you are a self-motivated individual who thrives on helping an organization grow, apply today to join our team as a Recruiter.</p>

<p></p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU’LL DO</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages assigned job requisitions from developing the recruiting strategy, sourcing candidates through various platforms, reviewing applicants, initiating and coordinating the interview process from phone screens to offer acceptance(s).
</li>
<li>Proactively and strategically sources candidates through various recruiting methods.
</li>
<li>Ability to build a pipeline of talented candidates, to make available to the needs of the company. Particularly sales and production candidates.
</li>
<li>Anticipates and understands the hiring needs of the company through frequent communication with the company’s leadership.</li>
<li>Assists strategic insight into the recruiting process. Makes recommendations for improving candidate flow, candidate experience, and the overall recruiting process.</li>
<li>Interfaces with community and civic organizations to seek opportunities to promote employment with the company, including career fairs, campus events, and the like.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, ABILITIES, AND REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 year’s experience in a recruitment role.
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>Bilingual Spanish speaking ability preferred.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with ability to collaborate effectively with all functions and levels of the organization.</li>
<li>Knowledge of sales, marketing, and recruiting principles and practices.
</li>
<li>Skill in operating personal computer and programs such as HRIS systems, spreadsheets, database, and word processing software.
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication.</li>
<li>Ability to understand, speak, read, and write in English.</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills.</li>
<br>
</ul>
