<h2>District Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">District Manager (Fort Lauderdale / Boca Raton)
<br><br>
<b>Sales – Maximizes sales results by completing the following:</b><br>
Drive profitable sales by properly preparing for and executing all corporate initiatives at the store level
<br><br>
Forecast sales and adjust inventory levels on all assigned stores
<br><br>
Track profitability, and oversee the control of expenses in the district
<br><br>
Coordinate repairs, supplies and services for assigned stores
<br><br>
Monitor inventory per square foot to support the sales plan
<br><br>
Customer Service
<br><br>
Ensure a customer focused environment in all assigned stores through training of management team.
<br><br>
Monitor the recovery process during visits to ensure that standards are met throughout the district
<br><br>
Address customer complaints/concerns in an urgent and friendly manner.
<br><br>
Ensure management team knowledge of customer service philosophy and upcoming/current advertising.
<br><br>
Manage staffing levels in all assigned stores to ensure proper balance and maintain customer service.
<br><br>
Merchandising and Presentation
<br><br>
Ensure that every effort is made to increase sales through merchandising, displays, and advertising preparation in all assigned stores
<br><br>
Ensure that stores are properly stocked in accordance with company merchandising guidelines, and that markdowns are completed timely and accurately per policy.
<br><br>
Assist with relocating and opening new stores – hire and train new staff, order supplies, and merchandise; executing floor plans per corporate direction.
<br><br>
Ensure timely execution of merchandise presentation programs including Green Card, E-mails and Seasonal displays all set and signed the prior evening the ad is in effect through proper communication and follow up in all store in the district.
<br><br>
Staffing and Training
<br><br>
Develop engaged and knowledgeable store management to drive sales, customer service, and to maintain proper store condition through good hiring decisions and thorough training.
<br><br>
Supervise store management teams; coach, counsel, evaluate performance, and recommend salary increases, promotions and terminations.
<br><br>
Train management team in all phases of store operations.
<br><br>
Provide support and guidance in all area of day-to-day management of all assigned stores.
<br><br>
Leadership and Communication – Builds effective teams by utilizing the following:
<br><br>
Motivate and impact team performance using exceptional people development skills.
<br><br>
Ensure Company policies and procedures are adhered to and enforced equitably and consistently.
<br><br>
Foster a work environment where associates are encouraged to communicate creative and innovative ideas.
<br><br>
Encourage associates to communicate any concerns to their supervisor, or to use the Open Door Policy.
<br><br>
Coach to improvement, and encourage giving and receiving constructive feedback.
<br><br>
Evaluate performance of associates and issue appropriate discipline, if necessary.
<br><br>
Loss Prevention and Safety
<br><br>
Ensure adherence to loss prevention policies, and enforce company policies and procedures in all areas of operations.
<br><br>
Audit each assigned store at least once per month, according to store audit guidelines with the store manager present.
<br><br>
Submit store audits to the regional manager within 24 hours.
<br><br>
Ensure all safety policies and procedures are followed in each store to create a safe environment for associates and customers.
<br><br>
Job Requirements
<br><br>
<b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS &amp; ABILITIES:</b><br>
Bachelor degree in Management or General Business, or greater than 5 years of retail management experience is required. Five (5) years of multi-unit retail management experience is preferred.
<br><br>
Must possess strong business acumen and solid operational skills, and be able to execute concepts related to accounting principles, inventory management, merchandising, and management of human resources.
<br><br>
Excellent communication skills required for effective interaction with customers and associates
<br><br>
Ability to lead, make decisions, explain procedures, convey information, analyze numbers, resolve problems or conflict, demonstrate reasoning, adaptability, conceptualizing, attention to detail, time management, organization and computer skills
<br><br>
Must demonstrate outstanding interpersonal skills in order to resolve any associate or customer complaints or issues that will promote a positive work environment and the service standards in accordance with company philosophy.
<br><br>
<b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
Works out of home and travels to stores using personal vehicle. May occasionally drive a rental car. A valid Driver’s License is required.
<br><br>
Frequently required to read and interpret written documents, and must be able to distinguish colors.
<br><br>
Must be able to frequently lift and carry products weighing less than 5 pounds. Will occasionally need to lift and carry products weighing up to 50 pounds. Occasional pushing and pulling of carts and product weighing 50 to 250 pounds.
<br><br>
Frequently reaching to various levels and directions to move/stack boxes of product and stock product on display racks.
<br><br>
Continuous standing; frequent walking, climbing, crawling, stooping, bending and twisting; and occasional kneeling needed in order to stand and /or stock product(s) on display racks
<br><br>
Continuous need to hear in order to respond to and interact with management, associates, and customers. Will need to hear requests in person, and via intercom and telephone.
<br><br>
Continuous verbal communication with management, associates and customers in person, and via intercom and telephone.
<br><br>
Occasional use of hand tools such as screwdriver, hammer, scissors, box knife, pricing guns, etc., used to assemble/disassemble/unpackage/prepare products, shelves, displays, etc.
