<h2>Supervisor, Integrated Claims (Disability & FMLA)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Matrix Absence Management is one of the top Third Party Administrators (TPA) supporting clients in absence management Compensation). We partner with our clients Human Resources department to manage Workers' Compensation, Disability and/or Leave of absences until the claim is resolved or the employee returns to work. Our highly customer centric approach is to try and duplicate the culture of our clients to make the experience seamless for employees. Our mobile app technology provides ease of use to our client and employee in reporting claim information.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Matrix Absence Management offers a full benefit package - Medical, Dental &amp; Vision, including 401K match. Matrix is a friendly and pleasant office environment; offering flexible schedules Monday-Friday.
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>The Supervisor Integrated Claims (DI/FMLA) will supervise a team of Claims Examiners as they investigate, evaluate and determine benefit eligibility of Disability and Leave of Absence claims in a timely fashion. Supervisor will also be responsible for developing and sustaining excellent client relationships.
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Functions &amp; Qualifications
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Supervisor will ensure compliance with policy, provisions, state and federal regulations.
</li>
<li>Team will exercise expense management through investigation, appropriate follow up, case management and claim settlements.
</li>
<li>Coordinate appropriate assessment and assignment of customer to suitable claims examiner.
</li>
<li>Promote effective working relationships among team and Sales, Underwriting and Brokers.
</li>
<li>Responsible for assisting in implementation of new accounts and ensuring ongoing satisfaction.
</li>
<li>Responsible for selection, development, motivation, appraisal and salary recommendations of reporting subordinates.
</li>
<li>Provide effective leadership as well as technical claim and/or product knowledge, achieving results through others.
</li>
<li>Clearly expresses ideas in oral, written and presentation form.
</li>
<li>Ability to interact, negotiate and respond to both internal customers and clients regarding claim administration, product or service issues, with positive problem-solving skill.
</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and manage changing workloads, meeting deadlines independently and through management of others.
</li>
</ul>
