<h2>Rotating Equipment Performance Center - Reliability Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i>You may know SKF as the world leading supplier of products and solutions in the rolling bearing business. In addition, we are one of the leading players in seals, mechatronics, lubrication systems, and services which include technical support, maintenance services, condition monitoring and training. The inherent skills and competence of our around </i>40, 000 employees help us to fulfill our objective of creating more intelligent, sustainable and innovative customer solutions. SKF is a truly global company with presence in more than 130 countries, serving an extensive range of industries and customers worldwide.</p>
<p>An outstanding opportunity is available for a Reliability Analyst with genuine experience and success in the area of proactive maintenance.</p>
<p>The REP Reliability Analyst will be responsible for delivering Remote Diagnostic Analysis and Reporting services as an analyst publishing reports as well as supporting both Internal SKF and external SKF USA customers.</p>
<p>Data analysis to determine faults &amp; severity and write detailed reports on your findings.</p>
<p>Communicate and deliver reports to customers about your findings and recommendations.</p>
<p>Troubleshoot complex machinery problems.</p>
<p>Perform multi-channel data analysis.</p>
<p>Support and work with SKF Application Engineers to determine Root Cause of any failures and work toward improvements and correct actions.</p>
<p>Work with other SKF Product Specialists, Technicians and sales staff in identifying opportunities and securing contracts through the provision of SKF Solutions.</p>
<p>Provide profitable technical and proactive reliability maintenance services to customers to achieve or exceed the agreed KPIs and document the cost savings delivered.</p>
<p>Periodic requirement to prepare and deliver technical presentations to customers and REP team.</p>
<p><b>Job requirements: </b></p>
<p>You have a several years proven experience including certification or equivalent with machinery vibration analysis of at least an ISO Category III.</p>
<p>You have a sound understanding and demonstrated experience working with other predictive maintenance technologies and practices including electrical and mechanical infrared inspections, In-place dynamic balancing, airborne ultrasound inspections, machinery alignment, online and off line motor current analysis, ODS, NDT inspections, oil and lubrication analysis.</p>
<p>You have a steel, wind, refinery, paper, mechanical, electrical, manufacturing or military background and \ or experience.</p>
<p>You have a working knowledge of CMMS and planning &amp; scheduling.</p>
<p>The ability to travel as required. The position will require approximately 80% office and 20% or less of in field work.</p>
<p>You are a self starter and can plan your time for effective utilization and allocation of your resources for service delivery.</p>
<p>You have a customer support focus and proactive communication skills.</p>
<p>You are fluent in spoken and written English.</p>
<p>Sound PC software knowledge</p>
<p>You have a strong desire and enjoy a career in equipment reliability.</p>
<p><b>Our offer: </b></p>
<p>The opportunity for you to work in an innovative and well-equipped professional environment which is part of a large successful company with an excellent reputation. You will join a team of well-motivated colleagues.</p>
<p>We look forward to see you develop as a professional who creates a career in SKF and we will support your steps.</p>
<p>Enjoy an excellent compensation package with best in class benefits.</p>
