<h2>Product Owner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Comcast Business offers technology solutions ranging from Ethernet, internet, and WiFi connectivity to voice, television, and managed Enterprise solutions to power businesses of all sizes to perform better. From small businesses to mid-market and large Enterprise organizations, Comcast Business serves business customers across the country. Powered by an advanced, Gig-speed network and backed by 24/7 technical support, Comcast Business is one of the largest contributors to the growth of Comcast Cable. The organization is the nation’s largest cable provider to small and mid-size businesses and has emerged as a force in the Enterprise market, recognized by leading industry associations as one of the fastest growing provider of Ethernet services.</p>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Product Owner role is responsible for the Sales CRM functionality in the Orion program. The Role will be insuring the end user sales needs are accounted for in creation of the functionality in our new Sales CRM platform. Works with a cross-functional team that encompasses Sales, Marketing, Operations, Finance, Master Data, Change Management and Product. Will quickly understand current processes that support the Sales, Sales Operations, Marketing, Provisioning, Customer Care, and Technical Operations and to define the future state that will be enabled through technology. Has in-depth experience of the Role of a product Owner on an Agile scrum team as well as knowledge of Sales and standard sales processes. Thrives in a fast-paced deadline-driven environment where end-user experience is a top priority.</p>
<p>
We are seeking a strong Product Owner to work on our Orion Sales CRM Agile development team.</p>
<p>
To be successful in this role, you should be able to identify user needs and work with cross-functional teams to manage releases.</p>
<p>
If you are a highly-motivated individual with a genuine interest in technology a passion to deliver cutting-edge solutions, we have the perfect job for you</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Core responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Works within an Agile Framework, maintains necessary tools, artifacts, and participates in all Agile ceremonies to deliver continuous value for our customers and the business</li>
<li>Understand the Comcast Business Sales user experience and interfaces when interacting with product and associated functionality</li>
<li>Own the creations of User Stories &amp; provide clarifications as necessary to the Agile and Business teams</li>
<li>Provide backlog management, iteration planning, and elaboration of the user stories</li>
<li>Work closely with Business Leadership and Product Management to create and maintain a product backlog according to business value or ROI</li>
<li>Deconstructs technical concepts and metrics to facilitate process development</li>
<li>Help to define success metrics for each product system or process impacts/needs and establishes a plan to track, monitor, and measure the success of those metrics</li>
<li>Completes assessments of business processes and documents each of the following areas: problem statements, process flows, gap analyses, and solution recommendations</li>
<li>Develops repeatable, and thus scalable, plans and processes in order to speed time to market and improve operational efficiency for Comcast Business Sales end users</li>
<li>Provide an active role in mitigating impediments impacting successful team completion of Release/Sprint Goals</li>
<li>Develops scalable processes to support and benefit the Company’s architecture vision</li>
<li>Interacts closely with matrixed cross-functional teams in order to validate/secure alignment</li>
<li>Diagnoses, corrects, and documents issues, risks, and problems using Quality Assurance (QA) practices</li>
<li>Keep abreast with Agile/Scrum best practices and new trends</li>
<li>Consistent exercise of independent judgment and discretion in matters of significance</li>
<li>Regular, consistent and punctual attendance. Must be able to work nights and weekends, variable schedule(s) as necessary.</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Secondary Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Regularly attend and actively participate in System Demos</li>
<li>Take feedback from System Demos back to teams and work to incorporate into backlog and upcoming iterations</li>
<li>Collaboration with Sales Operations Stakeholders and Product teams</li>
<li>Ensure Team Backlog is clean entering into Program Increment Planning (prioritized &amp; reflecting work the team needs to work on)</li>
<li>Bring the requested scope of the iteration to the session (via prioritized &amp; estimated backlog)</li>
<li>Ensure the iteration goals are aligned to the PI Objectives</li>
</ul>
