<h2>Door Coordinator - Schofield, WI</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Job ID: 161054</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Oldcastle BuildingEnvelope®, a CRH company, is the leading supplier of products specified to close the building envelope. They include: custom-engineered curtain wall and window wall, architectural windows, storefront systems, doors, skylights and architectural glass.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PREREQUISITES:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Previous shop floor or factory experience recommended.</li>
<li>
Requires physical strength and ability to lift an average of 50 to 70 lbs. on a regular basis.</li>
<li>
Must be able to communicate professionally with customers as well as co-workers.</li>
<li>
Mechanically inclined.</li>
<li>
SURPASS, EXCEL &amp; WORD knowledge are helpful.</li>
<li>
Tow-Motor Experience a plus<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>NATURE:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for accurately inspecting &amp; receiving product in from vendors.</li>
<li>
Will be responsible for organizing incoming hardware to active orders and stock.
</li>
<li>Person will need to proactively look in Surpass to assist with Coordination of upcoming jobs if the jobs are on schedule for delivery and communicate any issues or delay’s to the CSR/Estimator and department manager</li>
<li>
Must have ability to perform all duties necessary to assemble orders and prepare them for shipment.</li>
<li>
Work with supplies to work out any issues that may be happening on the delivery of items</li>
<li>
Able to work to achieve department goals in safety, quality and sales.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
FUNCTION:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>There is a dual functionality of the Inventory Coordinator:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Inspect all incoming shipments to ensure correct hardware was received and that it is in good condition. Receive all shipments into Surpass. Stage all custom hardware accordingly and put stock materials away in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Build door systems ensuring the highest level of quality.</li>
<li>
Must also be able to professionally communicate with customers to answer questions and assist with technical information. Utilization of computer software required for this function</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
ENVIRONMENT:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The shop floor working environment is subject to unusually warm temperatures during the summer months. Normal climate conditions of the region will also be present such as humidity. Glass cleaning materials as well as sealant used in fabricating insulated glass units can create some odors in the plant also. While the odors are within acceptable levels, they nevertheless can be smelled. Dust, pollen, and other typical airborne particulate matter are also present within the atmosphere of the shop floor.<br>
</p>
