<h2>Product Support Specialist (Defined Term)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Product Support Specialist (Defined Term)</b></p>
<p><b>Lake Mary, FL</b></p>
<p><b>Together we can make a Difference!</b></p>
<p>At Getinge we are dedicated and passionate about helping our customers save lives and ensure excellent care. We provide innovations for operating rooms, intensive-care units, sterilization departments and for life science companies and institutions. Our portfolio combines well-known product brands such as Maquet, Pulsion, Atrium, Lancer and Datascope – just to name a few.</p>
<p>A career at Getinge will provide career opportunities that both inspire and challenge. Here, you can make a difference every day.</p>
<p>The Product Support Specialist will provide office-based technical support for Lancer laboratory washers and autoclaves for service technicians, sales representatives and customers in the area of troubleshooting and issue resolution.</p>
<p><b>Job Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide after sales support for customers, service representatives, and sales representatives for Lancer washer and sterilizer products</li>
<li>Interface with customers, factory representatives, and internal team to provide resolutions to technical issues.</li>
<li>Provide oversight of customer service issues</li>
<li>Receive, modify, test, and ship machines and spare parts.</li>
<li>Perform general warehouse functions including inventory management, spare parts packaging and shipment</li>
<li>Travel to customer locations in order to perform installation, validation, and preventative maintenance on periodic basis</li>
<li>Assist in development specialized accessories and wash cycles required for specific customer cleaning applications</li>
<li>Provide assistance to other after sales team members in resolution of difficult technical/customer support issues</li>
<li>Maintain personal competence for meeting job responsibilities through effective use of product information and technical data provided by factory.</li>
<li>Order components through internal and external means.</li>
<li>Based upon design criteria, assemble/install modifications and options to stock equipment</li>
<li>Assist in training of service technicians as required</li>
<li>Log, track, and provide technical assistance in resolution of quality issues with the factory</li>
<li>Other activities as the need arises</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong technical aptitude</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills (verbal and written).</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize tasks.</li>
<li>Proactive and inquisitive</li>
<li>Intermediate skills using Outlook, Word, Excel, Adobe Acrobat, CAD Software, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associated degree in a technical discipline or equivalent.</li>
<li>Minimum (5) years demonstrated hands-on field experience, including ability to apply technical expertise and make judgements regarding systems and equipment of considerable complexity.</li>
<li>Exceptional troubleshooting and diagnostic skills, and ability to interpret electrical and mechanical drawings.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Quality Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build Quality into all aspects of their work by maintaining compliance to all quality requirements.</li>
<li>Attend all required Quality &amp; Compliance training at the specified interval.</li>
<li>Adopt the “Beyond Compliance Quality Culture” in the work environment; always meet and exceed requirements.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance to all FDA and Worldwide Quality &amp; Compliance regulations (As applicable to the job function).</li>
<li>Must have the education and experience to understand and comply with U.S. and Worldwide medical device regulations (As applicable to your job function).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Environmental/Safety/Physical Work Conditions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures environmental consciousness and safe practices are exhibited in decisions.</li>
<li>Use of computer and telephone equipment and other related office accessories/devices to complete assignments.</li>
<li>Must to be able to wear required personal protective equipment, including biological chemical protection, as required by customer SOP.</li>
<li>Must be able to complete series of inoculations when required.</li>
<li>May work extended hours based upon workload.</li>
<li>Physical requirements such as lifting specific weights &gt;70+ pounds.</li>
<li>Travel Requirements: estimated up to 20%. Occasional overnight travel and weekend departure as necessary.</li>
</ul>
