<h2>Software Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Evolus is a performance beauty company with a customer-centric approach focused on delivering breakthrough products. We are seeking a Product Manager to join our product team, where you will be leading a suite of innovative commerce and loyalty solutions for medical practices, medical spas, and partners nationwide within our Evolus Practice App.</p>
<p>
This role demands technical depth, principled thinking, a high bar for quality, structured communication, bold innovation, and a mentality of starting with the customer and working backwards. You will work alongside star teams from engineering, marketing, research &amp; design, legal, sales, and customer success. You will work to build intelligent and contextual experiences to shift and gamify ordering behavior, and drive positive impact for our customers, in a fast-paced, rapidly growing, technology environment. You should be comfortable in uncharted territory as we envision unique and differentiated solutions for our customers in an otherwise legacy market.</p>
<p>
We are looking for a Product Manager who is excited to make an impact in this industry and has the skills to match. If you join our team, you will be working on some of the most exciting opportunities and challenges we face, with a team that values growth, recognition, and camaraderie.</p>
<p><b>
Let’s talk about some of the key responsibilities of the role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead the ideation, technical development and launch of innovative products &amp; features in our mobile apps.</li>
<li>
Establish shared vision across the company by building consensus on priorities leading to product execution</li>
<li>
Drive product development with a team of world-class engineers and designers</li>
<li>
Synthesize customer and consumer feedback, research and market analysis to enhance user satisfaction</li>
<li>
Define and analyze key metrics that inform the success of products</li>
<li>
Learn &amp; understand Evolus’ strategic and competitive position and deliver products that are recognized as best in the industry</li>
<li>
Maximize efficiency in a constantly evolving environment where the process is fluid and creative solutions are the norm</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Your Skills and Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS degree required</li>
<li>
3+ years of Product Management experience working in fast-paced tech companies</li>
<li>
You have high customer empathy, with a desire to improve customers’ lives</li>
<li>
You are flexible and can quickly adapt to unexpected changes</li>
<li>
You are industrious and self-motivated</li>
<li>
You are data-driven and consistently leverage data to make decisions</li>
<li>
You have excellent communication and interpersonal skills with the ability to work well with different personality types and seniority levels</li>
<li>
You are technical or have aptitude and willingness to learn</li>
<li>
You are a collaborative team player</li>
<li>
Experience creating examples through wire frames and mockups</li>
<li>
Knowledge developing consumer products, technologies and platforms (preferred)</li>
</ul>
