<p><b>Our Mission</b></p>

<p><b>
Your Career</b></p>
<p>
As a member of the Palo Alto Networks sales team, you will report to the District Sales Manager for the U.S. Department of Defense. Working together with a Systems Engineer, you will be accountable for delivering creative solutions to our customers and growing your territory. You will be responsible for managing your territory to deliver above quota sales performance. Palo Alto Networks has chosen to go to market with Security Focused Channel partners; you will be responsible for working with appropriate channel partners and training them on the Palo Alto solutions specific to your customers’ requirements. You feel empowered by our product offerings - and love a technical challenge.</p>
<p><b>
Your Impact</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
DoD Sales Executive with a highly successful experience selling security appliances and/or software including but not limited to: Firewalls, SSL/IPSec VPNs, Security Proxies and Caches, Cloud Security solutions</li>
<li>
Previous success factors include over achievement of quota, top performer, history of initial seeding of accounts, and closing large enterprise deals</li>
<li>
Lead identifying and signing appropriate channel partners as well as training them on our solutions</li>
<li>
Guide your customers in their search to transition to a more secure online environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 Your Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent</li>
<li>
3-5 years of above quota sales experience as an IT Sales professional</li>
<li>
Self-motivated</li>
<li>
Strong communication (written and verbal) and presentation skills, both internally and externally</li>
<li>
Enterprise sales experience with and actionable Rolodex of decision-makers</li>
<li>
Superb organizational skills</li>
<li>
"Whatever it takes" attitude and motivation to deliver above quota performance</li>
<li>
Prior startup experience, experience being one of the first RSMs into an organization</li>
<li>
Experience working with Channel partners and understanding of a channel-centric go to market approach</li>
<li>
Secret or Top-Secret security clearance highly preferable</li>
</ul>
