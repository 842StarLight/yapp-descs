<h2>TopLine Research Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Futuri Media, named Tech Company of the Year by OHTec two years running, creates innovative interactive technology solutions for broadcasters.</div>
<div></div>
<div>We are seeking a TopLine Research Manager to be based out of our Cleveland, Ohio office.</div>
<div>
<br>
If you have a minimum of 5 years media research experience, a thorough understanding of analytics for media including Nielsen, Scarborough, MRI and other popular resources, and a bachelor’s or advanced degree in business or the sciences, we want to talk to you!<br>
<br>
<b>In this role you will:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Provide leadership and mentoring in order to grow our audience and sales intelligence consumer insights team.</li>
<li>Provide day-to-day management of Research Analysts to assure research requests are completed within deadlines and meet established operating standards.</li>
<li>Coach Research Analysts to effectively position primary and secondary data into story form that is easy for our clients to use in sales presentations.</li>
<li>Coach Research Analysts on the analysis and coordination of audience data for radio, tv and other media outlets.</li>
<li>Review TopLine deliverables to assure research analysts are creating business-oriented research stories for TL clients.</li>
<li>Develop, execute and improve data analytics and reporting dashboards within the TopLine app.</li>
<li>Analyze data gathered from a variety of sources and provide insights helpful to our analysts and to the sales departments we serve.</li>
<li>Work with TopLine Director and Futuri marketing department to develop advertising and communications strategies when TL releases updates and or new features.</li>
<li>Lead the continued upgrading of platforms for increased capability and stability</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>If you are:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Disciplined. A perfectionist.</li>
<li>A Problem Solver</li>
<li>Competitive</li>
<li>Have high work intensity</li>
<li>A strategic thinker</li>
<li>Have insatiable curiosity</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>And possess:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Strong analytic skills with experience in analysis and media research resources</li>
<li>Strong internet research, writing, reading, and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Strong organizational and analytical skills and have a high level of proficiency with PowerPoint and Excel.</li>
</ul>
