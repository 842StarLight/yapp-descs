<br>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>
Georgia-Pacific creates long-term value by using resources efficiently to provide innovative products and solutions that meet the needs of customers and society, while operating in a manner that is environmentally and socially responsible, and economically sound. Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia-Pacific LLC is one of the world's leading manufacturers and marketers of consumer products, building products, packaging, paper, cellulose and chemicals. The company employs more than 30,000 people at approximately 300 locations in North and South America.</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Georgia-Pacific is currently seeking an <b>Innovation Manager for the Consumer Innovation business</b> team in Atlanta, GA. Reporting to Director- Innovation, the successful candidate will use their general management skills and leadership capabilities to play an active role in the development and execution of the defined business vision and strategies. As a member of an agile team, the Innovation Manager is responsible for advancing projects with urgency, few resources and integrity.</p>
<br>
<br>
<div></div>
<p><b>A Day In The Life Typically Includes:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Wearing many different hats and functioning as an entrepreneur managing a startup business; key activities include consumer research, prototyping, digital/social/grass-root advertising and forming internal/ external relationships to drive ideas into successful executions
</li>
<li>Discovering consumer needs and brainstorming new solutions (products, services, digital) that are 10X better than existing alternatives</li>
<li>
Brainstorming creatives ways to market a new product, develop compelling brand positioning and outlining tactical steps to execute successfully</li>
<li>
Measuring results, synthesizing learnings and providing recommendations</li>
<li>
Working with internal &amp; external development teams to design, source &amp; manufacture new products, while meeting requirements for consumer needs, cost, quality and timing.</li>
<li>
Develop point-of-view of social/technological/economic trends to influence future portfolio</li>
<li>
Validate consumer demand, size of opportunity &amp; value proposition via rapid experimentation</li>
<li>
Learn new skills as needed (i.e. running digital ads, building website) to support experimentation</li>
</ul>
<br>
