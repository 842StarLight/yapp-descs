<b>
Description:</b><br>
<p></p>
<p>
Aramark is seeking a Menu Category Analyst who will lead menu category management analytics efforts within Aramark’s Marketing Center of Excellence (MCOE) on the Product Development Team. This role will support multiple brands and channels.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The ideal candidate is passionate about uncovering the “why” behind consumer purchase decisions through robust data mining, analytics and hypothesis testing. This analyst will be comfortable aggregating and synthesizing relevant data sets including but not limited to primary and secondary research, third party data, transactional data to develop a deep understanding of consumers, their needs and behaviors and key drivers that align with business objectives and support growth for Aramark.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Menu Category Analyst will focus on supporting the diverse needs of the Product Development and Culinary Innovation Teams and utilizes strong interpersonal, organizational and time management skills to prioritize tasks. This analyst will be a key contributor to ensure the timely delivery of menus to the field including providing content and developing field tools<br>
<br>
 Specific Responsibilities</p>
<p>
Menu Category Planning &amp; Analysis</p>
<ul>
<li>
Support the development of the Enterprise Menu Strategy to achieve sustainable long-term revenue, margin and other volume growth targets in line with the overall brand and business strategy</li>
<li>
Analyze sales and purchase data; establish benchmarks for making key menu category decisions</li>
<li>
Assess the item assortment, recommend adjustments to drive optimal category performance.</li>
<li>
Conduct detailed analysis and reporting, utilizing all available data resources; develop and execute score carding and valuation, to evaluate impact of implementation of assortment and Promotion initiatives</li>
<li>
Provide insights and recommendations by synthesizing data from a multitude of sources on customer, channel, and category strategy. Recommend actionable solutions based on a clear understanding of the analysis.</li>
<li>
Ability to create and deliver as an output “the story” which is supported by analytics and research.</li>
<li>
Supporting Menu Category development including following process design, supporting collaboration, accountability to timeline and financial budget.</li>
<li>
Assisting with the content for new menu releases</li>
<li>
Assisting with management and maintenance of menus and categories as assigned</li>
<li>
May perform other duties as required</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree (BA or BS Degree) in Business/Marketing required</li>
<li>
2-4 years of category management experience, including 1-2 years in category analytical and project management experience preferred</li>
<li>
Retail/Food Menu Management and/or menu strategy development experience preferred
</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite, especially Excel, PowerPoint, Access and SmartSheet</li>
<li>
Must be a self-starter, have strong project management skills and have the ability to work cross-functionally in a heavily, matrixed environment.
</li>
<li>This position will work closely with Global Supply Chain, Consumer Insights, and a broad range of other Aramark functional teams.</li>
</ul>
