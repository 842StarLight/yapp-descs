<p><b>
What You’ll Do</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
As a Sales Development Representative on the marketing operations team you will work closely with account executives and business developers to fill their pipeline with targeted and pre-qualified leads. Using smart prospecting skills, you will balance engaging with inbound leads and driving self-sourced leads for the sales pipeline.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Qualifying inbound leads: engage with prospects who request information about Dalet’s products and solutions, or who have become Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) based on their interactions with marketing activities, and qualify business opportunities for the sales team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Outbound prospecting for leads: proactively contact identified target accounts, via email, LinkedIn InMail or on the phone, to determine if there is a fit for Dalet’s products and solutions and what the next steps should be.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Industry and prospect research: conduct research about who works in what role at target accounts to determine who best to contact regarding Dalet product lines and solutions as well as identify likely business needs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supporting marketing activities: help craft and customize the right messaging to reach out to diverse pools of prospects, drive attendance and book meetings at events, provide feedback on messaging resonance.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supporting channel: identify potential partners in their market and working together to generate leads with or for our channel partners.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enrich CRM data: creating and managing account records in the CRM to ensure rich, accurate information is available for sales &amp; marketing activities.</li></ul>
<br>
