<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>About Press Ganey</b></p>
<p>
Press Ganey pioneered the health care performance improvement movement more than 30 years ago. Today, Press Ganey offers an integrated suite of solutions that enable enterprise transformation across the patient journey. Delivered through a cutting-edge digital platform built on a foundation of data security, Press Ganey solutions address safety, clinical excellence, patient experience and workforce engagement. The company works with more than 33,000 health care facilities in its mission to reduce patient suffering and enhance caregiver resilience to improve the overall safety, quality and experience of care.</p>
<p><b>
Position Description/Overview:</b></p>
<p>
Press Ganey currently has an exciting opportunity for a Product Manager in our Chicago, IL or Charlotte, NC office. Start the second half of 2019 by making an impact on transforming healthcare through data insights and analytics. The position will focus on expanded pulsing and feedback solutions for our Workforce clients including client self-service options and lifecycle surveys. The position will also focus on integrated analytics across Press Ganey solutions. The position will develop solutions to help clients understand the intersections of safety, quality, patient experience and caregiver engagement to prioritize efforts, reduce suffering, and meet patient and caregiver needs.</p>
<p>
The Product Manager is responsible for overseeing the successful development and execution of strategic Press Ganey products. The Product Manager will be part of a dynamic and fast-paced environment, working cross-functionally to design, build and bring to market products that deliver on the company’s vision and strategy.</p>
<p>
Product Management at Press Ganey is a team sport, with opportunities to work on many new or existing products and solutions across our businesses. Workforce, Patient Experience, and Integrated Analytics are growth markets which are part of our transformational solutions journey, and the product manager will be able to directly impact 2019 deliverables which improve the quality of care in leading hospital systems and medical centers nationwide.</p>
<p>
Our team focuses on driving outcomes – why do we do what we do, and how does it benefit patients, clinicians, or other key stakeholders. We incorporate the pragmatic marketing model and storytelling, with a sprinkling of agile, but we are far from a checkbox and buzzword environment. In fact, what distinguishes product management at Press Ganey is the shared strength of the entire team, all on the same mission, at every level of the organization.</p>
<p><b>
Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li>Gain a deep understanding of the market, competitive landscape, and client experiences.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop product strategy and roadmap by analyzing product performance, identifying and filling product gaps and generating new ideas that grow market share, improve client experience and drive growth.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Generate product buy-in with key stakeholders.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Translate product strategy into product requirements and prototypes.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Design, scope and prioritize requirements based on business and client impact.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work closely with Engineering to ensure product is brought to market according to business requirements and client expectations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Represent the company by visiting and meeting with clients to solicit feedback on products and services.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate with marketing and sales to develop product pricing, positioning, and promotional plans that are aligned with product strategy.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drive product launches with other key internal and external stakeholders.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide cross-functional project management oversight for product development initiatives, including pilot projects, product enhancements, and new product development.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul><li>4+ years of product management experience, preferably within healthcare.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience soliciting user and industry feedback: interviewing customers and non-customers, leading focus groups for product design/development.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with business cases, analytics and reporting, market trends/analysis, competition analysis, positioning, business modeling, and product roadmap development.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communication is critical; be able to tell your story effectively.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with customer/client management, and managing the tension between customers’ wants and needs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven ability to develop product strategies, including managing all aspects of a successful product through its lifecycle.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven problem solving ability, preferably within a customer environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven project management, time management and organizational skills and the ability to quickly adapt to multiple priorities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven ability to work collaboratively to ensure adequate resources are brought to bear to meet client needs. Put another way, the product team works at all levels of the organization; get used to player/coaches and servant leadership that drives for success.</li></ul>
<ul><li>The ideal candidate will be located in Chicago or Charlotte</li></ul>
<p><b>
Minimum Education:</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in a related field is required, MBA/MHA a plus.</li></ul>
