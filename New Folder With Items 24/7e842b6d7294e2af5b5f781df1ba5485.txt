<h2>Marketing Coordinator/Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Department</b>: Marketing
<br>
<b>Reports To</b>: Director of Marketing
<br>
<br>
Cornerstone Information Systems www.ciswired.com seeks a <b>Marketing Coordinator/Manager</b> for our corporate headquarters and sales support organizations. This position will be based in our Bloomington, IN headquarters.<br>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary</b></h3>
<br>
Cornerstone Information Systems, a growing technology company focused on the ever-changing dynamic travel industry, is looking for an experienced Marketing Coordinator/Manager. We’re searching for a highly qualified marketing person to work closely with our experts and customers to help define and communicate our collective vision for where the travel industry is going, and how we’re going to work together to get it there. From day one, this individual will contribute to existing marketing programs, while assisting with the development of new initiatives aligned with company and customer goals. This role will actively support our sales teams with revenue generating initiatives.
<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate will have prior experience with a wide range of marketing functions, including communications, advertising, branding, digital marketing, and social media. The marketing coordinator must be an organized multitasker able to handle many diverse projects at once, while ensuring projects get done on time and under budget.<br>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<br>
<i>
The responsibilities for the Marketing Coordinator/Manager will include but not be limited to participation in the following areas:</i>
<ul>
<li>Support the marketing function by scheduling and organizing the creation and deployment of digital-based content</li>
<li>
Prepare communications to clients in support of targeted marketing initiatives or to share our industry perspectives</li>
<li>
Solicit and summarize internal and external expert viewpoints and the latest industry news and report on emerging trends to management each month</li>
<li>
Work with cross-functional teams to coordinate day-to-day activities that lead to the completion of projects and tasks</li>
<li>
Provide direct support to the sales organization with product and company content, industry event coordination and client engagement activities.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Skills and Qualifications:</b></h3>
<b>
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing or advertising</li>
<li>
2+ years’ marketing, advertising or graphic design experience</li>
<li>
Knowledge of traditional marketing, digital marketing, content marketing, and social media marketing</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail and an ability to provide critical analysis of information</li>
<li>
Must be motivated and able to work with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Exceptional verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Travel, technology or related corporate experience is a plus</li>
<li>
Experience with Salesforce and related tools, such as Pardot, is a plus.</li>
</ul>
