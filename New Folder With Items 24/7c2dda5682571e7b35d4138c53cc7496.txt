<p>National Account Manager Job Description</p>
<p>Due to growth One Source Staffing and Labor is looking for the right person to join our team as an experienced National Account Manager. This person will be responsible for building and maintaining strong relationships with key accounts in the building restoration and commercial construction and Warehousing sectors to increase sales and profitability while seeking out and capturing profitable new business opportunities.</p>
<p>As a National Account Manager, you should be organized and have good negotiation and communication skills.In addition, you must be able to demonstrate an acute understanding of customer and client business needs in the Restoration/Construction industries to create and build long-term relationships that will achieve business goals.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<p>· Develop and maintain relationships with customers to ensure long-term successful partnerships</p>
<p>· Network with key customers to identify and address specific needs and opportunities to assist</p>
<p>· Act as the liaison between customers and internal teams ensuring clients’ requirements clearly defined and met</p>
<p>· Identify and attract prospective strategic customers</p>
<p>· Negotiate contracts and close new business</p>
<p>· Collect and analyze sales data and trends</p>
<p>· Maintain high customer satisfaction ratings, according to company standards</p>
<p>· Stay up-to-date with internal and external developments and suggest new ways to increase sales</p>
<p>Requirements</p>
<p>· Proven work experience and success as an Account Manager in a fast paced environment</p>
<p>· Solid experience in sales and customer service</p>
<p>· Demonstrable experience in negotiating and meeting client’s requirements</p>
<p>· In-depth understanding of sales performance metrics</p>
<p>· Hands on experience with CRM software and MS Excel</p>
<p>· Excellent analytical and organizational skills</p>
<p>· Strong communication skills and “Can Do” attitude</p>
<p>· Ability to lead and motivate team members to excel</p>
<p>· Availability to travel up to 30%</p>
<p>· Restoration /Construction experience Highly preferred</p>
