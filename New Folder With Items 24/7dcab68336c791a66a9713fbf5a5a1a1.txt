<h2>Storage Expert</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Storage Expert is one of the first line representatives to the property’s customers, assisting the Resident Storage Expert in the day-to-day operations of the property which include but are not limited to:
</p>
<p><i>Sales</i>
</p>
<ul><li>Achieve sales and revenue objectives by utilizing effective advertising/marketing opportunities, telephone and direct in -person interactions with potential customers
</li></ul>
<p><i>Customer Relations</i>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Promotes and maintains excellent customer relations by providing information and resolving problems effectively.
</li>
<li>Implements new ideas and concepts that would help enhance product services to both existing and potential customers.
</li>
<li>Resolves issues/problems in an effective, professional and positive manner.
</li>
<li>Represents the property and Company in a professional manner at all times.
</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Administration</i>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Performs all daily administrative functions as required to include but not limited to: receiving and properly processing all customer payments; making bank deposits; submitting and maintaining property trackers and other type reporting on a timely and as-needed basis; ensuring that all tenant files and all other property information is being properly maintained.
</li>
<li>Follows up on delinquent accounts in accordance to Company policy and state and local laws.
</li>
<li>Communicates operating results by submitting accurate and timely reports and furnishing financial information as requested.
</li>
<li>Maintains the highest level of professionalism while ensuring that the standards, philosophy and mission established by Company are met and carried out.
</li>
<li>Perform related duties as assigned by supervisor
</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Custodial and Maintenance</i>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Follows and implements the guidelines set forth in the Company Maintenance Checklist as is applicable to each specific property.
</li>
<li>Ensures that cleanliness and orderliness standards are met in the office, rest rooms, grounds and vacant units are cleaned on a timely basis.
</li>
<li>Regularly audits the physical conditions of a property and ensures that supplies are adequate
</li>
<li>Performs all light and minor maintenance and custodial duties in a method and environment that would help ensure the safety and well-being for team-members, customers and the general public.
</li>
</ul>
<p><i>L</i><i>ea</i><i>d</i><i>e</i><i>rship/Supervision</i>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains compliance with all company policies and procedures.
</li>
<li>Reports to: District Manager
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Essential Job Requirements</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in sales or retail environment preferred.
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills, including ability to effectively communicate with internal and external customers.
</li>
<li>Excellent computer proficiency (MS Office – Word, Excel and Outlook)
</li>
<li>Ability to work with minimum direct supervision.
</li>
<li>Must possess a valid driver’s license.
</li>
<li>Possess an outgoing and friendly personality and project a professional appearance.
</li>
<li>Well-balanced personal and managerial style: mature, professional approach to people and problems.
</li>
<li>Must be able to work under pressure and meet deadlines, while maintaining a positive attitude and pro viding exemplary customer service.
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and to carry out assignments to completion within parameters of instructions given, prescribed routines, and standard accepted practices.
</li>
<li>High school diploma or GED required.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Requirements</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to lift and carry up to 50 lbs.
</li>
<li>Must be able to stand for up to 8 hours at a time.
</li>
<li>Must be able to speak clearly and listen on telephone.
</li>
<li>Possess an outgoing and friendly personality and project a professional appearance.
</li>
<li>Ability to safely and successfully perform the essential job functions consistent with Company including meeting qualitative and/or quantitative productivity standards.
</li>
<li>Ability to maintain regular, punctual attendance consistent with Company and other federal, state and local standards</li>
</ul>
