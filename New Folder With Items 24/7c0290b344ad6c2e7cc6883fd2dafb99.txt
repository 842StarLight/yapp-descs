<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>The Company</b></p>
<p>
Staff Treats is a global leader dedicated to helping companies improve their workplace culture and transform their business environment through employee rewards and perks. We have an extensive client list that includes large financial institutions and global brands such as PepsiCo, Bank of America, Citibank, Visa and Staples. We're a fast paced, high-growth technology company with a positive, upbeat attitude looking for talented individuals to join our team in NYC.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role</b></p>
<p>
We are currently looking for a highly motivated, results-driven individual to join our Sales team. You don’t necessarily need to have many years of sales experience, but we are looking for the dedication and commitment to fast-tracking your career through developing our brand globally.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
You'll be responsible for actively seeking out and engaging with prospective clients to present our employee perks program to. You'll get to know our products and programs inside-out and offer prospective customers professional and clear insight into our SaaS software platform. Working to targets is a very important part of this role so being outcome focused is absolutely vital.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Candidates should be able to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Generate qualified meetings with prospective B2B clients for Staff Treat’s industry-leading HR software</li>
<li>Maintain all relevant information about clients, prospects, campaigns, and leads</li>
<li> Present, promote and sell programs/services using compelling language to C-Suite decision makers</li>
<li> Perform cost-benefit and needs analysis of existing/potential customers to meet their needs</li>
<li> Achieve agreed upon sales targets and outcomes</li>
<br>
</ul>
