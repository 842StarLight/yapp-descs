<b>The Role:</b><br>
This unique role blends the analytical and logical processing of sales compensation, with the fast pace and energy of the sales team. As a member of the commissions team, this role provides training and exposure to the in-demand field of Sales Comp, and high visibility and impact to executives carrying out the company strategy. The primary responsibilities of the role are the monthly processing and reporting of global commissions, fielding inquiries from the sales team, and managing our commissions software solution, Xactly. Additionally, there will be ad-hoc data analysis, systems management, and the need to partner with global sales leaders and executives. Success in this role will require an analytical and creative approach to problem solving, and the ability to communicate with clarity. These traits, plus a curiosity about sales compensation will make the ideal candidate.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Gain and maintain a deep understanding of the compensation plans, ForgeRock's strategy, products, and sales team structure. These will be guiding principles for the role.</li>
<li>Monthly Reconciliation of closed deals from Salesforce.com to Xactly, processing data within Xactly, and reviewing output for accuracy and adherence to the comp plan.</li>
<li>Preparing commission reports for stakeholders in accounting, finance and payroll.</li>
<li>Maintaining employee data in Xactly for new hires, terminations, and role changes.</li>
<li>Additional ad-hoc analysis and reporting. Commissions are high-visibility and impact, and often of interest to executives who need insights and data to support strategic decision making.</li>
<li>Provide timely, accurate and professional resolution of commission inquiries from the field, and process adjustments as necessary. Be a resource and valued partner to employees who receive sales compensation.</li>
<li>Support the annual sales compensation planning process. Creating compensation plans that align sales incentives with the company go-to-market strategy is key to the success of Forgerock.</li>
<li>Improve existing processes through creative problem solving and use of automation.</li>
<li>Collaborate and partner across functions to find synergies and strengthen the commission team's alignment with Forgerock initiates, strategy and goals.</li>
<li>Manage additional ad-hoc projects, such as software configuration, implementation, and more.</li>
<li>Involvement in Stock Comp (options) administration, inquiries and analysis.</li>
</ul>
