<b>Primary Duties:</b><br>
The Annual Giving Officer works to promote university fundraising priorities to alumni, parents, and friends. Reporting to the AVP for University Advancement, the incumbent is responsible for identification, cultivation, solicitation, and stewardship of donors and new prospects for gifts in the “leadership annual giving” ($1,500 - $24,999) range. Incumbent will be responsible for initiating relationships with new donor prospects for Chico State University by assessing giving capacity, affinity, and inclination through face-to-face visits. Traveling 7-12 days each month, the incumbent will participate as a member of the University Advancement division in the overall execution of a comprehensive development program with a particular focus on new prospect identification, qualification and project interest referral.<br>
<br>
The incumbent will be responsible for meeting agreed upon fundraising goals and metrics as they relate to the strategic needs of the University. Performance, as measured against these goals, will be evaluated annually.<br>
