<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>Clubessential provides cloud-based software solutions to the private club markets and is an Inc. 5000 fastest growing company. We are financially backed by Battery Ventures, a top-tier technology investment firm, helping fuel our current and future growth. We are a lean, fast-paced, passionate team of professionals who thrive on delivering exceptional products and service to our clients and teammates. Our dedication to client success through technology innovation, industry expertise, exceptional customer support and beautiful design has helped us attract the business of some of the top clubs in the country including Hilton, Four Seasons, Marriott, Valhalla Golf Club, Congressional Country Club, Pinehurst, and over 1,400 other clients.</p>
<p><b>Job Overview</b></p>
<div>As the first unified payments solution provider in the private club industry, Clubessential is seeking a Solutions Consultant to work with our sales team with a focus on increasing and accelerating our CE Payments product growth, including both online payments (ACH and credit card), as well as card present solutions. Successful consultants will demonstrate passion for the payments industry, as well as a desire to partner with leaders in the club industry to modernize a club's payments platform, and reduce the overhead associated with traditional third party payment processing solutions.</div>
<br>
<div>The Solutions Consultant - CE Payments will primarily sell merchant payment processing (including ACH and credit cards) and the corresponding software to our clients in the private club industry. Most of the work will be in the office participating in sales calls and providing comparative analysis. This role is responsible for the facilitation of merchant service business opportunities through B2B outreach.</div>
<br>
<div><b>The successful candidate will:</b></div>
 <ul>
<li>Be responsible for the facilitation of merchant service business opportunities through B2B outreach.</li>
<li>Understand the cost structures of merchant services</li>
<li>Develop profitable new business account relationships and increased profitability from sales calls to new and existing accounts.</li>
<li>Identify business opportunities based on knowledge of clients, markets, products, and services</li>
<li>Make sales presentations to existing and prospective clients informing them of the benefits of using the organization’s products and services to meet their needs.</li>
<li>Provide comparative analysis of customer’s current provider(s) and prepares a business case</li>
<li>Provide market feedback to product management and participates in weekly meetings</li>
<li>Highly motivated, outgoing individual with a proven sales background</li>
<li>A strong negotiator and the ability to adapt to customer needs to close deals</li>
