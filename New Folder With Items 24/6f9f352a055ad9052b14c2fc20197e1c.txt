<h2>Transportation & Logistics Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Transportation and Logistics Specialist will utilize software to enhance the efficiency of our sales and operations teams. This person will specialize in truck layouts on a daily basis. They will update and maintain customer and driver databases in routing software. The Transportation and Logistics Specialist will work with routing software to explore opportunities to enhance the efficiency of our delivery routes. They will assist in the daily routing of delivery trucks and holiday routing.</p>
