<p><b>Senior Business Analyst </b>sought by Sunquest Information Systems, Inc. in Tucson, AZ. 40 hrs/wk.</p>
<p>
Duties: Create and continuously improve complex reports and analysis of key performance metrics; Obtain necessary data required to complete analysis in support of key strategic initiatives; Identify, research, and report on the causes for fluctuations outside of anticipated parameters; Works with leadership to explain key discoveries, builds action plans, and drives them to completion; Identify key measurements that will impact the overall company performance and implement methods to drive into operations; Develop, deploy and maintain forecasting methodologies to ensure accurate projections for Solution Adoption teams; Mentor and guide other business analysts; Lead initiatives centered on the continuous improvement of key business systems; Lead initiatives centered on the improvement of key business practices and processes; All other duties and responsibilities as assigned including significant ad hoc analysis.</p>
<p>
Min. Requirements: Bachelor's degree or higher in management information systems, accounting, finance, economics, science, or mathematics and 5 years of experience in business analysis or equivalent which included reporting, data mining, reading and interpreting financial statements and identifying trend analysis.</p>
<p>
Skills: 5 years of experience in healthcare software industry; 5 years of predictive analytics which include creation of 12 to 60 month forward looking sales models; 5 years of advanced proficiency using SQL and advanced excel capabilities; 5 years of sales compensation modeling and variable compensation plan administration additional experience using salesforce, adaptive Insights, Softrax, or Netsuite Openair; 5 years advanced proficiency with Microsoft office (Word, Powerpoint, Outlook); 5 years proficiency with project management techniques required; 5 years of database administration and information system configuration experience required.</p>
