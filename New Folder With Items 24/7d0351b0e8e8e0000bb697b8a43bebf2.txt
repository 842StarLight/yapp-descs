<p><b>Customer Service QA Specialist</b></p>
<p><b>Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Customer Service QA Specialist performs a wide variety of phone and administrative duties. The Customer Service QA Specialist conducts follow up calls with our customers to provide feedback and addresses issues with the responsible people. The ideal candidate will have customer service experience, be motivated, dedicated, and a team player. In addition, they will have a high attention to detail and a genuine care to his/her work craft. Most importantly, the ideal candidate will be able to maintain a high level of business ethics.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include but are not limited to the following:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Contact customers, ask them to share their experience, document the conversation, and report back to senior management</li>
<li>Telephone/Email/Text scheduling and appointment coordination for sales resp to go back on a second visit</li>
<li>Identify the root causes of customer dissatisfaction and address those issues to the responsible people, follow up the actions</li>
<li>Answer web-submissions emails and phone messages promptly</li>
<li>Input large amounts of data accurately into Excel and SalesForce</li>
<li>Other tasks as requested by management</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and resolve customer service inquiries while building a relationship with the customer</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and/or Work Experience Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1+ years experience in a Customer Service role</li>
<li>Microsoft Excel &amp; Word</li>
<li>Experience with SalesForce a plus</li>
<li>MUST be conversational, with a strong grasp of English, both verbal and written</li>
<li>Ability to schedule meetings, manage incoming and outgoing phone calls and listen to voicemail</li>
<li>Ability to learn and apply new software &amp; programs</li>
</ul>
