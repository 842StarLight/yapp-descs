<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b><i>Edible Westchester</i></b> is a quarterly lifestyle magazine focused on local food, drink, travel, entertainment, and outdoor living. Produced by a small team of creatives, <i>Edible Westchester</i> is the go-to publication and social brand in the region, with the most engaged digital audience and sought after print content among competitors.</p>
<p>We are currently seeking a <b>Sales Representative</b> to join our team. As a Sales Representative for <i>Edible Westchester</i> magazine, you will be a part of a team that sells print and digital advertising to the variety of local businesses and partners in an assigned territory of the region.</p>
<p><b><i>Responsibilities</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Service existing accounts, obtaining orders, and establishing new accounts by calling on existing or potential accounts in an assigned territory</li>
<li>Focus sales efforts by studying existing accounts and past issues of <i>Edible Westchester. </i>Pitch and adjust content of sales presentations through the study of accounts</li>
<li>Submit orders by referring to rate card and media kit</li>
<li>Keep management informed by submitting monthly activity-results reports and territory analyses</li>
<li>Help to monitor competitive publications by gathering current marketplace information on pricing, products, new products, and accounts</li>
<li>Recommend changes in products, services, and policies by evaluating results and competitive developments</li>
<li>Resolve customer complaints by investigating problems, developing solutions, and making recommendations to management</li>
<li>Attend networking events within your assigned territory</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Qualifications &amp; </i></b><b><i>Requirements</i></b></p>
<p>-Part-time commitment, average 20-25 hours per week</p>
<p>-Have access to a vehicle with the ability to travel locally</p>
<p>-Have access to a laptop and smartphone</p>
<p>-Strong organizational skills necessary to multitask, and meet deadlines and quotas</p>
<p>-Some sales experience or a Bachelors in business preferred</p>
<p><b><i>Opportunities</i></b></p>
<p>- Receive commission for all sales generated</p>
<p>- Maintain a flexible schedule with the ability to work from home</p>
<p><b><i>About Edible Westchester</i></b></p>
<p>We dig deep to find the sustenance, from root to fruit. <i>Edible Westchester</i> is the first magazine committed to telling the stories behind what Westchester eats and drinks. From chefs and brewers to farmers and foragers, we celebrate the deliciousness that our land and people produce. Distillers. Restaurateurs. Cheesemakers. Butchers. Craftsmen.</p>
<p>Everything delicious, from the Hudson to the Sound.</p>
<p><i>Edible Westchester</i> joins a James Beard Award–winning network of about 100 locally owned Edible websites and magazines stretching across North America, each devoted to connecting geographic communities with the unique food and drink of their land. Our print edition will soon be available by subscription and at select distribution spots around the county. Please visit us at ediblewestchester.com, and keep in touch on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram—and check out our sister publication, <i>Edible Hudson</i><i>Valley</i>, too.</p>
