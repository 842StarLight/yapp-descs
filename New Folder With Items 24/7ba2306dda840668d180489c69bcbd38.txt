Job Description<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>HPe design architect requires a strong competence of HPe Storage, Compute, and Networking technology acumen while being able to well articulate the benefits. The ability to meet with customer technology leaders while providing educational guidance and solution selling requires a unique background to learn and a polish to be able to present to clients. This role will be integral in suggesting and helping design storage, data center infrastructure, and HPe compute and network solutions. This role will partner with the sales organization to identify new sales opportunities to present and sell HPe engineered solutions (HW/SW and some services). This candidate will work mainly with our sellers, partners and customers in an office setting with some travel to client sites required.</p>
<p>
HPe Mase Level Certification – This is HPE’s most esteemed and highly respected credential. It validates you have reached the pinnacle of Hewlett Packard Enterprise certifications. The Master ASE has rich technical skills in advanced enterprise solutions combined with IT business acumen. This certification level tells management, executives, and peers that you can align IT solutions to an organization’s financial and business goals. It provides assurance that you can design and implement complex solutions, from proposal stage to live deployment. It validates that you can use the full spectrum of Hewlett Packard Enterprise technology, solutions and services.</p>
<br>
