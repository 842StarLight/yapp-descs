<p>Description</p>
<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<p>1. Improve the Company’s market position and achieve financial growth.</p>
<p>
2. Support the growth and business development of the pipeline and pipeline facilities inspection, integrity, and non-destructive examination services for assigned area.</p>
<p>
3. Actively prospect new pipeline customers in the Midstream, Public Utility, and E&amp;PC business sectors.</p>
<p>
4. Cold call and research potential customers to identify decision makers, develop rapport and set-up meetings.</p>
<p>
5. Develop, organize, participate in and may lead customer meetings and presentations,</p>
<p>
6. Collect customer intelligence, insights, and work as part of an integrated team to create new business opportunities; participate in the customer bidding process.</p>
<p>
7. Coordinate business opportunities, contacts and services across the Business Development Team.</p>
<p>
8. Protect organization's value by keeping information confidential and current in Customer Relationship Management tool.</p>
<p>
9. Participate as part of an account management team with existing customers.</p>
<p>
10. Participate and actively work industry functions, such as association events and conferences, to cultivate new business opportunities and contacts.</p>
<p>
11. Submit weekly progress reports and ensure data is accurate; forecast sales targets and ensure they are met; track and record activity on accounts and contacts to meet targets.</p>
<p>
12. Provide intelligence on potential opportunities to the Cypress Corporate Development Department</p>
