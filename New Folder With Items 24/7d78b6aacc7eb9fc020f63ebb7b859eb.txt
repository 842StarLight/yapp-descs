<h2>Operations Manager Disaster Restoration</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Quality First Builders, LLC/QFB Restoration has been one of the top property restoration companies in central Florida since 1992. QFB provides restoration services to local home and business owners who have suffered property damage resulting from water, fire, mold, wind, or other catastrophic related events. Utilizing the most innovative tools of the trade, partnered with experience and sincere empathy toward each situation encountered makes QFB the top choice for most customers.</p>
<p>
QFB is seeking an Operations Manager with experience overseeing and leading a team of restoration professionals with a focus on increasing production efficiency, completing projects on time, maintaining budgets, and exceeding customer’s expectations.</p>
<p><b>
Job Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervise and lead a team of 15+ employees</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assist with recruiting, onboarding, training and mentoring</li>
<li>Provide expert advice, continuous support, and motivation to the team</li>
<li>Oversee scheduling of projects, delivery of services, and compliance</li>
<li>Conduct production meetings, review financial reports, and perform site inspections</li>
<li>Create and review scopes of work for accuracy and profitability</li>
<li>Actively monitor job costs and receivables</li>
<li>Maintain a "big picture" strategic view with an ability to solve problems</li>
<li>Review and translate policies into daily routine operations</li>
<li>Participate in “On Call” rotation program</li>
</ul> <p><b>Qualifications</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 10 years of experience in construction management</li>
<li>Broad business experience across multiple functions: Sales, Operations, Administration, Accounting, and Human Resources</li>
<li>Proficient in Xactimate software</li>
<li>Knowledge of Symbility, Time &amp; Materials, Dash, and other industry related software is preferred</li>
<li>Experience with preferred vendor programs (Alacrity, Contractor Connection, Nexxus, Sedgwick, etc.)</li>
<li>Knowledge of construction and understanding of building code</li>
<li>Strong writing and communication abilities</li>
<li>Ability to analyze financial reports and use the information to efficiently manage operations</li>
<li>Flexible schedule to support team, especially during CAT events such as hurricanes</li>
<li>Must have reliable means of transportation</li>
<li>IICRC certifications preferred</li>
<li>Spanish language skills are helpful, but not required</li>
<li>Must pass background and drug tests and carry acceptable driving record</li>
<li>Support company’s mission, vision, and values:</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Mission</b></p>
<p>
Provide emergency, mitigation and restoration services to our customers during a time of crisis and to exceed customer expectations with a focus on compassion, urgency, and by treating every home or business as if it were our own.</p>
<p><b>
Vision</b></p>
<p>
To be the benchmark for restoration and mitigation services in Central Florida while remaining on the cutting edge of technology, exceeding customer expectations, and building credibility within our industry.</p>
<p><b>
Values</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Mentoring - Taking an active interest in the growth, development, and success of others</li>
<li>Integrity - “Walk the talk”; authenticity in decisions and actions; open about failures</li>
<li>Caring - Genuine interest in others that comes from inner values</li>
<li>Truth - Courageous conversation; speaking openly; minimizing politics; willingness to disclose; truth controls actions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in construction, business, or similar major is preferred</li>
<li>10+ years related experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits &amp; Perks:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive salary with bonus based on performance matrix</li>
<li>Vehicle allowance, fuel card, cell phone, and laptop provided</li>
<li>Medical insurance with 50% of employee premium paid by the company</li>
<li>Optional dental, vision, and other supplemental insurance</li>
<li>Two weeks minimum paid vacation and nine paid holidays per year</li>
<li>401k with employer match and profit-sharing contribution after 1 year of service</li>
<li>Ongoing training and development</li>
<li>Business casual work environment</li>
</ul> <p>We are an EOE employer</p>
