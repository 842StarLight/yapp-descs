<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details
</b></h2>
<p>Salesforce.org is building a brand new social impact platform to engage an army of citizen philanthropists. We are seeking a Senior Product Manager to help build from the ground up as we embark on building the next generation of technology for nonprofits and their corporate partners.
<br>
We are looking for a Senior Product Manager who is very technical, highly product -oriented, and business driven to help us realize our vision. You constantly think about how to engage users in meaningful experiences, customer success, product strategy, and how to grow our business at a rapid pace. As a member of the founding team for a new area of our business, you will be required to wear multiple hats to help us develop and take to market the best platform and applications for a very large market.
<br>
Design for Scale - Design, implement and ship features that can scale to hundreds of millions of users;
<br>
Design for People - Lead feature design with a major focus on creating engaging experiences for people
<br>
API First - Have a passion for enabling an open ecosystem of partners
<br>
Think Mobile - Adopt, embrace, and promote agile and test-driven practices;
<br>
Drive Velocity - Value team excellence over individual productivity;
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Drive the vision, product strategy, and partner strategy to build significant value including native mobile apps or a portal for nonprofits and educational institutions to maximize value in our philanthropy network
</li>
<li>Gather user requirements; create and refine requirements in the form of user stories
</li>
<li>Work with developers to convert requirements into a technical design
</li>
<li>Serve as the single point of contact and subject-matter expert for the product and business teams using the product
</li>
<li>Collaborate with stakeholders to craft product roadmap and prioritize backlog
</li>
<li>Motivate development team with clear, elevating goals for product development
</li>
<li>Win the hearts and minds of every user with the most engaging, fast, and fun ways to engage in their passions
</li>
<li>Drive product launches and continuous engagement with Analysts and Press to keep the drumbeat and awareness of our product vision and releases. We define and lead the market.
</li>
<li>Work with VP of Product Management and with Product Marketing to own the messaging, pricing/packaging, demo, and all sales content such as competitive analysis/First Call Deck/etc for our products
</li>
<li>Synthesize diverse sets of input and information to drive (and own) product decisions
</li>
<li>Above all else, doing all the little things that drive the team to continuously iterate and improve the product
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Desired Qualifications
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Customer driven and customer facing product management experience
</li>
<li>Competitive, Driven, and want to build the best products in the industry. To be second is not good enough
</li>
<li>3+ years experience building nonprofit technology in giving, volunteering, or grants
</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in Product Management, Systems/Business Analysis, Software Engineering or related field
</li>
<li>Deep understanding of global nonprofit vetting, onboarding, and disbursement processes
</li>
<li>Solid hands-on experience building user stories and working closely with development teams and user experience teams.
</li>
<li>Ability to formulate and articulate strategy/vision and effectively drive consensus across the organization
</li>
<li>Must work extremely well with Engineering, Product Marketing, Partner and User Experience teams
</li>
<li>Excellent project management skills and a positive attitude
</li>
<li>Proven leadership, prioritization, and analytical skills
</li>
</ul>
