<h2>Project Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Project Accountant performs a variety of tasks related to the maintenance and processing of project cost and payment records, including providing overall support to operations staff with financial details.</p>
<p><b>
Essential duties + responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform responsibilities with excellence in service, integrity and building relationships as exemplified in DAVIS core values</li>
<li>Submit Subcontractor Request For Payments (RFP’s) to operations via workflow</li>
<li>Maintain vendor compliances with corporate and project specific contractual requirements (including pay application, insurance certificate, payment &amp; performance bond, change orders, stored material documents)</li>
<li>Follow-up with vendors as needed for appropriate compliance documentation</li>
<li>Analyze sales and use tax on Purchase Agreements and provide necessary reconciliations</li>
<li>Process subcontractor payments</li>
<li>Coordinate proper communication between operations, subcontractors and client personnel</li>
<li>Prepare owner and subcontractor lien releases</li>
<li>Create over / under billing analysis and identify causes of over / under billings</li>
<li>Create final project closeout report and profit analysis for Vice President review/approval</li>
<li>Participate in financial statement audit</li>
<li>Assist in client audit preparation</li>
<li>Assist Operations with monthly owner billing process</li>
<li>Create project savings / write down analysis</li>
<li>Monitor job cost details, proposing adjustments as needed to operations</li>
<li>Assist operations with Project Status Report (PSR) preparation</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
