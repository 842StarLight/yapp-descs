<div><b>Overview</b></div>
<div>
SmartBear helps the world’s most recognized companies develop the world’s best applications. SmartBear has played a role in the ride you ordered, the banking app you use, the item you posted for auction, or that flight you just purchased. SmartBear's award-winning suite of testing software enables organizations to meet frequent release deadlines without sacrificing software quality, all while reducing testing costs. <div>Due to massive growth and market success, SmartBear is continuing to hire employees rapidly to scale our company globally.<b> </b>We are looking for a B2B marketing expert to play a vital management role on our marketing team.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We are seeking an experienced SEO (Search Engine Optimization) Manager who will be responsible for managing and implementing on-the-page and off-the-page SEO strategies for our various websites, while also providing SEO guidance to marketing partners and agencies. The SEO Manager should have a combination of technical, content and analytical skills in order to increase organic search volume, visits and demand. The SEO Manager will partner with internal stakeholders and external agencies to develop, execute, monitor, analyze and continually improve all facets of our search initiatives while keeping the larger business goals in mind. This role will draw actionable conclusions from internal and external sources that can be measured and reported on, and will work under the direction of the Senior Manager of Digital Marketing.</div>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Consistently monitor performance and business metrics to understand organic search performance both site-wide and by business area and sales segment</li>
<li>
Understand and asses each business area’s needs as related to Search, and advise on how to best employ specific search engine optimization techniques while balancing overall holistic SEO</li>
<li>
Identify site-wide growth opportunities in core areas such as site infrastructure, website functionality &amp; usability, and technical SEO</li>
<li>
Work closely with our product marketing, demand generation and web and creative teams to deploy SEO strategy and tactics
</li>
<li>Work with agency of record to provide monthly reporting and SEO health checks, and be able to develop actionable insights from the data</li>
<li>
Partner with and manage relationships with external vendors</li>
<li>
Monitor daily performance metrics of organic search, optimization efforts and quickly respond to deviations</li>
<li>
Recommend and integrate the latest search engine optimization techniques</li>
<li>
Perform competitive analysis</li>
<li>
Help manage our Google Tag Manager property in order to measure SEO performance (absence of tracking)</li>
<li>
Prepare and communicate regular and ad hoc reporting on SEO performance and its impact on business metrics</li>
</ul></div>
