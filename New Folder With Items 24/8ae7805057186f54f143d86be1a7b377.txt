<h2>Digital Marketing Director - Maritz Corporate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>Maritz is a people company, grounding everything we do in the scientific study of human behavior. With 125 years of experience in human motivation and memorable experiences, Maritz knows what drives people. Familiar with the iconic tradition of the gold watch for retirement? Maritz invented that in 1929. Today, we continue to be a leading provider of incentive, reward, events and experience, helping half the Fortune 100 firms bring out the best in their stakeholders, through a deep understanding of what makes people tick.
<br>
<br>
But have you ever heard of Maritz? Our story has only begun to be told.
<br>
<br>
We are on the verge of a digital transformation. We’re counting on your immense passion for integrating big picture, data-driven thinking to sharpen our digital strategy and direction.
<br>
<br>
You possess a strong understanding of the digital landscape’s current and forthcoming capabilities, including best practices and emerging industry trends as it pertains to website UX, online search, paid social media campaigns, digital display (including retargeting), online video, audio / voice recording, podcasting, and e-mail. You know how to design platforms and campaigns that drive behavior. You harbor a passion for persuasion and influence.
<p></p>
<p><i>Summary:
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><i>We are looking for a qualified and enthusiastic Digital Marketing Director to take the lead in developing successful, long-term digital marketing strategies for Maritz. The Digital Marketing Director is in charge of developing an online marketing strategy and leading the implementation of that strategy centered on the objectives set by the Brand + Reputation Department.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><i>As the Digital Marketing Director, you have the opportunity to define our brand’s digital marketing strategy with a focus on expanding our digital community, presence and content across all key online channels. This role is responsible for the development and execution of the company’s digital marketing plans that drive awareness and brand initiatives. </i>You would be responsible for:
</p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Maritz.com, the MyMaritz intranet, and PeopleScience.com, leading an overhaul and redesign of the Maritz digital ecosystem leveraging behavioral insights and digital UX design.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Supporting Maritz’ business units as a center of excellence for constant internal solution platform testing, behavioral UX consulting, and digital marketing efficiencies.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Measuring cross-business unit employee engagement.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><i>The Digital Marketing Director will work closely with the Senior Director of Brand + Reputation, Director of Communications, the Marketing Support Manager and the Creative Manager to establish goals for the company’s digital marketing efforts, working alongside a team of talented marketing professionals and external partners in each of Maritz’ business units to identify and implement the strategies and tactics that support these goals.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><i>As a Digital Marketing Director, you should have in-depth marketing experience and a passion for continuous learning in ever-evolving digital technologies and best practices. You should have knowledge/passion for behavioral design-thinking and A/B testing. You’re ready to lead the transformation of all Maritz’ digital communication channels. You will journey-map, A/B test, and design customer and employee touchpoints with behavioral science principles in mind.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Through close work with different teams, you ensure consistency through all digital marketing channels and provide future-thinking ideas to build and maintain a strong company web presence.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Ultimately, you drive all digital marketing efforts to achieve our business objectives.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Reporting and Working Relationships:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Reports to Senior Director, Brand + Reputation. Works with corporate and business unit executive leadership as well as sales, marketing, market intelligence and operations teams. Works closely with senior levels within the organization, including VPs of marketing and sales, as well as technical professionals, including IT and web developers. Partners with internal stakeholders to move marketing and communications initiatives forward for Maritz.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Partner with cross-functional internal and external teams of designers, writers, and media managers.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Work closely with Social Media Manager, Director of Communications, Creative Manager, and Marketing Support Manager to clearly communicate strategic priorities and ensure key creative deliverables are built to perform against the Sr. Director’s strategic vision.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary Job Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>60% </b><b>Digital Strategy &amp; Execution: </b>Serve as a digital marketing and communications expert, counseling and collaborating with marketing and communications professionals within Maritz businesses. Research best practices in-and-amongst our competitors’ marketing space and make appropriate recommendations based on key business objectives. Defines and develops roadmaps, project requirements, status, process and performance for building, optimizing and growing web surfaces to support business goals. Help to ensure websites, marketing campaigns and collateral are aligned with brand standards. Lead the activation of content, design and functionality strategies. Evaluate, select and manage appropriate outside experts, vendors and partners to assist businesses with digital marketing efforts (e.g. SEO, web and marketing agencies and freelancers). Ensure measurable milestones, metrics and KPIs are implemented, tracked and aligned to business goals and objectives. Create strategies for effectively influencing Maritz’ key stakeholders (clients, prospects, employees, communities) via web, social media and other digital efforts. Develop content for websites, company intranet, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, blogs. Oversee the creation and adherence to social media content calendars.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>20% Digital Transformation: </b>Oversee a complete overhaul and redesign of Maritz.com – the first point of contact for the majority of visitors to the Maritz ecosystem. Additionally, overhaul and redesign the MyMaritz internal intranet. Maintain and support PeopleScience.com. Manage the Maritz and PeopleScience social media channels and blogs. Apply expertise in digital behavioral design to optimize our digital ecosystem with behavioral insights. Ensure all sites and communication channels are capable of frequent A/B testing for optimization and behavioral insight. A/B test to ensure employment of the behavioral sciences’ best practices by enlisting the work of in-house scientists, as well as a mix of design-thinking and UX as it pertains to developing and executing a 100% on-brand approach to all communication touchpoints set by the Brand + Reputation department.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>20% </b><b>Behavioral UX Center of Excellence: </b>Serve as support for Maritz’ business units marketing leads, particularly in behavioral design and UX. Traffic SEM keywords centrally to ensure efficiency and avoid duplicative bidding. Assist Sr. Director of Brand + Reputation to better understand how to utilize Maritz’ own solutions to better serve the corporate development teams, as well as each business unit by applying a “drink our own champagne” approach, implementing and iterating on the Maritz business units’ solutions for our own sites (e.g. employing employee recognition, enlisting survey administration, and creating and maintaining internal event registrations).
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications
</p>
<p>1. BA/BS degree or equivalent work experience
</p>
<p>2. Minimum of 5-7 years in digital marketing, managing paid search, display and paid social advertising, with defined KPIs
</p>
<p>3. Highly motivated, resourceful problem solver with strong analytical, verbal, and written communication skills
</p>
<p>4. Ability to work well cross-functionally with sales, sales ops, communications and creative teams, vendors and agencies to execute high quality programs on time
</p>
<p>5. Proficiency with marketing technology (Salesforce, marketing automation, Google Analytics, ABM)
</p>
<p>6. Excellent project management, interpersonal and organizational skills
</p>
<p>7. Proven experience in using data to make informed decisions
</p>
<p>8. Proven track record of analytical rigor around marketing campaigns, optimizing for key funnel metrics and driving for continuous improvement
</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in digital UX and behavioral design.
</li>
<li>Passion for behavioral science and psychology; familiarity with behavioral principles and capacity for recognizing and communicating their application in business contexts.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Location </b>: St. Louis, MO
</p>
