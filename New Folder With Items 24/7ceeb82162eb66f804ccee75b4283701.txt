<h2>Delivery Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements:</b>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><b>Education:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li><p>College Degree preferred, High School Diploma or GED a minimum
</p></li></ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><b>Experience:</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Mechanically inclined; Technical, mechanical use of hand tools
</p></li>
<li><p>2 years’ customer service experience
</p></li>
<li><p>On the job use of computer and software, proficient in Microsoft Office software
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience driving box trucks is a plus
</p></li>
<li><p>Possess a valid Oklahoma Driver’s License, be at least 21 and have reliable transportatio
</p></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions:</b>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Ensures safe and timely delivery of equipment to clients, assists in set-up of equipment, coordinating with IT,networking staff and sales team
</p></li>
<p></p>
<li><p>Follows established safety procedures for loading and unloading equipment
</p></li>
<li><p>Performs operational checks and assists with entering basic networking settings
</p></li>
<li><p>Receive inspect and check in all shipping and freight
</p></li>
<li><p>Unbox, tag, group and stage equipment by sales order for set up per sales order
</p></li>
<li><p>Completes and maintains appropriate paperwork as required for each specific task
</p></li>
<li><p>Operates company equipment in a safe and responsible manner (includes forklift, stair climber, dollies &amp; vehicles)
</p></li>
<li><p>Safeguards inventory and all personnel involved around the work area; maintains warehouse &amp; office in a clean &amp; orderly fashion
</p></li>
<li><p>Utilizes business process management software to receive freight and various other inventory control functions<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills, Knowledge &amp; Abilities</b>:<br>
The person capable of excelling in this position must:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Personify friendliness and exhibit ability to get along with others
</p></li>
<li><p>Possess exceptional organizational skills
</p></li>
<li><p>Be proficient with Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook
</p></li>
<li><p>IT literate, capable of quickly learning new software
</p></li>
<li><p>Demonstrate working knowledge of inventory flow dynamics
</p></li>
<li><p>Demonstrate a desire / urgency to accomplish tasks set before him
</p></li>
<li><p>Possess the interpersonal skills necessary to communicate effectively with all levels of associates and clients
</p></li>
<li><p>Communicate clearly in verbal and written English<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Requirements</b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Be able to stand and move freely, and sit for prolonged periods of time
</p></li>
<li><p>Be able to lift heavy objects from floor to over waist heights (up to 50 pounds)
</p></li>
<li><p>Be able to carry heavy objects up and down stairs
</p></li>
<li><p>No visible tattoos or piercings, except for ear piercings for women, that cannot be covered by appropriate business attire during work hours.<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Other Details:</b>
</p>
<p>Wage: Commensurate with experience
</p>
<p>Benefits: Made available after 90 days. Health, Dental, 401K
</p>
<p>Start Date: Immediately<br>
</p>

Submit application and resume.</p>
