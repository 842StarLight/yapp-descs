<p>Recognized as a #1 Top Workplace by the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette, Mid-Atlantic Surgical Systems is a regional agency for Arthrex (www.arthrex.com), a leading global medical device company specializing in orthopedics.</p>
<p>We are currently seeking a Senior Data Analyst to lead all reporting and analysis functions. This vital role works directly with company leadership and requires a high degree of accuracy, personal integrity, and professionalism. As the Senior Data Analyst, your daily work will make a meaningful impact on company success. Advanced Excel skills, including pivot tables, conditional formatting, and formulas, will be critical to success along with a willingness to embrace new technology to continuously improve the overall business.</p>
<p>The biggest attribute of our company is the positive, competitive and caring culture. This is the perfect opportunity for someone who wants to advance their career with fast growing company that is forecasted to double in size over the next five years. Learn more about Mid-Atlantic Surgical Systems at www.masurgical.com.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Build, maintain, and trouble-shoot sales compensation reporting systems</li>
<li>Support yearly compensation plan updates in quotas, rates, new hires, terminations and new plan implementation</li>
<li>Prepare monthly financial expense reports, along with recommendations for continuous improvement</li>
<li>Analyze systems and data to reduce inventory consignment fees, freight cost, and expired inventory</li>
<li>Proactively and continuously identify key data analysis projects</li>

</ul>
