<p><b>Primary Objective of Position:</b><br>

The Service Assistant is primarily required to handle the administrative function that impact the financial aspects of the field service area, which supports internal and external customers, as well as partners, to maintain good financial outcomes for the company.</p>

<li> Audit and confirm external service providersâ€™ time sheets and third-party cost breakdowns.</li>
