<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
The Cloud Channel Manager (CCM) wins, maintains, and expands relationships with current and prospective partners to assist in selling next generation solutions through TBI's vendor relationships. Primary sales activity will be focused on acquisition of new revenue from existing partners along with the recruitment of new-age partners that specialize in IT Transformation / Digital Transformation, such as MSP's, ISV's, VARS. The Cloud Channel Manager will provide additional support for these partners on an ongoing basis to develop the business strategically, with alignment of the TBI resources. CCM will be closely aligned with TBI's focus and culture on next generation technology promotion, and primarily focus on Hybrid Cloud, UC/CC, Security and SD-WAN as a primary focus. Secondary focus includes Mobility, Network and Traditional Voice Solutions.
<br><br>
The CCM will be the TBI Champion out in the field, promoting marketplace trends and how we help partners with sales enablement.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Recruits and collaborates with partners to achieve recruitment and sales goals</li>
<li>Creates and executes business plans as well as monthly/quarterly revenue targets with top 10 partners</li>
<li>Contacts each partner monthly at a minimum to consult on expanding business opportunities</li>
<li>Meets with the Director of Sales monthly to review partner outreach, sales metrics, and strategic opportunities</li>
<li>Achieves quarterly margin quota with an eye towards increase in cloud, UC/CC, SD-WAN and Security sales.</li>
<li>Perform on-going trainings for partners on latest marketplace trends with a focus on business impacts</li>
<li>Reports on progress of each newly recruited partner for quotes and orders</li>
<li>Consistent self-education on the latest in Channel to continually be able to apply value to discussions.</li>
<li>Attends TBI Sales Summit quarterly</li>
<li>Overnight travels approximately 20% of the time / Spends minimum 20% of time out in the field with partners</li>
<li>Works with TBI Marketing to develop go-to-market strategies with partners, with key vendors and alignment, which include campaigns and events.</li>
<li>Works with TBI Training to develop curriculums for key partners that require additional knowledge on specific technology subjects</li>
<li>Works with TBI Solution Engineers/ SME's during pre-sales scoping, through meetings via phone or in-person, to apply technical resources where needed.</li>
<li>Works with TBI Operations to align partners workloads with our process to ensure smooth partner experience to ensure satisfaction is being met</li>
<li>Overall liaison between partner and TBI resources, including escalation POC alongside Director.</li>
<li>Assistance in driving partners to TBI sponsored events.</li>
</ul>
