<h2>Sales Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At Red Hat, we connect an innovative community of customers, partners, and contributors to deliver an open source stack of trusted, high-performing solutions. We offer cloud, Linux, middleware, storage, and virtualization technologies, together with award-winning global customer support, consulting, and implementation services. Red Hat is a rapidly growing company supporting more than 90% of Fortune 500 companies.</p>
<br>
<p>
Make an impact on the success of Red Hat's customers and business. The Red Hat Sales team is looking for a Sales Account Manager to join us in the Southeastern U.S. In this role, you will be assigned specific accounts and customers within a geographical territory and work to gain a better understanding of the challenges that some of our biggest customers are facing. You'll collaborate with these accounts to discover new opportunities, use past solutions as blueprints for future success, and build relationships with lines of business and C-level executives to guide technological decision making. As a Sales Account Manager, you'll need to have experience selling technology in the general commercial sector. The role should ideally be located in FL, GA, SC, or NC.</p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Sell the complete Red Hat's portfolio of solutions within assigned enterprise accounts</li>
<li>
Promote the Red Hat's value proposition to prospective customers within a highly competitive market</li>
<li>
Manage complex sales cycles from prospecting to closing</li>
<li>
Maintain and develop new relationships within your assigned accounts</li>
<li>
Excel within a fast-paced organization</li>
</ul>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
5-7 years of sales experience within the software space</li>
<li>
Experience selling in both direct and partner models</li>
<li>
Knowledge of and passion for open source technology</li>
<li>
Ability to call widely into your accounts in the areas of application development, architecture, operations, leadership (C-suite), and lines of business</li>
<li>
Demonstrated knowledge of the market and competitive landscape</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Solid work ethic; motivated and enthusiastic</li>
<li>
Professional, courteous, and motivated as an individual contributor or when working within as part of a team</li>
<li>
Capable of handling challenging workloads in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>
Proven history of exceptional quota attainment</li>
<li>
Solid understanding of middleware and cloud technology</li>
</ul>
