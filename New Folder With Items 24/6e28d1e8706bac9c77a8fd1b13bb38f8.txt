<b>About Us:</b><br>
Venga is a CRM solution designed to enhance customer experiences. We've built a suite of tools for businesses to better understand and engage their customers, making it possible to build relationships with every customer, at scale. Venga offers solutions to restaurants and fitness studios, including partnerships with OpenTable and MINDBODY.
<br><br>
To date, Venga has collected data on more than 100 million customers and $6 billion in consumer purchases. Venga Dine has been adopted by hospitality industry leaders, including ThinkFoodGroup, Il Fornaio, Farmers Group, and Boka Group. Venga Move is currently being deployed by key fitness companies, including Y7 Studio, Pure Barre, and Barre3.
<br><br>
Venga is an independently managed subsidiary of Booking Holdings.
