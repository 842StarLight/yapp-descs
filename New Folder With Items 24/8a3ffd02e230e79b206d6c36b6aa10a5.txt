Want to make an impact with us as a Lead Cyber Security Cloud Architect?<br>
<br>
<b>What you’ll do …:</b><br>
<br>
Work in an enterprise setting across multiple security and cloud domains. You will have the opportunity to lead the technical direction of a CyberSecurity consulting team with focus on Digital Identity, Cloud, Application and Data Security, Threat Intelligence Management, Security Operations, Endpoint Security, Security Orchestration and Automation, including Solution Integrations &amp; deployments. This technical lead role will also support consulting delivery with on-site technical engagements with partners and customers as well as providing technical leadership on other engagements. Additionally, client engagements may include participating in pre-sales meetings, analyzing and defining customer requirements, and proposal development.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>You’ll thrive here because you… </b><b>:</b>
<div>
<ul><li>Have a strong track record of architecting and implementing security solutions in Cloud, enterprise and hybrid environments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage teams of technical security architects and engineers
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Are an expert in communicating technical risks to business customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Are highly experienced in digital Identity strategy, architecture and solutions across Authentication, Authorization, lifecycle management, SaaS/ On-prem solutions, Federations &amp; integrations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Are experienced in Secure network and connectivity Architectures involving enterprise-cloud, cloud-Internet and inter/intra-cloud/ Co-lo connectivity. Well versed in recommending &amp; designing cloud &amp; enterprise network controls such as firewalls, network ACLs, VPN, routing, switching, load balancers, monitoring, security and DNS.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Are experienced with large SecOps Program, processes and related technologies and architecture, including monitoring, Logging, Event/ incident management solutions and integrations across an enterprise and cloud environments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Have experience implementing endpoint security solutions strategy and architectures for vulnerability management, malware, data loss prevention, hardening, device management etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Have 10+ years of experience in an Enterprise IT environment and cyber security domains</li></ul>
<ul><li>Have strong verbal, written and interpersonal communication skills and the ability to influence and communicate with customers, vendors and partners, and across all levels of an organization</li></ul>
