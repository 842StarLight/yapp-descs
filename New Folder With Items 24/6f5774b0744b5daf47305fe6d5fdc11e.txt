<div><b>
About Asset Management Marketing:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Our marketing department is growing and it is committed to delivering an exceptional digital experience for its clients and is increasing its digital marketing capabilities to raise awareness for its expertise and drive adoption for its solutions. We are in a digital “build” mode and seek a talented, driven and forward-thinking digital marketing partner who is passionate about all things digital, who will shape and lead the expansion of our digital marketing strategy to help achieve the firm’s business and marketing objectives. This individual should be able to think strategically execute tactically, communicate exceptionally well, and will be a hands-on leader reporting to the Director of Digital Marketing</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The<b> Digital Marketing Senior Associate</b> will be responsible for planning, developing, implementing and managing digital customer acquisition programs. Additionally, the Senior Associate will be responsible for optimizing customer conversions through unified, personalized digital experiences across multiple digital touchpoints. She/he will serve as the business’s subject matter expert, partnering with other areas to help them understand digital best practices in digital customer acquisition and conversion. The ideal candidate will be solutions and results oriented, comfortable with some level of ambiguity, and excited about the opportunity to help define and evolve the role as the digital strategy matures. If you thrive on new challenges and are inspired about developing a digital marketing program and platform that delivers results, we encourage you to apply.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Partner with Channel and Product Marketing teams to develop targeted, multi-channel customer acquisition strategies and programs.</li>
<li>
Set-up, execution, budget management, optimization, and reporting/analytics of all digital acquisition campaigns</li>
<li>
Oversee Pay per Click and retargeting programs. Own agency relationship and performance results. Partner with Content Strategist and Product Marketing on keyword research (discovery and expansion); review/editing of ad creative/copy and ongoing management of campaigns.</li>
<li>
Establish acquisition and conversion funnel and attribution tracking protocol for all digital acquisition programs.</li>
<li>
Contribute to monthly and quarterly marketing performance metrics including development of insights and indicated actions.</li>
<li>
Optimize lead capture and conversion metrics across the user journey via targeting tactics, personalization, website User Experience, AB testing and marketing attribution</li>
<li>
Consult with Channel Marketing partners to optimize lead scoring and lead qualification model</li>
<li>
Build and deploy campaign landing pages</li>
<li>
Partner with Website teams to ideate and execute best practices in User Experience and conversion optimization. Gather website user intelligence including engagement and interaction metrics, content feedback and user feedback to develop action plans to enhance experience, task accomplishment and net promoter score.</li>
<li>
As subject area expert, provides comprehensive, in-depth consulting and leadership to team and partners at a high technical level</li>
</ul></div>
