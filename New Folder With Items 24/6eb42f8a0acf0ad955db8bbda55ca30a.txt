Demandbase tracks and catalogs all B2B web traffic and has amassed the largest business data-set in the world. We effectively identify (down to the individual level) who, at what company, is visiting the websites of our clients. This allows us to target marketing to that individual in a meaningful way. The Targeting platform is the first AI driven ad-targeting tool in existence and is revolutionizing the way companies are marketing their products.

We're currently looking for a Principal Product Manager to drive the design and delivery for our Targeting solution. Targeting is our account-based advertising/targeting solution. The PM in this role will act as a General Manager for this specific product line and should have the education and experience commensurate with this level of responsibility and ownership.
