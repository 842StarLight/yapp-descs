<h2>Leasing Manager/Assistant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Leasing Manager</b></p>
<p>Hampshire Assets seeks a Leasing Manager, reporting to the asset’s GM, who will be directly responsible for managing the day-to-day leasing functions, coaching the leasing team, and marketing/advertising programs for a 1,200 unit high-rise in Silver Spring, MD. This position is in need of an expert leasing and marketing professional that can drive the performance of the department to achieve and exceed the property’s budget goals while balancing exceptional customer service. The ideal candidate will have experience with resident communications to ensure the highest satisfaction during move in process and for renewals. This professional will need to understand market trends, marketing avenues, proficient in understanding floor plans and layouts and be adept in communicating and reporting statistics to management. Must be able to work in a fast paced environment with a collaborative team.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Set leasing and marketing strategy for the property</li>
<li>Oversee daily operations of Leasing and Leasing Staff.</li>
<li>Oversee and process applications and lease renewals.</li>
<li>Leadership responsibilities included training, supervising assigned staffs' day-to-day operations, coaching, setting expectations, and holding assigned staff accountable. Recommends appropriate disciplinary actions and assist in assigned staff write-ups as needed.</li>
<li>Advertising upcoming apartment vacancies, arranging showings, coordinating marketing events, and closely work with head of marketing in understanding and utilizing online tools.</li>
<li>Coordinating and gathering information to develop and produce weekly/monthly/quarterly/annual Leasing and Occupancy reports and summaries, including reporting to senior leadership and ownership.</li>
<li>Complete applications and lease/renewal documents</li>
<li>Review vacancies, walk tour path and maintain model apartments</li>
<li>Assist management with resident relations</li>
<li>Implement policies and procedures</li>
<li>Maintain solid understanding of competitive properties, market knowledge and critical market dynamics to Analyze market and occupancy trends and shop the competition to identify changing market conditions</li>
<li>Maintenance of waiting lists and logbooks according to corporate and regulatory standards.</li>
<li>Handle and provide responses to applicant's complaints, inquiries, and concerns.</li>
<li>Organize and maintain resident files per company procedure.</li>
<li>Recommend and track marketing avenues, oversee marketing campaigns and monitor traffic results</li>
<li>Responsible for updating the website and marketing sites</li>
<li>Coordinates with the Marketing department to manage and directs the community’s marketing, print and internet advertising, sales activities, leasing team members, and other resources to achieve established revenue, occupancy, resident retention, and leasing goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Partner with the operations team to create and track action plans to continuously improve occupancy, shop scores, customer satisfaction ratings, and other core processes in our residential management activities. Results will be tracked, and plans adjusted on an ongoing basis throughout the year to improve performance.</li>
<li>Assist with leasing/touring community, Lease and pre-lease apartments</li>
<li>Working weekends is required</li>
<li>Other tasks as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>*Qualifications</p>
<p>*</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated ability to read, write, and communicate effectively to comprehend and complete legal documents, coach the leasing team to effectively sell and explain apartment features, and answer questions.</li>
<li>Proficiency in executing sales skills, such as generating sales leads, conducting sales presentations, qualifying prospects, and closing.</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in word processing, spreadsheet, property management applications, and database management programs in order to complete required reports and employment documents.</li>
<li>Demonstrated mathematical skills necessary to add, subtract, multiply, and divide numbers, decimals, and fractions, and calculate percent’s in order to complete financial records, budgets, and other fiscal reporting information.</li>
<li>Demonstrated management and supervisory skills sufficient to lead, direct, evaluate, and mentor subordinate and peer employee staff.</li>
<li>Proficiency and fluency in using the internet for marketing, advertising, and sales-related circumstances, including using Craigslist, Google and other search engines, and navigating the internet and websites.</li>
</ul>
<p>We are looking for career-minded professionals who recognize the value of a career path. The ideal candidate will have a minimum of 3 years experience leasing at a multi-family community. The successful candidate must have strong organizational abilities, follow-up skills, and an attention to detail that often makes the difference with prospective and current residents. Must be able to multi-task and adapt to changing schedules that will include some weekends and holidays. This position includes showing apartments within the community.</p>
<p>In return, we offer competitive compensation, along with comprehensive benefits, including medical and dental and vision benefits, life insurance, a 401(k) and a corporate team environment with opportunity of advancement.</p>
