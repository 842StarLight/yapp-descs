<p><b>
Job Summary</b><br>
OmniVue, a leading provider of application and infrastructure management services has an immediate need for an experienced Dynamics GP Technical Support Consultant. This role will be responsible for supporting our Dynamics GP Client base as well as assisting in upgrades to meet and exceed our clients’ objectives. This person will also assist, as needed, on new implementations by taking on various tasks of the implementation process. Qualified individuals will have 5+ years of Information Systems experience, with at least 3 years of IT support experience. Experience with supporting Dynamics GP is required. Dynamics GP certification is a plus. Experience working with manufacturers and distributors is also a plus.</p>
