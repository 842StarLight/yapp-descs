<h2>Sr. Solutions Manager Downstream Marketing & US Region Marketing, MMS Pyxis Supply</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description Summary</b></h1>
This role will be responsible for Global Downstream Marketing and driving the US go-to-market plan for Pyxis Supply solutions.<br>
<br>
This role will have/develop working knowledge of the Pyxis Supply products and be able to demonstrate key product features and message the value offer (Level 1) as well as message and position a product’s contribution to the MMS solution offerings (Level 2). This role will lead the development of solution messaging for Pyxis Supply solutions. This role will support the sales organization in the sale of each product (Level 1) while also driving to a solution (Level 2) approach as measured by committed contracts, competitive conversions, sales revenue, and profitability. This position will support the market execution of all product components needed to achieve successful delivery of the Pyxis Supply solutions value propositions. This role will foster effective cross functional collaboration with platform, operations, sales, contracts, communications, sales training and other functions required to achieve marketing objectives.<br>
<br>
In addition to the above, this role may lead projects or initiatives outside the role’s scope of responsibilities.<br>
<br>
Key Success Metrics:<br>
<ul>
<li>Global revenue targets</li>
<li>Global product launch metrics</li>
<li>Customer experience metrics</li>
</ul>
