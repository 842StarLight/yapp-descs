<h2>Sr Technical Instructor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for a dynamic and creative Senior Technical Instructor to join the team and help us deliver world-class training to our customers and partners nationally and worldwide. In this role you will perform instructional delivery of approved course materials and teach multiple courses. You will work within a team but also be able to work independently. You are also able to teach classes virtually as well as at Splunk partner, customer, and contract facilities.</p>
<p>
Additionally, you will demonstrate the ability to lead major departmental projects and effectively work cross-functionally with members of other departments. You will be viewed as a leader and mentor by other team members and deliver multiple technically advanced courses.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Teach advanced technical classes and regularly participate in cross-functional projects.</li>
<li>
Lead major projects within the department and handle multiple projects/tasks with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>
Consistently meet or exceeds goal and/or project achievements.</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively, both verbally and written, with management, team members, and other departments.</li>
<li>
Anticipate and resolve common problems.</li>
<li>
Balance long and short term goals by prioritizing activities.</li>
<li>
Actively participate in curriculum planning sessions for new product training.</li>
<li>
Review training curriculum and provides feedback to instructional designers.</li>
<li>
Adapt well to changing job and project requirements.</li>
<li>
Recommend and implement solutions when project issues arise.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirements:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Extensive experience of software/technology training to various technical audiences (i.e. software engineers, systems administrators, developers, sales engineers etc.)</li>
<li>
Experience working as a Senior Technical Instructor within Education Services for an Enterprise Software Company</li>
<li>
Knowledge: Current Unix/Linux shell, command-line, vi editor experience a requirement!</li>
<li>
Linux Administration is a plus.</li>
<li>
Technical and troubleshooting skills in at least one software application or technology.</li>
<li>
You have excellent oral presentation and customer interaction skills.</li>
<li>
You are able to travel up to 50%.</li>
<li>
You are able to obtain a current passport and can travel to other countries and regions without restriction.</li>
<li>
Experience with Splunk or competitive application products are excellent but not required.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Unix a plus</li>
<li>
Ability to teach in multiple languages a plus (French, German, Italian)</li>
<li>
CISSP (Certified Security Specialist) a plus</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Education :</b></h2>
<ul><li>
BA/BS degree or equivalent experience in a technical or related field is required.</li></ul>
