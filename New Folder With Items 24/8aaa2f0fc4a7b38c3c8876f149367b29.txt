<h2>Logistics Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Basic Functions and Scope of Job:</b><br>
The Logistics Manager is responsible for executing warehouse and logistics coordination activities and designing process improvements related to warehouse and production support activities, such as: arrange shipping with preferred shipping vendors, and perform inventory transactions using SAP and WMS software to maintain inventory.
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities and Accountabilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Schedule inter-warehouse moves between Franklin warehouse and other Modula warehouses</li>
<li>Scheduled pick up and storage to hub of completed machines not yet ready for final delivery</li>
<li>Provide proper communications to departments of all shipped machines to customer or storage</li>
<li>Follow up and with carriers scheduled deliveries to customer sites</li>
<li>Schedule deliveries to customers based on feedback from Installation crew, dealer availability, and direct communication with customer</li>
<li>Receive and ship package and freight shipments</li>
<li>Pick material for production and sales orders</li>
<li>Develop and demonstrate an understanding of the receiving and shipping functions related to UPS, FedEx, common carrier, and other modes of shipping</li>
<li>Maintain, develop, and adhere to established department and company standards, procedures, and policies</li>
<li>Maintain a neat, clean, and safe working environment</li>
<li>Other duties as required</li>
</ul>
<b>Please note:</b> this job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties, or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities, and activities may change at any time, with or without notice. It is expected that this cross-functional role will perform other duties as assigned
<br>.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Qualifications and Skills:</b><br>
This role is pivotal in coordinating the movement of material and supervising the performance of internal personnel and relationships with third party vendors while meeting the needs of the company, our dealers, and our customers.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2-5 years of Logistics Management and/or Inventory Management experience is required, preferably in a manufacturing environment</li>
<li>Minimum education of a high school diploma or its equivalent; Bachelor's Degree in logistics or other related field preferred</li>
<li>Ability to operate a forklift, various material handling devices, and Modula Lift machine is essential; training can be provided on an as-needed basis</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of software such as (but not limited to) SAP, MPS, and WMS is essential; training can be provided on an as-needed basis</li>
<li>Understanding to International Harmonized Tariff Schedule (HTS &amp; Sched B Classifications) and understanding of International Shipping and documentation requirements (to all countries) is essential</li>
<li>Understanding of the manufacturing industry is essential</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, including proficiency in current versions of MS Office, is required; must be proficient in computer applications including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, and must be able to navigate custom databases and on-line document management software</li>
</ul>
