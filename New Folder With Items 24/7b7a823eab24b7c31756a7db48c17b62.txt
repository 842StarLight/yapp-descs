<h2>Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Responsible for acquiring new talent for various Company positions by cold sourcing, screening, evaluating, scheduling, and following-up with candidates in order to place them in positions with the Company. Also responsible for educating job seekers on open positions and Company background. All work activities will be performed on location in the community in which the open position will be filled.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Cold-source qualified candidates for open company roles by canvassing the community in which the open position will be filled.
</li>
<li>Build applicant sources by researching and contacting community services, colleges, employment agencies, social media, internet sites, and attending community events
</li>
<li>Build pipeline of potential candidates for future open positions
</li>
<li>Hand out company swag, goodies, and marketing materials to potential job seekers in order to obtain and maintain interest in open positions
</li>
<li>Providing job seekers organization and job specific information, opportunities, and emphasizing related benefits and perks
</li>
<li>Screen and evaluate job seekers using appropriate questionnaires for the position sought
</li>
<li>Schedule qualified candidates for interviews according to manager availability
</li>
<li>Contact scheduled interviewees to confirm their interview beforehand
</li>
<li>Contact interviewees to deliver verbal rejections and offers
</li>
<li>Actively seek feedback from applicants on their experience, and relay that feedback to the Human Resources team; provide personal feedback to Human Resources team and hiring managers in order to improve sourcing and recruiting practices
</li>
<li>Assist the Human Resources team with pre-employment activities, such as obtaining signed offer letters, verifying background and drug screening results, and providing candidates with information about onboarding, as needed in order to start new hires as quickly as possible
</li>
<li>Open positions will be assigned/delegated to each HR team member by the Director of Human Resources, typically by region
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<p><i>Required:
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Human Resources or equivalent
</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years’ experience in sales or applicant sourcing
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills in person, over the phone, and via email
</li>
<li>Proficient in use of computers, including software applications such as Microsoft Office Products (Word, Excel), and data base applications
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and as a team in a remote position
</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Preferred:
</i></p>
<ul><li>Experience with Applicant Tracking Systems<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Demands:
</b></p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is frequently, walking, standing, listening, verbally communicating, and using wrists, hands and fingers to enter data into computer systems. The employee may also be occasionally required to lift, pull push, or otherwise transport objects up to 50 pounds.</p>
