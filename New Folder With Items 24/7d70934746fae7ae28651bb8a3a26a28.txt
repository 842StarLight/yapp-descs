<h2>Seasonal Sales Associate Pottery Barn Furniture Retail Store</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description</p>
<p>Seasonal Sales Associate at Pottery Barn Home Furnishings Store, Trolley Square Mall.</p>
<p>Pottery Barn will be closed on Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Day, however, all seasonal associates will be required to be in town and work the weeks of the holidays.</p>
<p>PRIMARY FUNCTION: Provide customers with a unique shopping experience by utilizing excellent customer service, effective selling techniques and providing in-depth product knowledge in a fast-paced, specialty retail environment. Assist shipper/receiver and stockers with stock room and shipments.<br>Our Company , founded in 1956, Williams-Sonoma, Inc. is the premier specialty retailer of high-quality products for the kitchen and home in the United States. Our family of brands are Williams-Sonoma, Pottery Barn, Pottery Barn Kids, PBteen, West Elm, Williams-Sonoma Home, Rejuvenation, and Mark and Graham. These brands are among the best known and most respected in the industry. We offer beautifully-designed, stylish and functional products for every area of the home, including the kitchen, living room, bedroom, home office, closet, laundry room and even outdoor spaces. We've seen some big changes since our first brick-and-mortar store opened more than half of a century ago. What hasn't changed is our passion for high-quality products, functional design, outstanding customer service, and enhancing the lives of our customers and the communities where we operate. Today, we're a multi-brand, multi-channel, global enterprise supported by state-of-the-art technology and some of the most talented teams in retailing - and we're always looking for new energy and ideas. Provide customers with a unique shopping experience by offering excellent customer service.<br>We offer a flexible schedule, generous discount, and a fun, fast paced non-commission sales environment.<br>DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Greet and welcome customers to the store with enthusiasm.</li>
<li>Determine and understand customer needs by asking open-ended questions.</li>
<li>Explain product features and benefits, while addressing customer needs.</li>
<li>Suggest additional items while utilizing sales skills to build multiple sales.</li>
<li>Ring items on the cash register and either gift wrap or package items to avoid breakage or damage.</li>
<li>Regularly provide feedback to store manager on customer questions and reactions to merchandise and store.</li>
<li>Follow through on special orders and customer requests.</li>
<li>Receive, ticket, package, log and ship stock merchandise as assigned.</li>
<li>Locate and check merchandise in the stock room by utilizing the merchandise locator.</li>
<li>Maintain displays and stock room through regular cleaning, straightening and dusting.</li>
</ul>
<p>SKILLS/KNOWLEDGE:</p>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years of customer service and retail sales experience preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to lift and carry medium to large items several meters, weighing up to 35kg.</li>
<li>Good planning and time-management skills for executing multiple tasks simultaneously.</li>
</ul>
