<b>General Job Duties/Responsibilities:</b><br>
As Customer Service &amp; Billing Specialist, your patience, good listening skills, attention to detail and overall positive presence are at the core of your character—and the foundation for providing an outstanding customer experience. You’re our number one ambassador. With an undivided focus on the customer, you offer support every step of the way, from being a helpful voice on the phone to helping to resolve customer service issues with ease. Armed with a deep knowledge of current merchandise and promotions, you are confident in answering any question that may arise. You will also be responsible for upselling customers on older products/calling in inquiring about the product, and getting them to a Sales person. Behind the scenes, you demonstrate your knack for organization and efficiency. You must be good communicators, providing service to customers, and departments within Swiftpage. Must have good clerical skills, as well as computing, data entry and time management skills.
<br><br>
