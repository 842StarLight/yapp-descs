<h2>Senior Director, Marketing US Energy</h2>

<div>Ethicon, US LLC, a member of Johnson &amp; Johnson Family of Companies is recruiting for Senior Director, Marketing US Energy.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Ethicon</b>, part of the Johnson &amp; Johnson Medical Devices Companies, has made significant contributions to surgery for more than 60 years from creating the first sutures, to revolutionizing surgery with minimally invasive procedures. Our continuing dedication to Shape the Future of Surgery is built on our commitment to help address the world's most pressing health care issues and improve and save more lives. Through Ethicon's surgical technologies and solutions including sutures, staplers, energy devices, trocars and hemostats and our commitment to treat serious medical conditions like obesity and cancer worldwide, we deliver innovation to make a life-changing impact. For more information, visit www.ethicon.com.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
 <div>The Senior Director, Marketing will be the functional lead for the Energy Platform US Marketing Team and Strategic Specialties supporting the entire Ethicon portfolio. This role will report directly to the VP, Marketing and be a member of the Marketing Leadership Team. The Senior Director, Marketing will oversee the development/execution of all US Marketing strategies, business plans, and all aspects of commercial execution including customer &amp; market insights, branding, market segmentation, product positioning, KOL engagement, channel strategies, forecasting for the full Energy portfolio of products. The Senior Director Marketing will be responsible for Portfolio approach for the Colorectal specialty. It is also critical that the Senior Director Marketing represent the brand and strategic specialties to the enterprise, assuring focus on priorities, driving strategic alignment and securing resources with business partners.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>He/she will also be responsible for driving cohesive Energy and specialty strategies, building and managing C-Suite relationships with key customers, and partnering with the SCG organization on the Platform Contract Strategy. The Senior Director, Marketing will partner closely with Field Sales leadership and critical internal stakeholders including Sales Planning Operations, Professional Education &amp; Sales Training, &amp; all support functions (IT, Finance, HR) to ensure consistency in communication with the Sales force. This person has the responsibility to reach financial targets within specified budgets and timeframes. This person will ensure applicable company compliance with all relevant country and regional requirements, company regulations, policies and procedures.</div>
