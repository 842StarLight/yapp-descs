<div>
The AdOps Manager is responsible for the execution and balance of our direct display, programmatic and network partnerships across the Boats Group LLC publisher portals - Boats.com, BoatTrader.com, Yachtworld.com and CosasDeBarcos.com. This individual will help to oversee AdOps performance, delivery, reporting, tracking, analytics and execution. This role will focus on the overall monetization strategy and prioritization of our inventory and audiences across desktop, mobile and tablet display inventory. You will also be responsible for managing sales planners, working closely with the Yield Manger and Programmatic Sales Manager.
</div>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who You Are</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Programmatic Expertise: You are a DFP expert. You have experience working with ad serving technology, header bidding and other digital advertising technologies.</li>
<li>
Solutions Oriented: A creative problem solver who takes an analytical approach to balancing trade-offs to arrive at winning solutions.</li>
<li>
Process Minded: A strong attention to detail with a close eye on the success and effectiveness of current systems. You're able to remove roadblocks and inefficiencies in current workflows to improve time, quality and accuracy of ad execution.</li>
<li>
Operational Excellence: You will establish a base of best practices and standards to drive consistency and ensure that campaigns launch on time and meet the expectations of what was sold.</li>
<li>
Cross Functional Communicator: Strong consultative skills, engaging with cross-functional partners across the company to meet business objectives.</li>
<li>
Data Driven: You will manage the advertising operations KPIs as well as look at a full suite of analytics like Viewability, CTR, Conv, eCPM, eCPC, identify risks, opportunities and improvements for our advertising solutions.</li>
<li>
Resource Planning &amp; Prioritization: Help to set expectations internally regarding resources, schedules and budgets, as well as track capacity, forecast trends, and update the business as needs change.</li>
</ul></div>
