<h2>Senior Director, Global Partner Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>New Relic</b><br>
<b>Senior Director, Global Partner Marketing</b><br>
<b>San Francisco, CA</b></p>
<p><br>
<b>
Your Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
New Relic is looking for an innovative and engaging leader to join the team as Senior Director, Global Partner Marketing.</p>
<p>
In this critical role, you will report to the VP of Marketing and will drive the vision, strategy, and goals for a small, but focused Global Partner Marketing team that is responsible for marketing to, through and with partners of all types.You will lead a team of partner marketers who are responsible for partner engagement &amp; experiences, demand generation, alliances marketing, sponsorship, and global marketing programs.</p>
<p>
How do you know if you’ll be successful in the role? You should understand the value of the partner ecosystem in a high-growth environment and thrive in collaborative situations. You also enjoy working cross-functionally and have the ability to influence the strategy and direction of cross-functional teams.</p>
<p><br>
<b>
What You’ll Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Accountability for our global partner marketing strategy and framework. This includes all sell-to, with, through, and for partner marketing strategies and engagements across all partner types. Includes the strategy for global intersection points with regional marketing efforts</li>
<li>
Collaborate cross-functionally with corporate &amp; product marketing, partner operations, finance, product management, events, sales, and others to guide the execution of partner marketing and awareness programs on a worldwide level</li>
<li>
Deliver and lead the partner marketing experience across all channels. Craft immersive global partner marketing strategies that enhance the ecosystem, engage the partner community, and promote a positive partner experience with New Relic</li>
<li>
Set metrics, analyze data, and be accountable to achieving partner marketing business goals and targets. Work with operations to ensure proper data and alignment on performance</li>
<li>
Support large-scale global partner event engagements - both first and third-party. This includes guiding the partner experience and sponsorship sales strategy for global programs such as New Relic’s marquee customer and partner event, FutureStack</li>
<li>
Build relationships with top partners globally, and develop joint go-to-market strategies, programs, and deliverables that promote our strong position within the ecosystem</li>
<li>
Work with our global marketing team to execute demand generating activities, and make marketing materials available to the ecosystem</li>
<li>
Work with partner programs, operations and IT business applications teams to deliver on the infrastructure to enable a contemporary partner marketing experience, including through-partner marketing, the New Relic Partner Portal, MDF and incentive management</li>
<li>
Deliver timely, engaging, multi-channel partner campaigns, as the owner of the partner communication, engagement, and experiences discipline. Communicate with the New Relic Partner+ ecosystem and attract new partners through partner recruitment campaigns</li>
<li>
Talent development - You will grow, champion, and provide a platform for the success of your team</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Your Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
Must-have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
10+ years of experience in partner marketing and people management</li>
<li>
Ability to envision and execute on multi-dimensional global marketing plans including working in the field to generate successful go-to-market campaigns with global, regional, and local partners</li>
<li>
Current experience in a high growth enterprise software company and experience and expertise working with partners of all types: ISVs, MSPs, OEMs, Systems Integrators, Professional Services, Resellers, and Distributors</li>
<li>
Experience marketing for SaaS providers and/or the cloud ecosystem. Experience with top cloud providers such as AWS, Microsoft, Google, IBM, etc is preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Nice-to-have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to influence, motivate and work in a highly cross-functional environment to drive outcomes-based plans, programs, and execution</li>
<li>
Highly motivated, self-starter that can build and launch programs within budget and effectively handle multiple, concurrent timelines</li>
<li>
Experience in a dynamic, high-pressure, high-visibility role</li>
<li>
Excellent communication (written and verbal), reporting and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Ability to travel, including global destinations</li>
<li>
Product and market knowledge in relevant areas such as application development, software development, cloud, DevOps, digital customer experience, and emerging cloud technologies</li>
</ul>
<p>
Please note that visa sponsorship is not available for this position.</p>
