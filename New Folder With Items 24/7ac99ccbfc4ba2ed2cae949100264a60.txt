<h2>Power Systems Aftermarket Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for technical expert in generator controls, switchgear, and electrical systems to join our rapidly growing team. The ideal candidate also possesses a sales and customer service background. Selling Caterpillar parts, systems, and aftermarket solutions, we ensure product reliability to our customers. Milton CAT is seeking a key individual to help us grow and develop this business. This position involves traveling within the Milton CAT territory to customer locations as part of the Sales and Service teams.</p>
<p>
If you have a passion for Mechanical and Electrical Systems and would like the opportunity to support an iconic brand of the highest quality equipment, we look forward to hearing from you.<br>

</li>

</li>
<li>Generate and develop new business to meet business goals.
