<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p>Kontoor Brands, Inc. (KTB) is a global lifestyle apparel company with a portfolio of some of the world’s most iconic denim brands: Wrangler®, Lee® and Rock &amp; Republic®. We design, manufacture and distribute superior high-quality products that look good, fit right, and are crafted with sensitivity to our planet to give people around the world the freedom and confidence to express themselves.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our global community of more than 15,000 employees fosters a culture of integrity, collaboration, performance and entrepreneurial spirit. We are looking for inclusive, humble, creative and forward-looking employees with a passion for creating innovative apparel products, accessories, and solutions that are both meaningful and purposeful for consumers and employees.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Senior Director, Brand – Lee NAM: Become the Newest Member of Kontoor Brands
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Senior Director, Brand - Lee NAM serves as the brand leader for Lee North America and is responsible along with Global Lee Brand VP for developing and executing all brand communication strategies for the targeted lifestyles, consumers and retailers. It is responsible for effectively and efficiently driving demand creation activities for the brand, within the established brand positioning and brand playbook strategies.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Demand Creation: </b>Lead and develop targeted marketing programs, focused on key lifestyles and consumers, to drive awareness, interest and desire for the Lee brand and its products.
</li>
<li>
<b>Brand communications strategy: </b>Drive execution of b rand strategies and marketing plans, so as to consistently generate profitable revenue growth and build brand health and equity scores across key lifestyle business units.
</li>
<li>
<b>Brand Playbook: </b>Along with Global Lee Brand VP, President and Creative Director, develops the Brand Playbook and manages business units’ adherence to it. Works to ensure and preserve brand integrity and value in the marketplace.
</li>
<li>
<b>Marketing/Brand Leader </b>: Provides high level of marketing skills and expertise to the company by building, developing, and managing a strong Demand Creation team along with effective plans to achieve key objectives of the company.
</li>
<li>
<b>Communications Platforms. </b>Manages all communications initiatives that are appropriate and relevant for key lifestyles and target consumers. Works with staff and external agencies to develop unique, targeted and relevant consumer strategies which support brand plans.
</li>
<li>
<b>Retailer focus. </b>Works closely with key retailers and Sales to upgrade Lee’s presence, brand strength and sell-through with wholesale customers and with their retail environment.
</li>
<li>
<b>Marketing Budgets: </b>Oversees and manages marketing/brand budget and allocation of marketing dollars.
</li>
<li>
<b>Consumer Packaging: </b>Collaborates with lifestyle business units to ensure superior product packaging that is well-branded and easily understood by consumers.
</li>
<li>
<b>Presentations: </b>Develops branded retail presentations and assists Sales, Design and Merchandising with line presentations.<br>
</li>
</ul>
