<h2>Tax Specialist, VSP Vision Care</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>VSP Global is comprised of five complementary businesses that combine high-quality eye care insurance, high-fashion eyewear, customized lenses, ophthalmic technology and retail solutions, with employees in over 23 countries. No matter the role, we’re all focused on a singular mission: to help people see. Learn more by visiting https://vspglobal.com/cms/careers/
</p>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>General Summary
</p>
<p></p>
<p>With general supervision, prepares federal and state income tax returns, other tax returns, and estimated tax payments. Prepares tax provisions and workpapers and interfaces with external and internal auditors. Performs analysis and research involving tax issues.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Functions
</p>
<p></p>
<div>Assists with preparation of federal and state income, sales and use, and premium tax returns, files extensions, pays estimated taxes, and tax correspondence for multiple companies in multiple jurisdictions; coordinates tax return preparation with outside preparer<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Acts as liaison with external auditors to provide tax return, accounting information, documentation, and answer questions and resolve reporting issues<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Acts as liaison with internal audit for controls testing<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Prepares tax provision workpapers and drafts tax footnotes for financial reporting purposes<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Coordinates and handles routine tax audits conducted by different states, onsite and by mail<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Documents, reconciles, and maintains accurate records to support book/tax accounting decisions and statements<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Prepares documentation necessary to support audits on various taxes by multiple jurisdictions<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Maintains and implements tax return software<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Performs analysis and researches issues regarding taxes, including premium tax, sales and use tax, and other miscellaneous state assessments<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Performs all other duties as assigned
</div>
<p></p>
<p>Job Specifications
</p>
<p></p>
<div>Typically has the following skills or abilities:<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Bachelor’s degree in Tax, Accounting, Business Administration, or related experience<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Five years of technical and theoretical background in U.S. GAAP, including accounting for income tax<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Five years of tax related experience, including federal and state income tax for a corporation<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Ability to conduct various types of analysis, provide alternatives and make recommendations<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Proficient in spreadsheet applications (specifically MS Excel) and accounting systems<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Excellent verbal and written communication skills<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Strong willingness to learn, share knowledge, accept responsibility, handle multiple tasks concurrently<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Clean credit history as reported by credit report
</div>
