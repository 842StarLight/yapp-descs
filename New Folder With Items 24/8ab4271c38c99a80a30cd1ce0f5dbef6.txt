Job Responsibilities
<br><br>
In the Outside Sales role, you will call on small-to-medium sized businesses and demonstrate to potential customers the value of our online advertising products. We provide businesses with the tools they need to increase their SEO value, social media presence, and control their online reputation management. You will build relationships with Radio Reps in order to increase market leads. This position is 100% remote, working from a home office.
<br><br>
<b>Other responsibilities of the Outside Sales role include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Developing and managing a pipeline of potential opportunities</li>
<li>Partnering with Radio Account Executives to close business</li>
<li>Researching industry trends and competitive analysis</li>
<li>Working with client services to ensure customer success and retention</li>
<li>Achieving and exceeding monthly business development quotas</li>
<li>Mastering negotiation and time management skills</li>
</ul>
Job Requirements
<br><br>
For the Outside Sales Representative position, we are seeking an aggressive, confident, and competitive professional with a solid background in outside business development. You must be comfortable working independently and building strong business relationships.
<br><br>
<b>Other requirements include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Business / Marketing, or equivalent training and experience</li>
<li>2+ years of experience in Outside Business-to-Business Sales / Advertising</li>
<li>Track record of success in Sales and Business Development</li>
<li>Experience with SEO, websites, social media, daily deal, and/or advertising,preferred</li>
</ul>
