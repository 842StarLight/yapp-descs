<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>AUTOMOTIVE SALES CONSULTANT OVERVIEW:</p>
<p>Are you goal driven and enjoy being rewarded for a job well done? Are you results oriented, self-starting and enjoy a fast pace environment with the opportunity to grow within the company? We have numerous opportunities for skilled candidates that share our passion to commitment, service, and quality. If you are a motivated person who believes in being at the top of their game and contributing to the overall success of the organization we want to meet you! We are looking for a professional and outgoing Sales Consultant to join us in building and representing our brand.</p>
<p>AUTOMOTIVE SALES CONSULTANT RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul><li>Take the customers through a thorough buying process supported by established selling steps and procedures</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and relay a high level of product knowledge around vehicle features</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enhance the sales process by demonstrating the vehicles features and conducting a test drive</li></ul>
<ul><li>Transfer ownership of vehicle utilizing an established delivery process which insures the customer understands the vehicles operating features and warranty</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain an owner follow-up system that encourages repeat and referral business and contributes to customer satisfaction, loyalty and long term clientele</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist management in ensuring the lot is merchandised correctly to maximize sales</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prospect for new customers by making phone calls, sending emails, and visiting with service customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Close deals!</li></ul>
<p>AUTOMOTIVE SALES CONSULTANT QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<ul><li>Enthusiastic with high energy throughout the sales workday</li></ul>
<ul><li>Career minded, goal driven and client focused</li></ul>
<ul><li>Self-motivated and ability to work in a fast-paced environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong interpersonal and communications skills one-on-one and over the phone</li></ul>
<ul><li>Persuasive and able to overcome customer objections during the sales process</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient in basic business math including percentages</li></ul>
<ul><li>EAGER TO LEARN AND GROW</li></ul>
<ul><li>Capable of learning software systems used during sales process</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum high school diploma or GED equivalent required</li></ul>
