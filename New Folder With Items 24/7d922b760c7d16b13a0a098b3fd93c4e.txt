<h2>Floor Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Floor Supervisors are responsible for sales floor operations and providing direction to Sales Associates. As a member of the Store Leadership Team, they support the company mission: “Connecting Active People With Their Passions”. Come join the adventure at Wrentham Village Premium Outlets.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Provide direction and guidance to Sales Associates to ensure efficient and effective floor operations, customer service, coverage, and execution of visual standards.</div></li>
<li>
<div>Train Sales Associates on product knowledge and store procedures such as cash register transactions, product flow procedures, and visual standards</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Supervise and authorize cash register transactions made by Sales Associates.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Perform opening and closing procedures such as balancing registers, preparing cash bags, and activating security system.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Perform excellent customer service by assisting customers with questions, concerns and product information</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Provide efficient order management from placement of orders to delivery of goods.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Create and maintain an environment of strong sales and exceptional customer service through teamwork, coaching associate and leading by example.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Maintain a high professional standard of individual behavior by demonstrating respect, courtesy, and professionalism to align with Columbia Sportswear Company expectations.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Perform other duties as assigned.</div>
</li>
</ul>
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements:</b></h1>
<ul>
<li><div>High School Diploma or equivalent.</div></li>
<li>
<div>Prefer 3 years previous retail operations experience with at least one year of retail supervisory, management or leadership experience.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Combination of previous experience, education and relevant experience considered.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Skill in operating personal computers, POS systems, and various software packages including Microsoft Word and Excel are needed.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ability to communicate effectively with all levels of company personnel through oral and written means is required.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Effective retail math experience/aptitude/ability required.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Time management and ability to use good judgment and make sound decisions are required.</div>
</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Job Conditions:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Job may require hours that often exceed8 hours per day and/or 40 hours per week during peak periods.</div></li>
<li>
<div>Available to work store schedule – Sunday to Saturday.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Physical requirements include the ability to use telephone, computerized systems; perform repetitive movements of the arms and shoulders, stand for extended periods of time up to 8 hours per day, move freely around store, bend, twist, reach, squat, climb a ladder and regularly lift/carry up to 40 pounds.</div>
</li>
</ul>
