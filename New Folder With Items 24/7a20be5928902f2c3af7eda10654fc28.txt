<h2>Marketing Specialist / Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Blaine Brothers family of companies are currently seeking a marketing specialist / graphic designer to join the marketing team.
<br><br>
We are a Minnesota based family owned business with our main locations in Blaine and Inver Grove Heights. This position would office out of our North American Trailer Inver Grove Heights location. The ideal candidate will have 2-3 years of experience with traditional and online marketing, as well as demonstrable social networking experience, including social analytics. We like to work hard, play hard and take pride in everything we do!
<br><br>
<b>The job duties include but not limited to:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Champion brand guidelines, graphic design standards and copy/style guide adherence and organization of all marketing materials.</li>
<li>Create and refine creative processes and workflows as needed to improve efficiency and quality.</li>
<li>Create vision boards, seasonal look and feels, generate competitor and customer insights appropriate to role and provide recommendations for projects.</li>
<li>Design, edit, and produce assets to support a wide variety of printed and electronic materials like brochures, flyers, catalogs, newsletters, direct mailers, images assets, presentations, photography, and video. Oversee delivery of those materials across all channels.</li>
<li>Coordinate video planning, story boarding, editing, production and deployment of all videos.</li>
<li>Gather the appropriate information, copy and detail to include on the assets by partnering with internal and external team members in a streamlined manner, including verifying and double checking pricing, item descriptions, and vendor information for complete accuracy on sales materials.</li>
<li>Finalize quality art files for print production both large and smaller scales, manage print vendor relationships, gather quotes, and order materials on time.</li>
<li>Create, order, and own the process of delivering marketing specific materials, forms, clothing, uniforms, and swag. This includes managing the online ordering portal, managing the inventory and costs, day to day requests, communication and process around delivering in the most effective way.</li>
<li>Coordinate all events and sponsorships. Manage and plan tradeshow and event materials.</li>
<li>Manage day to day social media activities. Schedule and maintain posts and campaigns. Contribute to social media strategy and advertising.</li>
<li>Coordinate and deliver email marketing campaigns, blogs and customer surveys by building and delivering in the appropriate digital platform.</li>
<li>Prepare and contribute to marketing strategies and campaigns alongside Director of Marketing.</li>
<li>Project manage or lead pre-identified projects as needed.</li>
</ul>
