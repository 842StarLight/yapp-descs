<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About the role</b></h2>
<p>
Job Create bottoms up financial sales and inventory plans for the Old Navy Canada Girls Division on a quarterly basis. Determine the assortments, quantities, and flow timings to support the plans. Manage in season businesses by reporting out the past performances on a weekly basis. Determine promotional activity to maximize retail, margin, and unit sales. Manage and execute markdown activity. Reforecast all sales and inventory metrics on a monthly basis, given the current trend and inventory position.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who you are</b></h2>
<p>
Education: Bachelor’s degree or foreign equivalent in Business or a related field and 2 years of experience in job offered, retail, or a related occupation.</p>
<p>
Experience and/or education must include:<br>
1. Microsoft operating systems and Microsoft office softwares (Especially Excel for reporting, analysis and forecasting, understanding of Excel formulas, and Pivot tables to manipulate data);<br>
2. Proficiency in basic retail math required and performing analyses and presenting results;
<br>
3. Analyzing sales and profitability metrics;<br>
4. Performing financial, sales, and inventory analysis; and<br>
5. Identifying inefficient processes or underperforming areas to find solutions</p>
