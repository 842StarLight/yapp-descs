The team
<br>Our Finance team plays a critical role in ensuring accurate financial reporting, optimal financial management, and efficient operations across DocuSign. The team consists of Accounting, Financial Planning &amp; Analysis, Revenue Operations, Investor Relations, and Business Operations. The Finance team partners across all organizations in DocuSign to appropriately allocate resources, inform key business decisions, and facilitate the execution of our operational plans.
<br><br>
This position
<br>As part of the Sales Finance team, this person will be responsible for forecasting Global Commissions expense across all booking generating teams. This person will also be the finance business partner to the Sales Operation and Sales Support teams. The individual will be responsible for performing financial analysis and modeling, designing and preparing financial plans and budgets, as well as analyzing, interpreting, and communicating financial information to management.
<br><br>
This position reports to the Sr Manager of North America Sales Finance.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Own the Global Commissions forecasting inclusive of modeling and variance analysis, while partnering with the respective FP&amp;A, Sales Operations, and Sales Compensation teams</li>
<li>Lead Sales Operation and Sales Support business partners in building monthly, quarterly, and annual budget plans</li>
<li>Support the month-end, quarter-end and year-end corporate reporting requirements</li>
<li>Create presentations and reports to inform senior leadership on the financial performance of their teams and provide guidance on near-term and long-term opportunities and risk</li>
<li>Partner with the broader Go-To-Market Finance team on insight analyses, process improvements, and financial modeling</li>
</ul>
Basic Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree in Business, Finance, or related field</li>
<li>3+ years of experience in finance with emphasis on budgeting, forecasting, planning, analysis, and modeling</li>
<li>FP&amp;A experience at a publicly-traded company</li>
<li>Fundamental understanding of Subscription-as-a-Service (SaaS) business model</li>
<li>Proficient in Excel and PC</li>
<li>Proficient verbal and written communication skills</li>
</ul>
Preferred Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of sales compensation plans and quotas</li>
<li>High degree of analytical and problem-solving capability</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and balance multiple responsibilities simultaneously</li>
<li>Experience with Adaptive and NetSuite or other large well-known ERP / accounting systems</li>
<li>Ability to interact professionally with all levels of the organization</li>
</ul>
