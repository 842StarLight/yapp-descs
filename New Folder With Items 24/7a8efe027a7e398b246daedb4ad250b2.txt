<h2>Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Martindale-Avvo is looking for a full time Sales Development Representative to join our New Providence, NJ, team! The Sales Development Representative plays a critical part in supporting the Martindale-Hubbell sales team and will drive new business opportunities to provide marketing solutions to our law firm clients.<br>
<br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
<b>Lead Generation:</b> Prospect and engage with prospective clients via outbound and cold calling activity.<br>
<br>
<b>Relationship Building:</b> Grow and maintain an active pipeline, secure and identify qualified ‘sales ready’ leads by identifying key decision maker.<br>
<br>
<b>Knowledgeable:</b><b> </b>Ability to communicate effectively the benefits of digital marketing solutions, demonstrate urgency and understanding of client’s needs.<br>
<br>
<b>Achieve:</b> Understand key performance metrics, routinely meet and exceed sales quotas.<br>
<br>
<b>Must Haves:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 years of B2B sales experience, preferably in a telesales environment with a proven track record of success.</li>
<li>Self-starter, with a hunter mentality.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills with prior experience in building client relations.</li>
<li>Tech-savvy, experience in using MS Office or Google Suite applications.</li>
<li>Prior use and knowledge of Salesforce.com or equivalent CRM tools.</li>
</ul>
<br>
