<h2>Contact Center Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">BDC Manager
<br><br>
Napleton Automotive Group owns and operates 55 retail automotive dealerships in eight states. Our group employs 3,500 people nationwide and has rental car facilities, body shops, and a large real estate portfolio as well. Napleton ranked as 20th out of top 150 automotive groups in the U.S. for 2018 based on new car volume.
<br><br>
Napleton Automotive Group is looking to fill this position within the BDC which will contribute to the success of the dealerships by supporting the service departments through the activities of the call center. This position creates a climate for customer satisfaction through clear and responsive communication.
<br><br>
<b>Job Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Supports business by meeting goals established by BDC management by providing the customer with excellent service and responsive follow up.</li>
<li>Responsible for responding to all incoming and outgoing phone calls, emails, and other leads on a daily basis during regular business hours.</li>
<li>Follows up on incoming calls on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Able to communicate persuasively with customers to set and confirm appointments for the service departments as required.</li>
<li>Responds to CSI interviews/cases for sales and service.</li>
<li>Reviews website on a weekly basis and communicates any required revisions to the appropriate departmental manager.</li>
<li>Uses dealership computer software daily to satisfy job requirements.</li>
<li>Manages a team of BDC Representatives in order to effectively operate the BDC Operation.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>Job/Education Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>At least 3 years of previous Call Center experience required.</li>
<li>Excellent oral/written communication skills required.</li>
<li>Computer software experience with MS Office necessary</li>
<li>Strong customer service orientation essential for success.</li>
<li>Ability to work well on a team, and with other dealership management personnel</li>
</ul>
</div>
