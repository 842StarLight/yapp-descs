<div><b>Role Overview:</b></div>
<div>
The Financial Analyst position is an analytical role responsible for building tools that help our venture backed startup clients make sound financial and strategic decisions. The Financial Analyst will work closely with our CFO’s and VP’s by cleaning and analyzing data, building complex financial models, developing budget vs. actual analyses, and providing general analytical support to our clients. Experience with building financial models and budgets from scratch is a must and experience with early stage startups is highly preferred.</div>
<br>
<div>
<b>Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Build complex financial models across a variety of industries: SaaS, Fintech, consumer products, healthcare, etc.</li>
<li>Develop Key Performance Indicator (KPI) dashboards, monitor KPIs and highlight trends and variances.</li>
<li>Work with cross functional teams at the client (Engineering, Product, G&amp;A, Sales, etc.) to develop budgets.</li>
<li>Analyze and model ROI for client marketing programs</li>
<li>Support the client’s senior management team and department heads with in-depth analysis</li>
<li>Leverage internal client, Attivo-aggregated, and self-sourced relevant benchmark data to drive value-added analysis for clients that identifies opportunities and potential areas of improvement</li>
<li>Prepare presentations to Board of Directors and senior management team</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>Requirements</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>3+ year experience in Financial Planning &amp; Analysis</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Accounting or Finance, MBA a plus</li>
<li>Proven understanding of GAAP in constructing financial modeling and reports</li>
<li>Strong leadership skills required</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, presentation, and writing abilities</li>
<li>Advanced Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint skills</li>
<li>Highest standards of accuracy and precision; highly organized</li>
<li>Proven ability to meet deadlines and demonstrate effective problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to roll-up sleeves and work with team members in a hands-on way</li>
</ul>
