<h2>Service Desk Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Club at Los Gatos is seeking Service Desk Representatives to join our team! At The Club, we strive to provide the best member experience and take great pride in the quality of service, instruction and solutions for success under one roof. We seek energetic individuals who are passionate about helping others, delivering exceptional service and teamwork.</p>
<p>The Club's Service Desk Representative Job Description</p>
<p>Role and Responsibilities<br>The Service Desk Representative is a high profile customer service position delivering beyond our member's expectations. They contribute to member retention, as well as new membership sales. This person has the responsibility of being responsive to members; checking members in and acting as a resource for members' needs of goods and services while in the club facilities. The Service Desk Representative acts as the clubs first line of communication with members and creates a friendly and welcoming environment.</p>
<p>Specific Responsibilities:</p>
<p>Essential Functions</p>
<p>-Say, "Hi," and greet members/ guests enthusiastically and efficiently handle the check-in process<br>-Answer telephone and field calls<br>-Assist members with purchases of retail goods and club services<br>-Work as a TEAM with all other departments</p>
<p>Other Functions</p>
<p>-Be knowledgeable about all programs and activities offered by The Club<br>-Promote club services and activities<br>-Assist with keeping the club well stocked and clean<br>-Maintain Slack communication and log all maintenance and inventory needs<br>-Handle member injury and illness incident reporting</p>
<p>Qualifications and Education Requirements:</p>
<p>-Must be detail oriented, organized and highly responsive with a commitment to customer service<br>-Strong written and verbal communication skills<br>-Basic accounting skills, customer service skills, results oriented, basic computer skills, ability to multi-task, and commitment to the fitness industry<br>-Minimum GED/High School Diploma or equivalent<br>-AED/CPR/First Aid Certified<br>-Proof of citizenship or legal status</p>
<p>Physical and Working Conditions</p>
<p>-Ability to stand for several hours; ability to lift 25 lbs<br>-Fitness club environment.<br>-Must be able to work morning (4:30am-11am) and/or evening shifts (5:00pm-11:15pm) and/or weekend shifts (5:30am-1:00pm or 1:00pm-9:15pm)</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Salary: $13.00 to $16.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>customer service: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
