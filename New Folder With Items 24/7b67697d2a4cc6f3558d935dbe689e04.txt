<h2>Regional Good & Services Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Founded in 1921 Newmont Mining Corporation is one of the largest gold mining companies in the world. The North American region consists of multiple surface and underground operations spread throughout Northern Nevada and our Cripple Creek and Victor Mine in Colorado.</p>
<p>
Newmont prides itself on safety and embraces diversity, engages employees, fosters innovation, rewards high performance and develops great leaders.</p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
Dimensions
</div>
<div><p>Will have direct responsibility to implement continuously and execute Newmont Goldcorp´s sourcing and procurement strategy, policies and guidelines</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Purpose
</div>
<div><p>Responsible for the implementation, continuous improvement and execution of Newmont Goldcorp’s procurement strategy, policies and guidelines to drive shared goals and objectives across the entire supply chain and specifically for goods and service oriented categories and contracts that are regional scope. Provide counseling to Regional Category Management in develop procurement and sourcing vision for the region in alignment with Corporate and Regional objectives and builds strong working relationships with internal stakeholders. Identify and capture new enterprise spend areas to add value and drive cost savings in the overall regional Supply Chain. Actively demonstrate the Newmont Goldcorp´s values of Safety, Integrity, Sustainability, Inclusion and responsibility at all times.</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Essential Duties
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Work directly with Regional G&amp;S Sr. Manager and key stakeholders at site level to deliver on site safety and production targets and ensure alignment with regional and global targets, strategy, and policies. Work with regional and global Supply Chain team in the implementation, continuous improvement and execution of Newmont’s procurement strategy, policies and guidelines to drive shared goals and objectives.</li>
<li>
Is accountable for value-added, cost savings, and other performance metrics.Responsible to understand and drive the usage of SAP throughout Supply Chain and operations. Develop, recommend and implement programs to enhance working relationships and communication processes to ensure achievement of leading practice procurement objectives.</li>
<li>
Oversee the rationalization and selection of suppliers to optimize Newmont’s supply base; ensure strong working relationships with these suppliers are developed and maintained, and provide direction to monitor and continually improve suppliers’ performance. Review key agreements and contracts to protect and advance Newmont Goldcorp’s interests.</li>
<li>
Work with regional and global category managers in the category sourcing process and ensure alignment between site operations and regional and global contracts</li>
<li>
Identify and capture new regional spend areas where Regional and Global Supply Chain can add value and drive cost savings; aggressively track and improve non-compliance with preferred suppliers</li>
<li>
The above duties and responsibilities are representative of the nature and level of work assigned</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Training &amp; Experience
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business, Supply Chain, Engineering, Accounting or related field.</li>
<li>
Membership of a Contracts Management institute (or similar) desirable</li>
<li>
MBA desirable</li>
<li>
Ten (10) years of experience in strategic sourcing events at regional level.</li>
<li>
Mining experience preferred</li>
<li>
Strong computer skills using office software such as Excel, Word, PowerPoint, and Visio.</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of SAP or equivalent enterprise level software packages is necessary.</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul></div>
