<b>
Job Summary</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>We are seeking an experienced <b>Senior IT Systems Analyst</b> for our <b>IT Technology Partner Team</b> located in <b>Wilmington, MA</b>.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The <b>Senior IT Systems Analyst </b>will partner primarily with the Sales and Marketing, Corporate Communications and other Enterprise business functions to understand the business landscape through dialogue and observation and then leveraging this business knowledge along with their understanding of various technologies to “translate” that into identifying business needs/requirements, configuring a system or partnering with technical teams and/or developers to solve various business problems with technology. Additionally, this role may also support other business functions in a similar capacity.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Partners with other technical teams and/or developers to design, configure and build system specifications to work to solve various business problems with technology. Acts as a liaison with functional teams and other technology areas. Documents and may test functionality of the system; corrects deficiencies and may adjust configuration as required to reflect improvement in business process, data management/integrity, application effectiveness. Evaluates integration between applications and infrastructure, operational requirements, and characteristics/functionality of overall system. Responsible for meeting software compliance and company’s system architecture standards.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The following are responsibilities related to the <b>Senior IT Systems Analyst:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Applying technical and business knowledge to successfully perform their activities and achieve their accountabilities</li>
<li>Applying the TOC (Theory of Constraints) thinking tools</li>
<li>Unpacking the business problem, articulating the needs from business partners</li>
<li>Translating core business problems and needs into technology solutions</li>
<li>Asking good questions through the art of humble inquiry</li>
<li>Providing radical candor feedback to business partners</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<li>Prior experience in technology roles and environments leveraging Salesforce.com, and/or other Marketing tools such as Pardot, Drupal, SharePoint or other web development technologies. Additionally BI and Analytics tools and technologies is desired.</li>
<li>Prior experience with pricing, quote generation and contract management processes and technologies.</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills<br>
</li>
</ul>
