<h2>Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Job Overview:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Macys Merchandising systems manage the onboarding and maintenance of Product information, assortments and Pricing for all products that Macys sells. Members of team use cutting edge technologies and data techniques to discover solutions and build knowledge from the ground up, driving results that produce tangible impact and sustainable change. We are looking for Architect who have passion to build new robust software, deploy in cloud and is responsible for providing solutions for wide array of problems in order optimization, pick path optimization, work scheduling and assignment and optimized bin packing algorithms. The role involves building cutting edge solutions using modern technology and frameworks including Spring Boot, and Google Cloud platform technologies such as Google Pub/sub, GCP, GCE, Spark etc.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>The Architect role in Macy’s Technology is a hands-on leader in the design and development of cross-functional, multi-platform application systems. Translate business requirements into well-engineered, tested and deployed application systems that are used by the business and support these systems while in production. Interface with internal customers to determine the most efficient and cost-effective approach to meet business requirements and designs, develops, tests, installs, and deploys the required software applications. Develop solutions utilizing a variety of software technologies, including new code construction, modifications to existing modules, configuration and/or package implementation.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The Architect will work with the Merchandising Principal Architect to understand the current landscape of Merchandising Master data and how it is consumed in the enterprise. Help build out an integration roadmap for Stibo (STEP) to become the single point of Product data authorship. Design the inbound integration/outbound points as necessary for the STEP product. Contribute to building out the roadmap of the MDM capabilities and need to help drive the sunsetting of existing Product authoring systems. Perform other duties as assigned.</div>
