<h2>Events Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Events Marketing Specialist role is to support the Sr. Manager, Corporate Events and Global Marketing department in managing process documents, budget managing and event planning duties that help the development and execution of various event marketing elements.
<br><br>
This role will support the Global Marketing team with small and large high profile client events, meetings and conferences both internal and external facing.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain budget tracker documents, invoice processes, budget meetings as they relate to Event needs</li>
<li>Assist Director in Marketing and Sales events as needed</li>
<li>Keep track of contracts pertaining to event related vendors, venues and key contacts with legal team and stay organized on deadlines</li>
<li>Coordinate all program elements leading up to and onsite including accommodations, venues, activities, transportation, F&amp;B, etc</li>
<li>Provide support to the Director including meeting notes, presentation development, budget tracking and reconciliation, event proposals etc.</li>
<li>Organize a database of vendor relationships</li>
<li>Lead volunteers on day of event as well as welcome all clients/attendees</li>
<li>Be present on day of event with problem solving skills for anything not working</li>
<li>Assist in scouting locations, providing volunteer help and post-event breakdown</li>
<li>Sustain in-depth knowledge of event and media/marketing industry trends in order to help create unique event offerings that differentiate Integral Ad Science above the rest</li>
<li>Perform other related duties and tasks as assigned or as it becomes evident</li>
</ul>
<b>What you'll need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>At least 3+ years of relevant event marketing experience (such as advertising agency, media sales/marketing, event marketing, client-side marketing).</li>
<li>Four-year college degree or equivalent combination of skills, training and experience. Degree in marketing/communications preferred.</li>
<li>Detail oriented and comfort working with numbers (i.e. marketing budgets)</li>
<li>Ability to measure and track event success in partnership with the sales and demand generation team</li>
<li>Ability to complete projects accurately and in a timely manner</li>
<li>A clear understanding of the importance of client service and professionalism</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, both written and oral</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Must be solutions oriented and pro-active</li>
<li>Willing to travel as needed</li>
</ul>
