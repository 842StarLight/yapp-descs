<h2>Magazine Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>Five Star Professional (FSP) conducts market research to define and promote professional excellence in the professions we serve. The Five Star designation assists consumers in selecting a service professional based on an objective research methodology tailored to the specific profession.</p>
<p>Five Star Professional joins forces with local and national media partners to publish our research to consumers and promote Five Star-designated professionals in more than 45 markets across the United States.</p>
<p>Five Star Professional was founded in 2003 and is based in Eagan, Minnesota.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>We are seeking a high energy Inside Sales Representative to join our team. This person who joins the team in this position charged with spearheading the sales effort of a new, confidential product introduction. This product introduction will drive future growth for the organization. Weekly interaction with senior management team will be critical to the success of the position.</p>
