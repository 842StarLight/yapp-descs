<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Our Global Technology Services department is currently seeking a <b>Software Configuration Engineer</b> to join our team in Omaha.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As a Software Configuration Engineer, you are responsible for supporting the Intrado CI/CD stack (i.e. GitHub, Artifactory, Jenkins and RedHat Ansible Tower), as well as Bamboo license management servers. Our ideal candidate is someone who has a good understanding of software builds and the tools associated with software releases and is excited to be a part of the Intrado Digital Transformation!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Day to day responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assist in managing multiple systems including the Change Management System (CMS) taking steps to ensure it runs effectively according to established policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Manages the source code repository and release codes ensure it is completed in a timely and effective manner</li>
<li>
Assist in upgrades to the systems as necessary to ensure systems are up-to-date and remain efficient</li>
<li>
Assist in working with development teams to add new builds to the system for future use</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot and resolve issues pertaining to code disparity between production and quality assurance</li>
<li>
Adding and removing approved users from the system to ensure it is up to date</li>
<li>
Ability to participate in an on-call rotation as needed</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
</div>

<div>
Qualifications<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Education</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, MIS, or a related field required, however equivalent work experience in a similar position may be substituted to education</li>
<li>
Certifications may count towards experience</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Experience and Technical Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of experience in software engineering, systems administration, software configuration management or a related field required</li>
<li>
Experience with Windows and Linux operating systems</li>
<li>
Experience with RedHat Tower (Ansible), GitHub, Artifactory, Jenkins and/or SonarQube</li>
<li>
Experience with Puppet and Chef are preferred, but not required</li>
<li>
Strong troubleshooting and analytical skills</li>
<li>
Excellent communication both verbal and written</li>
<li>
Ability to work both independently and as part of larger team</li>
</ul>
