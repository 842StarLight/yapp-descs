<h2>Data Support Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p></p>
<div>
The Data Support Manager will expand Global Risk Management (GRM) Data Management Office (DMO) team to support and influence the definition and execution of the GRM data management strategy and provide oversight and reporting of the GRM strategy and execution of enterprise data programs.<br>
<ul>
<li>Support Risk DME accountabilities and collaborate with other DMEs as needed.</li>
<li>Expand and improve data management reporting and metrics to allow CRO, Risk DME and Executives to monitor and measure progress, prioritize and fund activities, support issues and address key CRO questions on data management including:</li>
<li>Identifying &amp; resolving the GRM data issues and risks; Develop the timing and plan for resolution;</li>
<li>Identify current state &amp; future state planned improvements for GRM data quality and ADS adoption</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
Role Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Support DME accountabilities including but not limited to: EDM/GRM adherence reporting, oversight &amp; escalation, FRY14 and RDA attestations/assessments, GRM Leadership team updates, GRM Risk ID, RCSA &amp; QBR, strengthening GRM data support roles, EDM,EDA, Audit, Compliance and regulatory mandates.</li>
<li>Learn and provide transparency on Risk area &amp; GRM area data management processes, assets, performance, issues, risks, projects and activities.</li>
<li>Influence and collaborate with Risk teams to define individual data strategies, plans and activities. Become a trusted advisor to the Risk area data teams.</li>
<li>Influence and collaborate with Risk teams to define and develop Risk Area (e.g. Credit Risk, Market Risk) &amp; GRM reporting and metrics (ADS adoption, Data Quality, Issue Management, Adherence) to monitor and measure multi-year data management efforts.</li>
<li>Support the tracking and remediation of data incidents / issues and drive best practices across GRM and Risk, Finance and Treasury.</li>
<li>Conducting and leading GRM DMO meetings and representing GRM DMO and DME in various data management forums.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
Business:<br>
<ul>
<li>7-10 years of Risk Management (e.g. Credit, Market, Operational) and/or Data Management experience.</li>
<li>5-7 years program management experience working on enterprise (e.g. BCBS239, Basel, CCAR), Risk (e.g. Risk Appetite, Forecasting, AML, CECL) or Data (e.g. EDM, EDA) programs.</li>
<li>5-7 data analysis and reporting experience.</li>
<li>3-5 years experience developing program management status reporting (e.g. data adherence, ads development) and operational metrics (e.g. issues, data quality, process performance, ads adoption).</li>
<li>3-5 years experience defining, developing and implementing business processes.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
Other:<br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent relationship and collaboration skills and an ability to work with many different groups (Enterprise EDMG and EDA teams, Technology) and management levels (Risk teams, Executives)</li>
<li>Ability to influence and obtain agreement on decisions and actions to be taken,</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal skills including ability to conduct meetings and provide Executive presentations,</li>
<li>Adaptability to ever changing regulatory, business and enterprise priorities while progressing overall priorities,</li>
<li>Excellent time management and organizational skills and ability to work independently but keep management team up to date</li>
<li>Strong data analysis skills and ability to draw conclusions from many disparate data sources and support finding with facts.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
Technical:<br>
<ul>
<li>Expert level using MS office including Excel, SharePoint, PowerPoint</li>
<li>2-3 years experience with Business Intelligence tools (e.g. Tableau, MicroStrategy). BI tool development a plus.</li>
<li>Query /program languages (SQL, Toad, Python, SAS), data tool (Callibra, Informatica) and issue management tools (eDIM, Jira) a plus</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<p>
Manage a group or process(potentially global). Work with business data users to define the use of data within risk systems. Recommend remediation of control gaps and implements effective risk controls of our data processes and systems. Participate in and lead user acceptance testing of data control processes. Understand existing risk systems, data flows, and infrastructure to identify gaps between business usage of data and current implementations. Recommend alternate data sources and processes to improve the control environment. Lead cross functional strategic efforts to remediate data quality issues. Proactively monitor and remediate incoming data used in risk measurement and reporting. Ensure the completeness, validity, and accuracy of data on a daily basis. Work with other groups as needed, including Middle Office, Sales and Trading, Technology, Compliance, Operational Risk, and Enterprise Capital Management to ensure effective controls over data for GMRM.</p>
