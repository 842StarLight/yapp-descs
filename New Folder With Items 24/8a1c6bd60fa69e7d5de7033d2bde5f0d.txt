<h2>Order Department Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Description</b><br>
<div><p><b>Overview</b></p></div>
<div>
The Department Order Supervisor is responsible forassisting in the training of these employees; assisting in the planning, assigning, and directing of their work; assisting in the appraising of their performance; addressing complaints and resolving problems.<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Processes SLO, Military, Retail, and Sample orders for merchandise received by mail, telephone, or personally from customer or company employee by performing the following duties</li>
<li> Edits orders received for out of stocks and nomenclature</li>
<li> Informs salesperson or customer of shipping date, anticipated delays, and any additional information needed by customer or salesperson</li>
<li> Writes or types order form, or enters data into computer, to determine availability of product for customer</li>
<li> Records or files copy of orders received</li>
<li> Check inventory control and notifies stock control departments of orders that would deplete stock</li>
<li> Routes orders to warehouse for filling and follows up on orders to ensure delivery by specified dates</li>
<li> Prepares invoices and shipping documents such as bill of lading</li>
<li> Recommends type of packing or labeling needed on order</li>
<li> Receives and checks customer complaints</li>
<li> Answers inquiries regarding product availability and order shipments</li>
<li> Confers with sales, shipping, warehouse, or common carrier personnel to expedite or trace missing or delayed shipments</li>
<li> Processes returns of merchandise, coordinating with warehouse and inventory control</li>
<li> Prepares credit memos for returns, mis-shipments, and cancelled orders
</li>
<li> Prepares various reports for management</li>
<li> Researches payment discrepancies as reported by the Accounting Department</li>
<li> Directly assists with the supervision of employees</li>
<li> Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with the organization's policies and applicable laws</li>
</ul>
<div><ul><li> Perform other related duties as assigned</li></ul></div>
