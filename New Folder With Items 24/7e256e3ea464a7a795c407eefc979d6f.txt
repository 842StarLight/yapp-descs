<h2>Dispensary Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Dispensary Agent Job Description</b></p>
<p>Dispensary Agents will assist the general manager in all administrative, clinical and operational functions within a fast-paced, clinically-oriented, environment. Agents will educate patients and caregivers on medical cannabis products under the direct supervision of a manager. Agents will promote a work environment that is positive, patient service-oriented and compliant within the guidelines of the state and company. The ideal candidate should be passionate about their role and have a strong willingness to help people become educated about medical cannabis.</p>
<p><b>Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist manager in on-boarding patients and filing state required documentation based on Compassionate Care Act guidelines</li>
<li>Electronically file and organize documents</li>
<li>Perform the timely triage of documents and stream-line tasks appropriately to the manager.</li>
<li>Answer phone calls from patients, schedule appointments, and any other functions necessary for Dispensary operations</li>
<li>Prepare and provide support in during audits</li>
<li>Accountable for accurately utilizing Point of Sale system</li>
<li>Provides outstanding patient service</li>
<li>Inspire patient confidence and create patient loyalty</li>
<li>Develop knowledge of various products and the Medical Cannabis Program and Federal law regarding medical cannabis</li>
<li>Execute dispensary protocols including safety protocols</li>
<li>Complies with all security and legal requirements</li>
<li>Ensure the store is well maintained</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by the Manager</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Compassionate and strong work ethic</li>
<li>A high school diploma or higher and 2+ years of experience working in retail setting or managed care/health care organization</li>
<li>Proven track record in ability to cultivate long- term patient/customer relationships</li>
<li>Previous retail experience in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Exceptional patient service skills; experience in health care related field is a plus</li>
<li>Desire to learn about our products in order to assist patients; must have a passion for medical cannabis</li>
<li>Knowledge of basic computer skills, Point of Sale software and cash management is a must</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and must have a team-player mentality</li>
<li>Ability to deal with problems and resolve in a professional manner</li>
<li>Must be able to accommodate scheduling expectations including weekend, evening, and holiday shifts</li>
<li>Must be 21 years of age and pass a background check</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
