Human Resources</b> develops and executes successful recruiting programs, learning and expertise-development initiatives, strategic staffing assignments, effective reporting and analytics, performance management processes, and work-life benefits and operations.</p>
<p><b>
RECRUITER</b></p>
<p>
We seek a creative and energetic recruiting professional<b> </b>to join our Evanston, IL office. Recruiters have responsibility for hiring talent across North America through creative sourcing, coordinating the resume review and interview process, and working collaboratively across offices with hiring managers and recruiters<b>.</b><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Source, pre-screen and attract/close candidates for experienced hiring opportunities;</li>
<li>
Manage requisitions for experienced hiring;</li>
<li>
Forge relationship with hiring managers, plan and execute sourcing strategy, design interview process (if necessary), etc.;</li>
<li>
Manage interview scheduling and logistics, including candidate evaluation;</li>
<li>
Collect feedback from internal stake holders and new hires for process improvement;</li>
<li>
Maintain recruiting tracking databases for statistical analysis;</li>
<li>
Support other recruiting projects and initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS degree with strong academic background required;</li>
<li>
Up to 3 years relevant recruiting experience, technology/software recruiting strongly preferred;</li>
<li>
Proficiency with MS Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint);</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational skills and an ability to multi-task;</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills;</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and commitment;</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work in a team environment;</li>
<li>
Good sense of humor appreciated.</li>
</ul>
