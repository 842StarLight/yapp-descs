<h2>Part-time HVAC Technical Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>General Filters is a privately held and family owned manufacturer of Whole House Residential Indoor Air Quality Products and Oil Filters. Located in Novi, MI and founded in 1937, the Company brings professional experience spanning over three generations of implementing strict quality and assurance systems to deliver premium dependable products and providing great customer service.</p>
<p><b>The Position</b></p>
<p>General Filters is looking for <b>Part-time</b> <b>HVAC</b> <b>Technical Support </b>to join its growing team and make a direct impact within the Company. This position is a great match for someone who has been or is currently a residential heating and cooling or HVAC Estimator, Technician, Installer, or Contractor, and considers themselves to be a heating and cooling expert!</p>
<p>This self-motivated individual is responsible for technical and product related telephone support calls which involve evaluation and troubleshooting. This may include tasks such as resolving installation issues, product recommendation, and identifying and troubleshooting issues. The goal of this position is to transfer technical expertise to sales agents, wholesalers, homeowners, as well as HVAC contractors and installers.</p>
