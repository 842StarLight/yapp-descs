<p><b>About TapClicks</b></p>
<p>
TapClicks is a leader in Marketing Reporting, Operations and Search Engine Optimization SaaS solutions for Enterprises, Agencies and Media companies. It has been recognized as #1 fastest growing company in Silicon Valley by Inc 500; the Best Technology Product or Service by MITX and #163 by Deloitte’s 2018 Technology Fast 500. TapClicks is the only end-to-end marketing operations platform that allows companies to streamline their marketing operations, manage and optimize campaigns and report progress from any device at any time. TapClicks uses cutting edge technology (big data analytics, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) to continuously deliver innovative solutions to our customers. TapClicks Marketing operations platform is widely used by over 400,000 end customers globally.<br>
</p>

<li>Support day to day needs of customer base through regular interactions and problem resolution</li>
<li>Drive customer engagement and adoption of the Tapclicks platform</li>
<li>Maximize customer satisfaction and eliminate customer attrition</li>
<li>Conduct business reviews with clients</li>
<li>Provide ongoing platform and data-driven-story-telling training to clients</li>
