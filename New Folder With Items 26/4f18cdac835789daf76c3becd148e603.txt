<h2>Returns Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Cisco Meraki is seeking an enthusiastic Returns Specialist to join our Operations team and multi-functional projects in our CH office. As the fastest growing cloud-managed networking team in the world, our products and technology architecture are changing the face of enterprise networking and making cloud-managed IT a reality. We also understand, by simplifying powerful technology we can free passionate people to focus on their mission. Are you ready to join the team?</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Process return transactions of incoming equipment from our warehouses</li>
<li>Authorize incoming return requests based on company policies; research outlier cases and coordinate with the account executives when higher level approval is necessary</li>
<li>Communicate with customers, partners &amp; distributors in regards to return logistics, credit processing &amp; exception cases</li>
<li>Coordinate with support techs, product management, sales executives and administrators to ensure that we provide the best possible customer experience</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements:</b></h2>
<p>Work and Education:</p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor's degree, preferably in a relevant field</li>
<li>2 - 3 years of transactional operations experience is highly preferable.</li>
<li>2+ years of clerical or administrative experience in a field where attention to detail is necessary, such as medicine or law, also considered.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Knowledge and Skills:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of RMA procedures is preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrable experience with data entry and invoice auditing.</li>
<li>Customer service background, preferably customers who want to return merchandise or products.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and teamwork skills.</li>
<li>Familiarity with Salesforce and Dynamics GP (or other ERP system) preferred.</li>
</ul>
