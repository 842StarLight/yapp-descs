<h2>Social Media Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The Social Media Specialist will play a key role in management and execution for all social media efforts and provide assistance with associated digital marketing efforts for Farmer Focus – SVO’s mission-based and values-driven, organic meat brand. The Social Media Specialist will be required to run social media campaigns and drive engagement by creating high-quality original content, engage social influencers, manage our community by responding to comments, and oversee all activity across platforms. This position will support company growth, new customer acquisition, and brand expansion. The Social Media Specialist will work closely with the existing Marketing and Sales teams to provide tools for success. <b><i>Candidates must submit a resume, cover letter and work samples with for consideration.</i></b></p>
<p><b>
Reasonable Accommodations Statement</b></p>
<p>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable qualified individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions Statement(s)</b></p>
<p><b>
Social Media Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage all company social media pages: create all organic posts and content, monitor all audience interaction with the pages, determine need for paid social media posts.</li>
<li>Ensure that all social media adheres to the Farmer Focus brand standards.</li>
<li>Formulate high-quality written and visual content for each social media campaign.</li>
<li>Build a social media presence by maintaining a solid online presence.</li>
<li>Monitor the company's brand on social media.</li>
<li>Build brand awareness by engaging relevant social media influencers.</li>
<li>Manage our online communities to ensure respectful and appropriate engagement.</li>
<li>Respond to comments on each of our accounts.</li>
<li>Oversee any customer service provided via social media.</li>
<li>Analyze data to determine whether social media campaigns have achieved their objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Digital Asset Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create, organize, and manage the digital brand assets library – photos, images, logos, etc.</li>
<li>Ensure that all digital assets adhere to the Farmer Focus brand standards.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Digital Campaign Coordination</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with our agency and assist in executing plans for paid digital advertising.</li>
<li>Work with the sales team, onboard all customers for social &amp; digital ad plans.</li>
<li>Collaborate with customers’ social media and digital marketing team members for co-marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Ensure that all social media and digital efforts adhere to the Farmer Focus brand standards.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Website Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize the existing CMS to Manage all appropriate content on the company’s website.</li>
<li>Ensure that all website content adheres to the Farmer Focus brand standards.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Budget &amp; Cost Management</b></p>
<ul><li>Be mindful of the brand’s Marketing budget. Compare quotes from various vendors/partners.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Miscellaneous</b></p>
<ul>
<li>May require travel to company events and trade shows.</li>
<li>Perform other marketing-related tasks as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>
 <b>Knowledge/Education/Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree with a major in Marketing or Communications, Graphic Design, Business, or related field.</li>
<li>At least 1-2 years of Social Media Marketing experience, preferably in the food industry or with a values-driven brand.</li>
<li>Experience developing social media campaigns.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication, and presentation skills. Must be able to convey information in a clear, concise and consistent manner.</li>
<li>Ability to develop the right voice for each social media platform.</li>
<li>Proven ability to build social media communities.</li>
<li>Understanding of graphic design principles.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work on cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to handle a variety of responsibilities simultaneously in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to set priorities in order to complete assignments in an efficient, accurate and timely manner.</li>
<li>Problem solving skills: ability to identify potential problems and proactively generate ideas for acceptable solutions.</li>
<li>Must possess: a sense of urgency, accountability, responsibility, ambition.</li>
<li>Positive, energetic, confident, professional and team-oriented attitude.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Other Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Vision (Near, Color, Depth Perception) Must be able to pass color vision test.</li>
<li>Ability to 1 – 2 flights of stairs 2 – 6 times daily.</li>
</ul>

WORK ENVIRONMENT:</b></p>

Work conducted primarily in an office environment but will be required to access the production environment. Must be able to tolerate the varying heat, wet and chill of the plant. Hearing and eye protective equipment required in the production area. Required to wear hair and beard nets in the production area.</p>
