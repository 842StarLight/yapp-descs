<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>This California based Account Executive position focuses on new customer sales and drives revenue of Curvature products &amp; services within a given territory. We are looking for an ambitious sales professional and leader to be part of a fast-growing company that rewards self-direction, celebrates innovation, and bends the trajectory of an industry in favor of our clients. This role will focus primarily on new business development within Mid-market and Enterprise organizations.</p>
<p><b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect, identify and build relationships with potential new clients and key decision makers</li>
<li>Manage and engage existing clients to drive further business growth</li>
<li>Manage sales process through prospecting, lead qualification, forecasting, resource allocation, account strategy and planning</li>
<li>Build an action plan, develop goals, strategies, tactics and timelines</li>
<li>Consult with clients and prepare proposals and quotes based on their needs</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of the market and competitors</li>
<li>Clearly and effectively articulate compelling value propositions and solutions for Curvature’s products to meet client requirements</li>
<li>Effectively demonstrate value proposition in person, on conference calls or using screen share technology</li>
<li>Manage complex sales-cycles utilizing Salesforce</li>
<li>Develop relationships with account managers to increase channel partnerships</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years’ experience in an IT sales role with demonstrated experience in consultative, enterprise-wide solution selling including ability to predict, build and present innovative and creative product offerings to targeted prospects including senior executives and technology teams</li>
<li>Consistent history of achieving and exceeding sales goals for the last 3 years</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in a related field such as Business, Marketing, Finance or IT</li>
<li>Demonstrated new business sales expertise with proven results</li>
<li>Experience in IT industry specifically in sales and/or contract management</li>
<li>Excellent negotiation and consultation skills including ability to overcome objections and close complex, large-scale deals, both directly and working in collaboration with key channel partners</li>
<li>Ability to identify and target the decision-maker within a prospect and listen, ask questions, solve problems, and close deals</li>
<li>Independent and self-motivated with the ability to work remotely and independently while achieving results</li>
<li>Strong business acumen &amp; consultative approach to sales</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience with Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and order management systems such as SalesForce and Oracle to manage sales process</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills with the ability to build customer relationships</li>
<li>Ability to work and promote a positive team environment</li>
<li>Results-oriented individual with the ability to multi-task and remain calm in a fast pace environment</li>
<li>Ability to respond to rapid change, perform duties with accuracy and with a strong degree of urgency</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills and the ability to solve problems and meet deadlines</li>
</ul>
