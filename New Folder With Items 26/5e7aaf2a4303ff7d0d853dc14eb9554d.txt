<h2>Site Accounting Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">High Tide Oil Company, Inc is currently seeking applicants for Site Accounting Representative.
<br>This position performs a variety of duties in a fast paced, high volume environment.
<br>A qualified candidate will be responsible for various bookkeeping and accounting processes including, but not limited to, cash handling, review department reports, processing day reports, inputting fuel deliveries and recording fuel gallons and making daily bank deposits for multiple locations.
<br>Daily commuting between 3 stations located in St. Clair Co is mandatory.
<br>Requirements
<br><ul>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Basic mathematical skills</li>
<li>Ability to handle large amounts of cash and count accurately</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office - Word, Excel, Creating PDF files</li>
<li>Prior bookkeeping experience</li>
<li>High school diploma</li>
</ul>
Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Compile and sort cash, coupons and other documentation daily for 3 stations located in St. Clair County</li>
<li>Insure every transaction, deposit, income, Bill of Laden is recorded promptly and efficiently</li>
<li>Create the daily reports for all 3 stations and uploading the reports into the company's QuickBooks software program</li>
<li>Maintain organized and accurate records of all credit card sales</li>
<li>Upholds all company policies and procedures</li>
<li>Protects company operations by keeping all information confidential</li>
</ul>
