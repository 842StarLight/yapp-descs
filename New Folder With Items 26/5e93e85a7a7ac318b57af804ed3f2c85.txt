<h2>Business Systems Analyst II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Vertex Inc is looking for a Business System Analyst who performs the analysis of product requirements as defined by the product managers, domain specialists, Value added resellers and potential or existing customers. Based on this analysis, creates business requirements, product and technical specifications, and analysis artifacts that are used by Design, Development, Testing, Technical Writing, and Training as the basis for the design, construction, and implementation of business and software solutions.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Essential Job Functions &amp; Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li><div><div>
Provides consultancy support to business stakeholders in defining business processes and their system needs.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Negotiates, facilitates, and communicates expectations between the business stakeholders and the development community.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Participates in planning meetings with one or more customers to implement product enhancements or new solutions within assigned product areas.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Provides factual content and interpretation to the feasibility study for development projects and standard enhancements.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Works closely with product managers, customer groups and domain specialists in the specification and analysis of software products using software process engineering, requirements engineering, product development methodologies, and the techniques and tools necessary for producing quality analysis artifacts.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Provides a business process engineering perspective during design review for projects.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Applies advanced business analysis techniques to completing assigned project tasks. Understands complex project methodologies working as a key project team member.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Provides status on tasks to the project manager, and proactively communicates any scheduling, scope, or cost issues. Provides a task/project status to all stakeholders.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Decompose high-level business and user requirements into functional requirements specified in an appropriate level of detail suitable for use by those must base their work on the requirements.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Represents requirements using alternative views, such as analysis models (diagrams), prototypes, or scenarios where appropriate</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Leads the requirements analysis and verification work effort ensuring that requirement statements are complete, consistent, concise, comprehensible, traceable, feasible, unambiguous, and verifiable, and that they conform to standards.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Participates in requirements prioritization.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Participates in peer reviews and inspections of requirements and analysis documents to ensure that the requirements were interpreted correctly.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Collects data on best practices, business processes, and procedures.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Identifies and researches potential issues. Solves development, process requirement, system, and operational problems.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
 Performs system analysis for enhancements and modifications to application and database systems.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
 Documents all modified and enhanced designs and analysis artifacts.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Understands basic financial information and the future direction of the market sectors for primary products and is able to apply this understanding to their analysis.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Serves as the project lead for business analysis tasks on highly complex projects with minimal supervision. Estimates and schedules business analysis tasks with knowledge of dependencies and risks. Communicates tasks and project related information to other business analyst team members in a timely an effective manner.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
 May coordinate the work assignments of other business analysts, and serve as a mentor to less experienced business analysts.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Assists Sales and Marketing with demos and presentations on the functionality of the application as well as the technical operations.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Lead or participate in other projects or duties as assigned.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Occasional travel may be required.</div></div></li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>EDUCATION, TRAINING:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
<br>
Bachelor’s Degree in related field, specialized training or equivalent work experience. Working towards a recognized Business Systems Analysis Certification preferred.</div></li>
<li><div>
5 years of Business Analysis experience in software development and 8 years of relevant technical, systems analysis, and business work experience.</div></li>
<li><div>
Tax or financial industry experience preferred.</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<b><br>
KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITES:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Requires a working knowledge of a Vertex product, requirements processing, business analysis and modeling concepts, information systems, software development lifecycle, facilitation and influencing abilities.</div></li>
<li><div>
Be knowledgeable in at least one business analysis method, and knowledge of all major methods (Procedural, Object Oriented, Joint Application Development, and Rapid Prototyping).</div></li>
<li><div>
Have a basic knowledge of database design and programming languages.</div></li>
<li><div>
Have experience in participating in and sometimes being the lead on large complex development projects.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrate a knowledge of the latest areas of new technology and how they could apply to their assigned projects.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to apply professional concepts, experience and company objectives in order to perform an in-depth analysis of situations or data to resolve complex issues in creative ways.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to work without supervision with latitude for independent decision making.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to network with key contacts outside own area of expertise.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to listen and understand information and communicate the same.</div></li>
<li><div>
Must possess strong interpersonal, organizational, presentation and facilitation skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Must be results oriented and customer focused</div></li>
<li><div>
Proficiency in Microsoft Office packages.</div></li>
<li><div>
Sufficient knowledge of business communication, including telephone, voicemail, and e-mail and operations of office machines, such as photocopier, scanner, and fax.</div></li>
</ul>
