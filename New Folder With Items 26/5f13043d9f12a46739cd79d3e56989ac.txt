<h2>Leasing Professional</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview:</b></p>
<p>
The Leasing Professional is responsible for coordinating all activities related to apartment rentals, move-ins and move-outs, and lease renewals. This position is also responsible for interacting directly with prospective and current residents to achieve maximum occupancy and ensure resident retention.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generates and handles traffic, qualifies prospective residents, leases apartments, prepares lease documentation, and completes move-in paperwork and procedures.</li>
<li>Collect rental deposits and rent.</li>
<li>Ensures models, make-readies, and designated show apartments are in touring condition daily.</li>
<li>Communicates, as requested and with approval, on social media and on-line advertising sites.</li>
<li>Orients prospective and current residents to the property and greets all guests promptly.
</li>
<li>Processes renewal paperwork and ensures documentation is delivered to current residents as required.</li>
<li>Maintains accurate and detailed records of traffic activity by entering all applicable information into the computer.</li>
<li>Follows up with all prospective residents via phone, mail or email and prospect portals.</li>
<li>Converts phone traffic into scheduled appointments with prospective residents in order to tour the community and enters all traffic into the computer in an accurate timely fashion.</li>
<li>Ensures excellent customer service to prospective and current tenants, and promotes a quality living experience for all residents.</li>
<li>Inspects apartments prior to resident move-ins and ensures apartments are in move-in condition by coordinating move-in/out needs and schedules with maintenance team.</li>
<li>Creates and maintains confidential resident files, which includes reviewing move-in paperwork with new residents.</li>
<li>Completes weekly leasing reports in accurate and timely fashion.</li>
<li>Ensures all open and close procedures are completed daily, including but not limited to, opening and closing show units and office.</li>
<li>Assists with ongoing resident relations throughout occupant’s tenancy by ensuring occupant’s requests are responded to promptly and accurately.</li>
<li>Assists with processing work order requests for residents and ensures work is completed in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Supports management by providing clerical support such as answering phone calls, filing, and processing property invoices, as needed.</li>
<li>Maintain a passing score (minimum of 75%) on all Mystery shopping reports.</li>
<li>Assist with planning and implementing resident activities in order to increase resident retention.</li>
<li>Assists with monitoring property curb appeal and assists the manager with reducing liability to residents/guest/employees and vendors.</li>
<li>Participates in Company-provided training such as fair housing, safety, nondiscrimination, and harassment prevention timely.</li>
<li>Complies with all applicable health and safety rules and regulations, as well as applicable local, state, and federal laws.</li>
<br>
</ul>
