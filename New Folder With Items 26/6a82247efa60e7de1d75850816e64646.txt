<h2>Business Development Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Specific Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Increase sales through building relationships with current and potential clients within Kitsap County territory.</li>
<li>Promote brand awareness by attending local networking events</li>
<li>Schedule, coordinate and administer continuing education classes for insurance agents and adjusters</li>
<li>Make sales calls to potential customers and referral sources</li>
<li>Maintain records of all marketing activities</li>
<li>Assist the Owner/General Manager with the marketing budget</li>
<li>Perform other duties as needed which may include cross-training in related positions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>College degree preferred with 1 or more years related experience</li>
<li>Valid Driver's License</li>
<li>Must occasionally lift and/or move up to 50 pounds</li>
<li>Computer literate, with working knowledge of work processing, business software and spreadsheet applications</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits:</b> Benefits package include Medical, Dental and Vision.</p>
