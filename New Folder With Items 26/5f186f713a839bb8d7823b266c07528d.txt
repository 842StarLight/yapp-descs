<h2>Customer Engagement Representative - SurePayroll</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Customer Engagement Representative is the primary point of contact supporting prospects and recently acquired clients from the onboarding phase through early use of products and services, and may also support existing clients with early use of added services and products. This position supports the identification of and response to conversion, client satisfaction, and retention risks at various stages of the client acquisition and onboarding process.</p>
<ul>
<li>Monitors prospect or client progress through the self-service process to identify potential impediments to successful enrollment completion. Initiates outbound contact to proactively offer support, influence prospect-to-client conversion, or otherwise maintain a positive customer experience.</li>
<li>Responds to inbound outreach from prospects or recently onboarded clients. Independently or in partnership with others, answers questions, resolves issues, or provides support related to products and services.</li>
<li>Consults with recently acquired clients to ensure an optimal new-user experience. Provides product and feature training when necessary, drives awareness of relevant ancillary products, and promotes client referral program and benefits.</li>
<li>Analyzes interactions in real time to identify prospect or client retention risk. Responds independently or partners with appropriate cross-functional partners to influence conversion or retention.</li>
<li>Recommends products, process, or system improvements to drive utilization, client satisfaction and retention, and related business objectives. Provides input to Product Management and IT as needed to inform the improvement prioritization process.</li>
</ul>
