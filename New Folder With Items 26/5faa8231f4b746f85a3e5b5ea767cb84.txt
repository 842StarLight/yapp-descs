<h2>Head of Product Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Position Overview
<br><br>
MemSQL is seeking a product marketing leader to develop our positioning and messaging in the market and help drive the next stage of growth for MemSQL.
<br><br>
Reporting directly to the CMO, you will be responsible for setting product marketing strategy, establishing priorities and goals for your team, and contribute to creating compelling product positioning. The ideal candidate will bring a clear ability to evangelize and differentiate MemSQL in the database market.
<br><br>
We are seeking a candidate with the desire to transform the data and database infrastructure category. You will have a proven track record of creating compelling product marketing initiatives and thought leadership. As a strategic thinker with the ability to influence at all levels, you will be able to deliver results across product launches, coverage, and company visibility.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will have deep knowledge of modern data infrastructure software, experience delivering proven, measurable marketing impact, and the ability to motivate and influence resources both internally and externally.
<br><br>
Role and Responsibilities
<br><br>
This role covers the full scope of product marketing, including product positioning, product launch campaigns, thought leadership, sales enablement, and evangelism.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Set and execute the product marketing plan for existing and new product introductions, market awareness, and industry leadership</li>
<li>Understand key capabilities, unique strengths, benefits, and use cases for MemSQL</li>
<li>Develop a full suite of content, positioning, and competitive materials for sales customers, media, and analysts</li>
<li>Influence and enhance company and product positioning to align solutions, industry verticals, and tangible business benefits</li>
<li>Represent MemSQL at industry events and conferences.</li>
<li>Build internal product and market knowledge across the company including training development and delivery</li>
</ul>
Required Skills and Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Deep understanding of product marketing including messages, positioning, and packaging</li>
<li>Solid knowledge of the data infrastructure market</li>
<li>Previous startup experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated success in launching new products and developing markets</li>
<li>Strong leadership, management, and communications skills</li>
<li>High-energy drive combined with ability to work as an individual contributor</li>
</ul>
