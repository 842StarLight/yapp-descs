<h2>Shop Foreman</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>The Shop Foreman (Foreman) responsibilities include management of the Manufacturing Process &amp; Shop Fabrication Personnel. Primary duties include 1.) Reviewing CAD Fabrication (shop) drawings, 2.) Coordination with Purchasing &amp; Operations for Fabrication Scheduling, 3.) Supervision &amp; direction of shop personnel to meet production schedules. 4.) Active monitoring of all Safety &amp; QC Processes. The Foreman actively coordinates &amp; modifies the Production Schedule based upon available resources and materials to most effectively meet shipping objectives.</p>
<p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Management</b><b>: </b> Responsibility for and Management of all Fabrication (Shop) Personnel, Processes, Equipment &amp; Resources. This includes (primarily) custom metal fabrication with some woodworking &amp; other, custom materials fabrication.</li>
<li>
<b>Production Scheduling: </b> Creation of the Weekly Production Map, assigning tasks to Shop Personnel with time targets &amp; provisions for tasks in completing jobs in production.</li>
<li>
<b>Shipping Coordination: </b> The timely provision to the Shipping department of the weights and dims of crated shipments, allowing Shipping to schedule timely pick-ups.</li>
<li>
<b>Shop Equipment Maintenance</b>: Ensure that shop machinery is used properly and receives scheduled in-house and authorized vendor maintenance.</li>
<li>
<b>Purchasing &amp; Receiving Coordination</b><b>: </b> Coordination with the Purchasing/Inventory Control Manager, to ensure goods are properly received, labeled and shelved or “job binned,” in accordance with company procedures.</li>
<li>
<b>Quality Control</b><b>: </b> ACTIVELY supervise the shipment of only high-quality, trouble-free, well-finished, well-tested &amp; well-packaged Products.</li>
<li>
<b>Shop Floor Organization</b><b>: </b> ACTIVELY ensure that Shop Floor Cleanliness &amp; Organization is maintained, scheduling regular light and quarterly, heavy clean-ups.</li>
<li>
<b>Communication: </b> Communicate &amp; Coordinate with Sales &amp; Engineering to ensure Products are produced in accordance with customer expectations for functionality, appearance and delivery schedules.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>JOB PREREQUISITES: </b></p>
<p>The prospective Shop Foreman must have a minimum of ten years of verifiable, successful experience with directing &amp; managing personnel at a CUSTOM SHEET METAL MANUFACTURING Company. The candidate must have extensive custom sheet metal fabrication experience and be used to operating in a FAST-paced environment. The candidate must have a strong mechanical aptitude, a <b>proactive</b>, problem-solving/troubleshooting propensity and a solid understanding materials properties and fabrication tolerances. The candidate must have strong communication and tactful negotiating skills, be proficient &amp; comfortable with Microsoft Office applications and have experience reviewing, interpreting &amp; questioning CAD Drawings of Sheet Metal parts and assemblies. Experience with QuickBooks Software and an ERP system is desirable.The candidate must be SELF-MOTIVATED, TIME MANAGEMENT-EFFICIENT and EXTREMELY ORGANIZED.</p>
