<h2>Regional Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<p>The Regional Sales Representative is accountable for achieving qualitative and quantitative objectives of the US Business Strategy for his or her assigned territory with the following responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and develop all aspect of the Business in the assigned territory</li>
<li>Manage and provide leadership to all customer Sales Representatives and distribution channels in the assigned territory</li>
<li>Attain Quantitative Targets:</li>
<li>Sales per the Annual sales plan</li>
<li>Expense Budgets of the sales area</li>
<li>Manage and Attain Qualitative Targets:</li>
<li>Recommend and execute marketing and sales strategies, plans and programs, to achieve targeted sales by customer and product groups</li>
<li>Evaluate changing customer needs, customer revenue potential and competitive position regarding current Business and development of new products or services</li>
<li>Establish and communicate sales and pricing policies to the customer base.</li>
<li>Provide feedback and recommendation of price strategies to Sales &amp; Marketing.</li>
<li>Training of sales personal and sales channel participants</li>
<li>Active participation in the selling efforts with Key accounts in the territory</li>
<li>Develop, expand and maintain an environment which motivates employees to achieve quantitative and qualitative targets</li>
<li>Fostering of a company culture of learning and personal responsibility with employee accountability for their work with their customers and fellow employees</li>
</ul>
