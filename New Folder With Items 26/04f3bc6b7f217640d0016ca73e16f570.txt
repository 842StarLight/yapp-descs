<h2>Product Development Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Senior Product Development Coordinator will act as a liaison between the design team, foreign office, production team, sample department and sales/merchandising.</b></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Specs:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with design team to create and/or maintain all spec sheets (prototype, sample lot, pricing/pre-production, approval, confirmation)</li>
<li>
Communicate all product and spec changes to foreign offices</li>
<li>
Coordinate all component clarifications between design team and foreign office and/or suppliers</li>
<li>
Assist with communication of information between design team and outside designers</li>
<li>
Create name lists</li>
</ul>
<p>
Samples:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for daily communication with foreign offices regarding prompt delivery of all sample components and sample lots</li>
<li>
Maintain tracking of all incoming sample lots</li>
<li>
Maintain organization of photo files for all sample lots</li>
<li>
Checking spec details and quality on all incoming new samples before they are distributed to sales force</li>
<li>
Coordinate with sample department on organization of all samples, including, lots and advertising samples</li>
<li>
Photograph, catalog, and organize outside buys</li>
</ul>
<p>
Pre-production:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with design team and sourcing department on alternative components for pricing</li>
<li>
Review sample lots with design and sales teams and communicate any component corrections that need to be executed for production and/or pricing</li>
<li>
Review and approval of all confirmation and approval specs from foreign offices</li>
<li>
Daily review of pre-production reports to insure timely delivery of approval and confirmation samples (for some divisions)</li>
<li>
Work with production team on review of confirmation samples and fitting of approval samples</li>
</ul>
<p>
Line Sheets:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain product information on line sheets and keep merchandisers abreast of any changes</li>
<li>
Generate product flashes for any pre-production changes</li>
</ul>
<p>
Other:</p>
<ul><li>
General labeling, filing and organization of product and paperwork</li></ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to multitask and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Exception interpersonal communication and organization skills</li>
<li>
Strong interest in fashion</li>
<li>
Proficient Photoshop a plus</li>
</ul>
