<h2>Client Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Renaissance Life &amp; Health is seeking an experienced group insurance Client Account Manager to join our growing team here in Indianapolis, IN!</b></p>
<p>The Operations Account Manager at Renaissance will provide customer-focused leadership of operational account management responsibilities for assigned key customers.</p>
<p><b>What will be required of this individual?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build, expand, and solidify relationships with existing clients at all levels within the company</li>
<li>Provide outstanding service to existing client base by answering product and service questions in a timely and efficient manner</li>
<li>Recommend information about other products and services for additional organic sales growth</li>
<li>Is the escalation point for operational issues with each account as they may arise; facilitates resolution through various internal teams</li>
<li>Responsible to meet and exceed customer’s needs by following up with customers, ensuring issues are resolved and customers are satisfied</li>
<li>Responsible to ensure SLAs are being met and to identify challenges to the manager if/when SLAs are at risk</li>
<li>Delegate items when appropriate to the internal staff to ensure all client deliverables are met timely</li>
<li>Facilitate minor claims, enrollment and billing resolution</li>
<li>Oversee accurate and timely policy and benefit plan implementation from beginning to end</li>
<li>Oversee the process of initial enrollment submissions, change requests, and terminations</li>
<li>Review employer applications, policies, summary plan descriptions for accuracy</li>
<li>Track and manage all activity on accounts</li>
<li>Ensure Overall Client satisfaction by proactively managing and anticipating the day to day needs of clients through regular phone contact and occasional clients visits</li>
<li>Establish a Client Service Plan (per company guidelines) with each client based on mutual goals and objectives and maintain a monthly progress report of Service Plan activities for management review</li>
<li>Develop and maintain solid relationships with vendors, as well as internal staff</li>
<li>Manage implementation of all carrier or plan changes including verifying plan benefits sold, rates, proper paperwork submission, and coverage verification</li>
<li>Support clients in attaining compliance with governmental requirements by staying informed of new legislation and participating in available training</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>*What must you possess to qualify?<br>*</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or combination of education and/or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>3+ years' experience of employee benefits account management, employee benefits assistance and/or benefits customer service experience required. Experience with an agency, health insurance carrier, PEO, or similar preferred</li>
<li>Strong Project management skills. Intermediate to advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office applications (Excel in particular) and demonstrated ability to learn new software systems</li>
<li><b>Life &amp; Health License (Required) or must obtain within first 90 days of employment</b></li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
