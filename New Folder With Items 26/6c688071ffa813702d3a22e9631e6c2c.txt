<h2>Full-time Continuity Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Cumulus Media | </b><b>Columbia, SC</b> is looking for a dynamic Full-time <i>Continuity Director</i> for our 5 station cluster.<b> </b>Our people work in a state-of-the-art facility, take pride in our community and value teamwork. We need a detailed-oriented person that can work in a fast-paced environment, stay focused on the task, and learn our various software platforms quickly.</p>
<p><b>Cumulus | Columbia, SC </b>currently features 5 stations in the Columbia, SC area and surrounding counties. Our stations include: WISW 1320-AM ESPN Sports Radio, WLXC KISS 103.1, WNKT 107.5 the Game, WOMG 98.5 and WTCB B106.7. The cluster of 5 stations reaches thousands of listeners on a daily basis.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include</b>; Link copy/material instructions to all Local/National Advertiser Orders scheduled in WideOrbit (our traffic, order and billing software); maintain organized files of traffic instructions by Advertiser/Air Date as well as managing daily the ads in the VCreative program; enter the Traffic Instructions for each Advertiser Order into WideOrbit, work with Sales department on all aspects of the advertising orders and download Network Orders into the Traffic System each week.</p>

</li>

<p>Complete all barter affidavits</p>
