<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
The Manager, Accounting will be responsible for a variety of daily and monthly financial accounting duties including account reconciliations and analysis, preparation of journal entries and related monthly close documents, financial records maintenance and monthly financial reporting. The position will supervise staff and requires strong knowledge of accounting principles and experience.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Oversee General Ledger, Accounts Receivable/Payable, and Payroll functions</li>
<li>Prepare and administer budgets</li>
<li>Manage and protect company cash and assets</li>
<li>Reconcile and analyze GL accounts including bank accounts, accounts receivable, pre-paid, accounts payable, accrued liabilities, deferred revenue accounts and other accounts as required</li>
<li>Review contracts and manage proper recognition of related revenue</li>
<li>Gather, compile, analyze and evaluate data from department heads for monthly financial statements, budget and forecasts</li>
<li>Respond to internal and external customer inquiries on a timely basis</li>
<li>Provide management and oversight of event settlements</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with department policies, procedures and 404</li>
<li>Develop, implement, and oversee procedures and controls for various business functions</li>
<li>Prepare ad hoc reports and analyses for management; create and oversee audit schedules and provide support as needed for both internal and external audits</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A minimum education level of: BA/BS Degree (4-year) (Advanced Degree Preferred) in Finance, Accounting or Related Field</li>
<li>A minimum of 5 years of related, accounting/finance work experience</li>
<li>Strong written and oral communication skills; communicates effectively with management pertaining to finance and operation activities</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of GAAP</li>
<li>Proficient in accounting software systems (JD Edwards a plus); proficient with MS Office, particularly Excel</li>
<li>Ability to accurately evaluate and input information using a moderately complex financial accounting system</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure and meet all deadlines</li>
<li>Demonstrated analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Ability to work extended hours as needed to accommodate monthly, quarterly and year-end closings</li>
<li>CPA - highly preferred</li>
</ul>
