<h2>Sr. Coordinator, Digital Marketing & e-Commerce</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Beach House Group, an awesome new company with giving at its core. We’re looking for a passionate, organized and detail-oriented Sr. Coordinator, Digital Marketing &amp; e-Commerce to join our fun, hard-working, results-driven, creative and collaborative eCommerce team! This position will assist in developing and administrating strong, innovative e-Commerce and digital marketing campaigns that use the latest in data-driven decision making, SEO, SEM, Paid Ads, Native ads, and online sales techniques. This position requires an experienced digital marketing and e-Commerce professional who understands site merchandising, content marketing, social media, link building, email marketing, analytics and performance reporting</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist VP of Digital and e-Commerce and leadership team in the development of global e-commerce marketing initiatives to support key seasonal/ yearly strategic programs</li>
<li>Apply understanding of site merchandising, inventory and site KPI's to optimize product sell through, revenue and margin under the direction of our e-Commerce managers</li>
<li>Leverage tactics such as search engine optimization, site content and other levers to further increase site visibility, promote an omni-channel customer experience in order to enhance the consumer’s connection with the brand and business</li>
<li>Help manage a catalog of products – uploads, asset management, maintenance, ensuring accuracy of inventory, image quality, etc</li>
<li>Assist in the execution of the eCommerce Calendar in conjunction with multiple brands to ensure alignment on campaigns</li>
<li>Assist in the day to day digital marketing, including SEO/SEM, marketing database, email, social media and display advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Coordinate the efforts of internal brand marketing teams and other 3rd party vendors.</li>
<li>Communicate e-Commerce program timing and roll-outs to internal teams for respective sub-brand, program and/or marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Manage and track performance for all assigned projects, and keeping manager informed of all recommendations and changes</li>
<li>Coordinate with internal and external teams on QA for new releases</li>
<li>Contribute to cross-functional monthly reporting and/or ad-hoc report requests to communicate performance results all assigned programs and/or sub-brand initiatives</li>
<li>Help measure and report the performance of all digital marketing campaigns, and assess against goals (ROI and KPIs)</li>
<li>Help identify and build conversion points and optimize online sales funnels</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-4 years of relevant digital marketing/e-commerce experience, in house or at an agency. Beauty and lifestyle experience preferred.</li>
<li>2+ years’ experience site merchandising and catalog management through Shopify and experience with a variety of third-party apps preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with web platform management, with a deep understanding of the competitive landscape</li>
<li>Experience in coordinating SEO/SEM, marketing databases, email, social media and/or advertising campaign efforts.</li>
<li>Solid knowledge of web and mobile analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Glew, or other BI tools and analytics platforms)</li>
<li>Experience in optimizing landing pages and user funnels</li>
<li>Demonstrated history of delivery in a dynamic, fast paced organization</li>
<li>Some experience in website optimizing pages and sales funnels; as well as executing email marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Resourceful and proactive, with strong organizational/project management skills and a keen attention to detail.</li>
<li>Ability to operate independently but respond to manager input and direction.</li>
<li>Strong ethics and values which support the organization’s goals and foster the appropriate culture.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of ad serving environment</li>
<li>Strong understanding of digital marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Analytical skills and data-driven thinking</li>
<li>Strong writing and editing skills.</li>
<li>Strong communications skills with the ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced, creative environment with team members at all levels.</li>
</ul>
