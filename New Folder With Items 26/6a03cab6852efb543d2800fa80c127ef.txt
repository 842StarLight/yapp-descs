<h2>Client Services Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Are you creative, curious, and motivated to succeed?
</p>
<p>At FIS you’ll have the opportunity to work on some of the most challenging and relevant issues in financial services and technology. Our talented people empower us, and we believe in being part of a team that is open, collaborative, entrepreneurial, passionate and above all fun.
</p>
<p>Headquartered in Jacksonville, Florida with offices located globally, FIS employs more than 55,000 people across 50+ countries, dedicated to helping our clients be ahead of what’s next. FIS offers more than 450 solutions and processes over $75 billion of transactions around the planet. FIS is a Fortune 500® company and is a member of Standard &amp; Poor’s 500® Index.
</p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING
</b></p>
<p>You will be providing general customer support (phone and email) for software applications, including a learning management system and suite of regulatory reporting (Call Report, FRY9, FR2900, etc.) solutions. The position involves end-to-end support, so you will also be exposed to billing, accounts receivables, sales, client retention, and other related activities.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Serves as primary point of contact to resolve outstanding issues and respond to client inquiries.
</li>
<li>Provides support in research and resolution of problems and inquiries.
</li>
<li>Coordinates with clients, relationship managers, and other appropriate areas to ensure clients are properly serviced, paperwork is properly executed, and all operational arrangements are in place to service assigned accounts
</li>
<li>Maintains comprehensive knowledge of applicable products, services, and company policies and procedures.
</li>
<li>Identifies additional opportunities to provide more products, services or other resources to customer and refers to managing director/relationship manager.
</li>
<li>Draws on bank accounting experience to implement automated call reporting solution by analyzing report extracts and mapping to call report line items
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WHAT YOU BRING FOR EXPERIENCE:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A college degree or at least of 2 years of experience in client relations, customer service, or similar field
</li>
<li>Prior financial services or banking experience preferred
</li>
<li>Microsoft Office Suite (Word/Excel/Outlook/PowerPoint) experience required
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills (will be dealing with staff at all levels in a wide array of financial institution clients), including excellent spelling, punctuation, and grammar.
</li>
<li>Excellent telephone skills
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>A HUGE PLUS IF YOU HAVE:
</b></p>
<ul><li>A plus if you have knowledge of accounting terminology
</li></ul>
