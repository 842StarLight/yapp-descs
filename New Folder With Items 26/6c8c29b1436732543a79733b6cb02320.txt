The world's leading private security organization, G4S, has an immediate job opportunity for a Security Control Center Operator. As a SCC Operator, you will respond and assist in reporting Emergency (Critical) and Non-Emergency (Non-Critical) events for a high profile client. As a SCC Operator, he or she shall be responsible for monitoring multiple security systems to aid in the safety and security of persons and properties. The SCC Operator is required to detect unfavorable situations, dispatch police officers and make proper notifications to the appropriate level of management.

Main Responsibilities
<br>Specific Duties and Essential Functions
<br><br>
Reporting and notifications; providing interface during critical and non-critical emergency events from report to resolution. In addition providing constant information updates wherever applicable in an effort to ensure situation, events, incidents are resolved in an appropriate manner.
<br>Access control; using CCTV to monitor exterior doors, building perimeters and sensitive building areas (data centers, executive areas, market room etc); acknowledge forced open and held open alarms; monitor badge activities using C*Cure; monitor and verify access; provide momentary door unlocks and access to card-reader doors and garage gates
<br>Customer service; ensure that all forms of communication (email, phone conversation etc) are all done in a courteous and professional manner; serving as an emergency information HUB system for a high profile client.
<br>Life safety and emergency response; ability to guide and assist clients during emergency and non-emergency situational phone calls
<br>Perform data entry in a timely, accurate and exclusive manner
<br>Escalate incidents to the Supervisor immediately when unable to manage the situation at his/her level or when procedure requires for this to be done
<br>Deal with issues and incidents that are potentially sensitive, confidential or may put persons at risk
<br>Provide alarm monitoring via access control systems and live monitoring via access control systems and live monitoring consoles (CCTV) for intrusion, robbery, duress and alarm events
<br>Review CCTV footage and create DVD’s which will be provided to Senior Management
<br>Dispatch security guards and/or police officers
<br>Perform active monitoring of various security systems, for reasons such as vagrancy and post robbery incidents
<br>Perform active monitoring of multiple new sources and social media
<br>Follow procedures, work instructions and other directions from Supervisors or Stakeholders
<br>Participate in departmental meetings as required
<br>Contribute to the development of procedures and work instructions by voicing concerns about potential time saving and efficient changes that could benefit the operation
<br>Follow G4S and the Clients’ codes of conduct
<br>Perform other duties as required
<br><br>
