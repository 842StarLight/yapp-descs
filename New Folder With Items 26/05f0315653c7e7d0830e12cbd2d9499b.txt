Highlights:</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>With a focus on consultative sales, you highlight our innovative voice, data and video network infrastructure solutions that simplify complex communications needs. As a National Account Manager, you empower clients to learn more and share your enthusiasm for our technology solutions. Through your expertise and understanding of each client’s needs, you outline uniquely beneficial combinations of our services that meet current needs and build demand for the future.<br>

<div>You are passionate about proactively acquiring and managing new enterprise accounts and converting them into long-term clients. You possess a keen ability for identifying roadblocks and overcoming obstacles to increase sales while enhancing the client experience. You feel comfortable traveling extensively to acquire and manage accounts within an assigned portfolio. You report directly to National Accounts Management for goals, guidance and assistance.</div>

<div><b>
What you will do:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Be an impactful member of the National and Carrier sales team through the achievement of monthly sales and revenue goals.</li>
<li>
Consult with prospective clients and grow key relationships within named agencies to develop client-centric product solutions for their complex business needs.</li>
<li>
Entice clients through comprehensive account management and face-to-face consultative needs analysis, and proposal creation being mindful of opportunities for new services, renewals and upsell potential.</li>
<li>
Improve sales through the conceptualization and implementation of a strategic sales plan which identifies target markets, industries and contacts for product portfolios.</li>
<li>
Maintain an accurate client database while building a network through referrals, prospecting calls, trade shows and peers.</li>
<li>
Qualify leads through requests of site surveys and the submission of a return on investment (ROI) and accompanying analyses.</li>
<li>
Conduct tactful and mutually beneficial contract negotiations and conversations with C-Level executives and key decision-makers.</li>
<li>
Maintain quality service and encourage retention through coordinated efforts with Sales Support, Sales Engineering and Marketing teams.</li>
<li>
Develop key sales skills by attending sales meetings and participating in training sessions.</li>
<li>
Perform additional duties related to this position as assigned.</li>
</ul>
