<h2>Product Marketing Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Product Marketing Internship at Viewpoint is a full-time summer internship that will be approximately 12 weeks and will be based in our Portland, Oregon headquarters. Viewpoint is a provider of innovative software solutions to the global construction and capital project industries. For over 40 years Viewpoint has been committed to bringing information technology-based efficiencies to construction projects and their stakeholders, engaging in a highly collaborative process with customers to ensure every product and service addresses key industry issues and digital transformations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The internship program provides college students the opportunity to learn from seasoned product teams and develop core skills in converting product strategies into business impact in a creative work environment. Interns will gain first-hand experience with the daily operations of product teams at an independent, medium-size company focused on delivering industry-leading, vertical software solutions in the global construction market.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the development of marketing and customer research tools, techniques and reporting
</li>
<li>Analyze market data and trends to provide technology, product and business insights to support executive decision-making
</li>
<li>Assist with gathering competitive data to aid better product positioning and sales intelligence
</li>
<li>Engage in the creative process for marketing collateral, presentations, website content, videos and client case studies.
</li>
<li>Coordinate product launch activities and sales campaigns
 </li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Targeting a student actively completing their bachelor’s or master’s degree in business or a related field
</li>
<li>Self-starter, tech-savvy, comfortable in getting started with missing data and dealing with ambiguity
</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills
</li>
<li>Applicants should have an undergraduate GPA of 3.0 or better
</li>
<li>Strong research and analytical abilities
</li>
<li>Ability to clearly and effectively communicate and sell ideas and actionable solutions to internal and (sometimes) external stakeholders
</li>
<li>Comfortable in multi-tasking and managing multiple projects
</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of Excel and PowerPoint required
</li>
<li>Marketing related job experience is preferred
</li>
<li>Enterprise Software (ERP and Salesforce) or technology experience a plus﻿
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Viewpoint is an Equal Opportunity Employer
</b></p>
<p>DISCLOSURE for US-BASED POSITIONS ONLY: Viewpoint requires a criminal background investigation, and employment and education verification as a condition of employment.</p>
