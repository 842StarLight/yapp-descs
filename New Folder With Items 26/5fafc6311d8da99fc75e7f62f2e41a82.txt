<h2>Senior Creative Director - Pharma Industry - Posted 10/22/19</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Victory Healthcare Communications is a fully integrated healthcare communications and promotional marketing firm. We offer marketing and advertising services to our pharmaceutical clients. The company seeks a passionate and professional Senior Creative Director to join the team. As a leader in providing innovative marketing plans and solutions for pharmaceutical companies--with an emphasis on design, writing, interactive media, marketing, and business management--our team has produced award-winning campaigns. We are dedicated to providing the winning marketing solution to overcome any challenges and seek a Creative Director who complements our business.</p>
<p>The Senior Creative Director will be responsible for developing and maintaining working relationships with product managers and customers to achieve multiple sales and marketing objectives. This person will manage the creative process and communication of accounts through business relationships, craft key internal and external business messages, and ultimately, present ideas to the clients. This is a hands-on role for a professional with a passion for creating high-quality marketing materials and programs. Candidates must have a proven record of creating and delivering new marketing tools or programs that produce a significant impact on sales.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate:</p>
<ul>
<li>Holds a Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 5-7 years of experience as a creative artist or creative director</li>
<li>Has 3+ years of experience in the pharmaceutical and/or medical product industries</li>
<li>Has experience constructing and making presentations to C-level executives in the pharmaceutical, consumer product, and medical product industries</li>
<li>Has account management experience</li>
<li>Proven ability to perform the typesetting process</li>
<li>A problem solving attitude to work as a consultant in solving the problems of the clients</li>
<li>Translates marketing requirements into action plans and deliverables</li>
<li>Personifies professionalism through proper appearance and demeanor</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate effectively with clients and internal team to implement designs and changes in a timely manner and within project deadlines</li>
<li>Produces results with a spirited and charismatic attitude</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>pharmaceutical advertising: 3 years (Required)</li>
<li>Senior level Creative Director: 5 years (Required)</li>
<li>Powerpoint, Word, Excel: 3 years (Required)</li>
<li>Adobe Creative Suite: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>Client Relationship Management: 3 years (Required)</li>
</ul>
