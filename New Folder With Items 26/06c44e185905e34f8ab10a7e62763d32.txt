<h2>Marketing Manager, Aftermarket & Government</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>The <b>Marketing Manager, Aftermarket &amp; Government</b> will develop, schedule and implement marketing communications programs as well create content and public relations plans to drive aftermarket sales growth and build brand/product/technology awareness among US government agencies/constituents and related media.<br>
<br>
The Marketing Manager will conduct research and analysis to capture Voice of Customer (VOC) and understand customer needs, buying habits, purchasing decision trees, and funding streams which he/she will translate into various business outputs including communications/messaging strategies, marketing automation programs, new growth paths, and route to market.<br>
<br>
As a key member of the marketing organization, and based in the America’s Headquarters located in Edgewood, MD, the Marketing Manager is responsible for strategic efforts related to the site including local publicity, government relations, and support to Human Research and Product/Technology functions. He/she will also be assigned projects to support the Global Marketing Organization, including specific efforts related to the worldwide Defense market.</p>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>Duties &amp; Responsibilities
</div>
<div>
<p>SPECIFIC DUTIES, ACTIVITIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE BUT ARE NOT LIMITED TO:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Critical interface between Sales and Segment Market Heads and a key contributor to the development of marketing objectives, plans, budgets, campaigns, content, messaging and intelligence for the assigned market and region.</li>
<li>
Develop customer reference library and produce, plan and execute cutting edge engagement programs utilizing social media, webinars, case studies, promotions, advertising, in-bound email, web site, sales aids/videos and other versatile efforts.</li>
<li>
Work with events manager to plan and execute trade shows, conferences, workshops and speaking engagements.</li>
<li>
Manage creative teams/agencies to develop marketing assets and content.</li>
<li>
Drive and execute proactive and reactive public relations efforts such as manage spokespersons, prepare press releases, maintain program milestone records, run press conferences, prepare public responses to congressional issues with our Government Affairs office.</li>
<li>
Develop segmented messaging frameworks and copy-writing.</li>
<li>
Capture VOC and perform and report on market research, analysis segmentation.</li>
<li>
Identifies regulatory issues and trends.</li>
<li>
Competitive Strengths, Weakness, Opportunities, Threats (SWOT), trend analysis.</li>
<li>
Develop and execute customer loyally/retention programs.</li>
<li>
Budget and marketing plan management.</li>
<li>
Drive marketing tracking / trending and reporting outputs.</li>
</ul>
