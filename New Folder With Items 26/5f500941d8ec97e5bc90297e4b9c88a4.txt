<h2>Service Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div><b>Description</b></div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Service Administrator will provide administrative support to the Service Department by updating service related systems and contacting customers on a regular basis. This position is also responsible for the completion of all paperwork related to the Service Department.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Monday - Friday first shift position 8am - 4:30pm</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>Major Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain Service Net Planner with PMs campaigns, repairs from drive-ins, call-ins, and 24/7 calls, and off lease/on lease. Start R/O’s, schedule pick-ups and vendor outside work.</li>
<li>Respond to customer inquiries regarding billing and scheduling. Greet customers at the location and assists customers via phone and e-mail.</li>
<li>Assist with parts inventories and cycle counts, procurement, storage and billing.</li>
<li>Complete yard checks and/or safety inspections</li>
<li>Assist with shop administrative processes and run reports as scheduled</li>
<li>Complete repair orders, fuel analysis, and all paperwork involved in daily office procedures</li>
<li>Recording attendance of shop personnel</li>
<li>Ordering supplies, mailing, and scheduling</li>
<li>Accident reporting and administration</li>
<li>Prepares, issues, and sends out receipts, bills, policies, invoices, warranties, statements, and checks</li>
<li>Operates computer terminal to input and retrieve data</li>
<li>Opens and routes incoming mail, answers correspondence, and prepares outgoing mail</li>
<li>Other projects and tasks as assigned by supervisor</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<p></p>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<ul><li>High School Diploma or equivalent required; Associates or Bachelors’ degree preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>2 years of experience in shop administration</li></ul>
<ul><li>2 years of customer service experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient skill with MS Office software</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent phone skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent verbal communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Regular, predictable, full attendance is an essential function of the job</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work the required schedule, work at the specific location required, complete Penske employment application, submit to a background investigation (to include past employment, education, and criminal history) and drug screening are required.</li></ul>
