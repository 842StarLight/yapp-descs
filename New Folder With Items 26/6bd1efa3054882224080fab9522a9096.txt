<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p><b>Why is this role important at Infobip?</b></p>
<p>
Imagine having the opportunity to develop business in a local market from scratch while at the same time being supported by a global organization with an established local presence in more than 60 countries worldwide. We are on the lookout for an enterpreunerial spirit to help establish our business operations in the US market. Onboarding you with extensive experience and industry knowledge, we are adapting our organization to think locally. We believe this role will be crucial to onboarding local enterprise clients that will boost the overall success of the US market, one of the key markets in Infobip's expansion.</p>
<p><b>
You know you are doing a good job when:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You can successfully develop and execute sales plans to achieve (or over-achieve) quarterly objectives.</li>
<li>
Your account strategies support you in developing and maintaining relationships with key decision makers.</li>
<li>
You effectively deliver our UVP to senior business and IT executives, resulting in sales and continuous growth of the business.</li>
<li>
You maintain a robust pipeline of opportunities, developed through market sector intelligence and targeted calling and email campaigns.</li>
<li>
You can comfortably manage the end to end sales process by engaging internal resources in the broader team such as Sales Engineers, Global Account Management, Support, Marketing and the management team in Vancouver and throughout the USA.</li>
<li>
You are proficient at Solution Selling and managing your process through Salesforce.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p><b>You're set up for success because:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You've been there, and done that! Your (minimum) 3 - 5 years of Enterprise Sales experience has equipped you with a solid tool kit and you're ready to hit the ground running.</li>
<li>
You're used to winning - You have demonstrated success selling into Enterprise Accounts, and are accustomed to over-achieving challenging targets.</li>
<li>
You like the chase - You go after the whales, and catch big fish consistently to deliver against your targets. You have the drive and initiative to continually seek out new sales opportunities.</li>
<li>
This is your space - B2B, SaaS sales is what you know, love, and thrive in!</li>
<li>
You are a resilient problem solver who looks for solutions on behalf of your customers.</li>
</ul>
