<p><b>
Position Summary
</b></p>
<p>The Billing Analyst is responsible for the timely preparation of monthly customer invoices in accordance with contract terms and billing processes and procedures.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities &amp; Essential functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ensures all tasks for monthly AR cycle are processed for relevant cut-offs, install imports, loss triggers, write-offs, cash application, file imports, write-offs and adjustments</li>
<li>
Reconciliation and remediation of unidentified payments</li>
<li>
Analysis of file loads, variance reconciliation</li>
<li>
Bank maintenance, creating new banks, end-dating old to increase cash hit rates</li>
<li>
Oversight of monthly checklist activities</li>
<li>
Process Invoices</li>
<li>
Suggest Improvements to Invoicing Process</li>
<li>
Analyze Unbilled Balances</li>
<li>
Produce Ad Hoc Billing Reports</li>
</ul>
