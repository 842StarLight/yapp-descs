<h2>Sales Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Producers Dairy is seeking a talented Sales Data Analyst for our Fresno location. This position is responsible for providing analytical support to our Sales Function. The Sales Data Analyst handles behind-the-scenes research and data analysis to help the sales team grow and manage our business. The Sales Data Analyst will be responsible for demonstrating our Producers Dairy Core Values and carrying out our purpose of Nourishing Lives.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Functions:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Approaching work with a Continuous Improvement (CI) mindset, and engaging in Producers Dairy Mission Statement to identify, value, prioritize, resource and sustain improvement opportunities.</li>
<li>Owning and promoting a Safety Culture by ensuring a safe work environment and promoting proper safety behavior.</li>
<li>Excellent attendance and punctuality is an essential function of this position.</li>
<li>Support the design, implementation and validation of sales business reports.</li>
<li>Develop expert knowledge of in-house data sources and extracting/analyzing data from these sources.</li>
<li>Collect and analyze data from multiple databases: Salient as well as any other sources to evaluate current and historical trends.</li>
<li>Create analytical reports and sales presentations supporting various departmental requirements.</li>
<li>Manage ad-hoc reporting requests from sales and sales leadership.</li>
<li>Assess ongoing reporting needs through investigation, analysis and evaluation to management.</li>
<li>Test, document and train sales team members on tools.</li>
<li>Knowledge of eRMS program and/or the ability to quickly learn new programs.</li>
<li>Work with sales to complete bids.</li>
<li>Understanding of milk costing and commodity changes.</li>
<li>Assist sales team with product set up, new item forms, product information requests and new customer set up.</li>
<li>Maintain and distribute all customer prices and promotional allowance calendars with accuracy.</li>
<li>Analyze both customer and SKU profitability through proforma process.</li>
<li>Generate weekly forecasts along with completing various monthly sales/volume reports.</li>
<li>Assist executive team with annual budgeting process.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
