<h2>Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>What we Offer:</b> Sierra Forest Products offers a comprehensive benefit package that includes health, dental, life and disability insurance, 401K and paid vacation. We fully train our people to equip you for success in your position and career growth. We are a dynamic company and provide fast growing careers for great people.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>About the Role: </b>The Office Administrator requires an individual that is a self starter and self motivated. This position will play an integral role in the customer service and organizational strength of our company. The Office Administrator will provide administrative support, greet and direct visitors and answer and respond to calls and emails.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Perform related clerical duties such as word processing, maintaining filing and record systems, faxing and photocopying.</li>
<li>Receive and verify invoices for incoming material with the purchase order.</li>
<li>Complete and file import paperwork.</li>
<li>Generate and send out margin reports to Sales Team and Managers.</li>
<li>Perform general accounting duties such as invoicing, accounts payable, accounts receivable and processing credit card payments.</li>
<li>Assist with inventory management.</li>
<li>Answer customer inquiries.</li>
<li>Process customer returns.</li>
<li>Generate and submit invoices to customers</li>
<li>Post payments to customer accounts.</li>
<li>Assist the Credit team with collection calls when necessary.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Skills &amp; Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Completion of College in a related field or 2-3 years of related experience</li>
<li>Experience with data entry and ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning software)</li>
<li>Experience working in a distribution or warehouse environment</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational and time management skills.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook are also required.</li>
</ul>
