<h2>Systems Engineer - Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Blue Canyon Technologies is a leading provider of turnkey small satellite solutions, including nanosatellites, microsatellites, and ESPA-class. We specialize in precision pointing platforms based upon our high-performance attitude determination and control components. BCT’s highly experienced staff has developed low cost, flight-proven, high-performance, high-reliability spacecraft systems and components that enable a wide range of missions for academic, commercial, and government applications. Our state-of-the-art spacecraft and subsystems are supporting LEO, GEO, Lunar, and interplanetary missions.</p>
<p>
Blue Canyon Technologies is looking for a Systems Engineer with an interest in Business Development to join our team. This engineer will be working in a team environment, with frequent opportunities for customer engagement, new business development, concept design, mission formulation, system analysis, and detailed design, analysis, integration and test, plus orbit operations.</p>
<p>
Required Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with all phases of the program life-cycle (business development, mission formulation, requirements flow-down, manufacturing, integration and test, and on-orbit operations)</li>
<li>Demonstrated responsibility for Space hardware missions for Commercial, Defense, Restricted, or NASA satellites.</li>
<li>System design experience, balancing performance, cost, power, emerging technologies, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to perform trade studies and parametric analyses</li>
<li>Evaluation of existing and emerging technologies</li>
<li>Ability to develop procurement documents (specification, SOW) and manage subcontracts</li>
<li>Ability to conceive and develop configurable products across a diverse set of customer needs</li>
<li>Ability to establish innovative, balanced technical and cost solutions</li>
<li>Work closely with customer to help define their needs and provide competitive solutions</li>
<li>Develop winning competitive solutions for the customer</li>
<li>Execute flight programs that meet technical and cost obligations for the customer</li>
<li>Work closely with other contractors/teammates in meeting customer needs</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with other Technical program leads and diverse domain expertise</li>
<li>Allocate driving requirements (both cost and technical)</li>
<li>Allocate key trades (both cost and technical)</li>
<li>Ensure all business is conducted using the highest ethical standards and in compliance with all applicable laws, statutes, and regulations as well as company policies and directives</li>
<li>A self motivated individual with good documentation and communication skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Desired Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience as a spacecraft systems engineer or subsystem engineer<br>
Strong systems engineering and technical management skills and demonstrated ability to grow new business opportunities and execute demanding flight programs</li>
<li>Ability to work rapidly with minimal supervision on projects involving both small and large engineering teams</li>
<li>Previous experience and skills in project definition, simulation, implementation, lab integration and test</li>
<li>Must be able to work in a highly collaborative, fast-paced environment with emphasis in rapid prototyping and fielding of capabilities</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Education &amp; Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Aerospace, Engineering, System, or Computer science discipline</li>
<li>Experience - At least 5 years of relevant experience</li>
</ul>
