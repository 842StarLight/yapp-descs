<h2>Sr. Account Executive, Corporate Sponsorships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>AEG Company:</b> The Ontario Reign Hockey Club</p>
<p><b>
Company Information</b><b>:</b> AEG Worldwide is the world’s leading sports and live entertainment company with operations in the following business segments:</p>
<ul>
<li>
AEG Facilities, which with its affiliates owns, manages or consults with more than 120 venues</li>
<li>AEG Presents, which is one of the largest live music companies in the world dedicated to live contemporary music performances, including producing and promoting global and regional concert tours, music events and world-renowned festivals</li>
<li>AEG Sports, which is the world’s largest operator of sports franchises and high-profile sporting events</li>
<li>AEG Global Partnerships, which supports each of AEG’s divisions through worldwide sales and servicing of sponsorships including naming rights, premium seating and other strategic partnerships</li>
<li>AEG Real Estate, which develops major sports and entertainment districts worldwide</li>
</ul>
<p>With offices on five continents, the company uses its global network of venues, portfolio of powerful sports and music brands, ticketing and content distribution platforms and its integrated entertainment districts to deliver the most creative and innovative live sports and entertainment experiences that inspire athletes, teams, artists and fans.</p>
<p><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>
AEG and Ontario Reign are seeking a Sr. Account Executive, Corporate Partnerships to grow and develop new partnership business for the organization. As a Sr. Account Executive, you will develop and sell integrated corporate partnerships consisting of Ontario Reign assets including but not limited to hockey signage, in-game promotions, digital advertising, social media, community programming, print advertising, IP rights, and corporate hospitality.</p>
<p>
The individual in this position will be expected to help grow the organization’s corporate partnerships by prospecting, building key local &amp; regional relationships, and nurturing existing clients while closing new business. This is an excellent opportunity for a developing sales person who can craft compelling solutions that positively position our partners to meet their strategic goals.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<p>
In this role, you will support the team’s business development efforts, with particular emphasis on building new business in assigned categories. Additional responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Continuously prospect to develop new business through outreach and networking, which includes growing existing relationships and offerings of assigned accounts. Maintains a consistent ‘pipeline’ of potential business.</li>
<li>Manage the sales process from initial touch point through contract execution while exceeding individual and team goals.</li>
<li>Build customer relationships through strategic conversations to understand organizational business objectives and goals; performs a basic client needs analysis with prospective partners and mirrors those objectives with a custom Ontario Reign partnership proposal that builds value for both the partner and our organization.</li>
<li>Entertain clients and prospects at home games and additional special events.</li>
<li>Assist in the execution of various organizational functions.</li>
<li>Involved in the servicing of clients all year long during regular office hours, at home games and at various functions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SPECIFIC JOB KNOWLEDGE, SKILL AND ABILITY:</b></p>
<p>
We are seeking an individual with a strong business development and/or corporate partnerships background with knowledge of strategic account management, sales process and solution selling. In addition, you may be a potential fit for this role if you have a mix of the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Proven prospector with a track record of securing initial meetings and phone calls</li>
<li>Preferred fluency in brand marketing and integrated partnerships</li>
<li>Desire to work as a collaborative, self-starter</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and flexibility to work within a rapidly changing environment</li>
<li>Ability to communicate articulately and confidently both verbally and in writing with individuals at every level of the organization</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Ability to work nights/weekends/holidays as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATION STANDARDS:</b></p>
<p>
Education:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum four-year college degree</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>
A three-year minimum of full-time sales and/or sponsorship experience</li>
</ul>
