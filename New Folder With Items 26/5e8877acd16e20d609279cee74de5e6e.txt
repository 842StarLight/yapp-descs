Sounds Like You? Please review the criteria below
<br>As a Customer Success Manager, you will serve as the trusted advisor for our customers and internal stakeholders to drive business value, offer advice, and grow accounts.
<br>The Customer Success Manager has a key role internally. This position is dynamic and highly cross-functional. Armed with key customer insight and knowledge, you will work regularly with the sales, support and many members of the account and supplier teams.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities for the role include:</b><br>
Manage the customer lifecycle for assigned accounts. You will need to build relationships with the key stakeholders with JLL supporting your accounts as well as with the customer and engage by preparing and presenting activity reports and value briefings, running QBRs, managing escalations, conducting regular status calls and ensuring high level of customer satisfaction.
<br>Strategize and identify new opportunities to grow your accounts by delivering savings and efficiencies through the JLL Marketplace.
<br>Perform deep dive quantitative and qualitative analysis to present insights that will drive incremental growth and adoption of JLL Marketplace.
<br>Become an expert on the platform and train customers at different levels of the organization.
<br>Be responsible for new customer onboarding and activation to ensure customers receive value quickly from the JLL Marketplace
<br>Monitor user adoption and system usage and recommend strategies to increase usage/adoption
<br>Engage with the product and supplier teams to drive customer requests and influence our roadmap.
<br>Liaise closely with the sales and support teams to ensure that customer requests and escalations are resolved.
<br>Develop JLL Marketplace champions and generate customer references.
<br>Be a true proponent of customer advocacy.
<br>Be a team player and coach other team members
<br><br>
Requirements
<br><br>
Ability and personality are paramount, but some specific traits, qualifications and experience we’ll value:
<br>A Bachelor’s degree in business management, marketing, or business operations is preferred.
<br>Minimum of five years of experience in external facing customer roles.
<br>Minimum of three years of experience in a Customer Success role or comparable.
<br>Strong analytical skills preferred.
<br>Previous experience in Supply Chain, CRE, or SaaS with exposure to B2B procurement systems is preferred.
<br>Knowledge of emerging procurement technologies and platforms.
<br>Knowledge and experience with Data Visualization tools – Tableau and Power BI.
<br>Work experience in an agile and fast-growing start-up environment is preferred.
<br>High comfort level interacting with and influencing executive-level professionals.
<br>Superior multi-tasking and project management skills in a constantly changing environment.
<br>Creative problem solver who can identify and efficiently sort critical information.
<br>Highly personable and can maintain focused expectations from peers.
