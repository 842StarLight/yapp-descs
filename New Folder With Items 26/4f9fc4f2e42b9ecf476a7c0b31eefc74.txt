<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Duties and Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Our Corporate Accounting department has an opportunity for a Financial Analyst. This department is staffed with finance and accounting professionals that have backgrounds in various corporate industries. At JBSS, you will have the opportunity to learn and excel with a winning organization!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We need a Financial Analyst (Corporate) at our HQ facility in Elgin, IL. You must have a strong aptitude for compiling financial analysis information to prepare month-end journal entries and reconciliations. Another key component to this job will be preparing detailed deductions reports and monthly notes for different programs and communicating them to the business managers. You will also be responsible for running monthly face-to-face meetings. If you have a strong background in accounting, here are some skills we need for you to have:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Accurately coding deductions from customer payments against the balance sheet.</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and cross-functionally.</li>
<li>
Good organization skills and ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Expertise in complex MS Excel spreadsheet analysis (pivot-tables, v-lookup, macros, etc.)</li>
<li>
Ability to master the functionality of the general ledger and various related reporting software used to analyze account activity.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Key responsibilities will include:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Reviews Trade Pro promotions against sales and prints contracts.</li>
<li>
Reviews IRI Scan Data and tabulate results.</li>
<li>
Prepares check requests for rebate and incentive payments. Review rebate check requests and indicates on G/L.</li>
<li>
Participates in quarter end and year-end audit process.</li>
</ul>
