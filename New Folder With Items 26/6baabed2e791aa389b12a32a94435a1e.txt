<p><b>
Job Description</b><br>
<br>
This is a unique opportunity to establish policies and practices to help Ampere grow. The Master Scheduler partners with Supply Chain and Manufacturing to issue production schedules to Ampere suppliers in line with forecasted demands. Working with with Sales and Product Management to develop and manage the forecast for long range material planning while considering current inventory, obsolescence, lead times, product life cycles, and production capacity.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>
Supply Plan</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Partner with Sales, Manufacturing, and Engineering team members to develop supply plan balanced against forecasted demand.</li>
<li>
Responsible for developing, implementing, and maintaining a supply strategy for the global distribution and manufacturing network, including contract manufacturers, for all production and pre-production products.</li>
<li>
Ownership of MRP system and ensuring of accuracy in inputs from multiple stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Propose inventory purchases to align with potential upsides in business as well as mitigate risks in down sides.</li>
<li>
Communicate with suppliers on ordering, ship to locations, allocation changes, and expected delivery dates.</li>
<li>
Provide feedback on demand changes and make recommendations to sales and management on customer needs.</li>
<li>
Set and manage the supplier future production requirements in support of global supply needs.</li>
<li>
Communicate needs to CEO and CFO.</li>
</ul>

Inventory
</b></p>

Partner with the finance team during month end close to act as the liaison between operations and finance for month and quarter end close activities.</li>
<li>
Monitor stock levels and maintain inventory dashboard.</li>
</ul>
