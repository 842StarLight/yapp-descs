<h2>Product Manager / Product Owner (Shelf Tags)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Overview:</b><br>
Our client is looking for an innovative Product Manager / Product Owner to join their Shelf Tags effort. The ideal candidate will work with the Business to identify products that will provide new capabilities to satisfy KPIs, such as increase in sales, customer traffic, etc. You’re joining a multidisciplinary team of product directors, product managers, and digital analysts to create product listings across the top eCommerce marketplaces.
<br><br>
We are preferred supplier to 7-Eleven. This position may be available for conversion to full time employment upon successful evaluation of performance. You’ll be working in a fast-paced, eclectic environment of talented professionals who are leading the industry in digital capabilities.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Design, manage, and execute on the vision for 7-Eleven’s Digital and Enterprise Products.</li>
<li>Establish or participate in Design Thinking, Agile, and LEAN rituals which establish rhythm for the team, promote ongoing communication, and iterative refinement of roadmaps.</li>
<li>Lead &amp; facilitate in-person research to gain deep customer understanding and empathy.</li>
<li>Turn customer insights into actionable strategies and priorities.</li>
<li>Identify immediate and/or upcoming user needs, then work with the team to develop a product plan that addresses them.</li>
<li>Set product vision and lead road mapping, goal setting, story writing, project execution, and product roll-out strategy.</li>
<li>You don’t believe in MVP, but MDP, Minimally Delightful Product.</li>
<li>Define learning agenda and key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure success/failure.</li>
<li>Gather and prioritize features and tasks for the product using internal tools, tracking and reviewing the product direction and current work frequently.</li>
<li>Strategically evolve and grow existing products and services to constantly deliver a better CX.</li>
<li>Manage collaboration and communication with other teams, both internal and external, to ensure teams have what they need and can work efficiently.</li>
<li>Test and iterate in small-scale pilots to validate concepts, refine them to achieve product-market fit, and then scale to drive vertical and horizontal economics.</li>
<li>Regularly and systematically use data to uncover opportunities, track product performance, and drive decision making.</li>
<li>Manage product budget.</li>
<li>Ability to manage backlog and write user stories.</li>
</ul>
<b>Basic Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree.</li>
<li>At least 7 years of Product Owner / Product Manager experience.</li>
<li>Minimum 1 years of experience in digital product management.</li>
<li>Experience in retail</li>
<li>2-3 years of experience translating business strategy and analysis into consumer/user facing digital products.</li>
<li>1-2 years of experience in Agile product management.</li>
<li>Product Management frameworks, human centered design, design thinking, empathy research.</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Competencies:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Digital product development management</li>
<li>Knowledge of retail product management</li>
<li>Stakeholder management</li>
<li>Agile</li>
<li>Design Thinking</li>
<li>Budget management</li>
<li>Backlog grooming</li>
<li>Ability to write user stories</li>
</ul>
Candidates must be able to demonstrate skills and may be asked to complete one or more assessments to qualify for this position.
