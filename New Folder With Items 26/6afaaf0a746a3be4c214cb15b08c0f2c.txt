SUMMARY</b></div>

WELL’s Mid Account Executive is responsible for closing new business to drive revenue. This includes prospecting, qualifying, solution selling, negotiations, and closing.</div>

Responsibilities include</b></div>
<div>
<div>Qualify, progress, and close new business</div>

<div>
<b><i>Own the industry</i></b><b> - healthcare terminology, workflows, value propositions, personas - and anything else that will help effectively communicate WELL to prospects</b>
</div>
