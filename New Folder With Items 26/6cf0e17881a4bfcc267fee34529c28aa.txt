<h2>MARKETING MANAGER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Verbatim Americas seeks a <b>Marketing Manager</b> to develop, implement and execute effective strategic corporate marketing &amp; digital marketing plans. Work collaboratively with the sales team and product managers to ensure marketing activities meet the needs of the organization. Manage Marketing staff, liaise with all 3rd party marketing support companies, lead market research efforts, quantify and optimize exposure and channel penetration of product, product info and company messaging.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Oversee staff responsible for management of Verbatim websites: verbatim.com, verbatimlighting.com and Verbatim extranet Digital Asset Mgmt System.</li>
<li>
Manage staff responsible for development of product packaging to ensure that packaging is completed on time, within budget and that all packaging configurations maximize space and reduce waste.</li>
<li>
Provide creative direction in adherence with Verbatim Brand Standards for all in-house and agency-created marketing materials, including: advertising, trade show materials and exhibits, packaging, channel marketing materials, promotional flyers, catalogs, retail graphics/displays etc.</li>
<li>
Manage content development, overseeing creation of product copy, ad/promotional copy, editorial content, and corporate brand messaging for all Verbatim product categories.
</li>
<li>Serve as Global Branding representative for Verbatim Americas.</li>
<li>
Research and communicate consumer and product trend info as well as consumer purchase drivers.</li>
<li>
Implement and execute strategy and direction for management of SEO for Verbatim.com and customer sites.
</li>
<li>Define, measure and conduct analysis of ROI.</li>
<li>
Achieve financial goals by preparing an annual budget; scheduling expenditures; analyzing variances; and, initiating corrective actions to meet departmental and company objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA degree in Marketing or Design and at least 5 to 7 yrs related experience in Corporate Marketing / Brand Marketing strategy, specifically: digital marketing, packaging design/development, photography direction, and advertising; or an equivalent combination of education and experience.
</li>
<li>Requires proficiency in Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, and Excel, as well as MAC interface and design applications Adobe Creative Suite - Illustrator, Photoshop and Acrobat Professional.</li>
<li>
Ability to work collaboratively with staff and teams across the organization, exceptional creative thinking and ability to produce strong and compelling marketing materials driven by innovative ideas, understanding the project initiatives &amp; brand objectives.
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills, excellent attention to detail, self-motivated and strong team player.
</li>
<li>Ability to translate marketing objectives of the organization into creative strategies and messaging, ensuring that each project meets the highest standards and organizational goals.
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers, and the general public.
</li>
<li>Ability to direct graphic development and photographic style.</li>
</ul>
