<p><b>
Your Career</b></p>
<p>
Using your experience as a Quality Assurance Test leader, help build the best products to combat cyberattacks by developing teams and processes to remove those threats.</p>
<p>
We are looking for a technical, hands-on manager, experienced with product lifecycles from inception to end-of-life with expert knowledge of Release Management best practices and processes. You are a focused mindset of continuous product quality improvements around stability, functionality, and customer success is key. You ideally have experienced critical issue management, you can assess any problem, remain calm under pressure while providing thoughtful resolutions. Excellent written, verbal communication and presentation skills are required as you will collaborate with Engineers, Customer Advocates, Program and Product Management.</p>
<p><b>
Your Impact</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Successfully execute our software release process to consistently deliver timely, high-quality releases has a huge effect on customer satisfaction and our bottom line</li>
<li>
Maintaining our software releases until their eventual End-of-Support/End-of-Life</li>
<li>
Developing and evolving the team and release processes to increase efficiency and quality</li>
<li>
Communicating and collaborating with customer facing teams to resolve critical issues</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Your Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years’ experience managing a QA team involved in both manual and automated testing</li>
<li>
2+ years’ experience delivering software releases</li>
<li>
Experience communicating directly with software developers, QA Engineers, customer services representatives, sales engineers, and customers from IT admins to C-class executives</li>
<li>
Strong QA Test Methodology fundamentals</li>
<li>
Strong bug tracking analysis skills</li>
<li>
Jira and Confluence experience desired</li>
<li>
Python experience desired</li>
<li>
Experience building and managing test labs/infrastructure</li>
</ul>
