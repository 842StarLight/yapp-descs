<p><b>Project Coordinator</b></p>
<p>
OneSource Building Technologies (OSBT) provides integrated, value-added solutions and services to large-scale national and international customers with a global service provider network, a single point of contact, accountability, consistent quality and on-time delivery.</p>
<p>
OSBT is seeking a driven and self-motivated Project Coordinator with our multinational investment bank and financial services company in Clifton, NJ . This individual is responsible for effective communication, dynamic project management and relationship building among diverse accounts and customers focusing on expeditious and quality delivery and client satisfaction.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Duties/Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide daily IT support services as requested from the client to include network and infrastructure support</li>
<li>Provide single point of contact for issues within the scope of services for this location</li>
<li>Filter all updates and issues including trouble and escalations to the HPE account management team</li>
<li>Manages all HPE queues in Service Now/Aperture/DataOne and other tools in making sure all tickets are up to date and escalate issues to HPE</li>
<li>Assist HPE with site (Clifton) oversee of people, process and daily workloads</li>
<li>Provide operational service engagement (guidance) to Networks, Storage, GBSS, LLET, MKt. Data</li>
<li>Provide guidance and required monthly data to the capacity planning team</li>
<li>Review all processes and procedures and update regularly to reflect current standards</li>
<li>Confirm and adhere to Data Centre auditing and other regulatory requirements</li>
<li>Attend daily/weekly data center related meeting to discuss on-going workload and projects</li>
<li>Create ORM requests for cabling and power for new devices being installed for the Data Center</li>
<li>Assist the team with management of scheduled Data Centre events (power down, IST and other related activities), Post Event Review / Lessons Learned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in the IT Telecommunications industry with the capacity to understand and manage multiple projects, customers, and assigned tasks</li>
<li>Proficient in Windows OS, Microsoft Office Software, and other software programs that may be required</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and some experience interacting with customers similar to that of performing work onsite</li>
<li>General understanding of cable plant components as necessary to perform physical cable plant</li>
<li>Customer oriented, team player, proactive approach</li>
<li>Ability to effectively create and document technical scopes and/or projects</li>
<li>Ability to interpret specifications, drawings, and construction documents</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and logical thought processes, with the ability to resolve simple to complex telecommunications estimates</li>
<br>
</ul>
