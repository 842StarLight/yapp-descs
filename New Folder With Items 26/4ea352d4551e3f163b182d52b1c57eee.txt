<h2>Customer Service Representative Needed for Accounting Department!</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Nevada Corporate Headquarters, Inc. NCH has been launching businesses and careers since 1989! We focus on inspiring employees to learn, grow and challenge themselves as part of our amazing team. We promote an environment that is fun, energetic and supportive!</p>
<p>
Right now, we're growing fast! We're seeking a Verifier to work in our Accounting Department and support the sales team by verifying client orders. This is a fast-paced position, designed to develop your skill sets and provide growth opportunities.</p>
<p>
As a Verifier, you will play a pivotal role in the continued growth of our company, while quickly processing orders and helping our sales team exceed goals. If you're ready to be on a fun, progressive team, NCH is the place for you!</p>
<p><b>
Below are the basic functions of this position:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The primary responsibility of the position will be to speak with clients on the phone to verify sales department orders for completeness and accuracy. The Verifier will also accept client payments over the phone. All calls with clients are recorded for training purposes.</li>
<li>
Invoicing of sales orders into SAGE 100 accounting software in a timely and accurate manner.</li>
<li>
Updating and reconciling sales boards.</li>
<li>
Reconciliation of daily sales invoices to customer payments.</li>
<li>
Filing and organizing daily and monthly files.</li>
<li>
Any other duties assigned by the VP of Finance.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Job Specifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent computer skills, including SAGE 100, Microsoft Dynamics, and Microsoft Office products.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written, with internal and external clients. A positive and professional phone demeanor is required.</li>
<li>
Must be able to Multi-Task.</li>
<li>
Must have the ability to deal with high volume and work in a fast-paced environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
This position pays $13.50/hour.</b></p>
