<div><b>Job Summary</b></div>
<div>The Customer Service Representative exceeds customer expectations by providing superior customer service through strong human relations skills, product knowledge, and sales skills.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assists walk-in and phone customers with technical questions and product knowledge in a timely manner suggesting selections that meet their needs and emphasizes product selling points.
</li>
<li>Establishes strong working relationships with employees, sales team and customers to ensure service levels are exceeding customer's expectations.
</li>
<li>Prepares sales orders and assists the customer in checking and loading merchandise.
</li>
<li>Makes follow-up calls to potential and existing customers to solicit additional business.
</li>
<li>Investigates and resolves customer complaints in a timely manner.
</li>
<li>Maintains a clean, professional sales center appearance and ensures that shelves are properly stocked.
</li>
<li>Assists in receiving, shipping, and general warehouse duties.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>Requirements:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED.
</li>
<li>1+ years of related inside sales experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of irrigation and landscape products.
</li>
<li>Strong customer-service attitude with the ability to deal directly with customers for a large portion of the day.
</li>
<li>Self-motivated and detail-oriented personality.
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced environment dealing with inventory, product allocations and customer issues.
</li>
<li>Ability to lift and move up to 75 pounds.
</li>
<li>Excellent communications skills, both written and verbal.
</li>
<li>Good computer skills including MS Office programs.
</li>
<li>Must be 18 years of age or older to apply.</li>
</ul>
