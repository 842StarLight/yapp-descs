<h2>Communications Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
The Communications Manager is responsible for supporting company communications for the Division. Excellent communication skills and ability to work with a variety of business leaders at multiple levels of the organization. The person will advise and develop key messaging and implementing creative tactics to ensure those messages are broadly understood. Reporting to the SVP of Human Resources this person is a strategic communicator who can recommend and execute communications strategies to engage employees and support change management initiatives.
<br><br>
Position Responsibilities
<br><br>
Lead internal communications and support external communications for the Saputo Dairy Foods USA, LLC division.
<br><br>
Plan Company meetings and develop presentation content and speeches for the President and other executives as needed to effectively communicate business strategy and objectives (e.g., quarterly employee meetings, biennial global convention, annual sales and operations conference, mid-year reviews).
<br><br>
Support Global projects by partnering closely with Corporate Communications to ensure consistent messaging, execution of key initiatives and compliance with corporate branding.
<br><br>
Compose and edit various communications (written and visual) e.g. intranet articles, business correspondence and videos, promotion and employee materials.
<br><br>
Manage media relations for the Division, field media inquiries, recommend and draft responses within established communication guidelines.
<br><br>
Manage and support crisis preparedness and mock exercises, act in the capacity of crisis-management Spokesperson.Provide communications support to special circumstances response team in the event of a recall or crisis scenario (press release, key messaging, Q&amp;A, media relations).
<br><br>
Support national non-profit partnership efforts and community outreach programs.
<br><br>
Building and updating Company SharePoint sites with visual and written content in coordination with IT and maintain company email groups as a point of broad communication distribution.
<br><br>
Coordinate with 3rd party vendors, identify opportunities and recommends changes to maximize vendor effectiveness and reduce costs.
<br><br>
Support and apply Saputo Values and Code of Ethics.
<br><br>
Performs other duties as assigned.
