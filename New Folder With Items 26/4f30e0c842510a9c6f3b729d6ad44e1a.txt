<b>Introduction:</b><br>
<br>
Toshiba Global Commerce Solutions is seeking a Service Delivery Manager to join our team in Indianapolis, IN. The Service Delivery Manager will be responsible to manage a team of service support representatives that maintain equipment and services for retail customers in Indianapolis, IN and surrounding areas. The Service Delivery Manager will establish and grow trusted advisor relationships with the client, leveraging the world-class Stability Team to drive our combined Hardware, Software, and Services business forward. The Service Delivery Manager will also work closely with all other TGCS teams (Client team, Maintenance team, Sales team) engaged on the account. As new Products and Services are introduced into the account, the Service Delivery Manager will participate in resource planning and allocation to meet the obligations resulting from the new Product and/or Service.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide leadership and mentoring to Services employees, especially junior employees and recent hires</li>
<li>
Establish credible operational relationships with clients around store applications and professional services to drive customer satisfaction and TGCS business results</li>
<li>
Leverage Services personnel to deliver projects on time and on budget</li>
<li>
Perform personnel administrative tasks such as expense reports, equipment orders, and vacation approval</li>
</ul>
