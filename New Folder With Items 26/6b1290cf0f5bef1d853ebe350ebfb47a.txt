<h2>Business Development Manager – Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The HPE OEM Business is seeking a highly motivated, business development specialist to assist in the identification of new OEM design win opportunities and solve business inhibitors. The OEM business development team global business unit based resources whose mission it is to develop strategic, high-value OEM business opportunities. We create long-term HPE OEM value through customers, markets, and relationships.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The HPE OEM Global Business Unit is a fast growing team managing a rapidly expanding segment of HPE’s business. We leverage the entire HPE product and solutions portfolio enabling OEM customers to build their solution on HPE’s best in class infrastructure platforms. We offer full or semi customization of our standard offerings which are then deployed in multiple industrial verticals and applications. Leveraging HPE’s global supply chain and service capability, we are able to deliver and support customer solutions around the world, giving OEM’s global reach to grow their business. HPE’s OEM business supports customers at the forefront of technology enabling innovation in Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, Internet of Things (IoT) and more within multiple vertical markets such as Healthcare, telecommunications, video surveillance and manufacturing.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The HPE OEM GBU is part of the Hybrid IT organization (HIT), Innovation is at the heart of HIT from the Edge to the Core. We strive to be at the forefront of new ideas and drive emerging technology. Our Converged Infrastructure Cloud to Edge portfolio offers industry leading solutions including Aruba, Edgeline, Proliant Gen10, Simplivity, 3Par, Synergy and much more. HPE technology is known for their technical design capabilities, superior quality and performance and a recognized leader in the market.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
We are looking for an autonomous, self-motivated individual with a desire to identify and drive new business opportunities, solve business inhibitors and deliver excellent customer experiences.</p>
