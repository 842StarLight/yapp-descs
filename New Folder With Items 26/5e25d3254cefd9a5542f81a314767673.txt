<b>
About this role</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>Applied IoT Solutions team are responsible for new SaaS, lighting and controls business development, and expanding existing relationships within multiple go to market channels. Applied IoT Solutions represents the entire range of Acuity Brands capability and promote Acuity’s Atrius IoT platform of software services and Atrius enabled lighting and control systems. As our solutions achieve rapid growth, successful candidates have strong income and career expansion potential.</p>
<p>
This position reports to the Vice President of Applied IoT Solutions team</p>
</div>
</div>

<b>
How you will contribute:</b>
</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Develops strong business relationships with line of business owners within the client’s organization from operating to executive levels</li>
<li>
Develops and maintain relationships with key internal customers to ensure effective communications and performance to plan</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Creates the business cases within the client’s organization to drive adoption &amp; expansion</li>
<li>
Establishes and executes business plans that drive revenue and achieve specific account objectives</li>
<li>
Delivers persuasive presentations to customers with the goal to get Acuity Brands product specified and purchased</li>
<li>
Must be highly organized and self-motivated</li>
<li>
Follow up and develop sales leads to deliver specification preference on construction bids</li>
