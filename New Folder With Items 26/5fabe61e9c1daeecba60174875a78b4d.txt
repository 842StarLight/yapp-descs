<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><i>Vic.ai is an NYC-based artificial intelligence company developing a platform that automates and augments the human component in the Accounting &amp; Finance departments. The accounting industry is on the verge of AI disruption, and Vic.ai is one of the driving forces behind it.</i></p>
<p>We help mid-market companies like Intercom and Nurx, and 4 of the top 10 accounting firms, including KPMG, PWC, and BDO, use Vic.ai.</p>
<p>The Vic.ai platform will automate the accounting tasks associated with AP, Bank, Expenses, and AR then start to provide actionable insights, advice and benchmarking - ultimately reaching our visionary goal of creating an Artificially Intelligent CFO.</p>
<p>Cowboy Ventures led our seed in 2017, and we have recently completed our Series A (to be announced), other notable investors are John Shaw, Jesse Lucas (Ubers finance team / #15th employee), Jason Stockwood (Group CEO of Simply Business), and Shan-lyn Ma (CEO of Zola).</p>
<p>Our company is re-thinking how a $200Bn industry works. We are a team of highly passionate people led by experienced entrepreneurs, and we are seeking enthusiastic, detail-oriented, curious disruptors.</p>
<p><b>The Role:
</b></p>
<p>We are seeking a Product Manager to provide leadership and product direction. Your responsibilities will include translating ideas into strategy and features and following product development from start to finish.</p>
<p>You have an entrepreneurial mindset, using creative problem solving and user-centric design thinking to build new platform features and modules. You will collaborate closely with engineering, design, client success, and other key business partners while reporting to the CEO.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and manage company's strategic product roadmap</li>
<li>
Drive the success of multiple product lines by formulating, prioritizing, and delivering differentiating capabilities</li>
<li>
Work alongside engineering to ensure the requirements are built to spec, deployed to a quality level, and provide the results you predicted</li>
<li>
Develop product strategy and roadmap based on market research, customer feedback, and close collaboration with Sales, Business Development, Implementation, and Customer Success teams</li>
<li>
Engage stakeholders to align on product strategy and priorities that deliver measurable results to our company and our customers</li>
<li>
Regularly communicate product roadmap and status of key initiatives to internal stakeholders and customers</li>
<li>
Define, track and improve key product metrics and success criteria for major product initiatives</li>
<li>
Oversee the entire product development lifecycle from conception to launch and post-launch adoption</li>
<li>
Effectively collaborate with our customers, customer-facing teams and engineering teams to develop requirements and drive projects to completion</li>
<li>
Work closely with customers, including conducting interviews and surveys</li>
<li>
Manage deliverables and custom development efforts required to onboard strategic accounts or partners</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4+ years of experience in product management role (B2B SaaS)</li>
<li>
Bachelor's or Master's degree in a related field</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to drive development and release of new software offerings</li>
<li>
Excited by the idea of managing multiple responsibilities at once in a startup environment, and ruthlessly prioritizing the highest impact work.</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize and manage multiple projects and issues in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Ability to perform ongoing analysis and market surveying<br>
</li>
</ul>
