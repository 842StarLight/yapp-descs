<h2>General Manager - LEE Filters, Burbank</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Reporting to the Managing Director of Lee Filters, the VP General Managers' role is to provide leadership and management to the US team, lead sales and be fully accountable and responsible for the sales and distribution operation in Burbank, California. This business unit services the whole of the North American market and reports into LEE management in the UK.</p>
<p></p>
<p>To deliver the planned growth, a new VP General Manager is required who is commercial and sales driven, is product / technically strong and is able to professionally lead operations of the US business. Responsibilities include the daily management and motivation of the team, driving sales, managing commercial processes and decision-making, ensuring adherence to local and group policies, ensuring compliance and the usual duties as required with running a business. Fundamental is the efficient running of Operations, and managing the Operations Manager to ensure that daily operations with stock, warehouse and distribution happen in a timely manner so we provide a constant high level of customer service and support.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Demand drives a business so the new VP General Manager must be market active and leading the external sales and business development team. It is critical that they build robust working relationships with major clients and the activities of LEE US are aligned with the needs of our valued US clients and the changing marketplace.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Working closely with the UK based management team, contribute to the global Sales, Marketing, New Product and HR strategies to develop the whole of LEE Filters to deliver sustained profitable growth. The US business has seen plenty of change and so the new VP General Manager will need to draw a line under the past and work with the new UK team and Panavision US to deliver on the market potential of North America, and develop the local team and infrastructure accordingly. Panavision in Woodland Hills provides shared services for Finance, IT and HR alongside the LEE UK team. This is an exciting general management role in a successful company that is part of a major market leading company. A real opportunity to drive change, deliver growth and really make your mark on a growing business in a large group.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Help fully integrate LEEUS into the LEE organization.</li></ul>
<p>• Build a “can do” culture and professionalize the US business so to best serve the US marketplace and deliver growth. • Be active in the market alongside the external sales and business development team to develop client relationships, drive sales, capitalize on market opportunities and collect market intel for new product development.</p>
<ul><li>Provide leadership and management to the whole team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Direct and coordinate activities of all areas of the business including operations, customer service, sales and distribution to maximize revenues and margins of LEE products.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare and submit a monthly sales forecast, stock order(s) and other financial reports as required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contribute to the LEE annual budgeting process specifying sales, margins, investment, resources and other needs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop a good working knowledge of all the company’s products and the US marketplace.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish regular reviews with all of the team; define personal objectives and monitor performance. Deal promptly with any people issues or needs with the support of LEE HR as required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review financial statements, sales and activity reports, and other performance data to measure productivity and make changes as necessary to optimize performance.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify opportunities to improve efficiencies, reduce costs and improve overall trading performance.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Best practice implementation with regard to working conditions, employment terms, plant and equipment maintenance and Health and Safety, with adherence to existing company and group policies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Determine staffing requirements and define the optimum structure needed to deliver growth. Work with HR to develop, recruit or train existing and new employees.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Submit the necessary financial reports on a timely basis as needed by the LEE management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversee the US stocking levels to ensure the market is serviced and sales are maximized.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help develop and implement product-marketing strategies including advertising campaigns and sales promotions.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Experience Required:</b></p>
<ul><li>Prior experience leading and operating a business at a Director or GM level</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in business, sales or related field, preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong people leadership and management skills</li></ul>
<p>• Proven track record in sales and business development • General Management and or Operations experience within a product sales and distribution business.</p>
<ul><li>Ideally B2B and B2C market experience in photographic, optics or similar product technologies sold via distribution into the motion picture / cinematography, entertainment and or consumer retail industries</li></ul>
<ul><li>Commercially astute, able to make pricing and other decisions to drive sales revenues and margins</li></ul>
<ul><li>Comfortable with being client facing and as the head of the US business</li></ul>
<p></p>

<p></p>
