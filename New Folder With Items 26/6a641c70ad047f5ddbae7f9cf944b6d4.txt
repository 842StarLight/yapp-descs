<p><b>COMPANY DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>As market leader in enterprise application software, SAP helps companies of all sizes and industries innovate through simplification. From the back office to the boardroom, warehouse to storefront, on premise to cloud, desktop to mobile device – SAP empowers people and organizations to work together more efficiently and use business insight more effectively to stay ahead of the competition. SAP applications and services enable customers to operate profitably, adapt continuously, and grow sustainably.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Location:</b> Reston, VA (and various client sites nationwide)</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Purpose and Objective:</b> SAP America, Inc. seeks a Business Processes Senior Consultant at our Reston, VA office location (and various client sites nationwide) to be responsible for process-specific solution consulting for specific applications and processes for the following SAP solutions: SAP S/4HANA; Suite on HANA; SAP R/3 Applications &amp; Finance and Controlling (FICO); and Financial Supply Chain Management (FSCM).</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Expectations and Tasks:</b> Solution consulting for the General Ledger (GL), Cost Center Accounting (GL), Accounts Receivable/Accounts Payable (AR/AP), Taxes, Profit Center Accounting (PCA), Profitability Analysis (CO-PA), Asset Accounting (AA), Internal Orders (IO) and Product Costing (CO-PC) solutions. Create business blueprints. Perform product implementation based on a blueprint. Oversee the product lifecycle from creating test cases, test scheduling, execution, key user training, Go-Live support, and post Go-Live support. Integrate solutions with other process areas such as Materials Management (MM), Enterprise Asset Management(EAM), Human Capital Management (HCM), Sales &amp; Distribution (SD), Production Planning (PP) and Project Systems (PS). Support in escalated projects. Perform feasibility studies and solution reviews. Support pre-sales activities. Take over responsibility from time to time as a sub project team leader. Take responsibility from time to time as team lead and support respective line manager(s) in people development tasks. Detail the planning of process implementation. Define detailed blueprint for development requirements. Perform corporate process implementation across all related applications (SAP/non-SAP, OnPremise and cloud-based). Develop and assure quality of process models. Drive the deployment of SAP Model Companies for specific area of business. 100% travel required to various client sites nationwide. Telecommuting available.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and Qualifications/Skills and Competencies:</b> Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business Administration, or a related field of study and 6 years of experience required. The company will also accept a Master's degree and 4 years of experience. The company will also accept three year degree or combination of degrees/diplomas as meeting the Bachelor's degree requirement.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work Experience:</b> Experience must involve 4 years of SAP ECC; SAP R/3; SAP Controlling, Product cost by production/process order, product cost by Sales order, variance analysis, Account based co-PA, reporting in CO-PA and Derivations in CO-PA; SAP Treasury - Cash Management and Liquidity Forecasting; SAP Asset Accounting – Asset under construction, Legacy Migration, Capital Lease scenario and Special Depreciation; SAP Finance – New GL, Document Splitting, Substitutions/Validations, subsequent activation of zero balance clearing; Pre-sales activities – Demo, Response to RFP’s etc; End to end process mapping and its integration for processes such as Procure to Pay (Material Management), Order to Cash (Sales and Distribution), Make to Order (Production Planning), Make to Stock, Repetitive Manufacturing, Maintain to Settle scenario with Line item settlement (Plant Maintenance), Hire to retire (Human Capital Management) and build to settle (Project Systems); Integrating FI-GL with Consolidations (BPC) for group reporting; product development, writing complex functional specifications, and writing business blueprinting documents; working with Fiori, Analysis Office and Lumira. 100% travel required to various client sites nationwide. Telecommuting available.</p>
