<h2>Principal Product Manager - SAFR</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>RealNetworks is seeking a Principal Product Manager for SAFR , our world-class facial recognition platform. This is a tremendous opportunity to play a critical role in the growth and development of an enterprise AI startup within a publicly traded company that has a track record of market-leading innovation.</p>
<p>
As the entrepreneurial product owner of SAFR for Security, you will manage the end-to-end lifecycle for our recently released SAFR for Security offering, disrupting the $40B physical security business worldwide and making it simpler for heads of security to keep physical spaces secure and accessible. You will chart the path for this offering to validated product/market fit and growth; create and champion a data-driven and market-informed product vision and strategy; and apply your customer obsession to develop and drive a product feature and release road map based on in-depth data analysis. This is an individual contributor role who will lead engineering, marketing, and sales colleagues through effective influence on a global basis, growing your team with the business over time.<br>
<br>
<b>Your work will entail:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Developing a keen understanding of target customers, their needs, and how we will help experience success with SAFR;</li>
<li>
Developing market mastery by working with customers, industry analysts, partners, and others;</li>
<li>
Defining features through writing detailed user stories that allow for efficient development and prioritization, managing design challenges, and executing delivery;</li>
<li>
Creating basic mock up and wireframes for product concepts;</li>
<li>
Prioritizing and overseeing execution of your product road map with skill and clarity;</li>
<li>
Developing product documentation, thought leadership, customer presentations, and other content assets;</li>
<li>
Collaborating with product team members and leadership to help ensure a cohesive, integrated product portfolio;</li>
<li>
Developing and measuring product KPIs, deriving key insights, and devising and executing experiments to find product solutions and optimize their performance through rapid, data driven iteration; and</li>
<li>
Building strong partnerships with stakeholders and thrive on collaboration and the creation of experiences that align with the SAFR Guiding Principles.</li>
</ul>
