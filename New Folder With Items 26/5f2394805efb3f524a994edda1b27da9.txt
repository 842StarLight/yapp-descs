<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>The Business Development Manager is a highly motivated self-starter who will lead and provide strategic sales initiatives, engage in marketplace sales activity and work closely with the Market Leaders, operational Executives and the Marketing Department on lead generation activities, sales activities and proposal generation activities. The role requires superior planning, communication and business development skills together with the ability to effectively collaborate with various internal senior associates to effectively close sales and achieve targets. To learn more please visit www.fsresidential.com/california</p>
<p><b>Note: Ideal candidate will have at least 3 years of experience selling services versus a commodity. Must enjoy cold-calling. Must be flexible with schedule as Board Meetings happen 1-2 times per week in the evenings. Base + commission position. </b></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the ongoing development, implementation and tracking of new client development operations and processes for FirstService Residential.</li>
<li>Develop and execute a sales strategy to obtain residential association management contracts that meet the FirstService Residential client-type strategy.</li>
<li>Assist in the growth of both current and potentially new market territories by:</li>
<li>o Providing trending and analysis associated with current processes, product types, service lines, regions of business, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>o Attending planning and business development meetings as requested.</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and directly participate in the sales process; deliver sales presentations to prospective clients.</li>
<li>Proficient and effective at giving client presentations and closing deals.</li>
<li>Continually seek out innovative ways to communicate our value to prospective clients.</li>
<li>Monitor competition and keep on top of industry trends; report findings to leadership team.</li>
<li>Manage sales process through utilization of CRM (Dynamics) - entering and updating lead/opportunity information, documents, generation of sales follow up schedule, and lead/opportunity communications.</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing team and participate in preparation of proposals and RFP responses; responsible for making site visits to obtain information for proposals.</li>
<li>Coordinate and/or participate in the strategy of sale presentations including internal preparation of materials, team member attendance, pre and post presentation meetings.</li>
<li>Serve as a resource person for FirstService Residential affiliate companies.</li>
<li>Other initiatives as identified by senior leadership.</li>
</ul>
<p>Education &amp; Experience</p>
<ul><li>3+ years of sales experience in a service related industry is preferred.</li></ul>
