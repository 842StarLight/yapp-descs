<h2>Director of Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Who you are</b></p>
<p>
ConXtech is seeking an energetic, self-driven Business Development Leader who will be responsible for growing the business by winning work and closing contracts. As a sales leader, you will identify opportunities where ConXtech adds value and develops relationships with decision makers and influencers, including project owners, engineers, construction managers, general contractors and industry consultants. The focus of this role is in the commercial industry, such as data centers, healthcare and office complexes. Knowledge and experience in the process industry will be considered positively as we further differentiate our business.</p>
<p>
You will develop value propositions to uniquely offer solutions to clients. These strategies will be led with a high degree of interaction and teamwork with the operation, engineering and manufacturing teams as well as senior management. This is an external facing role which means that you must enjoy meeting clients and have a passion to win work.</p>
<p>
You have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree or equivalent work-related experience</li>
<li>
Minimum 10 years of experience in Business Development and/or Account Management with success in winning work and developing new profitable contracts, preferably in a technical services and project execution environment</li>
<li>
Demonstrated network with owners, architects, engineers, contractors and related professional services relationships that could be leveraged to create business opportunities</li>
<li>
Strong business acumen, skills and maturity as well as the ability to work effectively with others</li>
<li>
Outstanding written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and effectively as part of a team, while handling multiple tasks and responsibilities simultaneously</li>
<li>
Proficiency managing multiple client situations, needs and inquiries simultaneously</li>
<li>
Ability to develop and maintain good cross enterprise working relationships</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you’ll be doing</b></p>
<p>
As a Director of Business Development for ConXtech, you’ll:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Win work!!!</li>
<li>
Develop close working relationships to position ConXtech for success in a most effective and productive manner</li>
<li>
Develop and effectively communicate ConXtech’s value proposition</li>
<li>
Provide strategic leadership to the internal engineering, operations and manufacturing</li>
<li>
Partner with senior management to influence key business decisions that grow a profitable business</li>
<li>
Develop and deliver sales meetings and presentations that clearly focus on client’s needs and the ConXtech value propositions to our commercial and industrial markets</li>
<li>
Negotiate and communicate complex concepts and business issues to a variety of audiences</li>
<li>
Individual will be required to follow through on executed projects to ensure that ConXtech is delivering its commitment and creating outstanding customer value</li>
<li>
Market event initiatives by calling on invitees/attendees to drive attendance, assess lead viability, and schedule prospect meetings at these shows</li>
<li>
Provide reports on pipeline movement</li>
<li>
Demonstrate positively ConXtech’s mission, vision and values both internally and throughout the marketplace</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Where you’ll be working</b></p>
<p>
You’ll be based at our fun, commuter friendly Pleasanton, CA office.</p>
<p><b>
Who we are</b></p>
<p>
ConXtech is a construction technology company based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Our innovative structural steel building systems enable structures to be designed, fabricated and erected Faster, Better, Safer &amp; Greener™ than any conventional alternative. The ConX System is the chassis inside over 10 million square feet of healthcare, data center, commercial, high density residential and industrial structures, and our R&amp;D team works collaboratively to improve our products, systems and processes. ConXtech works at the leading edge of the construction industry, introducing manufacturing methods and new approaches to increase efficiency, quality and safety throughout the design-to-delivery process.</p>
