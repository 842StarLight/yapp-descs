Job Overview:</b></div>
<div>
This position is unique in its design which will be rotational between various Finance and Accounting areas within GES. Your daily assignments may vary providing diversification and would be considered non-routine in nature. This individual will be assigned a supporting role, in which the individual will be responsible for filling various positions within a department (Accounts Payable, Credit, Billing, Cash Applications, Record to Report, etc.), that is experiencing a lack of resources, an increase in volume, process change or other specified need. The ability to adapt to different roles and changing environments, while maintaining a supportive and customer service focus is crucial for success.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Below is a SAMPLE of varing responsibilities to be assigned as the candidate rotates through SSAG.</b>:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Accounts Payable</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Process vendor invoices from receipt through payment.</li>
<li>
Enter properly approved invoices (pre-approved) into Oracle AP; complete peer review of entered pre-approved invoice batches.</li>
<li>
Ensure accuracy of data entered into financial system.</li>
<li>
Maintain excellent vendor relationships.</li>
<li>
Maintain vendor accounts in a current status by reconciliation of statements.</li>
<li>
Research and resolve invoicing and payment issues.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) requirements for invoice payment controls</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance with company accounting policies and procedures and IRS rules and guidelines.</li>
<li>
Ensure appropriate accrual of sales and use tax.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Billing</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Timely, accurate and complete billing of various revenue lines.</li>
<li>
Audit customer files in accordance with established procedures and produce timely client specific invoices for products and services within ‘days to invoice’ metrics /productivity standards.</li>
<li>
Ensure accuracy of pricing, labor hours, vendor invoices, products and services to price lists and contracts (where applicable) to begin the audit process.</li>
<li>
Ensure approval signatures as needed based on company policy and job guidelines are all obtained</li>
<li>
Research discrepancies as needed<ul>
<li>
Interact with show site personnel, sales, operations, internal clients, and others to compile accurate billing records and effectively solicit timely information for billing completeness</li>
<li>
Create, maintain, complete and ensure full record keeping of client/exhibitor billing files in hardcopy and electronic form per company policy, for Sarbanes-Oxley compliance purposes and as directed</li>
<li>
Perform duties in compliances with GES policies, procedures and regulations</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>

Credit</b></div>
