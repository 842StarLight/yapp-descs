<h2>Account Manager & Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>MAM Software Group, Inc. (NASDAQ Capital Markets: MAMS) is a leading global provider of on premise and cloud-based software solutions for the automotive and other industries.</i></b></p>
<p><b>Position Title</b>: Account Manager &amp; Business Development</p>
<p>We're looking for a results-driven sales representative who is an aggressive, quick learner to actively seek out and engage current and prospective customers. This position begins as an internal HQ office position, which is intended to grow to an outside sales position with on target success. The position will be based in our Blue Bell, PA office and with success, will move to an outside sales position which could be based anywhere in the continental US that is located near a major airport.</p>
<p>This position reports to our VP of Sales &amp; Marketing.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reach out to potential customer leads through cold calling and email campaigns</li>
<li>Upgrade sales to existing customers</li>
<li>Selling software &amp; services to prospective smaller tire &amp; service centers</li>
<li>Demonstration of all of the MAM software products to customers and potential customers</li>
<li>Maintaining positive business relationships to ensure future sales</li>
<li>Trade show support – initially 2-3 per year</li>
<li>Learn the automotive aftermarket</li>
<li>Establish, develop and maintain positive business and customer relationships</li>
<li>Achieve agreed upon sales targets and outcomes within schedule</li>
<li>Coordinate sales effort with team members and other departments</li>
<li>Supply management with reports on customer needs, problems, interests, competitive activities, and potential for new products and services</li>
<li>Keep abreast of best practices and promotional trends</li>
<li>Continuously improve through feedback</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Highly motivated and target driven</li>
<li>Excellent selling, communication and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Prioritizing, time management and organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to create and deliver presentations tailored to the audience needs</li>
<li>Relationship management skills and openness to feedback</li>
<li>Proficient working with MS Office or GoogleDocs</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Travel is minimal initially – but growing into an outside sale position where travel can be up to 4-5 nights per month. Present, promote and sell products/services using solid arguments to existing and prospective customers</i></p>
<p><b>Desired skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS/BA degree or equivalent</li>
<li>Automotive (tire or service) Industry knowledge is a plus</li>
<li>Proven work experience in sales and/or marketing</li>
</ul>
