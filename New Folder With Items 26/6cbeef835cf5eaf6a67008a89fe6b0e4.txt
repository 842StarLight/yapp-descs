<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The Deal Desk Analyst will support View’s rapid commercial growth by accelerating deal velocity while enforcing sound business process control. The deal desk analyst process manages quotes, commercial contracts, and orders in our quote-to-cash (QTC) business processes. As a Deal Desk Analyst, you will also perform data quality management activities to ensure high data integrity of View’s sales funnel. The analyst will work closely with Sales, Finance, Operations, Product Marketing, and Customer Success. He/she will play a critical role in customer engagement and sales order management for all projects arriving through the sales pipeline.</p>
<p>
Deal Desk is responsible for providing business process, legal and operational support to Sales in the closure of commercial agreements for View. As a Deal Desk Analyst, you will drive enhancements to the quote-to-cash processes and policies to increase business velocity and improve operational efficiencies. You will establish and maintain strong working relationships with View sales teams and other cross-functional stakeholders. You must have exceptional communication, organizational and time management skills and be comfortable juggling multiple tasks while meeting strict deadlines. At the same time, you should have a bias for execution and a high level of drive and creativity to constantly improve your area of responsibility.</p>
<p>
This role provides the opportunity to learn all about the fast-expanding dynamic glass market and have hands-on exposure to sophisticated quote-to-cash business processes. We seek a self-starter that is detail-oriented, displays excellent interpersonal skills and enjoys the challenges of working in a dynamic and collaborative environment. The Deal Desk Analyst will report to the Deal Desk Manager and will be a sales advocate and partner to internal organizations.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Interface closely with Sales, Finance, Operations, Product Marketing, and Customer Success</li>
<li>
Monitor and enforce CRM data integrity with relevant sales opportunities in support of the overall sales process</li>
<li>
Prepare cost budgetary pricing estimates and formal quotes</li>
<li>
Collaborate with field project managers to validate and process orders.</li>
<li>
Assist in generating top-down/bottom-up forecasting for sales and manufacturing</li>
<li>
Actively support continuous process improvements relevant to the sales activities/functions</li>
<li>
Build productive relationships and feedback loops with Legal, Finance, Sales and Product teams to drive operational and process efficiencies, interpret and negotiate contractual language, and manage approvals for all non-standard agreement language.</li>
<li>
Measure, monitor, and report select performance metrics.</li>
</ul>
