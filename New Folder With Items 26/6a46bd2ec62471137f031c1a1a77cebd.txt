<h2>Front Desk Sales/ Client Service Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>Description</p>
<p>
Each Elements Studio is independently owned and operated.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>The Wellness Membership Advisor (WMA) is directly responsible for client conversions and coordinating front desk operations.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The WMA is responsible for client conversions in order to meet studio goals and quotas to ensure growth and profitability through the sales of:</p>
<ul>
<li>Memberships to the Wellness Program</li>
<li>Packages</li>
<li>Gift Cards</li>
<li>Additional Visits (Rebooking)</li>
<li>Upgraded sessions such as longer sessions or hot stone</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>The WMA is responsible for adhering to established <i>“Service Path” </i>procedures and processes to ensure a flawless “Elements Experience” including, but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Greeting every client with a smile.</li>
<li>Creating a calm environment.</li>
<li>Answering phones professionally and converting phone calls into appointments.</li>
<li>Demonstrating complete knowledge of our massage model, therapist skill levels and price structure.</li>
<li>Proficiency with massage therapy terminology.</li>
<li>Matching clients and therapists correctly based on our level system and client’s problem areas.</li>
<li>Mastering MBO through accurate scheduling and completing the retail sales process.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Addressing emergency scheduling issues quickly and professionally.</li>
<li>Complying with all HIPAA rules and regulations and/or applicable state privacy and security laws.</li>
<li>Facilities management, including but not limited to, hourly studio checks to ensure massage rooms and restrooms are clean and properly stocked.</li>
<li>Completing tasks by deadlines as assigned by supervisor. (Example: Communicating with members and clients who have not visited the studio in the past 45 days with the goal of scheduling their next session.)</li>
<li>Other Duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Communicating with clients to determine areas of improvement within the Service Path and complete client satisfaction.</li>
<li>Providing helpful responses to requests from clients and involve management as needed.</li>
<li>Addressing client requests and disputes professionally and efficiently.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Communicating effectively with coworkers.</li>
<li>Providing timely and appropriate communication with studio management.</li>
<li>Being flexible.</li>
<li>Actively participating in staff meetings.</li>
</ul> <p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sales experience (1+ year preferred)</li>
<li>Customer service skills</li>
<li>Computer proficiency-Microsoft Office, Word, Excel, Internet Explorer</li>
<li>Retail or industry experience, a plus</li>
<li>Web-based scheduling, a plus</li>
<li>Attention to detail and ability to follow instructions (written and verbal) is essential</li>
<li>Ability to remain flexible in a constantly changing environment</li>
<li>Ability to remain confident and calm under stress</li>
</ul>
<p>We are a growing therapeutic massage studio and are seeking responsible Sales and Client Service Specialists to join our team.</p>
<p>Just a few of the benefits we will provide:</p>
<ul>
<li>A safe, warm, professional environment in which to work</li>
<li>Free Monthly Massage</li>
<li>Flexible Scheduling</li>
<li>Opportunities to grow</li>
</ul>
<p>Minimum requirement is 3 shifts per week. Average shift length is 4 to 7 hours. Must be available to work one evening shift and either a Saturday or Sunday.</p>
<p>Elements is a smoke free work environment.</p>
