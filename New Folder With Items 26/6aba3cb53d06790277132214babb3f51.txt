<div><h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description
</b></h2></div>
<div>
<div><div>This position ensures Leica Biosystems systems and products are supported effectively by well-trained field support representatives in all regions. Additionally, the Global Support Engineer provides technical leadership to the Global Support team, and ensures LBS customers hold the product reliability and support functions in high regard.<br>
</div></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Major Responsibilities
</div>
<div></div>
<div><ul>
<li><div><div>Subject matter expert, including understanding of instrument design, installation, setup, troubleshooting and repair of instrumentation
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Subject matter expert for associated software, networking and integration of systems into customer network and Laboratory Information Systems (LIS)
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Act as the field service / customer advocate for all instrument and service related issues reported from the field.
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Monitor and review daily escalations (level 3) through email and phone calls to provide rapid response for troubleshooting, remote support and resolution recommendations.
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Organize and lead conference calls with participation from field service engineers, scientists and sales channel managers to gather data and understand high priority escalations and then determine an appropriate action plan and required resources to resolve efficiently.
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Analyze instrument service log files and data to determine possible root causes for reported issues.
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Replicate customer instrument configuration to test possible fault scenarios and determine likely root causes for reported issues.
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Work closely with the R&amp;D, Supplier Quality and Production Engineering teams to analyses failure modes and determine countermeasures for containment and field actions.
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Represent the global service teams in reviewing change requests, new component and system designs for service and supportability.
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Review, approve and analyze warranty failures to determine possible systemic issues and develop appropriate action plans to improve product quality.
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Create, review and deploy Technical Service Bulletins to instruct support teams on change implementation to instrument configuration in the field.
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Develop, maintain and deliver technical training packs (globally) for standardised troubleshooting, repair and preventative maintenance procedures.
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Support and provide input to technical writer team to develop and maintain service manuals
</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>Support global high priority escalations as required, at site (international travel may be required at short notice).<br>
</div></div></li>
</ul></div>
</div>
