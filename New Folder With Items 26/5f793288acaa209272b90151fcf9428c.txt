<h2>Senior Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p>Association Management Center seeks a <b>Senior Marketing</b><b>Manager</b> to join our team. This role supports The Hematology/Oncology Pharmacy Association (HOPA). HOPA supports pharmacy practitioners and seeks to promote and advance hematology/oncology pharmacy to optimize the care of individuals affected by cancer.
</p>
<p>The Senior Marketing Manager will create and implement comprehensive marketing campaigns for all HOPA programs and products. Responsible for development of strategic communications plans for membership and industry supporters, as well as oversight of all HOPA publications and media relations. The Senior Marketing Manager will coordinate work with other marketing staff, education staff, sales staff, and consultants on implementation of the plans created to support HOPA’s strategic pillars.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities for the position include the following:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Campaign creation in support of educational products and programs</li>
<li>
Strategic membership recruitment, retention and engagement</li>
<li>
Retention strategies and communications plans for industry supporters</li>
<li>
Brand/logo management</li>
<li>
Media Relations</li>
<li>
Customer identification and market development</li>
<li>
Print and electronic communications</li>
<li>
Maintaining HOPA Website as an effective communication tool and resource for members</li>
<li>
Developing and implementing comprehensive innovative, high-impact strategies for integrated multi-channel marketing efforts to drive membership retention and strong ROI on educational programs and products and other organizational initiatives</li>
<li>
Managing brand/logo equity and ensuring brand tracking across multiple channels and brand/logo measures in reporting. Recommending changes in approach as needed to remain differentiated in market</li>
<li>
Optimizing marketing campaign effectiveness through analytics, reporting and analysis and making recommendations to evolve based on effectiveness, competitive landscape and emerging organizational needs</li>
<li>
Defining and evolving social media strategy and execution. Developing social media guidelines, best practices and leverage tools and resources</li>
<li>
Directing coordination of the editorial content of the website, eNewsletters, membership collateral, new member communications, customer outreach, brochures, advertising, educational promotions, press releases, etc.</li>
<li>
Serving as primary contact for all media relations-both traditional and digital.Overseeing marketing staff, providing project management, prioritization, deadlines, guidance, to ensure strong collaboration and high performance</li>
<li>
Managing internal department and vendor relationships. Strong budget and timeline management skills</li>
<li>
Partnering with the Executive Director and collaborating with the Director of Operations and the Director of Professional Development to achieve department goals</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as determined appropriate</li>
</ul>
