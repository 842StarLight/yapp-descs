<h2>Lead Software Engineer, Full Stack</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Job Overview:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>We are looking for a Lead Software Engineer with a focus on back end technologies to help build our new in-store mobile platform reporting to the Manager, Stores. This person will Lead and collaborate with a larger team of engineers and designers to develop experiences designed to support Macys instore colleague initiatives. The ideal candidate must be able to thrive in an environment where change is constant. This position requires the need to be extremely organized, highly collaborative, and results-driven. Individuals need to take initiative and reach out to other team members when they need assistance. Likewise, the Lead Software Engineer will be responsible for the leadership, development, and support of applications for a specific area, leveraging his/her expertise and knowledge of multiple systems. Perform other duties as assigned.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Functions:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and design the application experiences, ensuring pages are programmed responsively, error-free, cross-browser/cross-device compatible, highly scalable, and optimized for performance.</li>
<li>
Follow standards for development of tools and experiences, with focus on performance, scalability, and reusability.</li>
<li>
Stay current with the newest online technologies. Perform quality assurance on all new code from inception through integration, staging, and production environments.</li>
<li>
Collaborate effectively with team members as well as UX, QE, and release engineering team.</li>
<li>
Rapidly execute on solutions when problems or challenges have been identified.</li>
<li><div>
Consistently demonstrate ownership and on time delivery.</div></li>
<li><div>
Consistently demonstrate regular, dependable attendance and punctuality.</div></li>
<li>
Flexibility to work across all functions/levels as part of a dynamic team.</li>
<li><div>
Serve as a coach/mentor to less experienced developers.</div></li>
<li><div>
 Manages and delegates tasks as appropriate.</div></li>
</ul>
