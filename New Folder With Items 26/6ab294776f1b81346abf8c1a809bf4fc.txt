<p></p>iHeartMedia Stations<br>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Job Summary:
</b></p>Identifies and develops new business opportunities; keeps and grows existing client relationships; offers solutions that help clients achieve their business goals; closes business; meets set sales target.<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Identifies and solicits new business; builds and maintains a full pipeline of sales prospects.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Services and grows relationships in existing base of clients.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identifies client/agency needs and develops persuasive proposals to meet needs and opportunities.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Delivers effective sales presentations.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Steers clients based on market, platform and station information.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains client communication and ensures client satisfaction.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitors competition to continually find new account leads.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Negotiates rates based on iHeartMedia’s budgets.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Works collaboratively with internal partners to drive revenue.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures prompt payments.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follows all station procedures for preparing orders, resolving billing issues, submitting regular reports regarding sales, pipeline lists, forecasts and competitive analysis.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Creates effective marketing campaigns in cooperation with iHeartMedia resources.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Generates revenue and meets/exceeds established sales targets.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Candidate must drive their own vehicle with a valid driver’s license and state-mandated auto insurance
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Proficient in Microsoft Office suite and social networking platforms
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adept at prospecting and using effective consultative selling principles and practices
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong client service relationship-building skills
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to plan and organize, set priorities and multi task in a fast-paced environment
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Negotiation and closing proficiency
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Persuasive communication skills: verbal, written and presentation
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Independent; self-motivated; competitive; assertive
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understanding of market dynamics including demographics
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Stress tolerance especially with tight deadlines and financial pressures
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Flexibility and creativity
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Professional appearance
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong interpersonal skills
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bilingual (Spanish/English) a plus.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Work Experience
</p>
<ul><li>2+ years in media/advertising sales is preferred, but not required
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Education
</p>
<ul><li>High school diploma, college degree preferred
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Certifications
</p>
<ul><li>None required<br>
</li></ul>
