<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>
Appirio, a Wipro Company helps customers create next-generation Worker and Customer Experiences using the latest cloud technologies. Our free-thinking consultants deliver unmatched customer value by creating actionable strategies, delivering results quickly, and helping organizations adapt to a new experience-driven era. <p><b>
Brief Summary of Role</b></p>
<p>
Senior Project Managers will manage one or more projects for our customers providing day-to-day leadership to analyze their requirements, develop designs, and implement cloud-based solutions based on the Google Cloud Platform.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Be enthusiastic, professional, and confident while maintaining a focus on customer success</li>
<li>
Owns the entire project lifecycle from initiation through closure</li>
<li>
Monitors and manages project financials, scope, and schedule to ensure project stays on track for assigned client projects and meets Appirio financial targets.</li>
<li>
Drives implementations of high-quality cloud solutions</li>
<li>
Acts as primary day-to-day point of contact for the client and the operational aspects of the project. Objective focused on building strong customer relationships</li>
<li>
Facilitates or co-leads with customer project manager regular status meetings with project team, sponsors, and all stakeholders</li>
<li>
Generates and distributes timely status reports, time usage reports, and expense reports weekly</li>
<li>
Manages risks and issue tracking throughout the project.</li>
<li>
Adheres to the Appirio Way Methodology and possesses the ability to assess risks in process, people, or technology and adjusts project plans to address accordingly.</li>
<li>
Implements and adheres to a change management plan</li>
<li>
Assists consultant team members in the delivery of client presentations during requirements, design, pilot and user acceptance testing sessions. Reviews deliverables prepared by the team before sharing with the client.</li>
<li>
Contributes in at least one area of domain expertise (in addition to project management) such as business analysis, Agile development practices, GCP offerings, or PSA.</li>
<li>
Understands the Cloud Computing market. Can articulate the core benefits of Cloud Computing solutions, and understands basic implementation methodologies associated with Cloud solutions.</li>
<li>
Provides pre sales support such as change orders for your current engagements.</li>
<li>
Provides mentoring, guidance and general care and feeding to project team members, including offshore team members.</li>
<li>
Achieves published company utilization targets.</li>
<li>
Manages day-to-day client interaction, sets and manages client expectations, develops lasting relationships with client personnel that foster client ties.</li>
<li>
Communicates effectively with clients, including c-level, to identify needs and evaluate alternative business solutions.</li>
<li>
Contributes domain expertise (in addition to project management) in multiple areas such as business analysis, vertical or horizontal domain expertise, Agile development practices, Google cloud offerings or Google apps.</li>
<li>
Facilitates client strategy workshops and requirements sessions and helps lead the development / refinement of client business and IT strategies. Assesses architectural approaches and develops high level designs for the implementation of those strategies.</li>
</ul>
