<h2>Implementation Specialist II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Job Purpose:</b></div>
<div>
The Implementation Specialist II is responsible for the implementation of new orders for ABILITY products and services and assist others as they learn new products.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Guided by a mission to help, ABILITY, an Inovalon company, is a leading information technology company helping healthcare providers and payers simplify administrative and clinical complexity by enabling data-driven improvements in healthcare.</div>
<div>
Through specialized, easy-to-use applications and data analytics that work together, customers of all types and sizes across the continuum of care – rely on ABILITY to help optimize reimbursement, care quality and staffing.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
Our employee philosophy is simple: We hire smart, talented people and trust them to do their thing. As part of ABILITY, you’ll grow with a company that wants you to succeed. To support you, we provide the teams, the latitude, the support and the culture so you can try new things, broaden your experience, and grow and succeed with us. Join our team and help make ABILITY a great place to do great work.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Work with customers to lead the implementation process of all supported ABILITY products</li>
<li>
Documents customer requirements and configures applications to meet customer business processes</li>
<li>
Must be able to perform complex implementations with minimal supervision (Multiple Product Project)</li>
<li>
Answer inbound customer requests via telephone, email or any additional method of inbound communication</li>
<li>
Improve timelines on open projects within specified guidelines based on team averages</li>
<li>
Document all activities per defined process and procedures</li>
<li>
Resolve open orders within specified guidelines</li>
<li>
Identify beneficial product opportunities for existing customers and communicate to sales team via workflow process</li>
<li>
Lead functional testing of product releases</li>
<li>
Elevate issues following defined escalation procedures in a timely manner ensuring that all communications to customer and internal departments are completed</li>
<li>
Must be able to communicate valuable feedback and ideas to manager, SME or point of escalation</li>
<li>
Must be able to organize and manage workload efficiently
</li>
<li>Provide assistance to team members as needed</li>
<li>
Must be able to communicate valuable product and process feedback to manager, SME or point of escalation</li>
<li>
Place outbound calls to coordinate/schedule implementations</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain compliance with Inovalon’s policies, procedures and mission statement;</li>
<li>
Adhere to all confidentiality and HIPAA requirements as outlined within Inovalon’s Operating Policies and Procedures in all ways and at all times with respect to any aspect of the data handled or services rendered in the undertaking of the position;</li>
<li>
Fulfill those responsibilities and/or duties that may be reasonably provided by Inovalon for the purpose of achieving operational and financial success of the Company;</li>
<li>
Uphold responsibilities relative to the separation of duties for applicable processes and procedures within your job function; and</li>
<li>
We reserve the right to change this job description from time to time as business needs dictate and will provide notice of</li>
