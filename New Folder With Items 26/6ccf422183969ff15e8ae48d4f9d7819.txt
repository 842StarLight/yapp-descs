<h2>Junior Marketing Associate - Winter Seasonal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b><br>
Winter Seasonal - We are looking for a Junior Marketing Associate to assist with the planning and execution of marketing activities. The ideal candidate will have outstanding organizational, communication and time management skills. Having prior experience with a wide range of marketing functions, including communications, advertising, branding, digital marketing, and social media is a plus.</p>
<p>
You will support the marketing department in their daily activities, such as assist with daily snow reporting and facilitating the duties of the Senior Marketing Manager and marketing department. This is a full time position from November - April.</p>
<div>
<b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Support marketing operations by compiling, formatting, and reporting information and materials</li>
<li>Write and announce short snow report for a variety of ski reporting services</li>
<li>Communicate regularly with Mountain Operations, Information Desk, Marketing and Events, in order to compile, update and disseminate information on mountain conditions</li>
<li>Update multiple websites, including Stratton.com as the day’s conditions unfold</li>
<li>Help maintain and update internal communication of day’s conditions</li>
<li>Monitor mountain weather conditions for changes and follow up with reporting services</li>
<li>Assist other departments as needed</li>
<li>Special projects as assigned (film and/or written)</li>
<li>Smile, have fun, ski or snowboard on a daily basis</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
QUALIFICATIONS, KNOWLEDGE, SKILL AND ABILITY REQUIREMENTS</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills in both public speaking and writing. A writing sample may be required as a part of the interview process</li>
<li>Must be a creative, well organized team player with a high degree of energy</li>
<li>Basic web experience, or desire to learn (coding not required)</li>
<li>Must be able to multitask and work well under pressure</li>
<li>Must be able to work outside in all weather conditions</li>
<li><b>Advanced level skier/snowboarder</b></li>
<li>Must be able to work efficiently to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Must work well independently and as a member of a team</li>
<li>Experience with video editing software is preferred but not required</li>
<li>Be a morning person!</li>
</ul>
</div>
