<h2>Director, Product Management – Media Asset Management & BackOffice</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Want to change and personalize the complete video viewing experience? Want to help unlock value from video viewing and streaming? We are seeking an outstanding product manager to own and lead two products at SeaChange - Media Asset Management and Video Back Office.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
As the Director of Product Management you will be responsible for product strategy, roadmap, requirements, go-to-market, and achieving revenue targets for your products. You should have excellent business and communication skills, are comfortable with Agile and cloud-native development, and are obsessed with analyzing and monetizing consumer video consumption. You will work within a highly skilled cross-functional team with continuously software releases</p>
<p>
In this role you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Own the Media Asset Management product, which is an automated, rules, based, workflow engine managing the video content preparation for multi-screen streaming.</li>
<li>
Own the Video Back-Office product, which is an open platform that automates the control plane and user experience for multiscreen streaming.</li>
<li>
Develop product business justifications and plans, including financial and market analysis.</li>
<li>
Drive the launch calendar, producing detailed launch plans and controlling release scope.</li>
<li>
Prioritize and manage product backlog, mapping desired features to appropriate release schedules.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with peer product managers to leverage and integrate data from multiple applications for analytics and monetization.</li>
<li>
Work closely with architects and engineering on feature development and implementation.</li>
<li>
Participate in customer meetings and visits, providing product overviews and roadmaps and ensuring Sales has the necessary resources to drive revenue.</li>
<li>
Report on product revenue and budgets to achieve desired financial targets. Be accountable for product line gross margin and deliverables against plan.</li>
<li>
Create positioning and messaging in conjunction with Marketing.</li>
<li>
Option to work remotely 1-2 days a week.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years experience in Product Management or Software Development with a strong business acumen</li>
<li>
Experience with PayTV and OTT video systems</li>
<li>
Experience with cloud-native technologies</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills, verbal and written</li>
<li>
Strong customer engagement, communication, presentation</li>
<li>
Knowledge of modern software development practices</li>
<li>
BA/BS degree in Computer Science or Business domain</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience Asset Management and BackOffice</li>
<li>
Experience with market segmentation, product positioning, and competitive strategies</li>
<li>
Experience in market sizing, product value chain assessment, and software pricing</li>
<li>
Experience gathering market data to analytically determine business priorities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
About SeaChange</b></p>
<p>
SeaChange has pioneered solutions to help video providers across the globe manage and monetize their content. We are passionate about video, and are excited about the opportunities for innovation in advanced video services, especially in viewer experience personalization. By leveraging SeaChange solutions to drive increasingly personalized experiences to viewers, our customers can gain and retain viewers, reduce churn, and improve the monetization potential of their video business.</p>
<p>
As the video industry rapidly evolves to meet the “anytime, anywhere” demands of today’s viewers, SeaChange’s comprehensive content, business, advertising, and experience management solutions provide a mature, cloud-enabled, any-network platform of scalable core capabilities that video service providers, broadcasters, content owners and brand advertisers need to create the personalized, individual experiences that drive viewer engagement and monetization.</p>
