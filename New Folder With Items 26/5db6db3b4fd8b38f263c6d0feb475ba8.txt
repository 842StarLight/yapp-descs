<h2>Render Artist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Everlast Productions is a provider of production equipment and engineering solutions. With a vast inventory of equipment, Everlast Productions supplies cutting edge technology to a broad spectrum of clients in the corporate, TV, live events and broadcast markets. Clients depend on Everlast's extensive experience in Audio, Video, Lighting, LED, Cameras, Rigging and Custom Scenic more.<br><b>Rendering Artist </b></p>
<p><b>Description: </b></p>
<p>The Rendering Artist reports directly to the Creative Director. The Rendering Artist is responsible for working with the Creative Director to develop digital design visualizations for various events and functions. The Rendering Artist may at times work directly with the sales representative or project manager on specific jobs as designated by the Creative Director.</p>
<p><b>Main Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Creating a fully finished render of various environments that are in line with the design vision of the Creative Director.</li>
<li>Make edits to previously completed renderings.</li>
<li>Maintaining well organized database of renderings and other related files.</li>
<li>Editing and manipulating artwork.Maintaining an up to date work-log.</li>
<li>Maintaining a clean work environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Stay up to date with industry standards and new developments</li>
<li>Answering the phone and directing calls as needed.</li>
<li>Helping in preparations for client presentations.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by the Creative Director.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skill Set: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in 3D modeling software (i.e. Maya, 3D Max).</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite.</li>
<li>Strong sense of composition, color and design.</li>
<li>Able to complete projects in a timely fashion.</li>
<li>Able to work well under pressure under tight deadlines at times.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
