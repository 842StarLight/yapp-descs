<h2>Senior Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>The role:</b><br>
<br>
As the Senior Account Manager, you will be a member of our Client Services team &amp; work closely with our advertising clients, partners and the sales team, as well as the team in New York.<br>
<br>
On a daily basis, you'll be in contact with assigned clients to ensure smooth campaign delivery and performance. You’ll be creating and managing LoopMe's campaigns, reporting on the deal performance and analysing data. You will work closely with the other disciplines in delivering and exceeding client expectations. Campaigns are planned and managed using our proprietary platform, so candidates must be comfortable learning new software.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate loves all things mobile, programmatic, has strong analytical skills, is an eager learner, has a high attention for detail, along with good communication and client-facing skills.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and grow client accounts on programmatic and IO/ managed service</li>
<li>
Manage all aspect of the campaign/activation project to ensure full delivery of campaigns towards their KPIs (planning, monitoring and optimisation)</li>
<li>
Create and manage LoopMe’s PMPs across mobile video and rich media suited to client KPIs</li>
<li>
Driving incremental spend in-flight leveraging campaign performance, data/analytics and premium service and communication</li>
<li>
Oversee PMP daily optimizations and liaise with supply team to ensure/maximise spend</li>
<li>
Work cross-functionally with Product, Supply, Data Science and Creative teams</li>
<li>
Provide first-level support, troubleshooting underperforming or under-delivering campaigns</li>
<li>
Effective communication to all stakeholders (internal &amp; external), written and verbal</li>
<li>
Reporting and analysis including post-campaign analysis &amp; reports to maximize client results</li>
<li>
Liaise with 3rd party providers (e.g DCM, IRI, 9D, MOAT, IAS, etc.) to achieve desired campaign goals</li>
</ul>
<b>
Required skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience in campaign activation</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the Programmatic ecosystem e.g. DSP, SSP platforms, ad servers, ad tags etc.</li>
<li>
Experience in programmatic deal troubleshooting</li>
<li>
Exceptional attention to detail and organisation skills</li>
<li>
Superior verbal and written communication and customer service skills</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task and efficiently manage time and priorities</li>
<li>
A passion for mobile advertising as a whole, with knowledge of emerging trends</li>
<li>
Excellent data, analytic and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate complex concepts clearly<br>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<b>About LoopMe</b>
</div>
