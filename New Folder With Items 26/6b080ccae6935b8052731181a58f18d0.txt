<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details
</b></h2>
<p>Title: Manager, Shopper Research
</p>
<p>Division: Consulting - Retail
</p>
<p>Location: Boston, Massachusetts
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are seeking a dynamic shopper research leader for our syndicated insights portfolio. Reporting to the EVP of Retail Insights &amp; Operations, you will be responsible for managing the research strategy and operations for our evolving shopper survey assets. As Manager, Shopper Research Management, you have a deep understanding of research methodologies and design within the retail, supplier, and shopper environment. You have your pulse on where shopping preferences and behaviors are going and the knowledge that our retail and supplier clients need to succeed.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Kantar is a specialist growth consultancy. With over 1,000 analysts, thought leaders, software developers and expert consultants we help clients develop and execute brand, marketing, retail, sales and shopper strategies to deliver growth. Kantar owns market leading assets </b><b>including PoweRanking, GrowthFinder, </b><b>Global </b><b>Monitor, RetaiI IQ, RichMix, </b><b>XTEL and Marketing, Insights and Purpose 2020. We track 1200 retailers globally, have purchase data on over 200 million shoppers and forecast social, cultural and consumer trends across the world. </b><b>Join us and you’ll be part of 30,000 colleagues and 12 world leading research, data and insight brands at Kantar.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>KEY CAPABILITIES
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Success in this role will see you:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Owning and evolving our syndicated shopper research approaches, assets, and outputs
</li>
<li>Working collaboratively with internal partners to ensure research agenda and client business issues are translated into specific research questions that support the organization’s thought leadership content agenda and commercial strategy
</li>
<li>Working with project director to oversee fielding and data processing
</li>
<li>Leveraging relevant IT tools as required to manage data upload processes, data QA and processing
</li>
<li>Tracking broader industry developments in survey approaches and methods of analyses
</li>
<li>Serving as the insights team’s conduit to all syndicated shopper survey assets and a source of ad hoc survey support
</li>
<li>Owning data accuracy and accountability
</li>
<li>Managing internal and client-facing training needs, including serving as research “help desk” for clients who access our research data database and dashboard tools
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>KEY COMPETENCIES
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will be a seasoned shopper research expert with the following capabilities.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Questionnaire design and development expertise, translating insights needs into research instruments
</li>
<li>Familiarity with data analysis, data reporting, and cross-tabulation software such as mTab, Q, AskiaVista, MarketSight or similar
</li>
<li>Experience with survey programming platforms such as Confirmit, SPSS, Dimensions, or Qualtrics helpful
</li>
<li>Retail and/or CPG supplier industry experience in an insights or research role, on the client or research supplier side.
</li>
<li>Passion for understanding and tracking evolving shopper preferences and attitudes with relevance to retailers, suppliers and brands.
</li>
<li>Ability to develop and maintain productive relationships with internal stakeholders (e.g., insights, product and project management teams, broader data assets) and clients (e.g., suppliers, brands and retailers).
</li>
<li>An educator and self-proclaimed research nerd with a passion for knowledge-sharing and thought leadership advancement
</li>
</ul>
