<div>Global Head MAP Business Development Field Effectiveness, AWS Migrations Acceleration Program<br>
Would you like to help customers achieve business results by migrating to AWS? Would you like to develop and lead worldwide business development capability for large migrations, and manage a programmatic approach and mechanisms to help hundreds of companies retire their technical debt? Would you like to work closely with and lead migration v-teams that include sales/account leaders, Professional Services teams, Partners, Training, Business Development, Marketing, Legal, and Services teams?<br>
<br>
As global business development and field effectiveness leader for migrations, you will have an exciting opportunity to lead the world-wide approach and teams that engage with accounts and customer executives through the Migration Acceleration Program on a world-wide basis. You will apply the lessons learned from enterprises migrating at scale to AWS, and build/evolve our customer engagement and pre-sales approaches to help customers initiate large scale migrations on AWS. You will work backwards from the needs of the customers and the field teams to develop market leading mechanisms to elevate our capability worldwide. You will lead a matrixed team of migrations business development SMEs, and enable them with tools and mechanisms to be successful, including providing in-field business development support for complex and high value opportunities. You will own acquisition goal(s) for new migrations, including funnel development, and opportunity management. You must be comfortable leading v-teams across functions globally that includes business development teams, solutions architecture, marketing, partners, professional services, training, sales enablement, sales operations, and other related teams.<br>
<br>
The right candidate will have experience defining, selling, and delivering large scale transformation programs for enterprise customers. This includes demonstrated business development and selling experience, and experience in developing programmatic approaches to build and scale programs, strong program and project management skills, a customer-obsessed and collaborative approach, field engagement and enablement experience, strong data and metrics bias, a good understanding of cloud solutions, and a passion for helping customers transform using cloud technologies.<br>
<br>
Role &amp; Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Working backwards from customer needs, develop and evolve AWS migrations value proposition and engagement mechanisms.</li>
<li>Lead combination of direct and cross-functional virtual teams to help customers initiate their migrations journey to retire legacy debt and modernize their IT estate.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to build compelling proposals to help customers achieve business value from migrations, provide the path forward for customers to build migration plans and business cases, and ensure clarity on AWS’s value proposition(s) for the customer as they move their workloads to AWS.</li>
<li>Develop global mechanisms to programmatically deliver successful migration proposals to customers leveraging AWS field teams, technology and consulting partner channels, and self-service. GTM elements may include consultative workshops, reference materials, partner engagements, linkages to other Amazon programs and solutions, customer proposal and offer development, internal and external collateral, POCs, and success measures, etc.</li>
<li>Develop qualification and training approach to build out the community of resources to successfully win these engagements in each region, and ensure customer business outcomes are realized.</li>
<li>Build dashboards and reporting mechanisms to track progress and identify improvement opportunities. Periodically present progress to executive leadership.</li>
<li>Conduct customer briefings, present at customer events, further AWS thought leadership in the IT transformation domain</li>
</ul>
