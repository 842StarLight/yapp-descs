<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary</b></h2>
<p>Delivering compelling applications with analytics at their core has never been more crucial—or more complex. Logi is the only developer-grade analytics platform focused exclusively on embedding analytics in commercial and enterprise applications. Logi leverages your existing tech stack and supports unlimited customization and white-labeling, so you can quickly build a completely unique analytics experience.</p>
<p>Over 1,900 applications have trusted the Logi platform to deliver sophisticated analytics capabilities and power their businesses. The company is headquartered in McLean, Virginia, with offices in Ireland and England. Learn more at LogiAnalytics.com</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>As one of our Technical Account Managers (TAM), you will be responsible for building and maintaining post-sales relationships with Logi Analytics’ largest and most complex customers, focusing both on pro-active services and reactive support. You will serve as a trusted advisor to ensure successful implementations, that the assigned customers achieve targeted business benefits from their investment in Logi Analytics, and that the customers serve as a positive reference and continue their implementation of Logi Analytics’ software. Your technical abilities will be critical in identifying solutions, troubleshooting issues, and answering technical questions as they arise. TAMs are the customer’s primary liaison with Logi Analytics and are accountable for identifying and directly addressing business and technical issues. They must marshal resources across the Logi Analytics organization, coordinating interaction with the various Logi Analytics’ departments through timely and effective management of the case resolution process and will be the primary point of contact for escalations. The TAM will develop and maintain a working knowledge of the technical architecture and business requirements of the assigned customers. By maintaining a long-term relationship with their customers, a TAM gains an understanding of each customer’s overall business, goals, and technical requirements and will become a trusted resource to address customer requirements. This position requires a strong technical background, excellent client and project management skills, proven ability to understand various technical workflows when needed. Successful candidates must be action oriented and able to communicate clearly and effectively to both technical and business audiences at multiple levels.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Builds and grows relationships with both technical staff and senior management from the customer team.</li>
<li>Drives and tracks customer escalations, including partnering with the Product Management, Sustaining Engineering, and Professional Services teams.</li>
<li>Ensures that all cases opened for their accounts are being actively worked and progressing towards an expeditious resolution.</li>
<li>Diligently does the on boarding of new accounts</li>
<li>Partners closely with product management to ensure that customer requirements are logged and appropriately prioritized and also ensure that future product direction aligns with customer road map.</li>
<li>Actively contributes and leverages standards and best practices, staying current on both the technology and product fronts.</li>
<li>Contributes to the knowledgebase on lessons learned and unique issues experienced for any of their accounts.</li>
<li>Develops and mentors other TAMs to help improve their technical knowledge, project management skills, customer management skills, etc.</li>
<li>Travel may be needed up to 30 percent of the time.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Customer Relationship Management:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage all post-sales activity for customers through strong relationship-building, product knowledge, planning and execution</li>
<li>Maximizes the value of the customer's investment in Logi Analytics’ products and services.</li>
<li>Acts as single point of contact for all support requirements as they pertain to the assigned customers' environment</li>
<li>Ensures effective ownership, communication, coordination, and facilitation of support service activities between the customer and support teams, product management and account teams</li>
<li>Increase customer retention by conducting regular check-in calls for tactical items, and perform quarterly health checks for strategic reviews</li>
<li>Maintain a deep understanding of the product and speak with customers about the most relevant features/functionality for their specific business needs</li>
<li>Direct the delivery of customer technical services to achieve high customer satisfaction and trust</li>
<li>Track accounts to identify churn risk and work proactively to eliminate that risk</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Project Management:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate cross-functional Logi Analytics project teams, which could consist of professional services consultants, product management, engineers, and support staff</li>
<li>Develop and manage a strategic development plan for your customers, including identification of opportunities for growth</li>
<li>Escalate issues as necessary for confirmation action plan, resolution or other options as needed</li>
<li>Manage resources to achieve the most effective method of problem resolution by directing internal technical services engineers</li>
<li>Track and document status and time spent on customers to ensure appropriate per client resource spend</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Customer Advocacy:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Function as the voice of the customer and as the customer advocate, provide internal feedback on how we can better serve our business and enterprise customers</li>
<li>Work closely with product management and engineering on identification and tracking of new feature requests, product enhancement requests and bugs</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>General:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish and maintain working relationships with internal teams, including professional services consultants, product management, engineers, and support staff</li>
<li>Maintain current functional and technical knowledge of the entire Logi Analytics product line</li>
<li>Manage and drive competing requests across simultaneous client engagements</li>
<li>Provide clear and constructive product feedback to product management based on customer requirements</li>
<li>Help to document best practices in developing and deploying Logi Analytics solutions</li>
<li>Participate in internal projects that help scale the group, document and implement best practices, and improving our methodology and quality of implementations</li>
</ul>
