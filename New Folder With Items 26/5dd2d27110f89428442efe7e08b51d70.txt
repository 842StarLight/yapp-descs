<p></p>
<p>Purpose and Objective: SAP America, Inc. seeks a Business Processes Consultant at our Tempe, AZ office location (and various client sites nationwide) responsible for application and process specific solution consulting for a specific application and/or process area: creation of business blueprint, implementation based on a blueprint, creation of test cases, test scheduling and execution, key user training, go live support, and post go live support.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Expectations and Tasks: Provide support for escalated projects. Perform feasibility studies / solution reviews. Support pre-sales activities. Take over responsibility from time to time as a sub project team leader. Perform detailed planning of process implementation. Define detailed blueprint for development requirements. Responsible for industry and corporate process implementation across all related applications (SAP/non-SAP, OnPremise and Cloud). Develop and assure quality of process models. Apply basic knowledge of modeling standards and tools. Drive deployment of SAP Model Companies for specific area of business. Telecommuting available. 80% travel required to various client sites nationwide.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Education and Qualifications/Skills and Competencies: Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business Administration, or a related field of study and 5 years of experience required. Alternatively, the company will accept a Master’s degree and 3 years of experience.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Work Experience: Experience must involve 3 years with the following: Product Development and Portfolio and Project Management business processes; SAP Research and Development (R&amp;D); engineering portfolio solutions, including SAP Product Lifecycle Management, SAP Enterprise Portfolio and Project Management, SAP Commercial Project Management and SAP Product Lifecycle Costing; SAP software configurations; pre-sales activities for software licenses and services; software demonstrations; proof of concepts; implementation estimation; Model Company program and approach; software implementation methodologies such as SAP Activate and Agile. Will accept pre-baccalaureate experience. Telecommuting available. 80% travel required to various client sites nationwide.</p>
