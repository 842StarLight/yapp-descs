<p><b>About L'Oréal: </b>Founded by a chemist in 1907, L’Oréal leads the world of beauty with 35 global renowned brands such as Maybelline, Lancôme, Ralph Lauren, Urban Decay, Redken, Skinceuticals, Essie, Kiehl’s, and Clarisonic. With $26 billion (Euros) in annual sales and present in 140 countries, L’Oréal is a technologically advanced company, which manufactures and supplies over 4.5 billion units annually to the world. <b>L’Oréal Operations Americas </b>is an innovative, challenging, dynamic and diverse environment with the opportunity for cross-functional careers – from conception to consumer – in <b>Manufacturing, Supply Chain and Operational Package Development.</b> We have 45+ manufacturing plants and more than 200+ distribution centers worldwide; L’Oréal Operations Americas is committed to continuous improvement, people development, safety, quality and sustainability. An <b>MBA Supply Chain internship</b> in L’Oréal Operations gives you the opportunity to play an active part in L’Oréal’s entrepreneurial business, day in and day out as your steer your own project and deliverables over the course of eleven dynamic weeks.</p>
