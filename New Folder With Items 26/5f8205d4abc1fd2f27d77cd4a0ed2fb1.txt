<h2>Video Investor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The candidate will have individual buying responsibilities in stewardship of media buys across media outlets, and accounts. Responsibilities also include participation in campaign management under direction of account manager.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Main Duties and Responsibilities
</b><br>
Media schedule development – 30%
</p>
<ul>
<li>Gather rates, construct media buys in Core buying system, and send schedules
</li>
<li>Effectively negotiate &amp; steward buys across multiple accounts
</li>
<li>Serve as market expert, providing insight to the marketplace conditions and opportunities in specific markets or media outlets
</li>
<li>Work closely with sales representatives to promote advantageous relationships
</li>
</ul>
<p>Media schedule management – 35%
</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage market budgets and deliverables (GRPs, impressions, response data)
</li>
<li>React quickly to campaign results, and implement required optimizations as directed
</li>
</ul>
<p>Account management – 20%
</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist account manager in regard to maintaining budget and clearance spreadsheets and other campaign data
</li>
<li>Participate in campaign results meetings/calls with client and strategy teams
</li>
</ul>
<p>Administrative – 15%
</p>
<ul>
<li>Train and oversee Assistant Video Investor regarding media fundamentals, policies/procedures, and computer systems
</li>
<li>Represent Horizon Media and its clients in a professional manner at media industry events and presentations
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities
</b><br>
Responsible for overseeing a shared Assistant Video Investor as direct report
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge and Skills Required
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience working in fast-paced advertising/marketing environment
</li>
<li>2-years experience related to active buying
</li>
<li>Buy-management experience, analyzing all aspects of metrics
</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office programs
</li>
<li>Experience with a media-buying computer program
</li>
<li>Effective collaborator and team-player
</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time-management skills
</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Certificates, licenses and registrations
</b><br>
n/a
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Activity and Work Environment
</b><br>
A fast-paced and dynamic workplace with periods of high stress</p>
