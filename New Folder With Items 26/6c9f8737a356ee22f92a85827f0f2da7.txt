<h2>Global Marketing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description</p>
<p>Summary of duties and responsibilities: <b>Experience in Software Industry Preferred.</b></p>
<p>BST Global has a unique opportunity available for an inspiring and experienced leader to take the reins of all aspects and facets of the marketing strategy and brand management for a leading international software company.</p>
<p>BST Global values the importance of a clear and thoughtful marketing and communications strategy and the power it holds to propel the BST Global brand worldwide. BST Global is looking for an experienced professional that is passionate about leveraging the latest in marketing tactics to tell our story – what we believe, our culture, and what we are passionate about – to present and future prospects, clients, and employees.</p>
<p>The desired leader should have extensive experience and success in taking B2B, SAAS based products from initial conception to market introduction.</p>
<p>The Director of Global Marketing will lead the creation, management, and evaluation of global marketing campaigns and content on BST Global-owned channels, as well as external mediums. The Director of Global Marketing is responsible for developing comprehensive marketing programs that support strategic brand objectives and leading the marketing team and external creative agencies in executing said programs in order to drive awareness, engagement, and brand relevance on multiple platforms.</p>
<p>This position will be responsible for:</p>
<ul><li>Overseeing the design, development, and implementation of a global marketing and branding strategy and effective marketing campaigns for all aspects and facets of the company  * Support strategic business objectives by working with Senior Leadership to develop effective long-term marketing strategies and integrated annual and quarterly plans for marketing activities, programs, and channels, including, but not limited to: o BST Global website o Social Media o Blogging o Marketing Communications o Digital Advertising o Search Engine Optimization o Email Marketing o Conferences o Micro sites and banner advertisements o Digital events  * Lead Marketing Team in the successful execution of all aspects of marketing campaigns  * Oversee development and management of annual marketing budget, reporting to Senior Leadership on budget performance.</li></ul>
<p>SALES AND MARKETING – DIRECTOR OF GLOBAL MARKETING</p>
<ul><li>Work with Senior Leadership to develop creative vision for annual and quarterly marketing initiatives  * Work with internal stakeholders to ensure brand and messaging consistency across all customer touch points  * Work with internal Marketing Team to successfully execute all aspects of digital marketing campaigns  * Set benchmarks for marketing performance, output, operational efficiencies, and campaign optimization  * Advise senior leadership on corporate communications initiatives, aiding in the shaping of messaging and development of executive content  * Analyze and communicate performance against benchmarks for customer acquisition and marketing activities to internal stakeholders  * Support Marketing team through extensive knowledge of B2B marketing methodologies  * Stay current and informed on all new product campaigns, understanding their objectives and relevance, communicating to Marketing teams, and ensuring all involved know the roles they play in making campaigns successful  * Work closely with IT department to oversee implementation and maintenance of marketing technology stack, including but not limited to, CRM database, email marketing software, marketing automation software, and social media management solutions</li></ul>
<p>The successful candidate will be a leader who has:</p>
<ul><li>An entrepreneur who has proven experience being innovative, creative and thinking out of the box  * A proven track record in leading all things marketing and executing on strategy within the B2B, SAAS arena  * Proven people management, team, and leadership skills  * Extensive experience and command in marketing campaign management, content creation, social media marketing, search engine optimization, search engine marketing, online advertising, mobile, email marketing, website management, and blogging  * Strong integrated marketing communications and management skills including agency relationship management  * Passion for and experience with brand management and visual identity standardization  * Proven history of managing marketing budgets and internal processes with success in illustrating return on investment for marketing budgets  * Experience working with CRM databases (Microsoft Dynamics preferred)  * Experience working with marketing automation software solutions (Marketo a plus)  * Collaborative and creative approach with a distinctly positive attitude  * Strong interpersonal skills and teamwork commitment  * Excellent written and verbal communication skills  * Strong work ethic</li></ul>
<p>SALES AND MARKETING – DIRECTOR OF GLOBAL MARKETING</p>
<p>Education, prior work experience:  * 10+ years of experience in marketing and brand management in the B2B space including campaign management, digital marketing, marketing automation, website management, content marketing, and social media management  * Proven experience and success in taking B2B, SAAS products to market  * BA/BS degree in Communications, Journalism, Marketing or related field</p>
<p>Specialized License, Certifications:  * None</p>
<p>Classification: Exempt</p>
<p>Work Environment: This job operates in a professional office environment. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as desktop and laptop computers, phones, photocopiers, filing cabinets and fax machines.</p>
<p>Physical Demands: While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to engage in conversations over the phone, person-to-person and via email. The employee will frequently sit; stand; walk; and operate a computer and/or other standard office equipment.</p>
<p>About: BST Global is the leading provider of cloud-based Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and business management software and service solutions for architecture, engineering, and consultancies globally. Today, over 100,000 end users across six continents and 65 countries rely on BST Global’s software solutions to manage their projects, resources, finances and client relationships.</p>
<p>Founded in 1971, the privately held BST Global is headquartered in Tampa, Florida, USA and is a global organization with a presence in the Americas, Europe, Africa, Asia and Australia. With over forty-eight years of experience, BST Global is the industry leader and is strategically positioned for growth. Our employees enjoy a generous benefits package including paid time off, medical, dental, disability, life insurance, and 401k matching.</p>
