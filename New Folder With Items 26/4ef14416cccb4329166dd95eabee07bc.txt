<h2>Production Scheduler</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>PRODUCTION SCHEDULER</p>
<p></p>
<p>Basic Function:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Prepares the master production schedule and works with the production supervisors and Operations Manager to ensure schedule is met. Creates work orders and verifies SAP is set up properly for the production run. Works with project management group to schedule trial runs while maintaining inventory to meet customer requirements. Ensures work orders are closed in a timely manner and clears COGI.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>A strong business acumen, basic accounting skills and the ability to reason through ever changing scenarios are necessities for this position. Taking the initiative, strong math skills, and the ability to anticipate problems before they happen are also essential for success in this position. Actual SAP Experience in Buying &amp; Production Planning. Knowledge and experience in working with Excel reports from SAP, measuring delivery performance of suppliers, and analyzing data using KPI metrics.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p>Job Duties:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Creates work orders based on requirements in SAP</li>
<li>Schedules work orders</li>
<li>Issues work orders to the production department</li>
<li>Follow up with the production to ensure work orders are completed as scheduled and reschedules work orders as required due to downtime/ material discrepancies</li>
<li>Retrieves work order from production and review for accuracy</li>
<li>Check inventory for discrepancies and correct as required</li>
<li>Clear COGI once work order is returned</li>
<li>Close all work orders</li>
<li>Create MRP parameters so material is planned to ensure raw material and finished goods are available as required</li>
<li>Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities.</li>
<li>Accomplishes production planning and organization mission by completing related results as needed.</li>
<br>
</ul>
