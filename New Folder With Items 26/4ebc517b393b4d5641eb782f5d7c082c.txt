<h2>Administrative Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>CMSS’ Administrative Coordinator serves as our morning receptionist and administrative assistant to senior staff, interacting with a diverse group of individuals and organizations to create a welcoming and respectful environment and promote CMSS activities.</p>
<p>Duties:</p>
<p>· Promptly and reliably open the school at 7:45AM and greet Prelude Preschool families</p>
<p>· Answer incoming calls, assist with questions about any and all organizational information</p>
<p>· Provide general administrative and clerical support including data entry, mailing, scanning, faxing and copying and maintaining organizational and staff calendars including Executive Director calendar</p>
<p>· Support the enrollment team by demonstrating a thorough understanding and knowledge of lessons, classes, billing and registration to efficiently assist customers</p>
<p>· Greet numerous people in and out of the building while attending the front desk, ensuring our diverse clients, staff and visitors feel welcome and comfortable in our space</p>
<p>· Take and log tuition payments from students and parents and deliver the information to Director of Enrollment</p>
<p>· Other tasks as assigned</p>
<p>· Assist with e-newsletters, brochures, fliers and marketing materials as directed</p>
<p>· Act in a marketing and sales capacity with prospective and current students</p>
<p>· Assist Director of Enrollment and Executive Director with CMSS’ public relations needs and communications including press releases, advertising, and media coverage</p>
<p>· Other tasks as assigned</p>
<p>Required Qualifications:</p>
<p>Punctual and reliable for early morning arrival time<br>Strong administrative skills<br>Excellent written and verbal communication skills<br>Ability to multi-task and prioritize in a busy environment<br>Ability to follow a project through from start to finish with multiple interruptions<br>Highly skilled in interpersonal communication (greeting families upon arrival and departure, problem solving)<br>Comfortable and conversant with Microsoft Word, Excel, data entry, trouble shooting, Google platform<br>Able to quickly master new technology such as registration software<br>Curious and self-directed learner</p>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:<br>Bilingual (Spanish)<br>Graphic design skills, Photoshop/Indesign<br>Experience with social media, press releases, email marketing software</p>

We servemore than 700 students annually at our 127 State Street location, and anadditional 1,000 students each week in our community partnership programs. CMSS is an Equal OpportunityEmployer and actively seeks a diverse staff that reflects the communities thatwe serve.</p>
