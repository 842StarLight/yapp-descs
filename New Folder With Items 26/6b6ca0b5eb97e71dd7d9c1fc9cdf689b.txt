<h2>Yard Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b><b><i>:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan, organize, and delegate the responsibilities for the successful operation of all the yard operations.</li>
<li>
Supervise the shipping and receiving of equipment through assigned personnel.</li>
<li>
Supervise equipment inventory control and responsible for performing and reconciling weekly inventory audits.</li>
<li>
House number assignments to incoming machines and attachments.</li>
<li>
Assist Sales and Rental Coordinators when necessary for customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Supervise staging of new, rental, and used machines for shipping.</li>
<li>
Supervise organization, display, and overall appearance of new, rental, and used equipment yards.</li>
<li>
Assess inventory condition and coordinate cleanup and repairs.</li>
<li>
Manage and organize machine and attachment inventory.</li>
<li>
Select, train, and develop employees consistent with department and company needs.</li>
<li>
Provide regular and timely feedback that recognizes performance, motivates employees, and promotes self-development.</li>
<li>
Maintain accurate machine photo and customer damage records.</li>
<li>
Coordinate all activities with related departments and divisions.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Review and approve employee time.</li>
<li>
Enable Cashman to achieve its objective of <i>Safely Home. Everyone. Every Day.</i>™ by promoting a positive safety culture and adhering to all company safety policies, procedures and practices.</li>
<li>
Provide regular and timely feedback that recognizes performance, motivate employees and promote self-development.</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively, and actively seek and provide feedback.</li>
<li>
Select, train, and develop employees consistent with department/division goals.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Working knowledge of CAT equipment and components through mechanic experience and/or technical training.</li>
<li>
Certified and able to operate forklift.</li>
<li>
Ability to operate construction machinery.</li>
<li>
Proficient computer technology skills, including ability to utilize company software.</li>
<li>
Achieve company goals of customer satisfaction, market dominance, and profitability through leadership qualities demonstrated, timely employee feedback, and development of staff.</li>
<li>
Achieve high quality service standards and consistent cost controls.</li>
<li>
Effectiely work as a team leader and member, delegating when appropriate, assisting when needed and requesting assistance when needed.</li>
<li>
Assist in achieving budget consistently.</li>
<li>
Ensure adherence to company policies, procedures, and practices.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School diploma, G.E.D. or trade school equivalent.</li>
<li>
Minimum of five (5) years’ experience in heavy construction equipment service, support or an equivalent work experience, which includes supervisory or management experience.</li>
<li>
Ability to read and understand materials commonly used in the product service and maintenance function (service and parts manuals, technical bulletins, and other related articles).</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively communicate in both written and verbal form.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</b></p>
<p>
The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Professional attitude and appearance consistent with position and responsibilities.</li>
<li>
Self motivated and detail oriented and able to work with minimum supervision.</li>
<li>
Stand/walk 4-6 hours a day. Sit 2-4 hours a day.</li>
<li>
Occasionally/frequently lift 50-80 lbs.</li>
<li>
Must use hands for repetitive simple grasping, pushing, pulling, and fine manipulation.</li>
<li>
Must be able to occasionally bend, squat, and climb, including the ability to ascend and descend ladders.</li>
<li>
Must use feet for repetitive movement when operating equipment controls.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
WORK ENVIRONMENT:</b><br>
The work environment characteristics described here are representative of those an employee encounters while performing the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Shop and outside environment with exposure to adverse weather conditions including heat, cold, wind, rain, dust, and dirt.</li>
<li>
Work place will require usage of eye protection, rubber protective gear and hearing protection.</li>
<li>
Valid Nevada State Driver’s License with an acceptable driving records.</li>
</ul>
