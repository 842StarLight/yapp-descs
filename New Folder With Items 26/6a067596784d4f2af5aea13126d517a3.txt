<h2>Solutions Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>The goal of a Solutions Architect is to solve challenging problems for some of the biggest brands in the digital economy. You will work with customers like LinkedIn, Amadeus, Concur and other high profile organizations, as well as with some of the newest database technologies to deploy solutions that benefit millions of users use every day.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Solutions Architects are not your typical consultants – In this role, you will be responsible for making our most strategic customers successful. This team solves real world problems, using brand new technology which bundles indexing, key value access, nickel, N1QL and full text search.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Lead short / medium term engagements at the client location focusing on getting the product up and running, conducting architecture and use case reviews, sizing &amp; tuning Couchbase and recommending deployment topologies.
</li>
<li>Help our customers succeed by solving their challenging technical problems, all the way from design through to production operations. This means working on hands-on customer issues, and escalating problems as needed</li>
<li>
Assist with all phases of Couchbase implementations, starting with installation, architecture design and review, deployment topology planning, tuning and providing best practices</li>
<li>
Contribute to internal technical projects, which can include software development, benchmarking, troubleshooting</li>
<li>
Work closely with the sales team and presales team on technical escalations and help grow opportunities in existing accounts</li>
<li>
Assist with customer PoC/Pilots through effective management of acceptance criteria and issue escalation/resolution</li>
<li>
Work with all technical levels from managers, to architects and developers and explain the Couchbase Server technology and architecture</li>
<li>
Be the technical product expert for customers and stay up-to-date on the NoSQL competitive landscape</li>
<li>
Identify and write internal and external technical collateral, like typical deployment architectures or best practices</li>

</ul></div>

<div><b>About Couchbase</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Unlike other NoSQL databases, Couchbase provides an enterprise-class, multicloud to edge database that offers the robust capabilities required for business-critical applications on a highly scalable and available platform. Couchbase is built on open standards, combining the best of NoSQL with the power and familiarity of SQL, to simplify the transition from mainframe and relational databases.</div>
