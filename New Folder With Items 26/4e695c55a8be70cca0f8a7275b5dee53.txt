<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Scope of Job:</b></p>
<p>
Under the direction of the Agency Manager or Regional Agency Manager, incumbent will perform basic support functions and technical duties related to the production of insurance.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Primary Duties:</b></p>
<p>
Acts as first point of contact for routine customer requests and queries.</p>
<p>
Utilizes competencies and skills to provide a positive customer experience.</p>
<p>
Supports the insurance renewal process for the agency by providing complete renewal packets to designated agent.</p>
<p>
Works closely with Producers to service existing clients and assists in writing new business through quoting appropriate carriers.</p>
<p>
Performs functions on the agency management system proficiently and assists other employees, as needed.</p>
<p>
Reviews problems and communicates with appropriate personnel to obtain information when necessary.</p>
<p>
Continues to build personal skill set by participating in in-house or outside insurance, industry related courses or seminars as deemed necessary to expand product and system knowledge. Stays current with relevant products.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Secondary Duties:</b></p>
<p>
Work as a team with departmental counterparts on planning and implementing assigned projects and products.</p>
<p>
Participate on various committees and project teams as deemed appropriate by management.</p>
<p>
Attend, actively participate in organization, job specific training offered, and Quality Service programs.</p>
<p>
Adhere to all applicable Policies and Procedures of BankWest organization.</p>
<p>
Community involvement is strongly encouraged.</p>
<p>
Other duties as assigned.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<p>
Education:</p>
<p>
A high school diploma or equivalent.</p>
<p>
Must obtain Property and Casualty license within 6 months in the position.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Knowledge and Special Skills:</p>
<p>
Ability to work dependently and/or independently, under pressure, meet deadlines, and adjust work schedules as needed to complete job responsibilities.</p>
<p>
Ability to organize and handle multiple responsibilities while delivering quality external and/or internal Customer Service.</p>
<p>
Strong interpersonal skills to ensure effective communication with external and/or internal customers.</p>
<p>
Knowledge or ability to learn regulatory requirements pertinent to performance of specified duties in job description.</p>
<p>
Knowledge of insurance terminology/concepts, and mastery of required paperwork.</p>
<p>
Ability to skillfully use a variety of application software, learn computer programs, and comprehend the program output.</p>
<p>
Adhere to Federal Privacy Standards in addition to following BankWest requirements for electronic communication and Social Engineering standards.</p>
<p>
BankWest is a "Sales Organization" and all employees are expected to participate directly or indirectly with increasing BankWest's presence in their markets as appropriate.</p>
