<p>Do you have a natural talent for selling? We have an exciting opportunity for Outside Account Managers to join our team in Reno and Las Vegas! Strong selling skills as they relate to Virtualization, Cloud, Computing and Storage sales with existing and new clients are extremely important.</p>
<p>
The chosen candidate will work with management to develop account strategy, close contracts and maintain excellent client relationships.</p>
