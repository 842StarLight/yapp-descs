Summary
<br>Manages the activities of the Order Processor and Shipping Departments. Accountable to deliver required results in accordance with established standards and within budgetary constraints.
<br><br>
Key Result Areas
<br>1. Productivity and Results
<br><ul>
<li>Provides daily direction to Area Supervisors to accomplish operating results.</li>
<li>Delivers results in accordance with established productivity standards and business unit goals.</li>
<li>Applies sound motivational and employee relation techniques to maintain an engaged and productive work group.</li>
<li>Schedules and appropriately deploys assigned personnel to achieve performance goals.</li>
<li>Troubleshoots workflow disruptions and takes appropriate corrective action.</li>
</ul>
2. Staffing and Staff Development
<br><ul>
<li>Recruits quality personnel on a timely basis to maintain optimal staffing levels.</li>
<li>Directs supervisors to ensure all staff are properly oriented and trained.</li>
<li>Conducts performance reviews and sets goals and development plans for departmental personnel.</li>
<li>Administers corrective action as required in accordance with policy.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains sound professional relations with all employees within the work group to ensure a high level of employee engagement.</li>
</ul>
3. Operational Support and Control
<br><ul>
<li>Develops, recommends, and/or implements approved enhancements to processes, systems, policies, and procedures to improve operational efficiency, accuracy, and customer service levels.</li>
<li>Works with maintenance and support teams to effectively maintain equipment in optimal working order.</li>
<li>Reconciles payroll and attendance records on a timely basis in compliance with policies and established payroll standards.</li>
<li>Reviews the accuracy of methods and productivity standards and recommends appropriate adjustments</li>
<li>Provides input to the annual budgeting process</li>
</ul>
4.Safety and Quality of Results
<br><br>
<b>Competencies:</b><br>
In addition to Personal Leadership, Customer Service, Communication, Teamwork &amp; Results-Oriented Focus, Manager must exhibit the following:
<br><ul>
<li>Team Building - Treats team members with respect; builds teamwork; provides adequate support and tools; is approachable and accessible; accepts responsibility for own actions.</li>
<li>Performance Management - Sets realistic goals and establishes clear expectations; delegates work appropriately; forms assessments from facts; expresses respect &amp; support in disciplinary discussions.</li>
<li>Manages Workplace Issues - Exhibits fair and impartial judgment at all times; takes prompt action to resolve workplace issues; treats employees with respect; manages and resolves conflict in a constructive manner; helps team members understand organizational changes.</li>
<li>Develops Others - Provides ongoing feedback and coaching; recognizes motivates and praises excellent performance; helps to develop all team members; empowers others to do their job well.</li>
<li>Diversity - Fosters an inclusive workplace where diversity and individual differences are valued and leveraged to achieve the vision and mission of the organization.</li>
</ul>
<b>Education:</b><br>
Associate degree preferred or the equivalent in education and experience.
<br><br>
Qualifications
<br><ul>
<li>Experience in a high volume automated multi-shift distribution center. Experience in a facility that uses conveyers, RF scanning, and pick-to-ship container methods preferred.</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of inventory control and traffic management.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Word, Excel, and Windows. Able to learn and use in-house software applications.</li>
<li>Excellent organization, planning, and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills.</li>
<li>Able to lead and motivate employees by guiding, training, and offering counsel.</li>
</ul>
