<h2>Fire Alarm Installer/Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Growing Fire Safety Company located in Manchester NH servicing NH, MA, and ME is looking to add a team player to our staff.<p></p>
<p>The Fire Alarm Technician is responsible for performing scheduled or unscheduled maintenance and inspections on fire alarm, security, access control , and CCTV systems.</p>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience installing fire alarm and/or security systems</li>
<li>Knowledge of major fire alarm equipment brands (such as Firelite, Silent Knight, EST, Notifier, Gamewell/FCI, Radionics, Ademco)</li>
<li>Familiarity with applicable codes (i.e. NFPA 72)</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and integrity</li>
<li>Detail oriented and strong problem solving skills</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of personal computers including such programs as MS Word, Excel, and Outlook</li>
<li>Knowledge of and ability to utilize all tools pertaining to installation of fire alarm</li>
<li>Ability to install, bend and rough-in EMT and rigid conduit is preferred</li>
<li>Strong leadership and ability to work independently with minimal direction</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license with clear driving record</li>
<li>Able to pass Background Check and Drug Test</li>
<li>Ability to work a full-time schedule</li>
<li>Overtime may be required for this position with short notice</li>
<li>Participate in weekly revolving On-Call Status</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Licensed in Massachusetts &amp; Maine</li>
<li>Some knowledge of fire sprinkler systems</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Direct report to Management</li>
<li>Perform testing, troubleshooting, and repair of fire alarm, suppression and security systems/associated devices in accordance with company standards</li>
<li>Successfully perform C.O. inspections with respective AHJ (Authorities Having Jurisdiction)</li>
<li>Responsible for reading and understanding assigned project documentation and work orders including scope of work for installations, and determine if additional information is needed</li>
<li>Read and interpret blueprints, diagrams, software/systems, programs, schematics and operation/product manuals consistent with the level of training received</li>
<li>Prepare associated paperwork/forms for customer service activities &amp; return completed documentation required for master customer file, future inspections, service, accounting and payroll in a timely manner as required by procedures</li>
<li>Maintain confidential information related to services performed</li>
<li>Ability to write detailed reports of inspection deficiencies and provide information for quoting deficiencies</li>
<li>Partner with sales to grow and protect the service segment of our business</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Pay depends on your level of experience and education.</li></ul>
<p>
**Company offers benefits- Paid Vacation, Sick &amp; Personal Time, Contributes to Health Insurance.</p>
