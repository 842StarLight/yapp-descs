<h2>Senior Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Lakeside Software is a rapidly growing global leader in digital experience monitoring. Our goal is to help IT continuously improve end-user productivity by understanding and optimizing the technologies people use to get work done. Our technology provides insight into employee usage patterns to help our customers understand their total IT environment.</p>
<p>
As a Senior Product Marketing Manager, you will report directly to our Chief Marketing Officer. Corporate positioning, messaging, content, and sales enablement for our software product, SysTrack will all be in your purview. Additionally, you will support go to market activities for new product and feature launches, partnerships, and integrations.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Global branding, content creation (both external and some internal content), and positioning</li>
<li>Corporate website, SEO, and PPC strategy, projects, and performance</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with Sales, Engineering and Product Management teams serving as a leader in product launches, providing input during the product development process, and crafting corporate messaging and content strategy for all new developments</li>
<li>Play as both an individual contributor and strategic leader within the company</li>
<li>Provide event strategy and messaging for all corporate conferences and activities</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with lead generation to ensure healthy pipeline, sound email drips, and collateral support throughout the customer journey</li>
<li>Manage the marketing team and work alongside them to execute content calendar which includes, but is not limited to, the creation of content such as videos, whitepapers, solution briefs, blogs, and decks</li>
<li>Support integrations and business development teams in launching and marketing product integrations</li>
<li>Support the partner ecosystem with partner marketing support which includes, but is not limited to, creating partner marketing collateral, upkeeping the partner portal, and participating at partner events</li>
<li>Represent Lakeside Software at industry events including global conferences and regional events
</li>
<li>Manage analyst relations alongside CMO</li>
<li>Conduct and share competitive research with the sales team</li>
<li>Provide branding and messaging guidance to the global organization</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree</li>
<li>Minimum 5+ years marketing experience with a focus on product marketing</li>
<li>Ability to travel within the US and Internationally
</li>
<li>Proven track record crafting and conveying product narratives to different audiences</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and data-driven thinking</li>
<li>Positive attitude with bias to learn and succeed in a quickly changing business environment</li>
<li>Exceptional presentation, collaboration, interpersonal, problem solving, and verbal &amp; written communication skills</li>
<li>Tech Growth company experience a plus</li>
</ul>
