<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Hunt new business opportunities outside our existing client base.</li>
<li>Work closely with our internal marketing team to generate and develop new areas of business.</li>
<li>Monitor and track progress of new leads through to closing.</li>
<li>Develop relationships within new clients.</li>
<li>Travel with operations team when needed to close new business (roughly 20–30% of the time).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience developing new business opportunities</li>
<li>Knowledge of digital technologies and learning solutions that now complement textbooks</li>
<li>Knowledge of sales software a plus</li>
<li>Enthusiastic, self-motivated sales professional with 3–5 years of publishing industry experience</li>
<li>College degree</li>
<li>Excited to learn new technologies, and interested in following industry trends and changes</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
