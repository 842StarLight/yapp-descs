<h2>Manager of Performance Marketing and Demand Generation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>MANAGER OF PERFORMANCE MARKETING &amp; DEMAND GENERATION</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Combining a suite of software-enabled services with industry best practices, PartsSource empowers customers to drive costs out of healthcare supply chain delivery. By automating the highly complex workflow associated with procurement, PartsSource delivers the industry’s only online, fully-integrated medical products and repair parts marketplace. PartsSource facilitates millions of transactions annually by connecting 6,000 OEMs and suppliers to over 3,000 hospitals and 12,000 clinical sites worldwide.<br>
<br>
Founded in 2001, rapidly growing, and profitable, PartsSource continues to expand its marketplace of procurement and service solutions to ensure Healthcare is always on. PartsSource is answering the call for healthcare’s strong demand for game-changing eCommerce technology.<br>
</div>
<div><b>About the role:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Manager, Performance Marketing and Demand Generation will be responsible for driving the strategy and planning for paid search, affiliate, organic search, A/B site testing and optimization; helping to drive sales and engagement for the PartsSource suite of offerings. You will be responsible for ensuring digital and integrated marketing campaigns are driving qualified traffic, improving ROI and overall program performance, leveraging iterative testing and data analysis. This leader will play a pivotal role in building a best-in-class digital marketing and lead generation engine for PartsSource.</div>
<div>The ideal candidate has a proven track record for driving direct response digital marketing programs and integrated campaigns. You will manage site optimization, paid search, affiliate and organic search forecasts and plans, developing, enhancing and growing these channels through not only optimization efforts but also through championing and implementing new ideas and campaigns.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>What you'll do:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Partner with PartsSource marketing, sales, and product management teams to develop holistic performance marketing programs (SEM, eMail, Social, Affiliate, Partner, Demand and Display, etc.) for all channels</li>
<li>Manage development of marketing channel plans and goals to meet performance targets and ensure alignment with fiscal budgets and forecasts.</li>
<li>Lead account-based marketing, commercial launch, and targeted outreach campaigns to increase awareness and conversion of the PartsSource Solutions.</li>
<li>Develop paid search campaigns and new program initiatives with a focus on acquisition, engagement, and retention strategy, leveraging CRM lists and segmentation to optimize and meet customer strategy targets.</li>
<li>Partner with third party agency and manage search engine optimization efforts, working internally cross-functionally to develop content strategy and translate business goals into SEM strategies</li>
<li>Manage digital marketing promotional cadence and planning including development of new and innovative ideas, programs, measurement, and reporting on ROI and campaign performance</li>
<li>Socialize channel strategy, priorities, and goals to ensure cross-functional alignment and effective performance marketing management.</li>
<li>Oversee channel briefs, ensuring all requests meet campaign needs and timelines.</li>
<li>Define audience engagement strategies to ensure maximum potential for campaigns</li>
<li>Optimize traffic, conversion, and ROI based on brand, customer, and product growth strategies</li>
<li>Report performance on a monthly / quarterly basis, highlighting opportunities or trends for continuous improvement</li>
<li>Keep abreast of trends and tools to identify new opportunities for improvement</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Who you are:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Seasoned marketer with 6-10 years of professional B2B / B2C experience; healthcare experience preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of digital marketing best practices</li>
<li>Proven experience managing performance marketing budgets with effective returns and traffic growth.</li>
<li>Expert knowledge and use of marketing automation, and analytics tools across marketing channels.</li>
<li>Deep experience leading matrixed teams as direct manager or project manager</li>
<li>A team player comfortable with ambiguity</li>
<li>Outstanding written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>This role is located in Aurora, OH</li>
</ul>
