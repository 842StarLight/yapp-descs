<h2>Senior Electrical Design Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>COMPANY:</b><br>
CPS Products has built an outstanding product portfolio of tools, instruments and service equipment targeted to the HVAC/R technician. Headquartered in Miramar, Florida with wholly owned subsidiary locations in Europe, Australia and Canada the Company is well positioned to bring new products to the global market quickly and efficiently and is able to provide after sales support, training and service to our global customers.
<br><br>
While working with global supply partners, CPS assembles and manufactures much of what we design at our manufacturing facilities located in Miramar, Florida and Mississauga (Toronto), Canada. Our manufacturing strategy is to make the critical parts and components in house to ensure their quality and to outsource the non-critical "commodity" items.
<br><br>
Over the years CPS has invested in the tools, equipment, technology and people to manufacture both hi-tech electronic products as well as precision machined mechanical products and everything in between. The company has good products incorporating advanced technology.
<br><br>
The Senior Electronics Design Engineer is responsible for the design of analog and digital electronic circuits, sensor components and assemblies for new products, and the sustaining engineering for existing products, in support of the CPS Products HVAC and Automotive product offerings. The Senior Electrical Design Engineer will be responsible for new product development designs, and will be required to work on cross-functional product development teams. The duties of the position will include component engineering and product design, generation of new product design documentation, development of test plans, and the development of manufacturing processes to support new product designs. The Senior Electrical Design Engineer will be responsible for providing technical direction and work prioritization for subordinate engineering staff. CPS is looking for a candidate with the ability to uphold ONICON’s mission and vision. Must be accurate and pay attention to details. Willing to take on tasks as directed. Willing to take initiative when appropriate. Willing to be flexible as needed. Trustworthy, loyal, helpful, friendly, courteous, kind, cheerful, thrifty, and respectful.
<br><br>
