<h2>Ticket Seller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION: Ticket Seller</b></p>
<p><b>
DEPARTMENT: Ticket Office
</b></p>
<p><b>REPORTS TO: Ticket Office Assistant Manager</b></p>
<p><b>
FLSA STATUS: PT, Hourly, Non-Exempt, Non-Union</b></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
SMG, the leader in privately managed public assembly facilities, has an excellent and immediate opening for a Ticket Seller for SMG/U.S. Bank Stadium. Ticket Sellers work U.S. Bank Stadium events (does not include Vikings games).</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Accurately sells tickets as requested by patrons; accepts payment and makes change accurately.</li>
<li>Maintains accurate count when selling hard tickets or accesses computer for count of computer printed tickets.</li>
<li>Completes daily ticket sales report. Keeps accurate daily balance sheet of cash received and tickets sold; balances sales and change bank and submits cash to the Ticket Office Assistant Manager or Manager for audit.</li>
<li>Fills sales requests for tickets by telephone or mail, handles Will-Call window according to procedures, or other related duties as assigned by supervisor.</li>
<li>Demonstrates excellent customer service skills; responds promptly to customer needs, responds to requests for service and assistance, able to work independently and handle most Ticket Office questions without assistance.</li>
<li>Efficiently and courteously answers questions concerning prices, seating, and events, and gives information concerning coming attractions.</li>
<li>Files various records and reports. Performs related clerical work as assigned.</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Skills</b></p>
<p>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>Ability to work with minimum supervision</li>
<li>Physical ability to use hands to handle and count coins and currency</li>
<li>Close vision ability</li>
<li>Ability to work flexible hours including daytime, evening, weekends, and holidays</li>
<li>Operate computerized ticketing system and a personal computer using Windows and Microsoft Office software.</li>
</ul> <p><b>Minimum Education &amp; Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED)</li>
<li>3 to 6 months related experience and/or training or equivalent combination of education/experience</li>
<li>Ticketmaster experience preferred</li>
<li>Ticket Office, cashier, or Guest Services background preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Computer Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Operate computerized ticketing system.</li>
<li>Operate event booking/scheduling software.</li>
<li>Operate a personal computer using Windows, Office, and other standard office equipment.</li>
</ul>
