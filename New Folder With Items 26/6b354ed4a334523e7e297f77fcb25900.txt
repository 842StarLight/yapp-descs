<b>Summary:</b><br>
The Telecom Large Public Venue Business Development position will perform a key role in the Telecom and Technology division of MSB. Candidates for this position are required to have a minimum of 10 years' experience selling technology solutions, preferably Wireless or information Technology, to Large Public Venues. Large Public venues are defined as but not limited to Stadiums, Arenas, Convention Centers, Hospitals, Race Tracks and Airports. There will be a high-level execution at every stage of the pipeline development and sales process. This position will manage a set of the business' largest and most strategic prospects and accounts. The position is expected to maintain long term partner and client relationships while teaming with other staff to ensure flawless execution during the engagement management process.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to analyze project opportunities and determine how they relate to MSB capabilities</li>
<li>Complete territory management, lead qualification, identifying customers needs, following through on the development of targeted accounts, and recommending an overall product solution to address individual client requirements in a consultative fashion.</li>
<li>Making outbound calls and e-mails to potential and existing customers.</li>
<li>Following up on inbound sales leads from potential and existing customers.</li>
<li>Using the consultative sales process to understand customer problems, objections, concerns, and needs.</li>
<li>Setting and accomplishing monthly, quarterly, and yearly sales goals.</li>
<li>Building and maintaining customer relationships while growing their annual spend.</li>
<li>Participating in sales team and company meetings.</li>
<li>Identify broad market trends in the technology industry and work with management to develop new service offerings.</li>
<li>Work in a leadership role both internally and externally with clients.</li>
<li>Utilize IT industry knowledge and experience to develop comprehensive solutions for clients.</li>
<li>Participate in external conferences and present capabilities of MSB&amp;A.</li>
<li>Support growth of WLAN &amp; DAS practices via vendor specific training and identification of market opportunities.</li>
<li>Leverage existing relationships in LPV's to increase MSB's exposure and drive new revenue.</li>
<li>Position duties will evolve based on trends in the marketplace and company requirements.</li>
<li>Represent MSB in Contract Negotiations with clients.</li>
<li>Ability to understand client stakeholders roles and responsibilities throughout the sales cycle and effectively understand and address each groups specific concerns.</li>
<li>Travel is required.</li>
</ul>
