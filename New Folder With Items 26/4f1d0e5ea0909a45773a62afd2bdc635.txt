<h2>Strategic Accounts Director (US)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Who we are:</b></p>
<p>
We believe in a future where daily transportation is emission-free and sustained by smart charging infrastructure. That’s why we’ve made it our goal to accelerate the adoption of electric mobility by bringing the most accessible and scalable charging solutions to the world. However, the transition from gas to electric cars will change a great deal of what we know about transportation. We’re going to need EV charging infrastructure that is accessible, reliable, and future-proof. Team Business Development at EVBox is focused on educating stakeholders and building partnerships to drive towards a zero-emission future.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Who we’re looking for:</b></p>
<p>
We’re looking for a Strategic Accounts Director (US), who will be responsible for building relationships with significant partners and shared customers throughout North America. This includes driving growth through our most significant partners, and leveraging these partners as a channel to reach large workplace, retail, hospitality, and institutional customers. This role will include building programs through our partners to reach multi-site corporate customers within the U.S. and Canada, and building programs with said customers to reach their regional facilities through effective understanding &amp; communication of the solution architecture of our product portfolio, flawless implementation of EV charging solutions, and high levels of Customer Satisfaction.</p>
