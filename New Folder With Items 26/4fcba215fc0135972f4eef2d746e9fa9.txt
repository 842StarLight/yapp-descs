<h2>Outside Sales Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Beyond is seeking an experienced outside sales professional who is tired of the status quo and wants to help change the face of the merchant services industry. You must be the type of individual that enjoys working with other high performers and craves the challenge of closing deals. The Business Advocate is a W2 position that offers the ability to earn large weekly payouts based on your efforts and successes, while also building a longterm compounding residual stream.</p>
<p>In addition, every Beyond employee hired this year will have the opportunity to own part of the company. The Business Advocate is the heartbeat of our organization and works closely with the Division Director to provide point-of-sale, payment processing, lending, payroll, and integrated HR tools &amp; services to small and mid-size business owners.</p>
<p>*What You Will Do</p>
<p>*</p>
<ul>
<li>Grow your portfolio by full cycle sales, prospecting through closing and follow up, to new small and mid-sized businesses</li>
<li>Prospect through outreach emails, phone-calls, social media, and face-to-face meetings</li>
<li>Deliver solution driven presentations to business partners</li>
<li>Educate business partners on the payment processing industry</li>
<li>Organize your day and week around competing demands</li>
<li>Meet or exceed expected monthly and quarterly sales milestones</li>
<li>Work closely with local Team Lead and Division Director for prospecting, training, and development</li>
<li>Accurately track and record activities in Salesforce</li>
<li>Be a positive contributor to the organization</li>
<li>Nurture and qualify new leads that turn into quality appointments</li>
<li>Join local networking groups to build your brand</li>
<li>Maintain consistent attention to detail, self-discipline, and grit</li>
</ul>
<p>*Who You Are</p>
<p>*</p>
<ul>
<li>Proven sales professional, 3+ years' experience</li>
<li>Knowledge of the company's products, services, and business operations</li>
<li>Advanced presentation, interpersonal, time management, and organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work well under pressure</li>
<li>Ability to solve problems and find creative solutions to sales challenges</li>
<li>Desire to excel and grow within the organization</li>
<li>Salesforce CRM experience</li>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office applications, e.g., Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Ability to work both independently with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Multilingual candidates are a plus</li>
</ul>
