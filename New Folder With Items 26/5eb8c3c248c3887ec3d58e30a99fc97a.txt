<p><b>Fruit Expert™ Job Description</b></p>
<p>The Fruit Expert™ - Customer Service Associate ensures Edible® service standards are met and surpassed by providing customers with prompt service, and delivering a WOW experience.</p>
<p><b>Fruit Expert™ Customer Service Essential Job Functions and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul><li>Demonstrates a strong understanding of product knowledge and quality standards</li></ul>
<ul><li>Welcomes and connects with every customer to provide excellent customer service</li></ul>
<ul><li>Discovers customer needs and appropriately suggests products with every customer to enhance service and meet sales goals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists customers in the selection and purchase of Edible products</li></ul>
<ul><li>Uses effective sales approaches and displays strong knowledge of product line</li></ul>
<ul><li>Effectively converts customer inquiries into sales</li></ul>
<ul><li>Builds long-term relationships and builds the trust and loyalty of customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responds to customer needs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains customer service area and equipment in a clean and appealing manner</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manages difficult customer situations in a calm and professional manner</li></ul>
<p><b>Maintains Quality Store Operations </b></p>
<ul><li>As needed, prepares fruit arrangements, dipped fruit, and ETG products to Edible standards</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follows store policies and procedures for operational flow at each station</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs cleaning tasks in accordance with the cleaning standards and works as a team player</li></ul>
<ul><li>Presents oneself professionally &amp; demonstrates communication w/ customers and co-workers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follows standards for merchandising, stocking, rotating and storing all products</li></ul>
<p><b>Financial Contribution</b></p>
<ul><li>Follows cash handling and register policies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follows inventory stocking and recording guidelines</li></ul>
<ul><li>Takes responsibility to learn all aspects of the position</li></ul>
<ul><li>Learns and demonstrates creating the Edible Experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Learns and performs duties of customer Service, production, and/or delivery driver as needed</li></ul>
<ul><li>Learns and demonstrates all performance standards by completing in-store training and utilizing digital training portal on an ongoing basis</li></ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements: </b></p>
<ul><li>High School Diploma or equivalent preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Previous experience working in customer Service or food service establishment preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent customer service and communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated ability to sell and up‐sell products</li></ul>
<ul><li>Good math and money handling skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Positive attitude</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enjoys working in a fast‐paced environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drive and determination</li></ul>
<ul><li>Desire for personal and professional growth</li></ul>
<ul><li>Valid state driver’s license and a clean driving record</li></ul>
<ul><li>Computer skills including Microsoft software and register skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to lift, carry and/or push objects weighing up to 50 lbs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to stand, bend, reach and scoop throughout assigned shift</li></ul>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
<p>Hours per week:</p>
<ul><li>20-29</li></ul>
