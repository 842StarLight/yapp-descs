<h2>Product Lifecycle Management Implementation Specialist - Denver</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) Implementation Specialists work with many different leading edge firms to help them implement PLM technology to streamline processes and materially impact business success. In this role they support customer engagements throughout an implementation, including: the initial discovery phase, requirements gathering, configuration, training, issue resolution, upgrades, support handoff and overall project management. A PLM Implementation Specialist functions as a trusted advisor to the customer by proposing process optimization solutions based on their strong industry knowledge, in-depth product knowledge and business acumen. Individuals in this position also play a key role in developing IMAGINiT’s capabilities to deliver a first-class PLM solution through participation in pre-sales, marketing, and offering development activities.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assists in the creation of customers “as is” process maps documenting all the current company processes, terminology, tools in use, responsible parties, and other pertinent information.</li>
<li>
Participates in workshops to collect client data and document configuration needs for the project: client roles, job responsibilities, processes.</li>
<li>
Documents necessary field information (drop down, free form, type info needed, field restrictions).</li>
<li>
Partners with Sales to develop PLM business opportunities and participates in marketing events and other related activities to generate business opportunities.</li>
<li>
Identifies, assesses, and manages resolution of issues</li>
<li>
Executes PLM system implementation delivery tasks (configuration, scripting, rollout, testing, etc.)</li>
</ul>
</div>
