<p></p>
<p>The eCommerce Marketing Specialist to assist in the development and execution of our marketing and campaign efforts. Responsible for strategy, campaign evolution and reporting, we are seeking an extremely driven, quick-learning, and detail-oriented individual.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITiES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare key products for marketing by reviewing product listings and working cross functionally with ecommerce team to ensure products are set up for success.
</li>
<li>Plan, develop, and implement comprehensive online retail advertising strategies across all products and brands.
</li>
<li>Perform keyword research and write ad copy.
</li>
<li>Conduct A/B tests across campaign variables (e.g. ad types, categories, bidding) to reach the best results
</li>
<li>Analyze performance data and optimize campaigns to ensure they are continuously bringing quality traffic while maintaining a strong ROI
</li>
<li>Stay informed with eCommerce retail industry best practices, new trends and technologies, marketplaces, and platform updates (e.g. Amazon, Walmart.com, etc.)
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration, Marketing or business-related majors is required
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS &amp; SKILLS REQUIRED:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 1 year of eCommerce marketing/sales experience is preferred.
</li>
<li>Preferred example(s) of success: building and growing an online advertising campaign, showing how the campaign reached ACOS/ROAS targets.
</li>
<li>Implemented and ran campaigns to sell on Amazon or Walmart.com.
</li>
<li>AMS, Vendor Central, AMG, and AAP experience highly valued.
</li>
<li>Familiar with jargon (e.g. PPC, CPC, CPA, ROAS, bounce rate, above-the-fold, etc.).
</li>
<li>High attention to detail, strong organization skills, and follow-up capabilities.
</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and handle multiple projects.
</li>
<li>Strong verbal and communication skills.
</li>
<li>Enthusiasm and strong work ethic.
</li>
<li>A self-starter who can work both independently and as part of a team.
</li>
<li>Experience using business tools including Excel, Word, and PowerPoint as well as cloud sharing software such as SharePoint and GoogleDocs.
</li>
</ul>
