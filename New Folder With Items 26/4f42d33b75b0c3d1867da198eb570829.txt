<p><b>
Function Overview</b></p>
<p>
Whirlpool is seeking qualified candidates for a <b>Process Engineer -Test Systems to join our Refrigeration team in Amana, IA.</b> This position will provide engineering support to facilitate changes to test systems for new projects, tooling &amp; equipment modifications for a highly visible assembly line. The Amana division of Whirlpool is a 2M square foot manufacturing facility with four assembly lines, and home to multiple support areas including stamping, thermoforming, and material handling.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible and accountable to maintain OEE of Test Equipment, Tools &amp; Fixtures within the Assembly lines</li>
<li>
Responsible for developing, updating &amp; implementing process documents (PFMEA’s, Process maps, Control plans, Reaction plans) for various Test processes and Equipment</li>
<li>
Optimize test machine parameters to improve quality and delivery</li>
<li>
Work with Design Engineering, Advanced Manufacturing, and Quality Engineers to Analyze field problems, root cause analysis, and develop &amp; implement robust test solutions</li>
<li>
Hands on experience working with equipment to troubleshoot &amp; problem solve issues</li>
<li>
Work hand in hand with other engineers to improve horizontal expansion of various improvements to test parameters</li>
<li>
Train supervisor, team leads, and operators on equipment &amp; test tools required for general assembly of parts</li>
<li>
Responsible and accountable for delivering Safety, Quality, Cost, and Delivery Improvement targets</li>
<li>
Develop and Implement Autonomous Maintenance , Preventative Maintenance, and Focused Approach Projects within your technical areas.</li>
<li>
Approve or reject engineering changes</li>
<li>
Implement engineering changes on the floor</li>
<li>
Lead standard, major, advanced kaizens</li>
<li>
Learn and be a trainer on various WCM tools</li>
<li>
Develop and implement Capital and expense projects to reduce risk to the business.</li>
<li>
Work with various levels of the organization (Advanced Manufacturing, Central Manufacturing, New Product Launch, Engineering Platform) to implement new technologies at the plant.</li>
</ul>
