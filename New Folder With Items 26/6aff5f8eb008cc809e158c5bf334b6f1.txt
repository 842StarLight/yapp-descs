<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary
</b></h2>
<p>The <b>Vice President of Financial Planning &amp; Analysis </b>contributes to Surescripts success by being the central liaison between corporate and operational teams across the organization. As such, the individual in this role must be able to effectively interface, respond to and communicate with and for both the CEO and CFO on corporate priorities. The VP of Financial Planning &amp; Analysis ensures relevant data and analysis supports and influences company decision-making and that an effective system for corporate performance management is in place and operating successfully.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The individual in this role must master the Surescripts’ business model in order to advise management on ways to improve performance, minimize risk and capture new opportunities to the benefit of the organization. Additionally, he/she manages, prepares, administers, and directs the control of all aspects of Surescripts’ financial planning including bridging the long term strategic plan to reality. The VP of Financial Planning &amp; Analysis reviews and approves recommendations for financial planning and control, providing relevant fiscal information to executive management and the Board. He/she monitors line of business and department adherence to approved budgets. The VP of Financial Planning &amp; Analysis identifies and maintains the right mix of team competencies to maintain/grow “bench” strength and to ensure the efficient operation of the function. This includes hiring, developing and reviewing current and future personnel.
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<p><b>Specific </b><b>responsibilities </b><b>for </b><b>this </b><b>role </b><b>include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Corporate-wide budgeting &amp; forecasting – Collaborate with the CEO, CFO and other senior leaders to d evelop the annual operating budget and three year plan. Administer and maintain the accuracy, consistency and alignment of budgeting and forecasting information to support short- and long-term financial planning in addition to strategic intent/priorities.
</li>
<li>Management of financial reporting and analysis – Manage, prepare, analyze, validate and distribute monthly, quarterly and annual reporting packages inclusive of trends, ratios dashboards, visuals and explanations to all levels of management and the Board of Directors.
</li>
<li>Ad Hoc financial analysis – act as strategic partner and subject matter expert on new product evaluation, new and renewal contract negotiations including interplay and impact of leveraging incentives to advance contract execution and path to revenue.
</li>
<li>Partner with the CFO and other senior leadership on operational and strategic issues as they arise; provide strategic recommendations based on financial analysis and projections, and revenue/expense analysis.
</li>
<li>Lead FP&amp;A team in areas of talent management, performance management, development and selection.
</li>
<li>Investment decision support – provide advanced modeling and analysis in support of new business ventures (organic or M&amp;A), including expanded and bundled product offerings.
</li>
<li>Cost center management – manage, prepare, administer and communicate cost center level budgets and tracking against actuals.
</li>
<li>Advise Sales Operations as they establish annual variable incentive plans for the sales team (i.e. commissions) including reviewing draft plans and validating quarterly estimated payments.
</li>
<li>Messaging – provide support for and final packaging of all financial presentations internally and externally including executive and Board of Director level communications.
</li>
</ul>
