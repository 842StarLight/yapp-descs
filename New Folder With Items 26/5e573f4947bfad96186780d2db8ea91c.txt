<p>About Us</p>
<p>Active 911 (A911) is a leading innovator in creating technologies to help first responders save lives by getting to emergencies faster. Using a SaaS-based rapidly iterating subscription service, A911 provides location and incident details related from Computer Aided Dispatch Systems (CADS), to assist fire, rescue, police, and other first-responder/medical professionals to get to the scene of emergencies faster and with more accurate information on the scope of the incident. Active 911 was created by a firefighter with a vision to actively alert responding emergency personnel. Today, Active911 serves over 12,000 agencies with over 400,000 individual users.</p>
