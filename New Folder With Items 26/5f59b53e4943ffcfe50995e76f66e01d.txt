<p><b>JOB DUTIES:</b></p>
<p>Responsible for planning and implementing international and domestic trade programs for the promotion and sale of Florida agricultural commodities. Responsibilities include developing a trade marketing strategy in conjunction with the Trade and Development team to include inbound and outbound missions, recommended participation in trade shows, export seminars and webinars. Implementation includes identifying and recruiting participants, logistics, operations and written after action reports.</p>
<p>Prepare return on investment reports; evaluate sales impacts for tradeshow participants; maintain budgets, leads reports, contacts and other pertinent data. Capture key performance metrics and input results into the FDACS Salesforce platform.</p>
<p>Trade event attendance will be required at various regional, national and international events. Participate in trade show initial planning stages through close of show activities.</p>
<p>Maintain a comprehensive working knowledge and capability in general office procedures, databases and other technical capabilities as needed to achieve results in missions and projects.
</p>
<p>Perform other related duties as required.</p>
