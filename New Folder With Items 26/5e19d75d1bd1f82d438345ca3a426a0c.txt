<h2>Treasury Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b><i>Position Overview: </i></b><br>You may know us for our huge menu of delicious food…and for being recognized by Fortune Magazine as one of the “100 Best Companies to Work For®” six years in a row! What you may <i>not</i>know is our treasury department is an essential part of our company’s success.</p>
<p>Reporting to the Director of Treasury Operation, you will work with a team of 4 at The Cheesecake Factory corporate support enter in Calabasas Hills, CA. As the Treasury Coordinator, you will play an integral part in the company’s success by assisting with core aspects of Treasury retail operations for a global $2.3 billion publicly traded company with over 200 full-service restaurants and more than 39,000 total staff members.</p>
<p><b><i>You’ll thrive in this position if you are: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Driven by results: </b>you are conscientious and persistent about performing timely and high-volume transactions, while never hesitating to ask for more when you are done.</li>
<li>
<b>Exhaustively meticulous: </b>you have an unrelenting attention to detail, leaving nothing to chance, while understanding what deserves your immediate attention and what can wait.</li>
<li>
<b>Adaptable: </b>you are at ease in a fast-paced environment and you are able to change direction rapidly when priorities shift and deadlines change.</li>
<li>
<b>An experienced organizer: </b>you are skilled at juggling multiple priorities while keeping everyone and everything on track without missing a step.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Here’s what you’ll get to do: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Respond to merchant credit card processors’ chargeback and retrieval requests.</li>
<li>Devalue gift cards from fraudulent purchases.</li>
<li>Review credit card refund/sale requests from locations and submit for processing.</li>
<li>Post refund activity to the general ledger and sales reconciliation system.</li>
<li>Provide support to store operations and Information Technology on all critical cash and credit card related issues.</li>
<li>Resolve gift card issues that arise.</li>
<li>Serve as backup for wire and ACH processing.</li>
<li>Assist with New Restaurant set ups.</li>
<li>Assist Treasury Department in various clerical duties including faxing, copying and filing.</li>
<li>Assist Treasury Department with miscellaneous tasks as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b><i>What we require: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Excel and Word.</li>
<li>High school Diploma.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>What we prefer: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills needed to communicate with restaurant staff and vendors (i.e., banks, armored car service).</li>
<li>Experience in a challenging, multi-unit, retail environment.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to plan, organize, and manage multiple tasks and restaurant needs.</li>
<li>Self-motivated team player with strong analytical and problem-solving skills.</li>
</ul>
