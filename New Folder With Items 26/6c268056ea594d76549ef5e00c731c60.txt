<h2>Associate, Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<p><b>FULL-TIME JOB VACANCY</b><br>
<b>ASSOCIATE</b><br>
<b>Development and Outreach Department</b><br>
<b>New York</b><br>
<b>Application Deadline: October 2, 2019</b></p>
<p>
The Development and Outreach Department of Human Rights Watch (HRW) is seeking a highly-qualified applicant for the position of Associate to support the Marketing team. The Marketing team is responsible for the organization’s marketing fundraising initiatives in the United States. This position is based in New York and reports to the Marketing Manager, based in New York.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<p>
1. Assist the Marketing Manager and Director of Marketing with strategy and implementation of the direct mail program;</p>
<p>
2. Create, and help manage the development and publishing of, timelines for all direct mail and digital marketing campaigns; address changes in schedule and manage remediation actions for adjusted dates;</p>
<p>
3. Coordinate direct mail pieces through the internal review process and help manage workflow software;</p>
<p>
4. Coordinate with photo agencies and HRW Multimedia team to secure and approve photos in direct mail pieces;</p>
<p>
5. Assist with friendly-URL (FURL) page creation and tracking in e-CRM for monthly direct mail campaigns;</p>
<p>
6. Coordinate the direct mail and digital sections of integrated marketing calendars, campaign tracker, and FURL spreadsheet for all source coding, date tracking, and record keeping;</p>
<p>
7. Coordinate activities of the caging company as it relates to direct marketing, including but not limited to: acknowledgement procedures, inventory fulfillment, data capture, and implementation of new marketing initiatives;</p>
<p>
8. Support the Marketing Manager in liaising with Program, Communications, Advocacy and Legal for vetting processes;</p>
<p>
9. Support Marketing Manager in liaising with Development Operations for all technical aspects of the Marketing Program: manage implementation of acknowledgement language for direct mail pieces and other data needs as required; coordinate Marketing-Development Operations meetings;</p>
<p>
10. Help strategize, design, and implement cultivation and lapsed initiatives for donors and prospective donors;</p>
<p>
11. Process expenses and consultant invoices; assist with budgetary auditing processes and annual expense projections;</p>
<p>
12. Track and prepare direct marketing campaign collateral for archives;</p>
<p>
13. Provide administrative support to the team; and</p>
<p>
14. Perform other duties as required.</p>
<br>
<p>
<b>
Qualifications</b>:</p>
<p><b>
Education</b>: A bachelor’s degree is required.</p>
<p><b>
Experience</b>: A minimum of one year of relevant experience is required.</p>
<p><b>
Related Skills and Knowledge</b>:</p>
<p>
1. Prior office/administration experience and strong organizational skills with meticulous attention to detail are required.</p>
<p>
2. Excellent oral and written communication skills in English are required.</p>
<p>
3. Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to communicate with a variety of audiences are required.</p>
<p>
4. Ability to work well under pressure while juggling multiple tasks simultaneously is required.</p>
<p>
5. Ability to make sound decisions consistent with functions is required.</p>
<p>
6. Self-motivation and the ability to prioritize with minimal supervision and work independently as well as function as a member of a team with staff in multiple locations globally are required.</p>
<p>
7. Proficiency in computer packages including MS Office applications is required. Prior experience with e-CRM and / or databases a plus.</p>
<p>
8. Strong interest in human rights, international issues, and/or marketing, is a plus.</p>
<p><b>
Salary and Benefits</b>: Salary range starts at $ 47,436. Human Rights Watch offers comprehensive employer-paid benefits, including medical, dental, vision, disability and life insurance, an outstanding retirement savings plan and twenty (20) days of vacation per year.</p>
<p><b>
How to Apply</b>: Please apply immediately or by October 2, 2019 by visiting our online job portal at careers.hrw.org and attaching a cover letter and resume. No calls or email inquiries, please. Only complete applications will be reviewed and only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.</p>
<p>
If you are experiencing technical difficulties with your application submission, please email recruitment@hrw.org. Due to the large response, application submissions via email will not be accepted and inquiries regarding the status of applications will go unanswered.</p>
