<h2>Catering Coordinator/Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Facilitates daily catering operations. Including but not limited to order management, food production, delivery and set up of catering orders. Builds and maintains relationships with Catering clients by providing a high level of service with pick- up, delivery, and follow up to ensure client satisfaction.</p>
<p>Essential Job Functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>Daily/weekly planning &amp; execution of catering orders</li>
<li>Fast paced food production, with attention to detail</li>
<li>Order delivery &amp; set up</li>
<li>Builds relationships with catering clients</li>
<li>Assists in sales building efforts as directed</li>
<li>Effectively utilizes Catering software/CRM tools</li>
<li>Partners with LSM on any in store or off premise events that may have a catering impact</li>
<li>Resolves client issues to ensure repeat business</li>
<li>Communicates needs and opportunities effectively with management and necessary team members</li>
<li>Conducts site-visits as needed for new clients or large events</li>
</ul>
<p>Education &amp; Experience Required:</p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent required</li>
<li>Some food service and/or prior sales experience preferred</li>
<li>Prior catering experience</li>
</ul>
<p>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Highly motivated with ability to work independently</li>
<li>Organized &amp; detail oriented</li>
<li>Excellent customer relations skills</li>
<li>Excellent communications aptitude-both verbal and written</li>
<li>Conflict resolution/problem solving expertise</li>
<li>Basic computer knowledge</li>
<li>Ability to maintain client accounts/CRM profiles</li>
</ul>
