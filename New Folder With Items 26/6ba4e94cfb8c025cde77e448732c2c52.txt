<h2>Director, Demand Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Bazaarvoice connects brands and retailers to consumers, so that every shopping experience feels personal. From search and discovery to purchase and advocacy, Bazaarvoice’s solutions reach in-market shoppers, personalize their experiences, and give them the confidence to buy. Each month in the Bazaarvoice Network, more than a billion consumers view and share authentic content including reviews, questions and answers, and social photos across 6,000 brand and retail websites. Across the network, Bazaarvoice captures billions of shopper signals monthly - data that powers high-efficiency digital advertising and personalization with unmatched relevance.</div>

<div><b>
Position Summary</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Director of Demand Marketing will lead a team in charge of the strategy, execution, reporting, and optimization of all paid media, email marketing, marketing automation, and customer journeys to achieve our business and marketing objectives.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
The best candidate for this position brings a strategic orientation to demand generation and can design integrated programs that map to buyer pain points and which also consider market and competitive forces. He/she has strong capabilities in marketing campaign planning, content strategy, storytelling, managing/evaluating creative product to support demand generation efforts.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Marketing Manager will partner enthusiastically with stakeholders in product marketing, sales, client success, and other groups and use this partnership to design high-impact programs and drive effective collaboration. He/she will leverage strong relationships across Bazaarvoice to uncover innovative, breakthrough opportunities to address prospects and clients.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The selected candidate will be a self-motivated multi-tasker, comfortable working quickly and with significant ambiguity in a dynamic environment. He/she will relish new, daily challenges and be a creative problem solver and solution-oriented team member. Operational efficiency is a must, with clear understanding of demand generation metrics, marketing automation program execution, and campaign reporting. The selected candidate will also demonstrate command of integrated marketing strategy, a track record of driving effective sales alignment, and audience-centered storytelling.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Core Job Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Partner with senior sales and marketing leaders to drive develop, implement and manage growth marketing initiatives</li>
<li> Leverage ABM, Agile Marketing, and other proven sales and marketing leadership principles to create strong relationships with sales, sales operations, and marketing leaders to ensure alignment of marketing and business goals</li>
<li> Deliver qualified leads and conversions through owned, paid media, and social channels.</li>
<li> Optimize lead generation and sales conversion efforts in order to improve performance throughout the customer journey as measured by qualified lead/conversion volume, cost-per-lead/conversion or other relevant metrics</li>
<li> Manage the placement of all paid media to ensure optimal performance within given budget allocations</li>
<li> Work with Marketing peers to deliver paid, owned, and earned campaigns that support lead generation/conversion goals.</li>
<li> Collaborate with web and creative resources/agencies to develop high-impact content assets</li>
<li> Evaluate MarTech solutions to identify the correct mix of marketing technology based on business needs and budget</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Skills and Experience:</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li> 8-12 years’ progressive experience in demand generation, marketing operations and customer retention roles; with 5+ years in digital demand generation</li>
<li> Proven experience developing, managing, and delivering successful, integrated B2B demand generation campaigns from conception through implementation, measurement, and analysis.</li>
<li> Proven experience of managing, leading and developing a team.
</li>
<li> Deep experience in outbound demand generation including paid search, content syndication, email marketing, landing page optimization, social media, and webinars.</li>
<li> Power user of Salesforce CRM and marketing automation (Marketo, etc) and a passion for technologies that increase lead engagement and conversion.</li>
<li> Strong analytical and problem-solving capabilities</li>
<li> Experience with marketing dashboard/report development, delivery, and analysis including leveraging data from multiple sources</li>
<li> Proven communication and presentation skills in front of executive-level audiences.</li>
<li> Demonstrable experience collaborating with sales, sales ops, finance, business operations, and all marketing functions.</li>
<li> Ability to work in a fast-paced environment, ask questions and track down answers with confidence, and lead projects/programs with minimal direction</li>
<li> Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li> Experience in e-commerce a significant plus</li>
</ul>
