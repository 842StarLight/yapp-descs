Requirements</div></div>
<div><div>
We are looking for a passionate Data Analyst to join our growing business intelligence team. The BI Data Analyst will use data, tools, and analytic techniques to turn data into information, information into insight, and insight into business decisions. They will be responsible for presenting data and analysis in a clear and concise manner allowing the audience to quickly understand the results and recommendations so they act upon them and make data driven decisions. This role entails being involved in the entire lifecycle of the project from requirements gathering to support. They will also help monitor overall performance and work with the business to determine changes in order to identify improvements.<br>
<br>
We seek someone that is self-motivated and can handle project management responsibilities. The position is located at Milliken’s corporate headquarters in Spartanburg, SC. Hours are typically Monday – Friday 8:00 am – 5:00 pm. Any travel would be minimal.<br>
<br>
Key Responsibilities<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with developers, project managers, business analysts, and business users in developing new BI solutions including BW models and SAP Analytics Cloud visualizations.</li>
<li>Define the approach, project scope, schedule, requirements and deliverables for Data Science efforts.</li>
<li>Contribute to overall architecture and design</li>
<li>Interpreting data, analyzing results using statistical techniques</li>
<li>Assist with developing and implementing databases, data collection systems, data analytics and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality</li>
<li>Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets</li>
<li>Work with management to prioritize business and information needs</li>
<li>Locate and define new process improvement opportunities</li>
</ul>
<br>
Required<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Proven working experience as a data analyst or business data analyst</li>
<li>Technical expertise regarding data models, database design development, data mining and segmentation techniques</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of and experience with reporting packages</li>
<li>Knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing datasets (Excel, SPSS, SAS etc)</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Adept at queries, report writing and presenting findings</li>
</ul>
<br>
Preferred<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing datasets (Excel, SPSS, SAS etc)</li></ul>
