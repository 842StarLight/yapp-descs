<b>RESPONSIBILITY SUMMARY (Who we are looking for):</b> Our manufacturing company is looking for someone to operate CNC machines and help to enhance the productivity of our organization. The successful team member will operate machine(s) to produce the required number of parts with good quality.
<br><br>
<b>WORK HOURS (SHIFT):</b> 2nd Shift
<br><br>
<b>STARTING SALARY:</b> $13.00 (with $.50 increase every 3 months; $15.00 at end of 1st year)
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE (What you would be doing - we will train):
<br><ul>
<li>Perform measurements using instruments and gauges</li>
<li>Complete offsets and major tool changes as required</li>

<li>Performing duties necessary for regular production as well as development work</li>
<li>Following all required quality assurance procedures, monitoring SPC and tool insert usage for process changes</li>
</ul>
