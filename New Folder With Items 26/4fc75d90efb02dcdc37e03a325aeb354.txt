<p><b>
ABOUT UNITED COMMUNICATIONS:</b></p>
<p>
Headquartered in Chapel Hill, TN, United is a leading provider of advanced communications services in Middle Tennessee, providing residents and businesses with high-speed Internet, digital TV and phone service. United operates a state of the art fiber optic network and was one of the first companies in Tennessee to introduce a Fiber-to-the-Home product to residential customers. United prides itself on providing friendly local customer service and technical support to its customers living in Williamson and Marshall Counties and the surrounding areas (College Grove, Chapel Hill, Unionville, Fosterville, Estill Springs, Belfast, Flat Creek, Nolensville and portions of Brentwood).</p>
<p><b>
SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p>The Marketing Manager will drive all company marketing, advertising, promotional and PR initiatives and takes steps to measure, enhance, and enrich the position and image of a company through established organizational goals and objectives.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p>Duties include the following, but other duties may be assigned as required.</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with internal executives and outside marketing agencies to plan and execute all internal and external marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>Lead creation, production and delivery of content generation, press releases, advertisements and all other marketing materials across all media platforms.
</li>
<li>Expand product/service solutions and support customer service and sales team with clear product positioning, branding and pricing.</li>
<li>Nurture and enrich all external perceptions of the company and growth of market share.</li>
<li>Ensure consistent on-brand messaging to support customer acquisition and retention goals.</li>
<li>Prepare and oversee annual marketing budget.</li>
<li>Develop presentations, calendars, and reports on marketing activity and results with ROI and identified success metrics.</li>
<li>Oversee CRM tools to drive new customer leads and increase new customer acquisitions.</li>
<li>Engage customers through social media channels and review sites, including posts, lead campaigns, and customer follow-up messages and responses.</li>
<li>Analyze market trends, competitive data, and customer insights, and recommend changes to marketing and business development strategies based on analysis and feedback.</li>
<li>Deepen relationships with all local chamber organizations, as well as media/press to ensure the most effective messaging and positioning of the organization.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Education, Experience, Skills, and Licenses/Certificates:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years experience in marketing, communications or public relations with demonstrated success preferred.</li>
<li>Bachelor s degree in journalism, communications, marketing, or public relations or related field preferred.</li>
<li>Telecommunications/Internet/Cable/Service industry experience a plus.</li>
<li>Demonstrated skills, knowledge and experience in marketing strategy, product or service promotion, creative and content development, social media management, and market research.</li>
<li>Strong creative, strategic, analytical, organizational and personal sales skills.</li>
<li>Direct experience with design and production of digital advertising and print materials.</li>
</ul>

<li>Commitment to working with shared and in cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communications skills.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects within a fast-paced working environment.</li>
</ul>
