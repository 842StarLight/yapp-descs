<p><b>Summary: </b></p>
<p>The Mortgage Loan Post Closer is responsible for the timely and accurate gathering of post-closing conditions to ensure efficient sale of loans.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Accurately stack mortgage loan files after closing for shipment to investors</li>
<li>Assess post-closing deficiency reports and gather documentation sufficient for sale of loan</li>
<li>Communicate with loan officers, processors, underwriters and closers and any other appropriate parties to quickly and accurately obtain information required to clear noted deficiencies</li>
<li>Maintain clear and accurate documentation on progress and disposition of loan files</li>
<li>Maintain a high level of expertise relative to all mortgage loan documentation required to satisfy post-closing deficiencies including but not limited to disclosures, documents, appraisals, income and asset statements</li>
</ul>
