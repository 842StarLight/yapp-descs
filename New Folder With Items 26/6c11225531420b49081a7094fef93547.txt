<h2>Marketing Coordinator II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<p>This position is responsible for creating an overall auction marketing strategy and coordinating and implementing this within the location. As the Marketing Coordinator, there will be a close partnership with Corporate Marketing, the Senior Field Marketing Manager and Auction leadership to ensure that promotional efforts align with corporate goals and KPIs and support the broader strategy and needs of the market.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Function as a member of the Field Marketing team, providing marketing strategy and leadership at the auction location</li>
<li>
Work with auction leadership at assigned location(s) and Sr. Field Marketing Manager to develop marketing strategies that serve the needs of location consignors and overall location goals.</li>
<li>
Ensure strategies are delivered on time, within budget and increase KPIs at the auction.</li>
<li>
Plan and oversee execution of marketing activities for dealers within market and auction facilities including promotions, signage, social media, email and phone campaigns.</li>
<li>
Work with location finance to effectively manage marketing budget and budget spreadsheet.</li>
<li>
Effectively manage location website and social media platforms, creating ad plan for each.</li>
<li>
Ensure appropriate corporate marketing signage is displayed at the auction location.</li>
<li>
Create and implement KAM marketing plans as needed and submit COOP requests at the end of the promotional period.</li>
<li>
Track giveaways and promotional item inventory. Ensure appropriate reporting of promotional item receipt to the finance department.</li>
<li>
Work in auction lanes on promotions for commercial and dealer accounts.</li>
<li>
Work with manager to review work volumes, plan and continuously monitor staffing levels to ensure efficiency, quality work product, and effective customer service; review departmental performance against key performance indicators and metrics and develop and execute strategies for improvement.</li>
<li>
Partners with management to set an example in behavior, championing Cox/Manheim values and ensuring that all employees are treated with respect and that Safety is the #1 priority for employees and dealers.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned by management.</li>
<li>
Travel as required by corporate marketing and auction leadership.</li>
<li>
Assist with and Travel to sister auction location(s) to help with marketing efforts.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h4>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalent required. Bachelor's degree in business or marketing preferred.</li>
<li>
1-3 years of marketing and/or promotions and customer service leadership experience required.</li>
<li>
Previous experience in the auto industry preferred.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication (written and verbal), organization and interpersonal skills required.</li>
<li>
Good computer skills and fundamental graphic arts knowledge is essential. Knowledge of MS Office products and layout/design software required.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a high performance, fast paced team environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to work under pressure with time constraints.</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively in a constantly changing work environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to sit for prolonged periods of time.</li>
<li>
Ability to perform repetitive tasks; manual dexterity.</li>
<li>
Vision abilities required include close, distance, color and depth perception.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p><b>
Work Environment:</b></p>
<p><br>
Occasional exposure to outdoor weather conditions as well as noise, dust, and fumes in auction lanes.</p>
