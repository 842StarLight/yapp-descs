<h2>Coordinator - Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
Who are we?</b></p>
<p>
Live Nation Entertainment is the world’s leading live entertainment company comprised of global market leaders: Ticketmaster, Live Nation Concerts, Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship and Artist Nation Management. Ticketmaster is the global event ticketing leader with over 465 million tickets sold and more than 12,500 clients in 22 countries. Live Nation Concerts has become the single largest provider of live entertainment in the world producing over 25,500 shows annually for over 3,300 artists in 37 countries. Artist Nation Management is one of the world’s leading artist management companies, representing more than 350 artists. These businesses power Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship to deliver music marketing programs across 900 brands enabling advertisers to tap into the 63 million fans Live Nation Entertainment delivers annually through its live event and digital platforms. For additional information, visit www.livenationentertainment.com.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Entertainment, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
Across multiple live music venues, the Premium Seat Sales Coordinator is responsible for generating Premium Seat revenue which may include box suites, season tickets, PSLs, some hospitality events and other revenue-generating programs as added to portfolio of menu items to sell. Packages and inventory will be specific to assigned venue(s).</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist Director of Premium Seats in generating sales and administering all Live Nation Premium Seat Programs in the St. Louis area.</li>
<li>
Prepare, issue, and verify return of all Premium Seat contracts as advised by Director of Premium Seats</li>
<li>
Oversee and maintain accounts receivable</li>
<li>
Assist in maintenance of client relationships</li>
<li>
Act as a liaison with internal departments (i.e. Box Office, Finance, local marketers) along with Director of Premium Seats</li>
<li>
Manage client requests for additional ticket purchases</li>
<li>
Assist with the processing of PSS sales reports</li>
<li>
Maintain client email database and facilitate regular email updates on various items via Exact Target</li>
<li>
Coordination of on-site premium seat program responsibilities and client services for most concert events</li>
<li>
Interface with VIP Club catering staff to insure a smooth running Club on event days</li>
<li>
Conducts cold calls, prospecting for new clients</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned by management</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Associate degree minimum</li>
<li>
Bachelor degree preferred</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills</li>
<li>
High energy</li>
<li>
Excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>
Ability to multi task</li>
<li>
Creativity</li>
<li>
Highly organized</li>
<li>
Commitment to excellence</li>
<li>
Minimum 1-2 years of relative business experience</li>

Computer proficiency: Excel, Word, Power Point</li>
</ul>
