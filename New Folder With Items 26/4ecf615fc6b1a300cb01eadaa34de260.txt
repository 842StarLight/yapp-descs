<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
Responsible for providing strategic account management and financial<br>
sales leadership for an assigned portfolio of existing mid-market<br>
customers. Develop and manage overall account strategies for specific<br>
named account customers, including identification of incremental revenue<br>
opportunities and retention of embedded base services. Responsible for<br>
overall customer relationship management and customer satisfaction in<br>
addition to delivering annual customer revenue and retention objectives.<br>
<br>
Employees at all levels are expect to:<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Understand our Operating Principles; make them the guidelines for how</li></ul>
you do your job<br>
<ul><li>Own the customer experience-think and act in ways that put our</li></ul>
customers first, give them seamless digital options at every touchpoint,<br>
and make them promoters of our products and services<br>
<ul><li>Know your stuff-be enthusiastic learners, users and advocates of our</li></ul>
game-changing technology, products and services, especially our digital<br>
tools and experiences<br>
<ul><li>Win as a team-make big things happen by working together and being</li></ul>
open to new ideas<br>
<ul><li>Be an active part of the Net Promoter System-a way of working that</li></ul>
brings more employee and customer feedback into the company-by joining<br>
huddles, making call backs and helping us elevate opportunities to do<br>
better for our customers<br>
<ul>
<li>Drive results and growth</li>
<li>Respect and promote inclusion and diversity</li>
<li>Do what's right for each other, our customers, investors and our</li>
</ul>
communities</div>
<div>
<b>
Core Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul><li>Meet or exceed monthly sales quota through identification and closing</li></ul>
of incremental sales and revenue opportunities.<br>
<ul>
<li>Renew customer contracts to protect and grow existing revenue streams.</li>
<li>Maintain regular account contact to ensure positioning and alignment</li>
</ul>
of Comcast Business Services with assigned accounts.<br>
<ul><li>Initiate and deliver proposed solutions to meet the needs of the</li></ul>
assigned customers as it relates to Advanced Voice, Metro Ethernet or<br>
other Business Class products, as appropriate.<br>
<ul><li>Maintain customer satisfaction and serve as the primary escalation</li></ul>
point for any customer issues that arise.<br>
<ul><li>Manage the cultivation, execution and delivery of sales and services</li></ul>
to local and Regional accounts in the Enterprise and mid-market segment.<br>
<ul><li>Collaborate with Sales, Finance, and Operations leadership to develop</li></ul>
specific account management plans to meet assigned accounts needs and an<br>
overall strategy to optimize sales and retention opportunities.<br>
<ul><li>Position and sell Comcast Business Class services across multiple</li></ul>
organizational levels including but not limited to C-level and Executive<br>
level personnel.<br>
<ul><li>Management of existing revenue, sales oppoortunities, quota, funnels</li></ul>
and forecasts consistent with Region, Division, and Corporate sales,<br>
service, and operational goals and objectives.<br>
<ul><li>Consistent exercise of independent judgment and discretion in matters</li></ul>
of significance.<br>
<ul><li>Regular, consistent and punctual attendance. Must be able to work</li></ul>
nights and weekends, variable schedule(s) and overtime as necessary.<br>
<ul><li>Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li></ul>
</div>
