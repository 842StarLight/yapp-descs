<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>

<p><b>
Position Overview</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
The Staff Accountant is primarily responsible for timely and accurate preparation and analysis of financial information. A Staff Accountant should understand and ensure financial statements are in compliance with accounting standards. A Staff Accountant will also provide information to users that will enable them to make educated business decisions. The successful candidate will be organized and detail oriented, able to adhere to strict deadlines, and maintain a high degree of accuracy…while having fun in the process!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Compile and analyze financial information to prepare entries to general ledger accounts, documenting business transactions.</li>
<li>Communicate with management regarding financial information to ensure transactions are recorded appropriately.</li>
<li>Analyze financial information detailing income, expenses, assets, and liabilities and prepare balance sheet, profit and loss statement, and other reports to summarize current and projected company financial position.</li>
<li>Audit contracts, orders, and vouchers, and prepare reports to ensure they are booked accurately and timely.</li>
<li>Prepare and review detailed balance sheet account reconciliations, research and resolve any discrepancies.</li>
<li>Document and coordinate implementation of accounting and accounting control procedures.</li>
<li>Understand and apply generally accepted accounting principles within the business environment.</li>
<li>Prepare and file sales/use or other gross receipts tax returns as required.</li>
<li>Assist with annual budget preparation.</li>
<li>Calculate, track and reconcile commissions and bonuses, and submit required information for payroll processing.</li>
<li>Prepare/review and assist with bank account reconciliations.</li>
<li>Maintain confidentiality of personal information.</li>
<li>Interface with others in a professional manner and be a trusted go to person.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as required. These duties may include assignments in job classifications other than their own</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Required Attributes:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This position requires the use of a computer to perform virtually all functions of the job; computer literacy is a must.</li>
<li>Excel and Word are required</li>
<li>Other software used most frequently in this position includes Microsoft Outlook, Great Plains, Tracker Suite, T-Value, and Pivotal.</li>
<li>Must have the capability to read and comprehend general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures, and government regulations.</li>
<li>Ability to write reports, analysis, business correspondence, and procedure documents is critical.</li>
<li>Mathematical Ability to apply concepts such as fractions, rates, percentages, ratios, and proportions to practical situations.</li>
<li>Reasoning Ability to apply common sense understandings to carry out detailed instructions in written, oral, or diagram form.</li>
<li>Self-starter. Ability to work autonomously with little support.</li>
<li>Ability to work well under pressure and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Must be able to lift a minimum of 25 lbs.</li>
<br>
</ul>
