<h2>Sales Development Representative - Mid Market</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Slack is on a mission to make your working life simpler, more pleasant and more productive. We are looking for highly motivated Sales Development Representatives to join our fast growing Sales organization.</p>
<p>
Our culture is the north star that guides how we work together and with our customers. Are you looking to build something greater than yourself? Do you want to work alongside some of the most talented and dynamic people in the industry? If so, Slack just might be the place for you.</p>
<p>
We'll help you build your career in Sales while you collaborate with a diverse team of talented individuals. You are a self starter who is deeply passionate about customer happiness, read on.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What you will be doing</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Provide a world-class customer experience to all inbound leads expressing interest in Slack via multiple channels, including phone, email, and live chat.</li>
<li>Conduct context gathering and qualification calls with all inbound prospects in order to drive new sales pipeline.</li>
<li>Run full sales-cycle, lead to close, with Slack's emerging business prospects.</li>
<li>Become a product expert and learn Slack's selling practices.</li>
<li>Keep meticulous records of interactions with leads in our CRM (Salesforce.com.)</li>
<li>Identify inefficiencies and implement improvements in our process.</li>
<li>Manage multiple projects at a time and prioritize workload within the team to ensure that project timelines are met.</li>
<li>Work across the sales department to roll out new initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you should have</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>1+ years of full-time work experience, preferably in sales, marketing or customer success.</li>
<li>Commitment to pursuing a career in sales.</li>
<li>Demonstrated familiarity with enterprise SaaS.</li>
<li>Strong stakeholder management skills; ability to build relationships and facilitate decision making at all levels.</li>
<li>Consistent track record of overachieving.</li>
<li>Empathetic listener and persuasive communicator.</li>
<li>Competitive, resilient, and highly motivated.</li>
<li>A great attitude and love for teamwork.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required.</li>
</ul>
