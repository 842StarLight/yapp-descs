<h2>Channel Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">ABOUT THE ROLE
<br>--------------
<br><br>
The Channel Manager (CM) is responsible for achieving profitability, sales, partner satisfaction, and recruitment objectives from development of a successful channel program. Reporting to the Executive Vice President of Corporate Development the CM is responsible for developing the channel program and managing channel relationships while driving incremental business through the channel programs for A-LIGN.
<br><br>
<b>REPORTS TO:</b> EVP of Corporate Development
<br><br>
<b>PAY CLASSIFICATION:</b> Full-Time, Exempt
<br><br>
<b>Essential Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Successfully develops a channel program using industry best practices and personal knowledge</li>
<li>Successfully develops a criterion for channels deserving focus.</li>
<li>Successfully assess the market and prioritizes the top channels for focus</li>
<li>Successfully recruits targeted channel partners into the A-LIGN Program</li>
<li>Successfully enable channel partners, works with A-LIGN Marketing to engage channel partner and create launch plan and measure results</li>
<li>Ensures newly recruited channel partners are revenue generating and growing incremental business for A-LIGN</li>
<li>Works with A-LIGN sales to engage with Channel partners to drive rapport and add to A-LIGN Sales pipelines</li>
<li>Conduct Quarterly Business Plan review with channel partners to ensure milestones are being met and partnership is mutually beneficial to both parties</li>
<li>Build up channel partner practice at authorized A-LIGN distributors to leverage their partner base to grow A-LIGN sales</li>
<li>Go-to Point of Contact for channel partners and a resource to help them grow their business</li>
<li>Establishes productive and professional business relationships with key personnel in assigned channel partner accounts</li>
<li>Meet assigned targets and objectives by driving profitable sales volume through strategic partnerships in assigned accounts</li>
<li>Enforce partner engagement accountability via Salesforce activity logging</li>
<li>Proactively assesses, clarify, and validate partner(s) needs on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>Manages potential channel conflict through excellent communication internally and externally, and through strict adherence to channel rules of engagement</li>
<li>Ensures partner compliance with partner agreements</li>
<li>Sets and meets Quarterly recruitment targets for new channel partners into the A-LIGN Program</li>
<li>Channel partners report a high satisfaction rate with A-LIGN and their enablement on our products, pitch, and program</li>
<li>Newly recruited and onboarded channel partners are producing revenue in less than one quarter</li>
<li>Updated Salesforce hygiene on channel partner account statuses</li>
<li>Create Partner Portal for channel partners</li>
<li>Initiates and completes any training and development objectives, including demos, within the assigned accounts and required time frame</li>
<li>Provides weekly activity reviews highlighting performance to plan results and plans to close any gaps</li>
<li>Enlists the support of sales specialists, implementation resources, service resources, and other sales and management resources as needed</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
