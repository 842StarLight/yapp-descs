<div><b>
Position Summary</b></div>
<div>
In collaboration with sales, marketing and the product teams, the Solution Consultants are responsible for providing compelling client facing and virtual sales presentations while delivering total CDK solution positioning for our ARNA Base-Line Sales team. The SC's are connectors for cross team communication within Marketing to achieve sales goals and ensure accurate representation of CDK Products and Services.</div>
<div>
This is a home-shorted position which requires 70% travel in the Western part of the United States. The position can be located in any major city in the Western USA.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Position Responsibilities &amp; Essential Functions</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrate CDK products and services to prospects and clients in order to provide solutions for running their dealership operations more efficiently and effectively. Demonstrates the ability to communicate CDK’s product value that can be easily understood by the customer.</li>
<li>
Able to ask hurt questions that spur a dealer to further engage in the conversation and move a sale forward.</li>
<li>
Conducts OEM or dealer facing webinars.</li>
<li>
Gathers and disseminates information on sales product training, competitive information and regional sales tools.</li>
<li>
Maintain product demonstration room(s), equipment, documentation, computer system(s), and video/release library.</li>
<li>
Presents information that aligns with the sales triangle as well as Eloqui presentation techniques and is tailored for the audience.</li>
<li>
May participate in pre-demo discovery with Sales partner to build and solidify relationships as well as discover client needs.</li>
</ul>
