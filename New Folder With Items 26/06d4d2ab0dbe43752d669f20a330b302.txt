<h2>Technical Customer Support Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>The Opportunity</b>
<br>
<br>
Want to work for a company where you can have a genuine impact, be passionate about what you do, and work with a great team of people? At FastSpring, we work hard while keeping a fun, collaborative, and casual atmosphere. Headquartered in downtown Santa Barbara, our offices are just a few minutes from great restaurants, shops and the beach. If you're highly driven and want to work at an exciting, fast-growing e-commerce software company, consider joining the FastSpring team. We offer a dynamic work environment, competitive compensation, growth opportunities, benefits and more for the right candidate.
<br>
<br>
<b>The Position</b>
<br>
<br>
FastSpring is seeking an inquisitive, technologically motivated, self-directed Technical Customer Support Engineer to join our Customer Support Team. It will be your job to deliver exceptional customer support to our most valuable customers running on our ecommerce platform.
<br>
<br>
You are extremely independent and self-motivated, with the ability to ensure customer issues are resolved as expediently as possible. You have the ability to quickly assess and resolve complex problems across the FastSpring platform. You will be an integral member of our Customer Support team, and responsible for solution management of support tickets including, troubleshooting issues and/or questions, technical escalations, to liaising with product &amp; development. In this role, you'll be part of a team that values collaboration and celebrates your unique contribution.
<br>
<br>
You will be responsible for developing incremental changes, bug fixes and larger features for FastSpring's SaaS ecommerce platform, which serves the store-front, payment, digital fulfillment, and post-order needs of digital retailers worldwide.
<br>
<br>
You are a problem solver with a strong technical background that is passionate about technology and expanding your skillset. You MUST be highly motivated to meet and exceed customer expectations.
<br>
<br>
As a member of the Customer Support Team you interact with our customers daily via our ticketing system and make them better at what they do while improving our product. Being a Technical Support Engineer will require you to come up to speed on the product quickly. You will work with customers to ensure they experience the full power of our tools, providing training/adoption and working across teams to better our product.
<br>
<br>
Technical Customer Support Engineers (TCSEs) provide a crucial link between Customer Success Managers, Product Managers and Engineering. By operating in both the Engineering and Support realms, TCSEs drive the ongoing improvement of FastSpring's products, services, and user experience.
<br>
<br>
