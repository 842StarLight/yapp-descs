<h2>Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Norton Shopping Guarantee</b> is an innovative SaaS solution that helps eCommerce merchants provide their customers complete peace of mind when shopping. Merchants using the service see a significant increase in revenue and customer satisfaction on their website.</p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Norton Shopping Guarantee is seeking an experienced sales professional who will position our service to eCommerce retailers with annual online sales greater than $1 million. The Account Executive will manage the entire sales cycle (full cycle sales) from researching/identifying new merchants, pitching the service, and winning new business.</p>
<p><b>Roles &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>Research</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate eCommerce merchants that would benefit from Norton Shopping Guarantee in an ever-changing retail environment</li>
<li>Identify and prospect key decision-makers utilizing various leads databases and LinkedIn Sales Navigator</li>
<li>Leverage dynamic real-time sales &amp; marketing tools to assist in lead generation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Execution</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Actively call upon prospects and book new sales presentations on a weekly basis</li>
<li>Conduct WebEx presentations pitching the benefits of Norton Shopping Guarantee in a consultative sales approach</li>
<li>Overcome merchant objections and roadblocks in a composed, poised demeanor</li>
<li>Prioritize the needs of various opportunities and work with the internal technology team to move them through the pipeline</li>
<li>Win new business and assist Customer Success Manager with client on-boarding</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Reporting and Admin</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain accurate Salesforce reporting and monthly forecasting for internal stakeholders</li>
<li>Prepare and conduct internal reviews of pipeline and discuss roadblocks to closing new business with greater sales team</li>
</ul>
<p>---</p>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree</li>
<li>4+/- years of direct sales experience, ideally in an eCommerce, Software as a Service (SaaS), or technical environment</li>
<li>Proven track record of winning new business and executing B2B sales contracts at or above quota</li>
<li>Comfort and demonstrated effectiveness with telephone based sales</li>
<li>Analytical approach to problem solving with excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to think and react in a high-energy, fast paced work environment</li>
<li>Motivated by compensation and the ability to increase your earnings potential</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>eCommerce experience and overall knowledge of retail landscape</li>
<li>Ability to take ownership and execute on projects with minimal resources</li>
<li>Comfortability in a fast paced, constantly evolving and growing sales environment</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage multiple, competing priorities simultaneously</li>
<li>Experience with drip marketing and outbound email marketing automation via Salesforce Pardot/other sales tools</li>
<li>Knowledge of AB testing and ROI business case selling</li>
</ul>
