Job Description
<br>InfoArmor, headquartered in Scottsdale, Arizona and with offices in Chicago, Illinois, provides industry-leading identity protection and advanced threat intelligence that enable its clients to combat an ever-changing cyber threat landscape. We are passionate about our culture and offer team members rewarding opportunities in a fast-growing, dynamic environment.
<br><br>
Summary/Objective
<br>The InfoArmor Consumer Channels and Partnerships (CCP) business segment is looking to assemble a team that can help grow and scale a new business line focused on personal digital safety and identity protection, consisting of high-flying people who are strategic, creative and growth-minded. Our team consists of a diverse mix of professionals with expertise in channel development, technology, business, marketing, design, operations, and strategy.
<br><br>
The InfoArmor CCP business is expanding into new channels leveraging Allstate distribution, B2B partnerships as well as retail and digital distribution (B2B2C). This new business group is looking for a talented, experienced Client Account Manager to help us manage and grow our key clients through excellent client engagement and care. The Client Account Manager will be responsible for both overall client satisfaction as well as year over year account revenue growth.
<br><br>
Essential Functions
<br>? You are a skilled, self-motivated manager with 5 or more years of experience managing key clients for growth and excellent service, as well as launching new products and services to expand the relationship.
<br><b>? You have been in the trenches:</b> you know how to get things done. You can think strategically but can also roll up your sleeves and drive new opportunities across the line with detail and rigor garnering the support of InfoArmor leadership and support teams.
<br><b>? You are data-driven:</b> your beliefs are strong — you can come to the table with a clear point of view— but loosely held — what you think shifts in light of new data. You are able to compare various forms of data with a qualitative market perspective (lots of informative conversations within your account) and strong business instincts to frame a new opportunity, prioritize it and make the case it’s the next big bet.
<br><b>? You are a ‘bringing clarity out of chaos’ thinker:</b> you can help clear the fog, synthesize dozens of bits of insight and weave together a coherent, winning proposal that captures the needs of the client and their customers, and best utilizes the strengths of InfoArmor and Allstate.
<br><b>? You have a growth mind-set:</b> you are mature, curious, resilient, and can take the long view. You know, from experience, how tough it is to get something across the line, and then to manage it through its full lifecycle in order to extract its greatest value.
<br><b>? You are a team player:</b> you enjoy working cross-functionally in order to leverage your teammate’s strengths and experience.
<br><b>? The fine print:</b> you have a related degree (business, marketing, etc.). Ideal candidates have experience in one or more of the following: consultative technology sales, complex retail channel development, digital distribution, affinity marketing.
