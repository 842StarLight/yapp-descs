<h2>Manager of Purchasing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Leading wine and spirits distributor M.S. Walker is seeking a full-time Manager of Purchasing to direct and lead our Purchasing Team.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the oversight and execution of all direct procurement and inbound logistics activities for wine/spirit/water brands from approximately 500 global suppliers</li>
<li>Manage and mentor a dynamic team of purchasing and logistics professionals</li>
<li>Establish and maintain key performance indicators (KPI) for all levels of procurement activities to include, but not limited to, supplier performance, order placement performance, planner activity levels and overall performance, product financial performance (days on hand, aged inventory, laid-in cost control)</li>
<li>Analyze periodic and monthly sales data against inventory asset positions to ensure execution within the guidelines of ownership</li>
<li>Foster an environment that emphasizes inter-departmental communication by working with team members in both one-on-one and group settings</li>
<li>Require adherence to all Federal/State regulations and procedures to maintain legal compliance within the beverage alcohol industry
</li>
<li>Assist the director of supply chain to maintain the relationships with industry associations, customs brokers, freight service providers and last mile logistics teams to obtain timely and cost-effective services</li>
<li>Collaborate and encourage synergies with Brand Management team to validate customer demand, identify sales goals, establish depletion forecasts, perform product requirements planning and mitiage potential issues within the supply chain enterprise.</li>
<li>Identify items to Brand Management team that have either slow-moving and obsolete inventory or excessive backorder accruals to mitigate inventory issues
</li>
<li>Play a strategic role in fostering relationships with suppliers to meet enterprise requirements (forecast changes, supply chain concerns, product reliability issues)</li>
<li>Track service level agreements with suppliers to ensure adherence to shipment plans
</li>
<li>Understand, teach and apply the tenants of time-phased inventory planning; ensure supply chain plans sufficiently support sales forecasts and target dates</li>
<li>Implement procurement plans in support of sales goals and depletion forecasts</li>
<li>Aggregate internal sources of demand from all spokes of the corporate enterprise to build rolled-up forecasts and requirement plans</li>
<li>Define and maintain par levels and lead times for each supplier</li>
<li>Work with warehouse operations staff to assist with receiving and stock rotation issues</li>
<li>Assist internal A/R team to resolve invoice, billing, and credit memo discrepancies
</li>
<li>Direct internal Customer Service team to facilitate product returns and transfers</li>
<li>Crosstrain coworkers to provide coverage for tasks/responsibilities, as necessary</li>
<li>Oversee a subset of suppliers to perform demand planning function and generate purchase orders for submission to suppliers</li>
<li>Other related activities, as called upon by management/ownership</li>
</ul>
