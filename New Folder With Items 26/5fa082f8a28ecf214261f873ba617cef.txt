<h2>Associate Procurement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>Kautex - a division of Textron (TXT) - is headquartered in Bonn, Germany, and rated among the TOP 100 Automotive Suppliers worldwide. Kautex is a leading global supplier of plastic fuel systems, selective catalytic reduction systems (SCR) and clear vision systems and sensor cleaning solutions. Furthermore, the company produces and supplies camshafts and industrial packaging. Kautex operates in 14 countries with more than 6,000 employees.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Be globally responsible for the cost analysis and cost understanding of main bought-in products
</li>
<li>Ensure the understanding of component pricing within the organization</li>
<li>Assist Program Purchasing and Commodity Leads to understand costs associated with quality / part design / supply chain</li>
<li>Support innovative, cost-conscious solutions together with Purchasing, Engineering, Sales and other program functions</li>
<li>Further development of "should cost" calculation tools</li>
<li>Drive use of cost estimation tools e.g. through training of Buyers worldwide</li>
<li>Develop cost reduction ideas through close collaboration with different functions within the organization</li>
<li>Generate total landed costs improvements through initialization of workshops at suppliers</li>
<li>Identify and execute cost optimizations within the commodities</li>
<li>Understand the tooling cost for bought-in components</li>
<li>Execute tooling audits and evaluate deviation between invoiced and KT estimated cost</li>
</ul>
</div>
