<div><b>
Responsibilities &amp; Skills / Competencies</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Account Development Representative is responsible for successful outbound lead generation and qualification by actively engaging in outreach to potential new customers. The position is ideal for an individual interested in developing their enterprise technology sales skills, and requires someone who is persistent, flexible, and willing to learn. The Account Development Representative will act as vital member of the Account Development and Marketing teams, reporting to the Director, Account Development.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities include:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
90% Account Development
</b></div>
<div>Work closely with Marketing, Sales, and Alliance teams to establish effective lead follow up for ongoing campaigns, events, webinars, targeted account lists, and other activities. Produce measurable, qualified new business opportunities that contribute directly to revenue goals.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li> Identify qualified opportunities and schedule sales meetings for Account Executives.</li>
<li> Qualify leads as well as prospect into ideal organizations.</li>
<li> Effectively communicate the value of Secure-24 to prospects, driving engagement and opportunity development.</li>
<li> Invest time daily corresponding and engaging with senior leaders of large organizations via the phone, email, LinkedIn, and other outreach methods.</li>
<li> Manage and monitor work using CRM, sales automation tools, and other technologies.</li>
</ul>

10% </b><b>Travel/</b><b>Sales</b><b>/Event </b><b>Support</b>
