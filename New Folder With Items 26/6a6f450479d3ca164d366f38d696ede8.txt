<h2>INSIDE SALES REPRESENTATIVE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>D-Link Systems, Inc. (“D-Link") is a global leader in the design, manufacture and marketing of advanced networking, broadband, digital, voice and data communications solutions. Following our company motto, “Building Networks for People", D-Link continually meets the global networking and connectivity needs of digital home consumers, small office professionals, small to medium-sized businesses, and enterprise environments.</p>
<p>Join our team at D-Link as our technology makes new advances and changes the world! D-Link is looking for people who enjoy challenges and cutting-edge technology. If you are looking for a culture that supports the expression of ideas, then look no further!</p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>This job is located in the Sales Division of D-Link Systems in the Fountain Valley California Office</p>
<p><b>What We Are Looking For</b></p>
<p>As an Inside Sales Representative (ISR) you will be responsible for achieving revenue based sales quotes through prospecting, cold calling, identifying and qualifying opportunities within the assigned territory. This position is highly collaborative, requiring you build strong business relationships with The D-Link outside sales teams, sales engineers, partner sales reps, “C" Level to take deals through to close. This is a high-energy position, and the pace is quick, so you must be extremely organized and be able to manage and build a territory pipeline of up to 150 opportunities at one time. The ISR will manage all aspects of the sales cycle while remotely interfacing with customers. You should be comfortable making dozens of phone calls per day and closing a high volume of deals.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage new and existing business in assigned territory</li>
<li>Manage entire sales process from prospecting to close, including accurate pipeline forecasting and achieving sales quotas</li>
<li>Cultivate, grow andenable partners to successfully close D-Link business</li>
<li>Understand customer needs and requirements</li>
<li>Expertly demonstrate D-Link technology via use of cutting-edge technology to end users and channel partners</li>
<li>Aggressively prospect with your sales team through cold calls, emails, and events</li>
<li>Assist your outside sales team member in setting up meetings, finding value, producing quotes, and closing deals</li>
<li>Own your territory, take responsibility of production along with your corresponding team members</li>
<li>Consistently achieve quotas to ensure revenue objectives</li>
<li>Educate and qualify any inbound prospect to turn into ready to close deals</li>
<li>Interact with prospects via telephone, live chat, and email</li>
<li>Update all interactions in salesforce.com to ensure continuous process optimization</li>
<li>Become a trusted resource and develop superior relationships with prospects</li>
<li>Stay up to date on networking, wireless and IP Surveillance technologies, buyer personas, market trends, and competitors</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you’ll bring to the table: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>1+ years of B2B sales experience in a revenue closing role or 1+ years of Channel sales experience and an understanding of a 2 tier distribution model.</li>
<li>Proven track record of quota attainment in an inside sales capacity</li>
<li>The love for IT and ability to tap into technology</li>
<li>Coachable and hungry to learn key information in D-Link Product offerings</li>
<li>Experience with Point of Sale reporting and how to identify partner purchasing trends</li>
<li>Positive and energetic phone skills, excellent listening skills, strong written communication skills</li>
<li>Must have a proven track record of success in achieving measurable monthly lead development/sales goals</li>
<li>Ability to interact effectively with customers and team members to solve issues and quickly address concerns</li>
<li>Knowledge of Salesforce CRM software preferred</li>
<li>Strong work ethic with high energy and initiative</li>
<li>Must have the ability to understand and convey business issues and technical concepts</li>
<li>Highly detail oriented and precise in work</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and prioritize</li>
<li>Travel to territory on an as needed basis</li>
<li>Hunter mentality a must</li>
<li>BA / BS Preferred</li>
</ul>
