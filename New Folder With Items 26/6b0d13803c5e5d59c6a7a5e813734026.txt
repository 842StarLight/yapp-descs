<h2>Director of Commercial Business</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Responsibilities (Essential Job Functions)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish short and long-range goals for business development projects by identifying and prioritizing development opportunities to maximize aeronautical and non-aeronautical revenues and increase customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Leverage industry expertise, best practices, and key business drivers in the development of all short and long range plans and define results.</li>
<li>Identify and capitalize on business opportunities, both short and long-term.</li>
<li>Maintain flexibility to adapt priorities based on changing business needs.
</li>
<li>Execute the assigned strategic plan goals for the District.
</li>
<li>Establish and implement strategy to attract new commercial business operators, retain and secure growth from existing aeronautical and non-aeronautical tenants.
</li>
<li>Seek and secure cargo customers and users.
</li>
<li>Seek and secure general aviation/corporate customers and users.
</li>
<li>Develop and implement various pricing strategies for District revenue bases.</li>
<li>Oversee the implementation of commercial business programs and projects.</li>
<li>Represent the District as the business development leader by advocating, communicating, and supporting the strategic direction, priorities, goals, vision, mission, and values of the District.</li>
<li>Interact with developers, real estate professionals, business and property owners, lenders, community groups, legal counsel, elected officials, and citizen groups concerning development projects</li>
<li>Manage the development of RFP and RFQ documents for use of airport property or the operation of a business enterprise on airport property.
</li>
<li>Oversee and follow the policies and procedures of the District with regard to the negotiation, preparation, and processing of agreements.</li>
<li>Oversee the contract and lease development process for use of airport property or the operation of a business enterprise on airport property.</li>
<li>Oversee the business provisions of airline, concessions, rental car, and non-aeronautical agreements.
</li>
<li>Develop quality standards and apply those standards to agreements and policies to ensure that business partners meet or exceed customer service and operational excellence objectives.</li>
<li>Determine and manage the department budget and provide support and guidance related to airport expenditures and budgets.</li>
<li>Provide effective leadership, support, and development of the department employees, including performance management, coaching, training, motivation, and discipline.</li>
<li>Develop and submit timely and accurate reports. Manage the department toward achievement of budgeted revenue, net operating income, &amp; profitability targets. Enact corrective measures when unexpected costs or reduced revenues occur.
</li>
<li>Support air service development efforts, which include development of initiatives to align the Airport with business community stakeholder needs, while building solid business cases for local businesses to use the Airport.</li>
<li>Oversee assigned business continuity and emergency management responsibilities.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain computerized customer and prospect databases.</li>
<li>Develop and present commercial business development presentations.</li>
<li>Oversee the terminal building advertising sales program.</li>
<li>Manage frequent trade show coordination and preparation including attendance at various overnight/out of town events.</li>
<li>Manage frequent business development events including attendance at various evening/weekend events.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
