<h2>Service Delivery Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We’re growing fast and we’re looking for people who love to build things: products, companies, markets, and their careers! Think you have what it takes to work on one of the best teams in the industry? Read on.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b></h3>
<p>
ExtraHop’s Solutions Architecture team focuses on successful outcomes for our clients rather than racking up billable hours. As a Service Delivery Coordinator for this team, you will play a critical role in accomplishing these goals.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with Training and Sales teams to coordinate scheduling of short-term training engagements.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Solutions Architects and clients to schedule appropriate resources based on project requirements and availability.</li>
<li>
Generate recurring project status reports for Accounting and upper management.</li>
<li>
Ability to work with people at all levels of an organization, including different client personalities.</li>
<li>
Detail oriented and process driven, with strong administrative skills.</li>
<li>
Run project kickoffs and generate meeting notes.</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new tools and adapt new concepts as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Required Qualifications and Experience</b></h3>
<p>
We’re looking for a Service Delivery Coordinator who can help us grow to the next level as a company. As a starting point for this position, you’ll need the following background:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Associate’s degree or equivalent experience in Business or Information Technology</li>
<li>
Minimum of 1-3 years experience with Project Coordination</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge of Information Technology networking principles and application protocols</li>
<li>
Intermediate level skills with Microsoft Excel, must be familiar with formulas</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Excellent written communication skills</li>
<li>
Experience with or knowledge of Accounting and Billing is a plus</li>
</ul>
