<h2>Systems Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>What You'll Do</div>
<div>We are looking for Senior Systems Engineer for our Enterprise team to support customers in the New Jersey and Pennsylvania area. In this role, You will partner with our Enterprise account team in a pre-sales technical role. Your primary responsibilities include understanding Customer's network architecture, CXO priorities, and map Cisco products and solutions to help them solve business problems and achieve goals. You will be translating their business needs in to technology - Setting up Technical demonstrations, explaining features and benefits to customers and crafting and configuring products and services. Actively participate as a specialist and provides consultative support in their area of specialization to other team members.</div>
 <div>You will establish and maintain customer relationships at the executive / business decision maker level.</div>
 <div>You will develop &amp; present compelling business cases to customers, maintain expert-level knowledge of all elements of the business case, including the drivers of cost, benefit and risk.</div>
 <div>You will partner closely with Customer Engineers/Architects to align the technology architecture to the customer's desired business outcome and ensure customer success by orchestrating resources to assist the customer in realizing the full value of their technology investment.</div>
 <div>You will share detailed execution plans and progress with field leadership and account teams.</div>
 <div>You will enhance Cisco's solution portfolio over time, focused around key customer requirements and aligned with Cisco products and solutions.</div>
 <div>Engage, advise and mentor sales teams on Customer Experience imperatives and business value based consultative selling approaches</div>
<br>

Consistently striving to improve and reinvent yourself. This role allows flexibility, customer engagement, groundbreaking technology and career mobility.</div>

 <div>You have self-management skills to drive our business, and you are a team player.</div>
 <div>You have a proven CxO-level relationship experience</div>
