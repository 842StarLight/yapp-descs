<h2>Production Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>ASSA ABLOY is the largest global supplier of door opening solutions. The Group is represented on both mature and emerging markets worldwide, with leading positions in much of Europe, North America and Asia Pacific. ASSA ABLOY was formed in 1994 through the merger of ASSA in Sweden and Abloy in Finland. Since then ASSA ABLOY has grown from a regional company into an international group with around 47,000 employees and annual sales of SEK 71 billion. We are dedicated to improving our customers' lives through security, safety and convenience.</div>
<div></div>
<div>This Production Manager utilizes cross functional leadership, technical knowledge and communication skills to ensure customers receive a quality product, while meeting on time performance goals and continually striving to improve operational efficiencies and overall performance.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Plan, direct, and coordinate the manufacturing of products in line with company goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Develop a strong and effective team. Provide positive and objective coaching of team members.</li>
<li>Implement a team structure to grow and support the business.</li>
<li>Track employee performance to established standards and Company requirements and provide feedback on performance within a timely manner. Complete structured review forms as required.</li>
<li>Provide employees with counseling as necessary or corrective disciplinary action per Company Policy and processes.</li>
<li>Review and approve assigned employees schedules and time records daily and keep timekeeping records up to date to ensure accurate records and reporting. Submit other payment requests as needed.</li>
<li>Audit employee's quality and compliance to established work methods. Provide feedback or corrective action if necessary. Review job methods and ISO Procedures and Work Instructions for their areas of assigned responsibility.</li>
<li>Participate in or lead initiatives directed employee involvement and engagement.</li>
<li>Focus on results and achieve shipment goals (revenue and OTD).</li>
<li>Deliver on targets for key performance metrics: quality, cost/productivity, safety, LEAN/5S, inventory and on-time delivery.</li>
<li>Organize and direct workflow to meet production requirements in a cost-efficient manner.</li>
<li>Promote and drive compliance to all standard operating procedures.</li>
<li>Drive superior customer service (on-time delivery and responsiveness).</li>
<li>Advance a culture of continuous improvement by applying lean manufacturing, 5S and other appropriate techniques.</li>
<li>Promote environmental, health and safety philosophies and processes that reduce workplace injuries and environmental impact.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications/Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional leadership and decision-making skills</li>
<li>Strong leadership presence to motivate, influence, and train people</li>
<li>Lean practices and tools - able to move from the theoretical to practical implementation</li>
<li>Able to interact with a variety of company functions and a diverse group of personalities</li>
<li>Solid interpersonal and communication skills, both written and verbal, including the ability to interact well with customers</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, high growth industry; willing to be hands-on and take initiative</li>
<li>Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>High sense of urgency</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office suite</li>
<li>Proficient with ERP software and reporting tools; JDE preferred</li>
</ul>
