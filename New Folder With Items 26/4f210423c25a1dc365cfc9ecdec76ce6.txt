<h2>Sales Executive, Commercial Mid-Market - Philadelphia, PA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Position Description Overview</b></div>
<div>
This position is an individual contributor Sales position to be located in the Philadelphia, PA metropolitan area or in Southern New Jersey and will be responsible for selling Fiserv's enterprise suite of business solutions, including products such as Fiserv's Point-of-Sale solution (Clover), Security &amp; Compliance (TransArmor), On-Line Reporting (ClientLine), Check and ACH solutions (TeleCheck), Payroll Distribution services (Money Network), credit, debit, gift card and various cloud-based applications. Territory associated with this position is the state of Pennsylvania with primary focus on the Philadelphia metropolitan market, but also including Central Pennsylvania, South New Jersey and Central New Jersey markets.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position offers a tiered compensation structure which includes:</div>
<ul>
<li>Competitive base salary</li>
<li>Multi-year residuals</li>
<li>Commissions</li>
<li>New Hire commission floor</li>
<li>Annual achievement bonuses, including equity</li>
<li>Benefits including medical, dental, vision, 401K, etc.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Develop strong working partnerships with Commercial Bankers and Treasury Sales Officers for assigned Financial Institution partners with an objective of increasing volume and quality of referrals and leads from existing bank portfolios.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with senior Commercial Bank relationship teams to develop comprehensive and holistic sales strategies for strategic bank clients to maximize revenue opportunities with these high value clients.</li>
<li>
Self-sourcing of referrals and leads will be a requirement; therefore in addition to leveraging the assets and resources of Fiserv, this position requires experience and skills in prospecting, territory development, and networking.</li>
<li>
Under the direction of the Director of Sales, position will drive execution of departmental sales and revenue objectives including, but not limited to, pricing and profitability guidelines as well as individual contributor sales goals.</li>
<li>
Experience and expertise with CRM tools such as Salesforce is required as discipline, integrity and accountability in sales pipeline management is a requirement of this position.</li>
</ul>
