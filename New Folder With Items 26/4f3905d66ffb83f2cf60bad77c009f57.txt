<h2>HCIT Systems Administrator I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The HCIT Systems Administrator provides delivery, consultation, support, and technology solutions based on customer needs. They are responsible for servers and applications on premise, hosted or in the cloud. This role is focused on the management of customer environments including patching, monitoring, backups &amp; disaster recovery. This position will also have responsibility for some aspects of IT security. The position requires the management of projects, priorities and deliverables for Business Operation and support and will work with his manager to define project/task tracking and reporting, or other analytics as needed.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to support a robust and redundant server environment.</li>
<li>Support of both Microsoft &amp; Linux servers</li>
<li>Support Physical and virtual server environments</li>
<li>Manage customer patching and monitoring solutions</li>
<li>Manage Cloud backup solution</li>
<li>Manage &amp; Support Disaster recovery solutions</li>
<li>Field escalations from support on performance and application issues</li>
<li>Develop, maintain, and prepare documentation related to management of applications, including but not limited to: installation, maintenance, upgrades, patches, and support.</li>
<li>Ensure system documentation follows best practices, is up-to-date, accurate, and tested.</li>
<li>Manage Tradeshow and Demo equipment for sales team</li>
<li>Participation in the development of policies and procedures, which govern the applications.</li>
<li>Evaluate and get to root-cause on complex or difficult technical problems and escalate.</li>
<li>Support Helpdesk team and provide guidance and direction for problem resolution.</li>
<li>Follow detailed procedures while making recommendations for routine problem solutions.</li>
<li>Promote a positive customer relationship while working with other business teams and technical staff and departments.</li>
<li>Work with team to develop and maintain database backup &amp; recovery</li>
<li>Evaluate and make recommendations for current environment configurations and business processes</li>
</ul>
