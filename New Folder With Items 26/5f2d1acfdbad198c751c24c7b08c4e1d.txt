<h2>Sr. Digital Marketing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Brightline provides a unique and enriching opportunity to be a part of a new transportation alternative that is keenly focused on hospitality and guest service. The Brightline Marketing team plays a key role in supporting the sales efforts of the organization.</p>
<p>
As the senior digital marketer in the organization, you will create, lead and measure the implementation of digital marketing campaigns, as well as the development of the brand experience across all digital channels. You will assess marketing goals, provide insight, create digital plans and guide your team to achieve measurable goals.</p>
<p>
Essential Job Functions:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Build a high-performance digital team capable of driving digital traffic and lead gen required to meet the aggressive revenue goals across multiple segments</li>
<li>Monitor daily analytics: establish KPIs and metrics to demonstrate your impact on the business and report results in cohesive format to C-suite and ownership</li>
<li>Plan, design and manage digital experiences including website, email/CRM, content marketing, digital advertising, SEO and paid search, and social media</li>
<li>Work with internal executives, across multiple segments, to understand goals and timelines</li>
<li>Contribute as a key stakeholder in e-commerce decisions and roadmap</li>
<li>Hire, manage and train a team of internal digital specialists to drive traffic required to meet the organization's aggressive sales and revenue goals</li>
<li>Assist in scoping omni-channel campaigns, determine resources required and coordinate assignments to digital team</li>
<li>Ensuring positive internal client satisfaction by proactively keeping marketing leaders aware of issues and seeking clarification on any priorities</li>
<li>Hiring and supervising internal/external agency digital staff, setting KPI expectations, evaluating performance, maintaining training</li>
<li>Identify, evaluate, and implement new technologies</li>
<li>We are a new business model – agile and evolving. The job description isn’t meant to be a complete list of your qualifications or all the things you’ll do.</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>7+ years experience managing digital marketing/ecommerce for an early-stage growth company</li>
<li>Proven success developing and implementing integrated digital marketing campaigns with measurable results</li>
<li>Deep expertise with digital media planning, buying and analytics, including digital media, social media, programmatic media, and influencer campaigns</li>
<li>Previous experience managing a team of digital specialists across SEO, SEM, social, web and email</li>
<li>Comfortable with accountability for metrics and revenue</li>
<li>Ability to command a room of senior executives with strong presentation skills</li>
<li>Multitasks, prioritizes and meets deadlines in timely manner</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Excel, Word, PowerPoint</li>
<br>
</ul>
