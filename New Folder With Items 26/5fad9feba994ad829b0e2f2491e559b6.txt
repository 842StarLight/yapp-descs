<h2>Marketing Communications Manager Mgmt</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Microsoft is on a mission to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. Our culture is centered on embracing a growth mindset, a theme of inspiring excellence, and encouraging teams and leaders to bring their best each day. Growth mindset encourages each of us to lean in and learn what matters most to our customers, to create the foundational knowledge that enables us to make customer-first decisions in everything we do. In doing so, we create life-changing innovations that impact billions of lives around the world. You can help us achieve our mission.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are looking for a seasoned Integrated Marketing Lead (IML) to join the Central Marketing Organization (CMO) in a key leadership role responsible for integrated marketing working with the key discipline teams within CMO and across the business with the Business Groups (BGs), Communications/PR, Partner Organization (OCP), and Segments/Sales.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Central Marketing Organization (CMO) leads integrated marketing excellence in planning and execution across commercial Solution Areas, consumer business, segments including SMB and Partner, and Marketing engines to drive business outcomes for the Area. With integrated marketing excellence, events &amp; digital marketing expertise, and data-driven insights, CMO delivers quality customer and partner experiences at scale to maximize marketing impact across our connected customer journeys in alignment with global strategies and local business priorities. CMO embraces and promotes Global Demand Center (GDC) and Global Engagement Program (GEP) alignment and drives Area marketing governance with Sales (including CDS), Solution Areas, Communications, and OCP. CMO builds clear linkage with Area priorities such as Customer Adds, Consumption, Skilling and Country. In everything we do, CMO models our Microsoft leadership values, and nurtures a diverse and inclusive team.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Integrated Marketing Lead (IML) manages and leads a group of integrated marketing managers responsible for delivering business impact by implementing and augmenting global strategy through Global Demand Centers (GDCs) and Global Engagement Programs (GEPs) to deliver integrated local marketing programs that drive demand. Your team drives marketing communications planning, execution, and optimization across the buyer journey for our Azure, Modern Workplace, Biz Apps, and Digital Transformation/Industry solutions. You lead the connection between CMO and our internal Sales teams (Enterprise and SMC) and should drive marketing execution integration and alignment with the Partner team (OCP). You drive your Area and team to smartly maximize local marketing investments by fully exploiting all global content and marketing services, and augmenting local work aligned to global strategies.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Key local marketing stakeholders include the Business Group Leaders, Communications/PR, Enterprise and SMC Sales leaders and Partner organization leaders. You also partner closely with the CMO Shared Services leads in Events, Digital and Data Insights to ensure a connected customer journey. Your professional partnership and network extends to the Marketing Organization at Microsoft Corporate Headquarters.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The ideal candidate will be a strong performance marketer and a strong advocate for marketing when working with Segments/Sales organization. You have a strong customer focus with a drive to deliver the best solutions for our customers. You must enjoy being continually challenged in a multifaceted, matrixed and constantly changing environment. This is a management leadership role that combines strong business thinking, seasoned marketing leadership with Modern Marketing experience, Digital Marketing Competency, Marketing Automation foundations, and the ability to masterfully leverage global programs in your area.
</div>
