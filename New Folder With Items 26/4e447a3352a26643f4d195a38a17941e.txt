<h2>Sr. Producer Relations Liasion (Health Alliance Plan)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>To process commissions for the contracted/appointed producers that sell HAP’s commissionable products. Products include HAP and Alliance Health and Life, Inc. individual &amp; group fully-insured, Medicare supplement and Medicare Advantage. Managing the relationships and expectations of the appointed/contracted producers that sells HAP commission able product lines.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
PRINCIPLE DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Process appropriate files, fall-out reports and system flows to create monthly commission payments, statements and administrative fee payouts. Analyze commission report to determine over/underpayments.</li>
<li>
Maintain compliance requirements, process improvements, job aids, and flow charts for the department.</li>
<li>
Submit and oversee trouble tickets with external vendors to ensure issues are resolved.</li>
<li>
Update the Commission Call Center Statistical Analysis Report on a daily basis to track agent issues, system issues and problem resolution.</li>
<li>
Attend weekly meetings with the Commission Team, providing system and process updates.</li>
<li>
Update Producer Relations Management to resolve critical commission and Medicare administrative fee issues. Work toward resolving producer issues.</li>
<li>
Post on-line commission statements, create producer log-ins for access for the producer portal.</li>
<li>
Responsible for customer service for the preferred producers, managing agency (MA) HAA and individual products GAs. Responding to their inquiries and requests, regarding commissions as well as initiating dialog regarding accuracy and satisfaction. Review agency commissions audits with the principal producer.</li>
<li>
Process the implementation of mass commission schedule changes and business rules in commission systems. Work with IT to ensure the accurate deployment of configuration that supports commissions’ monthly operations and new releases of Facets.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the submission of commission’s system enhancement service requests.</li>
<li>
Partner with Accounts Receivable, M&amp;B, BCT, and Group Operations to resolve group issues, plan group updates, audit commissions and process manual adjustment.</li>
<li>
Collaborate and support vendor relationships and implementation of vendor activities. Provide assistance overseeing vendor performance. Collaborate with business partners in the creations and review of UAT test scenarios for system upgrades for InfoCRM, Facets, Pega, Colibrium and new HAP systems.</li>
<li>
Prepare department reports and assist with data analysis and data management related activities. Manage data needs and develop and run queries and reports as needed.</li>
<li>
Manage special commission projects, spreadsheets and presentations for the department.</li>
<li>
Perform other related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul></div>

Excellent verbal, written, and communication skills.</li>

Excellent time management skills to coordinate high volume of activities.</li>
<li>
High energy, strong leadership, analytical, project planning and staff motivation/coordination skills to enable efficient, timely task completion of deliverables that exceed expectations.</li>

Self-motivated, tactful and able to make decisions.</li>
