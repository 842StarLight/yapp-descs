<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a Marketing Specialist, you will work across functions and departments to bring our financial solutions to life. We’re looking for an enthusiastic team member to support overall marketing efforts. You will be an integral part executing strategic marketing plans so our organization can reach our goals from increasing brand awareness to yielding growth through meaningful sales support activities. Reporting to the Head of Marketing, you will help drive the overall strategy of the organization.</p>
<p></p>
<p><br>
The <b>Financial Services unit of Siemens (SFS) </b>is an international provider of business-to-business financial solutions. SFS helps facilitate investments, providing commercial finance, project and structured finance and leveraged finance solutions with specific asset expertise in the energy, healthcare, industry, and infrastructure &amp; cities markets. Employing more than 3,300 employees worldwide, SFS supports Siemens as well as other companies with their capital needs and acts as an expert manager of financial risks within the Siemens Group. As of September 30, 2018, the total assets amounted to $31.9 billion. Siemens Financial Services, Inc. undertakes activities for SFS in the U.S.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in outbound marketing activities, including developing and deploying marketing materials to customers to increase overall brand awareness (i.e. content development, strategic customer campaigns, promotion of customer success stories, etc.)</li>
<li>
Liaise with internal stakeholders to execute strategies and project manage target events</li>
<li>
Speak and present both internally and externally to promote our value proposition</li>
<li>
Coordinate with sales teams to develop necessary outputs to support sales campaigns through appropriate channels including pricing promotions, product launches, internal offerings, expansion efforts, etc.
</li>
<li>Reporting to the Head of Marketing, you will be responsible for individual tasks of marketing plans as assigned, complete marketing related reporting and contribute to budget planning,</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Knowledge/Skills, Education, and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
3-5 years of proven experience as marketing specialist or similar role</p></li>
<li><p>
BSc/BA in marketing, communications or equivalent</p></li>
<li><p>
Limited (20%) expected travel</p></li>
<li><p>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills - there is a heavy amount of writing and presenting/selling ideas in this role.</p></li>
<li><p>
Thorough understanding of marketing elements (including traditional and digital marketing such as Social media, e-mail campaigns, etc.) and market research methods</p></li>
<li><p>
Well-organized, detail oriented and strong executor</p></li>
<li><p>
Comfortable working in a collaborative, cross-team capacity. This role require you work across functions and departments to bring our solutions to life. You will be constantly interacting with teammates and stakeholders.</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Preferred Knowledge/Skills, Education, and Experience</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Solid computer skills, including MS Office, marketing software (Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Salesforce, Microsoft Publisher, Microsoft PowerPoint, Yammer etc.) and applications (Web analytics, Tableau, etc.)</p></li>
<li><p>
Knowledge of HTML, and web development tools (e.g. Eloqua) desired</p></li>
<li><p>
Commercial awareness partnered with a creative mind</p></li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Siemens AG</b> (Berlin and Munich) is a global technology powerhouse that has stood for engineering excellence, innovation, quality, reliability and internationality for more than 170 years. The company is active around the globe, focusing on the areas of power generation and distribution, intelligent infrastructure for buildings and distributed energy systems, and automation and digitalization in the process and manufacturing industries. Through the separately managed company Siemens Mobility, a leading supplier of smart mobility solutions for rail and road transport, Siemens is shaping the world market for passenger and freight services. Due to its majority stakes in the publicly listed companies Siemens Healthineers AG and Siemens Gamesa Renewable Energy, Siemens is also a world-leading supplier of medical technology and digital healthcare services as well as environmentally friendly solutions for onshore and offshore wind power generation. In fiscal 2018, which ended on September 30, 2018, Siemens generated revenue of €83.0 billion and net income of €6.1 billion. At the end of September 2018, the company had around 379,000 employees worldwide. Further information is available on the Internet at www.siemens.com.</p>
