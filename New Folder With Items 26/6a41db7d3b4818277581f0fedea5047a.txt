<h2>Senior Account Executive - Food Services Industry</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>DAR PRO Solutions is a U.S. based brand of Darling Ingredients focusing on providing professional waste cooking oil services to restaurants, supermarkets and others in the hospitality or food service industry. DAR PRO Solutions, through its network of facilities, provides customers with proprietary waste cooking oil equipment along with superior collection services.
</p>
<p><b>Job Summary
</b></p>
<p>The Senior Account Executive interacts with top-tier current &amp; prospective customers while assisting Account Executives in maintaining &amp; growing relationships through customer contact. A strong emphasis is put on prompt customer service and solicitation of potential customers including restaurants and food service establishments.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties/Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Profitable customer growth along with retention of high volume and multi-unit accounts in territory to meet or exceed company goals/objectives. Establishes new accounts by planning and organizing daily work schedule to call on existing or potential customers.</li>
<li>
Enhances sales staff accomplishments and competence by answering technical and procedural questions for less experienced team members; teaching improved processes; mentoring &amp; coaching.</li>
<li>
Understand product values; evaluate profitability of new and existing customers.</li>
<li>
Identify market opportunities through prospecting and coordinating activities with Inside Sales department.</li>
<li>
Develop and implement sales strategies and follow through to successful contract negotiation; prepare and execute service agreements.</li>
<li>
Utilize <i>Salesforce</i> to full capacity for in depth customer analysis</li>
<li>
All other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma or GED required.</li>
<li>
Minimum 3 years of experience in <b>restaurant industry sales</b> required.</li>
<li>
Valid Driver's License: clean driving record and personal vehicle are a must</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree in a business-related field preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience dealing with Corporate and large franchise operators is preferred.
</li>
<li>
<i>Salesforce</i> software experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel and Office.</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new programs.</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written skills.</li>
<li>
Work well with others in a team-based environment.</li>
<li>
Communicates clearly both verbally or in written form; excellent analytical skills.</li>
<li>
Leadership ability; able to direct personnel.
</li>
<li>Requires minimum supervision; makes timely, accurate judgments regarding production &amp; process quality.</li>
<li>
Highly organized; superior time management skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Physical Demands</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Frequently required to walk, stand, sit, climb, balance, stoop, bend, and kneel</li>
<li>
Frequently lifts up to 10 lbs.</li>
<li>
Long periods spent sitting at desk in front of computer screen.</li>
<li>
Regularly required reach with hands and arms.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Work Environment</b></p>
<p>
Job functions will be carried out in an office environment, plant environment, customer locations and vehicle.
