<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<br>
<div>
<b>Position Overview:</b><br>
<br>
Are you prepared to be enabled with the most relevant hardware and software in the Security Industry? Does your drive for success include personal development of engineering, technical and project management knowledge?
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>The Security Technician I Position is an entry level career position responsible for customer satisfaction.
</div></li>
<li><div>The primary goal of the position is to provide technical support, programming and commissioning of Siemens and third party security systems.
</div></li>
<li><div>The position is responsible to the Field Supervisor. The Field Supervisor includes QA/QC of rough-in, installation of devices and final terminations.<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Provide on-site field activities to include programming, startup and final commissioning.</div>
</li>
<li><div>Assist in supervising subcontractors.
</div></li>
<li><div>Provide exceptional customer service.
</div></li>
<li><div>Participates in sales to operations turnover meetings to evaluate accuracy of the sales estimate and job layout.
</div></li>
<li><div>May participate in initial job site walk through with contractors and/or building management.
</div></li>
<li><div>Analyzes plans, construction contract specifications, and codes to determine project requirements and risks; provides value engineering ideas and details.<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Required Knowledge/Skill, Education, and Experience:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li><p>Required Education - High School Diploma
</p></li>
<li>
<p>1+ years experience in the installation and programming of Security systems is preferred</p>
</li>
<li><p>Excellent verbal and written communication skills in English.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to work in the U.S. without a need for current or future sponsorship.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be at least 21 years old to participate in required Siemens vehicle plan, and must have a valid driver's license in good standing.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to read and understand design and construction documents.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to work in a variety of circumstances including climbing ladders, scaffolds and high-lift equipment as well as working in ducts, crawl spaces, and above ceilings.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be able to differentiate types/colors of wire.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Must be able to use hand-tools.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Must be able to carry and move equipment and tools weighing up to 50 pounds unassisted.</p></li>
</ul>
