<h2>Implementation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary:
</b></p>Want to join one of the nation's best employers? Interested in providing small and medium sized businesses with solutions that make them grow? Do you like serving customers and care about people? Then maybe Heartland is for you:
<br>
<br>
We're looking for team members who are excited by our mantra - Entrepreneurs Respectfully Serving Entrepreneurs, and who want to work in a culture built on care, focus and quality.
<br>
<br>
We need customer-focused, can-do, team-oriented folks who are ok having fun.
<br>
<br>
HR &amp; Benefits Implementation Specialists are experts on the Human Resources Information Systems (HRIS) and benefits solutions offered by Heartland Payroll Solutions. They work closely with clients and sales professionals to demonstrate product features and functions. They work directly with new and existing clients to setup and maintain online client content, and they may work with third party benefits providers on behalf of Heartland clients.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Details:
</b></p>
<p><b>What will you be doing?
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Have an advanced knowledge of all Heartland’s HRIS and benefits product solutions.
</b></li>
<li><b>Contact each new client who purchases HRIS/Benefits package, gather information to be loaded into Plus One HRIS system, proactively working with clients to launch their HR ‘go live’ date.
</b></li>
<li><b>Perform product demonstrations for customers via web conferencing applications.
</b></li>
<li><b>Act as a liaison between clients and third party benefit providers.
</b></li>
<li><b>Regularly meet with Information Technology representatives to discuss product functionality and expansion, meet regularly with supervisor and Product manager to discuss workflow and expectations, and regularly work with Regional Team’s to effectively integrate HR/Benefit boarding with standard payroll offerings.
</b></li>
<li><b>Utilizing all resources provided by Heartland Payroll Solutions to resolve inquiries in a timely and accurate manner while portraying professional, courteous and respectful demeanor.
</b></li>
<li><b>Adhere to all Heartland Payroll Solutions handbook policies, including attendance punctuality, PTO management, &amp; time management.
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What are the requirements?
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Assisting other departments as assigned by management.
</b></li>
<li><b>Complete special projects as assigned by management.
</b></li>
<li><b>Accountable for developing a basic knowledge of products, procedures and technologies.
</b></li>
<li><b>Other job-related duties as assigned by direct supervisor.<br>
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education –Bachelor’s Degree or qualified relative experience required.<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Must be Microsoft and Web Conferencing (GoToMeeting) literate.
</b></li>
<li><b>Minimum of 2 years experience as a payroll processor or human resource assistant.
</b></li>
<li><b>Must be able to assimilate new technology
</b></li>
<li><b>The ability to professionally demonstrate product functionality and identify product solution opportunities for clients.<br>
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Successful Candidacy:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Possess the ability to handle customers with sensitive issues, confidentially, while maintaining the professional Customer Services image of Heartland Payroll Solutions.
</b></li>
<li><b>Possess an advanced level of payroll knowledge.
</b></li>
<li><b>Meet or exceed quality, productivity, attendance, educational and punctuality standards as set forth by the Heartland Payroll Solutions leadership team.
</b></li>
<li><b>Coordinating Relationships – Will often work with sales people and employees from other departments both regional and non-regional.
</b></li>
</ul>
