<p><b><i>Practice Manager/Marketing Director </i></b></p>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>Fine Arts Skin &amp; Laser Facial Plastic Surgery is a state-of-the-art, innovative medical facility set in a fusion of a meticulously-curated art gallery and luxurious spa. Under the direction of Dr. David Hartman, our expert medical team provides aesthetic surgical, non-surgical and skin care treatments to enhance and rejuvenate our clients’appearances. Our team prides themselves on making the client experience exceptional every time with individualized service and attention to detail.</p>
<p>The Practice Manager/Marketing Director is responsible for assisting in achieving the company’s vision by ensuring all team members are equipped with the knowledge and resources needed to achieve their goals and ensure that the practice runs effectively. They also plan and implement all marketing strategies to generate leads and serve the current client base. This person reports directly to the Medical Director and Chief Financial Officer.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Management of medical office staff, including requests and concerns</li>
<li>Data management and manipulation using lead generating software, client management software</li>
<li>Client service including requests, concerns, answering phones, email and social media messages</li>
<li>Management of all social media accounts</li>
<li>Research of current trends and marketing strategies</li>
<li>Implementation of marketing plans including but not limited to: design, planning, copy writing, photography and distribution</li>
<li>Direct point of contact for vendors and contacts. Responsible for planning vendor visits and educational experiences</li>
<li>Creation and constant monitoring of new and current patient flow including lead generation, sales, points of contact, and overall client experience</li>
<li>Oversees event creation, direction of event planning and team execution</li>
<li>Assists with inventory and product selection·</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills/Traits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4-year degree in Business Management, Marketing or related field</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in a client service-based business. Medical and/or spa services a plus</li>
<li>Marketing strategy experience including sales</li>
<li>Passion for the beauty industry</li>
<li>Quick on their feet, team player, self-starter, technical,creative thinker with a personable, “thank you economy” client service mindset</li>
<li>Coaching mindset with a desire to always learn</li>
<li>Skillful writer, well-spoken, poised, and professional</li>
<li>Must be willing to travel for conferences/training opportunities(within the US, typically 2-3 times per year)</li>
<li>Knowledge of Adobe and Microsoft Suites</li>
<li>Knowledge of Google and social media tactics</li>
<li>Graphic design and photography experience a plus</li>
</ul>
