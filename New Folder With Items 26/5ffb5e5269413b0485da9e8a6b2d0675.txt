<h2>Staff Tax Accountant II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div><div><div>
<p>Are you looking for a shorter commute to work and a technologically advanced, paperless environment? Are you ready to work with a great team? Then the opportunity to join the DRDA, PLLC team is what you have been looking for.</p>
<p>
DRDA is a growing 30 person Clear Lake CPA firm that is seeking a Staff Tax Accountant II. The Firm has an excellent reputation in serving small to medium sized companies in a variety of industries . We offer a full range of services including assurance, tax and management consulting engagements.</p>
<div>
<b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Prepare level 2 individual, business, and state returns</li>
<li>Review payroll and sales tax reports</li>
<li>Review property tax renditions</li>
<li>Prepare and reference source documents, including bookmarking</li>
<li>Perform full-charge bookkeeper skills</li>
<li>Perform QuickBooks mapping</li>
<li>Prepare interim and annual compilations and preparation of financial statements</li>
<li>Prepare fixed asset schedules (acquisition &amp; disposition)</li>
<li>Research accounting and tax issues as required</li>
<li>Assist with tax planning projects for individuals and businesses</li>
<li>Consult with clients on accounting software as needed</li>
<li>Formulate and recommend solutions for client challenges to senior or manager</li>
<li>Meet utilization and realization standards</li>
<li>Assist with special projects on request, which may include assistance on audit engagements</li>
<li>Available to work during normal business hours</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to work overtime, after hours and/or weekends as needed to meet business demands with prior manager approval</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Technical Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Sound understanding of accounting concepts and theory</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge of tax laws</li>
<li>Fundamental ability to apply tax knowledge to client problems</li>
<li>Proficiency in CS Professional Suite, Microsoft Office Suite, QuickBooks, and Checkpoint Research</li>
<li>Able to read and understand technical forms and financial reports</li>
<li>Develop an understanding of all of DRDA services</li>
<li>Strong organizational, problem-solving, and analytical skills; able to manage priorities and workflow</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
Qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree (BA/BS) in accounting</li>
<li>5+ years recent work exp. preparing income tax returns with exposure to various responsibilities in public accounting.</li>
<li>Be proficient in CS Professional Suite, Microsoft Office Suite and QuickBooks</li>
<li>Demonstrate ability to plan and organize projects</li>
<li>Must be able to pass an Accounting Test and background check</li>
</ul>
</div>
