<h2>Acct Exec 3, Enterprise Direct Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Comcast Business offers technology solutions ranging from Ethernet, internet, and WiFi connectivity to voice, television, and managed Enterprise solutions to power businesses of all sizes to perform better. From small businesses to mid-market and large Enterprise organizations, Comcast Business serves business customers across the country. Powered by an advanced, Gig-speed network and backed by 24/7 technical support, Comcast Business is one of the largest contributors to the growth of Comcast Cable. The organization is the nation's largest cable provider to small and mid-size businesses and has emerged as a force in the Enterprise market, recognized by leading industry associations as one of the fastest growing provider of Ethernet services.</p>

<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
Responsible for the sale of Comcast Commercial Internet, Video, and<br>
Voice based services to mid-size and large businesses. Works as part of<br>
a team to drive sales using an array of prospecting activities and<br>
cultivation of relationships with institutions in designated<br>
territories. Designs and delivers live sales presentations to<br>
prospective clients, develops relationships with individual businesses<br>
and the community, and positions the Comcast brand as key components of<br>
the sales strategy, in keeping with Comcast's touchstones. Has in-depth<br>
experience, knowledge and skills in own discipline. Usually determines<br>
own work priorities. Acts as a resource for colleagues with less<br>
experience.<br>
<br>
Employees at all levels are expect to:<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Understand our Operating Principles; make them the guidelines for how</li></ul>
you do your job<br>
<ul><li>Own the customer experience-think and act in ways that put our</li></ul>
customers first, give them seamless digital options at every touchpoint,<br>
and make them promoters of our products and services<br>
<ul><li>Know your stuff-be enthusiastic learners, users and advocates of our</li></ul>
game-changing technology, products and services, especially our digital<br>

<ul><li>Win as a team-make big things happen by working together and being</li></ul>

<ul><li>Be an active part of the Net Promoter System-a way of working that</li></ul>

<ul>

<b>
Core Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul><li>Creates and delivers face-to-face sales presentations that demonstrate</li></ul>
knowledge of the latest Comcast products and services. Sells with goals<br>
of exceeding departmental financial and unit targets. Stays abreast of<br>
competitive landscape and emerging technologies to best position Comcast<br>
Business Services in the marketplace.<br>
<ul><li>Develops sales territory, including cultivation of local partnerships</li></ul>
and organizational affiliations. Actively generates new leads with<br>
targeted businesses through various prospecting activities, including<br>
cold calling, canvassing, customer referrals, and partner relationships.<br>
Actively seeks ways to promote and position the Comcast brand within<br>
territory.<br>
<ul><li>Retains customer base by delivering on the Comcast Credo, ensuring a</li></ul>
superior customer experience. Maintains and builds customer<br>
relationships to drive customer retention; works with internal teams to<br>
ensure operational efficiencies and service levels that meet and exceed<br>
customer expectations through strong customer service orientation with<br>
excellent follow up.<br>
<ul><li>Maintains accurate and quality sales records and prepares sales and</li></ul>
activity reports, as required.<br>
<ul><li>Attends out-of-office meetings with customers on a regular basis and</li></ul>
demonstrates excellent verbal and written skills and skill in<br>
presenting, persuading, and negotiating.<br>
<ul><li>Demonstrates some knowledge of Network Design, MAN technologies &amp;</li></ul>
designs including DSx, OC-x, WDM, Ethernet, Internet Technologies,<br>
Functionality &amp; Services, Voice Network Technologies (including VoIP),<br>
Data Networking Technologies, Functionality &amp; Services (LAN, MAN, WAN,<br>
VPN), Networking Protocols (with an emphasis on Layers 1,2, &amp; 3),<br>
Customer Premise Equipment (voice &amp; data), Business Continuity/Disaster<br>
Recovery concepts and E-rate Contracting Processes and Procedures.<br>
<ul><li>Consistent exercise of independent judgment and discretion in matters</li></ul>
of significance.<br>
<ul><li>Regular, consistent and punctual attendance. Must be able to work</li></ul>
nights and weekends, variable schedule(s) as necessary.<br>
<ul><li>Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li></ul>
