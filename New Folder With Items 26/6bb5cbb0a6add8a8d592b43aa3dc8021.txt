<h2>HR Generalist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The <b>Human Resource Generalist</b> is responsible for providing HR support for our Corporate and Sales business units. The Generalist will focus in the areas of employee relations, onboarding, employee engagement/recognition, compliance, and HR special projects. This position is both a transactional and tactical business partner. It will work closely with employees and S&amp;D Coffee management while supporting the strategic initiatives of the HR team and organization. This position requires a perceptive person who is capable of relating to individuals at all levels within the organization.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>
Employee Relations</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Provides excellent Human Resources support to all levels of the organization and to many different sites/locations.</li>
<li>Provides guidance and assistance to employees and management regarding interpretation, compliance, and enforcement of Human Resource policies and procedures, state and federal laws.</li>
<li>Works with employees and management to help answer questions, resolve concerns and/or investigate employee complaints/allegations.</li>
<li>Apprises management on the status of issues and concerns being addressed and recommendations for disciplinary actions as appropriate.</li>
<li>Conducts exit interviews, tracks data and evaluates trends.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Employee Engagement &amp; Onboarding</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates and actively participates in bi-weekly new employee orientation and onboarding.</li>
<li>Manages relocation services for existing and new employees.</li>
<li>Plans and executes various employee engagement events and activities throughout the year.</li>
<li>Coordinates HR social media and other employee communication resources.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Other Functions</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assists in administration of the annual employee performance management process.
</li>
<li>Facilitates the organization's random drug testing program.</li>
<li>Coordinates the unemployment claim process in a timely, efficient manner.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>*A bachelor's degree in Management, Human Resources, Psychology or related field and 2+ years human resources experience; or any like combination of education and experience. *Human Resource experience both in a corporate and manufacturing environment is strongly preferred *HR certification a plus *Firm understanding of employment laws. *Strong ability to help resolve employee issues and concerns. *Extremely proficient in Microsoft office suite products. *Strong analytical skills *Experience with ADP Workforce Now and Enterprise eTime a plus. *Excellent professional written and verbal communications skills. *Strong attention to detail and sense of urgency. *Strong organizational and administrative skills. *Strong initiative, ability to anticipate issues proactively. Equal Opportunity Employer / Vets / Disabled</div>
