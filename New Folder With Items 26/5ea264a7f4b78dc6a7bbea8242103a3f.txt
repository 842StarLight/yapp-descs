<h2>Solutions Architect, Software Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About:</b></p>
<p>
ClearScale is a leading cloud systems integration company and AWS Premier Consulting Partner providing a wide range of cloud services including: cloud consulting, architecture design, migration, automation, application development, and managed services.</p>
<p>
We help Fortune 500 enterprises, mid-sized business, and startups in verticals like: Healthcare, Education, Financial Services, Security, Media and Technology succeed with ambitious, challenging, and unique cloud projects. We architect, develop, and launch innovative and sophisticated solutions using the best cutting-edge cloud technologies.</p>
<p>
ClearScale is growing quickly and there is high demand for the services we provide. Clients come to us for our deep experience with Big Data, Containerization, Serverless Infrastructure, Microservices, IoT, Machine Learning, DevOps, Software Development and more.</p>
<p>
This is a unique opportunity for you to make a big impact, enjoy competitive compensation, work on a wide variety of projects for clients across multiple industries, and work alongside some of the best minds in the cloud.</p>
<p><b>
Summary:</b> Responsible for providing solution architecture, including architecture design and development services.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Act as a technical liaison between customers and delivery teams on projects;</li>
<li>
Educate customers on the value proposition of AWS, participate in deep architectural discussions to ensure solutions are designed for successful deployment in the cloud;</li>
<li>
As a key member of the business development and sales team, ensure success in developing, building and migrating applications and services on the AWS platform;</li>
<li>
Partner with the sales team, and help execute a sales strategy</li>
<li>
Scoping calls and SOW creation, provide a structured and complete informational background to the development team</li>
<li>
Gather requirements, design major aspects of the architecture of an application;</li>
<li>
Identify and suggest several approaches for the solution to be developed. Negotiate and find the most appropriate one with the customer.</li>
<li>
Negotiate on requirements, architect end-to-end solutions for multi layered applications on a variety of technology stacks;</li>
<li>
Create and review architecture documentation, ensure uniform application design standards are maintained;</li>
<li>
Collaborate with stakeholders to ensure the architecture is aligned with project requirements;</li>
<li>
Help software engineering team to identify proper tools and orchestration and make proper architectural decisions to hit project goals and to achieve maximum productivity;</li>
<li>
Collaborate with cross-functional roles to align development efforts with architecture roadmap;</li>
<li>
Capture and share best-practice knowledge within the AWS and ClearScale solutions architect community;</li>
<li>
Author or otherwise contribute to ClearScale customer-facing publications such as blog and case studies.</li>
</ul>
<p>
 <b>Knowledge, Skills, Abilities and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
8+ years hands on experience in software design and development;</li>
<li>
3 years hands on experience in consulting and/or outsourcing companies;</li>
<li>
Strong background in AWS platform and services. Know their in-born problems and limitations. Know how to design architecture layout for typical tasks: e.g. simple ETL pipeline, manage a IoT devices fleet, classic 3-layer web application with built-in scalability, typical data warehouse etc;</li>
<li>
Understanding fundamentals of different project management methodologies and frameworks (covering Agile, Lean Development, classical waterfall) applicable for custom software engineering;</li>
<li>
Understanding of micro services architecture patterns, area of applicability, restrictions and typical problems;</li>
<li>
3+ years hands on experience with cloud-based, infrastructure as a service or platform as a service solutions and principles;</li>
<li>
Experience in SOWs creation;</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively exchange information, news, ideas and meaning in business and technical environments;</li>
<li>
Know how to evaluate software architecture design;</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new technologies quickly.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education and Certification:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in engineering, computer science, or related discipline.</li>
<li>
AWS certifications highly desired.</li>
</ul>

You'll be a great fit if:</b></p>
