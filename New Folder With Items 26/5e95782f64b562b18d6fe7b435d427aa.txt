<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>
<p>
Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p><b>
Consumer Banking</b> is an industry leader in supporting homeowners and consumers, in addition to operating one of the most extensive banking franchises in the country. We serve mass market, affluent, and small business customers; as well as provide home and personal lending. Our focus is on delivering an exceptional experience for our customers through financial advice and guidance coupled with providing the products and services that will help them realize their financial hopes and dreams. We’ve built our team of top professionals by rewarding their accomplishments and ensuring they have what's needed to succeed.</p>
<p>
The <b>Enterprise Complaints Management Office (ECMO)</b> plays an integral role in having an enterprise view of our customer concerns, identifying risks in a timely manner, providing enhanced reporting capabilities at the line of business and Enterprise levels, and delivering a variety of benefits to our business. This Office includes four main functions: Executive Complaint Operations, Data Analytics and Reporting, Quality Assurance, and Complaints Shared Services. Together these teams focus on capturing, researching, and resolving customer dissatisfaction; executing deep root cause analysis; and proactively identifying continuous opportunities that are engineered to improve our customers’ experience and drive out dissatisfaction.</p>
<p>
The Shared services Operations Support team is actively searching for a Business Support Consultant 3 (BSC3) to join an exciting new leadership team. This position will reside within ECMO Shared Services and play a critical role as we support ECMO for Site Strategy across the Wells Fargo footprint. The person will partner with ECMO Leadership, Corporate Properties and business partners to find seats/locations supporting the “Ramp-Up” phase and ultimately the Strategic vision for placement of ECMO team members.</p>
<p>
A strong leader with proven critical thinking, analytical skills, and demonstrated senior operational credibility will be determining factors in selection. Successful candidates will be articulate and possess a demonstrated leadership capability; they will be impressive in their inter-personal effectiveness and with regard to their accomplishments. It requires a leader with strong relationship management, influencing, vision, operational, analytical and execution skills. A key differentiator will be the ability to influence and champion change. The role requires a sense of urgency, passion for results, people skills and personal accountability for driving results.</p>
<ul>
<li>Leadership: Proven ability to establish and gain buy-in for an idea or vision, and garner enthusiastic support across the organization to accomplish agreed-upon goals. Ability to influence business partners and key contributors that do not report directly to the BSC3.</li>
<li>Problem Solving: Ability to visualize, articulate, and solve problems. Accurately analyzes complex issues, ensuring identification of underlying root causes. Demonstrates the ability to solve problems under conditions of uncertainty and ambiguity. Generates alternative solutions and expertly handles obstacles.</li>
<li>Professional Skills: Capable prolific communicator that is comfortable working with a wide variety of corporate executives, sales, marketing, engineering, operations, customers and business partners. Proven teamwork skills; excellent interpersonal, written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>Creativity and Innovation: Able to think creatively and critically to identify opportunities to uniquely and effectively meet customer needs. Strong ability to assess market and competitive conditions and to develop creative, break-through approaches supporting improved customer experiences and operational efficiencies.</li>
<li>Analytical Thinking: Obtains and uses data necessary to identify opportunities or understand problems; uses quantitative techniques to make it actionable.</li>
</ul>
