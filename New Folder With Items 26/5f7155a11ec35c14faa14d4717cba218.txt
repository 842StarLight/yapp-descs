<h2>Lead Technical Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>As the Lead Technical Business Analyst, you will support the Cole Haan Planning Delivery Manager to realize functional and technical opportunities supporting business processes, ensure a stable application that delivers continuous results and support planning system project execution. This will include translating business requirements and use cases into technical and functional enhancement specifications, establishing and executing quality assurance, documentation and more. You will be responsible for unlocking roadblocks related to demand, sales and inventory planning, and execution through buying and allocation to achieve significant growth at Cole Haan.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
CORE ACCOUNTABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead and realize all planning systems development, including enhancements, major initiatives, and remediation of chronic issues</li>
<li>Support and maintain the enterprise planning application on a day-by-day basis</li>
<li>Establish baselines for both system and functional quality assurance to ensure clear and reliable user acceptance testing for all environment development</li>
<li>Perform systems testing and user quality assurance testing</li>
<li>Design and implement change management plans to ensure adoption of process improvement changes and technology solutions across the organization</li>
<li>Lead multiple agile, light to medium lift projects including business requirement gathering, establishing user acceptance baselines as well as documenting both functional and technical processes and design; Project types will include application version upgrades, customized enhancement implementations, and virtualization</li>
<li>Prioritize and balance enhancement and maintenance workload to deliver larger business gains and improving application reliability</li>
<li>Manage support requests as needed both internally as well as with external application vendors</li>
<li>Well organized documentation of both system and business processes in and surrounding the application</li>
</ul>

<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<div><div>
<p>Cole Haan is a pioneer in inventing and reinventing modern dress footwear, bags and accessories through our ZeroGrand and GrandSeries product lines. Customers who seek style, versatility and comfortable function in their products come to Cole Haan.</p>
<p>
Cole Haan is rapidly growing in the United States and abroad. The brand is sold in more than 45 countries through 110 branded retail stores within the United States, 200+ branded store and shop-in-shop locations internationally, on our global digital flagship, colehaan.com, and within leading department store and specialty retail partners throughout the World.</p>
<p>
Our headquarters is in Greenland, New Hampshire. We have offices in New York, Tokyo, Hong Kong, London, Amsterdam and sourcing offices in Asia.</p>
<p>
We seek hard-working employees who are entrepreneurial-minded, solutions-oriented team players who accept and own accountability, model integrity, value individuality and diversity, encourage teamwork and inspire creativity.</p>

<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<div><div>
<ul>

<ul>

<li>Experience in full life-cycle solution delivery projects (3-4 people, 2-6 months)</li>
