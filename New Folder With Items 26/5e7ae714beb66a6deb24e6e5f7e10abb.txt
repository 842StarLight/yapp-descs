<div>As a Technical Account Manager, you will work closely with developer partners to understand their needs and enable their success by providing consultative services and creative solutions to challenging product and technical issues. You will use your deep understanding of our products to unlock value for our partners and engage with Product, Engineering, and Partnerships teams as a partner advocate. You will also drive initiatives to scale solutions and make them easier to adopt, including representing Facebook at industry events, conferences and other community gatherings.
<br>
The ideal candidate will combine excellent technical, product and business skills to help partners succeed on our platforms while improving our products. This position is full-time and is based in our San Francisco office.
