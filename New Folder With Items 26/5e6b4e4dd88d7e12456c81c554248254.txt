<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
The Marketing Coordinator supports the company's sales and marketing efforts by coordinating and implement marketing communication projects; assisting with presentation materials, planning meetings, administrating programs and promotions; assisting with various projects such as newsletters, dealer and owner communications, social media projects; online communications and website projects and supporting the Marketing Manager and Marketing Communications Specialist in various other marketing and communications related projects.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the company's Social Media footprint, such as Facebook, Instagram Owners Blogs, etc. by monitoring feedback, developing topic calendars and posting regularly.</li>
<li>Tracking and organizing information such as web analytics, lead generation, invoices and budgets,other marketing results, etc.</li>
<li>Gathering and feeding information to third party partners such as dealers, boating magazines and marketing partners, marine product portal websites, NMMA, Statistical Surveys, Avala, as well as others.</li>
<li>Responsible for the company's newsletters to dealers and consumers, generating monthly newsletters to communicate product updates and changes</li>
<li>Assists in major events such as annual dealer meetings, photo shoots, training seminars, etc.</li>
<li>Assists with presentation materials such as product training, dealer meetings, colorboards, prospective dealer packets, and internal presentations for various meetings.</li>
<li>Assists with and administers the company's major online projects such as Dealer Portal and Build-A-Boats. Such projects require interaction with dealers, a clear understanding of product nuances, and use of the company's content and administrative systems.</li>
<li>Assist with the management of the company's wearables programs</li>
<li>Assist as needed in all marketing functions, including marketing materials development, dealer tools development, promotions, and various administrative functions.</li>
<li>Assists with the creation and editing of product photography.</li>
</ul>
