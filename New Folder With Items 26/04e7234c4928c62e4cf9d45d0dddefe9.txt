Bloomreach is seeking a motivated Customer Success Manager who loves driving revenue growth and successful outcomes for customers. You are passionate about growing your book of business with successful customer renewals, upsells and cross-sells. You are a resource quarterback, partnering with your customer to realize meaningful results while working directly with our Engineering, Product, Quality and Marketing teams.
