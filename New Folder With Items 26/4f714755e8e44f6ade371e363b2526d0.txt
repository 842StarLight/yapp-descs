<h2>Solutions Engineer III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>At Verizon Connect (VZConnect), we guide a connected world on the go.</p>
<p>
We’re in it to win it. Today we’re the #1 global provider of fleet management solutions for both enterprise and small/medium businesses. Our consumer products, like Hum, create a more connected ride with vehicle diagnostics, emergency assistance, and WiFi. And to top it off, our partnerships with major car manufacturers help us care for more drivers with our connected technologies.</p>
<p>
As a top 20 SaaS leader, we know our talent is the most important component to our success. We hire top talent and empower them to do their best work. As a division of Verizon, we combine the fun and excitement of a start-up environment with the resources, operational excellence, and brand recognition of an established tech giant.</p>
<p>
As a Solutions Engineer III, you will partner with Verizon Connect’s Senior Client Executives to develop value propositions for our platform products and conduct pre-sales presentations to sign Verizon Connect’s most strategic, high-profile prospects. You will be responsible for all technical aspects of proposed solutions working in close partnership with the Product Management, Engineering, and Post-Sales Services. This role will work closely with the regional Sales Directors and area Sales Vice Presidents to develop Pre-Sales engagement playbooks for the area Solutions Engineering team to adopt and implement.</p>
<p>
For this job, you'll bring extensive pre-sales experience with enterprise software selling within large Fortune 500 companies, a strong technical background, and detailed knowledge of web technologies, and enterprise SaaS applications. You must have a strong background in technology, Internet protocols, sales, systems information management, and systems design, including experience in consultative selling or solution-based sales. You also enjoy working with people and technology, and as such you have both the ability to interact with engineering teams as well as excellent relationship-building and communication skills.</p>
<p>
This position can be located in other valid Verizon locations or work remotely within a reasonable commute distance to a valid Verizon location in the Southeast US.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborate with regional and area sales leadership to land strategic customer opportunities.</li>
<li>
Partner with Senior Client Executives to provide support for the most strategic deals and opportunities in the East or West areas, which may include mentoring and support of assigned solutions engineers.</li>
<li>
Design and implement solution processes to align product and vertical selling strategies to the area team.</li>
<li>
Develop review process with regional SE teams for opportunity assessment and process improvement training and development programs.</li>
<li>
Communicate Verizon Connect value proposition to varying technical and non-technical clients, including executive ''C'' level management and end-users.</li>
<li>
Propose and/or design technical solutions to meet prospect's business, technical, and/or financial goals.</li>
<li>
Support and setup tradeshow demo playbooks.</li>
<li>
Partner with Sales to develop requirements documents, presentations, proposals, and ROI models, and coordinate with a team of support specialists, consultants, or project managers to execute sales objectives or to ensure that all pre-sales customer requirements are documented and transitioned.</li>
<li>
Maintain expert knowledge of Verizon Connect products to develop and present unique solutions, and maintain knowledge of emerging trends in technology and online advertising.</li>
</ul>

</p>

<li>
Willingness to travel.</li>
<li>
A valid driver's license.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Even better if you have:</p>

A degree.</li>

Seven or more years of technical sales or field sales experience.</li>
<li>
Experience in cross functional initiatives and experience in technical sales.</li>
<li>
Experience in large enterprise opportunities in your current role.</li>
<li>
Ability to adapt your presentations to the technical level of the audience, and comfort with presenting technical materials to large groups.</li>
</ul>
