Job Description
<br><br>
Suplari is seeking an experienced Regional Account Executive to focus and execute sales activities for Suplari.
<br><br>
As an integral member of our sales team, you will leverage your years of financial application/solution selling experience to execute the fundamentals of a complex sales cycle to the enterprise.
<br><br>
<b>What you’ll be doing:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify and prioritize potential sales opportunities within assigned Fortune 2,500 enterprise accounts to meet (and hopefully exceed) your quarterly sales quota.</li>
<li>Prospect in territory and identify significant business that fits Suplari’s solutions.</li>
<li>Delivering engaging product demos and highly compelling proposals.</li>
<li>Solution selling: consult with customers in defining success criteria and account strategy to ensure the customer understands the value of Suplari’s solutions for their business needs.</li>
<li>Report on all pipeline and sales metrics.</li>
<li>Create statements of work and negotiate profitable and favorable business terms with potential accounts.</li>
<li>Ensure accurate and up-to-date client records and data in Salesforce.</li>
</ul>
<b>To be successful in this role, we need you to have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 6 years SaaS B2B Enterprise selling experience.</li>
<li>Prior experience selling financial applications/solutions.</li>
<li>Value based solution selling ability and understanding of ROI and cost/benefit analysis.</li>
<li>Proven ability to prospect and manage a designated territory to maximize revenue growth.</li>
<li>Consistent track record of over achievement, exceeding quota, and high performance.</li>
<li>Excellent written/verbal communication and presentation skills, as you’ll be frequently communicating via phone/email, as well as conducting virtual and face to face product demos.</li>
<li>Domain knowledge or previous selling of procurement and sourcing software/solutions is a huge plus.</li>
<li>Ability to travel domestically up to 40% of the time.</li>
<li>A college degree (BA/BS).</li>
<li>Salesforce or similar CRM experience.</li>
</ul>
<b>You’d be an excellent fit for Suplari and our sales team if:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You are a self-motivated, high performer who can work with minimal oversight to get the job done.</li>
<li>You thrive as highly productive individual contributor in a collaborative sales environment.</li>
<li>You’re comfortable selling at a start-up company that is emerging in the market (think: successfully navigating change, and having a bias for action) and you are excited about putting your mark on something early.</li>
<li>You love being a part of a small, dynamic, and agile organization that encourages you to learn and grow.</li>
<li>You are passionate about using your experience and expertise to inspire the team.</li>
</ul>
