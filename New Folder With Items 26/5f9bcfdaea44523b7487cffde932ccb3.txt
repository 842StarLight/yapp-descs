<h2>Senior Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Green Plains Inc. is a publicly traded, diversified commodity processing business with agribusiness, ethanol production, cattle feed lot operations, commodity marketing, and distribution services. We are a growth company and one of the leading producers of ethanol in the world. Through our adjacent businesses we are focused on the production of high-protein feed ingredients and export growth opportunities.</p>
<p>
Green Plains Inc. has an immediate opening for a Senior Accountant at our Obion (Rives), TN location.</p>
<p>
The Senior Accountant is responsible for various daily, weekly and monthly financial reporting functions, including but not limited to review and postings of payable, receivable and payroll transactions.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions include but are not limited to:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review and post commodity and accounts payable batches to include proper commodity settlement, receipt verification, sales tax accuracy and authorizations.</li>
<li>
Research and resolve vendor calls regarding accounts payable.</li>
<li>
Verify and apply cash receipts to A/R transactions.</li>
<li>
Generate and print accounts payable and commodity payable checks.</li>
<li>
Prepare inventory reconciliations, month-end work papers and other month-end duties as assigned.</li>
<li>
Verify and enter daily bank transactions.</li>
<li>
Other duties as may be assigned to accomplish the safe and timely day-to-day operations within the administrative office.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Skills and Requirements</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Two-year accounting/business degree and 3-5 years accounting experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to handle multiple tasks</li>
<li>
Ability to work with all levels of staff (co-workers and management)</li>
<li>
Excellent Microsoft and Excel software skills</li>
<li>
Accurate data entry skills</li>
<li>
Excellent phone and verbal skills</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge of modern clerical practices</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Work Environment/Physical Demands:</b></p>
<p>
Ability to meet physical demands of the job includes but is not limited to frequent sitting, standing, frequent computer and telephone use, and some lifting or moving up to 10 lbs.</p>

All potential employees of Green Plains Inc. will be required to successfully pass a pre-employment drug screening and background check prior to employment.</p>
