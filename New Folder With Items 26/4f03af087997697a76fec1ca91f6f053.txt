Description</div>
<div>
<div>
The Senior Customer Service Representative supports customers and the sales team.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Essential Job Functions</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Create Header in SAP for all orders received by Pall, New Port Richey (10%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Enter customer purchase orders and amendments into SAP (adding lines the headers and existing orders) (17%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage blocks on orders in SAP (2%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Enter service orders (warranty returns), credits and debits into SAP (2%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Trouble shooting (resolve issues that arise and consistently strive for process improvements) (15%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Enter and maintain customer master database (5%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Support maintenance of pricing in SAP (1%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare and maintain status reports for customers and Sales (5%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare and maintain Pending Order Report daily (5%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Confirm delivery dates – using Excel and/or customer portals (10%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage assigned customers for status requests of existing orders (5%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Process expedite requests and provide data to Sales and/or customers (5%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Validate order entry (11%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Scanning and attaching documents to SAP sales orders (1%)</div></li>
<li><div>
Supporting Customer Service, Sales and Quality Teams with reporting and gathering data as needed (3%)</div></li>
<li><p>
Update Process &amp; Procedures as required (3%)</p></li>
</ul>
