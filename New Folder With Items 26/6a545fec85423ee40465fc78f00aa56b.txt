<b>WHO WE ARE LOOKING FOR:</b><br>
-----------------------
<br><br>
The Product Marketing Director and their team sit at the nexus of customer needs, the technology roadmap, competitive environment and the organization's go-to-market activities. Product Marketing works closely with Product Management, Sales, and Account Management to communicate the product vision and differentiation. Working with Marketing, the Product Marketing Director collaboratively develops strategy and go-to-market plans for all products. This role develops strategy, along with their direct reports, for product positioning in-market, life-cycle management, and competitive analysis across the portfolio. This role will is a global role, driving localization and customization as necessary.
<br><br>
-----------------------

<b>WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING:</b><br>
-----------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Proven ability to coach, lead, manage, and inspire a high-performing, fast-paced, results-oriented team in San Francisco.</li>
<li>Work with Product Management, Client Services and Sales to identify customer needs, key product enhancements, and new products.</li>
<li>Proven ability to build global go-to-market strategies and plans, then execute with a large multi-functional marketing organization.</li>
<li>Ability to support your team throughout the go-to-market process: developing clear KPIs, managing &amp; building insight-driven product marketing strategies that inform the go-to-market tactics, tracking campaign performance and reporting ROI back to senior management.</li>

<li>Understand broader market trends, our users &amp; be the expert on competition.</li>
