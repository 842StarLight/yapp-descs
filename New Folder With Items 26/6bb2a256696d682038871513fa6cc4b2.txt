<h2>General Manager - Metro West MA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>The General Manager (GM) is directly responsible for managing the profit, merchandise, operations and Team Members at their assigned A.C. Moore location. The person in the role must display leadership that will create an exceptional environment for customers and Team Members and must lead by example in providing excellent customer service to drive an experience that exceeds customer expectations. The GM has overall responsibility for performance management and the evaluation of Team Members. This position ensures that all Team Members are properly trained in providing exceptional customer service. The GM focuses on in-stock position and merchandising opportunities by reviewing various reports. This position has knowledge of seasonal trends, industry trends, and the competitive market landscape. The GM reviews the Profit &amp; Loss line items and makes decisions on ways to improve performance in his/her store. This position is responsible for executing advertising, profit management, and shrink reduction in the store. This position impacts the company by leading the store to success. This Team Member must act with the A.C. Moore Values in mind: People, integrity, desire to be the best, passion, enthusiasm and new ideas.</p>
<p><b>REPORTS TO: Regional Director</b></p>
<p><b>WORK SCHEDULE: Flexibility in scheduling; early mornings, evenings, weekends and holidays required.</b></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for creating a customer focused atmosphere in the store. Developing positive customer relations that will set the standard for Team Members.</li>
<li>Ensure purchases are processed with speed and accuracy by Team Members with a friendly demeanor, and recovering the store consistently to provide a positive shopping experience.</li>
<li>Personally walks side counters daily to ensure in stock position and P.I. accuracy.</li>
<li>Communicate and drive all corporate and store initiatives to Team Members via daily huddle meetings.</li>
<li>Drive marketing and advertising by maximizing product displays and placement.</li>
<li>Drive Reward and VIP Card sign-ups to meet company expectations.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Manage the essential job functions of all positions within the store.</li>
<li>Recruit, develop, train, and retain effective Team Members.</li>
<li>Manage Team Members effectively by providing constant feedback on performance.</li>
<li>Resolve performance issues.</li>
<li>Foster an environment of high team member morale.</li>
<li>Complete fair and accurate performance and salary reviews.</li>
<li>Oversee adequate and efficient scheduling of Team Members; responsible for ensuring overall payroll accuracy.</li>
<li>Directly manages the Custom Framing Specialist and the sales performance of the Frame Shop.</li>
<li>Directly manages Assistant Managers and multiple supervisors depending on volume.</li>
<li>Develops Assistant General Manager(s) to perpetuate strong GM bench strength for the company.</li>
<li>Ensure that displays are merchandised neatly with correct pricing and optimal planner placement within the store.</li>
<li>Plans for seasonal transitions and manages liability product sell through. Manages pack away process.</li>
<li>Promote and implement Fundraise with A.C. Moore program.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Effectively lead a business that involves planning, sales, and profit optimization.</li>
<li>Understand and make decisions based on the P&amp;L for the store. Monitor sales, margin, and inventory.</li>
<li>Analyze and make decisions based on reports of sales trends.</li>
<li>Represent the company within the local community.</li>
<li>Accountable for processing of freight from receipt to the sales floor within stated goal of 24 hours.
</li>
<li>Comply with and strictly enforce all A.C. Moore policies outlined in the Employment Handbook, including dress code and attendance, and companyâ€™s Standard Operating Procedures.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Maintain a store that is safe and clean according to company policy/procedure and OSHA/ADA standards.</li>
<li>Comply with and strictly enforce all FLSA employment practices.</li>
<li>Accountable for opening and closing the building by carrying keys. Accountable for being issued a unique alarm code and for being on the emergency call list and responding to any such emergencies/inquires.</li>
<li>Responsible for compliance with all questions on the Store Visit Checklist and Loss Prevention Audit related to job function.</li>
<li>Delegate short-term and long-term tasks and ensure completion through follow-up.</li>
<li>Adheres to deadlines as assigned by the company, the Regional Director and/or the Natural Insight compliance tool.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as requested.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE &amp; EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3 - 5 years of experience in retail management or a combination of a college degree and retail experience is required.</li>
<li>Flexibility in scheduling; evenings, early mornings, weekends and holidays required.</li>
<li>Understanding of retail sales, inventory, profit planning, and merchandising.</li>
<li>Experience with store operations and loss prevention.</li>
<li>Understanding of effective recruitment and training.</li>
<li>Microsoft Outlook, Excel, and Word experience required.</li>
<li>Proficiency with POS and other systems used to manage retail sales, expense, and operations.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and decision-making skills.</li>
<li>Strong business acumen.</li>
<li>Excellent people skills.</li>
<li>Strong verbal and communication skills.</li>
<li>Consistent positive attitude when interacting with team, customers, and colleagues.</li>
<li>Demonstrate ability to set goals and objectives and motivate team to achieve them.</li>
<li>Takes accountability and ownership for his or her own actions.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask while leading a team and working as a team player in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Experience with arts and crafts preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Frequently lifts, carries or otherwise moves and positions objects weighing up to 50lbs.</li>
<li>Ability to bend, turn, twist, kneel and/or squat up to 75% a day.</li>
<li>Must be able to move about the store and/or remain stationary, up to 8 hours per day.</li>
<li>Repetitive movement of hands and arms.</li>
<li>Ability to ascend and descend a ladder safely, preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to operate cardboard baler and pallet jack.</li>
<li>Frequently communicates with customers and/or others.</li>
</ul>
