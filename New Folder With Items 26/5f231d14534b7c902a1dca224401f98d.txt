<h2>Account Executive, Channel</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>As an Channel Account Executive, you are responsible for generating new cloud business through our partner companies. The primary duty is to develop opportunities that result in new revenue by identifying, educating, enabling, and empowering channel partners.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
The Channel Account Executive is tasked with performing daily activities designed to build strong relationships with the partner sales team, in addition to key stakeholders within the channel partners. Success will be measured by achieving sales quota and performing assigned partner activities and responsibilities.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Major Responsibilities/activities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Meet stated sales goals and push to exceed them</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Achieve and exceed annual sales quota</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Prospect, identify, develop and build strong relationships with new and existing partner sales contacts and their customer base.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Creatively and consistently communicate with partner sales teams to gain mindshare through all methods at your disposal; calls, emails, webinars, partner meetings and train, lunch and learns, and other related events</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Dynamically educate channel partners on Genesys Cloud. Including selling best practices, technology solutions, platform roadmap, and operational and partner program changes with the support of the channel solution consultants</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Identify and influence all levels of channel partner organizations, including sellers, operations, engineering, client support, and executive leadership</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Understand the positioning of Genesys and competitors to be able to lead thoughtful discussions on why they should invest their selling efforts on Genesys Cloud</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Establish strong working relationships internally across direct sales and leadership teams</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Provide deal strategy, support, and resources to channel partner to drive sales</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Manage opportunity registration process from initial submission to final approval</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Track and report on revenue and targets utilizing Salesforce</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Prepare and participate in weekly forecast calls and quarterly business reviews</div>
 </li>
</ul>
<div><b>Minimum Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>BS/BA in Sales or Marketing or equivalent experience</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>3+ years channel management or channel development experience within Channel community preferred</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Experience selling SaaS or Cloud based software</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Knowledge of CCaaS, UCaaS, and/or Genesys specific offerings preferred</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ability to develop and manage a sales pipeline</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Excellent presentation, verbal and written communication skills</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ability to coordinate efforts of multiple teams and team members to achieve common goal</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Creative, resourceful and a quick learner</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ability to travel 50% of time</div>
</li>
</ul>
