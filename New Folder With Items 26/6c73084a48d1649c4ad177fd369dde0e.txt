<h2>IT Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i><b>How the Position Works</b> </i><br>The Support Technician is the first line of support for all internal desktop, laptop and other device management and maintenance. He / She is responsible for interacting and supporting all users that you are assigned to so that you resolve all desktop related issues that arise in a responsible, efficient and timely manner. This position requires on-call support to perform maintenance activities during non-business hours. This position reports to the Team Lead - TechSupport.</p>
<p><b>Work Hours: </b> This person in this role is expected to be available Mon - Fri from 10 am - 7 pm. There is on-call responsibility and that includes weekends.</p>
<p><i><b>The specific responsibilities of the IT Desktop Support include;</b> </i><br>Take initial calls from end users, log incident and work to resolve issue<br>Provide first level diagnosis for potential resolution<br>Direct other calls to the appropriate Resolver Group<br>Escalate calls that cannot be resolved by either Level One or Level Two<br>Monitor tickets and prioritize work<br>Provide users with status of open tickets upon request<br>Process new hire, term and move requests, creating the appropriate work items within the ticketing system<br>Create profiles for new hires / modify existing profiles when required<br>Engage 3rd party vendors when needed and document accordingly<br>Troubleshoot hardware and software issues based on priority<br>Perform repairs for equipment failures<br>Connect new workstations to network and host systems<br>Perform equipment moves or equipment upgrades, if required<br>Perform application software installation<br>Perform physical inventories<br>Perform data migration services if requested<br>Maintain loaner laptop pool<br>Assist with desktop/laptop imaging and audio/visual requests, as needed<br>Any other activities assigned by your manager</p>
