<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Please review the following job description:
</b></h3>
<p></p>Work in partnership with the International Services Sales team to meet overall objectives and revenue plan and provide client service to valued clients, executing on the BB&amp;T behaviors to ensure delivery of the Perfect Client Experience.
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b><br>
Following is a summary of the essential functions for this job. Other duties may be performed, both major and minor, which are not mentioned below. Specific activities may change from time to time.
<br>
1. Serve as portfolio manager for assigned clients by addressing all client service inquiries and needs as follows:
<br>
a. Serve as primary client contact for assigned portfolio within International Services, demonstrating ownership and ability to cultivate strong business relationships with client base;
<br>
b. Collaborate with various BB&amp;T operational teams to resolve client-related service issues;
<br>
c. Responsible for timely assistance of BB&amp;T front-end modules (e.g. product performance, functionality, product delivery, relationship support);
<br>
d. Ensure client inquiries receive proper response and follow-up on a timely basis;
<br>
e. Join team meetings/training sessions on a periodic basis with International Sales team and other business units.
<br>
2. Accompany International Business Development Officers (BDOs) and other BB&amp;T Community Bank Relationship Managers on client visits from time to time.
<br>
3. Communicate new business and product opportunities to International BDOs.
<br>
4. Complete internal training, as required.
<br>
5. Provide assistance to BB&amp;T’s Commercial Client Service Units (CSUs) on international products and services.
<br>
6. From time to time, assist International BDOs in implementing internal and external training workshops and seminars.
<br>
7. Prepare reports of the assigned portfolio.
<br>
8. Utilize Salesforce.com to track activities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Skills and Competencies:
</b><br>
The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
<br>
1. Bachelor’s degree in Business-related field, or equivalent education and related training
<br>
2. Five years of experience in international services
<br>
3. Outstanding interpersonal and relationship management skills
<br>
4. Ability to persuade and lead without direct supervision
<br>
5. Ability to deal with several projects, processes and deadlines simultaneously
<br>
6. Demonstrated ability to deal with complex situations that require sound judgment and minimum supervision
<br>
7. Well-developed written and verbal skills
<br>
8. Demonstrated proficiency in basic computer applications, such as Microsoft Office software products
<br>
9. Ability to travel, occasionally overnight</p>
