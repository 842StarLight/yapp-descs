<h2>Account Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>THIS IS NOT JUST A JOB. THIS IS THE RIGHT TEAM, CULTURE, PURPOSE AND OPPORTUNITY!</b></p>
<p>SurgLogs is a state-of-the-art software solution leading the movement in digitizing and automating accreditation for ambulatory surgery centers and healthcare facilities across the US.</p>
<p>We are looking for ambitious and passionate individuals who enjoy sales and building relationships with clients to join our quickly growing team and lead the movement together!</p>
<p><b>About the Position: </b></p>
<p>As an Account Representative, you are responsible for engaging potential clients over the phone, feeling them out and being able to build relationships on the spot. This means you have to be comfortable on the phone, have great communication skills and never take no for an answer. You will also be responsible for scheduling and performing online demonstrations, attending conferences as well as building and maintaining your pipeline.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Handle the full cycle of sales from prospecting, setting appointments, scheduling/performing demonstrations, and closing deals.</li>
<li>Generating new business via outbound calls and emails</li>
<li>Provide in depth product knowledge and services to persuade potential clients</li>
<li>Schedule and perform online demonstration webinars</li>
<li>Document activities, tasks, and results in CRM</li>
<li>Required to meet monthly sales goals/objectives</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Have a positive attitude and strong work ethic</li>
<li>Be able to work independently and on a team</li>
<li>Possess great communication skills</li>
<li>Adaptable to new features and releases in software</li>
<li>Must be able to pass a background check</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Additional Preferences: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Possess a Bachelor’s degree, actively pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in a related field, and/or combined work experience</li>
<li>Experience in outbound phone sales</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation &amp; Benefits: </b></p>
<p>Starting pay rate is $18/hr during 90-day introductory period, and upon successful completion of introductory period $21/hr + 10% recurring commission (uncapped).</p>
<p>Example of Compensation with Recurring Commission:</p>
<p>Year 1: $50,000 in Sales = $5,000 commission + Base Salary</p>
<p>Year 2: $75,000 in Sales = $7,500 commission + Year 1 commission + Base Salary</p>
<p>Year 3: $100,000 in Sales = $10,000 commission + Year 1 commission + Year 2 commission + Base Salary</p>
<p>Benefits include competitive vacation and PTO package, paid holidays, 401(k) plan, company sponsored events, and many more! We also offer opt-in benefits programs, which are updated regularly to foster both professional and personal growth.</p>
