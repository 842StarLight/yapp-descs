<h2>Shipping and Receiving Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for a responsible Shipping &amp; Receiving Clerk to ship, receive, inspect and record shipments with raw material or products. You will be responsible for loading and unloading orders.</p>
<p>A Shipping and Receiving Clerk must have a great eye for detail, as well as the clerical skills to update records and follow up with suppliers.</p>
<p>The goal is to ensure we ship and receive the correct product and give the best service to truck drivers.</p>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft office, Excel</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Must be able to do multiple tasks simultaneously and work well with others</li>
<li>6 day a week operation, must be flexible in hours</li>
<li>Warehouse experience a plus</li>
<li>Bilingual a plus</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>Keen eye for detail</li>
</ul>
<p>Responsibilities &amp; Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Answer incoming calls in a courteous and professional manner</li>
<li>Verify all driver’s vehicle inspection reports and daily logs</li>
<li>Schedule inbound and outbound deliveries</li>
<li>Handle all shipping and receiving needs, checking in all drivers, handling paperwork</li>
<li>Create shipping documents and B/L’s</li>
<li>Input all inbounds, notify sales team of shortages and rejections</li>
<li>Communicate and schedule custom brokers, USDA and inspectors</li>
<li>Communicate with trucking companies</li>
<li>Create daily reports for sales team</li>
</ul>
<p>Great responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Print daily sales receipts and cross-check with computer software to ensure accuracy</li>
<li>Prepare and print shipping labels corresponding to shipping needs</li>
<li>Track inventory and report any shortages to sales team so we can update the website</li>
<li>Maintain a clean and safe work area and update supplies as needed</li>
<li>General shipping office duties (scanning, faxing, printing labels, processing necessary paperwork</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Shipping and Receiving: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
</div>
