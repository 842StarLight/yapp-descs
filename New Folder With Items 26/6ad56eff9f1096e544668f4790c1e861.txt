<h2>Order Entry Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>BIC Graphic is seeking a VLT - Order Entry Associate. This position would report to the Order Processing Supervisor. The Vendor Liaison Associate promptly and accurately transmits orders to the factories as well as identifies errors of transmission for possible problems or additional training issues. They communicate updated information received from factory direct vendors with customer service, elite, and sales reps.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develops and maintains knowledge of products, pricing, order classifications, shipping methods, and printing methods utilized in the order entry process.</li>
<li>
Develops a good understanding of the factory-direct processes used to decorate products and the needs of production for these processes.</li>
<li>
Promptly audits orders for completeness and accuracy.</li>
<li>
Creates purchase orders and promptly transmits orders to all of our vendors.</li>
<li>
Documents actions taken on all orders in the computer system, including when factories are contacted and routing of orders.</li>
<li>
Works with factories directly on production times, rush requests and customer requests.</li>
<li>
Calls and/or e-mails vendors to clarify unclear information or provide proof approval in order to complete processing of an order.</li>
<li>
Maintains orders based on responses from customers or vendors.</li>
<li>
Receives tracking from vendors and saves it in the system and ensures customers are notified.</li>
<li>
Reaches out to vendors when tracking hasn't been received on orders that are supposed to be closed.</li>
<li>
Answers e-mails from Asia regarding orders transmitted to them</li>
<li>
Maintains effective working relationships with other associates.</li>
<li>
Actively participates in and supports teamwork and continuous process improvement</li>
<li>
Demonstrates flexibility and commitment through cross-training and helping other departments consistent with business needs.</li>
<li>
Performs duties safely and in accordance with company safety policies.</li>
<li>
Acts as backup to order clarification and paper flow specialist positions.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school level education</li>
<li>
Previous office and customer service experience in a fast-paced setting a plus</li>
<li>
One year of order entry or ad prep experience</li>
<li>
Ability to use computer and associated software</li>
<li>
Speed and accuracy in data entry (minimum of 35 wpm); organized, detail-oriented and able to follow instructions a must</li>
<li>
Knowledge of products and process, strong verbal and written communication skills, strong problem-solving and decision-making skills required</li>
</ul>
