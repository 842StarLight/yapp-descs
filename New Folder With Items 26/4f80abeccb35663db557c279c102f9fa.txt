<b>PRINCIPAL ACTIVITIES</b><b>:</b><b>This position does the following in accordance with all applicable Federal, State and local laws / regulations and the Company’s policies, procedures and guidelines:</b><br>
</div>
<br>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li><p>Sales Support:Typing, filing, preparing reports, fulfilling any requests from District Managers and Division Manager.<br>
</p></li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li><p>Discount Processing:Process discounts through Incentive Automation system, or through SAP.Be familiar with all current Sales Bulletins, and all basic rules of incentivesPrepare reports as needed.<br>
</p></li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li><p>Harvest Rental:Send annual Harvest Rental contract.Track Harvest Rental units and rental payments in database.Division must confirm that all required rental payments have been received, and unit has been R&amp;I’d.Prepare reports when requested.<br>
</p></li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li><p>Accounts Payable:Ensure all bills are signed by the correct supervisor, coded properly, and given to appropriate Manager for signature.Track monthly expenses in budget spreadsheet.Prepare and distribute monthly report.</p></li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li><p><br>
Mail:Process incoming and outgoing mail daily.Maintain field personnel address stamps.Maintain field personnel and dealer addresses in Fed Ex system.<br>
</p></li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li><p>Administrative Support:Provide support to the Sales, Service and Parts Departments as needed.<br>
</p></li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li><p>Miscellaneous:Order supplies for Division personnel, copy machine maintenance, mail room upkeep, meeting set up, planning company functions.<br>
</p></li></ul>
