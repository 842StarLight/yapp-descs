<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Account Executive</b></p>
<p>MMGY Global believes the world is a big, beautiful place. We’ve made it our job, passion</p>
<p>and mission to inspire people to experience as much of it as humanly possible. As the</p>
<p>largest travel marketing firm of its kind, we work exclusively with premier travel,</p>
<p>hospitality and entertainment brands to create progressive marketing strategies based</p>
<p>on research and apply these insights to measurable channel marketing plans and</p>
<p>creative work that inspires action. Put another way: we know where travelers are, how</p>
<p>they think and what they want because we’re travelers ourselves. Join us and let’s go</p>
<p>places together.</p>
<p>We have an immediate opening for an Account Executive in our New York City office</p>
<p>working with our Myriad brand. Myriad offers integrated marketing services to</p>
<p>international destinations that are targeting the North American traveler.</p>
<p>This is a mid-level account management position involving a high degree of client</p>
<p>interaction and project management skills.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul><li> Interacting daily with client – phone, email, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work within the account team to develop and execute annual sales, marketing &amp;</li></ul>
<p>public relations plans for our clients (based on clients Scope of Work)</p>
<ul><li> Oversee execution of all regular tasks/paperwork associated with managing the</li></ul>
<p>account — client proposals, open jobs, develop creative briefs, proofing, etc.</p>
<ul><li> Coordinating and managing efforts with all internal agency departments,</li></ul>
<p>including media, creative, production, interactive, social, eCRM, scheduling and</p>
<p>accounting</p>
<ul><li> Presenting client deliverable's, including marketing plans, scopes of work,</li></ul>
<p>creative and more</p>
<ul><li> Creating and maintaining project timelines</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage final delivery of client reports and project status updates</li></ul>
<ul><li> Planning and maintenance of client budgets</li></ul>
<ul><li> Facilitating all client media plans, ad production and fulfillment</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborating with Project Management Office to manage and coordinate</li></ul>
<p>interactive digital projects through all phases of creative design, development</p>
<p>and launch</p>
<ul><li> Planning and maintenance of client budget</li></ul>
<ul><li> Following agency traffic and billing procedures</li></ul>
<ul><li> Leading team meetings, client conference calls and regular internal and external</li></ul>
<p>status calls</p>
<ul><li> Strategically directing accounts, including leading marketing planning and</li></ul>
<p>messaging meetings</p>
<p><b>Experience Requirements:</b></p>
<ul><li> 3+ years of advertising agency experience in account service or project</li></ul>
<p>management at the Coordinator level or higher, with at least one year as an</p>
<ul><li> Account Executive or Project Manager</li></ul>
<ul><li> Team-oriented approach</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent customer service skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to prioritize and multitask</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong verbal, written and presentation communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Comfortable working under tight deadlines</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proofreading, attention to detail and organizational skills are extremely important</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience in marketing strategy, principles and processes</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge of basic creative and production concepts/terms</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge of interactive strategy and management, including web, social,</li></ul>
<p>mobile</p>
<ul><li> Advanced knowledge of digital programs and content management systems</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge of traditional and digital media</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge of Microsoft Office software</li></ul>
<p>Knowledge of hospitality or travel industry is highly considered</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education Requirements:</b></p>
<ul><li> Four-year college degree</li></ul>
<p>Reports to: Group Account Director</p>
<ul><li>Occasional travel required</li></ul>
