<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Under the supervision of the Director of Analytical Sciences, the Research Scientist will develop, validate and transfer analytical test methods for Young Living products and ingredients, operate and maintain analytical instruments including GC/MS, GC/FID, HPLC, LC/MS, Polarimeter, Refractive Index, Densitometer and FTIR, and understand the operational and theoretical details of analytical instruments listed above.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<p>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of the job.</p>
<ul>
<li>Operate and maintain analytical instrumentation such as GC/MS, GC/FID, HPLC, LC/MS, Polarimeter, Refractive Index, Densitometer and FTIR</li>
<li>Develop sample preparation and test methods to validate ingredients and label claims for raw materials and finished products</li>
<li>Establish product specifications
</li>
<li>Create SOPs for research functions, duties, and operations
</li>
<li>Collect technical information crucial for the creation of products to support new and existing products</li>
<li>Provide research and development support to Operations, Product Management, Marketing, and Quality departments</li>
<li>Research and report results of published scientific literature</li>
<li>Partner with product development team members to characterize product ingredients</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate (read, write and speak) in English</li>
<li>Critically evaluate research, data, and analytical test results</li>
<li>Prioritize workload based on company needs and values</li>
<li>Decisive experimental decision making to achieve accurate and reliable test results</li>
<li>Work competently both individually and in a team environment</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge analyzing essential oils and natural products using HPLC, GC, FTIR, polarimetry, specific gravity, and refractive index instruments</li>
<li>Ability to work with other scientists to solve problems</li>
<li>Skilled in computer proficiency, including Microsoft products and other applicable software or applications</li>
<li>Knowledge of Chromeleon and/or Chemstation/Mass Hunter software is preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibility</b></p>
<p>This position has no direct supervisory responsibility but does serve as a coach and mentor for other positions in the department</p>
