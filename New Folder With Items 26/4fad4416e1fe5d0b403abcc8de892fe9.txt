<b>HR Strategic Communications - Consulting Senior Manager</b><br>
<br>
Deloitte Consulting’s Human Capital practice is a leading global advisor and implementation partner working with companies, governments, and organizations around the world. Our goal is to improve our clients’ organizational results and impact by focusing on challenges at the intersection of business and people.<br>
<br>
We are looking for an HR Strategic Communications - Consulting Senior Manager to join our team in Denver, CO.<br>
<br>
<b>Work you’ll do</b><br>
As a Business Development Senior Manager - HR Strategic Communications in the Human Capital as a Service practice of Deloitte’s Human Capital group, you will perform the following job responsibilities:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Drive Business Development initiatives and bring in new Projects</li>
<li>Attend sales calls, assess needs, develop proposals and deliver presentations.</li>
<li>Balance multiple assignments and schedule workload to meet established deadlines</li>
<li>Participate in presentations to other functional groups within the firm and engage in eminence building activities Human Capital</li>
<li>Participate in local and regional team-building events as well as national communications networking activities</li>
<li>Work with Client Executive Teams to understand their Human Capital goals
</li>
<li>Manage communications work streams from initial client meetings and concept development to final execution</li>
<li>Develop project plans and timelines and document project budgets</li>
<li>Develop strategic communication plans and campaigns addressing global human resources, benefits, compensation and change management issues</li>
<li>Keep abreast of technical developments in employee benefits and compensation, and communication trends; participate in outside targeted industry organizations.</li>
<li>Mentor junior resources</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Basic Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>8+ years of related work experience in HR-related consulting</li>
<li>Experience in Business Development and Sales of HR Consulting engagements</li>
<li>Project management and work plan development experience</li>
<li>Experience with or exposure to strategic change management and change communication strategies and assignments</li>
<li>Experience managing teams</li>
<li>Working knowledge of employee benefits, compensation and human resource programs</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, time management and follow through</li>
<li>Ability to work well with others in a team environment</li>
<li>Willingness to travel up to 80%</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Preferred:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience in a consulting firm</li>
<li>Experience working with designers to develop graphic identities and approaches, and select and
</li>
<li>Experience in managing relationships with outside vendors for design, production and distribution needs and on-line communications</li>
<li>Experience with benefits communication</li>
<li>CEBS certification</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>The team</b><br>
As part of Deloitte Consulting LLP, our Human Capital professionals work closely with clients around the world to develop effective and efficient people-centered solutions. Our team helps clients define their talent strategy and align it with their executive business vision using innovative strategic frameworks that enhance HR service delivery models, set the stage for continuously improving operational excellence, and reimagines the HR brand.<br>
<br>
Our consultants work collaboratively with clients to develop competitive rewards programs so that they can recruit and retain talent effectively. In addition to applying these skills in traditional settings, our consultants provide advisory services related to these issues in mergers and acquisitions, governance and risk management, enterprise cost reduction efforts, and other transformational business initiatives.<br>
