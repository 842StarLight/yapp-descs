<p><b>
Brief Summary of Role</b></p>
<p>
The Integration Architect will work with customers to analyze their requirements, architect and design solutions, and implement cloud to cloud and cloud to on-premise integration solutions.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Participate in pre-sales calls to conduct feasibility assessment and provide creative solutions and level-of-effort estimates for customer requests and requirements.</li>
<li>
Evaluate middleware solutions and make customer-specific recommendations</li>
<li>
Define system architecture for data integration and data management</li>
<li>
Clearly articulate integration best practices, design patterns, best practices, and recommendations to customer IT teams and also business sponsors.</li>
<li>
Provide solution and integration development with cloud and on-premise software including requirements, functional specs and integration design.</li>
<li>
Lead the integration of business processes across disparate systems.
</li>
<li>Drive the integration workstream including definition, design, implementation and go live activities.
</li>
<li>Work on multiple customer projects concurrently</li>
<li>
Review, monitor project teams adherence to coding standards &amp; implementation framework.</li>
<li>
Document project <i>best practices, learning and</i> share with the consulting team.</li>
<li>
Mentor consultants &amp; onboard new consultants to Salesforce Integration.</li>
</ul>
