<h2>Senior Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Based in Palo Alto, California, Cardinal Analytx Solutions is a predictive analytics company that leverages machine learning and clinical expertise to identify future cost and rising risk. We were founded with the vision of helping patients obtain better care, sooner. Most of healthcare today is very reactive – with care being delivered when someone is already ill. We believe that healthcare should be about keeping individuals and their families well – to prevent them from ever becoming sick. Through machine learning and the extensive experience of our founders, team, and advisers in healthcare, we are committed to helping healthcare organizations see around the corner so that they can take a proactive approach to helping patients.</p>
<p>
On the Product team, our mission is to discover, improve and deliver products that meet customer pain points and revolutionize the healthcare industry. You will work closely with product managers, data scientists, engineers and customer teams to understand how our data can be used to tell a compelling story to customers and find opportunities to productize analytics. In addition to strong analytical skills, this person has solid business acumen, communication skills and ability to drive at customer pain points.</p>
<p><b>
As a Data Analyst on the Product Team, You Will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop deep understanding our products, machine learning methodology and outputs, and our customers analytic pain points and needs.</li>
<li>
Translate your insights into stories, visuals and analytics that help drive product understanding and adoption.
</li>
<li>Collaborate and influence across cross functional stakeholders including sales, marketing, engineering, data science and clinical</li>
<li>
Be an integral part of the product team, contributing to and innovating on analytics and business intelligence</li>
<li>
Assess business value and feasibility of productizing analytics</li>
<li>
Document and translate analytical methodology into engineering requirements for scale / productization
</li>
<li>Present complex analytical concepts to internal and external stakeholders and executives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
You Will Love this Job If:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You are curious. You thrive in the unknown, the chaotic and the ambiguous. You are tenacious and courageous, moving people and projects forward.
</li>
<li>You can see the bigger picture while also getting into the details.
</li>
<li>You are passionate about making an impact in healthcare and want to work in a fast-paced environment.
</li>
<li>You excel not only at running analyses, but finding creative ways to visualize and tell a story with data that solves customer pain points</li>
<li>
You enjoy working with internal and external customers, discovering how to solve pain points and business needs with data and analytics
</li>
<li>You enjoy working with people and presenting your work</li>
<li>
You enjoy the details and appreciate the foundation of rigorous code, methodology, documentation, and QA</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
You Should Get in Touch if You:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Have at least 4+ years of industry experience</li>
<li>
Have extensive experience in advanced SQL</li>
<li>
Nice to have: Experience with at least one scripting language (e.g., R, Python)</li>
<li>
Have experience with multi-million record data sets and across many databases</li>
<li>
Have experience working with healthcare data</li>
<li>
Have experience working with both business leaders and technical experts</li>
<li>
Bachelors Degree required.</li>
</ul>
