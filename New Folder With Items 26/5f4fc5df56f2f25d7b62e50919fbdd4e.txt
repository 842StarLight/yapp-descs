<h2>Sales & Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Who We Are: </b></p>
<p>Classic Cosmetics, Inc. is a world-class Contract Manufacturer of Color Cosmetics based in Southern California since 1989. We are experts in our field and provide Turnkey manufacturing services to many of the world’s largest cosmetic companies, as well as many of the most popular social media and entertainment brands. Classic Cosmetics promotes a culture that encourages work-life balance and personal growth for its team members.</p>
<p><b>Who We Are Looking For: </b></p>
<p>We’re looking for a talented Sales &amp; Marketing Manager that will lead and be responsible for the development and implementation of business to business Marketing Strategic Plans to achieve marketing and sales objectives. If you have a deep understanding and/ or passion of the beauty industry with a strong knowledge of cosmetic manufacturing processes, we want to speak to you!</p>
<p><b>In order to be successful as our Sales &amp; Marketing Manager, you will have to: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement B2B Strategic Marketing programs, plans, and policies to drive sales.</li>
<li>Produce and oversee content for the company’s online presence and in-house publications.</li>
<li>Innovate concepts that create new business opportunities.</li>
<li>Establish and monitor annual Marketing, Advertising and Trade show plans and budgets.</li>
<li>Attend Industry trade shows and events, to promote company capabilities.</li>
<li>Generate market research analysis to track industry trends and competitor’s marketing activities.</li>
<li>Maintain a positive, professional manner and attitude in all business dealings.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge in cosmetic manufacturing.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>To be our Sales &amp; Marketing Manager you must have following qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree required. In Marketing or Communications preferred.</li>
<li>5+ years Sales &amp; Marketing Management experience.</li>
<li>Experience in Cosmetic or Personal care industry required.</li>
<li>Experience in Manufacturing industry required.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and copy writing skills crucial.</li>
<li>Proven track record of success in leading the development and implementation of integrated marketing programs, that drive business results.</li>
<li>Experience driving direct marketing innovation with clear results in customer acquisition, retention and sales.</li>
<li>Excellent negotiation and sales skills.</li>
<li>Able to work under pressure and close deadlines.</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills. Proficient in MS Office, Outlook, Adobe suite.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PLEASE ONLY APPLY IF YOU MEET THE REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: DOE</p>
<p>Location: Chatsworth, CA</p>
