As an Associate Director in CrossCountry’s Business Transformation Practice, you will serve as an extension of our leadership team to lead, develop, and build our finance transformation and optimization services. In this role you’ll help our clients optimize and align their operating model (organization, process, and technology) to be a better strategic business partner to the business.</div>

<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities: Client Delivery</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Partner with our clients to create, develop, plan, and execute effective and consensus-driven optimization and transactions strategies to include: assessing current state, visualizing desired state operating model, providing a clear roadmap to guide the transformation, ensuring organizational structure maximizes productivity and/or control issues, etc.</li>
<li>
Manage projects through creating, coordinating, and maintaining an integrated project plan as well as using project management tools</li>
<li>
Lead projects across the project lifecycle from current state documentation, gap analysis, requirements gathering, future state design, and implementation across the organizational, process, technology and data dimensions</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities: Practice Leadership</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Serve as a key leader in our practice by guiding and directing strategic firm and practice initiatives</li>
<li>
Lead marketing and business development efforts in support of all the firm’s services with a focus on continuing to develop Business Transformation's Finance Optimization service offerings</li>
<li>
Developing new client relationships as well as expanding at existing clients</li>
<li>
Participate in firm-wide committees and internal initiatives such as thought leadership, industry forums, recruiting, sales/business development, marketing, training, and organizational development</li>
<li>
Manage teams by guiding performance against objectives, creating an atmosphere of trust and collaboration, modeling leadership behaviors, and encouraging continuous improvement/innovation</li>
<li>
Lead coaching family by serving as a role model, coach, mentor and liaison to the leadership team</li>
</ul></div>
