<p><b>GENERAL SUMMARY</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>Be a part of our team and make a difference in our residentsâ€™ lives and your life, too. We do more than offer jobs, we offer careers and a great place to work. . We welcome you to join our team as we work together to provide the best retirement experience for all our residents at our CCRC. We are seeking a positive, compassionate, customer service-focused team member for the <b>Marketing Director </b>position. We are excited to have you meet our team where we believe in our core anchors of <b>People First...Always, Compassionate Action...Now, and Respectful Communication...Everywhere.</b></p>
<p>Acting as the lead sales professional, this position is responsible for presenting and selling the community to prospective residents resulting in deposits, reservations and settlements. Additionally, the Marketing Director, manages the occupancy process from first inquiry to receipt of deposit to move-in.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages the occupancy process from first inquiry to receipt of deposit to move-in in relation to the representation, negotiation and promotion of the community to that individual.</li>
<li>Works with the prospective resident, family members and key influencers (physician, attorney, trust officer, as appropriate) to facilitate a timely and beneficial commitment to the community.</li>
<li>Manages all information relating to the prospective resident/inquiry: communication with administration, communication with direct supervision and accurate documentation of all contact in the Leads Management System, REPS.</li>
<li>Promotes the occupancy process through ongoing contact with a prospective resident via telephone, personal visits, tours and home visit activities as appropriate.</li>
<li>Provides to the Marketing Director and Executive Director marketing planning information about opportunities or challenges, to be used in the development of strategies for maintaining optimal occupancy levels.</li>
<li>Understands and articulately represents all documents related to residency, such as Residency Agreements.</li>
<li>Conducts daily sales appointments with prospective residents.</li>
<li>Participates in and helps plan the community sales events.</li>
<li>Conducts speeches and presentations at community sales events.</li>
<li>Identifies, prospective leads through referrals, phone interactions, face-to-face interactions, advertising leads, etc. and follow-through to inspire them to take action.<br>
</li>
</ul>
