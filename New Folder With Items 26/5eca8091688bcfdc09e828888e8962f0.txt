<div>
National and Alamo at Boston Logan Airport has an exciting opportunity for a Full-Time Greeter! The Greeter provides superior, friendly, efficient service during all aspects of the rental car process by greeting and fulfilling club member needs as they relate to the Emerald Aisle rental process. The Greeter provides members with appropriate vehicle selection as determined by the Emerald Aisle program guidelines, creates and maintains a friendly, efficient environment for all customers, and assists in making all transactions quick and efficient. The Greeter performs all responsibilities with a focus on the needs of our customers and in accordance with our Quality Standards.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position pays $14.50/hour and has potential for a monthly customer service bonus and sales incentive which can significantly impact overall compensation. A comprehensive benefits package is available including medical,dental, and vision insurance, paid vacation time and holidays, profit sharing, and 401(k) with company match. The required full-time schedule is Sunday, Monday, Thursday, Friday, and Saturday from 3pm-11:30pm. A 30 minute break will be given each day.</div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li><div>Work proactively with drivers, service agents, handheld return agents and managers to ensure proper vehicle supply.</div></li>
<li><div>
Welcome members to the facility when they exit the bus or arrive on the lot.</div></li>
<li><div>
Direct customers to exit booth, provide local directions and maps and provide return directions where applicable.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist members with questions and concerns to minimize counter visits.</div></li>
<li><div>
Communicate customer service issues to management.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure that hangtag information is completed correctly.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain clean low mileage fleet mix requirements.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain Emerald Aisle for cleanliness.</div></li>
<li><div>
Thank member for their business.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide upgraded vehicles on request.</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform other customer service related duties in addition to those listed, to ensure our service meets the needs of our customers.</div></li>
</ul>
