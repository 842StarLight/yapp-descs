<h2>Central Customer Service Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Mobile Instrument Service and Repair, Inc., among the nation's leaders in repair and maintenance services for reusable surgical equipment, is seeking a <b>Customer Service Specialist</b> for our <b>Central Customer Service Division</b>. Our primary goal is to consistently provide a high level of support and technical experience to the team to maintain clients, generate revenue, and grow the client base. The ideal candidate will be professional, organized, a fast learner, have a positive attitude and be ready to get to work. You will demonstrate a concern for customer service and attention to detail. This is a Monday thru Friday 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM position with an hour lunch.<br>
<br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
Provide customer service to sales representatives and facilities<br>
Verify package profile and create a work order<br>
Provide price quote to customer or sales representative<br>
Obtain purchase order if not already assigned<br>
Prepare packaging slip<br>
Verify the total number of instruments on a work order<br>
Take bin to ship for processing<br>
Provide inside sales when necessary</div>
