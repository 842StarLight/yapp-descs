Overview
<br><br>
Onica's Global Customer Solutions (GCS) team acts as a trusted technical partner to our customers and works closely with Onica's sales, solutions and delivery teams. The GCS team is comprised of Customer Solutions Architects (CSAs) that are responsible for supporting sales as technical architects engaging with our customers during the pre-sales process. The Lead CSAs will act as leads within the team and are assigned to work with Onica's most strategic enterprise or large public sector accounts within a specific geography.
<br><br>
We're looking for a North East-based candidate for a Lead CSA role working with strategic accounts in the North East market. We're looking for senior candidates with strong professional experience working as consultants prior to joining our team. Flexibility to travel and meet customers within their assigned region, as well as members of the AWS team working with Onica at their offices or during various events, is a requirement for the role.
<br><br>
The Lead CSA will build a professional relationship with enterprise customers, acting as their trusted advisor. The role includes aspects of business development, client management, and technical consulting. The Lead CSA will, from time to time, assemble teams of Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) from the delivery teams, to deliver education to customers and demo Onica's and AWS's capabilities and help the customer with decisions related to technical direction, migration and transformation initiatives. They will have joint accountability with their Account Executive partner from Onica's sales team for the successful enablement, growth and retention of ongoing business opportunities between Onica and the clients they're assigned to.
<br><br>
