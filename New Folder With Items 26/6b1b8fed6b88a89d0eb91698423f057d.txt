<h2>Senior Account Manager - Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for a person who loves to work in a fast paced and dynamic environment. Are you looking to land a job where you can talk to customers about leading technology that can transform their world? We are seeking a high-energy professional with a proven track record of leading enterprise accounts who understands how technology is driving dynamic changes in exciting segments like Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Cloud and advanced software development.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This full-time position requires close working relationships with NVIDIA solution architects, product marketing, business units, and developer relations, to drive revenue for the company. This person should have the ability to shift easily from working with senior level executives and strategic decision makers to category managers and buyers both inside and outside the company.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Communicating NVIDIA product roadmaps, strategies and customer outcomes while deeply understanding how this helps our enterprise operation deliver value to our customers and increase revenue is key. This is a fantastic opportunity to be part of the exploding market opportunity for Deep Learning, Cloud, Data Center and Artificial Intelligence! Help build the next generation of computing and visualization solutions in the market!
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for all aspects of strategy creation and implementation, forecasting, sales account management, training and education with a large diverse strategic account.
</li>
<li>Manage customer engagement, business development and sales activity.
</li>
<li>Build and enhance strategic relationship, drive revenue growth and product footprint
</li>
<li>Direct experience in account management, business development or sales role representing systems, software with large Fortune 500 accounts
</li>
<li>Work closely with partners, OEMs, and ecosystem partners to build and execute go-to-market plans to accelerate growth and adoption of the enterprise product family
</li>
<li>Ensure tight technical collaboration between NVIDIA engineering and the customer to drive alignment and prioritization of initiatives.
</li>
<li>Building winning capabilities and leading change
</li>
<li>Evangelize the use of NVIDIA's platform, products and technologies to customers and partners
</li>
<li>Ability to travel as needed
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What We Need To See:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA degree
</li>
<li>8+ years in technical sales in Enterprise/Hyperscale or High-Performance Computing (HPC) Industries
</li>
<li>A real passion for getting things done in a dynamic sales and technology environment
</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record leading significant revenue and overachieving targets to meet stretch goals
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to provide thought leadership, think strategically and effectively communicate vision (both written and verbal) and influence cross-functionally
</li>
<li>Strong communication, presentation and business acumen
</li>
</ul>
