<h2>Technical Support Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Technical Support Manager is responsible for the day-to-day operation of the organization’s Technical Support team, ensuring the support and expertise is available to assist our dealers in a timely and efficient manner. This individual will drive the vision of the department to ensure we’re providing proactive value and support to all our dealers and partners</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the Technical Service team who provide in depth support to our dealers and partners through live phone and email support</li>
<li>Oversees daily activities of service staff including scheduling, assigning work, interviewing, hiring, evaluating performance and resolving problems through live phone and email support.</li>
<li>Develop and communicate dealer service report cards, create a distribution schedule to ensure we’re communicating effectively</li>
<li>Maintains service bulletins, coordinates communication to dealers and ensures all necessary updates are performed on PED inventory</li>
<li>Develops, maintains and uses CRM software (Sales Net) for the purpose of identifying issues and trends.</li>
<li>Create and distribute weekly service reports that include: Top call types, warranty detail, product specific detail</li>
<li>Answers questions and discusses complex issues with customers regarding services as specified in equipment warranty agreement.</li>
<li>Coordinates and develops all Service related training for the dealers including and not limited to new dealer training, service school training, annual schools for all lines.</li>
<li>Initiatives monthly Service Calls with Exmark and our outside support staff to ensure updates and trends are being discussed and identified</li>
<li>Share information gained from manufacturer training schools with all service staff.</li>
<li>Helps identify obsolete parts and equipment and determine a strategy for the sale or use</li>
<li>Responsible for the product back orders to ensure all teams have the most up to date ETA’s and information needed to communicate effectively with our dealers</li>
<li>Responsible for the aging inventory to ensure were maximizing all sales channels</li>
<li>Review warranty submissions by dealer to ensure compliance with the vendors requirements to maximize the warranty approval percentage</li>
<li>Ownership on the creation and cataloguing of the processes and procedures for both the technical service and parts fulfillment activities</li>
<li>Develop a succession planning for all positions on the team to ensure there is no knowledge or support gaps</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience </b></p>
<p>A minimum of two years’ service and repair experience; Associates degree or technical school certification required (sr. service technician) Management exp.</p>
<p>Work Schedule</p>
<p>The position is full time typically working 45+ hours a week Monday through Friday. Some travel will be required to visit dealer locations and service training's.</p>
