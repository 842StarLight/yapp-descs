<h2>Senior Copywriter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Here at Universal Parks &amp; Resorts™, you can enjoy an exciting culture where creating our unique Universal brand of thrilling experiences is a top priority. We are passionate about what we do and take pride in the exclusive ways we deliver fun.</p>
<p>
Comprised of Universal Orlando Resort, Universal Studios Hollywood, Universal Studios Japan, Universal Studios Singapore, and a new destination coming in Beijing, China; we are proud to offer a universe of extraordinary opportunities. It takes a special kind of person to work at all of our destinations. We value individuals who know how to be part of a team, who embrace fun and are serious about their work. It takes hard work, dedication, knowledge and just the right amount of Universal spirit to open our doors of opportunity. We champion the power of cultural diversity and inclusion and continuously strive to maintain a team that is a reflection of our global audience.</p>
<p>
Being a part of the Universal family also comes with extraordinary perks. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, career development, excellent growth opportunities, free park admission, complimentary passes, and more!</p>
<p>
Join us if you want to collaborate, innovate, develop, and deliver the most compelling entertainment experiences imaginable that drive growth for the Universal brand around the world. Experience a workplace that will inspire you to be Bold! Apply today.</p>
<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY</b>: Writes smart, engaging copy and leads conceptual development for projects in any medium. Actively collaborates with peers, teaming every day with Art Directors, other Writers, Producers, Editors and peers. Has strong experience creating videos and commercials for broadcast, social and other mediums. Uses their understanding of marketing strategy and creativity to champion idea innovation and to push beyond the expected. Is equally adept at brainstorming big-picture/blue-sky ideas and crafting detailed, in-depth experiential copy.</p>
<p><b>
MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Creates innovative solutions to support Marketing, Sales and communication strategies. Works as thought-leader and brand storyteller, bringing refreshing ideas for projects of all sizes. Utilizes their talents with a specific focus on writing and content creation for video, digital, social and web pieces. Maintains current knowledge of marketing strategies and trends.</li>
<li>Understands, embraces and evolves our unique Brand voice. Works daily alongside Art Directors, Producers, Editors, Project Managers, IMCs, Creative Directors and peers across departments to develop concepts and copy that work together to achieve our goals.</li>
<li>Keeps commitments by delivering exceptional work on-time and on-budget. Brings enthusiasm and positive thinking to every opportunity.</li>
<li>Writes smart, engaging, strategic, insightful copy and leads conceptual development to provide creative solutions for projects in any medium, especially video.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Deep understanding of creative strategy and history of working with creative briefs.</li>
<li>Proven ability to conceive, write and execute concepts that work in multiple mediums.</li>
<li>Strong experience in scriptwriting/ideating creative video content for broadcast or digital mediums is essential.</li>
<li>Must present work product/portfolio.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION:</b> Bachelor's degree from a four-year college or university in Communications, Journalism, Advertising or Broadcasting preferred.</p>
<p><b>
EXPERIENCE:</b> 5-7 Years of conceptual copywriting in an advertising agency or in-house advertising department environment, required. Candidates with fewer years may be considered if they’ve complete coursework or an advanced degree from a specialized advertising post-graduate school.</p>
<p><b>
ADDITIONAL INFORMATION: </b>Storyteller. Innovator. Thought partner. Always curious.</p>
<p>
Our writers collaborate to create insightful concepts and original ideas that engage, surprise and entertain our guests in thrilling and unexpected ways. They are able to simply and powerfully express our unique brand voice in every medium—from banner ads and brochures to short videos, broadcast work and beyond.</p>
<p>
Here, you’ll write smart and funny copy that will influence communications of all kinds with big-picture creative ideas and granular solutions. It is vital that you have the confidence and natural talent to work individually and with cross-disciplinary teams of art directors, producers and designers to develop original, clever and strategically sound creative concepts for every medium.</p>
<p>
Our copywriters are leaders who work alongside with Brand partners and fellow creative teams within our agency and across our company. We seek talent, spirit, energy and heart, not prima donnas. We know awards are cool. We have our share, but that’s neither here nor there.</p>
<p>
Our new Senior Copywriter will be an important part of our entire team, working with project management and creative leadership to ensure that you have the proper tools and information required to meet timelines and business goals. You will have a deep understanding of Creative Strategy and a history of working with Creative Briefs. You will help us grow as a creative catalyst and a champion for innovation and fresh ideas. You will be awesome. Actually, you probably already are—but don’t let it go to your head.</p>
<p>
Your talent, skills and experience will be rewarded with a competitive compensation package.</p>
