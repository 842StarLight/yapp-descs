<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview<br>
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<p>ErtelAlsop is looking for looking for a motivated marketing professional to join our team. This role requires the ability to learn and understand how filtration works at a functional level and translate that knowledge into useful documentation, examples, and stories for users with varying levels of technical expertise. You will be expected to develop intimate product knowledge and to learn to speak the language of a unique marketplace.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Marketing Coordinator will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Annual marketing planning</li>
<li>
Development and Execution of PPC Campaigns, Advertising, and Trade Shows</li>
<li>
Technical Content Writing/sourcing, Website SEO, and Email Campaigns</li>
<li>
Lead Generation and Marketing Automation (HubSpot &amp; Zoho)</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Responsibilities</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Manage digital marketing strategy to achieve performance-based goals while maximizing brand building activities for all sales markets (Pharma, F&amp;B, Cosmetics, Specialty Chemicals, etc)</li>
<li>
Drive end-to-end campaign development, partnering with marketing agency, and 3rd parties to ensure that strategy delivers against KPI’s and supports business objectives</li>
<li>
Own and actively optimize our digital marketing channels (paid and unpaid), including social, search, CRM, email, and website</li>
<li>
Develop, measure, and optimize KPI’s across campaigns and tactics for all areas of responsibility</li>
<li>
Build and manage sales and marketing KPI dashboards; analyze results and provide learnings &amp; recommendations on performance improvement</li>
<li>
Executing lead generation and lead management initiatives including data management, list acquisition and creation</li>
<li>
Generation of new paid search campaigns, ad groups, and accounts</li>
<li>
Adapt and convey technical material in a concise and effective manner</li>
<li>
Update and maintain user and technical guides, knowledge-base content, and release notes</li>
<li>
Basic website maintenance and page creation utilizing WordPress CMS</li>
<li>
Coordinate the display of related graphics</li>
<li>
Organize marketing trade shows and events</li>
<li>
Support the sales and marketing firm in daily administrative tasks</li>
</ul>
