<h2>Director, Strategic Account Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Postmates is on a mission to help people unlock the best of their cities -- and their lives -- with an industry-leading on-demand "anything" network. We're the first and still the leading company in the on-demand space -- with a fleet of more than 240,000 Postmates, the largest network of merchants in the U.S. and a brand that has become a verb in pop culture. We're looking for people who aren't afraid to be first -- or eccentric, and who are passionate about creating the new, new.
<br><br>
The Postmates Platform team is growing rapidly and you will help shape our products and programs alongside Product, Engineering, BD and BizOps. Through the Postmates Delivery API, our partners can offer on-demand delivery via their own website or app - Postmates provides the logistics and delivery fleet on the backend. This is more than just food - some of the biggest brands in retail leverage Postmates' Delivery as a Service - and the size of the opportunity in market is only increasing.
<br><br>
Our Strategic API Partners are critical to the success of Postmates, and we are looking for a leader to help us build and develop strategic relationships with high value merchants, lead and manage a team and drive significant revenue. As a Lead, you will be responsible for owning and growing Postmates' key merchant relationships to support and grow their business through our DaaS platform. You will be part advocate, part detective, and use data driven recommendations to support your portfolio of top-tier partners that have fully integrated with the Postmates' delivery API.
<br><br>
Your are self-motivated, customer-oriented, data-driven, and have exceptional interpersonal and relationship-building skills.
<br><br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Own key merchant relationships with our key accounts across all markets, including exclusive relationships</li>
<li>Manage a team of Strategic Account Managers - develop and implement new initiatives, strategies and programs to help increase the team's productivity and to standardize an excellent partner experience</li>
<li>Ideate, prioritize and execute a set of monthly promotions for each strategic brand on the Postmates platform</li>
<li>Prioritize and evangelize large Merchant product initiatives based on your knowledge and feedback from the merchants</li>
<li>Develop a merchant value proposition that elevates Postmates above the competition</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with our Product, Operations, and Sales teams to make sure top merchants have the best possible experience when working with Postmates</li>
</ul>
