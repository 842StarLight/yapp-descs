<h2>Director of Marketing - Full Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Director of Marketing develops and drives marketing strategies, analysis and insights for customer acquisition, customer relationship management, and programmatic marketing. Collaborates to develop financially sound, creative, compelling and differentiated marketing initiatives that drive sales, deliver a consistent effective message and increase awareness and preference for Borsheims and our Signature Service. Oversees all public relations for the organization. Trains, develops, provides on-going feedback, and one-on-one coaching.</p>
<p><b>The ideal candidate should: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>Min 10 years of agency or in-house marketing experience</li>
<li>Min 4 years of direct management experience</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of B2C Marketing</li>
<li>Ability to link team’s work to support company purpose, mission, values, goals, and strategies.</li>
<li>Knowledge of current marketing channels including digital, print, and social media</li>
<li>Excellent storytelling and messaging skills that appeal to audience segments</li>
<li>Ability to think strategically but also produce tangible deliverables and elements for multi-part campaigns</li>
<li>Familiar with SEO, PLA, CMS, PPC and CRM</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops and drives marketing strategies, analysis and insights for customer acquisition, customer relationship management, and programmatic marketing.</li>
<li>Collaborates with internal departments, external partners, and customers to develop financially sound, creative, compelling and differentiated marketing initiatives that drive sales, deliver a consistent effective message and increase awareness and preference for Borsheims and our Signature Service.</li>
<li>Oversees the development of all advertising and marketing content for both traditional and new media, including social media, email, print, web, audio and visual. Conceptualizes and launches multi-channel marketing campaigns to support the business development goals of the company. Oversees the development of the content calendar for all channels.</li>
<li>Oversees and develops the overall marketing budget. Maximizes the productivity of the advertising budget; reallocating resources from marginal programs to new ventures and programs that provide a greater return.</li>
<li>Acts as brand champion for the organization, ensuring all materials maintain Borsheims branding and Signature Service messaging.</li>
<li>Oversees and develops tracking metrics and success criteria for all marketing programs and activities. Reviews industry data, examines and analyzes statistical data, competitor information, sales, and salesperson input on customer perceptions, preferences, and buying habits to forecast future marketing trends, identify growth opportunities, guide marketing planning, build strong selling points and understands targets. Provides results to executive team.</li>
<li>Oversees and manages marketing supplier and partner relationships.</li>
<li>Oversees production of catalogs, including coordinating merchandise selection process, assisting buyers in negotiating co-op agreements with merchandise vendors; oversees photography, copy, mailing list preparation, color separations, paper and printing buying, staff orientation, and sales support materials.</li>
<li>With direction from the buying team, coordinates and negotiates local and national cooperative advertising and sales promotions with product vendors, fully utilizing vendors as a resource and image supporter.</li>
<li>Oversees all public relations efforts, including traditional and new media. Manages corporate communications and press relation activities in support of company events, happenings, or other items newsworthy to the public.</li>
<li>Trains, develops, provides on-going feedback, one on one coaching and administers employee performance reviews.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as requested by CFO.</li>
</ul>

<li>Ability to hear and speak well enough to converse with others in person, via telephone and paging system.</li>
<li>Dexterity to write in small print, operate a 10-key, operate a computer keyboard, and operate office equipment.</li>
<li>Stamina to sit for over eight hours.</li>
</ul>
