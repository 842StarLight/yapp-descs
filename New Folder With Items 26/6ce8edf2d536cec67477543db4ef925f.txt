<br>
<p>Xactly Corporation was founded to solve an important challenge: how to help people everywhere connect and unleash their human potential through seamless, perfectly aligned Sales Performance Management. Xactly’s comprehensive Sales Performance Management (SPM) suite leverages data from a sales performance AI platform to provide timely insights across the entire SPM value chain, from sales planning to execution, including compensation to optimization. Xactly takes Sales Performance Management from a dark art to a science, providing decision-makers with the data insights they need to tap the motivational power of their incentive compensation. With access to predictive analytics and benchmark data, enterprises can now optimize territories, design the right compensation and incentive plans and accelerate attainment and optimization of sales performance.</p>
<br>
<p>THE OPPORTUNITY</p>
<p>
This position is in charge of one of our most innovative teams here at Xactly, AlignStar. You will be responsible for managing and supporting the organization as a whole as well as being hands on in the development process. You will partner with other Engineering Managers in the company to lead and drive continuous process improvements and innovation as well as establish trust across the org with Product Managers, Support, Data Center Operations, and peer engineering teams.</p>
<br>
<p>As a functional Engineering Manager, you will help engineers to develop their career plans and assign project work tailored to their skill set. You will be a hands on engineer when needed to assist your engineering team to ensure quality code and perfect releases.</p>
<br>
<p>THE TEAM</p>
<p>
Xactly’s Engineering team is growing rapidly and we need someone who can work closely with recruitment to build out this SalesPlanning team! Covering offices in San Jose, Denver, Bangalore, and Toronto, we have a strong focus on cutting-edge and high-quality code and products. All engineers are encouraged to operate independently with the highly level of accountability, but also have the ability to be a team player and work with multiple teams.</p>
<p>
THE SKILL SET</p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of prior relevant technical lead or management experience</li>
<li>Master’s or Bachelor's plus 5 years experience in web application or platform development and architecture</li>
<li>Solid hands on experience with Scala and/or Java</li>
<li>Hands on experience with one or more key-value data store such as CouchBase, MongoDB, Hbase or Cassandra.</li>
<li>Experienced in mentoring, managing engineers, recruiting and retaining top talent.</li>
<li>Strong commitment to good engineering discipline and process including code reviews and delivering unit tests in conjunction with feature delivery</li>
<li>Ability to make decisions and work with minimal direction even under pressure</li>
<li>Technically deep enough to participate in roadmap prioritization.</li>
<li>Familiarity and experience with agile (scrum) development process</li>
<li>Experience establishing and reviewing business metrics with the ability to articulate and provide visibility to the team on various business metrics ensuring every new feature developed meets and is tracked towards those metrics.</li>
</ul>
