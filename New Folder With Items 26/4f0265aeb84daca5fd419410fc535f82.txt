<div>The vision of Merch by Amazon (MbA) is to provide customers with unlimited selection for any type of product that can be made on demand, and to produce and ship those products to Customers within two hours of order, anywhere in the world.<br>
<br>
Since launching in Q3 2015, we have scaled the business in the US, UK, and Germany via thousands of Content Creators and millions of designs to become the largest Print on Demand system globally — and we’re just getting started. We empower Content Creators of all sizes, from amateur designers to large brands, to scale their merchandise sales globally without the constraints of managing a supply chain.<br>
<br>
We are looking for an entrepreneurial and creative Site Merchandiser. You will be responsible for changing the way people discover products on Amazon.<br>
<br>

<li>Marketing: support Marketing for our largest brand partners; help them be successful in Merch.</li>
</ul>
Other Responsibilities<br>
<br>
<ul>

</ul>
