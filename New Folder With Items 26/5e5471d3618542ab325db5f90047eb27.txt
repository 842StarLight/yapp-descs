<p><b>Performance Manager, Display</b></p>
<p><br>
<b>
Reports to </b>Director, Programmatic &amp; Display/Video Performance</p>
<p><b>
Location </b>Atlanta, GA</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
Technology drives us. Armed with automotive’s most extensive data library, PureCars offers paid search, social, display, and video advertising to help dealerships reach the right consumer with the right vehicle at the right time. Recognized as one of seven Google Premier SMB Partners in automotive, our award-winning technology is flawlessly designed to drive high probability buyers to a dealer’s site, optimize traffic once on their site, and convert those customers in the showroom. Find out more here: PureCars</p>
<p><br>
<b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Performance Manager, Display/Video will plan, implement, analyze, and update automotive related display/video marketing campaigns utilizing third party RTB (real-time bidding) platforms and PureCars’ proprietary technology. The right candidate will work with Strategy Managers and Clients as needed to ensure strategic optimization initiatives are met.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create display &amp; video campaigns based on individual dealership goals and continually identify opportunities to make measurable improvements through the following actions:</li>
<ul>
<li>
Keyword, channel, and placement research, testing, and analysis</li>
<li>
Line Item &amp; Targeting bid management</li>
<li>
A/B testing; Segmented retargeting</li>
</ul>
<li>
Traffic campaigns, creative assets, and appropriate pixels and tags</li>
<li>
Communicate and coordinate as necessary with sales/customer experience team</li>
<li>
Monitor and report on primary display KPIs as well as manage campaign pacing/spending</li>
<li>
Monitor the marketplace, competitive landscape, industry trends, and third-party data sources to constantly identify new marketing opportunities</li>
<li>
Analyze weekly performance reports for dealers and implement campaign optimizations based on recommendations</li>
<li>
Additionally, this position may be required to perform other responsibilities as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Skills &amp; Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2-4 years of experience in display, video, and/or programmatic campaign management. Automotive industry, a plus</li>
<li>
1-3 years of experience with RTB (real-time bidding) platforms (i.e. DoubleClick Bid Manager, AdRoll, Simpli.fi, Yahoo Behavioral Targeting, Google Ad Network)</li>
<li>
Ability to analyze data from analytics packages, and internal data to make actionable decisions and recommendations</li>
<li>
Detail oriented with the ability to precisely manipulate large, multi-faceted data sets. Pivot tables and V-look-ups, a plus</li>
<li>
Time management and organizational skills in a deadline oriented environment</li>
<li>
Relationship management experience</li>
<li>
Four-year degree or equivalent experience</li>
</ul>
