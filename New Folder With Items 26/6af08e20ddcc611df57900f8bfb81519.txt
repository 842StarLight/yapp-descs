MISSION AND RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
We are looking for our future Demand Planner that will be responsible for all forecasting activities associated with customers and products. Creates and maintains forecast models, incorporating business intelligence and forecast information gathered from sales, marketing, finance, and other sources when &amp; if available/applicable.
<br><br>
He / She will be the integration point into the sales and commercial organization leading the dialogue each month for their respective customers and products. In addition, the position will work with Operations partners to ensure meeting service level goals for the Commercial Organization. The position will report to the Market Planning Leader for Coty Travel Retail Americas located in the Miami office.
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsabilities:</b><br>
Review &amp; maintain demand forecasts (operational forecasts) for multiple time horizons as part of a demand planning function.
<br>Review historical sales trends, research demand drivers, collect forecast data, maintain statistical forecast models, and evaluate forecast results.
<br>Reconcile significant variances and RCA to discuss during sales and marketing assumptions.
<br>Interact with a collaborative and consensus approach with Sales, Marketing and Finance to obtain and ensure that current and accurate information is used for alignment meetings &amp; reviews, focused on understanding demand forecast drivers.
<br>Use and maintain the Demand Planning software as the primary forecasting system tool.
<br>Closely coordinate and communicate customer action plans/IBA with supply planning.
<br>Work with Initiatives and Marketing groups to determine time-lines for the collection of promotional and new brand (or new sku) forecasts. Coordinate and collaborate in Pre-S&amp;OP meetings.
<br>Using SAP, BI, MFR, Micro strategy, and Manugistics systems, input data, generate reports that support S&amp;OP processes for TR Americas.
<br>Monitor and evaluate KPIâ€™s such as fill rate, forecast accuracy and conformance to plan in dollars and quantities. The process includes identifying gaps, recommending and implementing actions to improve performance in key areas.
<br>Prepare strategic and ad hoc analysis. This analysis can be brand specific or overall strategic in nature.
<br>Monitor sku performance and recommend sku rationalization initiatives
