<h2>Meetings and Congress Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary of Major Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Meetings and Congress Lead will lead strategic planning and execution of large commercial meetings such as scientific congresses and conferences across the organization. This role will work with leadership across all functions (including, but not limited to, Marketing, Medical Affairs, Corporate Affairs, Clinical &amp; Regulatory Affairs, Pipeline, Compliance, Sales, and Business Development) to create and execute an annual Congress strategy for Exact Sciences. The Strategic Meetings and Congress Manager will lead a cross-functional project team for each congress to coordinate all activities and logistics with the appropriate functions as well as any external agency partners.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Meetings and Congress Lead will also be responsible for holding cross-functional meetings such as, kick-off meetings, status meetings, after action reviews, and developing and maintaining documentation related to key learnings. The individual will be responsible for working with functional leads to review and manage budgets, in addition to determining return on investment and providing feedback during annual planning.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position requires a highly motivated individual who can work independently, think strategically with the ability to take insights and develop them into strategies. The successful candidate will demonstrate outstanding collaboration and project management skills including the ability to communicate effectively with cross-functional leadership, executive team, field-based teams, and external agency partners.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Build and develop the strategic and tactical annual companywide Congress strategy working closely with cross-functional leadership team.</li>
<li>
Manage planning for large strategic meetings and conferences.</li>
<li>
Manage planning for advisory boards across the enterprise.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain congress/conference calendar across all functions for the organization.</li>
<li>
Assist in development of annual advisory board plan with cross-functional leadership.</li>
<li>
Compile, monitor, and report on key metrics and other measures of success around congresses for the organization and communicate back to leadership team.</li>
<li>
Work with cross-functional leaders to assess and manage budgets around key congresses.</li>
<li>
Facilitate cross-functional activity planning and ensure flawless execution of Exact Sciences’ presence at the meetings.</li>
<li>
Manage any vendor relationships related to congress and advisory board execution, including any RFP process with new vendors.</li>
<li>
Work with compliance, legal and finance to ensure proper policies, contracts, &amp; procedures are followed.</li>
<li>
Motivate and lead teams of cross-functional resources in a matrixed organization with competing priorities, with or without direct authority or reporting relationships.</li>
<li>
Adaptable, open to change, and able to work in ambiguous situations with some direction and respond to new information and unexpected circumstances.</li>
<li>
Manage and prioritize strategies, initiatives, and workload proactively in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment.</li>
<li>
Exceptional project management skills including coordination, communication, and adherence to budget.</li>
<li>
Outstanding interpersonal, teamwork, and communication skill.</li>
<li>
Ensure effective on-site and off-site events.</li>
<li>
Regular and reliable attendance.</li>
<li>
Support and comply with the company’s Quality Management System policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Ability to work normal schedule of Monday through Friday during normal business hours, including occasional weekends.</li>
<li>
Ability to lift up to 25 pounds for approximately 5% of a typical working day.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel approximately 25% of working time, including some weekends.
</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
