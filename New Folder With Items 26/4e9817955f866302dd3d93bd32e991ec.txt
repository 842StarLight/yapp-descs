<h2>International Technical Support Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The International Technical Support Associate will be a service minded and reliable team member to create and manage the technical support help for our international channel partners. The International Technical Support Associate is responsible for satisfying our channel partner's needs for quick, thorough and reliable responses to a broad variety of service requests, ranging from product information, pricing, contracts updates, logistics status, requests for marketing support, and more.
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
<b>The primary job responsibilities will include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Providing exceptional remote technical support for our authorized dealers, distributors, and customers in Latin America and Brazil, using various means to troubleshoot technical concerns and research possible solutions.</li>
<li>Documenting and registering calls taken including encountered issues, resolutions, and follow-up required using the company's web-based support system.</li>
<li>Delivering a great customer experience by using internal resources and documentation for our products and services.</li>
<li>Assisting with pre-sales tech support for new product releases in coordination with the Sales Engineering team and technical account managers.</li>
</ul>
REQUIREMENTS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Fluency in Spanish.</li>
<li>1+ years of work experience in technical support, sales or technology related positions.</li>
<li>Background and strong desire to work for a technology company and quickly learn necessary technical skills to successfully perform daily duties</li>
<li>Demonstrated passion for providing the highest-quality customer service</li>
<li>Strong technical aptitude and computer skills (e.g., Web-based applications, Mobile applications, Excel, Word)</li>
<li>Ability to communicate with customers via email and phone in a clear and professional manner</li>
<li>A self-starter who is able to operate independently and is customer service oriented</li>
<li>Must possess the ability and commitment to meet deadlines even during high volume periods</li>
<li>Must be proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Ability to work in multiple technical mediums simultaneously while remaining attentive to customer and their concern</li>
<li>Self-motivated and disciplined individual that has experience working independently</li>
<li>Flexible working hours that can accommodate support needs for local business hours</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree is a plus</li>
</ul>
