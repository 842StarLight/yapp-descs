<h2>Marketing Communications Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<p>We strive for excellence while working in a balanced and fun atmosphere that rewards hard work, initiative and innovation. With a collection of award-winning, best-selling nursing and infant support products available, and more <i>always</i> in development, our company has earned a <i>place</i> as a player in the multi-million-dollar baby products industry. We are passionate about our products and love celebrating the joy babies bring. The same creative spirit that makes our products special, keeps the <i>work</i> environment here fun and energetic.</p>
<p>
The Marketing Communications Manager reports to the Director of Marketing and is responsible for defining and executing the Brand strategy. Your creativity, strong communication skills, effective project management and hands-on <i>work</i> style will further drive our brand growth and value of the Boppy brand. You will collaborate with our Marketing, Creative and Sales Teams. You must be a leader, team player, comfortable working in a dynamic, fast-paced environment and able to work overtime with little advance notice when necessary. You will lead special projects as assigned. Some travel is required.</p>

<ul>
<li>Marketing Campaign Planning &amp; Development</li>
<li>Creative Asset Development Process ensuring consistency</li>
<ul>
<li>Website</li>

<li>International Materials</li>
</ul>
