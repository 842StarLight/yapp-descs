<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Director of Cloud Computing Platforms and Information Security role will maximize company sales growth and profitability delivering Cloud Computing, End User Information Computing Technology, and deliver Information Security Governance, compliance and programs for the organization. The Director will lead the architecture and foundation of cloud computing platforms within the organization and develop strategic relationships with third party cloud vendors maximizing growth and scalability and information security principles by working closely with internal and external technical team members and with top management to help IT deliver the expected strategic benefits for the organization.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This individual will be skilled in working across departments to collaborate with Business Unit Executives, Business Managers, User Experience, Application Architects, Engineering Delivery Teams, Project Managers, QA, and Operations teams.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for department financials and annual expense budget.</li>
<li>
Accurately forecasts capital or operating expenditures to meet financial objectives on an annual basis for hardware, software and project costs for Information Technology and Information Security projects.</li>
<li>
Directs team responsible for the design and implementation of Cloud platforms, infrastructure hardware, end user equipment and software that enables business applications.</li>
<li>
Evaluates emerging computing architectures and technologies and recommends desirable computing platforms, and/or hardware and software upgrades for internal platforms relevant for the business.</li>
<li>
Directs and leads development of IT procedures, contracts and procurement strategies and setting up Cloud Services contracts or capitalized equipment procurement, operational maintenance and service level agreements.</li>
<li>
Directs and holds IT stakeholder responsibilities for Information Technology projects that include project timing, resource availability and overall staffing requirements.</li>
<li>
Directs the department with IT Security stakeholders to maintain and adhere to ISO Security 2700X Standards and PCI-DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) compliance in the company working with Internal Risk and Compliance and integrating IT Stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Directs Information Technology Department and Business Units defining Information Computing Technology Computer and Telecommunication Use Policies, Incident Response, and Security Awareness training.</li>
<li>
Directs Information Technology Department and Business stakeholders leading Information Technology business continuance team and participates in the creation and audit compliance of the companyâ€™s Business Continuance Plan.
</li>
<li>Support all efforts and initiatives across all business channels in an effort to maximize company sales growth and profitability.
</li>
<li>Key individual and focal point with business departments identifying key issues, IT investments, delivering IT services and improving service levels.</li>
</ul>
