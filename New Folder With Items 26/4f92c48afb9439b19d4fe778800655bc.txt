<p>The Client Services Technician role provides inbound customer service support for basic troubleshooting and resolution of service related problems for high speed internet and wireless gateways working from your own home office. This is an exciting work from home opportunity requiring you to work and engage with others in a virtual environment, while providing our customers with a high quality customer service experience. As a Client Services Technician you will be fully trained to perform the following duties.</p>
<p><b>Work From Home | US - Multiple, United States</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Actively listen to customer needs and offer appropriate education of products and services to prevent customer churn.</li>
<li>Respond real time to customer Inbound questions and problems.</li>
<li>Make outbound calls to touch base with new customers, thank them for their business and listen to their new customer experience</li>
<li>Utilize resources to upsell and explain competitive edge of enhanced products.</li>
<li>Effective use of persuasive techniques to overcome objections.</li>
<li>Educate customers in the use of installed products, service offerings, billing, charges, and product features.</li>
<li>Become a customer advocate; resolve customer concerns and situations calmly and courteously.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and resolve service and technical problems using remote control tools in an ever changing technical space</li>
<li>Meets retention, sales and customer service performance metrics.</li>
<li>Explain and instruct the customer on the value and operation of the product and service.</li>
<li>Focus on customer retention efforts</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to instantly connect with a customer</li>
<li>Demonstrate active learning skills, ownership and empathy in providing a customer with the service they need.</li>
<li>Ability to interpret a flow chart to deliver a work process.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to listen, diagnose troubleshoot and solve technical and service related issues.</li>
<li>Communicate technical directions over the phone to inexperienced computer users.</li>
<li>Ability to learn new technical concepts quickly.</li>
<li>Communicate and present complex information effectively on the telephone demonstrating a professional tone, voice inflection and intonation.</li>
<li>Strong organizational and multitasking skills.</li>
<li>Ability to follow written instructions and integrate changes in instruction effectively.</li>
<li>Strong written communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a very structured environment with scheduled rest and meal periods</li>
</ul>
