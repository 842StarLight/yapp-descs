<h2>Special Finance Manager, Used Car-Cadillac of Novi</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Cadillac of Novi seeking a finance manager who specializes in sub-prime/special financing!
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p>Responsible for managing the transaction process and generating finance profit through reserve income and the sale of F&amp;I products to retail, lease and cash customers. Also responsible for protecting the financial welfare of the dealership by adhering to company compliance guidelines, managing lender/vendor relationships and delivering on our promise to listen and provide a trusted, easy and enjoyable experience.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review, understand and comply with company and/or dealership standards related to reserve income, F&amp;I product penetrations/income, and Per-Vehicle-Retail (PVR) averages for retail (new and used) and lease customers.
</li>
<li>Review, understand and comply with company compliance guidelines in the following areas:
<ul>
<li>Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA)
</li>
<li>Privacy Notices
</li>
<li>Identity Theft Protection Program (ITPP) – Red Flag Compliance
</li>
<li>Office of Foreign Asset Control (OFAC)
</li>
<li>IRS 8300/FinCEN
</li>
<li>Adverse Action Notices
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Review, understand and comply with company Contracts-In-Transit (CIT) guidelines.
</li>
<li>Acquire and maintain all insurance licenses in accordance with state requirements.
</li>
<li>Review the Ken Garff Preferred Lender Program and have an understanding of programs and requirements for each lender participating in that program.
</li>
<li>Assist in the structuring of deals (when necessary) and submitting customer and vehicle information to lending sources for approval.
</li>
<li>Work directly with lending sources to increase the probability of deal approvals and the maximization of per deal profit.
</li>
<li>Secure the sale of the vehicle and protect the front end gross.
</li>
<li>Treat every customer in a friendly, professional manner, regardless of the customers financing or purchasing decision, or credit ability.
</li>
<li>Educate customers on manufacturer’s warranty coverage on new and used vehicles (when applicable)
</li>
<li>Outline the features and benefits of all F&amp;I products available to the customer and provide the opportunity to purchase those products, either as part of the vehicle transaction or on a stand-alone basis.
</li>
<li>Properly disclose all documents associated with the vehicle purchase, including finance/lease interest rates and loan terms.
</li>
<li>Collect and safeguard all money and fees required to satisfy loan requirements.
</li>
<li>Collect and verify all loan stipulation documents from customer as outlined in the lender approval.
</li>
<li>Ensure completeness and accuracy of all vehicle transaction documents (including customer and dealership signatures in the correct areas).
</li>
<li>Finalize all necessary documents and submit to the accounting office within the 3 day company standard
</li>
<li>Record 100% of vehicle transactions (F&amp;I cameras).
</li>
<li>Complete at least one F&amp;I training module per week.
</li>
<li>Participate in dealership and company required compliance training per company guidelines.
</li>
<li>Attend and participate in sales and/or manager meetings when instructed.
</li>
<li>Educate Sales Advisors on the proper endorsement of F&amp;I products and dealership financing.
</li>
<li>Attend and participate in daily “Save-A-Deal” meetings with other managers.
</li>
<li>Assist in any customer claims on any of the F&amp;I products, and follow up to make sure that claim is fully resolved
</li>
<li>Assist in the F&amp;I product cancellation process when applicable.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
