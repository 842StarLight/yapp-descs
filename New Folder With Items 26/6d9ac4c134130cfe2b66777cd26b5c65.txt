About The Job:</div>
<div>
Madison Logic is eagerly seeking a Enterprise Sales Director with a passion for knowledge sharing &amp; coaching. In this role you will work with some of the fastest growing brands in the global marketplace. Our team is the core of everything we do, and the selection process starts with you!</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Do:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Act as a player/coach, grow and manage a team of 3-4 Enterprise Sales Directors</li>
<li>
Assist with closing of strategic opportunities</li>
<li>
Consultative sales of Madison Logic’s portfolio of B2B products to Marketers</li>
<li>
Achieve annual, monthly and quarterly sales targets [as defined on an annual basis]</li>
<li>
Identify methods to promote and accelerate achievement of sales goals in the Region</li>
<li>
Penetrate assigned accounts, including new business, renewals and upsell</li>
<li>
Directly manage ongoing relationships with enterprise accounts in region, as appropriate</li>
<li>
Negotiate pricing and contractual agreements</li>
<li>
Build and manage a strong pipeline of potential clients sufficient to exceed annual sales goals</li>
<li>
Be a digital marketing consultant to Madison Logic’s enterprise and mid-market clients, including staying abreast of digital marketing best practices and applying this knowledge to assist their portfolio of clients in meeting their revenue goals</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with our Customer Success team to ensure successful new client onboarding along with a white glove level of support</li>
<li>
Work closely with internal teams to ensure clients' ongoing success and satisfaction</li>
<li>
Perform other duties and responsibilities as assigned</li>
</ul></div>
