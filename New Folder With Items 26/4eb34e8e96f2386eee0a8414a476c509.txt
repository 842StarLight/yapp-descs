<h2>Financial Pricing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ARGO is a leading provider of software and analytics solutions for both the financial services and healthcare industries. ARGO transforms business processes for financial service providers and healthcare organizations using proven business models and software innovation informed by real customer challenges, breakthrough technology, and rich analytics.</p>
<br>
The Financial Pricing Analyst reports to ARGO's CFO, and provides financial reporting and pricing analysis to be used for strategic and tactical decision making. The Financial Analyst interprets and implements financial modeling concepts or techniques related to strategic planning and forecasting. The Financial Analyst will work with the product management, product development, project implementation teams, sales teams and senior management.<br>
<br>
Position must be worked from our Richardson, TX office.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS</b><b>:</b><br>
<br>
Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Accounting, Finance, Economics or a Master’s degree in Statistics (with relevant minor) is required for the position. Other related majors will be reviewed.<br>
<br>
<b>WORK PERFORMED</b><b>:</b><br>
<br>
Job duties and responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned as needed.<br>
<div>
<ul><li>Conducts extensive research and industry analysis (Financial Services and Healthcare) to assist with product pricing, planning and forecasting</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develops, modifies, utilizes and archives analytical frameworks for product and cost benefit analysis (primarily using Excel, but other tools may be used)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides market segment analysis especially for healthcare provider</li></ul>
<ul><li>Delivers individual and group presentations of pricing models</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develops models, processes, and methods to analyze market volume and margin trends for develop short and long term forecasts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitors, gathers, analyzes, presents and archives earnings and trends for the Financial Services and Healthcare industries</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyzes, compares, and presents initial cost benefit proforma to actual results once initial implementation projects are complete</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyzes financial and operational data, draws conclusions and presents recommendations for decisions and actions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepares moderate to complex analytical reports including budgets, revenue and expense analysis, business trends analysis, and special ad-hoc projects</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides support for key performance indicators to senior management and executive team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Works with Accounting and integrates revenue projections into forecast modeling</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures the confidentiality and security of customer information</li></ul>
<ul><li>Builds strong relationships and work with individuals at all levels of the organization</li></ul>
</div>
<br>
