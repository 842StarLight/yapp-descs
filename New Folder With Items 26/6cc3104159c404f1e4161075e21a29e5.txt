<h2>Full-Time Center Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Center Associate delivers world-class customer service to all retail customers by receiving and processing packages for courier shipment and operating copiers, fax machines, binding equipment, laminating machines, and point-of-sale devices. The Associate expertly advises our valued customers by providing accurate information on our array of products, services, and best-value options and exhibits confidence by knowing best practices related to the industry.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate has previous retail sales experience, strong computer and internet skills, a high school diploma or GED, a friendly and genuinely helpful demeanor, and a professional appearance. He or she must be a fast learner who makes an effort to learn all aspects of the business in the shortest possible time.<br>
</p>
<b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b><ul>
<li>
Delivers outstanding customer service to walk-in customers and telephone inquiries</li>
<li>
Communicates effectively and continuously practices good listening with customers, co-workers, vendors, and leadership</li>
<li>
Takes ownership of customer's packing, shipping, and/or print needs and offers viable solutions</li>
<li>
Takes action to learn all product and service offerings, alternative solutions, and industry trends</li>
<li>
Operates all equipment, software, and devices in an expert fashion and is willing to teach others
</li>
<li>Maintains a clean and safe working environment</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b><ul>
<li>
High school diploma or GED required</li>
<li>
Good communication and people skills</li>
<li>
Strong customer service skills and abilities
</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, including Microsoft Office (Adobe InDesign/Photoshop a plus)</li>
<li>
Prompt, reliable, and responsible</li>
<li>
Neat, clean, and professional appearance</li>
<li>
Able to lift 40+ pounds</li>
<li>
Available on Saturdays</li>
<li>
Detail oriented, organized, and able to multi-task<br>
</li>
</ul>
