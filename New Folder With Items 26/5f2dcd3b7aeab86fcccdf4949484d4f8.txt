<h2>Manager - Investment Data Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About Clearwater Analytics®
<br><br>
Clearwater Analytics® is a global SaaS solution for automated investment data aggregation, reconciliation, accounting, and reporting. Clearwater helps thousands of organizations make the most of investment portfolio data with cloud-native software and client-centric servicing. Every day, investment professionals worldwide trust Clearwater to deliver timely, validated investment data and in-depth reporting.
<br><br>
Clearwater aggregates, reconciles, and reports on more than $3 trillion in assets across thousands of accounts daily for our Fortune 500 clients.
<br><br>
DESCRIPTION
<br><br>
As a Manager of Fixed Income, Derivatives &amp; Equities, you will be expected to serve as a subject matter expert for a variety of investment vehicles including publicly traded instruments as well as OTC derivatives and alternative investments. You will collaborate with teams across the company to ensure our data offering and asset modeling is truly world class and will be tasked with finding and implementing solutions when deficiencies are identified with an unwavering commitment to client success. In addition to a data centric focus, you will be called upon to support business development and client servicing initiatives which includes interactions with top professionals across some of the largest organizations in the world. To effectively fill this role, we challenge our team members to be thought leaders in the industry, positioning themselves to advise clients and prospects on best practices and clearly articulate Clearwater's value proposition regarding these investment vehicles.
