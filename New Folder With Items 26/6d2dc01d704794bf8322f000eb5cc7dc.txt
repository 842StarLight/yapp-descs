<h2>Dispatcher I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>The Dispatcher is responsible for efficiently routing trucks from supply points to customers. This</p>
<p>position requires having a good understanding of fuel supply and supply points, as well as general trucking</p>
<p>and petroleum transportation. This position is extremely customer service driven and is fast paced.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB EXPECTATIONS/DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor customer inventory levels to always ensure customer has the product while understanding the dynamics of consignment inventory.</li>
<li>Ensure that all orders are dispatched out to drivers, both internally and through common carriers.</li>
<li>Communicate with customer on receiving orders and update timelines for delivery.</li>
<li>Communicate delivery and customer instructions to driver.</li>
<li>Process (dispatch) sales order through Firestream software system.</li>
<li>Understand Department of Transportation regulations and hours of service.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul><li>High School Diploma is required, bachelor’s degree in Logistics is preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>3-5 years’ experience in a terminal dispatch, logistics and/or trucking role desired.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to read/write the English language.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to use a computer with proficiency in Word, Excel and e-mail, operate a copier, use the phone, pass/receive paperwork through the driver window, data entry, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to communicate clearly and professionally with third party carriers and internal personnel.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to focus on and accurately complete tasks in an energetic, fast paced, and continuously changing work environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attention to detail and the ability to follow established procedures is a must.</li></ul>
