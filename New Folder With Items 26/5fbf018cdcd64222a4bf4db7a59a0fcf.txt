<h2>Radio Sales Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>LBI Media, Inc.</p>
<p>Radio Sales Assistant</p>
<p>We are LBI Media, Inc., the largest privately-held, minority-owned Spanish-language broadcaster in the United States, which owns TV and radio stations operating in top Hispanic markets throughout the country; parent company of the Estrella TV Network, one of the fastest growing television networks in the U.S. We are currently looking for talented and passionate people who want to grow with us.</p>
<p>Position Summary:</p>
<p>The Sales Assistant position at LBI Media is an entry level role that can get you on the path to a Sales Associate career in TV or Radio Sales at any of our locations across the country. Sales Assistants provide sales support to Sales Managers and Account Executives for the Estrella TV Network, and/or our local TV or Radio Stations</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<p>· Work closely and act as liaison with advertising agency counterparts, clients, and internal departments, including Traffic, Marketing, Accounting and Research.</p>
<p>· Respond to all client requests in a timely manner.</p>
<p>· Execute all client orders insuring that they are in compliance with client and station requirements.</p>
<p>· Handle program and commercial changes and issue change notices to clients.</p>
<p>· Communicate effectively with multiple departments to coordinate sales packages for sales meetings.</p>
<p>· Assist Sales Managers and Account Executives in creating PowerPoint presentations and one sheets.</p>
<p>· Assist Account Executive on gathering promotional assets, approvals within tight deadlines.</p>
<p>· Work closely with Traffic to schedule promotional assets.</p>
<p>· Reconcile billing discrepancies with agencies and billing department.</p>
<p>· Perform other tasks as directed by Manager.</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Strong computer knowledge (Microsoft Office Word, Excel, PowerPoint).</p>
<p>· Organizational skills and the ability to prioritize.</p>
<p>· Excellent verbal and written communications skills with a strong customer service orientation,.</p>
<p>· Problem solving skills with the ability to troubleshoot and resolve issues.</p>
<p>· Detail oriented and ability to meet tight deadlines.</p>
<p>· Bilingual: ability to read, write and speak English/Spanish.</p>
<p>· Bachelor degree preferred.</p>
<p>Eligibility Requirements:</p>
<p>· Interested candidates must submit their resume to careers@ lbimedia.com</p>
<p>· Must be willing to work in Burbank, CA</p>
<p>· Must be willing to undergo a pre-employment background investigation</p>
<p>· Must have legal authorization to work in the United States</p>
<p>· Must be willing to work overtime on short notice</p>
<p>· Must be 18 years or older</p>
