<h2>Lead Project Management Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Support Verizon Credo through adherence to company policies, processes and practices.</li>
<li>
Provides Lead PM support or tower lead support for Medium to Large complex projects.</li>
<li>
Acts as primary customer interface by facilitating team meetings to include tracking and managing action items to meet client deliverables as well as distributing regular status reports to stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Build strong communication channels with client and internal departments such as Sales, Solutions &amp; Service with the ability to provide custom reporting, monthly dashboards, schedule integration and metrics/SPI management.</li>
<li>
Develops project plans that include resource, time and budget estimates for all project phases and milestones for internal and external parties.</li>
<li>
Provides support to solve a wide range of complex issues/problems.</li>
<li>
Provides general governance for intermediate to Large projects or programs.</li>
<li>
Responsible for satisfying customer expectations on ROI, product quality, functionality and timely delivery.</li>
<li>
Coordinates transition of programs from development to implementation.</li>
<li>
Implements recommendations that aid in the successful completion of projects within a product or functional area.</li>
<li>
Receives some supervision and has some latitude on achieving objectives related to specific program task, project or initiative.</li>
</ul>
