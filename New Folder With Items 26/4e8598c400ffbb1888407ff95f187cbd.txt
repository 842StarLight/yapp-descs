<h2>IoT Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Cold Jet is the world leader in developing innovative environmentally responsible cleaning solutions that help companies reduce maintenance costs, enhance product quality, prolong equipment life, and improve productivity and worker safety. Cold Jet’s extensive line of dry ice cleaning systems is used in a variety of industries, making productive use of recycled carbon dioxide while eliminating the need for chemicals and water in the cleaning process. Cold Jet is a private company with global headquarters in Loveland, Ohio USA and international operations in Europe, Asia, Canada, and Mexico. Cold Jet is an entrepreneurial and exciting place to work, the unquestioned leader in its industry. For more information on our company culture, please visit careers.coldjet.com.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Cold Jet is seeking an IoT Program Manager to drive the global development of our Cold Jet ConnectTM and Smart IoT connected cleaning and dry ice production systems. The ideal candidate will have an entrepreneurial mindset, excellent technical skills, and be comfortable with the management of system/software development as well as customer and company relationships. The candidate should be passionate about solving complex data analytic problems, and disciplined in best practice design disciplines.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage company’s IoT platform and strategy to execute development, production and customer-facing IoT programs.</li>
<li>
Lead cross-functional team (IT, Engineering, Customer Service, Sales) to execute corporate IoT strategy and projects. Manage projects from concept creation through implementation.</li>
<li>
Collect, maintain, and manage requirements for IoT features, quality and compliance, user data privacy, and cybersecurity to ensure the delivery of robust and compliant solutions.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with technical subject matter experts (SMEs) to identify and ensure effective data requirements, data capture and analytics of IoT products.</li>
<li>
Facilitate the definition of project scope, goals, milestones, deliverables, detailed tasks and resource requirements. Manage stakeholder expectations.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain project schedules and project budgets. Ensure decisions and outcomes are documented appropriately. Communicate project status including risks to all project stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Evangelize the IoT platform strategy and roadmap in collaboration with others on the team ensuring alignment of company objectives, product vision and user feedback.</li>
<li>
Work with sales, marketing, and customer service to define analytics requirements and models for successful leveraging of IoT data.</li>
<li>
Be equally at ease discussing tech details with dev team and communicating strategy to leadership team.</li>
<li>
Act as a liaison between customer and internal groups. Build relationships and collaborate with key stakeholders to ensure delivery of commitments.</li>
<li>
Build a strong understanding of customers and users to represent their goals/challenges and advocate for their experiences.</li>
<li>
Assess competitive smart device products and trends.</li>
<li>
Identify and rectify roadblocks for successful program completion.</li>
<li>
Develop and own tech partner relationships as necessary.</li>
<li>
Apply Project Management principles, practices and techniques for development and implementation of Cold Jet’s IoT technology initiatives in our two major product lines.</li>
</ul>

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to manage complex technical systems and customer programs through development, launch and start of production.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage low-level details of a project while understanding the bigger picture of the IT strategy.</li>
<li>
Proven experience of defining vision and delivering on Platform services (APIs, data, integrations, etc.).</li>
<li>
Self-directed and driven, with the ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills, and comfortable delivering presentations to executives.</li>
<li>
Able to be flexible as needs change, moving seamlessly between strategic and the tactical, with a focus on the end-user needs.</li>
<li>
Advanced analytical and problem-solving skills.</li>
</ul>
