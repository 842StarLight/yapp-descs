<h2>Driver</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Enterprise Rent-A-Car is the largest and fastest growing privately owned automotive rental and leasing company in the U.S. With over 10,000 branch offices worldwide, Enterprise ranks #1 in the Insurance Replacement Market with yearly revenues of over $24 billion.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Driver delivers vehicles and in some instances, customers, employees or vendors safely and timely to the appropriate destination(s) while giving helpful, courteous and professional customer service.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The person for this position will work out of our Tulsa Car Sales dealership at 4101 South Sheridan, Tulsa, OK.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This is an on-call position and will be for 20 to 25 hours a week, Monday thru Saturday. Hours are between 10:00 AM and 8:00 PM (Mon-Fri) and 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM (Saturday).</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<div>The Part Time Driver is responsible for the transporting of fleet cars and vans to and from rental locations, as well as navigating written directions.</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Drive and deliver vehicles locally or out of area as needed, following all rules of the road</div></li>
<li><div>
Deliver customers and vehicles to appropriate destination in a safe and courteous manner</div></li>
<li><div>
Ride with or follow another driver to drop off vehicle(s) or pick up multiple vehicles</div></li>
<li><div>
Organize travel route and ensure vehicle paperwork is accurate and timely</div></li>
<li><div>
May need to communicate via 2-way radio or cellular phone</div></li>
<li><div>
May be asked to clean vehicles</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform miscellaneous job-related duties as assigned</div></li>
</ul>

Equal Opportunity Employer/Disability/Veterans</i></div>

</div>
