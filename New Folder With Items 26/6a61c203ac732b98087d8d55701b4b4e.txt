<h2>Leasing Professional</h2>

<p><b>Leasing Position</b></p>
<p>Premier Development has an opportunity for a Leasing Professional available at a New Construction, Luxury Apartment Complex located in desirable Hillsborough, NJ. The Leasing staff is responsible for leasing apartments, serves as an initial contact between the public and the company, and strives to project and demonstrate the value and quality of our product and service.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<p>·Greets prospective residents and presents property through personal tours. Gathers information regarding prospective resident’s needs and details on a guest card; qualifies the prospective resident through conversation to ensure that standard criteria are met and we are finding the perfect home for our customers; conducts follow-up daily.</p>
<p>·Delivers superior customer service, delivers custom presentations on leasing calls, and assists current residents with service requests.</p>
<p>·Completes all leasing paperwork associated with leasing activity, including rental applications, rental and employment verification. Processes all rental paperwork in accordance with Fair Housing Guidelines.</p>
<p>·Responsible for handling resident’s issues, needs and complaints in a professional, fair and consistent manner. Displays diplomacy during all interactions.</p>
<p>·Gathers market information weekly and prepares reports identifying competitor’s rentals rates, concessions, promotions and property features.</p>
<p>·Pushes value of product and service. Maintains consistent awareness of sub-market to drive income growth</p>
<p>·Other duties as described in more detail in the job description.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications and Requirements</b></p>
<p>·Must work Weekends, both Saturdays and Sundays (required).</p>
<p>New Construction and Lease Up / Sales experience a plus</p>
<p>·Ability to analyze and prioritizes tasks assigned</p>
<p>·Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite</p>
<p>·Yardi software experience a plus</p>
<p>·Strong interpersonal skills and problem-solving ability</p>
<p>·Excellent oral and written communications skills, including presentation skills</p>
<p>·Solid business logic</p>
<p>·Follows instructions, performs duties with minimal supervision and is a team player</p>
<p>·Works effectively with owner, managers and colleagues. Positive and professional attitude at all times</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Sales: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>

<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Required)</li></ul>
