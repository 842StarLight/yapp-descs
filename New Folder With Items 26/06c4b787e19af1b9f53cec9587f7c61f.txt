<p><b>BOSTIK</b> is hiring! We are currently looking for a Strategic Accounts Manager for the Midwest region.</p>
<p>As a Strategic Accounts Manager, you are responsible for developing and executing account and market segment strategies to meet growth and profit expectations.
</p>
<p>We seek people who want to be a part of a growing, world-class organization. We also offer comprehensive benefits effective DAY 1… “<b>Stick</b>” with us!</p>
<p><b>
Job Dimensions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute account and market segment strategies to meet growth and profit expectations.</li>
<li>
Assess and develop future market/account opportunities. National MRO support, not boundary specific.</li>
<li>
Manage current customer relationships in order to maintain and grow business.</li>
<li>
Work constructively and actively participate in cross-functional teams. (MST. Market Segment Teams)</li>
<li>
Ensure that there is a robust sales pipeline that generates new business above 6% annual with a high value business stream emphasis.</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintaining and / or improving margins and negotiating pricing.</li>
<li>
Coordinate implementation of customer contracts and agreements where necessary.</li>
<li>
Practice safe work habits in accordance with OSHA, EPA guidelines and Bostik specific EHS expectations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Activities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand the customer’s needs and translation of these needs to management and appropriate technical resources.</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot account oppty’s and facilitate technical solutions between technical resource and the customer.</li>
<li>
Responsible for developing and implementing projects, establishing priorities and coordinating resources to achieve goals.</li>
<li>
Negotiate terms and execute Supply Agreements which position Bostik for long term strategic partnerships with customers, including: pricing, payment terms, incentive programs, growth commitments, and delivery terms.</li>
<li>
Understand and work with Salesforce software to upload appropriate customer and market information, including new contacts, on a timely basis.</li>
<li>
Continually monitor key account needs and communications for new projects.</li>
<li>
Ability to grasp Bostik product knowledge and relate it effectively for customer’s benefit. (Product Training and Distributor support is Key. In groups and on own)</li>
<li>
Compile Bostik presentations tailored to specific accounts and be able to present them with confidence.</li>
<li>
Participate in cross functional activities within other departments at Bostik including production, quality, customer service, R&amp;D and tech service.</li>
<li>
Work with distributors who may need assistance in product knowledge or product sampling /pricing.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Context and Environment</b></p>
<p>An evolving and competitive marketplace moving away from commodity business to value and technology partnerships with longer term Potentials with key accounts. Driving business primarily through indirect channels within this competitive environment.</p>
<p><b>
Accountabilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Accountable to the BU Sales Manager, Bostik Inc, &amp; the shareholders of Arkema.</li>
<li>
This role is accountable for managing all aspects of the business relationship for all customers in their territory.</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with business , marketing and technical teams to meet objectives.</li>
<li>
Accountable to Bostik for growth</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications / Experience Required</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum Bachelor degree, Technical Degree a plus. MBA preferred.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 5 years of Field Based Sales success. Distribution experience a PLUS.</li>
<li>
Exceptional interpersonal, communication, listening, organizational and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Strong computer skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a self-directed, hands on environment, independently, as well as in a team.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel 50-60% of the time (including regular overnight travel as needed).</li>
</ul>
