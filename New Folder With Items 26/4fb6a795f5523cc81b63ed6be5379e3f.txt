<h2>Senior Coordinator, Logistics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Senior Coordinator, Logistics - (190001BS)</div>
<div>
Description<div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Overview:
</b></div>
<div>This position performs a variety of duties in support of our organization’s RMA and freight claims processing. Within the scope of this position’s responsibilities, the incumbent must continually focus on the improvement of his/her processes in order to ensure the customer’s needs are met for high quality, cost effective logistics services while, at the same time, meeting corporate values and objectives.</div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:
</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>This position is responsible for, but not limited to, the following:</li>
<li>
Work with customer and its vendors to issue RMA’s for material that is damaged in transit.</li>
<li>
Process freight claims and all related activities in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Coordinate with customer’s vendors/freight carriers to receive freight claim payment in a timely fashion.</li>
<li>
Process receipt of freight claim payments, posting payments to claims and ensuring claim closure. Assemble and file all documents related to freight claims.</li>
<li>
Develop metrics associated with role. Track, categorize, and develop metrics for freights claims and related damage material claims. Reconcile departmental discrepancies to insure reporting accuracy.</li>
<li>
Process improvement: Work with Sales, Customer Service, the DC, and other internal departments to make sure activities related to freight and damaged material claims are processed in a timely and accurate manner. Offer recommendations to improve freight claim recovery ratio and related carrier performance.</li>
<li>
Responsibility to maintain reporting and operational metrics.</li>
<li>
Support local management in an administrative capacity.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key Results:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Number and duration of outstanding freights claim</li>
<li>
Count of RMA’s issued and length of time to issue/complete</li>
<li>
Number of unpaid claims from carriers – freight claim recovery ratio (claims that can or will not be collected</li>
<li>
Resolving open freight claims and customer related issue if there is a corresponding RMA.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>
Improvements to the freight claims process, freight carriers used and related internal material handling processes.</li></ul>
