<h2>Technical Specialist, Customer Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Overview:</b><br>
As a Technical Specialist, Customer Support at Weedmaps, you will be responsible for ensuring that our end users are able to successfully utilize all aspects of our platform with a focus on supporting our online ordering platform and moderating user reviews. Your passion for the marijuana industry coupled with your technical mastery of our products will allow you to resolve issues thoughtfully through email, phone, and chat to guarantee that experience for each end user is positive, helpful, and secure.
<br><br>
In addition to working with our site users you'll also have the opportunity to work directly with our internal technology teams to troubleshoot and report bugs that end users find on the website, mobile browser, or apps.
<br><br>
<b>The impact you'll make:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>This position requires superior written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Availability to work scheduled weekends and/or evenings on a rotational support basis</li>
<li>Field and efficiently diagnose customer reported technical, pre- and post-sales issues via phone, email, and chat</li>
<li>Provide clear, professional, &amp; friendly communications to both end users and internal Weedmaps contacts throughout the troubleshooting process until resolution</li>
<li>Efficiently escalate issues internally in the event the issue cannot be resolved via normal troubleshooting steps</li>
<li>Assess content against Weedmaps policies</li>
<li>Identify and take action against all reviews/forum posts that require moderation</li>
<li>Serve as a customer advocate internally to ensure that product expectations are not only met, but exceeded, in a timely manner</li>
<li>Provide detailed, shared notes on each customer interaction to ensure that all members of your team are up to date on issue progress</li>
</ul>
<b>What you've accomplished:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>4+ years experience in technical customer service/support role</li>
<li>Experience with Tier-1 software and/or hardware support</li>
<li>High level of interpersonal communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Efficient organizational skills and the ability to multi-task and prioritize in a high-volume, fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Capable of quickly adapting to new responsibilities and meeting new expectations in a rapidly growing company and industry</li>
<li>Ability to work independently with systematic troubleshooting techniques, judgment and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Extremely positive attitude and eagerness to work in a fast-paced, high-growth environment</li>
</ul>
<b>Bonus Points:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience working in a start-up technical support environment</li>
<li>A passion for the cannabis industry and the desire to develop the culture</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce, Jira, or other ticketing systems</li>
<li>Experience with Gmail &amp; Google Suite</li>
<li>Experience writing internal and customer-facing documentation</li>
</ul>

<b>About Weedmaps:</b><br>
Founded in 2008, Weedmaps is a leading technology and software infrastructure provider to the cannabis industry. Our suite of cloud-based software and data solutions includes point of sale, logistics and ordering solutions that enable customers to scale their businesses while complying with the complex and disparate regulations applicable to the cannabis industry. In addition, our platform provides consumers with information regarding cannabis products across web and mobile platforms, including listing local retailers and brands, facilitating product discovery and allowing consumers to educate themselves on cannabis and its history, uses and legal status. Headquartered in Irvine, California, Weedmaps employs more than 400 professionals around the world, with offices including Barcelona, Berlin, Boston, Denver, New York, Phoenix and Toronto.
