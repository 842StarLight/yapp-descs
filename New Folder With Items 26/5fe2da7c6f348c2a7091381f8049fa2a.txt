<h2>Seasonal Workforce Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are looking for a Workforce Analyst to join our Phoenix sales center. We are looking for determined, self-motivated and passionate professionals who wants to make a difference in people's lives and achieve their own personal and professional goals. We represent all the major insurance carriers and present clients with the options to meet their needs.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Efficiently manage call volume and labor resources for our inbound, outbound and work flow, while maintaining and exceed goals and expectations</li>
<li>Coordinate with operations and initiating contingency plans for unusual volume or agent availability situations</li>
<li>Provides essential input to management team so that staffing plans are based on analysis of historical performance and real-time data</li>
<li>Monitors adherence real time to ensure performance is within pre-set parameters, works with supervisors to resolve schedule adherence issues, escalating to Operations Managers if necessary</li>
<li>Manage the PTO and overtime process</li>
<li>Design and prepare reports to meet internal and client requirements. Interface with Operations Management to resolve conflicts between agent requests and/or needs of the business, making significant schedule changes when necessary</li>
<li>Management of various workforce management systems, scheduling software and CMS.</li>
<li>Familiarity with forecast and scheduling concepts an asset</li>
<li>Effectively leverages resources to create exceptional outcomes, embraces change, and constructively resolves barriers and constraints</li>
<li>Connects meaningfully with customers to build emotional engagement and customer advocacy</li>
<li>Simplifies complexity and integrates internal efforts to deliver an optimal customer experience</li>
<li>Engages others by gathering multiple views and being open to diverse perspectives, focusing on a shared purpose for overall success</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years prior Workforce Management experience</li>
<li>Prior experience using a Workforce Management tool</li>
<li>Intermediate to advanced proficiency with Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Experience in data analysis</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills both written and verbal</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills</li>
<li>Ability to interact with all levels of the business is a must</li>
<li>A positive, proactive attitude, energetic, highly motivated and a self-starter</li>
<li>Strong ability to multi-task</li>
<li>CMS experience required</li>
</ul>
