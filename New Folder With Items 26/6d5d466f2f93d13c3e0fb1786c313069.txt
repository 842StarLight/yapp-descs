JOB SUMMARY:</b></p>
<div>
<br>
<br>

<p>The role of the Cardiology Specialist is to focus on sales and deliver results for Welch Allyn Cardiology products in the primary care market in the Northeast region. The primary care or ambulatory market consists of physician offices, urgent care offices, VA and DOD facilities, community health centers and additionally defined healthcare organizations. The goal is to drive sales with an emphasis on larger, strategic cardiology deals and/or EHR connected opportunities within an assigned territory.</p>
