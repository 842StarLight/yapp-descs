<p><b>
About the role:</b><br>
MRR (Market Response Reps) are part of the larger SDR Organization tasked with running high-level qualification and discovery calls designed to drive the creation of ‘Top of Funnel’ sales opportunities.It is a highly competitive position at CrowdStrike given the explosive career opportunities within tech sales and at our company.</p>
<ul>
<li>
MRRs are tasked with managing ‘inbound’ lead sources with the goal of discovering and driving new business opportunities for mid-market and enterprise accounts and reps.</li>
<li>
MRRs Conduct high level qualification &amp; discovery calls with the security leaders such as C-suite, VPs, and other influential prospects.</li>
<li>
They will qualify inbound leads, work channel deals, as well as have the opportunity to close small deals.</li>
<li>
MRRs will become a cyber security expert and thought leaders within the Endpoint market.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with marketing and channel partners to execute on a successful pre-IPO go-to-market strategy.</li>
</ul>
