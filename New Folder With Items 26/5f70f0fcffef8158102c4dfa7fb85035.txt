<h2>Coordinator, Order Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Overview of Job Function:</b></p>
<p>Under general direction, this position will assist with the daily activities performed by the Sales Operations department. The Coordinator, Order Management will process customer orders and if required, facilitate interactions amongst the customer and Sales Operations. The Coordinator, Order Management will also analyze and recommend improvements to processes and procedures that support the Sales Operations department.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Principal Duties and Essential Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Purchase order review and clean-up.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Coordinate shipping and order detail with Operations and other departments.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Data entry into ERP system (Oracle) and Salesforce.com (SFDC)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Update and manage appropriate databases with required data as needed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Communicate order information as needed to internal and external customers as needed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist customers with any credit requests, order problem or shipping issue.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide support and maintain effective relationships with sales team and established customers.</p>
</li>
<li>Follow-up and communicate information to customers and Regional Sales Managers as needed.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Minimum of 3 years experience in a data entry environment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic MS Office/PC software skills required.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be detail-oriented and extremely organized.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to manage multiple tasks with shifting priorities and timeframes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent oral and written communication skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to effectively interface internally and externally with all levels of an organization.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrated ability to be flexible and adaptable while responding to multiple demands and timelines.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Successful completion of a background screening process including, but not limited to, substance screening, employment verifications, criminal search, OFAC, and SS verification.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Degree preferred</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to speak Spanish preferred</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Oracle and SFDC experience highly desired</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Prior experience in a sales order entry environment preferred</p>
