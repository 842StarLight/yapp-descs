THE ROLE
<br>The Office Manager will oversee the patient experience and administration of the clinic, including but not limited to: - Creating a high-end, welcoming service environment for patients - Supporting a friendly and positive work environment for colleagues - Managing and responding to inbound sales/booking inquiries, via phone or online - Scheduling patient appointments - Optimizing the staff schedule for maximum staff utilization - Setting up relationships with insurers, including supporting HENRY (company) and individual dentists on the team becoming in-network providers - Managing insurance authorizations, submissions, and collections for patient treatments - Overseeing patient-management software, ensuring accurate records and reporting - Building and supporting relationships with corporate clients. LOCATIONS WILL VARY THROUGHOUT NEW JERSEY AS THIS IS A MOBILE PRACTICE.
