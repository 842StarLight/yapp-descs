<h2>Customer Support Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>
A Customer Support Manager will manage the interface with DriveScale customers to deliver and maintain DriveScale products. The initial focus will be to establish automated support for DriveScaleâ€™s latest software downloadable products, which includes setup and maintenance of support tools such as customer self-help knowledge base, public community forums, and online ticketing system. This hands-on player-coach role will be the leader of customer support and be responsible for growing the team as the company grows.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer Engagement
<ul>
<li>Maintain 100% DriveScale customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Timely communication with customers concerning status of open support issues, agreeing priority based on customer business impact, setting clear expectations about next steps and timeframes. Responsible for delivering against commitments.</li>
<li>Ensuring that customer issues are resolved in a timely manner and to their satisfaction as measured by SLAs and customer satisfaction surveys.</li>
<li>Thoroughly documenting all communication, troubleshooting, and activity on issues reported by customers in Salesforce Service Cloud.
</li>
<li>Troubleshooting issues related to DriveScale products and 3rd party software or hardware.</li>
<li>Customer Training on DriveScale product, feature, and issue resolution procedures.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Customer Support Systems
<ul>
<li>Interface with R&amp;D Engineering / QA to become a DriveScale product expert.</li>
<li>Working collaboratively with other team members and members of other DriveScale teams such as R&amp;D Engineering, QA, Product Mgmt, and Sales.</li>
<li>Maintain customer support tools and environments, such as
<ul>
<li>DriveScale product download site</li>
<li>DriveScale knowledge base and communities (Salesforce Service Cloud)</li>
<li>DriveScale ticket system</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Customer Support Management
<ul>
<li>Maintain customer satisfaction support metrics (issues, tickets open/closed, etc)</li>
<li>On location customer business reviews (quarterly, yearly)</li>
<li>Hire and Lead a team of CSEs</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Ideal Candidate will have the following skills and experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of software support experience, preferably in a high tech startup.</li>
<li>Considerable experience leading a customer support team and establishing customer support processes and tools.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and work through problems independently.</li>
<li>Strong Communications skills (written &amp; verbal).</li>
<li>4-year technical degree or equivalent experience, BS Computer Science preferred.</li>
<li>Highly organized and self-starting.</li>
<li>Strong priority management skills and proven problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Experience with the following technologies:
<ul>
<li>Datacenter Server Installations</li>
<li>Linux/Windows System Administration</li>
<li>Network Administration (Ethernet/IP)</li>
<li>Salesforce Service Cloud, JIRA</li>
<li>Storage Administration experience is a plus (e.g. NAS, SAN, HCI, RAID).</li>
<li>SQL, MySQL, Cassandra database administration and troubleshooting is a plus</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
