<p>The Bookkeeper is responsible for applying all incoming payments to the appropriate invoices and accounts, performing any cash transfers, and balancing entries.</p>
<p>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<p>
Identifies and posts payments to the appropriate accounts and/or invoices. Takes additional steps to identify payments when necessary and balances daily deposits to the detailed bank report. Also processes necessary adjustments and refunds, effectively communicates with both the Credit and Collection Team and Biller and must meet daily, weekly and monthly deadlines. Performs other duties as assigned.</p>
</div>
<div><p>High School degree or equivalent required. 1-2 years minimum cash application experience preferred. Excellent data entry skills, strong analytical and problem-solving skills. Proficient in Excel and must have the ability to work independently and as part of a team. Must be detailed oriented with excellent time management skills. Ability to effectively elicit/provide information to and from appropriate individuals, including, but not limited to, Managers, Sales, Supervisors, Co-workers and Clients.</p></div>
