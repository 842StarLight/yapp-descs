<h2>Level 3 NCR Technical Support Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Technical Support Analyst is responsible for advanced field operations support on both hardware and software, providing pre and post-sales technical support as well as the development, enhancement and delivery of training courses and content for product lines supported by Burroughs.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Functions/Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Essential duties and examples of day to day responsibilities will include</p>
<ul>
<li>
Technical Support Analyst will work with multiple product lines but be primarily focused on NCR ATM and ITMs for use by financial institutions.</li>
<li>
Provide support for internal and external customers on hardware and software products. This includes over the phone or on-site support.</li>
<li>
Continually research and acquire OEM specific domain knowledge from the ATM sales, service and repair industries.</li>
<li>
Development of technical documentation for products including software and hardware.</li>
<li>
Development of training for Field and Support staff including Instructor-led and on-line classes.</li>
<li>
Deliver technical training to internal and external customers on the operation and maintenance of Burroughs Supported products as needed.</li>
<li>
As needed learn new products for the purpose of support, training, and training development.</li>
<li>
Maintain records of support and communicate progress to managers and customers as needed.</li>
</ul>
