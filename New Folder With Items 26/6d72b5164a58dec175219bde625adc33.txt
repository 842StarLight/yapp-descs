<h2>Service Technician I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Description: </b></p>
<p>Midwest Bus Sales has immediate openings for experienced, quality-minded Service Technicians in our El Reno and Tulsa Oklahoma locations. Midwest Bus Sales is one of the largest and fastest growing Thomas school bus dealers in the US. As a part of our team you will be performing major and technically difficult diagnostics, repairs, and overhauls/rebuilds, including vehicle inspections, and general maintenance repairs, all in a timely manner; ensuring quality, time standards, customer expectations, cost guidelines and reliability goals are met.</p>
<p>This position demands high mechanical skills on med/heavy duty trucks and buses with considerable knowledge of methods, material, tools and the operation of vehicles and equipment for diagnostic purposes. Highly detail oriented and organized in work. Ability to work cooperatively and collaboratively with a customer service focus with all levels of employees, management, and external agencies to maximize performance, creativity, problem solving and results. Must have initiative and the ability to act and to operate independently with minimal daily direction from manager to accomplish objectives.</p>
<p><b>Key Objectives: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform inspection, diagnosis and repair of electrical, hydraulic, suspension, brake and air conditioning systems.</li>
<li>Utilizes vehicle computer electronic systems to interpret failure modes to initiate assigned repairs.</li>
<li>Detailed completion of paperwork utilizing fleet maintenance software program associated with repairing vehicles, documenting parts usage, and accounting for repair times.</li>
<li>Maintains a clean, safe work area in compliance with Corporate Standards, and performs all work in accordance with established safety procedures.</li>
<li>Updates job knowledge by participating in education opportunities, reading technical and regulation publications.</li>
<li>Team player who is focused on overall Dealership success.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced work environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong organizational skills.</li>
<li>Strong communications skills both written and verbal.</li>
<li>Basic computer skills as used for Repair order completion.</li>
<li>Exceptional time management with strong desire to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Must own tools required to complete assigned work.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Expected Hours of Work</b></p>
<p>This is a full-time position. Hours of work are Monday through Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., 40 hours per week required. Must be available to work extended hours/weekends in peak business needs as required.</p>
