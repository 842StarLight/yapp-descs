<h2>Contracts Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>ProKarma is seeking an experienced Contracts Manager to join our growing team in Beaverton Oregon. This role will be responsible for drafting, negotiating and advising on various technology contracts (customer, vendor, channel). The successful candidate must be able to work independently and have excellent communication skills as this position involves direct contact with principal business owners. The successful candidate must be inquisitive, enjoy working hard and being continually challenged, and demonstrate sound judgment even in ambiguous situations. This position will work closely with the Legal department, Sales, Information Security and Finance to review contracts, to identify issues requiring legal/business approval, and to work with the customer's procurement/legal departments to expedite transaction close.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b>
<ul>
<li>Provide legal support for our enterprise sales team on a broad range of commercial agreements in support of sales transactions.
</li>
<li>Review and negotiate standard and non-standard agreements
</li>
<li>Exercise good judgment in making business decisions while providing contract interpretations and guidance to internal business teams to ensure compliance with contractual requirements.
</li>
<li>Ensure timely review of contract modifications and communication of changes and notices to the business team.
</li>
<li>Work closely with cross-functional teams of (Sales, Legal, Finance, and Operations) in order to resolve legal and business challenges
</li>
<li>Ensure that all required approvals are obtained prior to submittal of contracts for execution
</li>
<li>Conduct various contract related projects and other duties
</li>
<li>Manage contract lifecycle for vendor, customer and channel agreements</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Qualifications</b>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>
3 or more years of contracts management, paralegal, or other comparable experience within a law firm or legal department</li>
<li>
Strong written and oral communication skills, including drafting contractual language</li>
<li>
Strong computer skills (e.g., proficiency with SharePoint, MS Word, Excel, E-signature tools, Salesforce etc.)</li>
<li>
Track terms as required</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational skills, ability to manage multiple projects at once, follow through and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Desire to help improve and streamline processes</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently while being able to contribute to cross-functional teams</li>
<li>
Familiarity with information technology services
</li>
</ul>
