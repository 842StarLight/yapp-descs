<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Company Overview: </b>Merlin International provides next-generation cybersecurity technologies that protect government and commercial organizations. We offer a focused portfolio of cybersecurity software that secure the enterprise from endpoints to networks, from governance to risk management, from infrastructure to information. Our people seek innovative ways to deliver cyber technology which organizations need to protect their critical business assets while furthering their core mission.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Job Summary: </b>With headquarters in our new, modern office space in Tysons, VA our Account Executives find pressing cybersecurity challenges facing our customers and bring together the resources needed to solve them. You'll work with a portfolio of leading enterprise software providers, top-notch emerging technologies and a network of procurement enabling strategies to make it possible and easy for our customers to acquire and use cybersecurity software tools. First and foremost you'll be a customer advocate and use deep customer knowledge matched with creative alignment of technologies and acquisition practices to get the right software in use as soon as possible.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities and Duties:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Deeply understand your customer</b>: You are your customer's representative to and within Merlin. We believe in our product portfolio and would love to see your customer use software from it, but our shared success comes from your ability to solve their problems both pressing and latent.</li>
<li>
<b>
Actively engage</b>: The landscape of cybersecurity software, tools and solutions is evolving rapidly. Our customers and teams expect to hear from you routinely and with pertinent information. We use all manners of media to stay abreast of the latest news and updates and expect you to actively participate in both creating and sharing knowledge. Whether it is developing a whitepaper, sharing relevant stories on social media, speaking at relevant industry events or leading a focus group, your active participation is critical to success.</li>
<li>
<b>
Develop and mature your pipeline</b>: This is your business portfolio. Leverage your passion for cybersecurity, entrepreneurial creativity and in-depth understanding of challenges facing stakeholders in your territory to seek, find and win opportunities</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Qualifications and Skills:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Inspirational. Creative. Self-reliant: </b>You have the ability and desire to create new opportunities. You're tired of seeing "shelf-ware" and know that great software applied to the right problems can make a lasting difference for your customer. Because Merlin has a flat hierarchy and you have direct access to the Merlin Executives and Owners, you work tirelessly to challenge assumptions and build technology packages your customers will actually use.</li>
<li>
<b>
Think like a startup employee, with a twist</b>: As the cybersecurity marketplace – suppliers and customers alike – constantly changes, you must continuously learn, challenge the norm and seek new opportunities. Merlin's 20+ years in business gives you a strong, stable foundation to consider a much broader array of approaches to meet your customer's needs and close the sale.</li>
<li>
<b>
Be intellectually nimble</b>: From day one you'll be creating and refining your playbook while meeting with all levels of representatives within your customer. Likewise, the Merlin portfolio is highly curated yet is constantly growing. The only constant is that things change.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Benefits and Perks</b></div>
<div>
Merlin employees work hard and play hard. Our new office features multiple places to work unconstrained by typical office cubes. Our wellness package provides access to an on-site gym and includes medical, dental and vision insurances along with options for FSA and EAP. We offer 401(k) with employer match, PTO, and a work culture respectful of the fact that not everything in one's personal life is guaranteed to happen "after hours." Managers have a wide degree of flexibility to create schedules that enable great work-life balance for their teams.</div>
