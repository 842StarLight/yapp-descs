<h2>Graphic Design Internship (Fall 2019)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Title:</b> Graphic Design Internship<br>
<b>Department:</b> Marketing<br>
<b>Reports to: </b>Graphic Design Team<br>
<br>
<b>Internship Summary:</b><br>
Interns will have the opportunity to learn about the history, culture and vision of Orlando City SC, understanding the importance of how a professional sports organization works (which could include sales, marketing, public relations, operations, or the internal working of the Club), to further develop general critical thinking, problem solving, time management, communication, and writing skills.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Position Summary:</b> Orlando City SC is looking for a creative and driven Graphics Design Intern. This person will work under the direction of the Graphic Design team and will have the opportunity to learn how to design and produce marketing collateral for the Club. Concluding this internship this person will know how to design, prepare and present marketing collateral, within the marketing department for a professional sports team. <b>YOU MUST BE ABLE TO RECEIVE ACADEMIC CREDIT FOR THIS INTERNSHIP.</b><br>
</div>
<div><b><i>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assist with design of ticket collateral (single tickets, season tickets, season ticket holder books).</li>
<li>Work with design team on producing advertising material (print, out-of-home, etc.).</li>
<li>Assist with creation of interactive design (website design, Facebook images/tab design, etc.) for Orlando City, Orlando Pride, OCB and youth properties.</li>
<li>Assist with corporate sales fulfillment (when necessary) and collateral.</li>
<li>Provide input and assistance with overall look of branded signage at the stadium (JumboTron, LEDs, banners, etc.).</li>
<li>Support design team with presentations, collateral and photo editing.</li>
<li>Assist with minimum of 2-3 office days per week, or as needed.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
 <b><i>Qualifications:</i></b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Applicants should be working towards an undergraduate or graduate degree with an emphasis in graphic design, marketing, or related field. Candidates receiving credit for participating in our internship program are preferred.</li>
<li>Interested in pursuing a career in graphic design, marketing, etc.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign.</li>
<li>Exceptional reliability, interpersonal, communication, and organizational skills required.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail is a must.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of a wide variety of graphic design applications platforms.</li>
<li>Able to work non-traditional hours, in non-traditional settings, within a team atmosphere.</li>
<li>A sense of humor is a must. Prepare to tell us a good joke in your interview.</li>
</ul>
