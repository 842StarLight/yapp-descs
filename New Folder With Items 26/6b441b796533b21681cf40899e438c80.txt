<h2>Product Manager - myRisk Hereditary Cancer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Overview:</b><br>
Myriad Women's Health, Inc., the premier genetic screening and personalized medicine testing
<br>company is looking for a dynamic Product Manager of myRiskⓇ Hereditary Cancer, to develop, lead and execute the strategic commercialization plan. This would include creating marketing programs, developing product content/messaging, marketing tactics and monitoring ROI measurement of these activities. This individual will build strong relationships with key cross functional leaders, teams and stakeholders within Myriad Women's Health and across the Myriad Genetics, Inc. enterprise.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Commercialize key myRiskⓇ Hereditary Cancer enhancements</li>
<li>Execute marketing strategy, leveraging communication channels that are most relevant to our key segments - this includes the sales team, traditional and digital media, and other stakeholders (MD's, Genetic Counselors, clinical staff or clinic administrators, advocacy groups)</li>
<li>Manage the tactical execution of initiatives according to established timelines</li>
<li>Monitor internal and external resource progress on initiatives and ROI</li>
<li>Manage budgets, vendors and purchase orders</li>
<li>Support promotional needs and deliverables related to myRisk for Myriad Women's Health conference participation</li>
<li>Develop deep insights about our providers and patients and their journey throughout the myRiskⓇ Hereditary Cancer testing process.</li>
<li>Utilize insights to partner closely with the Women's Health Clinical Marketing, Product Development, Medical Affairs and Customer Success teams in the development of provider and patient innovations and improvements in order to drive volume growth in myRisk and enhance customer stickiness</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree is mandatory; MBA or relevant advanced degree is preferred.</li>
<li>At least 4 years' experience in healthcare marketing preferred Experience is desired in brand management, strategic plan development, relationship building, execution of tactical plans, and cross-functional work</li>
<li>Leadership and development of other marketing personnel is required and this type of experience will be helpful in the position.</li>
