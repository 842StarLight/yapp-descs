<h2>Director of IT Risk for MetLife Investments Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Location: </b>Whippany, NJ</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Role Value Proposition:</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>Given an updated business strategy to further build out the MetLife Investment Management (MIM) Business, Investments IT has a need for a senior representative who can be the face of the Investments IT Risk and Compliance efforts. The Director of Investments IT Risk and Compliance will serve as both the thought leader and delivery manager of IT Risk and Audit impacting Investments Technology. The subject matter expert will renew, drive and execute the global IT Risk and Compliance strategy for MetLife Investments Technology and serve as a senior professional within the organization on all subjects related to IT Risk, Compliance, and Audit. The candidate will be required to provide guidance to a dedicated team of IT professionals across the globe. The role will frequently interact with members of senior management to provide perspective, insights and feedback on IT Risk and Compliance from both an industry point of view and internal point of view interacting with Senior Leadership across Investments Technology, Investments Business, Compliance, CISO, IT Risk and Controls, Internal Audit (Business and IT), and External Audit. In addition, this role will require the candidate to develop and execute a process for best understanding third party vendor risk within our ecosystem or new vendors under review. The Lead will engage in future state IT strategy discussions to discuss global risk, policy, onboarding, new employee engagement, and more. Will work globally with the various MIM country leads and control officers to ensure local regulations are being met. The resource will ensure Capital Markets/Asset Management adherence to IT Risk and Controls including regulations from FINRA and SEC. Knowledge of Investments specific content such as best execution, credit risk, and loans processing is also critical. Role requires keeping abreast and understanding regulations as it pertains to Asset/Investments Management. This role will provide leadership during a period of rapid organizational change and industry advancement including the MIM growth, as well as technical efforts such as DevOps transition, increasing use of the cloud, and increasing client facing applications.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Generate Investments IT strategic roadmap to manage IT Risk and Compliance, aligned with the future state Business and IT Strategy. This entails partnering with Executives to understand future state, roadmap creation, and game plan for execution.
</li>
<li>Engage in future state business strategy discussions to discuss risk strategy, policy, onboarding, new employee engagement, and more; keeping abreast of and anticipate changes in regulatory regimes and implement appropriate changes to compliance practices and policies</li>
<li>
Be the key liaison, increase partnership and strengthen engagement/operating model with both Investments Technology, Investments Business, Compliance, CISO, IT Risk and Controls, Internal Audit (Business and IT), and External Auditors; effectively partner across the globe with MIM country leads and control officers to ensure local regulations known and being met</li>
<li>
Represent and interact with external Institutional clients or industry consultants in addition to MetLife’s internal cyber teams during sales meetings or client annual due diligence MIM reviews; represent Investments Technology at industry events including asset management and technology risk/compliance</li>
<li>
Ensure all IT Risk and Controls adhere to Capital Markets/Asset Management related regulations like FINRA and SEC
</li>
<li>Manage day-to-day IT Risk operations including risk waivers, oversight of application security scans, disaster recovery progress, third party vendor assessments; comprehend key third-party vendors and associated environmental risks and promote a plan for monitoring/remediation</li>
<li>
Manage day-to-day IT Audit operations including internal/external audits including partnering with MIM control officer (SOC 1/SSAE18)</li>
<li>
Execute key IT Risk programs including efforts such as California Consumer Privacy Act and GDPR</li>
<li>
Understand and educate IT staff on internal controls and relationship to IT job functions including the development of governance structures, frameworks, tools, and training guides to enable IT associates to best understand and manage risk</li>
<li>
Lead, manage and develop a diverse team of IT Risk professionals by optimizing the use of resources to achieve departmental and organizational objectives</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Business Experience and Technical Skills:</b></p>
<p>
Required:</p>
<ul>
<li>
7+ years in a senior leadership role with relevant risk management experience with deep and broad understanding of the financial services industry including familiarity with key risk management concepts and/or the risk types (market, investment, insurance, operational)</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree required, major in business or technology-related field preferred</li>
<li>
One or more of the following certifications: CISM, CISSP, CISA, CRISC, GSLC</li>
<li>
Managed Accounts or Investment Management business domain expertise across a combination of research, portfolio management, and trading and investment operations</li>
<li>
Progressive responsibilities in Technology, Compliance, Risk Management, and/or Audit with proven track record of working with complex operational environments and associated technology platforms needed to support these environments</li>
<li>
Servant leader that is focused on promoting a team culture of accountability and continuous improvement and removing drag forces to enable team success</li>
<li>
Expertise with documenting, measuring and instrumenting/automating key processes and track record of identifying and mitigating top operational risks &amp; history of successfully leading operational transformation initiatives</li>
<li>
Awareness to the risk and compliance guidelines for efforts such as GDPR, California Consumer Privacy Act, Dodd-Frank, Sarbanes-Oxley and like regulatory programs</li>
</ul>

Preferred:</p>

Experience with new technology trends including understanding of Scrum (Agile) and DevOps frameworks/tools (TFS, GIT, or similar), enterprise level cloud-based development, deployment, and auditing, including PAAS, IAAS, and SAAS, and/or prior experience in Digital API product development and service oriented architectures</li>
<li>
Master's / MBA</li>
<li>
Experience in a global investment management firm a major advantage</li>
