<h2>Dining Room Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position summary: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages daily fine dining restaurant operations and supervises servers, bussers, food runners.</li>
<li>Effectively communicates with Operations Manager and management team members.</li>
<li>Strives to continually improve guest and employee satisfaction and maximize the financial performance.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee the dining area, supervises service staff in accordance with operating policies.</li>
<li>Creates a positive team atmosphere among team members.</li>
<li>Sets high standards for appropriate team behavior on shift.</li>
<li>Works with restaurant staff to ensure proper food presentation and proper food-handling procedures.</li>
<li>Total receipts and balance against sales, deposit receipts, and lock facility at end of day.</li>
<li>Ensures new products and menu are executed properly following roll-out.</li>
<li>Effectively forecast restaurant needs.</li>
<li>Prioritize tasks effectively to ensure most important tasks are completed on time.</li>
<li>Seeks, listens, and responds to guest feedback.</li>
<li>Coaches team on how to exceed guest expectations.</li>
<li>Proficiency in using POS software and computer software to monitor inventory, track staff schedules and pay, and perform other record keeping tasks.</li>
</ul>
<p>Pay based off experience.</p>
