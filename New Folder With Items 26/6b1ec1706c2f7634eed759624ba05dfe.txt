<div><b>
Position Summary</b></div>
<div>
Your role as Customer Success Manager (CSM) will be to cultivate and grow post-sale relationships for Chatmeter’s top business and enterprise clients with the goal of retaining them as customers for life. As a Customer Success Manager, you’ll take a proactive vs. reactive approach to building rapport with customers. You’re maniacally focused on the adoption of the full-suite of Chatmeter services, naturally curious and are dynamic in a sense that they can work with different personalities and a broad range of customers who are in different phases of the customer life cycle.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
What You Will Do:</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Become the primary contact post-sales, quarterbacking the customer and herding cats internally through the on-boarding process and taking full ownership of the account's health post-implementation.</li>
<li> Establish a trusting relationship with customers: Day-to-day users, key stakeholders and executive sponsors. Support our on-boarding team and establish partnership goals with all customers.</li>
<li> Manage inbound customer communications and organize/escalate issues appropriately including: billing, legal, security, and technical inquiries.</li>
<li> Interface and collaborate with other Chatmeter teams: Engineering, Product Management, Sales, Support, Marketing and Executives to complete customer goals and, in general, to be the voice of the customer to provide visibility and/or escalations</li>
<li> Show grit and hustle to break through walls to renew customers and expand their business.</li>
<li> Communicate and educate customers on major updates and releases, conduct discovery on business use cases for new features and proper prioritization.</li>
<li> Analyze and monitor adoption and product utilization - alerting clients on any risk or general awareness based on these trending metrics</li>
<li> Conduct weekly customer health-checks with an emphasis on customer happiness</li>
<li> Partner with executives, sales &amp; product teams - alert of any risks and ensure a successful renewal on an account you own.</li>
<li> Serve as the voice of the customer and collect feedback to drive continuous improvement across all areas including product</li>
</ul>

What You Will Bring:</b></div>
<ul>
<li> 5 years of enterprise account management experience in a SaaS organization.</li>
<li> Excellent written and verbal communication skills - able to simplify complex topics in a friendly, approachable manner. Personality is key.</li>
<li> Flexible and able to juggle competing priorities and projects (internally and externally).</li>
<li> General knowledge of the top cloud-based applications in a standard sales stack: Salesforce, Google Apps, Marketo, etc.</li>
<li> Ability to work under pressure and deadlines</li>
<li> A considerate team player but self-driven to get things done</li>
<li> Interest to grow technically and a true problem-solver</li>
</ul>
