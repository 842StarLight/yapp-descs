<b>Team &amp; Function Overview:</b><br>
General Assembly (GA)'s Enterprise (B2B) sales team has established a new Enterprise Sales Development Representative role tasked with assisting our rapidly growing Enterprise team in growing GA's business through customer acquisition. This role will serve the integral purpose of supporting GA's Account Directors in executing GA's largest enterprise deals from initial contact to deal delivery. With GA's recent acquisition, B2B sales are a top priority for GA, and the members of this team will be significant players in driving the success of GA's Enterprise team. All Sales Associates will be a part of our Enterprise Sales Training program with the goal of moving Sales Associates into Account Director roles. We are accepting applications for Q4 2019 - Q1 2020.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
Top Funnel Activity
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Reach out to potential customers through email, phone and social media in order to qualify sales opportunities for the wider Enterprise team</li>

<li>Assist in the development and ever evolving team prospecting strategy</li>
<li>Provide potential customers with an articulate understanding of GA's mission as well as the products and services GA can provide</li>
<li>Manage and maintain a deep pipeline of potential customers</li>
</ul>
Sales Support
<br><br>

<li>Support tenured AE's to close large enterprise deals and help navigate through complex sales through call prep, deck creation and targeted follow up.</li>
</ul>
