<p>
KPMG is currently seeking a Director, Salesforce Alliance to join our Alliances organization.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Oversee the day to day alliance relationship and activities with salesforce.com (SFDC); Direct the alliance management, alliance enablement, and alliance pipeline, and support the solution development, sales, and marketing required by the alliance</li>
<li>
Manage the firm's participation in SFDC's formal program; including training, certifications, awards, software usage rights, opportunity registration, conferences, and joint marketing</li>
<li>
Lead development and approval of a joint business plan, support risk and legal contracts, and gain commitment from interested and affected individuals; provide the service networks with access to product management, development, support and training resources from SFDC</li>
<li>
Oversee the alliance launch and drive the enablement of SFDC; including supporting the development of joint offerings, sales kits, training, and marketing and communications plans; drive, measure, and manage internal/external campaigns to generate demand</li>
<li>
Educate and align SFDC field sellers / marketers with KPMG's SFDC practice and sales teams</li>
<li>
Provide stakeholders with updates on SFDC's organization and manage the firm's alliance relationship mapping and drive progress on multiple leads; thereby increasing the size of the Alliance Influenced Pipeline</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of seven years of experience in services or consultative business development with at least two years of experience managing a strategic alliance with SFDC</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree from an accredited college or university</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in building and nurturing win-win alliance relationships</li>
<li>
Strong networking and influence management skills that translate into building commitment and driving actions across organizational boundaries</li>
<li>
Highly credible communicator with excellent organization and project management skills and track record of delivering results</li>
<li>
Travel up to forty percent</li>
<li>
Applicants must be currently authorized to work in the United States without the need for visa sponsorship now or in the future</li>
</ul>
