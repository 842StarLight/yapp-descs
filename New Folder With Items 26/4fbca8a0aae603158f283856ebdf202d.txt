<b>Summary Job Description:</b><br>
A Business Development Representative is required to support the fast growing Enterprise sales team. The team is currently 3, but we have plans to grow the team to 40 by the end of 2020, creating fantastic career development opportunities for those individuals who join us now.
<br><br>
Lineten is growing quickly and we need ambitious individuals to grow with us. There will be ample opportunities for you to progress to becoming a Business Development Manager either within our Enterprise team or follow your predecessor into a Marketing role or in another part of the business.
<br><br>
As a Business Development Manager, you will be our eyes and ears on the ground in NY/LA, as well as help us expand our product throughout the US. You will work alongside our London based Sales and Growth Team to help find, capture, and onboard future clients to support our expansion within the US. Due to our expansion being so recent, the applicant would require to work from home until such time that an office is opened in NY/LA. Moreover, given the early nature of our expansion, we are looking for a candidate that will be flexible to support the growth of operations.
