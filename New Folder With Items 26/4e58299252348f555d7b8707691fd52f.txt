<p><b>Role Summary
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Provide consultation services to Arm &amp; Hammer Animal Nutrition customers which facilitate improved productivity through achieving desired outcomes.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Role Accountabilities and Responsibilities </b>(Describe the 5-7 major accountabilities)
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Animal Productivity Specialist will work closely with the Arm &amp; Hammer Animal Nutrition Channel Sales Account Managers and Enterprise Sales Account Managers to prioritize and allocate their support among Arm &amp; Hammer Animal Nutrition customers.
</p>
<p>Provide consultation services on ruminant operations including:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct on-farm evaluations for requested ruminant management services.
</li>
<li>Communicate on farm observations and opportunities
</li>
<li>Collaborate with Arm &amp; Hammer colleagues to optimize value of Arm &amp; Hammer products, services and programs with ruminant owners and herd managers.
</li>
<li>Delivery of ongoing Animal Productivity services
</li>
<li>Facilitate Workshops with veterinarians, nutritionists, and herd managers to improve on-farm monitoring throughout the transition period.
</li>
<li>Deliver on farm training
</li>
<li>Identify opportunities on operations and communicate with sales team
</li>
<li>Influence ruminant owners and herd managers on concepts that will achieve their needs and objectives
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and Experience
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>BS degree from accredited institution preferred in Animal or Dairy Sciences with 2 or more years of applicable experience
 </li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>or
</p>
<ul><li>5 years or more experience in ruminant operations and labor management.
</li></ul>
