<h2>Customer Relationship and Collections Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Who is WiLine Networks?</p>
<p>
WiLine Networks is a global leading provider of fast, flexible, affordable communications services solutions to businesses, building owners, wholesale providers. We offer configurable solutions that empower our customers to select the right combination of bandwidth and services to maximize productivity. Through a single broadband connection over our private network, we deliver voice, data, video and mobility solutions.</p>
<p><b>
What you will do in the role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage collections and ensure that all monies due to the company are collected in a timely and efficient manner.</li>
<li>Assist management in reviewing day to day collections activity.
</li>
<li>In conjunction with Billing &amp; Collections Manager review day to day collections activity, set effective collection targets and regularly track and reports progress.</li>
<li>Resolve escalated queries in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Ensure that credit policies and procedures are fully and consistently implemented.</li>
<li>Monitor manual documents ensuring they are raised in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Assist the Billing &amp; Collections Manager with month and quarter end reporting and close activities.</li>
<li>Work with Billing &amp; Collections Manager and colleagues to ensure continuous improvement in AR policies and processes.</li>
<li>Work closely with all areas of the business to resolve queries and maintain strong relationship with customers &amp; stakeholders including Customer Experience, Contracts, Service Delivery, IT, Customer Support and Sales Teams.
</li>
<li>Prepare and review monthly performance reports.</li>
<li>Monitor the Residential customer lead hotline and following up sales opportunities as appropriate. Monthly commission will be paid based on new accounts that are opened.
</li>
<li>Other duties and ad hoc projects as reasonably required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills &amp; qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Two-year associated degree or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>Five years experience in collections or a related field</li>
<li>Ability to maintain policies and procedures for the credit and collection processes.</li>
<li>Dispute management, resolution and escalation.</li>
<li>Excellent communication, interpersonal and organizational skills.
</li>
<li>Negotiating in a calm and confident manner to handle potentially difficult conversations.</li>
<li>Be competent in Microsoft Office and accounting software program.
</li>
<li>Ability to meet deadlines, prioritize and plan work activities, use time efficiently, set goals and objectives.
</li>
<li>Identify and resolve problems in a timely manner and gather and analyze information accurately and develop alternative solutions if/where applicable.
</li>
<li>Display a willingness to make decisions on own initiative, display sound and accurate judgment, support and explain the reasoning for decisions, include appropriate people in decision-making process, make timely decisions.<br>
</li>
</ul>
