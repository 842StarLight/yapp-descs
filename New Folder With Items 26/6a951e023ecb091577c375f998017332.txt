<h2>Accounts Receivable Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Smith Legacy Services (SLS) is a provider of administrative serviceslocated in Northeast San Antonio. We are seeking an Accounts Receivable Specialist witha strong work ethic and the ability to multi-task.</p>
<p>The Accounts Receivable Specialist is responsible for generatingaccurate, complete and timely invoicing and ensures that accounting proceduresare followed.</p>
<p>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Review and confirm supporting documentation for invoice completion; including sales orders, purchase orders, shipping and receiving documents</li>
<li>Prepare, generate and send out invoices, credit and debit memos</li>
<li>Verify proper accounting and sales tax treatment</li>
<li>Carry out reporting activities according to specific deadlines</li>
<li>Research and resolve billing discrepancies and customer inquiries</li>
<li>Effectively communicate with internal and external customers</li>
<li>Assist with month end and year end accounting duties</li>
<li>Assist department with other duties and responsibilities as assigned</li>
<li>New customer setup and maintenance</li>
</ul>
<p>Minimum Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree in Accounting or Business Management, or 2 years of accounting experience in lieu of degree</li>
<li>2 years of Accounting experience with a concentration in Accounts Receivables preferred</li>
<li>Detail oriented, able to work under tight deadlines and with a sense of urgency</li>
<li>Experience with QuickBooks Enterprise is preferred</li>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office, including the ability to operate computerized accounting and spreadsheet programs</li>
<li>Ability to work as a positive team member in support of department and organizational goals</li>
<li>Strong communication skills both written and verbal</li>
<li>Strong data entry skills with high level of accuracy</li>
<li>Demonstrates a sense of urgency and ability to meet deadlines</li>
</ul>
