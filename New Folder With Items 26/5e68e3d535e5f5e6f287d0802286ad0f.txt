<p>Floor Covering Estimator</p>
<p>Paulson’s Floor Coverings is currently hiring in our Commercial Flooring division to assist our Estimating Team in increasing monthly bid volume. Position will be subordinate to Senior Estimator, who will oversee and finalize generated bids as well as assign daily estimating tasks. We are looking for a detail‐oriented individual with strong computer skills, and experience in floor covering is preferred but not required. Applicants should have an excellent work ethic and possess strong organizational skills and attention to detail. A strong foundation in general computer skills is required. Knowledge of RFMS Measure or other take‐off software is recommended, as is experience reading blueprints and specifications, but this is an entry‐level position and we are willing to train.</p>
<p><b>Exceptional candidates will also possess: </b></p>
<p>‐A 2‐year or 4‐year degree in construction management, math, accounting, economics, computer science or related fields</p>
<p>‐Experience in construction estimating, floor covering sales or installation</p>
<p>‐Working knowledge of quantification/strong basic math skills, specifically measurement conversion</p>
<p>(linear footage to square footage, per‐piece to per‐unit, etc)</p>
<p><b>Duties to be performed will include but are not limited to: </b></p>
<p>‐Working with Senior Estimator to produce $2‐4,000,000 of flooring bids per month</p>
<p>‐ Reading and interpreting architectural plans and project specification manuals</p>
<p>‐ Using RFMS Measure (2D flooring‐specific CAD program) to estimate flooring quantities</p>
<p>‐ Helping process bids using Excel template and RFMS bid program</p>
<p>‐ Processing awarded contracts into installable jobs, including plan‐specific instructions for installers</p>
<p>‐ Once fully trained, accurate, independent estimation of approximately $250,000 of flooring bids per month (initially the position will be assisting, not impendent estimation)</p>
<p>‐ Cross‐training in product ordering, change order proposals, and project management/scheduling</p>
<p><b>We are offering a great compensation package, including: </b></p>
<p>‐ Competitive wage rates, compensation dependent on experience</p>
<p>‐ Full benefits, including health care, flex spending account, 401k and paid vacation after trial period</p>
<p>‐ Mileage reimbursement for work‐related travel</p>
<p>‐ An excellent work environment at a stable and lasting company. Our CCB# is 205 and we have been in business for almost 100 years.</p>
<p>Paulson’s Floor Covering is a drug free workplace: all employees are subject to a pre‐employment drug screen and background check.</p>
