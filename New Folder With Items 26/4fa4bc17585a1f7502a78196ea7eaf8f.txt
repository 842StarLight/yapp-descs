<h2>Sr. Provider Relations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The role of the Provider Relations Manager is to manage day-to-day staff and operations of the Sr. Provider Relations Team, ensuring service quality and productivity reports, market reports, market meetings are completed and attended to on a consistent basis. The Provider Relations Manager is accountable for performance at the individual representative level and is prepared to make sound decisions / adjustments regarding resource allocation whenever indicated in order to maintain performance. The Provider Relations Manager has a responsibility to provide continuous coaching and feedback to the representatives in order to enable and motivate them to perform at expected levels, which includes job performance (quality, productivity, compliance with regulatory requirements), attendance, work habits and adherence to departmental and human resources policies. The responsibilities of this position include but are not excluded the training and on-boarding of all Provider Relations and Network Contracting staff for the Florida Market. Additionally, this position is responsible for ensuring that all daily, monthly, quarterly reports are completed within guidelines as set by the department Director. This position will schedule monthly operational meetings with any and all action items to the team to ensure the needs of the market are met. This position will report directly to the Director of Provider Relations in achieving short and long term operational and strategic goals by developing and enhancing operational information. In addition, they will be assisting in researching and dissecting data within the organizations software tools to investigate problem trends and present solutions that will provide better overall service to the market and organization as a whole.</p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Manages and develops the Sr. Provider Relations staff to include training, goal setting, and motivation.</li>
<li>
Manages the Provider Relations staff and is responsible for the overall success of the market’s network of contracted specialists / ancillary providers.</li>
<li>
Represents Provider Relations as a decision-maker in various cross-functional meetings, projects and activities.</li>
<li>
Utilizes departmental data and information to analyze trends and identify opportunities for improvement.</li>
<li>
Develops training needs and training materials as needed by the markets.</li>
<li>
Participates in development and implementation of systems that support network operations and network management.</li>
<li>
Handles escalated provider issues in accordance with company policies.</li>
<li>
Maintains and produces educational tools for initial training to provider offices and works with all WellMed partners to ensure that educational tools for markets are current and most up-to-date.</li>
<li>
Maintains effective network support services by working effectively with the Medical Directors, Market Medical Directors, Regional Teams, Senior Leadership, and other corporate leaders.</li>
<li>
Analyzes trends of contracted provider visits and reviews all outlying visits to target areas of opportunity to provide re-education.</li>
<li>
Develops strategies to align contracted provider groups with company initiatives, goals (revenue and expense) and quality outcomes.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the Provider Relations &amp; Network Management Staffs’ daily tracking tools, claims logs, and all required documents completed timely.</li>
<li>
Corresponds with other Sr. Provider Relations team members in other markets to ensure that across market initiatives are followed and makes recommendations when variance arises.</li>
<li>
Prioritizes and organizes assigned workload while still meeting deadlines and delivering the best outcome possible.</li>
<li>
Provides support to internal customers within the organization as the subject matter expert.</li>
<li>
Participates in Network Management Meetings and makes market recommendations based on in-depth research and details surrounding market requests through analysis of claims, adequacy, and market competition.</li>
<li>
Engages providers and provider staff in analysis and evaluation of data improving processes and education.</li>
<li>
Produce, publish, and distribute scheduled reports to the market and have a clear understanding of materials.</li>
<li>
Ensures that all providers are operating with active contracts and within their approved billing codes to reduce claims issues and provides education and re-teaching when necessary.</li>
<li>
Performs all other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>

Required Qualifications:</b></div>

Strong working knowledge of Medicare healthcare operations.</li>

Understanding of medical care delivery and local market dynamics</li>

Strong interpersonal skills with ability to interface effectively both externally and internally with a wide range of people including physicians, office staff and other health plan staff.</li>

Excellent problem solving skills with effective follow through</li>

Face-to-face sales or customer service experience</li>

Experience in group presentations and training</li>

Strong business acumen, analytical, critical thinking, and persuasion</li>

Proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Access</li>
