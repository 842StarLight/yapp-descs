<h2>Point of Sale (POS) Support Technician 1 yr exp</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Position responsible for installation, maintenance and repairs on equipment for all assigned calls to assure continuity of customer operations and high levels of customer satisfaction.</p>
<p>Responsible for ownership of customer problems or incidents until the situation has been resolved to the customer's satisfaction and or in compliance with agreed upon Service Level Agreements.</p>
<p>Required to perform work on products and services of High complexity, specifically related to the Aloha Restaurant POS line of business; Make minor and major repairs and replace components on equipment such as PCs, workstations and peripherals, printers, and POS systems.</p>
<p>Responsible for assisting in site preparation, including installation of cable, staging of equipment, and testing of equipment; May also perform required modular swaps and unit replacements for customers in the Aloha POS Line of Business.</p>
<p>Build working relationships with customers and at the local level; Represents Pontchartrain Systems LLC in a manner that reflects positively on the image and reputation of the company<br>Performs periodic preventative maintenance on assigned products; Diagnoses problems, makes minor repairs, and replaces components (at the module level).</p>
<p>Perform Helpdesk/Telephone support duties as required. Including working full shifts or providing supplemental support during downtime and or on-call shifts.</p>
<p>Responsible for all Pontchartrain Systems LLC assets that will be used in the delivery of customer services (i.e. tools, software, vehicles, documentation, and intellectual property), or as assigned by management; Maintain an appropriate parts inventory as well as parts record keeping</p>
<p>Responsible for accurate and prompt reporting of calls and activity, as well as other reporting as required; Responsible for tracking and reporting all expenses incurred in the delivery of services, including telephone, private automobile, and/or public transportation</p>
<p>Responsible for capturing detailed customer asset information and other data required to complete any invoicing or billing activities</p>
<p>Responsible for collecting for COD accounts as necessary and promptly handing in such payments to the accounting department.</p>
<p>Will be dispatched daily on a call-by-call basis.</p>
<p>May be dispatched to take mass transit, and personal vehicle as required. May require prolonged travel, carrying and lifting tool kit/parts of varying weights (1- 50 lbs.); May require bending/squatting, walking/standing /sitting for prolonged periods; May be required to work on rotating shifts</p>
<p>Ensure staff productivity measures are achieved; Work together with Call Center Staff, implementation team, and Management</p>
<p>Hardware maintenance or install experience is required; Strong desktop hardware experience may be considered if experience is limited.</p>
