<h2>Sr Brand Copywriter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Why Vacasa
<br><br>
<b>We started with just one home and an idea:</b> to bring homeowners and renters together with smart technology and caring local teams. Today, we're the largest full-service vacation rental company in North America thanks to the people who give us their best every day. You'll fit right in here if you're curious, entrepreneurial, and thrive in a rapid-growth environment.
<br><br>
Why Brand at Vacasa
<br><br>
Brand is the left brain of Vacasa, putting big ideas into beautiful packages. We tell the world our story—and have a great time doing it. You'll create, collaborate, and help us shape our future as a beloved consumer brand.
<br><br>
What we're looking for
<br><br>
We're looking for an experienced writer to produce highly visible, highly valuable, and strategically critical content to tell the Vacasa story and support our business needs.
<br><br>
You'll generate ideas, collaborate with talented graphic and product designers, and meet with internal leaders and stakeholders to incorporate thoughts, feedback, and messaging in a consistent voice and tone.
<br><br>
This role will report to our TBH Creative Director.
<br><br>
What you'll do
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Tell the story of Vacasa by translating our brand strategy into engaging advertising copy (conversion focused), sales enablement collateral, key customer communications, video scripts, primary landing pages, email campaigns, and native social media.</li>
<li>Support our Product Design team with product copy (including positioning, user flow text, notifications, FAQs, and CTAs)</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing, PR, digital, and other internal teams to work conceptually from beginning stages to final execution for projects and campaigns.</li>
<li>Participate in project briefings to ensure creative efforts align with overall business strategy of projects.</li>
<li>Lead creative presentations and meetings as needed.</li>
<li>Mentor more junior writers as they hone their craft.</li>
</ul>
The skills you'll need
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience developing concepts and executing content creation for known brands or organizations, either on the client or agency side, across multiple channels.</li>
<li>Strategic thinking: you know how to ask the right questions to ensure the content you're creating meets our true business need, and how to align the answers with our brand strategy.</li>
<li>Balance: you can prioritize multiple projects and tasks according to business needs.</li>
<li>Collaborative spirit: you love to work with strategists and designers to create highly engaging and effective content.</li>
<li>You take brand personally: you can speak passionately about brands you admire, and you care about every communication holding fast to Vacasa's values and voice. You put yourself in the shoes of your customer and adapt your style to meet the goal of a piece.</li>
<li>Grammar guru: do we even need to say this? You have exceptional spelling and grammar skills, expect perfection in all communications, and educate your colleagues when needed.</li>
<li>Eagerness to grow and educate: even as a senior writer, you welcome feedback, ask for new opportunities, and pay attention to industry trends to stay at the top of your game—and love to share your expertise with the other writers on the team.</li>
</ul>

<li>Paid sick leave and holidays</li>
<li>Career advancement opportunities</li>
<li>Employee discounts</li>
<li>All the equipment you'll need to be successful</li>
<li>Great colleagues and culture</li>
</ul>
