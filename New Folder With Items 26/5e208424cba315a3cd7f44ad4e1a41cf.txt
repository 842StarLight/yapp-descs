<h2>Buyer/Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
LiveTrends Design Group is a fast-growing company that specializes in design, marketing, and distribution of unique home décor products for the North American mass-market. Many of our products feature high-end accessories, pottery and living plants. Our customers are some of the largest retail chains in the USA and Canada.
<br><br>
We are currently searching for a Buyer/Planner, to maintain ideal stock levels at LiveTrends Design ensuring on time delivery to all customers working with the order engineering team to design clean Item Masters and Bill of Materials for efficient Production Planning within cost expectations.
<br><br>
<b>Job Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>All purchasing inclusive of hard goods, live goods, accessories, packaging, and office supplies</li>
<li>Work with Inventory Management to ensure accuracy of inventory on hand against future purchase plans to be in line with Cash Flow requirements</li>
<li>Maintaining proper inventory levels to support forecast of new product development initiatives in order to not disrupt production efficiencies</li>
<li>Assist Sales with the forecasting/analysis as required</li>
<li>Work with Product Management to Set-up item masters to ensure in line with Cost and Gross Margin expectations before launch</li>
<li>Work with suppliers on Engineering Specifications and approval prior to start of production</li>
<li>Work with Production to ensure inventory on hand is continually reviewed against Material Resource Planning required from Operations to meet Customer Service Commitments</li>
<li>Place Set-up Item purchase costs, plan and place purchase orders accordingly and manage open/closed PO’s for accuracy of COGS following landed cost models</li>
<li>Follow up with Customs on Shipments as necessary and ensure compliance</li>
<li>Establish Yearly Cost Reduction Objectives to execute in support of improving Gross Margins</li>
<li>Develop KANBAN systems for 3rd party suppliers as necessary</li>
<li>Responsible for Purchase Programs for Indirect and MRO items</li>
</ul>
<b>Back-Up Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Serve as liaison and primary back-up to Product Engineering specials as defined by head of Product Development.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Enterprise Resource Planning Skills in JD Edwards, SAP, or similar systems is required with emphasis on Purchasing, Planning, Inventory Mgmt. &amp; Costing</li>
<li>2 Year Degree Minimum Requirement</li>
<li>Certificate from Purchasing &amp; Duty &amp; Tax courses</li>
<li>Minimum 2-3 years office work</li>
</ul>
