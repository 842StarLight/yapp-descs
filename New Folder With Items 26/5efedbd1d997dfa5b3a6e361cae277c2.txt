Job Summary
<br><br>
We are seeking a motivated, creative marketing manager to join our growing organization. In this position, you will oversee product development and monitor trends that indicate the need for new products and services. You will be responsible for all marketing, including product launches, web, and print campaigns. You will develop pricing strategies with the goal of maximizing the firm’s profits or share of the market while ensuring the firm’s customers are satisfied.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Prepare comprehensive marketing strategies that align with company goals</li>
<li>Supervise and manage the marketing team</li>
<li>Support sales team with up-to-date and aligned marketing materials</li>
<li>Plan, create and oversee the design, content, and production of all marketing materials</li>
<li>Create PowerPoint presentations and sales support</li>
<li>Plan promotional and awareness campaigns and support</li>
<li>Manage all online and print materials and campaigns</li>
<li>Manage all online avenues of production, including website, social media pages, email campaigns, and responses</li>
<li>Monitor, track, and document campaign results; analyze data/returns/customer response</li>
<li>Cultivate and integrate new ideas and campaigns that align with current and future sales goals</li>
</ul>

<li>Solid understanding of effective marketing strategies, materials, and channels</li>
<li>Experience preparing and presenting quarterly goals and forecasts for future projects</li>
<li>Strong analytical, financial, or pricing skills preferred</li>
