<b>About the Organization</b><br>
Founded in 1977, ZERO TO THREE works to ensure that babies and toddlers benefit from the early connections that are critical to their well-being and development. Our mission is to ensure that all babies and toddlers have a strong start in life. At ZERO TO THREE we envision a society that has the knowledge and will to support all infants and toddlers in reaching their full potential. Our Core Values Statement: We believe that <i>how</i> we do our work is as important as <i>what</i> we do. To learn more about ZERO TO THREE, please visit our website at zerotothree.org.<br>
<br>
<b>Summary</b><br>
ZERO TO THREE is seeking a Communications Insights Specialist to provide data expertise as we strive to improve growth, retention, engagement, and brand affinity. The ideal candidate will possess a passion for developing analytical insights to help optimize channel effectiveness and achieve marketing and sales goals related to membership, products, services, and events. The Marketing Insights Specialist will help address the complex challenges of audience research, product design, marketing effectiveness, engagement strategy, and return on investment and will help articulate how marketing contributes to our important mission.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b>
<ul>
<li>Develop analytical frameworks and metrics to measure ongoing business performance, understand key drivers, and improve campaign effectiveness</li>
<li>Develop reporting systems, including campaign performance dashboards, to report key metrics</li>
<li>Identify actionable insights and make recommendations to support strategic marketing decisions</li>
<li>Provide technical expertise in data mining, measuring business performance, and running research using mathematical, statistical, and experimental approaches</li>
<li>Help develop audience surveys and make strategic recommendations based on analysis of results</li>
<li>Assist with administration and development of Google Analytics and Google AdWords</li>
<li>Support targeting, testing, analysis, and reporting for digital marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Make recommendations on reporting software</li>
<li>Stay current on best practice and marketing analytics methodologies and trends</li>
<li>Be responsive to requests and proactive in suggesting improvements</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned for the purpose of ensuring the efficient and effective functioning of the department.</li>
</ul>
<br>

</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Resourcefulness, critical thinking, and initiative - must be able to identify a challenge, research options, and recommend a solution</li>
<li>Adept at writing reports and presenting findings in a cohesive and visually compelling manner</li>
<li>Technical expertise with data analytics, data manipulation, and data reporting</li>
<li>Experience with survey development, reporting, and analytics</li>
<li>Experience with sales and marketing data systems including CRM, CMS, web analytics, email marketing software, lead generation, and online ad platforms
</li>
<li>Self-motivated but understands how to operate and thrive in a collaborative atmosphere</li>

</ul>
