<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>JOB DESCRIPTION
</b></h2>
<p>We are looking for an Account Executive to join the team! As the Account Executive, Advanced TV you are responsible for developing and creating strategic sales proposals and collateral to increase revenue and sales for the FOX AIM (Audience Insight Manager) sales team. You report directly to the AIM Sales Vice President (FOX Corporation) and work hand-in-hand with the account management, research, revenue operations, and greater sales teams to craft strategic proposals and presentations while also helping to define and maintain the advanced TV, addressable and audience targeting sales strategy of Fox’s product offering. You are a self-starter that has a passion for knowing more and doing your best work, balanced with respect for the skills that others bring to the table!
</p>
