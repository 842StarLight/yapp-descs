<p><b>
Description of Job Duties and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p>Bring your strategic vision, excellent enterprise program governance and leadership skills to Ellucian and make a difference! At Ellucian, we deliver a broad portfolio of technology solutions that help higher education institutions navigate change, achieve greater transparency, and drive efficiencies. Right now, we are looking for a Delivery Director for our Central region within our North American Professional Services team. The Delivery Director is responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
The health of the region’s portfolio of customers during the Professional Services portion of their journey with Ellucian</li>
<li>
Profit and loss performance of the Central region, ensuring regional revenue and gross profit targets are met while also delivering accurate forecasts</li>
<li>
Cultivating a close partnership with regional sales leadership to drive pipeline, business expansion, and successful solution selling</li>
<li>
Driving timely delivery to contract – best practice, high quality, on-budget – achieving high-value outcomes for customers with high NPS and positioned for positive references</li>
<li>
Developing and maintaining key customer relationships in the region, delivering nominal levels of billable service (10%-20%)</li>
<li>
Maintaining a close partnership within the PMO/ Delivery Excellence team to ensure the full project management team are aligned to our common delivery methodologies, that base standards are known and followed and where growth opportunities within the teams are identified, that you partner with the PMO to drive this across the Professional Services Field Delivery</li>
<li>
Engaging and collaborating proactively and effectively with key sister organizations – other PS regions (NA and international), NA Consulting Practices, Global PS Operations, Digital Transformation, Delivery Excellence – to improve execution/offerings, refine methodologies/best practice, align strategies, and drive efficiencies</li>
<li>
Active participation and thought leadership in our global Delivery Excellence program, continuously improving PS delivery practices and the experience for our customers</li>
<li>
Ensuring effective collaboration and seamless transitions with other internal teams – creating a One Ellucian experience for our customers</li>
<li>
Regional leadership activities, including project oversight, monitoring and acting on regional delivery KPIs (e.g. backlog, utilization, project health), driving delivery excellence compliance, forecasting accuracy, escalation resolution</li>
<li>
People leadership responsibilities, including personnel management, mentoring/coaching, regional communications, addressing performance concerns, leadership development and employee engagement</li>
</ul>
