<h2>Market Development Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Accountable Results:</b><br>
The MDR's primary role will be to prospect new opportunities while partnering with account executives in identifying and targeting cold sales leads and converting them to warm leads. The MDR will assist account executives in preparing leads for conferences, WellSky-sponsored events and other sales and marketing efforts. This person will be responsible for uncovering the typical BANT (Budget, Authority, Needs, Timeline) criteria in regards to lead generation while also guiding potential clients and leads through the sales process.
<br><br>
<b>Key Activities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify and contact registered attendees of WellSky-sponsored events to schedule demos</li>
<li>Contact non-attendees to set up a private demo with an Account Executive</li>
<li>Assist Account Executives when needed to schedule demos for registered events' attendees</li>
<li>Assist when needed in calling registered attendants of conferences and industry events and gather more information about them as well as invite them to WellSky-sponsored events</li>
<li>Conduct research on pre-qualified and non-qualified web leads, incoming sales calls, and referral program leads</li>
<li>Provide software solution advice to pre-qualified and non-qualified leads and obtain more information about them</li>
<li>Work with account executives with any regional-specific outreach projects (i.e. letters, call campaigns, etc)</li>
<li>When applicable, up-sell additional products and services to customer base</li>
<li>Assist the sales coordinator when needed with administrative tasks</li>
</ul>
<b>Baseline Competencies:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in MS Office suite including Outlook, Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and communicate effectively</li>
<li>Ability to work autonomously and manage workload to complete weekly goals</li>
</ul>
<b>Job Competencies:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience working with Salesforce or other CRMs</li>
<li>1-3 years of previous lead generation, or general sales experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree preferred or equivalent years of experience</li>
</ul>
