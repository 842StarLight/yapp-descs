<h2>Sales Porter - Porsche</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Washes vehicle exterior, cleans interior and exterior windows, and wipes down doorjambs.
</li>
<li>Vacuums interior of vehicles to remove loose dirt and debris.
</li>
<li>Cleans engine and engine compartment with steam-cleaning equipment and various cleaning agents.
</li>
<li>Inspects vehicles for noticeable defects, such as dents, scratches, torn upholstery, and poor mechanical operation.
</li>
<li>Uses proper eye, hand, and body protection when using products that require protection.
</li>
<li>Fills vehicle with fuel.
</li>
<li>Applies dressing on tires and tire wells.
</li>
<li>Maintains showroom and stock vehicles in clean and presentable condition at all times.
</li>
<li>Keeps work area neat and clean.
</li>
<li>Operates all tools and equipment in a safe manner.
</li>
<li>Reports any safety issues immediately to management.
</li>
<li>Empties garbage into designated areas.
</li>
<li>Opens lot for business and closes lot when closing.
</li>
<li>Keeps service drive clear and clean at all times.
</li>
<li>Cleans customer lounge area and coffee area at all times.
</li>
<li>Assist Sales with Opening of Lot New and Used
</li>
<li>Fill Holes in New and Used Car Lines
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Ensure that no New/Used Cars are mixed. Builds boxes for each used car to hold its information/paperwork upon being stocked in.</p></li>
<li><p>
Makes sure all paperwork is up to date, printed, and placed in each individual used car box in conjunction with the Used Car Manager.</p></li>
<li><p>
Assists in placing used car paperwork in the windows for display, such as Buyers Guides, KBB Pricing sheets and other advertising material.</p></li>
<li><p>
Makes sure every new car has a laminated Maronie/MSRP Sticker placed in the front window at all times.</p></li>
<li><p>
Makes sure that the LoJack sticker in every car is not faded from the sun and replaces if it is.</p></li>
<li><p>
Reviews our cars to locate if any of our cars are not up to our standard cosmetically</p></li>
<li><p>
Align All Rows of vehicles</p></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Check to see if any vehicles need gas New/Used
</li>
<li>Make sure all New/Used Stickers are properly displayed
</li>
<li>Start and run all vehicles once per week, 5 Min.
</li>
<li>Check In any New Vehicles and Stock them in
</li>
<li>Clean any New Vehicles after PDI
</li>
<li>Arrange back Stock lot and keep organized
</li>
<li>Walk the lot and search for damaged vehicles
</li>
<li>Walk the lot and re wash any vehicles that need it
</li>
<li>Walk the lot and pick up any trash or garbage
</li>
<li>Sold Unit Details as needed
</li>
<li>Freshen up Showroom Vehicle Display
</li>
<li>Keep detail area organized and stocked
</li>
<li>Assist Sales in closing the lot
</li>
<li>Ensure all customer and lot vehicles are locked!
</li>
<li>New vehicle physical inventory
</li>
<li>Used vehicle physical inventory
</li>
<li>New vehicle photos
</li>
<li>Used vehicle photos
</li>
<li>Assist service porters when necessary
</li>
<li>Zurich Shield Application
</li>
<li>Full detailing of vehicles including buffing and paint touch up
</li>
<li>Other Duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>

</p>

<li><p>
Independently motivated with the ability to work within a team environment.</p></li>
<li><p>
Excellent customer service/satisfaction skills.</p></li>
<li><p>
Organized and computer proficient.</p></li>
<li><p><b>
GED or Equivalent High school Education</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p>Clean DMV.</p>
