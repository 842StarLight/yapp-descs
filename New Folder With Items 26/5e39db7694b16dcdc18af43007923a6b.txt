<p><b>Junior Sales Engineer</b></p>
<p><b>LumApps - The Social Intranet</b></p>
<p><b>New York City</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>About LumApps</b></p>
<p>LumApps is a category leader for B2B digital communication platforms. We have a unique positioning on the market: a social and collaborative intranet for forward-thinking companies that understand the power of communication to build a competitive advantage. Employees can access everything they need in one digital workplace: corporate news, documents, business apps and communities.</p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>Sales Engineers support our sales efforts which lead to technology justification and implementation of LumApps solutions. Our Sales Engineers act as trusted advisors within the sales cycle. Assisting in both pre- and post-sales, for advice and support.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>In-depth product knowledge and ability to determine solution fit</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Customer discovery, analysis, and solution design</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Customer site visits, operational information gathering, demonstrations, presentations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>RFP, RFI response in coordination with sales and other functions</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Sales Engineers must be active listeners and explorers - paying close attention to customer pain-points, challenges, and be able to provide feedback, defer judgment, respond appropriately, with a have a high level of self-awareness.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
