<h2>Studio Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>FULL TIME ONLY</p>
<p>Orangetheory Fitness is currently looking for fun, smart and passionate influencers to join our team and take us to the next level! If you are a passionate, positive and detail-oriented person that can talk to anyone, someone who enjoys teamwork and wants to be a part of a world class fitness company, someone who might just drop and do a burpee for fun, then you're probably the right person for us! We are looking for successful and motivated people who want to improve themselves and their career.</p>
<p>‪As an Orangetheory Studio Manager, you will be part of a dynamic team that positively affects the lives of our members on a daily basis! You will lead in the operation and success of a fast paced fitness studio and build relationships with OTF members and the community. The Studio Manager role manages all aspects of studio operations, with a strong focus on premium customer service as well as meeting sales expectations.</p>
<p>OTF Largo offers a staff development program that allows every staff member to grow and develop their own knowledge and skills, and also progressively increase their compensation based on achieving milestones and tasks. An ideal Studio Manager has strong customer service skills and sales experience that produce a successful Orangetheory family and studio. The Studio Manager is responsible for meeting and exceeding all key performance sales indicators for the studio, including membership sales and renewals, retail and concession sales, maximizing workout traffic, and maintaining premium customer service levels.</p>

<p>‪As a Studio Manager, you will:</p>
<p>* Establish and maintain an effective referral program</p>
<p>* Maintain accurate records using established OTF sales systems</p>
<p>* Conduct telephone inquiries/follow up calls/customer care calls</p>
<p>* Lead a comprehensive marketing program to generate leads and collect prospects</p>
<p>* Greet members/guests promptly, enthusiastically &amp; with a smile to create a friendly experience</p>
<p>* Manage daily studio operations and maintain an organized and clean studio always</p>
<p>* Participate in 1-2 OTF scheduled workouts per week</p>
<p>* Screen, interview and hire all studio positions, coaches with the assistance of the Head Coach</p>
<p>* Process accurate cash and credit card transactions</p>
<p>* Respond professionally and courteously to member requests and concerns</p>
<p>* Conduct proper membership documentation using OTF established forms/systems</p>
<p>* With the assistance of the Head Coach, ensure that all OTF workouts and fitness programs are delivered with excellence and IAW OTF standards</p>
<p>* Participate in all relative OTF training programs</p>

<p>* Excellent customer service skills</p>
<p>* Solid and strong sales skills (experience working in a sales quota bearing structure highly preferred)</p>
<p>* Solid verbal and written communication skills required</p>
<p>* Able to multi-task and excel in a busy environment</p>
<p>* Functional computer skills required – MS Office basic programs (word, excel), Internet, Apple/Mac and basic computer program software use</p>
<p>* Health &amp; Fitness minded people strongly preferred</p>
<p>* High school diploma required</p>
<p>* Flexible to work day, evening and/or weekend hours as needed</p>
<p>* Ability to market in local area (Largo/Seminole FL)</p>
