<h2>Contracts Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Requirements/Job Duties</b></p>
<div>
<p>The legal department has an opening for a Contracts Manager who will be responsible for the management, review and negotiation of commercial. The successful candidate must be very detail-oriented, self-motivated and deadline driven with prior in-house legal experience, preferably in the information technology industry.
</p>
<p>Responsibilities will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Review, redline and negotiate customer (third party) terms and conditions for sale of technology products and services for both commercial and/or government customers. These contracts may include Master Purchase Agreements, Reseller Agreements, Professional Services Agreements, Statements of Work, Subcontracts, NDAs, Teaming Agreements and other channel agreements.</li>
<li>Collaborate with other departments at WWT to engage Subject Matter Experts where needed.</li>
<li>Structure, draft and partner on contract negotiations both independently and in collaboration with the Associate General Counsel.</li>
<li>Act as primary liaison with WWT Associate General Counsel to facilitate legal review of non-standard requests from internal business teams or customers</li>
<li>Review Request for Proposal documents for legal risks and issues and guide the Proposals team to ensure legally-compliant and accurate proposals are submitted.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with internal departments to ensure proper execution of contracts and subcontracts.</li>
<li>Regularly completing and updating representations &amp; certifications, supplier information forms and insurance forms.</li>
<li>Prepare contract summaries for internal departments when needed to address any non-standard terms and conditions or approval is required.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<div><div>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree is required; J.D. or MBA a plus.</li>
<li>At least five (5) years' experience reviewing, redlining and negotiating Customer (third party) contracts or a broad range of standard commercial contracts.</li>
<li>A self-starter that can utilize their contract and risk knowledge as well as sound judgment to make decisions on complex or varied problems with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Superior writing, analytical and communication skills, including experience engaging with sales and senior management.</li>
<li>Ability to quickly analyze advanced and complex transactions and propose innovative, creative and workable solutions.</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure, address multiple competing priorities, and provide practical advice to business teams</li>
<li>Ability to develop strong relationships with internal business units as well as with business management.</li>
<li>Team player willing to support fellow coworkers with urgent tasks.</li>
<li>In-house legal department experience is strongly preferred.</li>
</ul>
