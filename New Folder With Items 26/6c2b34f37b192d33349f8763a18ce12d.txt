<h2>Software as a Service Coordinator</h2>

<div>EBSCO Information Services (EIS) provides a complete and optimized research solution comprised of e-journals, e-books, and research databases — all combined with the most powerful discovery service to support the information needs and maximize the research experience of our end-users. Headquartered in Ipswich, MA, EIS employs more than 3,300 people worldwide. We are the leader in our field due to our cutting-edge technology, forward-thinking philosophy, and top-notch workforce. EIS, a division of EBSCO Industries Inc., based in Birmingham, AL, is ranked in the top 200 of the nation’s largest, privately held corporations according to Forbes magazine. EBSCO is a company that will motivate you, inspire you, and allow you to grow. We are looking for the best. If you are too, we encourage you to explore our unique opportunities.</div>
<div>
Mission
<br>
The Software as a Service Coordinator reports to the Vice President of Global Software as a Service Sales. The Software as a Service Coordinator is responsible for administering, tracking, and coordinating the support activity for EBSCO's suite of SaaS Products (EBSCO Discovery Service, Full Text Finder, FOLIO, Stacks, Faculty Select, OpenAthens, Perpetua and all other affiliated products under the SaaS Suite).</div>
<div>Primary Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li>Handle all pricing requests and coordination with sales teams for OpenAthens, Stacks, Perpetua as well as future SaaS offerings
</li>
<li>Weekly summaries of all OpenAthens, Stacks and Perpetua quotes</li>
<li>Processing of orders for all ILS partners, EBSCO Discovery Service partner databases, EBSCO Discovery Service Custom Catalogs, Institutional Repositories as well as EBSCO Discovery Service and Full Text Finder trials</li>
<li>Creation and maintenance of all FOLIO Sandbox accounts and EDS Demo accounts</li>
<li>Point of contact for SaaS sales for Order Processing, Implementation Project Managers, Professional Services, Licensed Secondary Databases and Customer Support teams</li>
<li>Coordinate with Implementation team on pertinent trial information such as special requests and due dates
</li>
<li>Manage, track and completes all tasks coming through the EDS trial request email group</li>
<li>Complete various SaaS data projects as needed</li>
<li>Complete new projects focused around driving growth of SaaS suite</li>
<li>Coordination with SaaS VP on all administrative tasks
</li>
</ul>
