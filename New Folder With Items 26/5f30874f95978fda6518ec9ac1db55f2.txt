<h2>Technical Support Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Role
<br><br>
We are looking for a Technical Support Representative who will provide excellent technical assistance, and help reassure users through presenting thoughtful solutions and communicating that you care about their experience with Airtame.
<br><br>
You will join our international Customer Success Team, where we all strive to help our customers throughout the onboarding, activation, and retention phases to make their journey enjoyable.
<br><br>
Tasks
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Help our customers, big or small, with fully deploying Airtame into their organizations. That involves everything from troubleshooting with Airtame itself, to solving contextual issues related to a user's computer, access points, or the existing network setup within a particular setting.</li>
<li>Serve as a technical expert when covering correspondence via live chat, email, phone calls, and occasional customer visits.</li>
<li>Work alongside our sales representatives to help customers complete full rollouts of Airtame and, occasionally, help train resellers.</li>
<li>Channel feedback to the rest of the company, especially when collaborating with our engineering team to troubleshoot and define bugs as well as track their impact and fixes.</li>
<li>Contribute to the ongoing internal learning and success of our team by sharing knowledge through mentorship and collaboration.</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years of experience working in a position providing technical support through clear written and verbal communication.</li>
<li>Self starter, comfortable working in a fast-paced, growing environment</li>
<li>Service-minded and attentive to giving customers the best possible support and experience with our products.</li>
<li>Tech-savvy and excited about the future of technology trends.</li>
<li>Highly-motivated team player with a drive for getting things done and seeing unforeseen challenges as exciting ways for improvement.</li>
<li>Willing to research and learn more complex skills related to networking, sys-admin, and log file debugging.</li>
<li>Excellent English communication skills.</li>
</ul>
Bonus points
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Understanding the principles of wireless streaming technology.</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with corporate network administration, multicast &amp; VLANs management.</li>
<li>Experience with content writing for creating self-help articles and tutorials for our customers.</li>
<li>Experience providing customer service to a wide range of customers, from small businesses to big enterprises and educational institutions, with different levels of computer knowledge.</li>
</ul>
Benefits
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>You will join an ambitious company that values transparent work-life balance.</li>
<li>You will be in a professional setting where you can see your ideas put into action right away.</li>
<li>Competitive salary.</li>
<li>Flexible work environment.</li>
<li>3 weeks vacation in the first year and 4 weeks thereafter.</li>
<li>Daily lunch, drinks, snacks, and great coffee.</li>
<li>Health insurance.</li>
<li>Close collaboration with our European team, including visits to our office in the heart of Copenhagen.</li>
</ul>

<li>Let us know what drives your customer service orientation.</li>
</ul>
