<h2>Restoration Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Specific Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish and maintain a service schedule to ensure all service is delivered to meet customer requirements</li>
<li>Supervise restoration employees including training, and performance management</li>
<li>Communicate with customers throughout restoration process</li>
<li>Inspect the job sites frequently to verify the scope of work is being performed correctly, that it meets the projected timeline and that it complies with Rainbow International standards</li>
<li>Work with management to develop a business plan to generate prospective customers to meet the projected sales goals</li>
<li>Inspect, estimate, and manage multiple repair projects after incidents involving water, fire, or other types of damage</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED); five or more years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>Valid Driver's License with acceptable driving record</li>
<li>Ability to pass pre-employment background check</li>
<li>Estimating Experience - Xactimate required</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Computer literate, with working knowledge of work processing, business software and spreadsheet applications</li>
<li>Professional appearance and personality</li>
<li>Team player who can work independently</li>
<li>Must have or be willing to obtain the applicable certifications pertaining to the industry.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits: PTO, Medical, Dental and Vision.</b></p>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL DEMANDS</b><br>
<br>
While performing the duties of this job, the employee is frequently exposed to fumes or airborne particles. The employee is occasionally exposed to adverse weather conditions and toxic or caustic chemicals. May also involve confined spaces that one must crawl through. The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 50 pounds.</p>
