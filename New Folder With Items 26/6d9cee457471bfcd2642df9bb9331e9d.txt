<h2>SYSTEMS ARCHITECT</h2>

<p>Grass Valley is a global leader in hardware and software solutions that make television happen. We’re focused on empowering customers to create, control and connect content wherever, however and whenever it is consumed. We provide the innovative tools – cameras, instant replay, signal management and more – to help customers improve the efficiency of their operations while telling better stories to attract and keep viewers. With Grass Valley's integrated solutions that enable flexibility, efficiency, cost-effectiveness, quality and scalability, broadcasters and content creators have what they need to sustain their businesses.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Grass Valley is just one of the many brands that make up Belden’s impressive portfolio. Belden is a multi-billion dollar global organization of 10,000 talented people that supports the mission-critical network infrastructure and audio visual broadcast needs of many of the world’s best-known brands, placing us at the heart of the global transformation to a truly connected world. We are seeking driven, results-oriented team members to help us continue to grow and succeed.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>Provide systems architecture consultancy services and technical engineering support for the design and customization of digital broadcast systems. Assist the sales process by providing pre-sales technical support, including product demonstrations and system solution proposal generation for proprietary company products, and review and respond to RFIs and RFPs. Assess the customers' product and technical requirements. Generate and assess customer proposals. Assist with product design changes and customizations and review finalized product specifications and product design features with customers. Assist with solution delivery and troubleshooting application issues and provide training and post-sales technical support and account management. Generate high quality tender responses and proposals in conjunction with the sales organization. Produce supporting technical and contractual documentation for projects. Configure and deliver product demonstrations and customer proof-of-concept systems during the system architecture process. Assist with the provision of technical content for exhibitions, and serve as a close liaison with all other departments within the company. Assist with post-sales functions within Customer Service, and contribute toward the management and generation of departmental processes.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or its foreign equivalent in Media Technology, Broadcast Engineering, Computer Engineering or a related field.</li>
<li>Two (2) years of experience in Playout Automation and Integrated Playout solutions.</li>
<li>Other experience/skills must include:</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Morpheus Automation and ICE Integrated Playout implementation;</li>
<li>Global broadcast standards and technologies;</li>
<li>Appropriate playout standards as defined by the Society of Motion Picture and Television Engineers (SMPTE); and</li>
<li>Compressed IP playout methodologies.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>50% travel required to client sites.</li>
<li>Telecommuting is available.</li>
<li>Must have current authorization to be employed in the U.S. without employer sponsorship.</li>
<br>
</ul>
