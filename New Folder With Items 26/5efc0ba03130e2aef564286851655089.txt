<h2>Application Support Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>At United Shore the Application Support Analyst on our Application Support team helps in all stages of application development and support. In this position, you will get to work with other technical teams and every area of the business determining best course of action solutions to application level issues. Developing long-term meaningful relationships is one way in assisting in your work efforts, and it is easy here since we all work in an open environment on the same campus; just swing by someone’s desk or grab a coffee at our Starbucks.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Investigate and diagnose incidents to restore a failed IT service as quickly as possible</li>
<li>
Develop relationships with team members in the business and on other technical teams</li>
<li>
Create knowledge articles and business documentation for troubleshooting</li>
<li>
Respond to customer support requests and assist fellow teammates when needed</li>
<li>
Suggest new processes that will help streamline development</li>
<li>
Balance multiple projects and incidents at the same time in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Communicate complex technical issues and solutions in a clear, simple and understandable way</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT WE NEED FROM YOU
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Computer-related degree or equivalent experience
</li>
<li>2+ years of experience in incident management, requirements gathering and creating documentation throughout all stages of application development</li>
<li>
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Experience in effectively communicating with all levels of internal leadership</li>
<li>
Ability to work collaboratively on a team in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>
On-site attendance</li>
<li><b>
Able to work rotating shift between 8AM-8PM (e.g. 8-5 or 11-8)</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Financial Services or Mortgage industry knowledge and/or experience</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience working in an agile software development process</li>
<li>
SQL knowledge - understand how to look up data / create joins</li>
<li>
Project management experience</li>
<li>
BS or BA degree in Business, Communications or IT</li>
</ul>
