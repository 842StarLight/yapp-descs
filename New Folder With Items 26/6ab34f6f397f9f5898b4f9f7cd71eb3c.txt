<p>SERVICE TECH:

<p><b>Desert Downhole </b>has an immediate opening for a<b> Downhole Service Technician’s</b> in<b> Northern Colorado</b>. If you are interested in joining a growing company with a proven track record of success, this is your opportunity.</p>
<p><b>
Please submit Resumes to:</b> sgullette@desertdownhole.com</p>
<p>
 <b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Set-up, Install and troubleshoot various down-hole production equipment</li>
<li>Complete installation schematics</li>
<li>Abide by Company Safety Policies and Procedures</li>
<li>Field/City sales efforts in region to expand business</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Packer or Downhole Tool experience required</li>
<li>Experience in oil industry</li>
<li>Previous experience with packers, ESP’s, Gaslift and/or Plunger lift highly desirable</li>
<li>Ability to travel to various locations</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office software</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have a valid license</li>
<li>Ability to be on call 24/7</li>
<li>Must maintain a safe driving record as prescribed by Company policy</li>
<li>Must successfully pass a pre-employment drug and alcohol tests per company policy</li>
<li>Must be able to read, write and speak in English</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to move materials up to 100 pounds</li>
<li>Requires pushing and pulling up to 150 pounds of force, bending, standing, kneeling, carrying, sitting, climbing for extended periods of time</li>
<li>Requires use of PPE including but not limited to: safety glasses, steel toe boots, gloves and ear plugs</li>
<li>Requires exposure to moderate noise levels.</li>
<li>Exposure to warm, hot and cold temperatures</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PAY AND BENEFITS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Salary and bonus commensurate with experience</li>
<li>Truck, phone and computer provided or funded</li>
<li>Major medical, dental, AD&amp;D and life insurance provided</li>
<li>Company percentage match in 401k plan</li>
</ul>
