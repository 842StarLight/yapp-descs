<div>The Account Manager is responsible for developing long-term relationships, and managing and developing client accounts to initiate and maintain favorable relationships with clients and dealers. They will liaise between customers and internal teams to ensure the timely and successful delivery of products according to customer and account guidelines, and assume responsibility for the oversite of the project management of jobs through to completion.</div>

<li>Building relationships with clients based on trust and respect.</li>

<li>Identifying industry trends to help serve customer needs.</li>
<li>Resolving complaints and preventing additional issues by improving processes</li>
<li>Proving the highest level of customer service to dealers and customers.</li>
<li>Managing projects from beginning to post install.</li>
<li>Performs other job related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
