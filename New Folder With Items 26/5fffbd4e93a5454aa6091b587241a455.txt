<h2>The UPS Store Part-time or Fulltime Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>We offer wide variety of Printing and Shipping Solutions to local businesses. We are looking for a dynamic person for the role of Customer Service. Previous work experience at The UPS Store, Staples or FedEx is an asset but not required.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b>Excellent customer service skills / Retail Sales experience, good computer skills (Word, excel, Power point and Adobe Acrobat)</p>
<p>Knowledge of graphic designing will be an asset (Adobe In-design, Photoshop, Illustrator)</p>
<p>Retail customer service experience is a MUST.</p>
<p><b>Customer Service involves</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Smile and greet customers and determine how to exceed their expectations</li>
<li>Receive and process packages for carriers including lifting and weighing of packages on scale, accessing appropriate software or manifesting manually, and correctly labeling packages</li>
<li>Take ownership of the customer's shipping needs and offers viable solutions.</li>
<li>Operate copiers, fax machines, binding equipment and all other office machines in a safe, efficient and productive manner</li>
<li>Record all sales transactions on POS system (on Cash Register) and accurately process Cash, Cheque and Debit/Credit Card transactions</li>
<li>Close out cash at end of day including: reconciliation of cash, cheques, credit/debit cards, and preparation of daily bank deposits</li>
<li>Maintains a clean and safe working environment.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
<li>Ability to lift approximate 25-50 pounds.</li>
<li>All necessary training will be provided</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Uniforms are provided</li>
<li>Free parking</li>
<li>Bonus opportunity</li>
<li>Incentive based programs.</li>
<li>Formal on-job free training program</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Type: </b> Full-time OR Part-time</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
