<h2>National Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Securitec Screening Solutions is an industry leading background research intelligence firm that provides court data technology and research services to the background screening industry that drive accuracy, efficiency and cost savings. This includes the use of our Synaptec™ court data platform, large research, quality assurance and client services staff, an internal software division, a nationwide network of on-site court runners, and a large operations center in Roanoke Virginia. Due to our explosive growth, we currently have an opening for an experienced National Account Executive from the background screening industry.</p>
<p><b>NATIONAL ACCOUNT EXECUTIVE</b></p>
<p>Position Overview</p>
<p>The National Account Executive will report directly to the Vice President of Sales and Business Development and will lead efforts to identify, contact, qualify, and close new accounts and channel partnerships that generate revenue and create win-win business relationships for the client and the organization. In addition, the design and execution of target account-based marketing campaigns and aggressive prospecting efforts will be integral. Constant, regular attendance, participation &amp; presentation of material regarding services information at potential client meetings, industry conferences and at trade shows. In addition, the delivery of marketing/service presentations at corporate headquarters, client locations or on-line will be normal practice. Assistance in the preparation and calculation of service and pricing proposals. Continual interface interaction with executive management and the operations team to identify data service solutions. Navigational efforts between clients and Securitec finance and legal departments in negotiation of contract details. Utilization of salesforce software platform to manage contacts, chart opportunities, detail activities, and document pipeline information.</p>
<p>Required Skills &amp; Experience</p>
<p>· 5+ years of experience in the Background Screening Industry</p>
<p>· 5+ years in a direct or channel sales role managing a territory/territories</p>
<p>· 2+ years selling data services to Consumer Reporting Agencies (CRA’s) or other professional background screening firms</p>
<p>· Consistent achievement of defined sales targets, quotas &amp; objectives</p>
<p>· Bachelor or Advanced Degree from an accredited collegiate institution</p>
<p>· Outstanding communication and organizational skills with a strong attention to detail</p>
<p>· Proven research, analytical, problem solving and issue resolution skills.</p>
<p>· Finance knowledge and capability required for pricing model formulations</p>
<p>· Expert user of SalesForce.com and other background screening data platforms for documentation of orders and results</p>
<p>· The ability to manage multiple potential clients and prioritization of workload</p>
<p>· Professional business-oriented communication, interpersonal and presentation skills</p>
<p>· Strong human relations and teambuilding skills</p>
<p>· Proficient with MS Office tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).</p>
<p>Expectations &amp; Deliverables</p>
<p>· Remote position from the selected applicant’s home office anywhere within the country.</p>
<p>· Attractive compensation structure consisting of a competitive base salary plus bonus opportunities.</p>
<p>· Possible opportunity for advancement within the organization.</p>
<p>· Generous Benefit package available including health, dental, vision, group life, short term disability, ancillary benefits and a company matched 401K plan.</p>
