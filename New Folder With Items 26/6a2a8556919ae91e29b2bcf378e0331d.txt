<h2>Partnership Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div><div>
<p>Dealers United Performance Managers ensure the retention, growth, and success of Dealers United’s Agency-Partner accounts.</p>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Onboard new end-user accounts to Dealers United Partnership solutions</li>
<li>Communicate end-user results and successes to our reseller partners on a regular basis</li>
<li>Understand each client’s specific goals and Key Performance Indicators</li>
<li>Create B2C Facebook Ad campaigns (from planning, to campaign objective and budgeting, to ad formats) for partnership clients and ensure internal and delegated tasks are being delivered on-time and as expected by Partner</li>
<li>Report to Head of Partnership and VP of Sales</li>
<li>Establish strong, long-term client relationships and increase growth within the Partner’s portfolio</li>
<li>Suggest solutions and innovative ideas to meet client needs</li>
<li>Establish and measure key metrics</li>
<li>Prepare monthly sales and performance reports</li>
<li>Handle complaints and problems in a timely and effective manner</li>
<li>Act as the liaison between key customers and internal teams and work closely with Partnership sales team and our Partners</li>
<li>Test ads to determine highest performing creative, markets, and ad copy</li>
<li>Provide conversion-focused ad creative direction and optimize ad copy based on data</li>
<li>Ensure proper tracking, attribution, and consistency across client campaigns</li>
<li>Provide thought leadership, strategic insight, and clear communication (written and verbal) to exceed KPIs and hit deadlines</li>
<li>Analyze data to maintain and forecast monthly budget and ROI figures, and to provide key insights and recommendations on campaign performance</li>
<li>Stay current with best practices, strategies, legislation and industry standards related to Facebook Advertising</li>
<li>Establish a great working relationship with the Support/Development team in order to stay up to date on the status of the accounts in onboarding</li>
<li>Understanding the basic knowledge of the technology and set up behind each product</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>IDEAL CANDIDATE KNOWLEDGE SKILLS + ABILITIES</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work remotely, with an outstanding work ethic and excellent communication, task management and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Willingness to problem-solve, optimize processes, and make decisions on your own</li>
<li>Understanding of digital solutions and strategies, such as Google Analytics, Google AdWords, SEO, PPC, Facebook Advertising, or Tier III automotive metrics</li>
<li>Ability to be cross-departmentally trained</li>
<li>Ability to understand, produce, and communicate reports and metrics</li>
<li>An ability to address customer requests and issues in a timely manner</li>
<li>2+ Years Experience with Facebook Business Manager and ads
</li>
<li>Degree in Digital Marketing, Account Management or comparable major</li>
<li>Photoshop experience a plus</li>
<li>Goal-driven and self-motivated team with a passion for learning</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
