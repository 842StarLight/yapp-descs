<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b>:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Community Marketing Representative plans, co-ordinates &amp; executes all marketing efforts for FastMed Urgent Care and itsâ€™ clinics in the assigned region. These marketing efforts include community relations, events, collateral, advertising, business to business marketing, partnerships, sponsorships, market &amp; competitor research and additional activities as deemed necessary to promote the clinic.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Along with planning marketing tactics to drive clinic visit volume, the position encompasses a wide variety of tasks and involves dealing with both external clients as well as internal cross-departmental team members. A positive outgoing personality is important and networking is key to building strong working relationships that result in driving patient volume to FastMed Urgent Care clinics. Excellent oral and written communication skills are required to produce a productive, positive and efficient department and environment. The role requires diplomacy, persuasiveness, and interpersonal skills.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will be able to hit the ground running in a fast-paced, ever-changing environment and can quickly adapt to changes. Some weekend and late hours may be required, especially for events.</p>
<br>
 <p></p>
<p><b>Primary responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive new patient visits to clinics in the region through aggressive grassroots and business to business marketing tactics, by traveling to clinics and covering markets around the clinics</li>
<li>Develop &amp; maintain the community marketing plan &amp; calendar for the assigned region and execute tactics as planned</li>
<li>Work with the Regional Marketing Lead to ensure tactics are planned within budget and for maximum effectiveness to drive FastMed brand awareness and patient visits to our clinics in the region.</li>
<li>Conduct outreach to and maintain marketing database of both existing and potential relationships with community organizations like schools, businesses, non-profits and others</li>
<li>Report on effectiveness of marketing campaigns, programs, and activities and initiatives</li>
<li>Distribute and maintain product collateral and inventory to the communities served; work with Operations team members to maintain the same for clinics in the region</li>
<li>Coordinate events including meetings, speaking engagements, seminars, trade shows and exhibits</li>
<li>Other related and necessary tasks and projects related to accomplishing these responsibilities</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>

<li>Ability to work on own initiatives as well as be part of a team</li>
<li>A quick learner, self-starter and ability to prioritize an aggressive workload</li>

<br>
</ul>
