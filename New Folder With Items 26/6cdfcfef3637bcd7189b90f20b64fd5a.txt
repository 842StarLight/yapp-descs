<h2>Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Consultant manages and executes successful Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) deployments and fosters strong client relationships. The consultant provides high-level implementation, training, and professional services to a portfolio of assigned clients (including on-site visits as needed) to manage and utilize the business operation functions in Yardi software products.
</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Performs complex system and business process reviews and other service reviews (on-site as needed) to identify inefficient processes and makes sound recommendations for client productivity gains</li>
<li>Trains clients and manages continued software integrations, maintenance, and updates in collaboration with clients</li>
<li>Researches, analyzes, and resolves software functionality and reporting problems for clients</li>
<li>Uses positive influence with clients to effectively navigate sensitive client objections, communicate complex and/or sensitive messages, preemptively address potential client concerns, build sustaining alliances with clients, and influence positive outcomes through active counsel</li>
<li>Enlists support from management and peers to address complex client requests and resolve complex issues</li>
<li>Analyzes and interprets client operational needs and determines initial system configuration accordingly</li>
<li>Performs or assists with data mapping and conversion, user training, and other services to ensure successful software deployment</li>
<li>Determines and documents solutions and best practices to contribute to a solutions repository</li>
<li>Advises and institutes best practices relevant to clients in the application of Yardi products</li>
<li>Recognizes and refers opportunities to sell products/services to existing clients</li>
<li>Provides recommendations and informed direction to clients regarding ancillary products and services that align with client business and operational needs and goals</li>
<li>Collaborates internally with analysts, designers, programmers, sales teams, and marketing teams with a focus on meeting client needs and maintaining high-level customer service standards</li>
<li>Performs special projects related to department training and client support as well as other duties as assigned under supervision of Manager</li>
</ul>
<p><b>**This position requires up to 80% travel**</b></p>
<p>
Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Finance, Math, or Computer/Engineering Sciences or other equivalent relevant experience in the property management industry</li>
<li>Five plus (5+) years of experience in software or business process consulting or equivalent consulting experience interacting with customers</li>
<li>Three (3) years of experience managing projects on accounting, business process, property management, or real estate software</li>
<li>Experience in ERP systems</li>
<li>Experience positively influencing clients, peers and management</li>
<li>Experience independently managing and resolving a variety client issues</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage projects with multiple touch points</li>
<li>Proven excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Proven client-centered commitment and focus</li>
<li>Proven ability to meet critical deadlines and prioritize multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Collegial, team-oriented disposition with the desire and ability to establish cooperative working relationships with employees at all levels within Yardi, outside of Yardi, and with clients</li>
<li>Ability to think and act strategically by visualizing new possibilities, challenging assumptions, and recommending the implementation of new ideas or strategies desired</li>
<li>Yardi domain experience a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge of property management industry and Yardi’s competitors a plus</li>
<li>Regular attendance and a regular work schedule are essential functions of this job</li>
</ul>
