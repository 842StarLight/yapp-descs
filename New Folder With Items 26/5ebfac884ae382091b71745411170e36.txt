<h2>Distribution Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Distribution Administration Representative is responsible for executing the defined day to day activities put in place to ensure the overall good health of the Life and Annuity Quality of Business (quality business from quality producers). Primary activities include contracting new producers, updating producer contracts, state producer appointments and performing the ongoing evaluation of producer behaviors and sales practices with the intent to minimize sales risk and maintain efficient operating costs.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Process licensing applications for producers seeking to sell Nassau Re products</li>
<li>Complete tasks related to producer contracting (adds, changes, terminations), state appointments, routine compensation/payroll inquires, etc.</li>
<li>Input information into the system to reflect contract upgrades, downgrades, terminations, producer appointment additions, renewals and termination</li>
<li>Assist in the day to day activities as defined within the Quality of Business Program</li>
<li>Maintain the integrity of the licensing, contracting, compensation and quality of business systems to ensure conformity with Company policies and procedures and compliance with regulatory agencies</li>
<li>Analyze and process suitability supervision control discoveries to ensure they are completed and acted upon in a timely and effective manner</li>
<li>Diagnose, pinpoint and report out on relevant producer quality-related items</li>
<li>Performs miscellaneous duties as required by management.</li>
<li>Must maintain appropriate attendance.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills, Knowledge and/or Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated ability to effectively and successfully manage productivity and quality metrics</li>
<li>Basic product knowledge (Fixed Annuities)</li>
<li>Basic product knowledge (Life)</li>
<li>General knowledge of industry indexed, fixed and variable annuities</li>
<li>General knowledge of industry Life products, particularly in the middle market space</li>
<li>Ability to execute effectively and timely on project deliverables, and successfully operationalize new processes and concepts</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of multiple administration and workflow systems required (e.g., Suitability Smartform, AWD, P+)</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of PC software required including office applications, ability to develop spreadsheet solutions that can reduce risk and cost of corrections</li>
<li>Strong communication skills in order to ask clarifying questions and exchange information with internal and external parties</li>
<li>Expert research, analytical, and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Ability to trace problems to their source and see resolution through to completion</li>
<li>Understand relationships among transactions in order to anticipate, identify, and resolve problems</li>
<li>Organizational skills in order to handle and follow through on multiple tasks and multiple deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to work in an environment that includes multiple deadlines, exception processing, and escalations</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work collaboratively with and give advice and direction to others, including those who are not direct reports</li>
