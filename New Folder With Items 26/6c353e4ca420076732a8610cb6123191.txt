<h2>Consumer Lending Loan Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>GENERAL JOB DESCRIPTION:</b><br>
A Loan Officer is expected to be a friendly, helpful, customer service and sales oriented representative of the credit union who assists phone, walk-in, and internet customers with their Members Choice accounts and loans. The main focus of a loan officer is member service, with a primary focus on loan applications, loan closings, and product sales.</p>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<b>DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Qualify members for new loans via cross-selling, phone, walk-in, and internet</li>
<li>Promote and sell account and loan products including but not limited to: checking accounts, credit cards, personal loans, auto loans, and the products associated with those loans; MBI/VSC, GAP, Multishield/Tire &amp; Wheel and payment protection;</li>
<li>Provide financial direction and counseling to our members regarding all MCCU consumer loans. Make referrals to the mortgage and investment services departments when appropriate;</li>
<li>Process and handle accurate and timely paperwork associated with loans. May occasionally include assisting branch employees in qualifying and opening new accounts, or other account maintenance functions;</li>
<li>Manage time independently, be able to follow up promptly on all member requests ;</li>
<li>Utilize the software and hardware necessary to conduct business in the credit union. This includes but is not limited to: multiple line telephones, fax machines, voicemail, Outlook based mail and calendars, e-fax, Windows, Microsoft Office—Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, Symitar, and the internet;</li>
<li>Work all hours as assigned during the normal CU business hours of operation, and additionally attend meetings and volunteer opportunities that may be scheduled outside business hours. Some degree of flexibility regarding scheduling and working in other locations may be required;</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL JOB REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong knowledge of consumer lending, products and services and sales techniques.</li>
<li>At least one year’s experience with consumer lending, selling products and services or other relevant job experience.</li>
<li>Strong organizational and planning skills</li>
<li>Work some evenings and weekends</li>
<li>Manage multiple tasks simultaneously in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Provide friendly, prompt, professional and accurate service and support to all members and associates</li>
<li>Maintain a dependable record of attendance, punctuality and timeliness</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 hours of MCCU approved volunteer hours required per calendar year</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<b>SKILLS/ABILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to provide friendly, helpful, knowledgeable consumer lending solutions</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Proficient computer skills.</li>
<li>Professional appearance.</li>
<li>Bilingual skills a plus, but not required</li>
</ul>
</div>
