<h2>Digital Account Executive - Media Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Job Title: Digital Account Executive
</b></div>
<div><b>Reports To: Local Sales Manager</b></div>
<div><b>FLSA Status: Salary Exempt
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></div>
<div> </div>
<div>Are you passionate about Digital Marketing? The ideal candidate for this position will aspire to be a strategic partner with their clients. We are looking for people to join our growing digital sales team to help focus on customer success. You will be responsible for our client’s digital strategy, tactics, and performance, with a focus on a return on their advertising investment. You will also be responsible for building new relationships with businesses and advertising agencies. Time management and the ability to work independently are very important. Strong leadership, and communication skills are a must.</div>
<div>An understanding of multi-screen media consumption as well as strategic thinking and analytical skills are required.</div>
<div> </div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS OF POSITION INCLUDE, BUT ARE NOT LIMITED TO THE FOLLOWING*</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div><b>Manage the full lifecycle of the client relationship, from prospecting, cold calling, proposal, contract, implementation, optimization, reporting and renewal.</b></div>
</li>
<li><div><b>Prospecting intelligently for new business and continuously adding new business to your funnel
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Effectively manage a sales funnel using CRM; achieving appointment setting and presentation expectations
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Create and propose advertising recommendations to meet client marketing objectives
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Prepare various internal and client facing reports as required
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Complete all sales related paperwork for client onboarding and order entry
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Coordinate creative direction for clients using internal and external resources
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Attend and completes all aspects of sales training programs
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Adhere to collection procedures including processing of client contracts and confirmations
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Maintain product knowledge through continuous learning and research.
</b></div></li>
<li>
<div>
<b>Consistently achieve revenue targets on a monthly / quarterly basis</b>.</div>
<br>
 </li>
</ul>
