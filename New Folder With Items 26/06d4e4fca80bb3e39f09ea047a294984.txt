<h2>Growth Marketer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Product Growth Marketer</b><br>
</p>
<p><b>Reports to: Director of Marketing</b><br>
</p>
<p><b>Based at: San Francisco, CA Office (SOMA)</b></p>
<p>
LimeLight is a full-stack, enterprise-grade ecommerce platform used by over 10,000 merchants (customers), processing over 150 million transactions, valued at $4 billion in GMV. We combine advanced technology with ecommerce expertise and a desire to solve online seller problems to help entrepreneurs build and scale profitable companies.<br>
</p>
<p>The role of Growth Marketer, reporting to the Director of Marketing, will be responsible for ideating and executing marketing campaigns that drive growth. Helping to map and define LimeLight’s customer lifecycle by launching and optimizing growth marketing campaigns, from concept to completion.<br>
</p>
<p>In particular for this person will be comfortable with gathering insights to make data-informed decisions. Continually launching, AB testing and optimizing growth campaigns is what gets you excited!</p>
<p><b>
What you’ll do here:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be an ecommerce expert on our customers, the market, martech, and our competition across regions where LimeLight provides its platform.</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams (including c-level, marketing, product, sales and client success) to drive company growth.</li>
<li>Help build our MQL funnel by driving leads, prospects and wins and improving conversion through testing.</li>
<li>Take a data-driven approach to identifying the biggest growth opportunities.</li>
<li>Partner with our external performance marketing agencies to incorporate paid channels, SEO and content into growth campaigns.</li>
<li>Lead the development of landing pages and continually AB test for growth.</li>
<li>Test, measure, analyze and optimize everything, as well as define and track metrics of success across all digital channels to drive MQLs.</li>
<li>Manage all tracking for growth marketing surfaces to ensure we’re capturing and leveraging the right data.</li>
<li>Understanding ecosystems, user behaviors, and long-term trends to leverage for new growth marketing campaigns and disrupting key marketplace competitors.</li>
<li>Constantly on the look-out to identify new levers to improve key business growth KPIs.</li>
<li>Ability to go beyond the numbers, to share and teach others in the organization through the use of reporting.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Experience, Knowledge, and Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A degree isn’t required, relevant startup experience and demonstrated past performance / results will be considered alongside formal education.</li>
<li>3-5 years combined work experience with B2B customer segments, in marketing tech, SaaS, cloud or mobile technology, and/or relevant ecommerce industries.</li>
<li>3-5 years experience scaling, and growing previous software platforms through the use of growth marketing (nice to have: ecommerce).</li>
<li>3+ years experience working in digital marketing, using platforms such as Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager (Search Console), Google Ads, Social Advertising i.e. Facebook Ads and Salesforce (<i>or like CRM platforms</i>).</li>
<li>2+ years working in high-growth, startup environments.</li>
<li>Strong data skills, and expert knowledge of Excel and Google Analytics (Nice to have: SQL)</li>
<li>You thrive in a fast paced environment, proactive and creative problem-solver who likes to “think outside the box”.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to organize and prioritize work to drive results on-time and with high quality.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, ability to organize/prioritize resources and produce insightful reporting for management.<br>
</li>
</ul>

<li>$110K-$120K + bonus, based on skills and experience</li>
<li>Conference, learning and development budget approved as required<br>
</li>
</ul>

</ul>

Our passion is helping our customers realize exponential growth. To achieve this, LimeLight is expecting to grow 100% year over year and are rapidly expanding our team.</p>
