<h2>Store Receiving Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>General Purpose:</b> Responsible for receiving, inspecting, and ticketing of merchandise, general stocking, and shipping functions related to overall store operations. Provides support for both store and buying staff.<br>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Education/Experience Requirements: </b>High School Diploma required.</h1>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">Requires minimum of 2 yrs minimum of retail/customer service, inventory, stocking and/or shipping related work history
</h1>
<br>
<b>License or Certification Requirements: </b>none<br>
<br>
<b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b><br>
<b>Shipping:
</b><ul>
<li>Prepare store sales, online web orders, special orders, and returns to vendor for shipping.</li>
<li>Process for shipping using online support or by completing appropriate forms for designated carrier – UPS, FedEx, or USPS</li>
<li>Complete processing of web orders and shipping follow up with online customer.
</li>
<li>Maintain complete and accurate records of shipments for future reference.</li>
<li>Process damage claims for shipped packages. Provide follow up for customer and buyer or accounting.
</li>
<li>Assist in keeping track of on-hand inventory of shipping supplies.
</li>
</ul>
<b>Receiving/Stocking:
</b><ul>
<li>Receive daily deliveries. Identify and segregate merchandise by department or individual at museum.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Reconcile shipments to original purchase orders. Inspect merchandise for quality and report damages. Verify quantities and identify discrepancies in shipment. Ticket or label merchandise appropriately. Complete all related receiving paperwork.</li>
<li>Designate appropriate location for stock or back stock. Deliver stock to store as needed.</li>
<li>Follows thru on customer special orders and merchandise repairs.
</li>
<li>Maintain receiving area in neat, clean and orderly manner. Organize new receipts and back stock. Straighten props, display fixtures, and packaging supplies for efficient retrieval. Dispose of cartons, packing materials and refuse on daily basis.</li>
</ul>
<b>Customer Service:</b>
<ul>
<li>Assists with customer service and sales in Museum, exhibition, or sale stores on an as-needed basis. Knowledgeable of basic POS functions for sales transactions. Understands store customer service guidelines and policies.</li>
<li>Interacts with store staff, customers, and vendors to help resolve issues or provide answers to questions related to shipping or receiving.</li>
<li>Maintains knowledge of the Museum’s collection, galleries, and changing exhibitions. Is aware of special and/or daily events in Museum. Able to answer general questions for visitors if asked.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Knowledge, Skills and Physical Requirements</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of basic computer software programs, databases, email, and internet usage.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of general receiving practices.
</li>
<li>Able to complete work accurately, pay attention to detail. Uses sound judgment in decision-making and has basic analytical skills for problem solving.</li>
<li>Highly organized and maintains neat work area.</li>
<li>Professional communication skills – both written and verbal.</li>
<li>Self-motivated, adaptable, ability to adjust pace, accepting of challenges, and open to change.</li>
<li>Ability to work individually and function as part of a team. Capable of establishing respectful, supportive and cooperative working relationships within the department, the Museum, and the community. Contributes to an overall positive work environment. Committed to providing exceptional service.
</li>
<li>Maintains professional appearance and follows Museum Store dress guidelines.</li>
<li>Sit or stand for periods of time. Able to walk, reach, stoop, or bend.
</li>
<li>Ability to lift up to 50 lbs.
</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Work Schedule Requirements</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Work between 20-24 hours weekly</li>
<li>Schedule flexibility based on scheduling needs and business demands.</li>
<li>Support store staffing needs – provide coverage during lunch hours and for special events, weekends, nights, or holidays when necessary.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
Museum Store is open Monday - Sunday from 10am – 5pm and on Thursdays until 8pm (closed on Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Day -12/25). Open Mondays from Memorial Day through Labor Day.<br>
Milwaukee Art Museum is a EOE/M/W/Vets/Disability Employer.<br>
