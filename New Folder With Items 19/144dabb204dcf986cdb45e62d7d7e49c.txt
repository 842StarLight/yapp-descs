<h2>Account Executive- Boston</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>ZAGENO has become the world's largest biotechnology &amp; life science’s e-commerce marketplace. Our customers are scientists, procurement professionals and researchers from all over the world. We enable scientists to get the critical products for their research faster and cheaper, thus driving down the time &amp; cost it takes to reach a scientific breakthrough. Find out more at: https://zageno.com<br>
As a Business to Business (B2B) marketplace, we acquire and serve scientists &amp; purchasers by empowering them to identify optimal products among all vendors, handle their orders, optimize their purchasing processes and deliver the best customer service. We are searching for an exceptional individual to contribute to ZAGENO’s growth and success through new account acquisition for our marketplace in close collaboration with the sales team, VP of Sales and Chief Commercial Officer.
</div>

<h6 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your Tasks</b></h6>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Own and achieve monthly Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) and sales targets</li>
<li>
Build a robust GMV sales pipeline with accounts in assigned territory</li>
<li>
Learn and understand how to successfully position ZAGENO’s SaaS (Software as a Service) Marketplace offering and solution</li>
<li>
Successfully engage existing and prospective customers in calls and face-to-face meetings to drive sales opportunities forward to closure</li>
<li>
Prepare and deliver demonstrations that showcase the value of the ZAGENO solution to prospective customers (senior leaders, scientists, lab managers and procurement professionals)</li>
<li>
Perform proper account and territory planning</li>
<li>
Strategically orchestrate resources and collaborate both internally and externally throughout the sales process to win.</li>
<li>
Report to the VP, Sales</li>
</ul>
