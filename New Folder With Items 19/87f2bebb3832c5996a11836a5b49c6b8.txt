<h2>Purchasing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Purchasing Associate is at the service of the Gospel by purchasing inventory and maintaining needed stock levels at each Pauline Books and Media Center. Principle duties include:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Analyzing sales and inventory data to create purchase orders for stock replenishment on a weekly basis.</li>
<li>Frequently corresponding with book center managers to assist with special orders and stock requests.</li>
<li>Frequently corresponding with vendors to submit purchase orders and to follow up on order fulfillment issues.</li>
<li>Maintaining purchasing ledgers for each book center to track purchasing trends and to assure budget allocations.</li>
</ul>
The following skills are required to carry out the above listed duties:
<br><ul>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills for business correspondence with managers, vendor representatives, and purchasing colleagues.</li>
<li>Proficiency with MS Excel and MS Outlook.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and to meet weekly schedule deadlines.</li>
<li>Ability to learn using purchasing and data reporting software.</li>
</ul>
Although a college degree is preferred, it is not required. The position is full time Monday through Friday. The position pays $16 per hour and includes benefits. Please send your resume to [SEE ORIGINAL LISTING]</div>
