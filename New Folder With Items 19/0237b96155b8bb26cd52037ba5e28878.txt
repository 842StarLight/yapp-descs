<h2>Warehouse Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Exclusive Networks is a fast-growing company (50% growth YoY since 2008) reaching 1.8 Bn€, covering 4 continents through 27 countries and employing 1600 people. To continue our high growth, to get worldwide reach and to structure further our delivery capability, the group is looking to hire a <b>Warehouse Administrator</b>for its US warehouse located in San Jose CA. Headquartered in Boulogne-Billancourt, Exclusive Networks is focused on launching cybersecurity start-ups in Europe, Middle East, APAC and North America. Activities cover classic distribution (financing and logistics) as well as added value services (marketing, sales, technical support, services)</p>
<p><b>As a Warehouse Administrator, your responsibilities would include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Managing daily operations for order fulfillment</li>
<li>Entering tracking information for fulfilled orders in our system.</li>
<li>Receiving and fulfilling email deliverables</li>
<li>Monitoring of shipments, clearance process and keep track of delivery times. Tender shipments to carriers</li>
<li>Respecting Group compliance policy for dual use type of products, report gaps in compliance</li>
<li>Complying with Health and Safety Regulations</li>
<li>Learning and implementing 5S methodology within the warehouse</li>
<li>Monitoring inventory record and monthly cycle physical count</li>
<li>Managing RMA process and delivery Return to Vendor when relevant</li>
<li>Working with Finance to ensure GRN process are duly processed in system before AP payment processing</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>3+ years of experience in operations, warehouse organization and order fulfillment, transportation, Health &amp; Safety</li>
<li>OSHA Certification for Forklift operation preferred</li>
<li>Experience driving a box truck preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>Languages:</p>
<p>English fluency is a must, any other language a plus</p>
<p>Personality &amp; Skills</p>
<p>· Experience in a warehouse environment</p>
<p>· Good personal computer and business solutions software skills</p>
<p>· Capable of managing multiple objectives simultaneously</p>
<p>· Self-motivated, requiring minimal direct supervision</p>
<p>· Experience with NetSuite is a plus</p>
<p>· Experience in IT Distribution or Supply Chain industry is a plus</p>
