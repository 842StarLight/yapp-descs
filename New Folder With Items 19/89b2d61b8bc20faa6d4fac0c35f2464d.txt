Description:</b></p>
<p>
The Erus Sales position is a six figure opportunity that's not right for everyone. You must be willing to conduct at least five appointments a day, five days a week, Monday through Thursdays and Saturdays. Many of these appointments will be conducted around the metro area, requiring a good deal of travel. We do however believe that balance is a must for our sales team. As such, you will receive 2 days off per week and have set "working" hours each day based on appointments set through our call center.</p>
<p>
Our salespeople's primary responsibility is to set appointments and delight our clients. As a Retail Sales Rep, you must be able to manage your time and generate additional appointments through referrals and other self-generated devices we equip you with. An ideal candidate for this job will have great passion for selling, be great with people, and love to sell for a firm that is the best in its class.</p>
<p>
You will be a revenue generator for a high growth enterprise that doesn't accept excuses and reasons EVER. The Erus work environment is fast paced with high expectations, no excuses, and tremendous "share the riches" pay for performance.<br>
