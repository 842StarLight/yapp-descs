<h2>DATA ANALYST - 9153 73</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">SUMMARY
<br><br>
The objective of this role is to translate business questions and requirements into reports/projections that help guide business decisions. The candidate would work across departments and use their technical and analytical skills to provide insights into the business. This would be a highly focused position that would involve analyses across sales, customer service, plant, pricing, and finance data. The successful candidate must be a result-oriented, team player, and have a continuous process improvement mindset. Being a friendly, self-motivated team player with strong interpersonal skills is necessary for this role. Excellent verbal and written communication skills are mandatory.
<br><br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify relevant data sources and transform data as needed to perform analysis</li>
<li>Perform analysis, often on large sets of data, to extract actionable insights that will help drive decisions across the business</li>
<li>Interpret data, draw conclusions, and communicate recommendations to decision makers</li>
<li>Present analysis in a simple, accessible manner</li>
<li>Aid in the development of sophisticated pricing and behavioral models</li>
<li>Develop and maintain metrics to be used across various departments</li>
<li>Interact with sales, marketing, plant management, and finance to understand reporting needs</li>
<li>Work with management to identify opportunities for additional business application of statistical analysis</li>
<li>Prepare, run, and distribute monthly metrics/reports</li>
</ul>
