<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Waste Connections is looking for a High Energy Customer Service Representative to join the team at our Vancouver, WA. Previous waste experience would be a huge plus! 2+ years of Customer Service experience in a high call volume atmosphere highly preferred.
<br>Competitive Wage DOE.
<br>A typical schedule for this position is Monday-Friday from 8:00am-5:00pm.
<br><br>
<b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
Work in conjunction with other departments to resolve customer disputes &amp; demonstrate the ability to become proficient in the use of proprietary operational systems.
<br>Interviews customers and records interview information into computer for customer service, talks with customers by phone and in person, to receive orders for installation, turn on, discontinuance, or change in service.
<br>Fills out contract forms, determines charges for service requested, collects deposits, prepares change of address records and issues discontinuance orders.
<br>Demonstrate effective use of software applications, at a minimum MS Word and Excel proficient.
<br>Delivers services to customers in a manner that promotes goodwill. Interacts with customers and Waste Connections employees to determine service requirements and resolve problems or complaints in a courteous manner
<br>Work in a fast paced, cooperative, high call volume environment with very detailed expectations
<br>Solicit sale of new or additional services.
<br>Assist in other areas as needed
<br><br>
<b>WORKING CONDITIONS AND PHYSICAL EFFORT:</b><br>
Work environment is in a call center. Areas are clean, ventilated, and well lighted. Our Customer Service Reps normally have their own workstation or cubicle space and are equipped with a telephone, headset, and computer.
<br>CSR call areas can be noisy and work may be repetitious and stressful, with little time in between calls, while still providing excellent service. Some calls are evaluated to ensure high standards.
<br>Also, long periods spent sitting, typing, or looking at a computer screen are very common.
<br><br>
<b>MINIMUM JOB REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
Ability to read, write, comprehend and communicate in English.
<br>High school diploma or equivalent preferred.
<br>2+ years of Customer Service experience in a high call volume atmosphere highly preferred.
<br>Experience with MS Word, Excel, and Email preferred. Typing and 10-key skills are required.
<br>Excellent organization and communication skills.
<br>Ability to analyze and solve problems. Gather data, compile information, and prepare reports.
