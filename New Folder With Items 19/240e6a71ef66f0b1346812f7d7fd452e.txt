<h2>Assistant Regional Controller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Assistant Controller in our Scottsdale, Arizona headquarters will manage the daily details of the Accounting Department and ensure the Accounting Department runs smoothly, meeting internal and external reporting deadlines, producing financial statements that comply with company policy and GAAP. Works closely with the Controller to maintain a system of internal accounting controls to ensure the integrity of the accounting system.</p>
<p><b>
FUNDAMENTAL JOB DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Motivate, educate, promote, acknowledge and monitor the quality of effort and product produced by the accounting team.</li>
<li>
Supervise the preparation of monthly financial reports and ensure prompt and accurate financial reporting.</li>
<li>
Review/approve/post daily &amp; monthly journal entry transactions including home closing , month-end, monthly accruals, cash receipts, etc.</li>
<li>
Review month-end FLASH report and related validations.</li>
<li>
Review sales commissions; that are processed on a semi-monthly basis to payroll department.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the financial review meetings with division and region personnel, attend other division meetings such as budget reviews and operational meetings.</li>
<li>
Review account reconciliation schedules, bank reconciliations, and supplemental schedules.</li>
<li>
Review “budget to actual” comparison financial analysis and commentary.
</li>
<li>Review job cost analysis of closed homes.
</li>
<li>Assist to resolve accounting issues as they arise and follow up on discrepancies.</li>
<li>
Responsible for posting all payroll entries including paid time off accruals.</li>
<li>
Work with individuals in other departments within the company. Demonstrate good communication and customer service skills with individuals both within and outside the organization
</li>
<li>Maintain department policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</ul>
