<div><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Support customer proposals and negotiation efforts as required by sales management and system/service sales</li>
<li>Maintain system/service sales offer files including proposal/pricing information</li>
<li>Maintain all Value Purchasing Agreements and Alliance documentation</li>
<li>Assist service sales/system sales in order entry process</li>
<li>Assist in development and implementation of programs that increase KOB3 revenue in accordance with Inside Sales Stretch objective</li>
<li>Work with headquarters marketing and field sales to improve knowledge of Ovation PlantWeb products</li>
<li>Increase presentation knowledge and skills to better enhance ability to promote Ovation and Emerson products, services and features</li>
<li>Assist service sales personnel in the development of WDPF/Ovation life cycle programs</li>
<li>Work in unison with PWS headquarters and field sales personnel to provide technical, product capability and pricing information</li>
<li>Interact with customers and PWS personnel to support inquiries, resolve problems/issues and increase efficiency of overall sales process</li>
<li>Orchestrate/support Ovation PlantWeb product demos for customer presentations, regional user groups, and PWS sponsored events</li>
<li>Increase knowledge of power plant operator and engineering functions, along with plant processes to increase presentation effectiveness</li>
<li>Maintain Ovation demo systems to current hardware/software revision levels</li>
<li>Facilitate PATT updates, territory business plans and reports as required to support management and sales requirements</li>
<li>Develop demonstration skills for Plantweb, AMS, Foundation Fieldbus, and Emerson field devices</li>
<li>Organize and support “Live Meeting” presentations for territory business plans, sales training and other business functions</li>
</ul>
