<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities include:
</b></h1>
<p></p>
<p>The Business Data Analyst role at Bose is a blend skill set of data management tools (Data Quality, Governance, ERP, CRM and Data Analytics). The Business Data Analyst can speak data, process, business and technical language to add value and benefits solving data problem. He/She will facilitate capturing and documenting business, data, governance and technology assets.
</p>
<p>The Business Data Analyst role accelerates advanced analytics and Data Science time to value by executing a framework to answer the following questions:
</p>
<ul>
<li>What data is available (Data Catalog)?
</li>
<li>Is the data relevant and meaningful (Data classification)?
</li>
<li>What does the data mean (Technical/Business metadata, Business Glossary, and Reference Data)?
</li>
<li>Where did the data come from (Data Lineage)?
</li>
<li>What is the quality of the data (Data Quality)?
</li>
<li>Is the data verified (Certified Data)?
</li>
<li>How can I get access to the data (Processes)?
</li>
<li>Am I in compliance by using this data (Policy)?
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Implement a centralized data governance framework driving Bose to data driven decisions.
</li>
<li>Build enterprise systems centralized data catalogs enabling collaboration across functions and simplifying data discovery.
</li>
<li>Provide support to source systems and enterprise analytical platforms classifying data, adding data usage, and providing use case context around the data.
</li>
<li>Define and capture business and technical metadata to provide context, meaning and searchability around data and the data catalog. Define critical data elements business terms to drive common semantics across Bose. Build reference data and relate it to the critical data elements.
</li>
<li>Identify, define, measure data quality and develop critical data elements (CDEs) logical and physical lineage including data flow diagrams providing insight into focus areas applying a data governance framework.
</li>
<li>Organize Data Governance councils including functional stakeholders to align with the centralized Data Governance framework.
</li>
<li>Deliver education, training, and consulting across Bose to be an ambassador for driving data driven culture across Bose.
</li>
<li>Discover key stakeholders of data including owners, subject matter experts and existing Business Data Analysts.
</li>
<li>Work with cross-functional Business Data Analysts to understand, document and improve processes to access data (from request to provisioning). Streamline across Bose to reduce complexity executing workshops and aligning process stakeholders/owners.
</li>
<li>Provides support of Data Science and Advanced Analytics project/programs reducing administrative data governance tasks and action items.
</li>
<li>Partner with Legal, Information Security and HR to ensure compliance and security of data including how to manage data (masking, encrypting, pseudonymizing, synonymizing).
</li>
<li>Develop and align centralized policies and standards providing a baseline for functional policies.
</li>
<li>Develop and execute quarterly plans while aligning to Bose strategic vision.
</li>
<li>Track and measure wins including return on investment measuring quarterly governance results and value delivered.
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Skills, Knowledge and Experiences
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Candidate should have a minimum of 5 years of Master Data Management and solid experience understanding a Data Governance framework.
</li>
<li>Previous experience with Bose CE product/engineering/apps
</li>
<li>Solid knowledge of data relationship for structure and unstructured data.
</li>
<li>Previous experience in research working environment by governing data from internal and external partners.
</li>
<li>Work in very dynamic environment where changes are introduced rapidly in the business process, data flow and technologies.
</li>
<li>Experience in consumer industry focus on product data, sales and marketing
</li>

</li>
