<h2>Professional Services Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Working under the supervision of the Vice President of Professional Services, the Professional Services Group (PSG) Manager Is responsible for all the functions of the Professional Services Group department. The manager will oversee staff and implement strategies to increase productivity. The manager will conduct training to motivate and build the skills of team members. The manager will continually assess their employee productivity levels and help them to achieve their sales goals.
<br><br>
In addition, the manager will be directly responsible for the success of client engagement projects managed. The Project Manager works as project leader on PSG client engagements including but not limited to software application platform migration, User Interface development, service layer development, application feature development, and database and client retainer (Staff Augmentation) projects. For most projects, this will involve at least one trip to the customer site.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will have 1-3 years’ experience in managing staff and enterprise-level technology projects. They will be motivated by a desire to help businesses achieve their goals and be comfortable with multiple ongoing projects.
