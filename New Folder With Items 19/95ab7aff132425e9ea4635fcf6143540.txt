<h2>AV/Healthcare Systems Design Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Ronco is seeking an experienced, self-motivated <b>AV/Healthcare Systems Design Engineer</b> for its Specialized Division in the Albany, NY<b> </b>area. In this role, you will research and design healthcare, educational, and Audio Visual systems for installation at end-user sites. The AV/Healthcare Systems Design Engineer will provide pre-sales and post-sales support to sales and operation departments.</p>
<p><b>Accountabilities Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with account managers during the quoting process to ensure cohesion between design and customer needs</li>
<li>Assist with the bidding process by reviewing bid documents and making system or product recommendations to account manager</li>
<li>Conduct site surveys to collect accurate information for the purposes of quoting and system design</li>
<li>Initiate and complete engineering review process for new sales orders in a timely manner</li>
<li>Review all bid documents, including plans, specifications, and addenda as part of the post-sale engineering process</li>
<li>Create functional one-line and riser diagrams utilizing AutoCAD or similar software.</li>
<li>Review sales orders for an accurate bill of materials and communicate any changes to the Account Manager</li>
<li>Work closely with field operations personnel to ensure that installations are consistent with current system design and best practices</li>
<li>Generate as-built drawings to complete project closeout documentation</li>
<li>Stay apprised of current products by taking advantage of online and classroom training offered by the company</li>
</ul>
