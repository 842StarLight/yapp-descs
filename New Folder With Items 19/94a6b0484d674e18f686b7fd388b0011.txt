<h2>Sr Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Expedia<p></p>
<p><b>
CarRentals</b> is a small but rapidly growing company within the Expedia Group portfolio and the premier car rental booking company on the web. CarRentals powers three different brands across multiple points of sale on a single common platform, bringing suppliers and customers together to find the right car and the best price. CarRentals offers a phenomenal opportunity to work in a startup-like environment backed by the resources, benefits, and network of a large, multi-national online e-commerce company.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As a Senior Data Analyst, on the CarRentals Finance Tech team, you will build solutions to automate rhythm of business functions, apply statistical forecast models in support of financial and operational forecasting, and use data science to solve problems where the problem isn’t obvious. CR Finance is a small team with big ambitions to transform the role of Finance in business through use of data. Are you passionate about data analysis? Are you a master in VB or Python? Do you find travel fascinating and always wondered what it looks like behind the scenes?</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
About the Position</b></p>
<p>
CarRentals Finance is building a team of technologists to further our objective of transforming how Finance does business. We are looking for someone with a passion for using data to hypothesize, investigate and solve real-world problems. You will enjoy working with various data sets, spanning from finance, web, marketing, to booking data, over different data platforms, collaborating with bright minds from diverse groups on regular basis. You'll be able to see the impact of your work and analyses on the business and contribute deeply.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What you’ll do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You complete ad-hoc analysis requests using relational databases/AWS, Excel, Tableau (or Looker), Hive, Python, or R.</li>
<li>
You automate the boring, eradicate the mundane, through use of a keyboard and some code.</li>
<li>
You use data to find insights and make data-driven decisions to support and advise our leadership team.</li>
<li>
You collaborate with various teams – FP&amp;A, Accounting, Analytics and Data Engineering in pursuit of your mission.</li>
<li>
You will work with remote team members and foster relationships as part of a multi-functional project team.</li>
<li>
You take inconsistent data from multiple sources and transform it into a single, usable form.</li>
<li>
You design and implement reporting solutions as an end state of analyses.</li>
<li>
You enable internal partners to make data-driven decisions.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who you are:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree (majors in Business, Statistics, Computer Science, or Data Science) with 3+ years of relevant experience.</li>
<li>
You can write advanced SQL code and know basic ETL.</li>
<li>
Advanced Excel skills.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Tableau, Looker, or similar reporting tool.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Python or another mainstream scripting language.</li>
<li>
You have a bias for action.</li>
<li>
You think big and small to grasp the entire picture while taking care of details.</li>
<li>
Experience with the agile process and JIRA board.</li>
</ul>
