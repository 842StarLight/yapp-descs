<h2>Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The analyst will update internal tracking, create reporting and respond to escalations within the defined responsibilities.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Main Duties / Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build &amp; QA billing/pricing element tables (Interchange, Auth, Data Capture, etc.).</li>
<li>Provide assistance and feedback to the Senior Data Analyst(s) and respond to Operations, Product and Sales escalations.</li>
<li>Manage Card Brand and 3rd party invoices; query monthly invoices and generate P&amp;L reports in coordination with IT/DBA and Commissions/Finance teams.
</li>
<li>Build and maintain cross-processor interchange code and billing descriptor mapping grids (US &amp; Canada regions).
</li>
<li>Analyze impact of card brand updates/changes against all production pricing and billing elements.</li>
<li>Develop portfolio specific reporting/metrics using business intelligence tools e.g. MS Power BI.
</li>
</ul>
