<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>Mechanical Engineering Technician/ Project Manager</b></p>
<p><b>About Highwood: </b></p>
<p>Highwood Global LP is an equipment manufacturing company with a diversified group of clients in the aerospace, mining, commercial industrial and oil &amp; gas industries.</p>
<p><b>Position Overview: </b></p>
<p>The engineering technologist will be responsible for managing job-based projects and providing technical design support for sales and production.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand and adhere to quality management system requirements including AS9100, API, and AWS.</li>
<li>Project Design: design and manage the manufacture of Highwood products, and products designed by our customers.</li>
<li>Responsible for all aspects of projects including vendor selection, supply chain support, quality and design manuals on a project by project basis.</li>
<li>Maintain our standard of drawing and documentation control.</li>
<li>Work as a member of a team including production and sales teams.</li>
<li>Interacting with customers to approve drawings, revisions, processes and design.</li>
<li>Working with the production team to maintain schedules and quality.</li>
<li>Other related responsibilities including customer and field visits will be required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>3 + years of experience with SOLIDWORKS or 5+ years of experience with 3-D Modeling software</li>
<li>2+ years of experience in project management</li>
<li>Ability to multitask between design and project management on multiple projects.</li>
<li>3+ years of experience in an equipment manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>Must be able to read hydraulic, electrical and pneumatic schematics and perform changes as required .</li>
<li>Experience in design of hydraulic, electrical and pneumatic equipment is an asset.</li>
<li>Ability to maximize design and production efficiency while working with customers and the team at Highwood.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
