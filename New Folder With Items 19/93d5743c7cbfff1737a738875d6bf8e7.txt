<p><b>Key Responsibilities and Tasks</b></p>
<br>
<div></div>
<div> <div>
<div><div>
<div><b>Training Delivery related tasks:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Delivery of trainings (also international) in all offered channels and formats (e.g. Instructor-Led Training, customer specific training, SAP Live Class (respectively all virtual formats), Hybrid), specialist courses on programming topics – ABAP, ABAP on SAP Cloud Platform, SAPUI5, Fiori, SAP Cloud Platform</li>
<li>Active support of instructors from the internal and external network who are not teaching full time</li>
<li>Communication with customers beyond the class topics and solving of respective queries independently, acting as a trusted advisor for our customer and partners</li>
<li>Usage of integration knowledge to position the added value to our customers and partners within delivery and to develop / realize ideas for further Education products</li>
<li>Leading complex CST qualifications, including the respective coordination and customer contact. Managing customer expectations appropriately.</li>
<li>Taking over global content responsibility (ECM), also for innovative topics</li>
<li>Acquire detailed knowledge of various Enablement paths provided by SAP Education (not only focusing on ILT)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education Business Development related tasks:
 </b></div>
<ul>
<li>Delivery of content development activities, also for innovative topics (material, system, course description, SSG etc.) and adaptation of content related to customer requirements</li>
<li>Providing input within Learning Journey Design workshops for courses in the respective functional coordination area</li>
<li>Quality assurance with respect to material and system (e.g. content related reviews, demonstration and exercise tests, translation reviews) and new external/internal instructors (Readiness check)</li>
<li>On Demand development of Stay Current content</li>
<li>Support of Learning Needs Analysis</li>
<li>Leading Learning Hub and Learning Room activities</li>
<li>Leading Demand Generation activities (e.g. delivering Webinars) and other external and internal rollout tasks</li>
<li>Supporting Business Development activities (e.g. SAP Education Forums, Bootcamps)</li>
<li>Collaboration with other units inside SAP Education Delivery (e.g. Scheduling, Staffing)</li>
<li>Collaboration with units outside SAP Education Delivery (e.g. Education Sales, Production, Systems, Development, Consulting, University)</li>
<li>Proactively increasing internal/external network (e.g. Solution Management, Development, Consulting, Education Production, Partner etc.)</li>
<br>
</ul>
