<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p><b>Logistics Site Director - Senior - #TX017</b></p>
<p>Cummins is a place big enough to coach and develop a global workforce and create the world’s leading clean, engine technology. We’re also small enough for you to find your fit and personal passion with a team of dependable, innovative thinkers who are developing their careers within a diverse, inclusive, empowering environment.</p>
<p>Learn more about this role and how you can begin <i>Working Right</i> .</p>
<p>Our Corporate Business Unit delivers reliable, durable, high performing products to our global partners. Working in an innovative space, you’ll develop high tech solutions that will fuel your advanced career skill set and empower you to own your career. Our integrated businesses demand the talents and creativity of individuals with a wide range of skills and experience.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>This is an exciting opportunity for <b>Logistics Site Director - Senior - #TX017</b> in Dallas, Texas</p>
<p>Your impact will happen in these and other ways:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Direct and oversee cross-functional team to ensure speed, dependability and efficiency within Parts Distribution Center operations or warehouse, providing excellent product availability and service to Distributors, Aftermarket customers, and internal stakeholders.</p></li>
<li><p>
Oversee the development of logistics plans and procedures to ensure internal and client needs are met using data analytics, inventory management, Materials Requirements Planning (MRP), warehousing, material flow principles and project management.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ideate, design, and roll-out state-of-the art systems and solutions to improve supply chain operations performance.</p></li>
<li><p>
Drive innovation through analytics in highly complex, matrix environments.</p></li>
<li><p>
Utilize core principles of operations management and business analytics to redesign, optimize and control of logistics business operations.</p></li>
<li><p>
Lead data analytics with business simulation models to improve efficiencies in inventory management, warehousing and transportation processes of the logistics operations.</p></li>
<li><p>
Lead the development and utilization of data management and analytical models to deploy optimal network strategies to drive business efficiencies and meet customer delivery expectations.</p></li>
<li><p>
Work with corporate purchasing on RFQs and RFPs in evaluation and selection of the right logistics business partners for the business.</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible to manage, coach and coordinate the use of statistical models to optimize the inventory levels in the network.</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible to make effective decisions with the use of data analytics on supply chain quality and product packaging improvements to provide sustainability in end to end supply chain of CMI.</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible for delivering plan performance and developing and improving Key Performance Indicators (KPI's).</p></li>
<li><p>
Perform in-depth statistical analyses to set the annual operating plan with the use of data from various KPIs of logistics such as safety, quality (DPMO &amp; claims data), customer delivery, supplier past dues, customer back orders, labor rates, logistics cost and sales.</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop and utilize mathematical business models using labor throughput rates and production volumes to make decisions on periodic labor needs, warehouse and transportation capacity.</p></li>
<li><p>
Utilize knowledge of systems including Oracle, Xelus, GOMS, CQMS and JDA (RP) to manage upgrades and implementation.</p></li>
<li><p>
Direct and manage system configurations and customizations necessary to optimize logistics operations and new network deployment strategies.</p></li>
<li><p>
Align with internal business partners to garner support and advocacy of enterprise Supply Chain initiatives.</p></li>
<li><p>
Utilize statistical analysis and Design of Experiments (DOE) to evaluate process capability in order to make improvement recommendations.</p></li>
<li><p>
Champion continuous improvement projects that align with COS and Cross-Functional Excellence by leveraging common processes, tools and information systems and applying principles of Six Sigma methodologies, Lean, 5S, Kaizen, Kanban and Value Stream Mapping to drive effective business decisions in logistics.</p></li>
</ul>
