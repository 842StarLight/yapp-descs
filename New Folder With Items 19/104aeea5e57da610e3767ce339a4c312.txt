<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
The YouTube and Video Solutions team is the link between Google products and business. Your focus is to help turn innovation into complete, packaged tools that allow our customers to get the most of what they need from our suite of products. In doing so, you'll help to ensure that Google has adopted the right strategy for our products. You'll work within our 'Buying Experiences' pillar, the mission of which is to surface Google’s innovative video formats and audiences in ways that are simple and clear to buyers of all skill levels. As a strategic and forward-thinking individual, you will help video buyers around the globe build smarter campaigns for their marketing objectives.You'll work closely with teams to commercialize new and existing features, collect market feedback, and drive forward-looking strategy alongside product management. Your day to day could vary from hosting a client feedback session, white-boarding product solutions, developing communications materials, participating in a strategy review, and collaborating with regional/channel go-to-market on a launch plan. You can move nimbly between strategic thinking and project management. You can operate in a fast-paced and constantly changing environment. This is a great opportunity to work on a visible, cross-functional project of critical importance to the organization.</p>
<p>
Global Solutions is the link between Google products and business. Your focus is to help turn innovation into complete, packaged tools that allow our customers to get the most that they need from our products. As part of the Global Solutions team, you'll help to ensure that Google has adopted the right strategy for our products. Working in one of three specialized areas – Performance, Brand, or Publisher &amp; Platform – you'll use your expertise to help front-line Sales partners to work their magic quickly and effectively. You are passionate about all things digital, and want to help shape the world of online advertising.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Lead high visibility projects by working with global partners to audit, verify and collate best practices for each marketing objective.</li>
<li>Establish long term vision for video ads insights, recommendations and optimization in partnership with go-to-market and product management.</li>
<li>Represent the buy-side point of view with partner teams in product management and across YouTube and Video to ensure new formats and targeting solutions are intuitive and consumable.</li>
<li>Improve the touch-points of other systems to Google Ads, including planning and bulk tools like Google Ads Editor, Scripts and Bulk Upload.</li>
<li>Synthesize and prioritize product requests into a consolidated point of view that can be packaged and commercialized as solutions for our sales force.</li>
</ul>
