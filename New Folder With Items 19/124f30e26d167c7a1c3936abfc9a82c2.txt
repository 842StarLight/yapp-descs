<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>As a member of CoreLogic’s Science and Analytics Engineering team you will participate in the design, development, coding, testing, debugging, deployment and maintenance of machine learning models for the consumer credit and the global real estate market. This work will require you to work with data scientists/modelers to create new models using a variety of machine leaning techniques. You will be responsible for the implementation and integration of machine learning models with solution platforms and backend databases. Your responsibilities may include leading teams using agile methodology.Job duties include: Work with product managers and data scientists/modelers to understand and document the technical requirements for the systems that will run new machine learning models.Estimate the effort required to implement new machine learning models or modify existing modelsArchitect the software environments needed to both build and run the machine learning models. This will include both on premises and cloud-based environments.Work closely with the data scientists/modelers to code new models and improve existing modelsDesigning and implementing the software used to acquire and transform the data used in the machine learning models Work with other engineering teams to integrate models into CoreLogic’s productsLead teams of engineers and data scientists/models using agile methodologies and practicesMonitor and report to management on the status of project efforts, anticipating/identifying issues that inhibit the attainment of project goals and implementing corrective actionsFoster and maintain good relationships with customers and other teams within CoreLogic to meet expected delivery dates and service levels
<p></p>
