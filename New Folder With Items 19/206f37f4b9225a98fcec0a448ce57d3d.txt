<h2>Sr. Project Manager - Land Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>True Homes</b>, Best Workplace and National Housing Quality GOLD Builder, is growing in Charlotte, NC. Because of this growth, we are looking to add a new <b>Senior Project Manager of Land Development</b> to our Market Team in the North Charlotte area. We're searching for qualified Land Development professionals with <b>Grading, Site, General, Utility or Roadway Contractor experience in either commercial or residential environments</b>. At True Homes, we attribute our growth and success to cross-functional collaborative execution and we are searching for someone who is ready to make a big impact as part of our team., please apply!</p>
<p>The Senior Project Manager - Land Development is responsible for leading a team of internal and external resources to define, plan, execute, monitor and close out the Land Development projects for a specific market. The Senior Project Manager – Land Development executes a specific market’s land strategy including sales, starts, and closing budgets within their allocated capital budget. The projects will meet the Land Development team's quality standards and budgets established by the Operations Partner - Land Development.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Project Management and Schedule Conformance
<ul>
<li>Identify and creates standard and project specific due diligence, entitlement, development, and close out activities and estimate durations to create a project schedule and identify the critical path</li>
<li>Identifies all required permits and submission process necessary for development</li>
<li>Ensures due diligence tasks are executed prior to end of defined inspection period</li>
<li>Manage site plan approval and rezoning process to obtain minimum design efficiency requirements in addition to desired lot sizes, density, and product requirements to meet Market's strategies</li>
<li>Review due diligence materials with regard to assumptions, risks, and issues and make recommendation to Land Committee to extend due diligence period or proceed with project</li>
<li>Engages and leads design Trade Partners to ensure land development standards are being met in design and field work and sufficient progress is made regarding schedule and gate conformance</li>
<li>Manages and track permit submittal packages and approvals necessary for development; influences plan and permit reviewers to achieve desired designs and gate conformance</li>
<li>Guides and coordinates the value engineering process</li>
<li>Works with Land Administration on reviewing and assembling A&amp;D loan package</li>
<li>Oversees Trade Partner selection process through mutual gain negotiating</li>
<li>Submits final construction budget, schedule, and Trade Partner agreements for approval by Land Committee along</li>
<li>Monitors and controls schedules for all project activities through regular on-site meetings with Trade Partners and subcontractors</li>
<li>Identifies, records, tracks, and resolves actual and potential issues in all phases of development</li>
<li>Ensures communities are "Retail Ready" and adhere to maintenance and cleanliness standards</li>
<li>Coordinates, maintains, and reports the issuance of and tracking of capital and maintenance bonds in a timely manner to retire bonds at earliest possible instance</li>
<li>Records and archives project documents and lessons learned</li>
<li>Presides over Lot Turnover process to sales team</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Project Financial Performance
<ul>
<li>Effectively develop accurate budgets using historical costs, contractor bids, and verified quantities on our established budgeting templates.</li>
<li>Ensures equity and debt requirements for each land project and development phase is at or below market’s annual allocated capital.</li>
<li>Plans, acquires resources, and delivers projects at or below budgets that are underwritten to meet project goals.</li>
<li>Reviews change order requests for scope, cost, and schedule impacts and makes recommendations to Land Committee for approval</li>
<li>Reviews invoices against contracts, work completed, and job cost reports.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Market Leadership
<ul>
<li>Participates in Market Leadership team meetings discussing land and lot delivery</li>
<li>Reconciles Transition Report with Development Schedule to verify lot delivery per Market's goals</li>
<li>Manages Land Development's portion of Market's balance sheet</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as needed or required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in engineering or similar technical degree and 7 years of land development project management experience OR 12 years of land development project management experience in a corporate setting required</li>
<li>Detailed oriented and superior analytical skills required</li>
<li>Competency in reading civil engineering drawings and performing material take-offs</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office and Microsoft Project or other scheduling software required</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license and reliable personal transportation for occasional regional travel</li>
</ul>
