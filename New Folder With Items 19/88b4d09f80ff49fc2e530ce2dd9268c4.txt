<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our team is looking for an Account Executive to join our team in San Diego, CA.</p>
<p>
The Account Executive is a consultative sales role where you will be solving your client's B2B communication challenges by offering compelling software (SaaS), hardware, and professional services.</p>
<p>
As an Account Executive, you will enjoy a competitive salary, commission and bonus plan. Neopost provides engaging incentives throughout the year culminating with an annual (all expenses paid) trip to extraordinary locations for top performers. Neopost also provides sales education and career development programs to encourage professional growth.</p>

What Neopost USA Offers You:</p>
<ul>

<li>Veteran Programs</li>

</ul>
<p>Neopost USA is actively seeking candidates who possess these essential qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>1-5 years of sales experience</li>
<li>Strong customer service orientation</li>
<li>Solid computer skills</li>
<li>Exposure to CRM systems such as Salesforce</li>

</ul>
