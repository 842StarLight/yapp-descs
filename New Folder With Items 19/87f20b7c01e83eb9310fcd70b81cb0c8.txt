<h2>Sr. Manager, Product Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Metagenics is a global lifestyle medicine company, offering products and programs exclusively to health care practitioners. For over 30 years, we have been driven by a scientific concept: How the right nutrition can help people realize their best health possible. We don't compromise on safety, efficacy, or quality - to make sure that we deliver the best results possible. Time after time. Bottle after bottle. We carefully monitor all products to assure purity and safety, from formulation and ingredient selection all the way to the final product. We offer more educational support for health care professionals than anyone else in our field - keeping them in touch with the latest research and nutritional approaches to help manage today’s most common illnesses. Metagenics is dedicated to helping people live a happier, healthier life by realizing their genetic potential.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Position overview:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Sr.Manager/Director, Product Marketing will focus on executing commercial excellence within our Marketing team. This role plays in integral role in driving our product road maps, understanding our customer base, and using analytics to bring our products to market.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>This role requires strong partnering with functional leads and key stakeholders in Marketing, Regulatory, R&amp;D, Sales, and several other technical departments to drive commercial excellence. Must have the ability to juggle multiple projects of varying scope under tight deadlines.<br>
</div>
</div></div></div>
<div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>What you'll do:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Execute commercial excellence by performing the following activities:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Drive the product roadmap and priorities based on customer requirements, competitive insights, win/loss analysis and opportunities for innovation</li>
<li>
Collect, analyze and manage competitive intelligence. Provide constructive guidance to Product Management and Sales based on insight gained. Support the development and refinement of competitive positioning</li>
<li>
Partner with Product Management, Marketing and Sales teams to align efforts and effectively position and message our products by demonstrating unique value propositions to our customer segments across global markets</li>
<li>
Manage the analysis of the competitive environment, industry trends, feedback from customers, customer service and sales reps
</li>
<li>Development and execution of strategic framework and value proposition</li>
<li>
Educate team, employees, and other departments about industry marketing trends including: the development of future trends through novel product development and positioning Brainstorming ways to promote new products or initiatives</li>
<li>
Manage, analyze, and track P&amp;L budget with department head</li>
<li>
Develop, coordinate, deliver, and test marketing strategies across multiple channels on various platforms (campaigns, events, digital marketing, PR, social media)</li>
<li>
Analyze the ROI for marketing initiatives/campaigns, evaluating and troubleshoot performance of campaigns
</li>
<li>Development and implementation of the Brand strategy</li>
<li>
Developing the go to market and lifecycle management strategy for new and existing products</li>
<li>
Define through primary research and KOL development the voice of the customer</li>
<li>
Monitor and improve the website and digital presence through SEO</li>
<li>
Partner with Sales Force Effectiveness team to ensure marketing strategy is fully integrated into a defined sales process that is measureable and repeatable
</li>
<li>Partner with the company’s Sales team; enabling them to meet their commercial objectives by providing them with appropriate tools, materials and presentations</li>
<li>
Manage and develop Product Marketing Managers and Associates where applicable
</li>
<li>Other projects as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
