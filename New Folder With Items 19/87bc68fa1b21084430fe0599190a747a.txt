Oldcastle Coastal, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. <p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Description Summary</b><br>
</p>
<p>
This position is responsible for supporting the accounting functions in Accounts Receivable. Duties include, but are not limited to the following:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Contact customers for payment on past due accounts</li>
<li>
Process credit applications</li>
<li>
New customer set up and account maintenance</li>
<li>
Process credit memos to customers</li>
<li>
Respond to inquiries from customers, and research/resolve outstanding credit issues</li>
<li>
Maintain credit invoice and supporting documentation files</li>
<li>
Monitor credit holds in Account Receivable</li>
<li>
Respond to inquiries from sites and sales team</li>
<li>
Salesforce.com case maintenance</li>
<li>
Maintain unapplied payment report</li>
<li>
Process release of lien requests</li>
<li>
Process credit references</li>
<li>
Process sworn statement of account requests</li>
<li>
Handle customer payments received in house and send to lockbox when necessary</li>
<li>
Process tax exempt certificates</li>
<li>
Work with salespeople on their accounts/collections</li>
<li>
Handle notice to owner issues</li>
<li>
Maintain assigned customer accounts; reconciling and resolving open balances</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities related to the nature of the job may be assigned on a temporary or permanent basis as needed<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements/Education/Experience</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>
At least 2 years related experience</li>
<li>
Detail-oriented, strong organizational skills</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills</li>
<li>
Computer proficiency in Windows, Word, Excel and Power Point</li>
<li>
Excellent telephone skills</li>
<li>
Team player – ability to share information within the department and company</li>
</ul>
