<h2>Regional Sales Manager (Higher Education)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Collaborative Solutions is a leading global Finance and HR Transformation consultancy that leverages world-class cloud solutions to help deliver successful outcomes for its customers. As one of the longest-tenured consulting partners with Workday, Collaborative Solutions has a 98% customer satisfaction rate and has executed over 550 Workday Financial Management, Human Resources, and Payroll deployments for global Fortune 500 companies, medium-sized businesses, and education and government institutions.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are seeking a Regional Sales Manager who understands, or can ramp quickly to understand, the competitive landscape of our business. This individual should be competitive by nature and possess a hunger for human interaction. He/she should also be an exceptional listener and possess a high sense of urgency. This individual will also have experience specifically selling into the higher education marketplace.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop/plan your sales strategy and effectively position the company’s services
</li>
<li>Cultivate mutually beneficial relationships with Workday and develop in-person relationships with all in-region field offices
</li>
<li>Run complex sales cycles managing prospects and internal staff
</li>
<li>Manage existing customer accounts to determine future revenue opportunities
</li>
<li>Manage multiple, concurrent deals cycles maintaining accurate and timely customer, pipeline, and forecast data
</li>
<li>Manage to a quarterly sales quota
</li>
<li>Provide support for marketing activities and events
</li>
<li>Work with the Delivery Team to develop estimates for work and position us ahead of Collaborative competitors
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Skills and Requirements:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional presentation skills with the ability to present to all organization levels
</li>
<li>Ability to work with customer and internal team members to estimate services needs
</li>
<li>Ability to understand and effectively communicate the benefits of an on-demand/web services architecture
</li>
<li>Proven ability in managing complex sales cycles from start to finish with a track record of successful revenue attainment
</li>
<li>Familiarity with consultative selling methodologies
</li>
<li>Proven success in selling complex services and solutions in the higher education market
</li>
<li>Experience with Excel, Word and PowerPoint as well as using SaaS applications
</li>
<li>Ability to travel 50 to 75% to customer and workday locations for effective relationship development
</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree is required; a master's degree is preferred
</li>
</ul>
