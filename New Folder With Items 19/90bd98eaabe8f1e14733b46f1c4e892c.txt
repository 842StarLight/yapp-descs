<h2>Entry Level Leasing Consultant - Gold Mountain Village Apartments (Central City, CO)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>We are seeking a Leasing Consultant with a strong desire to make our residents feel at home. Ideal candidates are outgoing, have a contagious energy, and are interested in learning the business of property management. No experience is needed as we are willing to train.</p>
<p><b>Where is the job located?</b> Gold Mountain Village Apartments (Central City, CO)</p>
<p><b>Why work for Mission Rock? </b>Mission Rock Residential is a fast-growing property management company looking for fantastic, customer service driven team members. With growth comes advancement opportunities!</p>
<p><b>Great benefits! </b>Mission Rock Residential offers excellent medical, dental and vision insurance to include a Flexible Spending Account and Health Savings Account. We provide company paid life insurance along with paid short and long-term disability insurance, 11 paid holidays, vacation, sick time, and a 401k program with match. You are eligible to receive a discount on Verizon phone service and accessories (even if you are already on a Verizon plan). You may also be eligible to receive a 30% discount on rent if you live at the property that you work or a 20% discount if you choose to live at a sister property that we manage. These discounts do not apply to all of our locations.</p>
<p><b>The Job:</b> Our Leasing Consultants tour the community with prospective residents and support the overall property operations. Specific responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lease and pre-lease units</li>
<li>Assist current residents</li>
<li>Ensure model apartments are ready for show</li>
<li>Help maintain a neat and tidy office/clubhouse</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The Details:</b> Our Leasing Consultants should have:</p>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Previous sales and customer service experience</li>
<li>A positive attitude</li>
<li>Basic computer skills including familiarity with Microsoft Suite</li>
<li>Previous leasing experience (experience with community software programs a plus!)</li>
<li>Work hours are Monday to Friday / 8am - 5pm</li>
</ul>
<p><i>*** Employment with Mission Rock is contingent upon the successful completion of a drug test and background check ***</i></p>
