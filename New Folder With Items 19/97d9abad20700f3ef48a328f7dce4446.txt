<p><b>
Summary</b><br>
Our team is growing! Our Bind Technical Implementation Manager is responsible for managing and executing the setup of a new client across functional teams while working closely with both internal and external partners and actively mitigating risks and issues to ensure a successful system implementation. This role is part of an implementation team responsible for transitioning a customer from sales to active customer with the goal of building outstanding client relationships at Bind.</p>
<div>
<b>
Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Upon notification of sale, work closely with the Project Implementation Manager to capture all client details/requirements needed by each internal key implementation resource.</li>
<li>Use data and information provided by both internal and external partners to complete the technical build/configuration of a client to allow ongoing administration and support.</li>
<li>Ensure system configuration meets client requirements.</li>
<li>Overall accountability for the configuration and testing for clients.</li>
<li>Interact with project, product, and development resources throughout the company to establish and maintain knowledge of the products as well as enhancements and fixes, especially those relative to assigned clients.</li>
<li>Support the creation of standard policies and procedures that are effective, scalable and repeatable.</li>
<li>Work closely with internal resources to resolve complex issues relating to products and functionality especially those relative to assigned clients.</li>
<li>Work with all Bind teams/functions to identify, develop and improve processes for the implementation and administration of clients.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Requirements</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A BA/BS or equivalent experience</li>
<li>2+ years of experience implementing/supporting self-fund and/or fully-insured medical insurance administration</li>
<li>Detail oriented with the ability to manage a high volume of details within short and inflexible timeframes</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience in a healthcare environment with HIPAA requirements</li>
<li>Strong critical thinking, troubleshooting and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Highly organized with an ability to prioritize multiple tasks across multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Ability to work collaboratively at all levels and roles within an organization</li>
<li>Enjoy working in a fast-paced technical environment</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, including Microsoft Office Suite, and JIRA</li>
<li>Ability to quickly learn new software and functionality of new products</li>
<li>Self-starter with the ability to work with minimal direct supervision</li>
<li>Experience working with a start-up a plus</li>
</ul>
