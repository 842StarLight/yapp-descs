<b>The Company</b><br>
<br>
Mattamy Homes is the largest privately owned homebuilder in North America with operations across Canada and the United States. From a humble beginning in 1978, starting with the building and sale of a single home, Mattamy has gone on to build more than 90,000 homes in hundreds of communities across North America. In Canada, those communities stretch across the Greater Toronto Area, as well as in Ottawa, Calgary and Edmonton, while in the United States, the company is represented in nine metropolitan areas: Minneapolis-St. Paul, Charlotte, Phoenix, Tucson, Jacksonville, Orlando, Tampa, Sarasota and Naples. The scope of the company’s operations encompasses land acquisition, community design and development, housing and parkland design, and construction, with particular emphasis on creating communities of enduring value to homeowners.<br>

<div><b>The Opportunity</b></div>
<div>
The Talent Acquisition Manager will be accountable to lead the core processes of recruitment for the organization, as well as being the lead recruiter in providing end to end service for our internal clients. The successful candidate will have a strategic mindset with a practical approach and must be ‘hands on’ in providing full cycle recruitment service of the all day-to-day requirements in recruiting. Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Lead the recruiting function through leadership of systems and processes with an eye for continuous improvement</li>
<li>
Directly service full cycle recruitment needs for positions at all levels across the organization including;</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li> In partnership with the Senior Human Resources Manager and hiring manager, establish a recruiting strategy including target markets, the use of advertising or social media, referrals, or external recruiting agencies</li>
<li> Develop internal and external job postings
</li>
<li> Screen candidates (resume and telephone screening)
</li>
<li> Prepare a short list of candidates and prepare candidate summaries for the hiring manager
</li>
<li> Coordinate the selection process including interviews, testing and reference checking</li>
<li> Conduct thorough reference checks and coordinate background checks if applicable</li>
<li> Facilitate the final decision, prepare the details of the offer and present to the candidate and work with the hiring manager in preparation for orientation
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop working partnerships with managers to understand business requirements</li>
<li>
Develop recruitment strategies and coach managers in developing their skills in assessment and selection of candidates
</li>
<li>Manage entry level hiring strategies through building partnerships with educational institutions, participating in career fairs and conducting the ongoing recruitment process for Co-op students of various disciplines
</li>
<li>Maintain and analyze recruitment metrics</li>
<li>
Manage external partnerships with recruitment agencies and consultants</li>
<li>
Develop standardized recruitment processes, interviews guides and templates</li>
<li>
Participate as a member of the larger HR team in pursuing assigned HR accountabilities and projects, as well as relevant support in overall goals of the client groups.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned, including assistance with HR team projects<br>
</li>
</ul>

Education and Experience</b></div>

Experience working with an applicant tracking system.</li>
