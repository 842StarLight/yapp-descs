<p><b>
Job Description</b></p>
<p>
Anaqua is looking for a highly motivated IP Client Support Specialist who is eager to help us attain our continuing growth goals. The Client Solution Specialist will play a key role in launching a new offering for Anaqua, working closely with our patent clients around the world to support their analytics processing using our Acclaim software and services. This is a demanding role that requires exceptional analytical, customer relationship, consulting, and technology skills. We are looking for candidates that are truly passionate about supporting our users on a daily basis, while leveraging their patent analytics strengths!</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sales Engineering: Work with the Sales team and client prospects to develop solutions to outline capabilities and meet prospect requirements</li>
<li>New Client Onboarding: Implement the Acclaim product for clients, and train them on patent analytics in general and the Acclaim product in specific</li>
<li>Client Support: Work closely with our Client Success team to provide outstanding support to clients for their ongoing Acclaim product needs and questions</li>
<li>Client Consultation Projects: Produce customized reports for our clients, including patent analytics, landscape analysis, and decision support for licensing targeting, annuity decisions, pruning, etc.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Required Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Deep understanding of patent data content, including searchable patent and PAIR data, patent search use cases, and patent</li>
<li>Sales and/or client-facing support experience for a software product or SaaS solution analytics competitor landscape</li>
<li>Legal experience and/or Law Degree a plus</li>
<li>Solution-oriented consultative approach to working with clients</li>
<li>Self-starter with the ability to work independently in a dynamic, fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills, and the ability to organize and present technical material in an easily understood manner both verbally and written</li>
<li>High energy, positive attitude, with the ability to think creatively on your feet and handle multiple priorities</li>
<li>Ability to travel (up to 10%)</li>
</ul>
