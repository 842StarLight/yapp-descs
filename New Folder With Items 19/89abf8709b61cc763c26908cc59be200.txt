<b>Position Summary</b><br>
The Commissions Analyst will be responsible for aligning sales compensations and sales plan structures with overall business goals. Creating compensations plans for the sales reps, technical sales, and sales executives for multiple product lines. Delivering and explaining the specific plans. Calculating monthly and quarterly payments for those plans. Resolving questions about the plan and the treatment of individual sales, as they arise.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Partner with sales VPs and Execs in crafting sales compensation plans that align with leaderships goals.</li>
<li>Create, distribute, and collect the various compensation plans.</li>
<li>Calculate the monthly and quarterly payouts.</li>
<li>Be able to communicate different commission structures on different business lines to the sales representatives.</li>
<li>Work with Legal, Finance, and Sales Operations, to determine the meaning of contract language and to correctly or ensure proper set up in the payment and incentive systems.</li>
<li>Designs, builds, tests, and promotes new commission plans into Excel and Salesforce production environment. This person will work with others in Finance and Sales Ops to determine what system will be the best fit for Perforce.</li>
<li>Works with multiple teams in multiple regions to ensure monthly performance commission plans are setup and working properly.</li>
<li>Provides month-end reporting to Finance and CEO.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Required Education, Experience and Skills</b>
</div>

<li>Salesforce experience</li>
<li>Expert Excel experience</li>
<li>Ability to communicate to all levels within an organization and hold people accountable to their deliverables.</li>
<li>Python coding experience is unexpected but a plus</li>
</ul>
