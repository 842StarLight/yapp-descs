<h2>Senior Manager, Price Pack Architecture</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Imagine</b>…working for a company that knows that its people are the key to its success in the marketplace. A company in which achieving extraordinary results and having a stimulating work experience are part of the same process.</p>
<p></p>
<p>We cultivate and embrace a diverse employee population. We recognize that people with diverse backgrounds, experiences and perspectives fuel our growth and enrich our global culture.</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are looking for an individual who enjoys working in a fast-paced, team oriented environment, likes to be challenged, and values the opportunity to make a difference.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Senior Manager, Price Pack Architecture, will be charged with creating strategies to drive new price pack architecture solutions across the Campbell’s Soup Company portfolio in a manner that generates consumer demand to unlock growth opportunities against our core brands.</p>
<p></p>
<p>****This position can be based in Camden, NJ / Norwalk, CT and/or Charlotte North Carolina</p>
<p></p>
<p>Major Responsibilities
</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead the development of multi-channel PPA strategies across the entire Meals &amp; Beverages and Snacking Portfolios</li>
<li>Strategic recommendations to include price point optimizations, channel strategy and pipeline development of white space innovation opportunities</li>
<li>Build the PPA capability for Campbell’s new Center of Excellence, including development of a toolkit of guidelines, frameworks, and tools to enable business teams to successfully develop &amp; execute PPA strategies</li>
<li>Collaborate with business partners throughout the PPA process from strategy creation through in-market execution</li>
<li>The Senior Manager, Price Pack Architecture, will embrace and embody the Core Values of Campbell. He or she will partner seamlessly with internal stakeholders and external resources to deliver outstanding results. We are seeking a talented commercial professional who thrives in a high-performing environment that encourages creativity and risk-taking.
</li>
<li>This leader will serve as a ‘price pack expert’ embedded into a Center of Excellence. He/she will lead the develop a price pack architecture strategy across our robust portfolio of iconic brands to deliver against identified business objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p>Minimum Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>A strong commercial orientation with a track record of marketing success.
</li>
<li>8+ years of experience in a sophisticated environment within brand marketing or sales roles in a leading consumer packaged goods (CPG) company or other companies with strong marketing and brand pedigrees.
</li>
<li>3 to 5 years of demonstrated price pack architecture experience. The candidate will have experience developing frameworks that combine consumer insights and competitive information to enable the identification of price pack opportunities across a product portfolio. We are looking for a leader with a highly analytical and operational skill-set with a track record of designing new product variations based on consumer demand that have delivered against financial expectations.</li>
</ul>
<p>Critical Competencies for Success
</p>
<ul>
<li>Commercial Experience: The ideal candidate will have proven themselves as a marketer and/or sales leader with a track record of successful progression in another organization(s). They will bring a complete commercial functional skill set including: Strategy development, brand positioning and vision, product innovation, segmentation, insight development, communication development, visual identity channel marketing, advertising and promotions and customer engagement. Combined with a consumer first orientation, the strong operational and analytical skill-set that this candidate brings will enable the ability to methodically analyze product, channel, and pricing issues across our core categories and develop clear, compelling, fact based recommendations on product variations by channel.</li>
<li>Collaboration with Colleagues: The ideal candidate will possess an open and collaborative style. He or she will be able to work closely with peers to use consumer insights and price pack strategy as an enabler for all aspects of our business. The successful candidate will be an excellent listener who demonstrates a natural ability to lead. He or she must be able to enroll others with a compelling vision and inspire them to achieve beyond expectations. This individual should be a charismatic leader who is comfortable managing through ambiguity in a fast-moving, global environment. He/she is a resourceful self-starter who understands how to make things happen through people, while demonstrating a hands-on approach. He/she is a team player who readily shares credit and takes pride in shared success.
</li>
<li>Comprehensive Price Pack Architecture Skills: The ideal candidate will have proven success in leading innovative price pack architecture solutions. This includes the ability to ideate, validate, and commercialize on a set of opportunities leveraging an analytical approach that is more focused on making strategic ‘tweaks’ based on key attributes to existing products in our core categories versus build-from-scratch product innovations.
</li>
<li>Interpersonal/Influencing Skills: The successful candidate will possess strong interpersonal abilities and a down- to-earth and straightforward style; he or she will be highly credible, persuasive, and one who engenders trust. The ideal candidate is articulate, confident and able to inspire people in terms of personal style. He/she possesses high integrity, adaptability and flexibility, and is a respected leader who is seen as a catalyst in the organization who can organize, orient and motivate cross functional teams globally. This professional possesses a high level of emotional intelligence and interfaces fluidly with a range of personalities and functions. He/she has strong written and verbal presentation skills and has the ability to simplify the complex. He/she is able to present and support his or her point of view while considering the input of others.
</li>
<li>Campbell Values: The successful candidate will possess a strong ‘spike’ on ‘Dare To Disrupt’ with an experiment fast and learn sooner mindset. They are comfortable with being uncomfortable and possess a natural ability to get close and understand consumers and customers.
</li>
</ul>
