<p><b>Business Analyst</b></p>
<p><b>
WheresMyConcrete, A Division of U.S. Concrete</b></p>
<p><b>
Dallas Fort Worth</b></p>
<p>
WheresMyConcrete WMC provides innovative, cloud-based software solutions to the concrete industry that focus on efficiency, customer experience, and mobility. Products include dispatching, electronic billing, mobile tickets, sales tools, enhanced analytics, and live customer interaction. WMC utilizes modern web and mobile technologies in an agile, iterative environment. WMC is the software division of U.S. Concrete NASDAQ: USCR and services customers across the United States and Australia.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary
</b></p>
<p>The Business Analyst works directly with customers and internal team members to implement, train, support and define feature enhancements for the WMC product line. Success in this role requires hands-on involvement in all aspects of the application lifecycle management and strong relationships with fellow peers and managers across the business.</p>
<p>
This position will report directly to the Business Analyst Manager.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work closely with our Customers to support, develop, and implement scalable, robust solutions that increase efficiencies and improve the user experience</li>
<li>
Accountable for exploring, capturing, understanding, enhancing and documenting business requirements. Assignments can cover any business function and are unlimited in scope and complexity.</li>
<li>
Formulates and defines systems scope and objectives based on both business needs and a thorough understanding of IT systems and industry requirements.</li>
<li>
Develops training materials and conducts training sessions for end users as required.</li>
<li>
Oversees multiple high-visibility projects and initiatives, coordinating schedules and stakeholder communications with customers.</li>
<li>
Leads and manages project teams and works closely with customers.</li>
<li>
Works with and assist in managing vendors and contract resources.</li>
<li>
Supports day to day systems management, including new installs, new acquisitions, upgrading and troubleshooting of WMC.</li>
<li>
Monitors industry and technical trends, assessing impacts on current and future applications.</li>
<li>
Monitors project status and completion daily to ensure projects are on track and moving towards completion.</li>
<li>
Identifies and proactively mitigates project issues and risks.</li>
<li>
Travels occasionally as required.</li>
<li>
Other duties as required</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Functional experience in at least one business discipline. Must be able to understand and design basic business processes.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple tasks and projects simultaneously in a cross functional environment.</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication skills; able to manage meetings, lead project teams, train users and communicate effectively with senior management.</li>
</ul>
