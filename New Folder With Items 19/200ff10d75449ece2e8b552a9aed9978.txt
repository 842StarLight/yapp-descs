Saratoga Grove-Retirement and Assisted Living community in Downers Grove, is seeking a Full-Time Community Relations Director-Assisted Living Sales. We are seeking candidates who shares our vision &amp; commitment for enhancing the lives of our clients. This is an excellent career opportunity for individuals who are truly passionate about providing care for the elderly!
<br><br>
Description
<br><br>
The primary purpose of your job position is to build and grow census by developing, selling, marketing and providing prospective residents and/or responsible parties with appropriate information and assistance in choosing an Independent/Assisted Living Campus. .
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Summary of essential job functions
<br><br>
Responsible for accurately capturing and analyzing all inquiries to the facility via the prescribed tracking vehicle according to corporate standards.
<br>Submits press release ideas to Director of Communications regarding all newsworthy items and events of public interest.
<br>Submits weekly written reports to Executive Director and RMD on the status of move in’s and marketing activities for the week.
<br>Practices prudent fiscal management and closely monitors budget for marketing purposes.
<br>Develops and coordinates community-based education seminars and lectures. Invites local community and church groups to utilize space for meetings.
<br>Advises the Executive Director, Department Directors/Supervisors, etc., regarding the specific public relations matters of the facility.
<br>Regularly and customarily spends time in selling. Maintains a thorough knowledge of the centers, products and services, acuity, capabilities and referral relationships.
<br>Maintain an active involvement in the community, organization and associations. Example: Chamber of Commerce, local area agency on aging, senior groups, churches.
<br>Must be willing to work one weekend a month and as needed for special events.
<br>Skills necessary
<br><br>
A Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited college/university or equivalent preferred.
<br>Must possess three to five years sales experience, preferably in health care services.
<br>Must possess a thorough knowledge of principles of effective communication, mass media, advertising, policies, education, community relations, demonstration, organization structure, social and activity services, government relations etc., as they relate to senior living.
<br>Must possess the ability to plan, organize and effectively present ideas and concepts to community groups/agencies.
<br>Must possess the ability to communicate effectively, orally, and in writing.
<br>Must possess the ability to assimilate information from a variety of sources, analyze information and make recommendations to the General Manager.
<br>Must possess the ability to establish, implement, and maintain effective working public relations.
<br>Must be able to push, pull, move and/or lift a minimum of 50 pounds to a minimum height of 3 feet.
