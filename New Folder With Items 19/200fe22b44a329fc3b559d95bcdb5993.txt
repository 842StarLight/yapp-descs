<h2>Account Executive - Online Division</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Grand Canyon Education, formerly part of Grand Canyon University, became its own entity in July 2018 after the HLC approved GCU’s application to become a nonprofit institution. GCE will work in close partnership with GCU. Moreover, many positions with GCE are located on GCU’s main campus.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>If you’re passionate about education and have an entrepreneurial spirit, consider <b>an Account Executive position </b>with Grand Canyon Education, Inc. as your next exciting career move. GCE’s Online Division is seeking a self-motivated, business development professional to build awareness of our online academic programs for education, healthcare and business within the <b>Escondido, CA </b>territory. You will focus on building relationships with businesses/organizations to expand our reach to prospective students. You’ll also take an active role in supporting prospective students through the admissions process to guide them on the right path towards a successful online learning experience.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Starting salary is $65,000 a year.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work remotely within your assigned territory, managing and implementing strategies to increase prospective student inquiries.
</li>
<li>Cold calling key decision makers at prospective businesses/organizations to build relationships and create a pipeline of prospective students.
</li>
<li>Create and execute event/activity plans, which includes conducting informational, prospective student presentations.
</li>
<li>Counsel students on the enrollment process from lead generation through first few classes
</li>
<li>Maintain and grow inquiry and account databases.
</li>
<li>Significant regional travel within assigned territory.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3 years’ demonstrated success in outside business development, consultative sales or account management experience. Prior higher education experience a plus.
</li>
<li>A bachelor’s degree (highly preferred) or 5 years of related experience with a minimum of 60 college credits completed and progressing toward an undergraduate degree.
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and sales presentation skills with strong verbal and written communication skills.
</li>
<li>An entrepreneurial and determined attitude in order to meet performance targets, and the confidence to work independently without daily oversight.
</li>
<li>Computer/office software proficiency including MS Office Suite and CRM tools.
</li>
<li>Requires use of a personal vehicle, a valid driver's license and clean driving record.
</li>
<li>Cannot be delinquent or in default on a federal student loan as verified in your credit report. If in a rehabilitation program, your credit report must reflect that you are current, not delinquent, on your payments.
</li>
<li>Provide a positive example to students by supporting the University’s Doctrinal Statement, Ethical Position Statement and Mission of Grand Canyon University.
</li>
<li><b>Ability to work a flexible schedule including evening and weekend hours.</b></li>
</ul>
</div>
