<h2>Director, Voice of the Customer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>We are looking for a Voice of Customer expert to lead the company-wide Voice of Customer (VOC) program for LogMeIn.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The ideal candidate is an expert with proven success driving the architecture and implementation of a comprehensive VOC Program across all stages of the customer experience, inclusive of customer interaction, data analytics, closed-loop feedback, and multi-departmental project management.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The VOC Program Manager will be integral to both program design and implementation and ongoing management including identifying and driving business improvements necessary to achieve superior customer experience. The VOC Lead should be comfortable collaborating across departments and influencing the Executive Team to develop and evolve a compelling and differentiated customer experience.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
As a Program Manager for the Voice of Customer team you will deploy a centralized VOC platform and develop and champion the VOC program across the enterprise to gather customer feedback and drive action through insights. In addition, you will be responsible for developing an end-to-end survey strategy and consolidating external customer surveys at a transactional and relationship level and centralizing surveys on a single platform.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This is a hands-on role that requires personal initiative, curiosity, and an energetic desire to build an impactful VOC program</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The successful candidate will have experience in journey mapping, leading survey programs, developing unbiased best-practice survey questions, ensuring representative samples, developing rules to manage survey fatigue, closed-loop feedback processes, and designing/delivering reports on survey findings.</p>
<p>
The role requires an understanding of how to manage and deliver whole projects with minimal guidance while working with large matrix organization. Part of the role will be to champion the VOC program across the company, get buy in and participation from the BU teams and executives.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Own the development and delivery of the overall Voice of Customer Program across all of LogMeIn</li>
<li>
Design and deploy processes and systems to deliver and act on insight and enable close loop workflows</li>
<li>
Collaborate with BU and product teams to gain consistency on the approach and execution of the external survey strategy</li>
<li>
Develop and track VOC metrics in order to be able to communicate the impact of the program to the business</li>
<li>
Coordinate across own project team, and with other project teams; report on risks and issues</li>
<li>
Work closely and effectively with internal and external stakeholders, and build strong alliances by deploying strong team-working, organizational, communication, and customer service skills</li>
<li>
Effectively identify and communicate blockers and opportunities for improvement to operational workflows.</li>
<li>
Acts as an advocate for the VOC program and the interests of the customer</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience driving Voice of Customer/survey programs</li>
<li>
Experience with survey software and question design is essential</li>
<li>
Customer focused - Experience driving projects for customer focused lines of business such as sales, support, services, or success</li>
<li>
Experience in developing and managing complex programs in matrixed organizations</li>
<li>
Demonstrates ability to transform survey data into actionable insights and ensure closed-loop follow-up with customers</li>
<li>
Energetic, creative and persistent</li>
<li>
Amazing Communicator – Ability to communicate effectively across the experience / management spectrum, can ‘translate’ between technical and non-technical teams, and can empathetically communicate a customer’s pain to internal LogMeIn teams</li>
<li>
Results Driven. Proven ability to measure impacts of VOC programs, including Net Promoter and CSAT scores</li>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree with a technical or business focus preferred, applicable and relevant experience will be considered.</li>
</ul>
