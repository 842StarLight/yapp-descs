<h2>Mortgage Loan Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Alpha Mortgage is growing! We are seeking EXPERIENCED Mortgage Loan Officers for several locations in NC including Fayetteville and Wilmington. Candidates must be an NC Licensed Mortgage Loan Officer. </b></p>
<p>The Loan Officer will be responsible for discussing client’s needs, recommending the best loan products, helping customers put together a complete loan package and working with the underwriting team throughout the loan process while adhering to all mortgage compliance laws and regulations. The Loan Officer acts as a liaison between clients and Alpha Mortgage and will help qualified applicants acquire loans in a timely manner.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Attract new mortgage loan applications by developing relationships within the community, specifically with the real estate community; making sales calls to prospective customers.</li>
<li>Meet with customers to determine their needs and recommend the right loan products to help meet their goals.</li>
<li>Maintain an active knowledge base of all loan products and an understanding of the qualifications required of each applicant.</li>
<li>Evaluate credit worthiness by processing loan applications and documentation within specified limits.</li>
<li>Identify and recommend products that meets the customer’s needs and the company’s lending guidelines.</li>
<li>Communicate with clients to request from and provide information to them. Maintain and update records.</li>
<li>Review active loan files each day to determine if any documents are missing or what can be done to help the process.</li>
<li>Demonstrate exemplary customer service to build clients’ trust, loyalty and satisfaction throughout the loan process.</li>
<li>Develop referral networks to attract more clients.</li>
<li>Operate in compliance with laws and regulations and adhere to mortgage lending compliance guidelines.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Current NC Loan Originator license with NMLS</li>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Three years experience in the finance industry</li>
<li>Computer proficiency and familiarity with mortgage applications/software</li>
<li>Excellent communication and customer service skills</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Valid Drivers License</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor degree in Finance or related field</li>
<li>3 years Experience as a mortgage loan officer</li>
<li>Experience with Encompass</li>
<li>Sales experience</li>
<li>Bilingual - English and Spanish</li>
</ul>
