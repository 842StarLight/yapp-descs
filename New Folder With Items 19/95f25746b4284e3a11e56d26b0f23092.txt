<h2>Director, Web Management and Data Governance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description Summary</b></h1>
The Web Management and Data Governance role will be the corporate marketing leader and technical liaison for strategizing and leveraging marketing investments in BD web properties that enable the following BD marketing objectives:<br>
<ul>
<li>Commercial excellence: Support market activation through adoption and integration of marketing and enterprise platforms. Digital marketing commercial excellence platforms should scale to promote consistency and reuse of best-in-class digital market activation tactics across all BUs and regions.</li>
<li>Customer experience: Continuously evolve customer experience digital journey for public facing BD web properties by applying technology investments to support self-directed and evolving touch-free experience for BD customers.</li>
<li>Product launch excellence: Digital enablement for relevant stages of the product development lifecycle that require integration with BD web properties, and ecommerce workflows.</li>
<li>Digital enablement for market strategy planning: Collaborate in business unit market strategy planning process to ensure that web capabilities are aligned with go-to-market and market activation plans.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Additionally, this leader will ensure that BD’s web presence enables other essential operational, compliance, recruiting, legal, and corporate goals.<br>
<br>
In this capacity, the leader will work with business and functional content owners worldwide to publish a best-in-class web presence that supports commercial launch and growth strategies. Increasingly, bd.com must enable sales and marketing productivity through efforts that improve visitor experience, inform customer choices, and accelerate time-to-decision while building the BD brand.<br>
<br>
The leader should understand the medical technology industry while also demonstrating strategic insight into the larger ecosystem of our digital products and services. The ecosystem includes full stack marketing technology, enterprise IT, ERP, web-service architecture, database architecture, master data management, ecommerce, and web development. The leader will work with a cross-functional team to develop new ideas for improvements, enhance integrations across other digital disciplines, help enable analytics strategies, and support effectiveness across the entire web experience.<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and implement BD’s global, corporate web strategy, including dimensions around content, structure and navigation, search optimization, demand generation, community, education and awareness building, e-catalog and e-commerce, etc. Plan integration to align with customer experience initiatives</li>
<li>
Lead and maintain the core BD company website, including ~50 country websites and numerous other domains</li>
<li>
Lead web convergence activities as required during periods of integration (eg, acquisitions, mergers)</li>
<li>
Maintain accountability for periodic refreshes and improvements to web properties</li>
<li>
Develop, manage and deliver website testing strategy and online optimization roadmap for improved effectiveness</li>
<li>
Lead cross functional teams for site development, measurement, content development, testing, and deployment; analyze website behavior and performance metrics to develop user experience optimization and SEO.</li>
<li>
Oversee operational support of site and collaborate with IT to ensure high standard of technical performance and security. Lead systematic approach to managing mar-tech investments across the enterprise.</li>
<li>
Monitor performance KPIs for changes in trends; share insights and make recommendations to the marketing team</li>
<li>
Provide insights by working closely with Analytics leader, gathering qualitative and quantitative user feedback via multiple formats (e.g., onsite live feedback, heat maps, adobe analytics etc.)</li>
<li>
Verify and maintain the integrity of data sources and integration to promote reuse and inheritability across the digital ecosystem.</li>
<li>
Partner closely with Brand and Product Marketing, the Marketing &amp; Sales Enablement team, and other stakeholders on web initiatives</li>
<li>
Create useful roadmaps and reports for the team, management and executive levels</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
