<h2>Mechanical Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>TITLE: </b> Mechanical Engineer</p>
<p><b>REPORTS TO: </b> Director of Engineering</p>
<p><b>DEPT</b>: Engineering</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY OF DUTIES: </b> Prepare designs for components or complex custom products working from customer supplied data, engineering specifications, simple sketches or verbal instruction utilizing CADD software.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES: </b> 90%</p>
<p>Create BIM models with a high level of accuracy and detail with minimal supervision utilizing Revit software</p>
<p>Coordinate with our clients models, obtain Architectural and MEP models, link into our model, assist in the exchange of information with the entire design team, including attending client meetings to support projects and sales.</p>
<p>Work within an engineering team to revise and finalize component and product design/build drawings</p>
<p>Prepare documentation packages for manufacture and assembly and support the production floor on a daily basis to ensure they have what is needed to efficiently build projects to established engineering designs.</p>
<p>Revise drawings required</p>
<p>Meet required deadlines</p>
<p>Catalog final drawings in computer files as per company procedures</p>
<p><b>OTHER DUTIES: </b> 10%<br>Other reasonably-related duties</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b><br><b><i>Education</i></b>: BSME</p>
<p><b><i>Experience/Knowledge</i></b>:</p>
<p>Minimum 5 years mechanical design experience</p>
<p>Leading and managing MEP projects</p>
<p>Experience in the design of HVAC, hydronic piping systems and medical gas plumbing for various projects.</p>
<p>Hands-on mechanical assembly, fabrication, or repair experience a plus</p>
<p>Proficiency with AutoCAD / Revit</p>
<p>Extensive Knowledge of Revit drafting principles, practices, methods and techniques</p>
<p>Knowledge of Revit families, how to create and modify them.</p>
<p>Ability to create and customize Revit to advance in the development of it, is strongly desirable</p>
<p>Provide specification, design of HVAC and plumbing for architectural projects within defined budgets</p>
<p>Working knowledge of manufacturing theory and process</p>
<p>Familiarity with project planning, milestones, and deadlines</p>
<p>Demonstrates progressively more responsible work history</p>
<p><b><i>Abilities Skills</i></b></p>
<p>Excellent CADD, Microsoft Office Suite, and Internet research skills</p>
<p>Mechanical ability and visual aptitude</p>
<p>Professional manner with excellent interpersonal skills</p>
<p>Solid work ethic</p>
<p>Good judgement<br>  Excellent organizational and process skills</p>
<p>Critical thinking and problem-solving skills</p>
<p>Communication skills (speaking, listening, writing, grammar)<br>  Detail-oriented with high degree of accuracy<br>  Team-oriented to achieve company objectives<br>  Ability to deal with rapidly-shifting priorities</p>
<p><b>SUPERVISION REQUIRED: </b> Minimal</p>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES: </b> None</p>
<p><b>WORK SCHEDULE/ENVIRONMENT: </b> Full-time, Monday through Friday, 7:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., primarily in an office environment.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
