<h2>Pharmacy Help Desk Call Center Team Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support the Pharmacy Helpdesk Call Center Supervisor to monitor all incoming calls.</li>
<li>Support the Pharmacy Helpdesk Call Center Supervisor in running daily report in order to evaluate call center performance.</li>
<li>Support the Pharmacy Helpdesk Call Center Supervisor in setting the standard for team behavior by acting as a role model and mentor.</li>
<li>Support the Pharmacy Helpdesk Call Center Supervisor to Provide/Complete report, as requested.</li>
<li>Assist in coordinating resolution of situations with pharmacies, members or clients as outlined in policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Assist in the supervision and tracking of daily call center operational process and procedures and assures the improvement of quality and efficiency of the department.</li>
<li>Answer incoming pharmacy calls regarding claim transaction issues, reimbursement issues, payment issues, and others in accordance with PharmPix Pharmacy Helpdesk Call Center performance metrics.</li>
<li>Answer incoming providers’ calls in accordance with PharmPix Pharmacy Helpdesk Call Center performance metrics.</li>
<li>Answer incoming beneficiaries’ call in accordance with PharmPix Pharmacy Helpdesk Call Center performance metrics.</li>
<li>Answer incoming calls and perform outbound calls according to HIPAA Law standards.</li>
<li>Verify the accuracy of the information with the pharmacy, provider and or beneficiary.</li>
<li>Document all inquiries in the Customer Service Management Application.</li>
<li>Route the case to the corresponding internal department or the appropriate Health Insurance Carrier.</li>
<li>Send an e-mail to the appropriate person in the appropriate department.</li>
<li>Talk with pharmacies, providers and beneficiaries while typing comments in the system for each call.</li>
<li>Educate providers and beneficiaries regarding the process of claim transmission, reimbursement a pharmacies and payment.</li>
<li>Make outbound calls to pharmacies, providers and beneficiaries if necessary.</li>
<li>Assists the Pharmacy Helpdesk Call Center Supervisor to perform the daily schedule for the alerts in BACMAN Alert Application to ensure proper management of rejections at point of sale.</li>
<li>Assists the Pharmacy Helpdesk Call Center Supervisor to perform the schedule for alerts such as DURs (Drug Utilization Reviews), HMO (Health Maintenance Organization) among other rejections evaluations required by business.</li>
<li>Special project and other duties assigned by the Pharmacy Helpdesk Call Center Supervisor.</li>
<li>Supports all Quality Management Program initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>TRAINING &amp; EDUCATION</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Pharmacy Associate or Technical Degree or;</li>
<li>ExCPT or PTCB certification.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>LICENSURE / CERTIFICATION</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Puerto Rico Board of Pharmacy Technician Certification (preferred).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-3 years as pharmacy technician in PBM, retail or Health Industry.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PROFESSIONAL COMPETENCIES</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Fully bilingual English and Spanish.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Time Management Skills: Establishing priorities and accomplishing tasks in a timely manner PC skill (Microsoft System)/System oriented.</li>
<li>Customer Service Leadership skills: practicing, valuing, and support service to both internal department and external clients.</li>
<li>Demonstrated effective organizational, interpersonal and communications skills.</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communications skills.</li>
<li>Skill in analyzing situations accurately and taking effective action.</li>
<li>Problem solving skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work independently and with minimal supervision, demonstrating initiative and</li>
<li>Ability to handle sensitive or confidential information is critical.</li>
<li>Team work: ability to work in a team environment.</li>
<li>Able to effectively interact with internal departments, PharmPix clients, members and other healthcare professionals.</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret documents, write reports and correspondence.</li>
<li>Speak effectively before groups of PharmPix clients.</li>
</ul>
