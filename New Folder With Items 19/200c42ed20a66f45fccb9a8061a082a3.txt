<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
This position is responsible for planning, implementing and managing the activities of the Commercial Installation work force for a designated district. Key liaison between District installation work force and Corporate Installation. Plans, implements and manages the activities of the installation work force and ensures familiarity and training of product skills.
<br><br>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
Tracks and records daily/monthly/quarterly commercial installation activity to include individual installers' efforts and results.
<br>Establishes, maintains and coordinates a high level of quality and timely installation to customers for maximum retention.
<br>Serves as the primary contact for the district installation work force to provide input and feedback related to Corporate Installation.
<br>Assists commercial sales force by developing low installation costs and suggesting proper and varied applications of systems and systems designed.
<br>Conducts meetings monthly. Communicates current installation policies and procedures, techniques, demonstration of equipment, new products and services, sales promotions and area advertising.
<br>Develops and maintains an effective installation work force through recruiting, hiring, training, motivating and coaching techniques.
<br>Key interactions with General Manager, Regional Operations Manager, engineers, fire planners, estimators and sales organization.
