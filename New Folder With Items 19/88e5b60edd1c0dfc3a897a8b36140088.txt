<h2>Senior Product Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Overview</p>
<p>The Product Designer is responsible for developing and delivering color trends, product design cads, production bitmaps for Nester’s accounts. They will manage color development of materials as needed. They will stay on top of new trends and innovations</p>
<p>The Product Designer will report to the EVP of Product and will support sales in servicing the needs of our customers.</p>
<p>Role and Responsibilities</p>
<p>1. Design</p>
<p>a. Interpretation and Idealization</p>
<p>i. Understand and explain benefits and features of designs &amp; yarns</p>
<p>ii. Why &amp; how the designs apply to their needs</p>
<p>iii. Cost implications of design through machinery and material</p>
<p>b. Execution</p>
<p>i. Production Prep for Designs in machine ready bitmaps</p>
<p>ii. Color combinations, sizing, layout</p>
<p>c. Brand, Private Label &amp; License Design &amp; Color Development</p>
<p>d. Report and track development through Nester Systems</p>
<p>2. Seasonal Trends</p>
<p>a. Ongoing research for trend, color, design</p>
<p>b. On demand presentation for customers, customization for their brand</p>
<p>c. New yarns &amp; benefits</p>
<p>d. New innovations</p>
<p>3. Color Development</p>
<p>a. Custom yarn color developments/tracking/approvals/set ups</p>
<p>b. Production supplier color development tracking/approvals/set ups</p>
<p>c. Work to curate and update in house standard yarn palettes</p>
<p>4. In house customer presentations for products and trends</p>
<p>Other Duties as assigned.</p>
<p>Potential travel</p>
<p>Qualifications and Education Requirements</p>
<p>· High school diploma or equivalency, some college courses, two years of textile design experience related to the area of assignment; or an equivalent combination of training and experience.</p>
<p>· Microsoft Office Suite: Outlook, Word, Excel and PowerPoint</p>
<p>· Demonstrated creative skills in utilizing Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Photoshop</p>
<p>· Cad Software experience, creating bitmap files</p>
<p>· Adaptability to change</p>
<p>· Organization, attention to details, meeting scheduled deadlines without prompting</p>
<p>Other Desired Skills:</p>
<p>1. Knowledge of textiles, including fiber, yarns, finishing</p>
<p>2. Knowledge of knit fabrics and how they are constructed</p>
<p>3. Strong verbal presentation skills</p>
<p>4. Knowledge of color theory, and the balance of color &amp; scale in textiles</p>
<p>5. Ability to visualize 2D designs as a 3D product</p>
<p>6. Ability to juggle multiple projects and work effectively in a time-sensitive, high pressure environment.</p>
<p>7. Attention to detail, time management and organizational skills.</p>
<p>8. Works well with a team or independently.</p>
<p>9. Ability to absorb information rapidly and accurately</p>
<p>10. Keen sense of trend/market direction</p>
