Title: Manager - Property Tax<br>
Location: Oklahoma City, OK<br>
Area of Practice:<br>
<ul>
<li>Business Personal Property – Evaluating the physical, economic and functional characteristics of the clients’ property as well as taxability and exemption opportunities</li>
<li>Real Property – Establishing fair valuations through comparisons with similar properties in the area, detailed appraisals and comprehensive review</li>
<li>Complex Industrial Property – Analyzing the clients’ facilities from the real property all the way down to the equipment level</li>
</ul>
<br>
As a Manager in the Property Tax consulting practice, you will be integral in managing all aspects of the annual property tax cycle including attending hearings, settlement conferences and value negotiations.<br>
<ul>
<li>Rendition filing with state and local tax offices</li>
<li>Analysis of preliminary appraisal and value negotiation</li>
<li>Representation in protest hearings</li>
<li>Processing of statements for payment</li>
<li>Computation and projection of monthly tax estimates</li>
<li>Diligence review for Acquisitions &amp; Divestitures</li>
<li>Tax planning and optimization</li>
<li>Consulting for specialized projects</li>
</ul>
<br>
What you will do:<br>
<ul>
<li>Perform detail reviews of asset data and supporting documentation for determining proper valuations for purposes of quantifying tax assessments</li>
<li>Review and manage client contract terms, scope of services and fees. Oversee client billing and ensures timely collection of receivables</li>
<li>Manipulate and analyze raw data from various accounting and reporting systems</li>
<li>Proactively secure tax abatements and exemptions</li>
<li>Address complexities and issues that arise from client operations</li>
<li>Manage the preparation, review and processing of tax documents for clients</li>
<li>Maintain direct contact with clients and various appraisal districts</li>
<li>Continuously develop tax expertise, analytical capabilities, and soft skills</li>
</ul>
