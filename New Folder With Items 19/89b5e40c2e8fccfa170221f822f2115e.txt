<p><b>Overview of Xylem</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Xylem (XYL) is a leading global water technology company committed to developing innovative technology solutions to the world’s water challenges. The Company’s products and services move, treat, analyze, monitor and return water to the environment in public utility, industrial, residential and commercial building services settings. Xylem also provides a leading portfolio of smart metering, network technologies and advanced infrastructure analytics solutions for water, electric and gas utilities. The Company’s more than 16,500 employees bring broad applications expertise with a strong focus on identifying comprehensive, sustainable solutions. Headquartered in Rye Brook, New York with 2017 revenue of $4.7 billion, Xylem does business in more than 150 countries through a number of market-leading product brands.</p>
<p>
The name Xylem is derived from classical Greek and is the tissue that transports water in plants, highlighting the engineering efficiency of our water-centric business by linking it with the best water transportation of all – that which occurs in nature. For more information, please visit us at <b>www.xylem.com</b>.</p>

<p><b>
Brief Description/Job Summary
</b></p>
<p>Sensus, a Xylem brand, seeks to hire a Marketing Communications Manager. The Marketing Communications Manager should be an energetic, creative, and strategic thinker who will champion marketing communications that breakthrough the clutter and build brand equity with customers across a diverse set of products and services.</p>
<p>
The right candidate thrives in a lean, high-energy environment. They are a collaborative self-starter with a history of directing people, developing scalable processes and successfully managing projects to completion. This candidate is the ultimate “strategic doer”, with deep knowledge of marketing strategy, communications, and implementation.</p>
<p>
This is an opportunity to develop breakthrough communications programs, have a direct personal impact on Sensus customers, prospects, and help accomplish the company’s revenue goals.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties/Principal Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>The Marketing Communications Manager will report to the Director of Marketing Communications. They will be responsible for developing and delivering multiple effective communications strategies and programs on time and on budget. The ability to successfully engage with the business and manage multiple priorities and stakeholders in a fast-paced environment is essential.</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with product marketing and sales leadership to set market strategy</li>
<li>Define and execute strategy for media, campaigns and tradeshows</li>
<li>Work collaboratively in a team environment to achieve goals</li>
<li>Build awareness and preference</li>
<li>Manage agency partners</li>
<li>Write simply and clearly across various channels</li>
<li>Manage your budget</li>
<li>Track competitors and market trends</li>
<li>Develop compelling content and engage in web and social media</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
