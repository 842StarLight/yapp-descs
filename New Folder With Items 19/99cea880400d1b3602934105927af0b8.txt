<p>ROLE DESCRIPTION:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Cloud Business Associate possesses an entry-level knowledge of SAP and partner software solutions and participates in sales cycles as a member of the virtual account team (VAT team) in support of the sales account strategy.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>A Cloud Business Associate interacts with the SAP sales teams, prospective and existing customers through meetings, solution presentations, and follow-up discussions. The primary role of the Cloud Business Associate is to help develop and support the Secure HANA Cloud offering across the different operational lines which include contracts, sales, and pricing. This involves understanding customer needs for driving change into the offering, designing presentations and white papers, and contributing to sales strategies. In addition to offering support, a Cloud business analyst collaborates with the global product/ solution management to plan and execute business development strategies.</p>
<p>This is a fast-paced environment and a nimble, results-oriented team who is looking for someone that is detail oriented and brings a positive attitude, ready to embrace the opportunity to work on high-profile projects.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Offering Support:</p>
<ul>
<li>Aid in composing and delivering sales presentations covering the Secure HANA Cloud and other NS2 related cloud offerings. These presentations will be primarily to the internal sales teams and presales teams in the field. The presentations must articulate the sales message, differentiate SAP, and leave a strong and positive impression to audiences.</li>
<li>Play a key role in the personalization of sales content to ensure delivery of a simple, appealing and compelling customer presentation.</li>
<li>Learn and demonstrate knowledge of SAP solutions and Regulated Industries and maintain credibility with prospective stakeholders.</li>
<li>Support white papers and content creation in support of customer needs.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively present to customers “remotely” using virtual technologies.</li>
<li>Effectively work alongside additional teams who are there to support advisor’s success. (partners, marketing, contracts and product management).</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Demand Generation:</p>
<ul>
<li>Support one-to-many sales and marketing events both on-site and remotely.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Operational Activities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Review and update contracts supporting the cloud offerings</li>
<li>Analyze Cloud Service Provider contracts to identify potential efficiencies that can be quickly adopted in our organization</li>
<li>Assist with developing pricing and revenue models to accelerate the sales cycle.</li>
<li>Review Cloud Service Provider spend rates to forecast costs over time.</li>
<br>
</ul>
