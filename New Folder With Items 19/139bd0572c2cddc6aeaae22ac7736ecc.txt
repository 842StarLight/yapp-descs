<h2>Scrum Master</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Responsible for effective collaboration, facilitation, and leadership of agile teams and managing projects that meets Auto strategic goals. Mentors others on agile and project management methodology. Supports continuous improvement.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Facilitates the sizing of stories during the planning sprint, backlog grooming, or sprint planning sessions for the team.</li>
<li>Mentor team and stakeholders on Agile delivery methodology as part of Agile delivery organization
</li>
<li>Develops and maintains a productive working relationship with business owners, product owners, project sponsors, senior leadership, vendors and key clients.</li>
<li>Establishing team level cadence and driving team level ceremonies (e.g. sprint planning, team retrospectives, backlog grooming, etc.)</li>
<li>Drive the reduction of technical debt on mission critical systems
</li>
<li>Work with the teams to drive the definition and refinement of the program and team backlogs.</li>
<li>Escalate to leadership where needed and where possible remove impediments for the team(s).</li>
<li>Define and maintain metrics that provide visibility to stakeholders on team-level progress and quality.</li>
<li>Act as a liaison to assigned program directors and stakeholders. Take responsibility for managing dependencies between the program team and third parties, release managers, or between the team and other scrum teams.</li>
<li>Provide real time feedback to the methodology team based on experiences with software delivery teams.</li>
<ul>
<li>Ensures scope, goals, milestones, budget, risk, status, change requests and critical issues are effectively communicated to the client and project team.</li>
<li>Use historical velocity and planned velocity to assist team with commitments</li>
<li>Conducts formal review with business sponsor at project completion to confirm acceptance and satisfaction.</li>
<li>Tracks and measures satisfaction levels
</li>
<li>Independently develops and implements process improvement recommendations</li>
<li>Generates appropriate communication, process and educational plans for mitigating the disruption of change while facilitating identification and removal of obstacles to change.</li>
<li>Identify and remove technical and/or organizational challenges standing in the way of allowing your team to reach its full potential.</li>
</ul>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 3 years of ScrumMaster experience</li>
<li>Certified ScrumMaster (CSM or related certification mandatory)</li>
<li>3+ years of information technology experience</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Business, Management, Information Systems or Engineering; or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Thorough understanding and experience working with both agile and waterfall methodologies on large scale, technically complex projects.</li>
<li>Ability to deal with multiple projects.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills - written and oral
</li>
<li>Can support up to 25% travel
</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of the software development lifecycle (SDLC).</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated as well as creative and efficient in proposing solutions to complex, time-critical problems.</li>
<li>Data driven analysis skills for measuring and managing delivery.</li>
<li>Controls a program level view and status reporting on multiple projects and programs at a time.</li>
<li>Able to use Clarity/Open Workbench tool to determine how the intersection of multiple projects and programs impacts the department.</li>
<li>Excellent team player with the ability to influence and negotiate.</li>
<li>Ability to understand and communicate technical issues.</li>
<li>Highly adaptable to changing environment.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills and ability to work with diverse teams.</li>
<li>Situational awareness of when to listen and when to be assertive.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with a high attention to detail.</li>
<li>Ability to keep team engaged and on task.</li>
</ul>
