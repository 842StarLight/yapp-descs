<h2>Senior Manager, Pyxis Central Pharmacy Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description Summary</b></h1>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h1>
<p><b>
SUMMARY:</b><br>
This role will lead the downstream commercial go-to-market coordination and execution for Pyxis Inventory Management solutions and support the Enterprise Inventory Optimization (EIO) solution focused on driving adoption and confirming successful delivery. This role will drive the enterprise solution approach as measured by committed contracts, competitive conversions, sales revenue, profitability, customer adoption and satisfaction.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
This role will have/develop deep working knowledge of Pyxis Logistics (and related products) and be able to demonstrate key features and describe customer benefits while also presenting its contribution to the EIO solution.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The position reports to the Director, Global &amp; US Solutions Marketing (EIO) and will require close cross functional collaboration across platforms and global customer service.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Market intelligence &amp; buying process —be the expert on the buyer, the role they play in the buying process, how they buy and their key buying criteria, and who/what will influence their decision.</li>
<li>
Understand the competitive landscape—be an expert on competition and how they are positioned. Develop competitive sale strategies and tools that drive competitive conversions and retention.</li>
<li>
In conjunction with the connected med management team, develop a marketing plan for the overall solution you support, including specific product elements as needed with key activities and budgets to support the retention of existing customers and the acquisition of new customers.</li>
<li>
Create the go-to-market plans and manage the cross-functional implementation of the plan. Go-To-Market responsibilities include:<ul>
<li>
Define commercialization goals, objectives, success criteria and metrics and measure results</li>
<li>
Identify market segments and value offer/positioning</li>
<li>
Collaborate with sales leadership to create customer target list</li>
<li>
Adjust pricing strategy to meet US region needs</li>
<li>
Execute market shaping activities as appropriate</li>
<li>
Ensure that LCR results/evidence is available prior to launch</li>
<li>
Develop sales training plan</li>
<li>
Collaborate with contracts and platform teams to ensure that all quote to cash requirements are met prior to launch</li>
<li>
Ensure that sales compensation aligns with go-to-market strategy</li>
<li>
Ensure ROI tool is kept current with analytics capabilities.</li>
<li>
Develop support tools/processes to increase sales productivity and effectiveness in field, i.e. WhiteBoard, PPT, HIT tools, ROI etc.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Collaborate with product management, marketing communications, and sales to develop product positioning and messaging that resonate with our target buyer and supports BD MMS Way of Selling</li>
<li>
Develop customer success material to highlight best practices, including testimonials, case studies, videos, and webinars.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with HEOR to ensure that evidence generation projects meet US region needs.</li>
<li>
Support tradeshow and safety center activities</li>
<li>
Support RFP content development</li>
<li>
Track sales results and forecast</li>
<li>
Provide upstream marketing teams with customer feedback</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
LEADERSHIP:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrate strong and clear accountability for project performance as part of a cross-functional team, developing excellent working relationships with all functions critical to product development excellence</li>
<li>
Demonstrate leadership, learning agility and action orientation when facing uncertainty. Instill confidence through leadership and actions.</li>
<li>
Conduct and encourage frequent formal and informal communication within the team to ensure full engagement and efficient operation</li>
<li>
Intuitively and quickly assess people and situations and act accordingly. Demonstrate strong persuasion and negotiation capabilities.</li>
<li>
Promote and leverage diversity to achieve best outcomes and an inclusive work environment. Ensure all interested parties have opportunities to influence and contribute to decisions and outcomes.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate and promote leadership courage by doing what is right. Instill confidence in associates and customers.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate agility and action orientation when facing ambiguity, change and uncertainty. Effectively manage change and instill confidence.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in marketing, nursing, or pharmacy with at least 5 years of marketing experience in the medical device industry in a FDA regulated environment with both upstream and downstream experience including the launch of products and services. Capital, software, and services experience highly desirable.</li>
<li>
MBA is desired but not required</li>
<li>
5 years of experience</li>
<li>
Experience in planning, executing, and measuring marketing programs</li>
<li>
Experience in supporting and training salesforce for new product launches and on-going sales messaging.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong fit with BD values</li>
<li>
Strong customer focus</li>
<li>
Highly-motivated, execution-focused leader with the ability to self-define role and activities necessary for successful product commercialization</li>
<li>
Advanced analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Strong and effective communication skills</li>
<li>
Excellent command of information/data and the ability to drive decisions</li>
<li>
Organized and proactive with demonstrated ability to multitask and balance competing priorities</li>
<li>
Proven ability to reduce complex cross-functional requirements into easy to understand language and communicate effectively to a cross-functional team</li>
<li>
Proven ability in a regulated, complex medical device market.</li>
</ul>
