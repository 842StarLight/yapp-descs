<p><b>
Job Description Overview:</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>SAP SuccessFactors Marketing is looking for a strong, creative and experienced leader to head the Global Events program for the SAP SuccessFactors business. The events program is a pivotal component of our Marketing strategy used to drive brand awareness, build pipeline and drive customer satisfaction and loyalty. The events program is multi-faceted including marquee events annually in the US, Europe and APJ, various industry tradeshows, and participation in select global SAP events such as SAPPHIRE NOW.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The experienced leader in this role, will have strategic oversight for the entire end to end program working in partnership with key stakeholders in the Marketing team and various functions in the business to build a strategy to meet company goals.<br>
 </p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Owns Global Events strategy for SAP SuccessFactors and oversees the successful execution of the events.</li>
<li>Develop and execute a comprehensive events strategy involving key stakeholder parties in SAP SuccessFactors including Marketing, Sales, Customer, Supplier and Office of the President.</li>
<li>Oversee the budget ensuring appropriate cost controls and negotiations with vendors, partners, etc. to ensure the best price points are being achieved.</li>
<li>Manage the planning and timing of events to support Marketing’s strategy for business growth and acceleration of pipeline, including early securing of event locations and consistent theme development.</li>
<li>End-to-end Collaboration – Drive stakeholder alignment and greater collaboration around key SAP SuccessFactors global events. Drive early regional and executive stakeholder engagement in partnership with Field Marketing representatives or directly with regional SAP Marketing stakeholders. Provide end-to-end visibility and overall coordination of event-related workstreams, especially pre-event execution (promotions, branding, early interest and awareness through digital and social media) and post event execution, to document and communicate results.</li>
<li>Agency Management – Identify, engage, and contract suitable (internal/external) agencies to manage respective projects, including related budget and cost. Identify and recommend preferred agencies and manage vendor agreements to optimize investments.</li>
<li>Field Enablement – Consistently align with marketing and/or business stakeholders in region and encourage their regional field enablement. Understand the role of stakeholder management to roll out effective marketing activities, thus influencing internal stakeholders and making relevant decisions for all related marketing tactics and assets to optimize impact in the market. Constantly monitor use in order to optimize support and enablement, including but is not limited to re-formatting, adaptation of rollout plans, fine-tuning of target audiences, integration of new communication channels or routes, etc.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Progressive leader with deep level, cross-marketing experience, with particular expertise in Event program ownership for a highly-recognized brand.</li>
<li>Self- starting, high energy, charismatic leader with an outstanding track record of leadership, strategy, and successful execution</li>
<li>Examples of proprietary thinking which attempt to evolve the general discourse of events and marketing strategy</li>
<li>A provocative and contemporary understanding of the evolving role of Events in the digital age</li>
<li>Deep expertise in harnessing the power of analytics and research to better inform strategic solutions</li>
<li>An ability to go beyond strategy and bring brands to life experientially</li>
<li>Strong executive presence</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to lead, influence and work cross-organizationally with a broad range of peers and executives</li>
<li>Strong, highly-effective problem solver who excels at leading in an ever-changing environment</li>
<li>Extremely solutions orientated and collaborative</li>
<li>Skilled at management of agency and production partnerships to deliver successful events</li>
<br>
</ul>
