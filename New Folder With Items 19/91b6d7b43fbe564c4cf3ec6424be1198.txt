<h2>Software Account Executive - Telecommute</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview:</b></p>
<p>
To accommodate our growth, we are seeking B2B software sales professionals with experience generating new and recurring revenues through direct sales and marketing. This is a great opportunity for aggressive sales professionals who are driven to hunt for new software and professional services business in competitive environments. This position is telecommute/work from home with the option to work out of our St. Louis, MO office.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maximize revenue through net new software bookings and quota achievement</li>
<li>
Communicate directly with prospects, partners and clients at all levels of their organizations</li>
<li>
Proactively prospect target accounts and respond to marketing generated leads</li>
<li>
Secondary focus on upselling and cross selling software and professional services</li>
<li>
Opportunity discovery, professional proposal creation and presentation</li>
<li>
Adherence to established sales processes</li>
<li>
Consistently document CRM to deliver accurate and timely forecasts to management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>
Need to Have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience selling software solutions and/or professional services</li>
<li>
Records Information Management (RIM) experience</li>
<li>
Ability to establish and sustain relationships with prospects, partners and existing clients</li>
<li>
Consultative sales approach including strong negotiation skills and excellent follow-through</li>
<li>
Solid verifiable history of quota achievement</li>
<li>
Outstanding written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently with professional detail in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Ability to travel domestically and internationally up to 50%</li>
</ul>
<p>
Nice to Have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong personal industry network</li>
<li>
Experience with Microsoft SharePoint or SAP</li>
</ul>
