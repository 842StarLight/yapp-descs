<h2>Franchise Field Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>PURPOSE OF THE POSITION</b></p>
<p>Responsible for achieving regional sales and traffic objectives by leading, consulting, influencing and managing the local activation of the marketing process in collaboration with franchisees in their region and field and support center team members.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides inputs on campaign critical path process led by Brand Manager, completes critical tasks on time</li>
<li>Aligns and communicates with franchisees on campaign lineup, objectives, rationale, creative and timing, in collaboration with SCDC team</li>
<li>Obtains analyses campaign GOI, daypart data from franchisees, reports on results to SCDC team &amp; franchisees</li>
<li>Confirms menu roll-out plan and new product lineup on marketing calendar updates with SCDC team</li>
<li>Organizes, executes food shows for owners’ meetings re: new products. Gets franchisee alignment and feedback on new products, menu lineups, food costs, launch timing</li>
<li>Develops menu critical path, manages menu roll out process, updates team weekly with critical path updates</li>
<li>Communicates menu grid with menu lineup, food cost estimates, pricing recommendations, GOI forecasts, recipe deck in collaboration with SCDC team to franchisees for pricing or One net posting</li>
<li>Obtains all franchisee pricing, communicates to Brand Manager for menu design</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Promotion Activation: Campaigns</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides inputs on creative briefs to Brand Manager, reviews final creative materials</li>
<li>Communicates and distributes final campaign materials to franchisees for local implementation (finished creative, POP, etc.). Provides and communicates POP plan-o-gram to guide local merchandising plan</li>
<li>Monitors competitive activity, develops and shares report on quarterly competitive promotions for franchisees</li>
<li>Reviews, advises franchisees on local media objectives, strategies and plans by market, revises plans per data from ROI analysis, media efficiency measures. Approves all franchisee media plans</li>
<li>Facilitates campaign activation through local visits (1-3 groups per mo.). Assesses campaign, provides feedback to franchisees, SCDC team on findings, recommend course corrections based on sales, traffic, check, promotional product mix, mix or day-part shifts</li>
<li>Obtains franchisee marketing spending reports (general ledger reports) to ensure compliance with 1.5% local spending requirements and spending guidelines. Works with franchisees on plans to fix underspending</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Promotion Activation: Local Store Marketing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conducts local trade area audits once every 12 mos. for each location and as needed</li>
<li>Guides &amp; approves franchisee LSM programs and activation via monthly visits (1-3 groups per mo.)</li>
<li>Develops and maintains archive of LSM best practices with ROI results for franchisees.</li>
<li>Establishes system to communicate best practice’s programs via company communication and franchise web portal.</li>
<li>Obtains LSM creative materials or gets approval from Brand Manager, distributes creative to Franchisees</li>
<li>Manages local franchisee digital media schedule and message deployment, gets creative approval on digital materials from Brand Manager</li>
<li>Manages the timely schedule and deployment of franchisee CRM database recruitment.</li>
<li>Develops and shares monthly reports on digital, email, CRM results with franchisees and SCDC team</li>
<li>Guides, reviews &amp; approves franchisee grand opening marketing plan development</li>
<li>Supports grand opening marketing activities and events for new units, attends grand openings</li>
<li>Analyzes results of grand opening activities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Performance Feedback</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Obtains, evaluates and reports campaign GOI performance and daypart data weekly</li>
<li>Analyzes and reports monthly financial results that compare financial measures (sales, traffic, check) along with and other data. Identifies drivers of business results, performance gaps &amp; causes, makes action step recommendations</li>
<li>Develops analysis of media investments and sales, reports ROI and results from campaigns in business review meetings with franchisees using ROI template</li>
<li>Develops and reports pre-and post-menu analysis with SCDC Finance on the menu launch results, including mix and margin impact for categories, individual products, and overall food cost</li>
<li>Analyzes monthly results of LSM, digital and database marketing programs and reports ROI results using ROI template</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Strategic plan development, financial goals, budgeting</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Contributes to marketing plan &amp; calendar development and changes in collaboration with SCDC team and Brand Manager</li>
<li>Contributes to annual sales forecast in collaboration with SCDC team</li>
<li>Contributes to periodic situation analysis and SWOTS in collaboration with SCDC team and Brand Manager</li>
<li>Provides input into Brand Manager on development of regional ad fund budgets, obtains and reviews BDF reports from Brand Manager and communicates details of quarterly ad fund reports to franchisees</li>
<li>Routes invoices for processing to Brand Manager</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Slim Chickens systems principles and objectives (this competency appears in all job descriptions): </b></p>
<p>Is familiar with Slim Chickens’ established operating systems (e.g. quality, security, office environment, company policies, Safety); understands the responsibility to maintain familiarity with the systems and this position’s role in support of these systems.</p>
<p><b>Performs other duties as necessary in support of business objectives: </b>This position description is intended to guide the activities of the Franchise Field Marketing Manager and is not intended to limit the thinking and creativity of the person to the work of this function nor is it intended that this describe all the work that may be required of the person in this position.</p>
<p><b>Physical Activities: </b>Enters and locates information on a computer. Visually verifies information, often in small print. Employee regularly required to talk or hear. The employee frequently is required to sit, stand, and walk.</p>
<p><b>Travel: </b>Requires ability to travel extensively within assigned territory</p>
<p><b>Requirements and Competencies: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Influencing – ability to sell in a vision or plan for franchisees and gain commitment</li>
<li>Valid Driver’s License</li>
<li>Project Management – ability to lead franchise and work with SCDC teams to deliver results through others</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and attention to detail/proofreading skills</li>
<li>Personal characteristics include self-confidence, collaborative and service-oriented working style</li>
<li>Must have a mature presence to be able to successfully work with local community leaders and franchise owners/operators of all ages, personalities and backgrounds</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Technical Experience: </b>Proficient in typing and Microsoft Office Suite, strong in project management</p>
<p><b>Industry Experience: </b>Minimum of 2-3 years of marketing department experience or advertising agency experience. Previous experience in local marketing for a multi-unit restaurant is required. Previous experience working with regional franchised restaurants is preferred<b>.</b></p>
<p><b>Minimum Education</b>: Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Marketing, Advertising or related field or equivalent</p>
<p><b>Preferred Education</b>: MBA</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
