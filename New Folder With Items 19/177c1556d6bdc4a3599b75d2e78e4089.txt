<p><b>
Company Overview:</b><br>
Founded in 1986 by a group of real-time automation &amp; system solution engineers, Xyntek, Inc. is a global leader in real-time IT and industrial automation solutions. We continue to be one of Philadelphia's fastest growing consulting, design, real-time controls &amp; IT software and systems solutions engineering firms. Xyntek's mission is to form technological partnerships with world-class R&amp;D and manufacturing organizations to provide validated information technology and automated solutions, customized per their business and technology requirements. Our IT and automation engineering business units offer a full range of IT &amp; engineering design, development, and implementation services.</p>
<p><b>
Job Overview:</b><br>
Xyntek is seeking entrepreneurial, dedicated, and motivated employees that are interested in joining a fast-paced Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) team involved in the successful technical delivery of small and medium sized MES projects to our customers. Candidates will be involved in all aspects of MES from onsite assessments, requirements analysis, and detailed design generation through installation, site acceptance, and support.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties:</b>
</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will be comfortable in a lead role; capable of coordinating solutions to technical queries and providing input to project managers about estimates of effort and time through regular reporting. Projects will primarily be performed within the biotech and pharmaceutical industry. Previous experience in life sciences and of delivering projects in validated environments is essential.</p>
<p><b>
Tasks Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze existing development or manufacturing procedures and suggest improvements;</li>
<li>
Work effectively with customer and sales team to understand the customer's business needs, how a proposed solution will address those needs and be able to articulate this to the project team, and any third parties;</li>
<li>
Be the client's point of contact on all technical issues and perform high-level technical analysis and evaluation;</li>
<li>
Demonstrate our product and service capabilities;</li>
<li>
Work with a team of engineers to successfully implement Manufacturing Execution System (MES) small and medium sized projects;
</li>
<li>Focus on best practices and quality standards to create an atmosphere of success and sustainability in the project. Ensure that the Project Manager is fully informed, at the earliest opportunity, of any customer-initiated changes in scope;</li>
<li>
Develop the project design review and code review processes. Develop project template documents;</li>
<li>
Ensure that the defined requirements are delivered through good implementation (based on project configuration guidelines) and testing;</li>
<li>
Define a solution architecture that includes scope, sizing, system / operational requirements, integration/collaboration, project estimations and execution approach;</li>
<li>
Technical interface with procurement to develop the procurement list, including detailed specifications for any sub-contracted works and services where relevant, along with required delivery dates;</li>
<li>
Assist in mentoring trainee engineers in MES theory, software configuration and best practices;</li>
<li>
Create an inclusive and supportive environment for all personnel within the project team and actively support team members.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Abilities, Skills, &amp; Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A Bachelor's degree or equivalent in Engineering with at least 3-5 years' experience in the MES business with a focus on the life-science industry and a proven track record in project execution;
</li>
<li>Excellent knowledge of MES systems (e.g., Emerson Syncade, Rockwell ProPack Data, Werum PAS-X, POMSnet, iBASEt Soumina, Camstar, Werum, Apriso, SAP MII);
</li>
<li>Some knowledge of DCS systems, information/data management systems, relational databases and interfacing (especially OPC) is preferred;</li>
<li>
Significant experience implementing projects in a validated environment;</li>
<li>
Proven experience of MES project design (including high-level front-end design, functional design, and detailed design);</li>
<li>
Proven experience with MES development project life cycles (e.g., requirements analysis, design definition, design review, software development and review, solution implementation, testing and acceptance, traceability);</li>
<li>
Ability to quickly learn new skills and technologies;</li>
<li>
Knowledge of ERPs including integration with SAP (a plus);</li>
<li>
Knowledge of active directory, DNS, IP networks and virtualization;</li>
<li>
Experience working with remote teams;</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively with customers and employees at different levels in the organization;</li>
<li>
Must be proactive, have positive attitude, and a high-level personal integrity.</li>
</ul>
