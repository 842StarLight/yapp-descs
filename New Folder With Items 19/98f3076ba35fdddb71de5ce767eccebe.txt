<h2>Whole Home Sales Specialist (Base + Commission)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>B2C Sales Specialist</b><br>
<br>
Wayfair Sales has reinvented the way you shop for the home you love! Our dedicated sales agents are committed to providing a genuine, personal shopping experience by helping customers Discover More, Rest Assured and Feel Confident in their buying decisions. Our sales agents begin their career at Wayfair with an in-depth, paid training program that prepares them to help consumers feel confident in choosing to shop with Wayfair. Wayfair Sales agents focus on providing world-class customer experiences while building revenue for the family of Wayfair brands.<br>
<br>
<b>What You'll Do</b><br>
<ul>
<li>As a B2C Sales Specialist, you'll create an exceptional sales experience for customers by educating them on the features and benefits of Wayfair's B2C offerings on calls</li>
<li>You'll effectively manage priorities and time while engaging customers in a hybrid inbound/outbound calling environment</li>
<li>Continuously learn new classes of merchandise and deploy that knowledge to provide customers with expert guidance and product recommendations</li>
<li>You will train colleagues to be fellow experts of various product classes</li>
<li>You'll add and maintain customer data in Salesforce</li>
<li>Proactively collect and provide insights to Sales leadership on ways to improve sales offerings</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>What You'll Need</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of successful sales experience</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, both written and verbally</li>
<li>Highly organized and detail oriented skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated, goal-oriented, collaborative and coachable</li>
<li>Comfortable learning and using software applications required for the role</li>
<li>Comfortable and able to meet all essential physical job functions including sitting/standing for 8+ hours, using a keyboard and mouse, and using a telephone headset or phone</li>
</ul>
