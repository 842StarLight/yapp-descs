<li>Business development experience working with media agencies and large advertisers</li>
<li>Knowledge of the search landscape</li>
<li>Proven track record of delivering results and generating transformational growth</li>
<li>Analytical abilities and confidence in the use of date</li>

<br>
<div>Within Amazon Advertising, the Sponsored Ads Partnership team is responsible for the growth of Sponsored Ads, Amazon’s self-service cost-per-click ad platform, across Big Six agencies and Field advertisers.<br>
<br>
As a Principal SPM (Sponsored Ads Partner Manager) on CPG, you will be part of a growing team of advertising experts responsible for the growth of Sponsored Ads, Amazon’s self-service cost-per-click ad platform. You will use your 7 – 10 years of industry experience to provide strategic recommendations to CPG advertisers. With your deep and long-standing experience in auction dynamics, keyword bidding, and measurement attribution, consultative approach, and deep understanding of the digital advertising landscape, you will act as a strategic partner to clients, their agencies and third parties in your assigned vertical. You will closely work with the Sales team in charge of the CPG advertisers, aligning your engagement model with the advertisers' needs and strategy. In addition, you will also closely work with the Product and Marketing teams to help drive improvements on product, processes and Marketing programs.<br>
<br>
You must be results-driven, continually identifying opportunities for improving Sponsored Ads performance. You must also have strong analytical skills, and be able to work with complex data sets. In addition, candidates should be willing to work autonomously, handling multiple initiatives simultaneously and prioritizing accordingly to contribute to ambitious growth plans. This is a critical role as your efforts will drive advertiser commitment, revenue growth and product improvements.<br>
<br>
Key responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Manage a portfolio of CPG advertisers using Sponsored Ads and Amazon Stores.</li>
<li>Serve as a strategic expert, helping to guide media planning, execution and measurement.</li>
<li>Work closely with internal teams such as marketing, product and sales teams and ensure a consistent approach with our agencies and advertisers.</li>
<li>Facilitate API conversations and develop the use of our API across CPG advertisers.</li>
<li>Work with technical account management and product teams to identify and solve issues blocking advertiser performance.</li>
<li>Uncover the business needs of our CPG advertisers and their agencies and develop strong feedback mechanisms to inform and influence the product roadmap.</li>
</ul>

<li>Existing relationships with CPG Marketing decision makers</li>
