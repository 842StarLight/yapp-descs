<div><div><div><b>Core Job
</b></div></div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<div><b>Job Responsibility/Accountability</b></div>
</div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Customer Sales</b></div>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Provide excellent customer service while maximizing the dealership’s parts sales.</li>
<li>Receive, verify and stock incoming shipments</li>
<li>Pull customer orders, transfers, returns and ship accordingly.</li>
<li>Locate needed parts quickly and efficiently.</li>
<li>Maintain and inventory shipping and warehouse supplies</li>
<li>Clearly communicate parts pricing and expected arrival dates.</li>
<li>Inform customers immediately when expected parts arrival dates have changed or delays are expected</li>
<li>Communicate all problems, shortages or delays to the branch parts manager and or general parts manager and customers as soon as possible.</li>
<li>Maintain the parts showroom in a clean, properly stocked and orderly manner. Update posters and flyers as needed.</li>
<li>Follow all company and branch policies concerning invoicing, stocking and credits.</li>
<li>Must be able to follow directions from supervisor.</li>
<li>Must be able to interact efficiently and have a high level of cooperation with co-workers.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Cost Control</b></div>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Follow company guidelines when establishing parts selling prices.</li>
<li>Report and maintain list of “lost sale” information.</li>
<li>Ensure that all freight, restocking and or miscellaneous charges are properly charged as per company policy.</li>
<li>Review all requests for “Emergency Orders” with parts manager prior to placing orders.</li>
<li>Perform parts cycle counts and other inventory control.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Training</b></div>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Participate and utilize all parts training when available.</li>
<li>Maintain current knowledge of products represented by the dealership.</li>
<li>Actively participate in sales improvement training.</li>
<br>
</ul></div>
</div>
<br>

</div></div>

<li>High School diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>Valid drivers license and a safe driving record.</li>
<li>Must be able to pass a pre-employment Drug Test and Criminal Background check</li>
<li>Written and verbal communication skills, ability to demonstrate and state facts to all contact people.</li>
<li>Understanding of PC hardware/Software (Windows, MS Word, MS Excel, MS Outlook, MS PowerPoint) including all manufacturers web portals.</li>
<li>Understanding of Manufacturers products (specifications, applications, operations and structures ).</li>
<li>Understanding of Manufacturers Parts Policy and Procedure Manuals.</li>
<li>Detail oriented, organized and responsible.</li>
<li>May be required to drive a company vehicle to pick up or deliver parts (less than 10% of the time)</li>
</ul></div></div>
