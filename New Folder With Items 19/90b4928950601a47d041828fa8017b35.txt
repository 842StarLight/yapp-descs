<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p><b>I. Job Summary </b>| <i>Major goals and objectives.
</i></p>
<p>WALA, a Meredith Corporation TV Station is looking for a Marketing Assistant. The Marketing Assistant works closely with the Creative Services Director and Sales Staff to assist in the process of executing station sponsorships, advertising product placement and sponsored integration for all FOX10 platforms and programs. Tasks include organizing on-air calendar, gathering information from clients, coordinating sponsorships and monitoring ongoing sponsorships to ensure goals are met. The Marketing Assistant will also manage the on-air promotional inventory under direction of the Creative Services Director. Related tasks will include scheduling daily on-air promos in accordance with station/network priorities, creating promo usage reports, and maintaining the database of available on-air promos. The Marketing Assistant will also represent the station at various community events. The Marketing Assistant works with internal departments including sales, news, promotions, Studio 10 and engineering as well as with external customers.
</p>
