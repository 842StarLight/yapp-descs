<h2>Account Operations Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary: </b></p>
<p>The Account Operations Associate is a valuable member of the Account Operations team, which is responsible for managing efficient business operations and account-related projects. The Account Operations Associate’s primary function is to provide real-time support to a team of Account Managers and Sales Professionals, and liaison between various internal and external teams.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the preparation and revision of contracts for existing client portfolio and new business sales.</li>
<li>Serve as point-of-contact and resource for the Sales Team regarding contract negotiations, execution, and related needs</li>
<li>Coordinate the processing and documentation of contracts and related business paperwork</li>
<li>Liaise with external accounting, procurement, and legal departments to ensure timely completion of all tasks related to contract administration when requested</li>
<li>Liaise with internal accounting team for month end processes including contract reconciliation</li>
<li>Liaise with internal and external legal departments to manage contract red-lining processes</li>
<li>Provide contract summaries and ensure contract execution is in accordance with company policy</li>
<li>Immediately resolve time-sensitive needs through effective communication and decision making</li>
<li>Manage customer relationship management database, including data entry, data reconciliation, and advanced levels of reporting</li>
<li>Prepare and disseminate information to appropriate employees regarding contract status</li>
<li>Effectively manage the reconciliation of bulk-contract customers, ensuring that every client has a streamlined and efficient procurement experience</li>
<li>Provide ongoing support to the Campus Relations team as requested, including providing reports and metrics</li>
<li>Set up the kick-off meeting, notify support staff of timeline/needs, build the Loopio project, secure signatures/notary, export the project, review the document for any export errors, and then ensure proper communication for shipping.</li>
<li>Serve as the point person for the Loopio software</li>
<li>Train sales team members on using the Loopio software for RFP completion</li>
<li>Communicate updates to Loopio and the RFP coordination process to sales team members</li>
<li>Document and organize RFP materials in SharePoint and Loopio for easy access</li>
<li>Ensure RFPs are tracked in SalesForce for reporting</li>
<li>Chalk &amp; Wire Order Management</li>
<li>Other tasks as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education: </b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred, in communication, business administration, or similar field</li></ul>
<p><b>Qualifications/Experience: </b></p>
<ul><li>1 or more years of project management or related experience</li></ul>
<p><b>Skills/Competencies: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent written, verbal, and interpersonal communication skills</li>
<li>Must be able to use independent decision making, good judgement, and discretion in handling confidential information</li>
<li>Detail-oriented, while maintaining the ability to prioritize and facilitate multiple projects</li>
</ul>
