<h2>Leasing Consultant - Willow Glen</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Leasing Consultant</b></div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Job Summary:</b>
</div>
<div>A Leasing Consultant is Tribute’s first impression to potential and current residents. A Leasing Consultant will listen and understand the needs of a potential resident and show them that Tribute has what they want. Greeting potential and current residents, taking requests/complaints by phone or in-person, participating in outreach marketing activities, and assist in planning resident functions are some responsibilities of a Leasing Consultant.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Maintains relationships with Customers:
</b></div>
<div>prospects, residents, other on-site Associates, Community Manager, Regional Property Manager, vendors, competition, neighborhood, and Apartment Association members. Major Goals and Responsibilities. Responsible for all leasing tasks assigned by the Community Manager.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>General areas of responsibility are: </b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assists in the achievement of the Community's annual budget plan. </li>
<li>Accounting functions relating to leasing. </li>
<li>Leasing and marketing tasks. </li>
<li>All other related tasks assigned by supervisors </li>
<li>Upholding all Company policies, goals and the Company values</li>
<li>Participates in Social Media marketing to obtain traffic. </li>
<li>Participates in outside marketing efforts.</li>
<li>Leasing </li>
<li>Accurately quotes prices; maintain a 33% closing ratio; collect money; prepare leases; review leases and acquire signatures. </li>
<li>Answers questions and show apartments to potential residents to define what they wants </li>
<li>Follow-up regularly with potential leads </li>
<li>Provides courteous customer service, excellent telephone techniques, and good customer communication. </li>
<li>Provides information to prospects. </li>
<li>Sells the value of community features as they relate to the customer needs to close the sale Resident Relations </li>
<li>Create and maintain excellent and ongoing relationships with potential and current residents </li>
<li>Ensure a high level of customer service </li>
<li>Use customer service skills to take requests, complaints, comments, and resolve them quickly Administration </li>
<li>Assists in monitoring renewals and distribute and follow-up on renewal notices.</li>
<li>Assists in rent collection. </li>
<li>Assists in the resolution of resident complaints and issues. </li>
<li>Processes maintenance requests. </li>
<li>Distributes move-in packages; Follow up on all prospects.</li>
<li>Inspects apartments for readiness; show apartments. </li>
<li>May be periodically assigned to bookkeeping duties or assist in maintenance areas</li>
<li>Prepares and assists with all leasing paperwork </li>
<li>Review and sign leases with residents. </li>
<li>Process applications for approvals (credit check, rental history, etc.) </li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of the on-site computer system and all other duties as may be assigned from time to time.</li>
</ul>
