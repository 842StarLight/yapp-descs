<h2>Ecommerce Shipper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Shipper Retail is responsible for packing and shipping all items purchased from Amazon and Shop goodwill. Ensures items are properly packed and shipped in a timely and accurate manner; maintaining our 24 hour shipping obligations with all Amazon orders.</p>
<p>
RESPONSIBILITY LEVEL:</p>
<p>
Packing and shipping all items purchased from Amazon and Shopgoodwill. Ensures items are properly packed and shipped in a timely and accurate manner; maintaining our 24 hour shipping obligations with all Amazon orders. Maximize efficiency and process with all orders; using one piece flow and shipping over 1,200 pieces per day.</p>
<p>
PRINCIPAL DUTIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain production environment standards with a positive and team focused process.</li>
<li>
Print and process packing slips for all sold books through NeatoScan and all jewelry &amp; collectibles sold through Shopgoodwill.com.</li>
<li>
Examine shipment contents and verify correlating contents with records such for accuracy and quality; including pack sheets, invoices, and orders.</li>
<li>
Pack, seal, label, and affix postage to prepare materials for shipping using hand tools, tape guns, postage scales, packing paper/bubble wrap, and other shipping tools and supplies.</li>
<li>
Utilize Desktop Shipper shipping software accurately and efficiently to ensure all items are shipped using the most cost effective carrier.</li>
<li>
Move materials as needed throughout warehouse facility to support shipping carrier and transportation team in loading/unloading area.</li>
<li>
Excellent time management; completing assignments within established parameters and meet defined production goals.</li>
<li>
Maintains an organized and clean work area.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned to support the business and departmental needs.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma or GED.</li>
<li>
One year work experience in a retail or Ecommerce or production driven environment.</li>
<li>
Work varied schedule and flexible hours with a weekend availability as scheduled.</li>
<li>
Forklift certification, if required at site.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
CORE COMPETENCIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Communicate in a professional and team focused manner representing our mission and brand.</li>
<li>
Use effective written and verbal communication.</li>
<li>
Project a positive image of Goodwill to customers and coworkers in actions and appearance.</li>
<li>
Represent our guiding principles with team work, exhibiting Power of The Whole; working together with others towards a common goal.</li>
<li>
Maintains confidentiality of sales and other business metric information.</li>
<li>
Basic reading, writing and math skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
PHYSICAL/SENSORY DEMANDS:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Able to lift, push or pull a minimum of 50 pounds.</li>
<li>
Full range of motion, with the ability to stand for extended periods of time and move throughout the facility.</li>
<li>
Able to stand, bend, reach for duration of shift.</li>
<li>
Able to grasp merchandise.</li>
<li>
Able to visually observe merchandise.</li>
<li>
Able to hear and respond to internal paging system and warning devices on custodial equipment and forklifts</li>
</ul>
