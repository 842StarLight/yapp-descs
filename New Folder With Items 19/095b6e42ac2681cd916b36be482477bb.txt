<h2>Senior Director, Sales & Marketing, IT Solutions</h2>

The Senior Director, Sales and Marketing IT Solutions is responsible for long-range strategy planning and policy development to address complex business issues as well as the full systems development life cycle management of projects/programs supporting Eisai’s sales, marketing, market access and digital engagement solutions. This position has responsibility for aligning business needs and IT solutions with a focus on external customers and ultimately, patients/caregivers.
<br><br>
In this role, he/she will provide direction for business and technical resources, serve as the primary escalation point from project/program inception to delivery, and direct the definition and development of the project management infrastructure, methods, and quality plans. Serves as the strategic interface to business unit leads, forming alliances on projects, demand management, operational decisions, scheduling requirements/conflicts and vendor contract clarification.
<br><br>
Overall accountability for serving as the strategic interface with the commercial business functions selling and marketing Eisai products and services. The appointed leader will engage with these business functions for the purpose of business/IT strategy development, solution discovery, service management, risk management and relationship management.
<br><br>
Serves as the business relationship linkage between the business units and IT (at the executive level). Provides highly-valued strategic consulting level support and guidance around relevant business and IT initiatives.
