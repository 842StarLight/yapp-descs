<h2>Client Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>We are eClinicalWorks. We are a privately held leader in healthcare IT, providing comprehensive, cloud-based EHR/PRM solutions to medical professionals worldwide to improve workflows and reduce the risk of physician burnout.<br>
<br>
We care. We are committed to positive change. And that’s where you come in. Do you value creativity and innovation? Great, so do we. At eClinicalWorks, we share a passion for improving healthcare through dedication, education, and teamwork. Everyone has that one thing they’re really good at. We value your talent and want you to join our fast-paced, fun, and culturally diverse environment. Ready to make a difference? Apply today.<br>
<br>
Position Overview:<br>
This position is responsible for acting as a primary point-of-contact during the “Go-Live” period to facilitate new clients in adopting our software.<br>
<br>
Job Functions / Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Onsite visits for development and maintenance of mutually beneficial long-term relationships with customers for checking in on practice adoption after Go live and during GO live period
</li>
<li>Help practices with any questions and or technical issues through working with assigned TAMs or through Project lead TAMs by providing the connection and fixing the issue for the client so that it does not hold them up from running their practice show especially during go live</li>
<li>Identifying weaknesses and trying to help</li>
<li>Answer how to questions</li>
<li>Facilitating any needs for the client during the visit</li>
<li>Follow up and close all items reported at the visit via a call a week after the visit</li>
<li>Survey to follow this close loop to ensure that Satisfaction increases and references
</li>
<li>Measurable increase in customer satisfaction measures.</li>
<li>Maintain or increase recurring revenue from customers.</li>
<li>Decrease in executive level escalations from customers.</li>
<li>Educating customers in detail about the support structure at eCW and what to expect when contacting support and also all educational resources available for them.</li>
<li>Updating customers on the upcoming features in our software as well as stressing the importance and benefits in using the existing features which results to decrease in work load from practice end and increase in health care from patients point.
</li>
<li>Evaluating the client after the visit, make sure with the use of available resources, help the client with their requirements.</li>
<li>Help with any deployments</li>
<li>Work with clients to ensure they adopt more products and features as they come out</li>
</ul>
</div>
