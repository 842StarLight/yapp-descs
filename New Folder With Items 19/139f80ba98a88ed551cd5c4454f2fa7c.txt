<h2>Digital Marketplace Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Truck Hero Inc. is a fast growing corporation with the opportunities you need to excel in your career! Headquartered in Ann Arbor, MI. Truck Hero is one of the largest aftermarket automotive accessory companies in the United States.</p>
<p>
Truck Hero is currently searching for a top-notch Digital Marketplace Coordinator to support the execution of the corporate sales strategy and brand presence within all digital marketplace platforms including but not limited to Amazon, eBay, Walmart, etc. The coordinator will be responsible for organizing and maintaining product catalog listings and content for all Truck Hero brands, within all channels, in a way that aligns with the overarching marketplace and corporate marketing strategies.</p>
<p><b>
PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for maintaining product catalogs for all Truck Hero brands including new item setup, product availability, weights and dimensions, etc.</li>
<li>Organize and maintain product listing content for all Truck Hero brands across all marketplace channels including but not limited to product titles, marketing copy and descriptions/fitment data, images, enhanced content, and brand stores</li>
<li>Coordinate marketplace photography and video needs with marketing department and visual media team</li>
<li>Regularly review and resolve product, pricing, and listing issues to ensure consistent availability and accuracy of products and product listings</li>
<li>Create, maintain and implement consumer-focused and marketplace-specific sales copy and product descriptions</li>
<li>Implement processes for efficiently maintaining content across all marketplace platforms</li>
<li>Gather sales data and maintain weekly and monthly platform performance reports including but not limited to sales metrics, advertising, reviews, etc.</li>
<li>Manage new item set up process for new products across all platforms, including data load sheets, image and content association, keyword optimization, etc.</li>
<li>Maintain product catalogs within all reporting systems and tracking software</li>
<li>Coordinate with digital advertising team on integrating newly added items into digital advertising campaigns and launching strategic inventory load-in digital advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Assist with coordinating promotions across all marketplace channels and maintain promotional reporting</li>
<li>Manage incoming tickets for product issues across all marketplace channels</li>
<li>Report project plans, progress, and results</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<p><b>POSITION REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing or related field or three or more years of marketing/sales experience.</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge of e-commerce and strong product management skills. Experience with Amazon Vendor Central or Seller Central a plus.</li>
<li>Software Experience: Microsoft Office Suite with emphasis on Excel. Marketplace dashboard experience a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
REQUIRED SKILLS/ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Accountability: Accepts responsibility and accounts for his/her actions</li>
<li>Communication: Communicates effectively with others both orally and in writing</li>
<li>Financial Aptitude: Understands basic accounting information</li>
<li>Problem Solving: Finds a solution for, or deals proactively with work-related problems</li>
<li>Self-Motivated: Internally inspired to perform a task to the best of one’s ability</li>
<li>Management Skills: Achieves desired outcomes by setting goals and delivering results</li>
</ul>

PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>

</ul>
