<h2>RPA Program Management Support Specialist / PMO</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Employee reviews and processes for payment expense reports from company personnel supporting worldwide military operations. Disseminates current accounting codes and work authorizations to employees. Prepares financial incentive paperwork. Assists with financial analysis and reporting within the Program Management Office (PMO). Location of assignment is Las Vegas, NV. Under limited supervision, applies comprehensive program knowledge and subject matter expertise. Assists with multiple operations and multiple contract staffs in financial analysis, sales, indirect budget planning, and financial metrics analysis at the program office level. Prepares analysis and interprets business segment financial performance to support PMO forecast planning, cost controls, and year-to-year performance. May assist with program and/or SBU level corporate briefings and reports, and business development efforts.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Responsibilities:</b><br>
1. Review and process expense reports from worldwide military support operations.
<br><br>
2. Disseminates list of current charge codes and work authorizations to workforce as needed.
<br><br>
3. Prepares financial incentive paperwork for deployed personnel.
<br><br>
4. Assists multiple operations and multiple contract staffs in providing a broad range of financial services at the program level to support business planning initiatives, financial operations and performance monitoring.
<br><br>
5. Assists with performance forecasts for bookings, sales, and contract profit for financial reporting and for performance monitoring.
<br><br>
6. May assist in the preparation of PMO-level financial, cost, and budget reports and analysis as required.
<br><br>
7. Prepare financial Accounts Payable &amp; Timesheet corrections.
<br><br>
8. Generate and distribute daily/weekly Timesheet reports.
<br><br>
9. Monitor Timesheet corrections.
<br><br>
10. Research and prepare charge card receipt folder and clear suspense items.
<br><br>
Perform all other position related duties as assigned or requested.
<br><br>
