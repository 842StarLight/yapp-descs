<h2>Product Manager - Mobile, Value-Added Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b><br>
Responsible for launching and lifecycle managing value added services associated with Spectrum Mobile (e.g. device protection, trade-in, early upgrade, device financing, etc.). Developing business requirements, recommending and gaining approval for the roadmap projects, customer experience improvements, and additional revenue and non-revenue generating service and experience improvements (e.g. early upgrade in additional channels, process improvements with vendors and delivering new services) are at the center of a fast paced, highly visible effort.<br>
<br>
Other major responsibilities include coordination with sales and care channels, training on value added services, supporting operations, developing channel promotional programs, and reporting key metrics to the company.<br>
<br>
The role will report to the Director of Device Experience, but will operate in a highly matrixed environment with numerous key stakeholders.<br>
<br>
<b>MAJOR DUTIES AND </b><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
<i>Actively and consistently supports all efforts to simplify and enhance the mobile customer experience.</i><br>
<br>
Understands industry best practices. Knowledge and experience with product development process, preferably specific to the wireless or telecommunications space. Ability to clearly communicate product benefits and requirements to both management and cross functional teams.<br>
<br>
Informs and assists with gathering information to translate high-level process goals into specific sub-processes with appropriate documentation, develop communication plan for stakeholders and product development partners.<br>
<br>
Proactively identify analysis to support the effort and conduct with deep analytics when appropriate while maintaining an eye toward the bigger picture.<br>
<br>
Support the development and communication of Product Strategies dependent on new services/features to drive financial results. Works with engineering, product management peers, marketing stakeholders, training teams and key market areas on implementing strategy.<br>
<br>
Leads product launches and enhancements of products in the two Charter operating units to include working with Corporate Engineering, Corporate Marketing, IT and Wireless Technology within Charter.<br>
<br>
Ensures communication with all stakeholders is being managed effectively.<br>
