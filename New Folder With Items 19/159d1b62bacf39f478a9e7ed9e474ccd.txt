<h2>Database Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>DESCRIPTION</b></h1>
<p>Kimball Midwest, a national distributor of maintenance, repair and operation products, is searching for a Database Developer in our Information Technology Department for our Columbus, OH location. We are looking for an assertive, hardworking, motivated, and customer focused individual.</p>
<p>
Our Database Developer will be responsible for the design and coding of complex database solutions. Strong development skills are required for table design, stored procedures, functions, views and triggers. Project work includes ETL operations from ERP systems to data warehouses, syncs between applications, set-up and maintenance of replication and availability groups. Knowledge to troubleshoot performance issues and administrations tasks are required. Specific abilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong SQL skills for development, review and rework of SQL stored procedures, functions, views and triggers</li>
<li>Extract data from ERP systems and import into SQL databases</li>
<li>Configure, design, secure, and optimize SQL databases</li>
<li>Extract, transform and load data between various internal and external systems</li>
<li>Work with the system engineers to install new SQL servers / instance</li>
<li>Work with developers to ensure SQL scripts and data are optimized</li>
<li>Ensure backup and recoverability of SQL data</li>
<li>Develop reports in SQL Server Reporting Services</li>
<li>Set-up and administration of transactional and merge replication, using a remote distributor</li>
<li>Knowledge of configuring, monitoring and resolving issues with availability groups</li>
<li>Ability to work with query execution plans, wait statistics and other metrics to resolve performance issues</li>
</ul>
<p>
As a Kimball Midwest employee, you will experience why we have been listed as one of the Top Workplaces in Columbus six years in a row! Our sales revenue growth is dynamic, increasing from $1 million in 1983 to more than $240 million today. Throughout all our growth we have kept the family owned and operated culture alive. At Kimball Midwest, you are a name and not a number and we pride ourselves on our unique culture.</p>

</ul>
</li>

<li>Experience with relational databases in server-client and web environments</li>
