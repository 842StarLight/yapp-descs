<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
Our Account Manager is responsible for the end-to-end support of his/her own book of business which includes: campaign health, activation and performance of campaign activities including deal terms, IO’s, budgets, creative, reporting and billing. This role is heavy on day-to-day client support, troubleshooting and collaborating with the internal Delivery Team to ensure deal terms and performance expectations are managed. Client support will involve education of industry trends and competitive benchmarks and being able to present solutions to clients with confidence. Those successful in this role is independent, have solid project management skills, has high emotional intelligence and comfortable with managing a high volume of tasks.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Pre-Sales (Renewals and New Business)</li>
<li>Responsible for all media plan entry into Salesforce during pre and post-sales</li>
<li>Create Media Plans and RFP Decks for new and existing business.</li>
<li>Post Sales</li>
<li>Being primary point of contact for the customer on day-to-day account needs</li>
<li>Project manage the launch process; review media plan, issue and gather assets needed, QA all incoming and outgoing deliverables, manage client expectations on timelines and reporting</li>
<li>Manage all partner specific budget allocation, optimization, media partnership programs and added value details</li>
<li>Troubleshoot tracking and discrepancy issues; follow up with internal support teams or third party partners to resolve</li>
<li>Ensure accurate data entry and invoicing for goods &amp; services, confirm final billable with client and vendors, issue invoices, process approval requests for credits and make goods as needed.</li>
<li>Assist in training and onboarding of new team members</li>
</ul>

</ul>
<b>Preferred:</b><br>

<li>Previous digital media experience (internships, etc…)</li>
</ul>
