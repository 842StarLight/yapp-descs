<h2>Berrynaked Store Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>We are growing! And we need talented managers to help us accelerate and expand!<br>
<br>
<b><i>Job</i></b><b> Duties:</b><ul>
<li>
Completes store operational requirements <i>by</i> scheduling and assigning employees; following up on <i>work</i> results.</li>
<li>
Maintains store <i>staff</i> <i>by</i> recruiting, selecting, orienting, and training employees.</li>
<li>
Maintains store <i>staff</i> <i>job</i> results <i>by</i> coaching, counseling, and disciplining employees; planning, monitoring, and appraising <i>job</i> results.</li>
<li>
Achieves financial objectives <i>by</i> managing budget and expenditures; analyzing variances; initiating corrective actions.</li>
<li>
Identifies current and future customer requirements <i>by</i> establishing rapport with potential and actual customers and other persons in a position to understand service requirements.</li>
<li>
Ensures availability of merchandise and services <i>by</i> approving contracts; maintaining inventories.</li>
<li>
Formulates pricing policies <i>by</i> reviewing merchandising activities; determining additional needed sales promotion; authorizing clearance sales; studying trends.</li>
<li>
Markets merchandise <i>by</i> studying advertising, sales promotion, and display plans; analyzing operating and financial statements for profitability ratios.</li>
<li>
Secures merchandise <i>by</i> implementing security systems and measures.</li>
<li>
Protects employees and customers <i>by</i> providing a safe and clean store environment.</li>
<li>
Maintains the stability and reputation of the store <i>by</i> complying with legal requirements.</li>
<li>
Determines marketing strategy changes <i>by</i> reviewing operating and financial statements and departmental sales records.</li>
<li>
Maintains operations <i>by</i> initiating, coordinating, and enforcing program, operational, and personnel policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Contributes to team effort <i>by</i> accomplishing related results as needed.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Skills and Qualifications:</b><ul>
<li>
Leadership Skills: You will keep your employees motivated, resolve conflicts and make hard decisions for your employees. A successful store manager is a good leader.</li>
<li>
Time Management: You will be working with employees, customers and management. You might have to create schedules, order supplies and write reports. Time management is essential to make sure everything gets done.</li>
<li>
Math and Budgeting: Store managers are expected to keep and maintain a budget in almost every field. You will need to be confident in using math skills to make sure you know where your company's money is going.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Analytical Skills:</b>
<ul><li>You will be in charge of hiring new employees, and being a good judge of character will help ensure that you hire the right people to maintain an efficient and motivated team. You will also need analytical skills to be able to solve problems that <i>may</i> come up during a typical <i>work</i> day.</li></ul>
</div>
