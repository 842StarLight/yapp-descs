<h2>Associate Actuary</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Position Overview</b><br>
<p>The Associate Actuary takes ownership of the data used in specific advanced analytic exercises, throughout the full project from understanding business needs through to implementation, to contribute to maximizing the value produced from the exercise. Communicates effectively throughout the project with all stakeholders to ensure data is well understood, used effectively and used in accordance with the appropriate legal and best practice frameworks. Ensures data is cleaned ready for further advanced analysis and serves as a SME on data cleansing and manipulation within the group.</p>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities</b><br>
<p>On specific data and analytic projects takes responsibility for, and in many cases executes, the following activities to ensure that:</p>
<ul>
<li>
project is properly initialized by understanding the business objectives, timescales, constraints on the project etc.</li>
<li>
data requirements are properly defined and understood, including consideration of third party sources of data to enrich the data where appropriate</li>
<li>
data is received through secure processes in compliance with RGA data policies;</li>
<li>
sufficient understanding of the data is gained and documented, including how it was collected, how it may have changed over time etc.</li>
<li>
possible problems, errors and inconsistencies in the data are identified and actions taken to solve those issues;</li>
<li>
data is processed in preparation for more advanced analysis, which may include data cleaning, manipulation, partition, aggregation, mapping, conversion, basic statistics calculation, etc.;</li>
<li>
potential synthetic variables are identified and either communicate those possibilities to the modelling team and/or create synthetic variables directly;</li>
<li>
data transformation is performed as necessary;</li>
<li>
data is provided to data scientists in a way they can easily utilize it and ensure appropriate knowledge transfer takes place on the data and related domain knowledge to ensure they can do their job in the most effective way;</li>
<li>
the final modelling solution is reviewed to ensure data has not been utilized in error, outside of legal/regulatory/practice frameworks or that data items have not been fully utilized;</li>
<li>
continual liaison takes place with stakeholders to ensure they are well informed;</li>
<li>
implementation plans are reviewed and issues highlighted that could potentially arise due to differences in the data between what was used in the modelling and would be collected during live functioning of the system.</li>
<li>
All such activities will, to a greater or lesser extent as appropriate, necessitate working extensively with internal and external clients.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Management</p>
<ul><li>
Manages a small team of analysts to support successful attainment of the objectives set out in this job description.</li></ul>
<p>
Best practices design, development and maintenance</p>
<ul><li>
Owns the data process and documentation system with in the data science group to ensure efficiency and effectiveness, and communicate with the rest of modeling team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Assists in developing, maintaining and following RGA modeling best practice standards.</li></ul>
<p>
Explore current and emerging topics of interest to any of RGA’s business units or clients such as new statistical techniques, new mortality/morbidity development, and many others.</p>
<p>
Participates in special projects, committees, meetings and client presentations as required.</p>
<p>
Fosters positive relationships with key stakeholders.</p>
<p>
Provides assistance, as appropriate and directed, with the training of new personnel.</p>
<p>
Performs other duties as required</p>
