<h2>Graphic Applications Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Job Description</div>
<div>
<p><b><i>
MGI USA currently has an exciting opportunity for a Graphic Applications Specialist with strong design, technology and customer communication skills.</i></b></p>
<p><b>MGI CORPORATE INTRODUCTION</b><br>
As a world leader in digital printing and finishing equipment, MGI Digital Technology provides groundbreaking and innovative solutions for the graphic arts and packaging industries.</p>
<p>As we look forward to the new dawn breaking over the printing industry, we hope to have you play a part in MGI's goal of continuously Innovating a product portfolio that remains one step ahead of the curve, all while keeping the same company values that has led to our success: To remain an environmentally and ethically responsible company, constantly driven by innovation, and dedicated to providing graphic professionals with cutting edge tools that will help lead the print industry's revolution.</p>
<p><b>POSITION OBJECTIVE</b><br>
This position is responsible for designing, creating and producing digital print samples as well as supporting the Marketing &amp; Sales Teams with customer demonstrations. You will be responsible for ensuring the showroom equipment and prospect files are all tested and validated prior to each demonstration, running the product demonstration with the sales team, and doing Proof of Concept samples for customers in the field. In addition, you will be supporting the technical team on key missions ranging from product training to technical troubleshooting.<br>
<br>
<b>PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for testing and validating printing equipment prior to demonstrations.</li>
<li>Responsible for validating prospect files prior to demonstration and ensuring correct setup</li>
<li>Operating the showroom equipment during the demonstration and producing custom samples.</li>
<li>Design, creation and production new in-house sample files, marketing collateral &amp; sales tools</li>
<li>Managing custom in house marketing graphic and application needs (paper, foil, lamination)</li>
<li>Training new users and operators on new equipment utilization with software expertise</li>
<li>Travel will be required for customer support and participation in trade show events.</li>
<li>Managing the sales prospect Proof of Concept samples queue in a timely fashion</li>
<li>Communicate clearly and professionally with customers to handle requests efficiently; convey technical information in an easily understood manner; exceed customer expectations regularly</li>
<li>Identify urgent situations that require additional actions to satisfy the customer’s needs including escalations of technical issues and recommending machine operational solutions.</li>
<li>Understand client/partner support levels &amp; identify fee-based professional services activities</li>
<li>Provide operator and technical service training with ongoing remote and onsite support.</li>
<li>High degree of self-motivation to succeed and dedicated to skill development &amp; improvement</li>
<li>Actively participate in learning opportunities and completes assigned curriculum in a timely manner; applies knowledge to continuously improve the customer experience</li>
<li>Perform other projects and tasks as assigned by MGI management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS: EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Graphic Arts background with expert knowledge in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign (such as alpha channels, spot colors and variable data protocols for mask file creations).</li>
<li>University Degree and with Professional Experience preferred.</li>
<li>Proficiency in time management and organizational discipline</li>
<li>Ability to analyze, interpret and communicate technical data</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and organize work independently, without direct supervision</li>
<li>A Self-Starter with multi-tasking ability is a “must-have” job requirement</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ADDITIONAL SKILLS, REQUIREMENTS AND CERTIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Willingness to travel 20-30% (Domestic and International)</li>
<li>Strong responsibility, self-motivated, able to work under high pressure</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, communication, presentation and listening skills</li>
<li>Prior offset or digital printing press experience is a major plus</li>
<li>This position is located in Melbourne, FL.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MGI OFFERS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive compensation program.</li>
<li>Outstanding benefits package (including medical, dental, vision, company-provided long-term disability and life insurance).</li>
<li>401(k) plan with matching company contribution.</li>
<li>Generous holiday and paid time off schedules.</li>
<li>Highly visible, exciting work supporting the sale of advanced digital technology solutions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to sit or stand for long period of time in support of travel or customer meetings presentations, demonstrations, technical service calls and trade show activities.</li>
</ul>
