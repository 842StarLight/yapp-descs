<h2>Sales and Marketing Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>SOUTH COAST FACILITY SERVICES is a full-service MEP contractor that designs &amp; installs HVAC equipment, delivers Energy Services, and provides process piping &amp; direct digital control systems. Our mission is to provide high quality mechanical, electrical and plumbing solutions through service and retro-fit construction; provide continuous education to our employees; lead the industry with innovation and technology.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
SCFS is seeking a Sales and Marketing Administrator to work at our corporate office in Anaheim, CA. The Sales and Marketing Administrator will be responsible to work cooperatively with Sales Management and Account Executives and other members of the team to support in the continued success of the company.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The right individual will possess a positive can-do attitude, be a self-starter, driven person with prior experience providing support to others in a team environment.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Provide administrative support to sales department.</li>
<li>
Generate accurate and timely reports to be distributed daily, weekly or monthly.</li>
<li>
Schedule and coordinate sales meetings, charity events, networking events and travel arrangement for sales department team.</li>
<li>
Attend sales/client meetings on or off site and take meeting notes as necessary; prepare follow up correspondence from meeting notes to be distributed to sales team and Executive Management.</li>
<li>
Coordination, set up and attend trade show events on behalf of the company and the sales department</li>
<li>
Manage appointment calendar for sales department.</li>
<li>
Assist or create sales meeting presentations by creating new and/or editing current presentations.</li>
<li>
Complete and submit pre-qualifications.</li>
<li>
Work closely with outside sales Account Executives on new account opportunities</li>
<li>
Setup and maintain customer information in Salesforce database</li>
<li>
Manage CRM database as the Salesforce Administrator</li>
<li>
Track, project and service maintenance opportunities</li>
<li>
Manage social media accounts.</li>
<li>
Create, compose, edit written materials to include presentations, flyers, social media content</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, ABILITIES, AND COMPETENCIES:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office, Illustrator, or other graphic design software</li>
<li>
Advance reading &amp; writing skills to create, compose and edit written material</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills whether in person, phone or e-mails.</li>
<li>
Strong social media skills to include LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram</li>
<li>
Ability to assist in development and creation of presentations.</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently on projects.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and communication skills.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of advertising and sales promotion techniques; strong creative thinking skills.</li>
<li>
Maintain a professional appearance and providing a positive company image to the public.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>

EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE AND/OR LICENSES:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>

Bachelor's Degree in Business or Communication preferred</li>
<li>
Salesforce experience preferred
</li>

</li>

<li>
Knowledge of Microsoft office and Design software</li>
