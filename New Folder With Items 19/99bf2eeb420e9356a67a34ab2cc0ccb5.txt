<h2>Jr. Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Summary:</b></p>
<p>Entry level Project Management position. Will assist in management of commercial furniture projects. Learning and performing tasks related to design, order management, shipping and receiving, budget management, installation oversight and customer service.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Learn how to effectively communicate with internal &amp; external customers</li>
<li>Learn and create status reports</li>
<li>Work with project team to meet customer’s onsite installation goals.</li>
<li>Disperse order information to appropriate internal personnel (sales staff, operations, customer, etc.).
</li>
<li>Check bill of material against design drawings to ensure accuracy.</li>
<li>Review receiving information from field operation and matches to order; resolves discrepancies.</li>
<li>Assist with punch walk thru to establish the punch list; has product ordered that is needed to perform punch work and oversees punch list work to ensure it is done in a timely manner.
</li>
<li>Detail scope of work and evaluates job site to establish installation parameters.
</li>
<li>Solicit installation quotes from in house and subcontracted installation firms.</li>
<li>Coordinate receiving and delivery procedures to ensure timeliness and accuracy</li>
<li>Oversee installation and punch process.
</li>
<li>Articulate install plan to project stakeholders, and consistently send milestone updates</li>
<li>Perform all necessary tasks to ensure project execution is as smooth as possible</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Effective oral and written communication skills.
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal/customer service skills.</li>
<li>Ability to process computer data and to format and generate reports.
</li>
<li>Proficient in office computer software use (Word, Excel, Outlook, etc.)</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills.
</li>
<li>Handle multiple projects with deadlines in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Project a professional demeanor as well as using tact when dealing with others.
</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills required</li>
<li>Work Successfully in a team environment</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s Degree in a business related major, or equivalent combination of education and experience.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Travel:</b></p>
<p>Position will require a reliable form of transportation. Occasional regional travel to our offices located in Baltimore, Richmond and Virginia Beach.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Demands:</b></p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employees are regularly required to stand; walk; sit; use hands and fingers to handle or feel; reach with hands and arms; and talk or hear. The employee must regularly lift and/or move up to 25 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, color vision, and ability to adjust focus. Ability to perform the essential functions of the job with or without reasonable accommodation.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Working Conditions:</b></p>
<p>Some of the time in a general office environment. Work can be sedentary in nature, and may require standing and walking for up to 90% of the time. The working environment is generally favorable. Lighting and temperature are adequate with no hazardous or unpleasant conditions caused by noise, dust, etc. Work is also performed within construction sites and outside where there is extreme temperatures and hazardous and unpleasant conditions. PPE and safety training will be provided<b>.</b></p>
