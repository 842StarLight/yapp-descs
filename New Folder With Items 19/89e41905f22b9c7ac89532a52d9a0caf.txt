<h2>Community Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>PREVIOUS EXPERIENCE FOR POSITION</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A College Degree and/or 3 years of relevant customer care experience. The knowledge and ability to shape, meet and exceed Customer expectations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>GENERAL RESPONSIBILITY OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>
 <b>Sales Process</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Initiate contact with sales prospects through referrals, web site hits, demonstrated interest, or model home visits</li>
<li>Follow up all leads with phone calls, e-mails or personal notes</li>
<li>Qualify sales prospects' interest and ability to purchase a home</li>
<li>Maintain relationship with Realtor community by actively seeking new business</li>
<li>Monitor Realtor and customer referral programs and the customer traffic evaluation system</li>
<li>Responsible for inventory home MRIS listings</li>
</ul> <p><b>The Customer Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Shape Customer expectations through implementation of the Homeowner Guidelines</li>
<li>Set and manage customer relationship and continue to shape expectations from point of sale through home closing</li>
<li>Manage all purchase agreement requirements in a timely manner (i.e., Loan commitment, earnest money deposit, contingencies, etc.)</li>
<li>Lead the Pre-Construction Orientation with customer to ensure the home has been "built" correctly on paper (reconciliation of all sales and Design Center selections with Construction Start Package)</li>
<li>Enforce Augustine Homes' Safety Program Guidelines at sales center and homes under construction</li>
<li>Ensure customers and their guest(s) are accompanied on site an Augustine Homes' employee</li>
</ul> <p><b>Reporting Process</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare purchase agreements</li>
<li>Conduct &amp; report on Post-Contract Review Meeting</li>
<li>Prepare monthly market analysis on competition's prices (base &amp; structural options), floor plans, incentives and advertising</li>
<li>Maintain community lead tracking and analysis systems</li>
<li>Report weekly sales and traffic activity and analysis</li>
<li>Report weekly team meeting minutes</li>
</ul> <p><b>Community and Team</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee maintenance of models and inventory homes and create quarterly punch list for Superintendent</li>
<li>Conduct Weekly Community Team Meetings (discuss site issues, recent Change Orders, customer concerns, etc)</li>
<li>Provide hostess timesheets weekly (if applicable)</li>
<li>Conduct monthly safety audits of model and send completed form to Corporate Office</li>
</ul> <p><b>Systems and Processes</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review and forward weekly any community specific changes needed on all Augustine Homes related web sites</li>
<li>Maintain working knowledge of local community development plan, lot matrix, amenities, site plans, architectural plans, product specifications, Extraordinary Touches, Sales Price Release Memo (base price, structural &amp; Design Center selections), Design Center options, monotony code, Homeowner Guidelines and Homeowner Association documents</li>
<li>Promote Augustine Homes value added features (Extraordinary TouchesTM)</li>
<li>Ensure daily that community streetscape, housekeeping, safety and homesite/model signage meet Augustine Homes' performance standards (drive by and home site visits)</li>
<li>Maintain fresh inventory of sales collateral materials and office supplies/customer refreshments</li>
<li>Ensure model, sales trailer or inventory home is ready to open at least 15 minutes before opening time (fluff pillows, check light bulbs, music is turned on, sales collateral material in place, etc.)</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of community demographics and amenities: schools, local businesses, employers, parks, recreation facilities, etc</li>
<li>Maintain working knowledge of fair housing laws</li>
</ul> <p><b>Finance</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Liaison between the customer and the Settlement Coordinator to assist with closing, if necessary</li>
<li>Maintain working knowledge of types of available mortgages and financing options</li>
<li>Be aware of customer loan status</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALITIES REQUIRED FOR THIS POSITION</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Interpersonal Skills</li>
<li>Follow-up Skills</li>
<li>Team Player Mentality</li>
<li>Communication Skills</li>
<li>Technology Skills (Excel, Word, Publisher)</li>
<li>Time Management Skills</li>
<li>Organizational and Planning Skills</li>
<li>Desire to Succeed</li>
<li>Professional Appearance</li>
<li>Fully understand Company Philosophy: Mission, Vision and Value Statements</li>
</ul>
