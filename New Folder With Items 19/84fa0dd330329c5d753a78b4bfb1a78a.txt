<h2>Real Estate Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for a talented <b>Real Estate Assistant </b>in Augusta, GA to help an established real estate Agent manage and grow her business. This busy and fast-paced office offers a well-qualified candidate the opportunity to take projects from concept to completion. A skilled driver and go-getter will be able to quickly contribute in an impactful way, pushing the team toward greater levels of productivity. This role wears many hats and best suited for an experienced real estate professional as you will be expected to ‘take the reins’ and ‘manage up’.</p>
<p><b><i>
We are looking for a career Real Estate Support Professional. Prior experience in the real estate field is preferred but not required. Applicants seeking a position as a stepping-stone into sales—this is not the job for you.</i></b></p>
<p><b>
Does this sound like you?</b></p>
<p>
As a Real Estate Assistant, you have exceptional organizational and project management skills. You are analytical, assertive, detailed, and able to juggle multiple tasks efficiently all while staying focused. You are a team player and enjoy working in a collaborative environment. Your polished communication skills make it easy for people to receive your message.</p>
<p>
You love to learn new things and enjoy working in a fun and high-producing work environment. You are able to work under limited supervision, taking the initiative when necessary. You will take on your job duties with a sense of ownership, confidentiality, commitment, honesty, loyalty, and integrity.</p>
<ul><li>2+ years of executive-level administrative experience, or office management experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Real Estate experience preferred but not required</li></ul>
<ul><li>A proven history of implementing systems and processes</li></ul>
<p><b>
Areas of Focus:</b></p>
<p><b>
Create Structure</b>: Streamline existing systems, checklists, and procedures to ensure the smooth and efficient operation of the office. Take ownership of the contact database and nurture its growth and use in lead generation.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Event Planning</b>: Assist with planning Seller Seminars three times per year. Attend the events and offer suggestions for improvements and coordinate changes.</p>
<p><b>
Marketing Assistance:</b> Work with the team to develop and manage marketing initiatives and projects to increase lead generation (online and offline) and conversion. Research concepts for video marketing and social media posts.</p>
<p><b>
Support and Serve</b>: Provide an executive level of administrative support to your agent. This may include but is not limited to phone and email management, calendar organization, task management, project management, and holding your agent accountable for following up on leads. You will provide a concierge-level of customer service and serve as the first point of contact for clients.</p>
