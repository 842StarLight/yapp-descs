<h2>Asst. Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Herman &amp; Kittle Properties— Assistant Apartment Manager-Tax Credit<br>
Location: Magnolia Park - Jackson, MS</p>
<p>
**$750 Sign-on Bonus**</p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY:</b><br>
The assistant apartment manager is responsible and accountable for assisting in on-site operations at an apartment and community. The assistant apartment manager supports the organization’s mission and values by exhibiting the following behaviors: excellence and competence, collaboration, innovation, respect personalization, commitment to our community, and accountability and ownership. Be the Role-Model and Ambassador for Herman and Kittle Properties.</p>
<div>
<b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with potential residents to understand their needs and help them find apartments that meet those needs.</li>
<li>Capture sales—show and lease apartments to customers, helping them with paperwork and filing the paperwork accordingly.</li>
<li>Make sure that paperwork is complete, files are properly prepared, and date is entered into the computer system.</li>
<li>Build positive relationships with current residents to promote customer satisfaction and help maintain occupancy rates.</li>
<li>Market the property through advertising, print media, websites, social media, networking, word of mouth, referrals, etc.</li>
<li>Help with reception duties: greet residents/visitors, answer calls, complete work orders, etc.</li>
<li>Other duties as may be assigned from time to time.
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Previous tax credit experience is required</li>
<li>Must have strong communication and people management skills.</li>
<li>Must understand customers’ needs and respond to them, connecting them with the right solution; must be able to market and sell.</li>
<li>Must be very organized, with a strong attention to detail; must stay on top of paperwork.</li>
<li>Must be flexible and able to shift priorities if needed.</li>
<li>Language Skills - Ability to read, write, speak and interpret basic instructions, simple correspondence and present information one-on-one or in small groups.</li>
<li>Math Skills - Ability to add, subtract, multiply, divide using whole numbers, fractions and decimals. Ability to compare rate, ratio and percentages and interpret basic graphs.</li>
<li>Reasoning Skills - Ability to carry out detailed, but uninvolved written or oral instructions with common sense understanding. Ability to deal with problems that involve few variables in standardized situations.</li>
<li>Computer Skills – Beginner experience with MS Office.</li>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED)</li>
</ul>
</div>
