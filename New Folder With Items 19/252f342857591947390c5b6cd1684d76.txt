<div>Enterprise Holdings is the largest car rental provider in the world as measured by revenue and fleet. The company and its affiliate Enterprise Fleet Management – which combined offer a total transportation solution that includes extensive car rental and car-sharing services, truck rental, corporate fleet management and retail car sales – accounted for $24.1 billion in revenue and operated 2 million vehicles throughout the world in 2018. Enterprise Holdings’ annual revenues also place it near the top of the global travel industry, exceeding all other rental car companies, many airlines, and most cruise lines, hotels, tour operators and online travel agencies. Enterprise Holdings’ regional subsidiaries and Enterprise Fleet Management currently employ more than 100,000 people worldwide.<br>
<br>
Through its integrated global network of independent regional subsidiaries and franchises, Enterprise Holdings operates the Enterprise Rent-A-Car, National Car Rental and Alamo Rent A Car brands at more than 10,000 fully staffed neighborhood and airport locations. The Enterprise Holdings global network operates in more than 90 countries and territories, including North America, Central America, South America, the Caribbean and Europe, as well as parts of Asia-Pacific and the Middle East. Today, the company’s three brands serve more than 95 percent of the worldwide car rental market.</div>

<div>
The Corporate Marketing Department has an exciting opportunity for a Project Manager. The Project Manager is responsible for planning, directing, coordinating, and monitoring activities of one or more project teams involved in the analysis, design, development, and implementation of Marketing initiatives. The person in this position will leverage broad expertise, knowledge, and skills to resolve complex issues; contribute to the development of company objectives and principles and the achievement of goals in creative and effective ways. This position will collaborate with various teams in Marketing, IT and external agencies to define project scope and objectives, identify required skills and resources for project team, develop detailed work plans, and provide project status. In addition, this position anticipates and identifies project issues and risks, recommends solutions, and works with senior management to implement those solutions. This position will also have an opportunity to help develop creative technology and workflow solutions for the Marketing Department. Qualified candidates should be enthusiastic about learning and working with a wide variety of productivity tools.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead the operational, tactical, and strategic activities for the project team</li>
<li>
Collaborate with others to define project scope and objectives; develops project work plans, project charters, baselines, and tracking documents; controls project scope and budget; modifies work plans to reflect approved scope changes
</li>
<li>Plan and manage project activities within project plan and/or provides input into the tasks in the project plan</li>
<li>
Work on medium to large, complex initiatives with interdependencies where analysis of situations or data requires an in-depth evaluation of variable factors and proactive risk management</li>
<li>
Identify required skills and resource requirements for project team; identify and request resources and determine time requirements</li>
<li>
Anticipate and identify project issues and risks; recommends solutions; works with senior management to implement solutions</li>
<li>
Enforce conformance to quality standards, processes and procedures; conduct quality assurance audits and develop reports
</li>
<li>Create status reports for project sponsors that may include key indicators of project progress, risk, and success
</li>
<li>Provide escalation assistance; recommend potential solutions to issues; follow established escalation procedures; resolve moderately complex issues</li>
<li>
Collaborate with team members and management to define departmental standards, processes, and procedures</li>
<li>
Establish key project management performance metrics for the team and monitors to ensure compliance of established standards, processes, and procedures</li>
<li>
Act independently to determine methods and procedures on new or special assignments</li>
<li>
Work to achieve consensus and cooperation of others from multiple areas/departments</li>
<li>
Assist with the management of outside vendor relationships as necessary</li>
<li>
Manage the integration of vendor tasks including tracking and reviewing vendor deliverables</li>
<li>
Maintain established service level agreements to manage customer expectations and quality standards; identify opportunities for improvement
</li>
<li>Stay abreast of new technologies to best serve marketing goals</li>
</ul>
