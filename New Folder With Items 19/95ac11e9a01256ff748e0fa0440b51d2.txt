<h2>Sr. Design Manager, Brand Innovation (Beyond the Bottle)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Who We Are…</b><br>
PepsiCo Design &amp; Innovation is a creatively driven team that leverages the power of Design Thinking to solve brand and business problems that drive meaningful value and growth. Our team’s mission is to lead the innovation agendas in order to accelerate and transform PepsiCo’s Global brands and businesses beyond its core, unlocking the future of CPG. We innovate with intent; to solve real problems, for real people, in the real world. We innovate to incite positive change – for people, brands, business and the world around us.<br>
<br>
As part of our Beyond the Bottle business, we’ve recently launched a new hydration platform enabling our consumers to stay hydrated with great-tasting beverages, digitally track their hydration, and help meet the growing consumer demand for smarter and more sustainable on-the-go beverage options. Built to reflect how people drink water today, the new hydration platform is a connected ecosystem that responds to the rise in consumption of low-and-no-sugar drinks as well as heightened focus on plastic’s effect on the environment. As the Beyond the Bottle strategy evolves, Design &amp; Innovation will play a critical role in driving the hydration innovation agenda with a focus on health, sustainability, and elevating the consumer experience.<br>
<br>
<b>Our Opportunity...</b><br>
The Sr. Design Manager, Brand Innovation is a design leader who will drive the execution and management of comprehensive brand projects within our portfolio, on our Beyond the Bottle beverages business. You will leverage the power of Design Thinking and demonstrate superior ability to inspire and engage cross-functional teams to drive projects from ideation to execution with quality brand design solutions, directly executing them or in collaboration with external partners.<br>
<br>
The main objective of this role is to lead major brand design projects, strategies, and innovations. This entails creating or extending new brands while also planning, organizing, and implementing consistent design principles and processes to ensure that the design, strategy and production guardrails are met.<br>
<br>
<b>Accountabilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design Development: Directs 360 brand development including visual identity, user experience, consumer journey, digital, and activation for emerging platforms through cross functional collaboration. Generates creative design proposals, and style guides (visions, scenarios, concepts,communication materials) in order to visualize the desired output and share it with internal and external team members. Responsible for final design recommendations, decisions and approvals. Ensure thorough consideration of all project variables and that the final solution will perform as planned.</li>
<li>
Design Project Management: Responsible for partnering across disciplines within PepsiCo and external suppliers to ensure design assets are developed and delivered with quality, value, and on-time. Lead project team from idea phase to commercialization. Ensures each stakeholder understands his/her role and responsibilities throughout the creative process. Regularly reviews project status with appropriate leadership to verify that Company and Division expectations are met.</li>
<li>
Design Innovation: Participates in research activities and effectively translates consumer needs into actionable solutions. Integrate Design Thinking to generate critical business value for the organization.</li>
<li>
External Design Network: Supports the PepsiCo Design Organization in building and cultivating a strong external design network</li>
<li>
Design Leadership: Act as a coach and mentor to help guide and develop junior design talent.</li>
</ul>
