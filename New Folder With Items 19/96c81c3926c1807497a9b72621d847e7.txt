<p><b>Overview &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>We currently have a <b>full time</b> opening for a <b>Licensed</b> <b>Funeral Director.</b> This is the opportunity to be part of the Dignity Memorial® provider network and grow your career in the funeral, cremation and cemetery services business. For us, there is no greater responsibility than celebrating each life like no other and making a difference in the lives of people we serve.</p>
<p>This position interacts directly with client families and focuses on helping families make At-Need and/or Pre-Need arrangements and maintaining a premier level of client family satisfaction. Our Funeral Directors are looking for more than a career – it's a calling. Here is the chance to make a real difference helping families with Funeral Services at the time, or in advance of their time of need. This is a great opportunity to develop your career, with training and advancement. Truly a career you can feel GREAT about while still earning a terrific income.</p>
<p>We provide excellent structured training in the classroom, in the field, and on-line through our award winning <b><i>Dignity University</i></b> . Your responsibilities will include having conversations with families, educating clients on funeral options and becoming an “ambassador” of our firms.</p>

<p><b>SCI Overview</b></p>
<p><b>Who we are. What we do.</b></p>
<p>We’re more than North America’s largest provider of funeral, cremation and cemetery services. We are mothers, fathers, sisters, brothers, sons and daughters who are devoted to the communities where we live and work. We are more than 20,000 dedicated individuals who provide caring assistance to families in need, honor veterans and public servants and deliver lifesaving programs to help keep children and seniors safe.</p>
<p>We operate under the umbrella of the Dignity Memorial network of 1,800 funeral homes and cemeteries. Dignity Memorial providers care for more than 300,000 families each year and understand the importance of thoughtful, personalized arrangements. We believe creating meaningful ways to pay tribute to a loved one begins with compassion and is shaped by the understanding that each life is truly unique. For us, there is no greater responsibility than honoring and preserving the story of one’s life.</p>
