<p><b>Requirements/Job Duties</b></p>
<div>
<p>World Wide Technology, Inc. has an opportunity available for a Security Sales Specialist to join our Commercial Sales team working with Enterprise customers located in Texas, Oklahoma and Louisiana. This position will be focused on achieving goals by selling World Wide Technology's suite of Security products and services through multiple OEM's. Candidates who have experience selling Enterprise Strategic projects would be preferred.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Positioning Security solutions to large Commercial clientele.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively profile target accounts</li>
<li>Professional networking to build relationships with C-level executives and decision-making managers at targeted clients.</li>
<li>Working with an inside sales team in coordinating sales activities (order processing, marketing events, etc.).</li>
<li>Maintaining client and OEM relationships.</li>
<li>Continuing education on evolving networking, unified communication, virtualization, and storage technology.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div></div>

<div>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Strong Security technical acumen will be required.</li>
<li>Understanding the technical challenges and demands of Security solutions and products.</li>
<li>Ideal experience includes consultative solution.</li>
