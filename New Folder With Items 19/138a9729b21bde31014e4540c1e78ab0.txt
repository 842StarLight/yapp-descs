<h2>Convenience Store Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b>:</p>
<p>The Convenience Store manager is responsible for day to day store operations, including scheduling, training, and supervising employees and assistant manager. The Store manager is responsible for the store profits, and all controllable expenses including labor, inventory levels, and cash and inventory shortages.</p>
<p><b>Experience: </b> Must have 5 years of convenience store management experience, be proficient in Microsoft Office (including Excel and Outlook), experienced with Point of Sale systems (and be willing and able to learn ours), and willing to work late nights, early mornings, weekends, and holidays as needed.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the recruiting of, recommend for hire, and train, positive individuals to become members of our team ensuring excellent customer service.</li>
<li>Promote and resolve customer complaints, in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>Implement and enforce established daily operating procedures to ensure store is clean, adequately stocked, organized, and well kept.</li>
<li>Ensure all merchandise is stocked and displays are attractive, priced correctly, and displayed in a safe manner.</li>
<li>Maintain a kitchen that exceeds safety and cleanliness standards.</li>
<li>Monitor daily retail gasoline competitors and sending the prices to the corporate office in a timely manner as established by management.</li>
<li>Complete daily paperwork and computer entry in a timely manner as established by management.</li>
<li>Monitor cash over/short, inventory shrinkage, and drive offs daily.</li>
<li>Conduct Store meetings as needed with employees.</li>
<li>Have the physical ability to perform all duties of a store cashier regularly.</li>
<li>Understand all information in the daily reporting of store operations.</li>
</ul>
<p>Operations</p>
<ul>
<li>Implement Monthly promotions, insure all POS advertising/signage is properly posted at the proper time, and communicate with Marketing department to promote store.</li>
<li>Communicate and perform all price change request, mark downs / ups as requested by the Operations Manager.</li>
<li>Communicate any problems with merchandise pricing to management</li>
</ul>
<p>Administrative</p>
<ul>
<li>Implement and enforce all merchandising and vendor policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Enforce all Safety and Security Issues and report any and all unsafe conditions.</li>
<li>Report and process all employee and/ or customer incidents or accidents following company procedure.</li>
</ul>
