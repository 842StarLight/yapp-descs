<h2>Electrical Project Manager / Estimator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are a well established company, who focuses on creating a family style team culture, where personal and professional growth is nurtured. Our group of outstanding Electricians and Administrative Staff, demonstrate a high level of competence in their work, and operate with a high level of personal core values.</p>
<p>
If you are Hungry, Humble, and Smart, you may be a great fit to join our exceptional staff. Our interview process is unlike anyone else, because we only select those who will make a great addition to our current team.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Managing projects on estimates won
</li>
<li>Prepares estimate for use in selecting vendors, materials, lighting, switchgear, etc</li>
<li>Travel to job sites to gather information on materials needed and labor required.</li>
<li>Analyze specifications and technical documentation to prepare time, cost, materials, and labor estimates.</li>
<li>Procure materials upon award of project</li>
<li>Prepare proposal specifications and drawings to determine scope of work and required contents of estimate</li>
<li>Attend pre-bid meetings and/or tour job sites to confirm requisite take-offs</li>
<li>Maintain files of working documents as back-up for estimate figures, including information on pricing from suppliers obtained through direct contact, sales brochures, price lists, software uploads, etc.</li>
<li>Support other department personnel as required; may include assisting with estimates in other disciplines</li>
<li>Review design options and recommend best solution based on cost, engineering quality, and/or availability of materials</li>
<li>Interface with clients on bid-walks</li>
<li>Perform site visits to coordinate work with field team</li>
<li>As- Built Drawings via Adobe or AutoCad</li>
<li>Complete Project Submittals, RFIs and Close-out Documents</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of experience estimating and managing electrical construction projects</li>
<li>Must have electrical residential/commercial construction project experience</li>
<li>Stable employment history</li>
<li>Travel as needed</li>
<li> Working knowledge of electrical installation, estimating techniques, and cost control
</li>
<li>Ability to manage a team and their expectations</li>
<li>Knowledge of materials, methods, and the tools involved in the electrical industry</li>
<li>Knowledge of business and management principles involved in strategic planning</li>
</ul>
