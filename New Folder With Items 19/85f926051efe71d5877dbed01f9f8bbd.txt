<h2>Sales Automation Consultant - CPQ (VIRTUAL POSITION)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>In this position, you will be responsible for designing comprehensive solutions to support the sales process and improve productivity. You will lead projects that have broad organizational impact leveraging mobile enterprise solutions including Sales Automation, Configure/Price/Quote (CPQ), electronic signature, Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM), and data reporting and analytics. You'll perform Systems Administration of tools, lead business process reviews, evaluate and recommend solutions, define requirements, and participate in deployment of solutions. Act as the expert in business process, application functionality, and core sales automation applications.</p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure business realization of strategic objectives through the management of multiple highly complex projects with multiple dependencies. Lead all project phases, including initiation, planning, execution, monitoring, control and closure. Assess, manage, resolve and escalate (if necessary) risks and issues.</li>
<li>System Administration of enterprise wide automation tools, including Oracle CPQ Cloud, electronic signature and Robotic Process Automation (RPA). Create end-user testing scripts and release notes and lead UAT sessions.</li>
<li>Elicit and document accurate and complete Business Requirements for system enhancements from sales, implementation, legal, finance and other functional groups.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with other Sales Automation teams, functional groups, and IT Consultants as a subject matter expert, providing insight into potential process and technology solutions to business problems.</li>
<li>Ensure that all solutions are aligned to Sales Automation and ADP strategic objectives and mobile strategy. Provide input to improve processes and procedures to enhance the sales and client experience.</li>
<li>Provide advanced Support for all tools supported by the team. Provide regular updates to counterparts in Sales Tool Support on new solutions and improvements.</li>
<li>Develop and enhance strategic business partnerships by building trust with all business partners (Sales Solutions, Sales Tool Support, Finance, Implementation, Legal, Training, and Enterprise Applications).</li>
<li>Stay current with sales automation tools, industry standards and best practices.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Preference will be given to candidates who have the following:</p>
<p>
Education:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree or equivalent experience required.</li>
<li>Master's Degree preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience and Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated experience managing large scale projects involving the development and design of solutions for sales, marketing, implementation or legal. Strong team leadership and ability to delegate responsibility to all levels of the project team and hold team accountable for deliverables.</li>
<li>Strong track record of success working with a full spectrum of tools needed to design solutions, including CPQ or other pricing solutions, electronic/digital signatures, Robotic Process Automation (RPA), salesforce.com and other technologies that support a large mobile sales or implementation organization.</li>
<li>Oracle CPQ Cloud Admin Certification strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Strong Business Analysis skills with demonstrated ability to document complete, concise, and accurate business requirements to meet strategic objectives. Agile or Kanban experience preferred.</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills. Ability to communicate effectively with both business executives and technical staff and convey complex ideas. Strong collaboration skills.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, problem-solve, think and act strategically.</li>
<li>Work well under pressure with high tolerance for stress.</li>
<li>Outstanding organizational and time management skills for managing multiple diverse and conflicting activities,</li>
<li>Ability to work at a detailed level while maintaining the big picture of the overall system design.</li>
<li>Anticipate, identify and resolve problems in a timely manner. Assigns tasks to appropriate cross functional resources and follows up. Strong customer service focus.</li>
<li>PMP or Six Sigma knowledge a plus.</li>
</ul>Experience with ADP products/industry a plus.</div>
