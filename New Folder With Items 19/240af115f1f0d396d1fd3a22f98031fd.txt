<h2>Junior Software Sales / Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Bitfury Group develops and delivers both the software and the hardware solutions necessary for businesses, governments, organisations and individuals to securely move an asset across the Blockchain. The expertise of The Bitfury Group ensures successful, easy, fast, secure and cost-effective connectivity to the Blockchain.
<br><br>
We're looking for a driven sales professional who's passionate about selling early-adopter SaaS in multi-stakeholder, nascent and complex selling environments. You'll be working on a collaborative team to expand the Bitfury customer-base domestically and abroad.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Efficiently and effectively utilises a deep understanding of blockchain products and technology to inform and influence our product roadmap, support and execute on product plans.</li>
<li>Identify new strategic opportunities for the company via partner input and work collaboratively across all functions to the scope and prioritize these efforts.</li>
<li>Develop a detailed business account plan (in company CRM solution), including initiatives, client opportunities, any limitations/risks, timelines, potential revenue/sales for the target client opportunities.</li>
<li>Track, analyse, influence, and communicate key metrics to build opportunities and experiences that create scale and meaningful ecosystem value.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the marketing, customer success, and professional services teams to accomplish deliverables and expand your book of business.</li>
<li>Seek customer feedback and convey to the stakeholders and core teams that you collaborate with.</li>
<li>Keep up to date on industry trends and provide market/customer feedback to the product team.</li>
<li>On an annual basis, prioritize business account as to the prospect of growth, risk limitations, and establish objectives aligned with company strategic plan and growth opportunities.</li>
<li>On an annual basis, or based on industry information, develop new target clients.</li>
<li>Assess all the threats, risks, or limitations related to Bitfury's business or opportunities with respect to each client.</li>
<li>Acts in a manner consistent with Bitfury's core values and according to Bitfury's Code of Business Conduct and Ethical Behaviour.</li>
<li>Complies with standards and procedures of Bitfury's health and safety manual and Occupational Health and Safety regulations.</li>
</ul>
*
<br><br>
<b>Requirements and Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 1 year proven experience in full-cycle B2B or SaaS Sales.</li>
<li>Self-starter that can be hands-on and work independently as well as exhibit strong leadership skills to be a member of a greater team.</li>
<li>Must have strong planning, organisational, analytical and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Strong negotiation skills and a demonstrated record of successfully closing deals.</li>
<li>Attention to detail with the ability to see the bigger picture.</li>
<li>Multicultural approach, comfortable working with diverse cultures and mind-sets.</li>
<li>Appreciation to work in a global organisation with teammates in different time zones/regions.</li>
<li>Excellent communication (verbal &amp; written) with strong interpersonal skills and a strong customer service focus.</li>
<li>Willingness and ability to travel 50% of the time.</li>
</ul>
</div>
