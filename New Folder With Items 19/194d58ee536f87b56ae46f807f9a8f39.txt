<p><b>Sales and Marketing Manager Job Summary</b></p>
<p>We are currently seeking an ambitious and highly-skilled sales and marketing manager with a track record of exceptional, transformative management practices.</p>
<p>In this role, you will increase productivity, inspire and motivate junior employees, and optimize our company’s market share. You will have tremendous opportunities to develop innovative policies and campaigns that shape the future of our organization. Candidates for this role will be creative, charismatic, knowledgeable, and exceptionally well-versed in the latest sales and marketing techniques as well as tried-and-true practices.</p>
<p><b>Sales and Marketing Manager Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee day-to-day sales activity to achieve growth and hit sales targets by successfully managing the sales team</li>
<li>Continually assess our marketing techniques and their efficacy in affecting sales</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date on current market trends</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with the sales team to assess current projections</li>
<li>Own ultimate responsibility for successfully meeting or exceeding sales goals</li>
<li>Take calculated risks to increase profitability and brand recognition</li>
<li>Work in a hands-on fashion, building the team—provide motivation and inspiration</li>
<li>Own recruiting, objectives setting, coaching and performance monitoring of sales representatives</li>
<li>Set the precedent for excellence through leading by example</li>
<li>Build and promote strong, long-lasting customer relationships by partnering with them and understanding their needs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Sales and Marketing Manager Requirements and Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business or related field</li>
<li>5+ years’ experience in management in a corporate setting</li>
<li>Transformative approach to leadership that inspires and empowers others</li>
<li>Aware of the latest market trends and shifts, as well as projections for the future</li>
<li>Evidence of ability to innovate and implement change successfully</li>
<li>Exceptional communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Able to be persuasive and procure buy-in from upper management</li>
<li>Solid computer skills and awareness of web-based marketing and social media</li>
<li>Driven and committed to success while maintaining integrity</li>
</ul>
