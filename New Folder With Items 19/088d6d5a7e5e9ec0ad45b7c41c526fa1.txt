<h2>Director, Marketing (Digital & Media)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Who will this position appeal to?</b></div>
<div>This role will appeal to a highly experienced Marketer that has worked in the fast-paced arts and entertainment sector.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Position Summary</b><br>
The Director, Marketing (Digital &amp; Media) is responsible for strategic planning, tactical implementation and evaluating all aspects of marketing activation including owned and paid channels, to achieve and exceed revenue and audience goals for the Dr. Phillips Center for the Performing Arts. Provide leadership to support the organization in these key areas: Media Buying (online &amp;offline); Analytics; managing partner agencies; website management (partnering with business services/IT); and CRM Management.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Job Functions &amp; Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Provide vision and leadership in all facets of digital marketing &amp; media planning and buying to position the arts center both locally and nationally, while building and developing audiences and revenue.</li>
<li>Lead organizational digital strategy, establishing KPI’s that support business goals and ensuring performance through data attribution and analysis.</li>
<li>Lead development of communications calendar with team for social media, email distribution, blog posts and ongoing columns</li>
<li>Direct digital agency partner in all online media efforts to drive optimizations and site visits and grow conversion and revenue</li>
<li>Supervise all paid advertising plans, ensuring dollars are allocated for maximum return on ad spend</li>
<li>Oversee social media channels including developing strategy, establishing KPI’s and optimizing plan based on insights and analytics</li>
<li>Direct CRM platform marketing strategy and tactics.</li>
<li>Direct implementation of analytics and attribution across various mediums to gain customer insights and provide the organization with meaningful customized dashboards that inform business decisions.</li>
<li>Participate on peer Leadership team. Collaborate on group goals, support and report out on the marketing team’s progress on annual workplans and organizational goals.</li>
<li>Responsible for leading and directing team to include monitoring work assignments and productivity. Participate in the recruitment, selection and training of the team through ongoing performance evaluations and feedback.</li>
<li>Participate in building the organization’s strategic marketing plans and the department annual workplans. Responsible for meeting work plan objectives. Contribute to departmental budgeting and financial planning processes and ensure accurate execution and management of financial resources. Adhere to all financial guidelines (Budgeting, PO and RFP processes).</li>
<li>Follow and support all operational policies and procedures. Assimilate into the arts center culture through understanding, supporting and demonstrating the core values.</li>
<li>Maintain a professional and friendly relationship with other departments, colleagues, and partners.</li>
<li>Able to remain calm under pressure. Ability to work effectively with internal and external customers, some of whom will require high levels of patience, tact, and diplomacy. Maintain Dr. Phillips Center standards of customer service at all times.</li>
<li>Able to effectively communicate in English in both written and oral forms.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Knowledge, Skills and Experience</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>10+ years’ experience in the marketing field, <b>performing arts or live entertainment environment preferred</b>, including experience leading a successful marketing driven business model. 5+ years’ experience in digital marketing. 3+ years’ experience media buying</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of marketing analytics systems and tools</li>
<li>Transaction sales experience is strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Highly developed written and verbal communication, interpersonal, planning and organizational skills with the ability to persuade and inspire others. Ability to work effectively with diverse personalities and communicate successfully with all levels of the organization. Ability to formulate cross functional project teams in achieving goals.</li>
<li>Ability to synthesize and analyze complex financial, legal, and market research data and documents. Ability to understand and develop complex calculations for discounts, commissions, and profit/loss evaluations for individual shows and program series.</li>
<li>Strong computer proficiency skills. Experienced user for Microsoft Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook and Powerpoint).</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Education and Certifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, communications, business administration, public relations, or a related field. Master’s degree in business, marketing or related field is preferred</li>
<li>Google Analytics and Google AdWords certifications are highly desired</li>
<li>Experience using MailChimp, SproutSocial, Google Data Studio, and Tessitura are preferred</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Special Conditions of Employment</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>This position requires the ability to work a variable schedule, evenings, weekends, and holidays to meet operational needs.</li>
<li>Must be able to occasionally travel outside of Central Florida to attend meetings and conferences. Some local travel to off-site meetings and events is also required.</li>
</ul>
