<h2>Sales Development Representatives</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader">JFrog is looking for talented and motivated Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) to join our Sales team. As an SDR, you will be responsible for researching, contacting, and qualifying inbound potential customers.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
Initiate contact with inbound leads<br>
Research accounts, identify key players, generate interest and develop relationships with important stakeholders<br>
Provide potential prospects with information about JFrog and our product value<br>
Conduct conversations with decision makers from SMB and large Enterprise organizations<br>
Ensure prospects meet predefined qualification criteria\<br>
Schedule qualified customers for sessions<br>
Build and maintain a profitable sales pipeline<br>
Disseminate opportunities and educate Sales Managers as necessary<br>
Update salesforce to ensure efficient lead management and record-keeping<br>
</h4>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Desired Skills &amp; Experience</b><br>
Prior SaaS experience in a similar role - big advantage<br>
Ability to conduct in depth research via various online tools - must<br>
Self-starter with a track record of self-management in order to get the job done<br>
Pre-call planning and time management skills<br>
Proficient with standard corporate productivity tools (email, voicemail, MS Office,Google docs)<br>
Sales training and Salesforce experience is a plus<br>
Positive and energetic phone skills, excellent listening skills, strong writing skills<br>
Excited to work in a high-energy sales team environment</h4>
</div></div>
