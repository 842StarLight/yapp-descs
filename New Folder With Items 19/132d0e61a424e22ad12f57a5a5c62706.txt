<h2>SALESFORCE ADMINISTRATOR II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Do you have technical expertise with Salesforce and excellent customer service skills? Have you ever worked for a company whose motto is “Work hard, play hard, and love who you do it with”; who’s passionate about challenging you to think differently; and who cares about YOU the employee?</p>
<p>
This describes Holmes Murphy to a “T” as well as one of our latest job openings! We are looking for candidates that would be the perfect fit to join our team as a <b>Salesforce Administrator</b> <b>II </b>in the Information Technology department of our Waukee, IA office. If this sounds like your dream job, keep reading!</p>
<p><b>
Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze and creatively solve complex business problems leveraging the force.com platform in new and innovative ways.</li>
<li>Salesforce administration and configuration including workflow, validation rules, process builder, flows, lighting record pages, record types, data loader, dashboards, approval processes, change sets and deployments, and third-party integrations</li>
<li>Create complex reports and dashboards</li>
<li>Maintain security standards utilizing profiles, page layouts, and sharing settings.</li>
<li>Collaborate and knowledge share with a team of admins and developers</li>
<li>Monitor Salesforce releases and understand how new features can be applied to existing and new processes.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to effectively research and solve complex problems with creative and innovative solutions.</li>
<li>Knowledge of various cloud offerings of Salesforce including Sales, Service, and Communities. Marketo experience a plus.</li>
<li>Experience with Logic and Process Automation including, Visualforce, Apex, triggers, flows, process builder</li>
<li>Experience with App Development</li>
<li>Experience with Change Management including change sets</li>
<li>Understanding of sales business drivers, metrics and reporting</li>
<li>Requires effective interpersonal skills due to interaction with employees and business partner staff in all locations</li>
<li>Good oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Willing to travel for training and education of systems to our clients and employees</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>College degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field or equivalent job experience</li>
<li>Three years of Salesforce administration experience.</li>
<li>Salesforce Administrator 201 certification required</li>
</ul>
<p>If you’re ready to take your Salesforce Administrator experience to the next level, want the exciting opportunity to work for an amazing company, and you think you have what it takes to be a <b>Salesforce Administrator II</b> for Holmes Murphy, apply today!</p>
