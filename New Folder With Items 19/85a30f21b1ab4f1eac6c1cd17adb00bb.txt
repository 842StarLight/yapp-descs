<h4 class="jobSectionHeader">
Samsung NEXT Platform leverages the network, resources, and scale of Samsung to significantly impact the trajectory of NEXT startups, thereby enhancing the NEXT reputation in the global startup ecosystem. We’re looking for a seasoned expert to develop and execute better ways for delivering value to our NEXT portfolio companies across our rapidly expanding global locations, with a keen eye for customer markets and development around mobile network operators, gaming/mobile gaming, government agencies, and/or adtech in the US.</h4>
<p>
THE COMPANY</p>
<p>
Samsung NEXT started in 2012 with a vision to create breakout software and services and foster a startup culture at Samsung. Since then, our small group has become a global organization that has empowered thousands of entrepreneurs around the world who share our passion for executing against big and bold ideas and building, growing and scaling businesses.</p>
<p>
The broader NEXT team develops products, invests in startups and acquires businesses ready for scale. With a focus on cutting edge software and services such as IoT, AI, AR/VR, Data Analytics, Cybersecurity and other transformative frontier technologies, we collaborate with seasoned founders and talented companies, providing our capital, resources, expertise and deep connections.<br>
<br>
THE ROLE<br>
<br>
As a Platform BD Advisor, you will source, drive, and build key relationships between NEXT portfolio companies and large strategic partners across a diversity of tech verticals. This is designed to be a part-time and highly flexible role, up to 2.5 days per week.<br>
</p>
<p>
Things you'll be focusing on:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Meeting startups halfway, for an educational approach to understanding how to help mature early-stage startups into growth-stage startups capable of effectively partnering with global enterprises</li>
<li>
Working closely with the NEXT Platform and Ventures teams to identify specific portfolio company strategic business needs, likely working with 5-10 portfolio companies at a time.</li>
<li>
Actively coaching portfolio company leaders on the optimal way to approach strategic partnership discussions with large F500 organizations and then shepherding initial partnership explorations</li>
<li>
Sharing thought leadership publicly on panels and via written content to attract potential partners for our startups</li>
<li>
Assist as needed in the structuring and negotiating of partnerships and commercial agreements</li>
<li>
Act as an ambassador for NEXT amongst the startup, venture capital, university, and corporate communities</li>
</ul>
