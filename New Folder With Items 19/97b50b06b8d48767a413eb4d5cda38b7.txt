<h2>Vice President Systems Engineering</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At Impinj we expect to stimulate the RAIN rfid eco-system by providing technology components at different levels of encapsulation to allow partners to connect with our underlying technology at their appropriate level of capability, allowing them to create optimizations for their specific areas of focus. Our primary drivers for technology development are:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Best-in-class core technology</li>
<li>
Products that are uniquely positioned to offer best-in-class technology and robustness</li>
<li>
Vertically integrated solutions tailored to the largest opportunities that require significant technology expertise</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Role Description</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop Product Requirements in collaboration with Product Management, Marketing, Sales, Silicon Engineering, Software Engineering, Platform Engineering and the Executive Leadership Team</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with other engineering functions to decompose Products Requirements into Silicon, Software and Hardware Requirements</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with other silicon, software and hardware architects to define the architecture of the product(s)</li>
<li>
Lead advanced signal processing and algorithm development efforts to improve the performance of RAIN rfid solutions for targeted segments and use cases</li>
<li>
Lead the development of communication stack models that implement the RAIN rfid standard and proprietary extensions of the standard</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Role Attributes</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<i>
</i><i>Responsibility </i>– Ensure defined solutions are Best-in-Class as individual components, and as a solution. Maximize synergy across the portfolio with ease of adoption by customers and partners</li>
<li>
<i>
Communication skills</i> – The VP of Systems Engineering must have excellent written and oral communications skills with the ability to speak with customers, marketing, developers and executive leadership in a concise, cogent and convincing manner.</li>
<li>
<i>
Management skills</i> - This includes both organizational and technical leadership skills. The ability to lead a functional or matrixed team, which may be distributed and composed of very different skill sets. The ability to develop a team and individuals to drive adoption of best practices and methods</li>
<li>
<i>
Flexibility</i> – The VP of Systems Engineering works with various people from different areas, rapidly changing demands or even with changing business environments. Therefore, it is necessary to embrace change, advocate business needs with the team and support management initiatives.</li>
<li>
<i>
Analytic skills</i> – The ability to represent an abstract problem in the form of some finite real object of the system, which can be evaluated, designed and developed.</li>
</ul>
