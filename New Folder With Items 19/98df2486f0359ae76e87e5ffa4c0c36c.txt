<h2>Product Manager, Consumer API Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Product Manager will be responsible for overseeing a key strategic area of MINDBODY’s consumer facing products. They will be responsible for bringing services-oriented projects to market while achieving key business objectives. This role is responsible for overseeing and running a scrum team in charge of large strategic projects. The Product Manager will be responsible for creating and maintaining a backlog of work, creating business plans based on market research and data analysis, and pitching these product ideas to senior management. The Product Manager will be required to work cross functionally between the UX Design and Research, Marketing, Product Managers and Engineering teams to support the product vision and achieve strategic business objectives.</p>
