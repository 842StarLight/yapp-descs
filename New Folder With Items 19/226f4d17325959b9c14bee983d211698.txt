<h2>Collections - Account Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Gurstel Law Firm is a multi-state litigation law firm representing creditors in all aspects of the legal collection process. The Firm is headquartered in Golden Valley, Minnesota with offices in Arizona, Iowa, Nebraska, Utah, Wisconsin and California.</b></p>
<p><b>Job Title: ACCOUNT REPRESENTATIVE</b></p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>The Retail Account Representative is responsible for the collection of assigned accounts that are going through the pre-legal and judgment collection process. Responsible for recovering unpaid account balances and judgments owed by businesses and individuals in compliance with our standard operating procedures and client requirements. Account balances to be recovered by way of settlement, payment plans, garnishments, and other means of execution.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Professional communication skills with the ability to set up repayment plans and document all communication and transactions.</li>
<li>Time Management – can manage one’s own time and prioritize high volume of tasks.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications skills - Ability to write detailed letters, review reports, communicate effectively with both clients and consumer in professional and thorough matter.</li>
<li>Clerical - Knowledge of administrative and clerical procedures and systems such as word processing. The ability to manage files and records, create and send appropriate correspondence in accordance with state and federal laws, and prepare collection letters for attorney review and other office procedures and terminology.</li>
<li>Customer Service - Knowledge of principles and processes for providing customer service. This includes customer needs assessment, meeting quality standards for services, and evaluation of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Judgment and Decision Making - Consider the relative costs and benefits of potential actions to choose the most appropriate one and make proper recommendations for client review.</li>
<li>Complex Problem Solving – Identify complex problems and review related information to develop and evaluate options and implement solutions.</li>
<li>Tools/Technology Required: MS Word, MS Excel, MS Outlook, Word Perfect, copier, fax, and scanner.</li>
<li>All other duties as may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
