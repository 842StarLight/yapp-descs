<h2>Front Desk Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>NOW HIRING: Front Desk Administrator for our Issaquah location!</p>
<p>Those with modern, classic style and elevated customer service skills encouraged to apply.</p>
<p>At Weldon, the Front Desk Assistant is more than just a receptionist, they are a concierge. They are our first opportunity to engage with clients, and our last opportunity to impress them. They are our product knowledge experts, and our best brand ambassadors. Our FDA's are responsible for creating seamless, luxury experiences that consistently exceed expectations.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Represents elevated brand image through professional hygiene and dress standards</li>
<li>Greets clients as they arrive and ensures they are comfortable</li>
<li>Answers phones and schedules appointments using salon booking software</li>
<li>Builds familiarity and relationships with returning clients</li>
<li>Makes calls to follow up and confirm upcoming appointments</li>
<li>Offers and sells appropriate retail products</li>
<li>Monitors retail stock levels and replenishes products as necessary</li>
<li>Fulfills general front desk, administrative, and clerical tasks</li>
<li>Maintains shop cleanliness and safety standards</li>
<li>Assists stylists whenever possible</li>
<li>Uses critical thinking to solve problems independently</li>
<li>Must be able to perform any and all other duties assigned by supervisor</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Organized, professional, reliable, accountable</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal, communication, and listening skills</li>
<li>Flexible and able to adapt to business needs</li>
<li>Able to operate point of sale system and handle sensitive client information</li>
<li>Passionate and knowledgeable of salon industry products and services</li>
<li>Comfortable with selling products and services consistently</li>
<li>Able to work as part of a team</li>
<li>May be required to stand for extended periods of time</li>
<li>Previous salon experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>BENEFITS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Health Insurance, Dental available</li>
<li>PTO, Continuing Education Program</li>
<li>Competitive Hourly Wages and Commission</li>
</ul>
