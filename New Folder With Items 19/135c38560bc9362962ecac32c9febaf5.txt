<h2>FINANCIAL ANALYST</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p>Amsted Rail has an immediate opening for a Financial Analyst at its Granite City, IL location!
</p>
<p>Amsted Rail is the world’s leading provider of fully integrated bogie systems for the heavy haul freight market, with facilities spanning 40 locations across 10 countries and 6 continents. Through their state-of-the-art manufacturing processes, Amsted Rail is redefining industry standards for innovation and technology, providing customers worldwide with unmatched performance and reliability for the long haul.
</p>
<p>The Financial Analyst performs various duties to support the accounting, finance and reporting functions of the business and its operations. The ideal candidate is a detail-oriented, highly motivated self-starter interested in an accounting role that involves learning a business and working closely with operations while ensuring the accuracy and integrity of financial statements and reports.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with the month-end closing process, including preparation of journal entries, general ledger account analysis and variance analysis</li>
<li>Reconcile assigned accounts monthly with a focus of clearing reconciling items in a timely manner</li>
<li>Prepare monthly reports and supporting schedules for inclusion in Management’s monthly operations reporting package</li>
<li>Utilize existing systems and tools to enhance or streamline current reports to develop capability for real time data-driven analysis
</li>
<li>Assist with tracking and reporting of critical performance metrics and cost savings initiatives at North American foundries</li>
<li>Provide financial analysis and support for capital investments in manufacturing operations and innovation initiatives
</li>
<li>Assist with preparation of quarterly forecasts and annual budgets</li>
<li>Manage inter-company transfer sales ensuring accurate profitability for each business unit</li>
<li>Identify, recommend and implement innovative improvements to existing processes and internal controls to support operational improvements and cost saving initiatives</li>
<li>Perform additional tasks and ad-hoc projects as needed</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>B.S. in Accounting/Finance</li>
<li>MBA / CPA preferred</li>
<li>1- 3 years of progressive accounting (or related) experience, manufacturing experience a plus</li>
<li>Experience with ERP systems (SAP a plus)</li>
<li>Experience with consolidation software (i.e., CPM) a plus</li>
</ul>

Our commitment to employee’s professional satisfaction includes creating space and opportunities to be creative and an integral part of our innovation journey and business success; for employee’s personal fulfillment, we invest in everyone’s growth and development while ensuring a work environment that values them as an individual, with their unique set of strengths and beliefs.
</p>
