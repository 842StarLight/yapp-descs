<p>PT Leasing Consultant Baco Realty-Redding</p>
<p>BACO Realty Corporation has an immediate opening for a Part-time Leasing Consultant for our fast-paced, professionally managed apartment communities in Redding, CA. This position will report to the Property Manager &amp; District Supervisor.</p>
<p>BACO Realty Corporation is a dynamic professional property management firm with regional offices in Southern California, Northern California, Nevada and Tennessee. With over 70 apartment communities and single-family homes, we have a variety of housing options and locations to serve our residents. We offer a quality experience to our employees in the form of career growth opportunities, as well as a generous benefit package, including medical, dental, vision, and life insurance. Elective insurance additionally offered. Benefits also include vacation, sick leave, holidays, floating holiday and 401K.</p>
<p>Check us out online at www.bacoapartments.com</p>
<p>PRIMARY DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>* Completes lease forms or agreements, and collects rental deposits.<br>* Responsible for ensuring target apartments are ready for showing; responsible for maintaining work areas and office in a clean and orderly manner; need to have an awareness of the “curb appeal” of the property.<br>* Responsible for the proper maintenance of all resident and property files.<br>* Thorough knowledge of lease terms, specifications and all property policies.<br>* Fully informed of current rental rates, sizes, locations and all amenities of property<br>* Knowledgeable of required telephone and sales techniques.<br>* Develop full knowledge of application information required, screening processes, and policies regarding rentals.<br>* Maintain the on-going resident retention program under the direction of the property manager.<br>* Maintain contact with all apartment locator services and local businesses to provide informational material and update periodically.<br>* Responsible for maintaining a professional appearance and attitude at all times.<br>* Responsible for maintaining the Lease Renewal Program under the direction of the property manager.<br>* Responsible for reporting any unusual or extraordinary circumstances regarding the residents or the property.</p>
<p>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:<br>* Reliable transportation, a valid driver’s license and current proof of auto insurance required. Must be able to use own vehicle to transport self to different property sites to tour prospective residents.<br>* Excellent customer service skills, organized, detail-oriented, team player, reliable and capable of multitasking in a fast-paced work environment with basic understanding of social media marketing and computer knowledge: MS Word, Excel, and Outlook; Yardi software experience is a plus.<br>* Sales or retail experience required. Experience in Property Management is a plus but not required.<br>* Frequent walking, bending, and climbing stairs; Regular exposure to outside weather conditions.<br>* Ability to lift and/or move up to 10 pounds.<br>* Typing required.</p>
