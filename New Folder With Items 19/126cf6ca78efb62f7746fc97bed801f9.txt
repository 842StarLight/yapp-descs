DailyPay is looking for an organized, collaborative, and detail-oriented contracts paralegal with experience with MSAs, NDAs, and strong legal research and writing skills. You will report directly to the Head of Legal &amp; Compliance and work closely with sales, our CFO, and all other internal departments.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Review and negotiate NDAs, sales agreements, service orders, and SAAS agreements</li>
<li>Conduct legal research and writing</li>
<li>Assist Head of Legal &amp; Compliance with creation of internal policies and procedures</li>
<li>Assist with creation of materials for company-wide trainings</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>2-3 years of experience in a law-firm or in-house setting</li>
<li>Direct experience in the redlining process</li>
<li>Strong analytical, verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to take initiative, operate independently, and work cross-functionally</li>
<li>Strong time management skills</li>
</ul>
