<h2>Sr Commercial Underwriter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Sr. Commercial underwriter will be responsible for making informed credit extension recommendations to the Constructive Loans LLC Credit Committee. The borrowing clientele will consist of experienced and non-experienced Real Estate Investors seeking financing solutions for residential investment projects. The underwriter will play a key role in maintaining the quality of the firm’s assets and mitigating financial losses through the form of a detailed analysis of a borrower’s credit, financial profile, track record and collateral characteristics. Extensive knowledge of generally acceptable underwriting and lending practices is essential and required. The underwriter will assure that all credit recommendations and property financing loan approvals conform to Constructive Loans LLC Commercial Underwriting Guidelines and Investor purchase requirements. The underwriter will be responsible for maintaining all Service Level Agreements established by the Company. Extended work hours may be necessary during peak workflow periods. The Sr Commercial underwriter should always act in a manner consistent with the Company’s commitment to providing an outstanding experience for its clients.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
Develop a thorough understanding of all Constructive Loans LLC Commercial product and program underwriting guidelines, documentation requirements and processing, underwriting, closing and post-closing procedures.<br>
Work closely with the mortgage originators and processors to qualify mortgage loans.<br>
Work closely with the processors and closers to clear closing conditions.<br>
Review all mortgage loans for credit worthiness and compliance with Constructive Loans LLC Commercial underwriting standards.<br>
Make approval or denial recommendations for all mortgage applications.<br>
Assure that all mortgages are of investment quality acceptable for sale to the Company’s secondary market conduits.<br>
Must meet departmental minimum standards<br>
Ensure department security procedures are adhered to.<br>
Consistently maintain expected level of production.<br>
Act as a liaison between sales and operations management team.<br>
Identify and describe compensating factors when communicating loan exception requests when warranted.<br>
Produce detailed Credit Memorandums addressed to a Loan Approval and Credit Committee.<br>
Resolve various inquiries through clear and detailed explanations.</p>
<p><b>Required Qualifications:</b><br>
5-8 years of mortgage loan underwriting / credit analysis experience or comparable work experience</p>
<p>High-Net Worth and/or manual underwriting experience highly preferred<br>
Ability to work independently<br>
Self-motivated<br>
Must be detail oriented<br>
Ability to handle multiple priorities<br>
Ability to work in fast-paced environment and meet deadlines<br>
Excellent written and oral communication skills with the ability to effectively handle conflict and resolve problems<br>
Strong communication, analytical, organizational and problem-solving skills<br>
Strong working knowledge of Microsoft Office software programs<br>
Bachelor’s Degree preferred or equivalent and relevant work experience</p>
