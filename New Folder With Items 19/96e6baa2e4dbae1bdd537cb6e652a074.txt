<h2>Inspector Master / Trainer IV</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<div>Perform vehicle inspections and prepare related documentation of overall vehicle condition in support of the processing and sale of client/customer consigned vehicles in lane and online.</div>
<div>
</div>
<i>
Additionally, serves as training member of the Inspections team for an operating location with the primary responsibilities of providing vehicle inspections and inspection training and ICP testing support for the auction through classroom and hands-on training of Vehicle Entry staff.</i><br>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Verify vehicle identification.</li>
<li>
Enter basic vehicle identification information to database.</li>
<li>
Inspect vehicle equipment and options and document results.</li>
<li>
Assess and database enter any exterior vehicle damage.</li>
<li>
Take photographs/images of damages</li>
<li>
Inspect and enter condition of vehicle interior, tires, frame and mechanical components.</li>
<li>
Complete and verify data entry in support of documentation of vehicle condition.</li>
<li>
Perform post sale inspections (PSI) and enter results in appropriate database.</li>
<li>
Perform and document walk around inspections in support of online vehicle exchange (OVE) process.</li>
<li>
Review completed vehicle condition reports with inspectors – confirm and provide advice regarding report results.</li>
<li>
Attend meetings to share and receive job related information and complete training requirements.</li>
<li><i>
Provides training for Inspectors on all duties and responsibilities of the Inspector role through hands-on and classroom training as appropriate; trains other Vehicle Entry staff on use of related programs as needed.</i></li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalent required.</li>
<li>
ASE certification or background in automotive repair cost estimating preferred; ICAR Certifications a plus.</li>
<li>
Valid Driver’s License and safe driving record required.</li>
<li>
Basic computer software skills required.</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask required.</li>
<li>
Must be detail oriented.</li>
<li>
General knowledge of body and mechanical work preferred.</li>
<li>
Regularly required to stand, walk, reach, talk and hear.</li>
<li>
Frequently required to stoop, kneel, crouch and climb.</li>
<li>
Medium strength.</li>
<li>
Previous body shop experience, mechanical experience, estimating experience a plus, but not required.</li>
<li>
Vision abilities required include close, distance and color vision, depth perception and ability to adjust focus.</li>
</ul>
