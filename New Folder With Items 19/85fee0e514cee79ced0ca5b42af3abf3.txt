ROLE DESCRIPTION<br>

We are seeking a Content Marketing Manager for our Brand and Content Marketing efforts for Amazon Business. Brand and Content Marketing will span all industry segments with the objective to drive both awareness and support both lead generation and account based marketing campaigns.<br>

You will work closely with field marketing, customer engagement and sales to drive measurable results. You will be able to think big, invent and lead the creation and successful execution of a robust content marketing strategy, including defining and managing to performance metrics.<br>
