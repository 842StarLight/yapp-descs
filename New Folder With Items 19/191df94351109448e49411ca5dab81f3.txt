<h2>Regional Sales Manager | Pacific Region</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>Reporting to the SVP of National Sales, the Regional Sales Manager role will be responsible for managing Class Valuation’s success by leading the sales charge within a focused region. Focusing on your prospective targets within your region, you will be responsible for acquiring new order volume through the acquisition of new customers within your region.<br>
<br>
<b>In addition to our Key Skills &amp; Traits, here are specific responsibilities for this opportunity:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Maintaining an understanding of your region at all levels</li>
<li>Keeping tight records in Salesforce</li>
<li>Create and execute regional sales strategy to get the most out of your territory within the fiscal year and achieve all sales targets</li>
<li>Build challenger models against industry data to show how their business would benefit from rolling out Class Valuation’s services. Cause them to become a client through the execution of a proven sales methodology</li>
<li>Execute a stage driven sales methodology</li>
<li>Develop strategic account plans that target large prospective clients within your region</li>
<li>Work with “client onboarding” teams to seamlessly get your new clients onboarded and ordering.</li>
<li>Establish premium brand status within your region through the execution of Sales and Marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Create and execute trade show plan for the “Agreed shows we will be attending” work with sales and marketing leadership on the pre-trade show plans to maximize show attendance and justify self-attendance</li>
<li>Must achieve all sales goals and targets as set forth within the forecast</li>
<li>Position yourself as a trusted advisor and utilize a consultative model to build influence within industry</li>
<li>Work your LinkedIn network and social ecosphere to influence the market and your region towards Class Valuation</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>

</div>
<ul>
<li><b>Mortgage industry experience is a must</b></li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred</li>
