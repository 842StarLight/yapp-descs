 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p><b>Regional Property Director - Lewis Apartment Communities</b><br>
<br>
<b>Partner. Learn. Succeed. Grow.</b></p>
<p><b>
Lewis Apartment Communities</b>, a division of the Lewis Group of Companies, one of the nation’s largest privately-owned real estate developers, is actively seeking an experienced Regional Property Director to join the Sacramento regional office.<br>
<br>
<b><i>You are a leader. Role Model. Guide. Your staff are your greatest asset and you live that each work day.</i></b></p>
<p>
In your role, you would report directly to the Vice President of Property Management and provide supervision of overall operations at each Lewis-owned community in your residential portfolio, over 900 conventional units, all of which are located in the greater Sacramento area of Northern California.</p>
<p>
To be right for the job, and ready for it, you will have a minimum of <b>five years of multi-site residential property management experience.
</b></p>
<p><b>What you will do:</b> As one of our regional leaders, you will be responsible for multiple apartment communities, with emphasis on the following:</p>
<ul>
<li><i>
Leadership &amp; talent management with a high level of professionalism</i></li>
<li><i>
Coaching, mentoring, motivation and supervision of your community employees</i></li>
<li><i>
Preparation of annual plans and monthly financial reviews/variance reporting</i></li>
<li><i>
80% field work conducting site visits at properties within the portfolio.
</i></li>
<li><i>Continually evaluate each asset, identify key issues and work with assigned team to develop and implement plans of action for each community and its team</i></li>
<li><i>
Implementation and oversight of capital improvement projects</i></li>
<li><i>
Conduct regular property inspections and weekly site/staff visits with 80% or more of your time allocated to working in the field</i></li>
<li><i>
Maintain in-depth knowledge and analysis of competition/pricing in assigned market area(s)</i></li>
<li><i>
Proactive leadership and execution using key performance indicators as a compass to evaluate performance.
</i></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other requirements and preferences in our top candidate:</b> A BA/BS degree is a plus. Fair Housing knowledge is required. Solid MS Office skills are required. Experience with Onesite and/or Yieldstar software a plus. Marketing and sales experience is desirable. And of course, you will have great communication skills, written and verbal.</p>
<p><b>
Why you will do it:</b> Founded in 1955, the Lewis Group of Companies is one of the nation's largest privately held real estate organizations with more than $2 billion in assets. You will join a team dedicated to providing a superior living experience for families and an engaging career for its employees.<br>
<br>
<b>Growth:</b> Lewis has plans to develop even more land, growing our portfolio of Northern CA communities. With this in mind, the size of your portfolio will likely expand in the coming years, helping to grow your career as an asset management professional.<br>
<br>
<b>Salary &amp; Perks:</b> In addition to a competitive annual salary, Lewis offers annual bonus potential, a $500/month auto allowance, $100/month cell phone allowance, a variety of medical/dental benefits plans, a 401k with company match and immediate vesting, vacation/sick time, and much more.</p>
<p><b>
We’re all in. We hope you are too.
</b></p>
<p>This is your opportunity to join a name-recognized leader in real estate development and property management. Join Lewis Apartment Communities and you will be an important part of a winning team with a long history of successful apartment development and management.<br>
