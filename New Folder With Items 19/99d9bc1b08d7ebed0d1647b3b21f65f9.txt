<h2>IT Cloud Advisory Architect- Eden Prairie, MN</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Optum is looking for a Cloud Advisory Architect who is passionate about helping customer teams accelerate their journey to the cloud. Our architects are experienced technical professionals with the depth and breadth needed to help a variety of teams, regardless of their current cloud maturity. As a Cloud Advisory Architect, you will primarily work directly with customers, helping them to expedite their journey to the cloud and reducing the amount of time it might otherwise take them to recognize value from it.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
To find success in this role, you will need to partner with other architects, operations &amp; information security teams, as well as cloud engineering teams to craft the proper solutions and roadmaps for our customers as we evolve our cloud maturity. The ideal candidate will have production cloud experience, as well as past success working across the enterprise to accelerate cloud adoption, as well as experience leading deep architecture discussions and building technology roadmaps. They must be self-motivated with a demonstrated ability to think strategically about business, products, and technical challenges. Cloud Advisory Architects should have a passion for educating, training, and designing solutions for customers.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
Responsibilities<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Guide customers through internal processes on cloud adoption and enablement</li>
<li>
Partner with technical training teams on curating offerings available to customers and evangelize the offerings within the enterprise</li>
<li>
Build and/or deliver technical workshops for customers with varying levels of cloud platform experience</li>
<li>
Create content for, and engage with, the development community in the form of blog entries, forum posts, hackathons, office hours &amp; other mediums in order to raise interest, evaluation, and adoption of cloud</li>
<li>
Provide advisory services to customers based on a Statement of Work (SOW) that may include (but are not limited to):<br>
<div><ul>
<li>Cost estimation guidance</li>
<li>
Creation of architectures to exceed the needs of the customer, based on discovery that you will conduct and that adhere to internal policies</li>
<li>
Review of existing architecture(s) to determine gaps in best practices and adherence to internal policies</li>
<li>
Application modernization patterns</li>
<li>
Migration planning/guidance from non-cloud platforms to cloud platforms</li>
<li>
DevOps best practices including CI/CD, application &amp; infrastructure pipelines, Infrastructure as Code, etc.</li>
<li>
Engage with partners/vendors as needed to facilitate additional hands-on support for customers<br>
</li>
</ul></div>
</li>
</ul>
