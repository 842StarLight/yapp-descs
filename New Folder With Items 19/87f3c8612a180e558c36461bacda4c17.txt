<p><b>Senior Manager – Data Analytics</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Location: </b>Philadelphia<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Role Description</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Hands-on position that is accountable for the design, development, implementation, and support of the Big Data Analytics solutions on cloud in collaboration with Engineering and Data Operations team.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with business owners and teammates to solve business problems, which may include standard tool or custom algorithm development leveraging big data.</li>
<li>
Dive into large, noisy, and complex real-world customer TV and Digital Ad viewing data to do Marketing campaigns performance measurement using Big data Platform (DataBricks, Pyspark).</li>
<li>
Responsible for integrating data from multiple data sources (On-Premise &amp; Cloud) and create aggregated data sets for analytics.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Functional Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong understanding of database structures, query languages (e.g. SQL), fundamentals of mathematics, distributed systems (Hadoop), data science, and statistical concepts.</li>
<li>
Experience consolidating and integrating data from multiple sources (On-Premise &amp; Cloud).</li>
<li>
Ability to analyze, transform and aggregate large data sets (Big Data) using BI tools (Hive QL, Pyspark, Notebooks, AWS Athena, AWS Glue).</li>
<li>
Ability to covert Hive jobs to Spark Jobs using Pyspark and fine tune spark jobs for performance.</li>
<li>
Ability to Architect and Design Big Data Analytics solutions on cloud (AWS).</li>
<li>
Ability to build predictive models using historical data.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Media/Advertising industry.</li>
</ul>
