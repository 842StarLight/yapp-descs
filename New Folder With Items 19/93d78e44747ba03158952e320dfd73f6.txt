<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<b>Position Summary</b><br>
<br>
The Dispatch Team reports to the Yard Manager and General Manager and be the key liaison between the dispatchers and field team members (i.e. Management, Business Development, Drivers). The team will be the source of internal and external customer relationships related to the dispatching/scheduling of service technicians, customer service equipment and service issues.<br>
<br>
Each Dispatcher is responsible for the dispatch operations including assignments, schedules and dispatching drivers, trucks and equipment to work sites and customers on time and with a methodical process.<br>
<br>
Communicates with Customers and Yard Management to determine driver coordination and routing schedules and act as the single point of contact for drivers. Working with appropriate operational systems, monitors locations and assignments of all drivers, trucks and equipment including compliance of DOT and Company work hour policies and regulations.<br>
<br>
Represent the Company in a professional manner while interfacing with customers on product, delivery, pricing and related issues.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Qualifications</b><br>
<br>
Other duties may be assigned. A Dispatcher in this position must have the ability to:</div>
<ul>
<li>Best candidates will possess exceptional computer skills, highly knowledgeable and skilled in Microsoft products and Electronic Logging Devices (ELD).</li>
<li>Possess very strong interpersonal skills and be able to discuss dispatch issues with coworkers, customers and managers.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain positive customer relationships; represent the company in professional manner at all times; listen and resolve first line customer complaints and issues; provide feedback to Lead Dispatcher, Yard Manager and/or Business Development regarding customer complaints or issues on pricing, quality, credit et cetera.</li>
<li>Process customer orders in a timely and efficient manner such as (but not limited to) taking orders over the phone and/or computer, filling orders preparing orders be shipped, and log daily sales.</li>
<li>Route trucks, drivers and equipment; monitor and direct/redirect location and work assignment of all drivers and trucks.</li>
<li>Single point of contact for drivers.</li>
<li>Communicate with drivers on delivery status.</li>
<li>Works cooperatively as part of a team to efficiently meet customer needs.</li>
<li>Responsible for same-day and/or emergency routing of deliveries and services to customers.</li>
<li>Ensure accidents and/or unsafe conditions are reported and handled properly. Determines, along with shift management, appropriate actions including alerting the safety group of Health, Safety and Environmental (HSE) incidents, accidents, and injuries.</li>
<li>Maintain fleet in a safe manner in accordance with company and manufacture.</li>
<li>Prepare daily records and memos, and maintain reports and company records.</li>
<li>Review invoices for accuracy before they are processed including clarifying and/or resolving any open tickets and/or invoices.</li>
<li>Provide timely reports to Management and Business Development as requested.</li>
<li>Meet this position's physical demands.</li>
<li>Possess a broad knowledge of company products.</li>
<li>Other duties and projects as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
