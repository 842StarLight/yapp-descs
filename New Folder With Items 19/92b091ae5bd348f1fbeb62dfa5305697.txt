JOB PURPOSE
<br><br>
To drive revenue growth through creating and executing digital marketing strategies that generates leads. Position drives the digital vision and strategy and manages the website properties, email marketing and SEO/SEM campaigns. Responsible for stimulating an agile marketing environment that allows for a test and learn environment so we are constantly optimizing for lead generation. Responsible for analyzing regular reporting and measurement of site traffic, leads and revenue generated through marketing campaigns in the U.S. Position manages lead generational across multiple member segments and product offerings.
<br><br>
This position interacts closely with the EVP, Sales Director, client partners, and internal stake-holders to drive unique personalized customer experiences for our clients and prospective clients resulting in revenue generation and customer satisfaction.
<br><br>
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
<b>Lead Generation Strategy :</b> Own and drive lead generation strategy for marketing programs and work closely with other support teams, sales teams, PMO, and IT to implement strategy.
<br><br>
<b>SEO/SEM Strategy :</b> Work with agency partners and team members to devise and continuously improve SEO/SEM 2015 plan.
<br><br>
<b>Email Marketing Strategy :</b> Develops and deploys custom, tailored email and drip campaigns to targeted industry and product lines. Drive lead generation for new business revenue and cross-sell campaigns through the email channel.
<br><br>
<b>Agency &amp; Internal/External Partner Management :</b> Responsible for day-to-day relationship management with agency partners for all digital initiatives, as well as collaborative team leads in IT, Sales Enablement, PMO, Finance and Sales.
<br><br>
<b>Reporting/Marketing Analytics :</b> Works with analytics team to produce analytics/reporting on a regular basis and adhoc reports as requested. Responsible for reporting a holistic picture of the Omni channel digital effectiveness.
<br><br>
<b>Content Marketing :</b> Utilizing reporting/analytics collaborate with Communications Manager and help to define and drive plans for content marketing assets such as blogs, email campaigns, webinars, white papers, infographics, etc.
<br><br>
<b>Marketing Technology :</b> Own strategy and drive improvements for all platforms (ClickDimensions and Hubspot), project management software (Workzone), and CMS (Ektron). Partner with sales enablement and IT to constantly improve these platforms
<br><br>
<b>Budget Management :</b> Lead annual and quarterly planning for the digital strategy including the expense budget, and forecasting, focusing on ROI.
<br><br>
<b>Website Presence :</b> Supports the client vision with digital strategy. Maintains and constantly improves website experience, content and visibility. Support ongoing strategy for customer portals and eCommerce strategies.
