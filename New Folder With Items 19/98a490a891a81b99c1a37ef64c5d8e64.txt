<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>What You’ll Do</b><br>
<b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Key decision-maker for the global go-to-market strategy.</li>
<li>Define our personas and messaging by global segment</li>
<li>Define the ideal customer profile and buyer personas.</li>
<li>Conduct regular market research and define the Total Addressable Market.</li>
<li>Gather and own competitor intelligence and leverage that for our campaigns.</li>
<li>Partner with the product team and establish a process for the product roadmap.</li>
<li>Liaison with the global sales team to ensure the needs of the sales team are being met</li>
<li>Drive our content strategy for educating the market and acquiring new business.</li>
<li>Subject matter expert to ensure marketing initiatives are aligned with the needs of the market</li>
<li>Own our sales enablement content.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain our battle cards for key market segments</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Who We Need</b><br>
<b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years’ experience in product marketing or product management in the B2B SaaS space.</li>
<li>Razor-sharp writing and presentation skills with a knack for distilling complex ideas into compelling copy.</li>
<li>Proven understanding of B2B SaaS sales process, ideally at a FinTech provider, and experience working at the intersection of product, marketing, and sales.</li>
<li>Ability to manage expectations, opinions, and feedback from multiple stakeholders and on multiple projects at all times.</li>
<li>Demonstrated talent and appreciation for both qualitative and quantitative research and creating recommendations, strategies, and outputs based on that research.</li>
</ul>
