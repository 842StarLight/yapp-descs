<h2>Assistant Chief Pilot - Pilot Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Be a part of a team that is at the leading edge of flight safety, flight training, and flight operations. As an Assistant Chief Pilot, you will have the opportunity to work with professionals from across the industry and positively affect safety and operations of Boeing flight operations and that of our customers, world-wide. You will interact with pilots, managers, regulators, executives, peers, supporting team personnel, and Subject Matter Experts. This is a high visibility position. You will be leading a group of high-performing contract instructor pilots, reporting directly to the Chief Pilot, Pilot Services in Air Crew Operations – part of Training &amp; Professional Services.</p>
<p><br>
The successful candidate will manage contract pilots based all over the world. With occasional opportunities to act as pilot-in-command on flights.<br>
The Pilot Services Team is responsible for supporting Direct Sale Training and occasionally supporting Pro Forma Entry Into Service work in support of the Flight Training - Airplane team.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Operates aircraft and manages instructor pilots performing aircrew duties, including activities to prepare for and conduct safe flight operations; applies crew resource management principles to the operation of aircraft.</li>
<li>
Performs and manages activities to develop and conduct familiarization, qualification, standardization, flight and ground instruction for company and customer aircrew. Develops and executes project and process plans, implements policies and procedures and sets operational goals.</li>
<li>
Acquires resources for projects and processes, provides technical management of suppliers and leads process improvements.</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains relationships and partnerships with customers, stakeholders, peers, partners and direct reports.</li>
<li>
Provides oversight and approval of technical approaches, products and processes. Manages, develops, trains and evaluates instructor pilots.</li>
<li>
Job requires FAA ATP or Commercial certificate with the appropriate endorsements.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>At Boeing, we are all innovators on a mission to connect, protect, explore and inspire. From the sea bed to outer space, you’ll learn and grow, contributing to work that shapes the world. Find your future with us.</p>
