<h2>Associate Implementation Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The PayLease Project Manager will work with our new and existing clients to ensure the successful implementation of our payment, billing and utility expense management software services. To be successful in the role the Project Manager should have an expert level of knowledge in both project management methodologies and is adept at quickly analyzing and identifying client business needs. As a project manager, you own everything related to the successful implementation of all PayLease solutions and serve as the primary point of contact for both internal and external stakeholders. Our project managers possess a deep understanding of the client’s business goals and develop strong rapport to effectively create and execute onboarding project plans. As a member of the Professional Services team, you are the first point of contact in our clients’ post-sales experience and are responsible for ensuring an above average level of satisfaction with the onboarding process. Along with managing successful implementation projects, the Project Manager will meet both team and personal goals as defined by the Professional Services Manager.</p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct client workflow reviews, define the project scope and objectives, ensure all relevant stakeholders are involved in the plan and proactively identify potential issues with the technical feasibility of clients’ requirements</li>
<li>Manage project scope as agreed with the client and ensure that the project plan is fully executed on time and within scope</li>
<li>Create and maintain required project artifacts including project schedule, requirement documents and progress reports</li>
<li>Coordinate all internal and external resources for successful execution of projects</li>
<li>Liaise with the sales team during the Sale-to-Services transition and lead the Services-to-Support transition</li>
<li>Effectively communicate project expectations and progress to our clients, team members and the executive team in a clear and timely manner</li>
<li>Manage the creation and completion of product work orders</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with PayLease specifications and applicable regulatory bodies</li>
<li>Follow the established policies, procedures and workflows outlined for successful implementation</li>
<li>Continually strive to improve project management processes by enhancing best practices and proactively identifying areas of improvement</li>
<li>Embrace change while promoting the PayLease culture and spirit of teamwork</li>
<li>Demonstrate an increasing level of expertise of PayLease products and partner integrations</li>
<li>Contribute to ongoing internal content creation, documentation and audits</li>
<li>Stay informed and understand development release notes and how platform updates will impact product set-up</li>
</ul>
<p>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with CRM, project management, support &amp; ticketing software systems (SRP, Salesforce, JIRA, Smartsheets, Microsoft Office and Google Docs)</li>
<li>Proven ability to work in a fast paced, team centered work environment</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in the use of the internet (functions, navigating, researching, etc.)</li>
<li>Excellent communication – verbal, written, interpersonal with strong active listening skills</li>
<li>Ability to demonstrate effective problem solving skills and strong customer orientation</li>
<li>Detail-oriented with a positive and inquisitive attitude</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, prioritize, and collaborate with multiple departments and stakeholders</li>
<li>Exhibit a high degree of self-motivation and drive</li>
</ul>
<p>PREFERRED SKILLS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or an equivalent combination of training and experience</li>
<li>PayLease, industry &amp; client knowledge a plus</li>
<li>PMP certification a plus</li>
</ul>
