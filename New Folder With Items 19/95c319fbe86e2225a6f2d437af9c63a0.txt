<p>Under limited supervision, develops and executes strategy for one or more marketing initiatives. Resolves problems and makes recommendations related to complex matters and projects. Provides specialist-level analysis in one or more marketing disciplines. Recommends project budgets and forecasts anticipated budget spending. Coaches others in less senior positions.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
1. Product Development – Leads product development efforts for FedEx Fulfillment, FedEx Returns, or FedEx Hold at Location by passionately advocating on behalf of the customer, defining business requirements for new features, establishing success metrics, and developing and communicating product requirements in an Agile environment.<br>
2. Product Delivery – Brings new features to market by developing the implementation plan, coordinating cross-functionally with key stakeholders, establishing time schedules, and project managing the features from concept through tactical execution.<br>
3. Cross-functional Collaboration – Partners with IT, customer service, sales, operations and other groups across FedEx to manage and deliver business capabilities and system solutions. Drive business process changes necessary to simplify and optimize customer operations.<br>
4. Customer Advocate – Designs and conducts research and market analysis to build a deeper understanding of our target customer's needs. Ongoing communication with customers to understand needs and pain points.<br>
5. Subject Matter Expert – Serves as a subject matter expert both internally and externally.<br>
<br>
<b>Minimum Qualifications:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree/equivalent in Marketing, Finance, Computer Science or related business discipline.</li>
<li>
Three (3) years experience in marketing, advertising, sales administration, or related discipline.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the broad application of principles, theories, concepts and techniques in marketing disciplines, project management and information analysis.</li>
<li>
Strong human relations and communication/presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Moderately advanced organizational, quantitative and analytical skills, including experience in database marketing.</li>
<li>
Experience leading moderately complex initiatives in a corporate environment.</li>
<li>
Customer-focused orientation</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record of managing a successful product feature through its life-cycle, preferably software</li>
<li>
Experience working on an e-commerce fulfillment, Returns, or Hold products</li>
<li>
Experience working in a SAFe agile environment</li>
<li>
Innovative, results-oriented, self-starter, capable of coordinating projects simultaneously in a fast-paced team environment</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills with ability to build rapport with all levels of the organization as well as internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Skilled at working effectively with cross functional teams; strongly collaborative</li>
</ul>
