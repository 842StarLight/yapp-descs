<h2>Assistant Beverage Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SUMMARY: Supervises and coordinates the beverage service of all Club dining outlets, bars, Member and non-Member events and Club functions, as well as the Wine Shop retail store. Assist Food and Beverage Service Manager with planning, purchasing, executing and analyzing the entire beverage operation by performing the following duties personally or through subordinate employees.</b></h1>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>and other duties as may be assigned related to this position.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Attends weekly F &amp; B Meeting and any other meetings as assigned. Works closely with the Food and Beverage Manager and Food and Beverage Service Manager in developing the beverage program.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Holds daily lineup with Wine Shop and assigned beverage staff.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Plans and prepares work schedules for all bar coverage (Club, non-Club, Member, non-Member, and Wine Shop) and assigns employees to specific duties.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Coordinates purchases and sales and prepares merchandise displays and potential advertising copy for Wine Shop. Aids Members in the selection of wines, beverages, and other items.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Assists F&amp;B Service Manager with purchasing of alcoholic beverages for the Wine Shop and Club as needed. Receives, stores and inventories all products using Northstar.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Ensures the proper execution of monthly inventory of Wine Shop and Bars.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Coordinates consumption reports to enable proper billing of F &amp; B functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Ensures cleanliness of all areas of responsibility.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Spearheads maintenance &amp; repair of Wine Shop, bars and areas directly related to the beverage department.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Responsible for cost controls and proper utilization of stock.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Works with F&amp;B Service Manager and House Committee to plan all wine lists and wine pairings or promotions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Ensures proper billing of beverages in all areas and is vigilant in his efforts to improve these procedures.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Institutes ongoing sales methods and techniques to increase beverage revenues and Member satisfaction.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Maintains high standards in personal appearance and cleanliness.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Executes any necessary disciplinary actions for beverage employees with the supervision of the Food and Beverage Service Manager.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Adheres to departmental and company safety standards.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Supervises, motivates, trains and evaluates all beverage employees.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Prints analysis of actual and potential beverage sales based on costs on a monthly basis.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Responsible for keeping current with all trends in the beverage area.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITES: Manages subordinate employees. Is responsible for the overall direction, coordination, and evaluation of all beverage employees. Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with the Club’s policies, procedures and applicable laws. Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring, and training employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving problems.</b></h1>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>QUALIFICATIONS: To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</b></h2>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE: One year certificate from a college or a technical school and a minimum of three years prior experience in a similar position which incorporates customer relations skills and a general knowledge of wine; or an equivalent combination of education and experience.</b></h2>
<p><b>CERTIFICATES, LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS</b>: Valid driver’s license required for delivering goods. Florida Safe Serve Alcohol Certificate required. Florida Safe Food Handling Certificate required.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS: </b>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
 <p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to stand; walk; use hands to finger, handle or feel; and reach with hands and arms and may occasionally be required to sit; climb or balance; and stoop, kneel, crouch or crawl. The employee may regularly lift and/or move up to 50 pounds and may occasionally lift and/or move up to 100 pounds. To perform the essential functions of this job, the employee must have the sensory abilities to taste, hear, smell, and touch while cooking. The employee must be able to speak and to identify and distinguish colors</p>
