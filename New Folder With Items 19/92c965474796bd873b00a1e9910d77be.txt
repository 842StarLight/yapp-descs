<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Promote sales by actively attracting new patients to the office through heavy outbound dialing</li>
<li>
Confer with incoming patients to generate referrals for patient hearing health outreach</li>
<li>
Act as first point of contact for incoming patients; troubleshoot minor hearing aid issues</li>
<li>
Data entry and appointment scheduling using company scheduling protocols and internal scheduling database software</li>
<li>
Execute routine bank deposits and credit transactions; manage petty cash account</li>
<li>
Maintain weekly sales reports</li>
<li>
Package and ship supplies and equipment</li>
<li>
Determine specific patient insurance coverage by working directly with insurance companies</li>
<li>
Assist patients with qualifications for credit application as needed</li>
<li>
Order and maintain appropriate and approved office supplies</li>
