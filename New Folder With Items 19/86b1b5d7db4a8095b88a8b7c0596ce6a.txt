<h2>Sales Account Executive, Local Market Sales & Brand Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p>
Title: Sales Account Executive, Local Market Sales &amp; Brand Partnerships</p>
<p>
Location: New York, NY</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are we?</b></p>
<p>
Since its founding in 2008, Roc Nation has grown into the world’s preeminent entertainment company. We work in every aspect of modern entertainment—with recording artists and producers, songwriters and athletes. Our client list includes some of the world’s most recognizable names: from Rihanna, Shakira, and Big Sean, to Yoenis Cespedes, Todd Gurley, Leonard Fournette, and Robinson Cano. We are a full-service organization, supporting our diverse roster of talent via artist management, music publishing, touring, production, strategic brand development and beyond. We’ve forged strong partnerships with the world’s leading experts in artist management, technology, fashion, and philanthropy, and are redefining the business of entertainment.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Motivated, driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Roc Nation, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
Roc Nation, a Live Nation joint venture, is seeking an Account Executive, Local Market Sales &amp; Brand Partnerships to drive new advertiser acquisition in key local markets for the company’s roster of music and sports-based talent, event and social media offerings.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Summary:</b><br>
Responsible for developing, leading and closing sales efforts of local/regional advertisers in target U.S. markets. Specific focus on key markets that align with Roc Nation events and talent, including, but not limited to: New York, Los Angeles, Philadelphia, Miami, Washington D.C., Baltimore and others. Intensely seeks opportunities to grow business by executing the sales strategies dictated by VP, Branding Partnerships developing key relationships, actively pursuing new product business, and providing excellent account management.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive revenue in select local markets for Roc Nation talent and events</li>
<li>
Meet budgeted revenue goals on a monthly, quarterly, and annual basis</li>
<li>
Negotiate deals with local advertisers</li>
<li>
Build and maintain an accurate forecast for the quarter</li>
<li>
Maintain a file of potential target accounts in every target market, especially non-traditional sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Partner with support teams (i.e., Creative, Research, Activation) to create and deliver RFPs</li>
<li>
Ensure an exceptional experience for both advertisers and talent</li>
<li>
Develop high retention rate for advertisers</li>
<li>
Drive operational excellence</li>
<li>
Regular travel to key markets</li>
<li>
Must be able to work nights and weekends, variable schedule(s) as necessary.</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>
Minimum 5-10 years in either local media sales and/or local sports/event sponsorship sales</li>
<li>
Strong project and time management skills</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong business judgment</li>
<li>
Experience working with a high-performing sales organization</li>
<li>
Strong, detailed knowledge of enterprise and mid-sized business sales cycles</li>
<li>
Candidate must also be a self-motivated individual, quick on their toes, and show initiative to go the extra mile</li>
</ul>
