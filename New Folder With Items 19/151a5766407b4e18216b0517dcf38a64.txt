<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Job Summary
</div>
<div>
<div>NetApp is actively seeking an experienced Product Manager to drive NetApp HCI ecosystem and hybrid-cloud integrations. This role will define key hypervisor/container integrations, user configuration and management tools, and 3rd party solution integrations.</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<div><div>
<br>
<div><b>Key Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Establish the ecosystem vision and roadmap for the NetApp HCI product line.</li>
<li>Drive VMware virtualization, RedHat OpenStack/OpenShift, and hybrid-cloud integrations as well as requirements for our infrastructure configuration tools and user management workflows.</li>
<li>Lead the market and customer research efforts for these offerings.</li>
<li>Document the customer's user stories and partner with agile teams to drive features to completion.
</li>
<li>Work closely with NetApp’s service delivery teams to implement the offerings based on customer demand.</li>
<li>Create a spectrum of enablement collateral (presentations, white papers, demos, and training materials).</li>
<li>Deliver sales, engineering, and support trainings to NetApp customers, partners, and employees worldwide.
</li>
</ul>
</div></div>

</div>
<div><div><div>
<div><div>
<div>An ideal candidate should possess some combination of the following skills and experiences;</div>
<ul>
<li>Forward thinking… Deep understanding of the enterprise hyperconverged and hybrid-cloud market and the role open ecosystems play in delivering infrastructure as a service.</li>
<li>Hands-on... This is an individual contributor role and requires rolling up the sleeves to get it done.
</li>
<li>Geeky... NetApp operates in a very technical market and expects you to go deep with the technology. You’ll need to be able to hold your own in internal and external meetings.</li>
<li>Experienced… 5+ years in product management / technical marketing with a focus on the hyperconverged ecosystems.
</li>
<li>Educated... Bachelor's degree in Computer Sciences, Engineering, or equivalent technical experience. MBA desired.</li>
<li>Mobile... Ability to travel up to 25%.</li>
</ul>
