<h2>Educational Consultant – School Market (Florida)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Are you ready to put your energy and experience to work, make an immediate impact, and be a part of an exciting future? GoodheartWillcox Publisher, Experts in Career and Technical Education (CTE), is seeking a careeroriented, energetic, and selfmotivated sales professional based in the surrounding areas of a major metropolitan area of Florida. This position covers a territory that includes the state of Florida. As GoodheartWillcox expands our CTE product offerings and grows its customer base, this position is vital to drive future success and support the company’s continued growth. As a customer relationship expert, the individual in this position will be charged with increasing the Company’s overall sales and profits by the implementation and management of our sales and marketing objectives. Are you up to the challenge?</p>
<p><b>What you'll do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maximize the sales of GW products to existing and new accounts in an assigned territory to grow business.</li>
<li>Contact, sell and service teachers and administrators at middle and secondary schools.</li>
<li>Achieve sales goals for territory by effectively communicating with customers, planning sales coverage, managing resources, selling and servicing accounts via phone, email and social media.</li>
<li>Build strong relationships with customers by continually providing GW product information, news, service and support.</li>
<li>Research school programs and faculty to maintain current customer database information.</li>
<li>Use SAGE CRM software to add new contacts, enter customer sales calls, sample requests, and other account activities. Regularly run customer activity reports and customer sample followup reports. Enter and track all sales opportunities.</li>
<li>Work with the National Sales Director to insure alignment with overall sales strategy.</li>
<li>Report customer and product feedback, and market information within the territory.</li>
<li>Provide initial technical support for GW digital products and services.</li>
<li>Work with Editorial, Digital Media, Production, Marketing, and Sales departments to leverage products and develop new sales tools.</li>
<li>Travel via automobile and/or airplane to assigned territory to represent GW at related trade shows, conferences and/or customer requested meetings.</li>
<li>Attend annual GoodheartWillcox sales meetings either held in our Tinley Park office or outside location.</li>
<li>Provide and make webinar presentations to customers as needed.</li>
<li>Prepare and submit work related expenses according to GW policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you'll bring:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor degree in sales, marketing, or business.</li>
<li>Five years of sales or marketing experience. Experience in educational sales or publishing a plus.</li>
<li>Superior writing and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills and the ability to work successfully with a variety of people.</li>
<li>Must be wellorganized, selfmotivated, and highly energetic.</li>
<li>Must fulfill legal requirements of their state of residence to rent and drive a car.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Outlook, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Experience and knowledge of Customer Relationship Management software.</li>
<li>Ability to use web based screen sharing tools i.e. GoToMeeting or GoToWebinar.</li>
<li>Ability and desire to travel. Educational Consultants should expect the following:<ul>
<li>Travel via automobile and/or airplane within assigned territory to represent GW at related trade shows, conferences and to work prospective accounts and sales opportunities at least four working days of the week.</li>
<li>To be as active as possible throughout the territory with a minimum expectation of 25% in overnight travel. This can change dramatically however, depending on the territory may extend to 50% during peak times and state adoptions.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What it's like to work here:</b></p>
<p>At GoodheartWillcox Publisher, you will be working in a professional environment, supported by a passionate and energetic team that is invested in your growth and success. This is your chance to join an established employeeowned company that has been publishing for the Career and Technical Education field since 1921. Driven by the mission "We Build Careers," you will help students find and keep great careers, all while growing your own.</p>
<p>GoodheartWillcox has spent nearly a century building a culture that we are proud of. Our team members embody these core values:</p>
<ul>
<li>Roll up your sleeves</li>
<li>Do the right thing</li>
<li>Cando attitude</li>
<li>Always improving</li>
</ul>
<p>We are an employee owned company that offers a comprehensive compensation and benefits package that includes medical, dental, vision, life insurance, 401K match &amp; ESOP.</p>
<p>For consideration, please apply by sending your resume and cover letter to hr@gw.com.</p>
