<h2>Regional Fulfillment Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role Summary:</b></h2>
The Regional Fulfillment Leader has responsibility for supporting region wide modality specific backing management execution, driving equipment delivery and sales transfers. In addition the modality leader will have responsibility for managing continuous improvement projects in order management and business processes.<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<div><ul>
<li>
Reporting to the Fulfillment Manager, supporting Modality Order Backlog Management and Quarter Close Execution for the region, meeting Rev Rec expectations working with business stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Ensure Product Delivery Quality to Our Customers.</li>
<li>
Ensure order entry compliance, on time order quality and reliability.</li>
<li>
Manage interface with Supply Chain, Modality and Replenishment Planners, handle forward<br>
management on supply.</li>
<li>
Collaborate across the Customer Service management team to drive success and customer<br>
satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Support uptake in utilization of self-serve tools such as W2W within Customer Service.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with internal stakeholders to ensure customer needs are met and handled effectively,<br>
to ensure optimum response times for customers</li>
<li>
Support upstream awareness with Product Management</li>
<li>
Monitor performance of Customer Service teams within region for the Modality according to<br>
defined performance metrics, drive agreed Service Levels</li>
<li>
Projects &amp; improvement activities, Acquisition support as applicable</li>
<li>
Support cross-functionally on case management resolution for customers</li>
</ul></div>
