<h2>Business / Category Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Overview
</div>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Category Analyst is an integral member of the Marketing and Merchandising Category team and is responsible for conducting strategic advanced data analytics and visualization along with the creation/maintenance of business reporting. This position will support a variety of projects within the Category Management team.</p>
</div></div></div>
<div>Qualifications
</div>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<p>BA/BS in Business, Statistics, Mathematics, Accounting, or related discipline</p>
<p>
2 + years of data analyst experience, including specific experience working with large scale data and customer segmentation analysis</p>
<p>
Strong analytic skills with a high attention to detail</p>
<p>
Strong skills in MS Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)</p>
<p>
Knowledgeable in Data Visualization products such as Tableau, MicroStrategy, or Spotfire</p>
<p>
Solution based analytics and report development</p>
<p>
Methods for effectively planning project scope, creating processes and timelines accordingly</p>
<p>
Solid communication and presentation skills</p>
<p>
Demonstrated ability to obtain and interpret data</p>
<p>
Effective presentation of findings, insights and strategic recommendations</p>
</div>
</div></div></div>
<div>Responsibilities<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div><div>
<p>Using all available resources to analyze and interpret consumer and sales insights to identify consumer trends and identify potential marketing opportunities for our customers and stores</p>
<p>
Research and analyze data and other category-related information to identify trends, category performance, competitive threats, opportunities, etc. to support business decisions</p>
<p>
Use data sources to develop regular and ad hoc reports to include out of stock, opportunity gap, SKU rationalization, plan-o-gram analysis, promotion analysis, item tracking, pricing studies, financial performance, sales and movement.
</p>
<p>Provide internal business management with accurate and pertinent information that enhances and guides effective decision making</p>
<p>
Consult with internal business teams to provide analysis and insights designed to support key business decisions</p>
<p>
Project management which will include developing project plans, processes, and timeline</p>
<p>
Leverage analytical insights to develop actionable items for improvemen</p>
<p>
Conduct field analysis to make recommendations for improvement</p>
<p>
Design, manage, and run customized reports for category managers, directors, and executive</p>
<p>
Liaison with IT to ensure necessary data requirements and increase knowledgebase of how data flows , where it is stored, and how to access it in our enterprise system</p>
<p>
Determine market needs and/or gaps and assist in creating a strategy to capture new growth in the assigned areas</p>
<div>
Perform other duties as required and/or assigned</div>
<p>
Employee must be able to perform essential functions of the job with or without reasonable accommodation</p>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>
Attendance/Schedule</div>
<div>
<div>
Able to work a flexible schedule depending on the needs of the business; the typical workweek will be Monday through Friday, starting as early as 8 a.m. During new store openings, those hours may be extended beyond the normal work day and may, on limited occasions require weekend work</div>
<div>
Some travel required, including overnights</div>
