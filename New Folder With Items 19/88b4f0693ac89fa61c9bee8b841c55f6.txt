<h2>Industrial Technical Support & Inside Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Industrial Technical Support &amp; Inside Sales Job Description: </b></p>
<p>Work with distributors and customers by identifying their needs; in-depth understanding of products, equipment, and services.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2-4 years of relevant work experience handling industrial products</li>
<li>Desired candidate has an aptitude or background in machinery engineering or lubrication.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Technical Support &amp; Inside Sales Skills and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Diligently services existing accounts to maintain and grow the accounts.</li>
<li>Focuses sales efforts by studying existing and potential volume of dealers.</li>
<li>Provide quotes by referring to price lists and product literature.</li>
<li>Source lubricants, fittings, and other material as necessary</li>
<li>Conducting product inspections and repairs on returns and prepare analysis reports</li>
<li>Resolves customer complaints by investigating problems; developing solutions; preparing reports; making recommendations.</li>
<li>Maintains professional and technical knowledge of the product line and market trends.</li>
<li>Travels to provide on-site training and product recommendations.</li>
<li>Enjoys working with people in a variety of environments.</li>
<li>Contributes to the team by accomplishing related results as needed.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills to quickly identify customer needs.</li>
<li>Knowledge in industrial machinery and/or industrial reliability is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation: </b></p>
<ul><li>Salary Range: $45,000-52,000 Annual (Base salary plus Commission)</li></ul>
<p><b>Benefits: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>401K with automatic 3% company contribution, based on eligibility</li>
<li>Paid time off (sick, vacation, holidays)</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $45,000.00 to $52,000.00 /year</p>
