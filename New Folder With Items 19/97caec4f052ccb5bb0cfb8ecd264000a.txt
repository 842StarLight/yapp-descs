<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate and grow new business by successfully executing on a sales plan</li>
<li>Research potential customers and prospect in assigned sales territory</li>
<li>Present Aramark’s whole-product solutions</li>
<li>Build strong customer relationships and close sales</li>
<li>Conduct competitive market analysis and other sales-related research</li>
<li>Earn competitive base salary plus uncapped commissions( on average over $40,000.00 in commissions) on top of salary</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Minimum of two years business-to-business outside sales experience focused on new account generation, preferably selling a service</li>
<li>Demonstrated success in developing new business and generating sales leads within an assigned sales territory</li>
<li>Strong presentation and communication skills with a consultative selling approach</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office and contact-management software such as Salesforce, ACT, etc.</li>
<li>Willing to travel, including some overnight travel</li>
<li>A valid driver’s license</li>
<li>Successful completion of Criminal Background, Motor Vehicle Record, and Drug Screen checks</li>
<li>Cell phone sales experience is a HUGE plus</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $50,000.00 to $52,000.00 /year</p>
</div>
