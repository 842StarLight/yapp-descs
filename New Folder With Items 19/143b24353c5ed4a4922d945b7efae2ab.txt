<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>One of our clients is one of the fastest-growing software companies in Chicago West Loop is looking to hire Sales Development Representatives.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The base salary is up to $65,000 plus $15,000-$25,000 in commissions, which are uncapped.<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>We are looking for somebody with 1-5 years of sales experience. The company is looking for somebody who wants to work at a fast-paced technology organization, is passionate about sales, and is looking for the opportunity of advancement.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Description:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The BDR focuses on small to medium-size businesses and works with the Account Executives to close deals. Your job will be to set appointments and you'll be measured on the number of qualified appointments you set. You can expect to make 40 cold calls a day as well as send cold emails.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect and make cold calls as well as send cold emails</li>
<li>Schedule demos for salespeople</li>
<li>Strategize with manager</li>
<li>Able to conduct research on various industries and prospects</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><br>
</p>
<p><b>Experience/Skills Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-5 years of sales experience</li>
<li>Must have a passion to be in sales long-term</li>
<li>Demonstrate persistence and accountability</li>
<li>Comfortable in a competitive culture</li>
<li>Able to overcome objections</li>
<br>
</ul>

</p>
