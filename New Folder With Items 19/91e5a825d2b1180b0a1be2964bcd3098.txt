A global leader in advanced physical security solutions, LenelS2 delivers innovative security solutions, more choices to fit our customers’ needs and an experienced, responsive support team. We have an exciting opportunity to join our high performing team in a senior leadership role as our head of Product Management. This is an exceptional opportunity to join a corporate culture that is known for its innovative and growth-oriented vision.

<p></p>
<p>As LenelS2’s <b>Head of Product Management</b>, you lead a product development group with multiple product managers. You are a visionary and strategic; developing your team, forming high-impact product strategies, and seeing them through to fruition gets you going every day. You can explain a vision that inspires everyone from the engineers and designers in our team to the value added resellers (VARs) and CTOs of the Fortune 500 companies we partner with. You lead from the front by coaching, you sustain relationships that last and succeed, and build teams that come to work inspired.
</p>
