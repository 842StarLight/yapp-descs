<h2>RV Membership Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>We are looking for someone to sell RV vacations!</b></p>
<p>
Thousand Trails is looking for candidates who are passionate about camping and the RV life-style!</p>
<p>
ELS is looking for an enthusiastic and collaborative <b>Membership Specialist</b> to join our <b>Thousand Trails </b>team. The Membership Specialist will be selling RV vacations to current members and RVers traveling the country. The Membership Specialist is responsible for meeting membership sales targets and acts as a "Goodwill Ambassador" to potential customers to communicate the benefits of membership. This role provides education and builds awareness of the various membership products. This is a good opportunity for RVers and non-RVers with sales experience. More information about our resort can be found here.</p>
<p><b>
Job Duties (training will be provided):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
To implement membership awareness at the campground through welcome calls/emails, camp-in's, educational workshops, events, marketing materials, and other promotional methods.</li>
<li>
Identify prospects for membership and move through sales cycle by following steps of sales process.</li>
<li>
The Membership Specialist understands and can provide expert information on the new membership products.</li>
<li>
Utilize SalesForce reservation views to identify incoming guests for potential memberships.</li>
<li>
Contact incoming guests to welcome to park and introduce self as membership specialist.</li>
<li>
Work with park staff to handout membership materials at check-in.</li>
<li>
Attend park events and Manager meetings.</li>
<li>
Set appointments for membership presentation.</li>
<li>
Give effective sales presentations to interested guests.</li>
<li>
Curate Hot List and Prospect list in SalesForce for follow up.</li>
<li>
Obtain Member Referrals and maintain 30% referral business.</li>
<li>
Participate in regional RV Shows to generate additional incremental business.</li>
<li>
The Membership Specialist is responsible for corporate reporting to his/her Regional Sales Manager.</li>
<li>
Participates in incentive programs.</li>
<li>
Promotes Ambassador Program at park and engages with staff to encourage, reward, and recognize staff referral leads.</li>
<li>
The Membership Specialist is responsible for writing and managing membership contracts for new members.</li>
<li>
Understands and is knowledgeable of current membership promotions.</li>
<li>
Work week is Thursday through Monday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Basic computer skills and proficient in Microsoft Word programs, Office 365, Email, and cloud-based software sales programs.</li>
<li>
Professional behavior and appearance.</li>
<li>
Excellent communicator on phone, via email, and in-person.</li>
<li>
Customer oriented hospitality skills.</li>
<li>
Highly organized and detail oriented.</li>
<li>
Excellent prospecting and business development skills.</li>
<li>
Able to present benefits of products and close the sale.</li>
<li>
Thrives in results-oriented sales environment.</li>
<li>
Team oriented attitude.</li>
<li>
Self motivator and able to manage several different tasks at a time.</li>
<li>
Training to commence upon hire.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What do you get from us?</b></p>
<p>
In return for your excellent skills and abilities, we provide a comprehensive benefits package including medical, dental and vision plans; a generous 401k employer match; and paid vacations, holidays, and sick time.</p>
