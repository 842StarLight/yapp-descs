<div><b>
Job Summary
</b></div>
<div>Passageways is seeking a General Manager for our OnSemble business unit. This position will lead an incredible team of ~30 employees who are working to ensure that OnSemble is the best employee intranet solution available. We're looking to scale the product, increase the value it brings to organizations, and re-imagine what it's capable of. We're seeking a visionary leader who can also get into details. This position will be reporting to the CEO.</div>
<div>
Passageways has two offices in Indiana, one in Indianapolis and another in Lafayette. The OnSemble team has members in both locations so we'd prefer a leader who is willing to spend time at both offices as needed.</div>
