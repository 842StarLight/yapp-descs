<p><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<p>A-MAX Auto Insurance is looking for experienced and talented individuals with an entrepreneurial spirit to join our fast-paced Call Center Team, as a Customer Service Representative / Insurance Sales Agent. As a CSR / Sales Agent, you will work with a team that shares your desire to win while helping thousands of Texans find. The position pays a competitive wage with a generous commission structure that works well for those who are disciplined and follow our proven sales process.</p>

<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong listening, oral and written communications/interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Selling insurance policies to businesses and individuals in an inside sales environment -Ensure excellent service by explaining features, advantages, and disadvantages of various policies to promote the sale of insurance plans</li>
<li>Contact underwriter and submit forms to bind coverage</li>
<li>Perform administrative tasks, such as completing paperwork, filing, maintaining records and handling policy renewals daily</li>
<li>Goal-oriented: highly motivated insurance sales representative that is resourceful to achieve results with a sense of urgency</li>
<li>Problem-solving ability: apply and balance conceptual and analytical thinking by breaking down complex problems, evaluating alternative sources of action and their likely outcomes, and selecting the best alternative</li>
<li>Pay close attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Create and maintain business relationships with prospects and policyholders</li>
<li>Proven track record of trustworthiness, dependability and ethical behavior</li>
<li>Perform effectively in environments with frequent workload changes and competing demands</li>
<li>Overcome objections and close the sale</li>
<li>This job description is intended as a guide to reflect the principal functions of the job. However, it is not an all-inclusive listing of the required job functions and functions may vary. Further, the job description is subject to change at the discretion of the Managing Partner.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED</li>
<li>1+ year of Customer Service/Insurance Sales Experience preferred</li>
<li>Hold a valid and current County Mutual, Limited Lines or Property &amp; Casualty License in Texas (highly preferred, but not required)</li>
<li>Bilingual (Spanish/English) preferred, but not required</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of the Insurance Industry is a plus</li>
<li>Good organizational and interpersonal skills with a strong customer focus.</li>
<li>Basic Computer knowledge and possess strong written, verbal and people skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated intermediate to advanced written communication skills (spelling, grammar, and punctuation) and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Able to work independently with minimal / no supervision.</li>
<li>Proven track record of trustworthiness, dependability and ethical behavior</li>
<li>Ability to quickly learn any Company/Proprietary software is necessary.</li>
<li>The ability to work within safety guidelines as it pertains to AMAX standards of operations.</li>
<li>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is frequently required to stand; walk; use hands to finger, handle or feel; and reach with hands and arms.</li>
<li>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job.</li>
</ul>
