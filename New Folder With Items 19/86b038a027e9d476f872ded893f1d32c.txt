<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About NCR
</b></p>
<p>NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a leading software- and services-led enterprise provider in the financial, retail, hospitality, telecom and technology industries. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga., with 34,000 employees and does business in 180 countries. NCR is a trademark of NCR Corporation in the United States and other countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY &amp; KEY AREAS OF RESPONSIBILITY:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Position requires understanding of the Global Financial Self-Service Market (specifically ATM’s - industry, customer and competition) and NCR in-order to develop and manage opportunities to grow revenue and improve margins
</li>
<li>Be a solution evangelist for NCR and cash recycling technology in North America and other global markets
</li>
<li>Responsible for working with Pre-Sales particularly in North America to ensure smooth certification and market entry of new recycling solutions and products
</li>
<li>Required to drive multiple programmes to enable and ultimately create demand / results for the Financial business in targeted segments and geographies; Manage P&amp;L’s focused on profitable growth; Responsible for moving products through NCR’s Product Lifecycle Management process
</li>
<li>Responsible for Annual Business and Operational Plans including evolution of the strategy, market/competitive factors, revenue and investment projections, price/contract erosion, potential business development driven through Disruptive Innovation opportunities
</li>
<li>Required to work with Sales, Services Design &amp; Engineering, Service Delivery &amp; Operation leadership and across Lines of Business to solve complex business issues
</li>
<li>Responsible for specifying product requirements for current and future products by conducting market research through on-going communication with customers and industry analysts; Follows and analyzes market and competitive trends to ensure new product/functionalities meet customer needs
</li>
<li>Responsible for the alignment of revenue and profit within a product portfolio.
</li>
<li>Responsible for driving the transformation of product concept into a marketable solution with a cross functional team that includes members from engineering, marketing, services and finance
</li>
<li>Analyzing potential partner relationships for products in conjunction with the solution strategy and roadmap
</li>
<li>Accountable for all program metrics, including development schedule, cost, unit sales, revenue, margin, product quality, market share and customer satisfaction
</li>
<li>Gathers, prioritizes and documents product requirements and validates value proposition
</li>
<li>Supports and works directly with global sales organization for product training, sales strategy, customer presentations and RFP responses
</li>
<li>Provides direction, content and support for product marketing efforts including trade shows, marketing programs, webinars, and sales collateral
</li>
<li>Accountability for Cost Reduction for the portfolio
</li>
<li>Validates value propositions and develops business impact models
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</p>

</p>
