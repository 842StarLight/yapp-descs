<h2>Landscape Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Scott Byron &amp; Co.’s <b>Director of Client Relations </b>increase the Company’s sales opportunities by directing and supporting the Client Relations team members in a continuous effort to improve the quality and service we provide our Property Improvement clients.</p>
<p><b>Daily tasks: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proactive management, motivation, and training of Client Relations team.</li>
<li>Design, develop, and implement strategic site standards to address client requirements, in conjunction with the Production Manager.</li>
<li>Analyze and control expenditures of the department to conform to budgetary requirements.</li>
<li>Guide the Client Relations team in problem resolution; meet and interact with clients to assure client satisfaction and resolve problems, when needed.</li>
<li>Direct sales forecasting activities and set performance goals or each team member.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain periodic sales reports showing sales volume, potential sales, and areas of proposed client base expansion, in conjunction with the Production Manager.</li>
<li>Provide data and sales forecasts to production and buyer teams to ensure efficient scheduling and material procurement.</li>
<li>Provide various reports for executives on sales performance.</li>
<li>Develop, implement and maintain an incentive program that adheres to and promotes the Company’s operational and financial goals.</li>
<li>Manage, expand, and retain relationships with identified clients; both residential and commercial, with the goal of building profitable, long-term business relationships.</li>
<li>Create proposals and contracts and update client information utilizing Boss LM Operating Software.</li>
<li>Communicate routinely and consistently with all clients, both internal and external, via phone, text, email or other appropriate technology to maintain good communication, high standards, and positive relations.</li>
<li>Responsible to drive daily in a company vehicle with or without passengers.</li>
<li>Utilize appropriate technologies to facilitate information management and record keeping.</li>
<li>Communicate and resolve needs, issues, safety concerns and/or problems in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain good communication, high safety standards, and positive relations with coworkers, customers, and the public.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree required plus seven to ten years of related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>Account management experience is required, preferably in a green industry related position.</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in Microsoft Office and basic computer skills are required. Experience in Boss LM or other cloud-based management software is a plus.</li>
<li>Must demonstrate knowledge of the safety issues, regulations, and procedures utilized in public and private works activities.</li>
<li>Candidates must have a valid local driver’s license and good driving record.</li>
<li>A pre-employment drug test is required after offer of employment.</li>
<li>Will be subject to random drug screening. Must be 21 years or older.</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate in English, including: read, write, and with use of computers and phones, ability to speak in Spanish is a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers, coworkers, and the general public.</li>
</ul>
