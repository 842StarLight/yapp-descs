<h2>Sr. Product Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Senior Product Designer</b><br>
Canopy, Lehi UT</p>
<p>
Canopy is hiring an experienced Senior Product Designer to help us design the future of our software. Product Designers at Canopy are empowered to discover and design new disruptive products by thoughtfully crafting better experiences and interfaces while fostering a strong culture of design throughout the entire organization.</p>
<p>
We love Product Designers who are thoughtful, generous, practical and are great decision makers. Canopy's culture is highly collaborative and this role is for individuals who truly love to collaborate and mentor others daily across all cross-functional teams. We need strong team players who want to own a product's workflow, interactions, visuals, and have a passion for discovery and experimentation-turning real-life insights into beautifully designed solutions.</p>
<p><b>
What you'll be doing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Work directly with product and engineering teams to represent the design team, foster design discussions, make educated product and design decisions, and provide feedback in product planning and execution.</p></li>
<li><div>
Collaborate closely with design and product leadership to define your features and contribute to the overall direction of your product area.</div></li>
<li><div>
Design workflows, interactions, prototypes, sketches, and high-fidelity visuals for your team.</div></li>
<li><div>
Construct prototypes that accurately portray the designed interactions and experiences to further understanding and increase assurity.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work extremely close with engineering and quality assurance during build and release to ensure the highest quality implementation of the design.</div></li>
<li><div>
Solicit feedback from... well, everyone; including other product designers, graphic designers, engineers, product managers, sales and marketing to ensure a seamless overall user experience is created.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work in close collaboration with the design team to inform and improve design processes and take on a mentor role to elevate the skills of others on the design team.</div></li>
<li><div>
As we continue to grow, interview product design applicants and help build our inclusive design team and inform our hiring process.</div></li>
<li><p>
Help preserve Canopy's unique culture. We uphold strong values such as delivering an excellent product to our users, hiring passionate people for every position, and maintaining transparency across the organization.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you'll need to know / have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
5+ years of experience in an individual contributor role, on a product design team for desktop and/or mobile.</p></li>
<li><div>
A portfolio showcasing a foundation in great decision making, problem solving, typography, interaction and visual design, and ideation.</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience in working and collaborating with diverse teams in a dynamic, rapid growth environment.</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience working as part of a cross-functional product team with development, product management and quality assurance.</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience partnering directly with product managers to develop the strategy and rationale for features that are best suited for our users.</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience with prototyping as a means to explore, validate and convey design solutions.</div></li>
<li><div>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience fostering design culture and mentoring other designers as they grow.</div></li>
<li><p>
Experience mentoring and teaching others.</p></li>
</ul>

If this sounds like you, apply below. We'd love to hear from you!</p>
