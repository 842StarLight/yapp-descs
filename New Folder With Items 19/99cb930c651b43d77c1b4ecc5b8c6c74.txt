<h2>Regional HR Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>JOB SUMMARY:</b><br>
This is an HR generalist position that acts as a partner to Cimarex operating functions located in our Midland, Texas office. The primary purpose is to provide professional HR guidance and support to the Permian Exploration, Production and Drilling organizations located in Midland and the surrounding field offices, with the objective of adding value to the business.
<br><br>
The Midland-based Regional HR Manager will be responsible for working with managers and employees who are located in the Midland office or may be located in field offices associated with Permian Exploration, Production and Drilling activities. This is approximately 300+ people. Routine travel to Permian field locations will be necessary, with occasional travel to the other primary Cimarex offices in Tulsa or Denver also required.
<br><br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b> The following represents the majority of the duties performed by the position, but is not meant to be at all-inclusive nor prevent other duties from being assigned when necessary:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Contribute to the business strategy by helping Permian region leadership to identify, prioritize and build organizational capabilities, behaviors, structures and processes.</li>
<li>Support line managers in forecasting and planning their talent pipeline requirements in lines with the region’s business strategies.</li>
<li>Provide expert advice and coaching to managers and employees when appropriate.</li>
<li>Identify and analyze HR-related problems or issues, and then identify best practice solutions to them.</li>
<li>Act as liaison between the region and the Human Resources team to ensure HR services are aligned with the needs of the region’s workforce.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities where HR can add value to the business, and facilitate the means by which HR can exploit such opportunities.</li>
<li>Help ensure that a strong leadership and coaching culture permeates the region’s organization.</li>
<li>Help ensure that the Cimarex HR team is aware of opportunities to support business success in the Permian region.</li>
<li>Ensure coordination across HR team in a “brokerage” model of Center of Excellence support for: recruiting, compensation, benefits, data analytics, HRIS, etc.</li>
<li>Other tasks as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of experience in human resources in the oil and gas industry.</li>
<li>Substantial business acumen, including an understanding of the drivers of success in the exploration and production business.</li>
<li>Comprehensive understanding of the link between HR and business strategies.</li>
<li>Ability to identify ways for HR to support Cimarex business strategies.</li>
<li>Ability to engage, inspire and influence people.</li>
<li>Ability to articulate Cimarex values and help managers and employees understand how to apply these values in their day-to-day work.</li>
<li>Effective team player.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal, communication and client-support skills, including presentations to groups.</li>
<li>Understanding of how the various HR functions can provide support to regional managers and employees.</li>
<li>Proficient with major PC software such as MS Office.</li>
<li>Current knowledge of HR-related laws.</li>
</ul>
<b>WORKING CONDITIONS AND PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</b><br>
Work is typically performed inside an office facility. Exposure to conditions such as extreme heat or cold is not typical, but due to the nature of work is a strong possibility. In the event that employee is required to physically visit areas where actual operations are occurring, such as drilling locations or producing well-sites, personal protective equipment including, but not limited to head protection, hearing protection, safety glasses, safety shoes, and flame resistant clothing are required. Safety rules including OSHA and DOT requirements are strictly enforced. Employees must be willing to travel and work extended hours on short notice.
