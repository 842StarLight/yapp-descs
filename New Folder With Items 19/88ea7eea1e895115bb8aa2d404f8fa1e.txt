<h2>Marketing Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>1.</b><b> Position Summary:</b>
</h1>
<p>The <b>Market and Data Analyst</b> will work closely with the Lectra North America Marketing team to develop and execute market research and data management as well as support segmented and targeted lead generation and relationship development campaigns analyses. The Market and Data Analyst will be a key <b>Salesforce</b> and <b>Market</b> user, managing accurate data integration and supporting the marketing team’s data needs with these two key platforms. Responsibilities include creation and execution of reports, market research, analyze competitors, analyzes and accuracy of data, prospect and segment targeting, and development and implementation of recommendations to improve upon the data quality used for Lectra digital marketing activities.</p>
<p>The Market and Data Analyst will support the acquisition of new customers and increase the penetration of Lectra within the existing customer base resulting in greater revenue opportunities for the company.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>2.</b><b> DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
</h1>
<p><b>Market Analysis</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage, develop, and organize marquet knowledge and analysis (especially market potentials and specific regional needs) and monitor the evolution of these markets, both through the watch and the study of macroeconomic information, customers, prospects, potential partners and competitors.</li>
<li>Centralize and analyze all internal and external information to derive upcoming trends and additional business opportunities</li>
<li>Develop and propagate the knowledge of high potential ecosystems</li>
<li>Contribute to the development of the regional strategy as it pertains to marketing and the business</li>
<li>Build the regional marketing intelligence roadmap in accordance with corporate marketing intelligence team</li>
<li>Generate reports that analyze competition and customers</li>
<li>Manage the local research budget and the relationships with intelligence providers</li>
<li>Report regularly on progress and updates with regional management and corporate counterparts</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Data Analysis</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate and oversee all marketing customer list requests.</li>
<li>Support campaign planning by providing database export, analysis and recommendations</li>
<li>Execute and manage data related activities in campaign execution: data mapping, data import and Export, follow-up record update, etc.</li>
<li>Provide campaign level ROI tracking report</li>
<li>Help build customer profiling, segmentation analysis, data analysis, testing analysis and mining, external data enhancement, and lifetime value analysis.</li>
<li>Align with management to drive analytics around prospect and campaign effectiveness.</li>
<li>Plan and produce subsidiary weekly reports on marketing dashboard (overall and campaign specific)</li>
<li>Work closely with corporate operations team to retrieve business analysis data report for management (product mix sales, market outlook, installed base etc.) using our information systems</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
