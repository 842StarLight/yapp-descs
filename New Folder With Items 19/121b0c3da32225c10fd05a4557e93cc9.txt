<h2>Associate Executive Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Brightview Lake Tappan is located at 31 Hunt Road in Orangeburg, NY. This beautiful, newly opened community features Independent Living, Assisted Living, and Wellspring Village , a neighborhood dedicated to innovative Dementia Care.
</p>
<p>Having opened in April 2018, Brightview Lake Tappan joins many other Brightview communities in the surrounding area. Due to our company s growth, we have a brand new opportunity available! We are actively seeking an Associate Executive Director to join the team at Brightview Lake Tappan.
</p>
<p>The<b>Associate Executive Director</b> for Brightview Lake Tappan will embrace the vision, the mission and the vibrancy of the organization and will help lead a team in creating and maintaining a vibrant and engaging living environment for Brightview residents and a great place to work for its Associates.</p>
<p>In this role, you will work in an entrepreneurial environment and will be empowered to assist with leading your community with creativity and independence. You will help the Executive Director oversee day to day business operations to ensure exceptional quality and service, sustained resident satisfaction and associate engagement, operational efficiency, and strong financial results.</p>
<p>As the Associate Executive Director, you ll be involved in every facet of the operation Hospitality, Health Services, Dining, Programming, Sales, Maintenance, and the Business Office. You ll have the opportunity to truly make your mark while making a difference in the lives of both residents and associates.</p>
<p>We will leverage your existing leadership, management, and customer service skills, and in turn we will give you the experience and information you ll need to be successful in the future as an Executive Director for Brightview in this region.</p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>Supervisory responsibilities will be determined by community but generally consist of the direct oversight of three to five department heads, assisting the Executive Director with the direction of the remaining Department Heads and all other associates as indirect reports. You'll carry out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with Brightview's policies and procedures and applicable laws. Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring and training associates; planning, assigning and directing work; evaluating performance; rewarding and disciplining associates and addressing complaints and resolving problems.
</p>
<p><b>With our residents in mind, the right candidate will have:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor s degree in related field and/or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>One to three years of management experience in a similar environment.</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office products, internet and industry specific software programs for marketing, payroll and accounts payable/receivable.</li>
<li>Meet state licensing requirements (where applicable).</li>
<li>Excellent customer service, interpersonal, and communication skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to lead people and get results through others.</li>
<li>Ability to make independent decisions when circumstances warrant such action.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize, organize and manage multiple tasks.</li>
<li>Problem analysis and resolution at both strategic and functional levels.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate in English.</li>
</ul>

The communities include Independent Living, Assisted Living, and Alzheimer's Care options and we are currently experiencing very impressive and sustained growth.</p>
<p>Brightview is a privately held company based in Baltimore, Maryland, and we have been in business for more than 30 years. We develop, manage, and own residential real estate specializing in senior living communities in the Mid-Atlantic, NY Metro and New England regions.</p>
