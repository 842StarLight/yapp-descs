<h2>Senior Analyst, Web Analytics and Strategy, MRM//McCann</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div>
<p><b>Senior Analyst, Web Analytics and Strategy</b></p>
<p>
The ideal candidate has 4-6 years of experience in digital marketing with a strong emphasis on the Google Web Stack in support of campaign measurement, attribution, and optimization. The successful candidate will have solid footing in marketing strategy with prior experience in implementing marketing analytics best practices.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Core Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li>Expert level understanding of Web Analytics within the context of the multi-channel marketing mix—ideally with prior marketing agency or large enterprise experience in Google Analytics</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Strong background in tagging and tracking methodology to include administration of Google Tag Manager through to development of UTM parameters</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Strong background in data visualization beyond traditional Google Analytics reporting (e.g. Tableau, QlikView, Domo)</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Strength in developing data strategy deliverables involving the creation of scalable digital taxonomies spanning campaign, tactic, segment, and creative dimensions</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>The ideal candidate will be Google certified and will have worked in a consulting or implementation capacity in at least 1 prior role</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Depth in one or more of the following marketing domains is required:</li></ul>
<ul><li>Campaign effectiveness measurement</li></ul>
<ul><li>Statistical methods in marketing optimization</li></ul>
<ul><li>Site optimization (SEO is a plus)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Data profiling and exploration</li></ul>
<ul><li>Data taxonomy development</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead scoring model development</li></ul>
<ul><li>Cross platform data integration</li></ul>
<ul><li>Media optimization</li></ul>
<ul><li>Email program optimization</li></ul>
<ul><li>Landing experience optimization</li></ul>
<ul><li>Multi-touch attribution</li></ul>
<ul><li>Funnel optimization (Marketing and/or Sales)</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
-In addition to depth in the Google stack, exposure one or more of the following technology domains is a plus-</p>
<ul><li>Python (Analytics context)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Machine Learning Frameworks</li></ul>
<ul><li>A/B Testing Platforms</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adobe Analytics &amp; DTM</li></ul>
<ul><li>Marketing Automation Analytics (Marketo, Eloqua, or HubSpot)</li></ul>
<ul><li>CRM Analytics (Salesforce)</li></ul>
<ul><li>CDP Management &amp; Analytics (any major vendor)</li></ul>
<ul><li>DMP Management &amp; Analytics (any major vendor)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Multi-touch Attribution (C3, Visual IQ)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Tagging platforms such as Tealium</li></ul>
<ul><li>ETL &amp; Middleware experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Web API interaction</li></ul>
<ul><li>SQL experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Amazon AWS and/or Microsoft Azure</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong understanding of MS Excel</li></ul>
