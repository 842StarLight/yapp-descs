<h2>Shipping & Inventory Coordinator</h2>

Our careers include manufacturing, engineering, procurement, supply chain, finance, HR, product development, sales, marketing and an ever-expanding array of others to support our continuing rapid growth.</p>
<p>
Shipping and Inventory Coordinator</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
This position will be responsible for monitoring product leaving the warehouse. Duties will include, creating shipping documents, such as bill of ladings and packing slips. Monitors sales orders and plan efficiently that the most effective option is utilized to fulfill customer demands. Works closely with inventory and warehouse personnel to ensure orders are being fulfilled properly. Works with the SAP (ERP) system on a regular basis. Resolves customer and inventory issues that may arise. This is a Night position with hours of 4:30 pm - 4:30 am.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Functions &amp; Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Plan and coordinate transportation with warehouse personnel</li>
<li>
Visualize, facilitate, optimize the full order cycle from start to finish</li>
<li>
Track quality, quantity, stock, transport costs, delivery timelines, and productivity</li>
<li>
Address problems and complaints that may arise, quickly and satisfactorily</li>
<li>
Review data for errors and efficiencies and report information to the Distribution Manager</li>
<li>
Adheres to all plant Good Manufacturing Practices (GMPs) and good safety standard operating procedures. Follow all Food Contact Packaging Safety requirements as outlined in the SQF Quality Manual.
</li>
<li>Follows customer demands and special instructions to ensure orders are properly fulfilled.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Candidate Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5 years’ experience in a warehouse or supply chain role</li>
<li>
Proficient in standard WMS and SAP</li>
<li>
Strong data-entry skills and attention to detail</li>
<li>
Proficient with Microsoft Office software, especially MS Excel</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills and ability to work under pressure</li>
</ul>
