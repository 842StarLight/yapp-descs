<p><b>Why PerfectServe?</b></p>
<p>
 PerfectServe is building healthcare’s most advanced communication and collaboration solution to unify the entire care team across the continuum – from inpatient, outpatient, and patients at home. The secure platform uses advancements in mobility, AI and analytics to optimize scheduling, automate clinicians’ communication-driven workflows, eliminate non-clinical tasks and decrease clinician burnout, resulting in quicker time to treatment and superior patient outcomes.</p>
<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
This person is responsible for the support of Sales, particularly during the complex enterprise sales process, by providing technical product knowledge to potential clients. This person will provide technical knowledge about the PerfectServe platform and configuration by participating in presentations and product demonstrations. In addition, this position will support activities related to technical questions for RFI and RFP requests. The Technical Sales Support Manager will need to rely on his/her extensive experience and judgment to plan and accomplish stated goals.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Analyze client requirements and define and develop the appropriate product demonstrations that best highlight PerfectServe’s capabilities that meet these needs</p></li>
<li><p>
Co-present at sales meetings (phone, WebEx or live) providing technical expertise and application around the PerfectServe platform</p></li>
</ul>
<ul><li><p>
Answer technical questions supporting HIT, telecom, and desktop integration as it relates to PerfectServe solutions</p></li></ul>
<ul>
<li><p>
Clearly communicate to prospects our privacy and security controls</p></li>
<li><p>
Assist Sales and train new sales personnel as needed in platform configuration, technical product details, new product functionality, and privacy and security controls</p></li>
<li><p>
Technical liaison from prospective clients, communicating new market needs to product management and the R&amp;D team as appropriate</p></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>
Technical liaison for sales team with product management, R&amp;D, compliance, and implementation</p></li>
<li><p>
Help define and build demo instances for new members of the sales team. Ensure demo instances are defined similarly across the sales team and are consistent in scope and functionality.</p></li>
<li><p>
Participate in RFP/RFI responses as required by Sales and or CTO</p></li>
</ul>
