<h2>Sales Development Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Reporting to the Director of Sales, the Sales Development Rep is a key role within the CRS-Revention organization. This person will be responsible for finding, qualifying, documenting and turning over leads to the outside sales force.</p>
<p>This person will work closely with the Director of Sales and the marketing team in uncovering new business opportunities, creating awareness in key markets, setting appointments and presenting CRS-Revention solutions to the appropriate decision-makers within the prospect organizations.</p>
<p><i>Summary of Key Responsibilities: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Setup qualified client meetings per month (quota to be determined). A qualified meeting equals a meeting, via face to face interaction or online web demo, with the decision maker that has expressed interest in CRS-Revention solutions</li>
<li>Utilize data sources, Salesforce, industry publications to generate the appropriate leads in the assigned territories</li>
<li>Execute on campaigns designed by the marketing group</li>
<li>Meet with prospect stakeholders</li>
<li>Assist in making appointments for trade shows and other events</li>
<li>Qualifying prospects in accordance with sales process, determining project timeframe, key contacts and funding sources</li>
<li>Data entry (and maintenance) of prospect and contact data in CRM database</li>
<li>Targeted email campaigns</li>
<li>Account Blueprints and approach strategies</li>
<li>Providing feedback to management on effectiveness of phone and email campaigns</li>
<li>Support the Sales team to meet client’s needs when the Territory Manager is on the road.</li>
<li>Adhering to CRS-Revention selling processes, practices and rules of engagement</li>
</ul>

<li>Demonstrated experience with Microsoft Office (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>com experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience with database entry and management</li>
<li>Possess solid time management, priority setting and organizational skills</li>

</ul>
