<h2>Marketing Communications and Design Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>doxo is disrupting bill pay. Founded on the idea to create a simple, secure way to pay all your bills from a single account, we’re a rapidly growing startup with over 2.5 million users paying over 50,000 billers. We’re currently seeking a Marketing Communication and Design Specialist to assist our B2B and Consumer teams build out marketing collateral that will be used in print, online/web, email, video, and throughout doxo’s bill pay service. Part designer, part copywriter, this person will be fully responsible for producing engaging content that successfully communicates doxo’s simple, secure, all-in-one bill pay service.</p>
<p><b>
Major Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
The role is responsible for supporting the B2B and Consumer marketing efforts. This includes, but is not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Design and implement marketing campaigns to be used across multiple channels including email, print, direct mail, and social media. Create landing pages to educate and engage doxo billers and customers.</li>
<li>Craft compelling and well structured content for a variety of audiences.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with B2B and Consumer teams to promote key initiatives and product features, while demonstrating a clear understanding of campaign objectives and the abilities to execute them.</li>
<li>Possess a solid understanding of doxo’s biller and consumer audiences, relating this back into the design and copy to enhance engagement.</li>
<li>Write innovative and creative copy for acquisition and retention materials.</li>
<li>Create, develop, and manage ongoing newsletter and email campaigns to users through automated email systems.</li>
<li>Engage in the design and upkeep of the website.</li>
<li>Evaluate conversion success of landing pages and email communications and optimize accordingly.</li>
<li>Experience with video a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years experience creating marketing content and a demonstrable portfolio of works.</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with image editing and graphic design software like Photoshop, In-Design and/or Adobe Illustrator.</li>
<li>Experience with WordPress.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of HTML/CSS.</li>
<li>Strong aesthetic skills with the ability to combine various colors, fonts and layouts.</li>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communication skills with a strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>Proven ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously, accomplish objectives and work proactively.</li>
<li>Excellent problem solving, organizational, and time management skills.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience.</li>
</ul>
