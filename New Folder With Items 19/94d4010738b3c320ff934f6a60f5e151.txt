<h2>Digital Group Planning Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p>The 9thWonder Los Angeles team is looking for a Digital Group Planning Director to join our fast-growing team of diverse talents. You are a planner and strategist, guiding the strategy, identifying key measurements and implementing complex paid digital media solutions as a well as other emerging media channels for client initiatives. You are a team player but can take the ball and score on your own.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>As a Digital Group Planning Director, you:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide oversight of client accounts and lead a team of digital media planners.
</li>
<li>Prepare and present digital media plans, including objectives, strategy, rationale, and all backup materials.
</li>
<li>Lead the execution of digital media plans, including preparation of media budgets, schedules of insertions, and supporting materials.
</li>
<li>Ensure media authorizations are developed and approved per plans/project request.
</li>
<li>Prepare buy releases upon client authorizations as well as budget recaps for clients.
</li>
<li>Confirm final schedules adhere to established guidelines and objectives.
</li>
<li>Assist the Media Finance group in budget management and expenditures.
</li>
<li>Grow revenue within the current client base by identifying new and cross-product opportunities.
</li>
<li>Develop and optimize budgets to identify strengths and weaknesses and ensures proper financial allocation.
</li>
<li>Meet with media vendors to evaluate new information/services and to maintain good rapport with sales representatives; keep a high professional profile
</li>
<li>Participate in periodic meetings between account supervisors and clients.
</li>
<li>Provide proof of advertising performance reflected in accountability reports submitted by various media for client’s campaigns
</li>
<li>Are helping clients to make progress toward the achievement of their objectives and recognize the media’s contribution to overall plans.
</li>
<li>Have strong knowledge in Google Ads, Facebook, Google marketing platform, ad operations, and tagging
</li>
<li>Have experience working with analytic platforms, such as Google Analytics and Adobe Analytics
</li>
<li>Experience working with integrated digital campaigns (i.e., working with paid search, Facebook, and programmatic).
</li>
<li>Can derive business insights from media data and analytics.
</li>
<li>Must be an excel wizard!
</li>
<li>Experience working with Google Tag Manager or Tag Management Software is a plus.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Digital Group Planning Director has:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of media planning experience in an advertising agency.
</li>
<li>BA/BS Degree or equivalent experience.
</li>
<li>Strong leadership and critical thinking abilities.
</li>
<li>Deep understanding of syndicated research and ability to mine data/information for insights and strategies
</li>
<li>Curious, inquisitive mind.
</li>
<li>Strong knowledge and experience with Google Ads, Analytics, Mediaocean, Prisma, and Excel.
</li>
<li>Organized and an excellent teammate.
</li>
<li>Experience with local and national multi-market media planning.
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively lead and manage a digital media team.<br>
</li>
</ul>
