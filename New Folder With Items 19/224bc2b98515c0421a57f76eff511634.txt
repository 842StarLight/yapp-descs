<h2>Financial Analyst - Career Rotation Program</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<div>The Analyst &amp; Finance Rotation Program (AFCRP) is an entry-level program designed to provide recent college graduates a cross-functional, full encompassing financial over view. The Analyst (AFCRP) will participate in 3-4 distinct department rotations including Cylinder Control, FP&amp;A, pricing &amp; profitability analysis as well as other operations that are designed to build well rounded key technical financial skills.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL AREAS FOR DEVELOPMENT:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Strengthen financial skills in: Accounting, Operations, and Financial Planning and Analysis.</li>
<li>
Learn techniques in financial reporting, audit and compliance, as well as transactional operations finance.</li>
<li>
Follow specialized plan in order to build communication, provide leadership, presentation techniques, writing and other professional skills.</li>
</ul></div>
<div><b>
FINANCIAL OVERVIEW OF DEVELOPMENT MAY INCLUDE EXPERIENCE IN:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Working with the VP of Finance and other key region managers in close mentorship in order to learn the necessary skills to be successful in a financial leadership role as well as prepare for career advancement.</li>
<li>
Learn to support regional leadership and corporate FP&amp;A on developing and tracking EBITDA improvement opportunities; especially as it relates to operating efficiencies</li>
<li>
Develop and present financial analysis relating to operating metrics such as, gross margin, operating expenses, working capital, asset utilization, capital expenditures, and other metrics as needed.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the Regional operation and finance support teams to better understand historical financial results, trends and validate forecast assumptions.</li>
<li>
Support the annual budgeting and quarterly forecasting processes; working closely with Regional and Division leadership.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with investment decision finance concepts such as Net Present Value (NPV) and Internal Rate of Return (IRR).</li>
<li>
Creates and or maintains extensive financial analysis modeling in a mock type scenario in order to support the Division as well as Corporate FP&amp;A requests.</li>
<li>
Support the Division’s efforts to improve utilization of data warehouse reporting tools and participate in Super User, SAP software, Business Warehouse (BW) and Business Process (BP) support groups.</li>
<li>
Learn techniques in order to build strong relationships with the Division and Regional management teams.</li>
<li>
Other projects/initiatives as assigned.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
