<div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The role of the Associate Engagement Planner is to assist in the day-to-day stewarding of paid advertising campaign activity on assigned accounts. The Associate Engagement Planner is required to assist in the implementation of paid advertising programs and maintain supporting documentation. He/she is required to learn and know the procedures and processes of the day-to-day running of account operations as it relates to buying and reporting. We are seeking individuals who are dependable, proactive, curious and willing to learn!</p>
<p><i>
As this is an entry level position, training will be provided for more specialized media planning software (Double Click, MBox, IMS, @plan, etc.). He/she must have strong communication and organizational skills and be able to multi-task. This is a great opportunity for someone who is eager to learn and develop their career in the field of media.</i></p>
<p><i>
Prior to interviewing, candidate will be asked to complete a paid advertising related homework assignment.</i></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist planner to help maintain and organize paid advertising plan support documents
<ul>
<li>numerous excel spreadsheets, manage upcoming deadlines, track proposal submissions across multiple partners, billing reconciliation spreadsheet, etc.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Oversee the execution of paid advertising campaigns as it relates to digital media ad buying.</li>
<li>Manage paid advertising spend within DCM (Double Click Manager) creating insertions orders and updating as needed.</li>
<li>Ensure media plans are running as scheduled through reporting on campaign results
<ul>
<li>Weekly Reviews and Monthly Client Reporting</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Maintain budget summaries for various accounts</li>
<li>Coordination of publisher meetings and presentations in person and via conference calls</li>
<li>Maintain relationships with sales vendors from various partners</li>
<li>Reconcile media billings</li>
<li>Assist in creation of paid advertising client presentations</li>
<li>Strives to be a student of media, marketing and technology – continuously confronting the unknown and staying abreast of new trends in the marketplace</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
