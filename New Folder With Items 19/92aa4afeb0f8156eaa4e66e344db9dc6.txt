<h2>Digital Marketing Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<div>Koddi is looking for an Account Lead to join its growing Enterprise metasearch client services team.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>As an Account Lead, you will be responsible for partnering with premier travel brands to create data-based, automated solutions to achieve their greater business objectives. You will leverage our industry-leading technology to manage large scale digital marketing campaigns and examine trends to provide valuable and actionable insights to our clients. The primary role of the Account Lead is to make our Marketing Analysts successful by clearing the field of obstacles and helping them focus on making an impact for clients. The Account Lead serves as our most frequent client contact, often refining or consolidating communication from other points on our team. The Account Lead also ensures that each account has a clear service plan and problem solves any challenges. The role also contributes to the company culture. Koddi is a fast-growing technology company and needs people who are passionate self-starters.</div>
</div>
