<h2>Educational Teacher Trainer (Per Diem)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Educational Per Diem Teacher Trainers train successful implementations of our Language, Literacy and Math programs at our partner schools. While working in tandem with their counterparts in sales and customer success, our Per Diem Teacher Trainers strategically guide product usage, building engagement, and customer satisfaction through on-site interactions and trainings. They effectively apply both an analytical and interpersonal approach to their customer relationships. They use their expertise to coach administrators, principals, and teacher leaders to ensure a successful partnership, ultimately transforming the lives of the students we serve.</p>
<p>
Educational Per Diem Teacher Trainers are product specialists and master presenters whose primary responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Conducting professional development and in-service training on curriculum products for new and current users</li>
<li>
Conducting highly effective presentations of our curriculums to educators in the Salt Lake City, UT area</li>
<li>
Demonstrating a thorough understanding of curriculum</li>
<li>
Providing expertise on Imagine Learning's curriculum and reports integration</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
Required:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience working with adult learners, training teachers</li>
<li>
Strong computer skills in Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Excel and online/web-based tools</li>
<li>
Demonstrate a passion for K-12 public education</li>
<li>
Excellent written and oral communication skills, customer service and relationship building at all levels within the school system</li>
<li>
Ability to adjust on the fly, be flexible, and able to adapt to the immediate or unforeseen challenges</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and self-driven</li>
<li>
Ability to establish priorities, maintain schedules, and manage time effectively</li>
<li>
Frequent travel</li>
<li>
Must be able to lift and move up to 60 pounds</li>
</ul>
<p>
Desired:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree; education degree preferred or work in education-related field</li>
<li>
Experience delivering professional development</li>
<li>
District or school administration experience</li>
<li>
Previous experience training with technology products</li>
<li>
Basic understanding of hardware configuration, general networking, computer and mobile operating systems</li>
</ul>
<p>
This is an excellent opportunity with a successful education software company. We offer a competitive hourly wage and flexible schedule.</p>
<p>
The territory for this position includes the Salt Lake City, UT area and will specialize in our Language/Literacy, Math, Espanol products.</p>
