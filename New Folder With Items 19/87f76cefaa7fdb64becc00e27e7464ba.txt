<h2>Professional Housing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>A Professional Housing Consultant’s Key Responsibilities are:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain professional business activity with customers throughout the purchase process</li>
<li>Achieve sales goals including home sales and profitability</li>
<li>Maintaining a high level of customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Identify customer’s needs and provide realistic assistance in selection of home</li>
<li>Obtain credit information at the request of the customer</li>
<li>Obtain customer’s down payment prior to ordering the home</li>
<li>Close contract with the customer</li>
<li>Complete and manage orders with customers</li>
<li>Utilize follow up systems to track activity and results</li>
<li>Follow-up on sales leads from various sources including advertising, telephone and website</li>
<li>Identify local marketing opportunities for new business</li>
<li>Proactive follow up with prospective customers</li>
</ul>
<br>
Applicant Qualifications<br>
<br>
Personal Characteristics<br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent organization and time management skills</li>
<li>Ability to effectively and tactfully communicate with people</li>
<li>Great listening skills</li>
<li>Creative problem solving ability</li>
<li>Ability to work as part of a team, as well as individually</li>
<li>A high level of enthusiasm</li>
<li>Integrity</li>
</ul>
<br>
Additional Requirements<br>
<br>
Availability: Must be able to work evenings, weekends and some holidays<br>
<br>
Education: High School Diploma required; Bachelor’s Degree preferred<br>
<br>
Prior work Experience:<br>
<ul>
<li>At least 2 years’ experience in sales or business</li>
<li>Management background a plus</li>
<li>Bilingual skills a plus</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
