<h2>Processor - Intermediate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Completes discounting and other contract funding / booking related activities. Ability to attain maximum production while complying with company policies and procedures. Maintains industry best in class dealer service and processing turn times.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Timely review and audit new contracts for compliance with pricing / policy guidelines and state specific requirements.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a high volume environment while achieving cycle time and accuracy requirements.</li>
<li>Maintain high levels of accuracy and attention to detail; identifying any fraud or potentially fraudulent information.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with dealers, sales, and underwriting teams to resolve questions or deficiencies regarding contract packages.</li>
<li>Support various business and/or department level projects.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>HS diploma or equivalent required. 1+ years' contract processing experience. Associate's degree or Bachelor's degree in business or related may be used as a proxy for the experience.</li>
<li>Performs high level technically based processing activities and/or supporting tasks</li>
<li>Requires intermediate knowledge of Microsoft Office programs</li>
<li>Intermediate written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Willingness to work flexible hours (including weekends)</li>
<li>Consistently meets established productivity and turnaround time goals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Ally Overview</b></p>
<p>
Ally Financial Inc. (NYSE: ALLY) is a leading digital financial services company and a top 25 U.S. financial holding company offering financial products for consumers, businesses, automotive dealers and corporate clients. Ally's legacy dates back to 1919, and the company was redesigned in 2009 with a distinctive brand, innovative approach and relentless focus on its customers. Ally has an award-winning online bank (member FDIC), one of the largest full service auto finance operations in the country, a complementary auto-focused insurance business, a growing digital wealth management and online brokerage platform, and a trusted corporate finance business offering capital for equity sponsors and middle-market companies.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Business Unit/Enterprise Function Description</b></p>
<p>
Ally Auto's U.S. auto finance operations provide a wide array of financial products and services to automotive dealerships and retail automotive customers in the United States. Together with Dealer Products and Services, the U.S. auto finance team has built strong dealer relationships by offering high-quality wholesale financing services, attractive volume incentives and a full array of highly competitive products like extended service contracts, dealership insurance products and more.</p>
