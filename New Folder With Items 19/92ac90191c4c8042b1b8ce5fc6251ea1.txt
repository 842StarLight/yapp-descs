<h2>Event Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Tipalti is the only solution on the market today that streamlines and automates the entire supplier payment process and workflow under one holistic cloud platform. Tipalti eliminates up to 80% of the supplier payment and regulatory compliance workload for finance departments. Companies like Roku, Twitter, GoDaddy, and Indeed trust Tipalti to eliminate risk and save by streamlining and automating their supplier payments operations.</p>
<p>Tipalti is a licensed Financial Technology company founded in 2010 and whose headquarters are in San Mateo, California. We are growing at 200% year over year, have over $8.0B in spend (and growing) running through our platform, and support over 300 customers and 4,000,000 paid suppliers all over the world. We are the leader in supplier payment operations.</p>
<p>We are looking for a high-performing, self-driven Event Marketing Manager. The ideal candidate aspires to deliver flawless event experiences, is adept at working across multiple departments, and is motivated by seeing their work contribute to pipeline growth. This role is critical to reaching big demand generation goals and requires the ability to deliver quality outcomes while working under tight deadlines. To meet your goals, you’ll work with an amazing team supporting you with great content, marketing automation, and creative.</p>
<p>If you are up for the challenge, we would love to talk with you.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Deliver integrated marketing events to drive pipeline growth including Tipalti produced events, tradeshows, executive events, hands-on workshops, regional roadshows and more</li>
<li>Oversee the timely execution of logistics, budgeting, scheduling, creative themes, speaker acquisition and social media strategies as related to events</li>
<li>C-level campaigns: work with DemandGen and other supporting teams to develop event strategy and objectives; spearhead initiatives to meet objectives based on lead generation, customer advocacy, and more</li>
<li>Understand our target industries and customize programs as needed</li>
<li>Communicate all marketing plans, activities, and feedback through formalized event briefings and follow up</li>
<li>Assess event options and recommend which events to participate in and nature of participation</li>
<li>Cross collaborate with other marketing and sales channels – sales, partner marketing, product marketing, online, email, social, press, customer references and others to integrate communications and promotion of events</li>
<li>Coordinate event staffing and recruiting from within the organization</li>
<li>Manage lead acquisition to ensure smooth lead hand-off, nurturing and follow-up post event</li>
<li>Track program results, measure program success, and report metrics via SFDC and presentations to stakeholders and sales teams</li>
<li>Comfortable participating in Tipalti events, including speaking with prospects</li>
<li>Responsible for ordering and tracking event inventory including collateral, giveaways, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 3-7 years Marketing, preferably Events or Field Marketing Experience</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years marketing in B2B software</li>
<li>Passionate about delivering memorable, effective event experiences</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Meet deadlines while delivering high quality work</li>
<li>Exceptional attention to detail</li>
<li>Familiar with Salesforce.com and Marketo for tracking event effectiveness</li>
<li>Ability to influence others in a cross-functional team</li>
<li>Manage to budget</li>
<li>Personable with strong business acumen</li>
<li>Travel to events approx 30%</li>
</ul>
