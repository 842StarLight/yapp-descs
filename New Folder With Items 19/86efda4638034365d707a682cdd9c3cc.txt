<h2>Director, Rating and Underwriting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Responsible for a multi-billion dollar book of business working with Underwriting, Actuarial, Finance, and Business Segment executive leadership to determine the financial and business targets of the company and ensuring the execution of work within assigned department to meets those targets. Plan, forecast, monitor and evaluate department/corporate goals and objectives relating to group rating and underwriting, staff training, workflows, and operational procedures.</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Manage multi-billion dollar portfolio of business spanning multiple segments minimizing adverse selection through case positioning and consultation with brokers, agents, vendors, consultants, sales and customers to meet profitability targets.
</li>
<li>Serve as key negotiator for rates and contracts on assigned accounts to ensure BCBSM/BCN profitability targets are met and coordinate activities with regard to product and pricing strategies and their financial impact to customers.
</li>
<li>Lead assigned area in utilization of a consultative approach to analyze customer claim and demographic data, review requests for customized rating arrangements and make recommendations on the final rates, administrative costs and any benefit plan design changes to meet the needs of current and prospective customers.
</li>
<li>Provide leadership and analysis on BCBSM and BCNâ€™s value proposition (how our products add value to the customer) to internal and external stakeholders and partner with the business segments to meet corporate underwriting financial goals and associated P&amp;L objectives through risk assessment to safeguard the financial assets of the company.
</li>
<li>Support BCBSM &amp; BCN senior leadership by providing efficient and effective solutions to complex business problems. Utilizing collaboration and influence to provide special analysis or adhoc reports to various internal committees, presentation of findings and recommendations that improve financial health and segment growth and continuously monitor the regulatory and competitive environment to ensure implementation of necessary changes.
</li>
<li>Develop short and long term goals for assigned business unit to realize corporate, divisional and departmental objectives. Implement programs and action plans to achieve those goals ensuring continuous improvement.
</li>
<li>Direct staff of assigned Underwriting department. This includes supervising, training and development. Maintain team member motivation. Responsible for staff selection, performance evaluation, coaching and discipline.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a presence with key customers and interdependent areas to explain rate increases, implement changes in rating methodologies, and improve general business processes.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.<br>
</li>
</ul>

<b>Qualifications</b><div></div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in related field required. Master's Degree preferred.</li>
<li>Eight (8) years of experience in rating and underwriting required.</li>
<li>Five (5) years of supervisory experience required.
</li>
<li>Significant experience with: Actuarial and group insurance principles, health care benefits and claims processing procedures, rating formulas, self-funded and alternative financial arrangements.</li>
<li>Excellent analytical, organizational, planning and problem solving skills with an ability to handle multiple tasks.
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills required including the ability to present to executive leadership and external customers.
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, consultative and negotiation skills to interface with major account, union, internal support areas, or external organizations involved in servicing the account and positioning cases using sound business information.</li>
<li>Ability to apply understanding of competitive landscape to rating decisions and recommendations and teach it to team members.</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively in a team environment.</li>
<li>Ability to recognize and handle customer sensitive situations.</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate cross functionally to achieve enterprise objectives.</li>
<li>Ability to demonstrate flexibility and adaptability to manage ambiguity in a dynamic and changing environment.</li>
<li>Ability to stay informed and educate staff on federal and state laws and regulations related to health care delivery systems (HMO, PPO, ACO, etc.) and sales distribution channels required.</li>
<li>Other related skills and/or abilities may be required to perform this job.</li>

</ul>
