<div><b>Position Summary:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The<b> User Experience Design Associate</b> will obsess on the client, this is a hands-on thought leadership role. Working with a team of talented UX designers and business architects, you will lead the development and delivery of end-to-end client experiences. This is a leadership role requiring a customer-centric view into our solutions, our communications and our interactions. You will bring the smarts of a product manager to understand prioritization and how to measure outcomes. You will engage directly with clients and stakeholders to maintain client empathy and functional goals across the firm. You will work in a fast-paced, rapidly evolving, Agile environment on one of the firm’s top strategic goals<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Responsibilities</b>:</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with a team comprised of Business Architects, Client Leads, Process Designers and business partners to design and drive the ideal end-to-end client experience</li>

<li>Support journey sprints and reviews in partnership with the Product Manager(s)</li>

<li>Support the setup customer research and user testing (e.g., customer and staff panels, personas, analytics)
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain relationships across senior business stakeholders</li>
<li>Prepare, participate and present to senior executives regularly</li>
</ul>
