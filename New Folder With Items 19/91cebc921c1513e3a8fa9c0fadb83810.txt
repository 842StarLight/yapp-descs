<br>The role
<br>--------
<br><br>
As the Head of Accounts in New York, you will be responsible for managing significant portion of our NY business, with a focus on setting strategic growth plans for each of our client businesses. You will work closely with our Managing Director and executive team to define and work toward a clear vision for evolving our Account Management craft at Blue State. And you will be responsible for leading the Accounts team, working to develop our future leaders, including coaching, training, and establishment of personal development goals and pathways.
<br><br>
<b>Day-to-day responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Establish, maintain, and expand senior level relationships within existing client organizations - shared with MD, and other senior leaders of the office.</li>
<li>Work with account leads to develop strategic growth plans for all NY client accounts; accountability of quarterly revenue goals for a portfolio of clients</li>
<li>Responsible for managing the NYC accounts team - inclusive of all AC, AM, AD, SAD and VPs.</li>
<li>Responsible for the recruitment, training, development and retention of account team employees.</li>
<li>Redefine and maintain a standard for account management.</li>
<li>Collaborate with other members of account leadership across offices to spread and absorb learnings, create efficiencies and standardize our processes.</li>
<li>Identify new opportunities and establish relationships with prospective clients, playing an active role in cultivating new business within client organizations and with new logo clients</li>
<li>Lead project team transitions where necessary</li>
<li>Ensure the summation of our work -- inclusive of creative, strategy, execution -- meets the client's objectives and is solving their challenges i.e. making sure our accounts are focused on driving growth for our clients in the most efficient manner.</li>
<li>Lead regular account reviews with leadership as requested</li>
<li>On a monthly basis report burn/upsell/retention/scope to leadership and guidance on what we take to be the value of the relationship</li>
</ul>

<br>The team
<br>--------
<br><br>
Our Accounts team is at the heart of everything we do. Our charge is to take complex problems and break them down into solvable pieces. We are close partners with our clients and the face of Blue State to prospective clients. We help clients and prospects see opportunities more clearly and we help Blue State understand the challenges our clients face.
