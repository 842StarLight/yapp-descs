<h2>BPM Manager, WW Support Leader, Delivery Partner Management, Microsoft Customer Service and Support (CSS)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>With over 25,000 employees worldwide, the Microsoft Customer Service and Support team includes engineers, advocates, partner/sales/licensing specialists, and business leaders who serve our customers across 191 countries and 41 languages, providing best-in-class platforms and productivity services. Our Customer Services and Support (CSS) team is on the front lines with our customers - Enterprise, Commercial, Consumer Customers and Partners - helping them get the most from their technology investments every day.<br>
<br>
We are looking for a senior strategic business thought leader with demonstrated leadership experience managing global supplier networks and strategic partnerships at scale in Services/Technology to lead our outsourced delivery supplier management team within CSS.<br>
<br>
This role will have the responsiblity for managing the organziaton and associated proceses to support our delivery partner ecosystem – ensuring we maintain an effective enablement approach which ensures we are properly supporting our partners, understanding their needs and setting them up for success. This includes (but not limited to) managing a team of global professionals responsible for working directly with our delivery partners, fostering best practice sharing and innovation, management and delivery of requirements, and exective relationship / account management.<br>
</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Delivery Partner Relationship Management across a global complex supplier portfolio</li>
<li>
Manage overall partnership with suppliers, creating network of key contacts to understand capabilities and competencies of suppliers and building synergistic long term relationships</li>
<li>
Act as the primary point of contact for supplier issues and requests from within the business<br>
</li>
<li>Act as the primary interface between CSS and our global network of suppliers</li>
<li>
Manage service level agreement contracts and compliance with Microsoft supplier policies<br>
Supplier resource planning</li>
<li>
Manage supplier selection criteria, processing, contract management and termination; including interfaces with the Global Procurement Group to coordinate global supplier management<br>
</li>
<li>Streamline supplier sourcing process and manage supplier pipeline<br>
</li>
<li>Manage supplier mix and balance planning to prevent overdependence on one or a subset of partners, as well as ensuring fit, quality and competency of suppliers sourced<br>
</li>
<li>Plan future sourcing strategy as part of sourcing long rang plan<br>
Partner innovation and strategy development<br>
</li>
<li>Monitor the health and structure of the partner ecosystem, looking for areas of development or potential value-add to investigate through active interaction with Partners<br>
</li>
<li>Develop and define Partner strategy and vision to enhance partner relationships and plan execution for initiatives to drive towards additional business value in the Partner channel<br>
</li>
<li>Work with Partners to co-develop tools and processes to improve output and efficiency</li>
</ul>
<div>
Success Criteria:</div>
<ul>
<li>
High quality of supplier performance and ease of logistical supplier management<br>
</li>
<li>Consistent and skilled supplier staffing pipeline resulting from strong relationships<br>
</li>
<li>Industry leading time between supplier role vacancy and new hire competency<br>
</li>
<li>Strong performance from sourced supplier hires across functions and SBUs</li>
<li>
Achieving better than benchmark employee experience</li>
<li>
Enabling digital transformation of delivery through suppliers (as required).</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>15+ years of business leadership experience; self-motivated, confident working at a senior executive level and comfortable working in a matrixed organization</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in/understanding of managing a large global network of suppliers/vendors and of planning such networks in Service/technology industry, and of strong collaboration across a matrixed global organization</li>
<li>Demonstrated skills in building organizational partnerships and in global communication skills - across a complex stakeholder network in CSS, and a large global vendor base.</li>
<li>Demonstrated business acumen, being able to craft and lead right vendor strategy as per needs of business.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in leading organizations through to success - drafting right strategy, working as necessary with vendors and employees at different levels, investing effort in building right stakeholder partnerships, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to create effective relationships, listen, communicate, influence, and collaborate cross-team at all organizational levels</li>
<li>Relevant Bachelor’s degree or experience commensurate with job expectations for this leadership position is required.</li>
</ul>
