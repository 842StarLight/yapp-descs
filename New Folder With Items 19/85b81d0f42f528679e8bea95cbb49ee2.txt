<h2>Customer Service Representative – Salina, KS</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">You will be responsible for actively maintaining an ongoing relationship with customers and the ADM Commercial Team. Will have daily exposure to customers in a dynamic environment and will be responsible for managing many aspects of customer accounts. Representatives will have exposure to order entry/scheduling, accounts receivable, accounts payable and logistics management. Limited travel may be required.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
Work in a busy, customer service-oriented environment
<br>Partner with the Commercial Team to maintain excellent relationships with customers
<br>Interact with customers regarding markets and documentation needs
<br>Enter and manage sales and purchase contracts
<br>Process invoices and monitor accounts receivable and accounts payable
<br>Researching and obtaining resolution of customer issues
<br>Effectively manage positive relationships with ADM Assets, ADM Technical Team and Shared Services
<br>Cross training on all CSR responsibilities
<br>Other reasonable tasks as assigned by supervisor
<br>Produce settlements, checks and delivery sheets for customers/grain producers daily using grain accounting software system
<br>Assist the Scale clerk with weighing trucks, grading grain as needed

<br>Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, Word and Outlook required
