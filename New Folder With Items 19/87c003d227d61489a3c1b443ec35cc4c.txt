<h2>Marketing Services Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Under the direction of the Director of Marketing, the Marketing Services Manager will oversee a team of two in-house designers, and have the responsibility of shaping Davidoffâ€™s newly created Marketing Services Dept. In addition, the MSM will be a key stakeholder in driving alignment between local and global design teams, working to leverage both pools of resources to enable efficiencies and consistency of output, as well as working with external agency partners.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>include the following.</p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as primary point of contact for commercial and retail team needing access to marketing services support.</li>
<li>Lead graphic design team consisting of two direct reports. Inspire team through excellent organizational and time management skills, along with a meticulous attention to detail. Must be able to communicate ideas visually, verbally and in writing.</li>
<li>Execute the design requirements for new brand initiatives in close collaboration with the US Brand team and Global Marketing team. Support product design and consumer / trade asset development.</li>
<li>Establish a strong working relationship with Global Marketing team to ensure effective and efficient deployment of global brand creative execution following brand or initiative guidelines.</li>
<li>Plan, analyze, and create effective visual solutions that bring to life brand messages across print and electronic media using color, type, illustration, photography, animation, and various print and layout techniques.</li>
<li>Open to new ideas and influences and quick to react to changing trends. Monitor and understand competitors within the product category.</li>
<li>Develop marketing and commercial toolkits to increase sell-in and promote sell-through.</li>
<li>Monitor and manage budgets and ensure budget compliance and accuracy. Estimate all project costs with vendors and conduct negotiations to ensure compliance with those costs.</li>
<li>Communicate and coordinate with internal stakeholders or third-party vendors regarding sample creation and procurement.</li>
<li>Develop point-of-sales and promotional materials. Coordinate, develop and produce all advertising and sales support material.</li>
<li>Travel to trade shows, events and retail locations (owned and third party) as required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>Responsible for own work and required to supervise two direct reports.</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>Bachelor's degree (B. A.) from four-year College or university desirable; 5-6 years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. Retail marketing and digital experience, preferably from luxury retail.</p>
