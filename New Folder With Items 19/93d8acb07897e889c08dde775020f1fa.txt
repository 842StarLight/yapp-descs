<b>
Job Summary: </b>The Team Lead ensures that In-Shape provides an environment that is clean, safe, and enjoyable for members and employees. This role partners with the club Leadership to drive success and profitability of the club through delivery of an excellent club experience, high member and employee retention, and effective expense controls.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Able to use technology resources to accomplish job duties, including but not limited to: Email, Word Processing, Excel, Salesforce, Point of Sale systems, scheduling software, etc.</li>
<li>
Ensures all employees complete required training in the prescribed timeframes and follows-up to ensure training is properly recorded in the training database. At the direction of the GM, may assist in the hiring and training process.</li>
<li>
Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with ISHC policies and applicable laws, including:<ul>
<li>
Training employees in Welcome Desk, Housekeeping, Kids Zone and Fitness Advisor depts.</li>
<li>
Planning, assigning, and directing work.</li>
<li>
Monitoring appraisal performance of Welcome Desk staff/Housekeeping staff/Kids Zone staff/Fitness Advisor staff.</li>
<li>
Rewarding and recognizing team members for GREAT performances, reach out to management for guidance on how to handle staff members underperforming.</li>
<li>
Addressing complaints and resolving problems of members and team members.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Develops and reviews department schedules to ensure they are within the budgeted guidelines. This may be up to 4 different department schedules depending on club level, i.e. Welcome Desk, Housekeeping, Kids Zone, and Fitness Advisor. Works to provide optimum scheduling and coverage as to provide excellent member services. Ensures all schedules are entered and maintained in club timekeeping system.</li>
<li>
Works with GM/AGM on Human Resources and Payroll related activities/requirements for all paperwork for new hires, timekeeping, time off requests (with GM approval), audits of employee time records and all other employee matters in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Manages the proper completion and procedures including: Guest Waivers, Tanning Waivers, sales/follow up by other management staff as needed, audits and reports, and member renewals. Additionally may work with GM in lowering attrition at club.</li>
<li>
Works with GM to retain memberships and follow up on cancellations.</li>
<li>
Partners with GM or AM to support membership sales growth. Helps drive sales, especially during weekends.</li>
<li>
Assists with sales production as needed. Works with Front Desk staff to ensure they are capable of selling memberships, per company policy.</li>
<li>
Manages the proper completion and procedures including: AP management, deliveries, POS reviews.</li>
<li>
Supports GM to assure that company policies and procedures are consistent by clearly understanding them in order to properly train, communicate, and explain to employees. In the absence of GM, may provide direction to other departments.</li>
<li>
Maintains multiple departments inventory supplies to ensure the Club is in stock at all times by accurate record keeping. Orders supplies for Welcome Desk, Housekeeping and Kids Zone (where applicable); ex. towels, janitorial, pool/spa, cleaning chemicals, POS, equipment parts, office. Manages all Purchase Orders.</li>
<li>
Documents daily cleaning on cleaning checklists.</li>
<li>
Maintains or oversees pool and spa operations (where applicable). Participates in the annual Health Department Inspections with inspectors and maintains pool chemical logs per requirements. Checks and reviews status per county rules and regulations.</li>
<li>
Responsible for managing employee or member injury/accident incidents by investigating incident, completion of forms, follow-up on safety area, and report all facts to Human Resources and Management.</li>
<li>
Participates in “Team Clean” process to pick up a variety of items from varies sections of the club (weights, towels, trash, etc.). Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, distance vision, color vision, peripheral vision, depth perception and ability to adjust focus.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Requirements and Qualifications:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma or GED required. Advanced degree preferred.</li>
<li>
Excellent customer service and communication skills required.</li>
<li>
Ability to work well under pressure and time constraints and display positive behaviors in all member contacts.</li>
<li>
Ability to analyze, interpret company policies and communicate them to members and team members.</li>
<li>
Availability to work weekends and holidays required.</li>
<li>
Current CPR certification or ability to pass CPR certification within 60 days of hire date.</li>
<li>
Prior supervisory experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Prior experience in scheduling preferred.</li>
<li>
Prior inventory control experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Prior payroll experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Prior service industry experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Proficiency with MS Office.</li>
<li>
Ability to sit, squat, stand, walk, kneeling, bending/neck twist, twisting at the waist, balancing, pulling, pushing and stacking.</li>
<li>
Bilingual skills preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience with inventory, cash register use and balancing, making change, opening and closing procedures.</li>
<li>
Clear and effective written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Outstanding organization and strong time management skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent task completion and follow-through skills.</li>
<li>
Maintaining focus in a sometimes hectic and evolving environment.</li>
</ul>

<b>
Work Environment/ Physical Demands: </b>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is frequently exposed to fumes or airborne particles, changes in temperature/humidity; moving mechanical parts and vibration and exercise equipment; frequently required to stand, walk 4-8hours/8 hour day; use hands to finger, handle or feel; and reach with hands and arms; and frequently use both feet and move them forward/backward and side to side; seldom required to sit; frequently to climb, balance, stoop, kneel, crouch or crawl; and continuously reach and twist, including side-to-side movements; carry or move up to 25 pounds and occasionally lift, carry or move 26-50 pounds.</div>
