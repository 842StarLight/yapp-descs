<h2>Category Manager - Electrical Equipment/Services (2165)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>At ENGIE North America, innovation and excellence create a platform for growth. We have a range of businesses in the United States and Canada, including clean power generation, cogeneration, and energy storage; retail energy sales; and comprehensive services to help customers run their facilities more efficiently and optimize energy and other resource use and expense. Nearly 100 percent of our power generation portfolio is low carbon or renewable. Globally, ENGIE is the largest independent power producer and energy efficiency services provider in the world, with operations in 70 countries employing 150,000 people, including 1,000 researchers in 11 R&amp;D centers.<br>

<div><div>We are looking for talented and motivated individuals to create the future of energy and customer solutions. Join a rewarding and flexible work environment that encourages innovation and creativity to help customers meet their energy challenges today and in the future.<br>

<div><b>The Role: Category Manager Electrical Equipment/Services<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<div><b>Purpose
</b></div>
<div>This role will be part of North America Strategic Sourcing and Supply’s Team based in Houston, which function serves as one of the critical levers to achieve wider business transformation. This position will be responsible for developing and implementing overall category strategy for Electrical Equipment/Services Categories (Low/Medium/High Voltage Equipment, Lighting, etc.), optimizing its spend leverage across NA business entities to reduce Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) while managing Quality and Supplier Relationship Management (SRM).<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div>
<b>Location/Department
</b><br>
Houston/Procurement Department
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>Status
</b><br>
Exempt<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div><b>Essential Job Functions
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Partner with key internal stakeholders and drive alignment with senior management on Electrical Equipment/Services Strategies and Sourcing Plans
</li>
<li>Analyze spend, identify savings opportunity and develop action plans to leverage enterprise spend to improve TCO and deliver cost savings target
</li>
<li>Engage with key stakeholders at local business entities to understand the demand and business requirements
</li>
<li>Establish supplier selection criteria, lead competitive RFP bids, negotiate and drive final supplier selections in partnership with Engineering and Project Management
</li>
<li>Responsible for creation and documentation of competitive bidding process including cost breakdown template for Electrical Equipment/Services to be used across business entities
</li>
<li>Develop, align and implement category strategy playbook for NA with detailed communication plan in close cooperation with Engie Group Category Management Team
</li>
<li>Provide category expertise with deep knowledge of the supply market, conduct supply market analysis, identify potential supply and demand challenges/ opportunities and monitor technological advances
</li>
<li>Develop and nurture relationship with key suppliers to foster communication on forward looking strategies and long- range planning
</li>
<li>Work closely with Engineering, Project Management &amp; Health &amp; Safety to identify and qualify contractors and manage strategic suppliers through SRM program
</li>
<li>Provide category expertise to business teams to prepare for potential project bids
</li>
<li>Lead by example and deliver excellence in customer service to internal stakeholders in Plants operations
</li>
<li>Take responsibility for contract management activity and implement effective governance to monitor supplier performance and represent procurement in the resolution of disputes with suppliers
</li>
<li>Carry out other duties as assigned
<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
