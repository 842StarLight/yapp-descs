<h2>Financial Reporting - Senior Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>*Position based in Atlanta, GA office. Will have ability to work from home up to 2 days/week after the initial training period*</b></p>
<p>
This role prepares consolidates data and prepares financial reporting for BCD Travel's North America region; Ensures financial policies and procedures are adhered to so the assets of the company are secure, expenditures are properly outlined and contractual obligations are properly budgeted. This role is responsible for timely and accurate accounting and preparation of journal entries, reconciliations &amp; financial statements.</p>
<p><b>
Primary job duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Financial analysis</li>
<li>Coordinate with Country and Division owners on a monthly basis to ensure that their local books are closed in order to prepare consolidated reporting on a monthly basis for Actuals, and Forecast</li>
<li>Follow up with Country and Division owners on a monthly basis regarding their actual versus budget variance analysis and putting together a consolidated variance summary based on those submissions (using excel)</li>
<li>Preparation of a written monthly report explaining the monthly variances of our actual results compared to budget and forecast. This monthly report will include graphs and excel charts that will need to be updated</li>
<li>Uploading the actual results on a monthly basis into our global consolidation tool (SAP BPC)</li>
<li>Update the reconciliations in SAP BPC for AR, Provisions, Fixed Assets, Financial Fixed Assets and Equity</li>
<li>Preparation of the consolidated AR for the Americas region and comparing it to our targets to ensure we are within the required threshold</li>
<li>Extracting the foreign exchange rates from our master database and loading them in to PeopleSoft</li>
<li>Mapping of local country trial balances for upload into both PeopleSoft and SAP BPC</li>
<li>Utilize PeopleSoft N-vision to create, update and modify reports (heavy use of excel)</li>
<li>Utilizing SAP BPC to extract reports</li>
<li>Preparing various surveys that are required by the US Government (these are mostly financial in nature)</li>
<li>Prepare various reporting for clients relating to diversity spend</li>
<li>Assist with the monthly Forecast process (includes management of the ledgers and various reporting)</li>
<li>Assist with the annual Budgeting process, including consolidating and recording intercompany elimination entries</li>
<li>Assist with the Audit which includes preparing the annual Consolidating reporting package, as well as other requirements</li>
<li>Preparation of the daily transaction report (a graph showing our daily sales and transactions)</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years Accounting or finance experience in a corporate setting</li>
<li>Proficiency in accounting software and spreadsheets</li>
<li>Peoplesoft (N-Vision) and SAP Business Planning and Consolidation software (BPC) preferred</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, Word and PowerPoint - intermediate to advanced skill set to include producing charts/graphs for financial data</li>
<li>Excellent problem solving skills - anticipating, initiating and resolving issues.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work independently and with diverse, remotely-located, and cross-functional teams</li>
</ul>
