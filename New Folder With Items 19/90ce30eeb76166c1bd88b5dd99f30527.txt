<p><b>
What's the Job?</b><br>
</p>
<p>The <b>Business Development Director</b> will have a lead sales role and develop, track and execute on the strategic BD plan established in conjunction with leadership. As a co-author of the BD plan, and a top sales and development leader within the company, the BD Director will strategically identify and target the specific type of work necessary to achieve the vision of the company. They will develop long term relationships, generate leads, conduct research to identify opportunities that align with company targets, advance the sales process, facilitate the closing of new business and assist in achieving opportunity-based sales goals. The BD Director will acquire strategic targets that include multidisciplinary opportunities, ideally identifying opportunities that leverage the combined service offerings of the company.</p>
<p>
The BD Director will conduct research to identify leads and business targets through any appropriate means: telephone, email and in person. This individual will also actively participate in the development, planning and execution of the BD plan, while coordinating with other internal teams and colleagues to align pursuits with Advoc8 messaging and outreach. The BD Director will report to the Managing Director of Growth and will provide periodic updates to the leadership team.
</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities (Strategic):</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead efforts to prepare a comprehensive sales plan in support of strategic goals established by company leadership</li>
<li>Identify decision makers within targeted leads to begin sales process</li>
<li>Develop sales opportunities by engaging in introductory and initial discovery meetings, and helping to identify specific opportunities that exist</li>
<li>Build &amp; maintain strong relationships with key industry leaders who can facilitate additional opportunities for the company</li>
<li>Pursuit of C-suite level contacts (new and existing)</li>
<li>Pursuit of Fortune 100-500 prospects (new and existing)</li>
<li>Collaborate with appropriate team members to determine necessary strategic sales approaches</li>
<li>Confidently work within existing CRM to manage and track opportunities</li>
<li>Take a leading role in the maintenance and expansion of the companyâ€™s data of prospects</li>
<li>Set up sales presentations, client prospect introductions and other sales actions</li>
<li>Create and deliver qualified opportunities to other team members</li>
<li>Ensure the handoff of opportunities receive the appropriate follow-up in a timely and thoughtful way</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities (Team):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop, in coordination with Marketing, the necessary strategies, tools, materials, programs, events and other targeted relationship building efforts needed to acquire the desired work</li>
<li>Meet regularly with BD team to coordinate proposal, pitch presentation and other tactical BD efforts</li>

<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><i>
The ideal candidate is someone who is actively engaged in a sales role at an agency with major corporate brand clients, who has worked previously as an Account Director or Account Manager, preferably with a background in, or working knowledge of, experiential marketing tactics.</i></p>
