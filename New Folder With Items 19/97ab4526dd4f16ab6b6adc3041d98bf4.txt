<h2>Vice President of Customer Experience</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
Hayes Software Systems is a dynamic, well-established, and rapidly growing, software/SAAS company in the education vertical.</p>
<p>
We want to help you build a stable career and future, but we also want you to have fun while you do it. That's why we offer robust benefits like a 401(k) with company matching and perks like an open work environment, casual dress, free snacks/drinks, free parking, and regularly scheduled social events.</p>
<p>
Combining our established reputation with a culture often associated with startups makes Hayes an environment for talented, energetic, and ambitious people to thrive. We are just getting started.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
How our customers feel about their experience with Hayes Software Systems is critical to our core values, business strategy, and everything we stand for. Hayes is heralded by our K-12 school customers as the premier brand in education and the company that provides customer service like no other. We're bringing together our Customer Support and Customer Success groups into one cohesive team to serve this mission. To lead this effort, we are creating a new position and looking for an ambitious, driven, and collaborative VP of Customer Experience.</p>
<p><b>
Why You</b></p>
<p>
You'll have the ability to set an inspirational vision and establish clear objectives, goals, and milestones for the company's customer experience strategy. You will drive continuous improvement and champion positive change to ensure that we continue to exceed the expectations of our customers. The VP of Customer Experience will report directly to the Chief Operating Officer and will work with two already established teams full of talented people who are fantastic at what they do, care about the customer, and continuously showcase their energy, grit, and perseverance.</p>
<p>
You work closely with both customers and development teams every day, whether they are across multiple time zones or sitting right next to you. You work fast, succeed fast, fail fast, and are always learning. You are looking for an opportunity in a startup similar culture, with the backing of a grounded and profitable company with a history of growth. You are surrounded by smart, dedicated, fun-loving people. And importantly, you are proud of the company's mission and our impact. Candidates must have a collaborative style with a strong sense of urgency and desire to get the right things done.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead the vision and design of our Customer Experience program</li>
<li>
Optimize the entire post-sales customer journey to create raving fans and manage churn</li>
<li>
Champion our continued focus of a company-wide culture of obsessive customer focus</li>
<li>
Define operations metrics for the Success and Support teams and report on those metrics to leadership</li>
<li>
Measure and observe customer usage and satisfaction and incorporate those findings into product and service developments</li>
<li>
Optimize the feedback loop with training and onboarding to minimize tickets due to training gaps</li>
<li>
Align with Product Management to effectively understand and triage feature requests and champion the needs of our customers</li>
<li>
Engage deeply with customers, attending Health Checks/QBRs, and build close relationships with customer executives</li>
<li>
Optimize information share between Success and Support activities to identify gaps in customer understanding and address those through people-touch or tech-touch methods</li>
<li>
Act as a subject matter expert on emerging customer experience trends and best practices</li>
<li>
Influence future lifetime value through greater product adoption, customer satisfaction, up-sell/cross-sell campaigns and overall health scores</li>
<li>
Work closely with Sales and Marketing on customer advocacy efforts</li>
<li>
Coach and mentor Success and Support teams, encouraging continued professional learning and skill advancement</li>
<li>
Use a data-driven approach to building budget, growth, and retention plans</li>
<li>
Translate business objectives into a successfully executed strategy</li>
<li>
Advise Finance on pricing and billing strategies</li>
</ul>

Excellent people management skills; high emotional intelligence and consideration for team morale and individual career progression</li>
<li>
Experience building and scaling world-class teams; experience hiring, onboarding, and training</li>
<li>
Understand the development and implementation of large-scale, complex applications</li>
<li>
Experience working for a company that provides a 'mission-critical' product and an unwavering positive customer experience</li>
<li>
Experience in roles requiring metrics, analytics, and excel work</li>
<li>
Experience managing geographically distributed teams</li>
<li>
Experience with Salesforce and project management systems like Jira/Wrike is preferred</li>
</ul>
