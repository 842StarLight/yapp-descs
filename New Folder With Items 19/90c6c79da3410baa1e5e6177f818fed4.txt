<p><b>Job Summary
</b><br>
The Junior level Exhibit Designer functions as an integral member of the design staff, providing quality and creative custom exhibit/trade show booth designs for our customers.
<br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES (includes other duties as necessary) </b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Designing creative eye-catching tradeshow exhibits.
</li>
<li>Solid 3D and 2D software experience in the latest CAD and rendering packages. SolidWorks a plus
</li>
<li>Clearly and professionally present ideas and designs to partners and client during design meetings via face-to-face, phone, and email.
</li>
<li>Take design direction from the partners, client, sales representatives, and Department Manager.
</li>
<li>Operate effectively under time constraints and meet deadlines.
</li>
<li>Display ability to design to budget and estimate the project.
</li>
<li>Ability to creativity present concepts and ideas to sales and clients.
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.
</li>
<li>Able to work with minimal supervision, both alone and in teams.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions. <b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE
</b></p>
<ul><li>BA/BS 3D Design or equivalent from four-year College or technical school.
</li></ul>
<p><b>Other Key Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to design in 2D &amp; 3D. Must be detail oriented, able to prioritize tasks and able to work under pressure with multiple deadlines
</li>
<li>Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>Exposure or experience in tradeshow exhibit design, industrial design, or architectural design a must.
</li>
<li>Possess an understanding of exhibits as a marketing and communication tool</li>
</ul>
