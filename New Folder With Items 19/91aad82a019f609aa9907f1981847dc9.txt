<h2>Restaurant Assistant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Blue Heron Restaurant is seeking an Assistant Restaurant Manager that will assist the Restaurant Manager with<b> </b>front of house and banquet operations, including planning, organizing, staffing, training and controlling the operations of the restaurant. The goal of this position is to lead the delivery of our service promise:
</div>
<div>
<b><i>Superb Food, Impeccable Service and an Ambiance thatâ€™s Second to None</i></b>.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Educational Requirements: </b>A combination of practical experience and some formal education in restaurant management is desirable.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Experience and Background Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>A proven track record of success in FOH operations, BOH operations also preferred.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Point-of-Sale system operations</li>
<li>Knowledge of current culinary and service trends.</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of computer systems and software.</li>
<li>Ability to handle day-to-day problems in a calm and professional manner.</li>
<li>Ability to lead and train a front-of-house staff</li>
<li>Ability to step into any FOH role as needed, including hostess, bartender, server, expo, bus.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Responsibilities/Duties/Functions/Tasks:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Customer Service</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Maintaining the highest level of customer satisfaction is priority number one.</li>
<li>Assist in resolutions to complaints.</li>
<li>Provide excellent customer service on the phone and follow-up on all inquiries and leads for catered events and parties.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Staffing/Training:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assist in training staff to ingrain in them that fast, friendly service is their top priority.</li>
<li>Assist in ensuring staff are trained in food safety and alcohol service safety requirements.</li>
<li>Update job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities, reading professional publications and participating in professional organizations.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Financial:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assist in budget establishment and monitoring expenditures and directing changes to keep expenses within budget.</li>
<li>Assist in controlling costs by providing feedback to BOH on portion control and quantities of preparation, minimizing waste and ensuring high quality of preparation.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Operations:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Lead the successful preparation and delivery of private events and catered events.</li>
<li>Assist in monitoring all operations and make recommendations to the Manager as necessary to meet customer service, food quality and budgetary goals.</li>
<li>Inspect facilities routinely and make sure all repairs are documented and repaired.</li>
<li>Assist the Manager in ordering and receiving alcohol.</li>
<li>Maximize bar profitability by ensuring portion control and monitoring accuracy of charges.</li>
<li>Assist the Manager in maintaining safe, secure and healthy environment by establishing, following, and enforcing sanitation standards and procedures.</li>
<li>Maintain ambiance by controlling lighting, background music, linen service, glassware, dinnerware and utensil quality and placement. Monitor food presentation and service.</li>
</ul>
