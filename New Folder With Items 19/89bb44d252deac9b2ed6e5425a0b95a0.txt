<div>
We are seeking a driven, motivated leader who knows that being the best means having vision and following through on that vision. This is a highly empowering position requiring high energy and the ability to motivate, cultivate and mentor a high-performance team devoted to surpassing the goals you set for them. We value forward thinking, progressive and creative personalities. We provide the resources; you provide the leadership, inspiration and results. This is a chance to develop a unique culture and develop a personal and professional brand.</div>

<div>

<ul>
<li>
Overall attainment of hotel revenue and will oversee Group Sales, Leisure Transient Sales, Business Travel Sales, Catering Sales and Conference Services.
</li>
<li>Share in the development and execution of Revenue Management strategies in combination with the Director of Revenue Management and Vice President Managing Director.
</li>
<li>Insure group rates and pricing are properly managed as it relates to group sales and catering (rooms, F&amp;B, amenities, etc).</li>
<li>
Primarily responsible for all marketing efforts of the hotel to include e-Commerce, Direct mail, Advertising, etc.</li>
<li><div>
Leadership and management of a marketing/PR and Social Media partner to drive/create demand and awareness of the hotel complex</div></li>
</ul>
<div>
The Vice President of Sales and Marketing will be the key driver in hotel relations with, CVBs and the community.</div>
