<h2>Mechanical Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Mechanical Engineer</b></p>
<br>
<p><b>Reports To:</b></p>
<p>Director of Engineering</p>
<br>
<p><b>FLSA Classification:</b></p>
<p>Exempt</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Function &amp; Responsibility</b></p>
<br>
<p>Performs engineering design for sales orders, new product design and ECR's (engineering change requests) of a complex nature.</p>
<br>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Designs and executes layouts of complex products and equipment. Complexity of assignments requires considerable initiative, latitude, and independent judgment.</li>
<li>Performs original design work after receiving the design goal or problem, general method of processing, and engineering advice.</li>
<li>Performs routine engineering solutions for design problems that require simple stress analysis, strength of materials, interchangeability, manufacturing methods and cost reduction.</li>
<li>May provide work leadership to lower level designers and drafters and coordinating assignments.</li>
<li>May check completed drawings for accuracy and completeness.</li>
<li>Uses computer assisted drafting (CAD) equipment and software. SOLIDWORKS</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Resolves problems using solutions that involve new techniques, technologies, or concepts.</li>
<li>Works with management and production staff to determine engineering feasibility, cost effectiveness, and customer demand for new and existing products.</li>
<li>Maintains a working knowledge of new technologies which may improve operations, and develops recommendations accordingly.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities Required</b></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to design work flows and procedures.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate both orally and in writing in English.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>The ability to interact with all levels of management both internally and within customer/vendor base.</li>
<li>Computer literate with strong knowledge of SolidWorks and Microsoft office.</li>
<li>Knowledge of SolidWorks CAD best practices.</li>
<li>Knowledge of GD&amp;T and drafting practices.</li>
<li>Proficient with large assemblies, configurations, properties and drawing attributes.</li>
<li>Understanding of revision control and design process.</li>
<li>Must have strong organizational skills and pay close attention to detail.</li>
<li>Ability to plan, organize and work successfully on simultaneous projects.</li>
<li>Finish requirements and tolerances for manufacturing sheet metal and vacuum chambers.</li>
<li>SW PDM experience a plus especially Admin experience.</li>
<li>General knowledge of machine shop and welding process helpful.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Requires a Bachelor Degree in engineering or related field. Education may be substituted with applicable work experience.</li>
<li>3-5 years of work experience in a similar role with similar responsibilities ideally in a like industry.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<br>
<p>The work environment characteristics described here are representative of those an employee encounters while performing the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to stand; use hands to finger, handle, or feel; reach with hands and arms; and talk or hear. The employee is occasionally required to walk and stoop, kneel, crouch, or crawl. The employee must regularly lift and/or move up to 10 pounds, frequently lift and/or move up to 25 pounds, and occasionally lift and/or move up to 50 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, depth perception, and ability to adjust focus.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly exposed to fumes or airborne particles. The employee is occasionally exposed to moving mechanical parts. The noise level in the work environment is usually loud.</p>
