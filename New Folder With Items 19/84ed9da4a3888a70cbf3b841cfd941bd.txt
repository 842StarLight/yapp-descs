<div>Description: Mortgage Loan Officer – Full Time Outside Sales</div>

<div>
Responsible Life, Inc. was founded in the US in 2014.</div>
<div>

<div>
</div>

<div>

<div>
We are looking for an experienced, ambitious and driven Mortgage Loan Officer to grow with us.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>Our culture is different from that at banks and other mortgage lenders - but we like it that way.</div>
<div>
We are 100% focused on an excellent customer experience. We strive to be the very best in class.</div>
<div>
We take care of our Loan Officers at Responsible Life and we care about your success.</div>
<div>

Responsible Life recognizes the crucial role you play.</div>

</div>
<div>
<div><b>
Duties &amp; Responsibilities – Mortgage Loan Officer – Outside</b></div>
<div>
Establish a strong presence for Responsible Life including but not limited to, developing relationships with Realtors, builders, financial service providers and Community Groups</div>
<div>
Participate in all steps related to the origination of a mortgage loan including but not limited to, meeting with prospects, counseling borrowers, taking mortgage applications primarily face to face, as well as by phone</div>
<div>
Attend various community events, luncheons, and events to raise referral source awareness of the Company’s products and services</div>
<div>
Learn various borrowing programs and processes and stay current with changes and new products</div>
<div>
Learn and utilize various company technical systems and programs</div>
<div>
Adhere to all federal and state compliance guidelines relative to the position</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Qualifications – Skills &amp; Requirements – Mortgage Loan Officer</div>
<div>
Minimum 2 years of current outside sales origination experience, including established contacts/relationships with realtors, builders, financial planners and other relevant referral partners</div>
<div>
Thorough knowledge of all mortgage products and programs</div>
<div>
NMLS license required under the terms of the S.A.F.E. Act of 2008</div>
<div>
Good knowledge of retail product philosophy, policy, underwriting, procedures, documentation and systems</div>
<div>
Knowledgeable in regulatory, policy and compliance issues pertaining to the mortgage industry</div>
<div>
Experience in proactively soliciting new business and establishing relationships</div>
<div>
Good analytical skills necessary to evaluate credit requests</div>
<div>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills and the ability to work with a wide range of diverse clients</div>
<div>
Proficiency in standardized software applications including Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook as well as Social Media</div>
