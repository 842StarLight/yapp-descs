The Senior Sales Analyst (SSA) is responsible for the work of Sales Analysts as well as the creation of standardized and customized reports that collect, analyze and evaluate quantitative data, sales funnel flows and future forecasts. This encompasses processes such as evaluating daily, monthly and annually sales goals, quotas and forecasts. Additionally, collecting and analyzing data to evaluate existing/potential clients and markets, researching / evaluating competitors, product gaps, industry and customer growth trends and conditions. The SSA develops and maintains electronic systems to track demand, industry shifts and customer buying changes with reports and dashboards.
<br><br>
Reporting to the Director of Sales Operations, the SA works closely with key stakeholders and other key players such as finance, salesforce, operations and information technology, to ensure accuracy of data reported and works to develop recommendations for modifications as needed. The SA is also responsible for ensuring that the requirements defined by the business are being delivered and assists in the execution of deliverables.
