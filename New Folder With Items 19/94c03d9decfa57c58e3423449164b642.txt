<div>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Assume a critical role with the Prudential International Division’s Information Technology organization in supporting the International Insurance business units’ IT Leadership Teams in the delivery of secure, quality systems solutions and services to their respective customers.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Assist in formulating a long term Information Technology Strategy for the Division by creating and articulating a vision of a target IT environment grounded in business value and built on sound architectural principles for facile adaptation and expansion in an evolving global operations model.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div>Establish a viable, multi-year implementation roadmap aligned with the IT Strategy, Enterprise Standards, and International Insurance Technology focus areas such as: 1) Robust Information Security; 2) Enabling Business Strategies and Plans; 3) Stable Cost-effective &amp; Modern Production Platform; 4) Digital Technology enablement; 5) Delivery Modernization; and 6) Future State Architecture.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div>Work closely with the Business Unit IT Leadership to ensure appropriate technological refreshment activities are executed within their businesses and enforce the adoption of high-risk corporate standards and policies by all business units. Review all significant technology initiatives against the IT Strategy and Standards to ensure success in support of business priorities.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Establish oversight of the business technology teams to assure the successful delivery of large, complex, mission critical application development and integration projects. Encourage IT management’s consistent execution and oversight of a proven Systems Development Life Cycle methodology.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Evangelize a more strategic approach to development projects for common business functions through the adoption of a PII applications architecture, enterprise IT standards, and proven technology tools to provide more effective business solutions. Work effectively as a PII IT Team leader to leverage best practices and subject matter expertise across the Division.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Maintain key relationships across corporate functions spanning the enterprise with emphasis on Technology Management to further explore the infrastructure and service leverage opportunities across the international businesses.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Cultivate and maintain good working relationships with key technology vendors that support the businesses with critical platforms, software and/or services.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Proactively invest in building and maintaining staff with the skills required to implement the IT Strategy and support applications architecture.</div>

<br>
