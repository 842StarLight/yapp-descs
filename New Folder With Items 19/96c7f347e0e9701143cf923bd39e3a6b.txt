<p><b>
General Purpose and Scope of Position:</b></p>
<p>
Wilbur-Ellis is seeking an experienced Operations Manager for a small site location. This role will manage support services within the location including warehouse, dispatch, and purchasing. It is the responsibility of the Operations Manager to ensure each support service area operates efficiently in order to support the overall Area goals. Reports include field service, warehouse and office personnel. Critical to the position is the ability to champion, implement, and embrace change.</p>
<p><b>
Key Skills and Abilities Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of five years’ experience in distribution with demonstrated track record in management</li>
<li>Working knowledge of DOT rules and regulations</li>
<li>Ability to work independently with minimum supervision</li>
<li>Proficient in all Microsoft office applications and in particular Excel</li>
<li>Good interpersonal and team building skills with a positive attitude and ability to establish relationships with location personnel, peers and customers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Personal Attributes Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate excellent written and oral communication skills with an ability to lead and influence</li>
<li>Detail and accuracy orientation with an ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Understands, practices, and maintains confidentiality</li>
<li>Minimum five years’ experience in distribution with demonstrated track record in management</li>
<li>Associates degree or comparable combination of education and work experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Specific Responsibilities and Key Deliverables Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure that all customer inquiries are responded to in a timely, accurate and appropriate manner</li>
<li>Assist with electronic order entry and processing (work order, sales order, shipping documents, invoicing) utilizing JD Edwards Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software</li>
<li>Purchase products as needed and supervise all aspects of inventory control</li>
<li>Oversee all aspects of facility operations and support</li>
<li>Provide daily direction to support personnel</li>
<li>Provide support to other team members as required to attain location goals</li>
<li>Assist in development of annual operating budget and manage to plan</li>
<li>Set department goals and objectives that are aligned with regions strategic plan</li>
<li>Hire new employees as required</li>
<li>As part of an overall performance management plan, conduct annual performance reviews with direct reports</li>
<li>Provide coaching and feedback to employees as required throughout the year</li>
<li>Ensure that all direct reports are properly trained in their necessary duties and suggest training to improve required job skills</li>
<li>Address poor performance with progressive discipline and use of performance improvement plans</li>
<li>Promote positive work environment</li>
<li>Present a professional image through personal appearance and overall facility cleanliness</li>
<li>Attend EHSS training sessions and meetings when necessary</li>
<li>Comply with all Wilbur-Ellis safety and regulatory procedures</li>
<li>Other duties as required and assigned</li>
</ul>
