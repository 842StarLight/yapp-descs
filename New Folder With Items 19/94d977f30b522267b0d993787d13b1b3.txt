</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Generate business through proactive outreach to new customers via cold calling and strategic email campaigning</li>
<li>Quickly develop value-based business relationships with B2B customers</li>
<li>Identify customer needs and requirements</li>
<li>Promote and position the strategic &amp; tactical values of the Platform Management solution offerings</li>
<li>Schedule concrete next steps with the customer, territory account manager, and any appropriate technical resources</li>
<li>Seamlessly transfer sales opportunities to the territory account manager</li>
<li>Research, qualify, manage and track leads and related sales activities</li>
<li>Develop internal sales relationships with sales specialist and sales engineers</li>
<li>Function competently and competitively in a fast paced, senior North American sales environment.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>2+years of outbound B2B sales experience OR a combination of work experience and education</li>
<li>Demonstrable verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to learn quickly and think analytically</li>
<li>Active listening skills.</li>
</ul>

Preferences</b></div>
