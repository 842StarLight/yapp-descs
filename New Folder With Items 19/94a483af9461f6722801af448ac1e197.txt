<p><b>The opportunity:</b>

<p>As a Senior/Principal Technical Consultant in the eDOCS Professional Services Practice you will Lead Technical Consultants, Business Consultants, and Project Managers in the delivery of OpenText eDOCS Document Management solutions from a domain business practice and technical perspective. You will be responsible for delivering quality configurable and customized implementations that meet the customer’s needs in accordance with documented business, user and functional requirements.</p>
<p><b>You are great at:

<ul>

<li>Leading quality outputs for solution implementation planning, design, configuration, development, testing and overall technical delivery within a project’s timeline and in conjunction with the Project Manager</li>
<li>Designing, Configuring, and implementing on various platforms and solutions</li>
<li>Delivering configurations and customizations for small, medium and large sized projects</li>
<li>Perform projects deliverables and activities to completion with fellow technical team members</li>
<li>Participating in pre-sales when required.</li>
<li>Provide technical and functional input for RFI’s/RFP’s</li>
<li>Understanding and practical working experience in the Legal industry and the Legal Matter Lifecycle</li>
<li>Providing suggested enhancements to the eDOCS Product Management team based on customer feedback and industry trends and implementation experience</li>
<li>Helping with the growth of existing client relationships by being a trusted advisor as a technical lead resource</li>
