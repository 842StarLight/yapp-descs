<h2>Customer Service Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Logistics done differently.</b></p>
<p>
XPO Logistics, a leader in the transportation and logistics industry, is looking for a hardworking, highly skilled professional who knows how to get the best out of his/her team. As the Customer Service Supervisor, you will ensure customer satisfaction on an accelerated service commitment level, as well as lead the performance of a Customer Service Representative (CSR) team to provide maximum productivity and outstanding service. On our team, you’ll have the support to excel at work and the resources to build a career you can be proud of.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Pay, benefits and more.</b></p>
<p>
We are eager to attract the best, so we offer competitive compensation and a generous benefits package, including full health insurance (medical, dental and vision), 401(k), life insurance, disability and the opportunity to participate in a company incentive plan.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What you’ll do on a typical day:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinate and supervise the daily activities of the local CSR team to achieve a superior level of customer satisfaction; ensure customer service standards and procedures are followed</li>
<li>
Work directly with customers to research and resolve complex customer inquiries; identify root causes and develop effective solutions</li>
<li>
Act as the escalation point for customer care, both internally and externally</li>
<li>
Hire, motivate and develop direct reports; promote a high level of employee engagement</li>
<li>
Support and occasionally fill in for CSRs in the service center or district</li>
<li>
Ensure customer expectations are met and always act with the customer’s best interest in mind; advocate for best-in-class customer experiences and a customer-focused XPO culture</li>
<li>
Analyze, develop and monitor metrics and key performance indicators; provide appropriate reporting to senior management</li>
<li>
Proactively work with Operations, Sales, Marketing and Communications to continuously improve business practices and processes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you need to succeed at XPO:</b></p>
<p>
At a minimum, you’ll need:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree or 4 years of related work experience, or equivalent military experience</li>
<li>
3 years of leadership experience</li>
<li>
Experience building and improving processes, and establishing metrics to track and illustrate performance</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Office and CRM</li>
</ul>
<p>
It’d be great if you also have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Availability to work extended hours when necessary; travel occasionally</li>
<li>
Experience in a Less-than-Truckload (LTL) freight operations, transportation operations, logistics or supply chain environment</li>
<li>
Ability to quickly learn and achieve proficiency in new software applications</li>
<li>
Highly organized with proven ability to multitask; able to prioritize workload under pressure and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain high ethical standards and confidentiality</li>
<li>
Exceptional problem-solving skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Be part of something big.</b></p>
