<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Rare opportunity to oversee the catering services for the most beautiful and unique event spaces in Washington, DC, at the City’s only resort, The Omni Shoreham Hotel.</div>
<div>
<br>
Utilizing creativity and a keen attention to detail, the Director of Catering contracts new and repeat business opportunities in the forms of galas, weddings, social gatherings and high end corporate events. Additionally, the Director assists in the planning and execution of all events at the Omni Shoreham Hotel. This position oversees a team of Catering Sales Managers providing them with the assistance, training and motivation needed to ensure that all clients and guests receive the superior service they have come to expect from The Omni Shoreham.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Plans and coordinates a variety of large and small meetings, events, social affairs, fundraisers, galas and celebrations at the Omni Shoreham Hotel.</li>
<li>Clearly communicates each detail of the client’s requests to colleagues in the appropriate department(s) to ensure that all requests are met as efficiently and the event is executed to the client’s expectations.</li>
<li>Plans, evaluates, organizes and directs the activities of the Catering Sales Managers.</li>
<li>Responsible for anticipating needs, forecasting results, analyzing and arranging work processes for the Catering department.</li>
<li>Provide the necessary training, motivation, and leadership for all department personnel.</li>
<li>Ensures that all policies, procedures and guidelines are followed by department personnel.</li>
<li>Assists in the hiring of all departmental personnel.</li>
<li>Prepares performance goals and benchmarks for Catering Sales.</li>
<li>Writes and delivers performance evaluations for all departmental personnel.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains rapport with outside purveyors, who provide services for the Hotel and its clients through the Catering Department.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains efficient office procedures to facilitate the smooth operation of the department.</li>
<li>Ensures Catering Sales meet budgeted levels.</li>
<li>Ensures smooth flow of information from Catering Sales to Banquet Services and the Food &amp; Beverage Division.</li>
<li>Creates and maintains a good working relationship with the Banquet Management Team to ensure a high level of comfort with Banquet operations.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of seven (7) years of experience leading Catering Sales and exceeding sales quotas.</li>
<li>Minimum of five (5) years of experience working in hotels or resorts.</li>
<li>The ability to clearly communicate both verbally and in writing.</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational skills and strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>Strong leadership abilities.</li>
<li>Ability to problem solve and accommodate frequent changes.</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to work a varied schedule in order to oversee on-site events.</li>
<li>Four Year College Degree.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office and experience utilizing FDC/Delphi property management software a plus.</li>
</ul>
