<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About Frontier Communications:</b><br>
<br>
Frontier® Communications provides communications services to urban, suburban, and rural communities in 29 states. Frontier offers a variety of services to residential customers over its FiOS® and Vantage™ fiber-optic and copper networks, including video, high-speed internet, advanced voice, and Frontier Secure® digital protection solutions. Frontier Business™ offers communications solutions to small, medium, and enterprise businesses.<br>
<br>
<b>Solutions Sales Specialist:</b></p>
<p>
Frontier is developing an end-to-end cloud-based IT platform for hybrid cloud environments. As our enterprise customers migrate more and more of their applications to the cloud, this individual will work closely to Account Executives, Sales Engineers, and Solutions Architects to develop winning complex, multi-cloud, hybrid cloud commercial proposals. This role’s objective is to drive sales of Frontier’s Integrated Solutions through all channels.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Frontier Solutions Sales Specialists do not own accounts but are expected to engage with Frontier’s team of Account Executives to drive sales of Frontier’s Integrated Solutions. Individuals in this position must inspire and influence AE’s to raise opportunities within their accounts. Once cloud / integrated solutions are raised, Solutions Sales Specialists will take ownership for the process of developing the opportunity with the support of Sales Engineers, Solutions Architects, and the AE responsible for the account. Solutions Sales Specialists must understand customers’ motivations and business drivers to properly support positioning of Frontier’s competitive advantages.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Primary responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with Sales Engineers and Account Executives to develop winning complex solutions proposals for customers</li>
<li>
Drive AE’s to find cloud solutions opportunities. Help AE’s prospect and qualify leads daily</li>
<li>
Collaborate with solutions architects, sales engineers, account executives, the product team, and, often, with partners and vendors to develop winning architectural solutions, designs, and proposals</li>
<li>
Articulate Frontier’s value proposition through compelling customized narratives, product demos and engaging qualified expert speeches.</li>
<li>
Serves as the expert liaison to Frontier’s customers for more advanced information regarding cloud / security managed services</li>
<li>
Conduct high-level conversations with senior executives from prospective accounts</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
