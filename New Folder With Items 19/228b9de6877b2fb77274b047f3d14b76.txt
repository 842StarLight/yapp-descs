<h2>Material Support Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>Position Title:</i></b><b> Material Support Coordinator</b></p>
<p><b><i>
About Nortek Global HVAC</i></b></p>
<p>
Nortek Air Management is comprised of two businesses, Nortek Global HVAC and Nortek Air Solutions with annual sales of close to $1 billion across three market segments: Residential, Light Commercial and Custom Air Solutions. Our Company’s mission is Creating a Better Tomorrow Every Day. By living our core values of Safety, Integrity, Performance Driven Culture and Teamwork, we are a premier HVAC Company offering energy efficient and sustainable products and solutions that exceed customer expectations through employee’s commitment to quality, customer service and operational excellence.</p>
<p><b><i>
Position Summary:</i></b> The Material Support Coordinator is primarily responsible for keeping the order management and material to assure smooth production operations. Responsibilities include establishing Kanban setting, Production Order Management, Production order picking, managing material shortages, cycle counting, and general material flow into and out of the department.</p>
<p><b><i>
Position Key Attributes:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to personally live safe and hold themselves and other team members accountable by having their back in support of our Safety core value.</li>
<li>
Has integrity, works transparently and recognized for treating others with respect.</li>
<li>
Takes accountability to create and execute solutions that deliver desired results, and can “own it” in a Performance Driven Culture.</li>
<li>
Works collaboratively as one team in a Teamwork culture.</li>
<li>
Enjoys embracing and igniting change as a catalyst for improvement.</li>
<li>
A “builder” who is comfortable in a continuous improvement culture.</li>
<li>
Can thrive in a “legacy now” culture in which we strive to do the right thing for the company today, even if it is the hard thing, and leave behind a stronger company for the next generation of employees.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>
Why is this a great opportunity</i></b><b>?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Opportunity to be a key player in the smooth production order executing by maintaining the proper flow of RAW material to the lines.</li>
<li>
Develop experience in the complete supply chain flow within a manufacturing plant from supplier demands to end item shipment.</li>
<li>
Improve your SAP / ERP skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>
Position Responsibilities:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assure parts availability to meet the production schedule</li>
<li>
Cycle Counts Compliance</li>
<li>
DFT (Demand Flow Technology) Compliance</li>
<li>
Work with operations to set daily sequence schedule and manage material flow to assure uninterrupted production</li>
<li>
Root Cause for all material discrepancies</li>
<li>
Kanban sizing</li>
<li>
E-Kanban operation</li>
<li>
Material Placement and Material Flow</li>
<li>
Inventory accuracy</li>
<li>
Scrap Tracking/Root Cause</li>
<li>
Compliance Audits of other areas</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned by the Supervisor</li>
</ul>
