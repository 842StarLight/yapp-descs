<h2>Senior Network Solutions Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Geoverse</b></p>
<p>As the world becomes more and more connected so does the need for Geoverse Private and neutral-host cellular solutions. The Network Architect is an individual that wants to leverage their entrepreneurial spirit, drive and positive energy to help enterprises and campuses become smart, agile, and focused on experiences!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Role</b></p>
<p>The Network Architect will develop the network engineering and deployment strategy for the company. S/he will work on developing a long-term plan for the evolution of a next generation edge-enabled network that integrates the best practices of contemporary Data and Telecom networks. The role is responsible for the company’s end-to-end network architecture from the radio and core networks, to applications and services, including how services are designed, provisioned, billed, and supported. The Network Architect must be capable of developing and managing complex relationships with ecosystem partners. The Network Architect is a senior member of the company and reports to the CDO.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Job Functions</b></p>
<ul><li> Develop and document a comprehensive network strategy for the company and evolve the strategy as the company grows and markets change and mature.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Technologies that Geoverse will integrate will include cellular, Wi-Fi, fixed wireline and wireless and IoT platforms. Enterprises increasingly want a complete managed solution from a single entity and Geoverse must have a network architecture that delivers this. The Network Architect will be required to consider a seamless integration of all these technologies for a seamless customer experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Long term technology and product feature alignment with ecosystem partners. Identify, recruit, and maintain relationships with partners in the ecosystem including infrastructure providers, networking companies, IoT platform providers, enterprise voice &amp; messaging companies, among others which may be needed to execute on the product strategy and succeed in the market.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop and document a cross-platform, hybrid-cloud based systems architecture for the network. Portability and deployment of the Geoverse solution on major cloud service providers is required.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with ATNI’s shared engineering and operations teams to handover the deployment and operations of the network. Develop best practices to ensure a smooth and efficient transfer of the same.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide product support and demonstrations to sales, customers and channel partners. Includes developing and delivering sales support presentations and participating with sales in customer visits</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist with the development of financial models needed to support pricing/offer decisions</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop and implement appropriate mechanisms for measuring and maintaining high levels of customer satisfaction.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul><li> 10+ years’ experience in wireless, IoT and related businesses in senior network engineering roles.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Masters preferred. Related certifications a plus. Equivalent work experience will be considered.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong technical background in wireless, telecommunications, IT and WAN data networks.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Deeply interested in cellular network and WAN/LAN data technology.</li></ul>
<ul><li> History of repeated success at every level.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent presentation skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proven ability to work effectively with and across all levels within large and complex organizations.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Team player that collaborates within an internal set of multi-functional teams such as Technology, Engineering, Operations, Marketing, Program Management, Finance, Legal and Human Resources to ensure goals are achieved and exceeded</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with senior executives and ability to develop relationships at all levels</li></ul>
<ul><li> Thrives in a high-paced, changing work environment</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to multi-task while maintaining attention to detail and deadlines</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must have excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li></ul>
