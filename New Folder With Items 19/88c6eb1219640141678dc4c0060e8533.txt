<h2>RETAIL A+ COMPUTER TECH</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>MICRO CENTER</b> is the nation’s leading computer and electronic device big box retailer! Our technology super store has an industry-leading 40,000+ selection of premier computer products and the most knowledgeable staff in the business. Micro Center has been ranked by one of the leading consumer advocacy magazines as a top walk-in store to purchase a PC based on merchandise selection, service and pricing. Work with us and help us on our mission to be the best computer and electronic device store in the world!</p>
<p>
We are seeking self-motivated, friendly and knowledgeable <b>Retail A+ Computer Technicians</b> to diagnose and repair a variety of computer and electronic equipment and communicate with Service Department customers in our high-volume big box retail store.</p>
<p><b>
MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify, diagnose and document hardware failure or software problems on a variety of computer or computer-related equipment</li>
<li>Recommend components/configurations and assemble customized computer systems</li>
<li>Provide timely, complete and accurate information directly to customers via face-to-face, telephone or email communication for estimate approval and customer unit repair</li>
<li>Consistently achieve productivity, customer satisfaction and sales attachment goals of the service repair shop</li>
<li>Complete assigned work orders (diagnostics, upgrades or builds) within established timeframes</li>
<li>Complete all service system transactions accurately</li>
<li>Understand and utilize technical manuals and support resources</li>
<li>Keep up-to-date on retail products and current technologies and maintain appropriate technical certifications (CompTIA A+ required of all techs, Apple Certification a plus) to enable proper service of products as required by ASP partners</li>
<li>Maintain the department by ensuring a clean work area, tracking inventory, stocking and replenishing product and participating in merchandising activities</li>
<li>Support store operations by maintaining loss prevention awareness, assisting with Service Department activities and helping to prepare for physical inventory</li>
<li>Participate in open and close procedures</li>
<li>Perform other duties and tasks as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma or equivalent and knowledge of Microsoft Office required</li>
<li>CompTIA A+ certification or the ability to be certified within 90 days of hire</li>
<li>One year computer service repair or technical customer service experience, preferably in a production-oriented service environment</li>
<li>Ability to communicate professionally in person, via telephone and in writing and provide regular updates on customer work orders</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple customer issues and tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>Physical requirements: able to lift up to 50 lbs., stand for prolonged periods of time</li>
<li>Shifts will include hours before and after the store is open to the public and may include mornings, nights, weekends and holidays</li>
<li>Apple Certification, other technical certifications and additional years of experience a plus</li>
</ul>
