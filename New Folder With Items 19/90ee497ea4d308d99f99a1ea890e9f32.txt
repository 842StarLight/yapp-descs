<div><b>
Corporate Functions – Marketing &amp; Communications</b></div>
<div>
As part of this team, you’ll play a key role in helping Accenture execute its strategy. You’ll work with a diverse team of innovative marketing experts who focus on the following areas: global brand management and advertising; global events and sponsorships; internal communications; marketing research; media and industry analyst relations; corporate and financial communications; internet marketing; recruitment marketing; client-centric marketing; and industry, service line and geographic marketing campaigns.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
YOUR ROLE: Communication, Media and Technology Semiconductor Industry Marketing Manager</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Working with the High Tech Marketing Lead, and the key business stakeholders, you will be responsible for helping develop and implement the Semiconductor industry marketing strategy. Based on the business objectives you will develop, implement and measure marketing campaigns and activities, using a mix of marketing channels to showcase provocative, insightful content around a focused set of client-relevant issues.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Focusing on a content-led strategy you will help us to lead in the new by challenging traditional marketing channels to ensure we integrate digital and social marketing methods to develop, implement and measure marketing campaigns and activities - to showcase provocative, insightful content around a focused set of client-relevant issues, helping us to establish a clear, differentiated voice in the market.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
By packaging and promoting our perspectives and value delivered to our clients’ businesses across an integrated mix of traditional and digital marketing channels, we can enable our client-facing colleagues in sales and account management functions to engage clients in conversations around issues crucial to the growth of their businesses. The overall goals are to create deeper understanding of Accenture’s unique capabilities, help articulate Accenture’s role in the semiconductor industry ecosystem and establish a preference for Accenture in our target markets.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Candidates must be marketing professionals with experience in developing marketing plans and delivering creative, data-driven, integrated marketing programs across multiple channels such as thought leadership, digital marketing, social media and events to:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>create engagement and increase work with current clients
</div></li>
<li><div>open doors and build relationships with new clients
</div></li>
<li><div>create demand in new market segments
</div></li>
<li><div>help grow market share through strategies that support customer acquisition, engagement, loyalty, and cross-selling.</div></li>
</ul>
<div>Ideally you will understand or have experience in the Semiconductor industry as well as digital and business-to-business marketing, preferably in a professional services context. You will be pragmatic and self-assured in your ability to recommend the right marketing approach. You will be proactive, highly organized, a self-starter and experienced in developing content-based marketing campaigns, fluent command of the English language including superb writing and editing skills to help create content such as blog and social media posts. You must be a team player, with the ability to work with minimal supervision and be adept at creating gravitas and building senior stakeholder relationships.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
As well as the High Tech Marketing Lead, you will work closely with Accenture’s Semiconductor practice MDs and other marketers in the full range of Accenture businesses and functions. As a member of the broader Accenture Marketing &amp; Communications global team, you will collaborate with members of other teams; including internal comms, research &amp; insights team and media relations, to deliver market impact and support an integrated, consistent client experience.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
In this fast-paced role you need strong project management skills, confidence in managing senior stakeholders in our business, comfort with working in virtual teams in a matrixed organization and the ability to focus and manage competing priorities.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Key Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Responsible for the planning, execution, measurement and continuous improvement of semiconductor industry-specific marketing campaigns to help build awareness, generate sales leads and develop senior client relationships, including: events, digital marketing, social media marketing, corporate hospitality, sponsorships, conferences, advertorial, video, collateral, thought leadership development.</div>
</li>
<li><div>Create marketing activities to meet business objectives and create compelling marketing initiatives which will attract senior client executives.
</div></li>
<li><div>Develop and/or work strategically with Accenture’s roster agencies.

</div></li>

</div></li>

</div></li>
