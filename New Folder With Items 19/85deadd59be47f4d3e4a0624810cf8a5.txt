<h2>Inventory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Inventory Manager will be responsible for the management of our slow moving, aging and off-quality inventory, ensuring materials and products are sold or reworked to the fullest extent possible to minimize the need for scrapping. This position will be heavily involved in the Company long term production scheduling at all plants and inventory management of all inventory. You will work closely with Sales and Operations to ensure the right products are at the right place at the right time for the right customer. This position is responsible for identifying and standardizing supply chain best practices across all locations.</p>
<p>
Must have a strong background in supply chain, must possess a thorough understanding of materials, scheduling, inventory control and planning functions, as well as a solid understanding of a manufacturing environment. She or he will possess strong interpersonal and influencing skills and will be an effective communicator across all levels of the organization.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage inventory level parameters for raw materials, packaging and finished goods. Develop and drive the management of slow moving and obsolete inventory control program to ensure customer requirements are met, while mitigating financial loss.</li>
<li>Coordinates the disposition of aged/obsolete inventory with impacted departments, assist with the harmonization of product rework schedules at the plants between quality and operations. Identify and report reasons for inventory becoming obsolete and implement corrective actions to prevent recurrence.</li>
<li>Guide efforts to optimize our raw material and finished goods inventories with an eye to reducing lead time, maximizing service and minimizing working capital.</li>
<li>Develop a long-term master schedule for the plants that is based on historic analysis, forecast and stocking level requirements by planning, preparing, issuing the long-term production schedule and material requirements via an integrated computer software program to meet the necessary customer requirements.</li>
<li>Identify and lead opportunities to manage schedule adherence reporting and analyze for additional improvements with long term master scheduling and actual short-term schedule.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to handle multiple tasks and competing priorities at the same time with the ability to prioritize to achieve company objectives.</li>
<li>Other duties/projects as needed.</li>
</ul>
<div><p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE: </b>A Bachelor's degree in supply chain management or a related field or equivalent experienced is required. An MBA and/or profession certification (PMP, CPM, APICS) preferred. Must have 5+ years of experience in an operations /supply chain role with at least 2 years in inventory control preferably from a food industry. Must possess the ability to function well in a fast-paced team environment, leading and coordinating across multiple functions including Operations, Sales, Customer Service, Finance and Procurement.</p></div>
