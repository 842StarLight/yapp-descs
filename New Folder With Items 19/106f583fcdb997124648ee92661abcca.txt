<p>AllyO’s product is a flexible SaaS application platform hosted in the public cloud. Each customer has a unique implementation of the platform that services their needs. A solution engineer comprehensively understands the product and its capabilities and is the expert in customer specific implementations. Furthermore, a solution engineer can troubleshoot and fix issues whether configuration, code, or database that impact customer success. A solution engineer has an interesting mix of customer facing and technical challenges on a daily basis.</p>
<p>As a Solutions Engineer at AllyO, you will be instrumental in identifying, implementing, and leading teams developing tooling and designing productivity workflows to help the business scale and grow. You will partner with our Customer Success, Sales and Engineering teams to develop and deliver technical solutions and programs in a lead role that improve our business and our customers’ success. We are seeking someone who enjoys unique technical challenges and at the same time is passionate about customer experience &amp; team productivity.</p>
