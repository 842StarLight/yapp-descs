<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>As key facilitators between the client and internal departments, the Account Management team is an integral component of PrintMailPro.com.</p>
<p>From the initial stage of the request through delivery to the customer, the Account Manager is instrumental in coordinating with cross-functional internal teams to ensure customer needs are met. As a customer advocate, this position offers the opportunity to develop long-lasting customer relationships with clients of varied state, educational, and corporate industries. This is not an accounting position.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>- Responsible for managing client list to ensure needs are satisfied and exceptional services is provided on a day-to-day basis.</p>
<p>-Initiate client request by creating internal job document, ordering necessary materials, coordinating shipping or delivery of final product.</p>
<p>- Coordinate between customer and internal departments to ensure objectives and timelines are met.</p>
<p>- Answer client's queries pertaining to their accountsthrough email, phone, and in person meetings.</p>
<p>-Manage different organization's online ordering portals, including product set up and inventory.</p>
<p>-Assist with challenging client requests or issue escalations as needed.<br>-Generate various production management and customer required reports on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis.</p>
<p><b>Our Ideal candidate: </b><br>-Thrives in a quick-turn, high paced environment</p>
<p>-Able to prioritize and multi-task while maintaining sharp attention to detail<br>-Has experience working in a business setting and with delivering solutions to customer needs<br>-Learns quickly to understand new software interfaces<br>-Possesses strong verbal and written communication skills</p>
<p>-Knowledgeable with MS Office (particularly MS Excel and MS Word)</p>
<p>-Excellent listening and presentation abilities</p>
<p>-Natural relationship builder with integrity, reliability, and maturity</p>
<p>-Experience in the printing/mailing industry is preferred, but not required.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>sales: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
