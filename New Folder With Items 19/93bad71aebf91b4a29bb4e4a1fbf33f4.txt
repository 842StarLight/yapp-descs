<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>EverCommerce is seeking a Staff Accountant to join our growing Finance team. Reporting to the Controller, you will be responsible for the GAAP accounting of acquired entities.<br>
</p>
<p><b>You:</b><br>
You are a high-energy, detail-oriented person with a passion for accounting and numbers. You’re looking for a role at a growing company where you can learn the ins and outs of the business from the bottom up. You’re great at multi-tasking and a fast-paced office leaves you feeling energized—not drained.</p>
<p><b>
Us:</b><br>
EverCommerce is bringing together best-of-breed commerce solutions in the service sector, forming one service commerce platform that improves go-to-market strategy for technology companies and simplifies the software-buying process for business owners.</p>
<p>
Our team has extensive expertise in business operations, management, legal, finance, accounting, technology human resources, marketing and sales that, via the EverCommerce platform, we provide as a service to drive continued growth and success for growing service commerce companies. Our ecosystem enables companies to thrive in an environment of shared knowledge and resources with significant cost savings.</p>
<p>
We are building an extraordinary company and looking for talented, energetic, and motivated people to join our team.</p>
<p><b>
Where:</b><br>
EverCommerce headquarters is located in the heart of LoDo. Our collaborative, open-plan office space is filled with high-energy, passionate people who enjoy their work and each other's company.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist team with general ledger accounting</li>
<li>Reconcile accounts to monthly financial statements</li>
<li>Billing and accounts payable processing for multiple subsidiaries</li>
<li>General ledger postings and intercompany activity</li>
<li>Assist in month-end and year-end closing activities and analysis, including yearly audit</li>
<li>Work with the Controller and accounting team members with special projects of the Finance department</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Must Haves:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-4 years staff accounting experience</li>
<li>BS/BA degree in Accounting</li>
<li>Familiarity with month-end and year end close processes</li>
<li>Working knowledge of US GAAP standards and strong understanding of internal controls</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, be well-organized and have solid interpersonal and communication skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Nice to Haves:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Public accounting experience or a CPA</li>
<li>Experience with Intacct or other ERP software</li>
<li>Knowledge of acquisition and consolidation accounting</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits &amp; Perks:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive salary</li>
<li>Robust medical benefits &amp; 401(k)</li>
<li>Public transportation reimbursement</li>
<li>Gym stipend</li>
<li>Start with 17 vacation days plus your birthday off</li>
<li>Snacks, lunches, and happy hours</li>
</ul> <p>If you are looking for a company that is truly focused on empowering small businesses with superior technology, come join a company that rewards authenticity and supports energy with a passion.</p>
