<h2>Account Executive - Commercial Insurance Lines</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Description of role: </b></p>
<p>The Account Executive is responsible for promoting and selling assigned Pearl Insurance coverage(s) to potential clients over the phone and through email, mail, and fax. Handles servicing clients during their first year of policy coverage with Pearl.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prioritize sales leads from various sources, such as the Monthly Sales Funnel, Daily Activity Report, and leads from websites, database management, etc.…</li>
<li>Conduct a high volume of customer contact via the phone, email, mail, and fax in order to promote and sell assigned coverage(s).</li>
<li>Utilize marketing campaigns, advertising, and other marketing methods in order to meet monthly and annual sales goals in a defined territory.</li>
<li>Facilitate the application and underwriting process with the potential client and the internal underwriter; send the application to potential client; finalize the application and any applicable forms and prepare information for submission; follow up with the potential client when additional information is needed in order to complete the underwriting process.</li>
<li>Present the quote or estimate to the potential client; close the sale; attempt to gain market intelligence if unable to close the sale.</li>
<li>Document sales activities in the Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system.</li>
<li>Build and maintain customer relationships by responding to various questions, needs, and requests.</li>
<li>Represent Pearl Insurance at conventions and trade shows as necessary.</li>
<li>Serve as a back up to other sales representatives when they are not available.</li>
<li>Stay up to date on the product, market, and industry knowledge.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Property and Casualty insurance license required (or the ability to obtain).</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license with a record of safe driving preferred.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business management, marketing, or a related field</li>
<li>Previous sales and/or customer service experience, preferably within the insurance industry; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with a proven ability to interface effectively at all levels within an organization.</li>
</ul>
