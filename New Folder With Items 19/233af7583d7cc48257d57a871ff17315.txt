<h2>Sales Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB TITLE: Sales Operations Manager </b><br><b>REPORTS TO: Sr. Vice President, Sales </b></p>
<p><i>Kount is a leader in the digital fraud prevention space with over 5,000 customers, featuring some of the largest and most recognized brands in retail, ecommerce, and banking. Our technology uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to inspect billions of transactions to protect consumers and businesses against fraudulent activity and make online and mobile interactions smooth and enjoyable experiences. </i></p>
<p><i>Kount has an opening for a <b>Sales Operations Manager </b>in our <b>Boise, Idaho </b>office. </i></p>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>Seeking a bright, motivated, analytically inclined Sales Operations Manager to help us grow and evolve our sales operations function. Reporting to the SVP of Sales, the Sales Operations Manager works closely with the multiple internal stakeholders in Finance, Client Success and Sales Leadership on all sales related processes and projects. In addition, this role will play an integral part in driving operational efficiencies and effectiveness by innovating, streamlining and implementing best in class tools and business processes across the sales organization.</p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE, BUT ARE NOT LIMITED TO: </b></p>
<ul><li>Design and lead projects to improve sales processes and operational efficiency.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Own day-to-day operations supporting Sales related to Sales pipeline, quoting, ordering, and implementation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Implement systems to improve quality of sales data and maintain our customer records.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage Salesforce, sales automation and training programs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage third party sales operations tools and vendor management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Deliver analytical reports and insights to inform sales strategy.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in the coordination of sales forecasting, planning, and budgeting processes used within the sales organization.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide training and create help related materials for users as new sales applications are deployed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support existing Sales management in reducing back office administrative functions that allow the team to focus more on providing customer satisfaction, expansion and retention.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides immediate feedback to team member and participates in one-on-one sessions with assigned team member</li></ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul><li>5 years professional experience in sales operations for SaaS businesses or a related field.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated ability to solve problems and manage projects across multiple teams.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of Salesforce best practices and configuration.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Familiarity with sales automation tools.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Salesforce.com Administrator Certification or equivalent work experience (minimum 4 years).</li></ul>
<ul><li>4+ experience in advanced excel skills including vlookups and pivot tables.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication and analytical skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work independently, as well as part of a team, on multiple projects.</li></ul>
