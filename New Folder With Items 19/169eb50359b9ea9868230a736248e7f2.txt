<p>
The Financial Analyst will be assisting in the implementation of ASC 606. This individual should display strong attention to detail while also demonstrating the ability to analyze and communicate financial data efficiently.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following, but are not limited to:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Complete template analysis of existing and new revenue recognition methodology for each customer contract.</li>
<li>Read contracts and ascertain relevant information for template analysis.</li>
<li>Extract relevant details from historic use of Quickbooks.</li>
<li>Input results of analysis at Sales Order level in NetSuite.</li>
<li>Liaise with Deloitte’s, then company’s auditors on the revenue recognition analysis.</li>
<li>Assist with other ad-hoc accounting and compliance work such as filing of Sales Tax returns.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced skills in Excel with knowledge of spreadsheet functionality including v-look-up, pivot tables, and charts. A test will be administered as part of the interview process.</li>
<li>Demonstrated understanding of GAAP accounting methodologies and principles; detailed knowledge of ASC 606 is not required.</li>
<li>2-3 years’ experience in an accounting function involving General Ledger and Balance Sheet accounting.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize multiple/concurrent tasks, to solve problems, and to meet deadlines in a fast-paced and dynamic environment with minimal supervision is required.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate and interact professionally and effectively through written and oral communication with clients and coworkers.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting/Finance or similar level of education/working experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in NetSuite and Quickbooks preferred.</li>
<li>Must be a self-starter and possess the ability to work in a team environment.</li>
</ul>
