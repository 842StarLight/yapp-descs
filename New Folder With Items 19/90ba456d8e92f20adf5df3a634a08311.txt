<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Do</b></h2>
<p>Cisco is looking to identify a Global Account Manager with talent, drive and ambition to sell Cisco solutions and own relationships in support of Cisco’s global relationship with Enterprise customers. This role will involve leading the Cisco relationship up to a senior level with customers in EMEAR. It is a customer facing role supporting multiple countries in non-HQ locations and will require domestic and international travel.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<p>Implementation of the Global Teams sales strategy ensuring sales targets are met or exceeded in marketplace.</p>
<p>Develop and handle relationships with new and existing clients within your customer base to expand sales.</p>
<p>Identify new sales avenues/areas in order to fulfill revenue objectives</p>
<p>Maintain a high-profile Cisco presence in the market place through the creation of strong solutions with customers that turn a sales relationship into a long-term commercial partnership.</p>
<p>Control costs to ensure that expenditure is being handled in accordance with budgets.</p>
<p>Develop and deliver accurate sales forecasts.</p>
<p>Hit defined targets and contributing to overall profitability, success and positive image of Cisco in the marketplace.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who You'll Work With</b></h2>
<p>Cisco Systems is one of the most creative companies in the high-technology industry. We hire highly talented individuals who will contribute to Cisco’s leadership in delivering solutions that help customers achieve their business goals</p>
<p>This is an opportunity-based position, driving the full Cisco Product and Software portfolio; with specific focus on services growth (subscription portfolio, eg: optimization of services, technical services automation). You will participate in developing the competitive strategy for the accounts, in line with the HQ account leader, and orchestrating extended team capabilities including, systems engineering, architectural specialists, services specialists, legal and finance.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who You Are</b></h2>
<p>You will be able to work within an extended team to execute against a sales strategy, lead large complex deals and position the business value of IT solutions to CIO’s and business leaders within your account(s)</p>
<p>BS / BA or equivalent preferred</p>
<p>Formal training in sales techniques preferable</p>
<p>Consistent record of successful solution selling</p>
<p>Demonstrable experience selling complex solutions to executive management.</p>
<p>Ability to network within a customer organization to identify all key influencers and decision makers.</p>
<p>Ability to work well with team members and collaborate effectively across the extended account team or teams.</p>
<p>Previous experience of the Enterprise segment is helpful</p>
<p>Displays Commercial Focus, Negotiation Skills, Problem Solving, Critical Thinking and Decision-making Business English, other languages may be an advantage.</p>
