<p><b>
Function Overview</b></p>
<p>
Whirlpool is seeking qualified candidates for a <b>Continuous Improvement (CI) Quality Lead (Electronics and Internet of Things (IoT)</b> in Benton Harbor, MI to join our Global Electronics Quality organization. As a CI Quality Lead on our team, you will have the responsibility to create actionable insights and support the tactics to achieve the quality targets related to Electronics and IoT customer issues. You will collaborate with a global cross-functional team to develop detailed plans and actions to improve the quality of our products, while ensuring that lessons learned are captured for reuse by future projects. This role provides an excellent opportunity to be part of a dedicated and passionate team while working in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze data from service calls to define paths to quality improvement targets.</li>
<li>
Align with Product Quality Leads, Marketing, and Engineering to identify customer pain points and create plans to contain and correct issues on existing products.</li>
<li>
Document and maintain Lessons Learned to support Continuous Improvement and prevention of repeating failure modes on future products.</li>
<li>
Communicate pipeline of upcoming design changes to different stakeholders, including those throughout Manufacturing.</li>
<li>
Support Operations and Logistics with phase-in/phase-out plans of material during a design change.</li>
</ul>
