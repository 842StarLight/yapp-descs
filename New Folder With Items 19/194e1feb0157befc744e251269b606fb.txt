<h2>Trainer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p>Position Description
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Conducts and presents training programs and materials for company products and customers. Maintains and improves the classroom and workshop facilities for training programs.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Job Responsibilities
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Presents training material, handouts, teaching aids and new curricula.</li>
<li>
Prepares and improves classroom or workshop facilities for programs.</li>
<li>
Works with Customer Education Department to develop on-line training courses for products.</li>
<li>
Works with marketing and internal stakeholders with technical information for development of support materials</li>
<li>
Attends and participates at sales meetings, trade shows and speciality dealer/customer meetings as needed.</li>
<li>
May assist technical publications with development of repair instructions, owner manuals and service troubleshooting guides for products as needed.</li>
<li>
Makes field visits as required to help assist with issues.</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Job Skills Requirements
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Practical and theoretical knowledge of company products</li>
<li>
Project Management skills</li>
<li>
Ability to present and maintain a positive, professional image</li>
<li>
Solid presentations skills</li>
<li>
Proficient use of applicable software, especially MS Office Suite</li>
<li>
Strong oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to demonstrate leadership in meeting customer driven training needs and achieving results</li>
<li>
Fluent in English and primary language used in area of responsibility and/or location</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Education
</p>
<p></p>
<p>High School diploma
</p>
<p>General Education Degree
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Experience Requirements
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum three years of experience in outdoor power equipment industry and working with engines and equipment.</li>
<li>
Minimum three years of experience in training and/or public speaking</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Physical &amp; Environmental Requirements
</p>
<p></p>
<p>May require travel of a planned or urgent nature, both international and domestic<br>
</p>
