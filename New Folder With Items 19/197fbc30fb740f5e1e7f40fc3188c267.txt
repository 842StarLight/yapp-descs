Responibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Build and promote the Kirkwood Community through positive relations with residents and employees.</li>
<li>
Handle all resident issues with diplomacy, tact, neutrality, fairness, and confidentiality.</li>
<li>
Keep supervisor informed of all incidents.</li>
<li>
Introduce yourself to licensees within the first two weeks of their being checked in and continue to follow up with them throughout the season.</li>
<li>
Be alert to the needs of Licensees and make referrals when necessary to external services.</li>
<li>
Participate in team meeting with ideas for employee events and engagement.</li>
<li>
Ensure bulletin boards in all buildings are maintained, well decorated and kept up to date.</li>
<li>
Uphold and follow through with all Kirkwood License Agreement standards and rules.</li>
<li>
Assist Kirkwood housing staff in the office, cleaning, maintenance and snow removal as required.</li>
