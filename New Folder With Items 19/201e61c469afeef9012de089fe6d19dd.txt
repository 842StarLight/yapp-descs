<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The (Sr.) Director of Sales Operations will report to the CSO and will be critical in driving the organization toward achievement of strategic goals, operational excellence, and achievement of sales targets. You will be a key member of the sales leadership team, providing insights and analysis to guide our overall direction ensuring business plans that support rapid revenue growth and scalability of our operations. Our ideal candidate possesses a depth of experience building and leveraging data to enable rapid growth for SaaS companies.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>Planning</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and measure key inputs vital in developing Sales team goals, plans, policies and improvement opportunities</li>
<li>Work closely with the CSO and the executive team to translate strategy into reality</li>
<li>Strategic territory and account assignment strategy leveraging the latest techniques</li>
<li>Lead sales team annual compensation and incentive planning to drive high performance</li>
<li>Lead sales headcount and quota coverage planning</li>
</ul>
<p>Process, Systems, &amp; Tools Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Build and implement sales processes to maximize sales productivity and operational efficiency</li>
<li>Administer, synchronize and optimize technology stack including CRM</li>
</ul>
<p>Reporting and Analytics</p>
<ul>
<li>Full ownership of the bookings forecasting methodology and process</li>
<li>Diagnose and Improve existing sales funnel and pipeline management.</li>
<li>Together with Marketing and Account Development Teams Develop strategies to increase the flow of qualified leads in support of our sales goals</li>
<li>Coordinate with Finance, Marketing and other departments to provide meaningful reporting, metrics and analyses that deliver key business insights</li>
<br>
</ul>
</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>10 years of related experience (i.e. sales operations, finance, BI, sales, or general operations)</li>
<li>Degree in Business or related field from a four-year college or university: MBA preferred.</li>
<li>Strategic thinker with the ability to glean insights from processes, data, sales team feedback, industry best practices, continuous personal development, etc. to develop improved strategies</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience, knowledge and expertise within SaaS / Enterprise Software. Startup experience a plus.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>A strong track record of analytical and diagnostic skills</li>
<li>The curiosity to accomplish more and help the organization succeed in our sales goals.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong acumen and experience with both highly transactional, inbound, direct sales models, and mid-market/Enterprise inside and field sales models.</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of Salesforce.com and other sales productivity tools, including best practices for use, integrations, reporting.</li>
<li>Proven record of developing and implementing tactical initiatives that improve sales productivity, conversion rates, and sales team performance</li>
<li>Strong project and program management skills.
</li>
<li>Intimate knowledge of sales forecasting, pipeline analysis, lead routing and follow-up best practices, outbound lead generation, pricing, contracting, RFPs, proposals, and reporting/analytics</li>
</ul>
