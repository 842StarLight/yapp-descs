<h2>Associate Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>General Description</p>
<p>
A high performing Associate Project Manager will possess strong communication, technical, and project management skills to manage the deployment of a2z products and services to a portfolio of our entry-level clients. A strong orientation toward collaboration and teamwork is also required in-order-to work within the Professional Services department and across other departments, such as Tech and Sales &amp; Account Management, to meet or exceed client expectations.</p>
<div>
High-level responsibilities of this position include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Provide world-class support to a dynamic portfolio of a2z clients through the entire project life cycle</li>
<li>Coordinate with other business functions to ensure world-class customer service and in support of a2z’s business objectives</li>
<li>Develop and demonstrate expertise in the proper implementation of a2z products and services, and apply that expertise to support clients, Product Support Specialists, and other Associate Project Managers</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p>
This person will work closely with other Project Managers, the Director of Project Management and the Vice President of Professional Services to help ensure the success of the Professional Services team.</p>
<br>
<p>
Preferred Background/Experience</p>
<div>
Education<br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum B.S. degree in a technical discipline - preferably Computer Science, Engineering or Mathematics</li>
<li>PMI certification desirable but not required</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
Knowledge and Skills<br>
<ul>
<li>Customer facing communication, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Detail oriented and precise to ensure that a2z products are configured and deployed to client specifications</li>
<li>Highly organized and efficient – able to work on multiple client projects concurrently</li>
<li>Creative problem solving</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills (e.g., active listening, giving/receiving feedback, expectation setting, etc.)</li>
<li>Technical and business requirements analysis and writing</li>
<li>Proficient with HTML, CSS, Javascript, Relational Databases and Web Applications technology. Knowledge of AutoCAD is desirable</li>
<li>Knowledge of Smartsheet and Salesforce CRM is desirable</li>
<li>Knowledge of trade show industry and/or professional associations expected</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
Experience<br>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years of Project Management experience, preferably in a software, professional services, and/or trade show setting. Candidates with less experience but exceptional skills will be considered.</li>
<li>Experience with exposition management and marketing software is desirable</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<p>
<br>
Position Responsibilities</p>
<br>
<div>
Responsibilities to the Client:<br>
Associate Project Managers are expected to lead the deployment of a2z products and services for a diverse pool of clients, including:<br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain detailed project plans (using a2z’s standard systems and tools) that are aligned to clients’ trade show event cycle</li>
<li>Set up and configure all aspects of clients’ system</li>
<li>Coordinate and lead project team meetings</li>
<li>Liaise with clients to understand their needs and secure appropriate project buy-in/sign-off</li>
<li>Identify and lead cross functional project resources and manage their deliverables</li>
<li>Recommend functional solutions to clients and end users in support of their projects’ key objectives</li>
<li>Track progress of testing and participate in business acceptance testing</li>
<li>Keep projects on schedule, ensuring milestones are met, and changes in schedule or project scope are documented, communicated, and approved by all key stakeholders</li>
<li>Develop subject matter expertise related to product on both a technical and business level</li>
<li>Address customer service issues with affected clients</li>
<li>Work to deploy systems starting from requirements analysis and continuing through the first event/project cycle and beyond</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p>
Supervisory Responsibilities:<br>
Not a supervisory role, but Associate Project Managers are expected to work collaboratively with other Project Managers under the direction of the Director of Project Management</p>
<br>
<p>
Physical Demands</p>
<p>
The physical demands of this position are in keeping with general office functions: sitting at a desk, navigating an office environment, using a computer keyboard, using a telephone, etc. Some travel (less than 20%) is required of this position. Some lifting may be required; all packages will be less than 50 lbs.</p>
<br>
<p>
Work Environment</p>
<p>
The office is located on the 4th floor of an office building, requiring the use of an interior elevator or stairs. The full office area consists of both cubicles and private offices. Being located near Lake Kittamaqundi and the Mall in Columbia allows for easy access to walking areas for breaks. There is public transportation from limited areas (Howard Transit as well as local Baltimore/Washington Commuter buses.)</p>
<p>
Telecommuting is an option for qualified candidates not located proximate to the company’s office in Columbia, MD</p>
