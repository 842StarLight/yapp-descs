<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
As a business advisor and individual contributor, this role will support AEG Present's global operations, and will work closely with the operations, legal, and finance teams, and interact frequently with external auditors to establish clear, concise, and timely financial reporting. This role will analyze, document and communicate financial implications for new business acquisitions, new accounting pronouncements, etc., throughout the organization in accordance with the relevant Accounting Standards Codification (ASC's).
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Update and create accounting policies and memos on business combinations, new accounting pronouncements, variable interest entity consolidations, and other ad-hoc complex events.</li>
<li>Lead accounting operational and ASC compliance training efforts at newly acquired and consolidated entities.</li>
<li>Prepare non-controlling interest schedules for presentation to business partners.</li>
<li>Monitor new GAAP and SEC releases and help define and implement necessary processes and reporting changes.</li>
<li>Communicate the impact of changes in accounting requirements and provide necessary training to all finance &amp; accounting personnel.</li>
<li>Work closely with operations, legal, corporate/regional finance and accounting groups to address day-to-day business activities and associated accounting implications.</li>
<li>Source transaction specific accounting guidance via the FASB website and available publications from the SEC / public accounting firms online related to various tax matters such as sales tax, withholding tax, and document finding in business partner's K-1 reports.</li>
<li>Participate in ad hoc projects requiring specialized accounting and analysis.</li>
<li>Collaborate with external auditors on technical accounting topics, new accounting pronouncements, and its applications to ensure all transactions continued to be recorded in accordance with U.S. GAAP.</li>
</ul>
