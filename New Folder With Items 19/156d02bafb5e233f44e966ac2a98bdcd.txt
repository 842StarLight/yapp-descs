<h2>Entrepreneurial COO</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Market-leading internet-based fine jewelry retailer located in West Los Angeles, CA is seeking a seasoned entrepreneurial COO to take the company to the next level.</p>
<p>If you have a “whatever it takes” work ethic, thrive on clarity and accountability, and if you’re an empathetic but firm leader and are ready to “act like the owner,” there is a generous compensation and “write your own ticket” opportunity waiting for you.</p>
<p>Reporting directly to the CEO/Founder, this position provides the day-to-day leadership from the ground up and is responsible for achieving sales and profitability goals, optimizing operational capabilities, and increasing both customer satisfaction and retention.</p>
<p>Significant leadership experience in an e-commerce environment selling products direct to consumer is a requirement. Luxury product experience is highly preferred.</p>
<p>If you have a proven track record, the demonstrable experience, motivation, and demeanor to collaborate on and lead the growth strategy, this will be a career-changing opportunity.</p>
<p>Our early vision of the scope of this position includes but is not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate on and lead strategy development and execution</li>
<li>Lead ongoing cultural evolution</li>
<li>Collaborate with Founder/CEO, finance, and sourcing team to prepare, maintain, and meet budget</li>
<li>Prepare timely and accurate financial performance reports</li>
<li>Review, develop, and implement better business practices</li>
<li>Spearhead eCommerce, social media, and advertising strategies</li>
<li>Establish and maintain KPIs and accountability for each department/position</li>
<li>Manage/coach staff to achieve mutually agreed upon goals</li>
<li>Monitor money handling procedures, accounting, and bank processes</li>
<li>Attract, recruit/develop, and retain high-performing operations team</li>
<li>Maintain professional work environment and showroom</li>
<li>Manage work schedules, employee work load balance, and operational capacity across company</li>
</ul>
<p>If you believe you’re the one for this position, please complete the preliminary questions and requests that are part of the application process and provide your complete resume/CV along with a brief cover letter. The hiring company name will be provided to those who move past the first step in the interview process.</p>
<p>Please only apply if you meet the above qualifications. Please provide in either your resume or cover letter a brief sentence or two for each position on your work history describing the type of products/services sold and audience/selling method (b2b, b2c, ecommerce, brick &amp; mortar, call center, etc.). Thank you!</p>
