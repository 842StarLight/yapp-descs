<h2>Escrow Manager - TRI Pointe Assurance VA/MD</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Are you interested in joining an infectious company culture where accountability, top performance and teamwork are valued and rewarded?<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>TRI Pointe Assurance is looking for an experienced, detail orientated, problem solving individual to join our talented group as an Escrow Manager in our Maryland office.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Position Highlights: </b>Escrow Manager is responsible for establishing and overseeing efficient closing/settlement services, development of internal and external customer relationships, preparing, reviewing and maintaining branch budgets, and managing, developing and coaching branch staff.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure daily branch operations are conducted in accordance with operations standards</li>
<li>Establish and monitor processes for accurate and timely processing of title, scheduling, closing preparation, closing and post-closing</li>
<li>Coordinate with business partners to ensure smooth transitions and support between land, construction, sales, accounting and mortgage</li>
<li>Ensure quality customer service. Serve as point of contact for customer questions and concerns as needed</li>
<li>Monitor, review and respond to internal and state escrow and accounting audits</li>
<li>Ensure proper handling of all funds and closings</li>
<li>Prepare and adhere to departmental budgets with focus on expense reduction and maximizing revenue</li>
<li>Perform as a team member in allocating and coordinating the work flow</li>
<li>Maintain thorough and current knowledge of all federal, state, and local regulations as well as internal policies and procedures</li>
<li>Ensure that all security procedures are followed, branch is operationally sound, and satisfactory audits are achieved</li>
<li>Identify&amp; mitigate operational risk and to ensure ongoing adherence with compliance procedures</li>
<li>Ensure appropriate staffing to meet department needs</li>
<li>Utilize recruiting and selection tools/processes to build organizational talent</li>
<li>Delegate work according to employee’s abilities and skills</li>
<li>Evaluate employee performance and plan for compensation actions in accordance with that performance</li>
<li>Provides developmental opportunities through identification of internal and external training opportunities</li>
<li>Provides continuous coaching regarding functional and leadership as well as technical skills and behaviors</li>
<li>Maintain staff schedules to provide adequate coverage at all times</li>
<li>Facilitate regular employee meetings to discuss goals, disseminate company information, discuss operational issues, etc.</li>
<li>Supervise, coach, and develop staff regarding service expectations, policies, procedures, products, systems and banking transactions</li>
<li>Work with team members to resolve minor disciplinary issues via verbal counseling, and refer larger issues to the VP, Title and Escrow Services and HR</li>
<li>Other duties and projects as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Position Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent required</li>
<li>Notary Public required</li>
<li>Maryland Title License required</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years escrow operations/management experience required</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years at a supervisory level or above required</li>
<li>Ability to type 40+ WPM</li>
<li>Strong computer skills with working knowledge of Microsoft Office and title software applications</li>
<li>Specialize in RESPA with understanding of company's underwriting guidelines</li>
<li>Understanding of title and settlement rules and regulations</li>
<li>Effective communication, presentation, and organization skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
 </p>
<p><b>Company Highlights:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>TRI Pointe Assurance is a member of TRI Pointe Group® (NYSE: TPH), a family of premium regional homebuilders.</li>
<li>TRI Pointe Assurance provides title related services for TRI Pointe Group.</li>
<li>TRI Pointe Assurance benefits home buyers by offering a seamless buying and closing process.</li>
<br>
</ul>
