<h2>Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>We’re looking for an experienced Sales Executive to join our team. In this position, you will be responsible for all sales activities, from new lead generation through close of sale, in a nationwide territory. In addition, you will work with the VP of Marketing and Sales to generate new revenue and to achieve long-term business goals in line with AccountSight’s vision and values.</p>
<p>
To be successful in this position, you must be self-motivated, high-energy and competitive, with established relationships that can be leveraged to gather qualifying information, to uncover needs, and to generate sales.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul><li>Generating leads by contacting prospective clients by telephone (cold-call/inbound), onsite visits, referral partnerships, networking, and industry events</li></ul>
<ul><li>Displaying and/or demonstrating product value and benefit by contacting prospective clients by phone and Internet</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working independently within established guidelines towards building client relationships and closing sales cycles</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<p>
Aggressively prospect into corporations across the country</p>
<ul><li>Heavy B2B prospecting by telephone and email</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead generation for AccountSight products and services</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prospect/sales opportunity qualification</li></ul>
<ul><li>Handling and overcoming objections</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure excellent service and support to prospects and customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain weekly sales forecast and achieve quota</li></ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<ul><li>Excellent prospecting, communication, and written skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong listening and presentation skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>2+ years in software sales with proven history of success closing business</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<p>
SaaS experience a plus • Self-Starter / High Energy / Positive Attitude / Customer-Focused</p>
<ul><li>Experience in referral partner relationships, generating new business and building a customer base</li></ul>
<ul><li>Self-motivated to make a large number of phone calls per day</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to develop effective approaches to convert sales leads to opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track record of over-achieving quota (top 10-20% of company) in past positions</li></ul>
