<h2>Digital Content Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About Skyword</b><br>
Founded in 2010, Skyword empowers today's top brands to create unique content experiences that deliver customer value and drive business success. With its SaaS+ model, Skyword offers: a SaaS-based enterprise-level content marketing platform, industry-leading services, and a global community of storytellers. Today's leading brands have created and published more than one million original stories as articles, videos, images, infographics, podcasts, and animations on Skyword360. These stories have earned Skyword customers more than two billion visits organically, and Skyword has paid its freelance creatives more than $30 million on behalf of its customers. Skyword is a privately held company headquartered in Boston, MA. The company's technology center is located in Pittsburgh, PA. Current investors include Rho Acceleration, Cox Media Group, Allen &amp; Company, Progress Ventures, and American Public Media Group.The Professional Services Manager will work with their Associate Director to manage the content strategy and day-to-day execution for several client programs.</p>
<p>
The Content Manager owns all content ideation, keyword strategy and reporting, and provides support for client calls and meetings. All client programs are managed using the Skyword Platform. This is not a sales role! We need Marketing Managers to ensure our clients goals, targets, and message are meeting their goals and being heard by their community.</p>
<p>
This role is located in downtown Boston, hiring preference will be given to candidates in that market. Relocation assistance is not available for this role.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Primary Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Support clients with topic ideation through original research and data analysis</li>
<li>
Use keyword planning tools such as SEMrush and Google Keyword planner to conduct keyword research that aligns with client strategy</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Editorial Managers to ensure assignments are accurate prior to posting.</li>
<li>
Post assignments for writers in Skyword360</li>
<li>
Ensure assignments posted to the Skyword platform are claimed in a timely manner, and that all necessary content is submitted by the specified deadline.</li>
<li>
Support the Editorial Manager to reopen expired assignments and extend deadlines as needed.</li>
<li>
Gather and organize resources and materials as needed from clients to support content creation efforts.</li>
<li>
Update status documents and call agendas prior to Director and Account Manager client interactions.</li>
<li>
Support reporting needs that are agreed upon as part of contract.</li>
<li>
Identify trends and insights that demonstrate value and can be shared by the Account Manager to nurture relationship and uncover new opportunities.</li>
<li>
Join client calls as needed to provide the Account Manager, Director and Associate Director with support.</li>
<li>
Work with the Customer Success team to set up client programs using Skyword360 and provide clients with ongoing training, assistance and support</li>
<li>
Work with Editorial Managers to ensure contributors are properly trained on the content guidelines for their client programs</li>
<li>
Ensure delivery of monthly client program goals</li>
</ul>
