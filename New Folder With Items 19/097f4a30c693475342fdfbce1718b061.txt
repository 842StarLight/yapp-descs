<p><b>
Job Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
The Digital Commerce Media Manager is an essential role on the US MWC dCommerce Marketing team. The key responsibility of the Digital Commerce Media Manager is to “future proof” the Mars Wrigley business by understanding how consumers shop for confectionary products online, and developing strategies leveraging digital media and emerging capabilities to drive online sales. This role needs a passion for marketing combined with a passion &amp; curiosity for digital media and the role it plays in future-proofing our business.
</p>
<p>A key responsibility will be bringing the digital media strategy to life, through a ‘One Demand’ mindset and approach, to drive Reach, Awareness, and Conversion of the MWC portfolio online. The Digital Commerce Media Manager will champion the online consumer &amp; shopper to understand and interrupt the online path to purchase by driving reach / penetration, trial, and conversion for the Mars Wrigley Confectionery portfolio of products. The role will require excellence in cross-functional team collaboration, both locally and with global. In addition, the right person will possess an entrepreneurial mind-set, be comfortable with ambiguity and ready to drive change with a collaborative approach and a high level of business acumen.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop an approach to Test &amp; Learns in the digital media space to drive conversion / direct response / retargeting with consumers shopping online across Amazon, Click &amp; Collect customers, and new business models (InstaCart, Shipt, Google Express, etc.)</li>
<li>
Partner with Consumer &amp; Shopper Insights to consolidate and share out learnings and best practices with the Shopper Marketing and Global organizations, developing a playbook of opportunities, results, and best practices to help drive best-in-class execution and accelerate online sales.</li>
<li>
Partner with brand and shopper teams to identify, develop, test, and recommend digital commerce media activations that span brand and customer teams to test and scale new digital media approaches to reach consumers at every touchpoint of the digital &amp; omni-channel path to purchase (including direct ownership for activations with InstaCart, Shipt, MyWebGrocer, etc.).</li>
<li>
Develop and execute a syndicated paid search strategy across priority Brick &amp; Click customers. Continually review paid search results across e-retailers (PurePlay and Brick &amp; Click) and make recommendations to optimize paid search spend based on ROI.</li>
<li>
Keep pulse on digital commerce capabilities landscape by exploring, testing, and scaling new tools and technologies that will enable brand and customer touchpoints with the shopper (Artificial Intelligence, Voice, SmartCommerce, etc.)</li>
<li>
Partner with US Media, Brand, and Global teams to define and embed digital commerce into a new MWC digital ecosystem.</li>
<li>
Partner with the US Media and Social media teams to integrate digital commerce planning into annual brand and media planning processes, to help inform and influence media plans.</li>
<li>
Build digital / reach plus acumen by staying up to date with digital marketing trends &amp; new channels, and educating our brand partners and our retailers.</li>
<li>
Work with internal procurement to scope digital commerce projects and determine fees. Responsible for digital commerce media budget management.</li>
</ul>
