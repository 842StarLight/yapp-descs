<h2>Account Services Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>thunder::tech is seeking an Account Services Lead to join our Account Services Department (ASD). The ASD provides account management and planning, strategy services, project coordination, media buying and planning as well as business development services. As a truly integrated marketing agency, this department drives our business as we can be an account-driven agency for clients.<br>
<br>
An Account Services Lead is a curious person that is hungry to build new relationships, deepen existing ones and solve problems like no one else. thunder::tech is not a traditional agency and you’re not a traditional marketer. We value the creative solutions you have created and relationships you have developed in the past, not the shiny brands you may have rubbed elbows with. If having access to lots of crazy talent to dream up solutions to invent the future everyday sounds interesting – keep reading.<br>
<br>
Account Services Leads are proactive and eager to prospect for new opportunities including generating business from potential clients as well as managing and growing lasting client relationships. Account Services Lead are responsible for developing solutions and educating existing and potential clients, networking, and managing targeted outreach within the community. This position requires a motivated, organized and strategic thinker who is proactive and lives to solve problems and connect-the-dots for clients.<br>
