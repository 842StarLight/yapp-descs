<p><b>Field Clinical Representative- (Rhythm Management)<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>About the role:
</b></p>
<p>Promotes commercially-approved company products through education of current and potential customers, implant and patient clinic coverage within a defined region by providing clinical education, sales strategy and support in order to assist in achieving projected sales goals and increasing sales revenues within product segments. May also support day-to-day field execution and management of Boston Scientific clinical trials at selected clinical trial centers centers in accordance with all applicable SOPs.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your responsibilities will include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serves as primary resource for clinical support in the areas of surgical coverage, basic troubleshooting, programming, and patient follow-up for company products.
</li>
<li>Educates customers on the merits and proper clinical usage of company products by giving presentations and demonstrations using a wide variety of formats and platforms.
</li>
<li>Attends unit implants in the labs and operating room of hospital accounts and performs patient follow-up to assure customer and patient success with the implanted products.
</li>
<li>Develops relationships with hospital personnel (e.g. through casual conversation, meetings, participation in conferences) and to identifies key decision makers in order to facilitate future sales.
</li>
<li>Trains on and maintains knowledge of clinical trial protocols, CRM Clinical Department standard operating procedures, and compliance toward the regulations of world-wide regulatory bodies to meet corporate and departmental objectives.
</li>
<li>Manages clinical trial activity within his/her assigned territory which includes but is not limited to completing Interest Visits and Site Initiation Visits.
</li>
<li>Educates clinical investigators on clinical trial protocols, clinical process, and investigational products and features.
</li>
<li>Responds to customer needs and complaints regarding products and service by developing creative and feasible solutions or working with other related personnel (e.g., sales, clinical research, marketing, technical support) to develop optimal solutions.
</li>
<li>Follows company and divisional guidelines for managing commercial inventory and, if applicable, investigational product inventory.
</li>
<li>On-call duties may be required 24 hours a day, 7 days per week.<br>
</li>
</ul>
