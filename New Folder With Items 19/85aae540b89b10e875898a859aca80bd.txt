<p><b>What You Will Do
</b></p>
<p>The primary responsibilities of the Global Manager, Merchandise Planning, is to support in building the global financial product strategy and execute the product planning across all Global Retail environments.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>How You Will Make A Difference
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>PRODUCT
</p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with global Merchandising Managers on seasonal and long-term strategy for Global Merchandising/segmentation
</li>
<li>Develop analytical framework used to inform strategically segmented assortment strategies for own area of business
</li>
<li>Provide SKU count guidance to Merchandising and RD&amp;D teams using analysis of historical trend and market opportunities for own area of business
</li>
<li>Provide volume line planning guidance to Merchandising and Product Management for own area of business
</li>
<li>Report on SKU count and conduct SKU rationalization exercises using ABC analysis or other quantitative analysis for own area of business
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>OPERATIONS
</p>
<ul>
<li>Partner to provide new initiatives to enhance planning engagement and service to overall business process
</li>
<li>Provide direction for the global demand plan for own area of business that connects sales and product strategies to Ops/Supply Chain
</li>
<li>Partner with Merchandise Operations to develop planning calendar to align Merchandising, Finance, Sales, RD&amp;D, and operations milestones/deliverables
</li>
<li>Partner with regional counterparts in WHS and DTC to develop global merchandising plans
</li>
<li>Own handoffs of seasonal merchandising financial plans to the operations team, briefing big ideas and key brand initiatives
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>FINANCIALS/METRICS
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop seasonal revenue targets by channel and category that align with financial and brand strategies to be aligned on with ELG pre- and during seasonal planning for own area of business
</li>
<li>Partner with Merchandising managers to achieve global revenue &amp; margin through collaboration with finance, sales, and product teams
</li>
<li>Ensure Global Merchandising plans by channel and category aligns with financial and brand strategies
</li>
<li>Align with Product team to deliver on product mix that delivers margin targets
</li>
<li>Partner with Supply Planning, ensuring forecasts are connected to strategic focuses
</li>
</ul>
