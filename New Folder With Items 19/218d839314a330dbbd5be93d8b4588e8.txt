<h2>Experienced Franchise Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Alliance Franchise Brands, LLC - World Leader in Marketing and Visual Communications is adding to our Franchise Development team to focus on our RSVP Offers for Life and Home franchise concept.</p>
<p>The Franchise Development Manager will lead franchise new growth throughout the U.S. conduct effective calls on a daily basis, think strategically, build relationships, communicate effectively across a wide range of clients and execute with a high level of precision. The entry to this franchise is perfect for a professional seeking to own a well-respected business and is driven to work with high-end companies to spotlight their businesses through targeted marketing.</p>
<p>The role reports to the Vice President of Franchise Development.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Successfully achieve the goal awarding 10+ franchises on an annual basis</li>
<li>Work with VP Franchise Development on strategic initiatives and goals</li>
<li>Conduct a series of daily calls, on average 100+ to prospects</li>
<li>Develop benchmark criteria to measure efficiency and effectiveness of sales programs and activities</li>
<li>Coordinate new lead generation activities (email blasts, webinars, etc.)</li>
<li>Measure lead generation activities to track success, ROI</li>
<li>Follow-up with all prospect lead flows; brokers, internet, franchise member referrals</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the primary point of contact with qualified leads, and brokers</li>
<li>Represent the brand professionally, honestly, knowledgeably</li>
<li>Attend broker conferences and other targeted trade show events</li>
<li>Organize and host regularly scheduled Discovery Day events for prospects</li>
<li>Attend relevant meetings, onsite, webinar, etc.</li>
<li>Execute sales objectives with regular tracking and by providing progress reports</li>
<li>Work with legal, new member training, operations and administration to onboard new franchisees into the system</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Travel may be required from time to time</li></ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>7-10 years of franchise sales</li>
<li>Committed to results, proven history of accomplishing strategic sales goals</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to review and interpret financial and other legal documents</li>
<li>Process-oriented, disciplined to ensure process is navigated appropriately by all involved</li>
<li>Understanding of FDD and related franchise law</li>
<li>Ability to present and engage with Executive Team and other department heads</li>
<li>Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Successful sales record</li>
</ul>
