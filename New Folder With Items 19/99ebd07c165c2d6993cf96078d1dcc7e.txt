<h2>Enterprise Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
This position is responsible for support of and selling solutions to large enterprise-class clients in the Pacific Northwest region, or as required to provide consistent coverage for clients. The employee will work with potential and existing customers to determine their business challenges, articulate the business value of CBT’s solutions, manage opportunities in the sales pipeline, and ultimately increase revenue in assigned regions. The employee will maintain strong relationships with customers, OEM/Partner teams, and internal resources and manage the client relationship, especially through the delivery of solutions.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify, qualify, and manage new business opportunities to grow account revenue.</li>
<li>Develop new customer relationships and manage all facets of their accounts, including sales, joint solution development, contract negotiations and regular business reviews</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales Management and Marketing to create and implement a business plan that includes increasing market awareness, lead generation, pipeline management and goal achievement</li>
<li>Develop new customer relationships and manage all facets of their accounts to include solution sales, joint solution development, contract negotiations and conducting regular business reviews</li>
<li>Provide excellent customer service through effective communication, response follow-through, and advocacy for client issues within internal departments.</li>
<li>Track opportunities and sales activity and build contacts and assets information in SFDC.</li>
<li>Complete other duties as they may be assigned by Management.</li>
<li>Support the philosophy, goals, and objectives of the organization.</li>
<li>Adhere to safety policies and procedures in performing job duties and responsibilities.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Education:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor degree or 3-5 years of equivalent experience</li>
</ul>
<b>Skills and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Advocacy minded, with a solution seeking approach to customer’s issues</li>
<li>A minimum of 5 years of experience in solution-oriented sales</li>
<li>Understanding of competitive technologies and markets</li>
<li>Experienced in industry verticals such as, but not limited to healthcare, finance, oil &amp; gas, utilities, manufacturing</li>
<li>Ability to speak effectively before groups of customers, partners, or employees of CBT.</li>
<li>Able to work independently, be organized and have ability to multi-task.</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite and SalesForce.</li>
</ul>
<b>Other Information:</b> The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. While performing the duties of this job, the employee is frequently required to stand and to walk. The employee is frequently required to sit for long periods of time. The employee is frequently required to use hands to finger, handle, or feel. The employee is regularly required to talk or hear.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Employee must possess good communication, listening and interpersonal skills and;</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license and vehicle</li>
</ul>
