<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<ul>
<li>Develop/ provide input to growth strategy focused both on financial gain and customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Coordinate efforts of engineering sales team (seller/doers) by obtaining their input and developing a cohesive business development strategy.</li>
<li>Conduct research to identify new markets and customer needs.</li>
<li>Lead weekly business development meeting.</li>
<li>Promote the company’s services addressing or predicting clients’ objectives.</li>
<li>Follow industry trends locally and internationally.</li>
<li>Manage and improve relationships with existing clients.</li>
<li>Arrange business meetings with prospective clients.</li>
<li>Keep records of sales, revenue, invoices etc.</li>
<li>Provide trustworthy feedback and after-sales support.</li>
<li>Identify new markets for company business growth.</li>
<li>Identify new products for business growth.</li>
<li>Identify additional methods to penetrate existing customers.</li>
<li>Increase client base.</li>
<li>Develop in-depth knowledge of business products and value proposition.</li>
<li>Write business proposals.</li>
<li>Negotiate with stakeholders.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<br>
Required Experience<br>
<ul>
<li>BSc/BA in Business Administration, sales or relevant field highly desirable.</li>
<li>Proven experience as a business development manager, sales executive, or relevant role <b><i>within engineering consulting services</i></b>
</li>
<li>Experience with design and implementation of business development strategy</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (written, verbal, non-verbal)</li>
<li>Ability to build rapport with other employees and customers</li>
<li>Self-motivate with ability to motivate a team</li>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office and CRM software (e.g. Dynamics, Salesforce)</li>
<li>Time management and planning skills</li>
<li>Proven ability to negotiate</li>
<li>Conflict resolution</li>
<li>Proven sales track record; experienced working to and exceeding sales targets.</li>
</ul>
