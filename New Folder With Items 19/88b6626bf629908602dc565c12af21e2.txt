<h2>Digital Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<li>Analytics: tracking, monitoring and optimizing campaigns based on results</li>
<li>Content creation: using tools like Canva, Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Writing social media and website copy</li></ul>
<p>We are looking for a highly motivated self starter who is passionate about digital marketing, knows how to get results, and wants the opportunity to work in a thriving, growing company.</p>

<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
