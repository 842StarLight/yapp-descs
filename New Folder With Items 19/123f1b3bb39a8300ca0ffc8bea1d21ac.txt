<h2>Salesforce.com Functional Consultant - Customer Insights & Growth</h2>

<div>Accenture is a leading global professional services company, providing a broad range of services and solutions in strategy, consulting, digital, technology and operations. Combining unmatched experience and specialized skills across more than 40 industries and all business functions – underpinned by the world’s largest delivery network – Accenture works at the intersection of business and technology to help clients improve their performance and create sustainable value for their stakeholders. With approximately 469,000 people serving clients in more than 120 countries, Accenture drives innovation to improve the way the world works and lives.<br>

As <b>a Salesforce Functional Consultant</b> in our <b>Customer Insights &amp; Growth Management Consulting</b> practice, you’ll often be working with your team lead to solve key technology strategy challenges of clients by enabling Sales, Commercial Trade Promotion Management, Marketing Customer Service transformation, painting a picture of, and charting a journey from the current state to a to-be enterprise environment enabled via Salesforce platform.<br>
<br>
<b>As a Salesforce Functional Consultant, you’ll often be:</b>

<ul>
<li>Being involved in a functional capacity to help with the design and implementation of Sales, Commercial, Marketing Customer Service solutions developed on Salesforce.
</li>
<li>Being involved in the process and technology assessments to help to identify vulnerabilities in the current state of our clients and how these can be addressed through Salesforce.com cloud offerings, Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud, Commerce Cloud, Einstein Analytics building and applying industry and functional knowledge to support efforts in selling opportunities to existing and new clients
</li>
<li>Building and applying industry and functional knowledge to support efforts in selling opportunities to existing and new clients
</li>
<li>Developing, utilizing and sharing your skills as a Consulting practitioner to ensure market and client relevance
</li>
<li>Working with your team lead in the design and implementation of seamless and enriched end to end customer service experiences
</li>
<li>Working with your team lead in designing and implementing customer loyalty retention programs, smart service, and marketing traffic generation</li>
</ul>
</div>
