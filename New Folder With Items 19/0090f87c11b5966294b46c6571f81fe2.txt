<h2>SMB Relationship Manager I, North America</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Do you have a passion for driving customer success? Bullhorn, the industry leader in cloud-based CRM and operations solutions for the staffing industry, is looking to grow its team of Relationship Managers. As a Relationship Manager I, your mission is to ensure that your assigned SMB (small &amp; medium business) customers remain committed Bullhorn clients and advocates as they get the most out of their Bullhorn solution. You ‘own’ the customer after onboarding, for as long as they are a Bullhorn client!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>As an Relationship Manager I, a typical day might include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Facilitating check-in meetings with your customers to review their satisfaction and success.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sharing tips for better use of Bullhorn with your customers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Monitoring customer adoption &amp; usage of Bullhorn and suggesting ways the customer can increase value gained.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assisting customers through any challenges they might have getting the most out of Bullhorn, and serving as point-person for escalations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Identifying opportunities for customers to benefit from additional Bullhorn and partner capabilities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Serving as the customer’s post-onboarding point-of-contact for adoption and usage questions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Responding to customer questions/concerns sent to the Bullhorn Customer Care email address for SMB.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Acting as the voice of your customers back to the rest of Bullhorn.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Documenting customer interactions and ensuring prompt follow-up to any open issues.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reporting to Bullhorn management on the status of your assigned customers.</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>This job might be for you if you:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Have a Bachelor’s Degree, preferably in a business discipline.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Have strong problem-solving skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Show demonstrable passion for customer service and success.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Are an excellent communicator.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Are empathetic and positive, even in the face of challenges.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Are comfortable setting and managing expectations with a variety of personality types.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Are highly organized and can juggle a large number of assigned accounts effectively.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Can focus your efforts and work efficiently - you will have many customers and only so many hours in the day!</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Are excited by a fast-paced environment that constantly changes to suit customer need.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Are a quick learner of new technologies.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Are ready to continually grow, stretch, and learn!</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Bonus points for:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Experience in account management, relationship management, customer training, or customer success for a SaaS provider.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience with the recruitment industry is a huge plus!</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Strong culture makes Bullhorn a great place to work! Our offices offer a casual, collaborative environment with weekly catered-in lunch and breakfast, and monthly "First Thursday" social events. Bullhorn offers unlimited vacation, quarterly paid volunteer days through its philanthropic group Bullhorn Cares, and an open invitation to Bullhorn Allies groups, which celebrate and cultivate diversity and inclusion for all employees.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>You might be a fit for Bullhorn if:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You are energetic and enjoy working in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>You are confident and love sharing success with your team</li>
<li>You take ownership of your work and continuously strive for improvement</li>
<li>You love working in an agile environment and can be adaptable to changing priorities</li>
</ul>
