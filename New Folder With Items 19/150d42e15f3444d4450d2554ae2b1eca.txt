<h2>Retail Contract Management Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>the Retail Contract Management Analyst is responsible for creating and closing contracts for the retail sales department. Position includes monitoring multiple work queues and replying with prompt and accurate responses to all contracting requests. Position will also include coordinating with sales, account management, credit, structured transactions and legal for correct language and approvals.
</div>
<div></div>

<li>Create non-standard contract documents
</li>

</li>
<li>Has subject matter expert knowledge to facilitate and lead the negotiation and redline process between Sales, Risk Management and Legal
</li>
<li>Utilizes expert knowledge to assist Senior Analyst on the team with complex structures and negotiations
</li>
<li>Monitor and assess day-to-day related issues to ensure business practices, policies and procedures are executed in an effective manner

<li>Has subject matter expert responsibility for analyzing and developing improvements to business processes
</li>
<li>Actively work with various internal/external teams by providing data expertise, offering original perspectives, and challenging conventional views to better align decision making/perceptions with changing business needs.
</li>
<li>Create and maintain ongoing dialogue with strategic partner(s), internal personnel and TXU Governance to ensure a positive, valued relationship that has unambiguous alignment with business initiatives
</li>
