<p><b>About LenderClose</b>:</p>
<p>
LenderClose is a fast-growing startup modernizing the financial services industry by supercharging loan originators with technology. At our company in West Des Moines, you'll have the opportunity to join a talented team of sales professionals, software engineers and operation specialists. At LenderClose, our focus is to empower community lenders with the solutions they need to compete with corporate lenders.</p>
<p>
Join us if you're self-motivated, highly energetic and love to have fun!</p>
<p><b>
What is an Implementation Specialist?</b></p>
<p>
As an implementation specialist at LenderClose, you hold a critical role in the overall success of our clients. You are on the front lines of helping make sure the transition from prospect to partner is a success. A successful implementation specialist will be someone who is willing to roll up their sleeves and dive in to do whatever is needed to make sure their client has the best possible experience during implementation with our software platform.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage multiple clients through platform on boarding process</li>
<li>Lead engaging and informative product training sessions with customers</li>
<li>Completes follow up calls to ensure customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Become a subject matter expert of LenderClose products</li>
<li>Document customer feedback in CRM system</li>
<li>Thrives in a team environment where effective internal and client facing communication is critical to success</li>
<li>Contributes to team success through accomplishing related tasks as needed</li>
<li>Recommends potential solutions to clients based on needs</li>
</ul>

<li>Confident</li>

</ul>
