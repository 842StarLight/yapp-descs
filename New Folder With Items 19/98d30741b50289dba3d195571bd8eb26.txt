<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Shipping Clerk verifies and keeps records on incoming and outgoing shipments and prepares items for shipment.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Level:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Entry Level</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties/Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Use shipping software and automation equipment.</li>
<li>Stack products on pallets correctly.</li>
<li>Prepare, match and verify shipping information and manifest accurately.</li>
<li>Assist with stretch wrapping, loading trucks and all other tasks directed by Supervisor.</li>
<li>Adhere to customer shipping guidelines and the preparation of shipping information and manifest.</li>
<li>Adhere to strict Shimano’s 5S Principles.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience working in a warehouse/distribution center preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to work and multitask in a fast paced warehouse environment with a focus on customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure and within time constraints is required.</li>
<li>Must be able to properly stretch wrap product on pallets.</li>
<li>Ability to use shipping software, automation equipment and perform task in accordance with Shimano’s<br>
standard operating procedures and customer guidelines.</li>
<li>Must be able to meet and maintain performance standards set by the company.</li>
<li>Good attitude and take initiative. Takes pride in his/her work environment and embraces 5S principles</li>
<li>Good verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by Supervisor.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>High school diploma or GED equivalent</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>WORKING CONDITIONS</b></p>
<p><b>Environment:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Approximately 100% of the time performing job duties is spent indoors in a warehouse with limited heating or air conditioning.</li>
<li>Approximately 75% of the time is spent on the computer.</li>
<li>Noise level in the work environment is usually moderate.</li>
<li>The temperature of the work environment can be equal to the outside temperatures of the area.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Occasionally lift from overhead or below the waist.</li>
<li>Occasionally bend, twist, push, pull, climb, squat, crawl and kneel.</li>
<li>Frequently reach with hands and arms; climb or balance; stand and walk; grasp with hands and fingers; and lift (up to 50 lbs.).</li>
<li>Continuously sit, use hands to finger, handle or feel objects, tools, or controls.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Machines, Tools and Equipment</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Continuously use copier, fax, printers, and 10-key calculator.</li>
<li>Continuously use writing instruments, computer and telephone.</li>
</ul>
