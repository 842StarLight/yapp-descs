<h2>Showing Specialist - Part Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Come join Spaces, one of the largest leasing focused brokerages in Chicago. We’re looking for smart, people-friendly Showing Specialists to join our recently renovated Lincoln Park office. We seek excellent communicators who aren’t afraid to self-direct, problem solve, and work hard.</p>
<p> What You'll Be Doing</p>
<ul>
<li>Show apartments for some of our most important landlord accounts</li>
<li>Directly receive appointments of qualified and confirmed renters so you can focus on being an expert at customer service for our renters</li>
<li>Provide excellent client service, keeping renters engaged and happy in their touring experience and rental process</li>
<li>Gather information from tours and submit feedback to improve the process the next time around</li>
<li>Learn the real estate industry and develop an understanding of how to succeed</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>What We're Looking For</p>
<ul>
<li>Someone eager to learn, flexible, and people-friendly who is passionate about real estate</li>
<li>Part-time availability: weekdays, evenings, and/or weekends</li>
<li>Must be able to drive / travel / move quickly around the city to meet clients for many tours</li>
<li>Have, and/or willing, to obtain your interim leasing license upon employment</li>
<li>Must have an Android / iOS device</li>
<li>Organized and computer savvy, able to keep sales data up-to-date</li>
<li>2-4 year college degree (preferred)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>What You'll Have</p>
<ul>
<li>We will train you fully in our system and have you onboarded quickly and ready to see clients in your first week.</li>
<li>Technology: Our proprietary software captures thousands of unique leads each month. Our in-house concierge team pre-screens and qualifies all incoming traffic at no cost to you. You will receive unrivaled access to our network of prequalified renters.</li>
<li>$50/monthly phone reimbursement</li>
<li>Mileage reimbursement for showing travels</li>
<li>A fun office space with snacks and a fully stocked fridge</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Throughout the years, Spaces has remained dedicated to our employee-centric, friendly, and high-energy company culture that has been nurtured since the company’s inception. From boat parties to happy hours, we work hard and play hard.</p>
