<h2>VP of Finance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>VP, Finance – Reston/Herndon, VA</b><br>
<br>
<b>Company Overview:</b><br>
Our client – a growing, international, private equity-backed online software and technology (SaaS) platform supporting nonprofit and corporate customers, is seeking a Vice President of Finance.<br>
<br>
The VP of Finance should be able to work for a private equity owner who is focused on value creation and long-term growth. With roughly 150 professionals domestically and aboard, this individual will work out of their corporate headquarters located in Fairfax County, VA.<br>
<br>
<b>Role Overview:</b><br>
Reporting directly to the CEO, the VP Finance is expected to be a value-added senior leader to grow the business and drive value creation. The VP of Finance will be expected to drive strategy that optimizes the core elements of the business model, including, forecasting/budgeting, sales quotas, all accounting activities, customer contracts and financial oversight of the Payments division.<br>
<br>
From a financial planning, cash management, operational metrics reporting and compliance reporting perspective, the VP Finance will have responsibility to:
<ul>
<li>Steward planning processes (including annual strategic and operating plans and the budget process)</li>
<li>Ensure accurate, timely monthly reporting both internally and to private equity group (ownership)</li>
<li>Along with Controller, oversee all financial reporting, accounting, controls, treasury, audit and tax functions</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Manage cash flow – treasury reporting, banking relationships</li>
<li>Monitor the day-to-day financial activity to ensure the company is on track to meet its financial goals</li>
<li>Improve and deliver unit-based KPI dashboards and management tools</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop, upgrade and maintain sound financial business controls, policy and processes</li>
<li>KPI/metrics-based approach – orientation towards productivity measurement and improvement</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Oversee Key Performance Indicators (KPI’s) across the entire business model</li>
<li>Re-aligning the Finance organization from a “compliance” to a “value-add” team</li>
<li>Reviewing and reworking processes to drive performance, efficiencies and reporting</li>
<li>clear track record of achievement as a team player leading a finance organization that strongly contributed to organizational change, growth and profitability</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Experience/Credentials:</b>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of hands-on leadership experience in accounting and finance</li>
<li>Exposure to FP&amp;A is preferred</li>
<li>Start-up/turn-around experience preferred</li>
<li>CPA and/or MBA preferred</li>
<li>Should have served as a Director or VP level of Finance within internet/technology/SaaS company or firm</li>
<li>Assess, monitor and improve company culture and lead required change and growth in a collaborative and productive manner</li>
<li>Experience leveraging forward-looking risk mitigation strategies and tactics</li>
<li>Acquisition integration, restructurings / alignment and process improvement experience.</li>
<li>Experience working for a PE or VC-backed company strongly preferred</li>
<li>Experience with NetSuite and/or Salesforce is highly preferred</li>
<li>Command of great accounting practices, IFRS and GAAP; transaction accounting, forensic accounting, purchase accounting, goodwill, etc.</li>
<li>Experience with tax structure, standards and filings</li>
<li>Ensure financial controls, processes, discipline and philosophy</li>
</ul>Provide effective communication, mentorship and professional development within the accounting and finance team
<ul>
<li>Effectively communicate and build productive relationships with company-wide stakeholders</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Compensation:</b><br>
$175,000 - $200,000 base<br>
20% target bonus</div></div>
