<h2>Business Development Manager – North America</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for an ambitious and energetic Business Development Manager to join our North American team and to help us build and increase the position of Ultimaker in several market segments.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Business Development team inspires and support global organizations on how to create value with 3D printing in their manufacturing and design processes.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Would you like to make a difference in a 3D printing cutting edge company? Then, read on!</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What will you be doing?</b></h2>
<p>
You’ll report to the President-Ultimaker North America and to the Head of Global Business Development (located in the Netherlands). Besides you will work closely with colleagues across multiple business units and based in different geographies to coordinate work with customers who have multiple, global locations. Furthermore you will be collaborating closely with:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Channel and key account sales</li>
<li>
Marketing</li>
<li>
Applications engineering</li>
<li>
Product management teams</li>
<li>
Other internal stakeholders</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
The goal in this collaboration is to build business relationships with market leading customers and partners. Your strategic vertical markets will include but not be limited to automotive, aerospace, electronics, industrial equipment, consumer goods, healthcare.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Your focus will be on accelerating early adoption of Ultimaker new and existing products globally across the customer enterprise. You will be influencing and leveraging internal teams and partner resources to ensure customer success before full scale key account sales activities are initiated.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Business development activities will include: lead generation; customer pipeline and sales funnel management; customer engagement presentations, site scans and product, solution and application use case validation projects; strategic partner technology collaboration projects; business modeling, ROI analysis.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Specific responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Helping to develop a growth strategy focused both on financial gain and customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Maintaining and expanding the Ultimaker brand credibility &amp; market position</li>
<li>
Maintaining extensive knowledge of current market conditions conducting research to identify new markets and customer needs, in close conjunction with Market Intelligence</li>
<li>
Arranging business meetings with prospects &amp; clients</li>
<li>
Helping Global customers to improve their design or manufacturing processes with 3D printing, as a valuable Business Consultant for our customers</li>
<li>
Building and defends business cases and management team presentations with stakeholders</li>
<li>
Leading the process from site scan, to pilot project, to global contract</li>
<li>
Creating and leads project teams in order to facilitate the implementation of 3D printing needs of the customer</li>
<li>
Leading the bid process</li>
<li>
Promoting printers’ applications addressing or predicting clients’ needs and objectives</li>
<li>
Initiating pilot projects in line with the need of the customer</li>
<li>
Building long-term relationships with new and existing customers</li>
<li>
Working closely together with other company departments, such as Marketing, Sales, Market Intelligence, Product Management and R&amp;D</li>
<li>
Working in multidisciplinary teams and with different stakeholders in joined projects</li>
<li>
Reporting on sales funnel and project progress</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
More about Ultimaker</b></h2>
<p>
Ultimaker has been in operation since 2011, and over the years has grown to become a market leader, creating powerful, professional and accessible desktop 3D printers. With offices in the Netherlands, New York, and Boston, plus production facilities in both Europe and the US, Ultimaker’s team of over 400 employees continually strives to offer the highest-quality 3D printers, software, and materials on the market.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We offer you the chance to become a part of a young, fast-growing, technically advanced company. We are focused on further developing the Ultimaker, developing the world of 3D printing, and further expanding the skills of our people. Our company has developed a large and loyal customer base from creative makers to companies like BMW or Airbus.<br>
