<h2>Product Information Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>We're looking for geniuses. Dreamers. Inventors. Innovators. People with the will to get stuff done. Doers who'll rip an idea out of thin air. Make it a reality. Own it. And amaze their peers. Sound like you? Welcome home.</b><br>
<br>
We have an opening in the Information Technology Department for a Product Information Coordinator. This person collects, verifies, enters, and maintains product information data to ensure accuracy before downloading into retail systems. This person works as a liaison between retail, information technology, and other departments in order to resolve data issues.<br>
<br>
<b>Working Relationships:</b><br>
<br>
Accountable and Reports to: Director, Loyalty and Product Management<br>
<br>
Positions that Report to you: None<br>
<br>
<b>Primary Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Validates the accuracy of product information from vendor level to store level. This may include, corporate ads, corporate loyalty promotions, vendor costs, ecommerce and other related data that pertains to items within our retail &amp; corporate systems.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Works as a liaison between retail, information technology, marketing and procurement to resolve data issues.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Answers store questions/support calls regarding data downloads and loyalty promotions.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ensure all data fields are accurate and customer friendly.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Setup loyalty promotions and data.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Reports to work when scheduled and on time.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Secondary Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assists department supervisor in directing the work flow of the data information clerks.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Stays current on retail procedures and technology innovations in the area of product information management.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Identifies work flow problems, analyzes calls/projects and suggests improvements for the department.
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Performs other job related duties and special projects as required.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Skills and Abilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must have the ability to solve practical problems; variety of variable with limited standardization; interpret instructions.</li>
<li>Must be able to do arithmetic calculations involving fractions, decimals, and percentages.</li>
<li>Must possess the ability to file, post, and distribute materials; copy data from one record to another; interview to obtain basic information.</li>
<li>Must be able to physically perform sedentary work: exerting up to 10 pounds of force occasionally and/or a negligible amount of force frequently or constantly to lift, carry, push, pull or otherwise move objects including the human body. Sedentary work involves sitting most of the time.</li>
<li>Visual requirements include clarity of vision at 20 inches or less and up to 20 feet with or without correction and color vision.</li>
<li>Must be able to perform the following physical activities: Standing, walking, pushing, lifting, reaching, grasping, talking, hearing, and repetitive motions</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Working Conditions:</b><br>
<br>
The duties for this position are normally conducted in an office environment. There is significant pressure to meet deadlines and handle multiple projects in a day.<br>
<br>
<b>Equipment used to perform job:</b><br>
<br>
Windows based PC, printer, wireless devices, telephone, fax, copier, calculator, internet, product management software, and Microsoft Office products.<br>
<br>
<b>Confidentiality:</b><br>
<br>
Has access to confidential information including store inventory information, item costs, and margins.<br>
<br>
<b>Contacts:</b><br>
<br>
Has daily contact with other departments and retail store personnel.<br>
<br>
<b>Minimum Education required to perform job:</b><br>
<br>
High School diploma or equivalent experience.<br>
<br>
<b>Minimum Experience required to perform job:</b><br>
<br>
Over six months up to one year of similar or related work.<br>
