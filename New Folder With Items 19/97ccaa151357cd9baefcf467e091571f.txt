<h2>Senior-Level Contracts & Pricing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At Boeing, we are all innovators on a mission to connect, protect, explore and inspire. From the sea bed to outer space, you’ll learn and grow, contributing to work that shapes the world. Find your future with us.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
This Contracts Specialist position is providing senior level Contract Management Specialist support to the Government Satellite Systems (GSS) Business Unit in Seal Beach, CA.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This position is a key member of the GSS Contracts Team responsible for leading various Contracting actions to include tasks such as developing and negotiating complex timely and quality proposals leading to the contract administration/execution of these transactions.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Interface with Leaders at all levels of the Enterprise and with external Customers on a regular basis providing contract interpretations/determinations.
</li>
<li>Lead the development of contractual and pricing strategy, funding and expenditure profiles, provide proper disclosures within established company and customer policies, process, procedures and regulations.</li>
<li>
Make firm commitments on behalf of the Enterprise within established Delegation of Authority with Customers for various transactions.
</li>
<li>Primary interfaces will include working with Department of Defense (DoD) and industry prime customers in addition to the Defense Contract Management Agency and Defense Contract Audit Agency.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain effective working relationships with customer counterparts and the next level within the customer organization at a high rate of transaction frequency.</li>
<li>
Develop solutions to complex problems that require a high degree of ingenuity, creativity, and innovation.
</li>
<li>Develop solutions to unique challenges that may serve as precedent for future decisions.</li>
<li>
Operate and practice with a professional level of discretion.
</li>
<li>Provide Contracts guidance/determinations and technical and operational leadership for the program/ business management teams.
</li>
<li>Draft, review and negotiate Proprietary Information Agreements or other Non-Sales Agreements necessary to meet organizational objectives and protect the intellectual and proprietary property of Boeing and its customers.
</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with legal and company requirements.
</li>
<li>Coordinate flow-down and flow-up of prime terms and conditions with supplier management.
</li>
<li>Prepare company funds requests and obtain required management approval.
</li>
<li>Facilitate prompt payment and support achievement of company financial goals and objectives.
</li>
<li>Act as focal for audits as required.
</li>
<li>Serve as prime consultant and external spokesperson for the company on highly significant matters relating to policies, programs, capabilities, and long-range goals and objectives.
</li>
<li>This position requires a keen sense of urgency while maintaining a mindset that promotes leadership, safety, first time quality, working to emphasize speed and agility, honesty and candor.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
This position requires a Current and active Top Secret clearance and SCI Clearance with an active Single Scope Background Investigation (SSBI). An SSBI investigation date within the past 5 years is considered active.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Basic Relocation is available for eligible candidates as authorized.</b></p>

Basic Qualifications (Required Skills/Experience):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
This position requires a Current and active Top Secret and/or SCI Clearance with a current SSBI.
</li>
<li>A minimum of 5 years’ experience in contract formation, proposal development, negotiation, change management and execution/administration of complex USG contracts and/or subcontracts under USG primes.</li>
<li>
Significant experience working with the U.S. Government Federal Acquisition Regulations, agency supplements and contracting processes for proposals and contract execution.</li>
</ul>

</li>

<li>
Ability to advise all levels of management on contractual rights and obligations, risk avoidance and mitigation, strategy, and problem solving is desired.</li>
<li>
Ability to mentor and guide less experienced personnel is desired.</li>
