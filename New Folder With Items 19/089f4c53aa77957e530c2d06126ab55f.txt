<b>About the Position:</b><br>
Reporting to the Dayforce Product Owner, the Senior Business Analyst - Mobile Applications is responsible for gathering and analyzing market and technical requirements for implementing new software development projects, and completing and augmenting the current solutions and services within the Dayforce HCM product. They will be responsible to implement the On-Demand Pay product, including mobile application, integration with payroll, taxes, and other portions of the product. They will collaborate with Product Management, Developers, Sales, Implementation, Support and Clients throughout the development process to ensure the product is functionally
<br>complete and well-engineered. This is an excellent opportunity to join a rapidly growing innovator in a technically challenging and rewarding role. This role will be located in the Atlanta region.
<br><br>
We are looking for fun, intelligent, team oriented people who want to work in an agile environment and believe in our values: Customer Focus,Transparency, Diligence, Optimism, and Agility.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Analyze market requirements and produce design documents that describe how new software features and/or enhancements should function within the Dayforce application suite of products with emphasis on areas requiring technical expertise</li>
<li>Work closely with members of the Product Management and Development teams to translate market requirements into solutions and system designs that align with product development strategy</li>
<li>Partner with clients, internal stakeholders and development teams to understand needs and user experience to help shape the product</li>
<li>Assess risks and impacts on the existing system as new functions are added and identify potential technical problems or design flaws so that preventive action can be taken</li>
<li>Ensure that issues and action items are identified, tracked, reported on, and resolved in a timely manner</li>
<li>Support user acceptance testing processes to ensure that product requirements are fully satisfied</li>
<li>Create and deliver presentations to various internal/external users with varying levels of product knowledge</li>
<li>Exhibit innovation in the development of new and improved methods or approaches to problems</li>
<li>Lead special projects as needed</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Payments technology and Mobile solutions</li>
<li>Experience designing and analyzing Payroll/Human Capital Management solutions preferred</li>
<li>Experience writing product requirements for agile development teams</li>
<li>User Interface design experience</li>
<li>Experience with Agile / SCRUM product development methodologies</li>
<li>Highly organized, disciplined self-starter, capable of working independently or in close collaboration within an agile development team</li>
<li>Excellent consulting and collaboration skills</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication and presentation skills required</li>
<li>Ability to conceptualize, negotiate and sell ideas internally and externally</li>
<li>Ability to implement concepts, ideas and plans while functioning in a flexible,constantly changing environment necessary</li>
<li>Must be a strong team player as well as an individual contributor</li>
<li>Thrives when working in a fast-paced, creative, innovative technology environment with a history of results</li>
</ul>
