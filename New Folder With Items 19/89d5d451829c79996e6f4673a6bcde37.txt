<p><b>
FUNCTION OVERVIEW:</b></p>
<p>
The Model Based Systems Engineer is responsible for the end to end accountability from requirements to final delivery of plant models and simulation infrastructure, following Model Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) organization guidelines and tool-chain.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create, verify and validate new plant models (modelica language)</li>
<li>
Integration of plant models for different applications: model-in-loop (MIL), software-in-loop (SIL), processor-in-loop (PIL), hardware-in-loop (HIL) and System Level simulation</li>
<li>
Create and use simulation-based design methods to drive system optimization, improve module/component design and enhance product reliability</li>
<li>
Use planned experimentation tools and methods to support the physics-based approach to modeling and simulation
</li>
<li>Simulation architectures for different applications (control system simulation &amp; system performance models)
</li>
<li>Accountable for the creation of libraries, capture and reuse of knowledge through models</li>
<li>
Reuse (leverage) of models, simulations and tools across platforms and regions</li>
<li>
Development and use of Rapid Prototyping Tools (ex. DSpace, National Instruments, etc.);
</li>
<li>Collaboration across geographic boundaries to leverage global knowledge and delivery of winning solutions</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Master’s Degree in Engineering with specialization on modeling of physical systems (for instance thermals, HVAC, mechanics, electrical)</li>
<li>
1 plus years’ experience with analytical / 1D modeling of physical systems</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PREFERRED REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
PhD in the area of modeling of physical systems (preferably thermal / fluid systems)</li>
<li>
Ability to work cross functionally and globally</li>
<li>
Experience on plant modeling with system level tools (for instance Dymola, OpenModelica, Maplesim, Simulink, SimulationX, Simscape, Amesim, GT-Suite, etc..)</li>
<li>
Familiarity with System Engineering principles and tools (Requirements, SysML, V&amp;V methods)</li>
<li>
High fidelity physical modeling (ex. FEA, CFD)</li>
<li>
Experience with Matlab / Simulink applied to Controls Design and/or Simulation</li>
<li>
Experience with optimization methods and tools</li>
<li>
Knowledge in Controls &amp; Algorithm Design</li>
<li>
Experience with Rapid Prototyping Tools (ex. DSpace, National instruments, etc..)</li>
<li>
Experience with experimental testing, data gathering and analysis</li>
</ul>
