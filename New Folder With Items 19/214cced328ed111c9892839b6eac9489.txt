<b>We Make People Smile</b><br>
<br>
Magic Memories is a company that delights and amazes guests on the simple but universal concept of making people smile, and has driven that concept into its product, service, and innovation philosophy of capturing, creating and sharing memories worldwide. For over 20 years our company has offered retail professionals with great opportunities that encourage growth and advancement in our expanding enterprise.<br>
<br>
Picture yourself working in <b>Philadelphia, PA</b> in an exciting and dynamic atmosphere at our iconic attraction, <b>Terror Behind the Walls - Eastern State Penitentiary.</b><br>
<br>
We are seeking an outgoing, friendly, and motivated individual to be a part of a high energy team. If you possess a can-do positive attitude and an outstanding set of customer service or sales skills, then we’re looking for you as a Retail Photo Operations Manager of <b>Terror Behind the Walls - Eastern State Penitentiary</b><b>!</b> You will lead a retail team that will capture fun, interactive moments of guests at diverse attractions. These are published via both print and digital formats into storytelling products, then sold to our guests.<br>
<br>
We offer a fun job with great opportunities to expand your retail and leadership experience with a company that encourages growth and advancement! We offer competitive wages and incentives too!<br>
<br>
<b>As a Retail Photo Operations Manager:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive teams to achieve goals.</li>
<li>Run all operational aspects of a high-volume souvenir photography retail operation.</li>
<li>Develop, train and motivate staff to achieve desired guest service and financial results.</li>
<li>Provide floor supervision and administrative duties, ensuring the team stays focused on guest service and sales goals in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>You will ensure excellent customer service by responding to guest requests, expectations and needs.</li>
<li>You will have the opportunity to meet and interact with people from all over the world.</li>
</ul>
