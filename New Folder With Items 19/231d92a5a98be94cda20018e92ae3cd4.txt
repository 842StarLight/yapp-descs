<h2>Customer Service Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b><i>*Only candidates who officially apply through our external website will be considered. Site can be found at bottom of the post: </i></b></p>
<p>Sigma is a global food company that is dedicated to the production, distribution, and sales of refrigerated and frozen products such as cold meats, dry meats, cheese, yogurt, prepared meals and beverages. We have a strong diversified portfolio of well-positioned brands that market in the countries where we participate. We operate in 69 production plants, serving more than 590,000 customers in North, Central, South America, the Caribbean, and Europe.</p>
<p>Working for Sigma is like working for a family-owned business, we believe in taking care of our associates so they will be dedicated to producing high-quality products. You are not just applying for a job, this is the start to a new career whether your path takes you to the corporate headquarters or within the food production and distribution side of the business.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>Oversee and resolve customer service issues for both consumers and customers, oversee broker commissions and monitor warehouse inventory and order processing.</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Handle and resolve customer service issues from both consumers and customers, assure customer service is performed to the highest standard; report and track customer service statistics on a weekly basis including the order fill rate and on time delivery</li>
<li>Review inventory reports and follow up on assigned disposition recommendations.</li>
<li>Review orders and research any abnormalities.</li>
<li>Set up new customers and brokers.</li>
<li>Oversee broker commissions, including but not limited to entering the commissions, auditing accuracy of commission checks, ongoing maintenance of broker commission files, and research any accounts payable discrepancies to ensure timely check distribution.</li>
<li>Monitor warehouse and inventory; report and track weekly inventory details.</li>
<li>Send out timely shortage emails and sales data reports to sales team.</li>
<li>Monitor pricing and promotions by proactively collaborating with the sales department to ensure pricing and promotions are accurately entered and updated in the system, and promotion end dates are current.</li>
<li>Manage and ensure current information on all inventory and products and upload new item information across all required platforms.</li>
<li>Manage and respond to product information upload requests.</li>
<li>Maintain and update product information across all platforms in collaboration with Quality Assurance.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Five years of customer service and or order entry experience</li>
<li>Excellent communication, interpersonal and customer service skills, must be able to communicate effectively both in person and on the phone</li>
<li>Detail oriented, organized and capable of prioritizing workload to complete multiple tasks and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Able to work independently, but also collaborate well with other departments</li>
<li>Evaluating data using analytical and logical reasoning</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge with Microsoft Office, including skills with Outlook, Word and Excel.</li>
<li>Experience with ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS/WORK ENVIRONMENT</b></p>
<ul><li>Usual office environment with frequent sitting, walking, and standing, and occasional climbing, stooping, kneeling, crouching, crawling, and balancing. Frequent use of eye, hand, and finger coordination enabling the use of office machinery. Oral and auditory capacity enabling interpersonal communication as well as communication through automated devices such as the telephone.</li></ul>
