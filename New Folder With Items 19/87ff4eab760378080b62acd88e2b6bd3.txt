<h2>Client Service Relationship Associate</h2>

<p>Company Overview</p>
<p>
Every firm has a culture – the values, beliefs, methodology, attitudes and standards that reflect an organization’s DNA. But the truly inspiring firms – the game-changers, the industry leaders and the disruptors – have cultures that propel them to innovate and stand out. At Brown Advisory, we aim to be one of those inspired firms. Over the years, we have purposefully built and nurtured our client-first culture.</p>
<p>
Brown Advisory is an independent investment management firm committed to delivering a combination of first-class performance, strategic advice and the highest level of client service with offices in Austin, Baltimore, Boston, Carolinas, Delaware, London, New York, Virginia and Washington, D.C. The firm’s institutional and private clients are based in 50 states and more than 37 countries and territories and are served by over 650 colleagues worldwide, all of whom are equity owners of the firm.</p>
<p>
Signature Family Wealth Advisors is a multi-family office serving approximately 180 families and managing in excess of $4 billion. The firm has offices in Charlottesville, Norfolk, and Richmond. The vast majority of our clients are entrepreneurs whose wealth has been created by them and realized through either a partial or full sale of a business. Signature’s mission-empowering our clients to achieve what’s most important in their life-leads to tremendous job satisfaction and outstanding client retention. Signature was named as one of the Best Places to Work in Virginia for 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017 and 2018 by Virginia Business.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
POSITION DESCRIPTION: We are currently seeking a Client Service Relationship Associate who will partner with the Client Strategist in overseeing all of the pieces that intersect a client’s financial life. The position requires an understanding of the investment/financial industry and the ability to interact personally with sophisticated clients at a high level.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE: Bachelor’s degree required, preferably in a related field. Ideally, 5-10 years’ experience in financial services industry. Proficiency with Microsoft Office required. Completion of the Certified Financial Planner program and/or Series 65 helpful, but not required. Strict attention to detail and ability to prioritize is critical. Must have personal integrity and unquestioned ability to maintain confidential information.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The primary duties and responsibilities include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Work as part of a dedicated client team, managing the details of client relationships and assisting clients on a variety of matters – be a crucial team member in the brainstorming/strategizing aspect of client projects, drive and take ownership of client work, serve as the client’s primary contact when seeking certain answers and on certain tasks.</li>
<li>Interact with clients and manage various aspects of the relationship to ensure delighted clients – attend and serve a role in client meetings, confidently provide answers, document decisions, and carry out agreed-upon strategies.</li>
<li>Serve as liaison between client and Client Strategist, facilitating requests from either side and coordinating activity – understand appropriate level of involvement, communicate effectively with clients and team in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Be a subject matter expert and driver of the Signature process - anticipating outcomes and providing suggestions/directions of process implementation.</li>
<li>Gather and execute new account applications and maintenance of assigned accounts - thoroughly understand Signature’s resources, policies, and procedures. Fluent use of best technological solutions provided by firm.</li>
<li>Provide Client Strategists and other team members regular status updates on activities related to shared clients proactively– develop an organized system for tracking your work and notify the appropriate team members when there are updates, learn what needs to be prioritized and what can wait.</li>
<li>Handle communications with outside centers of influence such as CPAs, attorneys, etc. Work with accountants and estate attorneys to provide relevant tax and account information.</li>
<li>Institutional knowledge of all client cash needs/recurring transactions - regularly scheduled distributions, facilitate outside private investment capital calls, calculate and facilitate required minimum distributions, understand what constitutes sufficient cash levels for individual clients, and partner with FAA team when applicable. Capture transaction comments in portfolio software.</li>
<li>Assist with portfolio management, work with Portfolio Manager on monitoring cash, rebalancing portfolios, and in executing trades – understand our investment platform and thought process behind our asset allocation, industry rules and regulations.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with Operations on audits to ensure that client records in the firm’s database are thorough and accurate – maintain databases with updates, be proactive in obtaining missing information, while understanding the importance of how it impacts our clients.</li>
<li>Create client meeting agendas, organize materials, and contribute ideas for meeting topics. Understand the planning work beyond executing tasks and suggest ideas for impactful solutions.</li>
<li>Generate client reports either monthly or quarterly; drive meeting planning and preparation: reports, investment performance, sufficiency analysis, etc.</li>
<li>Maintain familiarity with client preferences, histories, and personalities. Anticipate client needs and proactively search for solutions.</li>
<li>Know what we can and cannot offer to do for a client because of regulatory reasons.</li>
<li>Have fun. Bring candor and an openness to learn new things. Be committed to working in an environment that takes seriously its Values, Beliefs and Behaviors.</li>
<br>
</ul>
