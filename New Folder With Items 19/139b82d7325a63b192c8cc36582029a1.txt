<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities (Other duties may be assigned.)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and administer computer networks and related computing environments including computer hardware, systems software, applications software, and all configurations.</li>
<li>Perform data backups and disaster recovery operations.</li>
<li>Diagnose, troubleshoot, and resolve hardware, software, or other network and system problems, and replace defective components when necessary.</li>
<li>Plan, coordinate, and implement network security measures to protect data, software, and hardware.</li>
<li>Configure, monitor, and maintain email applications or virus protection software.</li>
<li>Operate master consoles to monitor the performance of computer systems and networks, and to coordinate computer network access and use.</li>
<li>Load computer tapes and disks, and install software and printer paper or forms.</li>
<li>Design, configure, and test computer hardware, networking software and operating system software.</li>
<li>Monitor network performance to determine whether adjustments need to be made, and to determine where changes will need to be made in the future.</li>
<li>Confer with network users about how to solve existing system problems.</li>
<li>Research new technologies by attending seminars, reading trade articles, or taking classes, and implement or recommend the implementation of new technologies.</li>
<li>Analyze equipment performance records to determine the need for repair or replacement.</li>
<li>Implement and provide technical support for voice services and equipment, such as private branch exchange, voice mail system, and telecom system.</li>
<li>Maintain an inventory of parts for emergency repairs.</li>
<li>Recommend changes to improve systems and network configurations, and determine hardware or software requirements related to such changes.</li>
<li>Gather data pertaining to customer needs, and use the information to identify, predict, interpret, and evaluate system and network requirements.</li>
<li>Train people in computer system use.</li>
<li>Coordinate with vendors and with company personnel to facilitate purchases.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>Qualifications: </b>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education and/or Experience:</b> Bachelor's degree (B. A.) from four-year college or university preferred, or Technical degree 3-5 years’ experience in designing, creating and maintaining Access databases required and 3 years’ experience and/or training in general IS preferred; or equivalent combination of education and experience required. General knowledge of LAN/WAN, Cisco routers and switches, TCP/IP, AS400, Windows NT, Window/DOS operating systems, Citrix Metaframe, MS Office, EDI and general telecom technologies. Requires excellent interpersonal skills, tact, and judgment in dealing with co-workers, customers, and supplier.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Computer Skills</b>: To perform this job successfully, an individual should have knowledge of:</p>
<p><b>
Networking:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>TCP/IP knowledge, ie. Subnets, Routing, IP address schemes, VLANs and basic routing.</li>
<li>Hardware: Gateways, Switch, Router, firewall.</li>
<li>Networking command prompt commands, ie. Ping, tracert, netstat, ipconfig /all etc.</li>
<li>WINS, DCHP, DNS understanding, implementation</li>
<li>Ethernet adapter management, Wifi understanding, in order to be able to test and troubleshoot network connection problems.</li>
<li>Wireless Connection troubleshooting and configurations to hot spots. Wireless configurations for security, IE WEP 64 bit 128 bit or Mac address.</li>
<li>Being able to create Network Topology of existing network through understanding the layout, or creating a Network Diagram for a new network or addition to a network, via subnetting or supernetting.</li>
<li>Understanding the difference between routable and non-routable IP address.</li>
<li>Understanding the basics of fiber optics. Recognizing fiber cables vs. ethernet (Cat5) cables.</li>
<li>Recognizing fiber receiver/transmitter hardware. Understanding and knowing difference between both physical medias.</li>
<li>Knowledge of cabling, adapters and converters.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Operating Systems:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Windows 2003, XP, 7, 2000 Server, 2003 Server</li>
<li>Understanding how the registry works and registry security</li>
<li>Removal of spyware and viruses, understanding removal procedures</li>
<li>To be able to work on a PC and fix most issues without formatting, therefore preserving user data and configurations.</li>
<li>Update and Patches maintenance</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Software/Hardware:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of major software, i.e. Adobe products, Office suites, Internet explorer, Exchange Client and Server SW, Antivirus and security software, Ghost and other imaging software</li>
<li>Specific knowledge of Outlook/Exchange use/policies</li>
<li>The Ability to understand Tape Backup schematics and their software solutions</li>
<li>Quickly acquire knowledge and understanding of new and proprietary software</li>
<li>Knowledge of Emulation software (ie. Tera Term, ProComm) and procedures to connect to Mainframes.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Cell Phones and Bluetooth capable devices.</li>
<li>Blackberry device setup and troubleshooting</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>System Administration:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding internal Data Flow, i.e. Data collection at Mainframe, download to Roadnet, upload to Mainframe, download to Warehouse logistic systems, etc.</li>
<li>Will be responsible for supporting remote users</li>
<li>Maintaining a clean work environment – using company conventions, ie. PC naming conventions, E-mail address setup, User account setup, etc, maintaining well organized and clean Patch panel, office layouts, port layouts, etc.</li>
<li>Maintaining well organized and up-to-date licensing records and hardware inventories</li>
<li>Preserving/Backing up any data when rebuilding systems. High consciousness for failover procedures</li>
<li>The ability to troubleshoot via all resources available. Ability to utilize any and all resources available in order to successful complete the trouble shooting process independently</li>
<li>Optional but desirable: Understanding of Warehouse logistic systems, data flow, mechanical workings and interfacing software.</li>
<li>Understanding Battery backup systems</li>
<li>Utilizing industry procedures, conventions and standards</li>
<li>Understanding of Print and file servers, implementing both</li>
<li>The basics of active directory and knowledge of security permissions for PC and Server OS’s.</li>
<li>Understanding Remote Access, VPN tunnels, PCAnywhere, and VNC.</li>
<br>
</ul>
