<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Recently named as one of LA's Best Places to work in 2019, Prodege, LLC, an internet and media company that is dedicated to “creating rewarding moments” for its members by rewarding them with more than $700 million in cash and free gift cards since inception. Prodege is comprised of four consumer brands — Swagbucks, MyPoints, ShopAtHome, and InboxDollars— which deliver engaging content and valuable rewards to more than 100 million members worldwide. Prodege's complementary suite of business solutions helps brands boost engagement with consumers by making it easy to gather insights, listen to their audience and gain further exposure to drive brand loyalty.<br>
<br>
Prodege is looking for a rock star Product Owner with an excellent track record in bringing great digital experiences to users. The candidate should have extensive experience in collaborating with engineering, sales, product marketing and other cross-functional teams to drive product innovation.<br>
<br>
The P<b>roduct Manager</b> evaluates, analyzes, plans and manages the development of assigned products from conceptual stages through product life cycles to optimize revenue and meet consumer traffic, operational, financial, and corporate objectives. Typical products include new and existing desktop / mobile applications, lead generation, advertising, consumer experience workflows and B2B applications.<br>
<br>
Key responsibilities include leadership in an agile environment of/for: roadmap planning, platform/product definition, participation w/ technical specifications, testing, alpha/beta trials management, product launch/rollout across global markets, revenue modeling &amp; forecast and ongoing product operations management.<br>
<br>
Summary of essential job functions:<br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and take responsibility for assigned B2B, consumer desktop and mobile products. Keep things moving and past the finish line to product launch and beyond.</li>
<li>
Build products that deliver value by engaging and delighting consumers and clients.</li>
<li>
Leading cross-functional teams, both internal and external, to execute product plans on time, scope, and budget.</li>
<li>
Developing product strategy based on analysis of consumer needs, technology trends, competitive landscape, and business models</li>
<li>
Drive the development of the overall product roadmap for assigned products which includes defining objectives, budgets, competitive positioning and product needs for short and long term goals to ensure continued growth and competitive advantage in the marketplace.</li>
<li>
Work effectively with business line managers, sales, engineering, QA, and design to execute against prioritized and actionable product backlog following Agile methodology. Manage alignment of product backlog with the roadmap.</li>
<li>
Monitor key metrics for product effectiveness, engagement, retention, and growth. Develop product enhancements that improve these metrics. Utilize A/B testing of hypotheses to drive iterative development of improvements.</li>
<li>
Partner with business line managers, operations, sales and marketing groups on initiatives.</li>
<li>
Initiate, maintain, and evaluate relationships with partners and vendors.</li>
<li>
Communicate with stakeholders.
</li>
