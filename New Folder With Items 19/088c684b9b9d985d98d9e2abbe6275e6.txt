<h2>SVP of Commercial Banking</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
Provides leadership, direction and goals in the area of commercial deposit and loan production. Strategizes and presents high level information of commercial deposit and loan production specific initiatives and results that are consistent with and directly contribute to overall CMCU strategic initiatives and financial performance results.
<br><br>
Supports the strategic planning process through departmental planning and participates as requested in organizational level strategic planning. Aligns initiatives with business direction. Directs the commercial deposit and loan production efforts of CMCU in an efficient manner to assure that CMCU meets its strategic goals and objectives while minimizing risk.
<br><br>
Formulates and recommends policy and procedure changes as it relates to commercial deposit and loan production. Works with executives to approve policies which may have an organization-wide affect.
<br><br>
Directs and oversees managers’ efforts to grow commercial deposit and loan revenue as directed by executive management by successfully directing prospecting for new business and retaining and expanding existing member relationships.
<br><br>
Ensures commercial loan and deposit growth goals are attained while minimizing risks, Credit Union policies, regulations and procedures are followed, and Credit Union commercial loan portfolio quality is maintained.
<br><br>
Directs the overall commercial deposit and lending sales and member experience of the Credit Union in an efficient and profitable manner to assure that the Credit Union meets its strategic goals and objectives while minimizing risk.
<br><br>
Oversees the commercial deposit and loan product and service ownership strategy and road map. Analyzes economic, market and industry trends regarding commercial deposit and lending products and services, policies and pricing, recommending appropriate Credit Union response to ALM.
<br><br>
Recommends changes to commercial deposit and loan products and services, systems and processes and oversees the implementation and training of new and existing commercial deposit and loan products.
<br><br>
Oversees audit and compliance assessments related to commercial deposit and loan production.
<br><br>
Oversees the conformance to commercial deposit and lending policies, regulatory compliance, underwriting standards and internal controls throughout the organization, coordinating all support activities with cross-functional areas such as branch administration, commercial lending production and credit departments. Reviews reports for quality control to ensure operational procedures are being followed.
<br><br>
Oversees commercial deposit and lending software needs and changes to ensure compliance with applicable laws, regulations and policy. Makes decisions regarding system releases and upgrades in conjunction with the System Analyst and Infrastructure team, ensuring no interruption in product and service delivery.
<br><br>
Oversees vendor management for commercial deposit and loan production. Responsible for vendor due diligence. Manages after-hours vendor contact.
<br><br>
Participates in work groups and/or committees. Works in collaboration with Credit Union executives, vice presidents, management and staff to meet the goals and objectives of the Credit Union.
<br><br>
Oversees development, training and management of the performance and compensation of direct and indirect staff. Approves hiring decisions, and delivers performance reviews and compensation for staff. Responsible for commercial deposit and loan production results that are achieved through the successful management of staff.
<br><br>
Develops and manages the budget and the monthly rolling forecast that has a major impact to the overall success of the organization. Participates in budgeting for commercial deposit and lending products.
<br><br>
Provides leadership to community relations efforts, serving as a key ambassador for the Credit Union, interacting with key members of the industry and communities served.
<br><br>
EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE
<br><br>
Bachelor’s degree in a business or finance related field plus eight years progressively responsible management experience in a financial institution with eight years in business deposit or business lending operations, in a similar or greater size and complexity organization; or equivalent knowledge and skills gained through a minimum of twelve years related work experience.
