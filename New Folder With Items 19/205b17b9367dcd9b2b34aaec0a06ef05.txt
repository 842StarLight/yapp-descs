<li>Supports bringing new products to market by analyzing proposed product requirements and product development programs; preparing return-on-investment analyses; establishing time schedules with the development team.</li>
<li>Engages in software product pricing by utilizing market research data while reviewing production and marketing costs.</li>
<li>Develop and deliver concise presentations for internal and external audiences.</li>
