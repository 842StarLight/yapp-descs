<h2>Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Basic Purpose of Position:</b> Working under general supervision, the Program Manager will Plan and manage programs relative to attaining cost, schedule, and performance goals. Interface with customers (both internal and external) to communicate requirements such that all parties are working together to achieve the program goals.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Primary or Major Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Primary customer contact providing accurate, prompt, professional and courteous service in accordance with mutual expectations created through Sale Order delivery commitments or, if established, co-signed Manufacturing Service Agreements.</li>
 <li>Work with Sales, Engineering, Manufacturing, and Materials to insure necessary customer documentation is obtained, properly stored and distributed per established procedures.</li>
<li>Support customer ECOâ€™s managing both implementation and associated impact in terms of delivery and cost per established procedures.</li>
<li>Establish and change production priorities through the maintenance of Sale Order delivery dates, while measuring delivery performance to the original customer delivery commitment.</li>
<li>Effectively use company software to maintain and communicate real-time status of production in terms of cost, quality, and availability.</li>
<li>Work directly with the Customer and the internal teams in regards to project schedules, forecasts, order management, production issues, team meetings, action items, engineering change notices, and any other topics related to the project.</li>
<li>Work with the assigned Materials Buyer on supplier selection and supplier management issues as needed.</li>
<li>Perform customer account asset (account payable and inventory) management and monitor individual product and account margin performance.</li>
<li>Establish formal and informal communication processes with assigned accounts to maintain customer satisfaction when addressing complaints, concerns and product returns.</li>
<li>Responsible for P&amp;L performance of program.</li>
<li>Participate in and lead Customer program team communication meetings as required by Customer account.</li>
<li>Assist Sales in Pricing and Contract Negotiations when required.</li>
<li>Manage program lifecycle: development, engineering, manufacturing &amp; end-of-life.</li>
<li>Manage customer relationships: voice of the customer (VOC).</li>
<li>Negotiate key issues with the customer, both financial and project based.</li>
<li>Identify growth opportunities within current programs.</li>
<li>Develop bid strategy and coordinate bid efforts.</li>
<li>Analyze financial information relative to bids and program progress.</li>
<li>Support BDM in customer pursuit and lead generation.</li>
<li>Key liaison between company and internal/external customers.</li>
<li>Prepare and supply information for annual sales plans and budgets.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Minimum Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS in Business, Technical, or related field</li>
<li>5 to 10 years working in a Program, Projects or Customer Service environment within the CM or similar environment</li>
<li>Experience with Military customers or sub-contractors that sell into the Military</li>
<li>Must possess good working knowledge CM technology (specifically circuit board assembly)</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure and prioritize tasks</li>
<li>Ability to multitask</li>
<li>Demonstrated analytical problem solver with strong computer skills</li>
<li>Strong negotiation, interpersonal &amp; communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent oral, written and presentation skills</li>
<li>Understanding of the Defense acquisition and budget process</li>
<li>Ability to work with little or no supervision</li>
</ul>
