We pride ourselves on our supporting culture where you learn from failures, pick yourself back up and become the best version of yourself. <h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Role</b></h3>
<p>
The <b>Regional Manager, Enterprise Sales</b> is a sales management position responsible for leading a team of highly skilled, seasoned sales individuals consisting of Enterprise Account Managers.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Own regional sales number, revenue targets and sales projections</li>
<li>
Reports to the VP Regional Sales and will be responsible for building and developing a team</li>
<li>
Works closely with new as well as established channel partners to generate new business opportunities</li>
<li>
Partner with marketing team to ensure effective marketing strategy and channel team on channel strategy</li>
<li>
Ensure timely and accurate revenue, customer and management reports using in-house systems, evangelizing the use of SFDC for forecasting requirements and prospect tracking</li>
<li>
Lead, mentor and ramp all account managers on the team as well as partner closely with System Engineering team</li>
<li>
Work closely with Systems Engineers, Inside Reps, Field Marketing, Professional Services, and Channel Management to ensure target quotas as achieved and exceeded</li>
<li>
Travel up to 50%</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Experience Required</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of high-level management experience of 7 or more direct reports in a fast paced, high-reaching, ever-changing sales environment</li>
<li>
Extensive rolodex, with validated experience in interviewing, hiring, and building out high performance sales teams</li>
<li>
Minimum of 7 years in quota carrying sales role with demonstrated achievement</li>
<li>
Confirmed ability to work effectively with and across all levels of business and IT contacts within very large and complex organizations</li>
<li>
Extensive experience negotiating large 7 figure deals with extremely complex terms, conditions, price pressures and considerations</li>
<li>
Self-starter who takes initiative, works with limited direction, and has experience with companies pursuing and undergoing rapid growth</li>
<li>
Highly trusted individual who maintains and expect high standards for self and team</li>
<li>
BA/BS degree or higher</li>
</ul>
