<h2>Order Administration Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Order Administration Coordinator for Gear Solutions Engineering works with Product Engineering and Manufacturing Management to process incoming orders in SAP, prepare orders for acknowledgment to customers, process incoming and outgoing shipping transactions and coordinate incoming and outgoing customer material. Communicates regularly with customers, providing updates to schedules, quality concerns, or necessary changes to orders.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Enter and process all customer orders and order acknowledgements</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Verify completeness and accuracy of submitted orders</li>
<li>Coordination with Production scheduling and Project Coordinator</li>
<li>Coordination of release to manufacture with Product Engineering</li>
<li>Update shipments and deliveries as needed.</li>
<li>Provides overflow and secondary back-up support to Project Coordinator as needed</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Make order changes and updates in SAP and release requirements in SAP.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Support quotation follow up communicating with sales RSMâ€™s for updates and following up on specific orders as needed for feedback.</li>
<li>Creation and maintenance of SAP material master data (BoM / Router / Material numbers)</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
