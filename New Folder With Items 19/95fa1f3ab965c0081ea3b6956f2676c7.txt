<h2>Manager Business Relationship (IT)</h2>

<p>We are looking for a Business Relationship Manager (IT) who wants to take the next step in their career with one of the most successful Hospital systems in the United States in the Memphis, Tennessee area.</p>
<p>The chosen Business Relationship Manager (IT) will not only be able to work with an organization that has been recognized for numerous awards but will receive an excellent salary and comprehensive benefit package, among other perks.</p>
<p>The Business Relationship Manager (IT) is responsible for the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Must have Clinical or Clinical Research program management experience</li>
<li>Providing overall management and value delivery of the project management team by managing the business requirements within the cost, time-frame, and quality standards defined by the portfolio.</li>
<li>Serving as a liaison between organizational departments and Information Services to facilitate organizational and strategic initiatives.</li>
<li>Supporting the Information Services Project Management Office (IS PMO) by ensuring adherence to the project management processes for the department.</li>
<li>Supporting the Change Control process by ensuring adherence to the change control processes for the department.</li>
<li>Providing day-to-day supervision of Informatics project managers and processes.</li>
<li>Pursuing professional development opportunities, including learning new hardware, software and other computer-related concepts through journals, books, seminars, user group conferences, networking with industry peers.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in a Healthcare discipline, Business Administration or Management, Information Systems or related field is required</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or advanced degree in Clinical Research, Clinical Informatics, or a clinical specialty is a plus.</li>
<li>PMP, PMI-ACP, or PgMP certification required</li>
<li>Eight (8) years of program or portfolio management experience required in a dynamic and progressive technological environment required Five (5) years supervisory experience required</li>
<li>Three (3) years budgetary planning and management required</li>
<li>Active Project Management certification from Project Management Institute Experience with implementation of global clinical trial management system</li>
<li>Experience with archiving legacy systems CTMS</li>
<li>Experience with full lifecycle support of clinical trial management applications</li>
<li>Must have experience with Oncology</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Constituents: </b>You’ll be a full time employee with excellent benefits, comprehensive benefit package, vacation package, 401K plus so much more.</p>
