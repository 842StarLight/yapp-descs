<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>NACHURS ALPINE SOLUTIONS</b> is North America's leading manufacturer of specialty liquid fertilizers and liquid industrial products. Founded in 1946, fertilizers are marketed under the NACHURS, ALPINE and NASi brands. Manufacturing plants are located in Marion, OH, Corydon, IN, Red Oak, IA, St. Gabriel, LA, New Hamburg, ON and Belle Plaine, SK.</p>
<p>
Nachurs Alpine Solutions is seeking a Marketing Manager who will work out of our Marion facility.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
Reporting to the Vice-President of Marketing, the Marketing Manager is responsible for overseeing the development and execution of the marketing plan for the NACHURS, NASi, Fazio and Key Grow Brands in North America.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
POSITIONS REPORTING TO THE MARKETING MANAGER</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
NACHURS Marketing Coordinator</li>
<li>
NASi Marketing Coordinator</li>
<li>
NACHURS Events Coordinator</li>
<li>
Lead &amp; Tradeshow Manager</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul><li>
Lead market and competitive research and analysis.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Work with VP Marketing on product positioning..</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Company representative on various industry groups.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Manage and coordinate all marketing, advertising and related activities that occur at the corporate office.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Monitor, review and report on all marketing activities and results.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Work with marketing coordinators to develop an annual marketing plan in conjunction with the sales department, which details activities to follow during the fiscal year, which will focus on meeting company objectives.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Work with Lead &amp; Trade Show manager to evaluate trade show participation and to ensure that all leads are followed up on and reported in a timely manner.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Work with brand coordinators to manage marketing expenditures to ensure expenses remain within budget.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Work with the Graphic Designer to manage all aspects of print materials, website content and promotional materials.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Liaison with media and advertising agencies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Ensure that proper trademarks are maintained and utilized.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Coordinate and participate in promotional activities and trade shows to market products.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in writing and editing sales and marketing materials, which include articles, brochures, presentations and collateral.</li>
<li>
Provide regular content for the newsletters and website.</li>
</ul>
