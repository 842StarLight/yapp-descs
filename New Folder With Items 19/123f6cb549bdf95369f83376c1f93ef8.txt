<h2>Mortgage Loan Originator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Mortgage Loan Originator- 1st National Bank
<br><br>
<b>Essential Function:</b><br>
1st National Bank is looking for a stellar Mortgage Loan Originator to lead the opening of our newest office. The Mortgage Loan Originator is responsible for the generation of mortgage loans through realtor referrals, branch referrals, personally established book of business and referrals from 1st National Bank’s existing relationships.
<br><br>
Primary Responsibilities &amp; Duties
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for developing individual relationships with realtors and informing real estate offices of current products, programs and interest rates.</li>
<li>Participate in Realtor groups and other community related activities.</li>
<li>Inform clients of mortgage loan programs available and prepare them to bring required documentation to the application.</li>
<li>Interview and pre-qualify clients for mortgage loans according to guidelines established for various programs.</li>
<li>Enter a complete, accurate application on mortgage software, pull credit information and reconcile all liabilities with customer, send accurate information to underwriting and processing, obtain all required documentation.</li>
<li>Responsible for complying with all applicable regulations and disclosures associated with mortgage application.</li>
<li>Responsible for ongoing communication with client and notification of approval or denial of mortgage loan request.</li>
<li>Meet mortgage production and sales goals as established by Executive Vice President of Mortgage Lending.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Established leader in the mortgage industry with consistent and stellar sales history</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Computer literacy</li>
<li>Familiarity with secondary market underwriting guidelines.</li>
<li>Strong mathematical and computation ability.</li>
</ul>
What we can offer you
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>A great, honest small company that treats employees like family</li>
<li>Autonomy to make decisions and a strong support team that can close a loans very quickly to ensure you’re able to provide the best service to your clients</li>
<li>Competitive pay with a scaled commission structure</li>
<li>Full benefits package including medical, dental, 401K with match, etc.</li>
<li>Wide range of products and excellent rates to offer</li>
<li>Free trips to all employees that meet their volume goals</li>
</ul>
