<h2>Full Time Field Marketing Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Full Time Field Marketing Representative<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>To Apply: Please send a resume to: karissa.abdullah@mtigs.com
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Summary
</p>
<p>This
<br>
position is at the forefront of MTI Global Services’ Sales and Marketing
<br>
efforts to increase client sales at the retail store level. The
<br>
candidate should have diverse skills that include a focus on detail,
<br>
relationship building and the ability to communicate effectively with key
<br>
personnel (retail store sales associates, general managers, sales managers) and
<br>
consumers. This position combines weekly or bi-weekly training store
<br>
visits combined with periodic project based selling day and special events.
</p>
<p>Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Primary duty is to create consumer
<br>
awareness, product brand preference, and product mind share for hardware
<br>
and software companies at the retail store level
</li>
<li>Influence others to share
<br>
enthusiasm for both the company and the product line
</li>
<li>Ability to merchandise client
<br>
products and/or manage the client objectives in an assigned territory
</li>
<li>Position requires gathering client
<br>
product competitive intelligence at the store level
</li>
<li>Effectively collect and
<br>
communicate product information/data through appropriate channels
</li>
<li>Efficiently and effectively train
<br>
retail personnel on products and selling techniques
</li>
<li>Ability to visually access on-site
<br>
needs i.e., product and promotional/marketing material stocking
</li>
<li>Meet timely required reporting
<br>
deadlines
</li>
<li>Periodic travel to attend other
<br>
company sponsored training events or client sponsored special events, or
<br>
to cover other market areas as needed
</li>
<li>Occasionally assist in selling
<br>
days – selling products directly to customers in retail stores.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Skills
<br>
and Requirements:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Good knowledge and experience with
<br>
consumer electronics and various other consumer-based electronics products
</li>
<li>Technically savvy and computer
<br>
proficient
</li>
<li>Computer with daily email access
</li>
<li>Strong oral and written
<br>
communication skills
</li>
<li>Must have the willingness and
<br>
ability to build relationships at the retail store level on behalf of both
<br>
MTI Global Services and our clients
</li>
<li>Professional image and approach to
<br>
business
</li>
<li>Friendly demeanor, easy to
<br>
approach, energetic
</li>
<li>Group training, demonstrating or
<br>
presentation experience
</li>
<li>Retail sales or retail management
<br>
is a plus
</li>
<li>Quick learner who is
<br>
self-motivated
</li>
<li>Ability to self-train on client
<br>
product(s)
</li>
<li>Ability to lift 20 pounds, as
<br>
needed (for arranging store displays)
</li>
<li>Reliable transportation with valid
<br>
state driver’s license and proof of auto insurance<br>
</li>
</ul>
