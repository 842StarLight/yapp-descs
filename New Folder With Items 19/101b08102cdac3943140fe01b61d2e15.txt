<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Director of Marketing will oversee strategy and execution for Almo’s Direct-to-consumer e-commerce business by developing digital storefronts, growing sales, generating customer reviews and driving brand loyalty and customer service strategy. This position will work heavily within the Amazon 3rd Party Selling platform, among other 3P sites.</p>
<p><b>What you will do in this role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage three direct reports within the digital marketing and content marketing division</li>
<li>Lead effort for end to end digitally native brand introductions – market research, product design and testing, brand creation, launch, scale</li>
<li>Lead an ongoing strategic analysis of competitive landscape</li>
<li>Coalesce and leverage disparate data reservoirs to build knowledge of target market and improve targeting capabilities</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for advanced programmatic solutions to improve profitability, speed, and accuracy of a variety of business functions</li>
<li>Deploy a coherent strategy to integrate the following functions to achieve a profitable “conversion engine” for each digitally native brand and product line:
<ul>
<li>Customer service</li>
<li>Review augmentation</li>
<li>Performance Marketing – Programmatic search and display advertising</li>
<li>Community development – Digital PR, Social Media, Influencer</li>
<li>Search Engine Optimization (SEO)</li>
<li>Content creation</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
