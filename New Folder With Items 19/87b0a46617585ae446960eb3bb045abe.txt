<h2>Senior Regional Bank Sales Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">dv01 is the world's first end-to-end data management, reporting, and analytics platform offering loan-level transparency and insight into lending markets, making them more efficient for institutional investors and safer for the world. In a nutshell, we're doing our part to prevent a repeat of 2008.
<br><br>
This Bank Sales Specialist will specifically target our Bank Portfolio Management offering, which simplifies portfolio monitoring across whole loan exposure. Banks can monitor their portfolio with dv01's SaaS analytics, tooling and surveillance offerings and have a singular portfolio view across the institution, from traders, risk managers and research analysts to the finance and accounting teams. dv01 brings cleansed and normalized data that may exist in different systems within an institution into a uniform view, solving many problems for our clients.
<br><br>
You will be a trusted advisor to dv01's customers, including senior management; helping them succeed using our platform and ensuring they receive maximum value from our offerings. You will be responsible for driving this revenue stream and developing strong business relationships with regional banks. You will also drive the product development agenda as you understand customer requirements along the way. This is an entrepreneurial effort on your part, and you should be excited to kick off this journey!
<br><br>
This role reports into the COO and works with stakeholders across the company including the CEO, Sales, Account Management, Support, Integrations, Product and Engineering.
<br><br>
<b>You will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Proactively develop relationships and leverage existing client relationships with decision makers at banks for lead generation and client conversion</li>
<li>Negotiate and close the largest and most complex opportunities</li>
<li>Construct and execute opportunity plan, relationship plan and action plan</li>
<li>Sell across the organization into senior management, as well as traders, risk, finance and accounting</li>
<li>Leverage whole loan knowledge and expertise to meet and anticipate complex client needs and challenges, including regulatory challenges (CECL); anticipate changing industry dynamics</li>
<li>Solve unique and complex sales problems with a broad impact on the bank's business, requiring innovative thinking, developing sales solutions and influencing dv01's position within the market</li>
<li>Deep dive into dv01's Bank Portfolio Management product offering, allowing you to sell our offering with confidence</li>
<li>Collect client requirements to drive product development agenda or create new offerings or combinations to meet client needs</li>
<li>Be willing to travel</li>
</ul>
