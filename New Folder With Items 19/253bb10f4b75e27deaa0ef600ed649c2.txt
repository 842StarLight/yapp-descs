<h2>Network Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Description</b><br>
<p><b>Network/IT Support – Wicomico County</b></p>
<p><br>
Delmarva Document Solutions, a Platinum authorized Xerox Agency, is actively seeking an IT professional with excellent communication and organizational skills for a full-time Network Analyst position. This individual would join our team and provide network assistance and IT support to Delmarva Document Solution’s customers on the Delmarva Peninsula.<br>
</p>
<p>
Working for Delmarva Document Solutions, a locally owned independent Xerox Sales Agency, you will be backed by the world-class resources, products and solutions of Xerox Corporation.<br>
</p>
<div>
<b>
Job Description:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide pre-sale workflow solutions support to our sales team</li>
<li>Network Xerox equipment sold by our sales team</li>
<li>Train customers on newly installed Xerox equipment</li>
<li>Act as a training resource to our sales team for application and workflow solutions</li>
<li>Occasional delivery of small equipment</li>
<li>Provide remote help desk support to our customers</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>
Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>A bachelor’s degree or current and relevant IT certifications such as A+, Network, or other Microsoft certifications</b></li>
<li><b>Ability to demonstrate solid understanding of commonly used office software applications and basic networking principles</b></li>
<li><b>Experience with Windows 2008, Windows 2012, Windows 7 &amp; Windows 10</b></li>
<li><b>Experience with Active Directory</b></li>
<li><b>Experience with network security</b></li>
<li><b>Experience with onsite and/or remote troubleshooting</b></li>
<li><b>Virus protection technology knowledge</b></li>
<li><b>Understanding of LAN and WAN plans, designs and implementations</b></li>
<li><b>Excellent verbal communication skills</b></li>
<li><b>Knowledge with multifunction copier and printer technology is a plus</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Additional Requirements:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication and customer relation skills</li>
<li>Strong time management skills and ability to handle a fast-paced work environment</li>
<li>Must possess strong organization skills</li>
</ul>
<div>
Compensation:<br>
<ul>
<li>Earnings potential based on experience</li>
<li>Full benefits package including: health and dental insurance, life insurance, disability, and 401K (after 6 months)</li>
</ul>
</div>
