<h2>Shipping & Receiving Lead (A Shift)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Competitive Benefits Package Including Company-Paid Medical Premiums, Dental, Vision, Life, LTD/STD, Employer Paid Life Insurance, Paid Holidays, 401(K) with Employer Match, Paid Birthday Off!</p>
<p>
Located in Chili, NY, Sydor Optics is the global leader in custom, flat optics fabrication. With a strong commitment to innovation and a dedication to a pioneering legacy, Sydor Optics continues to attract the brightest minds and expert engineers to deliver world-class products.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Shipping and Receiving Lead receives and inspects all incoming shipments and prepares materials/pertinent documentation for processing and preparing outgoing shipments.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities and Duties:</b></p>
<p>
Preparation of all shipments with appropriate packaging, labels and documentation.
</p>
<p>Receive and unpack all incoming shipments.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Performing receiving inspection (metrological, quantitative and visual) on all incoming raw materials, tooling and supplies, documenting receiving in logbook and accounting software program and all measurements and counts on packing slips.
</li>
<li>Verify observed information with Sydor Optics purchase order information to verify compliance with orders. Portion parts in containers in the appropriate run/batch quantities and enclose job traveler packet prior to any processing.
</li>
<li>Enter all sales orders, confirm delivery and technical specifications and send order acknowledgement to customers.
</li>
<li>Update repeat job process instructions with current order information.
</li>
<li>Order materials, supplies and consumables and follow-up to ensure timely receipt.</li>
</ul>
<p><b> Secondary Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
File packing slips/certifications with customer file.
</li>
<li>Cleaning the assigned work area along with minor machine maintenance.
</li>
<li>Additional duties and responsibilities include, but are not limited to: assisting the management team members with maintenance, purchasing, answer telephone calls and take messages, preparation of reports, information gathering, calling on open purchase orders to verify shipment of purchased materials and tooling, including additional duties as delegated by members of the management team.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications/Skills/Education needed:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma (or equivalent).</li>
<li>1-3 yrs. experience in shipping and receiving preferred</li>
<li>Math skills to include: decimals, unit conversions, trigonometry and geometry</li>
<li>Basic interpretation of print specifications and tolerances</li>
<li>Good communication skills</li>
<li>Good computer skills and knowledge of Microsoft Excel &amp; Word, Internet Explorer, Outlook Express</li>
<li>Good eyesight (or corrected with eyewear)</li>
<li>Ability to lift objects up to 25 lbs.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Sydor Optics, Inc. is EOE/M/F/D/V.</p>
