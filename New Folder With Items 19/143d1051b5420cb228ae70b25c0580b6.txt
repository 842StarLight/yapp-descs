<h2>Internet Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for working with the Marketing team to develop the organization’s Web and mobile and digital communication strategies.</p>
<p><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities</b><b>: </b> includes but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for working with the Marketing team to develop the organization’s Web and mobile and digital communication strategies</li>
<li>Manages relationships with internal divisions and third party agencies to ensure that all online systems are developed, deployed and maintained on-schedule, on-budget and to the highest standards of excellence</li>
<li>Manages prospect database for digital communication and oversees omni-channel communications</li>
<li>Works with the Vice President of Interactive Gaming to develop strategy for all online initiatives</li>
<li>Manages priority and workflow of departmental projects relating to web and mobile app development</li>
<li>Understanding and optimizing user conversion marketing funnel, and web analytics goals</li>
<li>Assists with developing and implementing the marketing strategy for online programs</li>
<li>Accountable for all public websites and micro-sites as well as mobile apps, in-room tablet content, and prospect forms.</li>
<li>Guides and directs internet marketing activities, including website development, search engine optimization and email campaigns</li>
<li>Reports on measures, analysis and the effectiveness of internet marketing activities</li>
<li>Analyzes team members in the development of online creative ideas, campaigns and advertising on a national level</li>
<li>Ensures that online issues are effectively anticipated and addressed and appropriate actions are taken to solve problems</li>
<li>Develops programs for supporting internal clients to help publicize and generate participation in on-property events</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Secondary Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Works with the IT team to ensure that online platforms are appropriately integrated with the backend operations systems</li>
<li>Works on special projects and performs other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Education and Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors’ Degree in Marketing or a related field</li>
<li>Five years of online marketing, and digital messaging (texts, email, app notifications) experience including knowledge of SEO in a corporate marketing or agency environment</li>
<li>PPC advertising experience preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of leading edge, online sales and marketing techniques and technologies</li>
<li>Must have experience managing technical teams and agencies for the development, deployment and maintenance of online media and marketing platforms designed to drive visits and build business</li>
<li>Previous experience should demonstrate progressive responsibilities including a track record of managing complex, high volume work streams as a project leader and/or team member, preferably in a dynamic, high-pressure culture demanding high quality thinking and output on a timely basis</li>
<li>Experience with web design best practices, browser compatibility testing and delivering content to multiple platforms, including mobile web technologies</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and multi-tasking skills</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge of Word, Excel and Outlook</li>
<li>In lieu of a Degree and previously mentioned experience, nine years of online marketing (text, email and app notifications) and knowledge of SEO in a corporate marketing or agency environment may be considered</li>
</ul>
