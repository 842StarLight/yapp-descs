<h2>Sales Finance and Expense Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Support the deal desk finance team by:</li>
<li> Analyzing and closing out deals consisting of new, existing, and upsell opportunities</li>
<li> Updating revenue amortization schedules within Netsuite
</li>
<li> Supporting the deal desk management team by providing timely ad hoc reporting on critical issues</li>
<li> Reconcile month end MRR report to ensure accuracy
</li>
<li> Be the key Finance business partner supporting the Marketing team. Prepare monthly and quarterly Marketing management reporting - analyze results, distil/synthesize key trends, assess current and future business risk, and communicate key messages to management</li>
<li> Track and report Overtime spend across the business
</li>
<li> Become embedded with business partners across the company, establishing trust, building relationships and gaining a deep understanding of relevant drivers</li>
<li> Support the annual budget, quarterly forecast and long-term strategic planning processes</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Expertise in building Excel-based financial models, with an emphasis on aggregating and analyzing data to provide actionable insights</li>
<li> 2-4 years of relevant finance experience preferred, SaaS experience strongly preferred</li>
<li> Four year university degree in finance, economics or accounting required</li>
<li> Strong financial and operational planning skills</li>
<li> Excellent communication skills (written and verbal) with experience developing and delivering presentations</li>
<li> Ability to influence at all levels of an organization</li>
<li> Ability to multitask effectively, working in team environments or independently</li>
<li> Must be driven and highly motivated and able to work in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li> Experience with NetSuite is a plus</li>
</ul>
