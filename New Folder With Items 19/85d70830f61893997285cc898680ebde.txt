Planning Administration</b></p>

</ul>
<p><b>
HR Management</b></p>
<ul><li><p>
Recruitment &amp; contracting of company &amp; project staff;</p></li></ul>
<ul>
<li><p>
Employee development, &amp; training;</p></li>
<li><p>
Policy development &amp; documentation;</p></li>
<li><p>
Employee relations;</p></li>
<li><p>
Performance management &amp; improvement systems;</p></li>
<li><p>
Employment &amp; compliance to regulatory concerns &amp; reporting; company committee facilitation including planning, production, staff; &amp; arranging meetings &amp; agendas, attending &amp; minuting meetings;</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Project Management</b></p>
<ul><li><p>
Manage assigned projects &amp; content.</p></li></ul>
<ul><li><p>

Marketing &amp; PR</b></p>
<ul><li><p>
Manage advertising opportunities in other event program, press &amp; at venues.</p></li></ul>

<li><p>
Organise the availability of company members for media/PR events as necessary.</p></li>
<li><p>
Oversee content, production &amp; distribution of all marketing &amp; publicity materials (posters, program, flyers, mail outs, brochures etc) with Design Director &amp; project manager.</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage press development;</p></li>
<li><p>
Co-ordinate the invitation of potential future promoters &amp; supporters of the company.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Financial Management</b></p>
<ul><li><p>
Provide recommendations regarding investments &amp; cash strategies.</p></li></ul>
<ul>
<li><p>
Oversee preparation of annual budget, regular variance statements &amp; annual audit.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide vision regarding overall financial health of the company.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide vision &amp; leadership in long range fiscal planning to ensure the continuity &amp; solvency of the company.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide recommendations regarding effective utilisation of long &amp; short term debt, including refinancing &amp; purchasing/sales.</p></li>
<li><p>
Oversee fundraising efforts.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Production/QC</b></p>
<ul><li><p>
Insure accurate documentation of production &amp; quality control data &amp; records.</p></li></ul>
<ul>
<li><p>
Direct &amp; oversee site production activities &amp; personnel.</p></li>
<li><p>
Oversee &amp; ensure high safety standards at all times.</p></li>
<li><p>
Direct production activities to insure safety &amp; compliance with quality control standards, regulatory compliance, &amp; lease agreements.</p></li>
<li><p>
Oversee &amp;/or ensure good housekeeping at site at all times.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Administrative Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Ensure client &amp; vendor file integrity (documents, analytical information where required, communication notations, etc.).</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain general oversight &amp; insure accuracy of records including A/R, A/P, Inventory, etc.</p></li>
<li><p>
Assist in development of forms &amp; tools to increase company efficiency &amp; risk management.</p></li>
</ul>
