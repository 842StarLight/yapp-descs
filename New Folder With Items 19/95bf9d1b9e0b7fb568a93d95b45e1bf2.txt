<p>
Hewlett Packard Enterprise is seeking a Sales Strategy &amp; Planning Intern to work in the San Jose, CA office. This individual will assist in sales strategy, planning and operational activities and will work with various cross-functional teams in Hybrid IT business units, Finance, Marketing and IT. This individual will develop extensive hand-on on-job experience in market research, analytics, strategy development, executive presentation, planning, program management and negotiation skills</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The candidate for intern role is studying or has just completed Undergraduate studies (in areas like Operations/ Finance / Economics / Management Engineering / Business Administration) and is continuing education toward Masters degree.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Typical Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Supports data and business analyses to develop business plans.</li>
<li>
Supports the development of statistical and financial models for forecasting and reporting.</li>
<li>
Measures business performance and compares actual data to forecast values.</li>
<li>
Executes market research projects and gathers intelligence on current industry, technology, and consumer trends.</li>
<li>
Works with cross-functional teams to support the data collection process.</li>
<li>
Supports the execution of priority projects with direction.</li>
</ul>
