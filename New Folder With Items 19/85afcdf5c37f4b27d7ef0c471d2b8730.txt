<p><b>Job Decription</b></p>
<div>
<p>Project Engineers work closely with Field Applications Engineers to create proposals, design material handling systems and manage projects. This is an excellent entry level position that has potential to grow in to a Project Management or Technical Sales role after you gain a solid foundation of the material handling industry. Project Engineers at Bastian Solutions are given a high level of responsibility and autonomy to make decisions early in their career. If you are a competitive, entrepreneurial-minded engineer that is looking for a challenging career in a field that will never stop evolving, we want to talk to you!</p>
<p>
 <b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree in Engineering required. Industrial, Mechanical, Systems disciplines preferred.</li>
<li>Preferably 1 - 2 years of working experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership skills</li>
<li>Excellent technical problem solving capability (hardware, controls)</li>
<li>Strong scheduling and organizational skills</li>
<li>Understanding of financials, budgets, cash flow</li>
<li>Enjoys the Supply Chain Automation, logistics Software business environment.</li>
<li>Willing to travel 10 - 20% (travel will vary depending on specific Projects)</li>
<li>Enjoy working in team environment</li>
<li>Experience with AutoCAD or similar software</li>
<li>Familiarity with the local market</li>
</ul> <p><b>About Bastian</b></p>
<p>
Bastian Solutions, a Toyota Advanced Logistics company, is an independent material handling and robotics system integrator providing automated solutions for distribution, manufacturing, and order fulfillment centers around the world. Our team specializes in consulting, system design, project management, maintenance, and installation, while sourcing the best equipment and automation technology. We take great pride in providing exceptional service and flexibility to our customers.</p>
