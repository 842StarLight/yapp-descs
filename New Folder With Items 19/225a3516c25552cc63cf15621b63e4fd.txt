<h2>Sales Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Rental Beast is looking for a driven, highly productive and self-motivated Account Executive to find business opportunities and manage customer relationships.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will have experience in sales and customer service; be directly responsible for the preservation and expansion of our customer base; find opportunities and turn them into long-term profitable relationships based on trust and mutual satisfaction.</p>
<b>
Compensation:</b><p>
$100,000 (includes base + commission)</p>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><ul>
<li>
Unearth new sales opportunities through networking, connecting and consulting with inbound leads and marketing generated opportunities</li>
<li>
Provide professional after-sales support to enhance the customers’ dedication</li>
<li>
Expand Rental Beast’s penetration into existing clients</li>
<li>
Drive the entire sales process by engaging with prospective customers, utilizing technology to perform individualized product demonstrations and then drive the negotiation and close of the sale</li>
<li>
Prepare and present personalized online software demonstrations both in person and via GoToMeeting; guiding your prospects through the Rental Beast solution and buying process</li>
<li>
Achieve and exceed monthly revenue quotas while maintaining daily and weekly activity metrics</li>
<li>
Leverage Salesforce.com to track sales activities and prioritize your day</li>
<li>
Build and manage a strong pipeline of prospective customers and work with your management team to provide an accurate sales forecast</li>
<li>
Remain in frequent contact with the clients in your responsibility to understand their needs</li>
<li>
In this role, you will receive initial onboarding and ongoing sales and product training to become a knowledgeable consultant on the Rental Beast SaaS platform</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<b>
Qualifications:</b><ul>
<li>
A Bachelor’s degree or comparable proven experience as an Account Executive, or in other sales roles</li>
<li>
Proven track record of crushing goals and making your numbers for 1-3 years in a fast-paced and transactional sales environment</li>
<li>
Excellent communication/presentation skills and ability to build relationships quickly</li>
<li>
Great organizational and time-management skills</li>
<li>
You should be driven and competitive by nature, technologically savvy and a fast learner</li>
<li>
An individual contributor who loves to work and collaborate within a team environment to achieve set goals</li>
<li>
Good working knowledge of Salesforce.com or other similar CRM systems</li>
<li>
Experience in performing online product demonstrations via GoToMeeting or similar screen sharing solutions</li>
</ul>
