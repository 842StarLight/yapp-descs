<h2>Accounts Payable Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>SAC Incorporated is seeking an Accounts Payable Coordinator to join our growing team in Vermont. This position would report directly to the Accounting Manager, and support the Finance Department with financial, administrative and clerical responsibilities. This is a full-time position, though we would consider this role in a part-time capacity (20 hours/week) for the right candidate.</p>
<p>
At SAC, we believe a positive work culture makes all the difference. We seek professionals who believe in the power of teamwork, embody SAC’s progressive mindset, and champion our mission to create value for our clients, our partners and ourselves. Our most successful team members have passion in their work, and utilize their unique set of skills and experiences to bring value to each opportunity.</p>
<p><b>
Typical Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>AP invoice and data entry</li>
<li>Coordinate with Purchasing Department to reconcile invoice discrepancies</li>
<li>Review AP statements to ensure all invoices have been processed in system</li>
<li>Address and respond to vendor inquiries</li>
<li>Prepare and execute weekly check runs</li>
<li>Generate AP reports</li>
<li>Manage company credit card reconciliations</li>
<li>Scan and file vendor invoices electronically</li>
<li>File quarterly sales taxes</li>
<li>Assist with AR as needed</li>
<li>Prepare reports when requested by management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Skills &amp; Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate accounts payable capabilities</li>
<li>Must possess a high degree of accuracy and attention to detail</li>
<li>Understanding of financial accounting precepts and demonstrated ability to apply them in day-to-day applications</li>
<li>Strong communication skills both internally between departments and externally with vendors and customers</li>
<li>Must be proficient in Microsoft Excel and other Microsoft Office products such as Word, PowerPoint, Outlook, etc.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Dynamic’s GP accounting software is a plus</li>
<li>Must be able to work in a collaborative “team” environment, responsive to all members of the company across geographies, departments and levels</li>
<li>Must possess problem-solving abilities and have the confidence to make decisions independently</li>
<li>Must be able to travel periodically</li>
</ul>
