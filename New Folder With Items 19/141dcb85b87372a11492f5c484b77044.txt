<div>Want to change the lives of millions of readers by making it easy to read more and get more out of reading? As a Product Designer on the Revenue team, you’ll work with product managers, engineers, sales representatives, and designers to build products that enable publishers and advertisers to promote themselves on our experience, and for consumers to interact around the brands, books, and authors they love.<br>
<br>
Goodreads is currently reimagining our products and brand. In this role, you will help the team define a vision for growing our business and continually identify new opportunities to deliver value to both customers and our publisher/advertiser partners. You will be responsible for customer experiences from sponsored book placements and search results, to integrations with world-class Amazon products, including Prime, Kindle Unlimited, Audible, and more. You’ll design across all touch points including web, iOS and Android apps, and emails.<br>
<br>
Product Design at Goodreads brings together the practices of interaction design, visual design, and related disciplines into a single, user-centric design role. Our designers are generalists who translate user insights and business needs into product experiences that delight, generate visual concepts that exceed expectations, and work with developers to deliver those concepts with a seamless fit and finish.<br>
<br>
Goodreads is the world's largest site for readers and book recommendations, and we have over 90 million members. Our office is located in downtown San Francisco. Goodreads is an Amazon company, so you will have opportunities to partner with Kindle and Amazon teams to elevate the reading experience for millions of customers.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Own design for Revenue products that serve both consumers and advertisers.</li>
<li>Design ways to bring Amazon programs like Prime, Kindle Unlimited, and Audible to Goodreads in a way that delights our customers</li>
<li>Create user-centered designs that incorporate market trends, data, customer feedback, user research, and technical constraints.</li>
<li>Create flows, wireframes, design mockups, and prototypes to effectively conceptualize new advertising experiences.</li>
<li>Determine design direction for our Revenue products and craft, pitch, and validate long-term design strategies.</li>
<li>Drive user research projects to develop the understanding of the customer and validate design choices before and after development.</li>
<li>Advocate for design by sharing your work and presenting cross-functionally, while being able to precisely articulate design rationale.</li>
<li>Partner with product management and engineering teams to define roadmaps and prioritize requirements.</li>
<li>Develop scalable and flexible design frameworks to maintain clean and simple designs.</li>
<li>Give and solicit feedback from other designers to continually raise our bar for quality.</li>
</ul>
