<h2>Office Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Voted a Great Place to Work in 2018, we are the largest sales and marketing company in pre-arranged cremation services. With over 40 years in the industry, <i><b>Trident Society </b></i>helps over 60,000 families pre-plan each year. We are currently looking for an administrative professional to coordinate our office procedures of the day-to-day clerical tasks.</p>
<p>The office coordinator is responsible for supporting multiple teams with various administrative tasks. The person in this role will be a fundamental part of ensuring that our office operations run smoothly and successfully every day.</p>
<p><b>POSITION DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>· Support the sales team by processing and validating contractual agreements to ensure accuracy</p>
<p>· Provides exceptional customer service support by handling customer inquiries, questions, and updates</p>
<p>· Greets and assist visitors as they arrive at the office</p>
<p>· Answer and direct phone calls the appropriate party along with taking and delivering messages accurately</p>
<p>· Produce and maintain, same day checks, trust claims, and the reconciliation of bank records.</p>
<p>· Operate office equipment such as photocopiers, printers, fax machines, etc.</p>
<p>· Input statistical information into a CRM system and other databases</p>
<p>· Use of SOX compliance software to reconcile daily, weekly, and monthly reports</p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<p>· Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook)</p>
<p>· Time management and organizational skills</p>
<p>· Must have the ability to work independently or with a team</p>
<p>· Conveys information clearly and concisely in written and spoken communication</p>
<p>· Capable of setting and meeting priorities and deadlines.</p>
<p>· General clerical tasks, accounts payable, data entry, etc.</p>
<p>· Ability to maintain composure in a fast-paced office setting</p>
<p>· Notary license preferred but not required</p>
<p>· High school diploma or Equivalent</p>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE: </b></p>
<p>· 3- 5 years of administrative or related experience</p>
