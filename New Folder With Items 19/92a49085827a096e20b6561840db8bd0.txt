<h2>Retail Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>PhytoCare Vermont (PCVT), the fifth licensed medical Cannabis dispensary in Vermont is seeking an experienced Retail Manager to join our quickly growing team. PCVT is committed to delivering high-quality medical cannabis and exceptional patient care to Vermont medical patients. We run an organization that shows deep consideration for its employees, for the communities where we work, and for the environment. We are looking for a Dispensary Manager who shares our values.</p>
<p>The Dispensary Manager will be responsible for overseeing the successful operation of our retail sites. Reporting to the Director of Operations, the Dispensary Manager will manage professional dispensary staff, supervise compliance, security, and safety at all locations, and manage inventories. A standout candidate will have 4+ years of retail management experience, a demonstrated understanding of the medical cannabis industry, keen attention to detail, and strong interpersonal skills.</p>
<p>Full job descriptions can be found at: www.phytocarevt.com/employment. No phone calls please. Employees must successfully complete a criminal record check with the Vermont Crime Information Center. PCVT is an Equal Opportunity Employer.</p>
