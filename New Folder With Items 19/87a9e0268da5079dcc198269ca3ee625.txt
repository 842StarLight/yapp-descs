<h2>Pricing & Packaging Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div><b>The Pricing &amp; Packaging Manager will lead pricing &amp; packaging efforts across the entire business at Carta. As we grow and expand our business across multiple markets, products and business lines, this person will help drive alignment across teams for our pricing strategy. This role will be heavily collaborative. This person must be comfortable working across product marketing and sales teams to determine pricing strategy and plans as we launch new products.</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This person will own optimizing price points across various markets, and ensure Carta has operational excellence in the implementation of all pricing decisions. In addition, this person will work with product management and business operations teams to implement pricing changes within the product and in business systems and be responsible for the pricing roadmap.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As Carta continues to grow rapidly into new markets, this role is critical to our success. A successful person in this role will have a combination of skills across business strategy, analytics, project management, communication and collaboration.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Collaborate with leaders in sales, marketing, finance, and product organizations to craft new business models, define monetization strategies for new products and optimize existing pricing and packaging.</li>
<li>
Conduct qualitative and quantitative primary research into competitor pricing.</li>
<li>
Provide well-reasoned pricing change recommendations to Go-to-market leaders.</li>
<li>
Drive cross-sell and up-sell opportunities through pricing strategies and bundling.</li>
<li>
Lead pricing changes from approval by the pricing committee through to implementation.</li>
<li>
Define and evaluate Key Performance Indicators to track pricing performance and enable continuous improvements.
</li>
<li>Maintain official pricing documentation.</li>
<li>
Provide in-deal support to the sales team to assist in strategic opportunities and support ramping new sales team members.</li>
<li>
Build pricing training content for the sales team and train sales on how to approach pricing conversations with customers.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
