<p><b>Field Marketing Manager - Western, US</b></p>
<p><b>
Location is field-based/working remotely</b></p>
<p>
The primary role of the Field Marketing Manager is to provide local/regional marketing planning and implementation to Beltone’s network of retail stores to help with business growth. The field marketing manager will cover the following states: WA, OR, CA, AZ, ID, NV, UT and CO. This is a key marketing position and is responsible for working closely with regional management team to develop local marketing programs, as well be the liaison with corporate marketing. The Field Marketing Manager will be a well-rounded marketing professional with the ability to implement traditional, digital and local/outreach marketing. This position reports to the corporate Director of Field Marketing to connect the dots between corporate and local marketing. The ideal candidate for this role will have marketing experience working for franchise/dealer marketing in the following industries: QSR, telecom, hospitality, medical, automotive (tiers 1-2) or advertising/marketing agency experience with franchise/dealer clients. This position requires 40% travel.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as key regional marketing liaison between corporate and regional management team to understand the local business and develop marketing solutions to drive regional/local KPIs.</li>
<li>Serve as strong marketing advisor with can do attitude to develop and implement local/regional marketing plans.</li>
<li>Work alongside operations, Territory Sales Managers, regional corporate store leaders and Director of Field Marketing to develop local market customer plans, identify local marketing opportunities and troubleshoot as needed.</li>
<li>Work with the Director of Field Marketing to create a seamless process to share and gather information, provide advice and lead integration locally/regionally.</li>
<li>Participate in conference calls and meetings with regional clients to discuss marketing opportunities and share Beltone marketing capabilities.</li>
<li>Provide experience and data-backed advice when requested on:
<ul>
<li>General effectiveness of specific marketing ideas in a given market</li>
<li>Recommended timing of various marketing vehicles to maximize impact</li>
<li>How to maximize their marketing ROI</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Lead all implementation of marketing programs and follow up with results and refinements.</li>
<li>Lead the execution of customized local marketing programs for customers integrating and leveraging internal and external resources.Projects include traditional tactics (direct mail, advertising, print broadcast), referral and outreach programs, local extension of national launches and programs, events, sponsorships and digital tactics (website, paid search, display ads, Google AdWords, retargeting, social media, SEO).</li>
<li>Document marketing data for analysis.</li>
<li>Provide and present to small groups on multiple reports detailing accounts, projects and efficacy of programs where applicable.</li>
<li>Provide and present a quarterly summary report to assigned sales region representatives &amp; marketing peers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred; preferably marketing or business-related field</li>
<li>Minimum of 7 years marketing experience</li>
<li>Experience in retail, franchise/dealer marketing in the following industries - QSR, telecom, hospitality, medical, automotive (tiers 1-2) or advertising/marketing agency experience with franchise/dealer clients</li>
<li>Must have a strong account management and build strong relationships with internal clients</li>
<li>Experience developing marketing plans from a bottom-up budgeting approach</li>
<li>Excellent verbal presentation and written communication skills. Phone and email are the main communication platforms; strong Excel and PowerPoint skills are required</li>
<li>Detail-oriented and results-driven</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and prioritize work with a variety of internal and external customers</li>
<li>Ability to work well with internal clients (field and corporate) to understand their objectives, align on recommendations and execute against plan to achieve goals</li>
<li>Proven ability to generate buy-in on marketing recommendations</li>
<li>Ability to work with multiple projects simultaneously and deliver flawless execution</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate the strategic rationale of various marketing programs as they relate to driving growth in each account</li>
<li>Ability to understand opportunity identification and prioritization as it relates to achieving goals:
<ul>
<li>Must have exceptional project management skills, coordinating with various people, building/maintaining the marketing calendar, overseeing budgets and meeting fast-moving deadlines.</li>
<li>Understand marketing strategy and omni channel approach (print, digital, direct mail, broadcast, local outreach, promotions, etc)</li>
<li>Able to follow marketing process—planning and implementation</li>
<li>Have strong analytical skills</li>
<li>Must be able to juggle lots of projects at once in a very fast retail environment</li>
<li>Must be a consultant and inform, educate and mentor dealers at various levels of marketing know-how.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Must have ability to travel up to 40%</li>
</ul>
