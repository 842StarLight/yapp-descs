<h2>Technical Support Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Provide for Biotage instruments and related consumables technical support, troubleshooting, diagnoses, and repairs via phone, email, and onsite, as needed.
</li>
<li>Real time support to front line support for Sales and field Service as needed during Sales Demonstrations or Field Service Repairs.
</li>
<li>Provide cost estimates and generate quotes for trouble shooting and repair services.</li>
<li>Provide technical training as requested.</li>
<li>Meet established turn around and productivity targets as defined for calls handled and number of calls solved via phone and e-mail support
</li>
<li>Collection, data entry and timely reporting of all required information associated with Repair Depot, Demo Depot, KPIs and Technical Support interactions.
</li>
<li>Field travel and execution of Field Service Engineer duties from time to time as assigned.</li>
<li>Effectively manage assigned inventory and tools, keeping both well organized, in good working order and accurately reported.
</li>
<li>Professionally represent the organization to achieve a positive image.
</li>
<li>Ensure clear and effective communication with both internal and external customers.
</li>
<li>Reclaim parts and other components and refurbish instruments and high value components as assigned.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Education and Experience
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li><h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Two or four-degree: Technical, Engineering, and other related fields or equivalent combination of education and experience preferred.
</b></h3></li>
<li>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Military service a plus.</b></h3>
</li>
<li>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Strong organization, technical, planning, communication (oral &amp; written) and customer service skills</b></h3>
</li>
<li>1-2 years’ experience in an assignment working with biotechnical instrumentation and analytical process instrumentation systems a strong plus.</li>
<li>1-2 years’ experience working with Salesforce, ServiceMax, CRM, ERP, Dynamic 360, or any other service reporting application/system a strong plus.</li>
</ul>

</li>
</ul>
