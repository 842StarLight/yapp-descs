<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Accountant and Payroll Clerk
<br>Job Overview
<br>Essential duties and responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned as needed. Supports the accounting operations of the organization and assists with the processing of payroll data with unwavering accuracy. Must have a natural passion for math, and a strong understanding of the intricacies of assets, liabilities, profit and loss, and other financial transactions within an organization. Plays an enormously supportive role, keeping the organization's financial records with precision.
<br>Experience
<br>5-7 years progressive experience in the accounting/payroll field is required.
<br>Work with PAYCOM payroll processor preferred. If not Paycom, another payroll provider.
<br>A working knowledge of the practices and procedures of the accounting industry.
<br>Ability to effectively communicate details and requirements to other departments and guests.
<br>Working knowledge of computer using basic software.
<br>Excellent customer service skills.
<br>Strong organizational skills and accurate work practices.
<br>Friendly demeanor and positive attitude.
<br>Effective verbal communication.
<br>Ability to work in a team.
<br>Must maintain employee confidence by keeping information confidential.
<br>Ability to work in non-designated office environments
<br>Duties &amp; Responsibilities
<br>Execute mid-level general ledger account reconciliations, accurate reporting, and data verification, enabling senior staff to analyze options and make financial decisions aligned with company objectives.
<br>Monitor policies and procedures for Records daily sales and maintains accounting records by making copies, filing documents.
<br>Able to multi-task in a fast paced hospitality environment. Flexible and able to work with a team.
<br>Has complete knowledge of the accounting software and journal entries, particularly with regards to payroll entries.
<br><ul>
<li>team and individual responsibilities and will help accomplish accounting and organization mission by completing related tasks in timely and accurate manner. Gives and welcomes feedback. Contributes to building a positive team environment. Recognizes accomplishments of other team members.</li>
</ul>
Maintains the utmost confidentiality with regards to payroll and accounting information.
<br>Handles invoicing, accounts receivable and accounts payables as needed.
<br>Basic knowledge of generally accepted accounting principles in the US) GAAP
