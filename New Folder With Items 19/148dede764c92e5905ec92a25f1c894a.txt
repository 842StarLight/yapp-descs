<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The AFI Leasing and Collections department is one of the most difficult, urgent and accountable departments in our enterprise. The individual assuming this role will need strong leadership skills, an extensive marketing background, and a willingness to work long hours to see the task at hand through to 100% completion. The job being offered will require 5 days of maximum effort <b><i>in addition to </i></b>regularly working weekends. This role will be responsible for implementing effective marketing strategies across AFI's entire portfolio in order to achieve our occupancy goals. In addition to base compensation, chosen applicant will have extensive, individual performance based bonus opportunities along with separate bonuses to be paid out upon continued growth of the global enterprise:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Operational Performance Bonuses:</b>$10,000-$30,000/year</li>
<li>
<b>Horizon Bonuses:</b>$10,000-$30,000/year <i>OR MORE </i>based upon global success</li>
</ul>
