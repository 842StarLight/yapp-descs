<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>CitizenNet, a social data and marketing platform, which utilizes machine learning and artificial intelligence to put the right content, offer or advertisement in front of the right person at the right time is based in Los Angeles, CA and is led by CEO Dan Benyamin. The CitizenNet team is based in Los Angeles. Condé Nast’s acquisition in February 2017 of CitizenNet deepens Condé Nast’s data science talent and expertise and will be incorporated into Condé Nast’s proprietary data product “Condé Nast Spire,” to expand the audience targeting and data capabilities of Condé Nast beyond the company’s owned and operated properties to social platforms.
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h1>
<p>The Account Manager is a key part of the CitizenNet team, in charge of managing and developing key client relationships across leading brands using CitizenNet’s audience discovery and ad management software. Account managers work directly with clients throughout the day, creating actionable plans to achieve goals, providing key insights to inform ongoing strategy, and working with campaign managers to flight campaigns. Account Managers at CitizenNet showcase exceptional communication skills, the ability to recognize opportunities and problems quickly, close attention to detail and a willingness to learn quickly in a fast-paced, friendly, team-focused environment.
<br>
<br>
Primary Responsibilities:
<br>
<br>
Driving and overseeing daily client communications with multiple points of contact
<br>
Leading weekly client calls, meetings, and quarterly reviews to highlight account success and note ongoing initiatives and objectives
<br>
Developing client facing proposals that reiterate goals, detail strategy and showcase CitizenNet audience plans
<br>
Delivering end of campaign recaps outlining success and opportunities to scale performance or future growth
<br>
Analyzing CitizenNet generated audience data, historical ad performance and industry trends to make informed client-facing recommendations and internal decisions
<br>
Managing and training campaign managers, ensuring accurate flighting, client satisfaction and strong performance
<br>
As needed, aiding in creation and management of campaigns as well as monitoring and optimization
<br>
Being able to troubleshoot and problem solve campaign and account issues in a stress-free manner
<br>
<br>
Desired Skills &amp; Qualifications:
<br>
<br>
We are looking for candidates with strong client communication and management skills who are excited about technology, data analysis, social media, and advertising. Candidates must be motivated to succeed in a fast-paced team environment. A willingness to adopt new technologies and trends is extremely important for this position.
<br>
<br>
BA/BS Degree
<br>
1+ year of experience in digital account management
<br>
1+ years experience in digital ad operations with emphasis on paid social and data analysis
<br>
Previous use of social media platforms for marketing and/or advertising
<br>
Expertise in MS Excel and Powerpoint required
<br>
Exceptional attention to detail and strong organization skills
<br>
Ability to multi-task and manage projects
<br>
Strong communication skills and a dedicated team player
</p>
