<p><b>
About the role:</b></p>
<p>
As the first point of contact for potential customers you set the tone and the bar for the level of excellence expected in all future interactions they will have with LeagueApps. Partner with Senior Sales Consultants to understand sport segments and create qualified opportunities. Conduct a high volume of phone calls and emails daily. Participate in weekly sales meetings and provide feedback to executives to help contribute to the growth of company. Participate in ongoing sales training. Enthusiastically approach every call with league organizers as an opportunity to build the LeagueApps brand and grow the LeagueApps family of partners. This role reports to the Sales Generation Manager.</p>

<p><b>
About us:</b></p>
<p>
LeagueApps is a fast-growing, venture-backed company that is using data and technology to build the largest community around youth and local sports. We count professional leagues, teams and athletes as investors and partners, and reach millions of people every day through our best-in-class SaaS platform. We're looking for team members who want to be part of building the greatest local sports company in the world.</p>
<p>
LeagueApps operates at the intersection of sports, technology and community. Our best in class sports management platform powers youth and recreational sports organizations across the country, making sports seamless - in everything from registration to payments to communications. More than software solutions, we're creating a community, serving as a thought-leader and bringing insights on such important topics as encouraging girls in sports, injury prevention for youth players, and how technology will affect the future of sport.</p>
