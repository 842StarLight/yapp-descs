<h2>Business Applications Specialist - IT Experience Required</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Kalkreuth Roofing and Sheet Metal</b> is one of the top 10 roofing contractors in the country and strives to maintain a progressive vision for the future. Kalkreuth specializes in complex commercial exterior envelope applications in various markets throughout the country. The company boasts an extensive project portfolio including clients in the automotive, chemical, government, education and healthcare industries. With a wealth of resources and an extensive construction industry network, Kalkreuth has the capabilities to complete any project according to specifications.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Reporting to the Director of Information Technology, the Business Applications Specialist provides technical support for our company s business applications and/or the associated and supporting infrastructure and hardware. The Business Applications Specialist works in a highly collaborative, fast paced environment with a focus on continuous improvement of systems, processes, and skills. The Business Applications Specialist is a subject matter expert for IT Support in the architecture, performance, and functionality of our applications and/or the associated and supporting infrastructure and hardware in order to train others, resolve technical escalations, and engage with the business, development, infrastructure, and project personnel.</p>
<p><b>
Reports To: </b>Directly to Director of Information Technology</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitoring company data for accuracy that is entered by users and reporting improper usage.</li>
<li>Identifying, communicating and helping to correct errors made by system users.</li>
<li>Checking to ensure that all opportunities are up-to-date, in real-time.</li>
<li>Providing a central point of contact for all change requests and system alterations to business applications.</li>
<li>Creating and updating training manuals, policies, and procedures.</li>
<li>Provisioning new users, providing system training and customization.</li>
<li>Working with management to ensure key functionalities are understood.</li>
<li>Providing support for all system users. Escalating to senior IT staff if needed.</li>
<li>Building custom reports and dashboards specific to job functions.</li>
<li>Maintaining control of access rights, security settings, and user privileges.</li>
<li>Generating detailed process maps for sales, marketing, and business workflows.</li>
<li>Importing and exporting of data as required to synchronize back-end systems.</li>
<li>Working with senior IT staff to manage project implementation and back-end integrations.</li>
<li>Serves as communications liaison within IT and between IT and the business for releases, outages and critical business impacting situations ensuring that communications are timely, accurate and relevant.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>University degree or college diploma in information technology.</li>
<li>Strong analytical thinking, troubleshooting and problem solving skills within complex enterprise systems.</li>
<li>Must be solutions-oriented and have exceptional written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to write simple to complex queries in Microsoft SQL strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Must be team oriented and have good customer service/communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure, and adjust to changing priorities, circumstances and direction required.</li>
<li>Ability to learn and utilize new applications and software systems effectively and efficiently.</li>
<li>Effective performance with minimum supervision.</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive Wage</li>
<li>Medical, Dental and Vision Insurance</li>
<li>Disability (Short and Long-Term Disability)</li>
<li>Life Insurance</li>
<li>401(k)</li>
<li>Paid Time Off</li>
</ul>
