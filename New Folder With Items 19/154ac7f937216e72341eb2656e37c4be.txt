<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>In this key position the Visual Supervisor is primarily responsible for every aspect of the Living Spaces Visual SOP and secondarily, in partnership with the Logistics Supervisor, for every aspect of Living Spaces Brand SOP. The ultimate goal for the Visual Supervisor is to ensure world-class showroom presentations that are current and inspire guests by delivering the best possible Brand Experience in the store. This is accomplished by building and developing team members through the execution of Foundational Excellence (SOP, Talent, and Leadership).
