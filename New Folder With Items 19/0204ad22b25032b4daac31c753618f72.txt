<h2>Software Team Lead - Carrier Applications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div><b>SOFTWARE TEAM LEAD - CARRIER APPLICATIONS</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>REDCOM is looking to add a Senior Staff Engineering Lead to our Carrier Applications (IMSWorkX) Development team. Reporting to the VP of Engineering, the ideal candidate will have the technical skills and experience to be a major contributor to the design and implementation of applications and the leadership skills to provide day-to-day direction within the team developing the REDCOM IMSWorkX products.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Guide product definition, design and development activities across the full range of the REDCOM IMSWorkX products.</li>
<li>Participate in engineering projects as a senior engineer or project lead, including responsibility to plan the project, execute the plan, and report and manage deviations from plan to engineering management.</li>
<li>Develop functional requirements based on high level requirements and feature descriptions as specified by Product Management and Sales.</li>
<li>Produce schedules and budgets for assigned product development activities based on resources, work estimates, and business needs.</li>
<li>Provide direct oversight for team members – define and assign tasks, track progress, provide mentoring/coaching, conduct performance reviews.</li>
<li>Coordinate group efforts with other departments and functions including Product Management, Product Validation, Sales, and Customer Service.</li>
<li>Investigate and evaluate technologies and methodologies and apply them within the product development team.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Highly motivated with strong communication, time management, and leadership skills.</li>
<li>Expert level knowledge in one or more of the following: Java, Python, C++, Web Development, PostgreSQL, Linux, IP Networking Protocols.</li>
<li>Strong technical acumen and practical experience designing and developing software solutions.</li>
<li>Produces quality products on time and within budget.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple concurrent projects utilizing internal and external resources.</li>
<li>BS in a related technical discipline OR attainment of the same level of advanced knowledge through a prolonged course of specialized intellectual instruction (e.g., Industry recognized software, engineering, or telecom-related certification OR related university or military program of study)</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Additional Desired Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated experience leading and motivating development teams of varying size through the software development lifecycle.</li>
<li>Experience with Telecommunications protocols; including SIP, SS7, Sigtran</li>
<li>Proficiency with Ansible, Jenkins, SVN, Boost</li>
<li>Experience designing and developing RESTful APIs</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Special Working Conditions</b></div>
<ul>
<li>No special working conditions – work in office environment</li>
<li>Travel on an exception basis only</li>
<li>May require extended work hours to meet project or customer requirements.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Physical Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to work at a computer/computer workstation.</li>
</ul>
</div>
