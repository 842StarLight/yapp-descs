<h2>Administrative Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Under the direction of the Insurance and Billing Manager, provide complex analytical and accounting support to Arthur Ashe Student Health Service Accounting, Insurance, and Information Systems units. Major duties include managing billing for the health center and analyzing and reconciling the Student Health Insurance Plan (SHIP) totaling approximately 80,000 transactions annually, and annual sales exceeding 19 million dollars. Oversee accounts payables functions and customer service. Perform miscellaneous ad hoc projects.
