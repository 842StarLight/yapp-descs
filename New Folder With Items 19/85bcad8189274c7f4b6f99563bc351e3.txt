<h2>Customer Success Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>The Basics:</b><br>
Branch is hiring a Operations Manager to spearhead its Customer Success initiatives across the global organization. In this role, you will partner closely with Branch's VP of Customer Success and Support to understand our business objectives, prioritize them appropriately, and execute against the highest impact areas. You will support Customer Success Managers, Solution Engineers, Support Engineers, and the leaders that oversee these groups.
<br><br>
To be successful in this role, you should have a passion for driving key objectives for the business and turning data-driven insights into action. You should have a deep understanding of Customer Success and how to catalyze impact in post-sales activities. You should excel at balancing strategic planning with execution and be comfortable rolling up your sleeves to build. You have strong analytical chops and leverage them to solve problems. You should love leading complex and matrixed work and pursuing technical innovation to scale our business and drive results. As a key member of the Operations team, you will make a large impact on our rapidly growing global business.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Build processes to support Customer Success within existing and new systems to support the analysis of customer health, product usage, and CSAT data</li>
<li>Partner with Customer Success leadership to define, scope, and execute against quarterly and annual initiatives</li>
<li>Work with leadership to evaluate and implement improvements to account management and renewal processes, specially the vision &amp; strategy, roles &amp; responsibilities, rules of engagement, process flows, system &amp; Salesforce.com requirements, and reporting &amp; tracking</li>
<li>Lead and improve renewal forecasting and health monitoring</li>
<li>Drive operational efficiency by leveraging industry best practices and designing process, tool, and product improvements to scale our high-growth business and improve team productivity</li>
<li>Lead executive analyses and deliverables</li>
</ul>
<b>What We're Looking For:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of strategy &amp; operations experience such sales ops, customer success ops, or strategy consulting</li>
<li>Experience in Customer Success, specifically supporting renewals or account management functions</li>
<li>Passion for driving cross-functional projects end-to-end</li>
<li>Clear, concise and effective communication skills, both written and verbal. Ability to communicate the right level of information to executives and cross-functional teams at the right cadence</li>
<li>Self-starting attitude with the ability to multitask</li>
<li>Strong initiative and ability to work in a self-directed environment with a "can do" attitude and growth mindset</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to negotiate priorities across organizations</li>
</ul>
<b>Nice to Haves:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience as a Customer Success Manager at a Enterprise Saas company</li>
<li>Familiarity with Salesforce Einstein, Salesforce Service Cloud, Distribution Engine, and other Saas Sales Tools</li>
<li>Salesforce administration skills</li>
<li>Experience scoping technical specifications for developers</li>
<li>Deep analytics expertise</li>
</ul>
