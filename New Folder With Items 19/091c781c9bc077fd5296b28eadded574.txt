<h2>Manager, Forecasting & Competitive Intelligence</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Provide forecasting support within U.S. Pharma Commercial Insights based group on industry-best practices, using industry-standard tools for enhanced efficiency and reliability, and in close collaboration with Finance, Market Intelligence, and other functions. In addition to Forecasting duties, plan, execute, and deliver strategic and tactical Competitive Intelligence (CI) monitoring for Classic and Established Products (CEP). This role requires strong methodological and conceptual skills
</p>
<ul>
<li>Establish, document, and maintain USP Forecast/ Latest Estimate, Long-Range Forecast, as well as Consensus forecast and Commercial forecast
</li>
<li>Maintain SKU level forecasts for all brands across portfolio (including Puerto Rico)
</li>
<li>Support the Core Commercial Cycle (CCC) through the monthly LE process and CERPS loads
</li>
<li>Ensure accuracy of forecasts through industry standard accuracy and bias metrics; identify corrective actions as appropriate
</li>
<li>Articulate and document assumptions and risks in suitable repository and for select stakeholder information and alignment; lead forecast and projection efforts for launch planning
</li>
<li>Support US financial submissions and quarterly earnings call, as needed
</li>
<li>Collaborate with other staff within the Forecasting &amp; Marketing Mix Analytics function in modeling, visualizing, and pressure-testing scenarios, projections, and assumptions
</li>
<li>Collaborate and partner effectively with various stakeholders across the enterprise, including Marketing, Sales, Payer, Digital, Finance, and Technology teams; respond to senior management requests
</li>
<li>Utilize systematic processes to gather and summarize competitive information that is relevant to CEP
</li>
<li>Manage third party agencies/projects to source relevant CI
</li>
</ul>
