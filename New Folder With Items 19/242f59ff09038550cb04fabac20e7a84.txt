<div>Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Perform environmental, reliability and vibration testing per the applicable standard, test plan, or other requirements.</li>
<li>Accurately document testing results.</li>
<li>Maintain high standards of quality and safety.</li>
<li>Read and understand requirements for the testing to be performed.</li>
<li>Confirm the calibration status of all internal test equipment. Perform calibration of equipment.</li>
<li>Act as a liaison with clients during witnessed testing.</li>
<li>Understand and take action based on changes to test standards.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot test failures to determine causes.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
Requirements:<br>
<ul>
<li>Electronics engineering technology degree or equivalent directly-related work or military experience</li>
<li>Experience with PC hardware and software</li>
<li>Ability to read and follow standards, procedures, and test plans</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
<li>Self motivated</li>
<li>Good written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Experience with electronic test equipment such as oscilloscopes and spectrum analyzers.</li>
</ul>
