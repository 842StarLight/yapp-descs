<p>Mitek is hiring an Accounts Receivable Supervisor who will be responsible for the supervision of the day-to-day activities, and staff, related to processing accounts receivables, preparing account reconciliations and working with customers to resolve billing and collection issues.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you will do</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Day-to-day management of Order to Cash functions (billing, invoicing, cash application and collections)</li>
<li>
Review new customer contracts and set-up Sales Orders for invoicing/revenue recognition schedules</li>
<li>
Maintain relationships with key customers regarding accounts receivables and related collections.</li>
<li>
Ensure timeliness of customer cash collections</li>
<li>
Investigate variances and assist customers with billing questions</li>
<li>
Participate in month end close, including preparation and review of journal entries, reconciliations and variance analysis</li>
<li>
Collaborate with cross functional teams including Sales, Customer Support, Legal, etc. to resolve any issues that may arise</li>
<li>
Maintain the Order to Cash internal control environment</li>
<li>
Review current processes and workflows and make recommendations as needed to ensure more efficient and productive results</li>
<li>
Special projects and tasks as directed</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who you are</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Positive, hands-on supervisor, with a get-it-done attitude, and the capability to provide thought leadership as the business grows in headcount, revenue and geographical scope</li>
<li>
Detail oriented with excellent follow up skills</li>
<li>
Clear verbal and written communicator, with professional verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills with experience building strong relationships with internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Strong organizational and time management skills</li>
</ul>
