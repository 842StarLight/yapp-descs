<h2>Design Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>MiTek</b><b>®</b><b> USA Inc., a subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway, Inc</b>. is the world's leading supplier of state-of-the-art, engineered connector products, engineering services, and computer-driven machinery for the building component industry. We provide the industry's most advanced, most comprehensive, and most innovative software for design and manufacturing. Our products and services are backed up by a team of industry professionals who are dedicated to one goal – making our customers successful.</div>
<div></div>
<div>We are seeking a <b>Design Engineer</b> whose responsibilities would include design, testing and analysis of structural connectors to transfer seismic, high-wind and gravity forces for wood, steel and concrete components.</div>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist Engineering Manager with development, testing, and analysis of new products and product revisions.</li>
<li>Perform product design calculations for custom wood-to-wood, wood-to-steel, and wood-to-concrete connections following standard design procedures and applicable design standards and building codes.</li>
<li>Provide CAD operators with relevant product information for drawings including specifications, tolerances, dimensions and materials to be used to prepare detailed drawings and details for products.</li>
<li>Work with Customer Service Department to respond to technical questions from customers, sales, buidling officials, and outside engineers/architects regarding standard and custom connectors to meet their requirements.</li>
<li>Exercise good judgement in selection of designs to meet customer requirements within procedural guidelines.</li>
<li>Conduct technical research, prepare technical bulletins and communicate technical information as required.</li>
<li>Update design calculations and documents to comply with the latest building codes as required for national product evaluation reports (ICC-ES) and regional product approvals (LA City, Florida, etc.)</li>
<li>Analyze test reports and data from in-house testing and independent labs.</li>
<li>Provide assistance to Quality Assurance as required.</li>
<li>Performs other duties and projects requested by the Engineering Manager as required.</li>
<li>Attendance at work during the regularly scheduled shift is an essential duty. This includes, but is not limited to the ability to be present at work, arrive on time and be at the work station ready for work at the start of the shift.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Experience<br>
<p><b>SKILLS &amp; ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Education: </b>Bachelor's Degree (four year college or technical school)</li>
<li>
<b>Experience: </b>0-1+ years of experience</li>
<li>
<b>Computer Skills: </b>MS Office suite of products.</li>
<li>
<b>Other Requirements: </b>College degree in Civil or Structural Engineering field from an ABET accredited school. E.I.T. Certification required. P.E. certification a plus. Strong skills in algebra, trigonometry, physics, statistics and strength of materials is required.</li>
</ul>
