<h2>Marketing Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Brandfolder is seeking an organized, dependable, enthusiastic marketing intern to join our growing team. As an intern to the marketing team, you will make an impact in several areas, including product marketing and content. Tasks range from working with our product, sales, and marketing teams to effectively understand our product’s features to crafting sales enablement materials and creating public-facing content that drives demand.</p>
<p>
A highly motivated individual is key for this role. We value ideas and creativity, as well as someone who is not afraid to learn from multiple cross-functional leaders. This a great opportunity for anyone looking to begin a career in the marketing space and get their hands on a bit of everything! This isn’t an only grunt-work-grabbing-coffee type of internship.</p>
<p><b>
This internship will mainly focus on the following:</b></p>
<p>
 <b>Product Marketing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze competitive landscape and stay afloat of industry trends</li>
<li>
Assist in the go-to-market execution of new feature releases</li>
<li>
Ensure sales and customers are aware of and understand new features</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p><b>
Content</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create sales enablement battle cards, one-pagers, demo scripts, videos, sales slides, blogs, emails, and more</li>
<li>
Work with the product marketing manager and community marketing manager to select and craft client success stories</li>
<li>
Work with a video producer and own the creation and development of new feature video series</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p><b>
Content Support</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Newsletter content calendar and project management</li>
<li>
Post all blogs, updates, etc to appropriate channels</li>
<li>
Work with creative for design elements needed</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div>
