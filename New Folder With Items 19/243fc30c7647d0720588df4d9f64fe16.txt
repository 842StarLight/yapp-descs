At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>

Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p><b>
Wells Fargo Technology</b> sets IT strategy; enhances the design, development, and operations of our systems; optimizes the Wells Fargo infrastructure footprint; provides information security; and enables continuous banking access through in-store, online, ATM, and other channels to Wells Fargo’s more than 70 million global customers.</p>
<p>
This position is for a senior Tech Lead role in an FTE development capacity to support Account Management suite of applications around New Account Opening and Account Maintenance capability/business function. Specifically, this position supports the critical regulatory space of New account opening and Maintenance capabilities related to Brokerage accounts. An understanding of the client experience related to new account opening and maintenance, plus front and back office experiences needs is required to be successful.</p>
<p>
This role will also help in leading transformation initiatives around New Account Opening and Account Maintenance to enable and provide a faster, simpler and efficient user experience by leveraging Technology stack such as Angular JS, Web APIs, Business Process Automation, Events driven architecture and Agile Development methodology.</p>
<p>
This position supports a quarterly production release schedule and needs to be familiar with release management principles. This application area fully adheres to Wells Fargo SDLC standards and rigorous secure software development practices. A successful candidate must be able to partner with analysts, architects and other IT teams to assess highly complex business requirements and develop technical designs which meet the requirements while reducing technology maintenance, scale to accommodate all WFA Sales Channels, and maximize opportunities for configuration. A familiarity with Service Oriented Architecture is required. The ability to successfully delegate, coordinate and communicate to offshore development staff is required. This position must provide leadership to less experienced staff to accommodate and adhere to WF Technology Remediation Program requirements.</p>
