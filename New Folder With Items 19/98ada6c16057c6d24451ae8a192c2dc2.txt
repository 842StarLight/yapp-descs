<h2>Assistant Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Maximizes the collection of rents and monies due while maintaining resident records and property financial data. Assists the Property Manager in managing and maintaining the community.
<br>Essential Functions
<br><ul>
<li>Facilitates aspects of the apartment leasing process.</li>
<li>Responsible for administrative duties pertaining to general correspondence and resident files.</li>
<li>Assists in receipt and deposit of resident rents and fees.</li>
<li>Assists in preparation of property invoices for payment.</li>
<li>Assists the Property Manager in the oversight of eviction process.</li>
<li>Directs the work of staff at site(s).</li>
<li>Assists in maintaining daily time records for the office staff.</li>
<li>Responsible for processing recertifications/renewals as directed by Property Manager.</li>
<li>Conducts recertification interviews with residents.</li>
<li>Monitors residential rent collections (and vouchers) via Yardi reports, and keeps the Property Manager current with regard to any existing or potential problems.</li>
<li>Prepares purchase orders, per company policy, as needed.</li>
<li>Produces required reports for the property, utilizing company systems.</li>
<li>Promotes positive resident relations. Responds to resident issues and complaints.</li>
<li>Demonstrates respect and regard for the dignity of all residents, families, visitors, vendors and fellow employees to ensure a professional, responsible, and courteous environment.</li>
<li>Communicates effectively with internal and external customers with respect of differences in cultures, values, beliefs and ages, utilizing interpreters.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
Job Requirements
<br><ul>
<li>High School diploma or GED equivalent required. College education preferred.</li>
<li>1-3 years property management experience preferred.</li>
<li>Software/ Hardware: Proficiency in Microsoft Office is required. Knowledge of Yardi preferred.</li>
</ul>
