<h2>Vehicle Marketing Specialist (Detroit)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><ul>
<p>Tired of working in an office for a flat salary or an hourly wage? Do you thrive when you’re working on your own without someone watching over your shoulder throughout the day? Do you enjoy being outdoors? Then come join one of the biggest providers of on-the-lot services in the automotive industry!</p>
<p>The Vehicle Marketing Specialist is a unique position that blends photography and customer service at automotive dealerships that have contracted with AutoUpLink Tech for marketing content creation services. You will visit assigned, client sites multiple times per week to photograph vehicles to create content that contracted customers use to advertise vehicles to shoppers. The Content Creation team you will work in are service delivery experts that work with customers in a local geographic area.</p>
<p>With continued growth, we have found the need to locate someone who can deliver on-the-lot services to contracted clients. They will have the ability to grow and take on more as they prove themselves and hopefully will bring some new best practices we can implement. The ideal candidate will have 2 - 3 years photography experience and a working knowledge of automotive retail vehicle prep and listings online; however, all candidates with relevant skills and "mojo" will be considered! Salary will be commensurate with experience, including a super competitive benefits package.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About Us</b></h3>
<p>AutoUpLink Tech provides automotive retailers with applications and tech-enabled services, with a focus on inventory management and merchandizing, that allow retailers to interact with their customers throughout the shopper lifecycle from initial interest to vehicle ownership. Simply put, we bring people together around vehicles.</p>
<p>Our team includes brilliant people who share the goal to ethically design technology solutions that address customer needs in new and innovative ways. It’s exciting stuff, and you can be part of it. Work with amazing people doing amazing things. You’ll learn heaps and be challenged daily. Bring passion and dedication to your job and there’s no telling what you could accomplish.</p>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Visit client dealership locations daily to perform contracted services for customers</li>
<li>Photograph vehicles at client sites using a company-provided camera</li>
<li>Capture video of vehicles</li>
<li>Print window labels (i.e. window stickers and Buyer's Guides) and apply them to vehicles</li>
<li>Interact with dealership personnel during visits to ensure customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Synchronize (i.e. upload) the captured photos or video clips to company's cloud environment using company software</li>
</ul>
