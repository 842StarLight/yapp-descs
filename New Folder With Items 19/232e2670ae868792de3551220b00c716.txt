<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
As a Partner Engineer, you will contribute excellent communication and organizational skills and experience in cloud platform and infrastructure products, software development cycle, and SQL-based relational databases, bridging the gap between our customer’s needs and our partner’s ability to deliver on them.<br>
<br>
In this role, you will work closely with Business Development and Sales managers to grow and support the partner ecosystem. You understand the technical capabilities of our partners and are in a position to recommend the right partner to our customers. You will also contribute to customer adoption of Google Cloud by supporting partners as they illustrate how Google Cloud services can help customers innovate, grow, and bring their services to market faster. Additionally, you will help partners succeed in the migration and deployment phase, assuring they have full access to Google Cloud’s artifacts, tools, templates, and best practices.<br>
<br>
Google Cloud helps businesses and organizations empower their employees, serve their customers, and build what’s next – all with technology built in the cloud. Our products are engineered for security, reliability, and scalability, running infrastructure to applications, devices and hardware. And our teams are dedicated to helping our customers – developers, small and large businesses, educational institutions and government agencies – see the benefits of our technology come to life.</p>
<p>
From startups building their first app to billion-user consumer companies crunching big data, the Google Cloud Platform gives developers and businesses the most advanced data and analytics solution on the market, along with Google's secure, high performance infrastructure. Our team of technical account managers and software engineers work with partners across all functions to develop solutions that harness Google's planet-wide platform to solve the world's most challenging problems.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Recommend integration strategies, enterprise architectures, platforms, and application infrastructure required to successfully implement a complete solution on Google Cloud.
</li>
<li>Manage technical relationship with our partners and empowering them to drive a successful pilot or proof of concept.</li>
<li>Work with the team to identify and qualify partnership opportunities.</li>
<li>Support partners drive toward customer acceptance of the technical proposal, leading to a commercial proposal and eventual commercial agreement.</li>
<li>Work directly with partners during the migration phase to assure they have all the tools necessary to deliver a successful deployment.</li>
</ul>
