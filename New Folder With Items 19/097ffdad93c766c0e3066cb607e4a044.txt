<p>Overview</p>
<p>SitelogIQ was established to meet the need for a nationwide facility services company offering the full range of design, engineering, construction, management, energy and lighting solutions. With a combined history that spans more than 25 years, we bring the strength of several companies joining together to meet the growing needs of our clients. <p><b>Application Engineer - LED Lighting &amp; Building Controls</b></p>
<p>The Application Engineer will work as the primary liaison for the sales team and management to provide technical support for customers throughout the sales process. Additionally, the Application Engineer will support installation, provide sales and technical feedback to the related products and services, and support installation and customer satisfaction post-sale activities. All proposal and project management tasks required from beginning to end of the sales process.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities of Application Engineer: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Determine customer’s specific technical requirements and product feature needs.</li>
<li>Participate in site-surveys, pre-bid and pre-construction meetings, when needed; support installation activities.</li>
<li>Assist in determining customer’s project scope and definition of work.</li>
<li>Generate submittal packages (product data sheets; bill of materials; standard and customer drawings; layout and connection detail drawings; return on investment analysis; etc.).</li>
<li>Create standard drawings for typical product applications.</li>
<li>Generate quotes/detailed proposals.</li>
<li>Maintain all internal documentation and systems related to the audit and proposal process.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements &amp; Qualifications for Application Engineer: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Engineering required, preferably in Electrical engineering or Mechanical engineering.</li>
<li>Experience performing sales application engineering for the commercial lighting industry and/or lighting controls industries.</li>
<li>Proficient ability to use AutoCAD software.</li>
<li>Experience providing quotes and performing audits.</li>
<li>Understanding of project scheduling, task definition and effort estimation.</li>
<li>Ability to take project from concept to completion.</li>
<li>Strong design evaluation, documentation writing skills, problem solving skills, and attention to detail.</li>
<li>Will be expected to go above and beyond assigned tasks to improve the company and increase client satisfaction.</li>
<li>Travel will be required for site assessments.</li>
</ul>
