<h2>Marketing Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Marketing Intern</p>
<p>*Job Description:</p>
<p>Description</p>
<p>Intern is responsible for assisting management and staff with marketing initiatives such as direct mail &amp; email campaigns, database &amp; CRM management, creating mailing lists, preparation of collateral, social media postings and website updates.</p>
<p>Compensation:<br>Will receive a starting stipend of $10.00 per hour and an expectation of between 10-20 hours per week. Flexible hours available with prior approval of management.</p>
<p>*Qualifications:</p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<p>To be considered for this intern position the student must be enrolled as a full-time or part-time student in the marketing, sales, advertising, graphic design or related discipline.</p>
<p>Required Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Majoring in Marketing, Sales, Advertising, graphic design or Public Relations</li>
<li>Desires a hands-on experience from a growing marketing initiative</li>
<li>Familiar with Microsoft Operating software (XP, MS 7, MS Office, etc.).</li>
<li>Social media: LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, YouTube, Google Analytics</li>
<li>Some past leadership experience</li>
<li>Community and/or campus involvement</li>
<li>Strong administrative skills including standard software applications</li>
<li>Ability to adapt to changing environment</li>
<li>A quick learner and self starter</li>
<li>A multitasker that can support multiple priorities</li>
<li>Strong analytical &amp; problem solving skills</li>
<li>Clear communicator and record keeper</li>
<li>Good teamwork and interpersonal skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Types: Part-time, Internship</p>
</div>
