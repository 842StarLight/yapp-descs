<h2>Senior Application Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>The Senior Application Engineer is responsible for delivering industry-leading embedded software solutions for aerospace, military, automotive, and industrial customers. The AE is a critical customer-facing team member that will lead engineering teams to realize robust model-based system and software solutions using the SCADE product line. The AE must integrate with an elite technical sales team to achieve competitive solutions for current and prospective customers. The job involves travelling extensively to customers and industry conferences to deliver training, technical workshops, and consulting.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul><li>Consult at customer sites with tact and professionalism, delivering safe, viable, and robust embedded software solutions using SCADE</li></ul>
<ul><li>Take initiative to understand customer needs, requirements, and workflows</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead in coordinating and executing all technical activities throughout the sales opportunity such as customer meetings and product presentations, demonstrations and evaluations
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create and demonstrate embedded software solutions to streamline customer workflows</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in internal corporate initiatives to further enhance ANSYS products, presales/sales enablement and professional growth; actively participate in the development and mentoring of team members
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for articulating ANSYS’ value proposition which may encompass its entire suite of products; the presentations must affirm the sales message, differentiate ANSYS, and leave a strong and positive impression to audiences which can include senior company executives
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Instruct intermediate and/or advanced training classes on the usage of ANSYS products</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create elegant and useful solutions to solve complex problems by working across various teams and understanding the needs of our customers</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS</b></div>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in engineering or other technical discipline</li></ul>
<ul><li>A minimum of 5 years of experience in an engineering software environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with model-based software design technology</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with delivering software complying with international safety standards such as ISO 26262, RTCA DO-178B, Def Stan. 0055, IEC 61508, EN 50128, FDA 510(k), and DOD 2167A</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills, with the ability to facilitate and conduct webinars and presentations to customers
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong programming skills in C and embedded software are required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to interact effectively with senior business managers and C-level executives</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated skills in writing and presenting in fluent English</li></ul>
<ul><li>Logical problem-solving approach and the ability to clearly communicate the situation and proposed solution to the customer</li></ul>
<ul><li>Projects a professional image and strong business acumen during customer interactions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Possesses a sense of urgency, strong organizational and follow up skills</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></div>
<ul><li>MS or PhD degree preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>A minimum of 6 years of experience in an engineering software environment preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have passion and drive for personal technical excellence and high performance</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have customer service aptitude and maintain customer focus</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to travel up to 50%</li></ul>
