<p><b><i>
This is a work-from-home opportunity.</i></b></p>

<br>
<p><b>
Teleprospectors</b> contact businesses on behalf of hotels to inquire about guest room and meeting room needs. The information is recorded on a software program, and the leads are sent to the hotels for follow-up. Teleprospectors need to feel comfortable with “cold calls,” be skilled at getting through the gatekeeper and have access to a quiet home office environment during weekday business hours. A hotel sales background is preferred. <b>Bi-Lingual in Spanish and English is a PLUS!</b></p>
