<h2>Enterprise Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Title: </b>Sales Representative<br>
<b>Reports to: </b>Director, Enterprise Sales<br>
<b>Location: </b>REMOTE<br>
<br>
REC Solar is a trusted provider of high-quality solar and energy-saving solutions to a wide range of commercial customers nationwide, including businesses, governments and non-profits. The company provides financed end-to-end service offerings to help its customers reduce energy costs, decrease greenhouse gas emissions and demonstrate corporate sustainability. As a Duke Renewable Ventures portfolio company, REC has access to an unprecedented depth of expertise and partners that we can use to positively impact our customer’s energy strategies.<br>
<br>
Our team has unmatched expertise in helping customers maximize their solar investments. Their skillsets, passion and commitment to our clients sets us apart. We are looking for team members that want to make a difference at REC Solar, for our customers and in the world.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
<br>
As an Enterprise Sales Representative, you are the face of REC Solar to the largest companies in the country. You are responsible for developing relationships and energy opportunities for distributed generation solutions within the Fortune 1000 sector. We believe in utilizing a consultative sales approach to understand our customer’s needs so that we develop the proper solution to meet those needs.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
The successful candidate will join the Enterprise account team which grew revenue by &gt;300% in 2018 and is on track to exceed quota again in 2019. You will be expected to handle accounts across the country and there will be some travel required for customer meetings and conferences. The ideal candidate will be motivated, connected, organized, persistent, ethical and able to understand and present the technical and financial intricacies of solar and distributed generation solutions.</p>
<br>
<div>
<br>
Our compensation and incentive plan is highly competitive. Our workplace is casual yet hard-working. We have offices in Hawaii, Petaluma, San Luis Obispo and multiple people work remotely throughout the country.<br>
<br>
<b>Our ideal Sales Representative will</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Have demonstrated success meeting and exceeding annual quotas in excess of $20M selling direct specifically assigned accounts within the Fortune 1000</li>
<li>Develop within the Named Accounts list a steady pipeline of repeatable business</li>
<li>Have demonstrated success selling financed solutions</li>
<li>Must be able to demonstrate having won business using research and relationship derived insight to create a differentiated solution.</li>
<li>Must be able to demonstrate having maintained a trusted partner status to VP level and higher decision makers within the Fortune 1000. The ideal candidate will operate at this level within their client organizations.</li>
<li>Be able to articulate and deliver the full economic, strategic, and environmental value represented by the REC &amp; Duke Energy partnerships</li>
<li>Lead a team of subject matter experts in order to sell large scale on-site generation projects across multiple locations under a single contract.</li>
<li>Must have demonstrated experience utilizing a formal large account selling methodology</li>
<li>Must be able to demonstrate fluency with financial business metrics</li>
<li>Must be able to demonstrate a highly analytical thought process</li>
<li>Must be able to work in a team-selling environment, though with limited direct supervision.</li>
<li>Make ethical decisions while maintaining integrity on behalf of the best interest of the company at all times.</li>
<li>Proficiently perform all required sales Activities including sales prospecting, qualifying, account management, site evaluations, proposals, presentations, networking, relationship building, partnering, territory management, managing use of resources, etc.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to ensure their future success though active pipeline management</li>
<li>Diligently adhere to company standards regarding accurate CRM management (i.e. Salesforce: Input, update and manage leads, accounts and opportunities)</li>
</ul>
