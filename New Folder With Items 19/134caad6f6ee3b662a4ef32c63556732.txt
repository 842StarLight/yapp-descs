<p><b>Demand Planner</b></p>
<p>
At ResMed (NYSE: RMD, ASX: RMD) we pioneer innovative solutions that treat and keep people out of the hospital, empowering them to live healthier, higher-quality lives. Our cloud-connected medical devices transform care for people with sleep apnea, COPD and other chronic diseases. Our comprehensive out-of-hospital software platforms support the professionals and caregivers who help people stay healthy in the home or care setting of their choice. By enabling better care, we improve quality of life, reduce the impact of chronic disease and lower costs for consumers and healthcare systems in more than 120 countries. To learn more, visit ResMed.com and follow @ResMed.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Let's talk about the team and you:</b><br>
In this role, you'll be responsible for the management of the forecasting process for the North America region. You'll create all item level forecasts used in production. You'll participate in managing resultant inventory levels, including; safety stocks, slow and obsolete stock and inventory levels required to mitigate risk through new product introductions. You'll work directly with Product &amp; Sales Managers to ensure integrity of forecasts to marketing plans, and financial targets. You'll facilitate the reporting of forecast information and market drivers on a monthly basis through monthly forecast review meeting to aid in managing the Supply Chain.<br>
<br>
You will also monitor and report on forecast accuracy, product availability, on time delivery, inventory status and the phase-in/phase-out of products. This information is vital to monitoring performance against budget and sales targets.<br>
<br>
This role will also have the opportunity to be involved in projects managing business changes from time-to-time as these situations arise.<br>
<br>
<b>Let's talk about Responsibilities:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Utilizes historical data, statistical software, and marketing intelligence to develop product forecasts.</li>
<li>
Maintains forecasts and planning parameters in ERP system.
</li>
<li>Leads monthly forecasting meetings with Global and Local Product Managers and other Stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Ensures forecasts are updated and entered into Oracle Demand Cloud.
</li>
<li>Works with Product Managers to generate forecasts for new products and corresponding cannibalization plans. Ensures these are based on a sound framework that utilizes information obtained through in-depth discussions with regional Product Managers and Cross-Functional teams.
</li>
<li>Manages and publishes KPIs for forecasting process.
</li>
<li>Reviews on hand value inventory and slow-moving-and-obsolete-stock (SLOB) and highlights possible items to obsolete. Works with cross functional teams to develop strategies to minimize or clear stock.</li>
<li>
Ensures all activities are carried out in compliance with established quality control policies and work instructions.</li>
</ul>
