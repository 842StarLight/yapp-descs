<div>HDR currently has an opening for a Sr. GIS Technician with the technical skills and desire to help support our nationally recognized Freight Rail Group.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Based in our Atlanta, GA office, the Sr. GIS Technician will perform assignments using standard industry acceptable techniques and procedures. The candidate should have a strong academic background along with an initiative to perform Geospatial tasks related to Freight Rail. Candidate must be adaptable to conditions including fast-paced and deadline driven work environment.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
The primary duties include assisting in the analysis and defense of special assessments using GIS and other technical tools. Providing engineering and surveying services for sales and acquisitions of real estate property and ensures that safety and operating efficiency is not jeopardized by construction activity of others within or along the railroad right of way. Prepares and/or reviews surveys, plats, and legal descriptions of real estate property to be sold or acquired. Researches public and corporate land records to identify owners and source of title. Recognizes and resolves boundary line discrepancies. Works directly with outside agencies and their engineering representatives to resolve property issues.</div>
