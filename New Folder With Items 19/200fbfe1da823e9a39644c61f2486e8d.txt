<h2>Director of Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<p><b>Summary: </b>Responsible for planning staffing, directing, and leading the sales department relative to specific revenue production, and meeting or exceeding revenue objectives for occupancy, average daily rate (ADR) and NETADR by segment, and ancillary spending. The Director of Sales is a “hands-on” manager, actively involved on a daily basis in securing, qualifying and following up on leads to book specific business. Eligible for incentive bonusprogram.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Description</b>: The DOS is the department head of the sales department. He/she is in charge of training and supervising of the hotels sales team. Works closely with the General Manager and Marketing to determine appropriate sales strategies in all market segments towards maximizing the hotel revenues. Also responsible maximizing occupancy and revenue for conferences and banquets in in balance with transient revenue. Creates lucrative sales targets and networks business relations to various business, social, and affiliation group clients. Observes the market, identifies trends and monitors the main competitors and industry leaders that emulate our model. Based on current information, the DOS revises the budget into accurate forecasts. Manages key accounts and oversees the account management in all activities.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Duties:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Know your clients, i.e. what business are they in, what is their corporate culture, what went wrong/right at their last meeting (wherever it may have been held), look at their mission statement.</li>
<li>Greet each guest, enthusiastically and with a smile to create a friendly positive experience.</li>
<li>Conforms to AAA Four Diamond Standards.</li>
<li>Creating and managing Annual Strategic Plan</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Managing lead sources to ensure quantity and quality of leads</li>
<li>Working closely with marketing consultants to develop lead generation strategies</li>
<li>Seeking new clients through strategic outbound sales efforts</li>
<li>Conducting sales calls to key accounts or major prospects</li>
<li>Monitoring internal and external sales activities/performance to ensure revenue goals meet or exceed established plan and accurately reporting variance/projections monthly</li>
<li>Weighing the value of each piece of business against hotel objectives</li>
<li>Using Revenue Management resources to help make informed decisions and maximize revenue</li>
<li>Developing and maintaining positive relationships with peers, competitors and brand partners</li>
<li>Adjusting strategy based on competitive market and market segment knowledge</li>
<li>Ensuring that the property is maximizing the use of all company, brand and local programs</li>
<li>Recruiting the best possible sales team and reducing turnover through coaching, counseling and training</li>
<li>Providing support and coaching for team members to drive high levels of performance, job satisfaction, and personal growth</li>
<li>Training and empowering sales team members to exercise good judgment to make profitable business decisions</li>
<li>Ensuring that the hotel sales team is effectively using the tools available</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of forecasting sales and interpreting/analyzing results to plan and conducting budgetary analysis, including pricing review and yield management</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to understand customer requirements and translating these into sales solutions</li>
<li>Track record of leading and inspiring teams to meet or exceed sales goals</li>
<li>Point out opportunities and make suggestions for food and beverage, and other programs and services offered</li>
</ul></div>
<ul>
<li>Scheduling staff as needed, including weekend coverage, event coverage, and ability for clients to reach staff with messages and/or emails after normal business hours so that a sense of urgency and response can be maintained</li>
<li>Coordinate Corporate Outreach Sales Effort</li>
<li>Manage and develop Preferred Corporate Rate Accounts</li>
<li>Oversee/Supervise Social Food and Beverage Sales Segments</li>
<li>Maintain/be active in the community and with professional organizations including local Chambers of Commerce</li>
<li>Know local/geographical competition thoroughly</li>
<li>Assist in the annual Marketing Plan</li>
<li>Participate in Marketing Committee and Revenue Maximization meetings</li>
<li>Design Special Offers for various segments</li>
<li>Coordinate and assist with Reservations Dept.</li>
<li>Support company Green Efforts and Safety Committee</li>
<li>Resolve Client/Guest Issues</li>
<li>Perform all other duties as assigned by management.</li>
<li>Be fully aware of and comply with Inn’s policies and procedures as identified in the procedure manual and handbook.</li>
<li>Constantly monitor staff performance in all phases of service and job functions, ensuring that all procedures are carried out.</li>
<li>Interview, recommend hiring, schedule, train, develop, empower, coach and counsel, resolve problems, provide open communication, recommend and conduct performance and salary reviews, recommend discipline and termination, as appropriate.</li>
<li>Conduct training and have regular meetings to increase staff performance and knowledge.</li>
</ul>
