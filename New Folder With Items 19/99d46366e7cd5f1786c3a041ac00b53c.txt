<h2>Mortgage Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>A Mortgage company is seeking an experienced and detail-oriented Junior Loan Officer, Mortgage processor, Loan Officer, Mortgage Underwriter. Escrow Officer Assistant to join our growing team. In this position, you will act as a liaison between applicants and Real Estate Agents. Your duties will include meeting with applicants, preparing loan proposals, examining active loans, answering Real Estate Agents questions, as well as offering Real Estate Agents best in class service.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will have experience managing different types of loans including home loans, business loans, and individual loans. To succeed in this role, you should demonstrate excellent communication and interpersonal skills.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meet with loan applicants to identify their needs and collect information for loan applications.</li>
<li>Analyze active loan files on a regular basis and recommend solutions to speed up the loan process.</li>
<li>Complete loan contracts and teach clients on policies and regulations.</li>
<li>Interview applicants to define financial eligibility and establish debt payment plans.</li>
<li>Submit loan applications in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Prepare detailed loan proposals.</li>
<li>Reject loan applications and explain deficiencies to applicants.</li>
<li>Respond to applicant’s questions and resolve any loan-related issues.</li>
<li>Operate in compliance with laws and regulations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 3 years’ experience in the mortgage/banking industry.</li>
<li>In-depth knowledge of computers and mortgage/banking software.</li>
<li>Outstanding interpersonal and communication skills.</li>
<li>Customer service oriented with exceptional sales skills.</li>
<li>Relationship building with current and potential clients.</li>
<li>Previous experience as a Junior Loan Officer, Mortgage processor, Loan Officer, Mortgage Underwriter. Escrow Officer Assistant.</li>
</ul>
