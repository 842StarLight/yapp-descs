Role Overview</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Collins Aerospace, Commercial Aviation is looking for a Systems Engineering Architect. Reporting to the Assoc. Director of Engineering, you will be responsible for a wide range of solutions spanning network and software technologies. The candidate will work with customers, engineering management, program management, technical engineering leads across multiple disciplines, and engineering support groups. This is an engineering leadership position with focus on the full lifecycle of communications systems to provide solutions and support for both domestic and international customers. The organizational objective is to successfully design, develop, integrate, and maintain the network and software products that support product line in the connected aircraft arena. Backed by a team of experts in networking and airline cabin solutions, you will be the outward facing point of contact for partner and customer engagements.
</p>
