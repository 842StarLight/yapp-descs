<h2>Food & Beverage Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION: Staff Accountant</b></p>
<p><b>
DEPARTMENT: Finance
</b></p>
<p><b>REPORTS TO: Finance Manager</b></p>
<p><b>
FLSA STATUS: Non-Exempt<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<div>
ASM Global, a leader in venue management for facilities, has an excellent and immediate opening for the Food and Beverage Staff Accountant for ASM Global's Oklahoma City Convention Center {NEW}.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
Include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</div>
<ul>
<li>Maintains fixed asset detail records; performs account analysis.</li>
<li>Assists Accounting Manager in preparation of financial statements.</li>
<li>Reconciles A/R and A/P control accounts to detail aging.</li>
<li>Reconciles cash receipts and disbursements.</li>
<li>Performs monthly closing of sub ledgers.</li>
<li>Inputs journal entries.</li>
<li>Assists A/R and A/P clerks with coding of receipts and disbursements into the general ledger.</li>
<li>Performs account analysis on other general ledger accounts.</li>
<li>Performs any other functions delegated by accounting manager.</li>
<li>Maintains and audits box office ticket sales reports.</li>
<li>Maintains and audits parking sales.</li>
<li>Maintains telephone deposits for events.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Supervisory Responsibilities
</b></div>
<p>This position has minimal supervisory responsibilities other than assisting junior staff accountants, if applicable or other more recently hired staff accountants. Staff Accountant provides assistance to the accounting clerks within the department.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications
</b></p>
<div>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Education and/or Experience
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>B.S. in Accounting or Finance from a four-year college or university</li>
<li>Some part-time or summer internship experience in a position with accounting responsibilities preferred</li>
<li>Or similar combination of education and experience</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Skills and Abilities
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Thorough knowledge of financial and cost accounting</li>
<li>Excellent math skills and attention to details</li>
<li>Effective communication, interpersonal and organizational skills required</li>
<li>Ability to interact with all levels of staff including management and clients</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Computer Skills<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>To perform this job successfully, an individual should have knowledge of spreadsheets and word processing software and be willing to learn and use accounting and financial reporting software.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Other Qualifications
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to function independently with minimal supervision</li>
<br>
</ul>
