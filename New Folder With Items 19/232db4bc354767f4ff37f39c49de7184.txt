<p></p>
<p>Join SAIC and help address the nationâ€™s most pressing and complex challenges. SAIC works with our customers on missions that strengthen security and safety and protect the basic values of our society. We have an opening for a motivated, dedicated and energetic Program Control Analyst (PCA) to support one of our Programs within the Intelligence Community in Annapolis Junction, MD.</p>
<p>The position will report to one of the team's Program Control managers and will be responsible for business and financial functions and deliverables. The successful candidate will support a single large, complex contract, providing reports and deliverables for both internal and external customers. The ability to communicate effectively, both verbally and in writing is important, as well as being able to perform responsibilities with minimal direction and supervision.</p>
<p>Position duties will include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Preparation of financial internal/external reports to include Annual Operating Plan, Monthly Sales Forecasts, EACs/ETCs, WBS setup, charging practice determinations, purchase requisitions, monitoring subcontractor costs, review/approve/track subcontractor invoices, and prepare customer invoice backup documentation.</li>
<li>Interface with contracts, subcontracts, accounting department, accounts payable, accounts receivable, purchasing organizations, pricing department as well as program managers, corporate line management, and external customers.</li>
<li>Candidate will ensure compliance with all SAIC policies, internal controls, disclosed practices and contractual requirements.</li>
<li>Perform analyses and prepare reports in order to ensure that contracts are within negotiated and agreed- upon parameters and cost control guidelines.</li>
<li>Participate in the preparation of budgets and schedules for contract work, and performs and/or assists in financial analyses such as revenue, cost, profit, billed/unbilled, UR, and other variance analysis.</li>
<li>Review Request for Proposals (RFPs) and prepare pricing models.</li>
<li>Ensure adequate funding availability by maintaining accurate records of expenditures, and preparing expenditure projections, and working with Contracts to submit timely requests to the government for additional funding.</li>
<br>
</ul>
