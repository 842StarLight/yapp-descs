<h2>Digital Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Digital Marketing Specialist</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Purpose</b></p>
<p>Assists and coordinates the marketing of the organization's products and/or services.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li> Administers and maintains website content management system (CMS) and makes website update as needed</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manages Google AdWords including ad creation and improving conversation rates</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manages SEO for company websites to ensure favorable search engine rankings and conversion rates</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manages and develops content for company’s social media sites (LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provides analysis of results of search engine rankings, SEM and social media activities and updates marketing dashboards</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develops and oversees delivery of customer and salesforce newsletters. Manages email contact lists.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Writes Product Application Articles</li></ul>
<ul><li> Writes Success Story Articles</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assists in developing press releases &amp; distributes to media sources</li></ul>
<ul><li> Tracks media source pickups</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manages buyer’s guide listings</li></ul>
<ul><li> Digital media content creation including resources for websites video production</li></ul>
<ul><li> In-house Photography of custom chambers and other photos as needed</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assists with other sales &amp; marketing projects as required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Other duties may be assigned as deemed necessary by the supervising manager.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul><li> BS degree in Marketing, Digital media or relevant field</li></ul>
<ul><li> 1-5 years of experience as Digital Media Specialist or Digital Marketing Manager or similar role</li></ul>
<ul><li> Good knowledge of Photoshop, Final Cut Pro, Audacity or other media editing software</li></ul>
<ul><li> 1-5 years' experience with visual communication methods</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge of web design</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with content management</li></ul>
<ul><li> Sense of ownership and pride in your performance and its impact on company’s success</li></ul>
<ul><li> Critical thinker and problem-solving skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Team player</li></ul>
<ul><li> Good time-management skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Great interpersonal and communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Additional qualification in web design or animation is a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li> Working knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript,etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with administering Content Management Systems</li></ul>
<ul><li> Thorough knowledge of Google Ads and Google Analytics</li></ul>
<ul><li> Adobe Photoshop and/or Adobe Creative Suite</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Working Conditions</b></p>
<p>Mostly stationary work with occasional movement throughout the facility to access files, office machinery, etc. while working at a desk and a computer screen for an extended period of time. Employee must be able to manually enter data to the computer system and understand the screen and images that appear. Frequently lifting and carrying up to 10 lbs and occasionally lifting up to 50 lbs. Use of hands to hold, lift and handle materials with occasional climbing, bending and squatting.</p>
