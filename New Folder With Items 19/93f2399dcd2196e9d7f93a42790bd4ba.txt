<p><b>About World Business Lenders (www.wbl.com)</b></p>
<p>
World Business Lenders provides general purpose short-term real estate collateralized commercial loans to a broad customer base comprised of small and medium sized businesses throughout the country. The company operates as a hybrid MCA/commercial lender with a unique business model. With its disciplined approach to underwriting and risk management, WBL is committed to creating a pre-eminent brand that focuses on helping small businesses identify opportunities to grow. As a matter of corporate governance, the company is steadfast in maintaining diversity in its workforce and boardroom, embracing its social responsibility and strengthening its ties to the communities it serves.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
General Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Review and red-line listing agreements, sale contracts, and riders/addendums</li>
<li>Coordinate evictions</li>
<li>Work closely with counsel to pursue deficiency judgments</li>
<li>Responsible for assigning and maintaining relationships with Listing Agents nationwide</li>
<li>Responsible for preparing marketing plans on all homes before they are put on the market, including making list price recommendations</li>
<li>Responsible for preparing and submitting offers for approvals to Senior Management and recommendations for price reductions as necessary</li>
<li>Prepare and edit various correspondence, reports, spreadsheets, presentations, using Microsoft Office software; maintain associated files
</li>
<li>Assist in daily administrative roles and support (preparing and submitting invoices for approval, tracking payment, documenting REO status, etc.)</li>
<li>Performs other duties or projects as requested by manager</li>
<br>
</ul>
