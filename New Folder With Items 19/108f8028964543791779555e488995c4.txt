<h2>Certified Personal Trainer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>About the Position:</b><br>
Under the direction of the Assistant General Manager of Fitness, the Professional Fitness Trainer (PFT) provides evaluation of client status and delivers safe and professional exercise routines that are client based to help client achieve fitness goals. Responsible for achieving personal revenue goals each month while providing the best customer service and hospitality to all members and guests.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
Must meet minimum private training sales of 20 per month and maintain positive client retention
<br>Provide new member fitness consultations and evaluations
<br>Develop individual exercise programs based upon client's abilities, health, and goals
<br>Maintain accurate, current records on all client exercise programs and progress
<br>Accurately record each day work, the actual time worked and client sessions in the appropriate tracking system
<br>Active participation in meetings and training programs. Attend club sponsored developmental or educational seminars and programs
<br>Maintain valid trainer and CPR/AED certification
<br>Promote the company's safety goals and actively work towards ensuring a safe working environment
<br>Actively further corporate objectives and continuously support improvement and positive change
<br>Assist with special club projects, events and promotions
<br><br>
<b>NOTE:</b> This is not necessarily an exhaustive list of all responsibilities, skills, duties, requirements, efforts or working conditions associated with this job
<br><br>
Accredited Certification required.

Excellent customer service attitude and abilities.
<br><br>
Strong interpersonal and communication skills.
<br><br>
Well organized and detail oriented.
<br><br>
Oral Comprehension and Expression --Ability to understand and communicate so others can understand ideas and information.
