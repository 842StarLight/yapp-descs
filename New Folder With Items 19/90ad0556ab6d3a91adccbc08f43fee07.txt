<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
You're invited to join us in this seasoned sales role as you help identify and acquire large business accounts. This is more than just selling; it's about identifying customer needs and effectively selling solutions. We're looking for superior sales, prospecting, funnel and account management skills, but the rewards for a job well done are simply outstanding. Here's a closer look at the position:<ul>
<li>
You call on top levels of organizations to sell T-Mobile products and services</li>
<li>
This means gaining new business through cold-calling, networking and generating leads and referrals</li>
<li>
You'll be recommending wireless solutions and consulting on the utilization of enhanced services and products to drive your customer's productivity</li>
<li>
You'll also leverage your selling skills to assess the needs of your customers as they utilize our value plans</li>
<li>
Once a deal is on the table, it's up to you to negotiate contracts in terms of pricing, commitments, and implementation strategy</li>
<li>
You're also the point person for training new customers, coordinating internal resources, and enabling a smooth transition to the Support team</li>
<li>
To stay at the top of your game, you're continuously updating your knowledge of wireless products, services, industry trends and the marketplace</li>
<li>
As you meet and exceed monthly sales quota objectives, your commissions mean your earning potential is up to you</li>
</ul>
