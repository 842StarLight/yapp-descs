<h2>Trade Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our Marketing Team is expanding! Come join this dynamic team and become part of the Far Niente Family!</p>
<p>Founded in Oakville, California in 1885 and restored in 1979, Far Niente is consistently recognized as a benchmark producer of Napa Valley Chardonnay and Oakville Estate Cabernet Sauvignon. A dedication to winemaking in its highest form, paired with a focus on house style, vineyard ownership, social responsibility and legendary hospitality, has established Far Niente as one of the world's great wine estates.</p>
<p>Over the years, four exceptional wineries have been proudly added to the Far Niente Family of Wineries and Vineyards: Established in 1989, Dolce is the only American winery dedicated to producing a single late harvest wine. Our second Oakville winery, Nickel &amp; Nickel, focuses on 100-percent varietal, single-vineyard wines from some of Napa Valley’s finest vineyard appellations. In 2007, we launched EnRoute, which is committed to crafting focused, textured Pinot Noir from the Russian River Valley. Bella Union is our youngest winery and specializes in creating a single Cabernet Sauvignon from carefully selected Napa Valley vineyards.</p>
<p>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</p>
<ul><li>Serves as primary winery contact with trade organizations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develops and executes marketing programs and market analysis and surveys</li></ul>
<ul><li>In collaboration with President of Sales, develops templates for consistent presentations of our brands. Builds distributor and trade presentations for the sales team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Builds trade equity for the brand through the design and execution of trade strategies including advertising, promotion, public relations, digital marketing and special events and carries each strategy through to final deliverable, including print and digital execution.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develops strong trade communications programs through building and maintaining trade and distributor CRM.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Leads new packaging development for trade markets, in collaboration with production, procurement and creative management teams.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Researches and analyzes market data to provide competitive insights, competitive market sets and positioning for key brands.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manages brand projects, overall budget allocation, prepare and present branded presentations to both internal and external audiences.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Recommends annual trade media advertising plan including objectives, publications, brand presence and strategic creative direction within budgetary constraints.</li></ul>
<ul><li>In collaboration with Senior Manager of Consumer Marketing, manages all outside agency initiatives in support of the brand’s trade business objectives to include promotion agency, digital and advertising agencies if applicable.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with sales, key on and off premise strategic accounts, internal field sales, and distributor personnel, to develop marketing programs and identify opportunities to expand the business in existing channels of trade.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Visits key markets, new product launches, surveys, etc. and report brand and competitive findings as well as any market driven recommendations to VP of Marketing and Communications.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Successfully communicates and work with a large number of key internal groups across the organization on a regular basis.</li></ul>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's Degree, required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>7+ years of experience in a Brand Management / Marketing Management position in the wine industry trade/distributor channel.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Commitment to excellence and high standards.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience managing agencies to create creative point of sale / merchandising materials, which support brand positioning.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven desire and ability to think creatively, coupled with strong analytical and problem-solving skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communications skills; very articulate, strong public speaking and writing skills, strong interpersonal skills, strong cross functional skills, ability to work well within a team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent organizational and project management skills, ability to prioritize, and solid financial acumen.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong PC skills (Windows, Word, Excel, PowerPoint). Strong familiarity of Salesforce Sales Cloud and Marketing Cloud.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Successful candidate will be a very motivated self-starter and be able to work without daily guidance.</li></ul>

<ul><li>Versatility, flexibility, and a willingness to work within constantly changing priorities with enthusiasm</li></ul>
