<h2>VIP Host - Music Hall</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p>
HOB Entertainment, a division of Live Nation Entertainment, offers exceptional club venues nationwide. House of Blues Venues provide several genres of live music, from Rock, Hip Hop, Country, Rock en Espa√±ol, Blues, Jazz, Reggae and Pop. House of Blues venues are also known for their southern-style cuisine, juke-joint inspired atmosphere and the World Famous Gospel Brunch.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
House of Blues opened its doors on Thanksgiving Day in 1992, serving its first meal to the local shelters. Since opening, House of Blues has been committed to giving back to the community through the International House of Blues Foundation.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Entertainment, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
To sell VIP options in advance for every show at House of Blues Houston and to also be present on night of show to up-sell available VIP inventory.</li>
<li>
Serve as VIP host in the Music Hall on night of show to maintain constant communication with staff and clients to ensure that guests receive highly personal service and the highest level of standards at all times. .</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Sell Opera Boxes, bottle service and reserved stools in advance of every show and maintain monthly sales goal.</li>
<li>
Be present at every show to up-sell available VIP inventory to concert patrons.</li>
<li>
Serve as a resource of information for VIP clients regarding their experience within the House of Blues venue.</li>
<li>
Provide assistance for VIP clients regarding the timing of their arrival and concert times.</li>
<li>
Give proper recognition to Foundation Members, Live Nation Executives and VIP clients to instill appreciation for their membership and patronage.</li>
<li>
Coordinate operations on night of shows with Ops Director, servers and security.</li>
<li>
Build strong relationships with Foundation Room members and VIP clients and have the ability to anticipate their needs.</li>
<li>
Give constant observation of Opera Boxes and reserved stools to ensure guests are receiving highly personal service at all times.</li>
<li>
Maintain articulate vocabulary with the ability to adapt and socialize with current audience.</li>
<li>
Maintain exemplary and professional grooming standards.</li>
<li>
Required to occasionally work Special Events that are scheduled throughout the venue</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
Required:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>TABC / Responsible Alcohol Awareness Training Certification or Equivalent</li>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of high end restaurant operations, seating procedures, guest relations, problem solving</li>
<li>
Experience in selling to high-end, VIP clients</li>
<li>
Skill in diplomacy, handling multiple tasks, verbal skills, organizational skills, and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Ability to diffuse possibly volatile situations with tact and diplomacy</li>
<li>
Must have a flexible schedule</li>
<li>
Must be able to work in multi venues</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
Physical Demands/Working Environment:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Working environment is fast-paced, often loud and stressful</li>
<li>
Position requires extended periods of prolonged standing and working on your feet</li>
<li>
Must be able to lift or move up to 25 lbs using proper lifting techniques</li>
</ul>
