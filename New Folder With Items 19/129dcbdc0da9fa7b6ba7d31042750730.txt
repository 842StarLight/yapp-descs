<h2>Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>A large, family owned, metals manufacturing company with locations throughout the Midwest has two immediate openings for Sales Engineers at their Rockford, IL locations.</p>
<p>Working with the Sales team, the Sales Engineer will develop and deliver custom solutions, concepts and applications for customers and support them through the sales flow process.</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Execute on all aspects of a full sales cycle; prospecting, cold calling, developing/fielding RFQ’s, order entry, pricing, delivery and purchase orders.</li>
<li>Develop relationships and earn trust of key decision makers at a variety of levels; Presidents, General Managers, Purchasing Managers, Purchasing Manager, Sales and Engineering.</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal functions, including; Operations, Production Planning, Order Processing, Metallurgy, Customer Service, Purchasing, Supply Base Management team to manage client-specific projects.</li>
<li>Qualify new and existing customers and build strategic relationships with current and prospective clients</li>
<li>Forecast sales goals for customers as a relevant input into sales and capacity planning</li>
<li>Maximize company profit through product and customer strategies aligned with company objectives</li>
<li>Maintain professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops, reviewing professional publications, establishing personal networks and participating in professional societies.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of proven Sales Engineer experience</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree with a concentration in Engineering, Marketing, Promotions, Advertising, Sales, Business Administration or related field preferred.</li>
<li>Experience selling in the aerospace and defense markets a plus</li>
<li>Previous experience in engineering, metallurgy, quality, tooling design/build, and coatings a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience selling machined applications and knowledge of fabricated products a plus</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 3 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
