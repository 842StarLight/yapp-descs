<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p>Workday is the leader in enterprise-class, software-as-a-service (SaaS) Human Capital Management and Financial Management solutions for managing global businesses. As a Workday Senior Business Development Manager for our Professional Services organization, you will be responsible for planning, management, and execution of all Professional Services business development activities in your area. You will be a key team member of the Workday sales team, working alongside the Workday Account Executives, driving Workday Subscription sales in Workday’s Large Enterprise accounts.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You'll use your experience with consulting sales and project delivery to help prospects put together a plan for a successful project, including resources, timeline, and costs. You'll employ effective selling strategies to successfully position Workday’s SaaS Solutions as the cost effective, modern alternative, and you will be an expert in Workday’s innovative deployment methodology for medium sized organizations. You will work closely with Workday Service Partners to help them plan successful projects and grow their business. If you have a stellar consulting delivery and sales track record, are a self starter, and love working in a dynamic environment, then Workday is the place for you.
</p>
