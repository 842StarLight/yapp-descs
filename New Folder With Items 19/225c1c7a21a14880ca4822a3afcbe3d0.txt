<h2>APS Product Specialist - Chicago, IL</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>POSITION SUMMARY<br>
Works with sales staff in promoting the sale of Amada software within assigned territory. Provides software technical support to Sales Engineers, Regional Managers, and others. Assists Sales Engineers in all phases of software sales activities. Provides training to new Sales Engineers. Makes recommendations and suggestions to Product Manager concerning improvement of programs and procedures.<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:<br>
<ul>
<li>Provides technical information to Sales Engineers and Product Managers.</li>
<li>Maintains written activity reports, which are communicated to Product Manager.</li>
<li>Prepares advance planning reports, including semester goals and strategies.</li>
<li>Maintains regular contact and follow up with Sales Engineers and customers.</li>
<li>Prepares relevant reports, when necessary.</li>
<li>Provides on-site equipment support and demonstrations for customers.</li>
<li>Responds to customer inquiries.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
<br>
NON-ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:<br>
<ul>
<li>Prepares special reports, as needed.</li>
<li>Assists in projects/duties within the company or department.</li>
</ul>
<br>
REPORTING RELATIONSHIP:<br>
<ul><li>Product Manager</li></ul>
<br>
SUBORDINATE STAFF:<br>
<ul><li>None</li></ul>
<br>
KNOWLEDGE:<br>
<ul>
<li>Engineering degree or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of Amada products.</li>
<li>Experienced in the sales process as it relates to the sheet metal industry.</li>
</ul>
<br>
SKILLS:<br>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of Amada Software.</li>
<li>Computer knowledge (word processing and spreadsheets).</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (verbal and written), detailed-oriented.</li>
</ul>
<br>
TRAVEL:<br>
<ul><li>Extensive travel.</li></ul>
<br>
WORKING CONDITIONS:<br>
LIFTING: Average Weight: 10-50 lb. Frequency: Daily<br>
