</b><b>Position Summary</b>
</h2></div></div>
<div><div><div><p><b>
Position Summary:</b> The developer in this position will report to our Manager of Engineering and will work in a collaborative multi-disciplinary team environment with dedicated project managers. They will eventually be responsible for building and customizing eCommerce websites using the Magento EE &amp; CE platform, following Magento best practices. We ask that all of our developers become certified in Magento within the first year of employment. They will also be tasked with maintaining client repositories using GIT SCM while helping us advance our deploy process as well as building complex integrations with various systems such as ERPs, tax, shipping, and order management.</p></div></div></div>
