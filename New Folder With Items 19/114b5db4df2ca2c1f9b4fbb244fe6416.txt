<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The role of <b>Corporate Executive Chef</b> is to oversee the management, operation and financial results of the Culinary function. The role of Executive Chef is to satisfy corporate culinary needs including CEC meal preparation and presentation, marketing product ideation and evaluation, and broadly support sales efforts to build and nurture customer relationships that separate the CFGs experience from competition.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This person will have extensive food management operations, preparation and/or production and execution along with FOH/BOH operational management. This individual is responsible for ensuring that we exceed customer expectations across our B2B manufacturing, foodservice and retail customers, be partnered with sales teams and meet both Company and departmental objectives.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Corporate Culinary, Executive Chef Responsibilities</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
1. Provide meals and superior customer service as defined by Clemens Food Group within the scope of the position and Company policy<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage CEC kitchen</li>
<li>
Support stakeholder events (i.e. Food Challenge, shareholder meetings, and community events)</li>
<li>
Meal ideation for visits and events</li>
<li>
Shop for meal ingredients</li>
<li>
Meal preparation</li>
<li>
Serve meals and present concept information to the audience</li>
<li>
Clean-up and breakdown the event</li>
</ul>
<div>
2. Assist the Marketing/Innovation with the overall ideation, direction, coordination, evaluation and financial results of recipe and menu programs that are offered to retail customers, consumers, foodservice operators and end users</div>
<ul>
<li>
Ideation: Develop recipes for promotional and marketing purposes</li>
<li>
Market insights: Provide and present culinary trends, changes, innovations and key learning’s to internal and external customers</li>
<li>
Give presentations to stakeholders, including sales team, on product applications</li>
<li>
Participate in category initiatives</li>
<li>
Contribute and make recommendations to new product initiatives</li>
<li>
Identify and deliver new product ideas</li>
<li>
Support R&amp;D team by developing and validating cooking instructions</li>
<li>
Interact with strategic customers to develop product concepts, menu item ideation</li>
<li>
Participate in industry organizations (ACF, NRA, RCA, Culinary Schools)</li>
<li>
Assist in the selection and development of new recipes and preparation methods as well as standardized production recipes and portion specifications in accordance with consumer tastes, nutritional needs, product specifications, and established procedures and budgetary constraints</li>
</ul>

3. Sales Support: Supports sales organization with strategic accounts, operators and partner distributors.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Help to establish presentation technique and quality standards for all menu items and food production practices</li>
<li>
Lead culinary team in defining food show standards and be a resource to FS and Retail team</li>
<li>
Participate in internal and external customer visits with BD Team</li>
<li>
Attend food shows and represent CFG at industry events;</li>
<li>
Develop and manage Food Show SOP's and best practices;</li>
<li>
Standardize cutting process/product presentations for customer pitches</li>
<li>
Represent CFG in the media including print advertising, television and radio shows</li>
<li>
Develop relationship with key accounts and create velcro points with other chefs</li>
<li>
Develop, set-up, and execute internal photo shoots</li>
<li>
Support and participate in external product photo shoots</li>
<li>
Provide trouble shooting and solution providing for product issues with operators</li>
<li>
Participate in promotional activities (community events, barbeque competitions, farm show, demonstrations)</li>
<li>
Support sales team in creation of customer specific solutions</li>
<li>
Perform customer analysis and due diligence for purposes of meal ideation</li>
<li>
Creates effective and simple demonstration recipes and techniques that effectively help Street sales organization sell the profitability and flavor of products</li>
