<p>We are looking for an ambitious, creative, and metrics-driven Director of Demand Generation to lead our global demand generation strategy, fuel our sales growth, and support a growing sales team.</p>
<p><b>
Your Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead global, high growth lead generation strategy and team.</li>
<li>Develop and execute effective marketing programs to drive marketing interactions and leads.</li>
<li>Closely work with global sales team to ensure seamless alignment of goals, expectations, and execution.</li>
<li>Manage and drive marketing and sales funnel systems, analytics, and metrics to optimize program effectiveness and sales results.</li>
<li>Work with the marketing team to develop marketing assets for lead generation programs.</li>
<li>Initiate and manage field marketing activities and events</li>
<li>Manage third-party contractors, vendors, staff, and cross-functional teams.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have a minimum of seven (7) years of Lead Generation experience in B-2-B software/cloud markets.</li>
<li>Extensive track record of designing and implementing successful marketing programs, consistently generating and growing marketing interactions and qualified leads.</li>
<li>Experience with multi-channel sales models and a track record of effectively working with the sales team including inside sales, field sales and channel partners.</li>
<li>Experience developing, optimizing and managing the sales funnel utilizing CRM and e-marketing systems.</li>
<li>Extensive track record in email marketing, newsletters, and outbound e-marketing programs.</li>
<li>Strong background in market segmentation, database marketing, and list management.</li>
<li>Online marketing lead strategy experience, including landing page conversion optimization, search engine marketing, syndicated media channels, affiliate marketing, media buying, webinars, and social media integration.</li>
<li>Field marketing experience, including localization of programs and organizing tradeshow participation, as well as developing other events and user conferences.</li>
<li>Experience managing a team of direct and indirect reports and a history of productive vendor relationships</li>
<li>Experience working in international teams and marketing across multiple geographies.</li>
<li>Relevant degree.</li>
</ul>
