<h2>Professional Electrical Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Candidates should have a bachelor’s degree in electrical or civil engineering, a professional engineer license, and five or more years of experience in electric distribution design. Working knowledge of local, state, and federal electrical standards, permits, and procedures and experience with utility clients is required. Candidates must have knowledge of Microsoft Office and design software and excellent verbal and written communication skills. Some travel is required. Typical responsibilities include:
<ul>
<li>Manage and/or design various electric distribution projects including new services, cable and equipment replacements and upgrades, circuit conversions, URD’s, third party attachment make-ready, and network transformers.
</li>
<li>Manage technical and professional staff in production of, and pursuit of projects</li>
<li>
Lead sales efforts for design work</li>
<li>
Assist with proposals, marketing, project reports, and technical presentations</li>
<li>
Prepare and review scope, budget, and schedule for project tasks</li>
<li>
Develop and complete applications, permits, engineering reports, and specifications</li>
<li>
Prepare and review engineer estimates</li>
<li>
Prepare design cost estimates</li>
<li>
Monitor task budget and progress</li>
<li>
Prepare project status and other reports</li>
<li>
Communicate project details with other design engineers</li>
<li>
Conduct project cost reviews</li>
<li>
Attend and/or conduct client and project meetings</li>
<li>
Attend various meetings including pre-design, plan review, pre-construction, professional organization, staff, etc.
</li>
<li>Conduct on-site inspections as needed</li>
<li>
Attend seminars and/or training classes</li>
<li>
Maintain excellent client relations</li>
<li>
Maintain a safe working environment</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
