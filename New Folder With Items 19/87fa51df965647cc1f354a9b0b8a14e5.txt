<h2>Enterprise Inside-Sales Rep *High Tech,Telecom & Wireless Background Req</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY: </b><br>The Enterprise Inside-Sales Rep is responsible for driving new business opportunities, up-selling and cross-selling to customers across a wide range of high tech products and solutions.</p>
<p>This position requires an aggressive “hunter” mentality with the ability to create and work leads within the Telecommunications &amp; Technology space.<br><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Cold call prospective accounts to identify and schedule appointments for Field Sales team.</li>
<li>Answer all inbound phone, emails, chat messages from various communications channels</li>
<li>Triage inbound webforms, reviewing new submissions and send viable leads to the correct ISR, based on geography</li>
<li>Schedule meetings with target customers in specific US regions</li>
<li>Research contacts/leads for target customers in specific regions of US</li>
<li>Plan and prioritize sales activities and prospect contacts towards achieving stated targets.</li>
<li>Manage product mix, according to agreed business goals.</li>
<li>Propose client solutions to prospective customers and through the appropriate propositions and ethical sales methods.</li>
<li>Respond to and follow-up on sales inquiries in a timely and effective manner.</li>
<li>Use customer and prospect contact activities tools and systems and update relevant information/data into these systems.</li>
<li>Attend meetings and present business results and opportunities at external customer meetings and internal meetings, with other company constituents.</li>
<li>Attend initial and ongoing training to develop required knowledge, techniques and skills necessary to accurately articulate client solutions.</li>
<li>Service client needs, as instructed.</li>
<li>Back-up ISR team-members when unavailable due to PTO or other absences</li>
<li>US Based travel required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Skill Set &amp; Background: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong prospecting, verbal &amp; written communications skills-mandatory</li>
<li>Exceptional time management skills and ability to effectively multi-task</li>
<li>Excellent relationship building skills</li>
<li>Ability to generate leads while achieving a high level of customer and partner satisfaction</li>
<li>High-Tech, Telecom and Wireless Background mandatory</li>
<li>Professional training in strategic, value vision, solutions selling or social selling, a plus</li>
<li>Background selling business-to-business or appointment setting or lead generation, mandatory</li>
<li>Experience with SalesForce.com, Excel, PowerPoint, Word and other internal information tools are required</li>
<li>Self-motivated and able to work in a virtual environment</li>
</ul>
