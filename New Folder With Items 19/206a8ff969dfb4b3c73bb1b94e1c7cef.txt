<p>TaskToday is looking for a Social, Mobile, and Cloud-computing savvy Product Manager.</p>
<p>

Also help lead our HR compliance features. We’re looking for someone who can help bring a new perspective and fresh ideas. You will be responsible for gathering business requirements, doing functional design, and presenting HR features to customers, prospects, partners and analysts.</p>

5+ years enterprise applications experience</li>

Excellent understanding of HR and compliance requirements for large enterprises</li>

Functional knowledge preferred in one or more of the following functional areas: Name, Contact Info, National IDs, and Personal Info. Compliance areas: I-9, EEO, VETS-100, and other HR compliance reporting areas.</li>
<li>
Enjoy presenting product requirements and features to external audiences</li>
<li>
Research, summarize, and prioritize customer business requirements</li>
<li>

<li>

<li>
Leadership experience, decisive, fast learner, detail oriented, enjoy fast paced work environments</li>
<li>
Sense of humor</li>
