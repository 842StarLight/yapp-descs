<h2>Part time on Air Talent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Alpha Media –San Jose, CA</b> is seeking on air talent. Candidates should bring 1 year prior performance experience on radio, podcasting or through other content mediums. Required knowledge includes ability to bring personality and content to a radio format with pace, energy and focus. Knowledge of or aptitude for digital software in automation and sound editing is required. Familiarity and current engagement on multiple social media platforms is required. Be curious, collaborative, creative and open to direction.</p>
<p>
At Alpha Media, we have a passion for great radio and digital content. Our philosophy is to be smaller, faster, bolder, and work as a high functioning, best of class team at all levels. We hire and retain top talent who are unique, innovative and vibrant. We believe in creating progressive products, world-class events and building strong relationships in our communities.</p>
<p>
Headquartered in Portland, Oregon, Alpha Media operates multiple radio stations across the United States covering all formats.</p>
<p>
We are live and local and can’t wait to talk to you!</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities of this position may include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Engage with listeners via phone calls, contests, and social media.</li>
<li>Broadcast live from remote locations and events.</li>
<li>Operate control console.</li>
<li>Conduct on-air interviews.</li>
<li>Maintain traffic logs and transmitter readings.</li>
<li>Work with Promotions and Sales departments on creative content.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements of this position include the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of one-year on-air experience.</li>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge of all applicable FCC rules and regulations.</li>
<li>Experience in all Google suites programs.</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of audio editing software and broadcast equipment.</li>
<li>Must be a team player and work well with management and staff at all levels.</li>
<li>Ability to successfully execute station events and remotes.</li>
<li>Able to relate to the audience and have a strong listener interaction.</li>
<li>Public speaking skills and ability to interact with listeners and clients in a public setting.</li>
<li>Excellent problem solving and prioritizing skills.</li>
<li>Skilled in operation of a control board, remote broadcasting, and other related production equipment.</li>
<li>Be a positive member of a tight-knit team.</li>
<li>Must be creative and innovative with a strong work ethic.</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver’s license and fully insured personal vehicle.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preference may be given to candidates who have the above experience plus the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior broadcast industry experience.</li>
</ul>
