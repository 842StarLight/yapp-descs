<h2>Dealer Operations Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We're leaders. Innovators. Perfectionists. Constantly improving our products and practices, with our customers always at the forefront of everything we do. We’re the largest family-owned, retail seed company in the country and we’re looking for qualified leaders like you to help us grow. When you join Beck’s, you join a family. And it’s what we do together that sets us apart.<br>
<br>
This position is responsible for assisting the Dealer Operations Lead in the overall daily operating procedures, dealer programs, planning, organizing, data entry and administration support for the field sales team and dealer network.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Support Dealer Management Team via tasks outlined by Dealer Operations Lead</li>
<li>Managing required paperwork, processes and documentation associated with dealer recruitment and management</li>
<li>Create, organize and coordinate timely and effective communications to dealer network on a consistent basis</li>
<li>Support dealer income programs
<ul>
<li>Manage the dealer income calculator</li>
<li>Answer questions and provide analysis of dealer income</li>
<li>Create and process approved dealer draws</li>
<li>Editing, organization and sales reporting support for Special Operating Agreements</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Support tools that can be used to increase dealer productivity and performance</li>
<li>Protect and secure confidential business information</li>
<li>Interact with sales staff and dealers related to sales support</li>
<li>Help manage Dealer Management Team’s messages and voicemails and support overflow</li>
<li>Manage Outlook calendar for Dealer Management Team</li>
<li>Support New Dealer Training via assigned tasks</li>
<li>Prioritize important tasks based on urgency and timelines needed</li>
<li>Collect, filter and compile data as requested by the Dealer Management Team</li>
<li>Other duties as required by the Dealer Operations Lead</li>
</ul>
