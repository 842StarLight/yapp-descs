<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>JRK Residential Group has an immediate need for a PART TIME Leasing Consultant to join our team at Diamond Hillside Apartments , our multifamily apartment building located in Pittsburg, CA.</p>
<p>JRK is a dynamic and rapidly growing Real-Estate Investment firm that is comprised of a team of experienced professionals at all levels. Dedicated to providing value and service at the highest quality.</p>
<p>As a Leasing Consultant, you will help create the energy and enjoyment around our apartment homes. It starts with having a keen eye for discovering prospects requirements and matching those needs with their perfect apartment home.</p>
<p><b>What you'll do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Quickly and fully executes leasing strategies and directives to ensure proper presentation, pricing, and promotional activity</li>
<li>Greet prospects and tour market ready units</li>
<li>Qualifies prospects; determine needs, motives, and ability to rent</li>
<li>Executes necessary marketing plan to drive prospect traffic</li>
<li>Accepts applications for residency and submit completed paperwork</li>
<li>Collects deposits and informs tenants of deposit policies</li>
<li>Completes all move-in details</li>
<li>Contacts residents 60 days prior to lease expiration and initiates renewals</li>
<li>Timely and accurate reporting</li>
<li>Establishes friendly and helpful working relationships with residents to gain their loyalty and promote resident referrals</li>
<li>Properly executes Company performance guidelines</li>
<li>Executes promotional and markdown activity on a timely basis and according to Company guidelines and directives</li>
<li>Ensures compliance with all Local, State and Federal employment laws, including wage and hour,</li>
<li>Quickly and fully implements directives from Company staff and corporate headquarters</li>
<li>Maintains regular and consistent attendance and punctuality to ensure all essential duties are fulfilled to satisfactory level</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you'll need: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High-school graduate, College graduate preferred</li>
<li>Successful previous multifamily leasing experience</li>
<li>Proven track record in leasing and/or sales</li>
<li>Proficient in personal computer skills and in business-related computer software, including Microsoft Office and e-mail</li>
<li>Excellent understanding of sales process</li>
<li>Professional image</li>
<li>Good organizational skills</li>
<li>Team player with excellent leadership, analytical and reasoning skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Benefits include medical, dental, vision, FSA, 401k and much more!<br>To apply for this position, please reply with your resume.<br>We look forward to hearing from you!</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
