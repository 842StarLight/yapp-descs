<h2>Technical Support Analyst, Network Operations Center (NOC)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you passionate about problem solving and helping others? Are you interested in gaining the variety of hands on experience that only comes from working in an enterprise level data center? If so, we are looking for a Technical Support Analyst to join our network operations center in Columbia, SC, and you could potentially be a great fit for our team!</p>
<p>
Immedion is a growing data center services and IaaS provider, with facilities located across OH, IN and the Carolinas. We've been listed among the Best Places to Work in SC, and we're always looking for great people who can use their professional expertise to bring our customers the best possible solutions for their business.</p>
<p>
We believe in integrity and in always putting our customers first. Our people are problem solvers, team players, and are always looking to get better. As our Technical Support Analyst, you would be responsible for upholding those standards, as well as working in a dynamic team environment to directly support our growing customer base. You would also be a key stakeholder in supporting members of the technical and engineering teams in addressing product related issues specific to the local data center.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support of Immedion's customer base from our Network Operations Center (NOC) in a 24x7x365 environment</li>
<li>Provide immediate customer interaction support via call, e-mail and portal request</li>
<li>Consistent monitoring of events related to equipment, managed services, network and power</li>
<li>Perform routine facility and equipment readings throughout data center facility</li>
<li>Assist customers with onsite installation and service request</li>
<li>Ongoing development and training of supported products and services</li>
<li>Serve as backup to local teams for facility related activities</li>
<li>Maintain a working level of proficiency with all Immedion co-location, Cloud and managed service platforms</li>
<li>Establish security access via badge and biometrics for all new customers</li>
<li>Properly identify all visitors and provide escort to specified premise equipment</li>
<li>Actively monitor all security and video surveillance systems</li>
<li>Properly greet and check-in all visitors and vendors</li>
<li>Receive, pack and ship customer equipment as required</li>
<li>Ensure escalation events and issues are routed to proper departments in pre-determined periods of time</li>
<li>Notify Sales of potential up sell opportunities based on events or customer interactions</li>
<li>Provide initial and advanced troubleshooting of customer related issues and events</li>
<li>Support customized managed services per customer contracts</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by Supervisor or Manager</li>
<br>
</ul>
