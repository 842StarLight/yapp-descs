<h2>Senior Electrical & Instrument Person</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Clariant is a focused and innovative specialty chemical company based near Basel in Switzerland. Last year the company recorded CHF 6.6 billion in sales harnessing the talents of its 18 000 employees across 53 countries.
</p>
<p>Clariant’s portfolio is optimized for future growth, with a focus in four business areas: Care Chemicals, Catalysts, Natural Resources, and Plastics &amp; Coatings.
</p>
<p>We believe that our growth is all about our customers and their success. Investment in innovation capabilities and developing inspiring environments for courageous and determined minds are therefore key to reaching our goal.
</p>
<p>Join us and help create sustainable value through chemistry for customers all over the globe.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Senior Instrument/Electrical Person will have thorough knowledge of industrial instrumentation and electrical work including combustion systems. The incumbent will be a self starter, work well with others, able to work any shift and have good record keeping skills.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform electrical/instrumentation duties.
</li>
<li>Works under general supervision of a Maintenance Supervisor with regard to the design, installation, maintenance, and repair of electronic and pneumatic instruments and controls and electrical equipment.
</li>
<li>Performs minor mechanical job tasks; when these tasks are an integral part of the assigned instrument or electrical job.
</li>
<li>Responsible for the maintenance of spare parts and equipment; file of literature and circuit diagrams, safety and calibration checks; and record keeping for all checks.
</li>
<li>Expert utilization of work order systems and processes (e.g. SAP Maintenance Planner).
</li>
<li>Communicates and orders parts through Maintenance Planner and/or Maintenance Manager.
</li>
<li>Assists in maximizing the performance, safety, environmental responsibility and efficiency of all team members and plant systems, willing to work overtime, and available for unplanned or planned call ins.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Job requires a thorough knowledge of industrial instrumentation and electricity
</li>
<li>Ability to read blueprints; read and draw wiring diagrams and schematics
</li>
<li>Excellent safety record with a demonstrated ability to approach safety issues proactively
</li>
<li>High School Diploma or GED and Post secondary trade school or technical college education – Master Electrician license is highly desired (or attained within two years)
</li>
<li>Three or more years of industrial instrumentation and/or electrical maintenance experience.
</li>
<li>Skill in Microsoft Office (Word &amp; Excel), Outlook and SAP work order system, or comparable computer software.
</li>
<li>Solid working knowledge of industrial instrumentation/electrical maintenance practices and procedures, pneumatic equipment and fire alarm systems.
</li>
<li>Ability to safely and accurately troubleshoot and correct equipment malfunctions; perform system startups from manuals, schematics, or other reference materials. Contact manufactures tech support for help.
</li>
<li>Ability to safely work with up to 480 volts, run conduit, install equipment, size load requirements as per NFPA requirements.
</li>
<li>Ability to design configures, install and write PLC programs on GE systems, Fanuc and Cimplicity.
</li>
<li>The ability to install and program industrial process instrumentation (Flow, Pressure, Temperature, etc.)
</li>
<li>Knowledge of combustion systems, including their safety controls and limits. Ability to troubleshoot and repair these systems.
</li>
<li>Shift will be Wednesday, Thursday, Friday and every other Tuesday 7am - 7pm
</li>
<li>Must be open to on-call requirements<br>
</li>
</ul>
