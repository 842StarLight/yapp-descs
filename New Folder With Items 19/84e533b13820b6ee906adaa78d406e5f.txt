A day in your life as a Senior Customer Profitability Manager:</div>
<div>
Under direction of the Director, the Senior Customer Profitability Manager, uses broad knowledge of financial markets, risk management theory, banking principles, regulations, and operations to plan and forecast budget to achieve strategic initiatives and financial goals and objectives. Leads and motivates the Business Unit to achieve strategic goals and ensures the integrity of the objectives are accurate and complete. Proactive and influential when dealing with a wide variety of skillsets. Identifies new opportunities through analytics and creates solutions. Acts as the liaison between the department and various segmentation initiatives. Ensures timely and appropriate process implementation and solutions to create efficient and effective sales and service culture. Ensures business unit’s compliance with applicable laws, regulations, regulatory requirements and Bank policies and procedures, including but not limited to those related to Fair Banking, Anti-Money Laundering laws and regulations, Bank Secrecy Act and USA PATRIOT Act.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We’ll give you these important responsibilities…</div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Responsible for the sourcing of all centralized data and financial reporting information that is required for both internal and external stakeholders. Responsible for company-wide planning and forecasting. Researches and analyzes market conditions, regulatory environment, management practices and develops appropriate strategies. Reviews company’s forecast to ensure it is reasonable and captures material drivers. Works with business units to assist in the development of strategies, plans, forecasts and budgets that align with corporate and business unit goals and objectives.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provides analytic and coordination support for corporate strategic initiatives; ensure strategic initiatives align with each other and with overall corporate goals; help identify opportunities through analytics; provides data and analytics support for executive level inquiries; develops analytic resources and support information culture. Creates/revises/reports on company financial/accounting data, while constantly improving data gathering workflow and integrity.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provides high-level direction and guidance regarding the system integration and implementation efforts across multiple SAP applications, products and technologies. Manages software integration and technical documentation efforts. Oversees the review, analysis, and modification processes of programming systems including encoding, testing, and debugging to support the organization's Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) applications. Works with business units and IT to plan, design, develop, implement and integrate software solutions to enhance efficiency, productivity, workflow, and data integrity</div></li>
<li><div>
Leads annual corporate budget process. Leads departmental capital and operating budgets, human and material resource requirements, systems R&amp;D, management reports, and provides general leadership to help establish and achieve division and corporate wide goals and objectives.</div></li>
<li><div>
Participates in corporate strategic initiatives and implementations across the company. With a focus on projects that foster data and information driven decision making throughout the organization.</div></li>
<li><div>
Manages staff and operations, as required to ensure service level quality standards and deadlines are met, proper regulatory and operational policies and procedures are followed, controls are exercised, and errors are corrected; manages employee staffing, scheduling, compensation, performance management, appraisal, training and development, succession planning and affirmative action. Goal is to attract retain and motivate the team to achieve management business objectives and a favorable employee relations climate. Responsible for the timely and effective management of People Services forms and documents relevant to immediate staff. Leads the team by inspiring engagement and increasing the capabilities of others to optimize business results.</div></li>
</ul></div>
