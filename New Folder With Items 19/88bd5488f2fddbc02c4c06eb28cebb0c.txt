<h2>Logistics Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Job Purpose</b>: Individuals in this role need to have strong communication skills and ability to multitask. We are looking for high energy enthusiastic workers that are confident in decision making. Coordinates the handling of difficult and/or unusual situations and escalate to Account Manager when appropriate. Responsible for continuous Core 100 carrier development at all times. This position reports directly to Team Lead.</div>
<div><b>Core Functions:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for covering freight in defined zone and maintaining contact with driver throughout transit, including pick-up and delivery.</li>
<li>Build strong relationships with key contacts to achieve full customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Prioritize coverage accordingly, per freight in the system.</li>
<li>Ensures and provides quality service to both internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Participates and provides expertise as a member of the carrier sales department team.</li>
<li>Responsible for updating Account Manager regarding any possible issues that may arise.</li>
<li>Initiates required action for response to Account Manager Request for order changes, and communicates to appropriate Manager or Salesperson.</li>
<li>Important to stay informed of market conditions, competitive activities, advertising and promotional trends through the reading of pertinent literature.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Details of Function:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for achieving daily and weekly minimum expectations and KPIs with goals that vary regarding with given expectations by Team Lead.
<ul>
<li>6 loads a day</li>
<li>40 inbound a day</li>
<li>100 outbound a day</li>
<li>210 minutes on the phone a day</li>
<li>Weekly written changes to Core 100 Team Lead on every Friday</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Makes telephone calls and occasional in-person visits to build core and dedicated carriers</li>
<li>Research sourcing of carriers outside of Core 100 and TMS, through the use of TD Source, Safersys, SaferWatch and other sources</li>
<li>Must maintain and utilize Core 100 carriers</li>
<li>Responsible for validating a carrier’s qualification to our establishment carrier setup procedures prior to utilizing carrier</li>
<li>Provide back-up to other group members in the with job duties as required</li>
<li>Answers carrier inquires/ communication as required</li>
<li>Complete training that is assigned within the deadline</li>
<li>Required to work until the freight is covered at the request of your Team Lead</li>
<li>Provides COMPETITVE “market” pricing to Account Manager as requested</li>
<li>Supplies Management with oral and written reports on carrier problems, competitive activities and potential of new services</li>
<li>Responsible for actively sources, maintaining and utilizing Core 100 carriers</li>
<li>Other duties that can be assigned</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Requirements for Booking and Dispatching Freight</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Must have all driver information in the load (Driver Cell, Truck # and Trailer #)</li>
<li>Must talk to driver prior to pick up and confirm they have correct equipment and driver has all details pertaining to the load</li>
<li>Driver must opt into Loadtap tracking prior to picking up load</li>
<li>All loads must have a chatter with position update at least once daily</li>
<li>All contact with carrier must be put in writing in chatter feed in loads</li>
<li>Prebooks are required to be verified day prior to pick up and must be documented in load that carrier is still good for the load</li>
<li>All carriers must be vetted prior to giving them load tenders, even if they are approved in the system (Check TIA and Truckstop.com for write ups, verify we have updated contract, w-9, authority and insurance with C.L. Services, Inc. listed as a certificate holder.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
