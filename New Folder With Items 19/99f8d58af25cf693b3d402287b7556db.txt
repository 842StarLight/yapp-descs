<h2>DIGITAL MEDIA MANAGER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Digital Media Manager will manage the Dealership Business Development Center as well as provide additional support functions at the discretion of the General Manager.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities and Duties:</p>
<ul><li>Provide a defined list of production reports on Sales and Services Departments</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage Dealer Client Portfolios with GSM</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage Dealership website and report relevant analytics</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage Data Mining initiatives through the Dealership CRM</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide all relevant analytics, ROI and production reporting</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage Dealership Social Media Campaigns</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage Dealership Reputation Management campaigns</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide Customer Service support and CSI reporting</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage Fixed Operation Telemarketing campaigns</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage Service Advertising campaigns with Service Director and GM</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide strategic input relevant to all digital campaigns</li></ul>
<ul><li>Procure graphics for website specials and advertising initiatives</li></ul>
<ul><li>Submit Dealership website to AHM for compliance</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create monthly NCM report for 20 Dealership 20 Group participation</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain Dealership Advertising budget for submittal to Business Manager</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide input relevant to SEO and SEM campaigns based on analytics</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Initial responsibilities will include analytics and production reporting and development of your knowledge relevant to Dealership operations. The expected acclimation time for the Dealership CRM and Operating system would be 30-60 days. The responsibilities and duties listed above may or may not represent the entire scope of your job description. Over time, there may be special initiatives that you will manage with the support of other personnel.</p>
