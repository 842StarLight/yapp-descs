<h2>Security Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>BCS is a Data Center management firm managing a data center located in Milwaukee, WI. We are looking for a local professional to join our team, serving one of our valued Wisconsin clients.</p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>The Security Supervisor will be responsible for providing a visual security presence to ensure visitors, clients, and employees have a safe and pleasant experience while inside the Facility. Deter and detect suspicious activities and watch for criminal acts or site infractions which may pose a threat to the property, client or employees at the site. Provide excellent customer service to all individuals that enter the facility and further employee/management relations, goodwill and public relations to keep our customers for life.</p>
<p>Responsible for the supervision of internal and external physical security and associated security officers on the assigned shift to coordinates implementations with property management, construction, sales engineering, operations, engineering, and asset management. Additional responsibilities include the logistical management of all internal/external deliveries and shipments.</p>
<p><i>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each of the essential duties and responsibilities listed below with or without reasonable accommodation. The requirements listed below are representative, but not all-inclusive, of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.</i></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for both the Safety and Security Department of the facility</li>
<li>Creates, assigns and issues facility access badges to authorized personnel</li>
<li>Reports to all emergencies and follows emergency response procedures.</li>
<li>Ensure all packages/deliveries are accounted for and delivered.</li>
<li>Assists in the coordination or internal/external security investigations.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable of all contingency/emergency plans, procedures and corporate response teams.</li>
<li>Works with the Security Manager to coordinate customers special need requirements.</li>
<li>Respond in a timely and effective manner to customer inquiries, issues and complaints.</li>
<li>Assists in performing periodic audits to ensure compliance with written standards and policies.</li>
<li>Participates in site compliance audits.</li>
<li>Maintains a strong working relationship with fellow security officers and customers to ensure satisfaction with the Physical Security operations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>May supervise the receiving and security of shipments and deliveries.</li>
<li>Will supervise shift security officers.</li>
<li>Ensures enforcement of all physical protection policies for properties and personnel.</li>
<li>Supervises security officers and security consultants.</li>
<li>Trains security officers in the security awareness program, security hotline, and incident database.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of all applicable rules laws, codes, regulations and ordinances in performing security responsibilities.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable of and familiarization with emergency action plan and evacuation procedures</li>
<li>General knowledge and understanding of the properties of power and cooling, relative to building operations.</li>
<li>Ability to handle emergency and crisis situations efficiently and effectively.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze and solve problems involving several options with limited information. Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram or schedule form and to work independently.</li>
<li>Flexibility, including the willingness and ability to readily respond to changing circumstances and expectations. Openness to new ideas and procedures.</li>
<li>Good leadership, management, interpersonal, negotiation, organizational, analytic, and communication skills. The ability to build and lead teams and to work well in a collaborative, team-oriented environment. Ability to manage projects effectively.</li>
<li>The ability to lead through influence and be a proactive partner with a strong customer service orientation who builds relationships by building trust.</li>
<li>Solid work ethic and integrity, able to work effectively and be self-directed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<p>Essential duties are done both inside and outside, in climate-controlled and non-climate-controlled conditions. Exposure to wet (and humidity), extreme temperatures, noise, vibration, mechanical hazards, radiant energy hazards, electrical hazards, explosive hazards, and burn hazards. Climbing ladders and scaffolding as required to complete and supervise work.</p>
<p><b>Physical Demands</b></p>
<p>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job.</p>
<ul>
<li>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to frequently stand (8 or more hours); walk; use hands, handle or feel, and reach with hands and arms.</li>
<li>Ability to walk or stand for prolonged periods. Requires bending, stooping, reaching upward, and lifting up to 50 pounds.</li>
<li>Work is in a data center setting and will require effort associated with using a computer.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>POSITION TYPE/EXPECTED HOURS OF WORK</b></p>
<ul><li>Must be able to work varying shifts in a 24 x 7 x 365 environment</li></ul>
<p><b>Travel</b></p>
<p>Travel is minimal, if required.</p>
<p><b>Required Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of five years of experience as a security officer.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Access Control Systems and related software</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience working well under pressure with a proactive approach to unusual occurrences.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Data center operations or telecommunications experience, a technical understanding of the components of the data center or network operations and a demonstrated ability to quickly assimilate technical data, methods and procedures.</li>
<li>Proficient with Windows-based computer applications</li>
</ul>
