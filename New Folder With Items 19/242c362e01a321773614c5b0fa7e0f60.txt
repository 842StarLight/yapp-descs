<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
You will work directly with business leaders across Google to develop and oversee Google’s customer strategy, including building and managing a toolkit of programs and assets designed to guide and grow our customer relationships. You’ll create centralized content and resources to educate clients about what the power of cloud computing can do for them, then coordinate with leaders to implement these programs globally. You'll face the demands of a rapidly evolving industry, at times requiring you to swiftly navigate through ambiguity and use sharply-honed problem-solving skills to identify the best solutions for our customers.</p>
<p>
As a Strategy &amp; Operations Lead, you partner with the head of global Customer Engineering (CE) to define, execute, and land key strategic business priorities. You will perform strategic analysis, organizational planning, and process design to enable the organization to succeed and scale at incredible speed. You possess strong analytical abilities, keen insights, and passion for cloud computing technology make you a trusted advisor to executive and senior stakeholders. You will provide strategic/planning input and will devise new ways to grow and build our team. You are a communicator, and collaborate effectively with internal stakeholders and cross-functional teams to solve problems, implement new reporting solutions, and deliver consistently against high standards. A strategic thinker, you examine and analyze statistical data to identify patterns and trends that represent new opportunities for Google Cloud.</p>
<p>
Global Solutions is the link between Google products and business. Your focus is to help turn innovation into complete, packaged tools that allow our customers to get the most that they need from our products. As part of the Global Solutions team, you'll help to ensure that Google has adopted the right strategy for our products. Working in one of three specialized areas – Performance, Brand, or Publisher &amp; Platform – you'll use your expertise to help front-line Sales partners to work their magic quickly and effectively. You are passionate about all things digital, and want to help shape the world of online advertising.</p>
