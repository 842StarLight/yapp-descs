<h2>Account Manager / Customer Relations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Wild Ridge Lawn &amp; Landscape is a multi-dimensional company, providing a full spectrum of landscape and snow/ice management services to commercial, industrial, medical, municipal, and retail centers in the Indianapolis and surrounding areas.</p>
<p>Wild Ridge Lawn &amp; Landscape is a different kind of company – a team fueled by progressive spirit and managed with a level of passion that is uncommon in the industry. But that’s just one part of the equation. The roots of the company reflect an approach that is inspired by ownership who refuses to take shortcuts and values a job well done!</p>
<p>Wild Ridge is working to become the leading site management company in Indiana. We are transforming the green industry from the inside-out with a dependable, adaptable, proactive approach that creates greater value for our clients while delivering unexpected results worth remembering.</p>
<p>The Account Manager must be able to manage existing commercial maintenance clients and be able to generate new commercial customers as well. They must be driven to meet sales goals by renewing current clients and selling enhancement projects to current customer base. They also must be comfortable networking and generating new business. Knowledge of plants and previous horticulture experience is a must. Client retention rate is a key factor in succeeding in this role. Strong communication and relationship development skills are a must.</p>
<p><b>As a Customer Relationship Manager, Here’s What You’ll Do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain strong professional relationships with clients and facilitate open lines of communication via e-mail, phone or face to face.</li>
<li>Perform site visits, site evaluations, and formal meetings with our clients to ensure a high level of quality and service.</li>
<li>Prepare estimates and proposals for presentation to clients and potential clients.</li>
<li>Present enhancement ideas to existing clients, maximizing sales opportunities, and improving our client’s properties.</li>
<li>Educate clients on current landscaping trends and practices.</li>
<li>Assist in resolving issues, problems or concerns with our clients, expedite client service when needed.</li>
<li>Develop strong relationships with Wild Ridge team members to ensure that customer service and production teams are coordinated on serving the needs of our clients.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge and Skills Needed: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plant knowledge and previous horticulture experience preferred.</li>
<li>Landscape design experience not necessary but helpful.</li>
<li>Must be comfortable networking and generating new business.</li>
<li>Strong project management and customer relationship skills</li>
<li>A positive attitude and a knack for working in a cooperative team environment.</li>
<li>Must be extremely motivated and highly organized.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills, outgoing personality.</li>
<li>Ability to support multiple work assignments simultaneously.</li>
<li>Strong computer application skills needed.</li>
<li>Must take initiative and formulate a course of action to accomplish goals.</li>
<li>Must be adaptable and flexible in dealing with a variety of people.</li>
</ul>
