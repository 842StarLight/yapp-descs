<h2>Director - Water Meter Installation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>HydroCorp has been on an aggressive growth track for the last five years. In that time, we’ve doubled our workforce, opened four new regional offices, and added more service offerings for both municipal and industrial customers. If you’re looking for a career where you can make a real difference, HydroCorp presents an exciting opportunity.</p>
<p>We are currently accepting applications for the Director of Meter Installation. This leadership role will be responsible for general project management as well as technical oversight of water meter installation programs for our municipal clients throughout the company.</p>
<p>This position will require approximately 40-50% overnight travel. (Most of our current projects are in Minnesota, Michigan, and Wisconsin)</p>
<p><b>Benefits: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Paid Time Off (Vacation + Holidays)</li>
<li>Health, Dental, and Vision Coverage</li>
<li>Short Term Disability</li>
<li>Life Insurance</li>
<li>401k with Company Match</li>
<li>Vehicle and cell phone allowance</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Salary: $75,000 - $80,000 (depending on experience and qualifications)</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate effectively to all levels within the organization and manage client expectations to ensure customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Work closely with Human Resources on the retention and hiring of Project Supervisors and new Employees.</li>
<li>Foster and build a team environment and effectively sets and manages expectations and deliverables.</li>
<li>Maintain a positive working relationship with clients and employees</li>
<li>Review project staffing and deliverable schedules (scope of work) to ensure projects will be completed in a timely and quality manner</li>
<li>Work at the client site as needed to assist the team in the day-to-day management of the project</li>
<li>Oversee water meter installation orientation &amp; training for new hires</li>
<li>Mentor projects, leads and supervisors</li>
<li>Oversee safety training, performance &amp; investigations</li>
<li>Manage remote teams</li>
<li>Serve as the main point of contact with water meter install clients</li>
<li>Work closely with administrative/customer service team and monitors the quality of work team performance, appointment scheduling and resolves customer complaints/incidents</li>
<li>Ensure that proper equipment, software, reporting, and tools are in place for each project and team member</li>
<li>Open jobs and provides job updates to team members and keeps status reports updated weekly</li>
<li>Manage workflow of all meter installation projects to completion</li>
<li>Verify proper inventory and delivery of meters with distributor/water utility client to coincide with the installation schedule</li>
<li>Manage all internal status meetings with team as well as attends client status meetings when needed</li>
<li>Review RFQ’s and Project Scope of work details to assist in project quoting and ongoing project management</li>
<li>Alert Management regarding potential sales opportunities to secure additional projects</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of AMI water meter installation projects &gt; 20,000 meters</li>
<li>2+ years of progressively responsible related water meter installation</li>
<li>2+ years of experience leading teams</li>
<li>1+ years of electric and gas meter experience</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree preferred</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license</li>
<li>Must be willing to undergo a background check and drug screening in accordance with local law/regulations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated ability to organize, prioritize and manage multiple tasks and concurrent projects.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, listening, and written communication skills. Effective interpersonal and teamwork skills</li>
<li>Detailed oriented. Ability to meet project deadlines</li>
<li>Computer skills and familiarity with utilizing Microsoft office suite and other proprietary software programs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Environment: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>The environment will be inside and outside conditions</li>
<li>Exposure to heat, cold, fumes, dust, noise and water</li>
<li>Ability to adjust to inclement weather changes</li>
<li>Walking, sitting and standing for extended periods</li>
<li>Ability to lift and carry a minimum of 75 lbs, perform overhead activities, and climb ladders</li>
<li>Ability to perform assigned duties in a small or poorly lit space</li>
<li>Ability to use mechanical and electrical power tools</li>
</ul>
