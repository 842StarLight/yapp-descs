<h2>Lead / Recruiting Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>U.S. Installation Group (USIG) has been an exclusive provider of Installation and Renovation Services for the nation's largest home improvement retailer: for over twenty-six years. Based upon our continued success and growth in markets from Arizona to Boston, USIG is in need of additional great talent! This exponential growth has turned into opportunities of advancement not only for our internal employees, but also for new talent. We are currently seeking a Lead / Recruiting Coordinator in our Corporate Office.</p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>· Provide updates and track installer recruitment information in USIG's proprietary software.</p>
<p>· Following up with recruiting of installers until they are fully on-boarded and approved to work.</p>
<p>· Communicating with the mgmt. team in the facilities and at the Corporate office, as well as with potential installers</p>
<p>· Data Entry of new recruiting leads and updating existing lead information</p>
<p>· Scan and upload any documents collected from installers.</p>
<p>· Support Operations Managers</p>
<p>· As needed, call installer leads for assigned facilities to schedule appointments as well as send and collect documents for credentialing. (No Cold Calling)</p>
<p>· As needed, responsible for filtering through incoming calls to pre-qualify installers and to negotiate rates as needed.</p>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>· Bi-Lingual (Spanish) is a plus, but not required</p>
<p>· 2+ years of Customer Service experience, previous recruiting experience, and any sales experience will be a strong plus.</p>
<p>· Must have a strong phone presence and the ability to follow up.</p>
<p>· Ability to develop leads through direct solicitation techniques.</p>
<p>· Strong computer skills required (Microsoft Outlook).</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
