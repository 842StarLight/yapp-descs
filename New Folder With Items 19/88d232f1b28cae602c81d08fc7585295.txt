<h2>Tax Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Tax Manager is responsible for the oversight of the compliance and planning for all transaction taxes; including but not limited to sales/use, property, fuel and excise taxes for all companies within the affiliated group. The Tax Manager role requires strong communication skills and experience working with people at different levels within the corporate office and various business units. This position will report to the Director of Tax in the corporate office located in Irving Texas and manages the Tax Accountant.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the quarterly and annual tax provision (ASC 740) for the U.S. and Canadian group companies.</li>
<li>Manage the preparation and review of the U.S. consolidated income tax return and various state income tax returns.</li>
<li>Manage the preparation of federal and state estimated tax calculations and the respective payments.</li>
<li>Manage the preparation of the various sales tax returns</li>
<li>Manage the acquisition of property tax detail necessary for our outside consulting firm to render these properties in the various taxing jurisdictions.</li>
<li>Manage the unclaimed property filings as needed.</li>
<li>Maintain the tax calendar keeping all parties up to date on tax filing due dates.</li>
<li>Respond to tax notices and audit requests from the various taxing jurisdictions.</li>
<li>Assist in creating roles and responsibilities within the tax department.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div>
