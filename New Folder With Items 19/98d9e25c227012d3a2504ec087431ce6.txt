<b>Business Development Executive</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
<br>
FIS connects consumers to their money. We're the largest, Fortune 500, Fintech company in the world. Our solution is branded by our customers - banks and retailers - so you might not have heard of us. But, if you've used a mobile banking app, interacted with an ATM, or used a debit card to complete a financial transaction, you're most likely using FIS software. Our product is one that touches the average consumer on a regular basis, and makes the financial world go 'round.<br>
Headquartered in Jacksonville, Florida, FIS serves more than 20,000 clients in over 130 countries, and our technology powers billions of transactions annually that move over $9 trillion around the globe. FIS is a Fortune 500 company, ranked 392 on the Fortune 500 list and is a member of Standard &amp; Poor's 500® Index. Named a 2016 "World's Most Admired Company" by Fortune Magazine.<br>
The Business Development Executive (BDE) is responsible for identifying and developing new opportunities for the field sales teams to pursue. The BDE drives demand for the organization's products, services and solutions in a variety of ways, including further qualifying leads that have been generated by marketing or other lead generation campaigns. At times, the BDE may also be called upon to engage in several small-net fishing programs; cold calling; appointment setting and audience generation.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Qualify prospects against established criteria prior to passing them to field sales representatives as tele qualified leads (TQLs).<br>
</li>
<li>Generate new leads for the sales organization to pursue.<br>
</li>
<li>Receive pre-determined groups of prospects back from field sales representatives for further nurturing using both email and telephone channels<br>
</li>
<li>Process inbound leads both telephone and web based.<br>
</li>
<li>Comply with all lead management related service level agreements.<br>
</li>
<li>Provide accurate weekly forecasts to marketing and sales leadership on the volume of leads expected to achieve TQL status.<br>
</li>
<li>Track and manage prospecting, qualification and nurture activities within the organizations sales force automation platform.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<br>

<br>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to develop qualified appointments<br>
</li>

</li>
