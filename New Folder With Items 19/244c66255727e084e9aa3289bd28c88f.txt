<div>About Us

<div><div>Esko is the worldwide market leader with software for packaging buyers, designers and manufacturers and hardware products for the packaging, label, sign and display industries. With Headquarters in Gent - Belgium, Esko employs around 1,500 people worldwide. Its global sales and support organization covers Europe, the Americas, Asia/Pacific and Japan.In our factory we produce high-quality cutting tables, which offer an all-round cuttion solution for any application: from sign making, display production, sample making and short to medion rum corrugated production.<br>

<div><h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description
</b></h2></div>
<div>
<div><b>The Opportunity:
</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Provide overall technical sales &amp; solution definition support to the Account Managers and with the goal of increasing Software Sales throughout the North American region.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partner with Account Managers and channel partners to present Esko (e.g ArtiosCAD Enterprise, WebCenter, Automation Engine, Cape Systems) software solutions to targeted customers &amp; prospective customers.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support Account Managers in the validation of a potential project; lead the definition of the business requirements, via a workflow assessment and/or value stream mapping, resulting in a project scope &amp; estimated return on investment.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working with a Project Manager, translate the business requirements into an appropriate business solution and a detailed statement of work or detailed report.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide in-depth on-site and remote demonstrations of defined Esko solutions to potential &amp; existing customers.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform benchmark demonstrations and analysis to prospects &amp; customers using customer provided files.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide pre-sales level configuration diagrams to Account Managers and/or Sales Management.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Detect and drive sales opportunities for consultative services and systems integration.<br>
</li></ul>
