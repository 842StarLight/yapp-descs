<h2>Sr. Mgr-Digital Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Consumer Insight, Measurement &amp; Analytics (CIMA) is a Global Marketing &amp; Sales organization within Walt Disney Parks and Resorts (WDPR) that informs strategy development and decision-making through actionable data analytics, integration and insights.<br>
<br>
Within CIMA, the Digital Analytics team is focused on leveraging best practices in web analytics to capture, analyze and optimize our websites to drive Guest satisfaction and business outcomes. We use data to maximize the magic in Guest trips to the greatest vacation destinations in the world, including the Walt Disney World Resort in Florida, the Disneyland Resort in California, Hong Kong Disneyland Resort, and the Shanghai Disneyland Resort. We also support businesses such as Disney Cruise Line, Adventures by Disney and Disney Vacation Club.<br>
<div><div>The Manager, Digital Analytics must be an innovative problem solver with a passion for developing and growing a team. The leader must be able to inspire others and have a desire for continuous learning in the dynamic space of Digital Analytics.<br>
<br>
The position reports to the Vice President, Digital Analytics and Travel Operations, and leads multiple direct reports.<br>
</div></div>
