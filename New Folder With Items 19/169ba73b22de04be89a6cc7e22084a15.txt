<p>Intertek, a leading provider of ATIC<b> </b>(Assurance, Testing, Inspection and Certification) Services, is looking for a Data Entry Clerk to join our Industry Services team in Bishop, TX.
</p>
<p>Intertek’s Industry Services team provides technical solutions that help customers maximize operational efficiency, ensure asset reliability, improve quality, and minimize safety risks. Some of our many service areas include inspection, non-destructive and materials testing, technical training, engineering, surveying, QA/QC, and supplier auditing.
</p>
<p>The Data Entry Clerk will support the Industry Services business by providing clerical support at the client’s facility. We’re looking for a Data Entry Clerk with 3-5 years’ experience in a petro-chemical facility.
