<h2>Project Manager II (Non-Tech)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Brink’s U.S., a division of Brink’s, Incorporated, is the premier provider of armored car transportation, currency and coin processing, ATM servicing and other value added services to financial institutions, retailers and other commercial and government entities. The company has a proud history of providing growth and advancement opportunities for its employees. We have a challenging opportunity for a Project Manager – Cash-in-Transit (CIT)
<br>
<br>
Job Summary:
<br>
The Project Manager is responsible for planning, scheduling, coordinating, organizing and leading all aspects of medium to large sized projects as it pertains to new business implementations, existing customer conversions, and other strategic CIT initiatives. The incumbent provides support and coordination of CIT activities and assists in implementing strategies and tactics focused on product line growth and profitability.
<br>
<br>
Key Responsibilities:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Direct interaction and effective coordination with external customers, sales, operations and functional groups
</li>
<li>Project Lifecycle Management – Initiation, Planning, Implementing, Controlling &amp; Closing
</li>
<li>Cost &amp; savings tracking
</li>
<li>Developing, interpreting and defining project requirements – Statement of Work deliverables
</li>
<li>Effective task assignment and project organization
</li>
<li>Developing and managing a project timeline
</li>
<li>Determining project risk exposure and developing remediation &amp; migration plans
</li>
<li>Determining project performance (KPI) status – cost &amp; schedule
</li>
<li>Effective problem resolution – root cause corrective action
</li>
<li>Process and work standardization
</li>
<li>Managing concurrent competing project demands
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned or necessary
</li>
</ul>
