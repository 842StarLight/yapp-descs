<h2>Power Settlements Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<div><div>The Eugene Water &amp; Electric Board seeks an experienced Power Settlements Analyst to perform accounting and settlement responsibilities associated with the purchase and sale of electric power, financial products and renewable energy credits (REC) for the utility and client customers. The selected candidate will:<br>
<ul>
<li>Use computer software to verify data and billing for transactions including transmission, ancillary services products, BPA load imbalance, wholesale energy contracts and RECs.
</li>
<li>Negotiate directly with counterparties to settle billing disputes.
</li>
<li>Maintain and verify all power accounting transactions, generation and interchange data from the Energy Trading and Risk Management software.
</li>
<li>Prepare industrial customer electric power data requirements and billing.
</li>
<li>Produce tax reporting such as CILT (City of Eugene in Lieu Tax Payment).
</li>
<li>Reconcile monthly generation and wholesale sales with load and wholesale purchases (sources and uses).
</li>
<li>Maintain and assist in the development of accounting database and archive files of generation, interchange and transfers.
</li>
<li>Be responsible for power transaction reporting requirements including Sources and Uses, reallocation of transactions within BPA power contracts and WECC Unscheduled Flow. Assist short-term traders in trade and tag entry to allow for proper settlement and reporting.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Successful candidates will have high attention to detail, strong problem solving skills, and interest in finding efficiencies in all facets of their work.
</div></div>
