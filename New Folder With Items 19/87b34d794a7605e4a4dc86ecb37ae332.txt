<h2>Restaurant Manager - Fast Growing Superfood Cafe Franchise</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Vitality Bowls is excited to haveyou as part of our team and we are always looking for dynamic, energetic,enthusiastic, fun, and motivated individuals to join team Vitality Bowls.</p>
<p>
Originating in San Ramon, VitalityBowls specializes in Açaí bowls, fresh juice, smoothies, panini and salads.Tara and Roy Gilad started the superfood café concept together as a result ofdiscovering their daughter’s severe food allergies and wanting to find safe,healthy food alternatives. Since franchising began in 2014, the brand has seensignificant growth with more than 100 cafes open and in development. Named oneof the Top 100 new franchises in 2017 by <i>Entrepreneur, </i>VitalityBowls is redefining the fast-casual healthy food restaurant sector every day ateach of our locations across the country.</p>
<p><b>
Vitality Bowls Store Manager JobDescription Overview:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Increase sales at all corporate stores</li>
<li>Implement and ongoing management of marketing program</li>
<li>Manage labor and food costs</li>
<li>Order management</li>
<li>Monthly Inventory</li>
<li>Management of team member training and development</li>
<li>Management of allergen safety</li>
<li>Management of food product quality</li>
<li>Practice clear and effective communication</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Daily</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage ticket times, ensure quality food production, promote customer engagement</li>
<li>Check to make sure opening, closing, deep cleaning and daily prep checklists are being used</li>
<li>Actively coach, train and quiz your team about their knowledge of the company, our products and allergen safety</li>
<li>Enforce allergen safety and awareness. Observe employees making nut-free bowls or their procedures when handling PB, almond butter, almonds or gluten-free ingredients. Ensure allergen safety kit is intact and available</li>
<li>Run through the health inspections checklist (test sanitizer strength, check seals on gaskets/all refrigeration units, ensure each employee has a food handler’s card, ensure thermometers are in each refrigeration unit, ect.)</li>
<li>Ensure correct measurements are in use and employees are using level measurements</li>
<li>Go through products to ensure FIFO is occurring</li>
<li>Check for under-prepping or over-prepping</li>
<li>Check Sales vs. Labor and ensure we are adequately staffed. Ensure we are sending employees home early as needed (this should be checked three times/day. 11am, 3pm and 5pm)</li>
<li>Close out no show pickup orders in the POS</li>
<li>Count Petty Cash/Go to the bank to replenish when needed</li>
<li>When opening complete deposits by 11am</li>
<li>Practice clear, positive, and effective communication</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Weekly</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Manage each corporate store repair and maintenance needs-</b>any supplies needed that aren’t an emergency (emergency items should be communicated immediately)</li>
<li>
<b>Corrective Actions</b>-Collaborate with manager if corrective actions need to be addressed with team members</li>
<li>
<b>Celebrating the Successes</b>-Who is doing really well/Who is ready for a promotion or the next step/Who are we growing?</li>
<li>
<b>Labor and Sales Last Week</b>-What was your labor last week and why; What were your sales last week and why?</li>
<li>
<b>Marketing Plan this Week</b>-What is your plan this week to generate business?</li>
<li>
<b>Anything Else we need to know about? </b>Keep us informed.</li>
<li>
<b>What is your schedule this week?</b> This is so everyone knows when you are working and can stop by to visit with you!</li>
<li>
<b>Take inventory and place Sysco order based on Inventory</b>. Monitor and track any product borrowed if borrowed between multiple locations. Return any borrowed product immediately. Re-evaluate ordering if borrowing product or buying products takes place too frequently.</li>
<li><b>Place any special orders based on inventory as needed</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Monthly</b><b>:</b>
</li>
<li>End of Month Inventory—complete end of month inventory and send to store and Operation Managers.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
