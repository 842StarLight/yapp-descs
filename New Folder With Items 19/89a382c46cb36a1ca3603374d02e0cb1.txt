<h2>Local Store Marketer - Salary Position (Estimated Hourly Rates Provided)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Mission: The Local Store Marketer serves as a liaison between the restaurant and the community. Their mission is to drive sales by inviting customers into the store and educating them on the health benefits and services offered, connecting with key contacts in the local business area while developing relationships and partnerships within the community.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Promote the brand with enthusiasm, build relationships with influential members of the community through grassroots marketing efforts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sustain open communication with the local Taziki’s owner(s)/operator(s), store managers, FOH and BOH staff as well as catering staff</li></ul>
<ul><li>Research, schedule and follow through on appointments with new and existing customer base</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage Social Media outlets</li></ul>
<ul><li>Extensive menu knowledge (catering and dine-in), aiding in the education of products to clients, guests and staff</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate food shows for potential and valued clients as well as attend and assist with high profile catering and goodwill events</li></ul>
<ul><li>Represent brand culture and identity, displaying proper etiquette in networking situations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage community and charity partnerships via goodwill donations and marketing efforts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Distribute marketing collateral and promotional items within local market</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create market sales goals and effectively track weekly sales</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in weekly or monthly manager meetings providing updates on local marketing efforts and events</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sustain rapport with key accounts by making periodic visits; exploring specific needs; anticipating new opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Continue education and update knowledge by participating in educational opportunities; reading professional publications; maintaining personal networks; participating in professional organizations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adhere to all company policies and procedures (employee handbook)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Professional attitude and respect for coworkers, establishment, and the brand</li></ul>
