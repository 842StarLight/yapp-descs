<h2>MATERIAL HANDLER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Founded in 1925, MacLean-Fogg is a worldwide enterprise currently operating 40 global manufacturing facilities across 5 continents with annual sales in excess of one billion (USD) and a workforce of over 3,200 people. MacLean-Fogg is a privately held enterprise headquartered in Mundelein, IL USA and is comprised of two primary businesses, MacLean-Fogg Component Solutions and MacLean Power Systems. MacLean-Fogg Component Solutions (MFCS) is a leading manufacturer of fastener components, engineered components and engineered plastics for automotive, heavy truck, and other diverse industries. MacLean Power Systems (MPS) is a leading manufacturer of products for electric utility, telecommunications and civil markets.
<br><br>
Perform duties to assure proper movement of raw material and finished goods within entire plant as production needs dictate; perform materials movement related to work in process.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Monitor and recharge forklift equipment, check fluid levels as necessary.</li>
<li>Move material throughout plant using standard forklifts, according to flag light system and verbal instructions.</li>
<li>Weigh produced goods and report production using RFSmart.</li>
<li>Select proper raw material or work-in-process inventory and supply to machines for further processing.</li>
<li>Perform physical counts and weights of inventory on a regularly scheduled basis, enter data on special ticket.</li>
<li>Operate plant safety and closure equipment. Unload vendor trucks as assigned.</li>
<li>Transfer material from receiving area or production machine to washer/waxer area as assigned.</li>
<li>May monitor concentration levels of soap, rust preventative, and wax in appropriate tanks using refractometer; monitor water levels as assigned.</li>
<li>May drain and clean washer and waxer apparatus on regularly scheduled basis as assigned.</li>
<li>May unload washed/waxed parts from rollover to clean containers at end of cycle as assigned.</li>
<li>May receive and write initial rejection notices on customer returns, provide data to Quality Control Department.</li>
<li>Follow all Environmental and Quality Policies and follow appropriate procedures as required.</li>
<li>Follow safety rules and keep work area in a clean and orderly condition.</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><ul>
<li>High School education or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>Must be able to work from verbal instructions, written instructions and shop orders.</li>
<li>Must be able to operate a standard forklift, a roll-over lift truck and hand trucks.</li>
<li>Must be able to understand JDE/ RFSmart computer system, JDE/RFSmart procedures and raw material coding.</li>
<li>Must have basic understanding of production jobs and types of raw material processed in the Forming Department; Purchasing and Accounting procedures.</li>
<li>Must be a self starter with good organizational skills and a high level of reliability.</li>
<li>Must be a self-motivated team player.</li>
</ul>
