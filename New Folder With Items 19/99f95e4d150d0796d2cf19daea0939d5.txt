 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Title</b></h3>
Field Service Engineer 2 - IT (CCNA Required) [Tampa, FL]<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h3>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Title</b></p>
<p>
Field Service Engineer 2</p>
<p><br>
We challenge you to join Philips Healthcare as a Patient Monitoring Field Service Engineer where you will WOW our customers by using your strong technical skills in providing the best in class customer service!
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>In this role, you have the opportunity to</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
You will contribute to providing the right data, context, and approach which will change the way we do business and make a difference for our customers.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are responsible for (but not limited to)</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Install, service, conduct monthly PMs, and test complex IT networks following stringent real time patient data requirements.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot complex network issues to ensure acceptable Quality of Service.</li>
<li>Install complex, multi-phase systems composed of IT infrastructure and patient monitoring equipment in both clinical and non-clinical environments across the geography.</li>
<li>Provide technical review of system configurations to ensure viability of system performance.</li>
<li>Conduct monthly PMs on the equipment, Assets management</li>
<li>Lead and direct a diverse team of internal and external resources.</li>
<li>Own the customer experience through the life cycle of customer systems from installation through end-of-support.</li>
<li>Grow the MA&amp;TC business in the assigned geography within the scope of both equipment and service revenue and contract revenue.</li>
<li>Work within a dynamic customer care team with clinical, sales, and service partners to support the business activities at the highest level.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are a part of a team</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
This particular position will work within the MA&amp;TC Services organization. This division of Philips Healthcare specializes in the installation and service of patient monitoring devices, networks, and telemetry systems.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As hospital environments become more integrated the service organization is looking to expand its ability to install and service complex networks as well and consult with customers on high end integrations.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
To succeed in this role, you should have the following skills and experience</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>BS Electrical Engineering/Computer Science/Biomed disciplines with at least 3 years’ experience or AS Electrical Engineering/Computer Science/Biomed disciplines with 5 years’ experience in Healthcare / IT Technologies.</li>
<li>CCNA certification is required. Higher certifications are recommended.</li>
<li>Microsoft and other certifications are desirable (MCTS, MCITP, VM WARE, CCNA Wireless, CCNA Security, CCNP, CCNP Wireless, CCNP Security)</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of Patient Monitoring systems.</li>
<li>Influence internal and external resources to a stated objective without formal authority.</li>
<li>Effectively negotiate.</li>
<li>Deliver required results with high levels of customer satisfaction, both internal external.</li>
<li>Previous experience working in a hospital environment.</li>
<li>Must possess a valid driver’s license</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
