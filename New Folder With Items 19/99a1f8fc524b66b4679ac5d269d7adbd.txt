<h2>Business Development Manager // Enterprise Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We’re Leanpath, the global leader in food waste prevention. We’re on a big mission to make food waste prevention everyday practice in the world’s kitchens. We partner with leading foodservice and hospitality organizations – including Google, IKEA, Aramark and Sodexo – by providing smart food waste prevention solutions (hardware, software and services). We’ve been working on this problem, and only this problem, for more than 15 years. Along the way we’ve catalyzed a movement, created a new technology category, engaged many thousands of employees, and reduced millions of pounds of food waste.</p>
<p>
Are you excited to work on one of the most important global issues of our time? Are you passionate about fixing our broken food system and making a measurable difference in the battle against wasted food? Do you have a demonstrated track record selling complex, enterprise solutions in the foodservice or hospitality industry? Do you love the idea of being at a fast-paced start-up environment where you are a critical element to our success? Are you highly ambitious and motivated sales executive who consistently meets or exceeds your quotas?</p>
<p>
If you answered ‘yes’ to these questions, then we encourage you to read on…</p>
<p>
We are currently recruiting for a Business Development Manager to develop and grow assigned segments and geographies by developing new client partnerships with leading food businesses. This includes prospecting, discovery, pilot program coordination, and business case development. The ideal candidate will possess substantial experience selling enterprise solutions (ideally hardware/software within the foodservice and/or hospitality segment) to multinational enterprise customers. Experience in food services, navigating complex buying coalitions, developing territories and a strong entrepreneurial skill set is a must.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Tasks &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Territory planning and development</li>
<li>Lead generation and development</li>
<li>Research, establish and develop relationships with new prospects - build and maintain a healthy pipeline</li>
<li>Qualify and nurture high-potential opportunities</li>
<li>Negotiate and close complex multi-unit, multi-site contracts</li>
<li>Manage buying processes with a broad coalition of stakeholders</li>
<li>Develop vision, scope and business case for client programs, including the use of detailed growth models and performance assessments</li>
<li>Develop mastery of Leanpath solutions and industry landscape, including site scoping to map capabilities to client goals</li>
<li>Transition new customers to the Leanpath Operations and Customer Success Teams</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing on customer bespoke marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Communicate value proposition via writing and presentations to large and small audiences</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Compensation</b></p>
<p>
We offer a competitive base salary dependent on experience with a sales incentive program focused on results. We pay 100% of the employee’s premium for health, dental and vision coverage (employee pays for spouse or family). We currently offer 20 days of PTO to start in addition to paid holidays. We offer a flexible work environment with a group of smart, committed, friendly and supportive colleagues.</p>
