<h2>Receptionist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Receptionists are one of the first employees that have guest interactions in the retail environment! In addition to being organized, with the ability to multi-task, receptionists should be enthusiastic employees who can guide new, and existing members through the “check in” process! Receptionists will be responsible for verifying that customers meet all qualifications to obtain goods and services from Skymint, and provide them with their first experience while handling all aspects of a traditional office environment; including but not limited to: answering phones, taking messages for management, verifying confidential paperwork, and communicating effectively with customers and team members.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to pass a criminal background check
</li>
<li>Must sign a caregiver withdrawal form
</li>
<li>Must be authorized to live and work in the U.S.
</li>
<li>Must be 21 years old.
</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and communication skills
</li>
<li>Basic computer and information technology skills
</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to work flexible hours including evenings, weekends and holidays to meet the needs of employees with guidance and growth opportunities and ensure the dispensary is a positive work environment while maintaining the productivity of the business.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for greeting patients upon arrival and performing initial check in
</li>
<li>Assists new patients with questions and required forms
</li>
<li>Updates patient information in Treez; data entry, scanning ID’s
</li>
<li>Answers incoming phone calls and voice messages
</li>
<li>Assists Manager and Budtenders with various tasks upon request which may include product labeling, sales floor upkeep and light cleaning
</li>
<li>Reception may be asked to prep online orders during busy times</li>
</ul>
</div>
