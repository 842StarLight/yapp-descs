<h2>Technical Product Support Specialist I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>Johnson Controls is hiring a Technical Product Support Specialist in Marinette WI.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The mission of Johnson Controls Technical Services function is to provide quality service that exceeds customers' expectations, leading to the wide use and acceptance of our product offerings and solutions; increasing company value and mitigating business risk.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
What you will do</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Technical Services provides technical support to our internal/external customers, sales force, distributors and end users. The Technical Product Support Specialist provides efficient, effective and cordial technical support. This position reports to the Technical Services – Engineered Systems Manager.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
How you will do it</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Prepare Bill of Materials for distributors, both domestic and international</div>
<div>
Communicates with Field sales to follow up on quotes</div>
<div>
Design systems and coordinate drafting time as required</div>
<div>
Prepare sketches for Drafter/CAD Operator(s) to complete.</div>
<div>
Consult with engineers, architects, and other professional and technical personnel.</div>
<div>
Provide answers to technical questions from distribution, field sales and other customers.</div>
<div>
Gains customer acceptance by explaining or demonstrating cost reductions and operational improvements.</div>
<div>
Travel to customer sites to assist in on site hazard analysis when required</div>
<div>
Represent the Technical Service Department on technical committees and project teams.</div>
<div>
Assist Quality Services with warranty claims.</div>
<div>
Complies with federal, state, and local legal requirements by studying existing and new legislation.</div>
<div>
Anticipates future legislation, advising customer on product, services, or equipment adherence to requirements.</div>
<div>
Review literature to assure technical correctness prior to release to the field.</div>
<div>
Maintains professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops; reviewing professional publications; establishing personal networks; participating in professional societies.</div>
<div>
Review manufacturing drawings for accuracy</div>
<div>
Contributes to team effort by accomplishing related tasks as needed.</div>
</div>

</div>

<div>
<br>
</div>

Associates degree in Technology or Engineering or similar background through education or related experience.</div>
<div>
Experience</div>
<div>
1 to 3 years of work experience</div>

National Institute for Certification in Engineering Technologies (NICET) Level I in Special Hazard Systems or equivalent industry certification a plus.</div>
