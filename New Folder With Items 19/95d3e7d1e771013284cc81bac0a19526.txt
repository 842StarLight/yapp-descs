<b>Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>
Warehouse Material Handler/Order Picker</b></p>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Friendly individual with the ability and skills to meet, greet, and serve customers.</li>
<li>Effective team player with good communication skills that is proactive, positive, conscientious, organized and efficient.</li>
<li>Must be able to multi-task, prioritize workload, and enjoy working in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Basic computer skills, including knowledge and skill in the use of inventory management systems and office software (e.g. Email programs, Internet browsers)</li>
<li>Basic math skills sufficient to manage inventory, including addition, subtraction, multiplication and division</li>
<li>Experience with computer or barcode driven inventory management systems</li>
<li>Must be able to lift up to 75 pounds.</li>
<li>Forklift operation experience preferred.</li>
<li>Communicates well verbally and in writing, in English, and in a professional manner.</li>
<li>Capable of representing Johnstone Supply in a respectable and professional manner.</li>
<li>Professional and courteous demeanor when working with customers and colleagues.</li>
<li>Works in various warehouse roles as instructed by manager.</li>
<li>Must be able to work overtime when required.</li>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent certification preferred.</li>
<li>Must be able to pass a background check and a drug test.</li>
</ul>
