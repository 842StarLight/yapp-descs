<p>
We are MOSS, an award winning home remodeling company based in Northern Virginia and we are changing the game in the residential remodeling industry. Homeowners are tired of the hard sell, dishonest business practices, poor workmanship, poor service, and frankly what is often just a terrible experience when remodeling their homes. We are changing all that. We are building a company where innovative ideas and cutting edge techniques result in customer experiences that blow away our competition and the minds of our customers. Where legendary customer care results in customers for life. And where employees work together as a team to accomplish incredible things together.</p>
<p><b>
We’re growing fast and right now we’re seeking a game changing Project Designer to join our team.</b> What makes a game changing Project Designer? The person we are seeking is passionate about transforming a customer’s house into the home of their dreams. They truly believe that the remodeling industry can be better than it is now. We’re looking for someone who gets it. Someone who understands that it’s much more than providing just a remodeling design – it’s really about delivering a totally different experience. One that customers remember and talk about fondly for a very long time.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities for this key position include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>To capture the vision outlined by the sales consultant and homeowner in the proposal and bring it to life</li>
<li>To ensure a positive and exciting design experience for the homeowner</li>
<li>Provide each homeowner with a MOSS 360 experience</li>
<li>Build a relationship and trust with the homeowner</li>
<li>Take the homeowner from the beginning of the project to completion</li>
<li>To be the design resource on the team that facilitates a timely sales, design, and construction timeline</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications for this key position include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A demonstrated passion for remodeling design projects</li>
<li>A track record of high performance and achievement</li>
<li>Three to five years of industry experience in home remodeling and design is required</li>
<li>Interior Design or Architecture degree preferred</li>
<li>Experience with CAD software required. 20/20 and/or Chief Architecture highly preferred</li>
<li>Ability to interrelate with clientele on design projects through project management with follow-up being a key factor to success</li>
<li>Strong detail orientation, self-disciplined, creative and confident, problem solving and job coordination skills</li>
</ul>

We’re strongly committed to providing our employees with a stable work environment, highly competitive pay and benefits, plus plenty of opportunity for learning and personal growth. Creativity and innovation are encouraged in our culture of continuous improvement. And most importantly, our people are provided the same concern, respect and care within MOSS that we share with every MOSS customer.</p>
