<h2>Endowment & Foundation/Impact Investing Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div>Description:</div>
<div>
<p>The Endowment &amp; Foundation / Impact Investing Marketing Manager serves as the primary point of contact for Glenmede’s Endowment &amp; Foundation business as well as Impact Investing business.</p>
<p>This position is responsible for ensuring marketing activities for the businesses are in alignment with business strategy and priorities. This person will collaborate closely with Endowment &amp; Foundation business leaders, Impact Investing business leaders and members of the Sales and Marketing teams to ensure initiatives are successfully developed and executed. This position reports to the Institutional Marketing Manager.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p><b>Endowment &amp; Foundation / Impact Investing Marketing
</b></p>
<ul><li>Partner with marketing channel managers, the Endowment &amp; Foundation business line, Impact Investing business line and the Sales team in the development and execution of strategic marketing initiatives in support of business objectives and branding.</li></ul>
<p>o Initiatives may include, but are not limited to: campaigns (content, events, advertising, etc.), branding, surveys.</p>
<ul><li>Track engagement and Marketing ROI as it pertains to business line specific objectives.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate with the marketing budget manager to monitor, forecast, and report both the Endowment &amp; Foundation budget/spend as well as the Impact Investing budget/spend. Ensure projects align with business priorities and budget.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Represent Marketing at operating committee meetings and other key meetings as appropriate (i.e. Impact Investing Guiding Coalition).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in coordination of marketing activities for Impactivate, a Glenmede sponsored website used to promote thought leadership around all aspects of Impact.</li></ul>
<p>o Partner with the Director of Content to manage the internal and external workflow of content, including content development and coordination, collaboration with the Impact Investing Team, Compliance and Skyword.</p>
<ul><li>Serve as the person primarily responsible for relationship management and problem resolution across all marketing disciplines pertaining to both Impact Investing and Endowment &amp; Foundation Management.</li></ul>
<p><b>Firm Building
</b></p>
<p>Engage in department-specific and firm-wide initiatives to expand capabilities, improve user experience, and develop more efficient processes.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required.</li>
<li>5+ years of broad marketing experience required.</li>
<li>3+ years managing the lifecycle of complex, multi-disciplinary projects with the ability to collaborate, influence and present to staff at all levels.</li>
<li>Superior team orientation. Self-starter with a great deal of initiative.</li>
<li>Ability to prepare and deliver presentations to senior management.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Prior experience with Endowment &amp; Foundation Management preferred.</li>
<li>Exposure to and awareness of key impact investing publications and newsletters is preferred.</li>
<li>Prior experience with lead generation program preferred</li>
<li>Experience with B2B/B2C CRMs preferred</li>
</ul>
