<h2>Grinding Programming & Optimization Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><p>This position is responsible for conducting and coordinating product manufacturing programs and machine set-up criteria, while acting as a primary conduit between product engineering and manufacturing. This associate would be key to establishing and supporting manufacturing procedures, processes, and techniques across the facility. In addition, the associate would contribute to the development and execution of various grinding training programs for manufacturing Associates. This is a vital high-level role within the facility requiring a strong understanding of all facets of cutting tool manufacturing and advanced programming of CNC grinding equipment.</p></div>
<p></p>
<br><div>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop programs and attributes for the manufacturing of product on assigned projects
</li>
<li>Function as an engineering resource for the set-up of all jobs
</li>
<li>Continuously optimize and improve all product development initiatives and processes
</li>
<li>Assist with defining accurate time estimations for costing of various products
</li>
<li>Establish operating parameters throughout the facilities equipment and processes
</li>
<li>Be responsible for delivering programs and manufacturing definitions against a production plan
</li>
<li>Maintain all equipment software, updates, and related databases
</li>
<li>Liaise with manufacturing to support optimized operation and set-up of equipment
</li>
<li>Maintain close coordination and communication between engineering and manufacturing activities
</li>
<li>Share information in ways that promote, clarify, and keep the organization informed
</li>
<li>Review, analyze, and report production of products and performance of equipment weekly
</li>
<li>Identify training needs and assist with providing training
</li>
<li>Help to introduce new technology and implement new technology to manufacturing
</li>
<li>Work with Quality Assurance to ensure all design goals and necessary quality is defined and achieved
</li>
<li>Determine causes of non-conformity with respect to product, specifications, and/or operating or production problems
</li>
<li>Assist with implementing any corrective actions defined within manufacturing
</li>
<li>Support continuous improvement, lean, and 5S programs
</li>
<li>Assist with keeping safety and work rules and regulations, and departmental housekeeping standards
</li>
<li>Assist with the compliance of OSHA and EPA regulations<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Marginal / Less Essential Job Functions
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Report market trends in applications of cutting tools
</li>
<li>Assist in new product development as needed
</li>
<li>Attend assigned industry trade shows / sales conferences / customer visits as a technical advisor
</li>
<li>Assist in budget planning, distribution selection, and forecasting
</li>
<li>Provide feedback on all sales and marketing objectives<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Note: This description reflects the principal functions of the position for the purpose of job evaluation. Associate must adhere to changes in job requirements.</p>
</div>
<br><div>
<p><b>Educational Requirements
</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in engineering, Journeyman education, and/or five years’ technical sales experience<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Other Qualifications Desired
</b></p>
<p><i>Training &amp; Experience
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong background in programming and setting up multi-axis CNC tool and cutter grinders
</li>
<li>Proficient with tool grinding modeling software (Numeroto and Tool Studio)
</li>
<li>Advanced understanding of using inspection equipment critical to cutting tool geometry
</li>
<li>Ability to understand various round tool geometries
</li>
<li>Complete understanding of applying Diamond grinding wheels
</li>
<li>Experience submitting cost proposals, quoting costs, and documenting ROI is important to this role
</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Analytical and data processing capabilities
</li>
<li>Must have outstanding time management and organizational skills
</li>
<li>Good computer skills are critical, with an understanding of documentation
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft products and basic computer software
</li>
<li>Understanding of working with high precision tools
</li>
<li>Self-direction, tact, diplomacy and a clear, courteous, and professional manner<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>License Required: </i>Must possess and maintain a valid driver’s license and meet requirements set by KSPT insurance carrier.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Visual Acuity: </i>Must be able to visually assess tooling samples under varying magnification, inspect finished tool and other visual parameters with or without accommodation.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Hearing Ability: </i>Ability to monitor machine sounds to identify correct grinding conditions with or without reasonable accommodation.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Working Conditions </i>: Normal office environment utilizing personal computer, phone and related office equipment. Regularly required to walk, sit or stand. Constant use of upper extremities. Infrequent lifting up to 50 lbs. Frequent visits to various manufacturing facilities with exposure to hazards of automated machinery, noise, dust, oils, moving equipment, etc. Extensive travel is required.<br>
</p>
