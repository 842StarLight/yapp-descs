<h2>Executive Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Description
<br><br>
The Service Delivery Manager (SDM) is responsible for successful onboarding of customers into Seal Cloud Services (SCS). It also includes management of client relationships from a services delivery perspective at multiple, assigned accounts. SDM will work closely with Professional Services, Support, Cloud Operations and Sales teams to deliver and manage the contracted services to customer.
<br><br>
<b>The SDM is focused on:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Delivering service that meets SLA and delivers high customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Developing relationships and teams to continue to deliver quality service</li>
<li>Initiating and Managing the improvements required in service</li>
</ul>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Accountable for ensuring customer is onboarded into the SCS environment and successfully using the service with a high degree of customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Work with key partners such as DocuSign to establish strong connected support processes.</li>
<li>Build and maintain a Confluence dashboard to track key activities</li>
<li>Manage all the customer related onboarding activities and continue to provide consistent quality service throughout their contract period as per their defined scope and SLA</li>
<li>For a subset of customers act as the project manager for the onboarding and the contract ingestion phase of the project</li>
<li>Conduct interactive status meetings with customers, Cloud operations, Professional Services, and Dev technology teams to review ongoing activities and progress</li>
<li>Provide customers and internal management with regular reports and status updates, including timelines, milestones, risks, issues, decision and overall status</li>
<li>Facilitate communications between different teams where needed for special circumstances, customer problems, and high-visibility service items</li>
<li>Provides expert incident and problem management for high impact and critical customer issues and ensures root-cause analysis is conducted and a corrective action plan is followed through with any learning applied for future benefit</li>
<li>Escalate issues as needed to keep projects on target (within established parameters) and proactively work to resolve issues that may impact customer satisfaction or SLA</li>
<li>Partner to run and administer the Seal Support ZenDesk Portal</li>
<li>Identifies opportunities for process improvements</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Flexibility – this role is multi-faceted and you need to be too!</li>
<li>Strong client focus – ability to operate at a senior manager level</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in a technical or business discipline, or equivalent</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years' experience in software deployment/project management or similar</li>
<li>Proven project management skills with demonstrated experience in a software environment – includes formal project management techniques</li>
<li>Conversant in cloud technologies (GCP/AWS/Azure)</li>
<li>Familiarity with the Atlassian toolset (Jira &amp; Confluence)</li>
<li>ZenDesk experience a plus</li>
<li>Able to work independently with positive problem-solving attitude</li>
<li>Solid work ethic with a willingness to work extra as needed</li>
<li>Ability to function in a small company where processes are still being built out and a certain degree of ambiguity is often present</li>
</ul>
