<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Own campaign media planning from initial strategy to execution through a process that requires:
<ul>
<li>Participating in creative brainstorms to ensure the approach aligns with client KPIs and business strategy</li>
<li>Manage the pre-sale RFP process, which includes building winning media plans and advising on media tactics to best achieve client goals</li>
<li>Leading client kick-off calls</li>
<li>Gathering third-party assets and collaborating with AdOps for campaign launch</li>
<li>Providing screenshots, reporting, optimizations, and insights throughout the life of campaigns</li>
<li>Monitoring campaign performance start-to-finish to:
<ul>
<li>Ensure a successful partnership</li>
<li>Drive incremental revenue</li>
<li>Promote renewal opportunities</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Preparing and presenting client-facing decks that showcase performance, learnings, and future recommendations</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

Experience</i></p>

<li>Exceptional verbal and written communication skills, for both external and internal purposes</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Talents</i></p>
