<p>S-One Holdings Corporation is a global company headquartered in Sarasota, Florida, with a focus on the digital imaging industry, which the company pioneered in 1994. Designed from its inception, S-One was structured to partner with major brands and manufacturers who needed advanced marketing, an integrated and flexible supply chain, and technical sales capabilities.</p>
<p>
S-One consists of several subsidiary companies: ABAQA, Brand Management Group, LexJet, S-One Labels &amp; Packaging and Utopia Digital Technologies. Through its subsidiaries, S-One is the exclusive licensee for Fredrix wide-format canvas, HP Large Format Printing Materials, KODAK Wide-Formal &amp; PROFESSIONAL Inkjet Media.</p>
<p><i>
S-One is seeking a multi-faceted developer to enhance existing and build back-end integrations with consumer-facing e-commerce websites and work with our design team to reshape how people visualize the printing and graphics market using AR experiences. You will work collaboratively with our team to develop, plan and execute deliverables on time and on budget. You should be able to maintain various back-end aspects of a website including integrations, updates, moves, etc. You will be required to implement new features and enhance existing systems. You will help develop detailed functional, technical design, and test specifications.</i></p>
