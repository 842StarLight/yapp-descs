<h2>Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Property Manager position requires excellent skills in management and motivation of people. Applicants should possess the ability to deal with residents, prospects and vendors, allowing them to feel at ease and comfortable quickly.
</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Additionally the position requires the following:
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Excellent management and communication skills</li>
<li>Strong administrative and organizational skills</li>
<li>Strong time management skills and the ability to prioritize wisely</li>
<li>Professional Image</li>
<li>Strong customer service orientation</li>
<li>Good understanding of sales and marketing concepts and the ability to develop, implement and evaluate market plans</li>
<li>Knowledge of on-site maintenance requirements including dealing with vendors and contractors</li>
<li>Ability to close the deal</li>
<li>Ability to drive a car</li>
<li>Ability to operate and understand personal computer functions and management software packages</li>
<li>Ability to function as a team player</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Licenses: </b>A valid driver's license and current automobile insurance</p>
<p><b>
Training: </b>Generally, no prior formal training is required. However, satisfactory completion of in-house training in leasing, computers and property management is generally required within the first 90 days of employment.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Attendance: </b>Position requires the ability to work any of the seven days of the week, 52 weeks of the year. Due to the property staffing limitations, it is extremely critical that individuals be able to work their scheduled hours on a consistent basis and, if necessary, overtime hours when requested.</li>
<li>
<b>Equipment: </b>Position requires individuals to furnish their own automobiles or to operate a vehicle provided by the property to transport prospective residents on the property and to make bank deposits as required.</li>
<li>Position requires following company specified uniforms and/or dress code standards.</li>
<li>Position requires the ability to enter data into a computer and generate printed reports accurately.</li>
</ul>
