<p><b>SUMMARY
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>This position of <b>National Account Resource Coordinator/Project Administrator</b> is responsible for routine National Account Resource Coordination, service sall dispatch, project documentation, status monitoring, and administrative closeout of each project or service call. This position is required to closely interact with the National Account Project Managers and Account Managers to ensure the timely completion of tasks or issues.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</i></b></p>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates service resources for National Accounts</li>
<li>Schedules personnel, materials, sub-contractors and equipment based on project needs, skill sets, and availability.</li>
<li>Generates Work Order Documents, dispatches technicians, and notifies customers.</li>
<li>Responsible for customer interaction/satisfaction of contractors and owner representatives.</li>
<li>Communicates with all National Account Equipment Project Managers, National Account Technical Support and National Account Managers to assign/dispatch Controls Technicians and HVAC Technicians.</li>
<li>Coordinates operations interaction with the Parts Department and Sales/Fulfillment, as necessary.</li>
<li>Responsible for issuing purchase orders and reconciling purchase order issues as needed.</li>
<li>Process documents for service claims sales allowances, retrofit, and labor warranty.</li>
<li>Reviews project paperwork, complete calls, and forwards project paperwork as required.</li>
<li>Assists in Unit Verification processes, logs service start-up and service work requirements
</li>
<li>Facilitates warranty processes as needed.</li>
<li>Manages and distributes installation operation and maintenance manuals (IOMs).</li>
<li>Responsible for general administrative functions to support the National Account Team.</li>
<li>Flexibility to work overtime/weekends, on limited basis or as required</li>
</ul>
