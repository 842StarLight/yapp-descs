<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION PURPOSE:
</b></h2>
<p>The Manager, Tax &amp; Payroll reports to the Director, Tax &amp; Accounting. This position manages the day to day operations of both the Tax and Payroll Departments.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Plans, staffs, and leads the Tax &amp; Payroll activities for the Accounting Services Division, including managing associate performance in accordance with established guidelines, coaching and motivating direct reports, and administering compensation.</li>
<li>Participates in the development of operating goals and objectives for the departments; recommends, implements, and administers methods and procedures to enhance operations.</li>
<li>Provide consultation and recommendations on WoodmenLife’s product administration systems to insure such systems comply with established statutory requirements.</li>
<li>Manages the payroll process to ensure efficiency and accuracy of all payments and tax processing; including Home Office, Sales, National Board of Directors and Retirees.</li>
<li>Review of federal, state and local income tax returns, tax deposits, information returns and other tax filings to insure returns are filed timely and compliant with established regulatory requirements.</li>
<li>Develop career path objectives and establish strategic objectives for the department that direct associates to achieve division and organization strategic initiatives.</li>
<li>Prepare and manage the department budgets for Tax, Payroll and Information Reporting.</li>
<li>Coordinate tax research requests, provide counsel, direct and review tax policy on a variety of tax regulatory issues. Insure that systems are in place to capture, review, interpret, and disseminate relevant changes in tax law and or procedures.</li>
<li>Meets expectations for attendance and punctuality in accordance with the expectations established by your supervisor and WoodmenLife policy.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned by management.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Accounting/Finance or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>A minimum of 5 + years of relevant Tax, Accounting, Finance or Payroll experience in a corporate or another relevant financial position.</li>
<li>Three Years of Management Experience.</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of tax research software, tax filing software and information reporting software</li>
<li>Experience with Human Capital Management Systems including Payroll and Time and Attendance</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Compensation and Benefit Plans.</li>
<li>Demonstrates an advanced level of knowledge of spreadsheet software.</li>
<li>Must be proficient at managing projects across multiple business units through partnering with management to reach consensus in resolving compliance initiatives.</li>
<li>Proven problem solving, analytical and decision making skills.</li>
<li>Engages well with others and are passionate about providing an exemplary customer experience.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>DESIRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A CPA certification.</li>
<li>Masters of Taxation / Business Administration.</li>
<br>
</ul>
