<h2>Assistant Manager Cash Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p>Under the direction of the Cash Management Manager, assist and performs all required functions in accordance with regulatory compliance guidelines and prepare team for audits within Deposit Operations with special focus on Cash Management.Assist with the management and daily operations of the Cash Management area as well as lead back up during manager’s absence.Partner with the Manager to promote mentoring and coach staff to achieve goals.You are expected to lead by example with meetings and exceed individual goals.Manage and maintain staff to ensure proper coverage and foster a motivated, diverse team environment.Manage and ensure customers issues and questions are addressed to a satisfactory resolution.Streamline processes and create efficiencies to deliver outstanding customer experience and exceptional telephone support.
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Follow and assist with implementation of all directives, policies, procedures and changes in regulations pertaining to Cash Management with a focus in ACH Rules
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Performs designated functions of Cash Management services including Remote Deposit Capture (RDC), Positive Pay, Account Analysis, Lockbox, Internal Sweeps, Business Online and Business Mobile Banking, Business Bill Pay, Business Online Wires and ACH Origination such as:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Oversee and Assist with processing new customer applications and maintenance requests
</p></li>
<li><p>Assist with customer site visits, customer set ups, updating manuals and coordinating for all Cash Management Services
</p></li>
<li><p>Lead yearly audits for RDC and ACH Origination customers, review limits for appropriateness and assist with updating procedures, policies, FDICIA guidelines and risk assessments
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensure that processes and files comply with the National Automated Clearing House Association (NACHA) and other related regulations. Work with customers troubleshooting and resolving file formatting issues.
</p></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Perform check imaging review, and reconcile general ledger account entries as applicable with the cash management systems
</p></li>
<li><p>Accepting and sending originated ACH batch files, balance all files and correct exceptions
</p></li>
<li><p>Process daily business online wire requests by deadline
</p></li>
<li><p>Process monthly Account Analysis customer fees
</p></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Serve as first escalation point for customer Cash Management concerns, and Cash management system issues and outages by:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Tracking, recording and monitoring any concerns, issues or outages
</p></li>
<li><p>Engaging directly with vendors to research and resolve any issues or outages
</p></li>
<li><p>Reviewing system settings and propose any changes when applicable
</p></li>
<li><p>Creating appropriate internal and external communications regarding any issues or outages
</p></li>
<li><p>Engaging internal staff as needed to research and/or communicate any outages or issues
</p></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><p>Facilitate and provide customer support via phone, in person, secure e-mail and e-mail for Remote Deposit Capture (RDC), Positive Pay, Account Analysis, Lockbox, Internal Sweeps, Business Online Banking, Business Online Wires, ACH Origination and
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintain expert level of knowledge of the Bank’s cash management products and services, and managing inherent risks with these services
</p></li>
<li><p>Lead and/or assist Cash Management Manager with implementing policy and procedure changes and updates to rules and regulations to ensure compliance with state and federal guidelines as well as bank policies
</p></li>
<li><p>Lead and/or assist with implementing updates and/or changes to Cash Management systems and performing other functions to ensure compliance with state and federal guidelines as well as bank policies
</p></li>
<li><p>Create internal and external Cash Management training material and communications and lead and/or assist with internal trainings as defined by manager
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintain high level of communication with manager on Cash Management customer needs, vendor issues, program issues and system updates
</p></li>
<li><p>Provide support for Cash Management consultative sales functions in conjunction with Lenders, Private Bankers, Cash Management Sales, Branch Personnel and assist in ongoing relationship management activities
</p></li>
<li><p>Establish a positive working relationship to assist branches, Private Bankers, Cash Management Sales and related departments and provide internal customer support as deemed necessary in all areas of cash management and third party processes such as:
</p></li>
</ul>
<ul><li><p>ACH Originator set ups with the Loan Department
</p></li></ul>
<ul>
<li><p>RDC, Positive Pay, Account Analysis, Internal Sweeps, Business Online and Business Mobile Banking, Business Online Wires, and ACH Origination inquiries as well as advanced troubleshooting support
</p></li>
<li><p>RSA token activation, lost tokens and customer contact regarding tokens
</p></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Lead and/or facilitate Cash Management projects and group tasks and participate in bank-wide projects and group tasks as a Cash Management expert and as defined by manager
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Perform monthly monitoring, verification, reconciliation and reporting of selected Cash Management reports including:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Providing monthly Cash Management totals for various bank reports
</p></li>
<li><p>Account Analysis customer set ups and fees
</p></li>
<li><p>Reconcile business "test accounts"
</p></li>
<li><p>Municipal accounts
</p></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain and manage inventory and data integrity of all Cash Management software and hardware products including but not limited to:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>RDC scanner stock, including ink and cleaning cards
</p></li>
<li><p>RSA physical and software tokens
</p></li>
<li><p>Customer set ups and changes within all Cash Management programs
</p></li>
<li><p>Updating Account Analysis system settings
</p></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Manage multiple priorities with reporting and customer information systems on a daily basis
</p></li>
<li><p>Assist during outages
</p></li>
<li><p>Pursue development of supervisory tasks and skills
</p></li>
<li><p>Cross train to provide backup to Deposit Operations personnel as necessary to expedite workflow
</p></li>
<li><p>Perform additional duties as requested, needed or assigned<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<br>

</p></li>

</p></li>
<li><p>Perform additional duties as requested or assigned
</p></li>
