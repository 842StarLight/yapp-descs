<h2>Field Service Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Our Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
Do you have a knack for solving problems? Are you skilled in both front-end software packages and output devices? Do you have a passion for helping customers? Would you like to work independently, but be part of a fun, talented team? If so, our opportunity may be a great fit for you.</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
You will be responsible for providing quality installation, training, demos and service on a wide variety of software systems and output devices for both internal and external Midwest customers, which will require air and ground travel to and from customers' facilities within Midwest's service areas.</p></li>
<li><p>
You will provide technical assistance and equipment maintenance and repair to customers via phone, electronic means, and in person at customer locations.</p></li>
<li><p>
You will participate in branch open houses, on-site demos, and road shows to represent Midwest and demonstrate equipment operation, features, and benefits.</p></li>
<li><p>
You will maintain a high level of expertise on all computer and digital equipment that Midwest represents including, but not limited to, OKI, EFI, Roland, Mimaki, Epson, and HP.</p></li>
<li><p>
You will work with Sales Managers, Sales Representatives, and all other Midwest employees to maintain Midwest's position as an industry leader.</p></li>
<li><p>
You will be responsible for communicating progress of installations, repair work, and general functioning &amp; potential problems of equipment.</p></li>
<li><p>
You will maintain calendar availability for service calls.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Our Desired Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
High school diploma or equivalent and 5+ years of industry experience servicing graphics equipment or a combination of education and experience providing equivalent knowledge.</p></li>
<li><p>
Demonstrated comprehensive understanding of both front-end software packages (Corel, Adobe CS) and output devices (OKI, Gerber, Mimaki, Roland, and EFI/Rastek printers, etc.)</p></li>
<li><p>
Proficiency using Microsoft Office suite applications; previous experience with CRM systems a plus</p></li>
<li><p>
Strong aptitude in problem solving, critical thinking, and analysis</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to prioritize multiple tasks</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to work independently and as a team</p></li>
<li><p>
Effective communication skills both written and verbal and the ability to interact with employees, vendors, and customers in a friendly, professional manner</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to work with and take instruction from a wide variety of "customers"</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to travel extensively (including overnight)</p></li>
<li><p>
Must hold a valid driver's license and be able to maintain an insurable driving record</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to honor a defined schedule in order to provide service to customers in a timely manner</p></li>
<li><p>
Physical requirements include the ability to walk freely, climb stairs, and lift up to 65 pounds.</p></li>
</ul>
