<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Harte Hanks is a global marketing firm that specializes in helping brands win attention, build trust and earn loyalty in an ever-changing world. Experts in defining, executing and optimizing the customer journey, we create meaningful interactions that connect our clients with their customers in more powerful ways. From visionary thinking to tactical execution, we’re changing the game of customer engagement - we’re bringing the human back to marketing.
</p>
<p><b>Account Executive:
</b></p>
<p>Harte Hanks is looking for an experienced New York-based Account Executive
</p>
<p>.
</p>
<p>The ideal candidate has 2+ years of ad agency experience, with at least 1 year as an Account Executive, and brings a real passion for advertising and CRM.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are looking for someone who can bring a creative and strategic approach to drive continued innovation, while also having the ability to work closely within a team structure and help ensure collaboration in an environment that is fast-paced, collaborative and fun.
</p>
<p>The AE will work directly with clients and ensure all business needs are met while also bringing together the various agency resources, including Strategy, Creative, Analytics, and Production, with a balance of outstanding project management skills and attention to detail. We're looking for someone who really understands how to move the clients CRM efforts forward and brings the strategic chops to proactively raise the bar on a consistent basis.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Please note, this is NOT a sales job, and applicants without prior advertising agency or client-side CRM experience will not be considered.
</p>
<p>Ideally were looking for that person who is hungry for creative and strategic opportunities and is looking for new and unique challenges to help grow their skills. <i>Cultural fit is critically important to us. We believe great work is best achieved in an environment that’s fun and embraces new ideas, and always strive to give everyone the chance to push their skills in new ways.
</i></p>
<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide day-to-day management of assigned accounts to ensure projects are being progressed and delivered on time, on budget, along with providing regular status reports for clients and internal management
</li>
<li>Acts as day-to-day client liaison; establishes regular, open lines of communication.
</li>
<li>Keeps client informed of project changes that impact budget and schedule.
</li>
<li>Studies and understands clients’ businesses, target markets and competition.
</li>
<li>Work with clients and finance to ensure proper billing and agency payment
</li>
<li>Cultivate strong working relationships with clients and internal departments, including ensuring internal alignment across departments<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Required Education And Experience
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, communications or related field
</li>
<li>2-3 years of agency experience<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
