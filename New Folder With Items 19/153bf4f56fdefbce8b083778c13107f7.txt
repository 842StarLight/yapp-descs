<h2>The Produce / Floral Purchasing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Produce / Floral Purchasing Coordinator is responsible for the support of the administrative &amp; supply chain duties of the total department. This candidate must be well-organized, a team player and have the ability to multi-task in a fast-paced, ever-changing environment.</p>
<p>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides administrative support to the Directors of Produce / Floral Wholesale and Retail, Category Managers and Merchandising Team.</li>
<li>Provide customer service to all store locations in regard to questions, issues and concerns within the Produce / Floral Department, ordering and deliveries.</li>
<li>Responsible for learning, using, and maintaining internal wholesale / retail software systems.</li>
<li>Partners with the CMs &amp; Directors to monitor and track pricing, promotions, purchasing, costs, and inventory levels.</li>
<li>Responsible for all billings promotions, free fill, and all on-going discounts from start to finish.</li>
<li>Tracks progress of all promotions within supply chain and troubleshoot any issues, shortages, out-of-codes, transportation shortfalls, etc..</li>
<li>Prepares and presents comprehensive evaluation of data as requested.</li>
<li>Monitors daily, weekly and monthly performance specific to each store and compiles analysis for total company comparisons using internal software programs.</li>
<li>Monthly tracking specific to expenses, payroll and sales.</li>
<li>Develops clear and concise communication to identify opportunities.</li>
<li>Demonstrates knowledge of and supports company standards, policies and procedures, operating instructions and confidentiality standards.</li>
<li>Helps to creates pricing, promotion, assortment and shelf schematics for each assigned category, based on detailed analysis of past performance, results, industry best practices, and consumer trends.</li>
<li>Helps creates yearly business plan and budgets by category based on department/company initiatives, consumer and market trends.</li>
<li>Helps with correlation of data with vendors on pricing, product assortment, promotion, ads, services, promotional calendars, and support; acts as primary contact with vendors in assigned categories.</li>
<li>Researches, develops, and implements merchandising and ad plans to support category business plan and total department initiatives.</li>
<li>Maintains and reviews price files and competitive price checks to ensure we are priced competitively within each market we operate.</li>
<li>Works in conjunction with operations and other departments across the company to facilitate implementation of programs.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>Education and Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>B.S./B.A. degree in a business or accounting and/or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Must have a minimum of 5 years professional experience</li>
<li>Preferred grocery retail experience</li>
</ul>
<p>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced knowledge of financial acumen specific to profit &amp; loss statements and analysis.</li>
<li>Ability to adapt to the ever-changing retail environment while working in a cross-functional team environment.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to take initiative when problems arise.</li>
<li>Ability to adapt and be flexible in a variety of situations.</li>
<li>Must have advanced attention to detail with the ability to prioritize and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Excellent written communication and documentation skills.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal communication and interpersonal skills for dealing cross functionally with peers and senior management.</li>
<li>Must have expert knowledge with MS Office including Outlook, Word, and Excel.</li>
<li>Understanding of specific knowledge pertaining to Produce and encompassing departments.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask is essential.</li>
<li>Must be able to lead, support and contribute to team goals.</li>
<li>Flexible hours of availability</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Purchasing: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
