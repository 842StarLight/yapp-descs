<h2>Systems Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b><i>Job</i></b><b> Overview:</b>
</h4>
<p>An exciting opportunity to interface with clients and solve business needs through technology. <i>Job</i> allows participation in the pre-sales requirements gathering through the design, development and deployment phases. <i>Job</i> requires a self motivated individual who desires to stay current with technology and <i>apply</i> knowledge to client requested solutions. Typical projects center around Microsoft SharePoint, SQL and SQL Reporting Services and MS Office integration scenarios.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Duties:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Deploy, customize and maintain SharePoint Portal solutions for clients.</li>
<li>Designs and develops user interfaces to Internet/intranet applications. Maintains professional certifications through participating in educational <i>opportunities</i> including self study. Must be willing to learn new technologies and multitask in fast paced, fun, customer service oriented environment.</li>
<li>Integrates applications <i>by</i> designing database architecture and server scripting; establishing connectivity with network systems, search engines, and information servers. Knowledge of developing in an Active Directory environment a plus.</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate requirements, contributing to team meetings and report on activities.</li>
<li>Support clients <i>by</i> developing documentation and assistance tools as needed, troubleshooting development and production problems across multiple environments and operating platforms.</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Skills/Qualifications:</b></h4>
<p>Must be able to clearly communicate with customers and clients both verbally and in writing, Web User Interface Design, VB.NET &amp; ASP Programming Skills, Software Requirements, Software Architecture, Software Development Fundamentals, SQL Database design and development (SQL reporting services a plus), MS Office knowlege including InfoPath a plus.</p>
<p>
Travel on a daily basis is required and is typically limited to the tri-State Area. ALL <i>RESUME</i>'S SHOULD INCLUDE <i>SALARY</i> REQUIREMENTS IN ORDER TO BE CONSIDERED.</p>
