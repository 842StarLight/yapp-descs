<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>SUMMARY</p>
<p>The staff accountant is responsible for applying accounting principles and procedures to analyze financial information, prepare accurate and timely financial reports and statements and ensure appropriate accounting control procedures. The staff accountant will be required to perform the month end close, financial reporting, and variance analysis for one or more business unites. They are responsible for all accounting and tax functions for all assigned business unit(s) and responsible for daily cash balancing and special projects, as assigned.</p>
<p></p>
<p>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<p>1. Perform accounting and tax functions for all assigned business unit(s), as follows:</p>
<ul><li>Prepare monthly accrual basis financial statements, including all journal entries and related schedules.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Control depreciation records using Asset Keeper software.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare monthly bank reconciliation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain binders for equipment leases and maintenance contracts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain daily cash balance reporting and requisitions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare outside service and inter-company analyses.</li></ul>
<p>• Prepare work papers for annual outside Auditor review and tax preparation. •</p>
<p>Present monthly financial reports to Management and provide analysis on variances</p>
<ul><li>Solid understanding of materiality in both financial reporting and time management</li></ul>
<p>2. Comply with company rules and regulations.</p>
<p>3. Maintain confidentiality of company-sensitive data.</p>
<p>4. Keep manager/supervisor informed of activities and situations that will influence the achievement of corporate or department goals and objectives.</p>
<p>5. Perform other related duties as assigned to achieve the goals and objectives of the department and company</p>
<p></p>
<p>EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE</p>
<ul><li>Accounting degree (preferred) or equivalent.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of generally accepted accounting practices and principles</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of generally accepted auditing practices and principles</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of applicable sales tax regulations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge and experience with related computer applications</li></ul>
<ul><li>A minimum of 3 years related experience preferably in a manufacturing setting</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>COMPETENCIES</p>
<ul><li>Proficient in payables, receivables, invoicing and general ledger.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent analytical skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent time management skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient in Microsoft Office applications</li></ul>
<p>• Attention to detail and accuracy • Planning and organizing for effective time management with daily, weekly and monthly tasks</p>
<ul><li>Information and task monitoring</li></ul>
<ul><li>Problem analysis</li></ul>
<ul><li>Judgment and problem-solving</li></ul>
<ul><li>Exhibit flexibility to meet the needs of the Accounting Team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Positive attitude and pleasant demeanor</li></ul>
