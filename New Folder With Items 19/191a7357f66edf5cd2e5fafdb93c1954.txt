<h2>Stylist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Editorialist YX’s mission is to provide the most personalized online shopping experience in the world. Our highly client-centric approach uses deep data to take the infinite array of luxury goods available for sale online and to recommend the perfect product based on our clients’ lifestyles, preferences, and wardrobes. We define a luxury experience as one that is super-personalized—customized just for you. To our clients-a select and discerning group-it looks and feels like magic.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We love our work. We look for individuals who will go to amazing lengths for our clients, who are curious, creative problem solvers, humble, and driven to play a role in redefining the luxury goods industry.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
As a Stylist, you will be responsible for developing, styling and managing and maintaining wardrobes and portfolios for our most valuable clients to ensure our company client satisfaction, engagement and retention targets are exceeded. You will report to the Director of Styling and work closely with all members of the Styling Team, as well as Operations, and Engineering teams. In this role, you will onboard new clients: from their personal interview to owning and managing closet edits, to overseeing closet re-organizations to the delivery of the content in their Editorialist YX app, to the ongoing client-advisor relationship. You will engage regularly with your clients ensuring a seamless, exceptional luxury experience. You will assist in training Styling team members and lead by example. The Stylist effectively owns and executes these tasks to ensure the company’s success.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</div>
