<b>About the Role</b><br>
<br>
Founded in 2008 and with 300+ employees around the world, Mavenlink provides a SaaS platform for automating professional services businesses. We help our customers plan project staffing, deliver projects, manage budgets, and understand their company performance. Strong sales growth sees us expanding our team and growing our recruiting operations.<br>
<br>
As a Technical Recruiter, you’ll help shape recruiting for our product &amp; engineering teams. You’ll manage the full recruiting lifecycle, with strong focus on amazing candidate experience. Supported by our great team culture, you’ll work closely with hiring managers to help candidates understand how Mavenlink can grow their careers. Your work will have a significant impact on the continued success of the company.<br>
<br>
<b>What you’ll do</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Grow candidate pipeline through passive sourcing, research, events and referrals
</li>
<li>Build strong relationships with candidates</li>
<li>Bring energy and fresh-thinking to our recruiting approach
</li>
<li>Work with our leaders to grow Mavenlink’s profile in the technical community
</li>
<li>Collaborate with our wider recruiting team to grow our talent operation efforts
</li>
</ul>
<b>Who we’re looking for</b><br>
<ul>
<li>3+ years full-cycle recruiting experience</li>
<li>Ability to manage conversations with senior candidates</li>
<li>Up-to-date knowledge of creative sourcing strategies</li>
<li>Success placing engineers in competitive start-up markets</li>
<li>Collaborative approach to meeting new challenges</li>
</ul>
<b>Why you’ll love it here</b><br>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Supportive hiring managers. </b>Our hiring managers have a passion for recruiting, enjoy meeting candidates, and take pleasure in partnering with great recruiters. You’ll have their enthusiastic support in closing candidates.</li>
<li>
<b>Great interview process.</b> Candidates routinely call our process the best they’ve seen. We focus on positive, informative candidate experience by collaborating with candidates on work together. We’re also efficient: bring us a candidate on a Tuesday, we can have an offer for them by Thursday.</li>
<li>
<b>Leadership and influence</b>. You’ll be joining as we’re accelerating growth and ramping up our recruiting team. Your experience, insights, and creativity will help shape our approach to recruiting.</li>
</ul>
<h6 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Mavenlink is an Equal Opportunity Employer.</b></h6>
