<h2>Bookstore Attendant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Bookstore Attendant shall serve the Glen Eyrie Group (GEG) and fulfill the responsibilities listed below in the spirit of The Navigators calling, values and purpose: To advance the Gospel of Jesus and His Kingdom into the nations through spiritual generations of laborers living and discipling among the lost.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Interact with Bookstore and Coffee Shop customers aiding them in purchases</li>
<li>
Conduct point-of-sale transactions</li>
<li>
Prepare barista-style beverages using a super-automatic espresso machine and similar equipment</li>
<li>
Accept and facilitate product special orders on behalf of customers</li>
<li>
Receive, input, price and shelve new inventory
</li>
<li>Regular upselling of goods and services to customers</li>
<li>
Participate in product purchasing as needed</li>
<li>
Maintain a constant awareness and expertise in all Glen Eyrie Programs</li>
<li>
Manage and track daily opening, closing and administrative tasks</li>
<li>
Complete daily cleaning and organizing lists</li>
<li>
Participate in regular team meetings</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Belief in and adherence to the Statement of Faith and Mission of The Navigators.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates a sincere and mature walk with Jesus Christ</li>
<li>
Willingness to abide by the policies of the Glen Eyrie handbook</li>
<li>
Has prior retail experience</li>
<li>
Must be able to work a flexible schedule, 3 to 4 shifts a week including days, nights and weekends. Regular shifts are between 5 and 10 hours</li>
<li>
Strong customer care focus</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Microsoft Office suite</li>
<li>
Excellent computer skills and the ability to learn new software</li>
<li>
Excellent telephone skills</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Excellent cash handling skills</li>
<li>
Excellent math skills</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask in a very busy retail and food service environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to work unsupervised</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
