<h2>Marketing Compliance Manager-Legal Department-Gerber Life Insurance - White Plains, NY</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Provide Compliance support for insurance sales and product development. As directed by Compliance Director, work with internal and external business partners on legal, compliance and related matters.</p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Compliance Management: Taking into account business and legal priorities,assist with the development and implementation of compliance policies and procedures to achieve general corporate and insurance compliance goals including:</li>
<li>Providing guidance to business partners and implementing compliance policies and procedures</li>
<li>Participating on the Compliance Action Team, as required</li>
<li>Analyzing and responding to compliance questions</li>
<li>Recording and investigating alleged non-compliance with laws, regulations, and Company policies for appropriate handling</li>
<li>Maintaining and updating Legal/Compliance Department paper and electronic files, including policy forms</li>
<li>Recommend and implement enhancements and process improvements based on assessment of available regulatory and compliance systems and processes</li>
<li>Research and track regulatory developments to update compliance policies and procedures</li>
<li>Advertising Compliance Oversight:</li>
<li>Review marketing materials to ensure compliance with advertising and promotional regulations</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with internal and external business partners in reviewing and revising advertising to ensure that all advertising materials are in compliance with all applicable laws, regulatory requirements, policies, and procedures</li>
<li>Develop and disseminate educational materials to support internal and external teams in effective management of advertising compliance review process</li>
<li>Ensure branding standards are adhered to in connection with marketing efforts</li>
<li>Maintain and update the approved Style Guide</li>
<li>File advertising for approval, as required</li>
<li>Prepare and review new policy forms, ensuring adherence to legal and regulatory requirements</li>
<li>Manage form and rate filing process with states and the Interstate Compact, including preparing responses to objections based on independent review and analysis</li>
<li>Support the product development team in the review of products, including assisting with drafting and confirming compliance requirements</li>
<li>Maintain and update Company Disclosure Matrix to ensure product disclosures are provided, as needed</li>
<li> Ensure that documented compliance guidance is in place for internal associates as well as external business partners. Responsible for developing and updating product manuals for life and health products, as well as Legal/Compliance Standard Operating Procedures (SOP).</li>
<li> In consultation with Legal, research and analyze laws and regulations to provide written guidance to appropriate business units for implementation.</li>
<li>Prepare, coordinate, and file regulatory submission documents (e.g., data calls and mandatory reports)</li>
<li>Collect and analyze data required for timely reporting to regulatory agencies, and maintain filing calendar to track key dates for regulatory agency reporting.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>Leadership skills with the ability to energize and coach others</li>
<li>People skills including the ability to influence, gain commitment and effectively handle conflict</li>

<li>Excellent research, interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Ability to identify and analyze insurance laws and regulations for application to business operations</li>
<li>Ability to identify and effectively manage and resolve compliance issues</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to organize and prioritize work</li>
</ul>
