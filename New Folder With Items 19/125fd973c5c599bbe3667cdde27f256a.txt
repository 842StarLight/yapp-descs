<h2>Planning Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Department:</b> Supply Chain
<br><b>Reports to:</b> Vice President of Operations
<br><br>
Summary
<br>Contributes to the development and leads the implementation of supply and demand planning strategies to support the company’s growth and performance objectives. Primary responsibility is to optimize customer service levels through the balance of inventory across the supply chain network and maximizing forecast accuracy. Areas of responsibility include ERP (specifically SAP) planning and utilization, and product innovation coordination. Champions continuous improvement in our processes, our people, and our infrastructure.
<br><br>
This description is not intended to be construed as an exhaustive list of responsibilities, duties, or skills required for the job. This job description may be changed or assume additional duties at any time. The employee may be requested to perform different or additional duties as assigned.
<br><br>
Duties and Responsibilities
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions Only:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Integrate our people, service, product, and brand objectives in the development of supply and demand planning strategies to support the company’s growth and performance objectives.</li>
<li>Manage the strategy and execution of the demand and supply planning functions to optimize customer service performance as measured by overall fulfillment rates.</li>
<li>Manage the demand and supply planning functions to drive end-to-end supply chain efficiency as measured by inventory levels and overall operating cost. Develop and manage the corresponding planning department budget.</li>
<li>Routinely collaborate with sales and marketing leaders to lead and maintain an effective S&amp;OP process to include ongoing KPI reporting and review. Specifically, lead improvements in system forecast accuracy in order to optimize supply chain efficiencies, inventory levels, and ultimately fulfillment performance.</li>
<li>Champion continuous improvement culture with prioritization aligned with business strategies: Establish and implement process improvements that reduce transactional cost, increase profitability, promote cross-functional alignment, incorporates standard work instructions, and eliminates waste at all touch points to include policy review and design.</li>
<li>Manage and prioritize activities of the planning functions, and coordinate product innovation functions.</li>
<li>Manage supply and demand planning and forecasting review, and assist in product life cycle management.</li>
<li>Lead the review, analysis and implementation of the ERP (specifically SAP) system enhancements to support the execution of the company’s supply and demand planning strategies and customer needs.</li>
<li>Collaborates with Vice President of Operations and other administrative personnel to review achievements and discuss required changes in goals or objectives resulting from current status and conditions.</li>
<li>Provide strategic oversight in the creation/revision and implementation of best known programs, policies and practices across all areas of responsibility.</li>
<li>Directly interacts with functional groups, such as product development, marketing and sales operations for all R&amp;D activities.</li>
</ul>
Leadership Competencies
<br><ul>
<li>Cascade strategies by creating and reviewing analyses of activities, costs, and operations. Develop corresponding forecast data to determine department progress toward stated KPI goals, and coach accordingly.</li>
<li>Responsible for building, developing, and retaining talent necessary to drive a high-performance team, along with ensuring succession planning is in place.</li>
<li>Foster learning environment by supporting training and development of team.</li>
<li>Provide appropriate recognition as team reaches continuous improvement goals and objectives.</li>
</ul>
Supervisory Responsibilities
<br>Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with the organization's policies and applicable laws. Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring, and training employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving problems.
<br><br>
Qualifications Description
<br>Education and Experience
<br><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from four-year college or university with MBA preferred. Will consider experience in lieu of education.</li>
<li>Five years of relevant experience preferable in Consumer Packaged Goods industry</li>
<li>To perform this job successfully, an individual should have knowledge of spreadsheet software, ERP software (preferably SAP), internet software; inventory software; manufacturing software; order processing systems; and word processing software</li>
<li>Certifications such as those in Six Sigma, Operations or Inventory Management a plus.</li>
</ul>
Travel Time Must be able to travel up to 10% of the time.
