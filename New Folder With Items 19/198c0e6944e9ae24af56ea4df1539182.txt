<p><b>
Client Relationship Manager</b><br>
</p>
<p>
Are you looking for a high energy and dynamic role supporting clients where you will strive to become their trusted advisor? As a member of the Client Relationship Team, your focus will be on building client relationships, vetting new product releases, performing analytics and maintaining customer loyalty. Your primary responsibilities will involve building and supporting client relationships through the provision of frontline support and training, tracking, managing and reporting on client requests, monitoring solution implementations, and assisting with project management for select product expansion/roll-out opportunities.<br>

<li>
