<p>
Superlite, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Clean-up machinery and conveyors, maintain the surrounding area and keep equipment free of debris during downtime</li>
<li>
Operate equipment such as hoppers, bagging and sealer machines, forklift, palletizers, hydraulic equipment, powered tractors or vehicles, among others</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Clean floors and pick up reusable scrap for salvage, transport trash and debris away from production areas</li>
<li>
Operate a variety of hand tools</li>
<li>
Assist in the loading/unloading of trucks</li>
<li>
Maintain, verify, and count product inventory</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
