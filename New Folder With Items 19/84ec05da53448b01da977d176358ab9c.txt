<h2>Logistics Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Luxfer MEL Technologies is a global leader in the development, manufacture and supply of Magnesium and Zirconium based products and services to technology industries worldwide. We provide innovative products and solutions to customers worldwide for use in a broad range of markets including Aerospace, Automotive, Oil &amp; Gas, Ceramics and Healthcare. We have a wide range of products, including Hazardous, Non-Hazardous and Export shipments. We are an innovative technology driven business where we have a relentless drive and a passion to achieve high standards. At Luxfer Customer First, Integrity, Accountability, Innovation, Personal Development and Teamwork are our core values.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
Working alongside the Customer Service Team and reporting to the Head of Customer Services, you will be an effective Logistics Coordinator handling all aspects of deliveries, including being first point person for dispatch inquiries, dispatch documentation, inventory control, stock transfer documents, invoicing and arranging domestic/international freight carriers, along with other dispatch related communications and activities.
</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Generating all paperwork necessary to make each dispatch.</li>
<li>
Verify that packaging, labeling and paperwork are correct before the goods leave the premises.</li>
<li>
Creating Sales Invoices for dispatches and send to the customer by e-mail.</li>
<li>
Liaise with Production and Warehouse personnel to ensure that material is available for dispatch on time and to advise sales accordingly.</li>
<li>
Monitor dispatch dates daily and report any anticipated problems to Customer Services &amp; Sales as appropriate</li>
<li>
Arrange appropriate external transport to collect the goods and to confirm and check the price for each dispatch.</li>
<li>
Manage claims against transport providers for any failings such as overcharging, late or damaged deliveries.</li>
<li>
Assist in the quality activities to assure Luxfer MEL Technologies customers receive products that meet their specified product specifications.</li>
<li>
Maintain and develop a shared knowledge base of customer requirements, appropriate transport providers for each area and documentation requirements for each country.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Basic Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalent required. Associate's degree preferred</li>
<li>
2 - 4 years of experience handling logistics/transportation coordination</li>
<li>
Knowledge of export documentation and Transport of Dangerous Goods requirements</li>
<li>
Export/Import knowledge</li>
<li>
Previous experience with ERP software</li>
<li>
Ability to read, analyze, interpret and write general purchase orders and business correspondences</li>
<li>
Experience with DOT, Bill of Lading, and Freight forwarders</li>
<li>
Essential success attributes<ul>
<li>
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and maintain strict timelines as well as be part of a team</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and ability to prioritize tasks</li>
<li>
High level of accuracy and attention to detail.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
