<h2>Servers</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for talented, driven, attentive, and dedicated servers.</p>
<p>We are Rouge, a creatively focused, farm-driven restaurant in West Stockbridge, MA with an extensive beverage program.</p>
<p>This is a 4-5 day/week position, afternoon/evenings only. Full and part-time positions are available. Ultimately, we are looking for full-time employees who are interested in the opportunity for growth and promotion.</p>
<p>As a candidate, you must be communicative, professional, eager to learn, and fast on your feet. We have a small team of very dedicated FOH staff, so you must be a team player and reliable. Each team member is integral to the overall function of the restaurant.</p>
<p>-Servers will guide guests through their overall dining experience, discussing food and beverage options, handling allergies, placing orders, timing courses, up-selling food and beverage selections, and more. Servers are required to have a thorough understanding of, <i>and therefore hands-on experience with</i>, ALL OTHER FOH positions in the restaurant to be effective.</p>
<p>If you are interested please send us your resume and availability and we will respond quickly.</p>
