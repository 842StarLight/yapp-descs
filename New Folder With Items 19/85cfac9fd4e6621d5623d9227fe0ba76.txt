<h2>Community Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Who are we?</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Personify is the leading solution provider that empowers the best constituent-focused organizations to succeed. Through our constituent engagement software and services, we help our clients better interact with their constituents at every touch point. After 20+ years of innovation within the industry and hundreds of successful outcomes, our clients have trusted us to be their primary technology partner.</p>
<p>
As <b>Community Consultant, </b>you will serve as a member of Personify’s Small World Community team. This role serves as advisor and thought leader for clients using the Small World Community platform. The Community Consultant helps our customers effectively deploy, operate and promote their online community so that it achieves their organization’s goals.</p>
<p><b>
Principal Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Partner with clients to develop goals that prove the success, and value, of their community</p>
<p>
Recommend and implement strategy, marketing, messaging, social media, and community platform configurations based on these goals</p>
<p>
Deliver monthly and/or quarterly reports detailing community usage and momentum</p>
<p>
Coordinate with clients and 3rd party teams, lead status meetings with a high attention to detail, and clearly communicate action items</p>
<p>
Become an expert with our online community platform</p>
<p>
Deliver skill-building remote training for both community management and our software</p>
<p>
Verify and prioritize reported client issues with our support team</p>
<p>
Manage projects, project schedules, pace, input sessions, and milestones</p>
<p>
Assist with ongoing community engagement activities such as multi-channel and social media promotion, content editing and development, driving participation, and measuring results</p>
<p>
Identify opportunities for services and revenue-generating solutions to pass to our sales team</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
Undergraduate degree required; MBA or graduate degree is a plus</p>
<p>
Minimum of 3 years’ experience in consulting, marketing, or community management roles</p>
<p>
Strong communication, grammar, and presentation skills (written and verbal)</p>
<p>
Exceptional time and priority management. A service-oriented and positive attitude</p>
<p>
Experience working with non-profits or associations is preferred</p>
<p>
Project management skills, including identifying risks, setting expectations, and delivering a solution on schedule</p>
<p>
Working knowledge of, or an eagerness to learn, basic image, HTML, and CSS editing</p>
<p>
Proven track record of data-driven performance measurement</p>
<p>
Experience with SaaS community, social media, or CMS software preferred.</p>
<p>
Ability to function as a Social Media or Digital Marketing guru. Must have a strong understanding of online marketing techniques, social media, online community, and collaboration solutions.</p>
