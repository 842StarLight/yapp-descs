<h2>Business Continuity Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The <b>Business Continuity </b><b>Manager, </b>is responsible for driving and executing projects related to business continuity initiatives, plans, and procedures to meet multi-year business objectives. This position will lead the day to day analysis, design, and implementation of tailor-made solutions to enhance organizational performance, disaster preparedness thru recovery, and risk management for the enterprise. This role is responsible for working closely with stakeholders on multiple phases of business continuity, including providing status updates, identifying issues and solutions to senior management and leading enterprise-wide continuity projects. This position reports to the Director/Sr. Director.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
***Minimum experience and requirement reflected at the end (please see before applying)***</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Drive and execute assigned business projects with significant accountability to support meeting our multi-year objectives.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Act as a thought partner, strategic leader, and internal consultant.</li>
<li>
Meet expectations and metrics required to “get stuff done”.</li>
<li>
Perform high-level analysis, identify areas of improvement and create strategies, plans and business models (financial, market and/or operational) to resolve.</li>
<li>
Provide project leadership between business operations, information technology (IT) and other business units specific to business continuity initiatives.</li>
<li>
Advise leadership on project planning, tracking and implementation of key business continuity initiatives, coordinating both operational and system related aspects</li>
</ul>
<p>
Management responsibility for business contingency planning efforts with a major focus on establishing business unit contingency plans for critical areas, functions and applications</p>
<ul>
<li>
Understanding continuity and preparedness needs, as well as the necessity for establishing business continuity management policy and objectives for multiple site locations</li>
<li>
Manager will provide leadership in assessing, coordinating, developing and communicating recovery environment requirements and contingency plans to protect DaVita in case of unforeseen disruptions</li>
<li>
Assist in the development and implementation of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for all business continuity functions</li>
<li>
Participate in requirements gathering and implementation of new IT applications specific to continuity initiatives</li>
<li>
Maintain current knowledge of business continuity issues and trends through education, seminars, and research</li>
</ul>
<p>
Synthesize numerous sources of data and key analysis and translate into specific actions.</p>
<p>
Build strong relationships and collaborate with teammates, leaders, and clinicians aligned with the DaVita way.</p>
<p>
Drive the work of multi-disciplinary teams to develop effective and successful implementation strategies.</p>
<p>
Plan, organize and supervise direct report(s) to include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Employment activity such as hiring, promoting, job performance, evaluations, and disciplinary actions with appropriate approvals as required</li>
<li>
Review performance metrics with senior operations and DaVita leadership on an as-needed basis</li>
<li>
Manage departmental labor budget and miscellaneous expenses</li>
</ul>
<p>
Oversee patient information and records to ensure that all documentation is created and maintained in accordance with HIPAA regulations and to ensure the accurate service is provided</p>
<p>
Other duties as assigned.</p>
