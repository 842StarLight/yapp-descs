POSITION SUMMARY:</p>
<p>
The Poultry Nutritionistis responsible for performing nutrition-related duties including feed formulations, feed ingredient testing, field research on egg production and shell quality, interacting with employees in various areas of farms management and a liaison with sales and technical staff of vendors in the feed additive industry.</p>
<p>
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<li>
Formulate optimal feed rations for over 100 laying hen/pullet flocks each week for company and contract flocks utilizing Visionary program exclusive to MFI.</li>
<li>
Monitor feed ingredients received at mills for nutrient levels and appropriate quality evaluations.</li>
<li>
Work with corporate purchasing to evaluate best decisions for major feed ingredient suppliers based upon cost and available nutrient content.</li>
<li>
Interact with sales team members (sales/technical) with feed additive companies to consider evaluation feed products for testing or maintaining relationship/updating information with current suppliers.</li>
<li>
Participate in team efforts with production manager/supervisors to optimize production/profits.</li>
<li>
Maintain communication with feed mill personnel at corporate and contract mill sites.</li>
<li>
Work as part of a nutrition team with other nutritionist and feed ingredient procurement to evaluate current and future needs and plans for the nutrition program.</li>
<li>
Maintain communication with veterinarian on animal health decisions related to feed ingredients.</li>
<li>
Evaluation of production data and shell quality reports to monitor changes needed to optimize the nutrition program for each individual flock.</li>
<li>
Plan, conduct (track data) and evaluate field research to test feed additives and nutrient needs utilizing split-row houses on company farms.</li>
<li>
Manage 500-bird research house for more intense studies on nutritional evaluations for laying hens. Includes care of birds, feed mixing and record keeping as well as supervision of animal care helpers.</li>
<li>
Implement and track action plans for feed cost savings ideas.</li>
<li>
Participate in safety and biosecurity training meetings.</li>
<li>
Professional development via scientific meetings and nutrition conferences.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as needed.</li>
<p>
EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE REQUIRED:</p>
<li>
pHd or MS in Poultry Nutrition</li>
<li>
5 years poultry nutrition experience</li>
<p></p>
<p>
PREFERRED EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE AND SKILLS:</p>
<li>
Excellent verbal &amp; written communication skills and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Able to multi-task and solve problems effectively.</li>
<li>
Experience in utilizing feed formulation software.</li>
