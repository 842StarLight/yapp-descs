<h2>Vice President Customer Success</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Sounds Like You? Please review the criteria below
<br><br>
This Vice President of Customer Success is a senior leader within the Global Commercial Team who leads the Customer Success Team. The Vice President of Customer Success is responsible for driving customer adoption of the JLL Marketplace solution, reducing customer churn, identifying expansion opportunities and owning the customer satisfaction/net promoter score. The role requires superb client relationship management skills, demonstrated team development, clear communications, and the ability to manage cross-functionally through influence.
<br><br>
This position will report to the Chief Commercial Officer. The Vice President of Customer Success will align the customer success team’s values while inspiring confidence and generating excitement, enthusiasm and commitment towards the business goals. The main responsibilities and expectations include:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Build, manage, coach, and mentor a team of Customer Success Managers (CSMs)</li>
<li>Drive customer success through proactive value management</li>
<li>Influence customer lifetime value through higher adoption, customer engagement and overall health scores</li>
<li>Maintain strong renewal rates</li>
<li>Identify and qualify opportunities for both expansion of existing capabilities and identifying and positioning new JLL products and services to the customer base.</li>
<li>Thought leadership regarding account management, value management, customer onboarding, and customer success best practices</li>
<li>Develop and continuously evolve a multi-tiered (including tech-touch) approach to managing various JLL Marketplace customer segments</li>
<li>Drive CSM execution by reviewing account strategies, adhering to strong forecast discipline, and managing customer-focused campaigns led by the CSM organization</li>
<li>Active engagement and partnership with Sales to ensure successful customer management, sales goal achievement, and CSM goal achievement</li>
<li>Serve as a customer advocate within our organization, championing changes to the product that will increase usage, success, and satisfaction</li>
<li>Establish and maintain strategic relationships with key partners essential to driving customer success</li>
<li>Drive and develop new initiatives and other key programs as required to evolve our Customer Success strategy</li>
<li>Actively step in to support high level, global customer engagements</li>
</ul>
<b>The Requirements:</b><br>
Ideal candidate should have demonstrated the following competencies:
<br><ul>
<li>5+ years of customer success management experience with complex enterprise solutions</li>
<li>10+ years of consultative sales, business consulting and or account management experience</li>
<li>Experience building a multi-tier customer success management model</li>
<li>Experience with large enterprise implementation best practices</li>
<li>Proven success meeting and exceeding a quota</li>
<li>Proven success in rallying the various company functions to deliver customer value</li>
<li>Contract negotiation/renewal experience</li>
<li>Experience working with enterprise solutions with discrete manufacturing customers</li>
<li>Experience working with cloud/SaaS environment</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree required</li>
</ul>
