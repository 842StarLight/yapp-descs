<h2>Product Manager (Medical Device – Radiation Therapy)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Product Manager (Medical Device – Radiation Therapy)</b></h3>
<p>This is a <b>highly collaborative</b> role that <b>works cross-functionally</b> within our organization to drive assigned products from <b>strategy</b> to finished product and then serves as <b>a technical expert</b>. Do you have clinical experience in <b>radiation therapy</b> and desire to create tools used to ensure better patient outcomes? Read on!</p>
<p>Our ideal candidate has strong project management skills and experience in the medical device industry, or even better in the Radiation Therapy industry.<br>
<br>
This is a full-time position, working out of our Middleton, WI office.<br>
</p>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Areas of Responsibility</b></h5>
<p><b>Product Management</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with product development requirements for Patient Dosimetry products including voice-of-customer feedback, product development documentation (such as business plans, feature requirements and use scenarios), competitive product assessment and response prioritization, and obsolescence plans and execution. Product coverage subject to change based on business requirements.</li>
<li>
Define and develop appropriate marketing collateral to support Sales communication strategy and core messaging for assigned products, including product benefits, attributes, value proposition, product positioning, and competitive assessment and comparisons.</li>
<li>
Provide feedback into strategic product planning, marketing, and continuous product improvement efforts.</li>
<li>
Lead and facilitate customer collaborations with respect to research and support multi-departmental team effort to produce research publications and internal white papers to document and support assigned product claims</li>
<li>
Lead efforts to identify appropriate beta test/validation sites for all applicable product releases and provide support to R&amp;D for any customer follow up required to ensure timely feedback.</li>
<li>
Attend conferences, trade shows, scientific talks, and training events as scheduled by Manager</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Clinical Sales Support:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Define, recruit speakers or deliver product webinars, focusing on selection of material or orchestrating talk by existing users to generate interest in assigned product solutions by prospective customers.</li>
<li>
Coordinate appropriate SI reference sites for assigned products to maximize opportunities related to the collaboration and to ensure that reference site is fully functional and satisfied with SI products.</li>
<li>
Provide requested clinical support to Sales team for assigned products via remote webinars and on-site <b>pre-sales</b> technical demonstrations to customers following proper approval procedures</li>
<li>
Other duties may be assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS in a technical discipline (engineering, computer science, physics, etc.).</li>
<li>
PMP Certification and/or Medical Physics degree is highly preferred.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 3 years’ experience in cross-functional medical device (hardware/software) product development that includes project management.</li>
<li>
Ability to work on multiple, concurrent problems projects</li>
<li>
Ability to develop and deliver clear, crisp communications (written and verbal) that are customized to the needs of different audiences, ensuring timely and relevant communication.</li>
<li>
Ability to see the big picture and able to synthesize, analyze, and draw insights from data to inform the best path forward.</li>
<li>
Functions independently with little instruction on day-to-day work and general instructions on new assignments.</li>
<li>
Radiation Therapy industry experience and knowledge of medical device and regulatory standards highly preferred.</li>
<li>
Some travel, domestic or international, may be required.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with ISO and FDA regulations and standards a plus</li>
</ul>
