<div><b>Senior Product Manager – Media Solutions</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Location: San Francisco Bay Area, CA</div>
<div>Term: Full-time</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Intertrust </b>provides trusted computing products and services to leading global corporations – from consumer electronics companies and service providers to enterprise software companies. These products include the world’s leading media content protection platform, mobile app security solutions, and data privacy platforms.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Founded in 1990, Intertrust is based in Silicon Valley, with locations in San Francisco, London, Tokyo, Beijing, and Mumbai. The company has a legacy of invention, and its fundamental contributions in the areas of computer security and digital trust are globally recognized. Intertrust holds hundreds of patents that are key to Internet security, privacy, web services, and cloud computing.</div>
<div></div>
<div>To learn more, visit us at www.intertrust.com.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Description:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>As Intertrust grows its Product team in the San Francisco Bay Area, we are seeking a <b>Senior</b> <b>Product Manager– Media Solutions </b>to be part of the global team growing and scaling the product lines in the Media group. This is a challenging and rewarding opportunity.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Reporting to the Chief Product Officer as part of a small team, this role works with Product Engineering, Marketing, Sales and Support teams to gather, define and prioritize engineering and partnering investments to grow the Media Solutions business. The ideal candidate must have a deep understanding of the digital media industry and ecosystem, technical knowledge of media content protection technologies, experience in B2B product delivery, strong customer and strong analytical and verbal and written communication skills.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>What you will do:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate in a small team owning product for the ExpressPlay suite, which includes ExpressPlay’s cloud-based multi-DRM, Conditional Access (CA), Kiora solution for transportation and hospitality content delivery, and other related products.</li>
<li>Define and manage product roadmap and validate product concepts</li>
<li>Establish product and feature goals and develop a backlog</li>
<li>Write product requirement documents and user stories and ensure clear communication and coordination of requirements with the marketing, sales, and design and development teams</li>
<li>Analyze existing products for opportunities to innovate and improve, providing additional benefits to customers</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally to manage bottlenecks, escalate as needed, anticipate and make trade-offs, balance business needs versus technical constraints, and maximize business benefit while building great customer experiences</li>
<li>Provide accurate reporting and data analysis to all stakeholders on a regular basis</li>
<li>Research and identify new opportunities</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>What we are looking for:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of product management or solution architecture experience.</li>
<li>Solid understanding and experience in the media industry, including OTT media and traditional broadcast (DVB, ATSC), ideally with a focus on content protection technologies like DRM, CAS, and watermarking.</li>
<li>Express complex technical concepts in simple and intuitive communications for both technical and business B2B audiences.</li>
<li>Technical background or enough technical depth to understand positioning in a highly technical space</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills are crucial, as well as ability to analyze quantitatively, generate solutions, and prioritize project work</li>
<li>Demonstrated analytical and quantitative skills, ability to use data and metrics to back up assumptions and develop business cases</li>
</ul>

<li>Comprehensive benefits</li>

</ul>
