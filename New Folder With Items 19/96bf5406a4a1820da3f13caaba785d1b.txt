<h2>Receptionist/Customer Service - Mornings, Afternoons or Evenings</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>St. Andrew's Parish Parks and Playground is committed to enriching lives through parks, recreation and wellness.<br>
<br>
<b>Customer Service / Front Desk Receptionist Needed</b></p>
<p>
Various shifts throughout the day starting at 5:00 a.m. and ending at 9:00 p.m.</p>
<p>
We are seeking a fun and friendly customer service professional to offer <b>exceptional customer service </b>at the front desk of our Fitness/Recreation Facility. We are able to offer flexible hours per week and a free fitness center &amp; pool membership.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b><br>
<br>
Our Front Desk Teammates have the first interaction with both members and potential members and are an important point of contact.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>General responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Enthusiastically greet customers as they enter and leave the facility</li>
<li>Answer the phone in a friendly manner
</li>
<li>Answer member questions, review member accounts, assist in setting up new registrations</li>
<li>Address/resolve customer questions and concerns</li>
<li>Must be able to learn and use our software database and registration procedures</li>
<li>Process payments</li>
<li>Guide members in regards to membership discounts, special events, and other promotions</li>
<li>Perform various administrative and housekeeping duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b><br>
<br>
High school graduate or equivalent required</p>
<p>
Minimum of 1 year experience in customer service, administrative assistant, retail, sales and/or cashier experience preferred. Experience in health clubs or recreation facilities a plus<br>
<br>
Computer and typing experience required<br>
<br>
Basic record keeping practices and procedures<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
While performing the duties of this job, the employee is required to stand and/or walk for the entire shift. Must be able to reach with hands and arms, type, stand, bend, walk, talk and hear. The employee must frequently lift up to 20 pounds.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Hours of Work:</b></p>
<p>
Hours will vary according to availability. This is a Part-Time position, up to 29 hours per week. We are seeking candidates with flexibility to work a variety of hours. This position is non-exempt and reports directly to the Customer Service Director.</p>
