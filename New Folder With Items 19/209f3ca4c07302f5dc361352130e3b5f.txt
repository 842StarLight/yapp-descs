<h2>Delphi Software Engineer</h2>

<p><b>About NCR
</b></p>
<p>NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a leading software- and services-led enterprise provider in the financial, retail, hospitality, telecom and technology industries. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga., with 34,000 employees and does business in 180 countries. NCR is a trademark of NCR Corporation in the United States and other countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Position Location: Plano, Texas
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Are you experienced with Delphi OR have a strong C++/C# background with an interest in learning and working with Delphi? NCR is looking for a Delphi Software Engineer to join our StorePoint Point-of-Sale (POS) Product development team in Plano, Texas. In this exciting opportunity, you will develop and maintain NCR’s StorePoint, a multi-format intuitive touch-screen based point of sale application. This system is the industry leading POS solution for Travel Centers and Commercial Fuel used by the large Travel Centers (Pilot Flying J, Love's, Travel Centers of America, Petro, Town Pump, Sapp Bros, Irving Oil and many more).
</p>

<li>Design, development, and implementation of the StorePoint product.
</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>
<li>Drive application of SW development lifecycle with the team
</li>
<li>Advise others on SW Engineering standards and emerging best practices
</li>
<li>Provide support / guidance for more junior SW Engineers around effective application of SW dev lifecycle &amp; develop industry leading solutions for NCR’s customers and around more complex problem resolution

<li>Enable maintenance &amp; integration of code to effectively diagnose issues within the SW
</li>
<li>Identify patterns of issues, root causes and appropriate resolution to systems or processes
