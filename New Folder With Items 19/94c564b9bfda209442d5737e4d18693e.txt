<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Ensures the timely and efficient delivery of corporate business operations for the collection, analysis, presentation, and process management of a variety of data for sales development and implementation of functional activities. Provides business development management and technical expertise to other senior management, subordinates, and to clients, to generate incremental sales.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for developing the strategic vision and tactical application of the CROSSMARK methodology of trade development and integrated data analysis for the entire enterprise. This is accomplished through the application of best and custom practices. Provides forward-thinking category leadership and contribute to CROSSMARK Analytics industry intelligence Leads advanced analytics capabilities Provides leadership and operational support to the various Business Intelligence teams of CROSSMARK.</li>
<li>
Manages the acquisition, administration, and use of syndicated and non-syndicated data resources on behalf of the corporate enterprise. Enhances corporate business operations through development of consultative insights and presentations directed towards generating increased sales.</li>
<li>
Oversees the development of a broad range of strategic business planning including, but not limited to, category management, space management, product evaluation, promotion planning and evaluation, trade funds evaluation, and resource allocation.</li>
<li>
Provide business expertise to clients, customers, and other CROSSMARK managers to determine and develop effective strategies for application of analytical resources to increase sales.</li>
<li>
Develops and presents strategic business proposals to support both general and specific business operations and sales development projects.</li>
<li>
Supports business operations of the sales effort through interaction and consultation with sales executives and other business partners.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
