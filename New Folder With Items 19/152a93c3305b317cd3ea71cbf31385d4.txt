<p>
Agilysys is conducting a search for a Finance Manager to join our team. In this role, you will support management in meeting ongoing budgeting, forecasting and analysis needs for our Operations teams. You will assist in reaching the company goals and objectives regarding profitability, growth, returns on working capital and cash flows. As the successful candidate, you will add strategic value and direction to management and be positioned to take on increased responsibilities. In addition, you will work with budget owners to combine and analyze information related to their associated budgets. You will work closely with the Senior Finance Manager and Executive Team on special projects and be expected to manage deliverables of direct reports.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Development and communication of operating expense and sales incentive budget and forecast.</li>
<li>
Management of commission process from quota setting to sales team payment.</li>
<li>
Operational analysis and review of progress to budget for senior management.</li>
<li>
Create dashboards and reports in Business Intelligence tool.</li>
<li>
Provide unsolicited insights to senior management.</li>
<li>
Prepare ad hoc reports and special projects as needed.</li>
<li>
Direct management and review of Financial Analyst deliverables.</li>
<li>
Mentorship and development of multiple Financial Analysts.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Qualifications</b><br>

<li>
1+ years of related management experience.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Excel.</li>
<li>
Detail oriented, analytical and desire to be challenged.</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize multiple deliverables simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to interact with all levels of the company, from sales reps to executive management.</li>
</ul>
