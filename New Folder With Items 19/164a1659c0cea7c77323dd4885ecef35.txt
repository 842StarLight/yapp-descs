<h2>Enterprise Master Data Management Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB DESCRIPTION
</h1>
<div>
<div>Position: Enterprise Master Data Management Leader</div>
<div>Location: Irvine, Ca USA / Basel, CH / Dubai, UAE / Singapore, SG</div>
<div>Duration: Full Time</div>
<div>Reports to: VP, Global Quality - GIL</div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>The Enterprise Master Data Management (eMDM) Leader is a new Director level role within Agility’s Global Integrated Logistics (GIL) Division that is responsible for directing the organization in driving governance around how Master Data is being used within systems used to manage daily operations. This role has overall responsibility in advancing and enhancing current Master Data governance models that are in place, working with Stakeholders in the delivery of this model, working with our MDM Agile Team to ensure all governance considerations are built into internal systems, and working to build a strategy to ensure all Master Data remains accurate and remain aligned with our business strategy by using standardized and consistently implemented practices.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul><li>
<b>Master Data Governance</b>
<ul>
<li>Leading the evolution of our Master Data governance models to be in sync with a changing business landscape, building of modern technology, and key transformative initiatives taking place with the Operations and Finance organizations.</li>
<li>Ensuring that the optimal governance structure and compliance activities are communicated throughout the organization and transparent to all stakeholders</li>
<li>Develop change management communication to promote MDM governance models with internal stakeholders, as needed.</li>
<li>Defining organizational requirements for the resources, structures and cultural changes necessary to support successful governance of Master Data enterprise wide.</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>MDM Digitization</b>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for coordinating with our internal Agile Teams and functional stakeholders (Quality, Finance, Ops, Sales &amp; Commercial) in developing and maintaining technology based Data Management capabilities to be line with the vision of the organization</li>
<li>Support our system implementation efforts by being a key point of contact for our users.</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Stakeholder Management</b>
<ul>
<li>Ensure alignment with key stakeholders</li>
<li>Analyzing the current business and IT environment to detect critical deficiencies and recommend solutions for improvement as it comes to the governance and usage of Master Data within our internal systems</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
</div>
