<b>Job Summary:</b> The focus of this role is to lead the strategy and execution for the IT application and quality assurance function. The IT application and QA manager will support our business applications and the successful implementation of new functionality to the Shentel technology ecosystem. The role will manage a team responsible for the configuring, maintaining and support of business applications in the Shentel ecosystem. The role will also be responsible for testing new functionality and ensuring the smooth transition of changes into the Shentel production environment. Finally the role is responsible for the performance of software applications or systems software. Specifically this role will be responsible for our Application Support and Quality Assurance.
<br><br>

<b>a. Education:</b> Bachelor's degree in computer science, system analysis or a related study, or equivalent experience.
<br><b>b. <br>c. Exposure to multiple, diverse technical configurations technologies encompassing mainframe, distributed systems, on-premise, cloud, and Applications as a Service i.e. Salesforce and Site Core).
