<p>EUC (End User Computing) Solutions Architect (SA)</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Nutanix EUC SA combines deep understanding and scaling of End User Computing process and tooling with a keen understanding of how to address the critical business needs of enterprise customers through Nutanix products and solutions. The SA is a high-impact, high-visibility role, and is considered to be the pinnacle of the Systems Engineering career path at Nutanix.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support our sales teams on large and/or strategic opportunities for EUC workloads</li>
<li>Engage with senior business and technical decision makers throughout the sales process.</li>
<li>Present and discuss the value proposition(s) associated with your area of specialization.</li>
<li>Build and grow relationships with respective integration partners (Citrix, VMware, Microsoft, etc.) to strengthen the Nutanix competitive advantage.</li>
<li>Create and deliver technical content to CxO and Director level individuals within customers and partners, communicating company vision as well as solution architecture and strategy.</li>
<li>Build long-term business relationships and become a trusted advisor within select accounts to identify opportunities and sell Nutanix Solutions.</li>
<li>Cultivate, grow and leverage reseller/alliance partner relationships to increase sales and grow pipeline.</li>
<li>Develop and deliver technical readiness content to Nutanix Sales Engineers as needed.</li>
<li>Install, support, and maintain Nutanix solutions during Proof of Concept (POC) deployments.</li>
<li>Stay well ahead of the learning curve in your area of expertise and related business trends.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Deep subject matter expertise in Application and Desktop Virtualization along with Networking, Storage, and Platform Performance/Sizing.</li>
<li>The ability to ascertain and articulate the business and technical needs of a customer and align those to existing Nutanix offerings.</li>
<li>Experience designing and implementing application and desktop virtualization solutions across multiple client platforms and network infrastructures</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of technical sales techniques and processes including the ability to understand customer needs, overcome objections, assist in the development of business cases, and technically close deals.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communications skills, as well as excellent presentation skills.</li>
<li>Experience writing and delivering focused technical and non-technical content in front of large audiences of customers, partners, engineers and executives.</li>
<li>Experience in architecting, designing and deploying solutions in a high governance environment.</li>
<li>Ability to travel within the United States as required</li>
</ul>
