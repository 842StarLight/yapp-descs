<div><b>
Position Summary</b></div>
<div>
The Corporate Counsel, Ethics and Compliance will be responsible for assisting the CECO with the design, implementation, day-to-day operation, and review of all global corporate compliance efforts related to a listed public company. This position is responsible for assisting the CECO with aligning the geographic regions and various organizational / departmental functions around a uniform approach to enterprise risk management; implementing common standards and controls to address enterprise risk; educating and training leaders, employees, and third parties regarding these standards and controls; and working cross-functionally to monitor, audit, and investigate possible violations, all as further described below. Finally, this position ensures that compliance efforts and issues are effectively reported to the CECO.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></div>
<p>
As directed by the CECO, the Corporate Counsel, Ethics and Compliance will perform or assist with the following matters:</p>
<p><b>
Leadership</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Builds strong relationships with senior management, management, business unit leads, operations and other staff in all global locations to further the purpose of the role; works across functions, regions and business units to effectively further the Company’s compliance program.</li>
<li>
Communicates and collaborates with the ethics and compliance team in an effective manner, including the preparation of, and adherence to, budgets and operational plans to achieve desired objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Standards and Controls</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Reviews and maintains the Company’s standards of conduct for ethics and compliance, including the <i>Code of Business Conduct and Ethics</i> and compliance-related policies.</li>
<li>
Develops, maintains and periodically reviews the Company’s compliance policies, procedures and protocols to ensure adherence to applicable Rimini Street standards as well as applicable laws and regulations.</li>
<li>
Provides guidance and representation to management and employees on the changing requirements related to compliance, including, but not limited to, anti-corruption / anti-bribery, business courtesies, conflicts of interest, export controls, sanctions, privacy and related areas.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Risk Assessment</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Partners with Internal Audit to develop, perform, and maintain an annual enterprise-wide risk assessment intended to identify, assess, monitor and mitigate risks that may affect Rimini Street generally, so they are managed within acceptable risk tolerance levels.</li>
<li>
Assesses and provides guidance regarding ethics and compliance risks related to new laws, regulations and client contractual obligations.</li>
<li>
Understands and keeps up to date with Rimini Street business, services and the commercial environment in which it operates in order to assess changing risks and compliance objectives.</li>
<li>
Coordinates and assists with third-party due diligence initiatives with regard to prospective clients, vendors and partners to ensure appropriate risk mitigation as well as compliance with applicable laws and regulations.</li>
<li>
Assists with compliance due diligence and integration efforts related to mergers and acquisitions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Monitoring, Auditing and Response</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assists with the development and day-to-day operation of policies, programs, procedures and tools that encourage and allow employees to report suspected violations of the <i>Code of Business Conduct and Ethics</i> and Rimini Street’s compliance policies, procedures, and standards without fear of retaliation.</li>
<li>
Handles the day-to-day operation of processes and procedures for compliance case intake and resolution, including responses to the Company’s ethics and compliance hotline reports and the investigation and closure of matters.</li>
<li>
Conducts internal investigations regarding alleged misconduct; provides oversight, guidance, and coordination of effective internal investigations, risk assessments and compliance reviews.</li>
<li>
Recommends and partners with management, HR and/or business unit leaders on assessing compliance audit and investigation results, and recommending and executing appropriate responses to same.</li>
<li>
Designs and recommends remedial efforts and/or modifications to the Global Ethics and Compliance Program, the <i>Code of Business Conduct and Ethics</i>, and/or Rimini Street’s compliance policies, procedures and standards in response to compliance investigations and audits as required.</li>
<li>
Partners with the Office of the General Counsel in response to regulatory audits and government investigations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education and Training</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develops and delivers training regarding compliance laws and regulations, the <i>Code of Business Conduct and Ethics</i>, and all compliance policies and procedures in a clear, effective, and timely manner to management, employees, and third parties.</li>
<li>
Periodically reviews and updates compliance education and training programs to meet new compliance laws, regulations, client contractual obligations, updates to the <i>Code of Business Conduct and Ethics</i> and Rimini Street’s compliance, policies, procedures and standards, or in response to violations of same.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Reporting:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepares materials and makes presentations to Rimini Street’s management and/or employees as requested regarding the Company’s Global Ethics and Compliance Program as well as compliance matters generally.</li>
<li>
Assists with the preparation of materials and presentations to the Rimini Street Board of Directors and/or Audit Committee regarding the Company’s Global Ethics and Compliance Program as well as compliance matters generally.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
General</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Adheres to Rimini Street policies on ethics and integrity.</li>
<li>
Promotes a culture of compliance by fostering an environment of open and honest communication with all employees of all levels – reinforcing the importance of conducting oneself in an ethical manner.</li>
<li>
Drives operational efficiencies by setting measurable goals and working on leveraging and deploying new and existing technologies to improve efficiencies in the compliance programs across the organization.</li>
<li>
Regularly attends courses and seminars to keep up to date with regulatory, market and industry developments.</li>
<li>
May perform other additional duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
