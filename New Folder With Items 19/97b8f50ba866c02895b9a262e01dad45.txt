<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Areya Hospitality Management</b> , a well-established and innovative firm focused on acquiring, developing, repositioning, renovating and operating hotels, is now seeking a</p>
<p><b>Hotel General Manager</b> to take on the leadership role of one of our hotels in the Harrisburg area off the Interstate 81 Corridor.</p>
<p>The General Manager is responsible for overseeing all aspects of the hotel in accordance with Company requirements including; maximizing financial performance, high quality standards, superior guest satisfaction, sales and marketing and overall staff hiring and development.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Creates an operating environment that ensures consistent guest satisfaction.</li>
<li>Maintains product and service quality standards by conducting ongoing evaluations and investigating complaints</li>
<li>Monitors the performance of the hotel through verification and analysis of guest satisfaction systems and financial reports.</li>
<li>Initiates corrective action.</li>
<li>Develops accurate and aggressive short and long term financial objectives consistent with the budget, company goals and investor expectations.</li>
<li>Prepares financial reports for the company that clearly explain operational effectiveness, trends and variances.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains a pro-active human resource function to ensure employee motivation, training and development, wage and benefits administration, and compliance with established labor regulations.</li>
<li>Maintains an appropriate level of community public affairs involvement.</li>
<li>Executes marketing, sales, and operational activities producing results that meet or exceed the hotel’s business plan.</li>
<li>Ensures good safety practices assisting in the maintenance of proper emergency and security procedures.</li>
<li>Understands the regulations affecting hotel operations, ensures the hotel is operated in compliance with all applicable laws, ordinances, regulations, and requirements of any federal, state or municipal authority.</li>
<li>Plans and organizes the work of others.</li>
<li>Accepts full responsibility for managing an activity.</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p>Typically, directly supervises between 3 to 5 employees at the hotel, including all department heads. Indirectly supervises all hotel personnel up to 30 employees. Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with the Company’s policies, training programs, and applicable laws.</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE : </b></p>
<p>Absent extraordinary prior on-the-job experience, the General Manager position requires a High School diploma or general education degree (GED) and preferably either a two-year business degree or a four-year business or liberal arts degree (or equivalent combination of education and experience).</p>
<p>Requires an occupationally-significant combination of vocational education, apprentice training, on-the-job training, and essential experience in less responsible hotel skill level and management positions.</p>
<p>A Certified Hospitality Administrator or Certified Lodging Manager designation is preferred but not required.</p>
<p><b>LANGUAGE SKILLS: </b> General Managers must have developed language skills to the point to be able to:</p>
<p>Read and interpret documents in English such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals, newspapers, periodicals, journals, and manuals.</p>
<p>Write routine reports, correspondence, business letters, summaries, and reports in English using prescribed format, and conforming to all rules of punctuation, grammar, diction, and style.</p>
<p>Ability to speak effectively in English before groups such as customers or employees.</p>
<p><b>COMPUTER SKILLS</b> : General Managers must have sufficient computer skills that will allow them to be able to use, in a proficient manner, all Company-issued software programs implemented at the hotel, including but not limited to the following:</p>
<p>Microsoft Word</p>
<p>Microsoft Excel</p>
<p>Yield Management Systems programs</p>
<p>Property Management System (PMS) programs</p>
<p>Daily Revenue System (DRS) programs</p>
<p>Central Reservation System programs</p>
<p>Payroll programs</p>
<p>Internet browser programs</p>
<p>Electronic mail programs</p>
<p><b>HOURS: </b> Variable. Primarily week days, however times may vary based upon need. Typically, this position works day hours and including a certain amount of weekends and holidays regularly throughout the year as needed for events, functions, group arrivals, guest service, etc.</p>
<p>When responding, please submit your resume and / or cover letter along with salary history and references.</p>
<p>If you understand the importance of serving others and delivering superior experiences leading to profitable hotels, then you may the candidate we are looking for.</p>
<p>If this seems like a position you’re best suited for, then we eagerly look forward to hearing from you!</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
