<b>Position Description</b><br>
<p>The Routing Center Analyst is responsible the routing and daily maintenance of 6-8 divisions or other duties as assigned.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Job Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Non-standard working hours – normal start time is approximately 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM</li>
<li>Schedules division’s deliveries, including transfers, to meet customer requirements</li>
<li>Determines routing assignments using Route Optimization Tool for maximum route efficiency</li>
<li>Maintains route / customer file data base accurately in routing tool</li>
<li>Communicates with assigned division Logistics Managers to examine results and improvement opportunities</li>
<li>Provides routing manifests, daily delivery reports each night to assigned divisions’ Logistics Managers and CSRs (and Sales Reps, as required)</li>
<li>Provides analysis of Group routes identifying GP per Route Reports and delivery expense reduction (ie. route consolidation and overlapping routes) opportunities</li>
<li>Provides viable route solutions for all proposed new business</li>
<li>Makes decisions on deliveries outside of Service Offering Matrix rules</li>
<li>Provides On-Site reviews / training of divisions’ routes for cost / benefit evaluations</li>
<li>Provides divisions’ “Planned Routes vs. Actual Routes” performance reports to Logistics Managers and Group Operations Manager</li>
<li>Executes required report routines within Xata software to prepare Electronic Fuel Reporting.</li>
<li>Ability to manage: delivery (WCOGS) daily volume changes, customer special delivery requirements and diesel price volatility impact on delivery expense</li>
<li>Tracks DOT Hours of Service constraints / changes</li>
<li>Adjusts driver and equipment availability due to call-offs / equipment service requirements</li>
<li>Manages driver feedback, accuracy and timeliness; driver skill level/engagement; CSR feedback/interaction to accommodate customer requirements constrained by delivery expense.</li>
<li>Interacts with Sales Reps requesting special favors for their customers</li>
<li>Develops optimum routing solution for 50-75 trucks / routes for 6-8 assigned divisions</li>
<li>Provides backup routing solution support to other Veritiv Routing Centers routers as required due to illness, system failure /continuity of operations</li>
<li>Creates “What If” routing scenarios monthly for delivery expense savings opportunities</li>
</ul>
