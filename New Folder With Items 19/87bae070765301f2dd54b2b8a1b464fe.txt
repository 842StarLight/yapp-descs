<h2>Social Media Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The successful Social Media Manager defines and establishes the company’s organic social media strategy with the goal of increasing visibility, traffic and website conversions. This role leads the development of organization-wide social media management standards, policies, and rules of engagement for Entertainment Cruises’ family of brands.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
In addition to organic social media strategy, the Social Media Manager is responsible for cultivating and managing online communities on the company’s behalf. Excellent writing and editing skills are required, as is a strong customer service mindset.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<p><b>
Lead and plan our content strategy for our organic social media accounts</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop &amp; execute a comprehensive social editorial calendar in collaboration with local markets - aligned with brand guidelines and overall fleet marketing strategy - leveraging research and insights to promote and optimize content that resonates with our various guest segments</li>
<li>Continuously develop fresh ideas for content, campaigns and contests</li>
<li>Define key performance indicators and implement enterprise level measurement, analytics and reporting methods to gauge success</li>
<li>Actively monitor social media trends and best practices, delivering insights to drive continuous improvement and reinvestment strategies</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Community Management &amp; Guest Care</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Daily management of Entertainment Cruises’ social media properties including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, which includes creating, editing and publishing content to platforms</li>
<li>Empower regional marketing team to contribute to their social channels through a comprehensive training and onboarding program. Approve and schedule all posts from regional teams, providing ongoing guidance and feedback</li>
<li>Reply and respond to comments on social media channels, ratings and reviews, etc. in a timely manner and monitor public conversations via listening platform.</li>
<li>Flag potential issues and alter stakeholders and/or develop action plans and responses as appropriate, including partnering with customer service to escalate and resolve issues/inquires</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Influencer Marketing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with EC’s PR team to develop and execute a comprehensive influencer marketing strategy</li>
<li>Identify and pitch influencers and media partners to participate in regional campaigns. Develop a capable network of influencers across EC’s local markets.</li>
<li>Create a clear plan for every influencer campaign to ensure optimum service + maximum leverage</li>
<li>Plan and execute experiential events for influencers and media partners to showcase our brands/products</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Additional Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Content creation: collaborate with Creative Director in the planning, production, and execution of photography &amp; video shoots</li>
<li>Assist in-house digital advertising specialist and external ad agency with any paid social advertising needs (content creation, photo edits, copy review, etc.)</li>
<li>Subject matter experton social selling &amp; personal branding best practices, offering guidance and training to regional sales associates</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Job Specifications / qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>3-5 years of demonstrated social media experience, including writing, editing and publishing content for specific audiences</li>
<li>Strong business acumen and focus on conversions / data</li>
<li>Experience with key technology platforms, including, but not limited to social media publishing and monitoring applications (Sprout Social, Hootsuite, Sprinklr or similar), and Office 365 (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, etc.)</li>
<li>Agency experience preferred</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal and written communications skills, including proficiency in copywriting, proofreading, critiquing and instructing others</li>
<li>Experience analyzing data, identifying trends, summarizing results and making recommendations based on findings</li>
<li>Experience working across an organization and creating solid partnerships with all relevant stakeholders to gather business requirements and set priorities for social media initiatives</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal &amp; networking skills – you can enter a room of strangers and talk to anyone</li>
<li>Project management excellence; attention to details; keep commitments big and small</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>For purposes of this job description only, references to the ‘Company’ are to the following subsidiaries of Entertainment Cruises, Inc.: Premier Yachts, Inc., Mystic Blue Cruises, Inc., Seadog Ventures, Inc., Spirit Cruises, LLC, and Deep Sea Charters, LLC. You are employed by one of these subsidiaries, not by Entertainment Cruises, Inc.</li></ul>
