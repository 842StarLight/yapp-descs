<div><b>
What you'll do:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Marketing Operations Analyst collaborates with stakeholders across Operations, Marketing, and Sales. This person will be making recommendations on processes and tools to increase marketing efficiency, enable campaign launches, and perform continual measurement and optimization of marketing campaigns. This position will report to the Demand Generation team but will have cross-functional responsibility and will be required to work with people throughout the company. A person in this role is passionate about marketing, data, analytics and a ninja when it comes to business processes and systems.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage technical aspects of key marketing systems (marketing automation, CRM) used to generate, distribute, and report on leads across several channels, including but not limited to email, display advertising, pay-per-click advertising, telemarketing, and events</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain scalable processes that ensure best practices in campaign and lead management</li>
<li>
Create and maintain metrics reports on marketing and sales activities and effectiveness and business impact</li>
<li>
Proactively explore data to find trends, discover KPIs, and apply proper hypothesis testing</li>
<li>
Monitor campaigns to optimize performance based on industry best practices</li>
<li>
Provide recommendations that enhance future campaign performance and measurements</li>
<li>
Develop robust KPIs and scorecards for marketing performance monitoring</li>
<li>
Produce and maintain metrics reports on marketing and sales activities, effectiveness, and business impact</li>
<li>
Present analysis and findings to a variety of audience</li>
<li>
Evaluate new technologies and add-on applications to improve and optimize marketing team performance</li>
<li>
Create and maintain documentation of all processes</li>
<li>
Assist in performing necessary due diligence on marketing technology purchases</li>
<li>
Build marketing and sales performance dashboards</li>
</ul>
