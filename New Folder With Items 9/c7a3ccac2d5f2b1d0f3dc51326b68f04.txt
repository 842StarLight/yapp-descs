<div>CooperVision, a unit of The Cooper Companies, Inc. (NYSE: COO), is one of the world’s leading manufacturers of soft contact lenses and related products and services. The Company produces a full array of monthly, two-week and 1-day lenses, all featuring advanced materials and optics. CooperVision has a strong heritage of solving the toughest vision challenges such as astigmatism and presbyopia; and offers the most complete collection of spherical, toric and multifocal products available. Through a combination of innovative products and focused practitioner support, the company brings a refreshing perspective to the marketplace, creating real advantages for customers and wearers. For more information, visit www.coopervision.com.</div>

<div><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Reporting to the VP of Global Commercial Operations, we are seeking a Commercial Data Officer to be responsible for developing and governing our data and information strategy in order to drive business decisions and growth. This role will develop procedures and policies that help manage data as corporate asset, and work closely with various departments to collect, prepare, organize, protect and analyze data assets while ensuring that the company meets industry best practices. Other duties will include leading inter-disciplinary teams, improving and streamlining data requests within the company.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The successful candidate will have a proven track record in data life cycle management, leadership and information technology systems and tools. A strong background in IT project and commercial business is required.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<i>Commercial Data Officer Responsibilities</i>:
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Develop a thorough understanding of the Commercial business, tie business needs to appropriate data/information assets, harness/exploit data/information assets to support current and future business needs.</div></li>
<li><div>
In collaboration with IT, designing and implementing data strategies and systems, including the Global Commercial Data Dictionary and Asset Catalog and quality assessment.</div></li>
<li><div>
Lead, motivate and influence teams to align on common/standardized definitions, quality metrics and process globally.</div></li>
<li><div>
Overseeing the collection, storage, management, quality and protection of Commercial data.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensuring Commercial adherence to CooperVision data privacy policies and complying with data protection regulations.</div></li>
<li><div>
Determine where to cut costs and increase revenue based on insights derived from data.</div></li>
<li><div>
Effectively communicate the status, value, and importance of data collection to executive members and staff.</div></li>
<li><div>
Knowledge of relevant applications, big data solutions, and data management and lineage tools.</div></li>
<li><div>
Establishing &amp; leading data governance council and framework through participation of regional and cross business leaders and data stewards.</div></li>
<li><div>
Extensive experience with MDM processes and implementation of at least 1 leading MDM technology (informatica, Oracle, Reltio…etc.).</div></li>
<li><div>
Key contributor in Commercial led systems related projects as SME on data governance and best practices</div></li>
<li><div>
Approver/Reviewer of Global intake process for Commercial IT initiatives.</div></li>
<li><div>
Drive consistency, reliability and build trust in data sets across the Commercial organization.</div></li>
<li><div>
In conjunction with Commercial PMO, lead change management efforts across the organisation with regards to data integrity and governance.</div></li>
</ul>
