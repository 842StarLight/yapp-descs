<h2>Rental Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b>: Manage and maintain existing radio rental customer relationships and develop new relationships with radio rental prospects by visiting job sites, contractors’ offices, industrial facilities etc. in addition to cold calling. Maintain positive customer relations. Visit customer sites on a periodic basis to build business relationships and maintain accurate records</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b><b> </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Consulting with and taking rental orders from clients.</li>
<li>Coordinating equipment needs and planning delivery schedules to fulfill customers’ rental orders.</li>
<li>Responsible for rental equipment inventory control.
</li>
<li>Providing top-notch customer service to all company customers. Much of the customer service includes basic equipment application, troubleshooting, and customer rental inventory inquiries.</li>
<li>Keeping the General Shop Operations Manager involved in the pursuit of attaining rental revenue goals.</li>
<li>Light marketing of company’s rental services to local events and happenings in the office territory.</li>
<li>Planning and installing equipment for customer’s rental needs.</li>
<li>Computer programming of rental equipment.</li>
<li>Cleaning and applying company stickers to all rental equipment that goes out and comes in from customers.</li>
<li>Computer entry of rental orders.</li>
<li>Coordinating delivery and return of rental equipment to and from customers</li>
<br>
</ul>
