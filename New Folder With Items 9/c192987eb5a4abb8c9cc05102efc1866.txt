<h2>Enterprise Managed Services Sales Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ECS is seeking an <b><i>Enterprise Managed Services Sales Rep</i></b> to work in our <b><i>Fairfax, VA</i></b> office.</p>
<p><b>Job Description:</b><br>
Account Manager – Enterprise Managed Services (Cybersecurity and Cloud)</p>
<ul>
<li>The Managed Services Account Manager is a field sales executive that utilizes communication, consultative selling, and interpersonal skills to sell ECS's Enterprise Cybersecurity and Cloud business offerings.</li>
<li>You will be part of a growing Enterprise business for all verticals outside of Federal, Intelligence Community and Civilian markets to in successful growth of the managed services.</li>
<li>You will engage with our strategic partners, OEM’s and end customers to generate new business through opportunity identification, qualifying, shaping and closing.</li>
<li>You will also support our OEM partners and consult with cybersecurity management, solution architects, sales, and delivery leadership on best ways to manage and develop those relationships as well as potential new partnerships and go to market sales strategies to include point product sales, solution sales, professional services sales, and managed cybersecurity solutions and managed services sales.</li>
</ul>
<p>Primary Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and build partner as well as end-customer relationships.</li>
<li>Provide pre and post-sales support to customers offering clear advice and solutions</li>
<li>Conduct detailed research of the responsible accounts and arrange meetings.</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver customer presentations and demonstrations of products.</li>
<li>Marketing and promoting a portfolio of products by attending industry events.</li>
<li>Coordinate writing proposals, reports, and supporting literature.</li>
<li>Maintain various sales certifications as required.</li>
<li>Coordinate closely with sales operations, PMO, contracts, engineers, and marketing.</li>
<li>Responsible for providing periodic reports on sales activity including priorities and forecast to VP.</li>
<li>Generate a positive image of our company in the marketplace and support ongoing thought and sales leadership in partnership with our OEM partners.</li>
<li>Provide ongoing market feedback including client priorities and needs; competitive updates; as well as emerging technologies. Research latest IT products and solutions.</li>
<li>Identify and develop new business through networking, account research, cold-calling, and executing marketing plans.</li>
<li>Participate in a success-oriented, ethical, and accountable environment within the company.</li>
<li>Be an example for all in terms of ethics and compliance.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b><i>Undergraduate Degree</i></b></li>
<li>Strong ethics and integrity</li>
<li>Previous experience in selling professional and managed services meeting and exceeding quota.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Cybersecurity and Cloud Computing market and offerings</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills both oral and written including presentation and negotiation</li>
<li>Min 7 years of experience in a similar field working in OEM channel, sales, distribution, or VAR role with demonstrated performance and quota/sales achievement</li>
<li>Up to 50% travel within United States.</li>
</ul>
