<h2>Regional Customer Service Specialist- West Region</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Our Company</b></p>
<p>Kannegiesser ETECH has a true passion for making our customer’s operations better and providing innovative solutions to the challenges of modern commercial laundry automation.</p>
<p>We are a global leader in the design and manufacture of integrated commercial laundry solutions. We offer the complete integrated solution including soil sort, monorail systems, washing, extracting, drying, flatwork feeding, finishing and folding, full dry folding, garment systems and sortation systems, and data information and productivity monitoring software systems.</p>
<p><b>This position is an exciting opportunity for the right individual in a fun yet professional team driven work environment.</b></p>
<p><b>Job brief</b></p>
<p>We are looking for professional candidates to provide high level service and support for our customers in the West region of North America.</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide a high level of communication and support for customers in your region.</li>
<li>Focus on building strong and lasting relationships with customers in your region.</li>
<li>Communication liaison and point person between customers and key contacts within our company.</li>
<li>Frequent onsite customer visits, site analysis, and follow up to ensure constant contact, open communication, and issue management.</li>
<li>Prepare customer and internal service reports.</li>
<li>Review customer’s spare parts inventory to ensure they have the stock they need to be successful.</li>
<li>Assist regional sales team with seeking out new business and other sales related tasks.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities Required</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong communication, written, and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>3+ years in customer service experience preferred.</li>
<li>College degree preferred.</li>
<li>Laundry industry experience preferred</li>
<li>Organizing and coordinating skills.</li>
<li>Ability to travel within your region ~25-50% of your time.</li>
<li>Ability to successfully multitask.</li>
<li>A team player with a positive winning attitude.</li>
</ul>
