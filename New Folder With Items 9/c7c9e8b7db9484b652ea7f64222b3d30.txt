We offer a wide-range of career paths, and we value your effort and contribution to the team and overall Control group. The Associate Accountant is responsible for the below:<br>
<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for all lease related financial reporting (ASC 842), including maintenance of the lease accounting software (Co-Star)</li>
<li>
Coordinate the completion/review of the quarterly A-9 Schedule; partner with plant, warehouse, QFS Canada, sales finance, and supply chain finance personnel to accurately report all existing operating/financing lease obligations and forecast all future obligations</li>
<li>
Monitor and manage the clearing of aged STO/In-Transit inventory</li>
<li>
Review for completeness and accuracy, then record all journal entries prepared by the Warehouse Finance team</li>
<li>
Conduct the annual review over the adequacy of the Quaker and Gatorade hourly vacation accruals</li>
<li>
Partner with Gatorade SC Finance to understand periodic Fixed Asset write-off activity and verify appropriate classification and external reporting presentation</li>
<li>
Calculate and record periodic inventory deferrals for Quaker and Gatorade</li>
<li>
Prepare account reconciliations for assigned accounts and follow-up on unusual activity until cleared; perform cash flow forecasts and variance analysis for assigned accounts</li>
<li>
Manage the clearing of unassigned income postings each period end close; track recurring postings and work proactively with individuals involved to prevent future postings</li>
<li>
Perform SOX controls for assigned areas of responsibility each period and prepare documentation evidencing quarterly test work over these control</li>
<li>
Assist the Tax Department in year-end preparation of assorted Tax Packages</li>
<li>
Complete various ad-hoc reporting and analyses, as needed.</li>
</ul>
