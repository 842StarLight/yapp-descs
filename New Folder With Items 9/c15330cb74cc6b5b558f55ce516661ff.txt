<div><b>
Job Summary:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As part of the Reputation.com Professional Services (PS) team you will have a detailed knowledge of our applications, using this knowledge together with your business process and consultative skills to maximize our solutions in the sales process.
</div>
<div>Having represented the PS team in the sales cycle you will ensure seamless handover and transition to the PS implementation team, ensuring that customers’ requirements are fully understood and deliverable.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>The key responsibilities of the SC role, include:</div>
<div>
**Customer requirements analysis</div>
<div>
**Customer solution design</div>
<div>
**Assistance with detailed design specification</div>
<div>
**Produce Statements of Works (SOW)</div>
<div>
**Services cost estimation</div>
Responsibilities:<ul>
<li>
Analysis of customer requirements.</li>
<li>
Customer solution design.</li>
<li>
Leverage your business process and consultative knowledge to offer customers best practice advice and guidance during the sales process and into the solution design.</li>
<li>
Produce detailed estimation of PS effort and SOWs for the PS team.</li>
<li>
Represent the PS team in the sales cycle, providing application knowledge and skills where necessary.</li>
<li>
Ensure seamless transition between the Reputation.com Sales, PS, Support and Customer Success teams.</li>
<li>
Identify customer critical success factors and minimize risk.</li>
<li>
Assist with project planning and resourcing as required.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications:<ul>
<li>
Minimum 2-4 years of customer facing experience in a consulting, sales, customer success or account management role. Experience in an implementation role highly preferred.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree Required.</li>
<li>
The successful candidate should have strong customer management and business consultancy skills.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of SQL and JSON programming languages preferred.</li>
<li>
You will be comfortable at both consulting with and negotiating with C level executives, backed up with a strong understanding of their business objectives and project management skills.</li>
<li>
Key to this role is being able to articulate value, work within a project structure and timelines, inspire and sell the future of software as a service.</li>
<li>
As a trusted advisor and coach, it falls to the SC to identify how Reputation.com software can be effectively applied to support the achievement of a company's strategic business goals while keeping to a project scope and timeframe.</li>
<li>
The successful candidate must have excellent interpersonal skills, be a strong communicator, organized and analytical with the ability to work well under pressure.</li>
</ul>
