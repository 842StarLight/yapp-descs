<h2>Sr. Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About The Company: </b></p>
<p>We are a high-end boutique travel agency headquartered in Charlotte North Carolina with offices in Florida and Italy. Started in 2004, owner &amp; founder Nada Vergili developed a revolutionary way of travel in Europe with a concentration on Italy. The company is comprised of roughly 20 team members and has a company slogan of "Tours for people who don't go on tours." The organization provides luxury travel packages comprised of elegant tours, eclectic restaurants &amp; immaculate accommodations that include castles.</p>
<p><b>Objective: </b></p>
<p>The objectives of the Nada’s Italy Journey Expert position is to complete the process of a sale from start to finish while providing excellent customer service. This may be done over the phone, by email, and in-person via walk-in clients, in-person meetings, and at trade shows/company-sponsored events. The job includes tracking down new sales prospects and maintaining relationships with existing customers and potential future buyers. The employee will conduct research, coordinate, and implement the execution of specific sales strategies with the purpose of increasing the Company’s total annual sales volume.</p>
<p><b>General Job Duties &amp; Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Contribute to the overall efficiency of the sales process by communicating with clients and internally with our staff.</li>
<li>Set short and long-term sales objectives and keep track of personal weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annual goals. Communicate progress and changes to supervisors on a weekly basis. Evaluate and adjust sales tactics as appropriate to meet established goals. Create personal monthly, quarterly, yearly sales plans with supervisors.</li>
<li>Improve sales strategies by studying, evaluating, and re-designing processes; establishing and communicating results. Achieve sales objectives by preparing and completing action plans in a measurable way; resolving problems; identifying market trends; researching opportunities, determining sales system improvements; implementing change.</li>
<li>Recommend service enhancements to improve sales potential, backed by own market research, client surveys, and evaluations. Help develop sales and marketing materials including but not limited to advertising, promotional materials, newsletters, sponsorships or online content.</li>
<li>Provide administrative support to all sales‐related activities, such as processing customer’s orders, keeping sales statistics, supplying price quotations, and related activities. The position may include evaluating, investigating, and settling claims and complaints of customers.</li>
<li>Update job knowledge by reading travel industry publications and travel books relative to destinations offered by the company. Participate in professional organizations as well as tradeshows and company‐sponsored events. Stay up to date on local events, strikes, exhibits, entertainment &amp; attractions, tourism regulations, national and international news.</li>
<li>Learn basic Italian pronunciation in order to correctly pronounce cities and destinations included in itineraries.</li>
<li>Fulfill requests for information and materials promptly, tracking the priority of assignments. Follow up on all incoming emails and phone calls within 24 hours. Prioritize tasks and communicate progress regularly to supervisors. Provide accurate tracking of progress and completion of projects by using spreadsheets, databases, checklists, emails, and calendars.</li>
<li>Keep own workspace clean and free of clutter, organize paperwork, keep proper filing of documents, and organize emails for quick access.</li>
<li>Communicate internally and externally using clear and professional language; use correct written English, answer telephones using appropriate etiquette. Be available to be trained by supervisors. Perform other reasonable job duties as requested by supervisors.</li>
<li>Follow all Company policies and procedures; confidentiality of proprietary information; protect company assets; protect the privacy and security of customers’ and associates’ information. Do not divulge company information to any competitors of the Company or to any third party except when approved by supervisors.</li>
</ul>

Occasional Saturdays may be required to work in the office as well as small clerical responsibilities will be apart of the duties.</p>
