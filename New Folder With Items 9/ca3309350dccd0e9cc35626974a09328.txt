<h2>Dispatch Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Founded in 1965, Universal Supply is a trusted resource for the professional home builder, remodeler, and consumer of quality building supplies and services throughout New Jersey, southeastern Pennsylvania, northern Delaware, and north and eastern Maryland, and central Connecticut.</p>
<p>While products are important, our success is due to our greatest asset, our people. Universal Supply’s team of dedicated professionals has expert knowledge of our products and our industry. This knowledge sets us apart and enables us to provide consistent, unsurpassed customer service. With relationships being the backbone of Universal Supply, we are excited to partner with our customers and focus on their success.</p>
<ul>
<li>recognize and appreciate the important role and contributions all our associates make to our daily business operations. To continue the commitment in servicing our customers, we are currently seeking a Dispatcher to join our Hammonton, NJ Central Dispatch team.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>The Dispatcher will be responsible for coordinating shipping and customer delivery activities. This position will also direct company truck drivers on delivery priorities, routes and job site information.</i></b></p>
<p><b>Things you should know about working at Universal Supply Company:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>We are all about teamwork! All positions are hands on and we band together when necessary.</li>
<li>We support each other. We have local and corporate team members to help you along the way and partner on projects as appropriate.</li>
<li>We are a relaxed atmosphere, this is not a suit and tie environment.</li>
<li>We work hard. We are a metric driven organization and we are focused on keeping organized and on task.</li>
<li>We recognize the importance of each and every customer</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Summary of the Dispatcher’s Role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create efficient delivery schedules using Descartes routing solution with the goal of maintaining 100% “On Time Deliveries”.</li>
<li>Maintain a shipping schedule (specific requests) for each day to maximize deliveries according to the workload.</li>
<li>Coordinate building loads and trucking with branch and warehouse managers to accommodate the shipping schedule at all times while working to decrease truck turnaround time.</li>
<li>Confer with sales and customers on delivery delays and respond to inquiries pertaining to delivery times.</li>
<li>Handle all customer complaints and/or concerns in same day received.</li>
<li>Ensure drivers are executing routes according to plan, completing pickups, and taking photos as required and that DVIR’s are completed daily as per the policy.</li>
<li>You support existing customer relationships.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>We offer…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Health care benefits, starting the first of month after 30 days of employment</li>
<li>401(k) with company match</li>
<li>Closed holidays</li>
<li>Paid time off</li>
<li>Employee discount</li>
</ul>
<p><b>We want you to join our team if you can check these boxes:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have a minimum of 1 year dispatch experience and 2+ experience in a building materials or construction related industry.</li>
<li>You have good computer skills, familiar with dispatching software such as Descartes and MS Office products.</li>
<li>Ability to read maps and give accurate directions.</li>
<li>You have excellent oral communication skills and positive interpersonal skills; you have demonstrated time management and organizational skills.</li>
<li>You can work well under pressure, react positively to ever changing delivery circumstances, and coordinate with various levels of personnel.</li>
<li>You can work independently but can be a good team player.</li>
<li>You are able to maintain and enhance positive customer relations.
</li>
<li>You are organized, very attentive to details, with excellent follow-up skills.
</li>
<li>There are physical demands which may include exposure to extreme temperatures (in our warehouse/yard) and be able to lift up to 50 lbs. on a random basis, standing and walking for extended periods of time; bending or stooping.
</li>
</ul>
