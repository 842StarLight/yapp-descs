<h2>Director of Marketing & Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB DESCRIPTION
</h1>
<div><div>
<p>Senior Executive responsible for winning &amp; influencing customers by overseeing Product Management and Marketing functions. Participate in creation of the long-term strategic direction of the organization and drive tactical plan execution to achieve objectives. Create and execute product plans to grow sales and maximize profitability. Provide overall organization, staffing, direction, and guidance of the marketing and product management functions of the Company. This includes, but is not limited to: website management, catalog production, advertising, marketing literature, tradeshows, Ecommerce, calibration and digital marketing. This position will be located at our Global headquarters in Michigan City, IN.</p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT</b></p>
<p>A successful candidate will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead Voice of Customer use models analyzation, product ideation, and product life cycle management.</li>
<li>Define marketing materials and programs, and prepare three to five year plans regarding product vision and overall strategic product mix for top and bottom line performance.</li>
<li>Develop and implement the Company’s brand strategy.</li>
<li>Set the direction of all marketing, market analysis, and market communication activities including competitive market objectives that are to be implemented by the marketing team.</li>
<li>Work closely with the sales team; enable them to meet their commercial objectives by providing them with appropriate tools, materials, and presentations, target customers, digital marketing, trade shows, web, social media, advertising etc.</li>
<li>Responsible for strategic sales channel development and product strategies targeting the strengths and core competences of the Company where possible by leveraging established existing channels and providing economies of scale for field sales.</li>
<li>Establish the growth strategy and drive direct sales activity through Ecommerce.</li>
<li>Employ marketing strategies, follow up, and order process improvement.</li>
<li>Conduct competitive analysis of similar websites whether they are direct competitors or not and positions Dwyer accordingly.</li>
<li>Network and benchmark to find, understand, present, and implement best practice for Ecommerce growth.</li>
<li>Provide insight to strategic changes and implements fresh new ideas that will keep the Company vibrant, successful, and forward thinking.</li>
<li>Coach, mentor, and develop them to be promoted within the Company based on performance metrics, talent indicators, and other company programs that support succession planning.</li>
<li>Assist with special projects as required and demonstrate a sense of urgency and cooperation with cross-functional assignments.</li>
<li>Employ strategic thinking and business acumen and participates in senior management leadership, guidance and decision-making of the organization.</li>
<li>Motivate and create a team environment that is goal orientated and strives for continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Manage talent within the department effectively and holds self and others accountable to achieve optimum performance.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
</div>

<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB REQUIREMENTS
</h1>
<div>
<p><b>WHAT WE EXPECT</b></p>
<p>Key personality attributes that are essential to this position are enthusiasm, creativity, a great salesperson to the organization and to our customers, big picture oriented and being adaptive. Most important is being able to “live the brand”.</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years’ experience and track record of being the chief architect, director, and achieving company revenue goals at a senior level position.</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record with product ideation and Ecommerce channels.</li>
<li>Must have a proven track record of success in senior marketing and product management roles.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or related field required, MBA and Professional chartered marketer a plus.</li>
</ul>
