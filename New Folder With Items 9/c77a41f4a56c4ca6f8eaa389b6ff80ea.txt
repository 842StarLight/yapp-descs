<p><b>About Teleflex Incorporated</b></p>
<p>Teleflex is a global provider of clinically effective medical technologies designed to improve the health and quality of people’s lives. We apply purpose driven innovation – a relentless pursuit of identifying unmet clinical needs – to benefit patients and healthcare providers. Our portfolio is diverse, with solutions in the fields of vascular and interventional access, interventional cardiology, surgical, anesthesia, cardiac care, interventional urology, urology, emergency medicine and respiratory care. Teleflex employees worldwide are united in the understanding that what we do every day makes a difference. For more information, please visit teleflex.com.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Interventional Urology – </b>NeoTract is dedicated to developing innovative, minimally invasive and clinically effective devices that address unmet needs in the field of urology. As part of the Teleflex Interventional Urology business unit, NeoTract’s initial focus is on improving the standard of care for patients with benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) using the UroLift® System, a minimally invasive, permanent implant system that treats symptoms while preserving normal sexual function1. The FDA-cleared UroLift System is clinically proven to treat lower urinary tract symptoms due to BPH. The UroLift System lifts or holds the enlarged prostate tissue out of the way so it no longer blocks the urethra. There is no cutting, heating or removal of prostate tissue. More than 57,000 men worldwide have been treated with the UroLift System, which is available in the U.S., Europe, Australia, Canada, Mexico and South Korea. Join a dynamic, growing team that offers healthcare providers an array of medical technology solutions that make a difference in patients’ lives. <b><i>1 </i></b><i>*No instances of new, sustained erectile or ejaculatory dysfunction. McVary; Roehrborn, J Urology 2013 LIFT Study.</i></p>

<b>
Position Summary</b>
</div>
<div>
<div>The Corporate Counsel is responsible for supporting the daily operations for the NeoTract, Interventional Urology Business Unit (IUBU) Legal Department and legal affairs of the IUBU. The Corporate Counsel provides legal counsel to IUBU employees, and other key stakeholders. Reporting to Assistant General Counsel – Interventional Urology, this position demonstrates the IUBU’s values by partnering cross-functionally in a collaborative environment to support scaling and building a best-in-class medical device business.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Principal Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Provide general legal support to the IUBU on a variety of issues, identify areas of risk, recommend strategies, and identify strategic solutions that lead to successful business outcomes.</li>
<li>Advise internal clients on a broad range of legal matters, including, but not limited to, contract interpretation, trade secrets and IP ownership, dispute resolution, and privacy considerations.</li>
<li>Support the efforts of the IUBU legal team to achieve quarterly and annual objectives.</li>
<li>Build on the business unit’s continuous improvement environment by assisting with the development of processes consistent with Teleflex’s policies and the business unit’s objectives.</li>
<li>Review, negotiate and draft a wide range of contracts, including complex commercial agreements.</li>
<li>Assist with ensuring the Company conducts its business in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.</li>
<li>Partner within the Teleflex Legal Department.</li>
<li>Review sales, advertising, and promotional materials and programs.</li>
<li>Complete all work in compliance with the FDA and international regulatory bodies.</li>
<li>Contribute to the company culture of being collaborative, respectful, transparent, ethical, efficient, high achieving, and fun!</li>
</ul></div>
