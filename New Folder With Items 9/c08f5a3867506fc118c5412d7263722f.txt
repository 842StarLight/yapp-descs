<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop Manual Unit and System Test cases bases on project requirements.</li>
<li>
Review the requirements, develop extensive Unit Test cases and System Test cases to cover all requirements, including expected results.</li>
<li>
Execute Manual Test Cases and report results.</li>
<li>
Compare expected results with executed results, and report to Development any discrepancies.</li>
<li>
Develop complex SQL queries to assist with determining expected results and comparing with actual results.</li>
<li>
Follow approved SDLC and project management methodology.</li>
<li>
Assist with developing new processes to make Manual Testing environment more efficient.</li>
<li>
Service as a technical resource, mentor and trainer to other QA Manual Testers.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates the ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Creates documentation in accordance with tasks performed.</li>
<li>
Ensures written communication is accurate, timely and thorough.</li>
<li>
Adheres to accepted standards and procedures.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Four year college degree and 4 or more years professional experience or 6 or more years professional experience is required.</li>
<li>
Four or more years working with a Relational Database Management System and Manual Testing software.</li>
<li>
Strong written and oral communications skills.</li>
<li>
Experience writing and debugging Manual Unit and System Test cases.</li>
<li>
Ability to translate business requirements into a technical architecture using common business tool sets.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple priorities, operate and perform with little or no supervision.</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Two or more years working with Microsoft software products including SQL Server and Team Foundation Service.</li>
<li>
Degree in Computer Science.</li>
</ul>
