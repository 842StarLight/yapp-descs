<h2>ISR</h2>

<p>We are looking for an experienced salesperson who has experience in either software sales, trucking industry sales, or telematics/cellular sales. Someone who can learn extremely quickly and obsesses over every detail. You will be forced to learn a lot, so if you can't learn it quickly, it will be very difficult for you to keep up and meet expectations.</p>
<p>This is an opportunity to get groomed by someone who will mentor you, challenge you, hold you to a very high standard, be critical of you, and ultimately teach you everything that you didn't know you needed to know. Your understanding of startups and business will increase 10-fold, but only if you are the right person for the job.</p>
<p>Job Duties</p>
<ul>
<li>Calling inbound leads</li>
<li>Identify growth opportunities within established customer network for upselling and addon sales</li>
<li>Customize product lines to meet the specific needs of the Clients.</li>
<li>Access and analyze Client account information to determine “needs and wants” to maximize growth of revenue per account.</li>
<li>Follow up with buyers in a daily basis to ensure 100% satisfaction. Follow up on previous orders.</li>
<li>Retention sales, speaking with customers that put in cancellation to “save the sale”</li>
</ul>
<p>REQUIRED EXPERIENCE/SKILLS:</p>
<ul>
<li>2 Years inside Sales experience</li>
<li>Consultative selling technique</li>
<li>Excellent written &amp; verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Engaging personality with an ability to run a live demo with a technical and non-technical audience</li>
<li>A team player, quick thinker, and a fast learner</li>
<li>Must be very organized</li>
<li>Not afraid to discuss money</li>
</ul>
<p>DESIRED SKILLS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Spanish speaking is a huge PLUS</li>
<li>Passionate about winning; focused on energizing client relationships</li>
<li>SalesForce experience</li>
</ul>
