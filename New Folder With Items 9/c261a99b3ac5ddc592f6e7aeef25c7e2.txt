<p>Overview

<div><div><div>
<div>Leveraging deep integrations into retailers’ point-of-sale systems, InComm is revolutionizing the commerce experience through innovative payments technology. InComm leverages end to end solutions to bring branded activatable products to retailers both in store and digitally. Our process includes everything from card printing, inventory management and forecast analytics, to the development and integration of activation technology in point of sale machines world-wide. From there we leverage our backend platform as a service technology to drive product activation to the tune of several thousand card activations every second. Whether those consumers are activating prepaid products, paying bills, enjoying real-time discounts through a membership card, purchasing digital goods in-store or adding funds to an online account, InComm is there to provide unique gift-gifting opportunities, cater to on-the-go shoppers, deliver added value through loyalty programs and serve cash-based consumers.</div>

<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Act as a team lead in all aspects of audits including risk assessments, audit planning, audit testing, control evaluation, report drafting and follow-up and verification of issue closure</li>
<li>
Demonstrate appropriate prioritization, planning and organization skills to utilize time and resources effectively to ensure project deadlines and goals are met</li>
<li>
Perform analysis and evaluation of financial, operational and technology based business processes and general controls</li>
<li>
Thorough completion of work papers and draft reports prior to submission to management</li>
<li>
Provide coaching and feedback to staff members during the execution of audits and departmental initiatives</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain ongoing relationships with audit customers, as a trusted advisor in providing advisory support on internal controls and best practices</li>
<li>
Perform other related duties as required.</li>
<li>
Assist in audit scoping activities including resource planning and scheduling.</li>
<li>
Document project scope, work papers, issues and audit reports.
</li>
<li>Conduct a variety of audits and report findings to auditee and management.</li>
<li>
Assist in the documentation and maintenance of key processes, procedures and control activities details aligned to company standards.</li>
<li>
Complete daily audit fieldwork and report engagement status.</li>
<li>
Recommend standards and solutions for process improvements, strengthening controls, cost savings, automation, etc.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain productive working relationships with audit customers and gauge customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Assist with maturing the audit program to effectively evaluate business processes, based on leading practices, regulatory requirements, industry standards and operating environment.</li>
<li>
Assist with developing and maintaining the audit plan, including risk assessments and other related special projects.</li>
<li>
Cultivate a thorough understanding of the Company’s accounting, operational, IT, audit and compliance functions and be able utilize that knowledge to benefit of our stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Provide consulting guidance and pre-implementation reviews to audit customers.</li>
<li>
Assist on special projects as directed by leadership.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</p>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of relevant work experience</li>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree in Accounting, Business Administration, Information Systems or equivalent</li>
<li>

<li>
Experience performing audits independently</li>
<li>
Demonstrated knowledge of internal control concepts and experience in applying them to plan, perform, manage and report on the evaluation of various business processes, areas and functions</li>
<li>
General understanding IT general controls and application controls best practices</li>
<li>
General understanding of the technical aspects of accounting and financial reporting</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office applications</li>
<li>
Excellent teamwork and client service skills</li>
<li>
Self-motivated with a strong drive for achievement</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills in writing and presentations</li>
<li>
Comfortable working with various levels of client management, including members of senior and executive management</li>
<li>
Theoretical and practical knowledge of major risk and control frameworks or IT frameworks (e.g.; COSO, COBIT, ISO, NIST, etc.) is a plus.
</li>
<li>Excellent problem solving skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively with people even under challenging circumstances</li>
<li>
Experience working in highly regulated industry (Financial Services experience a plus)</li>

Big 4 public accounting and/or audit experience preferred</li>
</ul>
