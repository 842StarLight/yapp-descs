<h2>Smart Building IoT Solutions Account Executive-Midwest US States</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Position Overview:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Siemens Smart Infrastructure with Enlighted Inc. is currently searching for an experienced account executive to lead business development and sales activities for our IoT Business division. This individual will develop and deliver smart building sales solutions and services targeting existing Siemens customers in addition to growing the customer base. This exciting new role leverages the Enlighted Building IoT platform to lay the foundation of a smart building. In addition to the Enlighted solution, added IoT software applications will be sold to the building owner as a value-added service (SaaS). Collaboration with Siemens branch colleagues will allow additional solutions and services to be added to the overall smart building solution. This IoT Solutions Sales Executive will be based out of our North America Branch locations and report to the Branch Manager and IoT National Sales Manager</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This will be a remote position that will travel throughout the Midwest US states.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul><li>Collaborate with Siemens branch account executives to develop Smart Building opportunities within the existing Siemens customer base</li></ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Educate and promote the Enlighted IoT platform within the commercial construction market that includes, Architects, Engineers, General Contractors, Developers, Property Managers and Building Owners</li>
<li>Identify, reach, and engage key decision makers and influencers through social media, networking, personal connections, client references, and industry events</li>
<li>Develop a healthy sales pipeline by converting warm leads into well-qualified opportunities</li>
<li>Supports local sales management with strategic planning and forecasting. Research, develop and maintain competitive information resources.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 years of combined inside and outside sales experience, selling SaaS, enterprise software, real estate technology or building controls technology to senior executives and technical buyers at Fortune 500 companies</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree necessary, extra points for an MBA, or equivalent experience. You will be confident and comfortable in a variety of roles, ranging from “social seller” to “consultative sales professional” to “challenger,” and use your emotional intelligence to employ the most effective role</li>
<li>You have experience driving toward sales targets and meeting key performance indicators</li>
<li>Qualified Applicants must be legally authorized for employment in the United States. Qualified Applicants will not require employer sponsored work authorization now or in the future for employment in the United States.</li>
</ul>
