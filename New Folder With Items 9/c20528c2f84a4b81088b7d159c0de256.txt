<h2>Regional Account Executive- South Central Territory (Florida)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">As a Regional Account Executive, you'll be responsible for securing valid and profitable contracts for the Rehab and Respiratory business unit of WellSky by effectively developing and managing customer relationships, generating new leads, and executing the sales process. You'll be aligned with our Rehab solution and service line, and be a key player in expanding this growing line of business. As a Regional Account Executive for our Southeast region, you'll target inpatient rehab facilities, hospital outpatient facilities, and private practices. The ideal candidate is highly motivated and a strong hunter with excellent organizational skills and the ability to navigate the C-Suite. Must be a driven self-starter with strong written and oral communication skills. This opportunity is slated to have high levels of professional growth- apply today to be considered for this exciting opportunity!
<br><br>
<b>Accountable Results:</b><br>
Exceed sales quota by managing all aspects of the sales process
<br><br>
<b>Key Activities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Prospect for new opportunities and generate new leads</li>
<li>Qualify leads, follow up on existing leads, and schedule product demonstrations for opportunities</li>
<li>Generate proposals, price quotes and contract requests</li>
<li>Communicate features of products and services articulately and persuasively to clients</li>
<li>Track, maintain, and report sales pipeline, sales activity, and contact information within the CRM</li>
<li>Manage pipeline and forecast accurately</li>
<li>Visit prospects and client's in-person to build relationships, discuss products and assist with presentations</li>
<li>Attend industry conferences and sales meetings</li>
<li>Respond to the needs and requests of clients and WellSky management and staff in a professional and expedient manner</li>
</ul>
<b>Job Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>Must reside in Florida or Texas with access to a major airport (up to 50% travel required)</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of experience in direct sales with a proven track record of exceeding sales quota.</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years in Field Sales (Outside Sales)</li>
</ul>
