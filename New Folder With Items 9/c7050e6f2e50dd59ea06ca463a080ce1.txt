<h2>Assoc Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>IQVIA™ is the leading human data science company focused on helping healthcare clients find unparalleled insights and better solutions for patients. Formed through the merger of IMS Health and Quintiles, IQVIA offers a broad range of solutions that harness the power of healthcare data, domain expertise, transformative technology, and advanced analytics to drive healthcare forward.</p>
<p><b>
Consultant, Forecasting</b></p>
<p><b>
Role Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Are you an analytical, data-driven professional who likes client facing consultative roles? Are you interested in a job that offers an opportunity to be an onsite trusted advisor to our clients instead of a “back office” position? If so, IQVIA’s Commercial Outsourcing Servicing (COS) team is the place for you! Join our team as a forecasting consultant helping our clients bring efficiencies and effectiveness to their forecasting process while partnering across client teams (finance, market access, marketing etc.)</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
As an Associate Consultant/Consultant in Commercial Operations Services, you’ll be actively participating in the development, delivery, and management of forecasting, and related consulting projects:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Deploy to onsite engagement anywhere in the United States, location dependent on business demands; all assignments involve onsite presence at client locations</li>
<li>
Manage projects and work streams of reasonable, independently or with senior support</li>
<li>
Lead projects and initiatives with indirect oversight over analysts and communication with clients</li>
<li>
Analyze secondary data and desk market research (quantitative and qualitative) to identify key findings and trends</li>
<li>
Should be able work on Market and Epidemiology Assessment, Pipeline Analysis and Market Research, Forecasting Inline and Pipeline products with senior support</li>
<li>
Create and lead quantitative or qualitative analysis to support client deliverables and issues</li>
<li>
Independently create presentations for clients; attend, support and present at client meetings, independently or with senior support</li>
<li>
Assist in developing detailed documentation and specifications, including business process documentation, SOPs, client data scorecards</li>
<li>
Work willingly and effectively with others in and across the organization to accomplish team goal, both internally and externally</li>
<li>
Develop broad knowledge of related consulting methodologies and pharmaceutical market through the delivery of consulting engagements and participation in formal and informal learning opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<p>
Candidates interested in joining as a Consultant should have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Knowledge and understanding of the fundamental processes of pharmaceutical commercial business, their interaction, and the impact of external/internal influences on decision making, growth and decline</li>
<li>
Knowledge of key issues and current developments in the pharmaceutical and healthcare industries</li>
<li>
Knowledge of professional/trade associations, key people and companies.</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge in MS Excel, MS Access, MS PowerPoint and working knowledge of following software is a plus Excel VBA, @Risk, Crystal Ball, Flexicast®, Spotfire, SAS, SPSS,</li>
<li>
Should be able to design and carry out S&amp;ME patient studies: Source of Business, P&amp;C, &amp; Patient Flow, Market sizing and Oncology &amp; Data marts</li>
<li>
Demonstrate analytical, interpretative and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Well-developed written and verbal communication skills including presentations, leading meetings, business and report writing</li>
<li>
Excellent project management capabilities so that deadlines are met while retaining consistent, high quality outcomes</li>
<li>
Ability to think creatively and strategically, and Demonstrate analytical, interpretative and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Prior healthcare data experience is a plus: National and sub-national data (Physician, Patient, claims and epidemiology)</li>
<li>
Prior internship, project work, and/or working experience in any of the following: forecasting, market research, epidemiology, marketing insights, marketing analytics, primary market research, multichannel marketing, consumer marketing, statistics, predictive modeling, managed markets, HEOR and data mining</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>
In addition to the skills and experience above, candidates interested in joining the QuintilesIMS as an Associate Consultant/Consultant should have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Undergraduate or Masters degree in life sciences/Marketing MBA with 2-8 years of relevant experience in forecasting in healthcare industry</li>
<li>
An avid interest in the healthcare analytics and data; understanding of the healthcare market in US</li>
<li>
Strong business acumen and understanding of sales &amp; marketing business principles</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, interpersonal and team-working skills</li>
<li>
Exceptional written, numerical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>
Adaptability and an ability to learn quickly and apply new knowledge</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel at minimum 80% overnight</li>
<li>
Willingness to relocate anywhere within the United States for client engagements ranging in length from 3 to 24 months</li>
</ul>
