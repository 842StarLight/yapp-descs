<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Essential Job Functions:</b>
<ul>
<li>Answers calls and emails made to CSC.</li>
<li>Opens Support Tickets for calls and emails.</li>
<li>Tracks all calls received and communicates problem solutions and resolution to affected parties as appropriate.
</li>
<li>Follows-up with affected parties if applicable.</li>
<li>Escalates and notifies management of business impacting problems.</li>
<li>Performs 1st level problem resolution.</li>
<li>Handles all customer dispute calls and directs them accordingly.</li>
<li>Demonstrates sensitivity to the customer perspective.</li>
<li>Performs task oriented duties as assigned.</li>
<li>Develop an awareness and understanding of and adopt professional practices that incorporate the Mission, Vision and Diversity goals into day-to-day attitudes, practices, processes and activities.</li>
<li>Identify and report processes, policies and internal/external service providers that are not meeting customer needs, expectations or quality standards.</li>
<li>Identify and report to management singularly urgent or developing situations that may need department or corporate attention.</li>
<li>Participate in the quality initiatives of the CSC.</li>
<li>Able to analyze situations with careful attention to detail as it fits into the larger picture</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Able to effectively handle and manage tasks within specific time frames.</li>
<li>Able to easily and positively adjust to changing priorities and department processes.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively work with customers in a sensitive, flexible and yet professionally assertive (non-aggressive), proactive manner.
</li>
<li>Understands departmental and company policies and procedures, support processes.</li>
<li>Follows oral and written instructions accurately.</li>
<li>Effectively manages time and work flow to meet schedules.</li>
<li>Accurate data entry and multiple communication systems for sales orders, financial changes, and other customer information or data.</li>
<li>Submit workflow emails related to new contracts or changes.</li>
<li>Process customer requests for changes, generating reports, and access to mobile applications.</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Education and Experience:</b>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma</li>
<li>1 year of customer service experience</li>
<li>Customer Service Calls</li>
<li>Verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Basic understanding of the principles and concepts of the key technology, including, applications, ATM technology, ATM systems management/operations, and security.</li>
<li>Knowledge of personal computers and related software.</li>
<li>Knowledge of telecommunications and computer systems terminology.</li>
<li>Knowledge of telephone systems and Voicemail.</li>
<li>Knowledge of database systems.</li>
<br>
</ul>
