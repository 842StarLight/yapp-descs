<h2>Support Service Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Support Service Consultant
<p></p>
<p>Allegion’s Specification Services and General Contractors serve as the key entry point into the business’s sales funnel leading to Allegion’s continued growth and success. This position will help ensure the continued efficiency and productivity of Specification Writers and Sales Teams. This position requires excellent customer service skills with a focus on being outcome-oriented and willing to learn industry specific software. The ability to work in a fast-paced environment with strong attention to detail is essential.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Major Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reviews construction plans and specifications delivering a quantitative examination ensuring the documentation follows standards set by the Architectural firm
</li>
<li>Compute and prepare financial analysis of submitted documentation, identifying areas of unearned revenue and cost of missing hardware
</li>
<li>Communicates with Specification Writers and Project Coordinators regarding project scope, schedule, risks, etc.
</li>
<li>Assumes responsibility for the submittal review of division 8, including but not limited to, compliance, conversion, and pricing
</li>
<li>Maintains companywide product conversion database/library
</li>
<li>Utilize Microsoft Dynamics to create, share and connect the sales team to opportunities
</li>
<li>Lead and support bid tasks including the following: Convert competitor products, finalize conversion list pricing, verify subcontractors scope adherence to specification, resolve conflicts and questions submitted
</li>
<li>Manage email inbox for general contractor bid invitations and review plans and specifications to find new project leads
</li>
<li>Assist with management of our loyalty rewards program
</li>
<li>Manage the general contractor prequalification process
</li>
<li>Field inbound general contractor sales calls and connect them to the general contractor team
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate degree required, Bachelor’s Degree preferred
</li>
<li>2+ years of administrative support experience required. Experience with reading blueprints as well as estimating principles and policies preferred.
</li>
<li>Ability to learn industry specific software
</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects with accuracy and achieve deadlines under pressure
</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented with strong analytical skills
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel and Access. Experience with Bluebeam preferred.
</li>
<li>Can handle sensitive information with the highest degree of integrity and confidentiality
</li>
<li>Strong customer service and problem solving skills
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and manage own work load with minimal supervision or direction
</li>
</ul>
