<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Integrated Distribution Services, Inc (IDS) is a third party distribution company with facilities in Plainfield, IN and SLC, UT. For over fifty years, IDS has delivered a comprehensive mix of supply chain logistics services that includes warehousing, distribution, order fulfillment and transportation services to customers in diverse industries.</p>
<p>IDS Fulfillment specializes in order fulfillment for e-commerce, direct sales, retail and start-up companies serving a wide variety of industries as well as excelling in local public and contract warehousing.</p>
<p>IDS is seeking an experienced and highly self-motivated person for our Customer Service Representative position located at our Plainfield, IN facility.</p>
<p>Qualified applicants will perform all customer service activities such as responding to inquiries, processing orders, handling complaints, invoicing, special requests, etc. in a prompt courteous and effective manner.</p>
<p>***This is not a call center environment***</p>
<p><b>POSITION ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>Process and input all customer orders.</p>
<ul>
<li>Run various reports to check for product availability.</li>
<li>Generate all related paperwork and necessary information required for customer orders.</li>
<li>Coordinate special shipping requests with the operations department.</li>
<li>Track orders as required and notify customers of any activity concerning their order.</li>
<li>Handle returned merchandise in an efficient manner and ensure proper credit is given.</li>
<li>Act as liaison between the warehouse, transportation and the customer.</li>
<li>Oversee all paperwork associated with orders and maintain the corresponding files.</li>
<li>Maintain a current and accurate procedures manual that details the processing for each account.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>SECURITY</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Complies with all company inventory control and security procedures.</li>
<li>Understands the importance of maintaining secure control of the client’s inventory.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>SAFETY</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Is aware of and complies with basic warehouse safety and housekeeping standards.</li>
<li>Reports safety issues promptly to management.</li>
<li>Understands the importance of safety awareness when in the warehouse.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>EQUIPMENT USED</i></b></p>
<ul><li>Utilizes a computer terminal for the Warehouse Management System, shipping and receiving systems, and a variety of supporting software.</li></ul>
<p>The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skills, and/or abilities required to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul><li>1-2 years customer service experience (Fulfillment preferred)</li></ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate in English—both verbally and in writing.</li>
<li>Ability to interact with customers in a courteous and professional manner.</li>
<li>Ability to think logically and problem-solve in order to analyze situations, troubleshoot, and make sound decisions.</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Knowledge of basic computer use including Word and Excel.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS</b></p>
<p>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to stand and sit for periods of time and to move intermittently throughout the workday.</li>
<li>Ability to perform focused work with close attention to detail.</li>
<li>Must have strong sensory skills, such as good eyesight and good hearing.</li>
</ul>
