<p><b>ABOUT LOANPAL</b></p>
<p>
We are the world-positive lender. Our mission is to connect billions of dollars of capital to millions of homeowners that want to convert their outdated houses into modern, smart, energy-efficient homes. By unlocking access to numerous products that help people achieve a more sustainable lifestyle, we are revolutionizing the $350 billion+ home improvement industry. As such, we have become the financial ecosystem for mission-driven businesses who need fast and frictionless lending to empower their customers, and we help consumers save money with our modern lending technology.</p>
<p>
As part of our world-positive initiative, we are also the official sponsor of GivePower – a foundation that uses solar energy and storage technologies to deliver the most essential community services to regions of the world that need it most.</p>
<p>
If you have an unstoppable desire to make a meaningful impact on our planet and help mission-driven businesses and consumers achieve a more sustainable future, join us.</p>
<p>
This is an entry level mortgage lending operations position responsible for business operations support for mortgage sales and processing. The Loan Operations Analyst will work with the Vice President of Mortgage Operations and Business Executives on strategic business initiatives to maintain compliance, ensure efficiency, and an amazing customer experience. In addition the Loan Operations Analyst with support mortgage sales and loan processing. The Loan Operations Assistant will learn the mortgage lending business inside and out, to prepare for advancement within the company. We promote from within and use our small Loan Operations Assistant group serves as a feeder program for very lucrative Loan Processing and Underwriting roles.<br>
<br>
<b>Here’s what a typical day may look like:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work on business level reporting and daily operational needs. This includes utilizing internal software along with excel to communicate out loan related timeframes and needed tasks.</li>
<li>
Manage kick-back files and accountable for compliance related timeframes. Help ensure timeframes are always met.</li>
<li>
Become fluent in running Desktop Underwriter and Loan Prospector and interpreting results.</li>
<li>
Reviewing conditional approvals and assist in meeting conditions</li>
<li>
Assist with Manage loan pipeline for 1-5 bankers.</li>
<li>
Contact 3rd parties to get pay offs, notes, change of insurance.</li>
<li>
Contact with clients as needed. Print/Highlight disclosures that need to mail.</li>
<li>
Prepare business level reporting for Executives on pipeline movement and operational efficiencies</li>
</ul>
