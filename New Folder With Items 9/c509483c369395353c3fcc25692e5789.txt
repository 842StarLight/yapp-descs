<h2>Senior Manager, Customer Solutions Programs and Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing…
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Hot Accounts Team sits within Tableau’s Worldwide Technical Support organization. Technical Support is responsible for resolving our customers’ technical issues swiftly and with quality. Sometimes, issues become complex and then The Hot Accounts Team is sought out.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our team delights customers at critical junctures in order to retain and grow business. As a Program Manager for the Hot Accounts Team, you will be responsible for developing, executing, and scaling programs that are focused on delivering on our mission. You will work with internal colleagues to optimize internal processes and create the most simple, effective, and efficient experience possible. If we do our job right, our customers will not need us, and instead they can focus their energy on seeing and understanding data. The person hired for this role will align their efforts to directly and measurably improve the customer experience.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This particular program manager will be hiring and leading a team of three escalation case managers who will be globally distributed. The challenge this person must solve for is how we first put a band-aid on poor customer experiences. Then, more critically, this person must determine how we solve the issues at the root level. You will need to empower your team to work together to solve this challenge, using scalable processes and diligent measurement.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Some of the things you’ll be doing include …
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Analyze root causes of challenges across our Hot Accounts Team
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop solutions through a collaborative process with a focus on the user. These solutions could include documentation, process design, quality assurance, automation, outsourcing, training, or other scalable methods
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish repeatable and consistent processes to sustain and grow the program
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Measure results with a strong focus on impact
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Who you are…
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experienced. </b>10+ years of impactful experience, preferably with large global organizations or equivalent impact. Valid passport or willing to obtain one in the first six months of employment
</p>
<p><b>People Manager. </b>You : 1) are a good coach 2) empower the team 3) express interest and concern about your team’s well-being 4) are productive and results-oriented 5) communicate well 6) help with career development 7) have a clear vision and strategy for the team 8) have the key technical and/or functional skills to help advise your team
</p>
<p><b>Operator </b>You know how to break down complex services challenges. You think about the impact on the users. You identify the key measurements to monitor. You execute, you iterate, and you improve processes and people.
</p>
<p><b>Effective Communicator. </b>You need to influence without authority. You will be working with a diverse set of partners. You will need to communicate clearly in written and verbal formats with ability to moderate based on audience
</p>
<p><b>Collaborator. </b>Enjoy teamwork and proactively collaborates with other diverse stakeholders (we work with sales, developers, external customers, internal leadership)
</p>
<p><b>Growth Minded </b>You are never satisfied with the status quo and never say “that is how we do it here”
</p>
<p><b>Gritty </b>You have overcome battles in life, taken ownership, and become stronger because of it
</p>
