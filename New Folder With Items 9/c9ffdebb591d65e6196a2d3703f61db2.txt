<h2>Account Executive - Publishers</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">POSITION SUMMARY
<br><br>
Ranked one of the top 2019 Best Companies by Built In Colorado, AdAction Interactive is an industry leading mobile app performance advertising network. We are seeking a Manager, Publisher Development – Mobile Apps, to help prospect, manage, service, retain, and maximize revenue opportunities with new publishing partners. The successful applicant will be responsible for identifying and engaging new publishing partners, cross-departmental assistance with onboarding and technical integration, and initial management of publisher base.
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Prospect, assess, onboard, and initiate technical integration of direct publishing partners</li>
<li>Help develop and implement efficient and effective procedures related to the discovery, implementation, and testing of new publishing partners</li>
<li>Negotiate contracts and pursue long term relationships with clients</li>
<li>As appropriate, represent AdGem at designated trade shows and conferences</li>
<li>Utilize Salesforce to maintain account records and manage prospecting efforts</li>
<li>Advocate on behalf of clients to internally escalate any concerns and reasonable requests</li>
<li>Translate client objectives into action items for the Ad Operations and Performance Media teams</li>
<li>Monitor daily metrics, program performance, and traffic quality to identify areas for growth and improvement</li>
</ul>
EXPERIENCE &amp; QUALIFICATIONS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years of experience in prospecting, managing, and closing a sales cycle</li>
<li>Basic understanding of SDK integration and mobile ad serving</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate with developers and engineers around the world</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Ability to work well independently, in team environment, and cross-departmentally</li>
<li>Motivated individual, with a positive attitude, who consistently strives to achieve goals in a manner exceeding expectations</li>
<li>Must be results-oriented and possess an entrepreneurial spirit that thrives in a demanding, fast-paced work environment</li>
<li>Ability to multitask, problem solve, and think critically</li>
<li>Willingness to travel to meet with clients and attend industry events (2-4 times per year)</li>
<li>Intermediate Excel skills (pivot tables, functions, etc.) are required… you will be tested!</li>
<li>BA/BS required</li>
</ul>
