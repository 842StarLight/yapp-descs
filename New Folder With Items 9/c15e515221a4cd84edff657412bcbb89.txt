<h2>Waiter or Waitress</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>SERVER / SERVERS</b></p>
<p>Your role is to exceed our guest’s expectations and is key to our restaurants success as you contribute to delivering a perfectly crafted experience to our guests every time!</p>
<p>Your commitment to us:</p>
<ul>
<li>Deliver genuine hospitality and an exceptional dining experience by guiding our guests through our menu, while demonstrating a thorough knowledge of all our food and beverage offerings.</li>
<li>Take pride in providing timely, attentive, fun and friendly service to ensure our guests feel valued and well cared for.</li>
<li>Create positive first impressions that build loyalty in return guests.</li>
<li>Be a team player with a “can-do” attitude to deliver 100% guest satisfaction.</li>
<li>Taking guests’ orders .</li>
<li>Running food.</li>
<li>Handling cash &amp; other forms of payment.</li>
<li>Making proficient sales transactions - using a point-of-sale system.</li>
<li>Properly checks I.D.s – for guests who appear to be under 30 years old.</li>
<li>Demonstrating responsible alcohol service.</li>
<li>Demonstrating impeccable product knowledge.</li>
<li>Suggestive selling - using proven techniques.</li>
<li>Answering questions - accurately and politely.</li>
<li>Satisfying any/all needs of the guests - regularly double checking back with guests throughout their experience.</li>
<li>Maintaining a clean &amp; organized station – including pre-bussing &amp; table maintenance.</li>
<li>Performing opening, closing &amp; side duties – setting up/breaking down station, refilling condiments, cleaning ashtrays, etc.</li>
<li>Demonstrating knowledge of all table/station numbers.</li>
<li>Directing guests to areas &amp; restrooms.</li>
<li>Creating memorable experiences.</li>
<li>Entertaining guests to create “Raving Fans. ”</li>
</ul>
<p>Our commitment to you:</p>
<ul><li>Part-time or full-time hours at a highly competitive rate of pay.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Flexible scheduling to accommodate school, life and family.</li>
<li>Advancement Opportunities.</li>
<li>Team Member discounts.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Must be 18 years of age or older to serve alcohol (based on state regulations).</li>
<li>Alcohol certification and Serve Safe a plus!</li>
<li>Previous experience as a server is preferred, but not required.</li>
<li>Flexible to work a variety of shifts.</li>
</ul>
<p>Desired Skill(s):</p>
<ul>
<li>Handling cash accurately</li>
<li>Processing credit cards</li>
<li>Using a calculator</li>
<li>Using Point of Sale software</li>
<li>Making change accurately</li>
</ul>
