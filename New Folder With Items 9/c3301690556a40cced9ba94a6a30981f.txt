<h2>Marketing Operations Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Southwestern Family of Companies is searching for an experienced Marketing Operations Coordinator.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><i>
*** THIS IS A HUGE ROLE FOR ONLY THE RIGHT PERSON WITH HUGE GROWTH POTENTIAL IN THE FUTURE. READ THE DESCRIPTION TO THE END ***</i></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
This position will support the business operations and manage the traditional and digital marketing strategy of Southwestern Empowerment.<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>Our Marketing Operations Coordinator is responsible for initiating, creating, developing, and executing an omni-channel marketing strategy as well as assisting with the daily business operations designed to ignite Southwestern Empowerment in consistently hitting revenue and profitability objectives. We’re looking for a marketing savvy team member, with a passion for implementation, who has a high resilience to a fast-paced environment, and can perform in different roles and complete various tasks depending on the needs of Southwestern Empowerment during times of growth. The right candidate will be able to demonstrate success at attracting, engaging, and converting new website traffic into customers, converting existing customers into repeat clients, and genuinely engaging with our growing fan base.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key areas of action and responsibility include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Maintaining a world-class digital online presence with websites, social media, and customer experience in order to maximize overall revenue per visitor.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Devising organic and paid strategies to drive online traffic to SouthwesternEmpowerment.com and KatyKvalvik.com.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Developing and managing digital marketing campaigns and promotional calendars surrounding Empowerment personalities, offerings, events, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Overseeing the social media strategy for Southwestern Empowerment and its founder, Katy Kvalvik.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Utilizing a range of techniques including, but not limited to, email marketing, SEO, paid search, and affiliate marketing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Managing relationships with agencies and digital marketing technology vendors.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Developing, evaluating, and overseeing the implementation of A/B testing protocols and procedures for newsletters, opt-in forms, and online promotions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Creating and managing systems within our Customer Relationship Management (CRM) tool, Infusionsoft. Training new employees and contractors on how to use the system.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Working with Project Manager to coordinate and execute workshop and event logistics.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Contributing to pre and post-event correspondence with workshop and event attendees including, but not limited to, promotional and logistics emails, customer service, referral follow up, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Conducting post-project and event reviews and developing lessons-learned documentation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Completing a broad variety of administrative tasks for Southwestern Empowerment including, but not limited to, managing and completing expense reports, composing and preparing email and phone correspondence, arranging detailed travel plans, itineraries, and agendas.</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
