<h2>Tax Manager – Core Tax Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Summary
<br>The Tax Manager is responsible for advising clients on the tax implications of their business
<br>objections, evaluating and selecting alternative actions to lessen tax burden and cost of
<br>compliance, identifying different methods of complying with tax regulations while acting as the
<br>primary client contact for complex tax issues. In this role, Tax Manager is charged with applying
<br>knowledge to understand potential tax issues, and recognizing and communicating potential risks
<br>and potential changes in the tax policy.
<br><br>
