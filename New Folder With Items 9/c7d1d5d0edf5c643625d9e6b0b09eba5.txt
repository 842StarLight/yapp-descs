<p><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<p>In the role of Senior Developer, you will interface with key stakeholders and apply your technical proficiency across different stages of the software development life cycle including requirements elicitation, application architecture definition and design. You will play an important role in creating the high-level design artifacts. You will also deliver high quality code deliverables and lead code validation. You will be part of a learning culture, where teamwork and collaboration are encouraged, excellence is rewarded, and diversity is respected and valued. Lead developer will be responsible for legacy .net applications—light evolution and support, but dominant focus is on transitioning to Salesforce as an enterprise platform.</p>

<li>Design database schemas, conceptual data models, UI workflows and application architectures that fit enterprise architecture.</li>

<li>Understand User-Centered Design concept to build user interfaces that are modern and provide good user experiences</li>
<li>Gather information and feedback from end users to understand and develop project requirements.</li>
<li>Ensure project tasks are assigned and completed in a timely manner and project milestone dates are met.</li>
