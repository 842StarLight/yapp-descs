<b>Inside Sales Account Manager</b><br>
<br>
<b>Consistently adding value to customers… and your career!</b><br>
<br>
If you are looking to jump start your career in the exciting world of technology, there’s simply no better place to be than with Softchoice. As an Inside Sales Account Manager (ISAM), you take advantage of our award-winning partnerships with the most innovative companies. You will continually deliver exceptional results and grow our business (and your career) to new heights. Here’s how you’ll do it:<br>
<br>
<b>Sustain and diversify our business with legendary service</b><br>
<br>
Working as a duo with a field sales representative, you play a crucial role in delivering exceptional customer satisfaction and uncovering leads. While your partner hits the street, you maintain proactive relationships with key stakeholders, staying on top of everything from urgent needs to major transformational projects. You do so by consistently engaging and educating customers on the phone and by email, and by helping them use our online portal. Above delivering legendary customer service and smashing sales quotas, your mission is to sustain, and then diversify, a customer’s investment with Softchoice.<br>
<br>
<b>Challenging customers to do more</b><br>
<br>
Our most successful ISAM’s know there’s value in challenging customers to break out of their comfort zone. You will proactively learn about your customers’ objectives and challenges, developing a deep understanding of their business and spotting opportunities. With those insights, you lead new conversations and tailor compelling messaging to deliver continual added value.<br>
<br>
<b>A passion for learning?</b><br>
<br>
Our industry is constantly evolving. As our newest ISAM, so are you! You jump at the opportunity to learn about new technologies, business transformation trends and Softchoice offerings. You network with peers as well as major technology providers and customers, connecting the dots to overcome customer visions and pains with cutting-edge technologies.<br>
<br>
<b>As our ideal candidate, you are:</b><ul>
<li>
Able to support your customer’s needs, while consistently delivering added-value and pursuing new business</li>
<li>
Analytical and strategic, finding unique approaches to hitting your plan</li>
<li>
Intent on learning about the ever-changing world of enterprise technology</li>
<li>
Full of confidence and business acumen, with exceptional written, verbal and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Not afraid to break ground on new relationships and skillfully overcome challenges with customers and team members</li>
<li>
An effective, metric-driven sales person with a proven track record of success</li>
<li>
You will also have 1 to 3 years of sales or retail experience and a post-secondary education (University preferred).</li>
</ul>
<b>
How Do We Hire Great People?</b><br>
<br>
We believe in matching the right people for the right role. Once you’ve applied for a position, our team of Talent Advisors will review your resume and application against all open roles at Softchoice. We also keep in mind any future or upcoming roles you may be a fit for. As part of our selection, onboarding and development process, we utilize tools to help us better match candidates with our roles.<br>
<br>
Applying for a role at Softchoice is not complete until you have completed both the formal online application and the Predictive Index tool (PI). Completing the PI tool will takes no more than 10 minutes of your time.<br>
<br>
If we see a match with your skills and our open roles, we will reach out to you to schedule time to talk so that we can learn more about you and you can learn about us. Only those candidates selected for further consideration will be contacted directly. However, we will retain your application on file and would be pleased to contact you if further opportunities arise.<br>
