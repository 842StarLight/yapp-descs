<b>
Description</b><div>
</div>
<p>Intertek, a leading provider of ATIC<b> </b>(Assurance, Testing, Inspection and Certification) Services, is looking for an Inside Sales Rep to join our Connected World team in Lexington KY.<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>The Connected World team focuses on cyber security, software testing, and assurance services for network-enabled devices, such as computers, tablets, mobile devices, apps, software and wireless and connected products. The team provides services to help bring cutting-edge technology to market quickly with assurance of safety/security, quality, and performance.</p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p>The Inside Sales Rep will be responsible for direct sales activity on designated accounts with Account Managers in addition to handling inbound queries. We are looking for someone with previous inside sales experience and ability to work independently in a fast-paced, multi-tasking environment. The Inside Sales Rep will spend no more than 20% of the time outside of the office making sales calls, traveling to clients, etc.<br>
</p>
<div>
</div>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with Account Managers to support designated existing accounts</li>
<li>
Qualify leads and refer them to appropriate Account Manager
</li>
<li>Function as backup for Account Managers</li>
<li>
Perform client research, prospecting, and networking</li>
<li>
Attend and participate in trade shows</li>
<li>
Create and facilitate client presentations
</li>
<li>Maintain quote/order levels</li>
<li>
Prepare sales reports/forecasts , write and follow-up on proposals</li>
<li>
Maintain sales database</li>
<li>
Process sales transactions, specify appropriate standards, price, and credit terms</li>
</ul>
