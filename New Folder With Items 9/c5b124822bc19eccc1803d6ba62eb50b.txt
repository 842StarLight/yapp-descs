The objective of this role is to facilitate seamless business practices between MacLean Power Systems and internal and external customers. By providing thought leadership, process and technology recommendations, report development, implementation and on-going enhancements to processes, procedures, system changes. This candidate will be responsible for a variety of complicated tasks, communicate with all levels of the organization, and rely on experience and judgment to accomplish goals. Candidates will have deep analytical, technical and organizational skills with the ability to manipulate marketing data, develop forecasting and demand planning models, sales plans, customer budget updates and other various forecast data. Candidate will be adept at assembling data in support of business recommendations.
