<h2>Analytics Consultant 5 - Digital Customer Experience and Capabilities</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>
<p>
Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p>
The mission of <b>Marketing</b> is to build the best possible brand, experiences, and engagement with our customers and stakeholders that reflect our commitment to our customers and their needs and to driving key business and stakeholder outcomes. In support of this mission, Marketing is responsible for developing integrated marketing strategies across Wells Fargo’s business lines and geographies; leading brand strategy; leveraging deep insights and analytics about consumers and businesses; executing customer messaging across all channels and touchpoints; and building out capabilities that move toward real-time, always-on, personalized and compelling messaging and digital experiences.</p>
<p>
I&amp;A is seeking an Analytics Consultant 5 to join the Digital Customer Experience and Capabilities team within Insights &amp; Analytics. The ideal candidate should have a strong technical background in Teradata SQL scripting and Tableau visualization with an in-depth understanding of industry standard web analytics KPIs and digital sales funnel. This candidate should be able to get into the data to execute on ad-hoc insights and analytics requests from our business partners and produce and present the results to them. The candidate should also have hands-on experience deploying, integrating, and/or pulling data and insights from a modern-day enterprise capabilities stack (Google, Oracle, IBM, or Adobe). Lastly, the ideal candidate should have current experience performing analysis with a customer-focused and omnichannel look the customer journey. Ideal candidates currently sit in a Customer Journey and/or Capabilities Team and have a propensity to analyze at the level of Personalization and Customers.</p>
<p>
Specific job functions include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with our digital marketing consultant and managers, business partners, technology partners, and Offshore team to ensure our monthly updated Tableau Dashboard meets their needs and address any ad-hoc analysis and questions they might have</li>
<li>Coordinate training sessions with our partners to educate them of the contents and capabilities of the Tableau Dashboard and what our web analytics team can offer them on an ad-hoc basis</li>
<li>Be the subject matter expert on web analytics and take business ownership of the Tableau Dashboard</li>
<li>Engage and participate in the discussions of potential integration of new data sources and technology platforms into our Teradata Aster web analytics platform.</li>
<li>Identify and track project issues and risks, and communicate effectively with business, operations, and technology teams. Participate in end-to-end project management for key strategic and projects.</li>
<li>Identify, research, and resolve data issues discover by our partners and/or us</li>
</ul>
Within the MSCI and DCE support team, the primary engagement model involves:<br>
<ul>
<li>Web Analytics: the team is the enterprise center of excellence for web analytics and it is charged with business ownership of Aster as well as development of a long-term road-map for new web analytics reporting and capabilities (e.g. API integration, site performance dashboard, etc.)</li>
<li>Technical Consultation for Enterprise Capabilities: in addition to web analytics, the team partners with EDA and MM on platforms (MAT/C2T/etc.) and analytical integration of new technologies (Doubleclick tag deployment, DMP, etc.)</li>
<li>MSCI Leadership: in partnership with Research, Measurement, and Marketing the team leads the I&amp;A socialization for integrated support of MSCI projects</li>
</ul>
