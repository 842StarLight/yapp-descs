<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BSEE or equivalent level of experience and/or training; MSEE preferred</li>
<li>3-8 years of related experience or internships required</li>
<li>Applicants are expected to have a strong educational or practical background in fundamental RF concepts applicable to RF filter and switch components. Phone level system knowledge preferred</li>
<li>Impedance matching and clear understanding of the Smith Chart</li>
<li>RF testing skills with strong background with use of network analyzers, signal analyzers, signal generators, oscilloscopes, and lab equipment</li>
<li>PCB level debug/troubleshooting. Handset level preferred</li>
<li>Working knowledge of wireless standards including: GSM, W-CDMA, LTE, WiFi 802.11, Bluetooth, and GPS</li>
<li>Experience with linear simulations using EDA tools such as KeySight ADS and/or NI AWR</li>
<li>Excellent technical communication skills</li>
</ul>
