<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>THE JOB<br>

<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>
The Digital Media Coordinator/email will facilitate email marketing campaigns for THE ROW. This person will create creative assets, copy write and ensure processes are in place for all email campaigns. The ideal candidate will collaborate with various departments across THE ROW and with ERI Corporate Email Division to promote messaging, while ensuring that all content aligns with our brand strategies.</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<b>
E</b><b>SSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
This position is responsible for developing and ensuring that the content, graphics and marketing messages found on all company websites and mobile platorms, are accurate, up-to-date and represent the brand in the best possible manner.</li>
<li>
Maintains regular social media presence and content postings.</li>
<li>
Ensures website / mobile content is up to date.</li>
<li>
Takes direction from both property and corporate Digital Marketing department to reach desired goals in online sales and audience.</li>
<li>
Assists with special promotions operations and accommodates creative asset needs.</li>
<li>
Initiates new social media and online advertising “paid” placements with corporate department.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
