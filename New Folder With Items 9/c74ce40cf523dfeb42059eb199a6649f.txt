<p>Wanted: Fire door inspectors and repair men</p>
<p>We are America's premier provider of healthcare life safety services. You will learn job-specific skills from the best in the business with a competitive salary and benefits.</p>
<p>Are you an expert in installing/repairing swinging hinged fire doors? Are you the guy everyone calls when they need help? Broaden your skill set by joining our team of highly skilled fire door professionals at PREVENT Life Safety. Work with great people and inspect, install and repair fire-rated doors to keep our customers in compliance with fire safety codes.</p>
<p>Primary Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Inspecting fire-rated doors and charting locations/conditions (training provided)</li>
<li>Installing/repairing fire-rated doors and door closing/sealing mechanisms (hinged doors)</li>
<li>Interacting and communicating with our sales reps to convey progress made/additional work needed</li>
<li>Maintaining a professional demeanor while interacting and communicating with clients from healthcare facilities</li>
<li>Providing accurate documentation in a timely manner that will be included in final reports to ensure that the facilities are in compliance</li>
</ul>
