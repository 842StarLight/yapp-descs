<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>We’re currently looking for a Account Manager with 3 years of digital marketing experience to work in our Tempe office.</b> This is a strategic role in account management that will primarily involve building client relationships through regular, proactive and value-added communication, driving account strategy, campaign execution, and finally, team leadership and development. <b>This is not a sales role.</b></p>
<p>
Candidates should be familiar with all aspects of online marketing and be able to show a track record of successful outcomes with account management, performance and leadership.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Develop best-in-class digital marketing solutions for clients based on their goals and objectives</li>
<li>Account and campaign strategy development, ongoing results analysis, optimization and strategy refinement</li>
<li>Driving and delivering flawless client service by maintaining exceptional relationships with key decisions makers</li>
<li>Account direction and management including setting client expectations, project plans, budgeting and forecasting, and overseeing quality and accuracy of deliverables</li>
<li>Manage a team of interactive marketing professionals, create development plans and track training progress and opportunities for growth</li>
<li>Prepare for and attend all client and internal meetings, present reports, and daily communication with client</li>
<li>Research and pursue opportunities for client growth</li>
<li>Creation of proposals, monthly reports and work orders</li>
<li>Assistance with research and presentations for new business development as necessary</li>
<li>Embrace thought-leadership by staying up-to-date on industry news, technology trends and best practices</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of digital marketing experience (working with cross-functional teams is a plus)</li>
<li>A solid understanding of the dynamic interactive marketing industry (marketing principles, web development, measurement and analytics, internet technology, UI/UX, content strategy, etc.) and the ability to “connect-the-dots” across channels and touchpoints</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>An exceptional work ethic and the willingness to go above and beyond to get the job done while maintaining a supportive, empowering and positive attitude.</li>
<li>Takes initiative to innovate and solve problems without direct supervision and/or direction</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Strong analytical skills and business acumen are a must along with the ability to be detail- and deadline-oriented while managing multiple projects</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Integrate and analyze data from all forms of online media including paid search, organic search, display, email, social media, etc. to make decisions, optimize accounts and accomplish clients’ high-level goals</li>
<li>Direct experience with PPC, SEO, social media advertising, and email campaign strategy and execution.</li>
<li>A proven track record of account management experience and/or team/department leadership experience with experience managing a team</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Solid understanding of UI/UX including, but not limited to best practices, tools, conversion rate optimization, A/B and multivariate testing, etc.</li>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Google Analytics, Google AdWords, Microsoft Office product suite</li>
</ul>
