<p>Contribute to our success by:</p>
<div>
<div>The Vice President Product Management is responsible for the success of the products. This role is responsible for more than one product or more than one core functionality on which the product is dependent on. The Vice President Product Management interacts/ leads multiple product teams. This role works with new partnerships to develop new products and/or new integration capabilities. The Vice President Product Management is responsible for all aspects of the product including integration with other third party tools, both internal and external. This role has responsibility to identify, design and deliver on the capabilities needed by clients to successfully support their business in the Laboratory market, which may extend to custom development and integration of outside partners. This is supervisory position and requires management, technical and business expertise in a complex environment. The Vice President Product Management is ultimately responsible for the success or failure of the product in the market.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
Key Areas of Responsibility include (but not limited to)</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Manages multiple product lifecycles from innovation through delivery.</li>
<li>
Defines the long term strategy and vision for the core as well as ancillary product components; Rule engines, quality measures, core modules, integration of other CGM assets.</li>
<li>
Develop and execute on product enhancements, product roadmap, and product strategies to achieve the product objectives.</li>
<li>
Develop and lead the refinement of the product development process across multiple products that may have several subcomponents or integration points with other third party software or data sources.</li>
<li>
Identify and act on priorities across multiple products.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and lead customer research and oversee business analysts to gather functional requirements through use of internal subject matter expertise, interviews with current customers, prospects and other industry contacts, as well as research based on internet exploration and conference attendance.</li>
<li>
Remove roadblocks in working with other business units to accomplish deliverables.</li>
<li>
Understand the interoperability of the data processing and technical aspects of the primary product as well as the interoperability of other products integrating with the core product.</li>
<li>
Research, define and independently present a business case for new products, sunset of older products and/or new releases of existing products.</li>
<li>
Lead the completion of a cost benefit analysis to determine if large new modules will be profitable</li>
<li>
Lead creation and documentation of a Value of Investment or Return on Investment for Marketing and Sales use.</li>
<li>
Drive new third party relationships required to provide functionality needed in the product.</li>
<li>
Set the strategic direction and vision for the product.</li>
<li>
Support sales by being the product expert for an RFP perspective.</li>
<li>
Work with Marketing to develop and deliver sales and implementation training tools related to the product.</li>
<li>
Work closely with the operations, consulting and client Services teams to communicate product and data needs that will support successful implementation of the product.</li>
<li>
Work with Product teams to assist with designing training programs, UAT plans, and product marketing as they develop and execute their plans for the product.</li>
<li>
Work with Marketing to create and deliver product webinars and lead Product Advisory Council.</li>
</ul>
