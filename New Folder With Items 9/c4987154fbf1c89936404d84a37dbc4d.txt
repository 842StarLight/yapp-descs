<h2>Enterprise Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Description
<br><br>
This is an opportunity to join our Sales Team in a Corporate Sales Representative role and continue Appcast’s proven record of success. This individual will be responsible for selling Appcast media solutions (Saas) to direct employers. If you thrive in a fast-paced, hard-working environment and are comfortable demonstrating solutions to employers, then this is the opportunity for you.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Prospect, pitch and sell Appcast media solutions to direct employers</li>
<li>Qualify inbound leads</li>
<li>Develop and close sales opportunities</li>
<li>Use both traditional and electronic communication tools to contact prospective customers</li>
<li>Achieve sales targets on a monthly and quarterly basis</li>
<li>Accurately log all sales activity in a CRM (Salesforce.com)</li>
<li>Use natural curiosity, communication skills, and drive to improve your sales performance</li>
</ul>
Candidate Traits
<br><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>‘Hunter mentality’ for closing sales</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial attitude with a desire to learn and continuously improve</li>
<li>Detail oriented with a strong desire to get things done accurately, efficiently and timely</li>
<li>Ability to conduct online product demonstrations</li>
<li>Data-driven and solution oriented</li>
<li>Able to work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment</li>
<li>Self-motivated, driven and has a ‘go-getter’ attitude</li>
<li>Previous experience working with Salesforce or similar CRM tools</li>
</ul>
