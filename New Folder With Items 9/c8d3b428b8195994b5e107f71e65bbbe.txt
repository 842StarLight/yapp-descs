Job Overview
<br><br>
The Graphic Designer is responsible for creating visuals for print and online experiences. Projects will include designing graphics for landing pages, presentations, eBooks, brochures, social media, infographics, and website elements. Additionally, this role will create templates for email and other channels, to help our company be more efficient and brand consistent across all our efforts. The graphic designer will be responsible for ensuring that the AvidXchange brand is consistent in look and feel across different mediums utilizing our brand standards.
<br><br>
The graphic designer will collaborate with marketing teammates, as well as cross-functional teams, to support our website design and content marketing (blog, social media assets, and more). We’re looking for someone to bring new ideas for design and content creation to the team using your expertise and eye for great design.
<br><br>
Job Responsibilities
<br><br>
<b>Job Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Design print and digital assets for Marketing, Sales, Events, and other cross-functional departments</li>
<li>Ensure that internal and external assets fit within branding guidelines</li>
<li>Ensure projects completed ahead of timeline, provide status updates</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with all internal and external partners</li>
<li>Work as part of the team to maintain and update communications materials</li>
<li>Manage multiple projects simultaneously under tight deadlines</li>
<li>Maintain brand standards and guidelines</li>
<li>Develop a knowledge of AvidXchange’s offerings</li>
</ul>
