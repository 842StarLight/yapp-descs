<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>247 Sands, LLC</b>, a global sand trading company, is looking for highly motivated individuals to join our logistics team in our Houston (IAH) location. The ideal candidate must be able to multitask in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Interface and provide support to sales and management team.</li>
<li>Process and distribute purchase orders electronically on a daily basis. Transmits and prioritizes approved purchase orders and supporting documents.</li>
<li>Tracks order acknowledgement, prepares and communicates shortage and backlog reports, and provides visibility of potential interruptions.</li>
<li>Extensive communication with associates, customers and other third parties to ensure a high level of customer service.</li>
<li>Reviews, updates, and maintains open orders until they are closed. Resolves any discrepancies on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Coordination and tracking of shipments.</li>
<li>Enter all pertinent information into the proper management software systems and/or spreadsheets.</li>
<li>Complete and maintain daily operations reports.</li>
<li>Additional duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge &amp; Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of customer service experience via phone and email required</li>
<li>Petroleum or logistics experience preferred</li>
<li>Dispatching experience preferred</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of Microsoft Outlook/Advanced knowledge of Excel</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Characteristics</b></p>
