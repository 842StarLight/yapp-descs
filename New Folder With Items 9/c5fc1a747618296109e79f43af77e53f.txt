<h2>Director Of Implementation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Director of Implementation is responsible for the full life cycle implementation of Patra products and services across the enterprise. You are responsible for the effective integration and management of staff, services and solutions to achieve timely and effective implementations. Collaboration with sales and operational teams is key as you are responsible for the onboarding process for new and existing customers. Customer satisfaction and planned, cost effective implementations will be key measurements. The Director of Implementation’s ultimate goal is managing customer expectations, addressing their requirements and providing best practice recommendations.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements - Education and Experience</b></p>
<p>5+ years’ experience in project management, business process engineering, and leadership of cross-functional teams</p>
<p>Solid reporting/business intelligence experience</p>
<p>Insurance agency/broker insurance experience preferred</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, Abilities</b></p>
<p>Strong communications and interpersonal skills demonstrated ability to quickly build trust and relationships, ability to bridge communication gaps between sales and operations teams, resolve conflicts, and influence stakeholders at all levels</p>
<p>Strong technical aptitude and ability to quickly learn new products, lines of business, processes, software, systems, and client needs</p>
<p>Excellent organizational skills and ability to quickly break down large, complex initiatives into logical, manageable work packages, tasks, and sub-tasks for execution</p>
<p>Critical-thinking, business analysis, and process engineering skills to manage, contribute to, and foresee pitfalls in process-related deliverables</p>
<p>Ability to collaborate, negotiate, influence and build consensus across the organization</p>
<p>Ability to write workflows &amp; maintain documentation</p>
<p>Ability to communicate effectively and professionally</p>
<p>Strong sense of urgency, initiative, responsiveness, &amp; attention to detail</p>
<p>Maintain highest level of confidentiality</p>
<p>Exhibits a high level of positivity, energy, &amp; teamwork orientation</p>
<p>Proficient in using technology as a tool to maximize productivity/quality</p>
<p>Effective interpersonal skills, solid analytical, &amp; problem-solving skills</p>
<p>Stay abreast of general industry knowledge and trends</p>
<p>Ability to work independently and in teams</p>
<p>Positive attitude</p>
