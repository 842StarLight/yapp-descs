<h2>Leasing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Strategic Management Partners - SMP</b></p>
<p>A National Property Management Company currently has an Experienced Leasing Specialist position available at an apartment community in Columbia, SC.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate must have two years prior multi family residential experience with a primary marketing and/or sales responsibilities as they pertain to interfacing with prospective residents at the community, handling day-to-day issues with current residents, and coordinating the renewals of existing leases.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide excellent service to all residents, prospects and visitors to the community</li>
<li>Effectively lease apartments in accordance with budget guidelines.</li>
<li>Follow Fair Housing Standards in all matters when working with prospects and residents.</li>
<li>Conduct outside marketing activities in order to position as community of choice.</li>
<li>Coordinate the application review for each lease including credit and income verification.</li>
<li>Complete lease packets and files for each resident to include the lease, all addendum's and other proper documentation.</li>
<li>Ensure the condition of leased apartments prior to move-in, including a final inspection and walk through with resident.</li>
<li>Follow up with residents throughout their lease terms and coordinate the renewal process for each lease.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of MS Office Suite and Yardi</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Blue Moon Software.</li>
</ul>
