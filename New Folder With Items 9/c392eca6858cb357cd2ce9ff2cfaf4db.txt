<h2>Pre-Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview</b></h3>
<p>This position of Pre-Sales Engineer is a strategic technical resource for the scoutbee sales force. The Pre-Sales Engineer is responsible for actively driving and managing the technology evaluation stage of the sales process, serving as a solutions consultant on behalf of the customer and in conjunction with the sales team.</p>
<p>Pre-Sales Engineers identify technical issues assuring complete customer satisfaction through all stages of the sales process, establishing and maintaining strong relationships throughout the sales cycle. You will be an essential part of the sales organization at scoutbee, and will be a key player in the development of the pre-sales organization.</p>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
</h3>
<ul>
<li>Lead strategic and technical sales presentations &amp; demonstrations (online and face-to-face), in conjunction with the Sales/Account Executive.</li>
<li>Be available and engaged throughout the sales cycle to provide ongoing technical insight and product information as required to close deals.</li>
<li>Assist sales staff in assessing the potential application of company products to meet customer needs including participation in “discovery” meetings, RFP responses, and related qualification activities.</li>
<li>Develop, promote, and maintain product-related tools designed for repeat use by internal and external sales people.</li>
<li>Maintain ongoing technical product knowledge of all products - keep up-to-date on company literature, competition and market trends via attendance/participation inhouse training, travel &amp; contact w/relevant in-house and external engineering, management, industry specialists, trade journals etc.</li>
<li>Assist with product training of new sales personnel.</li>
<li>Ability to quickly learn technologies (experience with large-scale databases and cloud-based architectures is a plus).</li>
<li>Remain available and in contact with both internal Sales/Account Executives and external partners post contract for ongoing sales support and ensure a smooth customer handoff from the sales process to our Implementation team.</li>
<li>Liaise with our Product Management and Engineering teams to communicate client needs for the development of future product and platform enhancements.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors' Degree in Engineering, Information Technology, Computer Science, Software Development, or Business is required.</li>
<li>Industry experience with Procurement, S2P, and P2P technologies is strongly preferred.</li>
<li>1 - 3+ years’ experience in a technical pre-sale or customer-facing role.</li>
<li>A well-rounded, business savvy, professional who understands the customer relationship management market as well as Cloud- and SaaS-based software architecture.</li>
<li>Ability to deliver polished technical presentations and demonstrate how product provides business value to client stakeholders.</li>
<li>Must be organized and analytical and be able to eliminate sales obstacles through creative and adaptive approaches.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Understanding of subscription-based software sales processes and procedures.</li>
<li>Understanding of referral &amp; reseller partner contract processes.</li>
<li>Ability to manage many complex projects and prospect relationships at one time.</li>
<br>
</ul>
