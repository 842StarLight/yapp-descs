<p><b>We are seeking an experienced Director, Corporate Communications </b>. In this role, you will be working out of our Global HQ in Lake Mary, Florida. Reporting to the Vice President of Marketing, you will play a key role in how customers, employees, investors and the general public perceive our company. In this position, you are ultimately responsible for brand and reputation management. The Director, Corporate Communications is responsible for developing and implementing communications plans to effectively position FARO, its executives and offerings with current and prospective employees, customers, investors, opinion leaders, and the media. This position regularly provides strategic counsel on communications matters to business and functional leaders throughout the enterprise and collaborates closely with other stakeholders to ensure alignment of messaging, strategy and execution with employees and in the market.
</p>

<p><b>How you will make a difference @ FARO:
