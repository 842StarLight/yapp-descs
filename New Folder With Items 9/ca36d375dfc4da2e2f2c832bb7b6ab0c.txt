<p><b>PROCUREMENT AND SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGER</b></p>
<p>
AAK Foodservice, a division of AAK USA, a Swedish based, world leading producer of high value-added specialty vegetable oils and fats, is a National Foodservice Manufacturer and supplier recognized as the leader in Customer Service and Innovative Solutions. Our purpose is to improve the quality of life of our customers and our employees. We make it our mission to ensure the safety of all employees, develop a highly engaged workforce, and enable our customers to meet their goals while operating in a sustainable and ethical fashion.</p>
<p>
We make safe, enjoyable food for customers and consumers while adhering to our team values. At AAK Foodservice we encourage and challenge each other, by speaking honestly and openly and being committed and accountable to one another. We have high expectations of ourselves and rely on one another to produce an enhance trust within our team.</p>
<p>
We are currently seeking a <b>Procurement and Supply Chain Manager</b> for our Food Services Division manufacturing facility (approximately 180 employees) located in Hillside, NJ. This position is responsible managing the overall direction, coordination, and evaluation of procurement for the AAK Foodservice business, requiring the person in this role to develop not only key relationships cross functionally within the organizational structure, but also with vendors and strategic supply partners to improve the business. Work required independent and professional judgement. <b>This role will be a procurement expert foremost and Supply Chain as secondary.
</b></p>
<p><b>Accountable for:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Implementing procurement strategies to maintain security of supply and optimum value for money, through contract negotiation, pre-qualification contract activities, and tender management.</li>
<li>
Reviewing existing procedures and examining opportunities to streamline production, purchasing, product configuration, warehousing, distribution, and forecasting to meet production and distribution needs</li>
<li>
Direct activities to limit costs, improve accuracy, safety, and service to the customer</li>
<li>
Make decisions regarding the movement, storage, and processing of inventory with focus on the most efficient levels for full supply chain including plant, warehouse, and toll manufacturers</li>
<li>
Demonstrates strong leadership and managerial competencies, including integrity, accountability, communication skills, commitment to teamwork and excellence in responsibilities such as selecting, mentoring, coaching, supervising, motivating, and training procurement, supply chain, and purchasing staff.</li>
<li>
Coaches and mentors his/her Supply Chain teams to improve engagement with local technical/commercial stakeholders and to establish communication channels between the cross functional teams.</li>
<li>
Planning and implementing the overall supply chain strategy by collaborating closely with sales, operations, and customer service teams</li>
<li>
Assist logistics for the development of inventory maintenance, manufacturing, shipment and delivery, and returns on products</li>
<li>
Lead, develop and improve the S&amp;OP processes and policies, including Demand Planning/Management, using an interactive approach and methodology to align financial, operation and material plans.</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintaining scorecard of local suppliers in key categories for quality, delivery and costs and is a recognized subject matter expert (SME) providing training and leadership.</li>
<li>
Perform benchmark studies regarding alternative, more cost efficient supply chain scenarios</li>
<li>
Manages the preparation of solicitation documents and offers</li>
<li>
Conducts the evaluations of proposals, offers, and bids in accordance with internal policy and external local, state, and federal regulations while selecting the best supplier in accordance with solicitation requirements</li>
<li>
Lead discussions with external logistics providers, toll manufacturers and customer logistics departments.</li>
<li>
Assures suppliers deliver materials and services in accordance with standard of price, time, quantity, and quality</li>
<li>
Understand and support IT systems that support supply chain - recommend and support changes to enhance the performance of the group and policies to achieve objectives, ie inventory and service lead times.</li>
<li>
Continuously monitor and strive for zero out-of-stock positions by balancing supply with demand, setting and monitoring inventory policy by SKU, proactively identifying constraints and challenges and developing solutions to mitigate impacts all the while keeping internal customers clearly informed of developments.</li>
<li>
Support and communicate clearly and timely throughout the cycle of product development to product delivery to monthly replenishment, to ensure alignment with Sales and Operations.</li>
<li>
Responsible for providing the information for item set up and maintenance, to ensure data accuracy and proper operation of information systems.</li>
<li>
Ensure development and accountability to master schedule &amp; production plan</li>
<li>
Manage and monitor international shipments to the company to meet supply plans and company financial objectives.</li>
<li>
Conducts evaluations of staff for review and approval, takes corrective action as needed</li>
<li>
Remain current with the latest improvements and technologies employed within the planning and materials management profession and champion their incorporation into AAK practices.</li>
<li>
Estimate and evaluate a score card including OTIF and etc. by channel, customer and product group.</li>
<li>
Be a role model and demonstrate the company’s core values of respect, honesty, integrity, diversity, inclusion, and safety of others</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Procurement, Logistics, statistics, Business Management, Supply Chain Management, or related field</li>
<li>
5+ years experience in procurement, supply chain, and planning in a CPG environment</li>
<li>
Prefer experience with any forecasting application software – SAP, APO, JDA, I2, Manugistics, or Logility</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of all Microsoft Office applications</li>
<li>
Understanding of accounting or finance</li>
<li>
Must be able to comprehend legal documents</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical, statistical, communication, presentation and system skills with thorough attention to detail</li>
<li>
Must possess a Can-Do attitude</li>
<li>
The ability to analyze and process data and find creative solutions to identified issues</li>
<li>
Candidates must be authorized to work in the US, now and in the future, without sponsorship.</li>
</ul>
