<h2>Shipping and Receiving Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>Performs all computer functions necessary to track various aspects of goods.</li>
<li>Supports the shipment of goods produced and the received goods and supplies to be used in or to aid in the production/storage of goods.</li>
<li>Responsible for making pickup arrangements by the carrier specified on the customer’s purchase order, which is conveyed to the shipping clerk by final inspection via the shipping instructions.</li>
<li>Operates a computer system associated with in-motion equipment which is used to scan and weigh packages.</li>
<li>Sorts and moves packages that require special handling.</li>
<li>Maintains daily shipping records to verify the accuracy of incoming and outgoing shipments and orders.</li>
<li>Maintains daily driver check-in logs and truck sanitation logs.</li>
<li>Unpacks, examines and routes incoming shipments, rejects damaged items, records shortages, and corresponds with shipper to rectify damages and shortages.</li>
<li>Receives and processes all defective parts returned for replacement or credit.</li>
<li>May assemble orders and prepares goods for shipment.</li>
<li>Verify finished goods that are being shipped in-line with the correct sales order.</li>
<li>Coordinates with other departments to insure “ON TIME DELIVERY” to customers.</li>
<li>May Receive, unpack, inspect and store incoming materials and supplies.</li>
<li>May operate the stretch wrap banders.</li>
<li>Operate fork lift trucks or hand trucks to convey, move or hoist materials to proper departments or areas.</li>
<li>Maintains inventory of shipping materials and supplies.</li>
<li>Loads containers/trucks as appropriate.</li>
<li>Personal responsibility for following safety rules and all relevant SOPs. Adheres to GMPs</li>
<li>Reports defective materials or questionable conditions to the department supervisor.</li>
<li>Responsible for keeping an adequate inventory and supply of shipping materials on hand at all times.</li>
<li>Maintains the work area and equipment in a clean and orderly condition and follows prescribed safety regulations.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>

<p>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS: The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job.<br>Physical ability to repetitively lift, stand, climb, bend, use hand dolly for transporting packages.
