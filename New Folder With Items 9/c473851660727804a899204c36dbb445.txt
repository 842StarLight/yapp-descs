<b>Business Unit General Manager – Automotive Software</b><br>
<br>
DealerSocket, a leader in automotive software, is seeking a Business Unit General Manager to provide strategic leadership and have P&amp;L responsibility for a key business unit. The GM will be responsible for<br>
creating and executing the business unit’s go-to-market strategy, product strategy and prioritization of the roadmap, and cultivating customer (automotive dealer and OEM) relationships to achieve best-in-class customer experience.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li> Develop, maintain and administer strong business and organizational plan for the business unit which includes an operating budget and product strategy / roadmap prioritization plans</li>
<li> Responsible for business unit P&amp;L, achieving goals for bookings, revenue, and operating expenses within budget targets</li>
<li> Partner across functions and matrix-manage to achieve business unit’s priorities, key objectives, and results</li>
<li> Serve as a member of DealerSocket’s leadership team</li>
<li> Drive a lively and resilient entrepreneurial culture adept at navigating change</li>
<li> Track performance and provide accurate and timely forecasts / reporting on business unit metrics</li>
<li> Establish a strong performance management culture</li>
<li> Provide leadership through innovation, collaborating with sales, marketing, and technology leadership to identify key market opportunities and growth areas within the business unit</li>
<li> Acquire and integrate industry knowledge related to general trends, emerging technologies and competitors</li>
<li> Represent DealerSocket at industry events and customer meetings, support the sales and marketing teams and the customer success team in developing executive-level relationships</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Skills</b><br>
<ul>
<li> 15+ years of experience in sales, general management, product management, marketing for automotive software</li>
<li> Bachelor’s degree required; MBA preferred</li>
<li> Ability to operate and make timely decisions in an ambiguous, fast-paced, innovative environment</li>
<li> Proven track record of consistently achieving sales targets on a monthly, quarterly and annual basis</li>
<li> Expert ability to engage and generate interest from c-level executives and drive initiatives forward</li>
<li> Innovative mindset to analyze and improve work processes</li>
<li> Strong communication skills verbally, in writing and via presentation</li>
<li> Ability to analyze data to predict trends and likely outcomes</li>
<li> Expert knowledge of Salesforce.com or similar CRM software</li>
<li> Strong analytical skills, experience with data analysis, and ability to synthesize ideas into execution</li>
<li> Strategic and critical thinking mindset and capabilities</li>
<li> Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, Word, and Powerpoint</li>
<li> Must be able to travel as needed and relocate if required</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>About DealerSocket</b><br>
DealerSocket is a leading provider of software for the automotive industry. Our mission is to drive the future of automotive, by simplifying the experience for its customers and partners with a suite of seamlessly integrated products. DealerSocket’s suite of products includes advanced Customer Relationship Management (CRM), innovative Digital Marketing and Websites, robust Vehicle Inventory Management, insightful Analytics Reporting, and solutions to streamline dealer operations such as Desking, Credit Reporting, Compliance, and a Dealer Management System (DMS) for dealers of all sizes. To put it simply: we help dealers sell and service vehicles more profitably, while improving their customers’ experience. The DealerSocket Team is nearly 1,000 employees strong, serving over 8,000 dealerships.<br>
