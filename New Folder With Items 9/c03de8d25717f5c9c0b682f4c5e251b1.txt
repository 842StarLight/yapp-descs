<h2>Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Location:</b> National - preference to Chattanooga, San Francisco Bay, Atlanta
<br><br>
Company
<br><br>
CodeScience, Inc. is a professional services firms that enables organizations to deploy enterprise business processes into the cloud. The firm’s core offering is designing and building products for Salesforce ISV partners. The company also provides a range of services to Salesforce customers and on other SaaS platforms.
<br><br>
Position
<br><br>
Sales Engineers are responsible for supporting Business Development Managers during the sales process and making sure that the delivery team is positioned for success. In this job, you'll work with small clients, large clients, genius developers, and a host of cuddly CodeScientists obsessed with helping our clients. You will have the opportunity to demonstrate your Salesforce technical chops while also displaying familiarity with common web architecture themes. Besides talking with clients, you will also scope projects, do early designs for proposed solutions, and dabble in business modeling. Because the position straddles the business and technical realms, Sales Engineers at CodeScience are expected to keep a foot in each world.
<br><br>

<br><br>
<ul>

<li>You're bored silly with deploying Salesforce CRM, but get excited about doing the custom stuff on Force.com</li>
<li>You've worked at one of our bigger competitors and are ready to go where work quality and employee happiness reign over the profit-at-any-cost paradigm</li>
<li>You have deployed an AppExchange product into a Salesforce org</li>
<li>You spot bugs, UI improvements, and better ways to present information on almost every website or software application you use</li>
<li>You raise issues quickly instead of sweeping them under the carpet</li>

<li>You know how to communicate with and motivate software developers</li>
<li>You care more about content than form, unless it's about UI design, then you care about elegance and powerful simplicity</li>

Duties &amp; Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Scope Force.com based software application projects and communicate that scope to clients, product managers, and developers</li>
<li>Prepare client presentations, supporting documents, and visual communication tools in collaboration with Business Development Managers</li>
<li>Configure declarative portions of applications in Force.com</li>
<li>Identify changes to project scope and prepare change orders if needed</li>
<li>Manage internal and external team members to best leverage their skills</li>
<li>Maintain CRM data to support the sales process and provide insight into opportunity status</li>
<li>Develop business justification for product development expenditures based on different client markets</li>
<li>Build relationships with business and technical leaders at partners, clients, and internally at CodeScience</li>
</ul>
