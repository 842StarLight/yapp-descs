<h2>Director, Sales Call Centers</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Location(s)
</p>McAllen TX
<p></p>
<p><b>Details
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Are you an experienced leader with a track record energizing sales reps and driving growth? Are you familiar with call center operations and technology? Do you have a business owner mindset and are you motivated by a compensation package that rewards you for performance? If you’re already repeating “YES,” we should talk.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
This is a rare opportunity to take the reins of a hundred million dollar sales call center operation and grow it exponentially. As a key leader, you’ll shape the future of distribution in our company and our industry. Work with peers in Marketing and Product to identify opportunities and turbocharge growth. Apply problem-solving skills to turn friction points like TCPA compliance into competitive advantages. Leverage talent acquisition and development capabilities to build a world class sales team.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Directs operations of multi-site sales call centers including remote sales reps
</li>
<li>Aligns key metrics with strategic goals
</li>
<li>Hires and develops talent
</li>
<li>Oversees workforce management to meet the needs of the business
</li>
<li>Creates effective incentive plans
</li>
<li>Ensures compliance with applicable rules and regulations.
</li>
<li>Maintains operational transparency through reporting and effective communications
</li>
<li>Serves as frontline resource for customer insights.
</li>
<li>Coordinates with Product and Marketing teams
</li>
<li>Develops/expands distribution opportunities through avenues such as lead purchases and affiliate markets
</li>
<li>Maintains relationships with internal lines of business and brokered carrier markets to deliver relevant product offerings to customers
</li>
<li>Ensures business continuity, service levels and compliance
</li>
<li>Contributes to strategic planning
</li>
<li>Utilizes enterprise data science and business analytics resources to make fact-based decisions
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5 years sales management experience required, preferably in D2C and/or call center.
</li>
<li>4 year degree with Business/Operations Management or similar major required. [Candidates with outstanding prior experience and performance may be considered in lieu of a degree.]
</li>
<li>Property Casualty experience and producer license preferred.
</li>
<li>English/Spanish bilingual strongly preferred.
</li>
<li>Good presentation and communication skills required.
</li>
<li>Must have demonstrated interpersonal, training and sales skills.
</li>
<li>Position requires significant travel.
</li>
</ul>
