<h2>Solutions & Support Scientist, Bio (Midwest)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">-------------------------------
<br>Preferred Primary Work Location
<br>-------------------------------
<br><br>
Chicago
<br><br>
---------
<br>Territory
<br>---------
<br><br>
Midwest states with a home office in Chicago
<br><br>
--------------
<br>Overview
<br>--------------
<br><br>
<b>Work actively with assigned Territory team:</b> Sales Consultant and Sales Development Representative. Provide technical expertise throughout the entire sales and product promotion process. After sales, continue to provide the customer with additional product information, new product updates, training, reactive support, pro-active support, and general customer follow-up. Give technical presentations, workshops and product support that demonstrate advanced understanding of LI-COR Biotechnology products and their applications to research problems in biology. Actively promote and represent all LI-COR products.
<br><br>
---------------------------------
<br>Professional Qualifications
<br>---------------------------------
<br><br>
Education
<br>---------
<br><br>
Ph.D. in molecular biology, cell biology, biochemistry, genetics or related technical discipline with two (2) years Post-Doctoral appointment; or M.S. in disciplines noted above with eight (8) years of industrial or academic experience in a field of direct commercial interest to the Company; or B.S. in disciplines noted above with 10 years of industrial or academic experience in a field of direct commercial interest to the Company.
<br><br>
Experience
<br>----------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>LABORATORY: Performed independent research in molecular biology, DNA sequencing, genetic analysis, or protein chemistry; minimum three (3) years of laboratory experience after the degree is preferred.</li>
<li>INSTRUMENTATION AND SOFTWARE: Excellent aptitude for and a minimum of two (2) years' experience working with sequencing and/or infrared imaging instrumentation, computers and software, including analysis software packages or other analytical software used to evaluate and present biological data. High degree of knowledge in Windows operating system and computer networking required.</li>
<li>COMMUNICATION SKILLS: Excellent communications (written and verbal), interpersonal, and presentation skills. Demonstrated ability to teach, train and present technical information in varied situations, one-on-one to large formal meetings. Enjoys and works well with people.</li>
</ul>
General
<br>-------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Moderate to high overnight travel required (up to 70% overnight travel.)</li>
<li>Able to manage multiple priorities, projects, and tasks. Exceptional problem solving ability.</li>
<li>Highly developed sense of integrity, ethics and loyalty.</li>
</ul>
-------------------------
<br>Position Responsibilities
<br>-------------------------
<br><br>
Specific
<br>--------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>SALES SUPPORT: Give technical presentations for customers, including seminars and workshops. Conduct on-site product demonstrations including: help with logistics, install and start-up of systems, run samples, and adapt, optimize, or develop protocols to meet customer requirements. Participate in trade shows as needed. Help find new sales prospects. Maintain consistent customer contact for on-going sales of reagents, consumables, support contracts, additional equipment, as well as finding new sales prospects from existing users.</li>
<li>INSTALLATION AND TRAINING: Travel to the customer's site to set up and verify the operation of LI-COR Biotechnology products. Provide instrument, protocol and software training to users. Ensure that the customer is competent and proficient in the use and operation of LI-COR Conduct and/or participate in customer training courses.</li>
<li>CUSTOMER SUPPORT: Take calls from customers and sales personnel and provide technical assistance by phone or on-site regarding the application, use, and maintenance of LI-COR Biotechnology instruments, chemistries, reagents, protocols and software. Provide necessary follow-up and support to ensure a high degree of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>DOCUMENTATION &amp; REPORTING: Salesforce Documentation: Demonstration reports completed no later than two weeks after demo. Install reports/Install Dates in Asset/ training lists completed no later than two weeks after install documented under the Asset. All activities involving customer interactions (Call/e-mail/training/visits) should be documented in Salesforce. Expense Reporting: submit expense reports no later than two weeks after completion of the trip or month.</li>
<li>MARKETING: Gather information about customer needs, suggestions for product improvements, new product ideas, user issues, and competitive activities, for use by LI-COR.</li>
</ul>
General
<br>-------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Job will require a high percentage (up to 70%) of overnight travel.</li>
<li>A typical work week includes one day a week designated as a home office day and four days spent interfacing personally with customers.</li>
<li>Assist in training of LI-COR employees or representatives as needed.</li>
<li>Maintain excellent technical proficiency to perform job function.</li>
<li>Provide routine territory call reports as directed.</li>
<li>Other job related duties as required.</li>
<li>Lifting up to 75 pounds of force occasionally, and/or up to 25 pounds of force frequently, and/or a negligible amount of force constantly.</li>
<li>Pushing or pulling up to 100 pounds of force occasionally.</li>
</ul>
