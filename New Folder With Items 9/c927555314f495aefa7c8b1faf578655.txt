<h2>Solution Architect - Salesforce / Sales Technologies</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description:</b></h2>
<p>
Ready to shake things up? Splunk continues to be on a tear while enjoying incredible growth year over year. With growth comes scaling opportunities and the need to take the friction out of our processes, platforms, and systems to drive scale, efficiency, and effectiveness. As our Solution Architect - Sales Technologies you will have use your consistent record architecting rich technical solutions that automate complex business processes that simplify the salesperson experience while providing critical insights and visibility to leadership and partners. As we approach our first $2B+in annual revenue, it is critical that we get our processes and systems right for subsequent growth. This role is for you if possess a blend of business engagement skills, sales process knowledge, critical thinking, and a passion for frictionless systems.</p>
<p><b>
What you'll do: Yeah, I want to and can do that.</b></p>
<p>
You'll work cross functionally with the global Sales Technology delivery and the Enterprise Architecture teams to create holistic solutions that meet business and technology objectives while furthering Splunk’s long range business and technology goals.</p>
<p>
You'll use your keen business process understanding, appreciation for simplicity and user centered design, critical thinking, and a bit of elbow grease to create solutions and effectively engage with the delivery of Salesforce based solutions and technologies.</p>
<p>
You'll be responsible for defining standards, reference architecture and roadmaps for sales systems.</p>
<p>
You'll assess and understand the organization needs, breaks down project work to manageable tasks, ensure alignment of plans with the business partners, and ensure that designers and developers understand clearly what's required.</p>
<p>
You'll oversee the technical progress, ensuring the quality of the overall technical aspects of projects and operations are all in a day’s work.</p>
<p>
You'll apply your passion for great technology and maintain an ongoing, comprehensive understanding of current and emerging developments within the cloud-computing ecosystem to identify innovative ideas and proofs of concept to deliver against the existing and future needs of our customers.</p>
<p>
You'll be part of a fast-paced, entrepreneurial team responsible for delivering world-class member experiences across multiple channels, specifically Salesforce.com, and may include native iOS, Android, web, social integration, and APIs.</p>
<p><b>
What you'll need: I’ve already done that or have that.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of experience as a Solutions Architect delivering Salesforce solutions</li>
<li>
10+ years of IT experience implementing and supporting large scale technically complex solutions and integration of Salesforce with other systems</li>
<li>
Thorough and current understanding of major Salesforce platforms and technologies including Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Apex, Lightning, Force.com, Heroku, and Communities</li>
<li>
An ability to maintain an exceptional level of documentation including diagrams, standards, and project papers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What we offer you: Wow, I want that!</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A constant stream of new things for you to learn. We're always expanding into new areas, bringing in open source projects and contributing back, and exploring new technologies.</li>
<li>
A set of extraordinarily hardworking, innovative, open,fun and dedicated peers, all the way from engineering and QA to product management and customer support.</li>
<li>
Growth and mentorship. We believe in growing engineers through ownership and leadership opportunities. We also believe mentors help both sides of the equation.</li>
<li>
A stable, collaborative and supportive work environment.</li>
<li>
We don't expect people to work 12 hour days. We want you to have a successful time outside of work too. Want to work from home sometimes? No problem. We trust our colleagues to be responsible with their time and dedication, and believe that balance helps cultivate an extraordinary environment</li>
</ul>
