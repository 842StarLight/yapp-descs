 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Are you highly motivated? Interested in becoming a key part of a growing company with a culture of valuing employees and customers alike? Do you already enjoy providing solutions (or want to start) in helping customers grow their own business? If so, read on!
<br><br>
<b>Job Specifics:</b><br>
The Carrier Onboarding &amp; Monitoring position will act as the primary individual inside of Continental Logistics who will oversee our current carrier onboarding and compliance monitoring processes. This individual will also focus on continuously improving these processes as a team member working in harmony with operations management.
<br><br>
This individual will work directly with current and prospective motor carriers to ensure that their initial onboarding is handled thoroughly and completely before approving any motor carrier for use operationally. In addition, this individual will ensure that all company required and provided compliance information is accurate, complete and updated on a recurring basis in a continuous process to manage risk and/or avoid use; or discontinue use of under qualified carriers or carrier who become under qualified by internal standards over time.
<br><br>
The preferred candidate for this position will be previously familiar the third-party logistics/freight brokerage industry overall and ideally with compliance standards for motor carriers. In addition, the preferred candidate would also have vendor management software and transportation management systems experience to manage the distribution, verification, upkeep and collection of all necessary compliance related documentation. This preferred individual will also have been exposed or used systems such as Carrier 411 and/or FMCSA Safer for monitoring motor carriers. .
