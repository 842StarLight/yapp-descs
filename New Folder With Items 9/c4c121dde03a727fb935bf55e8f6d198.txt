<h2>Senior Revenue Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Role: Senior Revenue Accountant</b></div>
<div><b>Location: Minneapolis, MN</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Perforce develops DevOps tools that improve software quality and security as well as team productivity for several of the world’s leading companies, such as PIXAR, CD Projekt Red, NASA, Verizon, Honda, NVIDIA, and Johns Hopkins.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position Summary:</b></div>
<div>Perforce is looking for a Senior Revenue Accountant that will be a key member of a growing accounting team. The position will be responsible for accurate and timely month end close and financial reporting and will work closely with management to analyze overall operational performance. This will be a key position in the group and will be heavily relied upon to ensure accurate financial information is produced and appropriate accounting controls are followed. The candidate must demonstrate the ability to use accounting knowledge and previous experience to add value and assist in meeting the department's objectives while working in a fast-paced, continually changing, and high-growth environment.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Perform month-end close procedures associated with revenue including journal entry preparation, reconciliations, and variance analysis of revenue versus budget/forecast</li>
<li>Team with Sales Operations, Finance, and other departments to proactively manage invoicing and revenue recognition issues</li>
<li>Reconcile, rollforward, and analyze Deferred Revenue balance sheet accounts</li>
<li>Analyze and track pre- and post-acquisition revenue and AR to ensure proper US GAAP accounting</li>
<li>Analyze and report key revenue metrics and results</li>
<li>Analyze the AR allowance on a monthly basis</li>
<li>Provide management with the financial information necessary to make sound policy decisions</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop, implement, and document appropriate control procedures</li>
<li>Provide supporting schedules and analysis during the annual external audit</li>
<li>Implement process and system improvements as needed by the business and ensure the appropriate accounting treatment is setup</li>
<li>Analyze financial data and resolve accounting discrepancies and irregularities</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the annual budgeting and quarterly reforecasting process</li>
<li>Other accounting duties as requested and/or assigned</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Requirements:</b></div>
<div><b>Technical Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in accounting and three years or more of experience at a staff accountant level or higher</li>
<li>CPA (or progress toward) preferred</li>
<li>Software company experience preferred</li>
<li>Strong analytical, verbal, written, problem solving, and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Computer experience with MS Office applications (Excel, Word, PowerPoint) required</li>
<li>NetSuite ERP and Salesforce CRM experience preferred</li>
<li>Possess a working knowledge of GAAP and the ability to interpret and implement new GAAP guidance, including ASC 606</li>
<li>Experience in documenting and improving existing systems and processes</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Personal and Interpersonal Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Must have a proactive, positive attitude and be comfortable working in a fast paced, growth-oriented environment</li>
<li>Ability to act as a change agent and have experience in improving processes</li>
<li>High attention to detail and ability to manage/prioritize multiple tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain positive working relationships with other departments throughout the company</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team to foster a collaborative environment</li>
<li>Possess a strong initiative to search for and implement process improvements and efficiencies</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Working Conditions and/or Physical Demands</b></div>
<ul>
<li>This is a standard office position with extensive keyboarding and/or 10-key repetitive motion</li>
<li>Occasional light lifting of up to 30 lbs</li>
</ul>
