<h2>Sr. Regulatory Affairs Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<p>The Senior Regulatory Affairs Specialist will provide the day to day support for regulatory activities and global compliance for the company. Compiles all materials required in submissions, license renewal and annual registrations. Keeps abreast of regulatory procedures and changes. Directs interaction with regulatory agencies on defined matters. Recommends strategies for earliest possible approvals of new products. Drives and implements processes to ensure efficient regulatory review and assess regulatory risks.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul><li> Provide support for all regulatory activities related to software product development and commercialization of software medical devices.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Support all international regulatory activities in the EU CE, Canada and ROW.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Oversee preparation and filing of all domestic and international regulatory agencies; set internal standards for and review all foreign filings to assure adequacy and regulatory accuracy.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage budgetary requirements and ensure compliance of expenditures to meet budget for worldwide regulatory programs.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage FDA Unique Identifier Program.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Be an effective team player interfacing well with R&amp;D, Manufacturing Engineering, Manufacturing, Clinical, Marketing and Sales.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Other duties as required.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul><li> Evidence of leadership and a management style characterized by vision, teamwork, preparation, communication and negotiation.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Successful management of relationships at all levels of the organization including external relationships with FDA, Notified Bodies, and Distributors.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledgeable with CEBOS/MQ1(electronic document software) is a plus.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must have international regulatory experience in medical devices.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must have experience in FDAâ€™s UDI regulations.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledgeable in quality systems per FDA QSR and ISO 13485.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must have knowledge in EU MDD/MDR and MDSAP.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficient MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Adobe Acrobat, etc.</li></ul>
