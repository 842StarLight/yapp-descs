<p><b>What you will do:</b></p>
<p>
The Inventory Control/Verifying Team is responsible for the overall daily flow of products through the Distribution Center (DC). This individual works collaboratively with our customers in Sales, Customer Service, Purchasing, Accounting, and the DC departments. If you love research, problem solving, and numbers, this just may be the job for you. The tasks required are a mix of hands-on product handling, data analysis, planning, and collaboration with other DC departments. IC/Verifying is also a first contact person for any inventory, capacity, and order processing issues.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Allocate efficient space management for outbound functions</li>
<li>Guide sales orders to ensure on time shipping, and curate inventory to ensure its accuracy</li>
<li>Correct inventory placement issues that prevent order processing</li>
<li>Ensure that daily orders are complete in processing by the promised shipping dates</li>
<li>Make necessary changes to orders that are being processed</li>
<li>Resolve F9 log issues involving order processing</li>
<li>Fix individual items with slotting issues and weights and measure problems</li>
<li>Seek out opportunities to eliminate chronic problems in outbound order processing</li>
<li>Troubleshoot hold/release and inventory adjustment problems so SAP and High Jump are in balance</li>
<li>Track inventory movements to resolve lost and found issues</li>
</ul>
