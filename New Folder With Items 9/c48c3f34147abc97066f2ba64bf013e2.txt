<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
The Senior Commissions Analyst will support multiple operational, analytical and informational functions for the commission's team with an emphasis on speed, efficiency and execution. The objective of the position is to ensure commissions are properly assigned to the order placed confirming TBI partners are paid commissions accurately and on time.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Understands departmental responsibilities, policies, procedures, and processes in order to assist the department as needed</li>
<li>Reviews sub-agent questions, concerns, and commission issues</li>
<li>Uses Provider tools and contacts to gather missing information for orders and commissions</li>
<li>Reviews and audits current order records to ensure all information is properly added</li>
<li>Creates, edits, and maintains accurate order records in NetSuite in a timely manner</li>
<li>Processes monthly commission payments to partners</li>
<li>Creates reports and summaries in accordance with commission-related projects in a timely manner</li>
<li>Gathers, researches, and supplies data and information needed by commission's team</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>Job Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance or Business required</li>
<li>3-5 years related experience with experience in a fast paced sales environment preferred</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications, including Word, Excel, Outlook and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Must be highly organized and detail oriented</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Must work well within a team</li>
<li>Candidate must be an energetic, highly productive and well organized</li>
</ul>
