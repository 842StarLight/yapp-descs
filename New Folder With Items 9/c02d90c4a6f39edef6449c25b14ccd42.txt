<p><b>Position Purpose/Summary</b></p>
<p>
Plans, conducts, and coordinates a wide range of marketing and business development activities with a special emphasis on effective and efficient proposal and interview creation to win pursuits strategically. Assists with the information gathering and data entry for the firm’s marketing database (Deltek Vision, Cosential and Open Asset) and assists in its continual development and use. Assist the Marketing Manager, Proposal Team as necessary. Serve as the client collaboration team’s resident expert for internal marketing information regarding past and current projects in the local office. Assist other marketing coordinators and regional offices as needed. Mentor other marketing coordinators as appropriate.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Pursuits/Proposals/Interviews:
<ul>
<li>Collaborates with pursuit teams on proposals and interviews in terms of strategy, message development, writing and pursuit production as well as producing information packages for business development efforts.</li>
<li>Coordinates with Principals in Charge, Team Leaders and/or Business Development staff to develop proposal and/or interview content and serves as the project manager to make sure that all areas of the submission are thoroughly addressed.</li>
<li>Writes and edits proposals, collaborating with other team members when necessary.</li>
<li>Incorporates graphics, renderings, images that support and strengthen the strategic message.</li>
<li>Maintains proposal/interview standards as developed and upholds brand standards.</li>
<li>Ensures that quality assurance and quality control is performed for each and every proposal or interview.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Build BSA Brand Awareness
<ul>
<li>Represent BSA at industry, community and client events when appropriate.</li>
<li>Support BSA colleagues with materials and collateral at client meetings.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Business Development Support:
<ul>
<li>Contribute to the development of the marketing message and visuals necessary for trade shows, presentations and conferences as needed.</li>
<li>Work closely with PICs and Business Development staff to carry out strategic plans for the markets and the regional teams.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Information Databases:</li>
</ul>
<div><ul>
<li>Maintains storage and accuracy of project information, related to marketing efforts.</li>
</ul></div>
<ul>
<li>Leads project information gathering process, which includes using the knowledge of principals, other marketing team members, and existing resources. Also works to keep information current throughout the various phases of a project’s development.</li>
<li>Collaboratively produces new project sheets and content in a timely manner to be used in future pursuits.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Leadership
<ul>
<li>Mentors marketing coordinator staff.</li>
<li>Represents the firm to clients, peer organizations, and business associates.</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<div><ul>
<li>Lead qualification, proposal, and presentation activities as assigned.</li>
</ul></div>
<ul>
<li>Actively involved in professional and community organizations, such as SMPS</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>May be required to perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Critical Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Strategic thinking: </b>Develops strategies to achieve organizational goals; understands organization’s strengths and weaknesses; analyzes market and competition; identifies external threats and opportunities; adapts strategy to changing conditions.</li>
<li>
<b>Effective communication skills: </b>Able to clearly present information through the spoken or written word; reads and interprets complex information; listens well and is able to build consensus.</li>
<li>
<b>Demonstrates leadership skills: </b>Able to assume a role of authority, as necessary; attends weekly and monthly staff meetings and makes sound decisions and takes personal accountability for decisions.</li>
<li>
<b>Graphic design: </b>Experienced with design software including InDesign, PowerPoint, and others. Able to visually communicate a message in addition to the written word.</li>
<li>
<b>High accuracy and attention to detail: </b>Able to perform work and job function with precision and accuracy; realizes errors can have significant impact on desired results and is able to avoid errors in almost all circumstances; carefully monitors processes; concentrates on details and organizes and maintains a system of records.</li>
<li>
<b>Computer skills required: </b>Microsoft Windows environment, Microsoft Office: Word, PowerPoint, Excel; Design Tools: Adobe Creative Suite especially InDesign and Photoshop; Google: Mail, Calendar, Drive, Apps; previous experience with Deltek Vision, Prezi and Cosential software a plus</li>
</ul>
