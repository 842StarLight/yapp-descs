<div>
The leader of our North American Marketing and Sales Distribution Center (MSDC) will oversee, drive, and be responsible for the implementation of the new model. Their primary responsibility will be to manage the MSDC responsible for all marketing and content coordination, digital, demand generation, lead qualification and inside selling activities including the management of the CRM. This person will run the center from a profit and loss standpoint and be responsible to the Sodexo HQ leadership globally, as well as in North America for all numbers, return on investment, spend, forecasts, dashboards and executive reports. This person’s team will be assigned a quarterly lead and sales quota which they must attain. This person will be a key member of the North America sales and marketing leadership teams with executive interaction on a daily basis to initiate all new tenants of our digital transformation. The leader will interface between North American leadership and the executive teams Global HQ.<br>
The leader is responsible for significantly growing market share and manage the digital marketing and inside sales teams, including the ownership of the CRM for the region. The inside sales teams manage internal communications (phone, email, social, and web conference) to present possible solutions, expand the web of influence, qualify leads for the field salesforce and occasionally close revenue directly. The leader will also oversee, own and manage the digital marketing resources in North America including creative services, website coordination, demand generation, SEO, branding, event marketing and other services to drive value into new and existing customers, and even close sales. Accurate revenue forecasting is key to success in this role.</div>
