 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Account Development Manager</b></p>
<p>Original Impressions (OI) is a Postal Center International (PCI) Company, a leader in the print, mail and marketing industry. We are championed by a network of talented and committed specialists in every field who help enterprise-wide clients navigate print, mail and marketing project timelines with a full suite of integrated services – all in one place. Those who work within OI’s Account Services team are responsible for supporting and growing that network of enterprise-wide clients. In addition to driving sales revenue and market share through new client acquisition, the ideal candidate will incorporate a creative and analytical approach in connecting clients with services that drive value for their organization.</p>
<p>Original Impressions’ Account Development Managers are business-to-business sales professionals responsible for selling a full line of print management solutions including print, mail, fulfillment, signage, creative, marketing, packaging and e-business. We offer a base salary and compensation package that directly reflects the effort put in along with the support of our team.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive sales revenue and market share through lead generation, cold and warm prospecting and new client acquisition</li>
<li>Plan, analyze and execute sales budgets; set clear objectives; forecast future sales trends based on historical sales records/activity, recurring buys and current inventory levels</li>
<li>KPIs include but are not limited to: $500k annual new account sales, as well as percentages of new account acquisitions and closed quotes</li>
<li>Provide weekly updates on overall activities, major deals advancing, opportunities, competitor information, market needs, etc. Track details regarding “wins and losses” across sales portfolio to capture product gaps and best practices</li>
<li>Make 40 to 50 weekly phone calls, emails, LinkedIn correspondences</li>
<li>Track details regarding “wins and losses” across sales portfolio to capture product gaps and best practices</li>
<li>Set a minimum of three face-to-face appointments per week</li>
<li>Prepare proposals, ROI business cases, presentations, coordination of terms of the sale</li>
<li>Collaborate with clients to understand and identify their strategic vision, objectives and needs while aligning our products and services where business opportunities exist</li>
<li>Onboard new clients in partnership with the Account Manager and the CSR team to ensure smooth account transition</li>
<li>Maintain effective communication between departments within the organization to ensure maximum success, improved business processes and positive customer service</li>
<li>Manage multiple prospecting activities at the same time</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications/Skills/Behaviors</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree and/or equivalent experience in a B2B sales environment</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years previous sales experience required, preferably in a print and marketing services related industry environment</li>
<li>A proven track record in relationship building and networking skills with ability to interact effectively and professionally</li>
<li>Strong organizational, time-management, excellent oral and written communication, and problem-solving skills – written and verbal assessment is required</li>
<li>Aggressive solicitor and highly self-motivated to excel in sales</li>
<li>Individuals must be coachable and possess a positive attitude</li>
<li>Agility in a fast-paced manufacturing and services-based organization</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>CRM experience</li>
<li>Valid Driver's License</li>
</ul>
