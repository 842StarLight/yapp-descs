<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b><i>WHAT DOES A SHARED CALL CENTER REPRESENTATIVE DO?</i></b></p>
<p>
As a shared role, you are responsible for making each customers’ day. Understand their needs and provide a proper solution in a timely manner. Learn new systems and technology to accurately manage our clients’ accounts. Practice your new customer service techniques with every interaction.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Our Shared Call Center Representatives are responsible for the following tasks:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Respond to inbound customer service &amp; sales requests</li>
<li>
Understand customer needs and provide solutions</li>
<li>
Improve our partners reputation with customers</li>
<li>
Learn new systems and customer service techniques</li>
<li>
Escalate customer dissatisfaction when necessary</li>
<li>
Increase first contact resolutions without escalation</li>
</ul>
<p>
In addition to becoming the best-in-the-business, you will need to be confident, fully engaged, a team player, and dedicated. You are also responsible for bringing a positive and enthusiastic outlook to work each day!</p>
