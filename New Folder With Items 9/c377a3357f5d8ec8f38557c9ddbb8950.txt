<p><b>
Magento Architect</b></p>
<p>
LiveArea is looking for an experienced Magento Architect to develop solutions for our client’s product needs. This individual must have the ability to understand eCommerce needs and be able to translate that into real world solutions. The ideal candidate for this position would have a great sense of what makes an outstanding product and potentially be able to lead a team in executing this.</p>
<ul>
<li>Comfortable working in a close-knit, team environment where attitude and work ethic matter. Ability to actively communicate clearly and professionally with customers, partners and the LiveArea team whether in person, via email, phone, video, or chat</li>
<li>Consistent ability to self-manage through daily tasks</li>
<li>Detail Oriented and commitment to quality work</li>
<li>Passionate about innovation and technology, with a deep focus on continuous learning</li>
<li>Direct experience helping other eCommerce Businesses work through technical challenges</li>
</ul>
<p>Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with, and comfort working under a version-controlled environment</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of Mobile First, Responsive Web Design, and UX concepts - with the ability to put them into practice</li>
<li>Experience designing and implementing thorough development and testing workflows</li>
<li>Able to quickly learn new processes, software, and technology and apply it to your daily routine</li>
<li>Have experience optimizing web server configurations and scaling web applications</li>
<li>Looking for a chance to create engaging and interactive e-commerce applications</li>
<li>You love building both back-end and front-end code for new or existing web applications</li>
<li>You stay ahead of web developments and trends and are passionate about collaborating with like-minded teams of engineers to share your interests</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or a closely related field required; Master’s degree preferred</li>
<li>3 or more years Magento experience</li>
<li>7+ years’ development experience required; with 3+ years in professional services/consulting experience preferred</li>
<li>Magento 2 Certified with multiple years of direct Magento Development experience</li>
<li>Proficiency with PHP frameworks such as Zend framework</li>
<li>Very comfortable in HTML/CSS, JavaScript &amp; JQuery, XML, MySQL, PHP</li>
<li>Proficiency with PHP5 and MySQL from an OOP perspective, database schema design, LAMP Server administration</li>
<li>Proven experience with the Magento and 3rd Party APIs. You love working with web-based services and APIs to bridge communication and extend features from other applications or services. Unit testing/API testing experience, creating test plans.</li>
<li>Competent in web services integrations (REST/SOAP)</li>
<li>You want to participate in project planning, design review, and architectural meetings</li>
<li>Competent knowledge of e-commerce data organization and relationships</li>
<li>Store catalog (categories/products/attributes)</li>
<li>Sales orders (quotes/orders/invoices/shipments/credits)</li>
<li>Experience optimizing and refactoring application code, 3rd-party integrations, CRM, ERP, Fraud, Payments, or Shipping software a plus</li>
</ul> <p>Characteristics:</p>
<ul>
<li>Professional – unquestionable integrity, credibility, and character; Who has demonstrated high moral and ethical behavior</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written and presentation communication skills</li>
<li>Demonstrable ability to provide consulting services</li>
<li>Must possess a unique blend of business and technical savvy, a big-picture vision and the ability to articulate a vision of the total solution both internally and externally</li>
<br>
</ul>
