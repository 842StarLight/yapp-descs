<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>Responsible for directly supervising or working in a team lead (non-supervisor) role over a group of Integrated Capacity Solutions (ICS) broker, customer service, and/or support personnel. Works under general supervision.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Guides or directly supervises employees to effectively identify capacity with partner carriers and cover freight needs for customers in a profitable manner.
</p></li>
<li><p>May guide or manage sales employees to effectively identify freight/logistics needs from customers and sell/provide solutions to those customers, driving optimal revenue and optimal profitability. Maintain daily report with all newly approved carriers.
</p></li>
<li><p>May guide or manage customer service and support personnel to ensure excellent customer service and support, ensuring proper customer and partner carrier retention.
</p></li>
<li><p>Trains and develops subordinates or others on team.
</p></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Manage, execute and train employees on spot quote/price process for their branch.
</p></li>
<li><p>Markets, services and promotes JBHT's transportation products and services.
</p></li>
<li><p>Builds freight volume from existing customers.
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintain and build effective relationships with customers.
</p></li>
<li><p>Develop and manage a network of outside carriers to ensure adequate capacity for their assigned market.
</p></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Negotiate with carriers to meet margin requirements and ensure maximum profitability.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensure customer satisfaction and adequate service levels where freight is picked up and delivered using safe, reliable carriers.
</p></li>
<li><p>Manage all freight in transit by obtaining adequate updates from carriers/drivers to monitor movement and ensure on time delivery.
</p></li>
<li><p>Meets or exceeds margin and revenue goals for their assigned branch.
</p></li>
<li><p>Position requires full-time attendance.<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Qualifications:
</p>
<ul><li><p>Two to four-year degree from an accredited university and at least 18 months of transportation/brokerage experience and/or training; or, at least 24 months of transportation/brokerage experience and/or training.
</p></li></ul>
