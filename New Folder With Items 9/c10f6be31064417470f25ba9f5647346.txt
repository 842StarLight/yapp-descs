<h2>Product Manager- Competitive Analysis</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a Product Manager- Competitive Analysis for Infoblox, you will be charged with defining, driving and supporting Infoblox’ s competitive strategy and positioning. You will be responsible for developing the competitive analysis tools and campaigns for both internal and external use. You will be working cross functionally with Product Management, Product Marketing, Product Development and System Engineering teams to assess Infoblox competitive positioning needs, define competitive strategies and communicate competitive positioning to the sales organization.<br>
<br>
The Product Manager- Competitive Analysis will create competitive analysis and positioning papers, analyze competitor claims, craft and articulate counter examples highlighting Infoblox product’s key benefits clearly and concisely. This is an exciting opportunity for an experienced professional to join a fast-moving team in growing company that is a leader in its field.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Understand the DDI market and the competitive landscape, conduct SWOT analysis, understand competitors’ products/solutions, and be the focal point for all competitive analysis</li>
<li>
Work closely with product management, product marketing, corporate marketing and the field to ensure competitive differentiation for Infoblox product messaging, web content, campaigns, and corporate marketing content</li>
<li>
Create the appropriate messaging documents that can be used for both internal and external consumptions such as web pages, presentations, and press releases</li>
<li>
Respond to competitive product announcements and social media postings</li>
<li>
Educate the field about Infoblox’s positioning and advantages over the competition by developing the sales tools that support the selling process, including battlecards, competitive positioning comparisons, white papers, and training, aligning all competitive analysis information with enterprise product launches</li>
<li>
Develop the appropriate customer facing collateral that can be shared with customers, prospects and partners</li>
<li>
Act as a thought leader in the market and competitive environment, contributing regularly on the company’s blog and other social media outlets, driving interaction with industry analysts to influence their coverage of Infoblox products/technologies</li>
<li>
Develop the product roadmap by assessing the competitive landscape to identify product gaps, competitive threats, and strategic opportunities; provide product management and engineering with input into product and roadmap planning and releases</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3 to 6 years of experience in competitive analysis, product management/product marketing</li>
<li>
3+ years’ experience as a competitive strategist/evangelist in the network industry supporting enterprise accounts</li>
<li>
Proven ability to analyze markets, products, and competitors, and translate that information into useful intelligence and sales tools</li>
<li>
Solid understanding of major DDI/Security vendor product and marketing strategy, and overall market trends</li>
<li>
Strategic analysis and competitive positioning skills</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communications skills including competitive positioning technical whitepapers and responses</li>
</ul>

Education:</b></p>

Bachelors in Computer Science or a related technical field, or equivalent work experience</li></ul>
