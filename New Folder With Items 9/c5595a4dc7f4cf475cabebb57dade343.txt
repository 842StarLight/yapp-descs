<h2>Field and Channel Sales Enablement Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Are you looking to join one of the fastest growing software companies in the Silicon Valley? ThoughtSpot's Search &amp; AI-Driven analytics platform is revolutionising how the world uses data by making analytics 1000x faster and easier for non-technical business people.
<br><br>
The Field Sales and Channel Enablement Manager is the primary enablement partner for our Account Executives and Partner Sellers. As a member of the global GTM Enablement team, you will be responsible for leading the Field Sales enablement efforts, ensuring that all Field Sellers have the tools, skills and knowledge they need to successfully engage with our prospects and customers.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate is an experienced seller or professional services expert, who has worked with large enterprise clients. The candidate should also have a passion for teaching and/or coaching, who appreciates working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment and wants to be part of building the best enablement program reps will ever experience!
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Build, plan, and deliver sales bootcamp trainings to sellers and reseller partners, including internationally Improve AE onboarding KPIs (time to ramp, ASP in ramp)</li>
<li>Work with AEs and Sales Leaders to identify readiness gaps</li>
<li>Provide coaching and feedback Represent Enablement team in regional QBRs and planning sessions</li>
<li>Assist in the development of the global GTM enablement strategy &amp; operational plan Partner with PMM team to build sales assets/tools for your customers Enhance field-based selling skills, field coaching and feedback</li>
<li>~50% travel</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years supporting a direct B2B sales organization in an enablement/training function OR 7+ years Enterprise Sales or Customer Success experience</li>
<li>Experience with training and coaching</li>
<li>Excellent cross -functional collaboration and communication skills including, presenting and writing</li>
<li>High degree of accuracy and attention to detail in communications</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage priorities and deliver enablement activities that drive results</li>
</ul>
