<h2>Business Development Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Purpose:</b> Support the sales teams business development efforts by setting sales appointments. Develop and maintain strong customer relationships. Provide exceptional customer service to all Karl Customers through phone calls and correspondence.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b>
</p>
<p><b>Lead Generation</b>: Identify and build a pipeline of sales opportunities specific to assigned team needs. Work with management to develop customer profiles that support current offers and Dealership messaging strategies. Analyze customer information and purchase patterns to identify key customer contact opportunities that lead to customer purchases. Make outbound calls with the purpose of setting an appointment within a <i>30</i> day time frame. Keep current on inventory and relevant sales and service specials and communicate to our customers. Monitor active leads, tracking performance and communicating status to sales manager and/or team leader. Provide training and coaching as directed/needed. Help set appointments for specific time and date.
</p>
<p><b>Customer Service:</b> In addition to outbound appointment calls, assist team with inbound customer calls, responding to questions related to buying or service needs. Understand and communicate customer for life value proposition. Make outbound calls and send customized emails that build relationships with customers. Document all call information according to department operating procedures. Help create and support a work culture that fosters and rewards excellence and promotes teamwork and collaboration Consistently apply the Karl Way customer service philosophy, supporting long term customer relationships. Meet or exceed GM CSI requirements.
</p>
<p><b>Database Quality:</b> Responsible for maintenance and consistent quality of customer data. Consistently review and monitor information, making corrections as needed. Identify process gaps and make recommendations for changes that will improve quality of our customer data. Consistently follow department processes and procedures as they relate to customer, documentation and tracking.
</p>
<p><b>Professionalism: </b>Develop dealership and product knowledge. Research customer purchasing and service information prior to phone conversation to ensure we are using the customer's time in an effective and efficient manner. Input accurate and detailed notes in customer profiles so the previous conversations and information are available for anyone to pick up where you left off. Become familiar with sales processes and other related duties to direct questions to appropriate department if unable to accurately assist a customer's needs in a timely matter.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b> HS Diploma, previous call center or sales experience preferred, but not required. Excellent interpersonal skills, ability to work in a team environment. Excellent communication, presentation, time management and organizational skills. Proficient in MS software. Clean driving record and background check. Excellent communication skills (listening, verbal, &amp; written) Outgoing, energetic and a track record of exceeding goals</p>
</div>
