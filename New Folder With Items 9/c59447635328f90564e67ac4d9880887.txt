Our Firmware/Embedded Software team is looking for a mid- to senior-level Embedded Software Engineer. In this role you will develop new features, enhancements and firmware for our next generation of readers. These readers are installed in stores, hospitals, warehouses and other facilities and are used to identify, locate and authentic items tagged with RAIN RFID chips. Our platform of readers/gateways, chips and software allows Impinj to connect billions of items in the physical world to the Internet, thereby enabling the true Internet-of-Things.<br>
<br>
<p><b>What you will do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You will develop firmware for RAIN RFID reader and gateway products in C/C++. This could include air interface protocols, algorithms, APIs, hardware device controls, network communication features and other embedded features.</li>
<li>
You will partner with other teams to define requirements, functionality, architecture and develop object oriented designs</li>
<li>
You will assist in the mentoring and managing of a small team of engineers</li>
<li>
Contribute to and benefit from our world-class build system and continuous integration services</li>
<li>
Participate in Agile/Scrum iterative design and development process</li>
</ul>
