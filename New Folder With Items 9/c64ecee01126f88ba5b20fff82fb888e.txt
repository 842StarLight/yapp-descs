<h2>National Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Local Company is hiring National Sales Directors!</p>
<p>Our company’s proprietary technology forever changes the way enterprises operate and manage their communications. Fortune 500s and enterprises alike are discovering the undeniable value and benefits of dependable network communications. Our technology increases reliability, strengthens security, and enhances end-to-end centralized reporting while dramatically reducing costs. With a solution that works across the globe, we deliver unprecedented value and empowerment.</p>
<p>Our Customer Success team is a fast-paced group and is looking to grow by adding over-achieving National Sales Directors who have a strong work ethic and are excited by the opportunities that come with growing an innovative company.</p>
<p><b>About the Role</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The National Sales Director position is a quota carrying position.</li>
<li>Manage customer’s retention lifecycle, unearth expansion opportunities and close deals to achieve quarterly and annual targets.</li>
<li>You will direct business value assessment alongside our Senior Management Team to ensure our customers gain the highest return on investment.</li>
<li>The success of this role achieved by higher customer satisfaction, increased retention, and expansion velocity.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Role &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Display a Comprehensive understanding of our product and demonstrate a successful return on investment.</li>
<li>Build strong and profitable relationships with our clients</li>
<li>Drive high engagement and demonstrate value realization of our product</li>
<li>Partner with the Customer Success Team to maintain a high retention rate, ensure on-time renewals and create expansion opportunities (up-sell, add-on, cross-sell, etc.)</li>
<li>Proactively manage and grow the business footprint for a portfolio of assigned customers</li>
<li>Own an account-level relationship with clients through various mechanisms - online, traveling to customer locations for face to face meetings</li>
<li>Drive Customer Success playbooks and product adoption programs to drive better customer retention and expansion strategies</li>
<li>Comfortable with product level details - be able to handle questions pertaining to high-level technology requirements</li>
<li>Close renewal and expansion business, meeting and exceeding quota</li>
<li>Proactively identify and resolve account success blockers that may arise</li>
<li>Drive customer advocacy initiative for the designated portfolio of accounts.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4+ years of account management preferably with enterprise accounts</li>
<li>Ability to develop and deliver executive presentations</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills, particularly via phone and virtual meetings</li>
<li>Consultative and friendly</li>
<li>Proficiency in Sales Force</li>
<li>Ability to travel domestically and internationally as needed</li>
<li>Project management experience</li>
</ul>
