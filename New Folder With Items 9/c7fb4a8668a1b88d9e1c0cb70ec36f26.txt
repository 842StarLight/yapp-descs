<p><b>You can make a difference. </b>Viewpoint hires talented individuals as Application Support Specialist to help our customers troubleshoot business critical software issues. Specialist work closely with internal teams including Customer Success, Sales and Development to maximize the time to value for our customers.

<p></p>
<p><b>The Role:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assists customers with use of Viewpoint software core product(s) and point-solutions.
</li>
<li>Monitors, tracks, and documents customer support calls and related activities in CRM system. Uses SQL Query, reporting tools, and spreadsheets to examine underlying data.
</li>
<li>Assists other departments with application issues and development of the software.
</li>
<li>Develops Knowledge Base articles to document troubleshooting steps and common solutions to frequent calls for use by customers and peers
</li>
<li>Ability to quickly develop and maintain a high level of knowledge of (Viewpoint) software products and remain expert on current product knowledge both from an internal and from a user perspective.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to empathize with customers, actively listen; ability to always defuse situations rather than intensify one.
</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving skills, a strong customer-service orientation and commitment to insuring timely, quality solutions to customer issues.
</li>
<li>Ability to understand technical concepts and communicate them clearly to individuals with varied backgrounds and skill levels.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates accuracy and thoroughness and monitors own work to ensure quality, presents numerical data effectively, and can read and interpret written information.
</li>
<li>Well-organized, self-directed team player.
</li>
<li>Remains open to others’ ideas and exhibits willingness to try new things.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates excellent written and verbal communication skills. Listens effectively, transmits information accurately and understandably, and actively seeks feedback. Effectively presents and explains information to various group sizes and levels of knowledge.
</li>
<li>Prioritizes and plans work activities, uses time efﬁciently and develops realistic action plans.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates professionalism, discretion, and good judgment in all interactions with co-workers, and customers.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications </b><b>Required:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Bachelor or Associate degree in business with an emphasis in business, accounting, construction management or other relevant discipline.
</b></li>
<li>Strong understanding of general business, accounting and HR principles, particularly employee self-service, timecard entry, job cost.
</li>
<li>Intermediate level of experience with core MS Ofﬁce Suite (Outlook, Word, Excel)
</li>
</ul>
