<b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b>
</div>
<div>
To provide safe, reliable and cost-efficient vehicles by performing quality preventive maintenance inspections and repairs as well as performing expert diagnosis and repair as required to prevent breakdowns on road, reduce vehicle downtime and improve total fleet reliability.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul><li>
Perform required inspections and preventive maintenance on vehicles and equipment within planned time tolerances to maximize the safety, reliability, and cost efficiency throughout the life cycle of the fleet.<ul>
<li>
Perform FAI/CVI (Annual) inspections on all fleet vehicles including tractors, trailers, converter dollies, light-duty vehicles, and any other company-owned equipment in accordance with all federal, state and local legal and regulatory agencies. (Includes all state required inspections and frequencies).</li>
<li>
Perform quality preventive maintenance inspections per company methods on all fleet vehicles including tractors, trailers, converter dollies, lift-gates, light-duty vehicles, and any other company-owned equipment.</li>
<li>
Perform quality maintenance repairs as required to maximize safety and reliability on all fleet vehicles including tractors, trailers, converter dollies, lift-gates, light-duty vehicles, and any company owned equipment.</li>
<li>
Address all Driver Vehicle Inspection Report write-ups timely, and efficiently.</li>
<li>
Diagnose cause of any malfunction and perform repairs to manufacturer specifications including but not limited to engines, transmissions and clutches, drive-line, differential, steering and suspension, fuel and ignition system, exhaust systems (including regeneration systems), intake systems, electrical systems, brake systems, HVAC systems and lift-gate hydraulic, mechanical and electrical systems.</li>
<li>
Follow procedures including documenting all work performed on work orders.</li>
<li>
Learn and develop efficiency in Sysco computer programs</li>
<li>
Proficient in electrical meter testing</li>
<li>
Knowledge of controls and electric (DC low voltage)</li>
<li>
Electric motor knowledge (DC)</li>
<li>
Complete electrical repairs and welding projects as needed.</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
