<b>Oil, Gas &amp; Chemicals Quorum Manager</b><br>
Are you an Quorum professional experienced with clients in Oil, Gas &amp; Chemicals sector? Our practice is undergoing heavy growth and high demand for Quorum talent, while giving our talent the chance to work with world-class organizations solving key business issues in a team environment.<br>
<br>
<b>Work you’ll do</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Deliver OG&amp;C (Oil, Gas, &amp; Chemicals) Quorum projects</li>
<li>Lead and support Quorum pursuits and OG&amp;C practice development initiatives</li>
<li>Promote and participate in forums for sharing Quorum and OG&amp;C expertise, strengthening Deloitte’s collective knowledge and helping resolve our clients’ challenges</li>
<li>Collaborate on innovative solutions to support the future of Quorum and OG&amp;C</li>
<li>Support sales pursuits for Quorum implementations with OG&amp;C clients</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Required Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Functional/technical proficiency with at least 4+ full life-cycle QUORUM implementations and 8+ years of industry and/or consulting experience</li>
<li>At least 8 years of QUORUM experience implementing Quorum. Applications include:</li>
<li>QRA – Quorum Revenue Accounting</li>
<li>QCA – Quorum Cost Accounting / AFE – Authorization for Expenditure</li>
<li>QDO – Quorum Division Order</li>
<li>QLS – Quorum Lease</li>
<li>QFS – Quorum Financial Accounting</li>
<li>QQM – Quorum Query Manager</li>
<li><b>Must have Oil, Gas and Chemicals experience</b></li>
<li>Adept at designing and implementing technology-enabled business solutions for clients as part of a high-talent team and as a team lead or Project Manager on at least 3+ full cycle implementations</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent</li>
<li>Ability to travel 80-100% is REQUIRED</li>
<li>Limited immigration sponsorship may be available.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Preferred</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Master’s degree preferred</li>
<li>Experience defining systems strategy, developing systems requirements, designing and prototyping, testing, training, defining support procedures and implementing practical business solutions under multiple deadlines</li>
<li>Strong current hands-on configuration and design skills</li>
</ul>

<b>Oil Gas &amp; Chemicals</b><br>
The Oil, Gas &amp; Chemicals sector is facing unprecedented change in a quickly evolving digital era. Improving the customer experience has never been more critical to business success. Clients are increasingly focused on maintaining and growing their assets while simultaneously searching for cost-effective and safer ways to access, extract and sustain key resources. Deloitte currently serves the majority of the global Oil &amp; Gas and Chemicals corporations on the Fortune 500 list, assisting them in facing these business challenges. Moving forward, we have launched a growth strategy to at least double our marketplace footprint over the next 3-5 years. We are committed to helping our clients work with newly empowered customers, hyper-connected digital ecosystems, and expansion in data all while collaborating with Deloitte’s Offering Portfolios.
<br>
<br>
<b>Quorum</b><br>
Our Quorum consulting team helps clients modernize their core systems, building an agile architecture, powering the digital enterprise and enabling innovation for our clients. Serving over 75% of Oil, Gas and Chemical Fortune 500 companies allows for the development of industry-specific technical expertise and working side-by-side with client staff provides a transfer of industry knowledge. You will be surrounded by equally trained and enthusiastic Quorum practitioners, mentoring and coaching team members, and perfecting your project leadership and PMO skills.<br>
