<h2>Ad Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Associated Wholesale Grocers, Inc. (AWG) is the nation's largest cooperative food wholesaler to independently owned supermarkets, serving over 3,800 locations in more than half of the states in the country from ten distribution centers. In addition to its' cooperative wholesale operations, the company also operates subsidiary companies which provide real estate and supermarket development services, retail accounting, digital marketing services, and is a wholesale supply provider of health and beauty care, general merchandise, specialty/international foods and pharmaceutical supply. If you have a passion for retail, the drive to succeed in a fast-paced industry, we can't wait for you to join the team! Make us your Best Choice!</p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for profitable sales growth for AWG and our Super Ad Group Members. This sales position leverages scale to negotiate product costs, promotions, and other merchandising vehicles with AWG's vendor partners and works with AWG's Division teams to activate in the In-house Ad Group stores.</p>
<p><b>
Position Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years retail grocery experience or equivalent</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, verbal and written</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills Merchandising and/or sales experience preferred</li>
<li>Must be decision oriented and able to manage risk</li>
<li>Must be organized and able to coordinate numerous tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree and industry experience preferred including both Meat &amp; Grocery Department experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Leverage scale to negotiate improved costs of goods from vendors</li>
<li>Develop and implement promotions to maximize profitable sales for In-House Ad Group Members and AWG</li>
<li>Develop category promotion plans and work with AWG Category Sales and Division teams to activate in the In-house ad group stores</li>
<li>Develop sales promotions that allow market flexibility</li>
<li>Incorporate trade, consumer, shopper and national promotions into plans</li>
<li>Engage with Marketing to develop campaigns and incorporate other marketing tactics such as social and digital into plans</li>
<li>Manage vendor funding to maximize sales and profitability</li>
<li>Manage and/or oversee the timely and accurate input of promotions into the trade/promotional planning software</li>
<li>Work with 3rd party providers and be a subject matter expert on tools used in the promotional planning process</li>
</ul>
