<div>
About the Position:</div>
<div>
Ease is seeking an Accounts Receivable Specialist to join our Accounting and Finance team. The AR Specialist will be responsible for assisting the Revenue Manager in full cycle accounts receivable, collections, billing, refunds, discounts and credits, and ad hoc projects. This candidate should be energetic, a team player, and have knowledge of GAAP Accounting.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Assist in full cycle accounts receivable</li>
<li>
Work with Sales and Customer Success to ensure customer billing is accurate</li>
<li>
Record new customer accounts and customer adjustments, as required</li>
<li>
Maintain detailed reconciliation of various accounts and other schedules</li>
<li>
Provide detailed analyses and explanation of all transactions
</li>
<li>Provide support to the Revenue Manager and other key members of the Accounting team upon request</li>
<li>
Perform ad hoc projects as assigned by the Finance and Accounting department</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Experience/Skills Desired:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Accounting or Finance preferred</li>
<li>
Knowledge of GAAP Accounting preferred</li>
<li>
1 to 2 years' experience in related field</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Excel and Word</li>
<li>
Strong written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong organizational and analytical skills</li>
<li>
Ability to protect confidential information</li>
<li>
Strong ability to work both individually and in a team environment
</li>
</ul></div>
