<p>At <b>Perrin Tax and Accounting Services, Inc.,</b> you can be part of the fastest growing tax preparation company with a partnership business model that allows for unlimited earnings potential. We are a great team and looking for people who are go getters. This is in a way like a mini business for tax preparers.
