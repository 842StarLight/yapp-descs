<b>General Description and Essential Functions</b><br>
<br>
The Loan Operations Manager I leads the team supporting bank personnel with loan origination and/or servicing needs. The manager oversees the daily operations of the department to ensure that proper controls and processes are in place to meet service level agreements.<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Perform duties associated with managing and developing staff, including team building, mentoring, training and performance reviews.</li>
<li>Maintain quality control and ensure that all policies and procedures are followed</li>
<li>Produce and deliver reports to management and staff regarding productivity, quality and timeliness.</li>
<li>Provide quality customer service. This includes responding to internal customer inquiries, troubleshooting and resolving issues, both by telephone and email, in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Understand and process multiple types of inquiries in a professional, fast-paced, and high volume environment.</li>
<li>Operations contact for 3rd party vendor escalation issues.</li>
<li>Recognize opportunities and recommend changes to close gaps in processes.</li>
<li>Exercise discretion, strong interpersonal skills and independent judgment.</li>
<li>Act as a trusted resource for internal customers.</li>
<li>Drive change to improve efficiency and quality that ultimately reduces operating costs.</li>
<li>General understanding of regulatory requirements associated with consumer lending.</li>
<li>Understanding of various loan program/product requirements used by sales staff.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Qualifications/Experience</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent required. Bachelors degree preferred.</li>
<li>5+ years banking experience and 1-3 years of supervisory experience preferred; or demonstrated ability to perform the job functions.</li>
<li>Good working knowledge of Microsoft Office.</li>
<li>Detail oriented with leadership skills and capabilities.</li>
<li>Dependable and able to work within specific time constraints.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize multiple tasks in a deadline-driven environment; possess strong sense of urgency and responsiveness.</li>
<li>Advanced computer skills with the ability to assist in troubleshooting hardware/software issues.</li>
<li>Accurate data entry/transaction processing.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
</ul>
<br>
