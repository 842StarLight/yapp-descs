<h2>Office Manager/Bookkeeper - Perry County News</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Office Manager/Bookkeeper</b><br>
<b>Perry County News</b><br>
<b>Tell City, Indiana</b></p>
<p>
The Perry County News, a twice-weekly newspaper publishing on Monday and Thursday with over 4,500 circulation, is accepting applications for a full-time Office Manager/Bookkeeper. This position performs bookkeeping functions for Perry County News and two other weekly newspapers. The Bookkeeper also supervises customer service, including circulation duties, and oversees the front office.</p>
<p>
The Perry County News is located in Perry County, Indiana on the shores of the Ohio River. The newspaper offers a competitive benefits package including medical, dental, vision, life insurance, 401k and paid time off benefits. The Perry County News can also be found online at www.perrycountynews.com.</p>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent math and accounting skills</li>
<li>
Ability to perform tasks with a high degree of accuracy and thoroughness</li>
<li>
Experience with Excel, Word, and billing software</li>
<li>
3+ years bookkeeping experience including billing, cash receipts, deposit functions, and account reconciliations</li>
<li>
Excellent customer service skills; courteous and pleasant demeanor with the public</li>
<li>
Ability to assertively pursue collection of delinquent accounts</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task while maintaining a high degree of accuracy in a deadline driven environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for day-to-day management/supervision of classified/front desk/circulation and bookkeeping staff</li>
<li>
Ability to evaluate employee performance and make recommendations for improvement</li>
<li>
Record sales and cash receipt transactions timely and accurately, balancing and submitting all deposit transactions to company headquarters; process billings and adjustments through the SNAP and EIO systems as required</li>
<li>
 Accurately perform all bookkeeping functions in a timely manner according to LCNI's bookkeeping policies and practices, including account reconciliations, coding vendor invoices and timely submissions to company headquarters for payment</li>
<li>
Prepare, record and submit payroll to company headquarters by deadline and maintain confidentiality</li>
<li>
Handle account collections and credit approvals</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively with Publisher, other departments and with company headquarters</li>
</ul>
