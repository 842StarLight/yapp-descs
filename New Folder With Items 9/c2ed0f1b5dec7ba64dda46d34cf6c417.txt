<h2>Accounting Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>ABOUT US: </b></p>
<p>TargetPath is a consultancy that helps technology companies reach their potential faster with a higher probability of success. We provide a veteran team that works alongside the client to develop and execute business plan, go-to-market and operational strategy.</p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>As an Accounting Manager, you will supervise, monitor and evaluate all day-to-day accounting activities for TargetPath, Avocor, Austere and clients as needed. You will be responsible for processing periodic and ad hoc journal entries, account reconciliations, inventory reconciliations, general ledger maintenance, financial preparation, sales tax reporting, and other accounting responsibilities as assigned. Additionally, you will be establishing financial status by developing and implementing systems for collecting, analyzing, verifying and reporting financial information. This position is based locally in the TargetPath office in Wilsonville, OR.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan, implement and oversee overall accounting strategy</li>
<li>Oversee accounting daily operations</li>
<li>Take responsibility for accounts payable/receivable, cash receipts, check runs, general ledger coding, payroll, cash forecasting, bank account reconciliations, etc</li>
<li>Monitor and analyze accounting data</li>
<li>Set up and monitor accounting KPIs, present to stakeholders as needed</li>
<li>Inventory Accounting: using third party logistic provider or consignment partner reports and/or web portals, reconcile all transactions relating to inventory</li>
<li>Regularly produce financial reports or statements</li>
<li>Month end close management: bank reconciliations, inventory reconciliation, prepaids, rep commissions, reclass entries, capitalization and amortization, and chart of accounts maintenance</li>
<li>Adhere to proper accounting methods, policies and principles</li>
<li>Encourage other employees to adhere to standartds</li>
<li>Recruit and hire new employees, as well as providing training</li>
<li>Perform and oversee annual audits</li>
<li>Meet financial accounting objectives</li>
<li>Prepare weekly dashboards</li>
<li>W-9 management, 1099 preparation</li>
<li>Other accounting duties as needed</li>
</ul>
