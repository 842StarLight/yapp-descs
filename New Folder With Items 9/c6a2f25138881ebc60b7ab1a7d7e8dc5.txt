<div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
</div>
<div>
<p>Responsible for the strategic market and product development activities of the specified Carboline product line, along with advertising and promotions. Works closely with the Research &amp; Development laboratory, field sales representatives, technical service and sales management. Reports to Director – Global Product Line.</p>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements</b></p>
</div>
<div>
<p>4-year Business or Marketing degree or equivalent experience, minimum of 8 years marketing experience. Minimum of 5 years in Protective Coatings or Fireproofing industry.</p>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Physical Requirements</b></p>
</div>
<div>
<p>This position requires minimal physical activity but does require computer usage for an extended period of time – up to 8 hours in a day. No unusual environmental, lifting or exertion requirements are associated with this position. Will require occasional international or domestic travel up (30 – 40%).</p>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Overall management of the specified product line on a global basis.</li>
<li>Achieve annual sales and margin objectives.</li>
<li>Develop and carry out plans for promotion of the product line into the field.</li>
<li>Identify new strategic markets and applications for the product line.</li>
<li>Design promotional and marketing strategies to enhance the product line’s strength in the market.</li>
<li>Assist in managing the advertising, promotional and trade show functions by providing input on products and markets.</li>
<li>Arrange and follow-up on demonstrations of any newly developed product.</li>
<li>Investigate and recommend future improvements to the product line assure its superiority to its competition.</li>
<li>Acts as project leader for new product development projects as well as the primary liaison between Sales and R&amp;D in terms of prioritization of product development and/or testing needs.
</li>
<li>Act as Project Coordinator on major initiatives and follow-up on priorities.</li>
<li>Take on special assignments as directed by management.</li>
<li>Assist as needed in making joint calls with field reps to help develop buying and non-buying accounts.</li>
<li>Become involved in professional societies to promote the product line.</li>
<li>Ensure that Total Quality policies and procedures are met.</li>
<li>Committed to the Company’s safety and quality programs.</li>
</ul></div>
