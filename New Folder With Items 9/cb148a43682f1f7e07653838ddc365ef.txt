<h2>Sr. Relationship Associate - Sr. Teller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY
</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for completing all types of client transactions relating to existing accounts in an accurate, courteous and efficient manner. Knowledgeably addresses client questions and concerns. Identifies opportunities for cross-selling services and products and makes appropriate referrals.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides consistent level of high quality client service that exceeds our clientsâ€™ expectations</li>
<ul>
<li>Maintains a minimum of 95% score on all mystery shops</li>
</ul>
<li>Operates under assigned cash limits and processes client deposits, withdrawals, transfers, payments, and account maintenance activities efficiently and accurately</li>
<li>Places holds on transactions as determined by Bank procedures</li>
<li>Executes account stop payments as determined by Bank procedures</li>
<li>Processes daily mail, night drop and ATM transactions in dual custody as assigned, and balances ATM when applicable</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability in providing a great customer experience by engaging customers in conversation and educating customers on the various products and services available to them</li>
<li>Issues negotiable instruments in accordance with the Bank Secrecy Act.</li>
<li>Introduce customers to new products and services, and generate leads for other members of the sales team to close</li>
<li>Daily uses Transaction Management Systems (TMS)</li>
<li>Encodes and scans branch transactions each day</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Executes financial transactions in accordance with bank policies and procedures while maintaining an acceptable record in daily drawer balancing</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Prepares Currency Transaction Reports and Suspicious Activity Concern Reports as needed</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Effectively executes all service and referral routines to deepen client relationships</li></ul>
<br>
