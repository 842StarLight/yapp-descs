<h2>Senior Category Merchant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Description</b><div><b>
Position Summary:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This Senior Category Merchant (SCM) is responsible for Business Essentials categories in our US Retail Stores (USR) business. Plus, the SCM manages a Product Specialist and Project Specialist.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The SCM is responsible for achieving financial objectives by developing and executing a customer-centric strategy. This strategy will be inspired by deep customer insight and will include assortment planning, merchandise and vendor selection, negotiation, financial planning, supply chain and inventory turn implications, advertising plans, merchandise presentation, distribution, vendor relations and administration for assigned lines.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Primary Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Acquire a deep understanding of customer needs through research efforts, and field feedback.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop a realistic and effective business plan to achieve budgeted goals in sales, margin, and inventory. Execute that plan to deliver financial results.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop, execute, and effectively communicate strategy to the highest levels of the company. Ensure plans align with company strategic objectives and goals.</div></li>
<li><div>
Choose assortment items and vendors, influence pricing, and create an elegant customer experience.</div></li>
<li><div>
Negotiate for lower total delivered cost, volume incentives, rebates, advertising and other applicable allowances.</div></li>
<li><div>
Review and approve financial and item forecasts with Planners.</div></li>
<li><div>
Effectively partner and influence other cross-functional partners, such as Pricing, Site Merchandising, Marketing, Portfolio, Inventory + Planning, and more.</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage support team</div></li>
</ul></div>
