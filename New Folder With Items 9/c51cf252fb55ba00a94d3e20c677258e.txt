<h2>Leasing Specialist: Salish Flats</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Title: </b>Leasing Specialist</p>
<p><b>Status: </b>Full-Time</p>
<p><b>Reports to:</b> Property Manager</p>
<p><b>Property: </b>Salish Flats Apartments in Airway Heights, WA</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strategically caters each sales interaction (using location, building/unit amenities, etc) to target client’s preferences.</li>
<li>Implement creative sales techniques and relationship building with prospects to achieve high occupancy, meet sales goals, and encourage resident retention.</li>
<li>Stays abreast of competitor performance and offerings through conscientious monthly market survey completion.</li>
<li>Responsible for outreach marketing implementation as directed.</li>
<li>Processes all move-in applications and sign all leases with new residents.</li>
<li>Creates positive, welcoming, supportive environment for co-workers, residents, visitors, and property associates.</li>
<li>Treats all interactions with residents (prospective/current), visitors, and employees as an opportunity.</li>
<li>Complete maintenance call backs and complete outstanding work orders in OneSite software.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma (or equivalent education) required.</li>
<li>Sales, leasing, or marketing experience.</li>
<li>Excellent time management, organizational and written skills.</li>
<li>Enthusiasm, good attitude, trustworthiness, personal integrity and honesty.</li>
<li>Good human relation skills demonstrating the ability to deal with residents, peers and supervisors.</li>
<li>Able to work in fast-paced environment while remaining conscientious and adhering to policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Knowledge of computer software specifically Microsoft Office suite. Ability to learn property management software (Onesite) quickly.</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver’s license and pass criminal background check.</li>
<li>Some weekend coverage will be required.</li>
</ul>
