<h2>Inside Sales Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>GET TO KNOW ALORICA<br>
At Alorica, we only do one thing – make lives better, one interaction at a time. We’re a global leader in customer service and experience, serving the world’s biggest brands with tens of thousands of employees in hundreds of locations around the globe.<br>
<br>
JOB SUMMARY<br>
Seeking highly motivated individuals to join our team as Inside Sales Account Managers</div>
<div>The Store Account Manager for SMB is part of the Online selling team but focuses their efforts on selling hardware and software/cloud solutions to small medium and sometimes enterprise customers. In this role you will have to be able to flex your style to handle proactive volume with new business, but also act as account manager for MS unmanaged customers and drive repeat business through managing strong relationships with our customers.</div>
<div>This requires the ability to work independently and with great maturity ensuring customers are advised correctly on their product, their orders are put through and arriving at the customer in a timely fashion. The Store Advocate SMB has a passion for Microsoft Surface family as well as HoloLens as in many regions we are the sole trader of this amazing product. The SMB Advocate is an evangelist and relationship manager at the same time, well versed in dealing with procurement and marketing teams alike at the customer side.<br>
<br>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES<br>
Inbound / Outbound Sales Calls
</div>
<div>Communicate with customers effectively through Phone and Email interactions</div>
<div>Work in a fast-paced, collaborative and dynamic teaming environment &amp; effectively manage opportunities and pipeline through the sales cycle</div>
<div>Obsess over Microsoft’s customers and prospects to deliver a world-class customer engagement experience</div>
