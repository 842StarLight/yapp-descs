<p>Senior Account Executive – West/Midwest</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
As the leading custom payable solution for the Real Estate industry, Nexus is a high-growth software company continuing our expansion. We are looking for an experienced software Account Executive to be part of an aggressive and dynamic sales team focused exclusively on Commercial and Multi-Family Real Estate companies.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
You will be an active advocate within the marketplace of one of the most widely accepted, productive, and reliable Procure to Pay solutions available. This is a net new sales opportunity so the ability to effectively follow-up on marketing leads, build rapport, collaborate with internal resources and develop business quickly is a must. You’ll have state-of-the art tools, unmatched marketing support, and training to reach your goals.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Designated Market: Denver, CO; Phoenix, AZ; Salt Lake City, UT; or Las Vegas, NV – you must live in one of these markets to be considered for this role</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive new sales by qualifying inbound leads while simultaneously hunting for new opportunities</li>
<li>Executing all phases of the sales cycle including onsite demonstrations and webinars</li>
<li>Identify sale opportunities, develop a robust pipeline, track sales status, and generate sales reports using Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Prepare and negotiate customer proposals and demonstrate the extraordinary value proposition of Nexus' software products through compelling presentations, software demonstrations and case studies</li>
<li>Leverage experience to accelerate sales cycle and tap into new verticals</li>
<li>Channel customer feedback to marketing and development teams, and support sales and organizational team initiatives</li>
<li>Travel to local and non-local client sites, industry and trade shows up to 50%</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>BS or BA required</li>
<li>Must be able to travel up to 50%</li>
<li>Solid 5+ year track record of exemplary sales achievement of above quota performance and developing new business</li>
<li>Mid-level, mid-market with experience with business applications software environment, preferably SaaS, capable of complex sales</li>
<li>Successful track record of closing medium to large software deals</li>
<li>Real Estate and Finance Sales experience a plus</li>
<li>Outstanding interpersonal and oral communication skills with an enthusiastic team-oriented attitude</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to learn and to apply technical information quickly</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce is preferred</li>
<li>Aggressive, great attitude, organizational skills and a self-starter</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Benefits and Salary:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive salary plus commission commensurate with experience and ability</li>
<li>Unlimited PTO, comprehensive benefits package and 401(K) with company match</li>
<li>Fertile training ground for career development with a fast-growing company</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>This is a 100% remote position</p>
