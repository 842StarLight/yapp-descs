<h2>Marketing Assistant, Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Position Title: </b>Strategic Project Manager, Sales Planning
</div>
<div>
<b>Location: </b>Atlanta, GA</div>
<div>
<b>Reports to: </b>Director, Sales Planning<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>#LI-CF1</div>
<div>
The Sales Planning – Strategic Project Manager will identify and manage the implementation of strategic division-driven initiatives with the ultimate goal of engineering long-term sustainable top and bottom-line sales growth. These projects will focus on process efficiency and effectiveness, cross-functional collaboration, and change management.
</div>
<div>Additionally, the role will serve as a liaison between division leadership and field sales teams by providing two-way feedback on programs, initiatives, and results thereby driving better execution and sustainability of new initiatives and standard processes. The role will require the direct development of tools and tracking mechanisms.</div>
<div>Specific Responsibilities
</div>
<div><b>Core Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Understands the scope of programs as defined by the business requirements.
</li>
<li>Defines key planning milestones</li>
<li>
Identifies, confirms, and obtains participation from required cross-functional teams. Ensures team clearly understands roles and responsibilities for the duration of the program.</li>
<li>
Creates and executes program plans. Facilitates and drives the core team to identify and schedule all the work for end-to-end deployment required to fulfill program scope.
</li>
<li>Identifies goals and success criteria, drives the development and implementation of key goals, objectives, and success criteria for the program with key stakeholders and core team. Raises conflicts and resolves with the appropriate level of management engagement to build consensus. Obtains and assigns resources to the program plan.</li>
<li>
Analyzes areas of risk to avoid risky activities by identifying and managing critical paths and risk areas.</li>
<li>
Establishes contingency plans and identifies trigger events and assumes responsibility for initiation corrective actions.</li>
<li>
Tracks and manages the program schedule and upstream and downstream dependencies. Analyzes the actual performance against the plan and makes adjustments consistent with plan objectives.</li>
<li>
Drives action item resolution and track completion. Ensures program milestones area accomplished and ensures overall committed timelines are met. Documents all major plan changes in scope, approach, and date delivery.</li>
<li>
Communicates updates on major milestones and keeps all stakeholders informed of progress and issues. Identifies and manages proposed changes to program parameters and works with Division leadership to ensure appropriate processes and communications are followed.</li>
<li>
Monitors and ensures the quality of program deliverables.
</li>
<li>Uses independent judgment and discretion in matters of significance.</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
