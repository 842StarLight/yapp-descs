<div>
Essential Job Functions</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Track and report sales data, including updating sales totals on sales board and logging cancellations daily</li>
<li>Process sales orders and account changes</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and detailed notes in our CRM in a timely manner</li>
<li>Maintain and update the sales team’s SharePoint, including the convention calendar, and monthly sales calendar</li>
<li>Monitor generic sales email and phone queue and distribute leads/calls to appropriate account executive or sales development representative depending on situation</li>
<li>Register ITC for industry memberships and trade shows, including booking travel and accommodations as needed</li>
<li>Order and maintain sales supplies and trade show items, including shipping to trade shows and inspecting items when returned</li>
<li>Other support tasks as needed like expense reports and handling account executive’s calls</li>
<li>Monitor customer contact reports.</li>
<li>Reach out to clients via phone and email to build a positive working relationship.</li>
<li>Provide excellent service and foster client satisfaction.</li>
<li>Identify and process up sell opportunities.</li>
<li>Increase customer adoption and retention through follow up calls to achieve company revenue and retention goals.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and detailed notes in our CRM and enter all interaction daily. All notes should be filled out neatly, accurately, and timely.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<div>
<ul>
<li>Professional, upbeat and helpful attitude</li>
<li>Highly motivated and disciplined self-starter</li>

<li>Organized and detail oriented in time and task management</li>
<li>Exemplary written and oral communications skills</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office suite of products</li>
</ul>
<p>You’d be a <b>good </b>fit if you</p>
<ul>
<li>Can prioritize and manage multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>Have prior administration or sales support experience.</li>
<li>Have empathy, patience, and consistency to handle different personalities and provide the same level of service and knowledge regardless.</li>
<li>Have excellent communication skills to convey your meaning clearly and positively.</li>
<li>Have a cheerful attitude and strong worth ethic to meet team goals.</li>
<li>Enjoy talking to clients on the phone.</li>
<li>Are curious to learn about the businesses of our clients and like to discover opportunities where we can help them improve.</li>
<li>Can work with ITC’s different departments to coordinate client needs and help streamline processes.</li>
</ul>
<p>You’d be a <b>great </b>fit if you have</p>

<li>Outgoing personality with ability to develop relationships over the phone.</li>
<li>Can anticipate team needs and provide a high level of service.</li>
<li>Ability to successfully collaborate with different personality types and communicate with all levels of the company.</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure.</li>
