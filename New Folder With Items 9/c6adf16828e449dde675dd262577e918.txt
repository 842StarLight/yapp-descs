Who We Are</b></p>

<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role</b></p>
<p>
The new Leader will be comfortable within an evolving organization. S/he will develop, drive and implement the sales strategy and direct the management of all opportunities with global Enterprise clients to meet quarterly targets within each region. As a proven sales leader s/he will be responsible to motivate and drive the Global field sales teams to achieve company and sales objectives.</p>
<p><b>
The Vice President of Sales, Data Analytics will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide leadership to team of 50+ on overall sales strategy, cost optimization, and disciplined process management (pipeline review, asset management, sales achievement, etc.) to ensure growth of a platform business, increasing market share and profitable revenue growth.</li>
<li>
Be a key member of the executive management team. Contribute as a key member of the leadership team and provide counsel and input on strategy overall business evolution.</li>
<li>
Be accountable for business growth and achieving overall metrics (revenue, retention, cost control, customer satisfaction, workforce dashboards, etc.).</li>
<li>
Drive, support and influence BU business models, go-to-market strategies, and sales/marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>
Ensure optimum sales coverage through direct and partner sales resources.</li>
<li>
Coach and support sales teams and leadership in developing key and/or difficult account opportunities.</li>
<li>
Manage across the complex organization to achieve results for Micro Focus, partners, and clients.</li>
<li>
Provide on the ground support to the team to help negotiate and close deals.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate thought leadership by directing the customer's application of technology to new business problems.</li>
<li>
Create a performance driven culture.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate in-depth knowledge of the vertical segment and provide external leadership to industry community, and press.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Candidate Profile
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>In terms of the performance and personal competencies required for the position, we would highlight the following:</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Business Acumen</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 10+ years’ sales management experience – with P&amp;L responsibility - working direct with teams consisting of sales directors, sales managers, sales representatives, and field marketing.</li>
<li>
Experience leading a proactive sales team and growing market share.</li>
<li>
Verifiable track record of success as an executive sales leader, especially managing large deal pursuits involving multiple solution components and partners in support.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Executing for Results</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proven success in scaling a sales force in a large technology business. Preferably with experience growing a revenue stream of $60+ million and 70+ sales professionals.</li>
<li>
Proven experience selling enterprise software solutions.</li>
<li>
A passion and proven ability to implement world-class sales processes, productivity improvements, and training programs throughout a sales organization.</li>
<li>
Highly organized, assertive, and self-directed business style, with a strong ability to prioritize.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Leading Teams</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
History of retaining, developing, motivating, and mentoring a sales force capable of setting and meeting ambitious sales goals.</li>
<li>
Individual must be a team player, works effectively in a team-based organization, collaborates cross-functionally, and builds alignment around goals and objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Building Relationships and Using Influence</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Possess a strong work ethic, willing to go the extra miles, hands on yet can delegate well.</li>
<li>
Ability to push back and be aggressive in order to operate at senior levels and influence at all functions of the company.</li>
<li>
Outstanding communication skills, both written and verbal. Must have the proven ability to deliver concise, logical, written documents and the presence to deliver the company’s vision and product definitions to both large and small audiences.</li>
</ul>
