<h2>Senior HRIS Analyst (Workday)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Enterprise Holdings is the largest car rental provider in the world as measured by revenue and fleet. The company and its affiliate Enterprise Fleet Management – which combined offer a total transportation solution that includes extensive car rental and car-sharing services, truck rental, corporate fleet management and retail car sales – accounted for $24.1 billion in revenue and operated 2 million vehicles throughout the world in 2018. Enterprise Holdings’ annual revenues also place it near the top of the global travel industry, exceeding all other rental car companies, many airlines, and most cruise lines, hotels, tour operators and online travel agencies. Enterprise Holdings’ regional subsidiaries and Enterprise Fleet Management currently employ more than 100,000 people worldwide.<br>
<br>
Through its integrated global network of independent regional subsidiaries and franchises, Enterprise Holdings operates the Enterprise Rent-A-Car, National Car Rental and Alamo Rent A Car brands at more than 10,000 fully staffed neighborhood and airport locations. The Enterprise Holdings global network operates in more than 90 countries and territories, including North America, Central America, South America, the Caribbean and Europe, as well as parts of Asia-Pacific and the Middle East. Today, the company’s three brands serve more than 95 percent of the worldwide car rental market.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Senior HRIS Analyst represents the HRIS function globally, acting as a focal point between HR business partners, IT partners, technical integration owners, and internal and external parties on routine production support and recurring special and small-to-large scale projects. The Senior HRIS Analyst is a subject matter expert (SME) on a particular HCM system work-stream and provides support and acts as a back-up for other work-stream(s) as needed. This role is accountable for research, analysis, interpretation and documentation of business requirements as well as prototyping, testing, validating, deploying, training and supporting feasible functional solutions for the HCM system. This role involves project planning and administration of individual work items as well as support of company-wide HR initiatives.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Act as a project resource or project lead on assigned HR technology projects including management of project team activities, tasks, deliverables, timelines, and risks; Create project plans or task lists to inclusive of minor and major milestones, establish roles and responsibilities, execute and manage on small to medium scale projects (i.e. internal production support fixes, minor or major release cycles, system enhancements or special HR system projects)</li>
<li>
Lead and facilitate meetings with stakeholders (core team, vendor(s), management) to collect business and/or technical requirements and use cases; understand business, operational, and downstream system impact of issue or request, and translate requirements into feasible HCM system solutions</li>
<li>
Liaise between internal business and technical partners, as well as internal and external vendor(s) to identify, interpret and document HR business requirements and functional specifications; ensure issue(s) and resolution option(s) are understood clearly</li>
<li>
Own workstream area end-to end including research of overall system impact of proposed solutions (to other workstreams, downstream applications and integrations), configuration, staging of test data, execution and analysis of test results and system regression testing; Coordinate demos and playback sessions with stakeholders to ensure system solution offered meets business needs</li>
<li>
Research, analyze and troubleshoot simple to complex issues, not limited to own workstream; Independently leverage all tools and resources available (HCM learning and community resources, implementation tenants, etc) keeping global solutions in mind; Recommend and implement alternate, corrective and preventative/permanent solutions; Identify potential problems that may arise and communicate how problems will affect business</li>
<li>
Plan for, create, maintain, and execute test plans and QAT/UAT test scenarios, to ensure a comprehensive approach that covers regression testing, downstream systems / applications, and new end user functionality</li>
<li>
Provide input and/or develop HRIS processes, user guides, procedures and process documentation based on deployed solutions, system features or functionality; Provide information about how requirements may affect functional design documents; Lead training activities and demos with peer workstream leads, business and IT partners, and the change management team, cross-training on other areas of support</li>
<li>
Tier 3 support for internal customer support team and technical integration owners on system or data issues that required in-depth research and analysis for interim or permanent solutions; Research opportunities to minimize complexity by leveraging current systems investments and seeking out cost effective solutions</li>
<li>
Document, review, and propose solutions for identified gaps to ensure HR system meets business needs</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledge of HRIS technology and HR policies, processes and procedures; Presents information regarding enhancements and tracks and responds to questions from business and HRIS team members; Maintains HCM systems knowledge by seeking continuous training and education to stay current on functionality, enhanced features and compliance</li>
</ul>
