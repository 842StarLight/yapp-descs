<h2>Director of Digital Engagement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<p>Position Summary:<br>
The Director of Enterprise Digital Consumer Platforms is responsible for the planning, development, and delivery of our digital properties and customer experiences. The individual will insure the integration of various digital tools and platforms to insure a seamless and impactful patient, member and customer experience. Further, the individual will work closely with marketing, brand management, internal and external communication, creative design and other marketing stakeholder groups to insure a consistent experience across touchpoints. The position reports to the VP, Enterprise Marketing and Communications and ensures that project/department milestones and goals are met and adhering to approved budgets. The position requires management of various domain experts and receives inputs from multiple stakeholders from both Carle and Health Alliance.The individual must be capable of developing and managing relationships with peers, department leaders, technical experts (eg, IT, Finance, etc), and developers within Carle and Health Alliance. The individual will be responsible for the development of both creative and technical developers with a spirit of collaboration and dedication to timely delivery of products and features.
</p>
</div>
<div>

<b>EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS</b><br>
Bachelors Related Field. Masters preferred.<br>
<br>
<b>CERTIFICATION &amp; LICENSURE REQUIREMENTS</b><br>
None specified<br>
<br>
<b>EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS</b><br>
Ten (10) years Related Field<br>
<br>
<b>OTHER REQUIREMENTS</b><br>
Ability to partner with operational and clinical teams and marketing teams to effectively and efficiently delivery products; Ability to supervise and train employees, to include organizing, prioritizing and scheduling work assignments; Dynamic, versatile and capable of exploring new concepts and impactful designs; Strong problem solving and decision-making skills; Ability to multitask and effectively lead a creative team; Work collaboratively in a team-oriented environment; Ability to lead projects from concepts to completion; Thrive in a fast-paced, high job volume environment.
</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>Essential Functions:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute on the strategy for digital properties, including key performance indicators and other metrics for monitoring progress</li>
<li>
Develop business objectives for digital services and prioritize activities based on the potential impact on sales, service, cost, and timing and represent efforts to leadership, patients, customers, and other stakeholders</li>
<li>
Collaborate with operational and clinical leadership, strategic marketing, and sales groups to deliver customized user experiences based on customer profiles, role, and location.</li>
<li>
Insure internal and external engagement through expert groups, focus groups, usability studies, and customer feedback</li>
<li>
Represent the voice of the consumer/professional to in the digital experience to insure a seamless and positive experience within and across properties.</li>
<li>
Manage and integrate disparate expectations from multiple internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Maintain a global perspective with respect to web, portals, data sharing, CRM, apps, programming software, and other key digital modalities, regardless of departmental ownership, and drive initiatives to minimize development costs and improve user experiences.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with and stimulate thinking of operational leaders, strategy and marketing leadership, sales, IT groups, other other key stakeholder groups of Carle and Health Alliance to provide competitive and disruptive digital offerings consistent with our mission and vision.</li>
<li>
Assumes responsibility for personnel related duties that include interviewing, disciplinary actions, payroll reporting, scheduling paid leave, and enforcing requires rules and regulations. Monitors employee performance and ensures timely communications for the purpose of goal setting, performance evaluations, wages increases and continued personnel actions.</li>
</ul>
