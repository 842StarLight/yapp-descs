<h2>Office Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">General Office Clerk
<br><br>
Summary
<br>Assists Office Personnel and Office Manager by performing the following duties.
<br>Essential Duties
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Provides support to the Accounting Department.</li>
<li>Processes transactions, issues checks, and updates ledgers, etc.</li>
<li>Compiles records and reports.</li>
<li>Closes out daily work orders</li>
<li>Enters warranty claims into manufacturer system, resubmit claims when requried and ensure appropriate action taken.</li>
<li>Enters payments and other data into accounting system.</li>
<li>Prepare and maintain all warranty documents according to distributor and manufacturer requirement.</li>
<li>Reconciles invoices and schedules.</li>
<li>Greets and assists visitors at counter.</li>
<li>Provides information to customers, claimants, employees and sales personnel.</li>
<li>Handles communication with clients and vendors via phone, email, and in-person.</li>
<li>Receives, counts and pays out cash.</li>
<li>Prepare daily bank deposits and change orders.</li>
<li>Prepares stock inventory.</li>
<li>Operates office machines such as computer, adding machine, calculator and copier.</li>
<li>Performing basic office tasks, such as filing, data entry, answering phones, processing the mail, etc.</li>
<li>Assists with purchasing supplies for multiple departments.</li>
<li>Prepares paperwork for vehicle titling and registration.</li>
<li>Maintains professional appearance and neat work area.</li>
<li>Other tasks as assigned.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
<br><br>
General Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional time management and verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Proficiency with computers and bookkeeping software, strong typing skills.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and remain motivated and positive.</li>
<li>Commitment to working efficiently and accurately.</li>
<li>Ability to recognize accounting errors and correct them.</li>
</ul>
Education and/or Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Two to four years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED); or one to three months related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>One year certificate from college or technical school; or three to six months related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. .</li>
</ul>
Mathematical Skills
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Good math skills and the ability to spot numerical error</li>
<li>Familiarity with basic Accounting principles.</li>
<li>Ability to add, subtract, multiply and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions and decimals.</li>
<li>Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, interest, commissions, proportions, and percentages.</li>
</ul>
Reasoning Ability
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out detailed but uninvolved written or oral instructions.</li>
<li>Ability to use problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work closely with others.</li>
</ul>
Physical Demands
<br><br>
Some lifting and carrying will be required within reason of a typical office. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
<br><br>
Work Environment
<br><br>
Job requires sitting for long periods of time, often sitting in front of a desk or computer. Moving around dealership as needed to perform daily tasks. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</div>
