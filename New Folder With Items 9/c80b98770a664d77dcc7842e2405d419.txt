<h2>Operations Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SunBug Solar is a full-service solar energy company that designs and installs solar systems for a diverse customer base of homeowners, businesses and non-profits clients.</p>
<p>We provide an interesting and fun place to work. As a team, we demand a level of excellence and responsiveness from each other and take great pride in being the highest rated solar provider in Massachusetts.</p>
<p>SunBug’s Ops team is the backbone of our organization. Ops is responsible for supporting all the different teams at SunBug Solar. Qualified candidates for this position are extremely detail oriented, hard-working, have diverse experience handling complicated paperwork with deadlines, are motivated self-starters and are eager to learn the solar trade.</p>
<p>Primary responsibility for all paperwork necessary for each project:</p>
<ul><li>Permitting</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Ensures that all necessary documentation for permits are in place in Filemaker (Equipment cut sheets, layout drawings, one-line drawings…)</li>
<li>Prepare building permit</li>
<li>Utility interconnections</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Manage simplified and expedited interconnection applications/approvals, which means tracking the complicated process from beginning to end.</li>
<li>SMART registrations: Manage SMART paperwork registration and track process pre and post installation.</li>
<li>Project documentation status: Keep project documentation current so that it is clear to everyone working on the project what the status is and flag jobs that are ready to be built.</li>
<li>Residential Sales support: Running roof efficiency analyses through irradiance software tools. Assisting sales staff with contracts and change orders as required</li>
</ul>
<p>Assisting sales staff in documenting contracted projects as required.<br>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Relevant experience with administrative process and software</li>
<li>Strong computer and data entry skills are important</li>
<li>Demonstrated capacity to take responsibility and initiative with minimal supervision</li>
<li>After a period of training and learning, has the ability to determine, propose and propel to the next step forward in a complicated process</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
</div>
