<h2>Used Car Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>A major Louisville New Vehicle Dealership has an immediate opening at for a Used Car Sales Manager with extensive used car sales management and inventory experience in a highly competitive metro market. Inventory management, buying, auction experience, training and desking deals experience a must.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities </b><i><b>(include but are not limited to): </b></i></p>
<ul>
<li>Desk deals and coordinate used vehicle sales activities.</li>
<li>Attend vehicle auctions, purchase and recondition used vehicle inventory.</li>
<li>Meet monthly forecast for sales volume and sufficient gross to meet profit objectives.</li>
<li>Recruit, hire and develop a retail sales force.</li>
<li>Establish a positive relationship with customers.</li>
<li>Maintain vehicle inventory and days’ supply.</li>
<li>Help to ensure that salespeople are informed of all new inventory and current advertising efforts, including rebates and incentives.</li>
<li>Appraise incoming used vehicles.</li>
<li>Enthusiastically assist sales people in customer interactions.</li>
<li>Enhance customer satisfaction during the entire purchase experience.</li>
<li>Supervise sales associates to ensure individual and department sales quotas are met.</li>
<li>Partner with the Financial Services Department to develop and coordinate best practices for the most efficient and effective sales approach.</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned by management.</li>
<li>Demonstrates behaviors consistent with the Company’s Values in all interactions with customers, co-workers and vendors.</li>
</ul>

<li>Must have exceptional inventory management software skills, V-Auto / Homenet</li>
<li>Must consent to pre-employment drug screen</li>
<li>Steady work and performance history</li>
<li>Outgoing personality with expertise at developing relationships (i.e., a “people person")</li>

</ul>
