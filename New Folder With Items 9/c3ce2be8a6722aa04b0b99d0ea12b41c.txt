<h2>Training Professional (Learning and Development Training Specialist)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>General Description</b>:</p>
<p>The Training Professional occupies a key role as the initial facilitator/leader for OptimumCXs new Agents. Training the New Hire program, teaching and validating requisite skills and behaviors with consistency are key job functions. In addition to facilitating new hire classes, the Training Professional facilitates ongoing and enhancement training and may be assigned for specialized training projects. The Training Professional is expected to maintain expert status in the areas taught, and will regularly do so by handling routine customer interactions. The Training Professional will collaborate with enterprise and corporate training resources to provide feedback on products and suggestions as to how to improve learning effectiveness.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Training Professional Primary Job Functions</b>:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Thoroughly reviews all necessary materials to ensure a successful learning experience.</li>
<li>Facilitates classroom training to teach program product knowledge, call handling skills, customer service/sales skills and program process and procedures.</li>
<li>Assists Operations Director to prepare and deliver training on the floor that will improve the agents skills, knowledge and performance. Follows training class to Grad School when not engaged in other training.</li>
<li>Work with Corporate training to create training materials (Leaders guides, participant materials, job aids) as needed.</li>
<li>Gather, document and distribute clear information from updates received by corporate training and the client.</li>
<li>Attend regular training meetings, respond to emails and phone calls to ensure all program information is up to date and documented.</li>
<li>Monitors agent calls and agent performance to identify training gaps.</li>
<li>Responsible for submitting, channeling, and documenting all recommendations and/or revisions from the call center to the program Account Executive or Corporate Training professional.</li>
<li>Coordinate efforts with the Quality Team to assist in the quality improvement process of the agents.</li>
<li>Participate in calibration meetings with the Client, QA and OCX management team to ensure the information being trained is up-to-date.</li>
<li>Maintain class attendance and performance records of agents in training.</li>
<li>Complete OCX required Training.</li>
<li>When class is not in session, dedicates at least 2 consecutive hours weekly to taking/making program calls to remain up-to-date and in touch with program training needs. This requirement may be increased by client requirements or business needs.</li>
<li>Completes Tools and Techniques for Trainers.</li>
<li>Attend Day zero meetings and participate in NHO facilitation.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Training Professional Position Requirements</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal communication skills for training delivery; must have a dynamic and engaging classroom presence</li>
<li>Excellent written communication skills are required; must be able to write in a grammatically correct, clear and concise manner</li>
<li>Displays innovative, out of the box thinking to strategically solve problems</li>
<li>Helpful and attentive, displaying empathy at appropriate times</li>
<li>Must be proficient with word processing (MS Word), spreadsheet (Excel) and presentation (PowerPoint) software programs</li>
<li>Experience in designing and developing training material is preferred but not required, for assisting with the development of training manuals, job aids, tests, and classroom exercises</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills, the ability to perform effectively as a member of a team and ability to travel</li>
<li>Excellent consulting skills, (e.g., critical thinking, problem-solving, decision making, and influencing</li>
<li>Ability to creatively use resources and adjust to changes quickly and professionally</li>
<li>Ability to work independently with minimal supervision, but also in a team environment</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to monitor the method of communication and adjust as needed based on to whom and what they are communicating</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize assignments and projects based on importance and customer expectations</li>
<li>Must be able to pass a background check</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Training Professional Qualifications</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Must be at least 18 years of age</li>
<li>Must have a H.S. Diploma/GED or beyond</li>
<li>One to two years progressively responsible experience in a contact center environment</li>
<li>Candidates will have a combination of education and experience in applying adult education theories and the principles of systematic design of training and evaluation</li>
<li>Successful experience training adults</li>
<li>Ability to work multiple shifts as required</li>
<li>Open to a 45-50 hour work week</li>
<li>Completed training certification program(s)</li>
<br>
</ul>
