<h2>IT Support Technician (Part Time)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The IT Support Technician plays an integral part providing technical assistance and support to Restaurants, Field Management Teams and Central Support Office (CSO) personnel. This role provides level 1 support to all users of Noodles &amp; Company technology, escalating to other technical and management resources within the IT Department as needed to resolve immediate issues and eliminate future incidents. This job includes providing desktop support, POS troubleshooting, installation and deployment of workstations, application troubleshooting, printer and phone support.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Research, diagnose, resolve (root-cause) and document issues with all systems – Restaurant, CSO, desktop, laptop, mobile, etc</li>
<li>
Provide guidance on use of IT systems including workstations and network access</li>
<li>
Hardware installation and software configuration for new systems using configuration/hardening standards including documentation</li>
<li>
Responsible for the correct operation and monitoring of end-user systems</li>
<li>
Maintain inventory of computer parts and log of all repairs/service performed, including tracking of equipment replacements</li>
<li>
Execute projects as outlined by department leadership</li>
<li>
Most working hours will occur during the timeframes deemed “on-call” (evenings and weekends) – focused on emergencies (i.e. restaurant system down, critical system (email, accounting, reporting, etc) failure) – addressing lower priority incidents as time allows</li>
<li>
Execute processes for compliance with external regulations (PCI, SOX, etc) and internal standards</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
