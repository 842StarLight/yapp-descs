JOB DESCRIPTION</div>
<div>
<div>SUMMARY</div>
<div></div>
<div>The primary purpose of this position is to learn all aspects of operating a retail store. The incumbent is also required to perform all tasks in a safe manner consistent with corporate policies and state and federal laws.</div>
<div></div>
<div>RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
 Attend all Management Development Program (MDP) training classes.<br>
 Learn about all of the possible situations that can occur in a retail store to prepare for the<br>
 Assistant Store Manager or Store Manager position. Assist the Store Manager with the operation of the retail store and complete his/her duties in his/her absence.<br>
 Enforce all policies and procedures to ensure excellent customer service is provided and to maximize the P&amp;L performance of the store.<br>
 Ensure the appearance of the store is pleasing to the customer and meets the requirements of the corporate office.<br>
 Ensure the store opens and closes at the appropriate time.<br>
 Ensure the proper procedures are followed for cash transactions and bank deposits.<br>
 Interact with vendors to order ad, seasonal, and basic merchandise and ice cream (where applicable) for the store.<br>
 Ensure all merchandise is set up according to plan-o-grams received from the corporate office.<br>
 Execute weekly sales ads and price changes.<br>
 Process recalled, damaged, outdated, and transferred merchandise.<br>
 Receive merchandise deliveries from vendors and Rite Aid distribution centers.<br>
 Verify vendor invoice information is accurate and enter it into the accounts payable system.<br>
 Prepare the retail store for physical inventory by ensuring merchandise on the sales floor and in the stock room is in order and easy to access.<br>
 Analyze operating reports and make recommendations for improvement.<br>
 Utilize Staffworks® software to complete the associate work schedule.<br>
 Remain knowledgeable of the One-Hour Photo department and film processing, if applicable.<br>
 Assist the Pharmacy department when there is a high volume of customers. Request store maintenance when required.<br>
 Assist with the general maintenance of the store, both inside and outside.<br>
 Enforce all fire and safety rules and regulations set by the company and governmental agencies.<br>
 Provide leadership and development for associates by communicating career opportunities, providing regular performance feedback, and demonstrating RAPTAR (Recognition, Appreciation, Praise, Treat Associates Respectfully) behaviors.<br>
<ul><li>All duties described in this document are to be performed in keeping with the core values and service attributes consistent with the Rite Aid brand and strategy.</li></ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE</div>
<div></div>
<div> In accordance with state law, candidates must satisfy minimum age requirements to sell and/or supervise the sale of alcohol and/or tobacco products.<br>
 Ability to pass drug test.<br>
 Committed to providing customer service that makes both internal and external customers feel welcome, important, and appreciated.<br>
 Ability to preserve confidentiality of information.<br>
 Ability and willingness to move with purpose and a strong sense of urgency.<br>
 Ability to work weekends and extended days on a frequent basis.<br>
 Ability to work day or evening hours.<br>
 Accuracy and attention to detail.<br>
 Ability to organize and prioritize a variety of tasks/projects.<br>
 Familiarity with industry/technical terms and processes.<br>
 Ability to work within strict time frames and resolute deadlines.<br>
 High school diploma or general education degree (GED), plus one (1) to two (2) years' of retail experience; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</div>
