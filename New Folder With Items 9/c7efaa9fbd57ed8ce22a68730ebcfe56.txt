Requisition Summary
</div>
<div><p>PACCAR, manufacturer of Kenworth, Peterbilt and DAF commercial vehicles, seeks a high potential candidate for its Leadership Development Program. This 12-18 month management training program rotates through short term assignments in key areas. Upon successful completion, candidates are placed in mid management positions in one of the following fields: accounting, engineering, finance, human resources, information technology, leasing, operations, production or sales/marketing.</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Job Functions / Responsibilities
</div>
<div>
<p>This program is a fast track to leadership in which participants learn about PACCAR businesses, develop important relationships, and sharpen the skills necessary to carry them through to the next level of their careers.</p>
<p>
Participants in the program will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience a wide variety of rotational assignments over an 18 month period</li>
<li>
Develop versatility by working in business functions beyond the scope of previous studies or professional experience</li>
<li>
Receive mentoring from a PACCAR Senior Executive</li>
<li>
Gain international business experience at various PACCAR locations</li>
<li>
Outplace into a leadership position of significant responsibility within PACCAR</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
