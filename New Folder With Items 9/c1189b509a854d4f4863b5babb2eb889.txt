<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Please review the following job description:
</b></h3>
<p></p>Provides support to Brokers regarding all policies and/or key accounts. Partners with Broker to solicit, promote, sell, quote, and maintain renewals from existing agents as well as support new business.
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>Following is a summary of the essential functions for this job. Other duties may be performed, both major and minor, which are not mentioned below. Specific activities may change from time to time.
 </p>
<p>Promote new business and/or renewal business to achieve production/market criteria as defined by Broker with little or no supervision.
 </p>
<p>Negotiate terms with the underwriters, including price, deductible, enhancement and exclusions.
 </p>
<p>Select carriers to approach with accounts.
 </p>
<p>Assist and/or provide quotations for new business, renewals and endorsements through managing all company correspondence in a timely manner.
 </p>
<p>Prepare recommendations for agents showing fact information regarding best coverage information.
 </p>
<p>Process new business, including transference applications to CRC applications, issue binders and invoices, and check policy coverages for accuracy by CRC guidelines.
 </p>
<p>Process renewal business which may include locating files, preparing and mailing solicitor letters and/or preliminary submission information, issuing binders and invoices, and checking policy coverages for accuracy by CRC guidelines.
 </p>
<p>Acquire confirmations from retailers when accounts are bound.
 </p>
<p>Process cancellation and endorsement requests, including issuing binders and invoices, checking policy coverages for accuracy by CRC guidelines.
 </p>
<p>Prepare company profiles and research analysis for client visits.
 </p>
<p>Provide directions and supervision to team as requested by Broker.
 </p>
<p>Manage incoming calls from producers and companies.
 </p>
<p>Maintain good working relationship with current agents and other co-workers.
 </p>
<p>Maintain current knowledge of the insurance industry and sales ideas through regular attendance at workshops, seminars, literature reviews and/or formal continuing education.
 </p>
<p>Perform other duties, tasks, responsibilities and projects as assigned.
</p>
<p></p>

<p>Minimum three to five years wholesale insurance experience or its equivalent

 </p>
<p>Ability to review information, make decisions, and manage time effectively with minimal to no supervision
 </p>
<p>Ability to plan, organize and manage multiple priorities
 </p>
<p>Excellent verbal, written and presentation skills
 </p>
<p>Ability to deal with confidential matters appropriately
 </p>
<p>Possess strong interpersonal skills
 </p>
<p>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office software
</p>
