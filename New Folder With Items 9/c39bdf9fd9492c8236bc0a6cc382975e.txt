<p><b>Licensed Fire AlarmTechnician </b></p>
<p>***This position requires the ability to install, inspectand repair fire alarm systems in commercial and industrial buildings accordingto standards throughout a specific service territory.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>· Responsibilities of the job include but are notlimited to:</p>
<p>· Conduct service calls including; inspect,service and repair to the prescribed standards in allotted time frame</p>
<p>· Perform on-site emergency troubleshooting andrepairs</p>
<p>· Identify code and non-conformance issues of fireprotection systems, communicate with customers and assure any systemdeficiencies are promptly reported and corrected</p>
<p>· Work independently, with little supervision toorganize, prioritize, and communicate with customers to schedule daily route</p>
<p>· Maintain proper and accurate documentationidentifying services performed, deficiencies, repairs needed or completed usingpaperless software and company owned tablet.</p>
<p>· Communicate with coordinator about work load,customer info, feedback from field, etc.</p>
<p>· Participate in on-call emergency servicerotation</p>
<p>· Always present a professional appearance andattitude while on job sites, operating a company vehicle, or on companyproperty</p>
<p>· Territory growth including informationgathering, and up-selling.</p>
<p>· Stay up-to-date on changing technology,installation and testing methods. and industry standards</p>
<p>· Maintain vehicle inventory and keep companyowned vehicle and tools clean and organized</p>
<p>· Always adhere to company safe working policiesand procedures</p>
<p><b>SKILLS ANDSPECIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p>1-5 years previous industry experience</p>
<p>Must have a valid driver's license with less than 6 points,and pass pre-employment background and drug screenings</p>
<p>Must have Ohio State Fire Marshal Fire Alarm certification</p>
<p>Must have knowledge of NFPA standards</p>
<p>Ability to perform work in unusual and sometimes difficultpositions such as working within ducts, crawl spaces, climbing a ladder,outside in the heat or cold, on lift equipment, or dirty environments</p>
<p>Must be self-motivated</p>
<p>Ability to lift 50+ pounds above head</p>
<p>Must have excellent communication skills and positivecustomer service attitude</p>
<p>Sales aptitude a plus</p>
