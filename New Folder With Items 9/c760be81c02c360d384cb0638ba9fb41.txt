<h2>Manager, Design Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Wyndham Hotels &amp; Resorts is now seeking a Manager, Design Services to join our team at the Irving - 909 Hidden Ridge location in Irving, Texas.</p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
Job Summary
</div>
<div>
<p>This position is responsible for supporting the architectural needs of the Design &amp; Construction Department for Wyndham branded hotels.</p>
<p>
 ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide support to Franchisees and their outside consultants by communicating clearly and concisely as it pertains to the D&amp;C Design Guidelines and Brand Standards.</li>
<li>Manage multiple projects at one time at various stages of development</li>
<li>Provide conceptual designs for potential new construction and conversion projects.</li>
<li>Provide graphic media (renderings, sketches, diagrams, etc) for architectural enhancements on potential conversion properties using Bluebeam, Photoshop and Sketch-Up.</li>
<li>Review architectural plan specifications and design submittals for franchise new construction and conversions.</li>
<li>Maintain and update prototype plans and FF&amp;E standard manuals.</li>
<li>Conduct and review property improvement plans (PIP’s) for new and existing properties.</li>
<li>Coordinate with multiple other departments and personnel to align brand interests for property development.</li>
<li>Develop various design solutions that addresses Wyndham’s required design/operational programs.</li>
<li>Perform site visits to hotels under construction for inspections of construction materials, device and fixture locations and general compliance with documents and prototype standards.</li>
</ul>
