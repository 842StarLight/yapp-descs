<p>You will join a global team with a rare mix of diverse and exceptional talents – from engineering, computer science, architecture, maths and biology. A team that combines a strong technical focus with innovation, creativity and an entrepreneur spirit. The dynamic and evolving nature of our work requires us to continue to explore, develop and apply new technologies, which is what this role needs.</p>
<p>
The work is highly varied and challenging. It will involve analysis as well as consulting. Defining the problems as well as solving them. Developing solutions as well as reporting. Desk work as well as field work and client meetings. Using emerging software technologies as well as helping steer the development of in-house analytics and simulation tools. You would be working on international projects with first principles based problem solving, advanced data analytics, predictive modelling, and simulations.</p>
<p>
We are interested in candidates who are dynamic, enthusiastic and organised to work independently across all aspects of development, from research and generating user requirements to testing and integration.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
You will be responsible for supporting work winning opportunities, external marketing of our skills and technical liaison with our team in the UK undertaking people flow modelling, smart building and cities analytics.</p>
