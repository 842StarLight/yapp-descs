<h2>Marketing & Community Relations Manager - XFL Houston</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About the XFL
<br>-
<br><br>
The new XFL will reimagine football for the 21st century when it kicks off in early 2020. Delivering authentic, high-energy football for the whole family at an affordable price, the XFL will offer fast-paced games with fewer play stoppages and simpler rules. The league will launch with eight teams in markets across the country, 45-man active rosters, and a 10-week regular season schedule with a postseason consisting of two semifinal playoff games and a championship game. The XFL is a single entity that owns the league and all teams, employing professionals at the Stamford, CT headquarters as well as in each of the markets that the teams call home.
<br><br>
About the Role
<br>-
<br><br>
XFL Houston is seeking a Manager, Marketing &amp; Community Relations to help build the fan base, execute breakthrough awareness and promotional campaigns, engage fans across platforms and drive business performance that builds a foundation in the year leading up to the first game in early 2020 and beyond.
<br><br>
The Manager, Marketing &amp; Community Relations will report into Director, Marketing &amp; Community Relations help to acquire and engage fans across owned marketing channels, partnering closely with communications, social/content, sales, creative, community and partnerships.
<br><br>
What you will do
<br>-
<br><br>
Marketing
<br>-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Build marketing programs and advertising strategies to promote team initiatives, events, programs and ticket sales across multiple channels in support of overall strategic marketing plan.</li>
<li>Lead the execution of marketing programs from start to finish, driving collaboration with key stakeholders and leveraging internal processes and relationships.</li>
<li>Traffic creative projects with design team in a timely manner and enact processes to maintain brand integrity across all marketing materials and other internal and external communications.</li>
<li>Provide strategic media planning support and evaluate and reconcile advertising campaigns on an on-going basis to ensure all materials are placed and running as planned (including placement, copy and creative development and distribution for: radio &amp; TV, promotional events, digital, print ads and outdoor media).</li>
<li>Oversee and manage the team creative pipeline and deliverables throughout the approval and production process.</li>
<li>Manage email content strategy and execution of communication to CRM database.</li>
<li>Support execution of all STM, Grassroots, Community and fan events to engage fans in interactive and innovative ways (including the gameday experience, on-site activations, digital/social activation, grassroots football, influencer and fan promotions).</li>
<li>Manage all invoice and reconciliation processes on behalf of the marketing department.</li>
<li>Work with the ticket sales team on goals, revenue, ticket initiatives, promotional packages and single game ticket strategy to maximize ROI.</li>
<li>Work with the partnership team to activate and increase revenue opportunities while supporting the club and brand goals and initiatives.</li>
<li>As needed, manage fully staffed street and promotional teams who will help fulfill all of the organization's grassroots marketing efforts.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
Community Relations
<br>-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Build and execute strategic work plans that achieve measurable results and optimal use of resources to develop and grow community relations activations.</li>
<li>Develop and coordinate campaigns to connect with community, drive awareness and develop fan loyalty.</li>
<li>Develop and foster relationships with key community and government leaders to further support the integration of the team into the Greater Houston area.</li>
<li>Represent XFL Houston at key meetings and local events during normal business hours, as well as weekends and after hours if needed.</li>
<li>Create and manage the team's year-round community engagement schedule.</li>
<li>Serve as the primary liaison for all front office and player community appearances, including managing all logistics.</li>
<li>Vet, process and document all donation requests received by the team.</li>
<li>Work alongside the ticket and corporate sales staffs to develop and activate programs and initiatives in the Greater Houston area.</li>
<li>Draft speeches, talking points, presentations and other presentation materials as needed to ensure a turnkey process that allows staff members to easily and effectively present the brand in the community.</li>
<li>Develop monthly "XFL Houston in the Community" releases and detailed annual reports for internal and external purposes aimed at providing a snapshot of the team's efforts, impact and key insights from the activation.</li>
</ul>
How you will be measured
<br>-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Fan engagement measures, including size and growth of fan base, ticket sales, levels of engagement across channels and devices, fan retention and fan referrals.</li>
<li>KPIs: Brand recognition and public perception measurements.</li>
<li>Contribution to key revenue lines and delivery of the overall business plan.</li>
<li>Community engagement measures including securing earned media.</li>
</ul>
What you will bring
<br>-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of marketing with an emphasis on consumer-facing sports, media or entertainment industries with experience in advertising, creative development, promotion, writing ad copy and ad campaign creation.</li>
<li>Experience with media planning, including TV, radio, billboards, digital and social media advertising.</li>
<li>Ability to create and manage marketing plans (multi-channel, segmented, campaigns) and GTM strategies utilizing owned, paid and earned media to build fan base and drive key revenue lines.</li>
<li>Candidate must exhibit a passion for sports, marketing and football.</li>
<li>Experience managing and monitoring a budget.</li>
<li>Ability to work well with multiple stakeholders across the company, agency and client organizations.</li>
<li>A self-starter, energetic, resilient, enthusiastic m and the determination to ensure that deliverables, timelines and objectives are achieved.</li>
<li>Exceptional communications, sales/promotions and customer service skills, and must be adept at multitasking and extremely detail oriented.</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial spirit.</li>
<li>Superior verbal/written communications skills.</li>
<li>BS required.</li>
</ul>
