<h2>Growth Marketer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We’re looking for a creative and analytical Growth Marketer to join our Marketing team. You’ll help us reach the next level by providing quantitative insights into strategies to increase our growth, and by collaborating with our team to create growth-focused digital and offline marketing campaigns. Your insights will be the backbone for decisions that affect all areas of Tulip’s growth strategy. Sound exciting? Apply below!
<br><br>
<b>About us:</b><br>
At Tulip [https://tulip.co/], we’re using technology to empower the people who build our world. We created the first manufacturing app platform to allow process engineers to bring digital transformation to their factory floors. Factories run with Tulip are more efficient, their workers are more productive, and they make fewer mistakes. We call it bottom-up digital transformation. Join us if you want to help create the next industrial revolution.
<br><br>
<b>About you:</b><br>
You are exceptionally quantitative and very comfortable using analytical tools. You also enjoy thinking creatively, writing, and bringing new ideas to life. You are a collaborative and motivated self-starter, ready to roll up your sleeves and dive right in.
<br><br>
<b>About the role:</b><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Leverage our analytics infrastructure to collect and analyze marketing and sales data to uncover new opportunities and optimize existing performance.</li>
<li>Build, manage and optimize paid and organic inbound lead generation campaigns with advertising partners, influencers, media companies, and others based on data-driven hypotheses</li>
<li>Build monthly and quarterly inbound lead generation targets by channel and develop marketing programs to deliver expected lead volume within a specified budget</li>
<li>Promote our short and long-form content to new audiences in each of our target verticals</li>
<li>Write copy, determine creative strategy, and design ads and other assets to support your campaigns</li>
<li>Ongoing analysis of performance - clicks, views, etc, as well as report on earned media targets including company and brand awareness goals through weekly, monthly and quarterly reports</li>
<li>Monitor key performance indicators, make data-driven recommendations and advocate for internal resources required to drive the continued member growth via their respective marketing channel(s)</li>
<li>Develop A/B tests and multivariate experiments for landing pages, web pages, and ad copy tests</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>1 - 3 years of experience in digital marketing</li>
<li>Experience with marketing automation platforms like Hubspot</li>
<li>Experience with Google Analytics, paid search and paid social marketing channels</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to explain complicated concepts in clear and concise terms</li>
<li>A quick learner interested in learning more about the manufacturing industry</li>
</ul>
