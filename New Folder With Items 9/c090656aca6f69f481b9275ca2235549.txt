<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>About Us:</b><br>
At Healthx, we empower the healthcare experience, helping people live longer, healthier lives while lowering healthcare costs by keeping health plans, members, and providers connected. Our platform orchestrates the complex healthcare journey by delivering personalized experiences that drive cost savings and improve quality of care. With Healthx, engaged members are healthier members, and healthier members significantly reduce healthcare costs.
<br><br>
We are on a mission for good health, to revolutionize healthcare, and take the guesswork out of people proactively managing their own health. We are building some of the most innovative tech in the industry and are poised for significant growth. We are looking for innovative people with the passion and energy to join the cause.
<br><br>
<b>What we need:</b><br>
Own the business and market success of assigned Healthx products, ensure that the development team is working on things that are the most valuable. Orchestrate the sales, marketing, and services organizations to ensure each has a voice in determining product strategy and around the milestones of the roadmap. Responsible for conception through launch, including strategy, business case, market requirements, feature definition, acceptance testing, commercialization, and ongoing product performance vs. business case. Be the foremost advocate for the product(s) both inside the company and with customers in the market.
<br><br>
<b>Outcomes:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead others with your vision – internally and externally, be the voice of your products in industry and customer conversations</li>
<li>Establish product strategy and investment priorities. Be ready to explain not only what we are building but also why it is valuable and to whom</li>
<li>Aggregate market inputs from various sources, including healthcare industry trends, HCIT software market trends, competitive landscape, Healthx Advisory Board, end users, customers, and prospects</li>
<li>Establish, refine and communicate through the business and to customers a shared understanding of the vision and roadmap for your products; ensure the prioritized backlog aligns to the roadmap plan</li>
<li>Lead business case analysis and development, leverage your deep product, end user, and customer knowledge to recommend new market opportunities, new solutions, and major enhancements to existing solutions.</li>
<li>Support sales opportunities and RFP responses as required</li>

<li>Support sales opportunities and RFP responses as required</li>

<li>Drive company “readiness” in advance of product release: collaborate across all Healthx departments to ensure on-time commercialization and market launch of enhancements and new solutions; track and communicate progress and status</li>

<li>Problem solving and analytical thinking</li>
