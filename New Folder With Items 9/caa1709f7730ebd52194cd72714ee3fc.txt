<h2>Manager - Research and Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your responsibilities
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>In conjunction with the Product Strategy Group, develop and maintain product roadmap in alignment with business objectives.</li>
<li>Vet innovation ideas against business strategy, existing product roadmap and resources.</li>
<li>Research viability of conceptual product features and functionality for new products and improving existing products.</li>
<li>Assists as needed the PM and PLM groups to communicate product priorities across the company by collaborating with various functions and building consensus among them.</li>
<li>Develop relationships with sub-contract design and manufacturing partners who can provide R&amp;D resources and outsourced manufacturing capacity.</li>
<li>Collaborate with product marketing to effectively introduce products, coordinate advertising, participate in trade shows and other key activities during the life cycle of the product.</li>
<li>Develop and promote department standards and processes for product design that ensures technical design concepts, item lists, bills of material, etc. are properly documented and controlled. (SPDP)</li>
<li>Assists as needed the IP Group to document and control Intellectual Property; including working with Legal Department on contract development with partners which protects TKEs' interests.</li>
<li>Establishing quality standards and require supporting quality assurance standards from design, OEM and manufacturing partners.</li>
<li>Coordinate with the Product Strategy Group to integrate usability studies, testing, research and market analysis into product requirements to enhance user satisfaction.</li>
<li>Understand strategic and competitive position in the market and deliver innovative experiences that are recognized as best-in-class.</li>
<li>Attend industry tradeshows, distributor meetings and other events as necessary.</li>
<li>Maximize efficiency in a constantly evolving environment where the process is fluid and creative solutions are the norm.</li>
<li>Support and promote all thyssenkrupps’ safety programs.</li>
<li>Perform special projects and other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your profile
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>BS from four-year college or university with an undergraduate or graduate degree in Software Engineer or Computer Science required.</li>
<li>6+ years’ experience in a technical product development, project management position.</li>
<li>5+ years’ experience managing people and/or building cohesive teams.</li>
<li>Must demonstrate a strong aptitude for software development and agile/scrum methodologies.</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate the department and product vision.</li>
<li>Must have experience setting out the vision and roadmap for products.</li>
<li>Exemplary judgment regarding product usability, customer expectations, and creative product tradeoffs.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills to present data and product recommendations to engineering and marketing/sales management.</li>
<li>Strong leadership and team building skills.</li>
<li>Excellent proficiency using MS Office skills (PowerPoint, Word, Excel, Project or comparable Project Management Software).</li>
<li>Exceptional time management and organizational skills with a keen attention to detail.</li>
<li>Experience with Project Management Professional (PMP) processes highly desired.</li>
<li>Ability to travel sporadically for trade show and other customer/consumer related activities.</li>
</ul>
