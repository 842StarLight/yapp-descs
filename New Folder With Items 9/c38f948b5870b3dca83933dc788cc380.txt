<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Do you like the idea of managing a B2B enterprise software product that supports some of the biggest tech companies in the market? Do you want a chance to blend your technical, product, and project management experience together with your passion for software? Do you like the challenge of switching gears between strategic and tactical, evaluation and execution?</p>
<p>If this describes you, you’re a strong candidate for us:</p>
<ul>
<li>You’ve been working in a Product Manager role for at least 4 years, possibly as one of many Product Managers on a team.</li>
<li>You have experience implementing and maintaining structure and process.</li>
<li>Now you’re ready to take on new challenges, taking your career to the next level.</li>
<li>You’re familiar with the Salesforce CRM platform.</li>
</ul>
<p>You would be the first to hold <i>this</i> position at our company so you’d get to blaze your own path, applying all your prior product management experiences.</p>
<p>If this position description <i>lights your fire</i>, we want to talk to you. We have a great opportunity for a motivated, self-starter, driven by customer satisfaction and getting things done. We’re an established, small business with big plans. No bureaucracy, no politics, no BS. Our customer advocate software helps Sales teams win more deals and accelerate their sales cycles by leveraging customer advocacy—the future of sales and marketing.</p>
<p><b>Specific responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Managing release schedules and prioritization of development tickets</li>
<li>Management of product enhancement and feature requests</li>
<li>Providing quality control of product and product releases</li>
<li>Collaborating with CTO to determine product roadmap, direction, and future features</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Keeping internal and external team members up-to-date on ticket / project status’, and clients apprised of new and updated product information</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Owning product documentation needs assessment, production, and quality control, both internal and customer-facing</li>
<li>Technical assistance with client data migration and support cases</li>
</ul>
