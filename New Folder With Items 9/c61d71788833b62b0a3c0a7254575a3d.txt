<p><b>Job Description and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>IDEX Health &amp; Science, Rohnert Park, CA is looking for a talented, dynamic Supplier Quality Engineer (SQE). The SQE will ensure that products meet purchase specification and quality/reliability requirements. Additionally, they will drive process improvement through supplier manufacturing process, incoming inspection, and in-process data analysis. The SQE will interface and build cooperative working relationships with suppliers, customers, and internal organizations.</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Performing supplier quality assurance functions pertaining to external materials and component suppliers.</p></li>
<li><p>
Managing supplier databases, performing initial evaluations of new suppliers, and re-evaluation of existing suppliers, conducting audits of proposed new and existing suppliers, performing Risk Assessments and evaluations of suppliers.</p></li>
<li><p>
Leads quality improvement projects that drive continuous improvement through utilization of tools such as DOE, Six Sigma, DMAIC, FMEA, SPC, 8D Root Cause Analysis, and Lean.</p></li>
<li><p>
Applies appropriate control plans to achieve problem containment, and then drives corrective and preventative actions to address the determined problems and audit as required to verify effectiveness of actions.</p></li>
<li><p>
Processing, reporting, and tracking Supplier Corrective Action Reports (SCARs).</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible for supplier activities relating to: development, process capability, technical gap analysis, qualification, and monitoring of quality and on time delivery performance.</p></li>
<li><p>
Coordination of Material Review Board activities and feedback to supplier.</p></li>
<li><p>
Actively work with suppliers on closed loop corrective action process incorporating continual improvement and process improvement methods.</p></li>
<li><p>
Must be able to travel (by air, train, bus and/or automobile) to customer and supplier sites throughout the world. Travel estimated to comprise up to 20% of job-related activities.</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain composure and professional approach when dealing with public.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to make decisions logically.</p></li>
<li><p>
Capable of focusing on details with regards to procedures and workflow.</p></li>
<li><p>
Communicate clearly and excellently in English, verbally and in writing.</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintains a complete awareness of the cost of quality and promote activities to monitor and reduce quality costs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Coordinate the first article approval process between the organization and suppliers.</p></li>
<li><p>
Other assignments as needed.</p></li>
</ul>

<li><p>BS degree in engineering discipline.</p></li>
<li><p>
Minimum of 5 years experience as a supplier quality engineer required.</p></li>

Strong analytical skills required using Excel and MiniTab for example.</p></li>

Experience with electronic, electro-mechanical, and mechanical assembly.</p></li>
<li><p>
Experience with Six Sigma toolbox, FMEA preferred.</p></li>
<li><p>
Knowledge of ISO 9001:2015 or similar regulatory and international quality system standards desired.</p></li>
