<b>
Highlights:</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
As a Major Accounts Executive, quality service and client satisfaction are at the center of your daily focus. You accelerate the sales cycle by showcasing how our innovative business solutions simplify the technology and communications needs of healthcare organizations. Through your expertise and understanding of the client’s needs, you outline uniquely beneficial combinations of our products. You have a passion for proactively acquiring new enterprise healthcare accounts while managing an existing portfolio. You have a keen ability for identifying roadblocks and overcoming obstacles to increase business and enhance the client experience. You excel at regular travel to capture accounts within a specified footprint. You report directly to Strategic Sales Management for goals, guidance and assistance.</div>

<div><b>
What you will do:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Be an impactful member of the sales team through the achievement of monthly sales and revenue goals.</li>
<li>
Consult with prospective clients and grow key relationships within named accounts to develop client-centric product solutions.</li>
<li>
Entice clients by creating and presenting product proposals and benefits that align with their needs.</li>
<li>
Maximize sales through the conceptualization and implementation of strategic sales plans.</li>
<li>
Drive the sales process by identifying target contacts for product portfolios.</li>
<li>
Self-generate leads by building a network through referrals, prospecting calls, trade shows and peers while maintaining an accurate client database.</li>
<li>
Qualify leads through the submission of a return on investment (ROI) and accompanying analyses.</li>
<li>
Conduct tactful and mutually beneficial contract negotiations and conversations with C-Level executives including Chief Information Officer (CIO) and Chief Technical Officer (CTO).</li>
<li>
Maintain quality service and encourage retention through coordinated efforts with the Account Management team, Sales Engineering and Marketing teams.</li>
<li>
Develop key sales skills by attending sales meetings and participating in training sessions.</li>
</ul>
