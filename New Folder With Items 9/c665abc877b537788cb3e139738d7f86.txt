<h2>Strategic Account Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>AT&amp;T is leading the way to the future – for customers, businesses and the industry. We're developing new technologies to make it easier for our customers to stay connected to their world. Together, we’ve built a premier integrated communications and entertainment company and an amazing place to work and grow. Team up with industry innovators every time you walk into work, creating the world you always imagined. So, what do you say? Let’s do great things together.<br>
<br>
AT&amp;T Strategic Account Lead is considered an expert in providing wireline voice, data and wireless/mobility application and solutions to AT&amp;T large fortune customers. As an expert, the Strategic Account Lead is successful in the development and execution of strategies that focus on meeting and exceeding the business needs of our clients. An AT&amp;T Strategic Account Lead is successful in identifying key industry trends, leveraging market-based knowledge and the AT&amp;T portfolio to deliver strategic solutions to the client through a consultative sales approach.<br>
<br>
As a Strategic Account Lead you’ll focus on developing sales opportunities and presenting customized solutions to business customers. You’ll support the entire branch prospecting, qualifying, and gaining access to the operational leaders within our customer base. You’ll leverage your professional network and partner closely with the AT&amp;T Client Solution Executives to gain additional access into our customer base and position AT&amp;T to offer additional core services.<br>
<br>
Key responsibilities include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Partnering with customers to understand their business needs, issues, strategies and priorities to deliver value-added business solutions</li>
<li>Leverage industry knowledge, applications, and product knowledge to find and close opportunities</li>
<li>Overall revenue growth, customer satisfaction, and the full scope of AT&amp;T relationship performance within a portfolio of one or more assigned AT&amp;T customers. This includes both retention and acquisition activities</li>
<li>Build and Manage sales funnel, analyze and manage pipeline activity, monitor sales activity against assigned quotas, and lead Account Executives toward achieving and exceeding business results</li>
<li>Incorporate executive summary, Return on Investment (ROI) analysis and solutions design to develop customer-specific proposals and presentations</li>
<li>Manage the financial forecasting for said accounts. Ability to understand and articulate the client's financial goals and pressures and AT&amp;T's ability to deliver ROI</li>
</ul>
</div>
