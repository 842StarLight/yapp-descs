ESSENTIAL FUNCTION</b></p>
<p>
As Sensitech’s business develops to offer more service-based solutions in the compliance, security and logistics segments, there is a growing need to manage, facilitate and execute service contracts as they relate to our billing obligations. In this role, you will learn about Sensitech’s current offerings and find sustainable and scalable processes for long-term growth. You will also be a stakeholder as new services such as SaaS and DaaS are developed to ensure that contractual billing obligations are feasible and executed. This will require working cross-functionally and advocating for change in terms of technology and processes. The Service Contract Operations Manager will be a self-starter and adopt a hands on approach. You will interact with other customer facing teams and be responsible to advocate for innovative approaches to customer success. You will be a Leader in the Client Services organization and train colleagues on new processes around service contracts.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Service Contract Operations Manager is responsible for ensuring contractual service obligations are maintained and executed.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PRINCIPLE RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage, execute and maintain service billing obligations across all divisions of business such as subscriptions, renewals and fees.</li>
<li>
Develop scalable and sustainable processes by supporting new technologies and driving efficiencies related to maintenance of service contracts and agreements.</li>
<li>
Engage with customers, Sales and Program Management for new, changed, and recurring agreements.</li>
<li>
Represent Client Services in cross-functional teams for New Product Introduction (NPI) to establish service billing and tracking methods by understanding customer base and service offering.</li>
<li>
Collaborate on a regular basis with key departments for customer success: Client Services, Finance, Sales Analysis, Business Systems Group and Technical Support.</li>
<li>
Update various software platform system data to ensure billing accuracy.</li>
<li>
Participate in meetings with global offices for consistency and continuity of service offerings.</li>
<li>
Guide Client Service Associates to understand service billing function.</li>
<li>
Employ good documentation practices for SOXA compliance.</li>
<li>
Assist Client Services on ERP efficiencies.</li>
<li>
Meet time-sensitive deadlines and is able to solve daily challenges with minimal oversight.</li>
<li>
Incorporates ISO standards in service processes by documenting and educating personnel.</li>
<li>
Lead specific department initiatives as outlined by department objectives and Management to drive efficiencies.</li>
</ul>
