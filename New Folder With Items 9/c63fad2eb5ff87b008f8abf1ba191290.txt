<h2>HVAC Technical Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>As a Technical Support Specialist, you will provide technical support of Daikin Applied Air Handler products during the installation, startup, commissioning, service or maintenance phases of the product life cycle. This position will function within a call center and will report to the applied air handler customer support manager. Key responsibilities of the role include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Technical Support &amp; Issue Resolution
<ul>
<li>Assist field sales, contractors, and building automation companies with technical integration issues. This requires 50-70% of available time spent answering phones and replying to e-mails in a customer support environment</li>
<li>Act as a technical consultant for control group's product development programs, technical literature, training, and marketing efforts. Specifically support the following Daikin Applied controls products: Intelligent Equipment solution, VAV boxes (with and without controls), D3/VRV integration, Microtech Integrated Systems solution</li>
<li>Support areas include troubleshooting unit controls and systems hardware and software during installation, configuring, commissioning, troubleshooting, and Service; also building communications protocols and network troubleshooting</li>
<li>Research, Reporting &amp; Metrics
<ul>
<li>Use of Salesforce to log cases/calls, manage and track them to closure</li>
<li>Recommend modifications to released product or products</li>
<li>Maintain contacts with distribution, 3rd party, and service organizations to ensure timely resolution of field issues</li>
<li>Develop methods for field testing problem jobs/projects and utilizes technical resources such as simulators, protocol analyzers, integration service tools, and documentation</li>
<li>Keep informed on new products and other general information of interest to customers</li>
<li>Communicate suggested modifications to the company's products/services to meet customer requirements.</li>
<li>Participate in occasional product training of sales representatives.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-year degree in a technical field.</li>
<li>1+ years or professional work experience</li>
<li>Highly effective team player; able to develop and sustain team commitments and ownership.</li>
<li>Self-motivated team participant with the ability to initiate, lead and sustain problem-solving tasks.</li>
<li>Customer focused orientation with demonstrated business acumen.</li>
<li>Able to perform a mix of desk work (phone/email/ pc environment) and hands-on testing or verification / lab work is involved</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in the MS Office Suite and Outlook e-mail software</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree (Technical or Engineering), with related controls experience, networking, or integration. Understanding of HVAC theory, applications, with electrical and controls theory.</li>
<li>3+ years experience in commercial HVAC industry experience</li>
<li>Prior experience working in a technical support team</li>
<li>Outstanding relationship management, interpersonal, and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated example of taking initiative with a solid work ethic</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills, well developed listening skills, and ability to interpret business and/or client needs</li>
<li>Working knowledge of BACnet, LonWorks, MODbus or other building communication protocols</li>
</ul>
