<h2>Business Acct Exec 3, SMB Direct Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Comcast Business offers technology solutions ranging from Ethernet, internet, and WiFi connectivity to voice, television, and managed Enterprise solutions to power businesses of all sizes to perform better. From small businesses to mid-market and large Enterprise organizations, Comcast Business serves business customers across the country. Powered by an advanced, Gig-speed network and backed by 24/7 technical support, Comcast Business is one of the largest contributors to the growth of Comcast Cable. The organization is the nation's largest cable provider to small and mid-size businesses and has emerged as a force in the Enterprise market, recognized by leading industry associations as one of the fastest growing provider of Ethernet services.</p>

<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
Sell Comcast Internet, Data, Video and Voice services to small and mid-<br>
size businesses focusing mainly on advanced communications solutions<br>
including PRI, Hosted PBX, and multi-location opportunities. As part of<br>
sales process, create and deliver face-to-face sales presentations that<br>
demonstrate knowledge of the latest Comcast products and services.<br>
Promote the sale of bundled products to ensure the optimal solution for<br>
the customer. Sell with goal of exceeding departmental financial and<br>
unit targets. Stay abreast of competitive landscape and emerging<br>
technologies to best position Comcast Business Services in the<br>
marketplace.<br>
