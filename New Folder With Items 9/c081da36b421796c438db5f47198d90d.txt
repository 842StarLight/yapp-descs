<h2>Brand Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Circle K is a great place to work! Here is why:
</b><br>
<b>We know that you can work anywhere. However, working at Circle K is the start of something great! While you make it easy for our customers, we focus on you and your development! Our people make us who we are. We want to see you grow, so we put growing together at the forefront of everything we do. It is our duty to provide you with the tools and resources that you need to succeed. Joining Circle K means joining a team that is devoted to you!
</b>
</h2>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage projects related to Circle K’s brand image, products, and store signage.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal teams to promote internal products and programs, and coordinate launch programs internally and externally.
</li>
<li>Create, organize, and implement advertising campaigns with external marketing and branding consultants, art designers, copywriters, media buyers and printers while meeting brand standards.
</li>
<li>Support development of effective traditional and digital mass advertising campaigns to achieve sales objectives.
</li>
<li>Monitor and post on digital and social media platforms including content creation, publishing, and in-store digital signage.
</li>
<li>Ability to write communications, newsletters, advertisements, and presentations.
</li>
<li>Plan and lead events (grand openings, customer appreciation, local product launch).
</li>
<li>Review internal and external quantitative and qualitative market research studies and analyze findings.
</li>
<li>Observe and research marketing and c-store industry trends.
</li>
<li>Maintain professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops, and establishing personal networks.
</li>
<li>Help develop and manage the advertising budget.
</li>
<li>Work together as a team, and take lead on action items to meet goals.
</li>
<li>A successful candidate will possess professional skills such as (but not limited to) creativity, design, innovation, ability to present in front of leadership, resourcefulness, adaptability, thoroughness, and organizational/planning skills.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>To properly perform the duties of this position as outlined above, it is essential that the individual be able to meet the following physical demands:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to communicate accurate information efficiently in writing and verbally with associates, vendors, customers and other stakeholders in a manner consistent with business needs.
</li>
<li>Ability to frequently move or transport items weighing up to 30 pounds.
</li>
<li>Ability to use his/her hands in order to handle or grasp items of varying weight. The employee is frequently required to stand, walk, and reach with both hands, arms and legs.
</li>
<li>Ability to position self to stoop, bend, lift, twist, and reach.
</li>
<li>Ability to sit at a desk for long periods of time.
</li>
<li>Ability to operate a computer keyboard for data entry and observe computer screens accurately for data entry, use fax machines and copiers, and exchange information accurately for support via telephone communications.
</li>
<li>Ability to work under tight deadlines, interruptions, and stressful situations.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>The physical requirements described herein are essential functions of this job. The Company complies with all federal, state, and local laws concerning the employment of persons with disabilities, including providing reasonable accommodations to qualified individuals with a disability so they can perform the essential functions of a job unless doing so creates an undue hardship.

<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum required: Bachelor’s degree with a major in Marketing and/or Advertising with five years’ experience in branding, marketing, and/or advertising.
</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and attention to detail, with an ability to work effectively in a team environment across all levels within the organization.
</li>
<li>Manage multiple projects simultaneously in a fast-paced environment from beginning to end with little guidance, using project management skills.
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills to develop and maintain effective business relationships within and outside of the company.
</li>
<li>Experience in retail and/or c-store channel experience preferred.
</li>
<li>Background in traditional media production (radio, ooh, print), media buys, events, and reporting.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated involvement in digital advertising and social media including targeting, ad placement and media buys, content development, establishing and tracking KPIs, reporting, etc.
</li>
<li>Proficient in various computer software including Word, Excel and PowerPoint. Adobe Suite and GSP Signage Software experience considered a plus.
</li>
<li>Strong analytical, organizational, and interpersonal skills required.
</li>
<li>Previous background in a media or creative agency is an asset.
</li>
<li>Strong creative thinking.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of managing an advertising budget.
</li>
<li>A self-starter that is comfortable transition quickly into new situations and workgroups.
</li>
<li>Adapts easily to a changing environment, can manage multiple priorities, and meet deadlines.
</li>
</ul>
