<h2>Vinyl Production Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>General Description</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create Work Orders and update system with accurate completion dates.</li>
<li>Update schedules daily and post in production as required.</li>
<li>Prepare packets for production floor to operate. Includes work order, box and law label as necessary.</li>
<li>Assist in coordinating schedule changes with appropriate teams and estimate lead time or completion dates for sales representatives</li>
<li>Assist in monitoring progress of orders through production based on schedule requirements.</li>
<li>Follow up on raw material and supply orders to ensure delivery dates will be met.</li>
<li>Track documentation for FR 1633 for traceability of materials by Federal Government Standards.</li>
<li>Input daily production information for scorecard traceability.</li>
<li>Assist in supporting other teams (Accounting, Customer Reps, Sales Ops) with data as needed.</li>
<li>Drive Cycle Count Program</li>
<li>Support Manager, Supervisor and Scheduler as needed.</li>
<li>This position is required to support additional team initiatives that require administrative support that may or may not be technical in nature.</li>
<li>Learn scheduling process and schedule office warehouse operation.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>
Must comply with safety rules within all facilities. Several years of experience in manufacturing operations, plus heavy software experience with all MS Office products. Extensive knowledge of spreadsheets. Individual must have proven critical thinking and analytical skills, as well as excellent communication abilities.</p>
<p><b>
Educational/Certification Requirements</b></p>
<p>
High School Diploma with a minimum of 3 years manufacturing experience, preferably with working knowledge of bill of materials and production flow.</p>
