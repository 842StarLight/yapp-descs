<p>
ATI is a global manufacturer of technically advanced specialty materials and complex components. Our products support diverse markets and industries such as aerospace and defense, oil &amp; gas, electrical energy, medical, automotive, and other industrial markets.</p>
<p><b><i>
Summary:</i></b></p>
<p>
Utilize engineering talents to support new product, reduce cost, eliminate waste, and minimize the opportunities for defects. Assist Quality Manager by supporting the Quality Management System and participating as a DQR.</p>
<p><b><i>
Essential Functions:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist conducting conformance audits and approval of Internal procedures, External suppliers, Product compliance to customer requirements.</li>
<li>Work as DQR and/or PWA MCL representative.</li>
<li>Host customer representatives for the purpose of product release source inspection.</li>
<li>Participate in the investigation of product and process failures that impact customer product. Work with the customer in an ethical manner that helps resolve these failures.</li>
<li>Generate balloon prints, inspection plans, and AS9102 forms used by others.</li>
<li>Participate and provide documentation for root cause analysis, and corrective action processes.</li>
<li>Represent ATI during audits of Quality System, procedures, product and process.</li>
<li>Conduct training of ATI personnel in conjunction with corrective action resulting from internal or external auditor findings.</li>
<li>Support the QA Manager as needed in the review and development of Quality Programs.</li>
<li>Assist Engineering in developing the most economical means to manufacture and appraise the product.</li>
<li>Monitor and access effectiveness of the quality plan in assuring a product that satisfies our customers' needs.</li>
<li>Review customer and regulatory requirements and determine and create appropriate Quality System Procedures.</li>
<li>Participate in internal audits.</li>
<li>Participate in Corrective and Preventative Actions.</li>
<li>Preparation of documents for management review.</li>
<li>Review specification, Quality Systems, and GD&amp;T issues.</li>
<li>Review and approval of manufacturing practices and work instructions.</li>
<li>Obtain customer waivers/variations as required.</li>
<li>Provide ATI's input for customer and/or government specifications and drawings.</li>
<li>Coordinate customer quality visitation and survey reviews and report results.</li>
<li>Provide technical guidance working with sales, engineering and manufacturing.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain records of approvals, quality ratings.</li>
<li>Review drawings and aid in identification of areas of difficulty or added costs.</li>
<li>Contribute to continuous improvement and support 5S initiatives.</li>
<li>Generate suggestions for improving work; develop innovative approaches and ideas.</li>
</ul>

<div><b><i>Requirements:</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree in Engineering or equivalent years experience</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of aerospace requirements and knowledge of flight safety hardware requirements</li>
<li>Demonstrate ability to work with Windows based software</li>
<li>Read and understand blueprints</li>
<li>Communicate effectively in written and oral formal</li>
<li>Read and comprehend purchase order and specification requirements</li>
<li>Use and understand precision measuring tools</li>
<li>Work with little direction or supervision</li>
<li>Skills in problem solving techniques</li>
<li>Experience in a team work environment, with quality concepts of TQM and SPC</li>
<li>Demonstrate ability to handle multiple projects at the same time</li>
<li>Experience as a Designated Quality Representative</li>
</ul>
