<h2>Agile Coach</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Agile Coach is a leadership support role that will directly aide in the transformation of waterfall teams to high performing Agile teams by working with senior leadership, Scrum Masters, and stakeholders across multiple teams within Ally Financial. Core focus on supporting delivery transformation for Consumer Credit Operations within the Auto business unit - a mission critical business center for Ally Financial. The Agile Coach will interact with high level leadership and enable multiple strategic initiatives at Ally Financial.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Educate and coach team members and stakeholders across multiple workstreams on Agile Principles and Values
</li>
<li>Create and deliver Agile training for business and technology leaders and team members</li>
<li>Advocate for and influence Agile and Product / Project management practices across leadership and delivery teams
</li>
<li>Develop an "Agile center of excellence" within the Consumer Credit Operations space
</li>
<li>Advises and designs processes to accelerate high quality software delivery across a wide portfolio of initiatives
</li>
<li>Developing leaders across multiple teams and specialization areas to oversee Agile delivery teams
</li>
<li>Supports development of governance structures and program level reporting to executive leadership
</li>
<li>Working collaboratively with team leaders and cross-functional development teams to accelerate product development</li>
<li>Developing new Scrum Masters, Product Owners/ team leaders, &amp; Development Teams</li>
<li>Assisting in rolling out the Scrum framework to the larger organization</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 years knowledge and experience applying Agile practices and principles including Scrum and/or Kanban
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to launch, inspect and promote Agile practices at multiple institutions
</li>
<li>Proficiency in written, verbal and listening communication including strong presentation and facilitation skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to lead through change, advocate for and influence lean agile transition</li>
<li>Confirmed team player with the ability to work reciprocally with a variety of stakeholders from management to practitioners</li>
<li>Excellent social and influencing skills with demonstrated relationship-building skills</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate and work across the organization</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills with strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Willing to support travel up to 25%
</li>
<li>Experience as an Agile Coach across multiple coaching engagements</li>
<li>Scrum or Kanban Experience required</li>
<li>Launches and stabilizes teams and programs using Scaled Agile, Scrum and Kanban frameworks</li>
<li>Mentors Scrum Masters, Product Owners, Project Managers, and team members
</li>
<li>Assesses performance of teams and programs, finds opportunities for improvement and makes recommendations</li>
<li>Trains and guides on a variety of lean-agile subjects</li>
<li>Contributes to development and sharing of lean-agile standard methodologies</li>
<li>Provides consultancy and acts as SME on lean-agile principles and leading industry practices</li>
<li>Works as an agile team member of the Global PMO and Agile Transformation team</li>
<li>Knowledge of Auto Consumer Credit space (loans/lease/pricing/dealership interaction/automatic decisioning) a strong plus
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Ally Overview</b></p>
<p>
Ally Financial Inc. (NYSE: ALLY) is a leading digital financial services company and a top 25 U.S. financial holding company offering financial products for consumers, businesses, automotive dealers and corporate clients. Ally's legacy dates back to 1919, and the company was redesigned in 2009 with a distinctive brand, innovative approach and relentless focus on its customers. Ally has an award-winning online bank (member FDIC), one of the largest full service auto finance operations in the country, a complementary auto-focused insurance business, a growing digital wealth management and online brokerage platform, and a trusted corporate finance business offering capital for equity sponsors and middle-market companies.</p>

Ally Auto works directly with dealers nationwide to provide one of the most comprehensive suites of auto financing products and services in the industry. Ally offers new and used vehicle inventory financing, consumer financing, leasing, commercial loans and vehicle remarketing services. As a full-service provider, Ally understands dealer needs and is committed to helping them maximize sales and profitability. With nearly 100 years of industry experience, our employees' expertise allows us to provide our customers with the highest level of service, providing value that helps dealers build brands, better serve customers and stay ahead of the competition. It is a fast-paced, challenging and collaborative working environment. Our employees are focused on consistently delivering a superior customer experience.</div>
