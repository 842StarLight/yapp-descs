<p><b>WINTER PARK RESORT OVERVIEW:</b><br>
</p>
<p>Opened in January of 1940, Winter Park Resort is the oldest continuously operating resort in the U.S., and located just 67 miles NW of Denver, is the closest major ski resort to the City. Winter Park’s traditions are deeply rooted in railroad, logging, and the early exploration of Grand County and the local Fraser Valley. Our mission is to “create the best memories, again and again,” for our guests, our employees, our community, and our strategic partners.</p>
<p>
Headquartered in Denver, Colorado, Alterra Mountain Company is a community of 14 iconic year-round destinations, including the world’s largest heli-ski operation. The company owns and operates a range of recreation, hospitality, real-estate development, food and beverage and retail businesses. Headquartered in Denver, Colorado with destinations across the continent, we are rooted in the spirit of the mountains and united by a passion for outdoor adventure. Alterra Mountain Company’s family of diverse playgrounds spans five U.S. states and three Canadian provinces: Steamboat and Winter Park Resort in Colorado; Squaw Valley Alpine Meadows, Mammoth Mountain, June Mountain and Big Bear Mountain Resort in California; Stratton in Vermont; Snowshoe in West Virginia; Tremblant in Quebec, Blue Mountain in Ontario; Deer Valley in Utah; and CMH Heli-Skiing &amp; Summer Adventures in British Columbia. We honor each destination’s unique character and authenticity and celebrate the legendary adventures and enduring memories they bring to everyone<b>.</b></p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
The Pay Parking Operations Supervisor will follow all Winter Park policies and procedures, all applicable laws (federal, state, and local), and all health and sanitation codes.</p>
<p>
The Pay Parking Operations Supervisor manages the WPR pay parking operation in the Village Garage, Moffat, overflow and the Vintage Lots and access control operations at the Parking Services Hut. Is responsible for collection of fees, processing, reconciliation and reporting of daily revenues. ($1,000,000+ annually) Is responsible for enforcement of fee policies including identification of illegally parked vehicles, issuance of citations and daily reporting of violators to our designated collection agency. Is responsible for operation and maintenance of all pay parking and gate equipment. The Pay Parking Operations Supervisor is responsible for controlling all access to the core village, lodging, WPR administrative offices, F&amp;B loading dock, NSCD, and the Medical Clinic.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES:</b></p>
<p><i>
This job description is an overview of the scope of responsibilities and is not intended to be an inclusive list of job tasks and expectations. With the evolution of this resort and position, the responsibilities of this position may change.</i></p>
<ul>
<li><i>Interview, hire, train, supervise, discipline and schedule work of a 4-6 person crew performing pay parking operations duties.</i></li>
<li><i>Provide technical assistance and work direction to all support staff.</i></li>
<li><i>Manage opening and closing procedures including snow removal around all pay stations and Traffic Hut and signage.</i></li>
<li><i>Control of vehicle access into lodging check-in, reserved, visitor, Village Garage, pay overflow, short term, disabled and clinic parking. Control access of deliveries, shuttles, emergency vehicles and construction vehicles. Includes issuance of daily and temporary parking permits and codes for no charge use of pay parking spaces.</i></li>
<li><i>Daily enforcement of pay parking fees with the Genetec License Recognition system, OmniPark citation issuance and daily violation reporting to identified collection agency.</i></li>
<li><i>Collection of parking fees for Level 4, Moffat and Overflow parking through RTP. Collect all cash revenue from pay stations. Reconcile and prepare daily deposit report and deposit cash daily</i></li>
<li><i>Organize, maintain and reconcile various financial and usage reports. Maintain records.</i></li>
<li><i>Monitor SkiData gates &amp; T2 pay station equipment in the parking garage and Vintage lots. Manage pre- maintenance program, trouble shooting and repairs.
</i></li>
<li><i>Monitor and operate, Genetec License Recognition System equipment and software, OmniPark citation computer and printer.</i></li>
<li><i>Schedule service vendors for all equipment for annual and as needed maintenance/repairs. Order parts as needed including a spare part inventory.
</i></li>
<li><i>Assist guests and employees with directions and information in a friendly, professional manner. Effectively handle first-line conflicts and resolve problems.</i></li>
<li><i>Prepare and distribute HOA and Lodging access and parking information and access codes as needed.</i></li>
<li><i>Manage pay parking season pass sales.</i></li>
<li><i>Assist in regular parking operations as needed.</i></li>
<li><i>Advise Supervisor regarding problems, safety issues equipment deficiencies, signage requirements, etc.</i></li>
<li><i>Public and employee safety:</i></li>
<li><i>Be alert for potential hazards involving vehicles, pedestrians, and equipment. Take responsible action to prevent accidents.</i></li>
<li><i>Report and/or correct any unsafe conditions.</i></li>
<li><i>Assist with vehicle, public, and employee accidents as required. Follow accident reporting procedures.</i></li>
</ul>
