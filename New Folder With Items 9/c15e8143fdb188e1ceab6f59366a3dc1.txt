<p>
PRIMARY FUNCTIONS:</p>
<p>
ASI Federal Credit Union selects employees who love to learn, have a passion for helping the people in their community. Passionately display a desire to build rapport, offer personalized financial services, and expand relationships with members.</p>
<p>
The Account Resolutions Representative will contact members to collect delinquent loans and overdrawn deposit accounts. Receives members in person and by telephone; provides general credit union information. Interview members to learn reasons for loan delinquency and seek resolution.</p>
<p>
Conducts daily business interactions and maintains constant alignment with ASI core values and behaviors. Encourages others to act with mission, culture and core values in mind at all times. Initiative and judgment are required in the absence of specific direction from management. Ensures value through decision making that supports credit union goals and interests.</p>
<p>
BASIC REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of all federal, state and local regulations and laws applicable to debt collections.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of loan and collection policy and procedures.</li>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent.
</li>
<li>Proficiency with database software and MS Office suite.</li>
<li>Proven ability to overcome obstacles and get results.</li>
<li>Strong communication and negotiation skills.</li>
<li>1+ years’ customer service experience preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to diffuse escalated situations.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to multi-task with attention to detail.</li>
<li>Team player.</li>
</ul>
