<h2>College Recruitment Specialist - Engineering and Mineral Resources</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The Statler College of Engineering and Mineral Resources invites applications for the position of College Recruitment Specialist. The incumbent will plan and implement a student recruitment program designed to meet enrollment goals with specific student populations. The incumbent will also conduct programmatic and individual activities while functioning as part of the college outreach and recruitment team.<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
In order to be successful in this the role, the ideal candidate will:</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
Focus primary efforts in the areas of under-represented minorities and diversity initiatives to recruit a more inclusive student body, and recruitment at high school STEM competitions.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain articulation agreements and transfer pathways with a focus on increasing transfer student enrollment.</li>
<li>
Attend community college transfer days as established by supervisor.</li>
<li>
Create and develop specific communication plans using all available mediums for under-represented minorities and transfer students</li>
<li>
Establish contacts and subscribe to mailing lists for College Nights and Day programs and high school visits.</li>
<li>
Schedule a full fall and spring travel season. Supervisor will have final authority on which fairs or programs will be selected and a specific goal for high school visits and college fairs will be established by supervisor.</li>
<li>
Make all travel arrangements including hotel and rental cars.</li>
<li>
Attend local, regional and national high school college fairs. Transport materials, set up recruiting displays, and professionally represent WVU to prospective students, parents, and other constituents. Schedule and conduct high school visits at targeted high schools.</li>
<li>
Conduct seminar presentations in specific high school classes and for other organizations.</li>
<li>
Host fall and spring inquiry and applicant yield events in coordination with outreach and enrollment team.</li>
<li>
Attend university recruitment events in primary and non-primary markets.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and conduct one-on-one meetings for interested students and their families.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain positive relationships with high school principals, guidance counselors and teachers in an effort to recruit students to WVU.</li>
<li>
Engage in all Key recruiter, Strategic Outreach, and Key Communicator committees on campus</li>
<li>
Work and coordinate closely with WVU admissions and university relations.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Master’s degree in engineering, communications, or related field and two (2) years of related professional work experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Will consider a bachelor’s degree and three (3) years of related professional work experience.</li>
<li>
Experience is preferred in higher education, recruitment, marketing, public relations, counseling or sales.</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of customer service principles.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated excellent oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated understanding of the undergraduate curricular and co-curricular experience at WVU engineering focused or another major, public institution of higher education located in a college town environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to constructively and collegially interact with multiple members of the WVU community, including faculty and staff.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel occasionally (often overnight) and to work extended hours and weekends as needed.</li>
<li>
Ability to rapidly learn WVU admission requirements, basic housing and financial aid requirements and regulations.</li>
<li>
Physical capability to transport materials weighing an average of 25-40 pounds.</li>
<li>
Proficient understanding of web-based technology, including specific knowledge of Banner and Target X (or other CRM software)</li>
<li>
Ability to use Microsoft Office Suite, including Excel.</li>
</ul>
