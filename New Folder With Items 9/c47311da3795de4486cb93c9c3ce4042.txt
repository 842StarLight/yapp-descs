<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>SDR Position:</b></p>
<p>
 Xeeva is driving the development and delivery of the next generation of intelligent procurement and financial solutions. Our technology is used in over 40 countries and is available in 18 languages. In short, we’re growing fast and we’re searching for an amazing, experienced senior business development representative to grow with us. We want someone who’s positive, passionate, and ready to disrupt the status quo. Is that you?</p>
<p>
 <b>You are an excellent fit if:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> You LOVE talking to people on the phone.</li>
<li>You get excited by competition, technology, and noble approaches to solving problems.</li>
<li>You are a curious confident communicator able to act independently and in a team environment.</li>
<li>You are a self-starter and take initiative.</li>
<li>You are willing to learn quickly about the space we are in to be ready to start calling new prospects within 2 weeks of joining the team.</li>
<li>You have a high-degree of business acumen, are persistent, and creatively solve problems.</li>
<li>You thrive by learning about new sales and marketing tech to help you identify relevant prospects at target companies, and connect with those prospects using industry best practices across email, phone, and social channels.</li>
<li>Understand that by becoming an expert of our product’s offerings you will be able to communicate concisely how our solution solves business problems.</li>
<li>Build interest and identify qualified opportunities with new prospects based on your knowledge of customer environments, and how it relates to our experience.</li>
<li>Coordinate with multiple teams to schedule tailored demos of our solutions.</li>
<li>Crush your monthly and quarterly qualified demonstration quotas</li>
<li>Efficiently and gracefully countertop of the funnel objections, prospecting, cold calling, online, as well as have excellent interpersonal &amp; technical skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Bonus points if:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have B2B Enterprise SaaS experience.</li>
<li>You are familiar with solution-based selling.</li>
<li>You want to pursue a career in Enterprise SaaS sales.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Perks:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive base salary paired with an awesome no cap commission structure</li>
<li>Opportunity to start early in a fast-growing startup and be in the trenches with some pretty amazing people</li>
<li>Full benefits, including health, dental, disability, vision, and a matching 401k</li>
<li>Work from home days</li>
<li>Multiple career paths set up for career growth within Xeeva</li>
<li>Casual dress code and work environment</li>
<li>Monthly “Hump Day” happy hours and other team outings</li>
<li>ROWE (Results Only Work Environment)</li>
<li>Become a thought leader in a new space within procurement technology and spend time talking about cool things like AI and machine learning every day</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Things we love:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Positivity, passion, and creativity</li>
<li>Smart people with exceptional accomplishments, talents, and goals</li>
<li>The desire to continuously learn and grow and pass along best practices to colleagues and customers</li>
<li>Sincere empathy towards customers and teammates</li>
<li>Ability to speak with CEOs, VPs, Directors, and ask thought-provoking questions</li>
<li>Self-starters who quickly jump into action and stay calm in the midst of chaos</li>
<li>Phone, presentation skills, and strong interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Experience selling technology and/or software</li>
<li>Strong time management skills</li>
<li>Excellent written &amp; verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, startup environment with minimal direction</li>
<li>Hustle and persistence</li>
</ul>

If it’s the latter then apply right now, and use the space in your resume and cover letter to actually tell us who you are – not just what you’ve done. Of course, you should include info on some of your accomplishments, but for extra credit send us a sample of something that inspires you (not something you created, and no explanation needed).</p>
