<h2>Google Cloud Platform/Amazon Web Services Technical Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Overview &amp; Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Emergent is seeking a Technical Sales Specialist to join our Cloud Team. In this role, you will support Google and Amazon’s strategy to promote the sharing and adoption of Cloud Infrastructure solutions with Federal, State, and Local accounts by enabling customers to define, develop, and deploy their next-generation strategies. You'll be responsible for building strategic relationships with Google and Amazon sales team, Sales Specialists, Solutions Architects; as well as executives and IT organization leaders to promote the Google and Amazon portfolio of Cloud solutions.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities and Essential Duties:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Work with the enterprise sales teams to plan the overall strategy within a set of defined accounts</li>
<li>
Discover net new accounts and collaborate with Google and Amazon's presales teams to manage meaningful proofs of concepts</li>
<li>
Gain the trust of senior IT and business executives within key accounts and create long-term partnerships as well as the sales team at Google, Amazon, and within Emergent</li>
<li>
Work with enterprise sales teams to ensure Google or Amazon's solutions become the first choice for our strategic customers</li>
<li>
Define, develop and execute territory sales plan targeting US government agencies and System Integrators for Google and Amazon products</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain key relationships with clients, partners, vendors and/or manufacturers at all levels of the organization, including executives to advance the sales cycle and support the client post-sale.</li>
<li>
Perform prospecting activities to gain new business and advance the sales cycle, such as identifying and understanding customers’ issues and technical infrastructure; researching &amp; generating detailed account profiles</li>
<li>
Educate prospects on the capabilities of Google Cloud and Amazon Web Services, identifying the appropriate technologies to solve client problems</li>
<li>
Follow standard sales methodology to manage the end-to-end sales process, including collaboration with Google and Amazon channel sales to deliver proposals</li>
<li>
Building and supporting hands-on technical workshops at remote customer sites</li>
<li>
Building and running live Demos, webinars, and presentations for key IT decision-makers</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><div><div><div><b>Requirements:</b></div></div></div></div>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>
BS/BA degree (Computer Engineering, Computer Information System, or Computer Science preferred but not required) or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>
Experience and/or interest in the IT industry</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Desired Skills and Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years of experience in a solutions sales role</li>
<li>
Experience in a solutions sales role, including 2+ years, focused on infrastructure/cloud solutions</li>
<li>
Demonstrated competitive knowledge of opportunities related to infrastructure modernization/DevOps/Automation/Cloud</li>
<li>
Background in consultative customer engagement</li>
<li>
Broad technical experience with enterprise IT technologies, prior development/administrative experience a plus</li>
<li>
Outstanding written and verbal communication skills, as well as the ability to present to executives at large firms</li>
<li>
Ability to sell solutions in a multiproduct environment</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work with cross-functional teams and achieve success for customers</li>
<li>
Balance of strategic and tactical skills</li>
<li>
Experience in a customer-facing sales roles with demonstrated solutions-selling skills</li>
<li>
Strong presentation skills, including the ability to create effective PowerPoint presentations</li>
<li>
Ability to manage a complex enterprise sales cycle</li>
<li>
Understanding of the government procurement process</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Knowledge / Skills / Abilities (KSAs):</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to successfully complete a series of Red Hat online training courses to become an authorized certified Red Hat representative within 6 months of hire</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple issues and projects while maintaining a high level of detail</li>
<li>
Ability to convey information to customers in a clear and concise manner verbally and in writing</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently with limited direction</li>
<li>
Ability to solve a variety of problems with minimal to moderate complexity, adapting common solutions to unique customer situations.</li>
<li>
Ability to build and present live or virtual demo’s for the customer based around Red Hat’s Emerging Technology portfolio</li>
</ul>
