<h2>Inside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>In this role, the Inside Sales Representative needs to become familiar and fluent with each of the sales tools and processes to a level that allows them to assist a new team member on the particular system, tool or process.The ISR will be part of the local Inside Sales Team in Portland, Oregon and will report to the local Inside Sales Manager. This opening is not available for remote workers.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Administers, configures, updates and maintains CRM Leads and Opportunities.</li>
<li>
Fanatical prospecting to hit daily dial targets against Field Rostered Accounts for Enterprise Sales appointment setting.</li>
<li>
Schedules Networking demos across organizations including key stakeholders, product experts and buyer decision makers.</li>
<li>
Reviews information, makes changes and updates to data, pricing and configuration of quotes within limited parameters guided by your Field Sales partners</li>
<li>
Produces and analyzes reports and assesses results to identify trends and issues, performs root-cause analysis for lost deals and prospecting issues, develops recommendations for resolution and tracks effectiveness of remedies / new prospecting techniques</li>
<li>
Communicates issues to stakeholders</li>
<li>
Implements specified changes to data, content and systems to improve data quality, operational efficiency and effectiveness</li>
<li>
Customer Demos, Lead Management, process Non-Standard Pricing requests, assist with sales positioning on partner leads and work alongside partners to close opportunities</li>
<li>
Track sales opportunities (leads, deal registration, quotes etc.) and drive revenue generation activities</li>
<li>
Act as a liaison between partner, customers, and appropriate HPE Aruba reps to help partners close sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Demonstrate consistent achievement of sales quota</li>
<li>
Must have proficiency with various software applications programs including Microsoft Office and CRM tools such as Salesforce.com</li>
</ul>
