<b>Position Profile:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Planning of material movements, from firm/planned thru final inspection and shipment.</li>
<li>Work with production and stores to establish priorities and maintain a consistent flow of materials to the production floor.</li>
<li>Identify and develop new sources of supply where current global supplier capabilities are inadequate using existing kSARIA Supply Chain policies.</li>
<li>Develop, implement, and negotiate price and delivery terms with suppliers to minimize material cost, inventory levels, and transportation costs while supporting Production demand requirements.</li>
<li>Manage day to day procurement activities including internal and supplier communications; negotiate schedule and delivery changes, price changes, revised terms and special arrangements with suppliers.</li>
<li>Review MRP requirements to ensure component deliveries are scheduled in line with production requirements; review and issuing purchase orders and make necessary changes to requirements, schedule and price.</li>
<li>Manages relationships with key suppliers through monthly review of supplier scorecards and conducting supplier workshops to include performance management activity, review of supplier scorecard, supply chain risk elements, efficiency opportunities and other supplier management functions.</li>
<li>Optimize spend and provide minimum target annual savings through cost reductions, cost avoidance and operational improvements.</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of emerging technologies and business trends and incorporate into the strategic sourcing plan, where appropriate; evaluate price trends, market trends, and manufacturing process that impact overall commodity performance.</li>
<li>Negotiates and executes critical, complex, high-value awards either in conjunction with a buyer or independently; determine the commodity responsibility.</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as assigned. .</li>
</ul>
