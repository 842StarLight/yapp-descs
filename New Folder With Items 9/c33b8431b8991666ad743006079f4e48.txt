<div><b>Key Responsibilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Analyze current and past trends in key performance indicators including all areas of revenue, cost of sales, expenses and capital expenditures</li>
<li>
Monitor performance indicators, highlighting trends and analyzing causes of unexpected variance</li>
<li>
Ad-Hoc Reporting and Analysis</li>
<li>
Quarterly and Monthly Financial reports</li>
<li>
Implement and work with a Business Intelligence Tool and Dashboard reports</li>
<li>
Develop financial models and analyses to support strategic initiatives</li>
<li>
Analyze complex financial information and reports to provide accurate and timely financial recommendations to management for decision making purposes</li>
<li>
Supporting Senior Management Team and Departments heads with in-depth analysis</li>
<li>
Prepare presentations to Board of Directors and Senior Management Team</li>
</ul>
