<h2>Intake Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>Road to Baby, LLC is a reproductive consulting agency, specializing in Gestational Surrogacy and Egg Donation. We assist heterosexual and same-sex couples, as well as individuals in becoming parents through the help of Gestational Carriers and Egg Donors. This position reports directly to the Company COO.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Interview candidates</li>
<li>Provide information to surrogate candidates regarding agency process, compensation, benefits and all related required paperwork needed to accept an applicant into the program.</li>
<li>Compose and prepare confidential correspondence related to HIPPA and other medical history</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Request Surrogate and Egg Donor's medical records</li>
<li>Perform background checks on approved egg donor and surrogates</li>
<li>Point of contact with inquiring surrogate and egg donor candidates who approach our agency via the internet; website, marketing campaigns, by way of referral, etc.</li>
<li>Accurately prepare and process, all surrogate paperwork.</li>
<li>Screen preliminary inquiries of candidates who wish to become a surrogate or egg donors through our agency</li>
<li>Communicates directly, and on behalf of the Program Director with surrogate candidates regarding the agency process and guidelines</li>
<li>Keeps COO well informed of upcoming commitments and responsibilities regarding the onboarding process with potential surrogates</li>
<li>Provides leadership to build relationships crucial to the success of the organization</li>
<li>Maintain and organize electronic files</li>
<li>Participate in various office organizational and administrative projects as assigned</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong customer service and/or sales experience</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to convey a warm and professional tone via all electronic communication and phone conversations</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize multiple work assignments</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work independently on projects and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Listening skills coupled with maintaining confidential matters with discretion</li>
<li>Professional in all matters</li>
<li>High degree of confidence and professionalism</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Office software: Outlook, Word, Excel</li>
<li>Be extremely detail-oriented and organized.</li>
</ul>
