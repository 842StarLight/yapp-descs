<h2>CX Business Development Manager - Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Cisco's Customer Experience team is looking for a Solutions Business Development Manager (BDM) to help grow the business in USPS. This role is key to identifying marketplace demands for Cisco’s Customer Experience Service portfolio and works with our Sales organization to support the sale of the Services portfolio.<br>
</div>
<div>Who You’ll Work With<br>
<br>
The Business Development Management (BDM) team is a highly empowered collaborative team focused on driving successful insertion of services across Cisco sales activities. You will partner with Sales, Customer Success, and Delivery to drive new technology and service solutions for our customers. We have the opportunity for visibility and interaction with senior management, cross-team collaboration, engage and drive matrix-managed projects which have direct impact to the services business within the Americas Region.<br>
<br>
Our BDM team members have a breadth of knowledge across Cisco’s technology and services portfolio.<br>
<br>
Team members are expected to leverage this knowledge to develop solutions, scope services opportunities, and support sales based on customer requirements across all vertical markets and within the customer’s C-suite.</div>
<div>Responsibilities Include<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Drive engagement with account teams in planning and executing customer solutions and services sales strategy that span more than 1 architecture and drive a transformational dialogue.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Expand the vision for existing service opportunities and coordinate with Sales and Delivery through a Lifecycle approach.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Define and communicate portfolio positioning and value to target customer segments and Cisco stakeholders.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Discover and align customer business &amp; operational priorities to portfolio outcomes and capabilities through workshops with customers.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Create and deliver standalone and cross-portfolio Service quotes and proposals to customers with accurate deliverable scoping, sizing and pricing.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Collaborate with cross-functional groups (sales, delivery, product management, operations, marketing) to develop portfolio strategy, financial forecasts, offer packages and pricing, and customer proposals.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Contribute to the development of new service offerings.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Track and manage continuous pipeline of services and solutions for customer opportunities.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Work with service line management and Services Product Management in supporting the services lifecycle requirements.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Engage with Service Line delivery teams to insure consistent and accurate scoping of client delivery engagements</li></ul>
<br>
</div>
