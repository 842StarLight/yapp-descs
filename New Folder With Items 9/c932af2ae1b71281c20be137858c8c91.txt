<h2>Business Systems Analyst, Veeva Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>The Company:</b></div>
<div>Alder BioPharmaceuticals, Inc. is a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company focused on transforming the migraine treatment paradigm through the discovery, development and commercialization of novel therapeutic antibodies. Alder’s lead product candidate, eptinezumab, is a pivotal-stage monoclonal antibody (mAb) that inhibits calcitonin gene-related peptide (CGRP). Eptinezumab, if approved, will be the first-to-market infusion therapy for migraine prevention. Alder is also developing ALD1910, a preclinical mAb that inhibits pituitary adenylate cyclase-activating polypeptide-38 (PACAP-38) for migraine prevention.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Alder submitted a Biologic Application License to the FDA for eptinezumab in February 2019 and plans to obtain regulatory approval in the United States for eptinezumab for the prevention of migraines for patients that suffer with episodic migraine and chronic migraine. Advancing and commercializing eptinezumab in the United States is Alder’s priority however we are exploring partnership opportunities to commercialize eptinezumab outside of the United States.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Alder’s mission to is to forever change migraine treatment and give patients their lives back. This isn’t just something we say – it’s what we do. It’s our dedication each and every day to working to deliver drugs that can significantly improve the lives of those highly impacted by migraine. We do it for our spouses, our cousins, our friends, our co-workers, and the countless others suffering from migraine. For us, it’s personal.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>The Role:</b></div>
<div>The Business Systems Analyst, Veeva Solutions is responsible for providing direction and technical support for Alder’s Veeva CRM and Veeva Vault installations; which includes identifying business requirements, coordinating implementation of system changes, running UAT sessions, providing functional system support, and interfacing with Sales, Sales Operations, Business Analytics, and Training groups to ensure effective solution adoption. This position will engage various user groups to gather requirements, design solutions, and implement and maintain the Veeva environments.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Specific responsibilities will include:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Ensuring Veeva applications meet business needs by designing business enhancements, optimizing business processes, and addressing process gaps</li>
<li>Performing administrative functions in the Veeva CRM and Vault platforms; maintain user accounts, load data reports, resolve end-user technical and functional issues and monitor and report on system performance and utilization</li>
<li>Managing and triaging the operations support queue for incidents, service requests and enhancements</li>
<li>Preparing and facilitating User Acceptance Testing sessions with end users</li>
<li>Partnering with the Data Management group on Master Data Management projects and activities</li>
<li>Functioning as Project Leader for various upgrade and process improvement efforts</li>
<li>Managing internal and external resources engaged in the development and support of Veeva CRM and Veeva Vault</li>
<li>Providing Level 1 support for our HQ, Sales, Marketing end-users</li>
<li>Authoring and maintaining user and functional requirements, managing user acceptance testing, as well as documenting, managing and implementing system change requests</li>
<li>Supporting the delivery of end-user training</li>
<li>Maintaining Veeva system certifications and knowledge</li>
<li>Maintaining knowledge of the Veeva platform and related solution industry, objectively understands and evaluates the capabilities of tools and technologies; compares with the needs of the business and recommends potential solutions to business leadership</li>
</ul>

<li>At least 3 years of “hands-on” Veeva and/or salesforce.com CRM as well as Veeva Vault configuration and support is highly preferred</li>
<li>Veeva Qualifications/Certifications a major plus especially across CRM, Vault and Network</li>
<li>Solid domain knowledge of Master Data Management workings and processes such as Veeva Network/Reltio</li>

<li>Proven ability to communicate technical concepts to non-technical audiences, and business concepts to technical audiences</li>
<li>Demonstrated excellent Data Analysis skills and experience with data integrations and interfaces</li>
<li>“Hands-on” experience working with the following business areas: Sales, Marketing, Commercial, Medical Affairs</li>
