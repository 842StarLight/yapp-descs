<h2>ACCOUNT EXECUTIVE - SALESFORCE SERVICES</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Role</b></h3>
<p>Penrod, located in Milwaukee’s Historic Third Ward, and established offices in Minneapolis and Dallas, is seeking an Chicago based Sales Hacker / Hustler to join their rapidly growing team! We're looking for an energetic, dynamic, and driven individual with top-notch interpersonal skills for the role of Account Executive.</p>
<p>
Our Account Executive serves as a trusted advisor to our clients. This role is ideal for a technical consultant or sales executive with an in-depth understanding of sales effectiveness. This position involves travel; willingness to do so is a must.</p>
<p>
Penrod is a culture-driven consulting agency specializing in Salesforce.com. As a premier Salesforce.com partner, we help businesses develop and implement cloud solutions that create customer success.</p>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h2>
<p><b>What You Must Do To Be Successful In This Position:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Selling innovative solutions to new and existing accounts</li>
<li>Manage deal cycles and build partnerships with enterprise level decision makers.</li>
<li>Strategic account planning and management of partner relationships in region</li>
<li>Engaging companies and leading discussions around business processes</li>
<li>Become an expert in Penrod’s solutions for businesses of all sizes and adapt recommendations quickly to suit varying client needs</li>
<li>Build relationships with Salesforce team members to co-sell products and solutions in order to build your own book business</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What Skills &amp; Experience We’d Like You To Have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in selling professional services or technology solutions</li>
<li>Experience in Healthcare and Life Sciences - specifically Payer, Provider, Medical Device, and Pharma</li>
<li>Ability to lead conversations around vision and innovation</li>
<li>A knack for complex problem solving and business analysis</li>
<li>Self-motivated, entrepreneurial, and comfortable dealing with ambiguity</li>
<li>Ability to effectively prioritize and manage tasks within a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work and collaborate within a high-performing team</li>
</ul>
