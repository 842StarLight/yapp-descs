<p><b>About Midwest Tape</b></p>
<p>Midwest Tape is a library-dedicated full-service media distributor providing physical and digital media to thousands of libraries across North America. We are strong believers in continually innovating to better serve our customers and deliver the best experience possible for the staff and patrons of public libraries. Our International Headquarters is located in Holland, Ohio at the facility that we built in 2016, to house our distribution center and support teams for Midwest Tape, hoopla digital, the fastest growing app serving libraries and Dreamscape Publishing, the Library-First publisher of Audiobooks and Video. As we build our team to support the ongoing growth of our business, we are looking for enthusiastic team players dedicated to delivering best-in-class service for our library customers and their patrons.<br>
 </p>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Position Goal</b><br>
Increase efficiency and stability of current custom developed and acquired information systems. Increase collaboration with stakeholders and IT application owners to improve positive impact along with the quality of Midwest Tape's core applications.<br>
<br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
<ul><li>Enable delivery, communications, and design around robust internal IT systems</li></ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Contribute development code to some key projects in order to analyze patterns</li>
<li>Collaborate with several segments of the IT organization to deliver multi-platform products</li>
<li>Analyze and audit system workflow and code quality</li>
<li>Focus on supporting company growth through helping to focus on key stability areas</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Foster continuous improvement in both business processes and IT systems</li>
<li>Strengthen the relationship between analytic data / reporting and new development features</li>
<li>Contribute to project management strategic planning</li>
<li>Interact with release communications and quality assurance staff during the development cycle</li>
<li>Assist in managing the production support workload while strategically planning feature releases</li>
<li>Analyze current IT workflows with stakeholders to identify areas of inefficiency with internal systems</li>
<li>Strengthen current documentation and business education of data and systems operations</li>
</ul>
