<h2>Channel Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>phoenixNAP, a global provider of premier Cloud and IaaS solutions, is seeking a highly-motivated individual with a background in software and cloud solutions sales that is specific to the Master Agency, Cloud Brokerage, VAR, SI, &amp; MSP channel partner sales framework. Prospect should be highly skilled in channel leadership, governance, sales execution, team building &amp; management, and channel strategy. The role will report to the Channel Organization and execute a sustainable channel strategy that delivers accretive results to phoenixNAP’s indirect channel business. This quota-carrying position will be responsible for the creation and execution of a regional channel partner strategy, channel sales management, reseller enablement, unrelenting productivity, and new-market development. The individual will need to be well-respected within the regional market with a proven track record of success. A data-analytics approach, results-driven style, strong leadership attributes, and a commanding understanding of software and cloud channels are essential. The ability to influence and develop mutually beneficial peer relationships within phoenixNAP is also vital.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Regionally drive and proactively evolve a sustainable channel and alliance strategy which drives execution and predictable results in the respective area markets throughout the region.</li>
<li>As regional success increases, create job functions and descriptions to execute against the overall regional, national, and global channel strategy.</li>
<li>Ultimately build and lead a best-in-class channel sales team dedicated to the success of the region.</li>
<li>Excel at business planning and governance that is in direct alignment with phoenixNAP’s Channel Team.</li>
<li>Create and maintain executive relationships with partners aligned with phoenixNAP’s’ channel strategy.</li>
<li>Develop route-to-market strategies for phoenixNAP’s entire product portfolio within regional reseller base.</li>
<li>Align resources for co-development of partner owned solutions.</li>
<li>Introduce, develop and accelerate nascent growth for all phoenixNAP offerings within all applicable vertical markets.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Extensive cloud channel sales experience.</li>
<li>Proven success at managing Technology Services Brokerages and Master Agents for Colocation, Cloud and IaaS services such as: Intelisys, Telarus, Sandler, Bridgepoint etc.</li>
<li>Experience fostering and growing win-win relationships with large corporate resellers such as: CDW, SHI, Connection, Zones, PCM, Etc.</li>
<li>Proven track record of building and managing high performance teams is highly desirable.</li>
<li>8+ years of channel management, partner programs, and strategic alliance experience.</li>
<li>5+ yrs. experience in executive-interfacing roles with enterprise information technology vendors and solutions providers.</li>
<li>Documented software and cloud channel success with sustained execution and demonstrable results.</li>
<li>Strong background in software and cloud solutions development, route-to-market planning, and successful execution.</li>
<li>Must have strong technical, analytical, and financial acumen.</li>
<li>Approximately 50% travel and ability to work both remotely and independently is an imperative.</li>
<br>
</ul>
