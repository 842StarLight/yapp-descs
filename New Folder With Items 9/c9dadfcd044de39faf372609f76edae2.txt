<h2>Residential Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Property Manager is responsible for -the overall management of an assigned property to satisfy the<br>requirement of the ownership and tenants, to preserve and increase the value and integrity of the<br>property and to meet the financial objectives of the ownership and management.<br>Responsibilities<br>1. Marketing</p>
<p> Implement marketing initiatives to achieve occupancy goals<br> Maintain thorough product knowledge of the property and competitors<br> Manage and control advertising budgets<br> Advertise and fill vacancies<br> Review all move-in’s, move-out’s, and renewals for approval<br> Ensure leasing office, tour path and grounds, vacant apartments, amenities</p>
<p>and general curb appeal of the community is impeccably clean</p>
<p> Keep rental at optimum capacity<br> Maintain all statutory required records<br> Process monthly rent payments and security deposits</p>
<p>2. Resident Relations</p>
<p> Develop and maintain first class customer service relationships with</p>
<p>prospects and residents</p>
<p> Manage and resolve resident concerns<br> Monitor outstanding work orders and constantly communicates with</p>
<p>maintenance staff to ensure 24 hour guarantee</p>
<p> Plan, organize and conduct resident functions as needed and as directed</p>
<p>by Property Management<br> Enforce terms of rental agreements<br> Coordinate default proceedings and evictions</p>
<p>3. Maintenance</p>
<p> Ensure security of premises<br> Organize incoming and outgoing inspections and prepare inspection reports<br> Implement preventative maintenance programs<br> Assist with monitoring of capital improvement projects<br> Assist with obtaining bids and contracts from vendors<br> Ensure monthly R&amp;M expense cost do not exceed budgeted amount<br> Enter and process all PO’s and invoices</p>
<p>4. Staff Management</p>
<p> hire, train and evaluate property staff<br> Direct and control all personnel and resources to ensure property is properly</p>
<p>maintained and owner objectives are met</p>
<p>Skills and Requirements<br>Strong professionalism, communication and “people skills”<br>Superior sales skills, Verbal math and reasoning skills<br>Well organized with ability to prioritize and multi-task<br>Must possess attention to detail<br>Team oriented with ability to work independently<br>Computer skills, Microsoft word, and knowledge of internet, Email and Social Media<br>Please note that this job description is not all-inclusive. Any addition or deletion of duties may be changed at any time. Receipt or possession of this job description does not constitute a contract of employment.</p>
