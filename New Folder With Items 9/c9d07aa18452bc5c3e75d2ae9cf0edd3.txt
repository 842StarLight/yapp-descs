<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h3>
<p>
Job Description: Rockford Homes has an immediate full-time position for an Estimating Budget Analyst Team Member. This position is a 40 hour per week office position. On a daily basis, you will be responsible for pricing on Single Family construction and the maintenance of the Estimating Database.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><div><ul>
<li>
Responsible for maintenance of estimating database of homebuilder software and distribution of pricing information.</li>
<li>
Review and analyze cost changes to provide and maintain accurate sales pricing and housing budgets.</li>
<li>
Run purchase orders for all new starts.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain option pricing and description database for all home plans.</li>
<li>
Complete and verify information for reports</li>
</ul></div></div>

<li>
Construction knowledge is a MUST.</li>
