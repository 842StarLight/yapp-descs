<p><b>About Spectra Gutter Systems:</b></p>
<p>Spectra Gutter Systems is the largest manufacturer and distributor of high-quality aluminum and copper gutter system products for the building industry in the United States. With 21 regional distribution locations across the county and further growth anticipated in the near future, Spectra Gutter Systems is a recognized leader in gutter system manufacturing and wholesale distribution.</p>
<p><b>Job Details</b></p>
<p><b>Process Customer Orders</b></p>
<p>The most important duty completed by Order Pullers is correctly processing customer orders and uploading them into their employerâ€™s database. This involves competency with order processing tools, such as barcode scanners.</p>
<p><b>Check Stock Using Computer</b></p>
<p>Once Order Pullers process customer orders, the next step is to check if the items are in stock. This is done using computers, so it requires competency with inventory management software.</p>
<p><b>Assist Order Checker in Order Accuracy Efforts</b></p>
<p>Accuracy is of the utmost importance in this job. Customer satisfaction depends solely on orders being filled correctly and arriving in a timely manner. This process starts with Order Pullers and Order Checkers working together to ensure every order is accurate.</p>
<p><b>Prepare Shipping Labels</b></p>
<p>Order Pullers are responsible for boxing up orders and getting them ready for shipping. This involves accurately inputting customer shipping information, and then using a commercial label printing machine to print the shipping labels.</p>
<p><b>Load and Unload Freight Containers</b></p>
<p>Loading and unloading freight containers is where the physical demand of the job comes into play. This duty can involve lifting items up to 50 pounds. It can also involve operating heavy machinery, such as forklifts, to move heavy loads.</p>

Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p><b>Reading Comprehension Skills: </b>Order Pullers call upon their reading comprehension skills when processing customer orders. They must be able to read and understand the types of information typically present on customer orders and shipping labels.</p>
<p><b>Active Listening Skills: </b>Pulling orders is very much a team process. Order Pullers have to be good listeners in order to follow the direction given to them by the Warehouse Manager. Order Pullers typically use headsets to communicate with each other.</p>
<p><b>Organizational Skills: </b>Order Pullers have to be highly organized in order to maximize their efficiency. They call upon their organizational skills when processing and filling customer orders.</p>
<p><b>Basic Computer Skills: </b>Order Pullers need basic computer skills to navigate the order processing and inventory management software they use on a daily basis. This knowledge is typically acquired through on-the-job training.</p>
<p><b>Prioritization Skills: </b>Order Pullers have multiple orders to process over the course of a day. Some may even get bonuses depending on how many they can complete during a day. This means they must be able to prioritize tasks to figure out the most efficient way to fill a given order.</p>
