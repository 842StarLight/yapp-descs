<h2>Social Media Marketing Strategist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Primary Objective of Position: </b>To create, implement, and analyze customer digital marketing campaigns that exceed customer expectations and ensure customer satisfaction and loyalty.</p>
<p><b>Customer Relations</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the digital marketing expert for assigned accounts</li>
<li>Work with the client to gain a thorough understanding of digital marketing needs; documenting conversations in CRM software.</li>
<li>Follow up with customer while campaign is underway to review and to assess customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Troubleshoot issues quickly and work toward a complete resolution the first time; documenting issues and resolution</li>
<li>Analyze, forecast, and report digital marketing results. Extract insights from campaign data, identify relevant trends, and provide well developed, proactive recommendations to enhance and exceed overall client marketing goals and objectives</li>
<li>Provide the analysis and insight report to the customer and/or the sales consultant monthly</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Marketing Strategies and Tactics</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and present marketing strategies and campaigns to customer</li>
<li>Implement customer approved strategy and campaign by creating and managing tactics such as:</li>
<li>paid search,</li>
<li>paid social,</li>
<li>display marketing,</li>
<li>e-mail marketing, and</li>
<li>video marketing</li>
<li>Test and optimize bids, keywords, text ads, image ads, video ads, and landing pages during the campaign to improve campaign performance.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
