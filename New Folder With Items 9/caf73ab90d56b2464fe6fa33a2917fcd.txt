<h2>Accounting Manager - Boise, ID</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>TEAM UP WITH US!
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><div>As we expand into Boise we are looking for a dynamic and ambitious Accounting Manager to join our team. The Accounting Manager will report directly to the Corporate Controller, and from day one the Controller will spend a lot of time mentoring and training this person to get them up to speed.
</div></div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>DO YOU HAVE WHAT IT TAKES?
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>WHAT YOU’LL DO:</b></div>
<div>
The Accounting Manager will have his/her hands in all aspects of accounting including:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage Month-End Close</li>
<li>
Manage G/L, Accounts Payable, and Cash Applications team members</li>
<li>
Perform Accounting &amp; Analysis Projects for the CFO &amp; Controller</li>
<li>
Calculate Quarterly Bonuses &amp; Commissions</li>
<li>
Review Sales &amp; Use and Property Tax Returns</li>
<li>
Manage Expense Reporting Process through Concur</li>
<li>
Software Sales Revenue Recognition &amp; COGS
</li>
<li>Constantly look for ways to Innovate and improve our accounting process.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
WHAT YOU’LL NEED:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree; Accounting or other relevant field required</li>
<li>
4-7 years of experience in a senior accounting role, strong technical accounting knowledge.</li>
<li>
Supervisory experience required, experience working with remote teams preferred.</li>
<li>
CPA candidates only, CPA license is a bonus.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Intacct, BlackLine, Coupa, and Concur preferred, but not required.</li>
<li>
Superior skills in Excel, Word, PowerPoint, and Outlook required.</li>
<li>
High business ethics, team player, ambitious, smart, coachable, great communicator.</li>
<li>
Ideal candidate will have big goals, and the work ethic to make those goals a reality.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
