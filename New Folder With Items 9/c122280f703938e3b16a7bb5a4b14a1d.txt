<h2>Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB DESCRIPTION
</h1>
<div>
<p>WTSP 10, the CBS affiliate in Tampa (the 11th largest market), is looking for a dynamic <b>Sales Coordinator</b>! The Sales Coordinator will be responsible for interacting daily with local direct accounts and/ or local agencies with specific responsibilities around the maintenance of the local client, regular tracking of audience delivery and providing a high level of customer service.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities include but not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Daily interaction with existing agency and direct client business; this responsibility includes maintenance of client schedules on television and online, regular tracking of client audience delivery using Neilson and Comscore ratings, developing presentations, merchandising the station and generally providing a high level of customer service.</li>
<li>Assists Account Managers and Account Executives with national and/or local business in maintenance of contracts, order input, make goods, and overall service.</li>
<li>Manages the implementation of programs and schedules sold by the sales team; this includes entering orders and demonstrating proof of performance.</li>
<li>Understands, evaluates and manages sales inventory.</li>
<li>Some filing, answering phones and organizing of station events.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB REQUIREMENTS
</h1>
<div>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 1 year in a broadcast sales environment preferred.</li>
<li>1-3 years of WideOrbit Traffic and Sales experience preferred.</li>
<li>Experience working within Salesforce preferred.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office suite</li>
<li>Intermediate skill level working in Photoshop preferred</li>
<li>A high level of writing skills preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record of meeting deadlines and deliverables.</li>
<li>Creative and able to adapt quickly to change.</li>
<li>Solid interpersonal and customer service skills a must.</li>

</ul>
