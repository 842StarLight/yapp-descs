<h2>Call Center Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Virginia Tire &amp; Auto Call Center Agents love to engage with people. They are dedicated sales professionals who prioritize customer service and building relationships with your customers. They are expert multi-taskers, managing software programs and phone calls and have top-notch typing skills. They are product knowledge experts, constantly seeking new information, and can communicate that knowledge to our customers. They are genuine and attentive in all customer interactions.</p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU’LL DO</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the front line, point of contact for customers and act as a connection between their needs and our technicians.</li>
<li>Work efficiently both individually and as a team member to ensure each customer receives the best service possible – after all, we’re in this together!</li>
<li>Schedule appointments for customers, communicate status updates, and follow up as needed throughout their repair and after</li>
<li>Embody all aspects of the Pinnacle Performance Sales process and constantly strive for customer satisfaction in the service delivery</li>
<li>Be forward thinking, anticipating customer needs and being empowered to make the car care experience stress free</li>
<li>Demonstrate expert product knowledge of VTA tire collection and “tires&amp;” program</li>
<li>Answer 50-80 incoming calls per day and perform online chats in a timely, professional and friendly manner.</li>
</ul>
