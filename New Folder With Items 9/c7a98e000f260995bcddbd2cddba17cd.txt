<b>CLASS TITLE:</b> Product Marketing Manager – Digital Dentistry<br>
<br>
<b>LOCATION:</b> Hoffman Estates, IL<br>

<b><i>Who We Are:</i></b><br>
<i>Planmeca is one of the largest privately-held dental equipment companies in the industry. Our technology-driven product line includes digital, dental imaging (x-ray) equipment, dental CAD/CAM systems, dental units, and cabinetry. We provide imaging and software solutions for all dental specialties and partner with dental schools, government, universities, and dental service organizations. In the U.S., our products are available through authorized dealers and distribution partnerships.</i><br>
<br>
<b><i>What We Offer:</i></b><br>
<i>We offer a professional work environment, training, growth and advancement. Competitive compensation and a benefits package including a medical, dental, and vision plan, voluntary benefits including life, short-term and long-term disability, 401(k) savings plan with company match, holiday pay.</i><br>
<br>
<b><i>Come Join our Planmeca USA Team!</i></b><br>
<i>You are the dynamic individual we are seeking for our Product Marketing Manager to support our digital dentistry portfolio. This key position is responsible for developing and managing Planmeca’s marketing strategy for our digital dentistry products, including CAD/CAM and 3D printing. You as the ideal candidate should have progressive experience in the dental industry and proven results in developing multi-level marketing programs, sales and education tools and supporting product launches. Must be a self-starter with the aptitude to work in a fast-paced sales driven organization</i><br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES</b>:
<ul>
<li>Conduct ongoing market analysis in North America.</li>
<li>Work closely with the global product team to provide strategic direction on product development.</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with sales, technical support, training and product teams to deliver best-in-class customer experience.</li>
<li>Develop effective multi-channel (print, web, social, etc.) sales and marketing programs that support business strategy to grow the product segment.</li>
<li>Work closely with Key Opinion Leaders to develop content, case studies and programs that support product strategy.</li>
<li>Create sales tools that support our internal sales teams, dealer partners and KOL’s efforts to effectively demonstrate the return on investment and increased production for dental practices.</li>
<li>Manage development of campaigns with internal design and communications team.</li>
<li>Maintain product message maps with competitive market analysis to ensure competitive positioning and communication strategy.</li>
<li>Lead the development and delivery of presentations on digital solutions, both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Lead our lab partner marketing strategy.</li>
<li>Meets company standards pertaining to quantity and quality of work performed on an ongoing basis, performing all work-related tasks in a manner that is in compliance with all Company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Adheres to company policies, procedures and directives regarding standards of workplace behavior in completing job duties and assignments
</li>

</ul>
