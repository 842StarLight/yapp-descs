<h2>Account Manager, Scaled Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Overview:</b><br>
The Revenue team is the driving force of Weedmaps and we are looking to add an Account Manager to work with some of the world's fastest growing advertisers to manage and develop their businesses and brands on Weedmaps. Based in our Irvine office, a successful Account Manager will leverage analytical, strategic, and relationship-building skills to advise customers on advertising products, and help them achieve strong ROI on their spend with Weedmaps. The right person will be comfortable operating on a fast paced sales floor, and will be able to adapt quickly to change, while maintaining a positive attitude. Attention to detail is also critical in ensuring sales are administered and booked properly.
<br><br>
<b>The impact you'll make:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Analyze customers' marketing objectives and determine which Weedmaps advertising products will best achieve outcomes</li>
<li>Advise Account Executives on upsell opportunities with existing customers (based on performance, new product launches, etc.)</li>
<li>Advise customers how to optimize their Weedmaps listings and products to drive ROI</li>
<li>Lead ongoing client education for all product lines</li>
<li>Provide campaign updates to customers</li>
<li>Support administration and booking of sales to new and existing customers</li>
<li>Set up clients for ongoing billing and payments</li>
</ul>
<b>What you've accomplished:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BS/BA Degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>3+ years of experience in account management and growing a book of business (online, digital media, or software is a plus)</li>
<li>Experience working with software, ad technology, or digital marketing solutions</li>
<li>Experience working with Salesforce, including creating custom reports and analysis</li>
<li>Comfort with, and ability to operate at an aggressive, accelerated, iterative pace in a dynamic environment</li>
<li>Comfort with exercising own judgment and discretion in educating clients and making product recommendations</li>
<li>Strong planning and organizational skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate, present, and influence credibly and effectively</li>
</ul>

<b>About Weedmaps:</b><br>
Founded in 2008, Weedmaps is a leading technology and software infrastructure provider to the cannabis industry. Our suite of cloud-based software and data solutions includes point of sale, logistics and ordering solutions that enable customers to scale their businesses while complying with the complex and disparate regulations applicable to the cannabis industry. In addition, our platform provides consumers with information regarding cannabis products across web and mobile platforms, including listing local retailers and brands, facilitating product discovery and allowing consumers to educate themselves on cannabis and its history, uses and legal status. Headquartered in Irvine, California, Weedmaps employs more than 400 professionals around the world, with offices including Barcelona, Berlin, Boston, Denver, New York, Phoenix and Toronto.
