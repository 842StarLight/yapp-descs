<h2>Office Assistant / Receptionist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Tyson Furniture is hiring a <b>Office Assistant / Receptionist / Cashier</b> . This position answers and routes incoming calls, verifies the correctness of sales invoices, collects monies from customers, determines and provides customer balances and handles routine administrative duties as well as providing administrative support to other departments. This position is full-time with company paid life insurance, company payed health insurance, 401k, vacation and sick pay.</p>
<p><b>Apply</b> in person at Main Showroom 109 Broadway, Black Mountain Call: (828) 669-5000</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<p>High School diploma required.</p>
<p>Must be a problem solver.</p>
<p>Must be a self-starter.</p>
<p>Must be able to work Monday - Friday with alternating Saturdays.</p>
<p>Must be organized.</p>
<p>Must be able to work independently.</p>
<p>Must maintain personal appearance by following Tysons dress code requirements.</p>
<p>Strong computer skills are required.</p>
<p>Subject to random drug and alcohol testing.</p>
<p>Working Knowledge of MS Office applications Word and Excel.</p>
<p>Must possess basic math skills.</p>
<p>Self-motivated.</p>
<p>Positive attitude.</p>
<p>Excellent customer service skills.</p>
<p>Excellent time management skills.</p>
<p><b>Duties: </b></p>
<p>Answer multiple phone lines and route calls to appropriate personnel. Check voice mail and route messages appropriately. Post outgoing mail / Sort and distribute incoming mail. Administratively assist all departments as needed. Post invoices and receipts into the company's accounting software: Sage 50 Accounting (formally PeachTree Accounting) Verify the correctness of sales invoices with a 10 key adding machine. Interact with customers. Collect payments from customers. Perform data/order entry tasks on a routine basis.</p>
<p>This job description in no way states or implies that these are the only duties to be performed by this employee. Employee will be required to follow any other instructions and to perform any other duties upon the request of his/her supervisor.</p>
<p><b>Physical Demands: </b></p>
<p>The ability to perform all duties associated with the position includes, but is not limited to lifting up to 30 lbs., standing, bending, stooping, prolonged sitting, and repetitive motion associated with typing and answering phones.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Preferred)</li></ul>
