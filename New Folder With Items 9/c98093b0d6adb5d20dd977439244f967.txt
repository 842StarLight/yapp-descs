<p><b>
License and Title Coordinator</b>:</p>
<p>
This position will focus primarily on managing the vehicle registration and renewal process for all new and existing fleet vehicles. In addition, the Coordinator will become the subject matter expert and serve as the primary point of contact for state specific questions from Enterprise sales offices and customers.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate is a professional communicator, has excellent customer service skills and is a self-motivated team player.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
License and Title Representative:</b></p>
<p>
This person will primarily be responsible for mailing vehicle Registrations and Renewals while providing administrative support to the L&amp;T teams including maintaining the Xerox process, updating L&amp;T databases, distributing mail and paperwork to groups and other administrative duties.</p>
<p>
This is an excellent opportunity for someone who has excellent customer service skills and is extremely organized, detail-oriented, with the ability to juggle multiple responsibilities.</p>

Responsibilities:</b><br>
<div><b>License and Title Coordinator:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Act as primary contact for license &amp; title administrative needs for assigned state(s)</li>
<li>
Process vehicle initial registrations &amp; renewals</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain relationships with internal teams, state motor vehicle departments and license &amp; title services</li>
<li>
Monitor vehicle registration and renewal status with state motor vehicle departments and license &amp; title services</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot license &amp; title problems for customers and groups</li>
<li>
Negotiate state motor vehicle department and license &amp; title service processing fees</li>
<li>
Act as the liaison between customers, groups, and state motor vehicle departments</li>
<li>
Contact drivers requesting information required by states for renewals</li>
<li>
Prepare backup of transactions for groups</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
License and Title Representative:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Organize and create efficiencies in sending out vehicle Renewal stickers and license plates</div></li>
<li>
Assist and support Coordinators with renewals, registrations, and other assignments</li>
<li>
Create and update database notes for Renewals and Registrations</li>
<li>
Distribute mail for the L&amp;T teams and send weekly mail to group offices</li>
<li>
Assist with copying Registrations, Renewals and organizing paperwork to Xerox</li>
<li>
Assist with preparing drop offs and pickups of registrations to local DMV</li>
</ul>
