<h2>Customer Care/Administrative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>This position is full time, and offers a complete benefits package.<br>
Seeking an experienced <b><i>Internal Customer Care Representative</i></b> in our Fairfield, OH, facility.</p>
<p><b><i>
Duties of this position are:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answering incoming calls and inquiries from customers, potential customers and MSI-Viking outside sales team.</li>
<li>Providing timely and accurate answers to requests for information about our products and services.</li>
<li>Suggesting information about product alternatives, accessories and services.</li>
<li>Resolving product or service problems by clarifying the customer’s complaint; determining the cause of the problem; selecting and explaining the best solution to solve the problem; expediting correction or adjustment.</li>
<li>Opening and updating accounts by recording account information.</li>
<li>Preparing and processing customer quotes, orders, and changes according to established department policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Partner with sales reps to meet and exceed customer’s service expectations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Candidate Qualifications for this position are:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of three years customer service experience.</li>
<li>Some experience in manufacturing quality processes, precision gages and metrology systems and the servicing of same equipment is a major plus. However we may be willing to train the right person with an insatiable willingness to learn.</li>
<li>Excellent telephone skills including listening skills, speaking voice and a positive, friendly demeanor.</li>
<li>Ability to understand and focus on creating a positive customer experience.</li>
<li>Ability to maintain composure and a positive temperament in customer complaint situations.</li>
<li>Resolving conflicts, problem analyzing and creating solutions through in-depth, excellent product knowledge.</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills with experience in order entry, accounting (ERP) software and Microsoft Office programs.</li>
<li>Excellent time management and productivity skills.</li>
<li>Ability to be a team player and consistently contribute to the good of the company, the customer service department and sales team.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Additional Requirements:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to prove eligibility to work in the US as supported by E-Verify records in accordance with SC state law</li>
<li>Ability to regularly pass drug-free testing</li>
<li>Very strong work ethic and interpersonal ethics</li>
</ul>
