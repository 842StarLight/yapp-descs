<h2>Commercial Estimator - Seattle Area</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>A National leader in the commercial/industrial roofing industry, has an exceptional opportunity for an entry level, full time Roofing Estimator to support our sales and operations team in the <b>Seattle, WA</b>.</p>
<p><b>
Summary of Job Description:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Perform project take-offs from Architect/Construction Spec Drawings.</li>
<li>
Prepare project submittals for specified contract projects.</li>
<li>
Travel to customer's locations and inspect roofs and perform take offs</li>
<li>
Communicate with customers and check roofs for problem areas.</li>
<li>
Measures roofs and estimate the amount of roofing material and labor needed to re-roof.</li>
<li>
Will work closely with the sales force to provide them with accurate information for the creation of proposals for customers.</li>
<li>
Prepare estimates and quotations and detailed proposals through the use of a proprietary computer system</li>
<li>
Perform regular site visits to on-going projects to support operations and enhance the customer communication and experience</li>
<li>
Review projects and prepare regular job progress reports</li>
<li>
Will need to travel out of town occasionally.</li>
<li>
Market to new potential prospects in the area.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Organizational, Analytical, Problem Solving and People Skills.</li>
<li>
Understanding of new construction and specification driven bidding processes a plus.</li>
<li>
Ability to read/interpret architectural drawings and perform project take offs.</li>
<li>
Ability to compile and prepare project submittals.</li>
<li>
Ability to track project change orders.</li>
<li>
Able to complete project closeout documentation as required.</li>
<li>
Computer skills (proficient in MS Word, Excel and Bid Estimating Software)</li>
<li>
General Construction, Roofing and/or Sales Experience a Plus</li>
<li>
A Valid driver's license in good standing</li>
<li>
Able to climb up &amp; down ladders to minimum heights of 25 feet</li>
<li>
Experience with: THERMOPLASTIC, BUR, METAL, SHINGLES, EPDM, SPF FOAM and COATINGS a plus but will train the right individual.</li>
</ul>
