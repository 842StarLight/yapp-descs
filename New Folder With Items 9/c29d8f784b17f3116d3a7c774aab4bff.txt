<h2>Center Associate: Paso Robles</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Center Associate delivers world-class customer service to all retail customers by receiving and processing packages for courier shipment and operating copiers, fax machines, binding equipment, laminating machines, point-of-sale devices and various computer programs (Adobe Illustrator, Publisher and PhotoShop). The Associate expertly advises our valued customers by providing accurate information on our array of products, services, and best-value options and exhibits confidence by knowing best practices related to the industry.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate has previous retail sales experience, strong computer and internet skills, a high school diploma or GED, a friendly and genuinely helpful demeanor, and a professional appearance. He or she must be a fast learner who makes an effort to learn all aspects of the business in the shortest possible time.<br>
<br>
<b><i>PLEASE READ:</i></b><br>
The associate hired will work approx 32-40 hours per week. We are open M-F 8:00am-6:30pm and Saturdays from 9:00am-3:00pm. The associate will work the closing shift 12:00PM - 6:45PM each day M-F and every other Saturday (8:45am-3:15pm). Please do NOT apply if you can not work these hours. This is NOT a seasonal position.</p>
