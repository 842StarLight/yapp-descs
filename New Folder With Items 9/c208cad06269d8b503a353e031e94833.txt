<h2>Marketing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Marketing Analyst will report to the Business Marketing Manager and work closely with Product Strategy, Business Line teams and Operations to research and report on product and campaign performance, marketing strategy and execution. The Marketing Analyst will measure the performance of marketing activities aimed at driving awareness, brand equity and revenue growth for the bank across multiple business lines. The ideal candidate would work with finance to use analytical modeling to forecast the impact of marketing campaigns and programs, adhere to budget goals and build forward-looking marketing strategies.</p>
<p>
Duties and Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Support marketing and business lines with reporting, research and market intelligence to support product and promotion campaign planning and execution</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Actively produce, manage and analyze robust reports on banking products and performance across all business lines</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Actively manage and produce reports and monitor performance of sales activities and marketing programs and campaigns to analyze success and develop/recommend strategic improvements to key stakeholders.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Provide market intelligence, including information and insights regarding customers, customer behavior, markets, competitors and campaign effectiveness.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist in preparing final project deliverables, including spreadsheets, pivot tables and PowerPoint decks</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Partner with Marketing team members to serve as a key member of the Marketing team, leveraging expert-level marketing skills to drive key plan outcomes.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Develop targeted audience lists for current members and prospects based on data and demographics</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Work with finance and treasury to develop product profitability models backed by data</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist Marketing team with other projects as needed.<br>
</div>
</li>
</ul>
