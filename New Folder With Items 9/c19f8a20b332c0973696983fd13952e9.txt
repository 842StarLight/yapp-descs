<h2>Senior Manager, Entertainment Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>OVERVIEW OF THE COMPANY
</b></p>Fox Corporation
<p></p>Under the FOX banner, we produce and distribute content through some of the world’s leading and most valued brands, including: FOX News, FOX Sports, the Fox Network, and the FOX Television Stations. We empower a diverse range of creators to imagine and develop culturally significant content, while building an organization that thrives on creative ideas, operational expertise and strategic thinking.
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>JOB DESCRIPTION
</b></h2>
<p>We are looking for a Senior Manager to join the FOX Sales Entertainment Partnership team as a digital lead supporting the Vice President, Digital Entertainment Partnerships in the execution of Integrated Sales Partnership campaigns. You are responsible for execution of all digital elements across branded content campaigns for scripted and unscripted entertainment partnerships. You work closely with many different departments including the Creative Brand Partnership, Production, and Social teams, all based in LA. As the Senior Manager, you work directly with the Research and Sales teams in New York to ensure success of digital elements of these campaigns.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>A SNAPSHOT OF YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute branded content campaigns across digital platforms, whether in support of a larger linear partnership or as a stand-alone digital deal
</li>
<li>Coordinate campaigns with FOX social team and social media agencies
</li>
<li>Assist in strategy of social platform monetization for network partnerships
</li>
<li>Optimize branded content across social media platforms
</li>
<li>Proactively ideate digital and social branded content concepts
</li>
<li>Respond to incoming RFPs in conjunction with linear VPs to deliver effective multi-platform campaign
</li>
<li>Track, analyze, and communicate quantitative and qualitative metrics of branded content campaigns over the FOX digital footprint to agencies and advertisers
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU WILL NEED
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 5 years of digital media/advertising experience
</li>
<li>Experience managing social media budgets across multiple platforms
</li>
<li>Knowledge of products and best practices across popular social media platforms
</li>
<li>Detail oriented, team player, that can effectively communicate with high level internal stakeholders (including Sales, Legal, Research, Production, and Marketing), and external partners-in-content at the agency and client level.
</li>
<li>Proficiency in Photoshop and video editing software preferred, with the ability to efficiently create branded static and video mock-ups for clients
</li>
<li>Technology enthusiast preferred
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree is preferred
</li>
</ul>
