<p><b>Position Title
</b></p>
<p>Value Engineer (Warehouse Management - US)
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Location
</b></p>
<p>Virtual – United States
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Overview<br>
</b></p>
<p><br>
This position is part of the Global Value Engineering team which supports the global sales organization. The team is a presales type, client facing unit that creates and presents financially-based business cases for JDA’s clients as part of the sales cycle. This team interacts with clients throughout the week and travels to client sites as appropriate. Key team activities include discovery with the client, collaboration with other members of JDA’s sales team, creating value business cases and client presentations. Working on special projects is included in this job function.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to handle all Value Analysis tasks including discovery, business case creation, make revisions, client communication and executive presentation.
</li>
<li>Supports global sales organization. Regions include North America, EMEA, Latin America and Asia Pacific.
</li>
<li>Conducts effective discovery sessions with customer prospects.
</li>
<li>Defines and convincingly communicates JDA’s products and services value proposition.
</li>
<li>Broad understanding of JDA’s products and services with the goal of communicating the unique value position they provide the marketplace.
</li>
<li>Defines the general messaging and presentation of the value business case unique to specific sales cycles.
</li>
<li>Guides the value positioning to include addressing a client’s unique business challenge while considering competitive and industry standards.
</li>
<li>Guide and assist team to deliver highly skilled presentations to customer prospects.
</li>
<li>Works with CoE team managing the benchmarking library.
</li>
<li>Helps develop self-help tools and collateral for the global sales organization.
</li>
<li>Develops and maintains strong working relationships with all functions and teams within JDA to effectively drive the achievement of goals.
</li>
<li>Develops strong client relationships that will cultivate business opportunities.
</li>
<li>Drives and successfully leads team against measurable set targets, goals and objectives.
</li>
<li>Monitors and reports key team or individual performance information and metrics to the position’s manager.
</li>
<li>Works closely with peers and the position’s manager to set strategy for the team while working closely with peers to ensure proper implementation and follow-through.
</li>
<li>Participates in creating operational efficiencies for the team.
</li>
<li>Participates in prioritizing field and in-office issues to ensure effective resolution.
</li>
<li>Participates in weekly meeting with the CoE team.
</li>
<li>Key role in driving the quality and innovation of the value selling creation and presentations in sales cycles and building the framework surrounding all related activities to ensure that it can scale with success.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</b></p>

</li>
<li>MBA Preferred
</li>

</li>
<li>Supply Chain certifications preferred (APICS, Six Sigma, etc.)
</li>
<li>Has advanced skills in Microsoft Office
</li>
<li>5+ years in sales or sales supporting role (presales, consulting, etc.)
</li>
</ul>
