<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
As a Partner and Customer Engineer, you are responsible for providing technical solutions and managing relationships with Google's largest and most strategic online publishers. As a liaison between clients and our technical staff, you flawlessly lead deployments, implementations and integrations of a variety of our product lines (such as Search, Mobile, Video and Commerce). From monitoring product performance to debugging and troubleshooting product code, you are the resident expert that addresses partner needs. You are a technical expert with an entrepreneurial drive and passion for client management.</p>
<p>
We are a team working to get millions of employees and organizations to go “Mobile First” with Android. As experts in mobile computing, the Android Enterprise Business team helps small and large enterprises, educational institutions and government agencies to discover the wonders of going mobile and work smarter. Our technical teams work with partners to design and implement solutions for our customer organizations with custom features, security and support - all with Google’s philosophy of innovation and ease of use in mind.
</p>
<p>Businesses that partner with Android come in all shapes and sizes and no one solution works for all. Your knowledge of the Android, Enterprise Mobility Management (EMM) and enterprise software ecosystems combined with your technical expertise keeps the big picture in focus while being able to dive into the unique challenges associated with technical app partnerships and integration.
</p>
<p>The Android Enterprise Business team is focused on enabling enterprises everywhere to adopt Android as their preferred mobile device platform. You will play a critical role in supporting our enterprise customers in piloting and deploying the Android Enterprise platform.
</p>
<p>Whether working with next-gen chipset manufacturers or emerging market telcos, Google's Android Partner Engineering team helps solve some of the toughest mobile technology problems. We work with teams throughout Google to develop solutions for strategic partners around the globe. We maintain crucial relationships with and build technical solutions for our long-term partners who are making Android into a world-changing platform. But we're just getting started.</p>
</div>
