<p>
Director, Buisness Development – Payments Technology</p>
<p>
Atlanta, GA</p>
<p>
Our client, a leading international payments technology is seeking a Director, Business Development. They offer an innovative and cutting-edge technology in the electronic payments space.</p>
<p>
For their Atlanta office, they are building out a Business Development and Sales team to grow their business in North America.</p>
<p>
The Director, Business Development will lead and manage all sales and account management activities related to Software Licensing. This includes lead generation, new client prospecting, consultative selling, and closing deals within the banking and payments sector.</p>
<p>
Target clients will include large banks, payments processors, networks, merchants, and emerging payments companies. The candidate will report to the Head of Sales for US.</p>
<p>
<b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and implement sales strategy and plan</li>
<li>Develop and manage a sales pipeline</li>
<li>Proactively identify and pursue opportunities based on industry developments and changes</li>
<li>Acquire new clients through prospecting, networking, cold calling, and leverage of existing contacts</li>
<li>Meet / exceed sales revenue and margin targets</li>
<li>Participate in sales calls and internal meetings as well as industry trade shows and events</li>
<li>Lead, manage, and ensure completion of RFPs</li>
<li>Establish relationships across functional areas within assigned accounts, including Procurement</li>
<li>Stay current on trends and developments within payments industry (US / Global)</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Essential Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong knowledge of banking, fintech, cards, and payments industry (including emerging payments solutions)</li>
<li>Sales and business development experience as well as established senior level contacts</li>
<li>Experience in selling to large-scale enterprise level clients</li>
<li>Track recording of meeting / exceeding sales quotas</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills - ability to persuade and influence</li>
<li>Ability to develop and deliver presentations, create proposals, and sales materials; negotiate and close contracts</li>
<li>Strong sense of professionalism</li>
<li>Flexibility to travel across the US</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>5 – 7 years of experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we offer:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive compensation and incentive plan</li>
