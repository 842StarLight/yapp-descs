<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About You</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>You value building relationships. You are comfortable making connections and cultivating partnerships.</li>
<li>You take ownership of your accounts, and take pride in their success, as well as your own.</li>
<li>You are a skilled written communicator. You’ll use summaries and reports to communicate findings, objectives and plans to the team. You’ll also help manage the day-to-day rhythm of projects.</li>
<li>You think broadly, and know how to juggle the big picture with getting things done.</li>
<li>You love to set your own course with our shared set of goals.</li>
<li>At MITC, you’ll have project meetings and then you go make things happen. You default to action. You get to make things happen without someone saying so.</li>
<li>You can wear many hats and have a strong desire to learn.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Things You Might Do</b></h3>
<p>An MITC Project Manager is responsible for the implementation of multiple customer projects simultaneously. Due to projects being at different stages and requiring different resources, the ideal candidate will have great leadership skills and will be able to coordinate their activities from project initiation to completion. The position will require you to be able to travel roughly 30% of the time to meet with clients in their offices across the U.S.</p>
