<b>JOB SUMMARY</b>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The Center of Media Excellence manages all global paid media functions across Marriott Global Marketing and brand teams supporting over $260 MM in media spending. The Senior Program Specialist reports to the Director, Media Operations and Industry Relations working internally with the global market executives, external agencies and media vendors. The responsibilities include to the support and oversight of the media and marketing investments, budgets and payments. Other responsibilities include but are not limited to management and reconciliation of all agencies invoicing in compliance with contractual terms, oversight of audit procedures, administration of all sales vendor and agency information, scopes and contracts.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsible for administering all marketing and media programs companywide in scope. Acts as an expert for the media and marketing investments and budgets being administered and makes judgements regarding compliance with the programs’ terms and parameters. Answers questions and provides standard information pertaining to programs being administered, details on spending, industry information and other frequently asked questions with internal and external stakeholders. Explains procedures and processes surrounding the programs and provides technical guidance as necessary globally across all 30 brands and with internal marketing teams. Provides technical interpretation of the programs, plans and documents as requested. Acts as accounting, contract and reporting contact with internal and external stakeholders and across the COE. Assists with the administrative duties of all industry relations and media vendor programs, plans, meeting and events, playbooks and presentations and other projects assigned by the Director.</div>
