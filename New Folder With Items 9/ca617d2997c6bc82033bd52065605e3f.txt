<h2>MARKETING AND COMMUNICATIONS SPECIALIST</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>POSITION: MARKETING AND COMMUNICATIONS SPECIALIST, PS100633</b></p>
<p><b>LOCATION: </b> <b>San Rafael, CA</b> – Must be able to drive to various Bay Area locations</p>
<p><b>SALARY RANGE: </b><b>$83,636.80 - $101,080 annually, plus excellent benefits (40-hour workweek)</b></p>
<ul><li>
<i>(Employee pays 7% of salary/wage toward CalPERS retirement plan</i><b>*)</b>
</li></ul>
<p><b>POSITION DESCRIPTION: </b></p>
<p>Under the general direction of the Marketing and Communications Director, the Marketing and Communications Specialist develops and implements comprehensive marketing and public information, materials, publications, and community outreach programs; assists in maintaining the District’s websites, email databases, and social media accounts; oversees and assists in the production and distribution of transit materials; and manages bus and ferry signage programs. The Marketing and Communications Specialist provides clear, concise, accurate, and consistent information on a wide range of District activities and services to a wide range of stakeholders, including the general public, customers, and District staff.</p>
<p><b>MINIMUM JOB REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p><b><i>Education/Experience Requirements</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Four-year college degree with major course work completed in communications, journalism, English, marketing, business, or a related field. Additional qualifying experience may be substituted for the education requirement on a year-for-year basis. Applicants must submit evidence of a degree or a written statement supporting qualifying experience in lieu of degree.</li>
<li>Experience will include a minimum of two years of recent position-related experience in marketing, written communications, editing, and publishing field.</li>
<li>A minimum of two years’ experience using personal computers and software programs with advanced skills in Microsoft Office applications such as Power Point, Word, and Excel. Experience using a website content management system is preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and edit written materials for a wide range of audiences on a variety of topics relative to District activities and services, including transit guides and schedule cards, maps, pamphlets, newsletters, brochures, customer notices, website postings, booklets, signs, email-based customer announcements, and informational posters</li>
<li>Assist with selling of advertising space, as needed</li>
<li>Research, organize, and interpret complex information from various District departments on a wide range of policy, planning, and operations related subjects. Translate this information into effective communications for various stakeholders in appropriate formats</li>
<li>Implement innovative approaches while developing communication outreach programs to meet District goals and objectives</li>
<li>Manage the District’s employer outreach program to increase commute ridership on the District’s transit system</li>
<li>Assist in maintaining the District’s Internet and Intranet websites, including posting information and content, and maintaining the District’s employee and targeted customer email databases</li>
<li>Assist in maintaining the District’s social media accounts, posting important service and event information on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and Transit app</li>
<li>Represent the department on a wide variety of District projects concerning marketing and customer service matters</li>
<li>Represent the District and participate in working groups with local and regional partner agencies</li>
<li>Develop and administer informal contracts with outside vendors, including printers and advertisers</li>
<li>Respond to daily public information requests via the telephone, Internet, on social media, and in writing</li>
<li>Plan, organize, and participate in various District-related programs, presentations, and events</li>
<li>Identify target audiences for specific outreach efforts</li>
<li>Coordinate closely with District staff across the entire organization</li>
<li>Exhibit regular and reliable performance and attendance</li>
<li>Learn and follow the District’s safe working practices, and safety and health rules</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES: </b></p>
<p><b><i>Knowledge of: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Principles and practices of website content management preferred</li>
<li>Principles and practices of current trends in social media technology</li>
<li>Principles and practices of public and community relations</li>
<li>Principles and practices of research</li>
<li>Principles and practices of print and electronic media, including digital imaging and web applications</li>
<li>Principles and practices of presentation preparation, delivery strategies, and techniques</li>
<li>Theories and applications of graphic design, print processes, and layout techniques</li>
<li>Computer programs described under “Education and Experience”</li>
<li>Strong organizational and time management skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Ability to: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Complete assignments with a high degree of independence and initiative, demonstrating sound judgment</li>
<li>Research, compile, and summarize a variety of complex and technical information and materials needed to develop clear and concise, newsletters, staff reports, targeted brochures, letters, and various other forms of written materials</li>
<li>Communicate effectively, orally and in writing, to a wide range of stakeholders</li>
<li>Compose and edit clear, complete, and accurate informational, technical, and educational materials for the public and other targeted stakeholders</li>
<li>Establish and maintain effective working relationships with all personnel contacted in the course of work performed</li>
<li>Develop, track, and prioritize schedule for development and implementation of multiple concurrent projects with exacting deadlines</li>
<li>Learn quickly</li>
<li>Track exacting and numerous details while keeping focused on the big picture</li>
<li>Flexible to handle shifting deadlines and priorities</li>
</ul>

</ul>
