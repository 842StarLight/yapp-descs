<h2>Engineering Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This position will be responsible for providing support to, and management over, engineering operations including design implementation, design calculations, production drawings, estimate specific opportunities, and corresponding procedure documentation for internal &amp; external clients.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide technical support for sales team, customers and production team as well as interpretation of our ASME Codes and standards.
</li>
<li>Conduct bore/flow calculations for all our flow measurement equipment including Venturi Tubes, Flow Nozzles, and Orifice Plates using FE - Sizer flow measuring software, or like.</li>
<li>
Complete RFQ breakdown and proposal submission of complex product line i.e. ASME Code Pressure Vessels, Pig Launchers, Venturi Tubes, etc..
</li>
<li>Ensure conformance to all policies and procedures in accordance with required codes and customer specifications.
</li>
<li>Conduct Lunch and Learn, power point presentations at various customer client locations.
</li>
<li>Conduct code calculations for all our ASME pressure vessels using COMPRESS CODEWARE vessel software, or like.</li>
<li>
Provide detailed fabrication drawings for our standard product which include pipeline strainers, plate products, flow measurement equipment, fabricated piping equipment, ASME Code pressure vessels and other fabricated piping products using AutoCAD software.
</li>
<li>Acting as project manager for all our internal engineering jobs which will include overseeing your complete job from engineering and fabrication until shipment.</li>
<li>
Provide milestone fabrication schedules to the client, expediting jobs if necessary, and maintaining a good customer/client relationship.
</li>
<li>Prepare and submit all Vendor Data Documentation such as fabrication drawings, weld procedures, weld maps, inspection test plans, NDE procedures and other procedures accordingly.
</li>
<li>Breakdown of bid packages to include preliminary design, raw material and labor costs.
</li>
<li>Interact with shop personnel daily and assist with fabrication obstacles and field installation problems and services.</li>
</ul>
