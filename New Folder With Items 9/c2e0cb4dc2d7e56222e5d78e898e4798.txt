<p>We’re looking for an Artificial Intelligence Business Development Manager.

<p></p>
<p>To be considered for this role, you must have Artificial Intelligence experience including data, compute and inference. Including Machine Learning and Deep Learning.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
An Artificial Intelligence Business Development Manager is responsible for driving business growth by developing new business or new ways to grow revenue of existing business. Leads the Deal Pursuit team in preparing and communicating the customer business case/proposal for emerging markets, existing markets, and specific deals. Guides Pursuit &amp; account teams through opportunity creation and pursuit strategic planning, qualifying mega deals, and developing and substantiating the services value proposition. Leads the development of innovative principles and ideas, and provides the business value selling expertise for specific solutions. Devises and executes a growth plan that meets business goals for a given geographic and/or service business area; including accountability for achieving revenue &amp; order targets. Builds high-quality funnels that generate customer demand in partnership with marketing &amp; sales.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsibilities -
</p>
<ul>
<li>Leverages market research to intelligently focus pursuit time, resources and effort on winnable opportunities.</li>
<li>
Rigorously qualifies large opportunities for active pursuit based on the probability of success and the intelligent allocation of the company's resources.</li>
<li>
Develops client relationships and interest over the span of lengthy large deal cycles and continually builds &amp; reinforces client's perception of the company as a credible partner through active persuasion and education.</li>
<li>
Consultatively &amp; proactively positions the company early in deals to influence client strategy and shape deal (RFP) requirements towards the company's strengths. Move towards Sole Source.</li>
<li>
Develops and champions the business justification for the company services to ensure a successful engagement based on critical case review.</li>
<li>
Actively educates &amp; engages with others (EAMs, CBMs, Account team, Engagement Managers, Solution Architects, etc.) on the company business solutions, deal requirements &amp; challenges to facilitate effective RFP/RFI responses, successful closes and client satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Crafts pursuit strategies that masterfully guide account team pursuit activities across often lengthy sales cycles.</li>
<li>
Leads and manages a virtual team across the geographic area to drive the business development activities and report on a regular basis the progress on growth.</li>
<li>
Acts as lead for coaching/mentoring.</li>
</ul>
