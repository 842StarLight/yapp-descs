<div><b>Job Summary:</b></div> <div>The Business Development/Planning Manager has several purposes and distinct functions as follows: (1) Manage development and/or review of financially-focused business plans, strategic plans, pro forma analyses (e.g., procedures, products, physicians, services, projects, Certificates of Needs, etc). (2) Oversee development, review and presentation of quarterly Action OI Benchmarking analyses to Hospital Officers as well as utilization of Action OI data to support evaluation of FTE requests throughout the year and as part of the annual staffing budget; position will manage the Action OI Benchmarking coordinator position. (3) Develop financial schedules in support of physician contracts (medical direction, call, physician services). (4) Oversight responsibilities for capital planning, monitoring, budgeting, and reporting processes.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></div>
<div>
<p>Bachelor's degree in Finance/Accounting with a minimum of four years of experience in any one of the following: accounting, financial modeling, hospital staffing and statistics, benchmarking and budgeting. Excellent analytical, organizational, interpersonal and oral/written communication skills required.</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></div>
<div>
<p>Strong MS Excel and benchmarking experience and Master's in Business Administration preferred.</p>
