<p><b>BASIC FUNCTION:</b></p>
<div>Engages with members and provides superior member service via video interaction. Develops and deepens member relationships by identifying their financial goals and offering relevant products and solutions to fit their needs. Embraces and adopts Credit Union technology to assist members with their requests quickly and efficiently.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates professional behaviors at all times and interacts with members via video to build positive relationships. Displays confidence, positivity, and friendliness while presenting in front of a camera in a virtual environment.</li>
<li>Performs all teller-related transactions while operating Credit Union’s core banking systems. Transactions may include but are not limited to the following: deposits, withdrawals, transfers, payments, maintenance requests, check cashing, issuing and accepting negotiable instruments, and end-of-day balancing.</li>
<li>Utilizes tools and resources provided by the Credit Union’s sales and service program to meet and exceed goals.</li>
<li>Advises members on the features and benefits of all Credit Union products and services.</li>
<li>Assists members with new account openings.</li>
<li>Processes Visa, consumer, and home equity loan applications.</li>
<li>Connects with members to identify and cross-sell additional Credit Union products and services.</li>
<li>Takes ownership to resolve member issues and complaints.</li>
<li>Performs related duties and special requests as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
OTHER SKILLS and ABILITIES:</b><br>
Ability to sell and provide exceptional member service via video interaction. Ability to operate a personal computer and utilize appropriate software programs including word processing, spreadsheet applications, and the Credit Union’s core banking systems. Ability to operate a fax, calculator, teller system and copier.<br>
<br>
<b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE:</b><br>
Associate’s degree and two to four years of related experience and/or training or an equivalent combination of education and experience. Sales or referral experience in a goal or incentive-based environment preferred.<br>
<br>
<b>CERTIFICATES, LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS:</b><br>
Notary Public and Signature Guarantee.<br>
NMLS Registration and annual renewal.<br>
Completes all training and testing required for certification.</div>
