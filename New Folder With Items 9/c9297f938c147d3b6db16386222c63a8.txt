<h2>Nuclear Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
Provides overall operational management including: scheduling, departmental coordination, and project monitoring for work in process at the Nuclear Service Center. Acts as the liaison between the customer, sales, purchasing, engineering, quality, and production departments. Reviews and ensures accuracy of detailed quotations to ensure quoted margin and delivery schedule is correct prior to signoff by the GM. Creates and maintains project schedules while ensuring the production cycle for parts and repair orders is on schedule. Reports slipping jobs to the GM so corrective action can be taken to remedy late jobs. Responsible for overall direction, coordination and evaluation of the Administrative segment of the Nuclear Service Center business unit.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides leadership, coordination, communication, and operational direction for facility keeping cost, throughput, and resource allocation to meet deliverables required by internal and external customers on track.</li>
<li>Analyzes schedule performance and works with office and shop personnel in determining schedules of work required for repair, parts, and new pump orders.</li>
<li>Responsible for the tendering processes purchasing, invoicing, accounts receivable, &amp; expediting at the service center.</li>
<li>Central point of contact for customer questions regarding schedules and other inquiries at the service center.</li>
<li>Assists management with assessing production loads and evaluations of shop and personnel demands for the proper resource allocation, processes, and additions for business growth.</li>
<li>Assists management with inputs for corporate financial reporting.</li>
<li>Regular and reliable attendance</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SECONDARY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures all department and disciplines are kept abreast of department progress.</li>
<li>Supports, cooperates with, and participates in company-wide EHS Management System which includes following all safety, health and environmental policies and procedures. Supports and cooperates in company-wide Quality Management effort.</li>
<li>Supports and cooperates in company-wide "QA" program, process standardization efforts, and "Lean Manufacturing" effort.</li>
<li>Recommends capital spending to increase operational efficiency</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides leadership and supervision to Shop &amp; Administrative coworkers in Service Center. This includes interviewing, hiring, training, appraising performance and employee relations.</li>
<li>As part of the service center management team, this position can serve as a backup to the Service Center Manager.</li>
</ul>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in business, engineering, or operations management or equivalent work related experience in field is required.</li>
<li>Ten (10) years pump or other capital equipment industry work experience required, including an excellent understanding of customer-focused, service-based, manufacturing business.</li>
<li>Five (5) years nuclear experience required including primary reactor seal knowledge and ASME code preferred.</li>
<li>Must be able to adapt and lead in a high paced, dynamic, customer oriented, and ever changing team environment</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, strong organizational ability and detail orientation required. Good interpersonal skills and ability to build and lead in a team environment.</li>
<li>Proven ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions.</li>
<li>Possess capabilities to understand, write, and apply procedural-based work processes.</li>
<li>Solid computer and data entry skills, with proficiency in latest MS software including MS Project and Access.</li>
<li>Knowledge of SAP is preferred.</li>
</ul>
