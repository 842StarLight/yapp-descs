<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>WHO WE ARE</b><br>
Based in Los Angeles, CA, eatOS is a venture-backed, cloud-based Restaurant Software platform including Point of Sale, Self-Service Kiosks, Mobile and Online Ordering, Scheduling and Inventory Management.</p>
<p>
We build world-class software that empowers restaurants to succeed, from food trucks to enterprise restaurants with 100+ locations.</p>
<p>
We’re on a mission to disrupt a market of old, slow, expensive POS products and bring innovative products to worldwide restaurants.</p>
<p><b>
WHAT DO WE SEEK</b><br>
We are looking for candidates who will bring a positive and professional attitude and a willingness to roll up their sleeves and pitch in, who will take initiative to be the best at their jobs and who have the ambition to become a leader on their team.</p>
<p>
As an Account Executive, you will be the primary Point of Contact for your customers, you will be in charge of meeting customers, closing deals, managing onboarding and owning the customer relationship for its duration, including up-selling and cross-selling.</p>
<p>
Key responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Identifying potential new clients and business opportunities</li>
<li>Maintaining relations with existing clients</li>
<li>Checking in regularly with existing accounts and seeing how we can further help them</li>
<li>Understanding the clients’ business needs</li>
<li>Training the clients about the installation, updates and effective use in order to set your accounts up for success</li>
</ul>
<p>We are looking for candidates who have:</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent organizational skills, ability to handle diverse situations, multiple projects, and rapidly changing priorities</li>
<li>Sales and negotiating skills, the ability to think creatively to see value from both sides and get the deal done</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills and the ability to communicate with clients.</li>
<li>Exercises independent judgment with minimal direction from supervisor i.e. Self-starter, Problem- solver</li>
</ul>
<p>A plus if you have:</p>
<ul>
<li>1+ years experience in a similar profile</li>
<li>Experience in the Point of Sale business</li>
<li>Experience working at a startup (we love entrepreneurs!)</li>
<li>Experience with SAAS-based product</li>
<li>Experience in hospitality, food service or restaurant industries</li>
<li>Experience in giving presentations</li>
</ul>
