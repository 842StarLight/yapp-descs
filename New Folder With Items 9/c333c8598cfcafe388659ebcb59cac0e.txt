<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>The Claims Examiner position helps investigate and document incidents involving company equipment, cargo, and/or personnel. The position also helps manage claims made under our auto liability, general liability, worker’s compensation, or cargo insurance coverage while also ensuring the Company’s motor fleet is in compliance with federal, state, and local laws, rules, regulations and Company policies.
