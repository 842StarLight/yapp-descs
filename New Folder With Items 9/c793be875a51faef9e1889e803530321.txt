<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Purpose of Position:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Key contributor to process capacity and reliability improvement efforts for electronics</li>
<li>
Provide critical process capability information in support of business growth</li>
<li>
Support the definition of process improvement plans and develop associated budgets</li>
<li>
Lead the activities of plant-based engineering and operations personnel towards efforts to improve the robustness of manufacturing processes</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Principle Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Collect and evaluate plant process capacity calculation data:<ul><li>
Execute capacity calculation models to include:<ul>
<li>
Equipment throughput
</li>
<li>Setup and breakdown of cylinders</li>
<li>
Purge requirements</li>
<li>
Process yield / rework</li>
<li>
Analytical time</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Conduct process reliability assessments and improvement plans.<ul>
<li>
Process FMEA documentation
</li>
<li>Component failure history</li>
<li>
Finished goods quality performance</li>
<li>
Analytical BCP</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities to improve equipment uptime and reduce maintenance cost</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Identify incremental operational and engineering changes required to maintain manufacturing buffer.</li>
<div>
</div>
<li>
Provide feedback to Business Development on investments required to meet customer LRP’s.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Key Interfaces:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Head of Operations</li>
<li>

<li>
Director of Business Development</li>
<li>
Director of Commercialization and Continuous Improvement</li>
<li>
Electronics Plant Managers</li>
<li>
Plant Operations Managers</li>
<li>
Process and Controls Engineers</li>
</ul>
