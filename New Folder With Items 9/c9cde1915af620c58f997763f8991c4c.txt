<div>
<p>Advancement Coordinator and Database Administrator</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Reports to: </b>Chief Development Officer</p>
<p><b>Position:</b> Full-time, 12-month position, non-exempt</p>
<p>The Oaks Christian Advancement department is seeking a dynamic, tech savvy, self-starter to join the team, responsible for coordination of daily activities and management of the donor and donation database. This person will be a team player with knowledge and experience working with database integrity and CRM software. Experience with Blackbaud Raisers Edge, preferred. Must be self-motivated and able to manage time and tasks diligently.</p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree desired as well as experience in data entry, bookkeeping and/or experience in the field of fundraising or sales.
</p>
<p>The Advancement department is a high functioning team committed to the mission of Oaks Christian School and responsible for the overall fundraising and community events management.
</p>
<p><b>Goal: </b>Manage and streamline fundraising operations of the Advancement team and manage administration of the Raiser’s Edge database serving as a point of contact for database issues and questions. Ensure annual quality control of the data and utilization of all software.</p>
<p><b>Roles &amp; Associated Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li>Database Management
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Perform and manage data entry process in Raiser’s edge to ensure that accurate, updated and relevant information on donors, parents, alumni, alumni parents, grandparents, foundations and other OCS constituents is maintained</li>
<li>Process and record all the school’s gifts, pledges and pledge payments into the RE database daily</li>
<li>Generate pledge reminders for all donors on all current campaigns. Handle wire and stock transfers and all follow up with donors regarding declined payments</li>
<li>Create and send all gift acknowledgements in a timely manner-in accordance with the Gift Acknowledgment policy and procedures</li>
<li>Produce annual tax receipts for all donations/donors</li>
<li>Determine appropriate campaigns, funds and appeals for each gift to be processed</li>
<li>Coordinate with IT and Registrar to integrate constituency data when changes to enrollment occur.</li>
<li>Coordinate with Blackbaud to integrate Researchpoint, Address Finder, PhoneFinder, and all other Analytics products
</li>
<li>Create all donation forms in Online Express</li>
<li>Design and implement database queries upon request for information providing timely and accurate records, reports and lists</li>
<li>Develop and produce donor recognition lists, data files, prospective donor lists and other information using RE reporting tools.</li>
<li>Prepare all gifting summaries and donor recognition lists for the school’s annual report.</li>
<li>Prepare mailing lists for all Advancement mailings
</li>
<li>Track and report employee participation in charitable giving program. Submit payroll deduction changes to Payroll dept. Provide acknowledgment letters and tax receipts.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with IRS policies
</li>
<li>Serve as primary liaison between Advancement and Business Office for reconciling with the accounting department</li>
</ul>
<p>2. Event Income Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Create all event registration forms in Online Express</li>
<li>Work closely with Event Coordinator to ensure all event registration payments have been made prior to the event
</li>
<li>Carefully track all event income ensuring that every donation has been properly allocated and recorded</li>
<li>Support Event Coordinator to ensure a seamless check-in process for all events
</li>
<li>Act as point-person for all event software</li>
<li>Provide post-event reports with event income summary</li>
</ul>
<p>3. Payment Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and utilized payment processing methods (cash, check) and electronic systems utilized for donation intake such as:
<ul>
<li>Online Express</li>
<li>Diamond Mind</li>
<li>OneCause
</li>
<li>Bidding for Good</li>
<li>Square Payments</li>
<li>Mobile Cause</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Ensure monthly donation deposits from all systems and delivery methods input into RE reconcile with the General Ledger.</li>
</ul>
<p>4. Contracts &amp; Finance Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage execution process for all Advancement contracts and administer payments</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Work with accounting department to determine and assign proper budget account codes</li>
<li>Process all department invoice and contract payments</li>
<li>Manage and monitor department expenses</li>
<li>Communicate financial processes to Department and other associated departments</li>
<li>Provide revenue and expense guidance for annual budget process</li>
<li>Support annual Accounting audit requirements</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional duties and responsibilities may be assigned, as needed</p>
