<b>Purpose / Objective</b><br>
<br>
The Kessler Collection’s mission, Inspiring Places, Intuitive Service, Exuberant Guests, is the foundation that establishes the company’s culture, drives business strategies and influences our Grand Performers to provide exceptional service to our guests. The overall objective and purpose of the Front Desk Supervisor is to support our mission by leading the team in executing front desk related responsibilities to the Kessler Collection standards. This includes relentlessly inspiring, coaching and training staff to exceed our guests’ expectations.<br>
<br>
<b>Supervisory Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
