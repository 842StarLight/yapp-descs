<h2>Water Fire Mold Restoration Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>General Manager must live Santa Rosa Beach Fl or Freeport Fl. Must live 20 Min from location</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>The General Manager will be focused on running the day-to-day operations of a Local Restoration Company. The General Manager will be responsible for handling all Losses and Estimates. General Manager will prioritize high levels of customer satisfaction and is expected to exceed the customers’ expectations by fulfilling all contractual obligations while effectively communicating the details of the project throughout the job’s duration.</p>
<p>The General Manager will take an active role in making sure that the projects are collected with the parameters established during the estimate and approval process. This position is also responsible for assisting in maximizing return on investment, profit and loss, daily operation, planning, development and implementation of compensation, and sales functions.</p>
<p>Qualified candidates will be responsible for managing field and office personnel, logistics, equipment and consumable usage, and vehicle assignments and maintenance. Additionally this position is responsible for reviewing and approving time entries that impact job costs and billing, as well as assigned personnel time off requests. As needed you may be called to oversee large losses that require additional management and supervision.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<p>-Manage jobs Repairs and create and ensure estimates are being turned in on time and are profitable while helping maintain communication with customers and subcontractors. General Manager will review materials and subcontractor budgets to ensure correct pricing.</p>
<p>-Oversees all Mitigation Operations and ensures all relevant paperwork is being turned in and is accurate. Also reviews Mitigation Estimates for efficiency and profitability. Ensures Mitigation Crew and Management is coordinating with the customer and the insurance adjuster if applicable.</p>
<p>-Manages all Losses and Fires Jobs by meeting with the customer, coordination with all insurance adjusters, preparing a detailed estimate in a timely manner, and ensuring profitability from Mitigation to Contents, to Repairs and rebuilding. Ensures constant contact with all parties involved including subcontractors and maintains all necessary permits required.</p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate professionally with customers, subcontractors, employees and insurance companies</li>
<li>Coordinate and manage multiple residential, commercial, industrial and institutional projects of various complexities simultaneously</li>
</ul>
<ul>

<li>Provide technical explanations to clients, adjusters, consultants and others</li>
<li>Meet with estimators and superintendents regularly to ensure accuracy of all work being performed and regular contact with customers is maintained</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Visit job sites as necessary to ensure quality</li>
<li>Schedule daily work orders submitted from the estimators and project managers for restoration, contents and light rebuilds per staff capability.</li>
<li>Provide constructive feedback and direction to complete tasks on time and within budget</li>
<li>Ensure all work crews are appropriately uniformed</li>
<li>Meet with municipal inspectors and secure needed permits as required</li>
<li>Implement site-specific safety plans</li>
<li>Oversee all moisture mapping for water losses.</li>
<li>Coordinate after-hours emergency services as directed, including board-ups, water damage, fire and smoke damage, and vandalism</li>
<li>Respond to emergencies including after hour responses as necessary</li>
<li>Develop and manage a sustainable contents operational process</li>
<li>Ensure warehouse is sufficiently stocked with necessary supplies and equipment</li>

<li>Conduct regular two-way assessments of staff performance</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
