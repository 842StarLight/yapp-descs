What you'll do
<br><br>
<b>The Technical Sales Manager will be responsible for:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Having deep technical understanding of Catalant's product capabilities and serving as the technical point of contact and product subject matter expert during the sales process</li>
<li>Understanding client's business objectives, technical and product requirements and translating the client's needs into a compelling experience</li>
<li>Architecting and delivering compelling product experiences to a wide variety of audiences, ranging from C-Suite executives to technical stakeholders</li>
<li>Collaborating with internal stakeholders, including Business Transformation, Sales, Marketing, Design, Product and Engineering.</li>
<li>Familiarity with Python or other modern Object-Oriented development language is a plus.</li>
<li>As Catalant's regional footprint expands, the Technical Sales Manager will be responsible for building a team of Technical Sales Experts.</li>
</ul>
What you'll bring
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>3+ years' experience in a customer-facing technical sales or product management role with SaaS solutions</li>
<li>Strong presentation, demonstration and communications skills and ability to influence executive audiences</li>
<li>Team player mindset with entrepreneurial attitude</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to travel (up to 50% of time) to achieve commercial objectives</li>
