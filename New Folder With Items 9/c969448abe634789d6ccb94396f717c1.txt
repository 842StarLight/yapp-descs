<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>MAXIMUS is a leading operator of government health and human services programs in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia and Saudi Arabia. The Company delivers business process services to improve the cost effectiveness, efficiency and quality of government-sponsored benefit programs, such as the Affordable Care Act, Medicaid, Medicare, Children’s Health Insurance Program (CHIP), Health Insurance BC (British Columbia), as well as welfare-to-work and child support programs around the globe. The Company’s primary customer base includes federal, provincial, state, county and municipal governments. Operating under its founding mission of Helping Government Serve the People®, MAXIMUS has more than 16,000 employees worldwide.<br>
<br>
Job Summary: .<br>
The IT Project Manager will provide project management oversight of technology projects. This may be a self-contained project, or a large team within a more complex program. This position will define, implement, and manage scope, schedule, and resources so that all project milestones are delivered according to plan. The manager will utilize best practices in project management to achieve project goals, manage risks, and to successfully complete all planned activities.
<br>
<br>
Job Duties/Responsibilities:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Performs all project management functions; including work breakdown and cost estimation, scheduling, monitoring and tracking of technical progress against the defined timetables and budgets, and staff assignments and development</li>
<li>Manages project expectations, evaluates project needs, and communicates with stakeholders to ensure that program needs are met with respect to urgency and priority</li>
<li>Develops trust, credibility, and strong client relationships; seeks opportunities to add value to internal and external client initiatives</li>
<li>Supervises and manages staff assigned to the project and assigns/directs their activities</li>
<li>Reviews all project deliverables to ensure quality and accuracy to meet project, stakeholder, and contractual standards</li>
<li>Serves as the point of contact and client interface for project technology related issues</li>
<li>Manages activities of subcontractors utilized to deliver the technical solution</li>
<li>Defines and implements development plans for assigned projects in order to ensure staff is knowledgeable on the applications and technologies in use on the project and that project team understand and follows processes and procedures</li>
<li>Provides detailed direction during various stages of the lifecycle to include requirements analysis, testing and potentially programming support</li>
<li>Identifying, tracking, managing, and communicating risks and issues with stakeholders and upper management</li>
<li>Ensures that all appropriate costs are included in quarterly forecasts</li>
<li>Ensures that MAXIMUS is in compliance with all applicable requirements of the contact, state and federal regulations, and the terms and conditions of the RFP and proposal</li>
<li>Coordinates, prepares and manages technical presentations and functional demonstrations to internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>Ensures the project is in compliance with MAXIMUS standards and procedures</li>
<li>Responsible for adhering to established safety standards</li>
<li>Must be able to remain in a stationary position for an extended period of time</li>
<li>Occasionally lifts, carries, or otherwise moves items weighing up to 25 pounds</li>
<li>Work is constantly performed in an office environment</li>
<li>Performs other duties as necessary and assigned</li>
</ul>

<li>2-4 years of related experience in a PM/leadership role</li>
