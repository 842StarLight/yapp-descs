Job Description:</div>
<div>
Working directly with the Operations Manager, the Billing Coordinator's key role is to ensure Kukui receives payments for goods and services and records these transactions accordingly. The Billing Coordinator will be securing revenue by verifying and posting receipts and resolving any discrepancies.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Job responsibilities include but are not limited to:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Obtains revenue by verifying transaction information, computing charges and refunds.
</li>
<li>Preparing invoices.</li>
<li>
Identifying delinquent accounts and insufficient payments.</li>
<li>
Collects revenue by reminding delinquent accounts and notifying clients of insufficient payments.</li>
<li>
Maintains a low balance of account receivables.</li>
<li>
Obtaining and updating client payment info across all billing platforms.</li>
<li>
Investigating and resolving any invoice and payment irregularities.</li>
<li>
Protects organization's value by keeping information confidential.</li>
<li>
Process all add on and one-off billing</li>
<li>
Other various accounting or operational tasks.</li>
<li>
Answer the main and billing line and route the calls accordingly.</li>
</ul>
