<h2>Account Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SPECIFICATION: ACCOUNT DIRECTOR
</b></p>
<p><b>JOB LOCATION: </b><b>CITY, STATE
</b></p>
<p><b>Corporate Overview:
</b></p>
<p>GTT provides multinationals with a better way to reach the cloud through its suite of cloud networking services, including wide area networking, Internet, managed services and voice services. The company’s Tier 1 IP network, ranked in the top five worldwide, connects clients to any location in the world and any application in the cloud. GTT delivers an outstanding client experience by living its core values of simplicity, speed and agility. For more information on how GTT is redefining global communications, please visit www.gtt.net .
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<ul><li>An Account Director for GTT develops and manages relationships with prospective and existing Enterprise customers to gain strategic positioning with decision makers to secure new business and to retain and grow existing revenue.An Account Director is responsible for the implementation of strategic and tactical sales account plans.An Account Director meets monthly, quarterly, and annual sales goals set by the leadership team through aggressive prospecting and selling.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for identifying and penetrating prospective Enterprise customers headquartered in the assigned target market.
</li>
<li>Generate sales revenue by promoting GTT products and services to targeted prospective and existing accounts.
</li>
<li>Lead all stages of the sales cycle as needed to support the conversion of opportunities to sales.
</li>
<li>Maintain comprehensive knowledge of industry and customers to identify and propose unique solutions.
</li>
<li>Leverage knowledge to execute sales strategy that meets or exceeds revenue objectives.
</li>
<li>Create strong relationships based on trust, integrity, and customer satisfaction to effectively drive sales and repeat business.
</li>
<li>Track, analyze and report sales activity using GTT’s internal sales reporting tools.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will have the following:
</p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in direct sales in the telecommunications/technology sector
</li>
<li>Demonstrated success in consistently meeting or exceeding a monthly sales quota
</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of the enterprise customer base in the assigned geography
</li>
<li>A proven ability to generate leads, penetrate new accounts, and develop and manage a pipeline
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, verbal and written, facilitation and presentation techniques
</li>
<li>Strong leadership/team skills and a positive track record in executing sales process and coordinating among internal and external stakeholders
</li>
<li>Ambition and a willingness to learn and develop professionally
</li>
</ul>
