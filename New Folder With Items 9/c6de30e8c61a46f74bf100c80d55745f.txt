<h2>Lead Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We do the right things, right now. We do them in a way that is relevant to our clients. Become a part of our history as it continues to be written!
<br>
<br>
If you are interested and qualified for this role, we invite you to apply.
</p>
<p></p>The Lead Development Specialist is responsible for assisting in appointment generation through outbound calling efforts. This person will work as part of an outbound sales team that utilizes Salesforce to develop, manage and actively work leads. The Lead Development Specialist will report to the Sales Development Manager.
<p></p>
<p>Essential Functions/Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Qualify assigned leads by verifying existing information as well as gathering additional pertinent data through research efforts
</li>
<li>Develop relationships with prospects by making 75+ calls daily to make appointments for assigned market/line of business
</li>
<li>Document and log all activities in Salesforce so the all appropriate parties have real time access to the status
</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Sales Development Manager to develop and execute on outbound calling strategies
</li>
<li>Conduct daily database lead management by ensuring lead data and status is up to date
</li>
<li>Meet monthly calling and appointment quotas
</li>
<li>Contribute to team by offering innovative ideas to grow sales approach
</li>
<li>Leverage industry and geographical analysis in sales efforts
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Minimum Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities Needed to Perform Essential Functions of the Job:
</p>

<li>CRM experience
</li>

</li>
<li>Ability to execute repetitious tasks with a sense of urgency
</li>
<li>Requires at least 1-2 year(s) of relevant outbound sales experience
</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of financial reporting and organizational structures
</li>
<li>Basic sales skills and effective listening to understand prospect needs
</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills
</li>
<li>Strategic thinker that’s diligent, flexible, resourceful with strong self-starter mentality who possesses a strong work ethic
</li>
<li>High school diploma or General Education Degree (GED) required
</li>
<li>Associate’s or Bachelor’s degree and/or equivalent banking work experience preferred

</li>
