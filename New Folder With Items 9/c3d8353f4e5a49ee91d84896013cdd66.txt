Key Responsibilities
<br>
This role is a New Business focused logo hunter. You will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Achieve targets by selling Boomi's market leading iPaaS solution</li>
<li>
Maintain good business relationships with prospects to conduct needs analysis, relay product information and provide technical specifications and quotations</li>
<li>
Effectively develop a territory plan leveraging Partners, Marketing, BDRs, and your own effort to drive new business interest in your territory with an 'own your dirt' mentality</li>
<li>
Work closely with outside sales representatives to work through sales strategy and running sales cycles</li>
</ul>
