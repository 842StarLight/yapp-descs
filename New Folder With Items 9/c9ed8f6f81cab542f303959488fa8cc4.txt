<h2>Managed Content Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Offering an opportunity to be part of a high-performance team, enjoy exceptional benefits, and a supportive work-life balance, the <b>Gordon Flesch Company</b> is an employer of choice throughout WI, IL, IN and OH. The <b>Gordon Flesch Company</b> strives to recognize and appreciate our associate’s commitment, creativity, and the results they deliver every day for our customers and the community.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>In this role you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct presentations and demonstrations on the value of managed content software solutions
</li>
<li>Support Managed Content Consultants to achieve team and organizational revenue goals
</li>
<li>Lead meetings and guide clients through solution design
</li>
<li>Develop a complete workflow analysis to identify bottlenecks, pain points, etc. with assistance from client
</li>
<li>Create clearly scoped statement of work documentation with the assistance of sales consultant including software, services, and support; providing support as needed during presentation to the client
</li>
<li>Train power users while setting clear expectations for user acceptance testing, and train end users when required
</li>
<li>Provide continuous education to clients on managed content capabilities and solutions through account reviews
</li>
<li>Track project/billable time and provide weekly updates to management related to project status
 </li>
</ul>
<p><b>You should have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license</li>
<li>Prior experience utilizing technology to solve process problems
</li>
<li>Software sales or implementation experience
</li>
<li>Ability to obtain and maintain managed content certifications</li>
</ul>
