<h2>Seasonal Operations Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Suddath Companies is a multifaceted group of companies that specialize in worldwide corporate employee relocations, household moving, warehouse and logistics management and specialized transportation services.
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b><i>Why work at Suddath?
</i></b></h2>
<p>You'll be an important part of the seasonal team in our Jacksonville, Florida location on Western Way and enjoy a summer job with lots of variety. Use and develop your customer service and technical skills while you work with a supportive group of people.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><i>What you can expect:
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>meet people from all walks of life and career paths
</li>
<li>gain knowledge of our industry
</li>
<li>develop leadership skills
</li>
<li>learn software applications used in transportation management
</li>
<li>receive training on job skills and software applications
</li>
<li>gain exposure to a variety of career paths in the transportation industry
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Here's more about the job:
</i></b></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide administrative support to Dispatchers in the branch
</li>
<li>Copy, fax, scan paperwork to all parties necessary, including coordinators, sales staff, risk management, office staff and accounting department
</li>
<li>Assist in monitoring the progress of the daily moves to ensure timeliness and prevent service failures
</li>
<li>Assist Dispatcher in providing accurate weights; maintain log of all certified scale tickets and money collected
</li>
<li>Assist in scheduling activities as directed by the local dispatcher
</li>
<li>Answer phone calls effectively handling the needs of all callers
</li>
<li>Other duties as duties assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Able to work full-time hours during our peak season, May-September
</li>
<li>Detail-oriented with the ability to work in a fast paced environment
</li>
<li>Deadline-oriented, organized, dependable, able to multi-task, with excellent time management skills
</li>
<li>Able to pass pre-employment background check
</li>
</ul>
