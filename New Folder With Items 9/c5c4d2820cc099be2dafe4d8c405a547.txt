 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>

<b>Position Title: </b>Sr. Distribution Manager - Baby</div>
<div>
<b>Reports to: </b>Director, Supply Chain</div>
<div>
<b>Location: </b>Victorville, CA</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Job Summary:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<div>
The Sr. Manager is responsible for directing all site activities to meet operational objectives in Safety/Quality/Delivery/Service/Cost/Employee Engagement. This position will have oversight of a 1.43 million square foot location with two separate buildings and is responsible for shipping $725 million invoiced sales for Newell Brands - Baby ($620M), Consumer &amp; Commercial Solutions ($85M), and Food Divisions ($20M).</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This position will support a variety of initiatives including driving consistency in policies and best practices to support and encourage a productive workforce. This position is an integral role in helping the Company achieve its vision to transform Newell Brands into the leading next generation consumer products company.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Plan and direct the Baby, Consumer &amp; Commercial Solutions, and Food Divisions warehousing and shipping functions and all divisional traffic/transportation functions.</li>
<li>Manage the customer interface for key distribution/transportation metrics.</li>
<li>Manage a transportation plan that minimizes in-transit and distribution costs and support superior customer service objectives.</li>
<li>Manage inbound order flow to maximize shipping capacity through Commercial Products distribution network</li>
<li>Maintain strategic alliances with transportation providers whose business objectives match Commercial Products.</li>
<li>Manage processes to maximize quantity of shipments, internal labor productivity, and cubes per shipment for the carrier.</li>
<li>Manage a warehousing and shipping strategy; including third-party warehouse space.</li>
<li>Lead the Amazon Flex Beta Site for Newell Brands.</li>
<li>Deliver measurable improvements in safety, reliability, quality, and productivity by identifying, leading, and owning results for improvement projects.</li>
<li>Work closely with Human Resources to lead the team with integrity and to help establish and maintain a trusting, inclusive, and productive environment.</li>
<li>Coach and develop the management team members through annual objectives, work planning and reviews, and regular working and communication sessions.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><div>
Supervision Given: Provides leadership to Management members at the Graco Victorville CA facility.</div></div>
