<h2>Salesperson</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Sells new or used vehicles at a profit to the dealership, while ensuring customer satisfaction.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>This job has no direct supervisory responsibilities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b><b>include the following:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meets dealership sales goals.
</li>
<li>Greets customer and determines make, type, and quality of vehicle desired.
</li>
<li>Explains features and demonstrates operation of vehicle in showroom or on demonstration drive.
</li>
<li>Suggests optional equipment for customer to purchase.
</li>
<li>Computes and quotes sales price, including tax, trade-in allowance, and discount.
</li>
<li>Completes paperwork accompanying vehicle sales, including but not limited to, preparing sales slip or sales contract.
</li>
<li>Arranges for delivery and registration of vehicle.
</li>
<li>Researches availability of models and optional equipment.
</li>
<li>Engages in business development.
</li>
<li>Addresses customer concerns.
</li>
<li>Maintains CSI at or above Company standards
</li>
<li>Maintains an organized, clean and safe work area
</li>
<li>Participates in required training
</li>
<li>Records all hours worked accurately in company timekeeping system
</li>
<li>Follows Safeguards rules and regulations.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates the Company’s Core Values
</li>
<li>Complies with Company policies and procedures
</li>
<li>Observes all Federal, State, Local and Company safety rules and regulations in the performance of duties.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
