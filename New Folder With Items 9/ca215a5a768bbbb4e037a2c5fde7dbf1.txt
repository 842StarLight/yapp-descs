<h2>Optical Systems Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Searching for a role where you drive some of the future’s most promising innovations? FARO is an international company where ideas count more than hierarchy. We develop and market computer-aided 3D measurement and imaging devices and software. At FARO, smart, skilled people work together in an open atmosphere…. And challenging work never gets boring.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Become a part of this great team and start moving forward.

<p></p>
<p>We are seeking an experienced <b>Optical Systems Engineer </b>to work out of our Exton, PA office. Relocation is not available for this position. As a member of the LIDAR product development team, our Optical Systems Engineer will work on the on the product development of our laser-based 3D scanning devices.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>We have:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>An open mind for new ideas and methodologies
</li>
<li>Leading edge technology
</li>
<li>A dynamic team of professionals to work with
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You have:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>PhD in Optical Engineering
</li>
<li>5+ years of work experience in 3D laser scanning or closely related fields
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Familiar with the Following </b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in optical design, modeling and system performance evaluation
</li>
<li>Familiar with optical component fabrication and testing
</li>
<li>Hands on experience with opto-mechanical system alignment and testing
</li>
<li>Proficiency in Zemax is desirable
</li>
<li>Experience with opto-mechanical design to aid in optical system development
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>How you will make a difference @ FARO:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design and assist with the design of LiDAR scanning metrology systems
</li>
<li>Develop and implement techniques, algorithms, and approaches for high accuracy calibration of 3D scanning metrology systems
</li>
<li>Generate math models and/or otherwise perform calculations necessary to accurately predict performance, resolution, and measurement accuracy of 3D based systems
</li>
<li>Define artifacts and calibration standards for production and end user calibration
</li>
<li>Generate, edit, and/or direct generation of firmware and software code for control of products and/or test fixtures designed for use with 3D measurement products.
</li>
<li>Research vendors, technology, and competitive products in support of design projects involving structured light scanning and similar technology.
</li>
<li>Design and/or select LASERS, optics, filters, attenuators, controls, cameras, lenses and related technology in support of 3D scanning and measurement products
</li>
<li>Provide expert technical guidance in the use and optimization of scanning technology for 3D metrology
</li>
<li>Assemble and test breadboards, engineering models and fixtures as part of product development and/or production support
</li>
<li>Design, develop, and verify fixtures and processes for assembly, alignment, focus, measurement, and calibration of 3D measurement systems
</li>
<li>Generate drawings, specification, schematics, flow charts, bills of material, test procedures, assembly instructions, and other documentation necessary to specify product configuration to manufacturing, purchasing, vendors, and regulatory agencies.
</li>
<li>Assist and support manufacturing, customer service, and customers with the use of product and fixtures as required.
</li>
<li>Provide guidance for and assist with test, documentation, and communication of critical information necessary to obtain and maintain regulatory approval for laser-based products.
</li>
</ul>
