Job Description
<br><br>
Customer Success Manager is passionate about transforming health programs through the use of Fitbit solutions. You have the best customer management and advocacy skills around. You are organized and skilled at developing and implementing project plans. You are proactive by nature and thrive on providing guidance &amp; developing a plan alongside customers to ensure a successful customer-centric launch experience. You have impeccable relational skills and can create win/win environments for all parties that you work with. You are comfortable in a fast-changing environment and love the idea of helping to define best practices for a fast-growing team.
<br><br>
Customer Success Manager on the Fitbit Health Solutions team, you are a critical part of the client transition from Sales to Account Executives and own the critical initial interactions with the client post-sale, focused on a stellar customer experience and smooth product onboarding designed at reaching client performance indicators. You continue to support FHS' customer and internal teams post-implementation as needed to ensure successful adoption of products, including devices, software and services.
<br><br>
<b>Role responsibilities include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manages and owns implementation of Fitbit software program and services with external clients</li>
<li>Oversees and supports multiple implementation projects in parallel</li>
<li>Continues to support Account Executives post-implementation as a product expert, consulting as needed regarding best practices, product adoption and utilization</li>
<li>Collaborates across interdisciplinary teams (e.g. marketing, sales, customer support, etc.) as needed to improve effectiveness and efficiency of the team, in addition to improving customer service</li>
<li>Takes initiative to look for gaps and opportunities for improving product and service delivery to our clients</li>
<li>Evaluates client needs, develops configurations that support business processes. Resolve identified issues requiring changes to implemented configurations</li>
<li>May be involved in pre-sales product demonstrations or provide assistance in scoping projects or developing proposals.</li>
<li>Defines and executes on delivery and implementation plans, ensures that implementations are completed on time</li>
<li>Tests and trouble shoots final product set-ups</li>
<li>Provide training to customers and end users during and after implementation</li>
<li>Oversight responsibility to service clients with day-to-day administrative questions and technical needs during implementation</li>
<li>Partner with the Account Executive to build a delightful onboarding/implementation experience and successful transition to the accounts team</li>
<li>Attend and contribute to Quarterly Business Reviews with customers as needed to support Account Executives as product expert in an effort to drive account growth</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of two to four years experience in implementation/success and/or account management or equivalent history of increasing customer satisfaction, adoption, and retention with health plans</li>
<li>Knowledge of the health plan landscape, including familiarity with CMS operational requirements</li>
<li>Excellent project management skills and proven record in developing and executing an implementation project plan. PMP Certification a plus.</li>
<li>Skilled at utilizing Microsoft Excel and other reporting tools to analyze trends and translate these both for customers and internal stakeholders, particularly in regards to adoption of our products and services</li>
<li>Identify, articulate and represent client needs to product &amp; customer support</li>
<li>Strong technical skills and ability to translate client needs to product and engineering, including high level mapping of integrations</li>
<li>Proven ability to drive continuous value of our product(s)</li>
<li>Familiarity working with clients of all sizes</li>
<li>Impeccable written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Detail oriented and analytical</li>
<li>Strong team player but still a self-starter</li>
<li>Thrives in a multi-tasking environment and can adjust priorities on-the-fly</li>
<li>Travel as needed to support internal and/or external operations (estimated at 10% travel)</li>
</ul>
