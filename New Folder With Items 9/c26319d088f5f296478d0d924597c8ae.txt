 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Mid-Market Sales Manager, Multifamily Marketing<p>

<p>
Job Description</p>
<p>
<br></p>
<p>
CoStar Group’s database is the largest centralized, real-time database of commercial real estate information available. Fortune Magazine recently named CoStar Group 100 of the fastest growing companies in the world. Apartments.com, a division of CoStar, is the nation’s #1 apartment listing website supported by the industry’s largest professional research team.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As a CoStar <b>Mid-Market Sales Director/Manager for Multifamily Marketing, </b>you will be responsible for delivering frontline sales results through customer interaction and employee satisfaction. The Mid-Market Sales Manager is responsible for growing and developing their assigned region’s revenue by selling new advertising and info subscriptions on Apartments.com to new customers, growing existing customer revenue and ensuring high levels of customer service and high renewal rates.</p>
<p>
At CoStar, we create opportunity through a combination of reliable tools, resources, and deep understanding in today’s market. Our sales teams’ help our clients find relevant information, meet the right people, and make the deals that are critical to their success.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Perform complex tasks involving the coaching, counseling, and leading of the mid-market sales team</li>
<li>
Ensure adherence to established procedures and guidelines</li>
<li>
Work with peers on the mid-market sales team, coordinate with field sales team representatives and managers, and team with training and other sales support functions</li>
<li>
Ensure that all team members and the overall team achieves all assigned revenue goals</li>
<li>
Provide feedback to management and recommend changes and modifications to established procedures &amp; guidelines</li>
<li>
Serve as liaison between training, sales management, field sales and mid-market sales teams and other sales support functions</li>
<li>
Ensure frontline sales results</li>
<li>
Ensure a high level of customer satisfaction and employee satisfaction ratings</li>
<li>
Develop and communicate annual sales plans in support of the organizational strategy and objectives</li>
</ul>
