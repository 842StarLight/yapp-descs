<h2>Social Media Trader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>If you're the kind of person who obsesses over details, thrives in a collaborative environment, and jumps at the opportunity to take on new challenges, then this is the place for you!</p>
<p>
Ahalogy, a Quotient Brand, is seeking a creative, enthusiastic person with amazing communication and strategy skills to help develop and run our paid media executions on a variety of social platforms. Based in our newly constructed office in OTR (Cincinnati), you will work with our Project Managers to help develop and run robust, data-driven, successful digital media campaigns.<br>
<br>
<b>What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<p>
Your job will be a unique blend of digital strategy, media buying, and data analysis. You will lead and execute all of the paid media components of each of our programs. Most of your time will be spent uploading &amp; running these paid campaigns, making recommendations on when to flight the content, deciding which ad units to develop and test, following campaign performance closely, making adjustments and optimizations on a daily and weekly basis, and helping the Project Managers pull together final reports outlining the programs insights and learnings.<br>
<br>
<b>What You’ll Bring:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The ideal candidate will have a bachelor's degree with 1-3 years of digital (social) media experience.</li>
<li>
Previous social advertising planning and execution experience</li>
<li>
Experience with web Analytics platforms (Google is the big one)</li>
<li>
Proficient in excel (i.e. Vlookup, Macros, Sumifs etc…), Keynote, Pages, etc.</li>
<li>
Bid Management Tools (Sprinklr, Kenshoo, Marin, MediaMath, 4C Insights, etc.), a plus</li>
<li>
E-commerce, a plus</li>
<li>
Understanding and drawing insights from data</li>
<li>
Explaining your plans and summarizing complex information in a succinct way</li>
<li>
Attention to detail in managing paid marketing initiatives and data reporting</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical, critical-thinking, and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Keeping up to date on industry changes and the latest developments in digital advertising</li>
<li>
Work closely with our operations team around the globe to ensure campaigns launch on time and exceed client KPIs</li>
<li>
Communicate any risks to delivery &amp; performance and recommend solutions</li>
<li>
Help communicate expectations to clients</li>
<li>
Research/resolve issues in a timely manner</li>
<li>
A keen eye for detail is an absolute must</li>
</ul>
