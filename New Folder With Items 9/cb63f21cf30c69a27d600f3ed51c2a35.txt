<h2>Manager Supply Chain-Distribution & Logistics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Company Overview</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Alkermes plc is a fully integrated, global biopharmaceutical company developing innovative medicines for the treatment of central nervous system (CNS) diseases. The company has a diversified commercial product portfolio and a substantial clinical pipeline of product candidates for chronic diseases that include schizophrenia, depression, addiction, cancer and multiple sclerosis. Headquartered in Dublin, Ireland, Alkermes plc has an R&amp;D center in Waltham, Massachusetts; a research and manufacturing facility in Athlone, Ireland; and a manufacturing facility in Wilmington, Ohio. For more information, please visit Alkermes’ website at www.alkermes.com.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The primary areas of focus for the Manager Supply Chain – Distribution &amp; Logistics are transportation management, import/export controls, and trade compliance.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate for this role will be responsible for managing the global transportation network across all modes of transportation from manufacturing plants to 3rd party logistics providers (3PLs) and 3PLs to customers ensuring required service levels are met. The candidate will also ensure trade compliance conformance and import/export controls are followed. It is expected that strong collaborative working relationships are developed with various functional groups including Supply Chain, Marketing, Trade Operations, Quality Assurance and others to effectively provide the required services with high customer satisfaction. The candidate must be able to communicate effectively by tailoring messages appropriately with peers and management and to articulate point of view, decisions and data with tact and diplomacy. The candidate will understand cGMP and GDP regulations, and remain current with best practices. Lastly, the candidate should be able to lead complex investigations utilizing root cause methodologies and tools, and to overcome obstacles even in challenging circumstances.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Areas of Responsibility:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage global transportation network across the product life cycle through efficient planning and procurement of transportation services for products and materials</li>
<li>Develop shipping lanes and coordinate with Import/Export Brokers to expedite and assure timely delivery of products and goods throughout the network</li>
<li>Schedule transportation requirements, monitor movement, and intervene on exceptions</li>
<li>Manage imports and exports in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations to ensure product moves quickly through customs to consignee</li>
<li>Ensure proper trade compliance activities are conducted by Alkermes and business partners</li>
<li>Resolve problems concerning transportation, imports/exports, and trade compliance</li>
<li>Participate in carrier management processes, including selection, qualification, performance evaluation and account management</li>
<li>Establish and monitor supply chain-based performance measurement systems, delivering KPI’s around carrier, transportation costs, efficiency and service that support continuous improvement and attainment of all transportation performance objectives</li>
<li>Ensure changes in supply configuration, i.e., changes in logistics, suppliers, pack-outs, routes, costs, etc. are implemented effectively</li>
<li>Work with area management on establishing transportation and other departmental procedures</li>
<li>Assist with the evaluation, selection, and implementation of a Transportation Management System</li>
<li>Cross train with peers, providing support and acting as a backup as needed including management of 3PL’s and controlled substances</li>
<li>Manage SAP transactions or distribution of commercial product for US market</li>
<li>Monitor and support Advanced Shipping Notice process and all serialization activities</li>
<li>Understand and contribute to Supply Chain goals and objectives</li>
<li>Evaluate industry trends and solutions, work with the appropriate internal functional groups to introduce new solutions to the supply chain</li>
<li>Provide input into optimal packaging design and pallet layouts</li>
<li>Coordinate with Commercial Operations to monitor and manage sales trends, missed or incorrect shipments, large off-cycle shipments, and any other specific distribution requests</li>
<li>Continuously enhance knowledge and experience in distribution, logistics, transportation and import/export, including regulatory requirements</li>
<li>Manage and/or participate in key projects within the department</li>
<li>Provide feedback to Supply Chain Management related to the regular oversight of commercial product distribution</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of cGMP, GDP, and/or GCP
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of the domestic and international rules, regulations, benefits, and costs associated with all modes of transport for pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and biologics, including cold chain transportation
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of government agencies and regulations involved with the import/export and transportation of products and materials</li>
<li>Working knowledge of application and use of Incoterms</li>
<li>Knowledge of supply chain principles</li>
<li>Knowledge of Controlled Substances and DEA regulations</li>
<li>Strong skills in strategic planning, customer service, and math.</li>
<li>Take accountability for all project aspects from inception through successful delivery and owns outcomes</li>
<li>Able to remain current with new technologies and best practices, offer innovative ideas, make recommendations, and implement as appropriate</li>
<li>Understand business needs, identify priorities, and implement changes in a positive manner</li>
<li>Prioritize appropriately and identify clear objectives of projects and teams</li>
<li>Demonstrate a high level of professionalism and resilience during challenging situations</li>
<li>Communicate decisions to others based on the company objectives, and follow up to ensure compliance with decisions</li>
<li>Ensure that the team is aware of priorities and changes at all times</li>
<li>Builds collaborative relationships pro-actively across the business</li>
<li>Experience with identifying and leading continuous improvement initiatives, for example LEAN, Six Sigma</li>
<li>Continuously learn by participating in educational opportunities; reading professional publications; maintaining personal networks; participating in professional organizations</li>
<br>
</ul>
