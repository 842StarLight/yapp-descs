<h2>Market General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>BacklotCars is seeking a <b>Market General Manager </b>to join our growing sales team. We are an online marketplace for auto dealers and our platform is changing the way dealers buy/sell wholesale vehicles. We are looking for individuals who share our growth mindset, and who are ready to execute on ideas that will change an industry. This person will be responsible for acquiring new selling dealerships, account management and overall market growth. This person would manage all sell side activities within their territory and would also be responsible for driving new market launches.</p>
<p>Join us as we keep this expansion rolling. If you are excited to manage a disruptive marketplace and next-generation technology platform apply to the position below.</p>
<p><b>What you'll do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Focus on new business development by performing market &amp; customer prospecting while developing strategic, targeted sales campaigns that build strong customer relationships</li>
<li>Nurture customer relationships by fact-finding, follow up and identifying customers’ needs</li>
<li>Understand dealers current state process for diversification of wholesale inventory</li>
<li>Ability to present and perform demonstration of our solution/platform,</li>
<li>Set best practices with customers to ensure dealer's success in the usage of our solution</li>
<li>Track daily, weekly, monthly call activity and performance measurements against goals and sales targets using CRM software.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing a team of local inspectors, setting up and managing their weekly schedules</li>
<li>Maintain internal communication with other Market Development Managers distributed between various markets including around the country.</li>
<li>Understand your local market landscape and market competition while keeping a keen awareness of any changes happening holistically within the automotive industry.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You'll Need: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 5+ years’ experience in outside sales development or business development, big bonus points for having been exposed to technology vendors, start-ups, or SaaS-based environments.</li>
<li>Experience with the automotive sales industry or auto sales would be considered a definite asset</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills, time management skills, results driven</li>
<li>Demonstrable knowledge of CRM tools/software and best practices.</li>
<li>Ability to blend sales acumen, outstanding interpersonal skills, and enthusiasm to stay flexible in a fast-paced, changing environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Why You Want To Work Here: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Cool people, solving cool problems to disrupt the marketplace</li>
<li>We value your input. You will have the opportunity to not only lead the growth on a new market but help us to continually improve our products and solutions.</li>
<li>Opportunity to get in on the “ground floor” of a growing company.</li>
<li>Startup culture, catered lunches, bring your dog to work, competitive pay, employee stock option plan (ESOP), excellent medical benefits (health/dental/vision), flexible PTO schedule.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Who We Are: </b></p>
<p><b>BacklotCars</b>is an online marketplace for auto dealers and is changing the way they buy/sell wholesale vehicles.</p>
<p>Through BacklotCars, auto dealers, auto finance, and rental car companies can transact without having to go to physical auctions.</p>
<p>Our end to end solution is truly seamless for dealers, as BacklotCars provides vehicle inspections, transportation, and inventory finance.</p>
<p>Our mission is to make wholesale easy so dealers can be more successful.</p>
<p>The team at BacklotCars includes professionals from both inside and outside the auto industry, which gives us a unique perspective on its problems and innovative solutions. We blend our deep expertise in the auto industry and technology to modernize auto wholesale.</p>
<p>We were founded in Kansas City and graduated from 500 Startups, a prestigious accelerator in Silicon Valley. Since launching, we’ve seen tremendous growth and now help dealers buy and sell thousands of cars a month across 18 states.</p>
<p>As one of the fastest growing early-stage startups in the region, we’ve attracted investors from all over the world.</p>
<p>We are looking for individuals who share our growth mindset, and who are ready to execute on ideas that will change an industry.</p>
<p>If you are excited about disrupting a marketplace and next-generation technology platforms, please send us your information to join us as we keep the expansion rolling.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
