<h2>Sr. Finance Systems Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Team Description</p>
<p>
Our Business Operations team sets and drives key business initiatives across the company. We complement and support customer success, sales, and marketing teams across three critical areas: business planning, sales operations, and business systems &amp; tools development.</p>
<p>
Role Description:</p>
<p>
Medrio is looking for a Sr. Finance Systems Analyst to help us create a best in class financial system. As a Sr. Finance Systems Analyst you will own the vision for Medrio’s financial / business systems and quote to cash process. As part of the Business Operations team, you will be a trusted thought leader to drive:</p>
<ul>
<li>Operational excellence: Help our leaders run Medrio efficiently, which includes integrating disparate business systems as well as identifying and monitoring critical success metrics.
</li>
<li>Strategic knowledge &amp; insights: Develop unique insights on our business trends. Disseminate this knowledge across Medrio to inform key decisions and initiatives.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Own the conceptual product that is Medrio’s financial / business systems; partner with finance, IT and business operations resources to deliver a product our finance team loves</li>
<li>Own the roadmap for the integration of Medrio’s finance and business systems</li>
<li>Translate strategic and tactical KPIs into dashboards that enable data driven decisions</li>
<li>Identify issues and opportunities; propose process, data, or systematic solutions</li>
<li>Ensure timely, accurate and cost-effective solutions that meet project requirements</li>
<li>Maintain our critical finance systems: coordinate system upgrade activity, respond to issues and incidents and provide day-to-day administration of the critical business systems that drive our finance operations</li>
<li>Conduct analysis of Medrio systems and financial platforms. Use the latest tools to represent Medrio’s business &amp; technical processes</li>
<li>Draw from your experience to recommend changes in development, maintenance and system standards</li>
<li>Keep all of Medrio up-to-date on the conversion and implementation of finance solutions</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills/Areas of Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>A deep understanding of key financial business processes (Billing, Collections, sales compensation, Revenue Management, Quote-to-Cash)</li>
<li>Hands-on Experience with SaaS financial and integration applications like SFDC, NetSuite, Celigo etc</li>
<li>Exceptional communication with active listening skills, and ability to convey complex ideas in a clear, concise fashion to both technical and non-technical audiences</li>
<li>Experience working with systems that handle sensitive data</li>
<li>Project Management capabilities; develop work plans, estimate work efforts</li>
<li>Experience with full system implementation life-cycles (analyze, design, build, test, implement, support)</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in design and implementation of vendor software and/or building custom financial applications</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Finance or Accounting</li>
<li>Strong problem solving and analytical/critical thinking skills needed, as well as exceptional interpersonal skills</li>
<li>An obsession with performance, user experience and efficiency</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce and Excel is required</li>
<li>NetSuite Certification a plus!</li>
</ul>
