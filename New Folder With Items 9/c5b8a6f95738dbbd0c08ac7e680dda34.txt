<h2>VP, Brand Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>VP, Brand &amp; Buzz</b><br>
</p>
<p><b>What you’ll do:</b><br>
You’re going to be the voice of Tangoe, managing an 11-person team and outside resources who between them develop all external communications to analysts, press, social media and customers; generate positive brand perception, leads, content and messaging; give sales support and oversee company brand, events and customer programs (user groups). Reporting to the CMO you’ll be working closely with counterparts in Product Marketing and Lead Generation ensuring alignment and consistency of messaging, collaborating with the CEO and executive team on critical communications and events, and nurturing and championing “big creative ideas”. Your team are all experts in their fields, needing a leader to give direction and advice to help them meet their deliverables and position us in the market place as the #1 in Technology Expense Management. You’ll also be the lead steward for our internal corporate communications, building and strengthening cross-departmental collaboration, also working with the Talent &amp; Learning team on employee engagement initiatives.<br>
<br>
<b>Who you are:</b><br>
Integrity · Excellence · Courage · Selflessness are the values we live by and we look for that in all our staff. You already have solid experience and career growth in marketing AND communications and are an experienced people manager, great at team-building. You’re perfectly at home in the software/tech/telco B2B markets, with a proven track record of driving awareness, engagement and loyalty, creating highly-engaging, best in class strategies that resonate with the F500 market place and with staff internally. We’re looking for a leader who seizes opportunity, someone with focus, a tenacious drive, and a creative streak a mile wide who can take Tangoe through the next stage in our story. You’re a tech-savvy, highly-motivated (with a stellar work ethic), strategically-focused, highly-active evangelist, capable of inspiring others through strategic and tactical action complex marketing initiatives. You’re exceptionally data-driven, using that info creatively; able to thrive in a dynamic, fast-paced and feedback-driven environment. And you’re probably reading this advert thinking “I could have done that better”!<br>
<br>
<b>Where you’ll work:</b><br>
At our gorgeous office in Shelton, CT, with our inspirational new CMO. It’s in a great, easily accessible location, has great facilities and most of the Marketing team are based there too.<br>
<br>
<b>Who we are</b>:<br>
We have a Purpose: Helping millions of people work smarter every day.<br>
A Vision: We are the global standard for America's greatest brands.<br>
And we’re on a Mission: Helping our customers grow their bottom line.<br>
Our breadth of solutions, depth of services, and range of experience deliver the most robust IT and Expense Management platform in the market. In other words, we’re really rather fabulous, and Gartner rates us as “Visionary”! To learn more about us, check out our latest podcasts on our blog: https://www.tangoe.com/blog/<br>
<br>
<b>What we offer:</b><br>
A competitive salary, along with a whole stack of other benefits too! We're ambitious, friendly, driven, and passionate. If you want to be a part of a like-minded team in an evolving, challenging marketplace, then it’s worth us having a chat.<br>
