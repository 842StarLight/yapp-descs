<li>Account Manager, Inside Sales<br>
<br>
Key Responsibilities
</li>
<li>Focused on building relationships and becoming a trusted technology advisor to our customers.</li>
<li>
Includes out-bounding calls to drive new business acquisition with established and new customers.</li>
<li>
May have some involvement in outside sales leads with Sales Representative support.</li>
<li>
Ability to be a trusted business advisor to the customer and uses in-depth knowledge of Dell’s technology, products and services to help customers formulate direction.</li>
<li>
Ability to utilize strategic probing to identify, evaluate, and recommend alternative business solutions.</li>
<li>
Analysis of multiple market factors to both anticipate/identify customer problems/needs and recommends appropriate solution.</li>
<li>
Effectively balance demands from multiple stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Effectively balance short term and long term priorities.</li>
<li>
Develop and implement strategic account plans that drive the attainment of critical business objectives.</li>
<li>
Ability to be Involved in contract negotiation and possesses strong financial/business and pricing knowledge.<br>
<br>
Essential Requirements
</li>
<li>2+ years of sales/customer service experience</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills.</li>
<li>
Effectively use software packages such as Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of computer hardware, software, &amp; peripherals</li>
<li>
Demonstrate strong verbal and written skills<br>
<br>
Desirable Requirements
</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent, with a minimum of 2+ years sales, management or related experience.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of applicable Dell technology, products, and services and is able to identify how these products and services align to customer needs.<br>
