<p>About us: WhiteSource sells a platform to help companies manage the open-source software embedded in their products. WhiteSource identifies open-source components and dependencies in a customer’s proprietary software, providing tools to manage the compliance, security, reporting, and licensing of open-source components.</p>
<p>
Founded in 2011, WhiteSource now has more than 100 employees with offices in Israel and the U.S. WhiteSource became backed by Susquehanna Growth Equity in August 2018, enabling it to accelerate growth. In the past, it raised from 83North and Microsoft.</p>
<p>
WhiteSource is headquartered in Israel. Its U.S. offices are located in New York City and Boston.</p>
<p>
We are seeking an experienced and creative individual with a demonstrated track record selling software solution to Fortune 1000 companies.</p>
<p>
If you’re looking to grow your career at an innovative and well-funded startup that rewards success, this is the ideal opportunity for you!</p>
<p>
You must be comfortable selling technical solutions to all levels of a software development organization.</p>
