<h2>Real Estate Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div>For nearly two decades, Con Edison Solutions, Con Edison Development, and Con Edison Energy have been leading national providers of retail and wholesale energy services. Now, they are a part of Con Edison Clean Energy Businesses (CEB), a wholly-owned subsidiary of Consolidated Edison, Inc.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As we continue to grow, the CEB is seeking to hire a Real Estate Advisor to provide comprehensive land acquisition support to the development team. In this position, the Real Estate Advisor will acquire the land documents, mineral rights and other real estate interests necessary to complete the development of utility-scale renewable energy projects across the country. The Real Estate Advisor will assist the development team to prospect for new solar, wind and energy storage sites, including researching mineral rights, title issues and other land issues that could affect the feasibility of development of utility-scale renewable energy projects. The Real Estate Advisor will lead negotiations with landowners to secure options to lease or acquire easements, purchase and sale agreements, surface use agreements, manage the preparation and approval of all real estate rights and title agreements needed to complete project financings.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Acquisition/Disposition of Real Estate Interests:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Lead company’s efforts in acquisition and disposition of real estate interests for large energy projects in coordination with the company’s project development team</li>
<li>
Assist with new market and site analysis including investigation of land development criteria and land ownership</li>
<li>
Assist in due diligence for new land acquisitions
</li>
<li>Coordinate efforts among landowners, realtors, attorneys, surveyors, utilities, title companies, and other key project stakeholders</li>
<li>
Communicate and negotiate with landowners and other counter-parties in connection with real estate agreements</li>
<li>
Assist with drafting and execution of option, purchase and lease agreements, amendments, easements, crossing agreements, estoppel, subordination agreements, affidavits, etc.</li>
<li>
Acquire easements, rights-of-way, mineral rights and other real estate interests necessary to complete the development of utility scale renewable energy projects</li>
<li>
Review land use plans, zoning, and any environmental mitigation requirements</li>
<li>
Assist in preparing land project budgets; track and coordinate option payments</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Project Acquisition, Equity Sales and Financing:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct due diligence on real estate in connection with potential large energy project acquisitions including reviewing, analyzing and evaluating land contracts, option agreements, easements, Rights-of-Way (ROW), crossing agreements, title reports and ALTA surveys.</li>
<li>
Assist with review, resolution and reporting of title matters to support financing of projects including mineral rights, title issues, liens, and taxes.</li>
<li>
Address diligence queries of lenders and investors in connection with limited recourse and tax equity financing, and asset and equity sales.</li>
<li>
Perform curative title actions and manage procurement of title insurance necessary for acquisition and financing.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Existing Real Estate Interests:</b></div>
<ul><li>
Ensure compliance with existing option agreements, leases and real estate-related obligations in project and financing agreements in coordination with the company’s asset management team.</li></ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Education:</b></div>
<div>
A minimum of an Associate degree in Business, Real-Estate, Paralegal Studies or related area of study preferred.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of experience on real estate matters and title work required.</li>
<li>
7+ years of experience on real estate matters and title work for energy projects in lieu of degree preferred.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Job Skills:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Advanced real estate knowledge and experience in execution of complex real estate projects</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability and initiative to manage the process associated with acquisition and disposal of land rights</li>

Knowledge of title insurance underwriting practices and experience obtaining and negotiating title insurance products including curative title work, mineral rights and water rights.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of surveying and engineering drawings, including ability to analyze and evaluate surveys in relation to title and real estate documentation</li>
<li>
Practical experience with accessing County Assessor data and real estate GIS software systems</li>

Resourcefulness, with an eye for detail and investigative skills are essential</li>

Ability to research independently, multi-task and meet deadlines under tight time-frame</li>

Ability to analyze and develop solutions to resolve complex issues</li>
<li>
Excellent time management and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>

Demonstrates the ability to build working relationships within all levels of organizations</li>
<li>
Proficient in MS Word, Excel and Power-Point</li>

Travel up to 20% of time</li>
</ul>
