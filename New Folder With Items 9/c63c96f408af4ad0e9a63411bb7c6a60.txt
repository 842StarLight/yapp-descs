<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Sr. Analyst will be responsible for supporting mobile sales reporting and analysis for one of our large mobile carrier sales team. The Sr. Analyst will work closely with Sr. Manager of Sales Strategy to develop and manage sales strategies with mobile sales teams.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Role and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for supporting the development, coordination, and communication of business plans/strategies between SEA, Global HQ and customer teams through:
</li>
<li>Maintaining systems synergy and data consistency across all internal systems.
</li>
<li>Become the internal expert across multiple systems to facilitate data manipulation and reporting via Excel based output.
</li>
<li>Collaborate across multiple teams to develop cohesive reporting capabilities across Sales Operations. Assist in creating and evaluating business plans with exception based reporting.
</li>
<li>Streamline and continue to evolve reporting tools for Account teams to enable productive Monthly Business Review and Planning Process while identifying sales target status and product requirements to ensure fulfillment of product development and delivery to achieve sales plans.
</li>
<li>Responsible for gathering, analyzing and reporting of key market performance, competitive and sales data in order to identify risks and opportunities to the business.
</li>
<li>Liaison between the PI and Account teams to ensure system maintenance and eliminate roadblocks.
</li>
<li>Partner with various teams (Consumer Insights, SCM, etc) to ensure collaborative POV in reporting to simplify multiple Account team requests.
</li>
<li>Assist in the overall management and development of Sales Reporting and Analysis.
</li>
<li>Publish daily / weekly internal reporting to identify and monitor trending business risks and opportunities.
</li>
<li>Support business cadence to review performance and take action related to financial performance, demand planning, business risks and opportunities to better align plans to financial objectives.
</li>
<li>Develop real-time insights on market trends, shifts in sales behaviors, new category entrants, etc.
</li>
<li>Active engagement with customer teams, internal support teams and Executives.
</li>
<li>Superior Excel skills coupled with strong analytical capabilities and business savvy.
</li>
<li>Accurate and timely analysis working with large, multi-variable data sets.
</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple requests and reprioritization in a fast paced environment.
</li>
<li>Independent problem solver that demonstrates resourcefulness and takes initiative.
</li>
<li>Approachable individual that actively engagements with customer teams, internal support teams and Executives to achieve common goals.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree, preferably in business management or related technical field, from an accredited college or university. Advanced degree highly preferred.
</li>
<li>Should have a minimum of 5+ years of experience in a telecommunications, CPG, technology, and/or retail industries.
</li>
<li>Should have a minimum of 3 years in sales operations, product planning, statistics, finance, or related field
</li>
<li>Fluent in reading, writing, and verbal communications in English.
</li>
<li>Experience developing presentations using a variety of Global HQ and English PC applications methods, including word-processing and spreadsheet software.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Necessary Skills &amp; Attributes:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain excellent working relationships with all assigned levels within and outside the company.
</li>
<li>Plan, organize, and prioritize multiple assignments and projects.
</li>
<li>Review detailed product development, marketing documents and media materials as needed.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated competency in both oral and written communication modes for both internal and external personnel at various levels.
</li>
<li>Work independently and in a team environment in order to achieve customer business objectives, personal and team goals and complete assignments within established time frames and specifications.
</li>
<li>The ability to develop tasks and work assignments, based on sometime vaguely defined objectives.

</ul>
