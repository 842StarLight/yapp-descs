<h2>Regional Product Marketing Manager - Leisure & Longtail</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Travelport is the only true travel commerce platform in the world. As a Regional Propositions Manager at Travelport, you can thrive in the exciting world of Travel Tech.<br>
<br>
<b>Role &amp; Team</b><br>
<br>
You will come on board at a truly exciting time and as a member of the Global Propositions team. You will play a pivotal and crucial part as the senior and experienced product marketeer responsible for several important inbound and outbound product marketing activities across the Americas region.<br>
<br>
<b>The role has four key purposes…</b><br>
<ul>
<li>
Being the owner for the regional segment proposition for the entire geography of the Americas region and in agreement with the commercial leadership team for the region</li>
<li>
Key interface between Agency Commercial and C&amp;MO Global Product &amp; Proposition teams - through strong understanding of regional strategy and customer requirements; work through the individual items/requirements, apply and convey right prioritization to the delivery teams and measure success on delivery of prioritized requirements.
</li>
<li>Drive Travelport product &amp; technology adoption in the regions to meet targeted success matrix. Work with relevant internal &amp; external stakeholders in resolving barriers to adoptions.</li>
<li>
Responsible for clear articulation of our propositions at a regional level – including key regional products &amp; partnerships.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>The Regional Propositions Manager position will involve…</b><br>
<br>
<i>Outbound activities</i><ul>
<li>
Responsible for clear articulation of our propositions at a regional level – including key regional products &amp; partnerships.</li>
<li>
Sets targets and drives deployment and adoption of Travelport product portfolio in each region (SMO and Operator markets).
</li>
<li>Point person coordinating product launches in region – including delivering regular briefings / training to sales teams</li>
</ul>
<br>
<i>Inbound activities</i><ul>
<li>
Understand the regional/country commercial strategy and targets, map out product, solutions and partnership requirements.
</li>
<li>Plays a key role in triaging product enhancement requests as part of the overall product prioritization process.</li>
<li>
Runs regular (minimum quarterly) Customer Advisory Boards to provide structured input into global product decisions.
</li>
</ul>
