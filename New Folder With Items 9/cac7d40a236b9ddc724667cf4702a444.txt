JOB SUMMARY<br>
<br>
Supports management and organization of ongoing projects and initiatives. Creates and monitors project plans, priorities, timelines and budgets, as well as organizes and participates in stakeholder meetings. Manages a steady stream of content, ensuring it reaches the right audiences and channels both internally and externally.<br>
<br>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES<br>
Project Management<br>
<ul>
<li>Partners closely with Market Engagement leads during project planning, execution and measurement phases to ensure internal partner deliverables are met and feedback is shared/incorporated in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Creates, maintains and monitors project plans, priorities, timelines and budgets.</li>
<li>Organizes, prepares materials for, attends and participates in stakeholder meetings, then documents and follow-up on important actions and decisions.</li>
<li>Supports researching, testing and deploying of project management tools to facilitate better team workflow and collaboration.</li>
<li>Ensures thought leadership reaches appropriate audiences and channels when released via internal tracking and posting system.</li>
<li>Manages internal resources, such as design and research resources, as part of project execution.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Company &amp; Industry Knowledge<br>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates intellectual curiosity about the biopharmaceutical/medtech development and commercialization process.</li>
<li>Develops an understanding of internal partner needs to actively build trusted working relationships for the business by performing a range of well-defined assignments.</li>
<li>Actively contributes in group and team meetings, as well as in brainstorming, developing and researching programs based on company priorities.</li>
<li>Learns and embraces the company vision, values and structure to serve as an ambassador for them, and learns full range of company products, services and capabilities.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Professional Skills<br>
<ul>
<li>Communicates effectively in-person and in writing with utmost professionalism.</li>
<li>Understands and effectively utilize both internal and external research capabilities, including use of research and database tools.</li>
<li>Monitors and analyzes media coverage and other relevant communications vehicles for TL leads and prepares reports as needed.</li>
<li>Evaluates and analyzes information and data, including development of succinct, accurate summaries and analyses of research.</li>
<li>Develops and proposes tactical program elements that further team/internal stakeholder objectives.</li>
</ul>

QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in communications, liberal arts, business/marketing or sciences business and/or healthcare</li>
<li>Collaborates effectively with communications professionals across the business units to apply best practices to all communications.</li>
<li>Proven track record in managing and measuring work and communication effectiveness.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, deadline driven environment.</li>
<li>Open-minded, flexible and adaptable to change.</li>
<li>Possesses exceptional verbal, written and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Highly organized, self-motivated and be able to work effectively both independently and as part of a team.</li>
<li>Experience with billing, budget development and management required.</li>
<li>Must be an independent and critical thinker who is a strong team player with the ability to clearly express ideas and the confidence to work with senior leaders.</li>
<li>Experience with content management systems and project management software a plus.</li>
<li>Proactive problem solver able to identify emerging issues and actions to address.</li>
<li>Proven writing capabilities in a variety of formats including persuasive writing, memos and plans, related FAQs and other collateral materials.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications such as Word, PowerPoint and Excel.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work under pressure, to meet tight deadlines and to manage multiple projects simultaneously and autonomously.</li>
<li>Editing and proofreading skills, along with strong attention to detail while delivering against deadlines.</li>
<li>Works well in matrix environment.</li>
<li>Social media promotional skill set needed.</li>
</ul>
