<h2>Part-Time Center Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Center Associate is the backbone of The UPS Store. The Associate is responsible for delivering world-class customer service to all retail customers. He/She receives and processes packages for shipment, prepares files for printing, assists customers with notary service, and operates point-of-sale devices. The Associate expertly advises our valued customers by providing accurate information on our array of products, services, and best-value options. The Associate exhibits confidence by knowing best practices as related to the industry. The ideal Center Associate candidate will have retail sales experience, strong computer and internet skills, a high school diploma/GED, a friendly and genuinely helpful demeanor, a professional appearance, and will be a fast learner who makes an effort to learn all aspects of the business in the shortest possible time.</p>
<p>The UPS Store is a franchise that is locally owned and operated. We are not employees of United Parcel Service.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Delivers outstanding customer service to walk-in customers and telephone inquiries</li>
<li>Continuously practices good listening skills with customers, UPS Store team members, and leadership</li>
<li>Takes ownership of the customer's shipping needs and offers viable solutions</li>
<li>Takes action to learn all product and service offerings, alternative solutions, and industry trends</li>
<li>Operates all equipment, software, and devices in an expert fashion and is willing to teach others</li>
<li>Maintains a clean, organized, and safe working environment</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned</li>
<li>Maintains positive demeanor and energy while helping a high volume of customers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prompt, reliable, and responsible</li>
<li>Able to operate independently and be self-driven to complete tasks</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, including Microsoft Office and Adobe Suites</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Fluent English &amp; Spanish speaking is a plus</li>
<li>Saturday availability a must</li>
<li>High school diploma required</li>
<li>Able to lift 40+ pounds</li>
<li>Willing and able to work a very flexible, part-time schedule that may span Monday through Saturday during seasonal peak periods</li>
</ul>
<p><b>BENEFITS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Daytime hours, no Sundays</li>
<li>Uniforms Provided</li>
<li>Free Parking</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Advancement opportunities, ability to grow within the company</li>
<li>Company paid notary certification</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>

<ul><li>Customer Service: 1 year (Required)</li></ul>

<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Required)</li></ul>

</ul>
