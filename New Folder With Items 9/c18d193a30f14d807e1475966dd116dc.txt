<p><b>Project Manager - Zenner</b><b><br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p><b><br>
</b></p>
<p><b>This project management position is hands on and has no direct reports. Project management requires the individual to do prep work before going to the field and actually installing the equipment. The individual must be able to install equipment in the field and sometimes requires that work be performed in lift trucks. Field installation of MIUs and training of the customer is also a large part of the project manager responsibility. Association of hardware to software is required as well and is part of every installation. Incumbent must be hands on doing some of the work directly.<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<b>
</b><ul>
<li><b>Bachelor’s degree in a technical discipline or equivalent work experience</b></li>
<li><b>
Ability and or experience working with sub-contractors.</b></li>
<li><b>
Ability to travel at least 70% to 80% of the time</b></li>
<li><b>
Must have good oral and written communications skills.</b></li>
<li><b>
Must be proficient in using Microsoft project and Excel
 </b></li>
</ul>
