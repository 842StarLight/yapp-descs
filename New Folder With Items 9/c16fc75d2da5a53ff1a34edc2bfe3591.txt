<b><i>
About Nortek Air Management</i></b></p>
<p>
Nortek Air Management is a global, diversified HVAC company that leverages its strong brands, design and manufacturing capabilities, and business system to deliver industry-leading innovative air management solutions for lifestyle improvement at home and at work. Nortek Air Management comprises two, individual companies, Nortek Global HVAC and Nortek Air Solutions and employs 4,000 over 6 countries.</p>
<p>
Established in 1977, Nortek Air Solutions offers the innovation and expertise of the largest group of custom heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) brands in North America for your commercial, industrial, or institutional indoor environment. Air Solutions products are custom engineered to bring you the optimal solutions for maximizing energy efficiency, reducing annual operating costs, improving indoor air quality, and achieving low sound levels. They offer high quality systems in multiple shapes, sizes, and configurations for new construction and renovation or retrofit applications. Air Solutions is affiliated with the following industry-leading brands of HVAC solutions: Governair, Huntair, Mammoth, Temtrol, Venmar CES and Ventrol Air Handling Systems.</p>
<p><b><i>
Why is this a great opportunity</i></b>?</p>
<p>
You will be on the frontline of customer satisfaction, working with a passionate team that shares your goals for teamwork and success.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b><i>
Job Summary</i></b></p>
<p>
As our Service Tech, you will work with warranty related service issues for our HVAC/R, HVAC/C product lines and heat pumps. Service technician provide service and technical expertise to end users on equipment and guidance for outside sales and service representatives. In many cases, recommending our service and engineering departments to be more involved. Other aspects of the role include working on start-ups and commissioning of new products.<br>
<br>
We are looking for a high-quality individual to fill this position with our service team in any US location.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b><i>
Job Responsibilities</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Telephone (and other electronic media) to provide customers with information necessary to run their Nortek Group HVAC products and systems.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience with field installations, contractors, contracts and bidding (the commercial side); "systems" engineering and a variety of HVAC equipment (the equipment side); controls and software (the integration side).</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience in managing challenging jobs with the objectives to mitigate the impact on customer’s building/experience while also minimizing the financial burden on the company. This requires working with multiple entities on a job – building owners, general and mechanical contractors, engineers, and local servicing contractors.</li>
<li>
Formalize through development and documentation job responsibilities, methods, procedures for all areas.</li>
<li>
Work with engineering, manufacturing on problem resolution and final field and production fixes to service issues on our equipment.</li>
<li>
Be a part of an “Authorized Service Partner” program to include training on company equipment, a “certification process”, and marketing collateral. These partners would include local servicing contractors and/or the service arms of company’s independent sales representatives.</li>
<li>
Adapt at building/contributing to CRM systems.</li>
<li>
Keep up to date on trends, training and manufacturing at our plants.</li>
<li>
Participates in technology transfer initiatives among IT departments, R &amp; D, and Product Engineering</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 70%</li>
</ul>
