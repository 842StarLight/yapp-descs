<p><b>
Director of Sales, Digital Solutions – Washington DC</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a Director of Sales, Digital Solutions, based in the Washington DC Metro area.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul><li>Development of sales plans to effectively and efficiently cover the accounts within an assigned geography</li></ul>
<ul><li>Aggressively prospect and generate new contractual relationships with enterprise accounts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage the success, adoption and growth of existing customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Some travel required</li></ul>
<p><b>
Description</b>:</p>
<p>
The Director of Sales, Digital Solutions, is responsible for selling Digital Transformation solutions and consulting services to new and existing clients with a heavy focus on ServiceNow software and services.</p>
<p>
As the <b>Director of Sales</b>, you will:</p>
<ul><li>Lead business development efforts for Digital Solutions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with ServiceNow sales teams to identify potential target clients</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with CDI core sales team and other business partners to uncover potential opportunities in existing accounts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop understanding of ServiceNow portfolios and offerings, as well as CDI service offerings with knowledge of our capabilities outside of ServiceNow</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand market segmentation of target clients and potential fit with relevant business processes, technology platforms, market offerings and ecosystems</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop understanding of target client’s buying patterns based on industry knowledge, relationships, prior experience, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify target contacts and relationships within potential clients and conduct initial conversation(s) to explore opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Qualify opportunities and engage appropriate resources</li></ul>
<ul><li>Run a disciplined sales pipeline including lead management, qualification, bringing established sales methods to the sales process</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop overview materials to support initial meetings/conversation</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead preparations for more serious sales meetings and orals for qualified opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify opportunities (sole source/up for bid) and bring it to the business (functional) partners, evaluate opportunity alignment with client strategy</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contribute to pursuit processes by leveraging relationships for insights and influence, including determining “win” themes, aligning messaging with client needs, supporting proposal/orals materials preparation, and participating in oral sessions as appropriate</li></ul>
<p><b>
Required Skills/Experience</b>:</p>
<ul><li>Proven track record of success (5+ years) in related business application sales, engaging C-level decision makers</li></ul>
