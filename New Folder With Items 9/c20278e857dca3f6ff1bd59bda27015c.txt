<h2>Broker Commission Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>Lead the team responsible for administering Broker commissions; ensuring accurate and timely payments; and resolving inquiries originating from brokers, general agents, Account Management, Sales and Broker Services. Work directly with Brokers to resolve complex commission issues for PPO and HMO lines of business. Responsible as the first point-of-contact for all Broker related commission issues and questions. Responsible for Business Requirement Documents (BRD’s), Project Portfolio Management (PPM’s) Service Level Agreements (SLA’s), and managing the Sales Administration team off-shore.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Responsibilities:
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Manage broker commission process and staff.</div></li>
<li><div>Provide oversight of day-to-day Broker Commission administration and reporting.</div></li>
<li><div>Ensure that sales performance data is accurately posted to the Broker Commission System (Facets).</div></li>
<li><div>Resolve commission inquiries originating from brokers, general agents, Account Management, Sales and Broker Services within 24 hours.</div></li>
<li><div>Responsible for Broker Licensing and Appointments.</div></li>
<li><div>Review Service Level Agreements (SLA’s).</div></li>
<li><div>Oversee Electronic Funds Transfers (EFT) Broker Payment Initiative.</div></li>
<li><div>Manage reconciliation of monthly commission cycle and report issues to leadership immediately.</div></li>
<li><div>Provide oversight of Business Requirement Documents (BRD’s) process. Serve as leader for: commission related BRD’s to meet the expectations of the business; PPO transition BRD; ConnectiCare Broker transition to Facets; and ASO Broker BRD implementation in Facets.</div></li>
<li><div>Open and manage project portfolios as required to meet the needs of the business.</div></li>
<li><div>Review Commission Service Level Agreements. (SLA’s): confirm SLA’s for monthly commission cycle completion; develop Q&amp;A for commission SLA’s and make recommendations.</div></li>
<li><div>Serve as Point-of-Contact for Group Administration (Off-Shore) Broker Tower Leader for Commissions.</div></li>
<li><div>Review pending Inventory.</div></li>
<li><div>Manage outstanding service requests with off-shore.</div></li>
<li><div>Manage Commission Platform Implementation.</div></li>
<li><div>Participate in commissions software vendor demonstrations.</div></li>
<li><div>Lead implementation of new commission software solution.</div></li>
</ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>Qualifications:
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Bachelor’s degree, preferably in Finance, Business, or equivalent</div></li>
<li><div>5-7 years of relevant experience, including experience with Broker commission administration required</div></li>
<li><div>Additional years of related experience may be used in lieu of educational requirements required</div></li>
<li><div>Experience in successfully managing staff and processes required</div></li>
<li><div>In-depth knowledge of Broker and General Agent compensation plans required</div></li>
<li><div>Proven ability to manage commission software to ensure accurate and timely payments to Brokers required</div></li>
<li><div>Demonstrated ability to meet strict deadlines, prioritize work and multi-task required</div></li>
<li><div>Strong background with healthcare commission payment process required
</div></li>
<li><div>Knowledge and understanding of licensing and Broker appointments required</div></li>
</ul>
