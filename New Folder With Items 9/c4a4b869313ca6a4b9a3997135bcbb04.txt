<h2>Concierge (Part-Time)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
POSITION SUMMARY
<br>The Concierge is responsible for greeting guests, welcoming them to the resort, and assisting them throughout their stay. They provide information regarding what the resort and surrounding areas have to offer to our guests and must be able to make recommendations based on their needs. Position requires flexibility, creativity, eagerness to learn about community events, and the ability to effectively work in small and large groups.
<br><br>
The Concierge also provides all the information that guests need to enjoy their stay. This may include providing information on the facilities available and the services able to be booked as well as travel routes, available tours, schedules for outings and transportation availability.
<br><br>
CUSTOMER SERVICE
<br>Total commitment in providing superior, personalized customer service to all internal and external customers of King’s Creek. Respond to the needs of our customers with friendliness, integrity, responsiveness and sensitivity.
<br><br>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Communicate with and assist guests with questions regarding activities at the resort and surrounding areas.</li>
<li>Coordinate with the OPC-OS staff when assisting guests who are not scheduled for a tour.</li>
<li>Greet all guests during high flow wave times, check in, and all evening shifts (4-8 pm), hand out refreshments and direct them to the hospitality station for beverages.</li>
<li>Assist OPC-OS Staff when needed, make packets, greet guests, and hand out parking passes.</li>
<li>Handle procurement of tickets for special shows, events, tee times, spa services, assist with travel arrangements, tours of local attractions and dining reservations. Promote those who advertise in our KCP Explore book.</li>
<li>Hand out pamphlets and brochures that are available to guests, as needed; may make recommendations about activities that suit the guest.</li>
<li>Provide information that guests need to enjoy their stay; may include information on the available tours, information on travel routes, schedules for outings and transportation availability.</li>
<li>Sell tickets to area attractions, pay to play amenities, shuttle services and be responsible for cash bank and inventory of physical tickets</li>
<li>Enthusiastically promote programs, activities, advertisers and ticket sales of the Resort and serve as a role model in attendance and interaction at the events.</li>
<li>Point of contact for all local attraction vendors.</li>
<li>Monitor local activities in the surrounding area as it develops and changes; advise supervisor, guests and other departments informed of changes.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining Kingsmill inventory; obtaining guest signature on card check out form; taking deposits and returning funds when the card is returned; charging guest for unreturned cards.</li>
<li>Maintain a good working relationship with Kingsmill staff.</li>
<li>Review magazines weekly to ensure appropriate ads are printed. Should there be an inappropriate ad it shall be reported to Manager, Guest Relations for review.</li>
<li>Maintain the brochure racks with current and updated brochures and/or magazines (dispose of expired).</li>
<li>Ensure work station and surrounding work area is in well-kept manner and clear of disorder.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain positive working relationships with all staff.</li>
<li>Consistently display a positive attitude and cheerful spirit in all duties and interactions with guests and staff.</li>
<li>Maintain a professional appearance and manner at all times with guests and staff.</li>
<li>Attend all training/development sessions offered.</li>
<li>Attend all departmental meetings, as necessary.</li>
<li>Follow all established policies and procedures, e.g., uniform standards.</li>
</ul>
OTHER DUTIES
<br>Perform special projects, additional or different duties, from those set forth above, to address business needs and changing business practices as directed by supervisor.
<br><br>
WORKING CONDITIONS AND ENVIRONMENT
<br><b>Physical Demands:</b> Primary functions require sufficient physical ability and mobility to work in an active environment; to stand for prolonged periods of time; to occasionally stoop, bend, kneel, crouch, reach, and twist; to lift, carry, push, and/or pull up to 30 pounds of weight; to operate office equipment requiring repetitive hand movement and fine coordination including use of a computer keyboard; and to verbally communicate information. Specific vision abilities usually required by this job include close, distance, color and peripheral vision, depth perception and ability to adjust focus.
<br><br>
<b>Working Conditions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Part-time position</li>
<li>Varied work schedules to include evening, holiday, and weekend hours.</li>
</ul>
<b>Vacation:</b> Vacation is unavailable during peak time; Memorial Day through Labor Day, Thanksgiving weekend (Thanksgiving Day-Sunday) and December 23rd-New Year’s Day
<br><br>
<b>Environment:</b> The noise level in the work environment is usually moderate. Work is performed both indoors and outside.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
QUALIFICATIONS SUMMARY
<br><br>
Education
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma or GED required.</li>
</ul>
Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Customer service related work experience required, with an emphasis in vacation programs preferred.</li>
<li>Background in hospitality industry preferred.</li>
</ul>
<b>Job Knowledge and Abilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Basic knowledge of timesharing operations.</li>
<li>Knowledge of vacation ownership and its exchange companies.</li>
<li>Knowledge of personal computers and their various software applications.</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of the Williamsburg area geography, events, and activities.</li>
<li>General knowledge of neighboring area events and activities.</li>
<li>Ability to exhibit excellent guest relations skills.</li>
<li>Ability to be flexible and creative with high energy level.</li>
<li>Ability to present an outgoing personality with a sense of humor.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively; both verbally and in writing, using tact, patience and courtesy.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task with strong organizational skills.</li>
</ul>
