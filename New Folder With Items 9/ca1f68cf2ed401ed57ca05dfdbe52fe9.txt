<p></p>
<p><b>Who are we?</b></p>
<p>
Live Nation Entertainment is the world’s leading live entertainment company comprised of global market leaders: Ticketmaster, Live Nation Concerts, Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship and Artist Nation Management. Ticketmaster is the global event ticketing leader with nearly 500 million tickets sold and more than 12,500 clients worldwide. Live Nation Concerts is the largest provider of live entertainment in the world promoting more than 30,000 shows annually for nearly 4,000 artists in 40 countries. Artist Nation Management is one of the world’s leading artist management companies, representing more than 350 artists. These businesses power Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship to deliver music marketing programs across 1,000 brands enabling advertisers to tap into the over 90 million fans Live Nation Entertainment delivers annually through its live event and digital platforms.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Entertainment, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
Live Nation Entertainment’s Media &amp; Sponsorship Division is seeking Vice President, Business Operations to work closely with the SVP, Business Development in driving strategy, business operations, revenues, and overall organizational health. The VP Business Operations will act as a key senior advisor to the SVP and lead Group initiatives on operational excellence, resource allocation and fiscal planning as well as participate in overall strategic decisions.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Additionally, the VP Business Operations will act as a resource for large, complex deals with key strategic partners within the Media &amp; Sponsorship Division. The ideal candidate will effectively partner with Sales and Brand Management leadership in the ideation, development, and execution of multi-faceted agreements that require involvement with, and coordination of, multiple parts of the Live Nation/Ticketmaster ecosystem.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship architects, sells and manages the company’s national corporate sponsorship programs with the world’s most recognizable brands. It is an exciting, fast-paced team that collaborates with all facets of Live Nation’s business including venues, festivals, ticketing, artists, content, media (digital, social, mobile), promotions, and concert production. To learn more about our division, check out: https://livenationforbrands.com/.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the day-to-day operations for the E-Commerce Integrations Group, focused on continuous optimization in the pursuit of efficiency, agility, product quality and strategic impact.</li>
<li>
Solve problems and take corrective actions.</li>
<li>
Devise strategies and policies to ensure successful implementation of Group goals and profitability, in the current year as well as future years.</li>
<li>
Appoint managers and assign responsibilities, evaluate performance, optimize resourcing and other human resource activities.</li>
<li>
Lead financial budgeting, planning, reporting and tracking for Group, as well as defining KPI’s and monitoring success metrics, for overall Group health.</li>
<li>
Coordinate communications and activities with key stakeholders within the Live Nation / Ticketmaster ecosystem.</li>
<li>
Drive revenue growth by evangelizing Product Development priorities with Sales and Brand Management, creating Integration proposals for strategic partnerships, and communicating technical and product details simply and effectively to key stakeholders internally and externally.</li>
<li>
Participate in sales process, including deal point development, defining product scope, pricing, budget, resources and pipeline prioritization, and working across sales leadership, legal and relevant business stakeholders (e.g. Ticketmaster, festival promoters, Finance, Legal, etc.).</li>
<li>
Must be willing to travel up to 25% of the time, domestically and internationally, across LNE business units and to support key sales opportunities as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree, MBA required.</li>
<li>
10+ years of Business Operations with broad business management experience.</li>
<li>
5+ years Web, E-Commerce, Product Development or related technology experience.</li>
<li>
Executive presence and gravitas, effective at cultivating relationships of all kinds and at all levels, leadership that inspires respect and loyalty.</li>
<li>
Strong skills in economics, marketing, finance, and accounting, as well as excellent strategic, communication, and time-management skills.</li>
</ul>
