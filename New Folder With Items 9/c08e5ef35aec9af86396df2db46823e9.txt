<b>Highlights:</b><br>
<br>
As the Lead Enterprise Order Fulfillment Specialist, you focus on encouraging long-term client satisfaction and retention. You excel at accurately entering necessary client information in a timely manner across multiple systems for complex coaxial and fiber-based orders. You accelerate the order completion process through regular quality checks on service installations and construction contracts to ensure accuracy. You have developed a keen ability for identifying discrepancies and correcting them. You thrive in an office environment managing accounts across the Spectrum Enterprise footprint. You report directly to Senior Manager of Enterprise Order Fulfillment and Enterprise Complex Provisioning/Service Activation for goals, guidance and assistance.<br>

What you will do:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as a senior member of the team and ensure a reliable fulfillment process by mentoring and training team members and serving as a backup to leadership.</li>
<li>
Improve client satisfaction by ensuring accurate processing for complex accounts in the appropriate systems.</li>
<li>
Drive the order entry process by accurately submitting service orders for new and disconnect requests and initiate installations by scheduling services, upgrades and special requests.</li>
<li>
Ensure accuracy for all Service Level Agreements (SLAs), sales, service orders and the client database by regularly conducting cross-reference and quality checks.</li>
