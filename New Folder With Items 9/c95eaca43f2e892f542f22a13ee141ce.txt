<h2>International Product Manager - Digital Pathology</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><ul>
<div>
<p>The International Product Manager (IPM) is responsible for leading product life-cycle management for on-market and/or newly assigned digital pathology product/s, with an immediate focus on the Roche uPath enterprise software and other related digital products. The IPM will be the product champion and main conduit for all product-related communications. The IPM must work well across internal functions and global affiliates, and be able to create a positive, productive team environment that is focused on the customer</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drives and coordinates development of global marketing tools and activities (e.g., brochures, training, manuals, slide decks, internet/intranet sites and other e-media).</li>
<li>
Integrates regional marketing dynamics into product strategy using global markets, customer segmentation, VOC and competitive environment analysis</li>
<li>
Drives activities related to launching/promoting new and existing products.</li>
<li>
Develops marketing deliverables for product commercialization, including comprehensive global plans for specific product launches.</li>
<li>
Continuously monitor customer requirements and drive product line performance and required specification and quality improvement initiatives.</li>
<li>
Provides high level support and guidance to the affiliate companies on product issues.</li>
<li>
Plans and contributes to the International Business Team meetings (IBT), and affiliate meetings.</li>
<li>
Maintains up-to-date knowledge of the global market for product area.</li>
<li>
Initiates appropriate market research; monitors competitor activity and recommends and develops action to address them.</li>
<li>
Creates and fosters a strong team spirit and takes on the role of ‘product champion’.</li>
<li>
Cultivates and develops Key Opinion Leaders, product advocates, speakers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Formal Training/Education:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in marketing, science or a related field.</li>
<li>
Masters or MBA preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Three (3) – five (5) years of marketing/sales or related experience in the pharmaceutical, diagnostic or related industry.</li>
<li>
Must have software and/or Information Technology (IT) product/marketing management experience or equivalent.</li>
<li>
Preferred experience with commercializing software development and/or cloud-based solutions.</li>
<li>
Experience with anatomic pathology laboratory and/or LIS connectivity also a plus.</li>
</ul>
