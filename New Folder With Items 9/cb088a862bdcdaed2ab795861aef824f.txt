<div><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>Manages the day-to-day operation of the accounting office and assists the property Director of Finance and Accounting in providing financial leadership to the business. Primary responsibilities include financial analysis and financial reporting, budgeting/forecasting, audit and control, asset and liability reconciliation, working capital and cash control. Assists with capital expenditure plans (Return On Investment Analysis), owner relations and owner reporting.</div>

CANDIDATE PROFILE
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education and Experience</b></div>
<ul><li>4-year bachelor's degree in Finance and Accounting or related major; no work experience required.</li></ul>

<div><b>
CORE WORK ACTIVITIES</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Leading Accounting Team
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Utilizes interpersonal and communication skills to lead, influence, and encourage others; advocates sound financial/business decision making; demonstrates honesty/integrity; leads by example.</li>
<li>Celebrates successes and publicly recognizes the contributions of team members.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains open, collaborative relationships with employees and ensures employees do the same within the team.</li>
<li>Leverages strong functional leadership and communication skills to influence the management team and to lead own team.</li>
<li>Creates a working environment that enables the retention of top talent and where individuals perform at their best.</li>
<li>Encourages open dialogue between team members.</li>
<li>Assigns team members and other department managers clear accountability to accomplish goals.</li>
<li>Ensures disciplinary procedures and documentation are completed according to Standard and Local Operating Procedures (SOPs and LSOPs) and support the Peer Review Process.</li>
<li>Uses all available on the job training tools for employees.</li>
<li>Ensures property policies are administered fairly and consistently.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Maintaining Finance and Accounting Goals</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Submits reports in a timely manner, ensuring delivery deadlines.</li>
<li>Ensures profits and losses are documented accurately.</li>
<li>Achieves and exceeds goals including performance goals, budget goals, team goals, etc.</li>
<li>Develops specific goals and plans to prioritize, organize, and accomplish your work.</li>
<li>Monitors all taxes that apply, ensuring that taxes are current, collected and/or accrued.</li>
<li>Compiles, codes, categorizes, calculates, tabulates, audits, or verifies information or data.</li>
<li>Maintains a strong accounting &amp; operational control environment to safeguard assets, improve operations and profitability and manage business risks.</li>
<li>Generates and reviews financial reports that are linked to the plan’s financial goals.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Managing Projects and Policies
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Generates and provides accurate and timely results in the form of reports, presentations, etc.</li>
<li>Analyzes information and evaluating results to choose the best solution and solve problems.</li>
<li>Ensures that the P&amp;L is accurate (i.e., costs are properly matched to revenue, costs are recorded in the proper accounts) and statements are delivered to appropriate individuals in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Reconciles balance sheet and ensures account balances are supported by appropriate documentation in accordance with SOPs.</li>
<li>Oversees internal, external and regulatory audit processes and ensures compliance with Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).</li>
<li>Produces accurate and timely financial reports to support effective decision making.</li>
<li>Ensures compliance with standard and local operating procedures (SOPs and LSOPs).</li>
<li>Ensures tax rates used for sales/use tax are current and proper amounts are collected and/or accrued.</li>
<li>Documents tax exempt transactions.</li>
</ul>
