<p><b>
The Role</b></p>
<p>
We're looking for a Paid Search Manager to join the rapidly expanding team that is the backbone of our company. You'll directly manage search engine marketing (SEM) campaigns across both Google AdWords and Bing Ads, making optimizations and updates daily. Your complex and diverse portfolio of clients will be a mix of both big and small that constantly challenges you, and with extensive experience and/or a proven track record, you could be working with some of our biggest brands. You'll be trusted to oversee all account activity, collaborate with managers from other channels, and come up with campaign strategies for your book of clients.</p>
