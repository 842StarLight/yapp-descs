<h2>Principal Program Manager Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Are you interested in working for one of the most exciting products in Microsoft, passionate about exceeding customer expectations and advancing Microsoft’s cloud first strategy? Are you interested in a start-up like environment, passionate about cloud computing technology and driving growth in one of Microsoft’s core businesses? If so, then look no further than the Azure Customer Experience Team! Microsoft Azure provides customers with on-demand and infinitely scalable infrastructure and platform for customers to build, host, and scale service applications on the Internet through Microsoft’s global data centers.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
FastTrack for Azure helps customers onboard to Azure, getting them on track to “build it right, architect it right, the first time”.</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
FastTrack for Azure (FTA) takes a solution-centric approach, providing customers with Microsoft Azure engineers, tools and best practices to significantly increase customer’s success with Azure. Our team is looking for you, a strong player/coach for a Principal Program Manager Lead. You will lead a team of Program Managers that bring great teamwork, organization &amp; structure, inquisitive mind, positive attitude, high-energy leadership, and engineering execution to our customer-focus team chartered with enabling a world-class experience for Microsoft Azure cloud platform customers. This role is integral to overall operationalization of the customer onboarding experience and driving success with FTA engagements.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div><b>
Your areas of responsibility include:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Build a diverse team with capabilities to oversee the customer onboarding journey, the customer experience and maintain the overall customer relationship.</li>
<li>
Create an inclusive work environment where every employee can effectively engage, and wants to be part of the team driving customer success</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing feedback that helps your direct reports improve their performance, develops skills and supports their career aspirations as well as improves the overall program’s performance.</li>
<li>
Coach and mentor others in planning, organizing and coordinating work.</li>
<li>
As a player, manage your own customer’s onboarding journey, the customer experience and maintain the overall customer relationship.</li>
<li>
Work closely with FTA Engineer Leads in addressing customer solution needs and driving success with their Azure deployments.</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities where we can do better for our customers (process, tools, technology) to increase FTA’s overall impact.</li>
<li>
Maintain strong working relationships with stakeholders across engineering, sales, services and partner teams.</li>
<li>
Evangelize and teach others to reach beyond existing solutions and technologies to incorporate new ideas.</li>
<li>
Work closely with FTA engineers in addressing customer solution needs and driving success with their Azure deployments.</li>
</ul>
