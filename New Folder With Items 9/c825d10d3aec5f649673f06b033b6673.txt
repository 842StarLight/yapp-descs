<h2>Heavy Equipment Operator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Clarendon Farms, LLC, a subsidiary of Cox Enterprises is hiring a Heavy Equipment Operator who will be responsible for operating heavy equipment in a safe and appropriate manner. Heavy equipment may include trucks, front-end loaders, dozers, graders, compactors, and other pieces of equipment. The Heavy Equipment Operator must also clean, maintain and secure all equipment.</div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h4>
<ul>
<li><div>Operate all heavy equipment in a safe and efficient.</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform daily safety and maintenance checks</div></li>
<li><div>
Clean heavy equipment as scheduled and/or required</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure heavy equipment is safely and securely stored</div></li>
<li><div>
Advise supervisor of any requirements for maintenance or repairs</div></li>
<li><div>
Participate in routine maintenance</div></li>
<li><div>
Practice workplace safety</div></li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<div></div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li><div>
High School diploma or GED and valid Driver’s License</div></li>
<li><div>
knowledge of truck and equipment safety</div></li>
<li><div>
knowledge of operation of front end loaders, dozers, graders, compactors, and other pieces of heavy equipment</div></li>
<li><div>
knowledge of equipment maintenance and storage</div></li>
<li><div>
Knowledge of road construction and maintenance techniques</div></li>
<li><div>
Knowledge of equipment cleaning standards and procedures</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to operate single axel trucks in a safe and responsible manner</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to operate required equipment in a safe and responsible manner</div></li>
<li><div>
Client service and public interaction skills</div></li>
<li><div>
Team building</div></li>
<li><div>
Analytical and problem-solving skills</div></li>
</ul>
