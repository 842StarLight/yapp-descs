<h2>Director of Product Management – Audience Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Roku is looking for a senior and seasoned product manager to lead the development of the next generation of ad products for channel partners to build audiences on the Roku platform.
<br><br>
As a leader in the Roku's advertising products team, you will be the key owner of developing native ad units and formats for the Roku OS. You will work with UX designers and engineers to ensure these ad units provide high RoI to the publishers that can be measured and validated easily. You will lead product development and go-to-market plans for these new products.
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive Roku's strategy of developing its next generation of ad formats and creative units</li>
<li>Understand the needs of channel partners that are looking to optimize media spend dynamically based on performance</li>
<li>Assess product opportunities and develop business cases for new products and features</li>
<li>Author PRDs, feature guides and release plans to enable the team to develop products on time and within budget</li>
<li>Drive feature prioritization, process improvements, and product development with an eye on key KPIs</li>
<li>Employ rapid prototype and other iterative methodologies to create, test and iterate product designs</li>
<li>Proactively provide customer and business context to engineering team</li>
<li>Build and maintain relationships with key partners in the advertising ecosystem</li>
<li>Manage joint product development and rollout with partners</li>
<li>Provide thought leadership and lead extended cross-functional team – including engineering, analytics, sales, marketing, operations, finance and legal – to deliver on product objectives</li>
<li>Stay on top of ad tech, media, and general technology trends; translate them into POVs, share with product and business teams, devise ways to assimilate trends internally</li>
</ul>
<b>Position Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of experience in product management with a focus on ad tech, media and enterprise software</li>
<li>Knowledge of the digital advertising landscape, specifically with ad servers, DSPs, DMPs and other media buying platforms</li>
<li>Familiarity with the IAB standards such as VAST/VMAP/VPAID and comfortable with basic scripting and query languages</li>
<li>Experience in working with digital marketers, data scientists and media sales</li>
<li>Data-driven and an entrepreneurial self-starter who deals well with ambiguity</li>
<li>Strong, influential cross-functional team leader</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work independently</li>
<li>Minimum of a bachelor's degree (preferably in engineering); MBA preferred</li>
<li>Based in Los Gatos with the ability and willingness to travel</li>
</ul>
