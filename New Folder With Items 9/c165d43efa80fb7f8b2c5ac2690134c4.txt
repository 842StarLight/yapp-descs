<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Project Manager position is a full-time role located in Morrisville, NC. The successful Project Manager will coordinate the efforts of multiple Implementation Specialists as they implement financial services compliance SaaS products for RegEd clients. Implementation Specialists are product experts who work collaboratively with new clients as they implement a specific software solution. Because clients often purchase more than one solution, multiple Implementation<br>
Specialists may be engaged with a client at once. The Project Manager works to bring a unified<br>
project management presentation to the client, monitoring and reporting on the various implementations as they move through the requirements gathering,<br>
configuration, testing, and training phases of their respective implementations.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The successful Project Manager candidate will have excellent communications skills, written and oral, in dealing with internal and external audiences. They<br>
will work with other RegEd departments to help clients in their implementation, including the software development and QA teams, relationship managers, and<br>
sales teams. They will oversee multiple projects at once. While they do not need to be experts in any particular product, they will develop a broad understanding of all RegEd applications.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions/Competencies</b></p>
<ul><li> Work with multiple Implementation Specialists, who are managing several client implementations at once, to gather status updates and update combined project</li></ul>
plans.
<ul><li> Develop strong working relationships with clients as they move through complex multiple-product implementations that can last for several months. Provide guidance to the client and escalate concern to management of the implementation team.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage project plans to meet implementation timeframes and budget directives</li></ul>
<ul><li> Create compelling messages around priorities to keep clients and team members focused on achieving implementation goals</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborate with other departments to solve problems</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage client meetings effectively and efficiently, engaging multiple stakeholders</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with implementation department leaders to evolve and improve implementation processes,</li></ul>
as appropriate
<p></p>
<p><i>Employee must perform all of the essential functions/responsibilities with or without accommodation for classification in this position.</i></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Requirements, Skills, Abilities, Education/Certification/License/Training/Travel</b></p>
<ul><li> Bachelor's Degree. Business or Finance preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li> 3+ years project management experience required; additional technical client support, software implementation or similar experience preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent project management skills and strong working knowledge of Microsoft Office products including Word, Excel, Outlook,Project, and Visio required.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong communication skills (internal and client audiences); consistent, positive attitude; professional responsive,results-oriented, effective problem solver</li></ul>
<ul><li> PMP certification desired</li></ul>
<p>Knowledge of the securities and insurance industries a plus</p>
<ul><li> Travel up to 15%</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>We are an Equal Opportunity Employer, Gender/Minority/Veterans/Disabled</i></p>
