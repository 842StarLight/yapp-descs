<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Title: Business Development Manager</b></h2>
<p>Internet Mobile Solutions, a company founded in the Houston, Texas area, is now seeking individuals who have B2B sales and marketing experience, combined with the talent or track record of building a sales force.
</p>
<p>The proper candidate will have extensive knowledge and experience communicating the following topics; domain names, logo design, website design, Google Plus, mobile site design, Local SEO, SEO, PPC, text messaging , mobile advertising, organic growth and Google analytics.
</p>
<p>The proper candidate will have an understanding of and comfort with communicating by phone, email and in person with individuals of all economies. Candidates should also be proficient with the use of Excel, Word, Power Point, capable of compiling daily, weekly and monthly reports. Bi-lingual or multi-lingual individuals will be properly recognized.<br>
Military experience is a plus. A degree is not mandatory but will be considered.
</p>
<p>The proper candidate will be able to recruit, interview, train, inspire, motivate, hold accountable, develop and manage others in representing the Internet Mobile Solutions brand.
</p>
<p>Internet Mobile Solutions is seeking leaders in every state and major market area. We will provide leads, recruiting assistance, marketing materials, bookkeeping, payroll and services delivered.
</p>
<p>Internet Mobile Solutions offers compensations in the form of commissions, unlimited bonuses, overrides, and ownership. All promotions are equally offered based on productivity and growth.
</p>
<p>Interested qualified candidates must send a resume and a typed cover letter explaining why you feel you are the right individual to build and lead your market area.</p>
