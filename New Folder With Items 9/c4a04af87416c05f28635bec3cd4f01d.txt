Job Description
<br><br>
We are looking for an experienced Sr. Account Manager to join our Customer Success team. This position plays a critical role in ensuring the delivery of Origami Logic's platform is customized to meet the unique needs of each of our customer's organizational structure, challenges and stakeholder requirements. While applying your relationship, technical and project management skills, you will serve as the primary customer contact seeing their projects and other initiatives from infancy through to completion, ensuring successful definition, build, rollout and adoption of our platform for marketers supporting major global brands. This role will be assigned as a dedicated resource to a high-profile, top-tier customer account. Top notch consultative, professional presence and communication skills are critical.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Function as the critical link between our customer and internal resources to manage, track and execute all aspects of current implementations and the ongoing evolution of their marketing measurement program</li>
</ul>
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

<ul>

</ul>
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
