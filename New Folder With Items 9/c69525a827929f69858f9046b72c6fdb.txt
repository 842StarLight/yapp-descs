<h2>Communication Specialist (Contract)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<ul><li>
<b><i>This is a </i></b><b><i>12 week </i></b><b><i>contract position.</i></b>
</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As a Communications Specialist you will be responsible for planning, coordinating and executing marketing communication deliverables and activities in support of corporate goals, employee and customer engagement and the company's marketing programs for software products and services, as directed by department head and/or group managers.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
You will:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Create communications that advance the company’s business objectives – including increasing brand awareness and employee engagement, supporting sales lead generation and increasing customer retention and loyalty.</li>
<li>
Advise SAS divisions and global staff (as relevant) on how to meet specific objectives via appropriate communications tools and channels.</li>
<li>
Create internal and external communications, including brochures, announcements, ads, direct mail, newsletters, scripts, digital communications (video, audio, social media, web content, etc.) and other communication materials. Integrate both new and traditional media approaches to stay current with communication trends and target audiences. May also create or direct the creation of visual communications such as info graphics, photographs and videos.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintains relationships and engagement with relevant constituents, which may include employees, partners and community members in order to deliver and publish key company messages and engage in dialogue.</li>
<li>
Publish to, and works in, a number of content, communications or digital platforms including content management systems, blogging platforms, social media management platforms, etc.</li>
<li>
Stay abreast of industry trends, developments, and current technologies and shares information with management and staff.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What we’re looking for</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Bachelor's Degree in English, Journalism, Business, Advertising, Marketing, Communications, or related field. Course work in feature and/or news writing, advertising, copywriting, or related field preferred.</div></li>
<li>
<div>
5+ years of relevant experience. Experience with one or more of the following preferred:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Writing copy in a corporate environment or in an independent business-to- business, high-tech, or consumer-product advertising agency.</div></li>
<li><div>
Writing copy for marketing campaigns and brand promotions, including advertising (print and online), direct marketing or online channels.</div></li>
<li><div>
Script writing and event coordination experience.</div></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Knowledge, skills, and abilities required:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Knowledge of computer/software industry and marketing preferred.</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated ability to develop communications strategy to achieve corporate goals.</div></li>
<li><div>
Excellent written, verbal, and visual communications skills, with the ability to communicate effectively with a wide variety of audiences.</div></li>
<li><div>
Proficiency in marketing communication writing and editing. Strong organizational and creative problem-solving skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Familiarity with social media communications channels; ability to integrate these channels into plans &amp; strategies to achieve stated goals.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to manage multiple concurrent projects and meet deadlines.</div></li>
</ul>
