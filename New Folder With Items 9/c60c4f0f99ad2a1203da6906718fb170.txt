<h2>Production Maintenance Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Tennant Company is a recognized leader of the cleaning industry. Since 1870 we have been passionate about developing innovative and sustainable solutions that help our customers clean more spaces more effectively, addressing indoor and outdoor cleaning challenges. Our products include equipment for maintaining surfaces in industrial, commercial and outdoor environments; detergent-free and other sustainable cleaning technologies; cleaning tools and supplies; and coatings for protecting, repairing and upgrading surfaces. With more than $1B in revenue, Tennant Company operates in three geographic business units including the Americas, Europe, Middle East and Africa and Asia Pacific. Join the company committed to empowering our customers to create a cleaner, safer and healthier world with high-performance cleaning solutions that minimize waste, reduce costs, improve safety and further sustainability goals.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Tennant Company is currently seeking a Production Maintenance Supervisor for our Minneapolis Manufacturing facility. In this role you will:
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive Continuous Improvements in the Overall Equipment Effectivness (OEE) of production assets. Support the Manufacturing Services Value Stream’s mission to be the center of operational excellence delivering the highest level of customer satisfaction by focusing on continuous improvement activities and root cause problem solving.
</li>
<li>Manage and develop the Plant-1 Production Maintenance Team across 3-shifts of operation.
</li>
<li>Have responsibility (managed directly or subcontracted) for production or production support equipment projects including, but not limited to, new equipment deployments, retrofits, rebuilds, and decommissions. Support for electric infrastructure (power up to the asset) to be provided by the Facilities Maintenance Team.
</li>
<li>Work with Plant-1 Value Stream Managers and the Director of Operations to develop a 5-year Production Equipment Roadmap based on capacity needs, technology trends, and equipment sunset schedules.
</li>
<li>Work with Facilities Maintenance Supervisor to balance resources against demands, always wearing the ‘Tennant’ Hat; making the right trade-off decisions for Tennant.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>GENERAL RESPONISIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>PERSONNEL:
<ul>
<li>Provide leadership, coaching, and development of employees to meet safety, quality, cost and engagement goals.
</li>
<li>Develop and provide metrics related to budget and resource effectiveness for the production maintenance team.
</li>
<li>Supervise team with a commitment to their development through performance management, individualized development plans/opportunities, and visibility.
</li>
<li>Develop and deliver comprehensive maintenance and reliability tactical training to campus maintenance resources
</li>
<li>Regularly respond with advice to maintenance/equipment related questions, ensure access to up-to-date maintenance/operating procedures, and facilitate strong team communications activities.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>STRATEGY:
<ul>
<li>Develop a long term vision for transitioning maintenance to a Predictive Maintenance organization.
</li>
<li>Develop and implement strategies that accelerate and improve current maintenance practices and processes to improve Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE).
</li>
<li>Partner with worldwide operations group to coordinate and align maintenance activities in support of operational excellence. Provide leadership in a Lean Manufacturing environment; consistently driving out waste.
</li>
<li>Initiate, implement, and manage the plant maintenance program based on best practices in the industrial equipment manufacturing industry, with an emphasis on planning/scheduling, preventive/predictive maintenance, and safety, health &amp; environmental policies and procedures.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>CULTURE:
<ul>
<li>Deliver a Culture of Pride in Workmanship, Accountability for Performance, and Expectation for High Performance through the Production Maintenance Team; seeking opportunities to frequently illustrate what ‘Good’ looks like.
</li>
<li>Act as a Lean champion within the maintenance department by implementing Lean concepts that reduce or eliminate waste.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>PROCESS:
<ul>
<li>Identify required equipment and process upgrades and effectively manage associated projects
</li>
<li>Establish, maintain, and leverage value from a computerized maintenance management system (CMMS) for tracking work orders, planned/predictive maintenance, spare parts inventory/sharing, MRO management, and maintenance history of plant equipment.
</li>
<li>Analyze operational data and equipment performance history to deliver improvements in critical maintenance related metrics including: unplanned downtime, PM compliance, schedule compliance, Mean-Time-Between-Failures, and maintenance related costs for production assets.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIRED EXPERIENCE &amp; EDUCATION:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from an ABET accredited institution required in Electrical, Mechanical, or Maintenance Engineering degree.
</li>
<li>2+ years supervisory experience required.
</li>
<li>5+ years of experience of manufacturing maintenance
</li>
<li>SAP, CAD, preventative maintenance, energy management software experience is preferred.
</li>
<li>Experience with TPM, predictive and preventative maintenance, 5S, Standard Work concepts and tools.
</li>
<li>Background with CNC machine operating principles and maintenance requirements strongly desired.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIRED INTERPERSONAL &amp; BEHAVIORAL SKILLS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Effective communication skills with staff, peers, internal customers and vendors.
</li>
<li>Ability to problem-solve.
</li>
<li>Ability to concentrate on tasks, make decisions and work calmly and effectively in a high-pressure, deadline oriented environment.
</li>
<li>Ability to use sound judgment and discretion in dealing with highly confidential information.
</li>
<li>Ability to take direction and accept supervision.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work independently, organize and accurately prioritize work, be detail-oriented, understand when urgency is required and use good judgment in varied situations.
</li>
<li>Coach and mentor direct reports with a floor focused approach. Go to Gemba.
</li>
<li>Project management skills that keep continuous improvement on schedule and within scope.
</li>
<li>Clear understanding of problem solving processes and methods including A3.
</li>
<li>Ability to translate customer needs into operational practices.
</li>
<li>Know when to escalate issues that require additional technical or leadership support.
</li>
<li>Ability to influence direct reports, customers of maintenance and others at corporate level.
</li>
</ul>
