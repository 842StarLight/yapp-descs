<p><b>DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>Division Overview:</p>
<p>The mission of CBSi’s Integrated Marketing team is to drive revenue by translating and shaping assets into innovative, impactful experiences for brands and consumers. With revenue as our #1 focus, we balance business requirements and user experience, understanding both advertiser needs and consumer behaviors.</p>
<p>Consultative selling is at the core of what we do, and our focus is always on our client’s KPIs. We use data and insight-led inspirational storytelling, CBSi’s industry-leading technology and premium, captivating content, and world class design and production capabilities to develop solutions that drive results.</p>
<p>Role Details:</p>
<p>The Entertainment Site Strategy team is seeking a Coordinator to join our growing team. Site Strategy is a part of the larger CBS Interactive Integrated Marketing team that oversees advertising solutions for the CBS portfolio of premium brands.</p>
<p>The Entertainment Site Strategy team is responsible for developing and advancing sponsorship strategy for CBS Digital Entertainment brands (CBS, CBS All Access, Entertainment Tonight, ET Live, Inside Edition, Rachael Ray, Dr. Phil and The Doctors), empowering and educating Integrated Marketing and Sales on Entertainment solutions for brands, and innovating and improving our digital offerings in response to evolving client needs. The Site Strategy team manages the sales process from ideation to execution.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate is highly organized, solutions-oriented, and thrives in this fast-paced and quickly evolving marketplace. This role will support and specialize in pre-sales, helping to manage RFP responses, content ideation and general presentation materials across multiple brands and dayparts.</p>
<p>Your Day-to-Day:</p>
<ul>
<li>Drafting captivating and precise write-ups of sponsorship opportunities and associated online program components.</li>
<li>Creating compelling visual marketing materials, such as 1-sheeters and decks, leveraging Microsoft PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Crafting media plans that accurately capture all components of advertising programs and associated inventory projections/guarantees.</li>
<li>Participation in internal brainstorming sessions with appropriate CBS Interactive teams.</li>
<li>Vetting viability of content ideas and/or ad product capabilities with pertinent CBS Interactive teams, and properly assessing implications as it relates to client proposals.</li>
<li>Coordination with CBS Interactive and/or CBS Network teams on proposal development and deliverables.</li>
<li>Providing accurate status updates tied to progression of projects to key internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>Maintaining pre-sales tracker</li>
<li>Collaboration with product, content strategy and sales teams to respond to RFPs, client meetings and pitches.</li>
</ul>
