<br>
<div>As the <b>Marketing Manager,</b> you will play a key position in helping meet the strategic objectives for the Smart Home business within CenterPoint Energy. This Indianapolis-based position will manage the marketing efforts for a new subscription-based business in the smart home space. You will be responsible for subscriber adoption, retention and all marketing aspects for the smart home business.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Specifically, you’ll have the opportunity to:</div>
<ul>
<li>Be responsible for meeting subscriber adoption and retention goals while optimizing marketing spend and ROI.</li>
<li>
Develop marketing strategy, acquisition and retention plans and campaigns to launch new products and services; growing customer counts and profitability in each market</li>
<li>
Create plans and activities to open new markets for products and services</li>
<li>
Develop and nurture key relationships that enhance service to the customer and drive departmental goals</li>
<li>
Develop and execute rich content strategy that attracts a qualified audience to increase traffic, generate leads, and drive engagement</li>
<li>
Coordinate work efforts of others to ensure integration and completion of work against expectations</li>
<li>
Execute market and competitive analysis and research</li>
<li>
Create and implement a segmentation strategy to grow existing customer engagement and target prospects</li>
<li>
Demonstrates effective team-leadership skills to influence others to achieve organizational results and collaborate across functional boundries , possesses strong interpersonal skills to develop rapport, relationships and consensus.</li>
<li>
Manage the B2C marketing strategy and support business development efforts to acquire and maintain channel partners</li>
<li>
Develop process to provide insights into customer and campaign performance
</li>
<li>Support the development of annual budgets and plan</li>
<li>
Analyze, assess and report acquisition, retention, revenue, cost, and margin performance for markets and campaigns and describe variance from plans and forecasts</li>
<li>
Drive strategies around the use of emerging technologies, digital media, etc. to reach new segments of customers and build relationships with existing customers</li>
</ul>
