<h2>Cox Family Office Senior Accounting Analyst, Reporting & Analysis</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Cox Enterprises, Inc. is a highly successful communications, media and automotive enterprise founded approximately 120 years ago. This Atlanta based business and its well-established Family Office is 100% privately owned and operated by descendants of the founding family member. The primary mission of the Family Office is to serve the financial, accounting, tax, investment, legal and fiduciary/administrative interests of this multigenerational family, spanning generations two to five. The Family Office manages 14 individual households which are geographically dispersed throughout the United States, as well as internationally, and includes their related entities, trusts, businesses and foundations.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>This position supports the Family Office Financial Services function in a variety of accounting, financial reporting &amp; analysis responsibilities.</div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Maintain complete and accurate financial statements; prepare standard reporting packages.</li>
<li>
Support the monthly financial close process by processing the necessary journal entries, reconciliations and associated analysis for assigned entities and areas of responsibility.
</li>
<li>Interprets, analyzes and applies complex concepts in arriving at accounting results and projections.</li>
<li>
Provide excellent service to other Family Office professionals to help drive business decisions including reporting on actuals of financials and variances to forecast explanations.</li>
<li>
Provide subject matter expertise within our area of focus; resolve questions and/or problems.</li>
<li>
Monitor and optimize business processes by reviewing operational and financial processes to ensure compliance and effectiveness, identify anomalies and make recommendations.
</li>
<li>Utilize technology and reporting tools to effectively complete job duties and provide insights to Family Office function.</li>
<li>
Prepare ad hoc analyses, complete projects and perform other duties as assigned by management.</li>
<li>
Assist with accounting policy &amp; procedure updates, development and enforcement.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<br>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>
A bachelor’s degree in accounting and / or finance required.
</li>
<li>Three to five years of experience preferred in related field.
</li>
<li>Highly detail-oriented with ability to analyze financial data and prepare financial reports.
</li>
<li>Hardworking, approachable, trustworthy and dependable individual, with exceptional interpersonal skills. The individual should want to be part of a “team focused” organization.</li>
<li>
Strong ability to multi-task and work in fast-paced and dynamic environment.
</li>
<li>Excellent computer and software knowledge essential, including Microsoft Office suite (i.e. Excel, Word, PowerPoint, SharePoint, and Outlook) &amp; Accounting systems.</li>
<li>
Knowledge and experience with financial reporting tools and process automation tools preferred.<br>
</li>
</ul>
