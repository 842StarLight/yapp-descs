<p>
Oversees pre-sale and post-sale support of company product/systems/solutions used for biometric identification services. Provide technical support in sales and product demonstrations. Provides answers to customer inquiries. Implements complex system requirements for customers and/or prepares studies and analyzes existing systems. Determines system specifications, input/output processes and working parameters for hardware and software compatibility. Coordinates installation of subsystems and integration of total system. Provides technical support during preparation, installation and maintenance of the solution. Identifies, analyzes and resolves program support deficiencies. Develops and recommends corrective actions.</p>
