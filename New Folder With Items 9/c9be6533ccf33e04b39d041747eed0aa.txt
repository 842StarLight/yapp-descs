<h2>Account Manager Associate - K12</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>An Account Manager Associate provides operational and customer facing support to a qualified Account Manager or team of Account Managers. This position reports directly to a Sales Manager.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Key Areas of Responsibility</b></div>
<div>
The Account Manager Associate will be responsible for duties such as the following:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Drive client business with a focus on Core product offerings</div></li>
<li>
<div>Manage renewal business for software and support agreements</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Strengthen manufacturer relationships thru pipeline, monthly cadence, outreach</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Uncover ways to make existing business more profitable (bids, warranties, etc.)</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Enter quotes and orders as needed</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Manage purchase/procurement, preorders, bids, order release, etc.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Drive Ecommerce efficiencies with all clients</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Run extranet walk-throughs with new contacts and existing contacts where necessary</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Interact directly with customers to provide quotes, order status updates, or other requests</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Schedule/coordinate hardware staging/deployment with services</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Manage customer contract pricing (VIR and Cost Plus) and periodic reporting requirements</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Prospect new contacts on accounts
</div>
</li>
<li><div>Special projects upon request</div></li>
</ul>
