<div></div>
<div><b>Corporate Functions – Marketing &amp; Communications</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>As part of this team, you’ll play a key role in helping Accenture execute its strategy. You’ll work with a diverse team of innovative marketing experts who focus on the following areas: global brand management and advertising; global events and sponsorships; internal communications; marketing research; media and industry analyst relations; corporate and financial communications; internet marketing; recruitment marketing; client-centric marketing; and industry, service line and geographic marketing campaigns.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>YOUR ROLE</b>: <b>H&amp;PS Digital Marketing Associate Manager</b>
</div>
<div>The role will support the North America Business Development &amp; Sales (BD/Sales) team in execution of their CCM (Client Centric Marketing) and Sales campaigns for the Health &amp; Public Service industry. They will collaborate with the BD/Sales marketing team and work to ensure campaigns, and associated components are delivered on time and to spec. They will need to manage multiple projects across Health, Public Service and Accenture Federal Services for execution so they must be detailed orientated, have superior time management and excellent communication skills. Focus will primarily be on landing pages, email, social and metrics.
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Strategic conversations and relationship building with Sales marketing lead
</li>
<li>Communicate regularly and proactively with agencies and marketing about timelines
</li>
<li>Manage the development, review and publishing of new content on Accenture.com including detailed QA of page submission documents, submission of content, QA of preview pages, and trafficking of pages to appropriate marketing managers for final review and approval.
</li>
<li>Manage CCM/Sales A.com publishing pipeline, ensuring the highest priority pages are addressed in a timely manner with Site development team.
</li>
<li>Manage the development, testing, and execution of email campaigns including detailed QA of email submission documents, submission of content to email execution agency for testing, QA of test, and scheduling send date according to plan.
</li>
<li>Manage email vendor and email team relationships.
</li>
<li>Manage activation and set up of analytics tracking as needed.
</li>
<li>Manage the submission of videos to corporate YouTube channel
</li>
<li>Manage the submission of SlideShare assets to corporate SlideShare channel.</li>
</ul>
