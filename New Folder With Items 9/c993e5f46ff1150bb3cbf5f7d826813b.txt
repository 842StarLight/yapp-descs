<h2>Internal Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The Internal Account Manager is accountable for telephonic inbound/outbound sales, with the objective of generating revenue and/or profit margin normally in small to mid-size accounts.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct assessments of customer needs within assigned region or area.</li>
<li>
Establish customer pricing within published guideline.</li>
<li>
Participate in territory planning, customer planning and territory reviews. Prepare monthly pipeline and forecast reports by product line.</li>
<li>
Effectively manage assigned territory or product line.</li>
<li>
Maintain customer profiles and supply agreements.</li>
<li>
Manage customer pricing requests.</li>
<li>
Create and manage territory and account strategies to deliver on price and mix objectives.</li>
<li>
Provide product recommendations, samples, technical support, pricing and service information on demand.</li>
<li>
Provide product quotes and actively utilize value-selling tools and skills.</li>
<li>
Uncover and assess customer needs and develop and execute ROI.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate products and/or services and provide assistance in the best application of product and/or services.</li>
<li>
Proactively identify new accounts using screening techniques, referrals, and prospecting to potential customers within territory.</li>
<li>
Identify any customer concerns, investigate and resolve problems by recommending Avery Dennison solution sets.</li>
</ul>
