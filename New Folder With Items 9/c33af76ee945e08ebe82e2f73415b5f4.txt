<p>Responsible for managing multiple divisional, regional, company, and cross-operating company projects directly related to Cross Border revenue capture. Identifies strategic enterprise account / marketplace targets, develops customized opportunity reporting, and provides consultative angle to Sales. Manages and supports relationships as necessary. Coordinates and/or leads customer implementations when needed.</p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties/Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide day-to-day support to key enterprise accounts</li>
<li>
Identify key customer needs and bundle / position FedEx Cross Border enterprise solutions accordingly to support Sales; provide solutions architecture consultancy</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain eCommerce industry subject matter expertise</li>
<li>
Provide insights to customers on new industry developments and relate to Cross Border value drivers</li>
<li>
Support and lead key Cross Border Strategic Projects that directly support revenue generation</li>
<li>
Provide guidance to Marketing team on ecommerce customer needs and general industry subject matter expertise</li>
<li>
Provide insights to Marketing team on Cross Border Sales team support tools (Solution Point content, Collateral, etc.)</li>
<li>
Responsible for identifying and executing opportunities to generate revenue</li>
<li>
Generates reports, dashboards, and other measurement tools</li>
<li>
Complete account onboarding and implementation as necessary</li>
</ul>
<b>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities Required</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree/equivalent in related field; Master’s preferred</li>
<li>
At least five (5) years' experience working in an E-Commerce environment with knowledge of the e-Commerce industry including broad experience and understanding of modern e-Commerce websites</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to build and maintain strong relationships with customers</li>
<li>
Strong sense of ownership and ability to work independently or with limited supervision</li>
<li>
Demonstrated proficiency in quality management principles and applications</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to work well under pressure</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple projects concurrently with multiple phase deadlines</li>
<li>
Excellent time management, project management and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience in translating customer needs into solution requirements for implementation via a Sales, Marketing, or related support role providing direct customer interface</li>
<li>
Demonstrated proficiency and experience in managing customer relationships and enterprise business agreements</li>
<li>
Demonstrated proficiency in multi-regional, multi-organization enterprise level project management</li>
</ul>
