 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>Job Description
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Gather data about consumers, competitors, and market conditions including use of Market Databases, public and paid-for information as well as devising and evaluating methods for collecting data, such as surveys, questionnaires, and customer interviews</li>
<li>Collect and analyse market information to understand the market size, environment, needs, trends, and opportunities for the product line. Work with sales to collect direct customer feedback and with global marketing team on overall market analysis</li>
<li>Collect and analyse metrics related to sales volumes, revenues, and margins, pricing, market share, etc. to measure success of the product line strategy and report out results to marketing team</li>
<li>Monitor competitive behaviour and product performance in order to anticipate competitive response. Collaborate with Global Marketing Managers and Regional Marketing Managers to develop strategies to differentiate our products and services and defend our position</li>
<li>Support the global marketing team to develop a long-term strategic plan and where this product line aligns within the plan</li>
<li>Support data gathering and preparation of data for investor presentations and supporting Marketing Communication needs</li>
<li>5% travel as required</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div>Required Skills
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Work productively both independently and in a team environment</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively at all levels</li>
<li>Computer comfortable including Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Outlook, database software</li>
<li>Fluent in English and other local languages as required</li>
<li>Strong organizational, analytical and rationalization skills for analysing complex data</li>
<li>Ability to handle sensitive and extensive confidential data</li>
<li>Oral / Verbal presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to create reports and presentations for presentation</li>
</ul>
