<p><b>
Department Description:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The North America Consulting IaaS Delivery Practice provides architecture and implementation specialists that deliver professional services that align with and support the North America Sales organization and Oracle customer base.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The IaaS team consists of highly skilled consultants with deep technical knowledge that provide expert architecture guidance and implementation assistance with Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) services and Oracle infrastructure technology.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The growing demand for Oracle industry leading Cloud solutions has fueled significant growth in the need for Cloud services. This role will support the overall strategic needs of the organization as defined by the OCS leadership team.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Some of the responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li> Accelerate IaaS &amp; PaaS adoption and usage by customers and partners.</li>
<li> Assist Cloud project teams in deploying Oracle Cloud Infrastructure</li>
<li> Develop reusable Intellectual Property (IP) for the Consulting Delivery organization to help drive delivery efficiency and customer value.</li>
<li> Provide feedback to IaaS &amp; PaaS Product Engineering teams regarding product deficiencies and enhancements</li>
<li> Automation of environment creation, testing, and software deployments</li>
<li> Migration of customer environments to Oracle Public Cloud</li>
<li> Administrating software and tools in accordance with the client’s requirements</li>
<li> Installing, configuring and administration of operating systems and hypervisors</li>
<li> Work with PaaS and SaaS SME’s to deliver full cloud solutions for customers</li>
<li> Server builds and automation through scripting</li>
<li> System build configuration, tuning, testing and validation</li>
<li> Configuration of secure network connections via ssh</li>
<li> Creation and modification of VM templates and orchestrations</li>
<li> Server and OS tuning and testing for performance and scalability</li>
<li> Technical Troubleshooting and problem solving</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
ACCOUNTABILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li> Be a trusted technical advisor to customers and solve complex Cloud Infrastructure and Networking challenges.</li>
<li> Travel if needed for meetings, technical reviews, and onsite delivery activities</li>
<li> Proactively maintain knowledge of Oracle’s current and future IaaS products/ solution offerings</li>
<li> Generate relevant documentation, runbooks, and knowledge sharing content</li>
<li> Strong contributor as part of a technical implementation team</li>
<li> Experience managing internal or client-facing projects to completion; experience troubleshooting clients' technical issues; experience working with engineering teams, sales, services, and customers.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Job Description:</b></p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Junior consulting position for those who are continuing to develop their expertise. Receives general instruction on routine work and detailed instruction on new projects or assignments.<br>
<br>
As a member of a project team, follows standard practices and procedures to analyze situations/data and provide quality work products to deliver functional and technical solutions on applications and technology installations. Work involves some problem solving with assistance and guidance in understanding and applying relevant Oracle methodologies and practices. Implements Oracle products and technology in various industries to meet customer specifications.<br>
<br>
1-3 years of overall experience in relevant functional or technical roles. Undergraduate degree or equivalent experience. Product or technical expertise relevant to practice focus. Demonstrates competence in a minimum of one product or technology area. Ability to communicate effectively and build rapport with team members and clients. Ability to travel as needed.</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Desired Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li> 1-3 years of experience relevant to this position in any combination of engineering, and consulting in cloud computing, with increasing responsibilities, and with a special focus on infrastructure design and automation from the networking layer up. Undergraduate degree or equivalent experience. Product, technology or industry expertise relevant to the practice focus.</li>
<li> Hands on skills with Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) or solutions from other providers, such as Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure etc. would be of advantage, especially with PaaS and IaaS.</li>
<li> Knowledge of Cloud deployment automation tools such as Terraform, Ansible, Chef</li>
<li> Knowledge of networking that includes:</li>
</ul>

<p>
o Understanding of VPN solutions, MPLS, and enterprise network equipment like routers, firewalls, and switches.</p>

<li> Good understanding of IaaS Compute and operating systems, and compute migration methodologies and implementation techniques.</li>
<li> Knowledge and understanding of DevOps and cloud computing with experience in any of the following technologies:</li>
</ul>
<p>
o GIT, Chef / Puppet, Ansible, Docker, Kubernetes, and Mezos.</p>
<p>
o Experience with scripting languages, such as Python and Bash, JSON</p>
<ul>

<li> Working knowledge of Active Directory</li>
<li> Networking and Virtualization experience With Oracle VM, VMWare, KVM, Hyper-V</li>
<li> Database knowledge a plus (RAC, Data Guard, Golden Gate)</li>
<li> Analysis of cloud strategies, design and development of tools for Oracle products deployment in OCI.</li>
<li> B.Tech /M.Tech in Computer Science or Engineering or equivalent work experience</li>
<li> Legally entitled to work in the US</li>
<li> Ability to travel throughout the US and Canada, Mon – Thurs or as needed</li>
</ul>
