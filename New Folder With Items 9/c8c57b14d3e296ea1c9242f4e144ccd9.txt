At Bank of Hawaii, we’re passionate about providing our customers with exceptional financial service. We’ve made this happen for more than 120 years, and we’re looking for individuals who will contribute to that ongoing legacy.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Enter the <b>Relationship Service Representative</b>, whose purpose is learning about customers’ financial needs so that we can deliver an exceptional customer experience and develop meaningful customer relationships. From conducting routine teller transactions to educating customers on our new digital banking services, a day in the life of a Relationship Service Representative means being passionate about helping people. If all of this sparks your interest, keep reading.</div>
