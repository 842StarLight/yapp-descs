<h2>Sr. IT Security Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Position Summary:</b>
<br>
<br>
Penske Truck Leasing has an exciting opportunity to join our IT team! You will get to work with some of the latest technologies to deliver innovative solutions to our customers. We have opportunities available in Reading, Pennsylvania at our Corporate office or in out new IT Development and Collaboration Center in Tampa, Florida.
<br>
<br>
The Sr. IT Security Analyst’s will have the exciting opportunity to work within a Global Cyber Security department.
<br>
<br>
The main responsibility of the Sr. IT Security Analyst is to maintain a secure environment for the Company’s network, data, on premise and cloud based systems.
<br>
<br>
This role will also lead project scope development and options analysis and identify and recommend solutions to meet security needs, and implement chosen solution.
<br>
<br>
This role is involved in recommending security products and analyzing their performance. In addition, it is responsible for analyzing and reviewing annual SOX and other compliance reports.
