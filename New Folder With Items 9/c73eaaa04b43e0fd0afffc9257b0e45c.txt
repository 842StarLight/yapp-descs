<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilties</b></h2>
<b>
Job Description</b><br>
<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>
<p>
Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p><b>
Wells Fargo Technology</b> sets IT strategy; enhances the design, development, and operations of our systems; optimizes the Wells Fargo infrastructure footprint; provides information security; and enables continuous banking access through in-store, online, ATM, and other channels to Wells Fargo’s more than 70 million global customers.</p>
<p>
The Fixed-Income Production Support – Systems Support Analyst will provide support and maintenance of core fixed-income electronic trading and sales applications in production. The position is responsible for the triage and resolution of complex system and/or application issues within a controlled change-management environment. The candidate will provide on-call support for production applications and work closely with other team members, proprietary and vendor development staff and various other teams to resolve issues. The position requires analytical skills and the ability to provide solutions to issues. The candidate must be able to work within a high-paced environment and manage multiple priorities.</p>
<p>
The opportunity is to join our Charlotte, NC Wells Fargo team, with focus on covering the support of applications used within the Fixed Income front and middle office suite of applications, in addition to associated development projects both locally and globally. The team owns the holistic support of the front office technology platform globally including tactical enhancements, application upgrades, new application rollouts, maintenance and performance tuning.</p>
<p>
The team provides a depth of technology services to the organization whose strategy leverages both proprietary built systems that complement customized vendor applications. The team is in transition into building a truly global follow the sun model, in line with the business changes.</p>
<p>
Key Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Application Support of the front office e-trading platforms, including interfacing with front-office users daily</li>
<li>Troubleshooting business issues, solving problems to provide solutions to diverse infrastructure teams and the product aligned trading desks</li>
<li>Ownership and accountability for the operation, monitoring and integrity of the technology platform</li>
<li>Escalation support for the members of the team located in other regions including evenings and weekends on a rotational basis</li>
<li>Ownership and participation in the Problem and Incident management processes, being accountable for resolution, root cause analysis and technology and business reporting and communication.</li>
<li>Providing oversight for the stability of the production environment within the change management process ensuring policies are enforced and adhered to</li>
<li>Relationship management internally with the trading desks, operations teams, and other infrastructure partners</li>
<li>Navigate the wider technology organization; build relationships to manage the team's agenda with our critical technology partners</li>
<li>Interface with external vendors and their technical support and development organizations ensuring focus to resolution of issues and strategic enhancements</li>
<li>Proactively identify improvements and address them through independent thinking within a consensus driven team</li>
</ul>
