Job Description</div>
<div>
<p>
An Associate Field Engineer (AFE) will work on computer equipment in the Point of Sale (POS) industry. An AFE must have strong computer repair hardware and software skills. A technical degree, technical certifications (A+, Dell, HP, MCSE, etc…) or equivalent field experience is required. Equipment serviced includes items such as Cash Registers, Printers, Scanners, Self Checkout Machines, EFT Machines, and Servers. The manufacturer is mainly Fujitsu, but we also service many other makes and models such as HP, Dell, Toshiba, Cisco, NCR, etc… Our customers include Ralphs, Staples, Ross, TJMaxx, Marshalls, Payless, Footlocker and many other retail accounts.</p>
<p>
This position is a long term position with no expected end date. It is a Retail market and requires availability to work 7×24. Most work is done between 8 am and 10 pm, but there are installs and projects that occasionally require overnight work. The AFE will be scheduled to work 32+ hours/wk (varying days). AFEs will be required to drive throughout a large territory to cover service calls as they are needed. Overtime will be paid in accordance with state laws.</p>
<p>
Fujitsu will provide a laptop, cell phone, tools, and parts. AFEs will be required to have a dependable source of transportation (Car or truck) and valid driver license. Use of a personal vehicle will be reimbursed based on mileage. Current rate is $0.54/mile and varies. If/when possible, a Fujitsu vehicle will be provided and the AFE will need to provide safe parking and care of the vehicle.</p>
<p>
All AFEs will be required to go through a background check and drug screen. You must be able to legally work in the United States and have a valid driver license.</p>
