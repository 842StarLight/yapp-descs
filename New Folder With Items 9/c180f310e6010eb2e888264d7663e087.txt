<h2>Transition Stabilization Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div>The purpose of this position is to manage and triage the transition process for new clients from the Implementation phase to the Service Team. This position will work closely with the Client, Account Manager, Implementation Team, Benefits Team and the Service Team</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities / Duties:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Establish a relationship with the key client contacts and setup recurring meetings to effectively manage the transition process from Implementation to the Service Team</li>
<li>
Bring subject matter experts from the organization into discussions as needed to address specific issues / projects.
</li>
<li>Manage multiple clients in a high pace environment
</li>
<li>Manage the flow/timeline of the transition phase from Implementation to the Service Team while adhering to the established deadlines and escalate when necessary</li>
<li>
Facilitate discussions on open items between the customer and item owner and ensure final actions are taken before transitioning the customer</li>
<li>
Provide training on the Benefits Administration system to the client’s key Administrative Staff</li>
<li>
QC configuration of data prior to first payroll processing</li>
<li>
Identify and create/update client specific “Best Practices” for use by clients and service team, including processing checklists and additional auditing tools</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities to improve the implementation process</li>
<li>
Assist implementation team during peak periods on system configuration and client set-up</li>
<li>
Provide reporting updates to internal teams or management</li>
<li>
Lead employer group meetings to review functionality issues or participate in roll out discussions</li>
<li>
Manage the handoff process from Impl. to Service/Account Management to ensure employer group satisfaction and continuity</li>
<li>
Retain accountability for successful implementation projects</li>
<li>
Contribute to and/or maintain Standard Operating Process Documents (SOPS) and other processes performed</li>
<li>
Complete job-related training courses and/or seminars, as may be required for the job and/or assigned by Manager.</li>
<li>
All other duties as deemed necessary by management</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
