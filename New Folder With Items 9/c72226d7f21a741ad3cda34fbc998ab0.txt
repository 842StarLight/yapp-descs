<div>Housing Assistance</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Based on location of assignment, Johnson &amp; Johnson may offer housing assistance to co-op’s who live more than 50 miles from their work-site location. This housing assistance includes options for subsidized rent in a fully-furnished apartment close to the work-site location, or a stipend payment for candidates to use at their discretion. Daily transportation to and from the work-site location is the student’s responsibility, as most locations are not accessible via public transportation and company transportation is not provided.<br>
</div>

<b>Qualifications</b><br>
<div>A qualified candidate should meet the following criteria:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Candidates must be legally authorized to work in the U.S. and must not require sponsorship for employment visa status now or in the future (e.g. H1-B status)</li>
<li>
A minimum GPA of 3.0 Cumulative is strongly preferred
</li>
<li>Candidate must be actively enrolled pursuing a Bachelor or Master's degree ( but not necessarily taking classes)</li>
<li>
Basic proficiency with the Microsoft Office (Word, Excel and PowerPoint) is required</li>
<li>
Able to demonstrate strong analytical and quantitative skills, excellent communication and presentation skills and leadership qualities are required.
</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects and initiatives while maintaining a strong sense of urgency.
</li>
<li>Must be a self-starter with ability to work independently within a matrix environment where tight timelines exist</li>
<li>
Candidate must have reliable transportation to and from work on a daily basis for the duration of the co-op.
