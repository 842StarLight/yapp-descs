<h2>Agent Reservations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The incumbent in this position is responsible for handling hotel room reservation and concert ticket requests in an efficient, courteous manner and for up-selling the hotel to achieve maximum efficiency. Also responsible for handling casino guest and providing all the same services in relation to their player status.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
GENERAL ACCOUNTABILITIES</b></p>
<p>
(The following statements are intended as general illustrations of the work in this class and are not all inclusive for specific positions.)</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
1. Keep informed of all room types, packages and rates; describes rooms in a manner that will up sell the hotel.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
2. Keep informed of all concert information, ticket prices, seat assignments and offers concert information in a manner that will result in a sale.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
3. Offer alternative choices for rooms and concerts (days of week, dates, room types, seat assignments) to all callers where the initial request cannot be achieved.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
4. Check availability for all requested dates through the computer to ensure current and accurate status is given.Checks availability for all concert tickets requested to ensure current and accurate information is given.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
5. Read casino guest accounts and books reservations or offers complimentary based on player activity.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
6. Has a working knowledge of the PBX board and the policies and procedures of that department.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
7. Keep up on all functions and special events pertaining to the Hard Rock Hotel and Casino.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
8. Follow through on all changes pertaining to guest reservations and ticket requests.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
9. Cooperate and coordinate with all related departments to provide the best possible service.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
10. Provide superior service to all customers on each and every shift and has the resiliency to deal with the difficult customer.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
11. Use all the above to provide answers to inquiries on the Internet.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
WORKING CONDITIONS</b></p>
<p>
Must have ability to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Use the equipment associated with the position, including but not limited to SMS Software, Ticket Master, and a PC with Internet access/connection, NEC-Electra Professional Key telephone system and IGT Casino System.</li>
<li>Use a computer effectively to access, understand and input customer information.</li>
<li>Review and comprehend all necessary documentation.</li>
<li>Operates in a working environment that is sedentary in nature and subject to varying levels of noise.</li>
<li>Speak English is required.</li>
<li>Type 25 word per minute.</li>
<br>
</ul>
