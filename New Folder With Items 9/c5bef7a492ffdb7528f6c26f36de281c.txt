<h2>Shipping Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This position is responsible for shipping product out of the fulfillment center. This position also helps coordinate the daily shipping demands and provides feedback to CSRs, Salespersons, and Management as to the status of shipments.</p>
<p>
Basic functions of this position include:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Daily shipping of small parcel packages via UPS, Fed Ex, USPS through the shipping management software (Pitney Bowes TMS) by entering required data to process the shipments.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Shipping LTL shipments by obtaining rate quotes, process the shipment through the shipping software based on the rate quotes obtained, stretch wrapping, banding with a skid top, printing and securing skid tags, finalizing the paperwork, and notification of the appropriate carrier for pickup.</li>
<li>Work directly with CSRs and Sales Reps to ensure all customer shipments, deliveries, and pickups are completed in a timely manner and to the satisfaction of the customer.</li>
<li>Resolve, any, and all issues (delivered or shipped to an incorrect address, damaged product, lost product, late delivery, etc.) that pertain to a shipment, delivery, or a pickup.</li>
<li>Help with any and all other aspect of the fulfillment center as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong communication skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>Computer knowledge (Microsoft Office)</li>
<li>Ability to learn, and grasp the knowledge of the new software as introduced</li>
<li>Knowledge, or the ability to learn and understand the requirements of foreign shipments</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task</li>
<li>The ability to lift up to 70 pounds repeatedly</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced, high volume environment</li>
<li>Detailed orientated – each client is unique</li>
<li>Ability to operate manual and electric power pallet jack</li>
<li>Dependability - due to the limited number of employees able to use the shipping software.</li>
<li>Overtime will be required at times (early morning, evenings, weekends, &amp; possibly holidays)</li>
</ul>
<p>Physical Demands of this Position:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bending, Lifting, Twisting, Sitting, Stacking, and Walking frequently throughout the day</li>
<li>Lifting up to 70 pounds frequently</li>
<li>Pushing and pulling</li>
<li>Perform activities that use the whole body</li>
</ul>
<p>The person in this position “MUST” recognize they are the last person to handle the product prior to the end customer. Therefore, it is our goal that all employees use common sense and exercise the right to stop work when the quality of a job is uncertain, or questionable, until a determination has been made.</p>
