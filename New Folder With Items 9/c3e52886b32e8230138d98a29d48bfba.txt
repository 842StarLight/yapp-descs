<h2>Staff Accountant/Accounts Payable Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>ABOUT AGILIS SYSTEMS, LLC</b></p>
<p>We are a leader in the exploding space of GPS, location-based vehicle tracking and field service management systems. We provide fleet and asset tracking solutions for businesses and consumers served through our web-based and mobile SaaS solutions. Today we have more than 50,000 customers and track more than 175,000 vehicles and assets nationwide. We’ve been experiencing explosive growth, and we’re positioned for even more in the years ahead.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ABOUT THE POSITION</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a Staff Accountant / Accounts Payable Representative who will <i>be responsible for general accounting and accounts payable activities in our growing accounting department. The position is ideal for a self-driven individual with accounting experience who can deliver results in a timely basis, own the accounts payable process, create and propose process improvements, and be held accountable for time-sensitive deliverables.</i></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS – RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare journal entries, perform account reconciliations, and assist with month end closing process
</li>
<li>Process vendor invoices &amp; expense reports
</li>
<li>Reconcile credit card statements
</li>
<li>Prepare and perform weekly check runs</li>
<li>Process weekly/monthly online credit card payments</li>
<li>Research any Accounts Payable issues or discrepancies
</li>
<li>Prepare Purchase Orders when necessary</li>
<li>Process manual payroll checks when necessary</li>
<li>Maintain vendor files, including W-9’s, for all vendors</li>
<li>Administer sales and use tax process, including filing returns
</li>
<li>Maintain information for annual Personal Property Tax Declaration</li>
<li>Maintain confidentiality of organizational information</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement accounting procedures by analyzing current procedures; recommending changes</li>
<li>Continuously seeks quality process improvements</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's or Associates Degree in Accounting
</li>
<li>1+ years of experience in General Accounting and or Accounts Payable, preferred</li>
<li>Must be detailed oriented and accurate in Accounting process</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively, in writing and verbally, with vendors and all levels of the organization</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Excel, Word, and QuickBooks, preferred
</li>
<li>Ability to work both independently and collaboratively with all levels of the staff and management both as a self-starter and a team player</li>
<li>Must be able to plan, organize, multitask and prioritize tasks to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to develop department goals and objectives with minimal supervision to be consistent with corporate vision and strategies
</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge with Concur or other accounts payable systems, preferred</li>
</ul>

</ul>
