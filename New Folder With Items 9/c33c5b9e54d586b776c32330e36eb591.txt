<b>
Description</b><div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
<b>MISSION:</b> The Texas Education Agency (TEA) will improve outcomes for all public-school students in the state by providing leadership, guidance, and support to school systems.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>POSITION OVERVIEW</b>: The Department of Grant Compliance and Administration is seeking a highly motivated, experienced, and talented project manager to join a team committed to fulfilling the mission of TEA by managing accountability for agency contracts, procurements, and grants.</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Contract &amp; Grant Accountability Specialist (CGAS) performs advanced project management work which involves coordinating the planning, initiation and monitoring of contract, procurement, and grant projects at various levels of completion and communicating with project stakeholders, management, and other relevant parties.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>A successful candidate must demonstrate exceptional organizational skills, meticulous attention to detail, the ability to analyze and communicate complex processes, and outstanding customer service to all agency staff and stakeholders.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>This position reports to the Contracts &amp; Grants Accountability Manager and works under minimal supervision, with considerable latitude for the use of initiative and independent judgment.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Please note that a resume and tailored cover letter are required attachments for applying to this position. Incomplete applications will not be considered.</b></div>
<b><br>
</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Oversee and monitor multiple project timelines and financial data to ensure accurate tracking and reporting of project funding and status to stakeholders</div></li>
<li><div>
Track and monitor implementation of project processes and “time since last touch”</div></li>
<li><div>
Communicate, consult, and coordinate with department and agency staff to ensure accurate project monitoring</div></li>
<li><div>
Analyze systems, project phases, workflow, and processes to identify opportunities for continuous improvement to ensure compliance and efficiency</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist in the development of ongoing training for agency and division staff on project life cycles of contracts and grants</div></li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b><div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b></div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<b>Education: </b>Graduation from an accredited four-year college or university; major coursework in project management, business, public administration, finance, or a related field preferred.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Experience: </b>A minimum of four [4] years of experience in 1) project management, 2) administering and/or managing state and federal funds, 3) developing and/or monitoring contracts, grants, and procurement timelines and processes, or 4) customer service. Formal experience and/or certification in project management is preferred. New hires must possess or obtain the Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM)® or Project Management Professional (PMP)® certification within one year of employment.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Substitutions: </b>Each additional year of related experience above the required minimum may substitute for education on a year-for-year basis</div>
<br>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>OTHER QUALIFICATIONS:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Highly organized, with meticulous attention to detail and the ability to manage multiple projects at once and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Practical knowledge demonstrated use of project management theories, practices, and tools, including workflow and process mapping, applicable to projects of varied scope</li>
<li>
Experience in grant and contract administration, business, and accounting practices</li>
<li>
Highly skilled in Microsoft Office applications and managing the lifecycle of complex technical documents</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Smartsheet, or similar project management software, PeopleSoft Financials, Adobe Suite, and online survey tools such as Qualtrics</li>
<li>
Ability to engage in a collaborative, results-oriented team environment</li>
<li>
Committed to establishing and maintaining positive and effective working relationships with all levels of internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>
Self-starter that can take independent initiative to research, develop, and propose solutions to improve business practices and processes</li>
<li>
Fast learner who thrives in a high-performing environment</li>
<li>
A track record of performance and successful implementation across multiple concurrent projects</li>
<li>
A desire to self-reflect, give/receive feedback and continuously improve as part of a team committed to growth and continuous improvement</li>
</ul>
