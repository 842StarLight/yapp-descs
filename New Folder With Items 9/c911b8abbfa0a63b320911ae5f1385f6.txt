<h2>Marketing Website Producer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Committee for Children is growing, and we're looking for an experienced <b>marketing website producer </b>to join our team! As a member of the marketing and client relations (MCR) team, you'll have experience in website management and digital marketing and be a skilled web producer. This role requires collaboration with stakeholders across marketing, creative, and technology teams to identify and drive innovative website solutions that achieve organizational goals and increase customer engagement.
</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities Include</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Managing web projects from beginning to end, from gathering of requirements, working with other team members on design and development, to testing, launch, and support
</li>
<li>Managing the building and optimization of web pages with an eye to UX and marketing objectives</li>
<li>
Modifying web pages within the CMS tools (WordPress, Contentful, wiki, etc.) in response to team content updates and correction requests</li>
<li>
Updating and optimizing owned ecommerce solutions and placement in other ecommerce channels</li>
<li>
Working with web designers and developers to produce digital assets in accordance with CFC's brand guidelines</li>
<li>
Acting as primary liaison between marketing and MCR business stakeholders and web development team, collaborating and maintaining close relationships with cross-functional teams to ensure alignment</li>
<li>
Anticipating, troubleshooting, and fixing site health issues
</li>
<li>Building and maintaining complete project schedules that are constantly visible to key stakeholders and that drive projects through to completion
</li>
<li>Monitoring web analytics KPIs, creating reports, and identifying opportunities for optimization</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Five or more years of experience working in an in-house marketing department or advertising/marketing agency as a website producer, website manager, or website specialist
</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in marketing, business, or related field
</li>
<li>Experience working cross-functionally in a professional setting, synthesizing feedback from stakeholders such as technology, marketing, and sales</li>
<li>
Familiarity with using Microsoft Office 365, Wrike, specific content management systems (WordPress, Contentful and wiki website), marketing automation systems, and various analytic tools</li>
<li>
Experience managing interactive and mobile projects</li>
</ul>
