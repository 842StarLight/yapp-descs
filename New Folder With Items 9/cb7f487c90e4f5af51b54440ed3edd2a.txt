<h2>Engineering Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Target Start Date:</b> Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis
<br><b>Reports To:</b> Engineering Manager | Data Acquisition Group
<br><b>Locations:</b> San Francisco, CA, Los Angeles, CA, or New York, NY
<br><br>
Carbon Lighthouse is looking for motivated, resourceful, and well organized Engineering Coordinators to join our Engineering Team, the Team responsible for developing and delivering our clean energy service in buildings.
<br><br>
The Engineering Team has expanded rapidly in geography, headcount, and technical complexity, leading to an increasingly diverse and specific set of needs. This role is designed to focus attention on these needs, which include field deployments, equipment/sensor inventory management, data management, contractor and vendor interactions, project-based accounting, utility interactions, and internal information systems. This position will provide technical and logistical support that enables the entire Engineering Team to be more effective in pursuing our ambitious goals.
<br><br>
This position is a great opportunity for someone excited by a diverse array of responsibilities, and who wants to learn about running a complex engineering operation. The ideal candidate is proactive and a team player who is highly organized and efficient. Success in the medium term is bringing more efficiency to the growing complexity of engineering needs. Success in the long term is finding ways to simplify, automate, or eliminate these needs so Engineering Coordinators can work on the next set of challenges.
<br><br>
<b>About Carbon Lighthouse:</b><br>
Carbon Lighthouse is on a mission to stop climate change by making it easy and profitable for building owners to eliminate carbon emissions caused by wasted energy. The company’s unique approach to Efficiency Production goes deep into buildings to uncover and continuously correct hidden inefficiencies that add up to meaningful financial value and carbon elimination that lasts. Since 2010, commercial real estate, educational, hospitality and industrial customers nationwide have chosen Carbon Lighthouse to enhance building comfort, increase net operating income and achieve their sustainability goals.
<br><br>
We are a team that highly value question asking, getting it done, integrity, and teamwork. We appreciate a fulfilling work-life balance, prize transparency and communication, hold ourselves to high standards of performance and professionalism, strive for dynamism and innovation, and support our team members’ professional development.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage, maintain, coordinate, and troubleshoot an inventory of data loggers and sensor equipment</li>
<li>Organize and stock gear bags and other field equipment</li>
<li>Participate in on-site field work, including deploying/retrieving sensors</li>
<li>Download and run processing scripts for a variety of utility, Building Management System, weather, and other metered data</li>
<li>Set up automated alerts within a variety of HVAC and Solar PV software systems</li>
<li>Obtain OEM equipment information, building drawings, and other relevant information sources</li>
<li>Perform basic energy modeling work and/or information intake for modeling</li>
<li>Identify, prepare, and submit utility incentive documents, and gather/verify applicable utility rate schedules</li>
<li>Process, track, and communicate invoices, purchase orders, and other service related payments</li>
<li>Identify, onboard, vet, and maintain contractors and their required information</li>
<li>Schedule client and contractor bid walks, site visits, and other inspections</li>
<li>Process, receive, and compile RFPs and RFIs from bidding events</li>
<li>Update applicable Engineering and Sales information systems, and communicate required inputs to Team</li>
<li>Find and implement solutions to automate many of these responsibilities</li>
</ul>
