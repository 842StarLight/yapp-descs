Marketing Coordinator
<br><br>
Rakuten Marketing, (formerly LinkShare), now RAN has been ranked the #1 Affiliate Network for the 8th year in a row. Why go anywhere else?
<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
The Marketing Coordinator is responsible for timely and satisfactory execution of administration of client programs, contributing to client satisfaction and the achievement of client and Rakuten Marketing revenue goals. This is a strong support role working closely with Account Managers and Clients to provide the utmost level of service.
<br><br>
In this role, you will gain experience managing client accounts to implement marketing strategies across multiple verticals including travel, luxury brands, business to business and specialty retail; to expand market reach, increase engagement, and maximize omni-channel digital campaigns.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage communication and act as liaison between publishers, advertisers, account managers, and clients</li>
<li>Monitor client support phone calls and emails and route inquiries appropriately</li>
<li>Ensure client issues are resolved in a timely manner</li>
<li>Develop strategic plans to meet and exceed client goals</li>
<li>Review partnered affiliate websites to identify optimization opportunities</li>
<li>Monitor projects to ensure deadlines are met</li>
<li>Prepare and run performance reports</li>
<li>Create prospect lists using reports and independent research</li>
<li>Attend quarterly client visits to review top-line program performance and marketing strategy</li>
<li>Represent Rakuten Marketing at industry events/trade shows</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Skills and Experience:</b><br>
Entry level candidates welcome. Bachelor's degree required. Preferred experience could include business or marketing degree, 1-2 years marketing or administrative experience, customer service.
<br><br>
<b>Capabilities required:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record of execution</li>
<li>Technical Abilities: Must be able to quickly learn proprietary software to manage campaigns and report results. Solid proficiency with Excel for client-ready reports and presentations. Ability to learn PowerPoint and Rakuten tools and processes</li>
<li>Communication Skills: Exemplary written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Detail orientation</li>
<li>Attention to deadlines</li>
</ul>
Employment eligibility to work with Rakuten and it's brands in the U.S. is required as the company will not pursue visa sponsorship for these positions.
