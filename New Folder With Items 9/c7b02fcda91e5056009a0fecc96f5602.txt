<p>We are looking for a talented <b>Senior Financial Analyst </b>to join our team in our Raleigh, NC offices.</p>
<p>LTG is a fast-growing provider of services and technologies for digital learning and talent management, with revenues in excess of $160m and operations spanning the US, Europe and Asia. The Senior Financial Analyst is responsible for preparing and reporting on the Group's professional service projects, ensuring accurate and timely reporting to management and driving the effectiveness and efficiency of the reporting processes. The Senior Financial Analyst is also responsible for the preparation and reporting of the â€˜Incentive Compensation Plan' for the global sales team.
</p>
<p>The individual will work closely with the Sales and Professional Service organizations to oversee business process, completeness and accuracy of data recorded across the Company's ERP, CRM and internal project systems and the reporting of KPIs for the businesses. This position is located in Raleigh, NC and reports to the Director, Revenue Operations.</p>
<p><b>What you'll be doing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage professional service projects from initial setup to closing.</li>
<li>Review project performance with Professional Service and Finance management for completeness and accuracy.</li>
<li>Analyze monthly variances between actual results and budget for each Business Unit and provide business insights and recommendations.</li>
<li>Perform monthly service commission entries.</li>
<li>Maintain a thorough understanding of the organization's compensation plans to ensure payments are accurate and in compliance with plan documents.</li>
<li>Administer and manage day-to-day operations using SalesForce software. Track, research and resolve commission calculation enquiries.</li>
<li>Provide recommendations for operational efficiencies and process improvements</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>About You</b></p>
<ul>
<li>University degree in Accounting or equivalent.</li>
<li>Minimum 5-year experience in industry required with at least 2 years in a similar role</li>
<li>Must be proactive, detail oriented, and able to execute efficiently in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Strong people skills with the capacity to operate cross-functionally to resolve issues.</li>
<li>Strong technical skills including experience with Net Suite (or similar operating system) and SalesForce preferred. Advance Microsoft experience required.</li>
</ul>
