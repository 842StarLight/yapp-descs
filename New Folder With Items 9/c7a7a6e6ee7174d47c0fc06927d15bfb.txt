<b>About the Position:</b><br>
The Segment Marketing Manager will work closely with sales &amp; marketing leadership to design and execute customized marketing deliverables aimed at converting targeted prospect accounts within a specified segment. Your key mandate for this role will be to provide the sales team with targeted programs and tools that will enable them to strategically engage prospective contacts, move the conversation forward, eventually leading to a sale.
<br><br>
The successful candidate will have 5+ years of experience managing multi-channel, B2B marketing programs targeting enterprise level accounts, and will have solid experience working in close collaboration with sales.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Segment Marketing Lead and Enterprise sales team on ideas and strategy to penetrate targeted accounts using account-based marketing techniques</li>
<li>Manage kick-off of campaigns, set timelines and expectations for completion of tactics, provide regular updates to leadership, track results and ROI</li>
<li>Coordinate digital marketing, direct mail, and other tactics to drive engagement with specific executives within targeted accounts</li>
<li>Develop value-based messaging, assets and tools that will assist pre-sales team in securing introductory meetings with decision makers within identified accounts</li>
<li>Coordinate with broader marketing teams to create industry and/or account specific experiences (virtual and face-to-face)</li>
<li>Build strong working relationships and establish credibility with sales team and broader marketing organization</li>
<li>Responsible for supporting sales pipeline revenue targets as well as managing marketing plan and budget for assigned segment</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of hands-on experience launching and managing marketing campaigns to completion</li>
<li>Knowledge of Salesforce.com, and marketing automation systems</li>
<li>Experience in Account targeting and Executive targeting tactics</li>
<li>Strong project management, analytical and collaborative skills</li>
<li>A self-starter that can work within a matrixed marketing environment</li>
<li>Creative thinker that can adapt to new marketing strategies and tactics</li>
<li>Superior verbal and written communication skills</li>
</ul>
