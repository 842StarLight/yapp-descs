<h2>Creative Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Creative Project Manager is a team player that works with multiple key stakeholders within the agency to ensure we effectively plan and manage projects that produce great work and gets results for our clients. You are able to:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage work to efficiently flow through the agency and maximize the billable efficiency of the agency.</li>
<li>
Effectively plan projects, assign resources, manage projects, and schedule work weeks out at a time to 100% of the agency's capacity.</li>
<li>
Manage brand, marketing, and web engagements ranging from 3–12 months and from $25,000–$500,000 in scope.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
About the Agency:</b></div>
<div>
Up&amp;Up uplifts brands that uplift people. We provide brand, marketing, and digital services for our higher education clients to help them attract more of the right students.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Role Requirement</b></div>
<div>
You have agency experience. You have or are currently serving as a Project Manager, Resource Manager, Traffic Manager or Studio Manager (or similar role) with an advertising agency.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Role Functions</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
<b>
Project Planning. </b>Jobs/projects are properly estimated, planned, and scheduled, from the sales estimate stage to scheduling resources and tasks for the studio to do the work. You have an in-depth understanding of strategy, creative, marketing, and digital (web) scopes of work.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
<b>
Resourcing</b>. Resources are kept billable and are able to meet their billable quotas as you schedule work for team members. You also ensure at any given time, we have the right talent, and the right amount of talent, to do the work. You identify new talent and capabilities the team needs, and you work with HR to have a resource pipeline including freelance backups available for each of our roles. You may also recommend that we reduce headcount should we be over resourced.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
<b>
Traffic + Project Management. </b>Jobs/projects are on time and on budget while maintaining an excellent work standard. You collaborate with the Account Executives and our Producers to ensure work is flowing efficiently through the studio and back to and from the client. Producers have clarity about the specific work they have (currently and in the future) and the amount of time they have to do the work.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
<b>
Accountability.</b> You're a team player and can hold people accountable/responsible, regardless of role or title, to meet deadlines, ensure time entry, stay within budget, and avoid scope creep (from clients or internally).<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
<b>
Forecasting + Reporting. </b>You are able to forecast work out weeks at a time to ensure 100% billable capacity, report key job metrics and provide<i> insight </i>to finance for planning and forecasting (AGI, utilization, project expenses, time, etc.).<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
5+ years of relevant experience with an agency</div></li>
<li><div>
Familiar with project management best practices and methods</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience with resourcing and managing utilization</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience working with creatives and managing creative projects</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience managing web and digital marketing projects</div></li>
<li><div>
Proficient with forecasting and project management tools</div></li>
<li><div>
Technically savvy, with experience working in web-based tools such as Gsuite, Trello, Slack, etc.</div></li>
<li><div>
Proficient with spreadsheets, Google Sheets a plus</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to work with numbers—doing math in your head doesn't scare you!<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
