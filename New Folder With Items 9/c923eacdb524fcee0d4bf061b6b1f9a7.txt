<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Prospects for business customers primarily through cold calling, networking, social media, email – all leading to face to face meetings</li>
<li>Builds, develops and nurtures customer relationships with contacts at prospective client accounts and sold accounts</li>
<li>Responsible for complex, well-defined sales opportunities</li>
<li>Expected to drive an internal team towards a well-defined end goal and ultimate sale</li>
<li>Drives revenue and maintains accountability for set goals and objectives</li>
<li>Attains and exceeds monthly sales quotas, as well as appointment and business plan minimums</li>
<li>Prepares and presents professional proposals and service solutions specific to the customer’s needs</li>
<li>Actively participates in various sales and skills training, both company provided and self-paced</li>
<li>Develops sales plans and penetrates new accounts in an assigned territory</li>
<li>Works with various internal departments – operations, HR, finance, professional solutions / analysts to ensure high quality solutions and ongoing account management</li>
<li>Understands the full array of CBPS service offerings and appropriate target accounts for each</li>
<li>Asks critical first, second and third level qualifying questions to understand what it will take to win a deal</li>
<li>Utilizes Salesforce.com to track all activity related to the sales cycle</li>
<li>Engaged in supporting and growing current client accounts at the discretion of the sales and operations management team</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
