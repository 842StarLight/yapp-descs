<h2>Digital Strategist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Description</b></p>
<p>CJRW's Little Rock office is seeking a highly creative and skilled Digital Strategist to join its growing and award-winning Digital Team. The Digital Strategist will work to identify needs, goals, opportunities and challenges for clients. The Digital Strategist will also help cross-functional teams develop and evolve ideas to bring together brand goals, retailer objectives and consumer needs.<br><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Set and implement digital marketing strategies using all necessary tools (e.g. website, emails, social media and blogs)</li>
<li>Digital Strategist will work with online advertising platforms including, but not limited to: Bing, Google, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat and LinkedIn</li>
<li>Analyze web traffic metrics and suggest solutions to boost web presence</li>
<li>Monitor and optimize SEM, marketing and sales performance metrics to forecast trends</li>
<li>Keep up-to-date with audience preferences and proactively suggest new campaigns</li>
<li>Identify advertising needs</li>
<li>Establish best practices in digital marketing</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date with digital technologies development</li>
<li>Assist in the needs of CJRW as requested<b>Job Specifications/Qualifications</b>
</li>
<li>Past experience at a marketing agency or with a marketing position</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively both verbally and in written form</li>
<li>A working knowledge of basic computer office programs</li>
<li>Past experience with Google Analytics, Google Ads, Facebook/Instagram Ads</li>
<li>Ability to effectively organize workload and prioritize tasks</li>
</ul>
