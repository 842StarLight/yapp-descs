<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Purpose and Objective:</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>SAP America, Inc seeks a Presales Expert at our Burlington, MA (and various client sites nationwide) location to interact with prospective customers through executive meetings, discovery conversations, solution demonstrations, executive presentations and follow-up discussions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Expectations and Tasks:</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Assist U.S. based customers during sales cycles as a Solution Captain. Identify internal sales methodologies and support techniques to collaborate with sales and IVE teams to plan and execute business development strategies, using Design Thinking tools and techniques. Compose and deliver superior sales presentations covering SAP and partner software solutions to prospective customer audiences. Prepare and deliver value-based software demonstrations and presentations in support of sales cycles. Support RFx completion in support of customer proposals, and “remotely” present those proposals using virtual technologies such as SAP Virtual Studio and Adobe Connect. Provide limited post-sale support to key customers primarily to the project and implementation team to ensure a smooth transition. Lead and support Design Thinking workshops to promote new and innovative solutions for customers and prospects, and collaborate with the sales team to identify whitespace opportunities at accounts. Develop close relationships with sales teams in order to promote effective sales methodologies, and participate in demo system design and planning. Assist in configuration if needed, as well as participate in new product release input and testing and training of peers and serve as a champion for or participate as a leader in Solution Hubs. 80% travel required to various client sites nationwide. Telecommuting available.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Education and Qualifications/Skills and Competencies:</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Math, Business Administration or a related field and 7 years of experience required. Company will also accept Master's and 5 years of experience.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work Experience:</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Experience must include 5 years in the following: Participate in Design Thinking style solution discovery; Identify general Best Practices for organizations that source, manufacture, and move merchandise from international sources; Identify the implications of, organizations that drive their business process either via "fashion" planning or Category Management style business processes; Lead prospects through a complex decision tree as it relates to their current business practices to highlight the proposed solutions ability to support and provide unique business value; Identify prospects current landscape of solutions to assist with determination of absolute prerequisites vs new capabilities provided by suggested new components; Provide leadership to the extended Virtual Account Team by bring forth competitor solutions short comings and/or key differentiators; SAP Retail Software Solutions such as IS-Retail (SAP Retail ERP) with experience in Master Data Management; Procure to Pay; Rebates and Allowances; Inventory Control and Management; Warehouse Management; Merchandise Planning; Assortment Planning; Advanced Forecasting &amp; Replenishment; and Customer Activity Repository. 80% travel required to various client sites nationwide. Telecommuting available.</p>
