<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Federal Business Development Manager is responsible for new buisness in the Federal space. Is proficient at planning and executing an effective relationship with a System Integrator to develop strategy and maintain effective long-term business relationships at the senior level with key decision makers and influencers. Identifies and prioritizes HireVue opportunities and develops solutions strategies with the System Integrator. Strategically plans and facilitates and/or conducts complex negotiations reaching lasting agreements and commitments. Responsible for developing an effective pipeline account plan. Responsible for revenue, wallet share, and customer satisfaction in the Federal space.</p>
<p>
Role requires strong experience in both Business Development and strategic solution selling bringing together software, hardware, and services. An entrepreneurial mindset with both Commercial &amp; Federal Sales background is desired to create the most flexible offerings with the goal of increasing HireVue's revenue position. Also responsible for overall capture responsibilities (Sell Through) for several System Integrators (SIs) focused on the Federal Government. Demonstrated track record in dealing with all levels of buyers from C-Suite to procurement when appropriate. Farmiliarity with US Federal contracts and procurement practices and policies. Deep understanding of state and local legislative organizations and processes, as well as policies and policymakers of interest and importance in the company. Hunter mentality required to identify new, emerging or white space SIs and partners that could be added to the ecosystem to grow the overall business unit results. Strong understanding and proven business results relative to HireVue’s strategic imperatives around AI/ML, Analytics, Cloud and Security.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Skills and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Ability to maintain a sales pipeline/funnel at least 3x sales goals on a quarter to quarter basis</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Generating revenue and growth in Strategic Accounts within a market (eg., DoD, Federal Civilian, Intelligence)</li>
<li>Developing holistic account and agency campaigns resulting in measurable market share growth
</li>
<li>Work with systems integrators / partners to extend reach and drive adoption of HireVue solutions</li>
<li>Identify, specify and lead sales enablement activities required to support your accounts</li>
<li>Developing and executing key account strategies and customer-oriented sales plans; executing call plans from C-level to the system administrator and mobility program management team customer levels; engaging government and defense program managers and decision makers and influencers</li>
<li>Pipeline and portfolio management; forecasting account revenues</li>
<li>Creating account plans through visual tools (PowerPoint etc) and presenting to internal/external leadership to articulate HireVue’s compelling value proposition
</li>
<li>Own and fully manage responses to RFPs, RFIs and all other types of prospect communications</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal partners (e.g., product, engineering, customer success) and colleagues to provide use case and product feedback, develop fresh approaches to utilizing the system, understand winning strategies, and champion sustained success.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What We Value:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong Knowledge and Experience with US Federal contracts and procurement practices and policies; Ability to translate federal government strategies, budgets, policies, and other requirements to create sales strategies and plans of action</li>
<li>Provide accurate sales projections monthly and quarterly; proactively implement corrective action measures as needed</li>
<li>You strive for operational excellence, ability to effectively utilize your own time, and are passionate about demonstrating the power of technology to help tackle some of the most important ML challenges.</li>
<li>End state vision, focus, mindset; urgency to action; focus on critical path to objectives; development/articulation of deal close plans; automatically and instinctively knows which key factors govern any situation and outcome as well as risks and accelerators</li>
<li>Self-starter with excellent organizational and prioritization skills to execute with high tempo on a daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly and annual basis</li>
<li>Proactive, transparent, fact-based communicator; highly accountable and visible; excellent team building skills</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills - Ability to analyze data and provide recommendations that drive strategic account/business plan development and action.</li>
<li>Competitive, driven, persistent, and positive by nature; competitive individual and/or team sports background helpful</li>
</ul>
